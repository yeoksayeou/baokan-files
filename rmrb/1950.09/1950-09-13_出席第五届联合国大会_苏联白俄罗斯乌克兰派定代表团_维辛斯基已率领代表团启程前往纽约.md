Author:
Date: 1950-09-13
Page: 4
　　
　　Attending the Fifth UN General Assembly
    USSR, Byelorussian SSR, and Ukrainian SSR Appoint Delegations
    Vyshinsky Leads Delegation to New York
    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th] Comprehensive TASS dispatch of the 11th: The Council of Ministers of the USSR, the government of the Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic, and the government of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic have respectively appointed delegations to the Fifth UN General Assembly, which will open in New York on September 19th. The head of the Soviet delegation is Vyshinsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR; representatives are: Malik, Soviet Representative to the Security Council and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Panyushkin, Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Zarubin, Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom, and Arutiunian, member of the Special Committee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the USSR; deputy representatives are: Tadzhiayev, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic, and Babakhodzhayev, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic. Delegation advisors: Podtserob, Tsarapkin, Khvostov, Morozov, Roschin, and Zonov.
    The head of the delegation of the Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic is Kiselyov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Byelorussian SSR. Members are: Skoropogaty, Astapenko, and Gomsky.
    The head of the delegation of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic is Baranovsky, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Ukrainian SSR, members are: Voina, Stadnik, Demchenko, and Udovichenko.
    Vyshinsky, head of the Soviet delegation, and the delegations of the Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic and the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic, all departed for New York on the 11th.

### 出席第五届联合国大会  苏联白俄罗斯乌克兰派定代表团  维辛斯基已率领代表团启程前往纽约

1950-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　出席第五届联合国大会
    苏联白俄罗斯乌克兰派定代表团
    维辛斯基已率领代表团启程前往纽约
    【新华社十二日讯】综合塔斯社十一日讯：苏联部长会议，白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国政府和乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国政府已分别任定出席九月十九日于纽约开幕的第五届联合国大会的代表团。苏联代表团团长为苏联外交部部长维辛斯基；代表为：苏联驻安全理事会代表、苏联外交部副部长马立克，苏联驻美国大使潘友新，苏联驻英国大使查鲁宾及苏联外交部专门委员会委员亚鲁疆；副代表为：哈萨克苏维埃社会主义共和国外交部部长塔日达耶夫，乌兹贝克苏维埃社会主义共和国外交部部长巴巴霍德雅耶夫。代表团顾问：波德切罗夫、查拉普金、赫沃斯托夫、莫罗佐夫、罗辛及佐诺夫。
    白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国代表团团长为白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国部长会议副主席兼外交部长基塞列夫。团员为：斯科罗波加提、阿斯塔平科及贺姆斯科。
    乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国代表团团长为乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国部长会议副主席巴拉诺夫斯基，团员为：伏伊那、斯塔德尼克、杰姆琴科与乌多维琴科。
    苏联代表团团长维辛斯基，白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国代表团及乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国代表团均已于十一日启程赴纽约。
