Title: Vietnamese Young Pioneers Workers Return to Vietnam After Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1950-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, dated 26th: Vietnamese Young Pioneers workers, including Wu Guang and five others, came to Beijing, the capital of our country, on July 13th with the Vietnamese student delegation attending the Second World Student Congress. After staying for more than a month, they returned to Vietnam yesterday. During their stay in Beijing, they visited the North China Experimental Nursery, the Nursery of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the Beijing Municipal First and Second Nurseries, and the Jie Ru Nursery to learn about the work of childcare. They also visited the Beijing Summer Children's Park, the Young Pioneers Camp, and the Children's Reading Garden (jointly organized by the Beijing Municipal Library and the Youth Service Department) and had gatherings with children. The Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the Young Pioneers Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League also held seminars with them on the work of the Young Pioneers, children's education, childcare work, and maternal and child health to exchange information and experience.

### 越南少年儿童工作者来我国参观后返越

1950-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南少年儿童工作者来我国参观后返越
    【新华社二十六日讯】越南少年儿童工作者武光等六人于七月十三日随出席世界学生第二次代表大会的越南学生代表团来我国首都北京，逗留月余后，于昨日返越南。他们在北京期间，曾先后参观了华北实验保育院、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会托儿所，北京市立第一、第二托儿所、洁如托儿所，了解儿童保育工作情况；他们还访问了北京市暑期儿童乐园、少年营、儿童阅览园（北京市立图书馆与青年服务部合办）并和小朋友们联欢。中央人民政府教育部、中华全国民主妇女联合会、青年团中央委员会少年儿童部并曾就少年儿童队工作、儿童教育、保育工作、妇婴卫生等问题与他们举行了座谈会，交换情况和经验。
