Title: Korean People's Army Continues Offensive North of Daegu and on the South Coast
Author:
Date: 1950-09-13
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 12th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 11th that People's Army units on all fronts are overcoming the enemy's stubborn resistance and continuing their offensive.

　　People's Army units fighting north and northwest of Daegu on the east bank of the Nakdong River and People's Army units that liberated Angang and other important strategic strongholds are continuing their offensive operations.

　　On September 9th, People's Army units repelled an enemy counterattack under the cover of tanks in a battle south of Angang. In this area, People's Army units killed more than 300 enemy officers and soldiers, captured 57, and seized four medium tanks, five rocket launchers, and much other equipment.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 12th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 11th that People's Army units that liberated Wei, Dabudong, and occupied other important enemy defensive positions north of Daegu are continuing their offensive operations.

　　In the area south of Dabudong, American troops, including motorized divisions, are putting up fierce resistance in an attempt to stop the offensive of the People's Army units. The People's Army units are crushing the enemy's resistance, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy in personnel and equipment, and continuing to advance southward. The People's Army killed and captured many enemy officers and soldiers in these battles.

　　People's Army units on the South Coast are inflicting serious blows on the enemy, who are counterattacking from strong defensive positions, and are advancing everywhere.

　　On September 7th, People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down an American fighter plane on the east coast.

### 在大邱以北和南海岸方面  朝鲜人民军继续进攻

1950-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　在大邱以北和南海岸方面
    朝鲜人民军继续进攻
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十一日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线克服敌军的顽抗，继续进攻中。
    在洛东江东岸大邱以北和西北作战的人民军部队与解放安康和其他重要战略据点的人民军部队，继续进行攻击战。
    九月九日，人民军部队在安康以南的战斗中，击退了敌军在坦克掩护下的反攻。在该地区，人民军部队击毙敌军官兵三百余人，俘掳五十七人，缴获中型坦克四辆、火箭炮五门及其他装备甚多。
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十一日晚发表战报称：解放军威、多富洞并占领大邱以北其他重要敌军防御阵地的人民军部队，继续进行攻击战。
    在多富洞以南地区，包括摩托化师在内的美军进行猛烈抵抗，企图阻止人民军部队的攻势。人民军部队摧毁敌军的抵抗，使敌军在人员和装备上遭受重大损失，并继续向南推进。人民军在这几次战斗中，毙、俘敌军官兵甚多。
    南海岸方面的人民军部队，予凭借坚强防御阵地进行反攻的敌人以严重的打击，并正在各处挺进中。
    九月七日，人民军高射炮部队在东海岸击落美军战斗机一架。
