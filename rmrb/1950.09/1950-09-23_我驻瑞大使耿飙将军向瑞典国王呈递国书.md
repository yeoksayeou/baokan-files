Title: General Geng Biao, Our Ambassador to Sweden, Presents Credentials to the King of Sweden
Author:
Date: 1950-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, 22nd - General Geng Biao, our first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Kingdom of Sweden, arrived in Sweden on the 12th and presented his credentials to King Gustav V of Sweden on the 19th, which were accepted by Crown Prince Gustav Adolf on his behalf. (Note: The King of Sweden has not personally accepted credentials for a long time due to his advanced age.)

### 我驻瑞大使耿飙将军向瑞典国王呈递国书

1950-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　我驻瑞大使耿飙将军向瑞典国王呈递国书
    【新华社二十二日讯】斯德哥尔摩消息：我国驻瑞典王国首任特命全权大使耿飙将军，已于十二日抵达瑞典，并于十九日向瑞典国王古斯塔夫五世呈递国书，由王太子古斯塔夫·阿道尔夫代表接受。（按瑞典国王因年事过高，久已不亲自接受国书。）
