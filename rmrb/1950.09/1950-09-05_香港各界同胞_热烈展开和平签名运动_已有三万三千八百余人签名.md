Title: Hong Kong Compatriots Enthusiastically Launch Peace Signature Campaign: Over 33,800 Signatures Collected
Author:
Date: 1950-09-05
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 4th: The Hong Kong Ta Kung Pao and Wen Hui Pao newspapers, since initiating a signature campaign among their readers in early July for the Stockholm Peace Appeal, have received enthusiastic responses from compatriots in Hong Kong from all walks of life. According to preliminary statistics, the readers of the two newspapers who participated in the signing, including workers, vocational youths, students, teachers, women, children, and many industrialists and business people, as well as social celebrities, totaled more than 33,800. This signature campaign with broad representation fully demonstrates the determination of the Chinese people in Hong Kong to defend peace. The peace signature campaign is still continuing.

### 香港各界同胞  热烈展开和平签名运动  已有三万三千八百余人签名

1950-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　香港各界同胞
    热烈展开和平签名运动
    已有三万三千八百余人签名
    【新华社广州四日电】香港大公、文汇两报，自七月初发动读者在斯德哥尔摩和平宣言上签名后，已获得香港各界同胞的热烈响应。据初步统计，参加签名的两报读者，包括工人、职业青年、学生、教师、妇女、儿童和不少工商业界人士及社会名流等，共三万三千八百余人。这个有着广泛代表性的签名运动，充分证明了香港中国人民保卫和平决心。现和平签名运动仍在继续开展中。
