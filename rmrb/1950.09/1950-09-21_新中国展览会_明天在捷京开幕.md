Title: "New China Exhibition" to Open Tomorrow in Prague

Author: 
Date: 1950-09-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 20th: The "New China Exhibition," sponsored by the Czechoslovak government and the Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship Association, will open on the 22nd at the "House of Artists" in the central district of Prague. The exhibition is scheduled for one month. Exhibits include various Chinese pictures, woodcuts, New Year pictures, antiques, handicrafts, and various books and publications.

　　The Czechoslovak government, the Communist Party, the Trade Union Federation, and other mass organizations have formed a publicity committee for the exhibition. The committee will organize 500,000 people from all over Czechoslovakia to visit the capital. The Czech Radio Station has been introducing the content of the exhibition daily since the 13th, and all cinemas in Prague are showing slideshow advertisements for the exhibition.

　　During the exhibition, Prague will hold evening parties featuring Chinese films, music, poetry recitations, and lectures on Chinese issues. Chairman Mao Zedong's speech at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art, which has been translated into Czech, as well as the reports by Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, and others at the First National Congress of Literary and Art Workers of China, will be published successively during the exhibition.

### “新中国展览会”明天在捷京开幕

1950-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　“新中国展览会” 明天在捷京开幕
    【新华社布拉格二十日电】由捷克斯洛伐克政府和捷中友好协会主办的“新中国展览会”，将于二十二日在布拉格中心区“艺术家之屋”揭幕。展览会定期一月。展览品包括中国的各种图片、木刻、年画、古物、手工艺品及各种书籍出版物。
    捷克斯洛伐克政府、共产党和总工会及其他群众团体，已组成展览会的宣传委员会。该委员会将组织捷克各地的五十万人民至首都参观。捷广播电台已于十三日开始逐日介绍展览会内容，布拉格各电影院都在放映展览会的幻灯广告。
    展览会期间布拉格将举行晚会，节目有中国电影、音乐、诗歌朗诵，及中国问题的演讲。已译成捷文的毛泽东主席在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话，以及中华全国第一届文学艺术工作者代表大会上郭沫若、茅盾、周扬等人的报告，均将在展览会期间陆续出版。
