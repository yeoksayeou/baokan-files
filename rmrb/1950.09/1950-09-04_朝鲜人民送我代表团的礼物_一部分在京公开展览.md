Title: Gifts from the Korean People to Our Delegation: Part of Them on Public Display in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-09-04
Page: 1

Gifts from the Korean People to Our Delegation
    Part of Them on Public Display in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd] A portion of the gifts from the Korean people to the Chinese People's Delegation are on display in Beijing. These gifts were recently brought back by the Chinese People's Delegation, headed by Guo Moruo and Li Lisan, after their visit to Korea to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the Korean People's Liberation. Among them are war trophies, photographs, watercolors, books, and periodicals. With the consent of the delegation, the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations Affairs of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government has displayed the above-mentioned part of the gifts in the "Photo Exhibition in Commemoration of the Fifth Anniversary of the Liberation of the Korean People," organized by the Bureau, starting from September 1st, and has extended the exhibition period by two weeks to allow the general public to have a further understanding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 朝鲜人民送我代表团的礼物  一部分在京公开展览

1950-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民送我代表团的礼物
    一部分在京公开展览
    【新华社三日讯】朝鲜人民送给我国人民代表团的礼物一部分在京展览。这些礼物是最近以郭沫若、李立三为首的我国人民代表于赴朝鲜庆祝朝鲜人民解放五周年后带回来的，其中一部分是战利品、照片、水彩画、书刊等。中央人民政府文化部对外文化联络事务局征得代表团同意后已将上述一部分礼物自九月一日起陈列于该局所主办的朝鲜人民解放五周年纪念图片展览会中公开展览，并将展览会期延长两周，以便广大观众对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国有更进一步的认识。
