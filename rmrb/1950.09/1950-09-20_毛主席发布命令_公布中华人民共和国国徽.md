Title: Chairman Mao Issues Order Announcing the National Emblem of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1950-09-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 20th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government issued an order on the 20th announcing the National Emblem of the People's Republic of China. The full text of the order is as follows:

　　Order of the Central People's Government

　　The design of the National Emblem of the People's Republic of China and the explanation of the design, proposed by the Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, have been approved by the Eighth Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee, and are hereby promulgated.

　　This order is hereby issued.

　　Chairman Mao Zedong
　　September 20, 1950

### 毛主席发布命令  公布中华人民共和国国徽

1950-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席发布命令
    公布中华人民共和国国徽
    【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席于二十日明令公布中华人民共和国国徽。命令全文如下：
    中央人民政府命令
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第二次会议所提出的中华人民共和国国徽图案及对该图案的说明，业经中央人民政府委员会第八次会议通过，特公布之。
    此令
    主席  毛泽东
    一九五○年九月二十日
