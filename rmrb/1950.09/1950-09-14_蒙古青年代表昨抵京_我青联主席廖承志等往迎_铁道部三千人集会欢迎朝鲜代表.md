Title: Mongolian Youth Representatives Arrive in Beijing Yesterday; All-China Youth Federation Chairman Liao Chengzhi and Others Greet Them; 3,000 People from the Ministry of Railways Gather to Welcome Korean Representatives
Author:
Date: 1950-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: Mongolian People's Republic youth representatives from the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, Nadrodozhi, Sandag, and Ishkhan (female), arrived in Beijing by plane from Mongolia on the 13th. Representatives from the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, and the Beijing Student Federation came to the airport to welcome them. Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, extended a warm welcome to the Mongolian youth representatives on behalf of the Chinese youth. The Mongolian youth representatives expressed their heartfelt respect to the Chinese youth. A deep brotherly friendship between the youth of China and Mongolia was evident.

After a short rest upon arriving in Beijing, Nadrodozhi, Sandag, and Ishkhan (female) left Beijing this evening with Korean representative Quan Zonghuan to go to Shenyang, where they will join other national representatives of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation to visit and tour various places in Northeast China.

This newspaper reports: More than 3,000 union members and Youth League members from the directly affiliated organizations of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government gathered on the afternoon of the 13th to welcome Quan Zonghuan, the Korean representative of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation. The eleven road protection labor model candidates from the Public Security Bureau of the Ministry of Railways who attended the National Congress of Labor Model Representatives of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers also attended. Quan Zonghuan gave a report on the Korean National Liberation War at the meeting. At the meeting, labor model candidates such as Tang Zhongguo presented flowers to the Korean youth representative, and also presented a plaster statue of Chairman Mao and two copies of the "Railway Worker Pictorial." During the presentation of flowers and gifts and the report, applause often lasted for a long time, demonstrating the infinite care and respect of Chinese railway workers for the fighting Korean youth and people. ### 蒙古青年代表昨抵京  我青联主席廖承志等往迎  铁道部三千人集会欢迎朝鲜代表

1950-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒙古青年代表昨抵京
    我青联主席廖承志等往迎
    铁道部三千人集会欢迎朝鲜代表
    【新华社十三日讯】世界民主青年联盟代表团蒙古人民共和国青年代表那卓道之、桑达格、和伊什汉（女）三人十三日由蒙古乘飞机到达北京。中华全国民主青年联合总会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、北京市人民政府、青年团北京市工作委员会及北京市学生联合会等单位代表都到机场欢迎。中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志代表中国青年向蒙古青年代表致欢迎之忱。蒙古青年代表向中国青年致衷心的敬意，相互间充沛着中蒙两国青年深厚的兄弟友谊。
    那卓道之、桑达格、和伊什汉（女）抵京后稍事休息，即于今晚与朝鲜代表全宗焕相偕离京去沈阳，将与世界民主青年联盟代表团其他各国代表会合，共同在东北各地参观并访问。
    【本报讯】中央人民政府铁道部直属机关工会会员和青年团员三千余人，十三日下午集会，欢迎世界民主青年联盟代表团朝鲜代表全宗焕。铁道部公安局出席全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的十一名护路劳模候选人亦出席参加。全宗焕在会上作了关于朝鲜民族解放战争的报告。会上由劳动模范候选人唐忠国等向朝鲜青年代表献花，并献毛主席石膏像一座及《铁路工人画刊》两册，在献花、献礼和报告进行中，掌声常经久不息，表现了中国铁路员工对战斗中的朝鲜青年和人民的无限关怀和敬意。（附图片）
