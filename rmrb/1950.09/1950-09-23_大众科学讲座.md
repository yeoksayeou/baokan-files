　　Popular Science Lecture
    The Popular Science Lecture, jointly organized by the Youth Service Department and the Central People's Science Museum, will hold the fourth lecture of September at 7:00 PM on Saturday, September 23rd, at Yuying Middle School (Dengshikou). Comrade Wang Jinlin will speak on: "Paper". Please collect tickets at the Youth Service Department.

### 大众科学讲座

1950-09-23
第10版()
专栏：

　　大众科学讲座
    青年服务部、中央人民科学馆合办的大众科学讲座，于九月二十三日（星期六）下午七时在育英中学（灯市口）举行九月份第四讲。请王金林同志讲：“纸”。入场券请至青年服务部领取。
