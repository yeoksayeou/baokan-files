Title: Color Documentary Film "The Victory of the Chinese People" to Be Released Soon in Major Cities

Author:

Date: 1950-09-04
Page: 1

　　Color Documentary Film
    "The Victory of the Chinese People" to Be Released Soon in Major Cities
    [Xinhua News Agency, 3rd] The first print of the color documentary film "The Victory of the Chinese People" was previewed in Beijing. The film is a joint production of the Beijing Film Studio of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government and the Soviet Central Documentary Film Studio, directed by the famous Soviet director Varlamov. Filming began last winter, and it has been completed in ten months through the close cooperation of Chinese and Soviet filmmakers. The content of the film mainly depicts the heroic struggle of the Chinese people in the People's Liberation War and the great achievements of the Chinese people's revolution. The film is currently being printed in large quantities in the Soviet Union and is being rushed to China. It will soon be released simultaneously in major cities across the country on a date to be determined.

### 五彩记录影片  《中国人民的胜利》不久将在各大城市公映

1950-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　五彩记录影片
    《中国人民的胜利》不久将在各大城市公映
    【新华社三日讯】《中国人民的胜利》五彩记录影片第一部拷贝在京试映。该片系中央人民政府文化部电影局北京电影制片厂与苏联中央文献电影制片厂联合摄制的，由苏联名导演瓦尔拉莫夫编导。于去年冬季开始摄制，在中苏两国电影工作者的通力合作下，历时十个月，业已完成。该片内容，主要是描写中国人民在人民解放战争中的英勇斗争和中国人民革命获得胜利的伟大事迹。该片现正在苏联加印拷贝，赶运来华，不久即可择期在全国各大城市同时公映。
