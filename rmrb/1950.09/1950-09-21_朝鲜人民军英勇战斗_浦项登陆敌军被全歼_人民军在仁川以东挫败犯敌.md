Title: Korean People's Army Fights Heroically, Enemy Troops Landing at Pohang Annihilated, People's Army Defeats Enemy Attacks East of Incheon
Author:
Date: 1950-09-21
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 20th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 19th that on September 18th, People's Army units repelled enemy attacks on all fronts and engaged in fierce battles with the enemy.

　　On September 17th and 18th, People's Army units annihilated all enemy troops who had landed north of Pohang.

　　People's Army units continued to fight stubbornly against enemy landing forces in the Incheon area, thwarting the enemy's advance and suppressing and blockading them.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 20th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 19th that on September 19th, People's Army units repelled enemy counterattacks on all fronts, inflicting heavy losses, and continued to engage in fierce battles with the enemy.

　　North, northwest, and on the southern coast of Daegu, the enemy concentrated all their forces and launched multiple counterattacks. People's Army units thwarted the enemy's counterattacks, inflicting heavy losses, and continued to attack.

　　In the Incheon area, People's Army units continued to engage in fierce battles with the blockaded enemy landing forces.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 20th: TASS, Pyongyang, 20th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced that People's Army units east of Incheon, on the highway to Seoul, continued to engage in fierce battles with American and British landing forces. People's Army units put up stubborn resistance against the attacking enemy, who were supported by tanks and aircraft.

　　The enemy's attempt to cross the Han River in the Gimpo Airport area has been repelled by People's Army units.

### 朝鲜人民军英勇战斗  浦项登陆敌军被全歼  人民军在仁川以东挫败犯敌

1950-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军英勇战斗
    浦项登陆敌军被全歼
    人民军在仁川以东挫败犯敌
    【新华社平壤二十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十九日晨发表战报称：九月十八日，人民军部队在各战线上击退敌军的攻击，与敌军进行激烈的战斗。
    九月十七、十八两日，人民军部队歼灭了在浦项以北登陆的全部敌军。
    人民军部队在仁川方面与敌军登陆部队继续进行顽强的战斗，挫败了敌军的进展，并予以压制和封锁。
    【新华社平壤二十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十九日晚发表战报称：九月十九日，人民军部队在各战线上击退敌军的反攻，予以重创，并继续与敌军进行激战。
    在大邱以北、西北及南海岸地区，敌军集中全部兵力，多次进行反攻。人民军部队挫败敌军的反攻，予以重创，并继续进攻。
    在仁川地区，人民军部队与被封锁的敌军登陆部队继续进行酷烈的战斗。
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社平壤二十日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部宣称：人民军部队在仁川以东，通往汉城的公路上，继续与美、英登陆部队进行激战。人民军部队对在坦克和飞机掩护下进攻的敌军进行顽强的抵抗。
    敌军企图在金浦机场地区渡过汉江，已被人民军部队击退。
