Title: 462 Delegates to National Model Worker Representative Conference Have Reported

Author:
Date: 1950-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: As of today, 462 representatives from various units attending the National Conference of Representatives of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers have reported to the conference preparatory committee. This includes 399 representatives from major administrative regions, central government departments, and the national railway system, and 63 representatives from the military. Many representatives have been interviewed by numerous Chinese and foreign reporters at their residences.

In the evening, all the representatives were invited to attend a welcome reception hosted by the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and various people's organizations in Beijing.

### 出席全国劳模代表会议  已有四百六十二人报到

1950-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席全国劳模代表会议
    已有四百六十二人报到
    【新华社二十三日讯】出席全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的各单位代表，本日已有四百六十二人向大会筹备委员会报到。其中包括各大行政区及中央直属机关、全国铁路系统代表三百九十九人，部队代表六十三人。许多代表分别在住所接受中外记者多人的访问。
    晚间，全体代表应邀参加了北京市人民政府、中国共产党北京市市委员会、北京市各人民团体的欢迎晚会。
