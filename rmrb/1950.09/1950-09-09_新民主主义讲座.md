Date: 1950-09-09

　　New Democracy Lectures
    The New Democracy Lectures, sponsored by the Beijing YMCA, will be held on Saturday, September 9th at 7:30 PM. Mr. Zhang Naiqi, a representative of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, will give a special lecture on: "Experiences and Thoughts on Visiting Korea." All circles are welcome to attend.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-09-09
第8版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
    京市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座，定九月九日（星期六）晚七时半，特请中国人民访朝代表团代表章乃器先生讲：《访问朝鲜经过与感想》。欢迎各界前往听讲。
