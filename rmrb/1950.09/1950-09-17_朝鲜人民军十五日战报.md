Title: Korean People's Army Fifteenth Day War Report
Author:
Date: 1950-09-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th - The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 15th that: The People's Army units on the southern coast continue to attack the enemy.

The remaining troops of the U.S. 25th Division and the main force of the Marine Corps on the southern coast, relying on rivers and highlands to construct defensive positions, increased the deployment of tanks and heavy weapons, and launched fierce counterattacks continuously under the cover of aircraft.

The People's Army units repelled the enemy's counterattacks and continued to strike the enemy. The People's Army units operating on the east bank of the Nakdong River bravely launched offensive battles against the U.S. troops and the remnants of the Li Chengwan puppet army who concentrated a large number of troops in that area and attempted to counterattack.

The People's Army units, regardless of the continuous bombing by U.S. aircraft, crushed the enemy's frantic counterattacks, occupied the main strongholds of the U.S. defense line, and deepened into the enemy's defensive positions in many places to expand the battle results.

On September 13, the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery unit shot down a U.S. fighter plane that invaded the Pyongyang area.
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th - The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 15th that: The People's Army units continued to engage in fierce battles with the U.S. troops and the remnants of the Li Chengwan puppet army on all fronts.

The enemy, who suffered heavy losses due to the People's Army units' offensive, reinforced their troops and redeployed, attempting to carry out a dying resistance under the cover of aircraft.

The People's Army units crushed the enemy's resistance on all fronts and continued to inflict heavy losses on the enemy.

On the east coast front, the defeated troops of the so-called "Capital Division" and the Third Division of the remnants of the Li Chengwan puppet army, which suffered heavy blows, attempted to launch a fierce counterattack with the support of U.S. troops. The People's Army units in that area gave the enemy a fierce blow, thwarted the enemy's attempt to counterattack, and continued to launch offensive battles.

On the front south of Waegwan and Tabu-dong, the People's Army units repelled the enemy's repeated counterattacks, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy, crushing the enemy's resistance, and continuing to advance in many places.

### 朝鲜人民军十五日战报

1950-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军十五日战报
    【新华社平壤十六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十五日晨发表战报称：南海岸方面的人民军部队向敌人继续攻击中。
    在南海岸方面的美军第二五师残余部队和海军陆战队的主力部队，依据河川及高地构筑防御阵地，增调坦克和重武器，在飞机的掩护下连续进行猛烈的反击。
    人民军部队击退敌人的反击，继续打击敌人。在洛东江东岸方面行动中的人民军部队，对该方面集中大量兵力、企图反击的美军部队和李承晚残余伪军勇敢地进行攻击战。
    该方面的人民军部队不顾美军飞机的不断轰炸，粉碎了敌人的疯狂反击，占据美军防线的主要据点，并在许多地点深入敌人的防御阵地扩大战果。
    九月十三日，人民军高射炮部队击落侵袭平壤地区的美军战斗机一架。
    【新华社平壤十六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十五日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与美军部队和李承晚残余伪军继续进行激烈的战斗。
    由于人民军部队的进攻而遭受巨大损失的敌人，增强兵力进行重新部署，在飞机掩护下企图进行垂死的反抗。
    人民军部队在全部战线上，粉碎敌人的反抗，继续使敌人遭受重大损失。
    在东海岸方面战线上，遭受重大打击的李承晚残余伪军的所谓“首都师团”和第三师团溃败部队，在美军部队的支援下，企图进行猛烈的反击。在该方面的人民军部队，给予敌人猛烈的打击；挫败了敌人反击的企图，继续展开攻击战。
    在倭馆和多富洞以南的战线上，人民军部队击退敌人的多次反击，使敌人遭受重大的损失；粉碎敌人的反抗，在许多地点继续前进中。
