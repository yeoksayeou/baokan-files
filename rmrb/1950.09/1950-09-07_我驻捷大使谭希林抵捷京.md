Title: Our Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Tan Xilin, Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1950-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 6th. General Tan Xilin, the first appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Czechoslovak Republic, together with his wife and embassy staff, arrived in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, on the morning of the 5th. Those who went to the station to welcome him included Czechoslovak Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs Bolek, Taufer, London, and Sekaninová, Mayor of Prague Vasik, Commander of the Capital Garrison General Ejem, representative of the President's Office Knaru, representative of the Premier's Office Krasni, Soviet and various People's Democratic countries' envoys to Czechoslovakia, as well as Secretary-General of the Czechoslovak Central Council of Trade Unions Kolsi, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Youth League Hrzal, and over a hundred representatives from all walks of life.

### 我驻捷大使谭希林抵捷京

1950-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　我驻捷大使谭希林抵捷京
    【新华社布拉格六日电】我国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国首任特命全权大使谭希林将军偕夫人及大使馆人员等一行，于五日晨抵达捷京布拉格。赴车站欢迎的，有捷克斯洛伐克外交部副部长包列克、陶伐尔、隆登和塞卡尼诺娃，布拉格市长瓦西克，首都卫戍司令埃耶姆将军，总统办公室代表克那鲁，总理办公室代表克拉斯尼，苏联和各人民民主国家驻捷使节以及捷总工会总书记科尔斯基，捷青年联盟主席赫兹拉尔及各界代表百余人。
