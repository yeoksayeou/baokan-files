Title: Big Red Flowers Bloom All Over Beijing
Author: Shiyi (适夷)
Date: 1950-09-24
Page: 3

Big Red Flowers Bloom All Over Beijing
    Shiyi

    The heroes are coming to Beijing
    They are coming to Beijing on the Mao Zedong locomotive
    The Mao Zedong locomotive pulls into the East Station
    The heroes walk out from the platform

    Push and shove, push forward
    Let us shake their hands, let us embrace them
    Let us kiss them hard
    And lift them high above our heads

    The flower-presenting girls come forward
    Big red flowers bloom on the heroes' chests
    Big red flowers bloom on the roads the heroes walk
    Colorful paper confetti is scattered on the heroes
    The heroes are surrounded by joy

    Hey, Zhao Guoyou, hey, Tian Guiying
    Waving frantically, shouting desperately
    What we welcome
    Are our own relatives from afar

    Zhao Zhankui smiles at us
    Li Yong nods happily
    Liu Meicun salutes solemnly
    Zhao Guilan, with only one hand, claps with joy
    Grandma Rong Guanshou sheds tears of excitement
    The Mosuo girls and Guanshou
    Are happily a little shy

    So many people's heroes
    They have all come to Beijing
    Each one is a big red flower of the people
    Big red flowers bloom all over Beijing

### 大红花开满北京城
适夷
1950-09-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　大红花开满北京城
    适夷
    英雄们上北京来了
    坐着毛泽东号上北京来了
    毛泽东号开进东车站
    英雄们从月台上走出来了
　
    挤啊挤啊、挤上前去啊
    让我们去握手、让我们去拥抱
    让我们狠狠的亲亲他们
    把他们高高的举起头顶
　
    献花的女孩子迎上去了
    大红花开在英雄的胸头
    大红花开满英雄走过的路
    五色彩纸撒在英雄身上
    英雄们被欢乐包围起来了
　
    喂、赵国有、喂、田桂英
    拚命的招手拚命的唤啊
    我们欢迎的
    是自己远来的亲人
　
    赵占魁向着我们笑啦
    李永快乐的点着头
    刘梅村行个庄严的军礼
    一只手的赵桂兰也欢喜的拍手
    老奶奶戎冠秀兴奋的流泪
    摩些少女和国秀
    高兴得有点害羞
　
    多么多的人民英雄
    他们全上北京来啦
    一朵朵都是人民的大红花
    大红花开满了北京城
