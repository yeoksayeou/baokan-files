Title: Solemn Burial of the Remains of Former DPRK Vice-Minister of National Defense, Kang Kon; General Kim Il-sung and Others Personally Carried the Casket to the Cemetery; Supreme People's Assembly Posthumously Awarded the Title of Hero

Author: 
Date: 1950-09-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 16th: The funeral of Kang Kon, Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, former Vice-Minister of National Defense and Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, who gloriously sacrificed himself on the front lines of the liberation war on September 8th, was solemnly held here on the 11th. The leader of the Korean people, General Kim Il-sung, and Chairman Kim Tu-bong both attended in person, and all leaders of the Republic's government and leaders of various democratic political parties and social organizations also participated in the funeral. Foreign guests included Soviet Ambassador Sidorov, Chinese Ambassador Ni Zhiliang, Hungarian Minister Sandor, and Khorulausien Tsumidov, First Secretary of the Embassy representing Mongolian Ambassador Zhamso Rong.

　　The funeral was conducted in an atmosphere of deep sorrow and even greater animosity towards the American aggressors. At the funeral, Minister of National Defense Choe Yong-gon delivered a eulogy. Choe Yong-gon detailed the glorious achievements and great contributions of Vice-Minister Kang Kon in the national liberation war against Japanese imperialism and American imperialism, and he vowed that the Korean people were determined to completely annihilate the American invading troops and the remnants of the Syngman Rhee bandit army to avenge Vice-Minister Kang Kon. Subsequently, leaders of the Korean party and government delivered condolences, unanimously expressing their determination to inherit the deceased's legacy and fight to the end for the final victory of the Korean people's liberation war.

　　Amidst the solemn and stirring strains of a memorial song, General Kim Il-sung, Chairman Kim Tu-bong, and leaders of the party, government, army, and people personally carried the casket to the cemetery for burial.

　　It is reported that Vice-Minister Kang Kon was born in 1918 into a poor peasant family in Gyeongsangbuk-do. During his student days, he participated in the Communist Youth League in Northeast China. In 1933, he joined the anti-Japanese guerrilla forces led by General Kim Il-sung and resolutely carried out the anti-Japanese armed struggle for more than ten years. After the "August 15th" liberation, he returned to his motherland and made great contributions to the cause of creating the Korean People's Army. On August 25, 1948, he was elected as a delegate to the Supreme People's Assembly. After the establishment of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in September of the same year, he was appointed as Vice-Minister of National Defense and Chief of the General Staff of the People's Army. He made outstanding contributions in the Korean national liberation struggle against the armed invasion of American imperialism and the Syngman Rhee bandit gang.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 16th: The Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an order on the 9th, posthumously awarding the late Chief of General Staff Kang Kon the title of Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and also awarding him a First Class National Flag Order and a Gold Star Medal each.

### 朝鲜前民族保卫副相姜健遗体隆重安葬  金日成将军等亲自抬灵至茔地  最高人民会议追授予英雄称号

1950-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜前民族保卫副相姜健遗体隆重安葬
    金日成将军等亲自抬灵至茔地  最高人民会议追授予英雄称号
    【新华社平壤十六日电】九月八日在解放战争前线光荣殉国的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国英雄、前民族保卫副相兼人民军参谋总长姜健的葬礼，于十一日在此间隆重举行。朝鲜人民领袖金日成将军与金枓奉委员长均亲临参加，共和国政府各首长及各民主政党、社会团体领袖亦均参与葬礼。外宾参加者有苏联大使西地科夫、中国大使倪志亮、匈牙利公使鲜道尔、蒙古大使扎姆索伦代表大使馆一等秘书霍鲁劳先最米道夫等。
    葬礼在深沉的悲痛及对美国侵略者更大的敌忾气氛中进行。葬礼中由民族保卫相崔庸键致永诀词。崔庸键详述了姜健副相在抗击日本帝国主义与美帝国主义侵略的民族解放战争中的光荣业迹与伟大功勋，他并宣誓：朝鲜人民决心彻底歼灭美国侵略军队和李承晚残余匪军、为姜健副相复仇。继由朝鲜党、政首长致吊词，一致表示要继承死者遗志，为争取朝鲜人民解放战争的最后胜利而战斗到底。
    在悲壮的追悼曲声中，金日成将军、金枓奉委员长及党、政、军、民首长亲自抬灵至茔地安葬。
    按姜健副相于一九一八年生于庆尚北道的贫农家庭。学生时代在中国东北参加了共产主义青年同盟，一九三三年参加金日成将军所领导的抗日游击队，十余年如一日，坚决地进行了抗日武装斗争。“八、一五”解放后回到祖国，在创建朝鲜人民军的事业中，贡献很大。一九四八年八月二十五日，他当选为最高人民会议代议员。同年九月朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府成立后，被任为民族保卫副相兼人民军总参谋长。在反抗美帝国主义武装侵犯者与李承晚匪帮的朝鲜民族解放斗争中功勋卓著。
    【新华社平壤十六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议于九日发布命令，特授与故姜健总参谋长以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国英雄称号，同时授予一级国旗勋章和金星奖章各一枚。
