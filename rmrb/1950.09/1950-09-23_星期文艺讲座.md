The Weekly Literary Lecture
The Mass Literature Creation and Research Association and the Youth Service Department are jointly hosting the Weekly Literary Lecture. This week, Luo Changpei will be the speaker, and the topic is "The Expression of Sound in Literature and Art." After the lecture, Wei Changlin and Luo Shihai will perform the Leting Drum performance *Early Marriage Harms*. The address is the Youth Palace. The time is Sunday morning at 9:30 AM. Admission is by long-term ticket or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be collected from the Youth Service Department on Saturday.

### 星期文艺讲座

1950-09-23
第10版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座
    大众文艺创研会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲座，本周请罗常培主讲，讲题是“文艺中的声音表现”会后由魏长林、罗世海表演乐亭大鼓《早婚害》地址在青年宫。时间在星期日上午九时半。凭长期券、临时券入场，临时券可于星期六到青年服务部领取。
