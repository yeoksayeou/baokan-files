Title: “I Wore a Red Flower on My Chest!”
Author: Li Hong, Ding Mang
Date: 1950-09-29
Page: 3
People's Garden

　　“I Wore a Red Flower on My Chest!”
    Oral account by People's Navy Hero Li Hong, Recorded by Ding Mang
    No matter when, I will remember
    That I wore a red flower on my chest.
    I will never forget this honor in my lifetime
    Until the last breath of my life!

    When I operate at my gun station
    Even if I'm so tired my head is spinning;
    When I sail in terrifying waves
    Even if I'm so seasick I can't hold on;
    When I fight the enemy on the ocean
    Even if I am unfortunately wounded;
    When I have accepted a mission
    Even if ten thousand difficulties block me;
    No matter when, I will remember
    That I wore a red flower on my chest.

    This red flower is the Party's trust, the people's encouragement;
    Here is honor, and also hope.
    When I remember it,
    I gain endless strength!
    This strength will enable me to overcome everything,
    And make me forever look up to the sun!

### “我胸前戴过红花！”
李洪　丁芒
1950-09-29
第3版()
专栏：人民园地

　　“我胸前戴过红花！”
    人民海军英模李洪  口述  丁芒  笔录
    无论在什么时候我都将记起
    我胸前戴过红花。
    我一辈子忘不掉这份光荣
    直到我生命的最后一息！
　
    当我在炮位上操作
    即使累得头昏脑胀；
    当我在骇人的大浪中航行
    即使晕得支持不下；
    当我在海洋上和敌人决战
    即使不幸而身负重伤；
    当我接受了任务以后
    即使有万重困难把我阻挡；
    无论在什么时候我都将记起
    我胸前戴过红花。
　
    这红花是党的信托，是人民的鼓励；
    这里有荣誉，也有希望。
    我记起了它的时候，
    我就平添了无穷力量！
    这力量将使我战胜一切，
    使我永远抬头面向太阳！
