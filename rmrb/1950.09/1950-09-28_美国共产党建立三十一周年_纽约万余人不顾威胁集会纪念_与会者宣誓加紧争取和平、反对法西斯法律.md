Title: 31st Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of the United States: Over Ten Thousand People in New York Rally in Defiance of Threats; Participants Vow to Intensify the Struggle for Peace and Against Fascist Laws
Author:
Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 27th: On September 19th, fourteen thousand New Yorkers, defying increasing intimidation, attended a rally in Madison Square Garden to commemorate the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of the United States. The vow to intensify the struggle for peace and against fascist police-state laws was met with enthusiastic cheers from those present. Former City Councilor and Black leader Davis said, "The persecution inflicted upon us is proof of our growing strength." Thompson, chairman of the New York State Committee of the CPUSA, said, "We fought the last world war not to have MacArthur stationed in the Far East, but to establish democracy... We fought not to expand the colonial system on the Asian continent, but to enable the people to be in power."
　　Hall, in a report to the enlarged meeting of the National Committee of the Communist Party, announced that the Communists would not register under the McCarran Act. Hall said, "If the Party is declared illegal, it will still function, promote the creative genius of American workers, develop new forms of struggle, produce new leaders, and constantly renew and enrich the Communist Party."

### 美国共产党建立三十一周年  纽约万余人不顾威胁集会纪念  与会者宣誓加紧争取和平、反对法西斯法律

1950-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国共产党建立三十一周年
    纽约万余人不顾威胁集会纪念
    与会者宣誓加紧争取和平、反对法西斯法律
    【新华社二十七日讯】电通社纽约二十六日讯：九月十九日，一万四千纽约人不顾增长的威吓，在麦迪逊广场参加了纪念美国共产党成立三十一周年的大会。加紧争取和平和反对法西斯警察国家法律的斗争的宣誓，受到与会者热烈的欢呼。前市议员黑人领袖戴维斯说：“加于我们身上的迫害，正是我们的力量逐渐增长的明证。”纽约州美共州委会主席汤普生说：“我们进行上次世界大战，不是为了使麦克阿瑟驻留远东，而是为了建立民主……我们作战不是为了扩张在亚洲大陆上的殖民制度，而是为了要使人民能够当权。”
    霍尔在向共产党全国委员会的扩大会议作报告时宣布：共产党人不会按照麦卡伦法案去登记。霍尔说：“如果党被宣布非法，它仍将发挥作用，发扬美国工人们的创造天才，发展新的斗争形式，产生新的领袖，不断地更新并充实共产党。”
