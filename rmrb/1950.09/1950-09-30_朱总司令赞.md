Title: Ode to Commander-in-Chief Zhu
Author: Yang Yangzi
Date: 1950-09-30
Page: 3

People's Garden

　　Ode to Commander-in-Chief Zhu
    Yang Yangzi
    At the heroes' grand meeting, Commander-in-Chief Zhu sits in the center,
    His dignified face carries a spring breeze,
    Streaked white hair facing a hall full of red (note),

    The great hero has cultivated many heroes,
    And the heroes have performed many miraculous feats,
    --His achievements pile up one after another!
    Commander-in-Chief Zhu is the hero among heroes!
  Note: The heroes wear flowers and red sashes
        Mid-Autumn Festival, 1950, Beijing

### 朱总司令赞
杨扬子
1950-09-30
第3版()
专栏：人民园地

　　朱总司令赞
    杨扬子
    群英大会会英雄，
    朱总司令坐当中，
    威风的面上带春风，
    斑斑白发对着满堂红（注），
　
    大英雄培养出许多英雄，
    英雄们又立了许多奇功，
    ——他的成绩一重重！
    朱总司令是英雄中的英雄！
  注：英雄们带花挂红
        五○年中秋，北京
