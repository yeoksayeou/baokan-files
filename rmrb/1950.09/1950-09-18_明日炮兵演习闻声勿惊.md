Title: Artillery Exercise Tomorrow: Do Not Be Alarmed By the Sound
Author:
Date: 1950-09-18
Page: 6

Notice:

　　An artillery shooting exercise will be conducted by a unit of the North China Military Region in Huangqiying Barracks on the morning of September 19th. It is hoped that people from all walks of life will not be alarmed by the sound. If it rains, the date will be postponed.

### 明日炮兵演习闻声勿惊

1950-09-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　明日炮兵演习闻声勿惊
    华北军区某部定于九月十九日上午，在黄旗营房举行炮兵射击演习。希各界闻声勿惊。如果下雨，日期顺延。
