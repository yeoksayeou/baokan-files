Title: Central South Military Region and Fourth Field Army Model Hero Representatives All Arrive in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1950-09-15
Page: 1

　　Central South Military Region and Fourth Field Army
    Model Hero Representatives All Arrive in Wuhan
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 14th] The People's Liberation Army Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army selected a total of ninety-five representatives of combat heroes and labor models, who have now all arrived in Wuhan and are about to depart for Beijing. Among them are seventy combat hero representatives, including Liu Meicun, representative of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and a famous combat hero; Zhao Xingyuan, an excellent young commander; Pu Enshao, a maritime hero in the Hainan Island campaign; Li Shuting, the company commander of the Hainan Island landing hero company; and Guo Junqing, a people's female combat hero. The militia hero representatives are Jin Anguo, Pan Kang, and three others. In addition, there are ten labor model representatives and ten representatives of uprising troops attending as observers. When the heroes and models from various units departed, they were warmly welcomed by the troops and local people. Farmers in Mengxu, Guixian County, Guangxi Province, happily performed Yangko dances and lion dances in the heavy rain to see them off. The workers of the Xiang-Gui Railway scrambled to ask the heroes sitting in the train to sign their names as a souvenir.

### 中南军区暨第四野战军  英模代表全部抵汉

1950-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　中南军区暨第四野战军
    英模代表全部抵汉
    【新华社汉口十四日电】人民解放军中南军区和第四野战军共选出战斗英雄、劳动模范代表九十五名，现已全部抵达武汉，即将启程赴京。其中战斗英雄代表七十名，包括中国人民政协代表、著名的战斗英雄刘梅村，优秀的青年指挥员赵兴元，海南岛战役中的海上英雄蒲恩绍，海南岛登陆英雄连连长李树庭和人民女战斗英雄郭俊卿等。民兵英雄代表为金安国、盘康等五人。此外有劳动模范代表和起义部队列席代表各十名。各部英雄模范们出发时，均受到各部队及当地群众的热烈欢送。广西贵县蒙圩的农民在大雨中高兴地扭秧歌、舞狮子欢送，湘桂铁路的工友们则抢着请坐在车上的英雄们签名，作为纪念。
