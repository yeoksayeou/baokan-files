Title: China People's Oppose American Aggression Committee Decides to Convene a Meeting Tomorrow Afternoon to Welcome the Return of Our Delegation to North Korea

Author:

Date: 1950-09-02
Page: 2

China People's Oppose American Aggression Committee
    Decides to Convene a Meeting Tomorrow Afternoon
    To Welcome the Return of Our Delegation to North Korea
    [From Our Own Correspondent] The China People's Committee to Oppose American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea has decided to hold a rally for people from all walks of life in Beijing at 6:30 PM on September 3rd (Sunday) at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall to welcome the return of the Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea. Delegation leader Guo Moruo, deputy leader Li Lisan, and representatives Zhang Naiqi, Xu Guangping, and others will report on their experiences visiting North Korea. The conference will broadcast musical recordings presented to the delegation by the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and will screen the latest film presented to the delegation after the meeting. If it rains on that day, it will be postponed by one day.

### 中国人民反对美国侵略委员会  决定明天下午召开大会  欢迎我访朝代表团返国

1950-09-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国人民反对美国侵略委员会
    决定明天下午召开大会
    欢迎我访朝代表团返国
    【本报讯】中国人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会决定于九月三日（星期日）下午六时半，假座中山公园音乐堂召开北京各界人民欢迎访问朝鲜中国人民代表团返国大会。由代表团团长郭沫若、副团长李立三及代表章乃器、许广平等报告访问朝鲜的经过情形。大会将播送朝鲜民主主义人民共和国赠送代表团的音乐唱片，会后并放映赠送代表团的最新影片。该日如遇雨顺延一天。
