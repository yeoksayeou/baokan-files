Title: U.S. Unjustly Arrests Qian Xuesen and Others; Guo Moruo Sends Telegram to Dr. Joliot-Curie, Urging Condemnation of U.S. Imperialism's Shameless Atrocities
Author:
Date: 1950-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, sent a telegram to Dr. Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, on September 25th, appealing to him to call on scientists around the world to condemn the U.S. imperialist atrocities of unjustly arresting Dr. Qian Xuesen and others, and demanding their immediate release. The telegram reads as follows: Dr. Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, Paris:

　　Our country's expert in aeronautical mechanics, Dr. Qian Xuesen, was arrested by the American police when applying to return to China. Professor of physics Zhao Zhongyao and two students were arrested by the U.S. military stationed in Japan in Yokohama while returning to China. Such atrocities of trampling on human rights and destroying scientists have aroused widespread anger in Chinese scientific circles and among the Chinese people. Please call on scientists around the world, together with your council, to condemn the atrocities of U.S. imperialism and demand the immediate release of the arrested scientists.

　　Chairman of the China Peace Committee Guo Moruo
　　September 25, 1950

### 美国无理拘捕钱学森等  郭沫若致电居里博士  吁请谴责美帝无耻暴行

1950-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国无理拘捕钱学森等
    郭沫若致电居里博士  吁请谴责美帝无耻暴行
    【新华社二十五日讯】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会主席郭沫若九月二十五日致电世界和大主席居里博士，吁请号召全世界科学家谴责美帝无理拘捕钱学森博士等暴行，并要求立即予以释放。电文如下：巴黎世界和大主席居里博士：
    我国航空力学专家钱学森博士于申请回国时被美警拘捕，物理学家赵忠尧教授和学生二名在返国途中，在日本横滨为驻日美军拘捕。此等蹂躏人权、摧残科学家的暴行，已激起中国科学界及中国人民的普遍愤怒。请你和贵会号召全世界科学家对美帝国主义暴行加以谴责，并要求立即释放被捕之科学家。
      中国保卫世界和平大会委员会主席郭沫若
            一九五○年九月二十五日
