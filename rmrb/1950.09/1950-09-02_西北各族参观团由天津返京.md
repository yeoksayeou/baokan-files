Title: Northwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-09-02
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 1st - The Northwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation, which had previously visited Tianjin on August 28th, returned to Beijing on the 1st. The delegation stayed in Tianjin for five days, visiting six factories and Tanggu. They were warmly welcomed by all sectors wherever they went. The Northwest Ethnic Youth Visiting Delegation remains in Tianjin to continue their visit.

### 西北各族参观团由天津返京

1950-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北各族参观团由天津返京
    【新华社一日讯】曾于上月二十八日前赴天津参观的西北各族参观团，已于一日返回北京。该团在津逗留五日，参观了六个工厂，并曾往塘沽参观，所到之地备受各界热烈欢迎。西北各族青年参观团尚留天津继续参观。
