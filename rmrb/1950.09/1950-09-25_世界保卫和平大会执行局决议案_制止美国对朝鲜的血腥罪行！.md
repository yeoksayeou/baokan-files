Author: F· Yuēliào· Jūlǐ
Date: 1950-09-25
Page: 9

　　Resolution of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Congress

    Stop the Bloody Crimes of the United States Against Korea!

    The Executive Bureau of the World Peace Congress Committee, meeting in Prague on August 18th when the war broke out in Korea, felt the terrible danger that the conflict might become total, and was deeply concerned about the serious destruction of life and property caused by the massive bombing of civilians. Therefore, it urgently requested the Security Council to seek a permanent solution to the conflict based on the wishes of the Korean people, as follows:

    —Immediately stop bombing civilians, —Stop fighting, —Withdraw all foreign troops, —Listen to the opinions of all parties concerned.

    The Executive Bureau of the World Peace Congress firmly believes that this proposal expresses the will of millions of men and women. The Executive Bureau also believes that it expresses the desire of the overwhelming majority of people around the world to maintain and consolidate peace.

             Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Congress, F· Joliot-Curie

### 世界保卫和平大会执行局决议案  制止美国对朝鲜的血腥罪行！
Ｆ·约里奥·居里
1950-09-25
第9版()
专栏：

　　世界保卫和平大会执行局决议案
    制止美国对朝鲜的血腥罪行！
    世界保卫和平大会委员会执行局于八月十八日，在布拉格会议通过一项决议案，向联合国安理会及各国政府提出立即制止轰炸朝鲜平民等四点建议。决议案全文如下：
    世界保卫和平大会委员会执行局，在战争发生于朝鲜的时候举行会议，感到冲突可能变成全面性的可怕的危险，同时对于大规模轰炸平民所造成的生命物资的严重破坏，关怀至深，因而迫切地请求安全理事会，在下述的基础上，适应朝鲜人民的愿望，寻求对冲突的永久解决：
    —立刻停止轰炸平民、—停止战斗行为、—撤退一切外国军队、—听取有关双方的意见。
    世界保卫和平大会执行局深信这项建议，表达了千百万男女的意志。执行局同样相信它表达了全世界压倒的大多数人民维持并巩固和平的愿望。
             世界保卫和平大会执行局主席Ｆ·约里奥·居里
