Title: National Combat Heroes and Labor Model Representatives Gather in the Capital; Two Representative Conferences to Open Today; Preparatory Meetings Held Yesterday Afternoon; Agreed on Presidium List and Meeting Agenda and Exchanged Congratulations

Author:
Date: 1950-09-25
Page: 1

　　National Combat Heroes and Labor Model Representatives Gather in the Capital
    Two Representative Conferences to Open Today
    Preparatory Meetings Held Yesterday Afternoon
    Agreed on Presidium List and Meeting Agenda and Exchanged Congratulations
    [Xinhua News Agency, 24th] The National Combat Heroes Representative Conference, scheduled to open tomorrow (25th), held a preparatory meeting this afternoon. The preparatory meeting was presided over by Xiao Hua, Secretary-General of the Conference Preparatory Committee. 360 official representatives and 64 observers were present. The official representatives included 350 combat heroes, model workers, and militia heroes, as well as 10 heads of the hero model representative delegations from various military regions and field armies. The meeting began with a report on the conference preparation process by Wei Chuantong, Deputy Secretary-General of the Conference Preparatory Committee, followed by a report on the regulations for the review of the qualifications of representatives to the National Combat Heroes Representative Conference and the results of the review of the qualifications of representatives based on these regulations by Zhang Jingwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Conference Preparatory Committee, which was unanimously approved by the conference. The meeting enthusiastically approved the list of the Presidium of the National Combat Heroes Representative Conference and the conference agenda, and also approved a congratulatory telegram from all representatives of the National Combat Heroes Representative Conference to the National Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Model Representative Conference.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 24th] The National Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Model Representative Conference, scheduled to officially open tomorrow (25th), held a preparatory meeting at 3:30 this afternoon.
    At the preparatory meeting, Li Lisan, Deputy Director of the Conference Preparatory Committee, explained the significance of the State Council of the Central People's Government convening the National Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Model Representative Conference. Mao Qihua, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee, reported on the progress of the conference preparations. Wu Juenong, a member of the Preparatory Committee, gave a report on the review of representative qualifications. The meeting approved the above two reports, and approved the list of the Presidium and the Secretary-Generals and Deputy Secretary-Generals, as well as the conference agenda. Finally, it was decided to present a flag to the National Combat Heroes Representative Conference and deliver congratulatory remarks.

### 全国战斗英雄劳动模范代表云集首都  两代表会议今开幕  昨日下午分别举行预备会议  通过主席团名单会议日程并互致祝贺

1950-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国战斗英雄劳动模范代表云集首都
    两代表会议今开幕
    昨日下午分别举行预备会议
    通过主席团名单会议日程并互致祝贺
    【新华社二十四日讯】将于明日（二十五日）开幕的全国战斗英雄代表会议在今日下午举行了预备会议。预备会议由大会筹备委员会秘书长萧华主持。到会有正式代表三百六十名，列席代表六十四名。正式代表中包括战斗英雄、工作模范及民兵英雄三百五十名，各军区及各野战军英模代表团团长十名。会议先由大会筹委会副秘书长魏传统报告大会筹备经过，继由大会筹委会副秘书长张经武报告全国战斗英雄代表会议代表资格审查条例及根据该条例审查代表资格的结果，经大会一致通过。会议上热烈通过了全国战斗英雄代表会议大会主席团名单及会议日程，并通过全国战斗英雄代表会议全体代表致全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的贺电。
    【新华社二十四日讯】将于明日（二十五日）正式揭幕的全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议于今日下午三时半举行预备会议。
    预备会议上，会议筹备委员会副主任李立三解释了中央人民政府政务院关于召开全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的意义。筹备委员会秘书长毛齐华报告了会议筹备经过。筹备委员会委员吴觉农作了代表资格审查报告。会议批准了上述两报告，并通过了主席团和正副秘书长名单以及大会日程。最后决定向全国战斗英雄代表会议献旗并致祝词。
