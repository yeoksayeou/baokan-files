Title: Land Reform Completely Victorious in Liberated Areas of Southern Korea; Over 200,000 Farmers in Gyeonggi Province Receive Land Certificates

Author:

Date: 1950-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 23rd: According to the Korean Central News Agency, the South Korean Land Reform Guidance Committee of the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced on the 14th that the land reform work in the southern half of Korea has been completely victorious, with the exception of some unliberated areas in Gyeongsangnam-do and Gyeongsangbuk-do.

The total area of ​​land confiscated in the newly liberated areas of the southern half is 541,973.9 *町步* [Jeongbo]. A total of 1,163,158 tenant farmers, landless and land-poor farmers have obtained land, with a total area of ​​520,329.6 *町步* [Jeongbo] distributed.

The liberated farmers, freed from the oppression and exploitation of American colonialists, the Syngman Rhee gang, and feudal landlords, sincerely thank their leader, General Kim Il-sung.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 25th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Seoul: The final step in implementing land reform in Gyeonggi Province—issuing land deeds to farmers who have been allocated land—was completed on September 14th. 281,419 farm households have received certificates of land ownership and certificates of land cultivation rights from the People's Government, including 8,690 tenant farmers, 60,884 landless farmers, 144,541 land-poor farmers, 771 farmers cultivating state-owned land, and 66,533 owner-farmers. For the first time ever, the farmers obtained land deeds and became the masters of the land, feeling boundless excitement. Farmers in various places held celebration meetings one after another, unanimously vowing to strive for increased agricultural production and to support the People's Army.

### 朝鲜南部解放地区土改全部胜利完成  京畿道廿余万农民领得土地证

1950-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜南部解放地区土改全部胜利完成
    京畿道廿余万农民领得土地证
    【本报讯】据新华社平壤二十三日电：据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国农林省南半部土地改革指导委员会于十四日公布：朝鲜南半部的土地改革工作，除庆尚南、北道一部分未解放地区外，已全部胜利完成。
    在南半部新解放地区没收土地的总面积共五十四万一千九百七十三点九町步。获得土地的雇农及无地、少地的农民共有一百十六万三千一百五十八户，分配土地的面积计有五十二万零三百二十九点六町步。
    摆脱了美国殖民者、李承晚匪帮和封建地主的压迫与剥削而成为土地的主人的翻身农民，衷心地感激自己的领袖金日成将军。
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社汉城讯：京畿道各地实行土地改革的最后一个步骤——对分得土地的农民颁发地照的工作，已于九月十四日完成。二十八万一千四百一十九户农家，已从人民政府手中领得了土地所有权证明书与土地耕种权证明书，其中包括雇农八千六百九十户，无地农民六万零八百八十四户，少地农民十四万四千五百四十一户，耕种国有土地的农民七百七十一户，自耕农六万六千五百三十三户。农民们破天荒第一次得到地照，作了土地的主人，感到无限的兴奋。各地农民纷纷举行庆祝大会，一致宣誓为农业增产与支援人民军而奋斗。
