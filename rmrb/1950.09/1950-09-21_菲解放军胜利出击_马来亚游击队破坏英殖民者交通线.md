Title: Philippine People's Liberation Army Victorious Attacks; Malayan Guerrillas Disrupt British Colonialists' Transportation Lines
Author:
Date: 1950-09-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th. According to Agence France-Presse, Manila: The mayor of San Luis City in Pampanga Province, Philippines, and 38 policemen were captured by the Philippine People's Liberation Army (PLA) on the evening of the 13th in operations on the outskirts of the city. On the 12th, the Philippine People's Liberation Army attacked four towns and villages in Arayat, Pampanga Province, killing two members of the militia and wounding two policemen. In addition, 100 PLA soldiers invaded Bonny City in Laguna Province, south of Manila.
　　According to United Press, about 500 PLA soldiers have been continuously attacking towns near Manila since September 10. Long-distance buses between Manila and nearby towns have been suspended.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Recently, the Malayan People's Armed Forces have continued to actively strike at British colonial troops and police and disrupt the transportation lines of the British colonialists. According to Reuters Singapore: Malayan people's guerrillas are operating 12 miles north of the federal capital, Kuala Lumpur. The British colonial government has warned white people not to walk on roads five miles outside the suburbs of Kuala Lumpur after sunset. On August 20, the Malayan People's Armed Forces attacked British colonial police in Kedah State, killing one policeman and wounding five. On August 28, the Malayan People's Armed Forces opened fire on British colonial troops searching for "bandits" in Pahang State, killing one British soldier and wounding one British officer and one soldier. On the night of the 1st, the Malayan People's Armed Forces destroyed the railway trunk line from Kuala Lumpur to Penang, causing the pilot vehicle in front of a train to derail. On the 2nd, the People's Armed Forces caused a train from Manda to Gemas to derail in Pahang State.

### 菲解放军胜利出击  马来亚游击队破坏英殖民者交通线

1950-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　菲解放军胜利出击
    马来亚游击队破坏英殖民者交通线
    【新华社十七日讯】据法新社马尼拉讯：菲律宾邦邦牙省圣路易斯城的市长和三十八名警察，于十三日晚在城郊作战中被菲人民解放军俘获。菲人民解放军曾于十二日袭击邦邦牙省阿拉雅特的四处市镇和乡村，击毙民团两名，击伤警察两名。另有一百名解放军曾攻入马尼拉以南内湖省的邦伊市。
    另据合众社讯：菲人民解放军约五百人自九月十日以来，不断即向马尼拉附近城镇进攻。马尼拉与附近城镇的长途汽车均告停驶。
    【新华社七日讯】马来亚人民武装最近继续主动地打击英殖民军警，并破坏英殖民者的交通线。据路透社新加坡讯：马来亚人民游击队在联邦首府吉隆坡以北十二英里处展开活动，英殖民政府已警告白种人，于日落后不得在吉隆坡市郊五英里外的路上行走。八月二十日，马来亚人民武装在吉打州袭击英殖民警察，结果击毙警察一名，击伤五名。八月二十八日，马来亚人民武装在彭亨州境内向搜巡“匪徒”的英殖民军开火，击毙英兵一名，击伤英军官及士兵各一名。马来亚人民武装于一日夜破坏了自吉隆坡至槟榔屿的铁路干线，使一列火车前面的压道车出轨；二日，人民武装又在彭亨州境内使曼达开到占马士的火车出轨。
