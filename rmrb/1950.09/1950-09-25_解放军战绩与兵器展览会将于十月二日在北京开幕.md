Title: PLA Achievements and Weapons Exhibition to Open in Beijing on October 2nd
Author:
Date: 1950-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Preparations for the Chinese People's Liberation Army Achievements Picture Exhibition and Weapons Exhibition have been largely completed and will officially open in Beijing on October 2nd. The exhibits of achievements pictures include precious historical relics from the period of the Land Revolution and the Anti-Japanese War, as well as pictures and objects from all major battlefields and campaigns across the country during the four-year Liberation War, totaling more than 5,000 items. The exhibited weapons include more than 1,000 types of weapons captured by the People's Liberation Army from domestic and foreign enemies since the Nanchang Uprising, as well as those self-made in the liberated areas.

### 解放军战绩与兵器展览会将于十月二日在北京开幕

1950-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　解放军战绩与兵器展览会将于十月二日在北京开幕
    【新华社二十四日讯】中国人民解放军战绩图片展览会与兵器展览会的各项筹备工作现已大体完成，将于十月二日在北京正式开幕。战绩图片的展览品，包括土地革命战争时期和抗日战争时期珍贵的历史文物，以及四年解放战争中全国各战场各主要战役的图片、实物等约五千余种。展览的兵器包括了人民解放军从南昌起义以来自国内外敌人手中缴获及解放区自造的各种武器一千余种。
