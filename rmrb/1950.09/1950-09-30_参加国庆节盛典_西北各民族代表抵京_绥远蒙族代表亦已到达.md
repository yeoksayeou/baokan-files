Title: Northwest Ethnic Group Representatives Arrive in Beijing to Participate in National Day Celebration; Suiyuan Mongolian Representatives Also Arrive
Author:
Date: 1950-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Northwest ethnic group representatives and their song and dance troupe, as well as Mongolian representatives from Suiyuan Province in the North China region, arrived in Beijing today to participate in the National Day celebration. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government; Li Weihan, Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government and Chairman of the Ethnic Affairs Commission; Ulanhu, Vice Chairman of the Ethnic Affairs Commission; Qu Wu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; Yu Xinqing, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government; Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission; Yang Hansheng, Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government; Liu Chun, Member of the Ethnic Affairs Commission; Yang Jingren, Director of the General Office of the Ethnic Affairs Commission; Wang Baiping, Deputy Director of the Secretariat of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zhou Zijian, Deputy Director of the Secretariat of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, as well as representatives from various agencies, the National Hui Institute, the Institute for Nationalities, and over 600 people in waist drum teams.

The Northwest Ethnic Group Representative Delegation consists of 43 representatives (11 arrived yesterday) and a song and dance troupe of 42 people. This includes 16 representatives from Xinjiang Province, 7 from Qinghai Province, 5 from Gansu Province, 2 from Ningxia Province, 11 representatives from the military, and 2 representatives from the Northwest Military and Political Committee. Among them are 8 Uyghurs, 7 Huis, 6 Kazakhs, 5 Mongolians, 3 Hans, 2 Russians, Tibetans, Tatars and Sibos, and 1 each of Tajiks, Kyrgyz, Manchus, Dongxiangs, Salars and Tus. The head of the delegation is Wang Feng, Director of the Ethnic Affairs Committee of the Northwest Military and Political Committee. The deputy heads are Bai Haifeng (Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Forestry of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, Mongolian), Abuliezi (Director of the Public Security Department of Xinjiang Province, Uyghur), Meng Quanlu (Hui from Qinghai Province), Huang Xiang (County Head of Xiahe County, Gansu Province, Tibetan), Lieshiken (Commander of the Fifth Army of the People's Liberation Army, Russian), and Ma Teng'ai (Hui Islamic leader from Ningxia).

Two Mongolian representatives from Suiyuan Province in the North China region and five staff members also arrived today.

### 参加国庆节盛典  西北各民族代表抵京  绥远蒙族代表亦已到达

1950-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加国庆节盛典
    西北各民族代表抵京
    绥远蒙族代表亦已到达
    【新华社二十九日讯】来京参加国庆节的西北各民族代表团及其歌舞团、华北区绥远省的蒙族代表，已于二十九日到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠、中央人民政府政务院秘书长、民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉、民族事务委员会副主任委员乌兰夫、中央人民政府政务院副秘书长屈武、北京市副市长吴晗、中央人民政府办公厅副主任余心清、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任朱早观、中央人民政府文化教育委员会秘书长阳翰笙、民族事务委员会委员刘春、民族事务委员会办公厅主任杨静仁、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会秘书处副处长王百评、中央人民政府政务院秘书厅副主任周子健以及各机关、国立回民学院、民族学院等单位代表及腰鼓队六百余人。
    西北各民族代表团共有代表四十三人（昨日已到十一名）、歌舞团四十二人。计新疆省代表十六人、青海省七人、甘肃省五人、宁夏省二人、部队代表十一人，西北军政委员会代表二人。其中维吾尔族八人，回族七人，哈萨克族六人，蒙古族五人，汉族三人，俄罗斯族、藏族、塔塔尔族和锡伯族各二人，塔吉克族、柯尔克斯族、满族、东乡族、撒拉族和土族各一人。代表团的团长为西北军政委员会民族事务委员会主任委员汪锋，副团长为白海风（西北军政委员会农林部副部长，蒙族）、阿不列孜（新疆省公安厅厅长，维族）、孟全禄（青海省回族）、黄祥（甘肃省夏河县县长，藏族）列士肯（人民解放军第五军军长，俄罗斯族）及马腾霭（宁夏回教教长）等六人。
    华北区绥远省蒙族代表二人及随员五人亦于今日到达。
