Title: Over 20,000 People Rally in Dihua to Welcome the Central Northwest Delegation
Author:
Date: 1950-09-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, September 17th: More than 20,000 people from all ethnic groups and circles in Dihua City held a grand rally on the 15th to warmly welcome the Central People's Government Northwest Ethnic Groups Delegation. At the meeting, Burhan, Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang. Delegation leader Shen Junru, Deputy Leaders Sa Kongliao and Pengsike, and Wang Feng, Director of the Ethnic Affairs Committee of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, also delivered speeches to the masses. In his speech, Shen Junru called on people of all ethnic groups to unite more closely under the principles of the Common Program, work hard for production and construction, heighten vigilance, and thoroughly smash all conspiracies of American imperialism and its running dogs to continue enslaving our people. Representatives from all ethnic groups and circles in Dihua City, including Ganiyaz Haji, Grand Imam of the Uyghur people, Latif (Kazakh), Jin Zixiang (Hui), Kelunbai (Mongolian), and Maryam (Uyghur woman), also spoke. They represented the people of all ethnic groups and circles in the city and Muslims, expressing their gratitude and respect to the delegation. They shouted "Long live Chairman Mao" in different languages.

At the meeting, the Central Delegation presented the People's Liberation Army stationed in Xinjiang, various people's organizations, schools, mosques, and others with 25 banners embroidered with Chairman Mao's inscription "Unite All Ethnic Groups of the People's Republic of China". The Xinjiang Military Region troops and various organizations also presented banners to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De. People of all ethnic groups also presented banners and flowers to the delegation.

The delegation has been warmly received by the Party, government, military, and the Xinjiang People's Democratic League in recent days. They have also convened representatives from all ethnic groups and circles for discussions and individual visits to listen to the opinions and demands of the people of all ethnic groups and circles. ### 迪化市两万余人集会  欢迎中央西北访问团

1950-09-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　迪化市两万余人集会
    欢迎中央西北访问团
    【新华社迪化十七日电】迪化市各族各界同胞两万余人于十五日举行盛大集会，热烈欢迎中央人民政府西北各民族访问团。会上新疆省人民政府鲍尔汉主席代表全疆各族人民致欢迎词。沈钧儒团长、萨空了、朋斯克副团长及西北军政委员会民族事务委员会汪锋主任委员均向到会群众发表了演说。沈钧儒团长在演说中号召各族人民在共同纲领原则下更亲密团结起来，努力生产建设，提高警惕，彻底粉碎美帝国主义及其走狗企图继续奴役我国人民的一切阴谋。迪化市各族各界代表和维吾尔族加尼牙孜阿吉大阿訇、拉提甫（哈萨克族）、金子祥（回族）、柯伦拜（蒙族）、玛热亚木（维族妇女）等也先后讲话。他们代表全市各民族各界人民及伊斯兰教徒，向访问团致谢致敬。他们用不同的语言高呼：“毛主席万岁。”
    会上，中央访问团赠予驻新疆人民解放军、各人民团体、学校、清真寺等绣有毛主席亲笔题字的“中华人民共和国各民族团结起来”的锦旗二十五幅。新疆军区部队与各团体也向毛主席、朱总司令献旗，各民族人民并向访问团献旗、献花。
    访问团连日受到党政军及新疆人民民主同盟的热情招待，并先后召集各民族各界代表举行座谈和进行个别访问，听取各民族各界人民的意见和要求。（附图片）
    在西安市欢迎中央西北访问团大会上回民高擎访问团赠旗，欢呼“中华人民共和国各民族大团结万岁！”　吴铮摄
    西安市回民夹道摆设茶果等候欢送中央西北访问团前往甘、宁、青、新各省访问。                    吴铮摄
