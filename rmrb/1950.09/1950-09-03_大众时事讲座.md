Mass Current Affairs Lecture
The 15th lecture of the mass current affairs lecture series held by the Youth Service Department is scheduled to be held at 5:00 pm on Sunday, September 3rd at the Youth Service Department Stadium (opposite the Beijing Hotel on East Chang'an Street). Chen Hanbo, a member of the editorial board of World Knowledge Weekly, will speak on: "The Security Council—The Struggle of Two Roads."

### 大众时事讲座

1950-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　大众时事讲座
    青年服务部举办的大众时事讲座第十五讲，定于九月三日（星期日）下午五时在青年服务部球场举行，（东长安街北京饭店对面）请世界知识周刊编辑委员陈翰伯讲：“安全理事会——两条道路的斗争”。
