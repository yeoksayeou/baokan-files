Title: Korean People's Army Fights Resolutely, Blocking US Troops Advancing on Seoul; Southern Fronts Repel Enemy Counterattacks
Author: 
Date: 1950-09-20
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 19th, Pyongyang dispatch from TASS: According to information from the General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army: In the area east of Incheon and at Kimpo Airport, Korean People's Army units are engaged in fierce battles with US troops attempting to break into Seoul. The enemy is constantly suffering losses.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 19th, Pyongyang dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army issued a war bulletin on the morning of the 18th, stating: People's Army units on all fronts continue to engage in fierce battles with the enemy.
    People's Army units operating north of Daegu repelled continuous enemy counterattacks, engaging in intense battles and inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.
    In recent days, the People's Army units in this sector have killed or wounded more than 50 US troops and over 800 of the Lee Seung-man puppet army, captured more than 40 enemy soldiers, and seized five 81mm guns, four 60mm mortars, three rocket launchers, more than 20 light and heavy machine guns, more than ten automatic rifles, more than 50 rifles, ten radio stations, twenty telephones, and many other military supplies such as ammunition and clothing.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 19th, Pyongyang dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army issued a war bulletin on the evening of the 18th, stating: People's Army units on all fronts continue to engage in fierce battles with US troops and the remaining Lee Seung-man puppet army. Relying on strong defensive lines, the enemy mobilized all its forces in an attempt to continue their counterattacks. Under the close coordination of various branches of the People's Army, they effectively repelled the enemy's counterattacks, continuously inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.
    On September 17th, People's Navy units shelled US warships intruding into the waters near Ongjin. One enemy large gunboat was hit several times, its hull engulfed in fierce flames, and it fled southward in panic.
    The People's Navy Coast Guard shot down two US fighter planes near Incheon on September 15th and 16th.

### 朝鲜人民军坚强战斗  阻击进犯汉城美军  南线各地击退敌军反击

1950-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军坚强战斗
    阻击进犯汉城美军
    南线各地击退敌军反击
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社平壤十九日电：据朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部消息：在仁川以东地区和金浦机场，朝鲜人民军部队正与企图突入汉城的美军进行坚强的战斗。敌军正不断遭受损失。
    【新华社平壤十九日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十八日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上，与敌人继续展开激烈的战斗。
    在大邱以北作战中的人民军部队，击退连续反击的敌人，展开炽烈的战斗，给予敌人以严重的损失。
    该方面的人民军部队在最近几天的战斗中，毙伤美军五十余人、李承晚伪军八百余人、俘敌四十余人，并缴获八一公厘炮五门、六○公厘迫击炮四门、火箭炮三门、轻重机枪二十余挺、自动枪十余支、步枪五十余支、电台十部、电话机二十个及其他各种弹药与被服等许多军需品。
    【新华社平壤十九日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十八日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上，与美军部队和李承晚残余伪军继续着激烈的战斗。敌人依靠坚固防线，动员全部兵力，企图继续反击。人民军部队在各兵种部队的紧密协同活动下，有效地击退敌人的反击，连续给予敌人以巨大的损失。
    九月十七日人民海军部队炮击侵入瓮津附近海上的美军舰艇。敌大型炮艇一艘中弹数发，舰身在猛烈的火焰里，仓惶向南逃走。
    人民海军海岸警备队九月十五日至十六日两天在仁川附近，击落美军战斗机两架。
