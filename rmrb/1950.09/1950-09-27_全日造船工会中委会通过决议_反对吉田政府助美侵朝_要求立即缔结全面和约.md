Title: Central Committee of the All-Japan Shipbuilding Workers' Union Passes Resolution Opposing Yoshida Government's Aid to US Aggression Against Korea, Demanding Immediate Conclusion of a Comprehensive Peace Treaty
Author:
Date: 1950-09-27
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 24th. According to a United Press report from Tokyo: The Central Committee of the All-Japan Shipbuilding Workers' Union, one of Japan's major industrial unions, held a three-day meeting from the 19th to the 21st. On the last day of the meeting, attendees, by an overwhelming majority of 29 votes to 14, rejected the Yoshida government's proposal to assist the United Nations in its war of aggression against Korea. The proposal blatantly demanded the reorganization of the shipbuilding industry to fulfill the "military needs" of the United States. The conference passed its own resolution, calling for opposition to the war, demanding the early conclusion of a comprehensive peace treaty with Japan, and opposing the illegal "purge" of progressive union members.

### 全日造船工会中委会通过决议  反对吉田政府助美侵朝  要求立即缔结全面和约

1950-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　全日造船工会中委会通过决议
    反对吉田政府助美侵朝  要求立即缔结全面和约
    【新华社二十四日讯】据合众社东京消息：日本重要的产业工会之一的全日造船工人工会中央委员会，于十九日至二十一日举行会议三天。在最后一天会议上，与会者以二十九票对十四票的压倒多数，否决了吉田政府提出的所谓协助联合国进行侵略朝鲜战争的提案。该提案竟公然要求改组造船工业，以完成美国“军事上的需求”。大会通过了它自己的决议，号召反对战争，要求早日缔结全面对日和约，反对非法“整肃”进步的工会会员。
