Title: British People Oppose Sending Troops to Korea; London Builder Writes to Attlee: "Your Government's Deceptive Tricks are Foul"
Author:
Date: 1950-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, London, September 2nd: The Workers' Daily reports: The father of one of the British youths conscripted to go to Korea, A. Swinford, a builder from Lewisham, London, wrote in a letter to British Prime Minister Attlee: "I believe your government's action of forcing national servicemen to re-enlist in expeditionary forces is unprecedented." The letter goes on to say: "I highly doubt the legality of forcing these conscripted national servicemen to fight under the command of foreign officers. Your government's deceptive tricks are foul."

### 英人民反对派兵赴朝  伦敦一个营造商写信告艾德里：“你的政府的骗人把戏是很臭的”

1950-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　英人民反对派兵赴朝
    伦敦一个营造商写信告艾德里：“你的政府的骗人把戏是很臭的”
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社伦敦二日讯：工人日报报道：被征派往朝鲜的英国青年之一的父亲，伦敦利威施曼地方的营造商阿旋福德，在致英首相艾德里的信中写道：“我认为你的政府，强使国民兵在出征军团中改服现役的行动，是空前未有的。”该信进而写道：“我极怀疑强制这些被征召服国民兵役的青年们在外国指挥官统率下作战的合法性。你的政府的骗人把戏是很臭的。”
