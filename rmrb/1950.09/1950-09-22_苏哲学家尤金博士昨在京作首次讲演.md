Title: Soviet Philosopher Dr. Yudin Gives First Lecture in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-09-22
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, September 21st:  The famous Soviet scholar and philosopher Dr. Yudin, invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to lecture in China, gave his first lecture in Beijing today. The meeting was presided over by Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. In his welcoming speech, President Liu Shaoqi introduced Dr. Yudin's significant academic achievements and expressed his gratitude to the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries for its assistance to our country. Dr. Yudin then lectured on "The Transition Period from Capitalism to Socialism in the Soviet Union."

In the evening, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a banquet to welcome Dr. Yudin. Attendees included President Liu Shaoqi, Vice Presidents Li Jishen, Wu Yuzhang, and Huang Yanpei, Directors Peng Zhen and Chen Boda, Secretary-General Qian Junrui, Deputy Secretary-General Xiao San, and Soviet Ambassador to China Roshchin, Counselor Fedlin, Representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China Seregin, and more than forty others.

### 苏哲学家尤金博士昨在京作首次讲演

1950-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏哲学家尤金博士昨在京作首次讲演
    【新华社二十一日讯】应中苏友好协会总会邀请来中国讲学的苏联著名学者、哲学家尤金博士，今日在京举行第一次讲演。会议由中苏友好协会总会刘少奇会长主持。刘少奇会长在致欢迎词中介绍了尤金博士在学术上的重大成就，并对苏联对外文化协会对我国的帮助表示谢意。继由尤金博士讲演“苏联由资本主义到社会主义的过渡时期”。
    晚间，中苏友好协会总会设宴欢迎尤金博士，参加者有会长刘少奇，副会长李济深、吴玉章、黄炎培，理事彭真、陈伯达，总干事钱俊瑞，副总干事萧三，和苏联驻中国大使罗申、参赞费德林、苏联对外文化协会驻中国代表谢列金等四十多人。
