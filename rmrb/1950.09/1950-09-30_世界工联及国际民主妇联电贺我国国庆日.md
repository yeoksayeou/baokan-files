Title: World Federation of Trade Unions and Women's International Democratic Federation Send Congratulatory Messages on China's National Day

Author:
Date: 1950-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Louis Saillant, sent a telegram on September 21st to Liu Shaoqi, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, to celebrate China's National Day. The original text is as follows:
    Comrade Liu Shaoqi:
    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the Government of the People's Republic of China, the workers of the world express their wishes: We wish the All-China Federation of Trade Unions every success in its economic and social organization work, and in its political support for the work of the government headed by Comrade Mao Zedong. We extend our fraternal greetings to the Chinese workers and our sincerest encouragement to all the people of your country. Our trade union is the most reliable guarantee for defending peace and a tool for defeating the plans of the warmongers. We wish you great victory.
      Louis Saillant, General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, September 21st
    Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The Women's International Democratic Federation sent a telegram to Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, congratulating the new China on its National Day. The telegram stated: "On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we extend our warmest congratulations to our liberated Chinese sisters and Chairman Mao Zedong. Faced with the threat of world war, we especially congratulate you on your great contribution to strengthening the peace camp, on which the hopes of women and mothers around the world are placed!"

### 世界工联及国际民主妇联电贺我国国庆日

1950-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界工联及国际民主妇联电贺我国国庆日
    【新华社二十五日讯】世界工联总书记路易·赛扬本月二十一日致电我中华全国总工会名誉主席刘少奇庆祝我国国庆日。原文如次：刘少奇同志：
    值此中华人民共和国政府成立一周年纪念之际，全世界的工人特表示他们的愿望：谨祝中华全国总工会在其经济社会组织工作中，在其政治上支持以毛泽东同志为首的政府的工作中，获得一切胜利。我们特向中国工人致以兄弟的敬礼，向贵国全体人民致以最诚恳的鼓舞。我们工会乃是保卫和平的最可靠的保障，乃是击败战争贩子的计划的工具。祝你们得到伟大的胜利。
      世界工联总书记  路易·赛扬  九月二十一日
    【新华社二十七日讯】国际民主妇女联合会致电中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，祝贺新中国的国庆日。电文称：“欣逢中华人民共和国成立一周年纪念日，谨向已获得解放的中国姊妹们和毛泽东主席致最热烈的祝贺。面临着世界战争的威胁，我们特别对你们加强为全世界妇女与母亲们的希望所寄的和平阵营的伟大贡献表示祝贺！”
