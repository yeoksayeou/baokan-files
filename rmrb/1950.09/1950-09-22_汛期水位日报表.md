　　Flood Season Water Level Daily Report
    September 21st (with charts)
    Note:
    The Datong zero point shall be the benchmark for all stations in North China and along the Yellow River (Luo Kou uses Qingdao zero point). The Wusongkou zero point shall be the benchmark for all stations along the Yangtze River. The former Yellow River mouth zero point shall be the benchmark for the Huai River and the Grand Canal. The Guangxi assumed zero point shall be the benchmark for Wuzhou. The Dalian average sea level shall be the benchmark for Northeast China.
