Title: Romanian Exhibition Opens in Beijing; Vice Chairman Zhu and Over Two Hundred Others Attend Opening Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1950-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 11th: The "Exhibition of the Romanian People's Republic Marching Towards Socialism," sponsored by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, opened today at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace. Among those attending the opening ceremony were Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Lin Boqu, Secretary-General; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government; Dong Biwu, Vice Premier; Guo Moruo, Vice Premier; Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Ma Xulun, Minister of Education; Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wu Xiuquan, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Foreign guests attending the opening ceremony included Rudenko, Romanian Ambassador to China; Roshchin, Soviet Ambassador to China; Weiskopf, Czechoslovak Ambassador to China; Amashton, Swedish Ambassador to China; König, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China with the rank of Ambassador; Szafranko, Hungarian Ambassador to China; U Myint Toon, Burmese Ambassador to China; Muuk, Danish Minister to China; Labudan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy in China; Dai Peila-Sinski, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China; Choi Young, Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China; and Kumar, Secretary of the Indian Embassy in China. Also present were people from all walks of life in the capital and Chinese and foreign journalists, totaling more than two hundred people.

　　Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, in his speech at the opening ceremony, stated that the Romanian people and their government are very pleased that this exhibition can help the great Chinese people understand the Romanian people's struggle in socialist construction, and hope to see, in the shortest possible time, a New China exhibition showing the heroic struggle, great reforms, and high art of the Chinese people, exhibited in Bucharest. Vice Premier Guo Moruo and Minister Shen Yanbing delivered speeches highly praising Romania's construction achievements and pointed out the educational significance of this exhibition for the Chinese people. After the speeches, Ambassador Rudenko cut the ribbon and guided the guests on a tour.

　　The Romanian Exhibition was prepared over two months with the invitation of the Ministry of Culture of our Central People's Government and with the consent and strong assistance of the Romanian government. The Romanian government attached great importance to this exhibition, and specially shipped seven train cars with more than three hundred boxes of items from the country, and invited three experts to carefully design the venue, exhibiting it entirely in the Romanian national style. This is the largest exhibition held by Romania abroad, except for the one in the Soviet Union. The exhibition includes Romanian history, construction achievements, Soviet aid, artworks, utensils, clothing, and so on.

　　The exhibition is scheduled to open to the public on the 12th. After being exhibited in Beijing until the end of September, it is planned to be exhibited in Tianjin, Shenyang, Shanghai, Nanjing, Hankou and other places.

### 罗马尼亚展览会在京揭幕  朱副主席等二百余人参加揭幕礼

1950-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚展览会在京揭幕
    朱副主席等二百余人参加揭幕礼
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府文化部主办的“向社会主义迈进的罗马尼亚人民共和国展览会”，于今日在北京劳动人民文化宫揭幕。参加揭幕礼者有我国中央人民政府朱德副主席、林伯渠秘书长、中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理、董必武副总理、郭沫若副总理、黄炎培副总理、文化部沈雁冰部长、教育部马叙伦部长、外交部章汉夫副部长、外交部苏联东欧司伍修权司长、外交部情报司龚澎司长、中华全国总工会刘宁一副主席等。参加揭幕礼的外宾有罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科，苏联驻华大使罗申，捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使魏斯柯普夫，瑞典驻华大使阿马斯顿，德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希，匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯，缅甸驻华大使吴敏登，丹麦驻华公使穆克，蒙古驻华大使馆拉布丹临时代办，波兰驻华大使馆戴裴拉辛斯基临时代办，朝鲜驻华大使馆崔英参赞，印度驻华大使馆古玛尔秘书等人。与会者尚有首都各界人士及中外记者二百余人。
    罗马尼亚大使鲁登科在举行揭幕礼时致辞称：罗马尼亚人民及其政府，对于此次展览会可以帮助伟大的中国人民了解罗马尼亚人民在社会主义建设中的斗争而深感愉快，并且希望在最短期间内，看到表现中国人民的英勇斗争和伟大改革及高度艺术的新中国展览会，在布加勒斯特展出。郭沫若副总理和沈雁冰部长致词盛赞罗马尼亚的建设成就，并指出此次展览会对中国人民的教育意义。致词毕由鲁登科大使剪彩，并引导来宾参观。
    罗马尼亚展览会系经我国中央人民政府文化部邀请，经罗马尼亚政府的同意和大力协助下，历两个多月的筹备而成。罗马尼亚政府对此次展出极为重视，特从国内运来七个火车皮共三百多箱的物品，并请三位专家精心设计会场，完全以罗马尼亚的民族形式展出。罗马尼亚在国外举办的展览会，除在苏联外，这是最大的一次。展览会内容计有罗马尼亚历史、建设成绩、苏联援助、艺术作品、用具服装等等。
    展览会定十二日向市民开放。在京展览至九月底后，准备分赴天津、沈阳、上海、南京、汉口等地展出。
