Title: Beijing 1950 People's Sports Meet: Performance and Competition Items Finalized
Author:
Date: 1950-09-18
Page: 6

Beijing 1950 People's Sports Meet
    Performance and Competition Items Finalized

    The Standing Committee of the Beijing 1950 People's Sports Meet Preparatory Committee, after studying and discussing the performance and competition items proposed by the Design Committee, has officially finalized the performance and competition items for the event. The items are divided into three categories: group performances, special performances, and competitions (including track and field, ball games, and tug-of-war). The details of each category are listed below:

    1. Group Performances:
    (1) Content: Participating units can choose and create based on the following four categories:
        A. Gymnastics: such as freehand exercises, light apparatus gymnastics, apparatus gymnastics, formation changes, character formation, human figure formation, etc.;
        B. Dance: such as group dance, Yangko dance, sing-along games, etc.;
        C. Traditional Chinese Martial Arts: such as group boxing, weightlifting with traditional equipment, etc.;
        D. Skills: such as various ethnic forms of skills with sports value.
    (2) Points to Note: The following principles should be observed:
        A. Pay attention to sports value. There should be a certain amount of exercise, and it must be suitable for the physical and mental development of the performers;
        B. There can be certain political implications (such as combining with the celebration of National Day, defending world peace, etc.), but avoid being too formalistic;
        C. Pay attention to being neat, serious, lively, tense, and creative (combining with daily life);
        D. Coordinate with music as much as possible.

    2. Special Performances:
    Invited by the organizing committee to participate. Specific items will be decided by the program department of the organizing committee.

    3. Competition Items: (Track and Field, Ball Games, and Tug-of-War)
    (1) Workers' Division:
        A. Men's Workers' Group:
            (1) 100 meters (2) 1500 meters (3) 4x400 meters weight-bearing relay (each team of four, each person runs 100 meters, the weight-bearing item is a sack filled with sand, weighing 30 *jin*, carried on the shoulder) (4) Standing High Jump (5) Standing Long Jump (6) Shot Put (12 pounds) (7) Tug-of-War (8) Basketball (9) Soccer.
        B. Women's Workers' Group:
            (1) 60 meters (2) 4x100 meters (each team of four, each person runs 100 meters) (3) Standing Long Jump (4) Softball Throw (5) Basketball (6) Tug-of-War.
    (2) Farmers' Division:
        A. Men's Farmers' Group:
            (1) 100 meters (2) 4x100 meters relay (each team of four, each person runs 100 meters) (3) Tug-of-War.
        B. Women's Farmers' Group:
            (1) 60 meters (2) 4x50 meters relay (each team of four, each person runs 50 meters) (3) Tug-of-War.
    (3) Military and Police Division:
        A. Men's Military and Police Group:
            (1) 100 meters (2) 150 meters Obstacle Race (3) 4x400 meters Relay (each team of four, each person runs 400 meters) (4) 5000 meters (5) Standing High Jump (6) Standing Long Jump (7) Hand Grenade Throw (8) Tug-of-War (9) Basketball (10) Baseball.
        B. Women's Military and Police Group: Basketball.
    (4) Students' Division:
        A. Men's University Group: (1) 100 meters (2) 400 meters (3) 800 meters (4) 1500 meters (5) 5000 meters (6) 110 meters Hurdles (7) 1000 meters Medley Relay (four runners: the first runs 100 meters, the second runs 200 meters, the third runs 300 meters, the fourth runs 400 meters) (8) Standing High Jump (9) Standing Long Jump (10) Shot Put (16 pounds) (11) Triple Jump (12) Discus Throw (13) Hand Grenade Throw (wooden handle, total length 9.5 inches, weight 12 *shi liang*, iron head circumference 5.2 inches, iron head length 2.2 inches) (14) Pole Vault.
        B. Women's Senior High Group: (1) 100 meters (2) 200 meters (3) 4x100 meters Relay (4) 80 meters Hurdles (5) Standing High Jump (6) Standing Long Jump (7) Shot Put (8 pounds) (8) Discus Throw (9) Softball Throw.
        C. Men's Junior High Group: (1) 100 meters (2) 400 meters (3) 1500 meters (4) 3000 meters (5) 1000 meters Medley Relay (same as men's university group) (6) Standing High Jump (7) Standing Long Jump (8) Shot Put (12 pounds) (9) Discus Throw (10) Hand Grenade Throw (same as men's university group).
        D. Men's Elementary School Group: (1) 100 meters (2) 800 meters (3) 4x100 meters Relay (4) Standing High Jump (5) Standing Long Jump (6) Hand Grenade Throw (same as men's university group).
        E. Women's Junior High Group: (1) 100 meters (2) 4x50 meters Relay (3) Standing Long Jump (4) Softball Throw.
        F. Men's Elementary School Group: (1) 100 meters (2) 4x50 meters Relay (eight runners, each runs 50 meters) (3) Standing Long Jump (4) Softball Throw (5) Five-Star Race (each team of twenty-one, each runs approximately 60 meters).
        G. Women's Elementary School Group: (1) 60 meters (2) 4x50 meters Relay (same as men's elementary school group) (3) Standing Long Jump (4) Softball Throw (5) Five-Star Race (same as men's elementary school group).
    (5) General Division (Staff of Government Agencies, Organizations, and Citizens):
        A. Men's General Group: (1) 100 meters (2) 4x200 meters Relay (four runners, each runs 200 meters) (3) Standing Long Jump (4) Shot Put (16 pounds) (5) Basketball.
        B. Women's General Group: (1) 60 meters (2) 4x50 meters Relay (each team of four, each person runs 50 meters) (3) Softball Throw (4) Basketball.
            (Beijing News Agency)

### 京市一九五○年人民体育大会  表演及竞赛项目已制定

1950-09-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　京市一九五○年人民体育大会
    表演及竞赛项目已制定
    北京市一九五○年人民体育大会筹委会常委会已依据设计委员会提出的表演及竞赛项目研究讨论后正式制定了这次大会的表演及竞赛项目。这次的项目共分集体表演、特约表演及竞赛（包括田径赛、球类及拔河）三种，兹将各种内容分别列下：
    １、集体表演：
    （一）内容：各参加单位可参考下列四种项目选择创造：
    甲、体操：如徒手操、轻器械体操、器械体操、队形变换、组字、组人形等；
    乙、舞蹈：如集体舞、秧歌舞、唱游等；
    丙、国术：如团体拳术、器械举重等；
    丁、技艺：如各种民族形式的含有体育价值的技艺。
    （二）注意事项：应注意下列原则：
    甲、注意体育价值。要有一定的运动量，并须适合表演者生理与心理的发育情况；
    乙、可有一定的政治含意（如结合庆祝国庆日、保卫世界和平等），但避免过于形式；
    丙、要注意整齐、严肃、活泼、紧张，并富于创造性（结合平时生活）；
    丁、尽可能配合音乐。
    ２、特约表演：
    由大会特别约请参加，详细项目由大会筹委会节目部决定之。
