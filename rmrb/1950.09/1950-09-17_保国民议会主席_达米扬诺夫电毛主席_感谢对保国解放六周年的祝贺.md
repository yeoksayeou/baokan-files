Title: Chairman of the Bulgarian National Assembly, Damyanov, Cables Chairman Mao, Thanking Him for Congratulations on the Sixth Anniversary of Bulgaria's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1950-09-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 16th—
    Chairman of the Bulgarian National Assembly
    Damyanov Cables Chairman Mao
    Thanking Him for Congratulations on the Sixth Anniversary of Bulgaria's Liberation
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] Georgi Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, telegraphed Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China to thank him for his congratulations to the Bulgarian people on the sixth anniversary of Bulgaria's liberation. The telegram is as follows: To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    On behalf of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and myself, I would like to express my gratitude for your telegram of congratulations to the Bulgarian people on the occasion of the sixth anniversary of Bulgaria's liberation. For the sake of national construction, the Bulgarian people need peace, so they, together with the great Chinese people and other democratic peoples, will resolutely stand on the side of the peace camp headed by the invincible Soviet Union and the great Stalin, and use all their strength to fight against the Anglo-American imperialist instigators of new wars.
    Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly
    Georgi Damyanov, September 13th

### 保国民议会主席  达米扬诺夫电毛主席  感谢对保国解放六周年的祝贺

1950-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　保国民议会主席
    达米扬诺夫电毛主席
    感谢对保国解放六周年的祝贺
    【新华社十六日讯】保加利亚国民议会主席团主席乔吉·达米扬诺夫，电谢我中央人民政府毛泽东主席对保加利亚解放六周年的祝贺。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    谨以保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团及我个人名义，对您在保加利亚解放六周年之际致保加利亚人民的贺电表示感谢之忱。为了国家的建设，保加利亚人民需要和平，所以它和伟大的中国人民及其他各民主人民，将坚决地站在以不可战胜的苏联和伟大的斯大林为首的和平阵营方面，用一切的力量去进行反对英美帝国主义新战争挑拨者的斗争。
    保加利亚国民议会主席团
    主席  乔吉·达米扬诺夫  九月十三日
