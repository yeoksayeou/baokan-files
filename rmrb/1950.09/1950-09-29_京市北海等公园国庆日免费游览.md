Title: Beijing's Beihai and Other Parks Offer Free Admission on National Day

Author:

Date: 1950-09-29
Page: 12

Beijing's Beihai and Other Parks Offer Free Admission on National Day
    In celebration of the first National Day of the People's Republic of China, Beijing's Beihai Park, Western Suburban Park, and the Summer Palace will all offer free admission for one day on October 1st.

    In addition, to facilitate the grand celebration parade for people from all walks of life, Zhongshan Park will be closed to visitors during the day on October 1st but will be open in the evening.

### 京市北海等公园国庆日免费游览

1950-09-29
第12版()
专栏：

　　京市北海等公园国庆日免费游览
    为了庆祝中华人民共和国第一个国庆日，京市北海公园、西郊公园和颐和园都在十月一日免费游览一天。
    又，为便于各界人民举行盛大的庆祝游行，中山公园于十月一日白天停止游览，晚间开放。
