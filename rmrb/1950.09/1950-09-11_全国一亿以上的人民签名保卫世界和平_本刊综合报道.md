Title: Over 100 Million People Nationwide Sign Petition to Defend World Peace – Comprehensive Report from This Publication
Author:
Date: 1950-09-11
Page: 5

According to incomplete statistics collected on September 8th, a total of 100,684,748 people in China—over 20.08% of the country's total population—have signed the Stockholm Peace Appeal, unanimously expressing opposition to the instigation of a new war by American imperialism, advocating for the unconditional prohibition of atomic weapons, and considering any government that first uses atomic weapons against any country as a war criminal.

This is the oath of the world's people to defend peace. Every signature on the oath represents a living force in the peace camp. Therefore, the greater the number of signatures, the more powerful the warning to the American warmongers who are wielding atomic weapons. When the warmongers themselves feel absolutely isolated, the aggressive plans they have plotted will be more easily thwarted. The National Committee of the China Peace Committee, in order to further strengthen the forces of the peace camp and more effectively warn the warmongers, issued a call on August 12th to strive for 200 million signatures on the Stockholm Declaration. Now the number of signatures nationwide is even closer to the predetermined goal.

When the call to expand the peace signature campaign was issued, the number of signatures nationwide was less than 60 million; within the past month, it has rapidly increased to over 100 million. This huge progress not only demonstrates the great determination of the Chinese people to defend world peace, but also demonstrates the deep anger of the Chinese people aroused by the aggressive policies of American imperialism. This is because the peace signature movement is fundamentally closely integrated with the struggle against imperialist aggression.

According to statistics at the end of June, the number of peace signatures nationwide was only 26,710,367, which was the result of the collection in May and June. But by July 24th, it had increased to 44,423,549, meaning that in 24 days, it had increased by nearly double the total collected in May and June. In Hubei Province, the increase was even more than one and a half times. This rapid increase is obviously related to the widespread development of the movement against American aggression in Taiwan and Korea.

The tide of anger among the Chinese people caused by the atrocities of American aircraft on our northeastern border on August 27th and 29th further greatly promoted the national peace signature movement. This tide of anger has swept into rural areas. For example, the farmers in Nanyuan Town in the suburbs of Beijing, after hearing the news on August 28th that American planes had strafed our northeastern border compatriots, convened a meeting of street administrative group leaders that evening to discuss ways to continue to expand the peace signature movement. In Longzhuashu Village in the town, 775 people signed the peace declaration in one day; in Weizi Village, all 733 villagers signed; in Wudian Village, 1,718 people signed. The number of signatures in the three villages accounted for more than 80% of the town's total population.

During the recent period of strong national protests against American aggression, the people in various parts of the North China Administrative Region responded to the imperialist armed provocations by expanding the peace signature movement. The number of signatures surged from 12,593,000 to 19,978,343 in the ten days from August 25th to September 4th. In Hebei Province, the number increased by more than 4.5 million in the past ten days, while in Pingyuan Province, it increased from 500,000 to more than 3 million. The troops of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army continued to expand the peace signature movement in conjunction with the propaganda and education of the anti-American aggression movement. Many scattered and mobile troops and those who were away from their units signed the appeal, and the commanders and fighters of the various units wrote letters home, mobilizing their relatives and friends to sign the appeal to defend world peace. The troops stationed in rural areas also held village meetings or formed groups to conduct door-to-door propaganda and mobilize farmers to sign the peace declaration. In the past ten days, the number of peace signatures collected by the troops has been very large. In South China, people's organizations in Guangdong Province recently jointly issued a declaration calling on all the people of South China to unite, fully prepare their strength, and more widely launch the peace signature movement, penetrating into small and medium-sized cities and rural areas, so that the broad masses of the people can deeply recognize the true face of American imperialism, unite, and fight to smash any aggressive acts of American imperialists. The number of peace signatures in South China has also increased significantly in recent days.

The national peace signature movement is continuing to surge amidst the strong anti-aggression sentiments of the people in various regions. We believe that as long as this movement further penetrates into the vast rural areas, the goal of 200 million people set by the China Peace Committee can be achieved before the Second World Peace Congress. ### 全国一亿以上的人民签名保卫世界和平——本刊综合报道

1950-09-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　全国一亿以上的人民签名保卫世界和平
    ——本刊综合报道
    据九月八日所收集的不完全的统计，全中国已有一亿零六十八万四千七百四十八人——占全国人口总数百分之二十点零八强——在斯德哥尔摩和平宣言上签了名，一致表示反对美国帝国主义挑拨新的战争，主张无条件地禁止原子武器，并把首先使用原子武器去反对任何国家的那一个政府当作战犯看待。
    这是世界人民决心保卫和平的誓约。誓约上每一个签名都代表着和平阵营方面的一份有生力量。因此，签名人数愈多，对舞弄原子武器的美国战争贩子的警告就愈有力。等到战争贩子们自己感到绝对孤立的时候，他们所策动的侵略计划也就容易被挫败了。中国保卫世界和平大会全国委员会为了进一步加强和平阵营的力量，更有效地警告战争贩子，于八月十二日发出号召，争取两亿人在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名。现在全国签名人数已与预定目标离得更近了。
    当扩大和平签名运动的号召发出的时候，全国签名人数不满六千万，在最近一个月之内，迅速增加到一亿以上。这种巨大的进度不但表现着中国人民保卫世界和平的伟大决心，同时也表现着美国帝国主义侵略政策所激起的中国人民的深切愤怒。因为和平签名运动在基本上是和反对帝国主义侵略的斗争密切地相结合的。
    全国和平签名人数，据六月底的统计，还只有二千六百七十一万零三百六十七人，这是五、六两月征集的结果。但至七月二十四日，就增加到四千四百二十三万三千五百四十九人，这就是说，在二十四天中间，增加了将近五、六两月征集总数的一倍，其中如湖北省地区甚至增加了一倍半以上。这一种急速的增加显然是和反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动的广泛发展相关联的。
    八月二十七、二十九两日，美国飞机在我东北边境的暴行所引起的中国人民的怒潮，对于全国和平签名运动进一步起了巨大的推动作用。这一怒潮已经卷到了乡村区域。例如：北京郊区南苑镇的农民们，在八月二十八日听到美机扫射我东北边境同胞的消息后，当晚召开了街行政组长会议，讨论继续扩大和平签名运动的办法。该镇龙爪树村在一天内即有七百七十五人在和平宣言上签名，苇子村全村签名的有七百三十三人，庑店村有一千七百一十八人签名。三村签名人数已占该镇人口总数百分之八十以上。
    在最近全国强烈抗议美国侵略罪行的期间，华北行政区各地人民以扩大和平签名运动来回答帝国主义的武装挑衅行为。签名人数在八月二十五日至九月四日的十天内，由一千二百五十九万三千人激增至一千九百九十七万八千三百四十三人。河北省在最近十天内增加了四百五十余万人，平原省则由五十万人增加至三百万人以上。人民解放军华东军区及第三野战军各部结合了反对美国侵略运动的宣传教育继续扩大和平签名运动。许多分散流动的部队与外出人员都签了名，各部指战员并纷纷写信回家，动员亲友签名保卫世界和平。驻农村的部队也都召开村民大会，或组成小组，挨户进行宣传，发动农民在和平宣言上签名。在最近的十天中间，部队方面所征集的和平签名是有很大的数字的。在华南方面，广东省各人民团体最近联合发表宣言，号召全华南人民团结一致，充分准备力量，更广泛地展开和平签名运动，深入中小城市和农村，使广大人民深刻地认清美帝国主义的真面目，一致起来，为粉碎美帝国主义者的任何侵略行为而斗争。华南方面的和平签名人数在最近几天也就有着显著的增加。
    全国和平签名运动，在各地人民强烈的反侵略情绪中间，正在继续高涨着。我们相信：这一运动只要进一步深入到广大的乡村，那么中国保卫世界和平大会委员会所预定的两亿人的目标，在第二届世界保卫和平大会以前，是可以达到的。（附图表）
