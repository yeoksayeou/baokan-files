Title: East China, Central South, and Northwest Military Regions, and 3rd, 4th, and 1st Field Armies Enthusiastically Elect Heroic Models; Ningxia Military Region Elects Six Representatives Including Lü Shunbao
Author:
Date: 1950-09-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 30th: The East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, and the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army of the People's Liberation Army are enthusiastically carrying out the work of selecting combat heroes and labor models. It is expected that all army-level heroic model representatives from the two major military regions and field armies will be elected by early September. While electing heroic model representatives, the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, in conjunction with current tasks, conducted education on the situation and tasks of opposing American imperialist aggression against Taiwan and Korea. The troops called on all commanders and fighters to intensify training and study hard, and strive to be glorious heroic models in the battle to liberate Taiwan. When the representatives who were elected in the preliminary selection set off to participate in the second round of selection, grand farewell rallies were held. The commanders and fighters of each unit enthusiastically presented congratulatory letters, banners, and other gifts to the representatives. Many units also held memorial services to mourn the heroic martyrs who sacrificed in the War of Liberation while electing heroic model representatives. When the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army conducted re-elections at the regimental level, some units combined this election with heroic model conferences, bandit suppression celebration conferences, and production summary conferences.
    Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 2nd: The Northwest Military Region and the First Field Army of the People's Liberation Army have begun to select combat heroes and labor models. The Ningxia Military Region has selected six representatives, including the famous combat hero Lü Shunbao, to attend the National Combat Heroes Representatives Conference and the National Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Model Representatives Conference. When selecting heroes and models, each unit simultaneously summarizes the past experience in cultivating and educating heroes and models and carrying out heroic model movements; and holds exhibitions and introductions of combat achievements, production achievements, and heroic model deeds. The selection work of each unit will be completed in early September.

### 华东中南西北三军区暨三野四野一野  热烈评选英雄模范  宁夏军区选出吕顺保等六代表

1950-09-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　华东中南西北三军区暨三野四野一野
    热烈评选英雄模范
    宁夏军区选出吕顺保等六代表
    【新华社三十日讯】人民解放军华东军区暨第三野战军、中南军区暨第四野战军各部正热烈进行评选战斗英雄、劳动模范的工作。两个大军区和野战军的全军性的英雄模范代表预计九月初旬止可全部选出。华东军区和第三野战军各部，在选举英雄模范代表的同时，结合目前各项任务，进行了反对美国帝国主义侵略台湾朝鲜的形势与任务的教育。各部号召全体指战员加紧练兵、努力学习，争取在解放台湾的战斗中做一个光荣的英雄模范。各单位在初选中当选的代表们出发参加复选时，都举行了隆重的欢送大会。各部指战员并热烈地向代表们赠送贺信、贺幛和其他礼物。很多单位在选举英雄、模范代表的同时，还召开了悼念在解放战争中牺牲的英雄烈士的纪念会。中南军区和第四野战军在以团为单位进行复选时，有些单位结合此次选举召开了英雄模范大会、剿匪庆功大会以及生产总结大会。
    【新华社西安二日电】人民解放军西北军区暨第一野战军各部，已开始评选战斗英雄及劳动模范。宁夏军区已选出有名的战斗英雄吕顺保等六人为出席全国战斗英雄代表会议和全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的代表。各部在评选英雄、模范时，同时总结过去培养教育英雄模范以及开展英雄模范运动的经验；并举行战斗功绩、生产成绩和英雄模范事迹的展览和介绍。各部评选工作将于九月初全部完成。
