Title: New Knowledge Symposium

Author:

Date: 1950-09-23
Page: 10

The Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association is hosting a New Knowledge Symposium. A symposium on professional issues related to the light industry sector will be held at 7:00 PM on the 23rd at the Xiehe Auditorium on East Dan Santiao. We specially invite Wang Xinyuan, Deputy Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Light Industry, to preside. Business and industrial figures are welcome to attend.
          (Zhen)

### 新知识座谈会

1950-09-23
第10版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会
    民建京分会主办的新知识座谈会，定于二十三日下午七时在东单三条协和礼堂举行有关轻工业部门专业会议问题座谈会，特请中央人民政府轻工业部王新元副部长主持。欢迎工商界人士参加。
          （桢）
