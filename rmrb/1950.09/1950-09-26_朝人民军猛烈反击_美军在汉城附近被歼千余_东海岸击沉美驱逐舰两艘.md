Title: Korean People's Army Fiercely Counterattacks; Over a Thousand American Soldiers Eliminated Near Seoul; Two American Destroyers Sunk off the East Coast

Author: 
Date: 1950-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 25th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 24th that People's Army units are continuing fierce battles against the enemy on all fronts.

	In the areas of Yeongdeungpo, Gimpo, and Suwon, People's Army units are preventing the advance of American landing forces, engaging the enemy in fierce hand-to-hand combat, causing heavy losses to the enemy's manpower and equipment.

	People's Army units defending Seoul are continuing to counterattack the enemy, thwarting enemy attempts to infiltrate our defensive lines.

	During the defense of Seoul, the city's residents are fully mobilized, united as one, assisting the People's Army, and demonstrating patriotic devotion and bravery in the battle to defeat and eliminate the enemy.

	On September 22nd, People's Army units on the east coast fiercely shelled American warships attempting to invade waters near Pohang, sinking two American destroyers.

	On September 23rd, People's Army ground units and anti-aircraft artillery units on all fronts shot down seven enemy aircraft and one fighter plane that invaded north of the 38th parallel.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 25th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 24th that People's Army units are continuing fierce battles with the enemy on all fronts.

	American forces that landed in the Incheon area are encountering strong resistance from People's Army units and are being prevented from advancing. The enemy's personnel losses continue to increase.

	On September 23rd, part of the American forces, under the cover of aircraft, tanks, and artillery, attempted to approach Seoul. However, People's Army units launched a fierce counter-attack, repelling the enemy and thwarting their advance. In this battle, People's Army units destroyed five enemy tanks, four large-caliber cannons, over twenty various cannons, over thirty vehicles, and a great deal of equipment, and killed or wounded approximately 1,200 enemy soldiers.

	During the battle on September 23rd, People's Army units captured over 300 enemy officers and soldiers. On September 23rd, People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down three American fighter planes that invaded the northern half of the region.

### 朝人民军猛烈反击  美军在汉城附近被歼千余  东海岸击沉美驱逐舰两艘

1950-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝人民军猛烈反击
    美军在汉城附近被歼千余  东海岸击沉美驱逐舰两艘
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十四日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上继续对敌人进行激烈的战斗。
    人民军部队在永登浦、金浦、水原方面，阻止美军登陆部队的推进，向敌人展开猛烈的肉搏战，使敌人的有生力量和装备上遭受巨大损失。
    防御汉城的人民军部队，继续向敌人展开反击，挫折了敌人想要渗入我军防线的企图。
    在汉城防御战中，该市居民正全体动员起来，团结一致，协助人民军，并在击溃扫荡敌人的战斗中，发挥了爱国的献身性和勇敢性。
    九月二十二日，东海岸方面的人民军部队，对企图侵入浦项附近海面的美军船舰，加以猛烈的炮击，击沉了美军驱逐舰两艘。
    九月二十三日，各战线的人民军地面部队和高射炮部队，击落敌机七架，并击落了侵袭三八线以北的战斗机一架。
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十四日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与敌人继续进行激烈的战斗。
    在仁川地区登陆的美军部队，遭受人民军部队的坚强反击，被阻止前进。敌人兵力上的损失正继续增加着。
    九月二十三日，美军部队一部，在飞机、坦克和炮兵的掩护下，企图接近汉城。但人民军部队展开猛烈的反突击战，击退了敌人，挫败敌人的前进企图。在该次战斗中，人民军部队击毁敌坦克五辆，大口径炮四门，各种炮二十余门，汽车三十余辆以及很多装备，并毙伤敌人约一千二百名。
    在九月二十三日的战斗中，人民军部队俘掳敌军官兵三百余名。人民军高射炮部队，在九月二十三日，击落侵袭北半部地区的美军战斗机三架。
