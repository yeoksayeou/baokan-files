Title: Chinese Art Exhibition to Open in Moscow on October 1st
Author:
Date: 1950-09-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th—The "Chinese Art Exhibition," long awaited by the Soviet people, is scheduled to open on October 1st, the anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, at the Tretyakov Gallery in Moscow. Wang Yechiu and four others sent by China to Moscow to arrange the exhibition arrived in Moscow from Beijing a few days ago with various exhibits and are now preparing for it.

### 中国艺术展览会十月一日在苏京揭幕

1950-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国艺术展览会十月一日在苏京揭幕
    【新华社莫斯科十二日电】苏联人民渴望已久的“中国艺术展览会”，定于十月一日中华人民共和国成立一周年纪念日，在莫斯科的特列甲科夫艺术陈列馆举行。中国派来莫斯科负责布置展览会的王冶秋等五人，已于数日前携带各项展览品自北京抵莫斯科，现正着手筹备中。
