Title: Ningxia Various Ethnic Groups and All Circles Closely Unite to Protest American Aggression and Atrocities
Author:
Date: 1950-09-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 30th—People of all ethnic groups and circles in Ningxia Province unanimously protested against the American imperialist planes that intruded into our country's airspace and strafed our people. All the representatives attending the Third Session of the People's Representatives of All Circles in Yinchuan City sent a telegram expressing their support for Foreign Minister Zhou's just demand for the Security Council to sanction the U.S. military's atrocities. The Ningxia Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Ningxia Provincial Peasants' Association, the Ningxia Provincial Working Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Ningxia Provincial Democratic Youth Federation, the Ningxia Provincial Student Federation, and the Preparatory Committee of the Ningxia Provincial Women's Federation all sent telegrams supporting Foreign Minister Zhou's solemn protest to the U.S. government. Sun Diancai, Director of the Ethnic Affairs Committee of Ningxia Province, Na Changqi (Hui), Deputy Mayor of Yinchuan City, and Ma Fulong, a Hui Imam, all stated in written statements that people of all ethnic groups and circles in Yinchuan City must unite more closely, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the People's Government, and resolutely strive to defend the motherland's territorial sovereignty.

### 宁夏各族各界紧密团结  一致抗议美国侵略暴行

1950-09-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　宁夏各族各界紧密团结
    一致抗议美国侵略暴行
    【新华社西安三十日电】宁夏省各族、各界人民，一致抗议美帝飞机侵入我国领空，扫射我国人民。正在开会的银川市第三届各界人民代表会议全体代表，通电表示拥护周外长要求安理会制裁美军暴行的正义要求。宁夏省总工会、宁夏省农民协会、青年团宁夏省工作委员会、宁夏省民主青年联合会、宁夏省学生联合会、宁夏省妇女联合会筹委会均通电拥护周外长向美政府提出严正抗议。宁夏省民族事务委员会主任孙殿才、银川市副市长纳长旗（回民）、回民教长马福龙均在书面谈话中表示：银川市各族、各界人民要更亲密地团结起来，在中国共产党和人民政府的领导下，坚决为保卫祖国的领土主权而奋斗。
