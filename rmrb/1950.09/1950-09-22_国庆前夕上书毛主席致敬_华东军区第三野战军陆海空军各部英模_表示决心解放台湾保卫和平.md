Title: On the Eve of National Day, Letter to Chairman Mao Expressing Respect: Heroic Models of the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the East China Military Region's Third Field Army Express Determination to Liberate Taiwan and Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-09-22
Page: 2

    【This Newspaper's Report】According to the *Liberation Daily*, during the Hero and Model Congress held by the East China Military Region of the People's Liberation Army and the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Third Field Army, all combat heroes and work models wrote letters to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, expressing their determination to liberate Taiwan and defend peace in the Far East and the world. The letter from all the heroes and models of a certain unit of the Third Field Army stationed in northern Fujian said: "Our honors today should first of all be attributed to the correct leadership of the Communist Party and you, the cultivation of leaders at all levels, the cooperation and support of fraternal units, and the love of all commanders and fighters. We will learn more humbly, connect with the masses, build a regular national defense force, liberate Taiwan, consolidate national defense, and crush any aggressive actions by American imperialism, and strive to defend peace in the Far East and the world." The letter from all the heroes and models of a certain unit of the Third Field Army stationed in eastern Zhejiang said: "We will respond to all the calls and resolutions of you and the Hero and Model Congress. We will create more heroes and models in the battles to liberate Taiwan and Jinmen." The letter from all the heroes and models of the East China People's Navy said: "We swear to you that under your correct leadership, we will redouble our efforts to complete combat training, master ship skills, and build a strong people's navy to answer your cultivation and love." The letter from all the heroes and models of the East China People's Air Force also expressed their determination to strive to complete the glorious task of building a strong people's air force. And they said: "If American planes dare to invade our airspace and strafe our people again, we guarantee: we will resolutely destroy them."

### 国庆前夕上书毛主席致敬  华东军区第三野战军陆海空军各部英模  表示决心解放台湾保卫和平

1950-09-22
第2版()
专栏：

    国庆前夕上书毛主席致敬
　　华东军区第三野战军陆海空军各部英模
    表示决心解放台湾保卫和平
    【本报讯】据解放日报消息：人民解放军华东军区和第三野战军陆、海、空军各部召开英雄模范大会时，全体战斗英雄和工作模范纷纷写信向毛主席、朱总司令致敬，并表示解放台湾与保卫远东和世界和平的决心。驻闽北的第三野战军某部全体英雄模范的信上说：“我们今天的荣誉，应该首先归功于共产党和你们的正确领导、各级首长的培养、兄弟部队的配合支援，和全体指战员同志的爱戴。我们将更虚心学习、联系群众，为建设正规化国防军，解放台湾、巩固国防，和粉碎美帝国主义的任何侵略行为，保卫远东和世界和平而奋斗。”驻浙东的第三野战军某部全体英雄模范的信上说：“我们将响应你们和英模大会的一切号召和决议。在解放台湾、金门的战斗中创造出更多的英雄模范。”华东人民海军全体英雄模范的信上说：“我们向你们宣誓，在你们的正确领导下，加倍努力完成战斗训练，熟练舰上技能，以建设一支强大的人民海军来回答你们的培养与爱护。”华东人民空军全体英雄模范的信上也表示要努力完成建设一支强大的人民空军的光荣任务。并且说：“假如美机再胆敢侵犯我国领空扫射我国人民，我们保证：一定坚决把它们击毁。”
