Title: Our Country's Angry Protest Against the American Aircraft Atrocity; All Soviet Newspapers Published Prominently
Author:
Date: 1950-09-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 2nd: In the past few days, all major Soviet newspapers have prominently reported the Chinese people's angry protests against the U.S. invading Korean army's air force intruding into Chinese airspace. Pravda and other newspapers published the full text of Chinese Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's protest letter to Acheson and his two telegrams to the United Nations. They also published the Xinhua News Agency's rebuttal of U.S. State Department spokesman McDermott's sophistry regarding the U.S. aircraft's intrusion into our airspace, the commentaries of the Beijing Worker's Daily and Guangming Daily, and the protests of various democratic parties, organizations, Northeast workers, and Tianjin people. Pravda also published two dispatches from its Beijing correspondent, Visokov, describing the Chinese people's anger towards the American aggressors.

### 我国对美机暴行的愤怒抗议  苏各报均显著刊载

1950-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国对美机暴行的愤怒抗议
    苏各报均显著刊载
    【新华社莫斯科二日电】过去数天，苏联各重要报纸均以显著篇幅报道中国人民对美国侵略朝鲜军队的空军侵入中国领空的愤怒抗议。真理报及其他报纸都刊出了中国外交部长周恩来致艾奇逊的抗议书以及他致联合国两项电报的全文，并刊载了新华社驳斥美国国务院新闻官麦克德谟狡赖美机侵入我领空的电讯，北京工人日报及光明日报的评论和各民主党派、团体，东北工人以及天津人民的抗议。真理报还刊出了该报驻北京记者维索科夫关于这一事件的两篇通讯，描述中国人民反对美国侵略者的愤怒情绪。
