Title: National Conference on Workers' and Peasants' Education Scheduled to Open Officially on the 20th

Author:

Date: 1950-09-18
Page: 1

　　[News from Our Newspaper] The inaugural National Conference on Workers' and Peasants' Education, jointly convened by the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, has been in preparation for several months. A preparatory meeting began on September 18th, during which various regions reported on typical experiences and key issues. The conference will officially open on the 20th and is expected to last ten days. Representatives attending the conference include education ministers from major administrative regions across the country, directors of education departments and bureaus from provinces and municipalities, heads of cultural and educational departments from industrial sectors and trade unions, as well as model teachers and model students. The conference will discuss important issues such as workers' and peasants' spare-time education, the literacy campaign, workers' and peasants' accelerated secondary schools, and cultural supplementary schools.

### 全国工农教育会议定二十日正式开幕

1950-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国工农教育会议定二十日正式开幕
    【本报讯】中央人民政府教育部、中华全国总工会联合召开的首届全国工农教育会议，经数月筹备，九月十八日开始预备会，由各地区报告典型经验及主要问题。大会将于二十日正式开幕。预计会期十天。参加这次会议的代表为全国各大行政区教育部长，各省市教育厅局长及工会文教部长，各产业部门与工会文教部负责人及模范教师、学习模范。会议将讨论工农业余教育、识字运动及工农速成中学、文化补习学校等重要问题。
