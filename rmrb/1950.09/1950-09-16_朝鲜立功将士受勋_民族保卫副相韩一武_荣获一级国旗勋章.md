Title: Korean Award Ceremony for Meritorious Soldiers: Vice Minister of National Defence Han Il-mu Awarded First Class National Flag Order
Author:
Date: 1950-09-16
Page: 4

　　Korean Award Ceremony for Meritorious Soldiers
    Vice Minister of National Defence Han Il-mu Awarded First Class National Flag Order
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 15th] The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an order on the 7th to award medals of various grades to the soldiers of the People's Army who have performed meritorious service in the Liberation War. Han Il-mu, Vice Minister of National Defence and Commander of the Navy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, was awarded the First Class National Flag Order for his outstanding contributions to strengthening the Republic's naval forces and in the just war for the unification, freedom, and independence of the motherland.
    Kim Jeung-dong, an officer of the Korean People's Army, and Choi Jeong-woong, a soldier, were awarded the title of "Hero of the Republic" for their skillful command and heroic combat, and were awarded the First Class National Flag Order and the Gold Star Medal.
    Kim Young-soo, an officer of the Korean People's Army, and six others who had established special merits, were each awarded the First Class National Flag Order; Kim Kang and thirty-four others were each awarded the Second Class National Flag Order; and Kim Dan and twenty-nine others were each awarded the Third Class National Flag Order.

### 朝鲜立功将士受勋  民族保卫副相韩一武  荣获一级国旗勋章

1950-09-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜立功将士受勋
    民族保卫副相韩一武  荣获一级国旗勋章
    【新华社平壤十五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会七日发布命令，授予在解放战争中立功的人民军将士们以各级勋章。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫副相兼海军司令官韩一武，在加强共和国海军武力与在为祖国统一、自由、独立的正义战争中功勋卓著，荣获一级国旗勋章一枚。
    朝鲜人民军军官金增东、战士崔正雄，由于熟练指挥及英勇战斗，立下了英雄的伟勋，荣膺共和国英雄称号，并荣获一级国旗勋章和金星奖章各一枚。
    建立特殊功勋的朝鲜人民军军官金英洙等七人，各荣获一级国旗勋章一枚；金强等三十四人各荣获二级国旗勋章一枚；金檀等二十九人各荣获三级国旗勋章一枚。
