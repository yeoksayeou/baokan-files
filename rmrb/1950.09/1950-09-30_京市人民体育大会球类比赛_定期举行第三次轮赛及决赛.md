Title: Beijing People's Sports Games Ball Games Scheduled for Third Round and Finals
Author:
Date: 1950-09-30
Page: 10

The Beijing People's Sports Games ball games second week (September 24th) winning teams have been scheduled for the third round on September 30th, or the finals during the Games. The results of the September 24th games and the third round and final team names, times, and locations are announced below:

I. Men's Workers' Basketball: Shanggan Automobile Repair Factory defeated Qinghe Woolen Mill 41-22, People's Bank defeated Beijing Garment Factory 39-32, Beijing Telecommunications Bureau defeated Beijing Machine First Factory 56-10, Shijingshan Iron and Steel Factory defeated Changxindian Railway Factory 41-36, Trolley Repair Factory defeated Biological Products Research Institute 35-18, Ministry of Fuel Industry defeated New Photography Bureau Printing Factory 56-29, Beijing Machine General Factory defeated Beijing Machine Third Factory 39-26, and General Administration of Power Industry defeated Qianjin 47-38.

In the afternoon, People's Bank defeated Automobile Repair Factory 45-30, Beijing Telecommunications Bureau defeated Shijingshan Iron and Steel Factory 48-26, Ministry of Fuel Industry defeated Trolley Repair Factory 57-28, and Beijing Machine General Factory defeated General Administration of Power Industry 34-14.

The above four winning teams (People's Bank, Beijing Telecommunications, Ministry of Fuel Industry, Beijing Machine General Factory) will hold the finals on October 14, 15, and 16.

II. Men's Workers' Football:

Trolley Company defeated Post Office 3-2, Eighth District Xiyan Team defeated 70th Ordnance Factory 3-0, Telecommunications Bureau defeated Xin Ying 8-0, and Beijing Hotel defeated Ministry of Railways Printing Factory 7-2.

The above Trolley Company vs. Xiyan will be held at 2:00 PM on September 30th, and Telecommunications Bureau vs. Beijing Hotel will be held at 3:15 PM on September 30th, both at Huiwen Middle School for the third round, and the two winning teams will compete in the finals during the Games.

III. Women's Workers' Basketball:

North China Agricultural Machinery General Factory forfeited, Ministry of Fuel Industry won; the finals will be held between Beijing Garment Factory and Ministry of Fuel Industry during the Games.

IV. Men's Military and Police Basketball:

In the morning, Public Security Bureau defeated Bohai Troops 42-39, Discipline Inspection Brigade defeated Tank First Brigade Second Regiment 36-16, Artillery Troops defeated Public Security Central Column First Division 37-19, and Public Security Central Column Second Division defeated Navy Directly Subordinate Troops 31-27.

In the afternoon, Discipline Inspection Brigade defeated Public Security Bureau 31-22, and Artillery Troops defeated Public Security Central Column Second Division 57-23.

Discipline Inspection Brigade and Artillery Troops will hold the finals during the Games.

V. Men's Military and Police Baseball:

Public Security Central Column First Division forfeited; Artillery Troops and Tank First Brigade Second Regiment will hold the finals during the Games.

VI. Men's General Group Basketball:

In the morning, Cultural and Educational Committee defeated Qingguan Bureau 26-22, Ministry of Communications defeated Ministry of Education 56-27, Municipal Government Joint Team defeated Finance and Economics Committee 51-22, and Ministry of Finance defeated Ministry of Agriculture 39-31.

In the afternoon, Ministry of Communications defeated Cultural and Educational Committee 33-23, and Municipal Government Joint Team defeated Finance and Economics Committee 48-28.

Ministry of Communications and Municipal Government Joint Team will hold the finals during the Games.

### 京市人民体育大会球类比赛  定期举行第三次轮赛及决赛

1950-09-30
第10版()
专栏：

　　京市人民体育大会球类比赛
    定期举行第三次轮赛及决赛
    京市人民体育大会球类比赛第二周（九月二十四日）比赛优胜各队，已决定分别于九月三十日举行第三次轮赛，或于大会期间举行决赛。兹将二十四日比赛结果及第三次轮赛和决赛队名、时间、地点公布于后：
    一、男子工人组篮球：上干汽车修配厂以四十一比二十二胜清河制呢厂，人民银行以三十九比三十二胜北京被服厂，北京电信局以五十六比十胜北京机器一分厂，石景山钢铁厂以四十一比三十六胜长辛店铁路工厂，电车修造厂以三十五比十八胜生物制品研究所，燃料工业部以五十六比二十九胜新闻摄影局印刷厂，北京机器总厂以三十九比二十六胜北京机器三分厂，电业管理总局以四十七比三十八胜前进。
    下午人民银行以四十五比三十胜汽车修配厂，北京电信局以四十八比二十六胜石景山钢铁厂，燃料工业部以五十七比二十八胜电车修造厂，北京机器总厂以三十四比十四胜电业管理总局。
    以上四个优胜队（人民银行、北京电信、燃料工业部、北京机器总厂）在十月十四、十五、十六三日举行决赛。
    二、男子工人组足球：
    电车公司以三比二胜邮政局，第八区西燕队以三比零胜七十兵工厂，电信局以八比零胜新影，北京饭店以七比二胜铁道部印刷厂。
    以上电车公司对西燕于九月三十日下午二时，电信局对北京饭店于九月三十日下午三时十五分均在汇文中学举行第三次轮赛，两优胜队在大会期间决赛。
    三、女子工人组篮球：
    华北农业机械总厂弃权，燃料工业部优胜；大会期间由北京被服厂与燃料工业部决赛。
    四、男子军警组篮球：
    上午公安局以四十二比三十九胜渤海部队，纠察总队以三十六比十六胜战车一旅二团，炮兵部队以三十七比十九胜公安中央纵队一师，公安中央纵队二师以三十一比二十七胜海军直属部队。
    下午纠察总队以三十一比二十二胜公安局，炮兵部队以五十七比二十三胜公安中央纵队二师。
    纠察总队与炮兵部队在大会期间举行决赛。
    五、男子军警组棒球：
    公安中央纵队一师弃权；炮兵部队与战车一旅二团在大会期间举行决赛。
    六、男子普通组篮球：
    上午文教委员会以二十六比二十二胜清管局，交通部以五十六比二十七胜教育部，市府联队以五十一比二十二胜财经委员会，财政部以三十九比三十一胜农业部。
    下午交通部以三十三比二十三胜文教委员会，市府联队以四十八比二十八胜财经委员会。
    交通部与市府联队在大会期间举行决赛。
