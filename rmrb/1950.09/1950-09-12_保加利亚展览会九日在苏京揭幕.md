Title: Bulgarian Exhibition Opens in Soviet Capital on the 9th

Author: 
Date: 1950-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th: According to TASS, the Bulgarian National Economic and Cultural Construction Achievement Exhibition, showcasing Bulgaria's growing national strength, opened in Moscow on the 9th, the sixth anniversary of Bulgaria's liberation. The exhibition covers an area of 5,000 square meters and displays thousands of exhibits. The exhibition particularly features records of Dimitrov's life and revolutionary activities, and abundant materials demonstrating the friendship between the Bulgarian and Soviet peoples.

### 保加利亚展览会九日在苏京揭幕

1950-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚展览会九日在苏京揭幕
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：显示保加利亚国力增长的保加利亚国民经济与文化建设成就展览会，于九日保加利亚解放六周年纪念日在莫斯科揭幕。展览会占地五千平方公尺，陈列了数千件展览品。展览会特别展出了季米特洛夫的生活与革命活动纪录，和表现保苏人民友谊的丰富材料。
