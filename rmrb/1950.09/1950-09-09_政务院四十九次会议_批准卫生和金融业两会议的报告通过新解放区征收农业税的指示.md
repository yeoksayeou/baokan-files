Title: 49th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approved Reports from Health and Finance Conferences, Passed Instructions on Collecting Agricultural Tax in Newly Liberated Areas
Author:
Date: 1950-09-09
Page: 1

　　Government Administration Council's 49th Meeting
    Approved Reports from Health and Finance Conferences, Passed Instructions on Collecting Agricultural Tax in Newly Liberated Areas
    [Xinhua News Agency, 8th] The 49th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held at 3:30 PM on the 8th. After listening to and discussing the comprehensive report on the National Health Conference made by Li Dequan, Minister of the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government, and the report on the National Joint Conference of the Financial Industry made by Nan Hanchen, President of the People's Bank of China, the meeting approved the two reports. The meeting also approved instructions on the collection of agricultural tax in newly liberated areas and various personnel appointments and removals.

### 政务院四十九次会议  批准卫生和金融业两会议的报告通过新解放区征收农业税的指示

1950-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院四十九次会议
    批准卫生和金融业两会议的报告通过新解放区征收农业税的指示
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第四十九次政务会议于八日下午三时半举行。会议在听取和讨论了中央人民政府卫生部李德全部长所作的全国卫生会议综合报告和中国人民银行南汉宸行长所作的全国金融业联席会议报告后，批准了该两报告。会议并通过了关于新解放区征收农业税的指示及各项任免案。
