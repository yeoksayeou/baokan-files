Title: Representatives from East China and Northwest China Elected to Attend the National Model Worker Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-09-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th: East China and Northwest China administrative regions have elected representatives to attend the National Conference of Representatives of Model Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers.
    Among the 75 representatives from the East China region are Zhang Deqing, chief engineer of the Shanghai Public Transportation Company who created a new type of white coal car; Zeng Yicheng, a special-class meritorious ship worker from Fujian who bravely supported the liberation of Xiamen despite being seriously injured; Zhang Fugui of Fugui Village in Wendeng County, Shandong Province, who organized mutual aid to improve farming methods and led the entire village from poverty to prosperity; and Wang Daqiao, a special-class meritorious worker in guiding the Yi River in northern Jiangsu. After gathering in Jinan, the representatives will depart for Beijing, led by Zhao Yuhua, head of the East China delegation to the National Conference of Representatives of Model Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers.
    The Northwest region's five provinces of Shaanxi, Gansu, Ningxia, Qinghai, and Xinjiang have elected a total of 24 representatives and observers (still lacking three industrial representatives and five agricultural representatives from Gansu). Those elected include seven industrial models from machinery, post and telecommunications, textile, and oil mining enterprises who improved technology and heroically protected factories; and seventeen agricultural models from farmland water conservancy, forestry, cooperatives, animal husbandry, production and disaster relief, cotton planting, and horticulture. Among them are Wang Debiao, a special-class labor model from the former Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region famous for organizing production, and cooperative hero Liu Jianzhang, as well as two animal husbandry models from ethnic minorities. The representatives have already begun arriving in Xi'an from various locations. People from all walks of life in Xi'an held a grand welcome meeting and will hold a grand send-off meeting. The delegation is scheduled to leave for Beijing on the 19th.

### 出席全国劳模代表会议  华东西北选出代表

1950-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席全国劳模代表会议
    华东西北选出代表
    【新华社十七日讯】华东、西北两大行政区已选出出席全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的代表。
    华东区代表七十五人中有创造新式白煤车的上海公共交通公司总工程师张德庆，身负重伤仍英勇支援解放厦门的福建特等功臣船工曾亦成，组织互助改进耕作方法使全村由贫穷走向富裕的山东省文登县富贵村的张富贵，苏北导沂特等功臣王大锹等人。各代表将在济南集中后，由华东区出席全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议代表团团长赵毓华率领向北京出发。
    西北区陕、甘、宁、青、新五省已选出的代表和列席人员共二十四名（尚缺工业代表三名，甘肃农业代表五名）。当选者在工业方面包括机器、邮电、纺织、油矿等企业中改进技术和英勇护厂的模范七人，农业方面包括农田水利、林业、合作、畜牧、生产救灾、植棉、园艺等模范十七人，其中有原陕甘宁边区以组织生产著名的特等劳动模范王德彪和合作社英雄刘建章等，以及少数民族畜牧模范二人。各代表现已分由各地陆续抵达西安。西安各界曾举行盛大欢迎会并将举行盛大的欢送会。代表团预定于十九日首途赴京。
