Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Mathematical Institute Welcomes Professor Hua Luogeng
Author:
Date: 1950-09-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th: According to TASS, Professor Hua Luogeng, representative of the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation attending the 250th anniversary celebration of the German Academy of Sciences, made a sightseeing stop in Moscow on his way back to China. The Mathematical Institute of the Soviet Academy of Sciences held a welcoming ceremony on the 13th. Attendees included Academician Vinogradov, Director of the Mathematical Institute and Hero of Socialist Labor, Academician Lavrentiev, and many Soviet scientists.

Professor Hua Luogeng reported on the Chinese Academy of Sciences' active assistance to the Chinese people in economic and cultural construction.

The Mathematical Institute of the Soviet Academy of Sciences presented Professor Hua Luogeng with several scientific papers and a Russian version of Hua's own work on mathematical principles.

### 苏科学院数学研究所欢迎华罗庚教授

1950-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏科学院数学研究所欢迎华罗庚教授
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：中国科学院参加庆祝德意志科学院二百五十周年的代表团代表华罗庚教授，于返国途中在莫斯科逗留观光，苏联科学院数学研究所特于十三日举行欢迎大会。出席者有数学研究所所长、社会主义劳动英雄维诺格拉多夫院士、拉甫伦提叶夫院士和苏联科学家多人。
    华罗庚教授在会上报告了中国科学院积极协助中国人民进行经济和文化建设工作的情形。
    苏联科学院数学研究所赠给华罗庚教授若干科学论文，和俄文版的华氏本人关于数学原理的著作。
