Title: Introducing the Heroic Struggle of the Korean People Against Aggression: Zhang Naiqi and Six Others Invited to Broadcast

Author:
Date: 1950-09-03
Page: 6

Introducing the Heroic Struggle of the Korean People Against Aggression
Zhang Naiqi and Six Others Invited to Broadcast

Zhang Naiqi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, and delegation members Cai Tingkai, Yang Shiming, Ma Feng, Zhao Guoqiang, Qi Yun, and Li Geng, who recently returned from Korea, have been invited to give speeches on the Central People's Broadcasting Station from the 4th onwards, every evening from 8:00 to 8:30 PM, reporting on the heroic struggle of the Korean people in the war against aggression. The dates, speakers, and topics are as follows: September 4th, Zhang Naiqi will speak on: The Material Foundation of the Korean People's Victory; Cai Tingkai will speak on: Comforting Wounded Soldiers of the Korean People's Army. September 5th, Yang Shiming will speak on: My Discussions with the Heroes of the Korean People's Army; Ma Feng will speak on: The Fighting Korean Literary and Art Circles. September 7th, Zhao Guoqiang will speak on: The Korean Working Class in the Anti-Aggression Struggle; Qi Yun will speak on: Korean Women in Combat. September 8th, Li Geng will speak on: The Heroic Korean Youth in the Anti-Aggression War.

### 介绍朝鲜人民反侵略的英勇斗争情形  章乃器等七人应邀播讲

1950-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　介绍朝鲜人民反侵略的英勇斗争情形
    章乃器等七人应邀播讲
    日前由朝鲜回国的中国人民代表团秘书长章乃器和团员蔡廷锴、杨士明、马烽、赵国强、戚云、李庚等七人应邀，从四日起陆续于每晚八点到八点半在中央人民广播电台发表演讲，报告朝鲜人民在反侵略战争中英勇斗争的情形。演讲日期，主讲人和讲题如下：四日，章乃器讲：朝鲜人民胜利的物质基础；蔡廷锴讲：慰劳朝鲜人民军受伤战士。五日，杨士明讲：我与朝鲜人民军英雄们的座谈；马烽讲：战斗的朝鲜文艺界。七日，赵国强讲：在反侵略战斗中的朝鲜工人阶级；戚云讲：战斗中的朝鲜妇女。八日，李庚讲：反侵略战争中英勇的朝鲜青年。
