Title: Ghanaian New Ambassador to China Calls on Vice Foreign Minister Liu Xiao
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-19
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 18th: Ghana's newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China, Bediako Poku, called on Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Liu Xiao today to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Ambassador Poku arrived in Beijing on the evening of February 17th.

### 加纳新任驻华大使拜会刘晓副外长

1966-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    加纳新任驻华大使拜会刘晓副外长
    新华社十八日讯　加纳共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使贝迪亚科·波库今天拜会外交部副部长刘晓，商谈递交国书事宜。波库大使是二月十七日晚到达北京的。
