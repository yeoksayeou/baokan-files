Title: Vietnamese Foreign Cultural Committee and other Leaders Receive China's Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1966-02-17
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 16th: Leaders of the Vietnamese Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, the Ministry of Culture, and the Vietnam-China Friendship Association received the Chinese Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble led by Zhang Xing on the afternoon of the 15th.
    Those present at the reception included: Wu Guowei and Pham Hong, Vice Chairmen of the Vietnamese Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee; Tran Huy Lieu, Vice President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association; Huang Jun, Secretary-General; and Tran Tien, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture, among others.
    Also present at the reception were Chen Liang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, and Du Zhanchao, Cultural Counselor.
    In the evening, the Vietnamese Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee and the Ministry of Culture also hosted a banquet for the Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble. The Song and Dance Ensemble arrived in Hanoi yesterday.

### 越南对外文委等负责人接见我东方歌舞团

1966-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    越南对外文委等负责人接见我东方歌舞团
    据新华社河内十六日电　越南对外文化联络委员会、文化部和越中友好协会的负责人十五日下午接见了由张星率领的中国东方歌舞团。
    参加接见的有：越南对外文化联络委员会副主任武国威、范鸿，越中友协副会长陈辉燎、秘书长黄俊，文化部办公厅副主任陈进等。
    接见时，中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陈亮、文化参赞杜展潮也在座。
    当晚，越南对外文化联络委员会和文化部还设宴招待东方歌舞团。歌舞团是昨天到达河内的。
