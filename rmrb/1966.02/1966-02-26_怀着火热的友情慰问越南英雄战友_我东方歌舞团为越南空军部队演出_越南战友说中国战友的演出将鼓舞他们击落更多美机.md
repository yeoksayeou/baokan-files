Title: With Burning Friendship, We Greet Vietnamese Heroic Comrades in Arms: The China Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble Performs for the Vietnamese Air Force Unit; Vietnamese Comrades Say the Performance by Chinese Comrades Will Inspire Them to Shoot Down More American Planes
Author:
Date: 1966-02-26
Edition: 3

　　HANOI, February 24 (Xinhua) -- On the evening of February 23, the China Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble, with songs and dances of combat and friendship, gave an enthusiastic performance to greet a heroic unit of the Vietnamese People's Army Air Force.
    In the struggle against America for national salvation, this young and heroic unit of the Vietnamese People's Army Air Force, carrying forward the revolutionary heroism of a decisive battle for victory, has repeatedly and painfully annihilated American flying bandits and achieved brilliant results. The commanders and fighters welcomed their Chinese comrades in arms with joy of victory.
    The literary and art soldiers of the Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble also performed with burning friendship for the Vietnamese Air Force soldiers, with dances reflecting the Vietnamese northern people's "both production and combat," songs praising the hero Nguyen Van Troi, the "Female Militia" dance, and songs praising the great warrior Wang Jie, such as "The Revolutionary Furnace Burns Reddest," all of which were warmly welcomed. The Vietnamese Air Force soldiers said that they had just launched a campaign to learn from the great warrior Wang Jie. They are determined to take Wang Jie and Nguyen Van Troi as examples, fight bravely, and severely strike the American air pirates.
    After the performance, the Vietnamese Air Force heroes also had cordial discussions with the Chinese literary and art comrades, recounting their combat deeds of painfully striking the American flying bandits. They said that the wonderful performances of the Chinese literary and art comrades will further inspire and motivate them to fight bravely and shoot down more American bandit planes! The leaders of the Air Force unit, on behalf of all commanders and fighters, presented a photo of an American F-105 aircraft shot down by them to the Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble. At this time, Chinese and Vietnamese comrades in arms sang the song "Vietnam-China" together in Vietnamese, filled with intense fighting friendship.

### 怀着火热的友情慰问越南英雄战友　我东方歌舞团为越南空军部队演出　越南战友说中国战友的演出将鼓舞他们击落更多美机

1966-02-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　怀着火热的友情慰问越南英雄战友
　　我东方歌舞团为越南空军部队演出
　　越南战友说中国战友的演出将鼓舞他们击落更多美机
    新华社河内二十四日电  中国东方歌舞团二十三日晚上，以战斗和友谊的歌舞，向英雄的越南人民军空军某部队进行了热情的慰问演出。
    在抗美救国斗争中，这支年轻而英勇的越南人民军空军部队发扬决战决胜的革命英雄主义精神，一再痛歼美国飞贼，取得了辉煌的战绩。指战员们怀着胜利的喜悦，欢迎中国战友们。
    东方歌舞团的文艺战士们也怀着火热的友情，为越南空军战士演出了反映越南北方人民“边生产、边战斗”的舞蹈，歌颂英雄阮文追的歌曲、“女民兵”舞和歌颂伟大战士王杰的歌曲《革命熔炉火最红》等节目，都受到热烈的欢迎。越南空军战士们说，他们刚开展了向伟大战士王杰学习的运动。他们决心以王杰和阮文追为榜样，英勇战斗，狠狠地打击美国空中强盗。
    演出结束后，越南的空军英雄们还同中国文艺战友们进行了亲切的座谈，讲述了他们痛击美国飞贼的战斗事迹。他们表示，中国文艺战友们的精采演出，将更加鼓舞和激励他们去英勇战斗，打落更多的美国强盗飞机！空军部队的首长代表全体指战员把他们击落的一架美国Ｆ—105型美国飞机的照片赠送给东方歌舞团。这时，中越两国战友一起用越语高唱歌曲《越南—中国》，洋溢着一片炽烈的战斗友谊。
