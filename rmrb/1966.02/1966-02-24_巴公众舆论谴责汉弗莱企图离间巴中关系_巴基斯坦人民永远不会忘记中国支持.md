Title: Pakistani Public Opinion Condemns Humphrey's Attempt to Drive a Wedge Between Pakistan and China: The Pakistani People Will Never Forget China's Support

Author:

Date: 1966-02-24
Edition: 4

　　Pakistani Public Opinion Condemns Humphrey's Attempt to Drive a Wedge Between Pakistan and China
　　The Pakistani People Will Never Forget China's Support
    According to a Xinhua News Agency report from Rawalpindi, February 22nd: Pakistani political figures and newspapers have condemned U.S. Vice President Humphrey's remarks in Australia that attempted to sow discord between Pakistan and China, and expressed indignation at the United States' plot to coerce Pakistan into opposing China through "aid."
    According to a report in the *Pakistan Times*, political figures in Rawalpindi pointed out that Humphrey's speech demonstrated his disregard for the historical fact of the good neighborly relations between Pakistan and China. At the same time, Humphrey also does not understand that a country that defends its sovereignty will not trade away its national independence, no matter what pressure it faces.
    Chaudhri Fazal Elahi, Deputy Speaker of the National Assembly of Pakistan, told a *Pakistan Times* reporter on the 21st that Pakistan's relationship with China is very harmonious and will continue to be maintained in the future. He said that Humphrey's remarks threatening countries that accept U.S. aid to oppose China were "a despicable tactic." He condemned the United States for attempting to use aid to coerce recipient countries to arrange their foreign relations according to U.S. dictates.
    Mohammad Safdar, leader of the opposition party in the West Pakistan Provincial Assembly, said: The United States' global strategy is to "contain" China. If world peace is threatened, this threat stems from the situation created by the United States in Vietnam.  Jaha Bakhtiar, Chairman of the Council faction of the West Pakistan Muslim League, said: "The Pakistani people will never forget the firm support China gave when Pakistan's territorial integrity was threatened by Indian aggression."
    Mahmood Haq Usmani, General Secretary of the National Awami Party, said: "Pakistan will not trade its sovereignty and self-respect for economic assistance."

### 巴公众舆论谴责汉弗莱企图离间巴中关系　巴基斯坦人民永远不会忘记中国支持

1966-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴公众舆论谴责汉弗莱企图离间巴中关系
　　巴基斯坦人民永远不会忘记中国支持
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第二十二日电　巴基斯坦政界人士和报纸纷纷谴责美国副总统汉弗莱在澳大利亚散布挑拨巴中关系的言论，并且对美国企图通过“援助”来胁迫巴基斯坦反华的阴谋表示愤慨。
    据《巴基斯坦时报》报道，拉瓦尔品第的政界人士指出，汉弗莱的讲话表明他漠视巴基斯坦和中国之间的睦邻关系这个历史事实。同时汉弗莱也并不理解，维护自己的主权的国家不管在什么样的压力下也不会拿民族独立来作交易的。
    巴基斯坦国民议会副议长乔德里·法扎尔·拉希二十一日对《巴基斯坦时报》记者说，巴基斯坦同中国的关系十分融洽，今后还将继续保持下去。他说，汉弗莱发表谈话来威胁接受美国的援助国家反华，是“一种恶劣的手法”。他谴责美国企图利用援助来胁迫受援国家按照美国的旨意来安排它们的对外关系。
    西巴基斯坦省议会反对党领袖穆罕默德·萨夫达尔说：美国的全球战略是“遏制”中国。如果说世界和平受到了威胁，这个威胁正是从美国在越南造成的局势中产生的。西巴基斯坦穆斯林联盟理事会派主席雅哈·巴赫蒂亚尔说：“巴基斯坦人民永远不会忘记中国在巴基斯坦的领土完整受到印度侵略的威胁的时候给予的坚定的支持。”
    民族人民党总书记马哈茂德·哈克·乌斯马尼说：“巴基斯坦不会为了得到经济援助而拿自己的主权和自尊作交易。”
