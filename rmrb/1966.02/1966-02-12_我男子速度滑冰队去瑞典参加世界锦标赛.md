Title: Our Men's Speed Skating Team Leaves for Sweden to Participate in the World Championships
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-12
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, February 11th: The Chinese Men's Speed Skating Team, consisting of six members, left Beijing by plane today for Sweden to participate in the 1966 World Men's Speed Skating Championships to be held in Gothenburg from February 19th to 20th.
    This is the tenth time that Chinese men's speed skating athletes have participated in the World Championships since 1957.
    The athletes participating in this championship are: Wang Jinyu, Wang Wensheng, and Yuan Yingyi. The leader of the team is Li Tianling, Vice Chairman of the Heilongjiang Provincial Branch of the All-China Sports Federation, and the coach is Yu Kongrui.

### 我男子速度滑冰队去瑞典参加世界锦标赛

1966-02-12
第5版()
专栏：

    我男子速度滑冰队去瑞典参加世界锦标赛
    新华社十一日讯　中国男子速度滑冰队一行六人，今天乘飞机离开北京前往瑞典，准备参加二月十九日到二十日在哥德堡举行的一九六六年世界男子速度滑冰锦标赛。
    这是我国男子速度滑冰选手自一九五七年以来第十次参加世界锦标赛。
    参加这次锦标赛的选手是：王金玉、王文生和袁英一。领队是中华全国体育总会黑龙江省分会副主席李天伶，教练是于孔瑞。
