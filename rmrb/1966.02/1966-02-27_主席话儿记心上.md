Title: Chairman's Words Etched in Our Hearts
Author:
Date: 1966-02-27
Edition: 6

　　Chairman's words etched in our hearts,
　　Like the sun rising in our hearts.
　　Chairman's writings placed by our pillows,
　　Eyes closed, our hearts shine bright.
　　Always thinking of Chairman's words,
　　Like the sun rising in our hearts.
　　Chairman's quotations placed on our desks,
　　I feel like I'm by Chairman's side.
　　Whole body warmed, heart ablaze,
　　Daily adding new strength.
　　Chairman's words etched in our hearts,
　　Mind clear, eyes bright,
　　Global vision, seeing far,
　　Neither mountains of knives nor seas of fire can stop us.
　　Chairman's writings studied every day,
　　Constantly nurturing my growth.
　　Devoting my life to the revolution,
　　Unyielding and forever upwards.
　　Studying "On Practice" and "On Contradiction," heart shines bright,
　　Difficulties kicked away with one foot!
　　Shedding the backward oil industry label,
　　Daring to shoulder heavy burdens!

　　Listen Early, Be Free
　　If you want to manage the well freely,
　　First you must listen to Chairman Mao's words.
　　Listen to Chairman Mao's words early,
　　Managing the well will be free early;
　　Listen to Chairman Mao's words late,
　　Managing the well will be free late;
　　Don't listen to Chairman Mao's words,
　　Managing the well will not be free.

　　Revolution Forever, Progress Forever
　　One day without reading Chairman's books,
　　Working lacks energy;
　　Two days without reading Chairman's books,
　　Encountering difficulties, can't withstand;
　　Three days without reading Chairman's books,
　　Forgetting the past class suffering;
　　Long time without reading Chairman's books,
　　Walking the wrong path with eyes open;
　　Reading Chairman's books every day,
　　Revolution forever, progress forever.

　　Having the "Two-Point Perspective"
　　With the "two-point perspective" in mind,
　　Achievements don't lead to arrogance,
　　Difficulties don't cause fear,
　　Stumbling blocks don't cause us to bend.

**Named Entities**

毛主席  Mao Zhuxi
两论    "Liang Lun" (On Practice and On Contradiction)

### 主席话儿记心上

1966-02-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　主席话儿记心上
　　好象心头出太阳
　　主席著作枕旁放，
　　眼睛闭上心里亮，
　　时刻想着主席话，
　　好象心头出太阳。
　　主席语录桌上放，
　　我象身在主席旁，
　　浑身温暖心窝热，
　　天天增添新力量。
　　主席话儿记心上，
　　头脑清醒眼光亮，
　　心怀全球看得远，
　　刀山火海无阻挡。
　　主席著作天天学，
　　不断哺育我成长，
　　一辈子献身于革命，
　　百折不回永向上。
　　学了“两论”心生亮学了“两论”心生亮，
　　困难一脚踢个光；
　　甩掉石油落后帽，
　　千斤重担敢承担！　　
　　早听早自由要想井听人的话，
　　人先要听毛主席的话。
　　早听毛主席的话，
　　管井就早自由；
　　晚听毛主席的话，
　　管井就晚自由；
　　不听毛主席的话，
　　管井就不自由。　　
　　永远革命永进步一天不读主席书，
　　干起工作劲不足；
　　两天不读主席书，
　　遇到困难顶不住；
　　三天不读主席书，
　　忘记过去阶级苦；
　　长久不读主席书，
　　睁着眼睛走错路；
　　天天都读主席书，
　　永远革命永进步。　　
　　有了两分法脑子里有了“两分法”，
　　有了成绩喜不倒，
　　遇到困难吓不倒，
　　碰了钉子弯不了。
