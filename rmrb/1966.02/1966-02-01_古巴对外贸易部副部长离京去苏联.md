Title: Cuban Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Leaves Beijing for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1966-02-01
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Reigila, the Cuban Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, and others left Beijing by plane this morning for Moscow. They arrived in Beijing by plane from Hanoi on the 28th, after concluding their visit to Vietnam. On the evening of the 30th, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of our Ministry, hosted a banquet for the guests.

### 古巴对外贸易部副部长离京去苏联

1966-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴对外贸易部副部长离京去苏联
    据新华社三十一日讯　古巴对外贸易部副部长瑞格依拉等今天上午乘飞机离京前往莫斯科。他们是结束了在越南访问后，于二十八日由河内乘飞机到京。三十日晚，我对外贸易部副部长雷任民曾设宴招待客人。
