Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives Delegation from Chinese Magazine "Red Flag"
Author:
Date: 1966-02-25
Edition: 3

　　Hanoi, February 24 (Xinhua) – Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received this morning the delegation from the Chinese magazine "Red Flag" led by Fan Ruoyu, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Red Flag" magazine.

    Chairman Ho held a long and cordial conversation with the comrades of the delegation.

    Present at the reception were: To Huu, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and Dao Duy Tung, Editor-in-Chief of the magazine "Hoc Tap."

    Chen Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, and Du Zhanchao, Cultural Counselor, of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam were also present.

### 胡志明主席接见我《红旗》杂志代表团

1966-02-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席接见我《红旗》杂志代表团
　　新华社河内二十四日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明二十四日上午接见了由《红旗》杂志副总编辑范若愚率领的中国《红旗》杂志代表团。
    胡主席同代表团的同志们进行了长时间的亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有：越南劳动党中央书记处书记素友，《学习》杂志总编辑陶维松。
    中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陈亮和文化参赞杜展潮也在座。
