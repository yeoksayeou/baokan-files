Author:
Date: 1966-02-23
Edition: 4

　　Japanese Communist Party Publishes Collection of Writings by Kenji Miyamoto
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 21st. The first volume of "Current Tasks and the Japanese Communist Party," a collection of writings by Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, was published and released on the 20th by the Central Publishing Department of the Japanese Communist Party. The second volume of the collection will also be published soon.
    This collection is a sequel to the previously published "The Road of Struggle of Our Party," a collection of writings by Kenji Miyamoto. The book includes speeches and talks given by Kenji Miyamoto in the past four years on the tasks facing the Japanese Communist Party and the Japanese people.

### 日共出版宫本显治文集

1966-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　日共出版宫本显治文集
    新华社东京二十一日电　日本共产党总书记宫本显治的文集《当前的任务和日本共产党》上卷二十日由日共中央出版部出版发行。文集的下卷不久也即将出版。
    这部文集是以前出版的宫本显治文集《我们党斗争的道路》的续编。书中收编了宫本显治在最近四年期间就日本共产党和日本人民面临的任务发表的演说和谈话。
