Title: Picture
Author: Liu Changzhong
Date: 1966-02-23
Edition: 4

    On February 22nd, the Song and Dance Ensemble of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam gave its first performance in China. Bo Yibo, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, Yang Xiufeng, Member of the CPC Central Committee, President of the Supreme People's Court, and President of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association, and others watched the performance and went on stage to take a group photo with all the performers.
                                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Changzhong
