Title: Delegation of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association Concludes Visit to Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1966-02-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 17th – The delegation of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association, composed of Abukuus and Chen Quanbi, concluded its visit to Cambodia on the 17th and left Phnom Penh by plane.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included Hu Ning, Second Vice-Chairman of the Cambodian Journalists Association, Yu Wang, Secretary-General, representatives of the Cambodian Ministry of Information, and members of the Cambodian press.
    Yang Huisheng, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Cambodia, was also at the airport to see them off.
    On the 14th, the delegation visited the Cambodian Journalists Association, and on the morning of the 16th, they respectively paid visits to Xie Sang, Secretary of State for Information of Cambodia, and Xiao Sang, Director of the Office of the Head of State and Chairman of the Cambodian Journalists Association.
    Prince Kang Tor, Prime Minister of the Cambodian Cabinet, received the delegation on the morning of the 17th. They had sincere and cordial talks.
    The delegation held a farewell banquet on the evening of the 16th to entertain members of the Cambodian press. Shan Fushou, First Vice-Chairman of the Cambodian Journalists Association, and association leaders Hu Ning and Yu Wang, Director of the Information Bureau Qin Feng, and other members of the press attended the banquet.
    Abukuus and Chen Quanbi spoke at the banquet. They thanked Cambodia for its warm hospitality and praised the great achievements made by Cambodia under the leadership of Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk. Guests and hosts repeatedly toasted the unity and friendship of Asian-African journalists.

### 亚非记协书记处代表团结束对柬埔寨的访问

1966-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非记协书记处代表团结束对柬埔寨的访问
    新华社金边十七日电  由阿布库斯和陈泉璧组成的亚非新闻工作者协会书记处代表团十七日结束了对柬埔寨的访问，乘飞机离开金边。
    到机场欢送的有，柬埔寨新闻工作者协会第二副主席胡宁，秘书长于旺以及柬埔寨新闻部的代表和柬埔寨新闻界人士。
    中国驻柬埔寨大使馆文化参赞杨惠生也到机场欢送。
    代表团在十四日访问了柬埔寨新闻工作者协会，在十六日上午分别拜会了柬埔寨新闻国务秘书谢桑和国家元首办公室主任、柬埔寨新闻工作者协会主席肖桑。
    柬埔寨内阁首相康托尔亲王在十七日上午接见了代表团。他们进行了诚挚和亲切的谈话。
    代表团在十六日晚举行告别宴会，招待柬埔寨新闻界人士。柬埔寨新闻工作者协会第一副主席山福寿和协会领导人胡宁、于旺，新闻局局长钦丰以及新闻界的其他人士出席了宴会。
    阿布库斯和陈泉璧在宴会上讲了话。他们感谢柬埔寨的热情款待，赞扬柬埔寨在柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王的领导下取得的巨大成就。宾主为亚非新闻工作者的团结和友谊一再干杯。
