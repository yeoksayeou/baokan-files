Title: Algeria and the United States Sign Agreement: US to Give Algeria 200,000 Tons of Wheat
Author:
Date: 1966-02-28
Edition: 4

　　Algeria and the United States Sign Agreement
　　US to Give Algeria 200,000 Tons of Wheat
　　Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, February 24th – The United States and Algeria signed an agreement here on the 23rd, stipulating that the United States will give Algeria 200,000 tons of wheat, worth approximately 12 million US dollars, as a loan from the United States to Algeria.
　　In addition, the United States has decided to donate 88,200 tons of wheat and 5,000 tons of cooking oil to Algeria this year; to provide some food for an employment program in Algeria; and to supply 19,000 tons of wheat to workers in some greening projects in the Constantine region.
　　Algerian Minister of Industry and Power, Bélaïd Abdesselam, announced on the 15th that an American company had reached an agreement with the Algerian National Petroleum Commercial and Transportation Company to establish a joint venture to carry out drilling operations for Sahara oil.

### 阿尔及利亚同美国签订协定  美国将给阿二十万吨小麦

1966-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿尔及利亚同美国签订协定
    美国将给阿二十万吨小麦
    新华社阿尔及尔二十四日电　美国和阿尔及利亚二十三日在这里签订了一项协定，规定美国给予阿尔及利亚二十万吨小麦，价值约一千二百万美元，作为美国给阿尔及利亚的贷款。
    此外，美国决定今年还将赠给阿尔及利亚八万八千二百吨小麦和五千吨食用油；提供一些食品用于阿尔及利亚的一项就业计划；供应君士坦丁地区的一些绿化工地的工人一万九千吨小麦。
    阿尔及利亚工业动力部长贝莱德·阿卜杜萨拉姆十五日宣布，美国一家公司已经同阿尔及利亚全国石油商业、运输公司达成协议，建立一个混合公司，来进行撒哈拉石油的钻井工程。
