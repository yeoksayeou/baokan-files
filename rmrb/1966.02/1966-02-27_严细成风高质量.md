Title: High Quality Through Rigor and Meticulousness

Author:

Date: 1966-02-27
Edition: 6

　　High Quality Through Rigor and Meticulousness
　　Working Alone, Consciously Strict
　　Concentrated operations emphasize quality,
　　Dispersed construction remains the same,
　　Working alone, consciously strict,
　　Leadership absent, no deviation.
　　Strictness
　　Strictness can produce combat effectiveness,
　　Strictness can produce high standards;
  　Strict requirements can achieve meticulousness,
　　Meticulousness is also the embodiment of strictness;
　　Strictness and meticulousness are the specific combination
　　of revolutionary spirit and scientific spirit.
　　Ensuring Quality with a Red Heart
　　Building a refinery in the face of wind and rain,
　　Ensuring quality with a red heart.
　　Strictness in every millimeter,
　　Meticulousness in every thread.
　　Solid
　　Rather have one resounding “solid” than ninety-nine “passable.”
　　Thought and Screws
　　Thought loosens by a fraction,
　　Screw loosens by a thread,
　　Thought loses strength,
　　Screw falls off.
　　A Century-Long Plan
　　Work cannot be satisfied with no problems at the time; one must consider whether it will be safe for decades, even centuries.



**Named Entities**


人民日报   Renmin Ribao

### 严细成风高质量

1966-02-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　严细成风高质量
　　单人独马自觉严
　　集中作战讲质量，
　　分散施工不变样，
　　单人独马自觉严，
　　领导不在不转向。　　
　　严
　　严可以出战斗力，
　　严可以出高标准；
  　要求严可以做到细，　　
　　细也就是严的体现；
　　严和细就是革命精神
　　和科学精神具体的结合。
　　一颗红心保质量
　　顶风冒雨建炼厂，
　　一颗红心保质量。
　　严在每个毫米上，　　
　　细在每个丝扣上。　　
　　过得硬
　　宁可要一个当当响的“过得硬”，也不要九十九个“过得去”。
　　思想和螺丝
　　思想松一分，
　　螺丝松一扣，
　　思想松了劲，
　　螺丝掉下来。　　
　　百年大计
　　干活不能满足于当时不出问题，要想到它几十年、几百年能不能保险。
