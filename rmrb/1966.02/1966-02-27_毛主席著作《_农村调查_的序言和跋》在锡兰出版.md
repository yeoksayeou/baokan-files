　　Mao Zedong's Work "Preface and Postscript to 'Rural Surveys'" Published in Ceylon
    Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, February 26th - The Sinhala version of Chairman Mao Zedong's work, "Preface and Postscript to 'Rural Surveys'," has been published by the People's Publishing House in Colombo, Ceylon, and is now on sale.
    This book was translated by Ananda Kumara, Secretary-General of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association.

### 毛主席著作《“农村调查”的序言和跋》在锡兰出版

1966-02-27
第5版()
专栏：

　　毛主席著作《“农村调查”的序言和跋》在锡兰出版
    新华社科伦坡二十六日电　毛泽东主席的著作《“农村调查”的序言和跋》的僧伽罗文版，已经由科伦坡人民出版社在锡兰出版，并且开始出售。
    这本书是由锡中友好协会秘书长阿南达·库马拉翻译的。
