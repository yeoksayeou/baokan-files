Title: My Ministry of Railways Delegation Visits Vietnam at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1966-02-13
Edition: 4

    Our Ministry of Railways Delegation Visits Vietnam at Invitation
    (Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 12th) At the invitation of the Ministry of Communications and Transportation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Chinese Ministry of Railways Delegation, led by Guo Lu, Vice Minister of Railways, arrived in Hanoi on the 12th for a friendly visit.
    The China Railway Art Troupe Acrobatics Troupe came along with the delegation for a visit.
    Among those who welcomed them were Hong Xichun, Vice Minister of the Vietnamese Ministry of Communications and Transportation; Ha Deng En, Director of the General Railway Administration; Chen Huiliao, Vice President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, and others.
    On the evening of the 12th, Phan Trong Tue, Minister of the Vietnamese Ministry of Communications and Transportation, hosted a banquet to receive the delegation.

### 我铁道部代表团应邀访越

1966-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我铁道部代表团应邀访越
    据新华社河内十二日电　应越南民主共和国交通运输部的邀请，由铁道部副部长郭鲁率领的中国铁道部代表团，十二日到达河内进行友好访问。
    中国铁路文工团杂技团随同代表团前来访问。
    前往欢迎的有，越南交通运输部副部长洪赤心、铁路总局局长何登恩、越中友好协会副会长陈辉燎等。
    十二日晚上，越南交通运输部部长潘仲慧设宴招待代表团。
