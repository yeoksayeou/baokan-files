Title: Angolan People's Liberation Movement Representative Says: Answer Colonialist Violence with Revolutionary Violence
Author:
Date: 1966-02-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, February 4th. Luis Almeida, the representative of the Angolan People's Liberation Movement stationed in Algiers, said at a press conference held on the 4th to commemorate the fifth anniversary of the Angolan armed struggle: The liberation of Angola inevitably must come through the path of armed struggle.
    He said: "Any other path is impossible and inapplicable."
    He also said: "Our masses are urging us to do this, because they have already understood that only by adopting a tit-for-tat approach against the enemy, that is, answering colonialist violence with revolutionary violence, can the enemy be driven into a dead end."

### 安哥拉人民解放运动代表说  以革命暴力回答殖民主义暴力

1966-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

    安哥拉人民解放运动代表说
    以革命暴力回答殖民主义暴力
    新华社阿尔及尔四日电  安哥拉人民解放运动驻阿尔及尔代表路易斯·阿尔梅达四日在为纪念安哥拉武装斗争五周年而举行的记者招待会上说：安哥拉的解放不可避免地必须要通过武装斗争的途径。
    他说：“任何别的途径都是不可能的，也是不适用的。”
    他还说：“我们的群众催促我们这样做，因为他们已经懂得，只有对敌人采取针锋相对的办法，就是以革命的暴力来回答殖民主义的暴力，才能使敌人走进死胡同。”
