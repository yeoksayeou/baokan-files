Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives China Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 17th – Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received all members of the China Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble on the 17th and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    During the reception, the comrades of the Song and Dance Ensemble warmly greeted Chairman Ho Chi Minh and performed songs and dances expressing the deep militant friendship between China and Vietnam. Art workers from China and Vietnam also jointly sang "Ode to Ho Chi Minh" and "The East is Red" in both Chinese and Vietnamese.
    Present at the reception was Wu Quoc Uy, Deputy Director of the Vietnam Committee for External Cultural Relations.
    Chen Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, and Du Zhanchao, Cultural Counselor, were also present at the reception.

### 胡志明主席接见我东方歌舞团

1966-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席接见我东方歌舞团
    新华社河内十七日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明十七日接见了中国东方歌舞团全体人员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，歌舞团的同志们热情地问候胡志明主席，并且演出了表达中越两国深厚战斗友情的歌舞节目。中越两国的文艺工作者还用中越两国语言合唱了《歌颂胡志明》和《东方红》。
    接见时在座的有越南对外文化联络委员会副主任武国威。
    中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陈亮和文化参赞杜展潮接见时也在座。
