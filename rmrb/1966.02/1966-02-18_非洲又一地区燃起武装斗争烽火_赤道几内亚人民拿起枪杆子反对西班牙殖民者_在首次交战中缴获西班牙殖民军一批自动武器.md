Title: Another Region in Africa Ignites Flames of Armed Struggle; Equatorial Guinean People Take Up Arms Against Spanish Colonialists; Seize Batch of Automatic Weapons from Spanish Colonial Army in First Engagement
Author:
Date: 1966-02-18
Edition: 4

    Another Region in Africa Ignites Flames of Armed Struggle
    Equatorial Guinean People Take Up Arms Against Spanish Colonialists
    Seize Batch of Automatic Weapons from Spanish Colonial Army in First Engagement
    (Xinhua News Agency, Accra, 16th) A press release issued here, signed by Jesses Mba Owono, Secretary-General of the People's Ideological Movement of Equatorial Guinea, announced that the Equatorial Guinean people have "formally entered the stage of armed revolution. Our first batch of future liberation fighters in Equatorial Guinea, based in the Moni River mountainous region, has begun military action against Spanish colonialism."
    The press release stated, "The first battle with the Spanish colonial army took place in the town of Guadalupe in Mongomo County. The enemy lost some automatic weapons, and our fighters are using these captured automatic weapons to fight for the liberation of our country." The press release reiterated the determination of the Equatorial Guinean people to carry out the armed struggle to final victory. The release said: "Although we do not have many weapons or much equipment, we possess the most powerful weapon to strike at our oppressors - our loyalty to our motherland and the strongest desire to fight for independence and dignity."

### 非洲又一地区燃起武装斗争烽火  赤道几内亚人民拿起枪杆子反对西班牙殖民者  在首次交战中缴获西班牙殖民军一批自动武器

1966-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    非洲又一地区燃起武装斗争烽火
    赤道几内亚人民拿起枪杆子反对西班牙殖民者
    在首次交战中缴获西班牙殖民军一批自动武器
    据新华社阿克拉十六日电　在这里发表的由赤道几内亚人民思想运动总书记杰塞斯·姆巴·奥沃诺签署的一个新闻公报宣布，赤道几内亚人民已经“正式进入武装革命的阶段，我们赤道几内亚的第一批未来的解放军以莫尼河山区为根据地，开始对西班牙殖民主义展开军事行动”。
    新闻公报说，“在蒙戈莫县的瓜达利佩镇同西班牙殖民军发生了第一次战斗，敌人损失了一些自动武器，我们的战士正在使用这些缴获的自动武器来争取我国的解放。”新闻公报重申赤道几内亚人民决心要把武装斗争进行到最后胜利。公报说：“虽然我们还没有许多武器或许多装备，但是为了打击我们的压迫者，我们拥有最强大的武器——我们对祖国的忠诚以及争取独立和尊严的最强烈的愿望。”
