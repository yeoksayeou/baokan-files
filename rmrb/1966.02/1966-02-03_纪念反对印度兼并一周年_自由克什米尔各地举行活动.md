Title: Commemoration of the Anniversary of Opposition to India's Annexation Held in Various Locations in Azad Kashmir
Author:
Date: 1966-02-03
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, 31st -- According to a report in the Pakistani newspaper "Dawn" on the 30th: Commemorative activities were held in various locations in Azad Kashmir on the 29th to commemorate the first anniversary of the protest day against India's annexation of Jammu and Kashmir.
    The Indian President issued an order on December 21, 1964, announcing the annexation of Jammu and Kashmir as a state of India. Therefore, the people of Kashmir held a protest demonstration in Srinagar on January 29, 1965.
    The All Jammu and Kashmir Muslim Conference, in a message broadcast by Azad Kashmir Radio, said that no force in the world can make the Kashmiri people retreat from their legitimate demand for self-determination. The message said that the Indian rulers have not fulfilled their obligations in this regard.

### 纪念反对印度兼并一周年  自由克什米尔各地举行活动

1966-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念反对印度兼并一周年
    自由克什米尔各地举行活动
    新华社拉瓦尔品第三十一日电　据巴基斯坦《黎明报》三十日报道：自由克什米尔各地二十九日举行纪念活动，纪念反对印度兼并查谟和克什米尔的抗议日一周年。
    印度总统在一九六四年十二月二十一日曾经发布命令，宣布把查谟和克什米尔兼并为印度的一个邦，因此，克什米尔人民在一九六五年一月二十九日斯利那加市举行抗议示威。
    全查谟和克什米尔穆斯林会议通过自由克什米尔电台广播的信说，世界上没有任何力量能使克什米尔人民从他们自决的正当要求上后退。信中说，印度统治者没有履行他们在这方面承担的义务。
