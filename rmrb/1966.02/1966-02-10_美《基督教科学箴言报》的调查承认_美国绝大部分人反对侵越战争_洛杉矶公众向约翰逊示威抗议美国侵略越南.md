Title: US "Christian Science Monitor" Survey Admits Majority of Americans Oppose the War of Aggression Against Vietnam; Los Angeles Public Demonstrates Against Johnson, Protesting American Aggression in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-02-10
Edition: 4

    The US "Christian Science Monitor" Survey Admits
    Majority of Americans Oppose the War of Aggression Against Vietnam
    Los Angeles Public Demonstrates Against Johnson, Protesting American Aggression in Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, February 9th -- According to a report in the *Christian Science Monitor* on February 7th, at the Illinois Central Railroad Terminal in Chicago, an American worker, answering a reporter's question from that newspaper, said: "At least ninety percent of the people oppose this (Vietnam) war."
    The abstract of the report is as follows:
    Are ordinary Americans suddenly concerned about what they are doing in Vietnam?
    To get an answer, I stayed at the Illinois Central Railroad Terminal... The people selected were mostly blue-collar workers – men and women who could represent the majority of the American people. Here is what I learned:
    The vast majority of people oppose the war.
    With few exceptions, they don't know why Americans are fighting in Vietnam. They have heard various explanations but often find them unsatisfactory or nonsensical.
    A photocopying machine operator said: "Vietnam is one of the main topics everyone is talking about today. I know, and I think, we should pull out. First of all, we shouldn't have gone there at all... At least ninety percent of the people oppose this war. I am sure of that."
    A candy vendor said: "I have two children, and they are bound to get involved. I don't think we should mind that business. What happens there has nothing to do with us. Most of my friends think the same way. We feel like we've fought enough wars, and now is the time for us to stop doing all these things."
    A male employee said: "The question is why we ran over there in the first place."
    A wrestling contest founder said: "Everyone is talking about Vietnam... I don't understand: If communism is so bad, then why is it spreading, and spreading everywhere in the world... I don't know why we ran over there."
    Xinhua News Agency, February 9th -- Los Angeles dispatch: One hundred and fifty Los Angeles citizens, upon hearing the news on the 8th that US President Johnson would be arriving in Los Angeles by plane from Honolulu, immediately rushed to the airport to demonstrate against American aggression in Vietnam.
    Demonstrators waved signs outside the airport runway fence, with slogans protesting American aggression in Vietnam. Some signs also read: "You (Johnson, etc.) killed my son." Demonstrators distributed protest leaflets.

### 美《基督教科学箴言报》的调查承认  美国绝大部分人反对侵越战争  洛杉矶公众向约翰逊示威抗议美国侵略越南

1966-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    美《基督教科学箴言报》的调查承认
    美国绝大部分人反对侵越战争
    洛杉矶公众向约翰逊示威抗议美国侵略越南
    新华社九日讯　据《基督教科学箴言报》二月七日报道，在芝加哥伊利诺斯中央铁路总站，一个美国工人在回答这家报纸的记者的问题时说：“（美国）人民至少有百分之九十都反对这场（越南）战争。”
    这篇报道的摘要如下：
    一般的美国人对于他们在越南怎么办是不是突然关心起来了呢？
    为了求得答案，我呆在伊利诺斯中央铁路总站，……所挑选的人绝大部分是蓝领工人——能够代表大多数美国人民的男人和妇女。下面是我了解到的情况：
    绝大部分人反对这场战争。
    除了少数人以外，他们都不知道美国人为什么在越南作战。他们听见过各种解释，但是常常觉得不能令人满意或者不能言之成理。
    一个影印机操作手说：“越南是今天人人都在谈的主要话题之一。我知道，我认为，我们应当撤出来。首先我们就根本不应当到那里去……人民至少有百分之九十都反对这场战争。这一点我是有把握的。”
    一个卖糖果的妇女说：“我有两个孩子，他们势必会卷进去。我认为我们不应当管那个闲事。那里的事情和我们不相干。我的绝大部分朋友都是这样想的。我们觉得我们打仗打够了，现在是我们停止干这一切事情的时候了。”
    一个男职员说：“问题在于首先我们为什么跑到那里去。”
    一个摔角赛的创办人说：“人人都在谈论越南……我不了解：如果共产主义是那样坏，那末为什么它又在蔓延，而且在全世界到处蔓延呢……我不知道我们为什么跑到那里去。”
    新华社九日讯　洛杉矶消息：洛杉矶市民一百五十人八日在听到美国总统约翰逊将乘飞机从檀香山到达洛杉矶的消息后，立即赶赴飞机场举行示威，抗议美国侵略越南。
    示威者在飞机场跑道的栏杆外面挥舞着上面写有抗议美国侵略越南字样的标语牌。有的标语牌上还写着：“你们（约翰逊等）杀死了我的儿子”。示威者散发了抗议传单。
