Title: China-Ceylon Friendship Association Holds Reception to Celebrate the 18th Anniversary of Ceylon's Independence
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 3rd – The China-Ceylon Friendship Association held a reception this evening to celebrate the 18th anniversary of Ceylon's independence.
    Zhang Zhizhang, President of the China-Ceylon Friendship Association, together with Chu Tunan, Han Nianlong, Yan Wenjing, and other responsible persons from relevant departments, attended the reception with Kuruza, Counselor of the Ceylon Embassy in China, embassy officials, and other Ceylonese friends in Beijing.
    At the reception, Zhang Zhizhang and Kuruza raised their glasses, wishing for the continuous development of the profound friendship between the people of China and Ceylon.

### 庆祝锡兰独立十八周年  中锡友协举行招待会

1966-02-04
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝锡兰独立十八周年
    中锡友协举行招待会
    新华社三日讯　中国锡兰友好协会今天晚上举行招待会，庆祝锡兰独立十八周年。
    中锡友协会长张志让，以及楚图南、韩念龙、严文井等有关方面负责人，同锡兰驻中国大使馆参赞柯鲁斯和使馆官员、在北京的其他锡兰朋友，出席了招待会。
    张志让和柯鲁斯在招待会上举杯，祝愿中锡两国人民的深厚友谊不断发展。
