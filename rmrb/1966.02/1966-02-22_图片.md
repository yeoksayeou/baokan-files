Title: Liu Qingrui
Author: Liu Qingrui
Date: 1966-02-22
Edition: 2

    Ghana's newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China, Bediako Poku, presented his credentials to Chairman Liu Shaoqi on February 21. This is a group photo of Chairman Liu Shaoqi accepting the credentials with Ambassador Bediako Poku (fifth from the left) and others.
      Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Qingrui photographed
