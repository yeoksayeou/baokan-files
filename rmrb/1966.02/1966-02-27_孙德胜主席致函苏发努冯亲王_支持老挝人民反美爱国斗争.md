Title: President Ton Duc Thang Sends Letter to Prince Souphanouvong Supporting the Lao People's Anti-American Patriotic Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-02-27
Edition: 5

　　新华社河内二十六日电　越南祖国战线中央委员会主席团主席孙德胜二十四日写信给老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席苏发努冯亲王，谴责美帝国主义加紧侵略老挝的罪行，表示坚决支持老挝人民的爱国斗争。
　　信中指出，美帝国主义在扩大对越南南方和越南北方的侵略战争、不断地向柬埔寨王国进行挑衅和侵犯的同时，还加强在老挝的侵略活动，美帝国主义和它的走狗的恶毒阴谋是企图蚕食老挝解放区，消灭老挝人民的革命武装力量，并且制造紧张局势，为美国派遣地面部队直接侵占中、下寮地区、把战争扩大到印度支那全境找寻借口。
　　信中指出，老挝爱国武装力量发扬坚强的战斗精神，已经给予美帝国主义和它的走狗以沉重的惩罚，粉碎了它们的多次进攻，消灭了敌人的许多有生力量，击落了许多美国现代化飞机，老挝军民最近所取得的许多巨大胜利雄辩地证明：具有坚强不屈斗争传统和丰富战斗经验的英雄的老挝人民，是完全有力量来击败美帝国主义及其走狗的任何进攻阴谋的。
　　信中说，越南人民强烈抗议美帝国主义及其走狗对老挝人民所犯下的罪行，并彻底支持老挝人民的爱国斗争。越南人民愿同老挝人民紧密团结在一起，粉碎美帝国主义妄图把战争扩大到印度支那全境的阴谋计划。

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 26th - Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, wrote to Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, on the 24th, condemning the crimes of US imperialism in intensifying its aggression against Laos and expressing firm support for the Lao people's patriotic struggle.

The letter pointed out that while expanding the war of aggression against South Vietnam and North Vietnam and constantly provoking and invading the Kingdom of Cambodia, US imperialism is also strengthening its aggressive activities in Laos. The vicious plot of US imperialism and its lackeys is to attempt to nibble away at the Lao liberated areas, eliminate the revolutionary armed forces of the Lao people, and create a tense situation, seeking excuses for the United States to send ground troops to directly occupy the central and lower Laos regions and expand the war to the whole of Indochina.

The letter pointed out that the Lao patriotic armed forces, displaying a strong fighting spirit, have given US imperialism and its lackeys heavy punishment, shattered their many offensives, eliminated many enemy effectives, and shot down many modern American aircraft. The many great victories recently achieved by the Lao army and people eloquently prove that the heroic Lao people, with a strong tradition of unyielding struggle and rich combat experience, are fully capable of defeating any offensive conspiracy of US imperialism and its lackeys.

The letter said that the Vietnamese people strongly protested against the crimes committed by US imperialism and its lackeys against the Lao people and thoroughly supported the Lao people's patriotic struggle. The Vietnamese people are willing to unite closely with the Lao people to smash the conspiracy of US imperialism to expand the war to the whole of Indochina.
