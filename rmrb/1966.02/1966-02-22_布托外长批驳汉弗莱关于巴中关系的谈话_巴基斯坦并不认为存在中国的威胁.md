Title: Bhutto Criticizes Humphrey's Remarks on Sino-Pakistani Relations: Pakistan Does Not Believe There is a Chinese Threat
Author:
Date: 1966-02-22
Edition: 6

　　New China News Agency, Rawalpindi, 21st – According to a report by the Pakistan Press Association, Pakistani Foreign Minister Bhutto stated in Karachi on the evening of the 20th: "The US Vice President believes that Pakistan is 'fully aware of the threat from Communist China,' a view that surprises me. Pakistan's consistent position has been that the People's Republic of China does not pose any threat to the Indian-Pakistani subcontinent. This will remain Pakistan's attitude."
    The US Vice President Humphrey made these remarks in Canberra, Australia, after visiting Pakistan and India. The US Information Service reported his words.
    Bhutto said in a statement: "In particular, since the Sino-Indian border conflict and the subsequent US arms supply to India, Pakistan has repeatedly taken this position. The President of Pakistan and the Pakistani government have repeatedly stated clearly that there is no justification for continuing to provide arms assistance to India, as they believe there is no Chinese threat to India.
    Bhutto pointed out: "Humphrey is reported to have said that US economic assistance is 'especially' dependent on our (India and Pakistan's) 'common understanding of the activities and intentions of Communist China.' Our relationship with China and our explicitly opposed attitude to the premise of large-scale US arms assistance to India cannot justify the conclusion that Pakistan, the United States, or India have a common understanding of the activities or goals of the People's Republic of China."
    Bhutto said: "The United States is certainly free to attach whatever conditions it likes to the economic assistance it provides to developing countries. However, as far as Pakistan is concerned, it does not accept any restrictions on its independence in determining its foreign policy according to the requirements of its own national interests."

### 布托外长批驳汉弗莱关于巴中关系的谈话　巴基斯坦并不认为存在中国的威胁

1966-02-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　布托外长批驳汉弗莱关于巴中关系的谈话
　　巴基斯坦并不认为存在中国的威胁
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十一日电　据巴基斯坦联合通讯社报道，巴基斯坦外交部长布托二十日晚上在卡拉奇发表声明说：“美国副总统认为，巴基斯坦‘充分知道共产党中国的威胁’，这种看法使我感到惊异。巴基斯坦的一贯态度是，中华人民共和国并不形成对印度—巴基斯坦次大陆的任何威胁。这仍将是巴基斯坦的态度。”
    美国副总统汉弗莱是在访问巴基斯坦和印度以后，在澳大利亚堪培拉讲这番话的。美国新闻处报道了他的这些话。
    布托在声明中说：“特别是，自从中印边界冲突以及此后美国向印度提供武器以来，巴基斯坦一再采取这种立场。巴基斯坦总统和巴基斯坦政府曾经屡次明确表示，继续向印度提供武器援助是没有道理的，因为他们认为并不存在中国对印度的威胁。
    布托指出：“据报道汉弗莱说过，美国提供经济援助‘尤其’要取决于我们（印度和巴基斯坦）‘对共产党中国的活动和意图的共同理解’。我们同中国的关系以及我们明确地反对使美国向印度提供大规模武器援助的前提的态度，都不能证明这种结论是正确的，即：巴基斯坦同美国或印度对于中华人民共和国的活动或目标有着共同的理解。”
    布托说：“美国对于向发展中的国家所提供的经济援助爱附加什么条件就附加什么条件，这当然是美国的自由。但是，就巴基斯坦来说，它不接受对于它按照自己民族利益的要求而决定自己的对外政策的独立性的任何限制。”
