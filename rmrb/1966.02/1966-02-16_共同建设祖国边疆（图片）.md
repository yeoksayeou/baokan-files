　　共同建设祖国边疆
　　到黑龙江来建设的北京知识青年，经过一年的劳动，获得思想、劳动双丰收
　　新华社记者　许万育摄
　　西藏国营澎波农场场长王长义（右）将战斗的武器——铁锹，授给从首都来西藏建设的青年
  新华社记者　吴祖政摄
    西藏奴隶出身的桑嘎（右）在汉族技术员潘天锡的指导下，学习测量技术  马竞秋摄
    上海青年在新疆生产建设兵团塔里木垦区开荒生产，艰苦创业。虽然住在临时的草棚里，可是他们的劳动和生活却是很健康的愉快的
              新华社记者　闵福全摄

Jointly Build the Motherland's Border Regions
　　Beijing educated youth who came to Heilongjiang for construction have reaped a double harvest of ideology and labor after a year of work.
　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Wanyu, photographer
　　Wang Changyi (right), director of the state-owned Pengbo Farm in Tibet, presents the weapon of combat—the shovel—to the youth who came from the capital to build Tibet.
　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Zuzheng, photographer
    Sangga (right), who was born a slave in Tibet, is learning surveying technology under the guidance of Han technician Pan Tianxi. Ma Jingqiu, photographer
    Shanghai youth are reclaiming land and engaging in arduous pioneering work in the Tarim Reclamation Area of the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps. Although they live in temporary grass sheds, their labor and life are very healthy and happy.
              Xinhua News Agency reporter Min Fuquan, photographer
