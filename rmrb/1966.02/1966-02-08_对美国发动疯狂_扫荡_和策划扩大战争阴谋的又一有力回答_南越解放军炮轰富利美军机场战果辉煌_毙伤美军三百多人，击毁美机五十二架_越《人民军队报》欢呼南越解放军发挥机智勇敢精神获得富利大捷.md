Title: Another Powerful Answer to the US's Frenzied "Sweep" and Conspiracy to Expand the War: South Vietnam Liberation Army's Artillery Bombardment of Phu Loi US Airfield Achieves Glorious Results; Over 300 US Troops Killed and Wounded, 52 US Aircraft Destroyed; Vietnamese *People's Army Daily* Hails the South Vietnam Liberation Army for Displaying Ingenuity and Courage to Achieve the Great Victory at Phu Loi
Author:
Date: 1966-02-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 6th -- According to a report by the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency: On the evening of January 30th, the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces launched an attack on the Phu Loi airfield in Thu Dau Mot province, killing and wounding over 300 US invading troops and destroying 52 US helicopters.
    Preliminary reports say that on the evening of January 30th, the Liberation Armed Forces fiercely bombarded the Phu Loi airfield, the base of the US First Infantry Division, five kilometers southeast of the capital of Thu Dau Mot province, with mortars. The artillery fire launched by the Liberation Armed Forces turned the airfield into a sea of flames.
    In addition to inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy and destroying many helicopters, the Liberation Armed Forces also destroyed three enemy armored vehicles and three artillery pieces, and burned down many US military dormitories.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 7th -- The Vietnamese *People's Army Daily* published a commentary on the 7th, hailing the glorious victory of the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces' attack on the Phu Loi airfield in Thu Dau Mot province on the evening of January 30th, eliminating more than 300 US troops and destroying 52 enemy helicopters.
    The commentary said that this brilliant victory once again proves the leaping growth of the Liberation Army soldiers' combat power, demonstrating their ingenuity, courage, and excellent shooting skills. This victory also demonstrates the powerful strength of people's war; the Liberation Armed Forces receive the help and support of the local people, who become their eyes and ears, helping them to grasp the enemy's situation and ensuring the complete victory of the attack on the airfield.
    The commentary said that no matter where the US invading troops are stationed, or where they go to carry out "sweeps," they cannot escape the fierce attacks of the heroic South Vietnamese army and people.

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th -- Saigon News: The South Vietnam Liberation Army heavily damaged a US company on the night of the 5th, more than 30 kilometers from Saigon.
    According to Western news agencies, the company that was beaten was a company of the Second Brigade of the US 25th Division. The Liberation Army used hand grenades to attack the enemy at close range, inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy.
    United Press International reported that "the fighting was so close that most of the US casualties were caused by hand grenades."

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th -- Saigon News: On the evening of the 5th, the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces destroyed the bar of an enemy hotel in Vinh Long city, southwest of Saigon, with explosive devices and hand grenades.
    According to Western news agencies, eight American invaders were killed and wounded on the spot in the explosion.

### 对美国发动疯狂“扫荡”和策划扩大战争阴谋的又一有力回答  南越解放军炮轰富利美军机场战果辉煌  毙伤美军三百多人，击毁美机五十二架  越《人民军队报》欢呼南越解放军发挥机智勇敢精神获得富利大捷

1966-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    对美国发动疯狂“扫荡”和策划扩大战争阴谋的又一有力回答
    南越解放军炮轰富利美军机场战果辉煌
    毙伤美军三百多人，击毁美机五十二架
    越《人民军队报》欢呼南越解放军发挥机智勇敢精神获得富利大捷
    新华社河内六日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：一月三十日晚上，越南南方解放武装力量向土龙木省的富利机场发动袭击，打死打伤美国侵略军三百多人，击毁美国直升飞机五十二架。
    初步消息说，一月三十日晚上，解放武装力量用迫击炮猛烈轰击在土龙木省省会东南五公里的美国第一步兵师基地富利机场。解放武装力量发射的炮火，使这个机场变成了一片火海。
    解放武装力量除了使敌人伤亡惨重和击毁多架直升飞机以外，还击毁敌人三辆装甲车和三门大炮，烧毁许多美军宿舍。
    新华社河内七日电　越南《人民军队报》七日发表评论，欢呼越南南方解放武装力量一月三十日晚上袭击土龙木省富利机场，歼灭美军三百多名，击毁敌直升飞机五十二架的辉煌战绩。
    评论说，这一光辉战绩，再一次证明了解放军战士们战斗力量的飞跃成长，表现了他们的机智、勇敢精神和优秀的射击技术。这一胜利也说明了人民战争的强大力量，解放武装力量得到当地人民的帮助和支持，当地人民成为他们的耳目，帮助他们掌握敌人的情况，保证袭击机场的战斗取得完全胜利。
    评论说，不管美国侵略军队驻在哪里，也不管他们到什么地方进行“扫荡”，都逃脱不了英雄的越南南方军民猛烈的打击。
    新华社七日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放军五日夜间在离西贡三十多公里的地方，重创一连美军。
    据西方通讯社报道，挨揍的是美军第二十五师第二旅的一个连。解放军在距离美军很近的地方，用手榴弹打击敌人，使敌人遭到惨重伤亡。
    合众国际社报道说，“战斗是如此接近，以致美军大部分的伤亡是由于手榴弹造成的。”
    据新华社六日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量五日晚在西贡西南的永隆市，用爆炸装置和手榴弹炸毁了敌人的一个饭店的酒吧间。
    据西方通讯社透露，在爆炸中有八个美国侵略者被当场炸死和炸伤。
