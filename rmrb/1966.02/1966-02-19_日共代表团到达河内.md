Title: Japanese Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1966-02-19
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 17th - According to the Vietnam News Agency, a delegation from the Japanese Communist Party, led by General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party Kenji Miyamoto, arrived in Hanoi on the 17th for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party.
    The delegation members included: Standing Committee member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Secretariat Secretary Masayoshi Oka; Politburo members Tadahito Kurahara and Akira Yonehara; Secretariat Secretary Kazuyoshi Suna; Central Committee Alternate Members Koichiro Ueda, Kenjiro Ueda, and Akira Kudo.
    The delegation was warmly welcomed by First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party Le Duan, Politburo members Truong Chinh, Le Duc Tho, Nguyen Duy Trinh, Secretariat Secretary Nguyen Van Tran, Central Committee members Hoang Quoc Viet, Nguyen Lam, Central Committee Alternate Members Tran Quang Huy, Hoang Tung, Deputy Director of the Central Foreign Liaison Committee Tran Chi Hien, Deputy Director of the Central Office Minh Chau, and representatives from all circles in Hanoi.
    Korean Ambassador to Vietnam Ma Dong-san and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam Chen Liang also went to welcome the delegation.

### 日共代表团到达河内

1966-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    日共代表团到达河内
    新华社河内十七日电　据越南通讯社报道，由日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治率领的日本共产党代表团十七日到达河内。他们是应越南劳动党中央委员会的邀请前来进行友好访问的。
    代表团团员有：日共中央政治局常务委员、书记处书记冈正芳，中央政治局委员藏原惟人、米原昶，中央书记处书记砂间一良，中央候补委员上田耕一郎、上田建二郎、工藤晃。
    代表团受到越南劳动党中央第一书记黎笋，中央政治局委员长征、黎德寿、阮维桢，中央书记处书记阮文珍，中央委员黄国越、阮蓝，中央候补委员陈光辉、黄松，中央对外联络委员会副主任陈志贤，中央办公室副主任明珠，和河内市各界代表的热烈欢迎。
    朝鲜驻越南大使马东山和中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陈亮也前往欢迎。
