Title: Right-wing Thugs Smashed Our Consulate in Makassar, Injured Our Consul and Staff; We Lodged the Strongest Protest with the Indonesian Government. Note from the Foreign Ministry: This is a Heinous Incident Violating the Norms of International Relations and an Extremely Grave Provocation Against Our Country

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-28
Edition: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 27th – Han Nianlong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, summoned the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Zawawi, on February 27th and presented him with a note, lodging the strongest protest with the Indonesian government regarding the barbaric act of thugs organized by Indonesian right-wing forces attacking the Chinese Consulate in Makassar on February 25th, injuring the Chinese Consul and consulate staff. The full text of the note is as follows:

To the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia:

　　The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China presents its compliments to the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia and states the following regarding the barbaric act of thugs organized by Indonesian right-wing forces attacking the Chinese Consulate in Makassar and injuring the Chinese Consul and consulate personnel:

　　On February 25, 1966, following a mass rally presided over by the Governor of Makassar Province to pledge loyalty to President Sukarno, a group of over three hundred thugs calling themselves the "Indonesian University Students United Action Organization," armed with clubs, stones, and sharp weapons, rushed to the front of the Chinese Consulate in Makassar at 9:00 AM (local time), shouting slogans such as "Crush the People's Republic of China," "Expel the People's Republic of China," "Get rid of the Chinks," and "Long Live Nasution." At 9:15 AM, these thugs pushed down the iron gate of the consulate, stormed into the compound, and attacked anyone they encountered, hurling stones at consulate personnel. The Chinese Consul, Shi Jinkan, was beaten, suffering injuries to his chest and elbows. All other staff members of the consulate were also beaten and injured. Staff member Lin Shenmu suffered a ruptured abdomen with bleeding, his injuries being particularly serious. The thugs also brandished daggers to threaten people. At the same time, the thugs also smashed the consulate's offices and dormitories. The consulate's doors, windows, roof tiles, as well as office equipment such as iron desks, iron chairs, electric lights, fans, and telephones, were severely damaged. The thugs even smashed the statue of Chairman Mao Zedong, the beloved leader of the Chinese people, and stole the Chinese national emblem and the consulate's brass plaque, forcibly raising the Indonesian flag on the consulate's flagpole and writing "Long Live Nasution" slogans on the consulate's walls. This act of violence lasted for one hour and ten minutes.

　　It must be pointed out that two days before this atrocity, at 11:00 PM (local time) on the evening of February 23rd, thugs from the Indonesian right-wing forces had already carried out an attack on the Chinese Consulate in Makassar. They shouted anti-China slogans in front of the consulate and threw stones into the consulate compound. The Chinese Consul had lodged a strong protest with the head of the Makassar Provincial Government regarding this incident. The head of the provincial government promised to guarantee the safety of the consulate and to ensure that similar incidents would not occur again. The Chinese Embassy had also protested to the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, demanding that the Indonesian government effectively guarantee the safety of the Chinese Consulate in Makassar. However, the Indonesian government and the Makassar local authorities did not take the necessary measures. When the thugs attacked the Chinese Consulate in Makassar on February 25th, the military police present stood idly by, allowing the thugs to commit violence. Even after the thugs left, the military police unreasonably prevented the Chinese Consulate from taking down the Indonesian flag hoisted on the consulate's flagpole, and openly stated that this was an order from the Makassar base commander. After repeated negotiations by the Chinese Consulate, it was not until 6:00 PM that the Indonesian flag was taken down and removed. When the Chinese Consul protested the atrocity to the military and political leaders of Makassar in person, the local naval commander present even defended the thugs and made unreasonable accusations against China. These facts fully demonstrate that this act of violence, which was clearly planned by right-wing forces supporting Nasution, was condoned and supported by certain local military and political authorities.

　　This barbaric act by the Indonesian right-wing forces in destroying the Chinese Consulate in Makassar and injuring the Chinese Consul and consulate staff is an arrogant infringement on the privileges of diplomatic missions and the personal safety of consular personnel, a heinous incident that violates the norms of international relations, and an extremely grave act of provocation against the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people. The Chinese government and the Chinese people express their utmost indignation at this. The Chinese government lodges the strongest protest with the Indonesian government.

　　The Chinese government demands that the Indonesian government immediately issue a public apology for this incident, immediately punish the perpetrators and masterminds, immediately be responsible for returning the stolen Chinese national emblem and the consulate's brass plaque, compensate for all the losses of the consulate, ensure that similar incidents do not occur again, and take effective measures to protect the safety of Chinese representative organizations and personnel in Indonesia.

　　With the highest respect.

　　　　　　　　　　　Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of ### 右派暴徒捣毁我驻望加锡领事馆打伤我领事和工作人员　我向印度尼西亚政府提出最强烈抗议　外交部照会指出：这是破坏国际关系准则的骇人听闻事件，是对我国的极严重挑衅

1966-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　右派暴徒捣毁我驻望加锡领事馆打伤我领事和工作人员
　　我向印度尼西亚政府提出最强烈抗议
　　我外交部照会指出：这是破坏国际关系准则的骇人听闻事件，是对我国的极严重挑衅
    新华社二十七日讯　我国外交部副部长韩念龙二十七日召见印度尼西亚驻华大使查禾多，向他提交一份照会，就印度尼西亚右派势力组织的暴徒二月二十五日袭击中国驻望加锡领事馆、打伤中国领事和领事馆工作人员的野蛮暴行，向印度尼西亚政府提出最强烈的抗议。照会全文如下：印度尼西亚驻华大使馆：
    中华人民共和国外交部向印度尼西亚共和国大使馆致意，并就印度尼西亚右派势力组织的暴徒袭击中国驻望加锡领事馆、打伤中国领事和领事馆人员的野蛮暴行，申述如下：
    一九六六年二月二十五日，在望加锡省长主持下召开了效忠苏加诺总统的群众大会后，有一批自称是“印度尼西亚大学生统一行动组织”的暴徒三百多人，手持棍棒、石块和利器，于上午九时（当地时间）冲到中国驻望加锡领事馆门前，狂呼“粉碎中华人民共和国”、“驱逐中华人民共和国”、“赶走支那人”、“纳苏蒂安万岁”等口号。九时十五分，这批暴徒推倒领事馆的铁门，冲入院内，逢人便打，用石块猛击领事馆人员。中国领事师晋侃遭到殴打，胸部和肘部受伤。领事馆所有其余工作人员也都被殴打受伤。职员林慎木腹部被击破出血，伤势尤为严重。暴徒还拔出匕首进行威胁。与此同时，暴徒还捣毁了领事馆的办公室和宿舍。领事馆的门窗、屋瓦以及办公室内的铁桌、铁椅、电灯、风扇、电话机等用具，都遭到极为严重的破坏。暴徒还竟然打碎了中国人民敬爱的领袖毛泽东主席的塑象，劫走了中国的国徽和领事馆的铜牌，强行在领事馆的旗杆上升挂印度尼西亚国旗，在领事馆门墙上涂写“纳苏蒂安万岁”的标语。这次暴行历时一小时又十分钟。
    必须指出，在这次暴行前两天，即二月二十三日晚十一时（当地时间），印度尼西亚右派势力的暴徒，已经对中国驻望加锡领事馆进行过一次袭击。他们在领事馆门前狂呼反华口号，并向领事馆院内投掷石块。中国领事曾为此向望加锡省政府负责人提出强烈抗议。省政府负责人答应保证领事馆的安全，保证不再发生类似事件。中国大使馆也曾向印度尼西亚外交部提出抗议，要求印度尼西亚政府切实保证中国驻望加锡领事馆的安全。但是，印度尼西亚政府和望加锡地方当局，并没有采取必要措施。二月二十五日，暴徒袭击中国驻望加锡领事馆时，在场的军警居然袖手旁观，放任暴徒行凶。甚至在暴徒走后，军警还无理阻拦中国领事馆卸下升挂在领事馆旗杆上的印度尼西亚国旗，并公然声称这是望加锡基地司令的命令。经中国领事馆多次交涉后，仍拖到下午六时，才将印度尼西亚国旗卸下取走。中国领事就这次暴行向望加锡的军政负责人当面提出抗议时，在座的当地海军司令竟然为暴徒辩护，对中国进行无理指责。这些事实充分表明，这次显然由支持纳苏蒂安的右派势力策划的暴行，是受到当地某些军政当局纵容和支持的。
    印度尼西亚右派势力这次捣毁中国驻望加锡领事馆、打伤中国领事和领事馆工作人员的野蛮暴行，是对外交代表机构的特权和领事人员人身安全的蛮横侵犯，是破坏国际关系准则的骇人听闻的事件，是对中华人民共和国和中国人民极端严重的挑衅行为。对此，中国政府和中国人民表示极大的愤慨。中国政府向印度尼西亚政府提出最强烈的抗议。
    中国政府要求印度尼西亚政府，对这次事件立即公开道歉，立即惩办肇事者和主使者，立即负责送还被劫走的中国国徽和领事馆的铜牌，赔偿领事馆的一切损失，保证不再发生类似事件，并且采取切实有效措施，保护中国在印度尼西亚的代表机构和人员的安全。顺致最崇高的敬意。
                          中华人民共和国外交部
                          一九六六年二月二十七日于北京
