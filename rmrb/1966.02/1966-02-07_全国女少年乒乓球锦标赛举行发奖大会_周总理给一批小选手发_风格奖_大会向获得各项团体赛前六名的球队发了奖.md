Title: National Girls' Junior Table Tennis Championships Hold Award Ceremony; Premier Zhou Awards "Style Prizes" to a Group of Young Players; The Conference Presented Awards to the Top Six Teams in Various Team Events
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-07
Edition: 1

    National Girls' Junior Table Tennis Championships Hold Award Ceremony
    Premier Zhou Awards "Style Prizes" to a Group of Young Players
    The Conference Presented Awards to the Top Six Teams in Various Team Events
    (Xinhua News Agency, 6th) The 1966 National Twenty-one Unit Girls' Junior Table Tennis Championships held an award ceremony today at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium.

    Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, along with over 15,000 people from the capital, attended today's conference. Premier Zhou Enlai presented awards to twenty-two young female table tennis players who received the "Style Prize" for the championships. These players demonstrated excellent performance in political thought and competition style. As the awards were presented, the entire audience stood up and applauded warmly for a long time. Premier Zhou Enlai shook hands with each of the young players, greatly encouraging them.

    Premier Zhou Enlai also took a group photo with the "Style Prize" winners, as well as the young female table tennis players from various regions who won various competitions, and their coaches.

    Minister of Education He Wei, Vice-Chairman of the National Sports Commission Rong Gaotang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Hu Keshi, and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Beijing Municipal Party Committee and Vice Mayor of Beijing Wan Li presented awards to the top six teams in the four team events of the championships.

    After the awards ceremony, outstanding table tennis players Zhuang Zedong, Zhang Xielin, Lin Huiqing, Liang Lizhen, and some of the young female players participating in the championships gave wonderful table tennis performances.

    (Xinhua News Agency) The 1966 National Twenty-one Unit Girls' Junior Table Tennis Championships concluded on the 5th. The rankings of the top six teams in various team events are as follows:

    Elementary School Grassroots Team Event: Shanghai Julu Road No. 1 Elementary School Team, Taiyuan Railway No. 6 Elementary School Team, Guangzhou Baoqingfang Elementary School Team, Nanning Zhongshan North Section Elementary School Team, Jilin Hunjiang River Mouth Elementary School Team, Nanjing Yuhua Road Elementary School Team

    Middle School Grassroots Team Event: Shanghai No. 55 Middle School Team, Guangzhou No. 1 Middle School Team, Shenyang Yucai School Team, Beijing Normal University Affiliated Girls' Middle School Team, Fuzhou No. 5 Middle School Team, Jinan Shandong Experimental Middle School Team

    Mixed Team Event: Shanghai Team, Liaoning Team, Hebei Team, Jilin Team, Shanxi Team, Hubei Team

    Provincial and Municipal Representative Team Event: Shanghai Team, Heilongjiang Team, Guangxi Team, Hubei Team, Sichuan Team, Guangdong Team.

### 全国女少年乒乓球锦标赛举行发奖大会  周总理给一批小选手发“风格奖”  大会向获得各项团体赛前六名的球队发了奖

1966-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

    全国女少年乒乓球锦标赛举行发奖大会
    周总理给一批小选手发“风格奖”
    大会向获得各项团体赛前六名的球队发了奖
    据新华社六日讯　一九六六年全国二十一单位女少年乒乓球锦标赛今天在北京工人体育馆举行发奖大会。
    国务院总理周恩来同首都一万五千多人出席了今天的大会。周恩来总理向获得锦标赛“风格奖”的二十二名女少年乒乓球选手发了奖。这些选手在政治思想、比赛风格等方面都表现得很好。发奖时，观众全体起立，长时间热烈鼓掌。周恩来总理同小选手们一一握手，小选手们受到很大鼓舞。
    周恩来总理还同获得“风格奖”的选手，以及获得各项比赛优胜的各地女少年乒乓球选手和她们的教练一起合影。
    教育部部长何伟，国家体委副主任荣高棠，共青团中央书记处书记胡克实，中共北京市委书记处书记、北京市副市长万里，分别向获得锦标赛四项团体赛前六名的球队发了奖。
    发奖以后，优秀乒乓球运动员庄则栋、张燮林、林慧卿、梁丽珍等和参加这次锦标赛的一部分女少年选手作了精采的乒乓球表演。
    据新华社讯　一九六六年全国二十一单位女少年乒乓球锦标赛五日结束，获得各项团体赛前六名球队的名次如下：
    小学基层队团体赛：上海巨鹿路第一小学队、太原市铁路第六小学队、广州市宝庆坊小学队、南宁市中山北段小学队、吉林省浑江市河口小学队、南京市雨花路小学队
    中学基层队团体赛：上海市第五十五中学队、广州市第一中学队、沈阳市育才学校队、北京师范大学附属女子中学队、福州市第五中学队、济南市山东实验中学队
    混合队团体赛：上海队、辽宁队、河北队、吉林队、山西队、湖北队
    省、市代表队团体赛：上海队、黑龙江队、广西队、湖北队、四川队、广东队。
