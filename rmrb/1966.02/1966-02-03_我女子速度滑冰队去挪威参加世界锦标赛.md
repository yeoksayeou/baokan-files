Title: China's Women's Speed Skating Team Leaves for Norway to Participate in the World Championships

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-03
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd: The Chinese women's speed skating team, including four outstanding athletes, left Beijing by plane today for Norway, preparing to participate in the 1966 World Women's Speed Skating Championships to be held in Trondheim from February 12th to 13th.



**Named Entities**


中国    Zhōngguó (China)
挪威    Nuówēi (Norway)
北京    Běijīng (Beijing)
特隆赫姆  Tè lóng hè mǔ (Trondheim)
新华社    Xīnhuá shè (Xinhua News Agency)

### 我女子速度滑冰队去挪威参加世界锦标赛

1966-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    我女子速度滑冰队去挪威参加世界锦标赛
    据新华社二日讯　包括四名优秀选手的中国女子速度滑冰队，今天乘飞机离开北京前往挪威，准备参加二月十二日到十三日将在特隆赫姆举行的一九六六年世界女子速度滑冰锦标赛。
