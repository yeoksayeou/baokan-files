Title: Learn from Steel Warrior Mai Xiande; All-China Federation of Trade Unions Calls on Workers Nationwide to Learn Mai Xiande's Revolutionary Spirit and Promote Ideological Revolutionization; Communist Youth League Central Committee Decides to Award Comrade Mai Xiande the Title of Model Communist Youth League Member
Author:
Date: 1966-02-05
Edition: 2

    Learn from Steel Warrior Mai Xiande
    All-China Federation of Trade Unions Calls on Workers Nationwide to Learn Mai Xiande's Revolutionary Spirit and Promote Ideological Revolutionization
    Communist Youth League Central Committee Decides to Award Comrade Mai Xiande the Title of Model Communist Youth League Member
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions today issued a notice calling on workers nationwide to learn from Comrade Mai Xiande, the steel warrior.
    The notice pointed out that Comrade Mai Xiande is a good soldier of Chairman Mao and a good example for our workers to learn from. Every one of our workers should conscientiously learn from Comrade Mai Xiande's revolutionary spirit and promote the revolutionization of their own thinking. The notice called on workers nationwide to learn from Mai Xiande by, like him, diligently studying and applying Chairman Mao's works with deep class feelings, and working hard on the "application" aspect; resolutely listening to Chairman Mao's words, resolutely acting according to Chairman Mao's instructions, firmly remembering Chairman Mao's teachings, and wholeheartedly serving the Chinese people and the people of the world; like him, having profound class hatred and a high sense of combat readiness, and being ready at any time to eliminate invading enemies; and like him, fearing neither heaven nor earth, overwhelming all enemies, and fighting to the last breath.
    The notice said that today, as our country's magnificent Third Five-Year Plan has begun, we should even more learn from Comrade Mai Xiande's hard-bone spirit, be heroic and tenacious, unafraid of difficulties, and widely and deeply carry out the mass movement of comparing with, learning from, catching up with, helping, and surpassing, strive to complete the state plan, and make more outstanding contributions in the three great revolutionary movements.
    The notice required trade union organizations at all levels, under the leadership of Party committees at all levels, to actively carry out activities to learn from Comrade Mai Xiande, and through this activity, promote the conscious mass movement of studying Chairman Mao's works to a new high tide and promote the ideological revolutionization of workers.
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League today issued a decision on awarding the title of Model Communist Youth League Member to naval fighter Mai Xiande.
    The decision said that Comrade Mai Xiande, a turbine mechanic and member of the Communist Youth League from a certain unit of the South Sea Fleet of the Navy, was seriously wounded in the head during the sea battle to annihilate the U.S.-made Chiang warships "Jianmen" and "Zhangjiang," but with amazing perseverance and a tenacious revolutionary will, he persisted in fighting until victory. Comrade Mai Xiande's heroic actions demonstrated the high consciousness of a proletarian revolutionary fighter and the hard-bone spirit of overwhelming all enemies. The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League has decided to award Comrade Mai Xiande the title of Model Communist Youth League Member.
    The decision pointed out that the reason why Comrade Mai Xiande was able to perform meritorious service in battle and become a good soldier of Chairman Mao was because Comrade Mai Xiande, with deep class feelings, diligently studied and applied Chairman Mao's works, strictly followed Chairman Mao's teachings to demand of himself, transform his thinking, and strive to achieve revolutionization. The decision called on the youth of the whole country to be like Comrade Mai Xiande, diligently study and apply Chairman Mao's works, raise their proletarian consciousness to the highest level, deepen their hatred for U.S. imperialism, be ready at any time to oppose U.S. imperialism from imposing war on the people of our country, strive to realize the Third Five-Year Plan, and be promoters of the Chinese revolution and the world revolution.

### 向钢铁战士麦贤得学习  全总号召全国职工学习麦贤得革命精神促进思想革命化  共青团中央决定授予麦贤得同志模范共青团员的奖状

1966-02-05
第2版()
专栏：

    向钢铁战士麦贤得学习
    全总号召全国职工学习麦贤得革命精神促进思想革命化
    共青团中央决定授予麦贤得同志模范共青团员的奖状
    新华社四日讯　中华全国总工会今天发出通知，号召全国职工向钢铁战士麦贤得同志学习。
    通知指出，麦贤得同志是毛主席的好战士，是我们职工学习的好榜样。我们每一个职工都应该认真学习麦贤得同志的革命精神，促进自己思想的革命化。通知号召全国职工，学习麦贤得，就要象他那样带着深厚的阶级感情活学活用毛主席著作，在“用”字上狠下功夫；坚决听毛主席的话，坚决按毛主席的指示办事，牢牢记住毛主席的教导，全心全意为中国人民和世界人民服务；象他那样有深刻的阶级仇恨，有高度的战备观念，随时准备消灭来犯敌人；象他那样天不怕，地不怕，压倒一切敌人，有一口气就战斗下去。
    通知说，在我国宏伟的第三个五年计划已经开始了的今天，我们更应该学习麦贤得同志的硬骨头精神，英勇顽强，不畏艰险，广泛、深入地开展比学赶帮超的群众运动，努力完成国家计划，在三大革命运动中做出更出色的贡献。
    通知要求各级工会组织，在各级党委的领导下，积极开展学习麦贤得同志的活动，通过这一活动，把学习毛主席著作的自觉的群众运动推向新的高潮，促进职工思想革命化。
    据新华社四日讯　共青团中央今天发出了关于授予海军战士麦贤得模范共青团员奖状的决定。
    决定说，海军南海舰队某部轮机兵、共青团员麦贤得同志，在歼灭美制蒋舰“剑门号”和“章江号”的海战中，头负重伤，以惊人的毅力，顽强的革命意志，坚持战斗，直到胜利。麦贤得同志的英雄行为，表现了无产阶级革命战士的高度觉悟和压倒一切敌人的硬骨头精神。共青团中央决定：授予麦贤得同志以模范共青团员的奖状。
    决定指出，麦贤得同志所以能够在战斗中建立功勋，成为毛主席的好战士，是由于麦贤得同志怀着深厚的阶级感情，活学活用毛主席著作，严格按照毛主席的教导要求自己，改造思想，努力实现革命化。决定号召全国青年一定要象麦贤得同志那样，活学活用毛主席著作，把无产阶级觉悟提得高高的，把对美帝国主义的仇恨搞得深深的，随时准备反对美帝国主义把战争强加在我国人民头上，努力实现第三个五年计划，做中国革命和世界革命的促进派。
