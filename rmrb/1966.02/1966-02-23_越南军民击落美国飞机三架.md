Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three American Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-02-23
Edition: 4

　　越南军民击落美国飞机三架
    新华社河内二十二日电　据这里二十二日公布，越南北方河静和广平两省军民十八日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架，其中，河静省军民击落F—105型喷气式飞机一架，广平省军民击落A—4型喷气式飞机一架。
    新华社河内二十二日电　据这里公布：越南北方山萝省木州县秀囊乡军民，十八日下午用步枪击落美国A—6型喷气式飞机一架。

Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three American Aircraft
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 22nd - According to an announcement here on the 22nd, the military and civilians of Ha Tinh and Quang Binh provinces in northern Vietnam shot down two invading American bandit aircraft on the 18th. Among them, the military and civilians of Ha Tinh Province shot down one F-105 jet aircraft, and the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down one A-4 jet aircraft.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 22nd - It was announced here that the military and civilians of Xiu Nang Township, Moc Chau County, Son La Province in northern Vietnam shot down one American A-6 jet aircraft with rifles on the afternoon of the 18th.
