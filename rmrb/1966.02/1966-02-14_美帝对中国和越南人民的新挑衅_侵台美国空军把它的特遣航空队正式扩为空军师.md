Title: U.S. Imperialism's New Provocation Against the Chinese and Vietnamese People: U.S. Air Force Invading Taiwan Formally Expands its Task Force into an Air Division
Author:
Date: 1966-02-14
Edition: 2

    U.S. Imperialism's New Provocation Against the Chinese and Vietnamese People
    U.S. Air Force Invading Taiwan Formally Expands its Task Force into an Air Division
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th – Taipei Dispatch: As the Johnson administration plots to expand the war of aggression against Vietnam, U.S. imperialism formally expanded its Thirteenth Task Force Air, stationed in Taiwan, into the U.S. 327th Air Division on the 8th.
    According to a report in Taiwan's *New Life Daily* on the 8th, a U.S. Air Force spokesman invading Taiwan said that the U.S. 327th Air Division, in addition to occupying the Kungkuan Air Force Base, also occupies the Taipei, Chiayi, and Tainan airports, and controls the U.S. Matador missile squadron stationed in Tainan, the Tainan Base Group, and the U.S. Air Force's 50th Transport Aircraft Squadron, which recently moved into the Kungkuan Base. According to Western news agencies from Taipei, after the U.S. Thirteenth Task Force Air was expanded into the 327th Air Division, the number of U.S. Air Force personnel invading Taiwan will increase to 5,000. Prior to this, the C-130 "Hercules" aircraft squadron of the U.S. Air Force's 6217th Support Group had already been transferred to the Kungkuan Base in Taiwan.
    U.S. news agencies have bluntly stated that as the U.S. expands its war of aggression against Vietnam, Taiwan "can replace Okinawa as a U.S. military operations base" and "serve as a staging point for U.S. troops," and that the activities of the U.S. Air Force at Taiwan bases "are in support of the Vietnam War." Taipei's *United Daily News* even declared that today's Taiwan has become the "best" transit "station" for the U.S. in the Far East to "support the Vietnam battlefield," and that the Kungkuan Base is also the "largest base closest to mainland China" for the U.S. That is to say, the U.S. not only regards Taiwan as a base for expanding its war of aggression against Vietnam, but also as a base for its planned war of aggression against mainland China.
    U.S. imperialism has long occupied our territory of Taiwan and now uses it as a base for the war of aggression against Vietnam, which is not only a blatant new provocation against the Vietnamese people but also against the Chinese people.

### 美帝对中国和越南人民的新挑衅  侵台美国空军把它的特遣航空队正式扩为空军师

1966-02-14
第2版()
专栏：

    美帝对中国和越南人民的新挑衅
    侵台美国空军把它的特遣航空队正式扩为空军师
    新华社十三日讯　台北消息：在约翰逊政府策划扩大侵越战争的时候，美帝国主义把它侵驻在台湾的第十三特遣航空队于八日正式扩大为美国第三二七空军师。
    据台湾《新生报》八日报道，侵台美国空军发言人说，美国第三二七空军师除霸占公馆空军基地外，还霸占台北、嘉义、台南机场，并且控制驻扎在台南的美国斗牛士导弹中队、台南基地大队和最近驻进公馆基地的美国空军第五十运兵机中队。另据西方通讯社从台北发出的消息说，美国第十三特遣航空队扩大为三二七空军师后，侵台美国空军人员将增加到五千人。在此以前，美国空军第六二一七支援大队的C—130“大力士”式飞机中队已经调进台湾公馆基地。
    美国通讯社曾露骨地说，美国在扩大侵越战争时，台湾“可以替代冲绳作为美军作战基地”和“充当美军的集结待运地点”，美国空军在台湾基地的活动，“是在支持越南战争”。台北市《联合报》还宣称，今日的台湾，已成为美国在远东“支援越南战场”的一个“最佳”的转运“驿站”，公馆基地也是美国“距离中国大陆最近的一个庞大基地”。这就是说，美国不仅把台湾将作为它扩大侵越战争的基地，而且是它计划中的侵略中国大陆战争的基地。
    美帝国主义长期霸占我国领土台湾，现在又把它作为侵越战争的基地，这不仅是对越南人民而且也是对中国人民明目张胆的新的挑衅。
