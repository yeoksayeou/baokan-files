Title: Where Does the Spearhead of U.S. Imperialist Aggression Point? (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-01
Edition: 4

    Where Does the Spearhead of U.S. Imperialist Aggression Point?
    This is a map published in the January 31st issue of the American magazine *Newsweek*. It exposes the United States' establishment of a series of naval and air force bases and nuclear submarine bases in the Western Pacific and Asian regions, from Japan to Thailand, forming a so-called "crescent encirclement" against China. The magazine also wrote: "Today, there are more troops stationed in the Pacific than in Europe, and the eastern edge of Asia is currently the site of the largest U.S. military buildup since World War II."

### 美帝国主义的侵略矛头指向哪里？（图片）

1966-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

    美帝国主义的侵略矛头指向哪里？
    这是一月三十一日美国《新闻周刊》上刊登的一幅地图，它暴露了美国在西太平洋和亚洲地区，从日本到泰国建立一系列海空军基地和核潜艇基地，构成对中国的所谓“新月形包围圈”。这家杂志还写道：“今天驻在太平洋的军队比驻在欧洲军队还要多，目前亚洲东部边缘是第二次世界大战以来美国进行最大一次军事集结的场所”。
