Title: Conquering Lion Mountain
Author: Zhang Xingli
Date: 1966-02-05
Edition: 6

    Conquering Lion Mountain
    Soldier Zhang Xingli
  Lion Mountain abruptly rises a thousand *li*,
  Looking up, only a sliver of sky is seen.
  The mountain wind howls, a lion's roar,
  Majestically lying among the clouds.
  The Lion's Mouth, with cliffs and precipices,
  Jagged teeth protruding from its jaws.
  A waterfall cascades down a thousand *li*,
  Challenging the soldiers.
  Heroic soldiers, with steadfast will,
  Determined to conquer Lion Mountain.
  Antenna planted on the Lion's head,
  Cable tied around the Lion's neck.
  Making our home on Lion Mountain,
  Practicing martial arts day and night.
  Leading the lion through the clouds,
  Patrolling the motherland's ninefold heavens!

### 征服狮子山
张兴礼
1966-02-05
第6版()
专栏：

    征服狮子山
    战士　张兴礼
  千里突起狮子山，
  抬头只见一线天。
  山风呼啸狮子吼，
  威风凛凛卧云间。
  悬崖峭壁狮子口，
  巉巉牙齿生嘴边。
  一泻瀑布喷千里，
  要向战士来挑战。
  英雄战士意志坚，
  决心征服狮子山。
  天线插上狮子头，
  电缆狮子颈上拴。
  狮子山上把家安，
  日日夜夜把武练。
  牵着狮子云中走，
  巡逻祖国九重天！
