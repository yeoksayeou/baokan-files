Title: Romanian Representative at Disarmament Conference Says Non-Proliferation Treaty Doesn't Meet Demands of People of All Countries; Disarmament Committee Cannot Ignore U.S. Aggression Against Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-02-16
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, February 15th: According to a report from the Romanian News Agency, the Romanian representative Dumitrescu, speaking at the Geneva Seventeen-Nation Disarmament Conference on the 10th, condemned the American aggression against Vietnam, and pointed out that the Disarmament Committee cannot ignore the American aggression.
    Referring to the issue of non-proliferation of nuclear weapons, Dumitrescu said that in order to truly serve the cause of universal security and gain the trust of people of all countries, this issue needs to become an integral part of a set of effective measures aimed at eliminating the nuclear danger and achieving universal disarmament. He said that in this regard, Romania is considering measures such as prohibiting the use of nuclear weapons and all other weapons of mass destruction, and as a first step towards this measure, all nuclear states should first guarantee not to use such weapons, destroy existing stockpiles, abolish foreign military bases, and withdraw troops from the territory of other countries.
    Dumitrescu said that the delegations of many countries at the 20th United Nations General Assembly and at this Disarmament Conference have emphasized that a non-proliferation treaty that unilaterally defines obligations only for non-nuclear states does not meet the reasonable demand of the people of every country to have their equal rights in security guaranteed.
    He expressed opposition to the North Atlantic Treaty Organization's plan to establish a multilateral nuclear force and any other similar plans.
    Dumitrescu said that the American armed aggression against Vietnam poses a serious danger to peace and security in Southeast Asia and the world.
    He reiterated the position of the Romanian Socialist Republic: supporting the four-point proposition of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the program of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, which is the only legitimate representative of the South Vietnamese people.
    The Romanian representative pointed out: "When a member state of the Eighteen-Nation Committee, a major power, engages in aggression, intensifies world tension, and expands the danger to international peace and security, thereby seriously endangering our completion of the task entrusted to us, the Eighteen-Nation Committee cannot ignore it."
    Dumitrescu said that in today's world where more and more independent countries are emerging and being consolidated, world peace and security can only be built on the basis of strict respect for the independence and sovereignty of all countries, equality, and non-interference in internal affairs. He pointed out that military bases and foreign troops in the territory of other countries are factors that threaten international peace and security and have a negative impact on relations between countries.

### 罗马尼亚代表在裁军会议上说不扩散核武器协议不符各国人民要求  裁军委员会不能对美国侵越置之不理

1966-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚代表在裁军会议上说不扩散核武器协议不符各国人民要求
    裁军委员会不能对美国侵越置之不理
    新华社十五日讯　日内瓦消息：据罗马尼亚通讯社报道，出席日内瓦十七国裁军会议的罗马尼亚代表杜米特列斯库十日在发言中谴责美国侵略越南，并且指出，裁军委员会不能对美国的侵略行为置之不理。
    在谈到不扩散核武器的问题时，杜米特列斯库说，为了真正服务于普遍安全的事业和获得各国人民的信任，需要使这个问题成为一整套旨在导向消除核危险和实现普遍裁军的有效措施的一个组成部分。他说，在这方面，罗马尼亚考虑到的措施有：禁止使用核武器和其他一切大规模杀伤武器，而作为导向这一措施的第一步则是各核国家首先保证不使用这种武器、销毁已有的储存、取消外国军事基地和撤退在别国领土上的军队。
    杜米特列斯库说，许多国家的代表团在二十届联合国大会上和在这次裁军会议上都强调指出，单方面地仅仅给非核国家规定义务的不扩散核武器协议不符合每一国人民在安全方面的平等权利得到保障的合理要求。
    他表示反对北大西洋集团建立多边核力量的计划以及任何其他同类的计划。
    杜米特列斯库说，美国武装侵略越南对东南亚和全世界的和平与安全造成了严重危险。
    他重申罗马尼亚社会主义共和国的立场：支持越南民主共和国的四项主张和越南南方民族解放阵线的纲领，越南南方民族解放阵线是南越人民唯一合法的代表。
    罗马尼亚代表指出：“当十八国委员会的一个成员国、一个大国进行侵略活动、加剧世界紧张局势、扩大对国际和平与安全的危险，从而严重地危害我们完成受委托的任务的时候，十八国委员会不能置之不理。”
    杜米特列斯库说，在愈来愈多的独立国家出现并得到巩固的今天，世界和平与安全只能建立在严格尊重各国的独立和主权、平等和不干涉内政的基础上。他指出，在别国领土上的军事基地和外国军队是威胁国际和平和安全的因素并对国家间的关系发生消极的影响。
