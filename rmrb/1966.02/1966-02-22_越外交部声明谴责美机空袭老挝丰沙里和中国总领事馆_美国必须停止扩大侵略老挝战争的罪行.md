Title: Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Statement Condemns U.S. Air Raids on Phongsaly, Laos, and the Chinese Consulate General; The U.S. Must Stop the Crime of Expanding the War of Aggression Against Laos

Author:

Date: 1966-02-22
Edition: 6

　　Hanoi, February 21st (Xinhua) – The Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement on February 21st, strongly condemning the U.S. aircraft's bombing of Phongsaly City, Laos, and the strafing of the Chinese Consulate General in Phongsaly, Laos, on February 18th.

　　The statement pointed out that, in order to salvage its disastrous defeat in South Vietnam, U.S. imperialism continues to use aircraft to bomb the Laotian liberated areas, is intensifying preparations to send U.S. troops to occupy the central and lower regions of Laos, and is plotting to expand the war to the entire Indochina. These actions and plots of the United States are being strongly condemned by the Lao people, the people of other Indochinese countries, and the progressive people of the world.

　　The statement said that recently, the United States and the pro-American faction in Laos have again used aircraft to carry out indiscriminate bombing of the Laotian liberated areas, and used infantry to launch attacks on the Laotian patriotic forces in the Bolovens Plateau and Phou Kout regions. Its bombing and strafing of Phongsaly City is a new "escalation" and a step towards expanding the war to the entire Laos. This action grossly tramples on Laos' sovereignty and the 1962 Geneva Agreements on Laos, and exposes the hypocritical deceptive rhetoric of the U.S. government.

　　The statement said that the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam strongly condemns the U.S. bombing of Phongsaly City and the Chinese Consulate General in Phongsaly, and declares that the Vientiane group must also be held responsible for the consequences arising from their following the U.S. aggressors.

　　The statement said that the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam demands that the United States and its lackeys must cease all actions to intensify and expand the war in Laos, and demands that they must strictly abide by the 1962 Geneva Agreements on Laos. The statement said that the Vietnamese people firmly believe that the fraternal Lao people will certainly be able to shatter all the conspiracies of U.S. imperialism and will certainly win a glorious victory.

### 越外交部声明谴责美机空袭老挝丰沙里和中国总领事馆　美国必须停止扩大侵略老挝战争的罪行

1966-02-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　越外交部声明谴责美机空袭老挝丰沙里和中国总领事馆
　　美国必须停止扩大侵略老挝战争的罪行
    新华社河内二十一日电　越南民主共和国外交部二十一日发表声明，严厉谴责美国飞机十八日空袭老挝丰沙里市和扫射中国驻老挝丰沙里总领事馆。
    声明指出，美帝国主义为了挽救它在越南南方的惨重失败，不断使用飞机轰炸老挝解放区，加紧准备派遣美军侵占中、下寮地区，阴谋把战争扩大到整个印度支那。美国的这些行动和阴谋，正在遭到老挝人民、印度支那其他各国人民和世界进步人民的强烈谴责。
    声明说，最近，美国和老挝的亲美派又使用飞机向老挝解放区进行狂轰滥炸，使用步兵向波罗芬和富科特山地区的老挝爱国力量发动进攻。它向丰沙里市进行轰炸、扫射是一个新的“升级”和把战争扩大到整个老挝的步骤。这一行动粗暴地践踏了老挝的主权和一九六二年关于老挝问题的日内瓦协议，揭穿了美国政府的假仁假义的欺骗论调。
    声明说，越南民主共和国外交部严厉谴责美国轰炸丰沙里市和中国驻丰沙里总领事馆，并且声明：万象集团也要对由于他们追随美国侵略者所引起的后果负责任。
    声明说，越南民主共和国外交部要求美国及其走狗必须停止一切加强和扩大老挝战争的行动，并且要求它们必须严正履行一九六二年关于老挝问题的日内瓦协议。声明说，越南人民坚信，兄弟的老挝人民一定能够粉碎美帝国主义的一切阴谋，并且一定能够赢得辉煌的胜利。
