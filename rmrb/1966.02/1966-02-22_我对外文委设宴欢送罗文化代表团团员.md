Title: The Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Hosts a Farewell Banquet for Members of the Romanian Cultural Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-22
Edition: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 20th. Li Chang, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to three members of the Romanian Cultural Delegation: Hipos, Bolega, and George. Dumitru Gheorghiu, head of the Romanian Cultural Delegation and Romanian Ambassador to China, and Petrescu, Second Secretary of the Romanian Embassy in China and a member of the delegation, were also invited to attend.
    Shi Huaibi, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, was present as a guest.

### 我对外文委设宴欢送罗文化代表团团员

1966-02-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　我对外文委设宴欢送罗文化代表团团员
    新华社二十日讯　对外文委副主任李昌今晚设宴欢送罗马尼亚文化代表团的三位团员：希波什、波列加和乔治。罗马尼亚文化代表团团长、罗驻华大使杜米特鲁·乔治乌和代表团团员、罗驻华使馆二等秘书贝特勒斯库也应邀出席。
    出席作陪的有对外文委副主任史怀璧。
