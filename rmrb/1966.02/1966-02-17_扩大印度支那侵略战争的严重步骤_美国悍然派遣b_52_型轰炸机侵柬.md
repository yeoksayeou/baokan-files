Title: A Grave Step in Escalating the War of Aggression in Indochina: The United States Brazenly Sends B-52 Bombers to Invade Cambodia

Author:
Date: 1966-02-17
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 16th, Cambodian News Agency reported on the 16th that on the morning of February 7, a US B-52 aircraft intruded into the airspace over Longthum Village, Ratanakiri Province, Cambodia, and fired a rocket at Cambodian territory before flying away.
    In addition, on the afternoon of February 1, two US-South Vietnamese puppet regime aircraft intruded into the airspace of Prey Veng Province, Cambodia. On the morning of February 3, two US-South Vietnamese puppet regime aircraft again intruded into the airspace of Prey Veng Province. On the morning of January 30, about a platoon of US-South Vietnamese puppet troops fired from South Vietnamese territory at the Cambodian provincial defense forces at the Bien Bei outpost in Takeo Province, who were patrolling along the border. The Cambodian provincial defense forces fiercely returned fire, forcing the enemy troops to retreat.
    The news agency also reported on the 16th that at 5:30 on the 14th, Thai troops stationed in Harle, Thailand, again fired automatic weapons from Thai territory at Cambodian facilities in Chi Ne Sa, Koh Kong Province. That afternoon, Thai troops also shelled Cambodian territory from the Harle outpost. The Cambodian side retaliated with heavy weapons. The exchange of fire lasted for about thirty minutes.

### 扩大印度支那侵略战争的严重步骤  美国悍然派遣B—52型轰炸机侵柬

1966-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    扩大印度支那侵略战争的严重步骤
    美国悍然派遣B—52型轰炸机侵柬
    据新华社金边十六日电  据柬埔寨新闻社十六日报道，二月七日上午，一架美国B—52型飞机侵入柬埔寨腊塔纳基里省隆通村上空，并且在飞走以前向柬埔寨领土发射了一枚火箭。
    此外，二月一日下午，两架美国—南越伪军飞机侵入柬埔寨波萝勉省领空。二月三日上午，两架美国—南越伪军的飞机再次侵入波萝勉省上空。一月三十日上午，美国—南越伪军约一个排曾经从南越境内向正在沿边境进行巡逻的茶胶省卞贝哨所的柬埔寨省防军进行射击，柬埔寨省防军猛烈地予以还击，迫使敌军后退。
    这家新闻社十六日还报道，十四日五时三十分，驻扎在泰国哈勒地方的泰国军队再次从泰国境内用自动武器射击戈公省奇内萨地方的柬埔寨设施。这天下午，泰国军队还从哈勒哨所炮击柬埔寨领土。柬埔寨方面用重武器进行了还击。双方交火达三十分钟左右。
