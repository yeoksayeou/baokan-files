Title: Four Journalists from Congo (Brazzaville) Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-05
Edition: 4

    Four journalists from Congo (Brazzaville), led by Kaman Kay, administrative secretary of the 《Independent Weekly》 of Congo (Brazzaville), arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association for a visit to our country.



**Named Entities**


刚果（布）	Gāngguǒ (Bù) - Congo (Brazzaville)
卡曼凯	Kǎmànkǎi - Kaman Kay
《独立周刊》	《Dúlì Zhōukān》 - 《Independent Weekly》

北京	Běijīng - Beijing

### 刚果（布）四位新闻工作者到京

1966-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）四位新闻工作者到京
    新华社三日讯　以刚果（布）《独立周刊》行政书记卡曼凯为首的刚果（布）新闻工作者一行四人，应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。
