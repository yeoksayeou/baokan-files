Title: United Arab Republic and United States Sign New Grain Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-19
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 17th – According to a report in *Al-Ahram*, the United Arab Republic (UAR) and the United States signed a new agreement in Washington on the 16th, under which the United States will provide the UAR with wheat and grain worth 13 million Egyptian pounds.

    According to the agreement, the UAR will repay the loan in local currency in installments over a period of thirty years, with an annual interest rate of 4.5 percent. The first repayment will be made three years after the signing of the agreement.

    This is the second wheat and grain agreement signed between the two countries this year. The first agreement was signed in January, under which the United States will provide the UAR with wheat and grain worth 55,689,000 US dollars in the first half of this year.

### 阿联美国签订新的粮食协定

1966-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联美国签订新的粮食协定
    新华社开罗十七日电　据《金字塔报》报道，阿联和美国十六日在华盛顿签订了一项由美国向阿联提供价值一千三百万埃镑的小麦和粮食的新协定。
    根据这项协定，阿联将在三十年的期限内用当地货币分期偿付这笔粮款，年利为百分之四点五，第一次偿付是在协定签字三年之后。
    这是今年来两国之间签订的第二个小麦和粮食协定。第一个协定是在一月份签订的，根据第一个协定，美国今年上半年将向阿联提供价值五千五百六十八万九千美元的小麦和粮食。
