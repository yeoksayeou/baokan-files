Title: Chinese Goods Exhibited and Sold in Some French Cities
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-18
Edition: 4

    Chinese goods have recently been exhibited and sold in some French cities, attracting tens of thousands of viewers and customers.
    The Chinese goods exhibitions held in two large department stores in Paris, "Printemps" and "BHV," were relatively large in scale, with areas of over 1,000 square meters each. Song Zhiguang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in France, and French Secretary of State for Foreign Trade de Chambrun attended the opening ceremony of the Chinese goods exhibition and sale at "Printemps."
    In Nice, a city on the Mediterranean coast, the Galeries Lafayette department store is also exhibiting and selling Chinese goods.
    A Chinese art exhibition was recently held in Dijon, an important industrial city in central France.

### 法国一些城市展出和出售中国商品

1966-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    法国一些城市展出和出售中国商品
    据新华社巴黎电　法国的一些城市最近展出并且出售中国商品，吸引了成千上万的观众和顾客。
    巴黎的两家大商店——“春天商店”和“市府商店”举办的中国商品展出的规模都比较大，面积都在一千平方米以上。中国驻法使馆临时代办宋之光和法国对外贸易国务秘书德香布兰参加了“春天商店”的中国商品展出和出售开幕式。
    在地中海海滨城市尼斯，拉斐特百货商店也正在展出和出售中国商品。
    在法国中部重要工业城市第戎最近举办了中国艺术作品展览会。
