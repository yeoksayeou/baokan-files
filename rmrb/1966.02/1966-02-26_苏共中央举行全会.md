Title: CPSU Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1966-02-26
Edition: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 24th -- TASS reported that the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held a meeting in Moscow on the 19th.
    The meeting heard and discussed the report by Chairman of the USSR Council of Ministers Kosygin on the draft directives of the 23rd Congress of the CPSU concerning the five-year plan for the development of the Soviet national economy from 1966 to 1970. First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee Brezhnev spoke at the meeting.
    The meeting passed a corresponding resolution.

### 苏共中央举行全会

1966-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏共中央举行全会
    新华社二十四日讯　据塔斯社报道，苏联共产党中央委员会十九日在莫斯科举行了会议。
    会议听取并讨论了苏联部长会议主席柯西金作的苏共第二十三次代表大会关于一九六六年到一九七○年发展苏联国民经济五年计划的指示草案的报告。苏共中央第一书记勃列日涅夫在会上讲了话。
    会议通过了一项相应的决议。
