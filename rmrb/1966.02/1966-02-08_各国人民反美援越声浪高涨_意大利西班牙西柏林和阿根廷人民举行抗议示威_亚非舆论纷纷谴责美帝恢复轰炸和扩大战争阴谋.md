Title: People in Various Countries Vigorously Support Vietnam and Oppose the U.S.; People in Italy, Spain, West Berlin, and Argentina Hold Protests; Asian and African Public Opinion Condemns U.S. Imperialism's Conspiracy to Resume Bombing and Expand the War

Author:
Date: 1966-02-08
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency reports: Just as Johnson was personally in Honolulu plotting to expand the war of aggression against Vietnam, people in many countries continued to hold demonstrations demanding the immediate withdrawal of U.S. troops from South Vietnam; public opinion in various countries continued to strongly condemn the Johnson administration's war crimes.
    Masses in West Berlin demonstrated in front of the U.S. Embassy and Information Office on the 4th and 5th consecutively, chanting slogans such as "Americans Get Out of Vietnam".
    Many places in Italy have held demonstrations for days to protest the U.S. resuming the bombing of Vietnam.
    More than two thousand people in Spain demonstrated in front of the U.S. Embassy on the evening of the 4th to protest the U.S. resuming the bombing of Vietnam.
    Several thousand citizens of the Argentine capital demonstrated in front of the U.S. Embassy on the evening of the 4th, shouting anti-U.S. slogans and attacking the U.S. Embassy with stones.
    Cambodia's state radio station issued a commentary on the 4th, condemning U.S. imperialism's attempt to create a situation similar to the Korean War, expand the war of aggression against Vietnam, and strengthen its military deployment in Asia to prepare to expand the aggression to other parts of the Asian continent.
    The Pathet Lao Radio of Laos issued a commentary on the 4th, saying that the U.S. imperialists' resumption of bombing North Vietnam completely tore up the Johnson administration's false peace banner.
    Japanese Communist Party Senator Masao Iwama demanded at a meeting of the Budget Committee of the House of Councillors on the 7th that the Sato Cabinet immediately stop helping U.S. imperialism's aggression against Vietnam.
    Wei Yi, a representative of the South West Africa National Union, issued a statement on the 6th, saying: The people of South West Africa sternly condemn the United States for resuming the wanton bombing of North Vietnam and submitting the Vietnam issue to the United Nations. He declared that the people of South West Africa wholeheartedly support the struggle of the Vietnamese people.

### 各国人民反美援越声浪高涨  意大利西班牙西柏林和阿根廷人民举行抗议示威  亚非舆论纷纷谴责美帝恢复轰炸和扩大战争阴谋

1966-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    各国人民反美援越声浪高涨
    意大利西班牙西柏林和阿根廷人民举行抗议示威
    亚非舆论纷纷谴责美帝恢复轰炸和扩大战争阴谋
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：正当约翰逊亲自去檀香山策划扩大侵越战争的时候，许多国家的人民继续举行示威，要美军立即撤出南越；各国公众舆论继续强烈谴责约翰逊政府的战争罪行。
    西柏林群众四日和五日连续在美国大使馆和新闻处前示威并高呼：“美国人滚出越南”等口号。
    意大利许多地方连日举行示威，抗议美国恢复轰炸越南。
    西班牙两千多人四日晚在美国大使馆前示威抗议美国恢复轰炸越南。
    阿根廷首都数千市民四日晚在美国大使馆前示威，高呼反美口号，用石头袭击美国大使馆。
    柬埔寨国家电台四日发表评论，谴责美帝国主义企图制造一种同朝鲜战争相似的局势，扩大侵越战争，并正在增强它在亚洲的军事部署准备把侵略扩大到亚洲大陆其他地区。
    老挝巴特寮电台四日发表评论说，美帝恢复轰炸越南北方，彻底撕毁了约翰逊政府的假和平招牌。
    日共参议员岩间正男七日在参院决算委员会会议上要求佐藤内阁立即停止帮助美帝国主义侵略越南。
    西南非洲民族联盟代表维伊六日发表声明说：西南非洲人民严厉谴责美国重新滥炸越南北方和把越南问题提交联合国。他宣布，西南非洲人民全心全意支持越南人民斗争。
