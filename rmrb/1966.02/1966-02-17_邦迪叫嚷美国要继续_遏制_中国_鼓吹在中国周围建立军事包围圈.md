Title: Bundy Clamors that the US Should Continue to "Contain" China, Advocates Building a Military Circle of Encirclement Around China
Author:
Date: 1966-02-17
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch, Washington: William Bundy, the U.S. Assistant Secretary of State for Far Eastern Affairs, clamored at Pomona College in California on the 12th that the United States must continue to contain China and stated that it would intensify its coercion of Asian countries to join the United States in establishing a long-term military encirclement of China.
    Bundy said that the United States and Asian countries have a full "obligation" to commit to the policy of "containing" China. He said that the United States is striving to promote this policy through security alliance treaties, bilateral treaties, and military and economic assistance. He said, "To implement containment through action, that is the essence of what we are striving to do."
    Bundy had to admit that this approach by the United States is facing "enormous difficulties at present." He clamored that "a long-term presence of American troops is needed" to implement American military control over these countries. He clamored that "around the border of China," "the United States should provide the main and direct military force." At the same time, he also advocated the policy of using Asians to fight Asians, hoping that "one day," Asian countries "can take on more of (the United States') burden."
    Bundy妄想 to promote a policy of peaceful evolution in China, pinning his hopes on China's "next generation." He said, "It is unlikely to expect the current dogmatic leaders to change. But at a certain time, they will be replaced by a new generation of leaders."
    The U.S. ruling group is currently shifting their strategic focus from Europe to Asia, directing the spearhead of aggression and war against China. According to the U.S. Washington Post, the Far East and Pacific Affairs Subcommittee of the U.S. House Foreign Affairs Committee has been holding hearings for the past two weeks "in order to re-estimate the fundamental questions for the United States in Asia – Communist China and its rising power – policy." The Washington Post believes that this is a symbol of increasing official "concern" about China policy in the United States.

### 邦迪叫嚷美国要继续“遏制”中国  鼓吹在中国周围建立军事包围圈

1966-02-17
第3版()
专栏：

    邦迪叫嚷美国要继续“遏制”中国
    鼓吹在中国周围建立军事包围圈
    新华社讯　华盛顿消息：美国负责远东事务的助理国务卿威廉·邦迪十二日在加利福尼亚波莫纳学院叫嚷，美国要继续遏制中国，并且表示要加紧胁迫亚洲国家和美国一起建立长期对中国的军事包围圈。
    邦迪说，美国和亚洲国家有充分的“义务”致力于“遏制”中国的政策。他说，美国努力通过安全同盟条约、双边条约和军事、经济援助等办法来推行这一政策。他说，“用行动来实行遏制，这就是我们正在努力做的事情的本质”。
    邦迪不得不承认，美国的这一作法在“目前存在着巨大的困难”，他叫嚷“需要美国军队长期驻留”以实行美国对这些国家的军事控制。他叫嚣说，“围绕中国的边境”，要“美国提供主要的和直接的军事力量”。同时，他还鼓吹用亚洲人打亚洲人的政策，希望“有那么一天”，亚洲国家“能更多地挑起（美国的）这个担子”。
    邦迪妄想在中国推行和平演变政策，把希望寄托于中国的“下一代”。他说，“要指望现在的那些教条主义的领导人起变化，是不大可能的。但是在一定的时候，他们将会被新的一代的领袖们所代替”。
    美国统治集团目前正在把他们的战略重点从欧洲转到亚洲，把侵略和战争的矛头针对着中国。据美国《华盛顿邮报》报道，两周来美国众议院外交委员会远东和太平洋事务小组委员会一直在举行意见听取会，“以便重新估计对美国在亚洲的根本问题——共产党中国及其正在兴起的力量——的政策”。《华盛顿邮报》认为，这是美国官方对于对华政策越来越“关心”的象征。
