Title: Guinean Ambassador to China Departs for Home After Completion of Term

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-17
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 16th — The Ambassador of the Republic of Guinea to China, Camara Mamadi, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after being recalled at the end of his term.

### 几内亚驻中国大使离任回国

1966-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    几内亚驻中国大使离任回国
    新华社十六日讯　几内亚共和国驻中国大使卡马拉·马马迪奉调离任，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
