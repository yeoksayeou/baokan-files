    “May Second Movement”
    The "May Second Movement" is an anti-imperialist student organization in the United States, founded in 1964. On May 2nd of that year, hundreds of American university students held a gathering in New York to oppose the US government's armed aggression against South Vietnam, demanding that US troops withdraw from South Vietnam immediately. The "May Second Movement" got its name from this event. This organization is based on university students and has more than twenty branches in the United States. It conducts struggles against the US aggression against Vietnam in various ways, and advocates supporting national liberation movements in various countries.

### “五月二日运动”

1966-02-07
第4版()
专栏：资料

    “五月二日运动”
    “五月二日运动”是美国的一个反帝学生组织，成立于一九六四年。在这一年的五月二日，几百名美国大学生在纽约举行集会，反对美国政府武装侵略南越，要求美国军队立即从南越撤出，“五月二日运动”因此而得名。这个组织以大学生为基础，在美国已有二十多个分会。它通过各种方式进行反对美国侵略越南的斗争，并且主张支持各国民族解放运动。
