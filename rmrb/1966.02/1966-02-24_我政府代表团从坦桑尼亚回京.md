Author:
Date: 1966-02-24
Edition: 4

　　Our Government Delegation Returns to Beijing from Tanzania
　　Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd – The People's Republic of China government delegation, led by Xu Jie, Vice Minister of Geology, returned to Beijing by plane today after attending the celebrations for the second anniversary of the Zanzibar Revolution at the invitation of the Tanzanian government. Those who greeted them at the airport included Ji Pengfei, Xu Guangyi, Zhang Shuyi, Wen Chi, and other responsible persons from relevant departments. Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Wazir Juma, also greeted them at the airport.

### 我政府代表团从坦桑尼亚回京

1966-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　我政府代表团从坦桑尼亚回京
    新华社二十三日讯　由地质部副部长许杰率领的中华人民共和国政府代表团，应坦桑尼亚政府邀请参加了桑给巴尔革命胜利两周年庆祝活动后，今天乘飞机回到北京。到机场迎接的，有姬鹏飞、胥光义、张淑意、文迟等有关方面负责人。坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马也到机场迎接。
