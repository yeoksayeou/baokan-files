Title: Resolutely Counter the "Mopping-up" by the Pseudo-Army Supported by the United States: Congolese (L) Patriotic Armed Forces Night-raid Stanleyville Airport
Author:
Date: 1966-02-07
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th Dispatch from Léopoldville: The patriotic armed forces in northeastern Congo (L) attacked Stanleyville Airport on the night of the 2nd, engaging in fierce fighting with the white mercenaries and Mobutu's pseudo-army guarding the airport.
    The patriotic armed forces simultaneously attacked the city of Stanleyville, successfully penetrating the African quarter guarded by the pseudo-army.
    According to travelers arriving in Léopoldville from Stanleyville on the 4th, gunfire could be heard throughout the night in Stanleyville on the 2nd. They also said that Congolese (L) patriots distributed Lumumba's photos and pamphlets in the Stanleyville area during the day on the 2nd.
    It is reported that this attack on Stanleyville by the patriotic armed forces was a blow to the "mopping-up" operations of Mobutu's pseudo-army. Currently, Mobutu's pseudo-army, with the support of the United States, is conducting "mopping-up" operations against the patriotic armed forces south of Stanleyville, and distributing leaflets in an attempt to lure the patriotic armed forces to lay down their weapons. However, the patriotic armed forces responded to the enemy's conspiracy with resolute actions.

### 坚决反击在美国支持下的伪军“扫荡”  刚果（利）爱国武装夜袭斯坦利维尔机场

1966-02-07
第3版()
专栏：

    坚决反击在美国支持下的伪军“扫荡”
    刚果（利）爱国武装夜袭斯坦利维尔机场
    新华社五日讯　利奥波德维尔消息：刚果（利）东北部爱国武装二日夜间袭击了斯坦利维尔机场，同守卫机场的白人雇佣军和蒙博托伪军进行了激战。
    爱国武装同时袭击了斯坦利维尔市区，成功地攻入了由伪军驻守的非洲人区。
    据四日从斯坦利维尔到达利奥波德维尔的旅客说，二日晚上，斯坦利维尔城里通宵可听到枪声。他们还说，刚果
  （利）爱国者二日白天就在斯坦利维尔地区散发卢蒙巴的照片和小册子。
    据报道，这次爱国武装袭击斯坦利维尔是对蒙博托伪军的“扫荡”活动的一次打击。目前，蒙博托伪军在美国的支持下，正在斯坦利维尔以南地区对爱国武装进行“扫荡”，并且散发传单，妄图诱骗爱国武装放下武器。但是，爱国武装以坚决的行动回答了敌人的阴谋。
