Title: Patriotic Armed Forces of Congo (Leopoldville) Strengthen Combat on All Fronts; Puppet Troops in Panic; Mobutu and Others Rush to the East to Plan New Suppression

Author:
Date: 1966-02-02
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 31st Dispatch from Leopoldville: The patriotic armed forces of Congo (Leopoldville) have recently intensified their activities in all war zones, striking at Mobutu's puppet troops.
    According to reports, in the western war zone, the patriotic armed forces led by Mulele, disregarding the puppet troops' frantic "sweeping," have recently stepped up attacks on puppet troop strongholds in the Kwilu region and have established new bases in the Idiofa area there. The patriotic armed forces have destroyed many enemy transportation lines to impede enemy troop movements. The administrative head of the Mobutu clique in the Kwilu region, Takizala, admitted on the 28th that in this area, "most of the bridges and crossings are unusable."
    Under the constant attacks of the patriotic armed forces, the puppet troops in the Kwilu region are in a state of panic. News reports indicate that the puppet troops in Kikwit, the administrative center of the Kwilu region, have imposed a curfew. Other news reports state that Mobutu, the "commander-in-chief" of the Congolese (Leopoldville) puppet army, and Mulamba, the "Prime Minister" of the puppet regime, recently rushed to Kikwit to plan new suppressions.
    In the eastern war zone, the patriotic armed forces are fighting puppet troops commanded by white mercenaries in the Albertville area, and have killed some mercenaries in the fighting.
    In the northeastern war zone, the patriotic armed forces are actively carrying out activities to strike at the puppet troops in the Stanleyville area and some other areas in the northeast.

### 刚果（利）爱国武装在各线加强战斗  伪军惊慌万状蒙博托等赶往东部策划新的镇压

1966-02-02
第5版()
专栏：

    刚果（利）爱国武装在各线加强战斗
    伪军惊慌万状蒙博托等赶往东部策划新的镇压
    新华社三十一日讯　利奥波德维尔消息：刚果（利）爱国武装力量最近在各个战区加强活动，打击蒙博托伪军。
    据报道，在西部战区，由缪勒尔领导的爱国武装不顾伪军的疯狂“扫荡”，最近在克韦卢地区加紧袭击伪军据点，并在那里的伊迪奥法地区建立了新的基地。爱国武装破坏了敌人许多交通线，以阻挠敌军调动。蒙博托集团在克韦卢地区的行政负责人塔基扎拉二十八日承认，在这一地区，“大多数的桥梁和渡口都不能用了”。
    在爱国武装的不断打击下，克韦卢地区的伪军惊慌万状。消息说，在克韦卢地区行政中心基克韦特的伪军已在那里实行了宵禁。另有消息说，刚果（利）伪军“总司令”蒙博托和傀儡政权“总理”穆朗巴最近赶往基克韦特策划新的镇压。
    在东部战区，爱国武装部队正在阿伯特维尔地区同白人雇佣军指挥下的伪军作战，并在战斗中打死了一些雇佣军。
    在东北部战区，爱国武装在斯坦利维尔地区和东北部其他一些地区正积极展开打击伪军的活动。
