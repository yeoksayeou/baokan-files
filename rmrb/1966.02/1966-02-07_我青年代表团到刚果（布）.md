Title: Our Youth Delegation Arrives in Congo (Brazzaville)
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-07
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville, February 5th: The Chinese youth delegation to Africa, led by Hu Qili, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, arrived here on the evening of the 5th to participate in the celebration activities of the Congo (Brazzaville) Youth Festival. At the airport, they were warmly welcomed by Matoumpa-Mpolo, Executive Secretary of the Youth Organization of the National Revolution Movement of the Congo, Treasurer Boudesoumou Antoine, and other leaders.
    Li Chenguang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Congo (Brazzaville), was also at the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 我青年代表团到刚果（布）

1966-02-07
第3版()
专栏：

    我青年代表团到刚果（布）
    据新华社布拉柴维尔五日电　由中华全国青年联合会副主席胡启立率领的中国青年访非代表团一行七人五日晚上到达这里，参加刚果
  （布）青年联欢节的庆祝活动。在机场上，他们受到刚果全国革命运动青年组织执行书记马图姆帕·波洛，司库布德苏木·安托万和其他领导人的热烈欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的，还有中国驻刚果（布）临时代办李琛光。
