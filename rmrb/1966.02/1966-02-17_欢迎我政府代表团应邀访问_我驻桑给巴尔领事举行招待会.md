Title: Reception Held by Our Consul in Zanzibar to Welcome Our Government Delegation's Visit Upon Invitation
Author:
Date: 1966-02-17
Edition: 4

    Welcoming Our Government Delegation's Visit Upon Invitation
    Reception Held by Our Consul in Zanzibar to Welcome Our Government Delegation
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Zanzibar, February 15th:  Chinese Consul in Zanzibar, Liu Gan, held a reception here on the evening of the 15th to welcome the Chinese government delegation's visit to Zanzibar upon invitation.
    Tanzania's First Vice President Karume and his wife attended the reception.
    Consuls from various countries in Zanzibar also attended the reception.
    Xu Jie, the head of the Chinese government delegation and Vice Minister of Geology, toasted at the reception. He hoped that the friendship between the people of China and Tanzania would be further strengthened in the struggle against imperialism and colonialism.
    In his speech, Karume expressed his belief that "the long-standing friendship between Tanzania and China will continue to develop." He praised the assistance provided by the Chinese government as "the assistance we need most." He said that the Chinese experts in Zanzibar are doing an excellent job, demonstrating excellent moral qualities, which is the result of the teachings of Chinese leaders.
    A Chinese color documentary about Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Tanzania was screened at the reception and was warmly welcomed.

### 欢迎我政府代表团应邀访问  我驻桑给巴尔领事举行招待会

1966-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    欢迎我政府代表团应邀访问
    我驻桑给巴尔领事举行招待会
    据新华社桑给巴尔十五日电　中国驻桑给巴尔领事刘干十五日晚上在这里举行招待会，欢迎中国政府代表团应邀访问桑给巴尔。
    坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆和夫人出席了招待会。
    各国驻桑给巴尔领事也出席了招待会。
    中国政府代表团团长、地质部副部长许杰在招待会上祝酒。他希望中国和坦桑尼亚两国人民之间的友谊在反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争中进一步得到加强。
    卡鲁姆在讲话中表示相信：“坦桑尼亚和中国之间的悠久友谊将继续发展。”他赞扬中国政府所提供的援助是“我们最需要的援助”。他说，在桑给巴尔的中国专家工作很出色，表现了优秀的道德品质，这是中国领导人教导的结果。
    招待会上放映了周恩来总理访问坦桑尼亚的中国彩色纪录片，受到热烈的欢迎。
