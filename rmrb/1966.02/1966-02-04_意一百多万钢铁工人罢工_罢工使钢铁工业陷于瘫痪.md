Title: Italian Steelworkers' Strike Involves Over One Million; Strike Paralyzes Steel Industry
Author:
Date: 1966-02-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd, Rome Dispatch: 1.2 million Italian steel and machine manufacturing workers held a 24-hour nationwide strike on the 1st, protesting the refusal of capitalists and the government to meet their demands for re-negotiating and revising collective contracts, fully guaranteeing their union rights, and shortening working hours.
    The strike paralyzed Italy's steel industry.
    Among the strikers were 200,000 workers from state-owned enterprises, who held a nationwide strike on January 18th with the same demands.

### 意一百多万钢铁工人罢工  罢工使钢铁工业陷于瘫痪

1966-02-04
第4版()
专栏：

    意一百多万钢铁工人罢工
    罢工使钢铁工业陷于瘫痪
    新华社二日讯　罗马消息：意大利一百二十万钢铁和机器制造工人，一日举行了二十四小时的全国性罢工，抗议资方和政府拒绝他们提出的重订和修改集体合同、充分保障他们的工会权利和缩短工作时间的要求。
    这次罢工使意大利的钢铁工业陷于瘫痪。
    罢工者中包括二十万国营企业的工人，他们在一月十八日曾举行全国性的罢工，提出过同样的要求。
