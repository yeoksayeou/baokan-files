Title: Spokesman of the Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Issues Statement on the Vietnamese Chargé d'Affaires in the Soviet Union Receiving a Representative of the British Government, Accusing Wilson of Exonerating the U.S. Imperialists' Crimes of Aggression Against Vietnam; Vietnamese Chargé d'Affaires in the Soviet Union Condemns British Support for U.S. Policy of Aggression Against Vietnam When Receiving a Representative of the British Government
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-27
Edition: 4

　　Hanoi, February 25 (Xinhua) – Vietnam News Agency reported on February 25: The spokesman of the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued the following statement on February 25 regarding the Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the Soviet Union receiving a representative of the British government:
    On February 17, 1966, the British Ambassador to the Soviet Union paid a visit to the Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the Soviet Union, suggesting that during the visit of British Prime Minister Wilson to Moscow, representatives of the British government be given an opportunity to meet with representatives of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in order to further understand the position of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the Vietnam issue. The Chargé d'Affaires of the Vietnamese Embassy in the Soviet Union agreed to receive the British government representative.
    On February 23, 1966, Lord Chalfont, Minister of State for Disarmament Affairs and a member of the British government delegation visiting the Soviet Union, paid a visit to the Vietnamese Chargé d'Affaires at the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Chalfont, in the capacity of representative of Prime Minister Wilson, presented some views of the British government on the Vietnam issue.
    The Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the Soviet Union strongly condemned U.S. imperialism for seriously undermining the 1954 Geneva Agreements on Vietnam, carrying out armed aggression against South Vietnam, and conducting airstrikes against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, creating an extremely grave situation in Indochina and Asia.
    The Chargé d'Affaires of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam exposed the deception of the United States' so-called "unconditional discussions." He clearly stated that the four points of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam are a peaceful stance, are fully in line with the 1954 Geneva Agreements on Vietnam, and emphasized that the South Vietnam National Liberation Front is the sole and genuine representative of the people of South Vietnam.
    The Chargé d'Affaires of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam severely criticized the British government for not fulfilling its responsibility as one of the two chairmen of the 1954 Geneva Conference on Indochina and for openly supporting the U.S. policy of aggression against Vietnam. The views of the British government as expounded by Chalfont are fundamentally consistent with the views of the U.S. government.
    The above is a brief introduction to the meeting between the Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the Soviet Union and Chalfont. Any reports issued by Western news agencies that are inconsistent with the above are distortions of the facts.
    Regrettably, Prime Minister Wilson, in a television interview, said, when discussing the above meeting, that Hanoi holds the "key to peace" in Vietnam. This statement is a deliberate attempt to mislead public opinion that the current war of aggression against Vietnam was not launched by U.S. imperialism.
    As everyone knows, U.S. imperialism is the aggressor. Therefore, the path to achieving peace in Vietnam is that U.S. imperialism must stop its aggression, withdraw all U.S. and satellite troops from South Vietnam, and strictly implement the 1954 Geneva Agreements on Vietnam.

### 越南外交部发言人就越驻苏临时代办接见英政府代表事发表声明　指责威尔逊为美帝侵越战争开脱罪责　越南驻苏临时代办接见英政府代表时谴责英国支持美国侵越政策

1966-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南外交部发言人就越驻苏临时代办接见英政府代表事发表声明
　　指责威尔逊为美帝侵越战争开脱罪责
　　越南驻苏临时代办接见英政府代表时谴责英国支持美国侵越政策
    新华社河内二十五日电  越南通讯社二十五日讯：越南民主共和国外交部发言人二十五日就越南民主共和国驻苏联大使馆临时代办接见英国政府代表一事发表声明如下：
    一九六六年二月十七日，英国驻苏联大使拜会了越南民主共和国驻苏联大使馆临时代办，建议在英国首相威尔逊访问莫斯科期间，让英国政府代表有机会会见越南民主共和国政府代表，以便进一步了解越南民主共和国政府关于越南问题的立场。越南驻苏联大使馆临时代办同意接见英国政府代表。
    一九六六年二月二十三日，英国政府协办裁军事务国务大臣、英国政府访苏代表团团员查尔方特到越南民主共和国大使馆拜会了越南临时代办。查尔方特以威尔逊首相代表的身份提出了英国政府有关越南问题的一些意见。
    越南民主共和国驻苏联大使馆临时代办强烈谴责美帝国主义严重破坏一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议，对越南南方进行武装侵略和对越南民主共和国进行空袭，在印度支那和亚洲造成极其严重的局势。
    越南民主共和国临时代办揭露了美国的所谓“无条件讨论”的欺骗性。他清楚地说明了越南民主共和国政府的四项主张是和平的立场，是完全符合一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议的，并且强调指出，越南南方民族解放阵线是越南南方人民的唯一的真正代表。
    越南民主共和国临时代办严厉批评英国政府没有履行它作为一九五四年关于印度支那问题的日内瓦会议两主席之一的职责，而且还公然支持美国侵略越南的政策。由查尔方特所阐述的英国政府的意见同美国政府的观点基本上是一致的。
    以上就是关于越南民主共和国驻苏联大使馆临时代办接见查尔方特一事的简单介绍。西方通讯社所发的同上述的内容不符的任何报道都是歪曲事实的。
    令人遗憾的是，威尔逊首相在一次电视谈话中谈到上述会晤时说，河内掌握着越南“和平的钥匙”。这种说法是蓄意使舆论误解目前对越南的侵略战争不是美帝国主义发动的。
    众所周知，美帝国主义是侵略者，因此，实现越南和平的途径是，美帝国主义必须停止侵略，从越南南方撤走全部美国及其仆从国的军队，必须严格履行一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议。
