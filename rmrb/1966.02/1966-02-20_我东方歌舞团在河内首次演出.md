Title: China Oriental Song and Dance Troupe Gives First Performance in Hanoi

Author:

Date: 1966-02-20
Edition: 4

    Our Oriental Song and Dance Troupe Gives First Performance in Hanoi
    (Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 19th) The China Oriental Song and Dance Troupe gave its first performance at the Hanoi Grand Theater on the evening of the 18th. These literary and artistic fighters from Beijing, filled with militant friendship, sang and danced enthusiastically, expressing the Chinese people's high respect and brotherly greetings to the heroic Vietnamese people fighting on the front lines of the anti-American struggle.
    Pham Hung, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and Deputy Prime Minister of the government, Hoang Quoc Viet, President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, Ton Quang Phiet, Vice President, Ha Huy Giap, Deputy Minister of Culture, Vu Quoc Uy, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and heads of various ministries and mass organizations attended the performance.
    Chen Liang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, Du Zhanchao, Cultural Counselor, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam also watched the performance. An opening ceremony was held before the performance. Phan Huy Le, Deputy Director of the Music and Dance Department of the Vietnamese Ministry of Culture, and Zhang Xing, Head of the China Oriental Song and Dance Troupe, spoke, warmly praising the great friendship and militant solidarity between the Chinese and Vietnamese people. Zhang Xing said that the China Oriental Song and Dance Troupe's visit and performance in Vietnam is to learn from the heroic Vietnamese people, and to use literary and artistic weapons to fight alongside the Vietnamese people against our common enemy, US imperialism.
    The performance opened with the militant poem "Ode to Heroic Vietnam," and every program was warmly welcomed by the audience. In particular, the performance of the segment "Smashing Strategic Hamlets" from the large-scale dance "Fury in the Coconut Grove," which eulogizes the heroic Vietnamese people's anti-American struggle and is already familiar to the broad masses of Vietnam, was even more warmly welcomed by the audience.
    At the end of the performance, the Chinese literary and artistic fighters sang "Liberate the South." At this time, the stage and audience shouted in Chinese and Vietnamese: "Long live the great militant friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese people!"
    Deputy Prime Minister Pham Hung and others, accompanied by Chargé d'affaires Chen Liang, went on stage to meet all the actors of the song and dance troupe. Vietnamese literary and artistic workers presented flower baskets to their Chinese comrades.

### 我东方歌舞团在河内首次演出

1966-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    我东方歌舞团在河内首次演出
    新华社河内十九日电　中国东方歌舞团十八日晚上在河内大戏院举行了首次演出。这些来自北京的文艺战士们，满怀着战斗友情，高歌曼舞，热情地向战斗在反美斗争前线的英雄的越南人民，表达了中国人民的崇高敬意和兄弟般的问候。
    越南劳动党中央政治局委员、政府副总理范雄，越中友协会长黄国越、副会长孙光阀，文化部副部长何辉甲，对外文化联络委员会副主任武国威，以及各部和各群众团体的负责人出席观看了演出。
    中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陈亮、文化参赞杜展潮以及各国驻越南的外交使节也观看了演出。演出前举行了开幕式。越南文化部音乐舞蹈司副司长潘辉黎和中国东方歌舞团团长张星分别讲了话，他们都热烈赞颂中越两国人民的伟大友谊和战斗团结。张星说，中国东方歌舞团这次到越南访问演出，就是向英雄的越南人民学习，就是要用文艺的武器和越南人民并肩一起，同我们共同的敌人美帝国主义进行战斗。
    演出以《歌颂英雄的越南》的战斗诗篇揭开序幕，每一个节目都受到观众的热情欢迎。特别是已为越南广大群众所熟悉的歌颂英雄越南人民反美斗争史诗的大型舞蹈《椰林怒火》中的片断——“捣毁战略村”的演出，更受到了观众们的热烈欢迎。
    演出结束时，中国文艺战士们高唱《解放南方》之歌。这时，台上台下用中越两国语言齐声高呼：“中越两国人民伟大的战斗友谊万岁！”
    范雄副总理等在陈亮代办的陪同下登上舞台，接见歌舞团全体演员。越南文艺工作者向中国战友们献了花篮。
