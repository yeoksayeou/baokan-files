Title: Joyful Spring Snow
Author: Qu Hui Jin, Wei Ping
Date: 1966-02-23
Edition: 4

　　Joyful Spring Snow
　　Poet, Head of the Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe, Qu Hui Jin
　　Spring snow is like plum blossoms flying in the air, fluttering and falling,
　　I hope these snowflakes will bloom day and night.
　　A glance at the boundless fields covered in white snow,
　　It's as if someone's mill has scattered white flour all over the ground.
　　The white snow of the fraternal country warms my heart.
　　My hometown has been suffering from drought for a long time, and it is the time to change seeds,
　　Snowflakes, fly, fall all over the earth,
　　May you fly to the south, so that the southern land can also receive sweet rain.
　　Snow covers the road and makes it slippery, making it difficult for vehicles to travel,
　　Wait until the weather clears, then go to visit the Great Wall.
　　Sitting and watching this heavy snow, how excited I am,
　　I seem to hear the joyful sound of new sprouts growing on the ground.
　　Congratulations to the spring snow, no need for a glass of fine wine.
　　The heavy snow seems to cover the fields with a layer of flannel,
　　My heart is as warm as plants and trees,
　　What fine wine is more intoxicating than this spring snow?
　　Friends are shining with joyful eyes:
　　"Auspicious snow welcomes guests, foreshadowing a bumper year!"
　　My hometown is suffering from drought, and my friends share my worries,
　　Fraternal countries have always shared weal and woe.
                        (Translated by Wei Ping)

### 喜春雪
瞿辉瑾  韦平
1966-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　喜春雪
　　越南歌舞艺术团团长诗人  瞿辉瑾
　　春雪宛如李花飞舞，纷纷扬扬，
　　但愿这雪花呵，日夜开放。
　　乍看白雪覆盖的无边田野，
　　仿佛谁家磨房把白面撒满地上。
　　兄弟之邦的白雪温暖了我的心。
　　我的故乡久旱，正是换种时分，
　　雪花啊，飞舞吧，落遍大地，
　　愿你飞向南方，使南方的土地也分得甘霖。
　　雪覆路滑，车辆难行，
　　等到天晴，再去探望万里长城。
　　坐看这场大雪，心中多么激动，
　　我仿佛听到地上新芽茁长的欢声。
　　祝贺春雪，无须美酒一杯。
　　大雪仿佛给田野盖上一层绒被，
　　我的心也象草木一样无比温暖，
　　又有什么美酒比这春雪更令人沉醉。
　　朋友们闪耀着喜悦的双眼：
　　“瑞雪迎客，预兆着丰年！”
　　我的故乡正旱，朋友们也分忧，
　　兄弟之邦从来就共同冷暖。
                        （韦平译）
