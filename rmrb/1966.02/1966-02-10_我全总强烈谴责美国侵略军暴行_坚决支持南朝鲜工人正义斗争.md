Title: ACFTU Strongly Condemns the Atrocities of the US Aggressor Troops, Resolutely Supports the Just Struggle of South Korean Workers

Author:
Date: 1966-02-10
Edition: 3

    The ACFTU Strongly Condemns the Atrocities of the US Aggressor Troops, Resolutely Supports the Just Struggle of South Korean Workers
    (Xinhua News Agency, February 9) The All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) today sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, strongly condemning the bloody suppression by the US aggressor troops of striking workers in the Paju area of Gyeonggi Province, South Korea, on February 5.
    The ACFTU's telegram said: "This atrocity of the US aggressors arbitrarily persecuting South Korean workers fully exposes them as the irreconcilable enemies of the South Korean people and the root cause of all suffering and disasters. The Chinese workers most strongly condemn this atrocity of the US aggressor troops and resolutely support the just struggle of the South Korean worker brothers who demand severe punishment of the murderers and the satisfaction of their legitimate demands."

### 我全总强烈谴责美国侵略军暴行  坚决支持南朝鲜工人正义斗争

1966-02-10
第3版()
专栏：

    我全总强烈谴责美国侵略军暴行
    坚决支持南朝鲜工人正义斗争
    据新华社九日讯　中华全国总工会今天打电报给朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会，强烈谴责美国侵略军五日对南朝鲜京畿道坡州地区罢工工人的血腥镇压。
    全总的电报说：“美国侵略者这种任意残害南朝鲜工人的暴行，充分暴露出他们是南朝鲜人民不共戴天的敌人，和造成一切苦难与灾害的根源。中国工人最强烈地谴责美国侵略军的这个暴行，坚决支持南朝鲜工人弟兄要求严惩凶手和满足他们的正当要求的正义斗争。”
