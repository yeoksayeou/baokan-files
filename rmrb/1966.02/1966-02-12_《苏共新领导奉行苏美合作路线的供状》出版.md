    "A Confession of the New Soviet Communist Leadership's Pursuit of the Soviet-U.S. Collaboration Line" Published
    Xinhua News Agency, February 11th: The Red Flag magazine commentator's article, "A Confession of the New Soviet Communist Leadership's Pursuit of the Soviet-U.S. Collaboration Line," has been published by the People's Publishing House. This book will be distributed throughout the country starting from February 12th.
