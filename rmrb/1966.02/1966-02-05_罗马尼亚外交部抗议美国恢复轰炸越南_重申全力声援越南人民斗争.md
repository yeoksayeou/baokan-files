Title: Romanian Foreign Ministry Protests U.S. Resumption of Bombing of Vietnam; Reaffirms Full Support for Vietnamese People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-02-05
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, February 4th. Romanian newspapers on the 4th published a statement by the Romanian Foreign Ministry, strongly protesting the United States' resumption of bombing of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    The statement pointed out that the United States' resumption of bombing of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has aggravated the situation in Southeast Asia. This proves that "the so-called 'peace offensive' launched by Washington is only aimed at misleading public opinion. The United States is using this guise to greatly increase troops and strengthen the means of waging the Vietnam War, increasing its financial appropriations for military intervention, and preparing to intensify its aggression against the Vietnamese people."

    The statement said that the Romanian people and government once again condemn the United States' aggression against the Vietnamese people and express strong protest against the resumption of bombing of the territory of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    The Romanian Foreign Ministry reiterated that Romania fully supports President Ho Chi Minh's views, expressed in his recent letter, regarding the settlement of the Vietnam issue, as well as the program of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, the only legitimate representative of the South Vietnamese people.

    The Romanian people strongly demand the cessation of air raids and all infringements on the sovereignty and security of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the withdrawal of interventionist troops from South Vietnam, the implementation of the 1954 Geneva Agreements on the Vietnam issue, and respect for the sacred and inviolable right of the Vietnamese people to decide their own destiny according to their own wishes and interests without external interference.

    The statement concluded by saying that the Romanian government and people firmly believe that the Vietnamese people's struggle for independence, sovereignty, territorial integrity, and the reunification of their motherland will surely be victorious, and reaffirms the Romanian government and people's full support and fraternal support for this heroic struggle and the just cause of the Vietnamese people.

### 罗马尼亚外交部抗议美国恢复轰炸越南  重申全力声援越南人民斗争

1966-02-05
第3版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚外交部抗议美国恢复轰炸越南
    重申全力声援越南人民斗争
    新华社布加勒斯特四日电　罗马尼亚报纸四日刊登了罗马尼亚外交部的声明，强烈抗议美国恢复对越南民主共和国的轰炸。
    声明指出，美国恢复对越南民主共和国的轰炸，加剧了东南亚局势。这证明“华盛顿所发起的所谓‘和平攻势’的目的只不过是迷惑公众舆论。美国利用这一幌子大举增兵和加强进行越南战争的手段，增加了它进行军事干涉的财政拨款，作好了加紧侵略越南人民的准备。”
    声明说，罗马尼亚人民和政府再次谴责美国对越南人民的侵略，并表示强烈抗议恢复对越南民主共和国领土的轰炸。
    罗马尼亚外交部重申，罗马尼亚完全支持胡志明主席在他最近的信中，所表达的越南民主共和国关于解决越南问题的观点以及南越人民唯一合法的代表越南南方民族解放阵线的纲领。
    罗马尼亚人民强烈要求停止空中轰炸以及所有对越南民主共和国主权和安全的侵犯，从越南南方撤出干涉军，实施一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议，尊重越南人民在没有外部干涉的条件下，根据自己的愿望和利益决定自己命运的神圣的和不可动摇的权利。
    声明最后说，罗马尼亚政府和人民坚信越南人民争取独立、主权、领土完整和统一自己祖国的斗争必然胜利，并重申罗马尼亚政府和人民对这一英勇斗争和对越南人民正义事业的全力声援和兄弟般的支持。
