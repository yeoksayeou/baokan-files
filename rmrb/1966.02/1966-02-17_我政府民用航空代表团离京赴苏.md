Title: Our Government Civil Aviation Delegation Leaves Beijing for the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-17
Edition: 4

    Our Government Civil Aviation Delegation Leaves Beijing for the Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. To revise the Sino-Soviet Civil Air Transport Agreement, the Chinese Government Civil Aviation Delegation, led by Shen Tu, Deputy Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, left Beijing by plane today for Moscow to hold negotiations with the Soviet Government Civil Aviation Delegation.

### 我政府民用航空代表团离京赴苏

1966-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我政府民用航空代表团离京赴苏
    新华社十六日讯　为修订中苏民用航空交通协定，中国政府民用航空代表团在中国民用航空总局副局长沈图率领下，今天乘飞机离京前往莫斯科与苏联政府民航代表团进行谈判。
