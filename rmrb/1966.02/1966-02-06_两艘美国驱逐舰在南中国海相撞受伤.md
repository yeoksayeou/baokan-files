Title: Two U.S. Destroyers Collide and are Damaged in the South China Sea
Author:
Date: 1966-02-06
Edition: 4

    Two U.S. Destroyers Collide and are Damaged in the South China Sea
    Xinhua News Agency, February 5th. Honolulu dispatch: Two U.S. destroyers operating in the South China Sea collided on the 3rd and were damaged.

    According to United Press International on February 5th, quoting a U.S. Pacific Fleet spokesman, the two U.S. warships "were both heavily damaged." The two warships were: the destroyer "Brinkley Bass" and the guided missile destroyer "Waddell".

### 两艘美国驱逐舰在南中国海相撞受伤

1966-02-06
第4版()
专栏：

    两艘美国驱逐舰在南中国海相撞受伤
    新华社五日讯　檀香山消息：两艘在南中国海进行活动的美国驱逐舰三日相撞受伤。
    据合众国际社五日引述美国太平洋舰队发言人的话承认，这两艘美国军舰“都受了重伤”。这两艘军舰是：“布林克利·巴斯”号驱逐舰和装有导弹的“瓦德尔”号驱逐舰。
