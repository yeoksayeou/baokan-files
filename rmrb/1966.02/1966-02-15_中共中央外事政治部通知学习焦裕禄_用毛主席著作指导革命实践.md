Title: The CCP Central Foreign Affairs Political Department Issues Notice to Study Jiao Yulu; Use Chairman Mao's Works to Guide Revolutionary Practice
Author:
Date: 1966-02-15
Edition: 1

    According to Xinhua News Agency on the 14th, the CCP Central Foreign Affairs Political Department issued a notice on the 12th, requiring all party members and cadres in foreign affairs departments to take Comrade Jiao Yulu as an example and earnestly learn from him.
    The notice said that first, like Comrade Jiao Yulu, we must give prominence to politics, creatively study and apply Chairman Mao's works' positions, viewpoints, and methods to guide revolutionary practice, be consistent in word and deed, and do what we say. Second, learn from his thorough revolutionary spirit of "having the entire people in his heart, but only not himself," and his thought of serving the Chinese people and the people of the world "completely" and "thoroughly." In the international class struggle, we must have the spirit to overwhelm all enemies, establish the spirit of daring to make revolution, daring to struggle, not fearing war, and not fearing sacrifice, be able to withstand any tests and risks, be indestructible, incorruptible, and never change color. Third, learn from him to always maintain the glorious tradition of the Party, maintain the true qualities of the working people and a style of hard work and plain living, share the same breath and destiny with the masses, and always be loyal to the revolution, loyal to the Party, and diligently work for the revolution. Fourth, learn from his proletarian pioneering spirit and revolutionary work style.
    The notice requires the political work departments and Party and Communist Youth League organizations of all foreign affairs units to effectively organize this study and regard it as a component of the current movement to study Chairman Mao's works. It calls on everyone to use Comrade Jiao Yulu as a mirror to find shortcomings, and to use Comrade Jiao Yulu as an example to catch up from behind.

### 中共中央外事政治部通知学习焦裕禄  用毛主席著作指导革命实践

1966-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央外事政治部通知学习焦裕禄
    用毛主席著作指导革命实践
    据新华社十四日讯　中共中央外事政治部十二日发出通知，要求外事部门所有的党员、干部，都应以焦裕禄同志为榜样，认真地向他学习。
    通知说，首先，要象焦裕禄同志那样，突出政治，活学活用毛主席著作的立场、观点和方法，用以指导革命实践，言行一致，说到做到。第二，学习他“心里装着全体人民，唯独没有自己”的彻底革命精神，“完全”和“彻底”地为中国人民和世界人民服务的思想。要在国际的阶级斗争当中，具有压倒一切敌人的气概，树立敢于革命，敢于斗争，不怕战争，不怕牺牲的精神，经得起任何考验和风险，打不垮，腐蚀不了，永不变色。第三，学习他永远保持着党的光荣传统，保持着劳动人民的本色和艰苦朴素作风，与人民群众同呼吸共命运，永远忠于革命忠于党，勤勤恳恳为革命工作。第四，学习他那种无产阶级的创业精神和革命化的工作作风。
    通知要求各外事单位政治工作部门和机关党、团组织，切实把这个学习组织好，并且作为当前学习毛主席著作运动的一个组成部分。号召大家以焦裕禄同志为镜子找差距，以焦裕禄同志为榜样迎头赶上去。
