Title: Guangzhou Prepares to Commemorate the Centenary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth; Guangzhou Branch of the Preparatory Committee for the Centenary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth Holds First Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-20
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 19th – The Guangzhou branch of the Preparatory Committee for the Centenary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth held its first meeting this morning to discuss matters related to the commemoration of the centenary of Sun Yat-sen's birth.
    Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the CCP Central Committee's Central-South Bureau and Director of the Guangzhou branch of the Preparatory Committee for the Centenary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth, presided over the meeting and spoke. He said that Guangdong is the hometown of Sun Yat-sen. To commemorate Sun Yat-sen, we must carry forward his revolutionary spirit, unite closely under the leadership of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, resolutely oppose US imperialism and modern revisionism, and carry the revolution through to the end.
    Chen Yu, Wu Zhipu, Lin Liming, Ou Mengjue, Li Jianzhen, Chen De, Zeng Sheng, Guo Dihuo, Yang Kanghua, Deng Wenzhao, Huang Jie, Zhao Zhuoyun, Wang Lanxi, Jiao Linyi, Bo Huaiqi, Zhang Lucun, Feng Shen, Xiao Junying, Yi Meihou, Huang Youmou, Luo Jun, Luo Peiyuan, Chen Qiuan, Liang Guang, Li Dachao, Lin Zhicheng, Rao Weihua, Fang Shaoyi, all Vice Directors of the Guangzhou branch of the Preparatory Committee and members of the Guangzhou branch of the Preparatory Committee attended the meeting.
    After enthusiastic discussions, the meeting adopted the following measures for the commemoration of the centenary of Sun Yat-sen's birth: 1. Hold a commemorative meeting for the centenary of Sun Yat-sen's birth at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall in November 1966; 2. Visit the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall and Sun Yat-sen's former residence in Cuiheng Village; 3. Renovate the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall, renovate the Sun Yat-sen Monument on Yuexiu Mountain, decorate the Sun Yat-sen Monument on Huangpu Island, renovate Sun Yat-sen's former residence in Cuiheng Village, and organize the exhibition hall; 4. Organize the Huanghuagang and Zhu Zhixin's Tomb and the Liao Zhongkai Memorial; 5. Solicit commemorative articles, collect Sun Yat-sen's relics and materials, compile a collection of historical and literary materials, and produce a color documentary of the commemorative activities; 6. Hold various symposiums to publicize Sun Yat-sen's life and deeds; 7. Sun Yat-sen University and Sun Yat-sen Medical College will hold commemorative activities respectively; 8. Invite overseas Chinese and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao who are related to Sun Yat-sen's revolutionary cause to participate in the commemorative activities.
    The Guangzhou branch of the Preparatory Committee for the Centenary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth was established on the 17th.

### 广州筹备纪念孙中山先生诞辰一百周年  孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹委会广州分会举行首次会议

1966-02-20
第2版()
专栏：

    广州筹备纪念孙中山先生诞辰一百周年
    孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹委会广州分会举行首次会议
    新华社广州十九日电　孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹备委员会广州市分会，今天上午举行第一次会议，商讨孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念事项。
    中共中央中南局第一书记、孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹备委员会广州市分会主任陶铸，主持了会议，并在会上讲了话。他说，广东是孙中山先生的故乡，我们纪念孙中山先生，就要发扬他的革命精神，在党中央和毛主席领导下，紧密地团结起来，坚决反对美帝国主义和现代修正主义，把革命进行到底。
    筹委会广州市分会副主任陈郁、吴芝圃、林李明、区梦觉、李坚真、陈德、曾生、郭棣活、杨康华、邓文钊、黄洁、赵卓云、王阑西、焦林义、薄怀奇、张醁村、冯燊、萧隽英、蚁美厚、黄友谋、罗浚、罗培元、陈秋安、梁广、李达潮、林志澄、饶卫华、方少逸和筹委会广州市分会委员出席了会议。
    会议经过热烈的讨论，通过了关于孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念的办法：一，一九六六年十一月在中山纪念堂举行孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念大会；二，参谒中山纪念堂和翠亨村孙中山先生故居；三，修缮中山纪念堂，修缮越秀山中山纪念碑，修饰黄埔岛孙中山先生纪念碑，修缮翠亨村孙中山先生故居和整理陈列馆；四，整理黄花岗、朱执信先生墓地和廖仲恺先生纪念碑；五，征集纪念文章，搜集孙中山先生遗物和资料，汇编文史资料专辑，摄制纪念活动实况的彩色纪录片；六，举行各种座谈会，进行有关孙中山先生生平事迹的宣传工作；七，中山大学和中山医学院分别举行纪念活动；八，邀请同孙中山先生革命事业有关的华侨和港澳同胞参加纪念活动。
    孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹备委员会广州市分会，是在十七日成立的。
