Title: Photo
Author: Du Xiuxian
Date: 1966-02-20
Edition: 2

    On February 19th, leaders Zhou Enlai, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Ye Jianying, and others, received Air Force personnel who had performed meritorious service in combat by shooting down U.S. imperialist military aircraft and U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek gang aircraft that had invaded our country's airspace since January 1965: Wang Lin, Li Kui, Gao Changji, Dong Xiaohai, Feng Quanmin, Li Chunguang, Hu Yingfa, Zhang Yunbao, Zhang Huailian, Zhu Yilong, Lu Xiangxiao, etc., and took a group photo with them.
    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian
