Title: New 220,000-volt Air Circuit Breaker Designed and Manufactured in China

Author:
Date: 1966-02-04
Edition: 2

    A new 220,000-volt air circuit breaker, designed and manufactured in China, was recently produced at the Shenyang High-Voltage Switch Factory. This product has a breaking capacity of 8,000 megavolt-amperes and is an important electrical equipment used in large power grids. Appraisal proves that its performance is good.　Xinhua News Agency
