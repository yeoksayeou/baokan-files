Author:
Date: 1966-02-13
Edition: 4

    Vice Premier Bo Yibo Receives Romanian Cultural Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, February 12th. This afternoon, Vice Premier Bo Yibo received the head of the Romanian Cultural Delegation, Romanian Ambassador to China Dumitru Gheorghiu, and all members of the delegation. He held cordial and friendly talks with them.
    Present at the reception were Li Qitao, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others. (Attached picture)
    February 12th, Vice Premier Bo Yibo receives the Romanian Cultural Delegation headed by the Romanian Ambassador to China, Dumitru Gheorghiu (fourth from the right). Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen.

### 薄一波副总理接见罗马尼亚文化代表团

1966-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    薄一波副总理接见罗马尼亚文化代表团
    新华社十二日讯　薄一波副总理今天下午接见了罗马尼亚文化代表团团长、罗马尼亚驻中国大使杜米特鲁·乔治乌和代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有对外文委副主任李琦涛等。（附图片）
    二月十二日，薄一波副总理接见以团长、罗马尼亚驻中国大使杜米特鲁·乔治乌（右四）为首的罗马尼亚文化代表团。　新华社记者
　郑小箴摄
