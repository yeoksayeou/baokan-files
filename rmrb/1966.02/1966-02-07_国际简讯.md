	International Briefs
	United States Sells Large Quantities of Weapons to Israel
	Washington News: The United States State Department announced on the 5th that the United States has been selling tanks to Israel for some years.
	This is another action by the United States to further arm its tool of aggression in the Middle East, Israel, in order to threaten the security of Arab countries and people.
	In its statement, the State Department said, "For some years, in order to meet the modernization requirements [of the Israeli army], we have been selling various types of military equipment to the Israeli government," including tanks.
	British, Australian and New Zealand Hold So-Called "Defense" Talks
	Canberra News: British Defense Secretary Healey concluded talks in Canberra on the 3rd with Australian Prime Minister Holt and New Zealand Defense Minister Eyre on the so-called "East of Suez" region "defense" issues.
	It is reported that during the talks, Healey wanted Australia and New Zealand to help Britain share "defense responsibilities" and implement "defense cooperation." He also asked Australia to provide funds and land to establish a joint military base near Darwin in Australia. The communique issued after the meeting showed that no specific agreement was reached during the talks.
	Foreign Ministers of Pakistan, Turkey and Iran Hold Meeting
	Rawalpindi News: The fourth meeting of the Ministerial Council of the Pakistan, Turkey, and Iran "Regional Cooperation for Development" organization closed on the 4th in Islamabad, the new capital of Pakistan.
	The Foreign Ministers and Ministers of Foreign Affairs of Pakistan, Iran and Turkey attended the meeting.

### 国际简讯

1966-02-07
第3版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    美国出售大量武器给以色列
    华盛顿消息：美国国务院五日宣布，美国最近一些年来一直卖坦克给以色列。
    这是美国进一步武装它侵略中东的工具以色列，以威胁阿拉伯国家和人民的安全的又一行动。
    国务院在它发表的声明里说，“若干年来，为了满足（以色列军队的）现代化要求，我们一直向以色列政府出售各种类型的军事装备”，其中包括坦克。
    英澳新举行所谓“防务”会谈
    堪培拉消息：英国国防大臣希利三日结束了在堪培拉同澳大利亚总理霍尔特和新西兰国防部长艾尔等就所谓“苏伊士以东”地区“防务”问题举行的会谈。
    据报道，在这次会谈中，希利要澳大利亚和新西兰帮助英国分担“防务责任”，实行“防务合作”。他还要求澳大利亚提供资金和地盘，在澳大利亚的达尔文附近建立联合军事基地。会谈后发表的公报表明，这次会谈没有达成具体的协议。
    巴土伊三国外交部长举行会议
    拉瓦尔品第消息：巴基斯坦、土耳其和伊朗“地区性发展合作”组织部长理事会第四次会议四日在巴基斯坦新都伊斯兰堡闭幕。
    巴基斯坦、伊朗和土耳其的外长和外交大臣出席了会议。
