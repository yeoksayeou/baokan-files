Title: Hungarian Government Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-23
Edition: 5

　　Hungarian Government Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd. The Hungarian government trade delegation, led by the head of the delegation, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of Hungary, Szalai Béla, left Beijing by plane this afternoon to return home. Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Jiang Ming, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others. The Hungarian Ambassador to China, Halász József, and others also went to the airport to see them off.

### 匈政府贸易代表团离京回国

1966-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　匈政府贸易代表团离京回国
    新华社二十二日讯　由团长、匈牙利对外贸易部副部长萨拉伊·贝洛率领的匈牙利政府贸易代表团，今天下午乘飞机离开北京回国。前往机场送行的，有对外贸易部副部长江明等。匈牙利驻华大使郝拉思·尤若夫等也到机场送行。
