Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Writes Letter to Support the Struggle of the South African People
Author:
Date: 1966-02-07
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, February 6th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions recently wrote a letter to the South African Congress of Trade Unions on the occasion of the International Workers and Trade Unions "Support South Africa Day" on February 7th, expressing deep sympathy and resolute support for the arduous and heroic struggle of South African workers and people against the evils of colonialism, racial segregation, and racial discrimination, and for national liberation and equal rights.
    In the letter, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions expressed its firm belief that the deeply suffering South African workers and people will surely adhere to unity, persist in struggle, bravely rise up, and break the chains imposed on them by the US-led imperialism and the Verwoerd government. The letter pointed out that workers and people all over the world are supporting the South African workers and people, and the South African workers and people will surely win.

### 我全总写信支持南非人民斗争

1966-02-07
第3版()
专栏：

    我全总写信支持南非人民斗争
    新华社六日讯　中华全国总工会最近就二月七日国际工人和工会“支援南非日”写信给南非工会大会，对南非工人和人民为反对万恶的殖民主义、种族隔离和种族歧视，为争取民族解放、争取平等权利而进行的艰苦而英勇的斗争，表示深切的同情和坚决的支持。
    全总在信件中表示坚信，苦难深重的南非工人和人民必将坚持团结，坚持斗争，英勇奋起，打断以美国为首的帝国主义及维沃尔德政府强加在南非工人和人民身上的锁链。信件指出，全世界工人和人民都在支持南非工人和人民，南非工人和人民必胜。
