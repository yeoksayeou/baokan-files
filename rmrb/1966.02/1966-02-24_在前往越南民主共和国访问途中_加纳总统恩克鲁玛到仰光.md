Title: President Nkrumah of Ghana Arrives in Rangoon En Route to Visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-02-24
Edition: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 23rd - Dr. Kwame Nkrumah, President of the Republic of Ghana, arrived in Rangoon by special plane on the morning of February 23rd, en route to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

　　He will stay here for one day and leave here on the 24th for a friendly visit to China.

　　Nkrumah left Accra, the capital of Ghana, on February 21st. He made short stops in Cairo, Karachi, New Delhi, and other places along the way, and held talks with UAR President Nasser, Pakistani President Ayub Khan, and Indian Prime Minister Mrs. Gandhi, respectively.

### 在前往越南民主共和国访问途中　加纳总统恩克鲁玛到仰光

1966-02-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　在前往越南民主共和国访问途中
　　加纳总统恩克鲁玛到仰光
    新华社仰光二十三日电　加纳共和国总统克瓦米·恩克鲁玛博士在前往访问越南民主共和国途中二十三日早上乘专机到达仰光。
    他将在这里停留一天，二十四日离开这里顺道前往中国进行友好访问。
    恩克鲁玛是在二月二十一日离开加纳首都阿克拉的。沿途在开罗、卡拉奇、新德里等地作了短时间停留，并且分别和阿联总统纳赛尔、巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗、印度总理甘地夫人进行了会谈。
