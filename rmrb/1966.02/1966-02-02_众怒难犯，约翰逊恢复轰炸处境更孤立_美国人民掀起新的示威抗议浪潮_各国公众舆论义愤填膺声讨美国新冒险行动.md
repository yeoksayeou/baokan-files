Title: Defying Public Anger, Johnson Resumes Bombing, Further Isolating Himself. American People Launch New Wave of Demonstrations and Protests. Public Opinion in Various Countries Righteously Indignant, Denouncing America's New Adventure.
Author:
Date: 1966-02-02
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch – New York news: U.S. President Johnson brazenly announced the resumption of bombing of North Vietnam on January 31st, igniting a new wave of protests from the American people. That night, a group of New York citizens braved the severe cold to demonstrate overnight in front of the United Nations building.

    A group of young people also held a protest demonstration in Times Square, New York.

    On the same day, nearly two hundred people held a "sit-in" demonstration in Oakland, California. The authorities dispatched one hundred police officers and brought in prison vans in an attempt to threaten them to stop the demonstration. However, they ignored the intimidation, unafraid of arrest, and announced that they would continue the demonstration.

    Dozens of students and teachers at Stanford University in California went on strike on the same day and demonstrated on campus, holding signs that read: "Strike – Protest the Bombing!"

    This newspaper's comprehensive report, according to Xinhua News Agency: Public opinion in various countries strongly protested and condemned the Johnson administration's brazen announcement of resuming the bombing of North Vietnam and further expanding the new adventure of the war of aggression against Vietnam.

    An observer of the Korean Central News Agency published an article on the 1st, saying that this resumption of bombing is another serious and sinful step taken by the U.S. imperialists on the road of "gradually escalating" the Vietnam War. Their resumption of bombing once again exposes their vicious conspiracy: to achieve their despicable aggressive goals in Vietnam at any cost. Anyone who desires peace can only righteously condemn this blatant challenge by the U.S. imperialists.

    Romania's *Scînteia* published a commentary on the 1st, saying that the United States brazenly launched war actions against the independent and sovereign state of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam again. This confirms that the purpose of Washington's so-called "peace offensive" is merely to deceive world public opinion and, in reality, is a smokescreen to cover up its plan to intensify and expand its aggression in Southeast Asia.

    The Japanese Communist Party's newspaper *Akahata* published an editorial on the 1st, calling on all democratic forces and working people to strongly protest the Sato government, which is acting as a tool of the United States in the "peace offensive" and actively cooperating with the U.S. war of aggression. It urged them to step up the struggle against the U.S. using Japan as a base for aggression against Vietnam and to give the Vietnamese people more support morally and materially.

    Secretary-General of the Japan Socialist Party, Tomomi Narita, condemned the United States for resuming the bombing of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and expanding the war of aggression against Vietnam at the plenary session of the Japanese Diet on the afternoon of January 31st. He said that on the Vietnam issue, the Vietnamese nation is the victim, while the United States is the perpetrator. Only when the United States respects the Geneva Accords and withdraws its troops from South Vietnam can Vietnam restore peace.

    Narita condemned the Japanese Prime Minister's shameless act of attempting to persuade the victim to accept "peace."

    The United Arab Republic's *Al-Akhbar* published a commentary saying that the U.S. resumption of bombing will not achieve any results the U.S. expects, because months of bombing have not made Hanoi yield, but have strengthened its determination to fight. The commentary said that the Vietnamese people have won more international sympathy.

    Algeria's *El Moudjahid* published a commentary on the 1st, pointing out: "Johnson's resumption of bombing has once again aroused indignation among the people of all countries who love peace and uphold justice."

    The commentary said, "Today everyone sees that, in essence, there has never been a genuine 'peace' offensive, but only preparations for new war efforts. The resumption of air strikes against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam fully confirms this."

    British philosopher Bertrand Russell issued a statement on the 31st, saying that the United States' "resumption of bombing of North Vietnam most clearly illustrates what the so-called 'peace offensive' is all about. The Pentagon has no desire for peace; what they hope for is to control Southeast Asia."

    He said, "We have an obligation to awaken British and American public opinion until the American war machine cannot turn. Only in this way can we achieve the withdrawal of American troops from Vietnam."

    The British Vietnam Committee issued a statement on January 31st, saying that the whole world is disgusted with Johnson after he delivered a hypocritical broadcast speech justifying the resumption of bombing.

    The statement also revealed that the United States submitted the Vietnam issue to the United Nations Security Council "in an attempt to use the United Nations to cover up this aggression." "Despite the shameless support of Foreign Secretary Stewart, namely the Wilson government, for Johnson, this new attempt to exploit the United Nations cannot deceive anyone."

### 众怒难犯，约翰逊恢复轰炸处境更孤立  美国人民掀起新的示威抗议浪潮  各国公众舆论义愤填膺声讨美国新冒险行动

1966-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

    众怒难犯，约翰逊恢复轰炸处境更孤立
    美国人民掀起新的示威抗议浪潮
    各国公众舆论义愤填膺声讨美国新冒险行动
    新华社一日讯　纽约消息：美国总统约翰逊一月三十一日悍然宣布恢复对越南北方的轰炸，激起了美国人民新的抗议浪潮。当天晚上，一批纽约公众冒着严寒，在联合国大厦门前通宵示威。
    在“纽约时报广场”上，也有一群青年举行抗议示威。
    同一天，在加利福尼亚州奥克兰市，近二百人举行“静坐”示威。当局调来一百名警察，并且开来囚车，企图威胁他们停止示威。但是他们不顾威吓，不怕被捕，宣布要把示威进行下去。
    加利福尼亚州斯坦福市的数十名学生和教师在同一天罢课，并且举着标语牌在校园内示威，标语牌上写道：“罢课——抗议轰炸！”
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：各国公众舆论强烈抗议和谴责约翰逊政府悍然宣布恢复轰炸越南北方和进一步扩大侵略越南战争的新的冒险行动。
    朝鲜中央通讯社观察家一日发表文章说，这次恢复轰炸是美帝国主义者在把越南战争“逐步升级”的道路上又迈出了严重罪恶的一步。他们恢复轰炸再次暴露了他们的恶毒阴谋：不惜任何代价要实现他们在越南的卑鄙侵略目的。凡是希望和平的人，都只能义愤填膺谴责美帝国主义者的这种明目张胆的挑战。
    罗马尼亚《火花报》一日发表评论说，美国悍然向独立和主权国家——越南民主共和国再次发动了战争行动，这证实华盛顿所谓“和平攻势”的目的只不过是欺骗世界公众舆论，实际上是掩盖它制订加紧扩大侵略东南亚计划的幌子。
    日共机关报《赤旗报》一日发表社论号召所有民主力量和劳动人民强烈抗议在美国的“和平攻势”中充当美国的工具、并积极配合美国的侵略战争的佐藤政府，加紧进行斗争反对美国以日本为基地侵略越南，在道义上和物质上给越南人民更多的支持。
      日本社会党书记长成田知巳一月三十一日下午在日本国会全体会议上谴责美国恢复对越南民主共和国的轰炸，扩大侵略越南战争。他说，在越南问题上，越南民族是被害者，而美国是加害者。只有美国尊重日内瓦协议，从南越撤走它的军队，越南才能恢复和平。
    成田谴责日本首相企图说服被害者接受“和平”的无耻行径。
    阿联《消息报》发表评论说，美国恢复轰炸不会取得美国所想的任何结果，因为几个月的轰炸并没有使河内屈服，反而加强了它的斗争决心。评论说，越南人民赢得了更多的国际同情。
    阿尔及利亚《圣战者日报》一日发表评论指出：“约翰逊恢复轰炸再一次在爱好和平和伸张正义的各国人民中间激起愤慨。”
    评论说，“今天大家看到，说穿了，就不曾有过什么真正的‘和平’攻势，而只是为新的战争努力作的准备。恢复对越南民主共和国的空袭，充分证实了这一点。”
    英国著名哲学家罗素三十一日发表声明说，美国“恢复轰炸北越最清楚地说明了所谓‘和平攻势’是怎么一回事。五角大楼没有和平的愿望，他们所希望的是控制东南亚。”
    他说，“我们有义务唤起英国和美国的公众舆论，直到美国的战争机器不能转动为止。只有这样才能做到使美军撤出越南。”
    英国越南委员会一月三十一日发表声明说，在约翰逊发表为恢复轰炸作辩解的伪善广播讲话后，全世界都厌恶他。
    声明还揭露，美国把侵越问题提交联合国安全理事会
  “是企图利用联合国来掩盖这种侵略”。“尽管外交大臣斯图尔特，也就是威尔逊政府，对约翰逊表示了无耻的支持，这种利用联合国的新企图骗不了任何人”。
