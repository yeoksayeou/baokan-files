Title: Our Government Delegation Leaves Tanzania for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-23
Edition: 5

　　Our Government Delegation Leaves Tanzania for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, Dar es Salaam Dispatch: The Chinese Government Delegation headed by Xu Jie, Vice Minister of Geology, left here by plane on the 19th to return home after participating in the celebrations of the second anniversary of the Zanzibar revolution at the invitation of the Tanzanian government.
    Some leaders of Tanzanian mass organizations and Shi Gu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Tanzania, saw the delegation off at the airport. On the 18th, Tanzanian Second Vice President Kawawa received Vice Minister Xu Jie and members of the Chinese Government Delegation here and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

### 我政府代表团离坦桑尼亚回国

1966-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　我政府代表团离坦桑尼亚回国
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆电　由地质部副部长许杰率领的中国政府代表团应坦桑尼亚政府的邀请参加了桑给巴尔革命胜利两周年的庆祝活动之后，十九日乘飞机离开这里回国。
    坦桑尼亚群众组织的一些领导人和中国驻坦桑尼亚大使馆临时代办施谷，到机场为代表团送行。坦桑尼亚第二副总统卡瓦瓦十八日在这里接见了许杰副部长和中国政府代表团的团员，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
