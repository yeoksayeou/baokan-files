Title: American Women Demonstrate Outside White House Protesting Johnson Administration's Expansion of War of Aggression Against Vietnam; Danish People Demonstrate in Front of American Embassy in Support of Vietnamese People's Struggle

Author:

Date: 1966-02-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, February 10th: Approximately 1,300 women from all over the United States demonstrated outside the White House on the 9th, protesting the U.S. government's expansion of the war in Vietnam.

    The demonstrating women brought their children to the gates of the White House. The placards and banners they held read: "Don't send our sons to kill or be killed!" They also released black balloons with the slogan: "Stop the Slaughter!"

    The demonstrators issued a statement opposing the United States' resumption of bombing North Vietnam and the escalation of the war in the South.

    Xinhua News Agency, February 10th: More than two hundred Danes braved the cold on the 8th to demonstrate in front of the U.S. Embassy in Copenhagen, protesting the U.S.'s expanded war of aggression in Vietnam and expressing support for the fighting Vietnamese people. The demonstrators held up placards with the slogans: "U.S. Get Out of Vietnam!" "Stop Slaughtering the Vietnamese People!"

    Similar demonstrations were also held on the 6th in Aarhus, Denmark's second-largest city.

    A dispatch from the Finnish capital reported that demonstrations and mass rallies were held in Helsinki on the 8th to oppose the U.S.'s expanded war of aggression in Vietnam. Speakers at the mass rally said: "The United States has challenged world opinion, and we are prepared to meet the challenge." (With picture)
    February 9th, 1,300 women from all over the United States held demonstrations outside the White House, strongly demanding an end to the war of aggression against Vietnam. Fax photo (Xinhua News Agency)

### 抗议约翰逊政府扩大侵越战争  美国千余妇女在白宫外面示威  丹麦人民在美国使馆前示威声援越南人民斗争

1966-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

    抗议约翰逊政府扩大侵越战争
    美国千余妇女在白宫外面示威
    丹麦人民在美国使馆前示威声援越南人民斗争
    新华社十日讯　华盛顿消息：来自美国各地的约一千三百名妇女，九日在白宫外面示威，抗议美国政府在越南扩大战争。
    示威妇女带着她们的孩子来到白宫门前。在她们打起的标语牌和旗帜上面写着：“不要派我们的儿子去杀人或送死！”她们还放出黑色气球，上面带着标语：“停止屠杀！”
    示威者发表声明，反对美国恢复轰炸越南北方以及在南方使战争升级。
    新华社十日讯　二百多名丹麦人八日冒着严寒，在美国驻哥本哈根大使馆前示威，抗议美国在越南扩大侵略战争，表示声援战斗中的越南人民。示威者高举标语牌，上面写着：“美国滚出越南！”“停止屠杀越南人民！”
    六日在丹麦第二大城市奥尔胡斯也举行了同样的示威。
    来自芬兰首都的消息说，八日在赫尔辛基举行了示威和群众大会，反对美国在越南扩大侵略战争。在群众大会上讲话的人说：“美国已向世界舆论提出挑战，我们准备应战。”（附图片）
    二月九日，来自美国各地的一千三百名妇女在白宫外面举行示威，强烈要求结束侵越战争。　传真照片（新华社发）
