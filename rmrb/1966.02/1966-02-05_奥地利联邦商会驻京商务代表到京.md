Title: Austrian Federal Economic Chamber's Business Representative to Beijing Arrives

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-05
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, received Schmidt, the Austrian Federal Economic Chamber's Business Representative in Beijing, and Leksi, Director of the China Department of the Austrian Federal Economic Chamber, and Helmreich, Austrian Commercial Commissioner in Hong Kong, who accompanied him to Beijing, this morning. Present at the meeting were Hou Tong, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Li Chuan, Director of the Liaison Department.
    Schmidt, the Austrian Federal Economic Chamber's Business Representative in Beijing, and others arrived in Beijing on the 3rd. Hou Tong hosted a banquet for them that evening.

### 奥地利联邦商会驻京商务代表到京

1966-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

    奥地利联邦商会驻京商务代表到京
    据新华社四日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今天上午接见了奥地利联邦商会驻北京商务代表施密特和陪同他来北京的奥地利联邦商会中国司司长莱克西以及奥地利驻香港商务专员赫尔姆来希等。接见时在座的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席侯桐、联络部部长李川等。
    奥地利联邦商会驻京商务代表施密特等是在三日到达北京的。当晚，侯桐曾设宴招待他们。
