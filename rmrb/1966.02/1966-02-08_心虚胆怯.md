Title: Feeling Guilty and Timid

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-08
Edition: 4

    Feeling Guilty and Timid
    Johnson, in Honolulu, attacked "some particular advocates who advocate withdrawal from Vietnam." According to the Associated Press, this was directed at domestic critics in the United States.
    Johnson dared not face inquiries in Congress in Washington, so he ran to Honolulu to counter his critics. This can only indicate his feeling guilty and timid.

### 心虚胆怯

1966-02-08
第4版()
专栏：三言两语

    心虚胆怯
    约翰逊在檀香山攻击了“一些特别的鼓吹者主张在越南撤退”。据美联社说，这是说给美国国内批评他的人听的。
    约翰逊在华盛顿不敢面对议会中的质询，跑到檀香山去反击批评他的人。这只能表明他的心虚胆怯罢了。
