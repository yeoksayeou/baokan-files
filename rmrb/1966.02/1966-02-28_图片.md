    On February 27, the delegation of the Japanese Communist Party, headed by Comrade Kenji Miyamoto (first from the right), arrived in Nanning from Hanoi by plane to continue its visit to our country. Comrade Wei Guoqing (second from the right), First Secretary of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Committee of the CPC, and others warmly welcomed them at the airport.
                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Zhicheng (faxed photo)
