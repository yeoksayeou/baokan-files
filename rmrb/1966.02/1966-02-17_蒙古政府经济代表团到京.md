Title: Mongolian Government Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-02-17
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 16th. The Mongolian Government Economic Delegation, consisting of eight members led by Professor D. Tsegmid, Mongolian Ambassador to China, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon to discuss issues related to China-Mongolia economic and technical assistance. Welcoming them at the station were Yang Lin, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries, Liu Xianglun, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and responsible persons from relevant departments, as well as officials from the Mongolian Embassy in China.
    Tonight, Yang Lin held a welcoming banquet.

### 蒙古政府经济代表团到京

1966-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古政府经济代表团到京
    新华社十六日讯前来我国商谈中蒙经济技术援助问题的蒙古政府经济代表团一行八人，由团长、蒙古驻中国大使敦·策伯格米德教授率领，今天下午乘火车到京。到车站欢迎的，有对外经济联络委员会副主任杨琳、外交部第二亚洲司副司长刘祥纶和有关部门负责人，以及蒙古驻中国大使馆官员等。
    今晚，杨琳举行了欢迎宴会。
