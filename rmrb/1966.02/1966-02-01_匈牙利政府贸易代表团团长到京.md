Title: Head of Hungarian Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-01
Edition: 4

    Hungarian Government Trade Delegation Head Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th – Béla Szalai, Head of the Hungarian Government Trade Delegation and Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of Hungary, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Jiang Ming, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others were at the airport to greet him.  József Horváth, Hungarian Ambassador to China, and Kódmán, Deputy Head of the Hungarian Trade Delegation who arrived earlier, were also at the airport to greet him.

### 匈牙利政府贸易代表团团长到京

1966-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利政府贸易代表团团长到京
    新华社三十日讯　匈牙利政府贸易代表团团长、匈牙利对外贸易部副部长萨拉伊·贝洛今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有，对外贸易部副部长江明等。匈牙利驻华大使郝拉思·尤若夫和先期到京的匈牙利贸易代表团副团长科德门等，也到机场迎接。
