Title: Prince Souphanouvong Exposes American Lackeys Serving U.S. Imperialism's Plot to Invade Central and Lower Laos; Condemns Kong Le's Troops for Attacking Phou Khout Mountain; Patriotic Neutral Forces Command Calls on Officers and Soldiers of Kong Le's Troops to Recognize the Situation and Oppose the Attack
Author:
Date: 1966-02-24
Edition: 4

　　新华社康开二十二日电　据巴特寮电台二十二日广播：老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席、老挝三方民族团结政府副首相苏发努冯亲王最近打紧急电报给老挝国王说，贡勒军队进攻富科特山地区是一个极其严重的行动，其目的在于阻挠和破坏老挝人之间进行和平解决老挝问题的会谈，为美帝国主义企图派遣它的陆军直接占领中、下寮地区的阴谋创造条件。
　　电报说，不仅美帝国主义和库帕拉西—萨纳尼空集团要对这一行动承担责任，而且所有美国仆从也要承担责任。
　　苏发努冯亲王在电报中表示，希望老挝国王迫使美帝国主义及其走狗停止进攻富科特山，并把它们的军队全部撤回原地，希望三亲王进行会晤，以便找出稳定老挝局势的办法。
　　据新华社康开二十三日电　老挝爱国中立力量指挥部最近发表声明指出，美帝国主义及其走狗大规模进攻富科特山地区是美帝国主义扩大老挝战争的新步骤，企图配合它在越南日益猖狂的军事活动。
　　声明指出，富马亲王和贡勒越来越投降美帝国主义和为美帝国主义和右派的扩大战争计划服务，使贡勒部队中越来越多的官兵表示不满。他们的这种投降政策，肯定会给他们带来极其严重的灾难和促使老挝局势越来越严重。
　　声明还号召贡勒部队中被强迫来进攻富科特山的官兵们，赶快认清形势，起来反对这次进攻富科特山的命令。（附图片）

Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, 22nd dispatch. According to a broadcast from Pathet Lao Radio on the 22nd: Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Lao Patriotic Front Central Committee and Deputy Prime Minister of the Lao Tripartite National Union Government, recently sent an urgent telegram to the King of Laos stating that the attack on the Phou Khout Mountain area by Kong Le's troops is an extremely serious action, the purpose of which is to obstruct and undermine the talks between the Lao people to peacefully resolve the Lao issue, and to create conditions for the U.S. imperialists' plot to send their army to directly occupy the central and lower Laos areas.
　　The telegram said that not only must U.S. imperialism and the Kouprasith-Sananikone group bear responsibility for this action, but all American lackeys must also bear responsibility.
　　Prince Souphanouvong stated in the telegram that he hoped the King of Laos would force U.S. imperialism and its running dogs to stop attacking Phou Khout Mountain and withdraw all their troops to their original positions, and hoped the three princes would meet in order to find a way to stabilize the situation in Laos.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, 23rd dispatch. The Lao Patriotic Neutral Forces Command recently issued a statement pointing out that the large-scale attack on the Phou Khout Mountain area by U.S. imperialism and its running dogs is a new step by U.S. imperialism to expand the war in Laos, attempting to coordinate with its increasingly rampant military activities in Vietnam.
　　The statement pointed out that Prince Souvanna Phouma and Kong Le are increasingly surrendering to U.S. imperialism and serving the U.S. imperialist and right-wing plans to expand the war, causing increasing dissatisfaction among officers and soldiers in Kong Le's forces. Their policy of surrender will certainly bring extremely serious disasters to them and cause the situation in Laos to become increasingly serious.
　　The statement also called on the officers and soldiers in Kong Le's troops who have been forced to attack Phou Khout Mountain to quickly recognize the situation, rise up and oppose this order to attack Phou Khout Mountain. (Attached photo)
　　The anti-aircraft units of the Lao People's Liberation Army are on full alert. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Xue Tiejun.
