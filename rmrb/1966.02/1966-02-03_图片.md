    On February 2nd, Premier Zhou Enlai received the Guinean Economic Delegation headed by Baba Camara, Director of the Guinean Foreign Trade Bureau. Premier Zhou Enlai is having a cordial conversation with delegation leader Baba Camara (center). On the left is Guinean Ambassador to China, Camara Mamadi.
