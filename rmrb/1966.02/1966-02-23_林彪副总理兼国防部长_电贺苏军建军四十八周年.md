Title: Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao Sends Congratulations on the 48th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army

Author:

Date: 1966-02-23
Edition: 5

　　Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao
    Sends Congratulations on the 48th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army
    Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd - Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao sent a telegram today to Marshal R. Ya. Malinovsky, Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, congratulating him on the 48th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Marshal R. Ya. Malinovsky, Minister of Defense of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:
    On the occasion of the 48th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army, I would like to extend warm festive greetings to all officers and soldiers of the Soviet Army on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    May the Soviet Army, with its glorious history, maintain and develop its revolutionary traditions and make new contributions in the noble cause of defending the socialist revolution and construction achievements of the great Soviet people. May the great fighting friendship between the people and armies of China and the Soviet Union be tempered and shine even more brilliantly in the flames of the struggle against the most ferocious enemy of the people of the world, American imperialism.
          Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
                            Beijing, February 22, 1966

### 林彪副总理兼国防部长  电贺苏军建军四十八周年

1966-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　林彪副总理兼国防部长
    电贺苏军建军四十八周年
    新华社二十二日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪今天打电报给苏联国防部长罗·雅·马利诺夫斯基元帅，祝贺苏联军队建军四十八周年。电文如下：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟国防部长罗·雅·马利诺夫斯基元帅尊敬的部长同志：
    在苏联军队建军四十八周年的时候，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体指战员，向苏联军队全体官兵，致以热烈的节日祝贺。
    愿有着光荣历史的苏联军队，保持并发扬自己的革命传统，在保卫伟大的苏联人民的社会主义革命和建设成果的崇高事业中，作出新的贡献。愿中苏两国人民及其军队的伟大战斗友谊，在反对全世界人民最凶恶的敌人美帝国主义的斗争烈火中，得到锤炼，更加显示出灿烂的光辉。
          中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长　林彪
                            一九六六年二月二十二日于北京
