Title: Ceylon Journalists' Association Calls on President Sukarno to Protest Indonesian Rightists' Interference in AJAWA Affairs
Author:
Date: 1966-02-08
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, February 7th – The Ceylon Journalists' Association sent a telegram to Indonesian President Sukarno on the 5th, strongly protesting the interference by Indonesian right-wing forces in the internal affairs of the Asian-African Journalists' Association (AJAWA) and its secretariat, and the persecution of patriotic Indonesian journalists and newspapers.
    The telegram pointed out that AJAWA is an international organization that "has no relation to the internal affairs of any country, and no country or individual organization has the right to interfere in the organization's internal affairs." The telegram said, "This principle applies equally to Indonesia. Although the secretariat is located in Indonesia according to the decision of the First AJAWA Congress, Asian-African journalists respect the revolutionary spirit of the Indonesian people."
    The telegram demanded the immediate release of arrested Indonesian journalists, the lifting of the ban on the publication of patriotic newspapers, and the restoration of democratic life in Indonesia.
    On the 5th, the Ceylon Journalists' Association also wrote to the Indonesian Journalists' Association, strongly condemning the unreasonable actions taken by the new leadership of the Indonesian Journalists' Association against the AJAWA secretariat, and also condemning them for removing well-known journalists who actively participated in AJAWA work and a large number of patriotic journalists from their posts.
    On the same day, the Ceylon Journalists' Association expressed to the AJAWA secretariat its support for the secretariat's decision to meet temporarily in Beijing and to temporarily entrust the responsibilities normally assumed by the Acting Secretary-General to a collective.

### 锡兰新闻工作者协会致电苏加诺总统  抗议印度尼西亚右派干预亚非记协事务

1966-02-08
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰新闻工作者协会致电苏加诺总统
    抗议印度尼西亚右派干预亚非记协事务
    新华社科伦坡七日电　锡兰新闻工作者协会五日打电报给印度尼西亚总统苏加诺，强烈抗议印度尼西亚右派势力干预亚非新闻工作者协会及其书记处的内部事务，迫害印度尼西亚爱国的新闻工作者和报刊。
    电报指出，亚非新闻工作者协会是一个国际组织，它“同任何国家的内政没有关系，任何国家或任何个别组织都无权干预这个组织的内部事务”。电报说，“这个原则也同样适用于印度尼西亚。虽然根据亚非新闻工作者第一次代表大会的决定，把书记处设在印度尼西亚，而亚非新闻工作者是尊重印度尼西亚人民的革命精神的。”
    电报要求立即释放被逮捕的印度尼西亚新闻工作者，取消禁止爱国报刊出版的命令和恢复印度尼西亚的民主生活。
    锡兰新闻工作者协会五日还写信给印度尼西亚新闻工作者协会，强烈谴责印度尼西亚新闻工作者协会新领导对亚非新闻工作者协会书记处采取的无理举动，还谴责他们撤掉积极参加亚非新闻工作者协会工作的知名新闻工作者和大批爱国新闻工作者的职务。
    同一天，锡兰新闻工作者协会向亚非新闻工作者协会书记处表示，它支持书记处作出的到北京临时会合，并且暂时由集体来担负通常由代总书记担负的责任的决定。
