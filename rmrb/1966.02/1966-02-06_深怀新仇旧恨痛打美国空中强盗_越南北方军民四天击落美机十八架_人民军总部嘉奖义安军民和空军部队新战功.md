Title: Deeply Holding New and Old Grievances, Vigorously Beat American Aerial Bandits: North Vietnamese Army and People Shoot Down Eighteen American Planes in Four Days; People's Army Headquarters Commends the Military and Civilians of Nghe An and Air Force Units for New Combat Achievements
Author:
Date: 1966-02-06
Edition: 4

    Bearing deep new and old grievances, fiercely strike the American aerial bandits!
    The North Vietnamese army and people shot down eighteen American planes in four days
    People's Army Headquarters Commends Nghe An Military and Civilians and Air Force Units for New Combat Achievements
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 4th (via dispatch) – According to a comprehensive battle report released here on the 4th, from January 31st to February 3rd, the people and air defense forces in Nghe An, Quang Binh, Ha Tinh, and Vinh Linh in North Vietnam, together with the People's Army Air Force, shot down a total of eighteen American bandit aircraft and captured a batch of American pilots alive.

    On January 31st, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down three American aircraft, including one F-4 jet; the military and civilians of Nghe An Province shot down two American aircraft; the military and civilians of Ha Tinh Province shot down one American F-105 jet; and the military and civilians of the Vinh Linh area shot down one American AD-6 aircraft.

    On February 1st, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province shot down two American aircraft, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down one American AD-6 aircraft, and the troops guarding Hon Go Island shot down one American jet.

    On February 2nd, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province and the troops guarding Hon Go Island shot down two American aircraft.

    On February 3rd, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province and the People's Army Air Force engaged in a heroic battle against invading enemy aircraft, shooting down a total of five American jets and capturing a batch of American pilots alive.

    Thus, in the four days after American aircraft resumed bombing North Vietnam, the North Vietnamese army and people shot down a total of eighteen American aircraft.

    The above achievements have increased the total number of American bandit aircraft shot down by the North Vietnamese army and people from August 5, 1964, to February 3 of this year to eight hundred and seventy.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 5th (via dispatch) – The General Headquarters of the Vietnamese People's Army sent a telegram to the military and civilians of Nghe An Province and the Vietnamese People's Army Air Force units on the 4th, warmly commending their new combat achievements in shooting down five American aircraft on the 3rd.

    The telegram said, you have maintained a high degree of revolutionary vigilance and a spirit of constant readiness, resolutely annihilated the enemy, fought bravely, and cooperated closely, fought well, and continuously won victories.

    The telegram said, "This achievement once again embodies the unshakable determination of our military and civilians to defeat the American aggressors."

    The telegram also warmly commended and praised the achievements of the military and civilians of the Fourth Military Region, who have shot down a total of sixteen American aircraft since January 31 of this year.

    The telegram finally called on the military and civilians in these areas to deepen their hatred for the enemy, constantly raise their vigilance, quickly summarize experience, resolutely defeat the attacks of the American bandits, and strive to establish even more brilliant combat achievements.

    The Vietnamese *People's Daily* published a commentary on the 5th, warmly celebrating the new combat achievements of the military and civilians of Nghe An Province and the Vietnamese People's Army Air Force units in shooting down five American aircraft on the 3rd.

### 深怀新仇旧恨痛打美国空中强盗  越南北方军民四天击落美机十八架  人民军总部嘉奖义安军民和空军部队新战功

1966-02-06
第4版()
专栏：

    深怀新仇旧恨痛打美国空中强盗
    越南北方军民四天击落美机十八架
    人民军总部嘉奖义安军民和空军部队新战功
    新华社河内四日电　据这里四日公布的一项综合战报报道，从一月三十一日到二月三日的四天中，越南北方义安、广平、河静、永灵各地人民、防空部队和人民军空军，共打落美国强盗飞机十八架，活捉美国飞行员一批。
    一月三十一日，广平省军民打落美国飞机三架，其中有一架F—4型喷气式飞机；义安省军民打落美国飞机两架；河静省军民打落美国F—105型喷气式飞机一架；永灵地区军民打落美国AD—6型飞机一架。
    二月一日，义安省军民打落美国飞机两架，广平省军民打落美国AD—6型飞机一架，守卫昏果岛的部队打落美国喷气式飞机一架。
    二月二日，义安省军民和守卫昏果岛的部队打落美国飞机两架。
    二月三日，义安省军民和人民军空军对入侵敌机进行了英勇的战斗，共打落美国喷气式飞机五架，活捉美国飞行员一批。
    这样，越南北方军民在美国飞机恢复对越南北方轰炸后的四天中，共打落美机十八架。
    上述战果，使越南北方军民从一九六四年八月五日到今年二月三日打落美国强盗飞机的总数增至八百七十架。
    新华社河内五日电　越南人民军总司令部四日打电报给义安省军民和越南人民军空军部队，热烈嘉奖他们在三日打落五架美国飞机的新战功。
    电报说，你们保持了高度的革命警惕性和常备不懈的精神，坚决消灭敌人，勇敢战斗，紧密协作，打得好，接连赢得了胜利。
    电报说，“这一战功再一次体现了我国军民为战胜美国侵略者的不可动摇的决心。”
    电报还热烈嘉奖和表彰第四军区军民的战绩。他们从今年一月三十一日以来，共击落了十六架美国飞机。
    电报最后号召这些地区的军民加深对敌人的仇恨，不断提高警惕，迅速总结经验，坚决击败美国强盗的进攻，争取建立更辉煌的战功。
    越南《人民报》五日发表评论，热烈欢呼义安省军民和越南人民军空军部队三日击落美机五架的新战功。
