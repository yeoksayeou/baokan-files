Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Bombards Phu Cat Airport, Destroying 28 American Aircraft; Eighty American Aggressors and South Korean Puppet Troops Killed; Dishi Province Military and Civilians Capture Twelve Enemy Strongholds in Half a Month, Liberating Over 40,000 People
Author:
Date: 1966-02-15
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 13th (Electric Dispatch) – According to the South Vietnam Liberation Press Agency, on February 2nd, the South Vietnam Liberation Army bombarded the enemy's Phu Cat Airport north of Qui Nhon in Binh Dinh Province, destroying 28 American helicopters. The Liberation Army also killed eighty American aggressors and South Korean puppet troops, and destroyed five enemy trucks.
    The destruction of Phu Cat Airport seriously hampered the recent "mopping-up" operations carried out by the American and puppet troops in the Phu My district.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 14th (Electric Dispatch) – According to the South Vietnam Liberation Press Agency: In half a month from January 14th to 30th, the military and civilians of Dishi Province, South Vietnam, captured twelve enemy strongholds, liberating five townships with a population of over 40,000.
    In these battles, more than 260 enemies were eliminated, more than 150 various weapons and more than 50,000 rounds of ammunition were seized, and a 105 mm caliber artillery piece was destroyed, three iron bridges were blown up, a military vehicle was blown up, and one enemy aircraft was shot down and one damaged.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 12th (Electric Dispatch) – According to the South Vietnam Liberation Press Agency, on the night of January 18th, the Quang Nam Province Liberation Armed Forces, South Vietnam, simultaneously attacked many enemy strongholds in Tam Ky City, killing, wounding, and capturing a total of 223 enemies, including five American aggressors.
    In the battle to attack the Fourth Cooperative Zone, the Liberation Armed Forces killed 95 enemies, wounded 60, and captured 11 after only 30 minutes of combat.
    The Liberation Armed Forces also used mortars to bombard the bridgehead north of Tam Ky City, destroying many puppet army barracks and puppet regime offices, killing 57 enemies.
    In other news, on January 26th, guerrillas in Tuy Hoa County, Phu Yen Province, killed and wounded more than 50 South Korean puppet troops in Hoa Phong and Hoa My townships.

### 南越解放军炮轰符吉机场炸毁美机二十八架  打死美国侵略军和南朝鲜仆从军八十名  迪石省军民半月攻克敌人十二个据点解放四万多人口

1966-02-15
第3版()
专栏：

    南越解放军炮轰符吉机场炸毁美机二十八架
    打死美国侵略军和南朝鲜仆从军八十名
    迪石省军民半月攻克敌人十二个据点解放四万多人口
    新华社河内十三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方解放军二月二日炮轰平定省归仁以北敌人的符吉机场，击毁美国直升飞机二十八架。解放军还打死美国侵略者和南朝鲜仆从军八十名，击毁敌人卡车五辆。
    符吉机场的被毁严重地妨碍了美伪军最近在蓬山地区进行的“扫荡”。
    新华社河内十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方迪石省军民从一月十四日到三十日的半个月里，攻克了敌人的十二个据点，解放了拥有四万多人口的五个乡。
    在这些战斗中，共消灭敌人二百六十多名，缴获各种武器一百五十多件，子弹五万多发，并且摧毁一○五毫米口径的大炮一门，炸毁铁桥三座，炸毁军车一辆，打落打伤敌机各一架。
    新华社河内十二日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方广南省解放武装力量在一月十八日夜间，同时进攻三岐市敌军的许多据点，共打死打伤和俘虏敌人二百二十三名，其中包括五名美国侵略者。
    在进攻四协区的战斗中，解放武装力量仅经过三十分钟的战斗就打死敌人九十五名，打伤六十名，俘虏十一名。
    解放武装力量还用迫击炮猛烈轰击三岐市北面的桥头，摧毁伪军营房和伪政权办公处多座，打死敌人五十七名。
    另据报道，富安省绥和县游击队一月二十六日在和丰乡及和美乡打死打伤南朝鲜仆从军五十多名。
