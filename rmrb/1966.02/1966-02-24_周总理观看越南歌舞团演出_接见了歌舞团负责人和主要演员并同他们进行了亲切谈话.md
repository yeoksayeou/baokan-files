Title: Premier Zhou Watches Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe Performance, Receives Leaders and Principal Performers, and Holds Cordial Talks with Them
Author:
Date: 1966-02-24
Edition: 1

　　Premier Zhou Watches Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe Performance
　　Receives Leaders and Principal Performers and Holds Cordial Talks with Them
    Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd. Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council, watched the excellent performance of the Song and Dance Troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam tonight, and received the head of the Song and Dance Troupe Qu Hui Jin, deputy heads Fan Ting Liu, Jin Ping, and principal performers, and held cordial talks with them.
    Present at the performance and reception were Zhang Hanfu, Chu Tunan, Yan Jinsheng, Gao Yi, Sun Xiaocun, Ren Yinglun, Ma Sicong and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    Huang Bei, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and Le Songshan, Acting Head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front in China, were also present.
    Tonight, the Vietnamese literary and artistic fighters once again used revolutionary and passionate songs and dances to vividly reflect the heroic deeds of the Vietnamese army and people in resisting America and saving the nation, and portrayed the embarrassed situation of the American aggressors under the powerful blows of the people's war in South Vietnam. They also warmly praised the great fighting unity and fraternal friendship between the people of Vietnam and China through songs and dances. The dozens of wonderful programs performed tonight were warmly welcomed by the audience.
    After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai and responsible persons from relevant departments went onto the stage to meet with the Vietnamese artists and congratulate them on their successful performance. At this time, the Vietnamese artists applauded enthusiastically and sang the song "Liberate the South". (Photo attached)
    Premier Zhou Enlai took a group photo with the performers on stage after watching the performance of the Song and Dance Troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen

### 周总理观看越南歌舞团演出　接见了歌舞团负责人和主要演员并同他们进行了亲切谈话

1966-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理观看越南歌舞团演出
　　接见了歌舞团负责人和主要演员并同他们进行了亲切谈话
    新华社二十三日讯　中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来今天晚上观看了越南民主共和国歌舞艺术团的精采演出，并且接见了歌舞艺术团团长瞿辉瑾，副团长范庭六、团金平和主要演员，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    观看演出和接见时在座的，还有章汉夫、楚图南、颜金生、高沂、孙晓村、任映仑、马思聪等有关方面负责人。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北、越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长黎松山也在座。
    越南文艺战士们今晚再一次用饱含革命激情的歌舞，形象地反映了越南军民抗美救国的英雄事迹，描绘了美国侵略者在南越人民战争的强大打击下的狼狈情景。他们还用歌舞热烈歌颂了越中两国人民伟大的战斗团结和兄弟友谊。今晚演出的几十个精采节目，都受到观众的热情欢迎。
    周恩来总理和各有关方面负责人在演出结束后走上舞台，同越南艺术家们见面，祝贺他们演出成功。这时，越南艺术家们一边热烈鼓掌，一面唱起了《解放南方》歌曲。（附图片）
    周恩来总理观看越南民主共和国歌舞艺术团的演出后，上台和演员合影。
