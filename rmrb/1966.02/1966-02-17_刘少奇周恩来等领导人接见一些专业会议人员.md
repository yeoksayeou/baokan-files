Title: Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai and Other Leaders Receive Personnel from Some Professional Conferences
Author:
Date: 1966-02-17
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th – Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Deng Xiaoping, Li Fuchun, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, Bo Yibo, Ye Jianying and other leaders, this afternoon received personnel attending the National Industrial and Transportation Work Conference and the National Industrial and Transportation Political Work Conference, as well as personnel attending professional conferences convened in Beijing by the Ministry of Coal Industry, the Second Ministry of Machine Building, the Fourth Ministry of Machine Building, the Ministry of Finance, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the General Administration of Civil Aviation of China, the Travel and Tourism Administration, and the Committee for Historical Data Research of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 刘少奇周恩来等领导人接见一些专业会议人员

1966-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇周恩来等领导人接见一些专业会议人员
    新华社十六日讯　刘少奇、周恩来、邓小平、李富春、陆定一、康生、薄一波、叶剑英等领导人，今天下午接见出席全国工业交通工作会议和全国工业交通政治工作会议的人员，以及出席煤炭工业部、第二机械工业部、第四机械工业部、财政部、全国总工会、民用航空总局、旅行游览事业管理局、政协全国委员会文史资料研究委员会等单位在北京召开的专业会议的人员。
