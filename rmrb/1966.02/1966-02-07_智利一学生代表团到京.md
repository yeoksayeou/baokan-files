Title: A Chilean Student Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-02-07
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, February 3rd - A three-member delegation from the Student Union of the University of Concepción, Chile, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association for a visit to our country.



**Named Entities**

智利    Zhili (Chile)
康赛普西翁大学  Kangsaipuxiong Daxue (University of Concepción)
中拉友协  Zhong-La Youxie (Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association)
北京    Beijing
新华社  Xinhua News Agency

### 智利一学生代表团到京

1966-02-07
第3版()
专栏：

    智利一学生代表团到京
    新华社三日讯　应中拉友协邀请来我国访问的智利康赛普西翁大学学生联合会代表团一行三人，今天乘飞机到京。
