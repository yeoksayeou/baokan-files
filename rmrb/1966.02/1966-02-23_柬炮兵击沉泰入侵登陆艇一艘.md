Title: Cambodian Artillery Sinks Thai Invading Landing Craft

Author:

Date: 1966-02-23
Edition: 5

　　柬炮兵击沉泰入侵登陆艇一艘
    新华社金边二十二日电  据柬埔寨新闻社二十二日报道，二月十三日，柬埔寨炮兵部队在反击泰国侵略行动中击沉泰国ICM型登陆艇一艘。敌人在这次侵略中的伤亡是：被打死约三十人，其中包括这艘登陆艇上的一名军官，另外还有许多人被打伤和失踪。

Cambodian Artillery Sinks Thai Invading Landing Craft
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 22nd – According to a report from the Cambodian News Agency on February 22nd, on February 13th, Cambodian artillery forces sank a Thai ICM-type landing craft while counterattacking a Thai invasion. Enemy casualties in this invasion were: approximately thirty people killed, including an officer on the landing craft, in addition to many others wounded and missing.
