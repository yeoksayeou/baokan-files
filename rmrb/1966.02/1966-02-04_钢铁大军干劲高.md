Title: The Iron and Steel Army Has High Enthusiasm
Author: Wu Baozeng
Date: 1966-02-04
Edition: 6

　　War drums sound like thunder,
　　The ten-mile steel city is a sea of red,
　　Steel men and iron horses stir up hot waves,
　　Driving away the cold and ushering in spring.
　　Ushering in spring, spring comes early,
　　The iron and steel army has high enthusiasm,
　　Able to melt the snow in the cold winter,
　　Able to make the ice river surge.
　　The waves surge, a sea of red,
　　Molten iron and steel flowers compete in splendor.
　　We dare to attack the sky-high cutting edge,
　　We dare to explore the ocean's deep mysteries.
　　With lofty aspirations reaching the clouds,
　　Mao Zedong Thought as our guide,
　　Who dares to block the revolutionary torrent,
　　It will surely be buried under a thousand layers of waves.

### 钢铁大军干劲高
吴宝增
1966-02-04
第6版()
专栏：

    钢铁大军干劲高
    鞍山钢铁公司工人　吴宝增
  战鼓声声似雷鸣，
  十里钢城一片红，
  钢人铁马翻热浪，
  驱走严寒转来春。
  转来春，春来早，
  钢铁大军干劲高，
  能叫严冬雪融化，
  能叫冰河浪滔滔。
  浪滔滔，红一片，
  铁水钢花齐争艳。
  天高的尖端咱敢攻呵，
  海深的奥秘咱敢探。
  胸怀壮志凌云霄，
  毛泽东思想作指导，
  革命的洪流谁敢挡，
  千层浪下定埋掉。
