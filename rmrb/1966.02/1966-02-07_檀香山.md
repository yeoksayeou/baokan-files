Title: Honolulu
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-07
Edition: 4

    Honolulu, also known as Huonululu, is located on the island of Oahu in the Hawaiian Islands of the Pacific Ocean. It is the capital and largest port of the fiftieth state of the United States, the Hawaiian Islands. Due to its essentially central location in the mid-Pacific, shipping routes and air routes crossing the Pacific Ocean must pass through here, giving it important transportation and strategic significance.
    Since the war, the United States has intensified its pursuit of aggressive and war policies abroad, and Honolulu has become a lair for its military aggression in the Far East. The United States Armed Forces Pacific Command is located in Honolulu. Pearl Harbor, to its west, is one of the most important naval bases in the United States and is also the location of the Pacific Fleet Command under the United States Armed Forces Pacific Command. At present, U.S. imperialism is gradually shifting the focus of its global aggression strategy from Europe to Asia, and the Hawaiian Islands, including Honolulu, have become an important base for the U.S. "Pacific Strategy."
    Since U.S. imperialism launched the "Special War" in South Vietnam in 1961, Honolulu has also become a place where the U.S. authorities have intensified the planning of conspiracies for the war of aggression against Vietnam. Since that time, U.S. senior military and political leaders have held no fewer than ten military aggression conferences here. However, every Honolulu conference has failed to find a panacea to save the situation, and the United States is sinking deeper and deeper into the quagmire of the war of aggression against Vietnam, and is becoming increasingly uncontrollable.
                                                                                (Xinhua News Agency)

### 檀香山

1966-02-07
第4版()
专栏：新闻地理

    檀香山
    檀香山又名火奴鲁鲁，位于太平洋夏威夷群岛的阿胡岛上。它是美国第五十州的夏威夷群岛的首府和最大港口。由于它基本上位于太平洋中部的中心位置，横跨太平洋的船舶航线和航空线，都要通过这里，使它具有重要的交通和战略地位。
    战后以来，美国对外加紧推行侵略政策和战争政策，檀香山便成为它在远东进行军事侵略活动的巢穴。美国太平洋地区武装部队司令部就设在檀香山。在它西面的珍珠港是美国最重要的海军基地之一，也是美国太平洋地区武装部队司令部辖下的太平洋舰队司令部的所在地。目前，美帝国主义正在把它的全球侵略战略的重点逐步从欧洲转移到亚洲，包括檀香山在内的夏威夷群岛又成为美国“太平洋战略”的一个重要基地。
    自从美帝国主义一九六一年在南越发动“特种战争”以来，檀香山还成为美国当局加紧策划侵越战争阴谋的一个场所，从那个时候以来，美国高级军政头目在这里先后举行的军事侵略会议已不下十次之多。但是，每一次檀香山会议都没有找到挽救败局的灵丹妙药，美国在侵越战争的泥潭中却愈陷愈深，日益不可收拾了。
                （新华社）
