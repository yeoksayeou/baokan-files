Title: Spokesman of the Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Issues Statement Condemning the Philippines' Decision to Send Troops to Participate in the American War of Aggression Against Vietnam; Philippine Public Strongly Opposes the Government Providing Cannon Fodder for the US in its Aggression Against Vietnam

Author:

Date: 1966-02-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 10th -- The spokesman of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement on the 10th, strongly condemning the Philippine President for disregarding the opposition of Philippine public opinion and deciding to send 2,000 troops to South Vietnam to directly participate in the US imperialist war of aggression against the South Vietnamese people.

    The statement pointed out that although this dangerous decision of the Philippine President has not yet been approved by the Congress, it proves that the Philippine authorities are taking a new step forward in following the US policy of aggression against Vietnam. It is turning the Philippines into a logistical base for the US war of aggression against Vietnam and sending Philippine youth to South Vietnam as cannon fodder for the United States, realizing the American conspiracy to use Asians to fight Asians.

    The statement pointed out that the decision of the Philippine authorities is a blatant infringement on Vietnam's sovereignty and independence, violates the interests of the Philippine people, and will inevitably arouse public outrage among the working people and progressive public opinion in the Philippines.

    The statement said that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam strongly protests the above-mentioned decision of the Philippine authorities and declares that the Philippine government must bear full responsibility for the serious consequences caused by its direct participation in the US war against the Vietnamese people.

    Xinhua News Agency, February 10th -- Manila News: Philippine mass organizations and public opinion strongly oppose sending Philippine troops to South Vietnam to serve as cannon fodder in the US war of aggression.

    Edmundo Nolasco, President of the Philippine Film Workers Union, said on February 6th that the Philippines should not send its troops to South Vietnam, whether as combatants or "non-combatants." He pointed out that the Vietnamese people have always been victims of Western colonialism. The United States should not suppress the just demands of the Vietnamese people.

    The National Student Association of the Philippines recently submitted a declaration to Senate President Arturo Tolentino and House Speaker Cornelio Villareal. This declaration expresses opposition to providing military assistance to the Saigon puppet regime.

    Manila newspapers have continued to publish commentaries in recent days expressing opposition to the US forcing the Philippine authorities to send troops to South Vietnam.

    The Manila Times editorial on February 8th stated that Philippine public opinion is "increasingly opposed to undertaking unnecessary military obligations abroad." The newspaper denounced the United States for using coercive tactics, mentioning that the US side said that "if the Philippines sends troops to Vietnam, the United States will have the possibility of increasing aid." The newspaper said that Philippine soldiers should not shed blood for US "aid." "Even if we have no other sources of foreign aid," we can still survive. The newspaper also said that the United States has put pressure on the Philippines to obtain troops.

    An editorial in the newspaper on February 6th said, "Filipinos do not want to fight against Asians who are fighting for independence."

    Granada, a columnist for the Manila Chronicle, pointed out that the Americans' staying in Vietnam is "neither legal nor moral." He said, "The temptation to believe that intervention in Vietnam is for peace should be stopped."

    According to news recently published in Manila newspapers, among all twenty-four senators in the Philippine Senate, fourteen have expressed opposition to sending Philippine combat troops to help the United States conduct its war of aggression against Vietnam.

    According to Western news agencies, Philippine President Marcos agreed with his foreign and military advisors on the 5th that the Philippines would send 2,000 so-called "engineering personnel" and "security forces" to South Vietnam.

    The Manila Sunday Times commented on this matter on the 6th, saying that the Philippine government's dispatch of so-called "engineering personnel" to South Vietnam is a big "deception." Because "every place in South Vietnam is a battlefield," these personnel are actually participating in combat when they go to South Vietnam. The commentary said that any amount of US economic aid and loans "cannot compare to a drop of Filipino blood."

    The Manila Chronicle published a commentary on the 6th saying that Philippine President Marcos "has become the latest vassal of the United States in Southeast Asia."

### 越南外交部发言人发表声明  谴责菲律宾决定派兵参加美国侵越战争  菲律宾公众强烈反对政府为美国提供侵越炮灰

1966-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

    越南外交部发言人发表声明
    谴责菲律宾决定派兵参加美国侵越战争
    菲律宾公众强烈反对政府为美国提供侵越炮灰
    新华社河内十日电　越南民主共和国外交部发言人十日发表声明，强烈谴责菲律宾总统无视菲律宾舆论的反对，竟然决定派遣二千名军队去越南南方，直接参加美帝国主义反对越南南方人民的侵略战争。
    声明指出，菲律宾总统的这一危险决定虽然尚未得到国会通过，但它证明：菲律宾当局在追随美国对越南的侵略政策中正向前跨出新的一步。它正在把菲律宾变成美国侵越战争的后勤基地，并把菲律宾青年派到越南南方为美国充当炮灰，实现美国用亚洲人打亚洲人的阴谋。
    声明指出，菲律宾当局的决定，是粗暴地侵犯了越南的主权和独立，违背了菲律宾人民的利益，必然会在菲律宾劳动人民和进步舆论中引起公愤。
    声明说，越南民主共和国政府强烈抗议菲律宾当局的上述决定，并且宣布：菲律宾政府必须对它直接参加美国反对越南人民的战争引起的严重后果承担全部责任。
    据新华社十日讯　马尼拉消息：菲律宾群众团体和舆论强烈反对派菲律宾军队到南越去充当美国侵略战争的炮灰。
    菲律宾电影工会主席埃德孟多·诺拉斯科在二月六日说，菲律宾不应该派它的军队到南越去，不管是作为战斗人员还是“非战斗人员”。他指出，越南人一直是西方殖民主义的受害者。美国不应当压制越南人民的正义要求。
    菲律宾全国学生会最近把一项宣言交给了参议院议长阿图罗·托伦蒂诺和众议院议长科尔内略·比利亚雷亚尔。这项宣言表示反对给予西贡伪政权以军事援助。
    马尼拉各报在最近几天中继续发表评论，表示反对美国逼迫菲律宾当局出兵南越。
    《马尼拉时报》二月八日的社论说，菲律宾舆论“日益反对在国外承担不必要的军事义务”。这家报纸斥责美国使用了威逼利诱手段，它提到了美国方面说什么“如果菲律宾出兵越南，美国将有增加援助的可能”。报纸说，菲律宾的士兵不应当为美“援”流血。“即使我们没有其他外援来源”，还是可以照样活下去的。报纸还说，美国为了得到军队，已经对菲律宾施加压力。
    这家报纸在二月六日的一篇社论中说，“菲律宾人不要同那些正在争取独立而斗争的亚洲人作战”。
    《马尼拉纪事报》专栏作家格拉纳达指出，美国人呆在越南是“既不合法、又不道德的”。他说，“应该停止引诱人们相信干涉越南是为了和平。”
    据马尼拉报纸最近发表的消息说，在菲律宾参议院的全部二十四名参议员中，有十四人表示反对派遣菲律宾战斗部队帮助美国进行侵越战争。
    据西方通讯社报道，菲律宾总统马科斯同他的外交和军事顾问们在五日商定，菲律宾将派遣两千名所谓“工程人员”和“治安部队”到南越去。
    马尼拉《星期日时报》六日就这件事发表评论说，菲律宾政府派遣所谓“工程人员”到南越去是一个大“骗局”。因为“在南越每一个地方都是战场”，这些人员到南越去，实际上是参加战斗。评论说，任何数目的美国经济援助和贷款“都比不上菲律宾人的一滴血”。
    《马尼拉纪事报》六日发表评论说，菲律宾总统马科斯“已经变成了美国在东南亚的最新的侍从”。
