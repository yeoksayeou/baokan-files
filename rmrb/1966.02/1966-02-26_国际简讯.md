　　International Briefs
    Moroccan Cabinet Reorganized Again
    Rabat Dispatch: Moroccan King Hassan II appointed the former Minister of State, Mohammed Zeghari, as Deputy Prime Minister in charge of the Development Ministry on the 23rd. The former Minister of Development, Mohammed Cherkaoui, was appointed as Foreign Minister. The former Foreign Minister, Benhima, was reassigned as the Director of the Royal Cabinet.
    This is the second partial reorganization of the Moroccan cabinet in two days.
    Mauritanian Cabinet Reorganized
    Nouakchott Dispatch: Mauritanian President Daddah ordered the reorganization of the government on the 21st.
    In the new cabinet, Maloum Ould Brahim was appointed as Foreign Minister, the former President of the Supreme Court, Mohamed Lamine Ould Hamoni, as Minister of Interior and Justice, Ahmed Bezeid Ould Ahmed Miske as Minister of Defense, and Mohamed Salem Ould Moukeitirat as Minister of Finance, Planning and Public Affairs.

### 国际简讯

1966-02-26
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
    摩洛哥内阁再次改组
    拉巴特消息：摩洛哥国王哈桑二世二十三日任命原国务大臣穆罕默德·泽加里为负责发展部的副总理，原发展大臣穆罕默德·谢尔卡维为外交大臣。原外交大臣本·希马改任王室办公厅主任。
    这是两天来摩洛哥内阁的第二次局部改组。
    毛里塔尼亚内阁改组
    努瓦克肖特消息：毛里塔尼亚总统达达赫二十一日下令改组政府。
    在新内阁中，马卢姆·乌尔德·卜拉欣被任命为外交部长，前最高法院院长穆罕默德·勒明·乌尔德·哈莫尼为内政和司法部长，艾哈迈德·贝宰德·乌尔德·艾哈迈德·米斯克为国防部长，穆罕默德·萨勒姆·乌尔德·穆凯提拉特为财政、计划和公共事务部长。
