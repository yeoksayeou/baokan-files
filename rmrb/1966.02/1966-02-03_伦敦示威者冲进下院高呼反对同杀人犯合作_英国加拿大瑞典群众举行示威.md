Title: London Demonstrators Rush into House of Commons Shouting Against Collaboration with Murderers; Demonstrations Held by Masses in Britain, Canada, and Sweden
Author:
Date: 1966-02-03
Edition: 3

    According to a New China News Agency dispatch from London, February 2nd: British public opinion on the 1st launched a new wave of protests against the Johnson government's resumption of bombing of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and condemned the Labor government's support of American aggression. On the evening of the 1st, dozens of angry demonstrators in London rushed into the House of Commons while it was in session. In the visitors' gallery, they shouted slogans protesting against American aggression in Vietnam and the resumption of bombing. The demonstrators repeatedly stood up to question the members of Parliament: "Is the House of Commons going to discuss the bombing of Vietnam? Americans are slaughtering men, women and children," "You are guilty of murder." Some shouted: "We (Britain) are allies of some of the world's most extreme fascist governments, including the United States. We are cooperating with murderers." As police forced them out of the visitors' gallery, they shouted, "No one can silence us!"
    New China News Agency, Stockholm, February 2nd: A group of Swedish youth held a demonstration on the evening of the 1st to protest against the American resumption of bombing of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    The demonstrators first demonstrated in front of the American Embassy, holding up placards condemning America's barbarous actions. The demonstrators then marched to the residence of the American ambassador and presented the American ambassador with a letter of protest.

### 伦敦示威者冲进下院高呼反对同杀人犯合作  英国加拿大瑞典群众举行示威

1966-02-03
第3版()
专栏：

    伦敦示威者冲进下院高呼反对同杀人犯合作
    英国加拿大瑞典群众举行示威
    据新华社伦敦二日电　英国公众一日掀起一个新的抗议浪潮，反对约翰逊政府恢复轰炸越南民主共和国，并且谴责工党政府支持美国的侵略罪行。一日晚上，伦敦几十名愤怒的示威群众冲进正在开会的下院，他们在旁听席上高呼抗议美国侵略越南和恢复轰炸的口号。示威者不时站起来向议员们提出质问：“下院要讨论对越南的轰炸吗？美国人正在屠杀男男女女和儿童”，“你们犯有杀人罪”。有的人高呼：“我们（英国）是包括美国在内的世界上一些极端法西斯政府的同盟者。我们正在同杀人犯合作。”警察强迫他们退出旁听席的时候他们高呼：“谁也不能使我们沉默！”
    新华社斯德哥尔摩二日电　一批瑞典青年一日晚上举行示威，抗议美国恢复对越南民主共和国的轰炸。
    示威者首先在美国大使馆前示威，他们高举谴责美国的野蛮行为的标语牌。示威者接着游行到美国大使的住宅，向美国大使提出一封抗议信。
