Title: Japanese Communist Party Delegation Continues Visit in Guangzhou
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-17
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 16th (Xinhua) The Japanese Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, continued its visit in Guangzhou today.
    Comrade Kenji Miyamoto and some members of the delegation went to the Guangzhou Sports Club in the afternoon to watch a performance by outstanding Guangdong Province table tennis players.
    Japanese Communist Party delegation members Masayoshi Oka, Korehito Kurahara, Akira Yonehara, and Akira Kudo also visited the Dali People's Commune in Nanhai County, the Guangdong Folk Arts and Crafts Museum, and the South China Botanical Garden, respectively. In the evening, some members of the delegation watched the musical dance epic "The East is Red."
    Comrades Liu Ningyi, Wu Zhipu, Lin Liming, Zeng Sheng, Wang Lanxi, and Zhao Anbo accompanied the delegation on their visits today.

### 日共代表团在广州继续参观

1966-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    日共代表团在广州继续参观
    新华社广州十六日电　以日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治同志为首的日本共产党代表团，今天继续在广州参观访问。
    宫本显治同志和代表团的部分成员，下午前往广州体育俱乐部，观看了广东省优秀乒乓球运动员的表演。
    日本共产党代表团团员冈正芳、藏原惟人、米原昶、工藤晃等，今天还分别参观了南海县大沥人民公社、广东民间工艺馆和华南植物园。晚上，代表团的部分成员观看了音乐舞蹈史诗《东方红》。
    今天陪同代表团进行参观访问的，有刘宁一、吴芝圃、林李明、曾生、王阑西、赵安博等同志。
