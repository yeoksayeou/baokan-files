Title: Hungarian National Assembly Closes

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-01
Edition: 4

    Hungarian National Assembly Closes
    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 29th – According to Hungarian newspapers: The Hungarian National Assembly held a meeting here from the 27th to the 29th. The meeting discussed and approved the report on the 1966 national budget.


**Named Entities**


匈牙利	Xiongyali (Hungary)
匈牙利国民议会	Xiongyali Guomin Yihui (Hungarian National Assembly)
布达佩斯	Budapeisi (Budapest)

### 匈牙利国民议会闭幕

1966-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利国民议会闭幕
    新华社布达佩斯二十九日电　据匈牙利报纸报道：匈牙利国民议会二十七日到二十九日在这里举行了会议。会议讨论通过了关于一九六六年国家预算的报告。
