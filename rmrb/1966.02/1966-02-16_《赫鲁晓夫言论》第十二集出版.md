Title: Volume 12 of "Khrushchev's Speeches" Published
Author:
Date: 1966-02-16
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, February 15th. Volume 12 of "Khrushchev's Speeches" has been published by World Knowledge Publishing House and is being distributed throughout the country. This volume includes 91 publicly published speeches, talks, letters, telegrams, and answers to reporters' questions by Khrushchev from May to August 1959.

**Named Entities**

赫鲁晓夫  Hèlǔxiǎofū (Khrushchev)
世界知识出版社 Shìjiè Zhīshì Chūbǎnshè (World Knowledge Publishing House)
新华社    Xinhua News Agency

### 《赫鲁晓夫言论》第十二集出版

1966-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    《赫鲁晓夫言论》第十二集出版
    新华社十五日讯　《赫鲁晓夫言论》第十二集，已由世界知识出版社出版，在全国各地发行。这一集的内容，包括赫鲁晓夫在一九五九年五月至八月期间公开发表的讲话、谈话、函电、答记者问等，共九十一篇。
