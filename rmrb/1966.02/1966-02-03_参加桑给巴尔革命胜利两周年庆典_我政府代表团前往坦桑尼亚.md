Title: Our Government Delegation Leaves for Tanzania to Attend the Second Anniversary Celebration of the Zanzibar Revolution Victory
Author:
Date: 1966-02-03
Edition: 4

    To Attend the Second Anniversary Celebration of the Zanzibar Revolution Victory
    Our Government Delegation Leaves for Tanzania
    Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd. The Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Xu Jie, Vice Minister of Geology, left Beijing by plane this morning at the invitation of the United Republic of Tanzania to attend the celebrations for the second anniversary of the victory of the Zanzibar Revolution. The delegation members also included Zhou Zhaoyu, NPC deputy, Executive Committee Member of the All-China Women's Federation, Director of the Nanjing Women's Federation, and Jiang Baosheng, Vice Chairman of the Hunan Provincial Youth Federation.
    Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Ji Pengfei, Xu Guangyi, Gong Dafei, Shen Yuanhui, Qian Dawei, and other responsible persons from relevant departments. Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Wazir Juma, also saw the delegation off at the airport.

### 参加桑给巴尔革命胜利两周年庆典  我政府代表团前往坦桑尼亚

1966-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    参加桑给巴尔革命胜利两周年庆典
    我政府代表团前往坦桑尼亚
    新华社二日讯　中华人民共和国政府代表团在地质部副部长许杰率领下，应邀前往坦桑尼亚联合共和国参加桑给巴尔革命胜利两周年庆祝活动，今天上午乘飞机离开北京。代表团成员还包括全国人大代表、全国妇联执委、南京市妇联主任周兆瑜和湖南省青联副主席姜保胜。
    到机场送行的，有姬鹏飞、胥光义、宫达非、沈元辉、钱大卫等有关方面负责人。坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马也到机场送行。
