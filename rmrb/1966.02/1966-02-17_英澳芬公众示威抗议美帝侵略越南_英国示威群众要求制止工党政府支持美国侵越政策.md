Title: Public Demonstrations in Britain, Australia, and Finland Protest U.S. Aggression Against Vietnam; British Demonstrators Demand the Labour Government Cease Supporting U.S. Policies of Aggression Against Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-02-17
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, London Dispatch: Approximately four hundred British people braved the cold winds in Glasgow on the 12th to hold a demonstration against U.S. aggression in Vietnam.
    Members of the Rolls-Royce factory committee from Hillington participated in the demonstration, holding placards demanding "Stop U.S. Aggression."
    Thousands of pedestrians on the street expressed their support for the demonstrators. They shouted: "Let Vietnam decide its own future."
    In Ipswich, sixty-nine miles northeast of London, a group of demonstrators surrounded Labour Member of Parliament Dingle Foot on the 12th, demanding an end to the British Labour government's support for U.S. aggression in Vietnam.
    Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne Dispatch: Two hundred Australians held a demonstration outside the U.S. Consulate in Melbourne on the evening of the 11th to protest the U.S. resumption of bombing North Vietnam.
    Participants in the demonstration included pastors, freelancers, trade union members, and students.
    In a petition, the demonstrators demanded that the United States stop bombing.
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th dispatch: Helsinki dispatch: The Finnish people held a mass rally in the capital Helsinki on the 15th to protest U.S. aggression against Vietnam. The rally demanded that U.S. troops withdraw from South Vietnam.
    The rally also delivered a letter of protest to the U.S. Embassy in Finland.

### 英澳芬公众示威抗议美帝侵略越南  英国示威群众要求制止工党政府支持美国侵越政策

1966-02-17
第3版()
专栏：

    英澳芬公众示威抗议美帝侵略越南
    英国示威群众要求制止工党政府支持美国侵越政策
    新华社伦敦电　大约四百名英国人十二日冒着寒风在格拉斯哥举行示威，反对美国侵略越南。
    希灵顿的罗尔斯·劳埃斯工厂委员会成员参加了示威，他们拿着标语牌，要求“制止美国侵略”。
    在马路上的数以千计的行人表示支持示威者。他们高呼：“让越南决定自己的前途。”
    在伦敦东北六十九英里的伊普斯威奇，一群示威者十二日包围了工党议员丁格尔·富特，要求制止英国工党政府支持美国侵略越南。
    新华社墨尔本电　二百名澳大利亚人十一日晚在墨尔本美国领事馆外面举行示威，抗议美国恢复轰炸越南北方。
    参加示威的有牧师、自由职业者、工会会员和学生。
    示威者在一份请愿书中，要求美国停止轰炸。
    新华社十六日讯　赫尔辛基消息：芬兰人民十五日在首都赫尔辛基举行群众大会抗议美国侵略越南。大会要求美国军队从南越撤出去。
    大会还把一封抗议信交给美国驻芬兰大使馆。
