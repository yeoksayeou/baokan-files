　　International Briefs
    World Speed Skating Championships for Men and Women Concluded
    Gothenburg, Sweden Dispatch: The 1966 World Men's Speed Skating Championships concluded in Gothenburg, Sweden on the 20th. Athletes from the Netherlands, Sweden, Norway, and the Soviet Union secured the top six positions in the all-around competition, respectively. A total of thirty-seven athletes from fifteen countries participated in the competition.
    Prior to this, the 1966 World Women's Speed Skating Championships were held in Trondheim, Norway in mid-February. Soviet athlete Stenina and Korean athlete Kim Song-soon won the all-around title and runner-up, respectively.
    Syria Expands National Revolutionary Council
    Damascus Dispatch: The Syrian Presidential Council recently promulgated a decree amending the provisional constitution promulgated in April 1964, expanding the Syrian National Revolutionary Council.
    The expanded committee consists of one hundred and thirty-four people, while the committee established last August consisted of ninety-five people.
    The new committee includes new members of the regional leadership of the Arab Ba'ath Socialist Party and members of the Bitar cabinet formed on January 1.
    U.S. Mainland Population Exceeds 190 Million
    Washington Dispatch: According to a report by the U.S. Department of Commerce's Bureau of the Census on the 16th, as of January 1 this year, the U.S. mainland population was approximately 194,899,000.
    This figure represents an increase of 1.1 percent, or 2,122,000 people, compared to a year ago. It does not include the more than one million U.S. troops stationed overseas.

### 国际简讯

专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
    世界男女滑冰锦标赛已结束
    瑞典哥德堡消息：一九六六年世界男子速度滑冰锦标赛二十日在瑞典哥德堡结束。荷兰、瑞典、挪威和苏联选手分别获得了四项全能前六名。共有十五个国家的三十七名选手参加了这次比赛。
    在此以前，本月中旬在挪威特隆赫姆举行了一九六六年世界女子速度滑冰锦标赛。苏联选手斯捷尼娜和朝鲜选手金松顺分别获得四项全能冠军和亚军。
    叙利亚扩大全国革命委员会
    大马士革消息：叙利亚总统会议最近颁布法令，修改一九六四年四月公布的临时宪法，扩大叙利亚全国革命委员会。
    扩大后的委员会由一百三十四人组成，而去年八月成立的委员会是由九十五人组成的。
    新委员会包括阿拉伯复兴社会党的地区领导机构的新成员和一月一日组成的比塔尔内阁的成员。
    美国本土人口一亿九千多万
    华盛顿消息：据美国商务部国情普查局十六日报告，今年一月一日，美国本土人口约达一亿九千四百八十九万九千人。
    这项数字比一年前增加了百分之一点一，即二百十二万二千人。它不包括美国驻在海外的一百多万军队。
