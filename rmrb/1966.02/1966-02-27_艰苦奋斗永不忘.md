Title: Never Forget Hard Struggle

Author:

Date: 1966-02-27
Edition: 6

　　Never Forget Hard Struggle
　　Never Forget Revolutionary Tradition
　　Without houses, we sleep on the grassland,
　　Covering the sky and earth, our heads resting on bricks,
　　Never forget revolutionary tradition,
　　Strive to build the oilfield.

　　Good Housekeeper
　　The oilfield is our home,
　　We must cherish every nail and piece of wood,
　　Accumulate from small beginnings,
　　We must be good housekeepers.

　　Swinging the Great Hammer
　　Swinging the great hammer,
　　Pounding the yellow earth until oil flows;
　　Sweating like pouring rain,
　　Earthen walls rise above the head in the blink of an eye.
　　Swinging the great hammer,
　　Pounding the earth firm and the walls thick;
　　Today we move into the "rammed earth house,"
　　Better than red-tiled high buildings!

　　Overcoming Difficulties to Build the Oilfield
　　Oil workers study Mao Zedong's works,
　　Travel the world without fear of difficulty.
　　White clouds and stars as our brocade quilt,
　　The lawn as our bed, embracing our knees to sleep.
　　Everyone sets up a Foolish Old Man's ambition,
　　Overcoming difficulties to build the oilfield.

　　Save Funds to Extract Oil
　　No need for steel and cement,
　　Only earth and grass as materials,
　　Using local materials to build rammed earth houses,
　　Save funds to extract oil.

　　Revolutionary Heirloom
　　Straw sandals and sewing kit,
　　Revolutionary heirloom;
　　Seeing it, we don't forget our roots,
　　Revolutionary zeal is high.

　　What We Want is Thrift
　　Thinking of the collective,
　　Doing revolution,
　　Loving labor,
　　What we want is thrift.

　　Learn from the Red Army
　　Oil workers fear no difficulty,
　　We must learn from the Red Army climbing snow mountains,
　　Not afraid of the frozen ground three feet thick,
　　Enthusiasm is the icebreaker.

**Named Entities**

毛选	Mao Xuan (Selected Works of Mao Zedong)
红军	Hong Jun (Red Army)
干打垒  Gan Da Lei (Rammed earth construction)
愚公	Yugong (Foolish Old Man - from a Chinese fable about perseverance)

### 艰苦奋斗永不忘

1966-02-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　艰苦奋斗永不忘
　　革命传统永不忘
　　没有房子睡草原，
　　盖天铺地头枕砖，
　　革命传统永不忘，
　　奋发图强建油田。　　
　　好管家
　　油田就是咱的家，
　　一钉一木要爱它，
　　由少成多靠积累，
　　咱们要当好管家。
　　抡起大榔头
　　抡起大榔头，
　　砸得黄土冒油；
　　挥汗如淌雨，
　　土墙转眼过头。
　　抡起大榔头，
　　砸得土坚墙厚；
　　今朝住进“干打垒”，
　　赛过红瓦高楼！
　　战胜困难建油田
　　石油工人学毛选，
　　走遍天涯不畏难。
　　白云繁星当锦被，
　　草坪当床抱膝眠。
　　人人立下愚公志，
　　战胜困难建油田。
　　节约资金把油搞
　　钢筋水泥全不要，
　　只用土草作材料，
　　就地取材干打垒，
　　节约资金把油搞。
　　革命传家宝
　　草鞋针线包，
　　革命传家宝；
　　看到它不忘本，
　　革命干劲高。
　　要的是勤俭
　　想的是集体，
　　干的是革命，
　　爱的是劳动，
　　要的是勤俭。　　
　　学红军
　　石油工人不怕难，
　　要学红军爬雪山，
　　不怕地冻三尺厚，
　　干劲就是破冰船。
