Title: President Sukarno Announces Partial Cabinet Reshuffle; Nasution and Others No Longer Serve as Ministers in the Cabinet

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-24
Edition: 4

　　President Sukarno Announces Partial Cabinet Reshuffle
    Nasution and Others No Longer Serve as Ministers in the Cabinet
    (Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 23rd) – Indonesian President Sukarno announced a partial reshuffle of the Indonesian cabinet on the 21st.

    According to a report by Antara News Agency on the 22nd, the structure of the reshuffled cabinet is the same as before, that is, it consists of a cabinet presidium and fourteen ministerial groups. The cabinet presidium now has four deputy prime ministers. The Minister of Information is directly under the leadership of the presidium.

    The four deputy prime ministers are:
    First Deputy Prime Minister Subandrio
    Second Deputy Prime Minister Leimena
    Third Deputy Prime Minister Chairul Saleh
    Fourth Deputy Prime Minister Idham Chalid.

    The fifteen ministers appointed in the reshuffled government are:
    A.H. Sukarno, Engineer – Minister of Agriculture
    Suhadi, Engineer – Minister of National Research
    Sumardjo – Minister of Basic Education and Culture
    Madjuki Jatimm – Minister of Liaison between the Government and Islamic Scholars
    Major General Mursjid – Deputy Coordinating Minister of the Defense Ministry Group
    Air Vice Marshal Soeharnoko Harbani – Minister of Light Industry
    Major General Mulyadi – Ministerial Commander of the Navy
    Major General Hartono – Ministerial Deputy Commander of the Navy
    Basuki Rachmat – Minister of Veterans and Demobilization
    J.K. Tumakaka – Ministerial Secretary-General of the National Front
    Kurwet Kartaadiredja – Ministerial Engineering Manager of the Aviation Industry Command
    Muna’chat – State Minister, Presidential Advisor
    Lieutenant Colonel Syafei – State Minister, Special Security Affairs Advisor
    Asmara Hadi – Ministerial Deputy Speaker of the Cooperative Parliament
    Muhammad Hasan – Minister, Assistant Minister of Central Bank Affairs.

    The news agency said that the list of those who are no longer ministers in the cabinet is as follows:
    Coordinating Minister of the Defense and Security Ministry Group, Chief of Staff of the Armed Forces – Nasution
    Ministerial Commander of the Navy – Vice Admiral Martadinata
    Minister of National Research – Sudjono Djunedi Poesponegoro
    Minister of Basic Education and Culture – Mrs. Artati Marzuki Sudirdjo
    State Minister – Buki Soepeno
    Minister of Rural Development – Ipik Gandamana
    Coordinating Minister, Deputy Chairman of the Provisional People's Consultative Assembly – D.N. Aidit
    State Minister – Jodo
    Minister, Deputy Speaker of the Cooperative Parliament – Rukman
    Coordinating Minister, Speaker of the Cooperative Parliament Arudji Kartawinata was also excluded from the cabinet.

    The new cabinet established some new ministries. They are:
    The Tourism Affairs Ministry, headed by Sultan Hamengku Buwono (who is currently the Coordinating Minister of the Development Ministry Group)
    The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, headed by Air Vice Marshal Suriadarma.

    At the same time, the Defense and Security Ministry Group and the Armed Forces General Staff have been changed to the Defense Ministry Group, which is now led by Coordinating Minister Major General Sabini.

    The news agency said that some ministers have also been changed, for example:
    The Coordinating Ministry Group of Law and Internal Affairs was previously headed by Minister Wirjono Prodjodikoro, and the current Coordinating Minister is Sjaefroeddin, but Sjaefroeddin retains his position as the First Deputy Chairman of the Supreme Advisory Council. Wirjono Prodjodikoro became Minister and Chief Justice of the Supreme Court.
    Coordinating Minister of Finance Affairs Soemarno was replaced by Dr. Soeharto
    Soemarno became Coordinating Minister of National Development Planning. Coordinating Minister of the Ministry Group of Public Works and Power Suprajo has been replaced by Engineer Sutami (also Commander of the Emerging Forces Building Engineering Command). Major General Suprajo became the Chairman of the Audit Board.
    Minister of Basic Industry Hadi Thajeb has been changed. His position was given to former Minister of Light Industry Muhammad Jusuf. The position of Minister of Light Industry was given to Air Vice Marshal Soeharnoko
    The Ministry of Rural Development is headed by Engineer Soelachman, who is also the Minister of Irrigation
    The Ministry of Land Transportation, Posts and Telecommunications, and Tourism has been divided into three ministries. They are the Ministry of Land Transportation, headed by Lieutenant General Hidajat, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, headed by Air Vice Marshal Suriadarma, and the Ministry of Tourism, headed by Sultan Hamengku Buwono IX.
    The Ministry of Higher Education and Science is now headed by Acting Minister Leimena. The Ministry of Oil and Natural Gas is headed by Brigadier General Ibnu Sutowo.

    The news agency said that the Speaker of the Cooperative Parliament is now Soebami, and the deputy speakers are Sjarif Thajeb, Asmara Hadi, Musalin, and Sjahru.
    Other ministries remain unchanged.

### 苏加诺总统宣布局部改组内阁  纳苏蒂安等不再在内阁中担任部长职务

1966-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏加诺总统宣布局部改组内阁
    纳苏蒂安等不再在内阁中担任部长职务
    新华社二十三日讯　雅加达消息：印度尼西亚总统苏加诺二十一日宣布了印度尼西亚内阁的局部改组。
    据安塔拉通讯社二十二日报道，改组后的内阁的体制同过去一样，即它由一个内阁主席团和十四个部组组成。内阁主席团现在有四位副总理。宣传部长直接受主席团领导。
    四位副总理是：
    第一副总理苏班德里约
    第二副总理莱梅纳
    第三副总理哈鲁尔·萨勒
    第四副总理伊达姆·哈利德。
    在改组后的政府中被任命的十五位部长是：
    A·H·苏加诺工学士——农业部长
    苏哈迪工学士——国家研究部长
    苏马佐——基础教育和文化部长
    马朱基·亚蒂姆——政府同伊斯兰教学者联络部长
    穆尔西德少将——国防部组副统筹部长
    苏哈诺科·哈巴尼空军上将——轻工业部长
    穆尔亚迪少将——部长级海军司令
    哈托诺少将——部长级海军副司令
    巴苏基·拉赫马特——退伍军人和复员部长
    J·K·图马卡卡——部长级民族阵线秘书长
    库尔韦特·卡尔塔阿迪雷查——部长级航空工业指挥部工程经理
    穆纳查特——国务部长、总统顾问
    沙菲伊中校——国务部长、特别治安事务顾问
    阿斯马拉·哈迪——部长级合作国会副议长
    穆罕默德·哈桑——部长、中央银行事务部长助理。
    这家通讯社说，不再在内阁中任部长的名单如下：
    国防和安全部组统筹部长、武装部队参谋长——纳苏蒂安
    部长级海军司令——马塔迪纳塔海军中将
    国家研究部长——苏佐诺·朱内德·普斯波内戈罗
    基础教育和文化部长——阿塔蒂·马儒基·苏迪尔佐夫人
    国务部长——布基·苏佩诺
    农村发展部长——伊皮克·甘达曼纳
    统筹部长、临时人民协商会议副主席——迪·努·艾地
    国务部长——约多
    部长、合作国会副议长——鲁克曼
    统筹部长、合作国会议长阿鲁季·卡塔威纳塔也被排除出内阁。
    新内阁设立了一些新部。它们是：
    以苏丹·哈孟库·布沃诺（他现任发展部组统筹部长）为首的游览事务部
    以苏里亚达马空军上将为首的邮电部。
    同时，国防和安全部组和武装部队参谋部已改为国防部组，现在由统筹部长萨比尼少将领导。
    这家通讯社说，有些部长也作了改动，例如：
    法律和内政统筹部组从前由维尔约诺·普罗佐迪科罗任部长，现在的统筹部长是沙多诺，但是沙多诺保留了最高评议院第一副主席的职位。维尔约诺·普罗佐迪科罗成了部长、最高法院院长
    财政事务统筹部长苏马诺被苏哈托博士代替
    苏马诺成了国家建设计划统筹部长。公共工程和动力部组统筹部长苏普拉约基已被苏塔米工程师代替（兼新兴力量大厦工程指挥部指挥），苏普拉约基少将成了审稽机构的主席
    基本工业部长哈迪·塔耶布已变动。他的职位给了前轻工业部长穆罕默德·优素福。轻工业部长的职位给了苏哈诺科空军少将
    农村发展部交由苏拉赫曼工程师负责，他同时又是灌溉部长
    陆运、邮电和游览部已分成三个部。它们是由希达雅特中将为首的陆运部，以苏里亚达马空军上将为首的邮电部和以苏丹·哈孟库·布沃诺九世为首的游览部。
    高等教育和科学部现在以莱梅纳临时部长为首。石油和天然气部则以伊布努·苏托沃准将为首。
    这家通讯社说，合作国会议长现在是苏巴米亚，副议长是沙里夫·塔耶布、阿斯马拉·哈迪、穆萨林、夏胡
    其他部不变。
