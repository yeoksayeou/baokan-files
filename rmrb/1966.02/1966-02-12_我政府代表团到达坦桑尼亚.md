Title: Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Tanzania
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-12
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Dar es Salaam, 10th - The government delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Xu Jie, Vice Minister of Geology, arrived in Dar es Salaam on the 10th. They are visiting Zanzibar at the invitation of the Tanzanian government to participate in the celebrations of the second anniversary of the Zanzibar revolution.
    The Zanzibar Revolution Day was originally January 12th, but the celebrations were postponed to this week due to the Muslim Ramadan.

### 我政府代表团到达坦桑尼亚

1966-02-12
第5版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团到达坦桑尼亚
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆十日电　由地质部副部长许杰率领的中华人民共和国政府代表团十日到达达累斯萨拉姆。他们是应坦桑尼亚政府的邀请前往桑给巴尔参加桑给巴尔革命胜利两周年的庆祝活动的。
    桑给巴尔革命节原来是一月十二日，由于穆斯林斋戒节，庆祝活动推迟到本周举行。
