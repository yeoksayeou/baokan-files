Title: Zhou Enlai and Deng Xiaoping Pay Condolences to Vice Chairman Chen Shutong

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-20
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 19th. Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, Premier of the State Council, and Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, went to the Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park this morning to pay condolences to Vice Chairman Chen Shutong.
    Peng Zhen, Kang Sheng, Lin Feng, and Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Bo Yibo, Vice Premier of the State Council; Ye Jianying, Gao Chongmin, Cai Tingkai, Li Siguang, Fu Zuoyi, Shen Yanbing, Li Zhuchen, Xu Deheng, and Li Dequan, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the CPPCC; Yang Xiufeng, President of the Supreme People's Court; and Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate also went to the Zhongshan Hall today and yesterday to pay condolences. Rong Yiren, Huang Changshui, Hu Ziang, Le Songsheng, and Luo Shuzhang, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce; and Ji Fang, Chen Qiyou, and Su Ziheng, responsible persons of various democratic parties, also went to pay condolences.
    More than 1,000 people, including cadres of the organs of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the State Council, and the National Committee of the CPPCC, standing committee members and executive committee members of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, responsible persons of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, and friends of Vice Chairman Chen Shutong during his lifetime, also went to the Zhongshan Hall to pay condolences. Envoys and diplomatic officials from some countries stationed in China also went to the Zhongshan Hall to pay condolences to Vice Chairman Chen Shutong and presented wreaths.

### 周恩来邓小平吊唁陈叔通副委员长

1966-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来邓小平吊唁陈叔通副委员长
    新华社十九日讯　中共中央副主席、国务院总理、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席周恩来，中共中央总书记、国务院副总理邓小平，今天上午前往中山公园中山堂吊唁陈叔通副委员长。
    今天和昨天前往中山堂吊唁的，还有人大常委会副委员长彭真、康生、林枫、刘宁一，国务院副总理薄一波，政协全国委员会副主席叶剑英、高崇民、蔡廷锴、李四光、傅作义、沈雁冰、李烛尘、许德珩、李德全，最高人民法院院长杨秀峰，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞。前往吊唁的，还有中华全国工商业联合会副主任委员荣毅仁、黄长水、胡子昂、乐松生、罗叔章，各民主党派负责人季方、陈其尤、苏子蘅等。
    人大常委会、国务院、政协全国委员会的机关干部，全国工商联的常务委员、执行委员和北京市工商联的负责人，以及陈叔通副委员长的生前友好，共一千多人，也前往中山堂吊唁。部分国家驻中国的使节和外交官员，也到中山堂吊唁陈叔通副委员长，并献了花圈。
