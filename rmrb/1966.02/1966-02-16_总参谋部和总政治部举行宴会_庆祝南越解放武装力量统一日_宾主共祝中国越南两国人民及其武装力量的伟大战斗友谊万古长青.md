Title: General Staff Department and General Political Department Hold Banquet to Celebrate South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces Unification Day; Guests and Hosts Jointly Wish the Great Militant Friendship Between the Chinese and Vietnamese People and Their Armed Forces Will Last Forever

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-16
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 15th—The General Staff Department and the General Political Department of the Chinese People’s Liberation Army held a banquet tonight to celebrate the 5th anniversary of the Unification Day of the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces.
    At the banquet, which was filled with fraternal friendship, Li Tianyou, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People’s Liberation Army, Xu Liqing, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, and others, had cordial conversations and reminisced about their friendship with Le Tung Son, Acting Head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China, Hoang Bac, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and others. Li Tianyou and Le Tung Son delivered toasts at the banquet. They proposed a toast to the great militant friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese people and their armed forces lasting forever.
    Nguyen Thuan and Tran Ky Hai, Secretaries of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China, Colonel Tran Van Peng, Military Attaché of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and others, were also invited to attend the banquet.
    Gathering with the Vietnamese comrades tonight were also Zhang Chi Ming, Zhao Qimin, Cao Li Huai, Wang Chuanbin, Dong Xuelin, Pan Zhenwu and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 总参谋部和总政治部举行宴会  庆祝南越解放武装力量统一日  宾主共祝中国越南两国人民及其武装力量的伟大战斗友谊万古长青

1966-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    总参谋部和总政治部举行宴会
    庆祝南越解放武装力量统一日
    宾主共祝中国越南两国人民及其武装力量的伟大战斗友谊万古长青
    新华社十五日讯　中国人民解放军总参谋部和总政治部今晚举行宴会，庆祝越南南方解放武装力量统一日五周年。
    在洋溢着兄弟情谊的宴会上，中国人民解放军副总参谋长李天佑、总政治部副主任徐立清等同越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长黎松山、越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北等，亲切交谈，畅叙友情。李天佑和黎松山在会上先后祝酒。他们提议，为中国越南两国人民及其武装力量的伟大战斗友谊万古长青而干杯。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团秘书阮顺和、陈奇海，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆武官陈文彭上校等，也应邀出席了宴会。
    今晚同越南同志们欢聚的，还有张池明、赵启民、曹里怀、王传斌、董学林、潘振武等有关方面负责人。
