Title: Our "Red Flag" Magazine Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1966-02-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 17th: The Chinese "Red Flag" magazine delegation, led by Fan Ruoyu, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Red Flag" magazine, arrived in Hanoi on the afternoon of the 17th.
    The delegation is visiting Vietnam at the invitation of the editorial department of "Learning" magazine, the central political theory publication of the Vietnamese Labor Party.
    Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included: Tao Duy Song, Editor-in-Chief of "Learning" magazine; Tran Huy Lieu, Vice President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association; Song Shuang, Deputy Director of the Foreign Liaison Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party Central Committee; Tran Tong, Vice Principal of the Nguyen Ai Quoc Party School; Liu Guiqi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee; Nguyen Thanh Le, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "People's Daily"; Hoang Van Hien, Vice Chairman of the Vietnam Journalists Association; Hoang Tung, Director of the Vietnam News Agency; and Pham Van Hao, Acting Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Chen Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, also greeted the delegation at the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency, February 17th: The "Red Flag" magazine delegation, led by Fan Ruoyu, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Red Flag" magazine, left Beijing by plane today to visit Vietnam at the invitation of the editorial department of the Vietnamese "Learning" magazine. Members of the delegation include Xu Xing, member of the editorial board of "Red Flag" magazine, and Yao Luo, head of the international section.
    Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included: Hu Sheng, Wang Li, and Xu Liqun, Deputy Editors-in-Chief of "Red Flag" magazine; Wu Lengxi, Editor-in-Chief of "People's Daily"; and Yao Zhen, Deputy Minister of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee. Hoang Bac, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 我《红旗》杂志代表团到达河内

1966-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    我《红旗》杂志代表团到达河内
    新华社河内十七日电　由《红旗》杂志副总编辑范若愚率领的中国《红旗》杂志代表团，十七日下午到达河内。
    代表团是应越南劳动党中央政治理论刊物《学习》杂志编辑部的邀请，前来越南进行访问的。
    到机场欢迎的有：《学习》杂志总编辑陶维松，越中友好协会副会长陈辉燎，越南劳动党中央对外联络委员会副主任双松，阮爱国党校副校长陈宋，对外文化联络委员会副主任刘贵琪，《人民报》副总编辑阮成黎，越南新闻工作者协会副主席黄文戬，越南通讯社社长黄俊，外交部新闻司代理司长范文好等。中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陈亮也到机场迎接。
    新华社十七日讯　由《红旗》杂志副总编辑范若愚率领的《红旗》杂志代表团，应越南《学习》杂志编辑部邀请，今天乘飞机离开北京前往越南访问。代表团团员有《红旗》杂志编委徐荇、国际组组长姚洛。
    到机场送行的有：《红旗》杂志副总编辑胡绳、王力、许立群，《人民日报》总编辑吴冷西，中共中央宣传部副部长姚溱等。前往机场欢送的还有越南驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北等。
