Title: US Progressive Publication Calls on American People to Form a Broad Alliance Against the War of Aggression Against Vietnam, Pointing Out That the War of Aggression Against Vietnam Will Impose Severe Sacrifices and Suffering on the American People
Author:
Date: 1966-02-07
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 6th -- The latest issue of the magazine "Free Student," published by the "May 2nd Movement" in the United States, has published an editorial calling on people from all walks of life in the United States to form a broad anti-war alliance to resolutely struggle against the US government's policy of expanding its war of aggression abroad and intensifying persecution and suppression of the working people at home.
    This magazine pointed out that US imperialism is plotting to expand the flames of the war of aggression against Vietnam to North Vietnam, Laos, Cambodia, Thailand, and even China. The editorial stated, "This war will involve at least 500,000 American troops. ... We will see a full-scale war being waged in Southeast Asia," and then "This war will escalate into a war with China."
    The editorial exposed that one of the purposes of the Johnson administration's Christmas "peace offensive" was to "attempt to further confuse the American people, especially the anti-war movement, with the claim of seeking 'decent negotiations,' so as to make them do nothing."
    The editorial pointed out that people from all walks of life in the United States "are beginning to feel the pressure" of the war of aggression against Vietnam. "In addition to the large-scale conscription of black and white working-class youth, the US government will also conscript thousands of students to fight. Federal and local money... will also be invested in war activities against Vietnam. Few people other than government, military, and business figures can escape the impact of this war."
    The editorial emphasized that there is an objective basis for organizing a broad alliance against the Vietnam War. This objective basis is determined by the fact that "the war itself will impose severe sacrifices and suffering on a broad stratum of the American people: widows produced by the war, interruption of education, government undermining strikes in the name of 'national interest,' depriving citizens of their rights, and throwing fighters who oppose and attack the current system and government into prison or killing them." The editorial emphasized that "When our activities conflict with the interests of the government, we should not expect it to treat us with greater mercy than it treats the Vietnamese people." The editorial concluded, "We must build a peace movement that matches the strength of the (American ruling group). As the war expands, we must correspondingly assume more obligations and intensify organizational work in schools and in communities."

### 美进步刊物向美国人民发出号召  组成广泛的反对侵越战争联盟  指出侵越战争将把严重的牺牲和痛苦强加给美国人民

1966-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

    美进步刊物向美国人民发出号召
    组成广泛的反对侵越战争联盟
    指出侵越战争将把严重的牺牲和痛苦强加给美国人民
    新华社六日讯　纽约消息：美国“五月二日运动”出版的最新一期《自由学生》杂志发表一篇社论，号召美国各阶层人士结成广泛的反战联盟，为反对美国政府对外扩大侵略战争、对内加紧迫害与镇压劳动人民的政策而坚决斗争。
    这家杂志指出，美帝国主义正阴谋把侵越战火扩大到越南北方、老挝、柬埔寨、泰国、甚至中国。社论说，“这场战争至少将卷入五十万美国军队。……我们将看到一场在东南亚地区进行的全面战争”，然后“这场战争将升级为一场同中国的战争”。
    社论揭露约翰逊政府发动圣诞节“和平攻势”的目的之一就是“妄图用所谓谋求‘体面谈判’的主张来进一步迷惑美国人民，特别是反战运动，使他们无所作为”。
    社论指出，美国各阶层人民“正开始感受到（侵越战争的）压力”。“除了已经大规模征召黑人和白人工人阶级的青年入伍以外，美国政府还将征召数以千计的学生去作战。联邦和地方的钱……也将投入对越南的战争活动。除了政府、军方和企业界人士之外，很少人能逃脱这场战争正在发生的影响。”
    社论强调，目前存在着组织一个广泛的反对越南战争联盟的客观基础。而这种客观基础是由下面这一事实决定的，即：“战争本身将把严重的牺牲和痛苦强加在美国广大阶层人民身上：战争中产生的寡妇、教育的中断、政府以‘国家利益’为名破坏罢工，剥夺公民权利，把反对和攻击现制度和现政府的斗士投入监狱或加以杀害”。社论强调说，“当我们的活动同政府的利益相冲突的时候，我们不应当指望它（对待我们）会比对待越南人民有更大的仁慈”。社论最后说，“我们必须建立一个同（美国统治集团的）那种实力相匹敌的和平运动。随着战争的扩大，我们必须相应地承担更多义务，并且加紧进行在学校里和在居民中的组织工作。”
