Title: China and Romania Sign New Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-02-12
Edition: 5

    China and Romania Sign New Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan
    Xinhua News Agency, February 11th: The 1966-1967 Implementation Plan for the China-Romania Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Beijing today.
    Relevant personnel from China and Romania conducted friendly and comradely discussions in Beijing from the 7th to the 9th regarding the signing of this plan and reached an agreement. According to the plan, China and Romania will further develop cooperation in science, education, culture, art, news, radio, film, health, and sports from 1966 to 1967. During the implementation of the plan, both sides will exchange delegations and artistic performance troupes in education, culture, art, health, and news. The two sides will also hold art and photo exhibitions and exchange relevant materials.
    Li Qitao, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, signed on behalf of the Chinese side; Dumitru Gheorghiu, Romanian Ambassador to China and head of the Romanian cultural delegation, signed on behalf of the Romanian side.
    After the signing ceremony, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a banquet.
    Chinese officials present at the signing ceremony and banquet included: Chu Tunan, Wang Bingnan, Liu Zizai, Cui Yitian, Li Qi, Zhou Xinwu, and others.
    Romanian officials present at the signing ceremony and banquet included: all members of the Romanian cultural delegation and officials from the Romanian Embassy in China.

### 中罗签订新的文化合作协定执行计划

1966-02-12
第5版()
专栏：

    中罗签订新的文化合作协定执行计划
    新华社十一日讯　中国—罗马尼亚文化合作协定一九六六至一九六七年执行计划今天在北京签字。
    中罗两国的有关人员七日到九日在北京就签订这个计划进行了同志式的友好商谈，并达成了协议。按这个计划规定，中罗两国一九六六至一九六七年将进一步发展科学、教育、文化、艺术、新闻、广播、电影、卫生和体育等方面的合作。在执行计划期间，双方将交换教育、文化、艺术、卫生、新闻等方面的代表团和艺术表演团。双方还将互相举办美术和图片展览以及交换有关资料。
    代表中方签字的是对外文化联络委员会副主任李琦涛；代表罗方签字的是罗马尼亚驻中国大使、罗马尼亚文化代表团团长杜米特鲁·乔治乌。
    签字后，对外文委举行了宴会。
    中方出席签字仪式和宴会的各有关方面负责人有：楚图南、王炳南、刘子载、崔义田、李琦、周新武等。
    罗方出席签字仪式和宴会的有：罗马尼亚文化代表团全体成员和罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆官员。
