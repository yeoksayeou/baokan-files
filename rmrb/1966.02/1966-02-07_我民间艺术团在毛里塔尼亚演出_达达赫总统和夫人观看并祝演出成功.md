Title: Our Folk Art Troupe Performs in Mauritania; President Daddah and His Wife Watch and Congratulate on the Success of the Performance

Author:
Date: 1966-02-07
Edition: 3

    Our Folk Art Troupe Performs in Mauritania
    President Daddah and His Wife Watch and Congratulate on the Success of the Performance
    (Xinhua News Agency, Nouakchott) The Chinese Folk Art Troupe gave its premiere performance in Nouakchott, the capital of Mauritania, on the evening of January 24. Mauritanian President Moktar Ould Daddah and his wife watched the performance.
    Also watching the performance were Mauritanian Minister of Foreign Affairs and National Defense Mohamed Ould Cheikh, Minister of the Seal, Justice, and Interior Ahmed Ould Mohamed Salah, member of the Political Bureau of the Mauritanian People's Party Ba Ould Ne, and other prominent figures.
    Chinese Ambassador to Mauritania, Lü Zhixian, and diplomatic envoys from other countries also watched the performance.
    At the end of the performance, President Moktar Ould Daddah and his wife went on stage, shook hands with the Chinese artists one by one, congratulated them on the success of the performance, and took pictures with them.

### 我民间艺术团在毛里塔尼亚演出  达达赫总统和夫人观看并祝演出成功

1966-02-07
第3版()
专栏：中国文化在国外

    我民间艺术团在毛里塔尼亚演出
    达达赫总统和夫人观看并祝演出成功
    据新华社努瓦克肖特电　中国民间艺术团一月二十四日晚上在毛里塔尼亚的首都努瓦克肖特举行首次演出，毛里塔尼亚总统莫克塔·乌尔德·达达赫和夫人观看了演出。
    观看演出的还有毛里塔尼亚外交部长兼国防部长穆罕默德·乌尔德·谢赫，掌玺、司法部长兼内政部长艾哈迈德·乌尔德·穆罕默德·萨拉赫，毛里塔尼亚人民党政治局委员巴·乌尔德·奈等知名人士。
    中国驻毛里塔尼亚大使吕志先和其他国家的外交使节也观看了演出。
    演出结束时，莫克塔·乌尔德·达达赫总统和夫人走上舞台，同中国艺术家一一握手，祝贺演出成功，并且同他们一起照了相。
