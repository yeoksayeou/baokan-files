Title: US Resumption of Bombing Vietnam Provokes Public Outrage; People of Western European Countries Rise Up in Protest; Public Demonstrations in France, Holland, Spain, West Germany, Switzerland, etc., Denounce Johnson as Executioner
Author:
Date: 1966-02-05
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency: The public in Western Europe have gone to the locations of U.S. diplomatic missions in various countries to hold demonstrations, protesting the U.S. resumption of bombing North Vietnam.

    Over a thousand Parisians marched and demonstrated in front of the U.S. Embassy on the 3rd. At that time, the iron gates of the U.S. Embassy were tightly closed, and the office lights were turned off. The demonstrating masses gathered in the square repeatedly shouted: "Johnson is a murderer!" Many people waved placards while shouting slogans. The placards read: "Long live the National Liberation Front!" "U.S. troops get out of Vietnam!" "Americans go home!" "Stop the bombing!" Representatives of the demonstrating masses submitted a protest letter to the U.S. Embassy.

    In Vienna, the capital of Austria, hundreds of members of the public marched and demonstrated on the main streets of the city on the evening of the 2nd. The demonstrators held placards with the words: "Americans, get out of Vietnam." They carried a black coffin with the inscription: "American bombs are massacring Vietnamese women and children." The demonstrators dispatched a delegation to the U.S. Embassy to submit a resolution demanding that the U.S. stop the Vietnam War.

    In Amsterdam, Netherlands, about two hundred young people demonstrated in front of the U.S. Consul General's residence and also submitted a letter of protest.

    On the evening of the 1st, outside the American House in Hanover, West Germany, the masses held a march and demonstration, demanding that the U.S. stop its war of aggression against Vietnam. The police attacked the demonstrators and also arrested several people. The West German public also protested against the West German government's disregard for public condemnation and its preparation to send a "medical ship" and "engineering team" to South Vietnam to support the U.S. aggression.

    In Lausanne, Switzerland, hundreds of young people poured into the streets on the evening of the 1st to hold protest demonstrations. They shouted slogans: "American executioners!" "Stop the massacre!" "Vietnam belongs to the Vietnamese!" The authorities dispatched a large number of police to surround the marching crowds. They seized the demonstrators' placards and arrested several young people. But people continued to move forward and shouted the slogan: "Johnson is an executioner!"

### 美国恢复轰炸越南的新挑衅激起众怒  西欧国家人民群起而攻  法荷西德瑞士等国公众示威痛斥约翰逊刽子手

1966-02-05
第3版()
专栏：

    美国恢复轰炸越南的新挑衅激起众怒
    西欧国家人民群起而攻
    法荷西德瑞士等国公众示威痛斥约翰逊刽子手
    据新华社讯　西欧公众纷纷到美国驻各国外交机构所在地举行示威，抗议美国恢复对越南北方的轰炸。
    一千多巴黎人三日在美国大使馆前游行示威。这时，美国大使馆的铁门紧紧关闭，办公室的电灯也熄灭了。聚集在广场上的示威群众一再高呼：“约翰逊杀人犯！”很多人一边高呼口号，一边挥动标语牌。标语牌上写着：“民族解放阵线万岁！”“美军从越南滚出去！”“美国滚回去！”“停止轰炸！”示威群众派出代表向美国大使馆递交了抗议书。
    在奥地利首都维也纳，数百名公众二日晚上在城市的主要街道上游行示威。示威者拿着的标语牌，上面写道：“美国人，滚出越南去”。他们抬着一个黑棺材，上面写着：“美国的炸弹在屠杀越南的妇女儿童”。示威者派遣了一个代表团列队前往美国使馆，递交了要求美国停止越南战争的决议。
    在荷兰的阿姆斯特丹，大约二百个青年在美国总领事住所门前举行示威，还递交了一封抗议信。
    一日晚上，在西德汉诺威的美国大厦外面，群众举行了游行示威，要求美国停止对越南的侵略战争。警察袭击了示威者，还逮捕了几个人。西德公众还抗议西德政府不顾舆论的谴责，准备派“医疗船”和“工程队”到南越去支持美国的侵略行动。
    在瑞士的洛桑市，数百名青年一日晚上涌上街头，举行抗议示威。他们高呼口号：“美国刽子手！”“制止屠杀！”“越南属于越南人！”当局调来了大批警察包围游行队伍，他们夺走了示威者的标语牌，并且逮捕了好几个青年。但是人们继续前进，并且高呼口号：“约翰逊是刽子手！”
