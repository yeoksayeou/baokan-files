Title: Cambodian Anti-Aircraft Units Shoot Down Intruding US Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-02-03
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 2nd - According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on February 2nd, at noon on January 30th, a propeller-driven aircraft with US Navy markings, resembling a C-130 Hercules, intruded into Cambodian airspace to a height of about 1,800 meters and flew over a Cambodian outpost in the village of Kansomnar, Kandal Province, at an altitude of about 100 meters. Cambodian anti-aircraft units opened fire on the aircraft, which was hit and fled back to South Vietnam in a disheveled manner, crashing in Anlong. Before returning over South Vietnam, it jettisoned two fuel tanks and seven rockets on Cambodian territory. On January 25th, 26th, and 27th, US-South Vietnamese puppet aircraft intruded into Cambodian airspace over Kandal Province and White Horse (Kep) five times. On the morning of January 1st, approximately twenty-four South Vietnamese puppet soldiers intruded into Cambodian territory seven hundred meters deep in the village of Kottala, Svay Rieng province.

### 柬埔寨防空部队击毁入侵美机

1966-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨防空部队击毁入侵美机
    新华社金边二日电　据柬埔寨新闻社二日报道，一月三十日中午，一架具有美国海军标记的类似C—130赫格居里士式的螺旋桨飞机，侵入柬埔寨领空达一千八百米左右，在干丹省堪三纳村地方的柬埔寨哨所上空约一百米处飞行。柬埔寨防空部队向这架飞机开火，这架飞机中弹后狼狈地飞回南越，并且坠落在安隆地方。它在返回南越上空以前，在柬埔寨境内抛下两个汽油箱和七枚火箭。在一月二十五日、二十六日和二十七日，美国—南越伪军飞机五次侵入柬埔寨干丹省和白马上空。一月一日上午，大约二十四名南越伪军侵入柴桢省戈特拉村柬埔寨领土深达七百米的地方。
