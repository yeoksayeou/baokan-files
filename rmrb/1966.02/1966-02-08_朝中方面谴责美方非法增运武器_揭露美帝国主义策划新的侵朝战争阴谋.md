Title: North Korean Side Condemns the U.S. for Illegally Increasing Arms Shipments, Exposing U.S. Imperialism's Plot to Plan a New War of Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1966-02-08
Edition: 5

    Pyongyang, February 5th, New China News Agency - According to the Korean Central News Agency from Kaesong: The Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission strongly demanded that the U.S. side immediately cease illegally increasing arms shipments to South Korea and immediately remove all new weapons and combat equipment that it has illegally brought into South Korea.

      The Korean-Chinese side made this demand in a letter to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission on February 3rd. The letter mentioned that the U.S. side recently illegally shipped M-48 heavy tanks into South Korea and intends to ship high-speed destroyer transports, 155mm howitzers, 9-inch howitzers, reconnaissance planes, and other new weapons and combat equipment.

　　The letter stated that recently, behind the smokescreen of "peace talks," the U.S. ruling clique has desperately expanded the war of aggression against Vietnam, while at the same time facilitating the conclusion of the criminal "South Korea-Japan Treaty," attempting to introduce Japanese militarists into South Korea once again, and further establishing an aggressive "Northeast Asia Military Alliance." Simultaneously, the United Nations Command not only ships various new weapons and combat equipment into South Korea but also openly declares that it will continue to do so in the future. The aforementioned facts prove that the U.S. side is brazenly violating the Korean Armistice Agreement and is openly plotting a new war of aggression against Korea.

### 朝中方面谴责美方非法增运武器  揭露美帝国主义策划新的侵朝战争阴谋

1966-02-08
第5版()
专栏：

    朝中方面谴责美方非法增运武器
    揭露美帝国主义策划新的侵朝战争阴谋
    新华社平壤五日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社开城消息：军事停战委员会朝中方面强烈要求美方立即停止向南朝鲜非法增运武器，马上撤除它非法运进南朝鲜的一切新式武器和战斗装备。
      朝中方面在二月三日给中立国监察委员会的一封信中提出了这一要求。这封信提到，美方最近非法地把Ｍ—48重型坦克运进了南朝鲜，并打算运进高速驱逐运输舰、一百五十五毫米的榴弹炮、九英寸的榴弹炮、侦察机和其它新式武器和战斗装备。　
　信件说，最近，美国统治集团在“和谈”的烟幕后面，拼命扩大侵略越南的战争，同时促成罪恶的“韩日条约”的缔结，企图再次把日本军国主义者引进南朝鲜，进而成立侵略性的“东北亚军事联盟”。同时，联合国司令部方面不仅把各种新式武器和战斗装备运进南朝鲜，而且还公然宣布，它在将来要继续这样做。上述事实证明，美方悍然违反朝鲜停战协定，露骨地在策划发动另一场侵略朝鲜的战争的阴谋。
