Title: Northern Vietnamese People and Army Shoot Down a U.S. Aircraft

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-19
Edition: 4

    Hanoi, February 18 (Xinhua) – It was announced here on the 18th that the army and people of Thanh Hoa Province in northern Vietnam shot down an invading U.S. jet fighter on the afternoon of the 18th and captured the U.S. pilot alive.

### 越南北方军民击落美机一架

1966-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机一架
    新华社河内十八日电　据这里十八日公布：越南北方清化省军民十八日下午打落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架，并且活捉了美国飞行员。
