Title: After British Secretary of State Pays Courtesy Call to Vietnamese Chargé d'Affaires ad Interim in Soviet Union, Political Broker Wilson Spread Rumors and Deceived the Public, Sinking to the Extreme, Both Exonerating the U.S. Imperialists' Crimes of Aggression Against Vietnam and Promoting U.S. Imperialist War Blackmail
Author:
Date: 1966-02-27
Edition: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th—Moscow News: British Prime Minister Wilson, who is visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of Kosygin, sent his Secretary of State, Chalfont, on February 23rd to pay a courtesy call to the Chargé d'Affaires ad Interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the Soviet Union. During this visit, the Vietnamese Chargé d'Affaires condemned the aggression of U.S. imperialism and also condemned the Labor government's policy of supporting the U.S. aggression against Vietnam. However, after this visit, Wilson boasted and spread rumors, fully exposing the extent to which this British Prime Minister, who is intent on being a political broker, has degenerated.
　　According to Western news agencies, after the visit, the British officials, while concealing the truth of this scolding, proclaimed to Western journalists that they "had a good meeting." Wilson, in a televised address to Britain in Moscow on the 23rd, even boasted, saying, "For many months, we have been trying to open the door to contact with Hanoi," "We must try to get connected with them, and now we are connected." In his speech, he exonerated U.S. imperialism from its crimes of aggression, saying that "the key to peace in Vietnam is in Hanoi's hands." He also promoted U.S. imperialist war blackmail, saying that "we all agreed in Moscow that this war cannot be resolved militarily," "If there are no political consultations, this war will continue for many years."

### 英国务大臣拜会越南驻苏临时代办后　政治掮客威尔逊造谣惑众堕落至极　既为美帝开脱侵越罪责又为美帝战争讹诈张目

1966-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　英国务大臣拜会越南驻苏临时代办后
　　政治掮客威尔逊造谣惑众堕落至极
　　既为美帝开脱侵越罪责又为美帝战争讹诈张目
    新华社二十六日讯　莫斯科消息：应柯西金的邀请访问苏联的英国首相威尔逊，在二月二十三日派遣他的国务大臣查尔方特拜会了越南民主共和国驻苏联大使馆临时代办。在这次拜会中，越南临时代办谴责了美帝国主义的侵略，也谴责了工党政府支持美国侵略越南的政策。但是，在这次拜会后，威尔逊竟然大肆吹嘘，造谣惑众，充分暴露了这个一心想当政治掮客的英国首相，已经堕落到何种程度。
    据西方通讯社报道，在拜会结束后，英国官方一面隐瞒这次挨骂的真相，一面向西方记者扬言，他们“进行了一次良好的会谈”。威尔逊二十三日在莫斯科对英国发表的一次电视谈话中，更是自我吹嘘地说，“许多月来，我们一直设法打开与河内接触的大门”，“我们必须设法同他们接上线，现在我们已接通了”。他在谈话中为美帝国主义开脱侵略罪责，说什么“越南实现和平的钥匙掌握在河内手里”。他还为美帝国主义的战争讹诈张目，说什么“我们在莫斯科全都一致认为，这场战争不可能从军事上解决”，“如果没有政治会商，这场战争将要继续打许多年”。
