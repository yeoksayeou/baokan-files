Title: Celebrating the 16th Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance: Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union Holds Reception; Soviet Ambassador Holds Cocktail Party; Moscow Holds Evening Gathering
Author:
Date: 1966-02-16
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 14th. The Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union held a friendly reception on the 14th to celebrate the 16th anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance.
    Soviet attendees included First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs V.V. Kuznetsov, First Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Union of Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Gorshkov, and Deputy Chairman of the State Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Medvedev, among others.
    The Chinese film "The East is Red" was screened at the reception.
    Xinhua News Agency, February 15th. Soviet Ambassador to China Lapin held a cocktail party and reception this evening to celebrate the 16th anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance.
    Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Guo Moruo, Shao Lizi, Li Zhuchen, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan, and responsible persons from relevant departments Chu Tunan, Li Qitao, Jia Ruoyu, Cao Jinghua, and others were invited to attend the cocktail party and reception.
    The Soviet film "Thirst" was screened at the reception.

### 庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十六周年  我驻苏使馆举行晚会　苏大使举行酒会　莫斯科举行晚会

1966-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十六周年
    我驻苏使馆举行晚会　苏大使举行酒会　莫斯科举行晚会
    新华社莫斯科十四日电　中国驻苏联大使馆十四日举行友好晚会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十六周年。
    苏联方面出席晚会的有外交部第一副部长库兹涅佐夫、对外友好和文化协会联合会主席团第一副主席戈尔什科夫，对外文委副主席麦德维杰夫等。
    晚会上放映了中国电影《东方红》。
    新华社十五日讯　苏联驻中国大使拉宾今天晚上举行酒会和晚会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十六周年。
    中苏友好协会总会副会长郭沫若、邵力子、李烛尘，外交部副部长王炳南，各有关方面负责人楚图南、李琦涛、贾若瑜、曹靖华等应邀出席了酒会和晚会。
    晚会上放映了苏联影片《渴》。
    新华社莫斯科十四日电　十四日在莫斯科工会大厦举行文艺晚会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十六周年。
    晚会是由苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会、苏中友好协会、全苏工会中央理事会、苏联对外文化委员会和苏联文化部联合举办的。
    出席晚会的有苏联外交部第一副部长库兹涅佐夫，苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席团第一副主席戈尔什科夫，对外文化委员会副主席麦德维杰夫等人。
    中国驻苏联大使潘自力和使馆参赞等出席了晚会。
