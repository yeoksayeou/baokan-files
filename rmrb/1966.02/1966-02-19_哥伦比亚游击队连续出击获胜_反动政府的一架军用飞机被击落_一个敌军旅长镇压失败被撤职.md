Title: Colombian Guerrillas Achieve Successive Victories; A Military Aircraft of the Reactionary Government Was Shot Down; An Enemy Brigade Commander Was Removed from His Post After Failing to Suppress the Uprising

Author:
Date: 1966-02-19
Edition: 5

    Colombian Guerrillas Achieve Successive Victories
    A Military Aircraft of the Reactionary Government Was Shot Down; An Enemy Brigade Commander Was Removed from His Post After Failing to Suppress the Uprising
    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Bogota dispatch: A guerrilla force active in the province of Huila in central Colombia recently shot down a helicopter belonging to the Colombian Air Force with rifles.
    The guerrillas shot down the aircraft in the municipality of Algeciras, Huila province. A government army battalion commander, Tenerife de Neva, who was aboard the aircraft on a mission to "encircle and suppress" the guerrillas, was seriously injured. Recently, the government army launched "encirclement and suppression" operations against guerrillas in the provinces of Huila and Tolima, but were met with a strong counterattack from the guerrillas.
    The guerrillas, led by the famous guerrilla leader Manuel Marulanda, launched a counter-"encirclement" offensive against the troops of the government army's Fifth Brigade. More than 30 guerrillas attacked a patrol of the Fifth Brigade in the municipality of Palanadas, Tolima province, killing one soldier and wounding two.
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch, Bogota: Guerrillas active in the central mountain region of Colombia recently launched successive attacks against the reactionary government army in the provinces of Huila and Tolima, achieving victories.
    On January 29, the famous guerrilla leader Marulanda commanded his troops in a battle in the city of Palermo, Huila province, killing six government soldiers and wounding three, including four officers.
    At the same time, an anti-government guerrilla force killed five enemies and arrested two in the city of Planadas, Tolima province. In addition, the guerrillas seized a large number of weapons and other military supplies in an attack on a government army engineering battalion.
    Since the beginning of the year, guerrillas in the central Colombian provinces of Tolima, Huila, Cauca Valley, and Caqueta have all launched activities. The National Liberation Army in the northern provinces of Santander and Magdalena have also constantly attacked the government army. Recently, the famous Colombian social figure Camilo Torres also announced his participation in the Colombian National Liberation Army. According to reports, internal contradictions within the Colombian government army are increasingly intensifying, and the commander of the Fifth Brigade, responsible for suppressing the National Liberation Army, has been dismissed from his post due to defeat in battle. ### 哥伦比亚游击队连续出击获胜  反动政府的一架军用飞机被击落　一个敌军旅长镇压失败被撤职

1966-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚游击队连续出击获胜
    反动政府的一架军用飞机被击落　一个敌军旅长镇压失败被撤职
    新华社十七日讯　波哥大消息：活跃在哥伦比亚中部乌伊拉省的一支游击队最近用步枪击落了哥伦比亚空军的一架直升飞机。
    游击队员是在乌伊拉省阿尔赫西拉斯市管辖区击落这架飞机的。当时，乘这架飞机执行“围剿”游击队任务的一名政府军营长特内里费·德内瓦受重伤。最近，政府军对乌伊拉省和托利马省的游击队发动“围剿”，但是遭到游击队员有力的反击。
    由著名游击队领导人曼努埃尔·马鲁兰达率领的游击队向政府军第五旅的部队发动反“围剿”的进攻。三十多名游击队员在托利马省帕拉纳达斯市管辖区袭击了第五旅的一支巡逻队，打死一名士兵，打伤两名。
    新华社讯　波哥大消息：活跃在哥伦比亚中央山脉地区的游击队最近在乌伊拉省和托利马省连续出击反动政府军，获得胜利。
    著名游击队领导人马鲁兰达一月二十九日指挥他的部队在乌伊拉省帕莱尔莫市的一次战斗中，打死政府军六人，打伤三人，其中有四名军官。
    与此同时，一支反政府游击队在托利马省普拉纳达斯市打死了五个敌人，还逮捕了二人。此外，游击队在对一个政府军工兵营的袭击中缴获了大量武器和其他军用品。
    年初以来，哥伦比亚中部托利马省、乌伊拉省、考卡山谷省和卡克塔州的游击队都纷纷展开了活动。北部桑坦德省和马格达莱纳省的民族解放军也不断出击政府军。最近，哥伦比亚社会著名人士卡米洛·托雷斯也宣布参加哥伦比亚民族解放军。据报道，目前哥伦比亚政府军的内部矛盾正在日益加剧，负责镇压民族解放军的第五旅旅长由于在战斗中失利已被撤职。（附图片）
