Date: 1966-02-18

    On February 17th, the delegation of the Japanese Communist Party headed by General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Comrade Kenji Miyamoto, left Guangzhou to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Tao Zhu, Liu Ningyi and other comrades saw them off at the airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Pan Jiamin (facsimile photo)
