Title: American People Rise Up Again Against Their Own Imperialism; Masses in New York and Boston Brave Bitter Cold for All-Night Demonstrations; American Anti-War Organizations Call for Global Demonstrations on the 5th
Author:
Date: 1966-02-03
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd dispatch from New York: The wave of American people protesting the Johnson government's resumption of bombing of North Vietnam continues to surge.
    Nearly a thousand people in New York City, holding high the flags of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, staged a demonstration on the evening of the 1st. The demonstration chanted rhythmically, "End the war in Vietnam! Bring the troops home!" Many demonstrators wore black veils to protest the war of aggression against Vietnam. Nearly forty demonstrators were arrested by the police. At five o'clock that morning, a group of New York people who had been demonstrating all night continued to persist in the demonstration in freezing temperatures below zero, protesting the government's resumption of bombing North Vietnam. On the same day, about two hundred women held a protest demonstration in front of the United Nations building in New York.
    In Madison, Wisconsin, the organizers of last October's unprecedented "National Protest Day" activities – the American "National Coordinating Committee to End the War in Vietnam" – issued a call on the 1st, demanding that peace-loving people of all countries launch global demonstrations on the 5th to protest the American government's brazen resumption of barbaric bombing of North Vietnam.
    On the same day, the newly formed American "Veterans and Reservists to End the War in Vietnam" announced that thousands of American veterans and reservists would hold a protest rally in front of the White House in Washington on the 5th. A group of veterans expressed their intention to return their awards, military medals, and discharge certificates to Johnson.
    In Chicago, on the 31st, some people demonstrated and protested against U.S. Vice President Humphrey, who was there defending Johnson's resumption of bombing. That evening in Boston, dozens of people braved the cold to begin a twenty-four-hour demonstration outside the Federal Building.
    Xinhua News Agency, Ottawa, February 1st telegram: About one hundred Canadians demonstrated outside the U.S. Consulate in Toronto last night to protest the United States' resumption of bombing the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Demonstrators marched through Toronto's biggest snowstorm in nineteen years, holding signs demanding that the United States stop the war in Vietnam, and shouting slogans: "Get U.S. troops out!" "Stop bombing Vietnam!" "Let the American boys come home alive!"

### 美国人民再次奋起反对本国帝国主义  纽约波士顿群众冒严寒通宵示威  美国反战组织号召在五日展开全球性示威

1966-02-03
第3版()
专栏：

    美国人民再次奋起反对本国帝国主义
    纽约波士顿群众冒严寒通宵示威
    美国反战组织号召在五日展开全球性示威
    新华社二日讯　纽约消息：美国人民抗议约翰逊政府恢复轰炸越南北方罪行的浪潮继续高涨。
    纽约市近一千名群众一日傍晚高举越南南方民族解放阵线的旗帜，举行示威游行。示威队伍有节奏地高呼：“结束越南战争！把军队撤回来！”许多示威者佩带黑纱，表示对侵越战争的抗议。近四十名示威者被警察逮捕。这天上午五点钟，一批已经进行了通宵示威的纽约群众，仍然继续在零度以下的严寒气温下坚持示威，抗议政府恢复轰炸越南北方。这一天，还有约二百名妇女在纽约的联合国大厦前举行了抗议示威。
    在威斯康星州的麦迪逊市，去年十月空前规模的“全国抗议日”活动的组织者——美国“结束越南战争全国协调委员会”一日发出号召，要求各国爱好和平的人民在五日展开全球性大示威，抗议美国政府悍然恢复对越南北方的野蛮轰炸。
    同一天，新成立的美国“退伍军人和后备役军人结束越南战争组织”宣布，成千名美国退伍军人和后备役军人将在五日在华盛顿白宫门前举行抗议大会。一批退伍军人表示要把他们的立功绶带、军事奖章和退伍证书退还给约翰逊。
    在芝加哥市，三十一日一些群众对正在那里为约翰逊恢复轰炸进行辩解的美国副总统汉弗莱举行了示威抗议。这天晚上在波士顿市，数十名群众冒着严寒在联邦大厦外边开始举行二十四小时的示威。
    新华社渥太华一日电　约一百个加拿大人昨天晚上在多伦多美国领事馆门外示威，反对美国恢复轰炸越南民主共和国。示威者在多伦多十九年来最大的暴风雪中游行，他们举着标语牌，要求美国停止在越南的战争，并且高呼口号：“撤退美国军队！”“停止轰炸越南！”“让美国的小伙子活着回家！”
   （附图片）
    二月一日，纽约市群众不顾警察镇压，冒着严寒在“纽约时报广场”举行静坐示威，抗议约翰逊政府恢复轰炸越南北方。
