Title: Soviet Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate Soviet Army Day

Author:

Date: 1966-02-24
Edition: 4

　　Soviet Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate Soviet Army Day
    Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: Major General Romanov, Soviet Military Attaché to China, held a reception tonight to celebrate the 48th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.
    Xiao Jingguang, Vice Minister of National Defense; Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Xu Liqing, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Tang Tianji, Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department; and responsible persons from various military branches and related departments, including Du Yide, Cao Lihuai, Liu Shaowen, and Yu Zhan, were invited to attend. Soviet Ambassador to China, Lapin, as well as diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries in China, also attended the reception.

### 苏联驻华武官举行招待会庆祝苏军建军节

1966-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联驻华武官举行招待会庆祝苏军建军节
    新华社二十三日讯　苏联驻中国大使馆武官罗马诺夫少将今晚举行招待会，庆祝苏联军队建军四十八周年。
    国防部副部长萧劲光，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊、总政治部副主任徐立清、总后勤部副部长唐天际，以及各军种兵种和有关方面负责人杜义德、曹里怀、刘少文、余湛等，应邀出席。苏联驻中国大使拉宾，各国驻中国使节和武官也出席了招待会。
