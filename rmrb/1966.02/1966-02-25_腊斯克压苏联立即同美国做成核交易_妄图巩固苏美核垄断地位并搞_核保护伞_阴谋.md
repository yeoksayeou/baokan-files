Title: Rusk Pressures the Soviet Union to Immediately Conclude a Nuclear Deal with the United States, Attempting to Consolidate the US-Soviet Nuclear Monopoly and Plot a "Nuclear Umbrella" Conspiracy

Author:
Date: 1966-02-25
Edition: 3

　　New China News Agency, Washington, February 24th: U.S. Secretary of State Dean Rusk testified before the U.S. Atomic Energy Commission on the 23rd, demanding that the Soviet Union quickly reach an agreement with the United States on the issue of preventing nuclear proliferation, in order to consolidate the two countries' nuclear monopoly.

　　Rusk said, "If the Soviet Union is truly interested in preventing nuclear proliferation," then it "should be able to take immediate action and reach an agreement."

　　In his testimony, Rusk emphasized that striving for non-proliferation of nuclear weapons is "a major goal of U.S. foreign policy." He also propagandized that "as the number of nuclear states increases, it will make efforts by existing nuclear states to negotiate mutually beneficial nuclear arms control agreements more complex and difficult."

　　Rusk insisted that the so-called "nuclear arrangements" being negotiated between the United States and NATO countries, which aim to allow West German militarists to get their hands on nuclear weapons, "do not constitute nuclear proliferation"; "There is no contradiction between the United States' non-proliferation policy and the possible nuclear arrangements it has proposed to NATO." He also quoted U.S. President Johnson's letter to the Geneva Disarmament Conference, indicating that the United States, while reaching a deal with the Soviet Union on nuclear issues, is prepared to engage in a "nuclear umbrella" conspiracy, that is, non-nuclear countries will receive "strong support from the United States to deal with nuclear blackmail."

### 腊斯克压苏联立即同美国做成核交易　妄图巩固苏美核垄断地位并搞“核保护伞”阴谋

1966-02-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　腊斯克压苏联立即同美国做成核交易
　　妄图巩固苏美核垄断地位并搞“核保护伞”阴谋
    新华社二十四日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务卿腊斯克二十三日在美国原子能委员会作证时，要求苏联迅速就防止核扩散问题同美国达成协议，以便巩固两国的核垄断地位。
    腊斯克说，“假如苏联果真对防止核扩散感兴趣的话”，那它“就应该能够立即采取行动，达成协议”。
    腊斯克在证词中强调，争取不扩散核武器是“美国外交政策的一项主要目标”。他还宣传说，“随着核国家数目的增多，将使现有的核国家为达成互利的控制核武器协议而进行谈判的努力，变得更加复杂和困难。”
    腊斯克硬说，美国正在同北大西洋集团国家谈判中的、旨在让西德军国主义者染指核武器的所谓“核安排”，“并不构成核扩散”；“在美国的不扩散政策同它向北大西洋公约组织建议的可能的核安排之间并没有矛盾”。他还引美国总统约翰逊给日内瓦裁军会议信件的话，表示美国在同苏联就核问题达成交易的同时，准备搞“核保护伞”的阴谋，即无核国家将得到美国“有力的支持来对付核讹诈”。
