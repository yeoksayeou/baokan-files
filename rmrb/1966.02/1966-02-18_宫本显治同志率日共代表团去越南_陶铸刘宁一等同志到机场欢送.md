Title: Comrade Kenji Miyamoto Leads Japanese Communist Party Delegation to Vietnam; Comrades Tao Zhu and Liu Ningyi See Them Off at the Airport
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-18
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 17th—The Japanese Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Kenji Miyamoto left Guangzhou on the 17th for a visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included responsible comrades of the CCP Central Committee's Central-South Bureau, the CCP Guangdong Provincial Committee, and the CCP Guangzhou Municipal Committee: Tao Zhu, Wu Zhipu, Wang De, Wen Yucheng, Lin Liming, Yin Linping, Li Jianzhen, Yong Wentao, Zeng Sheng, Wang Lanxi, and others. Also seeing them off at the airport were Liu Ningyi, member of the CCP Central Committee, and Zhao Anbo, responsible staff member of the CCP Central Committee organs.
    The Japanese Communist Party delegation is scheduled to revisit our country after the conclusion of its visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to continue its visit.

### 宫本显治同志率日共代表团去越南  陶铸刘宁一等同志到机场欢送

1966-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    宫本显治同志率日共代表团去越南
    陶铸刘宁一等同志到机场欢送
    新华社广州十七日电　以宫本显治同志为首的日本共产党代表团，十七日离开广州前往越南民主共和国访问。
    到机场欢送的有，中共中央中南局、中共广东省委、中共广州市委的负责同志陶铸、吴芝圃、王德、温玉成、林李明、尹林平、李坚真、雍文涛、曾生、王阑西等。到机场欢送的，还有中共中央委员刘宁一和中共中央机关负责工作人员赵安博。
    日本共产党代表团预定在越南民主共和国的访问结束后，将再来我国继续访问。
