Title: Indian Newspaper Comments Say Castro’s Anti-China Actions “Provide Some Comfort to the Organization of American States”
Author:
Date: 1966-02-22
Edition: 5

　　Indian Newspaper's Comments
　　Say Castro’s Anti-China Actions “Provide Some Comfort to the Organization of American States”
    The *Hindustan Times* published an editorial on February 8 entitled "Castro's Attack," which stated: "China called on the entire Latin America to start a revolution last month during the seventh anniversary of the Cuban Revolution, but now Prime Minister Castro strongly condemns it for intending to subvert the Cuban armed forces with anti-Russian books and publications... Latin America is uneasy about the pro-Castro revolutions that once led the Organization of American States to take action. Now, the discord between Havana and Beijing should provide some comfort to the Organization of American States.”

### 印度报纸的评论  说卡斯特罗反华“使美洲国家组织得到一些安慰”

1966-02-22
第5版()
专栏：

　　印度报纸的评论
　　说卡斯特罗反华“使美洲国家组织得到一些安慰”
    《印度斯坦时报》二月八日发表了一篇题为《卡斯特罗的攻击》的社论说：“中国上月在古巴革命七周年的时候号召整个拉丁美洲闹革命，但是现在卡斯特罗总理强烈谴责它打算以反对俄国的书刊颠覆古巴武装部队……拉丁美洲对曾经导致美洲国家组织采取行动的亲卡斯特罗的革命感到不安。现在哈瓦那和北京发生不和，这应当使美洲国家组织得到一些安慰。”
