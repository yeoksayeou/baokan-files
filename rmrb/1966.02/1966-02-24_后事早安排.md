Title: Arrange the Funeral Affairs Early
Author: Chi Bei'ou, Miao Di
Date: 1966-02-24
Edition: 3

　　Arrange the Funeral Affairs Early
　　Poem by Chi Bei'ou, Illustration by Miao Di
    According to reports, the US military authorities invading Vietnam recently ordered 400,000 nylon "body bags" for the corpses of US soldiers from Japan.
　　Suffering repeated defeats on the battlefield,
　　Young master soldiers live and die.
　　"Moderate casualties" over a thousand,
　　"Slight losses" six or seven hundred.
　　Corpses that cannot be transported away,
　　Coffins that cannot be buried.
　　As the war continues to expand,
　　Funeral arrangements should be made early,
　　Hurry up and order a large number of nylon bags.
　　Simplify the packaging,
　　Speed up efficiency,
　　Shove one corpse into one bag.
　　Four hundred thousand is not too many,
　　Enough to use for a year and a half.

### 后事早安排
池北偶　苗地
1966-02-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　后事早安排
　　池北偶诗　苗地画
    据报道，侵越美军当局最近向日本订购四十万个包裹美军尸体的尼龙“尸体袋”。
　　战场上连遭惨败，
　　少爷兵活去死来。
　　“中等伤亡”一千多，
　　“轻微损失”六七百。
　　运不完的尸体，
　　埋不尽的棺材。
　　战争继续扩大，
　　后事宜早安排，
　　赶紧订制大批尼龙袋。
　　包装从简，
　　效率加快，
　　死一个往一只袋里塞。
　  四十万只不算多，
　　够用个一年半载。
