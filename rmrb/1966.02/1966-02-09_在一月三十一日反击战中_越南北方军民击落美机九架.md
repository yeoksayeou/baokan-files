Title: In the Counter-Attack on January 31st, North Vietnamese Soldiers and Civilians Shot Down Nine American Planes
Author:
Date: 1966-02-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 8th - According to supplementary news released here at noon today, the soldiers and civilians of Quang Ninh County in Quang Binh Province and Vinh Giang Commune in the Vinh Linh area each shot down an American plane in the battle against invading American bandit planes on January 31st.
    Thus, adding the previously announced seven planes, the soldiers and civilians of North Vietnam shot down a total of nine American planes in one day, January 31st.

### 在一月三十一日反击战中  越南北方军民击落美机九架

1966-02-09
第5版()
专栏：

    在一月三十一日反击战中
    越南北方军民击落美机九架
    新华社河内八日电　据这里今天中午公布的补充消息说，广平省广宁县和永灵区永江乡军民，在一月三十一日反击入侵的美国强盗飞机战斗中，各击落美国飞机一架。
    这样，加上前已公布的七架，越南北方军民在一月三十一日一天中，共击落美国飞机九架。
