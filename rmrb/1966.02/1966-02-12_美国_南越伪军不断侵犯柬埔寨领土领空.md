Title: U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Troops Continuously Invade Cambodian Territory and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-02-12
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 10th: According to the Cambodian News Agency on the 10th, U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops shelled Cambodia's Prey Veng Province twice on January 29th and 30th.
    The report said that from January 22nd to 30th, U.S.-South Vietnamese aircraft intruded into the airspace of Cambodia's Kandal Province, Svay Rieng Province, Prey Veng Province, and Kratie Province ten times.
    A news item from the same news agency on the 7th stated that on January 15th, a company of U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops, including five Americans, set out from the Au An Dong outpost in South Vietnam and invaded Svay Rieng Province.
    On January 21st, two U.S.-South Vietnamese helicopters intruded into Cambodian territory southeast of Adong in Prey Veng Province.
    The same news agency also reported on the 9th that on the evening of February 7th, a platoon of the Royal Cambodian Armed Forces detonated a landmine planted by Thai troops in Cambodian territory near Aubor in the O Smach area of Oddar Meanchey Province, killing two soldiers and wounding one.

### 美国—南越伪军不断侵犯柬埔寨领土领空

1966-02-12
第5版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军不断侵犯柬埔寨领土领空
    新华社金边十日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十日报道，一月二十九日和三十日，美国—南越伪军曾经两次炮轰柬埔寨波萝勉省。
    消息说，一月二十二日到三十日，美国—南越飞机曾经十次侵犯柬埔寨干丹省、柴桢省、波萝勉省和桔井省领空。
    这家新闻社七日的一则消息说，一月十五日，一连美国—南越伪军，其中有五名美国人，曾经从南越的奥安东哨所出发，侵入柴桢省。
    一月二十一日，曾经有两架美国—南越直升飞机侵入波萝勉省阿东东南的柬埔寨地区上空。
    这家新闻社九日还报道说，二月七日晚上，柬埔寨王家武装部队的一个排在奥多棉藏省奥斯马地方奥波尔附近的柬埔寨领土内，踏响了泰国军队埋设的一个地雷，有两名士兵被炸死，一名被炸伤。
