  The Party committee members of the Longquan Commune in Huaining County, Anhui Province, persist in creatively studying and applying Chairman Mao's works with problems in mind, and put political and ideological work first. The picture shows Li Yansong (right), secretary of the commune party committee and national model agricultural worker, studying Chairman Mao's works with members of the party committee.
