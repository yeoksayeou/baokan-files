Title: East German String Quartet Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-02-14
Edition: 5

    New China News Agency, February 13th—The Ulbricht String Quartet from the German Democratic Republic, invited by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit and perform in our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.


**Named Entities**

人民日报 The People's Daily
新华社  Xinhua News Agency
对外文化联络委员会 Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries
德意志民主共和国 German Democratic Republic
乌尔布利希弦乐四重奏组 Ulbricht String Quartet
北京  Beijing

### 民主德国一弦乐四重奏组到京

1966-02-14
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国一弦乐四重奏组到京
    新华社十三日讯　应对外文化联络委员会邀请前来我国访问演出的德意志民主共和国乌尔布利希弦乐四重奏组今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
