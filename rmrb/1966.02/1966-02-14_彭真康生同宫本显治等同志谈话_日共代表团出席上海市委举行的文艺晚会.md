Title: Peng Zhen, Kang Sheng Talk with Comrades Kenji Miyamoto and Others; Japanese Communist Party Delegation Attends Literary and Art Soiree Held by Shanghai Municipal Party Committee
Author:
Date: 1966-02-14
Edition: 1

    Peng Zhen, Kang Sheng Talk with Comrades Kenji Miyamoto and Others
    Japanese Communist Party Delegation Attends Literary and Art Soiree Held by Shanghai Municipal Party Committee
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 13th. Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, held talks with the Japanese Communist Party delegation headed by Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, this afternoon. The talks proceeded in a cordial and friendly atmosphere throughout.
    Also present at the talks were Liu Ningyi, Chen Pixian, Wu Lengxi, and Zhao Anbo.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 13th. The Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC held a literary and art soiree on the evening of the 13th to entertain the Japanese Communist Party delegation.
    At the soiree, members of the Japanese Communist Party delegation, including Korehito Kurahara, Wataru Miyahara, and Koichiro Ueda, accompanied by Comrades Kang Sheng and Liu Ningyi, watched the ballet "The White-Haired Girl" performed by the Shanghai Dance School.
    Also accompanying the Japanese comrades to the performance were Comrades Chen Pixian, Zhang Chunqiao, Liang Guobin, and others.
    After the performance, Comrades Korehito Kurahara, Wataru Miyahara, and others, accompanied by Comrades Kang Sheng, Liu Ningyi, and Chen Pixian, went on stage and warmly shook hands with the performers. At this moment, warm applause resounded throughout the audience.

### 彭真康生同宫本显治等同志谈话  日共代表团出席上海市委举行的文艺晚会

1966-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真康生同宫本显治等同志谈话
    日共代表团出席上海市委举行的文艺晚会
    新华社上海十三日电　中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真和政治局候补委员、书记处书记康生，十三日下午同以日共中央总书记宫本显治为首的日本共产党代表团进行了谈话。谈话始终在亲切友好的气氛中进行。
    谈话时在座的还有刘宁一、陈丕显、吴冷西、赵安博。
    新华社上海十三日电　中共上海市委十三日晚举行文艺晚会，招待日本共产党代表团。
    在晚会上，日本共产党代表团团员藏原惟人、米原昶、上田耕一郎同志等在康生、刘宁一同志陪同下，观看了上海舞蹈学校演出的芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》。
    陪同日本同志看剧的，还有陈丕显、张春桥、梁国斌等同志。
    演出结束后，藏原惟人、米原昶等同志由康生、刘宁一、陈丕显同志陪同走上舞台，同演员们亲切握手。这时，全场响起一片热烈的掌声。
