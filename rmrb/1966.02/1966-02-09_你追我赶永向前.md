Title: Striving Forward, Surpassing Each Other
Author: Huang Jingwen
Date: 1966-02-09
Edition: 6

    Raising a new high tide of production,
    Comparing, learning, catching up, helping, surpassing is even more important.
    Striving forward, surpassing each other,
    Spirited and high in fighting will.
    Raising a new high tide of production,
    Revolutionary vigor burns like fire,
    Iron arms swing, shaking mountains and rivers,
    Not even afraid of obstacles blocking the way!
    Raising a new high tide of production,
    Increasing production and practicing thrift is most important,
    A drop of oil, a handful of yarn,
    Must not be wasted easily.
    Raising a new high tide of production,
    The Daqing spirit must be remembered firmly,
    Self-reliance to create miracles,
    Each wave surpassing the last.

**Named Entities**

大庆	Daqing
苏州阀门厂	Suzhou Valve Factory
