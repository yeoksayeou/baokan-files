Title: Belgian Communist Weekly "People's Voice" Points Out: Castro Shouts for Unity but Engages in Division
Author:
Date: 1966-02-22
Edition: 3

　　Belgian Communist Weekly "People's Voice" Points Out
    Castro Shouts for Unity but Engages in Division
    Xinhua News Agency, 21st—Brussels Dispatch: The Belgian Communist Party's organ, the weekly "People's Voice," published a commentary on January 14 entitled "Castro Follows the Soviet Revisionist Leaders." The commentary said:
    Castro talks a great deal about imperialism, Marxism-Leninism, and the unity of the communist movement.
    In fact, he has chosen his position. Castro relies on the whims of the Soviet revisionist leaders, politically, ideologically, and economically.
    Step-by-step compromise will quickly lead to degeneration.
    Castro shouts for unity with his mouth but chooses division. He shouts "respect" for fraternal parties but actually engages in despicable and insidious slander against China. He carried out this slander at a mass rally in the center of Havana, and, coincidentally, just before the opening of the Tricontinental Conference.
    Castro emphatically affirmed that he had suggested exchanging two tons of Cuban sugar for one ton of Chinese rice, and he thought the validity of this transaction was long-term.
    He simultaneously indicated that China was doing everything possible to obstruct trade with Cuba...
  In fact, this was a shameful and completely hypocritical speech, a speech deliberately turning black and white upside down, and doing its utmost to engage in anti-China activities.

### 比利时共产党《人民之声》周刊指出  卡斯特罗喊的是团结干的是分裂

1966-02-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　比利时共产党《人民之声》周刊指出
    卡斯特罗喊的是团结干的是分裂
    新华社二十一日讯　布鲁塞尔消息：比利时共产党机关刊物《人民之声》周刊一月十四日以《卡斯特罗跟着苏联修正主义领导走》为题发表评论。评论说：
    卡斯特罗大谈特谈帝国主义，马克思列宁主义，以及共产主义运动的团结。
    事实上，他已选择了他的阵地。卡斯特罗仰仗苏联修正主义领导人的鼻息，在政治上、意识形态上和经济上都是如此。
    步步妥协就会很快地变质。
    卡斯特罗嘴里喊团结，却选择了分裂。他嘴里喊“尊重”兄弟党，但实际却对中国进行卑鄙而阴险的诬蔑。他是在哈瓦那市中心举行的群众大会上，而且，说来凑巧，恰恰是在三大洲会议开幕之前进行这种诬蔑的。
    卡斯特罗着重地肯定，他曾建议以两吨古巴糖换一吨中国大米，而且他以为这种交易的有效性是长期的。
    他同时表示，中国在千方百计地阻止同古巴的贸易……
  事实上，这是一个可耻的、完全虚伪的演说，这篇演说蓄意颠倒黑白，竭力进行反华。
