Title: Austrian Federal Economic Chamber Establishes Representative Office in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-02-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 10th -- Dr. Lehner, head of the China desk of the Austrian Federal Economic Chamber, who is currently visiting our country, held a reception tonight to mark the establishment of the Austrian Federal Economic Chamber's Representative Office in the People's Republic of China in Beijing and to introduce Mr. Schmidt, the Austrian Federal Economic Chamber's representative to the People's Republic of China, to relevant Chinese personnel. Among those present at the reception were: Hou Tong, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Li Chuan, Director of the Liaison Department; Zhang Xiancheng, Deputy Director of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; and Xie Shoutian, Deputy General Manager of the Bank of China.
    Helmreich, the Austrian Commercial Commissioner in Hong Kong, also attended the reception.

### 奥地利联邦商会在北京设立驻中国代表处

1966-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

    奥地利联邦商会在北京设立驻中国代表处
    新华社十日讯　正在我国访问的奥地利联邦商会中国司司长莱克西为奥地利联邦商会驻中华人民共和国代表处在北京设立，并介绍奥地利联邦商会驻中华人民共和国代表施密特与中国方面有关人员见面，于今晚举行招待会。出席招待会的有：中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席侯桐、联络部部长李川，对外贸易部副局长张先成，中国银行副总经理谢寿天等。
    奥地利驻香港商务专员赫尔姆来希也参加了这个招待会。
