Title: The Vietnamese Government Reiterates That the United Nations Has No Right to Interfere in Vietnamese Affairs; All Resolutions of the Security Council Interfering in the Vietnam Issue Are Completely Invalid; The United States' Submission of the Vietnam Issue to the Security Council Is an Attempt to Use the United Nations to Cover Up Its Expansion of the War of Aggression Against Vietnam and Force Vietnam to Accept American Terms
Author:
Date: 1966-02-02
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 1st—A spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement on February 1st regarding the United States' renewed submission of the Vietnam issue to the United Nations Security Council. The full text is as follows:

    According to an Associated Press report dated January 31, 1966, the United States government's representative to the United Nations, Goldberg, acting on instructions from President Johnson, wrote to the President of the United Nations Security Council, requesting that the Security Council convene immediately to allow the United States to report on the situation in Vietnam and propose a plan that could "open the path to the negotiating table."

    As in previous instances, the United States is once again attempting to use the United Nations to cover up its war of aggression against Vietnam and force the Vietnamese people to resolve the Vietnam issue according to American terms.

    The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam reiterates that, internationally, the review of the United States' war actions in Vietnam falls within the purview of the 1954 Geneva Conference on Indochina, and not within the purview of the United Nations Security Council. All resolutions of the United Nations Security Council interfering in the Vietnam issue are completely invalid.

### 越南政府重申联合国无权干涉越南问题  安理会干涉越南问题的一切决议都是完全无效的  美国把越南问题提交安理会是企图利用联合国来掩盖它扩大侵越战争，强迫越南接受美国的条件

1966-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    越南政府重申联合国无权干涉越南问题
    安理会干涉越南问题的一切决议都是完全无效的
    美国把越南问题提交安理会是企图利用联合国来掩盖它扩大侵越战争，强迫越南接受美国的条件
    新华社河内一日电　越南民主共和国外交部发言人一日就美国又把越南问题提交联合国安全理事会一事发表声明。全文如下：
    据美联社一九六六年一月三十一日报道，美国政府驻联合国代表戈德堡，根据约翰逊总统的指示，写信给联合国安全理事会主席，要求立即召开联合国安全理事会会议，让美国报告有关越南的局势，并提出一份可以“打开通向会议桌的途径”的提案。
    同前几次一样，美国这次又企图利用联合国来掩盖它扩大侵略越南的战争，强迫越南人民要按照美国的条件来解决越南问题。
    越南民主共和国政府重申，在国际方面，审议美国在越南的战争行动，是属于一九五四年关于印度支那问题的日内瓦会议的职权范围，不是属于联合国安全理事会的职权范围。联合国安全理事会干涉越南问题的一切决议都是完全无效的。
