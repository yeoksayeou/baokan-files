Title: British Public in London Braved the Rain to Hold Anti-American Demonstrations, Strongly Condemning the US for Resuming Bombing of North Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-02-07
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London, February 5th. The London public braved the rain on the afternoon of February 5th to hold demonstrations, strongly condemning the United States for resuming the bombing of North Vietnam and protesting against American aggression in Vietnam. Demonstrators submitted letters of protest to the US Embassy in London and the British Prime Minister's Office.
    Approximately five hundred demonstrators marched along the way, shouting slogans: "Americans get out of Vietnam!" "Yankees go home, go home, go home!" "Wilson is Johnson's poodle!" The placards held by the demonstrators also read: "Stop the American bombing! Americans get out, let Vietnam decide its own future!"
    Before the demonstration, approximately two thousand people held a protest rally in Trafalgar Square. The chairman of the rally, Roberts, strongly condemned the American war of aggression in Vietnam. He said that this "dirty, cruel, and disgusting war" must be stopped, and "the madman wielding nuclear weapons, the United States, must be restrained."
    Professor Lynd of Yale University, who is currently in London, stated at the meeting that "the US government's explanation for resuming the bombing (of North Vietnam) has once again deceived the American people and the people of the world." He said that while the US government is talking about "peace," it is stepping up its military build-up in South Vietnam and is now further escalating the war.
    Lynd also condemned the British Labour government for assisting and encouraging the aggressive policies of the United States.
    The meeting unanimously adopted a resolution expressing "the strongest protest against the US military's resumption of bombing of North Vietnam," demanding that the Johnson administration end "America's barbarous, dangerous war in Vietnam" and withdraw American troops from South Vietnam.
    The resolution also called on the British government to cease its support for American aggression.

### 英国伦敦公众冒雨举行反美示威  强烈谴责美国恢复轰炸越南北方

1966-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

    英国伦敦公众冒雨举行反美示威
    强烈谴责美国恢复轰炸越南北方
    新华社伦敦五日电　伦敦公众五日下午冒雨举行示威，强烈谴责美国恢复轰炸越南北方，抗议美国侵略越南。示威者纷纷向美国驻伦敦大使馆和英国首相府递交抗议信。
    大约五百名示威者一路上高呼口号：“美国滚出越南！”“美国佬撤出去，撤出去，撤出去！”“威尔逊是约翰逊的鬈毛狗！”示威者举着的标语牌上也写道：“制止美国的轰炸！美国撤出去，让越南决定它自己的前途！”
    在举行示威前，大约两千人在特拉法加广场举行抗议集会，大会主席罗伯茨强烈谴责美国的侵越战争。他说，必须制止这场“肮脏、残酷和令人作呕的战争”，“约束美国这个挥舞核武器的狂人”。
    正在伦敦的美国耶鲁大学教授林德在会上发言指出，“美国政府就恢复轰炸（越南北方）的原因所作的解释再次欺骗了美国人民和世界人民。”他说，美国政府在侈谈“和平”的同时，加紧在南越加强军事力量，现在又使战争进一步升级。
    林德还谴责英国工党政府帮助和鼓励美国的侵略政策。
    集会一致通过决议，表示“最强烈地抗议美军恢复对越南北方的轰炸”，要求约翰逊政府结束“美国在越南的野蛮的、危险的战争”，从南越撤走美国的军队。
    决议还要求英国政府停止对美国侵略政策的支持。
