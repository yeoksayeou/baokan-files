Title: Our Cultural Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-16
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 15th -- A Chinese cultural delegation led by Song Yiping, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Pyongyang on the 15th. The delegation is here to discuss and sign the 1966-1967 executive plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Kang Jiuyong, Vice Chairman of the Korean Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others welcomed the delegation at the station.
    Wang Peng, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, also welcomed the delegation at the station.

### 我文化代表团到达平壤

1966-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团到达平壤
    新华社平壤十五日电　由对外文化联络委员会副主任宋一平率领的中国文化代表团十五日到达平壤。代表团是前来商谈签订中朝文化合作协定一九六六——一九六七年执行计划的。
    朝鲜对外文化联络委员会副委员长康久永等到车站迎接。
    中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办王彭也到车站迎接。
