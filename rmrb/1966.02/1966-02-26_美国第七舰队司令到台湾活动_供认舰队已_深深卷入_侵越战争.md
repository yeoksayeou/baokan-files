Author:
Date: 1966-02-26
Edition: 3

　　U.S. Seventh Fleet Commander Carries Out Activities in Taiwan
    Admits Fleet is "Deeply Involved" in the War of Aggression Against Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, February 25th, Taipei Dispatch: Hayler, the U.S. Navy Seventh Fleet commander and butcher who participated in the massacre of the Vietnamese people, left Keelung this morning by warship to head to the South China Sea after three days of conspiratorial activities in Taiwan.
    According to an Associated Press dispatch from Taipei on the 23rd, Hayler admitted at a press conference, when talking about the American war of aggression against Vietnam, that his fleet was already "deeply involved in the fighting now going on in Vietnam." Approximately half of the American "bombing of Vietnam is being carried out by planes taking off from aircraft carriers." He also revealed that the Seventh Fleet is strengthening its fast boat force in order to conduct close-range "patrols" of the South Vietnamese waters.
    According to reports, Hayler held secret talks with Chiang Kai-shek during his stay in Taiwan and also visited Chiang's naval base.

### 美国第七舰队司令到台湾活动  供认舰队已“深深卷入”侵越战争

1966-02-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　美国第七舰队司令到台湾活动
    供认舰队已“深深卷入”侵越战争
    新华社二十五日讯　台北消息：参加屠杀越南人民的刽子手美国海军第七舰队司令海兰，到台湾进行三天阴谋活动后，今天上午离开基隆乘军舰前往南中国海。
    据美联社二十三日从台北发出的消息说，海兰在记者招待会上谈到美国侵略越南战争时供认，他的舰队已经“深深地卷入了现在正在越南进行的战斗”。美国“对越南的轰炸约有一半是从航空母舰上起飞的飞机进行的”。他还透露，第七舰队正在加强快艇的力量，以便对南越海面进行近距离“巡逻”。
    据报道，海兰在台湾期间，曾和蒋介石密谈，并参观了蒋军海军基地。
