Title: Romanian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-07
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, February 6th – The members of the Romanian cultural delegation who have come to our country to discuss and sign the 1966-1967 execution plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Hipos, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the Romanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Bolea, Chairman of the Mass Culture Committee of the State Committee for Culture and Art, and George, Chief of the Agreement Section of the Cultural Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The head of the delegation is Dumitru Gheorghiu, the Romanian Ambassador to China. Another member of the delegation is Petrescu, Second Secretary of the Romanian Embassy in China.

### 罗马尼亚文化代表团到京

1966-02-07
第3版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚文化代表团到京
    新华社六日讯　来我国商谈签订中罗文化合作协定一九六六年至一九六七年执行计划的罗马尼亚文化代表团团员、罗马尼亚外交部文化司副司长希波什、国家文化艺术委员会群众文化委员会主席波列加和外交部文化司协定处处长乔治，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长是罗马尼亚驻中国大使杜米特鲁·乔治乌，代表团的另一名团员是罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆二等秘书贝特勒斯库。
