Title: National Speed Skating Championships: Junior Girls A and B Group Competitions Conclude; Yanbian's Tian Cailian and Mudanjiang's Li Ming Win Group Championships

Author:
Date: 1966-02-01
Edition: 2

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, January 31st: The 1966 National Speed Skating Championships' Junior Girls A and B group competitions concluded today. The results of the top six athletes in both groups in the all-around competition exceeded the results of the champions of these two groups in last year's National Speed Skating Championships. Among them, the total all-around scores of the top three in the Junior Girls A group reached the Master of Sports standard. The champions of these two groups were fifteen-year-old Tian Cailian from the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture and fourteen-year-old Li Ming from Mudanjiang City.
    In today's 1,000-meter and 3,000-meter races for the Junior Girls A group, the results of the top six athletes all exceeded last year's national first-place results in these two events (1 minute 51.5 seconds and 5 minutes 58.7 seconds). Tian Cailian won first place in the 3,000 meters with a time of 5 minutes 42.8 seconds and fifth place in the 1,000 meters with a time of 1 minute 47.6 seconds. Combined with her first-place finish in the 1,500 meters yesterday (2 minutes 41.9 seconds) and fourth-place finish in the 500 meters (51.3 seconds), her total score was the highest, reaching 216.199 points. Changchun's Zhang Wanqiu scored 217.416 points, taking second place in the all-around. Xu Zhijiang from the Heihe Special District Team of Heilongjiang Province scored 217.516 points, taking third place in the all-around; she also won first place in the 1,000 meters today with a time of 1 minute 45.5 seconds. The total all-around scores of all three of them exceeded the Master of Sports standard of 218 points (the lower the score, the better the result).
    The Junior Girls B group only held three events: 500 meters, 1,000 meters, and 1,500 meters. Li Ming from Mudanjiang City finished fourth in today's 1,000-meter race with a time of 1 minute 53.6 seconds. Combined with her second-place finish in the 1,500 meters yesterday (2 minutes 56.1 seconds) and second-place finish in the 500 meters (54.8 seconds), her total score reached 170.300 points, taking first place. Xu Songyu from the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture scored 170.633 points, taking second place in the all-around. Fu Huimin from the Hulunbuir League of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region scored 172.250 points, taking third place in the all-around. She also won first place in the 1,000 meters with a time of 1 minute 51.5 seconds, which exceeded last year's national first-place result in this event (1 minute 56.2 seconds).

### 全国速度滑冰锦标赛  少年女子甲乙组比赛结束  延边的田彩莲和牡丹江市的李明分获小组冠军

1966-02-01
第2版()
专栏：

    全国速度滑冰锦标赛
    少年女子甲乙组比赛结束
    延边的田彩莲和牡丹江市的李明分获小组冠军
    据新华社长春三十一日电  一九六六年全国速度滑冰锦标赛少年女子甲组和乙组的比赛今天结束。这两个组获得全能前六名的选手的成绩，都超过了去年全国速度滑冰锦标赛这两个组冠军的成绩。其中少年女子甲组前三名的全能总分都达到了运动健将标准。获得这两个组冠军的分别是延边朝鲜族自治州十五岁的田彩莲和牡丹江市十四岁的李明。
    今天进行的少年女子甲组一千米和三千米的比赛，获得前六名的选手的成绩都超过了去年这两项全国第一名的成绩（一分五十一秒五和五分五十八秒七）。田彩莲分别以五分四十二秒八和一分四十七秒六的成绩，获得了三千米的第一名和一千米的第五名，加上她昨天一千五百米第一名（二分四十一秒九）和五百米第四名（五十一秒三）的成绩，结果总分最高，达到了二一六点一九九分。长春的张婉秋以二一七点四一六分获得全能第二名。黑龙江省黑河专区队的徐智江以二一七点五一六分获得全能第三名，她今天还以一分四十五秒五的成绩获得一千米的第一名。她们三人的全能总分都超过了二一八分的运动健将标准（总分越少成绩越好）。
    少年女子乙组只进行五百米、一千米和一千五百米三项比赛。牡丹江市的李明在今天的一千米比赛中，以一分五十三秒六的成绩获得第四名，加上昨天她获得一千五百米第二名（二分五十六秒一）和五百米第二名（五十四秒八）的成绩，结果总分达到了一七○点三○○分，居于首位。延边朝鲜族自治州的许松玉以一七○点六三三分获得全能第二名。内蒙古自治区呼伦贝尔盟的傅惠敏以一七二点二五○分获得全能第三名，她还以一分五十一秒五的成绩获得一千米的第一名，这个成绩超过了去年这项全国第一名的成绩（一分五十六秒二）。
