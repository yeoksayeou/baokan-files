Title: U.S. People in Fifteen Major Cities Demonstrate Against the War of Aggression Against Vietnam. Demonstrators Angrily Ask: "Johnson, How Many Youths Did You Kill Today?" Public in the Netherlands, Switzerland, and Britain Demonstrate, Demanding that the American Yankees Get Out of South Vietnam

Author:
Date: 1966-02-14
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch: New York news—People from all walks of life in the United States held demonstrations and marches in fifteen major cities, including New York, on the 12th, opposing Johnson's war of aggression against Vietnam and demanding that U.S. troops withdraw from Vietnam.

    On this day in New York, about five hundred people held a demonstration march to protest the Johnson administration's policy of aggression against Vietnam and the existence of racism in the United States. Most of the demonstrators were college and middle school students. The signs they held read: "Stop the War in Vietnam!" "Stop the Slaughter in Mississippi and Vietnam!" (Note: Mississippi is a state where racist activities are very rampant.) "Bring the Troops Back Now!" "Give Us Jobs, Not Uniforms!" They also chanted in unison: "Hey, hey, Johnson, how many youths did you kill today?"

    On the same day in Atlanta, Georgia, dozens of people held signs opposing the Vietnam War and demonstrated in front of the stadium. U.S. Secretary of State Rusk was inside the stadium attending a gathering and giving a speech promoting the Vietnam War. A spokesman for the "Southern Coordinating Committee to End the War in Vietnam," which organized this anti-war demonstration, Devan Wilder, announced that similar demonstrations would be held in southern cities such as Jackson, Nashville, Little Rock, and Richmond. About sixty people in New Orleans, Louisiana, also held a demonstration march on this day to protest the American war in Vietnam.
  Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch: In Western European countries, the demonstrations by the masses against American aggression in Vietnam are developing wave after wave.

    According to reports, more than two thousand people in Amsterdam, the capital of the Netherlands, held a massive demonstration on the 12th to oppose the American war of aggression against Vietnam. Holding up signs, they marched on the streets in the cold wind, constantly shouting slogans: "Yankees go home!" "Vietnam belongs to the Vietnamese!" "Oppose poison gas! Oppose napalm bombs!" The demonstrators also gave propaganda speeches to the large crowds that gathered.

    In Lausanne, Switzerland, hundreds of people held a gathering on the 12th. Speakers at the meeting pointed out that U.S. President Johnson's recent "peace offensive" was nothing more than a smokescreen for U.S. aggression against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    On the same day, more than a hundred citizens in London, the capital of Britain, also held a demonstration. They tried to break into Parliament to hold a rally protesting American aggression against Vietnam and opposing the British Labour government's support for American aggression. The British authorities dispatched a large number of police to disperse the demonstrators, and forcibly dragged away a young woman on the spot. However, the demonstrators remained unmoved and shouted slogans. Police authorities carried out large-scale arrests, and later admitted that sixteen demonstrators were arrested, but reports in the press revealed that at least twenty people were arrested.

### 美国人民在十五个大城市示威反对侵越战争  示威群众愤怒质问：“约翰逊你今天杀害了多少青年？”  荷兰瑞士英国公众纷纷示威要求美国佬从南越滚出去

1966-02-14
第5版()
专栏：

    美国人民在十五个大城市示威反对侵越战争
    示威群众愤怒质问：“约翰逊你今天杀害了多少青年？”
    荷兰瑞士英国公众纷纷示威要求美国佬从南越滚出去
    新华社十三日讯　纽约消息：美国各阶层人民十二日在纽约等十五个大城市举行了示威游行，反对约翰逊进行侵越战争，并要求美国军队撤出越南。
    这一天在纽约，约五百名群众举行示威游行，抗议约翰逊政府侵略越南的政策和在美国国内存在种族主义。示威者大部分是大、中学生。他们举着的标语牌上面写着：“停止越南战争！”“停止在密西西比州和在越南的屠杀！”（按：密西西比州是种族主义活动十分猖獗的一个州。）“现在就把军队撤回来！”“给我们职业，不要给我们军装！”他们还同声高呼：“嘿，嘿，约翰逊，你今天杀害了多少青年？”
    同一天在佐治亚州亚特兰大市，数十名群众举着反对越南战争的标语牌在体育馆门前示威。美国国务卿腊斯克正在体育馆里面参加一个集会，并且发表鼓吹越南战争的演说。组织这次反战示威的“结束越南战争美国南部协调委员会”发言人德万·怀尔德宣布，类似的示威将在杰克逊、纳什维尔、小石城和里士满等南方城市举行。路易斯安纳州新奥尔良市约六十名群众，也在这一天举行了示威游行，抗议美国进行越南战争。
  据新华社十三日讯　西欧国家群众反对美国侵略越南的示威，一浪接着一浪，不断发展。
    据报道，荷兰首都阿姆斯特丹两千多群众十二日举行声势浩大的示威，反对美国侵略越南的战争。他们高举标语牌，冒着寒风在大街上行进，并且不断高呼口号：“美国佬滚回去！”“越南是越南人的越南！”“反对毒气！反对凝固汽油弹！”示威者还对聚集起来的大量群众作了宣传演说。
    在瑞士的洛桑市，十二日有数百名群众举行集会。在会上讲话的人指出，美国总统约翰逊最近玩弄的“和平攻势”，只不过是为美国侵略越南民主共和国施放烟幕。
    同一天，英国首都伦敦的市民百余人也举行示威。他们试图冲入议会，举行抗议美国侵略越南和反对英国工党政府支持美国侵略政策的集会。英国当局派出了大批警察来驱散示威者，并且当场强行架走了一位女青年。但是示威者仍然屹然不动并且高呼口号。警察当局进行了大规模逮捕，并在事后承认，有十六名示威者被捕，但是据报刊透露，遭到逮捕的人至少在二十人以上。
