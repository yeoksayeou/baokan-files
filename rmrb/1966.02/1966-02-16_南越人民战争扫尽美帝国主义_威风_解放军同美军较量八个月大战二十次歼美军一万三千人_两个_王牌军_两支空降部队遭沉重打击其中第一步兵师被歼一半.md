Title: South Vietnamese People's War Sweeps Away the "Prestige" of U.S. Imperialism; PLA Fights U.S. Troops for Eight Months, Twenty Major Battles Annihilate 13,000 U.S. Troops; Two "Ace Divisions" and Two Airborne Units Suffer Heavy Blows, Including the First Infantry Division Being Half Annihilated
Author:
Date: 1966-02-16
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 15th - According to the South Vietnam Liberation Press Agency, from the end of May last year to January this year, during the eight months of the large-scale U.S. troop increase in South Vietnam, the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces continuously dealt heavy blows to the U.S. invading forces, thwarting the U.S. imperialist plot to seize the initiative on the South Vietnam battlefield by directly involving U.S. troops in the war. According to statistics, in just twenty large-scale battles, the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces annihilated nearly 13,000 U.S. troops, including the complete annihilation of ten U.S. battalions and fifteen U.S. companies, shooting down 160 aircraft of various types, destroying more than 140 amphibious armored vehicles and tanks, and 30 trucks.

    The report said that in order to save its failure in South Vietnam, U.S. imperialism has continuously sent its so-called "most elite" U.S. First Cavalry Division and other U.S. troops into South Vietnam since the end of May last year, and established a series of important military bases in Da Nang, Chu Lai, Phu Bai, Qui Nhon, Cam Ranh Bay, An Khe, Pleiku, Vung Tau, Bien Hoa, Ben Cat, and other places, attempting to use these U.S. troops and bases to launch offensives against the liberated areas in order to realize its "pacification" and expand the U.S.-puppet occupied areas plan. The report said that the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces adopted various combat forms, resolutely and bravely fought the enemy, sometimes attacking, sometimes ambushing, sometimes encircling and annihilating the "mopping-up" enemies, and sometimes holding the enemy back. The U.S. First Cavalry Division and First Infantry Division, which were heavily touted by U.S. imperialism as the "most elite," were also stripped of their prestige and became disoriented in the face of the heroic South Vietnam Liberation Army and guerrillas.

    The report said that the Liberation Army has grown rapidly in the battles against the U.S. invading forces. They are not only able to annihilate an entire U.S. battalion in one battle, but also able to annihilate the equivalent of an entire U.S. regiment in one fell swoop. According to statistics, the darling of U.S. Defense Secretary McNamara—the First Cavalry Division—has not only gone bankrupt tactically, but its troops have been annihilated by nearly 3,000, and 74 aircraft have been shot down; the First Infantry Division has successively had four battalions and three companies annihilated, totaling more than 5,500, accounting for about half of the division's strength. The Liberation Armed Forces also shot down more than 50 of its various aircraft, destroyed 117 M-113 amphibious armored vehicles and tanks, and 10 other military vehicles; the U.S. 173rd Airborne Brigade has lost half of its combat effectiveness; a brigade of the 101st Airborne Division has also been annihilated by more than 520, with more than 10 aircraft and more than 20 military vehicles destroyed.

    The report said that at present, the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces are continuing to severely attack U.S. troops and their puppet troops on various battlefields, preparing to give them even heavier blows.

### 南越人民战争扫尽美帝国主义“威风”  解放军同美军较量八个月大战二十次歼美军一万三千人  两个“王牌军”两支空降部队遭沉重打击其中第一步兵师被歼一半

1966-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    南越人民战争扫尽美帝国主义“威风”
    解放军同美军较量八个月大战二十次歼美军一万三千人
    两个“王牌军”两支空降部队遭沉重打击其中第一步兵师被歼一半
    新华社河内十五日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，从去年五月底到今年一月，即美国向越南南方大举增兵的八个月中，越南南方解放武装力量连续给美国侵略军以沉重打击，粉碎了美帝国主义妄图以美军直接参战来夺取南越战场主动权的阴谋。据统计，仅仅在二十次大规模的战斗中，越南南方解放武装力量就歼灭了美军近一万三千名，其中全歼美军十个营和十五个连，击落各种飞机一百六十架，击毁两栖装甲车和坦克一百四十多辆和卡车三十辆。
    报道说，美帝国主义为了挽救它在南越的失败，自去年五月底以来不断地把它吹嘘为“最精锐”的美国第一骑兵师和其他美国部队派进越南南方，并在岘港、朱莱、芙拜、归仁、金兰湾、安溪、波来古、头顿、边和、边葛等地建立了一系列重要的军事基地，妄图利用这些美军和基地向解放区发动进攻，以实现它的“绥靖”和扩大美伪占领区的计划。报道说，越南南方解放武装力量采取了各种各样的作战形式，坚强和英勇地打击敌人，有时出击，有时伏击，有时包围消灭“扫荡”的敌人，有时拖住敌人的腿打。被美帝国主义大肆吹嘘为“最精锐”的美国第一骑兵师和第一步兵师，在英雄的越南南方解放军和游击队的面前也被扫尽了威风，被打得蒙头转向。
    报道说，解放军在同美国侵略军的战斗中已经迅速成长，他们不仅在一次战斗中能够歼灭美军整个营，而且还能一举歼灭美军相当于整个团的兵力。据统计，美国国防部长麦克纳马拉的宠儿——第一骑兵师不仅在战术上已经破产，而且它的部队已被歼近三千名，被击落飞机七十四架；第一步兵师已先后被歼四个营和三个连，共五千五百多名，约占全师兵力的一半。解放武装力量还击落它的各种飞机五十多架，击毁M—113两栖装甲车和坦克一百一十七辆和其他军用车十辆；美国第一七三空降旅已丧失了一半战斗力；第一零一空降师的一个旅也已被歼五百二十多名，被击毁飞机十多架和军车二十多辆。
    报道说，目前，越南南方解放武装力量正继续在各个战场上痛击美军及其仆从军，准备给它们以更加沉重的打击。
