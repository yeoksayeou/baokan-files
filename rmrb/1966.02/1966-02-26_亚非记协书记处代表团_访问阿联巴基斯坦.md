Title: Delegation of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association Visits the United Arab Republic and Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1966-02-26
Edition: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 24th: A delegation from the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association (AAJA) concluded its visit to the United Arab Republic and left Cairo this morning.

　　The delegation consisted of Morrison (South Africa), Secretary of the Secretariat of the AAJA, and Sugiyama Ichibei (Japan).

　　During their visit to Cairo, they met with Hafez Mahmoud, Chairman of the Arab Republic of Egypt Press Association, and Sabri Abu al-Majid, Secretary-General of the Arab Journalists Association, and had friendly conversations with them. They also met with representatives of national liberation movements such as the Supreme Revolutionary Council of Congo (Léopoldville) and the Palestine Liberation Organization, as well as representatives of some African nationalist parties stationed in Cairo.

　　Prior to this, the delegation visited Pakistan. During their visit to Pakistan, the Pakistan Federation of Journalists (PFUJ) held a welcoming banquet for the delegation. Asrar Ahmed, President of the PFUJ, expressed full support for the AAJA Secretariat's position of withdrawing from Indonesia. Before the banquet, the delegation also held a meeting with more than sixty Pakistani journalists. Morrison gave a report on the activities of the AAJA and expressed support for the struggle of the Pakistani people against imperialism, old and new colonialism. Sultan Ahmed, President of the Karachi Journalists Association, expressed support for the activities of the AAJA.

### 亚非记协书记处代表团  访问阿联巴基斯坦

1966-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　亚非记协书记处代表团
    访问阿联巴基斯坦
    新华社开罗二十四日电　亚非新闻工作者协会书记处代表团结束了对阿联的访问，今天清晨离开开罗。
    代表团是由亚非新闻工作者协会书记处书记摩里逊（南非）和杉山市平（日本）组成的。
  他们在开罗访问期间，会见了阿联报业协会主席哈菲兹·马哈茂德和阿拉伯新闻工作者协会总书记萨布里·阿布勒·马吉德，并且同他们进行了友好谈话。他们还会见了刚果（利）革命最高委员会和巴勒斯坦解放组织等民族解放运动以及一些非洲民族主义者政党驻开罗的代表。
    在这之前，代表团访问了巴基斯坦。在巴基斯坦访问期间，巴基斯坦新闻工作者联合会为代表团举行了欢迎宴会。巴基斯坦新闻工作者联合会主席阿斯拉尔·艾哈迈德表示完全支持亚非新闻工作者协会书记处从印度尼西亚撤出的立场。宴会前，代表团还同巴基斯坦新闻工作者六十多人举行了会晤，摩里逊就亚非新闻工作者协会的活动作了报告，并对巴基斯坦人民反对帝国主义、新老殖民主义的斗争表示支持。卡拉奇新闻工作者协会主席苏丹·艾哈迈德对亚非新闻工作者协会的活动表示支持。
