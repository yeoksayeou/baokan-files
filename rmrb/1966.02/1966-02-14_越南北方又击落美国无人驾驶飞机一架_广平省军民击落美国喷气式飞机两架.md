Title: North Vietnam Shoots Down Another US Drone; Quang Binh Province's Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two US Jet Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-02-14
Edition: 4

    North Vietnam Shoots Down Another US Drone
    Quang Binh Province's Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two US Jet Aircraft
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 13th – According to an announcement here on the evening of the 13th, the military and civilians of Ninh Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down a US unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft that had intruded on the 13th.
    This is the 15th US unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft shot down by the military and civilians of North Vietnam.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 11th – According to an announcement here on the 11th, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down two US AD-6 jet aircraft on the 10th. Two others were damaged. According to another report, the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army sent an urgent telegram to the International Commission of Vietnam on the 11th, strongly protesting the US bandit aircraft's bombing and strafing of many residential areas and economic facilities in the provinces of Quang Binh, Ha Tinh, Nghe An, and Thanh Hoa on the 10th.

### 越南北方又击落美国无人驾驶飞机一架  广平省军民击落美国喷气式飞机两架

1966-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    越南北方又击落美国无人驾驶飞机一架
    广平省军民击落美国喷气式飞机两架
    新华社河内十三日电　据这里十三日晚上公布，越南北方宁平省军民，十三日打落入侵的美国无人驾驶高空侦察机一架。
    这是越南北方军民击落的第十五架美国无人驾驶高空侦察机。
    新华社河内十一日电　据这里十一日公布：越南北方广平省军民，十日击落美国AD—6型喷气机两架。另外击伤两架。另据报道，越南人民军总司令部联络代表团十一日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会，强烈抗议美国强盗飞机十日对广平、河静、义安、清化等省的许多居民区和经济设施进行轰炸、扫射。
