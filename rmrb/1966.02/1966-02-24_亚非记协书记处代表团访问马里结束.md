Title: Delegation of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association Concludes Visit to Mali

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-24
Edition: 4

　　新华社巴马科二十二日电　亚非新闻工作者协会书记处代表团结束了在马里的访问后，二十二日离开巴马科。
　　这个由亚非新闻工作者协会书记处常任书记阿·拉·阿布库斯（叙利亚）和陈泉璧（中国）组成的代表团是在二十日到达巴马科进行访问的。马里新闻和游览部长、马里全国新闻工作者联合会主席马马杜·戈洛戈二十一日接见和设宴招待了代表团。同天，代表团还同以戈洛戈为首的马里全国新闻工作者联合会负责人进行了会谈。

Xinhua News Agency, Bamako, February 22nd - The delegation of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association concluded its visit to Mali and left Bamako on the 22nd.
　　The delegation, composed of A. L. Abukus (Syria), Permanent Secretary of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association, and Chen Quanbi (China), arrived in Bamako on the 20th for a visit. Mamadou Gologo, Malian Minister of Information and Tourism and Chairman of the National Union of Journalists of Mali, received and hosted a banquet for the delegation on the 21st. On the same day, the delegation also held talks with the heads of the National Union of Journalists of Mali, led by Gologo.
