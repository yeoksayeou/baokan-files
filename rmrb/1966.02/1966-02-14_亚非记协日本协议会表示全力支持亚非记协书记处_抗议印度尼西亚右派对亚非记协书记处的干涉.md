Title: Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Japanese Committee Expresses Full Support for the AAJA Secretariat, Protests Indonesian Right-Wing Interference in the AAJA Secretariat
Author:
Date: 1966-02-14
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 13th -- The Afro-Asian Journalists' Association (AAJA) Japanese Committee issued a statement on the 10th, protesting the interference by Indonesian right-wing forces in the AAJA Secretariat in Jakarta and expressing full support for all measures taken by the Secretariat to date on this issue.
    The statement said that the Japanese Committee, in conjunction with the appeals and statements issued by the Secretariat on December 22nd of last year and thereafter, had carefully studied the turbulent situation in Indonesia since last October and had determined that the actions taken by the AAJA Secretariat were correct and appropriate.
    The statement emphasized that the illegal interference and suppression of the AAJA Secretariat by Indonesian right-wing forces are absolutely intolerable. "The AAJA, as an international mass organization, will not tolerate interference from any country or organization, nor will it interfere in the internal affairs of any country or organization." The statement also said: The new leadership of the Indonesian Journalists' Association regards the AAJA as its own appendage and interferes in the affairs of the AAJA, which is not permissible.
    The statement concluded by stating that the Japanese Committee is determined to hold high the banner of the AAJA Jakarta Declaration adopted in 1963, eliminate the divisive and destructive activities of the right-wing forces, strengthen solidarity with all progressive Afro-Asian journalists, and carry the struggle to final victory.

### 亚非记协日本协议会表示全力支持亚非记协书记处  抗议印度尼西亚右派对亚非记协书记处的干涉

1966-02-14
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非记协日本协议会表示全力支持亚非记协书记处
    抗议印度尼西亚右派对亚非记协书记处的干涉
    新华社十三日讯　东京消息：亚非新闻工作者协会日本协议会十日发表声明，抗议印度尼西亚右派势力对设在雅加达的亚非新闻工作者协会书记处的干涉，并表示全力支持书记处在这个问题上迄今采取的一切措施。
    声明说，日本协议会结合书记处去年十二月二十二日和以后发表的呼吁书和声明，注意研究了去年十月以来印度尼西亚的动乱局势，并且断定，亚非新闻工作者协会书记处所采取的行动都是正确和适当的。
    声明强调说，印度尼西亚右派势力对亚非新闻工作者协会书记处的非法干涉和镇压是绝对不能容忍的。“亚非新闻工作者协会作为一个国际群众组织不会容忍任何国家或组织的干涉，它也不会干涉任何国家或组织的内部事务。”声明还说：印度尼西亚新闻工作者协会的新领导把亚非新闻工作者协会看作它自己的附属物，并干涉亚非新闻工作者协会的事务是不能容许的。
    声明最后表示日本协议会决心高举一九六三年通过的亚非新闻工作者协会雅加达宣言的旗帜，消除右派势力的分裂和破坏活动，加强同一切亚非进步新闻工作者的团结，把斗争进行到获得最后的胜利。
