Title: Two Polish Medical Experts Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-02-07
Edition: 3

    Two Polish Medical Experts Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, February 4th – According to the implementation plan of the Sino-Polish health cooperation agreement, Marian Śliwiński, Director of the Department of Higher Medical Education and Scientific Research of the Polish Ministry of Health and Social Welfare and lecturer in cardiac surgery, and Janusz Bardach, Professor of Maxillofacial Plastic Surgery, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a professional inspection in our country.

### 波兰两位医学专家到京

1966-02-07
第3版()
专栏：

    波兰两位医学专家到京
    新华社四日讯　根据中波卫生合作协定执行计划，来我国进行专业考察的波兰卫生和社会福利部高等医学教育与科学研究司司长、心脏外科讲师马利安·希利温斯基和颌面整形外科教授杨努什·巴尔达赫今天乘飞机到达北京。
