Title: Equatorial Guinea
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-18
Edition: 4

    Equatorial Guinea, also known as Spanish Guinea, is located on the west coast of Africa, bordering the Gulf of Guinea and close to the equator. It consists of Fernando Po Island and some other islands, and the Muni River area on the African mainland. It has an area of more than 28,000 square kilometers and a population of approximately 250,000. The capital is Santa Isabel on Fernando Po Island.
    At the end of the 15th century, the Portuguese established colonial rule on Fernando Po Island. In 1778, Spain exchanged its territories in the Americas for Fernando Po Island and the Muni River area. By 1846, Spain had successively annexed other islands. In 1958, Spain sent a governor to rule.
    The Africans in this region have long suffered from the cruel rule of the colonizers, and their lives have been very miserable. With the vigorous development of the African national liberation movement, the voices of the people of Equatorial Guinea demanding independence and freedom have also risen. Under these circumstances, in order to maintain its colonial rule, the Franco government stepped up its political deception tactics. In December 1963, it held a "referendum" in Equatorial Guinea, passed "internal autonomy" regulations, and implemented "internal autonomy" on January 1 of the following year, but the real power was still in the hands of the Spanish colonizers. (Xinhua News Agency) (with picture)

### 赤道几内亚

1966-02-18
第4版()
专栏：资料

    赤道几内亚
    赤道几内亚又称西属几内亚。在非洲西岸，濒临几内亚湾、靠近赤道。它由斐南多波岛以及其他一些岛屿和非洲大陆上的莫尼河区组成。面积二万八千多平方公里，人口约二十五万人。首府是斐南多波岛上的圣伊萨贝尔。
    十五世纪末，葡萄牙人在斐南多波岛建立殖民统治。一七七八年西班牙用它在美洲的领地换取了斐南多波岛和莫尼河区，到一八四六年，西班牙又先后并吞了其它岛屿。一九五八年，西班牙派总督进行统治。
    这个地区的非洲人长期遭受殖民者的残酷统治，生活非常悲惨，随着非洲民族解放运动的蓬勃开展，赤道几内亚人民要求独立和自由的呼声也日益高涨。在这种情况下，佛朗哥政府为了维持它的殖民统治，加紧采取政治欺骗手段，在一九六三年十二月在赤道几内亚举行“公民投票”，通过“内部自治”法规，并且在第二年一月一日实行“内部自治”，但实权仍操在西班牙殖民者手中。　（新华社）（附图片）
