Title: Cambodian Defense Forces Repulse Invading Thai Troops
Author:
Date: 1966-02-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 8th (Dispatch) - According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on February 8th, the commander of Cambodia's Third Military Region announced preliminary information stating that Thai troops invaded Cambodian territory near Kao Romeat, a border village in Thmar Puok district of Battambang province, on the morning of February 7th.

    The invaders were resisted by a patrol of the Cambodian Defense Forces. After more than an hour of exchange of fire, the invaders fled back into their own territory.

    Two people on the Cambodian side were injured.

### 柬埔寨国防军击退入侵的泰国军队

1966-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国防军击退入侵的泰国军队
    新华社金边八日电　据柬埔寨新闻社八日报道，柬埔寨第三军区司令公布的初步消息说，泰国军队七日早晨侵入马德望省特马普县的边境村庄——考罗梅附近柬埔寨的领土。
    入侵者遭到柬埔寨国防军巡逻队的抗击。在双方交火一个多小时以后，入侵者逃回自己境内。
    柬埔寨方面有两人受伤。
