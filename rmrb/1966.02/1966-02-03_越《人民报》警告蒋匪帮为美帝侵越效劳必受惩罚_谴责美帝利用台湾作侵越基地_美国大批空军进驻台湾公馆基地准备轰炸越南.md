Title: Vietnamese "People's Daily" Warns Chiang Kai-shek Gang That Serving American Imperialism in Its Aggression Against Vietnam Will Be Punished, Condemns American Imperialism for Using Taiwan as a Base for Aggression Against Vietnam, Large Numbers of American Air Force Personnel Enter Gongguan Base in Taiwan to Prepare to Bomb Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-02-03
Edition: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 1st – The Vietnamese *People's Daily* published a commentary on February 1st condemning American imperialism for using Taiwan's Gongguan base as a base for its war of aggression against Vietnam.

　　The commentary pointed out that American imperialism's use of Taiwan as a base for its war of aggression against Vietnam exposes its ulterior motives for occupying this territory of China for fifteen years, namely, to use Taiwan not only as a springboard for attacking the Chinese mainland, but also to use it as a springboard for aggression against Asian countries.

　　The commentary said that Chiang Kai-shek, this political corpse, has further exposed his corrupt and despicable nature. He is always prepared to carry out any evil deed according to the orders of his American master.

　　The commentary said that the Vietnamese people sternly warn the Chiang Kai-shek gang that their crimes will not escape due punishment. The failure of American imperialism in Vietnam and in Asia is inevitable. The more the Chiang Kai-shek gang serves American imperialism, the more stubbornly they oppose the Vietnamese people, the Chinese people, and the Asian people, the more rapidly they will move towards their demise.

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd – American imperialism, colluding with the Chiang Kai-shek gang, is using some airports and ports on Chinese territory in Taiwan as military bases to expand its war of aggression against Vietnam.

　　According to reports, a spokesman for the U.S. Air Force in Taiwan announced in Taipei on January 29 that a squadron of U.S. Air Force C-130 "Hercules" aircraft and approximately one thousand U.S. Air Force personnel have entered the recently expanded Gongguan Air Force Base in central Taiwan. United Press International reported from Taipei that these U.S. Air Force personnel were to "support the (invasion) of the Vietnam War," and these aircraft will transport troops to South Vietnam or elsewhere. Western news agencies earlier revealed that the United States is also preparing to send B-52 strategic bombers stationed in Guam to Taiwan to carry out savage bombing of Vietnam.

　　The American aggressors also plan to dispatch approximately 2,000 U.S. Air Force personnel and two squadrons of C-130 "Hercules" aircraft to Gongguan Air Force Base. The U.S. Air Force authorities have decided to expand the U.S. 13th Air Task Force stationed in Taiwan into the 327th Air Division in early February.

　　At the same time, in order to meet the needs of expanding the war of aggression against Vietnam, American imperialism is colluding with the Chiang Kai-shek gang to accelerate the expansion of military ports such as Kaohsiung, Keelung, and Hualien.

　　American imperialism's occupation of Chinese territory in Taiwan, using it not only as a springboard for attacking the Chinese mainland, but also openly using it as a base for its war of aggression against Vietnam, is intolerable to the Chinese people, the Vietnamese people, and the Asian people. The aggressive crimes of American imperialism will inevitably receive due punishment. The Chiang Kai-shek gang's role as an accomplice in the American war of aggression against Vietnam will also not end well.

### 越《人民报》警告蒋匪帮为美帝侵越效劳必受惩罚  谴责美帝利用台湾作侵越基地  美国大批空军进驻台湾公馆基地准备轰炸越南

1966-02-03
第3版()
专栏：

    越《人民报》警告蒋匪帮为美帝侵越效劳必受惩罚
    谴责美帝利用台湾作侵越基地
    美国大批空军进驻台湾公馆基地准备轰炸越南
    新华社河内一日电　越南《人民报》一日发表评论，谴责美帝国主义利用台湾的公馆基地来作为侵越战争的基地。
    评论指出，美帝国主义利用台湾作为侵略越南战争的基地，暴露了它十五年来侵占中国这块领土的不可告人的野心，即不仅利用台湾作为进攻中国大陆的跳板，而且也利用它来作为侵略亚洲各国的跳板。
    评论说，蒋介石这具政治僵尸，更加暴露出他的腐朽和卑鄙的性质。他随时准备根据美国主子的命令，干任何罪恶的勾当。
    评论说，越南人民严厉警告蒋介石匪帮，它们的罪恶是逃不了应有的惩罚的。美帝国主义在越南和在亚洲的失败是注定了的。蒋介石匪帮越要为美帝国主义效劳，越顽固地反对越南人民、中国人民和亚洲人民，它们就越加速地走向死亡。
    新华社二日讯　美帝国主义勾结蒋介石匪帮，正在利用中国领土台湾的一些机场和港口，作为它扩大侵略越南战争的军事基地。
    据报道，侵台美国空军发言人一月二十九日在台北宣布，美国空军C—130“大力士”式飞机一个中队和大约一千名美国空军人员，已经进驻台湾中部最近扩建的公馆空军基地。美国合众国际社从台北发出的消息说，这些美国空军是为了“支持（侵略）越南战争”的，这些飞机将把军队运到越南南方或其它地方。西方通讯社早些时候透露，美国还准备把驻扎在关岛的B—52型战略轰炸机派到台湾，以便对越南进行野蛮轰炸。
    美国侵略者还计划调遣美国空军人员二千名左右和C—130“大力士”式飞机两个中队进驻公馆空军基地。美国空军当局已经决定，在二月初，将把侵驻在台湾的美国第十三特遣航空队，扩大为第三二七空军师。
    与此同时，美帝国主义为适应扩大侵越战争的需要，正伙同蒋介石匪帮加紧扩建高雄、基隆、花莲等军用港口。
    美帝国主义霸占中国领土台湾，不仅把它作为进攻中国大陆的跳板，而且公然把它作为侵略越南战争的基地，这是中国人民、越南人民和亚洲人民所不能容忍的。美帝国主义的侵略罪行必将受到应有的惩罚。蒋介石匪帮充当美国侵略越南战争的帮凶也决不会有好下场。
