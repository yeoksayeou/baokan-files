Title: Phoumi Vongvichit Leads Lao Delegation to Beijing En Route Home After Attending the Afro-Asian-Latin American People's Solidarity Conference
Author:
Date: 1966-02-23
Edition: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd: The Lao delegation, headed by Phoumi Vongvichit, Secretary-General of the Lao Patriotic Front and Minister of Information, Propaganda, and Tourism of the Lao National Unity Government, arrived in Beijing today on their way home after attending the Afro-Asian-Latin American People's Solidarity Conference. Greeting them at the airport were Wu Xueqian and Zhu Ziqi, Vice-Chairmen of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, and Wang Zhifan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

### 参加亚非拉人民团结会议后回国途中　冯维希率老挝代表团到京

1966-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加亚非拉人民团结会议后回国途中
　　冯维希率老挝代表团到京
    新华社二十二日讯　以老挝爱国战线党总书记、老挝民族团结政府新闻、宣传和游览大臣富米·冯维希为首的老挝代表团，参加亚非拉人民团结会议以后在回国途中于今天到达北京。前往机场欢迎的，有中国亚非团结委员会副主席吴学谦、朱子奇和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会书记处书记王植范等。
