Title: Japanese Communist Party Delegation Visits the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-23
Edition: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 21st (dispatch): According to the Vietnam News Agency, Okazaki Yoshiharu, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Japanese Communist Party and other members of the delegation, who are currently visiting here, visited the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front on the morning of the 21st.
    They were welcomed by Hoang Quoc Viet, member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, and Le Dinh Tham, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Fatherland Front and chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and others. Hoang Quoc Viet delivered a welcoming speech. He said that the visit of the Japanese Communist Party delegation is a great encouragement to the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation. He introduced to the distinguished guests the activities of the Fatherland Front in various aspects, especially the situation of uniting all the Vietnamese people to fight against the U.S. aggressors.
    Okazaki Yoshiharu expressed his deep admiration for the power of the fighting solidarity of the Vietnamese people. He emphasized the close relationship between the Vietnamese people and the Japanese people in the struggle against their common enemy, U.S. imperialism.

### 日共代表团访问越南祖国战线中央

1966-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　日共代表团访问越南祖国战线中央
    新华社河内二十一日电  据越南通讯社报道，正在这里访问的日本共产党代表团团员、日共中央政治局常务委员冈正芳以及代表团其他一些团员，二十一日上午访问了越南祖国战线中央委员会。
    他们受到了越南劳动党中央委员、越南祖国战线中央主席团委员黄国越，祖国战线中央主席团委员、越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探等人的欢迎。黄国越致欢迎词。他说，日本共产党代表团对越南的访问，对越南人民的抗美救国斗争是一个巨大的鼓舞。他向贵宾介绍了祖国战线在各个方面的活动情况，特别是在团结全体越南人民同美国侵略者进行斗争的情况。
    冈正芳对越南人民的战斗团结的威力深深表示钦佩。他强调指出了越南人民和日本人民在反对他们的共同敌人美帝国主义的斗争中的密切关系。
