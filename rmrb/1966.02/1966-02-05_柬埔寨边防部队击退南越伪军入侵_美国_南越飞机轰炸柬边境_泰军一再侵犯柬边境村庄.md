Title: Cambodian Border Troops Repulse Incursion of South Vietnamese Puppet Troops; US-South Vietnamese Aircraft Bomb Cambodian Border; Thai Troops Repeatedly Violate Cambodian Border Villages
Author:
Date: 1966-02-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 3rd dispatch: Cambodian News Agency reported on the 3rd: On January 15th, three US-South Vietnamese jet aircraft intruded into the airspace above the Datang post in Mondulkiri Province, Cambodia. Later, these aircraft flew to the Aohe area, five kilometers from the Datang post, and bombed the area for thirty minutes.
    On the same day, a US-South Vietnamese puppet four-engine aircraft intruded into the airspace above the Tabian Teluo post in Mondulkiri Province, Cambodia.
    In addition, on the morning of January 9th, twenty US-South Vietnamese puppet troops invaded Bawei Village in Svay Rieng Province, Cambodia. They fled after being countered by a Cambodian patrol.
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 4th dispatch: According to a Cambodian News Agency report on the 4th, at 17:00 on January 30th, armed personnel from Thailand invaded Cambodian territory in the Aopaer area of Oddar Meanchey Province, penetrating seven kilometers. They opened fire on local Cambodian villagers, killing one Cambodian villager and wounding one.
    At 17:00 on February 3rd, Thai troops fired artillery at a Cambodian post in the Aosima area of Oddar Meanchey Province for fifteen minutes, killing and wounding one Cambodian guard each.

### 柬埔寨边防部队击退南越伪军入侵  美国—南越飞机轰炸柬边境　泰军一再侵犯柬边境村庄

1966-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨边防部队击退南越伪军入侵
    美国—南越飞机轰炸柬边境　泰军一再侵犯柬边境村庄
    据新华社金边三日电　柬埔寨新闻社三日报道：一月十五日，三架美国—南越喷气式飞机侵入柬埔寨蒙多基里省达丹哨所的上空。后来，这批飞机飞往离达丹哨所五公里的奥霍地区，并轰炸这个地方达三十分钟。
    同一天，一架美国—南越伪军四引擎飞机侵入柬埔寨蒙多基里省塔卞特洛哨所上空。
    此外，一月九日上午，二十名美国—南越傀儡军曾经侵入柬埔寨柴桢省巴韦村。他们在受到柬埔寨巡逻队的反击之后才逃走。
    据新华社金边四日电　据柬埔寨新闻社四日报道，一月三十日十七时，来自泰国的武装人员侵入奥多棉藏省奥帕尔地区的柬埔寨领土深达七公里的地方。他们向当地柬埔寨村民开枪，打死柬埔寨村民一人，打伤一人。
    二月三日十七时，泰国军队向奥多棉藏省奥斯马地方的柬埔寨哨所开炮十五分钟，打死和打伤柬埔寨守卫人员各一人。
