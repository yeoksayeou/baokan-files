Title: Medical University Associate Professor Departs for Canada to Lecture

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-06
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, February 5th—According to the "Bethune Lectureship" agreement on mutual exchange of professors for lecturing between the China Medical University and McGill University of Canada, Fang Qi, associate professor of internal medicine at China Medical University, and another person, left Beijing for Canada today.

### 我医科大学副教授前往加拿大讲学

1966-02-06
第5版()
专栏：

    我医科大学副教授前往加拿大讲学
    新华社五日讯　根据中国医科大学和加拿大麦吉尔大学之间的“白求恩讲座”互派教授讲学的协定，中国医科大学内科副教授方圻等两人今天离开北京前往加拿大。
