Title: Cambodian Defense Forces Repulse Intrusion by South Vietnamese Puppet Troops
Author:
Date: 1966-02-02
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 1st - According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on February 1st, approximately 30 South Vietnamese puppet troops invaded Cambodian territory as far as 1,000 meters into the village of Ba Vet in Svay Rieng province on January 14th.

    The invaders exchanged fire with the Cambodian Defense Forces for 30 minutes before fleeing back into South Vietnamese territory.

    American-South Vietnamese puppet troop aircraft also intruded into the airspace of Svay Rieng province in Cambodia three times on January 10th and 18th.

### 柬埔寨国防军击退入侵南越伪军

1966-02-02
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国防军击退入侵南越伪军
    新华社金边一日电　据柬埔寨新闻社一日报道，约三十名南越伪军一月十四日侵入柴桢省巴韦村的柬埔寨领土深达一千米的地方。
    侵略者同柬埔寨的国防军交火三十分钟后，逃回南越境内。
    美国—南越伪军飞机一月十日和十八日还三次侵入柬埔寨柴桢省上空。
