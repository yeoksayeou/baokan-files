Title: Inherit and Carry Forward the "February 7th" Hard-Bone Revolutionary Spirit, Contribute to the Struggle of Building and Defending the Motherland; Workers in Beijing, Zhengzhou, Wuhan and Other Places Commemorate the 43rd Anniversary of the "February 7th" Strike
Author:
Date: 1966-02-08
Edition: 2

    According to Xinhua News Agency, February 7th: Workers in Beijing, Zhengzhou, Wuhan, Guangzhou and other places have held various activities in the past few days to commemorate the 43rd anniversary of the "February 7th" Great Strike. At the commemorative gatherings, people expressed their determination to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, inherit and carry forward the revolutionary predecessors of "February 7th" spirit of hard-bone revolution of not fearing death, and strive to realize the Third Five-Year Plan and oppose the aggressive conspiracies of American imperialism.
    The workers of Changxindian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Plant held a grand commemorative meeting tonight. Hang Baohua, an old worker of "February 7th," and Wang Feng, the director of the factory, introduced the glorious history of the "February 7th" struggle at the meeting. They hoped that all the workers of the factory would inherit and carry forward the anti-imperialist spirit of the "February 7th" strike. Zhang Jiang, a representative of young workers and militia, guaranteed in his speech on behalf of the younger generation of workers that he would study Chairman Mao's works well, take over the class of Mao Zedong Thought, take over the class of old workers of "February 7th," and make contributions to the construction of the motherland and the anti-imperialist revolutionary cause of the people of the world. Since mid-January, the workers of this factory have carried out commemorative activities in the form of gatherings, visits to old workers of "February 7th," visits to the "February 7th" Exhibition Hall, and publishing blackboard newspapers. A symposium of old workers of "February 7th" was also held recently, where the elders spoke freely about the excellent situation at home and abroad and thanked the Party for its meticulous care for them. They expressed their determination to cultivate the next generation of revolutionaries and work hard for socialist revolution and socialist construction. Some units under the Beijing Railway Bureau also widely held commemorative activities.
    More than 1,600 representatives of workers from all walks of life in Zhengzhou and old workers who participated in the "February 7th" strike held a commemorative meeting this morning at the former site of the Jinghan Railway Workers' General Union, now the "February 7th Memorial Hall." Wang Junzhi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Zhengzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Zhengzhou, spoke at the meeting about the significance of the "February 7th" strike, calling on all workers in the city to inherit and carry forward the revolutionary spirit of the "February 7th" struggle, and to take Mao Zedong Thought as the highest instruction for all work. He also called on all workers in the city to learn from Daqing, run Daqing-style enterprises, be Daqing-style workers, and to carry out a comprehensive and in-depth movement of emulation, learning, catching up, helping, and surpassing in increasing production and practicing economy, and to strive to complete the task of the first year of the Third Five-Year Plan. More than sixty old workers of "February 7th" and representatives of young workers in Zhengzhou also held a forum together. In their speeches, they all expressed their determination to carry forward the revolutionary spirit of "February 7th." Many units in Zhengzhou have held commemorative activities in the past few days.
    More than 800 people from all walks of life in Wuhan held a commemorative gathering this morning. Cui Bingliang, vice chairman of the Wuhan Federation of Trade Unions, expressed his condolences on behalf of the people of all walks of life in Wuhan to the old workers and families of martyrs who participated unswervingly in the "February 7th" struggle. When he talked about inheriting and carrying forward the revolutionary tradition and revolutionary spirit of the "February 7th" struggle, he pointed out that only by using Mao Zedong Thought as a guide, truly establishing a proletarian world outlook, and consciously doing as the revolutionary martyrs did, not seeking fame, not seeking profit, not fearing hardship, not fearing death, wholeheartedly for the revolution, wholeheartedly for the people, can we complete the great tasks of revolution and construction. After the meeting, the heads of various aspects of Wuhan, old workers of "February 7th," and family members of martyrs who attended the commemorative meeting went to the tomb of Martyr Shi Yang to lay wreaths.
    More than 1,000 old workers and advanced producers in Guangzhou held a commemorative gathering last night. The person in charge of the Guangzhou Federation of Trade Unions, the old workers of the Guangzhou Railway Bureau who once supported the "February 7th" struggle, and representatives of young workers all spoke at the meeting.
    More than 2,000 people from all walks of life in Fujian Province, Fuzhou City, and Minhou County went to the cemetery of "February 7th" martyr Lin Xiangqian today to sweep the tomb. They laid wreaths at the martyr's tomb and planted cypresses around the martyr's cemetery. Representatives from all walks of life also expressed their condolences to the martyr's wife, Chen Guizhen. Since the Spring Festival, more than 10,000 workers, farmers, students, cadres, and commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army in various parts of Fujian Province have gone to the martyr's cemetery to sweep the tombs.

### 继承和发扬“二七”硬骨头革命精神  在建设和保卫祖国斗争中作出贡献  北京郑州武汉等地职工纪念“二七”四十三周年

1966-02-08
第2版()
专栏：

    继承和发扬“二七”硬骨头革命精神
    在建设和保卫祖国斗争中作出贡献
    北京郑州武汉等地职工纪念“二七”四十三周年
    据新华社七日讯　北京、郑州、武汉、广州等地职工这几天举行各种活动，纪念“二七”大罢工四十三周年。人们在纪念集会上纷纷表示要高举毛泽东思想的伟大红旗，继承和发扬“二七”革命前辈不怕死的硬骨头革命精神，为实现第三个五年计划、为反对美帝国主义的侵略阴谋而奋斗。
    长辛店机车车辆厂职工今晚举行隆重的纪念大会。“二七”老工人杭宝华和这个工厂的厂长王峰在会上介绍了“二七”斗争的光荣历史，他们希望全厂职工继承和发扬“二七”的反帝斗争精神。青年工人、民兵代表张江在讲话中代表年轻一代工人保证一定要学好毛主席著作，接好毛泽东思想的班，接好“二七”老工人的班，为祖国的建设事业和世界人民的反帝革命事业作出贡献。这个工厂的职工从一月中旬起就采取集会、访问“二七”老工人、参观“二七”展览馆、出黑板报等形式开展了纪念活动。日前工厂还举行了“二七”老工人座谈会，老人们在会上畅谈了国内外的大好形势，并且感谢党对他们无微不至的关怀。他们表示要为培养革命的下一代，为社会主义革命和社会主义建设而努力。北京铁路分局所属的一些单位也广泛举行了纪念活动。
    郑州市各界职工代表和参加过“二七”罢工斗争的老工人共一千六百多人，今天上午在当年京汉铁路工人总工会成立所在地、现在的“二七纪念堂”举行了纪念大会。中共郑州市委书记处书记、郑州市市长王均智在会上讲述了“二七”罢工斗争的意义，他号召全市职工继承和发扬“二七”斗争的革命精神，把毛泽东思想作为一切工作的最高指示。他还号召全市职工要努力学习大庆，办大庆式企业，做大庆式工人，并且全面深入地开展比学赶帮超的增产节约运动，为完成第三个五年计划第一年的任务而奋斗。六十多名“二七”老工人和郑州市的青年职工代表还在一起举行了座谈会，他们在发言中都表示要发扬“二七”的革命精神。郑州市的许多单位这几天都举行了纪念活动。
    武汉各界八百多人今天上午举行了纪念集会。武汉市总工会副主席崔秉亮在讲话中代表武汉各界人民向坚贞不屈地参加“二七”斗争的老工人和烈士家属表示慰问。他在谈到继承和发扬“二七”斗争的革命传统和革命精神的时候指出，只有用毛泽东思想作指导，真正树立起无产阶级世界观，象革命烈士那样自觉地做到不图名，不图利，不怕苦，不怕死，一心为革命，一心为人民，才能完成革命和建设的伟大任务。会后，参加纪念会的武汉市各方面负责人和“二七”老工人、烈士家属一起到施洋烈士墓献了花圈。
    广州市一千多老工人和先进生产者昨晚举行了纪念集会。广州市总工会的负责人和当年曾经声援“二七”斗争的广州铁路局老工人以及青年工人的代表都在会上讲话。
    福建省、福州市和闽侯县各界人民两千多人今天前往“二七”烈士林祥谦的陵园扫墓。他们向烈士墓献了花圈，并在烈士陵园四周种植了柏树。各界代表还慰问了烈士夫人陈桂贞。春节以来，福建省各地已有一万多名工人、农民、学生、干部和解放军指战员到烈士陵园扫墓。
