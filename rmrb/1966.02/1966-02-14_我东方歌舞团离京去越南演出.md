Title: My Oriental Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Beijing for Performance in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-02-14
Edition: 5

    My Oriental Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Beijing for Performance in Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th – The Chinese Oriental Song and Dance Troupe, a group of fifty-six people led by Zhang Xing, left Beijing by train on the 12th at the invitation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for a performance visit. Li Qi-tao and other responsible persons from relevant departments, as well as officials from the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, went to the station to see them off.

### 我东方歌舞团离京去越南演出

1966-02-14
第5版()
专栏：

    我东方歌舞团离京去越南演出
    新华社十三日讯　应邀前往越南民主共和国访问演出的中国东方歌舞团一行五十六人，在团长张星率领下十二日乘火车离开北京。前往车站欢送的，有李琦涛等有关方面负责人；以及越南民主共和国驻华大使馆官员。
