Title: We are Revolutionary Railway Workers (Dialogue)
Author: Ning Baocai
Date: 1966-02-04
Edition: 6

We are revolutionary railway workers,
	Fighting on the transportation line.
	Our character, our image,
	Is exactly like the locomotive:
	We have bones forged of steel,
	We have the courage to charge forward;
	Our chests are full of blazing fire,
	Our bodies radiate heat and red light;
	We work with swift and decisive action,
	We speak with loud and resounding voices.
	We fear neither hardship,
	Our energy is full and abundant;
	We fear neither death,
	Our fighting spirit is incomparably strong.
	Marching north and south,
	We never leave the tracks;
	Our footing is steady,
	We stand firm in our position.
	Wind and rain cannot mislead us,
	Our minds have a clear direction.

We are revolutionary railway workers,
	Armed with Mao Zedong Thought.
	We understand
	The significance of our work.
	We know
	How a Chinese worker should act.
	Firmly guarding the country's steel transportation line,
	Pushing forward the socialist revolution.
	Hard training for solid skills,
	The tools in our hands are the guns of battle!
	Surging forward in the high tide of production,
	The engine crew and electrical workers are strong and the horses are sturdy.
	The competition to learn from, catch up with, and surpass each other is like a powerful force,
	The Five-Good competition is like a roaring wave.
	Burying ourselves in hard work,
	Adhering to the "Three Olds" and "Four Stricts".
	Utterly loyal,
	Achieving the "Four Alikes".
	Drive each rail spike,
	Showing our loyalty to the Party;
	Lay each sleeper,
	Thinking of the six hundred million people.
	Never be arrogant with achievements,
	Striving to reach greater heights.
	If the motherland wants to build cars and silver eagles,
	We will send iron and steel.
	If the motherland wants to build tall buildings and mansions,
	We will haul bricks and transport beams.
	If the motherland is climbing the peak of industry,
	We will send coal and machine tools.
	If the commune children are at war with nature,
	We will send fertilizer and ploughs.
	If thousands of high mountains are offering treasures for socialism,
	We will send drills and pneumatic guns.
	If ten thousand acres of sandy plains must produce cotton and grain,
	We will send seeds and seedlings.
	If aspiring young people want to go to the vast countryside,
	We will send them to the mountains and the countryside.
	Send the radio from Beijing to Tibet,
	Transport the timber from the Greater Khingan Range to the Huangpu River.
	Dazhai's corn, Daqing's oil,
	Bayan Obo's ore, Guangzhou's sugar.
	Load it up, load it up!
	Load it up,
	Load it up!
	Transport it to the north and south of the country,
	Send it to all directions.
	Let all manpower and materials,
	Generate heat and light for socialism.

We are revolutionary railway workers,
	The Party has given us boundless wisdom and strength.
	A red heart for the motherland,
	Our eyes look out at the world.
	The storms of the five continents surge in our hearts,
	The thunder of Asia, Africa, and Latin America resounds in our ears.
	Supporting our class brothers is our unshirkable duty,
	The responsibility for world revolution rests on our shoulders.
	The revolutionary era needs high speed,
	Then we will load more, run faster,
	Run faster, load more!
	Open the throttle wide, increase the firepower.
	Day and night,
	Our goal:
	Straight ahead.
	When the wind comes,
	Charge!
	When the rain comes,
	Go forth!
	If high mountains block the way,
	Move them!
	If rivers obstruct the path,
	Cross them!
	If the ground sinks,
	We will fill it!
	If the sky falls,
	We will hold it up!
	If we meet monsters and demons,
	Fight!
	If there are jackals, wolves, and tigers,
	Crush them into the mud!
	Trample thousands of difficulties under our feet,
	Sweep away all obstacles!
	With us railway workers here,
	Who dares to block the wheels of progress!
	With us railway workers here,
	Who dares to block the wheels of progress!

**Named Entities**

北京  Beijing
西藏  Xizang (Tibet)
兴安岭  Xing'anling (Greater Khingan Range)
黄浦江  Huangpu River
大寨  Dazhai
大庆  Daqing
白云鄂博  Bayan Obo
广州  Guangzhou
毛泽东思想  Mao Zedong Thought
三老四严  San Lao Si Yan ("Three Olds" and "Four Stricts," referring to honest attitude, honest behaviour, honest working style, strict demands, strict organization, strict attitude towards measurements, and strict observation of regulations)

### 我们是革命的铁路工人（对口词）
宁宝才
1966-02-04
第6版()
专栏：

  我们是革命的铁路工人（对口词）
  呼和浩特铁路局电务工程队通信工　宁宝才
  一
  我们是革命的铁路工人，
  战斗在运输线上。
  我们的性格、形象，
  跟火车头一模一样：
  有钢打铁铸的骨头，
  有冲锋陷阵的胆量；
  满腔熊熊烈火，
  浑身热气红光；
  干活雷厉风行，
  讲话高门大嗓。
  一不怕苦，
  干劲十足充沛；
  二不怕死，
  斗志无比坚强。
  南征北战，
  不离轨道；
  脚跟不乱，
  站稳立场。
  风吹雨打不迷路，
  头脑中有鲜明的方向。
   二
  我们是革命的铁路工人，
  毛泽东思想来武装。
  我们懂得，
  自己工作的意义。
  我们知道，
  中国的工人该怎么当。
  坚守祖国钢铁运输线，
  为社会主义革命推波助浪。
  苦练过硬本领，
  手中的工具就是战斗的枪！
  生产高潮激流勇进，
  车机工电人强马壮。
  比学赶帮超势如破竹，
  五好竞赛如滔滔巨浪。
  埋头苦干，
  坚持三老四严。
  忠心赤胆，
  做到四个一样。
  打一颗道钉，
  拿出对党的忠诚；
  铺一根枕木，
  要为六亿人民着想。
  有了成绩绝不骄傲，
  百尺竿头青云直上。
  祖国要造汽车银鹰，
  我们送铁送钢。
  祖国要盖高楼广厦，
  我们拉砖运梁。
  祖国在攀登工业高峰，
  我们送上煤炭、机床。
  公社儿女跟自然开战，
  我们送上化肥、犁杖。
  千座高山为社会主义献宝，
  我们送去钻头、风枪。
  万顷沙原要交出棉粮，
  我们送去种子、苗秧。
  有志青年要去广阔天地，
  我们送他上山下乡。
  把北京的收音机送到西藏，
  把兴安岭的木材运到黄浦江。
  大寨的玉米、大庆的油，
  白云鄂博的矿石、广州的糖。
  往上装，往上放。
  往上放，
  往上装！
  运往塞北江南，
  送到四面八方。
  让所有的人力物资，
  为社会主义发热发光。
  三
  我们是革命的铁路工人，
  党给了无穷的智慧和力量。
  一颗红心为祖国，
  两眼更把世界望。
  五大洲的风云在胸中激荡，
  亚非拉的雷霆在耳边喧响。
  支援阶级兄弟义不容辞，
  世界革命的责任担在肩上。
  革命的时代需要高速度，
  我们就多装、快跑，
  快跑、多装！
  大开汽门，火力加旺。
  日夜兼程，
  目标：
  正前方。
  风来了，
  冲！
  雨来了，
  上！
  高山拦路，
  搬！
  河水挡道，
  闯！
  地陷下去，
  我们填！
  天塌下来，
  我们扛！
  遇见牛鬼蛇神，
  打！
  有豺狼虎豹，
  碾成泥浆！
  千重困难脚下踩，
  万般障碍一扫光！
  有我铁路工人在，
  前进的车轮谁敢挡！
  有我铁路工人在，
  前进的车轮谁敢挡！
