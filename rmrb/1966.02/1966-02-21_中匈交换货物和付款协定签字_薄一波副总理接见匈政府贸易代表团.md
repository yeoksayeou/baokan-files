Title: China and Hungary sign exchange of goods and payments agreement; Vice Premier Bo Yibo receives Hungarian government trade delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-21
Edition: 4

　　China and Hungary sign exchange of goods and payments agreement
    Vice Premier Bo Yibo receives Hungarian government trade delegation
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th—The 1966 China and Hungary exchange of goods and payments agreement was signed this morning in Beijing.
    Vice Premier Bo Yibo attended the signing ceremony.
    Jiang Ming, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of our country, and Szalai Béla, head of the Hungarian government trade delegation and Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective parties. On the Chinese side, Lin Haiyun, Chen Boqing, and others participated in the signing ceremony.
    On the Hungarian side, Hungarian Ambassador to China Halász József, Deputy Head of the Hungarian government trade delegation Kodmon, and all members of the delegation participated in the signing ceremony. After the signing, Lin Haiyun, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade, hosted a banquet for all members of the delegation.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th—Vice Premier Bo Yibo received all members of the Hungarian government trade delegation led by Szalai Béla, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of Hungary, this morning and had a friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Lin Haiyun, Jiang Ming, Chen Boqing, and others. Hungarian Ambassador to China Halász József was also present.

### 中匈交换货物和付款协定签字  薄一波副总理接见匈政府贸易代表团

1966-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　中匈交换货物和付款协定签字
    薄一波副总理接见匈政府贸易代表团
    据新华社二十日讯　一九六六年中国和匈牙利交换货物和付款协定今天上午在北京签字。
    薄一波副总理参加了签字仪式。
    我国对外贸易部副部长江明和匈牙利政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长萨拉伊·贝洛分别代表双方在协定上签字。中国方面参加签字仪式的，还有林海云、陈伯清等。
    匈牙利方面参加签字仪式的，有匈牙利驻中国大使郝拉思·尤若夫、匈牙利政府贸易代表团副团长科德门和代表团全体成员。签字以后，对外贸易部代理部长林海云设宴招待代表团全体成员。
    新华社二十日讯　薄一波副总理今天上午接见了由匈牙利对外贸易部副部长萨拉伊·贝洛率领的匈牙利政府贸易代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有林海云、江明、陈伯清等。匈牙利驻中国大使郝拉思·尤若夫也在座。
