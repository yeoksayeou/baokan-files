Title: China and Guinea sign this year's trade protocol; Vice Premier Li Xiannian receives the Guinean Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-02-02
Edition: 5

    China and Guinea Sign This Year's Trade Protocol
    Vice Premier Li Xiannian Receives Guinean Economic Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, February 1st: The "Trade Protocol between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Guinea for 1966" was signed in Beijing today.
    Vice Premier Li Xiannian attended the signing ceremony.
    Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade Liu Xiwem and the head of the Guinean economic delegation, Baba Camara, Director of the Guinean Foreign Trade Bureau, signed the protocol on behalf of their respective governments.
    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Lin Haiyun, Hou Yefeng, Wang Mingjun, and other responsible individuals from relevant departments.
    On the Guinean side were all members of the Guinean economic delegation and the Guinean Ambassador to China, Camara Mamadi.
    After the signing, acting Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun held a banquet to entertain all members of the Guinean economic delegation.
    Xinhua News Agency, February 1st: Vice Premier Li Xiannian received the Guinean economic delegation led by Baba Camara this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Lin Haiyun, Liu Xiwem, Hou Yefeng, and other responsible individuals from relevant departments.
    Guinean Ambassador to China Camara Mamadi was also present.

### 中几两国政府签订本年度贸易议定书  李先念副总理接见几内亚经济代表团

1966-02-02
第5版()
专栏：

    中几两国政府签订本年度贸易议定书
    李先念副总理接见几内亚经济代表团
    新华社一日讯　“中华人民共和国政府和几内亚共和国政府一九六六年贸易议定书”今天在北京签字。
    李先念副总理出席了签字仪式。
    对外贸易部部长助理刘希文和几内亚经济代表团团长、几内亚对外贸易局局长巴巴·卡马拉，分别代表两国政府在议定书上签字。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有：林海云、侯野烽、王明俊等有关方面负责人。
    几内亚方面有几内亚经济代表团全体成员和几内亚驻中国大使卡马拉·马马迪。
    签字以后，对外贸易部代理部长林海云举行宴会，招待几内亚经济代表团全体成员。
    新华社一日讯　李先念副总理今天下午接见由巴巴·卡马拉率领的几内亚经济代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有林海云、刘希文、侯野烽等有关方面负责人。
    几内亚驻我国大使卡马拉·马马迪也在座。
