Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Sends Telegram Thanking Premier Zhou Enlai: Recently Signed Aid and Economic Agreements Between China and Vietnam Strengthen Vietnam's Power to Defeat American Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1966-02-12
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 11th: Premier Zhou Enlai received a telegram on the 9th from Premier Pham Van Dong of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The telegram expressed gratitude for China's support and assistance to the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national salvation. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:
    Recently, the negotiations between our government's economic delegation and the economic delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of China on aid and economic issues have been successfully completed. The various aid and economic agreements signed between our two countries this time are valuable assistance from China to us. They have strengthened our defense and economic capabilities, with the aim of defeating the American aggressors and making a positive contribution to the common revolutionary cause, consolidating and developing the militant unity of our two peoples.
    On behalf of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the Vietnamese people, I express my heartfelt gratitude to the Communist Party of China, the Government of the People's Republic of China, and the Chinese people for the support and assistance given to the just and certain-to-be-victorious struggle of the Vietnamese people against American aggression and for national salvation, in the spirit of proletarian internationalism. I also thank the Communist Party of China and the Government of the People's Republic of China for the meticulous and warm reception given to our government's economic delegation.
    With cordial greetings! I wish you good health!
        Pham Van Dong, Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, in Hanoi

### 范文同总理电谢周恩来总理  中越最近签订的援助和经济协定增强越南打败美国侵略者的力量

1966-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同总理电谢周恩来总理
    中越最近签订的援助和经济协定增强越南打败美国侵略者的力量
    新华社十一日讯　周恩来总理九日收到了越南民主共和国政府总理范文同的电报。电报对中国给予越南人民抗美救国斗争的支持和援助表示感谢。电报全文如下：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    最近，我国政府经济代表团同中华人民共和国政府经济代表团关于援助和经济问题的谈判已经圆满地完成。这次我们两国之间所签订的各项援助和经济协定，是中国对我们的宝贵援助，它加强了我们的国防和经济力量，目的在于打败美国侵略者，为共同的革命事业，巩固和发展我们两国人民的战斗团结作出了积极的贡献。
    我代表越南劳动党中央委员会、越南民主共和国政府和越南人民，对中国共产党、中华人民共和国政府和中国人民本着无产阶级国际主义的精神，所给予越南人民正义和必胜的抗美救国斗争的支持和援助表示衷心的感谢，并且感谢中国共产党和中华人民共和国政府对我国政府经济代表团给予了无微不至和热情的接待。
    致以亲切的敬礼！祝你身体健康！
        越南民主共和国政府总理范文同　于河内
