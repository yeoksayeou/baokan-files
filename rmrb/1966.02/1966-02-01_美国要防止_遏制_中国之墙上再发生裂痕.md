Title: The United States Wants to Prevent Further Cracks in the Wall of "Containment" Against China

Author:

Date: 1966-02-01
Edition: 4

    American Magazine *Fortune* published an article in its January 1966 issue entitled "The Strategy of the Pacific," which stated:
    For several months, the United States has been transferring the main force of its military forces from the Atlantic region to the Pacific on a scale that is becoming almost alarming. The U.S. military establishment is shifting from a strategy facing Russia and centered on NATO to a strategy facing China and centered on Hawaii.
    The troops urgently deployed to the Pacific since February are in most respects already stronger than the troops the United States has maintained in Europe for more than a decade to support NATO. The urgent reason for this rapid movement of American power is the need to save our long-term policy of containing communism from failing in Vietnam at the last minute. However, it must be combined with a new strategic system aimed at preserving any territory finally recovered in South Vietnam and preventing such shocking cracks from occurring again in the containment wall around China.
    If a true Pacific strategy is to be lasting, economical, and able to bring order to the chaotic situation, it must devise a way to make the talents of Britain, Taiwan, and Japan play a role.
    Ideally, within a few years, Japan, together with South Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, and the Commonwealth partners in the South China Sea, will form a major arc in the northeast, becoming a crescent-shaped containment circle around red China.

### 美国要防止“遏制”中国之墙上再发生裂痕

1966-02-01
第4版()
专栏：外论摘要

    美国要防止“遏制”中国之墙上再发生裂痕
    美国《幸福》杂志一九六六年一月号刊载一篇题为《太平洋的战略》的文章说：
    若干月来，美国以正在变得几乎惊人的规模把它的军事部队中的主要力量从大西洋地区转移到太平洋。美国的军事机构从一个面对俄国的、以北大西洋公约组织为中心的战略，正在转变为一个面对中国的、以夏威夷为中心的战略。
    从二月份紧急调往太平洋的部队，在大多数方面已经比十多年来美国为支持北大西洋公约组织而在欧洲保持的部队强大得多。美国力量的这种迅速调动的紧迫理由是，需要在最后关头挽救我们采取的遏制共产主义的长期政策不致在越南失败。但是，它必然同一个新的战略体系结合起来，这个新的战略体系的目的是保存在南越最后收复的任何地盘，并且防止在中国周围的遏制之墙上再发生这种令人震惊的裂痕。
    一项真正的太平洋战略如果要持久、经济、而且能够使紊乱局面变得井井有条，必须想出一个使英国、台湾和日本的才能发挥作用的办法。
    最理想的是，在若干年内，日本连同南朝鲜、台湾、泰国和南海的英联邦伙伴在东北部结成一个重要的弧形，成为在赤色中国周围的新月形遏制圈。
