Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Guinean Economic Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-03
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd: Premier Zhou Enlai received the entire Guinean economic delegation led by Baba Camara at noon today and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Lin Haiyun, Liu Xiwen, Hou Yefeng, and others. Guinean Ambassador to China Camara Mamadi was also present.

### 周总理接见几内亚经济代表团

1966-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见几内亚经济代表团
    新华社二日讯　周恩来总理今天中午接见了由巴巴·卡马拉率领的几内亚经济代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有林海云、刘希文、侯野烽等。几内亚驻我国大使卡马拉·马马迪也在座。
