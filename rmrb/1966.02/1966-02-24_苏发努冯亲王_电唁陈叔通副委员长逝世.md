Title: Prince Souphanouvong Sends Condolences on the Death of Vice-Chairman Chen Shutong
Author:
Date: 1966-02-24
Edition: 4

　　Souphanouvong Sends Condolences on the Death of Vice-Chairman Chen Shutong
    Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd. Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Premier of the State Council, received a telegram from Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front and Deputy Prime Minister of the Lao Tripartite National Union Government, expressing condolences on the death of Vice-Chairman Chen Shutong. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    I was deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Mr. Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China. On behalf of the Lao people, the Lao Patriotic Front, and in my own name, I extend my deepest condolences to Your Excellency, and through Your Excellency to the fraternal Chinese people, the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, the Government of the People's Republic of China, and the family of Mr. Chen Shutong.
      Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front
        Deputy Prime Minister of the Lao Tripartite National Union Government
                          Prince Souphanouvong
      February 19, 1966, in the Lao Liberated Area

### 苏发努冯亲王  电唁陈叔通副委员长逝世

1966-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏发努冯亲王
    电唁陈叔通副委员长逝世
    新华社二十三日讯　政协全国委员会主席、国务院总理周恩来，收到了老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席、老挝三方民族团结政府副首相苏发努冯亲王发来的吊唁陈叔通副委员长逝世的电报，电文如下：北京中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来阁下：
    我十分沉痛地获悉中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通先生逝世。我谨代表老挝人民、老挝爱国战线党，并以我个人的名义，向阁下，并通过阁下向兄弟的中国人民、中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会、中华人民共和国政府和陈叔通先生的家属表示沉痛的哀悼。
      老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席
        老挝三方民族团结政府副首相
                          苏发努冯亲王
      一九六六年二月十九日于老挝解放区
