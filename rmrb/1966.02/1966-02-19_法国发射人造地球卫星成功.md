Title: France Successfully Launches Artificial Earth Satellite
Author:
Date: 1966-02-19
Edition: 5

    French Successfully Launches Artificial Earth Satellite
    (Xinhua News Agency, Paris, February 17) France successfully launched the "Diapason" artificial Earth satellite using a "Diamant" three-stage rocket today at 8:33:39 AM Coordinated Universal Time. Thirteen minutes after the launch, the artificial satellite entered its orbit.
    This artificial satellite was launched from the French Hammaguir base in the Sahara. It weighs 19 kilograms and has a diameter of approximately 50 centimeters. The satellite is equipped with scientific instruments and will conduct measurements and observations of the Earth and its surroundings.

### 法国发射人造地球卫星成功

1966-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    法国发射人造地球卫星成功
    新华社巴黎十七日电　法国在今天国际标准时间八点三十三分三十九秒用“钻石”三级火箭发射了“调音义号”人造地球卫星。发射后十三分钟，人造卫星进入了轨道。
    这个人造卫星是在撒哈拉西部的法国哈马基尔基地发射的。它重十九公斤，直径约五十厘米。卫星上带有科学仪器，将对地球及其外围进行测量和考察。
