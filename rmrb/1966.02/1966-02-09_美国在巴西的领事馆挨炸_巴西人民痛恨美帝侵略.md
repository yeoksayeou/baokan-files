Title: US Consulate in Brazil Bombed: Brazilian People Hate US Imperialist Aggression
Author:
Date: 1966-02-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Rio de Janeiro, 6th - News: The US consulate in Alegre Port, Brazil was bombed again on the 5th.
    It is reported that this is the third time the US consulate has been bombed. Early in the morning on the 5th, a homemade bomb exploded at the consulate, damaging the building.
    The US Johnson administration launched a reactionary military coup in Brazil in April 1964 and established the terror rule of the Branco military dictatorship. This bombing is another expression of the Brazilian people's hatred of US imperialism.

### 美国在巴西的领事馆挨炸  巴西人民痛恨美帝侵略

1966-02-09
第5版()
专栏：

    美国在巴西的领事馆挨炸
    巴西人民痛恨美帝侵略
    新华社六日讯　里约热内卢消息：美国驻巴西阿雷格里港的领事馆五日再次挨炸。
    据报道，这是美国的这个领事馆第三次挨炸。五日清早，一颗土制炸弹在这个领事馆爆炸，炸坏了领事馆的房子。
    美国约翰逊政府一九六四年四月在巴西发动反动军事政变，建立了布朗库军事独裁的恐怖统治。这次爆炸事件，是巴西人民仇恨美帝国主义的又一个表现。
