Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai Send Congratulatory Telegrams on the National Day of Kuwait

Author:
Date: 1966-02-25
Edition: 1

　　Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai
    Send Congratulatory Telegrams on the National Day of Kuwait
    Xinhua News Agency, February 24th – Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on the 24th to Sabah al-Salim al-Sabah, Emir (Head of State) of the State of Kuwait, congratulating him on the National Day of Kuwait (February 25th). The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Highness Sabah al-Salim al-Sabah, Emir of the State of Kuwait, Kuwait:
    On the occasion of your country's National Day, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Highness and the people of your country. I wish your country prosperity and strength, and your people happiness.
            Liu Shaoqi
            Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                  February 24, 1966
    Xinhua News Agency, February 24th – Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on the 24th to Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah, Prime Minister of the Government of the State of Kuwait, congratulating him on the National Day of Kuwait (February 25th). The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah, Prime Minister of the Government of the State of Kuwait, Kuwait:
    On the occasion of your country's National Day, please accept my heartfelt congratulations and best wishes. I wish the friendly relations between China and Kuwait will develop day by day.
      Zhou Enlai
      Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                  February 24, 1966

### 刘少奇主席　周恩来总理  电贺科威特国国庆

1966-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘少奇主席　周恩来总理
    电贺科威特国国庆
    新华社二十四日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇二十四日打电报给科威特国埃米尔（国家元首）萨巴赫·萨利姆·萨巴赫，祝贺科威特国国庆（二月二十五日）。电文如下：科威特科威特国埃米尔萨巴赫·萨利姆·萨巴赫殿下：
    在贵国国庆的时候，我向殿下和贵国人民致以衷心的祝贺。祝贵国繁荣昌盛，人民幸福。
            中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                  一九六六年二月二十四日
    新华社二十四日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来二十四日打电报给科威特国政府首相贾比尔·艾哈迈德·萨巴赫，祝贺科威特国国庆（二月二十五日）。电文如下：科威特科威特国政府首相贾比尔·艾哈迈德·萨巴赫
    阁下：
    在贵国国庆的时候，请接受我衷心的祝贺和良好的愿望。祝中科两国的友好关系日益发展。
      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                  一九六六年二月二十四日
