Title: President Nkrumah Arrives in Our Country Today
Author:
Date: 1966-02-24
Edition: 1

　　President Nkrumah Arrives in Our Country Today
    Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd -- Dr. Kwame Nkrumah, President of the Republic of Ghana, has postponed his arrival in our country to February 24th due to unforeseen circumstances.


**Named Entities**

加纳共和国    Ghana Gongheguo
克瓦米·恩克鲁玛  Kwame Nkrumah
新华社    Xinhua News Agency
