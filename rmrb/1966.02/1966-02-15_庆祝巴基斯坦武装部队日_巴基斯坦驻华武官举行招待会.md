Title: Celebrating Pakistan Armed Forces Day: Pakistani Military Attaché in China Holds Reception
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-15
Edition: 4

    Celebrating Pakistan Armed Forces Day
    Pakistani Military Attaché in China Holds Reception

    Xinhua News Agency, February 14th – Colonel Zahid, Military Attaché of the Pakistani Embassy in China, and Air Force Colonel Khan, Air Force Attaché, held a reception tonight to celebrate Pakistan Armed Forces Day.

    Peng Shaohui, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Wang Xinting; Liang Biye, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Li Juikui, Political Commissar of the General Logistics Department; Han Nianlong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Wu Faxian, Du Yide, Liu Jinxuan, Kong Congzhou, and other responsible persons from various services and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army were invited to attend the reception.

    Colonel Zahid and Peng Shaohui proposed toasts at the reception, wishing for the friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Pakistan.

    Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 庆祝巴基斯坦武装部队日  巴基斯坦驻华武官举行招待会

1966-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝巴基斯坦武装部队日
    巴基斯坦驻华武官举行招待会
    新华社十四日讯　巴基斯坦驻华大使馆武官查希德上校和空军武官汗空军上校今晚举行招待会，庆祝巴基斯坦武装部队日。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉、王新亭，总政治部副主任梁必业，总后勤部政治委员李聚奎，外交部副部长韩念龙，以及中国人民解放军各军种、兵种负责人吴法宪、杜义德、刘金轩、孔从周等，应邀出席了招待会。
    查希德上校和彭绍辉在会上先后祝酒，他们提议，为中巴两国人民和军队之间的友谊干杯。
    各国驻华使节和武官也出席了招待会。
