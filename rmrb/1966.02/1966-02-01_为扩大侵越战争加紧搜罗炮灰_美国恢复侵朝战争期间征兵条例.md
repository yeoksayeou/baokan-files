Title: To Intensify the Search for Cannon Fodder for Expanding the War of Aggression Against Vietnam, the United States Restores Conscription Regulations from the Korean War Period
Author:
Date: 1966-02-01
Edition: 3

    To Intensify the Search for Cannon Fodder for Expanding the War of Aggression Against Vietnam
    The United States Restores Conscription Regulations from the Korean War Period
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 29th: The U.S. Selective Service System officially announced on the 28th the restoration of a conscription regulation from the Korean War period to further expand the scope of conscription and search for cannon fodder for expanding the war of aggression against Vietnam.
    The Johnson administration recently announced that it would expand the army by more than 110,000 men, bringing the total U.S. military force to approximately 3.1 million. To complete this expansion plan, U.S. conscription authorities recently held a series of meetings to study the issue of expanding the scope of conscription.

### 为扩大侵越战争加紧搜罗炮灰  美国恢复侵朝战争期间征兵条例

1966-02-01
第3版()
专栏：

    为扩大侵越战争加紧搜罗炮灰
    美国恢复侵朝战争期间征兵条例
    据新华社二十九日讯　华盛顿消息：美国征兵局二十八日正式宣布恢复一项朝鲜战争期间的征兵条例，以进一步扩大征兵范围，为扩大侵越战争搜罗炮灰。
    约翰逊政府最近宣布，要再扩军十一万多人，使美国总兵力达到约三百一十万人；为了完成这一扩军计划，美国征兵当局不久前曾召开了一系列会议，研究扩大征兵范围的问题。
