Title: Australian Prime Minister Willing to Act as an Accomplice in US Aggression Against Vietnam, Openly Clamoring for Support of US Resumption of Bombing of North Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-02-04
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, February 2nd. Australian Prime Minister Holt openly stated on the 1st that Australia supports the US decision to resume bombing the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    The Melbourne-published *Herald* published an editorial on the 2nd pointing out that Holt's statement demonstrates that Australia has not changed its Vietnam policy due to the change of prime minister (referring to the recent resignation of former Australian Prime Minister Menzies).

    The Australian Liberal Party government is one of the very few that provides cannon fodder for the US war of aggression against Vietnam. It has already dispatched 1,500 troops to South Vietnam, constantly suffering blows from the armed forces of the South Vietnamese people.

### 澳总理甘心充当美国侵越帮凶  公然叫嚷支持美国恢复轰炸越南北方

1966-02-04
第3版()
专栏：

    澳总理甘心充当美国侵越帮凶
    公然叫嚷支持美国恢复轰炸越南北方
    新华社墨尔本二日电　澳大利亚总理霍尔特一日公然说，澳大利亚支持美国恢复轰炸越南民主共和国的决定。
    墨尔本出版的《先驱报》二日发表社论指出，霍尔特的声明表明，澳大利亚并没有由于改换总理（按澳大利亚前总理孟席斯最近辞职）而改变它的越南政策。
    澳大利亚自由党政府是极少数为美国侵越战争提供炮灰者之一，它已派遣一千五百名军队到南越，不断受到越南南方人民武装的打击。
