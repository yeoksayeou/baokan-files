Title: Lies in "Digging Up Roots"
Author: Yang Jianzhai, Zhang Hongxun
Date: 1966-02-13
Edition: 2

	Lies in “Digging Up Roots”
    As an important issue in the revolutionization of institutions, the problem of excessive and cumbersome statistical reports needs to be thoroughly resolved. However, how easy is it to talk about "thoroughly"? Someone vividly said: This is like "wildfires that never die, revived by the spring breeze."
    What exactly is the problem?
              Shandong Tai'an　Yang Jianzhai　Zhang Hongxun
    Sending out so many forms and survey outlines requires "digging up roots" from top to bottom, focusing on "digging up" the "roots" in thinking, and seriously assessing the guiding role of these forms for practical work. Then, no matter how "strongly the spring breeze blows," they will no longer be "revived."　Editor

### 在于“挖根”
杨建斋　张洪训
1966-02-13
第2版()
专栏：互相启发

    在于“挖根”
    作为机关革命化中的一个重要问题，统计表报过多过繁的问题，需要得到彻底解决。但是，“彻底”两字，谈何容易？有人形象地说：这是“野火烧不尽，春风吹又生。”
    这究竟是什么问题呢？
              山东泰安　杨建斋　张洪训
    发那么多的表报和调查提纲，要从上到下地“挖根”，着重“挖”思想上的“根”，认真估量这些表报对实际工作的指导作用，那么任凭“春风劲吹”，就可以不再“生”。　编者
