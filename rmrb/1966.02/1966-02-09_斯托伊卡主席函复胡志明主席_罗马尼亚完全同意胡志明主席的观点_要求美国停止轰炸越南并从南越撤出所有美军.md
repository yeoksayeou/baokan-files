Title: Chairman Stoica Replies to President Ho Chi Minh: Romania Fully Agrees with President Ho Chi Minh's Views; Demands that the United States Stop Bombing Vietnam and Withdraw All U.S. Troops from South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-02-09
Edition: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, February 8th. Romanian State Council Chairman Chivu Stoica replied to a letter from President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, stating that Romania fully agrees with the views expressed in President Ho Chi Minh's letter regarding the path to resolving the Vietnam issue. Romania fully supports the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's four-point stance and the program of the National Liberation Front, the sole legitimate representative of the South Vietnamese people.
　　This reply was published in Romanian newspapers on the 8th.
　　The reply condemns the United States for resuming the bombing of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The letter states that the resumption of these aerial attacks once again confirms that the loudly proclaimed "search for peace" campaign by the U.S. government is merely intended to cover up its plans to intensify the war of aggression against Vietnam, attempting to impose the U.S.'s so-called "unconditional negotiation" terms on the Vietnamese people. This new act of aggression once again proves that the United States persists in blatantly trampling upon the basic norms of international law, disregarding the sacred right of the Vietnamese people to be the sole masters of their own country, exacerbating the already tense situation in Southeast Asia, thereby worsening the international atmosphere and threatening world peace.
　　The reply states that Romania demands: the cessation of aerial bombardment and any actions that undermine the sovereignty and security of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the withdrawal of all U.S. troops and other interventionist forces from South Vietnam, the implementation of the 1954 Geneva Accords on Vietnam, and respect for the legitimate right of the Vietnamese people to decide their own destiny according to their own will and interests.
　　Stoica assured in his letter that the Romanian people and government will continue to provide internationalist support and assistance to the fraternal Vietnamese people who are engaged in a selfless struggle against imperialist aggression and for the independence, sovereignty, integrity, and unity of their homeland.
　　The letter states that the cause of the Vietnamese people fighting for freedom and independence is just, and it is respected by the Romanian people and all progressive mankind. We firmly believe that victory will surely belong to the Vietnamese people, and the Vietnamese people will surely realize their legitimate aspirations.

### 斯托伊卡主席函复胡志明主席  罗马尼亚完全同意胡志明主席的观点  要求美国停止轰炸越南并从南越撤出所有美军

1966-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    斯托伊卡主席函复胡志明主席
    罗马尼亚完全同意胡志明主席的观点
    要求美国停止轰炸越南并从南越撤出所有美军
    新华社布加勒斯特八日电　罗马尼亚国务委员会主席基伏·斯托伊卡复信给越南民主共和国主席胡志明说，罗马尼亚完全同意胡志明主席信中所表示的关于解决越南问题的途径的观点。罗马尼亚完全支持越南民主共和国的四项主张的立场和越南南方人民唯一合法的代表——民族解放阵线的纲领。
    这封复信八日已经在罗马尼亚报纸上发表。
    复信谴责美国恢复对越南民主共和国的轰炸。信中说，恢复这种空中进攻再次证实，喧嚣一时的美国政府“寻求和平”的运动的目的，只不过是掩盖加紧侵越战争的计划，企图把美国的所谓“无条件谈判”的条件强加给越南人民。这一新的侵略行动再次证明美国坚持明目张胆地践踏国际权利的起码准则，无视越南人民成为自己祖国唯一主人的神圣权利，加剧已经如此紧张的东南亚局势，从而恶化了国际气氛并威胁了世界和平。
    复信说，罗马尼亚要求：停止空中轰炸以及任何破坏越南民主共和国主权和安全的行动，所有的美国军队和其他干涉军撤出越南南方，实施一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议，尊重越南人民根据自己的意志和利益自己决定自己命运的合法权利。
    斯托伊卡在信中保证，罗马尼亚人民和政府今后仍将向反对帝国主义侵略、为自己祖国独立、主权完整和统一而进行忘我斗争的兄弟的越南人民给予国际主义的支持和援助。
    信中说，越南人民为自由与独立而战的事业是正义的，它受到罗马尼亚人民和全体进步人类的敬重。我们坚信胜利必将属于越南人民，越南人民必将实现他们的正当愿望。
