Title: State Council Appointment and Removal List
Author:
Date: 1966-02-09
Edition: 3

    State Council Appointment and Removal List
    Xinhua News Agency, February 8th: The 160th plenary meeting of the State Council held on January 18th approved the appointment and removal of a number of personnel from state organs, embassies abroad, and universities. The list is as follows:
    Appointment:
    徐以新  Xu Yixin, 陈家康 Chen Jiakang as Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs;
    赵继昌  Zhao Jichang as Deputy Director of the Science and Technology Commission;
    刘旭 Liu Xu, 顾明 Gu Ming as Deputy Directors of the State Capital Construction Commission;
    杨叶澎  Yang Yepeng as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Chemical Industry;
    王立 Wang Li, 李玉堂 Li Yutang as Vice Ministers of the Fifth Ministry of Machine Building;
    王雨洛  Wang Yulu as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Textile Industry;
    田耕 Tian Geng as Deputy Director of the General Office of the State Council;
    王锦川  Wang Jinchuan as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the People's Republic of Albania;
    金畅如  Jin Changru as Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Romanian People's Republic;
    于梦欣  Yu Mengxin as Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic;
    杨惠生  Yang Huisheng as Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Kingdom of Cambodia;
    唐范宇  Tang Fanyu as Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of India;
    杨克毅  Yang Keyi as Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Syrian Arab Republic;
    张云 Zhang Yun as Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Swiss Confederation;
    李兴 Li Xing as Vice President of Jilin University of Technology;
    刘光汉  Liu Guanghan as Vice President of Beijing Foreign Trade Institute;
    纪增爵  Ji Zengjue as Vice President of Wuhan Technical College of Surveying and Mapping.
    Removal:
    曾涌泉  Zeng Yongquan from the post of Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; 宋养初 Song Yangchu from the post of Deputy Director of the State Economic Commission; 汪滔 Wang Tao from the post of Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; 王一木 Wang Yimu from the post of Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Ghana; 周震中 Zhou Zhenzhong from the post of Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Swiss Confederation.

### 国务院任免名单

1966-02-09
第3版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社八日讯　一月十八日举行的国务院全体会议第一百六十次会议，通过任免一批国家机关、驻外使馆和高等学校工作人员，名单如下：
    任命：
    徐以新、陈家康为外交部副部长；
    赵继昌为科学技术委员会副主任；
    刘旭、顾明为国家基本建设委员会副主任；
    杨叶澎为化学工业部副部长；
    王立、李玉堂为第五机械工业部副部长；
    王雨洛为纺织工业部副部长；
    田耕为国务院秘书厅副主任；
    王锦川为中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国大使馆参赞；
    金畅如为中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使馆文化参赞；
      于梦欣为中华人民共和国驻捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国大使馆文化参赞；
    杨惠生为中华人民共和国驻柬埔寨王国大使馆文化参赞；
    唐范宇为中华人民共和国驻印度共和国大使馆文化参赞；
    杨克毅为中华人民共和国驻阿拉伯叙利亚共和国大使馆文化参赞；
    张云为中华人民共和国驻瑞士联邦大使馆文化参赞；
    李兴为吉林工业大学副校长；
    刘光汉为北京对外贸易学院副院长；
    纪增爵为武汉测绘学院副院长。
    免去：
    曾涌泉的外交部副部长职务；宋养初的国家经济委员会副主任职务；汪滔的中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使馆参赞职务；王一木的中华人民共和国驻加纳共和国大使馆参赞职务；周震中的中华人民共和国驻瑞士联邦大使馆文化参赞职务。
