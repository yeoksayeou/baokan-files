Title: Ambassador Juma Holds Reception to Celebrate the First Anniversary of the Signing of the Tanzania-China Friendship Treaty
Author:
Date: 1966-02-21
Edition: 4

　　Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Signing of the Tanzania-China Friendship Treaty
　　Ambassador Juma Holds Reception
    Xinhua News Agency, February 20th – Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Waziri Juma, held a reception this evening to celebrate the first anniversary of the signing of the Tanzania-China Friendship Treaty.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Xie Fuzhi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and leading members of relevant departments Chu Tunan, Ding Xilin, Wang Daohan, Zhou Huamin, Yan Jinsheng, Cui Yueli, Zhang Tiesheng, and others attended the reception upon invitation.
    Ambassador Juma and Ji Pengfei spoke at the reception. They both stated that in the cause of opposing imperialism and new and old colonialism, and safeguarding Afro-Asian solidarity, the peoples of China and Tanzania will always stand together and advance together.
    Ambassador Juma said that the signing of the Tanzania-China Friendship Treaty strengthened the good relations between the two peoples. This friendly relationship is a strong bulwark against the war of imperialism and new and old colonialism. This friendly relationship helps to enhance the unity of the peoples of Tanzania and China and the unity between Afro-Asian countries.
    The Ambassador said that Tanzania has made great achievements in national construction, and in this regard, the people of the People's Republic of China have given us, and are continuing to give us, great help.
    The Ambassador concluded by saying that in the future, in the cause of opposing imperialism and new and old colonialism, in creating a better life for our people, consolidating the unity of the African and Asian continents, and striving for world peace, Tanzania and China will always stand shoulder to shoulder with other peace-loving countries around the world and advance together. Ji Pengfei said in his speech that the signing of the China-Tanzania Friendship Treaty further consolidated the profound friendship between the two peoples and brought the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries into a new stage.
    Ji Pengfei said that China and Tanzania have carried out fruitful cooperation in the cause of opposing imperialism and new and old colonialism, safeguarding Afro-Asian solidarity, and defending world peace. The comprehensive development of the friendly relations between China and Tanzania has set a good example for harmonious coexistence and friendly cooperation between Asian and African countries.
    Ji Pengfei said that at present, the peoples of our two countries are faced with the common tasks of opposing imperialism, consolidating national independence, and building our countries. Our two countries are faithful to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and have truly achieved mutual respect without imposing on others, treating each other equally without considering ourselves superior, and mutual benefit and mutual assistance without harming others to benefit ourselves. We believe that with the joint efforts of the two countries, the China-Tanzania Friendship Treaty will surely shine even more brilliantly.
    Diplomatic envoys of various countries to China attended the reception upon invitation.

### 庆祝坦桑尼亚中国友好条约签订一周年  朱马大使举行招待会

1966-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝坦桑尼亚中国友好条约签订一周年
　　朱马大使举行招待会
    新华社二十日讯　坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马今晚举行招待会，庆祝坦桑尼亚中国友好条约签订一周年。
    国务院副总理谢富治，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，以及楚图南、丁西林、汪道涵、周化民、颜金生、崔月犁、张铁生等有关方面负责人，应邀出席了招待会。
    朱马大使和姬鹏飞在招待会上讲了话。他们都表示，在反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义，维护亚非团结的事业中，中国坦桑尼亚两国人民将永远在一起共同前进。
    朱马大使说，坦桑尼亚中国友好条约的签订，加强了两国人民的良好关系。这种友好关系，正是反对帝国主义、新老殖民主义战争的坚强堡垒。这种友好关系有助于增强坦中两国人民的团结和亚非国家之间的团结。
    大使说，坦桑尼亚在国家建设方面取得了巨大成就，在这方面，中华人民共和国人民已经给了我们，并正在继续给予我们很大帮助。
    大使最后说，今后在反对帝国主义、新老殖民主义，在创造我们人民的美好生活，巩固亚非大陆的团结，以及争取世界和平的事业中，坦桑尼亚和中国将永远和全世界其他爱好和平的国家一起肩并肩地共同前进。姬鹏飞在讲话中说，中国坦桑尼亚友好条约的签订，进一步巩固了两国人民之间的深厚友谊，使两国的友好合作关系进入了一个新的阶段。
    姬鹏飞说，中国和坦桑尼亚两国在反对帝国主义和新、老殖民主义，维护亚非团结和保卫世界和平的事业中，进行了卓有成效的合作。中国和坦桑尼亚两国友好关系的全面发展，为亚非国家之间的和睦相处和友好合作树立了一个良好的范例。
    姬鹏飞说，目前，我们两国人民面临着反对帝国主义、巩固民族独立、建设国家的共同任务。我们两国都忠实于和平共处五项原则，真正作到了相互尊重而不强加于人，平等相待而不自视高人一等，互利互助而不损人利己。我们相信，在两国的共同努力下，中国坦桑尼亚友好条约必将放出更加灿烂的光辉。
    各国驻中国的使节应邀出席了招待会。
