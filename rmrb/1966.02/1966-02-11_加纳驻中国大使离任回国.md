Title: Ghanaian Ambassador to China Leaves Post for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 10th: Joe-Fio N. Meyer, Ambassador of the Republic of Ghana to China, has been recalled and left Beijing by plane this morning.
