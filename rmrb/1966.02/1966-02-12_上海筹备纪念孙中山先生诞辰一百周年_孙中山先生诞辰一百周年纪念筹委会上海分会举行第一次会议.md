Title: Shanghai Prepares to Commemorate the Centenary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth; Shanghai Branch of the Preparatory Committee for the Commemoration of the Centenary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth Holds First Meeting
Author:
Date: 1966-02-12
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 11th. The Shanghai Branch of the Preparatory Committee for the Commemoration of the Centenary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth held its first meeting on the morning of the 10th.

    Chen Pixian, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Director of the Shanghai Branch of the Preparatory Committee for the Commemoration of the Centenary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth, presided over the meeting and delivered a speech.

    Zhang Chunqiao, Liang Guobin, Yang Xiguang, Shi Ying, Song Jiwen, Li Gancheng, Zhang Chengzong, Zhao Zukang, Hu Juewen, Chen Wangdao, Chen Tongsheng, Shen Tilan, Liu Jingji, Li Zhaohuan, Meng Xiancheng, Zhou Gucheng, Wang Zhizhong, Wu Ruo'an, Lu Yudao, Zhang Qi, Zhang Haobo, Ba Jin, Wu Yaozong, Sha Yankai, Yang Junsheng, Xue Dubi, and other Deputy Directors and members of the Shanghai Branch of the Preparatory Committee attended the meeting.

    The meeting decided that Shanghai would hold the following activities to commemorate the centenary of Sun Yat-sen's birth: 1. Hold a commemoration meeting for the centenary of Sun Yat-sen's birth in November 1966; 2. Hold a ceremony to visit Sun Yat-sen's former residence on the day of the centenary of Sun Yat-sen's birth (November 12th); 3. Repair Sun Yat-sen's former residence; 4. Collect Sun Yat-sen's relics and materials, solicit commemorative articles, compile a special collection of historical and literary materials, and produce a documentary film of the commemorative activities; 5. Hold various symposiums and conduct publicity work on Sun Yat-sen's life and deeds.

    The meeting also approved the list of responsible persons for the office, secretarial, propaganda, data, and general affairs groups of the Shanghai Branch of the Preparatory Committee for the Commemoration of the Centenary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth.

    The Shanghai Branch of the Preparatory Committee for the Commemoration of the Centenary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth was established at the fifth meeting of the Standing Committee of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held on November 27, 1965.

### 上海筹备纪念孙中山先生诞辰一百周年  孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹委会上海分会举行第一次会议

1966-02-12
第2版()
专栏：

    上海筹备纪念孙中山先生诞辰一百周年
    孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹委会上海分会举行第一次会议
    新华社上海十一日电　孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹备委员会上海市分会，十日上午举行第一次会议。
    中共上海市委第一书记、孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹备委员会上海分会主任陈丕显主持了会议，并在会上讲了话。
    筹委会上海分会副主任张春桥、梁国斌、杨西光、石英、宋季文、李干成、张承宗、赵祖康、胡厥文、陈望道、陈同生、沈体兰、刘靖基、黎照寰、孟宪承、周谷城、王致中、吴若安、卢于道、张祺、张浩波、巴金、吴耀宗、沙彦楷、杨俊生、薛笃弼等和筹委会上海市分会委员出席了会议。
    会议决定上海市举行下列活动纪念孙中山先生诞辰一百周年：一、一九六六年十一月举行孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念会；二、在孙中山先生诞辰一百周年当天（十一月十二日）举行参谒中山故居的仪式；三、修缮中山故居；四、搜集孙中山先生遗物和资料，征集纪念文章，汇编文史资料纪念专辑，摄制纪念活动的实况纪录片；五、举行各种座谈会，进行有关孙中山先生生平事迹的宣传工作。
    会议还通过了孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹备委员会上海分会办公室和秘书、宣传、资料、总务等组的负责人名单。
    孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹委会上海市分会，是政协上海市委员会在一九六五年十一月二十七日举行的常务委员会第五次会议上成立的。
