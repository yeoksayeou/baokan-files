Title: First Vice President Karume Receives Chinese Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-02-13
Edition: 4

    First Vice President Karume Receives Chinese Government Delegation
    (Xinhua News Agency, Zanzibar, February 11) Karume, First Vice President of the United Republic of Tanzania, received the Chinese government delegation headed by Xu Jie, Vice Minister of Geology, at the State House here on the morning of February 11.
    The Chinese government delegation is here to attend the celebrations of the second anniversary of the Zanzibar revolution victory. Karume held a cordial and friendly conversation with the Chinese guests.

### 卡鲁姆第一副总统接见我政府代表团

1966-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    卡鲁姆第一副总统接见我政府代表团
    据新华社桑给巴尔十一日电　坦桑尼亚联合共和国第一副总统卡鲁姆十一日上午在这里的国家大厦接见了以地质部副部长许杰为首的中国政府代表团。
    中国政府代表团是前来参加桑给巴尔革命胜利两周年庆祝活动的。卡鲁姆同中国客人进行了亲切友好的谈话。
