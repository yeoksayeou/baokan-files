Title: Nasution Writes to Malinovsky Expressing Desire for Close Cooperation with Soviet Leaders, Malinovsky Hopes Everything in Indonesia Goes Well
Author:
Date: 1966-02-21
Edition: 4

　　Nasution Writes to Malinovsky
　　Expressing Desire for Close Cooperation with Soviet Leaders
　　Malinovsky Hopes Everything in Indonesia Goes Well
    Xinhua News Agency, 20th dispatch: Jakarta News: According to Antara News Agency, Indonesian Coordinating Minister of Defense and Security Nasution recently wrote a letter to Soviet Defense Minister Malinovsky, "expressing the hope for close cooperation between the two countries, especially between the armed forces of the two countries."
    Reportedly, the letter was delivered by Indonesia's new Deputy Air Force Commander Nuryadin on the 11th when he visited Soviet Defense Minister Malinovsky in Moscow.
    Antara News Agency reported: "The meeting took place in a cordial atmosphere, and Marshal Malinovsky expressed the hope that everything in Indonesia would soon proceed smoothly."

### 纳苏蒂安写信给马利诺夫斯基  表示要同苏联领导人密切合作  马利诺夫斯基希望印度尼西亚一切顺利进行

1966-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　纳苏蒂安写信给马利诺夫斯基
　　表示要同苏联领导人密切合作
　　马利诺夫斯基希望印度尼西亚一切顺利进行
    新华社二十日讯　雅加达消息：据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚国防和安全部组统筹部长纳苏蒂安最近写信给苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基，“表示希望两国进行密切合作，特别是两国武装部队进行密切合作。”
    据报道，这封信是印度尼西亚新任空军副司令努尔亚丁十一日在莫斯科拜访苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基时递交的。
    安塔拉通讯社的报道说：“会晤是在融洽的气氛中进行的，马利诺夫斯基元帅表示希望印度尼西亚的一切事情不久会顺利进行。”
