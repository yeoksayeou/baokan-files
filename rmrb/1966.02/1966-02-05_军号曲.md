Title: Bugle Call
Author: Xu Zifang
Date: 1966-02-05
Edition: 6

    Bugle Call
    Soldier Xu Zifang
  Red-tasseled bugle sounds,
  Summoning an old white-haired mama: "I've waited for you twenty years,
  Tonight, stay at mama's home!"
  Calling Little Lan to boil water for tea,
  And telling Big Hu to make up the bed,
  Mama's nimble fingers fly with the needle,
  Busily sewing socks and clothes for the soldiers.
  The flames of the Anti-Japanese War spread everywhere,
  In those days, cavalry galloped across Jiaodong,
  Snowy nights patching Liberation shoes,
  Dawn brings sewing old cloth socks.
  The world's events gather at the needlepoint,
  The night wind stirs white hair,
  Why does Mama look out the window?
  What she thinks of is the whole world.
  Good Mama, don't worry,
  We are all Eighth Route Army children,
  Straw sandals and cloth socks steady our steps,
  Holding high the Long March torch!

### 军号曲
徐子芳
1966-02-05
第6版()
专栏：战士诗选

    军号曲
    战士　徐子芳
  红缨军号嘀哒哒，
  催出白发老妈妈： “盼望你们二十年，
  今晚住在大妈家！”
  唤进小兰去烧茶，
  又叫大虎把铺搭，
  大妈指头银针跳，
  忙给战士缝衣袜。
  抗日烽火遍地燃，
  当年胶东走战马，
  风雪夜补解放鞋，
  天明又缝老布袜。
  五洲风云针尖聚，
  夜风吹动白头发，
  大妈为何望窗外？
  心里想的是天下。
  好大妈呵莫牵挂，
  我们都是八路娃，
  草鞋布袜步步稳，
  高擎长征大火把！
