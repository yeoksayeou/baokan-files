Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Intrude into Territorial Waters and Airspace of Fujian, Guangdong, etc. I Issue the 398th Serious Warning

Author:
Date: 1966-02-17
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th -- At 13:27 to 17:25 on February 16th, one U.S. warship intruded into the territorial waters east of Dongshan Island in Fujian Province. On the same day, from 7:31 to 10:54, one U.S. warship intruded into the territorial waters of Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 11:56 to 12:12 and from 17:05 to 17:13, three U.S. military aircraft successively intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 398th serious warning.



**Named Entities**

### 美舰美机侵入我福建广东等地领海领空  我提出第三百九十八次严重警告

1966-02-17
第2版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我福建广东等地领海领空
    我提出第三百九十八次严重警告
    新华社十六日讯　二月十六日十三时二十七分至十七时二十五分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省东山岛以东地区领海。同日七时三十一分至十时五十四分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领海。同日十一时五十六分至十二时十二分和十七时零五分至十七时十三分，美国军用飞机三架，先后侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛地区领空。对美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第三百九十八次严重警告。
