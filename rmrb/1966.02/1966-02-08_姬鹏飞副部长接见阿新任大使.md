Title: Vice Minister Ji Pengfei Receives New Algerian Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1966-02-08
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, February 7th -- Mohamed Cherif Sahli, the newly appointed Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Algeria to China, paid a courtesy call to Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei this afternoon to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials.
