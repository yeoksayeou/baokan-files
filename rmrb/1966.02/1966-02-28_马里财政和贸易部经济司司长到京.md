Title: Director of the Economic Department of the Ministry of Finance and Trade of Mali Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-28
Edition: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 26th—Oumar Coulibaly, Director of the Economic Department of the Ministry of Finance and Trade of Mali, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to our country at our invitation.
    Wang Mingjun, Deputy Director of the Fifth Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and others were at the airport to greet him.
    Birama Traoré, the Malian Ambassador to China, was also at the airport to greet him.

### 马里财政和贸易部经济司司长到京

1966-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　马里财政和贸易部经济司司长到京
　　新华社二十六日讯　应邀来我国访问的马里财政和贸易部经济司司长乌玛尔·库利巴利，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场迎接的有，对外贸易部第五局副局长王明俊等。
    马里驻中国大使比拉马·特拉奥雷也到机场迎接。
