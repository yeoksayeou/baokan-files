Title: Use the Jiao Yulu Spirit to Transform Hanyuan
Author: Yan Guifang, Su Wenzhong
Date: 1966-02-15
Edition: 2

    Comrade Jiao Yulu, holding high the red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, led the masses of Lankao in eliminating the "three evils," providing us with a revolutionary path to change the backward face of Hanyuan County with its poor mountains and bad waters. We must, like Comrade Jiao Yulu, personally organize and lead investigation teams, traverse the green mountains, conduct in-depth investigations, grasp first-hand information, clarify the patterns of water hazards of the Dadu River and the Chensha River, adapt to local conditions, investigate and plan one commune at a time, and lead the masses of the entire county to work hard hoe by hoe, so that the "spirit of Hancun, the determination of Qinzhai, the drive of Zhaoduolou, and the path of Shuangyangshu" that inspire the people of Lankao County can take root and blossom in the struggle to transform the poor mountains and bad waters of Hanyuan County.
              Secretary of the Hanyuan County Party Committee, Sichuan　Yan Guifang
                        Deputy Secretary　Su Wenzhong

### 用焦裕禄精神改造汉源
阎桂芳  苏文忠
1966-02-15
第2版()
专栏：

    用焦裕禄精神改造汉源
    焦裕禄同志高举毛泽东思想红旗，领导兰考群众除“三害”的光辉业绩，为我们改变汉源县穷山恶水落后面貌指出了一条革命的道路。我们一定要象焦裕禄同志那样，亲自组织和带领调查队，踏遍青山，深入调查，掌握第一手资料，把大渡河和沉沙河的水害规律弄清，因地制宜，一个公社一个公社地调查规划，带领全县群众一锄一锄地苦干，使鼓舞着兰考县人民的“韩村的精神，秦寨的决心，赵垛楼的干劲，双杨树的道路”，在汉源县改造穷山恶水的斗争中生根开花。
              四川汉源县委书记　阎桂芳
                        副书记　苏文忠
