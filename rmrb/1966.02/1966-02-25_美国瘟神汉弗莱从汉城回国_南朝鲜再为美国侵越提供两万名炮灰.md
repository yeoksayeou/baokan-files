Title: American Plague God Humphrey Returns Home from Seoul; South Korea to Provide Additional 20,000 Cannon Fodder for American Aggression Against Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-25
Edition: 3

　　American Plague God Humphrey Returns Home from Seoul
　　South Korea to Provide Additional 20,000 Cannon Fodder for American Aggression Against Vietnam
    (Our Dispatch) After reaching an agreement with the South Korean Park Chung-hee puppet clique to send an additional 20,000 South Korean puppet troops to South Vietnam as cannon fodder, U.S. Vice President Humphrey returned to Washington from Seoul on the 23rd.
    The agreement stipulates that the Park Chung-hee clique will send an additional division and a regiment to South Vietnam as cannon fodder. The increased number will be 20,000 people. A regiment will be sent around April 20, and a division will be sent in July.
    At a banquet in Seoul, Humphrey clamored that the United States "will not retreat an inch" in South Vietnam.
    According to an Associated Press report, Humphrey immediately held secret talks with Johnson after returning to Washington.

### 美国瘟神汉弗莱从汉城回国  南朝鲜再为美国侵越提供两万名炮灰

1966-02-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　美国瘟神汉弗莱从汉城回国
　　南朝鲜再为美国侵越提供两万名炮灰
    本报讯　美国副总统汉弗莱在同南朝鲜朴正熙傀儡集团就增派两万名南朝鲜伪军去南越充当炮灰一事达成协议后，已于二十三日从汉城回到华盛顿。
    这个协议规定朴正熙集团增派一个师和一个团去南越充当炮灰，增派的人数将为两万人，在四月二十日左右派出一个团，在七月间派出一个师。
    汉弗莱在汉城宴会上叫嚷美国在南越“不会退让一英寸”。
    据美联社消息说，汉弗莱回到华盛顿后，立即同约翰逊举行了秘密会谈。
