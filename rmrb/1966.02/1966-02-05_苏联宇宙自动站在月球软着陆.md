Title: Soviet Unmanned Spacecraft Achieves Soft Landing on the Moon
Author:
Date: 1966-02-05
Edition: 4

    Soviet Unmanned Spacecraft Achieves Soft Landing on the Moon
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 3rd (Telegraph) – According to TASS reports: The Soviet “Luna 9” unmanned spacecraft successfully made a soft landing (meaning a smooth landing rather than crashing onto the lunar surface) on the surface of the moon on the 3rd.
    According to reports, the Soviet “Luna 9” unmanned spacecraft, launched on January 31st, made a soft landing on the surface of the moon on February 3rd, landing in the area west of the Reiner and Marius craters in Oceanus Procellarum.
    The report stated that the unmanned spacecraft had established radio contact with Earth and that the instruments on board were functioning normally.

### 苏联宇宙自动站在月球软着陆

1966-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联宇宙自动站在月球软着陆
    新华社莫斯科三日电　据塔斯社报道：苏联“月球九号”自动站三日在月球表面软式着陆（即平稳着陆而不是撞在月面上）成功。
    据报道，一月三十一日发射的苏联“月球九号”自动站在二月三日在月球表面软着陆，着陆地点在风暴洋雷内尔和马里于斯环形山以西地区。
    报道说，自动站同地球的无线电取得了联系，自动站上仪器工作正常。
