Title: ACFTU Sends Telegram to Support the Dominican People's Anti-American Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-02-13
Edition: 4

    All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to Support the Dominican People's Anti-American Struggle
    Xinhua News Agency, February 12th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) announced today in a telegram sent to the Dominican Workers' United Federation that Chinese workers firmly support the just struggle of Dominican workers, students, and people demanding the withdrawal of US aggressor troops from the Dominican Republic and protesting US aggression against Vietnam.
    In the telegram, the ACFTU strongly condemned the US aggressors and their lackeys for suppressing the patriotic movement of the Dominican people and for the bloody atrocities of shooting Dominican patriots. It also expressed its firm belief that the patriotic struggle carried out by the unyielding Dominican people will surely achieve final victory.

### 我全总致电支持多米尼加人民反美斗争

1966-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我全总致电支持多米尼加人民反美斗争
    新华社十二日讯　中华全国总工会今天在发给多米尼加工人联合会的一份电报中宣布，中国工人坚决支持多米尼加工人、学生和人民要求美国侵略军撤出多米尼加、抗议美国侵略越南的正义斗争。
    全总在电报中强烈谴责美国侵略者及其走狗镇压多米尼加人民的爱国运动、枪杀多米尼加爱国者的血腥暴行，并且表示深信，坚强不屈的多米尼加人民所进行的爱国斗争，必将取得最后胜利。
