Title: Japanese Folk Musician Sings "Ode to Wang Jie" Praising Mao Zedong Thought for Illuminating the Hearts of the People
Author:
Date: 1966-02-16
Edition: 5

　　Japanese Folk Musician Sings "Ode to Wang Jie"
　　Praising Mao Zedong Thought for Illuminating the Hearts of the People
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 12th—The famous Japanese folk musician, Fumio Okamoto, sang "Ode to Wang Jie" to the Tokyo audience on the evening of the 11th, singing: Mao Zedong Thought is like a red sun, illuminating the hearts of the people with boundless brilliance; it is like a lighthouse, guiding the people forward!
　　"Ode to Wang Jie" is Okamoto's new work adapted from the story of Wang Jie—a Japanese-style ballad. This ballad absorbs some characteristics of Chinese Suzhou ballad singing based on the Japanese folk art "Shin-Nai."
　　Okamoto sang in the ballad:
　　Wang Jie's gun, we will carry it,
　　Wang Jie's song, we will sing it!
　　Wang Jie is not afraid of hardship, not afraid of death,
　　Wholeheartedly for the revolution,
　　Forever following the Communist Party,
　　Forever following Chairman Mao,
　　March forward!
　　Fumio Okamoto visited China in 1964. After returning to his country, he used some characteristics of Suzhou ballad singing to create and sing "Letter from South Vietnam" and some stories of the Japanese people's struggle. Okamoto's singing of "Ode to Wang Jie" was filled with love and respect for Chairman Mao and the revolutionary soldiers educated by Mao Zedong Thought. (Photo attached)

### 日本民族音乐家演唱“王杰赞歌”   歌颂毛泽东思想照亮人民的心

1966-02-16
第5版()
专栏：外国朋友谈中国

    日本民族音乐家演唱“王杰赞歌”
    歌颂毛泽东思想照亮人民的心
    新华社东京十二日电　日本著名的民族音乐家冈本文弥十一日晚向东京听众演唱“王杰赞歌”时唱道：毛泽东思想象一轮红日，照亮人民的心，光芒万丈；它象座灯塔，指引着人民前进的方向！
    “王杰赞歌”是冈本根据王杰的故事改编的新作——和风（日本）评弹。这种评弹是在日本民族曲艺“新内”的基础上吸收了中国苏州评弹的一些特点。
    冈本在评弹中唱道：
    王杰的枪，我们来扛，
    王杰的歌，我们唱！
    王杰不怕苦、不怕死，
    一心为革命，
    永远跟着共产党，
    永远跟着毛主席，
    向前进！
    冈本文弥曾在一九六四年访问过中国。回国后他运用苏州评弹的一些特点创造和演唱过“越南南方来信”和一些日本人民斗争的故事。冈本在演唱“王杰赞歌”时，充满了对毛主席和在毛泽东思想教育下的革命战士的热爱和尊敬。（附图片）
