Title: Korean Military Attaché in China Holds Film Reception to Celebrate the 18th Anniversary of the Founding of the Korean People's Army
Author:
Date: 1966-02-06
Edition: 5

    To Celebrate the 18th Anniversary of the Founding of the Korean People's Army
    Korean Military Attaché in China Holds Film Reception
    Xinhua News Agency, February 5th: Colonel Choe Je-ryong, Military Attaché of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, held a film reception tonight to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army.
    Li Tianyou, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Liang Biye, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, and responsible persons from relevant departments Zhao Qimin, Xu Shenji, Lao She, Pan Zhenwu, Jia Ruoyu, etc., were invited to attend.
    Park Se-chang, Ambassador of Korea to China, and military attachés of various countries' embassies in China also attended the reception. At the reception, the Korean feature film "Torpedo Boat No. 51" was shown. This film reflects the heroic deeds of the Korean People's Army Navy torpedo boats sinking the US aggressors' cruiser during the Korean People's Fatherland Liberation War.

### 庆祝朝鲜人民军建军十八周年  朝鲜驻华武官举行电影招待会

1966-02-06
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝鲜人民军建军十八周年
    朝鲜驻华武官举行电影招待会
    新华社五日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆武官崔济荣上校今晚举行电影招待会，庆祝朝鲜人民军建军十八周年。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长李天佑、总政治部副主任梁必业，以及有关方面负责人赵启民、徐深吉、老舍、潘振武、贾若瑜等应邀出席。
    朝鲜驻中国大使朴世昌，各国驻中国大使馆武官，也出席了招待会。会上，放映了朝鲜故事影片《鱼雷艇五一号》。这部影片是反映在朝鲜人民祖国解放战争时期，朝鲜人民军海军鱼雷艇击沉美国侵略者巡洋舰的英雄事迹。
