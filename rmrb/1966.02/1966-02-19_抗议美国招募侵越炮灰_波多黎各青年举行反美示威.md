Title: Puerto Rican Youth Hold Anti-US Demonstrations Protesting US Recruitment of Cannon Fodder for Vietnam War

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-19
Edition: 5

    Protesting US Recruitment of Cannon Fodder for Vietnam War
    Puerto Rican Youth Hold Anti-US Demonstrations
    (Xinhua News Agency, San Juan, 18th) San Juan dispatch: On the 17th, youth in San Juan, the capital of Puerto Rico, held demonstrations and marches in front of the parliament to protest the United States' recruitment of Puerto Rican youth to serve as cannon fodder on the Vietnam battlefield. They also protested the Puerto Rican Senate's passage of a resolution supporting the US war of aggression against Vietnam.
    The demonstrations were initiated by youth organizations of the Puerto Rican independence movement. The demonstrating youth held up placards condemning the US aggression against Vietnam and supporting the Vietnamese people's struggle.
    The Puerto Rican authorities immediately dispatched police to set up a cordon around the parliament.
    The struggle of Puerto Rican youth against US aggression in Vietnam and the recruitment of cannon fodder in Puerto Rico has never stopped. In order to suppress the anti-US struggle of young students, the Puerto Rican authorities, under US pressure, have formulated a new "School Law" which prohibits any demonstrations and gatherings in schools.

### 抗议美国招募侵越炮灰  波多黎各青年举行反美示威

1966-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    抗议美国招募侵越炮灰
    波多黎各青年举行反美示威
    据新华社十八日讯　圣胡安消息：波多黎各首府圣胡安的青年十七日在议会前面举行示威游行，抗议美国招募波多黎各青年到越南战场上当炮灰，同时抗议波多黎各参议院通过支持美国侵越战争的决议。
    这次示威游行是由波多黎各争取独立运动青年组织发起的。示威的青年高举着标语牌，谴责美国侵略越南，支持越南人民的斗争。
    波多黎各当局立即派出警察在议会附近布置了封锁线。
    波多黎各青年抗议美国侵略越南和在波多黎各招募炮灰的斗争一直没有停止。为了镇压青年学生的反美斗争，波多黎各当局在美国压力下制定了一项新的“学校法”，这项法律禁止在学校里举行任何示威和集会。
