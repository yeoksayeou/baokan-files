Title: Cuban Ministry of Foreign Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-08
Edition: 5

    Cuban Ministry of Foreign Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
    (Xinhua News Agency, February 7th) The Cuban Ministry of Foreign Trade delegation, led by Ismael Bello, Director of the Asian Socialist Countries Department of the Cuban Ministry of Foreign Trade, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. Zhu Jianbai, Deputy Director of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Yu Liguang, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Cuba, were at the airport to see them off. Fernández, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Cuban Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 古巴对外贸易部代表团离京回国

1966-02-08
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴对外贸易部代表团离京回国
    新华社七日讯　由古巴对外贸易部亚洲社会主义国家司司长伊萨梅尔·贝略率领的古巴对外贸易部代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。前往机场送行的有对外贸易部副局长朱剑白、中国驻古巴商务参赞于黎光等。古巴驻中国大使馆临时代办费尔南德斯等也前往机场送行。
