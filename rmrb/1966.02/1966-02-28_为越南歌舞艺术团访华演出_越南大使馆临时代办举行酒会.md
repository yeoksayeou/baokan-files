Title: Vietnamese Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception for Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe's Performance in China
Author:
Date: 1966-02-28
Edition: 3

　　For Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe's Performance in China
    Vietnamese Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception

    Xinhua News Agency, February 27th. Hoang Bac, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, held a reception tonight for the Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe's performance in China, to entertain responsible persons from our relevant departments.

    When Chargé d'Affaires Hoang Bac and Li Chang, Vice-Minister of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of our country, spoke at the reception, they praised the eternal fighting friendship and great unity between the people of Vietnam and China, and pointed out that the people of the two countries are standing together like brothers and are fighting to defeat the common enemy—U.S. imperialism. Chargé d'Affaires Hoang Bac said that this visit to China by the Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe will make new contributions to further consolidating the fighting friendship and unity between the people of Vietnam and China and to strengthening cultural cooperation between the two countries. Vice-Minister Li Chang said that the Chinese people have been greatly encouraged by the exciting revolutionary songs and dances performed by the Vietnamese artists. The Vietnamese literary and art workers' approach of rushing to the front lines of combat with the attitude of revolutionary fighters to create and perform is deeply admired by the Chinese literary and art workers.

    Yang Xiufeng, President of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association, thanked the Vietnamese literary and art fighters for bringing to the Chinese people the militant, united, and friendly songs and dances at the toast. He congratulated the Vietnamese people on the great victory of the struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation and wished the fighting friendship between the people of China and Vietnam would last forever. Invited to the reception were also Han Nianlong, Shi Huaibi, Li Qi, Fan Jin, Sun Xiaocun, Li Wei and other responsible persons from relevant departments, as well as people from literary and art circles in the capital.

    Tru Quynh, head of the Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe, Pham Dinh Sau, deputy head, Do Kim Binh, and members of the song and dance troupe, as well as Le Tung Son, acting head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Front for Liberation in China, attended the reception.

    Literary and art workers from Vietnam and China sang revolutionary songs from both countries at the reception, adding a warm atmosphere of fraternal friendship to the reception.

### 为越南歌舞艺术团访华演出  越南大使馆临时代办举行酒会

1966-02-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　为越南歌舞艺术团访华演出
    越南大使馆临时代办举行酒会
    新华社二十七日讯　越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北，今天晚上为越南歌舞艺术团访华演出举行酒会，招待我国有关方面负责人。
    黄北临时代办和我国对外文委副主任李昌在酒会上讲话时，赞颂了越中两国人民永恒的战斗友谊和伟大团结，指出两国人民象兄弟一样站在一起，正在为战胜共同的敌人——美帝国主义而斗争。黄北临时代办说，越南歌舞艺术团这次访问中国，将为进一步巩固越中两国人民的战斗友谊和团结，为密切两国的文化合作作出新的贡献。李昌副主任说，中国人民从越南艺术家们表演的激动人心的革命歌舞中受到了巨大的鼓舞。越南文艺工作者以革命战士的姿态奔赴战斗的前线进行创作、演出，使中国文艺工作者深为钦佩。
    中越友协会长杨秀峰在祝酒时感谢越南文艺战士给中国人民带来了战斗的、团结的、友谊的歌舞艺术。他祝贺越南人民抗美救国斗争的伟大胜利，祝中越两国人民的战斗友谊万古长青。应邀出席酒会的，还有韩念龙、史怀璧、李琦、范瑾、孙晓村、李伟等有关方面负责人，以及首都文艺界人士。
    越南歌舞艺术团团长瞿辉瑾、副团长范庭六、团金平和歌舞团成员，以及越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长黎松山，都出席了酒会。
    越中两国文艺工作者在酒会上演唱了越中两国的革命歌曲，为酒会增添了兄弟情谊的热烈气氛。
