	International Briefs
	General Ne Win Pays a State Visit to Ceylon
	Colombo dispatch: General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, arrived here by special plane on the afternoon of the 10th for a one-week state visit to Ceylon.
	President Daddah Concludes Visit to Mali
	Bamako dispatch: Mauritanian President Moktar Ould Daddah left here on the morning of the 9th to return home after a six-day official visit to Mali.
	In Dougabougou, the Mauritanian guests visited the sugar factory built with Chinese assistance to Mali.
	Congolese (Brazzaville) Youth Hold Demonstrations
	Brazzaville dispatch: On the morning of February 6, Congolese (Brazzaville) youth held a grand demonstration in Patrice Lumumba Square in the second largest city, Pointe-Noire, to commemorate Youth Day.
	The demonstrators held many placards with slogans such as "Oppose the US War of Aggression in Vietnam!" and "Oppose Neo-Colonialism and Imperialist Countries!"
	More Than 5,000 West German University Students Demonstrate
	Bonn dispatch: Four thousand university students in Göttingen, Lower Saxony, West Germany, braved the rain on the 10th to demonstrate in the streets, protesting against the Bonn government's reduction of subsidies for university construction. Some professors, lecturers, and teaching assistants also participated in the demonstration.
	Fifteen hundred university students in Saarbrücken, Saarland, held a similar demonstration on the 9th. The demonstrators' placards read: "Stopping construction is equivalent to stopping education." They also shouted slogans demanding the replacement of Chancellor Erhard.

### 国际简讯

1966-02-13
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    奈温将军到锡兰进行国事访问
    科伦坡消息：缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军十日下午乘专机到达这里，对锡兰进行一星期的国事访问。
    达达赫总统结束对马里的访问
    巴马科消息：毛里塔尼亚总统莫克塔·乌尔德·达达赫在马里进行了六天的正式访问后，九日上午离开这里回国。
    在杜加布古，毛里塔尼亚客人参观了中国帮助马里建设的糖厂。
    刚果（布）青年举行示威游行
    布拉柴维尔消息：刚果（布）青年二月六日上午在第二大城市黑角的帕特里斯·卢蒙巴广场举行盛大的示威游行纪念青年节。
    游行者举着许多标语牌，上面写着“反对美国侵越战争！”和“反对新老殖民主义和帝国主义国家！”等口号。
    西德五千多名大学生举行示威
    波恩消息：西德下萨克森州戈丁根市的四千名大学生十日冒雨在街头示威，抗议波恩政府缩减对大学建筑费用的补助。一些教授、讲师和助教也参加了示威。
    萨尔州萨尔布吕肯市的一千五百名大学生九日也举行了同样的示威。示威者的标语牌上写着：“停止建筑就等于停止教育”。他们还高呼口号，要求撤换总理艾哈德。
