Title: The German String Quartet Gives Its First Performance in China

Author:

Date: 1966-02-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 16th: The Ulbricht String Quartet from the German Democratic Republic gave its first performance in China in Beijing tonight.
    Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, Vice Minister of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Ding Xilin, and figures from the capital's music circles, along with the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Mr. Költ, enjoyed tonight's performance. During the intermission, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo met with the four performers, including Ulbricht.

### 德弦乐四重奏组举行访华首次演出

1966-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    德弦乐四重奏组举行访华首次演出
    新华社十六日讯　德意志民主共和国乌尔布利希弦乐四重奏组，今天晚上在北京举行访华首次演出。
    郭沫若副委员长、对外文委副主任丁西林以及首都音乐界人士，同德意志民主共和国驻中国大使柯尔特一起，欣赏了今晚的演出。在演出休息的时候，郭沫若副委员长接见了乌尔布利希等四位演奏家。
