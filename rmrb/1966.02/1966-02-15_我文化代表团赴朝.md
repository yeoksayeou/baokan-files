Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation Visits North Korea
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-15
Edition: 4

    Our Cultural Delegation to North Korea
    Xinhua News Agency, February 14th. A Chinese Cultural Delegation led by Song Yiping, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, left Beijing by train today for the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to discuss the annual execution plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Responsible persons from relevant units, including Shi Huaibi, Li Qi, Zhang Yingwu, and Liu Xianglun, went to the station to see them off.
    The Korean Ambassador to China, Pak Se-chang, also went to the station to see them off.

### 我文化代表团赴朝

1966-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团赴朝
    新华社十四日讯　由对外文委副主任宋一平率领的中国文化代表团今天乘火车离开北京，前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国商订中朝文化合作协定年度执行计划。
    有关单位负责人史怀璧、李琦、张映吾、刘祥纶等前往车站送行。
    朝鲜驻中国大使朴世昌也前往车站送行。
