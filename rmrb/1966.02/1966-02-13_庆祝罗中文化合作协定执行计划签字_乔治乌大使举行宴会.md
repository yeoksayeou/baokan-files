Title: Ambassador Gheorghiu Hosts Banquet to Celebrate Signing of Romanian-Chinese Cultural Cooperation Agreement Execution Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-02-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 12th. Romanian Ambassador to China, Dumitru Gheorghiu, hosted a banquet this noon to celebrate the signing of the 1966-1967 execution plan of the Romanian-Chinese Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Invited to attend the banquet were responsible persons from relevant departments, including Li Qitao, Wang Bingnan, Liu Zizai, Li Qi, and Zhou Xinwu.

### 庆祝罗中文化合作协定执行计划签字  乔治乌大使举行宴会

1966-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗中文化合作协定执行计划签字
    乔治乌大使举行宴会
    新华社十二日讯　罗马尼亚驻中国大使杜米特鲁·乔治乌今天中午举行宴会，庆祝罗中文化合作协定一九六六年至一九六七年执行计划签字。应邀出席宴会的，有李琦涛、王炳南、刘子载、李琦、周新武等有关方面负责人。
