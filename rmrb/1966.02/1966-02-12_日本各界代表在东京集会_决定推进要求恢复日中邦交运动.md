Title: Representatives from All Circles in Japan Gather in Tokyo, Decide to Promote Movement to Demand Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1966-02-12
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo Dispatch: Representatives from all circles in Japan held a gathering in Tokyo on the 7th, deciding to collect one million signatures demanding the restoration of Japan-China diplomatic relations from today until the end of March, so as to enable the ongoing signature campaign in Japan to collect 30 million signatures to reach its goal as soon as possible.
    This gathering was convened by all five member organizations of the Liaison Council for Promoting the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations: the Communist Party, the Socialist Party, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), the Japan-China Friendship Association, and the Japan Peace Committee. Nishiizawa Tomio, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Kuroda Hisao, chairman of the Audit Committee of the Socialist Party, Suzuki Seiichi, vice-director of the International Department of "Sohyo," and others attended the gathering. Miyazaki Seimin, Sakamoto Tokumatsu, Shiraishi Bon, Nishikawa Kagemi, Kobayashi Yuichi, and Fujita Shigeru also attended the gathering.
    The gathering decided to establish liaison councils for promoting the restoration of Japan-China diplomatic relations in all prefectures and determined the policy of organizing signature campaigns in all possible places.
    The gathering issued a letter to the people, calling on all those who advocate the restoration of Japan-China diplomatic relations to actively participate in this movement.

### 日本各界代表在东京集会  决定推进要求恢复日中邦交运动

1966-02-12
第5版()
专栏：

    日本各界代表在东京集会
    决定推进要求恢复日中邦交运动
    新华社讯　东京消息：日本各界代表七日在东京举行集会，决定从今天起到三月底为止，征集一百万个要求恢复日中邦交的签名，以使正在日本开展的征集三千万人签名的运动早日达到目标。
    这个集会是由促进恢复日中邦交联络会议的全部五个成员组织共产党、社会党、日本工会总评议会、日中友好协会和日本和平委员会召开的。日共中央政治局候补委员西泽富夫，社会党监察委员会委员长黑田寿男，“总评”国际部副部长铃木诚市等出席了这个集会。出席集会的还有宫崎世民、坂本德松、白石凡、西川景文、小林雄一和藤田茂等。
    集会决定了在所有各县成立促进恢复日中邦交联络会议，并确定了在一切可能的地方组织签名运动的方针。
    集会发表了告国民书，号召一切主张恢复日中邦交的人积极参加这个运动。
