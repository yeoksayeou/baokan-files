Title: Japanese Communist Comrades Visit Guangzhou

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-16
Edition: 1

　　新华社广州十五日电　以日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治同志为首的日本共产党代表团全体成员，在中共中央委员刘宁一和中共中央机关负责工作人员赵安博陪同下，今天上午参观了广州起义烈士陵园，并献了花圈。随后，还游览了广州白云山风景区。
　　陪同参观游览的，还有中共广东省委书记处书记林李明，中共广州市委第三书记曾生。
　　今天晚上，日本共产党代表团在中共中央中南局书记处书记吴芝圃以及林李明、曾生、赵安博等同志的陪同下，观看了中国人民解放军广州部队战士话剧团演出的话剧《英雄工兵》。

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 15th - The entire delegation of the Japanese Communist Party, led by Comrade Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, and accompanied by Comrade Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Comrade Zhao Anbo, responsible staff member of the Central Committee organs of the Communist Party of China, visited the Guangzhou Uprising Martyrs Cemetery this morning and laid a wreath. Afterwards, they also toured the Baiyun Mountain scenic area in Guangzhou.
　　Also accompanying the visit were Lin Liming, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Zeng Sheng, Third Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China.
　　This evening, the Japanese Communist Party delegation, accompanied by Wu Zhipu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central South Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, as well as Comrades Lin Liming, Zeng Sheng, Zhao Anbo, and others, watched the drama "Heroic Engineer" performed by the soldier drama troupe of the Guangzhou Troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
