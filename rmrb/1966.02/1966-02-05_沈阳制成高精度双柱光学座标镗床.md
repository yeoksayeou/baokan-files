Title: Shenyang Manufactures High-Precision Double-Column Optical Coordinate Boring Machine
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-05
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, February 4th - A crucial piece of equipment for developing precision instruments, meters, and other machines - a high-precision double-column optical coordinate boring machine - has been successfully trial-produced in Shenyang.
    This double-column optical coordinate boring machine can not only bore holes and mill, but also scribe and measure. Its positioning system adopts technologies such as metal line scales and optical devices, resulting in very high dimensional accuracy of the processed parts. The maximum error does not exceed 0.005 millimeters, equivalent to one-fourteenth of a human hair, reaching the international advanced level.
    This coordinate boring machine was jointly trial-produced through the collaboration of more than ten machine tool factories and related machine tool research departments in Shenyang, Harbin, Beijing, and other places.

### 沈阳制成高精度双柱光学座标镗床

1966-02-05
第2版()
专栏：

    沈阳制成高精度双柱光学座标镗床
    新华社沈阳四日电　发展精密仪器、仪表以及其他机器用的一种关键设备——高精度双柱光学座标镗床，已经在沈阳试制成功。
    这种双柱光学座标镗床既可以镗孔、铣削，又可以划线测量。它的定位系统采用了金属线纹尺、光学装置等技术，因而加工的部件尺寸精度很高，最大误差不超过千分之五毫米，相当于一根头发丝的十四分之一，达到了国际先进水平。
    这台座标镗床是由沈阳、哈尔滨、北京等十几个机床厂和有关机床研究部门共同协作试制的。
