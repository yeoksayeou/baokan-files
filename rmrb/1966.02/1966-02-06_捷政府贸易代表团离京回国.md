Title: Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-06
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, February 5th -- The Czechoslovak government trade delegation, led by Czechoslovak Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, M. Honusek, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Jiang Ming and others went to the airport to see them off. Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, J. Křístek, and others also went to the airport to see them off.

### 捷政府贸易代表团离京回国

1966-02-06
第5版()
专栏：

    捷政府贸易代表团离京回国
    新华社五日讯　由捷克斯洛伐克对外贸易部副部长米·霍努塞克率领的捷克斯洛伐克政府贸易代表团今日上午乘飞机离开北京回国。前往机场送行的有对外贸易部副部长江明等。捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使克日斯特克等也前往机场送行。
