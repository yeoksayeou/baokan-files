Title: The Vietnam Asian-African Solidarity Committee and the Committee for the Defense of World Peace Issue a Statement Protesting the U.S. Imperialists Directing the Indonesian Reactionaries to Slaughter Patriotic People, Exposing the U.S. Imperialists' Attempt to Transform Indonesia into a New-Type Colony of the United States
Author:
Date: 1966-02-24
Edition: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 23rd—The Vietnam Asian-African People's Solidarity Committee and the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace issued a statement on the 21st, strongly condemning the Indonesian pro-American reactionaries for their crimes of frantically slaughtering patriotic, democratic, and progressive individuals, including members of the Indonesian Communist Party.
    The statement said that the pro-American reactionaries conducting this white terror are conspiring to eliminate Indonesian patriots and destroy the revolutionary achievements obtained by the Indonesian people through decades of arduous struggle. The "trial" farce they are staging now is precisely to slander patriots and legalize their killing, while preparing to further suppress the progressive forces in Indonesia.
    The statement pointed out that the current actions of the Indonesian reactionaries are all directed by the U.S. imperialists. The U.S. imperialists are attempting, through this conspiracy, to destroy the unity of the Indonesian people, undermine the Indonesian revolution, and transform Indonesia into their new-type colony. It is precisely because of this that the U.S. imperialists are expressing great satisfaction with the serious situation in Indonesia today.
    The statement emphasized, "The Vietnam Asian-African People's Solidarity Committee and the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace strongly protest the barbaric white terror actions of the Indonesian pro-American reactionaries. We resolutely demand an immediate end to the slaughter and arrest of Indonesian patriots."

### 越南亚非团结委员会和保卫世界和平委员会发表声明　抗议美帝指挥印度尼西亚反动派屠杀爱国人士　揭露美帝妄图把印度尼西亚变为美国的新型殖民地

1966-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南亚非团结委员会和保卫世界和平委员会发表声明
　　抗议美帝指挥印度尼西亚反动派屠杀爱国人士
　　揭露美帝妄图把印度尼西亚变为美国的新型殖民地
    新华社河内二十三日电  越南亚非人民团结委员会和越南保卫世界和平委员会二十一日发表声明，强烈谴责印度尼西亚亲美反动派疯狂屠杀包括印度尼西亚共产党人在内的爱国、民主和进步人士的罪行。
    声明说，进行这场白色恐怖的亲美反动派正在阴谋消灭印度尼西亚的爱国者，摧毁印度尼西亚人民经历数十年艰苦斗争所得来的革命成果。它们现在所上演的“审判”丑剧正是为了污蔑爱国者，并且使它们的杀人行为合法化，同时准备再进一步镇压印度尼西亚的进步力量。
    声明指出，印度尼西亚反动派目前的行动，都是由美帝国主义者一手指挥的。美帝国主义者妄图通过这个阴谋，来摧毁印度尼西亚人民的团结，破坏印度尼西亚的革命，并且把印度尼西亚变为它们的新型殖民地。正因为如此，美帝国主义者对印度尼西亚今天的严重局面表示十分高兴。
    声明强调说，“越南亚非人民团结委员会和越南保卫世界和平委员会强烈抗议印度尼西亚亲美反动派的野蛮的白色恐怖行动。我们坚决要求立即停止对印度尼西亚爱国者的屠杀和逮捕。”
