Title: Cambodian Government Condemns American Warmongers for Creating Pretexts for Invading Cambodia; Cambodia Determined to Repulse All Attacks; Cambodian Border Guards Heroically Repulse Invasions by American-South Vietnamese Puppet Troops and Thai Troops
Author:
Date: 1966-02-26
Edition: 4

　　Cambodian Government Condemns American Warmongers for Creating Pretexts for Invading Cambodia
　　Cambodia Determined to Repulse All Attacks
　　Cambodian Border Guards Heroically Repulse Invasions by American-South Vietnamese Puppet Troops and Thai Troops
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 24th (Dispatch) - The Royal Government of Cambodia issued a statement on February 24th, exposing the U.S. imperialists' creation of pretexts to expand their war of aggression into Cambodia, and expressing Cambodia's determination to repel all attacks on its territory.
    The statement said: "U.S. Democratic Senator Stuart Symington, a member of the Senate Armed Services Committee and the Senate Foreign Relations Committee, asserted in a recently published report that the Viet Cong's transportation lines and supply lines pass through Laos and Cambodia, and that the communists use these two countries as sanctuaries. He called for drafting a plan to limit, if not completely prevent, the communists from gaining convenience in these two countries."
    The statement said: "The Royal Government angrily condemns Senator Symington, a war-monger long recognized by international public opinion, for his shameless clamor for open aggression against Cambodia."
    The statement pointed out: "Facts fully prove that there are neither bases of the National Liberation Front nor its transportation or supply lines on Cambodian territory. All these accusations are groundless. Therefore, Senator Symington cannot be interpreted as anything other than demonstrating an attempt to expand America's war of aggression to all countries, like Cambodia, that refuse the neo-colonialist rule that the United States attempts to impose on them."
    The statement said: "This report, written by the Senator after inspecting South Vietnam, indicates that the American military and political heads in Saigon and their South Vietnamese 'government' accomplices are insisting on pushing forward their plan for armed intervention in Cambodia, and are striving to obtain the approval of the American Senate and government."
    The statement said: "The Royal Government once again solemnly warns the American warmongers that Cambodia is determined to repel all attacks on its territory."
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 25th (Dispatch) - According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on February 25th, on the morning of February 7th, about forty South Vietnamese puppet troops invaded Takeo Province in Cambodia, and fired on the Cambodian border guards at the aforementioned post in Kaoh Bachi, 800 meters from a post in Takeo Province. The border guards fiercely counterattacked, forcing the invaders to retreat back into their own territory. One armed Cambodian villager was wounded.
    From February 7th to 20th, American-South Vietnamese aircraft intruded into the airspace of Takeo Province, Kampong Cham Province, Svay Rieng Province, Kandal Province, Kratie Province, and Prey Veng Province in Cambodia eighteen times.
    The Cambodian News Agency also reported that on February 24th, an American-South Vietnamese puppet troop aircraft attacked Ba Thu village in Svay Rieng Province, Cambodia, with machine guns and incendiary bombs, dropping at least twelve incendiary bombs.
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 24th (Dispatch) - According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on February 24th: At 13:30 on February 21st, Thai troops in several groups invaded Cambodian territory in the Lai Wang area of Koh Kong Province, Cambodian armed forces exchanged fire with the enemy for about ten minutes.
    At noon on February 18th, Thai troops shelled the Ou Smach border post in Oddar Meanchey Province, Cambodia. Cambodian armed forces retaliated with heavy weapons.
    This news agency reported on the 23rd that on the afternoon of February 4th, South Vietnamese puppet troops fired two shells at Cambodian territory, landing near a provincial defense force post in Prey Veng Province.

### 柬政府谴责美国好战分子制造侵柬借口　柬埔寨决心回击一切进攻　柬边防军英勇击退美国—南越伪军和泰军的入侵

1966-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　柬政府谴责美国好战分子制造侵柬借口
　　柬埔寨决心回击一切进攻
　　柬边防军英勇击退美国—南越伪军和泰军的入侵
    新华社金边二十四日电  柬埔寨王国政府二十四日发表声明，揭露美帝国主义为把侵略战争扩大到柬埔寨制造借口，表示柬埔寨决心回击对自己领土的一切进攻。
    声明说：“美国民主党参议员、参议院武装部队委员会和参议院外交委员会委员斯图尔特·薛明顿在最近公布的一份报告中断言，越共的交通线和供应线是通过老挝和柬埔寨的，共产党人利用这两个国家作为庇护所。他要求起草一项计划，以便即使不完全阻止也要限制共产党人在这两个国家中取得方便。”
    声明说：“王国政府愤怒谴责早已被国际舆论公认的战争贩子参议员薛明顿要对柬埔寨进行公开侵略的无耻叫嚣”。
    声明指出：“事实充分证明，柬埔寨领土上既没有民族解放阵线的基地，也没有它的交通线或供应线，这一切指责都是毫无根据的。因此，参议员薛明顿除了表明企图把美国的侵略战争扩大到象柬埔寨这样拒绝美国企图强加在它们身上的新殖民主义统治的所有国家以外，不可能作任何别的解释”。
    声明说：“这位参议员在视察南越后所写的这份报告指出，美国在西贡的军政头目及其南越‘政府’的帮凶坚持推行他们武装干涉柬埔寨的计划，并且力图取得美国参议院和政府的赞同”。
    声明说：“王国政府再一次向美国好战分子提出严正警告，柬埔寨将决心回击对自己领土的一切进攻”。
    新华社金边二十五日电  据柬埔寨新闻社二十五日报道，二月七日上午，约四十名南越伪军侵入柬埔寨境内的茶胶省，并且在茶胶省的一个哨所附近八百米的考巴恰地方，向上述哨所的柬埔寨边防军开火。边防军进行了猛烈回击，迫使侵略者退回到自己的境内。一名武装的柬埔寨村民被打伤。
    二月七日到二十日，美国—南越飞机曾经十八次侵入柬埔寨茶胶省、磅湛省、柴桢省、干丹省、桔井省和波萝勉省领空。
    柬埔寨新闻社还报道，二月二十四日一架美国—南越伪军的飞机用机枪和燃烧弹袭击了柬埔寨柴桢省的巴图村，至少投下了十二枚燃烧弹。
    据新华社金边二十四日电  据柬埔寨新闻社二十四日报道：二月二十一日下午一时三十分，泰国军队分几股侵入戈公省科波村赖旺地区的柬埔寨领土，柬埔寨武装部队同敌人交火约十分钟。
    二月十八日中午，泰国军队炮击了柬埔寨奥多棉藏省奥斯马边境哨所，柬埔寨武装部队用重武器进行了回击。
    这家新闻社二十三日报道，二月四日下午，南越伪军向柬埔寨领土发射了两发炮弹，落在波萝勉省一个省防军哨所附近。
