Title: "Five Hearts"
Author: Xuan Yaodong
Date: 1966-02-09
Edition: 6

    "Five Hearts"
    Shanghai State-Owned Cotton Mill No. 17 Xuan Yaodong
   When comparing to advanced models, have confidence;
   When learning from advanced models, first be humble;
   When catching up with advanced models, make a firm resolution;
   When helping those lagging behind, be sincere;
   When surpassing advanced models, have great ambition.
   With these "Five Hearts,"
   Production will take a great leap forward.

### “五心”
宣耀栋
1966-02-09
第6版()
专栏：

    “五心”
    上海国棉十七厂  宣耀栋
   比先进，要有信心；
   学先进，先要虚心；
   赶先进，要下决心；
   帮后进，就得诚心；
   超先进，要树雄心。
   有了这“五心”，
   生产大跃进。
