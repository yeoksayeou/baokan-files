Title: Hard to Tell Who is Worse

Author: 
Date: 1966-02-08
Edition: 4

    In Honolulu, U.S. President Johnson praised the South Vietnamese puppets for their "vigorous activities" in improving the people's lives. The South Vietnamese "Head of State" Nguyen Van Thieu, on the other hand, expressed his "great admiration for the outstanding combat spirit of the American soldiers."
    The nauseating mutual flattery of these two people is truly hard to say which is more disgusting.

### 难分伯仲

1966-02-08
第4版()
专栏：三言两语

    难分伯仲
    在檀香山，美国总统约翰逊称赞南越傀儡在改善人民生活方面“富有生气的活动”。南越的“元首”阮文绍则表示“非常钦佩美国军人的卓越的作战精神”。
    这两个人的肉麻当有趣的吹捧，真是很难说谁比谁更加令人作呕。
