Title: Australian Communist Vanguard Exposes Soviet Leaders' Conspiracy to Advocate "Joint Action"; Soviet Leaders Attempt to Control and Frustrate the Vietnamese People's Struggle; The So-Called "Joint Action" is a True Betrayal of the Vietnamese People

Author:

Date: 1966-02-25
Edition: 3

　　Australian Communist *Vanguard* Exposes Soviet Leaders' Conspiracy to Advocate "Joint Action"
　　Soviet Leaders Attempt to Control and Frustrate the Vietnamese People's Struggle
　　The So-Called "Joint Action" is a True Betrayal of the Vietnamese People

    Xinhua News Agency, February 24th Dispatch from Melbourne: The Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) organ, *Vanguard*, published an article in its third volume, fourth issue in February, exposing that the essence of the so-called "joint action" by socialist countries to aid Vietnam, advocated by the new Soviet leaders, is a "true betrayal."

    The article solemnly pointed out that the purpose of the "joint action of socialist countries" suggested by the new Soviet leaders is to more effectively "'control' the Vietnamese people, frustrate their struggle, and cause this struggle to die halfway."

    The article pointed out that the new Soviet leaders are currently touting the "enormous assistance" they provide to Vietnam and are heavily promoting their own "sincerity" and "integrity," but "this practice itself proves its disloyalty and faithlessness." The article said, "Does an honest person have to loudly proclaim his honesty and sincerity? Does he have to hang out a sign indicating honesty? No, it is precisely those who are dishonest who have to do this."

    The article then asked, "Furthermore, why are the Soviet leaders actively participating in the current seventeen-nation disarmament conference together with the 'evil aggressor' US imperialism?

    "Why do they say that the Vietnam issue is a problem unrelated to disarmament?

    "The Vietnam issue is certainly not a problem unrelated to disarmament or arms expansion. Vietnam is a victim of the military build-up being expanded by US imperialists. By holding disarmament talks with the US imperialists, as if the US imperialists really care about disarmament, the Soviet leaders are deceiving the people and protecting US imperialism.

    "Why are the Soviet leaders holding talks and mutually praising Harriman, Humphrey, and other US imperialist figures at a time when the United States is intensifying its aggression against the Vietnamese people?

    "Also, why do the Soviet leaders attack China together with US imperialism? Regardless of the motivation (and we say the motivation is the same), the result is the same—preparing to launch a war against China.

    "Why can the US imperialists withdraw troops from Berlin and Western Europe and concentrate them near Vietnam and China?

    "Why is it permissible to put aside the Berlin issue—the importance of which has always been emphasized by the Soviet leaders?

    "Is this assistance to the Vietnamese people?"

    The article emphasized that the new Soviet leaders and their followers "never talk about the assistance that the Vietnamese people are giving to the entire socialist world and all oppressed people." But, "The contribution made by the Vietnamese people is the greatest. They are saving world peace because they are defeating and weakening the main warmonger—US imperialism."

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne Dispatch: The Australian *Vanguard* published an article on the 17th regarding US Vice President Humphrey's visit to Australia, pointing out that Humphrey's visit is part of a new conspiracy by the United States on the Vietnam issue.

    The article said, "Australians will undoubtedly demand that Humphrey roll back and tell his masters that we do not want to have anything to do with their conspiracy."

    The article pointed out that the united front of the whole world against US imperialism is developing day by day, and US imperialism has many nooses around its neck, suffocating, "But, shamefully, their main protectors and volunteer saviors are the Soviet revisionist leaders."

    The article continued, if we accept their hypocritical demands for socialist countries to take "joint action" to support the Vietnamese people, that is to cut the noose around the neck of US imperialism.

    The article pointed out that it is impossible to unite with the Soviet revisionist leaders, because they believe that forming alliances with the reactionaries of India, Japan, and even West Germany, not to mention US imperialism, is "permissible."

### 澳共《先锋报》揭露苏共领导鼓吹“联合行动”阴谋　苏共领导力图控制和挫败越南人民的斗争　所谓“联合行动”是对越南人民的真正叛卖

1966-02-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　澳共《先锋报》揭露苏共领导鼓吹“联合行动”阴谋
　　苏共领导力图控制和挫败越南人民的斗争
　　所谓“联合行动”是对越南人民的真正叛卖
    新华社二十四日讯　墨尔本消息：澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）机关报《先锋报》在二月份出版的第三卷第四期中刊登一篇文章，揭露苏共新领导鼓吹的所谓社会主义国家采取援越“联合行动”的实质是“真正的叛卖”。
    文章严正指出，苏共新领导所建议的“社会主义国家联合行动，其目的在于更加有效地‘控制’越南人民，挫败他们的斗争，使这场斗争中途夭折”。
    文章指出，苏共新领导目前正在大肆吹嘘它对越南提供的“巨大援助”，并且正在大量宣传自己的“真诚”和
  “正直”，但是“这种做法本身恰恰证明了它的不忠不信”。文章说，“一个诚实的人难道必须大声宣扬自己的诚实和真诚吗？难道他必须挂出一块表明诚实的招牌吗？不，恰恰是那些不诚实的人才不得不这样做。”
    文章接着问道，“再有，苏联领导人为什么同‘罪恶的侵略者’美帝国主义一起积极参加目前的十七国裁军会议呢？
    “他们为什么说越南问题是同裁军问题无关的一个问题呢？
    “越南问题当然决不是同裁军或扩军无关的问题。越南是美帝国主义者正在扩充中的军备的受害者。苏联领导人通过同美帝国主义者举行裁军问题会谈，仿佛美帝国主义者当真关心裁军似的，这样就迷惑了人民，保护了美帝国主义。
    “正当美国加紧侵略越南人民的时候，为什么苏联领导人同哈里曼、汉弗莱及其他美帝国主义头子举行会谈和互相吹捧呢？
    “还有，为什么苏联领导人要同美帝国主义一道攻击中国呢？不管出于什么动机（而我们说动机是一样的），结果是一样的——准备对中国发动战争。
    “为什么美帝国主义者能够从柏林和西欧撤出部队，把它们集中到越南和中国附近呢？
    “为什么容许把柏林问题——这个问题的重要性一向是苏联领导人经常强调的——搁置一旁？
    “难道这是对越南人民的援助吗？”
    文章强调指出，苏共新领导及其追随者“从来不谈越南人民正在给予整个社会主义世界和全体被压迫人民的援助”。但是，“越南人民所作的贡献是最大的。他们正在拯救世界和平，因为他们正在打败并削弱主要的战争贩子——美帝国主义”。
    据新华社讯　墨尔本消息：澳大利亚《先锋报》十七日就美国副总统汉弗莱访问澳大利亚发表文章指出，汉弗莱的访问是美国在越南问题上推行新阴谋的一部分。
    文章说，“澳大利亚人无疑将要求汉弗莱滚回去，并且告诉他的主子们，我们不希望同他们的阴谋发生任何关系”。
    文章指出全世界反对美帝的统一战线日益发展，美帝国主义的脖子上套着许多绞索，喘不过气来，“但是，可耻的是，他们的主要保护者和志愿救命人竟是苏联修正主义领导人。”
    文章接着说，如果接受他们关于社会主义国家采取“联合行动”支持越南人的虚伪要求，那就是剪断美帝国主义脖子上的绞索。
    文章指出，同苏联修正主义领导人是不可能实行联合的，因为他们认为，同印度、日本甚至西德的反动派，更不用说同美帝国主义，结成联盟是“许可的”。
