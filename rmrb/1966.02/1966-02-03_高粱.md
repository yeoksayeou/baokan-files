Title: Sorghum
Author: 
Date: 1966-02-03
Edition: 2

　　Sorghum, also called Shushu, Shushu, Jiaozai, Xiaoshu, Lusui, etc., is one of the main grain crops cultivated in Northeast China and parts of North China.
　　Sorghum rice contains a large amount of protein, fat, carbohydrates, and vitamin B. In addition to being used for food, sorghum is an important raw material for brewing, starch production, and sugar production. By-products of sorghum processing, such as rice bran, distiller's grains, and powder residue, are good feed for livestock. Sorghum leaves can be used as livestock feed. Sorghum stalks are excellent fuel, and in many places, they are half a year's worth of firewood for farmers; they are also building materials and weaving materials. After threshing, the ears can be used to make brooms and cooking brushes.
　　Sorghum has characteristics such as drought resistance, waterlogging resistance, barrenness resistance, and alkali resistance. It is planted in both the north and south of China, with the main producing areas concentrated in North China, Northeast China, and the eastern part of Inner Mongolia. Most areas implement spring sowing, a small number of areas implement summer sowing, and individual areas have the habit of raising seedlings for transplanting.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（Xinhua News Agency）

### 高粱

1966-02-03
第2版()
专栏：小资料

    高粱
    高粱也叫蜀黍、秫秫、茭子、小秫、芦粟等，是我国东北和华北部分地区栽培的主要粮食作物之一。
    高粱米含有大量的蛋白质、脂肪、碳水化合物，以及乙种维生素。高粱除供食用外，是酿酒、制淀粉和制糖的重要原料。高粱加工后的副产品，如米糠、酒糟和粉渣等，是家畜的良好饲料。高粱叶可作牲畜饲料。高粱秆是极好的燃料，在许多地方是农民的半年烧柴；它又是建筑材料和编织材料。穗子脱粒后可以制做扫帚和炊帚。
    高粱具有抗旱、耐涝、耐瘠、耐碱等特性。我国南北方都有种植，主要产区集中在华北、东北、内蒙古东部地区。绝大部分地区实行春播，小部分地区夏播，个别地区有育苗移栽的习惯。
                （新华社）
