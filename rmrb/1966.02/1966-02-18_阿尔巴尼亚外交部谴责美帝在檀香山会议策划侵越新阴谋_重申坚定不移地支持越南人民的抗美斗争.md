Title: Albanian Foreign Ministry Condemns US Imperialism's New Plot to Invade Vietnam at Honolulu Conference; Reaffirms Unwavering Support for the Vietnamese People's Struggle Against US Aggression
Author:
Date: 1966-02-18
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 16th. The Albanian Foreign Ministry issued a statement on the 16th regarding the meeting held by US imperialism and the South Vietnamese puppets in Honolulu, saying, "The Honolulu meeting is a conference of robbers plotting against the Vietnamese people, and a new step by US imperialists to expand aggression in Vietnam and the entire Southeast Asia."
    The statement said, "The decisions of this conference once again expose the lies of the US imperialists and their servants regarding so-called 'seeking peace in Vietnam' and 'peace negotiations'."
    The statement pointed out that the Albanian Foreign Ministry fully supports the recent statements issued by the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Central Committee of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front regarding the Honolulu Conference.
    The statement pointed out that the US imperialists are implementing a "kill all, burn all, destroy all" policy in South Vietnam, resuming and intensifying the bombing of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and continuing to expand aggression. All this clearly reveals the deceptive nature of the US imperialists' so-called aim of this conference to realize a program of so-called "national reconstruction and the realization of political, social, and economic reforms."
    The statement said, "Like many such conspiracies plotted in the past, the new conspiracy plan formulated by Johnson in Honolulu will certainly be crushed by the resolute resistance and struggle of the heroic Vietnamese people."
    The statement said, "The US imperialists are responsible for violating the 1954 Geneva Accords on the Vietnam issue, occupying South Vietnam, launching unprovoked aggression against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and committing inhuman crimes against the Vietnamese people. The increasingly urgent task of peace-loving people and countries around the world is to condemn US aggression against Vietnam, express protest, and do everything possible to support the sacred struggle of the Vietnamese people, to stop the clutches of the imperialist aggressors, and force them to get out of Vietnam."
    The statement concluded, "The government of the People's Republic of Albania, which supports the heroic Vietnamese people, strongly condemns the Honolulu Conference as a new act of war by US imperialists, and reaffirms that the Albanian people unwaveringly support the fraternal Vietnamese people and firmly believe that the glorious struggle of the heroic Vietnamese people will achieve complete victory over the US imperialist aggressors."

### 阿尔巴尼亚外交部谴责美帝在檀香山会议策划侵越新阴谋  重申坚定不移地支持越南人民的抗美斗争

1966-02-18
第3版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚外交部谴责美帝在檀香山会议策划侵越新阴谋
    重申坚定不移地支持越南人民的抗美斗争
    新华社地拉那十六日电 阿尔巴尼亚外交部十六日就美帝国主义同南越傀儡在檀香山举行的会议发表声明说，“檀香山会议是强盗们策划反对越南人民的阴谋的一次会议，是美帝国主义者扩大在越南和整个东南亚侵略的新步骤。”
    声明说，“这次会议的决定，再一次暴露了美帝国主义者及其仆从的所谓‘在越南谋求和平’和‘和平谈判’的骗局。”
    声明指出，阿尔巴尼亚外交部完全支持越南民主共和国外交部和越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会最近就檀香山会议发表的声明。
    声明指出，美帝国主义者在越南南方实行“杀光、烧光和毁光”的政策，恢复和加强对越南民主共和国的轰炸以及继续扩大侵略，都清楚地揭露了美帝国主义者的所谓这次会议的目的是为了实现所谓“重建国家和实现政治、社会和经济改革”的纲领的欺人之谈。
    声明说，“就象过去策划的许多这样的阴谋一样，约翰逊在檀香山制定的新的阴谋计划，一定会被英雄的越南人民的坚决抗击和斗争所粉碎。”
    声明说，“美帝国主义者破坏一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议，霸占越南南方，对越南民主共和国发动无端的侵略，对越南人民犯下了惨无人道的罪行，他们应对这一切负责。全世界爱好和平的人民和国家的日益迫切的任务，是谴责美国侵略越南，表示抗议，并且尽一切可能支持越南人民的神圣斗争，来制止帝国主义侵略者的魔爪，迫使他们滚出越南去。”
    声明最后说，“支持英雄的越南人民的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府，强烈谴责作为美帝国主义者的战争新行动的檀香山会议，重申，阿尔巴尼亚人民坚定不移地支持兄弟的越南人民，并且坚信，英雄的越南人民的光荣斗争必将取得对美帝国主义侵略者的完全胜利。”
