Title: Indonesian Right-Wing Newspaper Comments Hail Castro's Anti-China Stance, Claiming "Ruthless Blows Against China" are a "Good Example of Maintaining Self-Respect"
Author:
Date: 1966-02-22
Edition: 5

　　Indonesian Right-Wing Newspaper Comments
    For Castro's anti-China cheering, think "relentless blow to China" is "a good example in maintaining self-esteem"
    《建国五项原则之火报》’s short commentary on February 7th stated: Relations with China are now being considered. May I ask, if it has already been considered, why still consider or continue to consider it? Hurry up! As the saying goes: Time waits for no one, do you know that? Time is precious.
    Fidel Castro of Cuba ruthlessly strikes at China. This is a good example of maintaining self-respect.
    《印度尼西亚先驱报》published an editorial on February 9th stating: We have our differences with Prime Minister Castro, especially since the September 30th Movement happened... This is a man of courage. He bravely resisted the United States and survived the most difficult intimidation. Today, he bravely resists another major power – People's China. In what is so far the most violent attack by any country on Beijing, Castro called China "hypocritical, false, double-dealing, poisonous, etc."
    No matter what the reason for this attack is – we do not claim to know the whole situation – we know one thing! Fidel is a man who knows how to defend the honor of his government and country!

### 印度尼西亚右派报纸的评论  为卡斯特罗反华叫好，认为“毫不留情地打击中国”是“在维护自尊心方面的一个良好范例”

1966-02-22
第5版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚右派报纸的评论
    为卡斯特罗反华叫好，认为“毫不留情地打击中国”是“在维护自尊心方面的一个良好范例”
    《建国五项原则之火报》二月七日的短评说：现在正在考虑同中国的关系。请问，如果已经考虑了，怎么还要考虑或者继续考虑呢？快点吧！俗话说：时间不等人，你知道吗？时间宝贵。
    古巴的菲德尔·卡斯特罗毫不留情地打击中国。这是在维护自尊心方面的一个良好范例。
    《印度尼西亚先驱报》二月九日发表社论说：我们与卡斯特罗总理有我们的分歧，特别是自从九月三十日运动发生以来……这是一个有胆量的人。他勇敢地抗拒了美国、并在最困难的恫吓下生存下来。今天，他勇敢地抗拒了另一个大国——人民中国，在迄今为止是任何国家对北京进行的一次最猛烈的攻击中，卡斯特罗称中国“伪善，虚假，口是心非，毒物，等等。”
    不管进行这次攻击的原因是什么——我们并不自称知道整个情况——我们知道一点！菲德尔是一个知道保卫他的政府和国家的荣誉的人！
