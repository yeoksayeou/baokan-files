Title: Presidium of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions Instructs All Trade Unions to Subscribe to Bonds
Author:
Date: 1949-05-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 5th. Moscow Dispatch: The Presidium of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions of the Soviet Union issued a decision instructing all trade union organizations to actively participate in the Soviet people's movement to subscribe to the fourth post-war national bonds. The Presidium of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions also called on all male and female workers, staff, engineers, technicians, scientific and artistic workers in industry, transportation, and agriculture, and all working people across the country to subscribe to bonds equivalent to three to four weeks' income per person.

### 苏职工会中委会主席团指示各职工会认购公债

1949-05-06
第3版()
专栏：

    苏职工会中委会主席团
    指示各职工会认购公债
    【新华社北平五日电】莫斯科讯：苏联职工会中央委员会主席团发布决定，指示一切职工会组织积极参加苏联人民认购战后第四次公债的运动。苏联职工会中央委员会主席团并号召工业、运输业及农业中的一切男女工人、职员、工程师、技师、科学与艺术工作者和全国一切劳动人民一起，以相当于每人三、四个星期的收入认购公债。
