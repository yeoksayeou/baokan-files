Title: Nanjing Municipal Trading Company Established
Author:
Date: 1949-05-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 5th—The Nanjing Municipal Trading Company, acting on the order of the Military Control Commission, was established on May 2nd. The company will establish several branch companies, with the first and second branch companies being established simultaneously with the general company. In order to solve the living difficulties of workers, students, and the laboring people, as well as the poor citizens, the first branch company has transported a batch of rice and flour and started selling it.

### 南京市贸易总公司成立

1949-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    南京市贸易总公司成立
    【新华社南京五日电】南京市贸易总公司奉军管会命令已于五月二日成立。该公司将设若干分公司，第一、二两分公司已与总公司同时成立。为了解决工人、学生及劳动人民和贫苦市民的生活困难，第一分公司已运到一批大米、白面、开始出售。
