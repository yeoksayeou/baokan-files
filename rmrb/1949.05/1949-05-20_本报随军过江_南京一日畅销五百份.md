Title: Our Newspaper Follows the Army Across the Yangtze River: 500 Copies Sold Quickly in Nanjing in One Day
Author:
Date: 1949-05-20
Page: 1
　　Our Newspaper Follows the Army Across the Yangtze River
  　500 Copies Sold Quickly in Nanjing in One Day
    [News from this newspaper] With the rapid development of our army's victory, the liberated areas have become connected as one. Postal and telecommunications are interconnected. People in the liberated areas of Northeast, North China, and Northwest China have subscribed to this newspaper one after another. Currently, in addition to thousands of copies already subscribed in major cities such as Shenyang, Changchun, Harbin, Jinan, and Xuzhou, after the liberation of Nanjing, this newspaper has also followed the vehicles across the Yangtze River. According to the first day's telegram, sales reached 500 copies.

### 本报随军过江　南京一日畅销五百份

1949-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　本报随军过江
  　南京一日畅销五百份
    【本报讯】随着我军胜利的迅速发展，解放区已连成一片，互通邮电，东北、华北、西北等解放区人民纷纷订阅本报，现除沈阳、长春、哈尔滨、济南、徐州等大城市已订有数千份外，南京解放后，本报也已随车过江，仅第一日电悉，即畅销达五百份。
