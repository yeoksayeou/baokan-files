Title: Shanghai-Hangzhou-Suzhou Triangle Area, Tianjin Restores Postal Service with Jiangxi, Hunan, and Zhejiang
Author:
Date: 1949-05-26
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Wuxi, 25th: Postal and telecommunications traffic is gradually resuming in the Shanghai-Hangzhou-Suzhou triangle area. The Songjiang Post Office has begun to restore postal service with Hangzhou, Wuxi, Suzhou, Jinshanwei, and the areas west of Shanghai, including Sijing and Xinzhuang. Long-distance telephone service is now available from Songjiang to Qingpu, Jinshan, Jiashan, and other places.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 25th: Tianjin has restored postal service with Nanchang and Jiujiang starting from the 24th. Postal delivery to newly liberated towns in Jiangxi, Hunan, and Zhejiang provinces has also been restored simultaneously.

### 沪杭苏三角地带　天津与赣湘浙　均恢复通邮

1949-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪杭苏三角地带
  　天津与赣湘浙
  　均恢复通邮
    【新华社无锡二十五日电】上海、杭州、苏州三角地带周围的邮电交通正逐渐恢复中。松江邮局已开始恢复与杭州、无锡、苏州、金山卫以及上海市西之泗泾、莘庄等地通邮。长途电话已可由松江通至青浦、金山、嘉善等地。
    【新华社天津二十五日电】天津市已自二十四日起与南昌、九江通邮，与赣、湘、浙三省各新解放城镇之邮递亦同时恢复。
