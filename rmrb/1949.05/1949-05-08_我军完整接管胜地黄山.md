Title: Our Army Completely Takes Over Scenic Huangshan

Author:
Date: 1949-05-08
Page: 1

    Our Army Completely Takes Over Scenic Huangshan
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] The People's Liberation Army has completely taken over Huangshan, a scenic spot in southern Anhui. After the People's Liberation Army crossed the Yangtze River, local residents, knowing the Liberation Army's policy of protecting scenic spots and historical sites, took special care to protect it from damage. After the Liberation Army entered the area on April 21, factories and shops continued to operate as usual, and schools did not suspend classes.

### 我军完整接管胜地黄山

1949-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我军完整接管胜地黄山
    【北平新华广播电台广播】人民解放军完整地接管了皖南风景名胜区黄山，人民解放军渡江以后，当地居民知道解放军保护名胜古迹的政策，因此特地加以保护，使它不受破坏，四月二十一号，解放军进驻该地以后，工厂商店照常营业，学校没有停课。
