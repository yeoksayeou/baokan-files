Title: Various Places in Bulgaria Hold "Pre-Election Meetings" to Prepare for Elections of Local People's Assemblies
Author:
Date: 1949-05-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 15th: TASS, Sofia: Preparations for the elections of local people's assemblies and people's assessors in Bulgaria have been completed. The last pre-election meetings of the working people were held on the afternoon of the 13th. The elections will begin at 7:00 AM on the 15th. At many pre-election meetings, the working people unanimously supported the Fatherland Front's election platform, demonstrating their firm determination to fulfill the Five-Year Plan for developing the national economy and their unwavering will to defend the cause of peace and international cooperation together with the great Soviet Union and the people's democratic countries.

### 保各地举行“选前会议”　筹选地方人民议会

1949-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　保各地举行“选前会议”
  　筹选地方人民议会
    【新华社北平十五日电】塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚地方人民议会与人民陪审员选举的准备工作已告完成。劳动人民的最后一次选前会议于十三日下午举行。选举将在十五日上午七时开始。在许多选前会议上，劳动人民一致拥护祖国阵线的竞选纲领，表明他们坚决履行发展国民经济的五年计划和毫不畏缩的与伟大的苏联和人民民主国家共同保卫和平与国际合作事业的意志。
