Title: Public Factories in Northeast Compete in Production; Match and Paper Factories Exceed Plans
Author:
Date: 1949-05-13
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Beiping Dispatch: According to news from Shenyang on the 12th, since the public factories in cities such as Qiqihar, Andong, Changchun, and Bei'an launched a production competition in early April, workers have demonstrated a high degree of enthusiasm, completing their original plans ahead of schedule, with some even exceeding their targets. The Nenjiang Match Factory in Qiqihar City originally planned to produce 750 boxes of matches in April, but in the first thirteen days alone, it produced 427 boxes, exceeding the original half-month target by 14 percent. Workers at the Andong Paper Mill renovated the fastest paper-making machine in the entire Northeast. Although it is only running at half speed, it is already producing six tons of paper per day. The Changchun Electric Power Bureau's power plant completed and exceeded its monthly production plan five days ahead of schedule. The fourteen public factories in Bei'an have also launched a surge in production competition. Among them, the leather factory originally planned to make 1,080 pieces of leather in April, but completed 1,629 pieces in 25 days.

### 东北各地公营工厂竞赛生产  火柴造纸等厂超过计划

1949-05-13
第3版()
专栏：

    东北各地公营工厂竞赛生产
    火柴造纸等厂超过计划
    【北平新华广播电台讯】沈阳十二号消息：齐齐哈尔、安东、长春、北安等城市的公营工厂，从四月初展开生产竞赛以来，由于工友们发挥了高度的积极性，提前完成了原定计划，有的还超过了计划。齐齐哈尔市嫩江火柴工厂，四月份原来计划出产火柴七百五十箱，但在最初的十三天内就出产四百二十七箱，多超过半个月原定产量的百分之十四。安东造纸厂职工装修好了全东北生产率最快的一部造纸机。现在虽然只有一半速度，每天就出纸六吨。长春电业局发电厂，提前五天完成并且超过了一个月的生产计划。北安十四个公营工厂，也掀起了生产竞赛的热潮。其中皮革厂原来计划四月份作一千零八十张皮子，结果在二十五天内就完成了一千六百二十九张。
