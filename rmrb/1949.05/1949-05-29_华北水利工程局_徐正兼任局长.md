Title: North China Water Conservancy Engineering Bureau: Xu Zheng Concurrently Appointed as Director

Author:

Date: 1949-05-29
Page: 1

　　North China Water Conservancy Engineering Bureau
  　Xu Zheng Concurrently Appointed as Director
    [This Newspaper's Report] The former North China Water Conservancy Engineering Bureau, previously taken over by the Tianjin Military Control Commission, has now been transferred to the North China People's Government for management and renamed the North China Water Conservancy Engineering Bureau, directly under the leadership of the North China Water Conservancy Commission. Xu Zheng, Deputy Director of the North China Water Conservancy Commission, concurrently serves as the Director of the Bureau; Cheng Run, a member of the North China Water Conservancy Commission, concurrently serves as the Deputy Director.

### 华北水利工程局　徐正兼任局长

1949-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北水利工程局
  　徐正兼任局长
    【本报讯】前由天津市军管会接收的原华北水利工程总局，现已移交华北人民政府接管，改名为华北水利工程局，直属华北水利委员会领导。该局局长由华北水利委员会副主任徐正兼；副局长由华北水利委员会委员成润兼。
