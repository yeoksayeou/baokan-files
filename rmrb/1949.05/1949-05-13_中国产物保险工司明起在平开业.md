Title: China Property Insurance Company to Open in Beiping Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-05-13
Page: 2

【This Newspaper's Report】The Beiping China Property Insurance Co., Ltd., under the leadership of the Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China, has completed its preparations and will officially commence operations on the 14th at the China Bank building, 37 West Jiaomin Lane. The company's business area includes Beiping, Zhangjiakou, and Shijiazhuang, and it is currently handling fire insurance, transportation insurance, and other insurance businesses. (Yu)

### 中国产物保险工司明起在平开业

1949-05-13
第2版()
专栏：

    中国产物保险工司明起在平开业
    【本报讯】北平中国产物保险股份有限公司，在中国人民银行北平分行领导下筹备就绪，定于十四日在西交民巷三十七号中国银行行址内正式开始营业。该公司业务区域有北平、张家口、石家庄等地，目前暂办理火险、运输险等项保险业务。          （宇）
