Title: Jubilation in Yan'an over Rainfall: Celebrating the Liberation of Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1949-05-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, May 24th: People from all walks of life in Yan'an are enthusiastically celebrating the liberation of Xi'an. When the good news arrived on the 20th, Yan'an was blessed with timely rain, which the people called a double blessing. Amidst cheers of victory, farmers in the suburbs rushed to plant crops while the rain lasted. Students from the Northwest Party School of the CCP and Yan'an University immediately launched a campaign to donate comfort items and letters to the soldiers on the front lines. The Yan'an garrison immediately held a celebration rally. Merchants busily prepared to go south to procure goods. At night, the new market was brightly lit. The Limin Woolen Mill, Yan'an University, the Northwest Party School of the CCP, the Yan'an Elementary School, and citizens held a torchlight parade.

### 延安喜雨中　欢庆西安解放

1949-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　延安喜雨中
  　欢庆西安解放
    【新华社延安二十四日电】延安各界人民热烈庆祝西安解放。二十日捷报传来时，延安正获时雨，人民称为双喜临门。市郊农民在祝捷声中，纷纷趁雨抢种。中共西北党校和延安大学学生立即发起捐献慰劳品和慰劳信给前线将士。延安驻军当即举行庆祝大会。商人们则纷纷准备南下办货。入夜，新市场灯火辉煌。利民毛织厂、延安大学、中共西北党校、延市完小和市民并举行火炬游行。
