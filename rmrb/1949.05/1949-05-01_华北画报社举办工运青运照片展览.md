Title: Hua Bei Pictorial Society Holds Exhibition of Photos on Labor and Youth Movements
Author:
Date: 1949-05-01
Page: 2

    Hua Bei Pictorial Society Holds
    Exhibition of Photos on Labor and Youth Movements
    [Ping City News] To commemorate "May Day" and "May Fourth," the Hua Bei Pictorial Society of the Political Department of the Hua Bei Military Region is holding a photo exhibition on industrial construction, the workers' movement, and the youth movement in the liberated areas at the Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park. It is scheduled to begin on May 1st and close on May 10th. At the same time, the Gulou People's Education Center is also holding a photo exhibition of industrial workers in the liberated areas. Today, on May Day, all factories and schools visiting in groups with proof letters will be admitted to the park free of charge. From May 2nd to May 10th, half-price discounts will be available. (Hua Bei Pictorial Society Exhibition Group)

### 华北画报社举办工运青运照片展览

1949-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    华北画报社举办
    工运青运照片展览
    【平市讯】华北军区政治部华北画报社，为纪念“五一”、“五四”，在中山公园中山堂举行解放区工业建设、工人运动及青年运动的照片展览。订于五月一日起开始，五月十日闭幕。与此同时，鼓楼民教馆亦举行解放区工业工人照片展览。今天五一，凡工厂、学校集体参观，持有证明信件者，一律免票入园。五月二日至十日间可享半价优待。      （华北画报社展览组）
