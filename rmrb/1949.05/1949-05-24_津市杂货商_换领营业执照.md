Title: Tianjin Grocery Merchants Exchange Business Licenses
Author:
Date: 1949-05-24
Page: 2

　　Tianjin Grocery Merchants
  　Exchange Business Licenses
    [Our Dispatch] The Tianjin Municipal Government's Bureau of Industry and Commerce convened a meeting of all grocery merchants in the city at the Shangping An Theater in the Southern District at 9:00 AM on the 22nd to urge all merchants to exchange for new business licenses. It was announced that the application procedures would be handled uniformly by the Grocery Industry Association, which would collect the applications and submit them to the Bureau of Industry and Commerce. Application forms were distributed, and the deadline for completion was set at ten days. The industry association has already begun handling the application matters for the businesses in the industry.

### 津市杂货商　换领营业执照

1949-05-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　津市杂货商
  　换领营业执照
    【本报讯】津市府工商局，于二十二日上午九时，假南市上平安戏院，召集全市杂货商开会，促使各商换领新营业执照。宣布申请手续由杂货业公会统一办理，汇集后转呈工商局。并发下申请表格，限十日内办理完竣。该业公会已开始办理该业商号申请事宜。
