Title: Taihang Industrial Company Sells Handicraft Machinery at Low Prices
Author:
Date: 1949-05-09
Page: 2

    Taihang Industrial Company
    Sells Handicraft Machinery at Low Prices
    [Taihang Electric] To help develop handicraft production, Taihang Industrial Company is selling spinning machines, weaving machines, wool spinning machines, sock knitting machines, manual cotton carding machines, cotton gins, sewing machines, bellows, fire tongs, wood saw blades, and pot stoves at low prices. It is also stipulated that handicraft cooperatives holding certificates from county and district governments can purchase at 5% below the selling price. Sales locations are: the company in Yangyi, Wu'an; the Third Warehouse in Changzhi; the Fifth Warehouse in Guantai, Anyang; the First Warehouse in Xingtai; and the Second Warehouse in Zuoquan.

### 太行实业公司  廉价出售手工业机器

1949-05-09
第2版()
专栏：

    太行实业公司
    廉价出售手工业机器
    【太行电】太行实业公司为帮助手工业生产发展，廉价出售纺纱机、织布机、纺毛机、织袜机、人力弹花机、轧花机、缝纫机、风弓、火钳、木锯条、淋炉等。并规定凡合作的手工业持有县区政府证明者，可低于售价百分之五。售货地址为武安阳邑本公司，长治市第三仓库，安阳观台第五仓库，邢台市第一仓库，左权第二仓库。
