Title: Chinese Students in Japan Commemorate the May Fourth Movement
Author:
Date: 1949-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th. Tokyo dispatch: Under the auspices of the Chinese Students Association in Japan, approximately 500 Chinese students in Japan and Japanese students held a commemorative meeting for the 30th anniversary of the "May Fourth" Movement at Waseda University on the afternoon of the 4th. Japanese Communist Party Senator Nakanishi Ko was one of the main speakers at the conference. At the end of the commemoration, Chinese students performed the Yangko dance.

### 中国留日学生纪念“五四”

1949-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中国留日学生纪念“五四”
    【新华社北平七日电】东京消息：在中国留日同学会主持下，中国留日学生及日本学生约五百人于四日下午在早稻田大学举行“五四”运动三十周年纪念大会。日共参议员中西功是大会的主要讲演人之一。纪念会结束时中国学生表演了秧歌舞。
