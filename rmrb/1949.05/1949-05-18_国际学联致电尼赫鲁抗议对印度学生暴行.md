Title: International Union of Students Cables Nehru Protesting Atrocities Against Indian Students
Author:
Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 14th: Prague dispatch: The International Union of Students, representing its three million members, cabled Indian Prime Minister Nehru on the 11th, protesting the recent atrocities committed by Indian authorities against students of Indian medical schools. The medical school students were attacked by police, resulting in deaths, injuries, and arrests, for protesting the Indian authorities' massacre of demonstrating women in Calcutta.

### 国际学联致电尼赫鲁抗议对印度学生暴行

1949-05-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际学联致电尼赫鲁抗议对印度学生暴行
    【新华社北平十四日电】布拉格讯：国际学联代表其三百万会员，于十一日致电印度总理尼赫鲁，抗议印当局最近对印度医科学校学生的暴行。医科学校学生因抗议印当局屠杀加尔各答示威妇女而遭到警察的杀伤和逮捕。
