Title: North China Trade General Corporation Helu Transfer Station Vigorously Organizes Urban and Rural Material Exchange
Author:
Date: 1949-05-29
Page: 1

[Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Tianjin, 28th dispatch: The North China Trade General Corporation's Helu Transfer Station is vigorously organizing urban and rural material exchange. From the end of February to now, over 1,200 boats of agricultural products have been transported to Tianjin via inland rivers from areas such as Central Hebei, Southern Hebei, and Hebei-Shandong-Henan. This included over 56 million jin of coarse and refined grains, over 17 million jin of cotton, and large quantities of white hemp, tobacco leaves, cottonseed, and mountain goods. More than 1,100 boats of industrial products were shipped from Tianjin to various places, including 7,700 pieces of cotton yarn, 174,000 bolts of cloth, 72 million jin of edible salt, 1.4 million jin of coal, and large quantities of alkali flour, kerosene, etc. Due to the development of urban-rural relations and the assistance of the People's Government, the inland river shipping industry has gradually prospered, and the lives of boatmen have also been somewhat guaranteed.

### 华北贸总河路转运栈　大力组织城乡物资交流

1949-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北贸总河路转运栈
  　大力组织城乡物资交流
    【北平新华广播电台广播】天津二十八号消息：华北贸易总公司河路转运栈，大力组织城乡物资交流，从二月底到现在，从冀中、冀南、冀鲁豫等区经过内河运到天津的农产品有一千二百多船，运到粗细粮五千六百多万斤、棉花一千七百多万斤和大批白麻、烟叶、棉子、山货等；从天津运往各地的工业品，有一千一百多船，运出棉纱七千七百件、布十七万四千匹、食盐七千二百万斤、煤一百四十万斤和大批硷面煤油等；由于城乡关系的开展和人民政府的扶助，内河船运业已经逐渐繁荣起来，船工生活也获得了一定保障。
