Title: Sixty Gold and Silver Jewelry Businesses in Beiping Approved for Preferential Price Exchange of Gold and Silver
Author:
Date: 1949-05-25
Page: 2

　　Sixty Gold and Silver Jewelry Businesses in Beiping
　　Approved for Preferential Price Exchange of Gold and Silver
　　[This Newspaper] Since the 20th, when gold and silver jewelry businesses in Beiping began submitting applications to the Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China for permission to exchange their original gold and silver holdings at preferential prices, sixty businesses have been approved in the past four days to exchange 299 taels, 8 mace, 9 fen, and 2 li of gold, 69,911 taels, 2 mace, and 6 fen of silver, and 121 silver dollars.
　　[Another News Item] Yesterday (the 24th), the following businesses were also approved by the Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China to exchange at preferential prices: Sanyang, Guangyu, Baohengxiang, Tianyixing, Tianbao, Tianbaoxiang, Baoyuan, Yuanchang, Yiju, Deyuan, Yufeng, Zhenglong, Zhengtai, Zhengda, Guanghua, Qialong, Baofeng, Honglinxiang, Xinji, and 19 others, approved to exchange a total of 124 taels of gold, 25,464 taels, 3 mace, and 1 fen of silver, and 70 silver dollars. (Yu)

### 平六十家金银饰物业　核准优价兑换金银

1949-05-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　平六十家金银饰物业
  　核准优价兑换金银
    【本报讯】平市金银饰物业自二十日开始向人民银行北平分行呈请准予按优待价格收兑原存金银后，四日来已有六十家经核准兑换黄金二百九十九两八钱九分二厘，白银六万九千九百十一两二钱六分，银元一百二十一枚。
    【又讯】该业昨日（二十四日）经人民银行北平分行核准按优待价格兑换者又有：三阳、广裕、宝恒祥、天义兴、天宝、天宝祥、宝源、源昌、益聚、德源、裕丰、正隆、正泰、正大、广华、洽隆、宝丰、宏林祥、新记等十九家，准兑黄金共一百二十四两，白银二万五千四百六十四两三钱一分，银元七十枚。（宇）
