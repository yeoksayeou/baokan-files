Title: Peiping Workers' Salaries: Millet Priced at Fifty-Nine Yuan
Author:
Date: 1949-05-16
Page: 2

　　Peiping Workers' Salaries: Millet Priced at Fifty-Nine Yuan
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Peiping Municipal General Labor Union, the Trade Corporation, and the Military Control Commission's Supply Department jointly decided that for the first half of this month, workers' salaries in various agencies and enterprises in Peiping Municipality will be paid with millet priced at 59 yuan per jin in People's currency. Salaries will be paid in the middle and at the end of each month.

### 平职工薪资米折价五十九元

1949-05-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　平职工薪资米折价五十九元
    【本报讯】平市各机关、企业部门本月上半月职工薪资，经市总工会、贸易公司、及军管会供给部等部门会同决定，小米每斤折价按人民券五十九元发给。每月发薪时间为月中及月底。
