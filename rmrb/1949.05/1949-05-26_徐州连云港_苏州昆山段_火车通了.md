Title: Xuzhou-Lianyungang, Suzhou-Kunshan Sections of Railway Open to Traffic

Author:
Date: 1949-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xuzhou, May 25th: The Xuzhou to Lianyungang section of the Longhai Railway's eastern line was opened to traffic on the 17th.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuxi, May 25th: The Suzhou-Kunshan section of the Beijing-Shanghai Railway was repaired and opened to traffic on the 18th. The Beijing-Shanghai Railway now runs from Nanjing to Kunshan.

### 徐州连云港　苏州昆山段　火车通了

1949-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　徐州连云港
  　苏州昆山段
  　火车通了
    【新华社徐州二十五日电】陇海路东线徐州至连云港段已于十七日通车。
    【新华社无锡二十五日电】京沪路苏州＠山段已于十八日修复通车。现京沪路已由南京通达＠山。
