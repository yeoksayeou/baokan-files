Title: The Soviet Union Issues Post-War Fourth National Bond to Absorb Private Capital and Develop the National Economy
Author:
Date: 1949-05-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th - The Soviet Council of Ministers has recently decided to issue the fourth post-war national bond, totaling 20 billion rubles. According to TASS, Moscow, 3rd: The new bond will absorb private capital to facilitate the implementation of the state plan for the restoration and development of the national economy in the fourth year of the post-war five-year plan, and to complete the entire five-year plan ahead of schedule. The Council of Ministers decided: The principal repayment period for the bond is set at twenty years. The bond and its proceeds, including bonuses, are exempt from tax obligations.

### 苏联发行战后第四次公债  吸收民间资金发展国民经济

1949-05-05
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联发行战后第四次公债
    吸收民间资金发展国民经济
    【新华社北平四日电】苏联部长会议顷决定发行总额达二百亿卢布的战后第四次公债。据塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：新公债将吸收民间资金以利于在战后五年计划第四年内实行恢复与发展国民经济的国家计划，并提前完成整个五年计划。部长会议决定：公债还本期定为二十年。公债及其收益部分，包括奖金在内，得豁免纳税义务。
