Title: Long-Distance Telephone Service Opens Between Tianjin and Taiyuan, Zhangjiakou
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-06
Page: 2

    Long-Distance Telephone Service Opens Between
    Tianjin and Taiyuan, Zhangjiakou
    [Tianjin, 5th dispatch] Long-distance telephone service between Tianjin and Taiyuan, Zhangjiakou will open today. Telegraph service between Tianjin and Jinan, Taiyuan, Baoding, Dezhou, Xuzhou, Bengbu, Nanjing, Zhenjiang, Wuhu, Wuxi, Jurong, Wujin, Suzhou, Changshu, Wujiang, Dangtu, and other places is also open.

### 津并张三市间长途电话开放

1949-05-06
第2版()
专栏：

    津并张三市间
    长途电话开放
    【天津五日电】津市与太原、张家口间长途电话，今起开放。津市与济南、太原、保定、德县、徐州、蚌埠、南京、镇江、芜湖、无锡、句容，武进、苏州、常熟，吴江、当涂等地电报亦均畅通。
