Title: National Youth Congress Scheduled to Open Today
Author: Jin Feng
Date: 1949-05-04
Page: 1

    National Youth Congress Scheduled to Open Today
    [Our Dispatch] The first National Congress of the All-China Youth, marking the great unity of democratic youth in China, is scheduled to officially open today in Beijing, China's ancient capital and the birthplace of the youth movement, on the occasion of the thirtieth anniversary of the May Fourth Movement and the first May Fourth commemoration since the liberation of Beiping.
    As of yesterday, there were 472 delegates attending the congress (including twelve alternates, from various liberated areas and areas under Kuomintang rule), including representatives of workers, peasants, students, and the People's Liberation Army. Participating groups included various associations of science, academia, literature, and journalism workers, as well as the China New Democratic Youth League, the Liberated Areas Youth Federation, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, the Young Men's and Women's Christian Associations, and others. In addition, there were representatives from minority nationalities, overseas Chinese, various democratic parties, and religious circles. They are all outstanding youth models and representatives of youth workers, such as Tian Guangwen, a combat hero of the White Tiger Company of the People's Liberation Army; Huang Xianghe, who received eight model worker awards and one hero medal; Liu Chunhua, a model trolley worker from Shenyang; Shi Zhenyu, a female militia hero representing the peasants; Ding Cong and Ye Qianyu, cartoonists representing the literary and art circles; Li Ji, author of "Wang Gui and Li Xiangxiang"; He Jingzhi, author of "The White-Haired Girl"; Zhao Shuli, author of "The Tales of Li Youcai"; Yan Jici, director of the Institute of Physics of the Beiping Research Institute representing the scientific community; Yuan Hanqing, professor at Peking University; Shen Zhiyuan and Qian Jiaju representing the academic community; and Wu Yaozong representing religious circles.
    The congress will hold a preparatory meeting this morning and an opening ceremony this afternoon.
            (Jin Feng)
    [Another Dispatch] Today is Youth Day. This morning, all cinemas in Beiping will provide free admission to youth from all walks of life. This afternoon, a Beiping municipal celebration of the May Fourth Youth Congress will be held. In the evening, 70,000 university and middle school students will hold simultaneous celebrations in five districts. The Beiping Library and the Peking University Library will exhibit May Fourth historical materials and materials on the history of the youth movement over the past thirty years.
          (Jin Feng)

### 全国青代会定今日开幕
金凤
1949-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    全国青代会定今日开幕
    【本报讯】在五四运动的三十周年和北平解放以后的第一次五四纪念的时候，标志中国民主青年大团结的中华全国青年第一次代表大会，定今日在中国的古都和青年运动的发祥地——北平正式开幕。
    出席大会的代表，截至昨日，已有四百七十二人（内候补十二人，包括各解放区与国民党统治区的），有工、农、学生与解放军的代表，参加的团体有科学、学术、文艺、新闻工作者各协会及中国新民主主义青年团、解放区青年联合会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国学生联合会、基督教男女青年会等；此外有少数民族、海外华侨、各民主党派和宗教方面的代表。他们都是优秀的青年模范和青年工作者的代表，如人民解放军代表白老虎连的战斗英雄田广文得到八个模范和一个英雄奖章的黄相和，工人代表沈阳模范电车工人刘春华，农民代表女民兵英雄侍振玉，文艺界代表漫画家丁聪、叶浅予、王贵与李香香的作者李季、白毛女作者贺敬之、李有才板话作者赵树理等，科学界代表——北平研究院物理研究所所长严济慈，北京大学教授袁翰青，学术界代表沈志远、千家驹，宗教界代表吴耀宗等。
    大会于今日上午开预备会，下午举行开幕式
            （金凤）
    【又讯】今日青年节，上午，全北平市所有电影院免费招待各界青年，下午举行北平市庆祝五四青年代表大会，晚上七万大中学生在五个分区同时举行庆祝晚会，北平图书馆和北京大学图书馆展览五四史料和卅年来青年运动史料。
          （金凤）
