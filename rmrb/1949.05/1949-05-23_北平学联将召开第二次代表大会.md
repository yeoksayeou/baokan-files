Title: Beiping Student Federation to Convene Second Representative Assembly
Author:
Date: 1949-05-23
Page: 2

【Ping City News】The Beiping Student Federation Executive Committee held its ninth meeting on the 22nd, summarizing the work of the three months since the conclusion of the First Representative Assembly, discussing the work plan before and during the summer vacation, and deciding to convene the first session of the Second Representative Assembly on June 4th. Representative registration dates are May 27th to May 31st.

### 北平学联将召开第二次代表大会

1949-05-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　北平学联将召开第二次代表大会
    【平市讯】北平学联执委会二十二日举行第九次会议，总结了第一次代表大会闭幕后三个月来的工作，讨论了暑假前及暑假期间之工作计划，决定在六月四日召开第一届第二次代表大会，代表报到日期为五月二十七日至三十一日。
