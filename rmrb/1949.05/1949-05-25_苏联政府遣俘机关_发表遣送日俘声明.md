Title: Soviet Government Repatriation Administration Issues Statement on Repatriation of Japanese Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1949-05-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, May 22nd: TASS, Moscow reports: The Soviet Government Repatriation Administration, under the Council of Ministers, recently issued a statement regarding the repatriation of Japanese prisoners of war, stating: Of the total number of 594,000 Japanese prisoners of war, 70,880 were directly released in the war zone in 1945. Between December 1, 1946, and May 1, 1949, 418,166 prisoners had been repatriated to Japan. Currently, only 95,000 prisoners of war remain in the Soviet Union, and with the exception of some individuals whose crimes require investigation, all will be repatriated. All repatriation costs incurred by the Soviet Union, in accordance with the agreement between the Soviet representative to the Allied Council for Japan and MacArthur Headquarters, must be reimbursed by the Japanese government.

### 苏联政府遣俘机关　发表遣送日俘声明

1949-05-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联政府遣俘机关
  　发表遣送日俘声明
    【新华社北平二十二日电】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议所辖遣俘机关顷就遣送日本战俘一事发表声明称：在总数五十九万四千名日本被俘官兵中，七万零八百八十名曾于一九四五年在战争地区直接予以释放。在一九四六年十二月一日至一九四九年五月一日期间，已有四十一万八千一百六十六名被遣回日本。目前滞留苏联的战俘只有九万五千名，其中除需要调查其罪行的某些人外，将全部予以遣送回国。苏联所付的全部遣俘费用，按照苏联驻盟国对日委员会代表与麦克阿瑟总部的协定，必须由日本政府偿还。
