Title: Five Polish and Czech Coal Mines Sign Agreement for International Production Competition, Polish-Czech Cooperative Association to be Established on July 1st
Author:
Date: 1949-05-07
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, May 5th: Cooperation and friendship between Poland and Czechoslovakia are continuously growing. Workers in three Polish coal mines and two Czechoslovak coal mines are engaging in international production competitions to see who performs better in improving productivity, enhancing work safety, and industrial hygiene. Representatives from both sides have signed an agreement for this competition. Simultaneously, representatives of the Polish and Czechoslovak cooperative movements have decided to establish a Polish-Czech Cooperative Association from July 1st, which will include all cooperative stores. Furthermore, the Polish-Czech Social Affairs Committee held a meeting in Warsaw at the end of April to discuss cooperation between the two countries in social policy and management. It is reported that the number of exchanges of experts, vacationers, and children between the two countries has increased. More than a thousand outstanding Polish workers will vacation in Czechoslovakia, and vice versa. The meeting also determined the principles of cooperation between the Social Affairs Committee and the Economic Cooperation Committee.

### 波捷五个煤矿订约进行国际生产竞赛  两国合作社协会七月一日成立

1949-05-07
第3版()
专栏：

    波捷五个煤矿订约进行国际生产竞赛
    两国合作社协会七月一日成立
    【新华社北平五日电】华沙讯：波兰与捷克斯洛伐克之间的合作和友谊不断增涨着。波兰的三个煤场和捷克斯洛伐克的两个煤场内职工们，进行国际的生产竞赛，看谁在提高生产率、改善工作安全及工业卫生方面作得更好。双方的代表已签订这种竞赛协定。同时，波兰与捷克斯洛伐克合作社运动的代表业已决定从七月一日起成立一个波捷合作社协会，它将包括一切合作社的商店。此外，波捷社会事务委员会于四月底在华沙举行了会议，讨论两国在社会政策和管理方面的合作。据称：两国间专家的交换、休假人员和男童的互相往来，其数目已有增加。一千多个波兰优秀劳动者将到捷克消度假日，捷克方面也是这样。会议并确定了社会事务委员会与经济合作委员会间的合作原则。
