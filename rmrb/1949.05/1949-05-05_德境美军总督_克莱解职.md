Title: U.S. Military Governor of Germany Clay Relieved of Duty
Author:
Date: 1949-05-05
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th -- According to an Associated Press report: U.S. President Truman announced on the 3rd that he is relieving Lucius D. Clay, the U.S. Military Governor of Germany, of his duties, effective May 15th.
