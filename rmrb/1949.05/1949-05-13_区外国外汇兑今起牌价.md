Title: Exchange Rate for Foreign Currency Outside the District Announced Today
Author:
Date: 1949-05-13
Page: 2
The exchange rate for foreign currency outside the district is revised to 1 to 16,000 yuan starting today.

In addition, the exchange rate for US dollars is 1 to 870 yuan, Hong Kong dollars is 1 to 138 yuan, and British pounds is 1 to 2200 yuan.

### 区外国外汇兑今起牌价

1949-05-13
第2版()
专栏：

    区外国外汇兑今起牌价
    【本报讯】今起区外汇兑牌价改订为一比一六○○○元。
    又美钞一比八七○元、港币一比一三八元、英磅一比二二○○元。
