Title: French Women's Youth League Holds Congress to Fight for Peace and Oppose Aggression Against Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th: TASS, Paris: The Fifth Congress of the French Women's Youth League opened on the 13th in Montreuil, Seine Province. Slogans in the conference hall called on French young women to fight to improve their living conditions, oppose the Vietnam War, and strive for "the first condition of happiness—peace."

　　Mrs. Vinson, representing the National Committee of the French Women's Youth League, gave a report, emphasizing that French young women are suffering hardships because France is participating in the preparations for new wars and the war in Vietnam. Speaking of the heroic and happy Soviet youth, she said: "We do not want to participate directly or indirectly in a war against the glorious country—the Soviet Union."

### 争取和平反对侵越　法女青年联盟召开代表大会

1949-05-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　争取和平反对侵越
  　法女青年联盟召开代表大会
    【新华社北平十六日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：法国女青年联盟第五届代表大会十三日在赛纳省蒙特里尔开幕。大会会场中的标语号召法国女青年进行斗争，以改善生活条件，反对越南战争，争取“幸福的第一个条件—和平”。
    代表大会主席团文森代表法国女青年联盟全国委员会作报告，强调指出法国女青年经受着艰难困苦，是因为法国参加了新战争的准备以及越南的战争。在讲到英勇的与幸福的苏联青年时说：“我们不愿直接或间接参加反对光荣的国家——苏联的战争。”
