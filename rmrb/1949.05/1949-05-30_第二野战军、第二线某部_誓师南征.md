Title: Second Field Army, a Certain Unit of the Second Line, Vows to March South

Author:
Date: 1949-05-30
Page: 1

　　Second Field Army, a Certain Unit of the Second Line
  　Vows to March South
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 29th. A unit of the Second Field Army's second-line forces held a rally on the 14th of this month in a certain area of the Hebei-Shandong-Henan region to vow to march south. The morale of the entire army is extremely high. This unit was formed by peasants from the Hebei-Shandong-Henan region who joined the army and returning soldiers. Before this expedition, they had undergone two months of rectification and training. At the mobilization rally, local Party, government, and military leaders and representatives of the people encouraged the unit to advance bravely, join the front-line troops, and quickly liberate all of China. The rear will continue to train troops, vigorously support the front line, and do a good job in production and preferential treatment for military families. Representatives of the unit stated that they would respond to the care and hope of the leaders of the region and the elders of their hometowns with positive actions.

### 第二野战军、第二线某部　誓师南征

1949-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　第二野战军、第二线某部
  　誓师南征
    【新华社北平二十九日电】人民解放军第二野战军第二线部队一部，于本月十四日在冀鲁豫区某地誓师南征，全军士气极为高涨。该部为冀鲁豫区参军农民及归队战士所编成，此次出征前曾经过两个月的整训学习。在进军誓师大会上，当地党政军首长及人民代表均勉励该部奋勇前进，汇合前方部队，迅速解放全中国；后方决继续训练部队，大力支援前线并做好生产和优待军人家属工作。该部代表表示，决以积极行动回答本区首长和乡里父老的爱护与希望。
