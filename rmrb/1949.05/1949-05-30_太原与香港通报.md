Title: Taiyuan and Hong Kong Notices

Author: 
Date: 1949-05-30

　　Taiyuan and Hong Kong Notices
　　[Taiyuan Telegraph] The Taiyuan City Telegraph Bureau has begun to accept and send Chinese and foreign plaintext telegrams to Hong Kong.
