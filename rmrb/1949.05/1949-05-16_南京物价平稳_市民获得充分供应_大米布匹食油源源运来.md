Title: Nanjing Prices Stable, Citizens Receive Adequate Supply, Rice, Cloth, and Cooking Oil Arrive Continuously
Author:
Date: 1949-05-16
Page: 1

[Broadcast by the Xinhua Broadcasting Station in Beiping] Nanjing, 15th dispatch: In the half-month since Nanjing's liberation, citizens have had sufficient supplies of essential necessities. The Nanjing Municipal Trade Corporation has continuously transported rice and cooking oil from various regions to regulate market needs. On the 10th of this month, rice was priced at 3,900 yuan per shi (unit of dry measure), and on the 12th, it fell back to 3,400 yuan. The Trade Corporation is now distributing tens of thousands of catties of grain to meet the needs of citizens. Cloth from Wuxi and other places is also arriving continuously. Currently, various prices in the city remain stable.

### 南京物价平稳　市民获得充分供应　大米布匹食油源源运来

1949-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京物价平稳
  　市民获得充分供应
  　大米布匹食油源源运来
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京十五号消息：南京解放以后，半个月来市民主要生活资料获得充分供应。南京市贸易总公司从各地不断运来大米食油调节市场需要，本月十号大米每石三千九百元，十二号回跌到三千四百元，现在贸易公司正配售数万斤粮食以供市民需要。无锡等地的布匹也正源源运来，现在市内各种物价都保持平稳。
