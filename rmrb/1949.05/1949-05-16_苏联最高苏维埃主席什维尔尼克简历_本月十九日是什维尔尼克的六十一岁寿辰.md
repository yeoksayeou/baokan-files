Biography of Nikolai Mikhailovich Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
    --This month, the 19th is Shvernik's 61st birthday
    Nikolai Mikhailovich Shvernik is an alternate member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. He was born on May 19, 1888, in a working-class family in St. Petersburg. He lived in an orphanage for six years. In 1902, he began learning to be a metal lathe operator.
    He joined the Bolshevik Party in 1905; he presided over underground activities in St. Petersburg, Nikolaev, Tula, Samara, and other towns and was repeatedly arrested.
    After the February Revolution in 1917, he was elected chairman of the factory committee of the Samara Pipe Factory. During the October Revolution of 1917, he served as chairman of the All-Russian Military Supply Workers Committee. Shortly after the October Revolution, he returned to Samara and was elected chairman of the local city Soviet. During the Civil War, he participated in the Eastern Front, first in a guerrilla unit and later as a political commissar of the 2nd Simbirsk Regiment. In 1919, he participated in the campaign against Denikin.
    In 1921, he was elected chairman of the Donetsk Regional Committee of the Metalworkers Union. At the 12th Party Congress, he was elected a member of the Central Control Commission of the Party. From 1923 to 1925, he served as a member of the Presidium of the Central Control Commission of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) and concurrently as People's Commissar of the Workers' and Peasants' Inspectorate of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic.
    In 1925, he was elected secretary of the Leningrad Regional Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks). At the 14th Party Congress, he was elected a member of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks). In 1926, he served as secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks). In 1927 and 1928, he served as secretary of the Ural Regional Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks). At the beginning of 1929, he was elected chairman of the Central Committee of the Metalworkers Union; in 1930, he was elected first secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, a position he held until March 15, 1944. At the 16th Party Congress, he was elected a member of the Organization Bureau of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks).
    Shvernik represents the Sverdlovsk constituency in the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. From January 1938 to March 1946, he served as chairman of the Chamber of Nationalities. In 1938, he was awarded the Order of Lenin in commemoration of his 50th birthday and his contributions to the working class. After the 18th Congress of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks), at the plenary session of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) held on March 22, 1939, he was elected an alternate member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks). On February 1, 1944, he was elected First Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR by the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.
    On March 4, 1944, he was elected Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic by the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic.
    On January 24, 1946, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR awarded Shvernik the Order of Lenin in commemoration of his victorious fulfillment of the tasks assigned by the government in supplying labor to industry, new construction, and the transportation system during the Great Patriotic War. On March 19, 1946, he was elected Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.
  (Translated by Jiang from "Soviet Calendar (1917-1947)")

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席什维尔尼克简历——本月十九日是什维尔尼克的六十一岁寿辰

1949-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃主席什维尔尼克简历
    ——本月十九日是什维尔尼克的六十一岁寿辰
    尼古拉·米海洛维奇·什维尔尼克，系联共（布）党中央委员会政治局候补委员，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席。于一八八八年五月十九日生于圣彼得堡的一个工人家庭中。曾住孤儿院六年。一九○二年，学做金属旋盘工。
    一九○五年加入布尔什维克党；曾在圣彼得堡、尼古拉耶夫、图拉、萨马拉及其他城镇主持地下活动，一再被捕。
    一九一七年二月革命后，当选为萨马拉烟斗厂工厂委员会主席。一九一七年十月革命期间，任全俄军需工人委员会主席。十月革命后不久即回萨马拉，当选为该地市苏维埃主席。内战期间，曾参加东线作战，初在一游击队中，后任辛比斯克第二团政委。一九一九年，曾参加征讨邓尼金之战。
    一九二一年，当选为金属工人工会顿涅茨区委员会主席。在第十二次党代表大会上当选为党中央监察委员会委员。自一九二三年至一九二五年，任联共（布）党中央监察委员会主席团委员，并兼任苏俄工农检察局人民委员。
    一九二五年，当选为联共（布）党列宁格勒区委员会书记。在第十四次党代表大会上当选为联共（布）党中央委员会委员。一九二六年，任联共（布）党中央委员会书记。一九二七年及一九二八年，任联共（布）党乌拉尔区委员会书记。一九二九年初，当选为金属工人工会中央委员会主席；一九三○年，当选为全苏职工会中央委员会第一书记，连任该职至一九四四年三月十五日。在第十六次党代表大会上当选为联共（布）党中央委员会组织局委员。
    什维尔尼克在苏联最高苏维埃中系代表斯维尔德洛夫斯克选区。自一九三八年正月至一九四六年三月，任民族院主席。一九三八年，荣获列宁勋章，纪念其五十寿辰及对工人阶级之功勋。联共（布）党第十八次代表大会举行后，于一九三九年三月二十二日所召开之联共（布）党中央委员会全会上，当选为联共（布）党中央委员会政治局候补委员。一九四四年二月一日，经苏联最高苏维埃选举为苏联最高苏维埃主席团第一副主席。
    一九四四年三月四日，经苏俄最高苏维埃选举为苏俄最高苏维埃主席团主席。
    一九四六年一月二十四日，苏联最高苏维埃主席团授予什维尔尼克以列宁勋章，纪念其在伟大卫国战争时期，在对工业、新建筑业及运输系统供应劳动力方面胜利完成政府所授予之任务。一九四六年三月十九日，当选为苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席。
  （江译自“苏联日历（一九一七—一九四七）”）
