Title: Commercial Telegrams Between Beiping and Shijiazhuang Now Available
Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 1

　　Beiping and Shijiazhuang
  　Commercial Telegrams Now Available
    [This News Agency] The Beiping Telecommunications Bureau has begun receiving and sending commercial telegrams to Shijiazhuang starting on the 27th.
    [Further News] Mail from Beiping to Shanghai, which was originally delivered via Hong Kong, is now being delivered via the Jinpu Railway. As for parcels to Shanghai, acceptance of them will also be resumed soon.

### 北平石庄间　商电畅通

1949-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平石庄间
  　商电畅通
    【本报讯】北平电信局自二十七日起收发发往石家庄商报。
    【又讯】北平寄往上海的邮件，原系取道香港运递，现都改由津浦路运递。至于寄上海的包裹，不久亦可恢复收寄。
