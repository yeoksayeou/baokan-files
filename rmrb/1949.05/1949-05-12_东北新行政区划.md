Title: New Administrative Divisions of Northeast China
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 3

Data:

    New Administrative Divisions of Northeast China: The Northeast Administrative Committee redivided the administrative divisions of Northeast China on the 21st of last month as follows:

    I. Liaodong Province

    Liaodong Province governs five municipalities and twenty-nine counties. The five municipalities are: Andong City, Yingkou City, Tonghua City, Liaoyang City, and Xi'an City. The twenty-nine counties are: Zhuanghe, Xinjin, Fuxian, Gaiping, Yingkou, Liaoyang, Haicheng, Xiuyan, Gushan, Kuandian, Fushun, Andong, Fengcheng, Huanren, Benxi, Xi'an, Hailong, Dongfeng, Shuofeng, Qingyuan, Xinbin (formerly Xingjing), Tonghua, Ji'an, Linjiang, Changbai, Fusong, Jingyu (merger of the former @jiang and Jinchuan counties, named in memory of the anti-Japanese hero Yang Jingyu), Huinan, and Liuhe. The provincial capital is located in Andong City.

    II. Liaoxi Province

    Liaoxi Province governs four municipalities and twenty-one counties. The four municipalities are: Jinzhou City, Siping City, Fuxin City, and Shanhaiguan City. The twenty-one counties are: Jin County, Jinxi, Suizhong, Xingcheng, Panshan, Tai'an, Liaozhong, Yixian, Xinmin, Zhangwu, Fuxin, Beizhen, Heishan, Changtu, Lishu, Faku, Kangping, Shuangliao (formerly Shuangshan and Liaoyuan combined), Kaiyuan, Tieling, and Changbei (newly established county, carved out of Changtu County). The provincial capital is located in Jinzhou City.

    III. Jilin Province

    Jilin Province governs two municipalities, twenty-two counties, and one banner. The two municipalities are: Changchun City and Jilin City. The twenty-two counties are: Yongji, Yushu, Jiutai, Dehui, Changchun, Shulan, Panshi, Jiaohe, Shuangyang, Yitong, Huadian, Dunhua, Nong'an, Qian'an, Fuyu, Huaide, Changling, Yanji, Helong, Wangqing, Hunchun, and Antu. The one banner is the Guoqian Banner. The provincial capital is located in Jilin City.

    IV. Heilongjiang Province

    Heilongjiang Province governs one municipality, forty counties, and two banners. The one municipality is: Qiqihar City. The forty counties are: Zhaodong, Zhaozhou, Nehe, Longjiang, Tao'an, Anda, Da'lai, Zhenlai (newly established county, formed from Zhendong County and the northern part of Da'lai), Tailai, Suihua, Hailun, Wangkui, Baiquan, Tai'an, Keshan, Qinggang, Lanxi, Qing'an, Kedong, Mingshui, Taonan, Lindian, Fuyu (including the Ike Ming'an Banner), Nenjiang, Gannan, Jingxing, Zhanyu, Kaitong, Anguang, Bei'an, Tongbei, Suileng, Dedu, Tie, Sunwu, Aihui, Shenma, Xunke (formerly Xunhe and Qike combined), Wuyun, and Foshan. The two banners are: Durbert Banner and Guohou Banner. The provincial capital is located in Qiqihar City.

    V. Songjiang Province

    Songjiang Province governs four municipalities and thirty-two counties. The four municipalities are: Harbin City, Mudanjiang City, Jiamusi City, and Xingshan City. The thirty-two counties are: Shuangcheng, Acheng, Hulan, Wuchang, Bayan, Bin County, Shangzhi (formerly Zhuhe County, named in memory of the anti-Japanese hero Zhao Shangzhi), Yanshou, Chaolan, Hailin, Ning'an, Fangzheng, Lalin, Tonghe, Dongning, Muling, Boli, Huachuan, Yilan, Fujin, Suibin, Tangyuan, Mibei, Fuyuan, Baoqing, Linkou, Raohe, Hulin, Mishan, Jixian, Jining, and Huanan. The provincial capital is located in Harbin City.

    VI. Rehe Province

    Rehe Province governs two municipalities, nineteen counties, and four banners. The two municipalities are: Chengde City and Chifeng City. The nineteen counties are: Chengde, Chifeng, Lingyuan, Pingquan, Jianchang, Jianping, Ningcheng, Weichang, Wudan, Qinglong, Ye Boshou, Longhua, Fengning, Luanping, Xinglong, Qingping (newly established county, formed from parts of Qinglong and Pingquan), Beipiao, Chaoyang, and Yangshan (newly established county, centered on Yangshan Town in southern Chaoyang). The four banners are: Kalaqin Right Banner, Aohan Banner, Wengniute Banner, and Kalaqin Left Banner. The provincial capital is located in Chengde City.

    VII. Four Directly-Administered Municipalities

    The four directly-administered municipalities are: Shenyang City, Fushun City, Anshan City, and Benxi City. (Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 10th)

### 东北新行政区划

1949-05-12
第3版()
专栏：资料

    东北新行政区划东北行政委员会于上月二十一日重划的东北行政区划如下：
    一、辽东省
    辽东省辖五个市，二十九个县。五个市为：安东市、营口市、通化市、辽阳市、西安市。二十九个县为：庄河、新金、复县、盖平、营口、辽阳、海城、岫岩、孤山、宽甸、抚顺、安东、凤城、桓仁、本溪、西安、海龙、东丰、铄丰、清原、新宾（原兴京）、通化、辑安、临江、长白、抚松、靖宇（原＠江、金川两县合并纪念抗日英雄杨靖宇命名）、辉南、柳河。省府设安东市。
    二、辽西省
    辽西省辖四个市、二十一个县。四个市为：锦州市、四平市、阜新市、山海关市。二十一个县为：锦县、锦西、绥中、兴城、盘山、台安、辽中、义县、新民、彰武、阜新、北镇、黑山、昌图、梨树、法库、康平、双辽（原双山、辽源合成）、开原、铁岭、昌北（新设县、原昌图县划出）。省府设锦州市。
    三、吉林省
    吉林省辖两个市、二十二个县和一个旗。两个市为：长春市、吉林市。二十二个县为：永吉、榆树、九台、德惠、长春、舒兰、磐石、蛟河、双阳、伊通、桦甸、敦化、农安、乾安、扶余、怀德、长岭、延吉、和龙、汪清、珲春、安图。一个旗为郭前旗。省府设吉林市。
    四、黑龙江省
    黑龙江省辖一个市、四十个县和两个旗。一个市为：齐齐哈尔市。四十个县为·肇东、肇州、讷河、龙江、洮安、安达、大赉、镇赉（新设县、镇东县及大赉北部合成）、泰来、绥化、海伦、望奎、拜泉、泰安、克山、青冈、兰西、庆安、克东、明水、洮南、林甸、富裕（伊克明安旗在内）、嫩江、甘南、景星、瞻榆、开通、安广、北安、通北、绥棱、德都、铁、孙吴、瑷珲。呻玛、逊克（原逊河、奇克合成）、乌云、佛山。两个旗为：杜尔伯特旗及郭后旗。省府设齐齐哈尔市。
    五、松江省
    松江省辖四个市、三十二个县。四个市为：哈尔滨市、牡丹江市、佳木斯市、兴山市。三十二个县为：双城、阿城、呼兰、五常、巴彦、宾县、尚志（原珠河县，纪念抗日英雄赵尚志命名）、延寿、朝兰、海林、宁安、方正、拉林、通河、东宁、穆棱、勃利、桦川、依兰、富锦、绥滨、汤原、蘼北、抚远、宝清、林口、饶河、虎林、密山、集贤、鸡宁、桦南。省府设哈尔滨市。
    六、热河省
    热河省辖两个市、十九个县和四个旗。两个市为：承德市、赤峰市。十九个县为：承德、赤峰、凌源、平泉、建昌、建平、宁城、围场、乌丹、青龙、叶伯寿、隆化、丰宁、滦平、兴隆、青平（新设县、青龙、平泉各一部合成）、北票、朝阳、羊山（新设县、以朝阳南部羊山镇为中心）。四个旗为：喀喇沁右旗、敖汗旗、翁牛特旗、喀喇沁左旗。省府设承德市。
    七、四直辖市
    四个直辖市为：沈阳市、抚顺市、鞍山市、本溪市。（新华社北平十日电）
