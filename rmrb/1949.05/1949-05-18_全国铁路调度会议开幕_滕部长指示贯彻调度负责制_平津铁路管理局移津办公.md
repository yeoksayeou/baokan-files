Title: National Railway Dispatch Conference Opens; Minister Teng Instructs on Implementing Dispatch Responsibility System; Tianjin-Beijing Railway Administration Moves Office to Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 1

[Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Beiping, 16th: The National Railway Dispatch Conference, convened by the Railway Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, opened in Beiping today. Attending the conference were 91 representatives from the Transportation Bureau of the Railway Department, the Northeast Railway General Bureau, and various compilation station dispatch offices under the jurisdiction of the four railway administrations of Pinghan, Jinan, Zhengzhou, and Shijiazhuang. The main purpose of this conference is to unify national railway dispatch, study the dispatch responsibility system, and standardize the system of dispatch reports and methods for calculating vehicle turnaround rates. Minister Teng Daiyuan pointed out at the beginning of the conference that implementing a unified dispatch report system, implementing the dispatch responsibility system, and improving freight car turnaround efficiency is a revolution in the history of Chinese railways. We should reform the corrupt, bureaucratic, and disjointed dispatch system left over from the Kuomintang rule, strengthen our sense of responsibility, strengthen mutual assistance and unity, and take railway construction to a higher level.
    [Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Beiping, 17th: The Railway Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission issued an order on May 6th deciding to move the Tianjin-Beijing Railway Administration from Beiping to Tianjin. The original Tianjin branch will be abolished, and an office will be established in Beiping.

### 全国铁路调度会议开幕　滕部长指示贯彻调度负责制  平津铁路管理局移津办公

1949-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国铁路调度会议开幕
  　滕部长指示贯彻调度负责制
    平津铁路管理局移津办公
    【北平新华广播电台广播】北平十六号消息：中国人民革命军事委员会铁道部召集的全国铁路调度会议，今天在北平开幕，到会的有铁道部运输局、东北铁路总局和平汉、济南、郑州、石家庄四个铁路局所属各编成站调度所各单位代表九十一人。这次会议的目的主要地是为了使全国铁路调度统一，并且研讨调度负责制，统一调度表报制度和车辆周转率计算方法等问题，滕代远部长在会议开始时指出，实行统一调度表报制度，贯彻调度负责制，提高货车周转效率，这是中国铁路历史上的一个革命，我们应该改革国民党统治时期遗留下来的那种腐朽的官僚主义的不统一的调度制度，加强责任心，加强互助团结，把铁路建设提高一步。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】北平十七号消息：中国人民革命军事委员会铁道部五月六号发出命令决定平津铁路管理局从北平迁到天津办公，原来的天津分局撤消，同时在北平成立办事处。
