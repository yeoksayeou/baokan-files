Title: Hefei Federation of Trade Unions and Youth Federation Establish Preparatory Committees
Author:
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, 10th. The preparatory committees for the Hefei Federation of Trade Unions and the Youth Federation were established on "May 1st" and "May 4th" respectively. Workers in the city held a workers' representative conference simultaneously with celebrating "May 1st" Labor Day. One hundred and twenty-six representatives from various industries attended. Representatives from the Anhui North Administrative Office, the Anhui North District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and the Hefei Municipal Government also participated. At the meeting, members of the Federation of Trade Unions were elected, with Wu Wei, Xue Guozhen, and Li Guangchang serving as chairman and vice-chairmen respectively. Representatives from all walks of life in Hefei held a representative meeting on the "May 4th" Commemoration Day and elected preparatory committee members for the Youth Federation. At the representative meeting, Zhang Fan, Secretary-General of the Anhui North District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, encouraged everyone to establish the concept of serving the people.

### 合肥职工总会与青联  成立筹备委员会

1949-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    合肥职工总会与青联
    成立筹备委员会
    【新华社合肥十日电】合肥市职工总会与青年联合会筹委会已于“五一”、“五四”先后成立。该市职工与庆祝“五一”劳动节同时召开职工代表大会，到各业职工代表一百二十六人，皖北行政公署、中共皖北区党委、合肥市政府均派代表参加。会上选出了职工会委员，由吴伟、薛国桢、李光昶分任正副主任。合肥市各界青年于“五四”纪念日举行代表会议，选出青联筹备委员，在代表会议上，中共皖北区党委秘书长张帆勉励大家确立为人民服务的观点。
