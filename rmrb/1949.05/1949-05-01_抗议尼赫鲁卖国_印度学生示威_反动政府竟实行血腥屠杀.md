Title: Protesting Nehru's Betrayal, Indian Students Demonstrate; Reactionary Government Carries Out Bloody Massacre

Author:

Date: 1949-05-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 29th -- According to Agence France-Presse: From the 21st to the 27th, Indian students in Calcutta continuously gathered and held demonstrations to protest Nehru's betrayal of Indian national interests to British and American imperialism at the London Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference. On the 27th, Indian police, under the instruction of the reactionary government, actually opened fire on a demonstration procession of more than 1,500 students, resulting in the deaths of four female students and three male students, with many injured. On the afternoon of the 28th, grieving and indignant students gathered again, carrying the bodies of the martyred students in a procession. The police again opened fire on the students, killing and wounding six students and arresting forty-eight.

### 抗议尼赫鲁卖国  印度学生示威  反动政府竟实行血腥屠杀

1949-05-01
第3版()
专栏：

    抗议尼赫鲁卖国
    印度学生示威
    反动政府竟实行血腥屠杀
    【新华社北平二十九日电】据法新社讯：加尔各答的印度学生于二十一日至二十七日连续集会并举行游行示威，抗议尼赫鲁在伦敦联邦总理会议上，把印度民族利益出卖给英美帝国主义。二十七日，印度警察在反动政府授意之下，竟向一千五百余学生的示威行列开枪射击，结果，死亡女生四名、男生三名，受伤多人，二十八日下午，悲愤的学生再度集合，行列中抬着牺牲烈士的遗体游行，警察又向学生开枪。死伤学生六名、被捕四十八名。
