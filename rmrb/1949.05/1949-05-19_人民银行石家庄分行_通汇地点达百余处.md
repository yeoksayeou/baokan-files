Title: People's Bank of China Shijiazhuang Branch: Correspondent Banks Reach Over 100 Locations
Author:
Date: 1949-05-19
Page: 2

　　[Shijiazhuang Dispatch] The remittance business of the Shijiazhuang Branch of the People's Bank of China with other locations has, to date, established correspondent banking relationships with 108 counties and cities, including Zhangjiakou, Xuanhua, Tangshan, Beiping, Tianjin, Jinan, Yidu, Xuzhou, Luoyang, Zhengzhou, Kaifeng, Yan'an, and Xingxian. From January to April this year, a total of 1,685 remittances were sent out, totaling 4,912,548,151.3 yuan in Renminbi. A total of 1,832 remittances were received, totaling 6,872,773,419.8 yuan in Renminbi. The bank is currently in discussions with Yantai, Linfen, Datong, Anyang, Xinxiang and other places to establish correspondent banking relationships.

### 人民银行石家庄分行　通汇地点达百余处

1949-05-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　人民银行石家庄分行
  　通汇地点达百余处
    【石家庄电】中国人民银行石家庄分行向外埠的汇兑业务，至目前止，已和张家口、宣化、唐山、北平、天津、济南、益都、徐州、洛阳、郑州、开封、延安、兴县等一百零八个县市互相通汇。自今年一月至四月计共汇出一六八五次，合人民券四，九一二，五四八，一五一点三元。共汇入一，八三二次，合人民券六，八七二，七七三，四一九点八元。现该行正与烟台、临汾、大同、安阳、新乡等地协商通汇中。
