Title: Northeast of Xi'an: Gaoling Liberated
Author:
Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 1

　　Northeast of Xi'an
  　Gaoling Liberated
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Shaanxi Central Front, 17th News: A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army's First Field Army liberated Gaoling County, northeast of Xi'an, on the evening of the 13th.

### 西安东北　解放高陵

　　西安东北
