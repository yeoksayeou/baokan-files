Title: Polish Women's Federation Congress Opens in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1949-05-24
Page: 3
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 23rd: Warsaw dispatch: The National Congress of the Polish Women's Federation opened here on the 20th.  Chairman of the Central Committee of the Women's Federation, Staszewska, in her report on the tasks of Polish women in the struggle to defend peace, said that 275,000 women have recently joined the Women's Federation, and 1,705 new women's organizations have been established in various enterprises. The Women's Federation has organized 676 literacy classes for illiterate people, and the Women's Federation, together with the Polish Peasants' Mutual Aid League, has organized 3,760 kindergartens nationwide.

　　The conference passed a resolution on the 21st saying: We will redouble our efforts in the struggle for peace. We condemn the war-mongers for using the Marshall Plan and the North Atlantic Treaty as tools of aggression to enable American imperialism to dominate the world. The resolution pointed out the uncompromising position of the majority of the Western European proletariat, especially the French and Italian proletariat, in their struggle against imperialism. The resolution also pointed out the great liberating force in the People's China, where the victorious People's Liberation Army defeated the lackeys of American imperialism. The conference also passed another resolution saying that we approve the decision of the Warsaw Women's Federation to contribute 500,000 hours of work to rebuild Warsaw and call on women across the country to follow their example.

### 波妇联大会在华沙揭幕

1949-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　波妇联大会在华沙揭幕
    【新华社北平二十三日电】华沙讯：波兰妇女联盟全国会议于二十日在此间开幕。妇联中央委员会主席斯塔捷斯卡作了关于在保卫和平斗争中波兰妇女的任务的报告中说，最近有二十七万五千妇女参加妇联，在各企业中已成立了一千七百零五个新的妇女组织，妇联的会员为不识字的人民组织了六百七十六个识字班、妇联会同波兰农民互助联盟在全国组织了三千七百六十所幼稚园。
    该会议于二十一日通过决议说：我们将在争取和平的斗争中加倍努力。我们斥责战争贩子利用马歇尔计划，北大西洋公约作为侵略工具，来使美帝国主义独霸世界。决议指出大多数西欧无产阶级，特别是法意无产阶级与帝国主义斗争的不妥协立场。决议也指出在人民的中国的伟大解放力量，那里胜利的人民解放军击败了美帝国主义的奴仆。会议又通过另一决议说，我们赞同华沙妇女联盟的决定、贡献五十万小时的工作来重建华沙、并号召全国妇女向她们看齐。
