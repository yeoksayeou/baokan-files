Title: On the Eve of the Four Foreign Ministers Meeting: US, Britain, and France Hold Separate Consultations; Contents Kept Completely Secret
Author:
Date: 1949-05-24
Page: 3
　　
　　On the Eve of the Four Foreign Ministers Meeting
  　US, Britain, and France Hold Separate Consultations
  　Contents Kept Completely Secret
    Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, May 23rd: Paris dispatch: On the eve of the Four Foreign Ministers Meeting, US Secretary of State Acheson, British Foreign Secretary Bevin, and French Foreign Minister Schuman held two meetings here on the 21st and 22nd. The contents of the meetings were not made public.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, May 21st: United Press Paris dispatch: The separate consultations held by senior US, British, and French diplomats on the eve of the Four Foreign Ministers Meeting concluded on the 19th. From the 14th to the 19th, the representatives of the three countries held a total of seven talks, the contents of which were kept completely secret. It is understood that the representatives of the three countries have drafted a complete plan, which will be submitted to the US, British, and French foreign ministers for reporting. US Secretary of State Acheson and British Foreign Secretary Bevin have left their countries for Paris. The separate US, British, and French foreign ministers meeting is scheduled to be held in Paris on the 21st.

### 四外长会议前夕　美英法单独会商　内容完全保守秘密

1949-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　四外长会议前夕
  　美英法单独会商
  　内容完全保守秘密
    【新华社北平二十三日电】巴黎讯：在四国外长会议前夕，美国务卿艾奇逊、英外相贝文、法外长舒曼于二十一日、二十二日在此间举行两次会议，会议内容未经公布。
    【新华社北平二十一日电】合众社巴黎讯：美英法高级外交人员在四国外长会议前夕单独举行的会商，已于十九日结束。自十四日至十九日，三国代表共举行了七次会谈，内容完全保守秘密。据悉，三国代表已拟定一完整的计划，将向美英法外长提出报告。美国务卿艾奇逊与英外相贝文已离本国前往巴黎。单独的美英法外长会议定于二十一日在巴黎举行。
