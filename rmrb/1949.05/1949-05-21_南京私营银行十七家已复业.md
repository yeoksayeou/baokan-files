Title: Seventeen Private Banks in Nanjing Resume Operations
Author:
Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 20th -- Seventeen private banks in Nanjing, including Continental, Shanghai, Jin Cheng, Xingye, Juxingcheng, and Zhongnan, resumed operations on the 18th. These private banks held a business meeting on the 17th, and in accordance with the "Provisional Measures for the Management of Private Banks and Money Shops in the North China People's Government," which was temporarily approved for implementation in the city by the Nanjing Military Control Commission, they discussed issues such as the future development of their business.

### 南京私营银行十七家已复业

1949-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京私营银行十七家已复业
    【新华社南京二十日电】南京市大陆、上海、金城、兴业、聚兴诚、中南等十七家私营银行已于十八日复业。这些私营银行十七日曾举行业务会议，依据南京军管会暂准在本市施行之“华北人民政府私营银钱业管理暂行办法”，讨论今后业务开展等问题。
