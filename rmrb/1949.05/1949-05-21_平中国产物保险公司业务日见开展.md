Title: Business of Ping China Property Insurance Company is Developing Rapidly
Author:
Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 2

Ping China Property Insurance Company

Business is Developing Rapidly

[Reported by this newspaper] The business of Ping Municipal China Property Insurance Company is developing rapidly. From its opening on the 14th to yesterday (the 20th), it has directly underwritten and co-insured with other companies a total of 53 policies, with a total insured amount of over 65.09 million yuan.

　　　　　　　　　　　　(Yu)

### 平中国产物保险公司业务日见开展

1949-05-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　平中国产物保险公司
  　业务日见开展
    【本报讯】平市中国产物保险公司业务日见开展，自十四日开业至昨（二十日）止，直接承保及同业分保共有五十三笔，总计保额为：六千五十九万余元。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　（宇）
