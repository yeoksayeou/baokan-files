Title: Shaanxi Central Our Army Fiercely Pursues Fleeing Enemy, Annihilating One Army and Another Division, Consecutively Capturing Thirteen Cities Including Huxian, Mei County, Fengxiang, Yongshou, and Lin you in Four Days
Author:
Date: 1949-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shaanxi Central Front, 24th dispatch: The People's Liberation Army, crossing the Jing River to pursue the fleeing enemy southwestward, annihilated one enemy army and one division in the Lin you mountainous area, and seized the opportunity to liberate Fengxiang County. On the 22nd, the Liberation Army completely surrounded the fleeing enemy 57th Army (comprising the 214th and 215th Divisions) and the 30th Division in the Shibaling area 50 miles southwest of Lin you and the Tiewangou area approximately 40 miles east of Fengxiang. After ten hours of combat, the entire enemy force was annihilated. The results of the battle are currently being verified. Prior to this, the Liberation Army had successively liberated the four county towns of Yongshou, Fufeng, Lin you, and Qishan on the 20th and 21st.

Xinhua News Agency, Shaanxi Central Front, 24th dispatch: The People's Liberation Army, crossing the Longhai Railway to pursue the fleeing enemy westward, liberated the important town of Guozhen, east of Baoji, on the 22nd, and annihilated one enemy security regiment north of the town. In addition to those killed and wounded, over 800 enemy soldiers were captured alive. Prior to this, another unit of the Liberation Army annihilated a portion of the enemy's Second Security Brigade, Sixth Regiment, while crossing the Wei River on the 20th. Furthermore, in the Xingping area, an enemy regiment was routed, and hundreds of enemy soldiers were captured.

Xinhua News Agency, Shaanxi Central Front, 24th dispatch: The People's Liberation Army, crossing the Wei River to pursue the fleeing enemy southwestward, liberated Huxian County southwest of Xi'an and Mei County to its west on the 21st and 22nd. Another unit of the Liberation Army liberated the important town of Ziwu, south of Xi'an. During the battles, one enemy company was annihilated, and a portion of the enemy's 12th Division was captured. In the four days from the 18th to the 22nd, the Liberation Army has liberated thirteen cities north and south of the Wei River, namely: Xi'an, Xianyang, Liquan, Xingping, Yongshou, Qianxian, Wugong, Fufeng, Lin you, Qishan, Fengxiang, Huxian, and Mei County.

### 陕中我军猛追逃敌　歼敌一军又一师  四天连克鄠县郿县凤翔  永寿麟游等十三座城市

1949-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕中我军猛追逃敌 　 歼敌一军又一师
    四天连克鄠县郿县凤翔  永寿麟游等十三座城市
    【新华社陕中前线二十四日电】越过泾河向西南追击逃敌的人民解放军，在麟游山区歼灭敌军一个军及一个师，并乘胜解放凤翔县城。二十二日解放军在麟游西南五十里之十八岭及凤翔东约四十里之铁王沟地区将逃敌五十七军（辖二一四、二一五两个师）及三十师团团包围，经十小时战斗，将该敌全部歼灭，战果正清查中。在此以前，解放军于二十、二十一两日先后解放永寿、扶风、麟游、岐山四座县城。
    【新华社陕中前线二十四日电】越陇海路向西追击逃敌的人民解放军，二十二日解放宝鸡以东重要市镇虢镇，并在该镇以北歼灭敌保安团一个团，除毙伤外，生俘敌八百余人。在此以前，另部解放军于二十日渡过渭河时，歼灭河防敌保二旅六团一部。另在兴平地区击溃敌军一个团，俘敌数百人。
    【新华社陕中前线二十四日电】通过渭河向西南追击逃敌的人民解放军，二十一、二十二两日解放西安西南的鄠县及其以西的郿县两座县城，另部解放军解放西安以南的重镇子午镇。在战斗中，歼灭敌军一个连，另俘敌十二师一部。由十八日至二十二日的四天中，解放军在渭河南北已解放十三座城市，即：西安、咸阳、醴泉、兴平、永寿、乾县、武功、扶风、麟游、岐山、凤翔、鄠县、郿县。
