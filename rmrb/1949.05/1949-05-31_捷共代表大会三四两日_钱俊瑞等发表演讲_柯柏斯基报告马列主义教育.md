Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party Congress Days Three and Four: Qian Junrui and Others Deliver Speeches; Kopřivová Reports on Marxist-Leninist Education
Author:
Date: 1949-05-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 30th: Prague dispatch: On the evening of the third day (the 27th) of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Congress, a mass celebration was held. President Gottwald was greeted with prolonged cheers and congratulations from the masses. Countless participants erupted in storm-like cheers to congratulate the representatives of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the representatives of various foreign Communist and Workers' Parties attending the Czechoslovak Communist Party Congress. Qian Junrui, representative of the Chinese Communist Party, Palmiro Togliatti, General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, Maurice Thorez, Secretary of the French Communist Party, and Samborski, Secretary of the Polish United Workers' Party, all delivered speeches at the meeting.

On the fourth day (the 28th) of the Congress, Aalto Aaltonen, Chairman of the Finnish Communist Party, and Grozos, representative of the Greek Communist Party, also extended congratulations to the Congress. At the morning session on the 28th, a report on Marxist-Leninist education was heard from Kopřivová, Minister of Propaganda and Culture, and member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party. Kopřivová concluded her report by calling on Czechoslovak Communists and all working people to unite more closely under the banner of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, to advance towards socialism, to devote themselves loyally to the Czechoslovak Communist Party, and to forever follow the Bolshevik Party and the genius people's leader Stalin. These words were met with prolonged storm-like cheers from the delegates. The afternoon session on the 28th continued to discuss the reports made by Gottwald, Slánský, Zápotocký, and Kopřivová. Erban, Minister of Labor and Social Affairs, Novomeský, Commissioner of Schools and Education of the Slovak Administrative Council, and other representatives spoke. At both the morning and afternoon sessions, delegations of workers and peasants extended congratulations to the Congress.

### 捷共代表大会三四两日　钱俊瑞等发表演讲  柯柏斯基报告马列主义教育

1949-05-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷共代表大会三四两日
  　钱俊瑞等发表演讲
    柯柏斯基报告马列主义教育
    【新华社北平三十日电】布拉格讯：捷共代表大会第三日（二十七日）晚间，举行群众庆祝大会。哥特瓦尔德总统受到群众经久不绝的欢呼祝贺。无数与会群众发出暴风雨似的欢呼声，祝贺苏联共产党代表及各外国共产党、工人党出席捷共代表大会的代表。中国共产党代表钱俊瑞、意共总书记托格里亚蒂、法共书记马尔梯及波兰统一工人党书记萨姆布劳斯基，都曾在会上发表演讲。
    大会第四日（二十八日），芬兰共产党主席亚尔托宁，希腊共产党代表格罗佐斯二十人均向大会致贺。二十八日上午会议听取了宣传与文化部长、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会主席团委员柯伯斯基关于马克思列宁主义教育的报告。柯伯斯基结束他的报告时，号召捷克斯洛伐克共产党员与一切劳动人民在马、恩、列、斯学说的旗帜之下，更紧密地团结起来，向社会主义迈进，忠诚地献身于捷克斯洛伐克共产党，永远追随布尔什维克党与天才人民领袖斯大林。这些话受到了代表们暴风雨似的长久欢呼。二十八日下午会议继续讨论哥特瓦尔德、斯兰斯基、萨波托斯基和柯柏斯基所作的报告。劳工与社会部长埃尔班，斯洛伐克行政委员会学校与教育委员诺伏密斯基和其他代表曾发言。在上下午会议上，均有工人和农民代表团向大会致贺。
