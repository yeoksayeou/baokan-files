Title: Protesting the Reactionary Education Bill of the National Congress: 150,000 Japanese Students Strike for One Day; 3,000 Students Rally and Demonstrate in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th: According to Agence France-Presse, Tokyo: 150,000 students from 130 universities and colleges across Japan went on strike for one day on the 24th to protest the so-called National University Law and Teacher Qualification Examination Law recently passed by the Diet. This nationwide action is the largest of the wave of protests and strikes that have taken place across the country since the beginning of this month. At the same time, students from 24 universities and colleges in Tokyo held a "Strive for Educational Freedom" rally in Hibiya Park on the afternoon of the 24th, after which 3,000 students held a demonstration march. According to a spokesperson for the All-Japan Student Federation, which organized the strike, the All-Japan Student Federation is scheduled to hold a national student representative conference on the 27th to summarize past actions and formulate future action plans.

### 抗议国会反动教育法案　日十五万学生罢课一天  东京三千学生集会示威

1949-05-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　抗议国会反动教育法案
  　日十五万学生罢课一天
    东京三千学生集会示威
    【新华社北平二十六日电】据法新社东京讯：日本各地一百三十所大学及专科学校的学生十五万人，于二十四日罢课一天，以抗议最近议会通过的所谓国立大学法及教员资格审查法。这一全国性行动是本月初以来各地抗议罢课浪潮中最大的一次。同时，东京二十四个大学及专科学校的学生于二十四日午后在日比谷公园举行“争取教育自由”大会，会后三千学生举行示威游行。据主持这次罢课的日本全国学联发言人称：全国学联已定于二十七日举行全国学生代表大会，以总结过去与拟定今后的行动方针。
