Date: 1949-05-13
Page: 4

    World Youth Trends
    ▲ The preparatory committee for the World Youth Festival, jointly organized by the World Federation of Democratic Youth and the International Union of Students, announced on April 28th that the World Youth Festival will begin on August 14th with a gathering at the Renna Voss Stadium in Budapest. The 10th World University Games will be inaugurated the following day by Hungarian President Szakasits. The agenda includes a parade of working youth, a solidarity day for world youth with the fighting youth of China, Greece, and Spain—Colonial Youth Day—and a peace demonstration.
    ▲ The Philadelphia branch of the American Youth for Progress recently wrote an open letter to the youth of Leningrad, expressing the desire of American progressive youth to uphold peace and Soviet-American friendship. The youth of Leningrad convened a congress of 2,000 representatives on April 26th, and after discussion, unanimously passed a reply to the Philadelphia branch of the American Youth for Progress, stating: "Dear friends, we extend our warm greetings to you, the progressive youth of Philadelphia. We wholeheartedly support your desire to strive for peace and consolidate friendship with the youth of the Soviet Union. The youth of Leningrad and all the youth of the Soviet Union equally support the cause of peace, democracy, and progress."

### 世界青年动态

1949-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    世界青年动态
    ▲世界青联与国际学联，共同组织的世界青年节筹备委员会，四月二十八日宣布：世界青年节，于今年八月十四日开始时，将在布达佩斯的伦那渥丝体育场举行集会。第十次世界大学运动会，将于第二日由匈牙利总统萨卡西兹揭幕。日程中有劳动青年的游行，有世界青年与中国、希腊、西班牙战斗青年的团结日——殖民地青年日，并将举行和平示威。
    ▲美国进步青年协会费城分会，最近写了一封公开信给列宁格勒的青年，表示美国进步青年拥护和平与美苏友谊的愿望。列宁格勒青年在四月二十六日召集了二千人的代表大会，讨论之后一致通过给美国进步青年协会费城分会复信内称：“亲爱的朋友们，我们谨向你们费城进步青年热烈敬礼，我们全心全意支持你们争取和平、争受巩固对苏联青年友谊的愿望。列宁格勒青年和全体苏联青年同样支持和平、民主与进步的事业。”
