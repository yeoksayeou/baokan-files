Title: Nearly Three Million Members in Czechoslovakian Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1949-05-20
Page: 3
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th: TASS, Prague: According to statistics released by the Central Committee of the Czechoslovakian Trade Unions, as of April 1, 1949, the Czechoslovakian Trade Unions had a total of 2,929,000 members. In the Czech region, there were 2,419,000 trade union members; in the Slovak region, there were 510,000 members. The Metalworkers' Union, with 544,000 members, is the largest union in the country. The Textile and Garment Workers' Union has 348,000 members, and the Construction Workers' Union has 223,600 members.

### 捷职工会会员近三百万

1949-05-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷职工会会员近三百万
    【新华社北平十八日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：据捷克斯洛伐克职工会中央委员会所发表的统计数字，捷克斯洛伐克职工会会员截至一九四九年四月一日为止，共有二百九十二万九千名。在捷克部分，职工会会员有二百四十一万九千名；在斯洛伐克部分，会员有五十一万名。拥有会员五十四万四千名的五金工人工会是全国最大的工会。纺织和被服工人工会有会员三十四万八千名，建筑工人工会有会员二十二万三千六百名。
