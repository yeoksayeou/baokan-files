Title: Shanghai Bandit Gang Ravages Schools
Author:
Date: 1949-05-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 5th: News from Shanghai on the 2nd: The Kuomintang bandit gang, trapped in Shanghai, drove most of the faculty and students of fifteen universities and colleges, including Jiaotong and Fudan, out of the schools. Students who were left homeless were confined to the campus, like prisoners.

### 上海匪帮摧残学校

1949-05-06
第3版()
专栏：

    上海匪帮摧残学校
    【新华社北平五日电】上海二日消息：困守上海的国民党匪帮将交通、复旦等十五个大学和学院的大部分教职员学生，赶出学校。无家可归的学生，则被闭在校内，形同囚犯。
