Title: Four Northeast Cotton Mills Launch Production Competition, Output and Quality Greatly Improved
Author:
Date: 1949-05-19
Page: 3

　　Northeast Four Cotton Mills Launch Production Competition
  　Output and Quality Greatly Improved
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Shenyang, 18th – Since the production competition campaign was launched in the various factories under the Northeast Textile Administration Bureau, both output and quality have greatly improved. The output of the Fourth Cotton Mill has increased by more than 20 to 25 percent compared to before the competition. In the Second Cotton Mill, the average yarn output per spindle per ten hours in the fine yarn workshop in February was 0.36 pounds, which has now increased to 0.389 pounds. In the First Cotton Mill, the average output of 16-count yarn per spindle per ten hours in the fine yarn workshop was 0.566 pounds in the week before the competition. In the second week after the competition began, the average output per spindle increased to 0.576 pounds. During the competition, the Second Cotton Mill not only strengthened leadership but also established a technical research group to study and improve equipment.

### 东北四个纱厂展开生产竞赛　产量质量大为提高

1949-05-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　东北四个纱厂展开生产竞赛
  　产量质量大为提高
    【北平新华广播电台广播】沈阳十八日消息：东北纺织管理局所属各厂展开生产竞赛运动以来，产量和质量都大为提高。第四纺织厂产量比竞赛前提高百分之二十到二十五以上。第三纺织厂二月份细纱间每锭十个钟头平均出纱零点三六磅，现在提高到零点三八九磅。第一纺织厂竞赛前一个星期细纱间每锭十个钟头平均产出十六支纱零点五六六磅，竞赛开始后第二个星期，每锭平均增加到零点五七六磅。第二纺织厂在竞赛期间除了加强领导以外，并且成立了技术研究组，研究改善设备。
