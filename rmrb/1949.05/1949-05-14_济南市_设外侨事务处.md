Title: Jinan City Establishes Foreign Affairs Office

Author:

Date: 1949-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 12th -- In order to strengthen foreign affairs work, the Jinan Municipal People's Government has obtained approval from the Shandong Provincial People's Government to establish a Foreign Affairs Office, which has now begun operations.

### 济南市  设外侨事务处

1949-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    济南市
    设外侨事务处
    【新华社济南十二日电】济南市人民政府为加强外侨事务工作，已呈准山东省人民政府成立外侨事务处，现已开始工作。
