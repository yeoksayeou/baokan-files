Title: The First Class of Students from North China Health School - 200 Participate in Work
Author: He Shaomei
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 2

    The First Class of Students from North China Health School
    200 Participate in Work
    The first class of the North China Health School, consisting of obstetrics and gynecology, midwifery, and acupuncture classes, held a graduation ceremony on the 4th of this month in Pingshan County, where the school is located. A total of 200 graduates were assigned to various locations, including Central Hebei, Taihang, Chahar, Taiyue, Southern Hebei, Shijiazhuang, and Central Shanxi, totaling 152 individuals. The remaining 48 individuals were assigned to work in directly affiliated agencies and schools under the North China Government.
        (He Shaomei)

### 华北卫生学校第一期学员  二百人参加工作
何少梅
1949-05-12
第2版()
专栏：

    华北卫生学校第一期学员
    二百人参加工作
    华北卫生学校第一期妇婴卫生班，助产班，针灸班，本月四日于学校所在地平山县举行毕业典礼。毕业学员共二百名，分配冀中、太行、察哈尔、太岳、冀南、石家庄、晋中等地共一百五十二名，其余四十八名分配华北政府直属机关和学校工作。        （何少梅）
