Title: Nanjing Garrison Headquarters Established; Xinhua Daily Begins Publication in Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1949-05-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 2nd: The Nanjing Garrison Headquarters and its Political Department were officially established on May 1st. The Nanjing Military Control Commission has specially appointed Chen Shiqu as Commander, Zhou Xing as Deputy Commander, Yuan Zhongxian as Political Commissar, and Jiang Weiqing as Deputy Political Commissar and Director of the Political Department. They have all arrived at their posts and are performing their duties.
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 1st: The Nanjing to Changzhou section of the Beijing-Shanghai Railway, a total of 140 kilometers, was restored to traffic on the 27th. The Changzhou to Wuxi section was damaged by retreating Kuomintang bandit troops, but railway workers have actively repaired it and completed the work.
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 30th: Xinhua Daily was launched today. The Nanjing branch of Xinhua News Agency has also been established simultaneously.
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 2nd: The Sunan branch of Xinhua News Agency was established in Wuxi City on April 27th and has begun issuing news reports.

### 南京警备司令部成立  新华日报在南京创刊

1949-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    南京警备司令部成立
    新华日报在南京创刊
    【新华社南京二日电】本市警备司令部、政治部于五月一日宣告成立。南京军事管制委员会特任命陈士渠为司令员，周兴为副司令员，袁仲贤为政治委员，江渭清为副政治委员兼政治部主任，刻已到职视事。
    【新华社南京一日电】京沪铁路南京至常州段已于二十七日恢复通车，全程一百四十公里。常州至无锡段曾遭溃退之国民党匪军破坏，现经铁路员工积极抢修完竣。
    【新华社南京三十日电】新华日报于今日创刊。新华社南京分社已同时成立。
    【新华社南京二日电】新华通讯社苏南分社于四月二十七日在无锡市成立，并开始发稿。
