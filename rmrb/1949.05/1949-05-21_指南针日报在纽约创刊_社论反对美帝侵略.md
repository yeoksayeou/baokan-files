Title: Compass Daily Launched in New York; Editorial Opposes U.S. Imperialist Aggression
Author:
Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th: TASS, New York: "Compass Daily" was launched in New York on the 16th. The editor and manager of the newspaper is Sackley, the former editor and publisher of the New York Post, who was fired by the New York Post for opposing the North Atlantic Treaty. In an editorial in the Compass Daily, Sackley stated that he has not changed his attitude, and the newly launched newspaper will oppose the North Atlantic Treaty and Washington's "bipartisan" policy of aggression.

### 指南针日报在纽约创刊　社论反对美帝侵略

1949-05-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　指南针日报在纽约创刊
  　社论反对美帝侵略
    【新华社北平十九日电】塔斯社纽约讯：“指南针日报”十六日在纽约创刊。该报主编及经理为前纽约邮报主编兼发行人萨克莱，他因为反对北大西洋公约而遭纽约邮报辞退。萨克莱在指南针日报社论中表示了他并不改变态度，新创刊的报纸将反对北大西洋公约及华盛顿“两党一致”的侵略政策。
