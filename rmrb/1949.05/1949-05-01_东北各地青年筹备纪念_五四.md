Title: Youth in Various Parts of Northeast China Prepare to Commemorate "May Fourth"
Author:
Date: 1949-05-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 30th. Youth in various parts of Northeast China are actively preparing to commemorate the May Fourth Youth Day. The preparatory committee of the Shenyang Municipal Youth League, the Youth Federation, the Student Federation, and other organizations have decided to organize youth from various factories, schools, and institutions to discuss the historical significance of the "May Fourth" Movement and the current tasks of Chinese youth, and to hold singing contests, poster competitions, and concerts. The Harbin Municipal Committee of the Youth League, the Education Bureau, the Student Federation, the Federation of Trade Unions, and other organizations have jointly established a Red May Movement Preparatory Committee to prepare for ball games and track and field events, and will hold an open-air evening party on "May Fourth". The Fushun Municipal Youth League is preparing to hold a forum for young workers and students. Jilin City plans to hold a city-wide youth commemorative meeting and bonfire party on "May Fourth". Similar preparations are also underway in Changchun, Jiamusi, Qiqihar and other places.

### 东北各地青年筹备纪念“五四”

1949-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    东北各地青年筹备纪念“五四”
    【新华社沈阳三十日电】东北各地青年积极筹备纪念五四青年节。沈阳市青年团筹委会、青年联合会、学生联合会等团体决定组织各工厂、学校、机关的青年，研讨“五四”的历史意义及目前中国青年的任务，并举行歌咏、壁报比赛及音乐会，哈尔滨市青年团市委会、教育局、学联、职工总会等联合组织红五月运动筹委会，筹备举行球赛与径赛，并于“五四”举行露天晚会。抚顺市青年团准备召开青年工人和学生座谈会。吉林市拟于“五四”举行全市青年纪念大会和营火晚会。长春、佳木斯、齐齐哈尔等地亦正在进行类似的准备工作。
