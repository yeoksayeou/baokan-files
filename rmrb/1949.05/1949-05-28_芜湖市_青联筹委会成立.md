Title: Wuhu City Youth Federation Preparatory Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1949-05-28
Page: 3

　　Wuhu City
  　Youth Federation Preparatory Committee Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, Wuhu, 27th] The Preparatory Committee of the Wuhu City Youth Federation was declared established on the 19th at the old site of the former Youth Center. The main tasks of the committee at present are to help workers in various factories resume work and production, organize literacy classes for workers, and organize young people to study revolutionary theory. The 28 cadres of the Youth Federation Preparatory Committee have been dispatched to various factories and schools to carry out work. In addition, the Youth Club of the Youth Federation Preparatory Committee was established on the 22nd, and an exhibition of books and photos of the Huaihai Campaign, etc., was held on the same day.

### 芜湖市　青联筹委会成立

1949-05-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　芜湖市
  　青联筹委会成立
    【新华社芜湖二十七日电】芜湖市青年联合会筹备委员会，已于十九日在前青年馆旧址宣告成立。该会目前主要工作，是帮助各工厂工人复工复业，组织工人识字班，组织青年学习革命理论。青联筹委会二十八个干部已分别到各工厂、学校进行工作。又青联筹委会的青年俱乐部，已于二十二日成立，并于是日展览图书及淮海等战役照片。
