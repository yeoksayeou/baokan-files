Title: Chahar Provincial People's Government Increases Water Conservancy Loans
Author:
Date: 1949-05-23
Page: 2

　　Chahar News: In order to guarantee increased production through irrigation and overcome the current drought, the Chahar Provincial People's Government has decided to increase small-scale water conservancy loans to the three special districts of Jicha, Chanan, and Yanbei by 1.5 million yuan (Renminbi), including 200,000 yuan for Jicha, 500,000 yuan for Chanan, and 800,000 yuan for Yanbei.

### 察省人民政府增拨水利贷款

1949-05-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　察省人民政府增拨水利贷款
    【察哈尔讯】察省人民政府为保证灌溉增产，克服目前荒旱，决定增拨冀察、、察南、雁北三个专区小型水利贷款一百五十万元（人民币），计冀察二十万元，察南五十万元，雁北八十万元。
