Title: Hangzhou Restores External Land and Water Transportation
Author:
Date: 1949-05-19
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 17th—The Hangzhou-Jinhua section of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway and the Hangzhou-Haining section of the Shanghai-Hangzhou Railway simultaneously resumed operation on the afternoon of the 12th. Telecommunications between Hangzhou and other places have been largely restored after active consolidation, and communication has begun with important towns in the North China and Northeast liberated areas. In terms of waterway transportation, since the 12th, eight shipping companies, including Huatong, have obtained ship permits and resumed navigation one after another.

### 杭州市对外水陆交通恢复

1949-05-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　杭州市对外水陆交通恢复
    【新华社杭州十七日电】浙赣铁路杭州至金华段和沪杭铁路杭州至硖石段已同时于十二日下午恢复通车。杭州与外埠的电讯交通经积极整顿已大部分恢复，并开始与华北、东北解放区的重要城镇通报通话。水路交通方面，十二日起，已有华通等八家轮船公司，领得船舶通行证，先后恢复通航。
