Title: "Service for Poland" Work Team Writes to Beirut, Representing Millions of Youth to Make Promises: Repairing Canals and Planting Forests to Help National Construction
Author:
Date: 1949-05-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th: Warsaw Dispatch: A youth delegation from the "Service for Poland" work team, when meeting with President Beirut on April 28th, presented him with a letter on behalf of the 1.2 million young men and women participating in this work team. The letter stated: In 1948, the youth of the work team built 1,559 rural clubs, 1,698 sports fields, and 1,740 kilometers of roads for the country, and so on. The youth pledged to work 69 million work-hours in 1949 and to complete the first half of the year's tasks one month ahead of schedule. The "Service for Poland" work team guarantees to build 600 kilometers of drainage canals, 3,100 kilometers of roads, excavate 1,843 kilometers of canals, plant 30,000 hectares of forests, and construct 1,608 cultural institutions and schools this year.

### “为波兰服务工作队”写信给贝鲁特  代表百万青年提出保证  修渠植林帮助国家建设

1949-05-09
第3版()
专栏：

    “为波兰服务工作队”写信给贝鲁特
    代表百万青年提出保证
    修渠植林帮助国家建设
    【新华社北平八日电】华沙讯：“为波兰服务工作队”的青年代表团，于四月二十八日谒见贝鲁特总统时，代表参加这一工作队的一百二十万男女青年，交给贝鲁特一封信。信里说：在一九四八年，工作队的青年们为国家建筑了一千五百五十九座农村俱乐部，一千六百九十八个运动场，一千七百四十公里公路等等。青年们保证在一九四九年作工六千九百万个工作时，并提前一个月完成头半年的任务。“为波兰服务工作队”保证在今年修筑排水渠六百公里，公路三千一百公里，掘渠一千八百四十三公里，种植森林三万公顷，建筑文化机关和学校一千六百零八座。
