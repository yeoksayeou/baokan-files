Title: Rural Ballad
Author: Tian Qi
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 4

　　Rural Ballad
    Tian Qi
    One

　
    Out the door,
    Across the gully;
    The donkey carries manure,
    Climbing up the hill.
    This year we've divided the land,
    From now on, there's work to do;
    Hurry and plow hard,
    Hurry and plant!

　　Two

　
    Out the door,
    Around the bend;
    Shoulder the hoe,
    Climbing up the hill.
    Two and a half mu a day,
    Five mu in two days;
    Melons and bean pods,
    Growing lush and green.

　　Three

　
    Out the door,
    Across the river;
    Take the sickle,
    Climbing up the hillside.
    It rained in the fields,
    The crops grow well;
    This year, produce more,
    Support the army at the front!

### 农家小调
田奇
1949-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　农家小调
    田奇
    一
　
    出了门，
    过山沟；
    毛驴驮粪，
    爬上山头。
    今年分了地，
    往后有活头；
    快加劲儿耕，
    快加劲儿种！
　
　　二
　
    出了门，
    转个弯；
    抗上锄头，
    爬上山头。
    一天二亩半，
    二天锄五亩；
    瓜和豆荚荚，
    长得绿油油。
　
　　三
　
    出了门，
    过了河；
    拿了镰刀，
    爬上山坡。
    田里下了雨，
    庄稼长的好；
    今年多生产，
    支援大军到前线！
