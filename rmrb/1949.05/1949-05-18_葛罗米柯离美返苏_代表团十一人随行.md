Title: Gromyko Departs from the United States to Return to the Soviet Union; Eleven Members of the Delegation Accompany Him
Author:
Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 3

　　Gromyko Departs from the United States to Return to the Soviet Union
　　Eleven Members of the Delegation Accompany Him
　　【Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 17th】New York Dispatch: Gromyko, head of the Soviet delegation to the United Nations General Assembly, departed from here on the 15th to return to his country. Accompanying Gromyko were eleven members of the Soviet delegation to the United Nations General Assembly.

### 葛罗米柯离美返苏　代表团十一人随行

1949-05-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　葛罗米柯离美返苏
  　代表团十一人随行
    【新华社北平十七日电】纽约消息：苏联出席联大代表团团长葛罗米柯，已于十五日离此返国。与葛罗米柯同行者，尚有苏联出席联大代表团团员十一人。
