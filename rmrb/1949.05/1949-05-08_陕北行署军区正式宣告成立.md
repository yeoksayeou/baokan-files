Title: Shaanxi North Administrative Office Military Region Officially Declared Established
Author:
Date: 1949-05-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 7th. The Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government announced on the 5th that the Yan'an, Suide, Yulin, Sanbian, and Huanglong sub-regions were designated as the Shaanxi North Administrative Region. The Shaanxi North Administrative Office, the CCP Shaanxi North District Committee, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army Shaanxi North Military Region were also officially established in Yan'an and commenced operations simultaneously. The original Yan'an Sub-region Administrative Office, the CCP Yan'an Prefectural Committee, and the First Military Sub-district have been abolished. The seven counties under their jurisdiction, namely Yan'an, Yanchang, Yanchuan, Zichang, An'sai, Zhidan, and Ganquan, will now be directly under the Shaanxi North Administrative Region. The Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government has specially appointed Cao Liru as Director of the Shaanxi North Administrative Office, Li Jinglin as First Deputy Director, and Cui Tianfu as Second Deputy Director. The Northwest Military Region has appointed Zhang Dazhi as Commander of the Shaanxi North Military Region, Li Hebang as Political Commissar, Niu Shutian as First Deputy Commander, and Hu Jingduo as Second Deputy Commander.

### 陕北行署军区正式宣告成立

1949-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    陕北行署军区正式宣告成立
    【新华社延安七日电】陕甘宁边区政府于五日宣布划延属、绥德、榆林、三边、黄龙五个分区为陕北行政区，陕北行署、中共陕北区党委、中国人民解放军陕北军区亦同时宣告在延安正式成立。并开始工作。原延属分区专署、中共延安地委、第一军分区已予撤销，其所属延安、延长、延川、子长、安塞、志丹、甘泉七县，今后均直属陕北行政区。陕甘宁边区政府特任命曹力如为陕北行署主任，李景林为第一副主任，崔田夫为第二副主任。西北军区任命张达志为陕北军区司令员，李合邦为政治委员，牛书田第一副司令员，胡景铎为第二副司令员。
