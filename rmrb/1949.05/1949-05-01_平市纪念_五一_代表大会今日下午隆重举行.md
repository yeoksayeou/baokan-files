Title: Ping City Commemorates "May Day" Representative Conference Held Grandly This Afternoon
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-05-01
Page: 2

Ping City Commemorates "May Day" Representative Conference
Held Grandly This Afternoon

[Report from this newspaper] Today (the 1st) is the "May Day" Labor Day. After careful preparation and arrangements, factories, institutions, organizations, schools, and all working people in Beiping will warmly celebrate this great festival today. The working class of Beiping is especially happy because this is the first "May Day" they can freely celebrate after being liberated. The Beiping City Commemorative "May Day" Representative Conference will be held grandly this afternoon. All factories in the city will also hold celebrations in different districts and launch various publicity and entertainment activities. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and the Preparatory Committee of the Pingjin Railway Bureau Trade Union and other units have erected red colorful arches and flags in Qianmen, Tiananmen, Sanzhaomen and other places. Factories and institutions have published their own wall newspapers. All factories and institutions in the city will have a day off, and theaters will show the Soviet film "The Stone Flower" for free to workers. (Bai Sheng)

### 平市纪念“五一”代表大会今日下午隆重举行
柏生
1949-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    平市纪念“五一”代表大会
    今日下午隆重举行
    【本报讯】今（一日）日为“五一”劳动节。北平各工厂、机关、团体、学校及全市劳动人民，经过周密的准备与布置后，将于今日热烈庆祝这一伟大节日。北平工人阶级尤其高兴，因为这是他们获得解放后，第一次能够自由的庆祝的“五一”节。北平市纪念“五一”代表大会将于今日下午隆重举行。全市各工厂并将分区举行庆祝大会，展开各种宣传娱乐活动。中华全国总工会、北平市职工总会筹委会、平津铁路局职工筹委会等单位分别在前门、天安门、三座门等地扎上了红色的彩牌旗。各工厂、机关都出版了自己壁报。全市各工厂机关均放假一日，各影剧院并放映苏联名片“宝石花”免费招待工人。      （柏生）
