Title: Ten Million Jin of Grain Will Be Shipped to Beiping Successively

Author:

Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 2

A total of ten million jin of grain will be shipped to Beiping successively.
    [Report from this newspaper] The North China Trade Corporation has instructed the Sui Meng Trade Branch to allocate ten million jin of grain to the Ping Branch Corporation. Shipments have already begun successively. The first batch of 480,000 jin arrived in Beiping from Jining yesterday.
    　　(Yu)

### 一千万斤粮将陆续运平

1949-05-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　一千万斤粮将陆续运平
    【本报讯】华北贸易总公司指令绥蒙贸易分公司拨供平分公司粮食一千万斤，已陆续开始起运，首批四十八万斤业于昨日由集宁运抵北平。
    　　（宇）
