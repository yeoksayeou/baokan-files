Title: PLA Naval Academy, etc., Telegraph to the National Party's Second Fleet in Revolt: Willing to Join Hands in Building the People's Navy
Author:
Date: 1949-05-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, May 27th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Naval Academy and Deng Zhaoxiang, the former captain of the Chongqing Cruiser, and all officers and men, have telegraphed to all officers and men of the Nationalist Navy's Second Fleet who revolted on the Nanjing River. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Commander Zhang of the East China Naval Headquarters, and General Lin Zun and all naval comrades of the revolting Nationalist Navy Second Fleet:

Amidst the continuous victories of the people of the whole country, the news of your glorious uprising has arrived. This news excites and encourages us. All comrades of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Naval Academy extend our warm and sincere welcome to you and pay our highest revolutionary respect. We are willing to join hands tightly in the future, unite as one, study theory diligently under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, Chairman Mao, and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, improve our skills, serve the people wholeheartedly, and strive to build a strong Chinese People's Navy and build a new China.

Chinese People's Liberation Army Naval Academy and former Chongqing warship captain
Deng Zhaoxiang and all comrades　　　　　　　　　　May 20th

### 解放军海军学校等　电起义国民党第二舰队  愿携手共建人民海军

1949-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　解放军海军学校等
  　电起义国民党第二舰队
    愿携手共建人民海军
    【新华社北平二十七日电】中国人民解放军海军学校及前重庆号巡洋舰舰长邓兆祥暨全体官兵，顷致电在南京江面上起义的国民党海军第二舰队全体官兵，电文如下：
    华东海军司令部张司令员转起义的国民党海军第二舰队林遵将军暨海军全体同志们：
    在全国人民不断胜利中，传来你们光荣起义的消息。这消息兴奋与鼓舞着我们。我们中国人民解放军海军学校全体同志，谨向你们表示热情与真挚的欢迎，并致以崇高的革命敬礼。愿今后我们紧紧地携起手来，团结一致，在中国共产党与毛主席、朱总司令领导下，努力学习理论，提高技术，全心全意为人民服务，为建立强大的中国人民海军和为建设新中国而奋斗。
    中国人民解放军海军学校及前重庆号舰舰长
    邓兆祥暨全体同志　　　　　　　　　　五月二十日
