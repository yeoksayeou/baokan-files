Title: Exchange Rate Outside the Region is One to One Hundred Thousand
Author:
Date: 1949-05-30
Page: 2

    Exchange Rate Outside the Region is One to One Hundred Thousand
    [This Newspaper] The Bank of China's exchange rate outside the region was adjusted yesterday (29th) to one to one hundred thousand. The exchange rate for Hong Kong dollars was increased to 150 yuan.

### 区外汇兑一比十万

1949-05-30
第2版()
专栏：

    区外汇兑一比十万
    【本报讯】中国银行区外汇兑牌价昨（二十九日）调整为一比十万。外汇牌价中港币提高为一百五十元。
