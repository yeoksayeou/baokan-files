Title: Nanjing Military Control Commission Takes Over National Central University; Two Thousand Staff, Students, and Workers Participate in the Takeover

Author:
Date: 1949-05-20
Page: 1

　　Nanjing Military Control Commission
    Takes Over National Central University
  　Two Thousand Staff, Students, and Workers Participate in the Takeover
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Nanjing, 19th - The Nanjing Military Control Commission's Cultural and Educational Takeover Committee formally took over Nanjing's National Central University on the 14th of this month. On that day, military representatives convened a meeting of the university's faculty, staff, students, and workers, explaining the significance of the takeover and the Communist Party's policy towards intellectuals, hoping that everyone would actively participate in the takeover work. A public notice was issued after the meeting. The university's faculty, staff, students, and workers responded enthusiastically. As of the 16th, more than one thousand nine hundred and sixty people had joined the takeover work group. Inventory work began on the 17th, and it is expected that the takeover work will be completed within this week.

### 南京军管会  接管中央大学　两千员生工友参加接管工作

1949-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京军管会
    接管中央大学
  　两千员生工友参加接管工作
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京十九日消息：南京军事管制委员会文教接管委员会本月十四日正式接管南京中央大学。军事代表当天召集该校教职员、学生和工友开会，说明接管意义和共产党对知识分子的政策，希望大家积极参加接管工作，会后并布告号召。该校教职员、学生、工友纷纷响应，到十六日为止，已经有一千九百六十多人参加接管工作组。从十七日起，开始清点工作，预计本周内接管工作可以完成。
