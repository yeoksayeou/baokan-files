Title: Transportation Inside and Outside Nanjing Restored
Author:
Date: 1949-05-05
Page: 1
　
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 3rd: Transportation inside and outside Nanjing has been restored. The city's small train between Xiaguan and Wudingmen resumed operation on the third day after liberation, running six round trips daily. City buses have also resumed service. Small steamships sailing on the Yangtze River near various docks resumed navigation on the 27th. Ferries between Xiaguan and Pukou are constantly traveling back and forth. Telephone service throughout the city is open, and domestic and international postal deliveries have been fully restored. In addition to the Nanjing-Changzhou section of the Shanghai-Nanjing Railway, the Nanjing-Wuhu section of the Nanjing-Guixi Railway is also operating as usual.
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 4th: The Changzhou-Wuxi section of the Shanghai-Nanjing Railway was repaired on the 27th of last month, thanks to the efforts of railway workers in Changzhou and Qishuyan. Mail service between Changzhou and other newly liberated cities in southern Jiangsu has been restored, and telegram service with Beijing, Tianjin, and other places has also been opened. Long-distance telephone service is now open between Nanjing, Jurong, Jiangyin, Wuhu, Wuxi, Suzhou, and Changshu.

### 南京市内外交通恢复

1949-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    南京市内外
    交通恢复
    【新华社南京三日电】南京市内外交通已告恢复。下关至武定门间的市区小火车于解放后的第三天即恢复行驶，每日对开六次。市区公共汽车亦已复业。航行于长江内河附近各码头的小轮已于二十七日复航。下关浦口间渡船往来不绝。全市电话畅通，市内外邮递则已全部恢复。除京沪路南京至常州段已通车外，京贵（溪）铁路南京至芜湖段火车亦已照常行驶。
    【新华社南京四日电】京沪铁路常州至无锡段，由于常州、戚墅堰两地铁路工人的努力抢修，已于上月二十七日修复。常州与其余江南新解放城市已恢复通邮，与北平天津等地电报亦已开放。长途电话已畅通南京、句容、江阴、芜湖、无锡、苏州、常熟等地。
