Title: Exchange Rates for Foreign Currencies Again Adjusted

Author:

Date: 1949-05-03
Page: 2

    Exchange Rates for Foreign Currencies
    Again Adjusted
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] The Bank of China's exchange rate for foreign currency will be revised from today (3rd) to 9,000 to 1, an increase of 2,000 yuan compared to the rate of 7,000 to 1 on the 2nd.
    In addition, foreign exchange rates will also be adjusted today (3rd). The newly set exchange rates are: US$1 = 740 yuan, HK$1 = 125 yuan. That is, 740 yuan of People's Currency for one US dollar, and 125 yuan for one Hong Kong dollar.

### 区外国外汇兑均再调整

1949-05-03
第2版()
专栏：

    区外国外汇兑
    均再调整
    【本报讯】中国银行区外汇兑牌价今（三日）起改订为一比九千，较二日一比七千再上提二千元。
    又外汇牌价，亦于今（三日）日予以调整，新定牌价：美汇七四○元，港汇一二五元。即人民券七四○元对美钞一元，一二五元兑港币一元。
