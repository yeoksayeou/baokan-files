Title: Stone Jingshan Power Plant Establishes Workers' Militia
Author: Bai Qing
Date: 1949-05-07
Page: 1

    Stone Jingshan Power Plant Establishes Workers' Militia
    [Reported by This Newspaper] The Beiping branch of the Jibei Electric Power Company and its affiliated Stone Jingshan Power Plant established workers' militia at the end of April and the beginning of May respectively, in order to protect the safety of the factory and strictly prevent sabotage by special agents. The Beiping branch company, according to the different production nature of each unit, divided into groups, and a part of the people undertook air defense work. (Bai Qing)

### 石景山发电厂成立工人纠察队
柏青
1949-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    石景山发电厂成立工人纠察队
    【本报讯】冀北电力公司北平分公司及其所属之石景山发电厂，为保护工厂安全，严防特务破坏，分别于四月底及五月初成立工人纠察队。北平分公司依据各单位生产性质的不同，分成小组，并以一部人担负防空工作。（柏青）
