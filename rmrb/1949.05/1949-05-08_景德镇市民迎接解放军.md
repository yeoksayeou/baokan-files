Title: Jingdezhen Citizens Welcome the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1949-05-08
Page: 1

    【North China New China Broadcasting Station Broadcast】Before the liberation of Jingdezhen, Jiangxi Province, the local people spontaneously organized to welcome the People's Liberation Army. After the remnants of the Kuomintang bandit troops, the 68th Army and the 96th Army, fled on April 27th, more than 3,000 workers, students, and citizens throughout the city voluntarily organized to maintain order. On April 29th, when the People's Liberation Army entered Jingdezhen, they were warmly welcomed by the local people. In particular, the workers were very happy to hear of the arrival of the People's Liberation Army and voluntarily explained the policies of the Communist Party and the People's Liberation Army to the local citizens.

### 景德镇市民迎接解放军

1949-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    景德镇市民迎接解放军
    【北平新华广播电台广播】江西省景德镇在解放以前，当地人民自动组织起来迎接解放军，四月二十七号，国民党匪军六十八军和九十六军残部逃跑以后，全市工人、学生、市民三千多人自动组织起来维持秩序，二十九号，解放军进驻景德镇的时候，受到当地人民热烈欢迎，特别是工人听到解放军到来，他们都非常高兴，自动向当地市民讲解共产党解放军的政策。
