Title: Pukou-Hefei Automobile Transportation

Author: 
Date: 1949-05-15
Page: 1

    Pukou-Hefei Automobile Transportation
    [Broadcast by the Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Nanjing, 14th: The highway bridges between Pukou and Hefei were destroyed by the Kuomintang bandit troops. After active repair by the staff of the Pukou Highway Section of the First Transportation Department of the Nanjing Highway Bureau, traffic was restored on the 10th.

### 浦口合肥通汽车

1949-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

    浦口合肥通汽车
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京十四号消息：浦口到合肥间的公路桥梁曾遭国民党匪军破坏，经南京公路局第一运输处浦口公路段职工积极抢修，已经在十号恢复通车。
