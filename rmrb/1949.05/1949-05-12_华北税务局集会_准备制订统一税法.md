Title: North China Taxation Bureau Holds Meeting, Prepares to Formulate Unified Tax Law
Author:
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 2

    North China Taxation Bureau Holds Meeting
    Preparing to Formulate Unified Tax Law
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The North China Taxation Bureau convened a meeting of responsible persons from various city and district bureaus at the bureau yesterday (the 11th). In addition to reviewing the work conducted by the North China tax bureaus since their establishment, and discussing the adjustments to staffing, etc., it will also discuss various preparations for formulating a unified tax law at the upcoming Second North China Regional Taxation Work Conference.

### 华北税务局集会  准备制订统一税法

1949-05-12
第2版()
专栏：

    华北税务局集会
    准备制订统一税法
    【本报讯】华北税务局于昨日（十一日）起在该局召开各市、区局负责人会议，除检查华北各税局成立以来工作，及调整编制等进行情形外，并讨论即将召开的华北区二届税务工作会议上制定统一税法的各种准备工作。
