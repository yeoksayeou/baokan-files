Title: Clearing Out Remaining Strongholds of the Kuomintang Bandit Gang: Complete Liberation of North China; Enemy Troops in Xinxiang Surrender, Enemy Defending Anyang Entirely Annihilated; Important Stronghold of Lingshan Recovered on the Outskirts of Qingdao
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th: The remnant isolated stronghold of Xinxiang city's Kuomintang bandit troops in northern Henan surrendered to the People's Liberation Army on the 5th. The Kuomintang bandit troops besieged in Anyang city continued to resist stubbornly. The People's Liberation Army launched a general offensive at 5:30 this morning and completely annihilated the enemy by 7:00. The North China Liberated Area is now completely liberated.

[Northern Henan Dispatch] Our People's Liberation Army unit, on its way south, at the request of millions of people in northern Henan and in order to thoroughly clear out the remaining bandits in Xinxiang, has cooperated with the People's Liberation Army on the northern Henan front and completely surrounded the enemy troops entrenched in Xinxiang city on the evening of the 3rd. The people in the suburbs of the city are all jubilant and are enthusiastically supporting our army. In order to minimize losses to the people of Xinxiang, our army has issued a notice to the isolated Kuomintang bandit troops of the 40th Army, ordering them to surrender quickly, move out of the city, and await reorganization. Otherwise, a general offensive will be launched against the city to completely annihilate them.

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 6th: The People's Liberation Army on the outskirts of Qingdao and Jimo recovered Lingshan, an important enemy stronghold 35 li north of Jimo city, on the 3rd, and pursued the fleeing enemy, killing a total of 140 enemies and capturing 156. They seized 14 light and heavy machine guns, 104 rifles of various lengths, and other items.

### 扫清国民党匪帮残余据点  华北完全解放  新乡敌军投降安阳守敌全歼  青岛外围收复重要据点灵山

1949-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    扫清国民党匪帮残余据点
    华北完全解放
    新乡敌军投降安阳守敌全歼
    青岛外围收复重要据点灵山
    【新华社北平六日电】豫北国民党匪军残余孤立据点新乡城的守军五日向解放军投降。困守安阳城的国民党匪军继续顽抗。解放军乃于今日上午五时半发起总攻，至七时将敌全部歼灭。华北解放区至此完全解放。
    【豫北电】我人民解放军某部在南进途中，应豫北数百万人民之请，并为澈底扫清新乡残匪，已配合豫北前线人民解放军，于三日晚上将龟缩新乡城之敌军完全包围。城郊人民莫不欢腾鼓舞，正热烈支援我军。为了新乡人民少受损失，我军已向孤岛国民党匪军四十军发出通牒，令其迅速投降，开出城外，听候改编。否则，即向该城发动总攻，予以彻底歼灭。
    【新华社济南六日电】青岛、即墨外围之解放军，于三日收复即墨城北三十五里的敌重要据点灵山、并追击逃敌，共毙敌一百四十名，俘敌一百五十六名。缴获轻重机枪十四挺，长短枪一百零四支，其他物品一部。
