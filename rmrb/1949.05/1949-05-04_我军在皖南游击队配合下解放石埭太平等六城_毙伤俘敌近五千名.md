Title: Our Army, with the Cooperation of the South Anhui Guerrillas, Liberated Six Cities Including Shitai and Taiping, Killing, Wounding and Capturing Nearly Five Thousand Enemies

Author: 
Date: 1949-05-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Jiangnan Front, 3rd: A unit of the People's Liberation Army, in coordination with the South Anhui People's Guerrillas, successively liberated six county towns, including Shitai, Taiping, and the previously reported Xiuning, Yixian, Qimen, and Shexian, within four days from April 25th to 28th. The enemy annihilated included the 96th Army officer corps, the 635th Regiment of the 212th Division of that army, and the pseudo-security forces of Shitai, Taiping, Jixi and other counties. A total of over 4,830 enemy soldiers were captured alive, over 130 enemy soldiers were killed or wounded, and over 30 light and heavy machine guns, over 580 rifles and carbines, and 2 six-zero mortars were seized. More than 200 members of the Shitai County Government Guard, carrying 6 light machine guns and more than 1,090 rifles, surrendered to the People's Liberation Army.

### 我军在皖南游击队配合下解放石埭太平等六城  毙伤俘敌近五千名

1949-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我军在皖南游击队配合下解放石埭太平等六城
    毙伤俘敌近五千名
    【新华社江南前线三日电】人民解放军某部在皖南人民游击队配合下，于四月二十五日至二十八日四天内先后解放石埭、太平及连前已报道的休宁、黟县、祁门、歙县等共六座县城。歼敌九十六军军官队、该军二一二师之六三五团及石埭、太平、绩溪等县伪保安部队等。总计生俘敌四千八百三十余名，毙伤敌一百三十余名，缴获轻重机枪三十挺，步马枪五百八十余支，六零炮二门。石埭县政府卫队二百余人，携轻机枪六挺，步枪一千零九十余支，向解放军投诚。
