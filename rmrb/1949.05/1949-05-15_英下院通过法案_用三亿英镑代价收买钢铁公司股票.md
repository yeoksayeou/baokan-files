Title: British House of Commons Passes Bill to Buy Steel Company Stock for 300 Million Pounds

Author:

Date: 1949-05-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th - According to the Associated Press, London: The British House of Commons passed the British steel industry "nationalization" bill on the 9th. The bill has now been submitted to the House of Lords for discussion. The bill stipulates the use of a huge sum of 300 million pounds to "purchase" the stocks of 107 steel companies. The actual management of these steel industries "nationalized" by the purchasers will still be "carried out by the people currently running it."

### 英下院通过法案  用三亿英镑代价收买钢铁公司股票

1949-05-15
第3版()
专栏：

    英下院通过法案
    用三亿英镑代价收买钢铁公司股票
    【新华社北平十三日电】据美联社伦敦讯：英下院于九日通过英钢铁业“国有化”法案。该案现已送交上院讨论。该法案规定以三亿英镑的巨额代价，“购买”一百零七家钢铁公司的股票。这些被购者归“国有”的钢铁业的实际经理工作将仍由“目前经营它的人们进行”。
