Title: I and the Delegation Arrive in Shenyang from Harbin; 50,000 People Welcome Us
Author:
Date: 1949-05-20
Page: 1

　　【Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station】 Shenyang, 19th—The first group of over thirty members of our country's delegation who returned victoriously from the World Congress of Defenders of Peace, led by delegation head Guo Moruo, arrived in Shenyang from Harbin by special train at 10:00 AM yesterday. Comrades Gao Gang and Li Fuchun of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Shenyang Mayor Zhu Qiwen, Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions Zhang Weizhen, and 50,000 workers, youth, women, and cultural groups went to the station to welcome them. After the delegation got off the train, they shook hands with the welcomers amidst the loud military music and songs, and accepted flowers from thirty-one female students. Immediately afterward, a grand welcoming meeting was held in the station square.

### 我和大代表团由哈抵沈　五万人欢迎

1949-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　我和大代表团由哈抵沈
  　五万人欢迎
    【北平新华广播电台广播】沈阳十九号消息：参加世界拥护和平大会胜利归来的我国代表团第一批三十多人，在团长郭沫若率领下，昨天上午十点钟乘专车从哈尔滨到达沈阳。中共中央东北局高岗李富春同志，沈阳市长朱其文，东北职工总会主席张维桢和工人、青年、妇女、文化团体五万人到车站欢迎，代表团下车后，在响亮的军乐声和歌声中和欢迎者握手，并且接受三十一个女学生的献花，随即在车站广场举行盛大欢迎会。
