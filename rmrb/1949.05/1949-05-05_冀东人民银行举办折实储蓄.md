Title: Jidong People's Bank Launches Indexed Savings
Author:
Date: 1949-05-05
Page: 2
    [Tangshan Electric] The Jidong Branch of the People's Bank of China has launched indexed savings starting from the 28th. The indexed calculation is based on the "standard commodity unit." One "standard commodity unit" includes one jin of Victory vermicelli, one jin of corn, and one chi of Three Swallows white cloth produced by Huaxin Textile Factory, the total price of these three fixed-quantity items. Tangshan has a large number of workers who live on salaries, and the launch of indexed savings will further stabilize their lives.

### 冀东人民银行举办折实储蓄

1949-05-05
第2版()
专栏：

    冀东人民银行举办折实储蓄
    【唐山电】中国人民银行冀东分行自二十八日起，举办折旧储蓄，折实计算以“标准实物单位”为准，一个“标准实物单位”包括胜利通粉一斤，玉米一斤，华新纱厂所出三燕白布一尺，三种定量物品的价格总和。唐山有广大靠薪给为生的职工，折实储蓄的举办将使他们的生活更趋安定。
