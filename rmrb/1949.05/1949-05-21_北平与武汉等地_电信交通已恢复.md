Title: Telecommunications and Transportation Restored in Beiping and Wuhan, etc.
Author:
Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 2

Telecommunications and Transportation Restored in Beiping and Wuhan, etc.

[This Newspaper's Dispatch] Commercial telegrams have been restored to twenty-six newly liberated cities in Beiping and Central China: namely Hankou, Wuchang, Hanyang, Anqing, Jiujiang, Fuliang, Poyang, Shangrao, Jiangshan, Yongkang, Lishui, Jiaxing, Jiashan, Shaoxing, Chang'an, Deqing, Xiaoshan, Yuhang, Haining (formerly 硖石), Wenzhou, Lanxi, Quzhou, Jinhua, Tunxi, Qimen, and Gutoutan. Furthermore, telegrams to and from Hong Kong have resumed transmission and reception since the 19th, but are limited to plain language. Chinese characters are charged at 40 centimes per character, and foreign characters at 80 centimes per character, calculated in Renminbi. Translation fees are charged according to domestic telegram rates. All sectors are requested to take note and utilize these services. (Telecommunications Bureau)

### 北平与武汉等地　电信交通已恢复
电信局
1949-05-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　北平与武汉等地
  　电信交通已恢复
    【本报讯】北平和华中新解放区又有二十六个城市的商报已然通畅：计汉口、武昌、汉阳、安庆、九江、浮梁、鄱阳、上饶、江山、永康、丽水、嘉兴、嘉善、绍兴、长安、德清、萧山、余杭、硖石、温州、兰溪、衢州、金华、屯溪、祁门、古鳌头。又发往香港电报自十九日起恢复收发，惟限用明语，华文每字四十生丁，洋文每字八十生丁，折合人民券收费，译费照国内电报收取，请各界人士注意利用。（电信局）
