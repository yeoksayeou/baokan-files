Title: Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region: Abolishing the Jin-Northwest Administrative Office, Establishing the Wuzhai Central Special District Office
Author:
Date: 1949-05-22
Page: 1

Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region

　　Abolishing the Jin-Northwest Administrative Office, Establishing the Wuzhai Central Special District Office

    [Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 21st] The Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government has recently decided to abolish the Jin-Northwest Administrative Office and establish the Wuzhai Central Special District Office, which will be under the leadership of the Jin-South Administrative Office. It will govern the Yan-South, Yan-North, and Lishi Special Districts, as well as other directly affiliated counties. Zhao Bingyi has been appointed as the Commissioner of the Central Special District Office, and Qiao Zhongyun as the Deputy Commissioner.

### 陕甘宁边区　撤销晋西北行署  成立五寨中心专署

1949-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕甘宁边区
  　撤销晋西北行署  成立五寨中心专署
    【新华社延安二十一日电】陕甘宁边区政府顷决定撤销晋西北行署，成立五寨中心专署，受晋南行署领导，下辖雁南、雁北、离石等专署及其他直属县，并任命赵秉彝为中心专署专员，乔钟云为副专员。
