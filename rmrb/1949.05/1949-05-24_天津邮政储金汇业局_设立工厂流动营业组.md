Title: Tianjin Postal Savings and Remittance Bureau Establishes Factory Mobile Business Group
Author:
Date: 1949-05-24
Page: 2

　　Tianjin Postal Savings and Remittance Bureau
　　　Establishes Factory Mobile Business Group
　　[This Newspaper's Report] The Tianjin Postal Savings and Remittance Bureau has submitted a request to the North China Postal and Telecommunications General Bureau to establish mobile business groups within factories in Tianjin. It is tentatively planned to send personnel to factories on each factory's payday to handle the development of draft business, in order to facilitate employees' remittances and develop business. The Postal and Telecommunications General Bureau also intends to notify various industrial cities of this method and promote its implementation as necessary, to further serve the broad masses of workers.

### 天津邮政储金汇业局　设立工厂流动营业组

1949-05-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　天津邮政储金汇业局
  　设立工厂流动营业组
    【本报讯】天津邮政储金汇业局，现已呈汇华北邮电总局，于津市各工厂内设立流动营业组，暂定每逢各工厂发薪日期，派员赴厂办理开发汇票事务，以便利职工汇寄款项与发展业务。邮电总局并拟将此办法通知各工业城市，按必要情形，推广办理，进一步为广大职工服务。
