Title: Provisional Working Committee of the China Democratic League in Beiping Holds General Meeting of League Members, Sends Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author:
Date: 1949-05-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 16th—The Provisional Working Committee of the China Democratic League in Beiping held a general meeting of league members at 9:00 AM yesterday (the 15th) in the Zi Min Hall of Peking University. The number of attendees exceeded two-thirds of the league members in Beiping. The meeting elected Huang Yanpei, Zhou Xinmin, Xin Zhichao, Chen Qiyuan, Wen Jiasi, Wu Han, and Du Renzhi as the presidium. After announcing the opening of the meeting, Shen Junru and Zhang Bojun, responsible Standing Committee members of the headquarters, delivered speeches, detailing the consistent political line of the China Democratic League and the current working principles. Wu Han reported on the arduous underground work carried out by the Provisional Municipal Branch of the China Democratic League over the past three years. He finally stated that since the liberation of Beiping, League comrades have been able to see the light of day again. The municipal branch of the China Democratic League can conduct public activities in a free atmosphere, and should thank the Chinese Communist Party and the People's Liberation Army. Therefore, the meeting resolved to send a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu. Finally, elections were held for the executive committee of the Beiping Municipal Branch. Wu Han, Wen Jiasi, Chen Dingwen, Du Renzhi, Guan Shixiong, Shen Yifan, Zhang Manjun, Zeng Zhaolun, and Li Helin were elected as executive committee members, and Yan Xinmin and Li Wenyi were elected as alternate executive committee members. After a luncheon, the meeting adjourned at 1:00 PM. The telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu stated briefly: Thanks to the correct leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the heroic and skillful fighting of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, all of China is about to be liberated. The final and comprehensive victory of the people's great revolution has been guaranteed. With the victorious progress of the revolutionary military, the construction of a new democratic China has become the urgent task of the Chinese people. As the political center of a new people's democratic China, Beiping's economic and cultural construction has special significance. Under the guidance of the Central Committee of the League, all comrades of the Beiping Municipal Branch of the League are willing to continue to accept the guidance of your party and Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, unite with all democratic parties and revolutionary people from all walks of life, cooperate and coordinate, and strive to build a new people's Beiping and a new people's China.

### 民盟平市临时工委会　开盟员大会　电毛主席朱总司令致敬

1949-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　民盟平市临时工委会
  　开盟员大会
  　电毛主席朱总司令致敬
    【新华社北平十六日电】中国民主同盟北平市临时工作委员会于昨日（十五日）上午九时，假北京大学孑民堂，召开市盟员大会，到会人数超过在平盟员三分之二以上。大会推举黄炎培、周新民、辛志超、陈其瑗、闻家驷、吴晗、杜任之为主席团。宣布开会后，由总部负责常委沈钧儒、章伯钧致词，详述民盟一贯的政治路线，及现阶段工作方针。吴晗报告三年来民盟临时市支部进行地下工作艰苦奋斗情形。最后说明北平解放以后，民盟同志得重见天日。民盟市支部可以在自由空气中，进行公开活动，应当感谢中国共产党和人民解放军。于是大会决议电毛主席及朱总司令致敬。最后进行北平市支部执行委员选举。吴晗、闻家驷、陈鼎文、杜任之、关世雄、沈一帆、张曼筠、曾昭抡、李何林九人当选执委，严信民、李文宜二人当选候补执委。午后一时到会盟员聚餐后散会。大会给毛主席朱总司令致敬电略称：由于中国共产党的正确领导，和中国人民解放军的英勇善战，全中国即将获得解放，人民大革命的最后全面胜利，已经有了保证，随着革命军事的胜利进展，新民主主义新中国的建设，已成为中国人民目前的迫切任务。作为一个人民民主新中国政治中心的北平，其经济文化建设，尤有特殊的意义，本盟北平市全体同志，在本盟中央指导之下，愿继续接受贵党和毛主席朱总司令的指导，团结各民主党派，各阶层革命人士，协同配合，为建设人民的新北平，建设人民的新中国而努力奋斗。
