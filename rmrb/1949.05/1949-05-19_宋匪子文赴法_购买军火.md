Title: Traitor Song Ziwen Goes to France to Purchase Arms

Author: 
Date: 1949-05-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 18th: Hong Kong Dispatch: Song Ziwen, one of the heads of the Four Big Families and a war criminal, departed for France on the 16th. The purpose of Song's trip to France is to purchase arms for the Kuomintang bandit gang and to participate in discussions on the "Pacific Pact," further colluding with imperialist aggressor groups.

### 宋匪子文赴法　购买军火

1949-05-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　宋匪子文赴法
  　购买军火
    【新华社北平十八日电】香港消息：四大家族首领之一战争罪犯宋子文已于十六日启程赴法。宋贼赴法的目的是为了替国民党匪帮购买军火，并参加“太平洋公约”的讨论，进一步勾结帝国主义侵略集团。
