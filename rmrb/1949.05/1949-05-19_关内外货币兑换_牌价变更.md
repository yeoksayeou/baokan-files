Title: Exchange Rate Change Between Inside and Outside the Customs
Author:
Date: 1949-05-19
Page: 2

Exchange Rate Between Inside and Outside the Customs
　　Price Change
    [Our Newspaper] The exchange rate between the currency inside and outside the customs has been changed to one RMB yuan for 220 Northeast or Great Wall currency, starting from May 16th.

### 关内外货币兑换　牌价变更

1949-05-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　关内外货币兑换
  　牌价变更
    【本报讯】关内外货币兑换，自五月十六日起改为人民券一元兑换东北或长城币二百二十元。
