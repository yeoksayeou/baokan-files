Title: Hangzhou Xinhua Bookstore Sells 5,000 Copies on First Day
Author:
Date: 1949-05-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, May 22nd. The Zhejiang Xinhua Bookstore in Hangzhou officially opened for business on the 16th of this month, and sold more than 5,000 copies of various books on the first day. On that day, two hours before opening time, groups of students, staff, teachers, and citizens of Hangzhou crowded at the bookstore door, requesting to open earlier. Half an hour after the store opened, all thirty-five hardcover copies of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" that were put out were snapped up. Among all kinds of books, Chairman Mao's various works are the most popular. After the bookstore closed at 6:00 p.m., people continued to come to buy books despite the rain.

### 杭州新华书店第一天售出五千册

1949-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　杭州新华书店第一天售出五千册
    【新华社杭州二十二日电】杭州市的浙江新华书店，已于本月十六日起正式营业，第一天即售出各种书籍五千余册。是日，在营业时间前二小时，杭市成群的学生、职员、教员和市民，就拥挤在书店门口，要求提早营业。该店开门后半小时，摆出的三十五本精装的“毛泽东选集”即被抢购一空。在各种书籍中毛主席的各种名著最受欢迎。是日下午六时，书店停止营业后，冒着雨仍不断有人来要求买书。
