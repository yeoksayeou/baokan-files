Title: Sixteen Hundred Tianjin Workers' Representatives Gather to Commemorate "May Day"; Comrade Li Lisan Attends and Speaks

Author:
Date: 1949-05-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 2nd. Over sixteen hundred workers and worker representatives from North China gathered in Tianjin yesterday to commemorate "May Day." Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Huang Kecheng, Director of the Tianjin Military Control Commission, Huang Jing, Mayor of Tianjin, and others attended the meeting. Huang Huoqing, Director of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee, gave the opening address, followed by a speech by Li Lisan. In his speech, he explained in detail the policy of developing production and benefiting both labor and capital, and pointed out that only by developing production can the living standards of all the people be improved. He called on everyone to study the resolutions of the Sixth National Labor Congress and Comrade Liu Shaoqi's report at the Tianjin Workers' Congress. Mayor Huang Jing called on workers in public factories to strengthen unity, workers in private factories should implement the policy of benefiting both labor and capital, work hard to produce, and support the front lines. Wang Liankai, a worker from the waterworks who successfully manufactured water meters, Huo Yinling, a representative of Tianjin staff, and Ling Biying, a representative of North China workers, also spoke at the meeting. Finally, the meeting passed a telegram of salute to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the People's Liberation Army on the Yangtze River front before closing.

### 津市职工代表千六百人  集会纪念“五一”  李立三同志亲往讲话

1949-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    津市职工代表千六百人
    集会纪念“五一”
    李立三同志亲往讲话
    【新华社天津二日电】天津市职工及华北各地职工代表一千六百余人于昨日集会纪念“五一”。中华全国总工会副主席李立三、天津市军管会主任黄克诚、天津市市长黄敬等均亲临参加。大会由天津市职工会筹委会主任黄火青致开会词后，继由李立三讲话。他在讲话中详细说明了发展生产、劳资两利的方针，并指出只有发展生产，才能提高全体人民的生活水平。他号召大家学习中国第六次劳动代表大会的决议和刘少奇同志在天津市职工代表大会上的报告。黄敬市长在讲话中号召公营工厂职工要加强团结，私营工厂职工应贯彻劳资两利的方针，努力生产，支援前线。大会继有自来水厂自制水表成功的工人王连凯、津市职员代表霍荫龄、华北职工代表凌必应等讲话。最后大会通过致电毛主席、朱总司令及长江前线人民解放军致敬后闭幕。
