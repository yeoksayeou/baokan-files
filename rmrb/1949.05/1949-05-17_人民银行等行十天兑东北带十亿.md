Title: People's Bank and Other Banks Exchange Ten Billion Northeast Coins in Ten Days
Author:
Date: 1949-05-17
Page: 2

    The People's Bank of China and other banks have been exchanging Northeast currency and Great Wall currency since the 4th. As of the 15th, a total of 1,000,755,195.75 Northeast currency and 202,845,800 Great Wall currency have been exchanged. (Yu)

### 人民银行等行十天兑东北带十亿

1949-05-17
第2版()
专栏：

    人民银行等行十天兑东北带十亿
    【本报讯】平人民、中国等银行自四日兑换东北币、长城币，截至十五日止共兑入东北币十亿零七千五百一十九万五千七百五十元、长城币二亿零二百八十四万五千八百元。（宇）
