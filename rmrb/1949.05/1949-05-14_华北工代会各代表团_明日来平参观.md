Title: North China Workers' Congress Delegations to Visit Beiping Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-05-14
Page: 2

    North China Workers' Congress Delegations
    to Visit Beiping Tomorrow
    [Our Newspaper] The delegations attending the North China Workers' Representative Conference visited the No. 1 Textile Factory of Tientsin Industrial and Mining Group, the Telecommunications Equipment Factory, the Tianjin Steel Plant, and the private Dongya Enterprise Company in turn yesterday and today (13th and 14th). When the delegates' cars arrived at each factory, they were warmly welcomed by the workers of each factory. It is reported that the delegations are scheduled to come to Beiping tomorrow (15th).

### 华北工代会各代表团  明日来平参观

1949-05-14
第2版()
专栏：

    华北工代会各代表团
    明日来平参观
    【本报讯】参加华北职工代表会议各代表团，昨今（十三、十四）两日轮流参观天津团营中纺一厂、电讯器材工厂、天津炼钢厂、私营东亚企业公司。当代表们的汽车抵达各厂时，受到各厂职工的热烈欢迎。据息，各代表团定明（十五）日来平。
