Title: Xinhua News Agency Headquarters Telegram to All Branch Offices and Bookstores
Author:
Date: 1949-05-10
Page: 1

To all newspaper offices, news agencies, and bookstores: The Subei Daily was launched on May 1st. It is hoped that you will send one to three copies of your publications to the newspaper office.
    Xinhua News Agency, May 9th

### 新华总社致各分社书店电

1949-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    新华总社致各分社书店电
    各报社、通讯社、书店：苏北日报已于五一创刊，希将你处出版物寄赠该社一份至三份。    新华总社  九日
