Title: North China Workers' Representatives Visit the Forbidden City and Beihai Park
Author:
Date: 1949-05-20
Page: 2

　　North China Workers' Representatives Visit the Forbidden City and Beihai Park
　　[This newspaper's report] The North China Workers' Representatives Visiting Group toured the Forbidden City, Beihai Park, and other places yesterday (the 19th) during the day. In the evening, they attended the second reception hosted by the preparatory committee of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions and other municipal organizations. Comrade Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee, also took time to attend.

### 华北职工代表参观故宫北海

1949-05-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北职工代表参观故宫北海
    【本报讯】华北职工代表参观团，昨日（十九日）白天到故宫、北海等处游览，晚间参加北平市职工总会筹委会等市级各机关团体第二次招待晚会，北平市委书记彭真同志也抽空前来参加。
