Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party to Convene Ninth National Congress; Gifts from Workers Nationwide Valued at Over 1.1 Billion Czech Koruna
Author:
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 10th: According to a Prague dispatch from the Electrotechnical News Agency: The Czechoslovak Communist Party will convene its Ninth National Congress on the 25th of this month. Gifts from workers across the republic to the Congress are valued at a total of 1,139 million Czech Koruna. These gifts were achieved by workers through reducing production costs, improving labor techniques and efficiency, and exceeding production targets.

### 捷克斯洛伐克共产党将召开九届代表大会  全国工人所献礼物值十一亿余捷币

1949-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克共产党将召开九届代表大会
    全国工人所献礼物值十一亿余捷币
    【新华社北平十日电】据电通社布拉格讯：本月二十五日，捷克斯洛伐克共产党将召开第九届代表大会。全共和国工人献给大会的礼物共值十一亿三千九百万捷币。这些礼物是工人们用减低生产成本、提高劳动技术和劳动效率超过生产目标所达成。
