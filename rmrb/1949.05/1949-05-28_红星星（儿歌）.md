Title: Red Star (Children's Song)
Author: Tian Qi
Date: 1949-05-28
Page: 4

　　Red Star (Children's Song)
    Tian Qi
    Red star,
    Little red flower.
    Little brother sees it
    And rushes to grab it,
    Little sister sees it
    And rushes to snatch it;
    Older sister sees it
    And comes to pull them apart:
    "Little brother,
    Don't grab it;
    Little sister,
    Don't snatch it;
    This is Dad's flower,
    Dad wants to take it,
    Take it to join the army,
    To cross the river and fight old Chiang;
    Defeat old Chiang and return home,
    I'll bring you two
    A pile of little red flowers!"

### 红星星（儿歌）
田奇
1949-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　红星星（儿歌）
    田奇
    红星星，
    小红花。
    弟弟看见
    抢着拿，
    妹妹看见
    抢着抓；
    姐姐看见
    来把他俩拉：
    “小弟弟，
    你别拿；
    小妹妹，
    你甭抓；
    这是爹的花，
    爹爹要带它，
    带上去参军，
    过江打老蒋；
    打垮老蒋返回家，
    给你俩
    拿回一堆小红花！”
