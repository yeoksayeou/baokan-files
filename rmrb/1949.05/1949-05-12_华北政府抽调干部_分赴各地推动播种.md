Title: North China Government Draws Cadres to Promote Sowing in Various Regions
Author: Xing Yi
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 2

North China Government Draws Cadres
To Promote Sowing in Various Regions

[Reported by this newspaper] To effectively promote sowing, inspect water conservancy construction, and provide specific assistance in eliminating pests, the Agricultural Department of the North China People's Government has specially selected sixteen administrative cadres and technical cadres. Together with more than thirty teachers and students from the North China University Agricultural College, they have organized production work groups and, since April 27th, have successively departed for areas including Eastern Hebei, Taiyuan, Southern Hebei, Hebei-Shandong-Henan, Taihang, Central Hebei, and Suiyuan to carry out work.

### 华北政府抽调干部  分赴各地推动播种
邢毅
1949-05-12
第2版()
专栏：

    华北政府抽调干部
    分赴各地推动播种
    【本报讯】华北人民政府农业部，为切实推动播种及检查水利兴修、具体帮助扑灭虫害等工作，特抽调行政干部、技术干部十六名，偕同华大农学院师生三十余名，组织生产工作组，于上月二十七日，先后分赴冀东、太原、冀南、冀鲁豫、太行、冀中、绥远等地进行工作。     
   （邢毅）
