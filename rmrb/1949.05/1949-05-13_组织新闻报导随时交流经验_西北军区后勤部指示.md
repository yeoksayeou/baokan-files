Title: Organize News Reports and Exchange Experiences at Any Time: Instruction from the Logistics Department of the Northwest Military Region
Author:
Date: 1949-05-13
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 11th. The Logistics Department of the Northwest Military Region instructed its subordinate units' leaders on the 5th to organize news reporting work and use this as one of their leadership methods. The instruction said: Exemplary events and experiences in the work of various departments should be published in newspapers and promoted; shortcomings and lessons in the work should be promptly used through news reports to carry out criticism and self-criticism, arousing everyone's vigilance. In order to further coordinate the logistics work with the needs of the rapidly developing victorious situation, all units must establish and strengthen communication and reporting work and exchange experiences at any time.

### 组织新闻报导随时交流经验  西北军区后勤部指示

1949-05-13
第3版()
专栏：

    组织新闻报导随时交流经验
    西北军区后勤部指示
    【新华社延安十一日电】西北军区后勤部，五日指示所属各单位领导和组织新闻报道工作，并以此作为自己的领导方法之一。指示中说：对于各部门工作中的模范事例和经验，要在报上刊登，加以推广；对于工作中的缺点和教训，应该随时通过新闻报导，展开批评和自我批评，引起大家警惕。为了使后勤工作进一步地配合胜利形势迅速发展的需要，各单位必须建立和加强通讯报导工作，随时交流经验。
