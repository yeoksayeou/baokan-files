Title: Four Foreign Ministers Conference to Open Soon; Soviet, U.S., and British Foreign Ministers Arrive in France
Author:
Date: 1949-05-23
Page: 3

　　Four Foreign Ministers Conference to Open Soon
  　Soviet, U.S., and British Foreign Ministers Arrive in France
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd: Paris dispatch: The Four Foreign Ministers Conference will open on the 23rd. Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky arrived in Paris by military aircraft on the 21st. Another plane also arrived in Paris on the same day, carrying General Chuikov and fifty Soviet experts and technicians. U.S. Secretary of State Acheson arrived in Paris by plane on the 22nd. British Foreign Secretary Bevin arrived in Paris by ship on the 19th.

### 四外长会议即将开幕　苏美英三外长抵法

1949-05-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　四外长会议即将开幕
  　苏美英三外长抵法
    【新华社北平二十二日电】巴黎讯：二十三日四国外长会议即将开幕。苏联外长维辛斯基已于二十一日乘军用机飞抵巴黎，另机一架，也在同日飞抵巴黎，其中有崔可夫将军和苏联专家、技术人员五十人。美国国务卿艾奇逊已于二十二日乘机抵巴黎。英外长贝文则已于十九日乘轮船抵巴黎。
