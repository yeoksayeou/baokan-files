Title: South China Guerrilla Representatives Present Flags to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author:
Date: 1949-05-05
Page: 1

    South China Guerrilla Representatives
    Present Flags to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu
    [This newspaper dispatch] At the First National Congress of the Chinese Youth, representatives of the People's Liberation Army South China Guerrilla presented flags to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu. The flag presented to Chairman Mao read: "Resolutely under your banner, wipe out the remnants of the enemy in South China." The flag presented to Commander-in-Chief Zhu and the People's Liberation Army expressed: "We resolutely carry out your orders, cooperate with you, and complete the final battle for victory." Li Yuzhen, a representative of the South China Guerrilla, also presented a speech to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu at the meeting, guaranteeing "to resolutely implement your instructions, develop our armed forces well, and welcome the southward advance of the large army to liberate the whole of South China." The representatives mentioned that the South China Guerrilla "correctly implemented Chairman Mao's Ten Military Principles, closely relied on the people of South China," and developed their forces thirty times in the past two years, "and in some places, they also carried out rent reduction and interest rate reduction." All the representatives applauded and congratulated them, shouting loudly. Comrade Liao Chengzhi read out a congratulatory telegram sent to the congress by the Nanjing Youth Federation. The Nanjing Youth Federation was established this year on May 1st. When Comrade Chengzhi read "We are pleased to hear that your conference has opened in peace, and we are unable to send representatives to participate. We send a special telegram of congratulations and wish the Chinese youth unity a long life," the entire audience applauded enthusiastically again, and the representatives expressed their greatest congratulations to the youth of this newly liberated city. The Northeast delegation also presented flags to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the North China People's Cultural Troupe specially presented flags to the congress, with the words "Petrel of the New Era" written on them. They also performed waist drums at the meeting to congratulate the representatives.

### 华南游击队代表  向毛主席朱总司令献旗

1949-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    华南游击队代表
    向毛主席朱总司令献旗
    【本报讯】在中华全国青年第一次代表大会上，人民解放军华南游击队代表向毛主席、朱总司令献旗。在献给毛主席的锦旗上写道：“坚决在你的旗帜下，肃清华南残敌。”献给朱总司令和人民解放军的锦旗上，表示：“我们坚决执行你的命令，配合你们，完成最后胜利一战。”华南游击队的代表李玉珍并在会上向毛主席、朱总司令献词，保证“坚决的执行你们的指示，好好的发展我们的武装力量，迎接大军南下，解放全华南。”代表们谈到华南游击队“正确的执行了毛主席的军事十大原则，紧紧的依靠着华南人民”，把自己的队伍在二年来发展了三十倍，“在有些地方，还进行了减租减息”。全体代表一齐鼓掌致贺，高声欢呼。廖承志同志宣读南京市青年联合会向大会发来的贺电。南京青联会是今年“五一”成立的。承志同志读到“欣闻贵会在平揭幕，未克推派代表参加，特电致贺，并祝中国青年团结万岁”，全场又一次狂热鼓掌，代表们对这一新解放城市的青年，表示了最大的贺意。东北代表团也向毛主席、朱总司令献旗，华北人民文工团特向大会献旗，旗上写着“新时代的海燕”。并在会上表演腰鼓，向代表致贺。
