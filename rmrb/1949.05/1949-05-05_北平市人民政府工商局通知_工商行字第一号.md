    Notice from the Bureau of Industry and Commerce of the People's Government of Beiping Municipality
    Industry and Commerce Document No. 1
    In order to restore and develop production and to provide guidance to industrial and commercial businesses, the People's Government of Beiping Municipality has decided to conduct a general registration of all public and private industrial and commercial enterprises in the city. This was announced on the 3rd of this month in Industry and Commerce Document No. 1. Following this, this Bureau immediately convened meetings with various trade associations to study the matter. As a result, it has been decided that the Bureau of Industry and Commerce will convene general meetings of all industries to convey the government's spirit and methods for conducting the general registration. The scheduled times and locations for the meetings are as follows:
    Weaving and Dyeing Industry: 2:00 PM on the 7th at the Beiping Chamber of Commerce Auditorium
    Machinery and Metal Manufacturing Industry: 2:00 PM on the 8th at the Beiping Chamber of Commerce Auditorium
    Knitting Industry: 2:00 PM on the 9th at the Beiping Chamber of Commerce Auditorium
    Factories and workshops that have joined the respective trade associations, as well as those that have not joined trade associations, are requested to send responsible persons to the meetings at the times and locations specified above to facilitate the process.
    This is hereby notified.
        May 5, 1949

### 北平市人民政府工商局通知  工商行字第一号

1949-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    北平市人民政府工商局通知
    工商行字第一号
    北平市人民政府为恢复并发展生产，予各工商业户以方向指导起见，决定全市公私营工商业进行普遍登记，业于本月三日以工商字第一号通告公布在案。本局遵即召集各该业同业公会研究结果，决定由工商局召开各业全体大会，传达政府进行普遍登记之精神及办法，兹决定大会召开时间及地点如下：
    织染业：七日下午二时  在北平市商会礼堂
    机器业及金属冶制业：八日下午二时  在北平市商会礼堂
    针织业：九日下午二时  在北平市商会礼堂
    希已参加各该业同业公会之工厂作坊及未参加同业公会之工厂作坊，均照上开时间、地点，派负责人到会参加，以利进行。特此通知
        中华民国三十八年五月五日
