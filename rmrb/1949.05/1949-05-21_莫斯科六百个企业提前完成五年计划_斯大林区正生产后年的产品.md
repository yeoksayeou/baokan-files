Title: Six Hundred Moscow Enterprises Complete Five-Year Plan Ahead of Schedule; Stalin District Already Producing Products for 1951
Author:
Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th -- The great goal of Soviet workers completing the Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule is gradually being realized. According to TASS: Six hundred enterprises in Moscow have completed their Five-Year Plan, and the Stalin District, one of Moscow's largest industrial areas, has also completed its Five-Year Plan. These factories and industrial areas are now producing products for 1951.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th -- Moscow News: The movement to produce first-class products initiated by Zhutkike, a worker at the Red Holm Textile Factory in Moscow, has rapidly developed into a huge scale. Ninety-nine percent of the Red Holm Textile Factory's daily products are now first-class goods. The number of workers participating in this movement in various industries has now increased to hundreds of thousands.

Two Stakhanovite workers from the Moscow Oblast Fine Cloth Factory, Rozhneva and Kononenko, recently launched a new patriotic movement to produce extra products with saved raw materials. The Moscow Oblast Fine Cloth Factory has already started this production. This initiative has been widely responded to in the light industry of Moscow and other cities in the Soviet Union.

### 莫斯科六百个企业提前完成五年计划  斯大林区正生产后年的产品

1949-05-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科六百个企业
  　提前完成五年计划
    斯大林区正生产后年的产品
    纺织业产品　全是头等货
    【新华社北平二十日电】苏联工人提前完成五年计划的伟大目标，正在逐步实现。据塔斯社讯：莫斯科已有六百个企业完成了五年计划，莫斯科最大的工业区之一的斯大林区全区都已完成了五年计划。这些工厂和工业区现在正生产着一九五一年的产品。
    【新华社北平二十日电】莫斯科讯：莫斯科红色贺尔姆纺织厂工人朱特基克所发起的生产头等产品的运动。已经迅速地发展为巨大的规模。红色贺尔姆纺织厂每天产品的百分之九十九都是头等货。现在各工业中参加这个运动的工人已增加到几十万人。
    莫斯科州细布工厂的两个斯达哈诺夫工作者，罗日涅娃和科诺年科，最近又发起用节省下来的原料生产额外产品的新的爱国运动。莫斯科州细布厂已经开始了这种生产。这个倡议在莫斯科和苏联其他城市的轻工业中都已得到广泛的响应。
