Title: Overseas Chinese Groups in San Francisco Telegraph Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Congratulating the Liberation of Nanjing and Other Cities
Author:
Date: 1949-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 29th. The Overseas Chinese Workers Mutual Aid Association of San Francisco, the Overseas Chinese Peace and Democracy Association, the American Branch of the Chinese Democratic Youth League, and the Light Cavalry Art Troupe jointly telegraphed Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu on April 29th, congratulating the great victory of the People's Liberation Army in crossing the Yangtze River. The original telegram stated: Mr. Mao Zedong, General Zhu De:
    We extend our warmest congratulations on your victorious crossing of the Yangtze River and the liberation of Nanjing and other cities. We are willing to give our fullest and most resolute support to your peace plan and the establishment of the People's Republic of China based on New Democracy.

### 旧金山华侨团体　电毛主席朱总司令　祝贺南京等城解放

1949-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　旧金山华侨团体
  　电毛主席朱总司令
  　祝贺南京等城解放
    【新华社北平二十九日电】旧金山华侨工人互助社、华侨和平民主协会、中国民主青年同盟美国分部和轻骑文艺社四团体曾于四月二十九日联名致电毛主席、朱总司令，祝贺人民解放军渡江作战的伟大胜利。原电称：毛泽东先生、朱德将军：
    对于你们胜利的渡江进军，南京以及其他城市的解放，我们敬致最热烈的庆贺。对于你们的和平方案以及以新民主主义为基础的中华人民共和国的建立，我们愿予以最充分、最坚决的支持。
