Title: Mongolia and Albania Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 3

　　Mongolia and Albania
  　Establish Diplomatic Relations
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 25th] TASS, Kulun: The government gazette of the Mongolian People's Republic announced the establishment of diplomatic relations with Albania. The "Unen" newspaper, in commenting on this matter, wrote: The establishment of diplomatic relations between the two republics is another important step towards consolidating cooperation between the peoples of Europe and Asia in the struggle for peace and security.

### 蒙古与阿尔巴尼亚　建立外交关系

1949-05-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　蒙古与阿尔巴尼亚
  　建立外交关系
    【新华社北平二十五日电】塔斯社库伦讯：蒙古人民共和国政府公报宣布与阿尔巴尼亚建立外交关系。“乌嫩报”在论及此事时写道：两共和国间的建立外交关系，是在人民争取和平与安全的斗争中，走向巩固欧亚两洲人民间合作的又一重要步骤。
