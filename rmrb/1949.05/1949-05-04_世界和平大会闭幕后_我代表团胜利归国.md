Title: After the World Peace Congress Concludes, Our Delegation Returns Victoriously
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 30th: Moscow dispatch: The Chinese delegation that participated in the World Congress of Supporters of Peace in Prague arrived in Moscow on the 29th on its way back to China. Soviet scientists, writers, and representatives of the people went to the airport to welcome them.

### 世界和平大会闭幕后  我代表团胜利归国

1949-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平大会闭幕后
    我代表团胜利归国
    【新华社北平三十日电】莫斯科讯：参加世界拥护和平大会布拉格会议的中国代表团，已于二十九日在返国途中抵达莫斯科。苏联科学家、作家和民众代表曾往机场欢迎。
