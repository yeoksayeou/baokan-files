Title: Beiping Palace Museum: Qing Dynasty Revolutionary Historical Materials to be Exhibited in a Special Room
Author:
Date: 1949-05-28
Page: 2

[Ping Shi News] To facilitate public understanding of the Qing Dynasty people's revolution process and the reasons for its failure, the National Beiping Palace Museum intends to open a special room to display the Qing Dynasty revolutionary historical materials in its collection. A preview is scheduled to be held at 3:00 pm on the 28th of this month in the Wenhua Hall of the Palace Museum.

### 北平故宫博物院　清代革命史料将辟专室展览

1949-05-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　北平故宫博物院
  　清代革命史料将辟专室展览
    【平市讯】为便于民众了解清代人民革命之过程及其失败之原因，国立北平故宫博物院近拟将所藏清代革命史料增辟专室陈列展览，并订于本月二十八日下午三时在故宫博物院文华殿举行预展。
