Title: Postal Service Between North China and Suzhou-Hangzhou Areas; Telecommunications to Cities in Northeast Restored
Author: Xiao Chuan
Date: 1949-05-11
Page: 2

    Postal Service Between North China and Suzhou-Hangzhou Areas
    Telecommunications to Cities in Northeast Restored
    [This newspaper dispatch] The North China Postal and Telecommunications Bureau has now issued an order to all post offices in the region, effective from the date of receipt of the order, to begin accepting ordinary mail, registered mail, newspapers, printed matter, and other mail to Datong, Xinxiang, Anyang, as well as Suzhou, Zhenjiang, Wuxi, Changzhou, Wuhu, and Hangzhou; and to accept parcels to Datong, Xinxiang, and Anyang. (Xiao Chuan)
    [Another dispatch] Telecommunications between Peiping and Shenyang have been restored since the 8th of this month. The city's telecommunications bureau can now receive and send telegrams to 340 important towns and cities within the Northeast region, including Harbin, Mudanjiang, Qiqihar, Hailar, Manzhouli, Wangyemiao, Jiamusi, Jilin, Changchun, Siping, Yanji, Andong, Anshan, Fushun, Yingkou, Dalian, Xintai, Wafangdian, Xinmin, Kaiyuan, Tieling, Benxi, Liaozhong, Sujiatun, Fengcheng, Haicheng, and Dashiqiao.

### 华北与苏杭等地通邮  发东北各城电讯恢复
笑川
1949-05-11
第2版()
专栏：

    华北与苏杭等地通邮
    发东北各城电讯恢复
    【本报讯】华北邮电总局现已通令全区各邮局，自接令之日起，开始收寄大同、新乡、安阳及苏州、镇江、无锡、常州、芜湖、杭州等地之平信、挂号、新闻纸、印刷品等邮件；并收寄往大同、新乡、安阳之包裹。（笑川）
    【又讯】北平和沈阳间电信已从本月八号起恢复，东北区内的哈尔滨、牡丹江、齐齐哈尔、海拉尔、满州里、王爷庙、佳木斯、吉林、长春、四平、延吉、安东、鞍山、抚顺、营口、大连、新台子、瓦房店、新民、开原、铁岭、本溪、辽中、苏家屯、凤城、海城、大石桥等三百四十个重要城镇的电报，本市电信局都可以收发。
