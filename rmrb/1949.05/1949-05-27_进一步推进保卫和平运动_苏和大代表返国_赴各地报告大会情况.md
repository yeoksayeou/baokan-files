Title: Further Promote the Peace Movement: Soviet and Other Delegates Return Home to Report on the Conference

Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 3

　　Further Promote the Peace Movement
  　Soviet and Other Delegates Return Home
  　Report Conference Situation to Various Regions
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th] Moscow Dispatch: After the Soviet delegation that attended the Paris and Prague Peace Conferences returned home, the All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge has held lectures in Moscow and many cities across the Soviet Union, inviting the delegates to report on the situation of the peace conferences, in order to further promote the movement to defend world peace in the Soviet Union. Speakers in Moscow included Soviet hero Marislov, writer Ehrenburg, President of the Ukrainian Academy of Sciences, Baradin, and representative of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, Kechimasov. The delegates unanimously declared that the conference once again demonstrated the Soviet Union's great international prestige. It showed that the people of the world regard the Soviet Union as an inviolable fortress of peace and closely, indivisibly link it with their struggle against the warmongers and for progress and democracy.

### 进一步推进保卫和平运动　苏和大代表返国　赴各地报告大会情况

1949-05-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　进一步推进保卫和平运动
  　苏和大代表返国
  　赴各地报告大会情况
    【新华社北平二十六日电】莫斯科讯：出席巴黎和布拉格和平大会的苏联代表团回国以后，全苏政治与科学知识普及协会已在莫斯科和全苏许多城市举办了演讲会，请代表们报告拥护和平大会的情况，以便在苏联进一步推进保卫世界和平的运动。在莫斯科演讲的有苏联英雄马里斯罗夫、作家爱伦堡、乌克兰科学院院长巴拉丁和苏联青年反法西斯委员会代表克齐马索夫。代表们一致宣称：大会再一次显示了苏联的巨大的国际威信。它显示了全世界人民都把苏联看作不可侵犯的和平堡垒，并且和他们反对战争贩子，争取进步与民主的斗争，密切联系，不可分割。
