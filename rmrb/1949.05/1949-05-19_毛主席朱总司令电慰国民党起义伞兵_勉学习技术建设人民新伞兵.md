Title: Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Send a Telegram to Comfort the KMT Uprising Paratroopers, Encouraging Them to Study Technology and Build New People's Paratroopers
Author:
Date: 1949-05-19
Page: 1

[Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Beiping, 18th—The officers and soldiers of the former KMT army’s 3rd Parachute Regiment who staged an uprising, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu on the 18th of last month to express their respect. Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu replied today to express their comfort and encouragement. The telegram reads as follows: To Regiment Commander Liu Nong, Deputy Regiment Commander Jiang Jian, and all officers and soldiers:

　　We celebrate your heroic act of breaking away from the KMT reactionary group and joining the People's Liberation Army. We hope you will work hard in your political and technical studies and strive to build China's new paratroopers!

　　Mao Zedong Zhu De May 18, 1949

### 毛主席朱总司令电慰国民党起义伞兵  勉学习技术建设人民新伞兵

1949-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席朱总司令电慰国民党起义伞兵
    勉学习技术建设人民新伞兵
    【北平新华广播电台广播】北平十八日消息：前国民党军伞兵第三团起义官兵，在上月十八日曾经打电报给毛主席和朱总司令致敬。毛主席和朱总司令今天复电慰勉。电文如下：刘农＠团长姜健副团长和全体官兵们：
    庆祝你们脱离国民党反动集团而加入人民解放军的英勇举动，希望你们努力于政治上和技术上的学习，为建设中国的新伞兵而奋斗！
  　　毛泽东　朱德　一九四九年五月十八日
