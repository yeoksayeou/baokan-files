Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party Ninth Congress Opens; Representatives from All Fraternal Parties Attend
Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 26th – Reports from various sources: The Ninth Congress of the Czechoslovak Communist Party opened on the 25th at the Industrial Palace in Prague. Italian Communist Party General Secretary Togliatti, French Communist Party Secretary Marty, British Communist Party General Secretary Pollitt, and Polish United Workers' Party Secretary Zambrowski all attended. The Communist Parties of China, Hungary, Bulgaria, Spain, Israel, Australia, Cuba, Venezuela, and other countries also had representatives or delegations in attendance.

### 捷共九届代表会开幕　各兄弟党均派代表参加

1949-05-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷共九届代表会开幕
  　各兄弟党均派代表参加
    【新华社北平二十六日电】各方报道：捷克斯洛伐克共产党第九届代表大会于二十五日在布拉格的工业宫开幕。意大利共产党总书记托格里亚蒂、法国共产党书记马尔梯、英国共产党总书记波立特、波兰统一工人党书记萨姆布劳斯基均参加，中国、匈牙利、保加利亚、西班牙、以色列、澳洲、古巴、委内瑞拉等国共产党，亦均有代表或代表团参加。
