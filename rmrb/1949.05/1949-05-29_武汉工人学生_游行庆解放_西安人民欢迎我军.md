Title: Wuhan Workers and Students Parade to Celebrate Liberation; Xi'an People Welcome Our Army
Author:
Date: 1949-05-29
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 27th: To celebrate the rebirth of Wuhan, workers and students in Hankou held a grand parade on the 23rd, after the Wuhan Military Control Commission of the People's Liberation Army was declared established. The parade started from Jianghan Road at 12:15 noon, passed through Zhongshan Avenue, Shengli Street, Yanjiang Avenue, Jianghan Customs, and turned to Dongting Street, circling the city for a week, ending at 4:00 PM. During the procession, the masses held high large portraits of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, red flags fluttered, and gongs and drums resounded. Citizens set off firecrackers and cheered along the streets. Shouts of "Long live Chairman Mao," "Long live Commander-in-Chief Zhu," and "Long live the People's Liberation Army" resounded in unison. On the morning of the 24th, workers, students, and business people from a total of 113 units in Wuchang also held a large parade of more than 20,000 people.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 26th (Delayed): The workers, students, and citizens of Xi'an warmly welcomed the People's Liberation Army. On the 20th, as soon as the People's Liberation Army rushed into the city and was clearing out the remaining enemies resisting in the puppet Public Security Bureau, crowds of people rushed forward to welcome them. On the 21st, when the People's Liberation Army marched into the city in formation, students from Northwest University, Northwest Institute of Technology, and other schools crowded the streets, shouting welcome slogans. Citizens competed to set off firecrackers. Public buses carried electrical workers to repair various electrical equipment everywhere. The workers at the city's northeast power plant also immediately began working intensely.

### 武汉工人学生　游行庆解放　西安人民欢迎我军

1949-05-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　武汉工人学生
  　游行庆解放
  　西安人民欢迎我军
    【新华社汉口二十七日电】为了庆祝武汉的新生，汉口工人学生于二十三日人民解放军武汉军管会宣告成立后，举行了盛大的游行。游行行列于正午十二时十五分由江汉路出发，经中山大道、胜利街、沿江大道、江汉关折向洞庭街，绕行全市一周，至下午四时结束。在队伍行进中，群众高举着毛主席与朱总司令巨幅画像，红旗招展，锣鼓喧天，市民燃放鞭炮，夹道欢呼。“毛主席万岁”、“朱总司令万岁”、“人民解放军万岁”之声，响成一片。二十四日上午九时，武昌工人学生及工商界总计一百一十三个单位亦举行二万余人的大游行。
    【新华社西安二十六日电】（迟到）西安市工人、学生、市民们热烈欢迎人民解放军。二十日，人民解放军刚冲进城内，正在扫清还在伪公安总局抵抗的残余敌人时，成群的人民就抢上前来欢迎。二十一日，当人民解放军整队入城时，西北大学、西北工学院等校的学生们，拥挤在街道上，高呼欢迎的口号。市民们争着燃放鞭炮。公用汽车载着电机工人，到处去修理各种电气设备。城东北发电厂的工人，也立即紧张地开始工作。
