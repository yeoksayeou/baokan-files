Title: Founding of the Communist Youth League of the North China Highway Bureau
Author: Cao Shibin
Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 2

　　Founding of the Communist Youth League of the North China Highway Bureau
    [Ping City News] After more than a month of preparation, the preparatory committee of the Communist Youth League of the North China Highway Bureau held its official founding meeting yesterday (the 20th) at 3:00 PM. Thirty-five League members and four alternate members attended the meeting. Director Cao emphasized in his speech that in the arduous construction of the new China, the only guarantee to avoid falling behind and making mistakes is what Chairman Mao said: "Study, study, and study again."
    [Another Dispatch] In order to connect with the families of employees and intensify study and participation in production, the female employees of the North China Highway Transportation Bureau established a women's association preparatory committee on the 19th of this month. (Cao Shibin, Steam Roller Team)

### 华北公路总局青年团成立
曹世斌
1949-05-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北公路总局青年团成立
    【平市讯】华北公路总局青年团筹委会，经一月余之筹备，于昨（二十日）下午三时举行正式成立大会，到会团员三十五人，候补团员四人。曹局长致词中强调指出：在新中国艰苦建设工作中，要想不落伍，不犯错误，惟一的保障就是毛主席所说的：“学习、学习，再学习”。
    【又讯】华北公路运输总局女职员，为联络职工家属，加紧学习和参加生产，于本月十九日成立妇女会筹备会。（汽碾队曹世斌）
