Title: Grain Continues to Arrive in Beiping: Over Ten Million Jin Transported in Two Days
Author:
Date: 1949-05-24
Page: 2

　　Grain Continues to Arrive in Beiping
　　Over Ten Million Jin Transported in Two Days

　　[This newspaper dispatch] Beiping Trading Company is transporting grain from Northeast and Northwest China to Beiping. On the 21st and 22nd, over 10,074,000 jin were transported to Beiping, including 168 train cars from the Northeast, totaling 10,008,000 jin, and 11 train cars from the Ping-Sui Railway, totaling 660,000 jin. The types of grain include: wheat, millet, sorghum, corn, buckwheat, etc. (Yu)

### 粮食源源抵平　两天运到千余万斤

1949-05-24
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　粮食源源抵平
  　两天运到千余万斤
    【本报讯】北平贸易公司由东北、西北运来之粮食源源到平，二十一、二十二两日运平者已有一千零七十四万斤，其中有由东北运来者一百六十八个车皮，计一千零零八万斤，由平绥线运来者十一个车皮，计六十六万斤。粮种有：小麦、小米、高粱、玉米、荞麦等。（宇）
