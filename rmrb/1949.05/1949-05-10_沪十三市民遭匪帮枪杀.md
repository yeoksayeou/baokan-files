Title: Thirteen Shanghai Citizens Gunned Down by Gangsters
Author:
Date: 1949-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th: Shanghai dispatch: The Shanghai Kuomintang gangster group, at the end of its rope, continues to frantically massacre the people. On the 6th and 7th, thirteen more citizens were shot and killed by the pseudo-police bureau on false charges of "creating disturbances" and "disrupting finance."

### 沪十三市民遭匪帮枪杀

1949-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    沪十三市民遭匪帮枪杀
    【新华社北平九日电】上海消息：日暮途穷的上海国民党匪帮继续疯狂屠杀人民，六、七两日中又有市民十三人被伪警察局假借“制造骚动”，“扰乱金融”等罪名枪杀。
