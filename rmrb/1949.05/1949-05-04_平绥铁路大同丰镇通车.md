Title: Ping Sui Railway: Datong-Fengzhen Section Opens to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1949-05-04
Page: 1
    (Sui Meng Telegram) The Datong-Fengzhen section of the Ping Sui Railway, after two days and one night of emergency repairs by the staff of Fengzhen and other stations (originally planned to take three days and three nights to repair), was completed before the May 1st Labor Day. On the morning of the 3rd, two trains had already departed from Fengzhen to Datong. The Railway Bureau has decided to officially sell passenger tickets between Fengzhen and Datong from the 5th of this month and handle passenger and freight transportation.

### 平绥铁路大同丰镇通车

1949-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    平绥铁路
    大同丰镇通车
    【绥蒙电】平绥铁路大同至丰镇段，经过丰镇等车站员工两天一夜的抢修（原拟三天三夜修通），已于五一劳动节前抢修完竣。三日上午已有两次车由丰镇开往大同，路局已决定从本月五日起正式售丰镇大同间客票，并办理客货运输。
