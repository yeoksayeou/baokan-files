Title: Kuomintang Second Coastal Defense Fleet Uprising to Join the PLA Navy; Twenty-Three Ships in Zhenjiang Simultaneously Surrender; East China Military Region Naval Headquarters Established
Author:
Date: 1949-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 11th: On April 23rd, when the People's Liberation Army's three powerful forces victoriously crossed the Yangtze River, General Lin Zun, commander of the Kuomintang Navy's Second Fleet, led 25 ships under his command in a glorious uprising on the Yangtze River near Badoushan, about eight miles northeast of Nanjing, to join the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy. The uprising naval officers and soldiers were warmly welcomed by the People's Liberation Army and the broad masses of the people.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 12th: Simultaneously with the uprising of 25 ships of the Kuomintang Navy's Second Fleet, on April 23rd, 23 Kuomintang naval ships on the Zhenjiang River surrendered to the People's Liberation Army. The surrendered ships included: eight gunboats: No. 1, No. 5, No. 52, No. 53, No. 68, No. 88, No. 102, No. 104; eleven infantry landing craft: No. 32, No. 302, No. 305, No. 306, No. 308, No. 303, No. 314, No. 317, No. 318, No. 708, No. 713; and four cruisers: No. 10, No. 21, No. 121, No. 123.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 11th: The Kuomintang Navy's Second Fleet, which revolted on the Yangtze River near Badoushan, consisted of 25 ships, including two destroyers: Hui'an and Ji'an; five gunboats: Yong Sui, Tai Yuan, Jiang Xi, An Dong, and Chu Tong; one medium landing ship: Meisheng; one infantry landing ship: Lian Guang; and the First Mobile Patrol Team's five gunboats: No. 2, No. 3, No. 4, No. 54, and No. 56; four infantry landing craft: No. 301, No. 311, No. 312, and No. 315; one cruiser: No. 20; one speedboat: No. 101; the Fifth Patrol Boat Team's two gunboats: No. 103 and No. 105; two cruisers: No. 22 and No. 23; and one infantry landing craft: No. 303. The Kuomintang Navy had a total of four fleets: the Yangtze River Defense Fleet, the First and Second Coastal Defense Fleets, and the Transportation Fleet. The uprising involved the Second Coastal Defense Fleet.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 11th: The Headquarters of the East China Military Region Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army was established on May 1st upon order. The General Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army appointed Zhang Aiping as Commander and concurrently Political Commissar of the East China Military Region Navy. Commander Zhang reported for duty on the same day.

### 国民党海防第二舰队起义参加解放军海军  镇江廿三艘舰艇同时投降  华东军区海军司令部成立

1949-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    国民党海防第二舰队起义参加解放军海军
    镇江廿三艘舰艇同时投降
    华东军区海军司令部成立
    【新华社南京十一日电】四月二十三日，当人民解放军三路强大部队胜利渡过长江时，国民党海军第二舰队司令林遵将军率所属舰艇二十五艘，于南京东北约八里之笆斗山江面光荣起义，参加中国人民解放军海军。起义的海军官兵受到人民解放军和广大人民的热烈欢迎。
    【新华社南京十二日电】与国民党海军第二舰队二十五艘舰艇起义的同时，四月二十三日，镇江江面上的国民党海军舰艇二十三艘向人民解放军投降。投降的舰艇计有：炮艇八艘，为：一号、五号、五十二号、五十三号、六十八号、八十八号、一零二号、一零四号；步兵登陆艇十一艘，为：三十二号、三零二号、三零五号、三零六号、三零八号、三零三号、三一四号、三一七号、三一八号、七零八号、七一三号；巡洋舰四艘，为：十号、二十一号、一二一号、一二三号。
    【新华社南京十一日电】在南京笆斗山江面上起义的国民党海军第二舰队，共辖有舰艇二十五艘，计有驱逐舰二艘：惠安号、吉安号，炮舰五艘：永绥号、太原号、江犀号、安东号、楚同号，中型登陆舰一艘：美盛号，步兵登陆舰一艘：联光号，及第一机动巡防队所辖炮艇五艘：二号、三号、四号、五十四号、五十六号，步兵登陆艇四艘：三零一号、三一一号、三一二号、三一五号，巡洋艇一艘：二十号，快艇一艘：一零一号，第五巡防艇队所辖炮艇二艘：一零三号、一零五号，巡洋艇二艘：二十二号、二十三号，步兵登陆艇一艘：三零三号。按国民党海军共有四个舰队，即：江防舰队，海防第一、第二舰队，运输舰队。此次起义者系海防第二舰队。
    【新华社南京十一日电】中国人民解放军华东军区海军司令部已于五月一日奉令成立，中国人民解放军总部任命张爱萍为华东军区海军司令员兼政治委员。张司令员已于当日到职视事。
