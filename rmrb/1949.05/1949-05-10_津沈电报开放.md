Title: Tianjin-Shenyang Telegrams Open
Author:
Date: 1949-05-10
Page: 1

    Tianjin-Shenyang Telegrams Open

    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 9th] Telegram service between Tianjin and Shenyang opened yesterday. Telegram service between Tianjin and other parts of Northeast China will also open soon. Furthermore, postal offices in Tianjin and other inland areas began accepting parcels for Datong yesterday.

### 津沈电报开放

1949-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    津沈电报开放
    【新华社天津九日电】天津沈阳间电报已于昨日起开放。津市与东北其他各地电报不久亦可开放。又津市及内地各邮局已于昨日起开始收受寄往大同包裹。
