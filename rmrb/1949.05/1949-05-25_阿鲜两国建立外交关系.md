Title: Albania and North Korea Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1949-05-25
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 24th: TASS, Tirana: An agreement has been concluded between the People's Republic of Albania and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on the establishment of normal diplomatic relations.

### 阿鲜两国建立外交关系

1949-05-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　阿鲜两国建立外交关系
    【新华社北平二十四日电】塔斯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国与朝鲜人民民主共和国间业已缔结关于建立正常外交关系的协定。
