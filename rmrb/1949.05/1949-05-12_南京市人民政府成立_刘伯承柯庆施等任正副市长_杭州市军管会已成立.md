Title: Nanjing Municipal People's Government Established; Liu Bocheng, Ke Qingshi, and Others Appointed as Mayor and Deputy Mayors; Hangzhou Military Control Commission Established

Author:
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 11th: The Nanjing Municipal People's Government has been formally announced as established by order of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission. Mayor Liu Bocheng, and Deputy Mayors Ke Qingshi and Zhang Linzhi, assumed their posts today.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 11th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Nanjing Office, was formally established on the 9th and has begun operations. The acting director of the office is Gao Jun.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 11th: The Hangzhou Military Control Commission was established on the 7th by order, with Tan Zhenlin as the director and Tan Qilong and Wang Daohan as deputy directors. The Hangzhou Garrison Command was also established on the same day, with Wang Jian'an as commander, Tan Qilong as political commissar, and Ji Pengfei as deputy political commissar and director of the political department.

### 南京市人民政府成立  刘伯承柯庆施等任正副市长  杭州市军管会已成立

1949-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    南京市人民政府成立
    刘伯承柯庆施等任正副市长
    杭州市军管会已成立
    【新华社南京分社十一日电】南京市人民政府已奉中国人民革命军事委员会命令正式宣告成立。市长刘伯承，副市长柯庆施、张霖之已于本日就职视事。
    【新华社南京十一日电】中华全国总工会，驻南京市办事处，已于九日正式成立，开始办公。该处代理主任为高骏。
    【新华社杭州十一日电】杭州市军事管制委员会已于七日奉命成立，主任为谭震林，副主任为谭启龙、汪道涵。杭州市警备司令部亦于同日成立，王建安为司令员，谭启龙为政治委员，姬鹏飞为副政治委员兼政治部主任。
