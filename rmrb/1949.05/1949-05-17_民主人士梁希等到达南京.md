Title: Democratic Figures Liang Xi and Others Arrive in Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1949-05-17
Page: 1

　　Democratic Figures Liang Xi and Others Arrive in Nanjing

    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Nanjing, 16th – News: Democratic figures Liang Xi, Zhu Yunshan, Zhao Chaogou, Yu Huancheng, Lei Xizhang, and Lu Yuwen, Secretary-General of the Kuomintang peace negotiation representative Li Zheng, and a group of twenty people arrived in Nanjing on the 13th from the North China Liberated Area. Liu Bocheng, Director of the Military Control Commission, sent personnel to welcome them at Xiaguan and accompanied them to the guesthouse for rest.

### 民主人士梁希等到达南京

1949-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　民主人士梁希等到达南京
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京十六号消息：过去在京沪地区的民主人士梁希、朱蕴山、赵超构、俞寰澄、雷锡章和国民党和谈代表李蒸秘书长卢郁文等一行二十人十三号从华北解放区到达南京。军管会刘伯承主任派员到下关欢迎，并陪往招待所休息。
