Title: Protesting Dutch Imperialist Aggression: Five Indonesian Students Leave Holland for Studies in Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1949-05-15
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 14th: Prague Dispatch: Five Indonesian students arrived in Prague on the 11th, having left Holland where they were studying on government scholarships in protest against the Dutch colonialists' aggression against Indonesia. According to an announcement by the Czech government on the evening of the 11th, the five Indonesian students will receive financial support from the Czech government to pursue their studies in Czechoslovakia.

### 抗议荷帝侵略  五名印尼学生离荷赴捷研读

1949-05-15
第3版()
专栏：

    抗议荷帝侵略
    五名印尼学生离荷赴捷研读
    【新华社北平十四日电】布拉格讯：五名印尼学生于十一日从荷兰抵达布拉格，他们原是在荷研读的官费生，因抗议荷兰殖民者侵略印尼而离荷。据捷政府十一日晚宣布，五名印尼学生将受捷政府的资助在捷求学。
