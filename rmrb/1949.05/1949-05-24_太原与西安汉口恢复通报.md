Title: Taiyuan, Xi'an, and Hankou Communication Restoration Announcement
Author:
Date: 1949-05-24
Page: 1

　　Taiyuan, Xi'an, and Hankou Communication Restoration Announcement
　　[Taiyuan Electric Dispatch] Communication between Taiyuan and Xi'an was restored on the 21st of this month. Long-distance telephone calls have also been test-connected and will soon be open for long-distance telephone service. Communication and telephone service between Taiyuan and Hankou have already been opened.

### 太原与西安汉口恢复通报

1949-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　太原与西安汉口恢复通报
    【太原电】本月二十一日太原至西安已恢复通报，长途电话亦已试通，即可开放长途电话业务。太原至汉口之通报通话业已开放。
