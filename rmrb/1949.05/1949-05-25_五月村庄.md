Title: Village in May
Author: Yi Ying (乙影)
Date: 1949-05-25
Page: 4

　　Village in May
　　Yi Ying
　　(I) Looking from the Village Head

　　May,
　　Scorching hot day,
　　Whirlwinds surround the old poplar tree,
　　Spinning around.

　　Wheat is budding,
　　The villagers are so happy,
　　Turn the waterwheel again,
　　To water the fields.

　　The water in the furrows,
　　Gleaming white,
　　Put in a little more effort today,
　　Harvest a little more grain in the autumn.

　　(II) Allotted Plot

　　Allotted plot,
　　Five mu and seven fen
　　—Aunt Zhang next door
　　Received it in the year before last.

　　We never had land before?
　　The poor people of today,
　　Are truly fortunate.

　　Ever since our family got the allotted plot,
　　Rising early and working late,
　　Mother and children all work hard together,
　　Last year we had a little savings,
　　In the spring we bought a little donkey.

　　(III) Supporting the Front Through Production

　　Little donkey,
　　Turn with vigor,
　　Water the wheat fields, then water the vegetable fields,
　　Water the garden well to prevent drought,
　　—Participate in this year's great production.

　　Sister-in-law Afu,
　　Sits by the side,
　　Spinning wheel in hand, making plans:
　　Spinning thread,
　　Weaving cloth,
　　Sewing undershirts,
　　To send to the soldiers at the front.

　　Waterwheel turns,
　　Spinning wheel turns.
　　Produce more, support the front more,
　　Liberate all of China,
　　Everyone will share the glory,
　　—How delightful!

### 五月村庄
乙影
1949-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　五月村庄
    乙影
　　（一）庄头望
　
    五月
    滚热的天，
    旋风围着老杨树
    打圈圈。
　
    麦子莠了穗，
    老乡好喜欢，
    再转一转水车，
    浇浇田。
　
    垄沟里的水，
    白晃晃，
    今天多出点力，
    秋天多打点粮。
　
　　（二）畦子地
　
    畦子地
    五亩七
    ——隔壁张大妈
    前年分得的。
　
    从前咱们哪有地？
    如今的穷人，
    真是好福气。
　
    自从咱家分了畦子地，
    早起趟（贪）黑
    娘儿几个一齐卖力气，
    去年有点小积蓄，
    春天买了条小毛驴。
　
　　（三）生产支有
　
    小毛驴，
    用劲转，
    浇完麦田浇菜田，
    浇了园子好防旱，
    ——参加今年大生产。
　
    阿福嫂，
    坐地边
    手摇纺车心盘算：
    纺线
    织布，
    缝汗衫
    送给前线战士穿。
　
    水车转，
    纺车转。
    多生产，多支前，
    解放全中国
    大伙有光沾
    ——多喜欢！
