Title: Meeting on the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Line in Zhuji, Shanghai-Hangzhou-Ningbo Road Liberates Shaoxing; We Control Over a Thousand Li of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway from Hangzhou to Dongxiang; Two Thousand Five Hundred Enemies Captured in Geyang and Xiaoshan
Author:
Date: 1949-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang-Jiangxi Front, 13th: The People's Liberation Army, advancing eastward along the central section of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway and southward from Hangzhou's Xiaoshan, has victoriously converged in the Zhuji area. Zhuji City was liberated on the 7th. By this point, the entire 1,100+ li of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway from Hangzhou to Dongxiang, Jiangxi, is under the control of the People's Liberation Army. Thanks to the valiant protection of the PLA and railway employees, all buildings along the railway remain intact. The section of the railway from Hangzhou to Quzhou will be open to traffic within days.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 13th: The People's Liberation Army, advancing eastward along the Shanghai-Hangzhou-Ningbo Railway from Xiaoshan, liberated Shaoxing on the 7th. The remnants of the enemy are fleeing eastward. Shaoxing is the hometown of Mr. Lu Xun, located southeast of Hangzhou. Formerly known as Kuaiji, it was once the capital of the State of Yue during the Spring and Autumn period under King Goujian. There are many famous historical sites and it is renowned for its wine production.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 13th: The People's Liberation Army, after crossing the Qiantang River, continued to pursue the fleeing enemy after occupying Xiaoshan County. On the evening of the 6th, in the area east of Linpu Town in southern Xiaoshan, they annihilated the entire 646th Regiment of the 216th Division of the 85th Army of the Kuomintang forces and two squadrons of the pseudo-Xiaoshan Self-Defense Force, capturing over 1,000 enemy soldiers, including the regimental commander and deputy commander. Only one PLA soldier was slightly injured.

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang-Jiangxi Front, 13th: The People's Liberation Army, clearing out remnants on both sides of the central section of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway, captured Hu Lianlai, commander of the 427th Regiment of the 143rd Division of the 68th Army, and Yang Hongxun, commander of the 428th Regiment, along with 1,500 other enemy soldiers near Longkeng Ridge, 60 li northeast of Geyang, on the 5th. They killed or wounded over 20 enemy soldiers and seized five mortars, fifteen 60mm guns, six heavy machine guns, fifty light machine guns, and over two hundred rifles and horses.

Further reports: Four more enemy generals have been identified among the prisoners captured along the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway: Zeng Jirui, Deputy Commander of the 106th Army; Du Yunzhong, Chief of Staff of the 68th Army; Ge Kairuo, Commander of the 81st Division; and Liu Qingpu, Deputy Commander of the division.

Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Broadcast: Zhejiang-Jiangxi Front, 13th: The 300+ li railway between Shangrao and Dongxiang on the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway has resumed traffic, thanks to the protection of vehicles and equipment and the active restoration work by employees at all stations. Employees at all stations remained at their posts and protected the railway and stations from any damage while the remnant bandits fled south. Employees at Yingtan Station only suspended work for one day during the enemy's retreat. After liberation, they immediately inspected the machinery and started the machines.

### 浙赣线上会师诸暨沪杭甬路解放绍兴  我控制浙赣路杭州到东乡千余里  戈阳萧山俘敌两千五百

1949-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    浙赣线上会师诸暨沪杭甬路解放绍兴
    我控制浙赣路杭州到东乡千余里
    戈阳萧山俘敌两千五百
    【新华社浙赣前线十三日电】沿浙赣路中段东进与杭州萧山南下的两路人民解放军已在诸暨地区胜利会师，诸暨城已于七日解放。至此，浙赣路由杭州至江西东乡一千一百余里铁路已全部为解放军掌握。在解放军及铁路员工奋勇保护下，铁路沿线一切建筑皆完整无损，自杭州至衢州段的铁路日内即可通车。
    【新华社杭州十三日电】由萧山沿沪杭甬铁路向东挺进的人民解放军，七日解放绍兴，残敌向东溃逃。绍兴是鲁迅先生故乡，位于杭州东南，古名会稽，春秋时越王勾践曾建都于此。当地名胜古迹甚多，并以产酒著名。
    【新华社杭州十三日电】越过钱塘江的人民解放军，在进占萧山县城后，继续向前追击逃敌，六日晚在萧山南临浦镇以东一带，捕歼国民党军八十五军二一六师之六四六团全部及伪萧山自卫队两个中队，生俘敌正副团长以下一千多名。解放军仅轻伤一人。
    【新华社浙赣前线十三日电】浙赣路中段人民解放军清剿两侧残匪，五日在弋阳东北六十里龙坑岭附近俘敌六十八军一四三师四二七团团长胡连来、四二八团团长杨宏勋以下一千五百名，毙伤敌二十余名，缴迫击炮五门，六零炮十五门，重机枪六挺，轻机枪五十挺，步马枪二百余支。
    又讯：浙赣沿线俘虏中又查出敌将级军官四名，即敌一零六军副军长曾纪瑞、六十八军参谋长杜允中、八十一师师长葛开若、副师长刘清浦。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】浙赣前线十三日电：浙赣铁路上饶到东乡间三百多里铁路，由于各站员工保护车辆器材，积极进行恢复工作，已恢复通车。各站员工，在残匪南窜时，都安守岗位，保护了铁路和车站，未受任何损坏。应潭车站员工，仅在匪军溃退时，工作停顿一天，解放后，立即检查了机械，开动起机器。
