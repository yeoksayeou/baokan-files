Title: Inter-regional Exchange Rate: 1 to 50,000
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-26
Page: 2

　　【Our Newspaper】The Bank of China adjusted the inter-regional exchange rate on the 26th to 1 to 50,000. Foreign exchange rates remained unchanged.

### 区外汇兑一比五万

1949-05-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　区外汇兑一比五万
    【本报讯】中国银行二十六日区外汇兑牌价调整为一比五万。外汇牌价均未动。
