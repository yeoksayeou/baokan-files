Title: Our Army Liberates Jiujiang, Captures Shanghai County, Shanghai Completely Isolated. Our Army Crosses the Yangtze at Wuhan, Captures Echeng and Yangxin in Succession. Wuhan Military Control Commission Established
Author:
Date: 1949-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Yangtze Front, 18th: The People's Liberation Army liberated Jiujiang, an important strategic and commercial city in the middle reaches of the Yangtze River, on the afternoon of the 17th. The defending enemy fled south. Jiujiang, formerly known as Xunyang, is the northern gateway to Jiangxi Province, bordering the Yangtze River to the north and connecting to Nanchang via the Nanxun Railway to the south. Guling on Mount Lu, south of the city, is a famous summer resort.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai Front, 18th: The People's Liberation Army liberated Shanghai County south of Shanghai City and the two strongholds of Xin Zhuang and Qibao Town northwest of it on the 15th. Now, all 61 county towns in Jiangsu Province, except for Baoshan and Chongming counties at the mouth of the Wusong River, have been liberated.

Xinhua News Agency, Yangtze Front, 18th: A unit of the Fourth Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, which crossed the Yangtze River on the river surface 200 miles east of Hankou, successively captured the two county towns of Echeng on the south bank of the Yangtze River and Yangxin south of it on the 15th and 16th. The defending enemy fled. The above two cities, together with the liberated Daye, are important producing areas of coal, iron, and limestone in eastern Hubei. In the past, the Hanyang Arsenal relied on this area for coal and iron supply.

Xinhua News Agency, Yangtze Front, 18th: In the People's Liberation Army's crossing-the-Yangtze operation east of Wuhan, five battalions of the 913th Regiment and the 915th Regiment of the Kuomintang Army's 35th Division stationed in Huangshigang revolted on the 15th and occupied the industrial town of Shihuiyao, responding to the People's Liberation Army's crossing of the river.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 18th: The Wuhan Military Control Commission of the People's Liberation Army, the Wuhan Garrison Command, and the Wuhan Municipal People's Government have been formally established under the order of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission. The director of the Military Control Commission is Tan Zheng, the deputy director is Tao Zhu, the commander of the Garrison Command is Xiao Jingguang, the political commissar is concurrently held by Tan Zheng, the deputy political commissar and director of the Political Department is Tang Tianji, and the mayor is Wu De峰.

Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 18th: Before the People's Liberation Army liberated Sanyuan on the 14th, it liberated Gaoling County north of Xi'an at midnight on the 13th. At the same time, it captured Luqiao Town north of Sanyuan County, annihilating most of the puppet Chunhua Self-Defense Regiment, and capturing more than 40 people below the rank of company commander. Another unit of the People's Liberation Army liberated Yunyang and Koutou Towns west of Sanyuan County. Before this, the People's Liberation Army occupied Liyang Town northeast of Gaoling on the 12th.

### 我军解放九江　又克上海县城上海完全孤立  武汉渡江我军连下鄂城阳新  武汉军管会成立

1949-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军解放九江
  　又克上海县城上海完全孤立
    武汉渡江我军连下鄂城阳新
    武汉军管会成立
    【新华社长江前线十八日电】人民解放军十七日下午解放长江中游重要的战略城市和商业城市九江，守敌南逃。九江，古名浔阳，为江西省北面门户，北临长江，南经南浔铁路通南昌。城南庐山的牯岭，为著名避暑胜地。
    【新华社上海前线十八日电】人民解放军十五日解放上海市以南之上海县城及其西北的莘庄、七宝镇两据点。现江苏全省六十一座县城，除吴淞口上之宝山及崇明两县城外，已全部获得解放。
    【新华社长江前线十八日电】在汉口以东二百里江面上渡过长江的人民解放军第四野战军一部，十五、十六两日连续攻占长江南岸的鄂城及其以南的阳新两座县城，守敌逃窜。上述两城与已解放的大冶，系湖北东部煤、铁、石灰的重要出产地，过去汉阳兵工厂，即赖该地供给煤、铁。
    【新华社长江前线十八日电】人民解放军在武汉以东渡江作战中，驻黄石港的国民党军三十五师九一三团和九一五团的五个营，于十五日举行起义，并占领工业市镇石灰窑，接应解放军渡江。
    【新华社武汉十八日电】人民解放军武汉市军事管制委员会、武汉市警备司令部、武汉市人民政府，已奉中国人民革命军事委员会命令正式成立。军管会主任为谭政，副主任为陶铸，警备司令部司令员为萧劲光，政治委员由谭政兼，副政治委员兼政治部主任为唐天际，市长为吴德峰。
    【新华社延安十八日电】人民解放军第一野战军于十四日解放三原之前，曾于十三日午夜解放西安以北之高陵县城，同时攻占三原县以北之鲁桥镇，歼伪淳化自卫团大部，俘中队长以下四十余人。另部解放军解放三原县以西之云阳、口头两镇，在此以前，解放军于十二日占领高陵东北之栎杨镇。
