Title: The Main War Criminal of Taiyuan, Bandit Dai Bingnan, is Arrested
Author:
Date: 1949-05-04
Page: 3

    Taiyuan Major War Criminal
    Bandit Dai Bingnan Arrested
    [Taiyuan Electric Dispatch] The Chiang bandit Dai Bingnan, commander of the Chiang bandit's 30th Army, was arrested on the 2nd by the Public Security Bureau of the Taiyuan Municipal People's Government. Bandit Dai was one of the main criminals wanted by the People's Liberation Army's Taiyuan Frontline Headquarters in this campaign. He not only dared to resist the heroic advance of the People's Liberation Army, but also, when we broke through the city, extensively set fires for destruction, causing serious damage to the lives and property of the people of Taiyuan.
    Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, 3rd -- The Taiyuan Garrison Command's Dispersed Soldiers Reception Center received more than 2,200 straggling officers and soldiers of the Kuomintang army on the 27th and 28th of last month. Among them were 460 officers of various levels, including Major General Ma Longhai, acting division commander of the 83rd Division, and Guo Youtu, lieutenant general second-class staff member. Another 605 officers below the rank of general have also applied for registration.

### 太原主要战犯  戴匪炳南落网

1949-05-04
第3版()
专栏：

    太原主要战犯
    戴匪炳南落网
    【太原电】蒋匪第三十军军长戴匪炳南已于二日为我太原市人民政府公安局逮捕。戴匪系此次人民解放军太原前线司令部通辑的主要罪犯之一，他不令仅敢于抵抗人民解放军之英勇前进，而且在我破城之时大肆纵火破坏，使太原市人民生命财产遭受严重损害。
    【新华社太原三日电】太原市警备司令部散兵收容所于上月二十七、二十八两天收容国民党军流散官兵达两千二百多名。其中有八十三师少将代理师长马龙海、中将二级干事郭右图等各级军宫四百六十名。另有将级以下军官六百零五名亦已申请登记。
