Title: North China Elementary Education Conference to Open Today in Peiping
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-05-20
Page: 2

　　North China Elementary Education Conference
  　Scheduled to Open Today in Peiping
    [This newspaper reports] The North China Elementary Education Conference, convened by the Education Department of the North China People's Government, will officially open at 8:00 AM today (the 20th) in the auditorium of Xiehe Hospital.
  　(Bai Sheng)

### 华北小学教育会议　定今日在平揭幕
柏生
1949-05-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北小学教育会议
  　定今日在平揭幕
    【本报讯】华北人民政府教育部召开的华北区小学教育会议于今（二十）日上午八时在协和大礼堂正式揭幕。  　（柏生）
