Title: Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Rectifies its Organization in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-05-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, May 12th - The Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party has begun rectifying its organization in Beiping. After some members of the Central Committee arrived in Beiping, they began to rectify the party's organization in Beiping, which had been operating underground during the Kuomintang's rule. A discussion meeting was held recently, resolving to establish a Beiping Municipal Party Affairs Rectification Committee, and appointing Han Zhuoru, Yan Xinmin, Zhang Yunchuan, Ji Fang, Qiu Elun, Wang Dalu, and Fang Rongxin as members, with Han Zhuoru concurrently serving as the director. After the establishment of the committee, it is now actively handling the registration and review of party members. Once the rectification is completed, a general meeting of party members will be convened to establish the Beiping Municipal Party Headquarters.

### 中国农工民主党整顿在平组织

1949-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中国农工民主党整顿在平组织
    【新华社北平十二日电】中国农工民主党开始整顿该党北平市的组织。该党一部分中央委员到平后，就对该党在国民党统治时期潜伏地下的北平市组织，着手整顿，日前举行谈话会，决议成立北平市党务整理委员会，并指派韩卓儒、严信民、张云川、季方、丘锷仑、王大鲁、方荣欣等七人为委员，由韩卓儒兼主任委员。该委员会成立后，现正积极办理党员的登记、审查等工作。等到整理完毕，就要召开党员大会，成立北平市党部。
