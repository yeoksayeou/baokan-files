Title: World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary Saillant Attends Moscow "May Day" Celebrations, Cheers to the Socialist Motherland Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-05-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 2nd: Moscow News: The General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Saillant, who participated in the 10th Congress of Soviet Trade Unions, attended the Moscow "May Day" celebrations. Saillant addressed the Moscow workers, cheering for the socialist motherland, the Soviet Union, and stating: On this "May Day," the World Federation of Trade Unions will call on workers' organizations in capitalist countries to unite all the forces of the working class in the struggle for peace and against instigators of new wars.

### 世界工联总书记赛扬  参加莫斯科“五一”庆祝会  向社会主义祖国苏联欢呼

1949-05-03
第3版()
专栏：

    世界工联总书记赛扬
    参加莫斯科“五一”庆祝会
    向社会主义祖国苏联欢呼
    【新华社北平二日电】莫斯科讯：参加苏联职工会第十届代表大会的世界工联总书记赛扬，参加了莫斯科“五一”节的庆祝会。赛扬对莫斯科工人演讲向社会主义祖国苏联欢呼，并称：值此“五一”节，世界工联将号召资本主义国家的工人组织在争取为和平的及对新战争挑拨者斗争中的，团结工人阶级的一切力量。
