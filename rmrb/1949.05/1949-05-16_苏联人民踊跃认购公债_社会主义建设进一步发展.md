Title: Soviet People Enthusiastically Subscribe to Public Bonds: Socialist Construction Further Developed
Author:
Date: 1949-05-16
Page: 3

　　Soviet People Enthusiastically Subscribe to Public Bonds
　　Socialist Construction Further Developed
　　The Soviet Ministry of Finance's fourth post-war public bond, issued on the 3rd, had been subscribed to the amount of 23,825 million rubles by May 8th, exceeding the quota by 3,825 million rubles. The enthusiasm and speed with which this public bond was subscribed fully demonstrates the great potential of the Soviet economy and the high patriotic spirit of the Soviet people. After obtaining this huge sum of public bond funds, the Soviet government will rapidly invest and expand production, further developing the cause of socialist economic construction.

### 苏联人民踊跃认购公债　社会主义建设进一步发展

1949-05-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联人民踊跃认购公债
  　社会主义建设进一步发展
    苏联财政部三日发行的战后第四次公债，至五月八日已被认购二百三十八亿二千五百余万卢布，超过定额三十八亿二千五百余万卢布。这次公债认购的热潮和完成的速度，充分显示了苏联经济的伟大潜力和苏联人民的高度爱国精神。苏联政府在得到这一笔巨额公债款项后，必将迅速地投入和扩大生产，使社会主义经济建设事业更进一步地发展。
