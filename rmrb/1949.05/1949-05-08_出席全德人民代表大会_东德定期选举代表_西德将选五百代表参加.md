Title: Attending the All-German People's Congress; East Germany to Hold Regular Elections for Representatives; West Germany to Elect Five Hundred Representatives to Participate
Author:
Date: 1949-05-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 6th: TASS, Berlin, May 5th dispatch: The nomination of representatives in the Soviet occupation zone of Germany and Berlin to attend the Third German People's Congress for the Struggle for German Unity and a Just Peace is nearing completion. Elections in the Soviet zone and Berlin are scheduled for the 15th and 16th of this month, and will elect 1,525 representatives. The proposal to convene the Third German People's Congress has also received a wide response in the western occupation zones of Germany. A delegation of Ruhr workers who participated in the May Day demonstration in Berlin welcomed the election of delegates to the Congress and issued the following statement: "The People's Congress movement must develop into a mass movement for peace, for trade union unity, and for German unity. This is the reason why we advocate establishing a united front with the working people in the Soviet occupation zone. Our strength and our unity will overcome the division of Germany." The three western occupation zones of West Germany will elect five hundred representatives to attend the Third German People's Congress.

### 出席全德人民代表大会  东德定期选举代表  西德将选五百代表参加

1949-05-08
第3版()
专栏：

    出席全德人民代表大会
    东德定期选举代表
    西德将选五百代表参加
    【新华社北平六日电】塔斯社柏林五日讯：德境苏占区及柏林提名出席争取德国统一及公正和平的第三届德国人民代表大会代表的工作行将结束。苏占区及柏林的选举定于十五、十六两日举行，将选出一千五百二十五名代表。召开第三届德国人民代表大会的提议，也得到德境西占区的广泛响应。参加柏林“五一”示威的鲁尔工人代表团，欢迎大会代表的选举，并发表声明如下：“人民代表大会运动必须发展成为争取和平、争取工会统一及争取德国统一的群众性运动。我们主张与苏占区劳动人民建立统一战线，其理由即在于此。我们的力量和我们的团结将克服德国的分裂。”西德三个占领区将选举五百名代表出席第三届德国人民代表大会。
