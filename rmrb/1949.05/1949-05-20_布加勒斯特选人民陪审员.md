Title: Bucharest Elects People's Assessors
Author:
Date: 1949-05-20
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th: Bucharest dispatch: Elections of people's assessors have begun in various parts of Romania. The election of Bucharest people's assessors took place on the 15th in eight major factories. At the election meetings, speakers emphasized the importance of the elections and the role of the people's judges.

### 布加勒斯特选人民陪审员

1949-05-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　布加勒斯特选人民陪审员
    【新华社北平十八日电】布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚各地开始选举人民陪审员。布加勒斯特人民陪审员的选举于十五日在八个主要工厂中进行。在选举会议上，演说者均着重指出选举的重要性和人民法官的作用。
