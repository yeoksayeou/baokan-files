Title: US, British, and Soviet Representatives Continue Discussions on German Issues
Author:
Date: 1949-05-05
Page: 3

    US, British, and Soviet Representatives
    Continue Discussions on German Issues
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 2nd] According to the United States Information Service in New York: A spokesman for the US delegation to the United Nations stated that on April 29th, the US Representative to the United Nations, Jessup, and the Soviet Representative to the Security Council, Malik, continued their informal talks on the Berlin issue and the reopening of the Foreign Ministers' meeting to discuss the German issue at the Soviet delegation's office at the United Nations, lasting two and a half hours. United Press London reported: A British Foreign Office spokesman announced on April 29th that on April 27th, the head of the British delegation to the United Nations General Assembly, McNair, and the head of the Soviet delegation to the United Nations General Assembly, Gromyko, held informal talks on the Berlin issue, with the US Assistant Secretary of State for United Nations Affairs, Rusk, also participating.

### 美英苏代表续商德国问题

1949-05-05
第3版()
专栏：

    美英苏代表
    续商德国问题
    【新华社北平二日电】据美国新闻处纽约讯：美驻联合国代表团发言人声称：四月二十九日，美国驻联合国代表耶塞普与苏联驻安理会代表马立克，就柏林问题及重开外长会议讨论德国问题一事，在苏联驻联合国代表团办公室内继续举行非正式会谈，历时两小时半。合众社伦敦消息：英国外交部发言人于四月二十九日宣布：四月二十七日，英国出席联合国大会代表团团长麦克奈尔，与苏联出席联合国大会代表团团长葛罗米柯，关于柏林问题曾举行非正式会谈，美国国务院专司联合国事务之助理国务卿鲁斯克亦参加。
