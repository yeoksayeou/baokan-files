Title: Pakistani Unions Oppose Government's Reactionary Bill; Demand Release of Arrested Leaders
Author:
Date: 1949-05-22
Page: 3

　　Pakistani Unions
  　Oppose Government's Reactionary Bill
  　Demand Release of Arrested Leaders
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th] TASS, Karachi dispatch: The Sindh Observer reported on the 17th that the Executive Committee of the Attock Oil Company Workers' Union in Pakistan's Punjab Province demanded the abolition of the Pakistani government's recently promulgated reactionary "Public Order Maintenance Act," demanded improvement of workers' living conditions and the implementation of universal and direct elections. The resolution passed by the Executive Committee demanded the release of all trade union and peasant association leaders arrested under the aforementioned reactionary bill.

### 巴基斯坦工会　反对政府反动法案　要求释放被捕领袖

1949-05-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦工会
  　反对政府反动法案
  　要求释放被捕领袖
    【新华社北平二十日电】塔斯社喀喇蚩讯：信德观察家报十七日消息称巴基斯坦旁遮普省的阿托基油公司职工会执行委员会要求废除巴基斯坦政府最近颁布的反动的“公共秩序维持法”，要求改善工人生活状况及实行普遍与直接的选举，执行委员会所通过的决议要求释放一切根据上述反动法案而拘捕的工会及农会领袖。
