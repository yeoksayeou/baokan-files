Title: Exchange Industrial Production Experience: Ping City to Hold Industrial Products Exhibition
Author: Bai Qing
Date: 1949-05-26
Page: 2

　　Exchange Industrial Production Experience
  　Ping City to Hold Industrial Products Exhibition
    [Our Newspaper] In order to mutually exchange industrial production experience, the Beiping City Industrial Association has decided to hold a city-wide industrial exhibition at Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park on June 6th. Currently, 84 public and private factories, including North China Electric Power Company, 409 Automobile Repair Factory, and Allen Pharmaceutical Factory, have signed up to participate in the exhibition. Exhibits include various industrial products, models, statistical charts, product photos, industrial improvement plans, and more. Yesterday (the 25th), the Industrial Association convened a meeting of representatives from various units planning to participate in the exhibition, where detailed discussions were held on various matters related to the exhibition.
    　　　　　(Bai Qing)

### 交流工业生产经验　平市将举行工业品展览
柏青
1949-05-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　交流工业生产经验
  　平市将举行工业品展览
    【本报讯】北平市工业会为互相交流工业产品之生产经验，定于六月六日于中山公园中山堂举行全市工业展览。现报名参加展览者有华北电业公司、四○九汽车修理厂、爱伦药厂等公私营工厂八十四个单位。展览品有各项工业产品、模型、统计图表、产品照片、工业改进计划等多种。昨日（二十五日）由工业会召集拟参加此次展览各单位代表会议，对展览会中之各种事宜作了详尽讨论。
    　　　　　（柏青）
