Title: The Greek Democratic Army Wins Counter-Encirclement Battle in Roumeli! Annihilates a Company of the Enemy in the Thessaly Region

Author:
Date: 1949-05-06
Page: 3

    Free Greece News Agency Report: After suffering defeat in the city of Lidoriki, the Greek Royalist Fascist army recently attempted to retake the city and encircle the Democratic Army forces in Roumeli. The Royalist Fascist army increased its combat troops, but failed again. The Democratic Army inflicted serious losses on the Royalist Fascist army and captured rich spoils of war. In the Thessaly region, Democratic Army forces annihilated a company of the Royalist army. In this area, the Royalist Fascists lost a total of 235 officers and soldiers.

### 希民主军在罗米利亚  反包围作战获胜  色萨利地区歼敌一个连

1949-05-06
第3版()
专栏：

    希民主军在罗米利亚
    反包围作战获胜
    色萨利地区歼敌一个连
    【新华社北平五日电】自由希腊通讯社报道：希腊保皇法西斯军在里多瑞基昂城遭受失败以后，日前企图重占该城并包围罗米利亚的民主军部队。保皇法西斯军增加了作战部队，但已再度失败。民主军予保皇法西斯军以严重损失，缴获丰富的战利品。色萨利地区，民主军部队歼灭了保皇军一个连。在这一地区，保皇法西斯损失官兵共达二百三十五人。
