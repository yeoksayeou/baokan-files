Title: Two Battalions of South Korean Puppet Army Mutiny, Fully Armed, Enter North Korea, Warmly Welcomed by North Korean People
Author:
Date: 1949-05-09
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 7th: Pyongyang Radio broadcast: A unit of the 1st Battalion of the 8th Regiment of the 6th Brigade of the South Korean Lee Seung-man puppet government army, mutinied on the morning of the 5th in the Chuncheon area south of the 38th parallel, and entered the Haicheng area of North Korea with full armament. Following this, the 2nd Battalion of the 8th Regiment, stationed in the Hongcheon area, also mutinied, carrying full armament and heading north into the Imise area. The two mutiny battalions were warmly welcomed by the border guards of the Korean Republic. (Note: The above place names are all transliterations.)
    Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 8th: Pyongyang Radio broadcast on the 7th: The two battalions of South Korean mutiny soldiers were warmly welcomed by the people of North Korea upon entering North Korea. They were greeted at every station along the way by representatives of the People's Committee of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, various democratic parties, mass organizations, and residents.

### 南鲜伪军两营起义  全副武装开入北鲜  受到北朝鲜人民热烈欢迎

1949-05-09
第3版()
专栏：

    南鲜伪军两营起义  全副武装开入北鲜
    受到北朝鲜人民热烈欢迎
    【新华社北平七日电】平壤电台广播：南鲜李承晚傀儡政府军第六旅八团一营一部，于五日晨在北纬三十八度以南的春城地区起义，并携带全副武装开入北朝鲜的海城地区。接着，驻在洪城地区的八团二营，亦举行起义，携带全副武装向北开入伊米塞地区。两营起义部队受到朝鲜共和国边防军的热烈欢迎。（按：以上地名均系译音）。
    【新华社北平八日电】平壤电台七日广播：两营南朝鲜起义官兵，在开入北朝鲜时，受到北朝鲜人民的热烈欢迎。他们在沿途各站都受到朝鲜人民民主共和国人民委员会、各民主党派和各民众团体的代表及居民的招待。
