Title: New Leadership of the Italian Socialist Party Calls on the Whole Party to Support Agricultural Workers
Author:
Date: 1949-05-26
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 24th: TASS, Rome: The second meeting of the new leadership of the Italian Socialist Party was held on the 21st. This meeting was to discuss organizational and political issues. The leadership decided to convene a meeting of representatives from various regions of the party in the next few days, with the participation of members of the Central Committee, secretaries of regional committees, secretaries of labor committees, and members of the lower and upper houses.
    After discussing the progress of the agricultural workers' strike, the Socialist Party leadership protested the police authorities' blatant violation of the constitution, interference in trade union struggles, and attempts to undermine the resistance of the working people, and called on the entire party to vigorously support the agricultural workers' struggle.

### 意社会党新领导机构  号召全党支援农业工人

1949-05-26
第3版()
专栏：

    意社会党新领导机构
    号召全党支援农业工人
    【新华社北平二十四日电】塔斯社罗马讯：意大利社会党新领导机构第二次会议于二十一日举行。这一次会议是为了讨论组织与政治问题。领导机构决定在今后数日内召开各区党代表会议，党的中央委员、区委书记、劳工委员会书记、下议员与上议员均将参加。
    社会党领导机构在讨论了农业工人罢工的进展以后，对于警察当局公然违犯宪法，干涉工会斗争，意图破坏劳动人民的抵抗，表示抗议，并号召全党大力援助农业工人的斗争。
