Title: Employee Salary Millet Equivalent Set at Fifty-Eight

Author:
Date: 1949-05-30
Page: 2

    Employee Salary Millet Equivalent Set at Fifty-Eight
    [This Newspaper] According to the relevant departments such as the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the millet equivalent for the salary of employees in various organs and enterprises in Ping City for the second half of May has been assessed and will be issued at 58 yuan per catty.

### 职工薪资米折价五十八

1949-05-30
第2版()
专栏：

    职工薪资米折价五十八
    【本报讯】五月份下半月平市各机关、企业部门职工薪资小米折价，经市总工会等有关部门评定，按每斤五十八元发给。
