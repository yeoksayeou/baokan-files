Title: Greek Democratic Army Wipes Out 12,000 Enemy Troops in April
Author:
Date: 1949-05-05
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 4th: Free Greece Radio broadcast: In April, the Greek Democratic Army's offensive in the Grammos mountain area caused the Royalist-Fascist army to lose 12,848 men. In the first four months of this year, the Royalist-Fascist army lost 30,500 men. The Royalist-Fascist army headquarters has投入all available troops, including recently recruited reserves, into the battles in the Grammos mountain area.

### 希民主军四月份作战  歼敌一万二千

1949-05-05
第3版()
专栏：

    希民主军四月份作战
    歼敌一万二千
    【新华社北平四日电】自由希腊电台广播：希腊民主军部队四月份在格拉姆斯山区的攻势，使保皇法西斯军损失一万二千八百四十八人。保皇法西斯军在本年的头四个月中损失了三万零五百人。保皇法西斯军司令部已将一切可用的部队，包括最近征募的预备队在内，全部投入格拉姆斯山区的战斗中。
