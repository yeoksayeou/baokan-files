Title: Tianjin and Beijing Newspapers Adjust Price Agreement
Author:
Date: 1949-05-31
Page: 1

Following the adjustment of prices by Tianjin and Beijing newspapers since May 16th, and after a half-month of implementation, there are still some inconsistencies. It is hereby jointly agreed that, starting June 1st, the minimum prices for various types of newspapers will be as follows:

　　一、Broadsheet (对开) four pages, white newsprint 20 yuan, colored newsprint 15 yuan;
　　一、Quarto (四开) eight pages, white newsprint 20 yuan, colored newsprint 15 yuan;
　　一、Quarto (四开) six pages, white newsprint 15 yuan, colored newsprint 10 yuan;
　　一、Quarto (四开) four pages, all 10 yuan.

Wholesale and preferential rates for workers and students shall not be lower than 70% of the listed price. Additional postage is required for deliveries to other locations.

### 平津各报调整报价协议

1949-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　平津各报调整报价协议
    平津各报自五月十六日起调整价格以来经半月来实施的结果，尚有若干未尽统一之处，兹自六月一日起，共同议定各型报纸最低价格如下：
    一、对开四版，白报纸二十元，片艳纸十五元；
    一、四开八版，白报纸二十元，片艳纸十五元；
    一、四开六版，白报纸十五元，片艳纸十元；
    一、四开四版，一律十元。
    批发及优待工人、学生不得低于七折，外埠须加邮运费。
