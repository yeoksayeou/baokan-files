Title: The People's Liberation Army Advances into Northeastern Jiangxi, Liberates Jingdezhen and Leping, and Encircles and Annihilates a Fleeing Enemy Army; Northern Shaanxi Liberated, including Tongchuan, Pucheng, and Four Other Cities
Author:
Date: 1949-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Yangtze River Front, 3rd: A unit of the People's Liberation Army, on the 30th, in the Guanwangmiao area, 40 li south of Leping City in northeastern Jiangxi, completely surrounded the fleeing enemy Sixty-eighth Army of the Fourth Pacification Zone. After battle, most of the enemy was killed or captured, and the remaining enemy is being surrounded and annihilated.

Xinhua News Agency, Yangtze River Front, 3rd: The People's Liberation Army, advancing towards northeastern Jiangxi, intercepted the fleeing enemy Ninety-sixth Army between Wannian and Leping on the 29th of last month, capturing over 330 enemy soldiers alive. During their southward advance, the Liberation Army also killed or wounded over 200 enemy soldiers in the area southwest of Zhide to northwest of Fuliang, and captured alive the regimental commander and deputy commander of the 422nd Regiment of the 141st Division and over 300 others. They seized four mortars, fifteen light and heavy machine guns, over ten vehicles, and a portion of other military supplies.

Xinhua News Agency, Yangtze River Front, 3rd: A unit of the People's Liberation Army, advancing towards eastern Jiangxi Province, liberated Jingdezhen (i.e., Fuliang City), famous for its porcelain production, and Leping County to its south on April 29th. On the way to Leping, the Liberation Army captured over 320 fleeing enemy soldiers from the 174th Division and other units. Jingdezhen is located in the northeastern part of Jiangxi Province, with a population of about 160,000. When the Liberation Army entered the town, it was warmly welcomed by the people of the entire city.

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi Front, 3rd: The local armed forces of the People's Liberation Army in northern Shaanxi successively liberated the four cities of Tongchuan, Pucheng, Yaoxian, and Fuping on the 28th of last month. The bandit troops in various places fled south in a panic.

### 大军挺进江西东北  解放景德乐平围歼逃敌一军  陕中解放铜川蒲城等四城

1949-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    大军挺进江西东北
    解放景德乐平围歼逃敌一军
    陕中解放铜川蒲城等四城
    【新华社长江前线三日电】人民解放军某部，三十日在江西东北部乐平城南四十里关王庙地区，将南逃之敌第四绥区六十八军团团包围，经战斗后，已毙俘敌大部，余敌正被围歼中。
    【新华社长江前线三日电】向江西东北挺进的人民解放军，于上月二十九日解放景德、乐平两地后，三十日在万年与乐平间，截击南逃之敌九十六军，生俘敌三百三十余名。解放军南进途中，并曾在至德西南至浮梁西北地区，毙伤敌两百余，生俘敌一四一师四二二团正副团长以下三百余人。缴迫击炮四门，轻重机枪十五挺，汽车十余辆，其他军用品一部。
    【新华社长江前线三日电】向江西省东部挺进的人民解放军一部，四月二十九日解放以出产瓷器著名的景德镇（即浮梁城）及其以南的乐平县城。在向乐平挺进的途中，解放军俘获溃散之敌一七四师等部三百二十余人，景德镇在江西省的东北部，全市人口约十六万。当解放军进驻该镇时，受到全市人民的热烈欢迎。
    【新华社陕中前线三日电】人民解放军陕中地方武装于上月二十八日相继解放铜川、蒲城、耀县及富平四城，各地匪军仓皇南逃。
