Title: The People's Evening News is Published Today!
Author:
Date: 1949-05-01
Page: 1

The People's Evening News is published today!
    Contents include:
    Local news of the day, important domestic and international news, youth self-cultivation, stories of the Anti-Japanese War, woodcuts, comic strips, lyrics, riddles, character-based paintings, questions and answers, Tianjin and Beijing's midday market quotations, solutions to currency exchange and remittance issues... The May 2nd evening edition will be published as usual.
