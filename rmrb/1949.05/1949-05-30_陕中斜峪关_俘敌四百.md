Title: Shaanxi Central, Xieyuguan: 400 Enemy Soldiers Captured
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shaanxi Central Front, 29th: The People's Liberation Army, sweeping up remnants of bandits south of the Wei River, routed the Hu bandit 36th Army in the Gaodian Town and Xieyuguan area southwest of ＠ County on the 24th, capturing over 400 enemy soldiers, including a regimental commander, seizing five trucks loaded with flour and some other weapons, ammunition, and military supplies, and destroying three grain-transporting trucks.

### 陕中斜峪关　俘敌四百

1949-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕中斜峪关
  　俘敌四百
    【新华社陕中前线二十九日电】向渭河以南扫荡残匪之人民解放军，二十四日于＠县西南高店镇、斜峪关地区击溃胡匪三十六军，俘敌团长以下四百余人，缴获满载面粉之汽车五辆及其他武器弹药军用品一部，击毁运粮汽车三辆。
