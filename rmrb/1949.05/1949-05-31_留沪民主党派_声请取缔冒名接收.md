Title: Democratic Parties in Shanghai Request the Banning of Impersonation and Takeover

Author: 
Date: 1949-05-31
Page: 1

　　[North China Xinhua Broadcasting Station Broadcast] Shanghai, 30th dispatch: Responsible persons of democratic parties remaining in Shanghai, including Luo Longji, Shi Liang of the China Democratic League, Chen Mingfu, Wu Yi Wu of the Sanmin Zhuyi Comrades Association, Guo Chuntao, Yang Hu of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Min Ganghou, Feng Yidai of the China National Salvation Association, Lian Ruiqi, Shen Baowen of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, etc., jointly issued a statement on the 29th, requesting the Shanghai Military Control Commission of the People's Liberation Army to severely ban those unscrupulous elements who, under the guise of democratic parties, engage in deception and illicit takeover. The original statement reads: Now that the Shanghai Military Control Commission of the Chinese People's Liberation Army has been established, all takeover work in Shanghai should be handled uniformly by the Military Control Commission. The democratic parties have never intervened. However, some people have been engaging in deception and illicit takeover under the guise of democratic parties. In addition to requesting the Military Control Commission to severely ban this behavior, this statement is hereby issued.

### 留沪民主党派　声请取缔冒名接收

1949-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　留沪民主党派
  　声请取缔冒名接收
    【北平新华广播电台广播】上海三十号消息：留在上海的中国民主同盟负责人罗隆基、史良，三民主义同志联合会陈明甫、吴艺五，中国国民党革命委员会郭春涛、杨虎，中国人民救国会闵刚侯、冯亦代，中国农工民主党连瑞琦、申葆文等，二十九号联名发表声明，请人民解放军上海市军管会严厉取缔若干假借民主党派名义招摇闯骗，冒名接收不良分子。声明的原文说：现在中国人民解放军上海军管会业已成立，所有上海一切应行接收工作概由军管会统一处理，各民主党派从未过问，竟有人假借民主党派名义，在外招摇闯骗，擅自接收，除请军管会严厉取缔外，特此声明。
