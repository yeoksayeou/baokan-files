Title: Kobe Workers Demonstrate Against City Council's Restriction of Human Rights
Author:
Date: 1949-05-24
Page: 3
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 21st: Tokyo dispatch: Kobe workers held a demonstration near the Kobe City Council on the 20th, opposing the so-called "Security Ordinance for Controlling Mass Demonstrations" passed by the council on the same day, which restricts the people's rights to freedom. On the 19th, Kobe workers had already gathered to oppose the city council's discussion of the bill. Japanese police attacked the assembled and demonstrating workers on both the 19th and 20th. As a result of the clashes between the police and demonstrating workers on the 20th, thirteen people were injured, and several workers were arrested.

### 神户工人示威　反对市议会限制人权

1949-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　神户工人示威
  　反对市议会限制人权
    【新华社北平二十一日电】东京讯：神户工人二十日在神户市参议会附近举行示威游行，反对该议会于是日通过限制人民自由权利的所谓“管制群众示威之保安条例”。十九日，神户工人即曾集会反对市议会讨论该法案。日本警察曾于十九、二十两日接连对集会与示威的工人进攻。二十日，警察与示威工人冲突的结果，十三人受伤，并有工人若干名被捕。
