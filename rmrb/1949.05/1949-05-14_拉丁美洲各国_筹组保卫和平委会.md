Title: Latin American Countries Prepare to Form Peace Defense Committees
Author:
Date: 1949-05-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th: Polish News Agency, Mexico: The president of the Latin American Labor Federation, Toledano, who returned from the Paris Peace Congress, said: This congress is one of the most important political events in recent years. He said: All Latin American representatives who participated in the Paris Congress have decided to immediately organize national peace defense committees in their own countries, and a grand Latin American Peace Defense Congress will be held in Mexico City on August 1st.

### 拉丁美洲各国  筹组保卫和平委会

1949-05-14
第3版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲各国
    筹组保卫和平委会
    【新华社北平十三日电】波兰通讯社墨西哥讯：自巴黎和平大会返国的拉丁美洲劳工联合会主席托列达诺说：这一大会是近几年来最重要的政治事件之一。他说：参加巴黎大会的一切拉丁美洲代表已决定立即在国内组织全国保卫和平委员会，并将于八月一日在墨西哥城举行盛大的拉丁美洲保卫和平大会。
