Title: Hungarian Trade Union Demands UN Rescue Greek Patriots
Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 25th. Budapest dispatch: The Hungarian Trade Union Council has sent a telegram to the United Nations, demanding that the United Nations rescue the hundreds of Greek patriots sentenced to death by the royalist fascists.

### 匈工会要求联合国　营救希爱国者

1949-05-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　匈工会要求联合国
  　营救希爱国者
    【新华社北平二十五日电】布达佩斯讯：匈牙利职工会委员会致电联合国，要求联合国营救百名为保皇法西斯判处死刑的希腊爱国者。
