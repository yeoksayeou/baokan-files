Title: Literary and Art Workers Xia Yan and Others Arrive in Beiping; Zhang Junxiang and Others Arrive in Yantai
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-05-11
Page: 1

    Literary and Art Workers
    Xia Yan and Others Arrive in Beiping
    Zhang Junxiang and Others Arrive in Yantai
    [This Newspaper] Literary and art workers Xia Yan, Xu Dixin, Chen Jingrong, Jiang Muliang, Jiang Tianzuo, and others arrived in Beiping via Tianjin on the 9th and 10th, respectively, to prepare for the upcoming National Congress of Literary and Art Workers in Beiping. Xia Yan said that the cultural circles in Hong Kong are now actively preparing to welcome the liberation of Shanghai and Guangzhou. Progressive cultural workers are actively collecting mass propaganda materials such as songs, radio dramas, and street plays, as well as propaganda materials on the policies of the liberated areas, and are rushing to collect various literary and artistic works related to the Kuomintang-controlled areas, Southeast Asia, and Hong Kong for the exhibition at the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers. (Bai Sheng)
    [Another Dispatch] Film and drama representatives Zhang Junxiang, Bai Yang, Ke Ling, Gu Zhongyi, and others, who are preparing to attend the upcoming National Congress of Literary and Art Workers, have reportedly arrived in Yantai from Hong Kong and will arrive in Beiping within the next few days. In addition, representatives attending the conference, including Zhou Erfu, Fu Luofei, Zhang Wenyuan, Huang Yaomian, Zhong Jingwen, and Shen Qiyu, will also arrive in Tianjin from Hong Kong soon. (Bai Sheng)

### 文艺工作者  夏衍等抵平  张骏祥等抵烟台
柏生
1949-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    文艺工作者
    夏衍等抵平
    张骏祥等抵烟台
    【本报讯】文艺工作者夏衍、许涤新、陈敬容、蒋牧良、蒋天佐等，于前昨（九、十）两日由香港经天津分别抵平，准备出席即将在平召开的全国文学艺术工作者代表大会。夏衍谈称：香港文化界现正积极准备迎接上海、广州的解放。进步的文化工作者纷纷搜集歌曲、广播剧、街头剧等群众性的宣传材料，及关于解放区政策的宣传资料，并赶集全国文学艺术工作者代表大会展览会上有关国统区、南洋、香港等地的各种文艺作品。（柏生）
    【又讯】准备出席不久召开的文学艺术工作者代表大会的影剧界代表张骏祥、白杨、柯灵、顾仲彝等，闻已由香港抵达烟台，日内即将来平。另外，参加大会的代表周而复、符罗飞、张文元、黄药眠、钟敬文、沈起予等，不久亦将由港抵津。（柏生）
