Title: The Reactionary Indian Government Remains a Part of British Imperialism; British Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference Concludes
Author:
Date: 1949-05-01
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 29th: Reports from various sources: The British Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference concluded on the 28th after a week of meetings. The meeting was conducted in extreme secrecy. The British government's communique issued on the 28th only mentioned the decision on the issue of India's relationship with the British Commonwealth. The communique stated: "The Government of India has notified the governments of other countries in the British Commonwealth that it hopes to transform India into a "sovereign independent republic" under the new constitution that is about to be implemented. However, the Indian government has stated that India hopes to continue to be a full member of the British Commonwealth and accept the British King as the head of the Commonwealth. The United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, India, Pakistan, and Ceylon hereby declare that they are still willing to maintain unity.

### 印度反动政府仍是英帝一分子  英联邦总理会议闭幕

1949-05-01
第3版()
专栏：

    印度反动政府
    仍是英帝一分子
    英联邦总理会议闭幕
    【新华社北平二十九日电】各方报道：英联邦总理会议于一周会议后已于二十八日闭幕。会议是在极端秘密中进行。英政府于二十八日发表的公报中，仅提及印度与英联邦关系问题的决定。公报说：“印度政府通知不列颠联邦其他各国政府，希望根据行将施行的新宪法，将印度变成一“主权独立的共和国。”然而印度政府曾经申言，印度希望继续为不列颠联邦中十足的一员，并接受英王作为联邦的首脑。联合王国，加拿大、澳大利亚、新西兰、南非、印度、巴基斯坦、和锡兰特此宣称，他们仍愿保持联合。
