Title: Telegrams Open Between Bengbu and Tianjin, Beijing, etc.
Author:
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 10th. Telegram services between Bengbu city and Beijing, Tianjin, Jinan, Xuzhou, and other places officially opened on the first of this month, and can be forwarded to Nanjing via Jinan.
    Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 10th. The Central Plains Liberated Area has opened postal services with newly liberated cities such as Nanjing, Wuxi, Wuhu, Anqing, Zhenjiang, and Suzhou.

### 蚌埠与平津等地通电报

1949-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    蚌埠与平津等地通电报
    【新华社济南十日电】蚌埠市与北平、天津、济南、徐州等地的电报已于本月一日正式开放，并可经济南发南京。
    【新华社郑州十日电】中原解放区已与南京、无锡、芜湖、安庆、镇江、苏州等新解放区城市通邮。
