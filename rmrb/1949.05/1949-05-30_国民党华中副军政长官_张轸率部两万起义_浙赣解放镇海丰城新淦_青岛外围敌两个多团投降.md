Title: Kuomintang Deputy Chief of the Central China Military and Political Administration, Zhang Zhen, Leads 20,000 Troops in Uprising; Zhenhai, Fengcheng, and Xingan in Zhejiang and Jiangxi Liberated; Two Kuomintang Regiments Surrender on the Outskirts of Qingdao
Author:
Date: 1949-05-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, May 29th: Zhang Zhen, Deputy Chief of the Kuomintang Central China Military and Political Administration, concurrently Chairman of Henan Province and Commander of the 19th Army Corps, led his troops in an uprising around Heshengqiao and Jinkou, south of Wuhan, on the 15th of this month, when Wuhan was liberated, and joined the People's Liberation Army. On the 14th, when White Bandit Chongxi urgently requested help from Wuhan, he forced the army corps to retreat south. The 312th, 313th, and 314th Divisions of the 128th Army, the 369th Division of the 127th Army, and approximately 20,000 officers and soldiers from one army headquarters and four divisions, unwilling to obey the reactionary orders, rose up under the leadership of Army Corps Commander Zhang Zhen. They repelled the encirclement and interception by White Bandit forces around Heshengqiao and joined the People's Liberation Army on the 16th, receiving a warm welcome from the local military and civilians.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, May 29th: The People's Liberation Army, having liberated Ningbo, continued to advance eastward, liberating the coastal town of Zhenhai on the morning of the 26th at 9:00 AM.
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, May 29th: The People's Liberation Army on the Zhejiang-Jiangxi front advanced south along the Gan River, liberating the two counties of Fengcheng and Xingan, east of the Gan River south of Nanchang, on the 21st and 22nd. The defending enemy fled.
    Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, May 29th: Two entire regiments and parts of three regiments of Kuomintang troops on the outskirts of Qingdao surrendered to the People's Liberation Army. The 6th Regiment of the 2nd Security Brigade of Shandong Province, stationed on Xuejiadao on the west coast of Jiaozhou Bay, and two companies of the 1st Regiment of the same brigade, stationed on Donglianhua Mountain in Jimo, defected to our side on the 27th. Prior to this, the 810th Regiment of the 270th Division of the 10th Army of the Kuomintang, stationed in the area south of Jimo, and most of the 2nd Regiment and one battalion of the 3rd Regiment of the Independent Brigade of the 11th Pacification Zone of the Kuomintang, stationed at the Yingkou stronghold north of Jimo city, surrendered to our garrison troops on the 24th and 25th respectively. The Kuomintang troops who surrendered at the Yingkou stronghold north of Jimo city brought with them one mortar, twenty-one light and heavy machine guns, and over one hundred long and short guns.

### 国民党华中副军政长官　张轸率部两万起义  浙赣解放镇海丰城新淦　青岛外围敌两个多团投降

1949-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　国民党华中副军政长官
  　张轸率部两万起义
    浙赣解放镇海丰城新淦
  　青岛外围敌两个多团投降
    【新华社汉口二十九日电】国民党华中军政长官公署副长官兼河南省主席兼十九兵团司令官张轸，于本月十五日武汉解放时，率部于武汉以南的贺胜桥、金口一带起义，加入人民解放军。十四日，白匪崇禧于武汉告急时，强迫该兵团向南撤逃。该兵团所属之一二八军三一二师、三一三师、三一四师，一二七军的三六九师，及一个军部四个师官兵约二万人，不愿服从反动命令，乃在兵团司令张轸率领下起义。在贺胜桥一带击退白匪的包围和截击，并于十六日加入人民解放军，受到当地军民的热烈欢迎。
    【新华社杭州二十九日电】解放宁波的人民解放军，继续向东挺进，二十六日上午九时解放沿海的镇海县城。
    【新华社南昌二十九日电】浙赣前线人民解放军沿赣江向南挺进，二十一、二十二两日解放南昌以南赣江东岸的丰城、新淦两座县城，守敌逃窜。
    【新华社济南二十九日电】青岛外围的国民党军两个团全部和三个团的各一部向人民解放军投降。驻胶州湾西岸薛家岛的国民党军山东保安第二旅第六团及驻即墨东莲花山的该旅第一团两个连，于二十七日向我投诚。在此以前，驻即墨以南地区的国民党的十军二七○师八一○团和驻即墨城北营口据点的国民党军十一绥区独立旅二团大部及三团一个营，于二十四、二十五两日分别向我驻军投降。即墨城北营口据点投降的国民党军，携来迫击炮一门，轻重机枪二十一挺，长短枪百余支。
