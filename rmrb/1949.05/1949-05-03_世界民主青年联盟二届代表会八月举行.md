Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Second Congress to be Held in August
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-03
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 30th. Polish News Agency dispatch: The Second Congress of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, which has 70 million members, will be held in Budapest from August 14th to 28th. The congress will be attended by 10,000 delegates representing member organizations from 50 countries. The congress will work under the slogan of striving for unity, lasting peace, democracy, and national independence.

### 世界民主青年联盟二届代表会八月举行

1949-05-03
第3版()
专栏：

    世界民主青年联盟二届代表会八月举行
    【新华社北平三十日电】波兰通讯社讯：拥有七千万会员的世界民主青年联盟第二届代表大会，将于八月十四日至二十八日在布达佩斯举行。出席大会的代表将达一万名，代表五十个国家的会员。大会将在为争取团结、持久和平、民主与民族独立而斗争的口号下进行工作。
