Title: The Ping Municipal People's Government Establishes the Pingxi Mining Affairs Office
Author:
Date: 1949-05-15
Page: 2

    The Ping Municipal People's Government Establishes the Pingxi Mining Affairs Office
    [This newspaper's dispatch] In order to strengthen the leadership and management of the mining industry in the Pingxi area, develop state-owned coal mines, and rectify private coal mines, the Ping Municipal People's Government has specially established the "Beijing Municipal People's Government Pingxi Mining Affairs Office," and appointed Cai Ping as the Director. From now on, all matters related to mining within the jurisdiction of various public and private mines will be handled by this office.

### 平市人民政府设平西矿务处

1949-05-15
第2版()
专栏：

    平市人民政府设平西矿务处
    【本报讯】平市人民政府为加强平西地区矿业之领导与管理，发展国营煤矿及整顿私营煤矿，特成立“北平市人民政府平西矿务处”，并委任蔡平为处长。此后凡辖境内各公私矿窑有关矿务之一切事宜均由该处办理。
