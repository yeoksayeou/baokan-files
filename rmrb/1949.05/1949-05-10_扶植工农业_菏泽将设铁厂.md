Title: Supporting Industry and Agriculture: Heze to Establish Iron Factory
Author:
Date: 1949-05-10
Page: 2

    Supporting Industry and Agriculture
    Heze to Establish Iron Factory
    [Ji Lu Yu Telegram] In order to support the light industry of Heze and the agricultural production of various regions in Henan, the Ji Lu Yu Industrial Company (formerly the Industry Department of the Ji Lu Yu Administrative Office) is now actively preparing to establish the Heze Branch Iron Factory within Heze City. The factory will manufacture a large number of various agricultural implements for farmers. In addition, the factory will also repair various machines. Currently, the factory is actively purchasing pig iron and is expected to officially start production in mid-May.

### 扶植工农业  菏泽将设铁厂

1949-05-10
第2版()
专栏：

    扶植工农业
    菏泽将设铁厂
    【冀鲁豫电】冀鲁豫区实业公司（系前冀鲁豫行政公署工业处）为扶植菏泽轻工业及河南各地之农业生产，现正于菏泽城内积极筹建铁工厂菏泽分厂。该工厂今将大量制造各种农具供给农民使用。此外，该厂并修理各种机器。目前该厂正多方收购生铁，可望于本月中旬正式开始生产。
