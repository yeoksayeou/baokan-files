Title: Students Strike Against Reactionary Government's "Suppression" Bill; Over 30,000 Japanese Students Strike; Coal Miners Enter Third Round of Strikes
Author:
Date: 1949-05-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 14th: Tokyo dispatch: 35,000 students belonging to the Japanese Student Union held a strike in the Osaka-Kyoto area on the afternoon of the 12th to protest against the Japanese reactionary authorities' reduction of education budget funds and the "suppression" bill imposed on students and teachers.
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 14th: Tokyo dispatch: Japan's 450,000 coal miners entered their third round of rolling strikes starting on the 14th. The strikes, which began on the 3rd, have already deprived the American and Japanese reactionaries of more than 200,000 tons of urgently needed coal.

### 抗议反动政府“镇压”法案　日学生三万余罢课  煤矿工人进入第三轮回罢工

1949-05-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　抗议反动政府“镇压”法案
  　日学生三万余罢课
    煤矿工人进入第三轮回罢工
    【新华社北平十四日电】东京讯：日本学生联合会所属三万五千名学生，十二日下午在大坂京都地区举行罢课，抗议日本反动当局削减教育预算费及加于学生和教师的“镇压”法案。
    【新华社北平十四日电】东京讯：日本四十五万煤矿工人自十四日起进入第三次轮回罢工。自三日开始以来的罢工中，已使美日反动派少得二十多万吨急需的煤。
