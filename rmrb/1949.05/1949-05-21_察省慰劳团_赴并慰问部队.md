Title: Chahar Province Comfort Group Visits and Extends Regards to Troops in Taiyuan

Author:

Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 1

Chahar Province Comfort Group Visits and Extends Regards to Troops
    [This dispatch] Organized by the Party, government, military, and people of Chahar Province, a comfort group led by Comrade Sun Yupei, President of the Chahar Province People's Court, carrying a letter of appreciation and a comfort fund of one million Renminbi, rushed to Taiyuan on the 16th of this month to express their regards to the troops. (Communication Group of the Civil Affairs Department of the Chahar Provincial People's Government)

### 察省慰劳团　赴并慰问部队

1949-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　察省慰劳团
  　赴并慰问部队
    【本报讯】察哈尔省党政军民组织的慰劳团，由察哈尔省人民法院院长孙雨培同志率领携带慰问信，慰劳款人民币一百万元，于本月十六日赶赴太原慰劳部队。（察省人民政府民政处通讯小组）
