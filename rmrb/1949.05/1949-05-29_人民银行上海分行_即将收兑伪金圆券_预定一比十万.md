Title: People's Bank of China Shanghai Branch to Redeem Fake Gold Yuan Notes; Exchange Rate Set at 1 to 100,000
Author: Yu
Date: 1949-05-29
Page: 1

　　People's Bank of China Shanghai Branch
  　About to Redeem Fake Gold Yuan Notes
  　Exchange Rate Set at 1 to 100,000
    [Report from this newspaper] The head office of the People's Bank of China contacted the Shanghai Branch via telegram at 2:00 PM yesterday (the 28th). It is reported that the Shanghai Branch has entered the city with the army and is fully prepared to start business next Monday, and will redeem fake Gold Yuan notes. The exchange rate between Renminbi and fake Gold Yuan notes is expected to be 1 to 100,000. (Yu)

### 人民银行上海分行　即将收兑伪金圆券　预定一比十万
宇
1949-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民银行上海分行
  　即将收兑伪金圆券
  　预定一比十万
    【本报讯】中国人民银行总行昨日（二十八日）下午二时已与上海分行以电讯取得联系，据悉：上海分行已随军进入市内，已筹备就绪，准备下星期一开始营业，并进行收兑伪金圆券。人民币与伪金圆券比值预定为一比十万元。（宇）
