Title: UN General Assembly Closes: Issue of Italian Colonies Postponed to September for Discussion
Author:
Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th: New York Dispatch: The Third Session of the Second United Nations General Assembly closed on the afternoon of the 18th. This session began on April 5th and lasted a total of 46 days. On the 18th, when the Bevin-Sforza Plan was rejected, the General Assembly passed another resolution postponing the decision on the handling of the former Italian colonies to the September General Assembly.

### 联大会议闭幕　意殖民地问题  延至九月讨论

1949-05-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　联大会议闭幕
  　意殖民地问题  延至九月讨论
    【新华社北平二十日电】纽约讯：第三届第二次联合国大会已于十八日下午闭幕。此次会议开始于四月五日，历时共四十六天。十八日，当贝文——史佛卓计划被否决时，大会又通过另一决议，将前意属殖民地处理问题延至九月大会再行决定。
