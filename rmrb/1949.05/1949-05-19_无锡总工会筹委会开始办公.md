Title: Wuxi Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee Begins Work
Author:
Date: 1949-05-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Wuxi, 18th—The Wuxi Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee began operations on the 12th of this month. In addition to preparing for the establishment of trade unions in various industries and factories, its current work plan focuses on assisting the Military Control Commission in resolving the resumption of work in factories and the employment of workers. Wuxi is a well-known light industrial city with many factories and a large number of workers.

### 无锡总工会筹委会开始办公

1949-05-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　无锡总工会筹委会开始办公
    【新华社无锡十八日电】无锡市总工会筹备委员会已于本月十二日开始办公。其目前工作计划除筹备成立各业各厂工会外，着重协助军管会解决工厂的复工和工人就业问题。按无锡为著名轻工业城市，工厂林立，工人众多。
