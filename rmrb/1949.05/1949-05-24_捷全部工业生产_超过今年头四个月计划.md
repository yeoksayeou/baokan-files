Title: Czechoslovakia's Total Industrial Production Exceeds Planned Target for First Four Months of This Year
Author:
Date: 1949-05-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 22nd: TASS, Prague: Czechoslovakia's total industrial production, excluding the food industry, has completed 102.2% of the original plan for the first four months of this year. The percentages of completion for various industries are as follows: the mining and coal mining industry completed 100.5% of the four-month plan, the electric power industry completed 102.5%, the iron and steel industry completed 104.1%, the hardware industry completed 99.9%, the chemical industry completed 101.9%, building materials and ceramics completed 102.1%, and the textile and clothing industry completed 106%.

### 捷全部工业生产　超过今年头四个月计划

1949-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷全部工业生产
  　超过今年头四个月计划
    【新华社北平二十二日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克全部工业，除食品工业以外，今年头四个月都已经完成了原定计划的百分之一百零二点二。各种工业完成计划的百分比如下：采矿与采煤工业完成了四个月计划的百分之一百点五，电力工业完成了百分之一百零二点五，钢铁工业完成了百分之一百零四点一，五金工业完成了百分之九十九点九，化学工业完成了百分之一百零一点九，建筑材料与陶器完成了百分之一百零二点一，纺织与被服工业完成了百分之一百零六。
