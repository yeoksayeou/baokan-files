Title: North China Beiping Party and Government Organs Host Banquet for North China Workers' Representative Visiting Group
Author:
Date: 1949-05-17
Page: 1
　　North China Beiping Party and Government Organs
　　Host Banquet for North China Workers' Representative Visiting Group

　　[Report from this newspaper] The North China Workers' Representative Visiting Group to Beiping toured the Summer Palace yesterday. In the afternoon, the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the North China People's Government, the North China Military Region, the Beiping Municipal Committee of the CPC, the Beiping Municipal People's Government, the Beiping Military Control Commission, and the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Federation of Trade Unions jointly hosted a banquet. Bo Yibo of the North China Bureau and Lan Gongwu, Vice Chairman of the North China People's Government, personally attended. A grand welcome party was held in the evening. After Xiao Ming, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a welcome speech, Ye Jianying, Mayor of Beiping, addressed the representatives. He briefly introduced various aspects of Beiping's production and construction work and sincerely welcomed the representatives to offer criticisms and suggestions to improve the work.

### 华北北平党政机关　欢宴华北职工代表参观团

1949-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北北平党政机关
  　欢宴华北职工代表参观团
    【本报讯】华北职工代表来平之参观团，昨日赴颐和园游览。下午由中共中央华北局、华北人民政府、华北军区、中共北平市委、北平人民政府、北平军管会、北平职工总会筹委会联合设宴招待，华北局薄一波同志，华北人民政府副主席蓝公武均亲往参加。晚间举行隆重的欢迎晚会，北平市职工总会筹委会主任萧明同志致欢迎词后，北平市长叶剑英同志向代表们讲话。他扼要的介绍了北平市生产建设工作的各方面情况，恳切欢迎代表们提出批评和建议，以改进工作。
