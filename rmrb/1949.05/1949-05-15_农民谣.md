Title: Peasant Rhyme
Author: Dong Chi
Date: 1949-05-15
Page: 4

    Peasant Rhyme
    Dong Chi
    (I)
    Little brothers and sisters,
    Don't run,
    We've come,
    You're in good hands:
    Fight Chiang's bandits,
    Capture war criminals,
    The days ahead will be good.
    (II)
    Eldest brother and sister-in-law,
    Don't be afraid,
    We poor people
    Are one family,
    Fight Chiang's bandits,
    Divide the land,
    There will be food and drink,
    There is a way.
    (III)
    We will have land,
    We will have soil,
    We will have cattle, horses, carts
    And houses.
    Plant cotton,
    Weave more cloth;
    Plant peanuts,
    Press more oil;
    Also plant sorghum and beans.
    Large granaries overflowing,
    Small granaries streaming,
    We will not worry about life in the future.

### 农民谣
冬池
1949-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    农民谣
    冬池
    （一）
    小弟小妹
    不要跑，
    咱们来了
    你顶好：
    打蒋匪，
    捉战犯
    以后的日子过得好。
    （二）
    大哥大嫂
    不要怕，
    咱们穷人
    是一家，
    打蒋匪，
    分土地，
    有吃有喝
    有办法。  
    （三）  
    也有地，
    也有土，
    也有牛马车辆
    和房屋。
    种棉花，
    多织布；
    种花生，
    多榨油；
    还种高粱和谷豆。
    大囤尖，
    小囤流
    以后生活不发愁。
