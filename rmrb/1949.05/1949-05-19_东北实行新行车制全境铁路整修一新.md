Title: Northeast Implements New Train Operation System, Entire Region's Railways Renovated

Author:
Date: 1949-05-19
Page: 3

[Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Shenyang, 18th - The Shenyang Railway Administration, thanks to the efforts of all its employees, has achieved considerable success in restoring and constructing railways. From the beginning of January to the end of April this year, in four months, they repaired a total of 151 kilometers of railway lines and 40 bridges, replaced 130,000 sleepers, and built many sidings and stations, increasing the train speed from 35 kilometers per hour to 55 kilometers per hour. Some routes have been increased to 75 kilometers per hour. In addition, they also repaired the telecommunications lines, telephones, and various signal lights that had been destroyed by the Kuomintang bandit troops. The overhaul of various vehicles has also exceeded targets, laying a material foundation for the new train operation system. The bureau has begun to implement the new train operation system in its jurisdiction from the 15th.

### 东北实行新行车制全境铁路整修一新

1949-05-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　东北实行新行车制全境铁路整修一新
    【北平新华广播电台广播】沈阳十八号消息：沈阳铁路管理局由于全体职工的努力在恢复和建设铁路工作方面获得了相当成绩，从今年一月初到四月底四个月内，一共修复了一百五十一公里铁路线和四十座桥梁，换了十三万根枕木，修了许多侧线和车站，把行车速度从每点钟三十五公里提高到五十五公里。有些路线提高到七十五公里。此外还修复了曾遭国民党匪军破坏的电讯线路、电话机和各种信号灯。各种车辆的检修也都超过任务，为新行车制打下了物质基础。该局从十五号起已经在所辖地区内开始实行新行车制。
