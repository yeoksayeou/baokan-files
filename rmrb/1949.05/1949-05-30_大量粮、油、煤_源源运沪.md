Title: Large Quantities of Grain, Oil, and Coal are Continuously Transported to Shanghai

Author:

Date: 1949-05-30
Page: 1

　　Large Quantities of Grain, Oil, and Coal
  　are Continuously Transported to Shanghai
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 28th] Large quantities of grain, oil, and coal are being transported to Shanghai by land and water routes. The East China Support-the-Front Committee plans to transport over 144 million jin of grain in the first month, of which the first batch of 20 million jin is already en route, and the remainder is packed and ready to go. The first batch of 1 million jin of raw oil has also been shipped. Large quantities of coal are currently being shipped from the Huainan Coal Mine.

### 大量粮、油、煤　源源运沪

1949-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　大量粮、油、煤
  　源源运沪
    【新华社济南二十八日电】大量的粮、油、煤正由水陆各路源源运往上海市。华东支前委员会准备在第一个月份运送的粮食将达一万万四千万斤以上，现首批两千万斤已在途中，余皆装满待发。首批生油一百万斤亦已启运。大量煤炭则正由淮南煤矿发运中。
