Title: UK House of Commons Approves Aggression Pact, Communist MPs Vote Against
Author:
Date: 1949-05-17
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th: London dispatch: British House of Commons Labor and Conservative Party members jointly voted on the evening of the 12th to approve the North Atlantic Aggression Pact. Two Communist MPs and four left-wing Labor MPs voted against it. War-mongering Conservative leader Churchill, in his speech, lavished praise on the United States for formulating this aggression pact, and said that the absence of Spain was a "serious gap in this strategic arrangement." He demanded that the British government send an ambassador to Madrid.

### 英下院批准侵略公约共产党议员投票反对

1949-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　英下院批准侵略公约共产党议员投票反对
    【新华社北平十五日电】伦敦讯：英下院工党与保守党议员于十二日晚联合投票，通过批准北大西洋侵略公约。共产党议员二人和工党左翼议员四人投反对票。战争贩子保守党领袖邱吉尔在发言中对美国制定这一侵略公约，满口谀扬，并说，没有西班牙参加，是这个“战略布置中的一个严重缺口”。他要求英政府派大使到马德里去。
