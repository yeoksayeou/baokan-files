Title: Tianjin Branch of Weixin Chemical Factory Resumes Production
Author:
Date: 1949-05-03
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 29th—The Tianjin branch of the Weixin Chemical Factory, which had ceased production before liberation, has resumed production after being taken over by the Tianjin Municipal Military Control Commission. The factory's scale ranks second in the North China pigment manufacturing industry, second only to the Qingdao main factory. Its main products are sodium sulfide, vulcanized blue, etc., which have always been sold in North China, Northeast China, and Shanghai. Currently, the factory plans to produce 100 tons of sodium sulfide and 30 tons of vulcanized blue per month.

### 维新化学工厂天津分厂恢复

1949-05-03
第2版()
专栏：

    维新化学工厂天津分厂恢复
    【新华社天津二十九日电】解放前即已停工的维新化学工厂天津分厂，经津市军事管制委员会接管后已恢复生产。该厂规模在华北颜料制造业中占第二位，仅次于青岛总厂。主要产品为硫化碱、硫化青等，一向销行华北、东北及上海等地。目前该厂计划月产硫化碱一百吨，硫化青三十吨。
