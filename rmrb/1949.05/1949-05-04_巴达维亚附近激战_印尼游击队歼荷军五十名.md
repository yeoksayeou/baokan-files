Title: Fierce Fighting Near Batavia: Indonesian Guerrillas Annihilate Fifty Dutch Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1949-05-04
Page: 3

    Batavia area fierce fighting
    Indonesian guerrillas annihilated fifty Dutch soldiers
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st】Batavia dispatch: The Dutch military headquarters announced in a public statement on the 28th that Indonesian guerrillas engaged in fierce fighting with Dutch troops near Batavia last week. The statement said that in this battle, fifty Dutch soldiers were killed or missing.

### 巴达维亚附近激战  印尼游击队歼荷军五十名

1949-05-04
第3版()
专栏：

    巴达维亚附近激战
    印尼游击队歼荷军五十名
    【新华社北平一日电】巴达维亚讯：荷军总部二十八日公报宣布，印尼游击队上周曾在巴达维亚附近与荷军展开激战。公报说，在这次战事中，荷军阵亡与失踪五十名。
