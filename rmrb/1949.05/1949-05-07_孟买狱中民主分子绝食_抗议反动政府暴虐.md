Title: Democratic Figures in Bombay Jail Go on Hunger Strike to Protest the Reactionary Government's Brutality
Author:
Date: 1949-05-07
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 5th: New Delhi dispatch: The assistant secretary of the Bombay Provincial Trade Union Congress announced that more than 250 imprisoned democratic figures in Bombay prisons began a hunger strike on the 3rd to protest the inhuman treatment they are receiving from the reactionary Indian government. The hunger strikers include the vice chairman of the All-India Trade Union Congress and the chairman of the Bombay Provincial Trade Union. More than 300 democratic figures in West Bengal prisons also began a ten-day hunger strike on April 21.

### 孟买狱中民主分子绝食  抗议反动政府暴虐

1949-05-07
第3版()
专栏：

    孟买狱中民主分子绝食
    抗议反动政府暴虐
    【新华社北平五日电】新德里讯：孟买省职工大会助理书记宣称：孟买监狱中二百五十余名被囚的民主分子三日开始绝食，抗议印度反动政府对于他们的非人待遇。绝食者中包括全印职工大会的副主席和孟买省职工会主席。西孟加拉狱中的三百余名民主分子，也曾于四月二十一日开始十天的绝食斗争。
