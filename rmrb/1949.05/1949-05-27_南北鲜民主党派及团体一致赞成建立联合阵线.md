Title: North and South Korean Democratic Parties and Organizations Unanimously Support the Establishment of a United Front
Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 3
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 25th: All democratic parties and organizations in South and North Korea have expressed support for the proposal to establish an All-Korean Democratic National United Front and have indicated their willingness to participate in it. The North Korean Federation of Trade Unions, with 500,000 members, and the Farmers' Federation, with 600,000 members, have both declared their enthusiastic welcome to this proposal. In addition, the North Korean Democratic Women's League, the Democratic Youth League, and the New Progressive Party have all issued similar statements endorsing the establishment of this united front.

### 南北鲜民主党派及团体一致赞成建立联合阵线

1949-05-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　南北鲜民主党派及团体一致赞成建立联合阵线
    【新华社北平二十五日电】塔斯社平壤讯：南朝鲜和北朝鲜的一切民主党派和团体都赞成成立全朝鲜民主民族联合阵线的建议，并表示愿意参加这一阵线。拥有五十万会员的北朝鲜职工会联合会和拥有六十万会员的农民联合会都声明热烈欢迎这一建议。此外，北朝鲜民主妇联、民主青联及新进步党，均发表了同样的声明，赞助建立这个联合阵线。
