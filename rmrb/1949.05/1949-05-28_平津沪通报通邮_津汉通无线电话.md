Title: Beijing, Tianjin, and Shanghai Resume Telegraph and Postal Services; Tianjin and Hankou Open Wireless Telephone Service
Author:
Date: 1949-05-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 27th: Telegraph and postal services between Beijing, Tianjin, and Shanghai are being restored. Shortly after the People's Liberation Army entered the urban areas, the telecommunications bureaus of Beijing and Tianjin successively resumed contact with the Shanghai Telecommunications Bureau. Mail to Shanghai is transported by the Tianjin-Pukou Railway. Furthermore, wireless telephone service between Tianjin and Hankou was also opened on the 27th.

### 平津沪通报通邮　津汉通无线电话

1949-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　平津沪通报通邮
  　津汉通无线电话
    【新华社上海二十七日电】北平、天津与上海之间正恢复通报通邮。在人民解放军攻入市区后不久，平、津两地电讯局即先后与上海电讯局恢复联络。发往上海邮件，系由津浦路车运送。又天津与汉口间的无线电话，亦已于二十七日开放。
