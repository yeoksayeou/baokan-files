Title: Soviet Marshal Vasilevsky Issues Order Celebrating Fourth Anniversary of Victory Over German寇

Author:
Date: 1949-05-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th: TASS Moscow reports: Soviet Marshal Vasilevsky, Minister of the Armed Forces of the USSR, has issued the following order in celebration of the anniversary of the victory over Fascist Germany (May 9):

    Comrades, soldiers, sergeants, junior officers, senior officers, and generals of the Army and Navy!

    Today is the fourth anniversary of our historic victory over Fascist Germany. During the Great Patriotic War, the Soviet people and their Armed Forces, under the leadership of the Bolshevik Party, dealt a fatal blow to German imperialism, defended the freedom and independence of their country, and rescued the people of all European nations from the horrors of Fascist enslavement. On the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the victory over Fascist Germany, I salute you and congratulate you.

    To commemorate Victory Day, I order: Today, May 9, in Moscow - the capital of our motherland, in the capitals of the constituent republics, and in the hero cities of Kaliningrad, Lvov, Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa, a thirty-gun salute will be fired in each city.

    Long live the heroic Soviet people and their glorious armed forces!

    Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union - the inspirer and organizer of our victory!

    Long live our great leader, Comrade Stalin!

    Eternal glory to the heroes who gloriously sacrificed themselves for our freedom and independence!

      Soviet Marshal Vasilevsky, Minister of the Armed Forces of the USSR (Signed)

### 苏联元帅华西列夫斯基发布命令  庆祝战胜德寇四周年

1949-05-10
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联元帅华西列夫斯基发布命令
    庆祝战胜德寇四周年
    【新华社北平九日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联武装部队部部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基为庆祝对法西斯德国胜利日（五月九日），顷发布命令如下：
    陆海军战士、士官、尉官、校官和将领同志们！
    今天是我们有历史意义地战胜法西斯德国的四周年纪念日。在伟大的爱国战争期间，苏联人民与他们的武装部队，在布尔什维克党的领导下，曾予德国帝国主义者以致命的打击，保卫了他们国家的自由与独立，并从法西斯奴役的恐怖下拯救了欧洲各民族人民。值此战胜法西斯德国四周年纪念之际，我向你们致敬并祝贺你们。
    为了纪念胜利日，我的命令：今天，五月九日，在莫斯科——我们祖国的首都、在各加盟共和国的首都以及在加里宁格勒、罗夫、列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、悉瓦斯托波尔、敖德萨等英雄城市，各鸣礼炮三十响。
    英雄的苏维埃人民及其光荣的武装部队万岁！
    苏联共产党——我们胜利的鼓舞者和组织者万岁！
    我们伟大的领袖斯大林同志万岁！
    为我们自由与独立而光荣牺牲的英雄们永垂不朽！
      苏联武装部队部部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基（签字）
