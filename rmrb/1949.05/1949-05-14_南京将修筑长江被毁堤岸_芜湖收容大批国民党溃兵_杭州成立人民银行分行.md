Title: Nanjing to Repair Damaged Yangtze River Embankments; Wuhu Shelters Large Numbers of Kuomintang Remnants; Hangzhou Establishes People's Bank Branch

Author:
Date: 1949-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping Broadcast: Nanjing, 13th—The Nanjing Military Control Commission and the Water Conservancy Commission, based on the damage to the Yangtze River embankments, have stipulated specific methods for repairing the embankments. On the morning of the 12th, the second batch of embankment repair workers departed. They will join the first batch of embankment repair workers to jointly organize a supervision office to uniformly lead the embankment repair work and maintain close contact with the Jiangpu and Liuhe County People's Governments. The embankment repair work will begin soon.

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping Broadcast: Wuhu, 13th—The Wuhu Military Control Commission's stray soldier shelter, from May 1st to May 5th, a total of 2,500 Kuomintang officers and soldiers were sheltered. The Kuomintang officers and soldiers who were sheltered came from many units, including Lieutenant Colonel Yao Xuerong, deputy head of the Marine Corps artillery regiment, and more than 100 other officers. More than 1,000 have now been given travel expenses and sent home.

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping Broadcast: Hangzhou, 13th—The Hangzhou Branch of the People's Bank of China officially opened for business on the 10th.

### 南京将修筑长江被毁堤岸  芜湖收容大批国民党溃兵  杭州成立人民银行分行

1949-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    南京将修筑长江被毁堤岸
    芜湖收容大批国民党溃兵
    杭州成立人民银行分行
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京十三日消息：南京军事管制委员会和水利委员会，根据长江堤岸破坏的情况，规定了修堤具体办法，十二日早晨，第二批修堤工作人员已经出发，他们将和第一批修堤工作人员共同组织监工所，统一领导修堤工作，并且和江浦，六合两县人民政府，密切联系，修堤工作很快就要开始。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】芜湖十三日消息：芜湖军管会散兵收容所，从五月一日到五日五天当中。一共收容了溃散的国民党军官兵二千五百多名，被收容的国民党军官兵番号很多，其中并且有海军陆战队炮兵团中校副团长姚学荣等军官一百多名，现在有一千多名已经发给了路费，遣送回家。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】杭州十三日消息：中国人民银行杭州分行已经在十日开始营业。
