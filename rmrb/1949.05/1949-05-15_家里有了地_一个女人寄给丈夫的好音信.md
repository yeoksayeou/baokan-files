Title: The Family Now Has Land
Author: Wang Wu
Date: 1949-05-15
Page: 4

The village held a meeting to distribute land,
　　We got six mu.
　　The elders said to give us good land,
　　Let the military dependents choose first,
　　Then distribute it to others.
　　My dear,
　　I am so happy,
　　From now on,
　　We will no longer live that life of hardship and scarcity.
　　This piece of land,
　　Is top-grade,
　　I heard it can yield several more dou of grain.
　　--The Farmers' Association said:
　　Let our family cultivate it first,
　　Now
　　Manure delivery and substitute farming are all ready,
　　Making me happy
　　Holding our child,
　　I go to our field every day to see the seedlings—
　　Seeing if they have come out yet,
　　If there are weeds growing,
　　I will pull them out.
　　The Farmers' Association takes care of us so well:
　　Sending rice and firewood,
　　Carrying water and helping with farming.
　　My dear,
　　Don't worry,
　　Although I have two children tugging at me,
　　Now
　　The empty spaces in the front and back yards,
　　I have all planted with corn and cabbage;
　　--The small white cabbage has already come out,
　　The three of us, mother and children,
　　Eat it so sweetly and fragrantly,
　　--We don't have to worry about food and clothing.
　　We also have land now,
　　I am determined
　　To engage in production,
　　You are at the front,
　　Be a vanguard!
　　If the Communist Party hadn't come,
　　For thousands and thousands of years,
　　We wouldn't even get a clod of earth!
            From Qinhuangdao

### 家里有了地  一个女人寄给丈夫的好音信
王芜
1949-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    家里有了地
    一个女人寄给丈夫的好音信
    王芜
    庄里开会分地，
    咱得了六亩。
    父老说给咱们好地，
    先让军属自己挑，
    挑完了再给旁人分。
    孩子他爸，
    我高兴，
    从今以后，
    我们不再过那掉顿的苦日子。
    这块地，
    属头等，
    听说能多打下粮食好几斗。
    ——农会说：
    先给咱们家耕，
    如今
    送粪代耕早齐全，
    乐的我
    抱着咱孩子，
    每天到咱的地里去看苗——
    看它出来没出来，
    要是有草长出，
    我就拔。
    农会照顾真周到：
    送米送柴，
    挑水与代耕。
    孩子他爸，
    你放心，
    虽有两个孩子坠我脚，
    现今
    前后院里的空地，
    我全种上苞米和白菜；
    ——已经下来小白菜，
    我娘三个
    吃的甜又香，
    ——吃穿都不愁。
    我们也有地啦，
    我决心
    来生产，
    你在前线，
    要当先！
    要不是共产党来了，
    我们千年万年，
    也捞不着一块土拉疙！
            于秦皇岛
