Title: Korean People's Assembly Closes; Approves This Year's National Budget; Sends Respectful Telegram to Marshal Stalin
Author:
Date: 1949-05-03
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th: TASS, Pyongyang: The Third Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, which opened on April 19th, has closed after completing its agenda. The Assembly approved the Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement concluded between the Korean government delegation and the Soviet Union, approved the national budget for the northern part of the Republic for 1949, and heard reports on the results of elections in the provinces, cities, and counties of North Korea. Finally, amidst thunderous applause, the Assembly passed a telegram of respect to Marshal Stalin.

### 朝鲜人民议会闭幕  批准本年度国家预算  电斯大林大元帅致敬

1949-05-03
第3版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民议会闭幕
    批准本年度国家预算
    电斯大林大元帅致敬
    【新华社北平三十日电】据塔斯社平壤讯：四月十九日开幕的朝鲜最高人民议会第三届大会在完成各项议程后，业已闭幕。大会批准了朝鲜政府代表团与苏联缔结的经济文化合作协定，批准了共和国北部一九四九年度的国家预算，听取了北朝鲜省、市、县选举结果的报告，大会最后在雷动的掌声中通过了向斯大林大元帅的致敬电。
