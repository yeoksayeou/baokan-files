Title: Tianjin-Pukou, Beining, and Longhai Railways: All Projects Soon to be Completed
Author:
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 1

    Tianjin-Pukou, Beining, and Longhai Railways:
    All Projects Soon to be Completed
    [This Newspaper's Report] The Railway Corps' emergency repair work on the Tianjin-Pukou, Beining, and Longhai railway lines and bridges is nearing completion. Preparations for the emergency repair work on the southern section of the Beijing-Hankou Railway are also actively underway and will commence soon. In order to promptly inspect the progress of the emergency repair and escort work, the Railway Corps Headquarters has organized two escort work inspection teams to inspect work on the Tianjin-Pukou and Longhai lines, respectively. The work teams were formed a few days ago and will depart on the 13th of this month.

### 津浦北宁陇海铁路  全部工程即将完成

1949-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    津浦北宁陇海铁路
    全部工程即将完成
    【本报讯】铁道纵队抢修津浦、北宁、陇海铁路工程线路及桥梁即将全部完成。平汉南段的抢修工作亦正积极准备不久开工。铁道纵队司令部为及时检查抢修工作及护运工作情况，特组织两个护运工作检查组，分赴津浦、陇海两线检查工作。工作组已于日前组成，本月十三日即将出发。
