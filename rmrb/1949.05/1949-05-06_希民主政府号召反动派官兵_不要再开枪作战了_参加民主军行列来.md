Title: Greek Democratic Government Calls on Reactionary Officers and Soldiers to Stop Shooting and Join the Democratic Army

Author: 
Date: 1949-05-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th: According to the Free Greece News Agency, the Greek Provisional Democratic Government issued a call to the royalist-fascist officers and soldiers on April 27th, urging them to defect to the Democratic Army and fight for genuine peace. The call stated: "On April 20th, the Greek Provisional Democratic Government requested the United Nations General Assembly and the Paris World Peace Congress to assist the Greek people in achieving peace. The royalist fascists rejected our call for peace, asserting that it was a so-called demonstration of our weakness. Can Greece establish peace? Our answer is yes, if we all fight for it, if we can all fulfill our responsibilities. Think about our proposal, stop fighting, don't fire another shot, isolate the running dogs who monitor you, choose the opportunity to form brotherhood with us, establish contact with the Democratic Army forces, organize democratic groups in your army, and join the ranks of the Democratic Army!"

### 希民主政府号召反动派官兵  不要再开枪作战了  参加民主军行列来

1949-05-06
第3版()
专栏：

    希民主政府号召反动派官兵
    不要再开枪作战了
    参加民主军行列来
    【新华社北平四日电】据自由希腊通讯社报道：希腊临时民主政府于四月二十七日向保皇法西斯官兵发出号召，劝告他们投到民主军方面来，为真正的和平而战斗。号召说：“在四月二十日，希腊临时民主政府，要求联合国大会和巴黎世界拥护和平大会援助希腊人民取得和平。保皇法西斯拒绝了我们的和平呼吁，并且断言这是所谓我们软弱的证明。希腊能建立和平吗？我们的回答是肯定的，如果我们都为它而战斗，如果我们都能尽我们的责任的话。想想我们的提议吧，不要再作战了，不要再开一枪，孤立那些监视你们的走狗，选择同我们结成兄弟的时机，与民主军的部队建立联系，在你们的军队中组织民主小组，参加到民主军的行列里来！”
