Title: Thousands of Businesses in Taiyuan Begin Operations
Author:
Date: 1949-05-06
Page: 1

    Taiyuan Thousands of Businesses Begin Operations
    [Taiyuan Electric Dispatch] Since the liberation of Taiyuan, nearly a thousand businesses have begun operations up to April 30th. Before liberation, there were a total of more than 1,600 businesses.

### 太原千家商号开始营业

1949-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    太原千家商号开始营业
    【太原电】太原自解放以来，直至四月三十日止，已有近千家商号开始营业。按解放前营业的共有一千六百余家。
