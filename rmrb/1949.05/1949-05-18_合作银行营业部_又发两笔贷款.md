Title: Cooperative Bank Business Department Issues Two More Loans
Author:
Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 2

　　Cooperative Bank Business Department
  　Issues Two More Loans
    [Beiping News] The Cooperative Bank Business Department has recently issued two more loans: First, 15,000 jin of corn to the Tsinghua Agricultural College for raising fish beside Longevity Hill. Second, 2 million yuan to the Haidian and Chengfu Cooperative Farms for increasing equipment and purchasing seeds and fertilizer.
    　　(Yu)

### 合作银行营业部　又发两笔贷款

1949-05-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　合作银行营业部
  　又发两笔贷款
    【北平讯】合作银行营业部现又发放两笔贷款：一、清华农学院玉米一万五千斤，用于在万寿山旁养鱼。二、海甸、成府合作农场二百万元，用于增加设备，购买种子肥料等。
    　　（宇）
