Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to Czechoslovak Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-05-30
Page: 3

　　CPC Central Committee
　　Sends Congratulatory Message to Czechoslovak Communist Party Congress
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 29th: CTK, Prague dispatch: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union recently sent a congratulatory message to the Ninth Congress of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, stating: The Communist Party of Czechoslovakia is recognized as the leading force in building a people's democratic state. After defeating the German fascist aggressors, the Czechoslovak people, under the leadership of the CPC, have created historical changes in the life of their country, are now developing their economy and culture along a new path, and have laid the political and economic foundations for socialism, victoriously building a people's democratic state. The CPC Central Committee congratulates the various nationalities of Czechoslovakia and their vanguard, the CPC, on achieving new victories in their struggle to cultivate a people's democratic order, for the independence of Czechoslovakia, for the further development of the national economy and national culture, for cooperation and mutual assistance with people's democratic countries and the Soviet Union, for peace and security, and against imperialist conspiracies. The CPC Central Committee firmly believes that the Czechs, armed with the theory of Marxism-Leninism and faithful to the principles of proletarian internationalism, will further strengthen and tighten their ranks and gloriously complete the great task of building a socialist society.

### 联共中央委员会　电贺捷共代表大会

1949-05-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　联共中央委员会
  　电贺捷共代表大会
    【新华社北平二十九日电】电通社布拉格讯：联共中央委员会顷电贺捷克共产党第九届代表大会称：捷克斯洛伐克共产党被公认为建设人民民主国家当中的领导力量。在战胜了德国法西斯侵略者之后，捷克斯洛伐克人民在捷共党领导下已在他们国家的生活中造成了历史性的改变，现在正沿着新的道路发展经济与文化，并奠定了社会主义的政治与经济基础，胜利地建设人民民主国家。联共中央委员会祝贺捷克斯洛伐克各民族及其先锋队捷共在为培植人民民主秩序、为捷克斯洛伐克独立、为进一步发展民族经济及民族文化、为与人民民主国家及苏联合作及互助、为和平与安全及反对帝国主义阴谋而进行的斗争中，获得新的胜利。联共中央委员会坚信，以马列主义的理论武装着的、忠实于无产阶级国际主义原则的捷克，必将进一步加强与紧密它的队伍，光荣地完成建立社会主义社会的伟大任务。
