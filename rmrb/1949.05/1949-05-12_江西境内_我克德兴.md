Title: We Capture Dexing in Jiangxi Province
Author:
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 1

    Jiangxi Province
    We Capture Dexing
    [Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang-Jiangxi Front, 11th] Delayed news: A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army liberated Dexing County, southeast of Jingdezhen, on the 3rd.
