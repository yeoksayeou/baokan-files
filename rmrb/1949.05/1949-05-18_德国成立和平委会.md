Title: Germany Establishes Peace Committee
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 14th: TASS reports: The German Preparatory Committee for the World Peace Congress and the German representatives attending the Paris and Prague World Peace Congresses held a joint meeting in Berlin. At the meeting, the German Committee for the Defense of Peace was established, with Johannes R. Becher, Arnold Zweig, and Anna Seghers as its chairmen. This committee will maintain regular contact with the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress in Paris.

### 德国成立和平委会

1949-05-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　德国成立和平委会
    【新华社北平十四日电】塔斯社柏林讯：德国拥护世界和平大会筹备委员会与德国出席巴黎与布拉格世界和平大会的代表在柏林举行联席会议。在会议上成立了德国拥护和平委员会席以约翰尼斯·海契尔、阿诺德·兹威格和安娜·西格斯三人为主，该委员会将与巴黎的世界和平大会常设委员会保持经常联系。
