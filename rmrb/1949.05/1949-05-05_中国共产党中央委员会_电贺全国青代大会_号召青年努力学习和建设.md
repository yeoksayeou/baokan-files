Title: Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends Telegram to the National Youth Congress, Calling on Youth to Study and Build Diligently
Author:
Date: 1949-05-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram of congratulations to the First National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation. The telegram reads as follows: To the representatives of the First National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation!

    We congratulate you on holding this representative conference in Beijing, the birthplace of the "May Fourth" Movement, on the occasion of the thirtieth anniversary of the "May Fourth" Movement. We hope that your conference will be a good summary of the Chinese youth movement over the past thirty years, and at the same time a good beginning for the Chinese youth movement in the new era. In the past thirty years, Chinese revolutionary youth, together with Chinese workers and peasants, have fought heroically, one after another, to overthrow the imperialist aggressors and the feudal comprador rule of China. This revolutionary task has now basically achieved victory. A new period will begin in the future. In addition to continuing to wage resolute struggles against imperialist aggressors and the remnants of Chinese counter-revolutionaries, patriotic youth across the country must focus their main efforts on the tasks of learning and construction, in order to rapidly restore and develop industrial and agricultural production and cultural and educational undertakings throughout China, so that the Chinese people, who have won military and political victories, can also achieve equally great victories in the economic and cultural fields. We believe that the brave Chinese youth will, as in the past, stand at the forefront of the people in fulfilling new tasks. We wish you success!
      Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, May 4, 1949

### 中国共产党中央委员会  电贺全国青代大会  号召青年努力学习和建设

1949-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会
    电贺全国青代大会
    号召青年努力学习和建设
    【新华社北平四日电】中国共产党中央委员会电贺中华全国青年第一次代表大会，电文如下：中华全国青年第一次代表大会的代表同志们！
    庆祝你们在“五四”运动三十周年之际，在“五四”运动发源地北平召开这次代表大会。希望你们的大会是过去三十年中国青年运动的一个良好的总结，同时是新时期中国青年运动的一个良好的开端。在过去三十年内，中国革命青年和中国工人农民在一起，前仆后继，为打倒帝国主义侵略者及中国的封建买办统治而英勇奋斗，这个革命任务现时已在基本上取得了胜利。今后将开始一个新的时期。全国的爱国青年除了必须继续与帝国主义侵略者及中国反革命残余作坚决斗争以外，必须把主要的努力放在学习和建设的任务方面，以便迅速在全中国恢复和发展工农业生产和文化教育事业，使军事上和政治上胜利了的中国人民，在经济上和文化上也得到同样伟大的胜利。我们相信，勇敢的中国青年在实现新的任务的时候，将和过去一样，站在人民的前列。祝你们成功！
      中国共产党中央委员会  一九四九年五月四日
