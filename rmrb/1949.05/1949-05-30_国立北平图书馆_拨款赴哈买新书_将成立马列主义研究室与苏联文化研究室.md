Title: National Peking Library Allocates Funds to Purchase New Books in Harbin; To Establish Marxism-Leninism Research Office and Soviet Culture Research Office
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-05-30
Page: 2

    National Peking Library
    Allocates Funds to Purchase New Books in Harbin
    To Establish Marxism-Leninism Research Office and Soviet Culture Research Office
    [Our Dispatch] The National Peking Library, in order to establish a Marxism-Leninism Research Office and a Soviet Culture Research Office, has allocated 100,000 RMB and sent personnel to Harbin to order books on Marxist-Leninist political and economic theory, as well as Soviet cultural books and magazines. At the same time, personnel have also been sent to study Russian cataloging methods and improve library management practices, drawing on the experience of the Lenin Library in the Soviet Union.
          (Bai Sheng)

### 国立北平图书馆  拨款赴哈买新书  将成立马列主义研究室与苏联文化研究室
柏生
1949-05-30
第2版()
专栏：

    国立北平图书馆
    拨款赴哈买新书
    将成立马列主义研究室与苏联文化研究室
    【本报讯】国立北平图书馆为开辟马列主义研究室和苏联文化研究室，特拨人民币十万元，派员赴哈尔滨订购马列主义政治经济理论书籍及苏联文化书籍杂志。同时并派人学习俄文编目法，参照苏联列宁图书馆经验改进图书馆管理业务。
          （柏生）
