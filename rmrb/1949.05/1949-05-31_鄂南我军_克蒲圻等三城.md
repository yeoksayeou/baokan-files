Title: Our Army in Southern Hubei Captures Three Cities Including Puqi
Author:
Date: 1949-05-31
Page: 1
Final News

　　Our Army in Southern Hubei
  　Captures Three Cities Including Puqi
    【Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 30th】The People's Liberation Army advancing towards southern Hubei has approached the border of Hunan Province. On the 24th and 25th, it successively captured three county towns on the Canton-Hankou Railway: Puqi, Chongyang to its southeast, and Tongcheng bordering Hunan. In the battle to capture Tongcheng, the Liberation Army captured over 800 enemy soldiers.

### 鄂南我军　克蒲圻等三城

1949-05-31
第1版()
专栏：最后消息

　　鄂南我军
  　克蒲圻等三城
    【新华社汉口三十日电】向鄂南方面发展的人民解放军迫近湖南省的边境，廿四、廿五两日连续攻占粤汉路的蒲圻及其东南的崇阳和与湖南交界的通城等三座县城。在攻占通城的战斗中，解放军俘敌八百余。
