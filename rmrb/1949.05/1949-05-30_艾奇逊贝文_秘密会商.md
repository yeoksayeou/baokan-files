Title: Acheson and Bevin Secret Meeting
Author:
Date: 1949-05-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 28th: Paris dispatch: U.S. Secretary of State Acheson and British Foreign Secretary Bevin held a secret meeting at the U.S. Embassy here on the 26th. The content has not been announced. According to the Associated Press: Acheson and Bevin may have discussed (1) issues in the Four Foreign Ministers Conference; (2) the Greek issue and the recent Soviet proposal; (3) the China policy issue; and (4) the Soviet proposal to prepare a peace treaty with Japan.

### 艾奇逊贝文　秘密会商

1949-05-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　艾奇逊贝文
  　秘密会商
    【新华社北平二十八日电】巴黎讯：美国务卿艾奇逊与英外相贝文于二十六日在此间美大使馆举行秘密会商，内容未经宣布。据合众社悉：艾奇逊与贝文可能谈及（一）四国外长会议中诸问题；（二）希腊问题及苏联最近的建议；（三）对华政策问题；（四）苏联所提准备对日和约问题。
