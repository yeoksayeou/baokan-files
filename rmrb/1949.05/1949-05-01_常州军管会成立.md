Title: Establishment of the Changzhou Military Control Commission
Author:
Date: 1949-05-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Yangtze River Front, 30th: The Changzhou Municipal Military Control Commission was established on the 28th, and a large number of its staff members have entered the city to work. Before the city's liberation, workers of the Dacheng Textile Factory, students of the Provincial Changzhou Middle School, and employees of the Zhongshan Daily News organized themselves to protect the factories and schools. Employees of enterprises such as the Electric Light Company, the Water Company, and the Dacheng Textile Factory did not stop working, and the city's sixteen middle schools and ninety-five primary schools did not suspend classes. Students everywhere posted news of victories and wall newspapers, disseminating broadcasts from Radio Beiping and news of the People's Liberation Army's victories on the Jiangnan front. Students at Changzhou Middle School are rehearsing a drama, preparing to perform it on "May Fourth" to welcome the People's Liberation Army.

### 常州军管会成立

1949-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    常州军管会成立
    【新华社长江前线三十日电】常州市军事管制委员会已于二十八日成立，该会大批工作人员入市工作。该市解放前，大成纺织厂工人，省立常州中学学生，中山日报社职工等均组织起来护厂护校。电灯公司、自来水公司、大成纺织厂等企业职工，没有停止工作，全市十六个中学和九十五个小学也没有停课。学生们到处张贴捷报和壁报，传播北平电台的广播和解放军在江南前线的胜利消息。常州中学学生正在排演话剧，准备于“五四”出演，欢迎人民解放军。
