Title: New Sons and Daughters Heroic Epic to be Serialized in This Newspaper
Author:
Date: 1949-05-23
Page: 1

Long Novel
　　New Sons and Daughters Heroic Epic
  　Will be Serialized in This Newspaper
    The new work "New Sons and Daughters Heroic Epic" by Kong Jue and Yuan Jing is a long novel written in popular form about the struggle of peasants in central Hebei against the enemy, with woodcut illustrations by Yan Han. Starting May 25th, it will be serialized on page four of this newspaper. Readers please pay attention.

### 新儿女英雄传　将在本报连续刊载

1949-05-23
第1版()
专栏：长篇小说

　　新儿女英雄传
  　将在本报连续刊载
    孔厥、袁静新作“新儿女英雄传”，是一部用通俗形式写冀中农民对敌斗争的长篇小说，有彦涵木刻插图，五月二十五日起，在本报四版连载，希读者注意。
