Title: Shenyang Commemorates May Day with Victory Celebration; Telegrams Respectfully Sent to Chairman Mao and Stalin
Author:
Date: 1949-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 2nd: The Shenyang City commemoration of "May Day" and celebration of the liberation of Nanjing sent telegrams of respect to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Marshal Stalin, and the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. The telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu stated: The working class of Shenyang is determined to restore and develop production with the greatest effort, unite with people from all walks of life to fully support the People's Liberation Army, eliminate all Kuomintang reactionaries remnants who dare to resist within the country thoroughly and cleanly as soon as possible, and build a politically and economically completely independent, industrialized, peaceful, democratic, and happy New China. In the telegram to Marshal Stalin, in addition to expressing gratitude to the heroic Soviet Red Army, the world's peace defender and the liberator of the Northeast people, it stated: The Chinese working class is determined to continue to consolidate the friendly cooperation between the Chinese and Soviet people and strive to defend world peace and democracy. In the telegram to the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, the conference congratulated the success of the Peace Congress, guaranteed to support the propositions and resolutions of the conference, and expressed indignation at the reactionary behavior of France in restricting the entry of representatives from various countries.

### 沈阳纪念五一祝捷大会  电毛主席斯大林致敬

1949-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    沈阳纪念五一祝捷大会
    电毛主席斯大林致敬
    【新华社沈阳二日电】沈阳市纪念“五一”、庆祝南京解放大会分电毛主席、朱总司令、斯大林大元帅及世界和平大会常设委员会致敬。给毛主席和朱总司令电称：沈阳市的工人阶级誓以最大努力恢复与发展生产，团结各阶层人民全力支援人民解放军，早日彻底干净消灭全国境内一切敢于抵抗的国民党反动派残余力量，建设一个在政治上、经济上完全独立的工业化的和平民主幸福的新中国。在致斯大林大元帅的电文中，除向世界和平保卫者、东北人民的解放者的苏联英勇红军表示感谢外，并称：中国工人阶级决心为继续巩固中苏人民的友好合作，保卫世界持久的和平民主而努力。在致世界和平大会常设委员会的电文中，大会祝贺和平大会的成功，保证支持大会的主张和决议，并对法国限制各国代表入境的反动行为表示愤慨。
