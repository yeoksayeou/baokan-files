Title: Beiping Restores Telegraph Communications with Six Cities in Central China
Author:
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 2

    Beiping Restores Telegraph Communications with Six Cities in Central China
    [Beiping City News] Following the resumption of communications between the city and 340 important towns in the Northeast District on the 8th of this month, telegraph communications with Zhengzhou, Luoyang, Kaifeng, Shangqiu, Xuchang, and Luohe in the Central China District were also restored on the 10th, and the sending and receiving of commercial telegrams has begun. (Military Control Commission of Beiping Telecommunications Bureau)

### 北平与华中六市恢复电报通讯

1949-05-12
第2版()
专栏：

    北平与华中六市恢复电报通讯
    【平市讯】本月八日本市与东北区的三百四十个重要城镇恢复通报后，自十日起，和华中区的郑州、洛阳、开封、商邱、许昌、漯河六个城市的电报通讯又告恢复，并开始收拍商报。    （军管北平电信局）
