Title: Qian'an Special Crops Complete Planting Plan
Author:
Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 2

　　【Tangshan Telegram】Qian'an County has completed the planting of field crops and special crops. The planting area of cotton, peanuts, and sweet potatoes has increased compared to previous years. This year, the county planted more than 46,000 mu of cotton, more than 34,000 mu more than last year; planted 150,000 mu of peanuts, more than 34,000 mu more than last year; and eliminated 4,980 mu of wasteland during spring plowing, allowing land ravaged by the Kuomintang bandits for many years to resume production. During the crash planting of cotton, the county dispatched 15 key cadres to lead the districts and villages, promoting the branches to mobilize the masses to plant cotton, with 18,000 mu of cotton planted by carrying water throughout the county. It also mobilized 37 percent of the county's female labor force to participate in production.

### 迁安特种作物完成播种计划

1949-05-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　迁安特种作物完成播种计划
    【唐山电】迁安全县完成大田和特种作物的播种工作。棉花、花生、白薯的种植面积均较往年增多。全县今年种棉花四万六千余亩，较去年多种三万四千余亩；种花生十五万亩，较去年多种三万四千余亩；春耕中消减荒地四千九百八十亩，使连年来在国民党匪帮摧残下荒芜的土地，得以恢复生产。在突击种棉时，县抽出主要干部十五名，分头到区村领导，推动支部发动群众种棉，全县担水种棉一万八千亩。并发动了全县百分之三十七的妇女劳动力参加生产。
