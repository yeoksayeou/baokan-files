Title: Polish Trade Union to Hold Delegate Congress on June 1st
Author:
Date: 1949-05-15
Page: 3
    Polish Trade Union
    To Hold Delegate Congress on June 1st
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 14th] Warsaw dispatch: The Central Committee of the Polish Trade Union decided on the 9th to convene a trade union delegate congress on June 1st. The plenary session also reviewed the "May Day" demonstration situation. This year, 9.5 million people in Poland participated in the "May Day" celebrations, an increase of 50% compared to last year, among which the number of farmers, women, and young people has greatly increased.

### 波兰职工会  定六一开代表大会

1949-05-15
第3版()
专栏：

    波兰职工会
    定六一开代表大会
    【新华社北平十四日电】华沙讯：波兰职工会中央委员会全会九日决定于六月一日召开职工会代表大会。全会并检讨了“五一”节示威情形。本年全波兰参加“五一”节庆祝者达九百五十万人，较去年增加百分之五十，其中农民、妇女、青年的人数，大为增加。
