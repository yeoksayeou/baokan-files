Title: Peking University Museum Holds Handicraft Exhibition
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-01
Page: 2

    Peking Municipality News: To welcome May Day and commemorate the May Fourth Return to School Day, the Peking University Museum is holding a Chinese Handicraft Exhibition. Items include porcelain, bronzes, weaving, lacquerware, and more. At the same time, the museum's library, illustrated book room, and school history room will also be open, all free of charge. All circles are welcome to visit. The museum has recently been moved by the school's general office to No. 2 Dongchang Hutong.

### 北大博物馆举办手工艺展览

1949-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    北大博物馆举办手工艺展览
    【平市讯】北大博物馆为欢迎五一节，和纪念五四返校节，特举办中国手工艺展览。计有瓷器、铜器、织造、漆器等项。同时该馆的图书馆、图录室和校史室，也一并开放，概不收费。欢迎各界参观。该馆近已由该校总办事处移至东厂胡同二号。
