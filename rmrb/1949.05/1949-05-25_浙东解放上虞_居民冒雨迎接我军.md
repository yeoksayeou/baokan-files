Title: Eastern Zhejiang Liberated Shangyu: Residents Braved the Rain to Welcome Our Army
Author:
Date: 1949-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 24th. The People's Liberation Army, advancing eastward across the Cao'e River, captured Shangyu County town on the south side of the Shanghai-Hangzhou-Ningbo Railway on the morning of the 22nd, braving the rain. The defending enemy fled to Yuyao to the east. The people of Shangyu warmly welcomed the Liberation Army. From inside the city all the way to the highway five li to the northwest, men, women, and children held umbrellas, cheering along the road. The streets and alleys were plastered with welcoming slogans.

### 浙东解放上虞　居民冒雨迎接我军

1949-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　浙东解放上虞
  　居民冒雨迎接我军
    【新华社杭州二十四日电】越曹娥江东进的人民解放军，于二十二日晨冒雨进占沪杭甬铁路南侧的上虞县城，守敌向其以东的余姚逃窜。上虞人民热烈欢迎解放军。从城内一直到西北五里的公路上，男女老幼撑着雨伞，夹道欢呼，街头巷尾都贴满了欢迎标语。
