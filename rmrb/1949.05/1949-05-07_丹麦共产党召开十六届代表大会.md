Title: Danish Communist Party Holds 16th National Congress
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-07
Page: 3

    Danish Communist Party Holds 16th National Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 6th] TASS reports: The Danish Communist Party began its 16th National Congress in Copenhagen on May 5th (the fourth anniversary of Denmark's liberation from Hitler's occupation). The agenda of the congress includes a report on the political situation since the 15th Congress in January 1946, a report on the struggle for peace and independence, and a report on the party's program. Representatives of fraternal communist parties have gone to Copenhagen to attend the congress.

### 丹麦共产党召开十六届代表大会

1949-05-07
第3版()
专栏：

    丹麦共产党召开十六届代表大会
    【新华社北平六日电】电通社讯：丹麦共产党五月五日（丹麦自希特勒占领下解放四周年纪念日）起在哥本哈根举行第十六届全国代表大会。大会议程包括自一九四六年一月第十五届代表大会以来政治形势的报告、为和平与独立而斗争的报告和关于党的纲领的报告。各兄弟共产党的代表已赴哥本哈根参加大会。
