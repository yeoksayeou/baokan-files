Title: Preparatory Committee for the Ping City Youth Federation Established
Author: Bai Qing
Date: 1949-05-01
Page: 2

    Ping City Youth Federation
    Preparatory Committee Established
    [Report from this newspaper] The Preparatory Committee for the Ping City Youth Federation, organized by the Preparatory Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China (Beijing), the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Municipal Student Union, the Women's Federation, and other organizations, was formally established yesterday (April 30th). Xiao Song, Du Ping (female), and five others were elected as representatives to the National Youth Congress. The preparatory committee members include Xu Liqun, representative of the Beijing Youth League, Zhang Hongshun, representative of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and Gu Qicong, representative of the Municipal Student Union, totaling over thirty people.
        (Bai Qing)

### 平市青联筹委会成立
柏青
1949-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    平市青联
    筹委会成立
    【本报讯】由中国新民主主义青年团北平市筹备委员会、市职工总会筹备委员会、市学生联合会，妇联会等发起组织之北平市青年联合会筹委会，已于昨日（四月三十日）正式成立，选出萧松、杜平（女）等七人为出席全国青年代表大会的代表。该会筹备委员为北平市青年团代表许立群、市职工总会代表张鸿舜、市学联代表古奇琮等三十余人。 
         （柏青）
