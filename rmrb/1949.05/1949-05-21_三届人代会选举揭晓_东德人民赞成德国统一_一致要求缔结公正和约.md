Title: Third People's Congress Election Results Announced: East German People Support German Reunification, Unanimously Demand Conclusion of a Just Peace Treaty
Author:
Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th: TASS, Berlin: The results of the election for the Third German People's Congress for Unity, Peace and Democracy were announced on the 18th by Berlin newspapers. The election results are as follows: The Soviet occupation zone of Germany and the Soviet occupation zone of Berlin have a total of 13,533,071 voters; of these, 12,887,234, or 95.2 percent, participated in the vote. The total number of valid votes was 12,024,221. Of these, 7,943,949 votes, or 66.1 percent, were in favor, and 4,080,272 votes, or 33.9 percent, were against. Thus, the results of the election for the Third People's Congress show that, with a high percentage of voters, two-thirds of the votes cast were in favor of German reunification, the conclusion of a just peace treaty, and the withdrawal of occupying forces. The democratic newspapers of Germany praised the elections held on the 15th and 16th as a great victory for the German People's Congress movement, which in a short time has grown from a movement of progressive forces to a movement of the vast majority of the German people.

### 三届人代会选举揭晓  东德人民赞成德国统一　一致要求缔结公正和约

1949-05-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　三届人代会选举揭晓
    东德人民赞成德国统一 　 一致要求缔结公正和约
    【新华社北平二十日电】塔斯社柏林讯：争取德国统一、和平与民主的第三届德国人民代表大会选举的结果，已于十八日由柏林各报揭晓，选举结果如下：德国苏占区和柏林苏占区共有选民一千三百五十三万三千零七十一人；其中一千二百八十八万七千二百三十四人，即百分之九十五·二参加了投票。有效票数。共一千二百零二万四千二百二十一票。其中，七百九十四万三千九百四十九票即百分之六十六·一投赞成票，四百零八万零二百七十二票即百分之三十三·九投反对票。这样，第三届人民代表大会选举的结果表明，在高度的投票百分比中，有三分之二的选票投票赞成德国统一缔结公正的和约和撤退占领军。德国民主报纸赞扬在十五、十六两日举行的选举，认为是德国人民代表大会运动的巨大胜利，这一运动在短短的时间中从人民进步力量的运动成长为绝大多数德国人民的运动。
