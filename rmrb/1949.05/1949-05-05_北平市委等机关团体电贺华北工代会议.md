Title: The CPC Beiping Municipal Committee and Other Organizations Send Congratulatory Telegrams to the North China Workers' Congress

Author:

Date: 1949-05-05
Page: 1

The All-China Student Federation, the CPC Beiping Municipal Committee and other organizations sent congratulatory telegrams to the North China Workers' Representative Conference, which opened in Tianjin. The All-China Student Federation stated in its congratulatory letter: "Let us unite more closely, under the leadership of Chairman Mao, and strive together to build a new democratic China." The CPC Beiping Municipal Committee's congratulatory telegram stated: We warmly celebrate the victorious opening of the conference and believe that the conference will unite all workers and staff, and under the correct leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, actively work hard to restore and develop production, thereby exerting the greatest strength to support the war to liberate Jiangnan and liberate the whole of China. The preparatory committee of the Beiping Municipal Federation of Trade Unions stated in its congratulatory telegram: We are prepared to join hands with our class brothers in various friendly regions, resolutely implement all the calls and resolutions of the conference, and strive to restore, transform and develop production, build a new Beiping, a new North China, and liberate the whole of China.

### 北平市委等机关团体电贺华北工代会议

1949-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    北平市委等机关团体
    电贺华北工代会议
    【本报讯】中华全国学生联合会、中共北平市委会等机关团体，纷纷致电庆贺在津开幕的华北职工代表会议。全国学联的贺函中称：“让我们团结得更紧密，在毛主席领导之下，共同为建设一个新民主主义的新中国而奋斗。”中共北平市委会贺电称：我们热烈地庆祝大会的胜利召开，相信大会必能团结全体职工，在中国共产党的正确领导下，积极努力恢复与发展生产，从而发挥最大的力量，支援解放江南解放全中国的战争。北平市职工总会筹委会贺电中称：我们准备和各友区阶级兄弟携手，坚决执行会议的一切号召和决议，为恢复改造和发展生产，建设新北平、新华北、解放全中国而奋斗。
