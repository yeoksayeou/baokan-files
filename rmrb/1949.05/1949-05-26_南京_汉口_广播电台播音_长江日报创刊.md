Title: Nanjing and Hankou: Radio Broadcasts, Yangtze Daily Inaugurated
Author:
Date: 1949-05-26
Page: 1

　　Nanjing and Hankou
  　Radio Broadcasts
  　Yangtze Daily Inaugurated
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 23rd: The Nanjing People's Broadcasting Station was officially established on the 18th. The station's medium wave frequency is 660 kilocycles at a wavelength of 454 meters, and its shortwave frequency is 5985 kilocycles at a wavelength of 50.01 meters. Broadcasting begins at 12:00 noon every day, with local news reports starting at 18:00.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 25th: After the liberation of Wuhan, the Hankou branch of the Xinhua News Agency began sending out news dispatches on the 22nd. The Yangtze Daily was also inaugurated on the 23rd, publishing one large broadsheet daily. The Hankou Broadcasting Station began broadcasting on the 23rd, with a frequency of 600 kilocycles and a wavelength of 500 meters.

### 南京·汉口　广播电台播音　长江日报创刊

1949-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京·汉口
  　广播电台播音
  　长江日报创刊
    【新华社南京二十三日电】南京人民广播电台于十八日正式成立。该台中波波长四五四公尺，周率六六○千周，短波波长五○·○一公尺，周率五九八五千周。播音时间每天十二时开始，十八时起报告本市新闻。
    【新华社汉口二十五日电】武汉解放以后，新华社汉口分社已于二十二日开始发稿。长江日报亦于二十三日创刊，日出对开一大张。汉口广播电台则于二十三日开始播音，周率六○○千周，波长五○○公尺。
