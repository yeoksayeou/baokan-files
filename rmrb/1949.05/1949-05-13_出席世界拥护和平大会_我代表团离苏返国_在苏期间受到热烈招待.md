Title: Attending the World Congress of Defenders of Peace, Our Delegation Departs Soviet Union for Home After Warm Reception
Author:
Date: 1949-05-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th: According to TASS, Moscow: The Chinese delegation attending the World Congress of Defenders of Peace left Moscow by plane on the 10th to return home. Those who went to the airport to see them off included V.Y. Denisov, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, writers Leonid Leonov and Konstantin Fedin, poet Sergei Mikhalkov, representatives of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions and the Anti-Fascist Committee of Soviet Women, and others. During its stay in the Soviet Union, in addition to a visit to Leningrad, our delegation in Moscow participated in the "May Day" parade and demonstration in Red Square, visited the Lenin Mausoleum and the Lenin Museum, the USSR State Lenin Library, the Tretyakov Art Gallery, the Museum of Oriental Art, the "In Memory of Ilyich" collective farm, the USSR Construction Exhibition, the Lomonosov Moscow State University, and the Timiryazev Agricultural Academy. Our delegation also met with Vavilov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and several figures from the Academy. The Soviet Writers' Union held a reception for our delegation. Several members of the delegation attended performances at various theaters in Moscow and visited the opening ceremony of the summer sports festival at the Dynamo Stadium. The day before leaving Moscow, Professor V.Y. Denisov, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a reception to entertain our delegation.

### 出席世界拥护和平大会  我代表团离苏返国  在苏期间受到热烈招待

1949-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界拥护和平大会
    我代表团离苏返国
    在苏期间受到热烈招待
    【新华社北平十二日电】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：出席世界拥护和平大会的我国代表团，已于十日乘飞机离莫斯科返国。往机场欢送者有苏联对外文化协会中央委员会主席捷尼索夫、作家里昂诺夫和费丁、诗人米哈尔科夫、全苏职工会和妇女反法西斯委员会代表等。我代表团在苏联期间，除一度赴列宁格勒参观外，在莫斯科曾先后参加红场的“五一”节检阅和示威，参观列宁墓和列宁博物馆、苏联国立列宁图书馆、特列捷科夫艺术博物馆、东方博物馆、“纪念伊里奇”集体农场、苏联建设展览会、莫斯科国立罗莫诺索夫大学、季米里雅采夫农业科学院。我代表团并会晤了苏联科学院院长瓦维洛夫和科学院若干人物。苏联作家协会曾举行招待会招待我代表团。代表团若干团员曾到莫斯科各戏院看戏，并参观狄纳莫运动场夏季运动节的开幕典礼。离莫斯科前一日，苏联对外文化协会中央委员会主席捷尼索夫教授曾举行招待会，招待我代表团。
