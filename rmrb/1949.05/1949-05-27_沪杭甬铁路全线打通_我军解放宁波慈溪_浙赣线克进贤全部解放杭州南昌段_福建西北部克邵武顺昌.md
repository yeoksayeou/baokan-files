Title: Shanghai-Hangzhou-Ningbo Railway Fully Open; Our Army Liberates Ningbo and Cixi; Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway Captures Jinxian, Entire Hangzhou-Nanchang Section Liberated; Northwest Fujian Captures Shaowu and Shunchang
Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 26th: The famous commercial port of Ningbo (i.e., Yin County) in eastern Zhejiang and Cixi to its northwest have been liberated. The People's Liberation Army, advancing eastward along the Shanghai-Hangzhou-Ningbo Railway, occupied Cixi County seat at 10:00 AM on the 24th, and then continued to advance bravely, liberating Ningbo at 11:00 PM that night, capturing a part of the enemy's 87th Army. Order within Ningbo is good, and the market is extremely calm. Ningbo is the terminus of the Shanghai-Hangzhou-Ningbo Railway, connected to the East China Sea by the Yong River in the east, and is the center of the province's maritime transport. With the liberation of Ningbo, the Shanghai-Hangzhou-Ningbo Railway is now fully open.

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang-Jiangxi Front, 26th: Belated news: Jinxian County seat on the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway southeast of Nanchang, and Zhangshu Town to its southwest, were liberated on the 15th and 20th, respectively. With this, the entire 1,300-li railway line from Hangzhou to Nanchang on the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway has been liberated.

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian-Jiangxi Front, 26th: On the 20th, the People's Liberation Army liberated the two county towns of Shaowu and Shunchang in northwestern Fujian, capturing more than 60 puppet Shunchang security team members and seizing more than 100 long and short guns. Another unit of the People's Liberation Army, with only one battalion, annihilated the majority of the direct subordinate team of the 57th Division of the 74th Army and part of its 170th Regiment in the area north of Songxi in northern Fujian on the same day, capturing more than 500 enemy soldiers.

### 沪杭甬铁路全线打通　我军解放宁波慈溪  浙赣线克进贤全部解放杭州南昌段　福建西北部克邵武顺昌

1949-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪杭甬铁路全线打通
  　我军解放宁波慈溪
    浙赣线克进贤全部解放杭州南昌段
  　福建西北部克邵武顺昌
    【新华社杭州二十六日电】浙江东部的著名商埠宁波（即鄞县）及其西北的慈溪已告解放。沿沪杭甬铁路东进的人民解放军于二十四日上午十时占领慈溪县城，并即继续奋勇前进，于当晚十一时解放宁波，俘守敌八十七军一部。宁波市内秩序良好，市面极为平静。宁波为沪杭甬铁路终点，东由甬江接东海，为全省海运的中心。宁波解放后，沪杭甬铁路即告全线打通。
    【新华社浙赣前线二十六日电】迟到消息：南昌东南浙赣路上的进贤县城及其西南的樟树镇，于十五日及二十日先后解放。至此，浙赣路杭州至南昌一千三百里的铁路线已全部解放。
    【新华社闽赣前线二十六日电】人民解放军二十日解放福建西北部的邵武、顺昌两座县城，俘伪顺昌保安队六十余名，缴获长短枪百余支。另部解放军于同日仅以一个营的兵力，在闽北松溪以北地区，歼敌七十四军五十七师直属队大部及其一七零团一部，俘敌五百余。
