Title: Successive Victories in Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Anhui, and Jiangxi: Our Army Liberates Hangzhou; Wannian, Jurong, Jixi, and Chun'an are Also Liberated; The Battle to Capture She County Annihilates a Total of Five Enemy Regiments
Author:
Date: 1949-05-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 5th: The People's Liberation Army liberated Hangzhou, the capital of Zhejiang Province, on the 3rd. Hangzhou is located southwest of Shanghai and is the connecting point of the Shanghai-Hangzhou Railway and the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway, as well as the terminus of the Grand Canal. Water and land transportation are both highly developed. The city has a population of approximately 500,000. West of the city is West Lake, known for its beautiful scenery.

    [Broadcast from the Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Nanjing, May 5th dispatch: The People's Liberation Army liberated Hangzhou, the capital of Zhejiang Province, on the 3rd. The defending enemy fled south. The People's Liberation Army entered the city in succession at 3:15 PM, receiving a warm welcome from the citizens. The city was covered with slogans welcoming the People's Liberation Army. Order was stable, and the electric light plant continued to supply electricity as usual that evening. The Huafeng Paper Mill and other factories quickly resumed operation.

    Xinhua News Agency, Eastern Jiangxi Front, May 5th: The People's Liberation Army, advancing southward in eastern Jiangxi, annihilated the majority of the Kuomintang bandit army's 68th Army and a part of the 46th Army south of Leping on the 2nd, and liberated Wannian, southwest of Leping and north of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway, on the 3rd. The People's Liberation Army captured alive Zhang Xunting, commander of the 143rd Division of the enemy's 68th Army, and Wu Bingjian, commander of the 174th Division of the 46th Army, and more than 4,000 others.

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 5th: It has been verified that Yang Gancai, commander of the Kuomintang bandit army's 20th Army, was killed by the People's Liberation Army in Wan＠Town, southeast of Wuhu, on the 25th of last month. Furthermore, Jurong, southeast of Nanjing, was liberated on the 24th of last month, and more than 700 members of the defending security regiment surrendered.

    Xinhua News Agency, Southern Anhui Front, May 5th: The People's Liberation Army liberated Jixi in southern Anhui and Chun'an in western Zhejiang on the 30th of last month and the 2nd of this month, respectively, and surrounded parts of the enemy's 106th Army and 73rd Army in the Weiping mountain area northwest of Gu'an. When Jixi was liberated, more than 2,000 members of the defending security regiment and others surrendered to the People's Liberation Army. The People's Liberation Army seized 120 rubber boats, 1.5 million rounds of ammunition, and more than 200 barrels of gasoline. The People's Liberation Army liberating Chun'an annihilated a battalion of the enemy's 106th Army in Puchi, northwest of the city, and seized more than 150 vehicles.

    Xinhua News Agency, Southern Anhui Front, May 5th: When the People's Liberation Army captured She County and the Beian Town northeast of it on the 29th of last month, it annihilated three regiments of the 282nd Division, one regiment of the 22nd Division, and one security brigade, totaling five regiments, capturing alive more than 6,400 enemy soldiers, including Guo Fengxian, commander of the 282nd Division. The People's Liberation Army suffered only 60 casualties.

### 苏浙皖赣连获胜利  我军解放杭州  万年句容绩溪淳安同告解放  攻克歙县之战歼敌共五团

1949-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    苏浙皖赣连获胜利
    我军解放杭州
    万年句容绩溪淳安同告解放  攻克歙县之战歼敌共五团
    【新华社南京五日电】人民解放军三日解放浙江省会杭州。杭州位于上海西南，为沪杭铁路和浙赣铁路的连接点，也是运河的终点。水陆交通均甚发达。全市人口约五十余万。城西有西湖，以风景秀丽著称。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京五号消息：人民解放军三号解放浙江省会杭州，守敌向南逃窜，解放军在下午三点十五分陆续进入市区，受到市民热烈的欢迎。市内贴满了欢迎解放军的标语，秩序安定，当晚电灯厂照常供电，华丰造纸厂等都迅速开工。
    【新华社赣东前线五日电】江西东部向南挺进的人民解放军二日在乐平以南歼灭南逃的国民党匪军六十八军大部和四十六军一部，并于三日解放乐平西南浙赣路以北的万年。解放军生俘敌六十八军一四三师师长张勋亭，四十六军一七四师师长吴丙监以下四千余人。
    【新华社南京五日电】国民党匪军二十军军长杨干才，经查明已于上月二十五日在芜湖东南的湾＠镇为解放军击毙。又南京东南的句容于上月廿四日解放，守敌保安团七百余人投降。
    【新华社皖南前线五日电】人民解放军在上月三十日和本月二日先后解放安徽南部的绩溪和浙江西部的淳安，并将敌一零六军和七十三军各一部包围于固安西北的威坪山区。解放绩溪时，守敌保安团等两千余人向解放军投降。解放军缴获橡皮船一百二十艘，子弹一百五十万发，汽油二百余桶。解放淳安的解放军在该城西北的普慈歼敌一零六军一个营，缴获汽车一百五十余辆。
    【新华社皖南前线五日电】人民解放军在上月二十九日攻克歙县及其东北的北岸镇时，歼敌二八二师三个团，二十二师一个团及保安旅一个团，共计五个团全部，俘敌二八二师师长郭奉先以下六千四百余人，解放军仅伤亡六十人。
