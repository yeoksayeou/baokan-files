Title: Preparations for May Fourth Celebrations by Youth in Various Parts of Northeast China
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 30th. Youth in various parts of Northeast China are actively preparing to commemorate the May Fourth Youth Day. The Shenyang Municipal Youth League Preparatory Committee, Youth Federation, Student Federation and other organizations have decided to organize youth from factories, schools and government agencies to discuss the historical significance of the "May Fourth" Movement and the current tasks of Chinese youth, and to hold singing contests, wall newspaper competitions and concerts. The Harbin Municipal Youth League Committee, Education Bureau, Student Federation, Federation of Trade Unions and other organizations have jointly formed a Red May Movement Preparatory Committee to prepare for ball games and track and field events, and to hold an open-air evening party on "May Fourth". The Fushun Municipal Youth League is preparing to convene a forum for young workers and students. Jilin City plans to hold a city-wide youth commemoration meeting and bonfire party on "May Fourth". Changchun, Jiamusi, Qiqihar and other places are also making similar preparations.

### 东北各地青年筹备纪念五四

1949-05-03
第3版()
专栏：

    东北各地青年筹备纪念五四
    【新华社沈阳三十日电】东北各地青年积极筹备纪念五四青年节，沈阳市青年团筹委会、青年联合会、学生联合会等团体决定组织各工厂、学校、机关的青年，研讨“五四”的历史意义及目前中国青年的任务，并举行歌咏、壁报比赛及音乐会。哈尔滨市青年团市委会、教育局、学联、职工总会等联合组织红五月运动筹委会，筹备举行球赛与径赛，并于“五四”举行露天晚会。抚顺市青年团准备召开青年工人和学生座谈会，吉林市拟于“五四”举行全市青年纪念大会和营火晚会，长春、佳木斯、齐齐哈尔等地亦正在进行类似的准备工作。
