Title: North China Workers' Representatives Present Flag to Chairman Mao; Commander-in-Chief Zhu Instructs on Construction Tasks
Author:
Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 1

　　North China Workers' Representatives Present Flag to Chairman Mao
  　Commander-in-Chief Zhu Instructs on Construction Tasks
    [Our Newspaper's Report] Yesterday (the 17th) at 2 PM, representatives of workers from North China presented a flag to their great leader Chairman Mao and listened to instructions from Commander-in-Chief Zhu. The inscription on the flag read: "We will forever struggle forward under your leadership for the cause of production and construction of the New China." During the flag presentation ceremony, the representatives enthusiastically cheered their leader, and shouts of slogans and applause shook the venue. After Chairman Mao shook hands with the two veteran worker representatives who presented the flag, Commander-in-Chief Zhu then spoke. He first pointed out that this North China Workers' Representative Conference was a success. He explained that the working class should shoulder the great historical task of building the New China. Therefore, they must thoroughly grasp the policies pointed out by Chairman Mao in all aspects, establish a new labor attitude, restore and develop industrial production, and closely coordinate with the development of agricultural production. After the meeting, all worker representatives, at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Youth Federation, the China New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, and the All-China Students' Federation, went to the Beijing Hotel for a dinner party. Comrades Li Lisan, Mr. Zhu Xuefan, Comrade Liao Chengzhi, Comrade Feng Wenbin, Comrade Cai Chang, and others personally came to offer toasts. Just as the news of the liberation of Hankou arrived, shouts of victory rose one after another, and the representatives were in extremely high spirits.

### 华北职工代表向毛主席献旗　朱总司令指示建设任务

1949-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北职工代表向毛主席献旗
  　朱总司令指示建设任务
    【本报讯】昨日（十七日）下午二时，华北职工代表向自己的伟大领袖毛主席献旗，并聆听了朱总司令的指示。代表们献的锦旗上写着：“我们永远在你们领导下，为新中国的生产建设事业奋斗前进”。献旗典礼举行时，代表们以狂热的情绪欢呼自己的领袖，口号声与鼓掌声震动会场。毛主席与两位献旗的老工人代表握手后，朱总司令继起讲话。他首先指出：这次华北职工代表会议是成功的。并说明：工人阶级应担负建设新中国的巨大历史任务，因此必须很好掌握毛主席所指出的四方八面的政策，建立新的劳动态度，恢复与发展工业生产，并很好与农业生产的发展相配合。会后，全体职工代表应中国共产党中央委员会、全国总工会、全国青年联合会、中国新民主主义青年团、全国妇女联合会、全国学生联合会的邀请，前往北京饭店聚餐。李立三同志、朱学范先生、廖承志同志、冯文彬同志、蔡畅同志等均亲临敬酒。适值汉口解放的消息传来，欢呼胜利的口号声，此起彼伏，代表们情绪至为热烈。
