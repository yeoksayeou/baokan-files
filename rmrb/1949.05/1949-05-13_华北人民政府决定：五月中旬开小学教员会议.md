Title: North China People's Government Decides: Elementary School Teacher Conference to be Held in Mid-May
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-13
Page: 1

    North China People's Government Decides: Elementary School Teacher Conference to be Held in Mid-May
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The North China People's Government has decided to convene a regional elementary school teacher conference in Ping (Beijing) on May 20th to summarize elementary education work since last September, formulate future elementary education guidelines, and discuss issues such as teachers, textbooks, and funding.

### 华北人民政府决定：五月中旬开小学教员会议

1949-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    华北人民政府决定：五月中旬开小学教员会议
    【本报讯】华北人民政府定五月二十日在平召开全区小学教员会议，以总结去年九月以来小学教育工作，制定今后小学教育方针及讨论师资、课本、经费等问题。
