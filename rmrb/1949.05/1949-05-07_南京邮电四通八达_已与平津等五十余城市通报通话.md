Title: Nanjing's Postal and Telecommunications are Well-Developed: Telephone Communication Established with Fifty Cities Including Beijing and Tianjin
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 6th -- Nanjing's telegram and telephone services are now smoothly connected to over fifty cities in the liberated areas. Telegrams from Nanjing can now reach over forty cities including Beijing, Tianjin, Baoding, Tangshan, Tanggu, Qinhuangdao, Jinan, Dezhou, Xuzhou, Bengbu, Hangzhou, Zhangjiakou, Taiyuan, Datong, and Gui Sui. Long-distance telephone service is open to over ten cities including Wuhu, Zhenjiang, Wuxi, Suzhou, and Changzhou. The Nanjing Post Office began charging postage on external mail starting from the 4th, according to the postal rates announced by the East China Postal Administration.

### 南京邮电四通八达  已与平津等五十余城市通报通话

1949-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    南京邮电四通八达
    已与平津等五十余城市通报通话
    【新华社南京六日电】南京市电报电话已与解放区五十余处城市畅通。南京对外电报现可通至北平、天津、保定、唐山、塘沽、秦皇岛、济南、德县、徐州、蚌埠、杭州、张家口、太原、大同、归绥等四十余城市。长途电话则与芜湖、镇江、无锡、苏州、常州等十余城市畅通。南京市邮局的对外邮递已于四日起按华东邮政管理局公布的邮资表收费。
