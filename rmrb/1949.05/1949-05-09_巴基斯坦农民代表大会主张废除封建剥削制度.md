Title: Pakistan Farmers' Congress Advocates Abolishing Feudal Exploitation System
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-09
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th. According to the Pakistani newspaper in Karachi, as reported by TASS: Pakistani farmers recently convened a farmers' congress in Larkana, Sindh Province. The congress put forward demands for the abolition of the feudal estate system and the tenancy system. Under the tenancy system, tenants are forced to pay two-thirds of their harvest to landlords, lessors, or usurers. The congress firmly advocated allocating all land to the farmers.

### 巴基斯坦农民代表大会主张废除封建剥削制度

1949-05-09
第3版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦农民代表大会主张废除封建剥削制度
    【新华社北平八日电】据塔斯社引喀喇蚩市巴基斯坦报纸的消息称：巴基斯坦的农民最近在信德省的拉卡纳召开农民代表大会。大会提出了废除封建大庄院制度及租佃制度的要求。在租佃制度下，佃户被迫要向地主、出租人或高利贷者缴纳其收获的三分之二。代表大会坚决主张分配全部土地给农民。
