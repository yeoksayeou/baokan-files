Title: All-Staff Meeting of the North China People's Government: Chairman Dong Biwu Speaks, Calling on Cadres to Economize and Intensify Learning
Author:
Date: 1949-05-11
Page: 1

    North China People's Government All-Staff Meeting
    Chairman Dong Biwu Speaks
    Calling on Cadres to Economize and Intensify Learning
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] Chairman Dong Biwu of the North China People's Government delivered a speech to all cadres of the North China People's Government yesterday (the 10th) at Zhongshan Park. Chairman Dong pointed out that government workers must work diligently under the victorious situation nationwide, with new and old cadres closely united, and serve the people well. Chairman Dong also called on cadres to economize on manpower and resources and to intensify learning, overcoming all difficulties in their work.

### 华北人民政府全体干部会  董必武主席讲话  号召干部节约及加紧学习

1949-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    华北人民政府全体干部会
    董必武主席讲话
    号召干部节约及加紧学习
    【本报讯】华北人民政府董必武主席昨日（十日）于中山公园对华北人民政府全体干部发表讲话。董主席指出政府工作人员在全国的胜利形势下必须认真工作，新老干部亲密的团结在一起，好好的为人民服务。董主席还号召干部节约人力物力与加紧学习，克服工作中的一切困难。
