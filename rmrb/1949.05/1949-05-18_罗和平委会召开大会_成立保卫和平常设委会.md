Title: Romanian Peace Committee Holds Congress; Establishes Standing Committee for the Defense of Peace
Author:
Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th: Bucharest Dispatch: The Romanian Committee for the Defense of Peace held a congress here. Romanian delegates who attended the World Congress of Supporters of Peace gave speeches at the congress, reporting on the proceedings of the congress. Prime Minister Groza, government officials, and representatives of the Romanian Workers' Party all attended the congress. A resolution was passed regarding the establishment of a standing committee for the defense of peace in Romania. The members of the committee include prominent artists, writers, representatives of the laboring intellectuals, and model workers.

### 罗和平委会召开大会　成立保卫和平常设委会

1949-05-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　罗和平委会召开大会
  　成立保卫和平常设委会
    【新华社北平十六日电】布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚保卫和平委员会在此间召开一个大会，大会上有出席世界拥护和平大会的罗马尼亚代表们发表演说，报告大会的经过。格罗查总理和政府要员及罗马尼亚工人党代表都参加大会。通过关于在罗马尼亚成立保卫和平常设委员会的决议。该委员会的委员中有著明的艺术家、作家、劳动知识分子代表以及劳动模范。
