Title: Our Army Expands on Both Sides of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway; Captures Yushan, Dongxiang, and Yujiang
Author:
Date: 1949-05-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Gan-dong Front, 7th: Our People's Liberation Army, expanding eastward and westward along the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway, liberated Yushan County, northeast of Shangrao, and Dongxiang County, west of Guixi, on the 5th. Yujiang County, northeast of Dongxiang, was liberated one day earlier.

### 我军向浙赣路两侧扩展  克玉山东乡余江

1949-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我军向浙赣路两侧扩展
    克玉山东乡余江
    【新华社赣东前线七日电】迟到：向浙赣路东西两方扩展的人民解放军，于五日解放上饶东北的玉山县城与贵溪以西的东乡县城。东乡东北的余江县城则已先一日解放。
