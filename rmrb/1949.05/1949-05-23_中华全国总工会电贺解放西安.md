Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to Congratulate on the Liberation of Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1949-05-23
Page: 1

　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to Congratulate on the Liberation of Xi'an
    [Our Newspaper] The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to congratulate the liberation of Xi'an. The original telegram is as follows: Commander Peng, Deputy Commanders Zhang and Zhao, and forwarded to all commanders and fighters of the First Field Army:
    The good news has arrived! We are delighted to learn that as soon as you left Guanzhong, you liberated Xi'an, the Kuomintang's counter-revolutionary fortress in the Northwest, and accelerated the liberation of the Northwest and the complete collapse of the Kuomintang's last remnants. We extend our warm greetings to you! And call on workers across the country to step up production, work hard on construction, and support the southward march of the army to rapidly complete the great victory of liberating all of China.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions

### 中华全国总工会电贺解放西安

1949-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国总工会电贺解放西安
    【本报讯】中华全国总工会电贺解放西安，原电如下：彭司令员、张、赵副司令员并转第一野战军全体指战员：
    捷报传来，欣悉你们一出关中就解放了国民党在西北的反革命堡垒——西安，加速了西北的解放和国民党最后残局的完全溃灭。我们谨向你们致热烈的敬礼！并号召全国工人，加紧生产，努力建设，支援南下大军，迅速完成解放全中国的伟大胜利。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国总工会
