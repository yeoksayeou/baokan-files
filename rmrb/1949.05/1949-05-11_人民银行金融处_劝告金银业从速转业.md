Title: People's Bank of China Financial Department Urges Gold and Silver Industry to Expedite Business Conversion

Author:

Date: 1949-05-11
Page: 1

People's Bank of China Financial Department
    Urges Gold and Silver Industry to Expedite Business Conversion

[Our Newspaper's Report] Following the North China People's Government's instruction on the ninth regarding the earnest implementation of gold and silver management measures, the Financial Department of the People's Bank of China headquarters again dispatched personnel to the Ping City Gold and Silver Jewelry Industry Association yesterday (ninth) at 6:00 PM. They invited industry representatives such as Wu Junru and 15 others to continue exchanging opinions on business conversion, redemption, and other matters. They also conveyed to the representatives to inform all businesses to expedite business conversion and avoid harboring wait-and-see or hesitant attitudes. (Yu)

### 人民银行金融处  劝告金银业从速转业

1949-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    人民银行金融处
    劝告金银业从速转业
    【本报讯】华北人民政府九日对有关部门发布认真执行金银管理办法后，人民银行总行金融处昨（九日）下午六时再度派员赴平市金银饰物业同业公会邀集该业代表武隽儒等十六人继续交换转业、收兑等意见，并传达各代表转告全体商号希望从速转业，勿存观望、犹疑心理。    （宇）
