Title: Togliatti Says the Victory of the Chinese People Has Changed the Face of the World
Author:
Date: 1949-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th: Rome, Electric News Agency: Togliatti, General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, delivered a speech in Sardinia on the 4th, praising the victory of the Chinese people, saying, "While a small group of people are dreaming of a new war, the great Chinese people have opened the road to achieving national power." Togliatti pointed out that the victory of the Chinese people is so great "that few people can understand its significance at present. But these victories have changed the face of the world, bringing five hundred million men and women to the forefront of free people." Togliatti finally called on Italian workers to draw encouragement from the experiences of the Soviet and Chinese people, "learn how to organize struggles and how to unite tightly to resist the enemy's attacks."

### 托格里亚蒂说  中国人民的胜利已经改变了世界面貌

1949-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    托格里亚蒂说  中国人民的胜利已经改变了世界面貌
    【新华社北平七日电】电通社罗马讯：意共总书记托格里亚蒂四日在撒丁发表演说赞扬中国人民的胜利称：“当一小撮人群正梦想着新战争的时候，伟大的中国人民却打开了取得全国政权的道路。”托格里亚蒂指出：中国人民的胜利是如此巨大，“以致目前很少有人能够了解它的意义。但是这些胜利已经改变了世界的面貌，把五亿男女带到自由人民的前线上来。”托格里亚蒂最后号召意大利工人从苏联和中国人民的经验中取得鼓励，“学习如何组织斗争和如何紧张团结以抵抗敌人的进攻。”
