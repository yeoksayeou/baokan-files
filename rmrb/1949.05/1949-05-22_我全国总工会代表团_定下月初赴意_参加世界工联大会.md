Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Delegation to Depart for Italy Early Next Month to Attend World Federation of Trade Unions Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-05-22
Page: 3

[Broadcast from the Xinhua Broadcasting Station in Beiping] Beiping, 21st: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has decided to send a delegation to attend the World Federation of Trade Unions Congress to be held in Milan, Italy, and to participate in the founding conferences of various industrial unions under the professional departments of the World Federation of Trade Unions. The delegation is scheduled to depart in early June, and Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, will accompany them.

### 我全国总工会代表团　定下月初赴意　参加世界工联大会

1949-05-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　我全国总工会代表团
  　定下月初赴意
  　参加世界工联大会
    【北平新华广播电台广播】北平二十一号消息：中华全国总工会决定派遣代表参加即将在意大利米兰召开的世界工联代表大会，并参加世界工联会职业部各种产业工会的成立大会，代表团准备在六月初起程，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一将一同前往。
