Title: Inter-German Trade Talks in Frankfurt
Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 3

　　Inter-German Trade
  　Talks in Frankfurt
    German News Agency reports: A delegation from the German Economic Commission, led by Alop, Director of the Inter-zonal and Foreign Trade Administration of the Soviet Zone of Germany, left Berlin for Frankfurt in western Germany on the 24th. The delegation will continue discussions with representatives of the Frankfurt Economic Council on the resumption of inter-zonal trade. These talks began last week in Berlin.

### 德境区际贸易在法兰克福商谈

1949-05-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　德境区际贸易
  　在法兰克福商谈
    德意志通讯社报道：由德国苏占区区际及对外贸易管理局局长阿洛普率领的德国经济委员会代表团，已于二十四日离柏林赴德国西部的法兰克福。代表团将与法兰克福经济委员会代表继续商谈恢复区际贸易，这一会谈系于上星期在柏林开始。
