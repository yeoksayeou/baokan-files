Title: Fourteen Businesses Including Lao Feng Xiang Approved to Exchange Gold and Silver Ornaments
Author:
Date: 1949-05-22
Page: 2

　　Lao Feng Xiang and Fourteen Other Businesses
  　Approved to Exchange Gold and Silver Ornaments
    [This Newspaper Reports] To use their existing gold and silver as capital for changing businesses, the gold and silver ornament industry in Beiping City requested to exchange them at preferential prices. As of noon yesterday (the 21st), the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China has investigated and approved the applications of fourteen businesses, including Lao Feng Xiang, Hong Chang, Wu Hua, Tian Yu Xing, Bao Shan, Qing Xing, Tian Feng Hou, Mei Feng, Qian Tai, De Sen, Feng Xiang, Bao Cheng, Tian Li Xing, and Hong Shun. The total amount of gold and silver requested to be exchanged at preferential prices is: 7,696 taels, 1 mace, 9 fen, and 2 li of gold; and 18,168 taels, 4 mace, and 1 fen of silver. (Yu)

### 老凤祥等十四家商号　核准兑换金银饰物

1949-05-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　老凤祥等十四家商号
  　核准兑换金银饰物
    【本报讯】平市金银饰物业呈请以优待价格收兑原存金银以充作转业资本，截至昨（二十一）日中午止，经中国人民银行北平分行，调查核准者已有老凤祥、宏昌、物华、天裕兴、宝善、庆兴、天丰厚、美丰、乾泰、德森、凤祥、宝成、天利兴、洪顺等十四家。呈请准予优待价格兑换之金银总额：黄金七千六百九十六两一钱九分二厘、白银一万八千一百六十八两四钱一分。　　　　　（宇）
