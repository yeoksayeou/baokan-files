Title: Central Plains Allocates Huge Sum to Restore Public Enterprises
Author:
Date: 1949-05-07
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, May 5th: The Central Plains People's Provisional Government recently decided to allocate 1 billion Central China currency units as funding for the restoration of public enterprises during the months of April, May, and June. In addition to various localities reorganizing existing public enterprises based on their specific circumstances, the Central Plains People's Provisional Government has made specific provisions for the restoration of various public enterprises in Zhengzhou, Kaifeng, Xuchang, Shangqiu and other places. The Central Plains People's Provisional Government is also beginning to plan for the protection and management of private enterprises. The democratic government will provide concrete assistance to private enterprises that are beneficial to the national economy.

### 中原拨发巨款恢复公营企业

1949-05-07
第3版()
专栏：

    中原拨发巨款恢复公营企业
    【新华社中原五日电】中原人民临时政府最近决定拨发中州币十亿元，作为四、五、六三个月份中着手恢复公营企业的经费。除各地根据不同情况整顿现有公营企业外，中原人民临时政府对郑州、开封、许昌、商邱等地应恢复之各种公营企业都作有具体规定。对于私营企业的保护和管理事宜中原人民临时政府亦在着手筹划。对有利于国民经济的私营企业，民主政府将予以具体帮助。
