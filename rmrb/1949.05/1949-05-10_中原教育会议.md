Title: Central Plains Education Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-05-10
Page: 3

    Brief News

    Central Plains Education Conference
    The Education Department of the Central Plains People's Government convened the Central Plains Education Conference in Kaifeng in late April. Forty-three education workers from various parts of the Central Plains attended. The conference determined that worker education should be strengthened in the future, and social education work should be carried out with cities as the focus.

### 中原教育会议

1949-05-10
第3版()
专栏：简讯

    中原教育会议
    中原人民政府教育部曾于四月下旬在开封召开中原教育会议，到中原各地教育工作人员四十三人，会议确定今后应加强职工教育，开展以城市为重点的社会教育工作。
