Title: Fierce Fighting in Various Parts of Java
Author:
Date: 1949-05-05
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th: A TASS dispatch from The Hague: According to a Dutch news agency, fierce fighting is taking place in several areas of Java, with particularly intense battles near Batavia. Fighting is also underway in the Yogyakarta and Surabaya areas. Positional warfare is being waged around Malang. In central and southern Sumatra, Dutch forces are conducting large-scale offensives against Indonesian guerrillas.

### 爪哇各地战事激烈

1949-05-05
第3版()
专栏：

    爪哇各地战事激烈
    【新华社北平四日电】塔斯社海牙讯：据荷兰通讯社消息，爪哇若干地区进行激战，巴达维亚附近的战事特别激烈。日惹和泗水地区亦在进行战斗。玛琅四周正在进行阵地战。在苏门答腊中部与南部，荷军正对印尼游击队进行大规模攻势。
