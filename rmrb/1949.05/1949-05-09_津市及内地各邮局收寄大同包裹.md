Title: Tianjin and Other Inland Post Offices Accept Parcels to Datong
Author:
Date: 1949-05-09
Page: 2

    Tianjin and Other Inland Post Offices
    Accept Parcels to Datong
    [Tianjin, 8th] From yesterday, Tianjin and other inland post offices began accepting parcels to Datong.
