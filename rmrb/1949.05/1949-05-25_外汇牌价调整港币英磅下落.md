Title: Foreign Exchange Rates Adjusted, Hong Kong Dollar and Pound Sterling Decline
Author:
Date: 1949-05-25
Page: 2

　　Foreign Exchange Rates Adjusted, Hong Kong Dollar and Pound Sterling Decline
    [This Newspaper] News: Today (25th), the Bank of China's foreign exchange rates, except for the US dollar which remained unchanged at 1,040 yuan, all others decreased. The Hong Kong dollar at 138 yuan fell 29 yuan, 7 jiao and 4 fen compared to the previous exchange rate, and the pound sterling at 2,208 yuan fell 475 yuan, 8 jiao and 4 fen. (Yu)

### 外汇牌价调整港币英磅下落

1949-05-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　外汇牌价调整港币英磅下落
    【本报讯】今日（二十五日）中国银行外汇牌价，除美汇未动仍为一千零四十元外，余均下降，港币一百三十八元较前次牌价落二十九元七角四分，英磅二千二百零八元，落四百七十五元八角四分。（宇）
