Title: Tangshan Qixin Cement Company: Output Increases, April Already Exceeds Pre-Liberation Levels
Author:
Date: 1949-05-11
Page: 2

    Tangshan Qixin Cement Company
    Output Increases
    April Already Exceeds Pre-Liberation Levels
    [Tianjin Dispatch] The cement production of Tangshan Qixin Cement Company continues to increase. In April, it reached 12,000 tons, exceeding the average monthly production level of over 11,000 tons before liberation. To assist the factory in replenishing equipment, repairing machinery, and striving to reach a monthly output of 21,000 tons, the Trade Corporation has decided to further adjust purchase prices.

### 唐山启新洋灰公司  产量增加  四月份已超过解放前

1949-05-11
第2版()
专栏：

    唐山启新洋灰公司
    产量增加
    四月份已超过解放前
    【天津电】唐山启新洋灰公司的洋灰生产量，继续增加，四月份已达一万二千吨，超过解放前平均月产一万一千多吨的生产水平。为协助该厂补充设备，修理机器，争取达到每月两万一千吨的产量，贸易总公司已决定进一步调整收购价格。
