Author:
Date: 1949-05-28
Page: 3

　　Circular of the Head Office of the People's Bank of China
  　General Document No. 1
    The Bank is issuing a ten-yuan People's Currency note, the design of which is as follows: Front: The background is ginger yellow, with the words "中国人民银行" (People's Bank of China) in the upper middle, a yellow-green and brown flower symbol on the right containing the horizontally written characters "拾圆" (Ten Yuan), the Deputy Manager's seal in the lower right, a train scene on the left, brown floral borders in the four corners, and the words "中华民国三十八年" (Year 38 of the Republic of China) in the lower middle. Back: The design is entirely green, with the words "中国人民银行" (People's Bank of China) at the top, the number "10" in the middle and in the four corners, and the year "1949" at the bottom. This is hereby announced.
    May 25, Year 38 of the Republic of China

### 中国人民银行总行通告　总发字第一号

1949-05-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国人民银行总行通告
  　总发字第一号
    本行发行人民券十元的一种，式样为：正面：底纹姜黄色，上方中间有“中国人民银行”字样，右方为黄绿色与茶色花符，内有横写“拾圆”字样，右下方有经副理章，左方有火车图景，四角花边为茶色，下方中间有中华民国三十八年字样。背面：图案全为绿色，上方有“中国人民银行”字样，中间及四角为“１０”字，下方有“１９４９”字样。特此通告中华民国三十八年五月二十五日
