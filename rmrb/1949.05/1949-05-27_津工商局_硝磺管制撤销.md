Title: Tianjin Industry and Commerce Bureau Abolishes Nitre and Sulfur Controls
Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 2

　　Tianjin Industry and Commerce Bureau

　　Nitre and Sulfur Controls Abolished

    [This newspaper report] The Tianjin Municipal Industry and Commerce Bureau, in order to facilitate industrial production in various regions, has decided to abolish controls on the import and export of nitre and sulfur in the city. From now on, merchants and civilians (excluding government procurement) can freely transport and sell these goods in and out of the city without requiring proof.

### 津工商局　硝磺管制撤销

1949-05-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　津工商局
  　硝磺管制撤销
    【本报讯】津市工商局为便利各地工业生产。特决定撤销硝磺出入该市之管制，凡商民（公家采购者除外）今后运销该货时，可自由出入，不需证明。
