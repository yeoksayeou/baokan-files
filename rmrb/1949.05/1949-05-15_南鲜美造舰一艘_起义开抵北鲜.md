Title: South Korean American-Made Warship Uprising Arrives in North Korea
Author:
Date: 1949-05-15
Page: 3

    South Korean American-Made Warship
    Uprising Arrives in North Korea
    Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 14th—According to a Pyongyang radio broadcast on the 13th: A U.S.-made warship of the South Korean reactionary government's Navy Department staged an uprising in Kunsan Port, South Korea, on the 10th. On the morning of the 11th, the ship, carrying full armaments, arrived at Wonsan Port in North Korea. The entire crew of the uprising was warmly welcomed by representatives of the Coast Guard of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea upon landing.

### 南鲜美造舰一艘  起义开抵北鲜

1949-05-15
第3版()
专栏：

    南鲜美造舰一艘
    起义开抵北鲜
    【新华社北平十四日电】据平壤电台十三日广播：南朝鲜反动政府海军部的美造兵舰一艘，十日在南朝鲜的金山港起义。十一日上午，该舰携带全副武装抵达北朝鲜的元山港。起义的全体船员在登陆时受到朝鲜人民民主共和国海岸防卫军代表的热烈欢迎。
