Title: Delegation Attending Peace Congress Tours Leningrad
Author:
Date: 1949-05-09
Page: 3
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th. According to TASS, Leningrad: The Chinese delegation attending the World Peace Congress in Prague arrived in the Soviet capital on April 29th and departed for Leningrad on May 6th for sightseeing. During the day, the delegation toured the city and visited the Leningrad Soviet Defense Museum. In the evening, they attended a performance at the Kirov Opera and Ballet Theatre.

### 我出席和平大会代表团游览列宁格勒

1949-05-09
第3版()
专栏：

    我出席和平大会代表团
    游览列宁格勒
    【新华社北平八日电】据塔斯社列宁格勒讯：出席世界拥护和平大会布拉格会议的中国代表团于四月二十九日抵苏京后，五月六日由莫斯科赴列宁格勒游览。代表团日间在市内观光，并参观列宁格勒苏联国防博物馆，晚赴基洛夫歌剧院观剧。
