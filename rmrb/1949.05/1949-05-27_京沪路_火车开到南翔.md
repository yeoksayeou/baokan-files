Title: Jinghu Railway: Train Reaches Nanxiang
Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 1

　　Jinghu Road
  　Train Reaches Nanxiang
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping Broadcast] Wuxi, 26th—News: The eastbound train on the Jinghu Railway, closely following the advance of the People's Liberation Army, reached Nanxiang Station, a suburb of Shanghai, on the 22nd. Workers from the Suzhou Engineering Section of the Jinghu Railway spent three days rushing to repair the Qingyang Port Iron Bridge between Kunshan and Nanxiang, which had been destroyed by the Kuomintang bandit troops.

### 京沪路　火车开到南翔

1949-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　京沪路
  　火车开到南翔
    【北平新华广播电台广播】无锡二十六号消息：京沪铁路东行车，紧随着人民解放军的前进在二十二号通达上海近郊的南翔车站。京沪铁路苏州工务段的工人曾经用三天的时间将昆山和南翔之间被国民党匪军破坏的青阳港大铁桥抢修完工。
