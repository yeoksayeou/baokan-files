　　Brief News
    [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 16th] Reports from various sources: British Foreign Secretary Bevin issued a statement in the British House of Commons on the 11th regarding aid to the Burmese reactionary government, stating that during the Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference in late April, the Prime Ministers of Britain, India, Pakistan, and Ceylon met to discuss the issue of providing aid to the Burmese Thakin Nu government. They unanimously hoped to do their best to support the Thakin Nu government. To expedite the implementation of this resolution, a special agency has been established in Burma, mainly composed of the ambassadors of various countries stationed in Burma, which will consider financial, military, and other aid issues.

### 简讯

1949-05-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　简讯
    【新华社北平十六日电】各方报道：英外长贝文十一日在英下院发表援助缅甸反动政府的声明称：在四月下旬英联邦总理会议期间，英、印、巴基斯坦与锡兰四国总理曾集会讨论缅甸达钦努政府所提的关于给该政府以援助的问题。他们一致期望尽力给达钦努政府以支持。为加速执行这一决议起见，一个专门的机构业已在缅成立，主要由各国驻缅大使组成，该机构将考虑财政、军火及其他援助问题。
