Title: Sending Off the Workers' Delegation Abroad: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Various Circles in Beiping Hold Evening Party
Author:
Date: 1949-05-31
Page: 2

To send off the Chinese workers' delegation attending the World Federation of Trade Unions Congress, the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Beiping Municipal Federation of Trade Unions decided to hold a farewell party at the Dahua Cinema at 8:00 PM this afternoon (31st). More than 800 people, including representatives of workers from various factories in Beiping, as well as representatives from various organizations and democratic figures, will participate. The film "The Bridge" will be screened after the meeting.
　　　　　　　　　　 (Publicity and Education Department of the Municipal Trade Union)

### 欢送工人代表团出国　全总暨平市各界举行晚会

1949-05-31
第2版()
专栏：

　　欢送工人代表团出国
  　全总暨平市各界举行晚会
    为欢送出席世界工联大会的中国工人代表团出国，中华全国总工会暨北平市职工总会筹委会定今（三十一）日下午八时在大华电影院举行欢送晚会。有北平各工厂职工代表及各机关团体，各民主人士共八百余人参加，会后并放映电影“桥”  　　　　　　　　　　（市职工会宣教部）
