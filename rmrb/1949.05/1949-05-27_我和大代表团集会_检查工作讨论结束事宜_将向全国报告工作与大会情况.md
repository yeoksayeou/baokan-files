Title: I Met with the Delegation to Inspect Work and Discuss Closing Matters; Will Report Work and Conference Situation to the Whole Country
Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 1

　　I Met with the Delegation
  　Inspecting Work and Discussing Closing Matters
    Will Report Work and Conference Situation to the Whole Country
    [Report from this City] The Chinese delegation attending the World Congress of Supporters of Peace held a plenary meeting at the Beijing Hotel at 2:00 PM on the 26th after returning to Beiping to review and discuss the work of the delegation and closing matters. The meeting decided to draft a work report by May 31st and report the work of the delegation and the situation of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace to the people of the whole country through news agencies and newspapers; and to convey the resolutions of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace regarding the organization of World Congress of Supporters of Peace branches in participating countries and the establishment of peace prizes. The meeting also resolved to send telegrams to the Soviet Union's Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Czech Association of Artists and Writers, and other people's organizations, to thank them for their warm reception of the Chinese delegation. After the delegation concludes its work, any unfinished matters will be handled by the head and deputy head of the delegation and the secretariat.

### 我和大代表团集会　检查工作讨论结束事宜  将向全国报告工作与大会情况

1949-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　我和大代表团集会
  　检查工作讨论结束事宜
    将向全国报告工作与大会情况
    【本市讯】出席世界拥护和平大会的中国代表团返平后，于二十六日下午二时在北京饭店举行全体会议，检查和讨论代表团的工作及结束事宜。会议决定于本月三十一日以前草成工作报告书，并经由通讯社和报社向全国人民报告代表团的工作和世界拥护和平大会的情况；并传达世界拥护和平大会关于组织与会各国的世界拥护和平大会分会和设置和平奖金等决议。会议并决议致电苏联对外文化协会和捷克文艺作家协会等人民团体，感谢他们对中国代表团的热诚招待。该国在结束工作后，未了事宜，即由代表团正副团长和秘书处负责办理。
