Title: Tianjin Trading Company Supports Private Industry
Author:
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 1

    Tianjin Trading Company
    Supports Private Industry
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 10th] The Tianjin Municipal Trading Company's department store, in order to support the development of private industries such as dyeing and weaving, knitting, rubber, and soap manufacturing, after investigating the raw material supply, production situation, and finished product sales of more than forty factories, has successively signed contracts with factories such as Juyuan Rubber Factory, Hesheng Soap Factory, and Guangdaocheng Knitting and Dyeing Factory. The company will supply raw materials to these factories for processing, or purchase their finished products.

### 天津贸易公司  扶助私营工业

1949-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    天津贸易公司
    扶助私营工业
    【新华社天津十日电】天津市贸易公司所属百货公司，为了扶植染织、针织、橡胶、肥皂等私营工业的发展，在调查了四十多家工厂的原料供给、生产情形、成品推销等情况之后，已先后与巨源橡胶厂、和生造胰厂、光道成针织漂染厂等订立合同，由该公司供给原料，使其加工或收购其成品。
