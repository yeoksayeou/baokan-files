Title: Qingdao Outskirts: Our Army Liberates Jimo; Longdong Recovers Qingyang
Author:
Date: 1949-05-29
Page: 1

　　Qingdao Outskirts
  　Our Army Liberates Jimo
  　Longdong Recovers Qingyang
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Jinan, 28th: The important stronghold of Jimo County, on the outskirts of Qingdao, was liberated on the 26th. The defending enemy fled south.
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Yan'an, 28th: A unit of the People's Liberation Army's local armed forces in eastern Gansu recovered Qingyang on the 20th of this month. The enemy defending the city fled towards Yimaguan. The people of Qingyang, who had been trampled upon by the Hu Ma bandit troops for more than two years, are now enthusiastically celebrating their liberation.

### 青岛外围　我军解放即墨　陇东收复庆阳

1949-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　青岛外围
  　我军解放即墨
  　陇东收复庆阳
    【北平新华广播电台广播】济南二十八日消息：青岛外围的重要据点即墨县城，已经在二十六日解放，守敌向南逃窜。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】延安二十八日消息：甘肃东部的人民解放军地方武装一部，本月二十日收复庆阳，守城敌人向驿马关逃跑。被胡马匪军践踏了两年多的庆阳人民，现在正热烈地庆祝解放。
