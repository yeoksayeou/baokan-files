Title: Postal Service Opens in Xinxing District, Shijiazhuang City
Author:
Date: 1949-05-19
Page: 2

　　Shijiazhuang City Xinxing District Opens Postal Service
　　[Shijiazhuang Electric] The Shijiazhuang Post Office has begun to accept ordinary letters, registered mail, newspapers, and printed matter to and from Datong, Xinxiang, Anyang, and Suzhou, Zhenjiang, Wuxi, Changzhou, Wuhu, Hangzhou and other places, and accepts parcels to Datong, Xinxiang, and Anyang.

### 石家庄市兴新区通邮

1949-05-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　石家庄市兴新区通邮
    【石家庄电】石家庄邮局已开始收寄大同、新乡、安阳及苏州、镇江、无锡、常州、芜湖、杭州等地之平信、挂号、新闻纸、印刷品等邮件，并收寄往大同、新乡、安阳之包裹。
