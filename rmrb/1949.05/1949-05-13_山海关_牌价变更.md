Title: Shanhaiguan Exchange Rate Change
Author:
Date: 1949-05-13
Page: 2

    Shanhaiguan
    Exchange Rate Change
    [This newspaper] Shanhaiguan People's Bank, Northeast Bank Joint Office, has announced that, starting from May 9th, one RMB will be exchanged for 250 Northeast Currency or Great Wall Currency.

### 山海关  牌价变更

1949-05-13
第2版()
专栏：

    山海关
    牌价变更
    【本报讯】山海关人民银行、东北银行联合办事处，自五月九日起人民券一元兑换东北币、长城币二百五十元。
