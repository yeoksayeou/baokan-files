Title: Chinese Academic Circles Hold Grand Gathering; Comrade Zhou Enlai Speaks, Encouraging Participation in the Construction of New Democratic Nation

Author: 
Date: 1949-05-06
Page: 2

    Chinese Academic Circles Hold Grand Gathering
    Comrade Zhou Enlai Speaks
    Encouraging Participation in the Construction of New Democratic Nation
    [This newspaper] On the afternoon of the 5th, the Cultural Takeover Committee of the Ping Municipal Military Control Commission held a symposium for academic circles at the Beijing Hotel at 3:00 PM. Over 200 people from academic circles attended, including Liang Xi, Fan Wenlan, Ma Xulun, Tang Yongtong, Lu Zhiwei, Shen Zhiyuan, Yan Jici, Yuan Hanqing, Zhang Zhirang, Shen Tilan, Zeng Zhaolun, Zhou Jianren, Hu Xiansu, Pan Shu, Hu Yuzhi, Li Jinxi, Yang Zhensheng, Yang Renxuan, Li Zong'en, Zhang Zigao, Wu Han, Xia Kangnong, Qian Duansheng, Fei Xiaotong, Fan Hong, Fei Qing, Qian Weichang, and Xiang Da. Comrade Zhou Enlai personally attended and gave a speech. This was a grand gathering of Chinese academic circles. At the beginning of the symposium, Comrade Zhou Yang delivered the opening remarks. Liang Xi, a former Nanjing Central University agricultural and forestry scientist, spoke, stating that the scientific community is willing to study revolutionary theory and hopes that the People's Government will organize the scientific community to serve the people and implement planned science. Mr. Shen Zhiyuan reported on the work of the Chinese Association of Scientific Workers. Comrade Zhou Enlai then spoke amidst applause. He explained in detail the historical process and lessons learned from the Chinese New Democratic Revolution, analyzed the difference between new democracy and old democracy, and explained from economic, political, military, and cultural aspects that the New Democratic Revolution must be led by the proletariat. Finally, he encouraged academic and scientific circles to actively participate in the construction of the New Democratic state so that science can truly serve the people. Afterwards, Professor Yuan Hanqing reported on the situation of the Chinese Association of Scientific Workers and proposed a suggestion to prepare for the convening of a national congress of scientific workers. Following this, Mr. Fan Hong and Comrade Fan Wenlan spoke. The meeting adjourned at 7:00 PM.

### 中国学术界举行盛会  周恩来同志讲话  鼓励参加新民主主义国家建设工作

1949-05-06
第2版()
专栏：

    中国学术界举行盛会
    周恩来同志讲话
    鼓励参加新民主主义国家建设工作
    【本报讯】平市军管会文化接管委员会于五日下午三时假北京饭店举行学术界座谈会，到有梁希、范文澜、马叙伦、汤用彤、陆志韦、沈志远、严济慈、袁翰青、张志让、沈体兰、曾昭抡、周建人、胡先＠、潘菽、胡愈之、黎锦熙、杨振声、杨人＠、李宗恩、张子高、吴晗、夏康农、钱端升、费孝通、樊弘、费青、钱伟长、向达等学术界人士共二百余人。周恩来同志亲临讲话，这是中国学术界的一次盛会。座谈会开始，周扬同志致开会词后，前南京中央大学农林学家梁希讲话称：科学界愿意学习革命理论，希望人民政府组织科学界为人民服务，并实行计划科学，沈志远先生报告中国科学工作者协会的工作概况后，周恩来同志在掌声中讲话，他详细地讲述了中国新民主主义革命的历史过程和经验教训，分析了新民主与旧民主的区别，从经济、政治、军事、文化各方面说明新民主主义革命必须由无产阶级领导。最后，鼓励学术界、科学界积极参加新民主主义的国家建设工作，使科学真正为人民服务。之后，袁翰青教授报告中国科学工作者协会情况，并提出筹备召开全国科学工作者代表大会的建议，继有樊弘先生、范文澜同志讲话，至七时散会。
