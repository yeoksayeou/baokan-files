Title: Tianjin-Shenyang Telegrams Open
Author:
Date: 1949-05-09
Page: 2

    Tianjin-Shenyang Telegrams Open
    [Tianjin, 8th dispatch] Telegram service between Tianjin and Shenyang is open starting today. Telegram service from Tianjin to other areas in the Northeast will also be gradually opened soon.

### 津沈电报开放

1949-05-09
第2版()
专栏：

    津沈电报开放
    【天津八日电】天津沈阳间电报今起开放。津市发往东北其他各处电报，不久亦将陆续畅通。
