Title: Increased Production at Tongchuan Public Coal Mine
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping Broadcast: News from Guanzhong, 21st: Production has increased at the Tongchuan Public Coal Mine. After the Tongchuan Public Coal Mine was re-liberated on April 27th, work resumed the next day, and the workers' enthusiasm for production is very high. According to statistics from two mines on the 11th and 12th, the average daily coal production per person during the Kuomintang's rule was 0.58 tons; now it has increased to 0.61 tons.

### 铜川公营煤矿产量增加

1949-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　铜川公营煤矿产量增加
    【北平新华广播电台广播】关中二十一日消息：铜川公营煤矿产量增加，铜川公营煤矿在四月二十七日重新解放后，第二天就开始复工，工人们生产热情很高。据十一日、十二日两个矿井的统计：在国民党统治时期每人每天平均产煤零点五八吨；现在已经提高到零点六一吨。
