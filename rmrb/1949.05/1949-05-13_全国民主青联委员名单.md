Title: List of Members of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation

Author: 
Date: 1949-05-13
Page: 1

    [This newspaper, Beiping News] The First National Congress of the All-China Youth, on the 10th, elected 87 official members and 22 alternate members of the National Committee of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation. In addition, there are 10 vacancies for official members, which will be filled by the newly liberated areas after the country is completely liberated in the future. The list of official members and alternate members is now announced as follows:

    Eighty-seven official members: Ding Zhihui, Qian Jiaju, Fang Hua, Wang Zheng, Wang Zhen, Wang Renshu, Wang Yizhen, Wang @, Gu Yuan, Ai Siqi, Zhu Dehai, Song Ping, Song Yiping, Keli Geng, Wu Xue, Wu Han, Wu Yaozong, He Li, He Qifang, Yu Guangsheng, Shen Zhiyuan, Shen Tilan, Sha Qianli, Lü Ji, Li Chang, Li Yongxiao, Li Boqiu, Li Xiangfu, Li Huichuan, Liu @, Hu Sheng, Hu Ziying, Hu Qiaomu, Ke Zaishuo, Shi Ruzhang, Fan Changjiang, Fan Xueyi, Gao Tang, Chen Beisheng, Chen Jiakang, Chen Jingqian, Lu Ping, Ma Feng, Ma Sicong, Xia Kangnong, Yuan Hanqing, Zhang Yuying, Zhang Xuesi, Huang Hua, Huang Ruotun, Huang Dingxin, Sun Qimeng, Gong Meibin, Shen Kangnian, Qu Tangliang, Feng Wenbin, Feng Baiju, Su Yu, Zeng Sheng, Fu Zhong, Qiao Mu, Dong Xin, Ge Zhicheng, Yang Chengwu, Yang Jingren, Liao Chengzhi, Lai Yali, Liu Shanben, Liu Daosheng, Liu Zunqi, Zhao Cang, Zhao Shuli, Jiang Nanxiang, Qian Sanqiang, Qian Junrui, Qian Weichang, Xie Bangding, Xie Xuehong, Xie Fucheng, Xue Chengye, Dai Baitao, Sa Kongliao, Han Tianshi, Luo Yi, Yan Jingyao, Yan Jici, Xiao Hua.
