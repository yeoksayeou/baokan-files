Title: Telegram Service Between Hangzhou and Beiping Now Open
Author:
Date: 1949-05-08
Page: 1

[Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Since the liberation of Hangzhou City on the 3rd of this month, external telecommunications have been rapidly restored. Telegram service between Beiping and Hangzhou officially opened on the 6th of this month. In addition, wireless telephone service between Tianjin and Nanjing also officially opened on the 7th.

### 杭州和北平电报已通

1949-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    杭州和北平电报已通
    【北平新华广播电台广播】杭州市在本月三号解放以后，对外电讯迅速恢复，北平和杭州之间的电报已经在本月六号正式开放。另外天津和南京之间的无线电话从七号起也正式通话。
