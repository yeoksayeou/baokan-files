Title: Absorbing Soviet Experience to Complete the Five-Year Plan: Report by the Czechoslovak Premier at the CPCz Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-05-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, May 29th: TASS, Prague—Czechoslovak Premier Zápotocký delivered a report at the Ninth Congress of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia on the 26th regarding the Five-Year Plan for the reconstruction and development of the national economy. The plan stipulates the massive development of heavy industry and the improvement of the economic welfare and culture of the working people. Zápotocký emphasized that Czechoslovakia's economy should develop along the lines of a planned system and establish closer economic and trade relations with people's democratic countries. The Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and the working people must absorb the rich experience of the Soviet Union and the Soviet people in implementing their Five-Year Plan when executing their Five-Year Plan. After the report, the meeting proceeded with discussions. The General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Slovak Communist Party, Bašťovanský, stated that Slovak workers and staff are actively implementing the Five-Year Plan to ensure the industrialization of Slovakia. Several party cadres and responsible persons from industrial departments also participated in the discussions.

### 吸收苏联经验　完成五年计划　捷共代表大会上捷总理报告

1949-05-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　吸收苏联经验
  　完成五年计划
  　捷共代表大会上捷总理报告
    【新华社北平二十九日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷总理萨波托斯基二十六日在捷克斯洛伐克共产党第九届代表大会上作了关于重建与发展国民经济五年计划的报告。该计划规定重工业的巨大发展和劳动人民经济福利与文化的提高。萨波托斯基着重指出：捷克斯洛伐克的经济，应当遵循计划制度的路线发展，并与人民民主国家建立更密切的经济与贸易关系。捷克斯洛伐克共产党和劳动人民在执行他们的五年计划时，必须吸取苏联和苏联人民实现五年计划的丰富经验。报告结束后，会议即进行讨论。斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会总书记巴斯托凡斯基称：斯洛伐克的工人与职员正积极执行保证使斯洛伐克工业化的五年计划。若干党的干部和工业部门的负责人员也参加了讨论。
