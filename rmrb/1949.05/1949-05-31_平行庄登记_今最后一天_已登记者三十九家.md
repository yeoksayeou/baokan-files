Title: Parallel Bank Registration: Last Day Today; Thirty-Nine Banks Already Registered
Author:
Date: 1949-05-31
Page: 2

Parallel Bank Registration
    Last Day Today
  　Thirty-Nine Banks Already Registered
    [Report from this newspaper] Today (May 31st) is the last day for private silver and money businesses in Beiping to register. As of yesterday (May 30th), a total of thirty-nine banks and firms in the city have completed the registration procedures with the Beiping Branch of the People's Bank, accounting for approximately sixty percent of all banks and firms.

　　　　　　　
　　　　　(Yu)

### 平行庄登记  今最后一天　已登记者三十九家

1949-05-31
第2版()
专栏：

　　平行庄登记
    今最后一天
  　已登记者三十九家
    【本报讯】北平私营银钱业登记期限今日（三十一日）为最后一天。截至昨日（三十日）止，本市各行庄已向人民银行北平分行进行办理登记手续者总计有三十九家，约占全部行庄百分之六十。　　　　　　　
　　
　　　　　（宇）
