Title: PLA Fourth Field Army Launches Large-Scale Yangtze River Crossing; Wuhan Tri-Cities Entirely Liberated; Continual Capture of Daye, Shihuiyao, and Other Locations on the South Bank of the Yangtze River
Author:
Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 17th: The People's Liberation Army entered Hankou at 6 PM on the 16th, and was warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life. The city is in good order. The defending enemy fled south.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 17th, 1600 hours: The Wuhan Tri-Cities have been entirely liberated. The People's Liberation Army completely liberated Hankou at 7 PM yesterday (the 16th), and then occupied Wuchang and Hanyang at 7 AM this morning. The People's Liberation Army was warmly welcomed by the people upon entering Wuhan. The "Eight Rules of Conduct" proclamation of the People's Liberation Army is posted all over the Tri-Cities.

Xinhua News Agency, Yangtze River Front, 17th: The People's Liberation Army Fourth Field Army's southward advance has, at dawn on the 15th, broken through the enemy's defense line in the central section of the Yangtze River, along the Tuanfeng to Tianjiazhen line east of Wuhan, carrying out a large-scale Yangtze River crossing operation on a front of over 200 li. As of the evening of the 16th, it has occupied Daye, Shihuiyao, Huangshigang (the above two locations are southeast of Echeng), Duanjiadian, and Huarong (the above two locations are northwest of Echeng) and other locations on the south bank of the Yangtze River. The various armies continue to cross the river and are vigorously expanding their operations.

### 解放军第四野战军大规模渡江　武汉三镇全部解放  长江南岸连克大冶石灰窑等地

1949-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　解放军第四野战军大规模渡江
  　武汉三镇全部解放
    长江南岸连克大冶石灰窑等地
    【新华社汉口十七日电】人民解放军于十六日下午六时进入汉口，受到各界市民热烈欢迎。市内秩序良好。守敌南逃。
    【新华社武汉十七日十六时电】武汉三镇已全部解放，人民解放军于昨（十六）日下午七时完全解放汉口，随即于今日早晨七时进占武昌和汉阳。解放军进入武汉时受到人民热烈欢迎，市内秩序良好，人民解放军约法八章的布告贴满三镇。
    【新华社长江前线十七日电】人民解放军第四野战军南下大军，已于十五日拂晓在武汉以东的团风至田家镇一线，突破长江中段敌军防线，实行二百余里宽正面的大规模渡江作战，至十六日晚止，已占领长江南岸的大冶、石灰窑、黄石港（以上两地在鄂城东南）、段家店、华容（以上两地在鄂城西北）等地，各路大军仍继续渡江，并猛烈扩展中。
