Title: Yan'an Celebrates May Day
Author:
Date: 1949-05-04
Page: 1
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] On the afternoon of May 1st, over 16,000 people, including workers from Yan'an's public enterprises and factories, representatives from various sectors, and citizens, held a grand rally to celebrate the "May Day" International Labor Day and the great victories of the People's Liberation Army in liberating Nanjing and Taiyuan. Speakers at the rally included Xi Zhongxun, Secretary of the Northwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Mingxuan, Vice Chairman of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government, Zhang Zhu, Chief of Staff of the Northwest Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, and labor hero Yang Buhao. They called on all workers and the broad masses of the people to resolutely support Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu's order to advance and completely eliminate the Kuomintang reactionaries who dared to resist. At the same time, they called on all workers to step up production and support the front lines. After the meeting, a grand parade was held, with Yangko teams from various factories, government agencies, and schools, as well as tank teams from the Northwest Military Region, appearing on the streets. The rumbling of the tanks, the sound of drums and gongs, and the cheers merged into one, fully demonstrating the great strength and confidence in victory of the people of the Northwest.

### 延安各界庆祝五一

1949-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    延安各界庆祝五一
    【北平新华广播电台广播】延安市各公营企业工厂工人、各界代表和市民一万六千多人，五月一号下午举行盛大集会，热烈庆祝“五一”国际劳动节和人民解放军解放南京太原的伟大胜利。在会上讲话的有中共中央西北局书记习仲勋、陕甘宁边区政府副主席杨明轩、人民解放军西北军区参谋长张注户和劳动英雄杨步浩等。他们号召全体工人和广大人民群众坚决拥护毛主席和朱总司令的进军命令，彻底消灭敢于抵抗的国民党反动派。同时号召全体工人加紧生产，支援前线。会后举行盛大游行，各工厂机关学校的秧歌队和西北军区的战车队同时出现在街头，隆隆的战车声、锣鼓声和欢呼，打成一片，充分表现出西北人民的伟大力量和胜利信心。
