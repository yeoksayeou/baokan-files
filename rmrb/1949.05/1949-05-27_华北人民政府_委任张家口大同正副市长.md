Title: The North China People's Government Appoints Mayors and Deputy Mayors of Zhangjiakou and Datong

Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 1

　　The North China People's Government
  　Appoints Mayors and Deputy Mayors of Zhangjiakou and Datong
    [This newspaper's dispatch] On May 24th, the North China People's Government appointed Sun Jingwen as the Mayor of Zhangjiakou City People's Government in Chahar Province, Quan Zhemin as the Deputy Mayor, Zhao Han as the Mayor of Datong City People's Government, and Li Tiesheng as the Deputy Mayor.

### 华北人民政府　委任张家口大同正副市长

1949-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府
  　委任张家口大同正副市长
    【本报讯】华北人民政府于五月二十四日委任孙敬文为察哈尔省张家口市人民政府市长，权哲民为副市长，赵汉为大同市人民政府市长，李铁生为副市长。
