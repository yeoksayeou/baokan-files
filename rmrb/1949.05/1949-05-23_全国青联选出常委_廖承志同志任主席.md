Title: All-China Youth Federation Elects Standing Committee Members; Comrade Liao Chengzhi Appointed Chairman
Author:
Date: 1949-05-23
Page: 1

　　All-China Youth Federation Elects Standing Committee Members
  　Comrade Liao Chengzhi Appointed Chairman
    [Beiping News] The National Committee of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation held its first meeting the day before yesterday (the 21st). Fifty committee members attended, with Liao Chengzhi as chairman. The meeting principally approved the revised draft of "The Youth Movement in the Chinese People's Liberation War and the Basic Tasks of Chinese Youth in the Future," and decided to declare to the World Federation of Democratic Youth that the Youth Federation of the Liberated Areas of China has been expanded into the All-China Democratic Youth Federation. Comrade Liao Chengzhi pointed out at the meeting that matters concerning China's selection of representatives to participate in the World Youth Festival and the Second World Youth Congress to be held successively in Budapest, Hungary, in August and September this year were resolved to be the responsibility of the Standing Committee. Finally, the Standing Committee was elected, including Liao Chengzhi, Qian Junrui, Xie Xuehong, Qian Sanqiang, Sha Qianli, Wu Han, Sa Kongliao, Shen Zhiyuan, Chen Jiakang, He Li, Dong Xin, Xie Bangding, Qu Tangliang, Mei Gongbin, Liu Shanben, Li Boqiu, Qian Jiaju, Qian Weichang, Fu Zhong, Chen Jingqian, Zhang Xuesi, Liu Shi, Shen Tilan, Wu Yaozong, and Li Huichuan, a total of twenty-five people. Liao Chengzhi was appointed Chairman, and Qian Junrui, Xie Xuehong, Qian Sanqiang, and Sha Qianli were appointed Vice-Chairmen.

### 全国青联选出常委　廖承志同志任主席

1949-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国青联选出常委
  　廖承志同志任主席
    【北平讯】中华全国民主青年联合总会全国委员会，前（二十一）日召开第一次会议，出席委员五十人，由廖承志任主席。会中对“中国人民解放战争中的青年运动及今后中国青年的基本任务”的修正草案，作原则上的通过，决定向世界民主青年联盟声明：中国解放区青年联合会已扩大为中华全国民主青年联合总会。廖承志同志在会上指出，中国选派代表参加今年八月与九月在匈京布达佩斯相继召开的世界青年节及世界青年第二次代表大会事宜，决议由常务委员会负责筹备。最后选举常务委员会，计选出廖承志、钱俊瑞、谢雪红、钱三强、沙千里、吴晗、萨空了、沈志远、陈家康、何礼、董昕、谢邦定、区棠亮、梅龚彬、刘善本、李伯球、千家驹、钱伟长、傅钟、陈敬谦、张学思、柳湜、沈体兰、吴耀宗、李汇川等二十五人。廖承志任正主席，钱俊瑞、谢雪红、钱三强、沙千里等任副主席。
