Title: The Third Class of Kaifeng Workers' School, 255 Students to Go South

Author:

Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 1

　　Kaifeng Workers' School Third Class of Students
  　255 Students to Go South
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 17th] The third class of over 500 students from Kaifeng Workers' School is about to graduate. Many students have signed up to cross the river and go south to work, and 255 have been approved by the school. The first and second classes of students who went south numbered 304, making a total of 559 students going south to work, including this group.

### 开封职工学校第三期学员　二百五十五名将南下

1949-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　开封职工学校第三期学员
  　二百五十五名将南下
    【新华社开封十七日电】开封市职工学校第三期五百余名学员即将毕业。学员中报名渡江南下工作者甚多，经校方批准者有二百五十五名。该校前第一、二两期南下学员已有三百零四名，连此次共有五百五十九名学员南下工作。
