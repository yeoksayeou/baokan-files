Title: Songjiang District Military Control Commission Established
Author:
Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 1

　　Songjiang District Military Control Commission Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, Wuxi, 20th] The Songjiang District Military Control Commission of the People's Liberation Army was established on the 16th. The Songjiang Local Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Songjiang Military Sub-district Command of the Southern Jiangsu Military Region, its Political Department, and the Songjiang District Administrative Commissioner's Office were also established on the same day. Songjiang District governs Songjiang City and nine counties (Songjiang, Jinshan, Qingpu, Fengxian, Nanhui, Chuansha, Shanghai, Jiading, Baoshan) and one city. The field troops of the People's Liberation Army who liberated Songjiang City strictly observed discipline, and the handover procedures were clear, so the city's order is well-maintained. Currently, the city's postal and telecommunications services are operating as usual.

### 松江区军管会成立
新华社
1949-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　松江区军管会成立
    【新华社无锡二十日电】人民解放军松江区军事管制委员会已于十六日成立。中共松江地方委员会，苏南军区松江军分区司令部、政治部及松江区行政专员公署亦于同日成立。松江区辖松江市及松江、金山、青浦、奉贤、南汇、川沙、上海、嘉定、宝山等九县一市。解放松江市的解放军野战部队严守纪律，移交手续清楚，因而该市秩序井然。现该市邮电照常畅通。
