Title: Shijiazhuang News Workers Support the Establishment of a National Organization
Author:
Date: 1949-05-17
Page: 1

　　Shijiazhuang News Workers
  　Support the Establishment of a National Organization
    [Shijiazhuang Telegraph] The journalistic circles here are extremely excited to learn that news workers in Peiping have initiated the establishment of a national organization. A telegram was sent to the All-China Association of Journalists in the Liberated Areas on May 15 to express their support.

### 石市新闻工作者　拥护成立全国性组织

1949-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　石市新闻工作者
  　拥护成立全国性组织
    【石家庄电】此间新闻界闻悉在平新闻工作者发起成立全国性的组织，极为兴奋。特于五月十五日致电中国解放区新闻记者联合会，表示拥护。
