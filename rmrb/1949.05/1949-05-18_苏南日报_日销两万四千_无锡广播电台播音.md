Title: Su Nan Daily: Daily Sales of 24,000 Copies, Wuxi Radio Station Broadcasts
Author:
Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 1

　　Su Nan Daily
  　Daily Sales of 24,000 Copies
  　Wuxi Radio Station Broadcasts
    【Xinhua News Agency, Wuxi, 17th】Since its founding in Wuxi, the Su Nan Daily has been selling over 24,000 copies daily. Newspaper vendors from other cities have come to negotiate for consignment sales. Currently, the newspaper has eleven distribution points in Changzhou, Suzhou, Yixing, Liyang, and other places. The newspaper has a reader service column to answer various questions and solve problems for readers. In addition, the Wuxi Radio Station began broadcasting a few days ago, with a wavelength of 270 meters and a frequency of 210 kilocycles.

### 苏南日报　日销两万四千　无锡广播电台播音

1949-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏南日报
  　日销两万四千
  　无锡广播电台播音
    【新华社无锡十七日电】苏南日报在无锡创刊后，已日销二万四千余份。外埠报贩纷纷前来接洽代售。现该报社常州、苏州、宜兴、溧阳等地设有十一个分销处。该报设有读者服务栏，专为读者解答各种疑难。又无锡广播电台已于日前开始播音，该台波长二七零公尺，周率二一零千周。
