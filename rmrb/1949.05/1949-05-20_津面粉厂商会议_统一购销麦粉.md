Title: Tianjin Flour Manufacturers Meeting: Unified Purchase and Sale of Wheat Flour
Author:
Date: 1949-05-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 19th. The Tianjin Municipal People's Government convened a meeting of flour manufacturers on the 17th, deciding, based on the principle of developing production and taking into consideration both public and private interests, that the Tianjin Municipal Grain Company and the Flour Industry Association would jointly form a wheat purchase and flour sales committee. This committee will, under the leadership of the government, unify the purchase of wheat and agree on flour selling prices, gradually guiding the production of private flour mills onto an organized and planned path.

### 津面粉厂商会议　统一购销麦粉

1949-05-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　津面粉厂商会议
  　统一购销麦粉
    【新华社天津十九日电】天津市人民政府于十七日召开各面粉厂商会议，决定根据发展生产、公私兼顾的方针，由津市粮食公司和面粉业公会合组购麦销粉委员会。这个委员会将在政府领导下统一采购小麦和协议面粉售价，逐使私营面粉厂的生产逐渐走上有组织有计划的道路。
