Title: North China Workers' Representative Delegation Leaves Beiping to Return
Author:
Date: 1949-05-22
Page: 2

　　The North China Workers' Representative Delegation Leaves Beiping to Return
　　[Our Paper's News] The North China Workers' Representative Delegation arrived in Beiping from Tianjin on the 15th of this month. After staying for five days, visiting scenic spots and historical sites of the ancient capital, and listening to reports from Comrades Zhu De, Bo Yibo, Dong Biwu, Nie Rongzhen, Peng Zhen, Liu Ningyi, etc., they left Beiping in batches by car starting at 11:00 AM yesterday (the 21st) to return to their work posts.
　　[Another News] The People's Railway Ministry's Beiping-Tianjin Railway Administration and the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping-Tianjin Railway Workers' General Union, taking advantage of the North China Workers' Representatives' visit to Beiping, yesterday morning (the 21st) invited railway worker representatives to hold a discussion meeting to study how to combine the specific situation after returning to transmit and implement the spirit of the North China Workers' Representative Conference. In the afternoon, there was a dinner, and in the evening, the representatives were invited to watch a movie.

### 华北职工代表参观团昨离平归去

1949-05-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北职工代表参观团昨离平归去
    【本报讯】华北职工代表参观团本月十五日由津抵平，先后逗留五日，遍访故都名胜古迹，聆听朱总司令、薄一波、董必武、聂荣臻、彭真、刘宁一等同志报告后，已于昨日（二十一日）上午十一时起陆续分批乘车离平，返回自己的工作岗位。
    【又讯】人民铁道部平津铁路管理局及平津路职工总会筹委会，乘此次华北职工代表来平之便，昨日（二十一日）上午邀请铁路职工代表开座谈会，研究回去后怎样结合具体情况，传达与贯彻华北职工代表会议精神。下午会餐，晚间招待代表看电影。
