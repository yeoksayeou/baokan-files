Title: Nanjing Resumes Postal Service with Xi'an, Wuhan, and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1949-05-29
Page: 1

　　Nanjing Resumes Postal Service with Xi'an, Wuhan, and Other Places
    [Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 27th] Postal services have been restored between Nanjing and the newly liberated cities of Xi'an, Xianyang, Jiujiang, and Wuhan.

### 南京与西安武汉等地　恢复通邮

1949-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京与西安武汉等地
  　恢复通邮
    【新华社南京二十七日电】南京市与新解放的西安、咸阳、九江及武汉等地已恢复通邮。
