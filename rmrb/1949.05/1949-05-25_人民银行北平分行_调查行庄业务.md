Title: People's Bank of Beiping Branch Investigates Bank and Native Bank Business
Author:
Date: 1949-05-25
Page: 2

　　People's Bank of Beiping Branch
  　Investigates Bank and Native Bank Business
    [This newspaper dispatch] In order to understand the business situation of various banks and native banks before handling the review of registration and capital increase of the Beiping banking industry, the Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China has formulated supplementary matters based on the "Provisional Measures for the Administration of Private Banks and Native Banks in North China" and has begun selective investigations. As of yesterday (the 24th), the following have been investigated: Banks: Datong, Zhengtai, and Juxingcheng. Native Banks: Puli, Junxinghe, Juyi, Xiangruixing, Jinhongyu, Yongtaicheng, and six others. (Yu)

### 人民银行北平分行　调查行庄业务

1949-05-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　人民银行北平分行
  　调查行庄业务
    【本报讯】中国人民银行北平分行为了在办理审核北平银钱业登记增资等事宜之前，先行了解各银行、钱庄业务情况，已根据“华北区私营银钱业管理暂行办法”，订出补充事项，开始择要调查，截至昨日（二十四）止，先后共调查有：银行：大同、正太、聚兴诚三家。钱庄：普利、峻兴和、聚义、祥瑞兴、晋鸿裕、永泰成等六家。（宇）
