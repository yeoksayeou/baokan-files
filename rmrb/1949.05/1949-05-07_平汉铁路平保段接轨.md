Title: Joining of the Pinghan Railway Pingbao Section
Author: Lu Dao
Date: 1949-05-07
Page: 2

　　Pinghan Railway
  　Joining of the Pingbao Section
    [This newspaper dispatch] The first and second engineering teams, actively rushing to repair the Pingbao section of the railway, successfully joined the tracks at 9:00 AM on May 3rd after three months of effort. The opening of the Pingbao line, which the people of Ping and Bao have been eagerly awaiting, can now be realized.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Lu Dao)

### 平汉铁路平保段接轨
鲁岛
1949-05-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　平汉铁路
  　平保段接轨
    【本报讯】积极抢修平保段铁路的第一、二两工程队，经过三个月的努力，已于五月三日上午九时胜利接轨。平保两地人民所急盼的平保通车即可实现。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（鲁岛）
