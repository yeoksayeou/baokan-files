Title: Polish Peace Committee Resolution: Establish Peace Defense Committees Everywhere, Mobilize the People to Strengthen the Struggle
Author:
Date: 1949-05-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 27th: Warsaw dispatch: The Polish Peace Committee held a meeting on the 24th, and heard a report on the World Congress of Supporters of Peace in Paris and Prague. The meeting passed a resolution stating that the powerful peace camp is now an invincible force capable of guaranteeing peace for progress and human welfare. The participants were unshakably convinced that striving for the further development of our country's industrial and agricultural production, the development of our science and culture, the enhancement of friendship between Poland and the Soviet Union and other People's Democratic countries, and the strengthening of our national defense forces--all this is strengthening the People's Poland, and our ability to make new contributions to the struggle of all countries for progress and lasting peace. The meeting suggested that the delegates, together with social organizations, should arrange meetings in various places to convey the situation of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace. At these meetings, provincial, municipal, district and village committees of peace defenders will be established; these committees will mobilize the laboring masses, people of all strata, believers and non-believers, forces and enthusiasm of all parties and non-party people, to fight for peace and to strengthen the People's Poland as an important factor for world peace.

### 波兰和平委会决议　普设保卫和平委会　动员人民加强斗争

1949-05-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　波兰和平委会决议
  　普设保卫和平委会
  　动员人民加强斗争
    【新华社北平二十七日电】华沙讯：波兰和平委员会于二十四日举行会议，听取了关于巴黎与布拉格世界拥护和平大会的报告。会议通过决议称为强大的和平阵营现在是一支能够为进步与人类福利而保证和平的不可征服的力量。与会者毫不动摇地深信：争取我国工业与农业生产的进一步发展，我国科学与文化的发展，波兰与苏联及其他人民民主国家的友谊的增进，和我国国防力量的加强--这一切就是加强人民的波兰，以及我们对各国争取进步与持久和平之斗争能有新贡献的因素。会议建议代表团各代表，会同社会团体，在各地布置传达关于世界拥护和平大会情况的会议。在这些会议上，将成立省、市、区与乡村的和平保卫者委员会；这种委员会将动员劳动群众、各阶层人民、教徒与非教徒、各党派与无党无派的力量与热情，为和平、为加强作为世界和平的重要因素的人民的波兰而斗争。
