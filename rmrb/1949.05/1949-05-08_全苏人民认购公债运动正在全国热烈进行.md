Title: The Nationwide Mass Movement to Subscribe to National Bonds is in Full Swing

Author: 
Date: 1949-05-08
Page: 3

    Nationwide Mass Movement to Subscribe to National Bonds is in Full Swing
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th] TASS reports: Soviet Minister of Finance Zverev announced on the 5th that the fourth national bond for the restoration and development of the national economy, issued on May 3rd with a value of 20 billion rubles, had been subscribed for 21.691 billion rubles by the evening of May 4th, exceeding the issuance amount by 1.691 billion rubles. In Moscow alone, the subscription amount reached 2.0071 billion rubles. The Moscow region subscribed for 700 million rubles, and the subscription amount of collective farmers in the region doubled compared to last year. The movement to subscribe to national bonds is in full swing across the country. The entire Soviet people believe that subscribing to the new national bonds is fulfilling their sacred patriotic duty. A True Daily editorial pointed out: The daily life of the Soviet state vividly proves the ever-increasing spiritual and political unity of Soviet socialism, the unbreakable friendship of all nationalities of the Soviet Union, and ardent Soviet patriotism. The attitude of the Soviet people in subscribing to the new national bonds once again proves this point.

### 全苏人民认购公债运动正在全国热烈进行

1949-05-08
第3版()
专栏：

    全苏人民认购公债运动
    正在全国热烈进行
    【新华社北平七日电】塔斯社讯：苏联财政部部长兹维列夫五日发表公报指出。五月三日发行的二百亿卢布的复兴与发展国民经济的第四次公债，截至五月四日晚间止已被认购二百一十六亿九千一百万卢布，超过公债发行额一十六亿九千一百万卢布。仅莫斯科市一地，认购额即达二十亿零七千一百万卢布。莫斯科州认购七亿卢布，该州集体农民认购额超过去年一倍。认购公债运动正在全国各地热烈进行中。全苏联人民都认为认购新公债是履行自己爱国的神圣天职。真理报社论指出：苏维埃国家每天的生活都足以生动地证明日益增强的苏维埃社会主义的精神与政治的团结，苏联各族人民牢不可破的友谊和热烈的苏维埃爱国主义。苏联人民认购新公债的态度，再次证明了这一点。
