Title: Wallace Begins Peace Speech Tour
Author:
Date: 1949-05-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 30th— TASS, New York: On April 27, the same day the U.S. Senate Foreign Relations Committee began hearing evidence for the North Atlantic Treaty, U.S. Progressive Party leader Henry A. Wallace also began a peace speech tour of fifteen major American cities. That evening, Wallace hosted a banquet in New York where Italian Socialist Senator Giglia and British Labour Party MP Hutchinson both delivered impromptu speeches denouncing the North Atlantic Treaty and the Marshall Plan as tools of war, and calling on the people of the world to actively promote the movement to maintain peace and expose warmongers. Giglia declared that the Italian Socialist Party and the Soviet people were in complete agreement, and said that the Italian people opposed the Gasperi government because the latter supported the North Atlantic Treaty. Hutchinson declared that the Marshall Plan allowed the United States to dominate the economies of other countries, especially Britain. Hutchinson called on the people to "shout loudly to protest the evil madness that is now driving us toward war."

### 华莱士开始和平演讲旅行

1949-05-03
第3版()
专栏：

    华莱士开始和平演讲旅行
    【新华社北平三十日电】塔斯社纽约讯：就在四月二十七日，美国参院外委会开始听取北大西洋公约举证的一天，美国进步党领袖华莱士也开始了十五个美国主要城市的和平演讲旅行。当晚，华莱士在纽约设宴，意大利社会党参议员古亚及英国工党议员赫金生均即席发表演说，斥北大西洋公约及马歇尔计划为战争工具，并号召全世界人民积极推进保持和平与揭发战争贩子的运动。古亚宣称：意大利社会党和苏联人民完全一致，他说：意大利人民反对加斯贝利政府，因为后者支持北大西洋公约。赫金生宣称：马歇尔计划使美国得以统治其他国家特别是英国的经济。赫金生号召人民“大声疾呼，抗议现在驱使我们走向战争的罪恶的疯狂行为”。
