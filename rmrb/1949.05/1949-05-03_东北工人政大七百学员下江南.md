Title: Seven Hundred Students from Northeast Workers' Political University Go South
Author:
Date: 1949-05-03
Page: 3

    Northeast Workers' Political University
    Seven Hundred Students Go South to Jiangnan
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 1st] The first class of 749 students from Northeast Workers' Political University graduated on the 28th of last month, and set out on the 29th to enter the pass and participate in work in the Jiangnan area. Before their departure, they received a warm send-off from the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, the Northeast Labor Bureau, and other units.

### 东北工人政大七百学员下江南

1949-05-03
第3版()
专栏：

    东北工人政大
    七百学员下江南
    【新华社沈阳一日电】东北工人政治大学第一期七百四十九名学员，已于上月二十八日毕业，并于二十九日首途入关，参加江南地区的工作。行前备受东北职工总会、东北劳动总局等单位的热烈欢送。
