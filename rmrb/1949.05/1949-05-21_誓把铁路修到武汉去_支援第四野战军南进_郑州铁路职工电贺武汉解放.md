Title: Vow to Extend the Railway to Wuhan to Support the Fourth Field Army's Advance Southward; Zhengzhou Railway Workers Send Telegram to Congratulate the Liberation of Wuhan
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 19th – People from all walks of life in Beijing and Zhengzhou celebrate the liberation of the three towns of Wuhan. The Party Committee of the Zhengzhou Railway Administration Bureau, the Zhengzhou Railway Administration Bureau, and the Preparatory Committee of the Zhengzhou Railway Workers' Union jointly sent a telegram to the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the workers of Wuhan to offer congratulations. The telegram to the Fourth Field Army stated: "Our railway workers vow to make the greatest effort to quickly repair the railway, drive locomotives to Wuhan, and support the army's southward advance to liberate all of China." The telegram to the workers of Wuhan stated: "The workers of Wuhan have a glorious history of struggle. During the 'February 7th' Great Strike of 1923, the General Labor Union of the Beijing-Hankou Railway moved from Zhengzhou to Jiang'an in Hankou, and waged a heroic struggle to fight for freedom, democracy, human rights, and against imperialism and its running dog warlords. This time, when the Kuomintang bandit troops fled Wuhan, you also made great efforts to protect a large number of valuable materials for the people. We extend our cordial greetings to you." The Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Democratic Youth Federation, the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and the Beijing Municipal Student Union also jointly sent a telegram to the youth of Nanjing and Wuhan to offer congratulations. The telegram pointed out: "The youth of the South and the North are comrades-in-arms against American imperialism and the reactionary rule of the Kuomintang. In the future, we will be partners in building a new democratic China together. We hope to strengthen contact in the future, intensify learning, work hard in production, and support the People's Liberation War to liberate all of China."

### 誓把铁路修到武汉去  支援第四野战军南进　郑州铁路职工电贺武汉解放

1949-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　誓把铁路修到武汉去
    支援第四野战军南进
  　郑州铁路职工电贺武汉解放
    【新华社北平十九日电】北平、郑州各界人民庆祝武汉三镇的解放。中共郑州区铁路管理局党委会、郑州区铁路管理局与郑州铁路工会筹委会并联名致电中国人民解放军第四野战军与武汉职工祝贺。在致第四野战军电中称：本路员工誓以最大努力，迅速把铁路修好，把机车开到武汉，支援大军南下解放全中国。在致武汉各业职工电中称：武汉工人有着光辉的斗争历史。在一九二三年的“二七”大罢工中，京汉铁路总工会曾从郑州移到汉口的江岸，为争取自由，争取民主，争取人权，反对帝国主义及其走狗军阀作了英勇的斗争。这次在国民党匪军逃离武汉时，你们又尽力为人民保护了大批宝贵的资材。我们特向你们致以亲切的慰问。北平市民主青年联合会筹委会、中国新民主主义青年团北平市筹委会和北平市学生联合会三团体亦联名致电南京、武汉的青年祝贺。电中指出：南方和北方的青年，是反对美国帝国主义和国民党反动统治的战友，今后更是一起建设新民主主义中国的伙伴。希望以后加强联系，加紧学习，努力生产，支援解放全中国的人民解放战争。
