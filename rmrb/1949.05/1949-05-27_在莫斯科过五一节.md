Title: Celebrating May Day in Moscow
Author: Guo Mou
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 4

Celebrating May Day in Moscow
　　Guo Mou
　　(I)
　　The human tide rolls with sonorous sound, everyone celebrates May Day with frenzy.
　　Red flags flutter in the wind without end, Red Square is today an ocean of fire.

　　(II)

　　Flying faster than the speed of sound, formations of divine eagles sweep over the capital.
　　Solemnly passing over Lenin's Mausoleum, thunderous roaring follows after.

　　(III)

　　Legendary and beautifully told is Katyusha, which once made the old lion sigh his life away.
　　Don't say that your heart is truly of iron, thousands of rockets shine brighter than flowers.
　　The Soviet rocket launcher named "Katyusha" is named after the heroine of Tolstoy's novel "Resurrection." Tolstoy's name Leo means lion, hence the reference to "old lion."

　　(IV)

　　Salutes boom and flower bombs are many, searchlights crisscross in the sky, weaving a net.
　　The square is like a sea of people all night, but all you see are old women dancing together.

### 在莫斯科过五一节
郭某
1949-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　在莫斯科过五一节
    郭某
　　（一）
    人涛滚滚声琅琅，五一腾欢众若狂。
    赤旆迎风行不断，红场今日火之洋。
　
　　（二）
　
    飞行快度速于声，成阵神鹰掠上京。
    穆穆列宁陵顶过，雷霆落后见轰鸣。
　
　　（三）
　
    传奇艳说卡秋霞，曾使狮翁没世嗟。
    漫道侬心真是铁，万千火箭灿于花。
    苏联火箭炮有名“卡秋霞”者，乃托尔斯太小说“复活”之女主人翁以命名。托尔斯太名Ｌｅｏ乃狮子之意，故此以“狮翁”称之。
　　（四）
　
    礼炮轰传花弹多，横空探照织成罗。
    广场竟夕人如海，但见婆婆对舞过。
