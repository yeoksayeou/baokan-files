Title: Pingjin Railway Bureau's Beiping Locomotive Depot to Repair "July 1st" Locomotive
Author:
Date: 1949-05-22
Page: 2

　　Pingjin Railway Bureau's Beiping Locomotive Depot
　　Will Repair "July 1st" Locomotive
　　[Report from this newspaper] Following the successful repair of the "May 1st Labor" locomotive by the Pingjin Railway Bureau's Beiping Locomotive Depot and Beiping Car Shop, in addition to continuing the repair of the "May 4th Youth" locomotive, in order to celebrate the Chinese Communist Party's "July 1st" birthday, they are also preparing to repair the "July 1st" locomotive as a gift for "July 1st."

### 平津铁路局北平机务段　将修理“七一”号车

1949-05-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　平津铁路局北平机务段
  　将修理“七一”号车
    【本报讯】平津铁路局北平机务段、北平车房继“五一”劳动号修理成功以后，除继续修理“五四”青年号外，为庆祝中国共产党“七一”生日，又准备修理“七一”号，作为“七一”的献礼。
