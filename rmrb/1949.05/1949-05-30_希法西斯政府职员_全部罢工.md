Title: Fascist Government Employees in Greece All Go On Strike
Author:
Date: 1949-05-30
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 27th: According to a TASS dispatch from Athens: A nationwide strike has begun among employees of agencies controlled by the Greek Royalist Fascist government—radio stations, hospitals, commercial and industrial agencies. The strikers are demanding increased wages.

### 希法西斯政府职员　全部罢工

1949-05-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　希法西斯政府职员
  　全部罢工
    【新华社北平二十七日电】据塔斯社雅典讯：希腊保皇法西斯政府所控制的机关--无线电台、医院、商务和工业等机关--职员的全国性罢工已经开始。罢工者要求增加工资。
