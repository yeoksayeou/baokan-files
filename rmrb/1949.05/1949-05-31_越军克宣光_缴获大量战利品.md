Title: Viet Minh Capture Tuyen Quang, Seize Large Amount of Spoils

Author:

Date: 1949-05-31
Page: 3

　　Viet Minh Capture Tuyen Quang
  　Seize Large Amount of Spoils
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 30th] A broadcast from Vietnamese radio stated that the army of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam recently captured the important city of Tuyen Quang, 100 kilometers northwest of Hanoi, and seized a large amount of spoils. The Viet Minh forces are currently pursuing the French troops retreating from Tuyen Quang.
    According to Agence France-Presse: Viet Minh forces destroyed the railway between Hanoi and Haiphong and the railway near Saigon on the 27th, overturning several enemy train cars and killing or wounding dozens of enemy soldiers.

### 越军克宣光　缴获大量战利品

1949-05-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　越军克宣光
  　缴获大量战利品
    【新华社北平三十日电】越南电台广播：越南民主共和国军队已于日前攻克河内西北一百公里的重要城市宣光城，缴获大量的战利品。越军刻正追逐自宣光退却的法军。
    又据法新社讯：越军曾于二十七日破坏河内海防间的铁路和西贡附近的铁路，倾翻敌火车厢多辆，毙伤敌数十名。
