Title: Pukou to Bengbu to Open to Traffic; Nanjing Zhonghuamen to Xiaguan Small Train Already Open
Author:
Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 1
　　[Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Nanjing, 16th Dispatch: The Pukou to Bengbu section of the Jinpu Railway, thanks to the active repairs by all workers, the project scheduled for twenty days has been completed five days ahead of schedule on the 15th. The trial run on the morning of the 15th went well. After the Pukou to Bengbu section of the Jinpu Railway opens to traffic, coal from the Huainan Coal Mine can be supplied to Nanjing in a steady stream.
　　[Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Nanjing, 16th Dispatch: The small train from Xiaguan to Zhonghuamen, with a total length of fifteen kilometers, has been restored to service through the active efforts of the Nanjing Railway Administration. It now runs six times a day, with a one-way ticket price of 15 yuan RMB per person.

### 浦口到蚌埠将通车　南京中华门下关小火车已通

1949-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　浦口到蚌埠将通车
  　南京中华门下关小火车已通
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京十六号消息：津浦铁路浦口到蚌埠段，由于全体工人积极抢修，预定二十天的工程已经提前五天在十五号完成。十五号早晨试车情形良好。津浦路浦口到蚌埠段通车以后，淮南煤矿的煤，就可以源源供应南京使用。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京十六号消息：全长十五公里由下关到中华门的小火车，经南京铁路管理处积极恢复，现在已经通车，每天来往六次，单程票价每人人民币十五元。
