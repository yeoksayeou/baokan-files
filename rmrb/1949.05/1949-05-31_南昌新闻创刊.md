Title: Inauguration of the Nanchang News

Author:

Date: 1949-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 30th. The Nanchang News was inaugurated in Nanchang on the 25th. The Nanchang Broadcasting Station also began broadcasting on the same day.

### 南昌新闻创刊

1949-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　南昌新闻创刊
    【新华社南昌三十日电】南昌新闻已于二十五日在南昌创刊。南昌广播电台亦自同日起开始播音。
