Title: Ural Steel Production Reaches Next Year's Target
Author:
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 3

    Ural Steel Production
    Reaches Next Year's Target
    (Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 11th) TASS, Sverdlovsk: Ural's metalworking enterprises in the Soviet Union have reached the targets set for 1950 in the production of pig iron and steel. This year, the Ural factories have utilized a number of technological reforms, making it possible to greatly increase the production of metal products, greatly reduce fuel consumption, and extend the use of Martin tubes and blast furnaces by one-third compared to last year. Compared to last year, Ural steelworkers produced 600 kilograms of steel from each square meter of furnace surface, and blast furnace workers produced 200 kilograms more of pig iron.

### 乌拉尔钢铁生产  达到明年目标

1949-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    乌拉尔钢铁生产
    达到明年目标
    【新华社北平十一日电】塔斯社斯维德洛夫斯克讯：苏联乌拉尔的五金企业在铣铁和钢的产量上已达到预定为一九五○年的目标。今年乌拉尔工厂利用了若干技术上的改革，从而有可能使五金产量大大增加，燃料的消耗费大为减少，马中管和鼓风炉的使用较去年延长三分之一。与去年相较，乌拉尔炼钢工人从每一平方公尺的炉面生产了六百公斤的钢，鼓风炉的工人多生产了两百公斤铣铁。
