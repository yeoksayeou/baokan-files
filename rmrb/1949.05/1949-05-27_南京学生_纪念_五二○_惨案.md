Title: Nanjing Students Commemorate the "May 20th" Massacre
Author:
Date: 1949-05-27
Page: 1

　　Nanjing Students
  　Commemorate the "May 20th" Massacre
    [Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 25th] May 20th marked the second anniversary of the "May 20th" massacre in which the Kuomintang bandit gang slaughtered Nanjing students. The Nanjing Student Union held a Nanjing Student Movement Historical Materials Exhibition and a Pictorial Exhibition at the Central University's College of Engineering building on that day to commemorate the event. On the first day, the audience reached 2,000 people. They expressed infinite admiration for the heroic struggle of the students.

### 南京学生　纪念“五二○”惨案

1949-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京学生
  　纪念“五二○”惨案
    【新华社南京二十五日电】五月二十日为国民党匪帮屠杀南京学生的“五·二○”惨案两周年，南京市学生联合会特于是日假中央大学工学院大楼举行南京学生运动史料展览会和画刊展览会，以示纪念。观众第一天即达两千人。他们对学生们英勇的斗争表示无限敬佩。
