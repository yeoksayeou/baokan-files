Title: Beiping Telegrams Reach Jiangnan; Beihai and Zhongshan Park Telegraph Booths Resume Operations
Author:
Date: 1949-05-10
Page: 1

    Beiping Telegrams Reach Jiangnan
    Beihai and Zhongshan Park Telegraph Booths Resume Operations
    [This Newspaper Reports] After the liberation of various locations in Jiangnan, external telecommunications have been rapidly restored. At present, telegram services between Beiping and the following twenty-plus cities are now open: Wuhu, Fanchang, Xuancheng, Ningguo, Nanling, Wuxi, Changzhou, Jiangyin, Suzhou, Zhenjiang, Wujiang, Changshu, Hushu Pass, Jurong, Ma'anshan, Dangtu, Pukou, Tangshan, Xuzhou, Bengbu, Hangzhou, Wuxing, Tonglu, Fuyang, Shexian, Liyang, Zhuji and others. Furthermore, the Beiping Telecommunications Bureau, to facilitate the public, will resume operations at the telegraph booths in Zhongshan Park and Beihai Park from the 9th of this month. The hours of operation will be from 1:00 PM to 8:00 PM daily, handling telegram sending and receiving, as well as retail sales of local and long-distance telephone services. Visitors from all walks of life are encouraged to utilize these convenient services nearby.

### 北平电报畅通江南  北海中山公园电亭恢复营业

1949-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    北平电报畅通江南
    北海中山公园电亭恢复营业
    【本报讯】江南各地解放后，对外电讯多已迅速恢复。现在芜湖、繁昌、宣城、宁国、南陵、无锡、常州、江阴、苏州、镇江、吴江、常熟、浒墅关、句容、马鞍山、当涂、浦口、汤山、徐州、蚌埠、杭州、吴兴、桐庐、富阳、歙县、溧阳、诸暨等二十余城市与平市间电报均已畅通。又：北平电信局为便利公众，自本月九日起将中山公园、北海公园两处电亭恢复营业，时间为每日下午一时至八时，办理收发电报及零售市内及长途电话业务，希各界游人注意就近利用。
