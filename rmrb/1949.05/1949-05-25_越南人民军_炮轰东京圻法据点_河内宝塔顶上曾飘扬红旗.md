Title: Vietnam People's Army Shells French Strongholds in Tonkin; Red Flag Once Flown Atop Hanoi Pagoda
Author:
Date: 1949-05-25
Page: 3

　　Vietnam People's Army
  　Shells French Strongholds in Tonkin
  　Red Flag Once Flown Atop Hanoi Pagoda
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 24th] According to a France-Presse dispatch from Hanoi: The army of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is exerting considerable pressure on the northwestern region of Tonkin. Two French military strongholds there have been shelled by the army of the Republic.

    Another dispatch: On May 19th, coinciding with the 58th birthday of Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the red flag of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam appeared on the top of a famous pagoda in the city center of Hanoi, which is controlled by the French army. Underground guerrilla activities in various cities have greatly increased.

### 越南人民军　炮轰东京圻法据点　河内宝塔顶上曾飘扬红旗

1949-05-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　越南人民军
  　炮轰东京圻法据点
  　河内宝塔顶上曾飘扬红旗
    【新华社北平二十四日电】据法新社河内讯：越南民主共和国军队正在对东京圻西北部地区施以相当强大的压力，该处法军的两个据点已受到共和国军队的炮轰。
    又讯：本月十九日，正值越南民主共和国总统胡志明五十八岁寿辰之际，法军控制的河内市市中心区一个著名宝塔的塔顶上，曾出现了越南民主共和国的红旗。各城市内的地下游击队活动大为增加。
