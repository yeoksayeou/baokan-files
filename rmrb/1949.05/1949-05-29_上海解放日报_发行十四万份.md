Title: Shanghai Jiefang Daily Issues 140,000 Copies
Author:
Date: 1949-05-29
Page: 1

　　Shanghai Jiefang Daily
  　Issues 140,000 Copies
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 28th. The Shanghai people's newspaper, Jiefang Daily, was launched today and has been welcomed by the citizens. The newspaper is two broadsheets, and today's circulation reached 140,000 copies. The East China General Branch of Xinhua News Agency entered the city to work yesterday (27th), and the Shanghai Branch of Xinhua News Agency was also established yesterday and has begun to issue news稿.

### 上海解放日报　发行十四万份

1949-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海解放日报
  　发行十四万份
    【新华社上海二十八日电】上海人民的报纸解放日报已于今日创刊，受到市民的欢迎。该报对开两大张，今日发行达十四万份。新华社华东总分社于昨日（二十七日）进入市内办公，新华社上海分社亦于昨日成立，并已开始发稿。
