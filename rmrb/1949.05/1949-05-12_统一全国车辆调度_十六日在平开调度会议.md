Title: Unified National Vehicle Dispatch: Scheduling Conference to be Held in Beiping on the 16th
Author:
Date: 1949-05-12
Page: 1

    Unified National Vehicle Dispatch
    Scheduling Conference to be Held in Beiping on the 16th
    [Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Broadcast] Beiping, 11th Dispatch: The Railway Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, in order to unify national vehicle dispatch and strengthen connections between regions, will hold a national scheduling work conference in Beiping on the 16th of this month, including representatives from the Northeast, North China, and East China. The aim is to unify national vehicle dispatch work, establish a unified reporting system, and strengthen the connection between various bureaus, the Railway Department, the Transportation Bureau, and the dispatching stations of each section between bureaus.

### 统一全国车辆调度  十六日在平开调度会议

1949-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    统一全国车辆调度
    十六日在平开调度会议
    【北平新华广播电台广播】北平十一号消息：中国人民革命军事委员会铁道部，召开全国车辆调度会议，该部为了统一全国车辆调度，加强各地联系，定于本月十六日在北平召开包括东北、华北、华东的全国调度工作会议，统一全国车辆调度工作，建立报表统一制度，加强各管理局、铁道部、运输局和局与局之间的各段站调度所的联系。
