Title: Datong Daily Begins Publication on the Fifth

Author:

Date: 1949-05-18
Page: 1

[Chahar News] The Datong Daily began publication on the fifth day after the peaceful liberation of Datong—the 5th of this month. It is an octavo-sized newspaper printed in lead type, with two pages. The Datong Branch of the New China News Agency was also established at the same time.

### 大同日报于五日创刊

1949-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　大同日报于五日创刊
    【察哈尔讯】大同日报于大同和平解放第五日——本月五日创刊。为八开铅印两版。新华社大同支社亦同时成立。
