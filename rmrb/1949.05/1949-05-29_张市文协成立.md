Title: Zhangjiakou City Literary Association Established
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-29
Page: 1

　　Zhangjiakou City Literary Association Established
    [Zhangjiakou Electric] The Zhangjiakou City Literary and Art Workers Association was formally established on the 22nd of this month. Tian Jian, Shen Zhong, and 13 others were elected as official committee members, and a telegram congratulating the convocation of the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers was approved.

### 张市文协成立

1949-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　张市文协成立
    【张家口电】张家口市文艺工作者协会于本月二十二日正式成立。选出田间、沈重等十五人为正式委员，并通过祝贺召开全国文学艺术工作者代表大会的电文。
