Title: Large Quantities of Grain Soon to Arrive in Beiping
Author: Yu
Date: 1949-05-04
Page: 2

　　Large Quantities of Grain Soon to Arrive in Beiping

　　[Report from this newspaper] The North China Trading Company has purchased and transported large quantities of grain from various regions to regulate the grain supply in Beiping, Tianjin, and other areas. In addition to the large quantity of grain purchased in the Northeast, the first batch of 40 million jin has already been shipped from Shenyang and other places. The company instructed the Suiyuan-Mongolia branch to allocate 10 million jin of miscellaneous grains to Beiping, which are now being concentrated in Jining, Fengzhen, and other places, and will be transported to Beiping as soon as transportation is restored. In addition, 15 million jin of food grain that should be allocated to Beiping from Chahar Province in May has already arrived in Beiping in twenty train cars. Transportation is currently underway in succession.
　　　　　(Yu)

### 大批粮食即将抵平
宇
1949-05-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　大批粮食即将抵平
    【本报讯】华北贸易总公司为调剂平津等地粮食，已在各地购运大批粮食。除在东北购得大批粮食，首批四千万斤，已由沈阳等地启运。该公司指令绥蒙分公司拨借北平之杂粮一千万斤，现已在集宁丰镇等地集中，俟交通恢复，即可运平。此外，察哈尔省五月份应拨平之食粮一千五百万斤，已有二十个车皮的粮食抵平。现正陆续运输中。　　　　
　　　　　（宇）
