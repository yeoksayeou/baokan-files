Title: British Imperialist Artillery Arrives in the Netherlands and Belgium, Inciting Protests from Working People; Demonstrators Shout "British Troops Get Out!"
Author:
Date: 1949-05-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th: TASS, The Hague: According to the Western European Union military treaty, 135 British artillery personnel arrived in Deventer, Netherlands on the 9th, carrying anti-aircraft guns. This incident aroused widespread protests from the working people of Deventer. On the same day, young people discussed on the streets and shouted slogans such as "No foreign troops on our territory!" "Down with the North Atlantic Pact!" "British troops get out!" They distributed leaflets calling on the people of the city to resolutely protest the North Atlantic Pact and oppose the American and British warmongers who want to turn the Netherlands into a bridgehead.

According to Agence France-Presse: In accordance with the Western European Union military treaty, a British heavy anti-aircraft artillery regiment stationed in Belgium arrived in Brussels on the 11th.

### 英帝炮兵抵达荷比两国  激起劳动人民抗议  示威者高喊英军滚出去

1949-05-14
第3版()
专栏：

    英帝炮兵抵达荷比两国
    激起劳动人民抗议
    示威者高喊英军滚出去
    【新华社北平十三日电】塔斯社海牙讯：根据西欧联盟军事条约，英国炮兵人员一百三十五人已于九日携带高射炮到达荷兰德文特。这件事激起了德文特劳动人民广泛的抗议。青年们当天就在街上讨论高喊“不要外国军队驻在我们领土上！”“打倒北大西洋公约！”“英国军队滚出去！”的口号。他们散发传单，号召该城人民坚决抗议北大西洋公约，反对想把荷兰变成桥头阵地的美英战争贩子。
    又据法新社消息：按照西欧联盟军事条约规定，派驻比利时的英国重防空炮兵团，已于十一日抵布鲁塞尔。
