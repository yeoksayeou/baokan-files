Title: Discussing the Training Work of Second-Line Corps: Northeast Holds Military Training Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-05-28
Page: 3

　　Discussing the Training Work of Second-Line Corps
  　Northeast Holds Military Training Conference
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 27th] The Northeast Military Region of the People's Liberation Army held a military training conference from the 21st to the 28th of last month to discuss the training work of the second-line regular army corps. Those present included responsible persons from various divisions and provincial military regions. At the meeting, Wu Xiuquan, Chief of Staff of the Northeast Military Region, and Zhou Huan, Deputy Director of the Political Department, gave specific instructions on military training and political education, and adopted the Northeast Military Region's half-year training plan and political work plan. Issues related to training logistics, equipment, and training materials were also appropriately resolved. Finally, Gao Gang, Political Commissar of the Northeast Military Region, spoke, pointing out that the future training mission is to train the troops in the Northeast into cadres, and to train every soldier in the second-line corps into a fully conscious revolutionary fighter wielding weapons, laying the initial foundation for the modernization of the troops. All units must truly learn modern technology, master technology, and improve their political awareness and cultural level.

### 讨论第二线兵团练兵工作　东北开练兵会议

1949-05-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　讨论第二线兵团练兵工作
  　东北开练兵会议
    【新华社沈阳二十七日电】人民解放军东北军区，在上月二十一到二十八日，召开全军区的练兵会议，讨论第二线正规兵团军的练兵工作。出席的有各师和各省军区的负责人。会上，东北军区伍修权参谋长，和政治部周桓副主任对于军事练兵和政治教育等问题，作了具体的指示，并通过了东北军区的半年练兵计划和政治工作计划。有关练兵的给养、装备和练兵器材等问题，也得到了适当解决。最后，东北军区高岗政委讲话，他指出今后的练兵任务，是要把东北的部队练成干部，把第二线兵团每个战士，都练成手执武器的充分自觉的革命战士，打下部队现代化的初步基础，各部队必须确实学会现代技术，掌握技术，提高政治觉悟和文化程度。
