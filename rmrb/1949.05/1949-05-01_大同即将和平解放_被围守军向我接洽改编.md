Title: Datong is About to be Peacefully Liberated; Besieged Garrison Contacts Us for Reorganization
Author:
Date: 1949-05-01
Page: 1

    Datong is About to be Peacefully Liberated
    Besieged Garrison Contacts Us for Reorganization
    [Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station, Taiyuan, 30th dispatch] Datong, the important military town in northern Shanxi, which has been besieged for half a year, will soon be peacefully liberated. The Kuomintang garrison in Datong has recently contacted the People's Liberation Army surrounding the city to discuss surrendering their weapons, handing over the defense areas, and awaiting reorganization.

### 大同即将和平解放  被围守军向我接洽改编

1949-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    大同即将和平解放
    被围守军向我接洽改编
    【北平新华广播电台太原三十日消息】围半年的山西北被部军事重镇大同日内即将和平解放，大同国民党守军已于日前向包围该城的人民解放军接洽放下武器移交防地，听候改编等问题。
