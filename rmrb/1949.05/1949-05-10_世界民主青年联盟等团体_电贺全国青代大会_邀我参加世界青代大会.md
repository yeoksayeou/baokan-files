Title: World Democratic Youth League and Other Organizations Send Congratulatory Telegrams to the National Youth Congress, Invite China to Participate in the World Youth Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 8th: The All-China Youth First Congress received congratulatory telegrams from youth organizations such as the World Federation of Democratic Youth, the Romanian Labor Youth League, and the North Korean Democratic Youth League. The telegrams are as follows:

I. Telegram from the World Federation of Democratic Youth, sent from Paris on March 9, 1949, to the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Youth Congress. Dear friends:

The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth extends our sincere congratulations to the All-China Youth Congress to be held on May 4, 1949.

The heroic struggle of Chinese youth is a beacon for peace-loving youth all over the world

The struggle of Chinese youth in the ranks of their heroic people is a beacon for all peace-loving youth in the world. Through their victory over American imperialism—the main pillar of Kuomintang rule—Chinese youth have convinced millions of young men and women that no imperialism is powerful enough to resist the people. Through their victorious advance, liberating their vast country from the stranglehold of feudal and bureaucratic capitalist bloodsuckers, Chinese youth have proven that youth can and will win a better, happier future through struggle; at that time, youth will no longer be bound by anything. The struggle of Chinese youth has greatly strengthened the common struggle of youth worldwide for lasting peace, for the national independence of all peoples, and for a better life.

We hope that these important experiences of Chinese youth can make appropriate contributions at the World Youth Congress to be held in Budapest between August and September this year. Therefore, while wishing your congress the greatest success, we once again extend a brotherly invitation to Chinese youth, asking you to send a large number of representatives to participate in the Youth Festival and the World Youth Congress. This World Youth Festival and World Youth Congress will be a great event in the history of the world youth movement dedicated to the cause of defending peace, striving for the victory of people's liberation, and striving for the greatest future of youth.

Your sincere friend,
President of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Boisson (signed)

II. Telegram from the Romanian Labor Youth League

Celebrating your New Democratic Youth Congress, I, on behalf of the Labor Youth of the Romanian People's Republic, extend warm fraternal greetings to you!

The victory of the Chinese people has dealt a fatal blow to imperialism

Our youth admire your heroic struggle against Chiang Kai-shek's reactionary army, supported by American imperialism. The success of the Chinese revolutionary army in liberating vast areas of China and establishing a democratic system in these areas has dealt a fatal blow to imperialism and provided strong support to the democratic camp led by the Soviet Union. We believe that your congress will arouse a broad new upsurge in the Chinese democratic youth movement. On the road to new victories, your new democratic motherland will be more united and more prosperous.

General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Labor Youth League, Ding Froliesgu

III. Telegram from the North Korean Democratic Youth League

To all representatives of the All-China Youth Congress in Beiping:

Long live the friendly unity of Korean and Chinese youth for peace and liberation!

On the occasion of the opening of the All-China Youth Congress, the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League extends fraternal congratulations and respect to all representatives and all Chinese youth. We Korean youth are watching the hundreds of millions of youth in China, which has extremely close economic and cultural ties with our motherland, and their heroic struggle under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party for the complete liberation and democratic development of China. In this struggle, the Chinese people and youth have liberated Nanjing, which has been the base of the Kuomintang reactionary regime for twenty years. This brilliant victory and the great achievements won have been warmly hailed by the people struggling for peace and security in the Far East. Your congress opens on the anniversary of the "May Fourth" Movement, which has profound historical significance in the Chinese people's liberation movement. We believe that this congress will further consolidate the unity of Chinese youth and will be an expression of a complete victory in the Chinese people's liberation struggle and a huge contribution to the cause of world peace. Long live the friendly unity of Korean and Chinese youth for peace and liberation!

Xuan Zhengmin, Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League

### 世界民主青年联盟等团体  电贺全国青代大会  邀我参加世界青代大会

1949-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    世界民主青年联盟等团体
    电贺全国青代大会
    邀我参加世界青代大会
    【新华社北平八日电】中华全国青年第一次代表大会收到了世界民主青年联盟、罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟、北朝鲜民主青年同盟等青年团体的贺电，电文如下：
    一、世界民主青年联盟贺电一九四九年三月九日自巴黎发，致中华全国青年代表大会筹备委员会。亲爱的朋友们：
    世界民主青年联盟秘书处，谨请你们向将在一九四九年五月四日召开的中华全国青年代表大会转致我们祝贺之忱。
  中国青年的英勇斗争是全世界爱好和平青年的灯塔
    中国青年在他们英勇的人民的行列中的斗争，是全世界所有爱好和平的青年的灯塔。由于他们对美帝国主义——国民党统治的主要支柱的胜利，中国青年已经使千百万男女青年深信，没有一个帝国主义之强大是足以反抗人民的。由于他们胜利的进军，把他们广大的国家从封建的和官僚资本的吸血者的扼制下解放出来。中国青年已经证明了，青年通过斗争能够而且也一定会赢得更好的幸福的前途；那时，青年将不再受任何束缚。中国青年的斗争，已经大大地加强了全世界青年争取持久和平，争取各国人民的民族独立，争取更好的生活的共同的斗争。
    我们希望，在今年八月与九月间在布达佩斯举行的世界青年代表会上，中国青年的这些重要经验，能做适当的贡献。因此，我们现在一方面敬祝你们大会获得最大的成功，同时我们要向中国青年，再一次作兄弟的邀请，请你们派遣大量代表参加青年节和世界青年代表大会。这次世界青年节和世界青年代表大会，在致力于保卫和平的事业，争取人民解放的胜利，争取青年最大的前途的世界青年运动史上，将是伟大的事件。
    你们诚挚的朋友
    世界民主青年联盟主席  波义松（签字）
    二、罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟贺电
    庆祝你们新民主主义青年代表大会，我代表罗马尼亚人民共和国劳动青年向你们致以热烈的兄弟的敬礼！
  中国人民的胜利，给予帝国主义以致命打击
    我国青年赞美你们抵抗美帝国主义援助下蒋介石反动军队的英勇斗争。中国革命军队解放了广大中国的领域，和在这领域上建立起民主制度的成功，给予了帝国主义以致命的打击，给予了以苏联为首的民主营垒强大的支持。我们相信，你们的代表大会将唤起中国民主青年运动广泛的新高潮，在获得新胜利的道路上，你们新民主主义的祖国，将更团结更繁荣。
    罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会总书记丁弗洛列士格
    三、北朝鲜民主青年同盟贺电
    北平全中国青年代表大会全体代表同志们：
  为争取和平与解放，朝中青年亲善团结万岁！
    兹值全中国青年代表大会开幕之际，北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会谨向大会各位代表及全体中国青年致以兄弟的祝贺与敬意。我们朝鲜青年注目着与我祖国在经济、文化上具有极密切关系的中国的亿万青年，在中国共产党领导下，为中国彻底解放与民主发展所进行着的英勇斗争。在这个斗争中，中国人民与青年已解放了作为二十年来国民党反动政权根据地的南京。这一辉煌的胜利以及所争得的伟大的成果，受着为远东和平与安全而斗争中的人民热烈的欢呼。你们的大会在中国人民解放运动上具有深远历史意义的“五四”运动纪念日开幕，相信这个大会必将使中国青年团结更加巩固，并将成为对中国人民解放斗争的完全胜利与世界和平事业巨大的贡献的一个表现。为争取和平与解放，朝中青年亲善团结万岁！
            北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员玄正民
