Title: Successive Victories on the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Front: 7,000 Enemies Annihilated Southwest of Jiangshan; Two Enemy Regiments and One Battalion Eliminated in the Battle of Baijiang Town
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang-Jiangxi Front, 10th: The People's Liberation Army annihilated over 7,000 men of the Kuomintang reactionaries' reconstructed counter-revolutionary armed forces of the Ninth Compilation Headquarters and others in the Jiangshan southwest area of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway on the 7th and 8th, and captured more than 40 vehicles. At the same time, the People's Liberation Army annihilated one battalion of the 18th Army (reconstructed after being annihilated in the Huaihai Campaign) in Jinxi, southwest of Guixi, and continued to pursue southwards towards Nancheng. On the 5th, the People's Liberation Army annihilated the remnants of the 68th Army at Yunji Pass, southwest of Qiankou in eastern Jiangxi, capturing more than 400 enemy soldiers and seizing eight heavy machine guns. Furthermore, the People's Liberation Army liberated Yushan on the 6th, capturing more than 20 enemy vehicles.

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang-Jiangxi Front, 10th: The People's Liberation Army annihilated the entire 574th Regiment, 575th Regiment, and one battalion of the 576th Regiment of the 192nd Division of the 106th Army in Baijiang Town, southwest of FenShui in western Zhejiang, on the 5th and 6th, capturing more than 2,700 enemy soldiers and seizing 80 light and heavy machine guns and more than 600 rifles and carbines.

### 浙赣前线连获胜利  江山西南歼敌七千  百江镇之役歼敌两团又一营

1949-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    浙赣前线连获胜利
    江山西南歼敌七千
    百江镇之役歼敌两团又一营
    【新华社浙赣前线十日电】人民解放军于七、八两日，在浙赣路江山西南地区，歼灭国民党反动派重建之反革命武装的第九编练司令部等共七千余人，缴获汽车四十余辆。同时，解放军在贵溪西南的金溪歼敌十八军一个营（该军系于淮海战役中被歼灭后重建者），并继续向南城追击。五日，解放军曾在江西东部潜山西南之云际关歼敌六十八军残部，俘敌四百余人，缴重机枪八挺。又解放军六日解放玉山，缴获敌汽车二十余辆。
    【新华社浙赣前线十日电】人民解放军于五、六两日在浙江西部分水西南的百江镇，歼敌一零六军一九二师五七四团、五七五团全部及五七六团一个营，俘敌二千七百余人，缴获轻重机枪八十挺，步马枪六百余支。
