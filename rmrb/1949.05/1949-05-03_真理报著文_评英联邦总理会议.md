Title: Pravda Article Comments on British Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-05-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 1st: Moscow dispatch: In a commentary on the British Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference's discussion of Anglo-Indian relations on the 25th, Pravda's commentator emphasized that the United States played a significant role in exacerbating the post-war crisis of the British Empire. American imperialism is systematically attacking the colonial empire positions of its "little partner." The constant chanting by American military figures about Australia's special strategic importance to the Pacific is not without selfish motives. The South African Dominion openly pursues a policy of division. Recently, American diplomats have been actively trying to bring India into the orbit of American expansion.

### 真理报著文  评英联邦总理会议

1949-05-03
第3版()
专栏：

    真理报著文
    评英联邦总理会议
    【新华社北平一日电】莫斯科讯：真理报评论员二十五日在评论英联邦总理会议中关于英印关系的讨论时，着重指出：美国在加深大英帝国的战后危机方面，起了很大的作用。美帝国主义有系统的进攻其“小伙计”的殖民帝国阵地。美国军方人士不休止地高唱澳大利亚对于太平洋具有特殊的战略重要性，并不是没有自私的目的的。南非自治领公然奉行分裂政策。最近美国外交人员更大事活动想把印度纳入美国扩张的轨道。
