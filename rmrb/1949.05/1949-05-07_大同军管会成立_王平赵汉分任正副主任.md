Title: The Datong Military Control Commission is Established; Wang Ping and Zhao Han Serve as Director and Deputy Director Respectively
Author:
Date: 1949-05-07
Page: 1

    Datong Military Control Commission Established
    Wang Ping and Zhao Han Serve as Director and Deputy Director Respectively
    [Chahar Telegram] The Datong Military Control Commission, the Datong Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and the Datong Municipal People's Government have all been formally established. The Director of the Military Control Commission is concurrently held by Wang Ping, Commander of the Chahar Military Region, and the Deputy Director is concurrently held by Zhao Han, Secretary of the Datong Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Mayor of the Datong Municipal People's Government. Li Tiesheng is the Deputy Mayor.

### 大同军管会成立  王平赵汉分任正副主任

1949-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    大同军管会成立
    王平赵汉分任正副主任
    【察哈尔电】大同市军事管制委员会、中共大同市委员会、大同市人民政府均已正式成立，军管会主任由察哈尔军区司令员王平兼任，副主任由中共大同市委书记兼大同市人民政府市长赵汉兼任。副市长为李铁生。
