Title: Benxi Coal and Iron Company: Red May Competition - Coal Production Increased by Half
Author:
Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 3

　　Benxi Coal and Iron Company
  　Red May Competition
  　Coal Production Increased by Half
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Benxi 19th Dispatch: Since the Benxi Coal and Iron Company launched the Red May production competition on the 3rd of this month, up to the 14th, the work efficiency of coal mines, steelmaking, machinery, coking, refractory materials, and iron-smelting factories has increased by more than 30% compared to before the competition. On the fifth day of the competition, all workers in the coal mine increased coal production by 44%.

### 本溪煤铁公司　红五月竞赛　煤产提高一半

1949-05-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　本溪煤铁公司
  　红五月竞赛
  　煤产提高一半
    【北平新华广播电台广播】本溪十九号消息：本溪煤铁公司，从本月三日展开红五月生产大竞赛以来，到十四日为止，煤矿、炼钢、机械、炼焦、耐火器材、炼铁工厂的工作效率比竞赛以前都提高了百分之三十以上。煤矿中的全体工人在开始竞赛的第五天，就使产煤量增加了百分之四十四。
