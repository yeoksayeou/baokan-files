Title: Shijiazhuang City Begins to Implement Thrift

Author:
Date: 1949-05-28
Page: 2

Shijiazhuang City Begins to Implement Thrift
    [Shijiazhuang Telegram] Shijiazhuang City, in response to the call from the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee for drought resistance, production, thrift, and preparation against famine, has launched a production and thrift campaign. The Shijiazhuang Municipal Committee of the CPC, the Municipal Government, the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and all agencies in the city have decided that from June 1st to the end of August, each person will save one to two liang of grain per day to prepare for famine relief. It is also stipulated to reduce special expenses, stop non-essential construction and equipment purchases, streamline non-essential personnel, and strictly check for wasteful phenomena. Currently, Shijiazhuang City is beginning to organize mutual assistance between government cadres and citizens, and to organize unemployed workers and the poor for production. Workers and staff of various factories in Shijiazhuang City have also put forward the slogan of "saving raw materials, utilizing waste materials, and reducing costs."

### 石市开始实行节约

1949-05-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　石市开始实行节约
    【石家庄电】石家庄市响应中共中央华北局抗旱生产节约备荒的号召，顷展开生产节约运动。中共石家庄市委、市政府、市总工会及全市各机关已决定自六月一日至八月底，每人每日节约粮食一两到二两，准备备荒救灾之用。并规定减少特殊费的开支，停止不紧要的建设和设备，精减勤杂人员，和严格检查浪费现象等。现石市正开始着手组织机关干部与市民间的互济互助运动，组织失业工人和贫民生产。石市各工厂职工也提出了“节省原料，利用废料，减低成本”的节约口号。
