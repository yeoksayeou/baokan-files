Title: Pinghan Railway: Xinxiang-Zhengzhou Section Opens to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1949-05-29
Page: 1

　　Pinghan Railway
  　Xinxiang-Zhengzhou Section Opens to Traffic
    [This Newspaper Reported] The Xinxiang-Zhengzhou section of the Pinghan Railway officially opened to traffic on the 25th. The section is 79 kilometers long. Repair work began on the 18th of this month and was completed on the 22nd. Coal from the Jiaozuo coal mine can now be transported in large quantities via Xinxiang to Zhengzhou for external sales.

### 平汉铁路　新乡郑州通车

1949-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　平汉铁路
  　新乡郑州通车
    【本报讯】平汉铁路新乡、郑州段已于二十五日正式通车。该段全长七十九公里，修路工作自本月十八日开始抢修，于二十二日竣工。现焦作煤矿的煤，已可经新乡转郑州大量外销。
