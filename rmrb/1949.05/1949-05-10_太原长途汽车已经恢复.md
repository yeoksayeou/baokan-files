Title: Long-Distance Bus Service in Taiyuan Resumes
Author: 
Date: 1949-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, 9th. Long-distance bus service between Taiyuan and places like Yuci, Qingyuan, and Xinxian has been restored. The municipal public bus system, established by the People's Government, officially began selling tickets on the 2nd, providing convenience for citizens.

### 太原长途汽车已经恢复

1949-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    太原长途汽车已经恢复
    【新华社太原九日电】太原市与榆次、清源、忻县等地的长途汽车业已恢复。市区公共汽车亦经人民政府创设，于二日正式售票，市民称便。
