Title: Ministry of Railways Establishes Railway Corps; Teng Daiyuan Concurrently Serves as Commander

Author: 
Date: 1949-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 30th -- In order to adapt to the victorious situation nationwide and to repair and construct railways throughout the country, the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission ordered on May 16th that the headquarters of the original 4th Field Army Railway Column be transferred to the Ministry of Railways to establish the Chinese People's Liberation Army Railway Corps, directly under the leadership of the Minister of Railways. Teng Daiyuan was appointed to concurrently serve as the commander of the Railway Corps, and Lü Zhengcao was appointed as the deputy commander. The Railway Corps was established in Beiping on the 26th and has four detachments under its command: the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 4th.

At the founding ceremony, Commander Teng called on all officers and soldiers to humbly learn from the advanced Soviet Union in socialist railway construction, to take learning technology as our work task, and to victoriously complete the construction of the people's railways throughout the country.

### 铁道部成立铁道兵团　滕代远兼任司令员

1949-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　铁道部成立铁道兵团
  　滕代远兼任司令员
    【新华社北平三十日电】为适应全国胜利的形势，修复和建设全国的铁路，中国人民革命军事委员会于五月十六日命令原第四野战军铁道纵队司令部拨归铁道部建制，成立中国人民解放军铁道兵团，受铁道部部长直接领导，并任命滕代远兼铁道兵团司令员，吕正操兼副司令员。铁道兵团已于二十六日在北平成立，下辖第一、第二、第三、第四等四个支队。
    在成立大会上，滕司令员号召全体指战员虚心向社会主义建设铁路先进的苏联学习，要把学习技术当作我们的工作任务，以胜利完成全国人民铁道的建设。
