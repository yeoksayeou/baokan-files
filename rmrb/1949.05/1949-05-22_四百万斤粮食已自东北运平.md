Title: Four Million Jin of Grain Have Been Shipped from the Northeast to Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-05-22
Page: 2
Economic News

　　Four Million Jin of Grain Have Been Shipped from the Northeast to Beiping
　　[This newspaper's report] In order to regulate grain supplies in various regions, the North China Trade Corporation purchased a large quantity of sorghum, corn, and other grains from the Northeast. Currently, four million jin have arrived in Beiping on the 20th, and the remainder is being transported in succession. (Yu)

### 四百万斤粮食已自东北运平

1949-05-22
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　四百万斤粮食已自东北运平
    【本报讯】华北贸易总公司为调剂各地粮食，曾于东北购得大量高粱、玉米等谷物，现有四百万斤已于二十日运抵北平，其余部分正陆续运输中。（宇）
