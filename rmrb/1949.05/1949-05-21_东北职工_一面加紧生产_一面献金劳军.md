Title: Northeast Workers: Speed Up Production and Donate Money to Comfort the Troops
Author:
Date: 1949-05-21
Page: 1
　　[Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Shenyang, 19th: After the victorious news of the People's Liberation Army's advance into the south, liberating Nanjing and vast areas, reached the Northeast, the workers of various state-owned enterprises in the Northeast, with joyful and excited feelings, stepped up production and launched a large-scale campaign to donate money to comfort the troops. From the 9th to the 11th of this month, within three days, various state-owned enterprises submitted a total of 370,175,000+ Northeast currency to the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions to be forwarded to the front lines as consolation money. In addition, there were more than two hundred letters of sympathy.

### 东北职工　一面加紧生产  一面献金劳军

1949-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北职工
  　一面加紧生产  一面献金劳军
    【北平新华广播电台广播】沈阳十九号消息：当解放大军进军江南解放南京和广大地区的胜利消息传到东北后，东北各国营企业职工都以欢欣鼓舞的心情，加紧生产，并且展开了大规模的献金劳军运动。从本月九号到十一号的三天内，各国营企业单位送交东北职工总会代转前方的慰劳金共计有东北币三万万七千零十七万五千多元。另外还有慰问信二百多封。
