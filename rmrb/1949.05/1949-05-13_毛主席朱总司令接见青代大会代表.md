Title: Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Receive Representatives of the Youth Congress

Author: 
Date: 1949-05-13
Page: 1

    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and Comrade Liu Shaoqi of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China received all the representatives of the First National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation on the 12th. When the national youth representatives saw the great leaders of the Chinese people, they burst into enthusiastic cheers, applause, and shouts of "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Commander-in-Chief Zhu!" which resounded continuously for a long time. Amidst solemn military music, the congress presented a banner to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu. The bright red banner read, "Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu: We learn from you and advance under your banner." Subsequently, the Southeast, Southwest, South China, and Taiwan delegations presented banners, their representatives passionately expressing their determination to do everything possible to welcome the southward advance of the People's Liberation Army and strive for the liberation of all of China. Comrade Liu Shaoqi and Commander-in-Chief Zhu addressed the representatives.

### 毛主席朱总司令接见青代大会代表

1949-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席朱总司令接见青代大会代表
    【北平新华广播电台广播】中共中央毛主席、朱总司令、刘少奇同志在十二日接见中华全国青年第一次代表大会的全体代表，当全国的青年代表们看见了中国人民伟大的领袖时，他们发出热烈的欢呼，鼓掌声和“毛主席万岁”“朱总司令万岁”的口号声，长时间地不断地响着。在庄严的军乐声中，大会向毛主席、朱总司令献旗，鲜红的大旗上写着“毛主席、朱总司令：我们向你们学习，在你们的旗帜下前进”。接着，由东南、西南、华南、台湾等代表团献旗，他们的代表热情地表示出他们要尽一切力量来迎接人民解放军南下，为解放全中国而努力。刘少奇同志和朱总司令向代表们讲了话。
