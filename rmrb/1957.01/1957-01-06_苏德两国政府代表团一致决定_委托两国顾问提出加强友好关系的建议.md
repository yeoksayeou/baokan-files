Title: Soviet and German Government Delegations Unanimously Decide to Entrust Advisors from Both Countries to Propose Suggestions for Strengthening Friendly Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-01-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, January 5th. TASS reported: On January 4th, the government delegations of the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic held their first meeting in the Kremlin to discuss measures for further developing and consolidating friendly relations between the two countries.

The Soviet side participating in the meeting included Soviet Premier Bulganin, First Deputy Premiers Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Saburov, and Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov.

The German Democratic Republic side participating in the meeting consisted of the entire delegation of the German Democratic Republic government, headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl.

After unanimously adopting the agenda of the meeting, Prime Minister Grotewohl delivered a lengthy speech in which he elaborated on the domestic and foreign policies of the German Democratic Republic.

Bulganin then spoke, extending greetings on behalf of the Soviet government and the entire Soviet working people to the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, and through it to the entire working people of the German Democratic Republic and all progressive forces of the German people. Bulganin expressed his belief that the sincere and friendly talks between the two delegations would inevitably further strengthen the fraternal cooperation between the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic, thereby further strengthening the strength of the socialist countries and promoting world peace.

After an exchange of views in a friendly atmosphere of mutual understanding and sincerity, the two delegations unanimously decided to entrust advisors and experts from both countries to study the relevant documents and put forward their own suggestions for consideration by the government delegations of both countries.

### 苏德两国政府代表团一致决定  委托两国顾问提出加强友好关系的建议

1957-01-06
第5版()
专栏：

    苏德两国政府代表团一致决定
    委托两国顾问提出加强友好关系的建议
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联和德意志民主共和国两国政府代表团4日在克里姆林宫就关于采取措施进一步发展和巩固两国友好关系的问题举行了第一次会谈。
    苏联方面参加会谈的，有苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁，第一副主席米高扬、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫，苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫。
    德意志民主共和国方面参加会谈的是以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团全体人员。
    在一致通过会谈日程以后，格罗提渥总理发表了长篇演说，他详细地陈述了德意志民主共和国的对内和对外政策。
    布尔加宁接着讲了话，他代表苏联政府和苏联全体劳动人民向德意志民主共和国政府代表团并通过它向德意志民主共和国全体劳动人民和德国人民的所有进步力量致意。布尔加宁表示相信，双方代表团诚恳和友好会谈的结果，必将使苏联和德意志民主共和国的兄弟合作越发加强，从而进一步加强社会主义国家的力量和促进世界和平。
    两国代表团在友好的相互谅解和诚恳气氛中交换意见以后，一致决定委托两国顾问和专家研究相应的文件并且提出自己的建议，交两国政府代表团审议。
