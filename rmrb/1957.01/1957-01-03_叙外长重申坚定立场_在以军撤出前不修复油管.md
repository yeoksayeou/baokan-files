Title: Syrian Foreign Minister Reaffirms Firm Stance: No Pipeline Repair Before Israeli Troop Withdrawal
Author:
Date: 1957-01-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch from London: Syrian Foreign Minister, Mr. Bitar, reaffirmed on January 1st that Syria will not begin repairing the section of the Iraq Petroleum Company pipeline that runs through Syrian territory until Israeli forces withdraw from the Sinai Peninsula and the Gaza Strip.

Bitar made these remarks during a conversation with a reporter from the London "Financial Times." Bitar also stated that the Syrian government refuses to compensate the company for the losses at the pumping station because it was not requested to provide protection.

### 叙外长重申坚定立场  在以军撤出前不修复油管

1957-01-03
第6版()
专栏：

    叙外长重申坚定立场
    在以军撤出前不修复油管
    新华社2日讯　伦敦消息：叙利亚外交部长比塔尔1日重申，叙利亚将不在以色列军队撤出西奈沙漠和加沙地区之前动工修复伊拉克石油公司在叙利亚境内的那一段油管。
    比塔尔是在同伦敦“金融时报”记者谈话时这样说的。比塔尔还说，叙利亚政府拒绝向公司赔偿帮普站的损失，因为它没有被请求给予保护。
