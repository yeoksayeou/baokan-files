Title: Preliminary Results of Polish Parliamentary Elections: National Unity Front Achieves Absolute Victory
Author: Xie Wenqing
Date: 1957-01-23
Page: 5

    Preliminary Results of Polish Parliamentary Elections
    National Unity Front Achieves Absolute Victory
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 22nd (Dispatch from our correspondent Xie Wenqing) – It is announced here that preliminary results of the Polish parliamentary elections are in: all members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, secretaries of the Secretariat, and almost all nominated central-level candidates have been elected as members of parliament; leaders of various democratic parties have also been elected. Therefore, it can already be considered that the Polish National Unity Front has achieved an absolute victory. It can also be said that the Polish United Workers' Party has achieved a significant victory in the elections.

### 波兰议会选举初步结果  全国统一阵线获得绝对胜利
谢文清
1957-01-23
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰议会选举初步结果
    全国统一阵线获得绝对胜利
    新华社华沙22日电　本社记者谢文清报道：此间宣布，波兰议会选举已经有了初步结果：波兰统一工人党中央委员会全体政治局委员、书记处书记和几乎全部被提名的中央一级候选人都已经当选为议会议员；各民主党派的领导人也已当选。因此，已经可以认为，波兰全国统一阵线已经获得绝对的胜利。也可以说，波兰统一工人党在选举中得到了重大的胜利。
