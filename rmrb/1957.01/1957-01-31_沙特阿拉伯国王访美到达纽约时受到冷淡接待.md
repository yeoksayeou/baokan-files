Title: Saudi Arabian King Receives Cool Reception Upon Arrival in New York for US Visit
Author:
Date: 1957-01-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 30th: Saudi Arabian King Saud arrived in New York by ship on January 29th. He is visiting the United States for two weeks on a state visit at the invitation of U.S. President Eisenhower.

After landing in New York, Saud stated that he would directly exchange views with U.S. government leaders on the relations between Saudi Arabia and the United States, as well as issues related to the entire Middle East region. He expressed his hope that this visit would lead to the strengthening of relations between the two countries.

According to news agency reports, Saud received a cool reception upon his arrival in New York. Although the U.S. authorities sent a destroyer to greet him and fired a salute, no officials from the state or city of New York were present to welcome Saud when the ship he was on docked.

### 沙特阿拉伯国王访美到达纽约时受到冷淡接待

1957-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯国王访美到达纽约时受到冷淡接待
    新华社30日讯　纽约消息：沙特阿拉伯国王沙特1月29日乘船抵达纽约。他是应美国总统艾森豪威尔的邀请，到美国作两个星期的国事访问的。
    沙特在纽约上岸后发表谈话说，他将就沙特阿拉伯和美国的关系以及对整个中东地区有关的问题同美国政府领导人直接交换意见。他希望这次访问将会导致两国关系的加强。
    据通讯社报道，沙特到达纽约时受到冷淡的接待。美国当局虽然派了驱逐舰去迎接，并且鸣放礼炮，但是在沙特乘坐的轮船靠岸时，纽约州和市的官员们都没有去欢迎。
