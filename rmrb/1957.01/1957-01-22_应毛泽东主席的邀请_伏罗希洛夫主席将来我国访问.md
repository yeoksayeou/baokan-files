Title: Chairman Voroshilov to Visit China at the Invitation of Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1957-01-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 21st:

    Chairman Voroshilov to Visit China at the Invitation of Chairman Mao Zedong
    Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has invited the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, to visit China. Chairman Voroshilov has accepted the invitation. Chairman Mao Zedong's letter of invitation and Chairman Voroshilov's reply are as follows:

To the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, Respected Comrade Voroshilov:

    On behalf of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, I warmly invite you, respected Chairman, to visit our country. I am convinced your visit will further consolidate the close cooperation and great friendship between our two countries. If you agree, please let us know the date you deem appropriate for the visit. I and all the people of China look forward to your arrival with great pleasure. I extend to you my highest respects.

                            Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong (signed)
                                                    January 6, 1957, in Beijing

To the Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Respected Comrade Mao Zedong:

    Please allow me, on behalf of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and myself personally, to express my heartfelt thanks for your warm invitation to visit the People's Republic of China. I accept your invitation with gratitude. If it is convenient for you, I am prepared to visit the fraternal People's Republic of China between April 15 and May 5, 1957.

    I extend my sincere greetings.

                         Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, K. Voroshilov
                                                     January 18, 1957

### 应毛泽东主席的邀请  伏罗希洛夫主席将来我国访问

1957-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    应毛泽东主席的邀请
    伏罗希洛夫主席将来我国访问
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席邀请苏联最高苏维埃主席团伏罗希洛夫主席来中国访问，伏罗希洛夫主席已接受邀请。毛泽东主席的邀请信和伏罗希洛夫主席的复信如下：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团主席敬爱的伏罗希洛夫同志：
    我代表中华人民共和国和中国人民热烈地邀请你，敬爱的主席同志来我国进行访问。我深信，你的访问必将进一步巩固和发展我们两国之间的亲密合作和伟大友谊。如你同意，请将你认为合适的访问日期，通知我们。我和全中国的人民以十分愉快的心情期待着你的光临。谨向你致最崇高的敬意。
                            中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东（签署）				　1957年1月6日于北京中华人民共和国主席敬爱的毛泽东同志：
    请允许我以苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团和我个人的名义对您热情地邀请我访问中华人民共和国表示衷心的感谢。我怀着感激的心情接受您的邀请。如果对您方便的话，我准备在1957年4月15日至5月5日期间访问兄弟般的中华人民共和国。
    顺致诚挚的敬礼。
                         苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席　克·伏罗希洛夫					　1957年1月18日
