Title: Polish Coal Mining Industry Exceeds Production Target for First Half of Month
Author:
Date: 1957-01-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 18th: According to news reports here, in the first half of January, the Polish coal mining industry overproduced by 6,976 tons of coal above the planned target. This is the first time in several months that the coal mining industry has fulfilled the production task for the first half of a month. In this half-month, fifty-three coal mines have already completed their production plans. This is mainly due to the decrease in absenteeism and the improvement of labor discipline.

### 波煤矿工业超额完成上半月生产任务

1957-01-19
第5版()
专栏：

    波煤矿工业超额完成上半月生产任务
    新华社华沙18日电　据这里报纸的消息说，在1月份的上半月里，波兰煤矿工业超计划生产了六千九百七十六吨煤。这是几个月以来煤矿工业第一次完成了一个月上半月的生产任务。在这半个月里，已经有五十三个煤矿完成了生产计划。这主要是由于缺勤率的下降和劳动纪律的改善所促成的。
