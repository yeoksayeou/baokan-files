Title: Chinese Government Delegation Pays Respects at Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1957-01-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 18th: The Chinese government delegation led by Premier Zhou Enlai paid respects at the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum on the morning of the 18th and laid wreaths.

	One wreath was inscribed: "Dedicated to the great revolutionary teacher V.I. Lenin."

	Another wreath was inscribed: "Dedicated to the great Marxist-Leninist J.V. Stalin."

	Accompanying the delegation to the mausoleum were Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Fedrin, Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin, Director of the Far East Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Gudev, and Lieutenant General Vodein, Commander of the Kremlin Guard.

### 我国政府代表团谒列宁斯大林陵墓

1957-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府代表团谒列宁斯大林陵墓
    新华社莫斯科18日电　周恩来总理率领的中国政府代表团在18日上午参谒了列宁斯大林陵墓，并且献了花圈。
    一个花圈上写着：“献给伟大的革命导师弗·伊·列宁”。
    另一个花圈上写着：“献给伟大的马克思列宁主义者约·维·斯大林”。
    陪同代表团谒陵的有苏联外交部副部长费德林、苏联驻中国大使尤金、外交部远东司司长顾德夫、克里姆林宫卫戍司令沃杰林中将。
