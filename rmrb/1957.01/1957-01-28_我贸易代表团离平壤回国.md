Title: Our Trade Delegation Leaves Pyongyang for Home
Author:
Date: 1957-01-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 27th. The Chinese government trade delegation headed by Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, concluded its visit to Korea and left Pyongyang by train this morning to return home.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Chen Banxiu, Minister of Domestic and Foreign Commerce of Korea, Park Sung-chul, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to Korea.

### 我贸易代表团离平壤回国

1957-01-28
第5版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团离平壤回国
    新华社平壤27日电　中国以对外贸易部部长叶季壮为首的中国政府贸易代表团结束了在朝鲜的访问，在今天早晨从平壤乘火车回国。
    前往车站欢送的有，朝鲜对内外商业相陈班秀、外务省副相朴成哲，中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光等。
