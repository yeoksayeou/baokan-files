On the Xisha Islands

Last spring, hundreds of workers and cadres braved the wind and waves, crossed the ocean, and came to the Xisha Islands of our motherland to mine valuable guano and explore for treasures. Their life there was difficult, but also very happy. These photos introduce the local scenery and some fragments of their lives.

                                  Photographed by Jia Huamin (Manuscript from China Youth Daily)

Below: Scenery of the Xisha Islands.　Right: Loading guano, a good raw material for chemical fertilizers, onto ships to transport it back to the mainland.　Left: Staff members of the Institute of Zoology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences are collecting specimens here.

### 在西沙群岛上（图片）
贾化民
1957-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    在西沙群岛上
    去年春天，有数百名工人和干部，冒着风浪，越过海洋，来到祖国的西沙群岛，挖取贵重的鸟粪和探寻宝藏。他们在那里很艰苦，但也很愉快。这几张照片所介绍的是当地风光和他们生活的一些片断。
                                  贾化民摄（中国青年报社稿）
    下图：西沙群岛的风光。　右图：将化学肥料的好原料——鸟粪，装上船，运回大陆去。　左图：中国科学院动物研究所的工作人员在这里搜集标本。
