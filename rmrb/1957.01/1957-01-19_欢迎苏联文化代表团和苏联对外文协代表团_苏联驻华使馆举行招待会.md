Title: Soviet Embassy in China Holds Reception to Welcome Soviet Cultural Delegation and Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, January 18th. The Soviet Embassy's Chargé d'Affaires ad interim in China, G.T. Tikhvinsky, held a reception this evening to welcome the Soviet Cultural Delegation headed by Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Delegation headed by Kairov.

    Chinese attendees at the reception included Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister Qian Junrui, Minister of Higher Education Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Education Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Health Li Dequan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Yuzhang, and figures from the capital's literary, artistic, and educational circles. Diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in China also attended the reception.

### 欢迎苏联文化代表团和苏联对外文协代表团  苏联驻华使馆举行招待会

1957-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    欢迎苏联文化代表团和苏联对外文协代表团
    苏联驻华使馆举行招待会
    新华社18日讯　苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办顾悌克夫今天晚上举行招待会，欢迎以苏联文化部部长米哈伊洛夫为首的苏联文化代表团和以凯洛夫为首的苏联对外文化协会代表团。
    中国方面出席招待会的有文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长钱俊瑞，高等教育部部长杨秀峰、教育部部长张奚若，卫生部部长李德全，外交部副部长姬鹏飞和中苏友好协会副会长吴玉章等以及首都的文艺、教育界人士。各国驻中国的外交使节也出席了招待会。
