Title: Premier Zhou Sends Congratulatory Message on Sudanese National Day
Author:
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 31st: Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Sudanese Prime Minister Abdallah Khalil on December 30th, congratulating him on the National Day of the Republic of Sudan. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Abdallah Khalil, Prime Minister of the Government of the Republic of Sudan:

On the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of Sudan, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to the Government of the Republic of Sudan, the people of Sudan, and to Your Excellency, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, and in my own name.

I am pleased to see that the friendly relations between our two countries, which have existed since the Asian-African Conference, have been enhanced, and I sincerely hope that this friendly relationship between our two countries will be further developed. I wish the Republic of Sudan prosperity and the people of Sudan happiness.

　　　　　　State Council of the People's Republic of China
　　　　　　　　　　　Premier Zhou Enlai
　　　　　　　　　　　December 30, 1956

### 周总理电贺苏丹国庆

1957-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺苏丹国庆
    新华社31日讯　周恩来总理30日致电苏丹共和国总理阿卜杜拉·哈利勒，祝贺苏丹共和国国庆。电文如下：苏丹共和国政府总理阿卜杜拉·哈利勒阁下：
    值此苏丹共和国国庆之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民，并以我个人的名义向苏丹共和国政府、苏丹人民和阁下表示热烈的祝贺。
    我高兴地看到，自亚非会议以来存在于我们两国之间的友好关系已有增进，我衷心地希望我们两国间的这种友好关系将会获得进一步的发展。祝苏丹共和国繁荣，苏丹人民幸福。
      中华人民共和国国务院
                      总理周恩来
                  1956年12月30日
