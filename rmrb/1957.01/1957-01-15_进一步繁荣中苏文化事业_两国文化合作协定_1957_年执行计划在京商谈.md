Title: Further Prospering Chinese and Soviet Cultural Undertakings: Negotiations on the 1957 Implementation Plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 14th: The Chinese government delegation, headed by Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture, and the Soviet government's cultural delegation, began negotiations in Beijing this morning on the 1957 implementation plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

    Soviet cultural delegation leader Mikhailov and Qian Junrui exchanged congratulatory messages at the beginning of the negotiations. They jointly expressed their desire to promote greater prosperity and development of the cultural undertakings of both countries and to further consolidate and develop the great friendship between the two peoples through fraternal cooperation in the cultural fields of China and the Soviet Union. They hoped that in addition to studying this year's implementation plan, both sides would also consider long-term plans for future cultural cooperation between the two countries during this negotiation.

    Participating in today's negotiations were all members of the Soviet cultural delegation, as well as Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Culture, Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Higher Education, and Cui Yitian, Vice Minister of Health, among others.

    After the meeting, Qian Junrui hosted a banquet at the Beijing Hotel for all members of the Soviet cultural delegation.

### 进一步繁荣中苏文化事业  两国文化合作协定1957年执行计划在京商谈

1957-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    进一步繁荣中苏文化事业
    两国文化合作协定1957年执行计划在京商谈
    新华社14日讯　以文化部代理部长钱俊瑞为首的我国政府代表团和苏联政府派遣的文化代表团，今天上午开始在北京商谈中苏文化合作协定的1957年执行计划。
    苏联文化代表团团长米哈伊洛夫和钱俊瑞在商谈开始时互致祝词，他们共同表示：愿望通过中苏文化事业方面兄弟般的合作，促使两国文化事业更加繁荣昌盛、两国人民的伟大友谊更加巩固和发展。他们希望双方在这次商谈中除了研究今年的执行计划之外，同时考虑两国今后文化合作事业的远景规划。
    参加今天商谈的有苏联文化代表团全体人员，以及文化部副部长张致祥，高等教育部副部长刘皑风，卫生部副部长崔义田等。
    会后，钱俊瑞在北京饭店设宴招待苏联文化代表团全体人员。
