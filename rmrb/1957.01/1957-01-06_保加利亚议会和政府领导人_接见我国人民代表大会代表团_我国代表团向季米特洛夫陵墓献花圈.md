Title: Bulgarian Parliament and Government Leaders Receive Delegation from China's National People's Congress; Chinese Delegation Lays Wreath at Dimitrov Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1957-01-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, January 5th: Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, received the delegation from the Chinese National People's Congress and the delegation from the Beijing Municipal People's Committee on the 4th.
    Present at the reception were Klisurski and Kulishev, Vice-Chairmen of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly; Kozovski, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the National Assembly; and Traikov, Member of the Presidium of the National Assembly, Secretary of the Agrarian Union, and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers.
    Also present at the reception was Zhou Zhu'an, Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria.
    During the reception, the hosts and guests engaged in cordial conversations and repeatedly stood up to toast the friendship between the two peoples.
    In his speech, Damyanov expressed a warm welcome to the delegation's visit to Bulgaria and emphasized the importance of the unity of socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union.
    Head of the delegation Peng Zhen, in his speech, first expressed gratitude for the invitation from the Bulgarian National Assembly and for the grand, enthusiastic, and brotherly reception and welcome extended to the delegation. Peng Zhen said: "There is a deep friendship between our two peoples. Our delegation's visit this time is to further strengthen mutual understanding between the Chinese and Bulgarian peoples, further strengthen the friendly unity between the Chinese and Bulgarian peoples, and strengthen the friendly unity between socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union. At the same time, it is also to learn from your various advanced experiences in socialist construction. We firmly believe that our visit will achieve our intended goals."
    On this day, the delegation paid tribute to the leader and mentor of the Bulgarian people, Dimitrov, at his mausoleum and laid a wreath.
    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, January 5th: Chairman of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers, Yugov, received the delegation from the Chinese National People's Congress and the delegation from the Beijing Municipal People's Committee on the morning of the 4th.
    Present at the reception were First Deputy Chairman of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers, Zhankov, Traikov (Secretary of the Agrarian Union), Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, R. Damyanov, Mikhailov, Chervenkov, Lukanov, and ministers.
    Also present at the reception was Zhou Zhu'an, Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria.
    Yugov and Peng Zhen both spoke during the meeting. They both emphasized the importance of the unity and cooperation between socialist countries, peace-loving countries around the world, and their peoples. Yugov said that the delegation's visit is a brilliant manifestation of the unbreakable friendship between the Bulgarian and Chinese peoples. He said that without the concerted and united efforts of socialist countries, any one of them would become a target for imperialist attacks.
    Peng Zhen agreed with Yugov's view on unity. He said, indeed, we are in great need of unity. We socialist countries not only need internal unity, but also greatly need the close unity and friendly cooperation between socialist countries, and need close unity and friendly cooperation with all peace-loving countries and peoples. Regardless of whether a country is large or small, regardless of the size of its population, this kind of unity and cooperation is needed. Only such unity can smoothly defend peace and smoothly develop the cause of socialist construction.
    In the evening, the delegation enjoyed a performance of a famous Bulgarian opera.

### 保加利亚议会和政府领导人  接见我国人民代表大会代表团  我国代表团向季米特洛夫陵墓献花圈

1957-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚议会和政府领导人
    接见我国人民代表大会代表团
    我国代表团向季米特洛夫陵墓献花圈
    新华社索非亚5日电　保加利亚国民议会主席团主席达米扬诺夫在4日接见了中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团。
    接见时在座的有保加利亚国民议会主席团副主席克利苏尔斯基、库利歇夫，国民议会执行委员会主席科佐夫斯基，国民议会主席团委员和农民联盟书记、部长会议第一副主席特莱科夫。
    接见时在座的还有中国驻保加利亚大使周竹安。
    接见时宾主间进行了亲切的交谈，并且一再起立为两国人民之间的友谊干杯。
    达米扬诺夫在讲话的时候，表示热烈欢迎代表团来保加利亚访问，并且指出了以苏联为首的社会主义各国团结的重要性。
    彭真团长讲话时首先表示感谢保加利亚国民议会的邀请，感谢对代表团的隆重的热忱的兄弟般的接待和欢迎。彭真说：“我们两国人民之间是有着深厚友谊的。我们代表团这次来访问是为了进一步加强中保两国人民之间的相互了解，进一步加强中保两国人民的友好团结和加强以苏联为首的社会主义各国之间的友好团结，同时也是为了学习你们在社会主义建设中的各种先进经验。我们深信，我们的访问将达到我们预期的目的”。
    在这一天，代表团到保加利亚人民的领袖和导师季米特洛夫的陵墓前致敬，献了花圈。
    新华社索非亚5日电　保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫在4日上午接见了中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团。
    接见时在座的有保加利亚部长会议第一副主席詹科夫、特莱科夫（农民联盟书记），部长会议副主席拉·达米扬诺夫、米哈伊洛夫、契尔文科夫、卢卡诺夫和部长们。
    接见时在座的还有中国驻保加利亚大使周竹安。
    于哥夫和彭真在会见时都讲了话。他们都强调指出了社会主义各国、全世界爱好和平的国家以及人民之间的团结和合作的重要性。于哥夫说，代表团的访问是保中两国人民牢不可破的友谊的光辉表现。他说，没有社会主义国家的一致和联合的努力，它们之间的任何一个都将成为帝国主义的打击对象。
    彭真赞同于哥夫对团结的看法。他说，的确，我们是十分需要团结的。我们各个社会主义国家不仅需要有本国内部的团结，而且也十分需要社会主义各国之间的亲密团结和友好合作，需要同所有爱好和平的国家及人民亲密团结和友好合作。无论国大国小，无论人口多少，都需要这种团结和合作。只有这样的团结才能顺利地保卫和平，顺利地发展社会主义建设事业。
    晚上，代表团欣赏了保加利亚有名的歌剧。
