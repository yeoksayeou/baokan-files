Title: Jordan Insists on Abolishing Anglo-Jordanian Treaty; Britain Forced to Agree to Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1957-01-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Amman, 20th - A spokesperson for the Jordanian Foreign Ministry said on the 20th that Jordan would request to begin urgent negotiations with Britain to terminate the Anglo-Jordanian Treaty.

Xinhua News Agency, London, 22nd - The British Foreign Office announced on the 22nd that Britain had informed Jordan that it was willing to "begin discussions immediately" with Jordan regarding the future of the Anglo-Jordanian Treaty. The Anglo-Jordanian Treaty stipulates that Britain has the right to maintain military bases and station military personnel in Jordan.

According to the announcement, the British government said in a note to the Jordanian government that it was "happy to know the Jordanian government's opinion on the location and date of holding such discussions." The British government's note was handed over to the Jordanian government by the British Ambassador to Jordan, Johnston, on January 16.

### 约旦坚持废除英约条约  英国被迫同意举行谈判

1957-01-23
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦坚持废除英约条约
    英国被迫同意举行谈判
    据新华社21日讯　安曼消息：约旦外交部的一位发言人20日说，约旦将要求同英国开始进行紧急谈判，以终止英国—约旦条约。
    据新华社22日讯　伦敦消息：英国外交部22日宣布，英国已经通知约旦，它愿意同约旦“立即开始讨论”英国—约旦条约的前途问题。英国—约旦条约规定英国有权在约旦保有军事基地和驻扎军事人员。
    据宣布，英国政府在给约旦政府的照会中说，它“乐于知道约旦政府对于举行这样的讨论的地点和日期的意见”。英国政府的这份照会是英国驻约旦大使约翰斯顿在1月16日转交给约旦政府的。
