Title: French Student Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 1

    French Student Delegation Returns Home
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The French student delegation headed by Claude Josseaud left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.
    The delegation visited China for twenty-five days. Minister of Higher Education Yang Xiufeng received all members of the delegation.

### 法国学生代表团回国

1957-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    法国学生代表团回国
    据新华社11日讯　以克劳特·何西沃耳为首的法国学生代表团今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    代表团在中国参观访问了二十五天。高等教育部部长杨秀峰曾经接见了代表团全体团员。
