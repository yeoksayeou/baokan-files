Title: Even If the Rooster Doesn't Crow, the Day Will Dawn (Indian Proverb)
Author:
Date: 1957-01-20
Page: 8

Even if you live on the shore, don't be friendly with crocodiles.
                 ＊
Dew cannot quench thirst.
                 ＊
Even if the rooster doesn't crow, the day will dawn.
                 ＊
Friendship should not be broken off hastily, and even if it is restored, it will leave aftereffects.
                 ＊
A dog, in its own street, calls itself a lion with unparalleled arrogance.
            Translated by Zhao Zhiqiang and Wang Sengdao

### 公鸡不鸣，天也会亮（印度谚语）

1957-01-20
第8版()
专栏：

    公鸡不鸣，天也会亮（印度谚语）
    即使在岸边住，也不跟鳄鱼友好。
                 ＊
    露水不能解渴。
                 ＊
    公鸡不鸣，天也会亮。
                 ＊
    友情不容草率断绝，再恢复也留下症结。
                 ＊
    狗在自己的大街上，唯我独尊地称狮子。
            赵志强、王僧道选译
