Title: Japanese Ice Hockey Team Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1957-01-18
Page: 4

    Japanese Ice Hockey Team Arrives in Beijing Yesterday

    (Our dispatch) The Japanese ice hockey team, making their first overseas trip in over twenty years, arrived in Beijing by train last night, led by Hiroki Onizuka, the team leader, prominent ice hockey figure, and executive director of the Japan Ice Skating Federation. This is the first Japanese sports delegation to visit our country.

    The guests were warmly welcomed after disembarking by Hu Xiaofeng, vice chairman of the Chinese Winter Sports Association, representatives of the All-China Sports Federation, representatives of the Beijing sports community, and athletes.

    The Japanese ice hockey team, a group of twenty-three, is visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese Winter Sports Association. They will play friendly matches against the Beijing team and a combined Beijing-Harbin team on the evenings of the 20th and 22nd, respectively. After the matches, they will visit and play games in Shenyang, Changchun, and Harbin.

### 日本冰球代表队昨抵京

1957-01-18
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    日本冰球代表队昨抵京
    本报讯　二十多年中第一次出国的日本冰球代表队，在领队、出色的冰球家、日本冰上联盟常务理事会理事鬼鞍弘起率领下，于昨晚乘火车到达北京。这是第一个来我国访问的日本体育团体。
    客人们下车后受到了中国冬季运动协会副主席胡晓风、中华全国体育总会代表、北京市体育界代表和运动员们热烈的欢迎。
    日本冰球代表队一行二十三人是应中国冬季运动协会的邀请来我国访问的。他们将在20日、22日晚上分别对北京队和北京、哈尔滨联队进行友谊比赛，比赛完毕后将去沈阳、长春和哈尔滨等地访问比赛。
