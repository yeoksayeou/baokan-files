Title: A Batch of Chinese Beef and Mutton Arrives at Port Suez
Author:
Date: 1957-01-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 2nd -- A refrigerated ship carrying the first batch of frozen beef and mutton supplied by China to the Egyptian people arrived at Port Suez on December 23rd. The ship carried a total of 1,138 tons of frozen beef and mutton, which timely met the New Year needs of the Egyptian people.

### 我国一批牛羊肉运到了苏伊士港

1957-01-03
第6版()
专栏：

    我国一批牛羊肉运到了苏伊士港
    新华社开罗2日电　装载中国供应埃及人民的第一批冷冻牛羊肉的冷藏船只已经在12月23日到达苏伊士港。这艘船一共装有一千一百三十八吨冷冻牛羊肉，它及时地供应了埃及人民新年的需要。
