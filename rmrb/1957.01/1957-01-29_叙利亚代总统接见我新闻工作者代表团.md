Title: Syrian Acting President Receives Chinese Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, January 28th: The Acting President of the Syrian Republic and Speaker of Parliament, Nazim al-Kudsi, received the Chinese journalists delegation on January 27th, who are visiting Syria at his invitation. The delegation arrived in Damascus from Cairo on January 25th.

In his conversation, Kudsi said that the Arab people are extremely friendly towards the Chinese people. He wished China prosperity and strength, and that it would become an important factor in the struggle for human peace.

The delegation is composed of reporters from the People's Daily, Xinhua News Agency, Guangming Daily, and "People's China".

### 叙利亚代总统接见我新闻工作者代表团

1957-01-29
第5版()
专栏：

    叙利亚代总统接见我新闻工作者代表团
    新华社大马士革28日电　叙利亚共和国代理总统、议会议长纳齐姆·库德西在27日接见了应邀到叙利亚访问的中国新闻工作者代表团。代表团是在1月25日从开罗到达大马士革的。
    库德西在谈话中说，阿拉伯人民对中国人民是极为友好的。他祝中国繁荣强盛，成为争取人类和平的重要因素。
    代表团由人民日报、新华社、光明日报和“人民中国”的记者组成。
