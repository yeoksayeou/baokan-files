Title: Savings in Inner Mongolia's Forest Industry
Author:
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hailar, January 4th: The Inner Mongolia forest industry system saved a total of more than 4.5 million yuan in 1956.

    At the beginning of 1956, the staff and workers of the Greater Khingan Mountains forest industry implemented team economic accounting, forming an atmosphere of meticulous calculation of expenses and emphasis on thrift in all aspects. At the same time, 331 advanced experiences were promoted, of which 87 alone saved more than 810,000 yuan. After the advanced experience of rational sawing was promoted in various lumber enterprises, the material output rate increased from the planned 68% to more than 72%.

### 内蒙古森林工业的节约

1957-01-05
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古森林工业的节约
    据新华社海拉尔4日电　内蒙古森林工业系统1956年共节约了四百五十多万元。
    1956年年初大兴安岭森林工业职工推行了班组经济核算，形成了花钱算细账，处处讲节约的风气。同时，还推广三百三十一项先进经验，仅其中的八十七项即节约了八十一万多元。各个制材企业推广了合理下锯的先进经验以后，出材率由计划68％提高到72％以上。
