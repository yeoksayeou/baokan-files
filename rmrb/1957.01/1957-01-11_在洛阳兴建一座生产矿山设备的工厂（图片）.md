Title: Construction of a Factory Producing Mining Equipment in Luoyang (Picture)
Author: Tang Maolin
Date: 1957-01-11
Page: 2

Construction of a Factory Producing Mining Equipment in Luoyang

A modern mining machinery factory in China—the Luoyang Mining Machinery Factory—is under intense construction. The civil engineering work is mostly completed, and some workshops have begun equipment installation. After completion, this factory will produce a large number of mining equipment such as drilling and hoisting equipment. The picture shows a corner of the construction site. Xinhua News Agency reporter Tang Maolin photographed.

### 在洛阳兴建一座生产矿山设备的工厂（图片）
唐茂林
1957-01-11
第2版()
专栏：

    在洛阳兴建一座生产矿山设备的工厂
    我国的一座现代化矿山机器厂——洛阳矿山机器厂，正在紧张施工，土建工程已大部分完成，有些车间已开始设备安装。这个工厂建成后，将大量生产钻探、起重等矿山设备。图为工地的一角。　新华社记者　唐茂林摄
