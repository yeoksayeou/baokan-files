January 8th, the Chinese government delegation celebrated the New Year's Fir Tree Festival with children at the Moscow Sports Palace. This is "Grandfather Frost" and "Snow Maiden" riding in a car around the venue to greet the audience. In the upper left corner is Premier Zhou Enlai in the stands. (Photo by V. Vologodsky)
