Title: The so-called Soviet "threat" is an excuse for the US to interfere in the Arab world. Syrian Foreign Minister Criticizes the "Eisenhower Doctrine"
Author:
Date: 1957-01-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 20th: According to the Press Trust of India: Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar indirectly mentioned the Eisenhower Doctrine here on the 19th, saying: "The so-called threat of Soviet infiltration into the Arab homeland is merely an excuse to cover up interference in the affairs of the Arab world."

He said: "I believe the Soviet Union has no ambitions toward these countries of ours, nor is it seeking any privileges in Syria and the Arab world."

Bitar is visiting India with President Kuwatly. He made his remarks during an informal conversation with journalists.

He said that Syria needs economic assistance and would welcome such assistance from any group, as long as it is not attached to any restrictions or conditions.

### 所谓苏联“威胁”是美国干涉阿拉伯世界的借口  叙利亚外长抨击“艾森豪威尔主义”

1957-01-21
第6版()
专栏：

    所谓苏联“威胁”是美国干涉阿拉伯世界的借口
    叙利亚外长抨击“艾森豪威尔主义”
    据新华社新德里20日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道：叙利亚外交部长比塔尔19日在这里间接提到艾森豪威尔主义的时候说：“所谓苏联渗入阿拉伯祖国的威胁，不过是为干涉阿拉伯世界的事务进行掩饰的借口而已。”
    他说：“我相信苏联对我们这些国家并不存什么野心，它也没有在叙利亚和阿拉伯世界谋求任何特权。”
    比塔尔正随同库阿特利总统在印度访问。他的谈话是在同新闻记者进行不拘形式的谈话时发表的。
    他说，叙利亚需要经济援助，并且将欢迎来自任何集团的这种援助，只要不附有任何限制和条件就行。
