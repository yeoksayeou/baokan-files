Title: Zhukov Accepts Invitation to Visit India
Author:
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd. According to TASS, at the invitation of the Indian government, the Soviet Minister of Defense, Marshal of the Soviet Union Zhukov, accompanied by his entourage, flew to India on the morning of January 23rd for a visit.

### 朱可夫应邀访印

1957-01-24
第5版()
专栏：

    朱可夫应邀访印
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社报道，苏联国防部长、苏联元帅朱可夫应印度政府的邀请，在1月23日上午偕同随行人员乘飞机去印度访问。
