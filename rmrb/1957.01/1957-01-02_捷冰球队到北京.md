Title: Czech Ice Hockey Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-02
Page: 4

    Sports News

    Czech Ice Hockey Team Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, December 30th – The twenty-two member “Bannik” Club ice hockey team from Vitkovice, Czechoslovakia, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. This is the first foreign ice hockey team invited to visit China for matches.
    The “Bannik” Club ice hockey team, led by Kenkus, a member of the Czechoslovak Ice Hockey Association, is a first-class ice hockey team in Czechoslovakia. Among the team’s seventeen members, five have been members of the Czechoslovak national ice hockey team.
    The “Bannik” Club ice hockey team will hold friendly matches with Chinese ice hockey teams in cities such as Beijing, Changchun, and Harbin. Their match with the Beijing ice hockey team will be held on January 3, 1957.

### 捷冰球队到北京

1957-01-02
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    捷冰球队到北京
    新华社30日讯　捷克斯洛伐克维特科维泽市“班尼克”俱乐部冰球队一行二十二人，今天下午坐飞机到达北京。这是第一个应邀到中国访问比赛的外国冰球队。
    这个由捷克斯洛伐克冰球协会委员肯库什率领的“班尼克”俱乐部冰球队，是捷克斯洛伐克的一级冰球队，这个队的十七名队员当中，有五名曾经是捷克斯洛伐克国家冰球队的队员。
    “班尼克”俱乐部冰球队将在北京、长春和哈尔滨等城市同中国的冰球队举行友谊比赛。他们同北京冰球队的比赛，将在1957年1月3日举行。
