Title: Greek Newspapers Criticize US Attempts to Exploit Cyprus, Pointing Out the Plan is Related to the Eisenhower Doctrine
Author:
Date: 1957-01-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 6th dispatch from Athens via TASS: Greek newspapers are criticizing the United States' plans to exploit Cyprus militarily.

    "Daily News" pointed out: "A few days ago, 'the US representative to Greece notified the Greek government that Washington had decided to cut economic aid to Greece by 50% (a reduction of 30 million US dollars)'." "Freedom Daily" commented on the US decision, writing: "The Greek government believes that this is a new method of indirectly, but actively, applying pressure (on Greece) in order to force Greece not to submit the Cyprus issue to the United Nations for discussion."

    "Tribune" wrote, discussing the US plan to exploit Cyprus: "Due to the new US policy in the Near East, British and American troops may jointly use Cyprus."

    "Evening News" stated that the US plan for Cyprus is related to the so-called "Eisenhower Doctrine" -- the US attempt to interfere in the internal affairs of Middle Eastern countries.

### 希腊报纸抨击美国企图利用塞浦路斯  指出这个计划同艾森豪威尔主义有关

1957-01-07
第6版()
专栏：

    希腊报纸抨击美国企图利用塞浦路斯
    指出这个计划同艾森豪威尔主义有关
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社雅典5日讯：希腊报纸抨击美国企图在军事方面利用塞浦路斯的计划。
    “每日新闻”指出：几天前，“美国驻希腊代表通知希腊政府，华盛顿方面决定削减50％对希腊的经济援助（削减三千万美元）”。“自由报”在评论美国的这个决定时写道：“希腊政府认为这是为了强迫希腊不将塞浦路斯问题提交联合国讨论而间接地、但是积极地（对希腊）施用压力的新手法”。
    “论坛报”在谈到美国利用塞浦路斯的计划时写道：“由于美国在近东的新政策，英美军队可能共同使用塞浦路斯”。
    “新闻晚报”说，美国对塞浦路斯的计划是同所谓“艾森豪威尔主义”——美国干涉中东国家内政的企图相关的。
