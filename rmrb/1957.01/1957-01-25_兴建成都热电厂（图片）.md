Title: Construction of Chengdu Thermal Power Plant (Picture)
Author: Li Rongqing
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 2
Building Chengdu Thermal Power Plant
    A Chengdu thermal power plant equipped with electronic automatic control equipment has begun construction next to the newly built Chengdu Power Plant. The power generation capacity of this power plant is equivalent to five times the current total power generation capacity of the Chengdu area. Li Rongqing (Contribution from Sichuan Daily)

### 兴建成都热电厂（图片）
李荣卿
1957-01-25
第2版()
专栏：

    兴建成都热电厂
    一座装有电子自动控制设备的成都热电厂在新建的成都电厂旁动工兴建。这个电厂的发电能力相当于现在成都地区发电能力总和的五倍。　李荣卿摄（四川日报社稿）
