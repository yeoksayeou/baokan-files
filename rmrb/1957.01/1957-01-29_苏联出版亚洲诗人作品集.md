Title: Soviet Union Publishes Anthology of Asian Poets

Author: 
Date: 1957-01-29
Page: 5

    Soviet Union Publishes Anthology of Asian Poets
    (Xinhua News Agency) The Soviet Literary Books Publishing House has published "Anthology of Asian Poets."
    This anthology collects the works of 230 poets from Afghanistan, Burma, Vietnam, India, Jordan, China, Korea, Lebanon, Malaya, Mongolia, Nepal, Syria and other countries.

### 苏联出版亚洲诗人作品集

1957-01-29
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联出版亚洲诗人作品集
    据新华社讯　苏联文艺书籍出版局出版了“亚洲诗人作品集”。
    这部诗集收集了阿富汗、缅甸、越南、印度、约旦、中国、朝鲜、黎巴嫩、马来亚、蒙古、尼泊尔、叙利亚等国家的二百三十位诗人的作品。
