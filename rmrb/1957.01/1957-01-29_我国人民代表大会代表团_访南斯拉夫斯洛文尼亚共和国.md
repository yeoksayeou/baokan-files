Title: Chinese National People's Congress Delegation Visits the Socialist Republic of Slovenia, Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1957-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ljubljana, January 28th. The Chinese National People's Congress delegation, headed by Peng Zhen, and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee delegation arrived in Ljubljana, the capital of the Socialist Republic of Slovenia, Yugoslavia, on the morning of the 26th for a visit.

Welcoming the Chinese guests at the train station were Kozak, Vice-President of the Slovenian National Assembly; Rus, Chairman of the Republic Chamber of the National Assembly; Krivic, member of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Slovenia and member of the Federal National Assembly; and Klemenčič, Vice-Chairman of the Ljubljana Municipal People's Committee, among others.

On that day, the delegation visited the Ljubljana Art Gallery and drove over sixty kilometers outside the city to Postojna to visit the world-famous stalactite caves. In the evening, the entire delegation met with Marinko, Chairman of the Slovenian National Assembly, Vice-Chairman Kozak, Tomšič, Secretary of the League of Communists of Slovenia, and other leaders of the republic's party, parliament, government, and prominent figures from all walks of life, more than eighty people in total, and held lengthy discussions on issues such as culture and education, healthcare, and communes.

On the morning of the 27th, the delegation visited the Ljubljana Municipal People's Committee, toured some cultural and social institutions, and attended a luncheon hosted by Marinko, Chairman of the Slovenian National Assembly. In the afternoon, the delegation returned to Belgrade by car.

### 我国人民代表大会代表团  访南斯拉夫斯洛文尼亚共和国

1957-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我国人民代表大会代表团
    访南斯拉夫斯洛文尼亚共和国
    新华社卢布尔雅那28日电　以彭真为首的中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团26日上午乘车到达南斯拉夫斯洛文尼亚人民共和国首都卢布尔雅那访问。
    在火车站欢迎中国客人的有斯洛文尼亚国民议会副主席科扎克、国民议会共和国院主席鲁斯、斯洛文尼亚共产主义者联盟中央委员和联邦国民议会议员克里维奇、卢布尔雅那市人民委员会副主席克累门集奇等人。
    代表团在这一天参观了卢布尔雅那市的画廊，并且驱车到市外六十多公里的波斯多伊纳参观世界闻名的钟乳石洞。当天晚上，代表团全体人员同斯洛文尼亚国民议会主席马林科、副主席科扎克、斯洛文尼亚共产主义者联盟组织书记托姆希奇，以及这个共和国的党、议会、政府等的其他负责人、社会各界名流等八十多人会面，就文化教育、卫生保健和公社等问题分别进行了长时间的座谈。
  27日上午，代表团访问了卢布尔雅那市人民委员会，参观了一些文化、社会机构，并且出席了斯洛文尼亚国民议会主席马林科的午宴。下午，代表团乘车返回贝尔格莱德。
