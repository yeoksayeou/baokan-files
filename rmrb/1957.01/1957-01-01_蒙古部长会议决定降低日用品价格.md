Title: Mongolian Council of Ministers Decides to Reduce Prices of Daily Necessities
Author:
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, 31st - The Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic has decided to reduce the prices of 413 daily necessities, effective January 1, 1957. The price reduction ranges from 20% to 80%.

Among the items with reduced prices are third-grade flour, millet, children's toys, and furniture. Among them, flour is reduced by 27%, and millet is reduced by 20%.

### 蒙古部长会议决定降低日用品价格

1957-01-01
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古部长会议决定降低日用品价格
    新华社乌兰巴托31日电　蒙古人民共和国部长会议决议从1957年1月1日起降低四百一十三种日用必需品的价格。价格降低的幅度从20％到80％。
    减价物品中有三等面粉、小米、儿童玩具和家具等，其中面粉减低27％，小米减低20％。
