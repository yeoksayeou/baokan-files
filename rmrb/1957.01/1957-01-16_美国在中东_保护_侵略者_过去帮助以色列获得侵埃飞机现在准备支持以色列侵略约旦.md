Title: US "Protects" Aggressors in the Middle East: Previously Helped Israel Obtain Planes to Invade Egypt, Now Prepares to Support Israel's Aggression Against Jordan
Author:
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 15th: TASS, Tel Aviv, January 14th: "Lamerhav" reported that at a recent meeting of the Central Committee of the United Labor Party of Palestine, Israeli Prime Minister Ben-Gurion, when discussing the preparation for the "Sinai Campaign" (i.e., the invasion of Egypt), said that some of the military equipment Israel obtained from NATO countries, especially from France, was with the approval of the United States. He said: "In April 1956, we did not obtain the consent of the United States, and it took a lot of effort to get the first batch of twelve 'Mystere' aircraft from France. However, after some time, with the knowledge of the United States, we obtained another twelve aircraft, this time with some American support."

Xinhua News Agency, January 15th: TASS, Tel Aviv, January 14th: According to the Washington correspondent of the Israeli "Haaretz," US government officials "are considering whether Israel's attempt to occupy the West Bank of Jordan is possible." The reporter continued, saying that undoubtedly, American government officials do not like the current Jordanian government.

The following situation deserves attention: Recently, the Israeli Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a series of statements condemning Jordan, insisting that armed "commandos" were based in Jordan and carrying out activities against Israel. As everyone knows, Israeli officials used the same condemnation as a pretext for attacking Egypt.

It is intriguing that influential groups in the United States are supporting the plan to partition Jordan precisely at a time when the State Department is trying to convince Arab public opinion that the purpose of the "Eisenhower Doctrine" is to ensure the national independence and territorial integrity of Arab countries.

### 美国在中东“保护”侵略者  过去帮助以色列获得侵埃飞机现在准备支持以色列侵略约旦

1957-01-16
第6版()
专栏：

    美国在中东“保护”侵略者
    过去帮助以色列获得侵埃飞机现在准备支持以色列侵略约旦
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社特拉维夫14日讯：“拉麦哈夫报”报道，在巴勒斯坦统一劳工党中央委员会最近举行的会议上，以色列总理本—古里安在谈到关于准备“西奈半岛战役”（即侵略埃及）时说，以色列从北大西洋集团国家得到的军备，特别是从法国得到的军备，有一部分是经过美国同意的。他说：“在1956年4月，我们没有获得美国的同意，费了很大劲才从法国得到第一批十二架“神秘式”飞机。但是，过了一些时候，我们在美国与闻其事的情况下又得到十二架飞机，这次在某种程度上是得到美国支持的。”
    据新华社15日讯　塔斯社特拉维夫14日讯：据以色列“国土报”驻华盛顿记者报道，美国政府人士“正在考虑以色列企图侵占约旦西部是否可能”。记者接着说，毫无疑问，美国的政府人士是不喜欢现在的约旦政府的。
    下述情况值得人们重视：最近以色列外交部发表了一连串声明来谴责约旦，硬说武装的“突击队”以约旦为根据地，进行反对以色列的活动。大家知道，以色列人士曾用同样的谴责作为进攻埃及的借口。
    美国有影响的集团恰恰要在国务院企图使阿拉伯国家的公众舆论相信“艾森豪威尔主义”的目的是保证阿拉伯国家的民族独立和领土完整的当儿，支持分割约旦的计划，这是耐人寻味的。
