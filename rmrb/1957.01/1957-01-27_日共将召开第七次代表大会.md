Title: Japanese Communist Party to Convene Seventh Congress
Author:
Date: 1957-01-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, January 26th, TASS dispatch: According to the "Akahata" newspaper, the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party held a plenary session on January 21st to discuss the party's policies in the field of culture and to strengthen the activities of the party's local organizations.

In the resolution adopted at the meeting, specific goals and tasks were put forward to all party members regarding the upcoming Seventh Party Congress. The meeting called on party organizations across the country to actively carry out activities, actively develop new party members, and expand the readership of the party's newspaper "Akahata." The meeting also adopted some other resolutions. (Attached picture)

On January 16th, Japanese students demonstrated in front of the U.S. Embassy in Tokyo, protesting U.S. interference in internal affairs and tyrannical rule in Okinawa, and protesting Eisenhower's Middle East policy. (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 日共将召开第七次代表大会

1957-01-27
第6版()
专栏：

    日共将召开第七次代表大会
    新华社26日讯　塔斯社东京讯：据“赤旗报”报道，日本共产党中央委员会在1月21日举行了全体会议，讨论了党在文化方面的政策问题和加强党的地方组织活动的问题。
    会议在通过的决议中就即将召开第七次党代表大会一事向全体党员提出了具体的目的和任务。会议号召全国党组织积极展开活动，积极发展新党员，扩大党的机关报“赤旗报”的读者。会议还通过了其他一些决议。（附图片）
    日本学生1月16日在东京美国大使馆门前示威，抗议美国在冲绳岛干涉内政、暴虐统制，并抗议艾森豪威尔的中东政策。　（新华社稿）
