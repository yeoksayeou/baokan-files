Title: Premier Zhou Bids Farewell to Kabul, Arrives in Kandahar; Ding Guoyu Hosts Reception for Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, January 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their party left Kabul by plane this morning for Kandahar, an important city in southern Afghanistan. Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan, Ding Guoyu, accompanied them.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Afghan Prime Minister Daud, Deputy Prime Ministers Ali Mohammed and Naim, cabinet ministers, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Afghanistan.

In his farewell speech (issued separately), Premier Zhou Enlai expressed his gratitude to the Afghan government and people for the warm welcome and sincere hospitality extended to him and all members of his party.

Prime Minister Daud asked Premier Zhou Enlai to convey to the Chinese people the Afghan people's sincere wishes for China's happiness and prosperity, and to convey his personal respect and greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong. He said he was confident that the Chinese Premier's visit would leave an indelible impression in the memories of his new friends in Afghanistan.

Xinhua News Agency, Kandahar, January 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their party arrived at the beautifully decorated Kandahar Airport at 11:30 this morning, flying from Kabul.

The Governor of Kandahar Province, Abdughani, welcomed the Chinese guests next to the airplane ladder. He said that the people of Kandahar had been waiting for the arrival of the Chinese Premier and Vice Premier for a long time. He said: "I believe your visit will help strengthen the friendship between China and Afghanistan."

In his reply, Premier Zhou Enlai expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome given by the people of this beautiful city. He said: "Afghanistan has a long tradition of struggle against colonialism. Your city is also a city that once engaged in anti-aggression wars. We would like to pay special tribute to your city."

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long reviewed the guard of honor. After boys in national costumes presented bouquets, a salvo of artillery fire immediately sounded, and the band played the national anthems of China and Afghanistan.

As the guests rode in cars through the welcoming crowd, people applauded warmly and waved flags. The ten-kilometer road leading to the governor's residence, where the Chinese guests were staying, was lined with people.

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, January 23rd: Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan, Ding Guoyu, held a reception in Kabul on the evening of the 22nd for Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their party's visit to Afghanistan.

Those invited to the reception included Afghan Prime Minister Daud, Deputy Prime Minister Ali Mohammed, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Mohammed Naim, cabinet ministers, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Afghanistan. Two uncles of the Afghan King, Marshal Mahmoud and Marshal Wali, were also invited to attend the reception. A total of one hundred and fifty people attended the reception. The reception ended at ten o'clock, and was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere throughout.

### 周总理告别喀布尔到达坎大哈  丁国钰为周总理举行招待会

1957-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理告别喀布尔到达坎大哈
    丁国钰为周总理举行招待会
    据新华社喀布尔23日电　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理等一行在今天上午乘飞机离开喀布尔前往阿富汗南部的重要城市坎大哈。中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰陪同前往。
    到机场欢送的有阿富汗首相达乌德，副首相阿里·穆罕默德和纳伊姆，内阁各部大臣和各国驻阿富汗外交使节。
    周恩来总理在告别词（另发）中对阿富汗政府和阿富汗人民给予他以及和他同行的全体人员的热烈欢迎和真诚接待表示感谢。
    达乌德首相请周恩来总理向中国人民转达阿富汗人民希望中国幸福繁荣的真诚祝愿，并且向毛泽东主席转达他个人的敬意和问候。他说，他确信中国总理的这次访问一定会在他在阿富汗新交的朋友的记忆中留下不可磨灭的印象。
    据新华社坎大哈23日电　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理等在今天上午十一时三十分乘飞机从喀布尔到达装饰得十分美丽的坎大哈飞机场。
    坎大哈省省长阿卜杜加尼在飞机的扶梯旁边欢迎中国客人。他说，坎大哈人民早就在等待着中国总理和副总理的到来。他说：“我相信，你们的访问将会有助于加强中国和阿富汗之间的友谊。”
    周恩来总理在致答词的时候表示感谢这个美丽城市的人民所给予的热烈欢迎。他说：“阿富汗有长期反对殖民主义的斗争传统。你们的城市也是一个曾经进行反侵略战争的城市。我们愿意特别向你们这个城市致敬。”
    周恩来总理和贺龙副总理检阅了仪仗队。在穿着民族服装的男孩子献了花束以后，立即传来了隆隆的礼炮声，乐队奏起了中阿两国的国歌。
    当客人们乘汽车经过欢迎的人群的时候，人们热烈鼓掌，并且挥动着旗子。在通往中国客人的住处的省长官邸的十公里的道路上，两旁挤满了人。
    新华社喀布尔23日电　中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰为周恩来总理和贺龙副总理等一行访问阿富汗，22日晚上在喀布尔举行招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的有阿富汗首相达乌德、副首相阿里·穆罕默德、副首相兼外交大臣穆罕默德·纳伊姆，内阁大臣们和各国驻阿富汗外交使节。阿富汗国王的两位叔父马茂德元帅和华利元帅也应邀出席了招待会。出席招待会的共有一百五十人。招待会在十点钟结束，一直充满了热烈友好的气氛。
