    Xia Wanchun's Tomb

    Some time ago, farmers in Songjiang County, Jiangsu Province, accidentally discovered the tomb of Xia Wanchun. Xia Wanchun was a national hero of the late Ming Dynasty. He knew the Five Classics at the age of five, excelled in poetry and prose at the age of nine, joined the army at the age of fifteen, and died for his country at the age of seventeen.

### 夏完淳的坟墓

1957-01-29
第7版()
专栏：新发现的文物

    夏完淳的坟墓
    前些时候，江苏省松江县农民无意中发现夏完淳之墓。夏完淳是明末的民族英雄。他五岁知五经，九岁善词赋古文，十五岁从军，十七岁殉国。
