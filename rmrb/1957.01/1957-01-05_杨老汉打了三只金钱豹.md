Title: Old Man Yang Trapped Three Leopards
Author: Guo Fuxiang
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 3

Hunting Activities

    Old Man Yang Trapped Three Leopards
    Xiawanzi in Tiantaiyong Township, Salinghe District, Keshiketeng Banner, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, is a forested area with dense trees and lush weeds. There are many wild animals here, and leopards often appear near the village. Therefore, the villagers are very worried. Old Man Yang, a man in his fifties who lives in this village, tried to trap a wildcat with a cangue trap the year before last. In October of last year, Old Man Yang continued trapping. So far, he has trapped three very large and fierce leopards, eliminating a major hazard for the local people and also engaging in sideline production. (Guo Fuxiang)

### 杨老汉打了三只金钱豹
郭福祥
1957-01-05
第3版()
专栏：打猎活动

    杨老汉打了三只金钱豹
    内蒙古自治区克什克腾旗萨岭河区天太永乡的下湾子，是一个树林茂密、杂草丛生的林区。这里，野牲口很多，豹子又经常在营子附近出现。因此，老乡们都很担心。住在这个营子的一位五十多岁的杨老汉，曾在前年用枷子枷的办法，试枷了一只土豹子。去年10月，杨老汉又继续枷。到目前为止，他已枷住了三只很大很凶恶的金钱豹，给当地人民除了大害，也搞了副业生产。　　　（郭福祥）
