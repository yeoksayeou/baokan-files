Title: Ambassador Liu Xiao Holds Banquet for Chinese Government Delegation's Visit to the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th — Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, held a grand banquet at the "Soviet" Hotel on the evening of the 17th for the Chinese government delegation's visit to the Soviet Union.

　　Nearly seven hundred guests gathered in the brightly lit hall. Among the distinguished guests were Soviet Communist Party and government leaders Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Saburov, Khrushchev, Mukhitdinov, Shepilov, Shvernik, and others.

　　Also attending the banquet were Tarasov and Sareyev, Deputy Chairmen of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Lobanov, Chairman of the Union Council, Komarov, Deputy Chairman of the National Council, as well as Soviet generals, writers, actors, social activists, heads of diplomatic missions to the Soviet Union, and Soviet and foreign journalists.

　　Famous figures from all walks of life in Moscow were invited to meet with the Chinese government delegation.

　　Also attending the reception were a group of delegates from the Chinese National People's Congress delegation, led by Deputy Head Cheng Qian, who are currently in Moscow.

　　The banquet proceeded very warmly. Premier Zhou Enlai first proposed a toast to the health and longevity of Chairman Voroshilov, Chairman Bulganin, and Comrade Khrushchev, and to the upcoming 40th anniversary of the victory of the October Socialist Revolution. Premier Zhou Enlai also said "Cheers" in Russian.

　　Chairman Bulganin proposed a toast to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai, and to the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples. He said "Cheers" in Chinese.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai also spoke warmly with a large group of foreign journalists surrounding him, inviting them to taste the Chinese specialty, Moutai liquor. Premier Zhou Enlai also had cordial conversations with diplomatic envoys.

　　Khrushchev then proposed a toast to the Chinese Communist Party, to the eternal friendship between the Chinese and Soviet Communist Parties, to the victory of Marxism-Leninism, and to Comrade Mao Zedong, Comrade Liu Shaoqi, and other leaders of the Politburo of the CPC Central Committee.

　　The banquet ended at half-past ten. Before the end, Ambassador Liu Xiao expressed his gratitude to all the guests attending the banquet.

### 为我国政府代表团访苏  刘晓大使举行宴会

1957-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    为我国政府代表团访苏
    刘晓大使举行宴会
    新华社莫斯科18日电　中国驻苏联大使刘晓17日晚间为中国政府代表团访苏在“苏维埃”旅馆举行盛大宴会。
    在灯火辉煌的大厅里聚集了将近七百位客人。在贵宾中有苏联共产党和政府的领导人布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、穆希金诺夫、谢皮洛夫、什维尔尼克等同志。
    出席宴会的还有苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席塔拉索夫和萨雷耶夫、联盟院主席洛巴诺夫、民族院副主席科马罗夫，以及苏联将军们、作家们、演员们、社会活动家们，各国驻苏联使节，苏联和外国记者们。
    莫斯科各界著名人物都应邀来和中国政府代表团会见。
    出席招待会的还有目前在莫斯科的由副团长程潜率领的中国全国人民代表大会代表团的一批代表。
    宴会进行十分热烈。周恩来总理首先提议为伏罗希洛夫主席、布尔加宁主席和赫鲁晓夫同志的健康和长寿干杯，为即将到来的十月社会主义革命胜利四十周年干杯。周恩来总理并且用俄语说“干杯”。
    布尔加宁主席提议为毛泽东主席、周恩来总理的健康，为中苏两国人民的友谊干杯。他用中国话说“干杯”。
    周恩来总理并和一大群围绕着他的外国记者们热烈交谈，请他们尝中国特产茅台酒。周恩来总理并和外交使节们进行了亲切的谈话。
    赫鲁晓夫接着提议为中国共产党、为中苏两国共产党永恒的友谊、为马克思列宁主义的胜利、为毛泽东同志、刘少奇同志等中共中央政治局的领导人干杯。
    宴会在十时半结束。在结束之前，刘晓大使向出席宴会的全体客人表示感谢。
