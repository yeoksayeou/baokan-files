Title: Indonesia and Egypt's Friendship is Eternal; Sukarno Expresses Eternal Support for Egypt
Author:
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 24th: Indonesian President Sukarno said on the 23rd that the friendship between the Indonesian and Egyptian nations is eternal, because they have established close relations as two nations in struggle.

Sukarno also said that the Indonesian people will continue to support the Egyptian people.

At a banquet held by the Egyptian Ambassador to Indonesia, Anrosi, on the 23rd, Sukarno also expressed his hope to visit Egypt again and stay longer. Antara News Agency said that if nothing else happens, Sukarno will visit some Asian and African countries in May this year, and will also visit South American countries.

### 印度尼西亚和埃及友谊是永恒的  苏加诺表示永远支持埃及

1957-01-25
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和埃及友谊是永恒的
    苏加诺表示永远支持埃及
    新华社雅加达24日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺23日说，印度尼西亚和埃及两个民族之间的友谊是永恒的，这是因为他们作为两个斗争中的民族建立了密切的关系。
    苏加诺还说，印度尼西亚人民将继续支援埃及人民。
    苏加诺23日在埃及驻印度尼西亚大使安罗西举行的宴会上还表示希望再次到埃及访问，并且逗留得更长久些。安塔拉通讯社说，如果没有其他事情，苏加诺将在今年5月访问一些亚非国家，并且将访问南美国家。
