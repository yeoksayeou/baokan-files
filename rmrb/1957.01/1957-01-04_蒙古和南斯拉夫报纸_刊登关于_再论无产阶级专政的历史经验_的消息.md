Title: Mongolian and Yugoslav Newspapers Publish News About "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship"
Author:
Date: 1957-01-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, January 3rd: The Mongolian "Unen" (Truth) newspaper published a news report on the front page on January 1st about the "People's Daily" publishing the article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship."

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 3rd: Belgrade News: The Yugoslav newspapers "Borba" (Struggle) and "Politika" both published news about the "People's Daily" publishing the article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" on December 30th. The news reports published by both newspapers mentioned the titles of the four questions discussed in the "People's Daily" article, and pointed out that the content of the article involved the speeches of Tito and Kadar.

### 蒙古和南斯拉夫报纸  刊登关于“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”的消息

1957-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古和南斯拉夫报纸
    刊登关于“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”的消息
    新华社乌兰巴托3日电　蒙古“真理报”1月1日在第一版上刊登了“人民日报”发表“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”文章的消息。
    新华社布拉格3日电　贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫“战斗报”和“政治报”都在12月30日刊登了“人民日报”发表“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文的消息。两报发表的消息都提到“人民日报”文章中所论述的四个问题的标题，并且指出文章内容涉及到铁托和卡德尔的演说。
