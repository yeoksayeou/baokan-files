Title: Egypt, Syria to Resume Negotiations on Establishing a Federation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 7th: Syrian Prime Minister al-Asali said on January 5th that the Syrian government will soon appoint a ministerial committee to begin negotiations with Egypt on the establishment of a federation between Syria and Egypt.

Last July, the Syrian Parliament requested the government to take steps to establish a federation with Egypt. The initial talks with Egypt were interrupted by the Anglo-French aggression.

### 埃叙将恢复建立联邦谈判

1957-01-08
第6版()
专栏：

    埃叙将恢复建立联邦谈判
    新华社7日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总理阿萨利1月5日说，叙利亚政府不久将任命一个部长委员会来开始谈判叙利亚和埃及建立联邦问题。
    去年7月，叙利亚议会要求政府采取和埃及建立联邦的步骤。同埃及的初步会谈由于英法的侵略而中断。
