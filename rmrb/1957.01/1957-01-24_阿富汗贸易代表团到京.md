Title: Afghan Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd—The Afghan Royal Government Trade Delegation, led by Head of Delegation Mohammad Rasul Yunosi, arrived in Beijing today by plane from Shanghai. The delegation of four arrived in Guangzhou on the 8th and visited Jingdezhen, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places.

### 阿富汗贸易代表团到京

1957-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗贸易代表团到京
    新华社23日讯　阿富汗王国政府贸易代表团在团长穆罕默德·拉苏勒·尤诺西率领下，今日从上海乘飞机到达北京。代表团一行四人在8日到达广州后，曾参观了景德镇、上海和杭州等地。
