Title: Paulist English Newspaper Publishes “Once More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat”; Indonesian and Austrian Newspapers Publish Related News

Author: 
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, January 4th. The Bulgarian newspaper "Workers' Cause" published the full text of "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" on January 2nd. "Fatherland Front", "Labor", "People's Youth", "Agricultural Banner", and "Cooperative Agriculture" also published the full text of this article on January 3rd.

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, January 4th. The Romanian newspapers "Spark", "Free Romania", "Labor", "Youth Spark", and "Bucharest News" today published excerpts of the "People's Daily" article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat."

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, January 3rd. The Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee organ, "Spark," published a prominent news item on page four on December 31st regarding the publication of the "People's Daily" article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat." The headline of the news item was: "The 'People's Daily,' the organ of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Publishes an Article of Utmost Importance to the Communist Movement."

    Xinhua News Agency, London, January 4th. At the request of a large number of readers, the British "Daily Worker" published a lengthy summary of the Chinese "People's Daily" article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" in three installments on January 1st, 2nd, and 4th.

    Prior to this, the "Daily Worker" had published on December 29th on the front page, in a half-column space, an introduction to the main points of the article by its reporter Alan Winnington from Beijing.

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 4th. The Indonesian "People's Daily" published news of the "People's Daily" article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" on January 3rd.

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 4th. The Austrian Communist Party organ, "Austrian People's Voice," published a short news item on the front page on December 29th regarding the publication of the "People's Daily" article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat." Its headline was: "Important Statement by the Chinese Communist Party – Statement on the Discussion Concerning the International Workers' Movement."

### 保罗英报纸刊登“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”  印度尼西亚和奥地利报纸发表有关的消息

1957-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    保罗英报纸刊登“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”
    印度尼西亚和奥地利报纸发表有关的消息
    新华社索非亚4日电　保加利亚“工人事业报”2日登载了“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”的全文，“祖国阵线报”、“劳动报”、“人民青年报”、“农业旗帜报”和“合作农业报”也在3日全文登载了这篇文章。
    新华社布加勒斯特4日电　罗马尼亚“火花报”、“自由罗马尼亚报”、“劳动报”、“青年火花报”、“布加勒斯特消息报”今天摘要登载了“人民日报”的文章“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”。
    新华社布加勒斯特3日电　罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会机关报“火花报”12月31日在第四版以显著地位登载了该报编辑部关于我“人民日报”发表“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文的消息。消息的标题是：中国共产党中央委员会机关报“人民日报”发表了一篇对共产主义运动具有极其重要意义的文章。
    新华社伦敦4日电　英国“工人日报”在广大读者请求下，在1月1日、2日和4日的报纸上分三次刊登了中国“人民日报”的“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文的长篇摘要。
    在这之前，“工人日报”去年12月29日曾经在第一版以半栏地位刊登了它的记者阿兰·魏宁顿从北京发出的对这篇文章的要点的介绍。
    新华社雅加达4日电　印度尼西亚“人民日报”1月3日刊登了中国“人民日报”发表“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”文章的消息。
    新华社柏林4日电　奥地利共产党的机关报“奥地利人民之声报”12月29日在第一版以显著地位刊登了关于“人民日报”发表“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文的简短消息。它的标题是：“中国共产党的重要声明——就有关国际工人运动的讨论的声明”。
