Title: Revolutionary Struggle Art Film Exhibition Month Opens in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1957-01-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The opening ceremony of the "Revolutionary Struggle Art Film Exhibition Month" was held today at the Guanghe Theater in Beijing, with more than 1,500 young people participating. This exhibition will run from January 10th to February 10th, continuously screening more than twenty excellent films in Beijing. This exhibition month is jointly sponsored by the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Culture, the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Education, the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League.

### 革命斗争艺术影片展览月在北京举行开幕式

1957-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    革命斗争艺术影片展览月在北京举行开幕式
    据新华社6日讯　“革命斗争艺术影片展览月”今天在北京广和剧场举行了开幕式，有一千五百多青年参加。这个展览从1月10日开始到2月10日结束，连续在北京上映二十多部优秀影片。这个展览月是北京市文化局、北京市教育局、北京市工会联合会、青年团北京市委员会联合举办的。
