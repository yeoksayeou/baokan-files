Title: When Western Countries Refused to Help Egypt, the Soviet Union Truly Helped Egypt - Nasser's Conversation with the Editor of the Greek "National Daily"
Author:
Date: 1957-01-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 17th, Cairo News: Egyptian President Nasser emphasized during a private meeting with Constantine Kouriatzis, editor of the Greek "National Daily," on the 16th, that when Western countries refused to help Egypt, the Soviet Union truly helped Egypt.

Nasser said that when the West wanted to internationalize the Suez Canal, Russia supported our point of view. When we were attacked, Russia issued warnings to the West. When we requested wheat, and other countries refused, the Russians gave it to us. He said, "These are the reasons why the Egyptian people are deeply grateful."

Nasser pointed out: "When the Russians helped us, the United States froze $50 million of Egypt's money, and Britain froze 150 million pounds. Twenty days ago, we asked the West to provide wheat, but they refused, demanding payment in dollars."

Nasser said, "This does not mean that I am pro-East or pro-West. I am pro-Egypt, and we want complete independence."

Referring to the U.S. Middle East policy, Nasser said, "We have studied the Eisenhower Doctrine and have asked the U.S. government to explain some of its ambiguous parts."

Kouriatzis asked Nasser to state his views on the future of Egypt's relations with Britain and France. Nasser said, "Everything depends on resolving the outstanding issues between Egypt and Britain and France." He said, "The Israelis are still in our country. How can we think about establishing relations?"

Kouriatzis asked Nasser what attitude the Egyptians would take if the Israelis did not leave Egyptian territory. Nasser replied, "The UN resolution must be fully implemented. The Israelis must leave Egyptian territory and retreat behind the demarcation line, because we have agreed to clear the Suez Canal. If they do not leave, there will be new trouble."

Kouriatzis asked what Egypt's Suez Canal policy was. Nasser replied, "We oppose internationalization. The Suez Canal is on our territory. What we nationalized was the Suez Canal Company, not the Suez Canal."

Kouriatzis asked if Israeli ships would be allowed to pass through the canal. Nasser said, "We believe that this issue is part of the Palestine problem." He said, "The 1888 treaty (the international convention on freedom of navigation of the canal) allows Egypt to take all steps to defend the security of the country."

Kouriatzis asked Nasser what he thought of the United Nations. He replied, "I value the UN's position. I did not trust their power, but after the aggression (against Egypt) occurred, I believed that they had power. They worked well, and they insisted on a ceasefire."

Referring to domestic issues, Nasser said, "Our main task now is to reorganize our country and implement the social policies in the new constitution." He said, "Elections will be held after the current difficulties are resolved, because we are now concentrating all the power of the country to cope with these difficulties." He said, "There are no plans to reorganize the government at present."

Kouriatzis asked Nasser about Egypt's attitude towards the Cyprus issue. Nasser said, "Our opinion on the Cyprus issue is very simple—to implement self-determination. After self-determination, the people can decide whether to agree to NATO establishing a base on the island. But I don't think they will ever agree to allow others to use these bases against us. Under the current regime, Cyprus is a base used for aggression."

### 当西方国家拒绝帮助埃及的时候  苏联真正帮助了埃及  纳赛尔对希腊“民族日报”编辑发表谈话

1957-01-18
第6版()
专栏：

    当西方国家拒绝帮助埃及的时候
    苏联真正帮助了埃及
    纳赛尔对希腊“民族日报”编辑发表谈话
    新华社17日讯　开罗消息：埃及总统纳赛尔16日在单独接见希腊“民族日报”编辑康斯坦丁·库里亚齐斯时强调指出，当西方国家拒绝帮助埃及的时候，苏联真正帮助了埃及。
    纳赛尔说，当西方要把苏伊士运河国际化的时候，俄国支持了我们的观点。当我们受到进攻的时候，俄国对西方发出了警告。当我们要求小麦，而其他国家拒绝的时候，俄国人给了我们。他说：“这些就是埃及人民热烈感激的原因。”
    纳赛尔指出：“当俄国人帮助我们的时候，美国冻结了埃及的五千万美元，英国冻结了埃及的一亿五千万英镑，我们在二十天以前要求西方提供小麦，但是他们拒绝了，他们要求用美元支付。”
    纳赛尔说：“这并不是说，我亲东方，或者亲西方。我是亲埃及，我们要完全独立。”
    在谈到美国的中东政策时，纳赛尔说：“我们已经研究了艾森豪威尔主义，并且已经要求美国政府解释其中一些含糊的部分。”
    库里亚齐斯请纳赛尔说明他对埃及同英、法关系的前途的看法。纳赛尔说：“一切都取决于解决埃及同英国和法国之间的悬而未决的问题。”他说：“以色列人仍然在我们的国内。怎么能想到建立关系的问题呢？”
    库里亚齐斯问纳赛尔，如果以色列人不离开埃及领土，埃及人将采取什么态度。纳赛尔回答说：“联合国的决议必须彻底履行。以色列人必须离开埃及国土，退到分界线后面去，因为我们已经同意清理苏伊士运河。如果他们不离开，那就会发生新的麻烦。”
    库里亚齐斯问，什么是埃及的苏伊士运河政策。纳赛尔回答说：“我们反对国际化，苏伊士运河在我们的领土上。我们收归国有的是苏伊士运河公司，不是苏伊士运河。”
    库里亚齐斯问，是否允许以色列船只通过运河。纳赛尔说：“我们认为，这个问题是巴勒斯坦问题的一部分。”他说：“1888年条约（关于运河通航自由的国际公约）允许埃及采取一切步骤来保卫国家的安全。”
    库里亚齐斯问纳赛尔对联合国的看法如何。他回答说：“我重视联合国的立场。我不信任他们的权力，但是在（对埃及的）侵略发生以后，我相信他们是有力量的。他们工作得很好，他们坚持了停火。”
    在谈到内政问题的时候，纳赛尔说：“我们现在的主要任务是重新组织我们的国家，实行新宪法中的社会政策。”他说：“选举要在解决了目前的困难以后举行，因为我们现在正在集中国家的一切力量来应付这些困难。”他说：“目前没有改组政府的打算。”
    库里亚齐斯问纳赛尔关于埃及对塞浦路斯问题的态度。纳赛尔说：“我们对塞浦路斯问题的意见很简单——实行自决。在自决以后，人民可以决定是否同意北大西洋公约组织在这个岛上建立基地。但是我认为，他们决不会同意让别人利用这些基地来反对我们。在目前的政权下，塞浦路斯是一个用来进行侵略的基地。”
