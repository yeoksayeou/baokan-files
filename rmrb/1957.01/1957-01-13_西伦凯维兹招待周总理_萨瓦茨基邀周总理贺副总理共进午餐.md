Title: Cyrankiewicz Hosts Premier Zhou; Zawadzki Invites Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He to Lunch

Author:
Date: 1957-01-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 12th: Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz held a grand reception on the evening of the 11th in the Hall of the Council of Ministers to host Zhou Enlai, head of the government delegation of the People's Republic of China and Premier of the State Council.

First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party (PUWP) Gomulka, Chairman of the Polish Council of State and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the PUWP Zawadzki, Speaker of the Parliament Dąbrowski, Vice Chairman of the Council of State and Chairman of the Democratic Party Kulczyński, and all other members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the PUWP attended the reception.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Poland were also invited to attend the reception.

Cyrankiewicz and Zhou Enlai spoke at the reception. (See page 4)

Subsequently, Gomulka also spoke. (See page 4)

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 12th: Polish Chairman of the Council of State Zawadzki invited Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long to lunch today in the Hall of the Council of State.

Members of the Chinese government delegation Wang Jiaxiang, Chinese Ambassador to Poland Wang Bingnan, and delegation staff members Qiao Guanhua, Xiong Fu, Zhang Yan, and Gong Peng were also invited to attend the lunch.

### 西伦凯维兹招待周总理  萨瓦茨基邀周总理贺副总理共进午餐

1957-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹招待周总理
    萨瓦茨基邀周总理贺副总理共进午餐
    新华社华沙12日电　波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹11日晚间在部长会议大厅举行盛大招待会，招待中华人民共和国政府代表团团长、国务院总理周恩来。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡，波兰国务委员会主席、波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员萨瓦茨基，议会议长邓博夫斯基，国务委员会副主席、民主党主席库尔钦斯基以及波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局所有其他委员，都出席了招待会。
    各国驻波兰外交使节也应邀参加了招待会。
    西伦凯维兹和周恩来在招待会上讲了话。（见第四版）
    接着，哥穆尔卡也讲了话。（见第四版）
    新华社华沙12日电　波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基今天邀请周恩来总理和贺龙副总理在国务委员会大厅共进午餐。
    中国政府代表团团员王稼祥、中国驻波兰大使王炳南和代表团随员乔冠华、熊复、张彦、龚澎也应邀参加了午餐。
