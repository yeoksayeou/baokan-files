Title: Dulles is Prescribing Wrong Medicine in Diplomacy; Democratic Senator Says a New Doctor is Needed
Author:
Date: 1957-01-29
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 28th -- Three influential Democratic senators have condemned Secretary of State Dulles's methods in executing American foreign policy.
    Senator Humphrey, a member of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee, advocated for an investigation into Dulles's execution of foreign policy during a meeting with reporters on the 27th. Humphrey said, "We have been given the wrong prescription, and I am afraid we need to change doctors." He also said, "Dulles has not diagnosed the (Middle East) situation or described it correctly. Dulles and the policies he pursues are increasingly losing trust. There is a growing sense that Dulles may very well become a cold war casualty, like Eden."
    Senators Sparkman and Kefauver supported Humphrey's criticism of Dulles.

### 杜勒斯在外交上乱开药方  民主党参议员说需要换医生

1957-01-29
第6版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯在外交上乱开药方
    民主党参议员说需要换医生
    据新华社28日讯　华盛顿消息：三个有势力的美国民主党参议员谴责国务卿杜勒斯执行美国外交政策的方法。
    参议院外交委员会委员汉弗来27日在接见新闻记者时主张对杜勒斯执行外交政策的情况进行调查。汉弗来说：“我们被开给了一个错误的药方，恐怕我们需要换一个医生”。他又说：“杜勒斯没有诊断出（中东）局势或者正确地说明它。杜勒斯和他所奉行的政策越来越失去信任。有一种越来越增长的感觉认为，杜勒斯很可能像艾登一样，成为一个冷战的伤号”。
    参议员斯巴克曼和克弗维尔支持汉弗来对杜勒斯的抨击。
