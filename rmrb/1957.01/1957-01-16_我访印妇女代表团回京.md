Title: Chinese Women's Delegation to India Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 15th: The Chinese Women's Delegation to India, led by Head of Delegation Shi Liang, returned to Beijing this afternoon from Kunming by plane.

### 我访印妇女代表团回京

1957-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我访印妇女代表团回京
    新华社15日讯　访问印度的中国妇女代表团，在史良团长率领下，已于今天下午从昆明乘飞机返回北京。
