Title: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Arrive in Himachal Pradesh, India for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1957-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 6th: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni and their party arrived in Mandi, a beautiful mountain city in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, on the southern slopes of the Himalayas, on the evening of January 5th. They were warmly welcomed by tens of thousands of people standing on the streets surrounding the hillside. The welcomers shouted slogans such as "Hindi Chini bhai bhai" (Indians and Chinese are brothers).

The guests arrived by train in Pathankot, Punjab in the morning, and then traveled by car along the snow-capped Dhauladhar range of the Himalayas to Mandi. On the 140-mile journey, almost every town and village had erected arches and hung flags. Mountain people dressed in various colors crowded the roads, beating drums and blowing curved long horns. They showered the guests' cars with flowers and garlands.

The Lieutenant Governor of Himachal Pradesh, Bajrang Bahadur Singh, the Chairman of the Mandi Municipal Committee, Tej Singh, and other local officials and prominent figures welcomed the guests on the lawn of the Governor's Residence and presented hadas. Tej Singh delivered a welcoming speech. Both the Dalai Lama and the Panchen thanked their hosts for their warm welcome and wished the people of Mandi happiness and prosperity.

In the evening, the Governor hosted a banquet to welcome the guests. He also presented the Dalai Lama and Panchen with two ivory carvings of Sakyamuni Buddha and other gifts.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼到达印度喜马偕尔邦访问

1957-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼到达印度喜马偕尔邦访问
    新华社新德里6日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼一行在1月5日晚上到达喜马拉雅山南麓印度喜马偕尔邦的美丽山城曼地。他们受到站在环绕着山坡的街道上的好几万人的热情欢迎。欢迎的人高呼“印地秦尼巴伊巴伊”（印中人民是兄弟）等口号。
    客人们在上午乘火车到达旁遮普邦的潘坦科特，然后乘车沿着喜马拉雅山支脉白雪皑皑的多拉达尔山到曼地。在一百四十英里长的旅途上，差不多每一个城镇和乡村都搭着牌坊，悬挂着旗帜。山民们穿着各种颜色的服装，拥挤在道路上，敲着鼓，吹着弯曲的长喇叭。他们把花和花环雨点般地向客人的车掷去。
    喜马偕尔邦副邦长巴季兰·巴杜尔·辛格、曼地市政委员会主席特季辛格，以及其他当地官员和著名人士在邦长府草地上欢迎客人，并献哈达。特季辛格致欢迎词。达赖和班禅都感谢主人的热情欢迎并且祝福曼地人民幸福繁荣。
    晚间，邦长设宴欢迎客人。他还赠给达赖和班禅两个象牙雕刻的释迦牟尼像和其他礼物。
