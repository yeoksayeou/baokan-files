Title: Zhukov Attends Indian Folk Dance Festival
Author:
Date: 1957-01-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, January 28th: According to TASS, Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov attended the Indian Folk Dance Festival held at the Delhi National Stadium on the afternoon of the 27th. When the Soviet guest appeared at the venue, everyone cheered and applauded to welcome him. Zhukov sat in the VIP seats next to Prime Minister Nehru.

    Marshal Zhukov watched the Indian folk dances with great interest. Prime Minister Nehru explained the content of the dances to him. Marshal Zhukov said, "It is very beneficial to hold these programs, as it will bring the various ethnic groups of the country closer together." Zhukov said that some Indian dances have very similar points to the dances of the Caucasian people and the Central Asian peoples of the Soviet Union.

    That morning, Zhukov attended the annual military sports groups performance held at the Delhi Stadium. Prime Minister Nehru, Chairman of the conference, extended a warm welcome to Zhukov.

### 朱可夫参加印度民间舞蹈节

1957-01-29
第5版()
专栏：

    朱可夫参加印度民间舞蹈节
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社德里讯：苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅27日下午参加了在德里国家运动场举行的印度民间舞蹈节。当这位苏联客人在会场上出现的时候，大家欢呼鼓掌表示欢迎。朱可夫坐在尼赫鲁总理旁边的贵宾席上。
    朱可夫元帅很感兴趣地观看了印度的民间舞蹈。尼赫鲁总理向他讲解舞蹈内容。朱可夫元帅说：“举行这些节目很有好处，它会使国内各族人民亲密起来。”朱可夫说，印度有些舞蹈同高加索民族和苏联的中亚细亚民族的舞蹈有很相同之点。
    这一天上午，朱可夫出席了在德里运动场上举行的一年一度的军事体育团体表演大会。大会主席尼赫鲁总理对朱可夫表示热烈欢迎。
