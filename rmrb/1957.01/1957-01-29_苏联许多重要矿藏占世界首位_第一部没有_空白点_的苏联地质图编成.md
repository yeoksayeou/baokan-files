Title: Soviet Union Ranks First in the World in Many Important Mineral Deposits; First Soviet Geological Map Without "Blank Spots" Compiled
Author:
Date: 1957-01-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th: Soviet Minister of Geology and Mineral Resources Protection, Antropov, said that the results of Soviet geological exploration work in recent years have confirmed that the Soviet Union's reserves of oil, iron, copper, manganese, lead, tungsten, and some other important mineral deposits rank first in the world. Coal reserves may also soon be ranked first in the world, because new, huge coal mining areas have recently been discovered successively in the Urals and the Soviet Far East.

In an article written for the Soviet "Literary Gazette" on the 26th, Antropov said that the achievements of Soviet geologists in finding raw materials for the uranium industry are also significant. Currently, they are searching for and exploring huge deposits of non-ferrous and rare metals in the Far East and Ukraine, which are of great significance for the development of electronic and atomic technologies.

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: TASS reported: The first Soviet geological map without "blank spots" has recently been completed. This is the result of years of hard work by Soviet geologists.

Soviet Deputy Minister of Geology and Mineral Resources Protection, Belyaevs, said that this geological map uses about one hundred and seventy shades of color and other symbols to represent the composition, age, and geological structure of various strata on the Earth's surface. Now, there are no unexplored areas in the Soviet Union.

According to this new geological map, geological exploration teams will be able to more purposefully explore for non-ferrous metals in Soviet Central Kazakhstan and Transbaikalia, tin in the Far East, and coal in Ukraine.

### 苏联许多重要矿藏占世界首位  第一部没有“空白点”的苏联地质图编成

1957-01-29
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联许多重要矿藏占世界首位
    第一部没有“空白点”的苏联地质图编成
    据新华社莫斯科26日电　苏联地质和矿藏保护部部长安特罗波夫说，最近几年来苏联地质探勘工作的结果已经证实苏联的石油、铁、铜、锰、铅、钨和其他一些重要矿藏的蕴藏量都占世界第一位。煤的储藏量也可能在最近列为世界第一位，因为，乌拉尔和苏联远东地区最近都陆续发现了新的巨大的煤矿产地。
    安特罗波夫26日在为苏联“文学报”写的一篇文章中说，苏联地质学工作者在为铀工业寻找原料方面获得的成就也是很大的。目前，他们正在远东和乌克兰等地寻找和探勘巨大的有色金属和稀有金属矿藏，这些矿藏对发展电子技术和原子技术有重大的意义。
    据新华社27日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联第一部没有“空白点”的苏联地质图最近已经编制完成。这是苏联地质学家多年来辛勤劳动的成果。
    苏联地质和矿藏保护部副部长别利亚耶夫斯说，这幅地质图用约一百七十种浓淡不同的颜色和其他符号来表示地球表面各种地层的成份、年龄和地质构造。现在，苏联已经没有未经考察过的地区了。
    根据这部新地质图，地质勘探队就能够更加有目的地探寻苏联中哈萨克和外贝加尔地区的有色金属、远东地区的锡、乌克兰的煤了。
