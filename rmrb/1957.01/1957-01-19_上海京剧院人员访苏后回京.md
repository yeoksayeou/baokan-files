Title: Shanghai Peking Opera Company Returns to Beijing After Visiting the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th — The Shanghai Peking Opera Company, after a two-month performance tour in the Soviet Union, returned to Beijing today with its entire staff, led by the director Zhou Xinfang.

### 上海京剧院人员访苏后回京

1957-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    上海京剧院人员访苏后回京
    据新华社15日讯　上海京剧院在苏联访问演出了两个月以后，全体人员在院长周信芳率领下今天回到北京。
