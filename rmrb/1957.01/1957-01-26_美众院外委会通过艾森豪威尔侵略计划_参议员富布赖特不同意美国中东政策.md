Title: US House Foreign Affairs Committee Passes Eisenhower Aggression Plan; Senator Fulbright Disagrees with US Middle East Policy
Author:
Date: 1957-01-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th dispatch from Washington: After more than ten days of debate, the US House Foreign Affairs Committee passed Eisenhower's Middle East plan on the 24th with a majority vote, without making any substantive revisions.

A joint meeting of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee and the Armed Services Committee continued to hear Dulles testify on the Eisenhower plan on the 24th. Democratic Senator Fulbright accused Dulles of making the United States' relations with foreign governments more strained than at any time this century. He worried that the policies Dulles was pursuing "are detrimental to our interests, will weaken the influence of the free world in the Middle East, are extremely detrimental to the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, and will damage our friendship with Britain and France."

Fulbright also said that what the Eisenhower administration is currently asking for is to be given "broad, unrestricted power—a blank check to use our money and our soldiers." He said this would add many "major new burdens" to "our already very heavy budget." He demanded that Dulles produce a white paper explaining the rationale behind the US's new Middle East policy.

### 美众院外委会通过艾森豪威尔侵略计划  参议员富布赖特不同意美国中东政策

1957-01-26
第6版()
专栏：

    美众院外委会通过艾森豪威尔侵略计划
    参议员富布赖特不同意美国中东政策
    据新华社25日讯　华盛顿消息：美国众议院外交委员会经过十多天的辩论以后，24日以多数票通过了艾森豪威尔的中东计划，没有作任何实质性的修改。
    参议院外交委员会和军事委员会联席会议24日继续听取杜勒斯就艾森豪威尔计划作证。民主党参议员富布赖特指责在杜勒斯的领导下，美国与外国政府的关系一直被搞得比这一世纪以来任何时候还要紧张。他耽心地说，杜勒斯在奉行的政策“是对我们的利益有害的，是会削弱自由世界在中东的影响的，是极不利于北大西洋公约组织和损害我们和英法的友谊的”。
    富布赖特又说，艾森豪威尔政府目前所要求的是给它以“广泛的、毫无限制的权力——一张可以使用我们金钱和我们士兵的空白支票”。他说，这就会在“我们已经十分沉重的预算”上再加上许多“重大的新负担”。他要求杜勒斯提出一项白皮书说明美国制定新中东政策的理由何在。
