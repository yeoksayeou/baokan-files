Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long and their entourage returned to Beijing by plane at 4 p.m. today from Kunming.
    Those who welcomed them at the airport included Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Nie Rongzhen, Bo Yibo, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Baoerhan, Vice Chairman of the National Defence Council Fu Zuoyi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, responsible persons of various departments of state organs, democratic parties and people's organizations, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    Heads of diplomatic missions, economic delegations and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 周总理和贺副总理回京

1957-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理和贺副总理回京
    新华社3日讯　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和随行人员今天下午四时由昆明乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、李富春、李先念、聂荣臻、薄一波，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培、陈叔通，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席包尔汉，国防委员会副主席傅作义，外交部副部长张闻天，国家机关各部门、各民主党派和人民团体的负责人，以及中国人民解放军的高级将领。
    各国驻中国的使节、经济代表团团长和外交官员也到机场欢迎。
