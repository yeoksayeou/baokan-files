Title: Cambodian National Assembly Approves Sangkum Reastr Niyum's New Cabinet; New Cabinet Will Adhere to the Political Direction of the Previous Cabinet and Uphold a Neutral Policy
Author:
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 4th January – After heated debate, the Cambodian National Assembly approved the new cabinet formed by Prime Minister Sangkum Reastr Niyum on the afternoon of the 3rd.

Sangkum Reastr Niyum said that the new cabinet will continue to pursue the political direction of the previous cabinet and adhere to a neutral policy.

In the new cabinet, Prime Minister Sangkum Reastr Niyum will concurrently serve as Minister of Foreign Affairs, Interior, Defense, Finance and Economics, and Comprehensive Construction. Former cabinet's Secretary of State to the Prime Minister's Office, Son Sann and Duong Chinn, will serve as the new cabinet's Secretary of State for Finance and Secretary of State for Commerce and Industry, respectively, replacing the two former cabinet members who were impeached by the National Assembly. The members of other ministries in the new cabinet are largely the same as in the previous cabinet.

### 柬国民议会批准桑云新内阁  新阁将奉行前内阁政治方针并坚持中立政策

1957-01-05
第6版()
专栏：

    柬国民议会批准桑云新内阁
    新阁将奉行前内阁政治方针并坚持中立政策
    新华社金边4日电　柬埔寨国民议会经过热烈的辩论以后，在3日下午批准了桑云首相所组成的新内阁。
    桑云说，新内阁将继续奉行前内阁的政治方针，并且坚持中立政策。
    新内阁由桑云首相兼任外交、内政、国防、财政经济和全面建设大臣。前内阁的首相府国务秘书山福寿和杜钦分别担任新内阁的财政国务秘书和工商国务秘书，以代替前内阁的这两个遭到国会弹劾的阁员。新内阁的其他各部阁员人选大部分同前内阁一样。
