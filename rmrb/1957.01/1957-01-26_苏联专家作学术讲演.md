Title: Soviet Experts Give Academic Lectures
Author:
Date: 1957-01-26
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: Soviet experts in Beijing today began giving academic lectures to Chinese scientific workers. The first topic today was "Mineral Processing and Its Role in the National Economy." This professional academic lecture series is jointly organized by the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Ministry of Higher Education, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the State Council's Bureau of Foreign Experts, and the China Association for Science and Technology. The first batch of topics prepared is divided into two categories: engineering technology and humanities, with a total of sixty-three. All topics will be presented by Soviet experts. It is expected that all lectures can be completed by the end of June.

In addition to Beijing, similar lectures will also be held in more than ten cities, including Shanghai, Wuhan, Chongqing, and Changchun, and will be presented by local Soviet experts.

This lecture series was organized based on the suggestion of the General Soviet Advisor to the State Council. Soviet experts in China are willing to squeeze time out of their work to introduce new Soviet scientific achievements and advanced experiences to Chinese scientific workers more widely through lectures, seminars, and other forms.

### 苏联专家作学术讲演

1957-01-26
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联专家作学术讲演
    据新华社24日讯　在北京的苏联专家今日开始向我国科学工作者作学术讲演。今天的第一个讲题是“选矿及其在国民经济中的作用”。这个专业学术讲座是中国科学院、高等教育部、中苏友好协会总会、国务院外国专家局和中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会等单位共同举办的。已拟好的第一批讲题分工程技术和人文科学两类，共六十三个。全部讲题都由苏联专家主讲。预计可在6月底前讲完。
    除北京外，上海、武汉、重庆和长春等十多个城市也将举办同样的讲座，由当地苏联专家主讲。
    这次讲座是根据国务院苏联总顾问的建议而筹办的。在中国的苏联专家们，都愿意在工作中挤出时间，用演讲、座谈等形式把苏联的新的科学成就和先进经验，更广泛地介绍给中国科学工作者。
