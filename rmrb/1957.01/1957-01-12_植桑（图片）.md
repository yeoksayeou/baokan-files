Title: Planting Mulberry Trees (Picture)
Author: Lu Yuxiang
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 3

    Planting Mulberry Trees
    The Xinglong Agricultural Cooperative in Suining County, Jiangsu Province, planted mulberry trees and raised silkworms in previous years, with considerable income. In 1956, they planted another 200 mu of mulberry trees. This is a picture of the cooperative members planting mulberry trees. Photo by Lu Yuxiang.
