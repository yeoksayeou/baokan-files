Title: Third National Women's Congress Scheduled for June in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 5th – At the 13th Standing Committee meeting of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation held this afternoon, it was decided to hold the Third National Women's Congress in Beijing in June this year.

    The purpose of convening the Third National Women's Congress is to: summarize the main experiences of women's work since the Second National Women's Congress; determine the future direction and tasks of the national women's movement, further unite the broad masses of women, expand the women's united front, and give full play to women's enthusiasm in order to build our country into a great socialist industrial power and strive for the complete liberation of women. The main agenda of the Third National Women's Congress is:

    1.  To hear and discuss the work report made by the Executive Committee to the Third National Women's Congress;
    2.  To revise the Constitution of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and submit a report on the revision of the Constitution;
    3.  To elect the members of the Third National Women's Federation Executive Committee.

    The meeting also studied and determined the number of delegates to participate in the Third National Women's Congress and the specific plan for allocating the number of delegates.

### 第三次全国妇女代表大会定六月在北京举行

1957-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    第三次全国妇女代表大会定六月在北京举行
    新华社5日讯　在中华全国民主妇女联合会今天下午举行的第十三次常务委员会会议上，决定在今年6月在北京举行第三次全国妇女代表大会。
    会议确定召开第三次全国妇女代表大会的目的是：总结第二次全国妇女代表大会以来妇女工作的主要经验；确定今后全国妇女运动的方针、任务，更进一步团结广大妇女群众，扩大妇女统一战线，充分发挥妇女的积极性，为把我国建设成为一个伟大的社会主义工业国和争取妇女的彻底解放而斗争。第三次全国妇女代表大会的主要议程是：
    一、听取和讨论执行委员会向第三次全国妇女代表大会作的工作报告；
    二、修改中华全国民主妇女联合会的章程和提出修改章程的报告；
    三、选举第三届全国妇联的执行委员。
    会议还研究和确定了参加第三次全国妇女代表大会的代表名额和具体分配名额的方案。
