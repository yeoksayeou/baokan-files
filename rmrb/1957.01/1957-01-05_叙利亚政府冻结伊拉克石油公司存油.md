Title: Syrian Government Freezes Iraqi Petroleum Company's Stored Oil
Author:
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 4th: Syrian Prime Minister al-Assali, in his capacity as military governor, issued an order on the 3rd: The Iraqi Petroleum Company must not dispose of the large quantity of crude oil stored in its facilities and oil stations in Syria without the prior permission of the Syrian authorities. This crude oil is estimated to amount to 180,000 tons.

### 叙利亚政府冻结伊拉克石油公司存油

1957-01-05
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚政府冻结伊拉克石油公司存油
    据新华社4日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总理阿萨利以军事总督的身份在3日下令：伊拉克石油公司事先非经叙利亚官方许可不得处理它储存在叙利亚的设施中和油站的大量原油。这些原油估计达十八万吨。
