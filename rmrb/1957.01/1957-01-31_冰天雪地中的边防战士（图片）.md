Border Guards in the Icy and Snowy Land
The border guards of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, in order to safeguard the security of the motherland and enable the people of the whole country to live a peaceful and happy life, perform their duties diligently day and night, braving severe cold and scorching heat, whether on ordinary days or during national festivals, guarding the motherland vigilantly at all times.
Left picture: The border patrol sets off. Right picture: Thank you for the support of the local people.
First picture below: Vigilantly guarding the border. Second picture: Helping each other through dangerous areas. Third picture: Even a suspicious footprint must be carefully tracked. Fourth picture: Melting snow to cook. (Manuscript from the PLA Pictorial) Dong Zhe, Wei Dezhong, Xu Kechang, Liu Feng, Zhang Ju Photography

### 冰天雪地中的边防战士（图片）
董哲　魏德忠　徐克昌　刘峰　张举
1957-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    冰天雪地中的边防战士
    中国人民解放军的边防战士们，为了保卫祖国的安全，使全国人民都能过和平幸福的生活，他们不分白昼黑夜，不避严寒酷暑，不论是平常的日子还是举国欢腾的盛节，他们时时刻刻都在辛勤地执行着自己的任务，警惕地保卫着祖国。
    左图：边防军巡逻队出发了。　右图：感谢当地人民的支援。
    下第一图：警惕地守卫在边防。　第二图：互相帮助，通过险区。　第三图：遇到一点可疑的足迹，也要作仔细的追察。　第四图：化雪做饭。　（解放军画报社稿）		董哲　魏德忠　徐克昌　刘峰　张举　摄影
