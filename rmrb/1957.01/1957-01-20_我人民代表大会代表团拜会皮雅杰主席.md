Title: Delegation of the National People's Congress Pays a Visit to Chairman Pijade
Author:
Date: 1957-01-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, January 19th: The delegation of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China and the delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee paid a visit to Pijade, Chairman of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, on the morning of the 18th. Wu Xiuquan, Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, accompanied the delegation.

Pijade had a cordial conversation with Peng Zhen, the head of the delegation, Li Jishen, Zhang Bojun, Hu Ziang, the deputy heads of the delegation, and the members of the delegation.

Present at the meeting were Simic and Sencur, Vice-Chairmen of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, and Zecevic, Chairman of the Federal Council.

Subsequently, the entire delegation traveled by car to Mount Avala in the suburbs of Belgrade and laid a wreath at the Tomb of the Unknown Hero built on the mountain peak.

### 我人民代表大会代表团拜会皮雅杰主席

1957-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我人民代表大会代表团拜会皮雅杰主席
    新华社贝尔格莱德19日电　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团18日上午拜会了南斯拉夫联邦国民议会主席皮雅杰。陪同代表团前往拜会的有中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权。
    皮雅杰同代表团团长彭真、副团长李济深、章伯钧、胡子昂和代表团团员们进行了亲切的交谈。
    拜会时在座的有南斯拉夫联邦国民议会副主席西米奇和申丘尔茨、联邦院主席泽契维奇。
    随后，代表团全体人员乘车前往贝尔格莱德市郊阿瓦拉山，向建筑在山峰上的无名英雄墓献花圈。
