Title: Chinese Red Cross Society Donates Funds to Aid Indonesian Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency News: The Chinese Red Cross Society remitted RMB 6,574.85 (equivalent to approximately 30,000 Indonesian Rupiah) to the Indonesian Red Cross Society on the 24th to aid flood victims in northern Sumatra and eastern and western Java.
    At the end of last year, the Chinese Red Cross Society sent a telegram to the Indonesian Red Cross Society expressing sympathy for the floods in Indonesia and informing them that the Chinese Red Cross Society was willing to provide assistance. The Indonesian Red Cross Society wrote to the Chinese Red Cross Society on January 10th, expressing gratitude for the Chinese Red Cross Society's concern and willingness to provide assistance.

### 中国红十字会捐款救济印度尼西亚灾民

1957-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会捐款救济印度尼西亚灾民
    新华社讯　中国红十字会24日汇给印度尼西亚红十字会人民币六千五百七十四元八角五分（约合三万印度尼西亚盾），救济苏门答腊北部及爪哇东部、西部各地的水灾灾民。
    中国红十字会在去年年底曾经打电报给印度尼西亚红十字会，对印度尼西亚遭到水灾一事表示同情，并且通知它中国红十字会愿意提供援助。印度尼西亚红十字会在1月10日写信给中国红十字会，对中国红十字会的关怀和准备给予援助表示感谢。
