Title: Savings Deposits Increase in Hefei

Author:

Date: 1957-01-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, 19th: Since the promotion of thrift, the balance of bank savings deposits in Hefei in December last year increased by 369,000 yuan compared to November. In August last year, workers and cadres received back pay, and had more money left over, but the bank savings amount for that month was still less than in December.

There are now more than 60,000 people in Hefei who have deposited money in banks. Among cadres and workers, those participating in savings account for about 60%.

### 合肥市储蓄存款增加

1957-01-20
第3版()
专栏：

    合肥市储蓄存款增加
    新华社合肥19日电　自从提倡节约以后，合肥市去年12月份的银行储蓄存款余额，比11月份增加了三十六万九千元。去年8月，职工和干部领到补发的工资，余钱较多，可是这个月的银行储蓄额还是小于12月。
    合肥市现在有六万多人在银行存了款。干部、职工中，参加储蓄的占60％左右。
