Title: Do Not Allow Foreign Powers to Interfere in Jordan's Internal Affairs Under the Pretext of "Protection" - Jordanian Prime Minister Opposes the "Filling the Vacuum" Doctrine
Author:
Date: 1957-01-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st - According to TASS, the "Spark" magazine published on the 20th contained an interview with Jordanian Prime Minister Suleiman Nabulsi by the magazine's reporter.

Regarding the Eisenhower Plan, Nabulsi said, "I must firmly state that we fundamentally do not accept the 'filling the vacuum' doctrine. The right to protect Arab countries should be held in the hands of the Arabs themselves. We do not allow any government to interfere in our internal affairs under the guise of 'protection.' Our independence is inviolable. Our freedom and sovereignty are not for sale."

Nabulsi stated that Jordan, as a sovereign state, only recently stepped onto the international stage. Previous governments, to a large extent, followed Britain's lead in international affairs. Now, the people of our country are determined to break the shackles of foreign rule over Jordan, and therefore, in foreign policy, we have broken away from dependence on foreign countries.

Nabulsi said that Jordan hopes to strengthen friendly ties with all countries that respect Jordan's sovereignty. He said, "Our main task is to strengthen brotherly cooperation with free Arab countries to consolidate our freedom and liberate other Arab nations from foreign dependence." He also discussed the issue of Arab economic cooperation, and the cabinets of Jordan and Syria have decided to establish an economic union between the two countries.

Regarding domestic issues, Nabulsi said that the current government is composed of representatives of various parties in the parliamentary majority. It believes that domestic problems can be resolved. It hopes to devote all efforts to developing the economy, exploiting national resources, and natural wealth. The government plans to implement social reforms and encourage the development of trade unions.

### 不许外国借口“保护”来干涉约旦内政  约旦首相反对“填补真空”主义

1957-01-22
第6版()
专栏：

    不许外国借口“保护”来干涉约旦内政
    约旦首相反对“填补真空”主义
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：20日出版的“星火”杂志发表了约旦首相苏莱曼·纳布西同这家杂志记者的谈话。
    纳布西在谈到艾森豪威尔计划时说，我应该坚决地说，我们原则上不接受“填补真空”的主义。保护阿拉伯国家的权利应该掌握在阿拉伯人自己的手中。我们不容许任何一个国家的政府以“保护”为名来干涉我们的内政。我们的独立是不容侵犯的。我们的自由和主权是不出卖的。
    纳布西说，约旦作为一个主权国家只是在不久之前才走上国际舞台。前几届政府在国际事务方面，在很大程度上是听从英国摆布的。现在，我国人民决心打破外国统治约旦的枷锁，因而在外交政策上也就摆脱了对外国的依靠。
    纳布西说，约旦希望同所有尊重约旦主权的国家加强友好的联系。他说，我们的主要任务是加强同自由阿拉伯各国兄弟般的合作，以巩固我们的自由，并且使其他阿拉伯民族摆脱对外国的依赖。他还谈到了阿拉伯国家经济合作的问题，约旦和叙利亚的内阁已经决定建立两国的经济联盟。
    纳布西在谈到国内问题时说，现政府是由议会中的多数派的各政党代表组成的。它相信，国内问题是能够解决的。它希望全力发展经济，开发民族资源和自然财富。政府计划实行社会改革和鼓励工会的发展。
