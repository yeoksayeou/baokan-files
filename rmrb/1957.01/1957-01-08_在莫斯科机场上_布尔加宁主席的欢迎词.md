Title: Chairman Bulganin's Welcome Speech at Moscow Airport
Author:
Date: 1957-01-08
Page: 1

    At Moscow Airport
    Chairman Bulganin's Welcome Speech
    Dear Comrade Zhou Enlai, Dear Comrade He Long, Dear Comrades of the Government delegation of the People's Republic of China!
    On behalf of the Soviet government, the Soviet people, and myself, I extend a warm welcome to you in Moscow, the capital of our motherland.
    Your visit to the Soviet Union is a joyous event for all Soviet people. The Soviet people have deep and sincere brotherly feelings for their great friends, the Chinese people.
    Dear Comrade Zhou Enlai, through you and all the comrades who have arrived, we salute the six hundred million Chinese people. Under the leadership of the heroic Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese people have achieved decisive victories in the socialist transformation of industry, agriculture, and commerce. The Soviet people are pleased with the achievements of the People's Republic of China in socialist construction, because this is also the achievement of the entire world socialist system, and a brilliant proof of the victory of Marxism-Leninism.
    The brotherly and unbreakable friendship between the Soviet and Chinese people is a guarantee of further strengthening the power of the socialist camp and a guarantee of lasting world peace. The friendship between the Soviet Union and China, like the friendship between all socialist countries, is built on complete equality, a desire to respect each other's national interests, and a common and vital desire for the further prosperity of the entire world socialist system. The more consolidated our friendship, the more guaranteed will be the cause of peace and progress for all peoples, and the bright future of all mankind.
    Dear Premier, please allow me to express my belief that the delegation led by you will make a valuable contribution to the further development of the friendship between our great peoples.
    Dear guests, I extend to you a warm welcome.
    Long live the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union!
    Long live the great Chinese people!

### 在莫斯科机场上  布尔加宁主席的欢迎词

1957-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    在莫斯科机场上
    布尔加宁主席的欢迎词亲爱的周恩来同志，亲爱的贺龙同志，亲爱的中华人民共和国政府代表团团员同志们！
    我以苏联政府、苏联人民和我个人的名义，在我们祖国首都莫斯科向您表示热烈的欢迎。
    您来苏联访问，是全体苏联人民愉快的事件。苏联人民对自己伟大的朋友中国人民是有深厚的、真诚的兄弟感情的。
    亲爱的周恩来同志，我们通过您和全体到来的同志们向全体六亿中国人民致敬。中国人民在英勇的中国共产党的领导下，在工业、农业和商业的社会主义改造中取得了决定性的胜利。苏联人民为中华人民共和国的社会主义建设中的成就而高兴，因为这同时也是整个世界社会主义体系的成就，是马克思列宁主义思想胜利的光辉证明。
    苏联人民和中国人民结成兄弟般的牢不可破的友谊就是进一步加强社会主义阵营各国威力的保证，是世界持久和平的保障。苏联和中国的友谊，如同各社会主义国家之间的友谊一样，是建筑在完全平等、互相尊重民族利益的愿望之上的，是建筑在为了整个世界社会主义体系进一步繁荣的共同切身相关的愿望之上的。我们的友谊愈加巩固，各国人民的和平与进步事业，全人类的光明的未来，就愈有保证。
    亲爱的总理同志，请让我表示这样的信念，以您为首的代表团来到苏联，将对进一步发展我们伟大人民间的友谊作出宝贵的贡献。
    亲爱的客人们，谨向你们表示热烈的欢迎。
    中苏两国人民间的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    伟大的中国人民万岁！
