Title: Aerial Survey of Qinghai-Tibet Railway Line
Author:
Date: 1957-01-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The aerial survey work for the first railway line from Qinghai Province to Tibet began in mid-November last year.

The starting point of this new railway line from Qinghai Province to Tibet connects with the railway line from Lanzhou to Qinghai, and the terminal is Lhasa, with a length of about 1,300 kilometers. The railway line will pass through the Tanggula Mountains at an altitude of about 6,000 meters.

The aerial survey of this line uses the latest methods. According to the plan, all aerial survey work will be completed before the end of April this year. The results of the aerial survey will provide topographic maps for route selection.

### 航测青藏铁路线

1957-01-11
第2版()
专栏：

    航测青藏铁路线
    新华社10日讯　第一条由青海省通向西藏的铁路线路的航空勘察工作，已经在去年11月中旬开始。
    青海省到西藏的这条新铁路线路的起点是同兰州到青海的铁路线相接，终点是拉萨，长约一千三百公里左右。这条铁路的线路将通过海拔六千公尺左右的唐古拉山。
    这条线路的航空勘察，采用了最新式的方法。按照计划全部航勘工作将在今年4月底以前完成。航空勘察结果将可以提出供选线用的地形图。
