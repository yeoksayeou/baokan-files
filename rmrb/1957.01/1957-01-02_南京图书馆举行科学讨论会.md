Title: Nanjing Library Holds Scientific Symposium
Author:
Date: 1957-01-02
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 31st: At the first library science symposium held at the Nanjing Library, Wang Changbing, Deputy Curator of the Nanjing Library, read his paper entitled "Research on How to Write the History of Chinese Library Science." Du Dingyou of the Zhongshan Library of Guangdong Province presented a paper on "The Question of Saving Library Building Area." Zhang Zunjian of the Hubei Provincial Library presented a paper on "The Legacy of Liu Xiang and Liu Xin in Bibliography."

The symposium was held in Nanjing from December 27th to 30th. More than 50 library science experts and library staff from all over the country participated in the symposium.

### 南京图书馆举行科学讨论会

1957-01-02
第7版()
专栏：

    南京图书馆举行科学讨论会
    据新华社南京31日电　在南京图书馆举行的第一届图书馆学科学讨论会上，南京图书馆副馆长王长炳宣读了他写的“对怎样编写中国图书馆事业史的研究”论文。广东省中山图书馆杜定友提出了“图书馆建筑面积的节约问题”论文。湖北省图书馆张遵俭提出了“刘向、刘歆在目录学方面的遗产”论文。
    这次讨论会是12月27日到30日在南京举行的。参加这次讨论会的有来自全国各地的图书馆学专家和图书馆工作人员共五十多人。
