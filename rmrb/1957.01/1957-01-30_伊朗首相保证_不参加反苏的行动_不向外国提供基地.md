Title: Iranian Prime Minister Guarantees No Participation in Anti-Soviet Actions, No Provision of Bases to Foreign Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-01-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, 28th (TASS dispatch): Iranian Prime Minister Hossein Ala received the delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union on the evening of the 27th. Speaking about a statement recently issued by TASS, he said that Iran would not provide bases to foreign countries and would not participate in actions against the Soviet Union.

Ala also told the delegation that the Iranian King's visit to the Soviet Union was a major factor in establishing good-neighborly relations between our two countries. We should continue to exchange cultural, sports, and trade delegations to develop cooperation between our two countries.

Following the conversation with the Iranian Prime Minister, the delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union visited Iranian Foreign Minister Adalan.

### 伊朗首相保证  不参加反苏的行动  不向外国提供基地

1957-01-30
第6版()
专栏：

    伊朗首相保证
    不参加反苏的行动
    不向外国提供基地
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社德黑兰28日讯：伊朗首相侯赛尼·阿拉在27日晚上接见苏联最高苏维埃代表团。他在谈到塔斯社不久以前发表的一项声明时说，伊朗不会向外国提供基地，不会参加反对苏联的行动。
    阿拉还向代表团说，伊朗国王对苏联的访问是建立我们两国之间的睦邻关系上的重大因素。我们应当继续互派文化、体育和贸易代表团，发展我们两国之间的合作。
    在同伊朗首相的谈话以后，苏联最高苏维埃代表团访问了伊朗外交大臣阿达兰。
