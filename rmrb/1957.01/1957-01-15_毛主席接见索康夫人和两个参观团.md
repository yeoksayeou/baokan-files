Title: Chairman Mao Receives Mrs. Sokang and Two Visiting Groups
Author:
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 14th: Chairman Mao Zedong received Mrs. Dekyi Lhazom Sokang, a member of the Preparatory Committee of the Patriotic Women's Association of Tibet, who was attending the Third National Conference on Women and Children's Welfare Work, this morning. At the same time, he received all members of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Visiting Group and the Liaoning Province Ethnic Minorities Visiting Group.

Present at the reception were Chen Yun, Acting Premier of the State Council, Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Xu Guangping, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Wang Feng, Liu Chun, and Sa Kongliao, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission.

During the reception, Mrs. Sokang presented Hada scarves to Chairman Mao Zedong, Acting Premier Chen Yun, Vice Premier Deng Xiaoping, and Vice Chairman Chen Shutong.

Chairman Mao Zedong and others also held talks with Mrs. Sokang.

### 毛主席接见索康夫人和两个参观团

1957-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见索康夫人和两个参观团
    新华社14日讯　毛泽东主席在今天上午接见了出席第三次全国妇女儿童福利工作会议的西藏爱国妇女联谊会筹备委员会委员索康夫人德几拉孜。同时，接见了内蒙古自治区参观团和辽宁省各民族参观团的全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有国务院代总理陈云、副总理邓小平，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、刘春、萨空了。
    在接见的时候，索康夫人向毛泽东主席、陈云代总理、邓小平副总理和陈叔通副委员长献了哈达。
    毛泽东主席等还同索康夫人进行了谈话。
