Title: Deputy Chairman of the USSR Council of Ministers Zavenyagin Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1957-01-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 1st (TASS) – The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union issued an obituary announcing: A prominent Soviet statesman, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Minister of Medium Machine Building, and Hero of Socialist Labor, A.P. Zavenyagin, passed away due to a serious illness.

Zavenyagin was born in 1901, joined the Communist Party in 1917, and participated in the civil war. After graduating from the Mining Academy in 1930, he participated in the work of developing the Soviet metallurgical industry. In 1955, he was appointed as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Medium Machine Building. He died of cardiac arrest on December 31, 1956.

### 苏联部长会议副主席查文尼亚金逝世

1957-01-03
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联部长会议副主席查文尼亚金逝世
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科1日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议发表讣告宣布：苏联杰出的政治家、苏联共产党中央委员会委员、苏联部长会议副主席、中型机器制造部长、社会主义劳动英雄阿·普·查文尼亚金因患重病逝世。
    查文尼亚金生于1901年，1917年加入共产党并且曾经参加国内战争。1930年他在采矿学院毕业后，参加了发展苏联冶金工业的工作。1955年被任命为苏联部长会议副主席和中型机器制造部长。1956年12月31日因患心脏麻痹症逝世。
