	International Briefs
	Shanghai Peking Opera Troupe Concludes Performances in Soviet Union
	The Shanghai Peking Opera Troupe of China has concluded its two-month visit and performances in the Soviet Union. The troupe performed a total of forty-three shows in nine cities including Moscow, Leningrad, Tallinn, Riga, Minsk, and Kiev, and was warmly welcomed by Soviet audiences.
	
	Our Acrobatic Troupe Concludes Performances in Belgium
	The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe concluded its visit and performances in Belgium and left Belgium for Luxembourg on the morning of the 6th. The acrobatic troupe held a farewell performance in Antwerp on the evening of the 5th.
	
	Belgian Zoo to Donate Animals to China
	The director of the Antwerp Royal Zoo, Walter Vanden bergh, told the head of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe at a recent reception that they had decided to send some animals to the Beijing Zoo to express their friendship with the Chinese people and their desire to develop cultural exchanges between the two countries. These animals will soon be shipped to Beijing.
	
	All-Soviet Artistic Film Script Competition Concludes
	The All-Soviet Excellent Artistic Film Script Competition organized by the Soviet Ministry of Culture and the Soviet Writers' Association to celebrate the fortieth anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution has concluded.
	
	Two thousand four hundred film scripts participated in this competition. Twenty-two scripts received prizes.

### 国际简讯

1957-01-08
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    上海京剧院结束在苏演出
    中国上海京剧院已经结束了在苏联为期两个月的访问演出。剧院共在莫斯科、列宁格勒、塔林、里加、明斯克、基辅等九个城市演出四十三场，受到了苏联观众的热烈欢迎。
    我杂技团结束在比利时的演出
    中国杂技团结束了在比利时的访问演出，6日上午离开比利时前往卢森堡。杂技团5日晚间在安特卫普举行了告别演出。
    比动物园将赠送我国动物
    安特卫普皇家动物园主任瓦尔特·范登伯格在最近的一次酒会上向中国杂技团的负责人说，他们决定送给北京动物园若干动物，以表示他们对中国人民的友谊和发展两国文化交流的愿望。这些动物不久将经海运运往北京。
    全苏艺术影片脚本竞赛结束
    苏联文化部和苏联作家协会为庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年组织的全苏优秀艺术影片脚本竞赛已经结束。
    参加这次竞赛的有二千四百个电影脚本。有二十二个脚本获得了奖金。
