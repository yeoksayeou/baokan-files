Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Visits Afghan King and Holds Talks with Prime Minister Daud Khan
Author:
Date: 1957-01-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, January 20th: After arriving in Kabul yesterday, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long signed the guest book at the Royal Palace at 3:00 PM and then paid a visit to King Zahir Shah. They spent half an hour talking with the King.

After leaving the Royal Palace, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long drove to the tomb of the late King Nadir Shah, where they laid a wreath.

After returning to their residence in the Prime Minister's Office, they met with Prime Minister Daud Khan and held talks with him.

### 周总理拜会阿富汗国王  并同达乌德汗首相举行会谈

1957-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理拜会阿富汗国王
    并同达乌德汗首相举行会谈
    新华社喀布尔20日电　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理在昨天抵达喀布尔以后，午后三点钟在王宫的来宾签名簿上签了名，然后拜会了国王查希尔·沙阿。他们同国王在一起谈了半小时。
    周恩来总理和贺龙副总理在离开王宫以后，驱车前往已故国王纳迪尔·沙阿的陵墓，他们在墓前献了花圈。
    他们在回到首相府的寓所以后，会见了达乌德汗首相，并且同他举行了会谈。
