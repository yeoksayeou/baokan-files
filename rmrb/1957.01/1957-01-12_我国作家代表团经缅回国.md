Title: Chinese Writers Delegation Returns Home via Burma
Author:
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 11th January: The Chinese Writers Delegation, headed by Mao Dun, left Rangoon for home this morning. They arrived in Rangoon on January 8th after attending the Asian Writers Conference in Delhi. During their stay in Rangoon, the delegation paid a visit to U Nu, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League and Chairman of the Burma Translation Society, on the 9th. U Nu hosted a banquet at noon on the 10th to entertain the delegation.

### 我国作家代表团经缅回国

1957-01-12
第5版()
专栏：

    我国作家代表团经缅回国
    据新华社仰光11日电　以茅盾为首的中国作家代表团已经在今天早晨离仰光回国。他们是在德里参加了亚洲作家会议以后在1月8日到达仰光的。代表团在仰光逗留期间在9日拜访了缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟主席、缅甸翻译协会主席吴努。吴努10日中午举行宴会招待代表团。
