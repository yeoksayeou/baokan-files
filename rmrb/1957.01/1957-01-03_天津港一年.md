Title: Tianjin Port in One Year
Author:
Date: 1957-01-03
Page: 2

    Tianjin Port in One Year
    Xinhua News Agency
    The last of 1956—the 434th international trading vessel—sailed out of Tianjin Port on December 30th. This largest port in North China overfulfilled its 1956 import and export plan by 13%, 76% higher than 1936, the year with the highest foreign trade volume in its history.
    Among Tianjin's trade with various regions of the world, trade with Asia and Africa has increased the most. Compared with 1955, trade with India increased by 72%, with Egypt by 26 times, direct trade with Thailand increased by more than 2,800 times, and trade with Japan has exceeded the level of 1936. In addition, trade relations have begun with Yemen, Oman, Iraq, Jordan, Saudi Arabia and other countries.
    Among the commodities exported from Tianjin this year, modern industrial products accounted for more than a quarter. Among them, large machine tools manufactured in China—universal screw lathes produced by the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, 30 kinds of dyes such as Salt Base Lake Blue produced by Tianjin Dyestuff Chemical Industry Company, and various daily-use light industrial products were exported from Tianjin for the first time.
    Steel plates, cranes, tractors, trucks, cement, rubber, fertilizer, and other commodities needed for China's industrial and agricultural production and construction imported from Tianjin also increased significantly compared with 1955.

### 天津港一年

1957-01-03
第2版()
专栏：

    天津港一年
    1956年的最后一艘——第四百三十四艘国际贸易商船于12月30日驶出天津港。我国华北这个最大的港口，超额13％完成了1956年的进出口计划，比它历史上对外贸易吞吐量最高的1936年还高76％。
    天津口岸对世界各个地区的贸易中，以对亚非地区的贸易额增长最多，以1956年和1955年比，对印度增长了72％，对埃及增长了二十六倍，对泰国的直接贸易增长了二千八百多倍，同日本的贸易已超过1936年的水平。此外，还同也门、阿曼、伊拉克、约旦、沙特阿拉伯等国家开始贸易往来。
    这一年从天津出口的商品中，近代工业产品已占四分之一以上。其中，我国制造的大型机床——沈阳第一机床厂出产的万能螺丝车床，天津染料化学工业公司生产的盐基湖蓝等三十种染料和各种日用轻工业品，在天津港出口还是第一次。
    我国工农业生产和建设需要的钢板、起重机、拖拉机、卡车、水泥、橡胶、肥田粉以及其他各种商品，从天津进口也比1955年增加很多。
                      （新华社）
