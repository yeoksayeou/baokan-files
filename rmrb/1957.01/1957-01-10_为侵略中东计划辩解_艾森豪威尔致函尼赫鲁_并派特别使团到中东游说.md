Title: Defending the Plan to Invade the Middle East, Eisenhower Writes to Nehru and Sends a Special Mission to Lobby the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1957-01-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 8th Dispatch from Washington: The White House Press Secretary announced on the 7th that Eisenhower had sent a letter to Nehru specifically addressing the President's special message to Congress regarding the new Middle East plan. The letter is of a private nature and will not be made public.

Xinhua News Agency, 8th Dispatch from Washington: U.S. President Eisenhower appointed Richards on January 7th to lead a special U.S. mission to the Middle East to "explain the Eisenhower Doctrine" to Middle Eastern countries.

### 为侵略中东计划辩解  艾森豪威尔致函尼赫鲁  并派特别使团到中东游说

1957-01-10
第6版()
专栏：

    为侵略中东计划辩解
    艾森豪威尔致函尼赫鲁
    并派特别使团到中东游说
    新华社8日讯　华盛顿消息：白宫新闻秘书7日宣布，艾森豪威尔已经发出一封信给尼赫鲁，内容是专门涉及总统向国会提出的新中东计划特别咨文，这封信是私人性质，不准备加以公布。
    据新华社8日讯　华盛顿消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔1月7日任命理查兹率领一个美国特别使团到中东去，向中东国家“解释艾森豪威尔主义”。
