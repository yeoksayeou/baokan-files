Title: Wang Jiaxiang, Member of Chinese Government Delegation, Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 20th—Wang Jiaxiang, member of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, who visited the Soviet Union, Poland, and Hungary at the invitation of the governments of the three countries, returned to Beijing by plane on the 20th.

### 我国政府代表团团员王稼祥返北京

1957-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府代表团团员王稼祥返北京
    新华社20日讯　应苏联、波兰、匈牙利三国政府邀请，前往访问的中华人民共和国政府代表团团员、中华人民共和国外交部副部长王稼祥于20日乘飞机返抵北京。
