Title: Polish State Council Decides to Vigorously Train Atomic Energy Experts
Author:
Date: 1957-01-17
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 15th: The Polish Press Agency reported that the Polish State Council recently held a meeting to discuss the training of experts in atomic energy research and practical applications.
    The meeting decided to open courses in nuclear physics, nuclear chemistry, chemical engineering of reactor materials, and atomic dynamics in some universities.
    In addition, the meeting also decided to open crash courses on the research and practical application of atomic energy for various professionals.
    Furthermore, the meeting also decided to send young scientific workers and independent researchers abroad on a large scale for study.

### 波国务委员会决定大力培养原子能专家

1957-01-17
第5版()
专栏：

    波国务委员会决定大力培养原子能专家
    据新华社华沙15日电　据波兰通讯社报道，波兰国务委员会最近开会讨论训练原子能研究工作和实际应用方面的专家的问题。
    会议决定在一些大学里开设原子核物理学、原子核化学、反应堆材料化工学、原子动力学等方面的课程。
    另外，会议还决定将为各种专业人员开设关于原子能的研究和实际应用的速成辅助训练班。
    此外，会议还决定大规模派遣青年科学工作者和独立的研究工作者到国外去学习。
