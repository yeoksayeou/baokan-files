Title: Cairo and Moscow Wireless Telephone Line Open
Author:
Date: 1957-01-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 12th. A wireless telephone line between Cairo and Moscow was directly connected on the 11th.

A statement released in Cairo said that after President Nasser's approval on the 10th, the wireless telephone line was opened on the 11th.

### 开罗和莫斯科无线电话线路开放

1957-01-13
第6版()
专栏：

    开罗和莫斯科无线电话线路开放
    新华社12日讯　开罗消息：开罗同莫斯科之间的无线电话已经在11日直接通话。
    开罗发表的公告说，经纳赛尔总统在10日批准后，这条无线电话线路已经从11日起开放。
