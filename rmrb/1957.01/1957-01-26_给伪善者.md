Title: To the Hypocrite
Author: Musa Garif
Date: 1957-01-26
Page: 8

    To the Hypocrite
    Soviet Union
    Musa Garif
    My clothes have many patches,
    Yet my good name is unharmed.
    Your clothes are all brocade,
    But your soul is full of patches.
      (Translated by Fang Xing)

### 给伪善者
穆萨·嘉里尔
1957-01-26
第8版()
专栏：

    给伪善者
    苏联
    穆萨·嘉里尔
    我衣多补丁，
    无损我令名。
    你衣皆锦绣，
    补丁满魂灵。
      （方行译）
