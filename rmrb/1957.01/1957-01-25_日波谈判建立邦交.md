Title: Japan-Poland Negotiations to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 23rd: The Polish Press Agency reports that foreign news agencies have recently published news about Japan conducting negotiations with Poland to establish diplomatic relations. The Polish Press Agency is authorized to declare that Poland and Japan are indeed conducting negotiations through their representatives to the United Nations on the issue of ending the state of war between the two countries and establishing diplomatic relations. The negotiations are expected to conclude soon.

### 日波谈判建立邦交

1957-01-25
第5版()
专栏：

    日波谈判建立邦交
    新华社华沙23日电　波兰通讯社的消息说：外国通讯社最近发表了关于日本在同波兰进行建立外交关系的谈判的消息。波兰通讯社受权声明：波日两国的确在通过两国驻联合国的代表进行关于终止两国间的战争状态和建立两国外交关系问题的谈判。谈判预定在最近结束。
