Title: Moscow performance of "Swan Lake" ballet to entertain Chinese delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th. The personnel of the People's Republic of China government delegation headed by Premier Zhou Enlai watched Tchaikovsky's ballet "Swan Lake" at the Bolshoi Theatre in the Soviet Union on the evening of January 9th, and visited some parts of the theatre.

Bulganin, Mikoyan, Khrushchev, Shepilov, Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov, and Gudyev, Director of the Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, watched the performance together with the guests in the central box decorated with the national flags of China and the Soviet Union.

Also among the audience were diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union and personnel from the Chinese Embassy.

After the performance, the government delegation of the People's Republic of China sent flower baskets to the actors. Today, the male and female leads were played by Kondratov and Plisetskaya, Honored Artists of the Russian Federation.

### 莫斯科演出“天鹅湖”舞剧招待我代表团

1957-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科演出“天鹅湖”舞剧招待我代表团
    新华社莫斯科10日电　以周恩来总理为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团人员，1月9日晚在苏联大剧院观看了柴可夫斯基的巴蕾舞剧“天鹅湖”，并且参观了这个剧院的一些地方。
    在装饰有中、苏两国国旗的中央厢座里，同客人们一块观剧的有布尔加宁、米高扬、赫鲁晓夫、谢皮洛夫以及苏联文化部长米哈伊洛夫和苏联外交部远东司司长顾德夫。
    在观众中间还有各国驻苏联外交使节和中国大使馆的人员。
    在演出结束以后，中华人民共和国政府代表团向演员们送了花篮。今天，扮演男女主角的是俄罗斯联邦功勋艺术家康德拉托夫和普利塞茨卡亚。
