Title: “New France” Weekly Publishes “On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat, Again”
Author:
Date: 1957-01-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, January 18th: The French Communist Party's publication, "New France" weekly, published the full text of the "People's Daily" article, "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat, Again," in its January 10th issue.

The editors of "New France" also marked each part of the "People's Daily" article with major headings, and further marked each part with subheadings.

### “新法兰西”周刊登载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”

1957-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    “新法兰西”周刊登载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”
    新华社18日讯　巴黎消息：1月10日出版的法国共产党的刊物“新法兰西”周刊全文刊载“人民日报”的文章“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”。
    “新法兰西”的编辑还对“人民日报”文章的每一部分标了大标题，每一部分中又分别标了小标题。
