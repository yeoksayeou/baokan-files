Title: Newspapers in Poland, Hungary, North Korea, Germany, Albania, and other countries published "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship"
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 31st. Many Polish newspapers published the "People's Daily" article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" on the 30th. "People's Tribune" published the full text of the article in two full pages.
	"People's Tribune," "Warsaw Life," and "Youth Banner" also published news about the "People's Daily" publishing this article on the 29th.

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 31st. Budapest's "People's Freedom" today published the first half of the Beijing "People's Daily" article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" (the second half will follow) in two and a half pages. "People's Will" today published a summary of the article in more than two pages.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 31st. Today's "Democratic Korea" and "Korean People's Army Daily" both published a summary of the "People's Daily" article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" on the fourth page. The Korean Central Broadcasting Station broadcast a summary of the article at 1:00 PM today.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 31st. "Neues Deutschland" yesterday published a detailed summary of the "People's Daily" article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" on most of the fourth page.
	"Neues Deutschland" also published a news item it wrote itself on the first page, introducing the content of the article. The title of the news item was: "The Soviet Union—The Center of the Communist Movement," with the subtitle: "The Beijing 'People's Daily' article discusses issues of the international workers' movement."

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 31st. The Albanian newspaper "Voice of the People" published the "People's Daily" article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" on the entire third, fourth, and seventh pages on the 30th. "Unity" published a summary of the article disseminated by Xinhua News Agency on the third page.

### 波、匈、朝、德、阿等国报纸  刊载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”

1957-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    波、匈、朝、德、阿等国报纸
    刊载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”
    新华社华沙31日电　波兰许多报纸在30日刊载了“人民日报”的
  “再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文。“人民论坛报”以整两版的篇幅刊载了这篇文章的全文。
      “人民论坛报”、“华沙生活报”和“青年旗帜报”在29日还登载了关于“人民日报”发表这篇文章的消息。
    新华社布达佩斯31日电　布达佩斯“人民自由报”今天以两版半的篇幅刊登了北京“人民日报”发表的文章“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”的前半部（后半部待续）。“人民意志报”今天以两版多的篇幅刊载了这篇文章的摘要。
    新华社平壤31日电　今天的“民主朝鲜报”和“朝鲜人民军报”都在第四版刊登了“人民日报”发表的文章“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”的摘要。朝鲜中央广播电台在今天下午一点钟广播了这篇文章的摘要。
    新华社柏林31日电　“新德意志报”昨天以第四版的大部篇幅刊载了北京“人民日报”发表的文章“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”的详细摘要。
    “新德意志报”还在第一版刊登了它自己编写的一条消息，介绍这篇文章的内容。这条消息的标题是：“苏联——共产主义运动的中心”，副题是：“北京‘人民日报’的文章论述国际工人运动问题”。
    新华社地拉那31电　阿尔巴尼亚“人民之声报”30日以第三、四、七版的全部地位刊登了“人民日报”发表的“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文。“团结报”以第三版地位刊登了新华社播发的这篇文章的摘要。
