Mangshi, Border City

Mangshi is the capital of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture in Yunnan Province. Three years ago, it was still a remote small village. Now, it has become a relatively prosperous city.

　　　　　　　　　　Photos by Cai Shangxiong and Wu Yinbo (People's Pictorial Press)

    The newly built People's Committee Building of the Autonomous Prefecture.
    Young Pioneers from Luxi No. 1 Primary School, Yunnan Province, playing in the suburbs of Mangshi.
    Luxi County National Hospital serving the local ethnic groups.
    New books in the Mangshi Xinhua Bookstore are welcomed by young people of all ethnic groups in Dehong.
