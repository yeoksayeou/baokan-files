Title: Syngman Rhee Won't Shed Tears Until He Sees the Coffin, Attempts to Tear Up the Armistice Agreement This Year
Author:
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 4th: Seoul dispatch: South Korean National Assembly Vice-Chairman Lee Jae-hak said today that one of Syngman Rhee's main goals for 1957 is to abolish the Korean Armistice Agreement.

He said that this was announced by Syngman Rhee during a meeting with him, and Syngman Rhee also said that he would organize a nationwide movement to abolish the armistice agreement.

Prior to this, Syngman Rhee had also openly stated in his "New Year's Message" that his soldiers are "now preparing to march north."

### 李承晚不见棺材不掉泪  企图在今年撕毁停战协定

1957-01-05
第6版()
专栏：

    李承晚不见棺材不掉泪
    企图在今年撕毁停战协定
    新华社平壤4日电　汉城消息：南朝鲜国会副议长李在鹤今天说，李承晚1957年的主要目标之一是废除朝鲜停战协定。
    他说，这是李承晚在同他会见的时候宣布的，李承晚还说要为废除停战协定组织一次全国性的运动。
    在此以前，李承晚还曾经在他的“新年文告”中公然说，他的士兵“现在正在准备向北挺进”。
