Title: Jordan Accuses the UN General Assembly of Israeli Troops Spying on the Jordanian Border
Author:
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, January 11th, New York: Jordan's representative to the UN General Assembly, Rifai, read a telegram he received from the Jordanian Foreign Minister at the end of the morning session of the General Assembly on the 10th. The telegram stated that a large number of Israeli troops had concentrated on the Jordanian border in recent days. The Jordanian Foreign Minister wanted the delegation to immediately contact the UN Secretary-General and notify the contents of the telegram to the delegations of various countries.

According to Rifai's announcement to the press, he had met with UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on this issue, and Hammarskjöld assured him that he would immediately request the United Nations Truce Supervision Organization in Palestine to submit a report on the situation as stated by Jordan.

Rifai has also requested that the representatives of Arab countries in the United Nations hold a meeting to discuss the issues raised by Jordan.

### 约旦向联合国大会控告  以色列军队窥伺约旦边境

1957-01-12
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦向联合国大会控告
    以色列军队窥伺约旦边境
    新华社11日讯　纽约消息：出席联合国大会的约旦代表里法伊在大会10日上午会议结束时宣读了他收到的约旦外交大臣的一封电报。电报说，最近几天，有大量以色列军队在约旦边境集中。约旦外交大臣要代表团立即同联合国秘书长进行联系，把这个电报的内容通知各国代表团。
    据里法伊向新闻界宣布，他已经就这个问题会晤过联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，哈马舍尔德向他保证将立即要求联合国巴勒斯坦停战监督机构就约旦陈述的局势提出报告。
    里法伊也已经要求联合国中阿拉伯国家的代表开会讨论约旦提出的问题。
