Title: New Year's Celebration (Photos)
Author: Wang Zhiyuan Cao Guijiang Gao Liang He Guozheng
Date: 1957-01-02
Page: 4

    New Year's Celebration
    Left picture: On New Year's Eve, in the beautifully decorated Juvenile Home, Father New Year extends New Year greetings to the children of Beijing, and the children from the Soviet Union and other People's Democratic countries who are participating in the celebration. Our reporter Wang Zhiyuan and Cao Guijiang photographed.
    Right picture: At the New Year's celebrations in the Beijing Knitting Factory, the No. 5 Public Bus Factory, the Winery, the Metrology Factory, and the Finance and Printing Factory, Father New Year offers congratulations to the artists who were invited to attend the evening parties.
    Our reporter Gao Liang photographed.
    Bottom left picture: The bells welcoming 1957 have rung, and a new year has begun. Let us laugh and sing for the prosperity and strength of our motherland and the lasting peace of the world.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter He Guozheng photographed.

### 新年联欢（图片）
王志渊　曹桂江  高粮  何国正
1957-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

    新年联欢
    左图：除夕晚上，在打扮得漂漂亮亮的少年之家，新年老人向参加联欢会的北京市少年儿童、苏联和人民民主国家的小朋友们贺年。本报记者　王志渊　曹桂江摄
    下图：在北京针织厂、公共汽车五厂、酿酒厂、度量衡厂和财印厂的联欢会上，新年老人向应邀出席晚会的艺术家们道贺。
    本报记者　高　粮摄
    下左图：迎接1957年的钟声响了，新的一年开始了。让我们为祖国的繁荣富强、世界的持久和平而欢笑、歌唱吧。
    新华社记者　何国正摄
