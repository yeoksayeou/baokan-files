Title: Two Soviet Musicians Return Home
Author:
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th — Soviet soprano soloist A. Alenichenko, bass soloist G. Gemilia and his wife, who were invited to visit and perform in China under the Sino-Soviet 1956 Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by plane and train on the 12th and 13th to return home.

During their stay in China, A. Alenichenko and G. Gemilia visited and performed in Tianjin, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other places, winning unanimous praise from audiences and musicians in various regions.

### 苏联两音乐家回国

1957-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联两音乐家回国
    据新华社13日讯　根据中苏1956年文化合作协定应邀来我国访问演出的苏联女高音独唱家阿列尼琴柯和男低音独唱家格米里亚及其夫人，12日和13日分别乘飞机和火车离北京返国。
    阿列尼琴柯和格米里亚在中国逗留期间，曾经到天津、上海、广州、武汉等地访问演出，获得各地听众和音乐界人士的一致赞扬。
