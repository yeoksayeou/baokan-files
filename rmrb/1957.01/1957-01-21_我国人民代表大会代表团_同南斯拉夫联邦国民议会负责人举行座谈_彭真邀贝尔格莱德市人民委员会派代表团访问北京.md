Title: Delegation of the National People's Congress of China Holds Symposium with Responsible Persons of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia / Peng Zhen Invites Delegation from Belgrade City People's Committee to Visit Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, January 20th – The delegation of the National People's Congress of China and the delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee held a symposium with responsible personnel of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia at 4:00 PM on the 19th.

    The Chairman of the Federal National Assembly, Pijade, first introduced to the delegation the organizational structure of the Federal National Assembly, the functions and powers of the Federal Council and the Council of Producers, the number of representatives in the two houses, and the election procedures. Subsequently, Pijade answered questions raised by members of the delegation. In addition to the entire delegation, the Chinese side attending the symposium included Wu Xiuquan, Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia; the Yugoslav side was represented by Vice Presidents of the Federal National Assembly Simić and Šentjurc, Chairman of the Federal Council Zečević, Chairman of the Council of Producers Božičević, Vice Chairman Vo. Popović, and heads of various committees of the two houses, totaling twenty-six people.

    The delegation attended a reception hosted by the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Serbia at 7:00 PM that evening. The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere until 9:00 PM. The Chinese side attending included the entire delegation and Ambassador Wu Xiuquan. The Yugoslav side was represented by Chairman of the Serbian National Assembly Stambolić, Vice Chairman Petrović, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the People's Republic of Serbia Veselinov, Chairman of the Federal National Assembly Pijade, Vice President Simić, Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs of the Federation Koča Popović, Deputy Secretary Vidić, Chairman of the Belgrade City People's Committee Minić, and others, totaling over a hundred people from both sides.

    At 9:30 AM that morning, a portion of the delegation, led by head of delegation Peng Zhen and deputy head Hu Ziang, visited the Rakovica Motor Factory. A discussion was held after the visit. Another portion of the delegation, led by deputy heads Li Jishen and Zhang Bojun, visited the Atomic Energy Institute near Belgrade.

    At 9:00 AM that morning, the delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee laid a wreath at the Cemetery of Liberators in Belgrade.

    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, January 19th – The delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee and a portion of the delegation of the National People's Congress of China visited the Belgrade City People's Committee on the afternoon of the 18th.

    Chairman of the Belgrade City People's Committee Minić warmly received the Chinese guests. Also present to receive the guests were Vice President of the Federal National Assembly Simić and several senior officials of the Belgrade City People's Committee.

    During the meeting between the hosts and guests, the head of the delegation and Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, invited the Belgrade City People's Committee to send a delegation to visit Beijing. Chairman Minić accepted the invitation.

    Subsequently, the Chinese guests attended a meeting held by the Belgrade City People's Committee regarding the draft urban social plan.

### 我国人民代表大会代表团  同南斯拉夫联邦国民议会负责人举行座谈  彭真邀贝尔格莱德市人民委员会派代表团访问北京

1957-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我国人民代表大会代表团
    同南斯拉夫联邦国民议会负责人举行座谈
    彭真邀贝尔格莱德市人民委员会派代表团访问北京
    新华社贝尔格莱德20日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团19日下午四点同南斯拉夫联邦国民议会负责人员举行座谈。
    联邦国民议会主席皮雅杰首先向代表团介绍了联邦国民议会的组织机构、联邦院和生产者院的职权和两院代表的人数以及选举的程序。随后皮雅杰回答了代表团团员所提出的问题。出席座谈会的中国方面除了代表团全体人员外，还有中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权；南斯拉夫方面出席的有联邦国民议会副主席西米奇、申丘尔茨，联邦院主席泽契维奇，生产者院主席鲍日契维奇、副主席沃·波波维奇和两院各委员会负责人等二十六人。
    代表团这一天晚上7时出席了塞尔维亚人民共和国国民议会的招待会。招待会在友好气氛中进行到9时方散。中国方面出席的有代表团全体人员和伍修权大使。南斯拉夫方面出席的有塞尔维亚国民议会主席斯坦鲍利奇，副主席彼特罗维奇，塞尔维亚人民共和国执行委员会主席维塞林诺夫，联邦国民议会主席皮雅杰，副主席西米奇，联邦外交国务秘书长科查·波波维奇，副秘书长维迪奇，贝尔格莱德市人民委员会主席米尼奇等。双方出席招待会的共有百余人。
    当天上午9时半，代表团一部分团员由彭真团长、胡子昂副团长率领参观了拉科维查摩托工厂，参观后举行了座谈。代表团另一部分团员由副团长李济深、章伯钧率领参观了贝尔格莱德附近的原子能研究所。
    当天上午9时，北京市人民委员会代表团曾去贝尔格莱德市解放者墓献花圈。
    新华社贝尔格莱德19日电　北京市人民委员会代表团和中国全国人民代表大会代表团的部分人员18日下午访问了贝尔格莱德市人民委员会。
    贝尔格莱德市人民委员会主席米尼奇亲切地接待了中国客人。在这里接待客人的还有南斯拉夫联邦国民议会副主席西米奇和贝尔格莱德市人民委员会的一些高级官员。
    在宾主会见的时候，代表团团长、北京市市长彭真邀请贝尔格莱德市人民委员会派一个代表团到北京访问。米尼奇主席接受了这项邀请。
    随后，中国客人参加了贝尔格莱德市人民委员会举行的有关讨论城市社会计划草案的会议。
