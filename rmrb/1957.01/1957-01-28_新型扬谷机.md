Title: New Type of Grain Winnowing Machine
Author: Jing Lan, Shao Cheng
Date: 1957-01-28
Page: 2

    New Type of Grain Winnowing Machine

    The Kaifeng Machinery Plant in Henan Province has successfully trial-produced the 10×ㄙ type grain winnowing machine.

    This grain winnowing machine is suitable for state-owned farms, agricultural production cooperatives, tractor stations, and grain depots of grain departments for winnowing, drying, cooling, and cleaning grains, as well as for conveying grains over a distance of fifteen meters. It can winnow ten tons of grain per hour.

    This grain winnowing machine is manufactured in imitation of the Soviet ВИМ—10уC type grain winnowing machine. It has a simple structure, is easy to adjust, and convenient to operate. Only one or two people are needed to adjust the machine and watch the operation while supervising the feeding of grain. (Jing Lan, Shao Cheng)

### 新型扬谷机
景岚  邵成
1957-01-28
第2版()
专栏：新技术新产品

    新型扬谷机
    河南省开封机械厂试制成功了—ㄍ——10×ㄙ型扬谷机。
    这种扬谷机适合于国营农场、农业生产合作社、拖拉机站及粮食部门的粮仓等用于扬场使谷物风干、降温、清净以及进行距离在十五公尺范围内的谷物运搬等工作。每小时可扬谷物十吨。
    这种扬谷机是仿照苏联的вим—10уC型扬谷机制造的，它的构造简单，容易调整、便于操作。只要有一、二个人把机器调整好，在看管粮食喂入的同时照看一下机器运转就行了。　（景岚、邵成）
