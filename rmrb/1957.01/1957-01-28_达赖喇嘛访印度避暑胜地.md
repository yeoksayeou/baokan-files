Title: Dalai Lama Visits Indian Hill Station
Author:
Date: 1957-01-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 27th: The Dalai Lama and his entourage visited Darjeeling, an Indian hill station at the southern foot of the Himalayas, on the 26th.

    The Dalai Lama gave blessings to the public in a square. He spoke before giving a brief sermon to the public. He expressed his gratitude for their warm welcome and hoped that the people of different races here would live peacefully according to the teachings of the Buddha.

    Two deputy ministers of West Bengal, Wangdi and Rai, held a grand luncheon on behalf of the Chief Minister of West Bengal to welcome the guests. At the banquet, the Dalai Lama congratulated India on its National Day.

    Before the luncheon, the guests visited the child welfare and maternity center in Darjeeling. The Dalai Lama donated funds to this institution.

    The Dalai Lama and his entourage returned to Kalimpong that afternoon.

### 达赖喇嘛访印度避暑胜地

1957-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛访印度避暑胜地
    据新华社新德里27日电　达赖喇嘛和他的随行人员在26日访问了位于喜马拉雅山南麓的印度避暑胜地大吉岭。
    达赖喇嘛在一个广场为公众祝福。在向公众简短地讲经以前，他讲了话。他对他们的热烈的欢迎表示感谢，并且希望这里的不同种族的人民按照佛陀的教义和平地生活。
    西孟加拉邦的两位副部长王迪和拉伊代表西孟加拉邦的首席部长举行盛大午宴欢迎客人。达赖喇嘛在宴会上祝贺印度国庆节。
    在午宴前，客人们参观了大吉岭的儿童福利和产科中心。达赖喇嘛向这个机构捐赠了款项。
    达赖喇嘛和他的随行人员在当天下午回到噶伦堡。
