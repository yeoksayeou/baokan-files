Title: A New Map Will Be Published
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-08
Page: 7

    Response to Criticism

    A New Map Will Be Published
    In a letter from reader Wu Jiaju published on page 7 of the People's Daily on December 26th of last year, a request was made for the publication of a more detailed map of China. Our publishing house is currently compiling a 1:3,000,000 scale atlas of China, which will be relatively rich and comprehensive in content. This atlas is expected to be published within this year.
                    Map Publishing House

### 新地图将要出版

1957-01-08
第7版()
专栏：对批评的反应

    新地图将要出版
    人民日报去年12月26日第七版刊载的读者吴家驹来信中，要求出版比较详细的中国地图。现在我社正在编制三百万分之一的中国地图集，它的内容比较丰富、全面。这本地图在今年内可以出版。
                    地图出版社
