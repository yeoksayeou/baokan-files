On the Construction Site of Wuhan Iron and Steel Complex
    The Wuhan Iron and Steel Complex has currently completed the first phase of construction of its auxiliary enterprises and has begun the construction of auxiliary projects such as metal structures and machinery repairs. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Hao Chunyi photo)
    The completed open-air prefabrication plant for concrete components is prefabricating concrete components. Steam rises gently through the thick canvas covering the prefabricated products.
    The completed concrete mixing platform. Each shift on this mixing platform can mix 165 to 180 cubic meters of concrete.
    A corner of the construction site of the Wuhan Iron and Steel Complex.

### 在武钢建设工地上（图片）

1957-01-12
第2版()
专栏：

    在武钢建设工地上
    武汉钢铁联合企业目前已完成了附属企业第一期工程的建设，并开始了金属结构、机械修理等辅助工程的建设。　新华社记者　郝纯一摄
    已建成的混凝土构件露天预制厂正在预制混凝土构件。蒸气透过复盖在预制品上的厚帆布袅袅上升。
    已建好的混凝土搅拌台。这个搅拌台每个台班可搅拌一百六十五到一百八十立方公尺混凝土。
    武钢建设工地的一角。
