Title: Another Batch of Beef and Mutton Shipped to Egypt
Author:
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 24th: China's second batch of frozen beef and mutton, totaling more than 1,100 tons, destined for Egypt, has recently been shipped for export. This batch of beef and mutton was processed by workers at meat processing plants in Xilin Gol League, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and other locations, according to the specifications requested by Egypt and in accordance with Islamic customs.

### 又一批牛羊肉运往埃及

1957-01-25
第3版()
专栏：

    又一批牛羊肉运往埃及
    新华社北京24日电　我国供应埃及的第二批冻牛、羊肉一千一百多吨，已在最近启运出口。这批出口的牛、羊肉是内蒙古自治区锡林郭勒盟等地肉类加工厂的职工按照埃及要求的规格和伊斯兰教习惯加工制成的。
