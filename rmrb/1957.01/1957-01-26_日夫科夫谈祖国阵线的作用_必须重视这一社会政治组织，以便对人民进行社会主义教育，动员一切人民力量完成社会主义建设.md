Title: Zhivkov Talks About the Role of the Fatherland Front: We Must Attach Importance to This Socio-Political Organization in Order to Conduct Socialist Education Among the People and Mobilize All the People's Strength to Complete Socialist Construction
Author:
Date: 1957-01-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, January 23rd: According to TASS, Todor Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, said recently at a meeting of the Fatherland Front organization in the Sofia region that the Central Committee of the Party believes that, under the current circumstances, the Fatherland Front is necessary to unite all the democratic forces of the Bulgarian people. The Fatherland Front should be developed as a socio-political organization for political and patriotic education of the people, as a mass pillar of the people's power, as a political and organizational form for strengthening the fighting alliance of the working class with all the working people in the country, and it should, of course, be led by the Communist Party, because no one can deny the leading role played by the Party in the ranks of the Fatherland Front.

At this work summary meeting, Zhivkov said that everyone knows that in the process of development along the socialist road, each people's democratic country has its own specific and unique social and economic forms. When we say that Bulgaria has some unique forms and characteristics in the process of socialist development, we mean that there is a Fatherland Front in our people's democratic historical system. Any underestimation and disregard for the role of this broad front as a socio-political organization and uniting all loyal and patriotic people in the country will cause us difficulties and hinder our efforts to conduct socialist education among the people and hinder us from mobilizing all the people's strength to complete the practical tasks of socialist construction.

Zhivkov finally said that, considering the great significance of Bulgarian-Soviet friendship as the driving force for the development of our socio-politics along the socialist road, the Central Committee of the Party believes that it is inappropriate to merge the work of strengthening and expanding Bulgarian-Soviet friendship with the activities of the Fatherland Front in the future. The upcoming Fourth Congress of the Fatherland Front will discuss and resolve the issue of how to launch an independent national movement to support Bulgarian-Soviet friendship under the leadership of the central and local Bulgarian-Soviet Friendship Committees, which is correct. It is also beneficial to resume holding Bulgarian-Soviet Friendship Month.

### 日夫科夫谈祖国阵线的作用  必须重视这一社会政治组织，以便对人民进行社会主义教育，动员一切人民力量完成社会主义建设

1957-01-26
第5版()
专栏：

    日夫科夫谈祖国阵线的作用
    必须重视这一社会政治组织，以便对人民进行社会主义教育，动员一切人民力量完成社会主义建设
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社索非亚23日讯：保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫最近在索非亚州的祖国阵线组织的一次会议上说，党中央委员会认为，在目前的情况下，祖国阵线是团结保加利亚人民的一切民主力量所必需的。祖国阵线应当作为一个对人民进行政治教育和爱国主义教育的社会政治组织、作为人民政权的群众支柱、作为加强国内工人阶级同全体劳动人民战斗联盟的政治形式和组织形式而得到发展，它当然应当受共产党的领导，因为谁也不能够否认党在祖国阵线的队伍中所起的领导作用。
    日夫科夫在这个总结工作的会议上说，大家都知道，各人民民主国家在沿着社会主义道路前进的发展过程中，各有自己具体的、独特的社会形式和经济形式。我们说保加利亚在社会主义发展过程中有着自己某些独特的形式和特点的时候，也就是指在我们人民民主的历史制度中有祖国阵线存在。任何过低估价和轻视这个作为社会政治组织和团结国内所有忠实的和爱国的人民的广泛阵线的作用，就会使我们造成困难，并且会妨碍我们努力去对人民进行社会主义教育，妨碍我们去动员人民的一切力量来完成社会主义建设的各项实际任务。
    日夫科夫最后说，考虑到作为我们社会政治沿着社会主义道路发展的动力的保苏友谊的巨大意义，党中央委员会认为今后把加强和扩大保苏友谊的工作同祖国阵线的活动合并起来是不适当的。即将举行的第四次祖国阵线代表大会将讨论和解决如何以中央和地方保苏友好委员会为领导，展开拥护保苏友谊的独立的全民运动的问题，这是正确的。恢复举行保苏友好月也是有益的。
