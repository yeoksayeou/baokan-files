Title: 朝、蒙报纸刊载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”  苏联出版了这篇文章的单行本并大量发行 (North Korean and Mongolian newspapers publish "Once Again on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship"; Soviet Union publishes a separate edition of the article and distributes it widely)
Author:
Date: 1957-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th: The "Rodong Sinmun" of North Korea yesterday and today continuously published the full text of the "People's Daily" article "Once Again on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" on its second, third, and fourth pages.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 6th: The Mongolian "Unen" today published the first, second, and third parts of the article "Once Again on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" from the "People's Daily" in full on its third and fourth pages. The remaining parts will be published in the next issue.

Prior to this, this newspaper and Mongolian Radio both reported the news of the "People's Daily" publishing this article. Mongolian Radio broadcast this article during its Chinese language broadcast on the 3rd.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th: The Soviet "Pravda Publishing House" has published a separate edition of the "People's Daily" article "Once Again on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" and has begun distributing it in large quantities.

### 朝、蒙报纸刊载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”  苏联出版了这篇文章的单行本并大量发行

1957-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    朝、蒙报纸刊载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”
    苏联出版了这篇文章的单行本并大量发行
    新华社平壤6日电　朝鲜“劳动新闻”昨天和今天在第二、三、四版连续刊载了“人民日报”发表的“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”的全文。
    新华社乌兰巴托6日电　蒙古“真理报”今天在第三、四版全文刊登了“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文的第一、第二和第三部分，其余部分将在下一期刊出。
    在这以前，这家报纸和蒙古广播电台都报道过“人民日报”发表这篇文章的消息。蒙古广播电台在3日的汉语广播时间广播了这篇文章。
    新华社莫斯科6日电　苏联“真理报出版社”出版了中国“人民日报”发表的文章“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”单行本，并已开始大量发行。
