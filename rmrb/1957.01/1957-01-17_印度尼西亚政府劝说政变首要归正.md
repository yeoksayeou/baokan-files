Title: Indonesian Government Persuades Leaders of the Coup to Return to the Right Path
Author:
Date: 1957-01-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 16th -- Antara News Agency reported on the 15th that the commander of the Third Infantry Regiment, Samosir, and the commander of the First Infantry Regiment, Gaharuddin, flew to Sumatra on the 16th, reportedly on orders to meet with Colonel Simbolon.

Antara News Agency learned that President Sukarno, in his capacity as Supreme Commander of the Armed Forces, also sent an envoy to meet with Simbolon. This envoy will urge him to return to the right path.

The Indonesian News Agency reported on the 15th that the commander of the Second Military Region stationed in South Sumatra, Barlian, had arrived in Padang, Central Sumatra, as a representative of the Army Chief of Staff, to hold talks with Hussein, chairman of the "Wild Bull Division Committee." It is understood that Barlian has already discussed with Hussein the planned meeting between the Army Chief of Staff and senior military officers from all of Sumatra.

### 印度尼西亚政府劝说政变首要归正

1957-01-17
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府劝说政变首要归正
    据新华社雅加达16日电　安塔拉通讯社15日报道，第三步兵团团长沙莫西尔和第一步兵团团长加哈鲁定在16日飞往苏门答腊，据说是奉命同西姆波朗上校会晤。
    安塔拉通讯社获悉，苏加诺总统也以武装部队最高统帅的身份派出了使者去会见西姆波朗。这个使者将要他回到正路上来。
    印度尼西亚新闻社15日报道，驻扎在南苏门答腊的第二军区司令巴利安已经以陆军参谋长代表的身份到达中苏门答腊的巴东，去同“雄牛师委员会”主席侯赛尼会谈。据悉，巴利安已经同侯赛尼讨论了计划召开的、陆军参谋长和全苏门答腊高级军官之间的会议的问题。
