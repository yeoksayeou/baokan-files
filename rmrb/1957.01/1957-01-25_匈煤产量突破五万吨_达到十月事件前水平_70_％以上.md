Title: Hungarian Coal Production Exceeds 50,000 Tons, Reaching Over 70% of Pre-October Incident Levels
Author:
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: According to a report by the Hungarian News Agency: On January 22nd, Hungary's coal production reached 50,213 tons, equivalent to more than 70% of the average daily production before the October Incident last year. This is the first time since October 23rd of last year that the 50,000-ton level has been surpassed.

### 匈煤产量突破五万吨  达到十月事件前水平70％以上

1957-01-25
第5版()
专栏：

    匈煤产量突破五万吨
    达到十月事件前水平70％以上
    据新华社24日讯　据匈牙利通讯社报道：匈牙利的煤产量，在1月22日这一天，已经达到五万零二百一十三吨，等于去年十月事件以前平均日产量的70％以上。这是去年10月23日以来第一次突破五万吨的水平。
