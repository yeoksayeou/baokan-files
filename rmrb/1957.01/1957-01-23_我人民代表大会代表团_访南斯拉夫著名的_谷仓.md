Title: Our National People's Congress Delegation Visits Yugoslavia's Famous "Granary"
Author:
Date: 1957-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Novi Sad, 22nd – The Chinese National People's Congress Delegation, led by Peng Zhen, and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee Delegation arrived in Novi Sad from Belgrade on the 21st for a visit. This city is the capital of the Autonomous Province of Vojvodina in the Serbian People's Republic.

Wu Xiuquan, the Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, accompanied the delegation.

On the same day, the entire delegation paid visits to the leaders of the Vojvodina Autonomous Province and the Novi Sad City Assembly and government, and met with heads of various departments. They held lengthy, cordial, and friendly talks with them on various issues related to agriculture. This autonomous province is Yugoslavia's famous granary, mainly based on agriculture and animal husbandry.

The delegation also split into two groups and separately visited the agricultural machinery factory in Novi Sad, the Agricultural Research Institute, and the "Becej" agricultural cooperative and the state-owned "Friendship and Unity" farm in the suburbs of the city.

That evening, Doronjski, Chairman of the National Assembly of the Autonomous Province of Vojvodina, hosted a banquet for the entire delegation.

After the banquet, the delegation left Novi Sad by train to visit the People's Republic of Bosnia and Herzegovina.

### 我人民代表大会代表团  访南斯拉夫著名的“谷仓”

1957-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我人民代表大会代表团
    访南斯拉夫著名的“谷仓”
    新华社诺维萨特22日电　由彭真率领的中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团21日从贝尔格莱德到达诺维萨特参观访问，这个城市是塞尔维亚人民共和国伏伊伏丁那自治省的首府。
    陪同代表团前往的有中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权。
    代表团全体人员在当天先后拜会了伏伊伏丁那自治省和诺维萨特市的议会和政府的领导人，并且和各部门负责人会了面，同他们就有关农业的各方面问题进行了长时间的、亲切、友好的交谈。这个自治省是南斯拉夫著名的谷仓，以农业和畜牧业为主。
    代表团还分成两个小组分别参观了诺维萨特市的农业机械工厂、农业研究所和市郊的“贝柴伊”农业合作社和国营“友谊团结”农场。
    当天晚上，伏伊伏丁那自治省国民议会主席多罗尼斯基设宴招待代表团全体人员。
    宴会结束以后，代表团乘火车离开诺维萨特前往波斯尼亚—黑塞哥维那人民共和国访问。
