Title: Australian "Forum" and Indian "New Age" Weekly Publish Article "More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship"
Author:
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 14th, Sydney Dispatch: The Australian "Forum" weekly published a detailed summary of the article "More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" published by the Beijing People's Daily on January 9th, occupying two pages.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 11th: The latest issue of the "New Age" weekly published a detailed summary of the People's Daily article "More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship," occupying two and a half pages.

### 澳大利亚“论坛”和印度“新世纪”周报  刊登“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文

1957-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚“论坛”和印度“新世纪”周报
    刊登“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文
    新华社14日讯　悉尼消息：澳大利亚“论坛”周报1月9日以两页的篇幅，刊载了北京人民日报发表的“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文的详细摘要。
    新华社新德里11日电　最近一期的“新世纪”周报以两页半的地位刊载了人民日报发表的“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”这篇文章的详细摘要。
