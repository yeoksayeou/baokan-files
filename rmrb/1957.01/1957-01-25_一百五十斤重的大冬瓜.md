Title: A 150-Jin Giant Winter Melon
Author: Sun Dexian
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 3

    Agriculture News

    A 150-Jin Giant Winter Melon
    The Mashazhen Agricultural Cooperative in Jianyang County, Fujian Province, had a bumper harvest of winter melons last year. The largest weighed 150 jin, and the average weighed 70 to 80 jin. The income from winter melons alone accounted for 56.5% of the total sideline income of the Mashazhen Agricultural Cooperative last year.
    The main reason for the bumper harvest of winter melons in the Mashazhen Agricultural Cooperative last year was that after collectivization last year, vegetable farmers were organized and paid attention to the cultivation and management of winter melons. (Sun Dexian)

### 一百五十斤重的大冬瓜
孙德籼
1957-01-25
第3版()
专栏：农业珍闻

    一百五十斤重的大冬瓜
    福建省建阳县麻沙镇农业社去年冬瓜获得了丰收，最大的一个重达一百五十斤，一般的也有七、八十斤重，麻沙镇农业社光是冬瓜收入就占去年副业总收入的56.5％。
    麻沙镇农业社去年冬瓜能获得丰收，主要还是由于在去年合作化以后，菜农组织起来了，注意了冬瓜栽培管理工作。　（孙德籼）
