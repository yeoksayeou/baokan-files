Title: At the Invitation of the Indian Government, Zhukov Will Visit India
Author:
Date: 1957-01-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd (TASS dispatch): The Indian government has invited Soviet Marshal Zhukov to visit India in late January 1957.

Marshal Zhukov has accepted the invitation from the Indian government.

### 应印度政府的邀请  朱可夫将访问印度

1957-01-04
第6版()
专栏：

    应印度政府的邀请
    朱可夫将访问印度
    新华社3日讯　塔斯社莫斯科2日讯：印度政府邀请苏联元帅朱可夫在1957年1月下旬访问印度。
    朱可夫元帅接受了印度政府的邀请。
