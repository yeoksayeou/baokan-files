Title: Dulles Anxious to Invade the Middle East; Urges Congress Again to Pass the Middle East Plan
Author:
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 15th. U.S. Secretary of State Dulles on the 14th once again demanded that Congress quickly pass the Eisenhower Doctrine plan.

Dulles asked Congress to take joint action with the U.S. President in "three important aspects" in the Middle East, namely: providing "military deterrent force"; providing military "aid" to Middle Eastern countries to help establish "loyal local forces"; and providing economic "aid."

Dulles also mentioned that he had considered using a "volunteer army" composed of "refugees" from communist countries.

### 杜勒斯急于侵略中东  再催国会通过中东计划

1957-01-16
第6版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯急于侵略中东
    再催国会通过中东计划
    据新华社15日讯　美国国务卿杜勒斯14日再次要求国会迅速通过艾森豪威尔主义计划。
    杜勒斯要求国会同美国总统一道来在中东采取共同行动的“三个重要方面”，这就是：提供“军事威慑力量”；给中东国家提供军事“援助”，帮助建立“忠实的当地部队”；以及提供经济“援助”。
    杜勒斯还谈到他曾经考虑过使用由来自共产主义国家的“难民”组成的“自愿军”。
