Title: Hubei Provincial Party Committee Decides Leaders to Go Deep into the Grassroots and Celebrate the Spring Festival with the People

Author:
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, January 11th. The Hubei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China recently decided that during the Spring Festival, Wang Renzhong, the First Secretary of the Provincial Party Committee, and the other five secretaries, members of the Provincial Party Committee, ministers of various departments, directors and bureau chiefs of the People's Committee, as well as the persons in charge of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, will go deep into grassroots units such as factories, schools, and agricultural cooperatives to make reports and celebrate the Spring Festival with the masses.

In its decision, the Provincial Party Committee also requested that the leaders of local, municipal, and county Party committees, as well as various organs, schools, and factories, should also go among the residents during the Spring Festival to have extensive contact with the masses and hold discussions and get-togethers. Each county party committee should also organize visiting groups to comfort the families of revolutionary martyrs, soldiers, and workers, as well as the masses in the old revolutionary base areas; convene symposiums for demobilized and transferred soldiers to listen to their opinions and help them solve their difficulties.

The decision of the Provincial Party Committee emphasized that leading comrades at all levels should, starting from this visit to grassroots units, maintain regular contact with these units in order to further strengthen the relationship between leaders and the masses.

### 湖北省委决定  领导干部深入基层与民同乐欢度春节

1957-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    湖北省委决定
    领导干部深入基层与民同乐欢度春节
    新华社武汉11日电　中共湖北省委员会最近决定，在春节期间省委第一书记王任重和其他五位书记、省委委员、各部部长、人民委员会各厅、局长以及中共武汉市委的负责人，分别深入到工厂、学校、农业合作社等基层单位去作报告，并且和群众一道欢度春节。
    省委在决定中还要求各地、市、县委，各机关、学校、工厂的领导人在春节期间也应到居民群众中去，和群众进行广泛接触，进行座谈和联欢。各县委还应组织慰问团，慰问烈、军、工属和老区的群众；召开复员转业军人座谈会，听取他们的意见，帮助他们解决困难。
    省委的决定强调指出，各级领导同志要从这次到基层单位开始，经常保持和这些单位的联系，以便进一步密切领导与群众的关系。
