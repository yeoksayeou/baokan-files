Title: The First Atomic Icebreaker is Under Construction
Author:
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 14th: The speed of the world's first atomic icebreaker, currently under construction in the Soviet Union, will reach 18 nautical miles per hour. This 16,000-ton displacement icebreaker will have 2.75 horsepower per ton of displacement, an index far exceeding that of the largest ordinary icebreakers. This matter, which was previously considered a fantasy, has now been transformed into reality by the immense creativity of the Soviet people. This was mentioned in a recent report published in the Soviet "Literary Gazette."

The report also stated that a panoramic view of the ship's interior can be seen from the deck. The engine room will soon be equipped with turbo generators, electric motors, and countless other machines. The atomic reactor will be housed in a reliable protective shell. There will be no need for stokers or mechanics on board. Instead, technicians of the "power watch," who will determine the reactor's power requirements and automatically control the power supply using sensitive electrical systems, will operate all power equipment. The total power of the generators used solely to propel the icebreaker will be over 30,000 kilowatts, with two additional 3,000-kilowatt auxiliary generators providing power for all other uses on the icebreaker.

The living conditions for the icebreaker's crew are very sophisticated. Each cabin will house only one or two people, and the ship will have a cinema, library, and a music room for music lovers. The entire ship is equipped with "artificial climate" devices.

The atomic icebreaker will be 134 meters long, with a maximum beam of over 27 meters, and a total power of 44,000 horsepower. It will be able to navigate and break ice continuously for up to twelve months without needing to enter any port.

### 第一艘原子破冰船在建造中  排水量一万六千吨，时速十八哩，能不断航行和破冰十二个月。

1957-01-15
第5版()
专栏：

    第一艘原子破冰船在建造中
    排水量一万六千吨，时速十八哩，能不断航行和破冰十二个月。
    新华社莫斯科14日电　苏联正在建造中的世界第一艘原子破冰船的速度将达每小时十八海哩。这艘排水量一万六千吨的破冰船每吨排水量所配备的动力为二点七五匹马力，这个指数远远超过了最大的普通破冰船的指数。这件事情在不久前还认为是一种幻想，今天已经被苏联人民的巨大创造力变为现实了。苏联“文学报”最近发表的一篇报道谈到了这些情况。
    这篇报道还说，从破冰船的甲板上可以看到船内的全景。机舱里面即将安装汽轮发电机、电动机和无数的其他机器。原子反应堆将安置在可靠的保护壳里面。船上不需要任何司炉和机械手。代替他们的是操纵所有动力设备的“动力岗哨”的技师，他们将确定反应堆需求动力的情况，运用灵敏的电线系统自动地控制动力供应。仅仅用来使破冰船开动的发电机的总功率就在三万瓩以上，另外还有两个功率各为三千瓩的辅助发电机，这两个发电机所发的电力将用于破冰船上的其他一切用途。
    破冰船航行人员的生活条件很考究。每个房舱仅住一、两个人，船上设有电影厅、图书馆和为音乐爱好者设置的音乐室。整个船上装置着“人造气候”的设备。
    原子破冰船的全长是一百三十四公尺，船身最宽的地方为二十七公尺多，全船的动力达四万四千匹马力。它能够不断航行和破冰达十二个月之久而不需要进入任何港口。
