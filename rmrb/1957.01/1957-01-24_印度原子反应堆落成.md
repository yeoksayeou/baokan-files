Title: Indian Atomic Reactor Completed

Author:
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 21st: Indian Prime Minister Nehru yesterday presided over the official inauguration ceremony for India's first atomic reactor and the Atomic Energy Agency at Trombay, sixteen miles from Mumbai. Several hundred scientists from various countries attended the ceremony, including Chinese representative Peng Huanwu.

### 印度原子反应堆落成

1957-01-24
第6版()
专栏：

    印度原子反应堆落成
    据新华社新德里21日电　印度总理尼赫鲁昨天在离孟买十六英里的特罗姆贝为印度的第一个原子反应堆和原子能机构主持了正式落成典礼。参加落成典礼的有来自各国的几百名科学家，其中有中国代表彭恒武。
