Title: Kachin National Congress and Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League Joint Statement Supporting Agreement on Sino-Burmese Border Issue
Author:
Date: 1957-01-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 10th -- The Burmese Kachin National Congress and the Kachin State Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League have issued a joint statement announcing their full support for the talks held in Beijing last November between Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League Chairman U Nu and Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai regarding the Sino-Burmese border issue.

The joint statement pointed out that the Sino-Burmese border issue should be resolved based on the following principles: based on justice and facts, according to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and upholding Sino-Burmese friendship.

The joint statement said: Based on these principles, both the Kachin National Congress and the Kachin State Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League support the agreement reached by U Nu and Zhou Enlai on the border issue.

The joint statement was issued after the Kachin National Congress and the Kachin State Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League held a meeting in Myitkyina on January 7th. The Kachin State government is jointly formed by the Kachin National Congress and the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League.

### 克钦国民大会和反法西斯人民自由同盟联合声明  支持关于中缅边界问题的协议

1957-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    克钦国民大会和反法西斯人民自由同盟联合声明
    支持关于中缅边界问题的协议
    新华社仰光10日电　缅甸克钦国民大会和克钦邦反法西斯人民自由同盟发表了一项联合声明，宣布它们完全支持反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努和中国总理周恩来去年11月在北京进行的关于中缅边界问题的会谈。
    联合声明指出，缅中边界问题应该根据下列原则解决，即：以正义和事实为基础，根据五项原则，并且要维护中缅友谊。
    联合声明说：根据这种原则，克钦国民大会和克钦邦反法西斯人民自由同盟都支持吴努和周恩来就边界问题所取得的协议。
    联合声明是在克钦国民大会和克钦邦反法西斯人民自由同盟1月7日在密支那举行会议以后发表的。克钦邦政府由克钦国民大会和反法西斯人民自由同盟联合组成。
