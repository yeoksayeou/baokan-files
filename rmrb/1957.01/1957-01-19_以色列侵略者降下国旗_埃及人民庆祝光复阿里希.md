Title: Israeli Aggressors Lower Flag, Egyptian People Celebrate the Recovery of Al-Arish
Author:
Date: 1957-01-19
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Cairo dispatch: According to a broadcast by Cairo Radio, 500 Egyptian border police entered Al-Arish on the 16th.

    The United Nations Emergency Force entered the city of 30,000 people on the 15th after the Israeli army withdrew. On this day, the masses celebrated the recovery of the city with jubilation, holding Egyptian flags and portraits of Nasser.

    At 9:45 a.m. on the 15th, the Israeli occupation authorities lowered the Israeli flag from the top of the office building in Al-Arish, with Israeli Army Chief of Staff Dayan also present. He told reporters: "We have to swallow the bitter pill." He said that the withdrawal from Al-Arish concluded the second stage of the withdrawal from the Sinai Peninsula.

### 以色列侵略者降下国旗  埃及人民庆祝光复阿里希

1957-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列侵略者降下国旗
    埃及人民庆祝光复阿里希
    新华社18日讯　开罗消息：据开罗电台广播，埃及边境警察五百人在16日进入阿里希。
    联合国紧急部队15日在以色列军队撤出后进入这个拥有三万人口的城市。这一天，群众举着埃及国旗和纳赛尔的肖像，欢欣鼓舞地庆祝这个城市的光复。
    在15日上午九点四十五分，以色列占领军当局降下了它在阿里希城内办公大厦顶上的以色列国旗，当时以色列陆军参谋长达扬也在场。他对记者们说：“我们必须吞下苦丸药。”他说，撤出阿里希就结束了撤出西奈半岛的第二阶段。
