Title: French and Czech Party Delegations Begin Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-01-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 17th: The Czechoslovak News Agency reported that the delegation of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party and the delegation of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party began talks in Prague on the morning of January 16th.

Representing the French Communist Party Central Committee at the talks were Piou, Vermeersch, Seguy, and Villon. Representing the Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee at the talks were Novotný, Široký, Fierlinger, Kopetsky, Hendrych, Koucký, and Soják.

The next meeting will be held on the morning of the 17th.

### 法捷两党代表团开始会谈

1957-01-18
第5版()
专栏：

    法捷两党代表团开始会谈
    新华社布拉格17日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：法国共产党中央委员会代表团和捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会代表团1月16日早晨在布拉格开始举行会谈。
    代表法共中央参加会谈的是皮佑、维美徐、塞吉和维戎；代表捷共中央参加会谈的是诺沃提尼、西罗基、费林格、柯别茨基、亨德里赫、科乌茨基和索契克。
    下次会谈将在17日早晨举行。
