Title: Premier Zhou to Visit Hungary
Author:
Date: 1957-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: To further strengthen the friendly and fraternal cooperative relations between the Hungarian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China, the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government has invited Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, to pay a friendly visit to Hungary. Premier Zhou Enlai has accepted the invitation and will travel to Hungary in mid-January after visiting the Soviet Union and Poland.

### 周总理将访问匈牙利

1957-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理将访问匈牙利
    新华社9日讯　为进一步加强匈牙利人民共和国和中华人民共和国之间的友好和兄弟般的合作关系，匈牙利人民共和国工农革命政府邀请中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来到匈牙利作友好访问。周恩来总理已经接受了邀请，将于1月中旬在访问苏联和波兰以后前往匈牙利。
