Title: Cambodian "People's Newspaper" Carries Article Welcoming Our Radio Station's Cambodian Broadcasts
Author:
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 4th: The Cambodian-language "People's Newspaper" published an article on January 1st titled "The Voice of Cambodia in Beijing," which stated: "We are very pleased that People's China is willing to dedicate two time slots each day on Beijing Radio to broadcast Cambodian language programs, broadcasting news that the Cambodian people need to know. We hope that through Beijing Radio's broadcasts, our people will be able to avoid being poisoned by the propaganda peddled by imperialism."
    The Cambodian language program of the Central People's Broadcasting Station of China began on December 15th of last year.

### 柬埔寨“人民报”载文  欢迎我电台的柬语广播

1957-01-05
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨“人民报”载文
    欢迎我电台的柬语广播
    据新华社金边4日电　柬埔寨文的“人民报”1日在一篇题为“柬埔寨的声音在北京”的文章中说：“我们很高兴人民中国愿意每天抽出两个时间在北京电台广播柬埔寨语节目，广播柬埔寨人民需要知道的新闻。希望通过北京电台的广播，我们的人民能够免受帝国主义贩卖的宣传的毒害。”
    中国中央人民广播电台的柬埔寨语节目是从去年12月15日开始的。
