Title: Port Said Scheduled to Open for Shipping Today
Author:
Date: 1957-01-08
Page: 6

    Port Said Scheduled to Open for Shipping Today
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 7th - Cairo newspapers reported today that Port Said harbor will open for shipping from January 8th.
    The Ports and Lighthouses Authority issued a statement yesterday announcing that ships entering Port Said should telegraph the Suez Canal Authority twenty-four hours in advance of arrival. Until further notice, ships are only permitted to enter the port between 8:00 AM and 3:00 PM local time.
    The first batch of evacuated people will return to Port Said tomorrow, totaling three thousand. These people have been living in social service buildings and schools throughout the country since leaving Port Said.
    Five hundred students from Cairo University will travel to Port Said on January 11th to participate in reconstruction work.

### 塞得港预定今日开放航运

1957-01-08
第6版()
专栏：

    塞得港预定今日开放航运
    新华社开罗7日电　开罗报纸今天报道，塞得港港口将从1月8日起开放航运。
    港口和灯塔管理局昨天发表公告宣布，开进塞得港的船只应该在到达以前二十四小时预先打电报通知苏伊士运河管理局。在另有通知以前，船只只许在当地时间八时至十五时之间驶进港口。
    第一批回去的疏散的人将要在明天回到塞得港，人数共有三千。这些人在离塞得港以后一直分别住在全国各地的社会服务事业建筑物和学校中。
    开罗大学五百名学生将在1月11日前往塞得港参加重建工作。
