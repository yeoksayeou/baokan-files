Title: Rahman Passes Through Bangkok, Says Malaya Will Not Join Southeast Asia Group
Author:
Date: 1957-01-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, 17th -- Abdul Rahman, Chief Minister of the Federation of Malaya, said in Bangkok on the 16th that Malaya will not join the Manila Treaty Organization. Rahman was passing through Bangkok on his way back home from London.

### 拉赫曼路过曼谷说马来亚不参加东南亚集团

1957-01-18
第6版()
专栏：

    拉赫曼路过曼谷说马来亚不参加东南亚集团
    新华社17日讯　曼谷消息：马来亚联合邦首席部长阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼16日在曼谷说，马来亚将不参加马尼拉条约组织，拉赫曼是从伦敦返国途中路过曼谷的。
