Title: French Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1957-01-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, a delegation of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party arrived in Prague by plane on the 15th.

	The delegation is composed of Pi Yu and Wei Meixu, members of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party Central Committee, Segi, alternate member of the Political Bureau, and Wei Rong, member of the Central Committee.

	Fierlinger, Kopecky, Hendrych, Koutský, and Sochurek were present at the airport on behalf of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party to welcome the delegation.

### 法共代表团到布拉格

1957-01-17
第5版()
专栏：

    法共代表团到布拉格
    新华社布拉格16日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，法国共产党中央委员会代表团在15日搭飞机到布拉格。
    代表团由法共中央政治局委员皮佑和维美徐、政治局候补委员塞吉和中央委员维戎等组成。
    代表捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会前往机场欢迎的有费林格、柯别茨基、亨德里赫、科乌茨基和索契克。
