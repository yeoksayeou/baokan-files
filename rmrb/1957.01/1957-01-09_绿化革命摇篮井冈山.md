Title: Greening the Revolutionary Cradle, Jinggang Mountain
Author:
Date: 1957-01-09
Page: 3

    Greening the Revolutionary Cradle, Jinggang Mountain
    (Our report) The Sui Chuan County of Jiangxi Province is organizing the broad masses to green the revolutionary cradle, Jinggang Mountain. Many trees on Jinggang Mountain were burned by the Kuomintang reactionaries before liberation. According to surveys, there are a total of 24,455 mu of barren hills. After liberation, the local area made many preparations for greening Jinggang Mountain, establishing the state-owned Jinggang Mountain Forest Farm and cultivating more than 50 mu of fir seedlings. From November 1st to December 6th last year, a tree planting and land preparation campaign was launched on Jinggang Mountain. In the ten townships of Chuangxi, Jianshe, Heping, Caolin, Duqian, Xiaqi, Huangao, Wujiang, Penzhu, and Jinggang, 67 agricultural cooperatives mobilized a total of 1,881 members to form a tree planting army, which marched onto Jinggang Mountain in teams to green the heroic Jinggang Mountain.

### 绿化革命摇篮井冈山

1957-01-09
第3版()
专栏：

    绿化革命摇篮井冈山
    本报讯　江西遂川县组织广大群众，绿化革命摇篮井冈山。井冈山的许多林木，在解放以前，曾被国民党反动派烧光。据调查，共有荒山二万四千四百五十五亩。解放以后，当地为绿化井冈山，作了许多准备工作，建立了国营井冈山林场，培育杉苗五十多亩。从去年11月1日到12月6日，在井冈山开展了造林整地运动。在窗溪、建设、和平、草林、堆前、下七、黄垇、五江、盆珠、井冈等十个乡六十七个农业社内，共调集了一千八百八十一名社员，组成一支造林大军，一队一队开上了井冈山，绿化英雄的井冈山。
