Title: Let Steamships Sail to Yunnan
Author:
Date: 1957-01-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 3rd - The first phase of dredging work has commenced on the shallow-water steamboat channel on the lower reaches of the Red River in Yunnan Province.

	This channel stretches over 220 *li* from the Manggeng ferry in the Honghe Hani Autonomous Region to Hekou on the Sino-Vietnamese border.

	Yunnan Province has four well-known rivers, the Nujiang, Lancangjiang, Honghe, and Jinshajiang, but due to numerous shoals and dangerous waters, none of them are navigable by ships.

### 让汽船航行到云南

1957-01-04
第2版()
专栏：

    让汽船航行到云南
    据新华社昆明3日电　云南省内红河下游浅水汽轮航道的第一期开凿工程开工。
    这条航道从红河哈尼族自治区的曼耕渡口到中越边境的河口，长二百二十多华里。
    云南省有怒江、澜沧江、红河、金沙江等四条闻名的河流，但是滩多水险没有一条能通行轮船。
