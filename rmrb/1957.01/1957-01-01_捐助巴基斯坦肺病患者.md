Title: Donation to Pakistani Lung Disease Patients
Author:
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 27th -- Premier Zhou Enlai, who is currently visiting Pakistan, has donated 40,000 rupees to the welfare of Pakistani lung disease patients.

Premier Zhou Enlai handed the donation to President Mirza before leaving Karachi for Peshawar and Lahore.

### 捐助巴基斯坦肺病患者

1957-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    捐助巴基斯坦肺病患者
    新华社卡拉奇27日电　正在巴基斯坦访问的周恩来总理捐助了四万卢比给巴基斯坦的肺病患者的福利事业。
    周恩来总理在离开卡拉奇去白沙瓦和拉合尔以前把这笔捐款交给了米尔扎总统。
