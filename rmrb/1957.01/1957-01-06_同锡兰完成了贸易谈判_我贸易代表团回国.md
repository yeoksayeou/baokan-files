Title: Trade Negotiations with Ceylon Completed, Our Trade Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th dispatch: The Chinese government trade delegation, led by Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, which visited Ceylon at the invitation of the Ceylon government, returned to Beijing this afternoon.

During its visit to Ceylon, the delegation completed the relevant trade negotiations and visited various places. The delegation visited Ceylon's rubber plantations, rubber processing plants, rubber research institutes, and tea research institutes.

### 同锡兰完成了贸易谈判  我贸易代表团回国

1957-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    同锡兰完成了贸易谈判
    我贸易代表团回国
    据新华社5日讯　应锡兰政府邀请前往锡兰访问的我国政府贸易代表团，在我国对外贸易部部长叶季壮率领下，已经在今天下午回到北京。
    代表团在访问锡兰期间，已经完成了有关的贸易谈判，并且在各地进行参观，代表团曾经参观了锡兰的橡胶园、橡胶加工厂、橡胶研究所、茶叶研究所等。
