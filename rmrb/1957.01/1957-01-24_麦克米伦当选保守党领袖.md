Title: Macmillan Elected Leader of the Conservative Party
Author:
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, January 23rd: Britain's new Prime Minister, Macmillan, was elected leader of the party on the 22nd at a secret meeting of 1,000 people, including the national executive committee, members of the upper and lower houses of parliament, and activists of the Conservative Party.

### 麦克米伦当选保守党领袖

1957-01-24
第6版()
专栏：

    麦克米伦当选保守党领袖
    据新华社23日讯　伦敦消息：英国新任首相麦克米伦在22日保守党举行的全国执行委员会负责人、上下院议员及活动分子一千人的秘密会议上当选为党的领袖。
