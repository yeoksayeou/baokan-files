Title: International Table Tennis Championships Conclude; Romanian, Czech and Hungarian Players Win Five Championships
Author:
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, January 15th: The 1957 International Table Tennis Championships held in Bucharest have concluded. Romanian players achieved the most outstanding results.

In the women's singles final, Romania's Zeller defeated multiple world champion Rozeanu (Romania) three games to zero. The two of them also won the women's doubles championship. In addition, two Romanian male players, Retea and Gartner, defeated Czechoslovakia's famous players Tokar and Stipcik three games to zero to win the men's doubles championship.

The mixed doubles championship was won by Czechoslovakia's Stipcik and Romania's Rozeanu.

Hungarian athlete Berczik won the men's singles championship.

Chinese player Wang Chuanyao won the men's consolation championship.

### 国际乒乓球锦标赛结束  罗捷匈取得五项冠军

1957-01-16
第5版()
专栏：

    国际乒乓球锦标赛结束
    罗捷匈取得五项冠军
    据新华社布加勒斯特15日电  在布加勒斯特举行的1957年国际乒乓球锦标赛已经结束。罗马尼亚选手获得了最优秀的成绩。
    在女子单打决赛中，罗马尼亚的泽勒尔以三比零击败了多次世界冠军获得者罗齐亚努（罗）。她们两人还获得了女子双打的冠军。另外两名罗马尼亚男子选手雷特和甘特纳以三比零胜捷克斯洛伐克的著名选手托卡和斯蒂匹克，获得男子双打冠军。
    获得男女混合双打冠军的是捷克斯洛伐克的斯蒂匹克和罗马尼亚的罗齐亚努。
    匈牙利运动员别尔切克取得男子单打的冠军。
    中国选手王传耀获得了男子安慰赛冠军。
