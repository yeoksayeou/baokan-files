Title: National Congress of Workers' Family Representatives to be Held in June This Year
Author:
Date: 1957-01-23
Page: 4

    National Congress of Workers' Family Representatives
    Will Be Held in June This Year
    Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd -- The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation have decided to hold the National Congress of Workers' Family Representatives in Beijing on June 4th this year.
    The task of this meeting is to summarize and exchange experiences in the work of workers' families, determine the current guidelines and tasks for the work of workers' families, mobilize the masses of workers' families to exert their potential strength, respond to the call of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to launch a campaign to increase production and practice economy, and better cooperate with workers to complete the state plan.
    In order to promote the preparation work for this meeting by all relevant parties in the country, the first plenary meeting of the preparatory committee was held on the 21st.
    The director of the preparatory committee for the National Congress of Workers' Family Representatives is Lai Ruoyu, chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; the vice directors are Zhang Yun, vice chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Yang Zhihua, director of the Women Workers' Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; the committee members include thirty people from all fields.

### 全国职工家属代表会议  将在今年六月召开

1957-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

    全国职工家属代表会议
    将在今年六月召开
    据新华社22日讯　中华全国总工会和中华全国民主妇女联合会决定，今年6月4日在北京举行全国职工家属代表会议。
    这次会议的任务，是总结和交流职工家属工作的经验，确定当前职工家属工作的方针和任务，动员职工家属群众发挥自己的潜在力量，响应中共中央提出的开展增产节约运动的号召，更好地配合职工完成国家计划。
    为了推动全国各有关方面做好这次会议的准备工作，21日举行了第一次筹备委员会全体会议。
    全国职工家属代表会议筹备委员会的主任是中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，副主任是中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴和中华全国总工会女工部部长杨之华；委员有各方面的人员共三十人。
