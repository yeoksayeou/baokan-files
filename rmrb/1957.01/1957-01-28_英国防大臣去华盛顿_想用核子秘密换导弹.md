Title: British Defense Minister Goes to Washington, Wants to Trade Nuclear Secrets for Missiles
Author:
Date: 1957-01-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 27th dispatch from New York: British newly appointed Minister of Defense Sandys arrived in New York on the 26th en route from London to Washington to meet with U.S. Secretary of Defense Wilson.

Comments from some British newspapers suggest that Sandys' trip aims to exchange the secrets of British nuclear weapons research for the supply of American missiles.

### 英国防大臣去华盛顿  想用核子秘密换导弹

1957-01-28
第6版()
专栏：

    英国防大臣去华盛顿
    想用核子秘密换导弹
    据新华社27日讯　纽约消息：英国新任国防大臣桑迪斯26日在从伦敦到华盛顿同美国国防部长威尔逊会谈的中途抵达纽约。
    英国一些报纸的评论认为，桑迪斯此行的目的是希望以英国核子武器研究的秘密去换取美国导弹的供应。
