Title: U.S. Plans to Build "Northern Tier Highway" in the Middle East, Linking Baghdad Pact and Bypassing the Suez Canal
Author:
Date: 1957-01-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: Washington dispatch: Pakistani Finance Minister Amjad Ali, who is currently visiting the United States, revealed on the 25th that the four Muslim countries of the Baghdad Pact intend to use the majority of the $200 million allocated to "aid" Middle Eastern countries under the "Eisenhower Doctrine" in 1957 to build a "Northern Tier Highway" connecting these four countries.

Amjad Ali told reporters that this plan had already been discussed in the Economic Committee of the Baghdad Pact and had been well received. Although the United States has not formally joined the Baghdad Pact, it participates in this committee.

He said that the highway would connect Karachi, Tehran, Baghdad, and Ankara, and extend all the way to Istanbul.

Reports say that this highway, approximately 3,000 miles long, would provide an alternative land transportation route between the Arabian Sea and the Mediterranean Sea, bypassing the Suez Canal.

U.S. Secretary of State Dulles, while testifying before the Senate on the 24th, refused to specify how the $200 million would be used to "aid" Middle Eastern countries, saying: "If we fix everything we intend to do, then this plan will not achieve its purpose."

### 美国打算在中东建筑“北层公路”  连结巴格达集团　绕过苏伊士运河

1957-01-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美国打算在中东建筑“北层公路”
    连结巴格达集团　绕过苏伊士运河
    新华社26日讯　华盛顿消息：正在美国访问的巴基斯坦财政部长阿姆亚德·阿里25日透露，巴格达条约四个穆斯林国家打算把“艾森豪威尔主义”计划在1957年度“援助”中东国家的两亿美元的大部分用来建筑一条连结这四个国家的“北层公路”。
    阿姆亚德·阿里对新闻记者说，这个计划已经在巴格达条约的经济委员会上进行了讨论，并且受到了良好的考虑。美国虽然没有正式参加巴格达条约，但是它参加了这个委员会。
    他说，这条公路将把卡拉奇、德黑兰、巴格达和安卡拉连接起来，一直通到伊斯坦布尔。
    有消息说，这条长约三千英里的公路将为阿拉伯海和地中海之间提供一条代替的陆上交通路线，以绕过苏伊士运河。
    美国国务卿杜勒斯24日在国会参议院作证的时候，曾经拒绝说明怎样使用这二亿美元来“援助”中东国家，他说：“如果我们对我们打算要做的每一件事都固定下来，那么这个计划就不会达到它的目的。”
