Title: Soviet News Department Director Hosts Chinese Journalists
Author:
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 11th: TASS reports that the Director of the Soviet Foreign Ministry's News Department, Ilyichev, held a reception on the 10th to welcome the Chinese journalists who accompanied the Chinese government delegation on their visit to the Soviet Union. Editors and journalists from major Soviet central newspapers, magazines, TASS, the Soviet News Bureau, and responsible staff members from the Soviet Foreign Ministry attended as guests.

### 苏联新闻司长招待我记者

1957-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联新闻司长招待我记者
    据新华社11日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部新闻司司长伊利切夫在10日举行招待会，欢迎随同中国政府代表团访问苏联的中国新闻记者。苏联中央各报纸、杂志、塔斯社、苏联新闻局的编辑和记者以及苏联外交部的一些负责工作人员出席作陪。
