Title: Israeli Cabinet Publicly Defies UN Resolution, Decides Not to Withdraw from Gaza Strip and Southern Sinai
Author:
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tel Aviv, January 14th (TASS): According to the "LaMerhav" newspaper, the Israeli cabinet decided at its final meeting on January 13th that it would not withdraw its troops from Sharm el-Sheikh in the southern Sinai Peninsula until Israel receives the so-called guarantee of navigation in the Gulf of Eilat that it demands. The Israeli government stated that it would firmly oppose the return of the Gaza Strip to the Egyptians and would also disagree with the United Nations monitoring the area.

### 以色列内阁公然抗拒联大决议  决定不撤出加沙地区和西奈南部

1957-01-16
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列内阁公然抗拒联大决议
    决定不撤出加沙地区和西奈南部
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社特拉维夫14日讯：据“拉麦哈夫报”报道，以色列13日内阁会议最后决定，在以色列没有得到它所要求的所谓埃拉特湾航行的保障之前，不从西奈半岛南部沙姆沙伊赫撤军。以色列政府声明，它将坚决反对把加沙地区归还埃及人，并且也不同意由联合国监管这一地区。
