Title: Peddling the "Eisenhower Doctrine": Dulles Meets with Hammarskjöld; British and French Governments Also Notified
Author:
Date: 1957-01-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st dispatch from New York: On New Year's Eve of 1956, after rushing from Washington to New York, U.S. Secretary of State Dulles held a hurried meeting with United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld. Dulles stated afterward that he met with Hammarskjöld at the request of Eisenhower to discuss the U.S. government's newly drafted military-economic plan for the Middle East.

The main contents of this plan, known as the "Eisenhower Doctrine," are twofold: First, to grant Eisenhower the power to use U.S. armed forces in the Middle East at any time; and second, to provide a two-year economic aid program totaling over $400 million, which any Middle Eastern country from the Strait of Gibraltar to the Arabian Sea can obtain. However, the amount each country can receive will be decided solely by Eisenhower.

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st dispatch from London: A British Foreign Office spokesperson said on December 31st that the British and American governments had not discussed the U.S. intentions in the Middle East.

The spokesperson said that U.S. Secretary of State Dulles had merely told the British Ambassador to the U.S., Harold Caccia, "a little information about the U.S. government's intention to request authority from Congress." Dulles did not give Harold Caccia any memorandum on U.S. policy in the Middle East.

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st dispatch from Paris: A French Foreign Ministry spokesperson said on December 31st that the United States had informed the French government of Eisenhower's Middle East plan.

The spokesperson did not specifically discuss the content of the plan. He merely said that the plan took into account the "threat" currently existing in the Middle East.

### 贩卖“艾森豪威尔主义”  杜勒斯同哈马舍尔德会谈  英法两国政府也得到通知

1957-01-02
第6版()
专栏：

    贩卖“艾森豪威尔主义”
    杜勒斯同哈马舍尔德会谈
    英法两国政府也得到通知
    据新华社1日讯　纽约消息：1956年除夕的那一天，美国国务卿杜勒斯在从华盛顿赶到纽约之后，同联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德进行了匆忙的会谈。杜勒斯在事后发表声明说，他是应艾森豪威尔之请去见哈马舍尔德的，目的是同他交谈关于美国政府新近拟订的对中东的军事—经济计划。
    这项被称为“艾森豪威尔主义”计划的主要内容有两点：一、给艾森豪威尔以随时可以在中东地区使用美国武装部队的权力；二、规定一项总额达四亿多美元的两年经济援助计划，从在直布罗陀海峡到阿拉伯海的任何中东国家都可以获得这笔援助。不过每一个国家可以获得多少，只有艾森豪威尔才能决定。
    新华社1日讯　伦敦消息：英国外交部发言人12月31日说，英美两国政府并没有就美国在中东的意图进行过讨论。
    发言人说，美国国务卿杜勒斯只不过告诉了英国驻美大使哈罗德·卡西亚“一点关于美国政府想要求国会授予权力的打算的情报”。杜勒斯并没有就美国在中东的政策给哈罗德·卡西亚任何备忘录。
    新华社1日讯　巴黎消息：法国外交部发言人12月31日说，美国已经把艾森豪威尔的中东计划告诉给法国政府。
    发言人没有具体谈到这个计划的内容。他只是说，这个计划考虑到了目前中东所存在的“威胁”。
