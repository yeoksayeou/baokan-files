Title: Small Windproof Matches
Author: Yu Xiaofan
Date: 1957-01-13
Page: 2

    Small Windproof Matches
    (Our newspaper dispatch) Ningbo Zhengda Match Factory in Zhejiang province, on the basis of successful trial production of windproof matches, produced another batch of small windproof matches at the end of last year.
    The small windproof matches were successfully trial-produced according to the principles of Soviet windproof matches. The shape of this kind of match is shorter and smaller than windproof matches. The matchstick is the same as ordinary matches, but the match head is three times longer than ordinary matches (about 10 mm). Its characteristic is strong wind resistance, so it is especially suitable for field workers. (Yu Xiaofan)

### 小型抗风火柴
虞孝范
1957-01-13
第2版()
专栏：

    小型抗风火柴
    本报讯　浙江宁波正大火柴厂，在试制抗风火柴成功的基础上，去年年底又生产了一批小型抗风火柴。
    小型抗风火柴是根据苏联的抗风火柴原理试制成功的。这种火柴的外形，比抗风火柴短小，火柴梗与普通火柴相同，但药头要比普通火柴长三倍（10公厘左右）。它的特点是抗风力强，因此特别适合野外工作者使用。　（虞孝范）
