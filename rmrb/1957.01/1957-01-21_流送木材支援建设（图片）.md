Title: Log Rafting Supports Construction (Pictures)
Author: Guo Weiqi
Date: 1957-01-21
Page: 2

    Log Rafting Supports Construction
    Liannan Yao Autonomous County in Guangdong Province exports a large amount of timber every year to support national construction. Left picture: Timber is floated from mountain streams into the Sanjiang River. Right picture: A large amount of timber gathers in the Sanjiang River, ready to be transported to various places.

### 流送木材支援建设（图片）
郭伟祺
1957-01-21

专栏：

    流送木材支援建设
    广东省连南瑶族自治县每年输出大批木材支援国家建设。　左图：木材从山溪流放出三江河。　右图：大批木材在三江河齐集，准备运送到各地去。
                郭伟祺摄（南方日报社稿）
