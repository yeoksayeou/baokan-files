Title: Jilin Exceeds Soybean Export Plan
Author: Deng Xiangrong
Date: 1957-01-11
Page: 3

    Jilin Exceeds Soybean Export Plan

    (Our newspaper reports) Jilin Province has exceeded its 1956 soybean export target of 118,000 tons.

    Jilin Province produces a large quantity of high-quality soybeans, which are the province's leading export. The quality of exported soybeans improved significantly last year. Currently, almost all of the tens of thousands of tons of soybeans inspected and shipped are first-class or special-grade products.
                      (Deng Xiangrong)

### 吉林超额完成大豆出口计划
邓向荣
1957-01-11
第3版()
专栏：

    吉林超额完成大豆出口计划
    本报讯　吉林省1956年的十一万八千吨大豆出口任务，已经超额完成。
    吉林省出产的大豆数量多，质量好，在全省输出品中大豆占首位。去年出口的大豆质量有很大提高，目前检查发出的十几万吨的大豆几乎都是一等品和特等品。
                      （邓向荣）
