Title: Northern Regions to Experience Gale-Force Winds Today; Temperature Drop of Around Ten Degrees

Author:

Date: 1957-01-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th – The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a gale and temperature drop message on the 26th as follows: A cold air mass currently located in Central Asia will invade from the north of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region in our country in a southeasterly direction. Initial estimates indicate that from the morning of the 27th to the morning of the 29th, the northern part of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, the Hexi Corridor in Gansu Province, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Northeastern Region, the North China Region, and the Bohai Sea will experience northwesterly gales of level five to seven, from west to east, and a temperature drop of around ten degrees Celsius.

### 北方地区今起有大风  温度将降低十度左右

1957-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    北方地区今起有大风
    温度将降低十度左右
    新华社26日讯　中央气象科学研究所26日发布大风降温消息如下：目前有冷空气在中亚细亚，将经我国新疆维吾尔自治区北部向东南侵袭。初步估计自27日晨到29日晨，新疆维吾尔自治区北部、甘肃省河西走廊、内蒙古自治区、东北地区、华北地区以及渤海等自西向东先后将有五级到七级的偏北大风，并且降温摄氏十度左右。
