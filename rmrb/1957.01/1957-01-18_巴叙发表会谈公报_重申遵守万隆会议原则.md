Title: Pakistan and Syria Issue Joint Communiqué, Reaffirming Adherence to Bandung Conference Principles
Author:
Date: 1957-01-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 17th. A joint communiqué issued by Pakistan and Syria on the evening of the 16th reaffirmed that "they will abide by the principles and resolutions of the Bandung Conference and guarantee to seek the settlement of international disputes on a just and equal basis."

The communiqué, issued after talks between the leaders of the two countries during Syrian President Kuwatly's visit to Pakistan, said: "These talks provided a useful opportunity to exchange views on all issues discussed. These talks proved that the two countries have much in common and have helped to better understand each other's views on all issues discussed. Both sides expressed satisfaction with this."

The communiqué stated: "Both sides reaffirmed their deep conviction of the need to maintain international peace and security within the framework of the United Nations and in accordance with the goals of the United Nations Charter."

### 巴叙发表会谈公报  重申遵守万隆会议原则

1957-01-18
第6版()
专栏：

    巴叙发表会谈公报
    重申遵守万隆会议原则
    新华社卡拉奇17日电　巴基斯坦和叙利亚在16日晚发表的一份联合公报中重申，“他们将遵守万隆会议的原则和决议，并且保证在公正和平等的基础上谋求国际争端的解决”。
    在叙利亚总统库阿特利访问巴基斯坦期间，两国领导人曾经举行了会谈，会谈后发表的这个公报说：“这些会谈提供了有益的机会来就所讨论的一切问题交换意见。这些会谈证明两国有许多共同之处，并且有助于更好地了解对方对所讨论的一切问题的看法。双方对这一点表示满意”。
    公报说：“双方重申他们深信有必要在联合国范围内和根据联合国宪章的目标维护国际和平和安全”。
