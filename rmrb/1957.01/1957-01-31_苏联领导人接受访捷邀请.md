Title: Soviet Leaders Accept Invitation to Visit Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1957-01-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 29th - TASS reports: The delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, currently on a state visit to the Soviet Union, conveyed to the Soviet government and the leaders of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union an invitation from the Czechoslovak government and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, inviting them to pay a friendly visit to the Czechoslovak Republic at any time the Soviet side deems appropriate. The Soviet side has gratefully accepted this invitation.

### 苏联领导人接受访捷邀请

1957-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联领导人接受访捷邀请
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科29日讯：目前正在苏联进行国事访问的捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团，向苏联政府和共产党的领导人转达了捷克斯洛伐克政府和捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会的邀请，请他们在苏联方面认为合适的任何时候到捷克斯洛伐克共和国作一次友好访问。苏联方面已经感谢地接受了这项邀请。
