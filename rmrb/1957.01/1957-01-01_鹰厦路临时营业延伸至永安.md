Title: Yingxia Railway Temporary Operation Extended to Yong'an
Author: Li Yuzhen
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 2

    Yingxia Railway Temporary Operation Extended to Yong'an

    (Our newspaper's dispatch) The Yingtan-Nanping section of the Yingxia Railway has been open for temporary operation since September 1, 1956. Starting January 1, 1957, it will be further extended to Yong'an Station, which is 409 kilometers from Yingtan Station, to handle passengers, luggage, parcels, and large and small cargo between stations on this line. (Li Yuzhen)

### 鹰厦路临时营业延伸至永安
李玉珍
1957-01-01
第2版()
专栏：

    鹰厦路临时营业延伸至永安
    本报讯　鹰厦铁路的鹰潭——南平间，已于1956年9月1日开办临时营业。1957年1月1日起将再延伸至距离鹰潭车站四百零九公里的永安车站，办理这条线上各站互相间的旅客、行李、包裹以及大宗的和零星的货物。　（李玉珍）
