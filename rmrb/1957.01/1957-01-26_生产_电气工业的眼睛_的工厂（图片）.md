Title: Factory Producing "Eyes of the Electrical Industry" (Picture)
Author: Yang Putao
Date: 1957-01-26
Page: 2

    Producing "Eyes of the Electrical Industry"
    People often compare instruments to the "eyes of the electrical industry." The Shanghai Joint State-Private Dahua Instrument Factory is now a factory capable of producing one hundred models and twenty thousand specifications of electrical instruments. The factory's instruments are installed on electrical control equipment in automated iron-smelting blast furnaces at Anshan Iron and Steel, the Fozi Ridge Reservoir on the Huai River, the Changchun First Automobile Manufacturing Plant, geological exploration departments, and scientific research institutions. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Putao photographed.

### 生产“电气工业的眼睛”的工厂（图片）
杨溥涛
1957-01-26
第2版()
专栏：

    生产“电气工业的眼睛”的工厂
    人们常把仪表比作“电气工业的眼睛”。上海公私合营大华仪表厂现在是一个能够生产一百种型号、两万种规格的电气仪表工厂。在鞍钢自动化炼铁高炉、淮河佛子岭水库、长春第一汽车制造厂、地质勘探部门、科学研究机构等电气控制设备上，都安装有这个厂出产的仪表。　新华社记者　杨溥涛摄
