Title: Polish and French Party Delegations Begin Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-01-29
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 28th. According to a report by the Polish News Agency, the delegation of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party and the representatives of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party began talks on January 27.

    The French side participating in the talks included: Étienne Fajon, Raymond Guyot, and Gustave Ansart, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, and Fernand Dupuy, member of the Central Committee.

    The Polish side participating in the talks included: Jerzy Morawski, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Edward Gierek, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, Marian Naszkowski, member of the Central Committee, and Józef Czesak, Director of the Foreign Liaison Department of the Central Committee.

### 波法两党代表团开始会谈

1957-01-29
第5版()
专栏：

    波法两党代表团开始会谈
    新华社华沙28日电　据波兰通讯社报道，法国共产党中央委员会代表团和波兰统一工人党中央委员会的代表在1月27日开始会谈。
    法国方面参加会谈的有：法国共产党中央委员会政治局委员艾蒂安·法戎、雷蒙·居约和居斯塔夫·昂萨尔，中央委员会委员费南·杜皮。
    波兰方面参加会谈的有：波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员叶日·莫拉夫斯基，中央委员会书记处书记爱德华·格雷克，中央委员会委员马里安·纳希科夫斯基，中央委员会对外联络处处长约瑟夫·切萨克。
