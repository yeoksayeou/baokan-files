Title: My Writers Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 18th — The Chinese Writers Delegation that attended the Asian Writers Conference in New Delhi, led by head of the delegation Mao Dun, deputy heads Zhou Yang and Lao She, returned to Beijing today.
	Some members of the delegation had already returned to Beijing earlier.

### 我作家代表团回京

1957-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我作家代表团回京
    新华社18日讯　出席在新德里举行的亚洲作家会议的中国作家代表团，今天在团长茅盾、副团长周扬、老舍率领下回到北京。
    代表团的部分团员已先返京。
