Title: Sino-Korean Trade to Increase Significantly This Year; Two Countries Sign Goods Exchange Protocol and China's Aid to Korea Protocol for This Year
Author:
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 24th. Negotiations between the government trade delegations of the People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea regarding trade in 1957 and China's aid to Korea have concluded smoothly in a harmonious and friendly atmosphere. Today, the 1957 Goods Exchange Protocol between the two countries and the Protocol on China's Aid to Korea were signed in Pyongyang.

The aforementioned two protocols stipulate that, based on the decision in the Sino-Korean government negotiation communique of November 1953, regarding the People's Republic of China's government providing RMB 800 million in aid to Korea from 1954 to 1957 to restore the national economy, the materials China will supply to Korea in 1957 and the materials China will export to Korea this year include: grain, cotton, cotton yarn, cotton cloth, coal, crude salt, steel, raw materials for the chemical industry, paper, and construction materials. The materials Korea will export to China this year include: iron sand, pig iron, tool steel, section steel, non-ferrous metals, cement, calcium carbide, fruits, seafood, and local specialties.

The trade volume between China and Korea this year and the variety of goods supplied to each other will both increase significantly compared to last year.

Representing China, Ye Jizhuang, Head of the Government Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China and Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the two protocols. Representing Korea, Chen Banxiu, Head of the Government Trade Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Minister of Internal and External Commerce of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, signed the protocols.

Chen Banxiu held a banquet tonight to entertain the Chinese Government Trade Delegation.

### 中朝贸易今年将有很大增长  两国签订今年换货和中国援朝议定书

1957-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中朝贸易今年将有很大增长
    两国签订今年换货和中国援朝议定书
    新华社平壤24日电　中华人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府贸易代表团之间关于1957年的贸易和中国援助朝鲜的谈判，已经在融洽和友好的气氛中顺利结束，今天在平壤签订了1957年两国货物交换议定书和中国援助朝鲜的议定书。
    上述两个议定书规定，中国方面根据中朝两国政府1953年11月谈判公报中关于中华人民共和国政府在1954年至1957年内以人民币八亿元援助朝鲜恢复国民经济的决定，在1957年内供应朝鲜的物资和中国本年度对朝鲜贸易出口的物资有：粮食、棉花、棉纱、棉布、煤炭、原盐、钢材、化学工业原料、纸张和建筑材料等。朝鲜本年度对中国贸易出口的物资有：铁砂、釸铁、工具钢、山形钢、有色金属、水泥、电石、水果、海产品和土特产等。
    今年中朝两国的贸易额和互相供应货物的品种都较去年有很大的增长。
    代表中国方面在两个议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团团长、中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮，代表朝鲜方面签字的是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府贸易代表团团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国对内外商业相陈班秀。
    陈班秀在今天晚上举行宴会，招待中国政府贸易代表团。
