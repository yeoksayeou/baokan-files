Title: First Group of Polish National Women's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 8th.

    First Group of Polish National Women's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, January 8th. The first group of fifteen members of the Polish National Women's Basketball Team, invited to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 8th, led by Wisakowski, Vice Chairman of the Polish Basketball Association. The second group of three members of the Polish team will arrive in the next few days.
    The Polish National Women's Basketball Team was the fifth-place team in Europe in 1956. They will hold friendly matches with the Beijing Women's Basketball Team and the "August 1st" Women's Basketball Team of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on January 11th and 13th respectively. At the end of January, after the Polish team finishes its visiting matches in Chongqing and Wuhan and returns to Beijing, they will also hold a friendly match with the Chinese Combined Team Women's Basketball Team.
    The thirteen members of the Polish National Women's Basketball Team are all around one meter and seventy centimeters tall, with one member reaching one meter and eighty-two centimeters. Three members of this team visited China in 1952. In that year, the Polish Women's Basketball Team won all its visiting matches in Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, and Shenyang. In 1954, when the Chinese Women's Basketball Team participated in the 12th World University Summer Games in Budapest, it defeated the Polish team with a score of 51 to 43. In 1955, when the Chinese Women's Basketball Team participated in the basketball competition of the 5th World Youth Festival in Warsaw, it lost to the Polish team with a score of 41 to 55. In 1956, when the Chinese Women's Basketball Team visited Poland again, it defeated the Polish team again with a score of 56 to 40.

### 波兰国家女子篮球队首批人员到北京

1957-01-09
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    波兰国家女子篮球队首批人员到北京
    新华社8日讯
  应邀来中国访问的波兰国家女子篮球队第一批人员十五人，8日下午在波兰篮球协会副主席维萨科夫斯基率领下坐飞机到达北京。波兰队第二批人员三人将在日内到达。
    波兰国家女子篮球队是1956年的欧洲第五名。她们将在1月11日和13日，分别同北京女子篮球队和中国人民解放军“八一”女子篮球队举行友谊比赛。1月底，当波兰队结束了在重庆和武汉的访问比赛回到北京以后，她们还将同中国的联队女子篮球队举行一场友谊比赛。
    波兰国家女子篮球队的十三名队员，身高都在一公尺七十公分上下，其中有一名队员身高达到一公尺八十二公分。这个队中有三名队员曾经在1952年到中国访问过。那一年，波兰女子篮球队在北京、上海、天津和沈阳的访问比赛中，都获得了胜利。1954年，中国女子篮球队在布达佩斯参加第十二届世界大学生夏季运动会的时候，曾经以五十一比四十三赢了波兰队。1955年，中国女子篮球队在参加在华沙举行的第五届世界青年联欢节的篮球比赛中，以四十一比五十五败给波兰队。1956年，中国女子篮球队再一次到波兰访问的时候，又以五十六比四十胜波兰队。
