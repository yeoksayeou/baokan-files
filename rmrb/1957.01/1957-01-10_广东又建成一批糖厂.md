Title: Another Batch of Sugar Refineries Completed in Guangdong
Author:
Date: 1957-01-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 9th. Recently, ten newly built medium and small-sized sugar refineries in Guangdong have successively been put into production. In addition to the construction and renovation of large-scale sugar refineries, Guangdong has newly built, renovated, and restored a total of fifty-one medium and small-sized sugar refineries in the past four years. The total sugarcane crushing capacity of these medium and small-sized sugar refineries is greater than that of the existing five major sugar refineries in Guangdong.

### 广东又建成一批糖厂

1957-01-10
第2版()
专栏：

    广东又建成一批糖厂
    据新华社广州9日电　广东最近有十个新建的中、小型糖厂先后陆续投入生产。广东除新建和改建大型糖厂之外，近四年来共新建、改建和恢复了中、小型糖厂五十一个。这些中、小型糖厂总的榨蔗能力，比广东现有五大糖厂还要多。
