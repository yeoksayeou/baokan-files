Premier Zhou's Telegram of Gratitude to Soviet Leaders

Dear Comrade Voroshilov! Dear Comrade Bulganin! Dear Comrade Khrushchev!

    As we conclude our visit to the Soviet Union, please allow me, on behalf of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China and in my own name, to express our heartfelt gratitude to you, dear comrades, and through you, to the fraternal Soviet people, for the boundless care and warm hospitality extended to us during our visit.

    I am deeply convinced that our visit has further strengthened the great friendship between our two peoples. I wish the fraternal Soviet people new achievements in the great cause of communist construction!

    I wish you health and happiness!
                        Zhou Enlai, January 19, 1957, in Tashkent

### 周总理给苏联领导人的感谢电

1957-01-20
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理给苏联领导人的感谢电亲爱的伏罗希洛夫同志！亲爱的布尔加宁同志！亲爱的赫鲁晓夫同志！
    当我们结束在苏联访问的时候，请允许我以中华人民共和国政府代表团和我个人的名义，向你们，亲爱的同志们，并且通过你们，向兄弟的苏联人民表示衷心的感谢，感谢你们在我们访问期间所给我们的无限的关怀和盛情的招待。
    我深信，我们的访问进一步加强了我们两国人民的伟大友谊。祝兄弟的苏联人民在伟大的共产主义建设事业中取得新的成就！
    祝你们健康、幸福！
                        周恩来　1957年1月19日于塔什干
