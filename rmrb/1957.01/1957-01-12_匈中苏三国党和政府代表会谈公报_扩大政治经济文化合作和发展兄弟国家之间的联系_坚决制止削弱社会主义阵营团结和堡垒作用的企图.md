Title: Communiqué of the Talks between the Representatives of the Parties and Governments of Hungary, China, and the Soviet Union: Expanding Political, Economic, and Cultural Cooperation and Developing Ties Between Fraternal Countries; Resolutely Stop Attempts to Weaken the Unity and Bulwark Role of the Socialist Camp
Author:
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 11th: Communiqué of the Talks between the Representatives of the Parties and Governments of Hungary, China, and the Soviet Union in Moscow

Representatives of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, the Communist Party of China and the Government of the People's Republic of China, and the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Government held talks in Moscow on January 10.

Participating in the talks were: Comrades Kádár, Marosán (Hungary), Comrades Zhou Enlai, Wang Jiaxiang, Hao Deqing (China), Comrades Khrushchev, Bulganin, Mikoyan, Shepilov (Soviet Union).

During the talks, the representatives of the aforementioned parties and governments exchanged views on issues related to expanding and consolidating contacts between the Communist and Workers' Parties of their respective countries, further developing fraternal relations and cooperation in various affairs, as well as some of the most important issues of the current international situation.

The talks were conducted in a sincere and friendly atmosphere. The participants in the talks expressed full agreement on the issues discussed. The representatives of the aforementioned parties and governments expressed their firm determination to expand political, economic, and cultural relations and comprehensive cooperation, to consolidate and further develop fraternal friendship ties between all socialist countries, and to resolutely stop all attempts to weaken the unbreakable unity and bulwark role of the socialist camp. They firmly believe that the Communist and Workers' Parties will systematically strengthen and develop mutual contact and fraternal ties based on the great Marxist-Leninist doctrine and the principle of proletarian internationalism, which serve as the unwavering foundation for the life and activities of revolutionary Marxist parties.

### 匈中苏三国党和政府代表会谈公报  扩大政治经济文化合作和发展兄弟国家之间的联系  坚决制止削弱社会主义阵营团结和堡垒作用的企图

1957-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    匈中苏三国党和政府代表会谈公报
    扩大政治经济文化合作和发展兄弟国家之间的联系
    坚决制止削弱社会主义阵营团结和堡垒作用的企图
    新华社莫斯科11日电　匈牙利、中国、苏联共产党和工人党及政府的代表在莫斯科会谈的公报
    匈牙利社会主义工人党和匈牙利工农革命政府、中国共产党和中华人民共和国政府和苏联共产党和苏联政府的代表在1月10日在莫斯科举行了会谈。
    参加会谈的有：卡达尔同志、马罗山同志（匈牙利），周恩来同志、王稼祥同志、郝德青同志（中国），赫鲁晓夫同志、布尔加宁同志、米高扬同志、谢皮洛夫同志（苏联）。
    在会谈中，上述党和政府的代表们就扩大和巩固各该国共产党和工人党之间的接触，就进一步发展兄弟关系和事务合作有关的问题，以及当前国际形势的一些最重要的问题交换了意见。
    会谈是在诚恳和友好的气氛中进行的。会谈的参加者就所讨论的问题表现了充分的一致。上述党和政府的代表们表示了自己坚定的决心，来扩大政治、经济和文化关系和全面合作，巩固和进一步发展所有社会主义国家之间的兄弟友谊联系，坚决制止削弱社会主义阵营牢不可破的团结和堡垒作用的一切企图。他们深信，各共产党和工人党之间将根据作为革命马克思主义政党生命和活动的不可动摇的基础的伟大马克思列宁主义学说和无产阶级国际主义原则，来系统地加强和发展相互接触和兄弟联系。
