Title: Manufacturing Porcelain for Ethnic Minorities
Author:
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 14th: Jingdezhen has begun to manufacture porcelainware favored by ethnic minorities in large quantities, including pomegranate soup bowls, pomegranate rice bowls, and blue and white openwork folding-edged plates. Porcelain painters, according to the customs and preferences of ethnic minorities, decorate the porcelain blanks with various designs such as double dragons, the Eight Treasures, Five Blessings and Longevity, and the Three Autumns.

This year, Jingdezhen plans to manufacture more than 42,800 dan of porcelain for ethnic minorities, an increase of 17.4% compared to last year.

### 为少数民族制造瓷器

1957-01-15
第2版()
专栏：

    为少数民族制造瓷器
    据新华社南昌14日电　景德镇开始大量制造各地少数民族喜爱的瓷器——石榴汤碗、石榴饭碗、青花玲珑折边盘等。画瓷工人根据少数民族风俗习惯和喜爱，在胎子上画上双龙、八宝、五福寿和三秋等各种装饰。
    今年景德镇计划为少数民族制造四万二千八百多担瓷器，比去年增加17.4％。
