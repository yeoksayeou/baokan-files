Title: Security Council Discusses Kashmir Issue; Pakistani Foreign Minister Demands Plebiscite in Kashmir

Author:

Date: 1957-01-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 17th: At the request of Pakistan, the United Nations Security Council held a meeting on the 16th to discuss the Kashmir issue. The Council last discussed this issue in December 1952, when it passed a resolution requesting the governments of India and Pakistan to negotiate a solution to the issue.

Pakistani Foreign Minister Noon made a statement at the meeting, demanding that the Security Council take measures to hold a plebiscite in Kashmir to decide whether Kashmir should be annexed to India or to Pakistan. He said that after the plebiscite, Pakistan would be willing to conclude a non-aggression treaty with India.

Noon also advocated that India and Pakistan withdraw their troops from Kashmir, and he requested the Security Council to immediately send a United Nations police force to Kashmir to replace the Indian and Pakistani troops.

Noon demanded that India not accept the new constitution passed by the Kashmir parliament, which stipulates the merger of Kashmir into India and comes into effect on January 26.

After Noon's speech, Indian representative Menon said that he would carefully study Pakistan's statement and would be prepared to state India's position after consulting with the Indian government.

The Council decided to hold another meeting on the 23rd to hear the Indian representative's speech.

### 安理会讨论克什米尔问题  巴外长要求在克什米尔举行公民投票

1957-01-18
第6版()
专栏：

    安理会讨论克什米尔问题
    巴外长要求在克什米尔举行公民投票
    新华社17日讯　纽约消息：在巴基斯坦要求下，联合国安全理事会16日举行会议，讨论克什米尔问题。理事会上次讨论这个问题是在1952年12月，当时曾经通过一个决议，要求印度和巴基斯坦政府进行谈判，以解决这个问题。
    巴基斯坦外交部长努恩在会上发表一个声明，要求安全理事会采取措施在克什米尔举行公民投票，以决定克什米尔归并于印度还是归并于巴基斯坦。他说，在举行公民投票以后，巴基斯坦愿意同印度缔结互不侵犯条约。
    努恩还主张印度和巴基斯坦从克什米尔撤出军队，他要求安全理事会立即派遣一支联合国警察部队到克什米尔去，以接替印度和巴基斯坦的军队。
    努恩要求印度不要接受克什米尔议会通过的新宪法，这个宪法规定把克什米尔合并到印度去，并且在1月26日生效。
    在努恩发言后，印度代表梅农说，他将要仔细研究巴基斯坦的声明，并且准备在向印度政府请示后表明印度的态度。
    理事会决定到23日再次举行会议，以听取印度代表的发言。
