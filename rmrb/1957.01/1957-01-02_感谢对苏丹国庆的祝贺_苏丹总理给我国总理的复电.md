Title: Thank You for Congratulations on Sudan's National Day. Sudanese Prime Minister's Reply to Our Premier

Author: 
Date: 1957-01-02
Page: 1

Thank You for Congratulations on Sudan's National Day.
Sudanese Prime Minister's Reply to Our Premier
To His Excellency the Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    On the occasion of Sudan's National Day, I am deeply grateful for your kind congratulations. On my own behalf, and on behalf of the people and government of Sudan, I extend my heartfelt thanks to Your Excellency, and wish you and the great Chinese people happiness and prosperity.

    I equally hope that the friendly relations existing between our two countries will continue to exist and develop.

                                    Abdullah Khalil, Prime Minister
                                    Khartoum, Sudan

### 感谢对苏丹国庆的祝贺  苏丹总理给我国总理的复电

1957-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    感谢对苏丹国庆的祝贺
    苏丹总理给我国总理的复电北京中华人民共和国国务院总理阁下：
    当苏丹国庆节之际，承蒙阁下亲切的祝贺，谨以我本人名义并代表苏丹人民和政府向阁下表示深切的谢意，并祝您本人和伟大的中国人民幸福、繁荣。
    我同样地希望存在于我们两国之间的友好关系将继续存在并发展。
                     总理　阿卜杜拉·哈利勒　于苏丹喀士穆
