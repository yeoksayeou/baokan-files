Title: Indonesian Cabinet Discusses Improving Central-Local Relations; Autonomous Provinces to be Established in Jambi and Riau Islands

Author:

Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 11th: The Indonesian cabinet met on the evening of the 9th to discuss measures to improve relations between the central government and local regions, and agreed to promulgate decrees to establish new autonomous provinces.

The areas slated for autonomous province status include Jambi and the Riau Islands in Central Sumatra. The National Regional Division Committee is currently studying relevant opinions on this matter and will submit them to the government in the near future.

The cabinet meeting also discussed local construction issues and agreed to accelerate the procedures for allocating funds for local construction.

### 印度尼西亚内阁讨论改善中央和地方关系  将在占碑和廖内群岛设自治省

1957-01-12
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚内阁讨论改善中央和地方关系
    将在占碑和廖内群岛设自治省
    据新华社雅加达11日电　印度尼西亚内阁会议9日晚上讨论了有关改善中央与地方关系的措施，同意颁布成立新的自治省的法令。
    成立自治省的地方包括中苏门答腊的占碑和廖内群岛。国家区域划分委员会正在研究有关这方面的意见，并且将在最近提交政府。
    内阁会议还讨论了地方建设问题，同意加速有关地方建设的拨款程序。
