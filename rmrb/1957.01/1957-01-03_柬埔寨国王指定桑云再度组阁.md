Title: Cambodian King Appoints Sangkum Again to Form Cabinet

Author:

Date: 1957-01-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 2nd: The Cambodian News Agency announced today that King Norodom Suramarit of Cambodia, after consulting with the Speaker of the National Assembly, the Speaker of the Council of the Kingdom, and Prince Sihanouk, President of the People's Socialist Community, designated Sangkum on January 1st to organize a new cabinet.

The previous Sangkum cabinet resigned on December 19th of last year.

### 柬埔寨国王指定桑云再度组阁

1957-01-03
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国王指定桑云再度组阁
    新华社金边2日电　柬埔寨新闻社今天公布的一项皇家通告宣布：柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特在同国民议会议长、王国会议议长和人民社会同盟主席西哈努克亲王商量以后，已经在1日指定桑云组织新内阁。
    前届桑云内阁是在去年12月19日辞职的。
