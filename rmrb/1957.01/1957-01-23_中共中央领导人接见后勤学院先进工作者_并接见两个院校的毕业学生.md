Title: CCP Central Leaders Receive Advanced Workers of the Logistics Academy and Meet with Graduates of Two Institutions

Author: 
Date: 1957-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, General Secretary Deng Xiaoping, and Politburo member Peng Dehuai, received advanced workers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Logistics Academy and the third graduating class of the accelerated course system this afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and General Secretary Deng Xiaoping, received all the graduating students of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cadre School this afternoon.

### 中共中央领导人接见后勤学院先进工作者  并接见两个院校的毕业学生

1957-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见后勤学院先进工作者
    并接见两个院校的毕业学生
    新华社22日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东、总书记邓小平、政治局委员彭德怀，在今天下午接见了中国人民解放军后勤学院先进工作者和速成系第三期毕业学生。
    新华社22日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东、总书记邓小平，在今天下午接见了中华全国总工会干部学校今年毕业的全体学生。
