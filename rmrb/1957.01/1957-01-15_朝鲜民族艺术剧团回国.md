Title: Korean National Art Theater Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 13th: The Korean National Art Theater returned to Pyongyang by train on the 12th after a two-month visit and performance tour in China. The troupe's visit and performance tour in China was conducted according to the 1956 Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Plan.

### 朝鲜民族艺术剧团回国

1957-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜民族艺术剧团回国
    新华社平壤13日电　朝鲜国立民族艺术剧团在中国进行了两个月的访问演出以后，在12日乘火车回到平壤。这个剧团是根据中朝1956年文化合作计划到中国去进行访问演出的。
