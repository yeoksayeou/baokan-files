Title: Large Quantities of Winter Bamboo Shoots Shipped via the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway

Author: 
Date: 1957-01-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 16th: Fujian's local and specialty products such as brown sugar, winter bamboo shoots, dried bamboo shoots, and shiitake mushrooms are being continuously supplied to the people of thirteen provinces and cities, including Shanghai, Beijing, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Shandong, Hunan, and Hubei, for the Spring Festival via the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway.

Winter bamboo shoots produced in the mountainous areas of Fujian are a popular non-staple food for the people of Shanghai, Jiangsu, and other places during the Spring Festival. Since the completion of the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway, the transportation time from Nanping to Shanghai has been reduced by one and a half days compared to the past, and the freight cost per dan has been reduced from more than four yuan to seven jiao. The loss rate has also been greatly reduced.

### 鹰厦路运出大量冬笋

1957-01-17
第2版()
专栏：

    鹰厦路运出大量冬笋
    据新华社福州16日电　福建出产的红糖、冬笋、笋干、香菇等土产品和特产品，正通过鹰厦铁路，源源供应上海、北京、江苏、浙江、山东、湖南、湖北等十三个省市人民过春节。
    福建山区出产的冬笋是上海、江苏等地人民过春节很喜爱的副食品，鹰厦铁路建成以后，由南平运到上海，时间比过去缩短了一天半，每担运费由四元多降低到七角。损耗率也大大降低。
