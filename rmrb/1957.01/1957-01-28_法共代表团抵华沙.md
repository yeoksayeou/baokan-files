Title: French Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1957-01-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 27th: According to a report by the Polish Press Agency, a delegation from the Central Committee of the French Communist Party arrived in Warsaw on the 26th. The delegation members are: Étienne Fajon and Raymond Guyot, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party; Gustave Ansart, alternate member of the Political Bureau; and Sénémaud Rieux, member of the Central Committee.

During its stay in Warsaw, the Central Committee delegation of the French Communist Party will discuss issues of common concern to both parties with representatives of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party.

### 法共代表团抵华沙

1957-01-28
第5版()
专栏：

    法共代表团抵华沙
    据新华社华沙27日电　据波兰通讯社报道，法国共产党中央委员会代表团在26日到达华沙。代表团人员有：法国共产党中央委员会政治局委员艾蒂安·法戎和雷蒙·居约、政治局候补委员古斯塔夫·昂萨、中央委员会委员塞纳·吕普伊。
    法共中央代表团在华沙停留期间，将同波兰统一工人党中央委员会的代表们讨论两党所共同关心的问题。
