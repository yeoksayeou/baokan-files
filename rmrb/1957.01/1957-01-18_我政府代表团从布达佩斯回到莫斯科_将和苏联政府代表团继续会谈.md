Title: Our Government Delegation Returns to Moscow from Budapest; Will Continue Talks with Soviet Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 17th: The Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, headed by Premier Zhou Enlai, returned to Moscow from Budapest by plane at 12:40 (Moscow time) on the 17th, after visiting Hungary.

Mikhail Mikoyan, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Foreign Minister Shepilov, Deputy Minister Fedin, Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin, and Gudev, Director of the Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, went to the airport to welcome the Chinese government delegation.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Hungarian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Pordányi, Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to the Soviet Union Banasik, and staff members of the Hungarian and Polish embassies.

Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and members of the Chinese National People's Congress delegation who had returned to Moscow after visiting Bulgaria also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

The Chinese government delegation will continue talks with the Soviet government delegation.

### 我政府代表团从布达佩斯回到莫斯科  将和苏联政府代表团继续会谈

1957-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团从布达佩斯回到莫斯科
    将和苏联政府代表团继续会谈
    新华社莫斯科17日电　以周恩来总理为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团，在访问匈牙利以后，于17日十二时四十分（莫斯科时间）乘飞机从布达佩斯回到莫斯科。
    苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬、外交部长谢皮洛夫、副部长费德林、苏联驻中国大使尤金、苏联外交部远东司司长顾德夫曾经前往机场欢迎中国政府代表团。
    到机场欢迎的还有匈牙利驻苏联大使波尔道茨基、波兰驻苏联大使馆临时代办巴纳克及匈、波两国大使馆的工作人员。
    中国驻苏联大使刘晓和访问保加利亚后回到莫斯科的部分中国全国人民代表大会代表团人员也到机场欢迎。
    中国政府代表团将和苏联政府代表团继续会谈。
