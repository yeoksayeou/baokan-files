Title: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Hold Farewell Reception in Delhi
Author:
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 11th: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni held a reception at the Ashoka Hotel here on the evening of the 10th to bid farewell to the Indian government and Indian friends. Indian President Prasad, Vice President Radhakrishnan, and Prime Minister Nehru all attended the reception.

Also attending the reception were Indian government ministers, army, navy, and air force generals, senior officials, and prominent figures from all walks of life in Delhi. Chinese Ambassador Pan Zili also attended the reception.

On the morning of the 10th, the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni visited Palam Airport to watch an air show. They will leave here on the 12th for Nalanda in Bihar, to present a portion of Xuanzang's skull relics on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China, and then visit eastern India.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼  在德里举行告别招待会

1957-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼
    在德里举行告别招待会
    新华社新德里11日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼10日晚在这里的阿输迦饭店举行招待会，向印度政府和印度朋友告别。印度总统普拉沙德、副总统拉达克里希南和总理尼赫鲁都出席了招待会。
    参加招待会的还有印度政府的部长、陆海空军将领、高级官员以及德里各界著名人士。中国大使潘自力也参加了招待会。
    10日上午，达赖和班禅曾赴巴兰机场参观航空表演。他们将在12日离开这里前往比哈尔邦的那烂陀，代表中华人民共和国政府赠送玄奘顶骨一份，然后去印度东部访问。
