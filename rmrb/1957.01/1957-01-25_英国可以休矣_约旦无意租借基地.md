Title: Britain Can Rest Easy: Jordan Has No Intention of Leasing Bases
Author:
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 6

    Britain Can Rest Easy
    Jordan Has No Intention of Leasing Bases
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 24th: Jordan's Minister of Education and Justice, Shafiq Arshidat, said in Cairo on the evening of the 23rd that Jordan has no intention of leasing any bases to Britain.
    He told reporters from the Middle East News Agency that leasing Jordanian bases to Britain is incompatible with the main purpose of abolishing the British-Jordanian treaty.

### 英国可以休矣  约旦无意租借基地

1957-01-25
第6版()
专栏：

    英国可以休矣
    约旦无意租借基地
    据新华社开罗24日电　约旦教育和司法大臣沙菲克·阿希达特23日晚在开罗说，约旦无意租借任何基地给英国。
    他对中东通讯社记者说，租借约旦基地给英国是同废除英国—约旦条约的主要目的不相容的。
