Title: Abundant Harvest of Oranges in Chenxi (Picture)
Author: Li Jilin, Li Bintao
Date: 1957-01-09
Page: 3

    Abundant Harvest of Oranges in Chenxi
    Chenxi County, Hunan Province, is a well-known citrus-producing area. In recent years, fruit farmers' production cooperatives have been organized, and under the guidance of the agricultural department, a series of technological reforms have been carried out. The 1956 orange harvest was abundant, increasing by 20% compared to 1955; the sales rate reached 85%, 17% more than in 1955.
    Below: One of the fruit-laden branches of the Muzhou Agricultural Cooperative, with a total of nine fruits, weighing three jin.
    Right: An orange from the Shadui Agricultural Cooperative, three cun five fen tall, three cun two fen wide, and weighing one jin two liang. The cooperative members sent it to the Agricultural Technology Extension Station to be forwarded to the Hunan Provincial Department of Agriculture.
                  Photos by Li Jilin and Li Bintao

### 辰溪柑桔丰收（图片）
李集蔺  李滨涛
1957-01-09
第3版()
专栏：

    辰溪柑桔丰收
    湖南辰溪县是著名的柑桔产地，近几年来，组织了果农生产合作社，在农业部门的指导下，进行了一系列的技术改革。1956年柑桔丰收，比1955年增产20％；外销率达85％，比1955年多17％。
    下　木洲农业社的结果累累的果枝之一，共九个果，重三斤。
    右　沙堆农业社的一个柑桔，三寸五分高，三寸二分宽，一斤二两重。社员们把它送给农业技术推广站转湖南省农业厅。
                  李集蔺、李滨涛摄
