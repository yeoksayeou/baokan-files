Title: Marshal He Long Watches Soviet Air Force Flight Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1957-01-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 10th: Marshal He Long, member of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, visited the Soviet Air Force at an airport in the suburbs of Moscow on the morning of January 10.

Accompanying Marshal He Long were Chief Marshal of the Soviet Air Force Zhigarev, Commander of the Moscow Garrison Marshal Moskalenko, and Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin.

Major General Han Zhenji, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, also accompanied the visit.

After reviewing the honor guard at the airport, Marshal He Long began to visit various jet aircraft lined up on the airfield. Chief Marshal of the Air Force Zhigarev introduced the combat performance of various aircraft to Marshal He Long. Marshal He Long expressed his appreciation. Soviet Air Force pilots demonstrated the take-off, formation flight, and landing of fighter and bomber aircraft for Marshal He Long.

After the visit, Marshal He Long had breakfast at the airport. During the meal, the hosts and guests congratulated each other on the friendship between the armies of China and the Soviet Union.

Before leaving, Marshal He Long took a group photo with the Soviet pilots as a memento.

### 贺龙元帅观看苏联空军飞行表演

1957-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙元帅观看苏联空军飞行表演
    新华社莫斯科10日电　中华人民共和国政府代表团团员、国务院副总理贺龙元帅，10日上午在莫斯科郊区一个飞机场参观了苏联空军。
    陪同贺龙元帅参观的，有苏联空军主帅日加列夫，莫斯科卫戍司令莫斯卡连科元帅，苏联驻中国大使尤金。
    中国驻苏联大使馆武官韩振纪中将也陪同前往参观。
    贺龙元帅在飞机场上检阅了仪仗队以后，就开始参观排列在机场上的各种喷气式飞机。日加列夫空军主帅向贺龙元帅介绍了各式飞机的战斗性能。贺龙元帅表示称赞。苏联空军飞行员为贺龙元帅表演了驱逐机、轰炸机的起飞、编队飞行和降落。
    参观以后，贺龙元帅在飞机场进早餐。在进餐的时候，主人和客人互相祝贺中苏两国军队的友谊。
    临行前，贺龙元帅和苏联飞行员一起摄影留念。
