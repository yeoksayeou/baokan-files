Author: Liu Sufang
Date: 1957-01-29
Page: 2

Reader's Letters

    Suggestions for Typists
    Currently, many units use six-point spacing for the line spacing when typing summaries, reports, explanations, and other documents, resulting in only twenty lines per mimeograph stencil. If five-point spacing is used, twenty-five lines can be typed on a mimeograph stencil, which means three hundred and twenty more characters can be typed per page, saving one-third of the paper, and the typed document is also clearer and more aesthetically pleasing.

    I suggest that higher-level leading organs notify typists in various places to type documents according to a certain standard for the number of characters and lines. This will not only save mimeograph stencils but also save printing paper. If the whole country does this, the amount saved will be considerable.
    
    Liu Sufang
    Qingdao Company, Shandong Province, China National Transportation Electrical Equipment Company

### 对打字员的建议
刘素芳
1957-01-29
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    对打字员的建议
    目前有不少单位缮打的总结、报告、说明等文件，都使用六分间格跳纵的行数，一张腊纸仅能打二十行。如果使用五分间格，一张腊纸纵跳二十五行，一张纸就能多打三百二十个字，能节约三分之一的纸，打出的文件也很清晰、美观。
    我建议上级领导机关通知各地的打字员，按照一定字数、行数的标准缮打文件。这样既能节约腊纸，又能节约印刷用纸。如果全国都这样做，节约的数字是相当大的。
    中国交通电工器材公司山东省青岛市公司　刘素芳
