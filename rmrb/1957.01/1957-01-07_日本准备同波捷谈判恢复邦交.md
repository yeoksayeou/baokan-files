Title: Japan Prepares to Negotiate with Poland and Czechoslovakia on Restoring Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-01-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. According to the Kyodo News Agency, the Japanese government plans to begin negotiations with Poland and Czechoslovakia within January regarding the restoration of diplomatic relations. Foreign Ministry sources also believe that because Japan has no outstanding issues with these two countries, the negotiations will proceed smoothly.

The Japanese government has already scheduled meetings between Japan's representative to the United Nations, Shunichi Kase, and Japan's ambassador to the United Kingdom, Nishiharu Hikaru, and representatives from Poland and Czechoslovakia respectively.

### 日本准备同波捷谈判恢复邦交

1957-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    日本准备同波捷谈判恢复邦交
    新华社6日讯　据共同社报道，日本政府预定在1月份内开始同波兰和捷克斯洛伐克谈判恢复邦交问题。外务省人士并且认为，由于日本同这两个国家没有悬案，谈判将会顺利进行。
    日本政府已经预定由日本驻联合国代表加濑俊一和日本驻英大使西春彦分别同波兰和捷克斯洛伐克的代表会谈。
