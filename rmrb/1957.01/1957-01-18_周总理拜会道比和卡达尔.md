Title: Premier Zhou Pays a Visit to Dobi and Kadar
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, January 17th. Premier Zhou Enlai, head of the Chinese government delegation, and delegation members Vice Premier He Long and Vice Minister Wang Jiaxiang, visited István Dobi, President of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, and János Kádár, Prime Minister of the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, at the Parliament Building on the 16th.

Accompanying the delegation was Hao Deqing, a member of the delegation and the Chinese Ambassador to Hungary.

Also accompanying the Chinese side were Qiao Guanhua, Xiong Fu, Zhang Yan, Gong Peng, and others.

Present on the Hungarian side were Vice Premier Münnich, Minister of State Marosán, Central Committee member of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, Kis, Minister of Foreign Affairs Horváth, and others.

The two sides held cordial and friendly talks.

### 周总理拜会道比和卡达尔

1957-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理拜会道比和卡达尔
    新华社布达佩斯17日电　中国政府代表团团长周恩来总理，团员、贺龙副总理、王稼祥副部长，16日前往议会大厦拜会匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比和匈牙利工农革命政府总理卡达尔。
    陪同代表团前往的有代表团团员、中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青。
    中国方面随同前往的有乔冠华、熊复、张彦、龚澎等人。
    匈牙利方面在座的有副总理明尼赫、国务部长马罗山、匈牙利社会主义工人党执行委员基什、外交部部长霍瓦特等人。
    双方进行了热忱友好的谈话。
