Dai Village Nocturne
　　
　　The crescent moon sinks into the sea of clouds,
　　A light mist drifts across the fields,
　　The little flowers on the fence have gone to sleep,
　　The Hulusheng is still whispering softly.

　　The ripe wheat ears tilt their heads,
　　Listening to the footsteps of the night wind:
　　—It's the girl coming,
　　Treading every inch of the path beside the village.

　　Who is gently tapping the elephant-foot drum in the distant village?
　　Oh, it is the palm of love knocking on the young chest.
　　The leaves of the mimosa close together,
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Sucking the sweet dew;
　　The girl avoids the affectionate gaze,
　　Covering her lips with a headscarf…

### 傣村夜曲
丁芒
1957-01-19
第8版()
专栏：

    傣村夜曲
    丁芒月牙儿沉没在云海里，田野飘游着青雾，篱笆上的小花已经睡去，葫芦箫还在嘤嘤私语。成熟的麦穗侧着头，倾听夜风的脚步：——这是姑娘走来了，踏遍了村边的小路。谁在遥远的寨子里轻声敲着象脚鼓？哦，这是爱情的手掌叩击着年青的胸脯。含羞草的叶子合拢了吮吸甜蜜的露珠；姑娘避开深情的眼光，用头巾把嘴唇遮住……（叶浅予插图）（附图片）
