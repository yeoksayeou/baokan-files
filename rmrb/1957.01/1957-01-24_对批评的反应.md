Title: Responses to Criticism
Author:
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 7

    Responses to Criticism
    Regarding the letter from reader Xu Shaofeng published in your newspaper on December 15th of last year, criticizing our publishing house for giving away too many books, our publishing house has studied this issue and proposed improvements. We have decided to reduce the number of books given away in the future.  Time Publishing House
    We have seen reader Liu Yi's letter published on page 7 of your newspaper on January 8th, criticizing various publishers for publishing too many book introduction pamphlets. Our publishing house has decided to stop publishing "Introduction to Literary Books and Periodicals" starting this year. People's Literature Publishing House

### 对批评的反应

1957-01-24
第7版()
专栏：对批评的反应

    对批评的反应
    你报去年12月15日刊载读者许少峰的来信，批评我社赠送书籍太多。我社对这个问题已进行了研究，并提出改进办法，确定今后减少赠送书籍。  时代出版社
    你报1月8日第七版刊载读者柳毅来信，批评各出版社出版的书刊介绍小册子太多，我们看到了。我社已决定从今年起停止出版“文学书刊介绍”。  人民文学出版社
