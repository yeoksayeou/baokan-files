Title: The lazy have no legs (Arabic proverb)
Author: Hu Anbei
Date: 1957-01-04
Page: 8

    The lazy have no legs
    (Arabic proverb)
  A sword wounds the body, words wound the soul.
         ＊
  The lazy have no legs.
         ＊
  The donkey went looking for horns and lost its ears.
         ＊
  A tree that is often transplanted rarely grows lush.
         ＊
  A scholar's mistake is like a shipwreck, it will cause many people to suffer.
              Compiled and translated by Hu Anbei

### 懒人没有腿（阿拉伯谚语）
胡安备
1957-01-04
第8版()
专栏：

    懒人没有腿
    （阿拉伯谚语）
  剑伤肉体，话伤灵魂。
         ＊
  懒人没有腿。
         ＊
  驴子去寻找牛角，结果丢了耳朵。
         ＊
  时常移植的树很少会长得茂盛。
         ＊
  一位学者的错误就像是一只失事的船，它将会使很多的人遭难。
              胡安备  辑译
