Title: Liberation Brand Automobile Arrives in Mangshi
Author:
Date: 1957-01-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, January 5th: The domestically produced Liberation brand automobile crossed the Lancang River and the Nu River, climbed over the Nu Mountains and the Gaoligong Mountains, and arrived in Mangshi, Yunnan Province's Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture, where cherry blossoms are in full bloom, on the 3rd.

On the 4th, which was the "street market day" in Mangshi, the news of the arrival of the domestically produced automobile spread, and farmers of various ethnic groups who were shopping in the market flocked to the car to watch. Wherever the car driver went, people surrounded him, asking him about the car's performance and advantages.

### 解放牌汽车开到芒市

1957-01-06
第2版()
专栏：

    解放牌汽车开到芒市
    新华社昆明5日电　国产解放牌汽车跨过澜沧江和怒江，翻过怒山和高黎贡山脉，3日来到了云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治州樱桃花盛开的芒市。
    4日，正是芒市的“街子天”，国产汽车来临的消息传开以后，正在市场上选购物品的各族农民，纷纷拥向车前观看。汽车驾驶员走到哪里，哪里的人就围上他，向他打听汽车的性能和优点。
