Author:
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 4

    In the Heroine Liu Hulan's Hometown
    To commemorate the martyr Liu Hulan, the people have built a martyr's cemetery in her hometown—Yunzhou West Village, Wenshui County, Shanxi Province. The martyr's belongings and souvenirs will be displayed here. People come from all over with reverence to visit Liu Hulan's former residence.
    Liu Hulan's father, Liu Jingqian, is an outstanding labor model. This is Liu Jingqian (right) talking with visitors. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Ruihua photo

### 在英雄刘胡兰的家乡（图片）

1957-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    在英雄刘胡兰的家乡
    人民为纪念刘胡兰烈士，在她的家乡——山西文水县云周西村修建了一所烈士陵园。烈士的遗物和纪念品都将陈列在这里。人们怀着崇敬的心情，从各地前来参观刘胡兰故居。
    刘胡兰的父亲刘景谦是出色的劳动模范。这是刘景谦（右）同来访者交谈。　新华社记者　张瑞华摄
