Title: Belarusian Vitebsk Workers Hold Symposium on Friendship Between Chinese and Soviet Peoples
Author:
Date: 1957-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 9th: According to TASS Vitebsk, January 8th, agitators from various factories and construction sites in the city of Vitebsk (in Belarus) held a symposium during the visit of the government delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union to discuss the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples and the achievements of the Chinese working people in socialist construction. The workers of this city are rushing to produce orders needed by Chinese industrial sectors.

　　Very enthusiastic discussions took place in the workshops of the Komintern Working Mother Machine Manufacturing Factory. This enterprise is manufacturing automatic and semi-automatic machines for the Chinese industrial sectors. At present, the workers are successfully producing the 1957 orders for China. Some workers have overfulfilled their production tasks by several times. The tool grinding machine factory will manufacture about two hundred sets of the best combined machines for China this year. The Kirov factory in Vitebsk is also manufacturing machine tools for China.

### 白俄罗斯维切布斯克工人  座谈中苏两国人民的友谊

1957-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    白俄罗斯维切布斯克工人
    座谈中苏两国人民的友谊
    据新华社9日讯　据塔斯社维切布斯克8日讯：维切布斯克市（在白俄罗斯）各工厂、工地的鼓动员们在中华人民共和国政府代表团到苏联访问的时候举行了座谈会，讨论中苏两国人民的友谊、中国劳动人民在社会主义建设中的成就。这个城市的工人们正在赶制中国工业部门需要的订货。
    科明捷伦工作母机制造工厂的各车间里进行了十分热烈的座谈。这个企业正在为中国的工业部门制造自动机和半自动机。目前，工人们正在顺利地生产中国1957年的订货。有些工人把生产任务超额完成了好几倍。工具磨床工厂今年将为中国制造两百套左右最好的联动机。维切布斯克的基洛夫工厂也正在为中国制造机床。
