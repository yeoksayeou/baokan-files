Title: India and Morocco Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-01-17
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 15th: According to an official announcement on the 14th, India and Morocco have decided to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic representatives at the ambassadorial level.

### 印度同摩洛哥建交

1957-01-17
第6版()
专栏：

    印度同摩洛哥建交
    新华社新德里15日电　据官方14日宣布：印度和摩洛哥已经决定建立外交关系，并且互派大使级外交代表。
