Title: A Train of Towed Transport on the Yellow River (Picture)
Author: Wang Ziliang
Date: 1957-01-19
Page: 2

    A Train of Towed Transport on the Yellow River
    At the end of last year, the Yellow River Shipping Office successfully experimented with a train-towing method of transportation on the Yellow River several times. Now, the Shipping Bureau is preparing to fully implement this advanced transportation method to give greater play to the role of ancient wooden sailboats. The picture shows a tugboat towing wooden sailboats.　　　　Photo by Wang Ziliang (Draft from Shandong Dazhong Daily)

### 黄河上的一列拖带运输（图片）
王子良
1957-01-19
第2版()
专栏：

    黄河上的一列拖带运输
    黄河航运办事处，去年年底在黄河上几次试验一列拖带法运输获得成功。现在，航运局正准备全面推行这一先进的运输法，以发挥古老木帆船的更大作用。图为拖轮拖带着木帆船行进。	　　　　王子良摄（山东大众日报社稿）
