Title: Prime Minister Grotewohl Presents a Batch of Gifts to Shanghai Children's Palace
Author:
Date: 1957-01-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th. German Democratic Republic Ambassador to China, Gipner, today in Beijing, on behalf of Prime Minister Grotewohl, presented a batch of scientific instruments and teaching materials for physics, electrical engineering, meteorology, biology, etc., as well as a small film projector to the Shanghai Children's Palace.

These gifts were promised to the Shanghai Children's Palace by Prime Minister Grotewohl during his visit to Shanghai in 1955.

When accepting these gifts, the person in charge of the Youth League Central Committee's Department of Children's Work expressed gratitude to Ambassador Gipner on behalf of the children of Shanghai and promised to send these gifts to Shanghai as soon as possible.

### 格罗提渥总理赠给上海少年宫一批礼物

1957-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥总理赠给上海少年宫一批礼物
    新华社12日讯　德意志民主共和国驻华大使纪普纳今日在北京代表格罗提渥总理送给上海少年宫一批物理、电机、气象、生物等科学仪器和教材用品，以及一架小型电影放映机。
    这些礼物是格罗提渥总理在1955年访问上海时答应送给上海少年宫的。
    接受这些礼物时，青年团中央少年儿童工作部负责人代表上海少年们向纪普纳大使表示谢意，并答应尽快把这些礼物送往上海。
