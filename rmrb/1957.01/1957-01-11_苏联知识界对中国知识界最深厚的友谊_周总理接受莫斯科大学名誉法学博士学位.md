Title: The Deepest Friendship of the Soviet Intellectual Circle towards the Chinese Intellectual Circle: Premier Zhou Receives Honorary Doctorate of Law from Moscow University
Author:
Date: 1957-01-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th. The personnel of the Chinese Government Delegation headed by Premier Zhou Enlai visited the magnificent Moscow University on the Lenin Hills on the afternoon of the 9th, and Premier Zhou Enlai accepted the honorary Doctorate of Law awarded to him by the University.

On the 9th, Soviet Deputy Ministers of Higher Education Stoletov and Prokofiev, Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin, and Moscow University President Academician Petrovsky accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and others on the visit.

At 3:30 p.m., when the distinguished Chinese guests entered the university, the entire gateway was crowded with faculty, staff, and students, who warmly applauded Premier Zhou Enlai and others in welcome.

Chinese students studying abroad also came to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai and others.

Academician Petrovsky received the distinguished Chinese guests in the President's office. The Academician provided a detailed introduction to the university's situation.

Subsequently, the guests visited the Geological Museum on the 27th floor, where Professor Ni Ermakov, the curator, introduced the various exhibits, including mineral specimens donated by China. After the visit, Premier Zhou wrote an inscription for the museum: "Long live the unbreakable friendship between China and the Soviet Union! Long live our common communist cause!" The guests then visited the classrooms, laboratories, and libraries of the Geology Department, where they were warmly welcomed everywhere. Several Soviet female students squeezed through the crowd to present Premier Zhou with a book about Moscow University.

Premier Zhou Enlai then went to visit the student dormitories of the Philosophy Department.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and others then proceeded to the University's Great Hall. Professors, staff, Soviet students, Chinese and foreign students had all gathered here to wait. The corridors leading to the hall were also packed with people. When Premier Zhou Enlai walked through the corridors, everyone rushed to shake his hand.

After Premier Zhou Enlai ascended the podium, applause and cheers resounded through the hall, lasting for a long time.

President Petrovsky delivered a welcoming speech. He said, "We, the entire faculty, staff, and students of Moscow University, are watching with extreme joy and excitement the great achievements of the great Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party in their socialist construction. The great victories of our Chinese friends make us all happy and fill our hearts with due pride.

We wholeheartedly welcome you, the head and members of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China. We feel that your visit to Moscow University is a great honor for us.

The scholars of Moscow University know and respect you very well, dear Comrade Zhou Enlai, an outstanding national and political activist of the People's Republic of China. They highly admire your contributions to national and international law in your capacity as Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. They express their respect for your participation in the formulation of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

In recognition of your contributions to national and international law, the Academic Committee of Moscow University unanimously decided at yesterday's meeting to elect you as an Honorary Doctor of Law of Moscow University. I have the honor to inform you of this and to present you with the certificate of Honorary Doctor of Law."

Academician Petrovsky then presented Premier Zhou Enlai with the certificate of Honorary Doctor of Law, and presented books about the history of Moscow University and the Moscow University Bicentennial Medal to Premier Zhou Enlai and others.

Kolev, the Director of the Law Department, delivered a speech congratulating Premier Zhou Enlai on receiving the Honorary Doctor of Law degree.

Student Derevnin of the History Department paid tribute to Premier Zhou Enlai on behalf of the Youth League members of the entire university; Chinese student Hu Zongpei then spoke, welcoming Premier Zhou Enlai.

Premier Zhou Enlai then delivered a speech. (Full text to be published separately)

When Premier Zhou Enlai left Moscow University, he wrote an inscription in the guest book, saying:

"I wish Moscow University greater achievements in cultural education and scientific research!

I wish the culture and science of the Soviet Union a prosperous development under the leadership of the glorious Communist Party of the Soviet Union!

Long live the great and unbreakable friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union!

Long live the invincible Marxism-Leninism!"

Vice Premier He Long and Deputy Minister Wang Jiaxiang also signed the guest book.

The visit ended at 6 p.m.

### 苏联知识界对中国知识界最深厚的友谊  周总理接受莫斯科大学名誉法学博士学位

1957-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联知识界对中国知识界最深厚的友谊
    周总理接受莫斯科大学名誉法学博士学位
    据新华社莫斯科10日电　以周恩来总理为首的中国政府代表团人员9日下午来到列宁山上参观了雄伟壮丽的莫斯科大学，周恩来总理并且接受了大学授予他的名誉法学博士学位。
    9日陪同周恩来总理、贺龙副总理等参观的有苏联高等教育部副部长斯托列托夫、普罗科菲耶夫，苏联驻中国大使尤金，莫斯科大学校长彼得罗夫斯基院士。
    下午三时三十分，当中国贵宾们进入大学的时候，整个大门通道挤满了教职员和学生，他们向周恩来总理等热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    中国留学生也来欢迎周恩来总理等。
    彼得罗夫斯基院士在校长室接待了中国贵宾。院士详细地介绍了学校的情况。
  随后，客人们到二十七层楼参观地质博物馆，由馆长聂耳马可夫教授介绍了各项展品，这里陈列有中国赠送的矿石标本。周总理在参观以后为博物馆题词：“中苏两国的牢不可破的友谊万岁！我们共同的共产主义事业万岁！”客人们接着参观了地质系的教室、实验室、图书馆。到处受到热烈的欢迎。几位苏联女学生挤出人群来，送给周总理一本关于介绍莫斯科大学的书。
    周恩来总理然后又到哲学系学生宿舍去参观。
    周恩来总理和贺龙副总理等接着前往大学的大礼堂。教授、职员、苏联学生、中国和各国留学生，都早就聚集在这里等候。通到礼堂的过道上也站满了人。当周恩来总理走过过道的时候，大家争先恐后来同他握手。
    周恩来总理登上讲台后，礼堂里掌声和欢呼声响成一片，久久不息。
    彼得罗夫斯基校长致欢迎词。他说，“我们，莫斯科大学的全体教授、职员和学生，都怀着极其欢欣鼓舞的心情，注视着伟大的中国人民在中国共产党领导下从事社会主义建设事业所获得的巨大成就。我们的中国朋友的伟大胜利使我们全都高兴，使我们的心里充满了应有的骄傲。
    我们衷心地欢迎你们，中华人民共和国政府代表团的团长和团员。我们觉得，你们来参观莫斯科大学，对于我们是莫大的荣幸。
    莫斯科大学的学者们十分了解，十分尊重你，亲爱的周恩来同志，中华人民共和国卓越的国家活动家和政治活动家。他们极其推崇你在中华人民共和国国务院总理、外交部长、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席的职位上，对于国家法和国际法所作的贡献。他们对你参加制订和平共处五项原则表示尊崇。
    为了表彰你在国家法和国际法方面的贡献，莫斯科大学学术委员会在昨天的会议上，一致决定选举你为莫斯科大学名誉法学博士。我有幸通知你这件事并授给你名誉法学博士证书。”
    彼得罗夫斯基院士随即把名誉法学博士学位的证书授予周恩来总理，并且把关于莫斯科大学的历史的书籍和莫斯科大学二百周年纪念章分别赠送给周恩来总理等人。
    法律系主任柯列夫致词，祝贺周恩来总理得到名誉法学博士学位。
    历史系学生杰列宁代表全校青年团员向周恩来总理致敬；中国留学生胡宗培接着讲话，对周恩来总理表示欢迎。
    周恩来总理接着讲话。（全文另发）
    周总理在离开莫斯科大学时在贵宾留言簿上题了词，他的题词说：
    “祝莫斯科大学在文化教育和科学研究事业中获得更大的成就！
    祝苏联的文化科学在光荣的苏联共产党的领导下日益繁荣！
    中苏两国人民伟大的、牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    战无不胜的马克思列宁主义万岁！”
    贺龙副总理、王稼祥副部长也在贵宾留言簿上签名留念。
    参观在下午六时结束。
