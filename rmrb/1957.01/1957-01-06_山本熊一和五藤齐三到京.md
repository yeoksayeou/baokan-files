Title: Yamamoto Kumao and Gotō Seizo Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 5th -- Yamamoto Kumao, Vice President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and Gotō Seizo, a standing committee member, arrived in Beijing today from Shanghai.

### 山本熊一和五藤齐三到京

1957-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    山本熊一和五藤齐三到京
    据新华社5日讯　日本国际贸易促进协会副会长山本熊一和常任委员五藤齐三今天从上海到北京。
