Title: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Lay the Foundation Stone for the Asian Buddhist Cultural Academy
Author:
Date: 1957-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Calcutta, January 18th: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni laid the foundation stone on the morning of January 17th for the Asian Buddhist Cultural Academy being built by the Indian Maha Bodhi Society.

At a gathering organized by the Maha Bodhi Society, the Dalai Lama told the three hundred people present that Buddhists "must strive to build friendship and understanding among mankind; with friendship and understanding, lasting peace and happiness can be achieved for all mankind." He said that the Indian Maha Bodhi Society has done a great deal of work in restoring holy temples and relics. He believes that the future Cultural Academy will be a major factor in promoting Buddhism.

In his speech, the Panchen praised the Maha Bodhi Society for establishing such an academy to revitalize Buddhism. He said that his trip to India enabled him to "call on the Indian people to revitalize Buddhism, establish peace, and fight against all evils."

Earlier, the president of the Indian Maha Bodhi Society, Sikkim Kumar, delivered a speech inviting the two guests to lay the foundation stone. As the cloth was unveiled from the cornerstone, many monks chanted scriptures.

The Secretary-General of the Indian Maha Bodhi Society also read a letter from Prime Minister Nehru, in which he thanked the Dalai Lama and Panchen for laying the foundation stone for the Cultural Academy.

That morning, the guests also visited the Calcutta Museum, and in the evening attended a reception organized by the Calcutta Hill People Reception Committee. This local organization, established to welcome the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, represents the Nepalese, Bhutanese, Sikkimese, and Indian hill people living in Calcutta, and warmly welcomed the guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Calcutta, January 17th: The Chinese Consul General in Calcutta, Liu Yufeng, held a reception here on the evening of the 16th for the visit of the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni. More than three hundred people attended the reception.

The Dalai Lama and Panchen received all the guests. Many Buddhists paid homage to the Dalai Lama and Panchen, and exchanged hadas with them.

Among those present at the reception were Mukherjee, Speaker, West Bengal Legislative Council and Chairman of the West Bengal Branch of the India-China Friendship Association, ministers of the state government, the Mayor of Calcutta, judges of the West Bengal High Court, government officials, social celebrities, and foreign diplomatic representatives.

West Bengal Governor Naidu held a grand banquet at the Government House that noon to entertain the Dalai Lama and Panchen. Indian Prime Minister Nehru attended the banquet.

That same morning, the guests visited India's largest library, the Calcutta National Library.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼  为亚洲佛教文化学院奠基

1957-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼
    为亚洲佛教文化学院奠基
    新华社加尔各答18日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼17日上午为印度摩呵菩提会筹建的亚洲佛教文化学院奠置了基石。
    达赖在由摩呵菩提会组织的集会上对三百个到会人士说，佛教徒“必须努力在人类当中建立友善和谅解，有了友善和谅解，才能为全人类谋得持久和平和幸福。”他说，印度摩呵菩提会在恢复圣庙和遗物方面作了很多工作。他认为，未来的文化学院将是宣扬佛教的一个重大因素。
    班禅在讲话中，对于摩呵菩提会为了重振佛法而建立这样的一个学院表示赞扬。他说，他在印度的旅行使他能够“呼吁印度人民重振佛法、建立和平和向一切邪恶进行斗争”。
    早些时候，印度摩呵菩提会会长锡金库马尔大君致词，请两位来宾奠基。当基石上的布幔揭开的时候，许多比丘诵起了经文。
    印度摩呵菩提会秘书长还宣读了尼赫鲁总理的来信，他感谢达赖和班禅为文化学院奠置基石。
    这天上午，客人们还参观了加尔各答的大博物馆，晚上又出席了加尔各答山地居民接待委员会所组织的招待会。为欢迎达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼而成立的这个地方组织，代表住在加尔各答的尼泊尔人、不丹人、锡金人和印度山地居民热诚地欢迎了客人。
    新华社加尔各答17日电　中国驻加尔各答总领事柳雨峰16日晚上在这里为达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的访问举行招待会。出席招待会的有三百多人。
    达赖和班禅接见了所有的来宾。许多佛教徒向达赖和班禅礼拜，并且同他们互赠哈达。
    出席招待会的有西孟加拉邦立法会议议长、印中友好协会西孟加拉分会主席慕克吉、邦政府的部长们、加尔各答市长、西孟加拉高等法院法官、政府官员、社会名流和各国外交代表。
    西孟加拉邦邦长奈都女士当天中午在政府大厦举行盛大宴会，招待达赖和班禅。印度总理尼赫鲁参加了宴会。
    同一天上午，客人们参观了印度最大的图书馆——加尔各答国立图书馆。
