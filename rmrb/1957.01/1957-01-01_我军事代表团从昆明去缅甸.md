Title: Our Military Delegation Departs from Kunming to Burma
Author:
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 31st. Our military goodwill delegation, invited to visit Burma and headed by Marshal Ye Jianying of the People's Republic of China, departed from Kunming by special plane this morning for Burma.

    Liu Minghui, Acting Governor of Yunnan Province, Yu Yichuan, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, General Xie Fuzhi, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, and Wu Mao Mao Duo, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and others, saw the delegation off at Kunming Airport.

### 我军事代表团从昆明去缅甸

1957-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团从昆明去缅甸
    新华社昆明31日电　应邀到缅甸访问的我国军事友好代表团，在团长、中华人民共和国元帅叶剑英的率领下，于今天上午从昆明乘专机赴缅甸。
    云南省代省长刘明辉、中共云南省委书记于一川、中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长谢富治上将以及缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂铎等人，到昆明机场为代表团送行。
