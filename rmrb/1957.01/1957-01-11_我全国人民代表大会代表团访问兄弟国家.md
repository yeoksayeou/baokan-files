Title: Our National People's Congress Delegation Visits Fraternal Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-01-11
Page: 5

Our National People's Congress delegation and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee delegation visited Czechoslovakia, Romania, and Bulgaria from December 2, 1956, to January 1957.

    (Top) Czechoslovak President Zapotocky has a cordial conversation with Comrade Peng Zhen, head of our delegation.
    (Center) Our delegation received a warm welcome from the Romanian people upon arrival in Bucharest.
    (Bottom) A corner of the mass rally in Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, welcoming our delegation.
                                              (Xinhua News Agency) (Attached pictures)

### 我全国人民代表大会代表团访问兄弟国家

1957-01-11
第5版()
专栏：

    我全国人民代表大会代表团访问兄弟国家
    我全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团从1956年12月2日到1957年1月在捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚和保加利亚进行访问。
    （上）捷总统萨波托斯基同我代表团团长彭真同志亲切地交谈。
    （中）我代表团到达布加勒斯特时，受到罗马尼亚人民热烈欢迎。
    （下）保加利亚首都索非亚欢迎我代表团的群众大会的一角。
                                              （新华社发）（附图片）
