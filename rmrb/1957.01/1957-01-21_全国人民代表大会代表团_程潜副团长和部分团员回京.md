Title: National People's Congress Delegation: Deputy Head Cheng Qian and Some Members Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 20th: Some members of the National People's Congress delegation, led by Deputy Head Cheng Qian, returned to Beijing this afternoon by jet plane. They had previously visited the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, and other countries. Some members of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee delegation also returned to Beijing on the same flight.

### 全国人民代表大会代表团  程潜副团长和部分团员回京

1957-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会代表团
    程潜副团长和部分团员回京
    新华社20日讯　全国人民代表大会代表团部分团员在副团长程潜率领下，在今天下午乘喷气式客机回到北京。他们曾先后访问了苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、保加利亚等国。北京市人民委员会代表团部分团员也同机返京。
