Title: International Anti-Tuberculosis Conference Opens in India
Author:
Date: 1957-01-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 8th: The 14th International Anti-Tuberculosis Conference opened on the 7th at the Vigyan Bhavan in New Delhi.

    Indian President Prasad, in his opening address, emphasized that ordinary people, especially those in underdeveloped countries, must make a concerted effort to use the increasing knowledge now available to combat tuberculosis.

    Eight hundred delegates from fifty countries are attending the conference, including a seven-member Chinese delegation headed by Huang Dingchen.

    Some time ago, the People's Republic of China was admitted as a member of the International Union Against Tuberculosis, along with El Salvador and Sudan. The Executive Committee of the International Union Against Tuberculosis also approved the admission of the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, and Iraq as member states.

    The conference will discuss various aspects of the problem of tuberculosis, including medical, biological, and social aspects, as well as solutions to these problems. In addition, seminars will be held on broad issues related to tuberculosis.

### 国际防痨会议在印开幕

1957-01-09
第5版()
专栏：

    国际防痨会议在印开幕
    据新华社新德里8日电　第十四届国际防痨会议7日在新德里科学厅开幕。
    印度总统普拉沙德在致开会辞时强调指出，普通人民、特别是不发达国家人民必须作出共同的努力，利用现在可以得到的愈来愈多的知识来对付痨病。
    出席会议的有五十个国家的八百名代表，其中包括以黄鼎臣为首由七人组成的中国代表团。
    前些时候，中华人民共和国被接纳为国际防痨联合会会员国，同时被接纳的有萨尔瓦多和苏丹。国际防痨联合会执行委员会也批准了苏、捷和伊拉克为会员国。
    这次会议将讨论防痨问题的医疗、生物和社会等各个方面以及解决这些问题的办法。此外，还将就有关痨病的广泛问题举行讨论会。
