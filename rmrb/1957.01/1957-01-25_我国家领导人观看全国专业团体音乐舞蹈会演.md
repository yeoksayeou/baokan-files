Title: Our National Leaders Watch the National Professional Music and Dance Performance

Author: 
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 24th. Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, other national leaders, and heads of various departments watched the National Professional Music and Dance Performance at Huairentang, Zhongnanhai, this evening.

### 我国家领导人观看全国专业团体音乐舞蹈会演

1957-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我国家领导人观看全国专业团体音乐舞蹈会演
    新华社24日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长和国家的其他领导人员及各部门负责人，今晚在中南海怀仁堂观看了全国专业团体音乐舞蹈会演。
