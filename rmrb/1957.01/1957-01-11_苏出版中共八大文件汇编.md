Title: Soviet Publication of "Collection of Documents of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China"
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th. The Soviet State Political Literature Publishing House has published the "Collection of Documents of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China." The total print run is 75,000 copies.
    The collection includes Comrade Mao Zedong's opening address to Congress, Comrade Liu Shaoqi's political report, Comrade Deng Xiaoping's report on the revision to the Party Constitution, Comrade Zhou Enlai's report on the proposals for the Second Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy, speeches by Comrade Zhu De and twenty-four other comrades, Comrade Mikoyan's congratulatory speech, congratulatory speeches from various democratic parties and non-party democratic figures in China, as well as the resolution of the Congress on the political report, the Congress's proposal for the Second Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy, the Constitution of the Communist Party of China, and the list of members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China elected by the Congress.

### 苏出版中共八大文件汇编

1957-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    苏出版中共八大文件汇编
    新华社莫斯科10日电　苏联国家政治书籍出版局出版了“中国共产党第八次全国代表大会文件汇编”。总出版份数是七万五千册。
    文件汇编包括有毛泽东同志的大会开幕词，刘少奇同志的政治报告，邓小平同志的关于修改党的章程的报告，周恩来同志的关于发展国民经济的第二个五年计划的建议的报告，朱德等二十四位同志的发言，米高扬同志的祝词，以及中国各民主党派和无党派民主人士的祝词，还包括有大会关于政治报告的决议，大会关于发展国民经济的第二个五年计划的建议，中国共产党章程，大会所选举的中国共产党中央委员会委员的名单等。
