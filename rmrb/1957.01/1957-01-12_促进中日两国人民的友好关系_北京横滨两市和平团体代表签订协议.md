Title: Beijing and Yokohama Peace Groups Sign Agreement to Promote Friendly Relations Between Chinese and Japanese Peoples
Author:
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Representatives of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Delegation of the Yokohama City Executive Committee for Sino-Japanese Citizen Exchange, composed of the Yokohama City Peace Committee of Japan, signed an agreement in Beijing today on strengthening friendly exchanges between the two cities and promoting cultural and economic exchanges.

Sun Fuling, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Kenji Sato, Head of the Delegation of the Yokohama City Executive Committee for Sino-Japanese Citizen Exchange, signed the agreement. They both spoke after signing.

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The full text of the agreement between the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Delegation of the Yokohama City Executive Committee for Sino-Japanese Citizen Exchange, composed of the Yokohama City Peace Committee of Japan, on strengthening friendly exchanges between the two cities and promoting cultural and economic exchanges, is as follows:

The Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Delegation of the Yokohama City Executive Committee for Sino-Japanese Citizen Exchange, composed of the Yokohama City Peace Committee of Japan, exchanged views fully in a harmonious atmosphere on the issue of further strengthening friendly exchanges and promoting economic and cultural exchanges between the two sides in the future. Both sides affirmed that strengthening friendly exchanges between the people of Beijing and Yokohama and promoting economic and cultural exchanges based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence is not only conducive to promoting the friendship between the Chinese and Japanese peoples and the normalization of relations between the two countries, but also conducive to safeguarding peace in Asia and the world. Based on the above views, both sides reached the following agreement:

一、 The two cities will conduct mutual visits to further promote friendship and mutual understanding between the Chinese and Japanese peoples.

二、 Both parties will make their utmost efforts to promote economic, cultural, and technological exchanges between the two countries.

### 促进中日两国人民的友好关系  北京横滨两市和平团体代表签订协议

1957-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    促进中日两国人民的友好关系
    北京横滨两市和平团体代表签订协议
    据新华社11日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会同日本横滨市和平委员会所组成的日中市民交流横滨市实行委员会访华代表团今天在北京签订了关于加强两市友好往来和促进文化、经济交流的协议。
    在协议上签字的，是中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会副秘书长孙孚凌、日中市民交流横滨市实行委员会访华代表团团长佐藤贤治。他们在签字后先后讲了话。
    新华社11日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会和由日本横滨市和平委员会所组成的日中市民交流横滨市实行委员会访华代表团关于加强两市友好往来和促进文化、经济交流的协议，全文如下：
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会和由日本横滨市和平委员会所组成的日中市民交流横滨市实行委员会访华代表团，就今后双方进一步加强友好往来和促进经济、文化交流问题，在和谐的气氛中，充分地交换了意见，双方确认：根据和平共处的五项原则，加强北京和横滨两市人民友好往来和促进经济文化交流，不仅有利于促进中日两国人民的友谊和两国邦交的正常化，而且有利于保卫亚洲和世界和平，双方根据以上见解，达成如下协议：
    一、两市进行相互访问，以进一步促进中日两国人民间的友谊和互相了解。
    二、双方为促进两国之间的经济、文化和技术交流尽最大努力。
