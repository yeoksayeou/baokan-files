Title: "People's Daily" of France and "Voice of Labor" of Switzerland Publish "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat (Part 2)"
Author:
Date: 1957-01-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 19th: "L'Humanité" previously published excerpts of the "People's Daily" article "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat (Part 2)" in two installments on December 29th and 31st of last year. The first was a relatively simple report, while the second was a detailed summary of the article.
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 19th: The Swiss Labour Party's organ, "Voice of Labor," published a detailed summary of the "People's Daily" article "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat (Part 2)" on January 7th.

### 法国“人道报”和瑞士“工人之声报”  刊载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”

1957-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    法国“人道报”和瑞士“工人之声报”
    刊载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”
    新华社19日讯　巴黎消息：“人道报”曾经在去年12月29日和31日先后两次摘要刊载了“人民日报”的文章“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”。第一次是比较简单的报道，而第二次刊载的是这篇文章的详尽摘要。
    新华社19日讯　日内瓦消息：瑞士劳动党机关报“工人之声报”1月7日刊载了“人民日报”文章“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”的详尽摘要。
