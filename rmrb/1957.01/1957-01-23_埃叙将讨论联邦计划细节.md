Title: Egypt and Syria to Discuss Details of Federal Plan
Author:
Date: 1957-01-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd dispatch from Damascus: Syrian Prime Minister al-Assali said here on the 21st that Syria will discuss the details of the plan to establish an Egyptian-Syrian federation with the Egyptian government. A Syrian ministerial delegation will depart for Cairo soon for this purpose.

Al-Assali returned to Damascus on the 21st after attending the Arab Quartet meeting. He spoke about the decision made at the meeting regarding providing financial assistance to Jordan. He said: "This is an act of national solidarity, even an obligation."

### 埃叙将讨论联邦计划细节

1957-01-23
第6版()
专栏：

    埃叙将讨论联邦计划细节
    据新华社22日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总理阿萨利21日在这里说，叙利亚将同埃及政府讨论建立埃及—叙利亚联邦计划的细节。一个叙利亚部长级代表团为此不久将动身前往开罗。
    阿萨利在出席阿拉伯四国会议后在21日回到大马士革。他谈到了这次会议所作出的关于给予约旦财政援助的决定。他说：“这是民族团结的行动，甚至是一种义务。”
