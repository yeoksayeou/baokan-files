Title: Chinese People's Congress Delegation Visits Various Places in Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1957-01-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 8th: The Chinese National People's Congress delegation and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee delegation left Sofia on the morning of January 7 to visit the Bulgarian cities of Plovdiv, Dimitrovgrad, and others.
    On January 6, the delegation visited the largest Bulgarian reservoir near Sofia, the Stalin Reservoir, and toured the picturesque Paulowitz resort.

### 我国人民代表大会代表团赴保各地访问

1957-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我国人民代表大会代表团赴保各地访问
    新华社索非亚8日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团在1月7日上午离开索非亚前往保加利亚的普罗夫迪夫、季米特洛夫格勒等城市进行访问。
    1月6日，代表团曾经参观了索非亚附近的保加利亚最大水库——斯大林水库，并且游览了风景如画的保罗维茨休养地。
