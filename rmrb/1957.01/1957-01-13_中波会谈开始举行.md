Title: Sino-Polish Talks Begin

Author:
Date: 1957-01-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 12th - The government delegation of the People's Republic of China and the government delegation of the Polish People's Republic began talks on the afternoon of the 11th.

The Polish side participating in the talks included: Gomulka, Zawadzki, Cyrankiewicz, Ochab, Zambrowski, Rapacki, Naszkowski, Kiryluk.

The Chinese side participating in the talks included: Zhou Enlai, He Long, Wang Jiaxiang, Wang Bingnan.

### 中波会谈开始举行

1957-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中波会谈开始举行
    新华社华沙12日电　中华人民共和国政府代表团和波兰人民共和国政府代表团11日下午开始会谈。
    波兰方面参加会谈的是：哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹、奥哈布、萨姆布罗夫斯基、拉帕茨基、纳希科夫斯基、基里洛克。
    中国方面参加会谈的是：周恩来、贺龙、王稼祥、王炳南。
