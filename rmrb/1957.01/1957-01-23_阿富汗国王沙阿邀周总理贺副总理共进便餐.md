Title: Afghan King Shah Invites Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He to Informal Dinner
Author:
Date: 1957-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, January 22nd: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long were invited to the Afghan Royal Palace at 8:00 PM on the 21st to have an informal dinner with King Zahir Shah.

Also invited to dinner were Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, Deputy Director of the Premier's Office Zhang Yan, and Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Ding Guoyu.

### 阿富汗国王沙阿邀周总理贺副总理共进便餐

1957-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗国王沙阿邀周总理贺副总理共进便餐
    新华社喀布尔22日电　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理在21日晚上八点钟应邀到阿富汗王宫同查希尔·沙阿国王共进便餐。
    应邀同进晚餐的还有中国外交部部长助理乔冠华、总理办公室副主任张彦和中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰。
