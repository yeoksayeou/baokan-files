Title: Guo Moruo and Shen Yanbing Receive Renowned Film Artist Joris Ivens
Author:
Date: 1957-01-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: Today morning, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing received the internationally renowned film artist and International Peace Prize laureate, Mr. Joris Ivens from the Netherlands, and his wife, who are currently visiting and lecturing in China.

    In the evening, Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, received Ivens.

    After arriving in Beijing on December 25th last year, Ivens visited the Beijing Film Academy, the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, and the August First Film Studio. He also gave reports on his creative experience and the filming process of several of his films to the creative staff of these two film studios.

    Ivens also visited Shanghai and Guangzhou. He returned to Beijing on the 23rd of this month.

    Ivens will continue to lecture in Beijing and exchange experiences with Chinese film workers.

### 郭沫若沈雁冰接见著名电影艺术家伊文思

1957-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若沈雁冰接见著名电影艺术家伊文思
    新华社25日讯　今天上午，文化部部长沈雁冰接见了正在我国访问、讲学的国际著名电影艺术家、国际和平奖金获得者荷兰约里斯·伊文思先生和夫人。
    晚间，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若接见了伊文思。
    伊文思在去年12月25日到京后，参观了北京电影学院、中央新闻纪录电影制片厂、八一电影制片厂等，并且向这两个电影制片厂的创作干部作了他的创作经历和几部影片拍摄经过的报告。
    伊文思曾去上海、广州进行了参观。是本月23日回到北京的。
    伊文思将在北京继续讲学，和我国电影工作者交流经验。
