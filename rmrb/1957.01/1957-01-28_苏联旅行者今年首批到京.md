Title: First Group of Soviet Tourists Arrives in Beijing This Year
Author:
Date: 1957-01-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, January 26th. According to the contract signed between the Chinese and Soviet travel agencies for mutual reception of tourists, the first group of Soviet tourists in 1957, a party of fifty-one people, arrived in Beijing today. Representatives from the China International Travel Service, the Beijing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Beijing Russian Language Institute, and the Soviet Embassy in China went to the station to welcome them. This group of tourists consists of professors and university students from Vladivostok, Khabarovsk, and other places. They will visit cities such as Beijing, Shanghai, and Tianjin.

### 苏联旅行者今年首批到京

1957-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联旅行者今年首批到京
    新华社26日讯　根据中苏两国旅行社签订的相互接待旅行者的合同，1957年苏联首批旅行者一行五十一人今天到达北京。中国国际旅行社、中苏友好协会北京市分会、北京俄语学院、苏联驻华大使馆等单位，都派代表前往车站迎接。这一批旅行者由海参崴、伯力等地的教授和大学生组成。他们将要参观北京、上海、天津等城市。
