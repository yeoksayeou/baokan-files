Title: Romanian Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 20th - The Romanian People's Republic trade delegation, led by Vidraşcu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of Romania, arrived in Beijing on the 20th.

### 罗马尼亚贸易代表团抵京

1957-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚贸易代表团抵京
    新华社20日讯　罗马尼亚人民共和国贸易代表团在罗马尼亚对外贸易部副部长维德拉希库率领下，于20日抵达北京。
