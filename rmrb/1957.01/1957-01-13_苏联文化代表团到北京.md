Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 12th: A Soviet cultural delegation, led by Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation members include Soviet Deputy Minister of Higher Education Gerashenko, Soviet Deputy Minister of Culture Ovid, Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Soviet Ministry of Health Matveyev, Deputy Director of the General Administration of Film Production of the Soviet Ministry of Culture Kalashnikov, Deputy Director of the General Administration of Publishing of the Soviet Ministry of Culture Rybin, and Director of the Department of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Ministry of Culture Boni, and six others.

The delegation is here to discuss further strengthening cultural cooperation between China and the Soviet Union with the Chinese government and to sign the 1957 implementation plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Culture Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Higher Education Liu Aifeng, Assistant Minister of Culture and Director of the Publishing Administration Huang Luofeng, Assistant Minister of Culture and Director of the Film Administration Wang Lanxi, and Deputy Secretary-General of the Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ge Baoquan, and others. Zhang Yingwu, the Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, who is now in Beijing, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Also welcoming the delegation at the airport was the Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, G.A. Goutikov, and others. (Attached picture)

### 苏联文化代表团到北京

1957-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联文化代表团到北京
    据新华社12日讯　苏联文化代表团今天在团长苏联文化部部长米哈伊洛夫率领下，乘飞机抵达北京。代表团团员有苏联高等教育部副部长格拉申科、苏联文化部副部长奥维德、苏联卫生部对外司司长马特维耶夫、苏联文化部影片生产总管理局副局长卡拉什尼科夫、苏联文化部出版总管理局副局长雷宾和苏联文化部对外司处长博尼等六人。
    代表团这次来是为了和我国政府商谈进一步加强中苏两国的文化合作事业，并签订中苏文化合作协定的1957年执行计划。
    到机场欢迎的有文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，文化部副部长张致祥，高教部副部长刘皑风，文化部部长助理、出版事业管理局局长黄洛峰，文化部部长助理、电影事业管理局局长王阑西，中苏友好协会总会理事会副秘书长戈宝权等人。现在北京的我国驻苏大使馆文化参赞张映吾也到机场欢迎。
    到机场迎接的还有苏联驻华大使馆临时代办顾悌克夫等。（附图片）
