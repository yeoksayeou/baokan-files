Title: My Trade Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1957-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 18th - The Chinese government trade delegation, headed by Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jiuzhuang, arrived in Pyongyang by train tonight.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Chen Banxiu, Minister of Foreign and Domestic Commerce of Korea, Park Sung-chul, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to Korea.

### 我贸易代表团到平壤

1957-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团到平壤
    新华社平壤18日电　以对外贸易部部长叶季壮为首的中国政府贸易代表团今天晚上乘火车到达平壤。
    到车站欢迎的有朝鲜对内外商业相陈班秀，外务省副相朴成哲，中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光等。
