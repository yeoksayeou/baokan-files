Title: Soviet Donbass Coalfield Shortens Working Day
Author:
Date: 1957-01-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 4th: All underground workers in the Soviet Donbass coalfield have begun working shortened workdays. January 2nd was the first working day of the new year, and on this day, miners working on coal faces in various mines began to implement a six-hour workday, while miners working in underground roadways work for seven hours.

    Simultaneously with the shortening of the workday, a new wage system and labor organization system are also being implemented. This has enabled almost all mines in the Donbass to improve their production targets, increase labor productivity, and raise miners' wages.

### 苏联顿巴斯矿区缩短工作日

1957-01-06
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联顿巴斯矿区缩短工作日
    据新华社莫斯科4日电　苏联顿巴斯矿区各矿井所有在地下工作的工人都开始按缩短后的工作日工作。1月2日是新的一年的第一个工作日，在各矿井采煤工作面上工作的矿工这一天开始实行六小时工作制，在地下巷道上工作的矿工的工作时间是七小时。
    和缩短工作日同时，还实行新工资制度和劳动组织制度。这使得顿巴斯几乎所有矿井都改进了生产指标，提高了劳动生产率和矿工的工资。
