Title: X-Ray Kymocardiograph

Author:

Date: 1957-01-28
Page: 2

New Technology, New Products

    X-Ray Kymocardiograph
    Hu Qiweng, a radiologist, and Shao Yuehan, a technician, from the First People's Hospital of Zhengzhou City, Henan Province, have recently successfully developed an X-ray kymocardiograph.
    The heart photographs taken by the X-ray kymocardiograph can show the movement of various parts of the heart's edge and can clearly show the entire appearance of the heart. It is of great significance for doctors to study the heart's movement in detail, diagnose heart disease, and determine the condition. (Zhengzhou Daily)

### X线心脏记波摄影器

1957-01-28
第2版()
专栏：新技术新产品

    X线心脏记波摄影器
    河南省郑州市第一人民医院放射科医师胡启文和技士邵约翰，最近试制成功了X线心脏记波摄影器。
    X线心脏记波摄影器摄出来的心脏照片，可以看到心脏边缘的各个部分的运动状况，可以看清心脏的全貌，对医生详细研究心脏运动的情况、诊断心脏病，确定病情有很大的作用。　（郑州日报）
