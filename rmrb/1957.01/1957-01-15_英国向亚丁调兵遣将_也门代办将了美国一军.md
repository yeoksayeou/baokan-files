Title: Britain Deploys Troops to Aden; Yemeni Chargé d'Affaires Calls the US's Bluff
Author:
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, London, 14th - The British "Daily Express" reported that Britain has drawn troops from East Africa and airlifted them to the Aden Protectorate to strengthen British forces in the Aden Protectorate.
　　Yemen recently announced that British troops in the Aden Protectorate have repeatedly launched attacks on Yemeni territory recently.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 14th - Yemeni Chargé d'Affaires to the United States, Ahmed Zabarah, said on the 12th that US President Eisenhower, in his Middle East policy message, stated that the United States would stop aggression in the Middle East. He then pointed out: "This (the British invasion of Yemen) is aggression."
　　Zabarah visited the US State Department on this day, requesting the United States to stop British aggression against Yemen.
　　Zabarah said that US Assistant Secretary of State Rountree had promised to discuss the matter with the British authorities.

### 英国向亚丁调兵遣将  也门代办将了美国一军

1957-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    英国向亚丁调兵遣将
    也门代办将了美国一军
    新华社14日讯　伦敦消息：英国“每日快报”报道，英国已经从非洲东部抽调军队，用飞机载往亚丁保护地，以加强亚丁保护地的英军力量。
    也门最近宣布，在亚丁保护地的英军最近曾经一再对也门领土发动了进攻。
    新华社14日讯　华盛顿消息：也门驻美国代办艾哈迈德·扎巴拉赫12日说，美国总统艾森豪威尔在他的中东政策咨文中曾经说过，美国将制止在中东的侵略。他接着指出：“这（英国进犯也门的行动）就是侵略。”
    扎巴拉赫在这一天访问了美国国务院，他要求美国制止英国对也门的侵略。
    扎巴拉赫说，美国助理国务卿朗特里已经答应同英国当局讨论这个问题。
