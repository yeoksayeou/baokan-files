    Many agricultural production cooperatives in the Qin Chuan area of Shaanxi Province, a nationally renowned wheat-producing region, are strengthening winter field management of wheat to create conditions for a bumper wheat harvest in 1957.
    Right picture: Members of the Dong Meng Wang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Wu Gong County are applying top dressing fertilizer to wheat fields.
    Bottom picture: Farmers in the Wei Hui Canal irrigation area of Shaanxi Province are diverting canal water to winter irrigate wheat fields.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Jiang Guoxian photographed.
