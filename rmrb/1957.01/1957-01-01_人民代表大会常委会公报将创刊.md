Title: People's Congress Standing Committee Gazette to be Launched

Author: 
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th -- The Gazette of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China will be launched in January 1957. The Gazette will mainly publish the following documents: (1) Documents adopted and approved by the National People's Congress meetings; (2) Documents adopted and approved by the meetings of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; (3) Decrees of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China and personnel appointments and removals; (4) Changes in the composition of the deputies to the National People's Congress and members of the Standing Committee

The Gazette of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress will be edited and published by the General Office of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, sold publicly, and distributed by the Beijing Post Office of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications.

### 人民代表大会常委会公报将创刊

1957-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    人民代表大会常委会公报将创刊
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会公报将在1957年1月创刊。公报主要刊登下例文件：（一）全国人民代表大会会议通过和批准的文件；（二）全国人民代表大会常务委员会会议通过和批准的文件；（三）中华人民共和国主席令和任免人员；（四）全国人民代表大会代表和常务委员会委员变动情况。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会公报，由全国人民代表大会常务委员会办公厅编辑出版，公开发售，由邮电部北京邮局发行。
