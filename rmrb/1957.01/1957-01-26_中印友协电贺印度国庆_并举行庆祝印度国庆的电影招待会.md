Title: Sino-Indian Friendship Association Sends Congratulatory Telegram for Indian National Day and Holds Film Reception Celebrating Indian National Day
Author:
Date: 1957-01-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 25th:

    Sino-Indian Friendship Association Sends Congratulatory Telegram for Indian National Day
    And Holds Film Reception Celebrating Indian National Day
    The Sino-Indian Friendship Association sent a telegram to the India-China Friendship Association today, congratulating them on the National Day of the Republic of India. The telegram reads as follows: Mr. Sundarlal, Chairman of the India-China Friendship Association, Dr. Chand, Chairman of the Council, Dr. Hussain, Chairman of the National Executive Committee.
    On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the founding of the Republic of India, we extend our sincere congratulations to the Indian government and people.
    We wish the Republic of India increasing prosperity, the Indian people greater happiness, and the friendly cooperation and cultural exchange between the Chinese and Indian people new and greater development.
    Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association
        January 25, 1957, Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: The Sino-Indian Friendship Association held a film reception tonight to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the founding of the Republic of India.
    At the reception, a documentary film introducing India's Five-Year Plan and the newly produced Chinese feature film "Family" were shown.
    The Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and embassy officials, as well as Indian friends in Beijing, were invited to attend the film reception.
    More than 300 people including Shi Liang, Zheng Zhenduo, Xia Yan, Zeng Zhaolun, and people from all walks of life attended the film reception.

### 中印友协电贺印度国庆  并举行庆祝印度国庆的电影招待会

1957-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中印友协电贺印度国庆
    并举行庆祝印度国庆的电影招待会
    新华社25日讯　中印友好协会在今天致电印中友好协会，祝贺印度共和国国庆。电文如下：印中友好协会主席森德拉尔先生理事会主席昌德博士全国执行委员会主席侯赛尼博士
    值此印度共和国成立七周年之际，我们谨向印度政府和人民致以诚恳的祝贺。
    祝印度共和国日益繁荣，祝印度人民更幸福，祝中印人民间的友好合作和文化交流获得新的更大的发展。
    中印友好协会会长　丁西林
        1957年1月25日北京
    新华社25日讯　中印友好协会今天晚上举行电影招待会，庆祝印度共和国成立七周年。
    招待会上，放映了介绍印度五年计划的纪录影片和中国新出品的故事片“家”。
    印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和大使馆官员以及在北京的印度朋友应邀出席了电影招待会。
    出席电影招待会的，有史良、郑振铎、夏衍、曾昭抡和各界人士三百多人。
