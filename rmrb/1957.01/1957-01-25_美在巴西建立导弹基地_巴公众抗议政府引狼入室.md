Title: US Establishes Missile Base in Brazil, Brazilian Public Protests Government Allowing a Wolf into the House
Author:
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Rio de Janeiro, 23rd -- Brazil and the United States signed a final agreement on the 21st allowing the United States to establish a missile observation station on the island of Fernando de Noronha in Brazil.
    For the past few months, the Brazilian public has been protesting to the government against allowing the United States to establish a military base on the island.

### 美在巴西建立导弹基地  巴公众抗议政府引狼入室

1957-01-25
第6版()
专栏：

    美在巴西建立导弹基地
    巴公众抗议政府引狼入室
    新华社23日讯　里约热内卢消息：巴西和美国21日签订了关于允许美国在巴西斐南多诺隆耶岛上建立导弹观测站的最后协定。
    最近几个月来巴西公众一直在向政府提出抗议，反对让美国在这个岛上设立军事基地。
