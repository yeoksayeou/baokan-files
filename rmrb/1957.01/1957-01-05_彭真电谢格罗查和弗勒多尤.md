Title: Peng Zhen Sends Telegram of Thanks to Groza and Furtado

Author:
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, January 4th: Peng Zhen, head of the delegation of the National People's Congress of China and the delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, sent telegrams from Sofia on January 3rd to Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Romania, and Furtado, Chairman of the People's Council of the city of Bucharest, respectively, expressing heartfelt thanks for the warm welcome and cordial reception the delegation received during its visit to Romania.

In the telegram to Groza, Peng Zhen also wished him a speedy recovery to health.

### 彭真电谢格罗查和弗勒多尤

1957-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真电谢格罗查和弗勒多尤
    新华社索非亚4日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团团长彭真，1月3日从索非亚分别打电报给罗马尼亚国民议会主席团主席格罗查和布加勒斯特市人民会议主席弗勒多尤，对代表团在罗马尼亚访问期间受到的热烈欢迎和亲切的接待表示衷心的感谢。
    在给格罗查的电报中，彭真还祝他的身体恢复健康。
