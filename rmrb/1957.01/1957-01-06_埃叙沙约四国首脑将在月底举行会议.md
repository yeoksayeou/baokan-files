Title: Four Heads of State from Egypt, Syria, Saudi Arabia and Jordan to Meet at the End of the Month
Author:
Date: 1957-01-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 4th, Cairo: Cairo Radio broadcast today that Egyptian President Nasser, Saudi Arabian King Saud, Syrian President al-Quwatli and Jordanian King Hussein will meet at the end of this month in Riyadh, the capital of Saudi Arabia.

### 埃叙沙约四国首脑将在月底举行会议

1957-01-06
第6版()
专栏：

    埃叙沙约四国首脑将在月底举行会议
    新华社4日讯　开罗消息：开罗电台今天广播说，埃及总统纳赛尔、沙特阿拉伯国王沙特、叙利亚总统库阿特利和约旦国王侯赛尼将在这个月底在沙特阿拉伯的首都利雅得举行会议。
