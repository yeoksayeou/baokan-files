Title: Premier Zhou Returns to Kunming
Author:
Date: 1957-01-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, January 2nd: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage returned to Kunming by special plane at 2:00 PM today after concluding their friendly visits to Vietnam, Cambodia, India, Burma, and Pakistan.

When Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and others stepped off the plane, Liu Minghui, Acting Governor of Yunnan Province, Xie Fuzhi, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Lieutenant General Chen Kang, Commander of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, and Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming, who were waiting at the airport, shook hands with Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long one by one and extended their greetings.

Also welcoming Premier Zhou at the airport were many responsible persons from the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and various democratic parties in Yunnan Province.

U Maung Maung Toe, Consul-General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and Gao Hongling, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, were also at the airport to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 2nd: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage left New Delhi by special plane at 10:00 PM on January 1st to return to China.

Indian Prime Minister Nehru and cabinet ministers, the chiefs of staff of the Army, Navy, and Air Force, as well as Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru and his wife, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to India saw them off at Palam Airport.

The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni also went to the airport to see them off.

Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili, embassy staff, and representatives of overseas Chinese also went to the airport to bid farewell.

Before boarding the plane, Premier Zhou Enlai told reporters: Although I only spent three days in New Delhi on my way back home this time, I accomplished a lot and saw the Bhakra-Nangal project, which is not only the largest in India but also world-famous. These have benefited us greatly.

Premier Zhou Enlai said that he and Prime Minister Nehru had lengthy talks, exchanged opinions, and also had contact with people from other fields. Finally, he congratulated the reporters on the New Year and, through the reporters, extended New Year and peace greetings to the Indian people and the people of the world.

After shaking hands with the well-wishers one by one, Premier Zhou Enlai boarded the plane. He waved goodbye and once again shouted, "Hindi Chini bhai bhai!" (Indians and Chinese are brothers!)

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 2nd: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long paid a New Year visit to President Prasad at the Presidential Palace on the afternoon of January 1st.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 2nd: Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru continued their talks for two hours at the Indian Prime Minister's residence on the afternoon of January 1st.

### 周总理回到昆明

1957-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理回到昆明
  新华社昆明2日电　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和随行人员在结束了对越南、柬埔寨、印度、缅甸和巴基斯坦等国的友好访问以后，今天下午二时乘专机回到昆明。
    周恩来总理、贺龙副总理等走下飞机时，等候在机场上的云南省代省长刘明辉、中共云南省委员会第一书记谢富治、中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长陈康中将和昆明市市长潘朔端等都一一同周恩来总理、贺龙副总理握手，并向他们问候。
    在机场上欢迎周总理的，还有政协云南省委员会、云南省各民主党派负责人等多人。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂铎、越南民主共和国驻昆明领事高鸿岭等，也都在机场上欢迎周恩来总理。
    新华社新德里2日电　周恩来总理，贺龙副总理和随行人员在1月1日晚上十点乘专机离开新德里回国。
    印度总理尼赫鲁和内阁部长，陆、海、空军的参谋长以及印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁夫妇和各国驻印度外交使节到巴兰机场送行。
    到机场送行的还有达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼。
    中国驻印度大使潘自力、大使馆人员和华侨代表也到机场送别。
    周恩来总理在登上飞机以前对记者们说：我这次在回国途中来到新德里虽然只有三天，但做了很多事情，看到了不仅是印度最大的而且是世界著名的巴克拉—南加尔工程，这些使我们受益很多。
    周恩来总理说，他和尼赫鲁总理进行了长时间的谈话，交换了意见，同时也和其他方面的人接触。最后他向记者们祝贺新年，并且通过记者向印度人民和世界人民祝贺新年与和平。
    周恩来总理同欢送的人一一握手后登上飞机，他在挥手告别时再一次高呼：“印地秦尼巴伊巴伊！”（印中人民是兄弟）
    新华社新德里2日电　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理1月1日下午到印度总统府向普拉沙德总统祝贺新年。
    新华社新德里2日电　周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理1月1日下午在印度总理府继续会谈两小时。
