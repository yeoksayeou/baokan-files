Title: Romanian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 29th

    Romanian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, January 29th - The Romanian Cultural Delegation arrived in Beijing this afternoon.
    The head of the delegation is Ion Pas, First Deputy Minister of the Romanian Ministry of Culture. The members are Ștefan Milcu, Academician of the Romanian Academy of Sciences and First Secretary of the Romanian Academy of Sciences, and Maria Ionescu, Head of the Asian Division of the Romanian Association for Foreign Culture. The delegation will consult with the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China to determine the 1957 implementation plan for the Sino-Romanian cultural cooperation agreement.

### 罗马尼亚文化代表团到达北京

1957-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚文化代表团到达北京
    新华社29日讯　罗马尼亚文化代表团今天下午到达北京。
    代表团团长是罗马尼亚文化部第一副部长扬·帕斯，团员是罗马尼亚科学院院士、罗马尼亚科学院第一书记斯蒂芬·米尔库和罗马尼亚对外文化协会亚洲股长玛丽亚·约尼斯库。代表团将同中华人民共和国文化部商定中、罗文化合作协定1957年执行计划。
