Title: Premier U Ba Swe Hosts Chinese Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, January 20th: Burmese Premier U Ba Swe held a banquet at his official residence on the evening of January 19th to entertain the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Marshal Ye Jianying.

Those present at the banquet included Deputy Prime Minister Thakin Tin, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Bo Mu Aung, cabinet ministers, Deputy Chief of Staff Dan Pe and Clift, and other senior military officers. Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and embassy staff were also invited.

On the morning of the same day, the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation visited General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Burmese Armed Forces. General Ne Win presented Marshal Ye Jianying and the delegation members with gifts.

Marshal Ye Jianying received representatives of the local overseas Chinese community that afternoon and attended a tea party held by the overseas Chinese to welcome the delegation.

### 吴巴瑞总理招待我军事代表团

1957-01-21
第5版()
专栏：

    吴巴瑞总理招待我军事代表团
    新华社仰光20日电　缅甸总理吴巴瑞1月19日晚上在他的官邸举行宴会，招待以叶剑英元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团。
    出席宴会的有副总理德钦丁、议会代表院议长波木昂、内阁部长们、副总参谋长丹佩和克利夫特以及其他高级军官等。中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和大使馆人员也应邀出席。
    当天上午，中国军事友好代表团曾经访问了缅甸国防军总参谋长奈温将军。奈温将军向叶剑英元帅和代表团团员赠送了礼物。
    叶剑英元帅曾经在当天下午接见了当地华侨的代表，并且参加了华侨为欢迎代表团而举行的茶会。
