Title: Panchen Erdini Returns to Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1957-01-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 30th. Panchen Erdini returned to Lhasa from India yesterday.

At 10:54 am yesterday, the special plane carrying Panchen Erdini arrived at Damshung Airport near Lhasa, Tibet. Panchen Erdini was warmly welcomed at the airport by Tibetan and Han responsible cadres from the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, the Tibet Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and other organizations.

Panchen Erdini’s main entourage, including Zasa Jiantung Jigme, had already returned to China by plane on the 26th.

### 班禅额尔德尼回拉萨

1957-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼回拉萨
    新华社拉萨30日电　班禅额尔德尼于昨天从印度回到我国拉萨。
    昨天上午十时五十四分，班禅额尔德尼所乘的专机抵达西藏拉萨附近的当雄飞机场。班禅额尔德尼在机场上受到了西藏自治区筹备委员会、中共西藏工委、中国人民解放军西藏军区等机关的藏族、汉族负责干部的热烈欢迎。
    班禅额尔德尼的主要随员扎萨詹东·计晋美等已在26日先期乘飞机回国。
