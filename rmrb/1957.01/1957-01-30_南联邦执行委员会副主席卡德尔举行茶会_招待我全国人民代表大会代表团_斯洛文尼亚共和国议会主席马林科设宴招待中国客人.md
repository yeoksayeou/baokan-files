Title: Kardelj, Vice-President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, Held a Tea Party to Host the Delegation of the National People's Congress of China; Marinko, President of the Assembly of the Republic of Slovenia, Hosted a Banquet for the Chinese Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-01-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, January 29th. Edvard Kardelj, Vice-President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, held a tea party at 5:00 PM on the 28th to host the entire delegation of the National People's Congress of China, headed by Peng Zhen, and the delegation of the People's Committee of Beijing Municipality.

Present at the tea party on the Yugoslav side were Aleksandar Ranković and Svetozar Vukmanović, Vice-Presidents of the Federal Executive Council, Vladimir Simić and Lidija Šentjurc, Vice-Presidents of the Federal National Assembly, and others.

Wu Xiuquan, the Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, was also invited to attend the tea party.

At the tea party, Vice-President Kardelj and Delegation Leader Peng Zhen both delivered speeches. Kardelj, on behalf of the Federal Executive Council, welcomed the delegation. Peng Zhen spoke about his impressions of Yugoslavia in his speech. In their talks, they both mentioned the profound friendship between the peoples of China and Yugoslavia and expressed their belief that the friendly cooperation between the two peoples would be further developed in the future.

Following this, Kardelj and Vukmanović introduced the organizational structure and functions of the Federal Executive Council, as well as the economic and production situation in Yugoslavia, and answered some of the questions raised by the guests.

Finally, Vice-President Kardelj said that they also wanted to learn more about China. However, in order not to increase the fatigue of the guests, it would be better to let a delegation from the Federal People's Assembly go to China to learn about it. Delegation Leader Peng Zhen immediately expressed his welcome.

The tea party continued in a friendly atmosphere until 8:00 PM.

Xinhua News Agency, Ljubljana, January 29th. The members of the delegation of the National People's Congress of China and the delegation of the People's Committee of Beijing Municipality attended a banquet given by Marinko, President of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Slovenia, Yugoslavia, at noon on the 27th.

The Yugoslav side present included: Kozak, Vice-President of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Slovenia; Tomšič, Secretary of the Slovenian League of Communists organization; Rus, President of the Republican Chamber of the National Assembly; Tihhi, member of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia; and Krivic, member of the Executive Council of the People's Republic of Slovenia.

In addition to all the members of the delegation, Wu Xiuquan, the Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, who accompanied the delegation on its visits to various places, also attended the banquet on the Chinese side.

Marinko and Peng Zhen spoke at the banquet successively (see page five).

### 南联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔举行茶会  招待我全国人民代表大会代表团  斯洛文尼亚共和国议会主席马林科设宴招待中国客人

1957-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

    南联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔举行茶会
    招待我全国人民代表大会代表团
    斯洛文尼亚共和国议会主席马林科设宴招待中国客人
    新华社贝尔格莱德29日电　南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席爱德华·卡德尔在28日下午五时举行茶会，招待以彭真为首的中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团全体人员。
    出席茶会的，南斯拉夫方面有联邦执行委员会副主席亚·兰科维奇、斯·伏克曼诺维奇，联邦人民议会副主席弗·西米奇和利·申丘尔茨等多人。
    中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权也应邀出席了茶会。
    在茶会上，卡德尔副主席和彭真团长都讲了话。卡德尔代表联邦执行委员会向代表团表示欢迎。彭真在讲话中谈了他对南斯拉夫的观感。他们在谈话中还都提到了中南两国人民的深厚友谊，并且表示相信中南两国人民的友好合作在今后将会得到进一步的发展。
    接着，卡德尔和伏克曼诺维奇向客人们介绍了联邦执行委员会的组织机构和职能以及南斯拉夫的经济和生产等情况，并且回答了客人们提出来的一些问题。
    最后，卡德尔副主席说，他们也很想多了解一些中国的问题。但是，为了不增加客人们的疲劳，最好还是让联邦人民议会代表团到中国去了解。彭真团长立即表示欢迎。
    茶会在友好的气氛中一直进行到晚上八时才结束。
    据新华社卢布尔雅那29日电中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团人员，27日午间出席了南斯拉夫斯洛文尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席马林科举行的宴会。
    南斯拉夫方面出席作陪的有：斯洛文尼亚人民共和国国民议会副主席科扎克，斯洛文尼亚共产主义者联盟组织书记托姆希奇，国民议会共和国院主席鲁斯，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员季赫里和斯洛文尼亚人民共和国执行委员会委员克里维奇等。
    中国方面出席宴会的除代表团全体人员外，还有陪同代表团到各地访问的中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权。
    马林科和彭真先后在宴会上讲了话（见第五版）。
