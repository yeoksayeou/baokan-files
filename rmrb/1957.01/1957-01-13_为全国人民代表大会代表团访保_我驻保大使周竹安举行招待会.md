Title: Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria Zhou Zhuan Holds Reception for NPC Delegation's Visit to Bulgaria
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 12th - On the evening of January 10th, Zhou Zhuan, Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, held a grand reception at the Bulgaria Hotel in Sofia for the visit of the Chinese National People's Congress delegation and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee delegation to Bulgaria.

The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Yugov, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Zhivkov, the Chairman of the National Assembly of Bulgaria, Georgi Damianov, and the Chairman of the Executive Committee of the National Assembly, Kozovski, attended the reception.

Ambassador Zhou Zhuan spoke first at the reception.

Peng Zhen, the head of the delegation, then spoke. He first expressed his heartfelt thanks for the warm welcome and cordial reception the delegation received during its visit. He announced that he was entrusted by Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, to invite a delegation from the Bulgarian National Assembly to visit China. He said with a smile to everyone that Comrade Georgi Damianov and Comrade Kozovski had happily accepted this invitation. Delegation leader Peng Zhen wished the People's Republic of Bulgaria increasing strength and prosperity, the Bulgarian people happiness, and the leading comrades of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers, the National Assembly, and the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party good health.

The following also spoke at the reception: Georgi Damianov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Traikov, Deputy Head of the delegation Zhang Bojun, Yugov, Deputy Head of the delegation Cheng Qian, Zhivkov, Deputy Head of the delegation Hu Ziang, and others.

Yugov said, "We know that we have many difficulties in socialist construction, but we believe that with the leadership of the Party and the united efforts of the people, and with the assistance of the Soviet Union and other socialist countries, we will be able to overcome these difficulties and achieve brilliant achievements." He asked the delegation to convey the cordial greetings of the Bulgarian people to comrades Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai, to the Chinese people, and to the Chinese Communist Party.

Georgi Damianov said, "Although the delegation has only visited our country for eight days, this visit has increased mutual understanding and friendship, and strengthened the unity of socialist countries led by the Soviet Union."

Zhivkov said, "Sino-Bulgarian friendship is eternal and unbreakable. Our strength lies in the fact that we are all led by parties loyal to the path opened up by the October Revolution, in the fact that our countries are all united in the socialist camp led by the Soviet Union, and in learning from the Soviet Union's advanced experience, which has worldwide significance."

### 为全国人民代表大会代表团访保  我驻保大使周竹安举行招待会

1957-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    为全国人民代表大会代表团访保
    我驻保大使周竹安举行招待会
    据新华社索非亚12日电　1月10日晚间，中国驻保加利亚大使周竹安在索非亚的保加利亚饭店为中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团访问保加利亚举行了盛大招待会。
    保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫、保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫、保加利亚国民议会主席格·达米扬诺夫、国民议会执行委员会主席科佐夫斯基等出席了招待会。
    周竹安大使首先在招待会上讲话。
    代表团团长彭真接着讲话。他首先对代表团在访问期间受到的热情欢迎和亲切接待表示衷心感谢。他宣布，他受中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇的委托，邀请保加利亚国民议会代表团到中国访问。他满面笑容地对大家说，格·达米扬诺夫同志和科佐夫斯基同志已经愉快地接受了这个邀请。彭真团长祝保加利亚人民共和国日益强大繁荣，祝保加利亚人民幸福，祝保加利亚部长会议、国民议会和保加利亚共产党中央委员会的领导同志们健康。
    在招待会上接着讲话的有：格·达米扬诺夫，保加利亚部长会议第一副主席特莱科夫，代表团副团长章伯钧，于哥夫，代表团副团长程潜，日夫科夫，代表团副团长胡子昂等。
    于哥夫说，我们知道，在社会主义建设中我们有很多困难，但是，在党的领导和全国人民团结一致努力下，在苏联和其他社会主义国家的援助下，我们相信一定能够克服这些困难，取得辉煌的成就。他请代表团把保加利亚人民的亲切的问候带给毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来等同志，带给中国人民和中国共产党。
    格·达米扬诺夫说，虽然代表团只在我国访问了八天，这次访问却增进了相互间的了解和友谊，增强了以苏联为首的社会主义各国的团结。
    日夫科夫说，中保友谊是永恒的、牢不可破的。我们的力量就在于我们都是由忠于十月革命所开辟的道路的党所领导的，就在于我们的国家都是团结在以苏联为首的社会主义阵营里，就在于学习具有全世界意义的苏联的先进经验。
