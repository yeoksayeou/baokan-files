Title: West Germany Wants a Piece of the Pie in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1957-01-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 7th: Bonn dispatch: West German government officials said on the 5th that they welcome the US President Eisenhower's plan for economic "aid" to Middle Eastern countries, as proposed in his message on Middle East policy, and believe that West Germany may participate in such "aid" measures to some extent.

### 西德想在中东分一杯羹

1957-01-08
第6版()
专栏：

    西德想在中东分一杯羹
    据新华社柏林7日电　波恩消息：西德政府官员们5日说，他们欢迎美国总统艾森豪威尔在中东政策的咨文中提出的对中东国家实行经济“援助”的计划，并且认为西德可能在某种程度上参加这样的“援助”措施。
