Title: UN Forces Arrive at Egyptian-Israeli Border; Israeli Troops Still Occupy Gaza and Gulf of Aqaba
Author:
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 23rd: A UN spokesman in Cairo announced on the 22nd that the UN Emergency Force had reached the entire Egyptian-Israeli border on the 22nd, with the exception of the Gaza Strip and the area along the Gulf of Aqaba.

Israeli troops are still occupying the Gaza Strip and the narrow coastal strip along the Gulf of Aqaba. The UN General Assembly has already passed a resolution on the 19th requesting UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld to ensure the complete withdrawal of Israeli troops and to submit a report to the UN General Assembly within five days. Therefore, January 24th is the deadline for the withdrawal of Israeli troops from these areas.

### 联合国部队到达埃以边境  以军仍霸占加沙和亚喀巴湾

1957-01-24
第6版()
专栏：

    联合国部队到达埃以边境
    以军仍霸占加沙和亚喀巴湾
    据新华社开罗23日电　联合国发言人22日在开罗宣布，联合国紧急部队22日已经到达除了加沙地带和沿亚喀巴湾地带以外的整个埃及—以色列边境。
    以色列军队现在仍然占领着加沙地带和沿亚喀巴湾的狭长海岸地带。联合国大会已经在19日通过决议要求联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德使以色列军队全部撤退，并且在五天内向联合国大会提出报告，因此，1月24日是以色列军队撤离这些地带的限期。
