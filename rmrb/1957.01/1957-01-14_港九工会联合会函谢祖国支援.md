Title: Hong Kong and Kowloon Trades Union Council Sends Letter Thanking the Motherland for Support
Author:
Date: 1957-01-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 12th: Hong Kong news: The Hong Kong and Kowloon Trades Union Council wrote a letter to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on the 10th, thanking the working-class brothers in the motherland for their concern and assistance to the Hong Kong workers during the Kowloon riots in October last year. The letter said: While our Hong Kong and Kowloon workers and compatriots were in the midst of the riots, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Guangdong and Guangzhou Municipal Federations of Trade Unions, the Shanghai Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, our dear working-class brothers across the country, and compatriots nationwide showed boundless concern for our suffering, and swiftly gave us tremendous assistance and support, enabling us to more effectively combat the Kuomintang secret agents' vicious conspiracies, protect our peaceful lives, and carry out the work of comforting the dead and healing the wounded, and providing post-disaster relief. Our Hong Kong and Kowloon workers personally experienced the brotherly concern and support of the workers and compatriots of the motherland, felt incomparable warmth, and expressed our greatest gratitude.

### 港九工会联合会函谢祖国支援

1957-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    港九工会联合会函谢祖国支援
    新华社广州12日电　香港消息：港九工会联合会10日写信给中华全国总工会，感谢祖国工人兄弟在去年10月九龙暴乱事件中给香港工人同胞的关怀和援助。信里说：正当我们港九工人和同胞处在暴乱的期间，全国总工会、广东省和广州市工会联合会、上海市工会联合会和全国亲爱的工人兄弟们及全国同胞们对我们遭遇的苦难无限关怀，迅速的给予我们以巨大的援助和支持，使我们更有力的打击了国民党特务的恶毒阴谋，保护自己的和平生活，进行了恤死扶伤、善后救济的工作。我们港九工人亲身体验到祖国工人和同胞亲逾兄弟的关心支持，感到无比的温暖，并表示最大的感谢。
