Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Graduates of Labor Cadre School
Author:
Date: 1957-01-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 25th. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi and Chen Yun, and General Secretary Deng Xiaoping, received all the students graduating this year from the Beijing Labor Cadre School of the Ministry of Labor this afternoon.

### 中共中央领导人接见劳动干部学校毕业生

1957-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见劳动干部学校毕业生
    新华社25日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东，副主席刘少奇、陈云，总书记邓小平，今天下午接见了劳动部北京劳动干部学校今年毕业的全体学生。
