Title: British Foreign Office Spokesman Plays Rogue, Insists Anglo-Egyptian Treaty Remains Valid
Author:
Date: 1957-01-04
Page: 6

    British Foreign Office Spokesman Plays Rogue
    Insists Anglo-Egyptian Treaty Remains Valid
    Xinhua News Agency, London, 3rd: A British Foreign Office spokesman said on the 2nd that Britain does not recognize Egypt's right to abrogate the Anglo-Egyptian Treaty concluded in 1954. He said that Britain believes the Anglo-Egyptian Treaty concluded in 1954 remains valid.
    This is the first time the British government has expressed its attitude towards Egypt's announcement of the abrogation of the Anglo-Egyptian Treaty.
    The British Foreign Office spokesman said that Britain has not yet decided what action it might take.

### 英外交部发言人耍赖皮  硬说英埃条约仍然有效

1957-01-04
第6版()
专栏：

    英外交部发言人耍赖皮
    硬说英埃条约仍然有效
    据新华社3日讯　伦敦消息：英国外交部发言人2日说，英国不承认埃及有权废除1954年缔结的英埃条约。他说，英国认为，1954年缔结的英埃条约仍然有效。
    这是英国政府对埃及宣布废除英埃条约的行动第一次表示态度。
    英国外交部发言人说，英国还没有决定它可能采取什么行动。
