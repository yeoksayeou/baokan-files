Title: Nasser Visits Soviet Industrial Exhibition Hoping to Promote Trade and Consolidate Egyptian-Soviet Friendship
Author:
Date: 1957-01-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 27th -- According to TASS, Egyptian President Nasser visited the Soviet Industrial Exhibition held in Egypt on January 26th.

President Nasser visited the exhibition with great interest. After his visit, Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Boryakov, who is currently in Egypt, told him that the Soviet government had decided to donate most of the exhibits to the Egyptian government after the exhibition ends. This includes: automobiles and tractors, agricultural and road construction machinery, electric motors, textile equipment and medical equipment, optical instruments, film equipment, and consumer goods.

Boryakov said, "We hope that these gifts will help the Egyptian government in restoring the national economy destroyed by the aggressors."

President Nasser said in his reply, "On behalf of the Egyptian people, I thank the Soviet government for this generous gift. The donation of these items is an expression of the development of friendly relations between Egypt and the Soviet Union. We want to see this friendship grow and expand day by day."

When leaving the exhibition, President Nasser wrote the following words in the guest book: "I am very pleased to visit the Russian exhibition held in Egypt. I am extremely impressed by the great progress made in all areas of work. I hope this exhibition will promote broader trade between the Soviet Union and Egypt, thereby further consolidating the friendship between our two countries."

### 纳赛尔参观苏联工业展览会  希望促进贸易往来巩固埃苏友谊

1957-01-29
第5版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔参观苏联工业展览会
    希望促进贸易往来巩固埃苏友谊
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社开罗讯：埃及共和国总统纳赛尔1月26日参观了在埃及举办的苏联工业展览会。
    纳赛尔总统以极大的兴趣参观了展览会。当他参观完毕以后，目前在埃及的苏联对外贸易部副部长鲍尔夏科夫对他说，苏联政府已经决定，在展览结束以后把大部分展览品赠送给埃及政府。其中包括：汽车和拖拉机、农业机器和筑路机器、电动机、纺织装备和医疗装备、光学仪器、电影装备和消费品。
    鲍尔夏科夫说，我们希望，这些赠品对于埃及政府在恢复被侵略者所破坏的国民经济方面会有所帮助。
    纳赛尔总统在致答词时说：“我代表埃及人民为这次惠赠向苏联政府致谢。赠送这些物品是发展埃及和苏联之间的友好关系的一种表示。我们要使这种友谊日益发展和扩大。”
    纳赛尔总统在离开展览会时在来宾留言簿上写了下列几句话：“我很高兴能参观俄国在埃及举办的展览会。对于在各方面工作中所取得的这种伟大进步，我极感惊异。我希望这次展览会会促进苏联和埃及之间更广泛的贸易往来，从而更进一步地巩固我们两国之间的友谊。”
