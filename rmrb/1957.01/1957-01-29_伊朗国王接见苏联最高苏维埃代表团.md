Title: Iranian King Receives Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, 27th - According to TASS, Tehran, on the 26th, the King of Iran, Mohammad Reza Pahlavi, received the delegation from the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

King Pahlavi and the members of the delegation held a free-ranging conversation for about an hour. The conversation touched upon various issues in the mutual relations between Iran and the Soviet Union, especially the expansion of economic, cultural and other ties between the two countries.

### 伊朗国王接见苏联最高苏维埃代表团

1957-01-29
第5版()
专栏：

    伊朗国王接见苏联最高苏维埃代表团
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社德黑兰27日讯：伊朗国王穆罕默德·札萨·巴列维在26日接见了苏联最高苏维埃代表团。
    巴列维国王和代表团人员进行了约一小时的无拘束的谈话。谈话涉及了伊朗和苏联互相关系中的各项问题，特别是扩大两国的经济、文化和其他联系的问题。
