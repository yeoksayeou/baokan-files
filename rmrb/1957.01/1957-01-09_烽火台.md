Title: Beacon Tower
Author: Yisha
Date: 1957-01-09
Page: 8

    Beacon Tower
    Yisha
    With millions of tons of earth
  Mixed with
  Hundreds of millions of drops of blood and sweat, countless beacon towers were built
  Along the Great Wall
  Extending into the interior
      Arranged in a straight line
    For thousands of years
    Here once rose
      Hundreds of millions of beacon fires
      That blood-red
        Flame of hatred
    Burned dry the tears of so many mothers…
    Finally, today has arrived
    People's labor
      Is not for war
      But for peace, raised on the beacon tower
  That blood-red
  Radiance, surpassing the beacon fire, is the red flag of the builders—extending from Beijing
  An iron arm grasps tightly
  The fists and destinies of dozens of fraternal nationalities
  Mothers, when you see this area
  Raise smoke and fire again
    Please do not panic
  That thick smoke
  Is the train breathing
  That glittering
  Is the workers welding steel beams
  …

### 烽火台
易莎
1957-01-09
第8版()
专栏：

    烽火台
    易莎用千万担土
  拌和着
  亿万滴血汗筑成的无数座烽火台
  顺着长城
  伸向内地
      一字儿摆开
    几千年来
    这里曾经举起过
      几亿次烽火
      那血红的
        仇恨的火焰
    把多少母亲的眼泪烧干……
    终于到了今天
    人们的劳动
      不是为了战争
      而是为了和平在烽火台上举起的
  那血红的
  赛过烽火的光焰的是建设者的红旗——从北京伸来了
  一条钢铁的臂膀把几十个兄弟民族的拳头
  和命运紧握在一起母亲呵当你看见这一带
  再升起烟火时
    请不要惊慌那浓烟
  是火车在喘气那亮闪闪的
  是工人们在焊接钢梁
  ……
