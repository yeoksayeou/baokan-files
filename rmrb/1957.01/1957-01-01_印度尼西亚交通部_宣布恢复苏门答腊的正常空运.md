Title: Indonesian Ministry of Transportation Announces Restoration of Normal Air Transport in Sumatra
Author:
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 31st - The Indonesian Ministry of Transportation announced that the Minister of Transportation has ordered the resumption of normal air traffic throughout the island of Sumatra, effective today, with the temporary exception of three regions, including Aceh, due to technical issues. All seaports in Sumatra, except those within military curfew zones, are open as usual. However, the docks of Belawan (the port of Medan), although within a military curfew zone, are also open as usual.

### 印度尼西亚交通部  宣布恢复苏门答腊的正常空运

1957-01-01
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚交通部
    宣布恢复苏门答腊的正常空运
    新华社雅加达31日电　印度尼西亚交通部宣布，交通部长下令从今天起恢复全苏门答腊岛的正常航空交通，只有亚齐等三地区因技术问题暂时作为例外。苏门答腊的海港，除在军事戒严区内者以外，都照常开放。但是，勿拉弯（棉兰的港口）的码头，虽然在军事戒严区内，也照常开放。
