Title: Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-01-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 3rd: A government delegation from the German Democratic Republic, led by Prime Minister Grotewohl, arrived in Moscow on the afternoon of the 3rd. The delegation is here to negotiate with the Soviet government on the further development of friendly cooperation between the two countries.

Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Zorin and the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union, König, went to the Soviet border to welcome the delegation.

At the Belorussian Station in Moscow, the national flags of Germany and the Soviet Union were hung, and the station was filled with welcoming crowds.

Among those welcoming the delegation at the station were Soviet government leaders Bulganin, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Saburov, Suslov, Shepilov, and others, as well as ambassadors from various countries to the Soviet Union and representatives from Moscow mass organizations.

Prime Minister Grotewohl reviewed the guard of honor and spoke to the people welcoming the delegation. In his speech, he expressed the hope that the friendship between Germany and the Soviet Union would be further consolidated and developed on the basis of internationalism and mutual understanding.

### 民主德国政府代表团抵莫斯科

1957-01-04
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府代表团抵莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科3日电　德意志民主共和国政府代表团在格罗提渥总理的率领下已经在3日下午到达莫斯科。代表团是前来同苏联政府谈判关于进一步发展两国之间的友好合作的问题的。
    苏联外交部副部长佐林和德意志民主共和国驻苏联大使柯尼希曾前往苏联边境迎接代表团。
    在莫斯科的白俄罗斯车站上，悬挂着德苏两国的国旗，车站中站满了欢迎的人群。
    在车站欢迎代表团的有苏联政府领导人布尔加宁、米高扬、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、苏斯洛夫、谢皮洛夫等，以及各国驻苏联大使和莫斯科群众团体的代表。
    格罗提渥总理检阅了仪仗队，并且向欢迎代表团的人讲了话。他在致词时表示希望德苏两国间的友谊在国际主义和互相了解的基础上进一步巩固和发展。
