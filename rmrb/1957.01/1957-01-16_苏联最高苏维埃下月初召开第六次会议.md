Title: The Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to convene its Sixth Session early next month
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 15th: TASS reports that the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union announced on the 14th that the Sixth Session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union will be held in Moscow on February 5th.

### 苏联最高苏维埃下月初召开第六次会议

1957-01-16
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃下月初召开第六次会议
    据新华社15日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团14日宣布：第四届苏联最高苏维埃第六次会议将于2月5日在莫斯科召开。
