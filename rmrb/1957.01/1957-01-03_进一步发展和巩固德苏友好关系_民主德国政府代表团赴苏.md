Title: Further Develop and Consolidate the Friendly Relations between Germany and the Soviet Union: East German Government Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-01-03
Page: 5

    Further Develop and Consolidate the Friendly Relations between Germany and the Soviet Union
    East German Government Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 2nd. According to today's "Neues Deutschland" report, in accordance with the agreement between the governments of the German Democratic Republic and the Soviet Union, the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl, departed from Berlin by special train on the evening of January 1st for Moscow. This government delegation will hold talks with the Soviet government on political, economic, and cultural issues in order to further develop and consolidate relations between the two countries.
    The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic includes: Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl (head of delegation), First Deputy Prime Minister Ulbricht, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Bolz, Deputy Prime Ministers Heinrich Rau, Nuschke, Loch, Schulz, Rauschner, Oesner, SED Politburo member M. Chidewan, alternate member of the Politburo, Norman, and the German Democratic Republic's Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Koenig, and others.

### 进一步发展和巩固德苏友好关系  民主德国政府代表团赴苏

1957-01-03
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步发展和巩固德苏友好关系
    民主德国政府代表团赴苏
    新华社柏林2日电　据今天的“新德意志报”报道，按照德意志民主共和国和苏联两国政府之间的协议，以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团在1月1日晚上从柏林乘专车启程赴莫斯科。这个政府代表团将同苏联政府就政治、经济和文化等方面的问题举行谈判，以便进一步发展和巩固两国之间的关系。
    德意志民主共和国政府代表团包括：德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥（团长）、第一副总理乌布利希、副总理兼外交部长博尔茨、副总理海因里希·劳、努舍克、洛赫、舒尔茨、劳施纳、厄斯纳、统一社会党政治局委员马·希德万、政治局候补委员诺曼和德意志民主共和国驻苏联大使柯尼希等人。
