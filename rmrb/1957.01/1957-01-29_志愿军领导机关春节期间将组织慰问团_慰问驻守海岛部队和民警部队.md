Title: Volunteer Army Leadership Organizes Spring Festival慰问团 to Comfort Troops Stationed on Islands and Public Security Police Forces
Author:
Date: 1957-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 28th - The leadership organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army has decided to organize four慰问团 during the Spring Festival to comfort troops stationed on islands and public security police forces in the demilitarized zone. The慰问团 will convey the warm concern of the people of the motherland to these troops and distribute慰问品 such as pens and handkerchiefs to each officer and soldier on behalf of the people of the motherland. Accompanying the慰问团 will also be cultural work troupes and film projection teams from various units.

### 志愿军领导机关春节期间将组织慰问团  慰问驻守海岛部队和民警部队

1957-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军领导机关春节期间将组织慰问团
    慰问驻守海岛部队和民警部队
    新华社平壤28日电　中国人民志愿军领导机关决定在春节期间组织四个慰问团，分别慰问驻守海岛部队和非军事区民警等部队。慰问团将向这些部队转达祖国人民的热情关怀，并且代表祖国人民把钢笔、手帕等慰问品分发给每个官兵。随同慰问团前去的还有各部队的文艺工作团和电影放映队。
