Title: Vietnam Cultivates Underwater Forests: A Green Forest Appears at Low Tide

Author:
Date: 1957-01-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 29th: The Democratic Republic of Vietnam is cultivating underwater forest resources. According to a reporter from the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, in 1956, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam planted more than 17 million underwater trees along the coast of the Red River Delta in northern Vietnam, and plans to plant more than 18 million trees this year.

In the coastal areas of Hai Ninh, Guang An, Jian An, Thai Binh, Nam Dinh and other places in the Red River Delta of northern Vietnam, underwater forests of high economic value grow everywhere. Whenever the tide recedes, lush green forests appear, with trees about three to four meters high. It is understood that many trees in Vietnam's underwater forests belong to the mangrove family, the same species as the underwater forests found in Fujian Province, China. This type of wood can be harvested for charcoal, and the tannin-containing bark can be used for tanning leather.

### 越南培植海底森林  潮水退落时就露出一片绿色树林

1957-01-30
第5版()
专栏：

    越南培植海底森林
    潮水退落时就露出一片绿色树林
    新华社河内29日电　越南民主共和国正在培植海底森林资源。据记者从越南民主共和国农林部获悉，1956年，越南民主共和国在越南北部红河三角洲沿海播种了一千七百多万棵海底树木，并且计划在今年再播种一千八百多万棵。
    在越南北部红河三角洲的海宁、广安、建安、太平、南定等地的沿海地区，到处生长着经济价值很高的海底森林。每当潮水退落时，一片片枝叶茂密的绿色树林就显露出来，树高约三、四公尺。据了解，越南的海底森林中很多树木属于红树科，同在中国福建省发现的海底森林的树种一样。这类木材可供采伐来烧木炭，含鞣酸的树皮可以用来鞣制皮革。
