Title: Indian President Receives Chinese Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 4th. Indian President Prasad received the Chinese women's delegation led by Shi Liang at the Presidential Palace on the afternoon of the 3rd.

    The delegation presented President Prasad with a crystal tripod as a souvenir. Head of the delegation, Shi Liang, said: The solidity of the crystal symbolizes the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Indian people.

    President Prasad inquired about the delegation's impressions of their trip to India. He said: The visit of the Chinese women's delegation to India will further strengthen the ties between the two countries. The President also took a group photo with all the delegates.

    The delegation then called on Vice President Radhakrishnan. The Vice President had lectured in Chongqing in 1944. He highly praised the rapid growth and progress of New China and expressed his willingness to visit Beijing if he had the opportunity. The Vice President said: China and India should unite in friendship, so that one billion people can actively build their respective motherlands and overcome the weakness and backwardness left by colonialism.

    The delegation presented the Vice President with an embroidered scroll as a souvenir.

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 4th. The Chinese women's delegation returned to New Delhi on the 3rd after visiting South India.

    The delegation flew from Mumbai to Bangalore, the capital of Mysore State, on December 30th last year. Subsequently, they visited Mysore City and Madras City. They visited women's relief organizations, factories, and rural areas in these places, and met with local women representatives. The delegation was warmly welcomed by local government authorities and women in various places.

### 印度总统接见我妇女代表团

1957-01-05
第5版()
专栏：

    印度总统接见我妇女代表团
    新华社新德里4日电　印度总统普拉沙德3日下午在总统府接见了由史良率领的中国妇女代表团。
    代表团向普拉沙德总统赠送了水晶鼎留念。史良团长说：水晶之坚固，象征中印人民友谊的牢固不破。
    普拉沙德总统问及代表团在印度的旅行观感。他说：中国妇女代表团访问印度将使两国间的联系更为加强。总统还同全体代表合影留念。
    代表团接着拜会了副总统拉达克里希南。副总统在1944年曾经到重庆讲过学。他对新中国成长进步的迅速极为称赞，并且表示愿有机会去北京访问。副总统说：中印两国应该友好团结，使十亿人民积极建设各自的祖国，克服殖民主义留下的积弱和落后。
    代表团向副总统赠送了一轴绣卷作纪念。
    新华社新德里4日电　中国妇女代表团访问南印度以后，在3日回到新德里。
    代表团在去年12月30日从孟买飞抵迈索尔邦首府班加罗尔市。随后，他们又访问了迈索尔市和马德拉斯市。他们在这些地方参观妇女救济机构、工厂和农村，并且同当地的妇女代表们会面。代表团在各地受到了地方政府当局和妇女们的热烈欢迎。
