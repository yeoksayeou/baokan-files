Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Advanced Workers of the Air Force Logistics and Students Graduating from Two Schools This Year
Author:
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 15th – Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Chen Yun, Vice Chairman, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, and Peng Dehuai, member of the Political Bureau, received this afternoon all the representatives of the Advanced Workers Conference of the Air Force Logistics of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and all the students graduating this year from the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League.
Xinhua News Agency, January 15th – Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Chen Yun, Vice Chairman, and Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, received this afternoon all the students graduating this year from the Central School of Political Science and Law Cadres.

### 中共中央领导人接见空军后勤先进工作者  并接见两个学校今年毕业的学生

1957-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见空军后勤先进工作者
    并接见两个学校今年毕业的学生
    新华社15日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东、副主席陈云、总书记邓小平、政治局委员彭德怀，在今天下午接见了中国人民解放军空军后勤先进工作者代表会议的全体代表和中国新民主主义青年团中央团校今年毕业的全体学生。
    新华社15日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东、副主席陈云、总书记邓小平，在今天下午接见了中央政法干部学校今年毕业的全体学生。
