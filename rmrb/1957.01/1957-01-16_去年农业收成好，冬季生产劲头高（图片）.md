Title: Good Agricultural Harvest Last Year, High Enthusiasm for Winter Production (Picture)
Author: Yuan Hao
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 3

    Good Agricultural Harvest Last Year, High Enthusiasm for Winter Production
    The Yongyuan Agricultural Cooperative of Shanglongwo Village in Jianping County, Hebei Province, harvested more than 66,000 jin of grain in 1956, transforming from a grain-deficient cooperative to a grain-surplus cooperative. Their enthusiasm for winter production is even greater.
                                      Photo by Yuan Hao (Xinhua News Agency draft)
    Every day, cooperative cadres gather here to collectively discuss and allocate production tasks.
    Constructing a 240-cubic-meter levee can protect the soil and water in the terraced fields.
    Reserving plenty of fodder to ensure livestock are fed well every day.
    The cooperative has more than 2,000 fruit trees, and protecting young trees is also an important task in winter.

### 去年农业收成好，冬季生产劲头高（图片）
袁浩
1957-01-16
第3版()
专栏：

    去年农业收成好，冬季生产劲头高
    河北建屏县上龙窝山庄永远农业社，1956年收获粮食六万六千多斤，由缺粮社变为余粮社。他们开展冬季生产的劲头更大了。
                                      袁浩摄（新华社稿）
    每天社干部们都要在这里集体商议分配生产任务。
    建筑一道二百四十方的护堤，可保住梯田里的水土。
    储备多多的饲草，让牲口每天都吃饱。
    社里有两千多株果树，保护幼树也是冬季的一项重要工作。
