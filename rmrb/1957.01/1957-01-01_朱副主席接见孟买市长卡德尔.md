Title: Zhu Vice-Chairman Receives Bombay Mayor Kader
Author:
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. Vice-Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China received the Mayor of Bombay, India, Kader this morning.

Present at the reception were Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice-Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, and Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy in China, Singh.

### 朱副主席接见孟买市长卡德尔

1957-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席接见孟买市长卡德尔
    新华社31日讯　中华人民共和国朱德副主席在今天上午接见了印度孟买市市长卡德尔。
    接见的时候在座的，有外交部副部长章汉夫，北京市副市长吴晗和印度驻中国大使馆临时代办辛格。
