Title: Delegation from the Russian Republic of the Industrial Cooperative Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. A delegation of five from the Russian Republic of the Industrial Cooperative of the Soviet Union, led by A. K. Ternov, Chairman of the Council of the Russian Republic of the Industrial Cooperative, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon at the invitation of the Central Bureau of Handicraft Industry and the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives.

The responsible persons of the Central Bureau of Handicraft Industry, the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives, and the Beijing Municipal Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives went to the station to welcome them. Romanin, Second Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, was also at the station to welcome them.

The delegation will visit Beijing, Nanjing, Suzhou, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Changsha, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other cities.

### 俄罗斯共和国工艺合作社代表团到北京

1957-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯共和国工艺合作社代表团到北京
    新华社4日讯　应中央手工业管理局、中华全国手工业合作总社筹备委员会邀请来我国访问的苏联俄罗斯共和国工艺合作社代表团一行五人，由团长、俄罗斯共和国工艺合作社会议理事会主席阿·克·捷尔诺夫率领，在今日下午乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有中央手工业管理局、中华全国手工业合作总社筹备委员会和北京市手工业合作总社的负责人。苏联驻我国大使馆二等秘书罗满宁也到车站欢迎。
  　代表团将到北京、南京、苏州、上海、杭州、长沙、广州、武汉等城市参观访问。
