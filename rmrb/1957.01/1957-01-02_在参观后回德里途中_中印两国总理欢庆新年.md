Title: Chinese and Indian Prime Ministers Celebrate New Year on the Return Trip to Delhi After the Visit
Author:
Date: 1957-01-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 1st. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long celebrated the New Year of 1957 with Prime Minister Nehru on the train on their way back to Delhi.

They spent New Year's Eve and welcomed the New Year in a special train on the way back to Delhi after visiting the Bhakra-Nangal Dam project.

The dining car was brilliantly lit with lamps and candles. Premier Zhou and Prime Minister Nehru jointly cut the New Year cake, and the guests had a good time together, congratulating each other on the New Year and praying for world peace.

Chinese, Indian, British, American, and French journalists who were accompanying the visit also attended this small New Year's celebration party.

### 在参观后回德里途中  中印两国总理欢庆新年

1957-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    在参观后回德里途中
    中印两国总理欢庆新年
    新华社新德里1日电　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理同尼赫鲁总理在旅途的火车里欢庆1957年新年。
    在参观了巴克拉—南加尔水闸工程返回德里途中的专车里，他们共度除夕，迎接新岁。
    餐车里布置得灯烛辉煌。周总理和尼赫鲁总理共同切开了新年蛋糕，宾主们在一起尽情玩乐，互相祝贺新年并且为世界和平而祝福。
    随同进行采访的中国、印度以及英、美、法等国记者也参加了这个小型的庆祝新年晚会。
