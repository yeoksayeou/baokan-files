Title: Premier Zhou Arrives in New Delhi and Holds Talks with Prime Minister Nehru
Author:
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage arrived in New Delhi today by plane from Afghanistan after concluding their visits to the Soviet Union, Poland, Hungary, and Afghanistan. Premier Zhou Enlai and his party will fly to Kathmandu tomorrow at the invitation of the Nepalese government for a visit.

Prime Minister Nehru warmly shook hands with Premier Zhou Enlai at the airport and exchanged greetings.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long reviewed the guard of honor and shook hands with the welcomers one by one. Also present at the airport to welcome them were the chiefs of staff of the Indian Army, Navy, and Air Force, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Chanda and other senior Indian government officials, diplomatic envoys from various countries to India, and Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to India, and all the officials of the embassy.

Premier Zhou Enlai spoke to a large group of Indian and other foreign journalists surrounding him at the airport. He told the reporters, "The situation in Budapest is very stable and the order is good. During our visit there, we could go out freely for sightseeing and shopping, even at night."

A reporter holding a newspaper containing Premier Zhou's speech in Moscow asked: "You said that a collective security system should be used to replace the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization and the Baghdad Pact, is that correct?"

Premier Zhou Enlai replied that yes, the Chinese government has proposed it many times. The Soviet government also supported it in the Sino-Soviet joint statement. Both of us advocate collective peace and oppose hostile military blocs.

Premier Zhou Enlai also told reporters that this collective security system was first proposed by Prime Minister Nehru, and "we are publicizing and promoting it."

A reporter asked: "The relationship between socialist countries is mutual respect for sovereignty and equality. Could you elaborate on that?"

Premier Zhou Enlai said: "There are no conflicting interests between socialist countries. We help each other to seek economic, cultural development and prosperity. We will not harm each other but unite with each other." A reporter then asked: "Including Yugoslavia?" Premier Zhou Enlai replied: "Yes."

In response to the question of "Did your visit help adjust the relationship between the Soviet Union and Eastern European countries?" Premier Zhou Enlai said: "Our relationship has been further improved and consolidated."

A reporter asked him if he would discuss these issues with Prime Minister Nehru. Premier Zhou Enlai smiled and said, "Old friends will talk about everything when they meet."

Premier Zhou Enlai was then accompanied by Prime Minister Nehru to the Indian Presidential Palace, where he will reside.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru held talks at the Indian Presidential Palace this afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, Kandahar, January 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai said this morning in Kandahar, an important city in southern Afghanistan, that he will bring the goodwill of the citizens of Kandahar to the Chinese people.

Premier Zhou Enlai made these remarks at the airport before leaving Kandahar. At the end of his five-day visit to Afghanistan, he once again expressed his gratitude to the Afghan government and people for inviting him to visit, and wished the Afghan people prosperity.

The Governor of Kandahar Province, Abdul Gani, and other local senior officials were at the airport to see him off.

### 周总理到达新德里同尼赫鲁总理进行了会谈

1957-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理到达新德里同尼赫鲁总理进行了会谈
    新华社新德里24日电　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和随行人员在结束了对苏联、波兰、匈牙利和阿富汗的访问之后，今天从阿富汗乘飞机到达新德里。周恩来总理一行应尼泊尔政府的邀请，将在明天飞往加德满都访问。
    尼赫鲁总理在机场和周恩来总理热烈握手，互相问好。
    周恩来总理、贺龙副总理检阅了仪仗队，并且和欢迎者一一握手。到机场欢迎的还有印度陆海空军参谋长、外交部副部长钱达和其他印度政府高级官员，各国驻印度外交使节，以及中国驻印度大使潘自力和大使馆全体官员。
    周恩来总理在机场对一大群包围着他的印度和其他国家的记者们谈了话。他向记者们说，“布达佩斯的情况很安定，秩序很好，我们在那里访问时，可以随便出去游玩买东西，甚至在夜间。”
    有一个记者手里拿着刊载有周总理在莫斯科的讲话的报纸问道：你说要用集体安全体系来代替东南亚条约组织和巴格达条约，是吗？
    周恩来总理回答说，是的，中国政府已经提过多次了。苏联政府在中苏联合声明中也加以支持，我们双方都主张集体和平，不赞成敌对性的军事集团。
    周恩来总理还告诉记者们说，这个集体安全体系是尼赫鲁总理首倡的，“我们是宣传而推广它”。
    有记者问：社会主义国家之间的关系是互相尊重主权和平等，可否请你申述一下？
    周恩来总理说：社会主义国家相互之间没有利害冲突。我们是互相帮助求得经济文化的发展繁荣。不会互相损害而要彼此团结。有记者又问：包括南斯拉夫吗？周恩来总理回答说：是的。
    周恩来总理在答复“你的访问是否帮助调整了苏联和东欧国家的关系？”的问题时说：“我们的关系是更加改善而且巩固了。”
    有记者问他，是否将和尼赫鲁总理谈这些问题。周恩来总理笑着说，“老朋友见面什么都要谈的”。
    周恩来总理接着由尼赫鲁总理陪同坐车前往印度总统府，他将住在那里。
    新华社新德里24日电　周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理今天下午在印度总统府进行了会谈。
    据新华社坎大哈24日电　周恩来总理今天上午在阿富汗南部重要城市坎大哈说，他将把坎大哈公民的善意带给中国人民。
    周恩来总理是在离开坎大哈之前在机场上说这些话的。他在结束在阿富汗的为期五天的访问的时候，再次表示感谢阿富汗政府和人民邀请他来访问，并且祝阿富汗人民繁荣。
    到机场欢送的有坎大哈省省长阿卜杜加尼和当地其他高级官员。
