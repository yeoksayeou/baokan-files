Title: Expansion of Xiaguan Power Plant (Picture)
Author: Qu Chaohong
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 2

    Expansion of Xiaguan Power Plant

    The expansion project of the Xiaguan Power Plant in Nanjing is undergoing winter construction. This expansion project is expected to be completed by the end of February 1958, at which time the plant's power generation will increase by about one time compared to now. This is the construction of the box-type raft foundation of the main plant building. 　　
    Photo by Qu Chaohong (Nanjing Daily)

### 扩建下关电厂（图片）
曲超宏
1957-01-16
第2版()
专栏：

    扩建下关电厂
    南京下关电厂扩建工程正在进行冬季施工。这个扩建工程预计在1958年2月底全部完工，到那时这个厂的发电量将比现在增长一倍左右。这是主厂房箱型底板基础工程正在施工。	　　曲超宏摄（南京日报社稿）
