Title: Tevosyan Appointed Soviet Ambassador to Japan
Author:
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th - TASS reported from Moscow on the 30th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed Ivan Fyodorovich Tevosyan as the Soviet Union's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Japan.

Because of Tevosyan's appointment as Soviet Ambassador to Japan, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has relieved Tevosyan of his duties as Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

### 捷沃西安任苏驻日大使

1957-01-01
第5版()
专栏：

    捷沃西安任苏驻日大使
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科30日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命伊凡·费奥多罗维奇·捷沃西安为苏联驻日本特命全权大使。
    由于捷沃西安被任命为苏联驻日本大使，苏联最高苏维埃主席团解除了捷沃西安所担任的苏联部长会议副主席的职务。
