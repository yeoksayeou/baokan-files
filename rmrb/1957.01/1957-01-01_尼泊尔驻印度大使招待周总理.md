Title: Nepalese Ambassador to India Hosts Premier Zhou

Author:
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 31st: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long, accompanied by Indian Prime Minister Nehru and Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru, left New Delhi at 10:00 PM on the 30th by special train to visit the Bhakra Nangal Dam.

Before their departure, the Nepalese Ambassador to India, Daman Shamsher Jung Bahadur Rana, hosted a reception that evening for Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage. Those attending the reception included Prime Minister Nehru and his daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, Defense Minister Katju, other Indian government officials, and prominent figures from society. Also present at the reception were Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili and foreign diplomatic envoys in New Delhi. Bainqen Erdini also attended the reception.

### 尼泊尔驻印度大使招待周总理

1957-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔驻印度大使招待周总理
    新华社新德里31日电　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理在印度总理尼赫鲁和印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁的陪同下，在30日晚间十点钟乘专车离开新德里前往钱地迦，参观巴克拉南加尔水闸。
    在他们出发以前，尼泊尔驻印度大使达曼·沙姆谢尔·忠格·巴哈杜尔·拉纳在这天晚上为周恩来总理，贺龙副总理和随行人员举行了招待会。出席招待会的有尼赫鲁总理和他的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人、国防部长卡特朱以及印度政府的其他官员们和社会著名人士。出席招待会的还有中国驻印度大使潘自力和在新德里的各国外交使节。班禅额尔德尼也出席了招待会。
