Title: East Germany Building Atomic Reactor
Author:
Date: 1957-01-04
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 2nd. The construction of East Germany's first atomic reactor and cyclotron building has been completed, and the installation of all its equipment and instruments will be completed within a few months.
    This news was announced by Professor Barwich, Director of the Central Institute of Nuclear Physics of East Germany, in a New Year's Eve interview with reporters. With the completion of this project, East Germany is scheduled to begin manufacturing radioactive isotopes and begin scientific research in neutron physics and atomic pile physics in 1957. The completion of this project marks the first step towards the task of building Germany's first nuclear power plant with a generating capacity of 70,000 kilowatts before 1960.
    Professor Barwich also said that, according to the technical agreement concluded between East Germany and the Soviet Union, East German designers and scientists have already begun technical design and experimental work with the help of Soviet experts. An East German scientific working group will soon go to the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research in the Soviet Atomic City to exchange views with scientists from fraternal countries in order to strive for the early completion of East Germany's first nuclear power plant.

### 民主德国建设原子反应堆

1957-01-04
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国建设原子反应堆
    新华社柏林2日电　民主德国装设第一个原子反应堆和回旋加速器的建筑已经建成，它的全部设备和器械的安装工作将在几个月之内完工。
    这个消息是民主德国核子物理学中央研究所所长巴尔维希教授在除夕对记者的谈话中宣布的。由于这个工程的完成，民主德国订于1957年内开始制造放射性同位素和开始中子物理学和原子堆物理学的科学研究工作。这个工程的完成使得在1960年以前建成德国第一座发电能力达七万瓩的原子能发电站的任务走完了第一步。
    巴尔维希教授还说，根据民主德国和苏联缔结的技术协定，民主德国的设计师和科学家已经在苏联专家的帮助下开始了技术设计和实验工作。民主德国的一个科学工作组即将到苏联原子城联合核子研究所去同兄弟国家的科学家们交换意见，以便争取使民主德国的第一座原子能发电站提前建成。
