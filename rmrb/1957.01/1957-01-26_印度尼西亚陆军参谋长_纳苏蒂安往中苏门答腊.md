Title: Indonesian Army Chief of Staff Nasution Goes to Central Sumatra
Author:
Date: 1957-01-26
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 24th: Army Chief of Staff Nasution flew to Central Sumatra this morning. Antara News Agency believes that the purpose of his trip is to "comprehensively resolve the military problems in Sumatra."

The report said that after Nasution arrives in Central Sumatra, he will meet with Lieutenant Colonel Hussein of the "Bull Division Committee" and then inspect the First and Second Military Regions and the Aceh Military Command.

A delegation led by Minister of Agriculture Karim has already begun talks with the "Bull Division Committee" yesterday. This delegation was sent to try to resolve the issue of Central Sumatra's secession from the central government.

### 印度尼西亚陆军参谋长  纳苏蒂安往中苏门答腊

1957-01-26
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚陆军参谋长
    纳苏蒂安往中苏门答腊
    据新华社雅加达24日电　陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安已经在今天上午坐飞机去中苏门答腊。安塔拉通讯社认为他此行是为了“全面解决苏门答腊的军事问题”。
    消息说，纳苏蒂安到达中苏门答腊以后，将同“雄牛师委员会”的侯赛尼中校会谈，然后巡视第一、第二军区和亚齐军事司令部。
    由政府农业部长卡里姆率领的代表团已经在昨天开始同“雄牛师委员会”会谈。这个代表团被派去设法解决中苏门答腊脱离中央的问题。
