Title: Hunters of Changbai Mountain
Author: Ma Hongpei, Lu Yafeng
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 3

    Hunting Activities

    Hunters of Changbai Mountain
    The hunting team, composed of eleven hunters including Luo Zhongxin and Cai Juzhe, members of the Manjiang Town Agricultural Cooperative at the foot of Changbai Mountain, bagged twenty-five wild animals in just a few days. Among them were two tigers, six black bears, and seventeen wild boars. The largest tiger weighed 223 jin (approximately 246 pounds) and sold for 845.17 yuan. The twenty-five wild animals sold for a total of 2,542.90 yuan, greatly increasing the income of the cooperative members.
    Before the new year, people from the Jilin Provincial Museum came to purchase wild animals, and the hunters accepted this new task and entered the mountains again.
              (Ma Hongpei, Lu Yafeng)

### 长白山的猎手
马洪佩  路雅峰
1957-01-05
第3版()
专栏：打猎活动

    长白山的猎手
    长白山下的漫江镇农业社的社员罗忠信、蔡巨哲等十一名猎手所组成的打猎队，在短短的几天中，就捕打了二十五只野兽。其中，老虎两只、黑熊六只、野猪十七只。最大的一只老虎，体重有二百二十三斤，卖了八百四十五元一角七分。二十五只野兽，共卖了两千五百四十二元九角钱，大大地增加了社员的收入。
    年前，吉林省博物馆来人购买野兽，他们又接受了这个新的任务，再次入山了。
              （马洪佩、路雅峰）
