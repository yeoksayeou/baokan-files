Title: Semiconductor Expert Lin Lanying Returns to Motherland After Studying Abroad in the US
Author:
Date: 1957-01-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 30th. Lin Lanying (female), who went to the United States for further study in 1948, returned to Guangzhou last night. Lin Lanying studied in the Physics Department of the University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia, USA, and she is a semiconductor researcher. She obtained her Ph.D. in 1955 and later worked as a research engineer at Sylvania Electric Products Inc. in the United States.
    When the reporter interviewed her, there was no trace of fatigue from the long journey on her face. Obviously, she was excited about returning to her motherland. From her conversation, it was learned that she had made up her mind a long time ago to return and serve the motherland after acquiring useful knowledge.

### 留美半导体专家林兰英返回祖国

1957-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    留美半导体专家林兰英返回祖国
    新华社广州30日电　1948年去美国留学的林兰英（女），昨天晚上回到广州。林兰英曾在美国费城的宾夕法尼亚大学物理系求学，她是研究半导体的。1955年获得博士学位，后来在美国西万尼亚电器公司任研究工程师。
    记者访问她时，她的脸上没有一点长途旅行后的倦容，显然她是为回到了祖国而兴奋。从她的谈话中得知，她很早就下了决心，要在学到有用的知识后回来为祖国服务。
