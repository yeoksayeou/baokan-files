Title: Fissures Emerge in Pakistan People's League; Secretary-General Expelled from Party
Author:
Date: 1957-01-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 17th - According to newspaper reports, Pakistan People's League leader Suhrawardy will convene a meeting here on January 25th with his key aides to discuss the situation arising from internal divisions within the West Pakistan People's League.

The Pakistan People's League Working Committee, at a meeting held on January 8th under the chairmanship of Suhrawardy, decided to expel Secretary-General Usmani from the party for opposing Suhrawardy's foreign policy.

Usmani later issued a statement saying, "I oppose Suhrawardy's foreign policy, and I feel that in doing so, I am expressing the will of the elected bodies of the East and West Pakistan People's League."

### 巴人民联盟发生分歧  总书记被开除出党

1957-01-18
第6版()
专栏：

    巴人民联盟发生分歧
    总书记被开除出党
    据新华社卡拉奇17日电　据报纸报道，巴基斯坦人民联盟领袖苏拉瓦底将召集他的主要助手于1月25日在这里开会，讨论西巴基斯坦人民联盟内部分歧所引起的局势。
    巴基斯坦人民联盟工作委员会在1月8日在苏拉瓦底主持下召开会议，决定把反对苏拉瓦底外交政策的总书记乌斯马尼开除出党。
    乌斯马尼后来发表声明说：“我反对苏拉瓦底的外交政策，我觉得我这样做表达了东西巴基斯坦人民联盟的选出的机构的意志。”
