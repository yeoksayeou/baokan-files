Title: Soviet and Czech Delegations Begin Talks; Voroshilov Hosts Banquet for Czech Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 27th: According to TASS, the Soviet government delegation began talks with the Czechoslovak Republic delegation, which is in the Soviet Union for a state visit, at the Kremlin in Moscow on the morning of the 26th. The talks were filled with a friendly and sincere atmosphere.

During the talks, the two sides fully exchanged views on the issue of further developing the fraternal relations between the Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia, and also exchanged views on relevant issues of the current international situation and other issues of mutual concern. The two sides were in complete agreement and mutual understanding on all issues covered in the talks.

Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: According to TASS, the delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, headed by President Zapotocky, laid wreaths at the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleums on the morning of the 26th. One wreath read: "To Lenin, the genius teacher and great leader of the working people of the world," and another wreath read: "To Stalin, the great fighter for socialism."

Xinhua News Agency, January 27th: According to TASS, on January 26th, Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, hosted a breakfast banquet in the Grand Kremlin Palace to welcome the delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, headed by President Zapotocky, who is on a state visit to Moscow.

The Soviet side attending the banquet included: Comrades Bulganin, Mikoyan, Molotov, Saburov, Suslov, Khrushchev, Brezhnev, Shvernik, Aristov, Belyayev, Pospelov, and others.

During the banquet, Voroshilov, Zapotocky, Khrushchev, Bulganin, Novotny, and Siroky repeatedly and cordially toasted each other in a friendly manner.

The breakfast banquet was held in a fraternal and warm atmosphere. (Attached picture)

### 苏捷两国代表团开始会谈  伏罗希洛夫设宴招待捷代表团

1957-01-28
第5版()
专栏：

    苏捷两国代表团开始会谈
    伏罗希洛夫设宴招待捷代表团
    据新华社27日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府代表团同前来苏联作国事访问的捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团26日上午在莫斯科克里姆林宫开始会谈。会谈充满了友好和诚挚的气氛。
    会谈中双方就进一步发展苏联和捷克斯洛伐克之间的兄弟关系问题全面交换了意见，并且就目前国际局势有关的问题和双方关心的其他问题交换了意见。双方在会谈涉及的一切问题上都是完全一致和相互谅解的。
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：以萨波托斯基总统为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团在26日上午到列宁和斯大林陵墓献花圈。在一个花圈上写着：“献给全世界劳动人民的天才导师和伟大领袖列宁”，在另一个花圈上写着：“献给伟大的社会主义战士斯大林”。
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：1月26日，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫在大克里姆林宫设早宴，招待正在莫斯科进行国事访问的以萨波托斯基总统为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：布尔加宁、米高扬、莫洛托夫、萨布罗夫、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、勃列日涅夫、什维尔尼克、阿里斯托夫、别利亚耶夫、波斯伯洛夫等同志。
    席间，伏罗希洛夫、萨波托斯基、赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁、诺沃提尼、西罗基等多次亲切友好地互相干杯。
    早宴在兄弟的、热诚的气氛中进行。（附图片）
    苏捷两国代表团开始会谈。右起第一人是萨波托斯基，左起是伏罗希洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁。
