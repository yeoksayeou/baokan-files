Title: Sino-Yugoslavia Issue Trade Negotiation Communique: Total Imports and Exports This Year to Reach Seven Million Pounds

Author:
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 1

    Sino-Yugoslavia Issue Trade Negotiation Communique
    Total Imports and Exports This Year to Reach Seven Million Pounds
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th - The People's Republic of China and the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia issued an economic and trade negotiation communique:

    The People's Republic of China and the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia conducted negotiations in Beijing in December 1956. The Sino-Yugoslav Science and Technology Cooperation Committee also held a meeting at the same time, and on January 4, 1957, in Beijing, signed a protocol on extending the effective period of the trade agreement and payment agreement signed in Belgrade on February 17, 1956. The total value of imports and exports for both parties amounts to seven million pounds. Representing our side in signing was Kong Yuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China. Representing the Yugoslav side was Kiro Gligorov, Head of the Yugoslav Economic Delegation and Secretary-General for General Economic Affairs of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia.
    At the same time, Shen Hong, Chairman of the Chinese group of the Sino-Yugoslav Science and Technology Cooperation Committee, and Dura, Chairman of the Yugoslav group, signed the charter of the Sino-Yugoslav Science and Technology Cooperation Committee on behalf of their respective sides.
    Attending the signing ceremony on our side were Li Zheren, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and relevant responsible personnel. Attending the signing ceremony on the Yugoslav side were Vladimir Popovic, Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Jaica Genovic, Deputy Head of the Yugoslav Economic Delegation, and all members of the delegation.
    During the negotiation process, the Yugoslav Economic Delegation also visited many cities, factories, and enterprises in China, and held discussions with leaders from Chinese government economic, planning, industrial, and commercial departments, mutually introducing situations, exchanging experiences, and enhancing mutual understanding.

### 中南两国发表贸易谈判公报  今年双方进出口总额为七百万英镑

1957-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中南两国发表贸易谈判公报
    今年双方进出口总额为七百万英镑
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国和南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国经济贸易谈判公报：
    中华人民共和国和南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国于1956年12月间在北京进行了谈判，中南科学技术合作委员会也同时举行了会议，并于1957年1月4日在北京签订了关于延长1956年2月17日在贝尔格莱德签订的贸易协定和支付协定有效期限的议定书。双方进出口总额为七百万英镑。代表我方签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长孔原，代表南方签字的是南斯拉夫经济代表团团长、南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会一般经济事务秘书长基罗·格里戈罗夫。
    与此同时，中南科学技术合作委员会中国组主席沈鸿和南斯拉夫组主席杜拉代表双方签署了中南科学技术合作委员会章程。
    我方参加签字仪式的有中华人民共和国对外贸易部代理部长李哲人，外交部副部长姬鹏飞和有关的负责人员。南方参加签字仪式的有南斯拉夫驻中国大使尤拉地米尔·波波维奇和南斯拉夫经济代表团副团长雅依察·盖诺维奇及全体人员。
    在谈判过程中，南斯拉夫经济代表团还参观访问了中国许多城市工厂企业，并同中国政府经济、计划、工业和商业部门的领导人员进行了座谈，相互介绍情况交流经验，增进了彼此的了解。
