Title: Elections in Xiaxi Township (Photo)
Author: Sun Fubei
Date: 1957-01-14
Page: 4

    Elections in Xiaxi Township
    Top photo: The voter list in Xiaxi Township, Nanhai County, Guangdong Province, has just been posted and is attracting many viewers.
    Right photo: Ye Jinshui (left) and Ye Ruili, members of the Sanlian Agricultural Cooperative in Xiaxi Township, Nanhai County, are both exactly eighteen years old this year. They received their voter certificates for the first time and felt indescribably happy.
