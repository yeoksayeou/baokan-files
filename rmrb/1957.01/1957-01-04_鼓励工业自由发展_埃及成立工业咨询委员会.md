Title: Egypt Establishes Industrial Advisory Committee to Encourage Free Industrial Development
Author:
Date: 1957-01-04
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 3rd - The Egyptian government has newly established an Industrial Advisory Committee. This committee has been established to provide advice to the Ministry of Industry on issues related to the development of industry in Egypt.

The committee is composed of a total of seventy members, including six entrepreneurs. The well-known Egyptian capitalist, Ahmed Abboud, is also participating in this committee.

Associated Press reporter Welton Wayne believes that the establishment of the Industrial Advisory Committee is an effort by Egypt to encourage more freely operated enterprises in the industrial sector. It will have a profound impact on the country's economic development.

### 鼓励工业自由发展  埃及成立工业咨询委员会

1957-01-04
第6版()
专栏：

    鼓励工业自由发展
    埃及成立工业咨询委员会
    新华社3日讯　开罗消息：埃及政府新成立了一个工业咨询委员会。这个委员会是为了在埃及发展工业的问题上向工业部提出意见而设立的。
    这个委员会一共由七十个委员组成，其中包括六名企业家。埃及著名的资本家艾哈迈德·阿鲍德也参加了这个委员会。
    美联社记者韦尔顿·韦恩认为工业咨询委员会的成立是埃及在工业方面鼓励更加自由经营企业的一种努力。它对这个国家的经济的发展具有深远的影响。
