Title: Breaking Ice to Transport Timber (Picture)
Author: Sima, Wu Qijiang
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 2

    Breaking Ice to Transport Timber
    In order to guarantee the progress of large-scale industrial construction and ensure the timely supply of needed timber, the Second Engineering Bureau of the Lanzhou Engineering Corporation organized forces to break through the ice floes and transport timber from the upper reaches of the Yellow River.
