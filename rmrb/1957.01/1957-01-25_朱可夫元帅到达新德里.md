Title: Marshal Zhukov Arrives in New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 24th: At the invitation of the Indian government, Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov and his entourage arrived in New Delhi today at noon aboard a Tu-104 jet airliner for a visit to India.

	He was welcomed at the airport by Indian Defense Minister Katju, cabinet ministers, the chiefs of staff of the army, navy, and air force, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Chanda, and other senior officials. Soviet Ambassador to India Menshikov, Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to India also welcomed him at the airport.

	Katju welcomed Zhukov's visit, saying that this visit would further strengthen the relations between the two countries. Zhukov expressed the same sentiment and conveyed the sincere love and friendship of the Soviet people to the Indian people.

	Zhukov will participate in the celebrations of India's National Day. He will visit India for three weeks.

### 朱可夫元帅到达新德里

1957-01-25
第5版()
专栏：

    朱可夫元帅到达新德里
    新华社新德里24日电　应印度政府的邀请前来印度访问的苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅和随行人员，在今天中午乘图—一零四喷气式客机到达新德里。
    他在机场受到印度国防部长卡特朱、内阁部长们、海陆空三军参谋长、外交部副部长钱达和其他高级官员的欢迎。苏联驻印度大使曼希科夫、中国驻印度大使潘自力和各国驻印度外交使节也到机场欢迎。
    卡特朱对朱可夫的访问表示欢迎。他说这次访问将进一步加强两国的关系。朱可夫表示了同样的感情，并且向印度人民转达了苏联人民的真诚的热爱和友谊。
    朱可夫将参加印度国庆节的庆祝活动。他将在印度访问三星期。
