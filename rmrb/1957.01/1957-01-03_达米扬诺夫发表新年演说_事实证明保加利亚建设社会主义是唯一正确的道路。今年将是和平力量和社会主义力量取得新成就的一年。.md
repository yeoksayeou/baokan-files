Title: Damyanov Delivers New Year's Speech: Facts Prove that Bulgaria's Construction of Socialism is the Only Correct Path. This Year Will Be a Year of New Achievements for Peace and Socialist Forces.
Author:
Date: 1957-01-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, January 2nd. Bulgarian National Assembly Presidium Chairman Damyanov delivered a New Year's radio address to the Bulgarian people on January 1st, saying that in the past year of 1956, the Bulgarian people further strengthened their friendship and cooperation with the Soviet Union and other socialist countries, and achieved great new achievements in the development of industry, agriculture, science, culture, and education. All these achievements convincingly demonstrate that the path of building socialism chosen by the Bulgarian people is the only correct path.

When talking about the counter-revolutionary riots organized by international reactionaries in Hungary and the imperialist aggression against Egypt, Damyanov said that the imperialists' aggressive plans were shattered by the unbreakable unity and internationalist support of the socialist forces and the world's peace forces. The forces of peace are constantly growing and becoming increasingly consolidated. There is no doubt that in 1957, the forces of peace and socialist forces will achieve new successes, and international reactionaries will suffer new defeats.

### 达米扬诺夫发表新年演说  事实证明保加利亚建设社会主义是唯一正确的道路。今年将是和平力量和社会主义力量取得新成就的一年。

1957-01-03
第5版()
专栏：

    达米扬诺夫发表新年演说
    事实证明保加利亚建设社会主义是唯一正确的道路。今年将是和平力量和社会主义力量取得新成就的一年。
    据新华社索非亚2日电　保加利亚国民议会主席团主席达米扬诺夫1日向保加利亚人民发表新年广播演说说，在已经过去的1956年，保加利亚人民进一步加强同苏联和其他社会主义国家的友谊和合作，在发展工业、农业、科学、文化和教育方面获得了巨大的新成就。所有这些成就令人信服地表明，保加利亚人民选择的建设社会主义道路是唯一正确的道路。
    达米扬诺夫在谈到国际反动派在匈牙利组织的反革命暴乱和对埃及发动的帝国主义侵略的时候说，帝国主义者的侵略计划被社会主义力量和全世界和平力量的牢不可破的团结和国际主义支援所粉碎了。和平力量不断壮大而且日益巩固。毫无疑问，1957年和平力量和社会主义力量会取得新的成就，国际反动派会遭到新的失败。
