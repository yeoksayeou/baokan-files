Title: Our Military Delegation Visits Taunggyi. People of Shan State, Burma, Welcome Chinese Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 15th: The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation arrived in Taunggyi, the beautiful capital of Shan State, on the afternoon of the 11th, and thousands of residents came to warmly welcome the delegation.

Colonel Dun Swe hosted a banquet for the delegation in the evening, followed by a Shan State dance performance.

Marshal Ye Jianying and members of the delegation toured the famous Inle Lake on the morning of the 11th, which is the largest lake in the Union of Burma and one of the most beautiful.

On the same day, the delegation also attended a tea party held for them by the Overseas Chinese in Taunggyi.

### 我军事代表团访问东枝  缅甸掸邦人民欢迎中国客人

1957-01-16
第5版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团访问东枝
    缅甸掸邦人民欢迎中国客人
    据新华社仰光15日电　中国军事友好代表团在11日下午到达掸邦美丽的首府东枝，成千名居民前来热烈欢迎代表团。
    登盛上校在晚间设宴招待了代表团，随后是掸邦舞蹈表演。
    叶剑英元帅和代表团团员们在11日上午游览了著名的英莱湖，这是缅甸联邦最大的一个湖，也是最美丽的一个湖。
    同一天，代表团还出席了东枝华侨为他们举行的茶会。
