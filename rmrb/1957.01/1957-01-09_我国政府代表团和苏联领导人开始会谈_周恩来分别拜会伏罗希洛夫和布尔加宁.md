Title: Chinese Government Delegation and Soviet Leaders Begin Talks; Zhou Enlai Visits Voroshilov and Bulganin Separately
Author:
Date: 1957-01-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th: The Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China began talks with Soviet leaders at 10:30 this morning in the Kremlin. The Soviet side participating in the talks included: Bulganin, Mikoyan, Khrushchev, Yudin, Fedin. The Chinese side participating in the talks included: Zhou Enlai, He Long, Wang Jiaxiang, Liu Xiao.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th: Premier Zhou Enlai paid a visit to Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, at 10:00 this morning.

Present during the visit on the Soviet side were Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov and Gorkin, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

On the Chinese side were members of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China: Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Wang Jiaxiang, and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao.

They held a thirty-minute conversation in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th: Premier Zhou Enlai paid a visit to Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, at 9:30 this morning.

Present during the visit on the Soviet side were Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov and Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin.

On the Chinese side were members of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China: Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Wang Jiaxiang, and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao.

They held a thirty-minute conversation in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere. (With pictures)

January 7th, Premier Zhou Enlai led the Chinese government delegation to Moscow. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai taking a group photo with welcomers after getting off the plane.

The scene of the meeting between the Chinese government delegation and Soviet leaders.

### 我国政府代表团和苏联领导人开始会谈  周恩来分别拜会伏罗希洛夫和布尔加宁

1957-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府代表团和苏联领导人开始会谈
    周恩来分别拜会伏罗希洛夫和布尔加宁
    新华社莫斯科8日电　中华人民共和国政府代表团今天上午十时三十分在克里姆林宫和苏联领导人开始会谈。苏联方面参加会谈的是：布尔加宁、米高扬、赫鲁晓夫、尤金、费德林。中国方面参加会谈的是：周恩来、贺龙、王稼祥、刘晓。
    新华社莫斯科8日电　周恩来总理在今天上午十时拜会了苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫。
    拜会的时候在座的，苏联方面有苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫和苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书戈尔金。
    中国方面有中华人民共和国政府代表团团员：国务院副总理贺龙，外交部副部长王稼祥和中国驻苏联大使刘晓。
    他们在友好融洽的气氛中进行了三十分钟的谈话。
    新华社莫斯科8日电　周恩来总理在今天上午九时三十分拜会了苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁。
    拜会的时候在座的，苏联方面有苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫和苏联驻中国大使尤金。
    中国方面有中华人民共和国政府代表团团员：国务院副总理贺龙，外交部副部长王稼祥和中国驻苏联大使刘晓。
    他们在友好融洽的气氛中进行了三十分钟的谈话。（附图片）
