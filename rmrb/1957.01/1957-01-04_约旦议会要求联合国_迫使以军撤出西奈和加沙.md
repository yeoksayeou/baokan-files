Title: Jordanian Parliament Demands UN Force Israeli Troops to Withdraw from Sinai and Gaza
Author:
Date: 1957-01-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Amman, 3rd January: The Jordanian Parliament resolved on the 2nd to send telegrams to the heads of Arab states and UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, urging them to make every effort to make Israel withdraw from the Sinai Peninsula and the Gaza Strip.

### 约旦议会要求联合国  迫使以军撤出西奈和加沙

1957-01-04
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦议会要求联合国
    迫使以军撤出西奈和加沙
    新华社3日讯　安曼消息：约旦议会在2日决议打电报给阿拉伯国家首脑和联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，要求他们尽一切努力使以色列撤离西奈半岛和加沙地带。
