Title: East German Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 13th – The German Democratic Republic trade delegation, led by Keelber, Deputy Minister of Foreign and Domestic Trade of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Beijing on January 12th and 13th.
    Xie Xuegong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of our country, and others went to the airport to greet them.
    Gipner, the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 民主德国贸易代表团到京

1957-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国贸易代表团到京
    新华社13日讯　德意志民主共和国贸易代表团在德意志民主共和国对外与国内贸易部副部长凯尔伯率领下，已于1月12日和13日先后到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有我国对外贸易部副部长解学恭等。
    德意志民主共和国驻中国大使纪普纳也前往机场迎接。
