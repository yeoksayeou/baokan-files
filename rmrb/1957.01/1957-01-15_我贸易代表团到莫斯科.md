Title: Our Trade Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 14th: Li Zheren, head of the Chinese trade delegation to the Soviet Union and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, arrived in Moscow on the 13th. Members of the delegation had already arrived in Moscow earlier.

The trade delegation will conduct trade negotiations with the Soviet government for 1957 between China and the Soviet Union.

### 我贸易代表团到莫斯科

1957-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团到莫斯科
    据新华社莫斯科14日电　中国赴苏贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长李哲人13日到达莫斯科。代表团的团员已经先期到达莫斯科。
    贸易代表团将和苏联政府进行1957年中苏贸易谈判。
