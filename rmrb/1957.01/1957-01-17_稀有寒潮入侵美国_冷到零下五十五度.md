Title: Rare Cold Wave Hits the United States, Cold Reaches Minus Fifty-Five Degrees

Author:

Date: 1957-01-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 16th: A rare cold wave has invaded several states in the northeastern United States. The temperature in Boonville, central New York State, dropped to minus 55 degrees Fahrenheit on the 15th, setting a record for the state's lowest temperature. Albany, the capital of New York State, was minus 20 degrees, and Burlington, Vermont, was minus 30 degrees.

Due to the extreme cold, schools in several towns in New York State have been closed.

### 稀有寒潮入侵美国  冷到零下五十五度

1957-01-17
第6版()
专栏：

    稀有寒潮入侵美国
    冷到零下五十五度
    新华社16日讯　纽约消息：一股稀有的寒潮侵入了美国东北部的几个州，纽约州中部的蓬恩维尔的温度在15日下降到了华氏零下五十五度，创这个州最低气温的纪录。纽约州首府阿尔巴尼为零下二十度，沸蒙特州的柏林敦为零下三十度。
    由于酷寒，纽约州好几个城镇的学校都停了课。
