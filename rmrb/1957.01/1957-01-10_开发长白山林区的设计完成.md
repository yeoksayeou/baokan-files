Title: Design for Developing the Changbai Mountain Forest Area Completed
Author: Qin Qin
Date: 1957-01-10
Page: 2

    The overall development and utilization planning design for the Changbai Mountain Forest Area, China's future forest industrial base, was fully completed in November 1956. This forest area boasts a vast forest area of over two million hectares. It is rich in valuable construction materials such as Korean pine, fir, Sakhalin spruce, red pine, Manchurian ash, walnut, and maple.
    In order to systematically develop and utilize the resources of this forest area, the state organized four survey teams to conduct over 4,000 kilometers of field surveys starting in 1954. After eight months of meticulous planning and design work by the design institution's technical personnel, the "Changbai Mountain Forest Area Development and Utilization Planning Design Plan" was compiled by November 1956. This plan outlines the grand construction prospects for the Changbai Mountain Forest Area over the next few decades.
    The plan (draft) stipulates that a major railway line, more than 200 kilometers long, will be built through the entire Changbai Mountain Forest Area, passing through Linjiang, Jingyu, Fusong, Antu, and other counties, by the beginning of the Second Five-Year Plan.
    The plan (draft) stipulates that during the Second Five-Year Plan period, this forest area will successively build five large-scale timber production enterprises – forest industry bureaus, twelve modern timber processing, forest chemical, and mechanical repair factories, numerous mechanized logging sites, and forty-two forest railways and forest area highways with a total length of nearly two thousand kilometers. These large-scale basic construction projects will transform the Changbai Mountain Forest Area into a modern forest industrial base with a complete mechanized production system. This forest industrial base will accumulate hundreds of millions of yuan in construction funds for the country each year.
    In addition to the plan (draft) having been submitted to the state by relevant departments for approval, the Fifth Design Institute of the Ministry of Railways and the Design Company of the Jilin Forestry Industry Administration will organize large numbers of surveying and design personnel to conduct large railway fixed surveys and surveying and design work for newly built forest industrial enterprises, starting this year, to ensure the timely and correct implementation of various construction projects.
                          (Qin Qin)

### 开发长白山林区的设计完成
秦勤
1957-01-10
第2版()
专栏：

    开发长白山林区的设计完成
    本报讯　我国未来的森林工业基地——长白山林区的总体开发利用规划设计工作，已于1956年11月全部完成。这个林区拥有二百多万公顷的广阔的森林面积。这里盛产着红松、杉松、鱼鳞松、赤柏松、水曲柳、胡桃、色木等珍贵的建筑材料。
    国家为了有计划地开发和利用这个林区的资源，从1954年起就组织了四个勘察队进行了四千多公里的野外勘察。设计机构的技术人员经过八个月的细致的规划设计工作，到1956年11月份已编出“长白山林区开发利用规划设计方案”。这个方案规定了长白山林区今后几十年内的宏大的建设远景。
    这个方案（草案）中规定，第二个五年计划初，这个林区将建设一条通过临江、靖宇、抚松、安图等县贯穿整个长白山林区的长达二百余公里的大铁路。
    这个方案（草案）中规定，第二个五年计划期间，这个林区将陆续建设五个大型的木材生产企业——森林工业局、十二个现代化的木材加工、林产化学和机械检修工厂、许多个机械化伐木场、四十二条总长近两千公里的森林铁路和林区公路。这些规模宏大的基本建设项目，将使长白山林区建设成一个拥有完整的机械化生产体系的现代化的森林工业基地。这个森林工业基地每年将给国家积累数以亿计的建设资金。
    这个方案（草案）除已由有关部门报请国家审定外，铁道部第五设计院和吉林森林工业管理局设计公司将分别组织大批勘察设计力量，从今年开始进行大铁路定测和新建森林工业企业的勘察设计工作，以保证及时正确地进行各项建设。
                          （秦勤）
