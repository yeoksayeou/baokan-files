Title: Saving Timber (Picture)
Author: You Wungu
Date: 1957-01-14
Page: 2

Saving Timber
The Chengdu Timber Processing Factory of the Southwest Engineering Management Bureau of the Ministry of Construction Engineering has adopted various methods to improve timber utilization and save timber. This has resulted in a timber utilization rate of 69.81%, exceeding the national unified standard. The photo shows workers at the Chengdu Timber Processing Factory carrying picture frame backing boards made from small pieces of wood into the warehouse.
Xinhua News Agency reporter You Wungu

### 节约木材（图片）
游云谷
1957-01-14
第2版()
专栏：

    节约木材
    建筑工程部西南工程管理总局成都木材加工厂，采取各种办法来提高木材利用率和节约木材。使木材利用率达到69.81％，超过全国规定的统一指标。这是成都木材加工厂的工人在搬运小木块制成的灰条板入库。
    新华社记者  游云谷摄
