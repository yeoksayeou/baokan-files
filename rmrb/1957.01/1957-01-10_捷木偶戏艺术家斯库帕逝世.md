Title: Czech Puppet Artist Skupa Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1957-01-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 9th: The famous Czechoslovak puppet artist Professor Josef Skupa passed away in Prague on the morning of January 8th at the age of sixty-five.

This Czechoslovak People's Artist and Peace Prize laureate created the puppet characters Spejbl and Hurvínek, who were loved by children and the general public.

### 捷木偶戏艺术家斯库帕逝世

1957-01-10
第5版()
专栏：

    捷木偶戏艺术家斯库帕逝世
    据新华社布拉格9日电　捷克斯洛伐克著名的木偶戏艺术家约瑟夫·斯库帕教授8日上午在布拉格逝世，享年六十五岁。
    这位捷克斯洛伐克的人民艺术家和和平奖金获得者，曾经创造了受到儿童们和广大人民欢迎的木偶人物斯佩伊布尔和胡维?克等。
