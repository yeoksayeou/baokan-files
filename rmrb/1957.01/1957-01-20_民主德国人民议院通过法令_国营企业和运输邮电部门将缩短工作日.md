Title: People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic Passes Decree: State-Owned Enterprises and Transportation and Postal and Telecommunications Departments to Shorten Working Day
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-20
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 19th. The People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic finally passed the decree on shortening working hours on the 18th. Starting March 1st, state-owned enterprises, transportation, and postal and telecommunications departments of the German Democratic Republic will begin implementing a 45-hour work week. The current work week is 48 hours.

### 民主德国人民议院通过法令  国营企业和运输邮电部门将缩短工作日

1957-01-20
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国人民议院通过法令
    国营企业和运输邮电部门将缩短工作日
    新华社柏林19日电　民主德国人民议院18日最后通过了缩短工作日的法令。从3月1日起，民主德国国营企业、运输业和邮电部门将开始实行每周四十五小时工作制。现在是每周工作四十八小时。
