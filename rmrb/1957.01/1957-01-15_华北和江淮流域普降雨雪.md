Author: 
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 1

　　Widespread Rain and Snow in North China and the Yangtze-Huai River Basin
　　This newspaper reports: From January 12th to the 14th at 2 PM, widespread rain and snow fell in regions including North China and the Yangtze-Huai River Basin. The largest precipitation occurred in areas such as Hunan, Jiangxi, southern Anhui, southern Jiangsu, and northern Zhejiang. Rainfall in Changsha was 37 millimeters, Shaoyang 44 millimeters, Hengyang 27 millimeters, Nanchang 76 millimeters, Yichun 41 millimeters, Jiujiang 58 millimeters, Anqing 43 millimeters, Tunxi 52 millimeters, Wuhu 35 millimeters, Nanjing 30 millimeters, Zhenjiang 32 millimeters, Shanghai 41 millimeters, Hangzhou 53 millimeters, Xinyang 20 millimeters, Suiping 40 millimeters, Zhengzhou 16 millimeters, Hefei 16 millimeters, Bengbu 28 millimeters, Huaiyin 38 millimeters, Zhengyangguan 33 millimeters, Hankou 18 millimeters, and Yichang 15 millimeters. The snow depth in the Huai River Basin area has reached three to seven inches. In North China, the Shandong Peninsula has the thickest snow, also reaching three to seven inches. Shanxi and Hebei have snow depths of two to four inches. This rain and snow is the largest and most widespread since the beginning of winter, and it is very beneficial for the growth of crops.

### 华北和江淮流域普降雨雪

1957-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    华北和江淮流域普降雨雪
    本报讯　华北、江淮流域等地区，自1月12日到14日14点，普降雨雪。湖南、江西、安徽南部、江苏南部和浙江北部等地区降水量最大。长沙37公厘，邵阳44公厘，衡阳27公厘，南昌76公厘，宜春41公厘，九江58公厘，安庆43公厘，屯溪52公厘，芜湖35公厘，南京30公厘，镇江32公厘，上海41公厘，杭州53公厘，信阳20公厘，遂平40公厘，郑州16公厘，合肥16公厘，蚌埠28公厘，淮阴38公厘，正阳关33公厘，汉口18公厘，宜昌15公厘。淮河流域一带的积雪深度已达三寸到七寸不等，华北地区以山东半岛的积雪最厚，也达到三寸到七寸之间，山西和河北的积雪也有二寸到四寸之间。这一次的雨雪是入冬以来最大的一次，面积也最广，对农作物的生长很有益处。
