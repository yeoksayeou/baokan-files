Title: Chinese Self-Funded Tourists Visit and Tour Leningrad
Author:
Date: 1957-01-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st. The first group of Chinese tourists to the Soviet Union have already spent four days visiting and touring "Lenin City" (Leningrad). They visited the revolutionary historical sites and museums of Leningrad, toured the cruiser "Aurora" and the Workers' Cultural Palace, and paid close attention to visiting the industrial enterprises here. They visited the Kirov "Electrical Engineering" Equipment Factory with great interest; the products of this factory are well-known in China. They also met with Chinese interns interning in Leningrad factories.

This group of self-funded tourists arrived in Moscow on New Year's Eve. After celebrating the New Year with the residents of the Soviet capital, they have visited Stalingrad, Sochi, and Kiev. In these places, they were received with very warm and friendly hospitality by the Soviet people everywhere.

### 我国自费旅行者  到列宁格勒参观游览

1957-01-22
第5版()
专栏：

    我国自费旅行者
    到列宁格勒参观游览
    新华社莫斯科21日电　中国第一批到苏联的旅行者已经在“列宁城”（列宁格勒）参观游览了四天。他们参观了列宁格勒的革命历史古迹和博物馆，游览了“阿芙乐尔”号巡洋舰和工人文化宫，并且很注意参观这里的工业企业。他们以极大的兴趣参观了基洛夫“电力电工”器材工厂，这家工厂的产品在中国是很有名的。他们还会见了在列宁格勒工厂实习的中国实习生。
    这批自费旅行者是新年前夕到达莫斯科的。他们和苏联首都的居民一道迎接了新年以后，已经到斯大林格勒、索契和基辅作了参观游览。在这些地方，他们到处受到苏联人民非常殷勤和友好的接待。
