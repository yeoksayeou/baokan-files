Title: Winter Vegetables (Picture)
Author: Liu Ming
Date: 1957-01-07
Page: 3

    Winter Vegetables
    The Shangying Advanced Vegetable Production Cooperative in Tong County, Hebei Province, has expanded its greenhouses from twelve to fifty-four in order to expand winter vegetable production. The vegetables inside are growing well. The tomatoes are already ripe, and the members are picking them.
                                Photographed by Liu Ming (Forward Newspaper Agency Manuscript)

### 冬季蔬菜（图片）
刘明
1957-01-07
第3版()
专栏：

    冬季蔬菜
    河北省通州市上营先进蔬菜生产合作社，为了扩大冬季蔬菜生产，将温室由十二间扩大到五十四间。室内蔬菜生长良好。西红柿已经熟了，社员们正在采摘。
                                刘明摄（前进报社稿）
