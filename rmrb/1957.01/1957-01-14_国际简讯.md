International Briefs

    Chinese Acrobatic Troupe Leaves Luxembourg for Italy
    Luxembourg News: The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe concluded its performance tour in Luxembourg and departed by train for Italy on the evening of the 11th.

    Yugoslavia and Albania Sign 1957 Trade Protocol
    Yugoslavia and Albania signed a trade protocol for 1957 in Belgrade on the 11th.
    The protocol stipulates a total trade value of two million US dollars, an increase of 25% compared to last year.

    Yugoslavia's Population Exceeds Eighteen Million
    According to the Yugoslav News Agency, Yugoslavia's population is now 18,096,000. The annual population growth in Yugoslavia is between 270,000 and 300,000.

    Soviet Union Establishes Rare Element Research Institute
    The Soviet Academy of Sciences recently established a Rare Element Mineralogy, Geochemistry, and Crystallography Institute. The institute's task is to comprehensively study the distribution patterns of rare elements in the earth's crustal rocks, study their minerals and origins, and determine the factors governing the concentration levels of rare elements in industrial-grade deposits. The institute faces a huge practical goal: to do everything possible to promote the development of the mineral raw material base of the Soviet rare metal industry.

    Hungary Tests Establishment of Atomic Reactor
    According to the Budapest "Evening News," the experimental work for Hungary's planned first atomic reactor is currently underway in the Buda Mountains.

### 国际简讯

1957-01-14
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    我杂技团离卢森堡赴意大利
    卢森堡消息：中国杂技团结束了在卢森堡的访问演出，11日晚乘火车前往意大利。
    南阿签订1957年换货议定书
    南斯拉夫和阿尔巴尼亚11日在贝尔格莱德签订了1957年的换货议定书。
    议定书规定贸易总值为二百万美元，比去年增加25％。
    南斯拉夫人口有一千八百多万
    据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫的人口现在有一千八百零九万六千人。南斯拉夫人口每年增长的数目在二十七万到三十万之间。
    苏联成立稀有元素研究所
    苏联科学院最近成立了一个稀有元素矿物学、地球化学和结晶学研究所。研究所的任务是：全面研究地壳岩石中稀有元素分布的规律，研究它们的矿物和产地，以及决定工业性产地的稀有元素集中程度的因素。研究所面前摆着一个巨大的实践目标：尽一切力量促进苏联稀有金属工业的矿物原料基地的发展。
    匈牙利试验建立原子反应堆
    据布达佩斯“晚报”报道，匈牙利计划建立的第一个原子反应堆的试验工作目前正在布达山中进行。
