Datong Cement Plant Goes Into Trial Production
The Datong Cement Plant, built with the help of the German Democratic Republic, began trial production six months ahead of schedule. This cement plant is a modern factory with a high degree of mechanization in its production process. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Ruihua)

    Datong Cement Plant exterior view.
    Workers in the quarrying workshop are mining ore to supply the cement plant as raw material.
    Workers are adjusting the rotary kiln.
    German Democratic Republic expert Schilling (center) is explaining firing technology to worker Wu Qingya (right).
    The mixing tank in the raw material workshop, which can hold 2,500 cubic meters of slurry.
    A corner of the central control room.

    大同水泥厂试生产
    德意志民主共和国帮助我国建设的大同水泥厂，比原计划提前六个月开始试生产。这座水泥厂，是一座设备现代化、生产过程高度机械化的工厂。　新华社记者　张瑞华摄
