Title: Egypt Loses 500 Million Egyptian Pounds - British and French Aggressors Should Bear the Responsibility for Compensation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 22nd - According to a report by the Middle East News Agency on the 21st, Egypt lost 500 million Egyptian pounds (one Egyptian pound is equivalent to one pound six pence) due to the British and French aggression against Egypt.

The news agency said that Egypt will demand full compensation for this amount and does not object to a foreign expert committee investigating its demands.

The Middle East News Agency said that the value of British and French property in Egypt that has already been confiscated is 170 million Egyptian pounds.

### 埃及损失五亿埃镑  英法侵略者应负赔偿责任

1957-01-23
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及损失五亿埃镑
    英法侵略者应负赔偿责任
    新华社开罗22日电　据中东通讯社21日报道，由于英法对埃及的侵略，埃及损失了五亿埃镑（一埃镑折合一英镑六便士）。
    这家通讯社说，埃及将要求全部赔偿这个数目，并且不反对外国的专家委员会调查它的要求。
    中东通讯社说，已经没收的英法在埃及的财产价值一亿七千万埃镑。
