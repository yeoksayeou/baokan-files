Title: One Rice Ear with Five Hundred and Ninety-Two Grains
Author: Sun Dexian
Date: 1957-01-30
Page: 3

    One Rice Ear with Five Hundred and Ninety-Two Grains
    (Our dispatch) In the Nanhuxiang Agricultural Cooperative of Pinghe County, Fujian Province, last year there was a paddy field with a rice plant that was six chi two cun tall, one of whose ears had a total of 592 grains. This is a rare good rice variety. The Nanhuxiang Agricultural Cooperative plans to keep the grains from this ear as seeds for this year. (Sun Dexian)

### 一穗稻子五百九十二粒谷
孙德籼
1957-01-30
第3版()
专栏：

    一穗稻子五百九十二粒谷
    本报讯　福建省平和县南湖乡农业社，去年有一丘稻田里，有一株六尺二寸高的稻子，其中有一个稻穗共结了谷子五百九十二粒。这是一株稀有的稻谷良种。南湖乡农业社准备把这个稻穗的谷粒留作今年的种子。　　　　　（孙德籼）
