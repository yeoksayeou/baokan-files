Title: If the Fox Preaches (Albanian Proverb)
Author: Zhao Zhiqiang, Wang Sengdao
Date: 1957-01-10
Page: 8

    If the Fox Preaches
    (Albanian Proverb) A fruit tree does not only bear one fruit; a person should not only have one friend.
    * When sheepdogs fight, the wolf will seize the opportunity.
    * Where the sunlight doesn't reach, the doctor needs to visit often. If the fox preaches, the rooster should think deeply.
        Selected and translated by Zhao Zhiqiang and Wang Sengdao

### 要是狐狸演说（阿尔巴尼亚谚语）
赵志强  王僧道
1957-01-10
第8版()
专栏：

    要是狐狸演说
    （阿尔巴尼亚谚语）果树不是只结一个果，人不该只有一个朋友。
    ＊护羊犬相咬，狼要钻空子。
    ＊阳光射不到的地方，医生需要常去。要是狐狸演说，公鸡就要深思。
        赵志强，王僧道选译
