The Chinese Academy of Sciences will award Science Medals, Certificates of Merit, and Bonuses to the authors of the above scientific research works. The bonus is 10,000 yuan for the first prize, 5,000 yuan for the second prize, and 2,000 yuan for the third prize. The awarding of medals and certificates of merit and the distribution of bonuses for collective work will be handled in accordance with Article 4 of the "Provisional Regulations on Science Awards of the Chinese Academy of Sciences." Written review opinions on the awarded works will be published in "Science Bulletin."

President Guo Moruo January 24, 1957
