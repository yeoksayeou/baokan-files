Title: U.S. Attempting to Delay Arab Liberation; Syrian Foreign Minister Criticizes "Eisenhower Doctrine"
Author:
Date: 1957-01-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 18th. Dacca dispatch: Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar said in Dacca on the 16th that the recent U.S. advocacy of a "vacuum" theory in the Middle East "is a means of imposing foreign military alliances, an attempt to delay the liberation of the Arab motherland."

The Syrian Foreign Minister, when discussing Eisenhower's Middle East plan, said: "Our attitude towards any proposal can only be determined based on our policy of not participating in any alliances." Bitar also believes that the policy of not joining any groups is the only way to expand the area of security and peace in cooperation with other countries in the world.

He said at a press conference that Arab countries must conduct serious research on the Eisenhower Doctrine in order to make decisions on a consistent basis.

Bitar pointed out: "Currently, a distorted propaganda campaign is being launched against Arab countries across the board. The main purpose of this campaign is to make us abandon the policies of active neutrality and non-alignment."

### 美国企图拖延阿拉伯解放  叙外长抨击“艾森豪威尔主义”

1957-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    美国企图拖延阿拉伯解放
    叙外长抨击“艾森豪威尔主义”
    新华社卡拉奇18日电　达卡消息：叙利亚外交部长比塔尔16日在达卡说，最近美国宣扬中东存在“真空”的理论“是一种企图把外国军事联盟强加于人的手段，是一种企图拖延阿拉伯祖国解放的手段”。
    叙利亚外交部长在谈到艾森豪威尔的中东计划时说：“我们对于任何建议的态度，只可能根据我们不参加任何联盟的政策来决定。”比塔尔还认为，不参加任何集团的政策是在同世界其他国家合作下扩大安全和和平地区的唯一方法。
    他在记者招待会上说，阿拉伯国家必须对艾森豪威尔主义进行认真的研究，以便在一致的基础上作出决定。
    比塔尔指出：“目前正在全面展开一个针对阿拉伯国家的歪曲宣传运动。这个运动的主要目的是要使我们放弃积极中立和不参加联盟的政策。”
