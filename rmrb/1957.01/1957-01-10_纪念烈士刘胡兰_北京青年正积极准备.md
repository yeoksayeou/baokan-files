Title: Commemorating Martyr Liu Hulan: Beijing Youth Actively Preparing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 9th: Beijing youth and various relevant sectors are actively preparing to commemorate the tenth anniversary of Martyr Liu Hulan's sacrifice.

Many factories, schools, and organizations are preparing to hold report meetings, story meetings, and discussion forums around January 12th to commemorate Liu Hulan, and are organizing young people to read Liang Xing's "A Brief Biography of Liu Hulan" and related articles published in newspapers and periodicals. The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League issued a notice to all levels of the League organization on the 3rd, stating that when commemorating Martyr Liu Hulan, young people should be educated to learn her great spirit of loyalty to the people and unwavering determination, and inherit and carry forward the glorious revolutionary traditions of Liu Hulan and thousands of martyrs.

The China Youth Art Theater will stage the play "Liu Hulan" again on the evening of the 12th. The China Children's Publishing House will reprint 40,000 copies of Liang Xing's "Biography of Liu Hulan" (i.e., A Brief Biography of Liu Hulan) from the 11th to the 13th, with added illustrations. Artists such as Wang Chaowen are sculpting a statue of Martyr Liu Hulan for the Liu Hulan Martyrs' Cemetery.

On the 12th, Beijing youth will hold a meeting at the Tianqiao Theater to commemorate the tenth anniversary of Martyr Liu Hulan's sacrifice.

### 纪念烈士刘胡兰  北京青年正积极准备

1957-01-10
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念烈士刘胡兰
    北京青年正积极准备
    据新华社9日讯　北京市青年和各有关方面积极准备纪念刘胡兰烈士牺牲十周年。
    许多工厂、学校、机关的青年都准备在12日前后举办纪念刘胡兰的报告会、故事会、座谈会等，并组织青年阅读梁星著的“刘胡兰小传”以及报刊上陆续发表的有关文章。中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会在3日向各级团组织发出的通知说：在纪念刘胡兰烈士的时候，应教育青年学习她忠于人民、坚贞不屈的伟大精神，继承和发扬刘胡兰和千百万烈士们光荣的革命传统。
    中国青年艺术剧院将在12日晚再度上演话剧“刘胡兰”。中国少年儿童出版社在11日至13日将再版梁星著的“刘胡兰传”（即刘胡兰小传）四万册，并增加了插图。艺术家王朝闻等正在为刘胡兰烈士陵园塑造刘胡兰烈士像。
    12日，北京市青年将在天桥剧场举行纪念刘胡兰烈士牺牲十周年大会。
