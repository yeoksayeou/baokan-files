Title: Chinese National People's Congress Delegation Visits Leaders of the Tirana City People's Council

Author: 
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, January 14th. The Chinese National People's Congress delegation, headed by Peng Zhen, visited the Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Tirana City People's Council, I. Zelkuba, Vice-Chairman Ya. Hatip, and Za. Saraku on the afternoon of the 12th.

    Also present at the meeting was Dai. Moziming, Secretary of the Tirana City Committee of the Albanian Labor Party.

    Peng Zhen first expressed his gratitude for the welcome and reception received in Tirana and stated his belief that the delegation's visit would enhance understanding and friendship between the people of China and Albania. Peng Zhen finally raised his glass and proposed a toast to the prosperity of Beijing and Tirana.

    That morning, accompanied by Fe. Shehu, Vice-Chairman of the Albanian People's Assembly, the delegation visited the Stalin Textile Factory on the outskirts of Tirana. Liu Changsheng, a member of the delegation and Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, introduced the situation of the Chinese people building socialism at a welcoming rally of textile workers. His speech was repeatedly interrupted by the textile workers' applause and cheers.

    In the evening of that day, the delegation also visited the Albanian Film Studio and watched a movie there.

### 我全国人民代表大会代表团访地拉那市人民会议领导人

1957-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我全国人民代表大会代表团访地拉那市人民会议领导人
    新华社地拉那14日电　以彭真为首的中国全国人民代表大会代表团12日下午访问了地拉那市人民会议执行委员会主席伊·泽尔库巴、副主席雅·哈蒂普和扎·萨拉库。
    会见时，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党地拉那市委员会书记代·默齐明也在座。
    彭真首先感谢在地拉那所受到的欢迎和接待，并且表示相信代表团的访问将增进中阿两国人民的了解和友谊。彭真最后举起酒杯，建议为北京、地拉那两个城市的繁荣干杯。
    当天上午，代表团在阿尔巴尼亚人民议会副主席费·谢胡的陪同下，参观了在地拉那市郊的斯大林纺织工厂。代表团团员、中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜在纺织工人们的欢迎大会上介绍了中国人民建设社会主义的情况。他的讲话不断为纺织工人们的掌声和欢呼声所打断。
    在这一天的晚间，代表团还参观了阿尔巴尼亚电影制片厂，并且在那里观看了电影。
