Title: Vietnam's "People's Daily" Publishes "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat"
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 2nd. The Vietnamese Labor Party's official newspaper "People's Daily" published the full text of the People's Daily article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" on New Year's Day, 1957, occupying nearly three pages from the third to the fifth page.
　　
Hanoi's Chinese-language "Xin Yue Hua Bao" also published the full text of this article on December 31.

### 越南“人民报”刊载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”

1957-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    越南“人民报”刊载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”
    新华社河内2日电　越南劳动党机关报“人民报”在1957年元旦以第三版到第五版将近三版的篇幅，全文刊载了人民日报的“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文。
    河内中文“新越华报”在12月31日也全文刊载了这篇文章。
