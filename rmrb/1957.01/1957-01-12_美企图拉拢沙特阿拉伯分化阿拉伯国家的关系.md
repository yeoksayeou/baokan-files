Title: U.S. Attempts to Woo Saudi Arabia to Divide Relations Among Arab Countries
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, January 11th -- According to TASS, New York, January 10th: The "Washington Post and Times Herald" revealed that the American diplomatic authorities are attempting to use Saudi Arabian King Ibn Saud's visit to Washington at the end of this month to "obtain Saudi Arabia's cooperation with (America's) new military and economic plans for the Middle East."
　　The newspapers pointed out that the United States will make special efforts to separate Saudi Arabia from the Arab countries that refuse to cooperate with the United States. The newspapers openly advocate creating divisions among Arab countries, making them fight each other.
　　Robert, the diplomatic commentator for the "Washington Post," also emphasized that the invitation to Saudi Arabia "is an important step in the U.S. government's plan to isolate... Egypt, Syria, and Jordan."

### 美企图拉拢沙特阿拉伯分化阿拉伯国家的关系

1957-01-12
第6版()
专栏：

    美企图拉拢沙特阿拉伯分化阿拉伯国家的关系
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社纽约10日讯：“华盛顿邮报和时代先驱报”透露，美国的外交当局正企图利用沙特阿拉伯国王伊本·沙特在本月底访问华盛顿的机会，“来取得沙特阿拉伯同（美国的）中东新的军事，经济计划的合作”。
    报纸指出，美国将要特别努力去做的是把沙特阿拉伯从拒绝与美国合作的阿拉伯国家中分离出来。报纸公然主张在阿拉伯各国之间制造分裂，使它们自己打自己。
    “华盛顿邮报”的外交评论员罗伯茨也强调指出，对沙特的邀请“是美国政府孤立……埃及、叙利亚和约旦的打算中的一个重要步骤”。
