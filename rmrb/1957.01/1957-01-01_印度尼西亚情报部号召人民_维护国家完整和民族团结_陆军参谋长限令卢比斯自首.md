Title: Indonesian Intelligence Department Calls on People to Safeguard National Integrity and National Unity; Army Chief of Staff Orders Lubis to Surrender

Author:

Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 31st - The Indonesian Intelligence Department issued a statement on the evening of the 29th through Jakarta Radio, calling on the Indonesian people to safeguard national integrity and national unity, so as not to cause "interference from foreign parties who want to overthrow the Republic of Indonesia."

The announcement called on all Indonesian people to fully assist in implementing Sukarno's order issued on the 28th for the sake of the safety of the nation, especially the stability of the situation in the First Military District of North Sumatra. In this order, Sukarno ordered all officers and soldiers of the First Military District to take resolute measures against those who violate laws and discipline.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 31st - The "Weekly News" reported on the 30th that Indonesian Army Chief of Staff Nasution had ordered former Deputy Chief of Staff of the Army Lubis, who was wanted and in hiding, to surrender before January 1, 1957. This order was issued on the 27th.

According to Antara News Agency Medan, Indonesian First Military District Commander Ginting issued an order on the 29th, ordering all officers in the military district to return to their posts within seven days, and those who fail to do so will be subject to decisive measures in accordance with military law.

According to a report from the intelligence office of the First Military District, all officers of the First Military District have returned, except for Major Ricardo Siahaan, who is on the run with Simbolon. The Indonesian Army Intelligence Office also said that the number of people following Simbolon has been decreasing, and sixteen people have already returned. Simbolon's whereabouts are still unknown.

In Central Sumatra, Hussein, the commander of the Fourth Infantry Regiment who launched the coup, continues to resist the central government. He broadcast through the Bukittinggi radio station, announcing that Commander Ginting's order to the First Military District was invalid for the Fourth Infantry Regiment, and asked the officers and soldiers of the Fourth Infantry Regiment to "abide by the orders and regulations of the Fourth Infantry Regiment."

### 印度尼西亚情报部号召人民  维护国家完整和民族团结  陆军参谋长限令卢比斯自首

1957-01-01
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚情报部号召人民
    维护国家完整和民族团结
    陆军参谋长限令卢比斯自首
    新华社雅加达31日电　印度尼西亚情报部29日晚通过雅加达电台发表公告，号召印度尼西亚人民维护国家完整和民族团结，以免引起“那些要推翻印度尼西亚共和国的外国方面的干涉”。
    公告号召全印度尼西亚人民，为了国家民族的安全，特别是北苏门答腊第一军区情况的稳定，要全力协助贯彻苏加诺在28日发布的命令。在这个命令里，苏加诺命令第一军区的全体官兵对违法乱纪的人采取坚决措施。
    据新华社雅加达31日电　30日的“星期新闻”报道，印度尼西亚陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安已经限令因被通缉而藏匿起来的前陆军副参谋长卢比斯在1957年1月1日以前出来自首。这个命令是27日发出的。
    据安塔拉通讯社棉兰消息，印度尼西亚第一军区司令京廷在29日发出命令，限令所有这个军区内的军官在七天内各返岗位，过期不到的将根据军法采取断然措施。
    据第一军区情报处的报告，第一军区的军官都已经回来，只有里卡多·西亚哈恩少校跟随西姆波朗在逃。印度尼西亚陆军情报处也说，跟随西姆波朗的人已经日益减少，有十六个人已经回来。到现在为止，还不知道西姆波朗的下落。
    在中苏门答腊，发动政变的第四步兵团团长侯赛尼继续采取抗拒中央政府的态度。他通过武吉丁宜电台发表广播，宣布第一军区司令京廷的命令对第四步兵团无效，并且要第四步兵团的官兵“遵守第四步兵团的命令和条例”。
