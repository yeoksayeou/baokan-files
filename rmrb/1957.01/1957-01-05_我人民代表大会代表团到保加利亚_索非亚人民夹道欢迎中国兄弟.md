Title: Our People's Congress Delegation Arrives in Bulgaria, Sofia Residents Line the Streets to Welcome Chinese Brothers
Author:
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, January 4th. The Chinese National People's Congress delegation headed by Peng Zhen and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee delegation arrived in Sofia on a special train on the morning of January 3rd. Accompanying them were Klisurski, Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, Minchev, Secretary of the Presidium of the National Assembly, Dimov, member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and Popzlatijev, Vice-Chairman of the Executive Committee of the National Assembly, who had traveled on the special train to welcome the delegation. Zhou Zhu'an, the Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, who had greeted the delegation at the Ruse station on the Bulgarian border, also arrived in Sofia on the same train.

After alighting from the train at Sofia station, the delegation warmly shook hands with the people who had come to welcome them, including Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, Kozovski, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the National Assembly, Jankov and Treikov, First Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, Mikhailov, Chervenkov, and Lukanov, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, Prahov, Chairman of the Central Council of Bulgarian Trade Unions, and Staykov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party.

Tens of thousands of Sofia citizens gathered in the snow-covered square in front of the station to hold a welcoming ceremony. They waved bouquets and pine branches, shouting slogans such as "Welcome distinguished guests from great China!" and "Long live Sino-Bulgarian friendship!"

Kozovski, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Bulgarian National Assembly, delivered a speech at the welcoming ceremony. He said: "Although our two countries are far apart, we are closely advancing shoulder to shoulder in the common struggle for the happiness of our people and world peace. Kozovski concluded by asking the delegation to convey to the 600 million Chinese people that the Bulgarian people will always be their loyal allies and brothers.

Peng Zhen, the head of the delegation, began to speak amidst thunderous cheers and applause. He said that Bulgaria is a country with glorious revolutionary traditions. Your country is the homeland of Comrade Dimitrov, the great communist fighter who is well known and admired by the Chinese people. Peng Zhen said, "You have a lot of advanced experience in all aspects of socialist construction. Our delegation is here to learn from your advanced experience, to deepen mutual understanding between our two peoples, and to enhance our unity."

When Peng Zhen finished speaking, a long period of cheers and applause erupted in the square.

After the welcoming ceremony, the delegation, accompanied by their hosts, traveled by car through the ranks of Sofia citizens cheering along the two-kilometer route to the delegation's residence - the Vrana Palace, located in the forest about twelve kilometers from Sofia.

At 5:00 p.m. on January 2nd, when the delegation arrived at Ruse station on the Bulgarian border by special train from Romania, they were warmly welcomed by the leaders of the local authorities and more than 15,000 citizens. The Ruse Municipal People's Congress also presented the head and deputy head of the delegation with a portfolio of municipal construction pictures.

### 我人民代表大会代表团到保加利亚  索非亚人民夹道欢迎中国兄弟

1957-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我人民代表大会代表团到保加利亚
    索非亚人民夹道欢迎中国兄弟
    新华社索非亚4日电　以彭真为首的中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团在1月3日上午乘专车到达索非亚。陪同抵达的有随专车前往迎接代表团的保加利亚国民议会主席团副主席克利苏尔斯基、国民议会主席团秘书明切夫、保加利亚共产党中央委员会委员迪莫夫和国民议会执行委员会副主席波普兹拉捷夫。前往保罗边境卢赛市车站迎接代表团的中国驻保加利亚大使周竹安也同车抵达索非亚。
    在索非亚车站下车后，代表团同前来迎接的人们一一亲切地握手，他们当中有：保加利亚国民议会主席团主席达米扬诺夫，国民议会执行委员会主席科佐夫斯基，部长会议第一副主席詹科夫、特莱科夫，部长会议副主席米哈伊洛夫、契尔文科夫、卢卡诺夫，保加利亚工会中央理事会主席普拉霍夫，保共中央书记斯塔伊科夫等人。
    聚集在车站前面积雪的广场上的几万索非亚市民举行了欢迎会。他们挥动着花束和松枝，高呼“欢迎来自伟大中国的贵宾！”、“中保友谊万岁！”等口号。
    保加利亚国民议会执行委员会主席科佐夫斯基在欢迎会上致词。他说：“虽然我们两国相距很远，但是我们两国在争取我们人民的幸福和世界和平的共同斗争中却紧密地并肩前进。科佐夫斯基最后请代表团转告六亿中国人民，保加利亚人民永远是他们的一个忠实的同盟者和兄弟。
    代表团团长彭真在雷动的欢呼声和掌声中开始讲话。他说，保加利亚是一个有光荣革命传统的国家。你们的国家是我们中国人民很熟悉和很敬仰的伟大的共产主义战士——季米特洛夫同志的祖国。彭真说，“你们在社会主义建设的各方面已经有很多的先进经验，我们的代表团这次来访问，是为了学习你们的先进经验，是为了加深我们两国人民之间的相互了解和增进我们之间的团结。”
    彭真讲完话时，广场上响起长时间的欢呼声和鼓掌声。
    欢迎会结束后，代表团在主人陪同下乘车穿过约两公里长的夹道欢呼的索非亚市民的行列，前往代表团的住地——距索非亚市约十二公里的坐落在森林里的伏拉尼亚宫。
    在1月2日下午五时，代表团乘专车从罗马尼亚进入保加利亚国境的卢赛车站时，曾经受到地方当局的领导人和一万五千多市民的热烈欢迎。卢赛市人民会议还赠送给代表团团长和副团长各一份市政建设画册。
