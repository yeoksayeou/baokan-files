Title: Congratulations on the Ninth Anniversary of Myanmar's Independence
Author:
Date: 1957-01-04
Page: 1

Mao Zedong's Message to President Ba U of Burma:
To His Excellency Dr. Ba U, President of the Union of Burma:

    On the occasion of your country's National Day, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and, through you, to the people of your country. I wish the Union of Burma prosperity and strength, and the traditional friendship between China and Burma to grow day by day.
                                                    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                                    January 2, 1957

Zhou Enlai's Message to Prime Minister U Ba Swe of Burma:
To His Excellency U Ba Swe, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma:

    On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Government of the Union of Burma.

    Over the past year, the traditional friendship and cooperative relations between China and Burma have been consolidated and developed on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. Recently, the friendly visits and sincere talks between the leaders of China and Burma are of great significance for further enhancing the friendly relations between the two countries.

    I wish the Burmese people greater success in building their country and safeguarding Asian and world peace, and I wish the friendly cooperative relations between China and Burma new development.
                                          Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                          January 2, 1957

### 祝贺缅甸独立九周年

1957-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺缅甸独立九周年
    毛主席致巴宇总统电缅甸联邦总统巴宇博士阁下：
    欣逢贵国国庆，谨向阁下并通过阁下向贵国人民表示我和中国人民的热烈祝贺。祝缅甸联邦繁荣富强，祝中缅两国的传统友谊日益增长。　
                  中华人民共和国主席毛泽东　1957年1月2日
    周总理致吴巴瑞总理电缅甸联邦总理吴巴瑞阁下：
    值此缅甸联邦独立九周年之际，我谨向阁下和缅甸联邦政府致诚挚的祝贺。
    一年来，中缅两国间的传统友谊和友好合作的关系，在和平共处五项原则的基础上获得了巩固和发展。最近，中缅两国领导人员彼此进行的友好访问和诚恳的会谈，对进一步增进中缅两国的友好关系有重大意义。
    祝缅甸人民在建设自己的国家和维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中获得更大的成就，祝中缅两国间的友好合作关系获得新的发展。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来　1957年1月2日
