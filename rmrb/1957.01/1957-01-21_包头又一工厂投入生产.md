Title: Another Factory in Baotou Goes Into Production
Author:
Date: 1957-01-21
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Since the new year, another newly built factory in Baotou City, the Baotou Sewing Factory, has gone into production. This year it will sew 70,000 pieces of clothing for workers building the Baotou Iron and Steel Industrial Base.
    In the first quarter of this year, three more newly built factories in Baotou City, namely brick and tile, motor repair, and food factories, will be put into production or start trial production successively. These factories are all built to meet the needs of the construction of the Baotou Iron and Steel Complex and urban development, and their production capacity is very large. After the new food factory is put into production, it can produce more than 800,000 kilograms of pastries every year.
    In the past few years, nineteen newly built factories in Baotou City have been put into production. Most of these newly built factories produce building materials such as bricks, tiles, sand, gravel, and refractory materials. With the progress of the construction of the Baotou Iron and Steel Complex, the number of builders coming to Baotou from all over the country is increasing day by day. Some of the newly built factories were built to meet the needs of the builders for daily necessities.

### 包头又一工厂投入生产

1957-01-21
第2版()
专栏：

    包头又一工厂投入生产
    据新华社讯　新年以后，包头市又有一个新建的工厂包头缝纫厂投入生产。它今年将给建设包头钢铁工业基地的职工缝制七万件服装。
    在今年第一季度内，包头市还有新建的砖瓦、电机修理、食品等三座工厂将陆续投入生产或开始试生产。这些工厂都是为了满足包头钢铁联合企业建设和城市发展的需要而建设的，生产能力都很大。新建食品厂投入生产以后，每年可以生产八十多万公斤糕点。
    包头市在最近几年中已经有十九座新建的工厂投入生产。这些新建的工厂，大都是生产砖瓦、砂石、耐火材料等建筑器材的。随着包头钢铁联合企业建设工作的进展，由全国各地来到包头的建设者日益增多。新建的工厂中，有一部分是为了满足建设者们对生活用品的需要而兴建的。
