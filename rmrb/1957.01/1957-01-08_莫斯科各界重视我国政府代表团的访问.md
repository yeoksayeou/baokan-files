Title: Moscow Circles Attach Importance to Visit of Chinese Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 7th - The visit to the Soviet Union by the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Premier Zhou Enlai, has become the central focus of attention for all circles of the Moscow public in recent days.

Today, "Pravda" published half-length portraits of Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long on the front page, and introduced their resumes. At the top of the newspaper, in bold type, was printed: Today, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Comrade He Long, Vice Premier, members of the government delegation and accompanying personnel arrive in Moscow for a friendly visit, and printed in Chinese and Russian: Warm greetings to the representatives of the fraternal Chinese people!

"Pravda" also published an editorial today entitled: May the unbreakable friendship between the Soviet and Chinese people continue to develop and consolidate! In addition, "Pravda" also published an article written by Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov, "For the Great Ideal of Friendship." The article described the extensive cooperation and growing cultural exchanges between China and the Soviet Union.

In the past two days, other Soviet newspapers have also generally published news, dispatches and pictures introducing the life and struggle of the Chinese people. Radio stations have also increased radio programs about China. Moscow cinemas have also been showing many Chinese films these days, such as "Daughters of China", "Chicken Feather Letter", "Dragon Beard Ditch" and "Flowers of the Motherland".

### 莫斯科各界重视我国政府代表团的访问

1957-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各界重视我国政府代表团的访问
    新华社莫斯科7日电　由周恩来总理率领的中华人民共和国政府代表团到苏联的访问，成了莫斯科各界公众近几天来注意的中心。
    今天“真理报”在第一版刊登了周恩来总理和贺龙副总理的半身像片，并且介绍了他们的简历。在报头上还用黑体字印着：今天中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志、副总理贺龙同志、政府代表团团员和随行人员到达莫斯科进行友好访问，并用中、俄文印着：向兄弟般的中国人民的代表们致热烈的敬礼！
      “真理报”今天还发表了一篇社论，题目是：愿苏中两国人民牢不可破的友谊不断发展和巩固！此外，“真理报”还发表了苏联文化部长米哈伊洛夫所写的文章“为了友谊的伟大理想”。文章中叙述了中苏两国间广泛的合作和日益发展的文化交流。
    这两天，苏联的其他报纸也普遍发表了介绍中国人民生活和斗争的新闻、通讯和图片。广播电台也增加了有关中国的广播节目。莫斯科的电影院这几天还上映了“中华女儿”、“鸡毛信”、“龙须沟”、“祖国的花朵”等许多中国影片。
