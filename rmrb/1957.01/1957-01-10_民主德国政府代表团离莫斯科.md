Title: East German Government Delegation Leaves Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-01-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch from Moscow: According to TASS, the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl, left Moscow by train on the evening of the 8th.

The Soviet party and state leaders Bulganin, Mikoyan, Saburov, Suslov, Khrushchev, Shepilov, and others were at the station to see the delegation off.

Premier of the State Council of China, Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and other members of the delegation of the People's Republic of China were also at the station to see them off.

Before his departure, Grotewohl spoke over a loudspeaker. He expressed satisfaction with the talks between the two countries, saying that the talks and the agreements concluded have far-reaching political, economic, and cultural significance and will help expand and strengthen our friendly relations. He said that it is particularly important that we have a common understanding that the socialist camp must continue to be strengthened and consolidated. He said that the workers and peasants of the homeland of Marx and Engels will continue the great cause of building socialism in the spirit of Marxism-Leninism. The results of our talks will encourage them and give them significant assistance.

Grotewohl expressed his gratitude to the Soviet people. He said, we are especially grateful to the workers of Moscow. This city of world significance, with its rich traditions, today points out the way forward for mankind, and its workers have expressed very sincere and friendly feelings towards us.

### 民主德国政府代表团离莫斯科

1957-01-10
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府代表团离莫斯科
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社报道：以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团于8日晚乘火车离开莫斯科。
    到车站欢送代表团的有苏联党和国家领导人布尔加宁、米高扬、萨布罗夫、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、谢皮洛夫等。
    中国国务院总理周恩来、副总理贺龙和中华人民共和国政府代表团的其他团员们也到车站欢送。
    格罗提渥临行前在扩音器前讲了话。他对德苏两国的会谈表示满意，他说，这次会谈和缔结的协定具有远大的政治、经济和文化意义，有助于扩大和加强我们的友好关系。特别重要的是我们有这样一种共同的见解：还必须继续加强和巩固社会主义阵营。他说，马克思和恩格斯的祖国的工人和农民将本着马克思列宁主义精神继续进行建设社会主义的伟大事业。我们这次会谈的结果会鼓励他们，给他们以重大的援助。
    格罗提渥对苏联人民表示谢意。他说，我们特别感谢莫斯科的劳动者。这个有世界意义的、有丰富传统的城市今天指出了人类前进的道路，它的劳动者对我们表示了十分真挚和友好的感情。
