Title: Mongolia and East Germany Sign Barter and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: After negotiations, the government delegations of the Mongolian People's Republic and the German Democratic Republic signed a barter and payment agreement for 1957 in Ulan Bator on December 30.

The two governments negotiated and decided that Germany will assist Mongolia in developing a plan to expand the Mongolian printing plant, add two printing workshops, and help establish a geographic survey station.

### 蒙德签定换货和支付协定

1957-01-01
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙德签定换货和支付协定
    据新华社讯　蒙古人民共和国和德意志民主共和国政府代表团经过会谈后，12月30日在乌兰巴托签定了1957年两国换货和支付协定。
    两国政府会谈并决定，德国将帮助蒙古作出扩大蒙古印刷厂的计划，增设两个印刷车间，并帮助建立一个地理测量站。
