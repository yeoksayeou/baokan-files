Title: Press Communiqué on Talks Between Chinese and Soviet Government Delegations: Both Sides Exchange Views on Sino-Soviet Relations and the International Situation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 9th: The government delegations of the People's Republic of China and the Soviet government began talks on the 8th and issued the following communiqué:

On January 8th, negotiations between the Soviet government delegation and the government delegation of the People's Republic of China began in the Kremlin, Moscow.

The Soviet side participating in the negotiations included: Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Bulganin, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU Khrushchev, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Mikoyan, Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov, and Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin. Also present at the negotiations were Deputy Foreign Minister Fedin and Member of the Collegium of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ilyichev.

The Chinese side participating in the negotiations included: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Marshal He Long, Vice Foreign Minister Wang Jiaxiang, and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao.

The negotiations were conducted in a friendly and warm atmosphere. During the negotiations, views were exchanged on various aspects of the issue of further developing friendly and cooperative relations between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China. The participants in the negotiations also exchanged views on the current international situation and issues of common interest to both sides.

Both sides determined that the negotiations will continue.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 9th: The government delegations of the People's Republic of China and the Soviet government issued the following communiqué after holding talks on the 9th:

On January 9th, negotiations between the government delegation of the People's Republic of China and the Soviet government delegation continued in the Kremlin, Moscow.

The Chinese side participating in the talks included: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, Vice Foreign Minister Wang Jiaxiang, and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao.

The Soviet side participating in the talks included: Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Bulganin, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU Khrushchev, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Mikoyan, Foreign Minister Shepilov, and Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin.

Also present at the talks were: Deputy Foreign Minister Fedin, Member of the Collegium Ilyichev, and Director of the Far East Department Goudov.

Today's talks, like yesterday's, were conducted in a warm and friendly spirit. In today's talks, the two sides continued to exchange views on various aspects of the issue of further developing friendly and cooperative relations between the Soviet Union and China, and also exchanged views on issues of the current international situation.

The talks will continue.

### 中苏两国政府代表团发表会谈新闻公报  双方就中苏关系和国际局势交换了意见

1957-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏两国政府代表团发表会谈新闻公报
    双方就中苏关系和国际局势交换了意见
    新华社莫斯科9日电　中华人民共和国政府代表团和苏联政府代表团在8日开始会谈，发表公报如下：
    1月8日，在莫斯科克里姆林宫开始了苏联政府代表团和中华人民共和国政府代表团之间的谈判。
    苏联方面参加谈判的有苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁，苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员和苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫，苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬，苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫，苏联驻中国大使尤金。出席谈判的还有苏联外交部副部长费德林，外交部部务委员伊利切夫。
    中华人民共和国方面参加谈判的有国务院总理周恩来，副总理贺龙元帅，中国外交部副部长王稼祥，中国驻苏联大使刘晓。
    谈判在友好热忱的气氛中进行。在谈判中对进一步发展苏联和中华人民共和国之间的友好合作关系的问题多方面地交换了意见。谈判的参加者同时对现在国际局势以及双方共同感到兴趣的问题交换了意见。
    双方确定，谈判将继续进行。
    新华社莫斯科9日电　中华人民共和国政府代表团和苏联政府代表团9日举行会谈后发表公报如下：
    1月9日，在莫斯科克里姆林宫继续举行中华人民共和国政府代表团和苏联政府代表团之间的会谈。
    中国方面参加会谈的有：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，副总理贺龙，外交部副部长王稼祥，中国驻苏联大使刘晓。
    苏联方面参加会谈的有：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁，苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员、苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫，苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬，外交部长谢皮洛夫，苏联驻中国大使尤金。
    出席会谈的还有：苏联外交部副部长费德林，部务委员伊利切夫，远东司司长顾德夫。
    今天的会谈同昨天一样，是在热烈友好的精神下进行的。在今天的会谈中，双方继续对进一步发展有关苏中两国之间的友好合作关系的问题多方面交换了意见，还对现在国际局势的问题交换了意见。
    会谈还要继续进行。
