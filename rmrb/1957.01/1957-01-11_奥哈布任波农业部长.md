Title: Ochab Appointed Minister of Agriculture of Poland
Author:
Date: 1957-01-11
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 10th: According to a Polish News Agency report on the 9th, upon the recommendation of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers, the Polish State Council has appointed Ochab, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, as Minister of Agriculture. At the same time, the State Council relieved Kuligowski of his position as Minister of Agriculture.

### 奥哈布任波农业部长

1957-01-11
第5版()
专栏：

    奥哈布任波农业部长
    新华社华沙10日电　据波兰通讯社9日报道，根据部长会议主席的建议，波兰国务委员会已经任命波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员奥哈布为农业部部长。同时，国务委员会解除了库利戈夫斯基所担任的农业部长的职务。
