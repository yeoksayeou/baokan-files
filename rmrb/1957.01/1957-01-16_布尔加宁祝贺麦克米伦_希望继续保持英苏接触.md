Title: Bulganin Congratulates Macmillan; Hopes to Continue Anglo-Soviet Contact
Author:
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 14th: TASS reported from Moscow: To the Right Honourable Harold Macmillan, Prime Minister of Great Britain, London:

    Please accept my congratulations and best wishes on your assumption of the office of Prime Minister of Great Britain. I hope that the direct contacts we established in Geneva and London can be maintained, which would be beneficial to the development of Anglo-Soviet relations.

              N. Bulganin
              January 12, 1957
                        Moscow

### 布尔加宁祝贺麦克米伦  希望继续保持英苏接触

1957-01-16
第6版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁祝贺麦克米伦
    希望继续保持英苏接触
    新华社讯　塔斯社莫斯科14日电：伦敦英国首相哈罗德·麦克米伦先生：
    欣逢阁下出任英国首相，仅向您祝贺，并表示最好的愿望。我希望，我们在日内瓦和伦敦所建立的直接接触能够继续保持，这对发展英苏两国关系是有利的。
              布尔加宁
              1957年1月12日
                        莫斯科
