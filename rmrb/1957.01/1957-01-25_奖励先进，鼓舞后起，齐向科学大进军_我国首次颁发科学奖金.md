Title: Rewarding the Advanced, Encouraging the Rising, and Marching Towards a Great Leap in Science: China Issues Science Awards for the First Time
Author:
Date: 1957-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th: The winning works for the Chinese Academy of Sciences' 1956 Science Awards (Natural Sciences Section) have been selected. There are thirty-four winning research achievements, including three first prizes, five second prizes, and twenty-six third prizes. The authors of these winning research achievements include researchers from the Chinese Academy of Sciences, university teachers, and engineering and technical personnel from various departments. The winners will receive gold medals, certificates, and bonuses. The bonus for first prize is 10,000 yuan, second prize is 5,000 yuan, and third prize is 2,000 yuan.

### 奖励先进，鼓舞后起，齐向科学大进军  我国首次颁发科学奖金

1957-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    奖励先进，鼓舞后起，齐向科学大进军
    我国首次颁发科学奖金
    新华社24日讯　中国科学院1956年度科学奖金（自然科学部分）得奖论著已经评选出来。得奖的研究成果共有三十四项，其中一等奖三项，二等奖五项，三等奖二十六项。这些得奖研究成果的作者包括中国科学院的研究人员、高等学校教师和业务部门的工程技术人员。得奖人将获得金质奖章、奖状和奖金。奖金一等奖一万元，二等奖五千元，三等奖二千元。
