Title: Pakistani Prime Minister on Kashmir Issue
Author:
Date: 1957-01-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, January 17th: Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy said in Karachi on the 16th that if the Kashmir issue is vetoed in the United Nations Security Council, and if discussion of this issue is not allowed, Pakistan will raise the issue in the United Nations General Assembly in order to obtain its ruling.

### 巴总理谈克什米尔问题

1957-01-18
第6版()
专栏：

    巴总理谈克什米尔问题
    据新华社卡拉奇17日电　巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底16日在卡拉奇说，如果克什米尔问题在联合国安全理事会中遭到否决，如果对这个问题的讨论没有被允许，巴基斯坦就要向联合国大会提出这个问题，以便取得它的裁决。
