Title: Chinese Government Delegation Bids Farewell to Hungary
Author:
Date: 1957-01-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 17th: The Chinese government delegation headed by Premier Zhou Enlai, at the invitation of the Hungarian government, left Budapest by plane at 8:15 this morning after a one-day visit to Budapest.

Those seeing the delegation off at the airport included Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic; Kadar, Premier of the Revolutionary Workers and Peasants Government; Münnich, Deputy Premier; Marosan, Minister of State; Horváth, Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Also present at the airport to see them off were the diplomatic envoys of various fraternal countries to Hungary, as well as Hao Deqing, the Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, and personnel from the embassy.

When Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice-Premier He Long arrived at the airport, the band played the national anthems of Hungary and China.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice-Premier He Long, accompanied by Premier Kadar and Deputy Premier Münnich, reviewed the guard of honor.

Premier Zhou Enlai addressed the well-wishers (see page 5).

Following this, Premier Kadar delivered a farewell speech (see page 5).

Children from Budapest presented flowers to Premier Zhou Enlai and others.

### 我国政府代表团告别匈牙利

1957-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府代表团告别匈牙利
    新华社布达佩斯17日电　以周恩来总理为首的中国政府代表团应匈牙利政府的邀请在布达佩斯访问一天后，已经在今天上午八点一刻乘飞机离开布达佩斯。
    在机场上送行的有匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比，工农革命政府总理卡达尔，副总理明尼赫，国务部长马罗山，外交部长霍瓦特等。
    到机场送行的还有各兄弟国家驻匈牙利外交使节和中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青和大使馆人员。
    周恩来总理和贺龙副总理到达机场时，乐队奏起了匈中两国国歌。
    周恩来总理、贺龙副总理在卡达尔总理、明尼赫副总理陪同下检阅了仪仗队。
    周恩来总理向欢送者讲了话（见第五版）。
    接着，卡达尔总理致送别词（见第五版）。
    布达佩斯的少年儿童向周恩来总理等献了花。
