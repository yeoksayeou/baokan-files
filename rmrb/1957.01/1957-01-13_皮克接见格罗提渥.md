Title: Pieck Receives Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1957-01-13
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 12th – According to a report by the German News Agency: On the 11th, Democratic German President Pieck received the government delegation returning from its visit to the Soviet Union.
    Prime Minister Grotewohl, head of the delegation, reported to President Pieck on the course of the talks with the Soviet government representatives.

### 皮克接见格罗提渥

1957-01-13
第5版()
专栏：

    皮克接见格罗提渥
    新华社柏林12日电　据德意志通讯社报道：民主德国总统皮克11日接见了访问苏联回来的政府代表团。
    代表团团长格罗提渥总理向皮克总统报告了同苏联政府代表会谈的经过。
