Title: On the Calculation of Gross Industrial Output Value
Author:
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 2

Answering Readers' Questions

    On the Calculation of Gross Industrial Output Value
    Editor's Note: Recently, our newspaper has received letters from readers asking why some figures in the report on the proposals of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China for the Second Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy differ from the figures published in the past by the State Statistical Bureau's communiqués and the report by Vice Premier Li Fuchun on the First Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy, and whether there are any errors. Here is the reply from the Comprehensive Statistics Division of the State Statistical Bureau on this issue.
    The figures in the report on the proposals of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China for the Second Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy, the figures in the State Statistical Bureau's communiqués in the past, and the figures in Vice Premier Li Fuchun's report on the First Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy are all correct. The reason why some figures for gross industrial output value and growth rates are different is because the scope included in these figures is different. In Vice Premier Li Fuchun's report and the State Statistical Bureau's communiqués in the past, the gross industrial output value for 1952 was 27.01 billion yuan, and the increase in 1957 compared to 1952 was 98.3%. This refers to modern industry and handicraft industry, not including the output value of handicraft cooperatives, groups, and individual handicrafts. In the report on the proposals of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China for the Second Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy, the increase in gross industrial output value in 1957 compared to 1952 was 90.3%. This refers to the total gross industrial output value including handicraft cooperatives, groups, and individual handicrafts. Because since the high tide of socialist transformation last year, handicrafts have basically achieved cooperation, and need to be included in the planning; the targets of the Second Five-Year Plan also include this part.
    It is entirely necessary to adjust the scope of statistical figures along with the development and changes in the economic situation. Comprehensive Statistics Division, State Statistical Bureau

### 关于工业总产值的计算问题

1957-01-24
第2版()
专栏：答读者问

    关于工业总产值的计算问题
    编者按　最近，本报收到一些读者来信，询问中共第八次全国代表大会关于发展国民经济的第二个五年计划的建议的报告中的若干数字，为什么同过去国家统计局所发表的公报和李富春副总理关于发展国民经济的第一个五年计划的报告中的数字不同，是否有错误。这里发表的是国家统计局综合统计司对这个问题的答复。
    中共第八次全国代表大会关于发展国民经济的第二个五年计划的建议的报告中的数字，和过去国家统计局发表的公报中的数字、李富春副总理关于发展国民经济的第一个五年计划的报告中的数字，都是正确的。其中若干工业总产值和发展速度的数字所以不同，是因为这些数字所包括的范围不同。李富春副总理的报告和过去国家统计局的公报中，1952年工业总产值是270.1亿元，1957年较1952年增长98.3％，这是指现代工业和工场手工业，不包括手工业合作社、组和个体手工业的产值；中共第八次全国代表大会关于发展国民经济的第二个五年计划的建议的报告中，1957年较1952年工业总产值增长90.3％，这是指包括了手工业合作社、组和个体手工业在内的全部工业的总产值。因为从去年社会主义改造高潮以后，手工业已基本上实现了合作化，需要纳入计划轨道；第二个五年计划的指标中，也包括了这一部分。
    统计数字的范围，随着经济情况的发展和变化进行调整，是完全必要的。　国家统计局综合统计司
