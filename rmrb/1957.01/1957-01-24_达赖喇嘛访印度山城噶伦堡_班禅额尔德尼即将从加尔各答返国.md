Title: The Dalai Lama Visits the Indian Mountain Town of Kalimpong; Panchen Erdini to Return Home from Calcutta

Author:
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 23rd: The Dalai Lama and his entourage arrived in Kalimpong from Calcutta on the 22nd and will return home from there.

The Panchen Erdini and his entourage will return home by plane from Calcutta on the 25th.

Kalimpong is a mountain town in northern West Bengal with a population of 20,000. Mount Kangchenjunga, a Himalayan branch with snow-capped peaks all year round, is located north of Kalimpong.

The Dalai Lama was welcomed by representatives of the people of Kalimpong when he landed at Bagdogra by plane. He drove to Kalimpong and was greeted by a large crowd three miles from the town. They offered milk tea to him and his entourage according to religious etiquette.

In Kalimpong, thousands of people lined the streets to welcome the Dalai Lama. Mountain people performed lion and peacock dances on the lawn of the Dalai Lama's residence. Pilgrims from Chinese Tibet performed drum dances for the Dalai Lama.

### 达赖喇嘛访印度山城噶伦堡  班禅额尔德尼即将从加尔各答返国

1957-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛访印度山城噶伦堡
    班禅额尔德尼即将从加尔各答返国
    新华社新德里23日电　达赖喇嘛和他的随行人员22日从加尔各答到达噶伦堡，他们将从那里回国。
    班禅额尔德尼和他的随行人员将在25日从加尔各答乘飞机回国。
    噶伦堡是西孟加拉北部的一个山城，有二万人口。山顶终年积雪的喜马拉雅山支脉康章忠格山就在噶伦堡的北面。
    达赖喇嘛乘飞机在巴格多格拉降落时受到噶伦堡人民代表的欢迎。他乘汽车驶向噶伦堡，在离噶伦堡三英里的地方就受到大批人的欢迎。他们按照宗教礼节向他和他的随行人员献奶茶。
    在噶伦堡，成千的人排列在道路两旁欢迎达赖喇嘛。山民们在达赖喇嘛住所的草地上跳狮子舞和孔雀舞。从中国西藏来的香客为达赖喇嘛跳了打鼓舞。
