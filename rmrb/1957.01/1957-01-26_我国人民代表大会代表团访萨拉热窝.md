Title: Chinese National People's Congress Delegation Visits Sarajevo
Author:
Date: 1957-01-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Sarajevo, January 24th: The Chinese National People's Congress delegation, headed by Peng Zhen, and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee delegation visited the People's Republic of Bosnia and Herzegovina in central Yugoslavia on the 22nd and 23rd.

    In Sarajevo, the capital of this republic, the delegation was warmly welcomed by the Republic's National Assembly, the Executive Committee, the Sarajevo County and City People's Committees, and people from all walks of life. Here, the delegation visited the city appearance, the historical museum, the photo exhibition hall where Archduke of Austria was assassinated during the outbreak of the First World War, and a wood processing factory in groups.

    The delegation also held discussions in groups with leaders of the republic, county, and city on issues related to municipal administration, communes, and autonomous enterprise management.

    On the evening of the 22nd, Pucar, Chairman of the National Assembly of Bosnia and Herzegovina, hosted a banquet in the Republic's National Assembly to entertain the entire delegation. On the 23rd, the entire delegation visited the Zenica Iron and Steel Complex, about eighty kilometers from Sarajevo, and held discussions with the enterprise's leaders. Zenica is the iron and steel base of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

### 我国人民代表大会代表团访萨拉热窝

1957-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我国人民代表大会代表团访萨拉热窝
    新华社萨拉热窝24日电　以彭真为首的中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团在22日和23日访问了南斯拉夫中部的波斯尼亚—黑塞哥维那人民共和国。
    在这个共和国的首都萨拉热窝，代表团受到了共和国国民议会、执行委员会和萨拉热窝县、市人民委员会以及各界人士的热烈欢迎。在这里，代表团曾经分组参观了市容、历史博物馆、第一次世界大战爆发时奥皇太子被刺的图片展览馆和一个木材加工工厂。
    代表团还分组同共和国和县、市领导人员就有关市政、公社的问题和企业自治管理等问题举行了座谈。
    22日晚间，波斯尼亚—黑塞哥维那国民议会主席普察尔在共和国国民议会设宴招待了代表团全体人员。23日，代表团全体人员参观了距离萨拉热窝约八十公里的泽尼查钢铁联合企业，并且同企业的负责人举行了座谈。泽尼查是南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国的钢铁基地。
