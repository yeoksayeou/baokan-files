International Briefs

    U.S. Unjustly Sentences Browder to Imprisonment
    The U.S. Federal Court, on the 18th, unjustly sentenced Earl Browder, the leader of the American Communist Party who recently returned home, to two years in prison and a fine of one thousand dollars on the so-called charge of "illegally entering the United States."
    Browder stated in court that he would refuse to accept this unreasonable verdict.

    Speidel Appointed Commander-in-Chief of Central European Ground Forces
    According to authoritative sources in Paris: Former Nazi General Speidel has been appointed Commander-in-Chief of the Central European Ground Forces of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, replacing French General Guillaume.

    Another Example of Sycophancy from Syngman Rhee
    Syngman Rhee issued a statement on the 19th, asking the Seoul City Council and citizens to rename Seoul as "Handu" because "foreigners" find the name "Seoul" difficult to pronounce.
                  (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 国际简讯

1957-01-21
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    美国无理判处鲍达希徒刑
    美国联邦法院18日以所谓“非法进入美国”的罪名无理判处不久以前回国的美国共产党领袖欧文·鲍达希二年徒刑和一千美元罚金。
    鲍达希在法庭上表示，他将拒绝接受这项无理判决。斯派达尔任中欧地面部队总司令
    据巴黎权威人士透露：前纳粹将领斯派达尔已被任命为北大西洋公约组织中欧地面部队总司令，以代替法国的纪尧姆将军。
    李承晚媚外又一例
    李承晚在19日发表声明，要汉城市议会和市民把汉城改名为“韩都”，原因是“汉城”这个名字“外国人”不好念。
                  （据新华社讯）
