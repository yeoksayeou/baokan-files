Title: Yunnan Province Exceeds Cotton Procurement Target
Author: Huanran
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 3

    Yunnan Province Exceeds Cotton Procurement Target
    (Our newspaper, Kunming, January 1st) By mid-December 1956, Yunnan Province had exceeded its state cotton procurement plan, achieving a 92.85% increase compared to the same period in 1955. In 1956, Yunnan's cotton production achieved an unprecedented large increase, with total output increasing by 83.64% compared to 1955, exceeding the production target for the final year of the First Five-Year Plan. During the cotton picking and procurement process, due to strengthened technical guidance, the implementation of a policy of pricing based on quality, and the promotion of the "five separations" method of separate picking, separate drying, separate ginning, separate storage, and separate sales, the quality of cotton has greatly improved.
                        (Huanran)

### 云南省购棉任务超额完成
焕然
1957-01-05
第3版()
专栏：

    云南省购棉任务超额完成
    本报昆明1日电　云南省1956年的棉花收购工作，到12月中旬止，已超额完成了国家计划，比1955年同时期收购实绩增长92.85％。1956年云南棉花生产获得了历史上从来未有过的大增产，总产量比1955年增加了83.64％，超过了第一个五年计划最后一年的生产指标。在棉花的采摘和收购中，由于加强了技术指导，贯彻了按质论价的政策，并推行了分摘、分晒、分轧、分存、分售的“五分”办法，棉花的品质有很大的提高。
                        （焕然）
