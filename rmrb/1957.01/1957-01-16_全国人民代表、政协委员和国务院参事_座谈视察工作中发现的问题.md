Title: National People's Congress Representatives, CPPCC Members, and State Council Counselors Discuss Problems Discovered During Inspection Work
Author:
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 12th. After returning from inspections across the country at the end of 1956, representatives of the National People's Congress, members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and State Council Counselors are holding group discussions starting from the 12th to further study the problems discovered during their inspection work.

Participation in the座谈会 is based on the principle of voluntary participation. More than 370 representatives and members, and 14 State Council Counselors have already participated in the 座谈会. The 座谈会 are divided into fourteen groups according to the issues. These issues are: agriculture, industry and commerce, medicine and health, culture and education, science and technology, industry, mining, and transportation, CPPCC work, social welfare, women and children, urban construction and urban services, ethnic and religious affairs, overseas Chinese affairs, and intellectuals. Representatives, members, and counselors can choose to participate in one or several of these topics for discussion.

### 全国人民代表、政协委员和国务院参事  座谈视察工作中发现的问题

1957-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表、政协委员和国务院参事
    座谈视察工作中发现的问题
    新华社12日讯　全国人民代表大会代表、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员和国务院参事在1956年年底从全国各地视察归来以后，为了进一步研究视察工作中发现的问题，从12日起举行分组座谈。
    座谈会是以自愿参加为原则。参加座谈会的代表和委员已有三百七十多人，国务院参事有十四人。座谈会按照问题分为十四个组进行。这些问题是：农业，工商业，医药卫生，文化教育，科学技术，工矿、交通，政协工作，社会福利，妇女、儿童，城市建设和城市服务，民族、宗教，侨务工作和知识分子等问题。代表、委员和参事们可以就这些问题选择一项或几项参加座谈。
