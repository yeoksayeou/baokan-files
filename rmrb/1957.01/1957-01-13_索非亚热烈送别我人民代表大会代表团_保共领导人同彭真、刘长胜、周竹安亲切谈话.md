Title: Sofia Warmly Bids Farewell to Our National People's Congress Delegation; Bulgarian Communist Leaders Hold Cordial Talks with Peng Zhen, Liu Changsheng, and Zhou Zhuan

Author: 
Date: 1957-01-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 12th: The Chinese National People's Congress delegation concluded its eight-day visit to Bulgaria and left Sofia on a special plane on the 11th for Albania.

Those seeing the delegation off at the airport included Georgi Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly; Kozovski, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the National Assembly; Traikov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers; R. Damyanov, Mikhailov, Chervenkov, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers; and other officials of the Bulgarian government.

The Chinese, Soviet, and Albanian ambassadors to Bulgaria also went to the airport to see them off.

Peng Zhen, the head of the delegation, spoke at the airport. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the warm welcome and cordial reception the delegation received during its visit.

On the 9th and 10th, the delegation conducted group visits and tours in Sofia.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 12th: Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party; R. Damyanov, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee; Taskov, Secretary of the Central Committee; and Dimov, member of the Central Committee, met on January 10th with Peng Zhen, member and Secretary of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, head of the Chinese National People's Congress delegation and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee delegation, as well as Liu Changsheng, member of the Central Committee of the CPC, and Zhou Zhuan, the Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, who are currently visiting Bulgaria, and had a warm conversation.

During the talks, information was exchanged on the situations of the two parties, and it was indicated that both sides' views on the issues discussed were in complete agreement.

The talks were conducted in an atmosphere of sincerity and complete mutual understanding.

### 索非亚热烈送别我人民代表大会代表团  保共领导人同彭真、刘长胜、周竹安亲切谈话

1957-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    索非亚热烈送别我人民代表大会代表团
    保共领导人同彭真、刘长胜、周竹安亲切谈话
    据新华社索非亚12日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团结束了在保加利亚为期八天的访问，11日乘专机离开索非亚前往阿尔巴尼亚。
    到机场送行的有保加利亚国民议会主席团主席格·达米扬诺夫、国民议会执行委员会主席科佐夫斯基、部长会议第一副主席特莱科夫、部长会议副主席拉·达米扬诺夫、米哈伊洛夫、契尔文科夫和保加利亚政府的一些其他官员。
    中国、苏联和阿尔巴尼亚驻保加利亚的大使也前往机场送行。
    代表团团长彭真在机场上讲了话。他对代表团在访问期间受到的热忱欢迎和亲切接待表示衷心感谢。
    在9日和10日两天，代表团在索非亚进行了分组访问和参观活动。
    新华社索非亚12日电　保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫，中央委员会政治局委员拉·达米扬诺夫，中央委员会书记塔斯科夫和中央委员迪莫夫1月10日会见了目前正在保加利亚访问的中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团团长、中国共产党中央政治局委员和书记处书记彭真，代表团团员、中共中央委员刘长胜和中国驻保加利亚大使周竹安，并且进行了热烈的谈话。
    谈话时交换了两党状况的情报，并且表明双方对讨论的问题的见解是完全一致的。
    谈话是在诚恳和完全相互了解的气氛中进行的。
