Title: My Trade Delegation Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 23rd: Kong Yuan, head of the Chinese trade delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, arrived in Prague by plane on the 22nd to negotiate the 1957 Sino-Czech trade agreement. The other members of the delegation had arrived here earlier.

### 我贸易代表团到布拉格

1957-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团到布拉格
    据新华社布拉格23日电　到捷克斯洛伐克商谈1957年中捷贸易协定的中国贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长孔原，22日乘飞机到达布拉格。代表团的其他人员已经在早些时候到达这里。
