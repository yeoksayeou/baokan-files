Title: Hungarian Leaders Host Banquet for Our Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, January 17th: Hungarian People's Republic Presidium Chairman Dobi and the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government Premier Kádár held a banquet at the Parliament Building on the 16th to welcome the Chinese Government Delegation headed by Premier Zhou Enlai.

    From the Hungarian side, those present at the banquet included Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Armed Forces and Public Security Münnich, Minister of State Marosán, Foreign Minister Horváth, member of the Executive Committee of the Provisional Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party, Kish, and Minister of Trade Rónai.

    From the Chinese side, those present included delegation members, Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Wang Jiaxiang, Chinese Ambassador to Hungary Hao Deqing, and delegation staff members Qiao Guanhua, Xiong Fu, Zhang Yan, Gong Peng, Li Huichuan, and others.

    At the banquet, Hungarian People's Republic Presidium Chairman Dobi delivered a speech, welcoming the Chinese Government Delegation. He said that the visit of the Chinese Government Delegation to Hungary from afar demonstrates friendship towards Hungary.

    Premier Zhou Enlai then gave a speech. He said that the Hungarian people have already won a great victory in the battle against imperialism and domestic saboteurs. All socialist countries and progressive forces congratulate you. However, the battle is not over, and imperialism is not reconciled to its defeat. You are now engaged in a second battle. We believe that with the experience of the first battle, you will also achieve victory in the second battle. We wish you more victories, and your victory is also our victory.

    Premier Kádár then delivered a speech, expressing gratitude for China's assistance to Hungary. He particularly mentioned the help that the "People's Daily" article "More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" gave to the Hungarian people in their struggle against revisionism. He also expressed gratitude for the visit of the Chinese Government Delegation to Hungary.

    The banquet proceeded in a very warm and friendly atmosphere. At the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to Sino-Hungarian friendship, to the victory of the Hungarian people, and to world peace.

### 匈牙利领导人欢宴我代表团

1957-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利领导人欢宴我代表团
    新华社布达佩斯17日电　匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比和工农革命政府总理卡达尔16日中午在议会大厦举行宴会，招待以周恩来总理为首的中国政府代表团。
    出席宴会的，匈牙利方面有副总理兼武装部队和公安部部长明尼赫、国务部长马罗山、外交部长霍瓦特、社会主义工人党临时中央委员会执行委员会委员基什、贸易部长罗奈伊。
    中国方面有代表团团员、国务院副总理贺龙、外交部副部长王稼祥、中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青和代表团随行人员乔冠华、熊复、张彦、龚澎、李汇川等。
    在宴会上，匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比致词，欢迎中国政府代表团。他说，中国政府代表团远道来匈牙利访问，这表示了对于匈牙利的友谊。
    接着，周恩来总理讲了话。他说，匈牙利人民在对帝国主义和国内破坏分子的作战中，已经打了一次大胜仗。所有的社会主义国家和进步力量都为你们庆贺。但是，战斗还没有结束，帝国主义不甘心它的失败。你们正在进行第二个战斗。我们相信，你们有了第一次的经验，你们第二次的战斗也会取得胜利。我们预祝你们取得更多的胜利，你们的胜利也就是我们的胜利。
    卡达尔总理接着致词，表示感谢中国对匈牙利的帮助。他特别提到了中国“人民日报”的文章“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”在匈牙利人民对修正主义所作的斗争中给予他们的帮助。他也对中国政府代表团在匈牙利的访问表示感谢。
    宴会一直在十分热烈友好的气氛中进行。在宴会上，宾主一再为中匈友谊、为匈牙利人民的胜利、为世界和平干杯。
