Title: Ambassador Ragheb Sends Letter of Thanks to Guo Moruo for Writing Poetry in Support of Egypt
Author:
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st: Egyptian Ambassador Ragheb wrote a letter on the 28th to Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Support Egypt Against Aggression and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, to thank him for writing poetry in support of Egypt.

The letter stated: "I would like to express my gratitude to Your Excellency on behalf of my government and people for your extremely moving poems in support of Egypt. The Egyptian people greatly appreciate your poems, which have been broadcast by the radio and published in various newspapers and periodicals." The letter also stated: "China and Egypt, two self-improving and peace-loving countries, have good relations of friendship and culture, and Egypt will never forget your effective contributions and sincere help in the formation and promotion of this friendly relationship between our two countries."

### 拉加卜大使函谢郭沫若写诗支援埃及

1957-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    拉加卜大使函谢郭沫若写诗支援埃及
    新华社31日讯　埃及驻中国大使拉加卜在28日写信给中国人民支援埃及反抗侵略委员会主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，感谢他写诗支援埃及。
    信中说：您写了极为动人心弦的诗篇支持埃及，我谨代表我国政府和人民向阁下表示谢意。埃及人民非常赞赏您的诗篇，电台已经把它广播出去，各报刊也已将它发表。信中还说：中国和埃及这两个自我奋发、爱好和平的国家有着良好的友谊关系和文化关系，埃及永远不会忘记您对于我们两国这种友好关系的形成和推进所作的有效贡献和真诚的帮助。
