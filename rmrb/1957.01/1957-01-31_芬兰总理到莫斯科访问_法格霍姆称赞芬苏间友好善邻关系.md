Title: Finnish Prime Minister Visits Moscow, Fagerholm Praises Friendly and Good-Neighborly Relations Between Finland and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-01-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 30th: Finnish Prime Minister Karl-August Fagerholm and his entourage arrived in Moscow by train on January 30th at the invitation of the Soviet government.

Welcoming the Finnish Prime Minister at the train station was Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Nikolai Bulganin, among others. After inspecting the guard of honor, the Finnish Prime Minister delivered a short speech. He praised the friendly and solid good-neighborly relations between Finland and the Soviet Union. He said that the Finnish-Soviet border is the most peaceful area in the world, and our visit is a visit of good neighbors to friendly neighbors. This visit to the Soviet Union will enable him to understand the life of the Soviet people and the achievements of the Soviet Union in all aspects, and more importantly, to have personal contact with the Soviet leaders.

On the day of his arrival in Moscow, Fagerholm was received by Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Nikolai Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

### 芬兰总理到莫斯科访问  法格霍姆称赞芬苏间友好善邻关系

1957-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

    芬兰总理到莫斯科访问
    法格霍姆称赞芬苏间友好善邻关系
    新华社莫斯科30日电　芬兰共和国总理法格霍姆和他的随行人员应苏联政府的邀请，在30日乘火车到达莫斯科。
    在火车站上欢迎芬兰总理的有苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁等人。芬兰总理在检阅了仪仗队以后，发表了简短的谈话。他称赞芬苏之间友好而又巩固的善邻关系。他说，芬兰和苏联的国境线是世界上最和平的地区，我们这次访问是好的邻居来访问亲善的邻居。这次访问苏联使他有可能了解苏联人民的生活和苏联各方面的成就，更重要的是能够和苏联领导人进行个人接触。
    在到达莫斯科的当天，法格霍姆就受到苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和部长会议主席布尔加宁的接见。
