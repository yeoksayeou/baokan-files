Title: Motherland Scenery (Picture)
Author: Zhang Yu
Date: 1957-01-07
Page: 4

    Motherland Scenery
    Going north along the Wu River in Guangdong Province, steep cliffs stand tall, peaks rise and fall, and the river meanders. The mountain scenery and waterscape form a beautiful picture. Every time the sun sets, the view from Jinji Ridge in Pingshi, Lechang County, is even more magnificent. Zhang Luo photographed (Southern Daily News Agency draft)

### 祖国风光（图片）
张浴
1957-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    祖国风光
    沿广东省的武江北上，峭岩耸立，峰峦起伏，江水蜿蜒，山光水色，构成一幅美丽的图画。每当夕阳西下，从乐昌县坪石金鸡岭上远眺，景色更加壮丽。　张　洛摄（南方日报社稿）
