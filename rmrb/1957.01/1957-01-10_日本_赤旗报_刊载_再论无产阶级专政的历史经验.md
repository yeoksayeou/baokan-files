Title: Japanese "Akahata" Publishes "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat"
Author:
Date: 1957-01-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 9th. The Japanese Communist Party Central Committee's newspaper "Akahata" published in full the Chinese "People's Daily" article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" on December 30th last year.

### 日本“赤旗报”刊载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”

1957-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    日本“赤旗报”刊载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”
    新华社9日讯　东京消息：日本共产党中央机关报“赤旗报”在去年12月30日全文刊登了中国“人民日报”的文章“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”。
