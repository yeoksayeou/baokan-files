Title: Chinese National People's Congress Delegation Visits Zagreb

Author: 
Date: 1957-01-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, January 26th. The delegation of the National People's Congress of China and the delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee have been visiting Zagreb, the capital of the People's Republic of Croatia, Yugoslavia, for two days.

The delegation arrived in Zagreb by train on the morning of the 24th amidst heavy snowfall. The Chinese guests were warmly welcomed at the train station by Mrazović, Vice-President of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Croatia, Sremec, Chairman of the Republican Chamber of the Assembly, Cacic, Chairman of the Producers' Chamber, Baltić, Vice-Chairman of the Zagreb Municipal People's Committee, and others. On the morning of the 24th, the delegation visited the Rade Končar Electric Motor Factory and held discussions on issues such as workers' committees and enterprise management. In the afternoon of the same day, the delegation visited the National Museum and Art Gallery, and watched an opera in the evening.

On January 25th, Peng Zhen, head of the delegation, and Hu Zi'ang, deputy head of the delegation, led some members of the delegation to visit Zagreb's largest department store. Subsequently, they held discussions with Č. Dušan, Chairman of the Economic Committee of the Croatian National Assembly, M. Dušan, Chairman of the Croatian Trade Association, as well as the manager of the department store, the chairman of the workers' management committee, and the secretary of the Party committee on issues such as the company's operations, employee wages, profit distribution, and prices.

Another group of members, led by Zhang Bojun, deputy head of the delegation, and Le Songsheng, vice mayor of Beijing, and Zhou Shutao, vice mayor of Tianjin, held discussions with the Republic's General Cooperative of Agricultural Production and the Zagreb Municipal People's Committee on agricultural production cooperatives and municipal issues respectively.

On the evening of the 25th, Bakarić, Chairman of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Croatia, hosted a banquet in honor of the delegation. Wu Xiuquan, the Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, also attended the banquet.

### 我国人民代表大会代表团  在萨格勒布市进行参观访问

1957-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我国人民代表大会代表团
    在萨格勒布市进行参观访问
    新华社贝尔格莱德26日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团已经在南斯拉夫克罗地亚人民共和国的首府萨格勒布市进行了两天的参观和访问。
    代表团在24日上午于大雪纷飞中乘火车抵达萨格勒布市。中国客人在火车站上受到克罗地亚人民共和国国民议会副主席姆拉佐维奇、议会共和国院主席斯雷梅茨、生产者院主席察齐、萨格勒布市人民委员会副主席巴尔蒂奇等人的热烈欢迎。24日上午代表团在这里参观了拉德·冈查尔电机制造厂，并且就工人委员会及企业管理等问题进行了座谈。当天下午，代表团参观了民族博物馆和画廊，晚上看了歌剧。
    1月25日，代表团团长彭真、副团长胡子昂率领部分团员参观了萨格勒布市最大的百货公司，随后同克罗地亚国民议会经济委员会主席察·杜山、克罗地亚贸易协会主席马·杜山以及这个百货公司的经理、工人管理委员会主席、党委书记等就公司的经营情况、职工工资、利润分配、物价等问题进行了座谈。
    另一部分团员由副团长章伯钧和北京市副市长乐松生、天津市副市长周叔弢率领分别同共和国农业生产合作总社和萨格勒布市人民委员会就农业生产合作社及市政问题进行了座谈。
    25日晚上，克罗地亚人民共和国人民议会主席巴卡里奇设宴招待代表团。中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权也出席了宴会。
