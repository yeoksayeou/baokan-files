Title: Syrian President Visits India
Author:
Date: 1957-01-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 17th: Syrian President Kuwatly arrived in New Delhi by plane this afternoon from Dhaka. He is visiting India for a ten-day state visit at the invitation of the Indian government. The Syrian President will stay in Delhi for three days.

### 叙总统访问印度

1957-01-18
第6版()
专栏：

    叙总统访问印度
    据新华社新德里17日电　叙利亚总统库阿特利在今天下午乘飞机从达卡到达新德里。他应印度政府的邀请到印度作十天的国事访问。叙利亚总统将在德里停留三天。
