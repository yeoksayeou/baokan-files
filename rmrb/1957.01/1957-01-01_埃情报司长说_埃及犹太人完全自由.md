Title: Egyptian Intelligence Chief Says Egyptian Jews Are Completely Free
Author:
Date: 1957-01-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 30th: Egyptian Intelligence Chief, Abdul Hatem, on the 29th refuted rumors that Egyptian Jews were being "mistreated and not allowed to leave their homes."
    Hatem said: "The propaganda of Britain and France is constantly trying to divert world public opinion from their criminal atrocities of invading Egypt."
    He said, "The thirty-five thousand Egyptian Jews are completely free and enjoy all the rights enjoyed by all other Egyptians."

### 埃情报司长说  埃及犹太人完全自由

1957-01-01
第6版()
专栏：

    埃情报司长说
    埃及犹太人完全自由
    新华社30日讯　开罗消息：埃及情报司司长阿卜杜勒·哈特姆在29日驳斥了所谓埃及犹太人正在遭到“虐待，不准他们离家外出”的谣言。
    哈特姆说：“英法的宣传正在不断企图转移世界舆论对它们侵略埃及的罪恶暴行的注意。”
    他说，“三万五千个埃及犹太人是完全自由的，并且享受着其它所有的埃及人所享受的全部权利。”
