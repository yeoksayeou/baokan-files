    “Lion Head Goose”
    The "Lion Head Goose" produced in Yuepu Township, Chenghai County, Guangdong Province, weighs up to 25 jin each. A batch has now been transported to Beijing and will be exhibited at the National Agricultural Exhibition. Photo by Zhang Luo (Southern Daily News)
