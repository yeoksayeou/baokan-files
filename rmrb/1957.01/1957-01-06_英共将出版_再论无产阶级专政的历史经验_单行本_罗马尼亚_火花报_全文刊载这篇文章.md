Title: British Communist Party to Publish a Pamphlet of "More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" Romanian "Spark" Newspaper Publishes the Full Text of the Article
Author:
Date: 1957-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, London, January 5th: The "Daily Worker" in London published a notice on January 4th stating that in view of the "great importance and widespread interest" in the Chinese "People's Daily" article "More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship," the British Communist Party will publish the full text of this article in pamphlet form.

The "Daily Worker" had already completed the final publication of a detailed summary of the article on the 4th.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, January 5th: The Romanian newspaper "Spark" today published the full text of the "People's Daily" article "More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" on the entire third and fourth pages. Yesterday, "Spark" and other newspapers here had already published a summary of the article.

### 英共将出版“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”单行本  罗马尼亚“火花报”全文刊载这篇文章

1957-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    英共将出版“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”单行本
    罗马尼亚“火花报”全文刊载这篇文章
    新华社伦敦5日电　伦敦“工人日报”在1月4日刊登一个通告说，鉴于中国“人民日报”的“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文的“极大重要性和人们对它的广泛兴趣”，英国共产党将用小册子的形式出版这篇文章的全文。
    “工人日报”在4日已经最后刊完了这篇文章的详细摘要。
    新华社布加勒斯特5日电　罗马尼亚“火花报”今天以第三、第四两个整版的篇幅全文刊载了“人民日报”的文章“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”。昨天，“火花报”和这里各家报纸都已经刊载了这篇文章的摘要。
