Title: Developing the Brotherly Friendship Between China and Poland: Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Warsaw; Premier Zhou Pays Courtesy Call on Polish Party and State Leaders
Author:
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 1

    Developing the Brotherly Friendship Between China and Poland
    Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Warsaw
    Premier Zhou Pays Courtesy Call on Polish Party and State Leaders
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 11th: The Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Premier Zhou Enlai, arrived in Warsaw at 10:00 AM (Warsaw time) today aboard a Tu-104 jet airliner.
    The weather in Warsaw today was clear. Dozens of Chinese and Polish national flags fluttered at the airport, along with banners written in Chinese and Polish: "Long Live the Brotherly Friendship Between Poland and China!" "Warm Welcome to Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China!"
    As the silver plane carrying the distinguished Chinese guests approached the airport, thousands of welcomers erupted in thunderous cheers.
    Those present at the airport to welcome the delegation included Władysław Gomułka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party; Aleksander Zawadzki , Chairman of the Polish Council of State; Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers; all other members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party; other Polish Party and government leaders; and representatives from all walks of life in Warsaw.
    Wang Bingnan, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, was at the airport to greet them.
    Also present at the airport to greet the delegation were diplomatic envoys from various countries to Poland.
    After Premier Zhou Enlai disembarked from the plane, he warmly shook hands with Cyrankiewicz, Gomułka, Zawadzki, and other Polish Party and government leaders. The military band played the national anthems of China and Poland. The Chinese guests received bouquets presented by nine children, including twelve-year-old Spajda.
    Subsequently, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long, accompanied by Cyrankiewicz, reviewed the guard of honor.
    Chairman Cyrankiewicz delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the Polish government and the Polish United Workers' Party. Premier Zhou Enlai then delivered a speech.
    After the speeches, Premier Zhou Enlai and Chairman Cyrankiewicz rode in the same car to the Belweder Palace in Warsaw. As the delegation's vehicles entered Warsaw, crowds of Warsaw citizens gathered on both sides of the streets, waving to greet the guests.
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 11th: Zhou Enlai, head of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China and Premier of the State Council, paid a courtesy call at 12:00 PM today on Polish Party and state leaders Gomułka, Zawadzki, and Cyrankiewicz, among others.
    Present on the Polish side during the meeting were members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Ochab, Polish Foreign Minister Rapacki, and Deputy Foreign Minister Naszkowski, among others.
    Present on the Chinese side were delegation members He Long, Wang Jiaxiang, Chinese Ambassador to Poland Wang Bingnan, and delegation staff member Qiao Guanhua, among others.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 11th: The Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Premier Zhou Enlai, flew from Moscow to Warsaw at 10:00 AM on January 11th aboard a Tu-104 jet airliner.
    Also on the same flight to Warsaw were the Polish Ambassador to China, Kirylok, and his wife.
    Present at the airport to see them off were Soviet First Deputy Premier Mikoyan, Foreign Minister Shepilov, Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin, and Director of the Far Eastern Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Gudev. Also present at the airport to see them off were the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in the Soviet Union, Jucewicz-Banaszak, and embassy staff. Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao and embassy staff also went to the airport to see them off.
    The Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China will continue talks with the Soviet Government Delegation after returning to Moscow.

### 发展中波两国的兄弟友谊  我国政府代表团到达华沙  周总理拜会波兰党和国家领导人

1957-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    发展中波两国的兄弟友谊
    我国政府代表团到达华沙
    周总理拜会波兰党和国家领导人
    新华社华沙11日电　由周恩来总理率领的中华人民共和国政府代表团今天上午十时（华沙时间）乘图—一○四式喷气客机到达华沙。
    今日华沙天气晴朗，机场上飘扬着数十面中波两国国旗和用中、波文字写成的标语：“波中两国的兄弟友谊万岁！”“热烈欢迎中华人民共和国总理周恩来！”
    当载着中国贵宾的银色飞机飞临机场上空时，数千欢迎者发出了响亮的欢呼声。
    到机场欢迎的有波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡、波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基、波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局所有其他委员，波兰党和政府的其他领导人，华沙各界代表等。
    中国驻波兰大使王炳南到机场迎接。
    到机场迎接的还有各国驻波兰外交使节。
    周恩来总理下机后，同西伦凯维兹、哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基和波兰党和政府的其他领导人亲切地握手。军乐队奏起了中波两国国歌。中国客人接受了十二岁女孩斯巴伊达等九个孩子献的鲜花。
    随后，周恩来总理和贺龙副总理在西伦凯维兹的陪同下检阅了仪仗队。
    西伦凯维兹主席代表波兰政府和波兰统一工人党致欢迎词。周恩来总理接着讲了话。
    讲话结束以后，周恩来总理和西伦凯维兹主席同乘一辆汽车驶往华沙的贝尔维德宫。代表团车辆进入华沙市区后，汇集在街道两旁的华沙市民向客人招手致意。
    新华社华沙11日电　中华人民共和国政府代表团团长、国务院总理周恩来，在今日十二时拜会了波兰统一工人党和国家领导人哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基和西伦凯维兹等。
    拜会时波兰方面在座的有波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员奥哈布、波兰外交部长拉帕茨基和外交部副部长纳希科夫斯基等。
    中国方面有代表团团员贺龙、王稼祥、中国驻波兰大使王炳南和代表团随员乔冠华等。
    新华社莫斯科11日电　周恩来总理率领的中华人民共和国政府代表团在11日上午十时乘图—一○四喷气专机从莫斯科飞往华沙。
    同机飞往华沙的有波兰驻中国大使基里洛克和夫人。
    到飞机场送行的，有苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬，外交部长谢皮洛夫，苏联驻中国大使尤金，外交部远东司司长顾德夫。到飞机场送行的还有波兰驻苏联大使馆临时代办尤切夫班纳克和使馆工作人员。中国驻苏联大使刘晓和使馆工作人员也到机场送行。
    中华人民共和国政府代表团将在返回莫斯科以后继续同苏联政府代表团会谈。
