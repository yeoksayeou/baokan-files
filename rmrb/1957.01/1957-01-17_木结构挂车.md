Title: Wooden Structure Trailer
Author: Jiamu
Date: 1957-01-17
Page: 2

New Technology, New Products

    Wooden Structure Trailer
    (This newspaper dispatch) The Beijing Automotive Transportation Company's Automotive Repair Factory and the Beijing Second Automotive Transportation Company's Maintenance Depot recently successfully trial-produced a double-axle automobile trailer using wood instead of steel. It performs well and can save 277 kilograms of steel per unit compared to existing iron-structure trailers.
                         (Jiamu)

### 木结构挂车
佳木
1957-01-17
第2版()
专栏：新技术、新产品

    木结构挂车
    本报讯　北京市汽车运输公司汽车修理厂和北京市第二汽车运输公司保养场最近用木料代替钢铁试制成功了一种双轴汽车挂车，性能良好，比现有的铁结构挂车，每部可以节省钢铁二百七十七公斤。
                          （佳木）
