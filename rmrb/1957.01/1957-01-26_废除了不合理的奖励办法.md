Title: Abolished Unreasonable Reward Methods
Author:
Date: 1957-01-26
Page: 2

Reaction to Criticism

    Abolished Unreasonable Reward Methods
    Letter from Zunyi Match Factory, Guizhou Province: The criticism of our factory in the article "Supervision Wins Awards" published on page two of the People's Daily on September 14, 1956, was correct. Since late September of last year, we have abolished this reward method and will strengthen technical management, improve workers' skills, and improve operations in the future to improve quality.

### 废除了不合理的奖励办法

1957-01-26
第2版()
专栏：对批评的反应

    废除了不合理的奖励办法
    贵州省遵义火柴厂来信：1956年9月14日人民日报二版刊载“监督得奖”一文对我厂的批评是对的。从去年9月下旬即废除了这种奖励办法，并在今后要加强技术管理，提高工人技术以及改进操作等方面来提高质量。
