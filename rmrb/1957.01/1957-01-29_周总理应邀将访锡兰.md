Title: Premier Zhou Invited to Visit Ceylon

Author:

Date: 1957-01-29
Page: 1

    Premier Zhou Invited to Visit Ceylon
    Xinhua News Agency, 28th – Premier of the State Council of the People’s Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, upon the invitation of the Ceylonese government, will pay a friendly visit to Ceylon from January 31st to February 5th.

### 周总理应邀将访锡兰

1957-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理应邀将访锡兰
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，应锡兰政府的邀请，将于1月31日到2月5日前往锡兰作友好访问。
