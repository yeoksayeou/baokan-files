Title: Japanese Children's Art Exhibition Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-01-22
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 20th—A Japanese Children's Art Exhibition opened today at Zhongshan Park in Beijing.

This exhibition features over three hundred crayon drawings, chalk drawings, watercolor paintings, and woodcuts. The exhibition is sponsored by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese People's National Committee for the Protection of Children; the exhibits were a gift from the Japan-China Friendship Association.

The exhibition is scheduled to end on February 10th.

### 北京举行日本儿童画展

1957-01-22
第7版()
专栏：

    北京举行日本儿童画展
    新华社20日讯　日本儿童画展今天起在北京中山公园展出。
    这次共展出三百多幅腊笔画、粉笔画、水彩画和木刻。展览会是中国人民对外文化协会和中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会举办的；展品是日中友好协会赠送的。
    展览会预定在2月10日结束。
