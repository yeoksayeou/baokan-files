Title: Italian "L'Unità" Publishes "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship"
Author:
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, January 15th: The Italian Communist Party's newspaper "L'Unità" published the full text of the "People's Daily" article "Once More on the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" on January 9th. The newspaper had previously published an abstract of the article.

### 意大利“团结报”刊登“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”

1957-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    意大利“团结报”刊登“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”
    新华社15日讯　罗马消息：意大利共产党机关报“团结报”1月9日全文刊载了“人民日报”的文章“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”。这家报纸以前曾经登载过这篇文章的摘要。
