Title: Japan Appoints Muto as Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 23rd. The Japanese Cabinet decided on the evening of the 22nd to appoint Kiichi Muto, Administrative Vice-Minister for Foreign Affairs, as Japan's first post-war ambassador to the Soviet Union. The post of Administrative Vice-Minister for Foreign Affairs will be taken over by Katsumi Ohno, the Japanese ambassador to West Germany.

### 日本任命门胁为驻苏大使

1957-01-24
第6版()
专栏：

    日本任命门胁为驻苏大使
    新华社23日讯　东京消息：日本内阁在22日晚上决定任命外务省次官门胁季光为战后日本第一任驻苏联大使。外务省次官的职务将由日本驻西德大使大野胜巳来担任。
