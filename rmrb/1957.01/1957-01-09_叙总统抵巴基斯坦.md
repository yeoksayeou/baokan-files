Title: Syrian President Arrives in Pakistan
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, January 8th: Syrian President Kuwatly arrived in Karachi by plane on the afternoon of January 7th. Kuwatly will be making a ten-day official visit to Pakistan.

Accompanying him on the visit are Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar, Commander-in-Chief of the National Armed Forces Nizamuddin, and other officials.

On his way from Damascus to Karachi, Kuwatly stopped in Riyadh, the capital of Saudi Arabia, for a day and held talks with Saudi Arabian King Saud.

### 叙总统抵巴基斯坦

1957-01-09
第6版()
专栏：

    叙总统抵巴基斯坦
    新华社卡拉奇8日电　叙利亚总统库阿特利7日下午乘飞机到达卡拉奇。库阿特利将在巴基斯坦进行十天的正式访问。
    陪同他进行访问的有叙利亚外交部长比塔尔、国家武装部队总司令尼扎姆丁以及其他官员。
    库阿特利从大马士革到卡拉奇途中，曾经在沙特阿拉伯首都利雅得停留一天，同沙特阿拉伯国王沙特进行会谈。
