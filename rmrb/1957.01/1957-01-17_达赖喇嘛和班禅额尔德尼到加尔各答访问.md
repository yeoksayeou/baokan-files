Title: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Visit Calcutta
Author:
Date: 1957-01-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Calcutta, 16th. The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni arrived here on the morning of the 15th by special train from Jamshedpur. They were warmly welcomed by officials and dignitaries from West Bengal and Calcutta. Liu Yufeng, the Chinese Consul General in Calcutta, and representatives of the overseas Chinese community were also present to welcome them.

　　As the guests crossed the massive Howrah Bridge on their way to the Grand Hotel, where they are staying, they were greeted by hundreds of thousands of people lining the streets.

　　Padmaja Naidu, the Governor of West Bengal, held a grand reception at Government House on the evening of the 15th to welcome the Dalai Lama and Panchen. Prime Minister Nehru also attended the banquet. Prime Minister Nehru had just returned from Santiniketan, where he attended the annual meeting of the International University.

　　Also present at the banquet were Roy, the Chief Minister of West Bengal, ministers, and Ghosh, the Mayor of Calcutta, and others.

　　The host introduced the Dalai Lama and Panchen to everyone. They spoke with the people present. Before they left, they came out with Nehru and Naidu to view the night scenery of Government House. Under the moonlight, Nehru briefly introduced the history of the city, explaining how it had grown from a small village into India's largest city.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jamshedpur, 15th. The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni visited the Tata Iron and Steel Company factory here on the afternoon of the 14th. They were warmly welcomed by factory director Jehangir, engineers, and workers.

　　The Tata Iron and Steel Company factory is the largest private factory in India, with approximately 30,000 workers and an annual production of approximately 800,000 tons of steel. The factory director held a tea party to entertain the distinguished guests from Tibet, China, and introduced them to prominent figures in the area.

　　The distinguished guests also visited the Tata Engineering and Locomotive Company factories, both of which belong to the Tata system. They also visited the National Metallurgical Laboratory here.

　　On the 13th, the Dalai Lama and Panchen arrived in Chittaranjan from Nalanda. They were guests at the Chittaranjan Locomotive Works that day. On the afternoon of the 13th, the distinguished guests also visited the Sindri Fertilizer Factory, established by the Indian government to develop agriculture.

　　On their way from Chittaranjan to Sindri, they stopped to visit the Maithon Dam, which is under construction.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼到加尔各答访问

1957-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼到加尔各答访问
    据新华社加尔各答16日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼15日上午从詹谢普尔乘专车到达这里。他们受到西孟加拉邦和加尔各答的官员们和社会名流的热烈欢迎。中国驻加尔各答总领事柳雨峰和华侨代表也到场欢迎。
    在客人们越过巨大的豪拉桥前往他们住的葛兰德饭店的途中，成千上万的人夹道欢迎。
    西孟加拉邦长帕德马加·奈都15日晚在政府大厦举行盛大招待会欢迎达赖和班禅。尼赫鲁总理也出席了宴会。尼赫鲁总理刚从山提尼克坦回来，他在那里参加了国际大学的年会。
    出席宴会的还有西孟加拉邦首席部长罗伊、各部部长、加尔各答市长高希等。
    主人把达赖和班禅介绍给大家。他们同到会的人谈了话。在他们离开之前，他们在尼赫鲁和奈都的陪同下出来观看政府大厦的夜景。尼赫鲁在月下简略地介绍了这个城市的历史，说明它从一个小的村庄变为印度最大城市的经过。
    据新华社詹谢普尔15日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼14日下午参观了这里的塔塔钢铁公司工厂。他们受到厂长杰汉吉尔、工程师和工人们热烈的欢迎。
    塔塔钢铁公司工厂是印度最大的一家私营工厂，约有三万工人，每年约生产八十万吨钢。厂长举行了茶会招待从中国西藏来的贵宾，并且把这里的著名人士介绍给他们。
    贵宾们还参观了塔塔机械和机车公司工厂，这两个工厂都是属于塔塔系统的。他们还参观了这里的国立冶金实验所。
    达赖和班禅13日从那烂陀到达奇特兰詹。当天他们在奇特兰詹机车工厂作客。贵宾们13日下午还参观了印度政府为了发展农业而建立的辛德利肥料工厂。
    他们在从奇特兰詹赴辛德利途中，曾经停下来参观正在建造中的迈顿水坝。
