Title: Sailor Returns Home
Author: Yan Zhen
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 8

Column:

    Sailor Returns Home
    Yan Zhen
    I am a sailor, demobilized and returned to my hometown.
    I once embraced the vast ocean with my arms.
    I once welcomed sudden rain and storms with my chest.
    I once broke through ten thousand acres of waves with my feet.
    Demobilized and returned to my hometown, I am still a sailor.
    I will accompany my beloved hometown, sailing towards happiness.
    I will treat my whole life as the time of departure,
    Until my silver beard flutters, until my white hair clusters.

### 水兵回到家乡
严阵
1957-01-05
第8版()
专栏：

    水兵回到家乡
    严阵我是一个水兵，复员回到家乡。我曾用我的臂膀，拥抱过辽阔的海洋。我曾用我的胸膛，迎接过骤雨暴风。我曾用我的双脚，踏破过波涛万顷。复员回到家乡，我还是一个水兵。我将伴随着亲爱的家乡，向着幸福航行。我把我的一生，全当作出航的时间，直到银须飘飘，直到白发团团。
