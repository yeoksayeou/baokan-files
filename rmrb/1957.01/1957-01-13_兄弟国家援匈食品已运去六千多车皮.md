Title: Fraternal Countries Send Over Six Thousand Train Cars of Food Aid to Hungary
Author:
Date: 1957-01-13
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest Dispatch: As of the 9th, socialist countries have transported a total of 6,220 train cars of food aid to Budapest and other industrial centers in Hungary, of which 4,284 train cars came from the Soviet Union.

    The Bulgarian people have voluntarily donated 1,000 tons of sugar and 1,000 tons of lentils, among other items, to the Hungarian people.

    The Democratic Republic of Vietnam has allocated 3 million rubles to purchase goods in Romania for transport to aid the Hungarian people.

### 兄弟国家援匈食品已运去六千多车皮

1957-01-13
第5版()
专栏：

    兄弟国家援匈食品已运去六千多车皮
    据新华社讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：到9日为止，社会主义国家运到布达佩斯和匈牙利其他工业中心的援助食品共有六千二百二十车皮，其中有四千二百八十四车皮是从苏联运来的。
    保加利亚人民自愿捐给匈牙利人民的物资已经有一千吨糖、一千吨扁豆等物品。
    越南民主共和国拨出了三百万卢布在罗马尼亚购买了货物运来援助匈牙利人民。
