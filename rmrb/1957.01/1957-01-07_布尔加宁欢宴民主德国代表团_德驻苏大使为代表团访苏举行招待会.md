Title: Bulganin Hosts Banquet for East German Delegation; German Ambassador to USSR Holds Reception for Delegation's Visit

Author: 
Date: 1957-01-07
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th: According to TASS, Soviet Premier Bulganin held a banquet in the Grand Kremlin Palace in Moscow on January 5th to entertain the German Democratic Republic government delegation headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl.
    Soviet leaders Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Saburov, Suslov, Khrushchev, Brezhnev, Shepilov, Furtseva, Aristov, Belyaev, Pospelov, and heads of various Soviet government departments attended the reception.
    The banquet was filled with a warm and sincere atmosphere. Bulganin and Grotewohl toasted each other.
    On the same day, the German Democratic Republic's ambassador to the Soviet Union, König, held a reception for the delegation's visit to the Soviet Union. Invited to attend the reception were Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Molotov, Khrushchev, Shepilov, and others, as well as heads of many Soviet government departments, workers in science and culture, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union.
    On this day, the delegation, led by Prime Minister Grotewohl, also visited the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum in Red Square and laid wreaths.
    On the evening of January 4th, the delegation, accompanied by Bulganin, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Khrushchev, and Shepilov, went to the Soviet Grand Theatre to watch Mussorgsky's opera "Boris Godunov".

### 布尔加宁欢宴民主德国代表团  德驻苏大使为代表团访苏举行招待会

1957-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁欢宴民主德国代表团
    德驻苏大使为代表团访苏举行招待会
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁1月5日在莫斯科大克里姆林宫举行宴会，招待以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团。
    苏联领导人卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、萨布罗夫、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、勃列日涅夫、谢皮洛夫、福尔采娃、阿里斯托夫、别利亚耶夫、波斯伯洛夫，以及苏联政府各部门的负责人出席了招待会。
    宴会充满了热烈和诚恳的气氛，布尔加宁和格罗提渥相互举杯祝贺。
    在同一天，德意志民主共和国驻苏联大使柯尼希为代表团的访问苏联举行了招待会。应邀参加招待会的有布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫、谢皮洛夫等，以及苏联政府许多部门的负责人、科学文化工作者和各国驻苏联的外交使节。
    这一天，代表团人员还在格罗提渥总理的率领下，前往红场参谒列宁和斯大林陵墓，并且献了花圈。
    1月4日晚上，代表团由布尔加宁、马林科夫、米高扬、赫鲁晓夫和谢皮洛夫等陪同前往苏联大剧院观看了莫索尔斯基的歌剧“鲍里斯·戈杜诺夫”。
