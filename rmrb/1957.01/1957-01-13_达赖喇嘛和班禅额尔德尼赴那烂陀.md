Title: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Go to Nalanda
Author:
Date: 1957-01-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 12th -- The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, together with Prime Minister Nehru and Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili, left Delhi today on the Indian President's special plane for Nalanda, the site of the ancient Buddhist university. The Dalai Lama will, on behalf of the Chinese government, present Xuanzang's skull relic to India at a grand ceremony there today, and Prime Minister Nehru will accept Xuanzang's skull relic on behalf of the Indian government.

Delhi newspapers have widely published articles introducing Xuanzang's life and travels, as well as his writings on the situation in India as he saw it during his travels.

"The Times of India" said today: "India highly values the remains of this person who made a very great contribution to strengthening relations between India and China."

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼赴那烂陀

1957-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼赴那烂陀
    据新华社新德里12日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼今天同尼赫鲁总理和中国驻印度大使潘自力一起，乘印度总统专机离开德里前往古老的佛教大学的所在地那烂陀。达赖喇嘛今天将在那里举行的一个隆重的仪式上代表中国政府赠送玄奘的顶骨给印度，尼赫鲁总理将代表印度政府接受玄奘的顶骨。
    德里报纸普遍刊登了介绍玄奘的生平和游历经过的文章，以及他所写的在他游历期间所看到的印度情况的著作。
    “印度时报”今天说：“印度非常珍视在加强印度和中国关系方面作出非常大的贡献的这个人的遗骨。”
