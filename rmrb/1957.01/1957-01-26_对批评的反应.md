Reactions to Criticism

    A letter from the Business Office of the Jinan Railway Administration: The criticism of us in the People's Daily article "Is Demanding Reunion a Sign of Backward Thinking?" on August 2, 1956, page 4, was correct. On September 6 of last year, we transferred Comrade Shao Fuyuan to work at the Tianjin Transportation Business Office of the Beijing Railway Administration.

### 对批评的反应

1957-01-26
第4版()
专栏：对批评的反应

    对批评的反应
    济南铁路管理局商务处来信：人民日报1956年8月2日四版的“要求团圆是思想落后吗？”文中对我们的批评是正确的。去年9月6日，我们已把邵福元同志调至北京铁路管理局天津运输营业所工作。
