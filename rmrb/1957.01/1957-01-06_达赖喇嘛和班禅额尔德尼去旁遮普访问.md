Title: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni to Visit Punjab
Author:
Date: 1957-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 5th. After resting in New Delhi for five days, the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, along with their entourage, departed on the evening of the 4th for Pathankot, a town in the northwestern part of Punjab State. From there, they will visit Mandi, Rewalsar, and Palampur, which are famous for their ancient temples.

The guests will return to New Delhi on January 8th, and then go to Nalanda and visit eastern India.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼去旁遮普访问

1957-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼去旁遮普访问
    新华社新德里5日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼以及他们的随行人员在新德里休息了五天以后，在4日傍晚动身前往旁遮普邦西北部的一个城镇帕坦科特。从那儿，他们将去访问以古庙著名的曼地、腊瓦萨尔和帕兰普尔。
    客人们将在1月8日回到新德里，然后去那烂陀，并且在印度东部访问。
