Title: Israeli Authorities Play Dirty, Defying UN Resolutions
Author:
Date: 1957-01-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jerusalem, January 21st: An Israeli Foreign Ministry spokesman said on the 20th that, "morally speaking," Israel cannot be required to strictly abide by United Nations resolutions.

This is the first official Israeli comment on the United Nations General Assembly's resolution on the 19th demanding the immediate withdrawal of Israeli troops from Egyptian territory.

It is reported that Israel has instructed its delegation to the United Nations to try to obtain an agreement within the five-day deadline set by the United Nations that guarantees Israel's "vital interests."

### 以色列当局耍赖  顽抗联合国决议

1957-01-22
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列当局耍赖
    顽抗联合国决议
    据新华社21日讯　耶路撒冷消息：以色列外交部的一位发言人20日说，“从道义上讲”，不能够要求以色列严格遵照联合国决议办事。
    这是以色列官方第一次正式评论联合国大会19日通过的要求以色列军队立即撤出埃及领土的决议。
    据报道，以色列已经训令它驻在联合国的代表团设法在联合国规定的五天限期内取得一项保证以色列“切身利益”的协议。
