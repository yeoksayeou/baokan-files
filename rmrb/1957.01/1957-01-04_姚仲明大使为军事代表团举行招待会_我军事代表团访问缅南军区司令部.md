Title: Ambassador Yao Zhongming Holds Reception for Military Delegation; Our Military Delegation Visits the Headquarters of the Southern Military Region of Burma
Author:
Date: 1957-01-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 3rd: Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming held a reception on the evening of the 2nd for the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation led by Marshal Ye Jianying's visit to Burma. Approximately eight hundred guests attended the reception held in the garden of the Chinese Embassy.

Among the guests were: General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, Brigadier General (Air Force) Clift, Deputy Chief of Staff, Sao Hkun Hkio, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities, Bo Mu Aung, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies, Mrs. Nu, senior officers of the Burmese Defense Forces, and diplomatic envoys from various countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 3rd: The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation led by Marshal Ye Jianying, accompanied by Brigadier General Kyaw Zaw, visited the headquarters of the Southern Military Region of Burma on the morning of the 2nd, and met with Commander Brigadier General Blake and officers of the headquarters.

At 9:00 am, the delegation visited the First Burmese Recruit Training Station, where they watched the exercises for training new recruits.

Marshal Ye Jianying and members of the delegation then visited the Mingaladon Air Force Station. An aircraft performed a ten-minute aerial display. When Brigadier General (Air Force) Clift, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, introduced the pilot to Marshal Ye Jianying, Ye Jianying expressed his great appreciation for the pilot's excellent performance and was extremely satisfied with the performance. The delegation was invited to lunch at the Burmese Air Force restaurant.

In the afternoon, the delegation visited the Burmese Army NCO School and the Mingaladon Base Military Hospital.

### 姚仲明大使为军事代表团举行招待会  我军事代表团访问缅南军区司令部

1957-01-04
第5版()
专栏：

    姚仲明大使为军事代表团举行招待会
    我军事代表团访问缅南军区司令部
    据新华社仰光3日电　中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明2日晚为叶剑英元帅率领的中国军事友好代表团访问缅甸举行招待会。约有八百位来宾参加了在中国大使馆花园里举行的招待会。
    来宾中有：缅甸国防军总参谋长奈温将军、副总参谋长（空军）克利夫特、民族院议长萧恢塔、代表院议长波木昂、努夫人、缅甸国防军高级军官、以及各国外交使节。
    新华社仰光3日电　由叶剑英元帅率领的中国军事友好代表团在觉温准将的陪同下，2日上午访问了缅南军区司令部，并且会见了司令布莱克准将和司令部的军官们。
    上午九时，代表团参观了缅甸第一新兵训练站，他们在那里参观了训练新兵的演习。
    叶剑英元帅和代表团团员们然后参观了明加拉顿空军站。一架飞机在空中作了十分钟的飞行表演。当缅甸国防军副总参谋长（空军）克利夫特把驾驶员介绍给叶剑英元帅的时候，叶剑英非常感谢这位驾驶员的精彩表演，他对这次表演极感满意。代表团应邀在缅甸空军餐厅进午餐。
    代表团下午参观了缅甸陆军士官学校和明加拉顿基地军事医院。
