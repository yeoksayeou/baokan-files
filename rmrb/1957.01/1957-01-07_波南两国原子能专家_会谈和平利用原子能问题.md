Title: Polish and Yugoslav Atomic Energy Experts Confer on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy

Author:

Date: 1957-01-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Polish atomic energy scientist delegation headed by William Billig, the Polish government's plenipotentiary representative on peaceful uses of atomic energy, held talks with representatives of the Yugoslav Atomic Energy Commission in Belgrade and issued a joint communique.

The communique stated that the two sides exchanged information on the organization of scientific research work and also studied the achievements of the two countries in the use of atomic energy and the possibilities for cooperation in this area.

### 波南两国原子能专家  会谈和平利用原子能问题

1957-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    波南两国原子能专家
    会谈和平利用原子能问题
    据新华社6日讯　由波兰政府和平利用原子能问题全权代表威廉·比利格率领的波兰原子能科学家代表团，同南斯拉夫原子能委员会的代表在贝尔格莱德举行了会谈，并且发表了会谈公报。
    公报指出，两国代表交换了关于组织科学研究工作的情报、同时还研究了两国在利用原子能方面的成就以及在这方面进行合作的可能性。
