Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Hold a Program to Comfort Coal Mine Workers During the Spring Festival
Author:
Date: 1957-01-30
Page: 4

Central People's Broadcasting Station
    Spring Festival Program to Comfort Coal Mine Workers

(Our report) The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a Spring Festival program to comfort coal mine workers nationwide. The program will include speeches by Xu Daben, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry, and the famous Xiangsheng (comedic dialogue) performer Hou Baolin. It will also feature excerpts from the Peking Opera "Borrowing Arrows with Straw Boats" and "The Case of Qin Xianglian" sung by the famous Peking Opera actors Ma Lianliang and Qiu Shengrong, a performance of the "Contradiction" scene from the Ping Opera "Little Son-in-Law" by the famous Ping Opera actress Xiaobai Yushuang, the Xiangsheng "Fenhe Bay" performed by Hou Baolin and Guo Qiru, as well as folk songs and national instrumental music. The program will be broadcast on January 30 on the station's first program from 7:30 PM to 8:15 PM and on February 1 from 3:00 PM to 3:40 PM.

### 中央人民广播电台  春节举办慰问煤矿职工节目

1957-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台
    春节举办慰问煤矿职工节目
    本报讯　中央人民广播电台将在春节举办对全国煤矿职工的慰问节目，内容有煤炭工业部副部长徐达本和著名相声演员侯宝林的讲话，还有著名京剧演员马连良、裘盛戎唱的“借东风”和“铡美案”中的一段，著名评剧演员小白玉霜唱的“小女婿”里的“矛盾”一场，侯宝林、郭启儒合说的相声“汾河湾”，以及民歌和民族器乐曲等节目。节目将在1月30日该台第一种节目下午七点三十分到八点十五分和2月1日下午三点到三点四十分广播。
