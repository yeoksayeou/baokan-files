Title: UN Deputy Secretary-General Visits Hungary to Discuss UN Aid to Hungary

Author: 
Date: 1957-01-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, January 8th: According to official reports here, UN Deputy Secretary-General, Mr. de Seynes, visited Hungary from January 4th to 7th, and held negotiations with relevant Hungarian parties on the issue of UN aid to Hungary.

    The head of the Hungarian delegation participating in the negotiations was Professor I. Vajda, a member of the Hungarian delegation to the United Nations.

    During Mr. de Seynes' stay in Budapest, the Premier of the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, Mr. Kadar, the Minister of Trade, Mr. Ronai, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Szarka, and other responsible persons received him.

### 联合国副秘书长访匈  商谈联合国援助匈牙利问题

1957-01-10
第5版()
专栏：

    联合国副秘书长访匈
    商谈联合国援助匈牙利问题
    新华社布达佩斯8日电　据这里官方报道，联合国副秘书长德塞内在1月4日到7日访问了匈牙利，同匈牙利有关方面就联合国援助匈牙利的问题进行了谈判。
    匈牙利方面参加谈判的代表团团长是匈牙利出席联合国代表团团员伊·瓦伊达教授。
    在德塞内逗留布达佩斯期间，匈牙利工农革命政府总理卡达尔、贸易部长罗奈伊、外交部副部长萨尔卡和其他负责人都接见了他。
