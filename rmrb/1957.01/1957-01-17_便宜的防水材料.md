Title: Cheap Waterproof Material
Author: Wu Yong
Date: 1957-01-17
Page: 2

New Technology, New Products

    Cheap Waterproof Material
    (By our correspondent) The Zhongnan First Engineering Company of the Ministry of Construction Engineering has recently trial-produced a new waterproof material, cold asphalt emulsion. It is made by mixing petroleum asphalt, asbestos fiber, and lime slurry with water. When using this material, workers can apply it to the roof like plastering, which is very convenient. In particular, it can greatly reduce project costs. According to the Zhongnan First Engineering Company's experiment, the cost of using cold asphalt emulsion is only 30 cents per square meter, while using asphalt felt costs 6 yuan per square meter, and the quality is exactly the same as asphalt felt. (Wu Yong)

### 便宜的防水材料
吴用
1957-01-17
第2版()
专栏：新技术、新产品

    便宜的防水材料
    本报讯　建筑工程部中南第一工程公司最近试制出一种新的防水材料冷沥青悬浮液。它是用石油沥青、石棉纤维和石灰浆加水搅拌制成的。使用这种材料，工人们在施工时可以像抹灰一样铺抹在屋面上，操作起来非常方便。特别是可以大大地降低工程造价。根据中南第一工程公司试验，使用冷沥青悬浮液，每平方公尺造价只要三角，使用油毛毡每平方公尺需要六元，而质量和油毛毡完全一样。（吴用）
