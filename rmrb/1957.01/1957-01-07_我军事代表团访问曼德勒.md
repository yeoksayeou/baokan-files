Title: Our Military Delegation Visits Mandalay
Author:
Date: 1957-01-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, January 6th: The Chinese military goodwill delegation led by Marshal Ye Jianying arrived in Mandalay, the ancient capital of Burma, on the morning of the 5th for a visit.

The delegation was welcomed by senior officers of the Northern Burma Military Region Command, the Mayor of Mandalay, and approximately one thousand citizens of Mandalay.

In the afternoon of the same day, the delegation traveled by car to Maymyo, the headquarters of the Northern Burma Military Region Command.

### 我军事代表团访问曼德勒

1957-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团访问曼德勒
    据新华社仰光6日电　以叶剑英元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团5日上午到达缅甸古都曼德勒访问。
    代表团受到缅北军区司令部的高级军官、曼德勒市市长和曼德勒的大约一千个市民的欢迎。
    当天下午，代表团乘车前往缅北军区司令部所在地眉谬。
