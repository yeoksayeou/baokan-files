Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation and Foreign Cultural Association Delegation Return Home; Soviet Russian Republic Industrial Cooperative Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-01-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 21st: The head of the Soviet Cultural Delegation, Mikhailov, and some members of the delegation, including Matveyev, Kalashnikov, and Boni, returned to their country by plane on the morning of the 21st.

    Three other members of the delegation have not yet returned home. Grashenko and Rybin visited Wuhan and Chongqing respectively; Ovid has already set off to visit the Mongolian People's Republic.

    Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: The Soviet Foreign Cultural Association delegation, led by the famous Soviet educator Kairov, after visiting Beijing, Chongqing, Kunming, Guangzhou, Nanchang, Shanghai and other cities, except for member Edlin, returned home by plane in batches on the 21st and 22nd.

    Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: The five-member delegation of the Soviet Russian Republic Industrial Cooperative Society returned home by train today.

### 苏联文化代表团和对外文协代表团回国  苏联俄罗斯共和国工艺合作社代表团回国

1957-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联文化代表团和对外文协代表团回国
    苏联俄罗斯共和国工艺合作社代表团回国
    据新华社21日讯　21日早上，苏联文化代表团团长米哈伊洛夫和一部分团员马特维耶夫、卡拉什尼科夫和博尼等乘飞机返国。
    另外还有三位团员尚未回国。格拉申科和雷宾分别赴武汉、重庆两地访问；奥维德在已启程去蒙古人民共和国访问。
    据新华社22日讯　由苏联著名教育学家凯洛夫率领的苏联对外文化协会代表团在访问了北京、重庆、昆明、广州、南昌、上海等城市以后，除团员艾德林外，已于21日和22日分批乘飞机返国。
    据新华社22日讯　苏联俄罗斯共和国工艺合作社代表团一行五人，今天乘火车回国。
