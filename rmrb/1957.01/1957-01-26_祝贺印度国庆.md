    Congratulations on India's National Day
    Telegram from Chairman Mao to President Prasad
    To His Excellency Dr. Rajendra Prasad, President of the Republic of India:
    On the occasion of your country's National Day, I extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the great people of India. May the close unity and friendly cooperative relations between China and India be increasingly consolidated and developed, may the Republic of India prosper, and may the people of India be happy.
              Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, January 25, 1957
    Telegram from Premier Zhou to Premier Nehru
    To His Excellency Jawaharlal Nehru, Prime Minister of the Republic of India:
    On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the founding of the Republic of India, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Government of the Republic of India.
    The Chinese people are pleased to see that as the initiating countries of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, the friendship between China and India has made considerable progress, the economic and cultural ties between the two countries are increasingly strengthened, and the cooperation between the two countries in international affairs has become closer. I sincerely hope that this friendly cooperative relationship will continue to be consolidated and developed. I wish Your Excellency and the Indian people greater achievements in the cause of seeking the prosperity of the Republic of India and safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.
          Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, January 25, 1957

### 祝贺印度国庆

1957-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺印度国庆
    毛主席致普拉沙德总统电印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德博士阁下：
    欣逢贵国国庆，谨向阁下和伟大的印度人民表示热烈的祝贺。祝中印两国的亲密团结和友好合作的关系日益巩固和发展，祝印度共和国繁荣和印度人民幸福。
              中华人民共和国主席毛泽东　1957年1月25日
    周总理致尼赫鲁总理电印度共和国总理贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁阁下：
    值此印度共和国成立七周年之际，我谨向阁下和印度共和国政府致诚挚的祝贺。
    中国人民高兴地看到，作为和平共处五项原则首倡国家，中国和印度之间的友谊已经有了长足的发展，两国在经济和文化方面的联系日益加强，两国在国际事务上的合作也已更加紧密。我热诚地希望这一友好合作关系将不断地巩固和发展。祝阁下和印度人民在谋求印度共和国繁荣和维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中获得更大的成就。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来　1957年1月25日
