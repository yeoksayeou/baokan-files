Title: Bulganin Hosts Banquet to See Off Chinese Government Delegation; Soviet Party and Government Leaders See Off Chinese Delegation at Vnukovo Airport
Author:
Date: 1957-01-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 19th: Soviet Premier Bulganin held a banquet at the Grand Kremlin Palace at 6:30 PM on the 18th to bid farewell to the Chinese Government Delegation headed by Premier Zhou Enlai.

From the Soviet side, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Saburov, Khrushchev, Shepilov, Furtseva, Shvernik, Pospelov, and others attended the banquet.

From the Chinese side, all members of the Chinese Government Delegation and their entourage were present. Some members of the Chinese National People's Congress Delegation, who were temporarily staying in Moscow on their way back home after visiting Bulgaria, were also invited to attend the banquet.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere. The hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Voroshilov, and the friendship between the two peoples. Chairman Bulganin delivered a speech at the banquet (to be released separately). Premier Zhou Enlai then delivered a reply (to be released separately).

The hosts and guests also enjoyed wonderful performances by some famous Soviet instrumentalists and actors at the banquet.

The banquet concluded at around 8 o'clock. As Premier Zhou Enlai and others left the venue, the song "Moscow-Beijing," familiar to the people of both China and the Soviet Union, was played in the hall.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 19th: The Chinese Government Delegation headed by Premier Zhou Enlai, which was invited to visit the Soviet Union, left Moscow on a Tu-104 jet special plane at 10:00 PM (Moscow time) on the 18th to visit Afghanistan.

At the Vnukovo Airport at night, more than a dozen spotlights illuminated the scene. Snow was falling. People from Moscow rushed to the airport, which is 30 kilometers from the city, to see off the distinguished Chinese guests.

Among those who came to see them off were Soviet Communist Party and government leaders Comrades Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Saburov, Khrushchev, Shepilov, Shvernik, ministers and generals of the Soviet government, and diplomatic envoys from various countries.

Personnel from the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union also came to the airport to see them off.

The airport was densely packed with well-wishers.

Premier Zhou Enlai entered the airport accompanied by the leaders of the Soviet Party and government. The band played the national anthems of the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union. Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Chairman Bulganin, reviewed the guard of honor.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and others bid farewell to those who came to see them off.

Chairman Bulganin delivered a farewell speech (to be released separately). He said "Goodbye!" in Chinese at the end.

Premier Zhou then delivered a reply (to be released separately).

Soviet Party and government leaders escorted the guests to the plane. They shook hands again to say goodbye.

The silver-white jet airliner swiftly flew into the night sky. After Premier Zhou Enlai and others arrive in Tashkent, they will transfer to an Il-14 aircraft to travel to Kabul, the capital of Afghanistan.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai and others to Tashkent were Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Fedrin, Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin, and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao.

Xinhua News Agency, Tashkent, January 19th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage, after a brief stopover in Tashkent, left Tashkent on the morning of the 19th to visit Kabul, the capital of Afghanistan.

### 布尔加宁设宴欢送我政府代表团  苏联党和政府领导人到伏努科夫机场送别我代表团

1957-01-20
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁设宴欢送我政府代表团
    苏联党和政府领导人到伏努科夫机场送别我代表团
    新华社莫斯科19日电　苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁18日下午六时半在大克里姆林宫举行宴会，欢送以周恩来总理为首的中国政府代表团。
    出席宴会的，苏联方面有伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、谢皮洛夫、福尔采娃、什维尔尼克、波斯伯洛夫等。
    中国方面有，中国政府代表团全体团员和随行人员。访问保加利亚后在回国途中暂时在莫斯科停留的中国全国人民代表大会代表团部分代表也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会在友好热烈的气氛中进行。宾主双方一再为毛泽东主席、伏罗希洛夫主席的健康和两国人民的友谊干杯。布尔加宁主席在宴会上致词（另发）。周恩来总理接着致答词（另发）。
    宾主双方在宴会上还欣赏了一些苏联著名器乐演奏家和演员的精彩表演。
    宴会在八时许结束。当周恩来总理等离开会场的时候，大厅内奏起了中苏两国人民都熟悉的歌曲“莫斯科——北京”。
    新华社莫斯科19日电　应邀请到苏联访问的以周恩来总理为首的中国政府代表团18日晚十时（莫斯科时间）乘图—104喷气式专机离开莫斯科，前往阿富汗进行访问。
    在夜晚的伏努科夫机场上，十多部聚光灯照耀着。天空飘着白雪。莫斯科人赶到这个离市区三十公里的机场上来送别中国的贵宾们。
    前来欢送的人中，有苏联共产党和政府的领导人布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、谢皮洛夫、什维尔尼克等同志，苏联政府的部长们和将军们，以及各国外交使节们。
    中国驻苏联大使馆人员也到飞机场来送行。
    机场上密密麻麻地排列着欢送的人群。
    周恩来总理在苏联党和政府领导人陪同下步入机场。乐队奏起了中华人民共和国和苏联两国国歌。周恩来总理由布尔加宁主席陪同检阅了仪仗队。
    周恩来总理、贺龙副总理等和送行的人告别。
    布尔加宁主席致送别词（另发）。他最后用中国话说“再见！”
    周总理接着致答词（另发）。
    苏联党和政府领导人送客人们走向飞机。他们再一次握手告别。
    银白色的喷气式客机迅速地飞向夜空。周恩来总理等到塔什干以后，将换乘伊尔—十四式飞机前往阿富汗首都喀布尔。
    伴送周恩来总理等到塔什干去的，有苏联外交部副部长费德林、苏联驻中国大使尤金和中国驻苏联大使刘晓。
    新华社塔什干19日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来、副总理贺龙和他们的随行人员在塔什干作短暂的停留以后，19日上午离开塔什干前往阿富汗首都——喀布尔访问。
