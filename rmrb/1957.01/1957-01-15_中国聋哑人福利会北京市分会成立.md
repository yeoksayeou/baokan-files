Title: Beijing Branch of the Chinese Association for the Welfare of the Deaf and Dumb Established
Author:
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 14th: The Beijing branch of the Chinese Association for the Welfare of the Deaf and Dumb was established on the 13th. At the inaugural meeting, Ma Yuhuai, Hong Xueli (deaf person), and twenty-three others were elected as members of the branch. The organizational charter of the branch was also adopted. The charter stipulates that this organization is both an institution guiding the welfare work of the deaf and dumb under the leadership of the government and a mass organization of the deaf and dumb themselves. Its task is to improve the production skills, political and cultural level of the deaf and dumb, guide them to participate in the country's socialist construction, and promote the development of the welfare cause for the deaf and dumb.

### 中国聋哑人福利会北京市分会成立

1957-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    中国聋哑人福利会北京市分会成立
    新华社14日讯　中国聋哑人福利会北京市分会已在13日成立。成立大会上选出了马玉槐、洪雪立（聋人）等二十五人为分会委员，并且通过了分会的组织章程。章程规定：这个组织一方面是政府领导下的指导聋哑人福利工作的机构，同时又是聋哑人自己的群众组织。它的任务是提高聋哑人的生产技术和政治、文化水平，引导聋哑人参加国家社会主义建设，促进聋哑人福利事业的发展。
