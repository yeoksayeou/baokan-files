Title: My Military Friendship Delegation Visits Various Parts of Burma
Author:
Date: 1957-01-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 13th: The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, led by Marshal Ye Jianying, visited the famous Chauk oil field and refinery on the morning of the 10th. The delegation was warmly welcomed by 5,000 residents and oil workers when it arrived in this Burmese oil center on the afternoon of the 9th.

    The delegation left Chauk by plane on the 10th for Kalaw. Kalaw is in Shan State and is known for its beautiful scenery.

    On the morning of the 9th, accompanied by Brigadier General Kyaw Zaw, the delegation visited the ancient Burmese capital of Pagan and also Kyaukse. In Kyaukse, the delegation toured the various pagodas in decorated oxcarts.

    On the 7th, the delegation arrived in Lashio from Maymyo. Welcoming them were Lashio Mayor Colonel Chit Myaing and the Chinese Consul General in Lashio, Wang Ze. In the evening, Colonel Chit Myaing held a banquet to entertain the delegation. On the way from Maymyo to Lashio, the delegation visited the famous Gokteik viaduct.

    On the morning of the 8th, Marshal Ye Jianying received representatives of the overseas Chinese in Lashio at the Chinese Consulate General in Lashio.

### 我军事友好代表团在缅甸各地访问

1957-01-14
第5版()
专栏：

    我军事友好代表团在缅甸各地访问
    新华社仰光13日电　由叶剑英元帅率领的中国军事友好代表团在10日上午访问了著名的梢埠油田和炼油厂。代表团在9日下午到达这个缅甸石油中心的时候，受到五千居民和石油工人的热烈欢迎。
    代表团已经在10日乘飞机离梢埠去葛鲁。葛鲁在掸邦，以风景优美著称。
    9日上午，代表团由觉温准将陪同参观了缅甸古都卜岸以及凉古，代表团在凉古乘着装饰起来的牛车参观了各个宝塔。
    7日，代表团从眉缪到达腊戍。欢迎他们的有腊戍市长漆密上校和中国驻腊戍总领事王泽。当晚漆密上校举行宴会招待代表团。在从眉缪去腊戍的路上，代表团参观了著名的可低桥。
    8日上午，叶剑英元帅在中国驻腊戍总领事馆接见了腊戍的华侨代表。
