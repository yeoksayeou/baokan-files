Title: Panchen Erdini Departs to Return to China
Author:
Date: 1957-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 28th - Panchen Erdini and his entourage left Calcutta by special plane this morning to return to China.

　　Panchen was invited by the Indian government's committee for the commemoration of the 2,500th anniversary of the Nirvana of Sakyamuni to participate in the commemoration ceremony. During his stay in India, he visited many Indian cities and important Buddhist centers.

　　Panchen reviewed the guard of honor at Dum Dum Airport in Calcutta. With him was Liu Yufeng, the Chinese Consul General in Calcutta.

　　Among those seeing him off at the airport were Surati, Joint Secretary of the West Bengal Provincial Government, military officers, and the President of the Maha Bodhi Society. Xie Yingrui, a representative of overseas Chinese in the National People's Congress of China, and others also saw him off.

### 班禅额尔德尼启程回国

1957-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼启程回国
    新华社新德里28日电　班禅额尔德尼一行今晨乘专机离开加尔各答启程回国。
    班禅是应印度政府纪念释迦牟尼涅槃二千五百周年工作委员会的邀请来印度参加这次纪念仪式的。他在印度期间访问了印度的许多城市和重要的佛教中心。
  班禅在加尔各答的达姆达姆机场检阅了仪仗队。和他在一起的有中国驻加尔各答总领事柳雨峰。
    在机场上送行的有西孟加拉省政府联合秘书苏拉蒂、军官们和摩呵菩提会会长。送行的还有中国全国人民代表大会华侨代表谢应瑞等。
