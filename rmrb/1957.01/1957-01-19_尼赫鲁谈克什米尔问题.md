Title: Nehru Talks About the Kashmir Issue
Author:
Date: 1957-01-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 18th. Delhi newspapers prominently published news on the front page on the 17th regarding the United Nations Security Council's discussion of the Kashmir issue at Pakistan's request.

Indian Prime Minister Nehru said at a public gathering in Calcutta on the 16th: "Kashmir cannot be given away to Pakistan."

Nehru said: India opposes the methods Pakistan is taking to resolve the Kashmir issue. He said that Pakistan's stockpiling of large quantities of arms and ammunition undoubtedly caused India's unease.

Nehru said that the United States not only supplies arms to Pakistan, but also to countries in West Asia, which seemed strange to him. This makes the world situation even more difficult.

### 尼赫鲁谈克什米尔问题

1957-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁谈克什米尔问题
    新华社新德里18日电　德里报纸17日在头版以显著地位刊载了联合国安全理事会根据巴基斯坦的要求讨论克什米尔问题的消息。
    印度总理尼赫鲁16日在加尔各答的一次公众集会上说：“不能把克什米尔奉送给巴基斯坦。”
    尼赫鲁说：印度反对巴基斯坦采取的解决克什米尔问题的方法。他说，巴基斯坦储存大量武器弹药，无疑引起了印度的不安。
    尼赫鲁说，美国不仅把军火供给巴基斯坦，而且还供给西亚国家，这在他看来是很奇怪的。这使世界局势更加困难了。
