Title: Widespread Rain and Snow in Vast Areas of China
Author:
Date: 1957-01-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, January 28th: Starting from the 27th, China experienced its second large-scale rain and snow event this year.

    The range of this rain and snow includes: in the west, the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, Gansu, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Guizhou provinces, etc.; in the east, the eastern coastal areas; in the north, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the northeastern provinces; and in the south, all areas north of the Nanling Mountains.

    By noon on the 28th, the snowfall in most areas north of the Huai River was 3-5 millimeters, and the snow accumulation was one to two inches. The snowfall was heavier in western Shandong, southern Hebei, and southern Shanxi, where the snow accumulation was two to three inches.

    The rainfall in Jiangxi, Hunan, Hubei, and southern Anhui was 5-10 millimeters. This rain and snow is very beneficial to the growth of overwintering crops.

    Currently, the rain and snow area is moving eastward, and it is estimated that the weather in most parts of China will improve on the 29th and 30th.

### 我国广大地区普降雨雪

1957-01-30
第3版()
专栏：

    我国广大地区普降雨雪
    新华社28日讯　从27日开始，我国出现了今年的第二次范围较大的雨雪。
    这次雨雪的范围：西面包括新疆维吾尔自治区、甘肃、陕西、四川、贵州等省，东面到东部沿海，北面包括内蒙古自治区和东北各省，南面包括南岭以北各地。
    到28日中午止，淮河以北大部地区降雪量是三—五公厘，积雪厚度有一到二寸，其中以山东西部、河北南部、山西南部下的雪较多，这些地方积雪厚度有二寸到三寸。
    江西、湖南、湖北、安徽南部的雨量有五—十公厘。这场雨雪对越冬作物的生长非常有益。
    目前雨雪区正向东移，估计29到30日我国大部地区天气将好转。
