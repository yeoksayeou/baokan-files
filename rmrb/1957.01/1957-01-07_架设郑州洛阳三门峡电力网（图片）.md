Author: Tang Maolin

Construction Dynamics

    Erecting the Zhengzhou-Luoyang-Sanmenxia Power Grid
    The pole erection project for the Zhengzhou to Luoyang section of the Zhengzhou-Luoyang-Sanmenxia power grid has been basically completed. On this construction line, stretching over one hundred kilometers, workers are braving the severe cold to erect high-voltage transmission lines.
                                Xinhua News Agency reporter Tang Maolin photo

### 架设郑州洛阳三门峡电力网（图片）
唐茂林
1957-01-07
第2版()
专栏：建设动态

    架设郑州洛阳三门峡电力网
    郑州—洛阳—三门峡电力网郑州到洛阳一段的竖杆工程，已经基本完成。在这段长达一百多公里的施工线上，职工们正冒着严寒架设高压输电线。
                                新华社记者　唐茂林摄
