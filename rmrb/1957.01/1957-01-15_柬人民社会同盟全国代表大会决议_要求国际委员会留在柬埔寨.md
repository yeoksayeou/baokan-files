Title: Cambodian People's Socialist Community National Congress Resolution Demands International Commission Remain in Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 14th: The Fourth National Congress of the Cambodian People's Socialist Community passed a resolution requesting the International Supervisory and Monitoring Commission to continue to remain in Cambodia.

The question of the International Commission's continued presence was discussed for two days, and speakers expressed great respect for the commission. One speaker said, "Beside our country, there are still several unjust countries that frequently cause us trouble. The International Supervisory Commission will defend us, and this is very important."

### 柬人民社会同盟全国代表大会决议  要求国际委员会留在柬埔寨

1957-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    柬人民社会同盟全国代表大会决议
    要求国际委员会留在柬埔寨
    据新华社金边14日电　柬埔寨人民社会同盟第四届代表大会通过决议，要求国际监督和监察委员会继续留在柬埔寨。
    关于国际委员会留驻问题一共讨论了两天，发言的人们表示十分尊重这个委员会。一个发言的人说：“在我国旁边，仍然有几个不公正的国家，它们经常向我们找麻烦。国际监察委员会会保卫我们，而这也是十分重要的。”
