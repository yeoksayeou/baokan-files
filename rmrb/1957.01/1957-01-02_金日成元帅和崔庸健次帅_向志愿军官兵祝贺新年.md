Title: Marshal Kim Il-sung and Vice-Marshal Choi Yong-kun Extend New Year Greetings to Officers and Soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers

Author:
Date: 1957-01-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 1st – Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, wrote a letter to General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, General Li Zhimin, Political Commissar, and all officers and soldiers, extending New Year greetings and presenting a large number of gifts.

The letter stated: "I would like to extend my heartfelt gratitude to you and all officers and soldiers of the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers, who are working to transform the Korean armistice into a solid peace and are providing strong assistance to the post-war reconstruction efforts of the Korean people.

"As we welcome 1957, I extend New Year greetings to all of you officers and soldiers, and I wish you even more brilliant achievements in the cause of jointly defending the peace line and consolidating peace in Asia and the world, together with the Korean People's Army."

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 1st – Vice-Marshal Choi Yong-kun, Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, sent a telegram to General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and Political Commissar Li Zhimin on January 1st, extending New Year greetings.

The telegram stated that in the past year, the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers, together with the Korean People's Army, have defended our country's lines and the peaceful labor of the Korean people like an impregnable wall; and have given tremendous assistance to our country's reconstruction efforts. As we welcome the New Year of 1957, I am entrusted by the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and by the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, to extend my heartfelt and warm congratulations and gratitude to you, and through you to all the officers and soldiers of the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers.

Choi Yong-kun also wished the Chinese People's Volunteers even greater victories and achievements in the new year, in the common cause of safeguarding the freedom and happiness of the people of both Korea and China, and the peace and security of Asia.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 1st – Vice-Premier and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Choi Yong-kun, and leaders of the Chinese People's Volunteers exchanged New Year greetings today.

Vice-Marshal Choi Yong-kun wrote a letter today to General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, General Li Zhimin, Political Commissar, and all officers and soldiers, warmly congratulating them on the New Year and presenting many gifts. The letter stated that he was entrusted by the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and by the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to extend his heartfelt and warm congratulations and gratitude to you, and through you to all the officers and soldiers of the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers. The letter also wished the Chinese People's Volunteers new achievements in defending the freedom and happiness of the people of both Korea and China, and in safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

The leaders of the Chinese People's Volunteers sent a telegram to Vice-Marshal Choi Yong-kun today, extending New Year greetings to him and to all officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army.

The telegram stated that the mutual friendship between the Chinese People's Volunteers and the heroic Korean People's Army has been further strengthened in the past year. All officers and soldiers of the Volunteers have learned many valuable experiences from the Korean People's Army and have received the incomparably warm care and assistance of the Korean people and the People's Army. The telegram expressed heartfelt gratitude to the Korean people and the People's Army and wished the Korean people and the People's Army even greater achievements in the new year in the peaceful construction of Korea and in the cause of defending peace in Korea and Asia.

### 金日成元帅和崔庸健次帅  向志愿军官兵祝贺新年

1957-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成元帅和崔庸健次帅
    向志愿军官兵祝贺新年
    新华社平壤1日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅写信给中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将、政治委员李志民上将及全体官兵祝贺新年，并馈赠了大批礼物。
    贺信中说：“我谨向为了把朝鲜停战转变为巩固的和平，并给予朝鲜人民战后恢复建设事业以有力帮助的你们和英雄的中国人民志愿军全体官兵，致以衷心的感谢。
    “在迎接1957年的时候，我向你们全体官兵祝贺新年，并预祝你们在和朝鲜人民军一起坚守和平防线、巩固亚洲与世界和平的事业中，取得更加辉煌的成就。”
    新华社平壤1日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫相崔庸健次帅1日打电报给中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇和政治委员李志民，祝贺新年。
    贺电说，在过去的一年中，英雄的中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军一起，铜墙铁壁般地保卫了我国的防线和朝鲜人民的和平劳动；并且对我国的恢复建设事业给予极大的帮助。在迎接1957年新年的时候，我受朝鲜劳动党中央委员会和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的委托，向你们并通过你们向英雄的中国人民志愿军全体官兵致以衷心的热烈的祝贺和感谢。
    崔庸健还预祝中国人民志愿军在新的一年中，在保卫朝中两国人民的自由幸福和亚洲的和平和安全的共同事业中，取得更大的胜利和成就。
    新华社平壤1日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相兼民族保卫相崔庸健次帅和中国人民志愿军领导人今天互相祝贺新年。
    崔庸健次帅今天写信给中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将、政治委员李志民上将和全体官兵热烈祝贺新年，并且赠送了许多礼物。贺信中说，我受朝鲜劳动党中央委员会及朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的委托，向你们并通过你们向英雄的中国人民志愿军全体官兵致衷心的热烈的祝贺和感谢。贺信并预祝中国人民志愿军在捍卫朝中两国人民的自由幸福和保卫亚洲及世界和平的事业中取得新的成就。
    中国人民志愿军领导人今天打电报给崔庸健次帅，向他和朝鲜人民军全体官兵祝贺新年。
    电报中说，中国人民志愿军和英雄的朝鲜人民军的相互友谊在过去一年中又得到了进一步的增强，志愿军全体官兵从朝鲜人民军那里学习了许多宝贵经验，得到了朝鲜人民和人民军无比亲切的关怀与帮助。电报中对朝鲜人民和人民军表示衷心的感谢，并预祝朝鲜人民和人民军在新的一年里在朝鲜的和平建设和保卫朝鲜与亚洲和平的事业中获得更大的成就。
