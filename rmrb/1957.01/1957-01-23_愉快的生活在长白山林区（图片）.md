Title: Happy Life in the Changbai Mountain Forest Area (Picture)
Author: Lang Qi
Date: 1957-01-23
Page: 2

    Happy Life in the Changbai Mountain Forest Area

    The staff and workers in China's famous forest area, the Changbai Mountain Forest Area, live a hardworking and poetic life all year round. Now, the forest area, dressed in winter clothes, has a particularly attractive scenery, adding more fun to the lives of its owners.

          Photo by Lang Qi (Xinhua News Agency draft)

    Bottom right picture: The river in the forest area is a natural skating rink in winter. For many years, no one has used it. This year, skating enthusiasts have appeared for the first time.

    Right picture: Go to "Jinjiang" to fish for delicious fine-scaled fish.

    Bottom picture: Guan Xigui (right), an inspector at the Linjiang Forest Industry Bureau Health Center, is a novice hunter, but his results this Sunday are not bad.

### 愉快的生活在长白山林区（图片）
郎琦
1957-01-23
第2版()
专栏：

    愉快的生活在长白山林区
    在我国著名的林区——长白山林区工作的职工们，终年过着辛勤而富有诗意的生活。现在，穿上了冬装的林区别有一番诱人的景色，给它的主人增添了更多的生活乐趣。
          郎琦　摄（新华社稿）
    下右图：林区的河流在冬季是天然的滑冰场。多少年来，没有人利用它。今年，第一次出现了滑冰爱好者。
    右图：到“锦江”去钓味道鲜美的细鳞鱼。
    下图：临江森林工业局卫生所检验士关喜癸（右）虽然是个狩猎的新手，但是，这个星期天他的成绩并不坏。
