Title: Vietnam National Assembly Plenary Session Discusses Struggle for Reunification of Vietnam; South Vietnamese Delegates Suggest Approving Policy of Consolidating North Vietnam and Striving for South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1957-01-14
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 13th: The Vietnam National Assembly continued its plenary session on the afternoon of the 12th to discuss the issue of the struggle for the reunification of Vietnam.

　　Huang Van Hoan, a member of the Central Committee of the Democratic Party, delivered a report to the National Assembly on behalf of the United Struggle Subcommittee and suggested that the National Assembly approve the government's proposed policy of consolidating North Vietnam and striving for South Vietnam, to build the North into a base for striving for the reunification of the Fatherland.

　　Huang Van Hoan pointed out that North Vietnam is the tomorrow of South Vietnam. To help South Vietnam, North Vietnam must have sufficient strength in economic, political, and military aspects, and North Vietnam should move towards socialism.

　　This representative from Saigon and Cholon said that future efforts should continue to be made to implement the Geneva Accords, and efforts should be made to overcome the obstruction of the South Vietnamese authorities and to restore normal relations between the North and the South.

　　After Huang Van Hoan spoke, seven delegates spoke successively on the issue of the reunification struggle. They pointed out the conspiracy of the Ngo Dinh Diem regime to sabotage national reunification and talked about the struggle of the people of South Vietnam, the people of the demilitarized zone, the ethnic minorities of the Central Highlands, and overseas Vietnamese demanding reunification.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 13th: The sixth session of the Vietnam National Assembly entered the stage of general discussion on the 12th. Drafts passed by the twelve subcommittees on united struggle, land reform, finance and economy, social culture, foreign affairs, national defense, ethnic affairs, budget, draft laws, constitutional amendment, improvement of the National Assembly organization, and document drafting after two days of research and three days of meetings will be reported to the Assembly by representatives elected by each subcommittee and discussed over approximately ten days starting on the 12th.

　　At the meeting on the morning of the 12th, Pham Van Bach, Deputy Minister of the Interior of the Vietnamese government, and Tran Cong Tuong, Deputy Minister of Justice, respectively submitted to the Assembly proposals on the principles for formulating three laws guaranteeing freedom of association, freedom of assembly, and personal freedom.

### 越南国民大会全体会议  讨论统一越南的斗争问题  南越代表建议批准巩固北越争取南越的方针

1957-01-14
第5版()
专栏：

    越南国民大会全体会议
    讨论统一越南的斗争问题
    南越代表建议批准巩固北越争取南越的方针
    新华社河内13日电　越南国民大会12日下午继续举行全体会议，讨论统一越南的斗争的问题。
    民主党中央委员黄文想代表统一斗争小组委员会在向国民大会作报告，并且建议国民大会批准政府提出的巩固北越、争取南越的方针，把北方建成为争取统一祖国的基地。
    黄文想指出，北越是南越的明天，为了帮助南越，就要使北越在经济、政治、军事等方面有足够强大的力量，就应当使北越走向社会主义。
    这位西贡、堤岸的代表说，今后还应当继续为实现日内瓦协议而斗争，并且要克服南越当局的阻挠，设法恢复南北正常关系。
    黄文想发言后，有七个代表先后就统一斗争问题发言。他们指出了吴庭艳政权破坏国家统一的阴谋，并且谈到南越人民、非军事区人民、西原少数民族和国外越侨要求统一的斗争情形。
    新华社河内13日电　越南国民大会第六次会议从12日起进入大会讨论的阶段。由统一斗争、土地改革、财政经济、社会文化、外交、国防、民族、预算、法令草案、修改宪法、健全国民大会组织、文件起草等十二个小组委员会经过两天研究和三天的会议讨论后通过的草案，将在从12日开始的约十天中，由各小组委员会推选的代表向大会提出报告并进行讨论。
    在12日上午的会议上，越南政府内务部副部长范文白和司法部副部长陈公祥分别向大会提出了关于制定保障结社自由、集会自由和人身自由等三个法令的原则草案的提案。
