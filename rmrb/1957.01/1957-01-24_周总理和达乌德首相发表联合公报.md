Title: Premier Zhou and Prime Minister Daoud Issue Joint Communiqué
Author:
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, January 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China and Prime Minister Daoud of the Kingdom of Afghanistan signed a joint communiqué at 6:30 pm on the 22nd. The signing ceremony was held at the Sadarat Palace. On the Chinese side, Vice Premier He Long and Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Ding Guoyu also attended the signing ceremony; on the Afghan side, Vice Prime Minister Ali Mohammad and Vice Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Naim also attended.

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, January 23rd: Joint Communiqué of Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China, and Sardar Mohammad Daoud, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan

At the invitation of Sardar Mohammad Daoud, Prime Minister of Afghanistan, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Vice Premier He Long and their entourage, arrived in Kabul on January 19, 1957, for a five-day official visit to Afghanistan. During their stay in Kabul, the Premier and Vice Premier were received by His Majesty the King of Afghanistan and held talks with Prime Minister Sardar Mohammad Daoud, Vice Prime Minister Ali Mohammad, and Vice Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sardar Mohammad Naim. During the discussions, which took place in a friendly and warm atmosphere, the leaders of Afghanistan and China exchanged views on many issues; they discussed issues directly related to Afghanistan and China, as well as issues of international concern.

They reached a consensus on further strengthening the existing friendly ties between the two countries on the basis of good neighborly relations. They considered economic and cultural relations that would be beneficial to both countries and decided to consider the possibility of developing such relations. The two Prime Ministers reiterated their support for the principles of the Bandung Conference and reaffirmed their support for the cause of cooperation and understanding among the Asian and African peoples in maintaining their freedom and independence and promoting world peace. Both sides believe that cooperation among the participating countries of the Asian-African Conference does not exclude cooperation between them and other countries in the world.

### 周总理和达乌德首相发表联合公报

1957-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理和达乌德首相发表联合公报
    新华社喀布尔23日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和阿富汗王国首相达乌德在22日下午六时三十分签署了联合公报。签字仪式是在萨达拉特宫举行的。参加签字仪式的，中国方面还有副总理贺龙，中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰；阿富汗方面还有副首相阿里·穆罕默德和副首相兼外交大臣纳伊姆。
    新华社喀布尔23日电　中华人民共和国周恩来总理和阿富汗王国达乌德首相的联合公报
    中华人民共和国周恩来总理，在贺龙副总理和他们的随行人员的陪同下，应阿富汗首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德的邀请，于1957年1月19日抵达喀布尔，在阿富汗进行五天的正式访问。在他们停留喀布尔的期间，总理和副总理受到了阿富汗国王陛下的接见，并且同萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德首相、阿里·穆罕默德副首相和萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆副首相兼外交大臣举行了会谈。在友好热情的气氛中进行讨论的过程里，阿富汗和中国的领导人员交换了对许多问题的意见；他们讨论了对阿富汗和中国直接有关的问题和国际上关心的问题。
    他们对于在善邻关系的基础上，进一步加强两国现有的友好联系方面取得了一致意见。他们考虑了对两国都有利的经济和文化的关系，并且决定考虑发展这种关系的可能性。两国总理重申他们支持万隆会议的原则，并且重申他们支持亚非人民为维护他们的自由和独立、促进世界和平而进行合作和了解的事业的目的。双方认为，亚非会议参加国家之间的合作并不排斥它们和世界其他国家之间的合作。
    两国总理相信他们之间的上述了解反映了他们充分遵守万隆会议的精神、联合国宪章的原则和他们各自的国家政策。
    阿富汗首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德应中国周恩来总理以前的邀请，将在1957年对他方便的日期正式访问中华人民共和国。
                        1957年1月22日于喀布尔
          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
          阿富汗王国首相　萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德
