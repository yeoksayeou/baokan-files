Title: Syrian Government Appoints Three Ministers to Negotiate Federal Union with Egypt
Author: 
Date: 1957-01-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 10th. The Syrian government has appointed a ministerial delegation of three members to negotiate with Egypt on the issue of establishing a Syrian-Egyptian federal union.

The three ministers are: Defense Minister Khaled al-Azm, Economy Minister Khalil Kallas, and Public Works Minister Faher Kayyali.

Previous negotiations between Syria and Egypt on the issue of establishing a federal union were interrupted by the Anglo-French-Israeli aggression against Egypt. The Syrian government's appointed delegation will travel to Cairo in a few days to resume negotiations.

### 叙政府委派三位部长同埃及谈判建立联邦问题

1957-01-11
第6版()
专栏：

    叙政府委派三位部长同埃及谈判建立联邦问题
    新华社10日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚政府已经委派一个部长级的三人代表团，同埃及谈判关于建立叙利亚—埃及联邦的问题。
    这三位部长是：国防部长哈勒德·阿泽姆、经济部长哈利勒·卡拉斯和公共工程部长法赫尔·卡雅利。
    叙利亚同埃及过去就建立联邦问题举行的谈判，由于英、法、以色列发动侵埃战争而中断。叙利亚政府任命的三人代表团将在几天内前往开罗，以便重新开始谈判。
