Title: Important Research Being Conducted at Shenyang Pneumatic Tool Factory: Using Domestic Steel to Replace Imported Materials
Author:
Date: 1957-01-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 22nd: The Shenyang Pneumatic Tool Factory is researching the use of various steels and nodular cast iron smelted domestically to replace imported nickel-chromium steel and chromium steel in the manufacture of pneumatic tools.

　　Since the beginning of January, the factory has been using carbon steel instead of chromium steel to produce three components of rock drills. The six components of pneumatic tools manufactured using various carbon steels and nodular cast iron to replace nickel-chromium steel and chromium steel are also undergoing testing in non-ferrous metal mines and iron mines. Foreign technical data indicates that pistons made of nickel-chromium steel generally last for 600 to 800 hours. The pistons made of carbon tool steel have already worked for over 400 hours and are still in use.

　　The factory produces a large number of rock drills, which previously used more than 73% nickel-chromium steel and chromium steel. The factory's central laboratory began experimenting with using domestic materials to manufacture pneumatic tools in the second half of last year. Currently, this experimental research has been expanded to relevant departments within the factory. If successful, the manufacture of various pneumatic tools will likely no longer require nickel-chromium steel and chromium steel.

### 沈阳风动工具厂进行重要的研究  用本国钢铁代替进口材料

1957-01-23
第2版()
专栏：

    沈阳风动工具厂进行重要的研究
    用本国钢铁代替进口材料
    新华社沈阳22日电　沈阳风动工具厂正在研究采用本国冶炼的各种钢和球墨铸铁，代替国外进口的镍铬钢和铬钢制造风动工具。
    从1月初起，这个厂生产凿岩机上的三种部件，就改用碳钢代替铬钢了。用各种碳钢和球墨铸铁代替镍铬钢和铬钢制造的风动工具上的六种部件，也正在有色金属矿和铁矿进行试验。外国的技术资料记载，用镍铬钢制造的活塞，一般使用六百到八百个小时，他们用碳工具钢制造的活塞，已经工作了四百多小时，目前还在继续使用。
    这个工厂大批生产的凿岩机，过去使用73％以上的镍铬钢和铬钢。这个厂的中心试验室，早在去年下半年就试验用本国材料制造风动工具。目前，这一试验研究工作，已扩大到厂内有关的部门。成功以后，再制造各种风动工具，估计就可以不用镍铬钢和铬钢了。
