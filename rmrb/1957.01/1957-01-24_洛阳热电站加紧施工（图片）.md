Title: Luoyang Thermal Power Plant Speeds Up Construction (Picture)
Author: Zhang Zhuquan
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 2

    Luoyang Thermal Power Plant Speeds Up Construction
    The Luoyang Thermal Power Plant, which will supply ample power to the First Tractor Factory and other factories, mines, and construction sites, is under intense construction. After the plant is completed, its initial output will be five times the existing power generation capacity of Luoyang, and later it will be more than twelve times greater. This is a corner of the construction site.
    Photo by Zhang Zhuquan (Contribution from Henan Daily)

### 洛阳热电站加紧施工（图片）
张祝泉
1957-01-24
第2版()
专栏：

    洛阳热电站加紧施工
    为供应第一拖拉机厂和其它厂矿、基建工地充沛动力的洛阳热电站正紧张施工。这座工厂建成后，在初期将等于洛阳现有发电量的五倍，后期将等于十二倍以上。这是工地一角。
    张祝泉摄    （河南日报社稿）
