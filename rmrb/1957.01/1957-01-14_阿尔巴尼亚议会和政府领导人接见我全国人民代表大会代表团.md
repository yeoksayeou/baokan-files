Title: Albanian Parliamentary and Government Leaders Receive Delegation from the National People's Congress of China
Author:
Date: 1957-01-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, January 13th: The Chairman of the Albanian People's Assembly, Marko, received the delegation of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, headed by Peng Zhen, on the afternoon of the 11th.

Present at the reception were Vice-Chairman of the People's Assembly, F. Shehu; Member of the Presidium of the People's Assembly, S. Bektashi; and Member of the People's Assembly and Vice-Chairman of the Democratic Front, Mirdal Shtura.

Xu Yixin, the Chinese Ambassador to Albania, was also present at the reception.

On the same evening, the delegation enjoyed a performance of Albanian song and dance.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, January 13th: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, Shehu, received the Chinese National People's Congress delegation, headed by Peng Zhen, on the 12th.

Present at the reception were Albanian government leaders Nushi, Baluku, Koleka, Muftiu, Teodosi, Kellezi and Shtura, among others.

Accompanying the delegation were Baholli, Secretary of the Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly, and Xu Yixin, the Chinese Ambassador to Albania. On the same day, the Chairman of the Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly, H. Leshi, also received the Chinese National People's Congress delegation headed by Peng Zhen.

### 阿尔巴尼亚议会和政府领导人接见我全国人民代表大会代表团

1957-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚议会和政府领导人接见我全国人民代表大会代表团
    新华社地拉那13日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席马尔科在11日下午接见了以彭真为首的中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会代表团。
    接见时在座的有人民议会副主席费·谢胡，人民议会主席团委员萨·贝克台西，人民议会议员、民主阵线副主席米达尔·什图拉。
    接见时，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使徐以新也在座。
    当天晚上，代表团欣赏了阿尔巴尼亚的歌舞节目。
    新华社地拉那13日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡在12日接见了以彭真为首的中国全国人民代表大会代表团。
    接见的时候在座的，有阿尔巴尼亚政府领导人努什、巴卢库、科列加、穆夫蒂乌、蒂奥多西、凯列齐和什图拉等。
    陪同代表团前往的是阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团秘书巴霍利和中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使徐以新。同日，阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席哈·列希也接见了以彭真为首的中国全国人民代表大会代表团。
