Title: Cairo Airport Open Day and Night
Author:
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 11th: The "Al-Ahram" newspaper reported on the 10th that, starting from the 9th, Cairo Airport has resumed 24-hour operation.

The newspaper stated that British and French transit passengers are allowed to land, but during their stay, they are only allowed to remain in the transit area and are not allowed to enter Cairo or have contact with people outside the airport.

### 开罗机场昼夜开放

1957-01-12
第6版()
专栏：

    开罗机场昼夜开放
    新华社开罗11日电　“金字塔报”10日报道，从9日开始，开罗机场恢复每天二十四小时开放。
    这家报纸说，英国和法国的过境旅客准许着陆，不过在他们停留期间，只准留在过境区内，不准进入开罗或者同机场外面的人接触。
