Title: Soviet Factories Save Metal
Author: Jun Qiang
Date: 1957-01-20
Page: 5

Soviet Factories Save Metal

The Gorky Automobile Factory in the Soviet Union achieved considerable success in saving metal last year. In one year, rationalization proposers and inventors put forward more than 15,000 proposals. Thanks to the innovators' improvement of technological operating procedures and the adoption of new technologies, the amount of metal required for automobiles has been reduced. For example, each "GAZ-51" truck used 19 kilograms less metal; each "Victory" car used 5 kilograms less metal. In this way, the factory saved a total of 10,000 tons of metal and 35 million rubles throughout the year.

Not long ago, the factory held another inspection on saving metal, and rationalization proposers and inventors actively participated in this work. They studied and adopted new and more perfect technological regulations, and continued to work hard to reduce the weight of automobiles on the premise of ensuring quality, and further strive to save metal.

＊　＊　＊

The largest Leningrad "Elektrosila" power machinery manufacturing plant in the Soviet Union has made great achievements in the struggle for technological progress last year. Within one year, the factory produced more than 20 new machines. "Elektrosila" factory products are not only famous throughout the country, but also exported to fifteen countries.

The factory has now begun to manufacture turbo-generators with a power generation capacity of 200,000 kilowatts. It is not only the first time to manufacture such huge turbo-generators in the "Elektrosila" factory, but also the first time in the entire Soviet Union. Due to the adoption of advanced technology, the weight of this machine has been greatly reduced. As a result, this powerful 200,000-kilowatt generator will be almost 40 tons lighter than a 150,000-kilowatt generator. This will of course save a lot of metal.

＊　＊　＊

The Vorobyov factory in Gorky City uses substitutes to save metal when manufacturing flour milling machines. This year, the factory plans to manufacture 1,100 flour milling machines. Each flour milling machine originally required 20 cast iron pipes, each weighing 4 kilograms. Now a new method of using plastic to manufacture this part has been developed, without affecting the quality of the machine at all. In this way, 88 tons of cast iron can be saved this year from this part alone. (Jun Qiang)

### 苏联工厂节约金属
君强
1957-01-20
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联工厂节约金属
    苏联高尔基城的汽车制造厂去年在节约金属方面曾获得了不少成绩。一年之间，合理化建议者和发明者提出了一万五千多项建议。由于革新者们改进工艺操作规程和采用新技术，使汽车所需的金属量减少了。例如，每一部“卡兹—51”牌载重汽车便少用了十九公斤金属；每一部“胜利”牌小汽车少用了五公斤金属。这样，工厂全年总共节约了一万吨金属，三千五百万卢布。
    不久以前工厂又举行了一次关于节约金属的检查，合理化建议者和发明者们都积极参加了这项工作。他们研究和采用新的更完善的工艺规程，在保证质量的条件下继续努力减低汽车的重量，进一步为节约金属而斗争。
            ＊　＊　＊
    苏联最大的列宁格勒的“电力”动力机器制造厂，去年在争取技术进步方面获得了巨大成绩。一年之内，工厂制出了二十多种新机器。“电力”工厂的出品不仅名闻全国，而且还向十五个国家输出。
    现在工厂已经开始制造发电能力为二十万瓩的涡轮发电机，制造这样巨大的涡轮发电机，不仅在“电力”工厂是第一次，而且在苏联全国也是第一次。由于采用了先进的工艺技术，便大大减低了这种机器的重量。结果，这种强大的发电能力为二十万瓩的发电机，将比发电能力为十五万瓩的发电机的重量几乎要轻四十吨。这当然就会节省大量金属。
          ＊　＊　＊
    高尔基城的伏罗比约夫工厂在制造磨面机时采用代用品来节约金属。今年这个工厂规定要制造一千一百架磨面机。每架磨面机上原需要二十个生铁制造的每个重四公斤的管子，现在已研究出用塑料来制造这种零件的新方法，而且毫不影响机器质量。这样，只从这一种零件上今年即可节约八十八吨生铁。　（君强）
