Title: Preparatory Committee of the China Association for Promoting Democracy Established in Chengdu

Author:
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, January 14th. The Preparatory Committee of the China Association for Promoting Democracy in Chengdu was established in Chengdu on the 13th.

The chairman of the preparatory committee is Yang Lizhi, and the vice chairman is Zhou Nengpan.

The eleven members of the preparatory committee include Tan Zhaowen, a renowned and outstanding mathematics teacher in Chengdu who has been teaching for fifty years.

### 中国民主促进会成都市筹委会成立

1957-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    中国民主促进会成都市筹委会成立
    新华社成都14日电　中国民主促进会成都市筹备委员会13日在成都成立。
    筹备委员会的主任委员是杨立之，副主任委员是周能泮。
    筹备委员会的十一位委员中，包括任教已五十年的成都著名优秀数学教师谭肇闻等。
