Title: Dunhuang Art Exhibition Opens in Poland
Author:
Date: 1957-01-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 11th: A Chinese Dunhuang art exhibition officially opened on the 10th in the exhibition hall of the Palace of Culture and Science in Warsaw. Polish government officials, people from all walks of life in Warsaw, and Chinese Ambassador Wang Bingnan attended the opening ceremony.

    Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, personally cut the ribbon. The Polish Minister of Culture and Art and Ambassador Wang Bingnan both delivered speeches at the opening ceremony. They pointed out the significance of this exhibition in promoting friendly relations between China and Poland.

    The exhibition featured seventy copied paintings and twenty-seven photographs. The audience highly praised the ancient Chinese painting art.

    After visiting, Cyrankiewicz wrote in the guestbook: "We Polish people, after visiting this exhibition, are filled with admiration for the ancient culture of the great Chinese people."

### 敦煌艺术展览会在波开幕

1957-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    敦煌艺术展览会在波开幕
    新华社华沙11日电　中国敦煌艺术展览会10日在华沙文化科学宫的展览大厅里正式开幕，波兰政府负责人员、华沙各界人士和中国大使王炳南等都出席了开幕式。
    波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹亲自剪彩。波兰文化艺术部长和王炳南大使都在开幕式上致词。他们指出了这次展览会对促进中波友好关系的意义。
    展览会共展出了七十幅临摹画和二十七幅照片。观众对中国古代绘画艺术极为赞扬。
    西伦凯维兹参观后在纪念簿上写道：“我们波兰人参观了这个展览以后，心里充满了对伟大中国人民的古老文化的赞佩。”
