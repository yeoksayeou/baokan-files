Title: Japanese Diet Member Yoshio Tomori Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - Japanese Socialist Party Diet member Yoshio Tomori, who visited China at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association, left Beijing by plane on the morning of January 2nd and returned to his country via Guangzhou.

Yoshio Tomori arrived in Beijing on December 25th of last year. During his time in Beijing, he and the Chinese Medical Association agreed to send medical delegations to each other in March 1957 to further exchange experiences in medicine and health, and to enhance friendly exchanges between medical professionals from China and Japan.

### 日本议员堂森芳夫回国

1957-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    日本议员堂森芳夫回国
    新华社3日讯　应中华医学会的邀请来中国访问的日本社会党国会议员堂森芳夫在2日上午乘飞机离开北京，经广州回国。
    堂森芳夫在去年12月25日到达北京。他在北京期间曾经和中华医学会商定在1957年3月间双方互派医学代表团，进一步交流两国在医药卫生方面的经验，增进中日两国医学界人士的友好往来。
