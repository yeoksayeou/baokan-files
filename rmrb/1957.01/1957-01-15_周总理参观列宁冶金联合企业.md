Title: Premier Zhou Visits the Lenin Metallurgical Combine

Author: 
Date: 1957-01-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Krakow, January 14th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long, accompanied by Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz, arrived at the Lenin Metallurgical Combine, the base of Polish socialist construction, at 12:30 on the 13th for a visit. They were warmly welcomed by the workers.

In a hall of the combine's office building, the enterprise's manager, Cichowicz, welcomed the distinguished Chinese guests on behalf of all the workers and introduced the enterprise's production situation to Premier Zhou Enlai. He presented gifts to the Chinese guests, including a photo album introducing the enterprise's development.

Premier Zhou Enlai, in thanking his hosts for their warm reception, said: This factory is the result of the Polish working class's labor and also the result of Soviet-Polish friendship and cooperation. Premier Zhou Enlai also congratulated the workers of the Lenin Metallurgical Combine, the Polish working class, and the Polish people on their achievements in the name of the workers of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, the Chinese working class, and the Chinese people.

Afterwards, Premier Zhou Enlai and others, accompanied by their hosts, toured various parts of the combine by car. The enterprise covers an area of more than 2,500 acres. The car traveled within the factory, which resembled a city. At each major production department, Premier Zhou Enlai and others got out of the car to observe. At this time, the workers who had gathered together warmly cheered the Chinese guests. Premier Zhou Enlai waved to them in greeting.

During the visit, Polish Minister of Metallurgical Industry, Żemajtis, told Premier Zhou that a significant portion of the enterprise's products are exported to China. Premier Zhou Enlai inquired in detail about the production process, quality, and specifications of various steel ingots and steel plates.

Premier Zhou Enlai and others did not leave the enterprise until around 2:00 PM, by which time it was snowing heavily.

### 周总理参观列宁冶金联合企业

1957-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理参观列宁冶金联合企业
    新华社克拉科夫14日电　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理由波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹陪同，在13日十二时半到达波兰社会主义建设的基地——列宁冶金联合企业访问参观，他们受到了职工们的热烈欢迎。
    在联合企业的办公大楼的一个大厅内，企业经理契霍维奇代表全体职工欢迎中国贵宾，并且向周恩来总理介绍了这个企业的生产情况。他向中国客人们赠送了礼品，其中有介绍企业发展情况的照片册一本。
    周恩来总理在感谢主人们的热情接待时说：这个工厂是波兰工人阶级的劳动成果，同时也是波苏友谊和合作的成果。周恩来总理还以鞍山钢铁公司工人、中国工人阶级和中国人民的名义祝贺列宁冶金联合企业工人、波兰工人阶级和波兰人民的成就。
    接着，周恩来总理等在主人陪同下坐汽车参观联合企业的各个部分。这个企业占地二千五百多英亩。汽车在宛如城市的工厂内行进，每到一个主要的生产部门，周恩来总理等都下车观看。这时，聚集在一起的工人们热烈地向中国客人欢呼。周恩来总理向他们挥手致意。
    在参观的时候，波兰冶金工业部部长热马伊蒂斯告诉周总理这个企业有相当大的一部分产品是输往中国的。周恩来总理详细地询问了各种钢锭、钢板的生产过程、质量和规格。
    到下午二时许，周恩来总理等才离开这个企业，这时天在下着大雪。
