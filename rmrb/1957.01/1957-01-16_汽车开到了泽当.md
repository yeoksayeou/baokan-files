Title: Cars Arrive in Tsetang
Author:
Date: 1957-01-16
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Tsetang, January 14th: The first batch of cars from Lhasa arrived in the ancient city of Tsetang on the banks of the Yarlung Zangbo River on the 12th.

　　At 1:00 PM, the convoy led by a Jiefang brand truck entered the welcoming arch, and people waiting in the heavy dust offered bouquets to the decorated vehicles. Elders holding "Quma" (a gift for honoring the gods, consisting of zanba and highland barley in a pot, decorated with flowers and ears of wheat) and wine jugs sprinkled zanba and highland barley on the cars and offered highland barley wine to the drivers.

　　Representatives from more than twenty Dzong and Kyi valleys along the Yarlung Zangbo River and the Himalayan mountains participated in the celebration of the opening of the Lhasa-Tsetang highway. Representatives from the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, the Shannan Kijiu-level Office, and the road construction headquarters said at the celebration that the opening of the Lhasa-Tsetang highway will promote the political, economic, and cultural development of the region.

### 汽车开到了泽当

1957-01-16
第2版()
专栏：

    汽车开到了泽当
    据新华社泽当14日电　第一批从拉萨开来的汽车，12日进入雅鲁藏布江边的古城泽当。
    下午一时，解放牌汽车领头的车队进入欢迎的彩门，在大风沙中等待的人们向彩车献了花束。捧着“曲马”（盆内装着糌粑、青稞，上面插着花束、麦穗，用以敬神的礼品）和酒壶的老人，向汽车撒糌粑、青稞，给司机敬了青稞酒。
    这天参加庆祝拉泽公路通车的有雅鲁藏布江沿岸和喜马拉雅山区二十多个宗溪的代表。西藏自治区筹委会代表、山南基巧级办事处和修路指挥部的负责人在庆祝会上说，拉泽公路通车，将促进这个地区的政治、经济和文化的发展。
