Title: Chairman of the Belgrade City People's Committee Hosts Banquet to Welcome Delegation of the National People's Congress of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1957-01-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, January 21st - The Chairman of the Belgrade City People's Committee, Minic, hosted a banquet at the Royal Palace Hotel here on the 20th at noon to welcome all members of the Chinese National People's Congress delegation and the Beijing City People's Committee delegation visiting Yugoslavia. The Yugoslav side attending the banquet included: Vice-Chairman of the Federal National Assembly Simic, Chairman of the Federal Chamber Zekovic, Chairman of the Producers' Chamber Bozicovic, Secretary of the Belgrade City Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia Jojkic, Vice-Chairman of the Belgrade City People's Committee and heads of various departments, and more than fifty others.

Zhou Qiuye, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Yugoslavia, was also invited to attend the banquet.

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of friendly unity and free conversation. During the banquet, Minic, Chairman of the Belgrade City People's Committee, and Peng Zhen, head of the delegation and Mayor of Beijing, spoke successively.

Minic said that he was particularly satisfied that the visit of the Beijing City People's Committee delegation, headed by the Mayor of Beijing, enabled direct contact between the capitals of China and Yugoslavia. He expressed his belief that mutual ties and friendly relations between China and Yugoslavia would continue to strengthen in the future. Minic said, "I sincerely hope that you will feel at home here, in Belgrade, and throughout our country."

Peng Zhen said that the friendly and cooperative relations between the people of China and Yugoslavia have continuously gained new development in the past two years, and the friendship between the people of Beijing and Belgrade is also increasing day by day. Peng Zhen said, "During this visit of our delegation to Belgrade, we learned about many of your actual situations, learned some advanced experiences, and received the warm friendship and welcome from you and the people. We feel very excited and happy."

Peng Zhen concluded, "I am honored and pleased to invite the Chairman of the Belgrade City People's Committee and its delegation to visit Beijing at a time you deem appropriate on behalf of the Beijing City People's Committee and myself personally. I believe that direct contact and connection between the two capitals, exchange of experience and mutual learning will certainly play a beneficial role in our socialist construction, in enhancing the friendship and unity between the two peoples, and in world peace."

Mayor Peng Zhen's invitation has been accepted by Minic.

That morning, part of the delegation held a forum with Minic, Chairman of the Belgrade City People's Committee, the Vice-Chairman, and heads of various departments, exchanging experiences on municipal issues. At the same time, part of the delegation visited the city of Belgrade.

All members of the delegation will visit several republics of Yugoslavia starting from the 21st.

### 贝尔格莱德市人民委员会主席举行宴会  招待我国人民代表大会代表团

1957-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    贝尔格莱德市人民委员会主席举行宴会
    招待我国人民代表大会代表团
    新华社贝尔格莱德21日电　贝尔格莱德市人民委员会主席米尼奇20日中午在这里的皇宫饭店设宴招待正在南斯拉夫访问的中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团的全体人员。南斯拉夫方面出席宴会的有：联邦国民议会副主席西米奇、联邦院主席泽契维奇、生产者院主席鲍日契维奇、南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟贝尔格莱德市委员会书记约伊基奇、贝尔格莱德市人民委员会副主席和各部门负责人等五十多人。
    中国驻南斯拉夫大使馆参赞周秋野也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会在友好团结、自由交谈的气氛中进行。席间，贝尔格莱德市人民委员会主席米尼奇和代表团团长、北京市市长彭真先后讲了话。
    米尼奇说，他感到格外满意的是，以北京市市长为首的北京市人民委员会代表团的来访使中国和南斯拉夫的首都之间能够进行直接的接触。他表示相信，今后中、南两国之间的相互联系和友好关系将会继续加强。米尼奇说：“我真诚地希望你们在这里、在贝尔格莱德以及在我国各地都会感到就像在你们自己的家里一样。”
    彭真说，中南两国人民的友好合作关系近两年来不断地获得了新的发展，北京市和贝尔格莱德市人民的友谊也在日益增进。彭真说，“这次我们的代表团在贝尔格莱德访问，了解了你们许多实际情况，学习了一些先进经验，受到了你们和人民群众的深厚友谊的接待和欢迎，我们感到很兴奋愉快。”
    彭真最后说，“我很光荣地、愉快地以北京市人民委员会和我个人的名义，邀请贝尔格莱德市人民委员会主席同志和它的代表团在你们认为适当的时间访问北京。我相信通过两国首都的直接接触和联系、交流经验和相互学习，对于我们的社会主义建设事业、对于增进两国人民的友谊和团结、对于世界和平必将起有益的作用。”
    彭真市长的这项邀请已经为米尼奇所接受。
    当天上午，代表团的一部分人员同贝尔格莱德市人民委员会主席米尼奇、副主席和各部门负责人举行了座谈，交流有关市政问题的经验。同时，代表团的一部分人员参观了贝尔格莱德的市容。
    代表团的全体人员将从21日起，到南斯拉夫的几个共和国去参观访问。
