Title: January 16th Designated as "Constitution Day" in Egypt

Author:

Date: 1957-01-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 15th: According to Cairo Radio, Egyptian President Nasser signed a decree on the 14th designating January 16th as Egypt's "Constitution Day."

The radio station said that the entire country will have a holiday on that day. Egypt's constitution was promulgated on January 16, 1956.

### 一月十六日埃及定为“宪法日”

1957-01-17
第6版()
专栏：

    一月十六日埃及定为“宪法日”
    新华社15日讯　开罗消息：据开罗电台广播，埃及总统纳赛尔在14日签署了一项法令，规定1月16日为埃及的“宪法日”。
    电台说，这一天全国将放假。埃及的宪法是在1956年1月16日颁布的。
