Title: Our Military Delegation Visits Southern Burma
Author:
Date: 1957-01-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 19th. The Chinese military friendship delegation led by Marshal Ye Jianying left Loikaw, the capital of Kayah State, on the 17th to visit southern Burma in Mawlamyine.

　　On the morning of the 15th, the delegation arrived in Loikaw from Loilem by plane as guests of Kayah State Chief Sao Wunna. Brigadier General Clift, Deputy Chief of Staff (Air Force) of the Burmese Defense Forces, accompanied the delegation. The delegation toured Loikaw on the 16th and visited historical sites.

　　The delegation left Taunggyi on the 13th for Loilem. During their visit to Loilem, the delegation visited the Myanmar Army Combat Training School and the Defense Forces Officer Training School, and observed various live-fire military exercises.

　　Colonel Kyaw Zaw, Principal of the Myanmar Army Combat Training School, hosted a banquet for the delegation on the evening of the 14th. Those attending the banquet included Brigadier General Clift, Colonel Aung Gyi, and the military attachés of the embassies of India, Yugoslavia, Indonesia, the United Kingdom, France, and Australia in Burma. Burmese officers and foreign military attachés spoke with Marshal Ye Jianying at the banquet.

### 我军事代表团访问南缅

1957-01-20
第5版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团访问南缅
    新华社仰光19日电　由叶剑英元帅率领的中国军事友好代表团17日离开加耶邦首府乐可前往毛淡棉访问南缅。
    15日上午，代表团作为加耶邦邦长萧翁纳的贵宾从腊撒乘飞机到达乐可。陪同代表团前往的有缅甸国防军副总参谋长（空军）克利夫特准将。代表团在16日游览了乐可，并且参观了古迹。
    代表团是在13日离开东枝前往腊撒的。在访问腊撒期间，代表团参观了缅甸陆军作战部队学校和国防军军官学校，并且参观了各种实弹军事演习。
    缅甸陆军作战部队学校校长觉梭上校在14日晚上设宴招待代表团。参加宴会的有克利夫特准将，昂季上校，以及印度、南斯拉夫、印度尼西亚、英国、法国和澳大利亚驻缅甸大使馆的武官。缅甸军官和外国武官在宴会上曾经同叶剑英元帅交谈。
