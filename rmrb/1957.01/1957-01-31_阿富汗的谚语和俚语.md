Afghan Proverbs and Slang
　　Someone asked the camel: “Why is your neck crooked?”
　　“What part of me is straight?” the camel laughed.
　　A person bitten by a snake fears colored ribbons.
　　A torn silk dress cannot even be used as a donkey's saddle.
　　Fox fur often brings disaster to the fox.
　　Boast to the enemy, tell the truth to friends.
　　Don't take off your straw sandals until you get to the water.

　　Zhang Guangqi Selected and Translated

### 阿富汗的谚语和俚语
张光启
1957-01-31
第7版()
专栏：

    阿富汗的谚语和俚语
    有人问骆驼：“为什么你的脖子是弯曲的？”
    “我的身上又有什么地方是平直的呢？”骆驼笑了起来。
    被蛇咬过的人害怕彩色带子。
    穿破了的绸缎衣服甚至不能用作驴子的鞍子。
    狐皮常常给狐狸带来灾难。
    对敌人要夸口，对朋友要讲真话。
    没有走到水边，不要脱草鞋。
        张光启　选译
