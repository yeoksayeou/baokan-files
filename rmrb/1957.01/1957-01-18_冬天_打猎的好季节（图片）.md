Date: 1957-01-18

    Winter - A Good Season for Hunting
    The Qianjin Agricultural Cooperative in Gulang County, at the foot of the Qilian Mountains in Gansu Province, organized six hunting groups to carry out winter hunting activities. The hunting groups are constantly achieving victories. This fox's howling is useless now; how happy the cooperative members of the hunting group are!
            Photo by He Furen
    The hunting group of the Liming Agricultural Cooperative in Tieli County, Heilongjiang Province, hunted more than fifty wild boars in fifty days in winter, earning more than 1,000 yuan. Several cooperative members are dragging their hunted wild boars back victoriously.
            Photo by Yu Min (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 冬天——打猎的好季节（图片）

1957-01-18
第3版()
专栏：

    冬天——打猎的好季节
    甘肃省祁连山下的古浪县前进农业社，组织了六个打猎组，开展冬季打猎活动。打猎组不断得到胜利。这只狐狸再嚎叫也没有用了；打猎组的社员多么高兴！
            何富仁　摄
    黑龙江省铁力县黎明农业社的打猎小组，在冬季的五十天里，就打了五十多只野猪，收入一千多元。几个社员正拖着他们猎获的野猪胜利而归。
            于敏摄（新华社稿）
