Title: Celebration of the Independence Day of the Union of Burma: Burmese Embassy in China Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1957-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Premier Zhou Enlai attended a reception given by U Ba Maung, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy in China, this evening to celebrate the Independence Day of the Union of Burma.

At the reception, attended by more than 400 Chinese and foreign guests, Premier Zhou Enlai proposed a toast to the lasting friendship between China and Burma, to the development of friendly cooperation between the two peoples, to the prosperity of the Union of Burma and the prosperity of its people, and to the good health of President Ba U of the Union of Burma.

Premier Zhou Enlai said that he had just returned from southern Asia, where he had paid friendly visits to five Asian countries, including the Union of Burma, and that he was pleased to be able to attend the reception celebrating the Independence Day of the Union of Burma before the start of another trip.

Premier Zhou Enlai also met and talked with the heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China.

At the beginning of the reception, U Ba Maung, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy, asked his guests to join him in a toast to the lasting friendship between China and Burma, to the prosperity of the great and friendly People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, and to the good health of Chairman Mao Zedong.

This evening, Vice Premier Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman Shen Junru, Vice Chairman Huang Yanpei, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian attended the reception. Among the guests were also heads of various departments of Chinese state organs, generals, and people from all walks of life in Beijing.

### 庆祝缅甸联邦独立日  缅甸驻华大使馆举行招待会

1957-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝缅甸联邦独立日
    缅甸驻华大使馆举行招待会
    新华社4日讯　周恩来总理在今天晚间出席了缅甸驻中国大使馆临时代办吴巴茂为庆祝缅甸联邦独立日举行的招待会。
    在这个有四百多位中外人士参加的招待会上，周恩来总理提议为中缅两国持久的友谊，为两国人民友好合作关系的发展，为缅甸联邦国家的繁荣和人民的富强，为缅甸联邦巴宇总统的身体健康干杯。
    周恩来总理说，他刚刚从亚洲南部回来，他曾到包括缅甸联邦在内的五个亚洲国家进行了友好访问，他高兴能在他的另一次旅行开始之前参加庆祝缅甸联邦独立日的招待会。
    周恩来总理还同各国驻中国的使节和外交官员们一一会面交谈。
    在招待会开始的时候，缅甸大使馆临时代办吴巴茂请他的客人和他一起，为缅中两国间的持久的友谊、为伟大的和友好的中华人民共和国和中国人民的繁荣、为毛泽东主席的身体健康干杯。
    今晚，彭德怀副总理、李富春副总理、沈钧儒副委员长、黄炎培副委员长和外交部副部长张闻天都出席了招待会。客人中还有中国国家机关各部门的负责人、将军们和北京社会各界人士。
