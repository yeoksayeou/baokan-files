Title: Soviet Leaders Receive Czechoslovak Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-01-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, January 26th—TASS reports: Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov; Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Bulganin; and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, successively received the delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic headed by Czechoslovak President Zápotocký on January 25th, who are in Moscow for a state visit.

On the same day, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Gromyko, also received Czechoslovak Republic delegation member and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David.

### 苏联领导人接见捷代表团

1957-01-27
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联领导人接见捷代表团
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、部长会议主席布尔加宁、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫25日先后接见了到莫斯科作国事访问的、以捷克斯洛伐克总统萨波托斯基为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团。
    苏联外交部第一副部长葛罗米柯也在同一天接见了捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团团员、外交部长戴维。
