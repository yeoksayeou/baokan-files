Title: Zhenjiang Vinegar Resumes Export
Author: Zhang Wenjing
Date: 1957-01-24
Page: 2

    Zhenjiang Vinegar Resumes Export

    (Our dispatch) The nationally renowned Zhenjiang Vinegar has resumed its export.

    Zhenjiang Vinegar, known for its fragrant, mellow, and delicious taste, won a gold medal at the Nanyang Industrial Exposition in 1909. However, after the outbreak of the War of Resistance Against Japan, its sales in the Nanyang area were cut off.

    In December 1956, the Jiangsu Provincial Foreign Trade Department signed an export contract for Zhenjiang Vinegar with the Zhenjiang Hengshun Vinegar Factory. The first batch of one hundred jars, totaling six thousand catties of Zhenjiang Vinegar, has been shipped to the Philippines and other areas for sale.
                        (Zhang Wenjing)

### 镇江香醋恢复出口
张文敬
1957-01-24
第2版()
专栏：

    镇江香醋恢复出口
    本报讯　闻名全国的镇江香醋恢复出口了。
    镇江香醋香醇味美，曾在1909年的南洋劝业会评比中得过金牌奖。但是在抗日战争爆发以后，在南洋一带的销路断绝了。
    1956年12月，江苏省对外贸易部门同镇江恒顺醋厂签订了香醋的出口合同，第一批一百坛六千斤香醋已运往菲律宾一带销售。
                        （张文敬）
