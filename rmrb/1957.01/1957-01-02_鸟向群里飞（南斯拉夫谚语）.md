Title: Birds Fly to the Flock (Yugoslav Proverb)
Author: Zhao Zhiqiang, Wang Sengdao
Date: 1957-01-02
Page: 8

    Birds Fly to the Flock
    (Yugoslav Proverb) Believing your eyes is stronger than believing your ears. A fool remains silent, thinking he can pretend to be clever.
    * A person bitten by a snake is afraid even of lizards.
    * Every bird knows to fly to the flock.
        Compiled and translated by Zhao Zhiqiang and Wang Sengdao

### 鸟向群里飞（南斯拉夫谚语）
赵志强  王僧道
1957-01-02
第8版()
专栏：

    鸟向群里飞
    （南斯拉夫谚语）相信眼睛比相信耳朵强。蠢人默而不语，以为能够装作聪明。
    ＊被蛇咬伤的人见了蜥蜴也害怕。
    ＊每个鸟儿都知道向群里飞。
        赵志强、王僧道辑译
