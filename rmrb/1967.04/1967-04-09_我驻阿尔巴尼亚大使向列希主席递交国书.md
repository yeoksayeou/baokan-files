Title: China's Ambassador to Albania Presents Credentials to Chairman Lishi
Author:
Date: 1967-04-09
Edition: 5

China's new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Albania, Liu Xiao, presented his credentials to Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, Haxhi Lleshi, at the Partisan Palace in Tirana on the morning of April 7.

Present at the presentation were: Secretary of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, Bilbil Krosi, and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nestor Nasi, among others.

All members of the diplomatic staff of the Chinese Embassy in Albania attended the presentation ceremony.

After presenting his credentials, Chairman Lleshi and Ambassador Liu Xiao had a very friendly and cordial conversation.

On April 6, Ambassador Liu Xiao met with Albanian Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nestor Nasi. Both sides had a friendly and cordial conversation and discussed the arrangements for the presentation of credentials.

### 我驻阿尔巴尼亚大使向列希主席递交国书

1967-04-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻阿尔巴尼亚大使向列希主席递交国书
    新华社地拉那七日电  中国新任驻阿尔巴尼亚特命全权大使刘晓七日上午在地拉那游击队宫向阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希递交国书。
    递交国书时在座的有：阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团秘书比尔比尔·克洛西、外交部长奈斯蒂·纳赛等。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆全体外交官员也出席了呈递仪式。
    递交国书后，列希主席和刘晓大使进行了非常亲切友好的谈话。
    四月六日，刘晓大使会见了阿尔巴尼亚外交部长奈斯蒂·纳赛，双方进行了亲切友好的谈话，并商谈了递交国书事宜。
