Title: The Burma Asian-African Solidarity Committee Announces Decision to Attend the Asian-African Solidarity Conference in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-04-16
Edition: 6

The Burma Asian-African Solidarity Committee recently announced its decision to attend the fifth Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference, which will be held in Beijing this year.

This decision was announced in a letter from U La Gwe, Secretary-General of the Burma Asian-African Solidarity Committee, to the General Secretary of the Permanent Secretariat of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Organization, who has become a tool of the Soviet revisionists.

### 缅甸亚非团结委员会宣布  决定出席在北京举行的亚非团结大会

1967-04-16
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸亚非团结委员会宣布
    决定出席在北京举行的亚非团结大会
    据新华社仰光十三日电　缅甸亚非团结委员会最近宣布决定出席今年将在北京举行的第五届亚非人民团结大会。
    这一决定是缅甸亚非团结委员会秘书长吴拉觉给已沦为苏修御用工具的亚非人民团结组织常设书记处总书记的一封信中宣布的。
