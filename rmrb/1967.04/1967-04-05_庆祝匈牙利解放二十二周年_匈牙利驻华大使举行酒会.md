Title: Celebrating the 22nd Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation: Hungarian Ambassador Hosts Reception
Author:
Date: 1967-04-05
Edition: 5

Hungarian Ambassador to China, Jószef Héra, held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the 22nd anniversary of Hungary's liberation.

Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council, Vice Chairman Zhou Jianren of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and officials from various relevant departments attended the reception. Invited guests also included diplomats from various countries stationed in China.

### 庆祝匈牙利解放二十二周年  匈牙利驻华大使举行酒会

1967-04-05
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利解放二十二周年
    匈牙利驻华大使举行酒会
    新华社四日讯　匈牙利驻中国大使郝拉思·尤若夫今天下午举行酒会，庆祝匈牙利解放二十二周年。
    国务院副总理陈毅，人大常委会副委员长周建人和各有关方面负责人出席了酒会。应邀出席酒会的还有各国驻中国使节。
