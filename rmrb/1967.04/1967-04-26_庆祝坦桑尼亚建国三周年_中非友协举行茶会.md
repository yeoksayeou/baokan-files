Title: Celebrating the Third Anniversary of the Founding of the United Republic of Tanzania
Author:
Date: 1967-04-26
Edition: 5

The China Africa Friendship Association held a tea party today to celebrate the third anniversary of the founding of the United Republic of Tanzania.

In his speech, Ding Xilin, vice-chairman of the China Africa Friendship Association, praised the achievements of the Tanzanian people in consolidating national independence and developing the national economy over the past three years.

Ambassador Mwaluko of Tanzania said in his speech that Tanzania adheres to the policy of anti-imperialism and anti-colonialism, supporting the national liberation struggles of the African people. The ambassador praised the Chinese people for their tenacious struggle against imperialism. The ambassador concluded by wishing Chairman Mao a long life and expressing the hope that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution led by Chairman Mao would achieve even greater victories.

Those present at the tea party included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and other relevant officials. After the tea party, the film "Chairman Mao is the Red Sun in Our Hearts" was screened.

### 庆祝坦桑尼亚建国三周年  中非友协举行茶会

1967-04-26
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝坦桑尼亚建国三周年
    中非友协举行茶会
    据新华社二十五日讯  中国非洲人民友好协会今天举行茶会，庆祝坦桑尼亚联合共和国建国三周年。
    中非友协副会长丁西林在致词中赞扬了三年来坦桑尼亚人民在巩固民族独立、发展民族经济方面所取得的成就。
    坦桑尼亚大使姆瓦卢科在讲话中说，坦桑尼亚坚持反帝反殖的政策，支持非洲人民的民族解放斗争。大使赞扬了中国人民同帝国主义进行了顽强的斗争。大使最后祝毛主席长寿，祝愿毛主席领导的中国无产阶级文化大革命取得更大的胜利。
    出席茶会的还有外交部副部长罗贵波等有关方面负责人。茶会结束后，放映了电影《毛主席是我们心中的红太阳》。
