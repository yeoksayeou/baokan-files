Title: Delegation of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Government Security Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-04-15
Edition: 6

A delegation from the Congolese (Brazzaville) government security led by Michel Bindi, head of the Congolese security department, arrived in Beijing by plane at noon today. The delegation consists of three people. They are in China at the invitation of the Ministry of Public Security for a friendly visit.

	Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Vice Minister of Public Security Yang Qiqing and Yu Sang.

	The Congolese (Brazzaville) Ambassador to China, Bazanga, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 刚果（布）政府保安代表团到京

1967-04-15
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）政府保安代表团到京
    新华社十四日讯　由刚果（布）保安部门负责人米歇尔·宾迪率领的刚果共和国（布拉柴维尔）政府保安代表团一行三人，今天中午乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应公安部邀请来我国进行友好访问的。
    到机场迎接的，有公安部副部长杨奇清、于桑等。
    刚果（布）驻中国大使巴赞加也到机场迎接。
