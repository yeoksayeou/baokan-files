Title: Onward Without Pause on the Revolutionary Path
Author: Dānfēng
Date: 1967-04-13
Edition: 6

The revolutionary flames burn fiercely,
	Consuming the decayed branches and rotten leaves accumulated over a thousand years,
	The filth and grime.
	To thoroughly uproot the soil that breeds revisionism,
	Let the foundation of the communist edifice be laid in our great motherland!

The surging waves of the revolutionary torrent roll forward,
	The wheels of history turn ceaselessly, swift as lightning.
	Young comrades, march forward with heads held high,
	On the long journey, never pause!

Who says that growing up under the red flag means we won't get lost?
	Life presents us with countless crossroads!
	Naturally, we will stumble along the way,
	But the moment we fall, we must stand up again!

We are full of vigor and in the prime of our youth,
	We fear neither thunderstorms nor raging winds and heavy rain;
	We are the sun at eight or nine in the morning,
	We must not be lazy or old-fashioned!

Attack the "self" in our minds fiercely, otherwise our thoughts will rust,
	Study the "Three Articles" forever, without end!
	Raise the sails and follow Chairman Mao forward,
	Let the ship of life sail towards the harbor of radiant light.
	Remember, we are the young generation nurtured by Mao Zedong Thought,
	The weight of a thousand pounds rests on our shoulders;
	Hasten your steps to meet the new battles,
	Let the red sun shine over the entire globe!

### 革命路上不停留
丹枫
1967-04-13
第6版()
专栏：

    革命路上不停留
    丹枫
    无产阶级文化大革命
      烈火摧枯拉朽，
    焚烧着积存千年的败
      枝腐叶，浊泥污垢。
    要把滋长修正主义的
      土壤彻底铲除，
    让共产主义大厦的基
      石在伟大的祖国铺
      就！
    革命的洪流波涛汹涌
      滚滚向前，
    历史的车轮风驰电掣
      转动不休；
    年青的战友们，昂首
      阔步朝前进啊，
    万里征途上永不停
      留！
    谁说生长在红旗下就
      不会迷路，
    生活中有多少让我们
      抉择的岔道口！
    自然，前进的途中免
      不了摔跤，
    但跌倒的片刻就应当
      是爬起的时候！
    我们朝气蓬勃，风华
      正茂，
    决不怕电闪雷鸣，风
      狂雨骤；
    我们是早晨八、九点
      钟的太阳，
    决不能懒懒散散，老
      气横秋！
    向头脑中的“私”字猛
      烈开火，否则思想
      就会生锈，
    学习“老三篇”，永远
      不会有个够！
    扬起风帆紧跟毛主席
      前进吧，
    让生命的船驶向霞光
      万道的港口。
    要记住我们是毛泽东
      思想哺育下的年青
      一代，
    千斤重担压在我们肩
      头；
    快迈步去迎接新的战
      斗，
    让鲜红的太阳早日照
    遍全球！
