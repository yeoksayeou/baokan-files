Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Raids U.S. Air Base
Author:
Date: 1967-04-25
Edition: 5

The brave and cunning armed forces of South Vietnam recently launched surprise attacks on the An Khe and Bien Hoa U.S. air bases in the provinces of Gia Lai and Kien Giang, respectively, achieving the glorious victory of destroying and damaging 53 enemy aircraft and annihilating nearly 300 U.S. invaders.

According to the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, the armed forces of Gia Lai Province launched a fierce and accurate artillery barrage on the night of April 8 to the early morning of April 9, targeting the U.S. 1st Cavalry Division's helicopter base and a nearby enemy outpost in the An Khe area. They destroyed 28 enemy helicopters and seven bunkers, killing and wounding 115 U.S. invaders. Since 1966, the armed forces of Gia Lai Province have attacked the An Khe U.S. air base four times, destroying 177 enemy aircraft and eliminating 682 U.S. invaders.

The armed forces of Kien Giang Province launched an attack on the Bien Hoa military airfield on the night of March 26, destroying and damaging 25 enemy aircraft of various types and killing and wounding 170 U.S. and puppet troops. At the same time, the liberation forces also shelled the enemy's military command post in U Minh, causing casualties and losses to the enemy.

Since February 19, 1966, the armed forces of Kien Giang Province have attacked the Bien Hoa military airfield five times, destroying and damaging 177 enemy aircraft, eliminating 835 U.S. and puppet troops, and destroying many facilities within the airfield.

According to the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, the armed forces of the New An-Ho Chi Minh City region have shot down and destroyed 47 U.S. aggressor aircraft in the first three months of this year, seriously damaging eight more. This is 27 more enemy aircraft than in the same period last year.

In their heroic battles, the military and civilians of this region not only shot down a large number of enemy aircraft but also killed a large number of U.S. invaders on the aircraft. The guerrilla forces of De Hoa District and Cang Buong Commune in Ho Chi Minh City shot down nine U.S. aggressor aircraft in a single day of battle, killing 80 U.S. invaders.

The heroic soldiers of a certain unit of the South Vietnamese Liberation Army, filled with deep hatred for the enemy, fiercely annihilated the U.S. aggressors. In one battle alone, they destroyed ten U.S. military vehicles.

### 南越解放军奇袭美空军基地  毁伤敌机五十三架，歼灭美国侵略军近三百名

1967-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放军奇袭美空军基地
    毁伤敌机五十三架，歼灭美国侵略军近三百名
    新华社河内二十四日电　英勇机智的越南南方解放武装力量最近先后奇袭嘉莱、芹苴两省的安溪和茶诺美空军基地，取得击毁击伤敌机五十三架，歼灭美国侵略军近三百名的辉煌战果。
    据越南南方解放通讯社报道，嘉莱省解放武装力量在八日夜间到九日凌晨，以猛烈和准确的炮火袭击安溪地区美国第一骑兵师的直升飞机基地和机场外围的一个敌人据点，击毁敌人的直升飞机二十八架、碉堡七个，打死打伤美国侵略军一百一十五名。嘉莱省解放武装力量从一九六六年以来已经四次袭击安溪美国空军基地，共击毁敌机一百七十七架，消灭美国侵略军六百八十二名。
    芹苴省解放武装力量，三月二十六日夜间袭击茶诺美军机场，击毁击伤敌人各种飞机二十五架，打死打伤美伪军一百七十名。
    在炮击茶诺机场的同时，解放武装力量还炮击乌门敌人军事支区指挥所，使敌人遭到伤亡和损失。
    芹苴省解放武装力量，从一九六六年二月十九日到现在，已经五次袭击茶诺美军机场，共击毁击伤敌机一百七十七架，消灭美伪军八百三十五名，摧毁机场内的许多设备。
    据新华社河内二十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方新安—堤岸地区解放武装力量，今年头三个月共击落和击毁美国强盗飞机四十七架，严重击伤八架。比去年同期击落和击毁的敌机多二十七架。
    这个地区的军民在英勇战斗中，不仅狠狠地揍下了大批敌机，而且大量歼灭了飞机上的美国侵略军。堤岸省德和县和庆乡游击队在一天的战斗中就击落美国强盗飞机九架，打死美国侵略军八十名。
