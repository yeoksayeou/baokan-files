Title: President Sekou Toure Refutes Rumors of a Coup in Guinea
Author:
Date: 1967-04-29
Edition: 4

President Sekou Toure of Guinea strongly denounced the fabricated rumors of a coup in Guinea spread by imperialists and strongly condemned the subversive activities of imperialism and old and new colonialism in Africa.

Toure said that they had received messages from London, Paris, the United States, and even Ivory Coast, claiming that a coup had taken place in Conakry. However, the people of Guinea are revolutionary people, and the so-called "coup" is merely an imperialist fantasy.

He emphasized that the only effective response to imperialist violence is revolutionary violence.

In discussing the African national liberation movement, he said that only by openly and uncompromisingly fighting imperialism can true freedom be achieved.

### 杜尔总统驳斥所谓几内亚“政变”的谣言  对帝国主义必须斗争决不能妥协

1967-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    杜尔总统驳斥所谓几内亚“政变”的谣言
    对帝国主义必须斗争决不能妥协
    新华社科纳克里二十四日电　几内亚总统塞古·杜尔二十三日晚上在科纳克里发表讲话时痛斥帝国主义捏造的几内亚发生政变的谣言，并且强烈谴责帝国主义和新老殖民主义在非洲进行颠覆活动。
    杜尔总统说，我们收到来自伦敦、巴黎、美国，甚至象牙海岸等地的消息，这些消息说什么在科纳克里发生了政变。但是，几内亚人民是革命的人民，所谓的“政变”只是帝国主义的幻想。
    他强调说，对帝国主义暴力唯一有效的回答是革命的暴力。
    他在谈到非洲民族解放运动时说，只有同帝国主义公开作不妥协的斗争才能取得真正的自由。
