Title: Jinan National Cotton Mill No. 4 Establishes New Revolutionary Production Order
Author:
Date: 1967-04-02
Edition: 2

Jinan National Cotton Mill No. 4's revolutionary workers and cadres completed the first quarter's production plan two days ahead of schedule and with overfulfillment. The cotton yarn production increased by 34.6 percent compared to the same period last year.

After the revolutionary faction seized power, the factory quickly established a new order for revolution and production. All revolutionary workers in the factory adhere to the eight-hour workday system and ensure that production preparations are completed before starting work, striving to maximize the use of work hours. Revolutionary workers also extensively carried out technological innovation activities.

### 济南国棉四厂建立革命生产新秩序  一季度棉纱产量超去年同期

1967-04-02
第2版()
专栏：

    济南国棉四厂建立革命生产新秩序
    一季度棉纱产量超去年同期
    据新华社济南三十日电　济南国棉四厂广大革命职工和革命干部提前两天超额完成了第一季度生产计划，棉纱产量比去年同期提高百分之三十四点六。
    这个厂的革命派夺权后，迅速建立了革命和生产的新秩序。全厂革命职工坚持八小时工作制，并且在上班前做好生产准备，力争最大限度地利用工时。革命职工还广泛开展了技术革新活动。
