Title: China and Zambia Sign Trade Agreement, Premier Zhou Receives Zambian Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-04-29
Edition: 5

The trade agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Zambia was signed in Beijing today.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Li Xiannian attended the signing ceremony. Before the signing ceremony, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Li Xiannian met with the Zambian government trade and economic delegation led by Zambian Minister of Commerce and Industry Chiba. They had a friendly and cordial conversation.

The representatives who signed the trade agreement on behalf of both sides were: Lin Haiyun, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade of China, and Chiba, Head of the Zambian Government Trade and Economic Delegation.

Those who attended the signing ceremony also included Liu Xihuan, Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade, Xie Feng, Deputy Director of the African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Qin Lijin, Chinese Ambassador to Zambia.

Subsequently, Vice Premier Li Xiannian and other relevant officials attended a reception hosted by Minister Chiba. At the reception, the hosts and guests toasted to the ever-developing friendship between the peoples of China and Zambia and the economic and trade relations between the two countries.

### 中国赞比亚签订贸易协定  周总理接见赞比亚客人

1967-04-29
第5版()
专栏：

    中国赞比亚签订贸易协定
    周总理接见赞比亚客人
    新华社二十八日讯　中华人民共和国政府和赞比亚共和国政府贸易协定，今天在北京签订。
    国务院总理周恩来，副总理李先念出席了签字仪式。在签字仪式以前，周恩来总理和李先念副总理接见了由赞比亚工商与对外贸易部部长钦巴率领的赞比亚政府贸易经济代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    代表双方在贸易协定上签字的是：我国对外贸易部代理部长林海云，赞比亚政府贸易经济代表团团长钦巴。
    参加签字仪式的，还有对外贸易部部长助理刘希文，外交部非洲司副司长谢丰，中国驻赞比亚大使秦力真等。
    随后，李先念副总理等有关方面的负责人出席了钦巴部长举行的招待会。在招待会上，宾主举杯祝中赞两国人民的友谊和两国的经济贸易关系不断发展。
