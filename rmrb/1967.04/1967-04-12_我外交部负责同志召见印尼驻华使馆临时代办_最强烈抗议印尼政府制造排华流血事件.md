Title: Chinese Foreign Ministry Official Summons Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires to Protest Bloody Anti-Chinese Incident
Author:
Date: 1967-04-12
Edition: 6

A responsible official of the Chinese Foreign Ministry summoned the Chargé d'Affaires of the Indonesian Embassy in China, Mr. Balen, today. The official strongly protested the serious incident that occurred on April 6 in Surabaya, East Java, where the military and administrative authorities looted the property of Chinese residents and fired shots, killing Huang Jianmin and injuring Yang Jingrong, Chen Yongzhen, and others.

The official demanded that the Indonesian government take satisfactory measures to address the incident and respond promptly.

### 我外交部负责同志召见印尼驻华使馆临时代办  最强烈抗议印尼政府制造排华流血事件

1967-04-12
第6版()
专栏：

    我外交部负责同志召见印尼驻华使馆临时代办
    最强烈抗议印尼政府制造排华流血事件
    新华社十一日讯　中国外交部负责同志今天召见印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办巴伦，就本月六日东爪哇西都文罗市军政当局抢劫华侨财产，并悍然开枪打死华侨黄健民和枪伤杨荆荣、陈永振等人的流血严重事件，向印尼政府提出最强烈的抗议，要求印尼政府作出令人满意的处理，并迅速作出答复。
