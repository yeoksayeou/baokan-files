Title: A Serious Step by the Johnson Administration to Expand the War in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-04-12
Edition: 6

According to a report from the New China News Agency on April 11, Bangkok: In accordance with the deployment of the imperialist black meeting convened by the U.S. in Guam to expand the war in Vietnam, the first batch of U.S. B-52 bombers officially arrived at the U-Tapao Air Base in Thailand on April 10.

According to reports from Western news agencies, the first batch of three B-52 bombers, after arriving in Thailand, will begin bombing Vietnam from the Thai base.

After the war monger Johnson convened the Guam black meeting and deployed the B-52 bombers to Thailand, this is a serious step by U.S. imperialism to expand the war in Vietnam, and a serious provocation to the peoples of Indochina and Southeast Asia. For a long time, U.S. imperialism has been using Thai bases as a foothold to invade Vietnam, Laos, and other Indochinese countries. In the past two years, 75% of the aircraft that bombed North Vietnam took off from Thai bases.

### 约翰逊政府扩大侵越战争的一个严重步骤  美国B—52型轰炸机悍然开进泰国

1967-04-12
第6版()
专栏：

    约翰逊政府扩大侵越战争的一个严重步骤
    美国B—52型轰炸机悍然开进泰国
    据新华社十一日讯  曼谷消息：根据美帝国主义在关岛召集的扩大侵越战争的黑会的部署，第一批美国B—52型轰炸机已于十日正式开进泰国梭桃邑乌塔保空军基地。
    据西方通讯社报道，第一批共三架B—52型轰炸机到达泰国以后，将开始从泰国基地对越南进行轰炸。
    战争贩子约翰逊召开关岛黑会以后，部署B—52型轰炸机进驻泰国，是美帝国主义扩大侵越战争的一个严重步骤，也是对印度支那和东南亚各国人民的严重挑衅。长期以来，美帝国主义一直利用泰国基地作为侵略越南和老挝和其他印度支那国家的据点。最近两年美国对越南北方进行轰炸，百分之七十五架次的飞机是由泰国基地起飞的。
