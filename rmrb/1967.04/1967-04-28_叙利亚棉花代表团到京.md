Title: Syrian Cotton Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-04-28
Edition: 6

A delegation from the Syrian Cotton Promotion Bureau, headed by Deputy Director Adna Attasi, arrived in Beijing on April 25th by airplane. The delegation consists of two people.

### 叙利亚棉花代表团到京

1967-04-28
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚棉花代表团到京
    新华社二十六日讯　以叙利亚棉花推销总局副局长阿德纳·阿塔西为首的叙利亚棉花代表团一行二人，二十五日乘飞机到达北京。
