Title: A Historic Victory in the Realm of Literature: Japanese *Chūō Shimbun* Highly Praises *The Song of Ouyang Hai*
Author:
Date: 1967-04-06
Edition: 5

    A Historic Victory in the Realm of Literature
    Japanese *Chūō Shimbun* Highly Praises *The Song of Ouyang Hai*

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 5th: The Japanese newspaper *Chūō Shimbun* recently published an article highly praising the great achievements made by Chinese revolutionary writers under the radiant guidance of contemporary Marxism-Leninism at its highest level—Mao Zedong Thought—in the literary field. The article points out that the birth of the Chinese revolutionary novel *The Song of Ouyang Hai* is a historic victory in the realm of literature.

    The article points out that through the process of Ouyang Hai growing into an outstanding proletarian warrior, we once again recognize that studying and applying Chairman Mao's works and arming oneself with Mao Zedong Thought is of paramount importance. The article says that the Chinese people, with Mao Zedong Thought as the banner, strive to elevate their thinking to the newest and highest level, and *The Song of Ouyang Hai*, through the form of literary art, successfully reflects this reality. This is a historic victory in the realm of literature.

### 文学史上划时代的胜利  日本《长周新闻》高度赞扬《欧阳海之歌》

1967-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    文学史上划时代的胜利
    日本《长周新闻》高度赞扬《欧阳海之歌》
    据新华社东京五日电　日本《长周新闻》最近载文，高度赞扬中国革命作家在当代最高水平的马克思列宁主义——毛泽东思想的光辉照耀下，在文学领域中所取得的新的巨大成就。文章指出，中国革命小说《欧阳海之歌》的诞生，是文学史上划时代的胜利。
    文章指出，通过欧阳海成长为卓越的无产阶级战士的过程，使我们再一次认识到活学活用毛主席著作、用毛泽东思想武装自己，是头等重要的事情。文章说，中国人民用毛泽东思想挂帅，努力使自己的思想达到最新和最高的境界，而《欧阳海之歌》通过文学艺术的形式，成功地反映了这一现实，这是文学史上划时代的胜利。
