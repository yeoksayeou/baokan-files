Title: Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Lin, and Premier Zhou Receive the Vietnamese Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1967-04-20
Edition: 1

Our most beloved and great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao, as well as Premier Zhou Enlai, received the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Vu Quang Dung. Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Lin Biao, and Premier Zhou Enlai had an extremely warm and friendly conversation with the envoy of the heroic Vietnamese people from the front lines of the struggle against American imperialism.

### 毛主席林副主席周总理接见越南大使  同来自反美斗争前线的英雄的越南人民的使者进行了极为亲切友好的谈话

1967-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席林副主席周总理接见越南大使
    同来自反美斗争前线的英雄的越南人民的使者进行了极为亲切友好的谈话
    新华社十九日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪同志以及周恩来同志，接见了越南民主共和国驻中国特命全权大使吴明鸾。毛主席、林彪副主席和周恩来总理同来自反美斗争前线的英雄的越南人民的使者，进行了极为亲切友好的谈话。
