Title: Excerpt from the Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenum of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party
Author:
Date: 1967-04-25
Edition: 6

The new leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, following in the footsteps of Khrushchev, is pursuing a Khrushchev-style revisionism without Khrushchev. Their line is to maintain the rule of imperialism and colonialism in the capitalist world and to carry out a capitalist restoration in the socialist world.

### 摘自党的八届十一中全会公报

1967-04-25
第6版()
专栏：

    苏共新领导集团，继承赫鲁晓夫的衣钵，实行没有赫鲁晓夫的赫鲁晓夫修正主义。他们的路线就是，在资本主义世界维护帝国主义、殖民主义的统治，在社会主义世界实行资本主义复辟。
                                    摘自党的八届十一中全会公报
