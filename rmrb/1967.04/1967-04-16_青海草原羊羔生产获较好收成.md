Title: Good Results in Lamb Production on the Qinghai Grasslands
Author:
Date: 1967-04-16
Edition: 2

Under the great call of Chairman Mao to "seize the revolution and promote production," the revolutionary herdsmen of various ethnic groups on the Qinghai grasslands have overcome a long period of drought and achieved good results in lamb production for the winter and spring of last year and this year. According to incomplete statistics, by the end of March, 2.45 million lambs had been born, an increase of 1.9 million over the same period last year. The lamb survival rate reached 87%, and in some places, it exceeded 95%.

(From Xinhua News Agency)

### 青海草原羊羔生产获较好收成

1967-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

    青海草原羊羔生产获较好收成
    在毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大号召鼓舞下，青海草原各族革命牧民战胜长时间的干旱，夺得去冬今春羊羔生产的较好收成。据不完全统计，到三月底已产羊羔二百四十五万多只，比去年同期多产十九万多只。羊羔成活率达到百分之八十七，有的地方达到百分之九十五以上。
                                    （据新华社讯）
