Title: Chinese-Romanian Friendship Delegation Arrives in Romania for a Friendly Visit
Author:
Date: 1967-04-01
Edition: 5

	At the invitation of the Romanian Foreign Cultural Liaison Association, the Chinese-Romanian Friendship Delegation arrived in Bucharest by plane on the 30th to visit Romania on a friendly mission.

	The delegation is led by Liu Luming, a member of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Romania and the political commissar of a certain army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Vice Chairman A. Conscica of the Romanian Foreign Cultural Association, Secretary J. Potar, and others.

	Li Bin, the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 中罗友好代表团到达罗马尼亚进行友好访问

1967-04-01
第5版()
专栏：

    中罗友好代表团到达罗马尼亚进行友好访问
    新华社布加勒斯特三十日电　应罗马尼亚对外文化联络协会邀请，中罗友好代表团三十日乘飞机抵达这里对罗马尼亚进行友好访问。
    代表团由中罗友协理事、中国人民解放军某军政治委员刘路明率领。
    在机场欢迎代表团的有罗马尼亚对外文协副主席阿·康斯齐卡、文协秘书约·波塔尔等。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办李斌也到机场欢迎。
