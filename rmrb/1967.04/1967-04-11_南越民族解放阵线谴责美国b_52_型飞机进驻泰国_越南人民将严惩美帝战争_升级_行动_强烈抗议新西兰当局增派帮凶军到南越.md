Title: The National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Condemns the Deployment of U.S. B-52 Bombers to Thailand
Author:
Date: 1967-04-11
Edition: 6

The National Liberation Front of South Vietnam strongly condemns the deployment of U.S. B-52 bombers to Thai military bases to intensify attacks on Vietnam. The statement warns that U.S. imperialism and the Thai reactionary regime must bear full responsibility for the serious consequences of their aggressive actions.

The statement points out that the deployment of U.S. B-52 bombers to Thailand is a serious step in the U.S. aggressors' increasingly frenzied escalation of the war in Vietnam and their savage massacre of the Vietnamese people. It is also a dangerous step in the U.S. policy of using the territories of its puppet states in Southeast Asia to wage its war of aggression against Vietnam. The U.S. aggressors' adoption of this action once again exposes their aggressive, warlike, and obstinate nature.

The statement says that the Thanom Kittikachorn clique, the faithful lackeys of U.S. imperialism, disregarding the opposition of the Thai and world peoples, blindly carrying out the orders of their American masters, have taken another dangerous step down the road of betrayal, turning Thailand into a forward base for the U.S. war of aggression against Vietnam.

The statement sternly warns U.S. imperialism and the Thanom Kittikachorn clique that the U.S. strategic air force will absolutely not intimidate or conquer the Vietnamese people. The various aggressive actions of the U.S. and its lackeys in South and North Vietnam will definitely be punished by the Vietnamese people.

According to Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi, the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Central Committee's External Liaison Committee issued a statement on the 6th, strongly protesting against the New Zealand government's recent decision to send an additional infantry company and some units from a camp stationed in Malaya to participate in the war of aggression against Vietnam.

The statement points out that this shows that the New Zealand government, despite strong opposition from the New Zealand and world peoples, continues to follow the path of being a lackey of U.S. imperialism.

The statement says that a few hundred New Zealand mercenaries will absolutely not be able to save the U.S. aggressors from their inevitable defeat.

The statement concludes that if the New Zealand authorities continue to serve the U.S. imperialist war of aggression against Vietnam, they will inevitably suffer a humiliating defeat, just like the U.S. aggressors.

### 南越民族解放阵线谴责美国B—52型飞机进驻泰国  越南人民将严惩美帝战争“升级”行动  强烈抗议新西兰当局增派帮凶军到南越

1967-04-11
第6版()
专栏：

    南越民族解放阵线谴责美国B—52型飞机进驻泰国
    越南人民将严惩美帝战争“升级”行动
    强烈抗议新西兰当局增派帮凶军到南越
    新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会对外联络委员会四日发表声明，强烈谴责美国B—52型轰炸机进驻泰国空军基地，以加紧对越南的袭击。声明警告，美帝国主义和泰国反动当局必须对它们的侵略行动所引起的严重后果承担全部责任。
    声明指出，美国B—52型轰炸机进驻泰国是美国强盗在越南日益疯狂地加紧战争“升级”和变本加厉进行野蛮屠杀的计划中的一个严重步骤，也是美国利用它在东南亚仆从国的领土来进行侵越战争总政策中的一个危险步骤。美国强盗采取这个行动，再一次暴露了它的侵略、好战和顽固的本质。
    声明说，美帝国主义者的忠实走狗他侬·吉滴卡宗集团不顾泰国和世界人民的反对，一味执行美国主子的命令，在卖国的道路上又迈出了危险的一步，把泰国变成美国侵越战争的前进基地。
    声明严厉警告美帝国主义者和吉滴卡宗集团说，美国的战略空军绝对吓不倒，也征服不了越南人民。美国及其仆从在越南南北方进行的种种侵略行动，一定会遭到越南人民的惩罚。
    据新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会对外联络委员会发言人六日发表声明，强烈抗议新西兰政府最近决定向越南南方增派一个步兵连和属于驻在马来亚的一个营的一些单位参与侵越战争的罪行。
    声明指出，这表明，新西兰政府不顾新西兰人民和世界人民的强烈反对，继续沿着充当美帝国主义走狗的道路走下去。
    声明说，几百名新西兰帮凶军是绝对挽救不了美国侵略军的彻底失败的命运的。
    声明最后指出，新西兰当局继续为美帝侵越战争效劳，就必定象美国侵略者一样遭到可耻的失败。
