Title: Celebrating the Seventh Anniversary of the Signing of the China-Nepal Peace and Friendship Treaty
Author:
Date: 1967-04-28
Edition: 6

The China-Nepal Friendship Association held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the signing of the China-Nepal Peace and Friendship Treaty.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 27th, the China-Nepal Friendship Association held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the signing of the China-Nepal Peace and Friendship Treaty.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association, Zhou Jianren, spoke first at the reception. He congratulated the continuous development of the traditional friendship between the peoples of China and Nepal.

Nepal's Ambassador to China, Rendi'er Suba, and his wife, as well as diplomatic staff from the embassy, were invited to attend the reception. Ambassador Rendi'er Suba, in his speech, wished Chairman Mao a long life and expressed his wish for the friendship between the peoples of Nepal and China to grow stronger.

Also present at the reception were Deputy Foreign Minister Luo Guibao, Deputy Director of the State Economic Commission Yang Lin, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Affairs Commission Ding Xilin, and representatives from the revolutionary masses of Beijing's proletariat.

### 庆祝中尼和平友好条约签订七周年  中尼友协举行招待会

1967-04-28
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝中尼和平友好条约签订七周年
    中尼友协举行招待会
    据新华社二十七日讯  中国尼泊尔友好协会今天下午举行招待会，庆祝中国尼泊尔和平友好条约签订七周年。
    人大常委会副委员长、中尼友好协会会长周建人在招待会上首先致词。他祝贺中尼两国人民传统友谊的不断发展。
    尼泊尔驻华大使伦迪尔·苏巴和夫人，以及使馆外交人员应邀参加了招待会。伦迪尔·苏巴大使在致词中祝毛主席万寿无疆，祝愿尼中两国人民的友谊日益发展。
    参加招待会的还有外交部副部长罗贵波、对外经委副主任杨琳、对外文委副主任丁西林以及首都无产阶级革命派的代表。
