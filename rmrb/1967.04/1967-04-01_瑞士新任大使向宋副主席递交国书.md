Title: Swiss New Ambassador Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Song
Author:
Date: 1967-04-01
Edition: 5

Swiss Federal New Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, Oscar Rossetti, presented his letter of credence to Vice Chairman Song Qingling of the People's Republic of China this afternoon, accompanied by diplomatic staff from the embassy.

After presenting the letter of credence, Vice Chairman Song Qingling had a friendly conversation with Ambassador Rossetti. Present were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and others.

### 瑞士新任大使向宋副主席递交国书

1967-04-01
第5版()
专栏：

    瑞士新任大使向宋副主席递交国书
    新华社三十一日讯　瑞士联邦新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使奥斯卡·罗赛蒂，今天下午偕同大使馆外交人员，向中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄递交国书。
    递交国书后，宋庆龄副主席同罗赛蒂大使进行了友好谈话。在场的有外交部副部长罗贵波等。
