	Brief News

	More than 20,000 Belgian Youths Protest Against U.S. Imperialism

	More than 20,000 Belgian youths took to the streets of Brussels on the 23rd in a massive demonstration, protesting against U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam, supporting the Vietnamese people's heroic struggle against U.S. aggression for national liberation, and the armed struggle of the Congolese (Kinshasa) people.

	Youth delegations from France, West Germany, the Netherlands, Britain, and other countries also participated in the march. The demonstrators chanted slogans such as: "Johnson: Murderer!" "Kosygin: Accomplice!" "U.S. aggressors, get out of Belgium, get out of South Vietnam!"

	Pakistan Foreign Minister Reiterates Kashmir's Right to Self-Determination

	Pakistan's Foreign Minister Pirzada on the 24th reiterated that unless the people of Jammu and Kashmir are allowed to freely exercise their inalienable right to self-determination without pressure, there can be no lasting peace on the Indian subcontinent. He said that the demand of the Kashmiri people to decide their future according to their own will is just, and this demand will ultimately be realized.

	Syria-Egypt Joint Statement Denounces Imperialist Conspiracies

	Recently, an Egyptian delegation led by Prime Minister Sulayman visited Syria.

	In a joint statement, both sides condemned U.S. and Israeli arming of Arab reactionaries and reaffirmed the importance of the struggle for the liberation of Palestine. The statement also denounced imperialist attempts to turn Jordan into a protectorate of Israel.

    简明新闻
    比利时两万多青年反美游行
    两万多名比利时青年二十三日在布鲁塞尔大街上举行声势浩大的游行示威，反对美帝国主义侵略越南、支持越南人民英勇的抗美救国斗争和刚果（金）人民的武装斗争。
    从法国、西德、荷兰、英国和其他国家来的青年代表团也参加了游行。示威者一路高呼：“约翰逊：杀人犯！”“柯西金：帮凶！”
  “美国侵略者从比利时，从南越滚出去！”等口号。
    巴外长重申克什米尔自决权
    巴基斯坦外交部长皮尔扎达二十四日重申，除非让查谟和克什米尔的人民自由地在不受压力的情况下行使他们不可剥夺的自决权，印度—巴基斯坦次大陆就不可能有持久和平。他说，克什米尔人民要求根据他们自己的意愿决定未来，是正义的，这一要求最后必将实现。
    叙阿公报谴责帝国主义阴谋
    由苏莱曼总理率领的阿联代表团最近访问叙利亚。
    双方在一项联合公报中抨击美国和以色列武装阿拉伯反动派，并重申巴勒斯坦解放斗争的重要性。公报还谴责帝国主义把约旦变成以色列的卫士。
                        （据新华社讯）
