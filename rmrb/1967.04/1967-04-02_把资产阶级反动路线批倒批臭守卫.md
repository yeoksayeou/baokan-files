Title: Overthrow and Expose the Bourgeois Reactionary Line
Author: Lú Yàncái
Date: 1967-04-02
Edition: 4

    Chairman Mao teaches us: "Who are our enemies? Who are our friends? This is the primary question for revolution."

    The biggest capitalist-roaders in the Party have confused right and wrong and reversed black and white on this primary question of revolution.

    As early as 1950, he extensively praised and beautified the enemy, slandered the revolutionary people, and propagated the reactionary film "The Last Days of the Dynasty," which advocated surrendering to the enemy.

    In this Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, he has gone even further, formulating a bourgeois reactionary line of "striking down a large number and protecting a small handful," using every means to shield capitalist-roaders in power, and fiercely suppressing the revolutionary mass movement.

    His landlord-bourgeois class stand is so clear! Over the decades, his nature has not changed at all. Proposing a bourgeois reactionary line in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is not accidental.

    The more the capitalist-roaders in power fear the revolutionary mass movement, the more we must rise up to rebel against them, fight them down, smash them, and expose them thoroughly. In the revolutionary struggle, let us thoroughly bury the old world and build a bright red new world!

### 把资产阶级反动路线批倒批臭守卫
卢燕财
1967-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　把资产阶级反动路线批倒批臭守卫
　　天安门战士　卢燕财
    毛主席教导我们：“谁是我们的敌人？谁是我们的朋友？这个问题是革命的首要问题。”
    党内最大的走资本主义道路的当权派正是在这个革命的首要问题上，混淆了是非，颠倒了黑白。
    早在一九五○年，他就大肆吹捧过美化敌人、污蔑革命人民、宣扬卖国主义的反动影片《清宫秘史》。
    在这次无产阶级文化大革命中，他更变本加厉，制定了资产阶级反动路线，“打击一大片，保护一小撮”，千方百计包庇走资本主义道路的当权派，疯狂压制革命的群众运动。
    他的地主资产阶级的立场何等的鲜明！几十年来，他的这个本性没有丝毫改变，在无产阶级文化大革命中提出资产阶级反动路线，决不是偶然的。
    党内走资本主义道路的当权派越是害怕革命的群众运动，我们越是要起来造他们的反，把他们斗倒、斗垮、斗臭，在革命斗争中彻底埋葬旧世界，建立一个红彤彤的新世界！
