Title: Delegation of Women from the (Orthodox) Japan-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-04-01
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation, a delegation of women from the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox) headquarters, consisting of 17 members led by Nakajima Chiyo, arrived in Beijing by plane last night. Vice President Xu Guangping of the All-China Women's Federation and member of the Presidium Shen Ziqing were at the airport to welcome them.

### 日中友协（正统）妇女代表团到京

1967-04-01
第5版()
专栏：

    日中友协（正统）妇女代表团到京
    新华社三十一日讯　应全国妇联邀请来中国访问的日中友好协会（正统）总部妇女访华代表团一行十七人，由中岛千代率领于昨晚乘飞机抵达北京。全国妇联副主任许广平、主席团委员沈兹九等到机场欢迎。
