Title: The People of Mali Warmly Welcome the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, Highly Praise the Great "January Revolution"
Author:
Date: 1967-04-05
Edition: 5

The people of Mali are enthusiastically welcoming the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China and highly praising the great "January Revolution." The picture shows Malian workers reading the French edition of "Beijing Weekly" which contains the article "A Letter to the People of Shanghai."

	New China News Agency Photographer
