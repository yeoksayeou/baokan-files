Title: Chairman Mao Receives Leaders of Revolutionary Committees from Six Provinces and Municipalities and Watches the Revolutionary Modern Ballet "White-Haired Girl"
Author:
Date: 1967-04-25
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao receives leaders of revolutionary committees from six provinces and municipalities and watches the revolutionary modern ballet "White-Haired Girl"
Vice Chairman Lin Biao and Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, and Li Fuchun accompany the reception
The orchestra plays "The Boat Sails on the Sea with the Helmsman at the Helm," and everyone shouts repeatedly: "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand ten thousand years!"

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: Our great leader Chairman Mao received the leaders of the revolutionary committees from Shanghai, Shanxi, Guizhou, Heilongjiang, Shandong, and Beijing provinces and municipalities this evening. Accompanying the reception were Vice Chairman Lin Biao, and Politburo Standing Committee members Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, and Li Fuchun. The leaders of the revolutionary committees from the various provinces and municipalities who were received include Zhang Chunqiao, Yao Wenyuan, Liu Geping, Zhang Riquing, Li Zaicheng, He Guangyu, Pan Fusheng, Wang Jiadao, Wang Xiaoyu, and Xie Fuzhi. Present at the reception were Jiang Qing, the first deputy head of the Central Cultural Revolution Group, and members of the Central Cultural Revolution Group, Wang Li, Guan Feng, and Chen Boda. After the reception, Chairman Mao, Comrade Lin Biao, Comrade Zhou Enlai, and the various leaders watched the revolutionary modern ballet "White-Haired Girl" performed by the Shanghai Dance School. After the performance, the orchestra played "The Boat Sails on the Sea with the Helmsman at the Helm," and all the performers rushed to the front of the stage, excitedly shouting repeatedly: "Long live Chairman Mao! Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand ten thousand years!" Chairman Mao, with a beaming smile, applauded together with Vice Chairman Lin Biao, Premier Zhou Enlai, and the other leaders. Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Biao also went up on the stage and took a group photo with the performers.

### 毛主席接见六省市革命委员会负责人并观看革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》

1967-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见六省市革命委员会负责人并观看革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》
    林彪副主席和周恩来陈伯达康生李富春同志陪同接见
    乐队高奏《大海航行靠舵手》，大家一遍遍地高呼：“毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”
    新华社二十四日讯　我们伟大领袖毛主席今天晚上接见上海、山西、贵州、黑龙江、山东、北京等省市革命委员会的负责人。陪同接见的有林彪副主席，中央政治局常委周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春同志。被接见的各省市革命委员会的负责人有张春桥、姚文元、刘格平、张日清、李再含、何光宇、潘复生、汪家道、王效禹、谢富治同志。接见时在座的有中央文革小组第一副组长江青同志，中央文革小组成员王力、关锋、戚本禹同志。接见后，毛主席、林彪同志、周恩来同志和各位负责同志一起，观看了上海市舞蹈学校演出的革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》。演出结束以后，乐队高奏《大海航行靠舵手》，全体演员一齐拥到舞台前，激动地一遍又一遍地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”毛主席满面笑容，同林彪副主席、周恩来总理等负责同志一起鼓掌。毛主席、林彪副主席还走上舞台，同演员们一起照了像。
    （附图片）
    我们的伟大领袖毛主席和林彪副主席、周恩来总理等负责同志，四月二十四日观看了上海市舞蹈学校演出的革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》。演出结束后，毛主席满面笑容，同林彪副主席等一起走上舞台，和演员合影。
