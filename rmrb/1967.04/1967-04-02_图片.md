Title: Revolutionary Workers of Shanghai Plastic Products Factory Produce Plastic Film for Spring Ploughing
Author:
Date: 1967-04-02
Edition: 2

The revolutionary workers and the broad masses of revolutionary workers at Shanghai Plastic Products Factory No. 1 are rushing to produce a large quantity of plastic film for agricultural use to support the spring ploughing in the countryside.
