Title: U.S. Plane Intrudes into Guangdong Province's Airspace, China Issues 430th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1967-04-03
Edition: 6

On April 2nd, at 10:55 AM to 11:17 AM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above the western region of Hainan Island in Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, issued the 430th serious warning.

### 美机侵入我广东省领空  我提出第四百三十次严重警告

1967-04-03
第6版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我广东省领空
    我提出第四百三十次严重警告
    新华社二日讯　四月二日十时五十五分至十一时十七分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省海南岛西部地区领海上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百三十次严重警告。
