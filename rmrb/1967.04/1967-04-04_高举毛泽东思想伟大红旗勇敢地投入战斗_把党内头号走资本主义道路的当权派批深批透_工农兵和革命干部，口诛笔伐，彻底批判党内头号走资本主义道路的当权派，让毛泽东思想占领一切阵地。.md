Title: Hold High the Great Red Banner of Mao Zedong Thought and Bravely Enter the Battle
Author:
Date: 1967-04-04
Edition: 1

Hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and bravely enter the battle. Thoroughly criticize the top-ranking capitalist-roaders in the Party. Workers, peasants, soldiers, and revolutionary cadres, speak out and write, thoroughly criticize the top-ranking capitalist-roader in the Party, and let Mao Zedong Thought occupy all positions.

The editorial staff of *Hongqi* magazine published an article titled "The Bourgeois Reactionary Line on the Cadres Issue Must Be Criticized" and Comrade Chen Boda's article "Patriotism or Treachery?" These articles have sparked a tremendous response nationwide. Workers, peasants, soldiers who are active in studying Chairman Mao's works, and revolutionary cadres have sent numerous articles and letters to this newspaper. They are greatly inspired by the great victory of the proletarian revolutionary line represented by Chairman Mao, and they are extremely indignant at the rampant promotion of revisionist goods by the top-ranking capitalist-roader in the Party. Below, we publish articles by Xie Yue, Gu Atao, Sun Leyi, and Zhang Ben. They explain from various angles, through their personal experiences, the great significance of uprooting the chief revisionist backstage manipulator in China, and express their resolute determination to carry the Cultural Revolution through to the end.

### 高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗勇敢地投入战斗  把党内头号走资本主义道路的当权派批深批透  工农兵和革命干部，口诛笔伐，彻底批判党内头号走资本主义道路的当权派，让毛泽东思想占领一切阵地。

1967-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗勇敢地投入战斗
    把党内头号走资本主义道路的当权派批深批透
    工农兵和革命干部，口诛笔伐，彻底批判党内头号走资本主义道路的当权派，让毛泽东思想占领一切阵地。
    本报讯　《红旗》杂志评论员文章《在干部问题上的资产阶级反动路线必须批判》和戚本禹同志的文章《爱国主义还是卖国主义？》的发表，在全国引起了巨大的反响。战斗在各个岗位的工、农、兵学习毛主席著作积极分子和革命干部，纷纷向本报来稿来信。他们对以毛主席为代表的无产阶级革命路线的巨大胜利，感到极大的鼓舞，对党内头号走资本主义道路的当权派猖狂地推行修正主义货色，感到极大的愤慨。下边我们编发了解悦、顾阿桃、孙乐义和张本同志的文章。他们从各个方面，用自己切身的体会，阐明挖掉中国这个修正主义总后台的重大意义，表达了他们把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底的坚强的战斗决心。
