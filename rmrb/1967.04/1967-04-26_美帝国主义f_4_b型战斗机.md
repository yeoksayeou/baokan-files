Title: The U.S. Imperialist F-4B Fighter Jet
Author:
Date: 1967-04-26
Edition: 4

The U.S. imperialist F-4B type fighter jet, which was shot down by our brave People's Liberation Army on the 24th, is the latest model of the U.S. imperialist navy's fighter jets. It is piloted by two crew members. Its maximum speed is between 2,300 and 2,500 kilometers per hour. Its maximum altitude is between 25,000 and 30,000 meters. It can stay in the air for up to four hours. It can carry four to six "Sparrow III" type air-to-air missiles or four "Sidewinder" type air-to-air missiles. The aircraft is equipped with an automatic pilot, infrared detection device, advanced navigation equipment, and a missile control system, among other things.

No matter what kind of latest model aircraft the U.S. imperialists use, and no matter how high or fast these bandit aircraft can fly, they cannot escape being shot down in the face of the People's Liberation Army of China, which is armed with the invincible Mao Zedong Thought.

### 美帝国主义F—4B型战斗机

1967-04-26
第4版()
专栏：资料

    美帝国主义F—4B型战斗机
    二十四日被我英勇的人民解放军击落的美帝国主义F—4B型强盗飞机，是美帝国主义海军最新式的战斗机。由两名飞行员驾驶。最大时速两千三百公里至两千五百公里。最大飞行高度两万五千米至三万米。空中飞行时间可达四小时。携带“麻雀Ⅲ”型空对空导弹四至六枚或“响尾蛇”型空对空导弹四枚。机上设备有自动驾驶仪，红外线侦测仪，新式导航仪和导弹控制系统等。
    不管美帝国主义用什么样的最新式飞机，也不管这些强盗飞机能飞多高多快，但是，它们在用战无不胜的毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民解放军面前，都逃脱不了被击落的命运。
                                        新华社
