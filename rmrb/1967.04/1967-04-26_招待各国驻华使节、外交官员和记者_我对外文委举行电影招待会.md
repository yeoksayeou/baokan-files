Title: Hosting Movie Screening for Foreign Diplomats and Journalists
Author:
Date: 1967-04-26
Edition: 5

The China Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a movie screening today, showcasing the feature-length color documentary "Chairman Mao is the Red Sun in Our Hearts," to entertain the diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China, as well as journalists from foreign countries based in China.

### 招待各国驻华使节、外交官员和记者  我对外文委举行电影招待会

1967-04-26
第5版()
专栏：

    招待各国驻华使节、外交官员和记者
    我对外文委举行电影招待会
    新华社二十五日讯　对外文化联络委员会今天举行电影招待会，放映大型彩色纪录片《毛主席是我们心中的红太阳》，招待各国驻中国使节和外交官员以及各国驻中国记者。
