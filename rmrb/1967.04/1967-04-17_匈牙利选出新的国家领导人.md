Title: Hungary Elects New National Leader
Author:
Date: 1967-04-17
Edition: 6

Hungary's new National Assembly held its first session on April 14.

The session, upon the recommendation of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the National Council of the Hungarian Patriotic People's Front, elected leaders for the National Assembly and the Presidium of the People's Republic. The election results were as follows: Pal Losonczy (former Minister of Agriculture) was elected Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic; Gyula Karoly (former Prime Minister) was elected Chairman of the National Assembly.

The session also, upon the recommendation of the Presidium of the People's Republic, approved the composition of the Revolutionary Government of Workers and Peasants, with Jenő Fock as the Prime Minister.

### 匈牙利选出新的国家领导人

1967-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    匈牙利选出新的国家领导人
    新华社布达佩斯十五日电　匈牙利新的国民议会于十四日举行了第一次会议。
    会议上，在匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会和匈牙利爱国人民阵线全国理事会的建议下，选举了国民议会和人民共和国主席团领导人。选举结果，洛松齐·帕尔（原农业部长）当选为匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席；卡拉伊·久洛（原政府总理）当选为国民议会主席。
    会上还根据人民共和国主席团的建议通过了工农革命政府的组成，由福克·耶诺任政府总理。
