Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Launches Heavy Attack on Quang Tri City, Inflicting Heavy Losses on U.S. Puppet Forces
Author:
Date: 1967-04-08
Edition: 5

	South Vietnamese Liberation Army Launches Heavy Attack on Quang Tri City, Inflicting Heavy Losses on U.S. Puppet Forces

	Liberation Forces Attack Nha Trang U.S. Airbase, Destroying 26 Enemy Aircraft

	Xinhua News Agency, April 7th: Saigon news: On the 6th, the liberation forces in Quang Tri Province, South Vietnam, launched a fierce attack on Quang Tri City and its surrounding areas. They briefly entered the city center, attacking U.S. military barracks, enemy strongholds, and military facilities, causing the enemy to suffer heavy losses.

	The U.S. news agencies exclaimed that this was the most ferocious and well-coordinated attack by the liberation forces on the U.S. puppet military positions in Quang Tri City to date.

	According to Western news agencies, in the early hours of the 6th, after a barrage of heavy artillery fire, brave liberation army soldiers, carrying explosive packages, charged into Quang Tri City, fighting the enemy to a standstill. Many enemy strongholds along the streets were attacked, and many enemy military facilities were destroyed.

	At the same time, two puppet military headquarters located eight kilometers south of the city were also attacked and captured by the liberation forces, with one puppet military commander killed on the spot.

	The airport, artillery positions, and U.S. "advisory" bases near Quang Tri City were also attacked.

	Initial reports indicate that in this well-coordinated attack, the liberation forces destroyed and damaged five enemy aircraft, killed and wounded 250 puppet soldiers, and also killed and wounded a number of U.S. aggressor troops; they destroyed seven armored vehicles.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 7th: According to the South Vietnamese Liberation News Agency, on the night of March 14th, the liberation forces in the Binh Dinh Province of South Vietnam launched a fierce attack on the Nha Trang U.S. airbase.

	According to initial reports, after a short battle, the liberation forces annihilated 100 U.S. aggressor troops, destroyed 26 aircraft of various types, damaged several more, destroyed six military vehicles, and demolished many enemy bunkers and facilities on the airbase.

	Nha Trang Airbase is an important U.S. airbase in central South Vietnam. Since 1963, the liberation forces have launched three fierce attacks on this airbase. In these three attacks, the liberation forces annihilated 230 U.S. aggressors and destroyed 49 enemy aircraft.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 7th: According to the South Vietnamese Liberation News Agency, from March 9th to 15th, the guerrilla forces and local troops in Tam Ky County, Quang Nam Province, fiercely intercepted the U.S. aggressor troops and South Korean mercenaries' "sweep" operations in the villages of Ky Thua and Ky Thang, and continuously attacked the enemy's military bases around Tam Ky County, annihilating 670 enemy troops, most of whom were U.S. aggressors; they shot down two helicopters.

	In the battle, many enemy platoons and companies were completely annihilated or severely damaged.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 7th: According to the South Vietnamese Liberation News Agency, the guerrilla forces and local troops in the provinces of Kon Tum and Dinh Tuong in South Vietnam have frequently launched attacks in the first three months of this year, annihilating nearly 900 enemy troops, including nearly 700 U.S. aggressors.

	The guerrilla forces and local troops in Kon Tum Province, in the first three months of this year, closely coordinated with the main forces to conduct large-scale operations, killing and wounding nearly 600 enemy troops, including nearly 400 U.S. aggressors, shooting down 18 enemy aircraft, and destroying many enemy barracks, bunkers, and weapons.

	The guerrilla forces and local troops in this province adopted various flexible tactics in battle. They would sometimes bravely infiltrate enemy strongholds to attack the enemy, and sometimes cleverly set up minefields and traps to annihilate the enemy.

	The local troops and guerrilla forces in Dinh Tuong Province, in the first three months of this year, continued to actively attack and intercept the enemy. According to statistics, they annihilated nearly 300 U.S. aggressors, shot down two helicopters, and seized a large number of weapons and military supplies.

### 南越解放军猛袭广治市重创美伪军  解放武装力量袭击芽庄美军机场毁敌机二十六架

1967-04-08
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放军猛袭广治市重创美伪军
    解放武装力量袭击芽庄美军机场毁敌机二十六架
    新华社七日讯　西贡消息：越南南方广治省解放武装力量六日向广治市和它的周围发动了猛烈袭击，他们曾一度攻进市中心，袭击了美军宿舍、敌军据点和军事设施，使敌人遭到了严重损失。
    美国通讯社惊呼，这是解放武装力量迄今为止对广治市美伪军阵地“发动的一次最猛烈和配合得最好的袭击。”
    据西方通讯社报道，六日凌晨，一阵猛烈炮火过后，英勇的解放军战士，带着炸药包冲进了广治市，打得敌人狼狈不堪，沿街许多敌人的据点都遭到了袭击，敌人许多军事设施都被炸坏。
    和这同时，位于城南八公里的两个伪军团部也遭到了解放武装力量的袭击，并被攻占，一个伪军团长被当场打死。
    广治市附近的机场、炮兵阵地和美军“顾问”驻地等也被袭击。
    初步消息透露，在这次配合得很好的袭击中，解放武装力量击毁和击伤敌机五架，打死打伤伪军二百五十名，另外还打死打伤了一批美国侵略军；击毁装甲车七辆。
    新华社河内七日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方庆和省解放武装力量三月十四日夜间猛烈袭击芽庄美军机场。
    据初步消息，解放武装力量经过短时间战斗，歼灭了美国侵略军一百名，击毁各种类型飞机二十六架，击伤多架，击毁军车六辆，摧毁敌人的许多碉堡和机场上的设施。
    芽庄机场是美军在南越中部的重要空军基地。自一九六三年以来，解放武装力量共三次猛烈袭击这个机场。在这三次袭击中，解放武装力量歼灭了美国强盗两百三十名，击毁敌机四十九架。
    新华社河内七日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方广南省三岐县游击队和地方部队，从三月九日到十五日，猛烈截击美国侵略军和南朝鲜帮凶军对岐茶、岐盛等乡进行的“扫荡”，并且接连进攻三岐县城周围的敌军驻地，消灭敌人六百七十名，其中大部分是美国侵略军；击落直升飞机两架。
    在战斗中，敌人许多排和连遭到全歼或被严重杀伤。
    新华社河内七日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方昆嵩和堤岸两省游击队和地方部队，今年头三个月，频繁出击，歼灭敌人近九百名，其中包括美国侵略军近七百名。
    昆嵩省游击队和地方部队在今年头三个月里，密切配合主力部队进行大规模作战，共打死打伤敌人近六百名，其中包括美国侵略军近四百名，击落敌机十八架，击毁敌人许多营房、碉堡和武器。
    这个省的游击队和地方部队在战斗中采取了各种机动灵活的战术。他们时而英勇插入敌人的据点打击敌人，时而巧设地雷阵和陷阱消灭敌人。
    堤岸省地方部队和游击队，在今年头三个月里，继续主动进攻和截击敌人。据统计，共消灭美国侵略军近三百名，击落直升飞机两架，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
