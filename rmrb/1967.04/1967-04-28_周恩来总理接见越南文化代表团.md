Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Vietnamese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-04-28
Edition: 6

Premier Zhou Enlai received the deputy chairman of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic's Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, Phan Hung, and the members of the Vietnamese cultural delegation led by him, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them this evening.

Present at the reception were Zhou Yiping and others.

Vietnamese Democratic Republic Ambassador to China, Vu Minh Quan, was also present.

(Photo attached)

The photo shows Premier Zhou Enlai posing with Phan Hung (third from the left) and other Vietnamese guests. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.

### 周恩来总理接见越南文化代表团

1967-04-28
第6版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见越南文化代表团
    新华社二十七日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上接见越南民主共和国对外文化联络委员会副主任范鸿，以及由他率领的越南文化代表团成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见的时候在座的有周一萍等。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾也在座。

    图为周恩来总理和范鸿（左三）等越南客人合影。　新华社记者摄
