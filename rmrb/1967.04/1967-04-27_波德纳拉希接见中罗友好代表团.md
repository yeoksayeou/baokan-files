Title: Podnarashu Receives Chinese-Romanian Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-04-27
Edition: 5

Romanian Communist Party Central Committee Standing Committee member and Deputy Prime Minister of the Socialist Republic of Romania, E. Podnarashu, received the entire Chinese-Romanian Friendship Delegation at noon on the 24th. The hosts and guests had a warm and friendly conversation.

After the reception, E. Podnarashu hosted a luncheon for the delegation. During the meal, E. Podnarashu said, "The Romanian people are genuinely happy about every achievement made by the Chinese people under the leadership of their tried-and-true leader and teacher, Chairman Mao Zedong, in socialist construction."

Those present at the reception and luncheon included Romanian Deputy Foreign Minister Mihai Marin and Vice Chairman of the Romanian Foreign Cultural Association A. Consica.

Chinese Charge d'Affaires in Romania, Li Bin, also attended the reception and luncheon.

### 波德纳拉希接见中罗友好代表团

1967-04-27
第5版()
专栏：

    波德纳拉希接见中罗友好代表团
    新华社布加勒斯特二十四日电　罗马尼亚共产党中央常设主席团委员、罗马尼亚社会主义共和国部长会议第一副主席埃·波德纳拉希，二十四日中午接见了中罗友好代表团全体成员，宾主进行了亲切、友好的谈话。
    接见后，埃·波德纳拉希设午宴招待了代表团。席间，埃·波德纳拉希说，罗马尼亚人民对中国人民在久经考验的领袖和导师毛泽东主席的领导下，在社会主义建设中所取得的每项成就都感到由衷的高兴。
    出席接见和午宴的有罗马尼亚外交部副部长米哈伊·马林和罗马尼亚对外文协副主席阿·康斯齐卡。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办李斌也出席了接见和午宴。
