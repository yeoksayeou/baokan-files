Title: Qingdao Steel Plant Unites All Possible Forces to Achieve New Success in the First Quarter
Author:
Date: 1967-04-02
Edition: 2

After seizing power, the revolutionary faction of Qingdao Steel Plant continues to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought. They unite all possible forces, concentrate on the revolution, and vigorously promote production, achieving new successes by completing the first quarter's production plan ahead of schedule.

The experimental workshop of this plant was the first to report completing the quarterly plan 28 days ahead of schedule. Steel, wire rods, steel pipes, thin plates, and shaped steel products all completed the national quarterly plan 11 to 22 days ahead of schedule. The quality of all products has improved, and the variety of products has increased.

### 青岛钢厂团结一切可以团结的力量  全厂第一季度取得新的成就

1967-04-02
第2版()
专栏：

    青岛钢厂团结一切可以团结的力量
    全厂第一季度取得新的成就
    据新华社济南三十日电　青岛钢厂革命派夺权以后，继续高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，团结一切可以团结的力量，狠抓革命，猛促生产，取得了第一季度全面提前完成生产计划的新成就。
    这个厂的试验车间以提前二十八天完成全季度计划首先报捷。钢、线材、钢管、薄板、型钢等产品也分别提前十一天到二十二天完成国家季度计划。各项产品的质量都有提高，品种增多。
