Title: Vietnamese Ambassador Presents Credentials
Author:
Date: 1967-04-12
Edition: 5

Vietnamese Ambassador Presents Credentials

New China News Agency, April 11 -- The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Vu Minh Luan, accompanied by the entire diplomatic staff of the embassy, presented his credentials to Vice Chairman Song Qingling this afternoon.

Vice Chairman Song Qingling had a warm and friendly conversation with Ambassador Vu Minh Luan.

Present at the presentation of credentials were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Hanchun and others.

### 越南驻华大使递交国书

1967-04-12
第5版()
专栏：

    越南驻华大使递交国书
    新华社十一日讯　越南民主共和国新任驻中国特命全权大使吴明鸾，偕同大使馆全体外交人员，今天下午向宋庆龄副主席递交国书。
    宋庆龄副主席同吴明鸾大使进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    递交国书时在场的，有外交部副部长韩念龙等。
