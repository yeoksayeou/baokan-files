Title: Leading Cadres of Yilan County Revolutionary Committee in Heilongjiang Province Work on the Frontlines of Spring Ploughing
Author:
Date: 1967-04-11
Edition: 3

The main leaders of the Yilan County Revolutionary Committee in Heilongjiang Province have gone deep into the frontlines of spring ploughing production. They are working alongside poor and lower-middle peasants, carrying black soil up the mountains, improving the soil, and striving to increase grain production this year.
