Title: Shanghai Railway Bureau East Station First Mechanical Team's Loading and Unloading Workers Persist in Studying and Applying Chairman Mao's Works
Author:
Date: 1967-04-13
Edition: 3

The loading and unloading workers of the First Mechanical Team at the East Station of the Shanghai Railway Bureau are steadfastly studying and applying Chairman Mao's works. They have taken to heart Chairman Mao's great call to "grasp revolution and promote production" and have put it into action, completing their tasks excellently.
