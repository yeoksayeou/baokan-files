Title: Raise High the Banner of the Revolutionary Criticism of Mao Zedong Thought and Actively Engage in the Struggle
Author:
Date: 1967-04-03
Edition: 1

Leaders of the Red Guards of the capital's colleges and universities and secondary schools have decided to mobilize all revolutionary teachers and students to thoroughly criticize, expose, overthrow, and annihilate the counter-revolutionary revisionist theories of the top capitalist-roader in the Party.

According to a report from our correspondent, leaders of the Red Guards of the capital's colleges and universities and leaders of the Red Guards of the capital's secondary schools, including Nie Yuanzi, Qiao Guobao, Tan Houlan, Han Aijing, and Li Dongmin, spoke to our correspondent about the publication of the editorial article "The Bourgeois Reactionary Line on the Cadres Issue Must Be Criticized" in the "Red Flag" magazine and the important article "Patriotism or Treason?" by Peng Zhen. They believe that these two articles have sounded the charge for a thorough criticism of the top capitalist-roader in the Party, marking a new and deeper stage in China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. They are determined to raise high the banner of the revolutionary criticism of Mao Zedong Thought, to mobilize all revolutionary teachers and students, to thoroughly criticize, expose, overthrow, and annihilate the counter-revolutionary revisionist theories of the top capitalist-roader in the Party, and to achieve new and decisive victories in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

### 高举毛泽东思想的革命批判旗帜积极投入战斗

1967-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    高举毛泽东思想的革命批判旗帜积极投入战斗
    首都大专院校和中学红代会负责人决心动员广大革命师生，把党内头号走资本主义道路当权派的一切反革命修正主义理论批深、批透、批倒、批臭。
    本报讯　首都大专院校红代会的负责人和首都中等学校红代会的负责人聂元梓、蒯大富、谭厚兰、韩爱晶、李冬民等同志，为《红旗》杂志评论员文章《在干部问题上的资产阶级反动路线必须批判》和戚本禹同志的重要文章《爱国主义还是卖国主义？》的发表，对本报记者发表谈话，认为这两篇文章吹响了彻底批判党内头号走资本主义道路的当权派的进军号，标志着我国无产阶级文化大革命进入了一个更加深入的新阶段。他们决心高举毛泽东思想的革命批判大旗，动员广大革命师生，把党内头号走资本主义道路的当权派的一切反革命修正主义理论，批深、批透、批倒、批臭，夺取无产阶级文化大革命的新的决定性的胜利。
