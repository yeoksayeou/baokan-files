	Brief News

	West German Masses Protest Against U.S. Aggression in Vietnam

	Recently, 150,000 people from all over West Germany participated in a massive Easter march over three days to protest against the U.S. aggression in Vietnam. This unprecedented march, divided into twelve groups, converged in nine major cities. They carried banners reading, "Johnson is a murderer!" and displayed pictures exposing U.S. atrocities in Vietnam. After the long march, mass rallies were held in various locations to express their protests.

	Yemenis Hold Anti-U.S. Demonstration

	Over 200 Yemeni workers and citizens recently demonstrated in Taiz to protest against the Saudi Arabian authorities, who, under the instigation of U.S. imperialism, murdered 17 Yemenis.

	The demonstrators carried banners reading, "Down with Imperialism!" As the procession passed the U.S. "Point Four Program" office, the demonstrators angrily shouted slogans such as, "No to dollars!" and "Yankees go home!"

	Puerto Rican Youths Protest Against Being Cannon Fodder

	The Socialist Alliance of Puerto Rico recently held a demonstration in Ponce, Puerto Rico, to protest against U.S. imperialism's forced conscription of Puerto Rican youths to serve in the Vietnam War.

	The Ponce Committee of the Socialist Alliance distributed a statement during the march. The statement expressed support for the Vietnamese people, who are fighting against the most vicious enemy of all peoples—the U.S. imperialists.

专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    西德群众示威反对美国侵越
    西德全国各地十五万群众最近连续三天举行声势浩大的复活节进军，抗议美国侵略越南的战争。这次规模空前的进军分成十二路，先后汇集到西德九个大城市。他们打着反对美国侵略越南的标语牌上写着：“约翰逊是杀人犯！”他们还举着揭露美国在侵越战争中的罪行的图片。在长途进军之后，各地群众都举行了盛大的抗议集会。
    也门人民举行反美示威游行
    两百多名也门工人和其他市民最近在塔伊兹举行示威，抗议沙特阿拉伯当局在美帝国主义的唆使下杀害十七名也门人。
    示威者举着“打倒帝国主义”的横幅标语。当示威队伍走到美国的“第四点计划”办事处的门前时，示威者愤怒高喊“不要美元”、“美国人滚出去”等口号。
    波多黎各青年反对充当炮灰
    波多黎各社会主义联盟最近在波多黎各蓬塞城举行示威游行，抗议美帝国主义强使波多黎各青年服义务兵役，到越南去充当美国侵越战争的炮灰。
    波多黎各社会主义联盟蓬塞委员会在游行中散发了一项声明。这项声明对正在同各国人民最凶恶的敌人——美帝国主义进行战斗的越南人民表示声援。
                          （据新华社讯）
