Title: Chairman Mao's Brilliant Works "The Three Articles" Published in Denmark
Author:
Date: 1967-04-18
Edition: 5

Chairman Mao's brilliant works "The Three Articles" — "In Memory of Norman Bethune," "Serve the People," and "The Foolish Old Man Who Removed the Mountains" — and "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People" have recently been published in Danish by the Danish "Future" Publishing House.

Since the end of last year, Chairman Mao's important works "On New Democracy," "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art," and "Speech at the National Conference on Propaganda Work" have been successively translated into Danish and published.

The publication "Guideline" of the Danish Communist Group on March 15 published in full Chairman Mao's article "Where Do Correct Ideas Come From?" to facilitate readers' study.

Title: "Quotations from Chairman Mao" Warmly Welcomed in Syria
Author:
Date: 1967-04-18
Edition: 5

The Arabic edition of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" has been warmly welcomed by the broad masses of the Syrian people. Since this precious book was published here, the initial print run of 3,000 copies was sold out within three weeks.

After obtaining this precious book, many readers could not put it down. Many readers, after leaving the bookstore, continued to read the book while walking on the street.

A young man named Tawil said: Chairman Mao is the leader of the revolutionary people of the whole world. Chairman Mao's books are the brilliant sun illuminating the globe. When I got a red "Quotations from Chairman Mao," I felt extremely happy and joyful. I always keep this precious book in my pocket; it is my most precious spiritual wealth.

A worker said: Although Chairman Mao is not here with us, every word he says has touched our hearts.

Another young man said: Chairman Mao's books are warmly welcomed by the revolutionary people of the whole world. Chairman Mao's books are full of revolutionary spirit, guiding us to fight against imperialism, revisionism, and reactionaries.

Reader Ibrahim said: I am eager to study this precious book. Chairman Mao is the red sun in my heart.

### 毛主席的光辉著作“老三篇”在丹麦出版  《毛主席语录》在叙利亚受到热烈欢迎

1967-04-18
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席的光辉著作“老三篇”在丹麦出版
    《毛主席语录》在叙利亚受到热烈欢迎
    新华社十四日讯　哥本哈根消息：毛主席的光辉著作“老三篇”——《为人民服务》、《纪念白求恩》、《愚公移山》——和《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》，最近由丹麦“未来”出版社用丹麦文出版。
    去年年底以来，毛主席的重要著作《新民主主义论》、《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》和《在中国共产党全国宣传工作会议上的讲话》已经先后译成丹麦文出版。
    丹麦共产主义小组的刊物《方针报》三月十五日全文发表了毛主席的文章《人的正确思想是从那里来的？》，以便于读者学习。
    新华社大马士革十六日电　《毛主席语录》阿拉伯文版受到叙利亚广大人民的热烈欢迎。这本宝书在这里出版以后，初版的三千册在三个星期内就全部售完。
    广大读者在得到这本宝书以后爱不释手。许多读者离开书店在街上走的时候还在翻阅这本宝书。
    一位名叫塔威勒的青年说：毛主席是全世界革命人民的领袖。毛主席的书是照亮全球的光辉灿烂的太阳。当我得到一本红彤彤的《毛主席语录》的时候，我感到非常幸福和喜悦。我总是把这本宝书放在口袋里，这是我最宝贵的精神财富。
    一位工人说：毛主席虽然不在我们这里，但是他的每一句话都说到了我们的心坎里。
    另一位青年说：毛主席的书受到全世界革命人民的热烈欢迎。毛主席的书充满着革命精神，指导我们同帝国主义、修正主义和反动派进行斗争。
    读者易卜拉欣说：我渴望学习这本宝书。毛主席是我心中的红太阳。
