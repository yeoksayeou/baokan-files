Title: Correctly Treat Revolutionary Youth
Author:
Date: 1967-04-09
Edition: 3

How to treat revolutionary youth is a matter of how to view the struggle between the two lines over the past few months. It is a matter of how to view the mass movement of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. It is a matter of whether one stands on the side of Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line or on the side of the bourgeois reactionary line. It is a matter of whether or not to cultivate and train successors to the proletarian revolutionary cause. If one denies the revolutionary youth, one is denying the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. If one suppresses the revolutionary youth, one is suppressing the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.
