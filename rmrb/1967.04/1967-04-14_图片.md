Title: Favorable Growth of Wheat in Fujian Province
Author:
Date: 1967-04-14
Edition: 3

Fujian Province's wheat is growing vigorously, and harvesting has begun in various parts of the province. The photo shows militiamen and revolutionary masses of Donghu Brigade, Dongfeng Commune, Putian County, working together to harvest wheat. Photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
