Title: Italian "Red Guards" Newspaper Enthusiastically Praises Our Country's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-04-06
Edition: 5

	Rome, April 5 (New China News Agency) — The March issue of the Italian Communist Youth League (Marxist-Leninist) organ, the "Red Guards," published an editorial stating: The revolutionary youth of Italy and the Red Guards of China share a common task in defeating imperialism, eliminating all monsters and demons, and completely eradicating capitalist exploitation and plunder from the earth, and in building a new socialist society worldwide. In this struggle, "a common leader unites us, that is, the Lenin of our times — Comrade Mao Zedong."

	The editorial emphasizes that for us today, learning and correctly applying Mao Zedong Thought is the guarantee of our victory.

	This issue of the "Red Guards" published the editorial along with a reply from the Beijing Red Guards of Mao Zedong Thought to the Italian Communist Youth League (Marxist-Leninist). It also published a photograph of Chairman Mao, with the caption "Our Great Leader."

	The editorial enthusiastically praises the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution personally initiated and led by Chairman Mao in China. It states: "China is successfully carrying out the Cultural Revolution. Every step of the cultural revolution is a step forward for the world's proletariat, a new achievement for the world revolution." The editorial exclaims: "The new experiences of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution are the revolutionary wealth of the world's proletariat."

	The editorial praises the new thing in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution — the Red Guards. It states that the Red Guards are carrying out a great revolution in China, building a new and stronger Great Wall in their country — a Great Wall composed of 700 million people educated by the thoughts of Chairman Mao Zedong, a Great Wall of the socialist revolution. It also states that the Cultural Revolution in China is fostering new socialist people, "liberating people from the various shackles of the old world, giving them a great weapon, a powerful weapon to resist imperialism and its running dogs — the weapon of Mao Zedong Thought."

	The editorial points out that the revolutionary youth of Italy and the Red Guards of China share a common desire, will, and leadership — Comrade Mao Zedong — to thoroughly defeat imperialism and its lackeys. It also points out that the Italian Communist Youth League (Marxist-Leninist) has the duty to struggle against the influence of imperialism and revisionism on the young masses, guiding the younger generation to fight for the socialist revolution. "To accomplish this task, we must arm ourselves with Mao Zedong Thought, study the works of Chairman Mao, and follow his teachings in our daily struggles."

	The editorial then enthusiastically praises Chairman Mao. It states: In the past, "a great revolutionary led the world's proletariat in struggle, that was Comrade Lenin, he was the person who made all reactionaries tremble, the leader of revolutionaries. Today, another great revolutionary leads the world's proletariat, that is Comrade Mao Zedong — the Lenin of our times, the teacher and leader guiding our struggle."

	The editorial points out that just as reactionaries and opportunists in the past maliciously slandered Lenin, today reactionaries and modern revisionists maliciously slander Chairman Mao. However, "this only increases the masses' admiration for this great commander."

### 意大利《红卫兵》报热情歌颂我国文化大革命  毛主席是全世界无产阶级的导师和领袖  中国红卫兵是文化大革命中培养出来的社会主义新人

1967-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    意大利《红卫兵》报热情歌颂我国文化大革命
    毛主席是全世界无产阶级的导师和领袖
    中国红卫兵是文化大革命中培养出来的社会主义新人
    新华社五日讯　罗马消息：意大利共产主义青年联盟（马克思列宁主义）机关报《红卫兵》三月号的社论说：意大利革命青年和中国红卫兵在击败帝国主义，消灭一切牛鬼蛇神，从地球上彻底消除资本主义剥削和掠夺，在全世界建立社会主义的新社会方面有着共同的斗争任务，在这一斗争中，“共同的领导把我们联合在一起，那就是我们时代的列宁——毛泽东同志”。
    社论强调指出，今天对我们来说，学习和正确地运用毛泽东思想是我们胜利的保证。
    这一期《红卫兵》在刊登中国毛泽东思想红卫兵首都兵团给意大利共产主义青年联盟（马克思列宁主义）的一封回信时，发表了这篇社论。它同时刊登了毛主席的照片，并且加上“我们伟大的领袖”题词。
    社论热情赞扬毛主席亲自发动和领导的中国无产阶级文化大革命，它说：“中国正在胜利地进行着文化大革命。文化革命的每一步都是全世界无产阶级向前迈进的一步，都是世界革命的一个新成就。”社论欢呼说：“无产阶级文化大革命的新经验是全世界无产阶级的革命财富。”
    社论赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命中的新生事物红卫兵，它说，红卫兵正在中国进行一场伟大的革命，正在他们的国家里建立一道新的更加强大的长城——由毛泽东主席的思想教育出来的七亿人民所组成的社会主义革命的长城。它还说，中国的文化大革命运动正在培养社会主义新人，“使人们从旧世界的种种束缚下解放出来，给他们以伟大的武器，反对帝国主义及其走狗的强大武器——毛泽东思想的武器”。
    社论指出，意大利革命青年和中国红卫兵有彻底打败帝国主义及其仆从的共同的愿望、共同的意志和共同的领导——毛泽东同志。它还指出，意大利共产主义青年联盟（马克思列宁主义）有义务同帝国主义和修正主义对青年群众的影响作斗争，引导年轻一代为社会主义革命而战斗，“为了完成这样的任务，我们必须用毛泽东思想武装自己，必须学习毛主席的著作，在我们的日常斗争中遵循他的教导。”
    社论接着热情歌颂毛主席。它说，过去，“一个伟大的革命家领导全世界的无产阶级进行斗争，这就是列宁同志，他是使一切反动派望而生畏的人，是革命者的领袖。今天，另一个伟大的革命家领导着全世界无产阶级，这就是毛泽东同志——我们时代的列宁，领导我们进行斗争的导师和领袖。”
    社论指出，就象过去反动派和机会主义者竭力诋毁列宁一样，反动派和现代修正主义者今天竭力诋毁毛主席，但是“这只会增加群众对这位伟大统帅的敬仰”。
