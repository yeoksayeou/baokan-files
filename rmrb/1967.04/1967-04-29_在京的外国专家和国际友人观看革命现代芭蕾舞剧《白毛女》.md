Title: Foreign Experts and International Friends in Beijing Watch the Revolutionary Modern Ballet "White-Haired Girl"
Author:
Date: 1967-04-29
Edition: 5

Foreign experts and international friends in Beijing were invited by the Foreign Languages Press to watch the revolutionary modern ballet "White-Haired Girl" performed by the Shanghai Dance School tonight. The performance was enthusiastically received by friends from various countries. They praised the ballet, saying it shines with the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought. After the performance, foreign friends excitedly shouted in unison: "Long live the Communist Party of China!" "Long live the Chinese proletarian cultural revolution!" "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!"

### 在京的外国专家和国际友人观看革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》

1967-04-29
第5版()
专栏：

    在京的外国专家和国际友人观看革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》
    新华社二十八日讯　在京的外国专家和国际友人今晚应对外文委邀请，观看了上海市舞蹈学校演出的革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》。今晚的演出受到各国朋友的热烈欢迎。他们称赞说，这个芭蕾舞剧闪耀着毛泽东思想的光辉。演出结束后，外国朋友激动地一遍又一遍地高呼：“中国共产党万岁！”“中国无产阶级文化大革命万岁！”“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”
