Title: Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee Established Today
Author:
Date: 1967-04-20
Edition: 1

	Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee Established Today
	Capital's Proletarian Revolutionaries and Revolutionary Masses Hold Celebration for the Establishment of the Revolutionary Committee

	Xinhua News Agency, April 19th

	In the midst of the triumphant songs of the proletarian revolutionary struggle for power and the great struggle and criticism against the top capitalist-roader within the Party, the temporary power organ of Beijing, which is illuminated by Mao Zedong Thought and consists of the "three-in-one combination" of revolutionary committees, will be formally established on April 20th. This is a great victory for Mao Zedong Thought! This is a great victory for the proletarian revolutionary line represented by Chairman Mao! This is a great victory for the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution! The proletarian revolutionaries and revolutionary masses of the capital will hold a grand celebration for the establishment of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee on the 20th.

	Central People's Broadcasting Station will start broadcasting the live coverage of the meeting at 1:45 PM on the 20th using the frequency of the first program. Beijing Television and Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will also broadcast simultaneously.

### 北京市革命委员会今日成立  首都无产阶级革命派和革命群众举行革命委员会成立和庆祝大会

1967-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市革命委员会今日成立
    首都无产阶级革命派和革命群众举行革命委员会成立和庆祝大会
    新华社十九日讯　在无产阶级革命派夺权斗争的凯歌声中，在对党内头号走资本主义道路当权派的大斗争、大批判中，闪耀着毛泽东思想光辉的北京市革命的“三结合”的临时权力机构——北京市革命委员会将于四月二十日正式宣告成立。这是毛泽东思想的伟大胜利！这是以毛主席为代表的无产阶级革命路线的伟大胜利！这是无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利！首都无产阶级革命派和革命群众将在二十日举行规模盛大的北京市革命委员会成立和庆祝大会。
    中央人民广播电台将从二十日下午一点四十五分开始，用第一套节目的频率，转播大会的实况。北京电视台、北京人民广播电台也同时转播。
