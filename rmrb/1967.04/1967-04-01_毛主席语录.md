Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-04-01
Edition: 1

All erroneous thoughts, all poisonous weeds, and all monsters and demons should be subjected to criticism. They must not be allowed to spread unchecked.

		*From the Speech at the National Conference on Propaganda Work of the Chinese Communist Party*
