Albanian "Warrior Daily" Marks the 97th Anniversary of Lenin's Birth

The Soviet Revisionist Leadership Completely Betrays Lenin's Cause

All Marxist-Leninists Around the World Will Hold High the Red Flag of Anti-Imperialism and Anti-Revisionism and March Forward Forever

	NEW DELHI, April 21 (Xinhua) -- The Albanian "Warrior Daily" published an article titled "Lenin on the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" on April 20 to commemorate the 97th anniversary of Lenin's birth. The article severely criticizes the Soviet revisionists' theories of the "state of the whole people" and "classless democracy."

	The article states that the modern Soviet revisionists have betrayed Lenin's cause. Like Kautsky and other leaders of the Second International, they use the slogan of the "state of the whole people" to deny the importance and necessity of the dictatorship of the proletariat.

	The article points out that, based on the experience of the Paris Commune and Marx's teachings, the proletariat must thoroughly smash the old state machine, including the bourgeois parliament, to achieve revolutionary victory. The bourgeoisie will never peacefully hand over power. The illusions of the Khrushchev revisionists about the bourgeois parliament are absurd. They advocate transitioning to socialism through peaceful means, doing a great favor for the bourgeoisie and reactionaries.

	The article states that experience has proven that in countries where socialism has already triumphed, the bourgeoisie has never given up the attempt to restore their lost "paradise." The working class of socialist countries standing on the Marxist-Leninist position should never weaken the dictatorship of the proletariat but should continuously strengthen it, preventing the exploiting classes from finding any opportunity to restore their rule. The article emphasizes that the dictatorship of the proletariat means democracy for the broad masses of the working people and dictatorship over the exploiting classes. Whether or not one acknowledges the Marxist-Leninist theory of the dictatorship of the proletariat is a fundamental issue of whether one stands on the side of Marxism-Leninism or revisionism.

	The article states that the so-called "state of the whole people" is a complete fallacy. Marxism-Leninism teaches us that there can be no classless state. Similarly, a socialist state cannot be classless. The state always has a class character. Under the guise of the "state of the whole people" and the "party of the whole people," the Khrushchev revisionists are turning the dictatorship of the proletariat in the Soviet Union into a new bourgeois dictatorship.

	The article states that imperialism, especially American imperialism, welcomes every new act of betrayal by the Soviet revisionist leadership in destroying the revolution and national liberation struggles as well as the socialist cause. The Soviet revisionist leadership has completely betrayed the interests of the Soviet people and Lenin's homeland.

	The article concludes by saying that no matter how the revisionists try to strangle Leninism, all Marxist-Leninists around the world are following Lenin's teachings and, in the struggle for the victory of the revolutionary cause, will hold high the red flag of anti-imperialism and anti-revisionism and march forward forever.

### 阿尔巴尼亚《战士报》纪念列宁九十七周年诞辰  苏修领导集团完全背叛列宁的事业  全世界马克思列宁主义者将高举反帝反修红旗永远前进

1967-04-22
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚《战士报》纪念列宁九十七周年诞辰
    苏修领导集团完全背叛列宁的事业
    全世界马克思列宁主义者将高举反帝反修红旗永远前进
    新华社地拉那二十一日电　阿尔巴尼亚《战士报》二十日为纪念列宁九十七周年诞辰发表题为《列宁论无产阶级专政》的文章，痛斥苏联修正主义者关于“全民国家”、“超阶级民主”等叛徒理论。
    文章说，苏联现代修正主义者背叛了列宁的事业。他们象考茨基和第二国际的其他头目一样，在“全民国家”的口号下，否定无产阶级专政的重要性和必要性。
    文章指出，根据巴黎公社的经验和马克思的教导，无产阶级要取得革命胜利，就必须彻底粉碎包括资产阶级国会在内的旧的国家机器。资产阶级决不会和平地让出政权。赫鲁晓夫修正主义者对资产阶级国会所寄予的幻想是荒谬的。他们鼓吹用和平的方法过渡到社会主义，为资产阶级和反动派帮了大忙。
    文章说，经验证明，在社会主义已经取得胜利的国家里的资产阶级，从来没有放弃过复辟他们已经失去了的“天堂”的企图。站在马克思列宁主义立场上的社会主义国家工人阶级，一刻也不应该削弱无产阶级的专政，而要不断地加强无产阶级专政，使剥削阶级找不到复辟的可能性。文章强调指出，无产阶级专政，就是说对广大的劳动人民群众实行民主，而对剥削阶级则实行专政。承认不承认马克思列宁主义关于无产阶级专政的理论，是站在马克思列宁主义一边，还是站在修正主义一边的原则问题。
    文章说，所谓“全民国家”，是彻头彻尾的谬论。马克思列宁主义教导我们，不可能有超阶级的国家。同样，社会主义国家也不可能是超阶级的。国家总是具有阶级性的。赫鲁晓夫修正主义者在“全民国家”和“全民党”的幌子下，正在把苏联的无产阶级专政变成新的资产阶级专政。
    文章说，帝国主义，特别是美帝国主义欢迎苏修领导集团破坏革命和民族解放斗争以及社会主义事业的每一个新的叛变行动。苏修领导集团完全背叛了苏联人民的利益和列宁的祖国。
    文章最后说，不管修正主义者怎样企图扼杀列宁主义，但是，全世界一切马克思列宁主义者正遵循着列宁的教导，在争取革命事业胜利的斗争中，将高举反帝、反修的红旗永远前进。
