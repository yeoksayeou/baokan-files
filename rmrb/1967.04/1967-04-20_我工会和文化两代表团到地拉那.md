Title: Chinese Trade Union and Cultural Delegations Arrive in Tirana
Author:
Date: 1967-04-20
Edition: 6

By plane, a Chinese trade union delegation led by Zhao Guoqing, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Tirana on the 18th.

The Chinese trade union delegation was invited to participate in the Sixth Congress of the Albanian Trade Unions and to observe the May Day celebrations.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the Chinese guests included: Yakov, Secretary-General of the Albanian Trade Unions Central Council, and Secretary Kambri, among others.

Chinese Ambassador to Albania, Liu Xiao, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

On the 18th, members of the Chinese cultural delegation, Zhang Junhua and Liu Chunlin, arrived in Tirana by plane. The delegation leader is Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Albania.

The delegation is coming to participate in the signing of the 1967-1968 implementation plan for the Sino-Albanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included: Treshka, Chairman of the Albanian Foreign Cultural Commission, and Spase, Vice-Chairman of the Albania-China Friendship Association, among others.

The delegation leader, Chinese Ambassador to Albania Liu Xiao, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 我工会和文化两代表团到地拉那

1967-04-20
第6版()
专栏：

    我工会和文化两代表团到地拉那
    新华社地拉那十八日电　由中华全国总工会书记处书记赵国强率领的中国工会代表团，十八日乘飞机到达地拉那。
    中国工会代表团是应邀前来参加阿尔巴尼亚工会第六次代表大会和“五一”观礼的。
    到机场迎接中国客人的有：阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会总书记亚科瓦，书记坎伯里等。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使刘晓也到机场迎接。
    中国文化代表团团员张俊华和刘春林十八日乘飞机到达地拉那。代表团团长是中国驻阿尔巴尼亚特命全权大使刘晓。
    代表团是前来参加签订中阿文化合作协定一九六七——一九六八年执行计划的。到机场迎接代表团的有：阿尔巴尼亚对外文委主席特雷斯卡，阿中友协副主席斯帕塞等。
    代表团团长、中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使刘晓也前往机场迎接。
