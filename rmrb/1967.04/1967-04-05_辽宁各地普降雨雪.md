Title: Heavy Rain and Snowfall Across Liaoning
Author:
Date: 1967-04-05
Edition: 3

From the afternoon of the 2nd to the night of the 3rd, various parts of Liaoning Province experienced a round of rain and snow. The areas of Chaoyang, which are typically prone to drought in the spring, received rainfall generally exceeding ten millimeters.

Currently, Liaoning Province is approaching the spring planting season. This round of rain and snow is beneficial for afforestation and the sowing of spring wheat.

### 辽宁各地普降雨雪

1967-04-05
第3版()
专栏：

    辽宁各地普降雨雪
    新华社沈阳四日电　辽宁省各地从二日下午到三日夜间，先后下了一场雨雪。历年春季易旱的朝阳地区降水量一般都在十毫米以上。
    目前，辽宁省即将进入春播时期，这场雨雪对植树造林和春小麦的播种很有利。
