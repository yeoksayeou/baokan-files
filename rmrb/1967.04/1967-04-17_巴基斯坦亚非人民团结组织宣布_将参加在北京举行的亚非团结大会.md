Title: Pakistan's Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization Announces Participation in the Afro-Asian Solidarity Conference to be Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-04-17
Edition: 6

	Pakistan's Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization announces
	participation in the Afro-Asian Solidarity Conference to be held in Beijing

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Lahore, April 16th—The Executive Committee of Pakistan's Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization recently held a meeting in Lahore, where a resolution was passed. The resolution declared that the decisions made by the Soviet revisionists at the illegal Eighth Afro-Asian Solidarity Council meeting held in Nicosia are invalid. It was also decided to participate in the Fifth Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Conference to be held in Beijing.

	The resolution stated that the results of the illegal Nicosia meeting show that the stance taken by the Executive Committee of Pakistan's Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization to boycott this meeting was correct.

	The resolution stated that the Fifth Conference to be held in Beijing "will become a historic milestone in the struggle for the liberation movement to completely eliminate the evil imperialist forces led by U.S. imperialism."

### 巴基斯坦亚非人民团结组织宣布  将参加在北京举行的亚非团结大会

1967-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦亚非人民团结组织宣布
    将参加在北京举行的亚非团结大会
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第十六日电　巴基斯坦亚非人民团结组织执行委员会最近在拉合尔举行的会议上通过一项决议，宣布苏联修正主义者在尼科西亚强行举行非法的第八届亚非团结理事会会议上所通过的决议无效，并且决定参加将在北京举行的第五届亚非人民团结大会。
    决议说，尼科西亚非法会议的结果表明，巴基斯坦亚非人民团结组织执行委员会采取抵制这一会议的立场是正确的。
    决议说，将在北京举行的第五届大会，“将成为争取彻底消灭以美帝国主义为首的全世界邪恶的帝国主义势力的解放运动斗争中的一个具有历史意义的里程碑”。
