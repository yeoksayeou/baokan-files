Title: Zambian Trade and Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-04-27
Edition: 5

The Zambian government trade and economic delegation, invited to visit China, arrived in Beijing this evening. The delegation is led by the Minister of Commerce and Foreign Trade of Zambia, Chimbwa.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun and others.

The delegation arrived in Guangzhou on the 21st and during their stay in Guangzhou, they visited the China Export Commodities Fair.

### 赞比亚贸易经济代表团到京

1967-04-27
第5版()
专栏：

    赞比亚贸易经济代表团到京
    新华社二十六日讯　应邀来我国访问的赞比亚政府贸易经济代表团，在赞比亚工商与对外贸易部长钦巴率领下，于今晚到达北京。
    到机场迎接的有对外贸易部代理部长林海云等。
    代表团是在二十一日到达广州的。
    赞比亚朋友在广州期间，曾参观了中国出口商品交易会。
