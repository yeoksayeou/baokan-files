Title: North Korean Chargé d'Affaires Holds Press Conference
Author:
Date: 1967-04-20
Edition: 6

North Korean Chargé d'Affaires Holds Press Conference

New China News Agency, April 18th: The Chargé d'Affaires of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's Embassy in China, Kim Jae-suk, held a press conference for Chinese and foreign journalists this morning to commemorate the seventh anniversary of the April 19th Uprising of the South Korean people. In the evening, Kim Jae-suk, the Chargé d'Affaires, held a film screening event. Relevant officials from our country were invited to attend the film screening event.

### 朝鲜临时代办举行记者招待会

1967-04-20
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜临时代办举行记者招待会
    新华社十八日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑，为纪念“四·一九”南朝鲜人民起义七周年，今天上午举行了中外记者招待会。晚间，金在淑临时代办举行了电影招待会，我国有关方面负责人应邀出席了电影招待会。
