Title: "Grasp the Revolution, Promote Production, Achieve Double Victory in Revolution and Production"
Author:
Date: 1967-04-12
Edition: 4

The book "Letter from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to Revolutionary Workers and Revolutionary Cadres in All Factories and Mines Across the Country," along with the short commentary "Put the Revolution in the First Place" from the Red Flag magazine, the editorial "Actively Support Factories and Mines to Grasp the Revolution and Promote Production" from the PLA Daily, "The Primary Task is to Support the Leftists Politically," and "The Transportation Situation is Excellent After the Takeover by the Harbin Railway Bureau" – these three typical materials have been compiled by the People's Publishing House into a book and published.

This book is titled "Grasp the Revolution, Promote Production, Achieve Double Victory in Revolution and Production." It began to be distributed in Beijing on April 12 and will also be distributed nationwide.

### 《抓革命，促生产，夺取革命和生产双胜利》一书出版

1967-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    《抓革命，促生产，夺取革命和生产双胜利》一书出版
    新华社十一日讯　《中共中央给全国厂矿企业革命职工、革命干部的信》，以及《红旗》杂志短评《把革命放在首位》，《解放军报》社论《积极支援厂矿企业抓革命促生产》、《首要的是从政治思想上支持左派》和《哈尔滨铁路局夺权后运输形势大好》等三篇典型材料，已由人民出版社汇集成书出版。
    这本书题名《抓革命，促生产，夺取革命和生产双胜利》，于四月十二日开始在北京发行，并将在全国各地发行。
