Title: Comrade Kang Sheng Meets and Hosts a Banquet for Comrades from the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninists)
Author:
Date: 1967-04-30
Edition: 3

Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, met and hosted a banquet for Comrade Osvaldo Becchi and Comrade Livio Lisciotti, members of the Political Bureau and the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninists) who are visiting China. They had a friendly and cordial conversation. Also present were Comrade Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and other comrades.

### 康生同志会见并宴请意共（马列主义）同志

1967-04-30
第3版()
专栏：

    康生同志会见并宴请意共（马列主义）同志
    新华社二十九日讯　中共中央政治局常委康生同志，今天会见并宴请了来我国访问的意大利共产党（马克思列宁主义）中央委员会政治局委员、书记处书记奥斯瓦尔多·贝契同志和里维奥·里萨利蒂同志，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。在座的有中共中央候补委员赵毅敏等同志。
