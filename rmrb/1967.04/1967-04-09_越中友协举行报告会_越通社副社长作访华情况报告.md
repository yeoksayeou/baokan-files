Title: Vietnam-China Friendship Association Holds Report Meeting
Author:
Date: 1967-04-09
Edition: 5

The Vietnam-China Friendship Association held a report meeting on the evening of April 5. Chen Qingchun, deputy director of the Vietnam News Agency, reported on his visit to China. Attendees included over 100 representatives from the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, including Vice Chairman Sun Guanguo, and relevant departments. Diplomatic officials from the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam were also invited to attend the meeting.

### 越中友协举行报告会  越通社副社长作访华情况报告

1967-04-09
第5版()
专栏：

    越中友协举行报告会
    越通社副社长作访华情况报告
    新华社河内电　越中友好协会五日晚上举行报告会，由越南通讯社副社长陈青春作访问中国的情况报告。出席报告会的有越中友协副会长孙光阀和有关部门代表一百多人。中国驻越南大使馆的外交官员也应邀出席了报告会。
