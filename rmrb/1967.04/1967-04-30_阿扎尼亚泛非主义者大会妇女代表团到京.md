Title: Delegation of the Women's Section of the Pan-Africanist Congress of Azania Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-04-30
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation, a four-member delegation from the Women's Section of the Pan-Africanist Congress of Azania (South Africa), led by Mrs. J. Segodje, arrived in Beijing today.

### 阿扎尼亚泛非主义者大会妇女代表团到京

1967-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    阿扎尼亚泛非主义者大会妇女代表团到京
    新华社二十八日讯　应全国妇联邀请来我国访问的阿扎尼亚（南非）泛非主义者大会妇女代表团一行四人，由团长杰·赛格杰夫人率领，今天到达北京。
