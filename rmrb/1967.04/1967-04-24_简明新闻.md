    Brief News

    Pakistani People Denounce U.S. War Hawk

    The notorious war monger, former U.S. Vice President Nixon, was met with angry condemnation by the local people when he arrived in Lahore, West Pakistan, on the 19th. When Nixon arrived at Lahore Airport, many people waved black flags in protest and shouted anti-American slogans: "Nixon get out!" "To hell with U.S. aid!" Nixon, who had planned to make a speech to reporters at the airport, seeing the situation was not good, did not speak and quickly got into a car and fled.

    Thai Soldiers Refuse to Fight in South Vietnam for U.S. Imperialism

    According to "The Voice of the Thai People" radio station, the policy of the Thanom-Praphat reactionary clique in Thailand, which forces soldiers to go to South Vietnam to serve as cannon fodder for U.S. imperialism's invasion of Vietnam, is encountering increasing opposition from Thai soldiers. The radio station reported that the Thai puppet Vice Premier Praphat recently admitted that 68 Thai soldiers have refused to go to South Vietnam.

    Two Thousand Brazilian Students Hold Anti-U.S. Demonstration

    About two thousand students in São Paulo, an important industrial city in Brazil, held a demonstration on the 21st to protest against U.S. cultural aggression in Brazil. The demonstration was held to protest the implementation of a cultural "aid" agreement signed by the U.S. and the former Brazilian military dictatorship of Castelo Branco. The demonstrators shouted, "Down with U.S. imperialism that seeks to destroy our culture!" They also burned a U.S. flag.

    简明新闻
    巴基斯坦人民怒斥美国战争瘟神
    臭名远扬的战争贩子、美国前副总统尼克松十九日到达西巴基斯坦拉合尔时，受到当地人民的愤怒谴责。
    当尼克松到达拉合尔机场的时候，许多人向他挥舞着表示抗议的黑旗，高呼反美口号：“尼克松滚蛋！”“美援见鬼去吧！”尼克松本来打算在机场向新闻记者发表谈话，一看形势不妙，话也不讲了，连忙钻进汽车，赶快溜走。
    泰国士兵拒绝去南越为美帝卖命
    据“泰国人民之声”电台报道，泰国他侬—巴博反动集团强迫士兵到南越去充当美帝国主义侵略越南的炮灰的政策，遭到愈来愈多的泰国士兵的反对。据这家电台说，泰国傀儡副总理巴博最近供认，目前已有六十八名泰国士兵，拒绝去南越。
    巴西两千学生举行反美示威游行
    巴西重要工业城市圣保罗的大约两千名学生二十一日举行示威游行，反对美国对巴西的文化侵略。这次示威是为抗议美国和巴西前布朗库独裁政权签订的一项文化“援助”协定开始生效而举行的。示威学生高呼：“打倒企图摧毁我国文化的美帝国主义！”他们还焚烧了一面美国国旗。
                            （据新华社）
