Title: Liberation Forces in South Vietnam Annihilate Over Six Thousand Enemy Troops
Author:
Date: 1967-04-23
Edition: 6

The liberation forces and people in the Quang Tri-Thua Thien region, the area around Saigon, and the Mekong Delta in South Vietnam, from late March to April 13, annihilated over 6,400 enemy troops, including more than 3,600 American invaders.

According to the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, from April 1 to 7, the people and military in the provinces of Quang Tri and Thua Thien south of the 17th parallel annihilated over 4,200 enemy troops, including more than 2,100 American invaders.

On April 4, the liberation forces launched simultaneous attacks on the 5/4 Hill, Dong Hu, and Long Thuy, annihilating over 400 enemy troops, including 200 American invaders. In the battle to capture the Long Thuy military training camp west of Hue City, the liberation forces annihilated 200 enemy troops, including 70 puppet military officers, in just a few minutes.

In the late night of April 5 and the early morning of April 6, the liberation forces attacked the La Vang, Chi Ha strongholds, and the city of Quang Tri, annihilating 1,828 enemy troops. They completely wiped out two enemy regiment headquarters, one battalion, ten companies, and three armored battalions (including one American armored battalion), destroyed 230 military vehicles, three aircraft, and eleven cannons, and burned eight fuel and ammunition depots.

From the late night of April 6 to the early morning of April 7, the liberation forces in Quang Tri Province heavily shelled the American base and artillery positions on Hill 241 and the American artillery positions at Yu Ling, killing and wounding over 1,700 American troops and destroying more than 20 heavy cannons.

The guerrilla forces and local units in Quang Tri-Thua Thien closely coordinated with the main forces, attacking the Hai Lang and Chau Phong military zones, blowing up five bridges on National Highway 1, and launching a series of attacks on 19 enemy strongholds from the northern part of Quang Tri Province to the southern part of Thua Thien Province, striking fear into the enemy.

In the areas around Saigon, the people and military in the provinces of Ba Ria, Bien Hoa, and Tay Ninh, from late March to April 8, coordinated their actions to annihilate over 1,500 American invaders.

From late March to April 7, the liberation forces in Ba Ria Province continuously intercepted and attacked enemy troops conducting "sweeping" operations along National Highway 2 and other areas, killing and wounding 326 American invaders and destroying 15 tanks and armored vehicles.

The liberation forces in Bien Hoa Province closely coordinated with the anti-"sweeping" operations of the people and military in Ba Ria Province. On the night of April 2, they heavily shelled the base of the American 11th Independent Armored Cavalry Regiment in the Chau Thoan area, annihilating over 1,000 American invaders, destroying 200 military vehicles, 35 helicopters, and 30 cannons. On the night of April 7, the liberation forces infiltrated the city of Bien Hoa, attacking the Bien Water oil depot and burning 120,000 liters of gasoline.

On the night of April 8, the liberation forces in Tay Ninh Province heavily shelled the American artillery positions in the Phu Loi area, 12 kilometers northwest of Han Tan City, annihilating over 200 American invaders and destroying four cannons and multiple tanks and armored vehicles.

In the Mekong Delta, the liberation forces also inflicted heavy losses on the enemy conducting "pacification" activities. On April 13, the liberation forces in the province of Can Tho thwarted a "sweeping" operation launched by the puppet 21st Division. Preliminary reports indicate that the liberation forces killed 400 puppet troops and shot down four enemy aircraft.

On the night of April 4 to the early morning of April 5, the liberation forces in the province of Chau Doc heavily attacked the enemy base in An Phu County, completely annihilating one company and two platoons of puppet troops, killing and wounding nearly 200 enemy troops.

On the night of April 1, the liberation forces in the Tam Quan area attacked the enemy stationed in the My An County, Phu Gia Pass, completely annihilating one puppet special forces company, killing and wounding 116 enemy troops.

### 南越解放武装力量又歼敌六千多  炮轰广治省美军驻地和炮兵阵地一夜毙伤侵略军一千七百多名

1967-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    南越解放武装力量又歼敌六千多
    炮轰广治省美军驻地和炮兵阵地一夜毙伤侵略军一千七百多名
    新华社河内二十一日电　越南南方广治—承天地区、西贡附近地区和湄公河三角洲的军民，从三月底到四月十三日期间，歼灭了敌军六千四百多名，其中有美国侵略军三千六百多名。
    据越南南方解放通讯社报道，十七度线非军事区以南的广治、承天两省军民，在四月份头七天中就消灭敌人四千二百多名，其中有美国侵略军二千一百多名。
    四月四日，解放武装力量在五四高地、东胡和隆寿等地，同时发起三次进攻，消灭敌人四百多名，其中有美国侵略军二百名。在攻克位于顺化市西面的隆寿军事训练营的战斗中，解放武装力量经过几分钟的战斗，就歼敌二百名，其中包括伪军军官七十名。
    四月五日深夜和六日凌晨，解放武装力量在进攻拉汪、慈下据点和广治市的战斗中，一举消灭了敌军一千八百二十八名，全歼敌人两个团指挥部、一个营、十个连、三个装甲营（其中包括一个美军装甲营），击毁军车二百三十辆、飞机三架、大炮十一门，烧毁汽油库和弹药库八座。
    四月六日深夜到七日凌晨，广治省解放武装力量猛烈炮击二四一高地的美军驻地和炮兵阵地以及犹灵美军炮兵阵地，打死打伤美军一千七百多名，击毁重炮二十多门。
    广治—承天游击队和地方部队同主力部队紧密配合作战，袭击了海陵和兆丰军事专区，炸毁了第一号公路上的五座桥梁，并对从广治省北部到承天省南部的伪军保安队、民卫队和“绥靖干部”的十九个据点，发动一系列的进攻，打得敌人胆战心惊，惊惶失措。
    在西贡周围地区，巴地省、边和省和土龙木省的军民，在三月底到四月八日协同作战，消灭美国侵略军一千五百多名。
    从三月底到四月七日，巴地省解放武装力量在第二号公路沿线和其他许多地方连续截击前往“扫荡”的敌军，打死打伤美国侵略军三百二十六名，击毁坦克、装甲车十五辆。
    边和省解放武装力量，同巴地省军民的反“扫荡”战斗紧密配合，在四月二日夜间猛烈炮击帅任地区的美国第十一独立装甲团基地，消灭美国侵略军一千多名，击毁军车二百辆、直升飞机三十五架和大炮三十门。四月七日夜间，解放武装力量又潜入边和市区，袭击边水油库，烧毁汽油一百二十多万公升。
    土龙木省解放武装力量，四月八日夜间猛烈炮击汉管市西北十二公里的平富地区的美军炮兵阵地，消灭美国侵略军二百多名，击毁大炮四门和坦克、装甲车多辆。
    在湄公河三角洲，解放武装力量也痛歼进行“绥靖”活动的敌军。芹苴省解放武装力量四月十三日粉碎了伪军第二十一师发动的“扫荡”。据初步消息，解放武装力量打死伪军四百名，击落敌机四架。
    朱笃省解放武装力量四月四日深夜到五日凌晨猛烈袭击安富县敌军驻地全歼伪军一个连、两个排，打死打伤敌人近二百名。
    同塔梅地区解放武装力量，四月一日夜间袭击驻在美安县平乔坡的敌军，全歼伪军一个别动连，打死打伤敌人一百一十六名。
