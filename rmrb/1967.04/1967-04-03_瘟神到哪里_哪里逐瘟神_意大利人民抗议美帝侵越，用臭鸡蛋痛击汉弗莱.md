Wherever the Plague God Goes, He is Driven Out
Italian People Protest U.S. Aggression in Vietnam, Pelting Humphrey with Rotten Eggs

According to a report from the New China News Agency on April 2nd, in Rome, Italy, U.S. Vice President Humphrey, who arrived on the 30th, was met with strong protests from the Italian people. Residents of Rome and Florence pelted Humphrey with rotten eggs and potatoes, condemning U.S. aggression in Vietnam.

Humphrey left Washington on March 27th to travel to various Western European countries to lobby for U.S. aggression policies. He has already visited Switzerland, the Netherlands, and West Germany and is scheduled to go to France and Britain.

The day after Humphrey arrived in Rome, thousands of students and workers demonstrated in front of the Italian Prime Minister's office and nearby streets. At that time, Humphrey was holding talks with Italian government officials.

During the demonstration, some young people hung flags of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and banners reading "Humphrey, go back" in front of St. Peter's Basilica in Rome.

On the 1st, nearly a thousand people demonstrated as Humphrey arrived in Florence. Along the route from Florence Station to Humphrey's residence, angry demonstrators threw lemons, potatoes, and rotten eggs at his car. Humphrey's face was hit by a lemon, and many potatoes, lemons, and rotten eggs were thrown into his car.

### 瘟神到哪里　哪里逐瘟神  意大利人民抗议美帝侵越，用臭鸡蛋痛击汉弗莱

1967-04-03
第6版()
专栏：

    瘟神到哪里　哪里逐瘟神
    意大利人民抗议美帝侵越，用臭鸡蛋痛击汉弗莱
    据新华社二日讯　罗马消息：美国副总统汉弗莱三十日到意大利活动，遭到意大利人民的强烈抗议。罗马、佛罗伦萨的居民用臭鸡蛋、土豆等痛击汉弗莱，谴责美国侵略越南。
    汉弗莱是从三月二十七日起离开华盛顿到西欧各国为美国的侵略政策进行游说。他已经去过瑞士、荷兰和西德，还将去法国和英国。
    在汉弗莱到达罗马的第二天，罗马数千名学生和工人在意大利总理办公厅前和附近的街道上举行了抗议示威。当时，汉弗莱正在那里同意大利政府官员举行会谈。
    在示威时，一些青年在罗马圣彼得大教堂面前，挂起了越南南方民族解放阵线的旗帜，和“汉弗莱滚回去”的横幅标语。
    一日，将近一千人在汉弗莱到达佛罗伦萨时举行抗议示威。在从佛罗伦萨车站到汉弗莱的住所的一路上，愤怒的示威者向他的汽车扔柠檬、土豆和臭鸡蛋等。汉弗莱的脸被一个柠檬击中，许多土豆、柠檬和臭鸡蛋被扔进他的汽车里。
