Title: Moroccan Ambassador to China Departs for Home
Author:
Date: 1967-04-12
Edition: 5

Moroccan Ambassador to China departs for home
New China News Agency, April 11th

Moroccan Ambassador to China, Filali, has been recalled and will leave today for his home country.
