Title: The Great Victory of Chairman Mao's Revolutionary Line – Warmly Congratulating the Birth of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee
Author:
Date: 1967-04-26
Edition: 2

The book "The Great Victory of Chairman Mao's Revolutionary Line – Warmly Congratulating the Birth of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee" has been published by the People's Publishing House. The contents of the book include: the letter of congratulations to Chairman Mao from the founding and celebration meeting of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, speeches by Premier Zhou Enlai, Comrade Jiang Qing, Comrade Xie Fuzhi, and Comrade Zhang Chunqiao at the founding and celebration meeting, as well as editorials from the People's Daily, Liberation Army Daily, Guangming Daily, and Beijing Daily welcoming the establishment of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee.

This book will be distributed in Beijing starting April 26 and will be distributed nationwide in due course.

### 《毛主席革命路线的伟大胜利——热烈欢呼北京市革命委员会诞生》一书出版

1967-04-26
第2版()
专栏：

    《毛主席革命路线的伟大胜利——热烈欢呼北京市革命委员会诞生》一书出版
    新华社二十五日讯　《毛主席革命路线的伟大胜利——热烈欢呼北京市革命委员会诞生》一书，已由人民出版社出版。全书内容包括：北京市革命委员会成立和庆祝大会给毛主席的致敬信，周恩来总理、江青同志、谢富治同志、张春桥同志在成立和庆祝大会上的讲话，以及《人民日报》、《解放军报》、《光明日报》和《北京日报》欢呼北京市革命委员会成立发表的社论。
    这本书于四月二十六日起在北京发行，全国各地也将陆续发行。
