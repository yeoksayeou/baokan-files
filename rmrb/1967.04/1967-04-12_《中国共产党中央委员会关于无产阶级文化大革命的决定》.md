Title: The Decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-04-12
Edition: 1

The Party's leadership must be adept at identifying the leftists, developing and strengthening the ranks of the leftist forces, and firmly relying on the revolutionary leftists. Only in this way can we, in the course of the movement, thoroughly isolate the most reactionary rightists, win over the middle elements, unite the majority, and, through the movement, ultimately achieve unity with more than ninety-five percent of the cadres and more than ninety-five percent of the masses.
