Title: Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, Xiao Hua, Yang Chengwu, and others listen to the revolutionary symphony "Sha Jianhu"
Author:
Date: 1967-04-27
Edition: 1

Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, Xiao Hua, Yang Chengwu, Zhang Chunqiao, Wang Li, Guan Feng, Chen Boda, Yao Wenyuan, Wang Dongxing, Ye Qun, and others listened to the Central Symphony Orchestra's performance of the symphony "Sha Jianhu" this evening.

The symphony "Sha Jianhu" is a symphony belonging to the proletariat and the working people. It is a brilliant example of carrying out art revolution in accordance with Chairman Mao's instructions to make the past serve the present, make foreign things serve China, and innovate. Tonight's performance was enthusiastically received.

### 周恩来陈伯达康生李富春江青萧华杨成武等同志听革命交响音乐《沙家浜》

1967-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来陈伯达康生李富春江青萧华杨成武等同志
    听革命交响音乐《沙家浜》
    新华社二十六日讯　周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青、萧华、杨成武、张春桥、王力、关锋、戚本禹、姚文元、汪东兴、叶群等同志，今晚听了中央乐团演奏的交响音乐《沙家浜》。
    交响音乐《沙家浜》是一部属于无产阶级和劳动人民的交响音乐，是遵照毛主席关于古为今用，洋为中用，推陈出新的指示，进行文艺革命的一个光辉样板。今晚的演出受到了热烈的欢迎。
