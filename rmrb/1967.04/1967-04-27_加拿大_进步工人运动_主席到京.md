Title: Chairman of Canada's "Progressive Workers' Movement" Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-04-27
Edition: 5

Chairman of Canada's "Progressive Workers' Movement" Arrives in Beijing

	New China News Agency, April 26th — Chairman of the Central Committee of Canada's "Progressive Workers' Movement," Jack Scott, and Central Committee member John Wood, at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Beijing today by airplane.

### 加拿大“进步工人运动”主席到京

1967-04-27
第5版()
专栏：

    加拿大“进步工人运动”主席到京
    新华社二十六日讯　加拿大“进步工人运动”中央委员会主席杰克·斯科特和中央委员约翰·伍德，应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。
