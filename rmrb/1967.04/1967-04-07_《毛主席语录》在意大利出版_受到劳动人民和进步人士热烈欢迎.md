Title: The Little Red Book Published in Italy
Author:
Date: 1967-04-07
Edition: 5

The Italian version of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" has recently been published in Italy by the Eastern Publishing House, receiving a warm welcome from Italian workers and progressive individuals.

The Italian edition of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" features a red cover, with a photograph of Chairman Mao and a dedication by Comrade Lin Biao printed before the text.

The first print run of this book was 5,000 copies, and a second print run of 20,000 copies is being prepared.

### 《毛主席语录》在意大利出版  受到劳动人民和进步人士热烈欢迎

1967-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    《毛主席语录》在意大利出版
    受到劳动人民和进步人士热烈欢迎
    新华社六日讯　罗马消息：意大利文版的《毛主席语录》最近在意大利由东方出版社翻译出版，受到意大利劳动人民和进步人士的热烈欢迎。
    意大利文版《毛主席语录》为红色封面，正文前印有毛主席的照片和林彪同志的题词。
    这本书的第一版印了五千册，第二版准备再印两万册。
