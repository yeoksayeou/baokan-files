Title: Support the Vietnamese People's Struggle Against U.S. Aggression for National Salvation
Author:
Date: 1967-04-02
Edition: 6

	Washington, April 1 (Xinhua) -- U Thant, who has been working for the U.S. imperialist "peace talks" deception on the Vietnam issue, gave a speech at a university in Michigan, USA, on March 31. In his speech, he exaggerated the horror of nuclear war. Students at this university held a demonstration in response, protesting against the U.S. aggression in Vietnam and supporting the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression for national salvation.

	The demonstrators held placards with slogans such as "Vietnam belongs to the Vietnamese people!", "Stop the U.S. murderers!", and "Victory will belong to the Vietnamese people!"

	U Thant dared not meet the demonstrating students and sneaked out through a side door of the university's auditorium.

### 支持越南人民抗美救国斗争  美国学生向吴丹示威

1967-04-02
第6版()
专栏：

    支持越南人民抗美救国斗争
    美国学生向吴丹示威
    新华社一日讯　华盛顿消息：在越南问题上为美帝国主义的“和谈”骗局奔走效劳的联合国秘书长吴丹三十一日到美国密执安州的一个大学讲演，他在讲演中大事渲染核战争恐怖。这个学校的学生为此举行示威，抗议美国侵略越南，并且支持越南人民的抗美救国斗争。
    示威者拿着标语牌，上面写道：“越南是越南人民的！”“制止美国杀人犯！”“胜利将属于越南人民！”
    吴丹不敢同示威学生见面，偷偷地从这所大学的礼堂旁门溜走了。
