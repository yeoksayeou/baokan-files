Title: China and Albania Sign Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1967-04-27
Edition: 5

The 1967-1968 implementation plan for the China-Albania cultural cooperation agreement was signed in Tirana on the 24th.

Representing the Chinese side to sign was the head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation and Chinese Ambassador to Albania, Liu Xiao. Representing the Albanian side to sign was the head of the Albanian Cultural Delegation and Chairman of the Albanian Foreign Cultural Commission, Treska.

All members of the cultural delegations of both countries attended the signing ceremony.

Also present at the signing ceremony were Albania's Minister of Education and Culture, Dejana, the President of the University of Tirana, Ili, and Albania's Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Malile, among others.

After the signing, Treska hosted a luncheon to entertain the entire Chinese Cultural Delegation. During the banquet, the hosts and guests engaged in very friendly and cordial conversations.

### 中阿签订文化合作执行计划

1967-04-27
第5版()
专栏：

    中阿签订文化合作执行计划
    新华社地拉那二十四日电　中阿文化合作协定一九六七——一九六八年执行计划，二十四日在地拉那签订。
    代表中国方面签字的是中国文化代表团团长、中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使刘晓；代表阿尔巴尼亚方面签字的是阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚对外文委主席特雷斯卡。
    两国文化代表团全体成员出席签字仪式。
    出席签字仪式的还有阿尔巴尼亚教育和文化部长德雅纳，地拉那大学校长伊利和阿尔巴尼亚外交部副部长马利列等。
    签字以后，特雷斯卡设午宴招待中国文化代表团全体成员。宴会上，宾主进行了十分亲切友好的谈话。
