Title: CPC Politburo Standing Committee Member Kang Sheng Meets Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) Leaders
Author:
Date: 1967-04-30
Edition: 5

On April 29, Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau Standing Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC) Central Committee, met with Osvaldo Becchi (second from the left), a member of the Political Bureau and the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist), and Livio Lisciotti (first from the left).

New China News Agency photographer.
