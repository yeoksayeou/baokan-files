Title: Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, Xiao Hua and Others Pose with Performers After Listening to the Central Orchestra's Performance of the Symphony "Sha Jianping"
Author:
Date: 1967-04-27
Edition: 2

Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, Xiao Hua and other comrades listened to the Central Orchestra's performance of the symphony "Sha Jianping" and then went on stage to pose for a photo with the performers. The photo was taken by a Xinhua News Agency reporter.
