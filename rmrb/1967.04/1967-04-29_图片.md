Title: "The Sea Voyage Relies on the Helmsman, All Things Grow with the Sun, Making Revolution Relies on Mao Zedong Thought"
Author:
Date: 1967-04-29
Edition: 3

"The sea voyage relies on the helmsman, all things grow with the sun, making revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought." The People's Liberation Army unit stationed in Beijing is actively promoting Mao Zedong Thought in the suburbs of Beijing. They firmly support the revolutionary faction of the proletariat in carrying the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end, and are determined to achieve victory in both revolution and production.
