Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Ambushes Near Qui Nhon, Destroying Over 300 Enemy Troops and Over 200 Vehicles
Author:
Date: 1967-04-02
Edition: 6

The South Vietnamese Liberation Army conducted an ambush near Qui Nhon on the afternoon of March 24, completely annihilating an enemy convoy carrying weapons and ammunition. They destroyed 148 enemy vehicles of various types and damaged 59 more. They killed, wounded, and captured 368 American, puppet, and South Korean mercenary troops, including the complete annihilation of one company each from the American and puppet forces, and one platoon from the South Korean mercenaries.

The Liberation Army also burned and destroyed many enemy weapons, including cannons and machine guns, and seized a large amount of weapons, ammunition, and military supplies.

According to a report from the South Vietnamese Liberation Communications Agency, the Liberation Army in the South Central Region recently held its second conference to honor combat heroes and those who have distinguished themselves in the fight against American aggression. The conference recognized the outstanding achievements of heroes who have fought on various fronts over the past two years in the struggle against American aggression and for national salvation.

The conference heard reports on the achievements of the South Central Region over the past two years and typical reports from 18 units and individuals. The conference decided to award the "Defeat American Aggressors" mobile red banner to a certain unit of the Liberation Army and the military and people of a certain province that have achieved outstanding results. Additionally, 81 units and individuals were awarded first, second, and third-class Liberation Medals.

The report stated that the awardees are all heroes who have made significant contributions on the battlefield. Guerrilla fighter Nguyen Van An, for example, eliminated 48 enemy troops and destroyed 23 weapons in a single battle. Liberation Army soldier Van Minh Long, along with five other soldiers, bravely and intelligently annihilated and repelled two enemy companies in a battle.

### 南越解放军在岘港附近进行伏击战  歼灭敌人三百多　毁伤军车二百多辆

1967-04-02
第6版()
专栏：

    南越解放军在岘港附近进行伏击战
    歼灭敌人三百多　毁伤军车二百多辆
    新华社河内一日电  据越南南方解放通讯社补充报道，越南南方广南省解放武装力量，三月二十四日下午在岘港南面进行的一次伏击战中，全歼敌人的一支运载武器弹药的车队，击毁敌人的各种军车一百四十八辆，打坏五十九辆；打死、打伤和俘虏美、伪军及南朝鲜帮凶军三百六十八名，其中包括全歼美、伪军各一个连和南朝鲜帮凶军一个排。
    解放武装力量还烧毁和击毁敌人许多武器，其中包括大炮和机关枪，缴获了大量武器、弹药和军用物资。
    据新华社河内一日电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方中部南区解放武装力量最近召开第二次战斗模范和歼美勇士大会，表彰两年来在抗美救国斗争中，在各条战线上创立了出色成绩的英雄战士。
    大会听取了中部南区两年来的战绩报告和十八个单位和个人的典型报告。会议决定把“决战决胜美国侵略强盗”的流动红旗，奖给取得优异成绩的解放武装力量某部和某省军民，同时分别授予八十一个单位和个人以一、二、三级解放战功勋章。
    报道说，受奖的人都是曾经在战场上立下了显赫战功的英雄战士。游击队员阮文安，曾经在一次战斗中消灭敌人四十八名，击毁武器二十三件。解放军战士文明长，同另外五名战士一起，机智勇敢地在一次战斗中消灭和击溃敌人两个连。
