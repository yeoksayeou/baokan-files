Title: British People Denounce Murderer Humphrey
Author:
Date: 1967-04-04
Edition: 6

On the evening of April 2, U.S. Vice President Humphrey arrived in London for a three-day talk with the British government. Large quantities of banners appeared on the streets of London, strongly protesting against U.S. aggression in Vietnam.

Humphrey and British Prime Minister Wilson will hold talks at the Prime Minister's residence, Chequers. On a wall with an arrow pointing to Chequers, it was written: "Meeting of War Criminals." Written across a road half a mile away was: "Vietnam War Conference." On a wooden fence a few yards ahead, it was written: "Murderer Humphrey!" Further ahead on the road, large white letters read: "Humphrey, Murderer in Vietnam."

The British authorities deployed seventy police officers to protect this U.S. Vice President.

### 英国人民怒斥杀人犯汉弗莱

1967-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

    英国人民怒斥杀人犯汉弗莱
    新华社伦敦三日电　在美国副总统汉弗莱二日晚上到达这里同英国政府举行为期三天会谈的时候，伦敦街头出现大量标语，强烈抗议美国侵略越南。
    汉弗莱和英国首相威尔逊将在首相别墅契克斯举行会谈。在标着通向契克斯的箭头的一座墙壁上写道：“战争罪犯的会议”。横写在离这里半英里的大道上的是：“越南战争会议”。在前面几码远的一道木栅栏上还写道：“杀人犯汉弗莱！”再前面，路上又用白色大字写道：“汉弗莱，在越南的杀人犯”。
    英国当局调了七十名警察来保卫这位美国副总统。
