Title: Tanzanian People Celebrate the Third Anniversary of the Founding of the Republic
Author:
Date: 1967-04-29
Edition: 4

Tanzania held various activities across the country on April 26 to celebrate the third anniversary of the founding of the United Republic of Tanzania.

In the capital, Dar es Salaam, a military parade, a march by people from all walks of life, and a mass rally were held. Tanzanian President Julius Nyerere, First Vice President Abeid Karume, and Second Vice President Rashid Kawawa attended these celebrations.

Nyerere inspected the ranks of the People's Defense Force, the police, and the National Service Corps, and spoke at the mass rally. He called on all Tanzanians to strive to implement the policy of self-reliance.

In Zanzibar City, marches and mass rallies were also held. First Vice President Karume, in his speech at the rally, called on the Tanzanian people to unite, stay vigilant, consolidate and defend independence, and rely on their own efforts to build Tanzania.

He pointed out: "The struggle for independence was a hard struggle, and consolidating and defending independence requires even greater vigilance. We will not allow our independence to be exploited by others."

Over the past two days, various activities such as marches, mass rallies, football matches, cultural performances, and traditional dances were held in other parts of Tanzania to celebrate the National Day.

### 坦桑尼亚人民欢庆建国三周年  尼雷尔总统号召人民实现自力更生

1967-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚人民欢庆建国三周年
    尼雷尔总统号召人民实现自力更生
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆二十六日电　坦桑尼亚全国各地二十六日举行各种活动，庆祝坦桑尼亚联合共和国建国三周年。
    在首都达累斯萨拉姆，举行了军事检阅、各界人民的游行和群众大会。坦桑尼亚总统尼雷尔、第一副总统卡鲁姆和第二副总统卡瓦瓦出席了这些庆祝活动。
    尼雷尔检阅了人民国防军、警察和全国服务青年的队伍，并在群众大会上讲了话。他号召坦桑尼亚全国人民都要努力为实现自力更生的政策而发挥作用。
    在桑给巴尔市，也举行了游行和群众大会。第一副总统卡鲁姆在大会上讲话时，号召坦桑尼亚人民团结起来，提高警惕，巩固和维护独立，依靠自己的努力来建设坦桑尼亚。
    他指出：“争取独立的斗争是艰苦的斗争，而巩固和维护独立，需要更高的警惕性。我们决不让我们的独立被别人所利用。”
    在过去两天中，坦桑尼亚全国其他各地也举行游行、群众大会、足球比赛、文娱活动、民族舞蹈表演等，庆祝国庆节。
