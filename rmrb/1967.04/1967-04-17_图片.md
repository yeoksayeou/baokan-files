Title: Revolutionary Workers of Shanghai No. 10 Cotton Mill Unite with the Masses and Revolutionary Cadres to Promote Production
Author:
Date: 1967-04-17
Edition: 2

Revolutionary workers of Shanghai No. 10 Cotton Mill have united with the broad masses and revolutionary cadres to seize the revolution and promote production. Production is thriving. This mill completed the first quarter's national production plan three days ahead of schedule. Three types of products have set new historical records in production rates, and the quality of most products has improved compared to the past.
