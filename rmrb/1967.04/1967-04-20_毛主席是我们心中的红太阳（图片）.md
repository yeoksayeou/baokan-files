Title: Chairman Mao is the Red Sun in Our Hearts
Author:
Date: 1967-04-20
Edition: 4

The large-scale color documentary film that the hundreds of millions of revolutionary people have longed for, marking the 1966 National Day, has begun its screenings.

Amidst the drumbeats of criticizing the number one capitalist-roader in the Party, and under the excellent situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the large-scale color documentary film "Chairman Mao is the Red Sun in Our Hearts - Celebrating the 17th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China" has begun screening in the capital and various parts of the country. This film truthfully recreates the touching scenes of Chairman Mao's heart-to-heart connection with the revolutionary masses, greatly inspiring the revolutionary enthusiasm of the broad masses, and encouraging their determination to thoroughly criticize and denounce the number one capitalist-roader in the Party and to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end.

Dear Chairman Mao, we have so many heartfelt words to tell you, and we have so many passionate songs to sing for you. Millions of red hearts are beating fiercely, and thousands of smiling faces are turned towards the red sun. We sincerely wish you, Chairman Mao, a long and healthy life! Chairman Mao is the red sun in our hearts, and the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought shines resplendently. We love Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought with all our hearts. Following Mao Zedong, we will forever make revolution. Following Mao Zedong, the world will be red! Look! Chairman Mao appears on the screen! We clap our hands and cheer, we are overjoyed. Chairman Mao! We are your good children, forever reading your books, listening to your words, and growing up quickly under the nurturing of your brilliant thoughts.

	Our hearts beat fiercely with the hearts of the Red Guards on the screen. Let us together wish the great teacher, great leader, great supreme commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao a long and healthy life! A long and healthy life!

A red sun rises in the east, and countless golden rays shine brightly. The screen displays the glorious image of the red sun in our hearts, Chairman Mao! We will definitely hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, denounce the number one capitalist-roader in the Party, and carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end.

Look! Chairman Mao, with a radiant face and a spirited demeanor, is waving to us! In the excellent situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in our country, being able to see on the screen the red sun in our hearts, whom we miss day and night, is our greatest happiness.

### 毛主席是我们心中的红太阳（图片）

1967-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

    毛主席是我们心中的红太阳
    亿万革命人民渴望已久的一九六六年国庆节大型彩色文献纪录片开始上映 
    在批判党内头号走资本主义道路当权派的战鼓声中，在无产阶级文化大革命的大好形势下，亿万人民渴望已久的大型彩色文献纪录片《毛主席是我们心中的红太阳——庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年》开始在首都和全国各地陆续上映了。这部影片真实地再现了毛主席和革命群众心连心的动人情景，极大地激励了广大革命群众的斗志，鼓舞了他们把党内头号走资本主义道路当权派批倒批臭、把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底的决心。
    敬爱的毛主席，我们有多少贴心的话要对您讲，我们有多少热情的歌要给您唱，千万颗红心在激烈地跳动，千百张笑脸迎向红太阳，我们衷心地祝福您老人家万寿无疆！毛主席是我们心中的红太阳，毛泽东思想的光芒灿烂辉煌。我们无限热爱毛主席，无限热爱毛泽东思想。跟着毛泽东，永远闹革命，跟着毛泽东，世界一片红！看！毛主席在银幕上出现了！我们拍手欢呼，我们欣喜若狂。毛主席呀！我们是您的好孩子，永远读您的书，听您的话，在您的光辉思想哺育下快快长大。
    本报记者摄
    我们的心和影片中红卫兵小将们的心一起激烈地跳动，让我们一起祝愿伟大导师、伟大领袖、伟大统帅、伟大舵手毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！
                            本报记者摄
    一轮红日东升，万道金光闪闪。银幕上映现出我们心中最红最红的红太阳毛主席的光辉形象！我们一定更高地举起毛泽东思想的伟大红旗，把党内头号走资本主义道路的当权派批倒批臭，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底。
                            本报记者摄
    看啊，毛主席满面红光、神采奕奕地在向我们招手！在我国无产阶级文化大革命的一片大好形势下，能够在银幕上见到我们日夜想念的心中最红最红的红太阳——毛主席，是我们最大的幸福。　本报记者摄
