Communist Party members should adopt an attitude of persuasion, not rejection, towards those who have made mistakes in their work, except for those who are beyond redemption. They should help them to change their ways and start anew.

		— *The Position of the Chinese Communist Party in the National War*

### 毛主席语录

1967-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    共产党员对于在工作中犯过错误的人们，除了不可救药者外，不是采取排斥态度，而是采取规劝态度，使之翻然改进，弃旧图新。
                            《中国共产党在民族战争中的地位》
