Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1967-04-19
Edition: 6

U.S. warships and aircraft intrude into our territorial waters and airspace
We issue the 432nd serious warning

New China News Agency, April 18th: On April 18th from 3:35 AM to 9:40 AM, one U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters east of Pingtan Island and the Dachen Islands in Fujian Province. On the same day, from 9:21 AM to 9:33 AM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Yongxing Island and Zhao Shuo Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on instruction, issues the 432nd serious warning.

### 美国军舰、飞机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百三十二次严重警告

1967-04-19
第6版()
专栏：

    美国军舰、飞机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百三十二次严重警告
    新华社十八日讯　四月十八日三时三十五分至九时四十分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省平潭岛、四礵列岛以东地区领海。同日九时二十一分至九时三十三分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、赵述岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百三十二次严重警告。
