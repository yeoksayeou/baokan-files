Title: Comrades Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and Others Watch the Revolutionary Modern Ballet "The White-Haired Girl"
Author:
Date: 1967-04-19
Edition: 1

Comrades Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, Xiao Hua, Yang Chengwu, Zhang Chunqiao, Wang Li, Guan Feng, Chen Boda, Yao Wenyuan, Wang Dongxing, Ye Qun, and others watched the performance of the revolutionary modern ballet "The White-Haired Girl" at the Capital Theater tonight by the Shanghai Dance School.

This ballet was born during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and has been praised as a shining gem of Mao Zedong Thought in the arts. It is on a par with revolutionary model operas like "Taking Tiger Mountain by Strategy" and has once again been warmly received by the capital's audience tonight.

After the performance of the revolutionary modern ballet "The White-Haired Girl," Comrades Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and others posed for a group photo with the performers. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 陈伯达康生江青等同志  观看革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》

1967-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    陈伯达康生江青等同志
    观看革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》
    新华社十八日讯  上海市舞蹈学校今天晚上在首都剧场演出了大型革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》。陈伯达、康生、江青、萧华、杨成武、张春桥、王力、关锋、戚本禹、姚文元、汪东兴、叶群等同志观看了演出。
    这出芭蕾舞剧是在无产阶级文化大革命中诞生的，曾被人们誉为闪耀着毛泽东思想光辉的艺术明珠，是同革命现代京剧《沙家浜》等齐名的革命文艺好样板，在今晚的演出中，它再一次受到首都观众极为热烈的欢迎。
    （附图片）
    在革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》演出后，陈伯达、康生、江青等同志与演员合影。　新华社记者摄
