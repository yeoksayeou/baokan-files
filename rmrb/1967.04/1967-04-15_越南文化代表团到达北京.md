Title: Vietnamese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-04-15
Edition: 6

The Vietnamese cultural delegation, led by Vice Chairman of the Vietnamese Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Phan Hong, arrived in Beijing by airplane today. The delegation is here to sign the 1967 implementation plan for the Sino-Vietnamese cultural cooperation agreement and to conduct a friendly visit.

### 越南文化代表团到达北京

1967-04-15
第6版()
专栏：

    越南文化代表团到达北京
    新华社十四日讯　由越南对外文委副主任范鸿率领的越南文化代表团，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团是前来签订中越文化合作协定一九六七年执行计划，并进行友好访问的。
