Title: Comrade Enver Hoxha Receives Our Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1967-04-14
Edition: 6

Comrade Enver Hoxha received our ambassador.

New China News Agency, Tirana, April 12 — On April 12, Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, received Liu Xiao, the new Chinese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Albania, at the Party Central Committee Building.

Comrade Hoxha had a very friendly and cordial conversation with Ambassador Liu Xiao.

Present at the reception were Behar Shytura, member of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania.

### 霍查同志接见我大使

1967-04-14
第6版()
专栏：

    霍查同志接见我大使
    新华社地拉那十二日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查十二日在党中央大厦接见了中国新任驻阿尔巴尼亚特命全权大使刘晓。
    霍查同志同刘晓大使进行了极为亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员贝哈尔·什图拉。
