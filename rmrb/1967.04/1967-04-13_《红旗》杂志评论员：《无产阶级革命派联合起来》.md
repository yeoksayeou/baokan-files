Title: The Revolutionary Masses Should Unite
Author: 
Date: 1967-04-13
Edition: 1

Only under the banner of Mao Zedong Thought, by implementing democratic centralism, and by achieving the great unity of the revolutionary masses of the proletariat, can we realize great democracy under the dictatorship of the proletariat, concentrate our strength to fight victorious battles, and establish a new revolutionary order.
