Title: McNamara Shouts for "Containment" of China
Author:
Date: 1967-04-14
Edition: 6

	Washington, April 12 (Xinhua) - U.S. Secretary of Defense McNamara on April 11 submitted to Congress the U.S. military "aid" plan for the next fiscal year. This plan shows that, with the close cooperation of the Soviet revisionist leadership, U.S. imperialism regards China as the main obstacle to its counter-revolutionary global strategy.

	According to McNamara's report, out of the total of $596 million in military "aid" for the next fiscal year, $282.1 million will be allocated to the Far East region. Of this amount, 88% will be specifically used to "strengthen" the military forces of the Chiang Kai-shek bandits and the South Korean puppet regime. McNamara shouted that "the situation in South Vietnam and our relations with Red China are the key to the Far East military aid plan," and that a major goal of U.S. military "aid" to the Far East remains the "strategic containment" of China.

### 麦克纳马拉叫嚷要对中国进行“抑制”  主张给蒋匪帮和南朝鲜傀儡大量军事“援助”

1967-04-14
第6版()
专栏：

    麦克纳马拉叫嚷要对中国进行“抑制”
    主张给蒋匪帮和南朝鲜傀儡大量军事“援助”
    据新华社十二日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国防部长麦克纳马拉十一日向国会提出美国下一财政年度的军事“援助”计划。这项计划表明，得到苏修领导集团密切配合的美帝国主义，把中国看作是它推行反革命全球战略的主要障碍。
    根据麦克纳马拉的报告，在美国下一财政年度总额为五亿九千六百万美元的军事“援助”中，有二亿八千二百一十万将用于远东地区，而其中的百分之八十八将专门用来“加强”蒋介石匪帮和南朝鲜傀儡集团的“军事力量”。麦克纳马拉叫嚷“南越局势和我们同赤色中国的关系是对远东军事援助计划的关键”，美国对远东军事“援助”的一个重大目标仍然是对中国进行“战略抑制”。
