Title: Delegation of Burundian Revolutionary Youth Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-04-30
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation, a delegation of three members from the Burundian Ruwagaso Revolutionary Youth Organization arrived in Beijing today. The delegation is led by François Kisamare, the Minister of National Education and Culture of Burundi and the External Affairs Secretary of the Ruwagaso Revolutionary Youth Organization.

### 布隆迪革命青年代表团到京

1967-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    布隆迪革命青年代表团到京
    新华社二十八日讯　应全国青联邀请前来我国进行友好访问的布隆迪鲁瓦加索尔革命青年组织代表团一行三人，在布隆迪国民教育和文化部长、鲁瓦加索尔革命青年组织外事书记弗朗索瓦·基萨马雷的率领下，今天到达北京。
