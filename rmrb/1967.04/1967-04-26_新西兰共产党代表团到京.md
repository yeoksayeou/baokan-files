Title: New Zealand Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-04-26
Edition: 5

A delegation from the New Zealand Communist Party, led by Ralph Hegeman, a member of the Political Committee of the National Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party, arrived in Beijing today by plane. The delegation was invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to participate in the celebrations for International Labor Day on May 1st and to conduct a visit and tour.

	Those who warmly welcomed the New Zealand Communist Party delegation at the airport included the alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhao Yimin, and others.

### 新西兰共产党代表团到京

1967-04-26
第5版()
专栏：

    新西兰共产党代表团到京
    新华社二十五日讯　由新西兰共产党全国委员会政治委员会委员拉尔夫·赫格曼率领的新西兰共产党代表团，应中共中央邀请前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和参观访问，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场热烈欢迎新共代表团的，有中共中央候补委员赵毅敏等。
