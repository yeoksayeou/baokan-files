	French People Protest Against U.S. Aggression in Vietnam
	Two hundred residents of Amiens in northern France held a demonstration on April 14 to protest against U.S. aggression in Vietnam. The demonstrators burned an American flag, which had a swastika, the symbol of Nazi Hitler, drawn on it. The demonstration took place when U.S. Ambassador to France, Mr. Bohlen, was in Amiens to preside over an American art exhibition.

	Italian People Hold Widespread Anti-U.S. Demonstrations
	People in Rome and other cities in Italy have held demonstrations in recent days to protest against U.S. aggression in Vietnam.

	Several hundred Italians demonstrated in front of the U.S. Embassy in Rome on April 12, opposing the U.S. war of aggression in Vietnam.

	Police arrived to suppress the demonstrators. The demonstrators bravely fought back with stones and whatever they could grab, defending themselves. In one hour of struggle, dozens were injured, and 35 demonstrators were arrested.

	In the past few days, people in other cities in Italy have also held anti-U.S. demonstrations and marches.

	150,000 Greek Construction Workers Strike
	150,000 Greek construction workers went on a 24-hour nationwide strike on April 12, demanding wage increases and improved working conditions.

	On that day, 2,000 striking workers in Athens held a rally and then marched to the Greek Ministry of Labor. The demonstrators shouted slogans against the Greek reactionary government and U.S. imperialism, and demanded, "Greece must withdraw from NATO."

	Spanish Students Protest Against Fascist Suppression
	Students in the Spanish capital Madrid held demonstrations for three consecutive days from April 11 to April 13, protesting against the suppression of students by the Franco fascist dictatorship and fighting for democratic rights.

	On April 12, several hundred students at Madrid University fought with mounted police who had stormed the school to suppress the demonstrating students. The police used batons to beat the students, attempting to disperse those who were rallying against police suppression. However, the students were fearless, shouting, "Down with suppression!" "Freedom to organize student unions!" and bravely fighting back with stones. The police arrested about 50 students.

### 简明新闻

1967-04-16
第6版()
专栏：

    简明新闻
    法国人民示威抗议美国侵越
    法国北部城市亚眠的二百名居民十四日举行示威，抗议美国侵略越南。示威群众烧掉一面美国国旗，这面旗上还画着一个作为纳粹希特勒标志的卐字。示威是在美国驻法国大使波伦到亚眠去主持一个美国的油画展览会时发生的。
    意大利人民广泛举行反美示威
    意大利首都罗马和其他城市的人民连日举行示威，抗议美国侵略越南。
    几百名意大利人十二日在美国驻罗马大使馆前举行示威，反对美国侵略越南的战争。
    警察赶来镇压示威者。示威者用石头以及随手能拣到的东西英勇地进行自卫反击。在一小时的搏斗中，有几十人受伤，三十五名示威者被捕。
    在过去几天里，意大利其他城市的人民也举行了反美示威游行。
    希腊十五万建筑工人举行罢工
    希腊十五万名建筑工人十二日举行了二十四小时的全国性罢工，要求增加工资和改善劳动条件。
    这一天，雅典的两千名罢工工人举行了集会，并且接着游行到希腊政府的劳动部。示威者高呼口号，反对希腊反动政府和美帝国主义，并且提出
  “希腊必须退出北大西洋集团”。
    西班牙学生抗议法西斯镇压
    西班牙首都马德里学生从十一日到十三日连续三天举行示威，抗议佛朗哥法西斯独裁政权镇压学生，争取民主权利。
    在十二日，马德里大学的几百名学生同冲入学校镇压示威学生的骑警搏斗。骑警用木棍殴打学生，企图驱散集会抗议警察镇压的学生，但是学生们毫不畏惧，一面高呼：“反对镇压！”“自由组织学生会！”一面奋勇用石块进行反击。警察拘捕了约五十名学生。（据新华社讯）
