Title: Zambian Government Trade and Economic Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1967-04-30
Edition: 5

The Zambian government trade and economic delegation led by the Minister of Commerce and Foreign Trade, Chimbala, left Beijing today to return home. Acting Minister of Foreign Trade, Lin Haiyun, and others went to the airport to see them off.

### 赞比亚政府贸易经济代表团回国

1967-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    赞比亚政府贸易经济代表团回国
    新华社二十九日讯　由赞比亚工商与对外贸易部部长钦巴率领的赞比亚政府贸易经济代表团，今天离开北京回国。对外贸易部代理部长林海云等前往机场欢送。
