Title: U.S. Warships and Military Aircraft Violate Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1967-04-30
Edition: 5

U.S. warships and military aircraft have violated our territorial waters and airspace. We issue the 433rd severe warning.

New China News Agency, April 29th

On April 29th, from 8:24 AM to 5:49 PM, a U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters off Dongshan Island to the east and northeast of Fujian Province and off Zhenhai to the east. On the same day, from 10:04 AM to 10:29 AM and from 11:13 AM to 11:29 AM, two U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Yongxing Island, Dong Island, and Zhao Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. In response to this military provocation by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issues the 433rd severe warning.

### 美国军舰和军用飞机侵犯我领海领空  我提出第四百三十三次严重警告

1967-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    美国军舰和军用飞机侵犯我领海领空
    我提出第四百三十三次严重警告
    新华社二十九日讯　四月二十九日八时二十四分至十七时四十九分，美国军舰一艘，先后三次入侵我福建省东山岛以东、东北和镇海以东地区领海。同日十时零四分至十时二十九分、十一时十三分至十一时二十九分，美国军用飞机两架，先后入侵我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛、赵述岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百三十三次严重警告。
