Title: Hoist the Banner of Revolutionary Criticism to Achieve Great Unity of the Revolutionary Masses
Author:
Date: 1967-04-26
Edition: 3

The bourgeois reactionary line is the main obstacle to achieving great unity among the revolutionary masses of the proletariat at present. To criticize the bourgeois reactionary line, it is necessary to direct the spearhead straight at the top capitalist-roader within the Party, and to closely combine this struggle with the tasks of struggle, criticism, and reform in one's own region, department, and unit. Through joint struggle, achieve great unity of the revolution.
