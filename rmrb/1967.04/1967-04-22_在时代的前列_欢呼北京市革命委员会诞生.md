Title: At the Forefront of the Era - Celebrating the Birth of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee
Author: Zheng Junhua
Date: 1967-04-22
Edition: 4

The red flags flutter in the east wind,
Cheers echo like thunder in the sky;
Ah, the drums of march beat loudly,
The proletarian revolutionaries stride forward with vigor and passion.

The first big-character poster of Marxism-Leninism,
Shakes the world.
It dispels the thick fog,
And ignites the revolutionary fire across the nation.

See the snowflake-like leaflets and big-character posters,
Flying over the Yellow River and the Yangtze,
To the distant Tibet, Xinjiang, and Hainan Island,
In bustling cities,
In quiet villages,
Where is there no spark of revolution!

The red horizon of the east,
Revolutionary people are all heroes.
The Red Guards open their powerful voices,
Issuing a challenge to the old world with a roar,
Sweeping away all harmful elements!

The bugle of the era sounds,
At the forefront of the cultural revolution, we charge ahead!

A steel-like army,
Forms a red arc in the sky;
The battle cry echoes through the mountains,
Like thunder shaking the heavens;
Raising high the beloved "Quotations from Chairman Mao",
Illuminating the globe with red light!

Look at the workers and peasants,
With an irresistible force,
Attacking the capitalist roaders in power!
Let all the ghosts and snakes be exposed in broad daylight,
Sweep away the revisionist clique of the old municipal committee!

We announce to the world our victory,
The Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee is born in struggle!

Countless eyes watch Beijing day and night,
Countless hearts beat with Beijing!
Revolutionary comrades around the world will cheer together:
Socialist China is thriving,
Imperialism and revisionism are on the decline!

Look at the new Beijing!
Young revolutionary cadres,
Smiles of victory on their faces;
The great call to "grasp revolution, promote production,"
Is deeply rooted in the hearts of the revolutionary masses of workers and peasants;
Revolutionary old cadres,
Have emerged and caught up,
Building new achievements for the revolution!

We all share a common belief:
Let the wheel of history
Forever revolve around the axis of Mao Zedong Thought!

See the ocean of red flags,
Dancing in the sky over Beijing,
Sing loudly, shout loudly,
Let Chairman Mao inspect our united troops!

Wave the "Quotations" in your hands,
With a voice that shakes the heavens,
Announce to the world:
We will raise the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought even higher,
We will draw the blueprint for a new Beijing!

Achieving victory in the struggle for power,
Is just the first step of the long march!
Carry on the revolutionary spirit,
Beat the drums of pursuit and victory!

A thriving people's revolutionary government,
Stands firmly in the capital!

      南岛，

      容；

      大号召，

    杀出来了，跟上去了，

      念：
    让历史的巨轮
      永远绕着毛泽东思想的
        轴心转动！
    看海洋般的红旗，
    在北京上空飞舞，
    尽情歌唱啊，尽情高呼，
