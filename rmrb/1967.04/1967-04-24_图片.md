Title: Workers at the Harbin Petroleum Fittings Factory Take Power
Author: Liu Jie  Cui Yanqin
Date: 1967-04-24
Edition: 3

After the proletarian revolutionary faction took power at the Harbin Petroleum Fittings Factory, they held high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, vigorously promoted the revolution, and vigorously pushed forward production. This is the rebels of the lathe group studying the product quality of the "top screw valve."
