Title: U.S. Ships and Aircraft Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1967-04-16
Edition: 5

U.S. ships and aircraft intrude into our territorial waters and airspace

We issue the 430th serious warning

New China News Agency, April 14th:

On April 14th, from 6:10 to 10:08 and from 17:26 to 18:07, one U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters east of Chongwu and Dongshan in Fujian Province. On the same day, from 9:18 to 9:50, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above Dong Island and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province; from 10:18 to 10:24, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above the northeastern region of Hainan Island, Guangdong Province. Regarding the military provocation by the U.S. warship and military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issues the 430th serious warning.

### 美舰美机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百三十一次严重警告

1967-04-16
第5版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百三十一次严重警告
    新华社十四日讯　四月十四日六时十分至十时零八分、十七时二十六分至十八时零七分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省崇武以东和东山以东领海。同日九时十八分至九时五十分，美国军用飞机一架侵入我广东省西沙群岛的东岛、永兴岛地区上空；同日十时十八分至十时二十四分，美国军用飞机一架侵入我广东省海南岛东北地区领海上空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百三十一次严重警告。
