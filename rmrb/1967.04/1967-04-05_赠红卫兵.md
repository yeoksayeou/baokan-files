Title: In Praise of the Red Guards
Author: Wú Démáo
Date: 1967-04-05
Edition: 5

	In Praise of the Red Guards
	By Vietnamese author Wu Demao

	1
	Red Guards, Red Guards!
	The seeds of revolution have grown.
	Raise high the red flag and charge ahead,
	Sweep away the demons and protect the revolution.

	2
	Red Guards, Red Guards!
	Destroy the old and build the new with great deeds.
	Remember Chairman Mao's words,
	Build the mountains and rivers and protect the revolution.

	3
	Red Guards, Red Guards!
	Skilled in both pen and sword.
	Learn from the People's Liberation Army,
	Filled with confidence, protect the revolution.

	4
	Red Guards, Red Guards!
	Keep Marxism-Leninism in your heart.
	Look at the world with great ambition,
	Eliminate the wolves and protect the revolution.

	5
	Red Guards, Red Guards!
	The seedlings grow thanks to the gardener.
	Glory belongs to the Communist Party,
	Chairman Mao, you are the wisest!

### 赠红卫兵
吴德茂
1967-04-05
第5版()
专栏：

    赠红卫兵
    越南　吴德茂
    一
    红卫兵，红卫兵！
    革命幼苗已长成。
    高举红旗向前闯，
    扫除妖魔保革命。
    二
    红卫兵，红卫兵！
    破旧立新建奇功。
    牢记毛主席的话，
    建设河山保革命。
    三
    红卫兵，红卫兵！
    能文能武样样精。
    学习工农解放军，
    满怀信心保革命。
    四
    红卫兵，红卫兵！
    马列主义记在心。
    放眼全球志气大，
    消灭豺狼保革命。
    五
    红卫兵，红卫兵！
    幼苗成长靠园丁。
    光荣属于共产党，
    毛主席呀最英明！
