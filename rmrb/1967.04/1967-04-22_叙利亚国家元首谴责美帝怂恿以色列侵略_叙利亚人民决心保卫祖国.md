Title: Syrian Leader Condemns U.S. Imperialism for Inciting Israeli Aggression
Author:
Date: 1967-04-22
Edition: 6

Syrian President Ataa al-Sisi on the 17th, in a speech at a military parade and mass rally held in Hama to celebrate the National Day, strongly condemned U.S. imperialism for inciting Israel to invade Syria.

He pointed out that since Syria gained independence, imperialism has repeatedly instigated Israel to invade Syria. The United States has even declared its readiness to use its Sixth Fleet to support this aggression.

He said, the failures of imperialism's aggression in South Vietnam, Africa, and Latin America, under the courageous struggles of the local peoples, have boosted the fighting spirit of the Arab people. Syria has given a firm and brave response to Israel's aggression.

He said: We know that imperialism and its lackeys will continue to conspire against Syria. But the Syrian people will spare no effort to defend every inch of our homeland. We will continue to fulfill our principled duty to liberate the land seized by Zionism. We will use people's war to confront the challenges of imperialism, Zionism, and reactionaries.

### 叙利亚国家元首谴责美帝怂恿以色列侵略  叙利亚人民决心保卫祖国

1967-04-22
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚国家元首谴责美帝怂恿以色列侵略
    叙利亚人民决心保卫祖国
    据新华社大马士革电　叙利亚国家元首阿塔西十七日在为庆祝国庆节在哈马市举行的军事检阅和群众大会上发表讲话，强烈谴责美帝国主义怂恿以色列侵略叙利亚。
    他指出，自从叙利亚独立以来，帝国主义不断地唆使以色列侵略叙利亚。美国还宣布准备以它的第六舰队来支持这一侵略。
    他说，帝国主义在南越、非洲和拉丁美洲的侵略在当地人民的英勇斗争下遭到的失败，加强了阿拉伯人民的斗争信心。叙利亚已经给以色列的侵略以坚定和勇敢的回答。
    他说：我们知道，帝国主义及其走狗要继续对叙利亚进行阴谋活动。但叙利亚人民不惜任何代价，要保卫我们祖国的每一寸领土。我们要继续履行从犹太复国主义手中解放被掠夺的土地的原则性的责任。我们要用人民战争来对付帝国主义、犹太复国主义和反动派的挑战。
