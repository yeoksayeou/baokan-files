Title: In Praise of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author: LÍ Chéngyǒng
Date: 1967-04-05
Edition: 5

	In praise of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution,
	It is sweeping the nation with thunderous force.
	The West fills its pages with twisted words,
	The revisionists raise a chorus of curses.
	Reading them makes my heart burst with rage,
	I cry out to heaven in indignation.
	Fortunately, a friend comes from Beijing,
	He tells me the true story of China.
	My friend is full of joy and excitement,
	He pours out his heartfelt feelings.
	"In this great revolution of our friend,
	The force is like a million thunderclaps.
	The commander is Chairman Mao,
	The vanguard is the Red Guards.
	With broad strokes, they drive out the snakes and pigs,
	With mighty waves, they chase away the crocodiles and whales.
	They uproot the poisonous trees of revisionism with all their roots,
	They ensure that the socialist mountains will be red forever.
	My friend dances with joy,
	The enemies tremble with fear.
	Great is the Chinese revolution,
	The vanguard of the world revolution."
	Revolution is originally about violence,
	How can there be "coexistence" and "peace"?
	What use is a grasshopper's arm against a chariot?
	The wheel of history rolls forward without stopping.
	Human history will write a glorious page,
	This is the typical example of the world cultural revolution of Marxism-Leninism.
	This is the magnificent and magnificent momentum of the Chinese nation,
	This is the brilliant and shining glory of Mao Zedong Thought!

### 赞中国文化大革命
黎诚勇
1967-04-05
第5版()
专栏：

    赞中国文化大革命
    越南　黎诚勇
    中国文化大革命，
    正在雷厉风行。
    西方满纸歪曲之语，
    现修一片咒骂之声。
    读后不胜肺欲炸，
    向天独作不平鸣。
    欣逢朋友来自北京，
    请为我介绍中国真情。
    朋友兴高采烈，
    吐尽衷心所倾。
    “友邦这次大革命，
    其势如万钧雷霆。
    统帅是毛主席，
    闯将是红卫兵。
    大刀阔斧逐蛇豕，
    巨浪长风追鳄鲸。
    挖掉修正主义毒树千条根，
    保证社会主义江山万代红。
    朋友手舞足蹈，
    敌人胆战心惊。
    伟哉中国大革命，
    世界革命的先锋。”
    革命原来是暴力，
    哪能“共处”与“和平”。
    螳臂当车有何用？
    车轮向前永不停。
    人类历史将写下辉煌的一页，
    这是世界文化革命的马列主义
      典型。
    这是中华民族的气势壮阔磅礴，
    这是毛泽东思想的光辉灿烂光
      明！
