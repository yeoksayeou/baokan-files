Title: Chairman Shukeiri Reiterates the Need to Liberate Palestine Through People's War
Author:
Date: 1967-04-17
Edition: 6

Chairman Shukeiri reiterates the need to liberate Palestine through people's war
Condemns self-proclaimed socialist countries for supporting Israel

	CAIRO, April 15 (Xinhua) -- Chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO) Shukeiri said at the opening ceremony of the second session of the Palestine Trade Union Federation held here on April 15 that Israel is an instrument of aggression established and supported by U.S. imperialism, therefore, "U.S. imperialism is the enemy of our national liberation."

	Shukeiri said: It is Israel and imperialism that have driven hundreds of thousands of Palestinian people out of their homes, turning them into refugees living in Gaza and other places. However, some countries claim to be socialist countries, but at the same time, they support, recognize, and collude with Israel. These are not genuine socialist countries. He said: "Whoever stands on the side of Israel is not a genuine socialist."

	Shukeiri highly praised China's support for the liberation cause of the Palestinian people and expressed gratitude to the Communist Party of China, the Chinese government, the Chinese people, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army. He said: "The People's Republic of China has not only issued many statements supporting the struggle of the Palestine Liberation Organization and the Palestinian people but has also provided us with the materials we need in our struggle against the enemy."

	Shukeiri pointed out that Palestinian workers are the vanguard of the Palestinian people in the struggle to return to their homeland. He said: "We will burn imperialism and reactionaries in the fire of revolutionary war. We will return to our homeland through the people's war."

	Shukeiri also condemned the U.S. aggression in Vietnam and expressed firm support for the Vietnamese people's just struggle against U.S. aggression for national salvation.

### 舒凯里主席重申要通过人民战争解放巴勒斯坦  谴责自称社会主义的国家支持以色列

1967-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    舒凯里主席重申要通过人民战争解放巴勒斯坦
    谴责自称社会主义的国家支持以色列
    新华社开罗十五日电　巴勒斯坦解放组织主席舒凯里十五日在这里举行的巴勒斯坦工会联合会第二次代表大会开幕会议上讲话时指出，以色列是美帝国主义建立和支持的一个侵略工具，因此，“美帝国主义是我们民族解放的敌人”。
    舒凯里说：正是以色列和帝国主义把成千上万的巴勒斯坦人民赶出自己的家园，变成难民，住在加沙和其他地方。然而，一些国家自称为社会主义国家，但同时又支持、承认和勾结以色列，这些不是真正的社会主义国家。他说：“不管是谁，只要他站在以色列一边，他就不是真正的社会主义者。”
    舒凯里高度赞扬中国支持巴勒斯坦人民的解放事业，并对中国共产党、中国政府、中国人民和中国人民解放军表示感谢。他说：“中华人民共和国不仅发表了许多支持巴勒斯坦解放组织和巴勒斯坦人民斗争的声明，而且向我们提供了我们在同敌人斗争中需要的物资。”
    舒凯里指出，巴勒斯坦工人是巴勒斯坦人民在争取重返家园的斗争中的先锋。他说：“我们将用革命战争的烈火烧死帝国主义和反动派。我们将通过人民战争的方式重返我们的家园。”
    舒凯里还谴责美国侵略越南，并表示坚决支持越南人民抗美救国的正义斗争。
