Title: A Soviet Spacecraft Crashes, Astronaut Dies
Author:
Date: 1967-04-27
Edition: 6

A Soviet spacecraft crashed, resulting in the death of the astronaut on board.

	According to reports from Western news agencies, American officials have "once again urged realistic cooperation between the Soviet Union and the United States in space programs" regarding the crash of a Soviet spacecraft.

	The Soviet spacecraft, named "Soyuz 1," was launched on the 23rd and crashed during its descent on the 24th. The astronaut aboard, Komarov, died in the crash. A report from TASS on the 23rd emphasized that this space mission was "conducted in the year marking the 50th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution," and was a "gift to this glorious day." The report described the spacecraft as a "great creative achievement."

	U.S. President Johnson sent a telegram to Soviet leader Podgorny on the 24th, stating that the crash was a "tragedy for all nations." NASA Administrator Webb also issued a statement on the 24th, noting that "the leaders of the United States and the Soviet Union have made many statements about the need for cooperation between the two countries," and that "President Johnson is ready to turn his strong desire for more effective cooperation into action."

### 苏联一艘宇宙飞船失事航行员死亡

1967-04-27
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联一艘宇宙飞船失事航行员死亡
    新华社二十六日讯　据西方通讯社报道，美国官员就苏联一艘宇宙飞船失事一事“再次敦促苏美之间在空间计划方面进行现实的合作。”
    苏联这艘宇宙飞船定名为“联盟一号”，是在二十三日发射的，二十四日在降落时失事，飞船上的航行员科马罗夫死亡。塔斯社二十三日的报道曾强调指出，这次宇宙航行是“在伟大十月社会主义革命五十周年的一年里进行的”，是“给这个光辉的日子的献礼”，“这艘飞船是巨大的创造性成就”。
    美帝头目约翰逊二十四日打电报给苏修头目波德戈尔内说什么这次失事“对一切国家都是一个悲剧”。美国国家航空和宇宙航行局局长韦伯二十四日发表声明指出，“美苏两国首脑已就两国都应寻求合作一事发表了许多声明”，“约翰逊总统准备把进行更有效合作的强烈愿望付诸行动。”
