Title: Celebrating the 22nd Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1967-04-04
Edition: 5

The China-Hungary Friendship Association held a film screening event this evening to celebrate the 22nd anniversary of Hungary's liberation. Representatives from relevant departments and revolutionary rebels from the capital attended the event. Hungarian Ambassador to China, Yurufu Halasi, was also invited to attend.

The film "Tunnel Warfare" was screened during the event.

### 庆祝匈牙利解放二十二周年  中匈友协举行电影招待会

1967-04-04
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利解放二十二周年
    中匈友协举行电影招待会
    新华社三日讯　中匈友好协会今晚举行电影招待会，庆祝匈牙利解放二十二周年。有关方面负责人和首都无产阶级革命派代表出席了电影招待会。匈牙利驻中国大使郝拉思·尤若夫应邀出席了电影招待会。
    招待会上放映了中国故事片《地道战》。
