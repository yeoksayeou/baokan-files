Title: China and Vietnam Sign Cultural Cooperation Execution Plan
Author:
Date: 1967-04-27
Edition: 5

The cultural cooperation agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for the year 1967 was signed in Beijing today.

The signatories were Zhou Yiping, Deputy Director of the China Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and Phan Hong, Deputy Director of the Vietnam Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee and head of the Vietnam Cultural Delegation, on behalf of the Vietnamese side.

Deputy Director of the China Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee Chu Tu-nan and Vietnamese Ambassador to China Vu Minh Loan attended the signing ceremony. After the signing, the China Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee held a banquet to welcome the Vietnam Cultural Delegation.

The Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Vu Minh Loan, held a banquet today to celebrate the signing of the 1967 execution plan for the Vietnam-China cultural cooperation agreement. Ding Xilin, Zhou Yiping, Zhang Ben, and other relevant officials were invited to attend the banquet. Also present was Nguyen Minh Phuong, acting head of the delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam resident in China.

### 中越签订文化合作执行计划  越南驻华大使为计划签订举行宴会

1967-04-27
第5版()
专栏：

    中越签订文化合作执行计划
    越南驻华大使为计划签订举行宴会
    新华社二十五日讯  中华人民共和国政府和越南民主共和国政府文化合作协定一九六七年执行计划，今天在北京签字。
    在执行计划上签字的，中国方面是对外文化联络委员会副主任周一萍，越南方面是越南对外文化联络委员会副主任、越南文化代表团团长范鸿。
    对外文委副主任楚图南，越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾参加了签字仪式。签字后，对外文委举行宴会招待越南文化代表团。
    新华社二十六日讯  越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾今天举行宴会，庆祝越中文化合作协定一九六七年执行计划的签订。丁西林、周一萍、张本等有关方面负责人应邀出席了宴会。出席宴会的还有越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳。
