Title: Establishment of the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture in Xinjiang Province; People of All Ethnic Groups and All Walks of Life Hold Celebration Rally in Yining City
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-06
Page: 3

The Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture of Xinjiang Province has been formally established. The first People's Representative Conference of the Autonomous Prefecture was held in Yining City from November 22nd to 28th. A total of 239 representatives from twelve ethnic groups, including Kazakh, Han, Uyghur, Mongolian, Xibe, Hui, Uzbek, Tatar, Kyrgyz, and Russian, attended the conference. The conference exercised the functions and powers of the People's Congress, elected Patihan (Kazakh) as the Governor of the Autonomous Prefecture, Li Huiyou (Han), Abdureyim Aigou (Uyghur), and Yusup Khan (Kazakh) as Deputy Governors, and Li Quan (Han), Abdurahman Tuhulov (Kazakh), and thirty-five others as members of the People's Committee of the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture of Xinjiang Province, formally establishing the People's Committee of the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture of Xinjiang Province.

The conference also elected Xia Yi Mairdan (Kazakh) as the President of the People's Court of the Autonomous Prefecture.

At the meeting, the representatives discussed and adopted various work reports.

During the conference, Saifudin, Fourth Secretary of the Xinjiang Sub-bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Bao Erhan, Chairman of the People's Government of Xinjiang Province, and Cao Danov, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Xinjiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, all came to offer their congratulations. Saifudin and Bao Erhan also gave instructions on the future work of the Autonomous Prefecture.

The conference received congratulatory banners from the National People's Congress Ethnic Affairs Committee, the National Ethnic Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, and other superior leading organs congratulating the establishment of the Autonomous Prefecture. Autonomous regions at the prefectural level in Xinjiang Province, the Aksai Kazakh Autonomous County in Gansu Province, and the Haixi Mongol, Tibetan, and Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture in Qinghai Province all sent representatives to the meeting to offer their congratulations. (Xinhua News Agency)

Xinhua News Agency:
More than 3,000 representatives from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture of Xinjiang Province gathered in Yining City on November 29th to celebrate the establishment of the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture. At the celebration rally, Bao Erhan, Chairman of the People's Government of Xinjiang Province, congratulated the establishment of the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture on behalf of the People's Government of Xinjiang Province and the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang. He said: The establishment of this autonomous prefecture marks the realization of the right of the Kazakh people to "be masters of their own affairs" under the leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao. Responsible persons from various people's organizations in Xinjiang Province and representatives from the Bayingolin Mongol Autonomous Prefecture and the Kizilsu Kirghiz Autonomous Prefecture of Xinjiang Province also delivered congratulatory speeches at the meeting. Finally, Patihan, Governor of the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture of Xinjiang Province, delivered a speech at the meeting to express his gratitude. He said: The establishment of the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture is the result of the joint efforts under the leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao and with the help of the Han people. It is also a new beginning for the greater prosperity and happiness of the Autonomous Prefecture in the future. On behalf of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture, he assured the people of all ethnic groups that they would conscientiously abide by the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, be absolutely loyal to the people's democratic system, further unite the people of all ethnic groups, gradually develop the political, economic, and cultural aspects of the Autonomous Prefecture, and jointly build our great motherland with the people of the whole country.

At the celebration rally, Young Pioneers of all ethnic groups in Yining City presented flowers to Patihan, Governor of the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture, and members of the People's Committee.

Finally, the conference unanimously passed a telegram of salute to Chairman Mao and the Chinese People's Liberation Army. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 新疆省伊犁哈萨克族自治州成立  各族各界人民在伊宁市举行庆祝大会

1954-12-06
第3版()
专栏：

    新疆省伊犁哈萨克族自治州成立
    各族各界人民在伊宁市举行庆祝大会
    新疆省伊犁哈萨克族自治州正式成立。自治州首届人民代表会议于十一月二十二日到二十八日在伊宁市举行。出席会议的有哈萨克、汉、维吾尔、蒙古、锡伯、回、乌兹别克、塔塔尔、柯尔克兹、俄罗斯等十二个民族的代表共二百三十九名。会议代行人民代表大会职权，选出帕提汉（哈萨克族）为自治州州长，李惠友（汉族）、阿不都热依木艾沟（维吾尔族）、玉素甫汉（哈萨克族）为副州长，李铨（汉族）、阿不都热合满土胡洛夫（哈萨克族）等三十五人为自治州人民委员会委员，正式成立了新疆省伊犁哈萨克族自治州人民委员会。
    会议还选出夏益买尔旦（哈萨克族）为自治州人民法院院长。
    会上，代表们讨论和通过了各项工作报告。
    会议期间，中共中央新疆分局第四书记赛福鼎、新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉和中国人民解放军新疆军区政治部副主任曹达诺夫等人都来祝贺。赛福鼎和鲍尔汉并对自治州今后的工作作了指示。
    会议收到全国人民代表大会民族委员会、中华人民共和国民族事务委员会等上级领导机关祝贺自治州成立的贺幛。新疆省各专区级的自治区、甘肃省阿克塞哈萨克族自治区、青海省海西蒙、藏、哈萨克族自治区等都派代表到会祝贺。　（新华社）
    新疆省伊犁哈萨克族自治州各族各界代表三千多人，十一月二十九日在伊宁市集会，庆祝伊犁哈萨克族自治州的成立。庆祝大会上，新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉代表新疆省人民政府和新疆各族人民祝贺伊犁哈萨克族自治州的成立。他说：这个自治州的成立，标志着哈萨克族人民在党和毛主席的领导下，实现了“当家作主”的权利。在会上致词祝贺的还有新疆省各人民团体的负责人和新疆省巴音郭楞蒙古族自治区代表、克兹勒苏柯尔克兹族自治区代表。最后新疆省伊犁哈萨克族自治州州长帕提汉在会上致词答谢，他说：伊犁哈萨克族自治州的成立，是在党和毛主席的领导下，在汉族人民的帮助下共同努力的成果。也是今后自治州更加繁荣幸福的新开端。他代表自治州人民委员会向各族人民保证：今后切实遵守中华人民共和国宪法，绝对效忠于人民民主制度，进一步团结各族人民，逐步发展自治州的政治、经济和文化，和全国人民共同建设我们伟大的祖国。
    庆祝会上，伊宁市各族少年先锋队员向伊犁哈萨克族自治州州长帕提汉和人民委员会委员献花。　
    最后，大会一致通过了向毛主席、中国人民解放军的致敬电。　（新华社）
