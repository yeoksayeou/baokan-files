Title: Arab League Countries Decide to Participate in Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-12-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 10th: News from Cairo: The Political Committee of the Arab League decided at its meeting on the 8th that Arab countries will participate in the Asian-African Conference scheduled to be held in Indonesia early next year.
    At its meeting on the 7th, the Political Committee also decided to bring the issue of the French colonial authorities' suppression of the Algerian people before the United Nations.
    The countries participating in the Arab League are Egypt, Syria, Lebanon, Iraq, Jordan, Saudi Arabia, Yemen, and Libya.

### 阿拉伯联盟国家决定参加亚非国家会议

1954-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟国家决定参加亚非国家会议
    【新华社十日讯】开罗消息：阿拉伯联盟政治委员会在八日举行的会议上决定，阿拉伯国家将参加预定在明年初在印度尼西亚举行的亚洲—非洲国家会议。
    政治委员会在七日举行的会议上，还决定把法国殖民当局镇压阿尔及利亚人民问题向联合国提出。
    参加阿拉伯联盟的国家有埃及、叙利亚、黎巴嫩、伊拉克、约旦、沙特阿拉伯、也门和利比亚八国。
