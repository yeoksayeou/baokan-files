Title: Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Liberation of Albania: Grand Military Parade and Festive Procession Held in Tirana
Author:
Date: 1954-12-01
Page: 3

According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, Tirana: To celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Albania, a grand military parade and festive procession were held in Tirana on November 29th.

Lining both sides of Stalin Avenue were workers from Tirana's factories, farmers from the outskirts of Tirana, cultural, scientific, and artistic workers, and people's representatives from various states who came to participate in the celebration.

Present on the reviewing stand were Albanian Labor Party and government leaders such as Hoxha and Shehu. Government delegations from the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and various people's democratic countries, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Albania, were also present on the stand.

At 9:00 AM, the military parade began. Albanian Minister of Defense, Lieutenant General Baluku, reviewed the troops standing on Stalin Avenue in an open-top car.

After the review, Baluku stepped onto the reviewing stand and delivered a speech. On behalf of the Labor Party and the government, he congratulated the troops and the people of the whole country on the tenth anniversary of liberation.

As Baluku concluded his speech, salutes were fired and the Albanian national anthem was played.

The marching parade began. Parade commander Major General Hasko rode in an open-top car at the head of the column. Following him were students from the Hoxha Military Academy. Then came infantry, border guard, and naval units. Subsequently, artillery units passed the reviewing stand in neat formation, and aircraft flew over the reviewing stand. At the rear were tank units. The audience standing on both sides of the street applauded enthusiastically for the troops being reviewed from time to time and shouted: "Long live our heroic army!"

After the parade, the festive procession of the people of Tirana began. Marchers carrying flags and banners walked past the reviewing stand, shouting "Long live the Albanian Labor Party and the government!" and "Long live world peace!" (Xinhua News Agency)

### 庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年  地拉那举行盛大的阅兵式和节日游行

1954-12-01
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年
    地拉那举行盛大的阅兵式和节日游行
    据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那讯：为了庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年，十一月二十九日在地拉那举行了盛大的阅兵式和节日游行。
    在斯大林大街两旁观礼的有地拉那各工厂的工人，地拉那郊区的农民，文化、科学和艺术工作者，从各州前来参加庆祝典礼的人民代表。
    在检阅台上的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和政府的领导人霍查和谢胡等。苏联、中华人民共和国、各人民民主国家的政府代表团以及各国驻阿尔巴尼亚的外交使节也在台上观礼。
    九时正，阅兵式开始。阿尔巴尼亚国防部长巴卢库中将乘着敞篷汽车检阅了站立在斯大林大街上的部队。
    检阅毕，巴卢库走上检阅台，发表了演说。他代表劳动党和政府向部队和全国人民祝贺解放十周年。
    巴卢库演说结束时，鸣起了礼炮并且奏起了阿尔巴尼亚国歌。
    阅兵分列式开始。检阅指挥官哈斯科少将乘敞篷汽车走在队伍的最前面。走在他后面的是霍查军官学校的学生。随后是步兵、边防军和海军部队。接着，炮兵部队以整齐的阵容通过检阅台，飞机从检阅台上空掠过。最后面的是坦克部队。伫立在大街两旁的观众不时向接受检阅的部队热烈鼓掌，并且高呼：“我们英勇的军队万岁！”
    检阅结束，地拉那人民的节日游行就开始了。游行者举着旗帜和标语牌走过检阅台前，高呼阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和政府万岁，高呼国际和平万岁！（新华社）
