Title: Japanese House of Representatives Passes Resolution to Invite Chinese Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-12-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 18th: Tokyo dispatch: The Japanese House of Representatives held a plenary session on the 17th and passed a resolution jointly proposed by various parties "Concerning the Invitation of a Chinese Trade Delegation."

    The resolution stated: The government should take appropriate measures to invite a Chinese trade delegation to Japan in order to promote Sino-Japanese trade.

    Xinhua News Agency, December 17th: Tokyo dispatch: The Special Committee for Trade Promotion of the Japanese House of Representatives, established on the 14th, passed a resolution requesting the government to take appropriate measures to realize the invitation of a Chinese trade delegation to Japan in order to promote Sino-Japanese trade. The committee also passed a resolution stating: In order to promote world peace and normal trade in the current economic recession, the committee requests the government to take appropriate measures to greatly relax export restrictions on the Soviet Union, China, and the People's Democratic countries.

### 日众议院通过邀请我国贸易代表团的决议

1954-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    日众议院通过邀请我国贸易代表团的决议
    【新华社十八日讯】东京消息：日本众议院十七日举行全体会议，通过了各党派联合提出的“关于邀请中国贸易代表团的决议”。
    决议说：政府应当采取适当措施，邀请中国贸易代表团到日本，以便促进日中贸易。
    【新华社十七日讯】东京消息：十四日成立的日本众议院贸易振兴特别委员会，通过决议，要求政府采取适当措施，使邀请中国贸易代表团到日本一事得以实现，以便促进日中贸易。委员会还通过一项决议说：为了在目前经济萧条的情况下促进世界和平和正常的贸易，委员会要求政府采取适当措施，大大放宽对苏联、中国和人民民主国家的出口限制。
