Title: CPPCC National Committee Meeting Opens, Chairman Mao Zedong Presides Over Opening Ceremony, Vice Chairman Chen Shutong Delivers Report on CPPCC Work, Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai Delivers Political Report
Author:
Date: 1954-12-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 21st: The first session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) opened in Beijing at 3:00 PM on the 21st. Four hundred and ninety-three members of the National Committee attended the meeting.
    The arrangement of the venue was solemn and simple. Portraits of Mr. Sun Yat-sen and Chairman Mao Zedong hung on the rostrum, with the emblem of the CPPCC in the middle.
    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the First National Committee of the CPPCC, presided over the opening ceremony of the meeting.
    When Chairman Mao Zedong and Vice Chairmen of the First National Committee of the CPPCC, Zhou Enlai, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, and Chen Shutong, ascended the rostrum, warm applause resounded throughout the hall.
    The meeting adopted the agenda of the session and elected the presidium and secretary-general of the session.
    Xinhua News Agency, December 21st: The first meeting of the first session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC was held at 3:40 PM on the 21st. At the meeting, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the First National Committee, delivered a work report of the First National Committee of the CPPCC; Zhang Bojun, a member of the Standing Committee of the First National Committee, gave an explanation on the draft of the CPPCC Charter; and Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the First National Committee, delivered a political report.
    The meeting will continue tomorrow.

### 政协全国委员会会议开幕  毛泽东主席主持开幕式陈叔通副主席作政协工作报告周恩来副主席作政治报告

1954-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会会议开幕
    毛泽东主席主持开幕式陈叔通副主席作政协工作报告周恩来副主席作政治报告
    【新华社二十一日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议，二十一日下午三时在北京开幕。出席会议的有全国委员会委员四百九十三人。
    会场的布置庄严而朴素。主席台上悬挂着孙中山先生和毛泽东主席的画像，中间挂着中国人民政治协商会议的会徽。
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会主席毛泽东主持会议的开幕式。
    当毛泽东主席和中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会副主席周恩来、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、陈叔通等走上主席台时，全场响起了热烈的掌声。
    会议通过了本次会议的议事日程，并选出了本次会议的主席团和秘书长。
    【新华社二十一日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议第一日会议在二十一日下午三时四十分举行。会上，第一届全国委员会副主席陈叔通作了中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会工作报告；第一届全国委员会常务委员会委员章伯钧作了关于中国人民政治协商会议章程（草案）的说明；第一届全国委员会副主席周恩来作了政治报告。
    会议将在明天继续举行。
