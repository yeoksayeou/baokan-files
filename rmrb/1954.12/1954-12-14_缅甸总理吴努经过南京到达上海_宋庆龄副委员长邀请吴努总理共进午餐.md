Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Arrives in Shanghai via Nanjing. Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Invites Prime Minister U Nu to Lunch

Author:
Date: 1954-12-14
Page: 1

[This article transmitted by:]
【This Newspaper Shanghai 13th Dispatch】Burmese Union Prime Minister U Nu and his wife and entourage arrived in Shanghai on the 13th, via Nanjing, on their way back to their country from Beijing.

In Nanjing, Prime Minister U Nu paid his respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and laid a wreath. Prime Minister U Nu also visited the scenic Linggu Temple, Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum and Xuanwu Lake in Nanjing. In the evening, Prime Minister U Nu and others watched the Xi opera "Double Grindstone" and "Women Representatives."

In Shanghai, Prime Minister U Nu visited the Yangshupu Power Plant, the Kongjiang New Village workers' residential area, the Shanghai Workers' Cultural Palace, the state-owned Shanghai No. 1 Printing and Dyeing Factory, the China Welfare Institute Kindergarten and the Children's Palace.

At noon on the 13th, Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, invited Prime Minister U Nu and his wife to lunch. In the evening, Prime Minister U Nu and others attended a grand banquet held by Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai. After the banquet, they watched the Yue opera "Chun Xiang Zhuan."

Accompanying Prime Minister U Nu were Wang Bingnan, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs and Director of the General Office, Yao Zhongming, Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and U La Maung, Burmese Ambassador to China.

### 缅甸总理吴努经过南京到达上海  宋庆龄副委员长邀请吴努总理共进午餐

1954-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴努经过南京到达上海
    宋庆龄副委员长邀请吴努总理共进午餐
    【本报上海十三日电】缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人及随行人员，在由北京返国途中，经南京于十三日到达上海。
    在南京，吴努总理曾晋谒了中山陵，并献花圈致敬。吴努总理还游览了南京名胜灵谷寺、明孝陵和玄武湖。晚间，吴努总理等观看了锡剧“双推磨”和“妇女代表”。
    在上海，吴努总理参观了杨树浦发电厂、工人住宅区控江新村、上海工人文化宫、国营上海第一印染厂、中国福利会幼儿园和少年宫。
    十三日中午，我国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄邀请吴努总理和他的夫人共进午餐。晚间，吴努总理等出席了上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年举行的盛大宴会。宴会后并观看了越剧“春香传”。
    陪同吴努总理前来的，有我国外交部部长助理兼办公厅主任王炳南、我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂。
