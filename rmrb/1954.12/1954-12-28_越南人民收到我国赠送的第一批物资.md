Title: The First Batch of Materials Donated by Our Country Received by the Vietnamese People
Author:
Date: 1954-12-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 27th: The first batch of 10,000 tons of rice and 5 million meters of cotton cloth donated by the Chinese People's Relief Association to the Vietnamese people arrived in Vietnam on the afternoon of the 26th, and was warmly welcomed by the Vietnamese people. This batch of materials includes more than 3,000 tons of rice and 3,800 packages of cotton cloth.

### 越南人民收到我国赠送的第一批物资

1954-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民收到我国赠送的第一批物资
    【新华社河内二十七日电】中国人民救济总会赠给越南人民的一万吨大米和五百万公尺棉布的第一批已在二十六日下午运到越南，受到越南人民的热烈欢迎。这批物资包括三千多吨大米和三千八百多包棉布。
