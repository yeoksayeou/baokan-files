Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-12-10
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Members of the Zhongxing Agricultural Production Cooperative in the suburbs of Yanji City, Yanbian Korean Autonomous Region, Jilin Province, enthusiastically hand over public grain and sell surplus grain to the state. This is a group of female members carefully selecting the best grain in preparation for handing over public grain.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    吉林省延边朝鲜族自治区延吉市郊中兴农业生产合作社的社员们踊跃缴纳公粮和把余粮卖给国家。这是女社员们正在精选最好的粮食，准备缴纳公粮。
