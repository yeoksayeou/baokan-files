Title: Premier Zhou Sends Condolences on the Death of Swiss Federal Vice President Etter
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1954-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 11th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram of condolence on the death of Josef Etter, Vice President of the Swiss Federal Council. The text of the message is as follows:

To Mr. Max Petitpierre, Head of the Political Department of the Swiss Confederation:

I was saddened to learn of the unfortunate death of Mr. Josef Etter, Member and Vice President of the Swiss Federal Council, on December 9th. I send my sincere condolences.

Zhou Enlai
Beijing, December 11, 1954

### 周总理电唁瑞士联邦副主席艾斯旭逝世
周恩来
1954-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理电唁瑞士联邦副主席艾斯旭逝世
    【新华社十一日讯】中华人民共和国国务院周恩来总理电唁瑞士联邦委员会副主席约瑟夫·艾斯旭逝世。唁文如下：瑞士联邦政治部长马克斯·彼蒂彼爱先生：获悉瑞士联邦委员会委员兼副主席约瑟夫·艾斯旭先生于十二月九日不幸逝世，特电致唁。
    周恩来　　　　一九五四年十二月十一日于北京
