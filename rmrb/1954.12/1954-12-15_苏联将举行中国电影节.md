Title: Soviet Union to Hold Chinese Film Festival
Author:
Date: 1954-12-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 10th: The Soviet Ministry of Culture will hold a film festival of the People's Republic of China in Moscow, Leningrad, and the capitals of various Union Republics from December 20th to 26th.

	During the film festival, the Soviet Union will screen four newly premiered Chinese feature films: "Sever the Devil's Claws," "Taking Huashan Mountain by Strategy," "Dragon Beard Ditch," and "Chicken Feather Letter." At the same time, previously screened Chinese feature films, as well as newsreels, will be widely shown.

### 苏联将举行中国电影节

1954-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联将举行中国电影节
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十日讯：从十二月二十日到二十六日，苏联文化部将在莫斯科、列宁格勒和各加盟共和国的首都举行中华人民共和国电影节。
    在电影节期间，苏联将放映四部初次上演的中国艺术影片：“斩断魔爪”、“智取华山”、
  “龙须沟”和“鸡毛信”。同时，还将广泛地放映过去曾上演过的中国艺术影片，以及新闻纪录片。
