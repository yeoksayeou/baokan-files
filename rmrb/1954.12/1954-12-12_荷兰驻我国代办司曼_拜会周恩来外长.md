Title: Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. Smit, calls on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-12-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 11th: Mr. Smit, Chargé d'Affaires of the Kingdom of the Netherlands to the People's Republic of China, called on Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, on the afternoon of the 11th to present his Letter of Credence. Present were Huang Hua, Director of the Department of Western European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 荷兰驻我国代办司曼  拜会周恩来外长

1954-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    荷兰驻我国代办司曼
    拜会周恩来外长
    【新华社十一日讯】荷兰王国驻中华人民共和国代办司曼，在十一日下午拜会中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来呈交委任书。在座的有：外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华和交际处处长王倬如。
