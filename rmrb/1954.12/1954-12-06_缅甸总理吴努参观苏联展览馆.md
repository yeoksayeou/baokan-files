Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Visits Soviet Exhibition

Author:
Date: 1954-12-06
Page: 1

Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Visits Soviet Exhibition
Xinhua News Agency, 5th December. Burmese Federal Prime Minister U Nu, his wife, and his entourage visited the "Exhibition of Achievements in Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction" on the morning of the 5th.

Prime Minister U Nu and others, accompanied by the Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, and others, visited the exhibition's Industrial Hall, Cultural Hall, Agricultural Hall, open-air theater, and the industrial and agricultural machinery, trains, and various automobiles displayed in the square. Afterwards, they had lunch together at the Moscow Restaurant.

After the visit, Prime Minister U Nu left a message in the guest book. He wrote:

"Visiting this Soviet Exhibition Hall is a very pleasant and exciting experience.

From this exhibition hall, we can draw many valuable experiences that are beneficial to Burma.

This exhibition hall is a clear example of the friendship between the Soviet Union and China."

Prior to this, Prime Minister U Nu and others also visited the jade Buddha statue in Tuancheng, Beihai Park.

Accompanying the visit were the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, and his wife, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

### 缅甸总理吴努参观苏联展览馆

1954-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴努参观苏联展览馆
    【新华社五日讯】缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人及随行人员五日上午参观了“苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会”。
    吴努总理等由苏联驻我国大使尤金等陪同参观了展览会的工业馆、文化馆、农业馆、露天剧场以及陈列在广场上的工业、农业机器，火车和各种汽车等。然后，他们到莫斯科餐厅共进午餐。
    参观毕，吴努总理在感想簿上留言。他写道，
    “参观这个苏联展览馆，内心里感到非常愉快的，同时也非常兴奋。
    从这个展览馆，可以吸取不少对缅甸有价值的宝贵经验。
    这个展览馆是一个显示着苏联和中国的友谊的显明例证。”
    在此以前，吴努总理等还到北海公园的团城参拜了玉佛像。
    陪同参观的有我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和夫人、外交部交际处处长王倬如等。
