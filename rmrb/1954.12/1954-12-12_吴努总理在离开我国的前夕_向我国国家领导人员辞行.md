Title: Prime Minister U Nu Bids Farewell to Chinese National Leaders Before Leaving Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 11th: The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, who was invited by the Government of the People's Republic of China to visit our country, bid farewell to Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De, Vice Chairman, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, and Vice Premiers Chen Yun and Chen Yi on the afternoon of the 11th, on the eve of ending his visit to Beijing and leaving our country.

Accompanying Prime Minister U Nu on his farewell visit was U Myint Thein, Judge of the Supreme Court of the Union of Burma.

Present at the farewell were U Khin Maung, Burmese Ambassador to China, and Yao Zhongming, Chinese Ambassador to Burma.

### 吴努总理在离开我国的前夕  向我国国家领导人员辞行

1954-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努总理在离开我国的前夕
    向我国国家领导人员辞行
    【新华社十一日讯】应中华人民共和国政府邀请来我国访问的缅甸联邦总理吴努，在即将结束北京的访问、离开我国的前夕——十一日下午向中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈云、陈毅辞行。
    随同吴努总理前往辞行的有：缅甸联邦最高法院法官吴敏登。
    辞行时在座的有：缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂和我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明。
