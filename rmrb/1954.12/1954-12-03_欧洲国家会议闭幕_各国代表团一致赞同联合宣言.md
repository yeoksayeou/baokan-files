Title: European Nations Conference Concludes; Delegations Unanimously Approve Joint Declaration
Author:
Date: 1954-12-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 2nd: TASS Moscow, December 1st dispatch: The Moscow European Nations Conference discussing safeguarding European peace and security on December 1st, 1954, issued a conference communique.

    On December 1st, the European conference discussing safeguarding European peace and security held its third session. The head of the delegation of the German Democratic Republic, Premier of the German Democratic Republic Otto Grotewohl, presided over the meeting.

    The conference heard speeches by the head of the Albanian delegation, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, Manush Muftiu; the head of the Romanian delegation, First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Romania, Chivu Stoica; the representative of the People's Republic of China, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian; the head of the Hungarian delegation, First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Hungary, András Hegedüs; and the head of the Bulgarian delegation, First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Anton Yugov.

    The next meeting is scheduled for 10:00 AM on December 2nd. At this meeting, a draft joint declaration from the governments of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of Poland, the Czechoslovak Republic, the German Democratic Republic, the People's Republic of Hungary, the People's Republic of Romania, the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and the People's Republic of Albania will be studied.

    Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd: According to TASS Moscow, December 2nd dispatch: The Moscow European Nations Conference discussing safeguarding European peace and security on December 2nd, 1954, issued a conference communique.

    On December 2nd, the European Nations Conference discussing safeguarding European peace and security held its fourth session – the closing session. The head of the Czechoslovak delegation, Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic, Viliam Široký, presided over the meeting.

    The conference discussed the draft joint declaration from the governments of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of Poland, the Czechoslovak Republic, the German Democratic Republic, the People's Republic of Hungary, the People's Republic of Romania, the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and the People's Republic of Albania.

    After discussion, all members of the delegations attending the conference declared: they approved the full text of the joint declaration. It was also decided that the signing ceremony of the joint declaration would be held at the Kremlin at 6:00 PM Moscow time on December 2nd.

    At the end of the meeting, the representative of the People's Republic of China, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian, delivered a speech.

    At the closing of the meeting, the head of the Czechoslovak Republic delegation, Viliam Široký, who served as the chairman of the meeting, delivered a closing speech.

### 欧洲国家会议闭幕  各国代表团一致赞同联合宣言

1954-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    欧洲国家会议闭幕
    各国代表团一致赞同联合宣言
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科一日讯：一九五四年十二月一日讨论保障欧洲和平和安全的莫斯科欧洲国家会议发表会议公报。
    十二月一日，讨论保障欧洲和平和安全的欧洲会议举行了第三次会议。德意志民主共和国代表团团长、德意志民主共和国总理奥托·格罗提渥担任主席。
    会议听取了阿尔巴尼亚代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席马努什·穆夫蒂乌，罗马尼亚代表团团长、罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议第一副主席基伏·斯托伊卡，中华人民共和国代表、中华人民共和国外交部副部长、中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天，匈牙利代表团团长、匈牙利人民共和国部长会议第一副主席安德拉希·赫格居斯和保加利亚代表团团长、保加利亚人民共和国部长会议第一副主席安东·于哥夫等人的发言。
    下次会议定在十二月二日上午十时举行。在这次会议上将要研究苏联、波兰人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克共和国、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国、保加利亚人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国各国政府的联合宣言草案。
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二日讯：一九五四年十二月二日讨论保障欧洲和平和安全的莫斯科欧洲国家会议发表会议公报。
    十二月二日，讨论保障欧洲和平和安全的欧洲国家会议举行第四次会议——闭幕会议。捷克斯洛伐克代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理威廉·西罗基担任主席。
    会议讨论了苏联、波兰人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克共和国、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国、保加利亚人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国八国政府的联合宣言草案。
    经过讨论，出席会议的各国代表团的全体团员们声明：他们赞同联合宣言的全文。会上并且决定十二月二日莫斯科时间下午六时在克里姆林宫举行联合宣言的签字仪式。
    在会议结束时，中华人民共和国代表、外交部副部长、中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天讲了话。
    在会议闭幕时，担任会议主席的捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团团长威廉·西罗基致闭幕辞。
