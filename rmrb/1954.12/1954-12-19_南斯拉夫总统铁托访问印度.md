Title: Yugoslav President Tito Visits India
Author:
Date: 1954-12-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 18th: Yugoslav President Tito arrived in New Delhi on the 17th. Indian President Prasad and Prime Minister Nehru were at the station to greet him.

Tito is in India for an eighteen-day visit at the invitation of the Indian government. He will then visit Burma. He is accompanied by Yugoslav Vice President Rankovic and other senior Yugoslav government officials.

### 南斯拉夫总统铁托访问印度

1954-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫总统铁托访问印度
    【新华社新德里十八日电】南斯拉夫总统铁托十七日到达新德里。到车站上迎接的有印度总统普拉沙德和总理尼赫鲁。
    铁托是应印度政府的邀请到印度作十八天访问的。他将接着访问缅甸。同行的有南斯拉夫副总统兰科维奇和其他南斯拉夫政府高级官员。
