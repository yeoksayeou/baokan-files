Title: Reception Held by the Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union for the Yugoslav Government Trade Delegation's Visit to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-12-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 27th (TASS dispatch): The Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia to the Soviet Union, D. Vidić, held a reception on the 27th for the Yugoslav government trade delegation headed by T. Todorović, Member of the Executive Council of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, who are visiting Moscow.

Among those present at the reception were V. M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; A. I. Mikoyan, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Trade of the Soviet Union; I. G. Kabanov, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, and other senior officials from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

Also present at the reception were the heads of some embassies and diplomatic missions in Moscow.

The reception proceeded in a warm atmosphere.

### 为南斯拉夫政府贸易代表团访问莫斯科  南斯拉夫驻苏联大使举行招待会

1954-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    为南斯拉夫政府贸易代表团访问莫斯科
    南斯拉夫驻苏联大使举行招待会
    【新华社讯】　据塔斯社莫斯科二十七日讯：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使德·维迪兹二十七日为以南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国执行委员会委员托多罗维奇为首的南斯拉夫政府贸易代表团访问莫斯科举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫、苏联部长会议副主席兼苏联贸易部长阿·伊·米高扬、苏联对外贸易部长伊·格·卡巴诺夫和苏联外交部和对外贸易部的其他高级官员。
    出席招待会的还有一些驻莫斯科大使馆和外交使团的首长。
    招待会在热烈的气氛中进行。
