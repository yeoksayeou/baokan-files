Title: Frontline Coastal Defense Troops and Public Security Organs in Fujian Annihilate a Band of Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Armed Special Agents

Author:

Date: 1954-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Frontline Fujian, 8th: The People's Liberation Army coastal defense troops and public security organs on the Fujian front, in coordination, annihilated a band of Chiang Kai-shek bandit armed special agents, killing three Chiang Kai-shek bandit armed special agents, and seizing two pistols, one radio transmitter, RMB 13.5 million, 203 silver dollars, and 77 gold rings.

On the night of November 28th, a band of three Chiang Kai-shek bandit armed special agents secretly landed on Xiyang Island in Xiapu County, attempting to seize an opportunity to sneak into the mainland and carry out sabotage activities. After learning of this, our coastal defense troops and public security organs immediately surrounded this band of Chiang Kai-shek bandit armed special agents on the morning of December 1st and shouted at them, ordering them to surrender; however, this band of Chiang Kai-shek bandit armed special agents relied on a stone house and stubbornly resisted with gunfire. Our People's Liberation Army coastal defense troops immediately killed all three Chiang Kai-shek bandit armed special agents on the spot.

### 福建前线海防部队和公安机关  歼灭一股蒋贼武装特务

1954-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线海防部队和公安机关
    歼灭一股蒋贼武装特务
    【新华社福建前线八日电】福建前线中国人民解放军海防部队和人民公安机关配合歼灭了一股蒋贼武装特务，打死蒋贼武装特务三名，缴获手枪二支、电台一部、人民币一千三百五十万元、银元二百零三个、金戒指七十七个。
    十一月二十八日夜晚，蒋贼武装特务一股共三人，偷渡到霞浦县西洋岛上，企图伺隙潜入大陆进行破坏活动。我海防部队和公安机关得悉后，当即于十二月一日早晨把这股蒋贼武装特务包围，并向这股蒋贼武装特务喊话，命令他们投降；但这股蒋贼武装特务竟依借一座石屋开枪顽抗。我人民解放军海防部队当场把这三名蒋贼武装特务全部击毙。
