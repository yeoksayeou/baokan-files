Title: Radford Engages in Activities in Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1954-12-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, December 21st:
    According to reports from Karachi, the capital of Pakistan, Chairman of the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff, Radford, arrived on the 19th. Upon arrival, he stated that he intended to advance the implementation of U.S. military "aid" to Pakistan. He also stated that his trip was related to the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization's military arrangements in the region.

### 雷德福到巴基斯坦活动

1954-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    雷德福到巴基斯坦活动
    【新华社二十一日讯】喀喇蚩消息：美国参谋长联席会议主席雷德福十九日到达巴基斯坦首都喀喇蚩。他在到达时表示，他要推进一下美国给予巴基斯坦军事“援助”的执行工作。他还表示，他此行和东南亚条约组织在这一地区的军事安排有关。
