Title: Guo Moruo Sends Telegram Congratulating Winners of Stalin International Peace Prize
Author:
Date: 1954-12-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, telegraphed on the 28th to congratulate the nine winners of the 1954 "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize. The telegram reads as follows:

    On behalf of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese people, I warmly congratulate you on winning the 1954 "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize and extend to you my highest respect. We firmly believe that, with the love and support of people all over the world, you will make even greater contributions to the struggle for strengthening the unity and peaceful friendship of all nations.

### 郭沫若电贺斯大林国际和平奖金得奖人

1954-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若电贺斯大林国际和平奖金得奖人
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若二十八日分别电贺一九五四年“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金的九位得奖人。电文如下：
    我谨代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民热烈祝贺您荣获一九五四年“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金，并向您致以崇高的敬意。我们深信您在全世界人民爱戴和拥护下，将在加强各国人民的团结及和平友好的斗争中，作出更大的贡献。
