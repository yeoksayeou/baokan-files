Title: Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Kingdom of Norway, Mr. J.A. Motzfeldt, calls on Foreign Minister Zhou
Author:
Date: 1954-12-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st—The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Kingdom of Norway to the People's Republic of China, Mr. J.A. Motzfeldt, called on the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, on the afternoon of the 1st to present his letter of credence. Present were Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 挪威王国驻我国临时代办  季茂登拜会周外长

1954-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    挪威王国驻我国临时代办
    季茂登拜会周外长
    【新华社一日讯】挪威王国驻中华人民共和国大使馆临时代办季茂登在一日下午拜会中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来，呈交其委任书。在座的有：外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华和交际处处长王倬如。
