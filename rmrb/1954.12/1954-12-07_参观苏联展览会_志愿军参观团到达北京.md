Title: Visiting the Soviet Exhibition: Volunteer Army Visiting Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-12-07
Page: 1

    Visiting the Soviet Exhibition
    Volunteer Army Visiting Group Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, December 6th] The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition Visiting Group, led by General Tan Shanhe, arrived in Beijing on the 6th.
    The visiting group consists of three hundred people, including commanders, political workers, combat heroes, model workers, meritorious personnel, and propaganda, education, cultural, and artistic work cadres from various arms of the Volunteer Army. They will begin visiting the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition on the 7th.

### 参观苏联展览会  志愿军参观团到达北京

1954-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    参观苏联展览会
    志愿军参观团到达北京
    【新华社六日讯】　中国人民志愿军苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会参观团在谭善和将军率领下六日到达北京。
    参观团共有三百人，团员中包括志愿军各兵种部队的指挥员、政治工作人员、战斗英雄、工作模范、功臣、宣传教育和文化艺术工作干部。他们将在七日开始参观苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会。
