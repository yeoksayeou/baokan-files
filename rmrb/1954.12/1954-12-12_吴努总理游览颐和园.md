Title: Prime Minister U Nu Visits the Summer Palace
Author:
Date: 1954-12-12
Page: 1

    [By this newspaper] Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his entourage, accompanied by Yao Zhongming, China's ambassador to Burma, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, visited the Summer Palace on the afternoon of the 11th. Prime Minister U Nu visited the Hall of Benevolence and Longevity, the Hall of Joy and Longevity, and the Long Corridor, and admired the magnificent Hall of Buddhist Fragrance, which towers majestically in front of Longevity Hill, in front of the Hall of Dispelling Clouds. Prime Minister U Nu also boarded the Qingyan Boat, overlooking the icy and snowy scenery on Kunming Lake. After the visit, Prime Minister U Nu had lunch at the Tingli Hall.

### 吴努总理游览颐和园

1954-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努总理游览颐和园
    【本报讯】十一日中午，缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的随行人员由我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明、外交部交际处处长王倬如陪同前往颐和园参观游览。吴努总理参观了仁寿殿、乐寿堂和长廊，并在排云殿前瞻仰巍然高耸于万寿山前山的金碧辉煌的佛香阁。吴努总理并登上清宴舫，眺望昆明湖上的冰光雪色。参观毕，吴努总理在听鹂馆进午餐。
