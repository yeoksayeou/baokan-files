Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1954-12-21
Page: 1

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China

    The Regulations on the 1955 National Economic Construction Public Bonds, adopted by the Third Session of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on December 20, 1954, are hereby promulgated.

    Mao Zedong
    Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    December 20, 1954

### 中华人民共和国主席令
毛泽东
1954-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    一九五五年国家经济建设公债条例已由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第三次会议于一九五四年十二月二十日通过，现予公布。
    中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
    一九五四年十二月二十日
