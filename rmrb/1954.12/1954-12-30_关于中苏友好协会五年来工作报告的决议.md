Title: Resolution on the Five-Year Work Report of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1954-12-30
Page: 1

Resolution on the Five-Year Work Report of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association

    The Second National Congress of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association approves the five-year work report of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association made by Qian Junrui, the Secretary-General of the General Association, entrusted by the Council of the General Association, and expresses satisfaction with the work achievements of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association under the leadership of Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the General Association. In the future, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association should continue to work hard in accordance with the regulations and the guidelines and tasks proposed in the work report adopted by this Congress, in order to better promote the people of the whole country to learn from the Soviet Union, further consolidate and develop the eternal and great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, so as to promote the socialist construction of the motherland and defend peace in Asia and the world.

### 关于中苏友好协会五年来工作报告的决议

1954-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    关于中苏友好协会五年来工作报告的决议
    中苏友好协会第二次全国代表会同意总会钱俊瑞总干事受总会干事会委托所作的中苏友好协会五年来工作的报告，并且对于中苏友好协会在总会刘少奇会长领导下获得的工作成绩表示满意。今后中苏友好协会应当按照本代表会所通过的章程和工作报告中所提出的方针和任务，继续努力，为更好地推动全国人民学习苏联，进一步巩固和发展中苏两国人民之间的永恒的和伟大的友谊，以推进祖国的社会主义建设，保卫亚洲与世界的和平而奋斗。
