Title: Chinese Film Week Opens in Moscow and Other Cities

Author: 
Date: 1954-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 21st: The Chinese Film Week began on December 20th in forty cinemas in Moscow, as well as many clubs, cultural palaces, and the capitals of the Soviet Union's republics. On the evening of the 20th, a celebration ceremony was held at the "Cinema Workers' House" in Moscow. Several hundred representatives from Soviet literary and artistic circles attended. Zhang Wentian, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, embassy staff, and the Chinese film delegation also participated in the celebration. Soviet Minister of Culture Alexandrov, famous director Gerasimov, and Zhang Junxiang, the head of the Chinese film delegation, spoke at the meeting. They pointed out the role of this film exhibition in promoting friendship between the peoples of the two countries. After the meeting, the new film "Sever the Devil's Claws" from the exhibition week was screened.

To welcome the film week, Moscow cinemas were decorated as if for a festival. The Chinese and Soviet national flags were hung at the entrances of the cinemas. The buildings of the "Capital," "Meeting Place," and "Grand Theatre" cinemas displayed portraits of the main characters from the Chinese films "Sever the Devil's Claws," "Chicken Feather Letter," "Taking Huashan Mountain Intelligently," and "Dragon Beard Ditch." A wreath made of lush green conifer branches was hung over the main entrance of the "Central" cinema. Many cinemas also hosted photo exhibitions in their lounges, showcasing many photos introducing the People's Republic of China, Chinese literary works published in the Soviet Union, and new Chinese films. Advertisements about the Chinese Film Week were posted everywhere on the streets of Moscow.

During the Chinese Film Week, eighteen feature films and documentary films will be screened.

### 中华人民共和国电影周在莫斯科等地开幕

1954-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国电影周在莫斯科等地开幕
    【新华社莫斯科二十一日电】十二月二十日，中华人民共和国电影周在莫斯科的四十个电影院、许多俱乐部、文化宫和苏联各加盟共和国的首都开始了。二十日晚间，在莫斯科的“电影工作者之家”举行了庆祝仪式。到苏联文学艺术界代表人士数百人。我驻苏联大使张闻天和使馆人员，我电影工作者代表团也参加了庆祝会。苏联文化部部长亚历山德罗夫、名导演格拉西莫夫、我电影工作者代表团团长张骏祥等人都在会上讲了话。他们指出这次电影展览在促进两国人民友谊中的作用。会后放映了展览周中的新片“斩断魔爪”。
    为了迎接这次电影周，莫斯科各电影院都像过节一样打扮起来。电影院的门口悬挂着中苏两国国旗。“首都”、“集议场所”和“大剧场”三个电影院的大楼，悬挂着中国影片“斩断魔爪”、“鸡毛信”、“智取华山”和“龙须沟”的主角的画像。“中央”电影院的正门挂着葱绿的针叶树枝叶制成的花圈。许多电影院的休息室内还举办图片展览，展出了许多介绍中华人民共和国、苏联出版的中国文学作品和中国新影片的照片。莫斯科的街道上到处都张贴着关于中国电影周的广告。
    在中国电影周期间，将放映十八部故事片和文献纪录片。
