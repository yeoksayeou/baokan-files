Title: Lao Government and Lao Resistance Government Hold Political Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-12-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 29th: News from Laos states that the Royal Lao Government and the Lao Resistance Government have agreed to hold a political conference. The conference commenced on December 26th in Khang Khay. Representatives from the Royal Lao Government included Minister of Agriculture and Forestry Thong Yi, Minister of Interior Ou Khoun, and Soukanrasith, a member of parliament from Thakhek Province. The Lao Resistance Government was represented by Phoumi Vongvichit, Nouhak Phoumsavan, and Sisavath Keobounphanh.

Information released before the conference indicated that the main topic of discussion was expected to be the establishment of a political coalition committee. The political coalition committee would serve as the supervisory body for national general elections. Once a new government is formed through these general elections, the political coalition committee would be dissolved.

### 老挝政府和寮国抗战政府举行政治会议

1954-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝政府和寮国抗战政府举行政治会议
    【新华社河内二十九日电】来自老挝的消息说，老挝王国政府和寮国抗战政府同意举行一次政治会议。十二月二十六日，会议已在康开召开。老挝王国政府的代表有农林大臣通异、内务大臣乌昏、他曲省议员苏堪实等，寮国抗战政府的代表是富米、诺哈和辛卡波等。
    据会前消息说，会议预定主要讨论成立政治联合委员会的问题。政治联合委员会将是全国普选的监察机关，普选产生的新政府成立后，政治联合委员会就要解散。
