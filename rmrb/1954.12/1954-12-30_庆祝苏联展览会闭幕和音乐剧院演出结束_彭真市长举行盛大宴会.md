Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Holds Grand Banquet to Celebrate the Closing of the Soviet Exhibition and the End of the Musical Theatre Performance
Author:
Date: 1954-12-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 29th:

    Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, held a grand banquet on the evening of the 29th to celebrate the conclusion of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition in Beijing and the end of the visit to China by the Soviet National Stanislavski and Nemirovich-Danchenko Musical Theatre of Moscow.

    Invited to attend were: personnel of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition working group, and the personnel of the Soviet National Moscow Musical Theatre headed by Polyakov.

    Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

    On the Chinese side, those attending the banquet included: Vice Premiers of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, He Long and Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Culture Ding Xilin, as well as other vice ministers from various ministries, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, Vice Mayors of Beijing Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party Liu Ren and members, members of the Beijing Municipal People's Government and heads of various departments, heads of people's organizations in Beijing, representatives from the capital's scientific, educational, literary, and journalistic circles, as well as engineers, technical workers, and model workers.

    A total of more than 800 people attended the banquet.

    At the beginning of the banquet, Mayor Peng Zhen spoke first. He said: The Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition has had nearly three million visitors in more than two months. Through exhibitions, seminars, reports, and on-site guidance, it has introduced the experiences of Soviet economic and cultural construction. The Soviet Exhibition has become our temporary university and temporary research institute. He also said: The Soviet National Moscow Musical Theatre has performed more than seventy performances, with more than 100,000 spectators. It has also introduced many valuable experiences to us through various methods, becoming our educational institution and our example. Mayor Peng Zhen finally expressed his gratitude to all the personnel of the exhibition and the musical theatre.

    Ambassador Yudin, President of the Soviet National Moscow Musical Theatre Polyakov, and Deputy Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition Smolyanov spoke successively, expressing their gratitude for the spirit of friendly cooperation shown by Chinese engineers, technical workers, workers, and cultural workers.

    At the meeting, Vice Premier He Long, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Minister Ye Jizhuang, and Vice Minister Ding Xilin also spoke.

    During the banquet, Beijing artists performed wonderful programs. The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝苏联展览会闭幕和音乐剧院演出结束  彭真市长举行盛大宴会

1954-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝苏联展览会闭幕和音乐剧院演出结束
    彭真市长举行盛大宴会
    【新华社二十九日讯】　北京市市长彭真在二十九日晚举行盛大宴会，庆祝苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会在北京结束工作和苏联国立莫斯科斯坦尼斯拉夫斯基与聂米罗维奇—丹钦科音乐剧院结束访问中国的演出。
    应邀出席的有：苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会工作团人员、以波利雅科夫为首的苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院人员。
    苏联驻华大使尤金和使馆人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    我国方面出席宴会的有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长伍修权，文化部副部长丁西林，以及其他各部副部长，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，北京市副市长张友渔、吴晗，中共北京市委员会副书记刘仁和委员，北京市人民政府委员及各部门负责人，北京市各人民团体负责人，首都科学、教育、文艺、新闻界人士以及工程师、技术工作者和劳动模范。
    出席宴会的共八百多人。
    宴会开始，彭真市长首先讲话。他说：苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会在两个多月中，有将近三百万观众参观，通过展览、座谈、报告和现场指导等方法，介绍了苏联经济及文化建设的经验。苏联展览会成了我们的临时大学、临时研究院。他又说：苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院演出了七十多场，有十多万观众观看，也通过了各种方法向我们介绍了许多宝贵的经验，成为我们的教育机关和我们的榜样。彭真市长最后对展览会和音乐剧院的全体人员表示感谢。
    尤金大使、苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院院长波利雅科夫和苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会副主任斯莫里扬诺夫相继讲话，他们对中国的工程师们、技术工作者们、工人们和文化工作者们所表现的友好合作精神表示谢意。
    会上，贺龙副总理、陈毅副总理、叶季壮部长和丁西林副部长也讲了话。
    在宴会进行中，北京的艺术工作者们表演了精彩的节目。宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
