Title: Beijing Circles Solemnly Hold Meeting to Support Resolutions of the World Peace Council Stockholm Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th:
Beijing's circles solemnly held a meeting in the capital this afternoon to support the resolutions of the World Peace Council Stockholm Conference.

The meeting was presided over by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace.

The venue was solemn and simple.

On the rostrum were: Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Cai Tingkai, Wu Yaozong, Liu Guanyi, Hu Yuzhi, Xu Deheng, Zhang Tiesheng, Chairman and Vice-Chairmen of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, representatives and specially invited representatives of the Chinese delegation to the World Peace Council Stockholm Conference, as well as Kameta Togo and Wantueleli, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and Cueto, a peace figure from Chile visiting China.

Attending the meeting were: members of the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace and the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as heads of various departments, cadres from national democratic parties, people's organizations, and various organs in Beijing, and people from the scientific, educational, literary, artistic, news, and publishing circles in Beijing, totaling more than 900 people.

Peace figures from various countries in Beijing also attended the meeting.

At the beginning of the meeting, Chairman Guo Moruo delivered an opening speech. He pointed out that the World Peace Council Stockholm Conference adopted a number of important resolutions, formulated a new program for the current world peace movement, and advanced the world peace movement another step forward. The success of the conference demonstrated the continued growth of the world peace forces. Guo Moruo thanked the Chinese delegation for gloriously completing the task entrusted by the Chinese people and expressed sincere congratulations to the entire delegation.

At the meeting, Wu Yaozong, a member of the World Peace Council and a representative of the Chinese delegation to the World Peace Council Stockholm Conference, delivered a "Report on the Situation and Achievements of the World Peace Council Stockholm Conference" on behalf of the Chinese delegation (full text is available on page 2 of today's newspaper).

Cueto then spoke at the meeting, conveying the respect of the Chilean people to the meeting, their sympathy and support for the Chinese people's struggle for final and complete liberation, and wishing the Chinese people continued progress and development. Cueto said: China, with its 600 million people, united with the Soviet Union and the people's democratic countries, guarantees peace. On the basis of such unity, coupled with the unity between the Latin American people and you, all countries will be able to enjoy peace, happiness, and prosperity in the future.

Kameta Togo then spoke, saying that the Japanese people support all the resolutions of the World Peace Council Stockholm Conference, support the struggle of the European people against the rearmament of West Germany, support the Chinese people's struggle to liberate Taiwan and restore its legitimate seat in the United Nations, and support the restoration of diplomatic relations between China and Japan based on the Five Principles in the Sino-Indian and Sino-Burmese joint statements.

Finally, the meeting unanimously adopted the resolution of Beijing's circles to support the resolutions of the World Peace Council Stockholm Conference.

### 拥护世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议决议  北京各界隆重举行大会

1954-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    拥护世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议决议
    北京各界隆重举行大会
    【新华社十七日讯】北京各界拥护世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议决议大会，十七日下午在首都隆重举行。
    大会由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若主持。
    会场庄严朴素。
    在主席台上的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、副主席，出席世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议的中国代表团代表和特邀代表郭沫若、陈叔通、蔡廷锴、吴耀宗、刘贯一、胡愈之、许德珩、张铁生和亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍、万徒勒里及智利来华访问的和平人士库埃托。
    出席大会的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员以及各部门负责人，全国各民主党派、各人民团体及北京市各机关干部，北京市科学、教育、文学、艺术、新闻、出版界人士共九百多人。
    各国的在京和平人士也出席了大会。
    会议开始时，郭沫若主席致开幕词。他指出世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议通过各项重要决议，为当前的世界和平运动制定了新的纲领，把世界和平运动向前推进了一步。会议的成功表明了世界和平力量的继续壮大。郭沫若感谢中国代表团光荣地完成了中国人民委托的任务，并向代表团全体表示衷心的慰劳。
    会上，由出席世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议的中国代表团代表、世界和平理事会理事吴耀宗代表中国代表团作了“关于世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议的情况和成就的报告”（全文见今日本报第二版）。
    库埃托接着在会上发言，他向大会转达智利人民的敬意，转达了智利人民对中国人民争取最后的彻底的解放的同情和支援，祝中国人民继续前进发展。库埃托说：六亿人口的中国同苏联和人民民主国家团结在一起，就保证了和平。在这样团结的基础上，加上拉丁美洲人民和你们的团结，就能够使各国在明天享受到和平、幸福和繁荣。
    龟田东伍接着讲话，他说，日本人民支持世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议的各项决议，支持欧洲人民反对重新武装西德斗争，支持中国人民解放台湾的斗争和恢复在联合国的正当地位，并支持中日两国根据中印、中缅联合声明中的五项原则恢复邦交。
    最后，大会一致通过北京各界拥护世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议决议的决议。
