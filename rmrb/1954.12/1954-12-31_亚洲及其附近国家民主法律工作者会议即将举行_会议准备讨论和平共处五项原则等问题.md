Title: Asian and Neighboring Countries' Democratic Legal Workers Conference to be Held Soon; The Conference is Preparing to Discuss Issues such as the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence

Author:

Date: 1954-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 29th: The Asian and Neighboring Countries' Democratic Legal Workers Conference will be held in Calcutta, India, from January 25th to 30th, 1955. According to Snaghangsu Acharya, Secretary of the All-India Democratic Lawyers Association, at a press conference held in Calcutta on the 25th, the main issues to be discussed at the conference are the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence advocated by Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru, the prohibition of all destructive weapons, the recognition of women's rights, and the safeguarding and expansion of civil rights. Acharya said that representatives from China, Ceylon, Mongolia, North Korea, Japan, Vietnam, Burma, Australia, New Zealand, Indonesia, Pakistan, Egypt, Iraq, Israel, Jordan, Syria, Lebanon, the Soviet Union, Nepal, and other countries are expected to attend the conference.

This conference is being convened at the initiative of the All-India Democratic Lawyers Association.

### 亚洲及其附近国家民主法律工作者会议即将举行  会议准备讨论和平共处五项原则等问题

1954-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    亚洲及其附近国家民主法律工作者会议即将举行
    会议准备讨论和平共处五项原则等问题
    【新华社新德里二十九日电】亚洲及其附近国家民主法律工作者会议将于一九五五年一月二十五日到三十日在印度加尔各答举行。据全印民主法律工作者协会秘书斯奈汉格苏·阿查雅二十五日在加尔各答举行的记者招待会上说，会议将要讨论的主要问题是周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理所倡导的和平共处的五项原则，禁止一切毁灭性武器，承认妇女权利以及维护和扩大公民权利的问题。阿查雅说，中国、锡兰、蒙古、朝鲜、日本、越南、缅甸、澳大利亚、新西兰、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦、埃及、伊拉克、以色列、约旦、叙利亚、黎巴嫩、苏联、尼泊尔和其他国家的代表可望参加这次会议。
    这次会议是由全印民主法律工作者协会发起召开的。
