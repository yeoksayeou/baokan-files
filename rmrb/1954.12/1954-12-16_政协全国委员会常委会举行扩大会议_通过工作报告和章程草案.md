Title: CPPCC National Committee Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting, Approves Work Report and Charter Draft
Author:
Date: 1954-12-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The 63rd meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held as an enlarged meeting on the 15th. The meeting discussed and approved the following documents to be submitted to the First Session of the Second National Committee: "Work Report of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference" and "Constitution of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (Draft)." The meeting also discussed and decided on the agenda (draft) of the First Session of the Second National Committee and the method of group discussions.

### 政协全国委员会常委会举行扩大会议  通过工作报告和章程草案

1954-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会常委会举行扩大会议
    通过工作报告和章程草案
    【新华社十五日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会常务委员会第六十三次会议在十五日举行扩大会议。会议讨论和通过了向第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议提出的各项文件——“中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会工作报告”和“中国人民政治协商会议章程（草案）”。会议还讨论和决定了第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议议程（草案）和分组讨论的办法。
