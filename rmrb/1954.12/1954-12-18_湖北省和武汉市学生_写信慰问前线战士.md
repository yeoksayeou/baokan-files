Title: Students in Hubei Province and Wuhan City Write Letters to Comfort Frontline Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1954-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, December 16th: With the New Year approaching, students from universities and middle schools in Hubei Province and Wuhan City are writing letters to comfort frontline soldiers. The Hubei Provincial Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace receives a large number of entrusted comfort letters to be forwarded to the front lines every day; within the 13th and 14th alone, it received 500 comfort letters.

	The entire class of second-year students majoring in hydraulic power equipment in the Power Department of the Central China Institute of Technology wrote to the air force soldiers on the Zhejiang front, thanking the People's Air Force for their contributions to the motherland and expressing their intention to study harder and more diligently in the future, as well as actively exercise. Students from Daye County Junior Middle School wrote more than 300 letters to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army soldiers.

### 湖北省和武汉市学生  写信慰问前线战士

1954-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    湖北省和武汉市学生
    写信慰问前线战士
    【新华社武汉十六日电】新年接近了，湖北省和武汉市各大、中学校的学生纷纷写信慰问前线战士。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会湖北省分会每天都收到大批嘱托转交前线的慰问信件；十三、十四日内，收到的慰问信就有五百封。
    华中工学院动力系水力动力装置专业二年级有一个班的全体学生，写信给浙江前线的空军战士们，感谢人民空军对祖国的贡献，表示今后在学习上要更加刻苦钻研，还要积极锻炼身体。大冶县初级中学学生写给中国人民志愿军战士的信有三百多封。
