Title: Prime Minister U Nu Leaves Beijing to Visit East China and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1954-12-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his wife, who were invited by the Government of the People's Republic of China to visit our country, left Beijing by plane at 9:00 am on the 12th. They will visit East China, South China, and other places before returning home. Accompanying Prime Minister U Nu on his visit to China were U Myint Thein, Judge of the Supreme Court of Burma, U Win Pe, Prime Minister's Secretary and Cabinet Secretary, U Thant, Prime Minister's Secretary, Colonel Aung Gyi, Staff Officer of the Army Department, Captain Ba Swe, Prime Minister's Aide-de-Camp, and Daw Khin Kyi Su, Prime Minister's Private Secretary, and three others, who also flew from Beijing on the same day. Accompanying Prime Minister U Nu were U Hla Maung, Burmese Ambassador to China, Wang Bingnan, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs and Director of the General Office, and Yao Zhongming, Chinese Ambassador to Burma.

Those who saw them off at the airport were: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Zihui, He Long, and Chen Yi, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Deng Xiaoping, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the China Democratic League Zhang Bojun, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Huang Yanpei, Chairman of the China Peace Committee Guo Moruo, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Chen Shutong, Vice President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Xu Guangping, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, and Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Nie Rongzhen, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China Zhang Hanfu.

Also present at the airport to see them off were: Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, ministers and directors of various ministries and commissions Luo Ruiqing, Shi Liang, Bo Yibo, Zhang Naiqi, Ye Jizhuang, Jiang Guangnai, Liao Luyan, Fu Zuoyi, Shen Yanbing, Yang Xiufeng, and Li Dequan, vice ministers and deputy directors of various ministries and commissions Lei Renmin, Lai Jifa, Wu Jingtian, Zhu Lizhi, Fan Shiren, Mao Qihua, Ding Xilin, Cai Tingkai, and Zhuang Xiquan, Director of the Bureau of Religious Affairs of the State Council He Chengxiang, as well as Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang, and Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru.

Also present at the airport to see them off were: Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Su Yu, Deputy Chief of General Staff Zhang Zongxun, and Deputy Director of the General Political Department Xiao Hua.

Also present at the airport to see them off was the Vice Mayor of Beijing, Zhang Youyu.

Also present at the airport to see them off were: Standing Committee Members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Shao Lizi, Zhang Zhizhong, Shi Fuliang, Ma Yinchu, and Huang Qixiang, Central Committee Member of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Long Yun, Vice Chairman of the China Democratic League Luo Longji, Vice Chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy Wang Shao'ao, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Peng Zemin, Chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party Chen Qiyou, Chairman of the Jiusan Society Xu Deheng, Vice Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Li Chunqing, and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League Hu Yaobang.

Also present at the airport to see them off were: Vice President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Qian Weichang and directors, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation Tian Demin, Secretary-General of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Yang Hansheng, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, Vice Presidents Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, and Qian Duansheng, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, Chairman of the Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee of Protestant Churches in China Wu Yaozong, Standing Committee Member Wang Zizhong, Acting President of the Buddhist Association of China Xiraojiacuo, Deputy Secretary-Generals Jujan, Guo Peng, and members of the Chinese cultural delegation who had visited Burma.

Members of the Burmese Embassy in China were also at the airport to see them off.

The diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China who were present at the airport to see them off were: Czechoslovak Ambassador Kang Shala, Polish Ambassador Kirilok, Indian Ambassador Raghvan, Vietnamese Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan, Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Koenig, Korean Ambassador Choi Il, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov, Albanian Ambassador Nase, Hungarian Ambassador Sikladen, Finnish Minister Sonck, Danish Minister Graeger, Swiss Minister Benoît, British Chargé d'affaires TW Williams, Dutch Chargé d'affaires Sman, Norwegian Chargé d'affaires Timohten, Swedish Chargé d'affaires Hammarskjöld, Romanian Chargé d'affaires Mentianu, Pakistani Chargé d'affaires Sultan Muhammad Khan, and First Secretary of the Indonesian Embassy Wiyogo Wirjodojo.

The Burmese and Chinese flags were flying at the airport.

As Prime Minister U Nu entered the airport, the band played the national anthems of Burma and China.

Prime Minister U Nu reviewed the guard of honor and accepted flowers presented by children from Beijing.

Prime Minister U Nu issued a written statement before his departure (full text to be published separately).

### 吴努总理离京去华东等地参观

1954-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努总理离京去华东等地参观
    【新华社十二日讯】应中华人民共和国政府邀请来我国访问的缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人，在十二日上午九时乘飞机离开北京，将在华东、华南等地参观后返国。随同吴努总理访问我国的缅甸最高法院法官吴敏登、总理秘书兼内阁秘书吴温佩、总理秘书吴丹、陆军部参谋昂季上校、总理侍从武官毕梭上尉、总理私人秘书都庆素等九人同日飞离北京。同机陪送吴努总理的有缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂和我国外交部部长助理兼办公厅主任王炳南、我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明。
    到机场欢送的有：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓子恢、贺龙、陈毅，最高人民法院院长董必武，中国共产党中央委员会秘书长邓小平，中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深，中国民主同盟副主席章伯钧，中国民主建国会总会主任委员黄炎培，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，北京市市长彭真，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令员聂荣臻，中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫。
