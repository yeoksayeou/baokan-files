Title: CPPCC Second National Committee Meeting Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1954-12-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 20th — The first session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) is scheduled to open on the 21st. The members attending this meeting have been reporting in.

Since late October, the Standing Committee of the First National Committee of the CPPCC has held several enlarged meetings to discuss the preparations for convening the first session of the Second National Committee, to deliberate and approve the list of members of the Second National Committee, and to discuss and approve the documents submitted to this meeting – "Work Report of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference" and "Constitution of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (Draft)."

Prior to the opening of this meeting, the members attending the meeting held group discussions on the "Work Report of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference" and the "Constitution of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (Draft)."

### 政协二届全国委员会会议今开幕

1954-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    政协二届全国委员会会议今开幕
    【新华社二十日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议已确定在二十一日开幕。出席这次会议的委员们已经陆续报到。
    从十月下旬以来，中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会常务委员会曾多次举行扩大会议，讨论召开第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议的各项筹备工作，协商和通过了第二届全国委员会委员名单，并讨论和通过了向这次会议提出的各项文件——“中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会工作报告”和“中国人民政治协商会议章程（草案）”。
    在这次会议开幕前，出席会议的委员们曾分组就“中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会工作报告”和“中国人民政治协商会议章程（草案）”进行了讨论。
