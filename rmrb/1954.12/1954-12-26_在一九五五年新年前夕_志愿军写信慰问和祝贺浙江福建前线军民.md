Title: On the Eve of the New Year in 1955, Volunteer Army Soldiers Write Letters of慰问 and Congratulations to the Military and Civilians on the Zhejiang and Fujian Frontlines
Author:
Date: 1954-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 25th: On the eve of the New Year, the vast numbers of commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army have been writing letters to the People's Liberation Army and the people of Zhejiang and Fujian, who are heroically defending the motherland's frontlines. They express their慰问 and congratulations, and encourage them to strive for new victories in the struggle to liberate Taiwan.

    Shen Zhihong, a second-class hero of a certain unit of the Volunteer Army who participated in the national people's delegation to慰问 the People's Liberation Army this spring, wrote in a letter to the People's Liberation Army: "Your heroic struggle and brilliant victories on the coastal defense front to defend the motherland have greatly inspired us. I sincerely congratulate you on continuing to make even greater achievements in the glorious struggle to liberate Taiwan, defend the integrity of the motherland's territorial sovereignty, and defend Asian and world peace."

    All the soldiers of the first squad of a certain unit, in a letter to the People's Navy unit that sank the Chiang bandit's "Taiping" warship, said: "The New Year is coming soon, let us once again congratulate you on your brilliant victory in sinking the Chiang bandit's 'Taiping' warship. We hope that you will overcome difficulties, continue your efforts, and strive for new victories!"

    The ninth company of a certain unit, which defended Height 432.8 during the counterattack in the summer of last year, repelled 18 counterattacks by an enemy reinforced battalion, annihilated more than 500 enemies, and was awarded a collective first-class merit, wrote in a letter to the people of the Zhejiang and Fujian frontline areas: "You are at the forefront of the motherland's coastal defense. Over the past few years, you have made your greatest efforts and achieved certain results in the production front and in supporting the People's Liberation Army in defending the motherland and liberating Taiwan. Your achievements have greatly inspired every one of our commanders and fighters. As the New Year approaches, we wish you continued and even greater contributions in the New Year."

    Many heroes, models, and cadres and soldiers from the Volunteer Army units who lived in the Zhejiang and Fujian frontline areas have also written letters to the People's Liberation Army and the people in the southeastern coastal areas of the motherland. The letters unanimously stated that in the New Year, they will continue to support the great struggle of the people of the motherland to liberate Taiwan with practical actions of vigilantly guarding their positions and striving for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue.

### 在一九五五年新年前夕  志愿军写信慰问和祝贺浙江福建前线军民

1954-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    在一九五五年新年前夕
    志愿军写信慰问和祝贺浙江福建前线军民
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】中国人民志愿军广大指挥员、战斗员在新年前夕纷纷写信给英勇守卫在祖国浙江、福建前线的人民解放军和这些地区的人民，表示慰问和祝贺，并鼓励他们在解放台湾的斗争中争取新的胜利。
    今年春季参加全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团的志愿军某部二级英雄沈志宏，在给解放军的信中写道：你们在海防前线上为保卫祖国所进行的英勇斗争和取得的光辉胜利，大大地鼓舞了我们。我衷心地祝贺你们在解放台湾、保卫祖国领土主权完整和保卫亚洲与世界和平的光荣斗争中继续建立更大的功勋。
    某部第一班的全体战士，在写给击沉蒋贼“太平号”军舰的人民海军部队的信中说：“新年快到了，让我们再一次祝贺你们击沉蒋贼‘太平号’军舰的光辉胜利，希望你们克服困难，再接再厉，争取新的胜利！”
    在去年夏季反击战中坚守四三二点八高地打垮敌人一个加强营十八次反扑、歼敌五百多名、荣立集体一等功的某部第九连，在给浙江和福建前线地区的人民的信中写道：“你们处在祖国的海防最前线，几年来，你们在生产战线上、在支援人民解放军保卫祖国、解放台湾的斗争中尽了最大努力，取得了一定的成绩。你们的成绩极大地鼓舞了我们每一个指战员。当新年将要到来的时候，我们预祝你们在新的一年中继续作出更大的贡献”。
    志愿军部队中许多英雄、模范和家住浙江、福建前线地区的干部和战士们，也纷纷地给祖国东南沿海地区的人民解放军和人民写了信，信中一致表示要在新的一年中，继续以警惕地严守阵地的实际行动和争取朝鲜问题的和平解决来支援祖国人民解放台湾的伟大斗争。
