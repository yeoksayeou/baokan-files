Title: Fourth World Forestry Conference Closes in India; Forestry Development in New China Attracts Great Attention from Delegates of Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi: The Fourth World Forestry Conference, held in Dehradun, India, closed on the 22nd. The conference adopted the report of the session. The report includes concluding recommendations on the topics discussed at the conference, as well as a list of the preparatory committee for the next conference. This list includes fourteen countries, including China, the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, France, the United States, and India.

During the conference, representatives from India and the Soviet Union delivered reports at the meeting. The report by the well-known Soviet forestry expert Sukachev on forest types was highly valued by the delegates attending the conference.

The rapid development of forestry in New China has attracted great attention from delegates of various countries. During the conference, the delegation of the People's Republic of China distributed a special booklet, "Forestry in New China," to delegates from various countries and displayed Chinese forestry pictures in the corridors of the venue. A bulletin issued by the conference secretariat praised the Chinese pictures.

After the closing of the conference, the Chinese delegation, accompanied by forestry officials from the Indian government, will visit forestry areas in Agra, Hyderabad, Madras, Calcutta, and other places for a three-week tour.

### 第四届世界林业会议在印度闭幕  新中国的林业发展引起各国代表的极大注意

1954-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    第四届世界林业会议在印度闭幕
    新中国的林业发展引起各国代表的极大注意
    【新华社新德里讯】在印度台霍拉登举行的第四届世界林业会议于二十二日闭幕。会议通过了本届会议的报告书。报告书包括对会议讨论的专题的结论性建议，以及下届会议筹备委员会的名单。这个名单包括中国、苏联、英国、法国、美国、印度等十四个国家。
    在会议期间，印度代表和苏联代表在会上作了报告。苏联著名林业专家苏卡切夫关于林型问题的报告获得出席会议的代表们的重视。
    新中国林业的迅速发展引起了各国代表的极大注意。中华人民共和国代表团在会议期间以“新中国的林业”专册分发给各国代表，并且在会场走廊里展出了中国林业图片。会议秘书处发表的公报对中国图片表示赞扬。
    中国代表团在会议闭幕后，将由印度政府林业官员陪同前往亚格拉、海得拉巴、马德拉斯、加尔各答等地的林业区进行三星期的参观。
