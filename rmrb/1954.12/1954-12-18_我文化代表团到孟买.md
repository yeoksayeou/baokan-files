Title: Our Cultural Delegation Arrives in Bombay
Author:
Date: 1954-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bombay, December 17th: The Chinese cultural delegation arrived in Bombay, the largest city on India's west coast, by train from Delhi on the morning of the 17th. They were warmly welcomed at the station by approximately 10,000 Bombay citizens. The delegation members were adorned with many garlands by the welcomers.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Bombay Mayor Patel and Bombay government officials, representatives from seventy mass organizations such as the Bombay Reception Committee for the Chinese Cultural Delegation, the India-China Friendship Association, the All-India Peace Council, Chinese Consul General in Bombay Yao Nian, foreign consuls in Bombay, and overseas Chinese.

Accompanying the Chinese cultural delegation to Bombay were General Gaur, from the Indian Central Reception Committee hosting the Chinese cultural delegation, Mr. Yunus, Deputy Secretary of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, and Kang Maozhao, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in India.

### 我文化代表团到孟买

1954-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团到孟买
    【新华社孟买十七日电】中国文化代表团十七日早晨乘火车从德里到达印度西海岸的最大城市孟买。他们在车站受到孟买约一万市民的热烈欢迎。代表团人员被欢迎者戴上了许多花环。
    到车站欢迎的有孟买市长巴特尔和孟买政府官员，招待中国文化代表团的孟买招待委员会、印中友好协会、全印和平理事会等七十个群众团体的代表，中国驻孟买总领事姚念和各国驻孟买领事和华侨等。
    陪同中国文化代表团到达孟买的有招待中国文化代表团的印度中央招待委员会的高尔准将、印度外交部副秘书尤努斯和中国驻印度大使馆文化参赞康矛召。
