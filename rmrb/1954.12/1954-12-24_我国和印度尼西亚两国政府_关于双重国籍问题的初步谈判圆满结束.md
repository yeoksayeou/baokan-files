Title: Preliminary Negotiations on Dual Nationality Between the Chinese and Indonesian Governments Successfully Concluded
Author:
Date: 1954-12-24
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd: The preliminary negotiations between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Indonesia on the issue of dual nationality have been successfully concluded.
    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, received the negotiating representatives of the Government of the Republic of Indonesia on the morning of the 22nd. Those received were: the head of the delegation of the Government of the Republic of Indonesia, A. Mononutu, Ambassador of the Republic of Indonesia to the People's Republic of China; members: Dr. Taib Napis, Deputy Director of the Legal Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Indonesia; Mr. Bersik, Chief of the China and East Asia Section of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Indonesia.
    Present at the reception were: Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China and Director of the Asian Department.

### 我国和印度尼西亚两国政府  关于双重国籍问题的初步谈判圆满结束

1954-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和印度尼西亚两国政府
    关于双重国籍问题的初步谈判圆满结束
    【新华社二十三日讯】中华人民共和国政府和印度尼西亚共和国政府关于双重国籍问题的初步谈判，已经圆满结束。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，特于二十二日上午接见印度尼西亚共和国政府的谈判代表。受接见者有：印度尼西亚共和国政府代表团团长、印度尼西亚共和国驻中华人民共和国大使阿·莫诺努图，团员：印度尼西亚共和国外交部法律司副司长塔伊布·纳皮斯博士，印度尼西亚共和国外交部亚洲司中国和东亚科科长伯锡克先生。
    接见时在座的有：中华人民共和国外交部部长助理兼亚洲司司长陈家康。
