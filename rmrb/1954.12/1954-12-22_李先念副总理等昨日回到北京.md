Title: Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Others Return to Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1954-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 21st: Li Xiannian, head of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, and delegation members Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of Agriculture, and others, who were invited to Tirana to attend the 10th anniversary celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of Albania, returned to Beijing on the 21st.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General, Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and East European Affairs.

Also present at the station to welcome them were Nasse, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China, and Guo Zuowen, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China.

Before arriving in the People's Republic of Albania, the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China visited the Romanian People's Republic.

### 李先念副总理等昨日回到北京

1954-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    李先念副总理等昨日回到北京
    【新华社二十一日讯】应邀到地拉那参加阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国庆十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长、国务院副总理李先念和团员、农业部副部长杨显东等，在二十一日回到了北京。
    到车站欢迎的有，国务院副总理陈毅、秘书长习仲勋，中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，外交部副部长伍修权、苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇等。
    到车站欢迎的还有，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻华大使纳赛，罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使郭佐文。
    中华人民共和国政府代表团在到阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国以前，曾访问了罗马尼亚人民共和国。
