Title: Song Qingling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Holds a Banquet to Bid Farewell to the Personnel of the Moscow Musical Theatre and Welcome the Delegation of the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-12-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. Song Qingling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, held a banquet on the afternoon of the 30th to bid farewell to the entire personnel of the Soviet National Moscow Stanislavsky and Nemirovich-Danchenko Musical Theatre who are about to leave Beijing for home, and to welcome the delegation sent by the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to China to attend the Second National Congress of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

The entire personnel of the Soviet National Moscow Musical Theatre, headed by Dean Polyakov, and Nikolai Alekseevich Vizhzhilin, Vice-Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Nikolai Kirillovich Goncharov, Vice-Chairman of the Education Group Committee of the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others, were invited to attend the banquet.

Also invited to attend the banquet were Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin and embassy staff, and personnel of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievement Exhibition.

Present at the banquet were Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Wu Yuzhang, Shen Junru, Cheng Qian, Li Dequan, Shen Yanbing, Shao Lizi, and Li Zhuchen, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Council, and some council members in Beijing.

Also present at the banquet were Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council, Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Dong Biwu and Bao Erhan, Vice Chairmen of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations, representatives of ethnic minorities, and people from the capital's scientific, educational, literary, news, and religious circles.

More than 900 people attended the banquet.

At the banquet, President Song Qingling spoke first. She said that the performance of the Soviet National Moscow Musical Theatre in our country is undoubtedly a major event in the history of Sino-Soviet cultural exchange, and at the same time, it has made outstanding contributions to the further consolidation and development of the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people.

Ambassador Yudin then spoke. He described the recent cultural exchange activities between China and the Soviet Union. He said that these activities fully demonstrated the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. Yudin said that the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association recently held its Second National Congress and elected Song Qingling, the world-famous peace fighter, as its President. We believe that under her leadership, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will surely accomplish its mission.

Vice President Shen Yanbing then spoke. He said that the outstanding performances of the actors of the Soviet National Moscow Musical Theatre in Beijing demonstrated the remarkable achievements of Soviet art, and Soviet art is our glorious example. He congratulated the Soviet artists on their greater achievements in art.

Polyakov, Dean of the Moscow Musical Theatre, and Nikolai Alekseevich Vizhzhilin, Vice-Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, also spoke. They expressed their deep gratitude to President Song Qingling and the Chinese people on behalf of the Musical Theatre and the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. They stated that Sino-Soviet friendship is eternal and unbreakable, and wished for the further development of Sino-Soviet cultural exchanges.

During the banquet, artists from China and the Soviet Union gave wonderful performances.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

After the banquet, a dance was held.

### 欢送莫斯科音乐剧院人员和欢迎苏联对外文协代表团  中苏友协总会会长宋庆龄举行宴会

1954-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    欢送莫斯科音乐剧院人员和欢迎苏联对外文协代表团
    中苏友协总会会长宋庆龄举行宴会
    【新华社三十日讯】中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄在三十日下午设宴欢送即将离开北京回国的苏联国立莫斯科斯坦尼斯拉夫斯基与聂米罗维奇—丹钦科音乐剧院全体人员和欢迎苏联对外文化协会派来我国参加中苏友好协会第二次全国代表会的代表团。
    以波利雅科夫院长为首的苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院全体人员和苏联对外文化协会理事会副主席尼古拉·阿列克谢耶维奇·维兹日林，苏联对外文化协会教育小组委员会副主席尼古拉·基里洛维奇·冈查罗夫等应邀出席了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的，还有苏联驻华大使尤金和使馆人员，苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会人员。
    出席作陪的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长林伯渠、李济深、吴玉章、沈钧儒、程潜、李德全、沈雁冰、邵力子、李烛尘，理事会秘书长钱俊瑞和在京部分理事。
    出席宴会的，还有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，国务院副总理李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会副主席董必武、鲍尔汉，各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，少数民族代表，首都科学、教育、文艺、新闻、宗教界人士。
    出席宴会的共有九百多人。
    宴会上，宋庆龄会长首先讲话。她说，苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院来我国演出，毫无疑义，是中苏文化交流史上的一件大事，同时对中苏两国人民伟大友谊的进一步巩固和发展，作了卓越的贡献。
    尤金大使接着讲话。他叙述了中苏两国最近一个时期的文化交流活动。他说，这些活动充分表现了中苏两国人民伟大的友谊。尤金说，中苏友好协会最近举行了第二次全国代表会，选举了世界著名的和平战士宋庆龄为会长，我们相信在她的领导下，中苏友好协会一定会完成自己的任务。
    沈雁冰副会长接着讲话。他说，苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院演员在北京的杰出的演出，显示了苏维埃艺术的卓越成就，苏联的艺术是我们的光辉榜样。他祝贺苏联艺术家们在艺术方面获得更大的成就。
    莫斯科音乐剧院院长波利雅科夫和苏联对外文化协会理事会副主席尼古拉·阿列克谢耶维奇·维兹日林也讲了话。他们代表音乐剧院和苏联对外文化协会向宋庆龄会长和中国人民表示深切的谢意。他们表示，中苏友谊是万古长存、牢不可破的，并祝中苏文化交流的进一步发展。
    宴会进行中，中苏两国艺术家们作了精彩的表演。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
    宴会后，举行了舞会。
