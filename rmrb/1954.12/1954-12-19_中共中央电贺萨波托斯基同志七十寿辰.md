Title: CPC Central Committee Congratulates Comrade Zápotocký on His Seventieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1954-12-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 18th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to Antonín Zápotocký, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, congratulating him on his seventieth birthday. The telegram reads as follows:

Dear Comrade Antonín Zápotocký:

On the occasion of your seventieth birthday, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends to you, one of the outstanding organizers and leaders of the Communist Party and working class of Czechoslovakia, fraternal congratulations. We wish you good health and new achievements in leading the Czechoslovak people to rapidly develop the entire national economy, especially in increasing agricultural production, improving the material and cultural living standards of the working people, and in the struggle to consolidate world peace and international security.

                                  The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                    December 16, 1954

### 中共中央电贺萨波托斯基同志七十寿辰

1954-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺萨波托斯基同志七十寿辰
    【新华社十八日讯】中国共产党中央委员会致电捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会政治局委员安托宁·萨波托斯基祝贺他的七十寿辰。电文如下：亲爱的安托宁·萨波托斯基同志：
    在您七十寿辰的时候，中国共产党中央委员会谨向您——捷克斯洛伐克共产党和工人阶级杰出的组织者和领导者之一，致以兄弟般的祝贺。祝贺您的身体健康，祝贺您在领导捷克斯洛伐克人民迅速发展整个国民经济，特别是在提高农业生产，提高劳动人民的物质和文化生活水平和为巩固世界和平与国际安全而进行的斗争中，获得新的成就。
                                  中国共产党中央委员会
                                    一九五四年十二月十六日
