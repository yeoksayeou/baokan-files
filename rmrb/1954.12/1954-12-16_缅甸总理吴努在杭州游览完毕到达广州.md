Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Arrives in Guangzhou After Touring Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1954-12-16
Page: 1
[Guangzhou, December 15th] Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, his wife, and his entourage, accompanied by Burmese Ambassador to China U Myint Maung, Assistant Minister of the Chinese Foreign Ministry and Director of the General Office Wang Bingnan, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming, arrived in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 15th by plane after touring Hangzhou on the 14th.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Tao Zhu, Acting Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office, Zhu Guang, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, and Wen Niansheng, Deputy Chief of Staff of the South China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, among others.

On the evening of the 15th, Prime Minister U Nu, his wife, and his entourage attended a banquet given by Tao Zhu, Acting Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, at his invitation.

While in Hangzhou, Prime Minister U Nu and his wife visited the "Three Pools Mirroring the Moon," Gushan, Yuquan, "Viewing Fish at Flower Harbor," Yue Fei Temple, and Lingyin Temple on the West Lake. Prime Minister U Nu also visited the famous Hangzhou Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory. The factory workers presented Prime Minister U Nu with a silk portrait of Burmese President Ba U and a silk portrait of himself. Prime Minister U Nu said to factory director Qi Liyi that when he saw these two silk portraits, he would remember this factory and those skilled silk weavers. On the evening of the 14th, Prime Minister U Nu was accompanied by Huo Shilian, Vice Chairman of the Zhejiang Provincial People's Government, and others for dinner.

### 缅甸总理吴努在杭州游览完毕到达广州

1954-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴努在杭州游览完毕到达广州
    【本报广州十五日电】缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人和随行人员在缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂、我国外交部部长助理兼办公厅主任王炳南和我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明陪同下，于十四日到达杭州游览后，十五日下午乘飞机到达广州。
    到机场欢迎的，有广东省人民政府代主席陶铸、外事处处长曹若茗，广州市人民政府副市长朱光，中国人民解放军中南军区副参谋长文年生等。
    十五日晚，吴努总理和他的夫人和随行人员应邀出席了广东省人民政府代主席陶铸举行的宴会。
    吴努总理和他的夫人等在杭州时，曾游览了西湖的“三潭印月”、孤山、玉泉、“花港观鱼”、岳王庙和灵隐寺。吴努总理还参观了著名的杭州都锦生丝织工厂，这个工厂的职工赠给吴努总理一幅缅甸联邦总统巴宇博士的丝织肖像和一幅他本人的丝织肖像。吴努总理对厂长祁立义说，当他看到这两幅丝织肖像时，他就会记起这个工厂和那些技艺精巧的丝织工人。十四日晚，吴努总理由浙江省人民政府副主席霍士廉等陪同共进晚餐。
