Title: Our Delegation Returns to Beijing After Attending the World Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-12-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Except for the head of the Chinese delegation, Liu Ningyi, who remains abroad for official duties, the other members of the Council, Cai Tingkai, Wu Yaozong, Liu Guan'yi, Guo Fang, Jin Zhonghua, and specially invited representatives Hu Yuzhi, Xu Deheng, etc., returned to Beijing on the 2nd and 5th respectively after attending the World Peace Council meeting in Stockholm.

### 出席世界和平理事会会议后  我国代表团回到北京

1954-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会会议后
    我国代表团回到北京
    【新华社七日讯】出席世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议的中国代表团除团长刘宁一因公尚留国外，其余理事蔡廷锴、吴耀宗、刘贯一、郭芳、金仲华及特邀代表胡愈之、许德珩等都已在二日和五日先后回到北京。
