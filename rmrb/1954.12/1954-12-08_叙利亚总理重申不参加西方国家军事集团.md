Title: Syrian Prime Minister Reiterates Refusal to Join Western Military Bloc
Author:
Date: 1954-12-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, December 6th, TASS dispatch from Damascus: According to an Associated Press dispatch from Damascus, Syrian Prime Minister Fares al-Khoury, speaking at a parliamentary session, reiterated his government's determination not to participate in "any Western defense pacts or Near East plans." He said: If Western countries raise the issue of "defense" with us again, we already have a universally known answer prepared: "No."

### 叙利亚总理重申不参加西方国家军事集团

1954-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚总理重申不参加西方国家军事集团
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社纽约五日讯：据美联社大马士革消息，叙利亚总理法雷斯·扈利在议会会议上发言时，再次声明他的政府决心不参加“任何西方防务公约或近东计划”。他说：如果西方国家再向我们提起“防务”问题，我们已经准备了人人都知道的答复：“不。”
