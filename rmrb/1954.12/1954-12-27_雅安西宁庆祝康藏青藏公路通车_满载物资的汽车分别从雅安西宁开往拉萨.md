Title: Ya'an and Xining Celebrate the Opening of the Kangzang and Qingzang Highways; Trucks Loaded with Supplies Depart from Ya'an and Xining for Lhasa

Author:

Date: 1954-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ya'an, December 26th: Over 20,000 people from all ethnic groups in Ya'an City gathered at the Ya'an City People's Square on the 25th to warmly celebrate the official opening of the Kangzang Highway. Amidst the celebrations, one hundred and forty trucks loaded with supplies departed from Ya'an for Lhasa. On this day, the main streets of Ya'an City were filled with red flags and colorful arches, as lively as a festival.

廖志高(Liao Zhigao), Chairman of the People's Government of Xikang Province, presented flags to all the workers who built the Kangzang Highway at the celebration rally. The flags were embroidered with the inscription by the great leader of the people of all ethnic groups, Chairman Mao: "Congratulations on the opening of the Kangzang and Qingzang Highways, consolidating the unity of the people of all ethnic groups, and building the motherland!"

Liao Zhigao then spoke at the rally, saying: "The construction of the Kangzang Highway itself is a symbol of the unity of our great motherland, a symbol of the great unity of all ethnic groups in our motherland, and a concrete manifestation of the high concern of the Chinese Communist Party, Chairman Mao, and the State Council of the People's Republic of China for the border areas and fraternal ethnic groups." He also said: With the opening of the highway, the political, economic, and cultural ties between Xikang Province and Tibet will become closer, which will be a boost and improvement to all aspects of work in Xikang Province, especially ethnic work. He finally pointed out that the people of all ethnic groups in Xikang have already given active support to the construction of the Kangzang Highway, and in the future, they should further strengthen the maintenance and management of the Kangzang Highway.

郑治彰(Zheng Zhizhang), Deputy Director of the Southwest Highway Engineering Bureau of the Ministry of Communications, introduced the features and construction process of the Kangzang Highway at the rally. 阿旺嘉错(Awang Jiacuo), Vice Chairman of the People's Government of the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Province, explained in his speech that the Kangzang Highway has already brought improvements to the lives of the Tibetan people. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Chinese Communist Party, Chairman Mao, and the State Council of the People's Republic of China for their concern, and to the hardworking road construction soldiers and civilians.

The assembly passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao, a congratulatory telegram to the Lhasa and Xining celebrations for the opening of the Kangzang and Qingzang Highways, and read out congratulatory telegrams from the Ministry of Communications, the Southwest Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, and the Party, government, and military organs of Xikang Province and various places to the Kangzang Highway Construction Headquarters.

The ribbon-cutting ceremony for the opening ceremony was held at 12:00 noon next to the Ya'an Station at the intersection of the Kangzang, Chuan Kang, and Kangdian Highways. The prominent words "2255 kilometers from Lhasa" on the Kangzang Highway milestone attracted thousands of people. Liao Zhigao, Chairman of the People's Government of Xikang Province, cut the colorful net amidst applause and music. One hundred and forty trucks loaded with tea and general merchandise immediately roared to life, slowly driving towards the vast Kangzang Plateau amidst firecrackers, applause, and flying flowers.

Xinhua News Agency, Xining, December 26th: More than 10,000 people from all ethnic groups in Qinghai Province solemnly held a rally in Xining City on the 25th to celebrate the opening of the Qingzang Highway. At the same time, thirty trucks loaded with supplies departed from Xining City and headed for Lhasa.

At 9:00 a.m., people of all ethnic groups participating in the meeting, wearing their respective ethnic costumes and holding colorful flags, marched from various streets to the meeting venue. A waist drum team and an art troupe composed of more than 400 people performed various programs at the meeting venue and on the streets. The Tar Monastery Lama Orchestra traveled by car early in the morning from a place forty miles away from Xining City to participate. Color arches were set up at the entrances of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, the Xining Office of the Panchen Lama of Tibet, and other institutions.

At the meeting, 高峰(Gao Feng), First Secretary of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, presented the road construction troops and workers with a banner inscribed by Chairman Mao: "Congratulations on the opening of the Kangzang and Qingzang Highways, consolidating the unity of the people of all ethnic groups, and building the motherland!" The entire venue resounded with warm applause. 孙作宾(Sun Zuobin), Second Secretary of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, spoke at the meeting. He said that the opening of the Qingzang Highway is a great joyous event in the lives of the people of the whole country, especially the people of Qinghai and Tibet. He expressed warm congratulations. Speakers then included 马辅臣(Ma Fuchen) (Hui nationality), Vice Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, 李书茂(Li Shumao), Deputy Commander of the Qinghai Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and representatives from all ethnic groups 阿热仓(A're Cang) (Tibetan), 官保加(Guan Baojia) (Mongolian), 松布(Song Bu) (Tu nationality), 梅亭(Mei Ting) (Salar nationality), and 哈木(Ha Mu) (Kazak nationality). They all warmly celebrated the successful opening of the Qingzang Highway and expressed their respect to the road construction troops and workers. The meeting also unanimously passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and a telegram of condolence to the road construction troops and workers.

Next, the ribbon-cutting ceremony for the opening was held. After 孙作宾(Sun Zuobin), Second Secretary of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, cut the ribbon, a float with a huge portrait of Chairman Mao and thirty trucks loaded with materials such as rice, flour, and gasoline slowly drove out of the West Gate amidst gongs, drums, firecrackers, and cheers, heading towards Lhasa on the Qingzang Highway. When the cars passed through the streets, the citizens cheered along the way and threw paper flowers to the drivers to express their respect.

### 雅安西宁庆祝康藏青藏公路通车  满载物资的汽车分别从雅安西宁开往拉萨

1954-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    雅安西宁庆祝康藏青藏公路通车
    满载物资的汽车分别从雅安西宁开往拉萨
    【新华社雅安二十六日电】雅安市各族各界人民两万余人，二十五日在雅安市人民广场集会热烈庆祝康藏公路正式通车。在庆祝声中有一百四十辆满载物资的汽车从雅安驶往拉萨。这天，雅安市的主要街道上布满了红旗和彩坊，像过节日一样热闹。
    西康省人民政府主席廖志高在庆祝大会上向修筑康藏公路的全体职工授旗。锦旗上绣有各族人民伟大领袖毛主席题词：“庆贺康藏、青藏两公路的通车，巩固各民族人民的团结，建设祖国！”
    廖志高接着在大会上讲话，他说：“康藏公路修筑的本身，就是我们伟大祖国统一的标志，就是我们祖国各民族大团结的标志，就是中国共产党、毛主席和中华人民共和国国务院对于祖国边疆和兄弟民族高度关怀的具体表现。”他又说：由于公路的通车，西康省和西藏在政治、经济、文化等方面的联系，便会更加密切，这对西康省的各项工作，特别是民族工作，将是一个推进和提高。他最后指出，西康各族人民已经给修筑康藏公路以积极支援，今后更应加强康藏公路的养护管理工作。
    交通部西南公路工程局副局长郑治彰在大会上介绍了康藏公路的面貌和修筑经过。西康省藏族自治区人民政府副主席阿旺嘉错在讲话中说明康藏公路已经给藏族人民带来了生活上的改善。他对中国共产党、毛主席和中华人民共和国国务院的关怀和筑路军工、民工们的辛勤劳动表示衷心感谢。
    大会通过向毛主席致敬电，向拉萨、西宁庆祝康藏、青藏两公路通车典礼大会的贺电，并宣读了交通部、人民解放军西南军区和西康省党、政、军机关及各地致康藏公路修建司令部的贺电。
    通车典礼的剪彩仪式，于十二时在康藏、川康、康滇三条公路交叉点的雅安车站旁边举行。康藏公路里程碑上醒目的“距拉萨二二五五公里”几个大字，吸引着千万群众。西康省人民政府主席廖志高在鼓掌声和音乐声中，剪落彩网。一百四十辆满载茶叶和百货的汽车立刻马达轰鸣，在鞭炮声、鼓掌声和鲜花飞舞中，缓缓地向着辽阔的康藏高原驶去。
    【新华社西宁二十六日电】青海省各族各界一万多人二十五日在西宁市隆重举行青藏公路通车典礼大会。同时，三十辆满载物资的汽车从西宁市出发，驶往拉萨。
    上午九时，参加大会的各族人民穿着本民族服装，高举各色彩旗，从各个街道走向会场。四百多人组成的腰鼓队和文艺队在会场和大街上表演各种节目。塔尔寺喇嘛乐队一早就从距西宁市四十里路的地方坐汽车赶来参加。青海省人民政府、西藏班禅驻西宁办事处等机关门口都搭起彩牌。
    会上，中共青海省委员会第一书记高峰，授给筑路部队和职工以毛主席题字“庆贺康藏、青藏两公路的通车、巩固各民族人民的团结，建设祖国！”的锦旗一面，全场响起了热烈的掌声。中共青海省委员会第二书记孙作宾在大会上讲话。他说，青藏公路的通车，是全国人民特别是青海、西藏两地人民生活中的大喜事。他表示热烈的祝贺。接着讲话的有青海省人民政府副主席马辅臣（回族），中国人民解放军青海军区副司令员李书茂和各族各界代表阿热仓（藏族）、官保加（蒙族）、松布（土族）、梅亭（撒拉族）、哈木（哈萨克族）等人，他们都热烈庆贺青藏公路的胜利通车，并向筑路部队和职工表示敬意。大会并一致通过了给毛主席的致敬电和给筑路部队、筑路职工的慰问电。
    接着，举行了通车剪彩仪式。中共青海省委员会第二书记孙作宾剪彩后，挂有毛主席巨幅画像的彩车和装载大米、面粉、汽油等物资的三十辆汽车，在锣鼓、鞭炮和欢呼声中缓缓开出西门，在青藏公路上向着拉萨前进。汽车通过街道时，市民夹道欢呼，并向司机抛撒纸花致敬。
