Title: Premier U Nu Visits and Inspects Shenyang, Anshan, Lüda and Other Places; National Women's Federation Holds Tea Party to Welcome Premier U Nu's Wife
Author:
Date: 1954-12-09
Page: 1
[By our correspondent in Dalian, December 8th] The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his entourage arrived in Shenyang from Beijing at 11:00 AM on December 6th. From the 6th to the 8th, Prime Minister U Nu and others, accompanied by Yao Zhongming, our ambassador to Burma, U La Maw, the Burmese ambassador to our country, and Wang Zhuoru, director of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, visited and inspected Shenyang, Anshan, Lüda and other places.

    In Shenyang, Prime Minister U Nu visited the Northeast Institute of Technology, the Shenyang Pneumatic Tool Factory, and the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall. Prime Minister U Nu visited the Architecture Library's reference room, the refractory materials laboratory, and several laboratories of the Metallurgy Library, as well as the mechanical practice factory at the Northeast Institute of Technology. When Prime Minister U Nu walked across the large playground of the Northeast Institute of Technology, he was warmly welcomed by thousands of students who were doing their between-class health exercises. Prime Minister U Nu spoke to the thousands of students on the steps, encouraging them to study hard and prepare to participate in the construction of New China. At the Pneumatic Tool Factory, Prime Minister U Nu visited the main workshops and the finished product packaging department. At the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall, Prime Minister U Nu visited more than sixty types of machine tools made by various factories in the Northeast, as well as various light and heavy industrial products. Prime Minister U Nu also left a message in the exhibition hall's guest book, praising New China's achievements in industry and expressing excitement for the great strides forward in New China's construction.

    In Anshan, Prime Minister U Nu visited the No. 7 automated iron smelting furnace, the large steel rolling mill, and the seamless steel tube plant of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, and toured the "February 19th" Park and the urban area.

    In Lüda, Prime Minister U Nu stood on the rooftop platform of the Dalian Port Authority Building and viewed the panorama of the seaport. Prime Minister U Nu also visited the Sino-Soviet Shipbuilding Company, Dalian No. 2 Glass Factory, Dalian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Manufacturing Factory, Crane Factory, Lüda Department Store, and the Northeast Resources Hall. Prime Minister U Nu met with the leaders of the company in the lounge of the Sino-Soviet Shipbuilding Company. He said that after coming to China, he had personally witnessed the Soviet Union's great assistance to the construction of New China. He wished world peace and the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union would last forever.

    Prime Minister U Nu also visited the first large-scale automobile manufacturing plant in New China, which is under construction in the Northeast.

    Xinhua News Agency, December 8th: The wife of the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and the Prime Minister's private secretary, U Kyin Sein, and others were invited to attend a welcome tea party held by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation on the afternoon of the 8th.

    The hosts who attended the tea party were: Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Standing Committee members Yang Zhihua, Kang Keqing, Cao Mengjun, Lu Cui, Li Baoguang, some executive committee members in Beijing, heads of various departments, and Zhang Xiaomei, Director of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, etc.

    The guests and hosts had a cordial conversation on issues related to women's rights and children's welfare in China and Burma. At the meeting, both sides expressed the common desire of women in the two countries to uphold peace and strengthen friendship. The tea party was held in a friendly atmosphere throughout.

### 吴努总理在沈阳、鞍山、旅大等地参观访问  全国妇联举行茶会欢迎吴努总理夫人

1954-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努总理在沈阳、鞍山、旅大等地参观访问
    全国妇联举行茶会欢迎吴努总理夫人
    【本报旅大八日电】缅甸联邦总理吴努和随行人员，在六日上午十一时由北京到达沈阳。六日到八日，吴努总理等在我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明、缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂和我国外交部交际处处长王倬如陪同下，先后在沈阳、鞍山和旅大等地参观访问。
    在沈阳市，吴努总理参观了东北工学院、沈阳风动工具厂和东北工业陈列馆。吴努总理参观了东北工学院的建筑学馆的资料室、耐火材料试验室和冶金学馆的几个实验室及机械实习工厂。吴努总理走过东北工学院的大操场时，受到正在进行课间劳卫操的数千学生的热烈鼓掌欢迎。吴努总理在台阶上向数千学生讲话，勉励他们努力学习，准备参加新中国的建设。在风动工具厂，吴努总理参观了主要车间和成品包装部。吴努总理在东北工业陈列馆，参观了六十多种东北各厂自制的机床和各种轻重工业的产品。吴努总理并在陈列馆的纪念册上留言，称赞新中国在工业上的成就，为新中国建设事业的大踏步前进而感到兴奋。
    在鞍山市，吴努总理参观了鞍山钢铁公司的第七号自动化炼铁炉、大型轧钢厂和无缝钢管厂，并游览了“二一九”公园和市区。
    在旅大市，吴努总理站在大连港务管理局大楼的屋顶平台上观看了海港全景。吴努总理还参观了中苏造船公司、大连第二玻璃工厂、大连机车车辆制造工厂、起重机器厂、旅大百货公司和东北资源馆。吴努总理在中苏造船公司的休息室里和公司领导人员晤谈。他说，他来中国后已亲眼看到苏联对新中国建设的巨大援助。他并祝世界和平和中苏两国人民之间的友谊万岁。
    吴努总理在东北还参观了正在兴建中的新中国第一座规模宏大的汽车制造厂。
    【新华社八日讯】缅甸联邦总理吴努的夫人和总理私人秘书都庆素等在八日下午应邀参加了中华全国民主妇女联合会的欢迎茶会。
    主人方面出席茶会的有：中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，常务委员杨之华、康克清、曹孟君、陆璀、李宝光和在京部分执行委员、各部门负责人，北京市民主妇女联合会主任张晓梅等。
    宾主双方就中缅两国有关妇女权利和儿童福利等方面问题进行了亲切的交谈。会上，宾主双方表示了两国妇女拥护和平、加强友谊的共同愿望。茶会始终在友好的空气中进行。
