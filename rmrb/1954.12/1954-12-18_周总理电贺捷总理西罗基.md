Title: Premier Zhou Cables Congratulations to Czechoslovak Premier Široký
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1954-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Viliam Široký, congratulating him on his appointment as Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic. The telegram reads:
To Comrade Viliam Široký, Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic:
    I am delighted to learn of your appointment as Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic. Please accept the warm congratulations from the government of the People's Republic of China and myself personally.
    I wish you new achievements in your future work.
                           Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                    December 16, 1954, Beijing

### 周总理电贺捷总理西罗基
周恩来
1954-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺捷总理西罗基
    【新华社讯】中华人民共和国国务院周恩来总理致电西罗基，祝贺他被任命为捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理。电文如下：捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理威·西罗基同志：
    欣悉您被任命为捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理，请接受中华人民共和国政府和我个人的热诚祝贺。
    祝您在今后的工作中获得新的成就。
                           中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                    一九五四年十二月十六日于北京
