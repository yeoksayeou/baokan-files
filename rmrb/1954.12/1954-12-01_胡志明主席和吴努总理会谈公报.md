Title: Communiqué on Talks between Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Prime Minister U Nu
Author:
Date: 1954-12-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 29th. A joint communiqué was issued today following talks in Hanoi between President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Prime Minister U Nu of the Union of Burma:
    “Today, President Ho Chi Minh and Prime Minister U Nu met and discussed various issues of common concern to Vietnam and Burma.
    Both the President and the Prime Minister are concerned with the consolidation of peace in Indochina. Peace in Indochina makes it possible for the countries of Indochina to live independently and achieve prosperity without any external interference, and to contribute to the maintenance of peace in Southeast Asia and the world.
    The President and the Prime Minister recalled the complete unanimity of views between the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the Union of Burma regarding the five principles established in the joint Sino-Indian and Sino-Burmese statements, and believe that the same principles should apply to relations between Vietnam and Burma and between other countries.
    President Ho Chi Minh and Prime Minister U Nu expressed their belief that the friendship between Vietnam and Burma will be strengthened and that relations between the two countries will become closer.”

### 胡志明主席和吴努总理会谈公报

1954-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席和吴努总理会谈公报
    【新华社河内二十九日电】越南民主共和国主席胡志明和缅甸联邦总理吴努二十九日在河内会谈后，发表联合公报如下：
    “今天，胡志明主席和吴努总理会见并讨论了对越南和缅甸共同有关的各项问题。
    主席和总理都关心到巩固印度支那和平的问题。印度支那的和平使得印度支那各国有可能在不受任何外来干涉的情况下独立生活和获致繁荣，并为维护东南亚和世界的和平作出贡献。
    主席和总理回顾了越南民主共和国政府和缅甸联邦政府关于中印和中缅联合声明中确定的五项原则的完全一致的看法，并且认为同样的原则应当应用于越南和缅甸之间的关系以及其他国家之间的关系。
    胡志明主席和吴努总理都表示相信：越南和缅甸的友谊将得到加强，两国之间的关系将会更趋密切。”
