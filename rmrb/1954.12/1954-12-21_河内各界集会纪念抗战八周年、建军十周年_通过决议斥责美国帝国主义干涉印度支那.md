Title: Hanoi Circles Hold Rally to Commemorate the Eighth Anniversary of the War of Resistance and the Tenth Anniversary of the Army's Founding; Adopt Resolution Condemning US Imperialism's Interference in Indochina
Author:
Date: 1954-12-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 20th: People from all walks of life in Hanoi held a rally on the afternoon of the 19th to commemorate the eighth anniversary of the nationwide resistance war in Vietnam and the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Vietnamese People's Army.

　　The conference was convened by the Vietnam National United Front. More than 700 people attended the conference, including ministers and deputy ministers of various ministries of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and representatives of people from all walks of life in Hanoi.

　　At the meeting, Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Propaganda of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, read out President Ho Chi Minh's message to the soldiers and civilians of the whole country on the occasion of the eighth anniversary of the nationwide resistance war in Vietnam on behalf of the government. It was warmly welcomed by all the representatives.

　　Major General Vuong Thua Vu, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Military and Political Committee, spoke at the meeting. He described the victories achieved by the Vietnamese people in the long-term resistance war. He condemned the conspiracies of the US imperialists, the French colonialists and their running dog Ngo Dinh Diem clique to sabotage the Geneva Accords. Vuong Thua Vu finally pointed out that the Vietnamese people's struggle to consolidate peace, achieve unification, and complete national independence and democracy will surely achieve final victory.

　　The conference unanimously passed a resolution condemning US imperialism's interference in Indochina. The resolution protested against the US imperialists' dispatch of Collins to South Vietnam to undermine the peace and unification of Vietnam. The resolution demanded that the Ngo Dinh Diem clique immediately stop coercing and luring North Vietnamese Catholics to move south, release peace advocates arrested in South Vietnam, and respect the freedom and democratic rights of the South Vietnamese people. The resolution demanded that the French Federation forces guarantee to immediately cease all acts violating the armistice agreement.

　　Prior to this, workers, women, youth, students, etc. from various factories in Hanoi held various rallies to oppose the acts of the US imperialists, French colonialists and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique in sabotaging the armistice agreement.

### 河内各界集会纪念抗战八周年、建军十周年  通过决议斥责美国帝国主义干涉印度支那

1954-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

    河内各界集会纪念抗战八周年、建军十周年
    通过决议斥责美国帝国主义干涉印度支那
    【新华社河内二十日电】河内各界人民在十九日下午举行了纪念越南全国抗战八周年和越南人民军建军十周年的大会。
    大会由越南国民联合战线召开。参加大会的有越南民主共和国政府各部部长、副部长和河内各界人民代表等七百多人。
    会上，越南民主共和国政府宣传部部长黄明鉴代表政府宣读了胡志明主席在越南全国抗战八周年纪念日向全国军民发表的文告。受到全体代表的热烈欢迎。
    河内市军政委员会主席王承武少将在会上讲话。他叙述了越南人民在长期抗战中所获得的胜利。他谴责美帝国主义者、法国殖民分子和他们的走狗吴庭艳集团破坏日内瓦协定的阴谋。王承武最后指出，越南人民所进行的巩固和平、实现统一、完成全国独立和民主的斗争一定会取得最后胜利。
    大会一致通过了一项谴责美帝国主义干涉印度支那的决议。决议抗议美帝国主义派遣柯林斯前往南越以破坏越南的和平和统一。决议要求吴庭艳集团立即停止胁迫和诱骗北越天主教徒南迁，释放在南越被捕的和平人士，尊重南越人民的自由民主权利。决议要求法兰西联邦部队保证立即终止一切违反停战协定的行为。
    在此以前，河内各工厂的工人、妇女、青年、学生等曾分别举行各种集会，反对美帝国主义、法国殖民者及吴庭艳集团破坏停战协定的行为。
