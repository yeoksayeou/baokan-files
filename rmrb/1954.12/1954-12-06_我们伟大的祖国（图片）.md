Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-12-06
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In Heping Village, Zhouyang Township, Jiaoxian County, Shandong Province, all 54 peasant households have voluntarily joined the agricultural production cooperative. Now, with the support of the Jiaoxian Agricultural Machinery and Tractor Station, they have used machinery to sow more than 260 mu of winter wheat.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    山东省胶县周杨乡和平庄五十四户农民，已全部自愿参加农业生产合作社。现在他们在胶县农业机器拖拉机站的支援下，已用机器播种了二百六十多亩冬小麦。
