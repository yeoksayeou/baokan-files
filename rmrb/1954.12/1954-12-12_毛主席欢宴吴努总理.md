Title: Chairman Mao Hosts a Banquet for Prime Minister U Nu
Author:
Date: 1954-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 11th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China hosted a banquet at Zhongnanhai on the evening of the 11th in honor of the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his wife.

Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his wife, Justice of the Supreme Court of Burma U Myint Thein, Prime Minister's Secretary and Cabinet Secretary U Win Pe, Prime Minister's Secretary U Than, Army Staff Officer Colonel Aung Gyi, Prime Minister's Aide-de-Camp Captain Ba Swe, Prime Minister's Private Secretary U Tin So, and the Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung were invited to attend the banquet.

Present as hosts were: Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Shaoqi, and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai.

Also present as hosts were: Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, and Committee Member Panchen Erdeni Qoigyijabu.

Also present as hosts were: Vice Premiers of the State Council Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Ministers of various ministries and directors of commissions Xie Juezai, Luo Ruiqing, Shi Liang, Qian Ying, Bo Yibo, Zhang Naiqi, Zeng Shan, Ye Jizhuang, Zhao Erlu, Chen Yu, Li Siguang, Liu Xiufeng, Sha Qianli, Zhang Bojun, Liao Luyan, Liang Xi, Fu Zuoyi, Shen Yanbing, Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Xiruo, and Li Dequan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming.

Also present as hosts were: President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Nie Rongzhen, Zhang Zhizhong, Long Yun, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Su Yu, Deputy Chief of General Staff Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Xiao Hua and Gan Siqi.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 毛主席欢宴吴努总理

1954-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席欢宴吴努总理
    【新华社十一日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东，十一日晚在中南海欢宴缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人。
    缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人、缅甸最高法院法官吴敏登、总理秘书兼内阁秘书吴温佩、总理秘书吴丹、陆军部参谋昂季上校、总理侍从武官毕梭上尉、总理私人秘书都庆素和缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂都应邀出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的有：中华人民共和国副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来。
    出席作陪的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措和委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞。
    出席作陪的还有：国务院副总理彭德怀、邓小平、邓子恢、贺龙、陈毅，秘书长习仲勋，各部部长和委员会主任谢觉哉、罗瑞卿、史良、钱瑛、薄一波、章乃器、曾山、叶季壮、赵尔陆、陈郁、李四光、刘秀峰、沙千里、章伯钧、廖鲁言、梁希、傅作义、沈雁冰、杨秀峰、张奚若、李德全，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权以及中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明。
    出席作陪的还有：最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻、张治中、龙云，中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕、副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任萧华、甘泗淇。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
