Title: China's New Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Cao Ying, Calls on Czechoslovak Deputy Foreign Minister Šimovič

Author:
Date: 1954-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 2nd: Cao Ying, the newly appointed Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Czechoslovakia, accompanied by Li Qingquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy, called on Czechoslovak Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Šimovič on the 1st to discuss matters concerning the presentation of credentials.

### 我国新任驻捷大使曹瑛拜会捷副外长西莫维茨

1954-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    我国新任驻捷大使曹瑛拜会捷副外长西莫维茨
    【新华社布拉格二日电】中华人民共和国新任驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛偕大使馆临时代办李清泉，在一日拜会了捷克斯洛伐克外交部副部长西莫维茨，商谈呈递国书事宜。
