Title: Vice Chairman Zhu and Chairman Liu Host Prime Minister U Nu
Author:
Date: 1954-12-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: Prime Minister U Nu of the Union of Burma and his wife were invited to a tea party hosted by Vice Chairman Zhu De and his wife, and Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and his wife, on the afternoon of the 3rd.

Mr. U Min Thein, Judge of the Supreme Court of Burma, Mr. U Win Pe, Prime Minister's Secretary and Cabinet Secretary, Mr. U Thant, Prime Minister's Secretary, and Colonel Aung Gyi of the Army Department, who accompanied Prime Minister U Nu on his visit to China, were also invited to attend the tea party.

Also present were Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, the Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, and member Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain.

Also present was Vice Premier of the State Council, Ulanhu.

Also present was President of the Supreme People's Court, Dong Biwu.

The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and his wife, and the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, and his wife were also present.

In the evening, Prime Minister U Nu and his wife attended a theatrical performance.

Accompanying them to watch the performance were Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De, and Premier of the State Council, Zhou Enlai.

Also accompanying them to watch the performance were Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Li Jishen, the Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, and member Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain; Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi and Ulanhu; and President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu.

Also accompanying them to watch the performance were the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming.

[Our Newspaper's Report] Accompanied by Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Prime Minister U Nu of the Union of Burma, his wife, and accompanying personnel continued their sightseeing and visits in Beijing on the morning of the 3rd.

Prime Minister U Nu and others visited the state-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory and were warmly welcomed by the workers. Accompanied by Yang Huijie, the female director of the factory, and Yu Qi, the deputy director, Prime Minister U Nu visited all the workshops of the factory and learned in detail about the entire production process of the factory. Prime Minister U Nu also inquired about the workers' living and welfare conditions. After the visit, Prime Minister U Nu took a group photo with the factory director and deputy director and left a message in the commemorative album, wishing all the workers of the factory happiness and hoping that they would continue to make contributions in the process of building a new China.

At 11:00 a.m., Prime Minister U Nu, his wife, and accompanying personnel visited the Hall of Prayer for Good Harvests, the Echo Wall, the Imperial Vault of Heaven, and the Circular Mound Altar in the Temple of Heaven. (Photo attached)

### 朱副主席刘委员长招待吴努总理

1954-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席刘委员长招待吴努总理
    【新华社三日讯】三日下午，缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人，应邀出席了中华人民共和国副主席朱德和夫人、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇和夫人的茶会。
    随同吴努总理访问我国的缅甸最高法院法官吴敏登、总理秘书兼内阁秘书吴温佩、总理秘书吴丹、陆军部参谋昂季上校等也应邀出席了茶会。
    在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、张澜、沈钧儒、黄炎培、陈叔通、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措和委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞。
    在座的还有国务院副总理乌兰夫。
    在座的还有最高人民法院院长董必武。
    缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂和夫人，中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和夫人也在座。
    晚间，吴努总理和他的夫人等出席了戏剧晚会。
    陪同观剧的，有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，国务院总理周恩来。
    陪同观剧的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措和委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞；国务院副总理陈毅、乌兰夫；最高人民法院院长董必武。
    陪同观剧的，还有缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂和我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明。
    【本报讯】缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人及随行人员，在我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和外交部交际处处长王倬如陪同下，三日上午继续在北京参观访问。
    吴努总理等参观了国营北京第一棉纺织厂，受到了职工的热烈欢迎。吴努总理在该厂女厂长杨慧洁和副厂长于琦陪同下参观了全厂的各个车间，详尽地了解了这个工厂的全部生产过程。吴努总理还询问了工人的生活和福利情况。参观毕，吴努总理同厂长、副厂长合影留念，并在纪念册上留言，祝工厂全体工人身心愉快，希望他们在建设新中国的过程中不断作出贡献。
    上午十一时，吴努总理和他的夫人及随行人员游览了天坛的祈年殿、回音壁、皇穹宇、圜丘等处。（附图片）
