Title: Soviet Foreign Cultural Association Representatives Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Second National Congress of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1954-12-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: At the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Nikolai Alekseevich Vyzhilin, Vice Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, and Nikolai Kirillovich Goncharov, Vice Chairman of the Education Group Committee of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, were specially sent by the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association to attend the Second National Congress of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to be held on the 28th. They arrived in Beijing by plane on the 27th.
    Among those who went to the airport to welcome them were Liao Jingtian, Ge Baoquan, and Yan Baohang, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Yang Hansheng and Cao Mengjun, members of the Council, as well as heads of local Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations who came to Beijing to attend the meeting.
    Vasikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Qi Gangning, Representative of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 参加中苏友协第二次全国代表会  苏联对外文化协会代表到京

1954-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    参加中苏友协第二次全国代表会
    苏联对外文化协会代表到京
    【新华社二十七日讯】应中苏友好协会总会邀请，苏联对外文化协会特派苏联对外文化协会理事会副主席尼古拉·阿列克谢耶维奇·维兹日林和苏联对外文化协会教育小组委员会副主席尼古拉·基里洛维奇·冈查罗夫前来参加将在二十八日举行的中苏友好协会第二次全国代表会。他们已在二十七日乘飞机抵达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中苏友好协会总会副总干事廖经天、戈宝权、阎宝航，理事阳翰笙、曹孟君和来京出席会议的各地中苏友好协会负责人。
    前往机场欢迎的还有苏联驻华大使馆参赞华司考和苏联对外文化协会驻华代表齐刚宁。
