Title: PLA on the Fujian Front Repulses a Band of Chiang Kai-shek Troops Landing to Harass
Author:
Date: 1954-12-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 26th—The Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front repulsed a band of Chiang Kai-shek troops landing to harass. At 02:20 on the 23rd, twenty-four Chiang Kai-shek bandits from Jinmen landed to harass the area near the lighthouse and Wu Gantou on Weitou Peninsula, Jinjiang County. They were immediately met with a head-on counterattack from a coastal defense unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and local militia. The bandits immediately fled back to the sea in a panic. We captured one Chiang Kai-shek bandit.

### 福建前线解放军  击退登陆骚扰的一股蒋贼军

1954-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线解放军
    击退登陆骚扰的一股蒋贼军
    【新华社福建前线二十六日电】福建前线中国人民解放军击退登陆骚扰的一股蒋贼军。二十三日二时二十分，蒋贼金门海匪二十四人，在晋江县围头半岛灯塔和五干头两地登陆骚扰，当即遭到我中国人民解放军某海防部队和当地民兵迎头反击，海匪当即狼狈逃下海去，我俘掳蒋贼海匪一名。
