Title: Corn Thresher Suitable for Mountainous Areas

Author:

Date: 1954-12-19
Page: 2

The staff of the Ankan Special District Farm Implement Factory in Shaanxi Province successfully modified a corn thresher suitable for use in the mountainous areas of southern Shaanxi. This machine can thresh both large and small corn cobs cleanly and efficiently.

### 适合山区使用的玉米脱粒机

1954-12-19
第2版()
专栏：

    适合山区使用的玉米脱粒机
    陕西省安康专区农具厂职工，改制适合陕南山区使用的玉米脱粒机成功。用这种机器，大小棒玉米都能脱粒干净，并且效率高。
