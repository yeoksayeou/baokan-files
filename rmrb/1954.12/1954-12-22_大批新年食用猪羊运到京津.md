Title: Large Quantities of Pigs and Sheep for New Year Consumption Shipped to Beijing and Tianjin
Author: Zhang Zu
Date: 1954-12-22
Page: 2

    Large Quantities of Pigs and Sheep for New Year Consumption Shipped to Beijing and Tianjin

    To ensure that city residents can enjoy fresh meat during the New Year, the Zhangjiakou branch of the China Food Company in Hebei Province has shipped out large quantities of fattened pigs and mutton sheep. As of December 11th, a total of more than 3,400 fattened pigs have been shipped to Beijing and Tianjin, and more than 1,800 mutton sheep have been shipped to Tianjin, Tangshan, and other places. In addition, more than 32,000 jin of eggs have been shipped to Beijing. (Zhang Zu)

### 大批新年食用猪羊运到京津
张组
1954-12-22
第2版()
专栏：

    大批新年食用猪羊运到京津
    为使城市人民在新年时吃到鲜美的肉食，中国食品公司河北省张家口分公司已运出大批肥猪、菜羊。到十二月十一日止，运往北京、天津两市的肥猪共三千四百多只，运往天津、唐山等地的菜羊共一千八百多只。此外，并有三万二千多斤鸡蛋运往北京。　（张组）
