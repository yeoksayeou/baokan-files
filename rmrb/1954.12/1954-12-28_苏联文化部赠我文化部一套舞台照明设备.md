Title: The Soviet Ministry of Culture Presents a Set of Stage Lighting Equipment to Our Ministry of Culture
Author:
Date: 1954-12-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, accepted a set of modern electrical lighting equipment for the stage presented by the Soviet Ministry of Culture on behalf of the Ministry of Culture on the 25th. Poliyakov, Director of the Soviet State Moscow Musical Theater, presented the gift on behalf of the Soviet Ministry of Culture. At the presentation ceremony, Zhang Zhixiang, on behalf of the Ministry of Culture and Chinese literary and art workers, expressed heartfelt thanks to the Soviet Ministry of Culture for its enthusiastic help.

This modern lighting equipment was specially prepared by the Soviet Ministry of Culture for the Soviet State Moscow Musical Theater's performance in China. The complete set of equipment includes 637 items, including automatically controlled transformers.

### 苏联文化部赠我文化部一套舞台照明设备

1954-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联文化部赠我文化部一套舞台照明设备
    【新华社二十五日讯】二十五日，中华人民共和国文化部副部长张致祥代表文化部接受了苏联文化部赠送的一套舞台用现代化电气照明设备。苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院院长波利雅科夫代表苏联文化部赠礼。在赠礼仪式上，张致祥代表文化部及我国文艺工作者对苏联文化部的热情帮助表示衷心的感谢。
    这套现代化的照明设备是苏联文化部专为苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院来我国演出而备置的。全套设备包括能自动控制的变电器在内，共六百三十七件。
