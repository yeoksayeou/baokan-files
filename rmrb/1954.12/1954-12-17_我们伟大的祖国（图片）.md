Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-12-17
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Jining-Erlian railway line was completed fourteen days ahead of schedule and reached Erlian. The first direct train from Jining to Erlian arrived at the Erlian station on the Sino-Mongolian border on December 12th. This is a scene of workers warmly welcoming the arrival of the train.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    集（宁）二（连）线已经提前十四天铺轨到二连。由集宁到二连第一趟直达列车在十二月十二日到达中蒙边境的二连车站。这是工人们在热烈欢迎列车到达的情形。
