Title: Chiang Bandit Planes Intruded and Harassed Toumenshan Island
Author:
Date: 1954-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang Front, 16th -- Chiang bandit planes intruded and harassed coastal islands of Zhejiang. At 23:15 on the 14th, one Chiang bandit PB-4Y aircraft intruded into the airspace over Toumenshan Island and dropped nine bombs. Our army suffered no losses.

### 蒋贼飞机窜扰头门山岛

1954-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋贼飞机窜扰头门山岛
    【新华社浙江前线十六日电】蒋贼飞机窜扰浙江沿海岛屿。十四日二十三时十五分，蒋贼PB—四Y型飞机一架，窜入头门山岛上空，投弹九枚，我军没受任何损失。
