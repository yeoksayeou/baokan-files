Title: Chinese PLA Song and Dance Troupe Holds Grand Performance in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-12-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 14th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe held a grand performance at the Bolshoi Theatre in Moscow on the 13th.

Soviet Communist Party and government leaders Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Shvernik, Suslov, Pospelov, and Shatalin attended and watched the performance.

Also present were ministers of various Soviet government departments, workers from Moscow enterprises, workers in science, culture, and the arts, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from many countries stationed in the Soviet Union.

Zhang Wentian, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, also attended the performance.

The hall of the Bolshoi Theatre was filled with spectators, and hundreds of thousands more listened to the song and dance troupe's performances via radio.

Before the performance began, the national anthems of China and the Soviet Union were played on stage. A delegation of ten people, including the famous Soviet actor B.A. Alexandrov, welcomed the song and dance troupe. Anisimov, the manager of the Bolshoi Theatre, delivered a speech expressing a warm welcome and congratulations to the song and dance troupe.

Chen Yi, the head of the song and dance troupe, then delivered a reply. He expressed his gratitude for the friendly reception the song and dance troupe had received in the Soviet Union, and expressed confidence that the mutual visits of song and dance troupes from China and the Soviet Union would further develop cultural exchanges between the two countries and play a certain role in the development of friendship between the two peoples.

At the start of the performance, members of the song and dance troupe sang songs such as "The Party is Our Helmsman," "On the Grassland Rises the Red Sun," "Who Knows Him," "Long Live Mao Zedong," and "Hello Moscow" in Russian and Chinese. They also performed programs such as the "Tea Picking and Butterfly Catching Dance," "Marching and Resting Dance," and the Peking Opera "Havoc in the Dragon Palace." The song and dance troupe's performances were warmly welcomed by the audience. Many programs were repeated at the audience's request.

After the performance, representatives of the Soviet art community presented flowers to the song and dance troupe, expressing their gratitude and respect.

### 我解放军歌舞团在莫斯科举行隆重演出

1954-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我解放军歌舞团在莫斯科举行隆重演出
    【新华社莫斯科十四日电】中国人民解放军歌舞团十三日在莫斯科大剧院举行了隆重演出。
    苏联共产党和政府领导人布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、什维尔尼克、苏斯洛夫、波斯伯洛夫、夏塔林出席观看这次演出。
    到场观看的还有苏联政府各部部长，莫斯科企业的工人，科学和文化艺术工作者，以及许多国家驻苏联的外交使节和官员。
    中国驻苏联大使张闻天也出席观看演出。
    大剧院的大厅坐满了观众，另外还有几十万人用无线电收听歌舞团的演唱节目。
    演出开始前，舞台上奏起中苏两国国歌。苏联著名演员鲍·安·亚历山德罗夫等十个人组成了一个代表团向歌舞团表示欢迎。大剧院经理阿尼西莫夫致词，对歌舞团表示了热烈的欢迎和祝贺。
    歌舞团团长陈沂接着致答词。他对歌舞团在苏联受到友好的招待表示感谢，并且表示信心：中苏两国部队歌舞团的相互访问将会使中苏两国的文化交流更加发展起来，对两国人民的友谊发展将起着一定的作用。
    演出开始，歌舞团人员用俄语和华语演唱了“党是我们的舵手”、“草原上升起不落的太阳”、“有谁知道他”、“万岁毛泽东”、“莫斯科你好”等歌曲，表演了“采茶扑蝶舞”、“行军休息舞”和京剧“闹龙宫”等节目。歌舞团的节目受到了观众的热烈欢迎。许多节目在观众要求下重演。
    演出结束后，苏联艺术界代表向歌舞团献花，表示感谢和敬意。
