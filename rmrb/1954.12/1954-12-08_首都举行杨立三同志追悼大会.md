Title: Memorial Service for Comrade Yang Lisan Held in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1954-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 7th: A memorial service for Comrade Yang Lisan, former Minister of the Financial Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, was held at 10:00 AM today. The remains of Comrade Yang Lisan were buried at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery this afternoon.

The memorial service was presided over by Zhou Enlai, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China. Those who accompanied the mourning included Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, and Minister of National Defense; He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Premiers of the State Council and Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council; Nie Rongzhen and Ye Jianying, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council; Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Wu Yuzhang and Xu Teli, members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Zhang Bojun, Minister of Communications of the People's Republic of China; Zhang Naiqi, Minister of Food; and Shao Lizi, member of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

After the memorial service began, the entire assembly observed three minutes of silence. Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, read the memorial oration. Premier Zhou Enlai reported on Comrade Yang Lisan's life and deeds at the meeting. Premier Zhou said: "The passing of Comrade Yang Lisan is a loss for us of an excellent Communist Party member, and for the Chinese People's Liberation Army of a loyal leader of rear service work and financial work." Premier Zhou pointed out:
	“After joining the army, Comrade Yang Lisan always held positions in our army's logistics and financial work. During the period of the Kuomintang traitorous group's cruel 'encirclement and suppression,' during the arduous 25,000-li Long March, and during the War of Resistance Against Japan and the War of Liberation, Comrade Yang Lisan made outstanding contributions to our army's logistics work in extremely difficult environments. After the national victory, under the collective leadership of the General Logistics Department Party Committee, Comrade Yang Lisan further advanced our army's logistics and financial work."

Premier Zhou then said: "Since joining the army in 1927, Comrade Yang Lisan has always been content with his work. Under all circumstances, he could subordinate his personal interests to the needs of the Party and the revolutionary cause, and happily accept any task assigned to him by the Party. He selflessly dedicated everything he had to the completion of these tasks and maintained the excellent qualities of hard work, simplicity, and integrity."

Premier Zhou Enlai said: "Comrade Yang Lisan passed away due to long-term arduous work and accumulated overwork. All our officers must learn from Comrade Yang Lisan's spirit and style of loyalty to the revolutionary cause, disregard for fame and position, dedication to hard work, care for comrades, and concern for comrades, and strive to resolutely liberate Taiwan and build a modern revolutionary army!"

The procession for encoffining began at 11:00 AM. Those who assisted with carrying the coffin and attended the funeral included members and alternate members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, heads of various ministries and commissions of the People's Republic of China, heads of various departments of the leading organs of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various special forces, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations: Zhou Enlai, Peng Dehuai, He Long, Chen Yi, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Wu Yuzhang, Xu Teli, Shao Lizi, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Naiqi, Su Yu, Xie Juezai, Bo Yibo, Teng Daiyuan, Luo Ruiqing, Tan Zheng, Xiao Hua, Gan Siqi, Song Renqiong, Chen Geng, Lai Chuanzhu, Xu Liqing, Wan Yi, Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Xiruo, Zhao Erlu, Deng Hua, Yang Yong, Song Yuhe, Rong Zihe, Lei Renmin, Gao Chongmin, Wang Shaoyu, Xu Guangping, Xu Deheng, Zhou Chunquan, Zhang Lingbin, Tang Tianji, Tang Ping, Yang Shangkun, Xue Zizheng, Xiao Xiangrong, Zhu Zaoguan, Li Da, Yang Chengwu, Huang Yongsheng, Chen Qihan, Zhang Zhen, Su Jing, Liu Zhijian, Wang Zonghuai, Li Tianhuan, Xu Guangda, Qiu Chuangcheng, Chen Xilian, Luo Shunchu, Wang Hongkun, Li Bozhao, Mo Yichang, etc., totaling more than 300 people. Following the coffin was Comrade Yang Lisan's widow, Li Qin, supporting the coffin.

When Comrade Yang Lisan's coffin was buried, all those attending the funeral circled the tomb once, bidding farewell to Comrade Yang Lisan, an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China and a distinguished soldier of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, with feelings of infinite sorrow.

### 首都举行杨立三同志追悼大会

1954-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    首都举行杨立三同志追悼大会
    【新华社七日讯】前中国人民解放军财务部部长杨立三同志追悼大会，在七日上午十时举行。杨立三同志遗体已于当日下午在八宝山革命烈士公墓安葬。
    追悼大会由中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记、中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来主祭，国务院副总理、国防委员会副主席、国防部部长彭德怀，国务院副总理、国防委员会副主席贺龙、陈毅，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻、叶剑英，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真、陈叔通，中国共产党中央委员会委员吴玉章、徐特立，中华人民共和国交通部部长章伯钧，粮食部部长章乃器，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会委员邵力子等陪祭。
    追悼大会开始后，全场默哀三分钟。中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇朗读祭文。周恩来总理在会上报告了杨立三同志生平事迹。周总理说：“杨立三同志的逝世，使我们失去了一位优秀的共产党员，使中国人民解放军失去了一位忠诚的后方勤务工作和财务工作的领导干部。”周总理指出：
  “杨立三同志参军以后，一直担负我军后勤工作和财务工作职务。在反对国民党卖国集团残酷‘围剿’时期，在艰苦的两万五千里长征时期，在抗日战争和解放战争时期，杨立三同志在极端困难的环境中，对我军的后勤工作，作出卓越贡献。全国胜利后，杨立三同志在总后勤党委集体领导下，使我军的后勤工作和财务工作又向前迈进了一步。”
    周总理接着说：“杨立三同志从一九二七年参军起，就一贯安心于自己的工作。在各种情况下，都能把个人利益服从党的需要和革命事业的需要，愉快地接受党所分配给他的任何任务。为完成这些任务而忘我地贡献出自己的一切，并保持了艰苦朴素和廉洁奉公的优良品质。”
    周恩来总理说：“杨立三同志因长期的艰苦工作，积劳成疾，与我们永别了。我军所有干部，都要学习杨立三同志忠于革命事业和不计名位、埋头苦干、爱护同志、关心同志的精神和作风，为坚决解放台湾而奋斗，为建设现代化的革命军队而奋斗！”
    十一时起灵，执绋和送葬的有中国共产党中央委员会委员和候补中央委员，中华人民共和国各部、委负责人，中国人民解放军领导机关各部门、各特种兵首长和各民主党派、各人民团体负责人周恩来、彭德怀、贺龙、陈毅、彭真、陈叔通、聂荣臻、叶剑英、吴玉章、徐特立、邵力子、章伯钧、章乃器、粟裕、谢觉哉、薄一波、滕代远、罗瑞卿、谭政、萧华、甘泗淇、宋任穷、陈赓、赖传珠、徐立清、万毅、杨秀峰、张奚若、赵尔陆、邓华、杨勇、宋裕和、戎子和、雷任民、高崇民、王绍鏊、许广平、许德珩、周纯全、张令彬、唐天际、汤平、杨尚昆、薛子正、萧向荣、朱早观、李达、杨成武、黄永胜、陈奇涵、张震、苏静、刘志坚、王宗槐、李天焕、许光达、邱创成、陈锡联、罗舜初、王宏坤、李伯钊、莫艺昌等共三百余人。灵后有杨立三同志遗属李琴扶榇。
    在杨立三同志灵柩安葬时，所有送葬的人都绕墓一周，他们怀着无限悲痛的心情，最后送别了中国共产党的优秀党员、中国人民解放军的杰出战士杨立三同志。
