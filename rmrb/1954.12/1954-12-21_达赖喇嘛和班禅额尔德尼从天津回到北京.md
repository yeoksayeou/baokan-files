Title: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Return to Beijing from Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1954-12-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:

    The Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, and the Standing Committee Member, Panchen Erdeni Chokyi Gyaltsen, accompanied by Liu Geping, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, have completed their visit to Tianjin and returned to Beijing by special train at noon on the 19th. Tibetan monks and lay officials who accompanied them on the visit, as well as family members of the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, also returned to Beijing on the same train.

    Those who welcomed them at the station included: Wang Feng, Sakongliao, Yang Jingren, and other members in Beijing from the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China; Xirao Gyatso, Acting President of the Chinese Buddhist Association; Ju Zan, Deputy Secretary-General; officials from the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing and the Panchen Erdeni's Office in Beijing; and Tibetan students from the Central Institute for Nationalities.

    Zhang Jingwu, Representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet, and Fan Ming and Wang Qimei, Deputy Political Commissars of the Tibet Military Region, also welcomed them at the station.

    After arriving in Tianjin on the 13th, the Dalai Lama, Panchen Erdeni, and their entourage visited the Tianjin Paper Mill, the Tianjin Printing and Dyeing Factory, the Tianjin Woolen Mill, the Tianjin Bicycle Factory, Tianjin University, Tanggu New Port, and the city of Tianjin; they also attended a welcome rally held by people from all walks of life in Tianjin.

    When the Dalai Lama, Panchen Erdeni, and their entourage left Tianjin on the morning of the 19th, Huang Huoqing, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chairman of the Tianjin Municipal Consultative Committee, and Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the CPC; Wu De, Mayor of Tianjin; Vice Mayors Bai Jian, Zhou Shutao, Li Gengtao, Wan Xiaotang; and more than 300 representatives from various organizations and groups went to the station to see them off.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼从天津回到北京

1954-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼从天津回到北京
    【新华社讯】全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措，常务委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，由中共中央统一战线工作部副部长刘格平陪同，已在天津参观完毕，十九日中午乘专车返抵北京。随同他们前往参观的西藏僧俗官员和达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼的家属等也同车返京。
    到车站欢迎的有：中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、萨空了、杨静仁和在京委员，中国佛教协会代理会长喜饶嘉错、副秘书长巨赞，西藏达赖驻京办事处、班禅驻京办事处官员和中央民族学院藏族学员等。
    中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武，西藏军区副政治委员范明、王其梅也到车站欢迎。
    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼等一行十三日到天津后，曾参观了天津造纸厂、天津印染厂、天津毛织厂、天津自行车厂、天津大学、塘沽新港和天津市容；出席了天津各界人民的欢迎会。
    达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼等在十九日上午离开天津时，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、天津市协商委员会主席、中共天津市委员会书记黄火青，天津市市长吴德，副市长白坚、周叔弢、李耕涛、万晓塘和各机关团体代表三百多人到车站欢送。
