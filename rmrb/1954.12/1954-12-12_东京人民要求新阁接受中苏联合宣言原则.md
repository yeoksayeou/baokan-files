Title: Tokyo People Demand New Cabinet Accept Principles of Sino-Soviet Joint Declaration
Author:
Date: 1954-12-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 11th—Tokyo dispatch: A mass rally was held in Tokyo on the afternoon of the 10th. The rally adopted a resolution demanding that the newly formed Hatoyama Cabinet accept the various principles put forward in the joint declaration of the Chinese and Soviet governments regarding relations with Japan. The resolution pointed out that these principles are the only way to maintain peace.

### 东京人民要求新阁接受中苏联合宣言原则

1954-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    东京人民要求新阁接受中苏联合宣言原则
   【新华社十一日讯】东京消息：十日下午，东京举行了群众大会。大会通过决议要求新组成的鸠山内阁接受中、苏两国政府关于对日本关系的联合宣言中所提出的各项原则。决议指出：这些原则是保持和平的唯一途径。
