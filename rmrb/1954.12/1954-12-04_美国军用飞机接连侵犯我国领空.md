Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Repeatedly Invade China's Airspace
Author:
Date: 1954-12-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: On December 2nd, U.S. military aircraft in three groups, totaling nine planes, successively intruded into the airspace over Guangdong and Guangxi provinces.

　　From 8:36 to 10:20 on the 2nd, a group of four U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yangjiang, Dianbai, Maoming, and Chixi in Guangdong Province, and Tengxian and Zhaoping in Guangxi Province.

　　From 8:45 to 10:20 on the same day, a group of four U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Dianbai, Wuchuan, Lianjiang, and Chixi in Guangdong Province.

　　From 9:08 to 10:12 on the same day, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace near Yangjiang and over the sea near Yangjiang in Guangdong Province.

　　The local people and the People's Liberation Army have expressed extreme indignation at the provocative actions of U.S. military aircraft in repeatedly invading China's airspace and are closely watching the development of this situation.

### 美国军用飞机接连侵犯我国领空

1954-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机接连侵犯我国领空
    【新华社三日讯】十二月二日美国军用飞机三批九架连续侵入我国广东省和广西省上空。
    二日八时三十六分到十时二十分，美国军用飞机一批四架，侵入我国广东省阳江、电白、茂名、赤溪，广西省藤县、昭平等地上空。
    同日八时四十五分到十时二十分，美国军用飞机一批四架，侵入我国广东省电白、吴川、廉江、赤溪等地上空。
    同日九时零八分到十时十二分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我国广东省阳江附近和阳江附近海面上空。
    当地人民和人民解放军对美国军用飞机再次侵犯我国领空的挑衅行动表示极大的愤慨，并正密切注视着这一形势的发展。
