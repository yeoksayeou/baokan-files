Title: Comrade Yang Lisan, Minister of Finance of the People's Liberation Army, Remains Returned to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-12-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: The coffin of Comrade Yang Lisan, Minister of Finance of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, escorted by Shi Xia, Deputy Military Attaché of our Embassy in the Soviet Union, arrived in Beijing by special plane at 4:00 PM on the afternoon of the 3rd.

Those who went to the airport to meet the remains included Peng Dehuai, He Long, Chen Yi, Deng Xiaoping, Nie Rongzhen, Su Yu, Huang Kecheng, Zhang Zongxun, Chen Geng, Wang Zhen, Tan Zheng, Xiao Hua, Gan Siqi, Xiao Jingguang, Liu Yalou, Song Renqiong, Lai Chuanzhu, Xu Liqing, Rong Zihe, Zhou Chunquan, Zhang Lingbin, Tang Tianji, Li Jukui, Zhu Liangcai, Luo Shunchu, Wang Hongkun, Qiu Chuangcheng, Su Jin, relatives and close friends of Comrade Yang Lisan, and representatives from various departments of the PLA leadership organs, various special forces, and the North China Military Region.

A ceremony was held to place the remains in the coffin. All those who met the remains and the family members of Comrade Yang Lisan were present to oversee the encoffining.

### 解放军财务部长杨立三同志遗体运回北京

1954-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军财务部长杨立三同志遗体运回北京
    【新华社三日讯】中国人民解放军财务部部长杨立三同志的灵柩在我国驻苏联大使馆副武官石侠护送下用专机在三日下午四时运抵北京。
    前往机场迎灵的有彭德怀、贺龙、陈毅、邓小平、聂荣臻、粟裕、黄克诚、张宗逊、陈赓、王震、谭政、萧华、甘泗淇、萧劲光、刘亚楼、宋任穷、赖传珠、徐立清、戎子和、周纯全、张令彬、唐天际、李聚奎、朱良才、罗舜初、王宏坤、邱创成、苏进和杨立三同志的亲属与挚友以及人民解放军领导机关各部门、各特种兵、华北军区等单位代表。
    举行入殓仪式。全体迎灵人员及杨立三同志家属均亲视含殓。
