Title: The Second Session of the National Committee of the CPPCC Closes: Mao Zedong Elected Honorary Chairman, Zhou Enlai Elected Chairman, Soong Ching Ling and Sixteen Others Elected Vice Chairmen
Author:
Date: 1954-12-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 25th—

    The First Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference has concluded victoriously on the 25th.
    Today's meeting elected Mao Zedong as Honorary Chairman of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. Zhou Enlai was elected Chairman of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Confernce, Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, He Xiang Ning, Li Weihan, Li Siguang, Chen Shutong, Zhang Bojun, Chen Jiageng, Panchen Erdeni Qoigy Gyaltsen, and Burhan were elected Vice Chairmen, and Xing Xiping was elected Secretary-General; the meeting also elected 65 members of the Standing Committee of the Second National Commitee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (list to be issued separately).
    Today's meeting was held in the morning and afternoon. The afternoon meeting began at 3 p.m. A total of 489 members attended the meeting.
    The meeting first unanimously adopted the resolution on the work report of the First National Committee (full text to be issued separately) and the Constitution of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (full text to be issued separately).
    Then, based on the nomination by Comrade Soong Ching Ling on behalf of the Presidium, the meeting unanimously elected Mao Zedong as Honorary Chairman of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. At this moment, the entire venue rose to its feet, with enthusiastic applause and cheers lasting for a long time.
    The meeting elected the Chairman, Vice Chairmen, Secretary-General, and Standing Committee members of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference by secret ballot.
    After the voting, the meeting was temporarily adjourned for the ballot counters to count the votes.
    At 8 p.m., the meeting resumed and announced the election results. At this moment, everyone applauded enthusiastically for several minutes.
    After announcing the election results, the meeting unanimously adopted the Manifesto of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (full text to be issued separately), the message of condolence to the Chinese People's Liberation Army (full text to be issued separately), the message of condolence to the Chinese People's Volunteers (full text to be issued separately), and the message of condolence to all officers and men of the People's Liberation Army, engineering personnel, and civilian workers who built the Kangzang and Qingzang Highways (full text to be issued separately).
    With this, the meeting closed in enthusiastic applause.
    Xinhua News Agency, December 25th—The First Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference concluded its general speeches on the morning of the 25th.
    The following people spoke at today's morning meeting: Fu Zuoyi, Wu Han, Wang Baozhen, Cheng Dan'an, Zhang Xiuzhu, Ji Chao Ding, Ye Yutian, Chagangegen, Tao Shuzeng.
    From the 22nd to the 25th, a total of 67 members spoke at the meeting. They all expressed their support for Vice-Chairman Zhou Enlai's political report, agreed with the work report of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference made by Vice-Chairman Chen Shutong, and with Comrade Zhang Bojun's explanation on the "Constitution of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference" (draft). In their speeches, they unanimously expressed their firm determination to oppose the U.S.-Chiang "mutual defense treaty" and to liberate Taiwan and eliminate the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique; they expressed their intention to continue to strengthen unity under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, work together, abide by and implement the Constitution of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and strive for the socialist construction and socialist transformation of the motherland. They also made suggestions on a number of issues related to socialist construction and socialist transformation in their speeches.
    The meeting continued in the afternoon. (Attached pictures)

### 政协二届全国委员会会议闭幕  毛泽东被推举为名誉主席周恩来当选为主席宋庆龄等十六人当选为副主席

1954-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    政协二届全国委员会会议闭幕
    毛泽东被推举为名誉主席周恩来当选为主席宋庆龄等十六人当选为副主席
      【新华社二十五日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议，已在二十五日胜利闭幕。
    今天的会议推举了毛泽东为中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会名誉主席。选举了周恩来为中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会主席，宋庆龄、董必武、李济深、张澜、郭沫若、彭真、沈钧儒、黄炎培、何香凝、李维汉、李四光、陈叔通、章伯钧、陈嘉庚、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞、鲍尔汉十六人为副主席，邢西萍为秘书长；会议还选举了中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会常务委员六十五人（名单另发）。
    今天的会议分上、下午举行。下午的会议三时开始。出席会议的委员共有四百八十九人。
    会议首先一致通过了关于第一届全国委员会工作报告的决议（全文另发）和中国人民政治协商会议章程（全文另发）。
    接着，根据宋庆龄委员代表主席团的提名，会议一致推举毛泽东为中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会名誉主席。这时，全场起立，热烈的掌声和欢呼声经久不息。
    会议以无记名方式投票选举了中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会主席、副主席、秘书长和常务委员。
    投票完毕后，会议随即暂时休会，由检票人进行检票。
    下午八时，会议复会，宣布选举结果。这时，全体热烈鼓掌达数分钟之久。
    在宣布选举结果以后，会议一致通过了中国人民政治协商会议宣言（全文另发），通过了致中国人民解放军慰问电（全文另发）、致中国人民志愿军慰问电（全文另发）和致修筑康藏、青藏公路的全体人民解放军、工程人员和民工同志们的慰问电
  （全文另发）。
    至此，会议在热烈的掌声中闭会。
    【新华社二十五日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议在二十五日上午结束了大会发言。
    在今天上午会议上发言的共有：傅作义、吴晗、王葆真、承澹酓、张修竹、冀朝鼎、叶雨田、查干葛根、陶述曾。
    从二十二日起到二十五日止，在会议上发言的共有六十七位委员。他们都表示拥护周恩来副主席的政治报告，同意陈叔通副主席所作的中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会工作报告和章伯钧委员关于“中国人民政治协商会议章程”（草案）的说明。他们在发言中一致表示了反对美蒋“共同防御条约”，一定要解放台湾和消灭蒋介石卖国集团的坚决意志；表示要继续在中国共产党领导下，加强团结，共同努力，遵守和实行中国人民政治协商会议章程，为祖国的社会主义建设和社会主义改造事业而奋斗。他们在发言中还就有关社会主义建设和社会主义改造中的若干问题提出了建议。
    会议在下午继续举行。（附图片）
