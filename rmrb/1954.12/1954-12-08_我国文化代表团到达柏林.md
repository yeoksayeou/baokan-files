Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1954-12-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: The Chinese cultural delegation, which is going to the German Democratic Republic, the Hungarian People's Republic, and the Polish People's Republic to negotiate and sign the 1955 executive plans for cultural cooperation agreements between China and these countries, left Beijing for Germany by plane on the 3rd. The delegation members include Hong Shen (Head of Delegation), Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, and Ai Dayan, Director of the Department of Student Affairs of the Ministry of Higher Education, etc.

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 7th: The Chinese cultural delegation, led by Head of Delegation Hong Shen, arrived in Berlin by plane on the 6th to discuss and sign the 1955 executive plan for the Sino-German cultural cooperation agreement.
    Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Dr. Harrich, State Secretary of Higher Education of the German Democratic Republic, and representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the German Association for Foreign Cultural Relations. Wang Yutian, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 我国文化代表团到达柏林

1954-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文化代表团到达柏林
    【新华社三日讯】赴德意志民主共和国、匈牙利人民共和国和波兰人民共和国谈判并签订我国与各该国文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划的我国文化代表团已于三日乘飞机离京赴德国。代表团组成人员包括对外文化联络局局长洪深（团长）、高等教育部留学生管理司司长艾大炎等。
    【新华社柏林七日电】为商谈和签订中德文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划的中国文化代表团，在团长洪深率领下，已在六日乘飞机到达柏林。
    到机场欢迎的有德意志民主共和国高等教育国务秘书哈里希博士和外交部、德国对外文化联络协会代表多人。我国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆临时代办王雨田也到机场欢迎。
