Title: Chairman Malenkov Receives Archbishop Alexius

Author: 
Date: 1954-12-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 12th: According to TASS, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, G.M. Malenkov, received Alexius, Archbishop of Moscow and All Russia of the Russian Orthodox Church, on the 11th.

### 马林科夫主席接见阿历修斯大主教

1954-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    马林科夫主席接见阿历修斯大主教
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫十一日接见了俄罗斯正教莫斯科和全俄罗斯大主教阿历修斯。
