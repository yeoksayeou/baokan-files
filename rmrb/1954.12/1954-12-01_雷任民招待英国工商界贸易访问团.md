Title: Lei Renmin Hosts British Business and Trade Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1954-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, held a reception on the 30th to entertain all members of the British business and trade delegation visiting China.

    Those attending the reception included: Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China; Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs; Zhan Wu, Deputy General Manager of the Bank of China; Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Cao Chongshu, Acting Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation; and managers of various export corporations.

    Also attending the reception were Mr. D.W.H. Lane, British Chargé d'Affaires in China; Counselor Addis; and personnel from the Chargé d'Affaires office.

### 雷任民招待英国工商界贸易访问团

1954-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    雷任民招待英国工商界贸易访问团
    【新华社三十日讯】中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长雷任民在三十日举行酒会，招待来我国访问的英国工商界贸易访问团全体团员。
    参加酒会的有：中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫，西欧非洲司司长黄华，中国银行副总经理詹武，对外贸易部第三局局长卢绪章，中国进出口公司代经理曹中枢，各出口公司经理等人。
    参加酒会的还有英国驻我国代办杜维廉，参赞艾惕思，及代办处人员。
