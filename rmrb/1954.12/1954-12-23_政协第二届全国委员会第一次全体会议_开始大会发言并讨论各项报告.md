Title: The First Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC Begins General Speeches and Discusses Various Reports
Author:
Date: 1954-12-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: The first plenary session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a general session for speeches on the afternoon of the 22nd.

    The following individuals spoke at today's session: Dong Biwu, Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Li Siguang, Li Dequan, Chen Jingyu, Cheng Qian, Chen Tie, Jiang Lifu, Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, Shi Jinmo, Tang Shengzhi, Chu Tunan, Sheng Pihua, Chen Chonggui, Wang Shuchang, Bai Haifeng, Du Runsheng, and Fang Dingying. The meeting took place from 3:00 PM to 7:00 PM.

    This morning, the members held group discussions on the work report of the First National Committee of the CPPCC delivered by Vice Chairman Chen Shutong, the explanation on the draft of the CPPCC charter given by Member Zhang Bojun, and the political report given by Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai.

    The meeting will continue on the 23rd.

### 政协第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议  开始大会发言并讨论各项报告

1954-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    政协第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议
    开始大会发言并讨论各项报告
    【新华社二十二日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议在二十二日下午进行大会发言。
    在今天大会上发言的共有：董必武、李济深、黄炎培、李四光、李德全、陈经畬、程潜、陈铁、姜立夫、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措、施今墨、唐生智、楚图南、盛丕华、陈崇桂、王树常、白海风、杜润生、方鼎英。会议从下午三时进行到七时。
    今天上午，委员们曾对陈叔通副主席所作的中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会工作报告、章伯钧委员所作的关于中国人民政治协商会议章程草案的说明和周恩来副主席所作的政治报告进行了分组讨论。
    会议将在二十三日继续进行。
