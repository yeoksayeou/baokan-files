Title: Czechoslovak National Assembly and Slovak National Council Elections Concluded; National Front Candidates Receive Enthusiastic Support from Voters
Author:
Date: 1954-12-05
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 2nd: The Czechoslovak Central Election Committee has released a public announcement regarding the results of the National Assembly and Slovak National Council elections held on November 28th.

The public announcement states: 99.18 percent of voters participated in the National Assembly elections, of which 97.89 percent voted for candidates of the National Front. Preliminary results of the Slovak National Council elections are: 99.1 percent of voters participated in the voting, of which 97.2 percent voted for candidates of the National Front.

The public announcement said: The results of the election demonstrate the patriotic attitude of the working people, their unity within the National Front, and their firm determination to build socialism and strive for peace.

### 捷国民议会和斯洛伐克民族议会选举结束  民族阵线候选人得到选民的热烈拥护

1954-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    捷国民议会和斯洛伐克民族议会选举结束
    民族阵线候选人得到选民的热烈拥护
　　【新华社布拉格二日电】捷克斯洛伐克中央选举委员会发表了关于十一月二十八日国民议会和斯洛伐克民族议会选举结果的公报。
    公报指出：参加国民议会选举的选民有百分之九十九点一八，其中有百分之九十七点八九的选民投票选举民族阵线的候选人。斯洛伐克民族议会选举的初步结果是：百分之九十九点一的选民参加了投票，其中百分之九十七点二的选民投了民族阵线候选人的票。
    公报说：选举的结果表明了劳动人民的爱国态度，他们在民族阵线中的团结，以及他们建设社会主义和争取和平的坚定决心。
