Title: Premier Zhou Receives the Head of the Vietnamese Transportation Delegation, Nguyen Van Tran
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 28th—Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received Nguyen Van Tran, the head of the transportation delegation from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on the 24th.
    Present at the meeting were: Lu Zhengcao, the head of the transportation delegation of the People's Republic of China, and Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs and Director of the Asian Department.

### 周总理接见越南交通代表团团长阮文珍

1954-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见越南交通代表团团长阮文珍
    【新华社二十八日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在二十四日接见了越南民主共和国交通代表团团长阮文珍。
    接见时在座的有：中华人民共和国交通代表团团长吕正操，外交部部长助理兼亚洲司司长陈家康。
