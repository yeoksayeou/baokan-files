    Resolution on the Work Report of the First National Committee
    (December 25, 1954)

    The First Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) approved the work report made by Vice Chairman Chen Shutong on behalf of the First National Committee, and expressed satisfaction with the work and significant achievements made by the First National Committee of the CPPCC under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong over the past five years. In the future, the Second National Committee and local committees of the CPPCC should continue to work hard to successfully complete their tasks in accordance with the Constitution of the CPPCC.

### 关于第一届全国委员会工作报告的决议

1954-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    关于第一届全国委员会工作报告的决议
    （一九五四年十二月二十五日）
    中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议同意陈叔通副主席代表第一届全国委员会所作的工作报告，并对于中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下五年来的工作和取得的重大成就，表示满意。今后中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会和地方委员会应当根据中国人民政治协商会议章程，为胜利地完成自己的任务而继续努力。
