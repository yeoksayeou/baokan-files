Title: Prime Minister U Nu Passes Through Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1954-12-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 16th: Burmese Union Prime Minister U Nu and his wife, on their way home after visiting China, arrived in Hanoi by plane at 10:45 AM on the 16th and continued on to Phnom Penh after a stopover of just over an hour.

Accompanying Prime Minister U Nu were: U Myint Thein, Judge of the Supreme Court of the Burmese Union; U Win Pe, Secretary to the Prime Minister and Cabinet Secretary; U Than, Secretary to the Prime Minister; Colonel Aung Gyi, Chief of Staff of the Army Department, and others.

Welcoming them at the airport were: Pham Van Dong, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Propaganda; and other government officials.

Also welcoming them at the airport were: Luo Guibo, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; and A. Lavrishchev, Ambassador of the Soviet Union to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Prime Minister U Nu and his wife, accompanied by Vice Premier Pham Van Dong, Minister Hoang Minh Giam, Ambassador Luo Guibo, Ambassador Lavrishchev, and others, had lunch at Gia Lam Airport. After a short rest after the meal, they left Hanoi by plane for Phnom Penh at 12:00 noon.

### 吴努总理路过河内

1954-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努总理路过河内
    【新华社河内十六日电】缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人在访问中国后回国途中，在十六日上午十时四十五分乘飞机到达河内，并在停留一个多小时后继续飞往金边。
    随同吴努总理前来的有：缅甸联邦最高法院法官吴敏登、总理秘书兼内阁秘书吴温佩、总理秘书吴丹、陆军部参谋昂季上校等。
    到机场欢迎的有：越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同、宣传部长黄明鉴和政府其他官员。
    到机场欢迎的还有：中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波、苏联驻越南民主共和国大使亚·拉夫里谢夫。
    吴努总理和他的夫人在范文同副总理、黄明鉴部长和罗贵波大使、拉夫里谢夫大使等陪同下，在嘉林机场进午餐，餐后略事休息，在十二时整乘飞机离开河内前往金边。
