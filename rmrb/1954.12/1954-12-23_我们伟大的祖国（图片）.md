Our Great Motherland
In order to comprehensively utilize the resources of the Yellow River in terms of flood control, irrigation, power generation, navigation, and soil and water conservation, large-scale surveys are being conducted in the upper reaches of the Yellow River. This is a scene of drillers working hard in the rapids to find rock data for future hydropower stations, irrigation stations and other projects.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为要在防洪、灌溉、发电、航运以及水土保持等方面综合利用黄河的资源，黄河上游正在进行大规模的勘测工作。这是钻工们紧张地在激流中寻找将来建立水电站和灌溉站等工程的岩石资料。
