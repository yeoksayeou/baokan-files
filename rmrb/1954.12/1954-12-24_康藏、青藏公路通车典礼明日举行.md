Title: Kangzang and Qingzang Highways Opening Ceremony to be Held Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1954-12-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, December 23rd: The full opening ceremony for the Kangzang and Qingzang Highways will be held on the 25th in Lhasa, Ya'an, and Xining, respectively. People from all walks of life in Lhasa will also hold a celebration rally on the same day. Major towns such as Xigaze, Gyangze, Heihe, Ali, Changdu, Dingqing, and Zhama will also hold celebrations. Currently, people of all ethnic groups in Lhasa are rushing to prepare for the celebration of the opening of the highways, eagerly awaiting the arrival of this joyous day. Many representatives from various regions who are to participate in the opening ceremony have already arrived in Lhasa, and some are rushing day and night to get here; the motorcade preparing to participate in the opening ceremony is gathering in the suburbs of Lhasa.

### 康藏、青藏公路通车典礼明日举行

1954-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    康藏、青藏公路通车典礼明日举行
    【新华社拉萨二十三日电】康藏、青藏公路的全线通车典礼，将在二十五日分别在拉萨、雅安、西宁等地举行。拉萨市各界人民并在当天举行庆祝大会。日喀则、江孜、黑河、阿里和昌都、丁青、札木等主要城镇，也将举行庆祝活动。目前，拉萨市各族人民正为迎接喜日的到来，赶办庆祝通车的各项准备工作。从各地来参加通车典礼的许多代表，已经到达拉萨，有的正日夜兼程前来；准备参加通车典礼的车队，正在拉萨市郊集结。
