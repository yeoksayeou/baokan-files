Title: Prime Minister U Nu Held Talks with Chinese National Leaders
Author:
Date: 1954-12-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: U Nu, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, who was invited by the Chinese government to visit China, held talks with Chinese national leaders after arriving in Beijing on the 1st.

    He held talks with Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of China, on the mornings of the 2nd, the afternoons of the 3rd, the 5th, and the 10th. He held talks with Deng Zihui, Vice Premier of the State Council of China, on the afternoon of the 4th. He held talks with Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council of China, on the afternoon of the 9th. He held talks with Chen Yun, Vice Premier of the State Council of China, on the morning of the 11th, and with Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Fu Zuoyi, Minister of Water Resources of China, in the afternoon.

### 吴努总理曾分别和我国国家领导人员会谈

1954-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努总理曾分别和我国国家领导人员会谈
    【新华社十二日讯】应中国政府邀请访问我国的缅甸联邦总理吴努，在一日到达北京后，曾先后和我国国家领导工作人员进行了会谈。
    在二日上午、三日下午、五日下午、十日下午同我国国务院总理周恩来进行了会谈。四日下午同我国国务院副总理邓子恢进行了会谈。九日下午同我国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇、我国国务院副总理邓小平进行了会谈。十一日上午同我国国务院副总理陈云进行了会谈，下午同中国科学院院长郭沫若、我国水利部长傅作义进行了会谈。
