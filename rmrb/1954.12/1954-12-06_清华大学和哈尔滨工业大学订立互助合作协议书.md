Title: Tsinghua University and Harbin Institute of Technology Sign Mutual Cooperation Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-06
Page: 3

    To strengthen the connection between schools, promote mutual cooperation, and exchange experiences, Tsinghua University and Harbin Institute of Technology have signed an agreement. The agreement stipulates that both universities should jointly exchange opinions in advance when hiring Soviet experts. After hiring, they can mutually invite experts to guide the teaching and research sections of the same major and solve problems related to the major. The two universities can also mutually send visiting teachers and graduate students to learn from Soviet experts, and when necessary and possible, mutually organize experts from both universities to give lectures. In addition, the agreement also stipulates that the two universities will exchange various teaching materials, such as teaching plans, syllabuses, lecture notes, teaching materials, course designs, graduation designs, and summaries of various teaching experiences. The same departments and teaching and research groups of the two universities can directly establish written contact, frequently exchange teaching experiences, and send representatives to participate in teaching research conferences held by both parties. In terms of scientific research, they should exchange scientific research topics and information on research progress. During the research process, they can provide mutual technical assistance and guidance, and cooperate in research when necessary.

### 清华大学和哈尔滨工业大学订立互助合作协议书

1954-12-06
第3版()
专栏：

    清华大学和哈尔滨工业大学订立互助合作协议书
    为了加强校和校之间的联系，互助合作，交流经验，清华大学和哈尔滨工业大学订立了协议书。协议书中规定，两校在聘请苏联专家时，事先应共同交换意见；聘请后，可互相请专家指导同一专业的教研室，并解决有关专业的问题。两校并可互派进修教师和研究生向苏联专家学习，在必要及可能时，互相组织两校专家讲学。此外，协议书中还规定了两校互相交流各种教学资料。如教学计划、教学大纲、讲义教材、课程设计、毕业设计和各种教学经验总结等。两校相同的系及教研组可以直接建立书面联系，经常交流教学经验，互派代表参加双方举办的教学研究会。在科学研究方面，应互相交换科学研究题目及进行研究的情况；在研究工作进行中，在技术上可互相协助和指导，必要时可合作研究。
