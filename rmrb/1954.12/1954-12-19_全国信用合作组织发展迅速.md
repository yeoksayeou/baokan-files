Title: National Credit Cooperative Organizations Develop Rapidly
Author:
Date: 1954-12-19
Page: 2

National credit cooperative organizations are developing rapidly throughout the country. According to incomplete statistics from the Rural Finance Bureau of the Head Office of the People's Bank of China, there are currently more than 83,000 credit cooperatives nationwide, an increase of nearly tenfold compared to the end of last year. As of the end of September this year, the number of members of credit cooperatives has reached 43 million. Forty-eight counties across the country have achieved the goal of having a credit cooperative in every township.

### 全国信用合作组织发展迅速

1954-12-19
第2版()
专栏：

    全国信用合作组织发展迅速
      信用合作组织正在全国各地迅速发展。据中国人民银行总行农村金融局到目前为止的不完全统计，全国已有信用合作社八万三千多个，比去年年底增加了近十倍。信用合作社社员到今年九月底已达到四千三百万人。全国已有四十八个县达到每个乡都有信用合作社。
