Title: Chairman Liu Sends Congratulations to Chairman Fierlinger of the Czechoslovak National Assembly
Author: Liu Shaoqi
Date: 1954-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:

Chairman Liu Sends Congratulations to Chairman Fierlinger of the Czechoslovak National Assembly

[Xinhua News Agency] Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Mr. Fierlinger, congratulating him on his election as Chairman of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Z. Fierlinger, Chairman of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic:

I am delighted to learn of your election as Chairman of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic. On behalf of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China and myself personally, I extend to you my warmest congratulations.

I wish you new achievements in the cause of seeking the happiness of the Czechoslovak people.

Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi
December 16, 1954, in Beijing

### 刘委员长电贺捷国民议会主席费林格
刘少奇
1954-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    刘委员长电贺捷国民议会主席费林格
    【新华社讯】中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇致电费林格，祝贺他当选为捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席。电文如下：捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席兹·费林格同志：
    欣悉您当选为捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席，我谨以中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会和我个人的名义，向您致热烈的祝贺。
    祝您在谋求捷克斯洛伐克人民的幸福的事业中获得新的成就。
          中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长　刘少奇
                                    一九五四年十二月十六日于北京
