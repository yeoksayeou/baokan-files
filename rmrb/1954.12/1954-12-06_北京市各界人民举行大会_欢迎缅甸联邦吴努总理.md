Title: Beijing People from All Walks of Life Hold a Meeting to Welcome Burmese Federal Prime Minister U Nu
Author:
Date: 1954-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th: People from all walks of life in Beijing held a grand welcome rally on the afternoon of the 5th to welcome Burmese Federal Prime Minister U Nu and his wife, who came to visit at the invitation of the Chinese government. The welcome rally was presided over by Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing.

    The atmosphere of friendship and joy pervaded the welcome rally venue. The national flags of China and Burma were hung on the sky curtain of the rostrum, and the front of the stage was filled with blooming flowers and evergreen palms. Opposite the rostrum hung a huge gold-lettered banner with the words "Long Live Sino-Burmese Friendship" in Chinese and Burmese. The venue was filled with male and female workers, students, staff, and citizens from factories, schools, organizations, groups, and streets who came to attend the welcome meeting.

    At 2:30 p.m., Prime Minister U Nu and his wife, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Mayor Peng Zhen, stepped onto the rostrum. At this moment, the audience stood up and applauded warmly for a long time. Also on the rostrum were Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung and his wife, as well as Burmese Supreme Court Justice U Myint Thein, Prime Minister's Secretary and Cabinet Secretary U Win Pe, Prime Minister's Secretary U Than, and Army Department Staff Officer Colonel Aung Gyi, who accompanied Prime Minister U Nu on his visit to China.

    Also on the rostrum were Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General, Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Liu Ren, Qian Duansheng, Jiang Guangnai, Vice Chairmen of the Beijing Municipal Consultative Committee, and heads of various people's organizations in Beijing.

    As the welcome meeting began, the band played the national anthems of Burma and China.

    Mayor Peng Zhen delivered a welcome speech first (full text to be published separately). On behalf of all the citizens of Beijing, he expressed a warm welcome to Prime Minister U Nu and, through him, extended his heartfelt respect to the citizens of Rangoon and all the people of Burma.

    Prime Minister U Nu then delivered a reply (full text to be published separately). At the end of his speech, the audience applauded warmly to welcome the representative of China's neighbor.

    The welcome meeting ended in warm applause.

    After the welcome meeting, the Central Song and Dance Troupe and the Central Experimental Opera House performed song and dance programs, and famous Peking Opera actor Mei Lanfang and others performed the Peking Opera "The Drunken Concubine." (Attached picture)

### 北京市各界人民举行大会  欢迎缅甸联邦吴努总理

1954-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市各界人民举行大会
    欢迎缅甸联邦吴努总理
    【新华社五日讯】北京市各界人民五日下午举行盛大欢迎会，欢迎应我国政府邀请前来访问的缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人。欢迎大会由北京市市长彭真主持。
    在欢迎大会的会场上，洋溢着友谊和欢乐的气氛。主席台的天幕上挂着中缅两国国旗，台前满布盛开的鲜花和常青的棕榈。在主席台的对面挂着一幅巨大的金字横幅标语，上面用中文和缅文写着“中缅友好万岁”。会场里坐满了从工厂、学校、机关、团体、街道前来参加欢迎会的男女工人、学生、工作人员和市民。
    下午二时三十分，吴努总理和他的夫人由周恩来总理和彭真市长陪同登上主席台。这时，全场起立，热烈的掌声经久不息。随同登上主席台的有缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂和夫人，以及随吴努总理访问我国的缅甸最高法院法官吴敏登，总理秘书兼内阁秘书吴温佩，总理秘书吴丹，陆军部参谋昂季上校。
    在主席台上的，还有中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅，秘书长习仲勋，北京市副市长张友渔，北京市协商委员会副主席刘仁、钱端升、蒋光鼐以及北京市各人民团体的负责人。
    欢迎大会开始，乐队奏起缅甸和中国国歌。
  彭真市长首先致欢迎词（全文另发）。他代表北京市全体市民向吴努总理表示热烈的欢迎，并通过他向仰光的市民和缅甸全体人民表示衷心的敬意。
    吴努总理接着致答词（全文另发）。在他讲话结束时，全场热烈鼓掌，向这位中国邻邦的代表表示欢迎。
    欢迎大会在热烈的掌声中结束。
    在欢迎大会以后，由中央歌舞团和中央实验歌剧院演出了歌舞节目，著名京剧演员梅兰芳等演出了京剧“贵妃醉酒”。（附图片）
