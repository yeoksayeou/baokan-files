Title: U.S. Authorities Intensify Planning for Military Occupation of Taiwan Area
Author:
Date: 1954-12-07
Page: 4

    U.S. Authorities Intensify Planning for Military Occupation of Taiwan Area
    [Xinhua News Agency, 6th] U.S. authorities, after signing the "Mutual Defense Treaty" with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, are now unfolding comprehensive arrangement activities regarding the U.S. military occupation of the Taiwan area. According to the Associated Press, the U.S. will jointly establish a military planning committee with the Chiang bandits in Taipei, which will serve as the "highest military planning, liaison, and intelligence agency." The U.S. is also arranging U.S. military air force and naval bases in Taiwan. U.S. Air Force officers are already surveying Taiwan's airports. The U.S. Fifth Air Force stationed in Japan and the U.S. Thirteenth Air Force stationed in the Philippines will dispatch air force units to Taiwan. At the same time, the U.S. Seventh Fleet's Task Force 72 is studying a plan to use the Tsoying naval base in Taiwan and other naval bases.

### 美国当局加紧策划对台湾地区的军事占领

1954-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

    美国当局加紧策划对台湾地区的军事占领
    【新华社六日讯】　美国当局在同蒋介石卖国集团签订“共同防御条约”以后，正在展开关于美国军事占领台湾地区的全面安排活动。据合众社报道，美国将和蒋贼在台北联合成立一个军事计划委员会，作为“最高的军事计划、联络和情报机构”。美国还在布置美军在台湾的空军和海军基地。美国空军军官已在观测台湾的机场。驻日本的美国第五航空队和驻菲律宾的美国第十三航空队将派遣空军部队到台湾去。同时，美国第七舰队第七十二特遣舰队正在研究一项使用台湾左营海军基地和其他海军基地的计划。
