Title: Indonesian Communist Party Announces List of Party and Non-Party Candidates Participating in Parliamentary Elections
Author:
Date: 1954-12-23
Page: 4

    Indonesian Communist Party
    Announces List of Party and Non-Party Candidates Participating in Parliamentary Elections
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 22] The Indonesian Communist Party has announced the list of Indonesian Communist Party and non-partisan candidates in sixteen electoral districts participating in the upcoming Constituent Assembly and parliamentary general elections. Among the hundreds of names on the list are not only Communist Party leaders, but also well-known professors, artists, educators, athletes, and representatives of workers', women's, and youth organizations.
    An editorial in the Indonesian "People's Daily" on the 21st stated that the list of candidates shows that the Indonesian Communist Party's aim is to organize a people's government representing the interests of all strata of the Indonesian people.

### 印度尼西亚共产党  发表参加国会选举的党和非党候选人名单

1954-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党
    发表参加国会选举的党和非党候选人名单
    【新华社雅加达二十二日电】印度尼西亚共产党发表了参加未来制宪议会和国会大选的十六个选区的印度尼西亚共产党和无党派人士候选人的名单。名单上的几百个名字当中，不仅有共产党领袖，而且还有著名的教授、艺术家、教育家、运动家以及工人、妇女和青年组织的代表。
    印度尼西亚“人民日报”二十一日的社论说，候选人名单表明，印度尼西亚共产党的目的是组织代表印度尼西亚各阶层人民利益的人民政府。
