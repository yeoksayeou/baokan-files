Title: US and Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Negotiate Agreement for US Military Occupation of Taiwan Bases
Author:
Date: 1954-12-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th: Following the signing of the so-called "Mutual Defense Treaty" with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, the US authorities are further plotting with the Chiang bandits to implement the specific measures of this treaty, including an agreement for the US military to use Taiwan bases. Sources reveal that negotiations to formulate the implementation details will be held soon. The sources also revealed that the US authorities plan to send air forces to Taiwan first. According to International News Service reports, the Chiang bandits have prepared air force bases that are "immediately available for use by the US Air Force."

At the same time, the US authorities are also arranging to give the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique more military and economic assistance. According to Agence France-Presse, the US authorities will soon conduct negotiations with the Chiang bandit Foreign Minister Yeh Kung-chao, who is currently in Washington, to conclude an agreement on financial assistance. Sources say that the Director of the US Foreign Operations Administration, Stassen, will arrive in Taiwan soon to discuss the issue of US economic aid with the Chiang bandits. According to Reuters, the US government is also arranging to give the Chiang bandits another batch of warships and jet aircraft.

### 美国和蒋贼谈判美军占领台湾基地的协定

1954-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    美国和蒋贼谈判美军占领台湾基地的协定
   【新华社五日讯】美国当局在同蒋介石卖国集团签订所谓“共同防御条约”以后，正在进一步和蒋贼策划实施这个条约的具体办法，包括美国军队使用台湾基地的协定。消息透露，拟订实施细则的谈判将在最近举行。消息并透露，美国当局计划首先派遣空军到台湾去。据国际新闻社报道，蒋贼已准备了空军基地，可“立即供美国空军使用”。
    同时，美国当局还在安排给予蒋介石卖国集团更多的军事和经济援助。据法新社报道，美国当局将在最近和正在华盛顿的蒋贼外交部长叶公超进行谈判，缔结一项关于财政援助的协定。有消息说，美国援外事务管理署署长史塔生将在最近赶到台湾去，和蒋贼讨论美国经济援助的问题。据路透社报道，美国政府也在安排给蒋贼另一批军舰和喷气飞机。
