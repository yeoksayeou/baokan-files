Title: Representatives from All Walks of Life Pay Respects to Comrade Yang Lisan
Author:
Date: 1954-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 6th: The hall for paying respects to the late Comrade Yang Lisan, former Minister of the Finance Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, was opened at 8:00 AM on the 5th. Wreaths from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Shaoqi, and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai were placed before Comrade Yang Lisan's remains.

From the 5th to 5:00 PM on the 6th, the following Central Committee members and alternate Central Committee members of the Communist Party of China came to pay their respects: Liu Shaoqi, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Wu Yuzhang, Teng Daiyuan, Zhang Jichun, Cheng Zihua. Heads of various democratic parties, people's organizations, and departments: Huang Yanpei, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Zhizhong, Shi Liang, Ye Jizhuang, Luo Longji, Xu Guangping, Huang Qixiang, Wang Shao'ao, He Changgong, Wu Jingxian, Cao Juru, Tao Xijin, Hu Yaobang, Luo Yi, Kang Keqing, Wu Maosun, Xue Zizheng. Senior generals of the People's Liberation Army: Lai Chuanzhu, Xu Liqing, Chen Qihan, Li Da, Deng Hua, Huang Yongsheng, Yang Yong, Wu Faxian, Gu Jingsheng, etc. At the same time, representatives from various leading organs of the People's Liberation Army, various special forces, the North China Military Region, various military academies and schools, and many organs and organizations in the capital, totaling more than 3,000 people, also came to pay their respects.

Peng Dehuai, He Long, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Su Yu, Huang Kecheng, Zhang Zongxun, Li Kenong, Luo Ruiqing, Tan Zheng, Xiao Hua, Gan Siqi, Song Renqiong, Chen Geng, and others paid their respects and participated in the vigil. Relatives of Comrade Yang Lisan and cadres from various leading organs and special forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army took turns guarding the remains.

### 各界代表吊唁杨立三同志

1954-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    各界代表吊唁杨立三同志
    【新华社六日讯】　前中国人民解放军财务部部长杨立三同志灵堂，已在五日上午八时开放。在杨立三同志遗容前陈放着中国共产党中央委员会、中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、副主席朱德、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇和国务院总理周恩来送的花圈。
    五日至六日下午五时前往吊唁的有中国共产党中央委员和候补中央委员：刘少奇、林伯渠、董必武、吴玉章、滕代远、张际春、程子华。各民主党派、各人民团体和各部门负责人：黄炎培、章伯钧、张治中、史良、叶季壮、罗隆基、许广平、黄琪翔、王绍鏊、何长工、武兢天、曹菊如、陶希晋、胡耀邦、罗毅、康克清、吴茂荪、薛子正。人民解放军高级将领：赖传珠、徐立清、陈奇涵、李达、邓华、黄永胜、杨勇、吴法宪、谷景生等。同时人民解放军各领导机关、各特种兵、华北军区、各军事院、校及首都许多机关、团体代表三千多人亦前往吊唁。
    吊唁并参加守灵的有：彭德怀、贺龙、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英、粟裕、黄克诚、张宗逊、李克农、罗瑞卿、谭政、萧华、甘泗淇、宋任穷、陈赓等。杨立三同志亲属和中国人民解放军各领导机关、各特种兵干部均轮流守灵。
