Title: Dutch Business and Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 11th.

    The Dutch business and trade delegation, consisting of nine members led by the head of the delegation, Mr. Ko Jo Schaap, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 11th.
    The Dutch business and trade delegation is organized by the Netherlands China Trade Committee. The members of the delegation represent the Dutch chemical, pharmaceutical, electrical equipment, machine manufacturing, shipping, and general import and export industries. During their visit, the delegation will discuss trade issues with the China National Import and Export Corporation, China's various export corporations, and the Overseas Transportation Company.
    On the evening of the 11th, Cao Zhongshu, acting manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, hosted a banquet to welcome the Dutch business and trade delegation.

### 荷兰工商界贸易访问团到京

1954-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    荷兰工商界贸易访问团到京
   【新华社十一日讯】荷兰工商界贸易访问团一行九人，由团长考·约·沙普率领，在十一日下午到达北京。
    荷兰工商界贸易访问团是由荷兰对华贸易委员会组织的。访问团的成员代表了荷兰的化学、制药、电工器材、机器制造、航运和一般进出口业。访问团在访问期间，将和我中国进出口公司、中国各出口公司和海外运输公司商谈有关贸易问题。
    十一日晚，中国进出口公司代经理曹中枢设宴欢迎荷兰工商界贸易访问团。
