Title: Pakistan Government Appoints Ahmed as Ambassador to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-12-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: The Pakistani government has appointed Sultanuddin Ahmed as Pakistan's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China.

### 巴基斯坦政府任命阿哈默德为驻我国大使

1954-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦政府任命阿哈默德为驻我国大使
    【新华社三十日讯】巴基斯坦政府任命苏尔丹乌丁·阿哈默德为巴基斯坦驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。
