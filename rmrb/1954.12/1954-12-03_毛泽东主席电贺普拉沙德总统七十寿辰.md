Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Sends a Congratulatory Message to President Prasad on His Seventieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1954-12-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to the President of the Republic of India, Rajendra Prasad, congratulating him on his seventieth birthday. The congratulatory telegram is as follows:
His Excellency Rajendra Prasad, President of the Republic of India:
	On the occasion of your seventieth birthday, please accept my sincere congratulations.
		Mao Zedong
		December 1, 1954, in Beijing

### 毛泽东主席电贺普拉沙德总统七十寿辰

1954-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席电贺普拉沙德总统七十寿辰
    【新华社二日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东致电印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德，祝贺他的七十寿辰。贺电如下：印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德阁下：
    在您七十寿辰之际，谨向您致诚挚的祝贺。毛泽东一九五四年十二月一日于北京
