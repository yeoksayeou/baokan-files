Title: Chinese Writers Association Delegation to the Soviet Union to Attend the All-Union Writers Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-12-12
Page: 1

    Invited to Attend the All-Union Writers Congress
    Chinese Writers Association Delegation to the Soviet Union

    Xinhua News Agency, December 11th: At the invitation of the Soviet Writers Association to attend the Second All-Union Writers Congress, the head of the Chinese Writers Association delegation and Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, Zhou Yang, delegation members and Vice Chairmen of the Chinese Writers Association, Ding Ling, Lao She, staff members Chen Bingyi, Gao Mang, etc., left for Moscow by plane on the 11th.

### 应邀参加全苏作家代表大会  我国作家协会代表团去苏联

1954-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加全苏作家代表大会
    我国作家协会代表团去苏联
      【新华社十一日讯】应苏联作家协会的邀请参加第二次全苏作家代表大会的中国作家协会代表团团长、中国作家协会副主席周扬，代表团团员、中国作家协会副主席丁玲、老舍，工作人员陈冰夷、高莽等，在十一日乘飞机去莫斯科。
