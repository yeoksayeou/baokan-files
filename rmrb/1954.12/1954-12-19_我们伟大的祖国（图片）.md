Our Great Motherland
The No. 2 Machining Assembly, No. 1 Forging and Temporary Casting and Sheet Metal Workshops of the state-owned Taiyuan Heavy Machinery Plant, which are under initial construction, have been successively put into production. This is the benzene tower required for the coking furnace chemical equipment of Anshan Iron and Steel, being assembled by the Li Fengchen team in the assembly section.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营太原重型机器厂初步建成的第二机工装配、第一锻压和临时铸工冷作等车间，已陆续投入生产。这是装配工段李凤臣小组正在装配鞍钢焦炉化工设备所需的苯塔。
