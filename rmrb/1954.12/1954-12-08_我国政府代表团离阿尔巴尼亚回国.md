Title: Chinese Government Delegation Leaves Albania for Home
Author:
Date: 1954-12-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 7th: The Chinese government delegation, headed by Li Xiannian, and including members such as Yang Xiandong, which had been invited to Tirana to attend the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of Albania's liberation, left Albania by ship on the 4th to return home. Government delegations from the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, North Korea, Mongolia, Vietnam, and other countries also left Albania on the same ship.
    Present at the port of Durrës to see them off were: Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of Albanian Party of Labour; Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers; members of the Political Bureau and government ministers of the Central Committee of the Labour Party; members of the Presidium of the People's Assembly; and heads of mass organizations.
    Also at the port to see them off were Xu Yixin, the Chinese Ambassador to Albania, and diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and other People's Democracies.
    Since arriving in Albania on November 28th, the Chinese government delegation, in addition to participating in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of Albania's liberation, also visited the "Ten Years of People's Power" exhibition, factories, agricultural production cooperatives, and agricultural research institutions in Tirana, Korçë, and other places. The delegation was warmly welcomed and received by the Albanian people everywhere it went.

### 我国政府代表团离阿尔巴尼亚回国

1954-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府代表团离阿尔巴尼亚回国
    【新华社七日讯】地拉那消息：应邀到地拉那参加阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长李先念和团员杨显东等，在四日乘船离阿尔巴尼亚返国。同船离阿返国的有苏联、保加利亚、朝鲜、蒙古、越南等国的政府代表团。
    到都拉斯港口欢送的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩·霍查，部长会议主席穆·谢胡，劳动党中央委员会政治局委员们、政府委员和人民议会主席团的委员们，以及群众团体的负责人等。
    到港口欢送的还有我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使徐以新，苏联和各人民民主国家驻阿外交使节。
    中华人民共和国政府代表团自十一月二十八日到达阿尔巴尼亚后，除参加阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年庆祝典礼外，还在地拉那、科尔察等地参观了“人民政权的十年”展览会、工厂、农业生产合作社和农业研究机关等。代表团到处受到阿尔巴尼亚人民的热诚欢迎和接待。
