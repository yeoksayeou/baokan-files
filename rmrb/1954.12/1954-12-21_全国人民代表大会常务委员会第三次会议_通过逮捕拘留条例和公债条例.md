Title: The Third Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Passes Regulations on Arrest and Detention and Regulations on Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1954-12-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 20th—The Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China held its third meeting on December 20th. After hearing the explanation of Zhang Su, Chairman of the Bills Committee, the meeting discussed and approved the "Arrest and Detention Regulations of the People's Republic of China." The meeting then discussed and approved the "1955 National Economic Construction Public Bond Regulations" proposed by the State Council.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会第三次会议  通过逮捕拘留条例和公债条例

1954-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会第三次会议
    通过逮捕拘留条例和公债条例
    【新华社二十日讯】中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会在十二月二十日举行第三次会议。会议在听了法案委员会主任委员张苏的说明后，讨论并通过了“中华人民共和国逮捕拘留条例”。会议接着讨论并通过了国务院提出的“一九五五年国家经济建设公债条例”。
