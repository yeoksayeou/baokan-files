Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message on Novotný's Fiftieth Birthday
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 15th
The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to Antonín Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, congratulating him on his fiftieth birthday. The telegram reads as follows: Dear Comrade Antonín Novotný:
    On the occasion of your fiftieth birthday, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends to you warm, fraternal congratulations. We wish you good health and congratulate you on achieving new successes in leading the Czechoslovak people in carrying out socialist construction and in the cause of defending world peace and European collective security!
    Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
              December 10, 1954

### 中共中央致电祝贺诺沃提尼五十寿辰

1954-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央致电祝贺诺沃提尼五十寿辰
    【新华社十五日讯】中国共产党中央委员会致电捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记安·诺沃提尼，祝贺他的五十寿辰，电文如下：亲爱的安托宁·诺沃提尼同志：
    在您五十寿辰的时候，中国共产党中央委员会谨向您致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。祝贺您身体健康，祝贺您在领导捷克斯洛伐克人民为进行社会主义建设，为保卫世界和平、保卫欧洲集体安全的事业获得新的成就！
    中国共产党中央委员会
              一九五四年十二月十日
