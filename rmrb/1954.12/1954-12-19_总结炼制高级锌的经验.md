Title: Summarizing the Experience of Refining High-Grade Zinc
Author:
Date: 1954-12-19
Page: 2

    In mid-November, the Non-ferrous Metals Industry Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Heavy Industry and other units jointly organized a working group to go to the First Smelting Plant of the Shuikoushan Mining Bureau in Hunan to summarize the operational experience of refining high-grade pure zinc, in order to promote it in other zinc smelters and provide technical data for the country to build large-scale zinc smelters.

### 总结炼制高级锌的经验

1954-12-19
第2版()
专栏：

    总结炼制高级锌的经验
    十一月中旬，重工业部有色金属工业管理局等单位，联合组织了工作组，到湖南水口山矿务局第一冶炼厂，总结炼制高级纯锌的操作经验，以便在其他炼锌厂中推广，并为国家兴建大型炼锌厂提供技术资料。
