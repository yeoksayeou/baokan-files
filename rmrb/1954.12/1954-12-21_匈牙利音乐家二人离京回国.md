Title: Hungarian Musicians Leave Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1954-12-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 19th:

    Two Hungarian musicians, the famous violinist Zathureczky and the Hungarian pianist Petri, left Beijing for home on the 19th.
    Zathureczky and Petri visited China in November according to the 1954 implementation plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. They visited Beijing, Tianjin, Nanjing and Shanghai and held concerts.

### 匈牙利音乐家二人离京回国

1954-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利音乐家二人离京回国
    【新华社十九日讯】匈牙利著名小提琴家扎图雷茨基和匈牙利钢琴家帕特里在十九日离开北京回国。
    扎图雷茨基和帕特里是根据中匈文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划，在十一月来我国访问的。他们曾在北京、天津、南京和上海等地进行参观访问，并举行了演奏会。
