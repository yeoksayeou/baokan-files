Title: Moscow Musical Theatre Presents Famous Ballet "Swan Lake" to Entertain Members of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference

Author:
Date: 1954-12-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 18th: The Soviet State Moscow Musical Theatre performed the world-famous Russian classical ballet "Swan Lake" at the Tianqiao Theatre in Beijing on the evening of the 18th to entertain members of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Also in attendance were Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and figures from the capital's cultural and artistic circles.

The Soviet artists' outstanding performance was warmly praised by the audience.

### 莫斯科音乐剧院演出著名舞剧“天鹅湖”  招待我人民政协第二届全国委员会委员

1954-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科音乐剧院演出著名舞剧“天鹅湖”
    招待我人民政协第二届全国委员会委员
    【新华社十八日讯】苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院十八日晚在北京天桥剧场演出世界著名的俄罗斯古典舞剧“天鹅湖”，招待中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会委员们。
    到场观剧的还有中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通等以及首都文化艺术界人士。
    苏联艺术家们的卓越的演出，受到了观众们的热烈赞赏。
