Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Reception for Members of the Soviet Folk Dance Ensemble and Moscow Musical Theatre
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th—Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China held a reception on the evening of the 30th to entertain all members of the Soviet State Folk Dance Ensemble and the Soviet State Stanislavski and Nemirovich-Danchenko Musical Theatre of Moscow.

    Artistic Director Moiseyev of the Soviet State Folk Dance Ensemble and all members of the dance troupe, and President Poliyakov of the Moscow Musical Theatre and all members of the theatre were invited to attend the reception.

    Also invited to attend the reception were Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin and Borisinko, Director of the Soviet Exhibition of Economic and Cultural Achievements in China, and others.

    On the Chinese side, those present as hosts included Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Director of the Second Office Lin Feng, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers Qian Junrui, Ding Xilin, Xia Yan, Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, and Vice Mayor Zhang Youyu.

    Also present as hosts were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, as well as well-known writers, dancers, musicians, directors, actors, and painters from the capital's literary and art circles.

    At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai, on behalf of the Chinese people and the Government of the People's Republic of China, expressed his cordial respect to the outstanding representatives of Soviet artists for their visit and performances in our country.

    He said that Soviet art is the most advanced art in the world because it belongs to the people, serves the people, and is a powerful weapon for the Soviet people in their struggle for a better future. Your work is a shining example for progressive artists all over the world. Your outstanding art will have a profound impact on the development of our country's art. The brotherly friendship between the great Soviet people and the Chinese people is a powerful and reliable guarantee for realizing the most urgent desire of all mankind today—world peace.

    Next, Moiseyev spoke. He said that we have visited many cities in China, further understood your country and your people, and more deeply recognized China's long and outstanding culture and the outstanding art of the Chinese people. He said that the great friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union contains profound historical significance and enormous strength; he congratulated the further consolidation and development of this friendship.

    Poliyakov then spoke. He said that we have seen many magnificent buildings in China, and we have seen China's classical and emerging art. All this unfolds before us a new and magnificent picture of great China, which will leave us with the most splendid and unforgettable memories of China in our lives.

    Ambassador Yudin also addressed the meeting, warmly congratulating the Chinese and Soviet artists on their new creative achievements.

    The reception took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 招待苏联民间舞蹈团和莫斯科音乐剧院全体人员  周恩来总理举行酒会

1954-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    招待苏联民间舞蹈团和莫斯科音乐剧院全体人员
    周恩来总理举行酒会
   【新华社三十日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在三十日晚举行酒会，招待苏联国立民间舞蹈团和苏联国立莫斯科斯坦尼斯拉夫斯基与聂米罗维奇—丹钦科音乐剧院全体人员。
    苏联国立民间舞蹈团艺术指导员莫依塞耶夫和舞蹈团全体人员，莫斯科音乐剧院院长波利雅科夫和剧院全体人员应邀出席了酒会。
    应邀出席酒会的，还有苏联驻我国大使尤金和苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科等。
    我国方面出席作陪的，有国务院副总理陈毅，秘书长习仲勋，第二办公室主任林枫，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长钱俊瑞、丁西林、夏衍、张致祥，外交部副部长伍修权，北京市市长彭真，副市长张友渔。
    出席作陪的，还有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，以及首都文学艺术界的著名作家、舞蹈家、音乐家、导演、演员和画家等。
    在招待会上，周恩来总理代表中国人民和中华人民共和国政府，向苏联艺术家的优秀代表们在我国的访问演出表示亲切的敬意。
    他说，苏维埃艺术是世界上最先进的艺术，因为它是属于人民的，是为人民服务的，它是苏联人民为着自己美好的未来而斗争的强有力的武器。你们的工作是全世界进步艺术家的光辉榜样。你们卓越的艺术，对于我们国家的艺术事业的发展，将给予深远的影响。伟大的苏联人民和中国人民间兄弟般的友谊，是实现今天全人类最迫切的愿望——世界和平的有力的和可靠的保障。
    接着，莫依塞耶夫讲话。他说，我们访问了中国很多城市，进一步了解了你们的国家、你们的人民，更深刻地认识了中国悠久而卓越的文化和中国人民杰出的艺术。他说，中苏两国人民伟大的友谊蕴藏着深厚的历史意义和巨大力量；他祝贺这一友谊获得进一步巩固和发展。
    波利雅科夫接着讲话。他说，我们在中国看到了很多宏伟的建筑，看到了中国古典的和新兴的艺术。这一切都在我们眼前展开了一幅伟大中国的新的宏伟景象，这将使我们从中国留下一生最灿烂的永不能忘的记忆。
    尤金大使也在会上致词，热烈祝贺中国的和苏联的艺术家们获得新的创造性的成就。
    酒会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
