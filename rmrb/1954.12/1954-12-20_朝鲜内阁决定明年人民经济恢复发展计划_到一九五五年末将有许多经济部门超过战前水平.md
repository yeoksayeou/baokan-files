Title: Korean Cabinet Decides on People's Economic Recovery and Development Plan for Next Year; Many Economic Sectors Will Exceed Pre-War Levels by the End of 1955
Author:
Date: 1954-12-20
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th) The Korean Central News Agency reports: The Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea recently passed a decision regarding the 1955 Plan for the Recovery and Development of the People's Economy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The decision states: In the struggle for the recovery and development of the people's economy after the war, the working people of the Republic, upholding the correct policies of the Party and the government, completed the 1954 Plan for the Recovery and Development of the People's Economy ahead of schedule and exceeding targets, achieving great results in various sectors such as industry, rural economy, transportation, postal and telecommunications, culture, and construction.

Based on the great achievements made in 1954, the patriotic enthusiasm and creative spirit of the people, and the continued technical and economic assistance from the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and other People's Democratic countries, the decision stipulates that by the end of 1955, many economic sectors will reach and exceed the 1956 levels and pre-war levels stipulated in the Three-Year Plan for the Recovery and Development of the People's Economy after the war.

According to the "Rodong Sinmun" report, in accordance with the provisions of the 1955 Plan for the Recovery and Development of the People's Economy, the total industrial output value in 1955 will increase by 59.8 percent compared to the planned figures for 1954, and the output will increase by 59.5 percent compared to 1949, exceeding the pre-war level. The total industrial output value for the entire year of 1955 will reach 102.4 percent of the 1956 target stipulated in the Three-Year Plan.

The total investment in basic construction in 1955 will increase by 34 percent compared to the planned figures for 1954. This huge amount of investment will be focused on continuing the recovery and construction of industry, agriculture, culture, healthcare, postal equipment, etc., and significantly improving the housing conditions of the broad masses.

### 朝鲜内阁决定明年人民经济恢复发展计划  到一九五五年末将有许多经济部门超过战前水平

1954-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜内阁决定明年人民经济恢复发展计划
    到一九五五年末将有许多经济部门超过战前水平
    【新华社平壤十九日电】朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁最近通过了关于一九五五年朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民经济恢复发展计划的决定。决定中说：在战后人民经济恢复发展的斗争中，共和国劳动人民拥护党和政府的正确政策，提前和超额完成了一九五四年人民经济恢复发展计划，在工业、农村经济、运输、邮电、文化、建设等各部门取得了巨大的成果。
    根据一九五四年取得的巨大成果，人民群众的爱国热忱和创造精神以及苏联、中华人民共和国和其他各人民民主国家的继续不断的技术上和经济上的援助，决定中规定：到一九五五年末，将有许多经济部门达到并超过战后人民经济恢复发展三年计划所规定的一九五六年的水平和战前水平。
    据“劳动新闻”报道，依照一九五五年人民经济恢复发展计划的规定，一九五五年的工业总产值将比一九五四年的计划数字增加百分之五十九点八，产量比一九四九年增加百分之五十九点五，超过战前水平。一九五五年的全年工业总产值将达到三年计划中规定的一九五六年指标的百分之一百零二点四。
    一九五五年的基本建设投资总额将比一九五四年计划数字增加百分之三十四。这个庞大的投资额将被重点地用来继续恢复建设工业、农业、文化、保健、邮电设备等和显著地改善广大群众的住宅条件。
