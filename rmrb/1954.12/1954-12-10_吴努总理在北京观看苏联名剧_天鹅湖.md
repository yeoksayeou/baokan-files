Title: Premier U Nu of Burma Watches Soviet Masterpiece "Swan Lake" in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th: Premier U Nu of the Union of Burma, his wife, and members of his delegation visiting China, watched the famous ballet "Swan Lake" performed by the Soviet National Moscow Musical Theater in Beijing on the evening of the 9th.

Accompanying them were Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Deng Zihui and He Long, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, and President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu of the People's Republic of China.

Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, also attended the performance.

### 吴努总理在北京观看苏联名剧“天鹅湖”

1954-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努总理在北京观看苏联名剧“天鹅湖”
    【新华社九日讯】九日晚，缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人以及随他来我国访问的人员在北京观看了苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院演出的著名巴蕾舞剧“天鹅湖”。
    陪同观剧的有：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，副总理邓子恢、贺龙，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、陈叔通，最高人民法院院长董必武。
    陪同观剧的还有苏联驻我国大使尤金院士。
