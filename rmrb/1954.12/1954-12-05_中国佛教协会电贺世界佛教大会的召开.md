Title: Chinese Buddhist Association Sends Congratulations on the Convening of the World Buddhist Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd—The Chinese Buddhist Association sent a telegram on December 2nd to congratulate the convening of the Third World Buddhist Conference. The telegram said:
　　The Third World Buddhist Conference, hosted by the Burmese Buddhist Association, is scheduled to open in Yangon on December 3rd. The Association hereby extends its congratulations to your conference on behalf of the Buddhists of the People's Republic of China. We wish your conference will contribute to the cause of promoting the sacred teachings and safeguarding world peace, based on Buddha's teachings of compassion, equality, universal salvation, and deliverance. Chinese Buddhists, under the leadership of the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, are harmoniously and unitedly engaged in quiet cultivation, meditation, studying the Dharma, and tirelessly dedicating themselves to the cause of peace. We deeply hope that under the light of Buddha's peace, we will work together with Buddhists from all over the world to strive for the happiness and tranquility of mankind, free from the scourge of war.

### 中国佛教协会电贺世界佛教大会的召开

1954-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    中国佛教协会电贺世界佛教大会的召开
    【新华社三日讯】中国佛教协会十二月二日电贺第三届世界佛教大会的召开，电文说：
    第三届世界佛教大会在缅甸佛教协会主持下定于十二月三日在仰光开幕。本会谨代表中华人民共和国佛教徒向贵大会致以贺意。祝贵大会本佛陀慈悲、平等、济世、救人之教义，对宏扬圣教、维护世界和平的事业尽其贡献。中国佛教徒，在达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼等领导下和合一致地静修、禅思、研习佛法、精进不息地致力于和平事业。我们深愿在佛陀和平之光的照耀下，为使人类免除战祸得以幸福安宁，与世界各国的佛教徒精进共勉。
