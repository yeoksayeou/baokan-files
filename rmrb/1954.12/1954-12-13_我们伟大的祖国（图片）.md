Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-12-13
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Soldiers building the Kangding-Tibet Highway are engaging in production while building the road in order to alleviate the burden on the people and improve their own lives. The picture shows the soldiers using a new-style horse-drawn plow for cultivation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    修筑康藏公路的战士们为了减轻人民的负担和改善自己的生活，一面筑路，一面生产。这是战士们正在用新式马拉犁进行耕耘。
