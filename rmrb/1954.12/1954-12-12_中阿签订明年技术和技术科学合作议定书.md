Title: China and Albania Sign Protocol on Technological and Technical-Scientific Cooperation for Next Year
Author:
Date: 1954-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 11th: The Sino-Albanian Joint Committee on Technological and Technical-Scientific Cooperation held its first meeting in Tirana from December 1st to 3rd. Both sides reached an agreement in an extremely friendly and understanding atmosphere and signed a protocol on technological and technical-scientific cooperation for 1955. According to the protocol, both sides will exchange experience in production. The protocol was signed by Qomi, Vice Minister of Agriculture of the People's Republic of Albania, on the Albanian side, and Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, on the Chinese side.

### 中阿签订明年技术和技术科学合作议定书

1954-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中阿签订明年技术和技术科学合作议定书
    【新华社十一日讯】地拉那消息：中阿技术和技术科学合作联合委员会，十二月一日到三日在地拉那举行了第一次会议。双方在极为友好和谅解的气氛中达成协议，签订了一九五五年的技术和技术科学合作的议定书。根据议定书规定，双方将互相交换生产方面的经验。在议定书上签字的，阿尔巴尼亚方面是阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国农业部副部长乔米，中国方面是中华人民共和国农业部副部长杨显东。
