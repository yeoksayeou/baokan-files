Title: Nepal-China Friendship Association Holds Public Gathering Demanding the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations with Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-12-17
Page: 4

    Nepal-China Friendship Association Holds Public Gathering
    Demanding the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations with Our Country
    [Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 16th] Kathmandu Dispatch: The Nepal-China Friendship Association held a public gathering in Kathmandu on the 11th. A resolution was passed at the meeting demanding the early establishment of diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China.
    A dozen speakers belonging to different political groups emphasized the necessity of establishing diplomatic relations with China at the meeting. The General Secretary of the Nepali Congress Party, Rikikesh Shaha, said in his speech that China is Nepal's great neighbor and diplomatic relations should be established with China.

### 尼中友协举行公众集会  要求和我国建立外交关系

1954-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    尼中友协举行公众集会
    要求和我国建立外交关系
    【新华社新德里十六日电】加德满都消息：尼（泊尔）中友好协会十一日在加德满都举行公众集会，会上通过决议，要求早日和中华人民共和国建立外交关系。
    属于不同政治团体的十来个发言人在会上强调和中国建立外交关系的必要性。尼泊尔国大党总书记里基克什·沙阿在发言中表示，中国是尼泊尔的伟大邻邦，应该和中国建立外交关系。
