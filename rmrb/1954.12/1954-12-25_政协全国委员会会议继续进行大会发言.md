Title: CPPCC National Committee Meeting Continues with Plenary Speeches
Author:
Date: 1954-12-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 24th: The First Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference continued its plenary speeches on the afternoon of the 24th.

The following individuals spoke at today's meeting: Guo Moruo, Zhou Shutao, Zhang Zhizhong, Gu Jiegang, Yu Lan, Huba Meng, Cao Gushui, Zhang Qilong, Bao Erhan, Sun Shuzhi, Li Chunqing, Lu Han, Chen Zupai, You Yangzu, Luo Guangwen, Zeng Zhenwu, Weng Wenhao, Deng Wenhui, Liang Shuming, and Zhang Dazhong. The meeting started at 3:00 PM and ended at 6:50 PM.

This morning, the members held group discussions. The meeting will continue on the 25th.

### 政协全国委员会会议继续进行大会发言

1954-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会会议继续进行大会发言
    【新华社二十四日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议，在二十四日下午继续进行大会发言。
    在今天大会上发言的共有：郭沫若、周叔弢、张治中、顾颉刚、于蓝、祜巴猛、曹谷水、张启龙、鲍尔汉、孙淑芝、李纯青、卢汉、陈祖沛、尤扬祖、罗广文、曾震五、翁文灏、邓文翬、梁漱溟、张大中。会议在下午三时开始，六时五十分结束。
    今天上午，委员们曾进行了分组讨论。会议将在二十五日继续举行。
