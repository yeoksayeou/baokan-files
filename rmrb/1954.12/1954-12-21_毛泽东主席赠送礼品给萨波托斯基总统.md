Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Presents Gifts to President Zápotocký
Author:
Date: 1954-12-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 20th: Cao Ying, the Plenipotentiary Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Czechoslovakia, presented gifts to the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Antonín Zápotocký, on the morning of the 19th on behalf of Chairman Mao Zedong to congratulate him on his 70th birthday.

    Present at the gift-giving ceremony was the Czechoslovak Minister of Foreign Affairs, David, and others.

    The gifts included an exquisitely carved ivory peacock, a pair of cloisonné flowerpots, a set of Fujian lacquer trays, and a set of drawnwork tablecloths.

### 毛泽东主席赠送礼品给萨波托斯基总统

1954-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席赠送礼品给萨波托斯基总统
    【新华社布拉格二十日电】中华人民共和国驻捷克斯洛伐克特命全权大使曹瑛，十九日上午代表毛泽东主席向捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基赠送礼品，祝贺他的七十寿辰。
    赠礼时在座的有捷克斯洛伐克外交部长戴维等。
    礼品包括一件精雕的象牙孔雀、一对景泰蓝花盆、一套福建漆盘和一套挑花桌布等。
