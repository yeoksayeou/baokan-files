Title: Congratulating the Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnam People's Army, China's Defense Minister Peng Dehuai Sends a Telegram to Vietnam's Defense Minister Vo Nguyen Giap
Author:
Date: 1954-12-22
Page: 1

    Congratulating the Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnam People's Army
    China's Defense Minister Peng Dehuai Sends a Telegram to Vietnam's Defense Minister Vo Nguyen Giap
   [Xinhua News Agency, 21st] Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on the 21st to congratulate the anniversary of the founding of the Vietnam People's Army. The telegram said:
    On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the Vietnam People's Army, I would like to extend warm congratulations to you and all the officers and soldiers of the Vietnam People's Army on behalf of all commanders and combatants of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    The Vietnam People's Army has carried out a long and heroic struggle for the freedom and independence of the motherland and has achieved brilliant victories. I wish the Vietnam People's Army further achievements in continuing to strive for the independence, freedom, and unification of the motherland.

### 祝贺越南人民军建军节  我国防部长彭德怀电越南国防部长武元甲

1954-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺越南人民军建军节
    我国防部长彭德怀电越南国防部长武元甲
   【新华社二十一日讯】中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀在二十一日致电越南民主共和国国防部长武元甲，祝贺越南人民军建军节。电文说：
    欣逢越南人民军建军节之际，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体指挥员、战斗员向您及越南人民军全体将士致以热烈的祝贺。
    越南人民军为了祖国的自由和独立，曾进行了长期的英勇斗争，并取得了光辉的胜利。谨祝越南人民军在继续争取祖国的独立、自由和统一的事业中，获得更进一步的成就。
