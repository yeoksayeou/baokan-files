Title: German Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th: The trade delegation from the German Democratic Republic, led by Norbert, Deputy Head of the delegation and Deputy Director of the Ministry of Foreign and Domestic Trade of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 17th to conduct trade negotiations for the 1955 Sino-German trade agreement.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of our country, Sun Chun, Deputy Director of the Import Bureau, Luo Baoyi, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau, and Zhang Guangdou, Commercial Counselor of our country's Embassy in the German Democratic Republic.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Dürrbeck, Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, Daix, First Secretary, and Baum, Commercial Commissioner.

### 德贸易代表团到京

1954-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    德贸易代表团到京
    【新华社十七日讯】前来我国进行一九五五年度中德贸易谈判的德意志民主共和国贸易代表团，由副团长、德意志民主共和国对外与国内贸易部副司长诺贝尔特率领，十七日乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有我国对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，进口局副局长孙纯、出口局副局长罗抱一和我国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆商务参赞张光斗等。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆商务参赞杜尔伯、一等秘书戴克斯、商务专员鲍牧等也前往机场迎接。
