Title: Pictures

Author:

Date: 1954-12-02
Page: 4

    Chairman Mao Zedong received Prime Minister U Nu of the Union of Burma on the evening of December 1st, who was visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese government.
    (Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan)
    Prime Minister U Nu arrived in Beijing by plane on December 1st. Accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, he reviewed the guard of honor at the airport.
    (Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie)
    Prime Minister U Nu was warmly welcomed by the citizens of Beijing as he walked out of the airport.
    (Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie)
    Prime Minister U Nu received flowers from children in the capital.
    (Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo)
