Title: National Automatic Screw Press Peanut Oil Extraction Training Class Opens

Author:
Date: 1954-12-07
Page: 2

    National Automatic Screw Press Peanut Oil Extraction Training Class Opens
    The National Automatic Screw Press Peanut Oil Extraction Training Class opened in Qingdao on December 3rd. The training class includes 87 staff members and technicians from provincial industrial departments, municipal industrial bureaus, oil and fat industrial departments, and oil pressing factories from ten provinces: Guangdong, Guangxi, Fujian, Sichuan, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Hebei, Henan, Anhui, and Shandong. The training period is one month.
    The automatic screw press peanut oil extraction training class is being held in accordance with the resolution of the National Oil and Fat Industry Advanced Experience Exchange Conference. The purpose is to train technical backbones in order to deeply promote advanced experience in the oil pressing industry, especially Li Chuanjiang's soybean oil extraction method. The training content mainly includes basic knowledge of oil extraction, Li Chuanjiang's soybean oil extraction method, screw press peanut oil extraction method, and general oil and fat testing knowledge. In addition, trainees will also study the general tasks of the country during the transition period, so that they can further clarify the role of local industry in the national transition period and the importance of the oil and fat industry, etc. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国自动螺旋机花生榨油训练班开学

1954-12-07
第2版()
专栏：

    全国自动螺旋机花生榨油训练班开学
    全国自动螺旋机花生榨油训练班十二月三日在青岛开学。参加训练班的有广东、广西、福建、四川、陕西、山西、河北、河南、安徽、山东十个省的省工业厅、市工业局、油脂工业部门的工作人员和榨油厂的技术人员和工人共八十七人。训练时间是一个月。
    自动螺旋机花生榨油训练班是根据全国油脂工业先进经验交流大会的决议举办的。目的是为了训练技术骨干，以便深入推广榨油工业中的先进经验，特别是李川江大豆榨油操作法。训练班的学习内容主要是榨油基本知识、李川江大豆榨油操作法、螺旋机花生榨油法和一般的油脂化验知识。此外，还将学习国家在过渡时期的总任务，使学员进一步明确地方工业在国家过渡时期的作用和油脂工业的重要性等。　（新华社）
