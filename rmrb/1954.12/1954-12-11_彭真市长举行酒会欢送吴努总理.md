Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Reception to See Off Prime Minister U Nu
Author:
Date: 1954-12-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 10th: Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen held a grand reception on the afternoon of the 10th to bid farewell to the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his wife, who are about to leave Beijing.

U Min Thein, U Win Pe, U Thant, Colonel Aung Gyi, Captain Ba Saw, and U Khin Swe, who accompanied Prime Minister U Nu on his visit to China, were also invited to attend the reception.

The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the reception.

On the Chinese side, those who attended the reception included Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Dalai Lama Tenzin Gyatso, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Ministers of various ministries Shi Liang, Ye Jizhuang, Jiang Guangnai, Fu Zuoyi, Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Xiruo, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Ministers of various ministries, Deputy Directors of various committees, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Zhang Youyu.

Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Su Yu, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Xiao Hua, and others attended the reception.

China's Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, also attended the reception.

Qian Weichang, Vice President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, and members of the council, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, some members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing, heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations, members of the Beijing Municipal People's Government and heads of various departments, Vice Chairman and members of the Beijing Municipal Consultative Committee, heads of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Beijing Municipal organizations of various democratic parties, heads of various people's organizations in Beijing, representatives from the capital's scientific, educational, literary, artistic, religious, and journalistic circles, as well as those who had visited Burma, and heads of various units that Prime Minister U Nu and others had visited in Beijing.

More than 500 people attended the reception.

The reception took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 彭真市长举行酒会欢送吴努总理

1954-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真市长举行酒会欢送吴努总理
    【新华社十日讯】北京市市长彭真在十日下午举行盛大酒会，欢送就要离开北京的缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人。
    随同吴努总理访问我国的吴敏登、吴温佩、吴丹、昂季上校、毕梭上尉、都庆素等九人都应邀出席了酒会。
    缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂和使馆人员也应邀出席了酒会。
    我国方面出席酒会的，有国务院副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措，最高人民法院院长董必武，国务院秘书长习仲勋，各部部长史良、叶季壮、蒋光鼐、傅作义、杨秀峰、张奚若，外交部副部长章汉夫，各部副部长，各委员会副主任，北京市副市长张友渔。
    出席酒会的，还有中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕，总政治部副主任萧华等。
    我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明也出席了酒会。
    出席酒会的，还有中缅友好协会副会长钱伟长和理事，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在京部分常务委员，各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，北京市人民政府委员和各部门负责人，北京市协商委员会副主席、委员，中共北京市委员会和各民主党派北京市组织的负责人，北京市各人民团体的负责人，首都科学、教育、文学、艺术、宗教、新闻界人士，以及曾访问过缅甸的人士，和吴努总理等在京参观访问过的各单位负责人。
    出席酒会的共有五百多人。
    酒会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
