Title: Negotiation on Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Traffic: Soviet Delegation Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1954-12-24
Page: 1

    Negotiation on Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Traffic
    Soviet Delegation Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd] The Soviet delegation of three people who came to China to discuss Sino-Soviet civil aviation traffic arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 23rd. The head of the delegation was Soviet Air Force Marshal Zhavaronkov, Director of the Civil Aviation Administration. The members of the delegation were Danilychev, Director of the Soviet International Airlines Administration, and engineer Velychkin.
    Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport were General Wu Faxian, head of the People's Republic of China Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Traffic Negotiation Delegation, delegation members Chen Ruiguang and Shen Tu, Deputy Commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Wang Bingzhang, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council Qi Yanming and Zhang Ce, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Liu Jingyu, Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru, and Deputy Directors of the Civil Aviation Administration of China Wang Fengwu and Li Ping.
    Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin and Deputy General Manager of the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Joint Stock Company, Bourfilov, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 商谈中苏民用航空通航事宜  苏联代表团昨日到达北京

1954-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    商谈中苏民用航空通航事宜
    苏联代表团昨日到达北京
    【新华社二十三日讯】来我国商谈中苏民用航空通航事宜的苏联代表团一行三人在二十三日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长是苏联空军元帅、民航总局局长热沃隆科夫，团员是苏联国际航线管理局局长达尼雷切夫和工程师魏立夏金。
    到机场欢迎的，有中华人民共和国中苏民用航空通航谈判代表团团长吴法宪将军，团员陈瑞光、沈图，中国人民解放军空军副司令员王秉璋将军，国务院副秘书长齐燕铭、张策，外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处处长王倬如，中国民用航空局副局长王凤梧、李平。
    苏联驻华大使尤金和中苏民用航空股份公司副总经理包尔菲洛夫也到机场欢迎。
