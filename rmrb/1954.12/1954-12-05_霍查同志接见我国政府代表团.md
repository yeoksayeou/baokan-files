Title: Comrade Hoxha Receives Our Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 2nd: Tirana dispatch: Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, received Li Xiannian, head of the delegation of the People's Republic of China, and members Yang Xiandong and Xu Yixin on November 29th.

Present at the reception were Shehu, Kabo, and Nushi, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Labor Party.

### 霍查同志接见我国政府代表团

1954-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    霍查同志接见我国政府代表团
    【新华社布拉格二日电】地拉那消息：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查，十一月二十九日接见了中华人民共和国代表团团长李先念，团员杨显东和徐以新。
    接见时在座的有劳动党中央委员会政治局委员谢胡、卡博和努什。
