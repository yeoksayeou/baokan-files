Title: PLA East China Naval Command and Zhejiang Frontline Command Commend Radar Station and Two Observation and Communication Stations
Author:
Date: 1954-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang Frontline, 30th — The East China Naval Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army issued a circular commending all personnel of a certain radar station on the Zhejiang Frontline, who assisted the People's Navy in sinking the Chiang bandit army's warship "Taiping" and were awarded a collective Third Class Merit.

The circular stated: "Thanks to your diligent and selfless work, you promptly discovered the actions of the Chiang bandit army's warships and accurately assisted the commanders in making operational decisions, enabling our heroic naval vessels to achieve the victory of sinking the Chiang bandit army's warship 'Taiping' with swift and sudden actions."

The circular also wished all personnel of this radar station to make persistent efforts, improve their professional skills, so as to cooperate with other troops to more effectively strike and annihilate the Chiang bandit army.

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang Frontline, 30th — The Zhejiang Frontline Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army recently issued a circular commending all personnel of two observation and communication stations and a certain radar station on the Zhejiang Frontline.

The circular stated: "Since November 1st, when our People's Liberation Army launched an offensive against the Chiang bandit army entrenched on the coastal islands of Zhejiang, thanks to your highly responsible and selfless work, you have promptly and accurately supplied the enemy's situation to the command organs, which has played a significant role in ensuring the victory of each battle."

The circular encouraged all personnel to sum up their experiences well, continue to work hard to strengthen observation and communication work, and further improve their professional skills, so as to ensure greater victories in future battles.

### 解放军华东海军司令部和浙江前线指挥机关分别表扬某雷达站和两个观通站全体人员

1954-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军华东海军司令部和浙江前线指挥机关
    分别表扬某雷达站和两个观通站全体人员
    【新华社浙江前线三十日电】中国人民解放军华东海军司令部通令表扬协助我人民海军舰艇击沉蒋贼军“太平号”军舰而荣立集体三等功的浙江前线某雷达站全体人员。
    通令中说：由于你们辛勤的忘我工作，及时地发现了蒋贼军舰的行动，准确地协助指挥员下达了作战的决心，使我人民海军英勇的舰艇能以迅速、突然的动作取得了击沉蒋贼军“太平号”军舰的胜利。
    通令中并祝这一雷达站全体人员再接再厉，提高业务水平，以便配合其他部队更有效地打击和歼灭蒋贼军。
   【新华社浙江前线三十日电】中国人民解放军浙江前线指挥机关最近通报表扬浙江前线的两个观通站和某雷达站全体人员。
    通报中说：自十一月一日我人民解放军向盘踞在浙江沿海岛屿上的蒋贼军发起攻势以来，由于你们高度负责、忘我的工作，及时地和确实地把敌情供给了指挥机关，对保证每次战斗的胜利起了很大作用。
    通报中勉励全体人员很好地总结经验，继续努力加强观通工作，进一步提高业务水平，以保证在今后的战斗中取得更大的胜利。
