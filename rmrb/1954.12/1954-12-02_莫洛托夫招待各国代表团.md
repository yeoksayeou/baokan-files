Title: Molotov Hosts Reception for Delegations from Various Countries
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 1st: According to TASS, Moscow, December 1st, the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, V.M. Molotov, held a reception on November 30th to welcome delegations from various countries participating in the Conference of European Countries on Ensuring Peace and Security in Europe.

    Those present at the reception included: the head of the delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz, and members of the delegation: Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Naszkowski and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Poland to the Soviet Union, Lewikowski; the head of the delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, Prime Minister Viliam Široký, and members of the delegation: Minister of Foreign Affairs V. David, Minister of Construction Industry E. Šlechta, Minister of Health J. Plojhar, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic to the Soviet Union, J. Vosahlik; the head of the delegation of the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister Grotewohl, members of the delegation, Deputy Prime Minister Ulbricht, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Bolz, Deputy Prime Ministers Nuschke and Loch, General Secretary of the Democratic Farmers' Party of Germany Ross, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union, Appelt; the head of the delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers András Hegedüs, and members of the delegation, Minister of Foreign Affairs János Boldoczki, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs András Sik, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to the Soviet Union, Ferenc Münnich; the head of the delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Chivu Stoica, and members of the delegation, Minister of Foreign Affairs Simion Bughici, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Grigore Preoteasa, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic to the Soviet Union, Rab; the head of the delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Anton Yugov, and members of the delegation, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Georgi Traykov, Minister of Foreign Affairs Mincho Neychev, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to the Soviet Union, Karlo Lukanov; the head of the delegation of the People's Republic of Albania, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Manush Muftiu, Minister of Foreign Affairs Behar Shtylla, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Albania to the Soviet Union, M. 1954-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫招待各国代表团
