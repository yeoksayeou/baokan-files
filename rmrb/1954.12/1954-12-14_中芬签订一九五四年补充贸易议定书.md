Title: China and Finland Sign 1954 Supplementary Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-12-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 13th. The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Finland, in order to further develop economic and trade relations between the two countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit, signed a 1954 Supplementary Trade Agreement in Beijing on December 13, after negotiations between representatives of both sides in Beijing.
    Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the agreement on behalf of the People's Republic of China, and Sun Shidun, Envoy of the Republic of Finland to China, signed the agreement on behalf of the Republic of Finland.
    Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ni Weiting, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and others attended the signing ceremony on the Chinese side.
    On the Finnish side, Selek, Counselor of the Embassy of the Republic of Finland in China, and Madila, Commercial Counselor, and others attended the signing ceremony.

### 中芬签订一九五四年补充贸易议定书

1954-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中芬签订一九五四年补充贸易议定书
    【新华社十三日讯】中华人民共和国政府和芬兰共和国政府为了进一步发展两国间以平等互利为基础的经济贸易关系，经过双方代表在北京商谈，十二月十三日在北京签订了一九五四年补充贸易议定书。
    代表中华人民共和国在议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长雷任民，代表芬兰共和国在议定书上签字的是芬兰共和国驻华公使孙士敦。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的有对外贸易部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国进出口公司副经理倪蔚庭等。
    芬兰方面参加签字仪式的有芬兰共和国驻我国公使馆参赞谢列克、商务参赞马狄拉等。
