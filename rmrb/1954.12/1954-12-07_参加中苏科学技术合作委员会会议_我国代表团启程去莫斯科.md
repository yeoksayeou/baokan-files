Title: Chinese Delegation Departs for Moscow to Attend Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 6th: The Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, established according to the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement signed in Beijing on October 12th, will soon hold its first meeting in Moscow. The delegation of the People's Republic of China, consisting of twenty people (six of whom had already gone to Moscow), led by Xue Muqiao, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission and Director of the State Statistical Bureau, left Beijing for Moscow by plane on the 5th.

### 参加中苏科学技术合作委员会会议  我国代表团启程去莫斯科

1954-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    参加中苏科学技术合作委员会会议
    我国代表团启程去莫斯科
    【新华社六日讯】　根据十月十二日在北京签订的中苏科学技术合作协定成立的中苏科学技术合作委员会，即将在莫斯科举行第一次会议。参加会议的中华人民共和国代表团一行二十人（其中六人已先去莫斯科），由国家计划委员会副主任兼国家统计局局长薛暮桥率领，在五日乘飞机离京去莫斯科。
