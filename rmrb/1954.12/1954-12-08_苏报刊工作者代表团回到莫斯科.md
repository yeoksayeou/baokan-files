Title: Soviet Newspaper Workers Delegation Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-12-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 6th: The Soviet newspaper workers delegation, which visited China at the invitation of "People's Daily," has returned to Moscow, according to TASS.

### 苏报刊工作者代表团回到莫斯科

1954-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏报刊工作者代表团回到莫斯科
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科六日讯：应“人民日报”的邀请到中国访问的苏联报刊工作者代表团，已经回到莫斯科。
