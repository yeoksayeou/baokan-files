Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Invites Natural Scientists from Beijing to a Symposium to Discuss Organizing Natural Scientists to Study Philosophy
Author:
Date: 1954-12-19
Page: 3

The Chinese Academy of Sciences' Study Committee invited more than 50 natural scientists from scientific research institutions and some universities in Beijing on the 15th to hold a symposium. The meeting discussed the issue of organizing natural scientists to study Marxist-Leninist philosophy and decided to establish a Philosophy Study Committee for Natural Scientists.

The symposium discussed the "Plan (Draft) on Natural Scientists Studying Marxist-Leninist Philosophy" proposed by the Chinese Academy of Sciences' Study Committee. Everyone agreed that studying Marxist-Leninist philosophy is an important and urgent task for natural scientists at present. At the same time, learning must implement the principle of linking theory with practice, closely integrate with the actual scientific and technological work and the actual situation of China's socialist construction, and learning should be based on the principle of voluntarism. Regarding the content and methods of learning, everyone believes that special studies can be conducted in conjunction with their scientific and technological work. Research topics can be roughly divided into the following four categories: certain basic principles of Marxist-Leninist philosophy; using the basic principles of dialectical materialism to study certain important theoretical problems in their own science; using the research results of their own science to illustrate or enrich certain basic principles of Marxist-Leninist philosophy; using dialectical materialist theory as a weapon to criticize some erroneous academic thoughts, viewpoints and works existing in the current natural science field. The learning method is mainly self-study, focusing on independent research, and when necessary, seminars, discussions, and mutual visits and research can be conducted, and irregular, prepared topical discussion meetings can be held. Scientists can make speeches on special research at the discussion meetings, and different opinions can also be debated at the meetings. The participants in the discussion meeting are selected according to the content of the special topic speech. After discussion, supplementation, and modification, the special topic speech can be written into an academic paper and published.

The symposium decided to establish a "Philosophy Study Committee for Natural Scientists." Everyone elected Guo Moruo, Li Siguang, Zhang Jiafu, Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, Pan Zinian, Qian Sanqiang, Zeng Zhaolun, Ai Siqi, Hua Luogeng, Hou Debang, Bei Shizhang and other twenty-eight people as members of the study committee, and Li Siguang as the director of the study committee. This study will begin in January 1955.

### 中国科学院邀请北京的自然科学家举行座谈会  讨论关于组织自然科学家学习哲学问题

1954-12-19
第3版()
专栏：

    中国科学院邀请北京的自然科学家举行座谈会
    讨论关于组织自然科学家学习哲学问题
    中国科学院学习委员会在十五日邀请了北京的科学研究机关和部分高等学校的自然科学家五十多人，举行座谈会，会上讨论了关于组织自然科学家学习马克思列宁主义哲学问题，并决定成立自然科学家哲学学习委员会。
    座谈会根据中国科学院学习委员会提出的“关于自然科学家学习马克思列宁主义哲学的计划（草案）”，进行了讨论，大家一致认为学习马克思列宁主义哲学是自然科学家当前的一个重要而迫切的任务。同时学习必须贯彻理论联系实际的原则，密切结合本门科学业务和我国社会主义建设的实际，学习应以自愿为原则。关于学习的内容和方法，大家认为可以结合本门科学业务进行专题研究。研究题目大体上可分以下四类：马克思列宁主义哲学的某些基本原理；以辩证唯物主义的基本原理研究本门科学的某些重要理论问题；以本门科学的研究成果来说明或丰富马克思列宁主义哲学的某些基本原理；以辩证唯物主义理论为武器来批判目前自然科学界中存在的一些错误的学术思想、观点及论著。学习方法以自学为主，着重独立钻研，必要时可以进行座谈、讨论和互相访问研究，并可举行不定期的、有准备的专题讨论会。科学家可以在讨论会上作专题研究的发言，不同意见也可以在会上展开争论。讨论会的参加人根据专题发言的内容来选定。专题发言经过讨论、补充、修改后，可以写成学术论文发表。
    座谈会上决定成立“自然科学家哲学学习委员会”，大家推选出郭沫若、李四光、张稼夫、陶孟和、竺可桢、吴有训、潘梓年、钱三强、曾昭抡、艾思奇、华罗庚、侯德榜、贝时璋等二十八人为学习委员会委员，李四光为学习委员会主任。这个学习将在一九五五年一月开始。
