Title: After Signing the US-Chiang "Treaty," the United States Gave Chiang Kai-shek Another Batch of Ships
Author:
Date: 1954-12-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 13th: The US authorities are providing Chiang Kai-shek with massive military aid in accordance with the war treaty signed with Chiang Kai-shek, in order to facilitate Chiang Kai-shek's war provocations against China. According to the United Press, the United States has decided to give Chiang Kai-shek twelve torpedo boats and two destroyers, of which six torpedo boats have recently been transferred to the Chiang Kai-shek navy, and the other six torpedo boats and two destroyers will be transferred before February next year. The Chiang Kai-shek navy will continue to send personnel to the United States on December 16 to receive training in driving these ships and receiving these ships. United Press also revealed that the United States recently transferred the second batch of Sabre jet aircraft to the Chiang Kai-shek air force. Currently, Chiang Kai-shek air force pilots are "undergoing intensive 'familiarization with aircraft performance' training" with the assistance of US officers.

### 美蒋“条约”签订后美国又给蒋贼一批舰艇

1954-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    美蒋“条约”签订后美国又给蒋贼一批舰艇
    【新华社十三日讯】美国当局正在根据同蒋贼签订的战争条约给予蒋贼大量的军事援助，以便于蒋贼对中国进行战争挑衅。据合众社透露，美国已经决定给蒋贼十二艘鱼雷艇和二艘驱逐舰，其中六艘鱼雷艇已经在最近移交给蒋贼海军，另外六艘鱼雷艇和两艘驱逐舰将在明年二月以前移交。蒋贼海军将在十二月十六日继续派遣人员到美国去受驾驶这些舰艇的训练和接收这些舰只。合众社还透露，美国还在最近把第二批佩刀式喷气飞机移交给蒋贼空军。目前，蒋贼空军驾驶员正在美国军官的协助下“进行紧张的‘熟悉飞机性能’的训练”。
