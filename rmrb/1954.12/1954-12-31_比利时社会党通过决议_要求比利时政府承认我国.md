Title: Belgian Socialist Party Passes Resolution Demanding Belgian Government Recognize Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-12-31
Page: 4

    Belgian Socialist Party Passes Resolution
    Demanding Belgian Government Recognize Our Country
    [Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Brussels] The Belgian Socialist Party, which jointly governs with the Belgian Liberal Party, held a congress on December 12th and 13th. At the meeting discussing Belgian foreign policy on the 13th, the congress passed a resolution by a vote of 694 to 9, which included a demand that Belgium recognize our country.

### 比利时社会党通过决议  要求比利时政府承认我国

1954-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    比利时社会党通过决议
    要求比利时政府承认我国
    【新华社讯】布鲁塞尔消息：和比利时自由党联合执政的比利时社会党在十二月十二日、十三日召开代表大会。在十三日讨论比利时对外政策的会议上，大会以六九四票对九票通过一项决议，其中要求比利时承认我国。
