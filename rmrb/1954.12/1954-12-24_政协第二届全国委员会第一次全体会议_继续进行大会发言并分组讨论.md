Title: The First Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC Continues with Plenary Speeches and Group Discussions
Author:
Date: 1954-12-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd: The First Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference continued with plenary speeches in the afternoon of the 23rd.

The following spoke at today's meeting: Chen Shutong, Ma Xulun, Situ Meitang, Luo Longji, Li Zhucheng, Xu Deheng, Guo Dihuo, Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain, Shen Yanbing, Shi Liang, Chen Qiyou, Li Zongen, Liang Xi, Liu Wenhui, Feng Youlan, Huang Qixiang, Jin Yuelin, Zhou Binglin, Dong Wenlong. The meeting lasted from 3:00 PM to 7:00 PM.

This morning, the members held group discussions. The meeting will continue on the 24th.

### 政协第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议  继续进行大会发言并分组讨论

1954-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    政协第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议
    继续进行大会发言并分组讨论
    【新华社二十三日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议在二十三日下午继续进行大会发言。
    在今天大会上发言的共有：陈叔通、马叙伦、司徒美堂、罗隆基、李烛尘、许德珩、郭棣活、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞、沈雁冰、史良、陈其尤、李宗恩、梁希、刘文辉、冯友兰、黄琪翔、金岳霖、周炳琳、董文隆。会议从下午三时进行到七时。
    今天上午，委员们曾进行分组讨论。会议将在二十四日继续进行。
