Title: China and India Sign Contract for China to Export Raw Silk to India
Author:
Date: 1954-12-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 17th: The China Silk Corporation and the Indian Central Silk Board signed a contract in New Delhi on the 15th for China to export raw silk to India. The contract stipulates that China will export 90 metric tons of raw silk to India before March 31, 1955, and stipulates that China will first export 50 metric tons to India before the end of this year.

### 我国和印度签订我国向印度输出生丝合同

1954-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和印度签订我国向印度输出生丝合同
    【新华社新德里十七日电】中国丝绸公司和印度中央生丝局十五日在新德里签订了中国向印度输出生丝的合同。合同规定在一九五五年三月三十一日以前，中国向印度输出生丝九十公吨，并且规定在今年年底以前，中国先向印度输出五十公吨。
