Title: Enlarged Meeting of the First Session of the CPPCC National Committee Standing Committee Held
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: The first session of the Standing Committee of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's political Consultative Conference held an enlarged meeting on the afternoon of the 27th. The meeting exchanged views on the "Conscription Law of the People's Republic of China (Draft)."

### 政协常委会第一次会议举行扩大会议

1954-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    政协常委会第一次会议举行扩大会议
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会常务委员会第一次会议在二十七日下午举行扩大会议。会议就中华人民共和国兵役法（草案）交换了意见。
