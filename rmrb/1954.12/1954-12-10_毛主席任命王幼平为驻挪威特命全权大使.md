Title: Chairman Mao Appoints Wang Youping as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Norway
Author:
Date: 1954-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th—Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China has appointed Wang Youping as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Norway.

### 毛主席任命王幼平为驻挪威特命全权大使

1954-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席任命王幼平为驻挪威特命全权大使
    【新华社九日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命王幼平为中华人民共和国驻挪威王国特命全权大使。
