Title: European Conference Held in Paris Against Paris Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-12-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, December 12th: A European conference was held in Paris on the 11th to discuss the problems arising from the plan to arm Germany and the prospects for general disarmament.
    Former French Prime Minister and Member of Parliament Edouard Daladier delivered the opening speech at the meeting. Political and public figures from France and several other Western European countries participated in the conference. Representatives from the Soviet Union and people's democratic countries were invited to attend, but were unable to do so due to not being granted entry visas.
　　The purpose of this conference was to oppose the ratification of the Paris Agreement.

### 巴黎举行欧洲会议反对巴黎协定

1954-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    巴黎举行欧洲会议反对巴黎协定
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社巴黎十二日讯：十一日巴黎举行欧洲会议，讨论因武装德国的计划所产生的问题和普遍裁减军备的前景。
    法国前总理和议会议员爱德华·达拉第在会上致开幕词。参加会议的有法国和其他几个西欧国家的政治和公众领袖。苏联和人民民主国家的代表曾被邀请参加会议，但是由于不能得到入境签证而未能参加。
　　这个会议召开的目的是反对批准巴黎协定。
