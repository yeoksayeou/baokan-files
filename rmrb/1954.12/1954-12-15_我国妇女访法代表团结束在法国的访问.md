Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Ends Visit to France
Author:
Date: 1954-12-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency:

    The Chinese Women's Delegation to France, headed by Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, concluded its ten-day visit to France and left Paris by plane on the 12th to return to China.

    During its visit to France, the delegation met with many public figures as well as well-known figures in the cultural and artistic circles and scientific and educational circles, and was warmly welcomed and received by people from all walks of life in France. People from all walks of life in France were greatly excited about the first visiting delegation from People's China.

    During the delegation's visit to France, Mrs. Ripkowsky and Focinal, members of the French National Assembly, Mr. Sarrailh, President of the University of Paris, Mrs. Gordon, Chairman of the French Women's Union, Professor De Lescure, Chairman of the France-China Friendship Association, Ms. Pleunon, Principal of the Sèvres Higher Normal School for Girls, and Judge Lagrance held receptions or banquets to welcome the Chinese delegation.

    The delegation visited the French National Assembly and the Senate of the Republic, the Paris City Hall, and other places.

    Before leaving, the delegation held a reception. More than 100 people, including Mrs. Joliot-Curie, attended the reception. Zhang Yun, the head of the delegation, delivered a speech at the meeting. She first expressed her gratitude to the French Reception Committee for its invitation and the enthusiastic reception from all walks of life. She believed that the visit had enhanced the friendship and understanding between the people of China and France. She expressed the hope that the people of the two countries would strengthen friendly cooperation, cultural exchanges, and trade, and stated that the Chinese people welcome people from all walks of life in France to visit China in order to further enhance understanding and friendship between the people of the two countries.

### 我国妇女访法代表团结束在法国的访问

1954-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我国妇女访法代表团结束在法国的访问
    据新华社讯：以中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴为首的中国妇女访法代表团在法国访问十天后，已经在十二日乘飞机离巴黎返国。
    代表团在访法期间会见了很多公众领袖以及文化艺术界和科学教育界知名人士，受到法国各阶层人民热情的欢迎和接待。法国各阶层人民对于来自人民中国的第一个访问代表团感到极大的兴奋。
    在代表团访法期间，法国国民议会议员里普科夫斯基夫人和弗西纳尔、巴黎大学校长沙拉伊、法兰西妇女联盟主席戈登夫人、法中友好协会主席德莱希教授、赛佛女子高级师范学校校长普勒侬女士和法官拉格朗斯夫人等曾举行招待会或宴会欢迎中国代表团。
    代表团曾参观了法国国民议会和共和国参议院、巴黎市政府等地。
    代表团在临行前举行招待会。出席招待会的有约里奥—居里夫人等一百余人。代表团团长章蕴在会上致词，她首先对法国接待委员会的邀请和各界人士的热情接待表示感谢。她认为代表团这次访问增进了中法两国人民的友谊和了解。她希望两国人民加强友好合作、文化交流和贸易往来，并且表示中国人民欢迎法国各界人士访问中国，以进一步增进两国人民间的了解和友谊。
