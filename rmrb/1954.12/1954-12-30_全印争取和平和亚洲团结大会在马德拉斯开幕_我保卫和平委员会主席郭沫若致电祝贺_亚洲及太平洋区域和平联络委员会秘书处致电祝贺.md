Title: All-India Congress for Peace and Asian Solidarity Opens in Madras; Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Sends Congratulatory Telegram; Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1954-12-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th: The All-India Congress for Peace and Asian Solidarity is scheduled to open in Madras on the 30th. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, sent a telegram to the congress on the 29th to express congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows: To the All-India Peace Council, forwarding to the All-India Congress for Peace and Asian Solidarity:

	On behalf of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and all the Chinese people, I extend my heartfelt congratulations and highest respect to the Congress!

	Peace defenders and people of all countries in the world highly value the great contributions made by the Indian peace forces in safeguarding Asian and world peace. I am deeply convinced that this congress, representing the Indian people's will for peace, will play a greater role in further strengthening the unity of the entire Indian people in the struggle to defend and consolidate Asian and world peace.

	Our peace-loving Chinese and Indian people have a traditional and profound friendship. This friendship has been further developed recently due to the continuous growth of economic and cultural exchanges between our two countries, as well as the mutual visits of the two Prime Ministers. The Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence put forward in the Joint Statement of the Chinese and Indian Prime Ministers have been warmly welcomed and supported by Asian countries and world opinion.

	We Asian people love independence and freedom. The Chinese people eagerly hope to unite more closely with the Indian people and other Asian countries to work together to oppose war and expand the zone of peace, and to establish collective peace and security in Asia and the world.

	I wish the Congress complete success!

	Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Guo Moruo
	December 29, 1954

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th: The Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee sent a telegram on the 29th to congratulate the opening of the All-India Congress for Peace and Asian Solidarity. The text of the telegram is as follows:

	"We are pleased to learn of the convening of the All-India Congress for Peace and Asian Solidarity and extend our warm congratulations to the Congress.

	We believe that this congress will make an important contribution to promoting the unity of the people of Asian countries and safeguarding the cause of peace in Asia and the world.

	We wish the Congress a complete success!"

### 全印争取和平和亚洲团结大会在马德拉斯开幕  我保卫和平委员会主席郭沫若致电祝贺  亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处致电祝贺
郭沫若
1954-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    全印争取和平和亚洲团结大会在马德拉斯开幕
    我保卫和平委员会主席郭沫若致电祝贺
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处致电祝贺
    【新华社二十九日讯】　全印争取和平和亚洲团结大会订于三十日在马德拉斯开幕，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若二十九日打电报给大会表示祝贺。电文如下：全印和平理事会转全印争取和平和亚洲团结大会：
    我谨代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和全中国人民向大会致以衷心的祝贺和崇高的敬意！
    和平保卫者和世界各国人民非常重视印度和平力量在维护亚洲和世界和平的活动中所作出的巨大贡献。我深信，这次代表印度人民和平意志的大会，对进一步加强团结全印度人民为保卫与巩固亚洲及世界和平的斗争，定将起更重大的作用。
    我们热爱和平的中印两国人民是有着传统的深厚友谊的，这种友谊特别在最近由于我们两国不断增长的经济与文化的交往，以及两国总理的相互访问，得到了进一步的发展。中印两国总理联合声明中所提出的和平共处五项原则已得到亚洲各国和世界舆论的热烈欢迎与支持。
    我们亚洲人民是热爱独立与自由的。中国人民热望与印度人民及其他亚洲各国人民更紧密地团结起来，为反对战争和扩大和平地区，为建立亚洲与世界的集体和平和安全而共同努力。
    祝大会胜利成功！
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席  郭沫若
    一九五四年十二月二十九日
    【新华社二十九日讯】　亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处二十九日打电报祝贺全印争取和平和亚洲团结大会的召开。电文如下：
      “欣闻全印争取和平和亚洲团结大会召开，谨向大会致以热烈的祝贺。
    我们相信，此次大会对于促进亚洲各国人民的团结和卫护亚洲以及世界的和平事业，将作出重要的贡献。
    祝大会圆满成功！”
