Title: Czechoslovak New National Assembly Holds First Meeting; Deputy Fierlinger Unanimously Elected Chairman of the National Assembly
Author:
Date: 1954-12-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 14th: The new National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic, elected on November 28th, held its first meeting in Prague on the 13th.

    Czechoslovak Republic Premier Široký, Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee First Secretary Novotný, First Deputy Premier Ďurianský and Čepička, as well as other leaders of the Party and government, attended the meeting.

    The opening ceremony of the meeting was presided over by Fierlinger, the chairman of the previous National Assembly. He presented to the National Assembly the list of the new government of the Republic, which was appointed by President Zápotocký on the 12th.

    At the meeting, all members of the National Assembly took an oath of allegiance to the Czechoslovak Republic and its people's democratic system in accordance with the provisions of the Constitution.

    The meeting unanimously elected Deputy Fierlinger as the Chairman of the current National Assembly.

### 捷克斯洛伐克新的国民议会举行首次会议  一致选举议员费林格为国民议会主席

1954-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克新的国民议会举行首次会议
    一致选举议员费林格为国民议会主席
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：十一月二十八日大选产生的捷克斯洛伐克共和国新的国民议会十三日在布拉格举行首次会议。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记诺沃提尼、第一副总理杜兰斯基和契皮茨卡，以及党和政府的其他领导人都出席了会议。
    上届国民议会主席费林格主持了会议的开幕式。他向国民议会提出了十二日经萨波托斯基总统任命的共和国新政府的名单。
    会上，国民议会全体议员依照宪法的规定宣誓效忠捷克斯洛伐克共和国和它的人民民主制度。
    会议一致选举了议员费林格为本届国民议会主席。
