Title: Albanian Photo Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th: An Albanian People's Republic photo exhibition opened at the Waterside Pavilion in Zhongshan Park, Beijing, on November 29th—the 10th anniversary of Albania's liberation. The exhibition is sponsored by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations.

    Among those attending the opening ceremony were Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; He Wei, Assistant Minister; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Yang Hansheng, Vice President; Wu Yuzhang, President of Renmin University of China; and representatives from various people's organizations, as well as figures from the capital's cultural and artistic circles and the press.

    Albanian Ambassador to China, Nasse, attended the opening ceremony.

    Also attending the opening ceremony were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

    Ambassador Nasse spoke first at the opening ceremony, saying: Our two peoples are separated by thousands of kilometers, but they are walking shoulder to shoulder on a common road. The exhibition opening today demonstrates the great desire of our two peoples to understand each other, and the eagerness to become familiar with the peaceful construction achievements of a distant brotherly country. Ambassador Nasse concluded by wishing that cultural cooperation between the two countries would be strengthened day by day, and hailed the friendship between the Chinese and Albanian people.

    Vice Minister of Culture Ding Xilin then spoke, pointing out that this rich and significant photo exhibition will enable the Chinese people to have a greater understanding of the great socialist construction being carried out by the Albanian people, and wished the exhibition success and the increasing development of cultural exchanges between China and Albania.

    The exhibition displays over one hundred photographs, introducing the construction achievements of the Albanian people in various fields.

### 阿尔巴尼亚图片展览会在北京开幕

1954-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚图片展览会在北京开幕
    【新华社二十九日讯】阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国图片展览会于十一月二十九日——阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年纪念日，在北京中山公园水榭开幕。展览会由对外文化联络局主办。
    出席展览会开幕式的，有文化部副部长丁西林，外交部副部长伍修权、部长助理何伟，对外文化联络局局长洪深，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长阳翰笙，中国人民大学校长吴玉章，以及各人民团体代表，首都文化艺术界、新闻界人士多人。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻我国大使纳赛出席了展览会开幕式。
    出席展览会开幕式的，还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
  纳赛大使在开幕式上首先讲话，他说：我们两国人民相隔几千公里，但他们肩并肩地走着共同的道路。今天开幕的这个展览会，表示出我们两国人民相互了解的伟大愿望，热望熟悉遥远的兄弟国家的和平建设成就。纳赛大使最后祝两国的文化合作日益加强，并欢呼中阿两国人民的友谊万岁。
    文化部副部长丁西林接着讲话，他指出这个内容丰富、意义重大的图片展览会，将使中国人民对阿尔巴尼亚人民进行的伟大社会主义建设有更多的认识，并预祝展览会的成功和中阿两国之间文化交流的日益发展。
    展览会上展出一百多幅图片，介绍了阿尔巴尼亚人民各方面的建设成就。
