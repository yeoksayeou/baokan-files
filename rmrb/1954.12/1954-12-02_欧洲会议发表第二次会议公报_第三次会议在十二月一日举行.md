Title: European Conference Issues Second Communiqué; Third Meeting Held on December 1

Author:
Date: 1954-12-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: TASS Moscow, November 30th: The Moscow European countries conference discussing safeguarding peace and security in Europe issued a communiqué.

On November 30th, the European conference discussing safeguarding peace and security in Europe held its second meeting. The head of the Polish delegation, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, Józef Cyrankiewicz, presided.

The meeting heard speeches from the head of the Czechoslovak delegation, Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic, Viliam Široký, and the head of the German Democratic Republic delegation, Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl.

The next meeting is scheduled for 2:00 PM on December 1st.

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: TASS Moscow, December 1st: The third meeting of the European countries conference on safeguarding peace and security in Europe was held at 2:00 PM on the 1st.

The head of the German Democratic Republic delegation, Otto Grotewohl, presided.

The head of the Albanian People's Republic delegation, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Albanian People's Republic, Mufti, spoke at the meeting.

### 欧洲会议发表第二次会议公报  第三次会议在十二月一日举行

1954-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    欧洲会议发表第二次会议公报
    第三次会议在十二月一日举行
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十一月三十日讯：一九五四年十一月三十日讨论保障欧洲和平和安全的莫斯科欧洲国家会议发表会议公报。
    十一月三十日，讨论保障欧洲和平和安全的欧洲会议举行第二次会议。波兰代表团团长、波兰人民共和国部长会议主席西伦凯维兹担任主席。
    会议听取了捷克斯洛伐克代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理威廉·西罗基和德意志民主共和国代表团团长、德意志民主共和国总理奥托·格罗提渥的发言。
    下次会议定在十二月一日下午二时举行。
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科一日讯：欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全会议第三次会议于一日下午二时举行。
    担任主席的是德意志民主共和国代表团团长奥托·格罗提渥。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议第一副主席穆夫蒂乌在会上发了言。
