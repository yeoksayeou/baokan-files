Title: Johnson, Dean of Canterbury, Calls on Churches to Protest American War Mongering

Author:

Date: 1954-12-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, December 22nd: TASS, London, December 21st: Dr. Hewlett Johnson, Dean of Canterbury, said on the 19th that the Church must protest the decision of the Atlantic Treaty nations to begin formulating plans for a hydrogen bomb war.

He asked, "Why is there so much talk about using these weapons? Who is threatening us? Certainly not the Soviet Union. The Soviet Union has never threatened war or the use of nuclear weapons."

Dr. Johnson pointed out that the Soviet Union has always advocated the prohibition of nuclear weapons. He said, "Montgomery has threatened to use these weapons. Which Russian general has threatened to use these weapons? Never."

Referring to the impact of war rhetoric on children and youth, Dr. Johnson pointed out that such rhetoric is not heard in the Soviet Union. He said, "In some parts of the world, children can grow up feeling completely safe. Why can't they here?"

### 约翰逊教长号召教会抗议美国战争叫嚣

1954-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    约翰逊教长号召教会抗议美国战争叫嚣
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十一日讯：英国坎特伯雷教长休勒特·约翰逊博士十九日说，教会必须抗议大西洋公约国家着手制订氢弹战争计划的决定。
    他问道：“为什么要这样大谈使用这些武器的问题呢？谁在威胁我们？当然不是苏联。苏联从来没有用战争或使用核子武器相威胁过。”
    约翰逊博士指出苏联一向主张禁止核子武器，他说：“蒙哥马利扬言要使用这些武器。那一个俄国将军扬言过要使用这些武器呢？从来没有。”
    约翰逊博士在谈到战争言论对儿童和青年的影响时指出，在苏联是听不到这种言论的；他说：“在世界上一部分地区，孩子们可以在充分感到安全的情况下成长起来。为什么在这里就不能呢？”
