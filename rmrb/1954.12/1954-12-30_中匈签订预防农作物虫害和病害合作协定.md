Title: China and Hungary Sign Cooperation Agreement on Preventing Crop Pests and Diseases
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency:

    China and Hungary signed a Cooperation Agreement on Preventing Crop Pests and Diseases in Beijing on the 28th. Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, signed on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, and Sikra Dán, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to the People's Republic of China, signed on behalf of the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic.

    The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Liao Tiren, Deputy Director of the General Administration of Commodity Inspection of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Xi Fengzhou, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Agriculture, Song Yanren, Deputy Director of the Plant Protection Bureau, and advisor Zhang Ruoqi. The Hungarian side included Csatordai, First Secretary of the Hungarian People's Republic Embassy in China, and Dőry, Attaché.

### 中匈签订预防农作物虫害和病害合作协定

1954-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈签订预防农作物虫害和病害合作协定
    【新华社讯】　中华人民共和国和匈牙利人民共和国预防农作物虫害和病害合作协定在二十八日在北京签订。代表中华人民共和国政府签字的是中华人民共和国农业部部长廖鲁言，代表匈牙利人民共和国政府签字的是匈牙利人民共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使希克拉丹。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的有外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、对外贸易部商品检验总局副局长廖体仁、农业部办公厅副主任席凤洲、植物保护局副局长宋彦人和顾问张若耆。匈牙利方面有匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书察托尔道伊和随员窦伯瑞。
