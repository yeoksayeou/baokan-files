Title: Czech Trade Delegation Leader Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-12-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 12th:
    The leader of the Czechoslovak Republic Trade Delegation, Czechoslovak Republic Minister of Foreign Trade, Mr. Dvorak, and others, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 12th.
    Those who greeted them at the airport included Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of our Ministry of Foreign Trade, Zhang Huadong, Director of the Import Bureau, Fu Shenglin, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau, and Li Zhuozhi, Commercial Counselor of our Embassy in the Czechoslovak Republic.
    Also greeting them at the airport were Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Konsala, and Commercial Counselor, Mr. Šal.
    Mr. Kitrich, Deputy Leader of the Czechoslovak Republic Trade Delegation, and Director of the Department of the Czechoslovak Republic Ministry of Foreign Trade, who arrived in our country last month, also came to greet them.

### 捷贸易代表团团长到京

1954-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    捷贸易代表团团长到京
    【新华社十二日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国贸易代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国对外贸易部部长德沃夏克等，在十二日乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场迎接的有我对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒、进口局局长张化东、出口局副局长傅生麟和我国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使馆商务参赞李琢之等。
    到机场迎接的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使康萨拉、商务参赞沙尔。
    在上月来到我国的捷克斯洛伐克共和国贸易代表团副团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国对外贸易部司长基特里赫等也前往迎接。
