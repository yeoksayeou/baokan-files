Title: China's New Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Cao Ying, Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1954-12-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 1st: Cao Ying, the newly appointed Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Czechoslovakia, arrived in Prague by plane on November 30th.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included: Iqi Ni, Director of the Protocol Department of the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Klička, Director of the Asian Department, Li Qingquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, and other embassy officials, as well as diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries to Czechoslovakia.

### 我国新任驻捷大使曹瑛到达布拉格

1954-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我国新任驻捷大使曹瑛到达布拉格
    【新华社布拉格一日电】中华人民共和国新任驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛，十一月三十日乘飞机到达布拉格。
    到机场欢迎的有：捷克斯洛伐克外交部交际司司长伊奇尼和亚洲司司长克利奇卡，中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆临时代办李清泉和大使馆其他官员，以及苏联和各人民民主国家驻捷外交使节等。
