Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Presents Manuscript of World Soil Map to Chinese Academy of Sciences
Author:
Date: 1954-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: At the Chinese Academy of Sciences' Executive Standing Committee meeting on the 9th, Corresponding Academician Kovda of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, entrusted by the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, presented a meticulously crafted manuscript of the World Soil Map from the Soil Institute of the Soviet Academy of Sciences to the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, accepted the gift.

In his presentation speech, Kovda said: "There are only two copies of this World Soil Map manuscript. One is in the Soviet Union, and the other is now being presented to the Chinese Academy of Sciences. This World Soil Map was created by Soviet soil scientists after the Patriotic War under the leadership of Academician I. P. Gerasimov of the Soviet Academy of Sciences. In creating this World Soil Map, Soviet soil scientists creatively applied the research results of soil scientists from around the world, including the Chinese soil data presented to the Soviet Academy of Sciences by the delegation from the Chinese Academy of Sciences to the Soviet Union in 1953. This World Soil Map scientifically proves that the world has abundant land resources and powerfully refutes the fallacy of scholars in imperialist countries who believe that the world's land resources are scarce."

Guo Moruo, on behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chinese scientific workers, expressed his gratitude to the Soviet Academy of Sciences. Guo Moruo said: "The Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences presenting such a precious gift to the Chinese Academy of Sciences is a concrete manifestation of the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences' recent suggestion for creative cooperation between Chinese and Soviet scientists."

### 苏联科学院赠我国科学院世界土壤图手稿

1954-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院赠我国科学院世界土壤图手稿
    【新华社九日讯】九日，在中国科学院的院务常务会议上，苏联科学院通讯院士科夫达受苏联科学院主席团的委托，将苏联科学院土壤研究所精心绘制的世界土壤图手稿一份赠送给中国科学院，由中国科学院院长郭沫若接受。
    科夫达在赠礼时致词说：这个世界土壤图手稿只有两份，一份在苏联，一份现在送给中国科学院。这个世界土壤图是苏联土壤学家在苏联科学院院士伊·普·格拉西莫夫领导下，在卫国战争后绘制的。苏联土壤学家在绘制这个世界土壤图时，创造性地应用了全世界土壤学家的研究成果，其中也包括了一九五三年中国科学院访苏代表团赠送苏联科学院的中国土壤资料。这个世界土壤图科学地证明世界上有着很丰富的土地资源，有力地驳斥了帝国主义国家的学者认为世界上土地资源贫乏的谬论。
    郭沫若代表中国科学院和中国科学工作者向苏联科学院致谢。郭沫若说：苏联科学院主席团把这样宝贵的礼物赠送给中国科学院，这是不久以前苏联科学院主席团建议中苏两国科学家进行创造性合作的具体表现。
