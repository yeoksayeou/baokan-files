Title: Closing of the Exhibition of Achievements in Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction
Author:
Date: 1954-12-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Exhibition of Achievements in Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction held in Beijing successfully closed on the 26th. From October 2nd to December 26th, a total of 2.76 million visitors from Beijing and other parts of the country came to visit. The exhibition also specially organized professional visits and learning sessions.

After the opening of the Exhibition of Achievements in Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction, Soviet experts sent by the Soviet government introduced and taught advanced experiences and science and technology to our audience. From the very beginning of the construction of the Soviet Exhibition Hall, our design and construction personnel, with the help of Soviet experts, learned advanced Soviet architectural science. After the opening of the exhibition, the Soviet government sent experts in various fields to impart much advanced experience and technology to our audience during the exhibition, including rich content in industry, agriculture, culture and art, and other aspects. The exhibition also received more than 1,000 questions from various places. Soviet experts carefully studied these questions and adopted various methods to meet the requirements of all parties, such as on-the-spot explanations, seminars, on-site guidance, writing letters, and sending instructions. The Soviet National Folk Dance Troupe and the Soviet National Moscow Stanislavsky and Nemirovich-Danchenko Musical Theatre came to China to hold grand performances in conjunction with the exhibition, exchanging experiences with Chinese drama, music, and dance circles. Soviet chefs from the Moscow Restaurant made ten reports to Chinese chefs, nutritionists, and public canteen managers on introducing their experience. They also went to the Beijing Hotel and the state-private joint venture Beijing Quanjude Roast Duck Restaurant to learn how to cook Chinese dishes.

During the exhibition, the sales department of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievement Exhibition received more than 1.4 million customers. Customers rushed to purchase more than 500 kinds of Soviet goods. More than 80,000 people tasted delicious Soviet food and wine in the Moscow Restaurant of the exhibition hall.

### 苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会闭幕

1954-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会闭幕
    据新华社讯：在北京举行的苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会二十六日胜利闭幕。从十月二日到十二月二十六日，北京和全国各地来京参观者达二百七十六万人。展览会还专门组织了专业参观和学习。
    苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会开幕后，苏联政府派来的各方面的专家，向我国观众介绍和传授了先进经验和科学技术。从苏联展览馆的建筑开始，我国设计人员和施工人员，就在苏联专家的帮助下，学习了苏联先进的建筑科学。在展览会开幕以后，苏联政府又派来了各方面的专家，在展览期间向我国观众传授了许多先进的经验和技术，包括工业、农业、文化艺术等各方面的丰富内容。展览会还接到各地提出要求解答的问题一千多件，苏联专家们认真研究了这些问题，并采取了当场讲解、座谈、到现场实地指导、写信、寄说明书等各种方法满足了各方面的要求。苏联国立民间舞蹈团、苏联国立莫斯科斯坦尼斯拉夫斯基与聂米罗维奇—丹钦科音乐剧院配合展览会来我国举行了盛大的演出，同我国戏剧、音乐、舞蹈界交流了经验。莫斯科餐厅的苏联厨师们向中国厨师、营养师、公共食堂管理人员作了十次介绍经验的报告。同时也曾到北京饭店和公私合营北京全聚德烤鸭店学习中国菜的烹调方法。
    展览期间，苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会售品处接待了一百四十多万顾客。顾客们竞购了五百多种苏联商品。有八万多人在展览馆莫斯科餐厅里尝到了美味的苏联酒菜。
