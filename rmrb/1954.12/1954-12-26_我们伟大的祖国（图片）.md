Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-12-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Workers at the state-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory are enthusiastically launching a labor emulation drive to comprehensively fulfill and overfulfill the state plan. This is a corner of the sliver doubling frame workshop. In the labor emulation drive, workers have reduced the rate of defective yarn by more than 60 percent, and the quality has significantly improved.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营北京第一棉纺织厂的职工正热烈展开劳动竞赛，为全面完成和超额完成国家计划而奋斗。这是钢丝并条车间的一角。工人们在劳动竞赛中减少了坏纱百分之六十多，质量有了显著提高。
