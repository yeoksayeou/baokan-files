Title: The State Council Held its Third Plenary Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-12-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 18th: The Third Plenary Meeting of the State Council of the People's Republic of China was held on the 16th. After listening to the explanation of Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, the meeting adopted the "Military Service Law of the People's Republic of China (Draft)". After listening to the explanation of Xu Liqing, Deputy Minister of the General Cadre Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the meeting adopted the "Regulations on Service of Officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (Draft)". After listening to the explanation of Lai Chuanzhu, Deputy Minister of the General Cadre Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the meeting adopted the "Regulations on Awarding Medals and Orders to Meritorious Personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army during the Chinese People's Revolutionary War (Draft)" and the "Proposal on Issuing Medals and Orders to Meritorious Personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in Defending the Motherland and Carrying out the Modernization of National Defense". After listening to the explanation of Jin Ming, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Finance of the People's Republic of China, the meeting adopted the "Regulations on National Economic Construction Bonds of 1955 (Draft)". The above-mentioned adopted documents will soon be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. In addition, the meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 国务院全体会议举行第三次会议

1954-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院全体会议举行第三次会议
    【新华社十八日讯】中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第三次会议在十六日举行。会议在听了国防委员会副主席聂荣臻的说明后，通过了中华人民共和国兵役法（草案）。会议在听了中国人民解放军总干部部副部长徐立清的说明后，通过了中国人民解放军军官服务条例（草案）。会议在听了中国人民解放军总干部部副部长赖传珠的说明后，通过了中华人民共和国授予中国人民解放军在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员的勋章、奖章条例（草案）和通过了关于颁发中国人民解放军在保卫祖国和进行国防现代化建设工作中有功人员勋章、奖章案。会议在听了中华人民共和国财政部副部长金明的说明后，通过了一九五五年国家经济建设公债条例（草案）。上述通过的各项文件，即将向全国人民代表大会常务委员会提出。此外，会议还通过了各项任免案。
