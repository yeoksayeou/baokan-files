Title: U.S. Supports Dutch and Australian Authorities in Strengthening Military Forces in West Irian
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 6th: The newspaper published a statement by Papale, a member of parliament representing the residents of West Irian. The statement said: With the support of the United States, the Netherlands and Australia are strengthening their military position in West Irian. Today, there are fifteen airports in West Irian, while there were only eight during the last World War. A considerable number of Dutch troops are stationed in West Irian.

### 美国支持荷、澳当局在西伊里安加强军事力量

1954-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    美国支持荷、澳当局在西伊里安加强军事力量
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社雅加达六日讯：报纸发表了代表西伊里安居民的国会议员帕帕勒的一篇声明。声明说：荷兰和澳大利亚在美国的支持下，正在西伊里安加强它们的军事地位。今天，西伊里安已有十五个飞机场，而在上次世界大战期间，只有八个飞机场。在西伊里安驻扎有相当多的荷兰军队。
