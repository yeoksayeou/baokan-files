Title: Mongolian Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1954-12-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 21st: The government trade delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, led by Mr. Batar, Minister of Trade of the Mongolian People's Republic, completed the Sino-Mongolian trade negotiations for 1955 in Beijing and signed the Protocol on Mutual Supply of Goods between China and Mongolia for 1955. They left Beijing by plane on December 21st to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Sun Chun, Deputy Director of the Import Bureau, Luo Baoyi, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau, and Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department.

Ochirbat, the Mongolian Ambassador to China, and Robsangchultum, Counselor, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 蒙古贸易代表团离京回国

1954-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古贸易代表团离京回国
    【新华社二十一日讯】蒙古人民共和国政府贸易代表团，在团长、蒙古人民共和国贸易部部长巴塔尔领导下，在北京完成了中蒙一九五五年贸易谈判并签订了中蒙两国一九五五年互相供应货物的议定书后，于十二月二十一日乘飞机离京返国。
    到机场送行的有我对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒、进口局副局长孙纯、出口局副局长罗抱一、交际处处长杨勉等人。
    蒙古驻华大使奥其尔巴特、参赞罗布桑楚勒图姆等也前往机场欢送。
