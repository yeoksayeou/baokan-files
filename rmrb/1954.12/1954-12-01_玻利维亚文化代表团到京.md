Title: Bolivian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the four-member Bolivian cultural delegation arrived in Beijing by plane on the 29th for a visit to China. The delegation includes psychology professor Alvarado, surgeon Vilarroel, architect Ferrufino and his wife.

### 玻利维亚文化代表团到京

1954-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚文化代表团到京
    【新华社三十日讯】应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的玻利维亚文化代表团四人在二十九日乘飞机到达北京。代表团有心理学教授阿尔瓦拉多，外科医师维拉罗埃尔，建筑师费鲁芬诺和夫人。
