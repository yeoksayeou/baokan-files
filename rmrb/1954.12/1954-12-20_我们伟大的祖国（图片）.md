    Our Great Motherland
    The Qiao Bei Great Embankment is located on the north side of Xiqiao Mountain in Nanhai County, Guangdong Province. The construction of this project can solve the drainage problems of more than 70,000 mu of farmland and prevent 210,000 mu of farmland within the embankment from being threatened by floods.
