Title: Cuban Poet Guillén Accepts Stalin International Peace Prize

Author:
Date: 1954-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to TASS, on the afternoon of December 24th, the award ceremony for the Stalin International Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Peoples" was held in the Hall of Columns of the Trade Union Building for the Cuban poet Guillén. Academician Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the Committee for Stalin International Prizes "For Strengthening Peace Among Peoples," presented the poet with a gold medal and a certificate on behalf of the committee, congratulating him and wishing him new achievements in his work and struggle for peace.

Soviet writers Tikhonov and Ehrenburg, Chinese female writer Ding Ling, Brazilian writer Amado, and Chilean poet Neruda extended warm congratulations to Guillén.

After receiving the award, Guillén delivered a speech. He said: Receiving the Stalin International Prize is a lofty honor, which not only fills me with gratitude but also places responsibility upon me.

I do not wish to see war again. Therefore, my poems speak of peace, an active and alert peace. On behalf of myself and my people, I express my gratitude to you, my friends, for this award, which belongs mainly to the Cuban people rather than any individual.

### 古巴诗人纪廉接受斯大林国际和平奖金

1954-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴诗人纪廉接受斯大林国际和平奖金
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：十二月二十四日下午，在工会大厦圆柱大厅举行了古巴诗人纪廉接受“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金的受奖仪式。“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会主席斯科贝尔琴院士代表委员会把一枚金质奖章和一份奖状授给这位诗人，向他表示庆贺，并且祝他在工作和争取和平的斗争中获得新的成就。
    苏联作家吉洪诺夫和爱伦堡、中国女作家丁玲、巴西作家亚马多和智利诗人聂鲁达向纪廉表示热烈的祝贺。
    纪廉受奖后发表了演说。他说：获得斯大林国际奖金是一种崇高的荣誉，它不仅使我充满感激之情，而且对我赋与责任。
    我不希望再看到战争。因此我的诗谈的是和平，一种积极的警醒的和平。我代表我自己和我国人民对这种主要是属于古巴人民而不是任何个人的奖赏向你们，我的朋友们表示感激。
