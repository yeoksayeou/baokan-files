Title: The Seventh National Congress of the Franco-Soviet Association Opens in Paris; General Petit States He Will Increase Exchanges with the Soviet Union and Strive to Safeguard Peace
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 11th Dispatch: According to a TASS report from Paris: December 10th marks the 10th anniversary of the signing of the Franco-Soviet Treaty of Alliance and Mutual Assistance and the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Franco-Soviet Association. On this day, the Seventh National Congress of the Franco-Soviet Association opened in Paris. Five hundred delegates from all over France attended the congress. A delegation from the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries also attended the congress.

    On the eve of the opening of the congress, General Petit, Vice-Chairman of the Franco-Soviet Association and Member of the Council of the Republic, pointed out in his answer to "l'Humanité" about the basic tasks of this congress and the development prospects of the Franco-Soviet Association: The Seventh National Congress of the Franco-Soviet Association must become a turning point: considering the role the Soviet Union is currently playing in the world, and considering that the world's attention is focused on France. Now is the time to appeal to the broad masses of the French people and ask them to participate in many aspects of the association's activities. Our goal is to restore and develop various different ways of interacting with the Soviet Union. Obviously, we should make special efforts to safeguard peace and establish an alliance with the Soviet Union and other countries.

### 法苏协会第七届全国代表大会在巴黎开幕  柏蒂将军表示将同苏联多多交往努力保障和平

1954-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    法苏协会第七届全国代表大会在巴黎开幕
    柏蒂将军表示将同苏联多多交往努力保障和平
     【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社巴黎讯：十二月十日是法苏同盟互助条约签订十周年和法苏协会成立十周年的纪念日。这一天，法苏协会第七届全国代表大会在巴黎开幕。出席大会的，有来自法国各地的五百个代表。出席大会的，还有苏联对外文化协会的代表团。
    大会开幕前夕，法苏协会副主席、共和国参议院议员柏蒂将军在回答“人道报”关于这次代表大会的基本任务和法苏协会发展前景的问题时指出：法苏协会第七届全国代表大会必须成为一个转折点：考虑到苏联目前在全世界所起的作用，并且考虑到全世界的注意力集中在法国身上。现在是向法国广大人民发出呼吁、要求他们参加协会许多方面的活动的时候了。我们的目的是要使得同苏联交往的各种不同的办法恢复起来，并且有所发展。显然，我们应该特别努力来保障和平，来同苏联及其他国家建立一个联盟。
