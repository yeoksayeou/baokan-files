Title: Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Soviet Embassy in China Thank Sympathies for Vyshinsky's Death
Author:
Date: 1954-12-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th (TASS): The Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced: The Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs expresses its gratitude to all organizations and individuals who sent telegrams to Soviet government agencies to express condolences on the death of Andrei Yanuarevich Vyshinsky, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

To the "People's Daily"

    The Embassy of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics in the People's Republic of China expresses its gratitude to all organizations and individuals who came to offer condolences on the death of Andrei Yanuarevich Vyshinsky, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.
            Beijing, December 18, 1954

### 苏联外交部和苏联驻华大使馆  感谢对维辛斯基逝世的吊唁

1954-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联外交部和苏联驻华大使馆
    感谢对维辛斯基逝世的吊唁
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科十六日电：苏联外交部公告：苏联外交部向所有致电苏联政府各机构吊唁苏联外交部第一副部长安·扬·维辛斯基逝世的组织和人士，表示感谢。
    致“人民日报”
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻中华人民共和国大使馆特向为苏联外交部第一副部长安·扬·维辛斯基逝世前来吊唁的各个组织和个人表示谢意。
            一九五四年十二月十八日，北京
