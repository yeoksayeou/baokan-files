Title: Joint Communiqué of the Negotiations between the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China and the Government Delegation of the Republic of Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1954-12-30
Page: 1

    The Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China and the Government Delegation of the Republic of Indonesia held preliminary negotiations in Beijing from November 2 to December 23, 1954, regarding the issue of dual nationality.

    The Chinese Government was represented in the negotiations by: Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China; Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs and Director of the Asian Department; Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Asian Department; and Lin Zhaonan, Deputy Commissioner.

    The Indonesian Government was represented in the negotiations by: Arnold Mononutu, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Indonesia to the People's Republic of China, Head of the Delegation of the Government of the Republic of Indonesia; Members: Dr. Sahardjo, Director of Constitutional Affairs, Ministry of Justice of the Republic of Indonesia; Dr. Thaib Napis, Deputy Director of the Legal Department, Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Indonesia; and Mr. Borsiek, Member and Secretary, Head of the China and East Asia Section, Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Indonesia.

    The two delegations conducted full consultations based on the principles of equality and mutual benefit, and in a spirit of mutual understanding, and successfully concluded the preliminary negotiations.

    Both delegations unanimously agreed that the preliminary negotiations have provided a good foundation for the ministerial-level negotiations on the issue of dual nationality to be held between the two countries in the future.

    The negotiations were conducted in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere from beginning to end.

### 中华人民共和国政府代表团和印度尼西亚共和国政府代表团谈判联合公报

1954-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国政府代表团和印度尼西亚共和国政府代表团谈判联合公报
    中华人民共和国政府代表团和印度尼西亚共和国政府代表团，已于一九五四年十一月二日至一九五四年十二月二十三日在北京举行了关于双重国籍问题的初步谈判。
    中华人民共和国政府方面参加谈判的有：中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫、外交部部长助理兼亚洲司司长陈家康、亚洲司副司长章文晋、副专员林兆南。
    印度尼西亚共和国政府方面参加谈判的有：印度尼西亚共和国政府代表团团长印度尼西亚驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使阿诺德·莫诺努图，团员：印度尼西亚共和国司法部宪法司司长沙哈尔佐博士、印度尼西亚共和国外交部法律司副司长塔伊布·纳皮斯博士和团员兼秘书印度尼西亚共和国外交部中国和东亚科科长伯锡克先生。
    两国代表团根据平等互利的原则和互相谅解的精神，进行了充分的协商，圆满地结束了初步谈判。
    两国代表团一致认为：此次初步谈判已经为将来两国所要举行的关于双重国籍问题的部长级谈判，提供了良好的基础。
    谈判自始至终是在友好融洽的气氛中进行的。
