Title: CPSU Central Committee and Others Congratulate Zápotocký on His Seventieth Birthday; Zápotocký Awarded the "Order of Building Socialism in the Homeland"
Author:
Date: 1954-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 19th: According to TASS, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, sent telegrams to the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Antonín Zápotocký, on the 19th, congratulating him on his seventieth birthday.

In their congratulatory telegram, the CPSU Central Committee and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union wished him new achievements in consolidating and developing the people's democratic system in Czechoslovakia, improving the material welfare of the working people, and strengthening the unbreakable friendship between the peoples of Czechoslovakia and the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 21st: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, on the occasion of the seventieth birthday of Antonín Zápotocký, President of the Czechoslovak Republic, the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic awarded him the "Order of Building Socialism in the Homeland" in recognition of his outstanding contributions to the socialist construction of Czechoslovakia.

### 苏共中央等祝贺萨波托斯基七十寿辰  萨波托斯基荣获“建设社会主义祖国勋章”

1954-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央等祝贺萨波托斯基七十寿辰
    萨波托斯基荣获“建设社会主义祖国勋章”
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十九日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议、苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，十九日分别致电捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基，祝贺他的七十寿辰。
    苏共中央和苏联部长会议在贺电中预祝他在巩固和发展捷克斯洛伐克的人民民主制度、增进劳动人民的物质福利和加强捷苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊的工作中取得新的成就。
    【新华社布拉格二十一日电】 据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社消息：在捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基七十寿辰的时候，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府授予他以“建设社会主义祖国勋章”，以表彰他对捷克斯洛伐克社会主义建设的卓越贡献。
