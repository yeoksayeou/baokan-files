Title: China's Newly Appointed Ambassador to Bulgaria, Zhou Zhuan, Arrives in Sofia
Author:
Date: 1954-12-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, December 3rd: Zhou Zhuan, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Bulgaria, arrived in Sofia by plane on the 2nd.
    Those who welcomed him at the airport included Alekseev, Director of the Protocol Department of the Bulgarian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Glekov, Director of the Second Department, Gong Ting, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Bulgaria, and embassy staff.
    Park Sung-chul, Chargé d'affaires of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Bulgaria, also welcomed him at the airport.

### 我国新任驻保大使周竹安到达索非亚

1954-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我国新任驻保大使周竹安到达索非亚
   【新华社三日讯】索非亚消息：中华人民共和国新任驻保加利亚人民共和国特命全权大使周竹安，二日乘飞机到达索非亚。
    到机场欢迎的有保加利亚外交部交际司司长阿列克谢耶夫和第二司司长格莱科夫、中国驻保加利亚大使馆临时代办宫亭和使馆人员等。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻保公使朴成哲也到机场欢迎。
