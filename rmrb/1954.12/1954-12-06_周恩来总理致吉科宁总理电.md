Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's Telegram to Premier Kekkonen
Author:
Date: 1954-12-06
Page: 1

To His Excellency Urho Kekkonen, Premier of the Republic of Finland:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of Finland, I extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency, the Government and the people of the Republic of Finland, and wish the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Finland will be further consolidated and developed.

    Zhou Enlai
    Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
    Beijing, December 5, 1954

### 周恩来总理致吉科宁总理电

1954-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理致吉科宁总理电芬兰共和国总理乌尔霍·吉科宁阁下：
    值此芬兰共和国国庆之际，谨向阁下、向芬兰共和国政府和人民致热烈的祝贺，并祝中芬两国的友好合作关系日趋巩固和发展。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
    一九五四年十二月五日于北京
