    Our Great Motherland
    Taiyuan Heavy Machinery Plant is actively preparing for next year's capital construction projects. The plant's total investment and construction area next year will be larger than any previous year. This is a portion of the steel being prepared for next year's capital construction, and management personnel are taking inventory.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    太原重型机器厂积极为明年基本建设工程进行准备工作。这个厂明年的全部工程投资和建筑面积都将比以往任何一年大。这是为明年基本建设准备的一部分钢材，管理人员正在清点。
