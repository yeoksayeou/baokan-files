Title: Prime Minister U Nu Visits Various Places in Northeast China
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 6th—Burmese Prime Minister U Nu left Beijing by plane this morning to visit various places in Northeast China. Accompanying Prime Minister U Nu to the Northeast were: U Min Din, Judge of the Supreme Court of the Union of Burma; U Win Pe, Prime Minister's Secretary and Cabinet Secretary; U Than, Prime Minister's Secretary; Colonel Aung Gyi, Staff Officer of the Army Department; Captain Bi So, Prime Minister's Aide-de-Camp; and three other accompanying personnel. Yao Zhongming, Chinese Ambassador to Burma, U La Maung, Burmese Ambassador to China, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied them. Those who came to the airport to see them off included: Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Wang Bingnan and Chen Jiakang, Assistant Ministers of Foreign Affairs, Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, and officials from the Burmese Embassy in China. Prime Minister U Nu's wife and the Prime Minister's private secretary, Du Qing Su, remained in Beijing.

### 吴努总理去东北各地参观

1954-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努总理去东北各地参观
    【新华社六日讯】　缅甸联邦总理吴努在六日晨乘飞机离开北京到东北各地参观。随同吴努总理前往东北的有：缅甸联邦最高法院法官吴敏登、总理秘书兼内阁秘书吴温佩、总理秘书吴丹、陆军部参谋昂季上校、总理侍从武官毕梭上尉和其他随行人员三人。我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明、缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂和我国外交部交际处处长王倬如等陪同前往。到机场欢送的有：中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部部长助理王炳南、陈家康，交际处副处长王拓和缅甸驻中国大使馆官员等人。吴努总理的夫人和总理私人秘书都庆素留在北京。
