Title: Philippine Liberation Army Launches Combat Operations
Author:
Date: 1954-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 10th: According to the "Manila Times," the Philippine People's Liberation Army launched combat operations on November 24th and 25th in the Manwang area of Nueva Vizcaya Province and the Mukamimi area of Pampanga Province. The Philippine government army suffered casualties.

### 菲律宾解放军展开战斗

1954-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾解放军展开战斗
    【新华社十日讯】据“马尼拉时报”消息，菲律宾人民解放军十一月二十四日和二十五日在新未示皆耶省的曼网地区和邦邦牙省的目加米米地区展开战斗。菲律宾政府军受到了伤亡。
