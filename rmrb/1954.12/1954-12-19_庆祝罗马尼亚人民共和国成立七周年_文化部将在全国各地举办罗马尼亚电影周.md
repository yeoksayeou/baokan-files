Title: To Celebrate the Seventh Anniversary of the Founding of the Romanian People's Republic, the Ministry of Culture Will Hold a Romanian Film Week Across the Country
Author:
Date: 1954-12-19
Page: 3

To Celebrate the Seventh Anniversary of the Founding of the Romanian People's Republic
The Ministry of Culture Will Hold a Romanian Film Week Across the Country

    The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China has decided to hold a "Romanian People's Republic Film Week" across the country recently to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic.

    The film week will be held in two batches. The first batch will be held in eleven cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Shenyang, Wuhan, Chongqing, Xi'an, Jinan, Nanjing, Kunming, Anshan, and Changchun, starting on the anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic on December 30; the second batch will be held in nine cities including Tianjin, Guangzhou, Taiyuan, Hangzhou, Nanning, Chengdu, Lanzhou, Dalian, and Harbin, starting on January 12, 1955.

    During the film week, the following films will be screened: "The Ideal Has Been Realized," which is set in the Romanian countryside before and after liberation, and expresses the hopes of the vast number of Romanian peasants moving towards socialism under the leadership of the working class through the growth of a young farmer. This film won the "Struggle for Social Progress Award" at the 7th International Film Festival. "In Our Village" tells the story of the struggle of Romanian working peasants, under the leadership of the Romanian Workers' Party, to establish collective farms. This film won the "Screenwriting Award" at the 6th International Film Festival.

    In addition, the color documentary "Romanian People's Republic" and the short documentaries "Happy Days" and "Road to Prosperity" will also be screened. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 庆祝罗马尼亚人民共和国成立七周年  文化部将在全国各地举办罗马尼亚电影周

1954-12-19
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚人民共和国成立七周年
    文化部将在全国各地举办罗马尼亚电影周
    中华人民共和国文化部决定最近在全国各地举办“罗马尼亚人民共和国电影周”，庆祝罗马尼亚人民共和国成立七周年。
    电影周先后分两批举行。北京、上海、沈阳、武汉、重庆、西安、济南、南京、昆明、鞍山、长春等十一城市自十二月三十日罗马尼亚人民共和国成立纪念日起举行；天津、广州、太原、杭州、南宁、成都、兰州、旅大、哈尔滨等九城市自一九五五年一月十二日起举行。
    电影周期间将放映下面这些影片：“理想实现了”是以罗马尼亚解放前后的农村为背景，通过一个青年农民的成长，表达了在工人阶级领导下向社会主义迈进的罗马尼亚广大农民的希望。这部影片曾荣获第七届国际电影节的“争取社会进步斗争奖”。“在我们的村子里”是叙述罗马尼亚劳动农民在罗马尼亚工人党领导下，为建立集体农庄所进行的斗争。这部影片曾获得了第六届国际电影节的“编剧奖”。
    此外还将放映彩色文献纪录片“罗马尼亚人民共和国”，短纪录片“快乐的日子”、“繁荣的道路”。　（新华社）
