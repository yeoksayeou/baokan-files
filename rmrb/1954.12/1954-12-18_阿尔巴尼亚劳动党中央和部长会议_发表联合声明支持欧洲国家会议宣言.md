Title: Albanian Labor Party Central Committee and Council of Ministers Issue Joint Statement Supporting Declaration of European Countries Conference

Author: 
Date: 1954-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 16th: The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania issued a joint statement on the European countries conference to guarantee peace and security in Europe.

The statement said that on December 14th, the joint meeting of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, after listening to the report on the work of the Moscow conference of European countries made by Manush Myftiu, head of the government delegation of the People's Republic of Albania participating in the Moscow conference, unanimously agreed to the results of the Moscow conference and the work of the government delegation of the People's Republic of Albania participating in the conference, as well as the declaration of the eight governments.

The statement said that the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania believe that the Moscow conference is a new and significant step by the Soviet Union and the participating countries in the struggle to maintain and consolidate peace and security in Europe, and that the decisions of the conference are fully in line with the vital interests and aspirations of the people of European countries.

The statement pointed out: The Albanian people are peace-loving people. Maintaining and consolidating peace in the Balkan Peninsula and Europe is a major issue of vital interest to them. They are resolutely opposed to the revival of German militarism. They will use all their strength to defend peace and security and victoriously build socialism.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央和部长会议  发表联合声明支持欧洲国家会议宣言

1954-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央和部长会议
    发表联合声明支持欧洲国家会议宣言
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社地拉那十六日讯：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议就欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全会议发表联合声明。
    声明说，十二月十四日，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议联席会议在听取了参加欧洲国家莫斯科会议的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长马努什·穆夫蒂乌所作的关于莫斯科会议的工作的报告后，一致同意莫斯科会议的结果和参加会议的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团的工作以及八国政府的宣言。
    声明说，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议认为，莫斯科会议是苏联和与会各国在维护和巩固欧洲和平和安全的斗争中的一个新的重大步骤，会议的决定是完全符合欧洲各国人民的切身利益和愿望的。
    声明指出：阿尔巴尼亚人民是爱好和平的人民。维持和巩固巴尔干半岛以及欧洲的和平对他们说来是攸关切身利害的重大问题。他们竭力反对复活德国军国主义。他们要用全副力量来保卫和平和安全，胜利地建设社会主义。
