Title: Closing Ceremony of the Moscow Musical Theatre Performance Held

Author: 
Date: 1954-12-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th: The closing ceremony of the visiting performance of the Soviet State Stanislavski and Nemirovich-Danchenko Moscow Musical Theatre was held in Beijing on December 29th.

The following were present at the closing ceremony: Vice Premiers of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, He Long, Chen Yi, and Li Fuchun; Vice Chairpersons of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Song Qingling, Li Jishen, and Guo Moruo; President of the Supreme People's Court, Dong Biwu; Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Zhang Dingcheng; Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Shen Yanbing, and Vice Minister, Qian Junrui; Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Lei Renmin; Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, He Wei; Deputy Directors of the Office for Receiving Soviet Exhibitions in China, Zhao Feng and Xiao Fangzhou; Council Member of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ge Baoquan; and well-known figures in the capital's literary and art circles, Ma Sicong, Zhou Weizhi, Li Ling, Wu Xiaobang, Chen Jinqing, and more than a thousand literary and art workers.

The Soviet side was represented by: Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, and embassy staff; Director of the Soviet State Moscow Musical Theatre, Polyakov, and the theatre's leading artists.

Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing delivered a speech on behalf of the Chinese literary and art circles at the closing ceremony, thanking the Soviet artists for their outstanding performances. He said that in the past two months, the Moscow Musical Theatre's 71 performances of operas, ballets, and concerts showcased the rich treasures and unparalleled achievements of socialist realism art to 110,000 audiences, leaving Chinese audiences with an unforgettable impression. The performances of the Moscow Musical Theatre will always be the most cherished part of their memories, from which they can draw tremendous inspirational strength. Shen Yanbing said: In addition to their very demanding performance schedule, the artists of the Moscow Musical Theatre also provided systematic and earnest assistance to our art workers through reports, seminars, and teaching. Our art workers are deeply moved by your highly "tireless in teaching" spirit, and in their future artistic endeavors, they will constantly recall your guidance and gain new benefits.

Director of the Soviet State Moscow Musical Theatre, Polyakov, then delivered a reply speech, saying: We have spent more than two months in the hospitable capital of the People's Republic of China. Here, we have performed operas and ballets, and have also seen the achievements of the People's China in the arts. Our contact with the Chinese working people and literary and art workers has enriched us even more. Due to our close and friendly relations with our Chinese comrades, we have a deeper understanding of our friends and a further recognition that the Chinese people are talented, hardworking, and unwavering in their belief in their bright future. On behalf of all the members of the theatre, Polyakov expressed his gratitude to the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese friends for their kind care and reception, and wished the friendship between the Soviet and Chinese peoples would last forever and develop and consolidate day by day.

After the closing ceremony, the artists of the Moscow Musical Theatre performed a scene from the opera "Taras' Family", a scene from the ballet "Shore of Happiness", and other wonderful song and dance programs. The Central Song and Dance Troupe Choir, the Central Experimental Opera House Orchestra, which had studied with the Soviet artists, and the theatre's choir and orchestra jointly performed parts of the musical program. Students from the Beijing Dance School also participated in the performance of some of the dance programs.

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th: The actors of the Soviet State Moscow Musical Theatre performed the ballet "Swan Lake" at the Tianqiao Theatre in Beijing on the evening of December 28th. This was the last performance of the Moscow Musical Theatre during its visit to our country.

Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liu Shaoqi, Premier of the State Council, Zhou Enlai, and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Guo Moruo, attended the performance.

Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, also attended the performance.

The outstanding performances of the Soviet artists were warmly welcomed. At the end of the performance, the Soviet artists received flowers from the capital's literary and art workers.

### 莫斯科音乐剧院举行演出闭幕式

1954-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科音乐剧院举行演出闭幕式
    【新华社二十九日讯】　苏联国立莫斯科斯坦尼斯拉夫斯基与聂米罗维奇—丹钦科音乐剧院二十九日在北京举行访问演出闭幕式。
    出席闭幕式的有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅、李富春，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、李济深、郭沫若，最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，中华人民共和国文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长钱俊瑞，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部部长助理何伟，接待苏联来华展览办公室副主任赵沨、萧方洲，中苏友好协会总会理事戈宝权，以及首都文艺界著名人士马思聪、周巍峙、李凌、吴晓邦、陈锦清和文艺工作者一千多人。
    苏联方面出席闭幕式的有：苏联驻华大使尤金和使馆人员，苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院院长波利雅科夫和剧院主要艺术家。
    文化部沈雁冰部长代表中国文艺界在闭幕式上致词，感谢苏联艺术家的出色的表演，他说，两个月中，莫斯科音乐剧院演出的七十一场歌剧、舞剧和音乐会，在十一万观众的面前展示了社会主义现实主义艺术的丰富宝藏和无比成就，给予中国的观众们以终身难忘的印象，莫斯科音乐剧院的表演将永远是他们记忆中最值得珍爱的，并且可以从中吸取巨大的鼓舞力量的一部分。沈雁冰说：莫斯科音乐剧院的艺术家们在十分紧张的演出任务之外，还通过报告、座谈、教课等方式，对我国的艺术工作者进行了系统和认真的帮助。我国的艺术工作者深深地为你们高度的“诲人不倦”的精神所感动，在他们今后的艺术劳动中将不断地回忆起你们的指点而得到新的益处。
    苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院院长波利雅科夫接着致答词，他说：我们在热情好客的中华人民共和国的首都度过了两个多月。在这里，我们进行了歌剧、舞剧的演出，也看到了人民中国在艺术方面的成就。我们与中国劳动人民和文艺工作者的接触，使我们更加充实了。由于与中国同志们亲密友好的联系，我们更深刻地了解了我们的朋友，更进一步认识到中国人民是天才的，爱好劳动的，对自己光辉的前途坚信不移的。波利雅科夫代表剧院全体人员对中华人民共和国文化部和中国朋友们给予他们的亲切关怀和接待表示感谢，并祝苏中两国人民的友谊永世长存和日益发展巩固。
    闭幕式结束后，莫斯科音乐剧院的艺术家表演了歌剧“塔拉斯的一家”、舞剧“幸福之岸”中的一幕及其他精采歌舞节目。曾经向苏联艺术家学习的中央歌舞团合唱队、中央实验歌剧院管弦乐团和剧院的合唱队、管弦乐团一同表演了部分的音乐节目，北京舞蹈学校的学生们也参加了部分舞蹈节目的表演。
    【新华社二十八日讯】　苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院的演员们二十八日晚在北京天桥剧场演出舞剧“天鹅湖”。这是莫斯科音乐剧院在我国访问中的最后一次演出。
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇、国务院总理周恩来、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若等到场观剧。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金也到场观剧。
    苏联艺术家们的卓越演出，受到热烈欢迎。在演出结束时，苏联艺术家们接受了首都文艺工作者的献花。
