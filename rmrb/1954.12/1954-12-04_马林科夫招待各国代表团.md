Title: Malenkov Receives Delegations from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 3rd: TASS Moscow, December 2nd: Georgy Maximilianovich Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, hosted a banquet in the Kremlin on the 2nd to receive the delegations from various countries attending the European countries' conference on ensuring European peace and security, which was held in Moscow from November 29th to December 2nd.

Attending the banquet were: the head of the delegation of the Polish People's Republic, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz, and delegation members: Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Naszkowski, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Polish People's Republic to the Soviet Union, Lewikowski; the head of the delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, Prime Minister Viliam Široký, and delegation members: Minister of Foreign Affairs V. David, Minister of Construction Industry E. Slechta, Minister of Health J. Plojhar, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic to the Soviet Union, J. Vosaglik; the head of the delegation of the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, delegation members: Deputy Prime Ministers Ulbricht, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Bolz, Deputy Prime Ministers Nuschke and Loch, General Secretary of the Democratic Farmers' Party of Germany, Ross, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union, Appelt; the head of the delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, András Hegedüs, and delegation members: Minister of Foreign Affairs János Boldoczki, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs András Szik, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to the Soviet Union, Ferenc Münnich; the head of the delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Chivu Stoica, and delegation members: Minister of Foreign Affairs Simion Bughici, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Grigore Preoteasa, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic to the Soviet Union, Rab; the head of the delegation of the Bulgarian People's Republic, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Anton Yugov, and delegation members: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Georgi Traykov, Minister of Foreign Affairs Mincho Neychev, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Bulgarian People's Republic to the Soviet Union, Karlo Lukanov; the head of the delegation of the Albanian People's Republic, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Manush Myftiu, and delegation members: Minister of Foreign Affairs Behar Shtylla, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Albanian People's Republic to the Soviet Union, M. Prifti; Observer of the People's Republic of China to the European Conference – Zhang Wentian, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Advisors Dong Yueqian and Wang Yutian.

The Soviet side was represented by: Comrades N.A. Bulganin, K.E. Voroshilov, L.M. Kaganovich, A.I. Mikoyan, V.M. Molotov, M.G. Pervukhin, M.Z. Saburov, N.S. Khrushchev, N.M. Shvernik, M.A. Suslov, A.N. Kosygin, V.A. Malyshev, I.F. Tevosyan, members of the Soviet delegation to the European Conference: First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union A.A. Gromyko, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation Ya.M. Puzanov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Ukrainian Republic N.T. Kalchenko, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Belorussian Republic K.T. Mazurov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Latvian Republic V.T. Latsis, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Estonian Republic A.A. Müürisepp, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Lithuanian Republic M.A. Gedvilas, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union V.A. Zorin; Marshals of the Soviet Union A.M. Vasilevsky and G.K. Zhukov, Air Marshal P.F. Zhigarev, Marshal of Artillery M.I. Nedelin, Admiral N.E. Basisty, Generals of the Army A.I. Antonov, G.K. Malandin and M.S. Malinin, General of the Army A.S. Zheltoy, Ambassadors Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to foreign countries N.P. Firyubin, N.A. Mikhailov, G.M. Pushkin, Director of TASS N.G. Palgunov, Editor-in-Chief of "Pravda" D.T. Shepilov, Editor-in-Chief of "Izvestia" K.A. Gubin, Members of the Collegium of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union M.V. Zimyanin, L.F. Ilyichev, V.S. Semenov, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union E.D. Kiselev, Deputy Director of the Third European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union M.G. Gribanov, and officials of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

Many people spoke at the banquet.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 马林科夫招待各国代表团

1954-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    马林科夫招待各国代表团
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二日讯：苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫二日在克里姆林宫设宴招待出席从十一月二十九日到十二月二日在莫斯科举行的欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全会议的各国代表团。
    出席宴会的有：波兰人民共和国代表团团长、部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹和代表团团员：外交部副部长纳斯科夫斯基和波兰人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使列维科夫斯基；捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团团长、总理威廉·西罗基和代表团团员：外交部长瓦·戴维，建筑工业部长埃·斯列赫塔，卫生部长约·普洛伊哈和捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻苏联特命全权大使雅·沃萨格里克；德意志民主共和国代表团团长、总理格罗提渥，代表团团员：副总理乌布利希，副总理兼外交部长博尔茨，副总理努舍克和洛赫，德国民主农民党总书记罗斯和德意志民主共和国驻苏联特命全权大使阿佩尔特；匈牙利人民共和国代表团团长、部长会议第一副主席安德拉希·赫格居斯和代表团团员：外交部长雅诺希·波尔多奇基，外交部副部长安德烈·西克和匈牙利人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使费伦茨·明尼契；罗马尼亚人民共和国代表团团长、部长会议第一副主席基伏·斯托伊卡和代表团团员：外交部长西米昂·布吉奇，外交部第一副部长格里戈勒·普利奥特亚萨和罗马尼亚人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使拉布；保加利亚人民共和国代表团团长、部长会议第一副主席安东·于哥夫和代表团团员：部长会议第一副主席格奥尔基·特莱科夫，外交部长明乔·涅伊切夫和保加利亚人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使卡洛·卢卡诺夫；阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国代表团团长、部长会议副主席马努什·穆夫蒂乌和代表团团员：外交部长贝哈尔·什图拉和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使米·普里夫蒂；中华人民共和国参加欧洲国家会议观察员——中华人民共和国外交部副部长张闻天，顾问董越千、王雨田。
