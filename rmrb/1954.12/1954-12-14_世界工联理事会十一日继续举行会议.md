Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Council Continues Meeting on the 11th
Author:
Date: 1954-12-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The World Federation of Trade Unions Council continued its meeting on the 11th.

At the morning session, World Federation of Trade Unions President Giuseppe Di Vittorio delivered a report on the second item on the agenda (The Charter of Trade Union Rights of Working People and the Launching of a Worldwide Movement to Defend and Expand Trade Union Rights and Democratic Rights).

During the afternoon and evening sessions, the discussion of Louis Saillant’s report continued and concluded on the evening of the 11th. More than thirty representatives of trade unions from Europe, Asia, the Americas, and Africa, who spoke during the discussion of Saillant's report, expressed their determination to fight to strengthen the united action of working people, to strive for peace and international security, and to oppose the revival of German militarism and the attempts to establish a dangerous hotbed of war in Europe. Representatives emphasized the need to strengthen the struggle for peace, to improve living and working conditions, and to strengthen the struggle to defend trade union rights and democratic freedoms.

### 世界工联理事会十一日继续举行会议

1954-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    世界工联理事会十一日继续举行会议
    据新华社讯：世界工会联合会理事会十一日继续举行会议。
    世界工联主席朱塞佩·维多里奥在上午的会议上就第二项议程（劳动人民的工会权利宪章和展开一个保卫和扩大工会权利和民主权利的世界范围的运动）作了报告。
    在下午和晚间的会议上，继续对路易·赛扬的报告进行讨论，这一讨论已经在十一日晚间结束。在讨论赛扬的报告时发言的三十多个欧洲、亚洲、美洲和非洲各国工会的代表，都表示了他们要为加强劳动人民的统一行动，争取和平和国际安全，反对复活德国军国主义和在欧洲建立危险的战争温床的企图而斗争的决心。代表们强调指出有必要加强争取和平、改善生活和劳动条件的斗争，加强争取保卫工会权利和民主自由的斗争。
