Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 9th. Moscow newspapers on the 9th all published, with about half a page, the statement issued by Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, regarding the so-called "Mutual Defense Treaty" signed between the United States and the Chiang Kai-shek clique.
