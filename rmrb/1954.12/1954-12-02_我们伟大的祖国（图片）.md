    Our Great Motherland
    The Han River Highway Bridge project, a component of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge project, has commenced construction. Upon completion, this bridge will connect with the highway section of the Yangtze River Bridge, linking the three towns of Wuhan together. This is a large batch of cement pipe piles used for the bridge construction.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    武汉长江大桥工程的组成部分——汉水公路桥工程已经开工。这座桥梁建成后，将同长江大桥公路部分衔接，把武汉三镇联接在一起。这是用以建设桥梁的大批水泥管桩。
