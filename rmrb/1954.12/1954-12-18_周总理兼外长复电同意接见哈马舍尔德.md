Title: Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Reply, Agreeing to Meet with Hammarskjöld
Author: Dag Hammarskjöld
Date: 1954-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th:

    Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Reply, Agreeing to Meet with Hammarskjöld
    [Xinhua News Agency, December 17th] Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai telegraphed United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on December 17th, agreeing to meet with Hammarskjöld in Beijing to discuss related issues. The full text of Premier Zhou Enlai's telegram to Hammarskjöld is as follows:

    To Mr. Hammarskjöld, Secretary-General of the United Nations, New York:

    Your telegram of December 10, 1954, expressing your desire to visit China, has been received.

    Regarding the American spy case mentioned in your telegram, our position has been detailed in a separate telegram.

    For the sake of peace and to ease international tensions, I am prepared to meet with you in our capital, Beijing, to discuss related issues.

    We welcome you to China. Please decide on a date yourself and notify us.

                                Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
                                    December 17, 1954, Beijing

        Attached: Telegram from United Nations Secretary-General to Premier Zhou Enlai

        To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai, Beijing:

    (4853) The United Nations General Assembly has requested me to endeavor to secure the release of the eleven United Nations Command personnel captured by Chinese forces on January 12, 1953, and all other detained United Nations Command prisoners of war. Considering all the facts and circumstances, the Secretary-General must personally assume special responsibility in this matter. Given my concern for this matter, I would appreciate the opportunity to discuss this matter with you in person. For this reason, I would like to ask if you could receive me in Beijing. I would suggest a visit shortly after December 26; if you accept my proposal, I would like to ask which date around that time would be suitable for you.

                                United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld
                              December 10, 1954, United Nations, New York

### 周总理兼外长复电同意接见哈马舍尔德
达格·哈马舍尔德
1954-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理兼外长复电同意接见哈马舍尔德
    【新华社十七日讯】我国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来于十二月十七日电复联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，同意在北京接见哈马舍尔德商谈有关各项问题。周恩来总理给哈马舍尔德的电报全文如下：纽约联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德先生：
    一九五四年十二月十日您表示要来中国访问的电报收到了。
    关于您来电中所提到的美国间谍案，我们的立场已详另电。
    为了和平，为了缓和国际紧张局势，我准备在我国的首都北京接见您，同您商谈有关的各项问题。
    我们欢迎您来中国，日期请您自己决定并通知我们。
                中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长　周恩来
                                    一九五四年十二月十七日北京
        附：联合国秘书长致周恩来总理的电报北京周恩来总理先生阁下：
    （四八五三）联合国大会已请我设法使一九五三年一月十二日被中国部队俘获的十一名联合国军人员和所有其他仍被拘留的联合国军被俘人员获得释放。考虑到一切事实和情况，在此事上秘书长必须亲自担负起特别的责任。鉴于我对此事的关心，我将欣赏获得机会来亲自同您商谈此事。为此理由，我愿请问，您是否能在北京接见我。我愿建议在十二月二十六日之后不久作一访问；如您接受我的提议，我愿请问，在那个时候左右的哪一个日期对您是合适的。
                               联合国秘书长　达格·哈马舍尔德
                              一九五四年十二月十日　纽约联合国
