Title: Albanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu Hosts Chinese Government Delegation to Celebrate the Signing of Long-Term Loan and Other Agreements Between China and Albania
Author:
Date: 1954-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 7th: Following the signing of the Long-Term Loan Agreement and the Exchange of Goods and Payments Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Albania in Tirana on December 3rd, Albanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu held a banquet to entertain all members of the Chinese Government Delegation.

Those attending the banquet included Labor Party and government leaders Hoxha, Lleshi, and others.

Government delegations from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries attending the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of Albania's liberation, as well as foreign envoys stationed in Albania, were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 庆祝中阿两国签订长期贷款等协定  阿部长会议主席谢胡招待我国政府代表团

1954-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝中阿两国签订长期贷款等协定
    阿部长会议主席谢胡招待我国政府代表团
    【新华社七日讯】地拉那消息：中华人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国关于长期贷款协定以及换货和付款协定于十二月三日在地拉那签字后，阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡特举行宴会，招待中华人民共和国政府代表团全体人员。
    出席宴会的有劳动党和政府的领导人霍查、列希等。
    苏联和各人民民主国家参加阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年庆祝典礼的政府代表团和驻阿尔巴尼亚的外交使节也应邀出席宴会。
