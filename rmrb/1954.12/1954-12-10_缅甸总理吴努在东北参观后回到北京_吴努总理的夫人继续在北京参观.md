Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Returns to Beijing After Visiting the Northeast; Prime Minister U Nu's Wife Continues Her Visit in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and his party returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 9th after visiting Northeast China. Yao Zhongming, China's Ambassador to Burma, U Hla Maung, Burma's Ambassador to China, and Wang Zhuo, Director of the Protocol Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who accompanied Prime Minister U Nu on his visit to the Northeast, returned to Beijing on the same plane.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Wang Bingnan and Chen Jiakang, Assistant Ministers of Foreign Affairs, Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, and many officials from the Burmese Embassy in China.

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th: The wife of Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and the Prime Minister's Private Secretary, U Khin Soe, visited the Beihai Kindergarten and the Beijing Municipal Women and Children Health Experimental Institute on the morning of the 9th.

Li Jingyi, Deputy Director of the Women and Children Welfare Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, accompanied them on the visit.

During the visit to Beihai Kindergarten, Prime Minister U Nu's wife learned in detail about the children's lives, studies, and education at the kindergarten. She visited the children's dormitories, medical room, and dining hall, and also received flowers from the children.

At the Beijing Municipal Women and Children Health Experimental Institute, Director Chen Benzhen introduced the work of the experimental institute to the guests and accompanied Prime Minister U Nu's wife on a visit to the delivery room, operating room, and nursery.

### 缅甸总理吴努在东北参观后回到北京  吴努总理的夫人继续在北京参观

1954-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴努在东北参观后回到北京
    吴努总理的夫人继续在北京参观
    【新华社九日讯】缅甸联邦总理吴努等一行在我国东北参观后，在九日下午乘飞机回到北京。陪同吴努总理到东北参观的我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明、缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂和我国外交部交际处处长王倬如同机返京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部部长助理王炳南、陈家康，交际处副处长王拓和缅甸驻中国大使馆官员多人。
    【新华社九日讯】缅甸联邦总理吴努的夫人和总理私人秘书都庆素等在九日上午参观了北京北海幼儿园和北京市妇幼保健实验院。
    陪同她们参观的有中华全国民主妇女联合会妇女儿童福利部副部长李静一。
    参观北海幼儿园时，吴努总理的夫人详细了解了幼儿园孩子们的生活、学习和教育等方面的情况。她参观了孩子们的寝室、医疗室和饭厅，还接受了孩子们的献花。
    在北京市妇幼保健实验院，院长陈本真向来宾们介绍了这个实验院的工作情况，并陪同吴努总理的夫人参观了产房、手术室和婴儿室等处。
