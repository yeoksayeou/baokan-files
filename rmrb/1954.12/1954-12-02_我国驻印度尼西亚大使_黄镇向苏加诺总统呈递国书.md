Title: Huang Zhen, China's Ambassador to Indonesia, Presents Credentials to President Sukarno

Author:

Date: 1954-12-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 1st: Huang Zhen, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Indonesia, along with Counselor of the Embassy Zhong Qingfa, Chen Shuliang, Xu Li, Military Attaché Lieutenant Colonel Mao Chen, First Secretary Guo Ying, and Lin Liyang, presented his credentials to the President of the Republic of Indonesia, Sukarno, on November 29th. Present at the ceremony were Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunaryo, Director of Protocol of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Kusumo Udojo, Director of the Asian-Pacific Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wijopranoto, Secretary-General of the Presidential Palace Pringgodigdo, Presidential Secretary Sidarto Brotodiningrat, and Presidential Adjutant Major Sugandi.
    Ambassador Huang Zhen delivered a eulogy to President Sukarno upon presenting his credentials, and President Sukarno delivered a response.

### 我国驻印度尼西亚大使  黄镇向苏加诺总统呈递国书

1954-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻印度尼西亚大使
    黄镇向苏加诺总统呈递国书
    【新华社雅加达一日电】中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚共和国新任特命全权大使黄镇，十一月二十九日偕大使馆参赞锺庆发、陈叔亮、徐力，武官茅琛中校，一等秘书国鹰和林黎扬向印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺呈递国书。在场的有印度尼西亚外交部长苏纳约，外交部交际处处长库苏莫·乌多约，外交部亚洲—太平洋司司长维约普拉诺托以及总统府秘书长普林戈迪多，总统秘书西达托·布罗托迪宁格拉特和总统副官苏干迪少校。
    黄镇大使在呈递国书时向苏加诺总统致颂词，苏加诺总统致答词。
