Title: Romanian Film Week Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-12-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 29th:

    The opening ceremony of the "Romanian People's Republic Film Week," sponsored by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, was held at the Capital Cinema in Beijing on the evening of December 29th.

    Among those who attended the opening ceremony were Shi Liang, Minister of Justice of the People's Republic of China, Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, as well as other vice ministers from various ministries, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Wang Lanxi, Acting Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, heads and representatives of central and Beijing municipal organizations and groups, figures from the capital's film, drama, and literary circles, and combat heroes and model workers.

    Also attending the opening ceremony were Romanian Ambassador to China, Guo Zuwen, and embassy staff.

    Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend the opening ceremony.

    More than a thousand people attended the opening ceremony.

    At the beginning of the opening ceremony, the band played the national anthems of Romania and China.

    Vice Minister Ding Xilin delivered the opening speech. He said: The Film Week will be held successively in twenty major cities in our country, starting from December 30, 1954. During the Film Week, two outstanding Romanian artistic films, "The Ideal Has Been Realized" and "In Our Village," will be screened to the vast audience in our country. Our audience will surely increase their understanding and friendship with the Romanian people from the life and struggles reflected in these films. He also said: In recent years, there have been frequent cultural exchanges between the people of China and Romania. According to the cultural cooperation agreement between China and Romania, delegations have been exchanged, exhibitions have been held, and films have been screened, which have played a positive role in strengthening cultural exchanges between the two countries and enhancing the friendship between the two peoples. He expressed his belief that the holding of this Film Week will be a complete success and will further develop cultural exchanges and friendly cooperation between China and Romania!

    Ambassador Guo Zuwen then spoke. He first expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China for holding this Film Week on behalf of the Government of the Romanian People's Republic. He said that in terms of film art, as in the economic, political, and cultural fields, the links between Romania and China have been strengthened and developed; the agreement between our two countries on the exchange of films is one of the proofs. He said that before this, the two countries had carried out many cultural exchange activities, the most recent being the wonderful performances of the Song and Dance Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in Romania and the performances of the Romanian folk music team in China; now, the Romanian Film Week is being held in China, and the Chinese Film Festival was also held in Romania this year. The working and fighting goals of our two peoples are the same, that is, to establish a peaceful, free and happy life, and the cultural and artistic exchanges between our two peoples show this common goal. Finally, he congratulated the continuous development of cultural exchanges between Romania and China.

    After the meeting, the excellent Romanian film "The Ideal Has Been Realized" was screened and was warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 罗马尼亚电影周在京举行开幕式

1954-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚电影周在京举行开幕式
    【新华社二十九日讯】　中华人民共和国文化部主办的“罗马尼亚人民共和国电影周”开幕式二十九日晚在北京首都电影院举行。
    参加开幕式的，有中华人民共和国司法部部长史良、文化部副部长丁西林、外交部副部长伍修权，以及其他各部副部长，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，文化部电影局代局长王阑西，中央和北京市各机关团体负责人和代表，首都电影、戏剧、文学界人士和战斗英雄、劳动模范。
    参加开幕式的还有罗马尼亚驻华大使郭佐文和使馆人员。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也应邀参加了开幕式。
    参加开幕式的共有一千多人。
    开幕式开始，乐队奏罗、中两国国歌。
    丁西林副部长致开幕词。他说：电影周自一九五四年十二月三十日起将在我国二十个主要城市中先后举行。在电影周期间，有两部罗马尼亚优秀艺术的影片“理想实现了”和“在我们的村子里”，将在我国广大观众面前映出。我国观众一定会从这些影片所反映的生活和斗争中，增加对罗马尼亚人民的了解和友谊。他又说：几年来，中罗两国人民间的文化已有了频繁的交流。中罗两国间根据文化合作协定，互派了代表团，举办了展览会，放映了电影等，这些对加强两国文化交流，增进两国人民的友谊都已经起了积极的作用。他表示，这次电影周的举办，必将获得胜利成功并将使中罗间的文化交流和友好合作获得进一步的发展！
    郭佐文大使接着讲话。他首先代表罗马尼亚人民共和国政府对中华人民共和国文化部举办这次电影周表示衷心的感谢。他说，在电影艺术方面，和在经济、政治、文化方面一样，罗中两国之间的连系已有加强和发展；我们两国关于交换影片的协定就是证明之一。他说，在这以前，两国进行了很多文化交流活动，最近一次是中国人民解放军歌舞团在罗马尼亚的精采演出和罗马尼亚的民间音乐队在中国举行演出；现在在中国举行罗马尼亚电影周，今年在罗马尼亚也举行了中国电影节。我们两国人民的工作和斗争目标是相同的，这就是建立和平、自由而幸福的生活，我们两国人民的文化、艺术交流就表示出了这个共同的目的。最后，他祝贺罗中两国间的文化交流不断发展。
    会后，放映了罗马尼亚的优秀影片“理想实现了”，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
