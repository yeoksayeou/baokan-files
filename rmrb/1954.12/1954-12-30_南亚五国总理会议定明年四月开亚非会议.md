Title: South Asian Five-Nation Prime Ministers' Meeting Sets April for Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-12-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Bogor, December 29th: The South Asian Five-Nation (India, Burma, Pakistan, Indonesia, Ceylon) Prime Ministers' Meeting opened in Bogor, Indonesia, on the afternoon of the 28th. The meeting has decided to convene the Asian-African Conference in April 1955.

The first meeting was held from 2:30 PM to 5:00 PM on the 28th. A communique issued by the meeting stated: "The Prime Minister of Indonesia and other Prime Ministers made general speeches. After that, the meeting began to discuss the Asian-African Conference issue. It has been decided to hold this conference in Indonesia in the last week of April 1955."

### 南亚五国总理会议定明年四月开亚非会议

1954-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    南亚五国总理会议定明年四月开亚非会议
    【新华社茂物二十九日电】　南亚五国（印度、缅甸、巴基斯坦、印度尼西亚、锡兰）总理会议二十八日下午在印度尼西亚的茂物开幕。会议已经决定在一九五五年四月召开亚非会议。
    第一次会议在二十八日下午二时三十分到五时举行。会议发表公报说：“印度尼西亚总理和其他总理们作了一般性发言，在这以后，会议开始讨论亚非会议问题。已经决定在一九五五年四月最后一个星期在印度尼西亚举行这个会议。”
