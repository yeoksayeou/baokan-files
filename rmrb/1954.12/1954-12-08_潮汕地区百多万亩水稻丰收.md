Title: More Than One Million Mu of Rice Harvested in the Chao汕 Area
Author:
Date: 1954-12-08
Page: 2

    The Chao(an)汕(tou) area of Guangdong Province has achieved a bumper harvest of rice on 1.44 million mu of land. It is estimated that the total output of early and late rice crops will increase by more than 140 million jin compared to last year. Among them, 700,000 mu of rice fields yielded between 980 jin and over 1,100 jin per mu. The agricultural production cooperative in Guanbu Township, Chaoyang County, led by Liu Sielie, a national model for rice production, had more than seven mu of high-yield fields with an average yield of 1,621.2 jin per mu. The average yield of more than 62 mu of rice fields in the entire cooperative was 1,339.49 jin per mu, an increase of 189 jin compared to last year.
                                    (Xinhua News Agency)

### 潮汕地区百多万亩水稻丰收

1954-12-08
第2版()
专栏：

    潮汕地区百多万亩水稻丰收
    广东省潮（安）汕（头）地区的一百四十四万亩水稻大丰收，估计早、晚两季稻谷共比去年可增产一亿四千多万斤。其中有七十万亩水稻每亩产量达到九百八十斤到一千一百多斤。全国水稻丰产模范刘赛烈所领导的潮阳县关埠乡的农业生产合作社，有七亩多丰产田平均每亩产量达一千六百二十一点二斤，全社六十二亩多稻田平均每亩也有一千三百三十九点四九斤，比去年增产一百八十九斤。
                                    （新华社）
