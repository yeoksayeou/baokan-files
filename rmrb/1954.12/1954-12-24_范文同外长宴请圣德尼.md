Title: Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong Hosts Dinner for Sainteny
Author:
Date: 1954-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 21st: According to Vietnam News Agency, Hanoi, December 21st, Pham Van Dong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received Sainteny, the General Representative of the Government of the French Republic to the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, at 4:00 PM on the 20th. After the reception, Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong held a reception to entertain Sainteny, members of the French delegation to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, as well as some French industrialists, businessmen, professors, doctors, and journalists in Hanoi. Debure Bilepisson, the Chief Representative of the French Federal Forces General Command's delegation to the Vietnam Central Joint Armistice Commission, and Debauver, the Chief Representative of the Liaison Delegation to the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam, were also invited.
    Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived during the banquet, shook hands with the French guests, and proposed a toast to the friendship between the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and France.

### 范文同外长宴请圣德尼

1954-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    范文同外长宴请圣德尼
    【新华社二十一日讯】据越南通讯社河内二十一日讯：越南民主共和国外交部长范文同在二十日下午四时，接见法兰西共和国政府驻越南民主共和国政府总代表圣德尼。接见后，范文同外长举行招待会，招待圣德尼、法国驻越南民主共和国代表团的团员们以及在河内的一些法国工商业家、教授、医生和新闻记者。法兰西联邦部队总司令部派驻越南中央联合停战委员会代表团首席代表德布勒比松、派驻越南国际监督和监察委员会的联络代表团首席代表德鲍弗也应邀到会。
    越南民主共和国主席胡志明在宴会进行时到场，和法国来宾们握手，并建议为越南民主共和国和法国之间的友谊而干杯。
