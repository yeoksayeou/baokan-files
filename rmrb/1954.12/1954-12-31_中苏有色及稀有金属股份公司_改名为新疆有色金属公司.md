Title: Sino-Soviet Non-Ferrous and Rare Metals Joint Stock Company Renamed Xinjiang Non-Ferrous Metals Company
Author:
Date: 1954-12-31
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, December 30th. After the transfer of the Soviet shares of the Sino-Soviet Non-Ferrous and Rare Metals Joint Stock Company, the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the People's Republic of China has decided that, from January 1, 1955, the former Sino-Soviet Non-Ferrous and Rare Metals Joint Stock Company will be renamed "Xinjiang Non-Ferrous Metals Company of the Non-Ferrous Metals Industry Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the People's Republic of China" (referred to as Xinjiang Non-Ferrous Metals Company), and Bai Chengming has been appointed as the general manager of the company.

### 中苏有色及稀有金属股份公司  改名为新疆有色金属公司

1954-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏有色及稀有金属股份公司
    改名为新疆有色金属公司
    【新华社乌鲁木齐三十日电】中苏有色及稀有金属股份公司苏方股份移交后，中华人民共和国重工业部决定，从一九五五年一月一日起，原中苏有色及稀有金属股份公司改为“中华人民共和国重工业部有色金属工业管理局新疆有色金属公司”（简称新疆有色金属公司），并任命白成铭为这个公司的总经理。
