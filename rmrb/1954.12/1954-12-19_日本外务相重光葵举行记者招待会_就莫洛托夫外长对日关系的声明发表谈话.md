Title: Japanese Foreign Minister Mamoru Shigemitsu Holds Press Conference, Issues Statement on Foreign Minister Molotov's Declaration Regarding Relations with Japan
Author:
Date: 1954-12-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New York, December 17th: TASS reports from Tokyo via United Press that Japanese Foreign Minister Mamoru Shigemitsu held a press conference on December 17th regarding Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov's declaration of December 16th concerning relations with Japan.

　　Shigemitsu said: "Molotov's declaration was unexpected… The fact that Russia, which rejected the San Francisco Peace Treaty, has issued this declaration is a clear step forward. We should pay attention to future developments."

　　The report states that Foreign Minister Shigemitsu held the press conference after a cabinet meeting convened by Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama, during which Japanese leaders apparently discussed Molotov's declaration.

### 日本外务相重光葵举行记者招待会  就莫洛托夫外长对日关系的声明发表谈话

1954-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    日本外务相重光葵举行记者招待会
    就莫洛托夫外长对日关系的声明发表谈话
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社纽约十七日讯：合众社记者自东京报道，日本外务相重光葵十七日在记者招待会上就苏联外交部长莫洛托夫十二月十六日关于对日本的关系的声明发表谈话。
    重光葵说：“莫洛托夫的声明是出人意料之外的。……拒绝旧金山和约的俄国发表这一声明，这是向前迈进了明显的一步。我们应该注意今后事态的发展。”
    记者报道，重光葵外务相举行记者招待会是在鸠山一郎首相召集的内阁会议举行之后，在这次会议上日本的领袖们显然是讨论了莫洛托夫的声明。
