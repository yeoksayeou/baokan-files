Title: China and the Soviet Union Sign Aviation Transportation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-12-31
Page: 1

    China and the Soviet Union Sign Aviation Transportation Agreement
    [Xinhua News Agency, December 30th] A communique on the signing of the agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics on the establishment of regular air traffic between China and the Soviet Union.
    The government delegations of the People's Republic of China and the Soviet government delegations conducted negotiations in a sincere, friendly, and completely mutually understanding atmosphere. Based on the results of the negotiations, the "Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics on the Establishment of Regular Air Traffic between China and the Soviet Union" was signed in Beijing on December 30, 1954. The agreement stipulates the establishment of air traffic between the two sides' civil aircraft between Beijing-Moscow, Urumqi-Alma-Ata, and Beijing-Chita.
    The agreement was signed by Wu Faxian, head of the delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of the Chinese side, and by S.F. Zhavoronkov, head of the delegation of the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, on behalf of the Soviet side.
    The signing of this agreement will further strengthen economic cooperation and cultural exchanges between China and the Soviet Union.
     [Xinhua News Agency, December 30th] The agreement between China and the Soviet Union on the establishment of regular air traffic was signed in Beijing on December 30th.
    Signing the agreement on behalf of the Chinese side was General Wu Faxian, head of the People's Republic of China-Soviet Union Civil Aviation Navigation Negotiation Delegation, and signing on behalf of the Soviet side was Air Force Marshal Zhavoronkov, head of the Soviet delegation who came to our country to discuss civil aviation navigation matters between China and the Soviet Union.
    Participating in the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Vice Foreign Minister Wu Xiuquan, Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet Union and Eastern Europe, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Chen Ruiguang and Shen Tu, members of the People's Republic of China-Soviet Union Civil Aviation Navigation Negotiation Delegation. On the Soviet side were: Yudin, Soviet Ambassador to China, Vasikov and Li Haiqiu, counselors of the Embassy, Danilichev and Velizhakin, members of the Soviet delegation who came to our country to discuss civil aviation navigation matters between China and the Soviet Union, and Baorfirov, Deputy General Manager of the China-Soviet Civil Aviation Joint Stock Company. ### 中苏两国签订航空交通协定

1954-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏两国签订航空交通协定
    【新华社三十日讯】中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟间签订关于中苏两国间建立定期航空交通协定的公报。
    中华人民共和国政府代表团和苏联政府代表团在真诚友好的和完全互相了解的空气中进行了谈判，根据谈判的结果，一九五四年十二月三十日在北京签订了“中华人民共和国政府和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府关于中苏两国间建立定期航空交通协定”。协定中规定建立中苏双方的民用飞机在北京—莫斯科、乌鲁木齐—阿拉木图和北京—赤塔之间的航空交通。
    在协定上签字者，中国方面为中华人民共和国政府代表团团长吴法宪，苏联方面为苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府代表团团长谢·费·热沃隆科夫。
    这个协定的签订将进一步加强中苏两国间的经济合作和文化交流。
     【新华社三十日讯】中苏两国间建立定期航空交通协定于十二月三十日在北京签字。
    代表中国方面在协定上签字的是中华人民共和国中苏民用航空通航谈判代表团团长吴法宪将军，代表苏联方面在协定上签字的是来我国商谈中苏民用航空通航事宜的苏联代表团团长热沃隆科夫空军元帅。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅，外交部副部长伍修权、苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处处长王倬如，中华人民共和国中苏民用航空通航谈判代表团团员陈瑞光、沈图，苏联方面有：苏联驻华大使尤金，大使馆参赞华司考、李海秋，来我国商谈中苏民用航空通航事宜的苏联代表团团员达尼雷切夫、魏立夏金，中苏民用航空股份公司副总经理包尔菲洛夫。（附图片）
