Title: Our People Support Post-War Reconstruction of the Vietnamese People; President Ho Chi Minh Sends Telegram Thanking Our People for Their Care and Assistance
Author: Hu Zhiming
Date: 1954-12-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd:

    Our People Support Post-War Reconstruction of the Vietnamese People
    President Ho Chi Minh Sends Telegram Thanking Our People for Their Care and Assistance
    [Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd] To express the Chinese people's support for the Vietnamese people's post-war recovery and peaceful construction, the Chinese People's Relief Association decided to donate 10,000 tons of rice and 5 million meters of cotton cloth to President Ho Chi Minh to assist the Vietnamese people. These materials are being shipped in succession. In a letter to President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Chinese People's Relief Association stated:
    After the Indochina armistice, the Vietnamese people, under the leadership of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and yourself, are making every effort to restore peace and construction. Considering the arduousness and difficulty of this task of reconstruction, our association is pleased to donate 10,000 tons of rice and 5 million meters of cotton cloth to the Vietnamese people to demonstrate the friendship and mutual care between the Chinese and Vietnamese people.
    May the brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese people grow and be strengthened day by day.
    President Ho Chi Minh telegraphed a reply to the Chinese People's Relief Association, expressing his gratitude for this brotherly concern and assistance from the Chinese people to the Vietnamese people. The full text of President Ho Chi Minh's reply is as follows:
Chinese People's Relief Association:
    I was pleased to receive your letter and learned that the Chinese people have sent the Vietnamese people gifts of 10,000 tons of rice and 5 million meters of cotton cloth.
    This friendly assistance given by the Chinese people to the Vietnamese people fully demonstrates the Chinese people's concern and support for the Vietnamese people. Please allow me, on behalf of the Vietnamese people, to express my heartfelt gratitude to your association and the Chinese people.
    Ho Chi Minh

### 我国人民支持越南人民战后建设  胡志明主席电谢我国人民的关怀和援助
胡志明
1954-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我国人民支持越南人民战后建设
    胡志明主席电谢我国人民的关怀和援助
    【新华社二十二日讯】中国人民救济总会为了表示中国人民支持越南人民战后的恢复和和平建设工作，决定以大米一万吨、棉布五百万公尺赠送胡志明主席，用以援助越南人民。这些物资正在陆续启运中。中国人民救济总会在给越南民主共和国胡志明主席的信中说：
    印度支那停战后，越南人民在越南民主共和国政府和您的领导下，正在全力恢复和平建设。我会鉴于恢复建设这一任务的繁重和困难，谨以大米一万吨，棉布五百万公尺赠给越南人民，以示中越两国人民友谊和相互间的关怀。
    愿中越两国人民兄弟般的友谊日益发展和巩固。
    胡志明主席电复中国人民救济总会，对于中国人民对越南人民这种兄弟般的关怀和援助表示感谢。胡志明主席复电全文如下：中国人民救济总会：
    我愉快地接到你会的来信，获悉中国人民送给越南人民大米一万吨，棉布五百万公尺的赠品。
    中国人民所给予越南人民的这种友谊的援助，充分证明了中国人民对越南人民的关怀和支持。请允许我代表越南人民向贵会和中国人民致以衷心的感谢。
    胡志明
