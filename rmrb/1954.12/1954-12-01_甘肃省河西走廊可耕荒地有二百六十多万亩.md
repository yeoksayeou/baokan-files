Title: More than 2.6 Million Mu of Arable Wasteland in the Hexi Corridor of Gansu Province

Author:
Date: 1954-12-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency
    The Gansu Provincial People's Government's Department of Agriculture and Forestry State-Owned Farm Wasteland Survey Team has surveyed more than 2.6 million mu of arable wasteland suitable for establishing farms in the Hexi Corridor area of Gansu Province. This wasteland is mainly distributed in the Shule River basin of Yumen and Anxi and has irrigation conditions. The soil quality of the wasteland has been tested and analyzed indoors and observed outdoors by the survey team, proving that it is entirely suitable for the growth of crops. This year, some farmers tried planting spring wheat, and the yield per mu reached about 400 jin.

### 甘肃省河西走廊可耕荒地有二百六十多万亩

1954-12-01
第2版()
专栏：

    甘肃省河西走廊可耕荒地有二百六十多万亩
    甘肃省人民政府农林厅国营农场荒地勘测队，已在河西走廊地区勘测出宜于建立农场的可耕荒地二百六十多万亩。这些荒地主要分布在玉门、安西的疏勒河流域，有灌溉条件。荒地土质经过勘测队室内化验分析和室外观察的结果，证明完全宜于农作物的生长。今年有些农民试种的春小麦，每亩产量达到四百斤左右。（新华社）
