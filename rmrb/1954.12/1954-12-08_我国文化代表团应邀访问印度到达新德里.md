Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation Arrives in New Delhi on Invitation to Visit India
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 7th. The People's Republic of China's cultural delegation, invited by the Indian government to visit India, arrived in New Delhi by plane on the afternoon of the 6th, led by the head of the delegation, Zheng Zhenduo, and the deputy head of the delegation, Zhou Erfu.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included: Captain R.K. Anand, representative of the President of the Republic of India; Mrs. Indira Gandhi, Chairman of the Central Reception Committee for welcoming the People's Republic of China's Cultural Delegation and daughter of Prime Minister Nehru; A.K. Chanda, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; S.A. Husaini, Joint Educational Advisor of the Ministry of Education; A.D. Pandit, Chief Commissioner of Delhi State; as well as other government officials and members of parliament from various political parties in India.

Also welcoming the delegation at the airport were: Dr. Saifuddin Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council; Major General Sahib Singh Sokhey, Vice-Chairman; Chatur Narain Malaviya, Secretary-General of the India-China Friendship Association; Mrs. Uma Nehru, head of the India-China Friendship Association delegation that recently visited China; Mrs. Ammu Swaminathan, head of the Indian Women's Delegation; representatives of the All-India Trade Union Congress and the All-India Women's Federation; artists, cultural workers, and students.

Shen Jian, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the People's Republic of China Embassy in India, other embassy staff, overseas Chinese in Delhi, and Chinese students studying in India also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

Diplomatic representatives of various countries stationed in India also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

A total of 2,500 people welcomed the delegation at the airport. Many Indian students held various flags with slogans such as "Long Live India-China Friendship" and "Long Live World Peace" written in Chinese and Indian. People shouted "Indian and Chinese people are brothers" to the delegation members and threw colorful flower petals on them.

Zheng Zhenduo, the head of the delegation, delivered a speech to the welcoming crowd, saying: For more than two thousand years, the people of China and India have maintained friendly relations and cultural exchanges. The friendship between the two peoples is now playing an important role in the solemn cause of defending Asian and world peace. The delegation's visit to India will further strengthen the friendship and cultural relations between our two countries. He finally expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Indian government for its kind invitation and the welcome the delegation received.

### 我国文化代表团应邀访问印度到达新德里

1954-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我国文化代表团应邀访问印度到达新德里
    【新华社新德里七日电】应印度政府邀请访问印度的中华人民共和国文化代表团一行在团长郑振铎、副团长周而复率领下在六日下午乘飞机到达新德里。
    到机场欢迎的有：印度共和国总统代表鲁·克·阿南德上尉，欢迎中华人民共和国文化代表团的中央招待委员会主席、尼赫鲁总理的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人，外交部副部长阿·库·钱达，教育部联合教育顾问斯·阿·侯赛尼，德里邦首席专员阿·德·潘迪特，以及其他政府人员和印度各政党的国会议员。
    到机场欢迎的还有：全印和平理事会主席赛福丁·克其鲁博士，副主席萨希布·辛格·索克少将，印中友好协会秘书长查图尔·纳拉茵·马拉维亚，最近曾经访问中国的印中友好协会代表团团长乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人，印度妇女代表团团长安穆·斯瓦米纳坦夫人，全印总工会和印度全国妇女联合会的代表，艺术家，文化工作者和学生等。
    中华人民共和国驻印度大使馆临时代办申健和大使馆其他人员以及德里华侨和在印度学习的中国学生也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有各国驻印度外交代表。
    到机场欢迎的共有二千五百人。许多印度学生拿着各种旗帜，上面用中文和印度文写着“印中友好万岁”、“世界和平万岁”等口号。人们向代表团人员高呼“印中人民是弟兄”，并且把彩色的花瓣抛在他们身上。
    代表团团长郑振铎向欢迎群众发表谈话说：两千多年来，中印两国人民一直保持友好关系和文化交流。两国人民之间的友谊在保卫亚洲和世界和平的庄严事业中现在正起着重要的作用。代表团的访问印度，将会更进一步加强我们两国之间的友谊和文化关系。他最后对印度政府的善意邀请和代表团所受到的欢迎表示衷心的感谢。
