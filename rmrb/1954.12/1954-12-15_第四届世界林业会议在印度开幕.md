Title: Fourth World Forestry Conference Opens in India
Author:
Date: 1954-12-15
Page: 4

    Fourth World Forestry Conference Opens in India
    [Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 13th] The Fourth World Forestry Conference opened on the 11th in the central hall of the Forest Research Institute in Dehradun, Uttar Pradesh, India. A total of 426 delegates attended the conference, 206 of whom represented 52 countries and four international organizations.
    The People's Republic of China delegation, headed by Liu Chengdong, attended the conference.
    Indian President Dr. Prasad delivered the opening address at the opening session.
    Indian representative and Director General of Forestry Ranganathan, Soviet representative V. N. Sukachev, and Canadian representative D. A. Macdonald were elected as chairmen of the conference. Representatives from Argentina, China, France, Iraq, and New Zealand were elected as vice-chairmen of the conference.
    The conference will be held until December 22nd. The conference will discuss the current situation of world forest protection and management, the protective and productive functions of forests, and the utilization of forest products. The conference pays special attention to tropical forestry. Detailed issues under these main topics include shelterbelts, principles of soil and water conservation, artificial cultivation, desert control, and shifting cultivation.

### 第四届世界林业会议在印度开幕

1954-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    第四届世界林业会议在印度开幕
    【新华社新德里十三日电】第四届世界林业会议十一日在印度北方省台霍拉登林业研究所的中央大厅开幕。参加这次会议的代表共四百二十六人，其中二百零六人代表着五十二个国家和四个国际组织。
    以刘成栋为首的中华人民共和国代表团参加了会议。
    印度总统普拉沙德博士在开幕会议上致开幕词。
    印度代表林业总监兰加纳丹、苏联代表维·纳·苏卡切夫和加拿大代表德·阿·麦克唐纳当选为会议主席。阿根廷、中国、法国、伊拉克和新西兰的代表当选为会议的副主席。
    会议将举行到十二月二十二日。会上要讨论世界森林保护和管理的目前情况、森林的保护功能和生产功能以及对林产品的利用。会议特别注意热带的林业。这些主要题目下的细节问题包括防护林带、水土保持的原则、人工培植、沙漠的控制和轮流栽种等。
