Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-12-09
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
　　The expansion project of Daye Steel Plant in Huangshi City has received strong support from Shanghai workers. This mud blowing boat, transferred from Shanghai to Huangshi City to participate in the pond-filling project, is dredging mud from the Yangtze River and blowing it to the expansion site.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
　　黄石市大冶钢厂的扩建工程，得到了上海工人的有力支援。这艘从上海调到黄石市参加填塘工程的吹泥船，正挖出长江水匠的泥浆，把它吹送到扩建的工地去。
