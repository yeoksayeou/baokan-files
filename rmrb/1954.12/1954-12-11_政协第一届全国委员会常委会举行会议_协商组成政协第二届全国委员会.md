Title: The Standing Committee of the First National Committee of the CPPCC Holds a Meeting to Consult on the Composition of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 10th—The sixty-second meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) was held on the afternoon of the 4th in an enlarged session. The meeting discussed the preparatory issues for convening the First Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC.

    Prior to this meeting, the democratic parties, people's organizations, and relevant parties had conducted multiple consultations on the organizational issues of the CPPCC and unanimously agreed that it was not necessary to hold the Second Plenary Session of the CPPCC. Instead, the democratic parties, people's organizations, and other units would nominate candidates, and after consultation and confirmation by the Standing Committee of the First National Committee of the CPPCC, the Second National Committee of the CPPCC would be directly formed.

    The meeting discussed and approved the list of members of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC (see page 2 of today's newspaper). This committee is composed of 559 people from 29 units.

    The meeting also decided that the First Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC would be held on December 20th.

### 政协第一届全国委员会常委会举行会议  协商组成政协第二届全国委员会

1954-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    政协第一届全国委员会常委会举行会议
    协商组成政协第二届全国委员会
  定二十日召开第二届全国委员会全体会议
    【新华社十日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会常务委员会第六十二次会议在四日下午举行扩大会议。会议讨论了召开中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议的筹备问题。
    在这次会议举行之前，各民主党派、各人民团体和各有关方面对于中国人民政治协商会议的组织问题，曾经进行多次协商，一致同意不必举行中国人民政治协商会议第二届全体会议，而由各民主党派、各人民团体及其他单位提出人选，经过中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会常务委员会协商确定后，直接组成中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会。
    这次会议讨论和通过了中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会委员名单（见今天本报第二版）。这个委员会是由二十九个单位的五百五十九人组成的。
    会议还确定中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议在十二月二十日召开。
