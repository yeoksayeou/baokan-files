Title: China Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 11th: The China Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe, after completing its performance tour in the German Democratic Republic, the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and the Mongolian People's Republic, returned to China in late October. It immediately joined the art troupe jointly organized by the Ministry of Culture and the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to慰问 (offer comfort) to the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area, and went to Lushunkou to conduct慰问 (comfort) performances for the Soviet troops. It completed its mission and returned to Beijing on December 11th.

The acrobatic troupe departed on June 13th, visiting 47 cities and towns of various sizes in East Germany, Bulgaria, and Mongolia, giving 81 performances to an audience of over 401,380 people. The performances were warmly welcomed by the people of the fraternal countries.

During the acrobatic troupe's visit to the three countries, Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria G. Traikov, Deputy Chairmen G. Zhankov and R. Damyanov, member of the Politburo of the Bulgarian Communist Party To. Zhivkov, Prime Minister Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Damba, and Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic Zedib, all watched their performances. Prime Minister Tsedenbal also received the leaders of the acrobatic troupe.

During its visits to the three countries and while passing through the Soviet Union, the acrobatic troupe, in addition to performing, also held several observation sessions and forums with the circus and acrobatics circles of Germany, Bulgaria, Mongolia, and the Soviet Union, exchanging experiences in acrobatic arts. During its visit to the Mongolian People's Republic, Chinese acrobats also learned acrobatic programs from Mongolian acrobats.

### 我国重庆杂技艺术团回京

1954-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我国重庆杂技艺术团回京
   【新华社十一日讯】前往德意志民主共和国、保加利亚人民共和国、蒙古人民共和国作访问演出的中国重庆杂技艺术团已在完成出国访问演出任务后于十月下旬返国；并即参加了文化部和中国人民解放军总政治部联合组织的慰劳驻旅顺口地区苏军艺术团，前往旅顺口对苏军部队进行慰问演出。十二月十一日完成任务回到北京。
    杂技艺术团从六月十三日出国，前后在民主德国、保加利亚、蒙古人民共和国访问了四十七个大小城市，演出八十一场，观众四十万一千三百八十多人。演出的节目受到各兄弟国家人民的热烈欢迎。
    杂技艺术团访问三国期间，德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥，保加利亚部长会议第一副主席格·特莱科夫、副主席格·詹科夫、拉·达米扬诺夫，保共中央政治局委员托·日夫科夫，蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，蒙古人民共和国人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席泽狄布等都观看了他们的表演。泽登巴尔总理还接见了杂技艺术团的负责人。
    杂技艺术团在访问三国和途经苏联期间，除演出外，还和德、保、蒙三国以及苏联的马戏杂技界举行了多次的观摩和座谈会，交流了杂技艺术经验。在访问蒙古人民共和国期间，我国的杂技演员还和蒙古杂技演员互相学习了杂技节目。
