Title: U Nu Emphasizes in Rangoon that Our Country Sincerely Hopes for Peaceful Coexistence
Author:
Date: 1954-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 21st: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, at a press conference held in Rangoon on the 20th after returning from his visit to China, said, "China sincerely hopes for peaceful coexistence." U Nu also said that Chinese leaders support the planned Asian-African Conference.

Regarding the Chinese people's attitude toward the Taiwan issue, Prime Minister U Nu said, "They have already said that they want to liberate Taiwan, and I don't think they are people who don't keep their word."

He also spoke about Sino-Soviet friendship. He said that this friendship is solid, and said that China and the Soviet Union are "loyal to each other."

Prime Minister U Nu asked the reporters not to ask him about his impressions of China's internal affairs. He said, "I don't want to make judgments about their form of government."

He expressed his hope to go to the United States in the near future.

### 吴努在仰光强调我国真诚希望和平共处

1954-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努在仰光强调我国真诚希望和平共处
    【新华社二十一日讯】仰光消息：缅甸总理吴努访问中国回国后，二十日在仰光举行的记者招待会上说：“中国是真心诚意希望和平共处的。”吴努又说，中国领袖们支持计划中的亚非会议。
    关于中国人民对台湾问题的态度，吴努总理说：“他们已经说过，他们要解放台湾，我不认为他们是说话不算数的人。”
    他还谈到中苏友谊。他说，这种友谊是巩固的，并说，中苏是“彼此忠实的”。
    吴努总理要求记者们不要问他关于他对中国内政的印象。他说，“我不想对他们的政府形式下判断。”
    他表示希望在不久以后去美国。
