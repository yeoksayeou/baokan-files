Title: Indian President Receives Head of Chinese Cultural Delegation; Prime Minister Nehru Hosts Banquet for Chinese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 8th — The President of the Republic of India, Dr. Prasad, received Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation visiting India at the invitation of the Indian government, and Zhou Erfu, the deputy head, at 4 p.m. on the 7th.

Present at the reception was Shen Jian, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in India.

Also present at the reception was Yunus, Deputy Secretary of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 9th — Indian Prime Minister Nehru hosted a luncheon at his official residence on the 8th to entertain all members of the Chinese Cultural Delegation visiting India at the invitation of the Indian government.

Those present included: Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan, Indian government ministers and senior officials, Madam Uma Nehru, head of the Indian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation that recently visited China, Madam Ammu Swaminathan, head of the Indian Women's Delegation, as well as more than one hundred well-known Indian artists, cultural workers, and journalists.

Shen Jian, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in India, and Kang Maozhao, Cultural Counselor, were also invited to attend.

After the luncheon, Indian artists performed folk dances from various parts of India.

### 印度总统接见我国文化代表团负责人  尼赫鲁总理设宴招待我国文化代表团

1954-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    印度总统接见我国文化代表团负责人
    尼赫鲁总理设宴招待我国文化代表团
   【新华社新德里八日电】印度共和国总统普拉沙德博士，在七日下午四时接见了应印度政府邀请访问印度的中华人民共和国文化代表团团长郑振铎和副团长周而复。
    陪同受接见的有中华人民共和国驻印度大使馆临时代办申健。
    接见时在座的有印度外交部副秘书尤努斯。
    【新华社新德里九日电】印度总理尼赫鲁八日在他的官邸设午餐招待应印度政府邀请访问印度的中国文化代表团全体人员。
    出席的有：印度副总统拉德哈克里希南，印度政府的部长们和高级官员，最近到中国访问的印中友好协会代表团团长乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人、印度妇女代表团团长安穆·斯瓦米纳坦夫人，以及印度著名艺术家、文化工作者和新闻工作者等一百多人。
    中华人民共和国驻印度大使馆临时代办申健和文化参赞康矛召也应邀出席。
    午餐后，印度艺术工作者表演了印度各地民间舞蹈。
