Title: Indian Vice President Receives Head of Chinese Cultural Delegation, Indian Education Minister Holds Reception to Welcome Chinese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-12-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 8th: The Vice President of the Republic of India, S. Radhakrishnan, received Zheng Zhenduo, head of the People's Republic of China cultural delegation visiting India at the invitation of the Indian government, along with deputy head Zhou Erfu and Secretary-General Lou Shiyi on the 7th.

Shen Jian, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in India, was present during the reception.

Yunus, Deputy Secretary of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was also present at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 8th: The Indian Minister of Education, Maulana Abul Kalam Azad, held a reception in the garden of the Indian Presidential Palace on the afternoon of the 7th to welcome the People's Republic of China cultural delegation visiting India at the invitation of the Indian government.

Those attending the reception included: the President of the Republic of India, Rajendra Prasad; Vice President S. Radhakrishnan; Prime Minister Nehru; ministers of the Indian Central Government; senior officials of various departments; and the Chief Minister of Delhi State, Prakash, as well as Indian artists, cultural workers, and journalists.

Shen Jian, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in India, and other members of the embassy were also invited to attend the reception. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to India also attended the reception.

Approximately 300 people attended the reception.

On the morning of the same day, Zheng Zhenduo and Zhou Erfu, the head and deputy head of the Chinese cultural delegation, along with some members of the delegation, accompanied by P. Singh, representative of the Indian President, Yunus, Deputy Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Charge d'Affaires Shen Jian, visited Gandhi's cremation site and laid a wreath.

On the afternoon of the same day, the head and deputy head of the Chinese cultural delegation, accompanied by Charge d'Affaires Shen Jian, paid a visit to Indian Education Minister Azad. During the meeting, Azad welcomed the Chinese cultural delegation and expressed his hope that the exchange of delegations and other cultural activities between China and India would further strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries.

### 印度副总统接见我国文化代表团负责人印度教育部长举行招待会欢迎我国文化代表团

1954-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

    印度副总统接见我国文化代表团负责人
    印度教育部长举行招待会欢迎我国文化代表团
    【新华社新德里八日电】印度共和国副总统萨·拉德哈克里希南在七日接见了应印度政府邀请访问印度的中华人民共和国文化代表团团长郑振铎、副团长周而复和秘书长楼适夷。
    陪同受接见的有中华人民共和国驻印度大使馆临时代办申健。
    接见时在座的有印度外交部副秘书尤努斯。
    【新华社新德里八日电】印度教育部长毛·艾·卡·阿扎德七日下午在印度总统府花园举行招待会，欢迎应印度政府邀请访问印度的中华人民共和国文化代表团。
    出席招待会的有：印度共和国总统拉·普拉沙德、副总统萨·拉德哈克里希南、总理尼赫鲁、印度中央政府的部长们、各部高级官员和德里邦首席部长佩卡什，以及印度艺术家、文化工作者和新闻工作者等。
    中华人民共和国驻印度大使馆临时代办申健和大使馆其他人员也应邀出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有各国驻印度外交使节。
    出席招待会的共约三百人。
    当天上午，中国文化代表团正副团长郑振铎、周而复和代表团部分团员，由印度总统代表普·辛格、外交部副秘书尤努斯和申健代办陪同，往谒甘地火葬处并献花圈。
    同日下午，中国文化代表团正副团长在申健代办陪同下，拜会了印度教育部长阿扎德。在会谈时，阿扎德表示欢迎中国文化代表团，并希望中印两国互派代表团和其他文化联系活动将进一步加强两国之间的友好关系。
