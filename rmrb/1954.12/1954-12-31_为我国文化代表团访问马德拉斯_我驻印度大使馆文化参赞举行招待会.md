Title: Chinese Embassy Cultural Counselor Holds Reception for Chinese Cultural Delegation Visiting Madras
Author:
Date: 1954-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Madras, December 30th: Kang Maozhao, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in India, held a reception at the Connemara Hotel in Madras on the 29th for the Chinese Cultural Delegation's visit to Madras.

Those invited to attend included: Madras Governor Prakasa, Chief Minister K. Nadar and other senior officials; Madras Legislative Council Speaker P.V. Cherian and members of parliament; All-India Peace Council Chairman Kitchlew, Vice-Chairmen Anand and Kumarappa, Chairman of the India-China Friendship Association Sundarlal, Stalin International Peace Prize laureates Andre Andrein and Prit, and renowned Australian peace activist Mrs. Street, who are currently in Madras to participate in the All-India Conference for Peace and Asian Solidarity; well-known artists and people from all walks of life, consuls of various countries in Madras, and overseas Chinese, totaling approximately 500 people.

### 为我国文化代表团访问马德拉斯  我驻印度大使馆文化参赞举行招待会

1954-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    为我国文化代表团访问马德拉斯
    我驻印度大使馆文化参赞举行招待会
    【新华社马德拉斯三十日电】中华人民共和国驻印度大使馆文化参赞康矛召为中国文化代表团到达马德拉斯访问，二十九日在马德拉斯康尼马拉旅馆举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有：马德拉斯省长普拉卡萨、首席部长卡·纳达尔等高级官员；马德拉斯省立法会议议长普·维·契里安和议员们；目前到马德拉斯来参加全印争取和平和亚洲团结大会的全印和平理事会主席克其鲁，副主席阿南德和库马拉帕，印中友好协会主席森德拉尔，“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人安德烈·安德琳和普里特，澳大利亚著名和平人士斯崔特夫人；著名的艺术家和各界人士，各国驻马德拉斯领事和华侨等约五百人。
