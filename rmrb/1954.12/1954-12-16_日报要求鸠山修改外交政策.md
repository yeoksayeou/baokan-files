Title: Newspaper Demands Hatoyama Revise Foreign Policy
Author:
Date: 1954-12-16
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 14th: Many Japanese newspapers are urging the Hatoyama cabinet to "adjust Japanese diplomacy" in accordance with changes in the world situation.

    "Yomiuri Shimbun" demanded that the Hatoyama cabinet "revise Yoshida's diplomacy" because this foreign policy is "outdated."

    "Tokyo Shimbun" also expressed the hope that the Hatoyama cabinet would "adopt a new attitude on the issue of adjusting relations with Communist China."

    "Asahi Shimbun" in its editorial on the 11th demanded that the Hatoyama cabinet adopt a new foreign policy and not repeat the mistakes of the Yoshida cabinet. The editorial said: "This error has unnecessarily hindered the path to restoring diplomatic relations with Communist countries."

### 日报要求鸠山修改外交政策

1954-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

    日报要求鸠山修改外交政策
    【新华社十四日讯】东京消息：日本许多报纸敦促鸠山内阁根据世界形势的变化来“调整日本外交”。
    “读卖新闻”要求鸠山内阁“修改吉田的外交”，因为这种外交政策是“不合时宜”的。
    “东京新闻”也表示希望鸠山内阁“在调整和共产党中国的关系这一问题上采取一种新态度”。
    “朝日新闻”在十一日的社论中要求鸠山内阁采取新的外交政策，不要重犯吉田内阁的错误。社论说：“这种错误已不必要地阻碍了和共产党国家恢复外交关系的道路。”
