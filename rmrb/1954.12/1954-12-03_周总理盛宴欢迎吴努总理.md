Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Grand Banquet Welcoming Prime Minister U Nu
Author:
Date: 1954-12-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd—Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, held a grand banquet on the evening of the 2nd to welcome the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his wife, who are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese government.

    Accompanying Prime Minister U Nu on his visit to China were nine people, including U Myint Thein, Judge of the Supreme Court of Burma; U Win Pe, Prime Minister's Secretary and Cabinet Secretary; U Thant, Prime Minister's Secretary; Colonel Aung Gyi, Staff Officer of the Army Department; Captain Ba Swe, Prime Minister's Aide-de-Camp; and U Toe Khin Swe, Prime Minister's Private Secretary, all of whom were invited to attend the banquet.

    The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

    The Chinese side present at the banquet included: Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De; Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liu Shaoqi.

    Also present at the banquet were: Vice Premiers of the State Council Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Ministers of various ministries and Directors of Commissions Xie Juezai, Luo Ruiqing, Shi Liang, Qian Ying, Bo Yibo, Zhang Naiqi, Zeng Shan, Ye Jizhuang, Zhao Erlu, Chen Yu, Liu Xiufeng, Jiang Guangnai, Jia Tuofu, Sha Qianli, Teng Daiyuan, Zhang Bojun, Liang Xi, Shen Yanbing, Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Xiruo, Li Dequan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Ministers of various ministries and Vice Directors of Commissions, and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen.

    Also present at the banquet were: Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, and members Kunlun, Situ Meitang, Wu Yuzhang, Li Shucheng, Li Xuefeng, Lin Feng, Zhou Jianren, Zhou Chunquan, Shao Lizi, Hu Qiaomu, Shi Fuliang, Gao Chongmin, Xu Teli, Panchen Erdeni Qoigyijabu, Wei Guoqing, Ma Mingfang, Ma Xulun, Zhang Su, Chen Shaoxian, Cheng Zihua, Huang Kecheng, Peng Zemin, Liu Geping, Liu Lantao, Luo Longji, Tan Zheng.

    Also present at the banquet were: President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Fu Zuoyi, Long Yun, and members Yu Xuezhong, Wang Shitai, Wang Hongkun, Wang Bingzhang, Wang Zhen, Li Mingyang, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Zhang Zongxun, Chen Shiju, Chen Qihan, Chen Geng, Chen Xilian, Peng Shaohui, Yang Chengwu, Yang Yong, Wan Yi, Liao Hansheng, Liu Yalou, Zheng Dongguo, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Su Yu, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Xiao Hua, Gan Siqi, and senior generals.

    The Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, Yao Zhongming, also attended the banquet.

    Also present at the banquet were: Standing Committee members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Huang Qixiang, Chen Qiyou, Qian Sanqiang, Vice Chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy Wang Shaoyao, Standing Committee member of the Central Committee of the China Zhi Gong Party Yan Xichun, Vice Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Li Chunqing, and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League Hu Yaobang.

    Also present at the banquet were: Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Liao Chengzhi, Vice President of the China-Burma Friendship Association Qian Weichang and members of the council, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Lai Ruoyu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Xu Guangping, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation Liu Daosheng, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation Tian Demin, Secretary-General of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Yang Hansheng, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhou Gengsheng, Qian Duansheng, Qiao Guanhua, Standing Committee member of the Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee of Protestant Churches in China Wang Zizhong, Vice Director of the China Islamic Association Da Pongsheng, Vice Director of the China Hui National Culture Promotion Association Ma Songting, Acting President of the Buddhist Association of China Xirao Gyatso, Deputy Secretary-General Jue Zan, Guo Peng, and other leaders of people's organizations.

    Also present at the banquet were: scientists Zhang Jiafu, Wu Youxun, Fan Wenlan, Pan Zinian, Hua Luogeng, Chen Kangbai, Hou Debang, Tu Changwang, Zhou Peiyuan, educators Hu Xikui, Jiang Longji, He Xilin, Fu Zhongsun, Sun Xiaocun, literary and art figures Ding Ling, Lao She, Mei Lanfang, Cheng Yanqiu, Xie Bingxin, Liu Baiyu, Chen Baichen, Wu Zuoren, Cai Chusheng, Chen Qitong, journalists Wu Lengxi, Wang Yunsheng, Mei Yi, Chen Yongwen, Chang Zhiqing, Fan Jin, and representatives of various ethnic groups and members of the former Chinese cultural delegation to Burma.

    The foreign envoys and diplomatic officials present at the banquet were: Czechoslovak Ambassador Kansara, Polish Ambassador Kirylok, Indian Ambassador Raghavan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Hoang Van Hoan, Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat, Indonesian Ambassador Mononutu, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic König, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Choi Il, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov, Albanian Ambassador Nase, Hungarian Ambassador Szikra-Dániel, Finnish Minister Sundström, Danish Minister গ্রেগersen, Swiss Minister Bénédict, British Chargé d'affaires Duthie, Dutch Chargé d'affaires Smidt, Norwegian Chargé d'affaires Gjedde, Swedish Chargé d'affaires Hammarskjöld, Romanian Chargé d'affaires Menténu, and Pakistani Chargé d'affaires Sultan Muhammad Khan.

    Representatives of the Republic of Indonesia who have come to China to negotiate the issue of the nationality of Chinese citizens in Indonesia, as well as members of the Indonesian agricultural delegation and the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam were also invited to the banquet.

    More than 700 people attended the banquet.

    Before the banquet began, Premier Zhou Enlai introduced Prime Minister U Nu to national leaders, heads of various departments, leaders of various democratic parties, people's organizations, and people from all walks of life.

    At the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech first (full text to be published separately). At the end of his speech, he raised his glass to wish President Ba U of the Union of Burma good health. The band played the Burmese national anthem.

    Prime Minister U Nu then delivered a speech (full text to be published separately). At the end of his speech, he raised his glass to wish Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China good health. The band played the national anthem of the People's Republic of China.

    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. (Attached pictures)

### 周总理盛宴欢迎吴努总理

1954-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理盛宴欢迎吴努总理
    【新华社二日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在二日晚上举行盛大宴会，欢迎应我国政府邀请来我国访问的缅甸联邦总理吴努和他的夫人。
