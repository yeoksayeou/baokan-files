Title: Soviet Party and Government Leaders Visit the All-Union Architectural Exhibition
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 5th: According to TASS, Soviet Communist Party and government leaders Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Molotov, and Khrushchev, accompanied by Sokolov, Chairman of the State Construction Committee of the USSR Council of Ministers, Yudin, Minister of the USSR Building Materials Industry, Kapitonov and Furtseva, Secretaries of the Moscow City Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, visited the permanent All-Union Architectural Exhibition in Moscow on the 4th. In the open-air exhibition area, they viewed exhibits demonstrating the use of prefabricated reinforced concrete and large concrete blocks in industrial, agricultural, and residential construction, as well as machinery and equipment produced by factories under the Road Machinery Manufacturing Department, the Metallurgical Industry, and the Chemical Industry Enterprise Construction Department of the Soviet Building Machinery, which extensively mechanize construction and assembly engineering. In the "Residential Building and Agricultural Building Exhibition Hall," they also viewed standard designs for residential, agricultural, and other buildings constructed using reinforced concrete components and large concrete blocks.

After visiting the exhibition, the Soviet Communist Party and government leaders also visited residential building construction sites on Shabolovka Street and Oktyabrskoye Pole in Moscow to observe the construction of houses, and visited small buildings constructed in the suburbs of Moscow in Sokolniki.

### 苏联党和政府领导人参观全苏建筑展览会

1954-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联党和政府领导人参观全苏建筑展览会
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党和政府的领导人布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫，在苏联部长会议国家建设委员会主席索科洛夫、苏联建筑材料工业部部长尤金、苏联共产党莫斯科市委员会书记卡皮托诺夫和福尔采娃等人陪同下，在四日参观了莫斯科常设的全苏建筑展览会。在展览会的露天展览场上，他们参观了说明在工业、农业和住宅建设中使用装配式钢筋混凝土和大型混凝土块的情况的展览品，以及苏联建筑机器的筑路机器制造部、冶金工业和化学工业企业建造部所属各工厂出产的使建筑和装配工程广泛机械化的机器和机械。他们在“住宅建筑和农业建筑展览馆”还参观了用钢筋混凝土构件和大型混凝土块建筑住宅、农业用建筑物和其他建筑物的标准设计。
    在展览会参观后，苏联共产党和政府的领导人又到莫斯科的沙街和十月田野区的住宅建筑工地上参观了建筑房屋的情形，并到索科尼基参观了在莫斯科市郊区所建造的小楼房。
