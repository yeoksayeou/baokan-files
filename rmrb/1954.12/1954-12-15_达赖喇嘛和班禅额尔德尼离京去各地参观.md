Title: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Leave Beijing to Visit Various Places

Author:

Date: 1954-12-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 13th: Dalai Lama Tenzin Gyatso, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Panchen Erdeni Chokyi Gyaltsen, a member of the Standing Committee, left Beijing by special train at 9:00 AM today to visit other places.

Accompanying them on their visits to other places are: Liu Geping, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Phuntsok Wangyal, Deputy Director of the First Department of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, and others.

Accompanying them on their visits to other places are more than 100 monks, lay officials, and family members and attendants of the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, including Living Buddha Lincang, Living Buddha Chijiang, Living Buddha Karmapa, Sogkon Wangqing-Gelek, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Ji Jinmei, Lobsang Sandan, Yaoxi Zeren Zhuoma, Liuxia Tudeng Taba, Gonglin Wusijincun, and Ngawang Jinba.

Yang Jingren and Sa Kongliao, Vice Chairmen of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, members in Beijing, and Shi Rao Jiatsuo, acting president of the Buddhist Association of China, and others saw them off at the station.

[This newspaper] reported: Dalai Lama Tenzin Gyatso, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Panchen Erdeni Chokyi Gyaltsen, a member of the Standing Committee, arrived in Tianjin by special train at 12:10 PM on the 13th. Huang Huoqing, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chairman of the Tianjin Municipal Consultative Committee, and Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and more than 300 representatives from all walks of life in Tianjin welcomed them at the station. After the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni got off the train, they accepted flowers presented by Young Pioneers.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼离京去各地参观

1954-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼离京去各地参观
    【新华社十三日讯】全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措，常务委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞今日九时乘专车离京去外地参观。
    陪同他们去外地参观的有：中共中央统一战线工作部副部长刘格平、中华人民共和国民族事务委员会第一司副司长平措旺阶等。
    随同他们去外地参观的有：林仓活佛、赤江活佛、噶玛巴活佛、索康·旺清格勒、阿沛·阿旺晋美、计晋美、洛桑三旦、尧西·泽仁卓玛、柳霞·土登塔巴、公林·乌斯晋村、纳旺金巴等僧俗官员及达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼的家属和随员共一百多人。
    到车站欢送的有：中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任杨静仁、萨空了和在京委员及中国佛教协会代理会长喜饶嘉错等人。
    【本报讯】全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措，常务委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，已在十三日十二时十分乘专车到达天津。到车站欢迎的有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、天津市协商委员会主席、中国共产党天津市委员会书记黄火青和天津市各界代表三百多人。达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼下车后，接受了少年先锋队队员的献花。
