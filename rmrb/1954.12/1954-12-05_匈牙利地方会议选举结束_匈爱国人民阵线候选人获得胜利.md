Title: Hungarian Local Council Elections Conclude; Patriotic People's Front Candidates Victorious
Author: 
Date: 1954-12-05
Page: 4

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 2nd】According to the Hungarian News Agency, in the local council elections held on November 28th, 97.4 percent of the electorate voted, of whom 97.9 percent voted for the candidates of the Patriotic People's Front, according to an announcement by the Hungarian National Election Committee.

### 匈牙利地方会议选举结束  匈爱国人民阵线候选人获得胜利

1954-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利地方会议选举结束
    匈爱国人民阵线候选人获得胜利
　　【新华社布拉格二日电】据匈牙利通讯社讯：根据匈牙利全国选举委员会发表的公告，在十一月二十八日举行的地方会议选举中，有百分之九十七点四的选民投了票，其中百分之九十七点九的投票选民投了爱国人民阵线候选人的票。
