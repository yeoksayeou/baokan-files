    Our Great Motherland
    The Yabulai Salt Lake in the Alxa Banner of the Mongolian Autonomous Region of Gansu Province, contains abundant natural crystalline white granulated salt. The workers of this salt lake completed 156% of the state's original production plan this year. The workers of this salt field are packaging the salt in preparation for shipment.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    甘肃省蒙古自治区阿拉善旗境内沙漠中的雅布赖盐池，蕴藏着丰富的天然结晶的颗粒洁白的食盐。这个盐池的工人们今年完成了国家原订生产计划的百分之一百五十六。这个盐场的工人正在将盐装包，准备外运。
