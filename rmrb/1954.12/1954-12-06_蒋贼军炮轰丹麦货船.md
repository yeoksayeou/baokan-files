Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Shell Danish Cargo Ship
Author:
Date: 1954-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Hong Kong: According to the captain of the Danish cargo ship "Emily Maersk," Billert Flemming, on November 29th, his cargo ship, en route from Japan, was shelled on the 28th by Chiang Kai-shek bandits stationed on a small island approximately eighty miles northeast of Xiamen. He pointed out that two hours before the Chiang Kai-shek bandits began shelling, an American aircraft had flown over his ship.

### 蒋贼军炮轰丹麦货船

1954-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋贼军炮轰丹麦货船
   【新华社讯】香港消息：据丹麦货船“埃米利·马尔斯克号”的船长比尔特·弗林明十一月二十九日说，他的从日本开出的货船二十八日遭到盘踞在厦门东北八十英里左右一个小岛上的蒋贼军的炮轰。他指出，在蒋贼军开始炮轰之前两小时，有一架美国飞机曾在他的船上空飞行。
