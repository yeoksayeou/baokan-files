Title: Snowfall in Inner Mongolia and North China
Author:
Date: 1954-12-01
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency

    Snow fell in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, North China, and successively in East China and North-Central China from November 26th to 29th. This snowfall was the most widespread since the beginning of winter this year. It extended from the Sino-Mongolian border in the north, connected to the rainy area of the Yangtze and Huai River basins in the south, from west of the Hetao area to the coast in the east. This snowfall was also the heaviest since the beginning of winter this year. The southern part of Rehe Province, where the snowfall was heaviest, had more than half a foot of snow. The North China Plain generally had more than ten millimeters, with the Shijiazhuang area in central Hebei Province reaching twenty-three millimeters; on the 28th, the snow in Beijing was four inches thick. The snow near Hetao was also about three inches thick. Currently, the rain and snow in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and North China have stopped.

### 内蒙古和华北等地降雪

1954-12-01
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古和华北等地降雪
    十一月二十六日到二十九日间，我国内蒙古自治区、华北地区和华东、中南北部先后下雪。这场雪是今年入冬以来下得最广的一次。北自中蒙边境，南和江淮流域雨区相连，西自河套以西，东到海滨。这场雪也是今年入冬以来下得最大的一次。其中下得最大的热河省南部地区，积雪半尺以上。华北平原一般都超过十公厘，河北省中部石家庄一带达到二十三公厘；二十八日北京积雪厚达四寸。河套附近积雪也有三寸左右。现在，内蒙古自治区和华北地区雨雪已停。（新华社）
