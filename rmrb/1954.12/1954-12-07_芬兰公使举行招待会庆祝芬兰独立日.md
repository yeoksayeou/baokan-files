Title: Finnish Envoy Holds Reception to Celebrate Finnish Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1954-12-07
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, December 6th: Finnish Envoy to China, Sun Shudong, held a reception on the evening of the 6th to celebrate Finnish Independence Day.
    Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, was invited to attend the reception.
    Also attending the reception were Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Li Jishen and Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso; member Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain; Secretary-General of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan; Ministers and Vice Ministers of various ministries; Deputy Directors of various commissions; Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhang Zongxun; Vice Mayor of Beijing, Zhang Youyu; and heads of organizations such as the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, as well as figures from the capital's science, literature and art, and journalism circles.
    Also attending the reception were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.
    A total of more than 300 people attended the reception.
    At the reception, Envoy Sun Shudong and Premier Zhou Enlai delivered speeches (full text published separately).
    The reception proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere.
    A dance was held after the reception.

### 芬兰公使举行招待会庆祝芬兰独立日

1954-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰公使举行招待会庆祝芬兰独立日
    【新华社六日讯】　芬兰共和国驻我国特命全权公使孙士敦六日晚举行招待会，庆祝芬兰独立日。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的，还有国务院副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措和委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，国务院秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，各部部长、副部长，各委员会副主任，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊，北京市副市长张友渔，以及中国人民对外文化协会、中国人民外交学会、中国国际贸易促进委员会等团体的负责人，首都科学、文学艺术、新闻界人士。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    出席招待会的共有三百多人。
    在招待会上，孙士敦公使和周恩来总理都讲了话（全文另发）。
    招待会在友好、热烈的气氛中进行。
    在招待会后，还举行了舞会。
