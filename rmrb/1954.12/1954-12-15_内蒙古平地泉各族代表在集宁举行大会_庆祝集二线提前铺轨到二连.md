Title: Inner Mongolia Pingdi Quan Representatives of All Ethnic Groups Hold a Meeting in Jining to Celebrate the Advanced Track Laying of the Ji'er Railway to Erenhot
Author:
Date: 1954-12-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jining, December 14th. Representatives from all ethnic groups and all sectors of Pingdi Quan Administrative Region, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, held a meeting in Jining at 2:00 PM on December 14th to celebrate the advanced track laying of the Ji'er Railway to Erenhot.

    Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the People's Republic of China, Wang Zaitian, Standing Committee Member of the Inner Mongolia Sub-bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Zhao Zhanshan, Secretary of the Local Committee of the CPC Pingdi Quan Administrative Region, Li Wenjing, Vice Chairman of the Pingdi Quan Administrative Region, Hexingge, Deputy League Leader of Xilingol League, Soviet experts Kondakov, Halikov, Ivan Yok, as well as representatives of railway workers, labor models, and advanced workers from the Third Engineering Bureau of the Ministry of Railways attended the meeting. More than 900 people attended the meeting.

    The meeting began in the solemn singing of "Ode to the Motherland." First, Zhao Zhanshan, Secretary of the Local Committee of the CPC Pingdi Quan Administrative Region, spoke. Zhao Zhanshan congratulated all railway workers who completed the track laying task ahead of schedule in the severe winter, braving strong winds and heavy snow. He said: This railway has created favorable conditions for the development of production and the improvement of life for farmers and herdsmen in the administrative region. Therefore, the laboring people of all ethnic groups in the region are jubilantly looking forward to the opening of this railway.

    Next, Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Ministry of Railways, spoke (full text of the summary to be issued separately).

    Wang Zaitian, Standing Committee Member of the Inner Mongolia Sub-bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, spoke. He said: The starting and ending points of this railway in our country are both in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. It has a significant role in promoting the construction undertakings of our autonomous region, is conducive to the development of China's socialist construction undertakings, and will further strengthen the political, economic, and cultural ties between China, the Soviet Union, and Mongolia. Wang Zaitian expressed cordial greetings and congratulations on behalf of the Inner Mongolia Sub-bureau of the CPC Central Committee and the government, military region, and people's organizations of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. He said: In the future, the people of the entire Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region will further exert their greatest efforts in terms of manpower, materials, and financial resources to support the construction of this railway.

    Han Hongfu, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Political Department of the Ministry of Railways, Xiao Hongqi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, Hexingge, Deputy League Leader of Xilingol League, Pandi, Deputy Banner Leader of the Right Wing Rear Banner of Chahar, Jia Huaiji, Acting Director of the Third Engineering Bureau of the Ministry of Railways, and representatives of farmers and herdsmen and model road builders also spoke at the meeting. Zhang Yulian, Deputy Director of the Xinfen Agricultural Production Cooperative in Jining County, said: The construction of the railway allows us to enter socialist society faster. It makes the lives of farmers more fulfilling and happier. Ding Wanchen, a national railway labor model, said: We must ensure that we complete the task of opening to traffic and joint transportation in 1955 and do a good job in increasing production and practicing economy.

    The meeting concluded with the reading of congratulatory letters from various places. Jia Huaiji, Acting Director of the Third Engineering Bureau, and Wang Chunhua, Director of the Political Department, accepted the flags presented by the party and government and mass organizations of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the Pingdi Quan Administrative Region, Xilingol League, etc.

    In the evening, the Pingdi Quan Administrative Region held a party to entertain all railway workers.

### 内蒙古平地泉各族代表在集宁举行大会  庆祝集二线提前铺轨到二连

1954-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    内蒙古平地泉各族代表在集宁举行大会
    庆祝集二线提前铺轨到二连
    【新华社集宁十四日电】内蒙古自治区平地泉行政区各族各界代表十四日下午二时在集宁举行大会，庆祝集二线提前铺轨到二连。
    中华人民共和国铁道部部长滕代远，中共中央内蒙古分局常务委员、内蒙古自治区副主席王再天，中共平地泉行政区地方委员会书记赵展山，平地泉行政区副主席李文精，锡林郭勒盟副盟长和兴革，苏联专家康达柯夫，哈利克夫，依万约克，以及铁道部第三工程局铁路职工代表、劳动模范和先进工作者等都参加了大会。到会的共九百多人。
    大会在“歌唱祖国”的庄严歌声中开始。首先由中共平地泉行政区地方委员会书记赵展山讲话。赵展山对在严寒季节里冒着大风大雪提前完成铺轨任务的全体铁路职工祝贺。他说：这条铁路为行政区内农牧民的生产的发展和生活的改善创造了有利条件，因此全区各族劳动人民都欢欣鼓舞地期待着这条铁路的通车。
    接着，铁道部部长滕代远讲话（全文摘要另发）。
    中共中央内蒙古分局常务委员、内蒙古自治区副主席王再天讲话。他说：这条铁路在我国境内起点和终点都在内蒙古自治区，它对我们自治区的各项建设事业都有重大促进作用，有利于我国社会主义建设事业的开展，也将进一步加强中、苏、蒙三国之间政治、经济、文化的联系。王再天代表中共中央内蒙古分局和内蒙古自治区的政府、军区、人民团体表示亲切的慰问和祝贺。他说：今后内蒙古自治区全区人民将更进一步从人力、物力、财力方面尽最大努力来支援这条铁路的修建。
    在大会上讲话的还有铁道部政治部宣传部部长韩洪富、中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席萧洪启、锡林郭勒盟副盟长和兴革、察哈尔右翼后旗副旗长潘迪、铁道部第三工程局代局长贾怀济及农牧民代表和筑路劳动模范等。集宁县辛奋农业生产合作社副社长张玉莲说：铁路的修建使我们更快地走入社会主义社会，它使农民们生活得更美满，更幸福。全国铁路劳动模范丁万臣说：我们一定要保证在一九五五年内完成通车联运任务，做好增产节约工作。
    大会最后宣读了各地寄来的贺信，第三工程局代局长贾怀济、政治部主任王春华接受了内蒙古自治区及平地泉行政区、锡林郭勒盟等党政及群众团体的赠旗。
    晚上，平地泉行政区举行晚会招待全体铁路职工。
