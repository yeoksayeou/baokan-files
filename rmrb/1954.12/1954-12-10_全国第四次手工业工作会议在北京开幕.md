Title: Fourth National Handicraft Work Conference Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-12-10
Page: 2

    Fourth National Handicraft Work Conference Opens in Beijing
    The Fourth National Handicraft Work Conference opened in Beijing on the 8th. More than 100 representatives attended the meeting, including those from the departments in charge of handicrafts under the Party committees of various provinces and cities, the handicraft management systems of various provinces and cities, the handicraft production cooperative federations, and relevant central departments.
    The conference will review the organizational development and production plan execution of handicraft production cooperatives in 1954, summarize work experience, discuss the main problems existing in handicraft work, and propose specific solutions. The conference will also discuss the organizational development and production plan for handicrafts in 1955, study the main measures for implementing and completing the plan, discuss the responsibilities and staffing of handicraft management organs at all levels, and the business and staffing of handicraft production federations at all levels. In addition, the conference will also discuss the articles of association of the city (county) handicraft production cooperative federations, handicraft production cooperatives, handicraft supply and marketing production cooperatives, the organizational rules of the handicraft supply and marketing production groups, and other related issues.
    At the opening ceremony, Bai Rubing, Director of the Central Handicraft Administration Bureau, delivered the opening speech. He asked the delegates to conscientiously summarize the experiences of the past year according to the requirements of the conference, and study the tasks for 1955 and the specific measures to ensure the completion of the tasks, and work together to make the conference a success, so that the conference can achieve the expected results.

### 全国第四次手工业工作会议在北京开幕

1954-12-10
第2版()
专栏：

    全国第四次手工业工作会议在北京开幕
    全国第四次手工业工作会议八日在北京开幕。出席会议的，有各省市党委系统主管手工业的部门各省市手工业管理系统、手工业生产社联合社以及中央有关各部门的代表共一百多人。
    会议将检查一九五四年手工业生产合作社的组织发展和生产计划执行的情况，总结工作经验，讨论手工业工作中存在的主要问题，并提出解决问题的具体方案。会议并将讨论一九五五年手工业组织发展和生产计划，研究执行和完成计划的主要措施；讨论各级手工业管理机关的职责和编制，各级手工业生产联社的业务和编制；研究全国手工业联社的筹备工作。此外，会议还要讨论市（县）手工业生产合作社联合社、手工业生产合作社、手工业供销生产合作社的章程和手工业供销生产小组的组织简则及其他有关的问题。
    在开幕式上，由中央手工业管理局局长白如冰致开幕词。他要求到会代表根据会议要求，认真总结一年来的经验，和研究一九五五年任务和保证完成任务的具体措施，共同努力把会议开好，使会议能得到预期的效果。
