Title: China and Vietnam Sign Various Protocols and Agreements on Transportation and Water Conservancy Issues
Author:
Date: 1954-12-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th: After talks in Beijing between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on transportation and water conservancy issues, various protocols, agreements, additional protocols to the agreements, and meeting minutes regarding transportation and water conservancy issues between China and Vietnam were signed on December 24th.

When signing the Protocol on Assisting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Repairing Railways, the Protocol on Assisting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Restoring Postal and Telecommunications Services, the Meeting Minutes on Assisting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Repairing Highways, Shipping, and Water Conservancy Issues, and the Meeting Minutes on Civil Aviation Traffic between China and Vietnam and Assisting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Establishing Civil Aviation Stations and Meteorological Equipment, the signatories were: on behalf of the Chinese side, Lu Zhengcao, Head of the Transportation Delegation of the People's Republic of China and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the People's Republic of China; and on behalf of the Vietnamese side, Nguyen Van Tran, Head of the Transportation Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Transportation and Public Works of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

When signing the Postal Agreement, the Telecommunications Agreement, and the Additional Protocols to the two agreements, the signatories were: on behalf of the Chinese side, Wang Zigang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China; and on behalf of the Vietnamese side, Nguyen Van Tran, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Transportation and Public Works of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister and Director of the Asian Affairs Department; Wang Shitai, Vice Head of the Transportation Delegation of the People's Republic of China; Wang Zigang; and delegation members Liu Jianzhang, Gao Dengbang, Su Younong, Chen Ruiguang, and Ma Ben. On the Vietnamese side were: Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China; and Pham Binh, Second Secretary.

On the evening of the 24th, Lu Zhengcao, Head of the Transportation Delegation of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet in honor of Nguyen Van Tran, Head of the Transportation Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 中越签订交通和水利问题各项议定书和协定

1954-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中越签订交通和水利问题各项议定书和协定
    【新华社二十八日讯】中华人民共和国政府同越南民主共和国政府在北京进行关于交通和水利问题的会谈以后，十二月二十四日签订了中越两国间关于交通和水利问题的各项议定书、协定和协定的附加议定书和会谈记录。
    双方在签订关于援助越南民主共和国修复铁路议定书，关于援助越南民主共和国恢复邮政电信议定书，关于援助越南民主共和国修复公路、航运、水利问题会谈记录，关于中、越两国民用航空通航和援助越南民主共和国建立民用航空站、气象设备问题会谈记录时，代表中国方面签字的是中华人民共和国交通代表团团长、中华人民共和国铁道部副部长吕正操，代表越南方面签字的是越南民主共和国交通代表团团长、越南民主共和国交通公政部副部长阮文珍。
    双方在签订邮政协定、电信协定和两个协定的附加议定书时，代表中国方面签字的是中华人民共和国邮电部副部长王子纲，代表越南方面签字的是越南民主共和国交通公政部副部长阮文珍。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念，外交部副部长章汉夫、部长助理兼亚洲司司长陈家康，中华人民共和国交通代表团副团长王世泰、王子纲，团员刘建章、高登榜、苏幼农、陈瑞光、马奔，越南方面有：越南驻我国大使馆临时代办周亮、二等秘书范平。
    二十四日晚间，中华人民共和国交通代表团团长吕正操设宴招待越南民主共和国交通代表团团长阮文珍。
