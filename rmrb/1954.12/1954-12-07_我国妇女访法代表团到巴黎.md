Title: Chinese Women's Delegation to France Arrives in Paris
Author:
Date: 1954-12-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 6th: The Chinese Women's Delegation to France, led by head of delegation Zhang Yun, arrived in Paris on the 3rd and 4th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Madame Brunot Delamarre, Professor Jean Delsinne, chairman of the guidance committee of the France-China Friendship Association, Madame Mollet, secretary of the French Women's Union, and French women and people from all walks of life who had visited China.

The delegation is visiting at the friendly invitation of the French Reception Committee headed by Madame Brunot Delamarre.

The head of the delegation is Zhang Yun, vice chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. The members include: Lei Jieqiong, vice provost of Beijing College of Political Science and Law, Lu Shijia, professor of Tsinghua University, Qi Yun, a women's worker, and Zou Dexin, editor of "New China Women."

### 我国妇女访法代表团到巴黎

1954-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

    我国妇女访法代表团到巴黎
    【新华社六日讯】　中国妇女访法代表团由团长章蕴率领，在三日、四日先后飞抵巴黎。
    代表团代表到达时，前往机场欢迎的有布吕纳·德拉马尔夫人，法中友协指导委员会主席让·德莱希教授，法兰西妇女联盟书记摩莱夫人和访问过中国的法国妇女界和各界人士。
    代表团是应以布吕纳·德拉马尔夫人为首的法国接待委员会的邀请，前往作友好访问的。
    代表团的团长是中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴，团员有：北京政法学院副教务长雷洁琼、清华大学教授陆士嘉、妇女工作者戚云、“新中国妇女”编辑邹得心等。
