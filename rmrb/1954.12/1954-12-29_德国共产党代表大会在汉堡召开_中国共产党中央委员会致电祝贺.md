Title: German Communist Party Congress Convenes in Hamburg; Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1954-12-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 28th: TASS, Dusseldorf reports that the Central Committee of the German Communist Party announced: According to the resolution of the 14th Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the German Communist Party, the German Communist Party Congress is scheduled to convene in Hamburg on December 28th this year.

The agenda of the congress includes: the report of the Central Committee of the German Communist Party, the report of the Central Inspection Committee, the report of the Party Discipline Inspection Commission, and the election of central party organs.

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to Max Reimann, Chairman of the German Communist Party, congratulating the German Communist Party Congress convened in Hamburg on the 28th. The telegram reads as follows: To Comrade Max Reimann, Chairman of the Central Committee of the German Communist Party, and to all delegates of the German Communist Party Congress:

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends its heartfelt congratulations to the German Communist Party Congress! We firmly believe that the convening of this congress of the German Communist Party will enable the German people to achieve greater success in strengthening the struggle against the revival of German militarism, striving for the peace, democracy, unity, and European collective security of Germany, striving for the legal status of the German Communist Party, and defending the interests of the German working class! The Chinese people and the people of the world support your struggle. Congratulations on the success of the congress!

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 德国共产党代表大会在汉堡召开  中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺

1954-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    德国共产党代表大会在汉堡召开
    中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社柏林讯：德意志通讯社杜塞尔多夫消息，德国共产党中央委员会宣布：根据德国共产党中央委员会第十四次全体会议的决议，德国共产党代表大会定在今年十二月二十八日在汉堡召开。
    代表大会的议程包括：德国共产党中央委员会的报告，中央检查委员会的报告，党监察委员会的报告和选举党中央机构。
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国共产党中央委员会致电德国共产党主席雷曼，祝贺二十八日在汉堡召开的德国共产党代表大会。电文如下：德国共产党中央委员会亲爱的马克斯·雷曼同志并转德国共产党代表大会全体代表同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向德国共产党代表大会致衷心的祝贺！我们深信，德国共产党这次代表大会的召开，必将使德国人民在加强反对复活德国军国主义，争取德国的和平、民主、统一和欧洲集体安全，争取德国共产党的合法地位、保卫德国工人阶级的利益的斗争中，获得更大的成就！中国人民和世界人民支持你们的斗争。祝贺大会的成功！
    中国共产党中央委员会
