Title: Comrade Zhu De Leads CPC Delegation to Warsaw to Attend Polish United Workers' Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-03-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 6th:

    Invited to Attend the Polish United Workers' Party Congress
    Comrade Zhu De Leads CPC Delegation to Warsaw
    Xinhua News Agency, March 6th: A Chinese Communist Party delegation led by Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, left Beijing for Warsaw by Tu-104 aircraft this morning at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party to attend the Third National Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party. Members of the Chinese Communist Party delegation include: Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Wu Xiuquan, Member of the CPC Central Committee, and Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland. Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, Li Xiannian, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Members of the Central Committee, Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Kang Keqing, Vice Chairman of the National Women's Federation. Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin and Hungarian Ambassador to China Nogradi also went to the airport to see them off. Polish Ambassador to China Kirilok returned to Warsaw on the same plane. Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 6th: The Chinese Communist Party delegation led by Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, arrived in Moscow at 3:20 pm today by Tu-104 aircraft on its way to Poland to attend the Third Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Kozlov and Mikoyan, Members of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, Pospelov, Alternate Member of the Presidium, Tarasov, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Kuznetsov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    Also present at the airport to welcome them were Polish Ambassador to the Soviet Union Gaide and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao. (Attached picture)
    Comrade Zhu De waves goodbye to Comrade Zhou Enlai and others who saw him off at the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao

### 应邀参加波兰统一工人党代表大会  朱德同志率中共代表团去华沙

1959-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加波兰统一工人党代表大会
    朱德同志率中共代表团去华沙
    新华社6日讯　由中共中央副主席朱德率领的中国共产党代表团，应波兰统一工人党中央委员会的邀请参加波兰统一工人党第三次全国代表大会，已经在今天上午乘图—一○四飞机离开北京前往华沙。中国共产党代表团的团员有：中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，中共中央委员伍修权和中国驻波兰大使王炳南。前往机场送行的有：中共中央副主席周恩来，中央政治局委员李先念，中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员廖承志、刘宁一，外交部副部长曾涌泉，全国妇联副主席康克清。苏联驻中国大使尤金、匈牙利驻中国大使诺格拉第也到机场送行。波兰驻中国大使基里洛克同机回华沙。新华社莫斯科6日电　由中共中央副主席朱德率领的中国共产党代表团，在应邀前往波兰参加波兰统一工人党第三次代表大会的途中，在今天下午三时二十分乘“图—一○四”飞机抵达莫斯科。
    前往机场欢迎的有：苏共中央主席团委员科兹洛夫、米高扬，主席团候补委员波斯别洛夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席塔拉索夫，外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫等。
    到机场欢迎的还有波兰驻苏联大使盖德和我国驻苏联大使刘晓。（附图片）
