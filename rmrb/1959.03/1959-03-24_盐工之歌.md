Title: Song of the Salt Workers
Author: Liu Fuyong, Jiao Zhende
Date: 1959-03-24
Page: 2

Song of the Salt Workers

　　Making brine cannot be done without seawater,
　　drying salt cannot be done without the sun,
　　the liberated salt workers,
　　will never be separated from the Communist Party.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Jiao Zhende)

　　Zhang Yu boiled the sea with fire,
　　salt workers boil the sea with ponds,
　　we don't need the Dragon King's third princess,
　　we just want the Dragon King to offer salt treasures.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Liu Fuyong)

### 盐工之歌
刘福永  焦振德
1959-03-24
第2版()
专栏：

    盐工之歌制卤离不开海水，晒盐离不开太阳，翻了身的盐工，永远离不开共产党。
          （焦振德）张羽煮海用火烧，盐工煮海用池熬，不要龙王三公主，只要龙王献盐宝。
        （刘福永）
