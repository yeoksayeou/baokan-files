Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Receives Swedish Journalist
Author:
Date: 1959-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 27th—Vice Premier Chen Yi received Swedish Broadcasting Corporation journalist, Weikebumu, this afternoon.

Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present at the reception.

### 陈毅副总理接见瑞典记者

1959-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见瑞典记者
    新华社27日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了瑞典广播公司记者魏克布姆。
    接见时在座的有外交部新闻司副司长徐晃。
