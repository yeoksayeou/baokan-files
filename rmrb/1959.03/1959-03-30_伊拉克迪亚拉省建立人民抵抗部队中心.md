Title: People's Resistance Forces Center Established in Diyala Province, Iraq
Author:
Date: 1959-03-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, March 29th. Khalil Saeed, commander of the Iraqi Third Division stationed in Diyala, north of Baghdad, said in a statement to the local people that the Commander-in-Chief of the Iraqi Armed Forces has agreed to establish a center for the People's Resistance Forces in Diyala, and that the work of training the People's Resistance Forces will begin within a week in five centers in Diyala Province.

### 伊拉克迪亚拉省建立人民抵抗部队中心

1959-03-30
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克迪亚拉省建立人民抵抗部队中心
    新华社巴格达29日电　在巴格达以北迪亚拉驻防的伊拉克第三师师长哈利勒·赛义德在一项告当地人民的声明中说，伊拉克武装部队总司令已经同意在迪亚拉建立人民抵抗部队的中心，并且将在一星期以内，在迪亚拉省五个中心开始训练人民抵抗部队的工作。
