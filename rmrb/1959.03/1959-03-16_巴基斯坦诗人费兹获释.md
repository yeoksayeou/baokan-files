Title: Pakistani Poet Faiz Released
Author:
Date: 1959-03-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi. Faiz Ahmed Faiz, the famous Pakistani poet and editor-in-chief of the "Pakistan Times" and "Today" newspaper, was released in Lahore on the afternoon of the 12th.
    Faiz was arrested on December 16 last year. The authorities arrested him on the grounds that he had violated the "Public Safety Law." He attended the Asian-African Writers' Conference held in Tashkent last year.

### 巴基斯坦诗人费兹获释

1959-03-16
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦诗人费兹获释
    新华社卡拉奇电　巴基斯坦著名诗人、“巴基斯坦时报”和“今天报”的总编辑费兹·艾哈迈德·费兹12日下午在拉合尔获释。
    费兹是在去年12月16日被捕的。当局逮捕他的理由是他触犯了“公共安全法”。他曾出席去年在塔什干举行的亚非作家会议。
