Title: Taiyuan Nitrogen Fertilizer Plant Under Construction (Picture)
Author: Zhou Shuming
Date: 1959-03-21
Page: 2

Taiyuan Nitrogen Fertilizer Plant Under Construction
The Taiyuan Nitrogen Fertilizer Plant is one of the four major nitrogen fertilizer plants built with the assistance of the Soviet Union. It is now under accelerated construction. Our reporter Zhou Shuming photographed it.
