Author:
Date: 1959-03-25

Fujian Front in Spring

    Fujian front soldiers and the people on the front lines together are devoted to the spring agricultural production.
        Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Xuezeng photographed

    Fujian front female militia, in their spare time from labor, come to the artillery positions to learn artillery operations from the artillery soldiers. Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Xuezeng photographed

    Liu Hulan martyr's mother, Hu Wenxiu, came to the Fujian front with the Shanxi Provincial People's Fujian Front慰问 (comfort) delegation to express their regards. This is Mother Liu with the soldiers at the front.
      Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Xuezeng photographed

    The Shanxi Provincial People's Fujian Front慰问 (comfort) delegation came to the front lines to慰问 (express regards) to their relatives. This is them performing programs for the soldiers. Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Xuezeng photographed

### 福建前线的春天（图片）

1959-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

    福建前线的春天
    福建前线的战士们和前线人民一同投入春耕大生产。
        新华社记者　李学增摄
    福建前线的女民兵在劳动之余，来到炮兵阵地、向炮兵战士学习操炮。　新华社记者　李学增摄
    刘胡兰烈士的母亲胡文秀随山西省人民福建前线慰问团来到福建前线慰问。这是刘妈妈和前线战士们在一起。
      新华社记者　李学增摄
    山西省人民福建前线慰问团来到前线慰问亲人，这是他们在为战士们表演节目。      新华社记者　李学增摄
