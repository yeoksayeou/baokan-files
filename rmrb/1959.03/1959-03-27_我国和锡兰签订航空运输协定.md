Title: China and Ceylon Sign Aviation Transport Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-03-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 26th: The Civil Aviation Delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Civil Aviation Delegation of the Government of Ceylon, based on the principle of equality and mutual benefit, conducted full and friendly consultations on various issues related to the establishment of civil aviation traffic between China and Ceylon. Today in Beijing, they signed the "Aviation Transport Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of Ceylon" and the "Protocol on the Aviation Transport Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of Ceylon."

Signing the agreement and protocol on behalf of China was Wang Shoudao, head of the Civil Aviation Delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of China and Minister of Communications. Signing on behalf of Ceylon was Maitripala Senanayake, head of the Civil Aviation Delegation of the Government of Ceylon and Minister of Transport and Engineering of Ceylon.

Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ceylon's Ambassador to China, Gooneratne, and all members of both delegations attended the signing ceremony. In the evening, Wang Shoudao held a banquet to bid farewell to all members of the Civil Aviation Delegation of the Government of Ceylon.

### 我国和锡兰签订航空运输协定

1959-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和锡兰签订航空运输协定
    新华社26日讯　中华人民共和国政府民用航空代表团和锡兰政府民用航空代表团根据平等互利的原则，对中锡两国间建立民用航空交通的各项问题进行了充分和友好的协商后，今天在北京签订了“中华人民共和国政府和锡兰政府航空运输协定”和“中华人民共和国政府和锡兰政府关于航空运输协定的议定书”。
    在协定和议定书上签字的，中国方面是中华人民共和国政府民用航空代表团团长、交通部长王首道，锡兰方面是锡兰政府民用航空代表团团长、锡兰运输工程部长迈特里帕拉·森纳那亚克。
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，锡兰驻华大使高伯拉瓦和双方代表团的全体人员参加了签字仪式。晚上，王首道举行宴会欢送锡兰政府民用航空代表团的全体人员。
