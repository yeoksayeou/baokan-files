Title: American Scientist Admits Soviet Cosmic Rocket Thrust is a Hundred Times Greater Than American Rockets
Author:
Date: 1959-03-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 9th. American scientist, Moaz, said on the 7th that the Soviet Union has surpassed the United States in rocket technology. The thrust of the Soviet Union's cosmic rocket launch is equal to one hundred times the thrust of the American cosmic rocket "Pioneer IV" launched not long ago.

He said that if the Soviet Union wants to land its launched cosmic rocket on the moon, it can be expected to succeed. The factual basis for this conclusion is that after the Soviet cosmic rocket rises to within the moon's gravitational range, its fourth-stage engine is activated to propel it forward.

According to his opinion, humans will be able to land on the moon within two years.

### 美国一科学家承认  苏联宇宙火箭推力百倍于美国的火箭

1959-03-10
第5版()
专栏：

    美国一科学家承认
    苏联宇宙火箭推力百倍于美国的火箭
    新华社9日讯　纽约消息：美国科学家莫兹7日说，苏联在火箭技术上超过了美国。苏联发射宇宙火箭的推力，等于美国不久前发射的宇宙火箭“先驱者四号”推力的一百倍。
    他说，如果苏联想把它发射的宇宙火箭在月球上降落的话，那可以预料一定会成功的。这个论断的事实根据是：苏联宇宙火箭上升到月球引力范围内以后，它的第四级发动机才开动起来，推动它继续前进。
    根据他的意见，在两年以内，人类就可以登上月球。
