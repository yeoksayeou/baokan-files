Title: 30,000 Japanese Small and Medium Business Owners Oppose Government's Heavy Taxes
Author:
Date: 1959-03-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 4th -- Tokyo Dispatch: Simultaneously with the workers launching their spring struggle, thirty thousand small and medium business owners from various industries of the Japan National Federation of Small Business Associations today conducted collective bargaining with government tax offices in more than one hundred locations nationwide, demanding a halt to the collection of harsh taxes and recognition of self-assessment of tax amounts by business owners.

　　In Tokyo, after negotiating with district tax offices, the business owners also joined the march of the National People's Anti-War and Unemployment Grand Parade, which had just arrived in Tokyo today.

### 日本三万中小企业主  反对政府苛捐杂税

1959-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    日本三万中小企业主
    反对政府苛捐杂税
    新华社4日讯　东京消息：日本全国商工团体联合会的三万家各行业的中小企业主今天在工人展开春季斗争的同时，在全国一百数十多个地方同政府的税务署进行集体谈判，要求停止征收苛刻的捐税和承认企业主自己申报税额。
    在东京，企业主们到各区税务所谈判后，还参加了今天刚到达东京的日本人民反对战争和失业国民大游行队伍的游行。
