Title: Sudanese Government Willing to Mediate A-I Relations
Author:
Date: 1959-03-21
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, March 20th: The Sudanese government issued a note to the diplomatic representatives of Arab countries stationed in Khartoum on the 19th, stating that Sudan is willing to mediate the current tense relations between the United Arab Republic and Iraq.
    A Sudanese government spokesman said: "We hope this mediation will be successful. Our wish is to see all Arab countries united in their views." The Sudanese government has sent a copy of this note to the headquarters of the Arab League in Cairo.

### 苏丹政府愿调解阿伊关系

1959-03-21
第6版()
专栏：

    苏丹政府愿调解阿伊关系
    新华社20日讯　喀土穆消息：苏丹政府19日向驻喀土穆的阿拉伯各国外交代表发出了一份照会，表示苏丹愿意调解阿联和伊拉克之间目前的紧张关系。
    苏丹政府发言人说：“我们希望这项调解成功。我们的愿望是看到所有阿拉伯国家意见一致。”苏丹政府已经把这份照会的副本送交设在开罗的阿拉伯国家联盟总部。
