Title: International Commission for Vietnam Concludes: Felt's Activities in South Vietnam Violated the Geneva Accords
Author:
Date: 1959-03-29
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 28th. According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency, the International Commission for Vietnam, in a letter to the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army on February 17th, notified the People's Army side that the International Commission had concluded that the entry of Felt, Commander-in-Chief of the United States Pacific Command, into South Vietnam on September 19th of last year, violated Article 16(e) of the Geneva Accords on the Cessation of Hostilities in Vietnam.
    Felt arrived in Saigon from Bangkok on September 19th of last year after attending the SEATO military advisors meeting in Bangkok. In Saigon, he met with personnel from the U.S. Military Assistance Advisory Group, and also met with Ngo Dinh Diem and many high-ranking military personnel from South Vietnam, discussing the implementation of U.S. military plans to further create tension in Indochina and Southeast Asia.

### 越南国际委员会作出结论  费耳特到南越活动违反日内瓦协议

1959-03-29
第3版()
专栏：

    越南国际委员会作出结论
    费耳特到南越活动违反日内瓦协议
    新华社河内28日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南国际委员会在2月17日写信给越南人民军总司令部联络代表团，通知人民军方面说，国际委员会已经作出结论，认为美国太平洋地区部队总司令费耳特去年9月19日进入南越，违反了日内瓦会议关于在越南停止敌对行动的协定第十六条巳款的规定。
    费耳特去年9月在出席了在曼谷举行的东南亚条约组织军事顾问会议以后，在去年9月19日从曼谷到达西贡。他在西贡接见了美国军事顾问团的人员，还会见了吴庭艳和南越的许多高级军事人员，同他们讨论了关于实施美国军事计划以进一步在印度支那和东南亚地区制造紧张局势的问题。
