Title: Swiss Confederation Appoints René Naville as Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1959-03-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Swiss Confederation has decided to appoint Mr. René Naville as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Swiss Confederation to the People's Republic of China.

### 瑞士联邦任纳维义为驻华大使

1959-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞士联邦任纳维义为驻华大使
    新华社20日讯　瑞士联邦已决定任命雷纳·纳维义先生为瑞士联邦驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。
