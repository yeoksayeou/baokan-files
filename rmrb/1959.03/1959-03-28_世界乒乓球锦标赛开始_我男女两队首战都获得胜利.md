Title: World Table Tennis Championships Begin, Our Men's and Women's Teams Both Win Their First Matches
Author:
Date: 1959-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: The 25th World Table Tennis Championships began at 9:00 AM (Dortmund time) on the 27th in Dortmund, West Germany. The Chinese men's and women's teams both achieved victory in their opening matches. The men's team defeated the Portuguese team with a score of 5-0.

The Chinese men's team players were: Rong Guotuan, Wang Chuanyao, Yang Ruihua; the Portuguese team players were: Kurher, Kalua, Luo.

The scores of this match were:

Rong Guotuan – Kurher 2-0 (21-14, 21-7)
Wang Chuanyao – Kalua 2-1 (20-22, 21-17, 21-16)
Yang Ruihua – Luo 2-0 (21-14, 21-14)
Wang Chuanyao – Kurher 2-0 (21-11, 23-21)
Rong Guotuan – Luo 2-0 (21-13, 21-18)

In the World Table Tennis Championships, the Chinese men's team and the Portuguese team have been placed in the same group for the third time. The first time was at the 23rd Championships in 1956, when the Chinese team won 5-1. The second time was at the 24th Championships in 1957, when the Chinese team again won with a score of 5-0.

In the women's team event on the 27th, seeded teams China, Romania, and Japan all defeated Spain, Greece, and Italy with scores of 3-0, respectively.

The scores of the Chinese women's team's victory over the Spanish team were as follows:

Sun Meiying – Navarro (21-11, 21-6)
Qiu Zhonghui – Hespital (21-2, 21-16)
Sun Meiying and Qiu Zhonghui (doubles) – Hespital and Guli (22-20, 21-7)

In the second day's schedule, the Chinese men's team will play against Ireland and Belgium, and the Chinese women's team will play against Switzerland and the Netherlands.

The situation of the men's team event on the 27th was as follows: Seeded teams Hungary, China, and Japan have already played their matches and defeated their opponents with an absolute advantage of 5-0. The fourth seeded team, Czechoslovakia, only began their matches in the afternoon of the 27th.

The men's team matches ended in the morning: First group: Hungary beat Switzerland 5-0, Romania beat Venezuela 5-0, Brazil beat Norway 5-1.

Second group: China beat Portugal 5-0, West Germany beat Jamaica 5-0, Sweden beat Ireland 5-0, United Arab Republic beat Belgium 5-4.

Third group: Yugoslavia beat Denmark 5-0, India beat Canada 5-0, Japan beat Italy 5-0, German Democratic Republic beat the Netherlands 5-4, Austria beat Luxembourg 5-0.

Fourth group: Wales beat Malta 5-0, England beat Greece 5-0, Spain beat Lebanon 5-1.

### 世界乒乓球锦标赛开始  我男女两队首战都获得胜利

1959-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    世界乒乓球锦标赛开始
    我男女两队首战都获得胜利
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：第二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛27日上午九时（多特蒙德时间）在西德多特蒙德开始。中国男女两队首战告捷。男队以五比零的比数击败葡萄牙队。
    中国男队出场的是：容国团、王传耀、杨瑞华；葡萄牙队出场的是：库尔赫、卡卢阿、罗。
    这一场的比数是：
    容国团——库尔赫二比零（二十一比十四，二十一比七）
    王传耀——卡卢阿二比一（二十比二十二，二十一比十七，二十一比十六）
    杨瑞华——罗二比零（二十一比十四，二十一比十四）
    王传耀——库尔赫二比零（二十一比十一，二十三比二十一）
    容国团——罗二比零（二十一比十三，二十一比十八）
    在世界乒乓球锦标赛中，中国男子队和葡萄牙队已经是第三次分在同一个小组里了，第一次是1956年第二十三届，当时中国队以五比一获胜，第二次是1957年第二十四届，中国队以五比零的比数又获得了胜利。
    在27日女子团体赛中，种子队中国、罗马尼亚和日本队都以三比零分别战胜了西班牙、希腊和意大利队。
    中国女子队击败西班牙队的比数如下：
    孙梅英——那瓦罗（二十一比十一，二十一比六）
    丘钟惠——赫斯匹塔尔（二十一比二，二十一比十六）
    孙梅英和丘钟惠（双打）——赫斯匹塔尔和固里（二十二比二十，二十一比七）
    在第二天的比赛日程中，中国男子队将对爱尔兰队和比利时队，中国女子队将对瑞士和荷兰队。
    27日男子团体赛情况如下：比赛中种子队匈牙利、中国和日本队都已经进行了比赛，而且都是以五比○的绝对优势击败了他们的对手。第四组种子队捷克斯洛伐克队27日下午才开始比赛。
    男子团体比赛上午结束：第一组：匈牙利胜瑞士——五比○，罗马尼亚胜委内瑞拉——五比○，巴西胜挪威——五比一。
    第二组：中国胜葡萄牙——五比○，西德胜牙买加——五比○，瑞典胜爱尔兰——五比○，阿联胜比利时——五比四。
    第三组：南斯拉夫胜丹麦——五比○，印度胜加拿大——五比○，日本胜意大利——五比○，德意志民主共和国胜荷兰——五比四，奥地利胜卢森堡——五比○。
    第四组：威尔士胜马耳他——五比○，英格兰胜希腊——五比○，西班牙胜黎巴嫩——五比一。
