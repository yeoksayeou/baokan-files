Title: Indian Newspapers Comment on Iraqi-UAR Relations: Iraq Unwilling to Merge with UAR; Nasser Has No Reason to Interfere
Author:
Date: 1959-03-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 20th: The "Free Press" published an editorial today commenting on the situation caused by the attacks on Iraq by the authorities of the United Arab Republic, stating, "The current quarrel is not in the interest of the Arabs, but only satisfies those countries that the Arabs drove away at great cost."

The newspaper said, "What is even more regrettable is: Frankly, the cause of the quarrel is not a question of communism or nationalism, but of egoism. It is incomprehensible why the UAR cannot show a more conciliatory and tolerant spirit in letting Iraq plan its affairs in its own way."

Commenting on Iraq's current unwillingness to merge into the United Arab Republic, the newspaper said, "This cannot be a reason for Nasser to interfere in the internal affairs of another sovereign Arab country." The newspaper also pointed out, "Baghdad has no special obligation to formulate policies that please Cairo. Moreover, it has enough prestige and strength to quell sudden rebellions."

The latest issue of the cartoon magazine "Shankar's Weekly" pointed out, "Once any conflict occurs, it is not the Egyptians or the Iraqis who will benefit, but the foreign-operated Iraqi oil companies." The weekly pointed out that Iraq's position on the issue of whether to merge with the UAR is "based on sound political reasons." It said, "Iraq's Qasim, to some extent, represents the more rational and calmer aspect of Arab nationalism."

A telegraphic report from the "Blitz" weekly's London correspondent in its latest issue stated, "Obviously, the current developments between Iraq and the UAR can easily benefit the imperialist countries."

### 印度报纸评论伊阿关系  伊拉克不愿意与阿联合并  纳赛尔没有理由加以干涉

1959-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    印度报纸评论伊阿关系
    伊拉克不愿意与阿联合并
    纳赛尔没有理由加以干涉
    新华社新德里20日电　“自由新闻”今天发表社论，评论由于阿拉伯联合共和国当局对伊拉克的攻击而引起的局势说，“目前的争吵对阿拉伯不利，而只是使那些由阿拉伯人花了很大的代价才赶走的国家感到满意。”
    这家报纸说，“更可惜的是：坦率地说，争吵的起因不是为了共产主义或民族主义的问题，而是为了利己主义。在让伊拉克按照自己的方式来计划自己的事务这个问题上，不能理解为甚么阿联不能表现出较为和解和容忍的精神。”
    报纸在评论目前伊拉克不愿意合并到阿拉伯联合共和国中的问题时说，“这不能成为使纳赛尔干涉另外一个阿拉伯主权国家的内政的理由。”报纸还指出，“巴格达方面没有要制订使开罗欢心的政策的特别义务。而且，它有足够的威信和力量来平息突发的叛乱。”
    最近一期的漫画杂志“山卡尔周刊”指出，“一旦发生任何冲突，能够得到利益的不是埃及人或伊拉克人，而是外国开办的伊拉克石油公司。”这家周刊指出，伊拉克在是否同阿联合并这个问题上所采取的立场，是“以健全的政治理由为基础的。”它说，“伊拉克的卡塞姆，在某种程度上，代表了阿拉伯民族主义的比较合理和比较冷静的一方面。”
    最近一期的“闪电”周报刊登的这家报纸驻伦敦记者的一篇电讯报道说，“显然，目前伊拉克和阿联之间的发展情况，很容易让帝国主义国家得到好处。”
