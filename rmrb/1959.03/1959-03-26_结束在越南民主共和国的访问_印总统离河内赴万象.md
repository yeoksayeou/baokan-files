Title: End of Indian President's Visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam: Departure from Hanoi to Vientiane
Author:
Date: 1959-03-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi: After a three-day friendly visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Indian President Prasad left Hanoi by plane on the morning of the 25th for Vientiane, the capital of the Kingdom of Laos.

The citizens of Hanoi gathered on both sides of the nearly ten-kilometer-long road from the Presidential Palace to the airport, cheering and bidding farewell to President Prasad.

President Prasad, accompanied by President Ho Chi Minh, reviewed the guard of honor at the airport and then delivered a farewell speech.

In his speech, he expressed his gratitude to President Ho Chi Minh for his brotherly feelings and the warm reception given to him and his entourage.

President Prasad said that the Indian people highly value the brotherly friendship of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam with the Republic of India. He expressed his sincere hope that the brotherly friendship between the two countries, which dates back to ancient times, will become even closer in the future.

President Prasad concluded by shouting: "Long live the Democratic Republic of Vietnam! Long live the friendship between Vietnam and India!"

In his farewell address, President Ho Chi Minh said that President Prasad's visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam would help further consolidate peace in Asia, Africa, and the world. He said that in his talks with the Indian President, they had reached consensus on many issues, especially on strengthening and developing the friendship between the peoples of Asia and Africa and strengthening relations between the two countries.

President Ho Chi Minh asked President Prasad to convey the profound friendship of all the Vietnamese people to the Indian government and the fraternal Indian people, and to convey his personal warm wishes to Vice President Radhakrishnan and Prime Minister Nehru.

Those who went to the airport to see off the Indian President included: Prime Minister Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Deputy Prime Ministers Truong Chinh, Phan Ke Toai, and Pham Hung, Deputy Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly Ton Quang Phiet, ministers and deputy ministers of various government departments, heads of various parties and people's organizations, diplomatic envoys of various countries to Vietnam, representatives of the Vietnam International Commission, and people from all walks of life in Vietnam.

### 结束在越南民主共和国的访问  印总统离河内赴万象

1959-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在越南民主共和国的访问
    印总统离河内赴万象
    本报综合报道　据新华社河内电：印度总统普拉沙德在越南民主共和国进行了三天的友好访问以后，25日上午乘飞机离开河内，前往老挝王国首都万象。
    河内市民聚集在从主席府到机场的近十公里长的大路两旁，向普拉沙德总统欢呼送别。
    普拉沙德总统在胡志明主席的陪同下，在机场检阅了仪仗队，然后发表了告别讲话。
    他在讲话中，表示感谢胡志明主席对他的兄弟般的感情和对他以及他的随行人员的亲切接待。
    普拉沙德总统说，印度人民很重视越南民主共和国对印度共和国的兄弟友谊。他表示衷心希望两国之间自古以来的兄弟友谊在今后将变得更加亲密。
    普拉沙德总统最后高呼：“越南民主共和国万岁！越印两国之间的友谊万岁！”
    胡志明主席在致送别词时说，普拉沙德总统对越南民主共和国的访问有助于进一步巩固亚非和世界的和平。他说，他在同印度总统的会谈中，在许多问题，特别是关于巩固和发展亚非各民族之间的友谊和加强两国关系的问题上都取得了一致的看法。
    胡志明主席要求普拉沙德总统把全体越南人民的深厚友谊转达给印度政府和兄弟的印度人民，并且把他个人的亲切祝愿转达给拉达克里希南副总统和尼赫鲁总理。
    到机场欢送印度总统的有：越南民主共和国总理范文同，副总理长征、潘继遂、范雄，国会常务委员会副委员长孙光阀，政府各部正副部长，各党派和人民团体负责人，各国驻越南的外交使节，越南国际委员会的代表以及越南各界人士。
