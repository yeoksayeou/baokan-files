Title: Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Deng Xiaoping Host Banquet to Greet Indonesian Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-03-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: Comrades Liu Shaoqi and Zhou Enlai, Vice-Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee, and Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Indonesian Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade D.N. Aidit. The Chinese Communist Party representatives attending the banquet included: Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee; Lu Dingyi and Kang Sheng, alternate members of the Political Bureau; Yang Shangkun, alternate member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee; and Xiong Fu and Li Qixin, responsible personnel from central organizations. (See photo)

    Leaders of the CPC Central Committee host a banquet to welcome the Indonesian Communist Party delegation. The photo shows a group picture before the banquet. The fourth person from the left in the front row is D.N. Aidit, General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao.

### 刘少奇周恩来邓小平同志举行宴会  招待印度尼西亚共产党代表团

1959-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇周恩来邓小平同志举行宴会
    招待印度尼西亚共产党代表团
    新华社13日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇、周恩来，总书记邓小平，今晚设宴招待以艾地同志为首的印度尼西亚共产党代表团。中国共产党方面参加宴会的有：中央政治局委员彭真，候补委员陆定一、康生，中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员刘宁一和中央机关负责工作人员熊复、李启新。（附图片）
    中共中央领导人设宴招待印度尼西亚共产党代表团。图为宴会前合影。前排左起第四人是印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地。　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
