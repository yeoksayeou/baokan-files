Title: The CCP Central Committee and the State Council Issue a Call to Industrial, Transportation, Infrastructure, and Financial & Trade Workers: Immediately Launch a New and Greater Upsurge in Production; National Conference of Advanced Collectives and Advanced Producers to be Held in Beijing in the Fourth Quarter

Author:

Date: 1959-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd—Notice from the CCP Central Committee and the State Council regarding the convocation of a National Conference of Advanced Collectives and Advanced Producers in Socialist Construction in the Fields of Industry, Transportation, Basic Construction, and Finance & Trade.

    1958 was a year of overall Great Leap Forward in China’s socialist construction. Under the leadership of the Party, the people of the whole country, through the Rectification Movement, greatly enhanced their socialist consciousness, highly developed their revolutionary vigor, implemented the Party’s general line for socialist construction, and realized the people’s commune system in the countryside, which enabled our country's construction work in all aspects to win unprecedentedly great victories. In 1958, the total industrial and agricultural output value increased by about 70%, and major products such as grain, cotton, steel, coal, and machinery all doubled compared with 1957. Transportation, finance & trade, and scientific, cultural and educational undertakings have all greatly developed, creating a favorable situation for a greater leap forward this year. In the great and heroic struggle of 1958, tens of millions of workers in industry, transportation, basic construction, and finance & trade on the economic front, carried forward the communist spirit, broke down superstitions, emancipated their minds, and, in the spirit of being masters of the country, carried out selfless labor and created many amazing miracles. After the implementation of the 1959 construction plan, all employees on the industrial, transportation, basic construction, and finance & trade fronts, in order to achieve a greater, better, and more comprehensive leap forward, and to complete and overfulfill the national production and construction plans ahead of schedule, are launching a new production upsurge with higher enthusiasm and greater efforts on the basis of the achievements already made, making great efforts in the technical revolution, and constantly creating new achievements.

    In order to commend the exemplary actions of the advanced collectives and advanced producers who have emerged on the industrial, transportation, basic construction, and finance & trade fronts in the Great Leap Forward in socialist construction, to summarize and promote their advanced experiences, and to welcome the future more great tasks of socialist construction, the CCP Central Committee and the State Council decided to convene the National Conference of Advanced Collectives and Advanced Producers in Socialist Construction in the Fields of Industry, Transportation, Basic Construction, and Finance & Trade in Beijing in the fourth quarter of this year. Enterprises and units in the above-mentioned systems, all those advanced collectives and advanced producers who have made outstanding achievements in mass movements, technical revolution and technical innovation, improved labor productivity, made significant improvements in enterprise management, and contributed to the study of advanced science and technology and inventions, can send representatives to attend this conference. The distribution of representative quotas, the method of generating representatives, and the specific date of the meeting will be notified separately.

    After receiving this notice, the party and government leading organs and trade unions, communist youth leagues, women's federations, and science and technology association organizations of all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions should immediately carry out extensive and in-depth publicity work among the broad masses of workers and staff, fully mobilize the masses, promote advanced experiences, and on this basis, mobilize and organize the broad masses of workers and staff to continue to go all out, strive for the top, learn from the advanced, catch up with the advanced, and set off a new and even greater upsurge in production and construction, and strive to create a greater number of advanced collectives and advanced individuals in all aspects of the economic front, and welcome the convening of this conference with more, better, and more outstanding achievements.
                          March 21, 1959

### 中共中央和国务院向工业交通基建财贸职工发出号召  立即掀起新的更大的生产高潮  第四季度将在京召开先进集体和先进生产者代表会议

1959-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央和国务院向工业交通基建财贸职工发出号召
    立即掀起新的更大的生产高潮
    第四季度将在京召开先进集体和先进生产者代表会议
    新华社23日讯　中共中央、国务院关于召开全国工业、交通运输、基本建设、财贸方面社会主义建设先进集体和先进生产者代表会议的通知
    一九五八年是我国社会主义建设事业全面大跃进的一年。全国人民，在党的领导下，经过整风运动，大大提高了社会主义觉悟，高度发挥了革命干劲，贯彻执行了党的社会主义建设总路线，在农村中实现了人民公社化，使我国各方面建设工作赢得了空前伟大的胜利。一九五八年工农业总产值增长70％左右，粮食、棉花、钢铁、煤炭、机械等主要产品，都较一九五七年增产一倍，交通运输、财贸和科学文化教育事业都有很大的发展，为今年的更大跃进创造了有利的形势。在一九五八年伟大英勇的斗争中，在经济战线上的工业、交通运输、基本建设和财贸工作中的几千万职工，发扬了共产主义精神，破除迷信，解放思想，以国家主人翁的姿态，进行了忘我劳动，创造了许许多多惊人的奇迹。一九五九年的建设计划开始执行以后，工业、交通运输、基本建设和财贸战线上的全体职工，为了实现更大、更好、更全面的跃进，为了提前和超额完成国家生产建设计划，正在以更高的热情，更大的干劲，在业已取得的成就的基础上掀起了一个新的生产高潮，大闹技术革命，不断创造出新的成绩。
    为了表扬在大跃进中工业、交通运输、基本建设和财贸战线上涌现出的先进集体和先进生产者在社会主义建设事业中的模范行动，总结和推广他们的先进经验，迎接今后更加伟大的社会主义建设的任务，中共中央和国务院决定于今年第四季度在北京召开全国工业、交通运输、基本建设、财贸方面社会主义建设先进集体和先进生产者代表会议。在上述这些系统中的企业和单位，凡是在大搞群众运动，大闹技术革命和技术革新，提高了劳动生产率，在生产上有突出成绩，在企业管理上有重大改进，在钻研先进科学技术和创造发明方面有贡献的先进集体和先进生产者，都可以选派代表出席这次会议。各地区代表名额分配、代表产生办法以及会议的具体日期，另行通知。
    各省、市、自治区党政领导机关和工会、共青团、妇联、科学技术协会组织接到这个通知后，应当立即在广大职工群众中广泛深入地进行宣传工作，充分发动群众，推广先进经验，并在这个基础上动员和组织广大职工继续鼓足干劲，力争上游，学先进，赶先进，掀起一个更为巨大的生产和建设的新高潮，力争经济战线上的各方面工作中涌现出更大量的先进集体和先进人物，用更多、更好、更卓越的成就来迎接这次大会的召开。
                          一九五九年三月二十一日
