Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Leads CCP Delegation to Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1959-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 8th. The Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Vice Chairman Zhu De of the CCP Central Committee, arrived in Warsaw at 1:30 PM today by special plane. The delegation, invited by the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, will attend the Third National Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party, scheduled to open here on the 10th.

The Polish Ambassador to China, Kirylok, accompanied the delegation on the same flight.

Welcoming the delegation at the airport were: Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Ochab and Cyrankiewicz; Secretary of the Central Secretariat, Albrecht; and Central Committee member, Cyr, among others.

Also welcoming the delegation at the airport were Wang Bingnan, member of the CCP delegation and our country's ambassador to Poland, and Katona, the Hungarian ambassador to Poland.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 8th. The Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Vice Chairman Zhu De of the CCP Central Committee, which will attend the Third National Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party, left here by plane this morning for Warsaw.

Seeing the delegation off at the airport were: Members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Kozlov and Mikoyan; Alternate Member Pospelov; Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Tarasov; and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Kuz’metsov.

Also present to see them off were: the Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim in the Soviet Union, Korolczyk; the Hungarian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Boldoczki; and the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao.

### 朱副主席率中共代表团抵华沙

1959-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席率中共代表团抵华沙
    新华社华沙8日电　中共中央副主席朱德率领的中国共产党代表团，在今天下午一时半乘专机抵达华沙。代表团应波兰统一工人党中央委员会的邀请，将参加订于10日在这里开幕的波兰统一工人党第三次全国代表大会。
    波兰驻华大使基里洛克陪同代表团同机到达。
    到机场欢迎的有：波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员奥哈布、西伦凯维兹，中央书记处书记阿尔布列赫特，中央委员西尔等。
    到机场欢迎的还有中共代表团团员、我国驻波兰大使王炳南，匈牙利驻波兰大使卡托纳。
    新华社莫斯科8日电　中共中央副主席朱德率领的参加波兰统一工人党第三次全国代表大会的中国共产党代表团，今天上午离开这里乘飞机前往华沙。
    前往机场送行的有：苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员科兹洛夫、米高扬，候补委员波斯别洛夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席塔拉索夫，外交部副部长库兹?佐夫。
    前往欢送的还有：波兰驻苏联临时代办科罗耳契克、匈牙利驻苏联大使波尔多茨基、中国驻苏联大使刘晓。
