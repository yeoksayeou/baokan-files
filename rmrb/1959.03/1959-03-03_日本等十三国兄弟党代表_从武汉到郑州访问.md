Title: Representatives of fraternal parties from thirteen countries, including Japan, visit Zhengzhou from Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1959-03-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 2nd - Miyamoto Kenji, General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party; Kasuga Shoichi, member of the JCP Central Committee Presidium; Nishizawa Tomio, Deputy Director of the JCP Central Committee; Luis Corvalán, General Secretary of the Chilean Communist Party; Pompeyo Márquez, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Venezuela; Alonso Ojeda Olaechea, member of the Political Bureau; Pedro Ortega Díaz, member of the Political Bureau; Guillermo García Ponce, member of the Central Committee; Gilberto Vieira, General Secretary of the Colombian Communist Party; Joaquín Moreno Guayas, member of the Political Bureau; Raúl Acosta, General Secretary of the Peruvian Communist Party; Jorge del Prado, member of the Central Committee; Humberto Ramírez, member of the Central Political Committee of the Bolivian Communist Party; Elías Muñoz, member of the Central Executive Committee of the Ecuadorian Communist Party; Felybe Pérez Leutene, Secretary of the Central Financial Committee of the Argentine Communist Party; Wilfredo Velázquez, Organization Secretary of the Las Villas Provincial Committee of the Popular Socialist Party of Cuba, and representatives of the People's Vanguard Party of Costa Rica, the People's Party of Panama, the Communist Party of Brazil, and the Communist Party of Paraguay, accompanied by Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, arrived in Zhengzhou from Wuhan this morning.

Those who went to welcome them included: Wu Zhipu, First Secretary of the Henan Provincial Committee of the CPC; Zhao Wenfu and Li Li, Secretaries of the Provincial Committee Secretariat; Song Yuxi, member of the Standing Committee of the Provincial Committee; Wang Lizhi, First Secretary of the Zhengzhou Municipal Committee of the CPC; Liu Bi, Secretary of the Municipal Committee Secretariat, and other responsible cadres.

In the afternoon of the same day, representatives of the fraternal parties, accompanied by Zhao Wenfu and Li Li, Secretaries of the Henan Provincial Committee Secretariat of the CPC, Wang Lizhi, First Secretary of the Zhengzhou Municipal Committee of the CPC, and Liu Bi, Secretary of the Municipal Committee Secretariat, visited the Xijiao People's Commune in Zhengzhou. The representatives visited a sugar factory, a flour mill, a sewing factory, a vegetable experimental farm, as well as public welfare facilities such as homes for the elderly, nurseries, maternity hospitals, and bathhouses in a production brigade. The representatives were warmly welcomed by the commune members during their visit.

In the evening, the Henan Provincial Committee of the CPC hosted a banquet and held an evening party to entertain the representatives of the fraternal parties.

When the representatives of the communist parties of various countries left Wuhan this morning, Zhang Pinghua, Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee Secretariat of the CPC, Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the CPC, and Song Yiping and Liu Huinong, Secretaries of the Secretariat, went to see them off.

### 日本等十三国兄弟党代表  从武汉到郑州访问

1959-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    日本等十三国兄弟党代表
    从武汉到郑州访问
    新华社郑州2日电　日本共产党总书记宫本显治、日共中央干部会委员春日正一、日共中央副部长西泽富夫，和智利共产党总书记路易斯·科尔巴兰，委内瑞拉共产党中央委员会书记处书记庞佩约·马凯斯、政治局委员阿伦索·奥里达·奥拉耶切阿、政治局委员皮得罗·欧尔特卡·吉阿斯、中央委员吉耶尔莫·古阿尔丁，哥伦比亚共产党总书记希尔伯托·维埃拉、政治局委员候阿金·毛列诺·古阿斯，秘鲁共产党总书记腊乌尔·阿哥斯塔、中央委员霍尔海·德尔·布腊多，玻利维亚共产党中央政治委员会委员翁伯托·拉米雷斯，厄瓜多尔共产党中央执行委员会委员厄利阿斯·穆纽斯，阿根廷共产党中央财务委员会书记弗利贝·培斯罗特尼克，古巴人民社会党拉斯维利亚斯省委组织书记威尔夫利多·维拉斯克斯和哥斯达黎加人民先锋党、巴拿马人民党、巴西共产党以及巴拉圭共产党的代表，由中共中央书记处书记王稼祥陪同，今天上午由武汉到达郑州。
    前往欢迎的有：中共河南省委第一书记吴芝圃，省委书记处书记赵文甫、李立，省委常务委员宋玉玺，中共郑州市委第一书记王黎之，市委书记处书记刘必以及其他负责干部。
    当天下午，各兄弟党的代表在中共河南省委书记处书记赵文甫、李立，中共郑州市委第一书记王黎之，市委书记处书记刘必等陪同下，访问了郑州市西郊人民公社。代表们参观了一个生产大队的制糖厂、面粉厂、缝纫厂、蔬菜试验场以及敬老院、托儿所、妇产院、洗澡塘等公共福利事业。代表们在参观的时候受到了社员群众的热烈欢迎。
    晚上，中共河南省委设宴并举行晚会招待各兄弟党的代表。
    各国共产党代表今天上午在离开武汉的时候，中共湖北省委书记处书记张平化，中共武汉市委第一书记宋侃夫，书记处书记宋一平、刘惠农等曾前往送行。
