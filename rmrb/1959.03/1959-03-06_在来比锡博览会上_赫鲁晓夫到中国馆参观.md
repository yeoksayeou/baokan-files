Title: At the Leipzig Fair Khrushchev Visits the Chinese Pavilion
Author:
Date: 1959-03-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Leipzig, 5th—Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, visited the Leipzig Fair this morning and visited the Chinese Pavilion.

Khrushchev, accompanied by Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party, and Deputy Premier Rau, arrived at the Chinese Pavilion. Xiao Fangzhou, head of the Chinese Exhibition Group participating in the Leipzig Fair, introduced the various exhibits of the Chinese Pavilion this year to the distinguished guests.

### 在来比锡博览会上  赫鲁晓夫到中国馆参观

1959-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    在来比锡博览会上
    赫鲁晓夫到中国馆参观
    新华社来比锡5日　电苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫今天上午参观了来比锡博览会，并且到了中国馆参观。
    赫鲁晓夫由德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥、德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希和副总理劳陪同，来到了中国馆，参加来比锡博览会的中国展览团团长萧方洲向贵宾们介绍了今年中国馆展出的各种展品。
