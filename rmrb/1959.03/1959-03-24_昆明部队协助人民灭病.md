Title: Kunming Troops Assist People in Eliminating Disease
Author:
Date: 1959-03-24
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 23rd: Medical workers from the People's Liberation Army Kunming area troops have assisted people of minority nationalities in border regions to eliminate diseases and pests. In over four months, they have basically eliminated schistosomiasis in eleven townships, receiving praise from the local people of all ethnic groups.

### 昆明部队协助人民灭病

1959-03-24
第6版()
专栏：

    昆明部队协助人民灭病
    据新华社昆明23日电　解放军昆明地区部队医务工作者，协助边疆少数民族人民灭病除害，四个多月内在十一个乡镇中基本上消灭了血吸虫病，受到当地各族人民赞扬。
