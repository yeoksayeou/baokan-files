Title: Ceylon Ambassador to China Holds Reception for Visit of Two Ceylonese Delegations to China
Author:
Date: 1959-03-25
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, March 24th. The Ceylonese Ambassador to China, Wilmot A. Perera, held a reception tonight for the Ceylonese Civil Aviation Delegation, headed by the Minister of Transport and Engineering, Maithripala Senanayake, and the Ceylonese Industry and Fisheries Delegation, headed by the Minister of Industry and Fisheries, Mr. de Zoysa, who are visiting China. Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Minister of Communications Wang Shoudao, Acting Minister of Light Industry Deng Jie, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and others, were invited to attend the reception.

### 为锡兰两个代表团访问我国  锡兰驻华大使举行招待会

1959-03-25
第5版()
专栏：

    为锡兰两个代表团访问我国
    锡兰驻华大使举行招待会
    新华社24日讯　锡兰驻华大使高伯拉瓦为以运输工程部长迈特里帕拉·森纳那亚克为首的锡兰民用航空代表团和以工业和渔业部长德席尔瓦为首的锡兰工业和渔业代表团访问中国，在今晚举行了招待会。国务院副总理陈毅，交通部长王首道，轻工业部代理部长邓洁，外交部副部长姬鹏飞等，应邀出席了招待会。
