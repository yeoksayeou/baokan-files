Title: Mao Zedong Sends a Congratulatory Message on the Danish King's Birthday and Danish National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram congratulating King Frederik IX of Denmark on his birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Denmark. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Majesty Frederik IX, King of the Kingdom of Denmark:

On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Denmark, I, on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, extend my warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of your country. I wish the friendly relations between China and Denmark further development. I wish the Kingdom of Denmark prosperity, the Danish people happiness, and Your Majesty good health.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong, March 10, 1959

### 毛主席电贺丹麦国王寿辰和丹麦国庆

1959-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺丹麦国王寿辰和丹麦国庆
    新华社10日讯　毛泽东主席致电祝贺丹麦国王斐德烈九世寿辰和丹麦王国国庆，电文如下：丹麦王国国王斐德烈九世陛下：
    欣逢陛下寿辰和丹麦王国国庆，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人名义，向陛下和贵国人民致以热诚的祝贺。祝中、丹两国间的友好关系进一步的发展。祝丹麦王国繁荣，丹麦人民幸福和陛下身体健康。
                中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1959年3月10日
