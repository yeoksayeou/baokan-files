Title: Ministry of National Defense Holds Song and Dance Soirée to Welcome Algerian Military Delegation; Distinguished Guests Visited the Great Wall and the Ming Tombs Reservoir Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1959-03-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 30th. The Ministry of National Defense held a song and dance soirée this evening to warmly welcome all the distinguished guests of the Algerian Military Delegation headed by Omar Oussedik, Secretary of State for the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria.

When the distinguished guests, accompanied by Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, and others, entered the venue, more than 800 officers of the People's Liberation Army's Army, Navy, and Air Force warmly applauded to welcome them. During the evening, the Cultural Troupe of the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army performed wonderful song and dance programs.

At the end of the performance, Delegation Leader Omar Oussedik and others, accompanied by Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, Major General Luo Yijing, Major General Wang Shaonan, and Naval Major General Zhang Xuesi, ascended the stage to present flower baskets to the performers and shook hands with them cordially.

Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, Major General Luo Yijing, Major General Wang Shaonan, Naval Major General Zhang Xuesi, and other generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army accompanied the distinguished guests to watch tonight's performance.

During the day today, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, and Colonel Zhang Bingyu, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau, and others, visited the ancient Great Wall and the famous Ming Tombs Reservoir.

List of Distinguished Guests of the Algerian Military Delegation:

The head of the Algerian Military Delegation is Omar Oussedik, Secretary of State for the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria. The members of the delegation are: Colonel Slimane Zirès, Major Slimane, Major Kaci, Major Azzedine, Lieutenant Sadok Gherrar, Lieutenant Alahoum; Secretaries Taha Debaga and Brahim Ghafa.

### 国防部举行歌舞晚会  欢迎阿尔及利亚军事代表团  贵宾们昨天参观了长城和十三陵水库

1959-03-31
第5版()
专栏：

    国防部举行歌舞晚会
    欢迎阿尔及利亚军事代表团
    贵宾们昨天参观了长城和十三陵水库
    新华社30日讯　国防部在今天晚上举行歌舞晚会，热烈欢迎以阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府国务秘书奥马尔·乌西迪克为首的阿尔及利亚军事代表团全体贵宾。
    当贵宾们由国防部代表杨秀山中将等陪同进入会场时，人民解放军陆海空军军官八百多人热烈鼓掌欢迎。晚会上，由解放军总政治部文艺工作团演出了精彩的歌舞节目。
    演出结束时，代表团团长奥马尔·乌西迪克等由刘志坚中将、萧向荣中将、杨秀山中将等陪同，登上舞台，向演员们赠送花篮，并同演员们亲切握手。
    刘志坚中将、萧向荣中将、杨秀山中将、罗亦经少将、王绍南少将、张学思海军少将等中国人民解放军将领，陪同贵宾们观看了今晚的演出。
    今天白天，贵宾们由国防部代表杨秀山中将和外事处副处长张秉玉上校等陪同，参观了我国古老的长城和著名的十三陵水库。
    阿尔及利亚军事代表团贵宾名单
    阿尔及利亚军事代表团团长是阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府国务秘书奥马尔·乌西迪克。代表团的团员有：斯利曼·齐勒斯上校、斯利曼少校、卡西少校、阿泽丁少校、萨迪克·格拉勒中尉、阿拉胡姆中尉；秘书塔哈·德巴加和秘书卜拉欣·加法。
