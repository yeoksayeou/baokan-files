Title: Iraqi Color Documentary "Dawn of Iraq" Premieres in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-03-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd: The color documentary "Dawn of Iraq" begins showing in Beijing today.

The film records Iraq's beautiful scenery, fertile land and mineral resources, hardworking people, long history and culture, and the enormous changes before and after the establishment of the Republic of Iraq. Many scenes show the Iraqi people building their new lives after shaking off the shackles of imperialism and the feudal dynasty, and preparing to repel provocations from imperialists and reactionaries at any time. Peace-loving countries and people around the world extend friendly hands to this newly born republic in the Middle East. The profound friendship between the Chinese and Iraqi people is also recorded in the film.

The color documentary "Dawn of Iraq" was produced by the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio of China, with the enthusiastic assistance of the government and people of the Republic of Iraq during the production process.

### 介绍伊拉克的彩色纪录影片  “伊拉克的曙光”在北京上演

1959-03-23
第6版()
专栏：

    介绍伊拉克的彩色纪录影片
    “伊拉克的曙光”在北京上演
    新华社22日讯　彩色纪录片“伊拉克的曙光”，今天开始在北京上映。
    影片纪录了伊拉克美丽的风光，富饶的土地和矿藏，勤劳的人民，悠久的历史文化和伊拉克共和国成立前后的巨大变化。许多镜头表现出伊拉克人民在挣脱帝国主义和封建王朝的锁链以后，正在建设自己的新生活和准备随时回击帝国主义和反动分子的挑衅。世界爱好和平的国家和人民都向这个中东新生的共和国伸出友谊之手。中伊两国人民的深厚友谊也纪录在影片中。
    彩色纪录片“伊拉克的曙光”是我国中央新闻纪录电影制片厂摄制的，摄制中曾得到伊拉克共和国政府和人民的热情协助。
