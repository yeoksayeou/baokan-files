Title: Punish the Criminals Who Participated in the Shawwaf Rebellion; Iraqi Court Sentences Four Traitors to Death; All Attendees Cheer and Applaud in Support of the Just Verdict
Author:
Date: 1959-03-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, March 28th. The High Military Court tonight sentenced four air force pilots who participated in the Shawwaf rebellion to death. They are: Colonel Abdul Naji, Captain Kassem Azawi, Lieutenant Ahmed Ashur, and Fadhel Nasser.

The court reconvened at 8:00 PM tonight. After a judge read out the details of the defendants' collaboration with the traitor Shawwaf, Court President Mahdawi announced the court's verdict. He said, "According to Articles 9 and 12 of the Baghdad Penal Code, Article 11 of the Constitution, and Article 46 of the Military Management Law, these defendants should be sentenced to death." At that moment, everyone in the audience stood up to show their respect for the court's verdict.

General Prosecutor Amin gave a brief speech, saying, "In the name of the heroic city of Mosul, in the name of its loyal and innocent martyrs, in the name of the liberated Arab nationalism, in the name of the heroic martyrs Abdul Shawi and Kamil Kazanchi, in the name of the Iraqi and Kurdish people who heroically defended the city of Mosul, in the name of the Turkmen, Assyrians, and Armenians, in the name of all these people, I extend the highest respect and gratitude to the president and judges for the just verdict they have made against these traitors.”

All the attendees cheered and applauded, and shouted: "Long live our heroic republic!"

### 制裁参加沙瓦夫叛乱的罪犯  伊法庭判处四名卖国贼死刑  所有旁听者欢呼鼓掌拥护公正判决

1959-03-30
第5版()
专栏：

    制裁参加沙瓦夫叛乱的罪犯
    伊法庭判处四名卖国贼死刑
    所有旁听者欢呼鼓掌拥护公正判决
    新华社巴格达28日电　高级军事法庭今晚对四个参加沙瓦夫叛乱飞机驾驶员判处死刑。他们是：阿卜杜勒·纳吉上校、卡塞姆·阿扎维上尉、艾哈迈德·阿舒尔中尉和法德赫勒·纳赛尔。
    法庭在今天晚上八时重行开庭，在一位审判官宣读了关于被告们同卖国贼沙瓦夫合作的情况后，法庭庭长马赫达维宣布了法庭的判决。他说，“根据巴格达刑法第九条和第十二条、宪法第十一条和军事管理法第四十六条，应对这些被告判处死刑。”当时所有旁听的人都站起来以表示他们尊重法庭的判决。
    总检察官阿明发表简短讲话，他说，“我以英勇城市摩苏尔的名义，以它的忠诚的、无辜的烈士们的名义，以解放了的阿拉伯民族主义的名义，以英勇的烈士阿卜杜勒·沙维和卡米勒·卡赞希的名义，以英勇保卫摩苏尔城的伊拉克和库尔德人民的名义，以土库曼人、亚述人和亚美尼亚人的名义，以所有这些人的名义，为了庭长和审判官对这些卖国贼所作出的公正判决，向他们致最高的敬意和谢意。”
    所有的旁听者都欢呼鼓掌，并高呼：“我们英勇的共和国万岁！”
