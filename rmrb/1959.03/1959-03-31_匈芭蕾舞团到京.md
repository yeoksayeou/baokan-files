Title: Hungarian Ballet Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-03-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 30th: The nineteen-member Hungarian State Opera House Small Ballet Troupe, led by troupe leader Beringer Éva, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

This ballet troupe is visiting China for two weeks to perform in accordance with the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Starting April 3rd, they will perform excerpts from classical and modern ballets for the audience in the Capital Theater in Beijing.

### 匈芭蕾舞团到京

1959-03-31
第5版()
专栏：

    匈芭蕾舞团到京
    新华社30日讯　匈牙利国家歌剧院小型芭蕾舞团一行十九人，在团长白林盖·爱娃率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    这个芭蕾舞团是根据中匈文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国作两星期访问演出的，他们将从4月3日起在首都剧场为北京市观众演出古典及现代芭蕾舞片断。
