Title: Women's Federation and Science and Technology Association Mobilize Female Workers and Scientific and Technological Workers to Welcome the Heroes' Conference

Author: 
Date: 1959-03-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: The All-China Women's Federation today called on female workers across the country to join with male workers in achieving greater, better, and more outstanding accomplishments to welcome the convening of the National Conference of Advanced Collectives and Advanced Producers in Socialist Construction in Industry, Transportation, Capital Construction, and Finance and Trade. In its appeal, the All-China Women's Federation requested that women's federations at all levels, under the unified leadership and overall arrangements of the Party, cooperate with trade union organizations and relevant parties to conduct extensive and in-depth publicity and mobilization work among all female workers; continuously discover, cultivate, and support advanced collectives and advanced individuals emerging in various production activities. It also called for real attention to the special difficulties in the lives of female workers and to properly manage various collective living welfare programs, creating better conditions to support female workers in making outstanding achievements in production.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: The National Committee of the Science and Technology Association today issued a notice to science and technology associations at all levels across the country, calling on scientific and technological workers across the country to exert greater effort and make more and better new achievements to welcome the National Conference of Advanced Collectives and Advanced Producers in Socialist Construction in Industry, Transportation, Capital Construction, and Finance and Trade. The notice requires science and technology associations at all levels and their members, under the unified leadership of Party committees at all levels, to cooperate with trade unions, the Communist Youth League, and women's federations to vigorously popularize scientific and technological knowledge among the broad masses of workers and peasants and improve their scientific and technological level. It seeks to promote a broader and more in-depth mass movement for technological revolution throughout the country. It also calls for a continuous improvement in labor productivity in industry and agriculture and the use of more and better new achievements to welcome the convening of the conference.

### 妇联和科协动员女职工和科学技术工作者迎接群英大会

1959-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    妇联和科协动员女职工和科学技术工作者迎接群英大会
    据新华社25日讯　全国妇女联合会今天号召全国女职工，要和男职工一起，以更大、更好、更卓越的成就，来迎接全国工业、交通运输、基本建设、财贸方面社会主义建设先进集体和先进生产者代表会议的召开。全国妇联在号召书中要求各级妇联组织在党的统一领导和全面安排下，与工会组织及各有关方面互相协作，向全体女职工进行广泛深入的宣传动员工作；不断地发现、培养和支持各项生产中涌现出来的先进集体、先进人物，还要切实注意女职工生活上的特殊困难，办好各项集体生活福利事业，为支持女职工在生产上做出突出的成就创造更好的条件。
    据新华社25日讯　科学技术协会全国委员会今天向全国各级科学技术协会发出通知，号召全国科学技术工作者鼓足更大的干劲，以更多更好的新成就来迎接全国工业、交通运输、基本建设、财贸方面社会主义建设先进集体和先进生产者代表会议。通知要求各级科协和广大会员，在各级党委的统一领导下，配合工会、共青团和妇联，在广大工农群众中，大力普及科学技术知识，提高工农群众的科学技术水平。在全国范围内促进一个更加广阔、更加深入的技术革命群众运动。不断提高工农业劳动生产率，用更多更好的新成就迎接大会的召开。
