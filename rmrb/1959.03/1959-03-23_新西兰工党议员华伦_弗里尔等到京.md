Title: New Zealand Labour Party MP Warren Freer Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-03-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd. Warren Freer, a New Zealand Labour Party MP and Chairman of the Parliamentary Committee on Industry and Commerce, his wife, and two leading figures from local New Zealand Labour Party organizations, F. Newton and his wife, and J. R. Beefeater, a group of five in total, arrived in Beijing this morning for a friendly visit to our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 新西兰工党议员华伦·弗里尔等到京

1959-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    新西兰工党议员华伦·弗里尔等到京
    新华社22日讯　新西兰工党议员、议会工商委员会主席华伦·弗里尔和他的夫人，以及新西兰工党地方组织的两位负责人：弗·牛顿和他的夫人、杰·尔·贝费特一行共五人，应中国人民外交学会邀请，今天上午到达北京来我国进行友好访问。
