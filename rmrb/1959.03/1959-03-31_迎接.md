Title: Welcoming
Author: He Jeye Fu
Date: 1959-03-31
Page: 8

    Welcoming
    Uyghur族 He Jeye Fu
My right hand is placed on my fiery chest, extending to you the greetings of our ethnic group.
Welcoming you to Xinjiang, may we become eternal companions.
From the Pamir Plateau to the banks of the Yangtze River, a carpet woven of flowers is spread;
Bringing to our beloved ones the wishes of the people of Xinjiang.
The silk threads of Suzhou, a thousand strands of endless affection,
Have been tightly bound in our hearts for thousands of years.
The land of the Tarim is vast and boundless,
Waiting for you to build a beautiful garden.
Come on, brothers, come with us,
Hand in hand to the frontier of the motherland.
There, let us work together, live together,
Let us forever, forever, joyfully gather together!

### 迎接
赫捷耶夫
1959-03-31
第8版()
专栏：

    迎接
    维吾尔族  赫捷耶夫我的右手放在火热的胸口，向你们致以民族的敬礼。迎接你们到新疆，愿我们成为永恒的伴侣。从帕米尔高原到长江两岸，铺起了鲜花织成的地毯；给我们亲爱的人，带来了新疆人民的心愿。苏州的丝线，千缕绵绵，千百年就紧结在我们的心间。塔里木的土地广阔无边，等待你们去建设美丽的花园。走吧兄弟们，和我们走吧，手携着手儿到祖国的边疆。在那里共同劳动，共同生活，让我们永远、永远地欢聚一堂！
