Title: Ode to Anshan
Author: Wei Chuantong
Date: 1959-03-01
Page: 8

    Ode to Anshan
    Wei Chuantong
    Three Poems in Sixteen-Character Meter

    Mountain!
    East and West Mountains each fitted with a saddle.
    Flame trees,
    Always pleasing to watch at night!

    Mountain!
    On Gathering Immortal Terrace, no immortals are seen.
    People are valiant and spirited,
    One step can ascend to heaven!

    Mountain!
    Steel City's fine name is known far and wide.
    Leap Forward flowers,
    Bloom luxuriantly therein!

### 咏鞍山
魏传统
1959-03-01
第8版()
专栏：

    咏鞍山
    魏传统
    十六字令三首
    山！
    东西两山各配鞍。
    火焰树，
    夜夜总堪看！
    山！
    聚仙台上不见仙。
    人英武，
    一步可登天！
    山！
    钢都美名远近传。
    跃进花，
    盛开在其间！
