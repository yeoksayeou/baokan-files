Title: I Donate 10,000 Tons of Wheat to Relieve Famine Victims in Yemen
Author:
Date: 1959-03-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 20th - Crown Prince Badr of Yemen met with Chen Jiakang, the Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic and concurrently Envoy to Yemen, here today. Zhang Yue, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Yemen, was also present.

Ambassador Chen Jiakang, on behalf of the Chinese government, donated 10,000 tons of wheat to the Yemeni government to relieve famine victims in Yemen.

Ambassador Chen Jiakang expressed support for the struggle of the people of southern Yemen against colonialism.

### 我赠送一万吨小麦  救济也门饥荒灾民

1959-03-22
第3版()
专栏：

    我赠送一万吨小麦
    救济也门饥荒灾民
    新华社开罗20日电　也门王太子巴德尔今天在这里接见了中国驻阿联大使兼驻也门公使陈家康。在座的还有中国驻也门公使馆参赞张越。
    陈家康大使代表中国政府赠送一万吨小麦给也门政府，以救济也门的饥荒灾民。
    陈家康大使表示支持也门南部人民反对殖民主义的斗争。
