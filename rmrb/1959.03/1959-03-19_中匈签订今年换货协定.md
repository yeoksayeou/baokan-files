Title: China and Hungary Sign This Year's Goods Exchange Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 17th: A goods exchange and payment agreement for 1959 between China and Hungary was signed in Budapest on March 17th.

According to the agreement, the volume of goods exchanged between the two sides in 1959 will significantly increase compared to previous years. China will supply Hungary with metal ores, oilseeds, chemical and textile raw materials, machinery and equipment, and consumer goods. Hungary will supply China with complete sets of equipment, thermal power station machinery, electrical equipment, instruments, steel, and pharmaceuticals.

Signing the agreement on behalf of Hungary was Foreign Trade Minister Ince Jeno; signing on behalf of China was Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang.

### 中匈签订今年换货协定

1959-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    中匈签订今年换货协定
    新华社布达佩斯17日电　中国和匈牙利两国1959年换货和支付协定17日在布达佩斯签订。
    根据协定，双方1959年的换货额将比前几年有显著的增加。中国将供给匈牙利金属矿、油籽、化工和纺织原料、机器设备和消费品等。匈牙利将供给中国成套设备、火电站机器、电气设备、仪器、钢材和药品等。
    在协定上签字的，匈牙利方面是对外贸易部长英切·耶诺；中国方面是对外贸易部副部长李强。
