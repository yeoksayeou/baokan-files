Title: Standing Committee of the CPPCC Approves List of Third CPPCC National Committee Members
Author:
Date: 1959-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held its 52nd meeting (expanded) today. The meeting deliberated on the list of members of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC. Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, explained the preliminary list proposed by various parties and groups after full consultation. After discussion, the meeting approved the list of members of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC.
The meeting also discussed the date for convening the first session of the Third CPPCC National Committee and organizing CPPCC members to conduct inspection tours.

### 政协常委会通过第三届政协委员名单

1959-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    政协常委会通过第三届政协委员名单
    新华社10日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会今天举行第五十二次会议（扩大）。会议就政协第三届全国委员会委员名单进行了协商，政协全国委员会主席周恩来对由各党派各团体经过充分协商以后提出的初步名单作了说明。会议经过讨论，通过第三届政协全国委员会委员的名单。
    会议还协商了召开第三届政协第一次会议的日期和组织政协委员视察工作等问题。
