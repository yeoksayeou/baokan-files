Title: Czechoslovak Puppet Film Art Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-03-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 12th: The Czechoslovak Puppet Film Art Exhibition opened today in Beijing's Zhongshan Park. This exhibition, presented in our country according to the 1959 implementation plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement, is jointly sponsored by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of the People's Republic of China, the and the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association.

### 捷木偶电影艺术展览会在京开幕

1959-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    捷木偶电影艺术展览会在京开幕
    据新华社12日讯　捷克斯洛伐克木偶电影艺术展览会今天在北京中山公园开幕。这个展览会是根据中捷文化合作协定1959年执行计划在我国展出的，由中华人民共和国对外文化联络委员会、中国捷克斯洛伐克友好协会联合举办。
