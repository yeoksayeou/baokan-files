Title: End of Visit to North Korea: Japanese Communist Party Delegation Leaves Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1959-03-01
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 28th -- The Japanese Communist Party delegation, headed by Miyamoto Kenji, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, left Pyongyang by plane this morning after concluding its visit to North Korea.
　　Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul, and Kim Chang-man, Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Kim Il, Member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and First Vice-Premier of the Cabinet; Nam Il, Member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and Vice-Premier of the Cabinet; and responsible cadres of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party.

### 结束在朝鲜的访问  日共代表团离开平壤

1959-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在朝鲜的访问
    日共代表团离开平壤
    新华社平壤28日电　以日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治为首的日本共产党代表团在结束了对朝鲜的访问以后，今天上午乘飞机离开平壤。
    到机场送行的有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴金哲、金昌满，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会常务委员会委员、内阁第一副首相金一，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会常务委员会委员、内阁副首相南日和劳动党中央负责干部。
