Title: U.S. Restricts Venezuelan Oil Imports; Venezuelan Public Condemns U.S. for Selfishness

Author: 
Date: 1959-03-28
Page: 5

Caracas dispatch: The U.S. government's quota restrictions on Venezuelan oil imports have provoked strong reactions from Venezuelan political, economic, and parliamentary circles.

The report said that these people unanimously believe that the primary step to be taken is to make Venezuela no longer dependent on the U.S. monopoly on the country's oil. Newspapers emphasized that the unanimous opinion is expressed, as directly proposed by the Federation of Associations of Industry and Commerce, to abolish the reciprocal treaty with the United States and establish commercial relations with all socialist countries willing to purchase Venezuelan oil.

### 美国限制委内瑞拉石油进口  委公众斥美国损人利己

1959-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    美国限制委内瑞拉石油进口
    委公众斥美国损人利己
    加拉加斯消息：美国政府对委内瑞拉石油进口定额的限制引起委内瑞拉政界、经济界和议会人士的强烈反应。
    消息说，这些人士一致认为，必须采取的首要步骤是使委内瑞拉不再依赖美国对这个国家石油的垄断。报纸强调指出，一致的意见表现在像工商业协会联合会直接提出的那样，要求废除同美国的互惠条约并且同一切有意购买委内瑞拉石油的社会主义国家建立商业关系。
