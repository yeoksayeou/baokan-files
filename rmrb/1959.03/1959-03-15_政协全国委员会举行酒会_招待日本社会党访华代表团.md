Title: CPPCC National Committee Holds Reception to Host Japanese Socialist Party Delegation

Author:
Date: 1959-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 14th: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a reception this afternoon to host the Japanese Socialist Party Delegation led by Inejiro Asanuma.

At the reception, which was filled with a friendly atmosphere, Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, had warm conversations with Inejiro Asanuma and other Japanese friends. In his two toasts, Guo Moruo suggested a toast to the health of the friends of the Japanese Socialist Party Delegation, and to the happiness and prosperity of the Japanese people.

In his toast, Inejiro Asanuma proposed a toast to China's victory in the struggle against nature. When he bid farewell, he said, "I eagerly hope that Japan and China will restore diplomatic relations soon."

Those present at the reception included Zhang Xiruo, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Changsheng, Li Dequan, Chu Tunan, Lei Renmin, Shao Lizi, Ma Yinchu, Xu Guangping, Xu Deheng, Cai Tingkai, Lu Han, Da Puhan, Xirao Jiacuo, Ji Chaoding, Xie Nanguang, Zhao Puchu, and others.

Also present at the reception were some members of the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee, heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and well-known figures from literary, scientific, educational, journalistic, and religious circles in the capital.

Japanese peace advocate Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also invited to attend the reception.

The Japanese Socialist Party Delegation visited Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, this afternoon. In recent days, the delegation has visited the National Agricultural Exhibition, people's communes, factories, and schools in Beijing. (Attached picture)

### 政协全国委员会举行酒会  招待日本社会党访华代表团

1959-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会举行酒会
    招待日本社会党访华代表团
    新华社14日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会今天下午举行酒会，招待由浅沼稻次郎率领的日本社会党访华代表团。
    在充满友好气氛的酒会上，政协全国委员会副主席郭沫若同浅沼稻次郎等日本朋友们热烈交谈，郭沫若在他两次的祝酒中，建议为日本社会党访华代表团朋友们的健康，为日本人民的幸福和繁荣干杯。
    浅沼稻次郎在祝酒中提议为向自然斗争取得胜利的中国干杯。他在告辞的时候说：“热望日本和中国早日恢复邦交”。
    出席酒会的有张奚若、廖承志、刘长胜、李德全、楚图南、雷任民、邵力子、马寅初、许广平、许德珩、蔡廷锴、卢汉、达浦生、喜饶嘉错、冀朝鼎、谢南光、赵朴初等。
    出席酒会的还有政协全国委员会部分常务委员，各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，首都文艺、科学、教育、新闻、宗教界等各方面的著名人士。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一等应邀出席了酒会。
    日本社会党访华代表团在今天下午访问了中国红十字会总会会长李德全。代表团最近几天在北京曾参观了全国农业展览会、人民公社、工厂和学校等。（附图片）
