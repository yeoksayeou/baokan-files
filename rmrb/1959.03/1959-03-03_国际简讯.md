　　International Briefs

　　Japanese Coal Miners Launch Strike

　　Tokyo Dispatch: Approximately 41,000 coal miners from the Mitsui Mining Company in Japan began a 24-hour strike on March 2nd, demanding that the company rescind its so-called "enterprise reorganization" plan. The workers believe that this plan indicates the company's intention to use the name of "enterprise reorganization" to arbitrarily dismiss workers.

　　Indian Finance Minister Presents This Year's Budget

　　New Delhi Dispatch: Indian Finance Minister Desai presented the government's budget for the period from April 1959 to March 1960 to the Parliament on February 28th.

　　The budget estimates revenue at 7.5751 billion rupees and expenditures at 8.3918 billion rupees, resulting in a deficit of 816.7 million rupees. In the budget, civilian expenditure is 5.965 billion rupees, and defense expenditure is 2.4268 billion rupees.

### 国际简讯

1959-03-03
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    日本煤矿工人展开罢工斗争
    东京讯　日本三井矿山公司约四万一千名煤矿工人从2日开始举行二十四小时罢工，要求资方取消所谓“企业整顿”方案。工人们认为，这个方案表明资方企图利用“企业整顿”的名义，任意解雇工人。
    印财长提出今年度预算
    新德里讯　印度财政部长德赛2月28日在议会提出政府从1959年4月到1960年3月为止的预算。
    预算估计收入是七十五亿七千五百一十万卢比，支出是八十三亿九千一百八十万卢比，赤字是八亿一千六百七十万卢比。在预算中，民用开支是五十九亿六千五百万卢比，国防开支是二十四亿二千六百八十万卢比。
