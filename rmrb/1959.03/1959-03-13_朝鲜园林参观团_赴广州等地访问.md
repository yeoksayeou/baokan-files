Title: Korean Garden Visiting Group Visits Guangzhou and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1959-03-13
Page: 4

    The Korean garden work visiting group of ten people, headed by Kim Jeong-hee, Deputy Chairman of the Korean State Construction Commission, left Beijing on the 12th to visit Guangzhou and other places.
    This work visiting group came to China for a visit according to the 1959 Sino-Korean cultural cooperation plan and arrived in Beijing on March 8.

### 朝鲜园林参观团  赴广州等地访问

1959-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜园林参观团
    赴广州等地访问
    本报讯　以朝鲜国家建设委员会副委员长金正熙为首的朝鲜园林工作参观团一行十人于12日离京赴广州等地访问。
    这个工作参观团是根据中朝1959年文化合作计划来我国进行访问、于3月8日到达北京的。
