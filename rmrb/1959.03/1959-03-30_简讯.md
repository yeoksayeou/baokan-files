    Brief News
    Indian President Prasad Leaves Vientiane to Return Home
    Vientiane dispatch: Indian President Prasad concluded his visit to Laos on the morning of the 27th and left Vientiane by plane via Bangkok to return home.
    Crown Prince Savang Vatthana hosted a banquet on the 25th on behalf of the King of Laos in honor of President Prasad. President Prasad held a farewell banquet on the 26th.
    The Soviet Union and other countries will establish embassies in Ghana
    Accra dispatch: The Ghanaian Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a statement on the 28th stating that the governments of the Soviet Union, Lebanon, and Ethiopia will soon establish embassies in Accra, the capital of Ghana.
    The Yugoslav Embassy in Ghana was established in Accra last week.
    The statement said that Japan is currently establishing an embassy here.
    Soviet dam expert delegation leaves the United Arab Republic to return home
    Cairo dispatch: The Soviet high dam expert delegation led by Vasily Komzin, director of the Lenin Volga Hydropower Station in the Soviet Union, left Cairo for Moscow on the 25th after completing various aspects of research on the Aswan Dam project.
    UAR Vice President Amir received all members of the delegation on the 24th.

### 简讯

1959-03-30
第5版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    印度总统普拉沙德离万象回国
    万象讯：印度总统普拉沙德在27日上午结束对老挝的访问，乘飞机离开万象取道曼谷回国。
    萨旺王储曾经在25日代表老挝国王举行宴会招待普拉沙德总统。普拉沙德总统在26日举行了告别宴会。
    苏联等国将在加纳设大使馆
    阿克拉讯：加纳外交部28日发表一项声明说，苏联、黎巴嫩和埃塞俄比亚等国政府不久将在加纳首都阿克拉设立大使馆。
    南斯拉夫驻加纳大使馆上星期已经在阿克拉建立。
    声明说，日本目前正在这里建立大使馆。
    苏水坝专家代表团离阿联回国
    开罗讯：由苏联列宁伏尔加水电站站长瓦西里·科姆津率领的苏联高水坝专家代表团，在完成对阿斯旺水坝工程的各方面的研究以后，25日离开开罗回莫斯科。
    阿联副总统阿密尔在24日接见了这个代表团的全体团员。
