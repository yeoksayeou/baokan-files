Title: Arab League Foreign Ministers to Hold Emergency Meeting to Discuss Relations Between Iraq and the United Arab Republic

Author:
Date: 1959-03-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 24th. The Arab League Council issued a statement here today saying that the foreign ministers of the Arab League member states will hold an emergency meeting in Beirut on March 31 to discuss "the situation arising from the current state of relations between Iraq and the United Arab Republic." The statement was issued after the Arab League Political Committee held a meeting today. At the meeting of the Political Committee, with the exception of the UAR and Iraq, the decision was unanimously adopted. Neither the UAR nor Iraq participated in the vote. The Political Committee also requested the heads of Arab League member states to mediate on this issue.

The Arab League Political Committee held a total of three meetings on this issue.

### 阿拉伯联盟各国外长将举行紧急会议  讨论伊拉克和阿联的关系问题

1959-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟各国外长将举行紧急会议
    讨论伊拉克和阿联的关系问题
    新华社开罗24日电　阿拉伯联盟理事会今天在这里发表声明说，阿拉伯联盟成员国的外交部长将在3月31日在贝鲁特举行紧急会议，讨论“伊拉克和阿拉伯联合共和国目前的关系状态所产生的情况”。这个声明是在阿拉伯联盟政治委员会今天举行会议后发表的。在政治委员会会议上，除阿联和伊拉克外，一致通过这个决定。阿联和伊拉克都未参加投票。政治委员会也要求阿拉伯联盟成员国的首脑在这个问题上进行斡旋。
    阿拉伯联盟政治委员会一共就这个问题举行了三次会议。
