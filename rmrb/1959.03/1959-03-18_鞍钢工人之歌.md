Title: Songs of Anshan Steel Workers
Author:
Date: 1959-03-18
Page: 2

        One
    The flames in the furnace roar,
    The molten steel flows mightily,
    Red and bright waves surge,
    My heart, too, is stirred like a storm.
    The steel needs a good furnace temperature,
    Qualified components have tenacious properties,
    Holding the sampling spoon,
    Nervous and excited,
    Anxiously awaiting the moment of joy.
    The clang, clang sound of tapping the steel,
    Happiness fills the heart,
    A smiling face,
    Sweat seeping into the rolling molten steel.
      (Steelworker Peng Dongjing)
        Two
    The ball mill roars,
    The ore separator sings along,
    The Great Leap Forward soars to the sky,
    The hearts of the ore dressing workers bloom with joy.
          (Dagushan Ore Dressing Worker Yang Guangyu)
        Three
    The open hearth is a spring,
    The molten steel never runs dry;
    The open hearth is a treasure,
    The molten steel is better than gold.
    A shovel of ore, a drop of sweat,
    Refining steel beside the open hearth;
    A steel drill, a heart,
    Refining good steel and spreading good news.
                (Hu Zhiqiang)

### 鞍钢工人之歌

1959-03-18
第2版()
专栏：

    鞍钢工人之歌	一
    炉膛的火焰熊熊，
    钢流滚滚奔流，
    红亮的波浪翻卷，
    心里也是风起云涌。
    钢液需要好炉温，
    合格的成分有坚韧的性能，
    握着样勺，
    紧张、兴奋，
    焦急地等待喜悦的一瞬。
    当当当的出钢声，
    幸福洋溢心胸，
    满脸笑颜，
    汗水渗入翻滚的钢水中。
      （炼钢工人　彭冬劲）
        二
    球磨机轰隆隆地响，
    矿选机伴随着歌唱，
    生产大跃进冲天起，
    选矿工人心花怒放。
          （大孤山选矿工人　杨广余）
        三
    平炉是个泉，
    钢水流不完；
    平炉是个宝，
    钢水比金好。
    一锹矿砂一滴汗，
    平炉身边把钢炼；
    一把钢钎一颗心，
    炼出好钢传佳音。
                （胡志强）
