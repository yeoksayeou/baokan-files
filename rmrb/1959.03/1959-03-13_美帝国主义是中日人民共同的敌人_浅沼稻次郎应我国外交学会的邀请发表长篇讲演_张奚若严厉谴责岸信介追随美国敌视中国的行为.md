Title: American Imperialism is the Common Enemy of the Chinese and Japanese People. Inejiro Asanuma Delivers a Long Speech at the Invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. Zhang Xiruo Sternly Condemns Nobusuke Kishi for Following the United States in Antagonizing China.
Author:
Date: 1959-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 12th—Inejiro Asanuma, head of the Japanese Socialist Party delegation visiting China, delivered a long speech entitled "The Japanese Socialist Party's Struggle to Restore Japan-China Diplomatic Relations" at a lecture held at the CPPCC Auditorium tonight at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs (full text on page six). In his speech, he pointed out that American imperialism is the common enemy of the Chinese and Japanese people and expressed his determination to fight against American imperialism. In his speech, he also strongly expressed their determination to fight for the restoration of Japan-China diplomatic relations. Asanuma Inejiro first elaborated on the current international situation in his speech, then described the Socialist Party's struggle against the Nobusuke Kishi Cabinet, and introduced in detail the Socialist Party's basic policy for opening up Japan-China relations. Asanuma Inejiro's speech was constantly greeted with applause from more than 1,400 listeners.

Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, spoke at the beginning of the lecture (full text on page six), welcoming Mr. Inejiro Asanuma to give a speech at the invitation.

In his speech, Zhang Xiruo listed facts and sternly condemned the Nobusuke Kishi government, which represents the potential imperialist forces in Japan, for following the American aggressors and antagonizing the Chinese people. Zhang Xiruo said that the Chinese people must warn Nobusuke Kishi that as long as Nobusuke Kishi is determined to act as an accomplice of the United States in its aggression against China and Asia, and stubbornly continues to implement the reactionary policy of antagonizing China, creating two Chinas, and obstructing the restoration of normal relations between China and Japan, Sino-Japanese relations will not improve, Sino-Japanese trade will not be restored, and Nobusuke Kishi will only suffer the consequences.

Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Liu Changsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, attended the lecture tonight.

Japanese peace figure Kinkazu Saionji and others in Beijing attended tonight's lecture.

### 美帝国主义是中日人民共同的敌人  浅沼稻次郎应我国外交学会的邀请发表长篇讲演  张奚若严厉谴责岸信介追随美国敌视中国的行为

1959-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    美帝国主义是中日人民共同的敌人
    浅沼稻次郎应我国外交学会的邀请发表长篇讲演  张奚若严厉谴责岸信介追随美国敌视中国的行为
    据新华社12日讯　日本社会党访华代表团团长浅沼稻次郎今晚应中国人民外交学会的邀请，在政协礼堂举行的讲演会上，发表了题为“为恢复日中邦交而斗争的日本社会党”的长篇讲演（全文见第六版）。他在讲演中指出：美帝国主义是中日两国人民共同的敌人，并且表示要为反对美帝国主义进行斗争。他在讲演中还强烈地表达了他们为恢复日中邦交而斗争的决心。浅沼稻次郎在讲演中首先畅述了当前的国际形势，接着叙述了社会党对岸信介内阁进行斗争的情况，详细介绍了社会党的打开日中关系的基本方针。浅沼稻次郎的讲话不断博得一千四百多名听众的鼓掌欢迎。
    中国人民外交学会会长张奚若在讲演会开始时讲话（全文见第六版），对浅沼稻次郎先生应邀发表讲演表示欢迎。
    张奚若在讲话中列举事实，严厉谴责代表日本潜在帝国主义势力的岸信介政府追随美国侵略者、敌视中国人民的行为。张奚若说，中国人民必须警告岸信介，只要岸信介决心充当美国侵略中国和亚洲的帮凶，执迷不悟地继续执行敌视中国、制造两个中国和阻挠中日正常关系恢复的反动政策，中日关系就无法好转，中日贸易也无法恢复，只能使岸信介自己自食其果。
    参加今晚讲演会的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎、全国总工会副主席刘长胜、全国妇联副主席李德全等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一等参加了今晚的讲演会。
