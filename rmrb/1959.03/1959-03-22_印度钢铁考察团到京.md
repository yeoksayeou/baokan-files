Title: Indian Steel Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-03-22
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, March 21st: The Indian steel delegation headed by K.S. Raghupathi, Secretary of the Indian Ministry of Steel, Iron, Coal and Fuel, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.
    At the airport, they were welcomed by Zhai Ziqiang, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry and Director of the Foreign Liaison Department, and Wang Zhixi, Deputy Director of the Steel Department of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, and others.
    The delegation will stay in our country for two or three weeks and will visit Beijing and other regions.
    This afternoon, Wang Heshou, Minister of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, and Liu Bin, Vice Minister, and others met with all members of the delegation and had friendly talks.

### 印度钢铁考察团到京

1959-03-22
第3版()
专栏：

    印度钢铁考察团到京
    新华社21日讯　印度政府钢、铁煤炭和燃料部秘书克·斯·雷古帕蒂率领的印度钢铁考察团，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    在飞机场上，他们受到了冶金工业部办公厅主任、对外联络司司长翟自强和冶金工业部钢铁司副司长王之玺等人的欢迎。
    代表团将在我国停留两、三个星期，并将在北京和其他地区进行参观访问。
    今天下午，冶金工业部部长王鹤寿，副部长刘彬等接见了代表团的全体人员，进行了友好的谈话。
