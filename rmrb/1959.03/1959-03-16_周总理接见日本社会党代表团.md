Title: Premier Zhou Receives Japanese Socialist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 15th: Premier Zhou Enlai received this morning Asanuma Inejiro and the entire members of the Japanese Socialist Party delegation visiting China led by him: Katsumata Seiichi, Okada Munetsuji, Soya Masaru, Nakazaki Satoshi, Sata Tadataka, Tanaka Toshio, attendants Hirosawa Kenichi and Watanabe Ichiro.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi was present at the reception.

Also present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, Liao Chengzhi, Li Dequan, Chu Tunan, Lu Xuzhang, Ji Chao Ding, Wen Zhangjin, Zhao Anbo, and others.

Japanese journalists who came to China with the delegation were also present at the reception.

Japanese people in Beijing were also present.
After the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi had lunch with all the members and attendants of the Japanese Socialist Party delegation. (Attached picture)
Premier Zhou receiving Asanuma Inejiro and the Japanese Socialist Party delegation visiting China. The picture shows the scene during the reception. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen.

### 周总理接见日本社会党代表团

1959-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本社会党代表团
    新华社15日讯　周恩来总理今天上午接见了浅沼稻次郎和由他率领的日本社会党访华代表团的全体团员：胜间田清一、冈田宗司、曾弥益、中琦敏、佐多忠隆、田中稔男，随员广泽贤一和渡边郎。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长陪同接见。
    接见时在座的，有张奚若、廖承志、李德全、楚图南、卢绪章、冀朝鼎、文章晋、赵安博等。
    接见时在座的还有随代表团来中国的日本记者。
    在北京的日本人士也在座。
  接见后，周恩来总理和陈毅副总理兼外交部长同日本社会党访华代表团的全体成员和随员，共进午餐。（附图片）
