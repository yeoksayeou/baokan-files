Title: German Democratic Republic Founding Committee Prepares to Celebrate Tenth Anniversary of Founding
Author:
Date: 1959-03-15
Page: 3

    German Democratic Republic Founding Committee
    Prepares to Celebrate Tenth Anniversary of Founding
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 13th: The Presidium of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic and the Presidium of the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany held a joint meeting on the 12th to discuss preparations for celebrating the tenth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.
    Prime Minister Grotewohl said at the meeting that the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Republic should inspire us to continue to achieve great successes in political, economic, and cultural aspects, based on the achievements we have already made in the struggle for peace and building a new socialist life. The mass movement to prepare for the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the nation will raise the socialist consciousness of the people and consolidate the legitimate Germany - the German Democratic Republic. Grotewohl pointed out that industrial and agricultural workers have already launched labor competitions to welcome the tenth anniversary of the founding of the nation.
    A joint committee was established at the meeting to carry out the work of preparing for the celebration activities.

### 德意志民主共和国成立委员会  筹备庆祝建国十周年

1959-03-15
第3版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国成立委员会
    筹备庆祝建国十周年
    新华社柏林13日电　德意志民主共和国部长会议主席团和民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席团12日举行联席会议，讨论筹备庆祝德意志民主共和国成立十周年的问题。
    格罗提渥总理在会上讲话说，共和国成立的十周年应当激励我们在争取和平和建设社会主义新生活的斗争中已经取得的成就的基础上，继续在政治、经济和文化方面取得巨大的成就。筹备庆祝建国十周年的群众运动，将提高人民的社会主义觉悟，并将巩固合法的德国——德意志民主共和国。格罗提渥指出，工农业劳动者已经展开劳动竞赛，来迎接建国十周年。
    会上成立了一个联合委员会，来进行筹备庆祝活动的工作。
