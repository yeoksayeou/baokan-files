		International Briefs
		CPSU Moscow Regional Committee Discusses Organizational Issues
		Moscow dispatch: The Moscow Regional Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (CPSU) held a plenary session on March 2 to discuss organizational issues.
		The meeting decided to remove Comrade Kapitonov from the positions of First Secretary of the CPSU Moscow Regional Committee and member of the Standing Committee of the CPSU Moscow Regional Committee.
		Comrade Demichev was elected as the First Secretary of the CPSU Moscow Regional Committee.
		U.S. President Invites Macmillan to Visit the U.S.
		Washington dispatch: White House Press Secretary Hagerty announced on March 3 that U.S. President Eisenhower had invited British Prime Minister Macmillan to visit Washington "at his convenience" to discuss the Berlin situation and Macmillan's trip to Moscow.
		According to Hagerty, Eisenhower extended this invitation in a letter to the British Prime Minister on the evening of March 2.

### 国际简讯

1959-03-06
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    苏共莫斯科州委会讨论组织问题
    莫斯科讯：苏联共产党莫斯科州委员会2日举行全体会议，讨论了组织问题。
    会议决定免去卡皮托诺夫同志苏共莫斯科州委员会第一书记和苏共莫斯科州委员会常务委员会委员的职务。
    选举杰米契夫同志为苏共莫斯科州委员会第一书记。
    美国总统邀麦克米伦赴美
    华盛顿消息：白宫新闻秘书哈格蒂3日宣布，美国总统艾森豪威尔已经邀请英国首相麦克米伦“在他方便的时候”访问华盛顿，讨论柏林局势和麦克米伦的莫斯科之行。
    据哈格蒂说，艾森豪威尔这个邀请是在2日晚上给英国首相的信中发出的。
