Title: Japanese Communist Party Secretary General Miyamoto Kenji Visits Zhengzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-03-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, March 3rd: Miyamoto Kenji, Secretary General of the Japanese Communist Party, visited the state-owned Zhengzhou No. 3 Cotton Textile Factory this morning, accompanied by Li Wen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Liu Bi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Zhengzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.

The person in charge of the Zhengzhou No. 3 Cotton Textile Factory introduced the factory's production and workers' welfare to Miyamoto Kenji. Afterwards, Miyamoto Kenji visited the factory's various production workshops, and focused on visiting the factory's canteen, lactation room, nursery, mother-infant building, as well as the employee family dormitories and employee collective dormitories.

During the visit, Miyamoto Kenji was warmly welcomed by the staff and workers' families.

### 日本共产党总书记  宫本显治在郑州参观

1959-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    日本共产党总书记
    宫本显治在郑州参观
    新华社郑州3日电　日本共产党总书记宫本显治，今天上午在中共河南省委书记处书记李文、中共郑州市委书记处书记刘必陪同下，参观了国营郑州第三棉纺织厂。
    郑州第三棉纺织厂负责人向宫本显治介绍了全厂生产和工人生活福利事业的情况。接着，宫本显治参观了工厂的各个生产车间，并着重地参观了工厂的食堂、哺乳室、托儿所、母子楼以及职工家属宿舍和职工集体宿舍。
    在参观的时候，宫本显治受到了职工群众和工人家属的热烈欢迎。
