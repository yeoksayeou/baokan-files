Title: International Women's Federation Sends Letter to UN Human Rights Commission Demanding Respect for the Basic Human Rights and Independent Aspirations of the African People
Author:
Date: 1959-03-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 21st. The Women's International Democratic Federation has written to the United Nations Human Rights Commission, expressing its indignation at the persecution of the people of the Congo and Nyasaland, and demanding respect for the Declaration of Human Rights.

The Women's International Democratic Federation, in its published communique, stated that it demands an immediate end to the suppression, and respect for the just aspirations of the Congo and African people for national independence.

### 国际妇联致函联合国人权委员会  要求尊重非洲人民的基本人权和独立愿望

1959-03-23
第5版()
专栏：

    国际妇联致函联合国人权委员会
    要求尊重非洲人民的基本人权和独立愿望
    新华社柏林21日电　国际民主妇女联合会写信给联合国人权委员会，对刚果和尼亚萨兰人民遭受迫害表示愤怒，并且要求尊重人权宣言。
    国际民主妇联在它发表的公报中说，国际民主妇联要求立即停止镇压行动，尊重刚果和非洲人民争取民族独立的正当愿望。
