	International Briefs

	Indian President Prasad Arrives in Vientiane
	Vientiane Dispatch: Indian President Prasad arrived in Vientiane, the capital of Laos, at noon on the 25th. At 4 p.m. that day, President Prasad and his entourage paid a visit to Crown Prince Savang.

	Cárdenas Agrees to Hold Summit Conference
	Mexico City Dispatch: Former Mexican President Cárdenas, who recently visited China, held a press conference on the 24th and spoke on the international situation and domestic issues.

	Cárdenas believes that holding a high-level meeting is of great significance. He said, "If a high-level meeting can be held, then the conversations conducted at the meeting will be of great benefit to all countries in the world, especially to Latin American countries." He expressed his hope that the upcoming summit conference of heads of government will be able to find a way for the coexistence of different systems in the East and West.

	Nasser Returns to Cairo
	Cairo Dispatch: President Nasser of the United Arab Republic returned to Cairo on the afternoon of the 25th from Damascus. Before returning to Cairo, Nasser had met with Lebanese President Chehab in Lebanon. Nasser went to Damascus on February 22 this year. He stayed in the Syrian region for more than a month.

### 国际简讯

1959-03-28
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    印度总统普拉沙德到达万象
    万象讯：印度总统普拉沙德25日中午到达老挝首都万象。当天下午四点，普拉沙德总统同随行人员拜会了萨旺王储。
    卡德纳斯赞同举行首脑会议
    墨西哥城讯：最近访问中国的墨西哥前总统卡德纳斯在24日举行了一次记者招待会，就国际形势和国内问题发表谈话。
    卡德纳斯认为举行高级会议有重大意义。他说，“高级会议若能举行，那么会上所进行的交谈将对世界各国，特别是对拉丁美洲各国有很大益处。”他表示希望，即将举行的政府首脑会议将能找到东西方不同制度共处的途径。
    纳赛尔回到开罗
    开罗讯：阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔25日下午从大马士革回到开罗。回开罗以前，纳赛尔曾经同黎巴嫩总统谢哈布在黎巴嫩某地会晤。纳赛尔是今年2月22日到大马士革去的。他在叙利亚地区停留了一个多月。
