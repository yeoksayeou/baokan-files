Title: Sichuan Vigorously Develops Acid and Alkali Production
Author:
Date: 1959-03-21
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, March 20th: Sichuan Province is taking practical measures to vigorously increase the production of three acids (sulfuric acid, nitric acid, hydrochloric acid) and two alkalis (caustic soda, soda ash) to ensure the needs of the growing steel production and other industrial production.

Chongqing, a city with a relatively well-established chemical industry, has now placed the increase of acid and alkali production in a primary position. Chongqing Tianyuan Chemical Plant, Chongqing Dyestuff Factory, and other chemical enterprises have recently launched mass technical revolution movements focusing on acid and alkali production. In addition to focusing on the production of existing enterprises, Sichuan Province is also concentrating its efforts on building two new modern acid-alkali enterprises—Sichuan Chemical Plant and Zigong Soda Ash Plant.

### 四川大力发展酸碱生产

1959-03-21
第2版()
专栏：

    四川大力发展酸碱生产
    据新华社重庆20日电　四川省正在采取切实措施大力增产三酸（硫酸、硝酸、盐酸）两碱（烧碱、纯碱），保证钢铁生产和其他工业生产日益发展的需要。
    化学工业较有基础的重庆市，现在已把增产酸、碱放在首要地位。重庆天原化工厂、重庆染料厂等化工企业，都在最近开展了以酸、碱生产为主的群众性的技术革命运动。除了抓紧现有企业的生产以外，四川省还在集中力量建设两个新的现代化酸碱企业——四川化工厂和自贡纯碱厂。
