Title: Indonesian Communist Party Delegation Leaves Beijing After Visit to China, Invited to Hanoi

Author: 
Date: 1959-03-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: The Indonesian Communist Party delegation led by General Secretary D.N. Aidit, having concluded their two-week visit to China, left Beijing by plane this morning for Hanoi. They were invited by the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    Those who saw them off at the airport included: Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Huang Kecheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Liu Ningyi, member of the CPC Central Committee; and Li Qixin, a responsible staff member of the CPC Central Committee organs. Nguyen Khang, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and Li Yonghao, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, were also present at the airport to see them off.

    Before Comrade Aidit and others left Beijing, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, hosted a farewell banquet for them. Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, alternate secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; and Liu Ningyi, member of the CPC Central Committee, attended the banquet.

### 结束在我国访问　应邀前往河内  印度尼西亚共产党代表团离京

1959-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    结束在我国访问　应邀前往河内
    印度尼西亚共产党代表团离京
    新华社27日讯　印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地率领的印度尼西亚共产党代表团，已经结束在中国为期两周的参观访问，今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往河内。他们是应越南劳动党中央的邀请到越南民主共和国进行参观访问的。
    到机场欢送的有：中共中央政治局委员陈毅、中央书记处书记黄克诚、中央委员刘宁一、中共中央机关负责工作人员李启新。到机场欢送的还有越南民主共和国驻华大使阮康，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李永镐。
    在艾地同志等离开北京之前，中共中央总书记邓小平曾设宴为他们送行，出席宴会作陪的有：中共中央政治局委员彭真，中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员刘宁一。
