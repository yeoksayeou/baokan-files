Title: Zawadzki Holds Farewell Banquet, Bids Farewell to Brothers Party Representatives from China, Albania, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-03-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 19th: Polish State Council Chairman and Polish United Workers' Party Central Political Bureau member Zawadzki held a farewell banquet on the afternoon of the 18th for the delegations of fraternal parties from China, Albania, Korea, Mongolia, Morocco, Canada, and other countries who were invited to attend the Third Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party.  Attending the banquet were Polish United Workers' Party Central Political Bureau members Jedrychowski, Loga-Sowiński, and Rapacki, as well as members of the Presidium of the Third Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party.  At the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted the internationalist friendship.

### 萨瓦茨基举行饯别宴会  欢送中阿等国兄弟党代表

1959-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    萨瓦茨基举行饯别宴会
    欢送中阿等国兄弟党代表
    新华社华沙19日电　波兰国务委员会主席、波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员萨瓦茨基18日下午为应邀出席波兰统一工人党第三次代表大会的中国、阿尔巴尼亚、朝鲜、蒙古、摩洛哥、加拿大等兄弟党代表团举行饯别宴会。出席宴会的有波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员英德里霍夫斯基、洛加—索文斯基、腊帕茨基以及波兰统一工人党第三次代表大会的主席团委员。宴会上宾主双方不断为国际主义的友谊干杯。
