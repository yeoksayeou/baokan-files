Title: Squaring the Circle (Picture)
Author: Zhang Qingyun
Date: 1959-03-25
Page: 2

    Squaring the Circle
    Zheng Quanbin (left), a national advanced producer, technical innovation expert at the Jiaozuo City Machinery Manufacturing Plant, and a Communist Party member, has recently created a square milling machine after improving the universal punch press and universal casting platform, and completing twenty-four years' worth of work in one year, enabling the lathe to machine not only round components but also square ones.
    This is him working beside the square milling machine.
                            Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Qingyun

### 方圆两可（图片）

专栏：

    方圆两可
    全国先进生产者，焦作市机械制造厂技术革新能手、共产党员郑全斌（左）在改进万能冲床、万能铸件台、一年完成二十四年工作量之后，最近又创制了一部铣方机，使车床不仅能车元件而且能车方件了。
