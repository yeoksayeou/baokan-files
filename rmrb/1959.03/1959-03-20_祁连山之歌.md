Title: Song of the Qilian Mountains
Author: Yu Zhenji, Liu Chongde
Date: 1959-03-20
Page: 2

    Song of the Qilian Mountains
    People call Li Bai a poetry immortal,
    A hundred poems he could compose with a jug of wine;
    Industrial vanguards need no wine,
    With iron hammers they forge ten thousand poems.
        (Yu Zhenji)
    The Red Army fears not the Long March's hardship,
    Heroes fear not wind and snow's cold;
    Learn from the Red Army's heroic spirit,
    Aspire to battle fiercely in the Qilian Mountains.
        (Liu Chongde)

### 祁连山之歌
余振基  刘崇德
1959-03-20
第2版()
专栏：

    祁连山之歌
    人称李白诗中仙，
    斗酒能赋诗百篇；
    工业尖兵不用酒，
    铁锤打出诗万千。
        （余振基）
    红军不怕远征难，
    英雄哪怕风雪寒；
    学习红军英雄胆，
    立志大战祁连山。
        （刘崇德）
