Title: Võ Nguyên Giáp Leads Delegation Through Beijing En Route to Warsaw to Attend Polish United Workers' Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-03-08
Page: 1

    Invited to Warsaw to Attend Polish United Workers' Party Congress
    Võ Nguyên Giáp Leads Delegation Through Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, March 7th – At the invitation of the Polish United Workers' Party, Võ Nguyên Giáp, head of the Vietnamese Labor Party delegation and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, and delegation member Tố Hữu, Central Committee member, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of March 6th. They departed Beijing for Warsaw on the morning of March 7th. Peng Dehuai, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Chen Geng and Liu Ningyi, members of the Central Committee, and Luo Guibo, alternate member of the Central Committee, were at the airport to welcome and see them off. Phạm Bình, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, and Fū Lātuō, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, and Counselor Dēng Bùnícīji, were also at the airport to welcome and see them off.

### 应邀赴华沙参加波兰统一工人党代表大会  武元甲率代表团过北京

1959-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀赴华沙参加波兰统一工人党代表大会
    武元甲率代表团过北京
    新华社7日讯　应邀参加波兰统一工人党第三次代表大会的越南劳动党代表团团长、越南劳动党中央政治局委员武元甲和团员、中央委员素友，于3月6日下午到达北京；3月7日上午离开北京飞赴华沙。前往机场欢迎和欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员彭德怀，中央委员陈赓、刘宁一，中央候补委员罗贵波。越南驻中国大使馆临时代办范平，波兰驻中国大使馆临时代办傅拉托、参赞邓布尼茨基等，也到机场欢迎和欢送。
