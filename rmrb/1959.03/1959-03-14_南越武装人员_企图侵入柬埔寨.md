Title: South Vietnamese Armed Personnel Attempt to Invade Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1959-03-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, March 13th: According to a report today from the Cambodian News Agency, two groups of South Vietnamese armed personnel, equivalent to several hundred regular soldiers and equipped with sophisticated weapons, attempted to invade Cambodian territory in the border areas near Koh Andet and Peak Krabas in Takeo Province, and Koh Thom in Kandal Province, in order to create disturbances.

The news agency said that the Cambodian local authorities have taken some necessary precautionary measures.

### 南越武装人员  企图侵入柬埔寨

1959-03-14
第5版()
专栏：

    南越武装人员
    企图侵入柬埔寨
    新华社金边13日电　据柬埔寨新闻社今天报道，相当于几百名正规军的两股配备有精良武器的南越武装人员，企图在柬埔寨茶胶省的科赫安代特和佩克腊巴斯、干丹省的科赫通附近边界地方侵入柬埔寨国境，制造骚乱。
    这家新闻社说，柬埔寨地方当局已经采取某些必要的预防措施。
