Title: Coal Miners Launch Red Banner Competition; Radio Rally Today
Author:
Date: 1959-03-02
Page: 2

Coal Miners Launch Red Banner Competition
Radio Rally Today

(Our report) The Ministry of Coal Industry and the Central People's Broadcasting Station will jointly hold a "National Coal Miners Red Banner Competition" radio rally on March 2nd.

The Ministry of Coal Industry recently held a national coal mine cadres meeting in Beijing. At the meeting, various regions, mining bureaus, and coal mines summarized their experiences from the Great Leap Forward in 1958 and launched a fervent upsurge to strive for the Red Banner and ensure the completion of this year's production target of 380 million tons of coal. To this end, the Ministry of Coal Industry and the Central People's Broadcasting Station are jointly holding the "National Coal Miners Red Banner Competition" radio rally.

The radio rally will be broadcast on the first program of the Central Station from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM on March 2nd, and rebroadcast on the second program from 6:30 PM to 9:30 PM in the evening.

### 煤矿职工开展红旗竞赛  今日举办广播大会

1959-03-02
第2版()
专栏：

    煤矿职工开展红旗竞赛
    今日举办广播大会
    本报讯　煤炭工业部和中央人民广播电台将在3月2日联合举办“全国煤矿职工红旗竞赛”广播大会。
    煤炭工业部最近在北京召开全国煤矿干部会议，会上各地区、各矿务局、各煤矿总结了1958年大跃进的经验，并且掀起了鼓足干劲夺红旗，确保完成今年生产三亿八千万吨煤的热潮。煤炭工业部和中央人民广播电台为此特联合举办“全国煤矿职工红旗竞赛”广播大会。
    广播大会的时间是3月2日下午两点到五点，在中央台第一种节目播出；晚上六点半到九点半在第二种节目重播。
