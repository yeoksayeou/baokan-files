Title: Algerian Military Delegation Arrives in Beijing, General Huang Kecheng Hosts a Banquet for Distinguished Guests from a Heroic Country
Author:
Date: 1959-03-30
Page: 1

    Algerian Military Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    General Huang Kecheng Hosts a Banquet for Distinguished Guests from a Heroic Country
    (Our dispatch) An Algerian military delegation headed by Omar Oussedik, Secretary of State of the Republic of Algeria, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 29th. At the airport, the distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by General Huang Kecheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense, as well as senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and officers and soldiers of the Navy and Air Force.
    That evening, General Huang Kecheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense, hosted a banquet to warmly welcome the distinguished guests, and on behalf of our Ministry of National Defense and all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, expressed his lofty respect to the representatives of the heroic Algerian People's Liberation Army and to the Algerian people who are fighting. ### 阿尔及利亚军事代表团到京  黄克诚大将欢宴来自英雄国家的贵宾

1959-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚军事代表团到京
    黄克诚大将欢宴来自英雄国家的贵宾
    本报讯　以阿尔及利亚共和国国务秘书奥马尔·乌西迪克为首的阿尔及利亚军事代表团，29日下午乘飞机到达北京。在机场上，贵宾们受到国防部副部长黄克诚大将以及中国人民解放军高级将领和海陆空军官兵的热烈欢迎。
    当天晚间，国防部副部长黄克诚大将举行宴会，热烈欢迎贵宾，并代表我国国防部和中国人民解放军全体官兵，向英勇的阿尔及利亚人民解放军的代表们，向正在战斗中的阿尔及利亚人民，表示崇高的敬意。（详见第四版）（附图片）
