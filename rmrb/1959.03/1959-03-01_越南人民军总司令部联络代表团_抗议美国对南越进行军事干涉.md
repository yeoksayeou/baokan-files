Title: Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army Protests US Military Intervention in South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1959-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 28th -- According to Vietnam News Agency: The Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army recently protested the increasing number of US military personnel illegally entering South Vietnam for military intervention.

In two protest letters sent to the International Commission, the delegation requested the International Commission to pay attention to the US military intervention in South Vietnam and to take resolute measures to ensure the implementation of the Geneva Accords.

It is reported that US Pacific Fleet Commander Hopwood recently conducted a three-day inspection tour in South Vietnam.

During his stay in South Vietnam, Hopwood discussed the issue of expanding armaments in South Vietnam with Ngo Dinh Diem and the US military assistance advisory group stationed in South Vietnam.

Earlier, a US military delegation headed by Collins and US Pacific Area Forces Commander Harry Felt also conducted activities in Saigon for the same purpose.

### 越南人民军总司令部联络代表团  抗议美国对南越进行军事干涉

1959-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军总司令部联络代表团
    抗议美国对南越进行军事干涉
    新华社河内28日电　据越南通讯社消息：越南人民军总司令部联络代表团最近就越来越多的美国军事人员非法进入南越进行军事干涉一事提出抗议。
    代表团在向国际委员会发出的两封抗议信中，要求国际委员会注意美国对南越进行的这种军事干涉，并且要求采取坚决措施保证日内瓦协议的履行。
    据报道，美国太平洋舰队司令霍普伍德最近在南越进行了为时三天的视察旅行。
    霍普伍德在南越停留期间，曾经同吴庭艳和美国驻南越的军事援助顾问团讨论了关于南越的扩充军备问题。
    早些时候，以柯林斯和美国太平洋地区部队总司令哈里·费耳特为首的一个美国军事代表团也为了同样目的到西贡进行过活动。
