Title: National Spring Track and Field Championships Chengdu Region Competition Concludes
Author:
Date: 1959-03-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Report:
    National Spring Track and Field Championships
    Chengdu Region Competition Concludes

    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Chengdu region competition of the National Spring Track and Field Zonal Competition has concluded.

    During the competition, three athletes reached the Master of Sports standard. They were Liu Deyun from the People's Liberation Army team and Li Zhanchao from the Shaanxi team in the 10-kilometer race walk, and Hong Yi from the Shaanxi team in the marathon. In addition, 36 athletes reached the first-class athlete standard, and 185 athletes reached the second-class athlete standard. Athletes from various regions broke the highest records of their respective provinces and regions 86 times. The Ningxia team and the Qinghai team broke their respective province's highest records of 1958 more than 20 times each.

    Three hundred and eighty-four track and field athletes from seven units, including the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, Qinghai Province, Gansu Province, Shaanxi Province, Guizhou Province, and Sichuan Province, participated in this competition. The results of the competition for total team scores were: Sichuan team first, Shaanxi team second, People's Liberation Army team third, Guizhou team fourth, and Gansu team fifth.

### 全国春季田径赛  成都赛区比赛结束

1959-03-01
第3版()
专栏：

    全国春季田径赛
    成都赛区比赛结束
    据新华社讯　全国春季田径分区赛成都赛区比赛结束。
    比赛中，有三名运动员达到健将级标准，他们是十公里竞走的解放军队的刘德云和陕西队的李战朝，以及马拉松赛跑的陕西队的宏义。另有三十六人次达到一级运动员标准，一百八十五人次达到二级运动员标准。各地运动员打破了所在省区最高纪录八十六项次，宁夏队和青海队打破本省1958年最高纪录都在二十项以上。
    参加这次比赛的有中国人民解放军、宁夏回族自治区、青海省、甘肃省、陕西省、贵州省和四川省等七个单位的三百八十四名田径运动员。比赛结果，团体总分：四川队第一，陕西队第二，解放军队第三，贵州队第四，甘肃队第五。
