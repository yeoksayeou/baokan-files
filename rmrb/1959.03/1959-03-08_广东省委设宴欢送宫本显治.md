Title: Guangdong Provincial Party Committee Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Kenji Miyamoto
Author:
Date: 1959-03-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 7th: The Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, Kasuga Shoichi, member of the JCP Central Committee Presidium, and Nishizawa Tomio, Deputy Director of the JCP Central Committee, who are about to return to their country.

　　Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, made a special trip back from the countryside to bid farewell to Comrade Kenji Miyamoto and other comrades.

　　Also present at the banquet tonight were Wen Minsheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, alternate secretary Yin Linping, Wang De, First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and other responsible cadres of the Guangdong Provincial Committee and the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.

　　This morning, Comrade Kenji Miyamoto and other comrades, accompanied by Yin Linping, alternate secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Wang Yanguang, Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Wang Yongxin, First Secretary of the Guangzhou Suburban Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, visited the Xin'jiao People's Commune in the suburbs of Guangzhou. In the evening, they watched a performance by the acrobatic troupe.

### 广东省委设宴欢送宫本显治

1959-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    广东省委设宴欢送宫本显治
    新华社广州7日电　中共广东省委今晚设宴欢送即将回国的日本共产党总书记宫本显治、日共中央干部会委员春日正一、日共中央副部长西泽富夫。
    中共广东省委第一书记陶铸，特地从乡下赶回来欢送宫本显治等同志。
    出席今晚宴会的还有中共广东省委书记处书记文敏生，候补书记尹林平，中共广州市委第一书记王德以及中共广东省委、广州市委其他负责干部。
    今天上午，宫本显治等同志由中共广东省委书记处候补书记尹林平、中共广州市委秘书长王焰光和中共广州市郊委第一书记王永新等陪同，访问了广州市郊区新滘人民公社。晚上，观看了杂技团的演出。
