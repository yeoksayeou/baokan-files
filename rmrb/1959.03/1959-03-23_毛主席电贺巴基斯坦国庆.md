Title: Chairman Mao Cables Congratulations on Pakistan's National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-03-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd

    Chairman Mao Cables Congratulations on Pakistan's National Day

    Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd – Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to General Muhammad Ayub Khan, President of Pakistan, on March 21st, congratulating him on Pakistan's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:

    To His Excellency General Muhammad Ayub Khan, President of Pakistan:

    On the occasion of Pakistan's National Day, I extend my congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Pakistan on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish Pakistan prosperity and its people happiness.

  Mao Zedong
  Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                        March 21, 1959

### 毛主席电贺巴基斯坦国庆

1959-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺巴基斯坦国庆
    新华社22日讯　毛泽东主席21日致电巴基斯坦总统穆罕默德·阿尤布汗将军，祝贺巴基斯坦国庆。电文如下：巴基斯坦总统穆罕默德·阿尤布汗将军阁下：
    欣逢巴基斯坦国庆，我代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，向阁下和巴基斯坦人民表示祝贺。祝巴基斯坦国家繁荣、人民幸福。
  中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                        1959年3月21日
