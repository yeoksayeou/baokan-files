Title: People from All Walks of Life in Britain Support the Struggle of the People of Nyasaland
Author:
Date: 1959-03-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, London, March 10th: People from all walks of life in Britain have expressed their support for the struggle of the people of Nyasaland for freedom and independence.

    Scottish miners' leaders held a meeting yesterday and passed a resolution protesting the Nyasaland incident. The resolution said that the miners supported the struggle of the people of Nyasaland for higher wages, improved working conditions, freedom and independence.

    A student assembly convened by twelve societies of "University College" in London passed a resolution expressing regret over the massacre of Africans and the arrest of Dr. Banda, leader of the Nyasaland African National Congress. The resolution, passed by more than 300 people, called on the British Parliament to recognize the aspirations of the African people for national independence. The assembly also decided to send a telegram in support of the people of Nyasaland.

    Kanyama Chiume, the person in charge of the Nyasaland African National Congress, spoke at the student assembly. In addition, the Socialist Medical Association also strongly protested the arrest of Dr. Banda.

### 英国各阶层人民  支持尼亚萨兰人民斗争

1959-03-11
第5版()
专栏：

    英国各阶层人民
    支持尼亚萨兰人民斗争
    新华社伦敦10日电　英国各阶层人民纷纷表示支持尼亚萨兰人民争取自由和独立的斗争。
    苏格兰矿工领袖昨天举行会议，通过了关于抗议尼亚萨兰事件的决议。决议说，矿工们支持尼亚萨兰人民要求提高工资、改善工作条件、争取自由和独立的斗争。
    伦敦的“大学学院”的十二个社团召开的学生大会通过了一项决议，对屠杀非洲人和逮捕尼亚萨兰非洲人国民大会领袖班达博士一事表示遗憾。这个由三百多人通过的决议要求英国议会承认非洲人民争取民族独立的愿望。大会还决定打电报支持尼亚萨兰人民。
    尼亚萨兰非洲人国民大会负责人卡尼亚姆·丘梅在学生大会上讲了话。另外，社会主义医学协会也强烈抗议逮捕班达博士。
