Title: Further Strengthen the Profound Friendship Between the Vietnamese and Indian Peoples: Grand Rally in Hanoi Welcomes Indian President; Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Premier Pham Van Dong Hold Welcoming Banquet
Author:
Date: 1959-03-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 23rd: Two hundred thousand people in Hanoi held a grand rally this afternoon at Ba Dinh Square, the sacred site of the August Revolution, to welcome Indian President Prasad's visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

President Prasad delivered a speech amidst the sustained cheers of the masses. He said, "I feel as if I am now living among a respectable nation, whose long history and recent resurgence can completely make you proud."

President Prasad said: "We are very concerned about peace and deeply feel that peaceful coexistence between nations is necessary." He said, "I can assure you that there will be the most friendly relations and the most wonderful exchanges between our two countries. I extend my best congratulations to you for the realization of your plans to improve the people's lives and make the country progress."

President Prasad finally expressed his gratitude for the warm reception given to him by the Vietnamese people. He concluded, "The friendship between our two countries will surely be consolidated and developed forever."

Subsequently, Chairman Ho Chi Minh delivered a brief speech saying that the warm welcome given by the Vietnamese people to President Prasad for the past two days fully demonstrates the profound friendship between the Vietnamese and Indian peoples. As President Prasad said, the friendship between our two countries will surely be consolidated and developed forever. Chairman Ho Chi Minh then led the entire audience in chanting "Long live Vietnamese-Indian friendship! Long live world peace!"

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 23rd: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a state banquet for Indian President Prasad at the Presidential Palace on the evening of the 22nd.

Present at the banquet were Premier Pham Van Dong, Deputy Premiers Truong Chinh, Pham Hung, and Phan Ke Toai, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly Ton Duc Thang, Presidium Member of the Central Committee of the Fatherland Front Bui Ky, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Vietnamese People's Army Major General Hoang Van Thai, representatives of various parties and people's organizations, Chairman of the Hanoi Administrative Committee Tran Duy Hung, as well as representatives of the diplomatic missions stationed in Vietnam and Chairman Ansari of the Vietnam International Commission.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh said in his speech at the banquet that, based on the "Panchsheel," the friendship between the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Republic of India is developing day by day.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh said that the Vietnamese people hope that the Geneva Accords can be thoroughly implemented in Indochina. The unification of Vietnam is the most urgent desire of the Vietnamese people and a guarantee for peace in Southeast Asia and the world.

President Prasad said in his speech at the banquet that in the current era, Asian and African countries are becoming an increasingly powerful force, a force determined to liberate themselves from foreign shackles and overcome their own weaknesses. There are already increasingly clear signs that peace can certainly be maintained, and the Indian people are determined to follow the path of peace and strive to serve peace.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 23rd: Premier Pham Van Dong held a grand banquet at the "Guest House" in Hanoi this evening for Indian President Prasad's visit to Vietnam. Chairman Ho Chi Minh attended the banquet, as did Deputy Premiers Truong Chinh and Phan Ke Toai, ministers of various government ministries, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly Ton Quang Phiet, diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam, and representatives of the International Commission.

### 进一步加强越印两国人民的深厚友谊  河内盛大集会欢迎印度总统  胡志明主席和范文同总理举行了欢迎宴会

1959-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    进一步加强越印两国人民的深厚友谊
    河内盛大集会欢迎印度总统
    胡志明主席和范文同总理举行了欢迎宴会
    新华社河内23日电　河内二十万人民今天下午在八月革命圣地巴亭广场举行盛大集会，欢迎印度总统普拉沙德访问越南民主共和国。
    普拉沙德总统在群众经久的欢呼声中发表了讲话。他说，我觉得我现在像生活在一个可尊敬的民族当中，它的悠久历史和近来的复兴完全可以使你们感到自豪。
    普拉沙德总统说：我们非常关心和平，深刻地感到各民族之间的和平相处是必要的。他说，我可以向你们保证，我们两国之间会有最友好的关系和最美好的往来。我为你们的改善人民生活和使国家进步的计划的实现，向你们致以最美好的祝贺。
    普拉沙德总统最后对越南人民的热情接待表示感谢。他最后说：“我们两国的友谊必将永远巩固和发展。”
    随后，胡志明主席发表简短的讲话说，两天来，越南人民对普拉沙德总统的热诚欢迎，充分表现了越印两国人民之间深厚的友谊。正如普拉沙德总统所说，我们两国的友谊必将永远巩固和发展。胡志明主席接着带领全场高呼“越印友谊万岁！世界和平万岁！”
    新华社河内23日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明22日晚上在主席府为印度总统普拉沙德举行国宴。
    出席宴会的有越南民主共和国总理范文同，副总理长征、范雄、潘继遂，国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜，祖国战线中央委员会主席团委员裴?，越南人民军副总参谋长黄文泰少将，各党派和各人民团体的代表，河内行政委员会主席陈维兴，以及驻越南的外交使团的代表和越南国际委员会主席安萨里。
    胡志明主席在宴会上致词说，在“潘查希拉”的基础上，越南民主共和国同印度共和国的友谊正在日益发展。
    胡志明主席说，越南人民希望日内瓦协议在印度支那能够得到彻底履行。统一越南是越南人民最迫切的愿望，也是对东南亚和世界和平的保障。
    普拉沙德总统在宴会上致词说，在目前的时代，亚非国家正成为一支日益强大的力量，一支坚决争取解放自己以摆脱外国枷锁和克服本身弱点的力量。目前已经出现和平一定能够得到维护的日益明显的迹象，印度人民决心走和平的道路，竭力为和平服务。
    据新华社河内23日电　范文同总理今晚为印度总统普拉沙德访问越南在河内“迎宾馆”举行盛大宴会。胡志明主席出席了宴会，出席宴会的还有副总理长征、潘继遂，政府各部部长、国会常务委员会副委员长孙光阀，各国驻越南外交使节和国际委员会的代表等。
