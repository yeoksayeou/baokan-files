Title: End of Exhibition in Cambodia; Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-03-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, March 6th: The delegation of the Chinese Construction Exhibition, led by acting head Gu Fusheng, left Phnom Penh for home today. The delegation arrived in Cambodia last December to hold the Chinese Construction Exhibition jointly arranged by China and Cambodia. During the exhibition, more than one million people from all over Cambodia visited the exhibition.

### 结束在柬埔寨展览  我展览会代表团回国

1959-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在柬埔寨展览
    我展览会代表团回国
    据新华社金边6日电　中国建设展览会代表团今天在代理团长顾福生率领下，离金边回国。代表团在去年12月抵达柬埔寨，在那里举办了由中柬两国共同安排的中国建设展览会。在展出期间柬埔寨全国各地一百多万人参观了展览会。
