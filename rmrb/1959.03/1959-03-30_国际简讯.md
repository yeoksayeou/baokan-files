International Briefs

    German Democratic Republic Again Decides to Increase Workers' Wages
    Berlin Dispatch: Following the decision in February to increase the wages of miners, chemical industry workers, teachers, and educators, the Presidium of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic made another decision on March 26 to increase the wages of more than 275,000 workers in other production and enterprise departments, effective April 1.

    Celebrations of Teachers' Day in Various Places in Czechoslovakia
    Prague Dispatch: Various places in Czechoslovakia celebrated Teachers' Day on the 28th.
    A celebration meeting was held in Prague. The Minister of Education and Culture of Czechoslovakia, Kahuda, spoke at the meeting. He praised the work achievements of teachers on behalf of the government.

    "Spartak" Men's Volleyball Team Leaves Beijing for Korea
    After visiting China for more than three weeks, the "Spartak" Men's Volleyball Team from Brno, Czechoslovakia, left Beijing on the 29th to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 国际简讯

1959-03-30
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    德意志民主共和国再次决定提高职工工资
    柏林讯　继2月间决定提高矿工、化学工业职工以及教师和教育工作者的工资之后，德意志民主共和国部长会议主席团3月26日又作出决定，自4月1日起提高另外一些生产和企业部门的二十七万五千多名职工的工资。
    捷各地庆祝教师节
    布拉格讯　捷克斯洛伐克各地28日庆祝教师节。
    布拉格举行了庆祝大会，捷克斯洛伐克教育和文化部长卡胡达在大会上讲了话。他代表政府嘉奖教师的工作成绩。
    “斯巴达克”男子排球队 离京去朝鲜
    捷克斯洛伐克布尔诺市“斯巴达克”男子排球队在中国访问了三个多星期以后，已经在29日离开北京去朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问。
