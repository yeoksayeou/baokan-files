Title: China and Germany Sign Trade Contract at Leipzig Fair; Germany to Supply China with Equipment for Power Stations, etc.
Author:
Date: 1959-03-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 11th: The German Democratic Republic will supply China with complete sets of equipment for thirty-two power stations and one set of turbine units from 1959 to 1962. This trade contract was signed on the 9th at the Leipzig Fair. In addition, an agreement for the German Democratic Republic to supply China with equipment for two cement plants was also signed at the same time.

### 中德在来比锡博览会上签订贸易合同  德将供应我电站等项设备

1959-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    中德在来比锡博览会上签订贸易合同
    德将供应我电站等项设备
    新华社柏林11日电　德意志民主共和国从1959年到1962年将供应中国三十二个电站的全套设备和一套透平机组。这一贸易合同9日在来比锡博览会上签字。另外德意志民主共和国供应中国两个水泥厂的设备的协议书也同时签字。
