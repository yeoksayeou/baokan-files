Title: Wu Lengxi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, Hosts a Banquet for a Group of Japanese Journalists
Author:
Date: 1959-03-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th: Wu Lengxi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, hosted a banquet tonight for eleven Japanese newspaper, news agency, and radio station reporters who accompanied the Japanese Socialist Party delegation on their visit to China.

Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, and representatives from the capital's press circles, including Zhou You, Gao Tian, and Zhang Fan, attended the banquet.

### 我记协副会长吴冷西  宴请一批日本记者

1959-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我记协副会长吴冷西
    宴请一批日本记者
    新华社16日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长吴冷西今晚设宴招待随同日本社会党访华代表团来我国访问的日本报纸、通讯社和广播电台记者十一人。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长王芸生和首都新闻界代表周游、高天、张帆等出席了宴会。
