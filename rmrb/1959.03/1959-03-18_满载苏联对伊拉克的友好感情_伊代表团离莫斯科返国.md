Title: Filled with the Soviet Union's Friendly Feelings Towards Iraq: Iraqi Delegation Leaves Moscow for Home
Author:
Date: 1959-03-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 17th: According to TASS, the government delegation of the Republic of Iraq left Moscow by plane today to return home.

The Iraqi delegation conducted a three-week visit to the Soviet Union. During the visit, they held negotiations with the Soviet government delegation and signed the Soviet-Iraqi Economic and Technical Cooperation Agreement.

At Vnukovo Airport, Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Kuzmin, and the head of the Iraqi Republic delegation, Minister of Economy Kubba, delivered speeches. In his speech, Kuzmin emphasized that the Soviet government leaders were very pleased to establish direct contact with prominent figures from the Republic of Iraq. He expressed the hope that such contact would continue to develop in the future. He said that the mutual understanding reached on economic and technical cooperation between the Soviet Union and the Republic of Iraq, and the signing of the agreement, were an important contribution to developing the interests of the people of both countries and developing broad friendly economic ties. The pleasant results of this negotiation were achieved because the relations between the two countries are based on equality, mutual respect for independence and sovereignty, and non-interference in internal affairs.

In his reply speech, Kubba said that we are returning home filled with friendly sentiments, which we have been able to feel every day in the Soviet Union. He said that the Iraqi people know that the people of all countries are supporting the Iraqi people, and the Soviet people are at the forefront. We are extremely pleased because our mission to the Soviet Union has been successfully completed, and the Soviet-Iraqi Economic and Technical Cooperation Agreement has been signed. We have no doubt that this agreement will become a solid foundation for enhancing friendly relations between our two countries and between all countries in the Near East and your country.

### 满载苏联对伊拉克的友好感情  伊代表团离莫斯科返国

1959-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    满载苏联对伊拉克的友好感情
    伊代表团离莫斯科返国
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：伊拉克共和国政府代表团今天乘飞机离开莫斯科返国。
    伊拉克代表团在苏联进行了为期三周的访问，在访问期间，同苏联政府代表团举行了谈判，并且签订了苏联和伊拉克经济和技术合作协定。
    在伏努科夫机场，苏联部长会议副主席库兹明和伊拉克共和国代表团团长、经济部长库巴都讲了话。库兹明在致词中着重指出，苏联政府领导人对于同伊拉克共和国著名人士建立直接接触感到非常高兴。他表示希望，这种接触在今后将得到不断发展。他说，苏联和伊拉克共和国在经济技术合作问题上所取得的相互谅解和签订的协定，是对发展两国人民的利益，发展广泛的友好经济联系的重要贡献。这次谈判所以取得了令人愉快的结果，是因为两国的关系是建立在平等、互相尊重独立和主权、互不干涉内政的基础上的。
    库巴在致答词中说，我们满载友好的盛情回国，这种友好感情是我们在苏联每天都可以感受到的。他说，伊拉克人民知道，各国人民都在支持伊拉克人民，而苏联人民是站在最前列的。我们感到万分高兴，因为我们访问苏联的使命已经胜利完成，并签订了苏联和伊拉克经济和技术合作协定。我们毫不怀疑，这个协定将会成为我们两国之间以及所有近东国家和你们的国家之间增进友好关系的巩固基础。
