Title: Ceylon Ministry of Industry and Fisheries Delegation Arrives in China for Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-03-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd: A four-member delegation from the Ceylon Ministry of Industry and Fisheries, headed by Ceylon's Minister of Industry and Fisheries, Mr. De Silva, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a friendly visit to China at the invitation of Minister of Light Industry, Li Zhuchen.

    Acting Minister of Light Industry, Deng Jie, and others welcomed the guests at the airport.

### 锡工业和渔业部代表团来我国进行访问

1959-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    锡工业和渔业部代表团来我国进行访问
    新华社22日讯　以锡兰工业和渔业部长德席尔瓦为首的锡兰工业和渔业部代表团一行四人，应轻工业部部长李烛尘的邀请来我国进行友好访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    轻工业部代理部长邓洁等到机场欢迎客人。
