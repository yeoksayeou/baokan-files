Title: Peng Dehuai Sends Telegram to Lieutenant General Dorj to Congratulate the 38th Anniversary of the Founding of the Mongolian People's Army
Author:
Date: 1959-03-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 17th: Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai sent a telegram today to Lieutenant General B. Dorj, Minister of Military and Public Security of the Mongolian People's Republic, congratulating the 38th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Army.

The congratulatory telegram stated: "On the occasion of the 38th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Army, I, on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, extend warm congratulations to you and, through you, to all officers and soldiers of the fraternal Mongolian People's Army."

The congratulatory telegram also "wished the Mongolian People's Army greater achievements in training and supporting national construction. May the fraternal friendship between the people and armies of China and Mongolia be further consolidated and developed."

### 祝贺蒙古人民军建军三十八周年  彭德怀电道尔吉中将

1959-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    祝贺蒙古人民军建军三十八周年
    彭德怀电道尔吉中将
    新华社17日讯　国防部长彭德怀元帅今天致电蒙古人民共和国军事公安部部长巴·道尔吉中将，祝贺蒙古人民军建军三十八周年。
    贺电说：“值此蒙古人民共和国人民军建军三十八周年之际，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体官兵，向您，并通过您向兄弟般的蒙古人民军全体官兵致以热烈的祝贺。”
    贺电还“祝蒙古人民军在训练和支援国家建设中取得更大的成就。祝中、蒙两国人民和军队间兄弟般的友谊日益巩固和发展。”
