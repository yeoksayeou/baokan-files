Title: Liaoning Province Launches Agricultural Scientific Research Based on the "Eight-Character Constitution" for Agriculture
Author:
Date: 1959-03-25
Page: 6

Based on the "Eight-Character Constitution" for Agriculture
Liaoning Province Launches Agricultural Scientific Research

(By our newspaper) Liaoning Province's agricultural scientific research departments will this year, based on the "Eight-Character Constitution" for agriculture, focus on large-scale high-yield and concentrate on the province's main crops to carry out agricultural scientific research.

This year's key research tasks are: completing the province-wide soil survey; investigating and studying the economic water use and rational irrigation techniques for major crops; stepping up the investigation and research of new fertilizer sources and methods for manufacturing soil fertilizer, as well as rational fertilization systems; vigorously propagating and promoting existing improved varieties, while selecting and breeding thirty to forty new varieties of various crops suitable for deep plowing, heavy fertilization, close planting, and irrigation conditions; strengthening the investigation and research of comprehensive cultivation techniques for water, fertilizer, soil, seed, and density for major crops in various regions of the province; and studying pest control methods after close planting.

### 根据农业“八字宪法”  辽宁省展开农业科学研究

1959-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

    根据农业“八字宪法”
    辽宁省展开农业科学研究
    本报讯　辽宁省农业科学研究部门今年依据农业“八字宪法”，以大面积丰产为纲，以省内主要作物为主，展开农业科学研究工作。
    今年重点研究任务是：完成全省土壤普查工作；调查研究各主要作物的经济用水和合理灌溉技术；加紧调查研究新的肥料来源和土化肥的制造方法，以及合理施肥制度等；大力繁殖推广现有良种，同时选育适于深耕、多肥、密植和灌溉条件下的各种作物新品种三十个至四十个；加强省内各地区各主要作物的水、肥、土、种、密的综合栽培技术的调查研究；研究密植后的害虫防治办法。
