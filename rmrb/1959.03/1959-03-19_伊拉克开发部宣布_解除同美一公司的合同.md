Title: Iraqi Development Ministry Announces Cancellation of Contract with American Company
Author:
Date: 1959-03-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 17th. The Iraqi Development Ministry announced on the 16th that it has decided to cancel the contract it signed with the Tippetts-Abbett-McCarthy-Stratton Company.

In a memorandum, the Development Ministry explained that the reasons for cancelling the contract included: the company's consulting fees were too high; the company's accounts were unclear; there were no accounts for the company's fixed assets; and insufficient attention was paid to economizing during the preparation of designs.

### 伊拉克开发部宣布  解除同美一公司的合同

1959-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克开发部宣布
    解除同美一公司的合同
    新华社巴格达17日电　伊拉克开发部16日宣布，它决定解除它同蒂普特斯·阿贝德·麦卡锡和斯特雷洪公司订立的合同。
    开发部在一项备忘录中解释解除这项合同的理由包括：这家公司的谘询费昂贵；公司的账目不清；公司的固定资产没有账目；在准备设计时不注意节约。
