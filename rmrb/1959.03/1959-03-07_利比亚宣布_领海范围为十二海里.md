Title: Libya Announces Territorial Sea Limit of Twelve Nautical Miles
Author:
Date: 1959-03-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tripoli, 6th - According to an announcement by Libyan authorities on the 4th, King Idris I of Libya has approved a new law stipulating that the territorial sea limit of Libya is twelve nautical miles.

### 利比亚宣布  领海范围为十二海里

1959-03-07
第5版()
专栏：

    利比亚宣布
    领海范围为十二海里
    新华社6日讯　的黎波里消息：据利比亚当局4日宣布，利比亚国王伊德里斯一世已经批准了一项关于规定利比亚的领海范围为十二海里的新法律。
