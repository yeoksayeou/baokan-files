Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Meets with Staff of Our Embassy in Hungary and Students Studying Abroad
Author:
Date: 1959-03-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 21st. Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, who were invited to participate in the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Hungarian Soviet Republic, met with all the staff of the Chinese Embassy in Hungary and Chinese students studying in Hungary at the Chinese Embassy in Hungary on the 21st.
    During the meeting, Vice Chairman Zhu De and Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun addressed everyone.

### 朱副主席会见我驻匈使馆人员和留学生

1959-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席会见我驻匈使馆人员和留学生
    新华社布达佩斯21日电　应邀前来参加匈牙利苏维埃共和国成立四十周年纪念的朱德副主席和国务院秘书长习仲勋，21日在中国驻匈牙利大使馆，会见了使馆的全体工作人员和在匈牙利学习的中国留学生。
    会见时，朱德副主席和习仲勋秘书长向大家讲了话。
