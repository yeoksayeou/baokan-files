Title: Omani Patriots Fiercely Attack Invading Troops
Author:
Date: 1959-03-12
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, March 9th: A statement released on March 9th by the Office of the Imam of Oman in Cairo stated that Omani nationalists recently blew up the Nakbah Road in Upper Awaliq, cutting off the land transportation of British troops. They also occupied the Askub Road, forcing British troops to rely solely on air transport for communication.

### 阿曼爱国者痛击侵略军

1959-03-12
第6版()
专栏：

    阿曼爱国者痛击侵略军
    据新华社9日电　阿曼教长驻开罗办事处9日发表声明说，阿曼民族主义者最近炸毁在上阿瓦莱克区的纳克巴公路，断绝了英国军队的陆路交通。他们还占领了阿斯库布公路，使英国军队只能依靠空运来联系。
