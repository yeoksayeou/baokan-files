Title: Further Strengthening Economic Cooperation Between the Two Countries: China and Romania Sign This Year's Trade Protocol
Author:
Date: 1959-03-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd: After friendly and cordial negotiations between the trade delegations of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Romanian People's Republic, a protocol on the exchange of goods and payments for the year 1959 was signed in Beijing today.

According to the protocol, the total value of goods exchanged between the two countries in 1959 will increase compared to last year. China will supply Romania with mineral products, soybeans, livestock products, tea, silk, and various daily necessities, etc.; Romania will supply China with drilling machines, blowers, power station equipment, cement equipment, tractors, tank cars, and petroleum products, etc.

Signing the protocol on the Chinese side was Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and on the Romanian side was Mihai Ciobanu, Head of the Government Trade Delegation of the Romanian People's Republic and President of the Romanian Chamber of Commerce.

### 进一步加强两国经济合作  中罗签订今年换货议定书

1959-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    进一步加强两国经济合作
    中罗签订今年换货议定书
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国政府和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府经过双方贸易代表团友好融洽的谈判，今天在北京签订了关于1959年度交换货物和付款的议定书。
    根据议定书规定，1959年两国交换货物的总额将比去年有所增长。中国将供应罗马尼亚矿产品、大豆、畜产品、茶叶、丝绸和各种日用品等；罗马尼亚将供应中国钻探机、鼓风机、电站设备、水泥设备、拖拉机、油罐车和石油产品等。
    在议定书上签字的，中国方面是对外贸易部副部长林海云，罗马尼亚方面是罗马尼亚人民共和国政府贸易代表团团长、罗马尼亚商会主席米哈伊·乔巴努。
