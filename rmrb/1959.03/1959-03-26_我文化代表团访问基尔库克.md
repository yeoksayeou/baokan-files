Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation Visits Kirkuk
Author:
Date: 1959-03-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, March 25th. The Chinese Cultural Delegation, headed by Burhan, left Baghdad by plane this morning for Kirkuk. The delegation will pay a visit to the northern region of Iraq for several days.

In the past week, the Chinese Cultural Delegation held talks with the Iraqi government on the conclusion of a cultural agreement between the two countries. In addition, the delegation also made sightseeing visits, including visits to some educational and health institutions, as well as the Daura Refinery, a government-operated refinery with an annual refining capacity of one million tons of crude oil.

The Chinese Cultural Delegation and the Cultural Delegation of the German Democratic Republic attended a reception held by the Iraqi Academy of Letters on the evening of the 22nd to welcome the two delegations.

### 我文化代表团访问基尔库克

1959-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团访问基尔库克
    新华社巴格达25日电　以包尔汉为首的中国文化代表团今天早晨乘飞机离开巴格达前往基尔库克。代表团将在伊拉克北部地区作为期数天的访问。
    在过去一星期中，中国文化代表团同伊拉克政府就缔结两国之间的文化协定举行了会谈。此外，代表团还进行了参观访问，其中包括参观了一些教育和卫生机构以及政府经营的每年能提炼一百万吨原油的道拉炼油厂。
    中国文化代表团和德意志民主共和国文化代表团22日晚上出席了伊拉克文学院为欢迎这两个代表团而举行的晚会。
