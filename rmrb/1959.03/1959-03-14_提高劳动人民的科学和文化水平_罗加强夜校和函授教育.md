Title: Raise the Scientific and Cultural Level of the Working People: Romania Strengthens Night Schools and Correspondence Education
Author: 
Date: 1959-03-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 12th—The newspapers here published on the 11th the resolution of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and the Council of Ministers regarding secondary and higher correspondence schools and night school education.

The resolution pointed out the achievements Romania has made in cultivating the younger generation. Currently, there are more than 2.3 million students in primary, secondary, and higher education institutions in Romania.

In order to expand the ranks of talent for socialist construction and to meet the urgent need of the working people to study, the resolution pointed out the necessity of reorganizing and adjusting correspondence schools and night school education. The purpose of night school and correspondence school education is to ensure the cultivation of more and more workers, technicians, engineers, and other cadres with high technical, cultural, and scientific levels from among the ranks of working people directly engaged in production.

### 提高劳动人民的科学和文化水平  罗加强夜校和函授教育

1959-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    提高劳动人民的科学和文化水平
    罗加强夜校和函授教育
    新华社布加勒斯特12日电　这里的报纸在11日刊登了罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会和部长会议关于中等和高等函授学校和夜校教育的决议。
    决议指出了罗马尼亚在培养青年一代方面所取得的成绩。目前，罗马尼亚中小学校和高等学校共有二百三十多万学生。
    为了扩大社会主义建设人才的队伍和满足劳动人民要求学习的迫切需要，决议指出有必要对函授学校和夜校教育进行改组和调整。夜校和函授学校教育的宗旨是，保证在直接从事生产的劳动人民队伍中间培养出越来越多的、有高度技术和文化科学水平的工人、技师和工程师等干部。
