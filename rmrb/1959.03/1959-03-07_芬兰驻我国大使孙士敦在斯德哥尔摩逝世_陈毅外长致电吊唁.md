Title: Finnish Ambassador to China, Sundström, Passes Away in Stockholm; Foreign Minister Chen Yi Sends Condolence Telegram
Author:
Date: 1959-03-07
Page: 4

    Finnish Ambassador to China, Sundström, Passes Away in Stockholm
    Foreign Minister Chen Yi Sends Condolence Telegram
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th March: Finnish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China, Mr. Sundström, passed away in Stockholm, Sweden on March 5th due to a serious illness.
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th March: Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, today sent a telegram to the Finnish Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Ralf Törngren, expressing condolences on the death of the Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Sundström. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Mr. Ralf Törngren, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Finland:
    I am deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of His Excellency Mr. Sundström, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Finland to the People's Republic of China, in Stockholm. I extend my deepest condolences and kindly request that you convey my condolences and sympathies to Ambassador Sundström's family.
                              Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
                                          March 6, 1959

### 芬兰驻我国大使孙士敦在斯德哥尔摩逝世  陈毅外长致电吊唁

1959-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰驻我国大使孙士敦在斯德哥尔摩逝世
    陈毅外长致电吊唁
    新华社6日讯　芬兰共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使孙士敦因患重病不治，于3月5日在瑞典斯德哥尔摩逝世。
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天致电芬兰外交部长拉尔夫·陶格伦，吊唁芬兰驻中国大使孙士敦逝世。电文如下：芬兰共和国外交部长拉尔夫·陶格伦先生阁下：
    惊悉芬兰共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使孙士敦阁下在斯德哥尔摩不幸逝世，谨致深切的哀悼，并请阁下向孙士敦大使的亲属转致哀悼和慰问。
                              中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
                                          一九五九年三月六日
