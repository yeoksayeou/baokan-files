Title: Danish Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception to Celebrate the Danish King's Birthday and Danish National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-03-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 11th. Danish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Andersen, held a reception tonight to celebrate the birthday of King Frederick IX of Denmark and Danish National Day.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, social figures from all aspects of the capital, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were invited to attend the reception.

In his toast, Vice Premier Chen Yi proposed a toast to the health of the King of the Kingdom of Denmark, the prosperity of the Kingdom of Denmark and the happiness of its people, and to world peace.

Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Andersen expressed his gratitude to the guests attending the reception, and he proposed a toast to the happiness and well-being of the Chinese people and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong.

### 庆祝丹麦国王寿辰和丹麦国庆  丹驻华代办举行招待会

1959-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝丹麦国王寿辰和丹麦国庆
    丹驻华代办举行招待会
    新华社11日讯　丹麦驻中国大使馆临时代办安徒生今晚举行招待会，庆祝丹麦国王斐德烈九世寿辰和丹麦国庆。
    陈毅副总理、沈钧儒副委员长、最高人民法院院长董必武、外交部副部长罗贵波，首都各方面的社会人士，各国驻中国使节应邀出席了招待会。
    陈毅副总理在祝酒中，提议为丹麦王国国王的健康、丹麦王国繁荣和人民幸福，为世界和平干杯。
    安徒生临时代办向出席招待会的客人们表示感谢，他建议为中国人民的幸福和康乐，为毛泽东主席的健康干杯。
