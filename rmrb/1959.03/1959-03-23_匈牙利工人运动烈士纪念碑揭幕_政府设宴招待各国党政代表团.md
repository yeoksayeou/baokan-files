Title: Monument to Hungarian Workers' Movement Martyrs Unveiled; Government Hosts Banquet for Party and Government Delegations from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-03-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 21st: On the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Hungarian Soviet Republic, residents of Budapest solemnly held the unveiling ceremony of the Monument to the Martyrs of the Hungarian Workers' Movement at the Kerepesi Cemetery this afternoon.

Hungarian Party and state leaders such as Kadar, Münnich, and Dobi attended the unveiling ceremony. Party and government delegations from various countries, invited to participate in the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Hungarian Soviet Republic, also came to pay their respects to the revolutionary martyrs of Hungary.

Kish Károly, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, spoke at the unveiling ceremony. He called on people to inherit the revolutionary traditions of the martyrs, learn from their noble patriotism and internationalism, and accelerate the construction of socialism with practical actions.

After the unveiling of the monument, Party and state leaders laid wreaths at the monument to the martyrs.

Party and government delegations from various countries and representatives from Hungarian social organizations and people from all walks of life in the capital also laid wreaths successively.

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 21st: The Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic held a grand reception in the Parliament Building tonight, hosting Party and government delegations from the Soviet Union, China, and other fraternal countries who came to participate in the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Hungarian Soviet Republic, as well as veteran revolutionaries from Hungary and abroad.

Hungarian Party and state leaders Kadar, Münnich, Dobi, and others attended the reception.

Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, spoke at the reception. He elaborated on the great international and historical significance of the Hungarian Soviet Republic.

### 匈牙利工人运动烈士纪念碑揭幕  政府设宴招待各国党政代表团

1959-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利工人运动烈士纪念碑揭幕
    政府设宴招待各国党政代表团
    新华社布达佩斯21日电　在纪念匈牙利苏维埃共和国成立四十周年之际，布达佩斯居民今天下午在盖莱佩斯公墓隆重地举行了匈牙利工人运动烈士纪念碑揭幕仪式。
    匈牙利党和国家领导人卡达尔、明尼赫、道比等都出席了揭幕仪式。应邀前来参加匈牙利苏维埃共和国成立四十周年纪念的各国党政代表团也前来向匈牙利的革命先烈致敬。
    匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员基什·卡罗伊在揭幕仪式上讲了话。他号召继承烈士们的革命传统，学习他们的崇高的爱国主义和国际主义精神，以实际行动来加速社会主义的建设。
    纪念碑揭幕后，党和国家的领导人向烈士纪念碑献了花圈。
    各国党政代表团和匈牙利各社会团体、首都各界人民的代表也先后献了花圈。
    新华社布达佩斯21日电　匈牙利人民共和国主席团今天晚上在国会大厦举行了盛大的招待会，招待了前来参加匈牙利苏维埃共和国成立四十周年纪念活动的苏联、中国和其他兄弟国家的党政代表团，以及匈牙利和外国的老革命战士们。
    匈牙利党和国家领导人卡达尔、明尼赫、道比等出席了招待会。
    匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比在招待会上讲了话。他阐述了匈牙利苏维埃共和国的伟大的国际意义和历史意义。
