Title: Lanzhou Chemical Plant Partially Put Into Production
Author:
Date: 1959-03-21
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 20th March: The first phase of the synthetic ammonia project of the fertilizer section of the Lanzhou Chemical Plant has been completed ahead of schedule and put into production on the 16th. This large-scale project took only one and a half years from construction to completion, shortening the original construction period by one-half, and the overall quality of the project was rated as excellent.

On the 16th, the factory workers held a grand commencement ceremony in Xigu District, Lanzhou. Wu Liangping, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Chemical Industry, A.I. Kuznetsov, representative of the Economic Cooperation Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, I.G. Naumov, Director of the Soviet State Chemical Committee's Technical Bureau of Experts in China, and party and government leaders of Gansu Province and Lanzhou City all came to attend the ceremony. Wu Liangping praised the workers for their selfless labor and expressed his gratitude to the Soviet experts who helped build the factory, as well as to the support from all provinces and cities across the country. A.I. Kuznetsov spoke at the meeting, congratulating the handover of this phase of the project for production. Jiao Shanmin, Director of the State Acceptance Committee and Secretary of the Gansu Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Deng Baoshan, Governor of Gansu Province, Yang Zheng Guo, Secretary of the Party Committee of the Third Engineering Bureau, Guo Yimin, Secretary of the Party Committee of Lanzhou Chemical Plant, Soviet experts, and worker representatives all spoke at the meeting.

### 兰州化工厂部分投入生产

1959-03-21
第2版()
专栏：

    兰州化工厂部分投入生产
    据新华社兰州20日电　兰州化工厂肥料部分第一期合成氨工程提前建成，16日投入生产。这期规模很大的工程，从施工到建成只用了一年半的时间，比原定工期缩短了二分之一，工程质量总评为优良。
    16日，建厂职工在兰州西固区举行了隆重的开工生产典礼大会。化学工业部副部长吴亮平，苏联驻我国大使馆经济合作参赞代表阿·依·库兹涅佐夫，苏联国家化学委员会驻中国专家技术局局长伊·格·纳乌莫夫，以及甘肃省、兰州市的党政领导人，都赶来参加了典礼大会。吴亮平对职工的忘我劳动表示赞扬，对帮助建厂的苏联专家表示感谢，对全国各省市的支援表示感谢。阿·依·库兹涅佐夫在会上讲话，祝贺这期工程的移交生产。国家验收委员会主任、中共甘肃省委书记焦善民，甘肃省省长邓宝珊，第三工程局党委书记杨振锅，兰州化工厂党委书记郭宜民，苏联专家，以及工人代表等，都在会上讲了话。
