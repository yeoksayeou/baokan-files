Title: Burmese Military Attaché to China Holds Reception Celebrating the 14th Anniversary of the Founding of the Burmese Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1959-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: Burmese Military Attaché to China, Colonel Liu Dang, held a reception this evening to celebrate the 14th anniversary of the founding of the Burmese Armed Forces.

General Yang Chengwu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, was invited to attend the reception. He extended warm congratulations to the Burmese Armed Forces on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. General Yang Chengwu said that he firmly believes that, while adhering to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, the friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Burma will surely develop day by day.

Colonel Liu Dang delivered a speech at the reception. After outlining the 14-year history of the Burmese Armed Forces, he said that Burma and China have always been two of the most harmonious neighbors. Especially after each gained independence, the two countries have maintained and adhered to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, which has further enhanced the close friendship between the armed forces of the two countries.

Also invited to attend the reception this evening were General Peng Shaohui, General Li Jukui, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice Admiral Luo Shun Chu, Lieutenant General Wu Faxian of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Wang Zheng, Lieutenant General Liu Shao Wen, and other senior generals and officers from various services and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei also attended the reception.

Burmese Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Wu Ba Mao, as well as diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries to China also attended the reception this evening.

### 庆祝缅甸国防军建军十四周年  缅甸驻华武官举行招待会

1959-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝缅甸国防军建军十四周年
    缅甸驻华武官举行招待会
    新华社27日讯　缅甸联邦驻我国武官柳当上校今晚举行招待会，庆祝缅甸国防军建军十四周年。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长杨成武上将应邀出席了招待会。他代表中国人民解放军全体官兵向缅甸联邦国防军致以热烈的祝贺。杨成武上将说，他深信在坚持和平共处五项原则的情况下，中缅两国人民和军队的友谊必将日益发展。
    柳当上校在会上首先致词。他概述了缅甸国防军十四年的建军历史后说，缅甸和中国一直是两个最和睦的邻邦。特别在各自获得独立以后，两国保持并遵守了和平共处的五项原则，这样，就更加增进了两国武装部队之间的亲密友谊。
    应邀出席今晚招待会的，有彭绍辉上将、李聚奎上将、萧向荣中将、罗舜初海军中将、吴法宪空军中将、王诤中将、刘少文中将等中国人民解放军各军种各兵种的高级将领和军官。出席招待会的，还有外交部副部长姬鹏飞。
    缅甸驻华临时代办吴巴茂、各国驻华使节和武官也出席了今晚的招待会。
