Author: Wang Zhishun, Yuan Hong
Date: 1959-03-27
Page: 2

Song of the Electric Furnace Workers

　　Electric furnace workers are heroic men,
　　Steel flowers splash and arcs gleam.
　　Raising the steel ladle to make a waterfall,
　　It seems a rainbow has fallen into the workshop.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Wang Zhishun)
　　Ten thousand miles of cloudless blue sky,
　　A mighty army surges forward for high yields;
　　Let the test samples flow like water,
　　Test and check them as they come, seizing the time.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Yuan Hong)

### 电炉工人之歌
王志顺  袁洪
1959-03-27
第2版()
专栏：

    电炉工人之歌电炉工人英雄汉，钢花飞溅弧光闪。举起钢包造瀑布，疑是长虹落车间。
            （王志顺）万里无云好青天，千军万马闹高产；任凭试料如流水，随到随检抢时间。
              （袁洪）
