Title: Khrushchev to Visit Poland
Author:
Date: 1959-03-06
Page: 4

    Khrushchev to Visit Poland
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th - TASS reported from Moscow on the 4th: The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Khrushchev, accepted the invitation from the leaders of the People's Republic of Poland to pay a friendly visit to Poland with a Soviet party and government delegation in July this year.
  The Soviet party and government delegation will attend the celebrations of the 15th anniversary of the founding of People's Poland.

### 赫鲁晓夫将访问波兰

1959-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫将访问波兰
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科4日讯：苏共中央第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫接受了波兰人民共和国领导人的邀请，将在今年7月参加苏联党和政府代表团到波兰作友好访问。
  苏联党和政府代表团将参加人民波兰建国十五周年的庆祝典礼。
