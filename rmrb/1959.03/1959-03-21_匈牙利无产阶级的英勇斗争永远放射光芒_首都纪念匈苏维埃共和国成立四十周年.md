Title: The Heroic Struggle of the Hungarian Proletariat Will Forever Shine: The Capital Commemorates the 40th Anniversary of the Hungarian Soviet Republic
Author: 
Date: 1959-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: On the eve of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Hungarian Soviet Republic, more than 1,000 people from all walks of life in the capital held a grand gathering in the CPPCC Auditorium to solemnly commemorate this historically significant festival.

On March 21, 1919, following the victory of the Russian October Revolution, the Hungarian proletariat established the world's second proletarian state—the Hungarian Soviet Republic. Although it existed for only 133 days before being brutally strangled by imperialist reactionaries, the heroic struggle of the Hungarian proletariat will forever shine upon humanity. Today, communism is surging across the world with overwhelming force and thunderous power. This magnificent scene aroused boundless joy among the people from all walks of life in the capital who attended the commemorative meeting. At 7:00 p.m. tonight, Dong Biwu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and responsible comrades from relevant departments, including Zhang Xiruo, Liu Ningyi, Qian Junrui, Zeng Yongquan, Zeng Yifan, Qu Wu, Deng Tuo, Feng Jiping, and Zhang Xiuzhu, accompanied the Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nográdi, and the Soviet Embassy's Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, Antonov, onto the rostrum. At this moment, thunderous applause echoed through the hall for a long time.

The commemorative meeting was presided over by Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Dong Biwu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, first spoke at the meeting (full text on page 5). He said: Forty years ago, influenced by the victory of the great October Socialist Revolution in Russia, on March 21, 1919, the Hungarian proletariat established the world's second proletarian state, which is the glory of the Hungarian proletariat and working people.

Dong Biwu said: Following the great October Socialist Revolution, news of the Hungarian proletarian revolutionary struggle also spread to China, arousing the ardent sympathy and cheers of the Chinese people and bringing new encouragement to the Chinese revolutionary movement.

Dong Biwu said: The people of China and Hungary have long forged a profound fighting friendship in their common revolutionary struggles. Now, the friendship that has long existed between the people of our two countries continues to develop and deepen. In the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, the Chinese and Hungarian peoples stand shoulder to shoulder against the common enemy and advance hand in hand on the socialist road.

Comrade Dong Biwu wished the Hungarian people, under the correct leadership of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, to continue to carry forward their glorious revolutionary traditions and continuously achieve brilliant successes in the struggle for the complete victory of socialism and communism.

Hungarian Ambassador to China Nográdi then spoke (full text on page 5). He said: The Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the entire Hungarian people attach particular importance to this anniversary, because in celebrating this festival, we are also celebrating a new, huge, and historically significant victory for our common ideology—Marxism-Leninism.

Ambassador Nográdi narrated the history and great significance of the founding of the Hungarian Soviet Republic. Then, he described how the Hungarian people bravely waged a long struggle against imperialism and achieved a great victory, saying that we have advanced several centuries in these forty years, but this speed is only the beginning for us. Taking the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China as examples, we will advance even more rapidly. The great goal of the final victory of communism has already appeared before our eyes. We will enter this society together, shoulder to shoulder.

The speeches of Comrade Dong Biwu and Ambassador Nográdi were warmly welcomed by the audience.

The commemorative meeting began with the national anthems of China and Hungary and ended with the Internationale.

In the lounge of the venue, historical pictures of the Hungarian Soviet Republic and propaganda posters from the period of the Hungarian Soviet Republic were exhibited.

### 匈牙利无产阶级的英勇斗争永远放射光芒  首都纪念匈苏维埃共和国成立四十周年

1959-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利无产阶级的英勇斗争永远放射光芒
    首都纪念匈苏维埃共和国成立四十周年
    据新华社20日讯　在匈牙利苏维埃共和国成立四十周年的前夕，首都各界人民一千多人在政协礼堂举行盛大集会，隆重地纪念这个有重大意义的历史节日。
    1919年3月21日，匈牙利无产阶级继俄国十月革命胜利之后，建立了世界上第二个无产阶级国家——匈牙利苏维埃共和国，虽然它只存在了一百三十三天就被帝国主义反动派残暴地扼杀了，但是，匈牙利无产阶级的英勇斗争却永远向人类放射光芒。今天，共产主义正以排山倒海之势，雷霆万钧之力，磅礴于全世界。这一美妙的景象，激起了参加纪念大会的首都各界人民的无限欢乐。今天晚间七时，中共中央政治局委员董必武和各有关部门的负责人张奚若、刘宁一、钱俊瑞、曾涌泉、曾一凡、屈武、邓拓、冯基平、张修竹等，陪同匈牙利驻中国大使诺格拉第、苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办安东诺夫登上主席台。这时候，春雷般的掌声长久地震荡着会场。
    纪念大会由对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若主持。中共中央政治局委员董必武首先在大会上讲话（全文见第五版）。他说：四十年前，在俄国伟大的十月社会主义革命的胜利的影响下，1919年3月21日，匈牙利的无产阶级建立了世界上第二个无产阶级国家，这是匈牙利无产阶级和劳动人民的光荣。
    董必武说：继伟大的十月社会主义革命之后，匈牙利无产阶级革命斗争的消息也传到了中国，引起了中国人民的热烈同情和欢呼，给中国的革命运动带来了新的鼓舞。
    董必武说：中匈两国人民早就在共同的革命斗争中结成了深厚的战斗友谊。现在，我们两国人民之间早已存在的友谊不断发展，日益加深。在以苏联为首的社会主义阵营里，中国人民和匈牙利人民肩并肩地反对着共同的敌人，手携手地在社会主义道路上大踏步前进。
    董必武同志祝匈牙利人民在社会主义工人党的正确领导下，继续发扬他们的光荣革命传统，在争取社会主义和共产主义彻底胜利的斗争中，不断地取得辉煌成就。
    匈牙利驻中国大使诺格拉第接着讲话（全文见第五版）。他说：匈牙利社会主义工人党和全体匈牙利人民特别重视这个纪念日，因为我们庆祝这个节日，同时也是庆祝我们的共同思想——马克思列宁主义获得新的巨大的具有历史意义的胜利。
    诺格拉第大使叙述了匈牙利苏维埃共和国成立的历史和巨大意义。接着，他叙述了匈牙利人民如何英勇地与帝国主义进行了长期斗争，并取得了伟大的胜利以后说，我们在这四十年当中前进了几个世纪，但这种速度对我们来说不过只是开始。以苏联和中华人民共和国作为榜样，我们将要前进得更为迅速。共产主义最后胜利的伟大目标已经出现在我们的眼前了。我们将在一起肩并肩地进入这个社会。
    董必武同志和诺格拉第大使的讲话受到了全场的热烈欢迎。
    纪念大会在匈中两国的国歌声中开始，在国际歌声中结束。
    在会场的休息室里，展出了匈牙利苏维埃共和国的历史图片和匈牙利苏维埃共和国时期的宣传画。
    今天的纪念大会，是由对外文化联络委员会、中匈友好协会、中华全国总工会、中共北京市委员会联合举行的。各兄弟国家驻中国的使节和外交官员也出席了大会。
    纪念大会结束后，诺格拉第大使举行了酒会。最后，放映了匈牙利苏维埃共和国的文献纪录片。（附图片）
    董必武同志在首都各界纪念匈牙利苏维埃共和国成立四十周年大会上讲话，主席台右起第七人是诺格拉第大使。
    新华社记者　牛畏予摄
