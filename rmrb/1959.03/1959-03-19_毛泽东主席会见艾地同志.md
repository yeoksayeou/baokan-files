Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Meets Comrade Aidit
Author:
Date: 1959-03-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 18th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China met with Aidit, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Indonesia, on the evening of March 18th and had a cordial and friendly conversation.
    Present at the meeting were Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the CPC, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the CPC.
    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 18th: Aidit, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Indonesia, accompanied by Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the CPC, arrived in Wuhan by special plane from Beijing this afternoon.
    Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the CPC, and Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the CPC, were at the airport to welcome him.
    In the afternoon, Comrade Aidit visited the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge and the Wuhan Heavy Machinery Plant. (Attached photo)

### 毛泽东主席会见艾地同志

1959-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席会见艾地同志
    新华社武汉18日电　中共中央主席毛泽东于3月18日晚上会见了印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地，进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    会见时在座的有中共湖北省委员会第一书记王任重、中共中央委员刘宁一。
    新华社武汉18日电　印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地，在中共中央委员刘宁一陪同下，今天下午乘专机从北京到达武汉。
    到机场欢迎的有中共湖北省委员会第一书记王任重、中共武汉市委员会第一书记宋侃夫。
    下午，艾地同志参观了武汉长江大桥和武汉重型机床厂。（附图片）
