Title: Ploughing Girl Walks in the Clouds
Author: Mi Gu
Date: 1959-03-08
Page: 8

    Poetic Imagery

    Ploughing Girl Walks in the Clouds
    Shanxi Folk Song
    Mi Gu's painting: Terraces bend and glisten with silver light, resembling white clouds circling the mountains. A ploughing girl walks in the clouds, like the Weaver Girl meeting the Cowherd. (Attached image)

### 犁地姑娘云中走
米谷
1959-03-08
第8版()
专栏：诗情画意

    犁地姑娘云中走
    山西民歌
    米谷画梯田弯弯闪银光，好似白云绕山岗，犁地姑娘云中走，如同织女赶牛郎。（附图片）
