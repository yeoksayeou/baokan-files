Title: Chinese Red Cross Donates Rice to Indonesian Disaster Victims; Ambassador Huang Zhen Visits Minister Zojomartono to Hand Over Bill of Lading
Author:
Date: 1959-03-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 4th. Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, visited Indonesian Minister of Social Affairs, Mulyadi Zojomartono, this morning to hand over the bill of lading for one hundred tons of rice donated by the Chinese Red Cross to the Indonesian Red Cross.

The Chinese Red Cross is donating this batch of rice to express the Chinese people's concern and sympathy for the victims of the Indonesian floods.

The Indonesian Minister of Social Affairs said that he would immediately hand over the rice to the Indonesian Red Cross for processing. He also expressed his gratitude to the Chinese people. Also present was Perenka Hu, Director of the Department of Foreign Relations of the Indonesian Ministry of Social Affairs, who is also the Vice President of the Indonesian Red Cross.

### 我红十字会赠大米给印度尼西亚灾民  黄镇大使访佐约马托诺部长转交提单

1959-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    我红十字会赠大米给印度尼西亚灾民
    黄镇大使访佐约马托诺部长转交提单
    新华社雅加达4日电　中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇今天上午访问了印度尼西亚社会事务部长穆尔雅迪·佐约马托诺，向他转交了中国红十字会赠给印度尼西亚红十字会的一百吨大米的提单。
    中国红十字会赠送这批大米，表示中国人民对印度尼西亚水灾受害者的关怀和同情。
    印度尼西亚社会事务部长说，他将立即把这批大米交给印度尼西亚红十字会处理。他同时对中国人民表示感谢。在座的还有印度尼西亚社会事务部对外关系司司长佩伦卡胡，他同时是印度尼西亚红十字会副会长。
