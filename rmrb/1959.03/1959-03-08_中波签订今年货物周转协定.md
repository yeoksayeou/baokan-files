Title: China and Poland Sign This Year's Goods Turnover Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-03-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 6th. China and Poland signed the 1959 Goods Turnover and Payment Agreement in Warsaw on the 6th.

Representing the Polish side, the agreement was signed by the Polish Minister of Foreign Trade, Mr. Trompczynski. Representing the Chinese side, the agreement was signed by the Chinese Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Li Qiang. The Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Wang Bingnan, also attended the signing ceremony.

According to the provisions of the agreement, the trade volume between the two countries in 1959 will increase compared to 1958. The agreement stipulates that this year China will supply Poland with various non-ferrous metal raw materials, oils and fats, rice, raw materials for the textile and tanning industries, rubber, tea, fruit products, silk, and other commodities. Poland will supply China with complete sets of equipment, various machine tools, machines, locomotives, diesel engines, excavators, steel, and other items.

### 中波签订今年货物周转协定

1959-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    中波签订今年货物周转协定
    新华社华沙6日电　中国和波兰两国政府6日在华沙签订了1959年货物周转和付款协定。
    代表波兰方面在协定上签字的是波兰对外贸易部部长特隆姆普钦斯基，代表中国方面在协定上签字的是我国对外贸易部副部长李强。我国驻波兰大使王炳南也参加了签字仪式。
    根据协定的规定，1959年两国贸易额将比1958年有所增长。协定规定今年中国将供给波兰各种有色金属原料、油脂油料、大米、纺织工业和制革工业的原料、橡胶、茶叶、水果制品、丝绸以及其它商品。波兰将供给中国成套设备，各种机床、机器、机车、柴油机、挖土机和钢材等。
