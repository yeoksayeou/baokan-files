Title: Khrushchev to Visit Leipzig Fair at Invitation

Author:
Date: 1959-03-01
Page: 4

    Invited to Visit Spring Fair
    Khrushchev to Visit Leipzig
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th—According to TASS, Moscow: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, at the invitation of the government of the German Democratic Republic, will visit the Leipzig International Spring Fair, which is scheduled to open on March 1st.

### 应邀参观春季博览会  赫鲁晓夫将赴来比锡

1959-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参观春季博览会
    赫鲁晓夫将赴来比锡
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫，应德意志民主共和国政府的邀请，将前往参观订于3月1日开幕的来比锡春季国际博览会。
