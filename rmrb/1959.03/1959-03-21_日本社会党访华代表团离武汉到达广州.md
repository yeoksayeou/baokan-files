Title: Japanese Socialist Party Delegation Leaves Wuhan, Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-03-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 20th. The head of the Japanese Socialist Party delegation, Inejiro Asanuma, and some members of the delegation arrived in Guangzhou today from Wuhan by special plane.

　　Mayor of Guangzhou Zhu Guang, Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Du Guoxiang, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou and Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Sun Leyi, and others welcomed the guests at the airport.

　　In the afternoon, Sun Leyi accompanied the guests on a visit to the Xin'jiao People's Commune in the suburbs.

　　In the evening, Mayor Zhu Guang hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the members of the delegation and accompanying reporters who are about to leave China.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, March 20th. The head of the Japanese Socialist Party delegation, Inejiro Asanuma, and most of the delegation members, left Wuhan by plane today to visit Guangzhou. Liu Huinong, the Mayor of Wuhan, Sun Yaohua, the Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs in Wuhan, and Xiong Fei, the Vice Chairman of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, saw them off at the airport.

　　Delegation members Soji Okada and Toshio Tanaka will also leave Wuhan to return to Beijing.

### 日本社会党访华代表团离武汉到达广州

1959-03-21
第6版()
专栏：

    日本社会党访华代表团离武汉到达广州
    新华社广州20日电　日本社会党访华代表团团长浅沼稻次郎和代表团的部分团员，今天上午由武汉乘专机到达广州。
    广州市市长朱光，中国人民外交学会理事杜国庠，广州市副市长、中国人民外交学会理事孙乐宜等到机场欢迎客人。
    下午，孙乐宜陪同客人访问了市郊区的新滘人民公社。
    晚上，朱光市长设宴欢送即将离开中国的代表团人员和随团记者。
    新华社武汉20日电　日本社会党访华代表团团长浅沼稻次郎和大部分团员，今天乘飞机离开武汉前往广州访问。到机场送行的，有武汉市市长刘惠农、中国人民外交学会在武汉的理事孙耀华和政协武汉市委员会副主席熊飞。
    代表团团员冈田宗司、田中稔男，也将离武汉返北京。
