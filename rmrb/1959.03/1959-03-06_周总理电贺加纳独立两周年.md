Title: Premier Zhou Cables Congratulations on the Second Anniversary of Ghana's Independence
Author:
Date: 1959-03-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 5th: Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Ghanaian Prime Minister Kwame Nkrumah, congratulating him on the second anniversary of Ghana's independence. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency Kwame Nkrumah, Prime Minister of Ghana:
    On the occasion of Ghana's Independence Day, I, on behalf of the Government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and, through Your Excellency, to the Government and people of your country.
    I wish the Government and people of your country new achievements in safeguarding national independence, building their own country, and promoting friendship and solidarity among Asian and African countries, and I wish further development of the relations between China and Ghana in accordance with the Bandung spirit.
     Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, March 4, 1959, Beijing

### 周总理电贺加纳独立两周年

1959-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺加纳独立两周年
    新华社5日讯　周恩来总理致电加纳总理克瓦米·恩克鲁玛，祝贺加纳独立两周年。电文如下：加纳总理克瓦米·恩克鲁玛阁下：
    欣逢加纳独立纪念日，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民，向阁下并且通过阁下向贵国政府和人民表示热诚的祝贺。
    祝贵国政府和人民在维护民族独立、建设自己国家和促进亚非各国友好团结的事业中获得新的成就，祝中国和加纳之间的关系，根据万隆精神，获得进一步的发展。
     中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　1959年3月4日于北京
