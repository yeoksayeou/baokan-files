Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Leaves Moscow for Home

Author: 
Date: 1959-03-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 27th: Vice Chairman Zhu De, head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, and member Xi Zhongxun, who were invited to participate in the 40th anniversary celebrations of the Hungarian Soviet Republic, left Moscow on the morning of the 27th to return to Beijing.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Kirichenko, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, Kosygin, alternate member of the Presidium, Pospelov, and other party and government leaders.

Hungarian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Boldoczki, and Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, Zhang Weilie, also saw them off at the airport.

The plane will stop in Irkutsk overnight before flying to Beijing.

### 朱德副主席离莫斯科回国

1959-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席离莫斯科回国
    新华社莫斯科27日电　应邀参加匈牙利苏维埃共和国四十周年纪念活动的中国党政代表团团长朱德副主席、团员习仲勋，在27日上午离莫斯科返北京。
    到机场送行的有苏共中央主席团委员基里钦科、主席团候补委员柯西金、波斯别洛夫及其他党政领导人。
    匈牙利驻苏联大使波尔多茨基、中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办张伟烈也到机场送行。
    这架飞机将在伊尔库次克停留一夜，然后飞往北京。
