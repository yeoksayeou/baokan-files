Title: List of Names of the Preparatory Committee for the National Conference of Advanced Industrial Collectives and Advanced Producers
Author:
Date: 1959-03-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 25th — The CPC Central Committee and the State Council have decided to convene a national conference of advanced collectives and advanced producers in socialist construction in industry, transportation, basic construction, finance and trade in the fourth quarter of 1959. They have also designated the responsible persons of the relevant departments to form the preparatory committee for the conference, responsible for the preparatory work. The list of the chairman, vice-chairmen and all members of the preparatory committee is as follows:

Chairman: Li Fuchun

Vice-Chairmen: Li Xiannian, Bo Yibo, Li Xuefeng, Liu Ningyi, Zeng Shan, Ma Mingfang, Cai Chang, Hu Yaobang, Li Jiebo

Members: (Listed in order of the number of strokes in their surnames) Wang Jiong, Wang Zhen, Wang Zhijie, Wang Shoudao, Wang Heshou, Ye Jizhuang, Rong Zihe, Sun Zhiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Liu Qian, Liu Ningyi, Liu Changsheng, Liu Dachao, Liu Xiufeng, Liu Lanbo, Song Chuan, Song Renqiong, Song Shaowen, An Lifu, Sha Qianli, Du Yanqing, He Yingcai, Yu Qiuli, Li Siguang, Li Xiannian, Li Chunming, Li Xuefeng, Li Dengfeng, Li Fuchun, Li Jinghan, Li Jiebo, Li Zhulu, Shao Ziyan, Chen Shaomin, Fan Changjiang, Luo Qiong, Luo Yuchuan, Rong Gaotang, Yao Yilin, Zheng Tianxiang, Zheng Jinghua, Zhao Erlu, Zhao Guoqiang, Hu Yaobang, Li Zaiwen, Xi Zhanyuan, Ma Wenrui, Ma Mingfang, Kang Yonghe, Liang Buting, Xu Zhizhen, Xu Deheng, Huang Minwei, Cao Juru, Zeng Shan, Peng Tao, Cheng Zihua, Fu Zuoyi, Zhang Jing, Zhang Tianmin, Zhang Qingchun, Zhang Zhaomei, Zhang Xiuzhu, Zhang Weizhen, Zhang Jingfu, Zhang Linzhi, Yang Zhihua, Jia Tuofu, Jia Tingsan, Cai Chang, Gu Dachun, Jiang Guangnai, Liao Luyan, Teng Daiyuan, Bo Yibo, Han Guang
