Title: Tito Clique Pursues Great-Serbian Chauvinist National Policy: Albanians in Yugoslavia Live in Misery
Author:
Date: 1959-03-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 9th: The Albanian Telegraph Agency reports that the editorial department of the Albanian newspaper "People's Voice" recently published an article reporting on the tragic lives of Albanians living in Belgrade and Kosovo.

The article states that the situation of Albanians living in Belgrade and Kosovo is extremely miserable. This illustrates what the so-called Yugoslav "better solution of the national question than all other countries" and the "Marxist" policies of the Belgrade leaders really amount to.

The article says that Albanians in Belgrade do not have stable jobs, as a "New York Times" reporter put it: "The jobs that no one else wants to do are always done by Albanians."

"People's Voice" writes that the living conditions of Albanians in Belgrade are also very poor. They use the basements of buildings under construction as temporary housing. When a Belgrade family agrees to let them live in the basement, they consider it an unexpected stroke of luck. Some have to live in the caves of castles built in the Turkish era, and in the summer, they use benches in nearby parks as beds.

The article exposes the Great-Serbian chauvinist national policies pursued by the Tito clique in Kosovo, saying that the Titoists try every possible means to make Albanians living in Kosovo leave their hometowns, sending them to Turkey, and to remote cities in Yugoslavia, where their future is to be servants.

The article points out that Kosovo is a mirror that clearly reflects the true face of Yugoslav revisionists on the national question.

### 铁托集团奉行沙文主义民族政策  南斯拉夫的阿尔巴尼亚人处境悲惨

1959-03-10
第5版()
专栏：

    铁托集团奉行沙文主义民族政策
    南斯拉夫的阿尔巴尼亚人处境悲惨
    据新华社9日讯　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚“人民之声报”编辑部最近发表了一篇文章，报道居住在贝尔格莱德和科索沃的阿尔巴尼亚族人的悲惨生活。
    文章说，居住在贝尔格莱德和科索沃的阿尔巴尼亚人的处境是非常悲惨的。这说明了所谓南斯拉夫“比其他所有国家更好地解决了民族问题”和贝尔格莱德领导人的“马克思主义”政策究竟是什么东西。
    文章说，在贝尔格莱德的阿尔巴尼亚人没有固定的工作，正如“纽约时报”记者所说的：“谁也不愿干的工作，总是由阿尔巴尼亚人来干。”
      “人民之声报”写道，在贝尔格莱德的阿尔巴尼亚人的居住境况也是十分可怜的。他们把正在建造中的大厦的地下室当作临时住宅。当一家贝尔格莱德人同意让他们住在地下室里时，他们认为这是出乎意外的幸运。有的人只得住在土耳其时代修建的城堡的洞穴中，而在夏天，他们就把附近公园里的凳子当床。
    文章揭露铁托集团在科索沃奉行的沙文主义民族政策说，铁托分子想尽一切办法，使居住在科索沃的阿尔巴尼亚人离乡别井，把他们遣送到土耳其、遣送到南斯拉夫的遥远的城市去，而他们的前途是充当仆从。
    文章指出，科索沃是一面镜子，清楚地照出了南斯拉夫修正主义者在民族问题上真正的面貌。
