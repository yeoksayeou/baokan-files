Title: Khrushchev Arrives in the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1959-03-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 4th. Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Khrushchev, and his entourage arrived in the German Democratic Republic this morning aboard a Tu-104 aircraft. Khrushchev is visiting the Leipzig Spring International Fair at the invitation of the Government of the German Democratic Republic.

Accompanying Khrushchev was the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union, König.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included: Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl; First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Ulbricht; Chairman of the People's Chamber, Dieckmann; Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Bolz; leaders of democratic parties and social organizations; and generals of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic.

Also present at the airport to welcome him were: the leaders of the Soviet government delegation participating in the Leipzig Fair, Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Zasyadko; Soviet Ambassador Pervukhin; Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Forces in Germany, Army General Zakharov, and foreign diplomatic envoys. Khrushchev and Grotewohl exchanged greetings. Then, Khrushchev and his entourage traveled by car from the airport to Leipzig.

### 赫鲁晓夫到德意志民主共和国

1959-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫到德意志民主共和国
    新华社柏林4日电　苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员今天上午乘“图—一○四”式飞机到达德意志民主共和国。赫鲁晓夫是应德意志民主共和国政府的邀请，前来参观来比锡春季国际博览会的。
    陪同赫鲁晓夫前来的有德意志民主共和国驻苏大使柯尼希。
    到机场欢迎的有：德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥、德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希、人民议院主席狄克曼、副总理兼外交部长博尔茨、民主党派和社会团体领导人、德意志民主共和国国家人民军将领。
    前往机场欢迎的还有：参加来比锡博览会的苏联政府代表团领导人、苏联部长会议副主席扎夏迪科、苏联大使别尔乌辛、驻德苏军总司令扎哈罗夫陆军上将和各国外交使节。赫鲁晓夫和格罗提渥互相致词。接着，赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员从机场乘汽车前去来比锡。
