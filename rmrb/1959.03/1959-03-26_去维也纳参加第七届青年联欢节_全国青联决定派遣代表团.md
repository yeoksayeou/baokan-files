Title: National Youth Federation Decides to Send Delegation to Vienna for the 7th World Youth Festival
Author:
Date: 1959-03-26
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, March 25th. The All-China Youth Federation held its third meeting of the Standing Committee this afternoon and decided to send a delegation of three to four hundred people to participate in the 7th World Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship Festival.
    This festival will be held from July 26th to August 4th this year in Vienna, the capital of Austria, and is expected to be attended by 17,000 young people from all over the world. The All-China Youth Federation and relevant parties are actively coordinating the preparations for the Chinese youth delegation's participation in the festival.
    At today's meeting, Liu Xiyuan, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, also reported on the Asian-African Youth Conference held in Cairo in February this year. The committee members present unanimously expressed their support for the resolutions of the Asian-African Youth Conference.

### 去维也纳参加第七届青年联欢节  全国青联决定派遣代表团

1959-03-26
第6版()
专栏：

    去维也纳参加第七届青年联欢节
    全国青联决定派遣代表团
    新华社25日讯　中华全国青年联合会今天下午举行常务委员会第三次会议，决定派遣一个由三、四百人组成的代表团，参加第七届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节。
    这一届联欢节今年7月26日至8月4日在奥地利首都维也纳举行，预计将有来自世界各国的一万七千名青年参加。关于中国青年代表团参加联欢节的准备工作，已由全国青联和各有关方面积极配合进行。
    今天的会上，全国青联主席刘西元还就今年2月在开罗举行的亚非青年会议的情况作了报告，到会委员们一致表示支持亚非青年会议的决议。
