Title: International Association of Journalists Awards Prize to Glezos
Author:
Date: 1959-03-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The International Organization of Journalists (IOJ) held an award ceremony in Berlin on March 16th to present the 1958 International Journalism Prize to the renowned Greek patriot and former president of the Athens "Avghi" newspaper, Manolis Glezos. The IOJ awarded Glezos this prize in recognition of his outstanding contributions to the cause of defending peace and democracy.

At the award ceremony, IOJ Deputy Secretary-General Petrov presented the award on behalf of the IOJ Secretariat.

In his speech, Petrov said that the IOJ Secretariat will do everything in its power to fight for Glezos's freedom.

After the speech, Petrov handed the prize money and an honorary membership certificate from the IOJ to Papadhimitriou, the acting president of "Avghi" and a member of the Greek Parliament, who came to accept the award on Glezos' behalf.

At the award ceremony, a letter from Glezos' wife, Tasia Glezos, to the IOJ was also read. In her letter, she said that she could not come to receive the award because the Greek authorities would not give her a passport to travel abroad. She said that the cause of peace for all peoples is sacred, and Glezos resolutely fights for this cause, and wherever he is, he will continue this struggle.

### 国际记协授奖给格列索斯

1959-03-19
第5版()
专栏：

    国际记协授奖给格列索斯
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：3月16日在柏林举行了国际新闻工作者协会授给著名的希腊爱国者、前雅典“黎明日报”社社长格列索斯1958年度国际新闻工作者奖金的授奖仪式。国际新闻工作者协会授予格列索斯这一笔奖金，以表彰他在保卫和平和民主的事业中的杰出贡献。
    在授奖仪式上，由国际新闻工作者协会副秘书长彼得罗夫代表协会秘书处授奖。
    彼得罗夫在讲话中说，国际新闻工作者协会秘书处将尽一切力量为格列索斯重新获得自由而斗争。
    讲话后，彼得罗夫把奖金和一份国际新闻工作者协会的荣誉会员证交给前来代领奖金的“黎明日报”社代理社长、希腊议会议员帕帕迪米特里昂。
    在这个授奖仪式上，还宣读了格列索斯夫人塔西娅·格列索斯给国际新闻工作者协会的信。她在信中说，由于希腊当局不给她出国护照，她不能前来领奖。她说，各国人民的和平事业是神圣的，格列索斯坚决地为这一事业而斗争，不管他在哪里，他都会继续这种斗争。
