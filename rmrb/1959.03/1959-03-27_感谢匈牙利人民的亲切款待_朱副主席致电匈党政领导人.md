Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Sends a Telegram to Hungarian Party and Government Leaders, Thanking the Hungarian People for their Kind Hospitality
Author:
Date: 1959-03-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 25th. Zhu De, head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, sent a telegram to János Kádár, First Secretary of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, István Dobi, President of the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic, and Ferenc Münnich, Prime Minister of the Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, on March 25th, upon leaving Hungarian territory.

The telegram stated, "With reluctance, we leave the territory of the Hungarian People's Republic, deeply cherishing the memory of you, the Hungarian comrades, friends, and all the Hungarian people."

The telegram continued, "The days spent in your country are unforgettable. We have witnessed the revolutionary spirit and heroic nature displayed by the Hungarian people in socialist construction, and we have once again felt the profound fraternal friendship of the Hungarian people towards the Chinese people. We are very grateful for the warm welcome and kind hospitality you and the Hungarian people have extended to us."

The telegram concluded, "Dear comrades, please accept our warm wishes. We wish the Hungarian people even greater achievements in socialist construction, and we wish the Hungarian People's Republic greater prosperity and strength. We wish the fraternal friendship between our two peoples greater consolidation and development."

### 感谢匈牙利人民的亲切款待  朱副主席致电匈党政领导人

1959-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    感谢匈牙利人民的亲切款待
    朱副主席致电匈党政领导人
    新华社布达佩斯25日电　中国党政代表团团长朱德副主席25日在离开匈牙利国境时，曾致电匈牙利社会主义工人党第一书记卡达尔、匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比和工农革命政府总理明尼赫。
    电报说，我们怀着依依惜别的心情离开匈牙利人民共和国的国土，我们深深地怀念着你们，怀念着匈牙利的同志们、朋友们和全体匈牙利人民。
    电报说，在你们的国家里所度过的这些日子是令人难忘的。我们亲眼看到匈牙利人民在社会主义建设中所表现的革命气概和英雄本色，我们又一次体会到匈牙利人民对中国人民深厚的兄弟的情谊，我们十分感谢你们和匈牙利人民给予我们的热烈欢迎和亲切款待。
    电报最后说，亲爱的同志们，请接受我们的热烈祝愿。祝匈牙利人民在社会主义建设中取得更巨大的成就，祝匈牙利人民共和国更加富强和繁荣，祝我们两国人民之间的兄弟友谊更加巩固和发展。
