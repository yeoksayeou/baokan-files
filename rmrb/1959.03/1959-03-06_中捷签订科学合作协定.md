Title: China and Czechoslovakia Sign Scientific Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-03-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th. The 1959-1961 Scientific Cooperation Agreement and the 1959 Implementation Plan between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences were signed in Prague yesterday afternoon.

The representatives signing the agreement were Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Njedlý, President of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences.

According to the agreement, the two academies of sciences will cooperate closely and assist each other. They will invite or send scientific personnel to each other to give lectures, visit, assist in work, or exchange experiences. The two sides will also provide each other with various scientific materials and help each other train young scientific personnel. The agreement also stipulates that the two academies of sciences will jointly conduct twelve research and investigation projects of mutual interest as needed and as possible.

### 中捷签订科学合作协定

1959-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    中捷签订科学合作协定
    新华社布拉格4日电　中国科学院和捷克斯洛伐克科学院1959—1961年科学合作协定和1959年执行计划昨天下午在布拉格签字。
    双方签字的代表是中国科学院副院长吴有训和捷克斯洛伐克科学院院长尼耶德利。
    根据协定，两国科学院将密切合作，相互援助，互相邀请或派遣科学工作人员到对方讲学、访问、协助工作或交流经验。双方并将互相提供各种科学资料，互相帮助培养青年科学工作人员。协定还规定，两国科学院将根据需要与可能共同进行十二项双方都感兴趣的研究和考察工作。
