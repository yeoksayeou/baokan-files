Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Issues Statement: Saluting the People of Nyasaland and Central Africa
Author:
Date: 1959-03-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 21st: The World Federation of Trade Unions issued a statement here today, expressing firm support for the struggle of workers and people in Nyasaland and Central Africa for independence, and strongly protesting against the new crimes of imperialism in these regions.

The WFTU, in its statement, paid tribute to the heroic struggle of the workers and people of Nyasaland and Central Africa against being forcibly incorporated into an artificial federation, and condemned the brutal persecution against them by British imperialism.

The statement emphasized that the people of Nyasaland and Central Africa are not isolated, and that the workers and people of Africa and the world will always support them.

The statement concluded by calling on workers and trade unions around the world to support the demands of the workers and people of Nyasaland and Central Africa to end colonialist enslavement and exploitation.

### 世界工联发表声明  向尼亚萨兰和中非人民致敬

1959-03-23
第5版()
专栏：

    世界工联发表声明
    向尼亚萨兰和中非人民致敬
    新华社布拉格21日电　世界工会联合会今天在这里发表的一项声明中表示，坚决支持尼亚萨兰和中非工人和人民争取独立的斗争，并且强烈抗议帝国主义在这些地区的新罪行。
    世界工联在声明中向尼亚萨兰和中非工人和人民为反对强迫并入人为的联邦而进行的英勇斗争表示敬意，并且谴责英帝国主义对他们的残暴迫害。
    声明强调指出，尼亚萨兰和中非的人民并不是孤立的，非洲和全世界的工人和人民将永远支持他们。
    声明最后号召全世界工人和工会支持尼亚萨兰和中非工人和人民要结束殖民主义奴役和剥削的要求。
