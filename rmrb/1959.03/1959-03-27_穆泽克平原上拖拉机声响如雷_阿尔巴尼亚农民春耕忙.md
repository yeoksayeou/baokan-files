Title: Tractor Sounds Thunder Across the Muzek Plain: Albanian Farmers Busy with Spring Plowing
Author:
Date: 1959-03-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana - Albanian agricultural cooperatives, state farms, and individual farmers are making every effort to carry out spring plowing and sowing.

	The sound of tractor horns resounds across the vast plains of Muzek, Malik, Urg, and Sadri Milio. This spring, most of the land will be cultivated by machines, and the state has built twenty-five new agricultural machinery stations to help rural workers. It is estimated that the amount of machine work in rural areas this year will increase almost fifteen times compared to 1950.

	Before liberation, Albania had only thirty-two tractors. Currently, Albania has approximately two thousand eight hundred tractors and many other agricultural machines. This year, a large number of new agricultural machines will also be shipped from the Soviet Union and other socialist countries. (Attached photo)
	
	Tractor drivers at the "Red Star" state farm in Kanza, Albania, are plowing the land to prepare for planting vegetables. Albanian News Agency (Xinhua News Agency)

### 穆泽克平原上拖拉机声响如雷  阿尔巴尼亚农民春耕忙

1959-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    穆泽克平原上拖拉机声响如雷
    阿尔巴尼亚农民春耕忙
    新华社地拉那电　阿尔巴尼亚的农业合作社、国营农场和个体农民正在全力进行春耕播种工作。
    拖拉机的汽笛声响遍了穆泽克、马立克、乌尔古和萨德里米辽阔的平原。今年春季大部分的土地将用机器来耕种，国家新建了二十五个农业机器站来帮助农村劳动者。预计今年农村中的机器工作量将比1950年增加差不多十五倍。
  解放前，阿尔巴尼亚仅有三十二台拖拉机，目前阿尔巴尼亚约有二千八百台拖拉机和许多其他农业机器。今年从苏联和其他社会主义国家还将运来大批新的农业机器。（附图片）
    阿尔巴尼亚坎扎的“红星”国营农场的拖拉机手在翻耕土地准备种植蔬菜。　阿尔巴尼亚通讯社稿（新华社发）
