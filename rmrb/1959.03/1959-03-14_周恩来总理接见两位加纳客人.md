Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Two Guests from Ghana
Author:
Date: 1959-03-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: Premier Zhou Enlai received Mr. Gamaliel Kofi Amegbe and Mr. Moses Fairertey Kojo, two guests from Ghana who are currently visiting China, this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Mao Dun, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity, Ke Hua, Director of the West Asian and African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Mingyuan, Acting Secretary-General of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity. (Photo attached)

### 周恩来总理接见两位加纳客人

1959-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见两位加纳客人
    新华社13日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的加纳客人加米苏·可非·阿麦格倍和摩西·费尔却艾尔特·哥霍霍。
    接见时在座的有中国亚非团结委员会副主席茅盾、外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华和中国亚非团结委员会代理秘书长王明远。（附图片）
    周总理接见加纳客人加米苏·可非·阿麦格倍（左）和摩西·费尔却艾尔特·哥霍霍（右）。
                                    新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
