Title: 20,000 West German Residents Migrate to the German Democratic Republic in Three Months
Author:
Date: 1959-03-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 25th: According to a report by the German News Agency, nearly 20,000 West German residents have migrated to the German Democratic Republic in the past three months. In February, the number of immigrants increased by 45% compared to the same period in 1958.

    The majority of these immigrants are miners, metallurgical industry workers, and young people under the age of twenty-five. The government of the German Democratic Republic has helped them find jobs and made proper arrangements for their families.

    The government of the German Democratic Republic has recently received applications from many West German physicians, engineers, technicians, and workers requesting to reside in the German Democratic Republic.

### 三个月来有两万西德居民迁居德意志民主共和国

1959-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    三个月来有两万西德居民迁居德意志民主共和国
    新华社柏林25日电　据德意志通讯社报道：在过去三个月中，有将近两万名西德居民迁到了德意志民主共和国。2月份，迁入人数比1958年同期增加了45％。
    这些迁入的居民大多数是矿工、冶金工业的工人和二十五岁以下的青年。德意志民主共和国政府帮助他们找到工作，并且给他们的家庭作了妥善的安排。
    德意志民主共和国政府在最近期间接到西德许多医师、工程师、技术员和工人的申请书，要求在德意志民主共和国居住。
