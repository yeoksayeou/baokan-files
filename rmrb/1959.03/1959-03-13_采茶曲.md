Title: Tea-Picking Song
Author: Ba Mu
Date: 1959-03-13
Page: 8

    Tea-Picking Song
    Ba Mu
    Busy tea-picking in the Southern Mountains,
    Tea trees are sprouting,
    The hillside changes into new clothes,
    Delighting ten thousand families.
    A group of farm girls,
    Carry baskets into the mountain hollow,
    Arriving in the tea forest,
    Like butterflies gathering flowers.
    Rows of tea trees,
    New leaves fill the branches,
    Emerald grows from within,
    The bamboo basket cannot hold it all.
    This year's spring is the earliest,
    Spring comes and trees bloom,
    The tea forest celebrates the harvest,
    Songs welcome the colorful clouds.

### 采茶曲
巴牧
1959-03-13
第8版()
专栏：

    采茶曲
    巴牧
    南山忙采茶，
    茶树正发芽，
    山坡换新衣，
    乐煞万人家。
    一群农家女，
    提篮进山洼，
    来到茶林内，
    好似蝶采花。
    茶树一行行，
    新叶满枝垭，
    碧玉从中长，
    竹篮装不下。
    今年春最早，
    春来树开花，
    茶林庆丰收，
    歌声迎彩霞。
