Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Meets with Chinese Students Studying in Poland
Author:
Date: 1959-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 15th. Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the CPC Central Committee, who were invited to attend the Third National Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party, accompanied by Wang Bingnan, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, met with Chinese students studying in Poland and staff of the embassy at the Chinese Embassy in Poland on the morning of the 15th.

    During the meeting, Vice Chairman Zhu De and Comrade Wang Jiaxiang spoke to everyone successively.

### 朱德副主席会见我留波学生

1959-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席会见我留波学生
    新华社华沙15日电　应邀出席波兰统一工人党第三次全国代表大会的中国共产党中央委员会副主席朱德和中共中央书记处书记王稼祥、中共中央委员伍修权，15日上午由中国驻波兰大使王炳南陪同在中国驻波兰大使馆会见了在波兰学习的中国留学生和大使馆工作人员。
    会见时，朱德副主席和王稼祥同志先后向大家讲了话。
