Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives Aidit
Author:
Date: 1959-03-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 29th: According to today's "People's Daily" report, Chairman Ho Chi Minh received the Indonesian Communist Party delegation headed by the General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, Aidit, on the 28th. Chairman Ho Chi Minh had a cordial conversation with Comrade Aidit and others.
　　Present at the meeting were Le Duan, Truong Chinh, Nguyen Duy Trinh, Pham Hung, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and Ung Van Khiem, member of the Central Committee.
　　At noon on the 28th, some members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Labor Party had lunch with Comrade Aidit and others.

### 胡志明主席接见艾地

1959-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席接见艾地
    新华社河内29日电　据“人民报”今天报道，胡志明主席28日接见了以印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地为首的印度尼西亚共产党代表团。胡志明主席同艾地同志等进行了亲切的交谈。
    接见时在座的有，越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员黎笋、长征、阮维祯、范雄和中央委员会委员雍文谦。
    28日中午，劳动党中央委员会政治局的部分委员同艾地同志等共进午餐。
