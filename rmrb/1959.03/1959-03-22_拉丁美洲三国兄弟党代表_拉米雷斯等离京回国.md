Title: Latin American Three Brothers Party Representatives Ramirez and Others Leave Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1959-03-22
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 21st—O. Ramirez, member of the Central Political Committee of the Bolivian Communist Party, G. Guardin, member of the Central Committee of the Venezuelan Communist Party, and the representative of the Paraguayan Communist Party, totaling three people, who were invited to visit our country, left Beijing by plane today to return to their countries.

　　Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, and Li Qixin, responsible staff member of the central organs, were at the airport to see them off.

### 拉丁美洲三国兄弟党代表  拉米雷斯等离京回国

1959-03-22
第3版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲三国兄弟党代表
    拉米雷斯等离京回国
    新华社21日讯　应邀来我国访问的玻利维亚共产党中央政治委员会委员翁·拉米雷斯、委内瑞拉共产党中央委员吉·古阿尔丁和巴拉圭共产党的代表等共三人，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中共中央政治局候补委员康生，中央委员刘宁一，中央机关负责工作人员李启新等。
