Title: Soviet-German Delegation Talks
Author:
Date: 1959-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 10th. The Soviet delegation and the delegation of the German Democratic Republic held talks this afternoon.

Participating on the Soviet side were Khrushchev, Zorin, and Bieruzin; on the German Democratic Republic side were Grotewohl, Ulbricht, Dieckmann, Correns, as well as members of the Politburo of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and the Deputy Prime Ministers of the Government of the German Democratic Republic.

### 苏德两国代表团会谈

1959-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏德两国代表团会谈
    新华社柏林10日电　苏联代表团和德意志民主共和国代表团今天下午举行会谈。
    参加会谈的苏联方面有赫鲁晓夫、佐林和别尔乌辛；德意志民主共和国方面有格罗提渥、乌布利希、狄克曼、柯伦斯，以及德国统一社会党中央政治局委员和德意志民主共和国政府的副总理。
