Title: Japanese Socialist Party Delegation Holds Talks with Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1959-03-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The Japanese Socialist Party Delegation and the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs held talks on the 11th and 12th in a friendly atmosphere.

    The members of the Japanese Socialist Party Delegation participating in the talks included: Inejiro Asanuma, head of the delegation; and members: Seiichi Katsumada, Soji Okada, Masaru Sone, Satoshi Nakazaki, Tadataka Sata, Minoru Tanaka; Attendants: Kenichi Hirosawa and Ichiro Watanabe.

    The Chinese side participating in the talks included Zhou Gengsheng, Chu Tunan, Liao Chengzhi, Yang Hansheng, Ji Chaoding, Zhao Anbo, Wu Maosun, Xiao Xiangqian, and Chen Yu.

### 日社会党代表团和我外交学会举行会谈

1959-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    日社会党代表团和我外交学会举行会谈
    新华社12日讯　日本社会党访华代表团和中国人民外交学会于11日和12日在友好的气氛中进行了会谈。
    日本社会党访华代表团参加会谈的，有团长浅沼稻次郎，团员：胜间田清一、冈田宗司、曾弥益、中崎敏、佐多忠隆、田中稔男，随员：广泽贤一和渡边郎。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有周鲠生、楚图南、廖承志、阳翰笙、冀朝鼎、赵安博、吴茂荪、萧向前、陈宇。
