Title: The Tibetan Local Government and Upper-Class Reactionary Clique Collude with Imperialism to Betray the Motherland and Launch a Rebellion; State Council Orders Dissolution of the Tibetan Local Government; Decides that the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region Will Exercise the Functions and Powers of the Tibetan Local Government

Author: 
Date: 1959-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th -- Order of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

Upon investigation, the majority of the Kalons of the Tibetan local government and the upper-class reactionary clique, colluding with imperialism, gathering rebellious bandits, harming the people, abducting the Dalai Lama, tearing up the seventeen-article agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet, and on the night of March 19th, directing the Tibetan local army and rebels to launch a full-scale attack on the People's Liberation Army stationed in Lhasa. This act of betraying the motherland and undermining unity is intolerable under the law of the country. In order to safeguard national unity and ethnic solidarity, in addition to instructing the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to thoroughly quell the rebellion, it is hereby decided that the Tibetan local government will be dissolved from this date and the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region will exercise the functions and powers of the Tibetan local government. During the period when Dalai Lama Danzeng Jiacuo, Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region, is abducted, Panchen Erdini Qoiji Gyaltsen, Vice Director, will serve as Acting Director. Appoint Pabala Gelielangjie, Standing Committee Member of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region, as Vice Director; Appoint Apei Awangjinmei, Standing Committee Member and Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region, as Vice Director and Secretary-General. Remove the following eighteen people from their positions as members of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region and all other positions, and punish them separately in accordance with national laws: Suokang Wangqing Gele, Liuxia Tudeng Taba, Xianka Jumai Doje (Xiasu), Yutuo Zhaxidunzhu, Chijiang Luosang Yixi, Gazhang Luosang Rizeng, Dala Luosang Sandan, Kaimo Sonam Wangdui, Ronglangse Tudeng Nuosang, Pala Tudeng Weideng, Ouxie Tudeng Sangque, Langselin Banjor Jigmei, Minjilin Jiaxiang Jianzan, Jiariben Caiwang Duoji, Pangqiu, Weisajianzan (Gongdelin Zasa), Gongga Lama, Chupu Garma Ribei Dorje. Furthermore, appoint Deng Shaodong, Zhan Huayu, Hui Yiran, Liang Xuanxian, Cuike Dengzhu Zeren, Zhan Dong Luosang Langjie, Gaden Chiba Tudeng Gunga, Jianbai Cili, Apei Caidan Zhuoga, Duoji Caidan, Xirao Dengzhu, Jianzan Pingcuo, Luosang Cicheng, Qunjue, Pingcuo Wangqiu, and Wang Peisheng as members of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region. It is hoped that the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region will lead the monks and laypeople of Tibet, unite as one, work together, assist the People's Liberation Army in quickly quelling the rebellion, consolidate national defense, protect the interests of the people of all ethnic groups, stabilize social order, and strive to build a new democratic and socialist Tibet.
  This order.
        Premier Zhou Enlai
          March 28, 1959

### 西藏地方政府和上层反动集团勾结帝国主义背叛祖国进行叛乱  国务院命令解散西藏地方政府  决定由西藏自治区筹备委员会行使西藏地方政府职权

1959-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏地方政府和上层反动集团勾结帝国主义背叛祖国进行叛乱
    国务院命令解散西藏地方政府
    决定由西藏自治区筹备委员会行使西藏地方政府职权
    在达赖喇嘛被叛匪劫持期间，由班禅额尔德尼代理主任委员
    撤销叛国分子索康·旺清格勒等十八人的自治区筹委会委员和一切职务，并按国家法律分别给予惩处
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国国务院命令
    查西藏地方政府多数噶伦和上层反动集团，勾结帝国主义，纠集叛匪，进行叛乱，残害人民，劫持达赖喇嘛，撕毁关于和平解放西藏办法的十七条协议，并且于三月十九日夜间指挥西藏地方军队和叛乱分子向驻拉萨的人民解放军发动全面进攻。这种背叛祖国、破坏统一的行为，实为国法所不容。为维护国家统一和民族团结，除责成中国人民解放军西藏军区彻底平息叛乱外，特决定自即日起，解散西藏地方政府，由西藏自治区筹备委员会行使西藏地方政府职权。在自治区筹备委员会主任委员达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉错被劫持期间，由班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞副主任委员代理主任委员职务。任命自治区筹备委员会常务委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰为副主任委员；常务委员兼秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美为副主任委员兼秘书长。撤销叛国分子索康·旺清格勒，柳霞·土登塔巴，先喀·居美多杰（夏苏），宇妥·扎西顿珠，赤江·罗桑益西，噶章·洛桑日增，达拉·洛桑三旦，凯墨·索南旺堆，绒朗色·土登诺桑，帕拉·土登为登，欧协·土登桑却，朗色林·班觉久美，敏吉林·嘉祥坚赞，呷日本·才旺多吉，庞球，威萨坚赞（功德林扎萨），贡噶喇嘛，楚普噶玛巴·日贝多吉十八人的自治区筹备委员会委员和一切职务，并按国家法律分别给予惩处。并任命邓少东，詹化雨，惠毅然，梁选贤，崔科·登珠泽仁，詹东·洛桑朗杰，噶登赤巴·土登滚噶，坚白慈里，阿沛·才丹卓噶，多吉才旦，协绕登珠，坚赞平措，洛桑慈诚，群觉，平措旺秋，王沛生十六人为自治区筹备委员会委员。望西藏自治区筹备委员会，领导全藏僧俗人民，团结一致，共同努力，协助人民解放军迅速平息叛乱，巩固国防，保护各民族人民利益，安定社会秩序，为建设民主和社会主义的新西藏而奋斗。
  此令。
        总理　周恩来
          一九五九年三月二十八日西
