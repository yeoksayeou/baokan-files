Title: Three Weightlifters Break National Records in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-03-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 26th -- Three weightlifters from Guangdong Province broke three national records last night while participating in the weightlifting competition of the Guangdong Provincial Games.

    In the flyweight division, Chen Yongbo, a weightlifting athlete from Guangzhou, achieved initial success by pressing 100 kg, breaking the national record of 98.5 kg held by Tao Lianlu, a worker athlete from Nanjing. Another athlete, Li Jiyuan of the Guangzhou Military Region, refreshed his own national record of 96 kg with a snatch of 97.5 kg in the same division.

    In the middleweight division, Shu Gang's snatch reached 113.5 kg, breaking the national record of 113 kg held by Huang Qianghui.

### 三名举重选手在广州打破全国纪录

1959-03-27
第6版()
专栏：

    三名举重选手在广州打破全国纪录
    新华社广州26日电　广东省有三个举重运动员25日晚在参加广东省运动会举重比赛时，打破了三项全国纪录。
    在最轻级比赛中，广州市的举重运动健将陈永博首传捷报，以推举一百公斤的成绩，打破了南京工人选手陶连禄保持的九八·五公斤的全国纪录。另一个运动健将、广州部队的黎纪元参加这一级测验时，以抓举九七·五公斤的成绩刷新了他自己保持的九十六公斤的全国纪录。
    在中量级比赛中，运动健将舒刚抓举的成绩达到了一一三·五公斤，打破了黄强辉保持的一一三公斤的全国纪录。
