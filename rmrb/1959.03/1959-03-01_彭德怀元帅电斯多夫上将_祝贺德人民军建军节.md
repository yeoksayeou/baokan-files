Title: Marshal Peng Dehuai Sends Telegram to General Stoff, Congratulating the People's Army of Germany on Army Day

Author:

Date: 1959-03-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of China, sent a telegram on the 27th to General Willi Stoph, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic, congratulating the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic on its third anniversary.

    The congratulatory telegram stated: "Over the past three years, under the leadership of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the National People's Army has made tremendous achievements in building the army and in military and political training, vigilantly defending the construction and security of the socialist cause of the German Democratic Republic. I wish the National People's Army will make even greater contributions in the future to the cause of striving for the democratic and peaceful reunification of Germany and to the cause of safeguarding peace in Europe and the world.

    The congratulatory telegram stated, "The friendship between the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic will forever be unbreakable."

### 彭德怀元帅电斯多夫上将  祝贺德人民军建军节

1959-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    彭德怀元帅电斯多夫上将
    祝贺德人民军建军节
    新华社28日讯　我国国防部长彭德怀元帅，27日打电报给德意志民主共和国副总理兼国防部长威利·斯多夫上将，祝贺德意志民主共和国国家人民军建军三周年。
    贺电中说：“三年来，国家人民军在德国统一社会党的领导下，在建军和军事政治训练方面，已经取得了巨大的成就，警惕地捍卫着德意志民主共和国社会主义事业的建设和安全。祝国家人民军今后在争取德国的民主和平统一的事业中，在维护欧洲和世界和平的事业中作出更大的贡献。
    贺电说：“中国人民解放军和德意志民主共和国国家人民军的友谊，永远是牢不可破的。”
