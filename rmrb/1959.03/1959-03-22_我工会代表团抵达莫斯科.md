Title: Our Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1959-03-22
Page: 3

    Our Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Moscow
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 21st. The Chinese trade union delegation, led by Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Moscow by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the 12th Congress of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions.
    Prokhorov, Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, and Khrestiyaninov, Chairman of the Moscow City Council of Trade Unions, were present at the airport to greet the delegation.
    Li Zewang, Counselor of our Embassy in the Soviet Union, also went to the airport to meet the delegation.

### 我工会代表团抵达莫斯科

1959-03-22
第3版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团抵达莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科21日电　应邀前来参加全苏工会第十二次代表大会的中国工会代表团，在中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜率领下，今日下午搭飞机抵达莫斯科。
    前往机场迎接的有全苏工会中央理事会书记普罗霍罗夫、莫斯科市工会理事会主席克列斯蒂扬尼诺夫。
    我国驻苏联大使馆参赞李则望也到机场迎接。
