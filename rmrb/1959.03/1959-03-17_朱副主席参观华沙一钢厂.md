Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Visits Warsaw Steel Plant
Author: 
Date: 1959-03-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 16th - Zhu De, the head of the Chinese Communist Party delegation and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, who was invited to attend the Third Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party, visited the Warsaw High-Quality Steel Smelting Plant at noon today.

Accompanying Vice Chairman Zhu De on the visit were Żymajtys, the Polish Minister of Heavy Industry, Szydz, the Secretary-General of the Economic Committee of the Council of Ministers, and Kiryluk, the Polish Ambassador to China.

Wang Bingnan, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, also accompanied the visit.

The director of the smelting plant, the secretary of the factory's Party committee, and the secretary of the local Party committee warmly welcomed Vice Chairman Zhu De and briefed him in detail on the factory's production situation and the work of the Party organization.

After a cordial conversation with the factory leaders, Vice Chairman Zhu De wrote in the guestbook: "Salute to the Polish working comrades, and wish you greater achievements in building socialism." Subsequently, Vice Chairman Zhu De visited the various production workshops in detail and was warmly welcomed by the workers.

### 朱副主席参观华沙一钢厂

1959-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    朱副主席参观华沙一钢厂
    新华社华沙16日电　应邀参加波兰统一工人党第三次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长、中共中央副主席朱德，今天中午参观了华沙优质钢冶炼厂。
    陪同朱德副主席参观的，有波兰重工业部长热马伊蒂斯，部长会议经济委员会秘书长希兹和波兰驻中国大使基里洛克等人。
    中国驻波兰大使王炳南也陪同前往参观。
    冶炼厂厂长、工厂党委书记和当地区的党委书记热烈欢迎朱德副主席，并且向他详细介绍了工厂生产情况和党组织的工作情况。
    朱德副主席在和工厂领导人进行了亲切谈话后，在贵宾留言簿上写道：“向波兰工人同志们致敬，祝你们在建设社会主义中取得更大的成就”。随后，朱德副主席详细地参观了各个生产车间，受到了工人们的热烈欢迎。
