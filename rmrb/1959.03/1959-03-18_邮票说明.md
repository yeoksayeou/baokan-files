    Stamp Description
    These are commemorative stamps issued by Hungary for the 80th anniversary of the Paris Commune. One of them features L. Francsics, a Hungarian worker and active participant in the Paris Commune. These two stamps were sent in by Yang Yulin, a reader of this newspaper from the Institute of Metallurgy and Geology. (Image attached)

### 邮票说明

1959-03-18

专栏：

    邮票说明
    这是匈牙利发行的巴黎公社诞生八十周年的纪念邮票，其中一张是巴黎公社积极参加者之一匈牙利工人L·弗兰直。这两张邮票是本报读者冶金地质研究所杨玉林同志寄来的。（附图片）
