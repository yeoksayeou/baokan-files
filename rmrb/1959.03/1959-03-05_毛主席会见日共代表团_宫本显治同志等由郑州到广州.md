Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Japanese Communist Party Delegation; Comrade Kenji Miyamoto and Others Arrive in Guangzhou from Zhengzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-03-05
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 4th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, met with the Japanese Communist Party delegation consisting of Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, and Shoichi Kasuga , Member of the Central Committee Presidium of the Japanese Communist Party, in Zhengzhou on the afternoon of the 3rd and had a cordial conversation.

    Comrades from the Chinese Communist Party who participated in the meeting and conversation included: Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi, General Secretary Deng Xiaoping, Politburo Member Peng Zhen, and Secretary of the Secretariat Wang Jiaxiang.
    Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 4th: Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, Shoichi Kasuga, Member of the Central Committee Presidium of the Japanese Communist Party, and Tomio Nishizawa, Deputy Director of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, left Zhengzhou by special plane this morning for Guangzhou.

    Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Peng Zhen, Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, were at the airport to see them off.

    Also at the airport to see them off were Wu Zhipu, First Secretary of the Henan Provincial Committee of the CPC, Zhao Wenfu and Li Li, Secretaries of the Provincial Party Committee, and Wang Lizhi, First Secretary of the Zhengzhou Municipal Committee of the CPC, and others.
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 4th: Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, Shoichi Kasuga, Member of the Central Committee Presidium of the Japanese Communist Party, and Tomio Nishizawa, Deputy Director of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, arrived in Guangzhou by plane from Zhengzhou today. Wen Minsheng, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC, Yin Linping, Alternate Secretary, Wang De, First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPC, and other responsible cadres of the Guangdong Provincial Committee and the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPC, went to the airport to welcome them. In the evening, the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests.

### 毛主席会见日共代表团  宫本显治同志等由郑州到广州

1959-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席会见日共代表团
    宫本显治同志等由郑州到广州
    新华社郑州4日电　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东，3日下午在郑州会见了由日本共产党总书记宫本显治、日共中央干部会委员春日正一组成的日本共产党代表团，并进行了亲切的谈话。
    中共方面参加会见和谈话的有：副主席刘少奇、总书记邓小平，政治局委员彭真，中央书记处书记王稼祥。
    新华社郑州4日电　日本共产党总书记宫本显治、日共中央干部会委员春日正一、日共中央副部长西泽富夫，今天上午乘专机离开郑州前往广州。
    到机场欢送的有，中共中央总书记邓小平，中央政治局委员彭真，中央书记处书记王稼祥。
    到机场欢送的还有中共河南省委第一书记吴芝圃，省委书记处书记赵文甫、李立和中共郑州市委第一书记王黎之等。
    新华社广州4日电　日本共产党总书记宫本显治、日共中央干部会委员春日正一和日共中央副部长西泽富夫今天乘飞机从郑州到达广州。前往机场欢迎的，有中共广东省委书记处书记文敏生、候补书记尹林平，中共广州市委第一书记王德以及广东省委、广州市委其他负责干部。晚上，中共广东省委设宴招待贵宾。
