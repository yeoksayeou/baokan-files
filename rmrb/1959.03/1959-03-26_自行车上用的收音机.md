    Radio for Bicycles
    The Tianjin No. 7 Cultural and Educational Supplies Production Cooperative has created a model 58 two-tube radio for use on bicycles.

    This radio is small in size and easy to carry. It does not require additional antenna or grounding wires for listening to broadcasts indoors or outdoors. The sound quality is good, and the power consumption is low, about half that of a typical three-tube radio.

    This radio can be installed on a bicycle. Connect one end of the wire to the terminal and the other end to one pole of the bicycle hub. After the wheel rotates, pull out the metal antenna, and you can listen to the radio while riding at a moderate speed. It can be used at home like an ordinary radio.

### 自行车上用的收音机

1959-03-26
第2版()
专栏：跃进橱窗

    自行车上用的收音机
    天津市第七文教用品生产合作社，创造出一种58型自行车上用的两灯收音机。
    这种收音机体积小，携带方便，在室内外收听广播不必另加天、地线，音质好，耗电量小，相当一般三灯收音机耗电量的一半。
    这种收音机可装置在自行车上，把导线的一端接在接线柱上，把另一端接在自行车滚的一极。车轮转动后，把金属制天线拉出，用中等速度行驶即能收听广播。在家庭使用时，与普通收音机相同。
