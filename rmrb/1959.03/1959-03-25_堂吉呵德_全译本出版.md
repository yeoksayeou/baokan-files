Title: Complete Translation of "Don Quixote" Published
Author:
Date: 1959-03-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: The complete Chinese translation (two volumes in total) of the Spanish Renaissance masterpiece "Don Quixote" has been published by the People's Literature Publishing House and will be available for distribution throughout the country in late March. In the past, only the first part of "Don Quixote" had been translated and published in China; the second part had never been translated.

### “堂吉呵德”全译本出版

1959-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

    “堂吉呵德”全译本出版
    据新华社23日讯　西班牙文艺复兴时期的巨著“堂吉呵德”的全部中译本（共两本），已经由人民文学出版社出版，3月下旬即可在全国各地发行。过去，我国只翻译出版过“堂吉呵德”第一部，第二部一直没有译本。
