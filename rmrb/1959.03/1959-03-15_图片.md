On March 11th, more than 100,000 people in Baghdad, the capital of Iraq, participated in the funeral of Kamel Kazanchi. Lawyer Kazanchi was a famous leader of the Iraqi national movement and a peace fighter. He was killed by the traitor Shawwaf in the recent Mosul rebellion. The picture shows the masses carrying Kazanchi's portrait to participate in the funeral.
