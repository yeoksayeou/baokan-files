Title: Japanese Communist Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session; Miyamoto Kenji Reports on Visits to the Soviet Union, China, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-03-24
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 23rd: Japanese Communist Party General Secretary Kenji Miyamoto announced at a press conference today that the Japanese Communist Party held its Fifth Plenary Session of the Central Committee on the 20th and 21st. At the plenary session, Miyamoto reported on the activities of the Japanese Communist Party delegation attending the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, visiting Czechoslovakia, China, and North Korea, and issuing joint statements with the Communist Party of China and the Workers' Party of Korea, respectively. Miyamoto also emphasized the significance of the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the significance of strengthening solidarity with fraternal parties through this visit.
    Masakazu Kasuga gave a supplementary report at the plenary session.
    Miyamoto said at the press conference that the Fifth Plenary Session unanimously approved the report and supplementary report and adopted the "Resolution on the Report of the Activities of the JCP Delegation Attending the 21st Extraordinary Congress of the CPSU and Visiting Czechoslovakia, China, and Korea."
    Miyamoto said that the plenary session also discussed the Japanese Communist Party's policy on the upcoming local elections and the House of Councillors elections and adopted two documents on election issues.

### 日共中央举行全会  宫本显治作访问苏中等国的报告

1959-03-24
第5版()
专栏：

    日共中央举行全会
    宫本显治作访问苏中等国的报告
    新华社23日讯　东京消息：日本共产党总书记宫本显治今天在记者招待会上宣布，日本共产党在20日和21日举行了中央委员会第五次全体会议。在全会上，宫本作了日本共产党代表团出席苏共第二十一次代表大会，访问捷克斯洛伐克、中国、朝鲜，同中国共产党和朝鲜劳动党分别发表联合声明等活动的报告。宫本并且强调指出了苏共第二十一次代表大会的意义和通过这次访问加强了同各兄弟党的团结的意义。
    春日正一在全会上作了补充报告。
    宫本在记者招待会上说，第五次全体会议一致同意报告和补充报告，并且通过了“关于日共代表团出席苏共第二十一次非常代表大会，访问捷克斯洛伐克、中国、朝鲜的活动的报告的决议”。
    宫本说，全会还讨论了日本共产党对即将来临的地方选举和参议员选举的方针问题，并且通过了关于选举问题的两项文件。
