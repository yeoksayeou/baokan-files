Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives French Journalist Mendes
Author:
Date: 1959-03-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 11th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the French journalist Tibor Mende this afternoon. Present at the reception were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见法记者芒德

1959-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见法记者芒德
    新华社11日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了法国记者狄包尔·芒德。接见时在座的有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃。
