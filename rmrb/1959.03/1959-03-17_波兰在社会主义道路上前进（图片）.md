Title: Poland Advances on the Socialist Road (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1959-03-17
Page: 6

Poland Advances on the Socialist Road
    The Polish capital, Warsaw, has rapidly healed the wounds of war since liberation and has been rebuilt to be even more magnificent. This is a bird's-eye view of Warsaw. (Submitted by the Polish Central Photo Agency)
    The coal industry is one of Poland's important industrial sectors. This is an exterior view of the enormous Dębińsko coal mine. Poland will undertake large-scale construction projects in the next seven years. This is a huge building under construction in Warsaw.
                    (Submitted by the Polish Central Photo Agency)
    Poland's machine manufacturing industry has developed greatly. This is a large batch of Mara binding machines produced by the Poznań Harvester Factory. (Submitted by the Polish Central Photo Agency)
    Polish agriculture is increasingly using machinery for cultivation. This is an agricultural cooperative in the Opole province using machines for spring plowing. (Submitted by the Polish Central Photo Agency)
    Poland is vigorously developing animal husbandry. This is a flock of sheep returning from the pasture. Polish children receive the cordial care of the Party and the government. Look how lovely these healthy and lively children are!

### 波兰在社会主义道路上前进（图片）

1959-03-17
第6版()
专栏：

    波兰在社会主义道路上前进
    波兰首都华沙在解放后迅速地医治了战争创伤，而且建设得更加壮丽，这是华沙鸟瞰。	  （波兰中央图片社稿）
    煤炭工业是波兰重要工业部门之一，这是巨大的邓宾斯科煤矿外景。在今后七年内，波兰将进行大规模的建筑工程，这是正在施工中的华沙一座巨大建筑物。
                    （波兰中央图片社稿）
    波兰的机器制造业获得很大发展。这是波兹南收割机工厂生产的大批马拉捆束机。                      （波兰中央图片社稿）
    波兰农业正在越来越多地运用机器耕作，这是奥普列省的一个农业合作社在用机器进行春耕。
                      （波兰中央图片社稿）
    波兰大力发展畜牧业。这是牧场归来的羊群。波兰的儿童得到党和政府的亲切关怀。看，这些健康的活泼的孩子多么可爱！
