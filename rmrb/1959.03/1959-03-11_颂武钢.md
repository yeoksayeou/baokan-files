Title: Ode to Wuhan Steel
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1959-03-11
Page: 2

    Ode to Wuhan Steel
    Guo Moruo
    Iron waves surge like fiery dragons,
    Thousand-degree heat reflects a red face,
    Wuhan Steel follows closely after Anshan Steel,
    The scale of construction is bold and grand.
      January 25, 1959

    颂武钢

    铁浪奔流似火龙，
