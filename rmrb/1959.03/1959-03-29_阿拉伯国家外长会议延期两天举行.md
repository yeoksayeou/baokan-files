Title: Arab Foreign Ministers Meeting Postponed for Two Days
Author:
Date: 1959-03-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 28th - The Arab League has decided to postpone the emergency meeting of Arab Foreign Ministers to April 2nd.

Arab League Secretary-General Hassouna has notified member states of the decision to postpone the Arab Foreign Ministers meeting by two days at the request of Moroccan Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Abdallah Ibrahim. The Moroccan Prime Minister said he had some matters to attend to.

The meeting was originally scheduled to be held in Beirut on March 31st to discuss "the situation arising from the current state of relations between Iraq and the UAR."

Apart from the Jordanian Foreign Minister and the Tunisian Foreign Minister who have indicated they cannot attend the meeting, the other eight Arab League member states have agreed to participate in the Foreign Ministers meeting.

According to the "Al-Ahram" newspaper today, Arab League Secretary-General Hassouna will begin contacting the ambassadors of the member states today to discuss the agenda of the meeting.

### 阿拉伯国家外长会议延期两天举行

1959-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯国家外长会议延期两天举行
    新华社开罗28日电　阿拉伯联盟决定，阿拉伯国家外交部长紧急会议延期到4月2日举行。
    阿拉伯联盟秘书长哈苏纳应摩洛哥首相兼外交大臣阿卜杜拉·易卜拉欣的请求，已经把阿拉伯国家外长会议推迟两天的决定通知各成员国。摩洛哥首相说，他还有一些事情要料理。
    这个会议原定3月31日在贝鲁特举行，讨论“伊拉克和阿联目前的关系状况所产生的情况”。
    除了约旦外交大臣和突尼斯外交部长曾经表示不能出席会议以外，其他八个阿拉伯联盟的会员国都同意参加外长会议。
    据“金字塔报”今天说，阿拉伯联盟秘书长哈苏纳今天将开始同各成员国的大使接触，以讨论会议的议程。
