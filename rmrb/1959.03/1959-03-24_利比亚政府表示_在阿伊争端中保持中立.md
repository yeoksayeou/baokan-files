Title: Libyan Government States Neutrality in Arab-Israeli Dispute
Author:
Date: 1959-03-24
Page: 5

    Libyan Government States
    Neutrality in Arab-Israeli Dispute
    Xinhua News Agency, Tripoli (Libya), March 22nd: The Libyan government issued a statement on the 21st, reiterating its neutrality in disputes between Arab countries.
    The statement warned parents not to allow their children to be exploited by others to the detriment of the country.
    This statement was issued after demonstrations by students and mobs on the 20th, who demonstrated in support of UAR President Nasser against Iraqi Prime Minister Qassem.

### 利比亚政府表示  在阿伊争端中保持中立

1959-03-24
第5版()
专栏：

    利比亚政府表示
    在阿伊争端中保持中立
    新华社22日讯　的黎波里（利比亚）消息：利比亚政府21日发表声明，重申它在阿拉伯国家相互之间的争端中保持中立。
    声明警告父母们不要让他们的子弟为别人所利用而损害国家。
    这项声明是在20日学生和暴徒举行示威之后发表的，这些人举行示威表示拥护阿联总统纳赛尔反对伊拉克总理卡塞姆。
