Title: Multiple Businesses Offer Wide Opportunities
Author:
Date: 1959-03-15
Page: 7

Many avenues for diverse economic activities,
Don't overlook any opportunity, big or small,
Children gather grass to raise fish,
Grandmothers feed pigs and raise geese.
              (Hunan Folk Song)

    婆婆喂猪又养鹅。
