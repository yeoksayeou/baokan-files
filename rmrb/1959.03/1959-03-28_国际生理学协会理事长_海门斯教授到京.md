Title: President of the International Union of Physiological Sciences, Professor Heymans, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-03-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 27th. Professor Heymans of Belgium, President of the International Union of Physiological Sciences, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 26th at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. He will lecture, visit, and tour in our country.

### 国际生理学协会理事长  海门斯教授到京

1959-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    国际生理学协会理事长
    海门斯教授到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国科学院邀请来我国的国际生理学协会理事长、比利时海门斯教授，在26日下午乘飞机到达北京。他将在我国讲学并进行参观、访问。
