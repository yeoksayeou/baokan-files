Title: I Wish to Become a Passionate Bird
Author: Mi Gu
Date: 1959-03-06
Page: 8

Poetry and Painting

    I Wish to Become a Passionate Bird
    Jiangxi Folk Song Mi Gu Painting With braids jumping, face flushed, carrying a hundred jin of load as fast as the wind, I wish to become a passionate bird, flying with the wind to my sister's home. (Picture attached)
