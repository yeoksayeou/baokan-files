Title: United Action to Launch the Fifth Spring Struggle: Two Million Japanese Workers Hold Strikes and Rallies
Author:
Date: 1959-03-26
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 25th: More than two million workers belonging to the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo) and neutral trade unions simultaneously held strikes and rallies throughout the country today, launching the fifth unified action of the Spring Struggle.
    The workers are demanding higher wages, the implementation of a national uniform minimum wage system, and the safeguarding of workers' democratic rights.
    Coal miners from fourteen large coal mining companies are continuing their indefinite nationwide general strike, which began on the 23rd.
    Workers in the steel industry, metal mining industry, chemical industry, shipbuilding industry, and electrical machinery industry held strikes in important work sectors. Seventy-three taxi unions in the taxi industry held the first nationwide strike in the history of the taxi workers' movement today.
    Employees in the public utilities sector simultaneously held workplace meetings in approximately eighty locations across the country today.
    In Tokyo, two thousand workers held a rally in front of the Diet and passed a resolution demanding the implementation of a national uniform minimum wage of 8,000 yen per person per month, and opposing the minimum wage bill submitted to the Diet by the Kishi Nobusuke government.

### 展开第五次春季斗争的统一行动  日二百万工人举行罢工和集会

1959-03-26
第5版()
专栏：

    展开第五次春季斗争的统一行动
    日二百万工人举行罢工和集会
    新华社25日讯　东京消息：属于日本工会总评议会和中立性工会的二百多万工人今天在全国各地一齐举行罢工和集会，展开了春季斗争的第五次统一行动。
    工人们要求提高工资、实行全国统一的最低工资制和维护工人的民主权利。
    十四家大煤矿公司的煤矿工人正在继续进行从23日开始的无定期全国性大罢工。
    钢铁工业、金属矿业、化学工业、造船工业和电气机械工业的工人在重要的工作部门举行了罢工。出租汽车业的七十三个工会今天举行了出租汽车工人运动史上第一次的全国规模罢工。
    公用事业部门的职工今天在全国约八十个地方一齐举行了工作场所大会。
    在东京，两千名工人在国会前面举行大会，并且通过决议，要求实行全国统一的每月每人八千日元的最低工资制，反对岸信介政府向国会提出的最低工资制法案。
