Title: The governments of Sweden, Norway and Denmark invite Khrushchev to visit

Author: 
Date: 1959-03-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th -- TASS reported from Moscow on February 27th: On February 25th, the ambassadors of Sweden, Norway and Denmark to Moscow paid a visit to Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, and on behalf of their governments, invited the Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Khrushchev, to visit their countries in August 1959.
    Gromyko said that he would convey this invitation to Khrushchev.

### 瑞典挪威丹麦三国政府  邀请赫鲁晓夫前往访问

1959-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典挪威丹麦三国政府
    邀请赫鲁晓夫前往访问
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科27日电：2月25日，瑞典、挪威、丹麦三国驻莫斯科大使拜会了苏联外交部长葛罗米柯，并代表他们的政府邀请苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在1959年8月访问他们的国家。
    葛罗米柯说，他将把这一邀请转告赫鲁晓夫。
