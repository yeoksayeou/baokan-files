Title: State Council Plenary Meeting Discusses Education Issues, Approves Six Documents on Education Work
Author:
Date: 1959-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd: The 86th Plenary Meeting of the State Council was held this afternoon.

The meeting mainly discussed education issues. After hearing an explanation from Minister of Education Yang Xiufeng, the meeting approved the following documents: 1. The Circular of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the State Council on the Rectification of Full-Time and Part-Time Institutions of Higher Education Newly Established in 1958; 2. The Circular of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the State Council on Continuing to Eliminate Illiteracy and Consolidate and Develop Spare-Time Education in Rural Areas; 3. The Regulations of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the State Council on Experimentally Reforming the Education System; 4. The Regulations of the State Council on the Arrangement of Teaching, Labor, and Life in Full-Time Schools; 5. The Regulations of the State Council on the Supplementation, Training, and Allocation of Faculty in Institutions of Higher Education; 6. The Circular of the State Council on Paying Attention to Resolving the Problems of Necessary School Building Construction, Instruments, Equipment, and Raw Material Supply for Institutions of Higher Education and Secondary Schools.

The meeting heard a report from the Science and Technology Commission on carrying out measurement work and approved the proposal on unifying the names of measurement units in China.

The meeting also ratified the Consular Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic and decided to submit this treaty to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and approval.

Finally, the meeting approved the decision of the State Council on changes in the administrative divisions of counties and cities and also approved appointments and removals.

### 国务院全体会议讨论教育问题  通过有关教育工作的六个文件

1959-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院全体会议讨论教育问题
    通过有关教育工作的六个文件
    新华社22日讯　国务院全体会议第八十六次会议，在今天下午举行。
    这次会议主要讨论了教育问题。会议听取了教育部长杨秀峰的说明后，通过了以下几个文件：一、中共中央和国务院关于整顿1958年新建的全日制和半日制高等学校的通知；二、中共中央和国务院关于在农村继续扫除文盲和巩固发展业余教育的通知；三、中共中央和国务院关于试验改革学制的规定；四、国务院关于全日制学校的教学、劳动和生活安排的规定；五、国务院关于高等学校师资的补充、培养和调配问题的规定；六、国务院关于注意解决高等学校、中等学校所必需的校舍建筑、仪器、设备及原材料供应等问题的通知。
    会议听取了科学技术委员会关于开展计量工作的报告，并且通过了关于统一我国计量单位名称的建议。
    会议还核准了中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国领事条约，并决定将这个条约提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议最后通过了国务院关于县、市行政区划变更的决定，并且通过了任免事项。
