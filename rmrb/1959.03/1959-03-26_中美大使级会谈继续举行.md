Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1959-03-26
Page: 4

    Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 24th. The 88th meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw at 2:00 PM today. The meeting lasted two hours.
    After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would continue on May 19th at 2:00 PM.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1959-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社华沙24日电　中美两国大使级会谈第八十八次会议今天下午二时在华沙举行。本次会议历时两小时。
    会后双方宣布，下次会议将在5月19日下午二时继续举行。
