Title: Xiang Xiuli's Deeds Exhibition Opens

Author: 
Date: 1959-03-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 6th. The Xiang Xiuli Comrade Deeds Exhibition opened in Guangzhou.

　　More than seven hundred youth representatives from hundreds of units attended the opening ceremony held at the Youth Cultural Palace on the afternoon of the 4th. Lai Dachao, member of the Standing Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Situ Meifang, deputy secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Rong Fengping, Xiang Xiuli's mother, spoke at the meeting. Qu Mengjue, secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, visited the exhibition on the 4th and met with Rong Fengping, Xiang Xiuli's mother, and Xiang Xiuli's father, husband, and other relatives, along with Lai Dachao and others.

　　Zhang Hanming and more than forty other youth representatives who attended the National Congress of Active Builders of Socialism held a forum after the visit to study Comrade Xiang Xiuli's communist qualities.

### 向秀丽事迹展览会开幕

1959-03-07
第6版()
专栏：

    向秀丽事迹展览会开幕
    新华社广州6日电　向秀丽同志事迹展览会在广州开幕。
    数百个单位的七百多名青年代表，4日下午参加了在青年文化宫举行的开幕式。中共广州市委常委赖大超、共青团广州市委副书记司徒梅芳和向秀丽的妈妈容凤屏在会上讲了话。中共广东省委书记处书记区梦觉4日参观了展览会，并同赖大超等一起会见了容凤屏老妈妈和向秀丽的父亲、丈夫及其他亲人。
    出席全国青年社会主义建设积极分子代表大会的代表张汉明等四十多位青年代表，在参观后举行了学习向秀丽共产主义品质的座谈会。
