Title: Our Trade Delegation Returns Home from Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1959-03-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 19th - The Chinese Government Trade Delegation concluded its visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and returned home by plane today. Those who saw them off at the airport included Vietnamese Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ung Van Khiem, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Trade Li Ban and Deng Yuezhou, Chairman of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association Bui Ky, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee Tran Duy Hung, and Secretary of the Hanoi Municipal Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party Tran Minh Tuyen, among others.

### 我贸易访问团离越南回国

1959-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    我贸易访问团离越南回国
    新华社河内19日电　中国政府贸易访问团结束它在越南民主共和国的参观访问以后，今天乘飞机回国。到机场送行的有越南外交部副部长雍文谦，对外贸易部副部长李班、邓曰洲，越中友好协会会长裴?，河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴和越南劳动党河内市委员会书记陈名宣等。
