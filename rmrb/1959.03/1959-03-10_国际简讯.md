		International Briefs

		Omani Nationalists Inflict Heavy Blows on British Aggressor Troops
		Cairo Dispatch: A communiqué issued on the 8th by the Omani Imam's office in Cairo stated that fierce battles were taking place in areas near the Green Mountains between Omani nationalists and British aggressor troops. The two-day battle caught them by surprise. Fighting is currently ongoing.
		German Communist Party of Lower Saxony Announces Election Platform
		Berlin Dispatch: The West German Lower Saxony party organization of the German Communist Party has issued an election platform for the upcoming state parliamentary elections, calling on voters to support candidates who lead the people's movement for the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany and against atomic armament.
		Former Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama Dies
		Tokyo Dispatch: Former Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama passed away on the morning of the 6th due to heart disease.
		Increased Influenza Mortality Rate in Britain
		London Dispatch: British statistical authorities announced on the 7th that since the beginning of this year, the number of deaths from influenza in England and Wales has reached more than three thousand, more than double the number in the same period last year.

### 国际简讯

1959-03-10
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    阿曼民族主义者痛击英侵略军
    开罗讯：阿曼教长驻开罗办事处8日发表的公报说，在绿山附近一些地区，阿曼民族主义者同英国侵略军正在进行着激烈的战斗。这次持续了两天的战斗使他们大吃一惊。目前，战斗仍在继续进行中。德共下萨克森州发表竞选纲领
    柏林讯：德国共产党西德下萨克森州党组织就该州即将举行的议会选举发表竞选纲领，号召选民支持那些领导争取缔结对德和约和反对原子武装的人民运动的候选人。
    日本前首相鸠山一郎逝世
    东京讯：日本前首相鸠山一郎因患心藏病于6日上午逝世。英国流行性感冒死亡率增加
    伦敦讯：英国统计部门7日宣布，今年开始以来，英格兰和威尔士患流行性感冒死亡的人数已达三千多人，比去年同期增加了一倍多。
