Title: Premier Zhou Receives the Ceylon Civil Aviation Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council received this afternoon the entire Ceylon Civil Aviation Delegation headed by Maitripala Senanayake, Minister of Transport and Public Works of Ceylon.

Present at the reception were Wang Shoudao, Minister of Communications; Zhao Guangyuan, Acting Director of the Civil Aviation Administration; Chen Ruiguang, Deputy Director of the Civil Aviation Administration; He Ying, Deputy Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Also present was Ceylon's Ambassador to China, Gooneratne.

The delegation of seven members came to China at the invitation of Wang Shoudao, Minister of Communications of our country, to discuss issues related to the opening of civil aviation routes between China and Ceylon and to pay a friendly visit. The delegation arrived in Beijing by train yesterday afternoon. (Attached picture)

### 周总理接见锡兰民用航空代表团

1959-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见锡兰民用航空代表团
    新华社21日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天下午接见了以锡兰运输工程部长迈特里帕拉·森纳那亚克为首的锡兰民用航空代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有，交通部长王首道，民航局代理局长赵光远、副局长陈瑞光，外交部第一亚洲司副司长何英等。
    在座的还有锡兰驻华大使高伯拉瓦。
    代表团一行七人，是应我国交通部部长王首道的邀请，来我国商谈有关中锡两国民用航空通航问题并进行友好访问的。代表团于昨日下午乘火车到达北京。（附图片）
