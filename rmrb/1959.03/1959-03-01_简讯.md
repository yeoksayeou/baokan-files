    Brief News
    Thailand Announces Lifting of Blockade on Thai-Cambodian Border
    Bangkok Dispatch: According to Thai radio broadcasts, the Thai Ministry of Interior officially announced the lifting of the blockade on the Thai-Cambodian border on the 27th.
    Under the instigation of the United States, relations between Thailand and Cambodia became strained last year, and Thai authorities announced the blockade of the Thai-Cambodian border on November 25th of last year.
    Cambodian Parliament Condemns Treasonous Activities of Chau Musket
    Phnom Penh Dispatch: The Cambodian National Assembly unanimously passed a motion on the 27th condemning the treasonous activities of Chau Musket and demanding that he be executed.
    The National Assembly also decided to revoke the parliamentary qualifications of Slapeau and Thong Son because they were implicated in Chau Musket's treasonous conspiracy.

### 简讯

1959-03-01
第4版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    泰国宣布解除对泰柬边境的封锁
    曼谷讯：据泰国电台广播，泰国内政部27日正式宣布解除对泰国和柬埔寨边境的封锁。
    在美国的挑拨下，泰国和柬埔寨的关系在去年一度趋于紧张，泰国当局在去年11月25日宣布了封锁泰柬边境。
    柬议会谴责琼·莫舒皮的叛国活动
    金边讯：柬埔寨国民议会27日一致通过一项关于谴责琼·莫舒皮的叛国活动的动议，并且要求将他处死。
    国民议会同时决定撤销斯拉波和通松的议员资格，因为他们同琼·莫舒皮的叛国阴谋有牵连。
