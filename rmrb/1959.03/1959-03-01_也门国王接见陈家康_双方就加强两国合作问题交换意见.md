Title: Yemen King Receives Chen Jiakang, Both Sides Exchange Views on Strengthening Bilateral Cooperation

Author: 
Date: 1959-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Taiz, 27th -- Yemen King Imam Ahmad received Chen Jiakang, the People’s Republic of China’s Envoy to Yemen, on the afternoon of the 27th.

The two sides exchanged views on conducting economic cooperation between the two countries and further strengthening friendship between the two countries.

Envoy Chen Jiakang had met with Yemen Crown Prince Badr in Sana on February 19.

### 也门国王接见陈家康  双方就加强两国合作问题交换意见

1959-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    也门国王接见陈家康
    双方就加强两国合作问题交换意见
    新华社塔兹27日电　也门国王伊马姆·艾哈迈德27日下午接见了中华人民共和国驻也门公使陈家康。
    双方就两国间进行经济合作以及进一步加强两国间的友谊问题交换了意见。
    陈家康公使曾经在2月19日在萨那会见了也门王太子巴德尔。
