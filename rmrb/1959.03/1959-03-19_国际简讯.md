International Briefs

    Indian President Prasad Visits Siem Reap
    Phnom Penh dispatch: Indian President Prasad and his entourage, accompanied by Prince Sihanouk, visited Siem Reap on the 16th and 17th. The residents of Siem Reap warmly welcomed the distinguished Indian guests.
    800 Striking Australian Miners Achieve Victory
    Sydney dispatch: Eight hundred miners at the Broken Hill mine in Australia achieved victory in a strike that lasted more than ten days. The strike forced the management to rehire a miner who had been dismissed due to illness.

### 国际简讯

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    印总统普拉沙德访问暹粒
    金边讯：印度总统普拉沙德和他的随行人员在西哈努克亲王陪同下，在16日和17日访问了暹粒。暹粒居民热烈欢迎了印度贵宾。
    澳八百名罢工矿工获得胜利
    悉尼讯：澳大利亚布洛根希尔矿场的八百名矿工在一次持续十多天的罢工中取得胜利。这次罢工迫使资方重新雇用一个因病而被辞退的矿工。
