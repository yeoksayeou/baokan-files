Title: Promoting a Production Upsurge Through "Four Grasps" and "Eight Checks": Qinghai Organizes Inspection Teams to Go Deep into Factories
Author:
Date: 1959-03-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xining, 1st. The Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China recently organized an industrial and transportation work inspection team of 2,500 people. Starting from February 28, they have been going to factories and enterprises in the Xining area to inspect production, help factories and enterprises summarize and exchange experiences from the Great Leap Forward in production in 1958, promote a high tide of industrial production, and ensure the completion of the first quarter plan.

The inspection team is headed by Zhang Guosheng, Secretary of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and includes Vice Governor Gao Keting, leading cadres and general cadres of the industrial and transportation systems, and factory workers. There are more than ten sub-teams under the general team. The inspection methods mainly involve "Four Grasps" and "Eight Checks." The Four Grasps are: grasp ideology, grasp production, grasp livelihood, and grasp enterprise management. The Eight Checks are: check ideology, check plans, check measures, check coordination, check quality, check conservation, check drive, and check safety. The inspection will last for one and a half months.

### 通过“四抓”“八查”促进生产高潮  青海组织检查团深入工厂

1959-03-02
第2版()
专栏：

    通过“四抓”“八查”促进生产高潮
    青海组织检查团深入工厂
    新华社西宁1日电　中共青海省委最近组织二千五百人的工业交通工作检查团，从2月28日起陆续到西宁地区各工厂企业检查生产情况，帮助工厂企业总结和交流1958年生产大跃进的经验，促进工业生产高潮，保证完成第一季度计划。
    检查团由中共青海省委书记处书记张国声担任团长，成员包括副省长高克亭，工业交通系统的领导干部、一般干部和工厂工人，总团下面设十多个分团。检查的方法主要是“四抓”、“八查”，四抓是：抓思想、抓生产、抓生活、抓企业管理；八查是：查思想、查计划、查措施、查协作、查质量、查节约、查干劲、查安全。检查的时间为一个半月。
