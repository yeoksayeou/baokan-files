Title: New Economic Cooperation Agreement Signed Between the Soviet Union and North Korea; Soviet Union to Provide North Korea with 500 Million Rubles Worth of Machinery and Equipment
Author: 
Date: 1959-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 17th -- TASS reports: The Soviet Union and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea signed an economic cooperation agreement in Moscow on the 17th. According to the agreement, the Soviet Union will provide North Korea with various machinery, equipment, and technical assistance worth 500 million rubles to assist the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in building a thermal power plant with a generating capacity of 200,000 kilowatts, a factory producing ammonia and vinyl chloride, a flax mill and a woolen mill, as well as expanding the Kim Chaek Iron and Steel Works and the Pyongyang Silk Weaving Factory.

The agreement stipulates that the Korean side will provide the Soviet Union with its usual exports to repay for these equipment and technical assistance.

The Soviet Union will provide the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, free of charge, with process specifications and other technical documents needed to organize the production of the aforementioned enterprises.

### 苏朝签订新的经济合作协定  苏联将向朝鲜提供价值五亿卢布机器设备

1959-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏朝签订新的经济合作协定
    苏联将向朝鲜提供价值五亿卢布机器设备
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国17日在莫斯科签订了一项两国经济合作协定。根据协定，苏联将向朝鲜提供价值五亿卢布的各种机器设备和技术帮助，以协助朝鲜民主主义人民共和国建设一座发电能力为二十万瓩的火力发电站、生产氨和氯乙烯基的工厂、麻纺厂和毛织厂，以及扩建金策制铁所和平壤丝织厂。
    协定规定，朝鲜方面将向苏联提供它通常的输出品来偿付这些设备和技术帮助。
    苏联无偿地把为组织上述企业的生产所需要的工艺程序说明书和其它技术文件等交给朝鲜民主主义人民共和国。
