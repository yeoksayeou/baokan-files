Title: Personages from All Circles in Beijing, Shanghai, and Nanjing Commemorate the 34th Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Death
Author:
Date: 1959-03-13
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, March 12th: Cai Tingkai, Zhang Zhizhong, and Xiong Kewu, Vice Chairmen of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, along with Shao Lizi, Chen Shaoxian, Chen Qiyuan, Jiang Guangnai, Weng Wenhao, Liu Fei, He Guiyan, and other members of the Central Committee and various committees of the RCCK, totaling more than 60 people, went to the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall in Biyun Temple, Western Hills, this morning to pay their respects and presented flower baskets to the portrait of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. He Xiangning, Vice Chairman of the RCCK, who is currently in Guangzhou, also sent people to present flower baskets before Mr. Sun Yat-sen's portrait. After the memorial ceremony, everyone also visited Mr. Sun Yat-sen's cenotaph and the exhibition room of his revolutionary achievements.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 12th: Leading figures from the Party, government, democratic parties, and people's organizations in Shanghai paid their respects at the Former Residence of Sun Yat-sen on Xiangshan Road at 8:30 this morning and presented wreaths to commemorate the 34th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's death.

Lin Boqu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, who is currently inspecting Shanghai, also went to the Former Residence of Sun Yat-sen today to present a wreath.

The leading figures from the Party, government, democratic parties, and people's organizations in Shanghai who paid their respects at the Former Residence of Sun Yat-sen today included: Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, and Mayor of Shanghai; Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee; Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Jin Zhonghua and Sheng Pihua, Deputy Mayors of Shanghai; Zhao Zukang, Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the RCCK and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Hu Juewen, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC; and representatives of local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, March 12th: Today is the 34th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's death. More than 30 representatives from the Jiangsu Province and Nanjing Municipal People's Committee, the Jiangsu Province and Nanjing Municipal Committees of the CPPCC, the Jiangsu Province and Nanjing Municipal local organizations of various democratic parties, and various people's organizations went to Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum this morning to pay their respects at Mr. Sun's tomb and presented wreaths in front of the mausoleum.

### 北京上海南京各界人士  纪念孙中山逝世34周年

1959-03-13
第7版()
专栏：

    北京上海南京各界人士
    纪念孙中山逝世34周年
    新华社12日讯　今天是孙中山先生逝世三十四周年纪念日，中国国民党革命委员会副主席蔡廷锴、张治中、熊克武，民革中央委员和各委员会委员邵力子、陈劭先、陈其瑗、蒋光鼐、翁文灏、刘斐、贺贵严等六十多人，在上午前往西山碧云寺晋谒孙中山纪念堂，并向孙中山先生遗像献花篮。现在广州的民革副主席何香凝，也派人在孙中山先生遗像前献了花篮。纪念仪式结束后，大家还参观了孙中山先生衣冠冢和生平革命事迹展览室。
    新华社上海12日电　上海市党、政府和各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，今天上午八时半在香山路瞻谒中山故居，并且献了花圈，纪念孙中山先生逝世三十四周年。
    正在上海视察的中共中央政治局委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠，今天也前往中山故居献了花圈。
    今天瞻谒中山故居的上海市党、政府和各民主党派、人民团体的负责人有：中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施，中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，上海市副市长金仲华、盛丕华、民革上海市委员会主任委员、上海市副市长赵祖康，政协上海市委员会副主席胡厥文，以及各民主党派、各人民团体地方组织的代表等。
    新华社南京12日电　今天是孙中山先生逝世三十四年纪念日，江苏省及南京市人民委员会、政协江苏省及南京市委员会、各民主党派江苏省及南京市地方组织以及各人民团体的代表三十多人，在今日上午前往中山陵晋谒孙先生陵墓，并各在陵前献了花圈。
