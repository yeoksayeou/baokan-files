Title: Asanuma Inejiro and Others Visit Zhang Xiruo
Author:
Date: 1959-03-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th March: Asanuma Inejiro, head of the Japan Socialist Party delegation to China, and all members of the delegation, visited Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, this morning.

Present at the meeting were Zhou Gengsheng, Vice President of the Institute of Foreign Affairs, and relevant responsible persons Wu Maosun, Xiao Xiangqian, Xie Nanguang, Yang Chunsong, and others.

The Japan Socialist Party delegation to China visited the Summer Palace yesterday.

### 浅沼稻次郎等访问张奚若

1959-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

    浅沼稻次郎等访问张奚若
    新华社9日讯　日本社会党访华代表团团长浅沼稻次郎和代表团全体人员今天上午访问了中国人民外交学会会长张奚若。
    会见时在座的，有外交学会副会长周鲠生和有关负责人吴茂荪、萧向前、谢南光、杨春松等。
    日本社会党访华代表团昨天游览了颐和园。
