Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live Coverage of Capital's "March 8th" Commemoration Meeting Today
Author: 
Date: 1959-03-07
Page: 6
	Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Women from all walks of life in the capital will hold a gathering on the morning of the 7th to commemorate International Women's Day "March 8th." The Central People's Broadcasting Station will begin broadcasting live coverage of the commemoration meeting using its first program starting at 9:20 a.m. on the 7th. Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will also broadcast simultaneously.

### 中央人民广播电台今转播首都“三八”纪念大会实况

1959-03-07
第6版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台今转播首都“三八”纪念大会实况
    新华社6日讯　首都各界妇女将在7日上午举行集会，纪念“三八”国际妇女节。中央人民广播电台将从7日上午九点二十分开始，用第一种节目转播纪念大会的实况。北京人民广播电台也同时转播。
