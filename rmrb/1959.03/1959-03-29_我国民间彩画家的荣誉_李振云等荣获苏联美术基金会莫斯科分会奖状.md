Title: Honors for Chinese Folk Painters: Li Zhenyun and Others Awarded Certificates by the Moscow Branch of the Soviet Art Fund
Author:
Date: 1959-03-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th:  Li Zhenyun and Zhao Quanfu, two Chinese folk painters with over 30 years of experience in painting beams and rafters, received certificates from the Moscow Branch of the Soviet Art Fund and the 1957 annual prize from the Moscow Union of Soviet Artists today at the headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in Beijing.  They are painters in the classical architecture team of the Beijing Municipal Real Estate Administration Bureau's Construction Engineering Company and were awarded for their participation in the painted decorations of the "Beijing Restaurant" in Moscow in 1957.

The award ceremony was presided over by Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.  Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, presented the certificates and prizes on behalf of the Soviet Art Fund and read out a congratulatory letter from the Moscow United Decorative Arts Factory.

Representatives from the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Chinese Artists Association, and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association also attended the award ceremony.

### 我国民间彩画家的荣誉  李振云等荣获苏联美术基金会莫斯科分会奖状

1959-03-29
第2版()
专栏：

    我国民间彩画家的荣誉
    李振云等荣获苏联美术基金会莫斯科分会奖状
    新华社28日讯　我国两位有三十多年画栋经验的民间彩画家李振云、赵全福，今天在北京中苏友好协会总会接受了苏联美术基金会莫斯科分会的奖状和莫斯科苏联美术家协会1957年年度奖品。他们是北京市房地产管理局修建工程公司古典建筑队的画工，是因为1957年参加修建莫斯科“北京饭店”的彩画装饰工作而获奖的。
    授奖仪式由中苏友好协会副秘书长戈宝权主持。苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫代授奖状和奖品，并宣读了苏联莫斯科装饰艺术联合工厂的贺信。
    参加授奖仪式的还有对外文化联络委员会、中国美术家协会和北京市中苏友好协会的代表。
