    Brief News
    Indian Engineers Go to Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia
    New Delhi Dispatch: The Indian government is successively sending Indian engineers to the Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia to participate in preparing detailed reports on the construction of heavy machine manufacturing plants, forging and casting factories, and coal mining machine factories in India. These projects are being built with assistance from the Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia.
    Soviet High Dam Expert Delegation Arrives in Cairo
    Cairo Dispatch: A Soviet high dam expert delegation, led by Vasily Komzin, director of the Lenin Volga Hydroelectric Power Station, arrived in Cairo on the 10th.
    This expert delegation came to the United Arab Republic to conduct final research on the detailed implementation plan for the first phase of the Soviet Union's construction of the Aswan High Dam in the United Arab Republic.

    简讯
    印度工程师赴苏捷
    新德里讯　印度政府正陆续派遣印度工程师前往苏联和捷克斯洛伐克，参加准备关于在印度建设重型机器制造厂、锻铸工厂和采煤机器工厂的详细报告。这些工程是由苏联和捷克斯洛伐克援助建设的。
    苏联高水坝专家代表团抵开罗
    开罗讯　苏联高水坝专家代表团10日在列宁伏尔加水电站站长瓦西里·科姆津的率领下到达开罗。
    这个专家代表团来到阿联，是为了对苏联在阿联建设阿斯旺高水坝第一期工程的详细执行计划进行最后的研究。
