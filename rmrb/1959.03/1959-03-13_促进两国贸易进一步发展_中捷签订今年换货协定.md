Title: China and Czechoslovakia Sign This Year's Goods Exchange Agreement to Further Promote Trade Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-03-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 12th: A goods exchange and payment agreement for 1959 between China and Czechoslovakia was signed in Prague on the 9th.

According to the agreement, the total value of goods supplied by both sides in 1959 will increase compared to last year. China will significantly increase its supply of rice, cotton, feed, molybdenum concentrate, and mercury to Czechoslovakia, and will export wheat to Czechoslovakia for the first time. Other goods provided by China to Czechoslovakia include pig iron, iron sand, tungsten concentrate, tin, soybeans, oils and fats, oilseeds, hides and furs, silk, food, tea, and tobacco. Czechoslovakia mainly supplies China with industrial products. Among them, complete sets of industrial equipment, especially power generation equipment, will increase. The supply of heavy-duty machine tools and small hydropower station equipment suitable for rural areas will see a significant increase. In addition, Czechoslovakia will also supply China with forging and pressing equipment, diesel engines, electric motors, blowers, tractors, various instruments, and chemical raw materials. The agreement was signed on the Chinese side by Li Qiang, head of the Chinese government trade delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and on the Czechoslovak side by Fr. Krajcir, head of the Czechoslovak government trade delegation and Minister of Foreign Trade.

Cao Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Jia Shi, Deputy Head of the Chinese government trade delegation, Grigol, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Czechoslovakia, Hamouz, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, and others attended today's signing ceremony.

### 促进两国贸易进一步发展  中捷签订今年换货协定

1959-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    促进两国贸易进一步发展
    中捷签订今年换货协定
    新华社布拉格12日电　中国、捷克斯洛伐克1959年换货和支付协定9日在布拉格签字。
    根据协定，1959年双方供应货物的总额将比去年有所增加。中国供应捷克斯洛伐克的大米、棉花、饲料、钼砂和水银的数量将有较大增加，并将首次向捷克斯洛伐克输出小麦。中国向捷克斯洛伐克提供的货物还有生铁、铁砂、钨砂、锡、大豆、油脂、油料、皮毛、丝绸、食品、茶叶和烟草等。捷克斯洛伐克供应中国的主要是工业产品。其中，成套工业设备，特别是发电设备将有增加。重型机床和适用于农村的小型水力发电站设备的供应将有显著增长。此外，还将供应中国以锻压设备、柴油机、电动机、鼓风机、拖拉机、各种仪器和化工原料等。在协定上签字的，中国方面是中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长李强，捷克斯洛伐克方面是捷克斯洛伐克政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部部长弗·克拉伊契尔。参加今天的签字仪式的有中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛、中国政府贸易代表团副团长贾石、捷克斯洛伐克外交部第一副部长格里哥尔、对外贸易部第一副部长哈莫乌兹等人。
