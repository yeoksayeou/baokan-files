Title: Castro Distributes Land to Farmers in Western Cuba
Author:
Date: 1959-03-05
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Havana: Cuban Prime Minister Castro went to Pinar del Río province in western Cuba on March 1st to distribute 25,000 acres of land to farmers.
    It is reported that the land distributed to farmers this time was purchased by the Cuban government from the Cuban Land Company and the Pérez Vila brothers for 430,000 US dollars. Of the 25,000 acres of land, more than 8,000 acres are suitable for growing tobacco, and the rest is forest and grassland. According to the land reform law of the Cuban Provisional Government, 150,000 farmers in Oriente Province had already been allocated land in February. That land distribution work was carried out by a task force led by Castro.

### 卡斯特罗到古巴西部把土地分给农民

1959-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    卡斯特罗到古巴西部把土地分给农民
    据新华社讯　哈瓦那消息：古巴总理卡斯特罗1日到古巴西部比那尔德里奥省，把二万五千英亩土地分给农民。
    据报道，这次分给农民的土地，是古巴政府用四十三万美元向古巴土地公司和培雷兹·维拉特兄弟购买的。在二万五千英亩土地中，有八千多亩适宜于种烟草，其余是森林地和草地。根据古巴临时政府的土地改革法，奥连特省十五万农民已经在2月间分到了土地。那次分配土地的工作是由卡斯特罗率领的一支工作队进行的。
