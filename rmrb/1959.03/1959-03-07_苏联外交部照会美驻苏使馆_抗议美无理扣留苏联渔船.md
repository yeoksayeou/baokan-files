Title: Soviet Foreign Ministry Note to U.S. Embassy in the Soviet Union Protesting Unreasonable Detention of Soviet Fishing Vessels by the U.S.

Author:

Date: 1959-03-07
Page: 4

    Soviet Foreign Ministry Note to U.S. Embassy in the Soviet Union
    Protesting Unreasonable Detention of Soviet Fishing Vessels by the U.S.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 5th: TASS Moscow, March 5th—The Soviet Foreign Ministry handed a note to the U.S. Embassy in Moscow, protesting the unreasonable detention and inspection of the Soviet fishing vessel "New Russia" by U.S. warships.
    The note stated that the Soviet fishing vessel "New Russia," fishing in the high seas of the North Atlantic, did not engage in any behavior that damaged undersea cables or disrupted transatlantic cables. The note pointed out that reports appearing in American newspapers concerning this matter are purely fabricated.
    The note pointed out that all attempts to detain the Soviet fishing vessel were provocative in nature.
    The note demanded that the U.S. government take all necessary measures to ensure that similar unreasonable actions do not occur in the future.

### 苏联外交部照会美驻苏使馆  抗议美无理扣留苏联渔船

1959-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联外交部照会美驻苏使馆
    抗议美无理扣留苏联渔船
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科5日讯：苏联外交部交给美国驻莫斯科大使馆一份照会，抗议美国军舰无理扣留和检查苏联渔船“新俄罗斯克号”。
    照会中说，在大西洋北部公海上捕鱼的苏联渔船“新俄罗斯克号”并没有任何损坏海底电线或是破坏横断大西洋的电缆的行为。照会指出，美国报纸上出现的有关这个问题的报道纯系捏造。
    照会指出，扣留苏联渔船的全部企图都具有挑衅的目的。
    照会要求美国政府采取一切必要的措施，保证今后不再发生类似的无理行动。
