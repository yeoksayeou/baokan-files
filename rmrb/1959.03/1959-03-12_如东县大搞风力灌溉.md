    Rudong County Vigorously Develops Wind-Powered Irrigation
    Rudong County, Jiangsu Province, is vigorously developing wind-powered irrigation. The county plans to use wind power to irrigate all 125,000 mu of paddy fields this year. From the beginning of January to the present, the county has already produced more than 500 windmills and expects to complete the original plan of 4,000 to 6,000 units by early April.
    Rudong County estimates that using wind-powered irrigation this year can save 3.4 million labor days.　　　　　　　　　　　(Xu Fang)

### 如东县大搞风力灌溉
许芳
1959-03-12
第4版()
专栏：读者中来

    如东县大搞风力灌溉
    江苏如东县大力发展风力灌溉，全县计划今年有十二万五千亩水稻田全部利用风力灌溉。1月上旬开始到目前，全县已制成风车五百多台，预计4月上旬可完成原计划四千到六千台的任务。
    如东县估计，今年利用风力灌溉，可以节省出三百四十万个劳动日。　　　　　　　　　　　（许芳）
