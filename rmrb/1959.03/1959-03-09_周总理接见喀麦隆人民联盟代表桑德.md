Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Cameroon People's Union Representative Sande
Author:
Date: 1959-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Premier Zhou Enlai received Jean-Paul Sande, representative of the Cameroon People's Union, this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity; Zhou Gengsheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Wang Mingyuan, Acting Secretary-General of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity. (Photo attached)

### 周总理接见喀麦隆人民联盟代表桑德

1959-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见喀麦隆人民联盟代表桑德
    新华社8日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了喀麦隆人民联盟代表让·保罗·桑德。
    接见时在座的有中国亚非团结委员会副主席李德全，中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生，外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华，中国亚非团结委员会代理秘书长王明远。（附图片）
    周恩来总理和喀麦隆人民联盟代表让·保罗·桑德亲切交谈。新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
