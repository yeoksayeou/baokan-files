Title: Hungary Passes Family Allowance Decree
Author:
Date: 1959-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest. The Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, at its meeting on the 26th, passed the Labor Department's decree on revising family allowances. According to the new decree, starting from April, family allowances for families with three or more children, or children's allowances for single working women, will be generally increased by one to two times.

### 匈通过家庭补助法令

1959-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    匈通过家庭补助法令
    据新华社布达佩斯讯　匈牙利工农革命政府在26日举行的会议上，通过了劳动部关于修改家庭补助金的法令。根据新法令，有三个或三个以上的子女的家庭补助金或单独谋生的妇女的小孩补助金，从4月份开始，都将普遍提高一、两倍。
