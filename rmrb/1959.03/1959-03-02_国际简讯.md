International Briefs

    British Cast Iron Production Decreased Last Year
    London Dispatch: According to the latest figures released on the 27th by the British Iron and Steel Federation, Britain's cast iron production in 1958 was 3,403,200 tons, a decrease of 8% compared to 1957.
    
    U.S. Forbids Greece to Export Wheat
    Athens Dispatch: It is reported that West Germany and other countries recently wanted to buy 300,000 tons of wheat from Greece. However, the United States forbids Greece to export wheat.

### 国际简讯

1959-03-02
第5版(国际简讯)
专栏：

    国际简讯
    英去年铸铁生产下降
    伦敦讯：根据英国钢铁联合委员会27日发表的最新数字，1958年英国的铸铁产量是三百四十万三千二百吨，比1957年下降了8％。
  美国禁止希腊输出小麦
    雅典讯：据报道，西德等国最近要向希腊购买三十万吨小麦。但是，美国禁止希腊输出小麦。
