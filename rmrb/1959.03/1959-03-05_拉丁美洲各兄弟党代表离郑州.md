Title: Latin American Fraternal Party Representatives Leave Zhengzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 4th: Luis Corvalán, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Chile; Pompeyo Márquez, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Venezuela; Alonso Ojeda Olaechea, member of the Political Bureau; Pedro Ortega Díaz, member of the Political Bureau; Guillermo García Ponce, member of the Central Committee; Gilberto Vieira, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Colombia; Joaquín Moreno Guas, member of the Political Bureau; Raúl Acosta, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Peru; Jorge del Prado, member of the Central Committee; Elías Muñoz, member of the Central Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Ecuador; Humberto Ramírez, member of the Central Political Committee of the Communist Party of Bolivia; Felipe Pescador Roth, Secretary of the Central Finance Committee of the Communist Party of Argentina; Wilfredo Velázquez , Organizational Secretary of Las Villas Provincial Committee of the Popular Socialist Party of Cuba; and representatives of the Communist Parties of Brazil, Paraguay, Costa Rica's People's Vanguard Party, and Panama's People's Party, left Zhengzhou by special plane this morning after visiting Zhengzhou, heading to Beijing and Shanghai in two groups.

Wu Zhipu, First Secretary of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhao Wenfu and Li Li, Secretaries of the Provincial Committee Secretariat, and Wang Lizhi, First Secretary of the Zhengzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, were at the airport to see them off.

Yesterday afternoon, representatives of the fraternal parties, accompanied by Li Li, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Liu Bi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Zhengzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, visited the state-owned Zhengzhou No. 3 Cotton Textile Factory and were warmly welcomed by the factory leaders and workers.

### 拉丁美洲各兄弟党代表离郑州

1959-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲各兄弟党代表离郑州
    新华社郑州4日电　智利共产党总书记路易斯·科尔巴兰，委内瑞拉共产党中央委员会书记处书记庞佩约·马凯斯、政治局委员阿伦索·奥里达·奥拉耶切阿、政治局委员皮德罗·欧尔特卡·吉阿斯、中央委员吉耶尔莫·古阿尔丁，哥伦比亚共产党总书记希尔伯托·维埃拉、政治局委员侯阿金·毛列诺·古阿斯，秘鲁共产党总书记腊乌尔·阿哥斯塔、中央委员霍尔海·德尔·布腊多，厄瓜多尔共产党中央执行委员会委员厄利阿斯·穆纽斯，玻利维亚共产党中央政治委员会委员翁伯托·拉米雷斯，阿根廷共产党中央财务委员会书记弗利贝·培斯罗特尼克，古巴人民社会党拉斯维利亚斯省委组织书记威尔夫利多·维拉斯克斯，巴西共产党、巴拉圭共产党、哥斯达黎加人民先锋党、巴拿马人民党的代表，在郑州参观以后，今天上午乘专机离开郑州，分两路前往北京和上海。
    到机场欢送的有中共河南省委第一书记吴芝圃，省委书记处书记赵文甫、李立和中共郑州市委第一书记王黎之等。
    各兄弟党代表昨天下午由中共河南省委书记处书记李立、中共郑州市委书记处书记刘必陪同，参观了国营郑州第三棉纺织厂，受到这个厂的负责人和职工们的热烈欢迎。
