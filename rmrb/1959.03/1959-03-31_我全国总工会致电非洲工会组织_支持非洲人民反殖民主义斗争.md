Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Telegrams to African Trade Union Organizations in Support of the African People's Struggle Against Colonialism
Author:
Date: 1959-03-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 29th March: The large-scale struggle of the African people for national independence and against colonial rule has received the resolute support of the Chinese people. In a telegram of support to the Nyasaland Trade Union Congress on the 25th, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions stated: "The era of colonialists doing as they please is over. We firmly believe that your struggle for national independence, with the support of people all over the world, will surely achieve final victory."

On the 28th, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions also sent telegrams and letters to trade union organizations such as the Northern Rhodesia Trade Union Congress, the Kenya Federation of Labor, the Uganda Trade Union Congress, the Tanganyika Federation of Labor, and the South African Trade Union Congress. It pointed out that the recent struggles of the Central African people for national independence, against colonial rule, and racial oppression, along with the ongoing anti-colonial struggles in other African regions, have ignited a raging fire of anti-colonialism throughout Africa, shaking the last colonial stronghold of imperialism in the world. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions expressed its deep respect to the Central African people, and stated: "The Chinese people will always stand with the African people and fight against imperialist aggression and to end the colonial system."

### 我全国总工会致电非洲工会组织  支持非洲人民反殖民主义斗争

1959-03-31
第6版()
专栏：

    我全国总工会致电非洲工会组织
    支持非洲人民反殖民主义斗争
    新华社29日讯　非洲人民展开的大规模的争取民族独立、反对殖民统治的斗争得到中国人民的坚决支持。中华全国总工会25日在致尼亚萨兰工会大会的支援电报中说：“殖民主义者为所欲为的时代已经过去了。我们深信你们争取民族独立的斗争，在全世界人民的支持下，必将取得最后胜利。”
    28日，中华全国总工会又打电报和写信给北罗得西亚工会大会、怯尼亚劳工联合会、乌干达工会大会、坦噶尼喀劳工联合会和南非工会大会等工会组织，指出最近中非人民争取民族独立、反对殖民统治和种族压迫的斗争，与其他非洲地区正在进行的反殖民主义斗争使整个非洲燃烧起反殖民主义斗争的熊熊烈火，动摇了帝国主义在世界上的最后殖民阵地。中华全国总工会对中非人民表示深切的敬意，并且说：“中国人民将永远和非洲人民站在一起，为反对帝国主义侵略和结束殖民主义制度而奋斗。”
