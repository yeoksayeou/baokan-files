Title: At the Invitation of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Aidit Leads Delegation to Visit Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1959-03-29
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 28th: A delegation of the Indonesian Communist Party, headed by General Secretary Aidit, arrived in Hanoi by plane today.

　　The delegation, at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, will pay a week-long visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

　　Welcoming the delegation at the airport were: Le Duan, Truong Chinh, Nguyen Duy Trinh, and Pham Hung, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party; Ung Van Khiem, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Tran Danh Tuyen, Secretary of the Hanoi Municipal Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party; Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee; representatives of the National Assembly and various people's organizations.

　　Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Mo Yanzhong, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, and Sunarjo, Consul General of Indonesia in Vietnam.

### 应越南劳动党中央的邀请  艾地率代表团到河内访问

1959-03-29
第3版()
专栏：

    应越南劳动党中央的邀请
    艾地率代表团到河内访问
    新华社河内28日电　以印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地为首的印度尼西亚共产党代表团今天乘飞机到达河内。
    代表团应越南劳动党中央的邀请，将在越南民主共和国进行为时一周的参观访问。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有：越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员黎笋、长征、阮维祯、范雄，越南民主共和国外交部副部长雍文谦，越南劳动党河内市委员会书记陈名宣，河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴，国会代表和各人民团体的代表。
    到机场欢迎代表团的，还有中国驻越南大使馆临时代办莫燕忠和印度尼西亚驻越南总领事苏纳约。
