Title: Shenyang Implements Sea, Land, and Air Intermodal Transport

Author: 
Date: 1959-03-31
Page: 3

Column: Extensive Transportation Network

    The Shenyang Railway Bureau and departments of civil aviation, shipping, and highway transportation have reached an agreement to organize passenger intermodal transport by sea, land, and air. Now, at the seaports of Dalian, Yantai, Qingdao, and Longkou, and at the main stations of the Shenyang and Jinan Railway Bureaus, passengers can purchase sea-land intermodal tickets and check luggage between these points. In addition, from the airports of Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Lanzhou, Chongqing, Wuhan, Kunming, and other cities, one can directly buy tickets to the Dalian Station, Anshan Station, Benxi Station, and Wafangdian Station of the Shenyang Railway Bureau. At these railway stations, one can also purchase airplane tickets to the aforementioned airports for taking flights from Shenyang.
    On the highway, from the main stations on the Shenyang-Andong line, one can purchase highway bus tickets via Andong Station to counties such as Zhuanghe and Xiuyan. Similarly, at the bus stations on this highway, one can also purchase train tickets.

### 沈阳实行海陆空联运

1959-03-31
第3版()
专栏：四通八达

    沈阳实行海陆空联运
    沈阳铁路局和民航、航运、公路等部门达成协议，组织海、陆、空的旅客联运工作。现在，在海上大连、烟台、青岛、龙口港，铁路上沈阳、济南两铁路局的主要站，都可以相互间购得海陆联运客票，并能承运行李。此外，从北京、上海、广州、成都、兰州、重庆、武汉、昆明等航空站能直接买到阳沈铁路局的大连站、鞍山站、本溪站、瓦房店站等站的客票，在这些火车站也能购到上述航空站的飞机票到沈阳搭乘飞机。
    在公路上，从沈（阳）安（东）线上的主要站，可以购得经由安东站去庄河、岫岩等县的公路汽车票，同样在这条公路上的汽车站也可以购到火车票。
