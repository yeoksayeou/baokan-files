Title: Our Country's Cultural Delegation Sets Off for Invited Visit to the Republic of Iraq
Author:
Date: 1959-03-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, March 5th:

    Our country's cultural delegation, consisting of twelve members, departed from Beijing by plane today for a friendly visit to the Republic of Iraq at the invitation of the government of the Republic of Iraq. The head of the delegation is Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Director of the China Islamic Association. The deputy head of the delegation is Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and Vice President of the China-Iraq Friendly Association. The members are Wang Huanxun, Wang Yafan, Yan Jingqing, Ma Jian, Li Guanglin, Hou Yimin, and Jiang Yan.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included Shen Bocun, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and representatives from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Health, the China Peace Committee, the China Islamic Association, the Chinese Writers Association, and the China-Iraq Friendship Association.

### 应邀访问伊拉克共和国  我国文化代表团启程

1959-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀访问伊拉克共和国
    我国文化代表团启程
    新华社5日讯　应伊拉克共和国政府的邀请去伊拉克共和国进行友好访问的中国文化代表团一行十二人，已经在今天自北京乘飞机启程。代表团团长是中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉，副团长是对外文化联络委员会副主任、中国伊拉克友好协会副会长罗俊。团员有王焕勋、王亚凡、严镜清、马坚、李广林、侯一民、江炎。
    到机场欢送的有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副秘书长申伯纯，对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林，以及全国人民代表大会常务委员会、外交部、卫生部、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国伊斯兰教、中国作家协会、中国伊拉克友好协会等单位的代表。
