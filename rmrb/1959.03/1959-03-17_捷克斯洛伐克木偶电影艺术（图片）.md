Author: 
Date: 1959-03-17
Page: 8

Czechoslovakian Puppet Film Art
	Left picture: A scene from Jiri Trnka's widescreen color puppet film "A Midsummer Night's Dream." In this puppet film, Duke Theseus's castle and Queen Hippolyta of the Amazons are decorated very lavishly.
	Bottom picture: A scene from "A Bouquet of Folk Songs" by children's puppet film director Hermína Týrlová. In this film, the author expresses the joy and hope of children playing, and shows the beauty of the Czechoslovakian countryside and the poetic folk songs and dances.

### 捷克斯洛伐克木偶电影艺术（图片）

1959-03-17
第8版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克木偶电影艺术
    左图：是耶齐·透恩卡的宽银幕彩色木偶片“仲夏夜之梦”的一个镜头。在这部木偶片里，席修斯公爵的城堡和希波丽达·亚玛森女王都装饰得非常华丽。
    下图：是儿童木偶电影编导荷·泰尔洛娃的作品“民歌一束”中的一个镜头。作者用这部影片表现了孩子们游戏时的欢乐和希望，表现了捷克斯洛伐克农村的美景和充满着诗意的民歌、舞蹈。
