Title: Indonesian Communist Party General Secretary Comrade Aidit Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th March: The Indonesian Communist Party delegation, which attended the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, led by the head of the delegation, General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, Comrade Aidit, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon from Moscow. Those who welcomed him at the airport were Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat, Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, and Xiong Fu and Li Qixin, responsible staff members of the organs of the CPC Central Committee. (Attached picture)
    General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, Aidit (left), accompanied by General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Deng Xiaoping, walks out of the airport.

### 印度尼西亚共产党总书记  艾地同志到达北京

1959-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党总书记
    艾地同志到达北京
    新华社12日讯　参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会的印度尼西亚共产党代表团，在团长、印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地率领下，今天下午从莫斯科乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有：中共中央总书记邓小平、书记处候补书记杨尚昆、中央委员刘宁一和中共中央机关负责工作人员熊复、李启新等。（附图片）
    印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地（左）在中共中央总书记邓小平陪同下步出机场。　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
