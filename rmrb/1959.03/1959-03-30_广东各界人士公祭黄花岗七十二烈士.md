Title: Guangdong Personages from All Circles Publicly Commemorate the Seventy-Two Martyrs of Huanghuagang
Author:
Date: 1959-03-30
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 29th. Today is the anniversary of the martyrdom of the Seventy-Two Martyrs of Huanghuagang. Personages from all circles in Guangdong held a public memorial ceremony in front of the tomb of the Huanghuagang martyrs.
    The public memorial ceremony was presided over by Yao Yuping, an elder who participated in the "March 29th" Uprising and Director of the Counselor's Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee. Those accompanying the ceremony included Guo Dihuo, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province; Zhang Boquan, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC); Li Langru, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou; Chen Qiuan, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPPCC; and Tan Tiandu, Deputy Director of the United Front Work Department of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China. During the public memorial ceremony, all stood in silence in front of the martyrs' tomb and presented wreaths to pay their respects.

### 广东各界人士公祭黄花岗七十二烈士

1959-03-30
第6版()
专栏：

    广东各界人士公祭黄花岗七十二烈士
    新华社广州29日电　今天是黄花岗七十二烈士殉难纪念日，广东各界人士在黄花岗烈士墓前举行公祭。
    公祭由曾参加“三·二九”之役的老人、广东省人民委员会参事室主任姚雨平主祭。陪祭人有广东省副省长郭棣活、政协广东省委员会副主席张?村、广州市副市长李朗如，政协广州市委员会副主席陈秋安，以及中共广东省委统战部副部长谭天度等。公祭时，全体在烈士墓前肃立默哀，并献花圈致敬。
