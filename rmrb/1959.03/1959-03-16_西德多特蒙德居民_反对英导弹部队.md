Title: Residents of Dortmund, West Germany, Oppose British Missile Units
Author:
Date: 1959-03-16
Page: 5

    Residents of Dortmund, West Germany,
    Oppose British Missile Units
    (Xinhua News Agency, Dortmund) Peace activists in Dortmund, West Germany, held a demonstration on the 14th called "Europe Against Atomic Death and Atomic Weapons," opposing the stationing of the British 47th Missile Regiment at the Dortmund-Brackel airport. The demonstration began on the evening of the 13th with a "warning vigil" and will continue until the 16th.
    The Dortmund City Council, controlled by the West German Social Democratic Party, also protested the stationing of the British missile unit three miles from the city, which has a population of 625,000.
    The first contingent of one hundred personnel from the British missile unit arrived here in February.

### 西德多特蒙德居民  反对英导弹部队

1959-03-16
第5版()
专栏：

    西德多特蒙德居民
    反对英导弹部队
    据新华社多特蒙德讯　西德多特蒙德的和平战士14日举行了一次“欧洲反对原子死亡和原子武器”示威，反对英国第四十七导弹团驻扎在多特蒙德—布腊克耳机场。这次示威是从13日晚间以举行“警告性守夜”而开始的，这次示威将继续进行到16日。
    在西德社会民主党控制下的多特蒙德市政委员会也表示抗议英国导弹部队驻扎在离这个拥有六十二万五千人口的城市三英里的地方。
    英国导弹部队第一批人员一百人已在2月间到达这里。
