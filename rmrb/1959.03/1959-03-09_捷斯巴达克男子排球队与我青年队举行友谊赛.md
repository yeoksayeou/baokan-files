Title: Spartak Men's Volleyball Team Holds Friendly Match with Chinese Youth Team
Author:
Date: 1959-03-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: The "Spartak" Men's Volleyball Team from Brno, Czechoslovakia, played a friendly match with the Chinese Youth Men's Volleyball Team in Beijing on the 8th. As a result, the home team won three to two. The scores for the five sets were 15-8, 12-15, 15-12, 6-15, and 16-14.

	Subsequently, Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Xiaoping, along with the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušiak, and the head of the visiting team, Mr. Urbanik, walked from the rostrum to the court to shake hands with the players from both sides and take a group photo. The "Spartak" Men's Volleyball Team from Brno will soon visit Wuhan.

### 捷斯巴达克男子排球队与我青年队举行友谊赛

1959-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

    捷斯巴达克男子排球队与我青年队举行友谊赛
    据新华社8日讯　捷克斯洛伐克布尔诺市“斯巴达克”男子排球队8日在北京和中国青年男子排球队进行了一场友谊比赛。结果，主队以三比二获胜。五局的比数是十五比八、十二比十五、十五比十二、六比十五、十六比十四。
    随后，国务院副总理邓小平和捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克、客队领队乌尔班尼克等，由主席台走到球场上同双方队员握手，并合影留念。布尔诺市“斯巴达克”男子排球队即将到武汉访问。
