Title: Supreme Soviet Delegation Arrives in London for Friendly Visit to Britain
Author:
Date: 1959-03-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London, March 13th: According to TASS, a delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, led by Suslov, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet, arrived in London today aboard a "Tu-104" aircraft. They are visiting Britain at the invitation of the Anglo-Soviet Parliamentary Committee. The delegation also includes Soviet Supreme Soviet representatives Ponomarev, Satchukov, and others.

Julius Silverman, Chairman of the Anglo-Soviet Parliamentary Committee, welcomed the Soviet guests at London Airport, saying, "I believe your visit will help establish better mutual understanding, mutual happiness, and peace between the people of our two countries."

In his reply, Suslov conveyed the sincere respect of the Soviet people to the British people and expressed gratitude for the invitation from the Anglo-Soviet Parliamentary Committee.

Suslov said, "Our sincere desire, like that of our government and all the Soviet people in international relations, is to do everything possible to promote the relaxation of international tensions, end the 'Cold War,' and consolidate and develop friendly ties with the British people and the people of other countries."

Suslov said, "We note with satisfaction that in recent times, our two governments have taken certain steps to promote mutual understanding. But only the future will show to what extent these steps to promote mutual understanding will lead to concerted efforts to resolve international problems. It is in this area that the parliaments of our two countries can play a beneficial role in continuing the work begun by the heads of government of our two countries. We hope that in our talks in Britain, we will exchange views on consolidating the interests of peace and on ways and means of resolving pressing international problems."

### 应邀到英国进行友好访问  苏最高苏维埃代表抵伦敦

1959-03-15
第3版()
专栏：

    应邀到英国进行友好访问
    苏最高苏维埃代表抵伦敦
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社伦敦13日讯：由苏联最高苏维埃联盟院外交委员会主席苏斯洛夫率领的一批苏联最高苏维埃代表今天乘“图—一○四”飞机到达伦敦。他们是应英苏议会委员会的邀请到英国进行访问。这批代表中还包括苏联最高苏维埃代表波诺马烈夫、萨丘科夫等人。
    在伦敦机场欢迎苏联客人的英苏议会委员会主席朱利叶斯·锡弗尔曼向苏联客人表示欢迎，他说，我相信你们的访问将有助于建立我们两国人民之间更好的互相谅解、彼此的幸福和和平。
    苏斯洛夫致答词时转达了苏联人民对英国人民衷心的敬意，并对英苏议会委员会的邀请表示感谢。
    苏斯洛夫说，我们真诚的愿望同我国政府和全体苏联人民在国际关系方面的愿望一样，这就是：尽一切力量促进缓和国际紧张局势、结束“冷战”、巩固和发展同英国人民和其它国家人民的友好联系。
    苏斯洛夫说，我们满意地指出，在最近期间，我们两国政府采取了某些促进相互谅解的步骤。但只有将来才会表明，这些促进相互谅解的步骤会在多大程度上导致协同一致地解决国际问题。正是在这方面，我们两国的议会，在继续进行两国政府首脑所开始的事业中，将能够发挥有益的作用。我们希望，在我们将在英国举行的会谈中，将就巩固和平的利益、解决迫切的国际问题的途径和方法交换意见。
