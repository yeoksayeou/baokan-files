Title: German Dramatists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 10th: A four-member delegation of dramatists from the German Democratic Republic, led by Karl Kayser, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China under the 1959 Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Welcoming them at the airport were Li Chao, Acting Secretary-General of the Chinese Dramatists Association, Ma Yanxiang, Deputy Director of the Arts Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, and Wu Xue, Director of the China Youth Art Theater, among others.

### 德戏剧家代表团到京

1959-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    德戏剧家代表团到京
    新华社10日讯　根据1959年文化合作协定前来我国访问的德意志民主共和国戏剧家代表团一行四人，在团长卡尔·凯塞尔率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国戏剧家协会代秘书长李超，文化部艺术事业管理局副局长马彦祥，中国青年艺术剧院院长吴雪等人。
