Title: Second National People's Congress to Convene First Session in April; To Hear Government Work Report, Decide on 1959 National Economic Plan, Elect and Appoint National Leaders; First Session of the Third CPPCC National Committee to Be Held Simultaneously
Author: 
Date: 1959-03-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The first session of the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China and the first session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference are scheduled to convene simultaneously in Beijing on April 17th. The joint meeting of the 107th session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the 53th session of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, held this afternoon, decided to issue a joint notice.
The main agenda of the first session of the Second National People's Congress is to hear the government work report, decide on the 1959 national economic plan, review and approve the 1958 national final accounts and the 1959 national budget, and elect and appoint national leaders.
The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress decided to issue an announcement from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

	Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
	National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference	Joint Notice
	National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
		March 11, 1959
	The Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China is scheduled to convene its first session in Beijing on April 17, 1959. The main agenda of the meeting is to hear the government work report, decide on the 1959 national economic plan, review and approve the 1958 national final accounts and the 1959 national budget, and elect and appoint national leaders.
	The first session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference is scheduled to convene in Beijing on April 17, 1959, simultaneously with the first session of the Second National People's Congress. Deputies to the National People's Congress and members of the National Committee of the CPPCC should register before April 16.
	In order to understand the situation and prepare opinions, NPC deputies and CPPCC members who are able to inspect work before the meeting can inspect work locally or in various places.

### 全国人代会二届首次会议四月召开  听取政府工作报告、决定1959年度国民经济计划、选举和决定国家领导人员  政协第三届全国委员会首次会议同时举行

1959-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人代会二届首次会议四月召开
    听取政府工作报告、决定1959年度国民经济计划、选举和决定国家领导人员
    政协第三届全国委员会首次会议同时举行
    新华社11日讯　中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议和中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第一次会议定于四月十七日在北京同时召开。今天下午举行的全国人民代表大会常务委员会第一百零七次会议和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第五十三次会议联席会议，决定发出联合通知。
    第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的主要议程为听取政府工作报告，决定一九五九年度国民经济计划，审查和批准一九五八年国家决算和一九五九年国家预算，选举和决定国家领导人员。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会决定发布全国人民代表大会常务委员会公告。
  全国人民代表大会常务委员会
  中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会　联合通知
  中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
                              一九五九年三月十一日
    中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会定于一九五九年四月十七日在北京召开第一次会议，会议的主要议程为听取政府工作报告，决定一九五九年度国民经济计划，审查和批准一九五八年国家决算和一九五九年国家预算，选举和决定国家领导人员。
    中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第一次会议定于一九五九年四月十七日在北京和第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议同时召开。全国人民代表大会代表、政协全国委员会委员都应于四月十六日以前报到。
    为了了解情况，准备意见，人大代表、政协委员在会议以前凡能够视察工作的，可以就地或者到各地视察工作。
