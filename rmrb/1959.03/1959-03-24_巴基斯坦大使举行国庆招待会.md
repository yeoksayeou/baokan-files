Title: Pakistani Ambassador Holds National Day Reception
Author: 
Date: 1959-03-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: Pakistani Ambassador to China, Malik, held a reception at the Beijing Hotel tonight to celebrate Pakistan's National Day.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, people from all walks of life in the capital, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were invited to attend the reception.
    In his speech, Vice Premier Chen Yi proposed a toast to the prosperity of Pakistan, the happiness of its people, peace in Asia and the world, and the health of Pakistani President Ayub Khan. In his speech, Ambassador Malik proposed a toast to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and the prosperity of the Chinese people.

### 巴基斯坦大使举行国庆招待会

1959-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦大使举行国庆招待会
    新华社23日讯　巴基斯坦驻华大使马立克今晚在北京饭店举行招待会，庆祝巴基斯坦国庆。
    国务院副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，首都各方面的人士和各国驻华使节，应邀出席了招待会。
    陈毅副总理在致词中，提议为巴基斯坦国家繁荣、人民幸福，为亚洲和世界的和平，为巴基斯坦总统阿尤布汗的健康干杯。马立克大使在致词中，提议为毛泽东主席的健康和中国人民的繁荣干杯。
