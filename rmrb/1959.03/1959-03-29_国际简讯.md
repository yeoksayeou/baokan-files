International Briefs

Indonesian Government Forces Recapture Important City in Southern Tapannuli
    Jakarta News: According to news from Medan, Lieutenant Maha, a news officer of the Indonesian First Military Region, said on the 24th that government forces had recovered all the important cities in the southern part of the Tapannuli region in Central Sumatra.

Omani Nationalists Kill Over Sixty More British Aggression Troops
    Damascus News: The office of the Omani Imam in Damascus announced on the 25th that Omani nationalists had killed over sixty British aggression troops in a recent battle in the Green Mountain area and nearby villages. The battle was launched by British ground forces with the support of British paratroopers and the air force.

French Armed Forces Ministry Requests Additional Military Spending
    Paris News: The French government's Minister of Armed Forces has requested an additional 110 billion francs in military budget.

    If this additional military spending is approved, then France's military expenditure this year will increase from the original 1,576 billion to 1,686 billion.

### 国际简讯

1959-03-29
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    印度尼西亚政府军收复打板奴里南部重要城市
    雅加达讯：据从棉兰发到这里的消息说，印度尼西亚第一军区新闻官员马哈中尉24日说，政府军已经收复了中苏门答腊打板奴里地区南部的所有重要城市。
    阿曼民族主义者又打死六十多名英国侵略军
    大马士革讯：阿曼教长驻大马士革办事处25日宣布，阿曼民族主义者最近在绿山地区和附近村庄进行的一次战斗中打死了六十多名英国侵略军。这次战斗是英国地面部队在英国伞兵部队和空军支援下发动的。
    法武装部队部要求追加军费
    巴黎讯：法国政府武装部队部长要求追加一千一百亿法郎的军事预算。
    如果同意了这项追加军费，那么今年法国军费开支便将从原来的一万五千七百六十亿增加为一万六千八百六十亿。
