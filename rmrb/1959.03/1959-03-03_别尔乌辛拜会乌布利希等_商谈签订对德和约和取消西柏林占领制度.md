Title: Berzin Meets with Ulbricht and Others to Discuss Signing a German Peace Treaty and Abolishing the Occupation Regime in West Berlin
Author:
Date: 1959-03-03
Page: 4

    Berzin Meets with Ulbricht and Others
    To Discuss Signing a German Peace Treaty and Abolishing the Occupation Regime in West Berlin
    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd, Dispatch: According to TASS, on February 28th, the Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Berzin, entrusted by the Soviet government, met the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party, Ulbricht, and the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl, and exchanged views with them on the issues of signing a German peace treaty and abolishing the occupation regime in West Berlin.

### 别尔乌辛拜会乌布利希等  商谈签订对德和约和取消西柏林占领制度

1959-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    别尔乌辛拜会乌布利希等
    商谈签订对德和约和取消西柏林占领制度
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使别尔乌辛2月28日受苏联政府的委托，拜会了德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希和德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥，并且和他们交换了关于签订对德和约和取消西柏林的占领制度问题。
