Title: Zhu Vice-Chairman Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1959-03-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 26th: Zhu De, Vice-Chairman of the People's Republic of China and head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation invited to participate in the 40th anniversary celebrations of the Hungarian Soviet Republic, and delegation member Xi Zhongxun, arrived in Moscow from Kiev at noon on the 26th.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Kirichenko, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the CPSU, Kosygin and Pospelov, alternate members of the Presidium, and Zorin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs. Hungarian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Boldoczki, and Zhang Weilie, Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, also greeted them at the airport.

The Chinese Party and Government Delegation arrived in Kiev from Hungary on the 25th.

### 朱副主席抵莫斯科

1959-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    朱副主席抵莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科26日电　应邀参加匈牙利苏维埃共和国四十周年纪念活动的中国党政代表团团长朱德副主席、团员习仲勋，26日中午由基辅抵达莫斯科。
    到机场欢迎的有，苏共中央主席团委员基里钦科、主席团候补委员柯西金、波斯别洛夫，外交部副部长佐林等。匈牙利驻苏联大使波尔多茨基，中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办张伟烈也到机场迎接。
    中国党政代表团是在25日由匈牙利到达基辅的。
