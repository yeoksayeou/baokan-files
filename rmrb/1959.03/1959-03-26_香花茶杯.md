    Fragrant Flower Tea Cup
    The Shanghai No. 1 Glass Factory has recently produced a type of fragrant flower glass cup. The flower paint is mixed with fragrance, so it can emit aroma. When hot water is poured into this glass or hot tea is brewed, the fragrance is even stronger. The fragrance of the fragrant flower cup can last for about half a year.

### 香花茶杯

1959-03-26
第2版()
专栏：跃进橱窗

    香花茶杯
    上海市玻璃一厂最近制成一种香花玻璃杯。在花的涂料中拌有香料，因此能放射香气。如果在这种玻璃杯内冲热水或泡热茶时，香气更大。香花杯的香气约能持续半年左右。
