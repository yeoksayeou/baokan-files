Title: Which Animal Runs the Fastest?
Author:
Date: 1959-03-28
Page: 8

Which animal runs the fastest? Leopard - 30.5 meters per second; Antelope - 26 meters per second; Kangaroo - 20 meters per second; Horse - 19 meters per second; Rabbit - 19 meters per second; Greyhound - 15 meters per second; Elephant - 11 meters per second; Turtle - 5 centimeters per second; Snail - 1 millimeter per second.
(Gao Wei translated from the Soviet "Youth Technology")

### 哪种动物跑得快？

1959-03-28
第8版()
专栏：

    哪种动物跑得快？豹——每秒30.5公尺；羚羊——每秒26公尺；袋鼠——每秒20公尺；马——每秒19公尺；兔——每秒19公尺；猎犬——每秒15公尺；象——每秒11公尺；龟——每秒5公分；蜗牛——每秒1公厘。
  （高伟译自苏联“青年技术”）
