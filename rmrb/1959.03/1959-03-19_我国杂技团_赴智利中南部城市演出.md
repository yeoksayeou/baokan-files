Title: China Acrobatic Troupe Performs in Central and Southern Chilean Cities
Author:
Date: 1959-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Santiago: After concluding its performances in Santiago, the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, led by Zhou Erfu, departed on the morning of the 17th for a tour of performances in cities in central and southern Chile.

During its stay in Santiago, the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe gave thirty-five performances, attracting an audience of 140,000.

### 我国杂技团  赴智利中南部城市演出

1959-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我国杂技团
    赴智利中南部城市演出
    据新华社讯　圣地亚哥讯：由周而复率领的中国杂技团结束了在圣地亚哥的演出以后，已经在17日上午离开圣地亚哥到智利中南部的一些城市进行访问演出。
    中国杂技团在圣地亚哥期间曾举行三十五场演出，观众达十四万。
