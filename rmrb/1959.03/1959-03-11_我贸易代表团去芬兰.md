Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Leaves for Finland
Author:
Date: 1959-03-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The People's Republic of China government trade delegation, consisting of six people led by Zhang Guangdou, left Beijing by plane this morning for Finland to discuss and sign the 1959 trade agreement between China and Finland.

### 我贸易代表团去芬兰

1959-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团去芬兰
    新华社10日讯　去芬兰商谈签订中芬两国1959年贸易协定的中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团一行六人，在张光斗率领下今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往芬兰。
