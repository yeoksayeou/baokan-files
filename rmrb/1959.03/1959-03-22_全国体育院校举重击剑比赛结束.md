Title: National Sports Colleges Weightlifting and Fencing Competition Concludes
Author:
Date: 1959-03-22
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, March 21st. The 1959 National Sports Colleges Weightlifting and Fencing Competition, held in Xi'an, concluded on the 20th after six days.

　　More than 400 athletes from fifteen sports colleges and three sports vocational schools participated in the competition. They competed in seven weightlifting categories, as well as men's and women's foil, men's épée and sabre fencing.

　　The results of the competition: Beijing Sport University won first place in the overall weightlifting and fencing team score, Wuhan Sport University won second place, and Shanghai Sport University won third place.

　　In the seven weightlifting categories, the total scores of the champion athletes are as follows: Flyweight: Li Weishan from Beijing Sport University, 267.5 kg; Bantamweight: Luo Songxi from Wuhan Sport University, 285 kg; Lightweight: Zhong Xingchang from Wuhan Sport University, 312.5 kg; Middleweight: Yi Chenghong from Beijing Sport University, 315 kg; Light Heavyweight: Jiang Lianrong from Shenyang Sport University, 335 kg; Middle Heavyweight: Peng Keguang from Beijing Sport University, 330 kg; Heavyweight: Wang Zhangyu from Beijing Sport University, 312.5 kg.

　　In the fencing competition, Xiang Qiaode from Wuhan Sport University won the women's foil champion; Wen Guogang from Wuhan Sport University won the men's foil champion; Tao Jinhan from Beijing Sport University won the men's épée champion; and Chen Jingxi from Shanghai Sport University won the men's sabre champion.

### 全国体育院校举重击剑比赛结束

1959-03-22
第2版()
专栏：体育新闻

    全国体育院校举重击剑比赛结束
    新华社西安21日电　在西安举行的1959年全国体育院校举重、击剑比赛历时六天，已在20日结束。
    参加这次比赛的有十五个体育学院、三个体育专科学校的四百多名运动员。他们共进行了七个等级的举重比赛，以及男、女轻剑，男子花式剑和重剑的击剑比赛。
    比赛结果：举重、击剑团体总分第一名是北京体育学院，第二名是武汉体育学院，第三名是上海体育学院。
    在七个等级举重比赛中，获得冠军选手的总成绩如下：最轻量级：北京体育学院李维善二六七·五公斤；次轻量级：武汉体育学院罗松喜二八五公斤；轻量级：武汉体育学院锺兴常三一二·五公斤；中量级：北京体育学院裔程洪三一五公斤；轻重量级：沈阳体育学院姜连荣三三五公斤；次重量级：北京体育学院彭可光三三○公斤；重量级：北京体育学院汪章瑜三一二·五公斤。
    在击剑比赛中，武汉体育学院向巧德获得女子轻剑冠军；武汉体育学院文国刚获得男子轻剑冠军；北京体育学院陶金汉获得男子花式剑冠军；男子重剑冠军获得者是上海体育学院陈静析。
