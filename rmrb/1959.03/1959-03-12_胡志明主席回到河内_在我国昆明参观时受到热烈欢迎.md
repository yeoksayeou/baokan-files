Title: President Ho Chi Minh Returns to Hanoi, Warmly Welcomed During Visit to Kunming, China
Author:
Date: 1959-03-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 11th: President Ho Chi Minh returned to Hanoi at 5 p.m. today. He announced to the more than 4,000 people who enthusiastically welcomed him at Gia Lam Airport that President Sukarno had agreed to visit Vietnam in a few months.

This afternoon, as the silver plane landed at Gia Lam Airport, the crowd expressed their boundless joy to President Ho Chi Minh, who stepped off the plane, with thunderous cheers.

Present at the airport to welcome him were Vietnamese Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly Ton Duc Thang, Deputy Prime Ministers Truong Chinh and Phan Ke Toai, Politburo members of the Vietnam Workers' Party Le Duan and Hoang Van Hoan, and many leaders of the Vietnamese Party, government, military, and people's organizations. Heads of diplomatic missions in Vietnam and representatives of the International Commission for Vietnam also came to the airport to welcome him.

After reviewing the guard of honor, President Ho Chi Minh spoke to the welcoming crowd. He spoke of President Sukarno's brotherly reception of him and the enthusiastic welcome he received from the people of Indonesia. He emphasized that the grand welcome given to him by the Indonesian government and people was a welcome to the heroic Vietnamese people.

President Ho Chi Minh also mentioned the warm reception he received from Burmese national leaders during his two stopovers in Burma.

He said that he was very happy to have the opportunity to pass through Kunming and see the leaping progress in local industry and agriculture with his own eyes.

President Ho Chi Minh's speech brought great excitement to the welcomers and won thunderous applause.

Subsequently, President Ho Chi Minh, accompanied by Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, entered the city in an open-top car. On the nearly ten-kilometer journey from the airport to the Presidential Palace, he was greeted by 30,000 people cheering along the way.

After returning to Hanoi, President Ho Chi Minh immediately sent telegrams to President Sukarno and the Indonesian people, expressing his heartfelt thanks for their warm reception.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 11th: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam left Kunming by special plane at 3:30 p.m. today to return to his country. Deputy Prime Minister Pham Hung, Minister of Education Nguyen Van Huyen, and Deputy Minister of the Prime Minister's Office Phan My of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam also returned home on the same plane.

Those who saw him off at the airport included Xie Fuzhi, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China; Yu Yichuan, Secretary of the Provincial Committee and Governor of Yunnan Province; Qin Jiwei, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming; Zhao Zengyi, First Secretary of the Kunming Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming; representatives of all ethnic groups in Yunnan Province and Vietnamese nationals in Kunming, totaling more than 1,400 people.

Le Tung Son, the Vietnamese Consul in Kunming, and the staff of the consulate also went to the airport to see him off.

Sunarjo, Consul General of Indonesia in Vietnam, flew to Vietnam on the same plane.

President Ho Chi Minh arrived in Kunming on the 9th while returning from a visit to Indonesia. During his stay in Kunming, President Ho Chi Minh visited the Kunming Iron and Steel Plant, the Yunnan Nationalities Institute, and addressed all the students. President Ho Chi Minh also visited scenic spots such as Daguan Pavilion, West Mountain, and Anning Hot Springs, and was warmly welcomed by the people of Kunming.

### 胡志明主席回到河内  在我国昆明参观时受到热烈欢迎

1959-03-12
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席回到河内
    在我国昆明参观时受到热烈欢迎
    新华社河内11日电　胡志明主席今天下午五时回到河内。他向到嘉林机场热情迎接他的四千多人宣布苏加诺总统已同意过几个月后来越南访问。
    今天下午，当银白色的飞机飞降嘉林机场时，在场群众以震天的欢呼向步下飞机的胡志明主席表达了他们的无限欢欣之情。
    今天到机场欢迎的有越南总理范文同，国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜，副总理长征、潘继遂，越南劳动党政治局委员黎笋、黄文欢，以及越南党、政、军和人民团体的许多负责人。各国驻越南使节、越南国际委员会代表也到机场欢迎。
    胡志明主席在检阅仪仗队后向欢迎的群众讲话。他讲到了苏加诺总统对他的兄弟般的接待，讲到印度尼西亚各地人民欢迎他的盛情。他强调指出，印度尼西亚政府和人民对他的盛大欢迎，就是对英雄的越南人民的欢迎。
    胡志明主席还讲到他两次途经缅甸时受到缅甸国家领导人的盛情接待。
    他说，他很高兴有机会路过昆明，亲眼看到了当地工农业的跃进局面。
    胡志明主席的讲话给欢迎者带来很大的兴奋，博得震耳的掌声。
    随后，胡志明主席在范文同总理陪同下乘敞篷汽车进入市区。在从机场到主席府近十公里的路途上，他受到三万民众的夹道欢呼。
    胡志明主席在回到河内后，立即打电报给苏加诺总统和印度尼西亚人民，对他们的盛情接待表示衷心感谢。
    新华社昆明11日电　越南民主共和国胡志明主席今天下午三时半离开昆明乘专机回国。越南民主共和国副总理范雄、教育部部长阮文煊、总理府副部长潘美也同机回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共云南省委第一书记谢富治，省委书记、云南省省长于一川，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长秦基伟，中共昆明市委第一书记赵增益，昆明市市长潘朔端，云南省各族人民和在昆明的越南侨民等一千四百多人。
    越南驻昆明领事黎松山和领事馆的工作人员也到机场欢送。
    印度尼西亚驻越南总领事苏纳约同机飞往越南。
    胡志明主席是访问印度尼西亚返国途中在9日到达昆明的。在昆明期间，胡志明主席参观了昆明钢铁厂，云南民族学院，并且向全体学员讲了话。胡志明主席还游览了大观楼、西山和安宁温泉等风景名胜区，受到昆明市人民群众的热烈欢迎。
