Title: Lebanese Prime Minister Karami Says Lebanon Pays Attention to the Development of Arab-Israeli Relations
Author:
Date: 1959-03-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, March 18th - Lebanese Prime Minister Karami said here this afternoon that the Lebanese government has not yet adopted a clear attitude towards the United Arab Republic's movement against Iraq.

Prime Minister Karami said at a press conference, "I regret that we have not yet adopted any clear attitude, because we are still paying attention to the development of the situation, so that we can later decide on an attitude that will help the higher interests of the Arab world."

Karami said that the visiting UAR delegation did not discuss any economic issues with the Lebanese government. He said, "The UAR delegation is here to return the visit of our delegation. We have no formal agenda to discuss."

Prior to this, Lebanese Interior Minister Eddé said on the 13th that Lebanon has no intention of supporting one side against another, because Lebanon's policy is based on neutrality and reconciliation.

### 黎巴嫩总理卡拉米表示  黎巴嫩注意阿伊关系发展

1959-03-21
第6版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩总理卡拉米表示
    黎巴嫩注意阿伊关系发展
    据新华社贝鲁特18日电　黎巴嫩总理卡拉米今天下午在这里说，黎巴嫩政府对阿联反对伊拉克的运动还没有采取明确的态度。
    卡拉米总理在记者招待会上说，“我感到遗憾，直到现在我们还没有采取任何明确的态度，因为我们仍在注意着事态的发展，以便能在以后决定我们有助于阿拉伯的更高利益的态度”。
    卡拉米说，正在访问中的阿联代表团没有同黎巴嫩政府讨论任何经济问题。他说，“阿联代表团来此是对我们的代表团进行回拜。我们没有讨论的正式议程”。
    在这以前，黎巴嫩内政部长埃迪曾经在13日说，黎巴嫩无意支持一方来反对另一方，因为黎巴嫩的政策是以中立和和解为基础的。
