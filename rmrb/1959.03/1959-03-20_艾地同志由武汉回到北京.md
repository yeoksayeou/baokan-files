Title: Comrade Aidit Returns to Beijing from Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1959-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, Aidit, accompanied by Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, returned to Beijing from Wuhan by special plane this morning, and will continue his tour.

### 艾地同志由武汉回到北京

1959-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    艾地同志由武汉回到北京
    新华社19日讯  印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地，由中共中央委员刘宁一陪同，今天上午从武汉乘专机回到北京，将继续进行参观访问。
