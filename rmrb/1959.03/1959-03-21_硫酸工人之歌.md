Title: Song of the Sulfuric Acid Worker
Author: Zhang Renjie
Date: 1959-03-21
Page: 2

    Song of the sulfuric acid worker
    Erecting giant towers into the clouds,
    Motors roar, spring thunder arrives,
    Leap forward, leap forward again,
    Sulfuric acid like the Yangtze River tide.
    (Worker of Shanghai Sulfuric Acid Plant, Zhang Renjie)

### 硫酸工人之歌
张仁杰
1959-03-21
第2版()
专栏：

    硫酸工人之歌架起巨塔入云霄，马达轰鸣春雷到，跃进跃进再跃进，硫酸如同长江潮。
    （上海硫酸厂工人　张仁杰）
