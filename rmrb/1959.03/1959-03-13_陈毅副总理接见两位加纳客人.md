Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Two Guests from Ghana
Author:
Date: 1959-03-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — Vice Premier Chen Yi received Ghanaian guests Gamisu Koffi Amegbe and Moses Felchio Elte Kohokoh, who are currently visiting our country, this afternoon.
    Present at the reception were He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Mingyuan, Acting Secretary-General of the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee.

### 陈毅副总理接见两位加纳客人

1959-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见两位加纳客人
    新华社12日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的加纳客人加米苏·可非·阿麦格倍和摩西·费尔却艾尔特·哥霍霍。
    接见时在座的有外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷，中国亚非团结委员会代理秘书长王明远。
