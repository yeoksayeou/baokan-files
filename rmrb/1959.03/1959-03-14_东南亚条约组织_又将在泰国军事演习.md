Title: Southeast Asia Treaty Organization to Hold Military Exercises in Thailand Again
Author:
Date: 1959-03-14
Page: 5

　　Southeast Asia Treaty Organization
　　To Hold Military Exercises in Thailand Again
　　Xinhua News Agency, March 13th, Bangkok: According to Bangkok newspapers, the two consecutive military exercises recently held in Thailand by the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization cost the Thai government 4 million baht.

　　It is reported that this aggressive military bloc's recently held naval exercises are scheduled to begin again in Bangkok. Five U.S. minesweepers will arrive in Thailand on the 16th. The news says that the Bangkok police authorities have issued orders to the police to pay attention to protecting the safety of American officers and soldiers.

### 东南亚条约组织  又将在泰国军事演习

1959-03-14
第5版()
专栏：

    东南亚条约组织
    又将在泰国军事演习
    新华社13日讯　曼谷消息：据曼谷报纸报道，东南亚条约组织最近在泰国连续举行的两次军事演习使泰国政府耗费了四百万铢。
    据报道，这个侵略性军事集团预定在最近举行的海军演习又将在曼谷开始。五艘美国扫雷艇将于16日抵达泰国。消息说，曼谷警察当局已经发出命令，要警察注意保护美国官兵的安全。
