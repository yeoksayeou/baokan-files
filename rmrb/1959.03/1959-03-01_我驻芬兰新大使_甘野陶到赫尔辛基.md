Title: Our New Ambassador to Finland, Gan Yetao, Arrives in Helsinki
Author:
Date: 1959-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 27th -- Gan Yetao, the new Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Finland, arrived in Helsinki by train from Moscow yesterday evening.

### 我驻芬兰新大使  甘野陶到赫尔辛基

1959-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻芬兰新大使
    甘野陶到赫尔辛基
    新华社27日讯　赫尔辛基消息：中华人民共和国驻芬兰共和国新大使甘野陶昨天晚上从莫斯科乘火车到达赫尔辛基。
