Title: At the Invitation of Sweden, Norway and Denmark, Khrushchev to Visit in August
Author:
Date: 1959-03-15
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, March 14th, Moscow dispatch: According to TASS, Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Khrushchev, at the invitation of Sweden, Norway, and Denmark, will visit these countries in August this year.
    The Soviet Foreign Minister, A. Gromyko, entrusted by the Soviet government, has informed the representatives of the diplomatic missions of these three countries in Moscow of this matter.

### 应瑞典挪威丹麦三国邀请  赫鲁晓夫定八月前往访问

1959-03-15
第3版()
专栏：

    应瑞典挪威丹麦三国邀请
    赫鲁晓夫定八月前往访问
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫应瑞典、挪威和丹麦三国的邀请，将于今年8月访问这些国家。
    苏联外交部部长安·葛罗米柯受苏联政府的委托已将此事通知了这三个国家驻莫斯科的外交使团的代表。
