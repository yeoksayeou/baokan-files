Author:
Date: 1959-03-14
Page: 6

Leaping Forward, a New Look in One Year
The First Anniversary of the Founding of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region
March 5th is the first anniversary of the founding of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. In the past year, the autonomous region's economic and cultural education undertakings have greatly developed. This set of pictures introduces some of the construction of the autonomous region. Nanning's appearance is becoming more beautiful day by day. This is the newly built Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Economic and Cultural Achievement Exhibition Hall.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Tan Zhiqiang photo

In the past year, the industry of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region has developed rapidly, producing many products that could not be produced in the past. The Liuzhou Electric Motor Factory has recently manufactured the first 320 kVA transformer in the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region.
Zhao Huanggang photo (Xinhua News Agency draft)

The Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Guixian Sugar Factory, which crushes 1,500 tons of sugar cane per day, produces a large quantity of white sugar to support all parts of the country.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Tan Zhiqiang photo

Since the beginning of spring in the rural areas of Guangxi, the entire region has launched a spring plowing production campaign centered on accumulating fertilizer, actively implementing the Eight-Point Charter for Agriculture, and striving for a bumper summer grain harvest. This is the Ban Gun People's Commune in Ningming County transplanting rice seedlings according to the new specifications (medium-density planting).
Tang Qingsen photo (Xinhua News Agency draft)

In the past year, the number of students in universities and colleges has increased by two times. The picture shows a corner of the campus of the newly established comprehensive university, Guangxi University.
Xinhua News Agency reporter He Guozheng photo

One of the key national construction projects this year, the Guangxi Xijin Hydropower Station, is under intense construction.
Xinhua News Agency reporter He Guozheng photo

Guangxi has many rivers, which will be gradually comprehensively developed and utilized. The Zhaoping Large Hydropower Station on the Guijiang River is currently undergoing surveying.
Xinhua News Agency reporter He Guozheng photo

### 跃进一年面貌新  广西僮族自治区成立一周年（图片）

1959-03-14
第6版()
专栏：

    跃进一年面貌新
    广西僮族自治区成立一周年
    3月5日是广西僮族自治区成立一周年纪念日。一年来，自治区的经济和文化教育事业都有很大发展。这组图片介绍了自治区的一些建设情况。南宁的面貌一天比一天美丽了。这是新建的广西僮族自治区经济文化成就展览馆。
    新华社记者　谭志强摄
    一年来广西僮族自治区的工业有了飞速的发展，生产出许多过去不能生产的产品。柳州电机厂最近已制成了广西僮族自治区第一台320伏安的变压器。
    赵黄岗摄（新华社稿）
