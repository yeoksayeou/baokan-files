Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Visits Bogor Accompanied by President Sukarno
Author:
Date: 1959-03-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 1st. Chairman Ho Chi Minh, accompanied by President Sukarno, visited Bogor today. Beginning today, Chairman Ho Chi Minh will undertake a week-long visit to Bogor, Bandung, Yogyakarta, Solo, Surabaya, and Bali.
    Along both sides of the sixty-kilometer highway from Jakarta to Bogor, Indonesian and Vietnamese Democratic Republic flags fluttered in the wind. Many farmers came to the roadside to welcome Chairman Ho Chi Minh.
    The city of Bogor was decorated with flags and erected archways. When Chairman Ho Chi Minh and President Sukarno drove through the city, tens of thousands of Bogor citizens welcomed Chairman Ho Chi Minh and shouted, "Long live Uncle Ho!"
    President Sukarno led Chairman Ho Chi Minh on a visit to the beautiful Bogor Presidential Palace. Chairman Ho Chi Minh took photos with sculptors and then with girls from West Irian who came to welcome him.
    Chairman Ho Chi Minh dined at the Cipanas Presidential Villa, about forty kilometers from Bogor. Chairman Ho Chi Minh, President Sukarno, and his wife participated in a communal dance. Vietnamese Democratic Republic Deputy Prime Minister Pham Hung, Minister of Education Nguyen Van Huyen, and other Vietnamese guests were also invited to participate in the communal dance.
    Chairman Ho Chi Minh is scheduled to spend the night at the Bogor Presidential Palace and will fly to Bandung with President Sukarno on the morning of the 2nd.

### 在苏加诺总统陪同下  胡志明主席访问茂物

1959-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    在苏加诺总统陪同下
    胡志明主席访问茂物
    新华社雅加达1日电　胡志明主席今天在苏加诺总统陪同下访问了茂物。从今天开始，胡志明主席将在茂物、万隆、日惹、梭罗、泗水和巴厘进行为时一周的访问。
    在从雅加达通往茂物的六十公里长的公路的两侧，到处都迎风飘扬着印度尼西亚和越南民主共和国的国旗。许多农民到路旁来欢迎胡志明主席。
    茂物城内到处悬挂着旗帜并且搭起了牌楼。当胡志明主席和苏加诺总统乘车穿过城市的时候，茂物的成千上万的市民欢迎胡志明主席，并高呼“胡伯伯万岁！”
    苏加诺总统引导胡志明主席访问美丽的茂物总统府。胡志明主席同雕刻工作者一起照了像，随后又同从西伊里安来欢迎他的姑娘们一起照了像。
    胡志明主席在距茂物约四十公里的吉帕纳斯的总统别墅进餐。胡志明主席同苏加诺总统和他的夫人一起参加跳集体舞。越南民主共和国副总理范雄、教育部长阮文煊和其他越南客人也被邀请参加跳集体舞。
    胡志明主席预定在茂物总统府过夜，并且在2日上午在苏加诺总统陪同下乘飞机前往万隆。
