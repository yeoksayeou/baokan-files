Title: Soviet Government Delegation Visits Kashmir
Author:
Date: 1959-03-16
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 15th: The Soviet government delegation, currently visiting India, arrived in Srinagar, the capital of Jammu and Kashmir State, by plane on the 14th.
    Welcoming the delegation at the airport were Kashmir State Premier Bakshi and other state government officials.
    Bakshi hosted a banquet on the evening of the 14th to entertain the Soviet government delegation.

### 苏联政府代表团访问克什米尔

1959-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联政府代表团访问克什米尔
    新华社新德里15日电　正在印度进行访问的苏联政府代表团在14日坐飞机到达查谟和克什米尔邦的首府斯利那加。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有克什米尔邦总理巴克希和邦政府其他官员。
    巴克希在14日晚间设宴招待苏联政府代表团。
