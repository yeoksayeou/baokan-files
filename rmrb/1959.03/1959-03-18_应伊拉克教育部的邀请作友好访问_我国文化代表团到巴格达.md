Title: At the Invitation of the Iraqi Ministry of Education, China's Cultural Delegation Pays a Friendly Visit to Baghdad
Author:
Date: 1959-03-18
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 17th. China's Cultural Delegation, headed by Burhan, vice chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and chairman the China Islamic Association, arrived in Baghdad, the capital of Iraq, by plane today. The delegation is visiting at the invitation of the Iraqi Ministry of Education.

The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport. Among those who greeted them at the airport were Brigadier General Mohieddin Abdul Hamid, Minister of Education; the Director General of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; members of the preparatory committee of the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association; and people who had visited China.

As the delegation disembarked, the welcomers and airport workers applauded and cheered enthusiastically. They shouted slogans such as "Long live the friendship between the Iraqi and Chinese people!"

Brigadier General Hamid delivered a speech on behalf of the Iraqi government and people, warmly welcoming the delegation. He emphasized the inherent cultural and friendly relations between Iraq and China.

Burhan expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome from the Iraqi people. He said that the delegation brought Premier Kassem and all the people of Iraq the best wishes and high respect of the Chinese people. He said, "The victory of your great revolution is not only a major contribution to peace in the Middle East and the world, but also valuable support for our struggle to liberate Taiwan. At the same time, it enables us to restore and develop long-interrupted friendly and cultural relations."

### 应伊拉克教育部的邀请作友好访问  我国文化代表团到巴格达

1959-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    应伊拉克教育部的邀请作友好访问
    我国文化代表团到巴格达
    新华社巴格达17日电　以中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中国伊斯兰教协会主席包尔汉为首的中国文化代表团今天乘飞机到达伊拉克首都巴格达。代表团是应伊拉克教育部邀请来访问的。
    代表团在飞机场受到热烈的欢迎。到机场欢迎的有教育部长毛希丁·阿卜杜勒·哈米德准将、外交部技术合作局总监、伊中友好协会筹备委员会委员们以及访问过中国的人士等。
    当代表团下飞机时，欢迎的人和机场工人热烈鼓掌和欢呼。他们高呼“伊拉克和中国人民友好万岁”等口号。
    哈米德准将代表伊拉克政府和人民致词热烈欢迎代表团。他强调了伊拉克和中国固有的文化关系和友好关系。
    包尔汉对伊拉克人民的热情欢迎表示感谢。他说，代表团给卡塞姆总理和伊拉克全体人民带来了中国人民最好的祝愿和崇高的敬意。他说，“你们的伟大革命的胜利不仅是对中东和世界和平的重大贡献，而且是对我们解放台湾的斗争的宝贵的支持。同时，它使得我们能够恢复和发展中断已久的友好关系和文化关系。”
