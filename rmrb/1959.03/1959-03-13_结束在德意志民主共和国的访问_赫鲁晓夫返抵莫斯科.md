Title: Khrushchev Returns to Moscow After Ending Visit to the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1959-03-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 12th, Moscow dispatch: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, returned to Moscow by plane this afternoon from the German Democratic Republic, according to TASS.

    Those who greeted him at the airport were: Aristov, Belyaev, Brezhnev, Kirichenko, Kozlov, Mikoyan, Suslov, Furtseva, Pospelov, Kosygin, Polyansky, and others.

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 11th: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, accompanied by the Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Pervukhin, and the Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Forces in Germany, Army General Zakharov, visited the Soviet troops stationed in Germany this afternoon.

    The soldiers, officers, generals, and their families of the Soviet army warmly welcomed Khrushchev and held a welcome rally.

    Khrushchev and Zakharov both spoke at the meeting. Zakharov assured the Party Central Committee and the government that Soviet soldiers will do everything possible to fulfill their obligations to the motherland.

    After the meeting, Khrushchev visited the barracks where the soldiers lived.

### 结束在德意志民主共和国的访问  赫鲁晓夫返抵莫斯科

1959-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在德意志民主共和国的访问
    赫鲁晓夫返抵莫斯科
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫今日下午乘飞机由德意志民主共和国返抵莫斯科。
    到机场迎接的有：阿里斯托夫、别利亚耶夫、勃列日涅夫、基里钦科、科兹洛夫、米高扬、苏斯洛夫、福尔采娃、波斯别洛夫、柯西金、波利扬斯基等。
    新华社柏林11日电　苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使别尔乌辛和驻德苏军部队总司令陆军大将扎哈罗夫的陪同下今天下午访问了驻德苏军部队。
    苏军的士兵、军官、将军以及他们的家属们热烈地欢迎赫鲁晓夫，并举行欢迎大会。
    赫鲁晓夫和扎哈罗夫都在会上讲话。扎哈罗夫向党中央委员会和政府保证，苏联军人将尽一切努力履行自己对祖国的义务。
    会后，赫鲁晓夫参观了战士住的营房。
