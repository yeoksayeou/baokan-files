Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-03-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th: Vice Chairman Zhu De, who led the Chinese Communist Party delegation to the Third Congress of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Chinese Party and Government delegation to the 40th Anniversary Commemoration of the Hungarian Soviet Republic, returned to Beijing by special plane this morning.

Xi Zhongxun, a member of the Chinese Party and Government delegation participating in the 40th Anniversary Commemoration of the Hungarian Soviet Republic, returned to Beijing on the same flight today.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Tan Zheng and Huang Kecheng, Secretaries of the CPC Central Committee; Zhang Jingwu and Wang Feng, Alternate Members of the CPC Central Committee; Gong Zirong, Deputy Director of the General Office of the CPC Central Committee; Qi Yanming, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council; and Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Also at the airport to welcome them were Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin, Hungarian Ambassador to China Nogradi, Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China Furato, and staff members of the embassy. (Attached photo)

### 朱德副主席回到北京

1959-03-29
第2版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席回到北京
    新华社28日讯　率领中国共产党代表团参加波兰统一工人党第三次代表大会和率领中国党政代表团参加匈牙利苏维埃共和国四十周年纪念活动的朱德副主席，今天上午乘专机回到北京。
    参加匈牙利苏维埃共和国四十周年纪念活动的中国党政代表团团员习仲勋，今天同机回到北京。
    前往机场迎接的，有中共中央书记处书记谭政、黄克诚，中共中央候补委员张经武、汪锋，中共中央办公厅副主任龚子荣，国务院副秘书长齐燕铭，外交部副部长曾涌泉。
    到机场迎接的，还有苏联驻华大使尤金、匈牙利驻华大使诺格拉第、波兰驻华临时代办傅拉托和大使馆的工作人员。（附图片）
