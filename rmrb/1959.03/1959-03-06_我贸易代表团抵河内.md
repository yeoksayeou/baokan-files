Title: Our Trade Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1959-03-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 5th: The People's Republic of China Government Trade Delegation, a group of seven people led by the head of the delegation, Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, arrived in Hanoi by plane at noon today.
	Those who welcomed them at the airport included Nguyen Van Tran, Vice Chairman of the Vietnamese State Planning Commission, Dang Viet Chau, Deputy Minister of Vietnamese Foreign Trade, Le Trung Chan, Deputy Minister of Commerce, and Le Loc, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
	He Wei, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, and Fang Yi, representative of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Trade's Economic Representative Office in Vietnam, also greeted them at the airport.
	The delegation will make a short visit to various mines and factories in Vietnam.

### 我贸易代表团抵河内

1959-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团抵河内
    新华社河内5日电　中华人民共和国政府贸易访问团一行七人，在团长、广东省省长陈郁率领下，今天中午乘飞机抵达河内。
    到机场欢迎的有越南国家计划委员会副主任阮文珍、越南对外贸易部副部长邓曰州、商业部副部长黎忠瓒、外交部亚洲司司长黎禄等。
    中国驻越南大使何伟、中国对外贸易部驻越南经济代表处代表方毅也到机场迎接。
    访问团将到越南各矿山、工厂作短时期的参观访问。
