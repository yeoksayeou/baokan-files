Title: Our Trade Delegation Arrives in Helsinki
Author:
Date: 1959-03-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 12th: Zhang Douguang, Deputy Head of the Chinese Trade Delegation and Manager of the China National Transport Machinery Import Corporation, and four other members of the delegation arrived in Helsinki, the capital of Finland, yesterday. Lu Xuzhang, Head of the delegation and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of China, and another member of the delegation will arrive later.

### 我贸易代表团到芬京

1959-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团到芬京
    新华社12日讯　赫尔辛基消息：中国贸易代表团副团长、中国运输机械进口公司经理张斗光和其他四个团员昨天到达芬兰首都赫尔辛基。代表团团长、中国对外贸易部副部长卢绪章和另外一个团员将随后到达。
