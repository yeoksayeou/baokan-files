Title: Further Strengthening Cultural Cooperation: China and Romania Sign This Year's Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1959-03-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 10th: China and Romania signed the 1959 implementation plan for the cultural cooperation agreement between the two countries today in Beijing.

    The signing ceremony was held this morning. Qu Wu, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Teodor Zamfirescu, the Romanian Ambassador to China, signed the implementation plan on behalf of their respective governments. After the signing, Vice Chairman Qu Wu and Ambassador Zamfirescu delivered speeches. They pointed out that the 1959 cultural cooperation agreement implementation plan between China and Romania would not only be successfully completed but would also lay a good foundation for the further development of cultural cooperation between the two countries in the future. They wished the cultural cooperation between the two countries would develop day by day.

    After the signing, Qu Wu held a small reception to express congratulations.

### 进一步加强文化合作  中罗签订今年执行计划

1959-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    进一步加强文化合作
    中罗签订今年执行计划
    新华社10日讯　中国和罗马尼亚两国文化合作协定1959年执行计划今天在北京签字。
    签字仪式在上午举行。我国对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武和罗马尼亚驻中国大使查哈勒斯库分别代表中罗两国政府在执行计划上签了字。屈武副主任和查哈勒斯库大使在签字以后先后讲了话。他们指出，中罗1959年文化合作协定执行计划，不仅将胜利完成，而且将为今后两国文化合作事业的进一步发展奠定良好的基础。他们祝两国之间的文化合作事业日益发展。
    签字以后，屈武举行小型酒会表示祝贺。
