Title: Central Leaders Receive Representatives to the Two Conferences
Author:
Date: 1959-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 12th—Central Committee leaders Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, Peng Dehuai, He Long, Li Xiannian, Kang Sheng, and Hu Qiaomu received this afternoon all the representatives attending the Chinese People's Liberation Army Signal Corps Activist Representative Conference and all the representatives attending the National Conference on Workers' Education in Industrial and Mining Enterprises.

### 中央领导人接见两会议的代表

1959-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中央领导人接见两会议的代表
    新华社12日讯　中共中央领导人刘少奇、周恩来、董必武、彭真、陈毅、彭德怀、贺龙、李先念、康生、胡乔木，在今天下午接见了出席中国人民解放军通信兵积极分子代表大会的全体代表和出席全国工矿企业职工教育工作会议的全体代表。
