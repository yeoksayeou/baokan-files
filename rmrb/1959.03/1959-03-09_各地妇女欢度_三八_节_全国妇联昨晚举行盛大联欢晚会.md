Title: Women Across the Nation Celebrate “March 8th” International Women’s Day; All-China Women's Federation Held a Grand Evening Party Last Night
Author:
Date: 1959-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: Women in various parts of the country celebrated "March 8th" International Women's Day with joy and excitement. Women in cities such as Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Wuhan, Chongqing, Xi'an, Hohhot, Harbin, Fuzhou, and Xining held gatherings on the 7th or 8th to warmly celebrate their holiday. Women representatives from all fronts in these cities reported on their achievements in the Great Leap Forward at the meetings, presented gifts to celebrate the holiday, and expressed their determination to achieve even greater leaps forward. Representatives of local Party committees also attended the meetings and spoke, congratulating the women on the tremendous achievements and progress they have made in industrial and agricultural production, social welfare undertakings, culture, education, health, science, and other work, and wished them even greater victories this year.

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: The All-China Women's Federation held a grand evening party with more than 4,000 people participating in the CPPCC Auditorium tonight to celebrate "March 8th" International Women's Day.

Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi and Li Fuchun, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation Cai Chang, Vice Chairpersons Li Dequan, Xu Guangping, Liu Qingyang, and Kang Keqing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and responsible persons from central and Beijing municipal departments attended the evening party.

Invited to the party were diplomatic envoys from various countries to China and their wives, experts from the Soviet Union, various people's democratic countries and other countries and their wives, and many international friends who are currently visiting Beijing, totaling more than 2,000 people.

At 7:30 p.m., Li Dequan, Vice Chairperson of the All-China Women's Federation, announced the opening of the evening party. She extended a warm welcome to the foreign guests on behalf of the All-China Women's Federation. She wished that women and children of all countries could obtain happy lives and that the forces for world peace would become even stronger and the friendship between the people of all countries would further develop.

During the evening party, colorful cultural programs and various games attracted joyful crowds. Chinese hosts and foreign friends enjoyed themselves in fourteen game rooms and dance halls, spending a pleasant evening on this holiday.

### 各地妇女欢度“三八”节  全国妇联昨晚举行盛大联欢晚会

1959-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    各地妇女欢度“三八”节
    全国妇联昨晚举行盛大联欢晚会
    据新华社讯　全国各地妇女欢欣鼓舞地度过“三八”国际妇女节。上海、天津、沈阳、武汉、重庆、西安、呼和浩特、哈尔滨、福州、西宁等城市的妇女，分别于7日或8日集会，热烈庆祝自己的节日。这些城市各条战线上的妇女代表，在会上汇报了跃进成绩，向节日献礼，并且表示了更大跃进的决心。各地党委代表也到会讲话，祝贺妇女们在工农业生产、社会福利事业、文教卫生、科学和其他工作中所取得的巨大成就和进步，并预祝她们今年获得更大的胜利。
    据新华社8日讯　全国妇女联合会今晚在政协礼堂举行了有四千多人参加的盛大联欢晚会，庆祝“三八”国际妇女节。
    国务院副总理陈毅、李富春，全国妇联主席蔡畅、副主席李德全、许广平、刘清扬、康克清，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中央和北京市各部门负责人参加了联欢晚会。
    应邀参加晚会的，有各国驻中国使节和他们的夫人，苏联、各人民民主国家和其他国家的专家和他们的夫人，以及正在北京访问的许多国际友人共两千多人。
    七时三十分，全国妇联副主席李德全宣布联欢晚会开始，她代表全国妇联向外宾表示热烈欢迎。她祝各国妇女儿童都能获得幸福的生活，祝世界和平力量的更加壮大和各国人民之间的友谊更加发展。
    联欢晚会中，丰富多彩的文娱节目和各类游戏吸引着欢乐的人群。中国主人和外国朋友在十四个游戏室和舞厅内尽情欢乐，愉快地度过了这个节日的夜晚。
