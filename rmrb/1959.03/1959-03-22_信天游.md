Title: Xintianyou (Folk Song)
Author: Zhu Xuzhong (collected in Mizhi, Shaanxi)
Date: 1959-03-22
Page: 8

　　　　　Xintianyou

　　　　　　(Collected by Zhu Xuzhong, Mizhi, Shaanxi)

　　　　　Red flowers bloom on the mountaintops,
　　　　　The establishment of the commune brings happiness.
　　　　　Picking Ganoderma lucidum in the grass is good,
　　　　　The commune is a bridge to heaven.
　　　　　The red flag on the high mountain flutters,
　　　　　Let's wholeheartedly build the commune well.
　　　　　A sheepskin towel holds sugar,
　　　　　Each commune member is beaming with joy.
　　　　　Chairman Mao's words are kept in mind,
　　　　　Workers, peasants, students, soldiers, all walk together.
　　　　　Sunflowers bloom facing the sun,
　　　　　We will always follow the Communist Party.
　　　　　Biological children follow their parents,
　　　　　Chairman Mao leads us to paradise.
　　　　　Uproot the roots of poverty and plant the roots of wealth,
　　　　　The Party's kindness is deeper than the sea.
　　　　　Pairs of magpies fly in the sky,
　　　　　Carrying heartfelt words to Chairman Mao:
　　　　　The lark cannot sink when crossing the river,
　　　　　Thinking of the commune, we can't forget you!

### 信天游

1959-03-22
第8版()
专栏：

    信天游
   （陕西米脂朱序忠收集）一朵朵红花山顶顶开，办起了公社幸福来。草里头挑草灵芝好，公社就是上天桥。高山上的红旗哗啦啦飘，齐心要把社办好。羊肚子手巾包沙糖，一个个社员喜洋洋。毛主席的话儿记心头，工农学商兵一道道走。向日葵开花向太阳，咱永远跟着共产党。亲生的儿女跟爹娘，毛主席引咱上天堂。穷根子拔了栽富根，党的恩情比海深。一对对喜鹊空中飞，知心话捎给毛主席：百灵子过河沉不了底，想起了公社就忘不了你！
