International Briefs

    Tunisia and Algeria Hold Talks
    Tunis: Tunisian President Bourguiba held a 45-minute meeting in Tunis on the 22nd with the Algerian government delegation headed by Ferhat Abbas, Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria. After the meeting, Prime Minister Abbas said that they discussed issues related to Tunisia and Algeria.

    Philippine Students Condemn Government for Banning Participation in World Youth Festival
    Manila: The "Philippine Herald" reported on the 19th that the National Union of Students of the Philippines recently condemned the government's decision to prohibit Philippine students from attending the 7th World Youth and Student Festival to be held in Vienna in July this year.

### 国际简讯

1959-03-24
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    突尼斯和阿尔及利亚举行会谈
    突尼斯讯：突尼斯总统布尔吉巴22日在突尼斯同以阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理费尔哈特·阿巴斯为首的阿尔及利亚政府代表团举行了四十五分钟的会谈。阿巴斯总理在会谈后说，他们讨论了有关突尼斯和阿尔及利亚两国的问题。菲学生谴责政府禁止参加世界
    青年联欢节
    马尼拉讯：“菲律宾先驱报”19日报道，菲律宾全国学生会最近谴责政府禁止菲律宾学生出席将于今年7月在维也纳举行的第七届世界青年与学生联欢节的决定。
