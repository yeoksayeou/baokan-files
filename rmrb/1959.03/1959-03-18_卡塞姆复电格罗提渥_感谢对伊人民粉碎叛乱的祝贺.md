Title: Kassem Replies to Grotewohl, Thanking Him for Congratulations on the Iraqi People's Crushing of the Rebellion
Author:
Date: 1959-03-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 16th. Iraqi Prime Minister Kassem replied on the 14th to the congratulatory telegram from German Democratic Republic Premier Grotewohl regarding the Iraqi people's crushing of the Shawwaf rebellion.

Prime Minister Kassem stated in the telegram: "I received with great respect and gratitude your telegram congratulating our country on crushing the despicable conspiracy against our young republic. The generous sentiments and lofty wishes you expressed towards my beloved nation have deeply affected me and all the Iraqi people. While I hasten to express my heartfelt gratitude to you, I wish you success in your work and good health, and I wish the people of your country progress and prosperity."

Premier Grotewohl stated in his telegram on March 12th, "As the attempt by imperialist forces to subvert your government, which is supported by the broad masses of the people, has failed, I congratulate you and, through you, the Iraqi people."

### 卡塞姆复电格罗提渥  感谢对伊人民粉碎叛乱的祝贺

1959-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    卡塞姆复电格罗提渥
    感谢对伊人民粉碎叛乱的祝贺
    新华社巴格达16日电　伊拉克总理卡塞姆在14日复电德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥关于祝贺伊拉克人民粉碎了沙瓦夫叛乱的贺电。
    卡塞姆总理在电报中说：“我怀着非常尊敬和感激的心情收到了您祝贺我国粉碎反对我们年青的共和国的卑鄙阴谋的电报。您向我的可爱的国家所表示的慷慨的感情和崇高的祝愿，对我和全体伊拉克人民产生了深刻的影响。在我赶紧向您表示衷心感激的同时，我祝贺您工作顺利，身体健康，并预祝贵国人民取得进展和繁荣。”
    格罗提渥总理在3月12日的电报中说，“在帝国主义势力想颠复您的为广大人民群众所支持的政府的企图遭到了失败的时候，我向您、并通过您向伊拉克人民祝贺”。
