Title: "Providing Charcoal in Snowy Weather"
Author: Li Chengsi
Date: 1959-03-12
Page: 4

Column: From the Readers

    "Providing Charcoal in Snowy Weather"
    Officers and soldiers of a certain unit of the Dalian Garrison are helping the Dalian Aquatic Products Cultivation Farm to rush the seaweed seedling separation. The current water temperature is a good season for seaweed growth, but the cultivation farm lacks labor. If this opportunity is not seized to separate the seedlings, it will affect the completion of the seaweed production task. The employees of the cultivation farm said, this is truly "providing charcoal in snowy weather." (Li Chengsi)

### “雪里送炭”
李成思
1959-03-12
第4版()
专栏：读者中来

    “雪里送炭”
    大连驻军某部队官兵帮助大连水产养殖厂突击海带分苗。目前的水温正是海带菜成长的好季节，但养殖厂却缺乏劳动力，如不抓紧这一时机分苗，就会影响海带生产任务的完成。养殖场职工说，这真是“雪里送炭”。　　　　　　　（李成思）
