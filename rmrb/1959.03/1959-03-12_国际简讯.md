International Briefs

    Japanese 20 Unions Win Wage Increase Struggle
    Tokyo dispatch: In a powerful spring struggle, twenty unions affiliated with the Japan Synthetic Chemical Industry Workers' Federation have forced capitalists to agree to increase workers' wages. The minimum wage increase is 1,000 yen per person per month, and the highest is 2,387 yen.
    Turkish Workers Demand the Right to Strike
    Istanbul dispatch: The executive committee of the Istanbul Electrical, Gas, and Municipal Transportation Workers' Union held a meeting on the 8th, demanding that workers be given the right to strike.
    UAR Implements Company Law for the Cairo Tram Company Operated by Belgians
    Cairo dispatch: Newspapers reported on the 8th that the UAR Ministry of Municipal and Rural Affairs has decided to immediately implement the provisions of the 1951 Company Law for the Cairo Tram Company operated by Belgians, without further postponement.
    According to this law, any company operating in the United Arab Republic must establish its board of directors within the Republic and will involve reorganizing the board to include five Egyptians and two foreigners. Up to now, the board has only had one Egyptian representing the government of the United Arab Republic.
    Tokyo's Population Ranks First Among World Cities
    Tokyo dispatch: According to an announcement by the Tokyo Statistics Bureau on the 10th, Tokyo's population has reached 8,899,533, making it the most populous city in the world.
    Tokyo's population exceeds London's 8,270,000 and New York's 7,790,000.

### 国际简讯

1959-03-12
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    日二十工会赢得增加工资斗争
    东京讯　在强大的春季斗争中，日本合成化学产业工会联合会所属的二十个工会已经迫使资本家同意增加工人的工资。每人每月的工资增加额最低为一千日元，最高的为二千三百八十七日元。
    土耳其工人要求罢工权利
    伊斯坦布尔讯　伊斯坦布尔电气、煤气和市交通工人工会执行委员会8日举行会议，要求给予工人罢工的权利。
    阿联对比商经营的开罗电车公司实施公司法
    开罗讯：据报纸8日报道，阿联城乡事务部决定对比利时人经营的开罗电车公司立即实施1951年的公司法条款，而不再予延期。
    根据这项法律，任何在阿拉伯联合共和国营业的公司，都必须把董事会设在这个共和国境内，并将涉及到改组这个公司的董事会，以便使这个董事会包括五个埃及人和两个外国人。到目前为止这个董事会仅有一个代表阿拉伯联合共和国政府的埃及人。
    东京人口居世界城市首位
    东京讯：据东京统计局10日宣布，东京人口已经达到八百八十九万九千五百三十三人，成为世界上人口最多的城市。
    东京的人口超过了伦敦的八百二十七万人和纽约的七百七十九万人。
