Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi Meets Comrade Aidit
Author:
Date: 1959-03-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th. Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, met this afternoon with D.N. Aidit, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Indonesia, and had a warm and friendly conversation. Present at the meeting were Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee. (Attached picture)
    Comrade Liu Shaoqi and Comrade Aidit (center) in cordial conversation.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter
          Meng Qingbiao [photographer]

### 刘少奇同志会见艾地同志

1959-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇同志会见艾地同志
    新华社16日讯中共中央副主席刘少奇今天下午会见了印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地，并且进行了热情友好的谈话。会见时在座的有中共中央总书记邓小平、中央委员刘宁一。（附图片）
    刘少奇同志和艾地同志（中），在亲切地谈话。
    新华社记者
          孟庆彪摄
