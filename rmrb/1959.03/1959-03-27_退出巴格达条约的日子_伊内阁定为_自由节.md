Title: Day of Withdrawal from Baghdad Pact Designated "Freedom Day" by Iraqi Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1959-03-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, March 26th – Iraqi Prime Minister Qasim announced in a statement on the evening of the 25th that the Iraqi cabinet has decided to designate March 24th, the day Iraq withdrew from the Baghdad Pact, as "Freedom Day."

The statement said that this day is a national holiday for the Iraqi people and will be celebrated every year from now on.

### 退出巴格达条约的日子  伊内阁定为“自由节”

1959-03-27
第5版()
专栏：

    退出巴格达条约的日子
    伊内阁定为“自由节”
    新华社巴格达26日电　伊拉克总理卡塞姆在25日晚上发表一项声明宣布，伊拉克内阁决定把3月24日即伊拉克退出巴格达条约的日子定为“自由节”。
    声明说，这一天是伊拉克全国人民的节日，今后将每年举行庆祝。
