Title: Chairman Mao Meets Du Bois and Strong
Author:
Date: 1959-03-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, March 13th: Chairman Mao Zedong today met in Wuhan with Dr. Du Bois, the famous American black scholar and member of the World Peace Council, and his wife, and the American writer Anna Louise Strong, and hosted a luncheon in their honor. Present at the meeting were Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General, and Tang Mingzhao, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. (Attached pictures)
    Top: Chairman Mao Zedong meets with and hosts a banquet in Wuhan for Dr. Du Bois (right), a famous American black scholar, his wife, and American writer Anna Louise Strong (center).
    Right: Photo after the meeting. The fifth person from the left is Du Bois, the sixth person from the left is Du Bois's wife, and the third person from the left is Strong.
                              Xinhua News Agency Reporter Hou Bo (Fax photo)

### 毛主席接见杜波伊斯和斯特朗

1959-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见杜波伊斯和斯特朗
    新华社武汉13日电　毛泽东主席今天在武汉接见并设午宴招待美国著名黑人学者、世界和平理事会理事杜波伊斯博士及其夫人和美国作家斯特朗。在座的有：中共湖北省委第一书记王任重，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林、副秘书长朱伯深，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员唐明照。（附图片）
    上：毛泽东主席在武汉接见并设宴招待美国著名黑人学者杜波伊斯博士（右）及其夫人和美国作家斯特朗（中）。
    右：图为接见后合影。左第五人是杜波伊斯，左第六人是杜波伊斯夫人，左第三人是斯特朗。
