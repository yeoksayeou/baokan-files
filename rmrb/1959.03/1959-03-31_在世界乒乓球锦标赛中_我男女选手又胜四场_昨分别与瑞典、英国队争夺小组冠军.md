Title: At the World Table Tennis Championships, Our Men's and Women's Teams Win Four More Matches; Yesterday, They Competed with Sweden and the British Team Respectively for the Group Championship
Author:
Date: 1959-03-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Dortmund Electric Dispatch. The Chinese Table Tennis Team won both of the last two matches they participated in on the 29th and the two matches on the afternoon of the 30th at the 25th World Table Tennis Championships. The women's team defeated the Danish team with a score of 3-0 on the 29th, and defeated the American team with the same score on the afternoon of the 30th; the men's team won against the West German team with a score of 5-2 on the 29th, and defeated the United Arab Republic team with a score of 5-0 on the afternoon of the 30th.

Since the Chinese women's team started participating in the competition, their competitive condition has been good. After defeating the Danish and American teams, they have won seven consecutive victories (all 3-0), unstoppable, with every set being 2-0, without losing a single game. However, they still have to fight a decisive battle with the English team on the last day. The English team has also won six consecutive victories, losing only one set, so they are the biggest rivals in the competition for the group championship. This decisive battle will be a fierce contest.

After defeating West Germany and the United Arab Republic, the men's team has also achieved seven consecutive victories. In the group, they have one remaining match against one of their strong rivals, Sweden. Sweden has not lost a single match so far. The competition between China and Sweden is expected to be very intense.

### 在世界乒乓球锦标赛中  我男女选手又胜四场  昨分别与瑞典、英国队争夺小组冠军

1959-03-31
第6版()
专栏：

    在世界乒乓球锦标赛中
    我男女选手又胜四场
    昨分别与瑞典、英国队争夺小组冠军
    新华社多特蒙德电　中国乒乓球代表队在29日参加的二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛团体赛的后两场比赛和30日下午的两场比赛，又都获胜。女子队在29日以三比○的成绩胜丹麦队，在30日下午以同样的比数击败美国队；男子队在29日以五比二胜西德队，在30日下午以五比○的比数击败阿联队。
    中国女子队参加比赛以来，竞技状况一直良好，在战胜丹麦队和美国队后，已七战七捷（都是三比○），所向披靡，每盘比数都是二比○，没有失过一局。不过，最后一天还要同英格兰队决战。英格兰队也六战六胜，只失过一盘，因此是争夺小组冠军中的最大劲敌。这场决战将是一场激烈争夺战。
    男子队在战胜西德和阿联后，到现在也已七战七捷，在小组内剩下和劲敌之一瑞典的一场比赛了。瑞典至今也没有失败过一场，中国和瑞典的对抗，竞争想必会很激烈
