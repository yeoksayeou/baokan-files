Title: Chairman Mao Sends Telegram to Congratulate Tunisia's Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1959-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to President Bourguiba of the Republic of Tunisia on the 18th, congratulating Tunisia's Independence Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Habib Bourguiba, President of the Republic of Tunisia:

On the occasion of your country's Independence Day, I would like to extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and the Tunisian people on behalf of the Chinese people. I wish Your Excellency and the Tunisian people new achievements in safeguarding national independence and seeking national prosperity. May the friendship between the Chinese and Tunisian peoples grow day by day.

Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, March 18, 1959

### 毛主席电贺突尼斯独立纪念日

1959-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺突尼斯独立纪念日
    新华社19日讯  毛泽东主席18日致电突尼斯共和国总统布尔吉巴，祝贺突尼斯独立纪念日。电文如下：突尼斯共和国总统哈比布·布尔吉巴阁下：
    值此贵国独立纪念日之际，我谨代表中国人民向阁下和突尼斯人民表示衷心的祝贺。祝阁下和突尼斯人民在维护民族独立和谋求国家繁荣的事业中获得新的成就。祝中国和突尼斯两国人民之间的友谊日益发展。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东　1959年3月18日
