Title: Mongolian Youth Delegation Leaves Beijing for Inner Mongolia

Author:
Date: 1959-03-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 14th: The delegation of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League, which came to China for a visit at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, concluded its visits to Beijing, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Shanghai, Jinan, Qingdao and other places, and left Beijing by train today to visit the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. They will return home after visiting Ulanhot and Baotou.

Luo Yi and Liu Xiyuan, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Wang Zhaohua, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, were at the station to see them off today.

### 蒙古青年代表团  离京赴内蒙访问

1959-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古青年代表团
    离京赴内蒙访问
    新华社14日讯　应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会邀请来我国访问的蒙古革命青年团代表团，结束了在北京、武汉、广州、杭州、上海、济南、青岛等地的访问，今天乘火车离开北京去内蒙古自治区访问。他们将在访问乌兰浩特、包头后返国。
    今天到车站欢送的有共青团中央书记处书记罗毅、刘西元，共青团北京市委员会第一书记王照华等。
