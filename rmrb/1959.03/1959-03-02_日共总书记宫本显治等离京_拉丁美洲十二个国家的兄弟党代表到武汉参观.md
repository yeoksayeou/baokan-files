Title: Japanese Communist Party General Secretary Kenji Miyamoto and Others Leave Beijing; Representatives of Brother Parties from Twelve Latin American Countries Visit Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1959-03-02
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st March. Japanese Communist Party General Secretary Kenji Miyamoto, JCP Central Committee member Masao Kasuga, and JCP Central Committee Deputy Minister Tomio Nishizawa, left Beijing today to visit other places and will then return home via Guangzhou.
    The Japanese Communist Party leaders Kenji Miyamoto and others returned to Beijing from Pyongyang on the 28th of last month.
    Present at the airport to see them off today were Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Kang Sheng, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau; Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat; Liu Ningyi and Wu Xiuquan, members of the Central Committee; and Xiong Fu, a responsible staff member of the Central Committee of the CPC.
    Also at the airport to see them off was Kinkazu Saionji, a Japanese peace figure in Beijing and Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee.
    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, March 1st. Japanese Communist Party General Secretary Kenji Miyamoto, JCP Central Committee member Masao Kasuga, and JCP Central Committee Deputy Minister Tomio Nishizawa arrived in Wuhan from Beijing today.
    Representatives of brother parties from twelve Latin American countries also arrived in Wuhan from Beijing today. They are: Luis Corvalán, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Chile; Pompeyo Márquez, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Venezuela; Alonso Ojeda Olaechea, Member of the Political Bureau; Pedro Ortega Díaz, Member of the Political Bureau; Guillermo García Ponce, Member of the Central Committee; Gilberto Vieira, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Colombia; Joaquín Moreno Guas, Member of the Political Bureau; Raúl Acosta, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Peru; Jorge del Prado, Member of the Central Committee; Humberto Ramírez, Member of the Political Political Committee of the Communist Party of Bolivia; Elías Muñoz, Member of the Central Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Ecuador; Félice Pescrotcnic, Secretary of the Central Finance Committee of the Communist Party of Argentina; Wilfrido Velázquez, Organizational Secretary of the Las Villas Provincial Committee of the Popular Socialist Party of Cuba; and representatives of the People's Vanguard Party of Costa Rica, the People's Party of Panama, the Communist Party of Brazil, and the Communist Party of Paraguay.
    Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, also arrived in Wuhan from Beijing today.
    Zhang Pinghua and Zhao Xinchu, Secretaries of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the CPC, Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the CPC, Song Yiping and Liu Huinong, Secretaries of the Secretariat, and other responsible cadres of the Hubei Provincial Committee and Wuhan Municipal Committee of the CPC were there to welcome them.
    In the evening, the Hubei Provincial Committee of the CPC hosted a banquet and a soiree to entertain the representatives of the brother parties.

### 日共总书记宫本显治等离京  拉丁美洲十二个国家的兄弟党代表到武汉参观

1959-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    日共总书记宫本显治等离京
    拉丁美洲十二个国家的兄弟党代表到武汉参观
    新华社1日讯　日本共产党总书记宫本显治、日共中央干部会委员春日正一、日共中央副部长西泽富夫，今天离开北京到外地参观，然后经广州回国。
    宫本显治等日本共产党领导人是上月28日从平壤回到北京的。
    今天到机场欢送的有中国共产党中央委员会副主席周恩来，政治局候补委员康生，书记处候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员刘宁一、伍修权，中共中央机关负责工作人员熊复等。
    到机场欢送的还有在京的日本和平人士、亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长西园寺公一。
    新华社武汉1日电　日本共产党总书记宫本显治、日共中央干部会委员春日正一和日共中央副部长西泽富夫今天由北京到达武汉。
    拉丁美洲十二个国家的兄弟党代表也在今天由北京到达武汉。他们是：智利共产党总书记路易斯·科尔巴兰，委内瑞拉共产党中央委员会书记处书记庞佩约·马凯斯、政治局委员阿伦索·奥里达·奥拉耶切阿、政治局委员皮得罗·欧尔特卡·吉阿斯、中央委员吉耶尔莫·古阿尔丁，哥伦比亚共产党总书记希尔伯托·维埃拉、政治局委员侯阿金·毛列诺·古阿斯，秘鲁共产党总书记腊乌尔·阿哥斯塔、中央委员霍尔海·德尔·布腊多，玻利维亚共产党中央政治委员会委员翁伯托·拉米雷斯，厄瓜多尔共产党中央执行委员会委员厄利阿斯·穆纽斯，阿根廷共产党中央财务委员会书记弗利贝·培斯罗特尼克，古巴人民社会党拉斯维利亚斯省委组织书记威尔夫利多·维拉斯克斯和哥斯达黎加人民先锋党、巴拿马人民党、巴西共产党以及巴拉圭共产党的代表。
    中共中央书记处书记王稼祥也在今天自北京到达武汉。
    前往欢迎的有中共湖北省委书记处书记张平化、赵辛初，中共武汉市委第一书记宋侃夫、书记处书记宋一平、刘惠农以及湖北省委、武汉市委的其他负责干部。
    晚上，中共湖北省委设宴并举行晚会招待各兄弟党的代表。
