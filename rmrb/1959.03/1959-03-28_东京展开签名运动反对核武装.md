Title: Tokyo Launches Signature Campaign Against Nuclear Armament
Author:
Date: 1959-03-28
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 26th: The Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs launched a signature campaign in Tokyo yesterday against Japan's nuclear armament and against the revision of the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty."
    According to a broadcast by Japanese radio, in approximately one hour, more than 500 people signed at Maebashi Station alone. According to the plan, this signature campaign will continue until April 15th.

### 东京展开签名运动反对核武装

1959-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    东京展开签名运动反对核武装
    新华社26日讯　东京消息：日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会昨天在东京展开反对日本实行核武装和反对修改日美“安全条约”签名运动。
    据日本电台广播，在大约一小时内，仅在前桥车站一个地方就有五百多人签名。根据计划，这次签名运动将继续到4月15日为止。
