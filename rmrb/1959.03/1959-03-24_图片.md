    Wang Ruizhen (second from the right), a member of the Communist Youth League and a female worker in the spinning workshop of the Jinzhou Cotton Textile Factory in Liaoning Province, who was awarded the title of labor model for three years, has consistently and conscientiously implemented and executed various advanced experiences promoted by the national textile industry, reducing roller lap-ups to 0.2%. She is passing on her advanced experience to others.
                                Xinhua News Agency reporter Miao Ming photo
