Title: Sino-German Academies of Sciences Sign Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 18th: A scientific cooperation agreement and a 1959 implementation plan between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic were signed in Berlin yesterday.

According to the agreement, the two academies will conduct close cooperation in natural sciences and social sciences research, and provide each other with necessary assistance. The agreement also stipulates that the two academies should closely cooperate and support each other in participating in the activities of international scientific organizations.

The representatives who signed the agreement were Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Steinicke, Vice President of the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic.

### 中德科学院签订合作协定

1959-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    中德科学院签订合作协定
    新华社柏林18日电　中国科学院和德意志民主共和国科学院科学合作协定及1959年执行计划昨天在柏林签字。
    根据协定，两国科学院在自然科学和社会科学研究方面将进行密切的合作，相互给予必要的协助。协定并规定了两国科学院在参加国际科学组织活动中要紧密合作、相互支持。
    双方签字的代表是中国科学院副院长吴有训和德意志民主共和国科院学副院长施太尼次。
