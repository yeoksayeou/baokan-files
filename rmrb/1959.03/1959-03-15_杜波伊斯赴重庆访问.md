Title: Du Bois Visits Chongqing

Author:
Date: 1959-03-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, March 14th: The renowned American Black scholar Dr. W.E.B. Du Bois and his wife, accompanied by Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General, left Wuhan by boat today for a visit to Chongqing. At the dock to see them off were Zeng Dun, Chairman of the Hubei Provincial and Wuhan Municipal Branches of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Yu Heiding, Vice President of the Hubei Provincial and Wuhan Municipal Branches of the Chinese People’s Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Xu Shuzhen, Deputy Director of the Wuhan Municipal Women's Federation, and others. During their four days in Wuhan, Dr. Du Bois and his wife visited the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, the Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation, and the Heavy Machine Tool Plant.

### 杜波伊斯赴重庆访问

1959-03-15
第3版()
专栏：

    杜波伊斯赴重庆访问
    新华社武汉14日电　美国著名黑人学者杜波伊斯博士和他的夫人，今天由中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林、副秘书长朱伯深陪同，乘轮船离开武汉去重庆访问。到码头送行的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会湖北省和武汉市分会主席曾惇，中国人民对外文化协会湖北省和武汉市分会副会长于黑丁，武汉市妇女联合会副主任徐淑贞等。杜波伊斯博士和他的夫人在武汉的四天中，曾先后参观了武汉长江大桥、武汉钢铁公司和重型机床厂。
