Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Messages on Cambodian King's Birthday and National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-03-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 5th. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to congratulate King Norodom Suramarit of the Kingdom of Cambodia on his birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia. The telegram reads as follows:
Your Majesty King Norodom Suramarit of the Kingdom of Cambodia:

    On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia, I would like to express my sincere congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of your country on behalf of my country and all the Chinese people, and in my own name. May the friendship between China and Cambodia last forever, may the Kingdom of Cambodia prosper, and may the people be happy. May Your Majesty live a long life.

                                                    Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, March 3, 1959

Xinhua News Agency, March 5th. Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, to congratulate King Norodom Suramarit of Cambodia on his birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia. The telegram reads as follows:
Your Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia:

    On the occasion of the birthday of His Majesty King Norodom Suramarit of Cambodia and your country's National Day, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Highness and the government of the Kingdom of Cambodia on behalf of the Chinese government and in my own name.

    Over the past year, with the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Cambodia and Your Highness's re-visit to our country, the friendly relations between our two countries have been further developed. The friendship between China and Cambodia, which is based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, is in the interests of the people of both our countries and is conducive to peace in Asia and the world. Under Your Highness's leadership, the Cambodian people are making new efforts to build their own country. The Chinese people are delighted with the achievements of the Cambodian people. I wish the government and people of the Kingdom of Cambodia greater success in their cause of safeguarding national independence and building their own country. May the profound friendship and close cooperation between the people of China and Cambodia be increasingly consolidated and developed.

                                                    Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, March 3, 1959, in Beijing

### 毛主席　周总理电贺柬埔寨国王寿辰和国庆

1959-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席　周总理电贺柬埔寨国王寿辰和国庆
    新华社5日讯　毛泽东主席致电祝贺柬埔寨王国诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特国王寿辰和柬埔寨王国国庆。电文如下：柬埔寨王国诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特国王陛下：
    值此陛下寿辰和柬埔寨王国国庆之际，我谨代表我的国家和全体中国人民并且以我个人的名义向陛下和贵国人民表示诚挚的祝贺。祝中柬友好万古长青，祝柬埔寨王国繁荣，人民幸福。祝陛下万寿无疆。
                                                    中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1959年3月3日
    新华社5日讯　周恩来总理致电柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克，祝贺柬埔寨国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特寿辰和柬埔寨王国国庆。电文如下：柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下：
    值此柬埔寨国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特陛下寿辰和贵国国庆之际，我谨代表中国政府并且以我个人的名义向殿下和柬埔寨王国政府致以热诚的祝贺。
    一年来，随着中柬两国外交关系的建立和殿下再次访问我国，我们两国间的友好关系获得了进一步的发展。建立在和平共处五项原则基础上的中柬两国间的友谊，是符合我们两国人民的利益的，而且有利于亚洲和世界的和平。柬埔寨人民在殿下的领导下，正在为建设自己的国家进行着新的努力。中国人民对柬埔寨人民所取得的成就感到高兴。祝柬埔寨王国政府和柬埔寨人民在维护民族独立和建设自己国家的事业中取得更大的成就。祝中柬两国人民间的深厚友谊和亲密合作日益巩固和发展。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　1959年3月3日于北京
