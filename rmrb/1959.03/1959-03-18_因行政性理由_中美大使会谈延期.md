Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Postponed Due to Administrative Reasons
Author:
Date: 1959-03-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 16th -- The eighty-eighth meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks, originally scheduled for March 19th, has been postponed to 2 p.m. on March 24th by mutual agreement due to administrative reasons.

### 因行政性理由  中美大使会谈延期

1959-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    因行政性理由
    中美大使会谈延期
    新华社华沙16日电　原订于3月19日举行的中美两国大使级会谈第八十八次会议，因行政性理由经双方协议，要延期至3月24日十四时举行。
