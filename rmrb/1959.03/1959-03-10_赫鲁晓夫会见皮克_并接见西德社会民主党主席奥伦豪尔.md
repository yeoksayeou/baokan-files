Title: Khrushchev Meets Pieck and Receives West German Social Democratic Party Chairman Ollenhauer
Author:
Date: 1959-03-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 9th: Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Khrushchev today paid a visit to German Democratic Republic President Pieck. Khrushchev extended warm, fraternal greetings to President Pieck. Khrushchev and Pieck then held a cordial half-hour conversation. They discussed international issues such as Macmillan's visit to the Soviet Union and the French elections.
    Present at the conversation were East German leaders Ulbricht, Grotewohl, and others. From the Soviet side were Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Zorin and Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Pervukhin.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 9th: According to TASS, Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Khrushchev received the Chairman of the German Social Democratic Party, Ollenhauer, at the Soviet Embassy in the German Democratic Republic on March 9th.
    During the meeting, the two sides discussed issues related to the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany, the elimination of the occupation regime in West Berlin, and the consolidation of peace and security in Europe, and unanimously agreed that the above issues should be resolved through peaceful means and negotiations.

### 赫鲁晓夫会见皮克  并接见西德社会民主党主席奥伦豪尔

1959-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫会见皮克
    并接见西德社会民主党主席奥伦豪尔
    新华社柏林9日电　苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫今天拜会了德意志民主共和国总统皮克。赫鲁晓夫向皮克总统致以热情的、兄弟般的敬意。赫鲁晓夫和皮克接着进行了历时半小时的亲切的谈话。他们谈到了麦克米伦访苏和法国选举等国际问题。
    谈话时在座的有德意志民主共和国领导人乌布利希、格罗提渥等。苏联方面在座的有苏联外交部副部长佐林和苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使别尔乌辛。
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫9日在苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使馆接见了德国社会民主党主席奥伦豪尔。
    接见时，双方谈到了有关缔结对德和约、消除西柏林占领制度以及巩固欧洲和平和安全等问题，并且一致认为，上述问题应该通过和平途径和谈判的方法获得解决。
