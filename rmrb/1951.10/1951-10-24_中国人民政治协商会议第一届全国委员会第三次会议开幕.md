Title: The First Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1951-10-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: The Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) opened in Beijing on the 23rd.

    One hundred and forty-three members of the National Committee attended today's meeting. Those attending the meeting as observers included members of the Central People's Government Committee, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, chairmen and vice-chairmen of local consultative committees, heads of local people's governments, and representatives specially invited by the Standing Committee of the National Committee through consultation, including representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, representatives of industrial labor models, representatives of agricultural labor models, representatives of old revolutionary base areas, representatives of minority nationalities, representatives of industry and commerce, representatives of experts, representatives of educators, representatives of literary and art workers, representatives of youth workers, representatives of women workers, representatives of overseas Chinese, representatives of religious circles, and social figures, totaling 518 people.

    The meeting opened at 3 p.m. Chairman Mao Zedong of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference delivered the opening speech. Today's meeting heard the political report by Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government, and adjourned at 5 p.m.

    The meeting will continue to hear reports tomorrow. (Picture attached)

### 中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议开幕

1951-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议开幕
    【新华社二十三日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议于二十三日在京开幕。
    出席今日会议的有全国委员会委员一百四十三人。列席会议的有中央人民政府委员会委员、中央人民政府各部门负责人、地方协商委员会主席副主席、地方人民政府负责人以及全国委员会常务委员会协商特别邀请的中国人民志愿军代表、中国人民解放军代表、工业劳动模范代表、农业劳动模范代表、革命老根据地代表、少数民族代表、工商界代表、专家代表、教育工作者代表、文艺工作者代表、青年工作者代表、妇女工作者代表、华侨代表、宗教界代表、社会人士等共五百一十八人。
    会议于下午三时开幕。人民政治协商会议全国委员会毛泽东主席致开会词。今日的会议听取了中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理的政治报告，下午五时休会。
    会议明日将继续听取报告。（附图片）
