Title: Foreign Guests Visiting Beijing Continue to Tour and Visit Various Places in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-10-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Foreign guests who were invited to participate in our National Day celebration continued their tour and visits in Beijing today. This morning, delegations from the Soviet Union, Romania, Hungary, Poland, and India visited the Peking Union Medical College Hospital. The Vietnam People's Observation Delegation visited Zhangguozhuang Village in the suburban rural area, and V.K. Rao, a member of the Indian Goodwill Delegation to China, visited Xiaohongmen Village in the suburbs. In the afternoon, Obalin, a member of the Soviet People's Observation Delegation, visited the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute. Yunusov and István Rusznyák, President of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, visited the Institute of Applied Physics and the Institute of Modern Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 来京外宾继续在北京各处参观访问

1951-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    来京外宾继续在北京各处参观访问
    【新华社六日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的外宾今天继续在京参观访问。苏联、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、波兰和印度代表团上午参观了协和医院，越南人民观礼代表团参观了郊区农村张郭庄，印度亲善访华团团员维·克·劳参观了郊区的小红门村。下午，苏联人民观礼代表团团员奥巴林参观了华北农业科学研究所，尤努索夫和匈牙利科学院院长伊斯特万·鲁斯尼亚克参观了中国科学院应用物理研究所和近代物理研究所。
