Title: Government Administration Council Holds 105th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-10-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 5th—The 105th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 5th. The meeting heard and approved the comprehensive work report of the Ministry of Light Industry from January to August 1951, presented by Minister Huang Yanpei. The meeting also passed several appointment and removal cases.

### 政务院举行一零五次会议

1951-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行一零五次会议
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百零五次政务会议于五日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府轻工业部黄炎培部长所作的该部一九五一年一月至八月工作综合报告。会议并通过任免案多起。
