Title: People in Various Regions of Romania Celebrate Army Day
Author:
Date: 1951-10-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 5th. TASS reports: The Romanian people celebrated the first Army Day on the 2nd (Note: The Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly announced in early August this year that October 2nd would be Romanian People's Republic Army Day). The Bucharest Municipal People's Committee and the Municipal Committee of the Workers' Party held a celebration meeting at the Military Theater. Representatives of the Romanian army and people from all walks of life in the capital attended. When the leaders of the Romanian Party and government appeared on the rostrum, the attendees cheered enthusiastically. General Bodnaras, Minister of the Romanian Armed Forces, delivered a report at the meeting, discussing the history of the growth of the Romanian Armed Forces and its current tasks. His report was repeatedly interrupted by cheers from the participants, such as "Salute the Soviet Red Army, the example for the Romanian Army" and "Salute Stalin." At the end of the meeting, a congratulatory telegram was passed to Stalin and Romanian people's leader Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej. In the evening, people from all walks of life in Bucharest held a celebration reception at the Palace of Military Personnel.

Celebration meetings were also held in Stalin City, Cluj, Timisoara, Galați, and other cities in Romania.

### 罗各地人民庆祝建军节

1951-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    罗各地人民庆祝建军节
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚人民在二日庆祝第一届建军节（按罗马尼亚国民议会主席团在今年八月初宣布十月二日为罗马尼亚人民共和国建军节）。首都布加勒斯特市人民委员会和工人党市委员会在军人剧院举行了庆祝大会。参加者有罗马尼亚军队的代表和首都各界人民。罗马尼亚党和政府的领袖在主席台上出现时，到会者热烈欢呼。罗马尼亚武装部队部部长波德纳拉斯将军在会上作了报告，论述罗马尼亚武装部队成长的历史和当前的任务。他的报告一再被与会者“向罗马尼亚军队的榜样苏联红军致敬”和“向斯大林致敬”的欢呼声所打断。大会最后通过向斯大林和罗马尼亚人民领袖乔治乌—德治致敬电。晚间，布加勒斯特各界人民在军人之宫举行了庆祝酒会。
    在斯大林城、克罗什、的密索拉、加拉兹和罗马尼亚其他城市也举行了庆祝大会。
