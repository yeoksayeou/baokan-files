Title: First Group of China's Delegation Departs to Attend the Second World Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-10-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: A portion of the Chinese delegation attending the Second World Peace Council Meeting, including Mao Dun, Cai Tingkai, Mei Ru'ao, Liu Guan-yi, Chen Dingmin and others, totaling eleven people, departed from Beijing by plane at 6:00 AM today for Moscow, and will then proceed to Vienna to attend the meeting. Those who saw them off at the airport included Liu Ningyi, Wu Han, Xu Baoju, Ai Qing, Wu Maosun, Liao Mengxing, Dong Yueqian, Gong Peng, Zhao Zhongchi, Cui Yueli and more than thirty others.
    Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: The list of the Chinese delegation attending the Second World Peace Council Meeting is as follows:
    Head of Delegation: Guo Moruo.
    Council Members: Ma Yinchu, Mao Dun, Zhang Bojun, Cai Tingkai, Wu Yaozong, Mei Ru'ao, Xiao San (abroad).
    Secretary-General: Liu Guan-yi, Deputy Secretary-General: Chen Dingmin.
    Staff: Liu Qun, Yan Mingzhi, Wang Weiping, Lü Xia, Deng Ziruo, Li Bingquan, Zheng Jingkang, Yu Yidi, Bai Yangling, Wang Wenyi.

### 出席二届世界和平理事会会议  我国代表团首批人员启程

1951-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    出席二届世界和平理事会会议
    我国代表团首批人员启程
    【新华社二十三日讯】出席世界和平理事会第二届会议的中国代表团部分人员茅盾、蔡廷锴、梅汝璈、刘贯一、陈定民等十一人，在今日上午六时乘飞机离京赴莫斯科转赴维也纳参加会议。到机场欢送的有：刘宁一、吴晗、许宝驹、艾青、吴茂荪、廖梦醒、董越千、龚澎、赵仲池、崔月犁等三十多人。
    【新华社二十三日讯】出席世界和平理事会第二届会议的中国代表团名单如下：
    团长：郭沫若。
    理事：马寅初、茅盾、章伯钧、蔡廷锴、吴耀宗、梅汝璈、萧三（在国外）。
    秘书长：刘贯一、副秘书长陈定民。
    工作人员：刘群、阎明智、汪维平、吕霞、邓子若、李炳泉、郑景康、余贻倜、白扬灵、王文仪。
