Title: Indian and Other Countries' Delegations Leave Shenyang for Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1951-10-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 14th: The Indian Goodwill Visit Delegation to China and the people's observation delegations from Indonesia, Burma, Pakistan, and other countries left Shenyang for Tianjin at 9:30 PM on the 14th. Those who saw them off at the station included Liu Zhiming, Vice Chairman of the Northeast General Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Jiao Ruoyu, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, and representatives from various people's organizations and democratic parties in the Northeast and Shenyang. Before their departure, the various people's organizations jointly hosted a farewell banquet.

On the morning of the 14th, the delegation visited rural areas in the suburbs of Shenyang. In the afternoon, some representatives visited the mosque and listened to a report on the economic situation in the Northeast.

### 印度等国代表团离沈赴津

1951-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    印度等国代表团离沈赴津
    【新华社沈阳十四日电】印度亲善访华团及印尼、缅甸、巴基斯坦等国人民观礼代表团于十四日午后九时三十分离沈阳去天津。到车站欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会东北总分会副主席刘芝明、沈阳市副市长焦若愚和东北与沈阳市各人民团体、各民主党派代表等。临行前，各人民团体联合设宴欢送。
    十四日上午，代表团曾到沈阳市郊农村参观。午后，部分代表参观了清真寺，并听取关于东北经济情况的报告。
