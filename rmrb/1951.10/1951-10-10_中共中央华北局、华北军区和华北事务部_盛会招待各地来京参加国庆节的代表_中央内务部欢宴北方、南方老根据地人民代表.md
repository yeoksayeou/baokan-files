Title: CCP Central Committee North China Bureau, North China Military Region, and North China Affairs Department Host Grand Reception for Representatives from Various Regions to Celebrate National Day in Beijing; Central Ministry of Interior Hosts Banquet for People's Representatives from Northern and Southern Old Revolutionary Base Areas
Author:
Date: 1951-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The Chinese Communist Party Central Committee North China Bureau, the Chinese People's Liberation Army North China Military Region, and the Central People's Government Government Administration Council North China Affairs Department, held a grand reception at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall at 2:00 PM on the 8th to host over two thousand people who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations. The attendees included people's representatives from the Northern and Southern old revolutionary base areas, combat heroes of the People's Liberation Army and the People's Volunteer Army, militia models from the North China old liberated areas, industrial labor models, agricultural labor models, labor models from the Huai River and other water conservancy projects, overseas Chinese returning to observe the celebrations, representatives of various ethnic minorities, and model workers from private industrial and commercial sectors.

At the meeting, Nie Rongzhen, Second Secretary of the North China Bureau and Commander of the North China Military Region, and Bo Yibo, First Secretary, delivered speeches successively, both encouraging all representatives and heroic models to work even harder.

At the meeting, the North China Bureau, North China Military Region, and North China Affairs Department also presented gifts to the representatives and heroic models from various sectors.

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The Central People's Government Ministry of Interior hosted banquets on the 8th and 9th respectively to entertain people's representatives from the Northern old revolutionary base areas and the Southern old revolutionary base areas. Xu Teli, Shen Junru, Peng Zemin, Zhang Bojun, Tan Pingshan, Li Liuru, Li Weihan, Xu Deheng, He Changgong, Zhu Xuefan, Xiao Hua, Deng Yingchao and other leaders of various departments of the Central People's Government and heads of various people's organizations were invited to attend.

At the beginning of the banquet, Xie Juezai, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Interior, delivered a speech, encouraging all representatives to firmly remember Chairman Mao's words: "Carry forward the revolutionary tradition and strive for greater glory," and work together with the people of the whole country to consolidate and build our great motherland. The people's representatives from the old revolutionary base areas all expressed their gratitude to the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao for their concern and hospitality. During the banquet, they frequently toasted to the health of Chairman Mao and the great victory of the Chinese people.

### 中共中央华北局、华北军区和华北事务部  盛会招待各地来京参加国庆节的代表  中央内务部欢宴北方、南方老根据地人民代表

1951-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央华北局、华北军区和华北事务部
    盛会招待各地来京参加国庆节的代表
    中央内务部欢宴北方、南方老根据地人民代表
    【新华社九日讯】中国共产党中央华北局、中国人民解放军华北军区和中央人民政府政务院华北事务部，在八日下午二时假中山公园音乐堂举行盛大的招待会，招待来京参加国庆节观礼的北方、南方老根据地人民代表、人民解放军和人民志愿军的战斗英雄、华北老区的民兵模范、工业劳动模范、农业劳动模范、治淮和其他水利事业的劳动模范、归国观礼的华侨、各少数民族代表和私营工商业的模范工作者共两千余人。
    会上华北局第二书记兼华北军区司令员聂荣臻和第一书记薄一波先后讲话，他们一致勉励各位代表和英雄模范更加努力工作。
    会上华北局、华北军区和华北事务部并给各个代表和各业英雄模范送了礼物。
     【新华社九日讯】中央人民政府内务部于八、九两日分别欢宴北方老根据地和南方老根据地的人民代表。应邀出席的有中央人民政府各部门首长和各人民团体负责人徐特立、沈钧儒、彭泽民、章伯钧、谭平山、李六如、李维汉、许德珩、何长工、朱学范、萧华、邓颖超等。
    宴会开始时，中央人民政府内务部部长谢觉哉致词，勉励全体代表牢牢记住毛主席的话：“发扬革命传统，争取更大光荣”，和全国人民一道努力巩固和建设我们伟大的祖国。老根据地人民代表都十分感谢中央人民政府和毛主席对他们的关怀和款待。席间频频为毛主席的健康和中国人民的伟大胜利而干杯。
