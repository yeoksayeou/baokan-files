Title: Preparatory Committee of the China National News Publication and Printing Workers' Union Hosts News Workers from Foreign Observer Delegations
Author:
Date: 1951-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: The Preparatory Committee of the China National News Publication and Printing Workers' Union held a tea party today to host news workers from various foreign observer delegations attending the National Day celebrations. Eight news workers from Vietnam, India, Burma, Indonesia, and Pakistan were invited to attend. Fan Changjiang, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China National News Publication and Printing Workers' Union, and heads of various departments attended the reception.

Fan Changjiang said in his speech: We warmly hope to cooperate with journalists from Asian countries who support peace. A united front of Asian peace journalists with a common purpose will surely have a significant impact on the cause of peace in Asia.

Pakistani representative Iftikharuddin stated: The example set by China will be followed by Asian countries. He hoped that news workers from Asian countries would strengthen ties and intensify the struggle against imperialist aggression and for peace in the Far East and world peace. Indian representative Karangji congratulated New China on the achievements of its news and broadcasting undertakings over the past two years and expressed that India's progressive press stands together with Chinese news workers. Vietnamese representative Nguyen Xuan Thuy said: Coming to China has given me a more concrete understanding of China's greatness. China's news undertaking has grown, and we must learn from China's experience. Indonesian representative Tabrani and Burmese representative Tekin Lun Du both expressed that the people of Asia should unite to oppose imperialism. After the symposium, a banquet was held, during which the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the unity of the Asian people.

### 我新闻出版印刷工会筹委会  招待各国观礼团的新闻工作者

1951-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我新闻出版印刷工会筹委会
    招待各国观礼团的新闻工作者
    【新华社六日讯】中国新闻出版印刷工会筹备委员会今天举行茶会，招待参加国庆节的各国观礼代表团中的新闻工作者。应邀出席的有越南、印度、缅甸、印尼、巴基斯坦的新闻工作者八人。中国新闻出版印刷工会筹委会主任范长江及各部负责人都出席招待。
    范长江致词说：我们热忱地希望和亚洲各国的拥护和平的记者合作。一个有共同目的的亚洲和平新闻记者的阵线，对于亚洲和平事业必将发生重大影响。
    巴基斯坦代表伊夫提卡鲁丁发言说：中国所树立的榜样，亚洲的国家都会跟着作。希望亚洲国家的新闻工作者加强联系，加强反对帝国主义侵略和争取远东和平与世界和平的斗争。印度代表卡朗吉对新中国新闻和广播事业两年来的成就表示祝贺，并表示印度进步的新闻界和中国新闻工作者是在一起的。越南代表阮春水说：到中国来，对中国的伟大认识得更具体了。中国新闻事业已经成长起来，我们要学习中国的经验。印尼代表塔勃兰尼和缅甸代表德钦伦都表示亚洲人民应当团结起来反对帝国主义。座谈结束后举行宴会，席间宾主一再为亚洲人民的团结而干杯。
