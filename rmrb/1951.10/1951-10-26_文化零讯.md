Date: 1951-10-26
Page: 3

    Cultural Snippets
    ▲ The first detachment of the Chinese Red Cross International Medical and Disease Prevention Service Team returned to Beijing on the 24th, after completing its mission in Korea. The team consisted of 139 members, including medical workers from various local Red Cross branches and other medical institutions, who departed from Beijing for Korea in March of this year. The team was responsible for internal and external medical treatment and various epidemic prevention work in various parts of Korea. Over the past seven months, it has achieved considerable results and has been well received by the Korean people. When the team arrived in Beijing, representatives from the China People's Anti-American Aggression Aid Korea General Association, the Chinese Red Cross, the China People's Relief General Association, the Chinese Medical Association, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the Korean Embassy in China, totaling more than 200 people, welcomed them at the station.
    ▲ Guangzhou held its Second People's Sports Games from the 14th to the 18th of this month. A total of 7,470 athletes participated in the Games, including 951 worker athletes and 317 farmer athletes. The Games included track and field, team events, ball games, swimming competitions, and various group performances. During the Games, 53 athletes broke the records of the city's First Sports Games. In particular, the performance of worker athletes has improved significantly. For example, worker Chen Shuhuang's high jump result reached 1.74 meters, which is close to the national record.

### 文化零讯

1951-10-26
第3版()
专栏：文化零讯

    文化零讯
    ▲中国红十字会国际医防服务队第一大队，在朝鲜完成工作任务后，于二十四日返抵北京。该队队员共一百三十九人，包括红十字会各地分会和其他医疗机构的医务工作者，在今年三月由北京前往朝鲜。该队在朝鲜各地分别担任内外各科医疗及各种防疫工作，七个月来，有不少成绩，深受朝鲜人民欢迎。该队到达北京时有中国人民抗美援朝总会、中国红十字会、中国人民救济总会、中华医学会、中华全国民主妇女联合会和朝鲜驻华大使馆等单位代表二百多人到车站欢迎。
    ▲广州市在本月十四日至十八日举行全市第二届人民体育运动大会。参加大会的运动员有七千四百七十人，其中工人运动员有九百五十一人，农民运动员三百一十七人。大会举行了田径、集体竞技、球类、游泳等比赛和各种集体表演。大会各项竞赛中有五十三人打破了该市第一届运动会纪录。特别是工人运动员的成绩有显著进步。如工人陈书煌的跳高成绩达一·七四公尺，已接近全国最高纪录。
