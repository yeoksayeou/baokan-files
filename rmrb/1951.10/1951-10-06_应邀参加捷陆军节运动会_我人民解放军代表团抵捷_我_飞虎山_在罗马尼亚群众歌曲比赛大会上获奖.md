Title: PLA Delegation Arrives in Czechoslovakia to Attend Czechoslovak Army Day Games; Our "Flying Tiger Mountain" Wins Award at Romanian Mass Song Competition

Author:

Date: 1951-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 4th: At the invitation of the Czechoslovak Ministry of National Defense, the Chinese People's Liberation Army delegation, led by General Liu Daosheng and General Xiong Botao, arrived in Prague by train from Moscow on the 1st to participate in the Army Day Games, which will begin on the 6th. General Ratovizka, Deputy Minister of the Czechoslovak Ministry of National Defense, and representatives of the Czechoslovak People's Public Security Forces, the Militia, and Army sports organizations greeted them at the station. Colonel Yang Yazhong, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, also welcomed them at the station. Ratovizka delivered a welcome speech on behalf of General Čepička, the Czechoslovak Minister of National Defense. General Liu Daosheng delivered a reply. The delegates walked out of the station amidst cheers from the Czechoslovak Army and Militia and military music, and proceeded to the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia to attend the National Day reception.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 4th: The Mass Song Competition jointly organized by the Art Committee of the Romanian Council of Ministers and the Romanian Composers Association was held in Bucharest from September 22nd to 27th. The theme of this competition was to sing for peace and friendship among nations. Twenty-four works by composers from the Soviet Union, China, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, and Albania participated in the competition. According to the judging results of the competition, the first prize was awarded to "Defend Peace" by Deli of the Soviet Union, "Song of Friendship" by Barady of Romania, and "Song of the Peace Warrior" by Moratli of the Soviet Union. Kohan and Asla Ai'er of the German Democratic Republic, Zakharov of the Soviet Union, and Criscu of Romania won the second prize. "Flying Tiger Mountain," composed by Chinese musician Zhang Wengang and with lyrics by Guan Hua, won the third prize. At the opening ceremony of the competition, Li Ling, representative of the All-China Federation of Music Workers, made a report on the musical work of the Chinese people. Li Ling was also appointed as a member of the judging committee of the conference.

### 应邀参加捷陆军节运动会  我人民解放军代表团抵捷  我“飞虎山”在罗马尼亚群众歌曲比赛大会上获奖

1951-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参加捷陆军节运动会
    我人民解放军代表团抵捷
    我“飞虎山”在罗马尼亚群众歌曲比赛大会上获奖
    【新华社布拉格四日电】应捷克斯洛伐克国防部邀请来捷参加从六日开始的陆军节运动会的中国人民解放军代表团，由刘道生将军和熊伯涛将军率领，在一日自莫斯科乘火车抵布拉格。赴车站欢迎的有捷国防部副部长拉托维茨卡将军，捷人民公安部队、民兵和陆军体育团体的代表多人。中国驻捷大使馆陆军武官杨亚中上校也到车站欢迎。拉托维茨卡代表捷国防部长契皮茨卡将军致欢迎词。刘道生将军致答词。代表们在捷陆军与民兵的夹道欢呼声和军乐声中步出车站，迳赴我国驻捷大使馆参加国庆节招待会。
    【新华社布拉格四日电】罗马尼亚部长会议艺术委员会和罗马尼亚作曲家协会联合主办的群众歌曲比赛大会，已于九月二十二日至二十七日在布加勒斯特举行。这次赛会以歌唱和平与各国友好为主题。参加比赛的有苏联、中国、德意志民主共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚和阿尔巴尼亚作曲家的二十四件作品。赛会评判结果，荣获一等奖的有苏联戴理的“保卫和平”，罗马尼亚巴拉第的“友谊之歌”，苏联莫拉特里的“和平战士之歌”。德意志民主共和国的高汉和阿斯拉爱佛尔，苏联的查哈罗夫和罗马尼亚的克里斯库荣获二等奖。我国音乐家张文纲作曲、管桦作词的“飞虎山”荣膺三等奖。在赛会的开幕典礼上，中华全国音乐工作者协会代表李凌曾作了关于中国人民音乐工作的报告。李凌并被聘为大会的评判委员会委员。
