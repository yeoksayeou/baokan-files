Title: Departure of Railway and Automotive Personnel from Central South China and Qingdao to Aid Korea

Author:

Date: 1951-10-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Railway workers and automotive drivers from the Central South region and Qingdao Municipality continue to depart for Korea in batches. The eleventh group of the Resist America, Aid Korea volunteer transportation team, composed of workers from the Nanchang Branch of the Shanghai Railway Bureau, and the seventh group of volunteer drivers to Korea from Qingdao Municipality, set off from their respective locations on the 13th and 8th of this month. The first group of volunteer automotive aid teams, organized by automotive drivers from the Central South region, departed from Hankou on the 22nd of last month.

### 中南地区和青岛市援朝铁路汽车人员出发

1951-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中南地区和青岛市援朝铁路汽车人员出发
    【新华社十九日讯】中南地区和青岛市的铁路职工、汽车司机继续分批赴朝。上海铁路局南昌分局职工组成的第十一批抗美援朝志愿运输队和青岛市第七批志愿赴朝司机已分别于本月十三日、本月八日由两地启程。中南地区汽车司机组织的第一批志愿援朝队，已于上月二十二日由汉口出发。
