Title: Iranian Authorities Close Progressive Newspaper
Author:
Date: 1951-10-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran dispatch, from TASS: The Iranian progressive newspaper "Bisou Ayamdi" was sealed up by the Iranian police bureau on October 17. "Rahneema Milet" was published on the 18th to replace "Bisou Ayamdi." The newspaper said, "Bisou Ayamdi" was sealed up by the police authorities according to the plan to launch attacks and suppress progressive newspapers and organizations.

### 伊朗当局封闭进步报纸

1951-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    伊朗当局封闭进步报纸
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社德黑兰讯：伊朗进步报纸“比苏·阿杨第报”在十月十七日为伊朗警察局查封。“拉赫尼马·米利特报”在十八日代替“比苏·阿杨第报”出刊。该报说：“比苏·阿杨第报”是警察当局按照对进步报纸和组织发动进攻和镇压的计划查封的。
