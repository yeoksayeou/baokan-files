Title: Telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to General Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1951-10-25
Page: 1

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    To Comrade Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army:
        On this first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers going abroad to assist the Korean People's Army in resisting American aggression, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China warmly congratulates the Korean people and the Korean People's Army under your leadership on the great victories they have achieved in the anti-aggression war. In the struggle against the common enemy, the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army have fought side by side, further consolidating the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples. The Chinese people will forever stand with the Korean people, work together to achieve a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue, strive for the victorious conclusion of the Korean people's anti-aggression struggle, and jointly fight to defend peace in Asia and world peace. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                October 24, 1951

### 中国共产党中央委员会  致金日成将军的贺电

1951-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会
    致金日成将军的贺电朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成同志：
    当此中国人民志愿军出国协助朝鲜人民军抵抗美国侵略的一周年之际，中国共产党中央委员会热烈地祝贺在您领导下的朝鲜人民和朝鲜人民军在反侵略战争中所获得的伟大胜利。在反对共同敌人的斗争中，中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军并肩作战，已进一步巩固了中国人民和朝鲜人民之间的兄弟般的友谊。中国人民一定永远和朝鲜人民站在一起，为实现和平解决朝鲜问题，争取朝鲜人民反侵略斗争的胜利结束，为保卫亚洲和平与世界和平而共同奋斗。中国共产党中央委员会
            一九五一年十月二十四日
