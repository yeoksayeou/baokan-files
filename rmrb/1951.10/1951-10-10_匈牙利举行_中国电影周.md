Title: Hungary Holds "Chinese Film Week"
Author:
Date: 1951-10-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: To celebrate the National Day of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese Film Week held in Budapest, the capital of Hungary, opened on September 30th. Rónai Sándor, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, attended the opening ceremony. Also present were Dobi István, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Hungary, Mihályfi Ernő, Deputy Minister of Culture, leaders of the Hungarian Working People's Party, representatives of various people's organizations, and many diplomatic envoys from China, the Soviet Union, and various People's Democratic countries to Hungary. Mihályfi Ernő, Deputy Minister of Culture of Hungary, delivered the opening speech. He emphasized the importance of the friendship between the Hungarian and Chinese people in strengthening the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union. Those present frequently cheered enthusiastically for the friendship between the people of all countries, for Mao Zedong, Rákosi Mátyás, and Stalin.

During the Chinese Film Week, the Chinese films "Daughter of China" and "Boundless Light," dubbed in Hungarian, will be screened first.

### 匈牙利举行“中国电影周”

1951-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利举行“中国电影周”
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：匈牙利为庆祝中华人民共和国国庆而举行的中国电影周，九月三十日在匈牙利首都布达佩斯揭幕。匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席罗奈伊出席了开幕典礼。出席的还有匈牙利部长会议主席道比、文化部副部长米哈依费、劳动人民党领袖们和各人民团体的代表，以及中国、苏联、各人民民主国家驻匈外交使节多人。匈牙利文化部副部长米哈依费致开幕词。他强调指出匈中两国人民的友谊对于加强以苏联为首的和平民主阵营的重要性。到会者频频为各国人民间的友谊、为毛泽东、拉科西和斯大林热烈欢呼。
    在中国电影周中，匈牙利语对白的中国影片“中华女儿”和“光芒万丈”将首先放映。
