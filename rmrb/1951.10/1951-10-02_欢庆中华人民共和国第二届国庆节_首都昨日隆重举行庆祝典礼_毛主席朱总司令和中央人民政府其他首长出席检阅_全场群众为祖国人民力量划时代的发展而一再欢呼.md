Title: Celebrating the Second National Day of the People's Republic of China, the Capital Held a Grand Celebration Ceremony Yesterday, Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and Other Leaders of the Central People's Government Attended and Inspected, the Entire Crowd Repeatedly Cheered for the Epoch-Making Development of the Motherland's People's Strength
Author:
Date: 1951-10-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st of the month. The celebration ceremony for the Second National Day of the People's Republic of China was grandly held in Tiananmen Square, Beijing, today. More than 400,000 people participated in the ceremony.

Today, Tiananmen Square appeared particularly solemn and magnificent. A huge portrait of Chairman Mao Zedong, the creator of the People's Republic of China, hung on the Tiananmen gate tower, and rows of large red flags fluttered in the wind on both sides of the gate tower. On the square opposite Tiananmen, 70,000 citizens of the capital were concentrated. They held countless red flags, turning the square into a sea of red. The wide avenue in front of Tiananmen had been paved with granite, cylinder bricks, and cement bricks to allow the People's Liberation Army, equipped with modern heavy weapons, to pass for inspection.

Today's attendees for the inspection included Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Premier of the Government Administration Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Cheng Qian, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, and Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Luo Ronghuan.

Also on the reviewing stand were members of the Central People's Government, heads of various commissions, ministries, committees, institutes, administrations, and banks, leaders of the People's Liberation Army's army, navy, and air force, standing committee members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing, and leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

Nearly 10,000 people attended the viewing ceremony today.

On the distinguished guests' viewing platform were guests invited from afar by various people's organizations in our country: Ilya Ehrenburg, member of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council and Soviet writer, and his wife; Pablo Neruda, member of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council and Chilean poet, and his wife; the Soviet People's Delegation for the National Day celebrations headed by Solovyov; the Bulgarian People's Delegation for the National Day celebrations headed by Georgi Karaslavov; the Romanian People's Delegation for the National Day celebrations headed by Ileana Răceanu; the Hungarian People's Delegation for the National Day celebrations headed by István Vás; the Korean People's Delegation for the National Day celebrations headed by Hyun Heun; the Czechoslovak People's Delegation for the National Day celebrations headed by Gusta Fučíková; the Polish People's Delegation for the National Day celebrations headed by Jerzy Putrament; the Mongolian People's Delegation for the National Day celebrations headed by Ts. Damdinsüren; the German Democratic Republic People's Delegation for the National Day celebrations headed by Diter; the Vietnamese People's Delegation for the National Day celebrations headed by Tôn Đức Thắng; the Indian Goodwill Delegation to China headed by Sundar Lal; the Burmese People's Delegation for the National Day celebrations headed by Dachaing Go Dou Mai; the Indonesian People's Delegation for the National Day celebrations headed by Tabrani; and Ibrahim Jalees, representative of the Pakistani people.

Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic personnel from various countries who watched the ceremony on the distinguished guests' viewing platform included Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czech Ambassador Weiskopf, Korean Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Head of the German Diplomatic Mission with the rank of Ambassador König, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasuren, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Vietnamese Representative with the rank of Ambassador Hoàng Văn Hoan, Swedish Ambassador Söderblom, Burmese Ambassador U Hla Maung, Danish Minister Munk, Swiss Minister Renzoli, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy Isa Mahdi, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Pakistani Embassy Ahmed Ali, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Doding, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy Fekete, and other embassy staff, as well as British government negotiation representative Lan Lainai, Norwegian government negotiation representative Golan, and Dutch government negotiation representative Hou Weimin.

Foreign guests who watched the ceremony also included the Vietnamese People's Delegation to China headed by Hoàng Quốc Việt, all members of the German Democratic Republic Cultural Delegation, Soviet experts who were invited by our government to assist our country in economic construction, representatives of the Liaison Bureau of the Asian-Australian Trade Unions of the World Federation of Trade Unions, members of the Hungarian Women's Federation delegation to North Korea, and youth representatives from various countries participating in the World Youth Peace Festival.

Also on the viewing platform were representatives of various ethnic groups, members of the Overseas Chinese returning to China for sightseeing, professors, and scholars.

There were also 98 representatives of combat heroes of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and 63 representatives of combat heroes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. One hundred and forty-seven model engineers and workers in industry, thirty-eight agricultural labor models, and dozens of model technicians and workers in Huai River harnessing and other water conservancy projects. Among these nationally renowned figures were People's Volunteer Army combat heroes Guo Zhongtian, Zhou Wenjiang, etc.; People's Liberation Army tank hero Shen Xu, bayonet hero Liu Sihu, naval combat hero Wang Daming, air force hero Liu Yaozong, etc.; labor models in state-owned industries Jiang Wanshou, Guo Shude, Ma Hengchang, Shi Yuhai, Liu Dezhen, Zhang Shaoru, Hao Jianxiu, Zhang Shuyun, Tian Guiying, etc., and labor model in private industry Chen Ronggen, etc.; agricultural labor models Li Shunda, Yang Tiancai, etc.; Huai River harnessing special-class meritorious worker Xie Hongyou and model female worker Gan Caihua, etc.

Also on the viewing platform were 994 representatives from the old revolutionary base areas in the South and North, 100 model workers in private industry and commerce, senior cadres from various government agencies, democratic parties, and people's organizations in the capital, and representatives from various places attending various professional conferences.

Chairman Mao Zedong ascended the reviewing stand at 9:50 a.m., and the whole audience applauded enthusiastically and continuously.

Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government, announced the start of the celebration ceremony. The whole audience stood at attention, the military band played the national anthem, and 28 artillery salutes were fired.

The military parade began. The military parade was commanded by Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government. Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, inspected all the troops under review in a review car. After the inspection, Commander-in-Chief Zhu ascended the reviewing stand and read out the order of the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to the national armed forces and militia, followed by the divisional parade of the armed forces.

Leading the parade was the students of the Military Academy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. They are all senior commanders of the People's Liberation Army who have experienced many battles and achieved great feats, and are now studying modern military science in the Military Academy. The audience applauded thunderously to express their respect to them.

Following them were the students of the Senior Infantry School of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the students of the Tank School, the students of the Artillery School, the students of the Naval School, the students of the Aviation School, the paratrooper units, and the infantry units.

Next came the militia contingent. They are representatives of the militia from the old liberated areas of North China, all combat heroes and model workers, and the audience applauded warmly to welcome them.

Afterwards, the cavalry units came over. Behind the cavalry units, the air defense units came over, the towed artillery units came over, the motorized infantry came over, and the armored units came over. They were operating the world's first-class weapons. Various calibers of artillery and various light and heavy tanks constituted a steel torrent, passing in front of the reviewing stand.

After the heavy tanks rumbled past, batches of various types of aircraft from the People's Air Force flew overhead.

The Chinese people now possess a modern national defense force, which is an epoch-making development of the strength of the Chinese people. The entire crowd repeatedly cheered for the growth of the forces defending the motherland and world peace.

After the military parade, the celebration parade began. Leading the way was a team of 18,000 Young Pioneers. They cheered "Long live Chairman Mao" as they passed in front of the reviewing stand. Two young team members ran up to the reviewing stand and presented a bouquet of flowers to Chairman Mao. At this time, the whole audience applauded and shouted "Long live Chairman Mao" and "Long live the People's Republic of China." One team of Young Pioneers released a large flock of pigeons as they passed the reviewing stand, expressing their determination to defend peace. One team of team members held up flowers to form the words "Resist America, Aid Korea" and invited 30 representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to march with them.

Following them were a team of 120,000 industrial and construction workers from the capital. They held up many banners and models, showing their achievements in increasing production and donating in the patriotic emulation campaign and their technical creations and improvements. As they passed in front of the combat heroes of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, they warmly cheered and saluted the heroes.

Following them were a team of 30,000 farmers from the suburbs of the capital, a team of 70,000 people from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and agencies, a team of 80,000 students from secondary schools and above in the capital, and a cultural and art army composed of 8,000 people from various cultural and art groups in the capital.

The parading crowds carried forward giant portraits of Chairman Mao and Sun Yat-sen and Chairman Mao's close comrades-in-arms Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De. They repeatedly cheered Chairman Mao, cheered the motherland, cheered the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, which had won great victories on the Korean front, and cheered the powerful People's Liberation Army.

The parading people carried forward giant portraits of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin. They expressed their warm respect for the Soviet Union, the fortress of world peace and democracy, they cheered the great friendship between China and the Soviet Union, and cheered Marshal Stalin, the great teacher and leader of all mankind, long live.

The parading people also held up giant portraits of national leaders Kim Il-sung, Ho Chi Minh, Bairut, Gottwald, Pieck, Dej, Rákosi, Chervenkov, Hoxha, Choibalsan, Tokuda Kyuichi, Thorez, Togliatti, Foster, Pollitt, and Ibárruri. They cheered and paid tribute to the heroic Korean people, paid tribute to the heroic Vietnamese people, and paid tribute to the Japanese people striving for independence, democracy, and peace. The parading people repeatedly paid tribute to the people of Asian countries, paid tribute to the fraternal countries and people of the peace camp, and paid tribute to the people of other countries.

The parading people angrily shouted slogans against US imperialism's aggression against Taiwan and Korea, against the illegal US-British imperialist peace treaty with Japan, and against US imperialism's rearmament of Japan. In the slogans, the parading people called on the people of the whole country to unite as one, strengthen the movement to resist America and aid Korea, and defeat US imperialism's aggression and new war plans.

When the cultural and art army reached the reviewing stand, everyone raised red and yellow cloths to form the words "Long Live Chairman Mao" and "Long Live Stalin." At this time, firecrackers sounded, and many national flags, military flags, and red flags burst out in the blue sky. After the cultural and art army passed by, the citizens' teams in the southern part of the square gathered in front of the reviewing stand and cheered Chairman Mao for a long time and enthusiastically. The celebration ceremony ended in cheers and applause.

1951-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

    欢庆中华人民共和国第二届国庆节
    首都昨日隆重举行庆祝典礼
    毛主席朱总司令和中央人民政府其他首长出席检阅
    全场群众为祖国人民力量划时代的发展而一再欢呼
    【新华社一日讯】中华人民共和国第二届国庆节庆祝典礼，今日在北京天安门广场隆重举行。参加典礼的共有四十多万人。
    今天天安门广场显得格外庄严和雄伟。天安门城墙上悬挂着中华人民共和国创造者毛泽东主席的巨像，城楼两旁成排的大红旗迎风飘动。在天安门对面广场上，集中着首都市民群众七万人。他们举着无数面红旗，使广场形成一片红色的海洋。天安门前宽广的大道已铺上了花岗石、缸砖和水泥砖，以便受检阅的装备着现代化重武器的解放军通过。
    今天出席检阅的，有中央人民政府主席毛泽东、副主席朱德、刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深、张澜，秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理周恩来，人民革命军事委员会副主席程潜，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，最高人民检察署检察长罗荣桓。

    今天出席观礼的将近一万人。

    在观礼台上的还有南方和北方的人民革命老根据地的代表九百九十四人，私营工商业的模范工作者一百人和首都各政府机关、各民主党派、各人民团体的高级干部，和出席各种专业会议的各地代表。
    毛泽东主席在上午九时五十分登临检阅台，全场热烈鼓掌不息。
    中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠宣布庆祝典礼开始。全场肃立，军乐队演奏国歌，礼炮齐鸣二十八响。
    阅兵式开始。阅兵式由中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会代理总参谋长聂荣臻任总指挥。中国人民解放军总司令朱德乘检阅车检阅了全体受阅部队。检阅后，朱总司令登临检阅台宣读中国人民解放军总部给全国武装部队和民兵的命令，接着进行武装部队的分列式检阅。
    走在最前面的部队是中国人民解放军军事学院的学员。他们都是身经百战和功勋卓著的解放军高级指挥员，现在军事学院里研习现代化的军事科学。全场掌声雷动，对他们表示敬意。
    接着是中国人民解放军高级步兵学校的学员、战车学校的学员、炮兵学校的学员、海军学校的学员、航空学校的学员，伞兵部队、步兵部队。
    接着前进的是民兵大队。他们是华北老解放区民兵的代表，都是战斗英雄和工作模范，全场热烈鼓掌向他们表示欢迎。
    随后，骑兵部队过来了。在骑兵部队的后面，防空部队过来了，牵引炮兵部队过来了，摩托化步兵过来了，装甲兵部队过来了。他们操纵着全世界第一流的武器。各种口径的大炮和各种轻重型坦克，构成一个钢铁的巨流，在检阅台前走过。
    当重坦克隆隆地开过去之后，人民空军的各式飞机一批一批地凌空而过。
    中国人民如今已拥有现代化的国防武装，这是中国人民力量划时代的发展。全场群众为保卫祖国和世界和平的力量的增长而一再欢呼。
    阅兵式结束后，庆祝游行开始。领先的是少年儿童队一万八千人的队伍。他们欢呼着“毛主席万岁”走过检阅台前。两个小队员跑上检阅台，把一束鲜花献给毛主席。这时全场鼓掌，高呼“毛主席万岁”和“中华人民共和国万岁”。有一队少年儿童队员在走过检阅台时放出一大群鸽子，表示他们保卫和平的意志。有一队队员高举着鲜花组成“抗美援朝”的字样，并且请了中国人民志愿军三十位代表和他们一同行进。
    首都产业工人和建筑工人十二万人的队伍相继跟进。他们高举着许多字标和模型，表明他们在爱国主义竞赛中增产捐献的成就和技术上的创造和改进。他们走过中国人民志愿军战斗英雄面前时，热烈地向英雄们欢呼致敬。
    接着是首都近郊农民三万人的队伍，各民主党派、各人民团体和各机关七万人的队伍，首都中等以上学校八万人的队伍，和首都各文艺团体八千人组成的文艺大军。
    游行的群众抬着毛主席和孙中山和毛主席的亲密战友刘少奇、周恩来、朱德的巨像前进。他们一再地欢呼毛主席，欢呼祖国，欢呼在朝鲜前线获得伟大胜利的中国人民志愿军，欢呼强大的人民解放军。
    游行者抬着多幅的马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的巨像前进。他们对世界和平民主的堡垒苏联表示热烈的敬意，他们欢呼中苏两国伟大的友谊，欢呼全人类的导师和领袖斯大林大元帅万岁。
    游行者也高举着各国人民领袖金日成、胡志明、贝鲁特、哥特瓦尔德、皮克、德治、拉科西、契尔文科夫、霍查、乔巴山、德田球一、多列士、托里亚蒂、福斯特、波立特、伊巴露丽的巨像。他们欢呼着向英勇的朝鲜人民致敬，向英勇的越南人民致敬，向争取独立、民主、和平的日本人民致敬。游行者一再地向亚洲各国人民致敬，向和平阵营各兄弟国家和人民致敬，向其他各国的人民致敬。
    游行者愤怒地高呼着反对美帝国主义侵略台湾和朝鲜、反对美英帝国主义的非法对日和约、反对美帝国主义重新武装日本的口号。游行者在口号中号召全国人民团结一致，加强抗美援朝运动，打败美帝国主义的侵略行为和新战争计划。
    文艺大军走到检阅台前时，大家举起红色黄色的布块，组成“毛主席万岁”和“斯大林万岁”的字样。这时花炮震响起来，在碧空中爆裂出了许多面国旗、军旗和红旗。文艺大军过去之后，广场南部的市民队伍向前聚集检阅台前，向着毛主席长久热烈欢呼。庆祝典礼在欢呼和掌声中结束。
