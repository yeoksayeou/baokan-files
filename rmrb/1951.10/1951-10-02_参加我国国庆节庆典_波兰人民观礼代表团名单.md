Title: List of the Polish People's Delegation to Observe the National Day Celebrations in China

Author:

Date: 1951-10-02
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, 30th) The list of the Polish People's Delegation invited to participate in our National Day celebrations is as follows:

    Head of Delegation: Jerzy Putrament, Member of the World Peace Council, Vice Chairman of the Polish Peace Committee, Secretary-General of the Polish Writers' Association.

    Members: Stanisław Strakacz, Member of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Railway Workers' Union.

    Leon Kerga, Vice Chairman of the Polish Farmers' Mutual Aid Union.

    Maria Wieniawska, Member of the Presidium of the Polish Women's League.

    Dariusz Wędzorek, Secretary of the Executive Committee of the Polish Youth League.

### 参加我国国庆节庆典  波兰人民观礼代表团名单

1951-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    参加我国国庆节庆典
    波兰人民观礼代表团名单
    【新华社三十日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆典的波兰人民观礼代表团名单如下：
    团长：耶齐·普特拉曼特，世界和平理事会理事、波兰和平委员会副主席、波兰作家协会秘书长。
    团员：斯坦尼斯他夫·斯特拉哈赤，波兰工会中央理事会理事、铁路工会执行委员会主席。
    勒翁·柯尔加，波兰农民互助联盟副主席。
    玛丽亚·维聂亚日，波兰妇女联盟主席团委员。
    达得留兹·韦卓列克，波兰青年联盟执行委员会书记。
