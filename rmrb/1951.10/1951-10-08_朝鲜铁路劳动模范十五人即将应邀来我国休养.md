Title: Fifteen North Korean Railway Labor Models to Visit China for Recuperation
Author:
Date: 1951-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: The National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union recently invited labor models from North Korean railway workers to recuperate at Chinese railway workers' sanatoriums to express the Chinese railway workers' high respect and love for the Korean people, the Korean People's Army, and the fraternal North Korean railway workers. This invitation has been agreed to by the Central Committee of the Korean Transportation Workers' Trade Union and has decided to send fifteen railway labor models to China for recuperation. They will arrive in China soon.

Xinhua News Agency: Zheng Qiaoxie, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Transportation Workers' Trade Union (i.e., the Korean Transport Workers' Union), flew to Beijing on September 11 to visit Chinese railway workers and convey the resolutions of the International Transport Workers' Federation.

Zheng Qiaoxie attended the Vienna meeting of the Administrative Committee of the International Union of Land, Air and Water Transport Workers in Austria in mid-July. During his stay in the Chinese capital, Zheng Qiaoxie visited the Changxindian Railway Factory, the Fengtai Locomotive Depot, the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, and the preview of the National Railway Exhibition. Zheng Qiaoxie returned to his country on September 14. On his way back, he visited the Northeast Materials Exchange Exhibition in Shenyang and several railway worker sanatoriums and rest homes at the Huangguantun Railway Factory. He was warmly welcomed by the vast number of Chinese workers during his visits to various factories.

### 朝鲜铁路劳动模范十五人即将应邀来我国休养

1951-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜铁路劳动模范十五人即将应邀来我国休养
    【新华社六日讯】中国铁路工会全国委员会不久前邀请朝鲜铁路职工中的劳动模范前来中国铁路工人休养院休养，以表示中国铁路工人对朝鲜人民，朝鲜人民军以及朝鲜铁路工人兄弟的崇高敬意与热爱。此项邀请，已得朝鲜交通劳动者职业总同盟中央委员会的同意，并已决定派遣十五名铁路劳动模范前来我国休养。他们不久即可抵达我国。
    【新华社讯】朝鲜交通劳动者职业总同盟中央委员会（即朝鲜运输工会）委员长郑乔爕，曾于九月十一日飞抵北京访问我国铁路工人及传达运输工会国际的决议。
    郑乔燮曾于七月中旬在奥地利参加陆空运输工人工会国际行政委员会维也纳会议。郑乔爕在我国首都期间曾参观了长辛店铁路工厂、丰台机务段、石景山钢铁厂等地，并参观了全国铁路展览会的预展。郑乔爕在九月十四日返国。返国途中，他在沈阳参观了东北物资交流展览会及皇姑屯铁路工厂、铁路职工业余休养所数处。在访问各工厂时，受到我国广大职工的热烈欢迎。
