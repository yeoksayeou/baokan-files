Title: Czechoslovakian Embassy Holds Reception to Celebrate Czechoslovak Armed Forces Day
Author:
Date: 1951-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - The Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Weisskopf, and Military Attaché Lieutenant Colonel Prochazka, held a reception on the afternoon of the 6th to celebrate the Czechoslovak Republic Armed Forces Day.

Invited to attend were: Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Nie Rongzhen, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government Huang Yanpei, members of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Fu Zuoyi and Cai Tingkai, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Xiao Hua, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Minister of Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Forestry and Land Reclamation Liang Xi, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Minister of Education Ma Xulun, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government Yu Xinqing, and over one hundred heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and senior generals of the People's Liberation Army.

Diplomatic envoys to China who attended the reception included: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic with Ambassadorial Rank Koenig, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Vietnamese Ambassadorial Representative Hoang Van Hoan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Dodaing, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy Fekete, and others.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝捷建军节捷大使馆举行招待会

1951-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝捷建军节捷大使馆举行招待会
    【新华社六日讯】捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使魏斯柯普夫暨武官普罗查中校，为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克共和国建军节，在六日下午举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有：中国人民解放军代总参谋长聂荣臻、中央人民政府政务院副总理黄炎培、人民革命军事委员会委员傅作义、蔡廷锴、中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华、外交部副部长伍修权、贸易部长叶季壮、林垦部长梁希、文化部长沈雁冰、教育部长马叙伦、中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，以及中央人民政府各部门首长和人民解放军高级将领一百余人。
    出席招待会的各国驻华使节有：苏联大使罗申、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、德意志民主共和国外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、越南大使衔代表黄文欢、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、匈牙利大使馆临时代办费凯尔特等。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。
