Title: The Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea Congratulatory Telegram to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1951-10-25
Page: 1

    To Commander Peng Dehuai and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army:

    On your great victories won in the past year of fighting abroad, we, on behalf of the people of the whole country, extend to you our highest respect and warmest congratulations.

    Dear Comrades! In the past year, in order to defend our motherland, to aid the people of Korea, and to defend peace in the Far East and the world, you have demonstrated unparalleled determination, bravery, and amazing hard work and endurance in battle, and demonstrated the great spirit of lofty patriotism and internationalism. You have given the enemy a series of severe blows, thereby consolidating the security of the motherland, helping the Korean people regain liberation from under the enemy's iron heel, and making great contributions to the great cause of defending world peace. We, on behalf of the people of the whole country, thank you.

    The people of the whole country are now enthusiastically responding to the call of our great leader Chairman Mao, continuing to strengthen the work of resisting America and aiding Korea to support your operations. In order to enable you to more effectively eliminate the enemy and win greater victories, we specially present to you the donations from the people of the whole country for 1,500 aircraft that our association has already received. We guarantee that the 475 million people of the whole country will resolutely stand behind you. May you gain more victories, and we wish you good health.

    The Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea
                                 October 24th

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会  致中国人民志愿军的贺电

1951-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民抗美援朝总会
    致中国人民志愿军的贺电彭德怀司令员并转中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员同志们：
    对于你们出国作战一年来所赢得的伟大胜利，我们谨代表全国人民向你们致以崇高的敬意和热烈的祝贺。
    亲爱的同志们！一年来你们为着保卫我们的祖国，为着援助朝鲜的人民，为着保卫远东与世界的和平，在战斗中表现了无比的坚决勇敢和惊人的刻苦耐劳的精神，表现了高度的爱国主义和国际主义的伟大精神。你们已经给了敌人一系列的严重打击，从而巩固了祖国的安全，帮助了朝鲜人民从敌人铁蹄下重获解放，并为保卫世界和平的伟大事业作了巨大的贡献。我们谨代表全国人民向你们致谢。
    全国人民现在正在热烈地响应我们的伟大领袖毛主席的号召，继续加强抗美援朝的工作，来支持你们的作战。为着使你们更有效地去消灭敌人，争取更大的胜利，我们特将本会业已收到的全国人民的一千五百架飞机的捐款，献给你们。我们保证：全国四亿七千五百万人民都坚决作你们的后盾。愿你们得到更多的胜利，并祝你们身体健康。中国人民抗美援朝总会
                十月二十四日
