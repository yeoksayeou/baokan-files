Title: Pakistani Prime Minister Assassinated
Author: 
Date: 1951-10-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 17th—TASS reports: Pakistani Prime Minister Liaquat Ali Khan was assassinated on October 16th while speaking at a rally in Rawalpindi. Khan was shot twice just as he began his speech, suffering fatal chest wounds.

### 巴基斯坦总理被刺死

1951-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理被刺死
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社消息：巴基斯坦总理列雅格特·阿里汗十月十六日在拉瓦尔品第的一个集会上演讲时，被人谋杀。阿里汗刚开始演讲即被击中两弹，胸部受伤致死。
