Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-10-15
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The people of the old revolutionary base areas are responding to Chairman Mao's call with practical actions: "Carry forward the revolutionary tradition and strive for greater glory." This is Zou Guizhang, a labor model from Jinggangshan, and the rice he harvested. He said, "We haven't had such a good harvest in twenty years. The current bumper harvest is entirely brought to us by Chairman Mao."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    老革命根据地的人民以实际行动响应毛主席的号召：“发扬革命传统，争取更大光荣。”这是井冈山的劳动模范邹贵章和他收割的稻子。他说：“我们二十年没有过这样的好收成，现在的丰收完全是毛主席带给我们的。”
