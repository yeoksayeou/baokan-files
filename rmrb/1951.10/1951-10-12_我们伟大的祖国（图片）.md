Author:
Date: 1951-10-12
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Shazhouba, Ruijin, Jiangxi, was the center of the Central Base Area during the Second Domestic Revolutionary War. When Chairman Mao led the revolution here, he personally led the local people in production and built wells. The local people have now erected a wooden sign beside the well, which reads: "When drinking water, never forget those who dug the well; always remember Chairman Mao."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    江西瑞金沙洲坝是第二次国内革命战争时期中央根据地的中心。毛主席在这里领导革命的时候，曾亲自率领当地人民进行生产，修筑水井。当地人民现在在水井旁立了一个木牌，上面写着：“吃水不忘开井人，时刻想念毛主席。”
