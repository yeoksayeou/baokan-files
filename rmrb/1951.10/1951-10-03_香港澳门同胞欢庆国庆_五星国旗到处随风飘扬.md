Title: Compatriots in Hong Kong and Macau Celebrate National Day: Five-Star Red Flags Fluttering Everywhere
Author:
Date: 1951-10-03
Page: 2

Compatriots in Hong Kong and Macau Celebrate National Day
Five-Star Red Flags Fluttering Everywhere

    Hong Kong compatriots from all walks of life celebrated the second National Day of the People's Republic of China. As early as September 30th, more than 120 groups and schools began to hold celebrations, with a total participation of 200,000 people. On the morning of October 1st, five-star red flags fluttered everywhere. At noon, firecrackers celebrating the event resounded throughout the area. The China National Aviation Corporation's large shop window featured a model of Tiananmen Square, and the area in front of the window was crowded with viewers all day long. Celebratory gatherings from various sectors were held separately. The celebration meeting of the industrial and commercial sectors was attended by representatives from 36 major industrial and commercial groups, including the Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, the Chinese Manufacturers' Association, and the Gold and Silver Exchange, totaling more than 1,600 people. The meeting called on all industrialists and businessmen to prepare to welcome the upcoming large-scale construction upsurge in the new China. The meeting also sent a telegram to the Central People's Government to express respect. The Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions held a grand cocktail party at noon. More than 100,000 workers from the 48 trade unions affiliated with the Federation also held commemorative meetings separately. Many trade unions also held exhibitions of pictures of the new China, tea parties, and current affairs forums to further enhance the understanding of the motherland among the broad masses of workers. More than 1,000 staff members participated in the celebration meeting of the film industry. A total of 33 units participated in the celebration meeting of the press and publishing circles. Many schools also held celebration gatherings. Some schools also designated October as "Patriotic Month" to carry out patriotic education. (Xinhua News Agency)
    Compatriots from all walks of life in Macau warmly celebrated National Day on October 1st. National flags were displayed throughout the city. On the main streets, Macau compatriots erected 40 towering archways, each several dozen feet high, adorned with 17,000 electric lights emitting a dazzling brilliance. Under the call of "Loving the Motherland with One Heart," more than 10,000 compatriots in Macau had been busy for more than ten days preparing for the grand National Day celebrations. The celebration meeting of representatives from all walks of life was held at 9:00 am on the 1st, attended by more than 2,000 representatives from the Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, the Federation of Trade Unions, women, students, and the cultural circles. The meeting called on all compatriots in Macau to consolidate and expand the patriotic democratic movement, strengthen and expand unity, and respond to all the calls of the motherland. A luncheon and various recreational programs were held after the meeting.
                                    (Xinhua News Agency)

### 香港澳门同胞欢庆国庆  五星国旗到处随风飘扬

1951-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    香港澳门同胞欢庆国庆
    五星国旗到处随风飘扬
    香港各界同胞欢庆中华人民共和国第二届国庆节。在九月三十日，就有一百二十多个团体、学校开始举行庆祝。参加的共达二十万人。十月一日清晨，五星国旗到处随风飘扬。午间，到处响起庆祝的鞭炮。中国航空公司的大橱窗内设置了一座天安门的模型，窗前整天都被观众围得水泄不通。各界的庆祝会是分别举行的。参加工商界庆祝国庆节大会的有华商总会、中华厂商联合会、金银贸易场等三十六个主要工商团体的代表共达一千六百余人。大会号召全体工商业家准备迎接即将到来的新中国的大规模的建设高潮。大会并通电向中央人民政府致敬。港九工会联合会在中午举行了盛大的鸡尾酒会。该会所属四十八个工会十多万工人也分别举行纪念会，许多工会还分别举行新中国图片展览会、茶叙会、时事座谈会，使广大工人进一步认识祖国。电影界的庆祝大会有工作人员千余人参加。参加新闻出版界的庆祝大会的共有三十三个单位。许多学校也都举行庆祝集会。有些学校并将十月份定为“爱国月”，进行爱国主义教育。（新华社）
    澳门各界同胞十月一日热烈庆祝国庆节。全市国旗招展，在各中心街道上，澳门同胞扎了四十座高达数丈的牌楼，牌楼上装着一万七千盏电灯散发出灿烂夺目的光辉。在“万众一心热爱祖国”的号召下，澳门一万多同胞曾为庆祝国庆盛典忙碌了十多天。各界代表庆祝国庆大会于一日上午九时举行，到会的有中华总商会、工会联合会、妇女、学生、文化界代表二千多人。大会号召全澳门的同胞巩固和扩大爱国民主运动，加强与扩大团结，响应祖国一切的号召。会后并举行聚餐和各种游艺节目。
                                    （新华社）
