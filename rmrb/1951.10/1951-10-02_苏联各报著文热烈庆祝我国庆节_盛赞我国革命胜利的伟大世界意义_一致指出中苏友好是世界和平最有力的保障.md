Title: Soviet Newspapers Publish Articles Warmly Celebrating China's National Day, Praising the Great World Significance of China's Revolutionary Victory, and Unanimously Pointing Out that Sino-Soviet Friendship is the Most Powerful Guarantee of World Peace
Author:
Date: 1951-10-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st - September: Soviet newspapers all published lengthy articles on September 30th, warmly celebrating the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The articles published by various newspapers emphasized the great world significance of the victory of the Chinese revolution and the great achievements of the new China in the political, economic, cultural, and military fields over the past two years. They unanimously pointed out that Sino-Soviet friendship is the most powerful guarantee of world peace.

    The *Pravda* published an article by Kovalev, "The Unbreakable Friendship of the Soviet and Chinese People," which stated: "The victory of the Chinese revolution is a new and most powerful blow to the world imperialist system and all imperialist aggressive plans after the October Revolution. The peace and democratic camp, headed by the Soviet Union, has gained the People's Republic of China, a powerful fighter for peace and international friendship. The great friendship between the Soviet and Chinese people is growing stronger day by day. The Soviet people pay close attention to and are concerned about the construction achievements of the People's Republic of China. The great friendship between the Soviet and Chinese people inspires all peace-loving people fighting for peace, and this friendship serves the noble cause of defending world peace now and in the future."

    The *Izvestia* published an article by Matinov, "The Great Achievements of the People's Republic of China," which pointed out: "The liberation of China from imperialist and feudal oppression is the greatest victory of all progressive humanity. The tremendous achievements of the People's Republic of China in the past two years demonstrate the great vitality of the people's democratic system. The Chinese people are convinced that the path they have chosen is correct. They are increasingly closely united around their government, around the long-tested leader of the Chinese working people - the Communist Party, and around the leader of the Chinese people, Mao Zedong. The Soviet people extend warm congratulations to their glorious allies and friends. The entire progressive humanity congratulates the Chinese people on the second anniversary of the People's Republic of China."

    The *Red Star* published an article by Yevgenyev, "The Great Festival of the Chinese People," which said: The victory of the Chinese revolution "has the greatest significance for all peace-loving people. The heroic Chinese Communist Party is the inspirer and organizer of the victory of the Chinese revolution. While carrying out construction, the Chinese people are strengthening their national defense. The Chinese People's Liberation Army - the pride and glory of the Chinese people - vigilantly defends the borders of its country and is full of determination to complete the unification of all of China. On the great National Day of China, all the Soviet people and peace-loving people all over the world wish the Chinese people new achievements in building their great country."

    The *Labor Daily* published an article by Vladimiriva, "The Great Festival of the Chinese People," which stated: "The People's Republic of China has entered the world stage as a new and powerful force against imperialism. It shines like a huge lighthouse, illuminating the path of national liberation for all oppressed people in Asia. The People's China has proved in the two years since its establishment that it not only desires peace but is also good at defending peace. People's China consolidates the camp of world peace with every achievement in national construction, economic construction, and cultural construction. These achievements are the result of the competent leadership of the Chinese Communist Party armed with Marxism-Leninism."

    The *Komsomolskaya Pravda* published an article by Stanichenko, "The Glorious Victory of the Chinese People," which stated: "The second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China is another glorious milestone in Chinese history and an event of great international significance."

    The *Moscow Pravda* published an article by Nikolayev, which stated: "The great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union is destined to make the imperialist aggressors' conspiracy in the Far East inevitably go bankrupt. This friendship is a guarantee for the People's Republic of China to achieve new achievements in its third year of nation-building."

### 苏联各报著文热烈庆祝我国庆节  盛赞我国革命胜利的伟大世界意义  一致指出中苏友好是世界和平最有力的保障

1951-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联各报著文热烈庆祝我国庆节
    盛赞我国革命胜利的伟大世界意义
    一致指出中苏友好是世界和平最有力的保障
    【新华社莫斯科一日电】九月三十日，苏联各报都发表长篇论文，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国建立两周年。各报发表的论文都强调指出中国革命胜利的伟大世界意义和新中国两年来在政治、经济、文化、军事等方面的伟大成就，并一致指出中苏友好是世界和平最有力的保障。
    真理报发表柯沃略夫的《牢不可破的中苏人民的友谊》的论文说：“中国革命的胜利是继十月革命之后，对世界帝国主义制度和帝国主义一切侵略计划的一个新的最有力的打击。以苏联为首的和平民主阵营已得到了中华人民共和国这个保卫和平与国际友好的强有力的战士。伟大的中苏人民的友谊正在一天天加强。苏联人民非常注意和关心中华人民共和国的建设成就。中苏两国人民的伟大友谊，鼓舞着为和平而斗争的全体爱好和平的人类，而且这种友谊现在和将来都是为保卫世界和平的崇高事业服务的。”
    消息报发表马提诺夫的《中华人民共和国的伟大成就》的论文指出：“中国从帝国主义和封建的压迫下解放出来，是整个进步人类的最伟大的胜利。中华人民共和国两年来建设的巨大成就，表明了人民民主制度的伟大活力。中国人民确信他们所选择的道路是正确的，他们越来越紧密地团结在自己政府的周围，团结在久经考验的中国劳动人民的领导者——共产党的周围，团结在中国人民的领袖毛泽东的周围。苏联人民谨向自己光荣的盟友和朋友致热烈的祝贺。整个进步人类在中华人民共和国两周年纪念日都向中国人民祝贺。”
    红星报发表叶甫根涅夫《中国人民的伟大节日》的论文说：中国革命的胜利“对于一切爱好和平的人民来说有最伟大的意义。英雄的中国共产党是中国人民革命胜利的鼓舞者和组织者。中国人民在进行建设的同时，正在加强着国防。中国人民解放军——中国人民的骄傲和光荣——警觉地保卫本国的国界，充满着完成全中国统一的决心。在伟大的中国国庆日，全体苏联人民和全世界爱好和平的人民，都愿望中国人民在建设自己伟大的国家中获得新的成就。”
    劳动报刊载乌拉季米罗娃的《中国人民伟大的节日》的论文说：“中华人民共和国已作为新的强大的反对帝国主义的力量，走进了世界舞台。它像一座巨大的灯塔，向亚洲一切被压迫人民照耀着民族解放的道路。人民中国在建立以来的两年中证明了它不仅愿望和平，而且善于保卫和平。人民中国以自己在国家建设、经济建设和文化建设中的每一个成就巩固着全世界和平的阵营。这些成就都是以马列主义学说武装起来的中国共产党的干练领导的结果。”
    共青真理报刊载斯塔尼秦科的《中国人民的光荣胜利》的论文说：“中华人民共和国建立两周年是中国历史上又一个光荣的里程碑，是有巨大国际意义的事件。”
    莫斯科真理报刊载尼古拉耶夫的论文说：“中苏两国人民的伟大友谊注定了帝国主义侵略者在远东的阴谋将不可避免地归于破产。这个友谊是进入建国第三年的中华人民共和国获得新成就的保障。”
