Title: Our Ambassador to North Korea, Ni Zhiliang, and Others Awarded North Korean National Flag Medals and Awards
Author:
Date: 1951-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 3rd. The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea held an award ceremony on September 29 to reward the diplomatic envoys of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and various people's democratic countries stationed in Korea for their achievements in strengthening the friendly relations between Korea and fraternal countries. General Ni Zhiliang, our ambassador to North Korea, was awarded the First Class National Flag Medal of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Chai Junwu, Counselor of our Embassy, was awarded the Second Class National Flag Medal, and Liu Xiangwen, Second Secretary, was awarded the Third Class National Flag Medal. In addition, twenty-two members of our embassy staff were awarded Labor Medals.

The award ceremony was personally presided over by Chairman Kim Tu-bong. Those who also attended the ceremony included: Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Heon-young, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Labor Party Park Jeong-ae, and others.

### 我驻朝鲜大使倪志亮等荣获朝鲜国旗勋章及奖章

1951-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻朝鲜大使倪志亮等荣获朝鲜国旗勋章及奖章
    【新华社平壤三日电】朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会为奖励苏联、中华人民共和国和各人民民主国家驻朝鲜的外交使节，在加强朝鲜和各兄弟国家之间友好关系的工作上所做出的成绩，在九月二十九日举行授勋典礼。我国驻朝鲜大使倪志亮将军获朝鲜人民共和国一级国旗勋章，我大使馆参赞柴军武获二级国旗勋章，二等秘书刘向文获三级国旗勋章。此外，我大使馆馆员二十二人获劳动奖章。
    授勋典礼由金枓奉委员长亲自主持。参加典礼的尚有：内阁副首相兼外务相朴宪永、劳动党中央书记朴正爱等。
