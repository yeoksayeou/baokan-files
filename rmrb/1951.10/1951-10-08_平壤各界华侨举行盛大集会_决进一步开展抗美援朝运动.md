Title: Compatriots from All Circles in Pyongyang Hold Grand Meeting, Resolving to Further Develop the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement
Author:
Date: 1951-10-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang: Compatriots from all circles in Pyongyang held a grand meeting here on September 30th to celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    The meeting was presided over by the Pyongyang Municipal Overseas Chinese Association. Ma Yusheng, Vice Chairman of the North Korean Overseas Chinese United General Association, spoke at the meeting. He represented the overseas Chinese in Korea in paying respects to Chairman Mao, the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Chinese People's Volunteers, and all the people of the motherland. He said: The new China of peace, democracy, independence, and prosperity that the overseas Chinese have longed for for many years has been realized under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and the Communist Party. The new China has brought infinite glory to the overseas Chinese. After detailing the great achievements of the motherland's construction undertakings and the motherland people's Resist America, Aid Korea Movement over the past two years, he called on all overseas Chinese to further launch the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement, increase production, donate planes and artillery to support the front, avenge the compatriots killed by American imperialism, and strive to defend the motherland and world peace.
    The meeting sent a telegram to Chairman Mao congratulating him on National Day. The congratulatory telegram first paid tribute to Chairman Mao. The telegram stated: We are convinced that the people of the motherland and overseas Chinese can only victoriously build a new China by uniting under your banner and advancing heroically. The telegram, after recounting the various atrocities committed by American imperialism against overseas Chinese in Korea, said: We are determined to further enhance our patriotism and internationalism, unite with the Korean people, actively produce, support the front, and strive to defeat the American aggressors.
    The meeting also sent a telegram to General Peng Dehuai, commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, expressing respect.

### 平壤各界华侨举行盛大集会  决进一步开展抗美援朝运动

1951-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤各界华侨举行盛大集会  决进一步开展抗美援朝运动
    【本报讯】据新华社平壤电：平壤各界华侨于九月三十日在此间举行盛大集会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立二周年。
    大会由平壤市华侨联合会主持。北朝鲜华侨联合总会副委员长马玉声在大会上讲话，他代表朝鲜华侨向毛主席、中国共产党、中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军以及祖国全体人民致敬。他说：华侨多年来渴望的和平、民主、独立和富强的新中国，在毛主席和共产党的英明领导下实现了。新中国给华侨带来了无限的光荣。他在详细地介绍了两年来祖国建设事业和祖国人民抗美援朝运动的伟大成就后，号召全体华侨进一步展开抗美援朝运动，增加生产，捐献飞机大炮支援前线，为被美帝国主义残杀的侨胞报仇；为保卫祖国、保卫世界和平而奋斗。
    大会致电毛主席祝贺国庆。贺电首先向毛主席致敬。电文说：我们确信，祖国人民和海外华侨只有团结在您的旗帜下英勇前进，才能胜利地建设新中国。电文在叙述了美帝国主义对朝鲜华侨的种种暴行后说：我们决心进一步提高爱国主义和国际主义思想，和朝鲜人民团结一致，积极生产，支援前线，为击败美国侵略者而奋斗。
    大会同时致电中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军，表示敬意。
