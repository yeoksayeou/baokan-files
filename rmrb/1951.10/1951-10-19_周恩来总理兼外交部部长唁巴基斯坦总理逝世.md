Title: Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Sends Condolences on the Death of the Prime Minister of Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1951-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the Central People's Government, sent a telegram to Pakistani Foreign Minister Chaudhry Muhammad Zafrulla Khan on October 18th, expressing condolences on the death of Pakistani Prime Minister Liaquat Ali Khan. The telegram reads as follows:

To Mr. Chaudhry Muhammad Zafrulla Khan, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Pakistan:

I was deeply saddened to learn of the assassination and death of His Excellency Prime Minister Ali Khan of your country. I hereby send a telegram to express my condolences.

Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China.

Beijing, October 18, 1951

### 周恩来总理兼外交部部长唁巴基斯坦总理逝世

1951-10-19
第1版()
专栏：
