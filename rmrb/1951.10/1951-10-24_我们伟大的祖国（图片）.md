Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-10-24
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In the struggle against American imperialist aggression, the Chinese People's Volunteers have demonstrated a high degree of internationalism, patriotism, and revolutionary heroism, and have produced many combat heroes and model workers. This is a delegation of combat heroes of the Volunteers, returning home with honors to participate in the Second National Day celebration. Young students lift them up high and cheer them enthusiastically.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军在反对美帝国主义侵略的斗争中，表现了高度的国际主义、爱国主义和革命英雄主义的精神，涌现出许多战斗英雄和工作模范。这是满载荣誉归国参加第二届国庆节的志愿军战斗英雄代表。青年学生们把他们高高举起，向他们热烈欢呼。
