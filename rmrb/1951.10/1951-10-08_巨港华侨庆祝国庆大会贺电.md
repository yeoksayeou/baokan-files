Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Overseas Chinese Celebration of National Day in Jambi

Author:

Date: 1951-10-08
Page: 4

    [Our Newspaper Dispatch] The Overseas Chinese Celebration of National Day in Jambi, Sumatra, Indonesia, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao to congratulate him on National Day. The original telegram is as follows: To Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    In these short two years, under the wise and correct leadership of our Central People's Government and Chairman Mao, our motherland has achieved brilliant accomplishments in political, economic, cultural, educational, and international construction undertakings. This has led our people onto a path of unified, consolidated, and victorious progress, unprecedented in history. Undoubtedly, our people will continue to achieve countless and greater victories in the future, and will make even greater contributions to the cause of world peace and democracy. Its future will be filled with boundless radiance. We will forever unite under the banner of Chairman Mao's leadership and do everything in our power to build a new China. Today, as we celebrate the second anniversary of our great People's Republic of China, we are filled with unparalleled excitement and encouragement. We send this telegram to celebrate and extend our highest respects.

            Overseas Chinese Celebration of National Day in Jambi    October 4th

### 巨港华侨庆祝国庆大会贺电

1951-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    巨港华侨庆祝国庆大会贺电
    【本报讯】印度尼西亚苏门答腊岛巨港华侨庆祝国庆大会致电毛主席，祝贺国庆节。原电如下：中华人民共和国北京中央人民政府毛主席钧鉴：
    在这短短的两年来我中央人民政府和毛主席的英明的正确的领导之下，使得我祖国无论在政治、经济、文化教育和国际上等建设事业，都获得了光辉灿烂的成就，使我全国人民走上了历史上从未有过的统一团结巩固的胜利道路，而且毫无疑问，今后我国人民将接连着得到无数的更大的胜利，同时对世界和平民主事业将更有伟大的贡献，其前途将是万丈的光芒。我们永远团结在毛主席的旗帜的领导下，为建设新中国尽一切力量。今天当庆祝我们伟大的中华人民共和国第二周年国庆的时候，实有无比的兴奋和鼓舞。特电庆祝，并致崇高的敬礼。
            巨港华侨庆祝国庆大会    十月四日
