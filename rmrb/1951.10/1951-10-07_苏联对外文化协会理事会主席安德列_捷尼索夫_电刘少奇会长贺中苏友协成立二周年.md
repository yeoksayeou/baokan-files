Title: Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Andrei Denisov, sends a telegram to Chairman Liu Shaoqi to congratulate the second anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association

Author: 
Date: 1951-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th. Professor Andrei Denisov, Chairman of the Council of the All-Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, sent a telegram to Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, congratulating the second anniversary of the founding of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The telegram reads as follows:

Chairman Liu Shaoqi:

On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Council of the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries extends warm greetings to you and all the Chinese friends of the Soviet Union. The further strengthening of the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union will be conducive to ensuring the cause of peace and security in the Far East. The Council of the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries hopes that the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will achieve new successes in all its undertakings.

Chairman of the Council of the All-Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries
Andrei Denisov, Professor

### 苏联对外文化协会理事会主席安德列·捷尼索夫  电刘少奇会长贺中苏友协成立二周年

1951-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联对外文化协会理事会主席安德列·捷尼索夫
    电刘少奇会长贺中苏友协成立二周年
    【新华社五日讯】全苏对外文化协会理事会主席安德列·捷尼索夫教授致电中苏友好协会总会刘少奇会长，祝贺中苏友好协会成立二周年。电文如下：刘少奇会长：
    值此中苏友好协会成立二周年之际，苏联对外文化协会理事会谨向您和全体中国的苏联之友致热烈的祝贺。中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊的进一步加强，将有利于保证远东和平和安全的事业。苏联对外文化协会理事会希望中苏友好协会在其一切事业中获得新的成就。全苏对外文化协会理事会主席
        安德列·捷尼索夫教授
