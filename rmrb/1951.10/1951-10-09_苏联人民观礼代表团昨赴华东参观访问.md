Title: Soviet People's Observation Delegation Left Yesterday for East China for a Visit
Author: 
Date: 1951-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Soviet People's Observation Delegation, invited to attend our National Day celebrations, left Beijing tonight, accompanied by Yan Baohang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Zhao Zhongchi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; and Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, went to the station to see them off.

### 苏联人民观礼代表团昨赴华东参观访问

1951-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联人民观礼代表团昨赴华东参观访问
    【新华社八日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的苏联人民观礼代表团在今晚离北京，由中苏友好协会总会副总干事阎宝航陪同前往华东参观访问。中苏友好协会总会副总干事赵仲池，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会秘书长刘贯一，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一和中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒等人均往车站送行。
