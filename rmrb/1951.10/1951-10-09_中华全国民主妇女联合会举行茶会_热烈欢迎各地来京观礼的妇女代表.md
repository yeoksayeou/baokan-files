Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Holds Tea Party, Warmly Welcoming Women Representatives from Various Regions to Beijing for the National Day Observance

Author:
Date: 1951-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a tea party on the morning of the 8th to welcome women representatives from the old revolutionary base areas in the South and North, women representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and female model workers and agricultural labor models who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day observance. Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, and Xu Guangping, vice-chairpersons of the All-China Women's Federation, as well as standing committee members, executive committee members, heads of various departments in Beijing, and Zhang Xiaomei, chairperson of the Beijing Women's Federation, attended the reception. Approximately 200 guests and hosts were present. Deng Yingchao, vice-chairperson of the All-China Women's Federation, delivered a welcoming speech, expressing cordial greetings to the women representatives from the old revolutionary base areas, and encouraging them to uphold the glorious tradition of the revolution, improve their work after returning, and make the glory even more glorious, and the models even more exemplary. Chen Shaomin, executive committee member of the All-China Women's Federation and chairperson of the National Committee of the China Textile Trade Union, also spoke at the meeting. Subsequently, Hu Wenxiu, mother of female hero Liu Hulan; Jiang Shuhua, female representative of the Chinese People's Volunteers; Tian Guiying, China's first female train driver; Liu Laidi, representative of Ruijin County from the old central revolutionary base area; Li Liying, revered as the "revolutionary mother" by the people of the Chao-Shan region; and Ye Lian, representative from the old revolutionary base area in eastern Guangdong whose hands were chopped off by Chiang Kai-shek's bandits, spoke one after another. They unanimously expressed their gratitude for Chairman Mao's leadership and their willingness to stand fast at their posts, take the lead in working hard, strive to produce, intensify the effort to resist America and aid Korea, and strive for the early arrival of the final victory.

### 中华全国民主妇女联合会举行茶会  热烈欢迎各地来京观礼的妇女代表

1951-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主妇女联合会举行茶会
    热烈欢迎各地来京观礼的妇女代表
   【新华社八日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会于八日上午举行茶会，欢迎来京参加国庆节观礼的南方和北方老根据地的妇女代表，中国人民志愿军的女代表和工人农业劳动模范女代表。全国妇联副主席邓颖超、李德全、许广平和在京的常委、执委及各部负责人、北京市妇联主席张晓梅等都出席招待。宾主共约二百人。全国妇联邓颖超副主席致欢迎词，亲切地慰问了各老根据地妇女代表，并勉励她们坚持革命的光荣传统，回去后把工作做的更好，使得光荣更加光荣，模范更加模范。全国妇联执委、中国纺织工会全国委员会主席陈少敏在会上也讲了话。接着女英雄刘胡兰的妈妈胡文秀、中国人民志愿军女代表姜淑华、中国第一名女火车司机田桂英、中央老根据地瑞金县代表刘来娣、被潮汕地区人民尊称为“革命母亲”的李黎英、被蒋匪砍掉了双手的粤东老根据地代表叶连等十人相继发言，她们一致表示感谢毛主席的领导，愿坚守自己的岗位，带头努力工作，努力生产，加紧抗美援朝，争取最后胜利的早日到来。
