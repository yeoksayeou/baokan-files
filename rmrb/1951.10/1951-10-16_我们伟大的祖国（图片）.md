Author:
Date: 1951-10-16

    Our Great Motherland
    The farmers of New China are extremely interested in agricultural machinery. Agricultural labor models and farmer representatives from various counties and districts in Shandong are visiting the state-owned Guangbei Farm to see automatic combine harvesters. The picture shows the driver introducing the performance and usage methods of the machinery to the visitors.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新中国的农民对于农业机器感到极大兴趣。山东各县、区农业劳动模范及农民代表正在国营广北农场参观自动联合收割机。这是驾驶员在向参观者介绍机械的性能和使用方法的情形。
