Title: People of Various Countries Celebrate China's National Day; Soviet Artists Hold Celebration Party
Author:
Date: 1951-10-10
Page: 4

    People of Various Countries Celebrate China's National Day
    Soviet Artists Hold Celebration Party
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th] The Soviet House of Art Workers held a celebration party on the evening of the 3rd to celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Zhang Wentian, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, embassy staff, and the Chinese film workers delegation were invited to participate. Portraits of Stalin and Mao Zedong were hung on the rostrum, with the national flags of China and the Soviet Union on either side. The party began with a screening of a film of Chairman Mao Zedong announcing the founding of the People's Republic of China. When Chairman Mao appeared on the screen, the audience applauded warmly. The party was hosted by Zhubov, a Stalin Prize winner and People's Artist of the Soviet Union. Amidst applause, he introduced Ambassador Zhang Wentian to the attendees and invited him to sit on the rostrum. Kiakintova, a Stalin Prize winner and People's Artist of the Russian Federation, spoke at the meeting about the achievements of the new China's culture and art. Academician Makarova of the Soviet Academy of Social Sciences, who lectured in China last March, reported on her impressions of visiting the new China. Yutkevich, a Stalin Prize winner and Honored Artist, discussed the achievements of Chinese cinema. Poet Kirsanov recited a poem dedicated to the new China. Finally, famous Soviet musicians and actors performed entertainment programs.

### 各国人民庆祝我国国庆节  苏联艺术家举行庆祝晚会

1951-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    各国人民庆祝我国国庆节
    苏联艺术家举行庆祝晚会
    【新华社莫斯科五日电】苏联艺术工作者之家在三日晚间举行中华人民共和国成立二周年庆祝晚会。我国驻苏联大使张闻天、大使馆人员和中国电影工作者代表团应邀参加。主席台上挂着斯大林和毛泽东的画像，两旁挂着中苏两国国旗。晚会上首先放映了毛泽东主席宣布中华人民共和国成立的影片。当毛主席在银幕上出现时，全场热烈鼓掌。晚会由斯大林奖金得奖人、苏联人民演员朱波夫主持。他在掌声中介绍张闻天大使给到会者，并邀请他到主席台就座。斯大林奖金得奖人、俄罗斯联邦人民演员基阿清托娃在会上叙述了新中国文化和艺术的成就。曾在去年三月来我国讲学的苏联社会科学院院士马卡洛娃报告了访问新中国的观感。斯大林奖金得奖人、功勋艺术家尤特杰维奇论述了中国电影的成就。诗人基尔山诺夫即席朗诵了献给新中国的诗。晚会最后由苏联著名的音乐家、演员演出文娱节目。
