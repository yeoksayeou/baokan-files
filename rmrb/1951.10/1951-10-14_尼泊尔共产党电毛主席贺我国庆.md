Title: Nepalese Communist Party Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao to Congratulate China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1951-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The Nepalese Communist Party sent a telegram to congratulate China's National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the historically significant founding of the People's Republic of China, the first session of the Nepalese Communist Party, held from September 23rd to September 29th, extends warm congratulations to you, the Chinese Communist Party, and the great Chinese people. This session pledges to strengthen the long-standing friendship between the peoples of China and Nepal, which British and American imperialism, with the help of its Asian running dogs, is attempting to harm.

Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and Nepal!

Naya Shatak Ghatamantu, Nepalese Communist Party
October 1st

### 尼泊尔共产党电毛主席贺我国庆

1951-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔共产党电毛主席贺我国庆
     【新华社十三日讯】尼泊尔共产党致电祝贺我国国庆，电文如下：中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志：
    值此具有历史意义的中华人民共和国成立之日，尼泊尔共产党九月二十三日至九月二十九日举行之第一届会议谨向您、中国共产党及伟大的中国人民致以热烈的祝贺。本届会议保证加强中、尼两国人民悠久的而为英、美帝国主义借其亚洲走狗之助企图危害的友谊。
    中、尼两国人民友谊万岁！
    尼泊尔共产党纳亚·沙答克·加特孟图
    十月一日
