Title: The Soviet Union Speaks Up for Justice in the Security Council, Iranian Media Unanimously Expresses Gratitude
Author: 
Date: 1951-10-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, October 10th: The Soviet representative's just stance in the United Nations Security Council against the Anglo-American imperialist violation of Iranian sovereignty and interference in Iran's internal affairs has won praise and gratitude from Iranian public opinion. The "Bisuy Ayandeh" newspaper praised the Soviet Union on the 6th as a "protector of weak countries." "Kayhan" and many other newspapers hold the same view.

### 苏联在安理会上仗义执言  伊朗舆论界一致表示感谢

1951-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联在安理会上仗义执言
    伊朗舆论界一致表示感谢
    【新华社十日讯】德黑兰讯：苏联代表在联合国安全理事会上反对英美帝国主义侵犯伊朗主权、干涉伊朗内政的正义立场，获得了伊朗舆论界的赞扬与感谢。“比苏·阿扬第报”六日盛赞苏联是“弱小国家的保护者”。“凯亨晚报”及其他许多报纸也都持同样的意见。
