Title: German Democratic Republic's Second National Day: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams Respectively; Marshal Stalin Sends Telegram to Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1951-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th:

    German Democratic Republic's Second National Day
    Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams Respectively
    Marshal Stalin Sends Telegram to Grotewohl

    [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai sent telegrams to President Wilhelm Pieck and Premier Otto Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic on the 6th, respectively, to celebrate the second National Day of the German Democratic Republic. The texts of the two telegrams are as follows:

    Chairman Mao's Congratulatory Telegram to His Excellency Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic:

    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, I, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and the German people, and wish the German people greater achievements in the cause of striving for the unification, democracy, and independence of Germany, and safeguarding peace in Europe and the world.

        Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong

    Premier Zhou's Congratulatory Telegram to His Excellency Otto Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic:

    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the establishment of the German Democratic Republic, please accept my warm congratulations to Your Excellency, your government, and the people of your country. I wish the friendly cooperation between the Chinese and German people will be increasingly consolidated and developed.

            Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China

    [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] TASS, Moscow, 7th: J.V. Stalin's congratulatory telegram to Premier Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic is as follows:

    To Comrade Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Berlin:

    Please accept my congratulations on the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, Comrade Premier.

    I wish the German people, the German government, and you personally new achievements in building a unified, independent, democratic, and peace-loving Germany.

        J.V. Stalin (signed)

### 德意志民主共和国第二届国庆节  毛主席周总理分别致电祝贺  斯大林大元帅电贺格罗提渥

1951-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国第二届国庆节
    毛主席周总理分别致电祝贺
    斯大林大元帅电贺格罗提渥
    【新华社七日讯】毛泽东主席与周恩来总理六日分别致电德意志民主共和国皮克总统与格罗提渥总理，庆祝德意志民主共和国第二届国庆，两电电文如下：毛主席贺电德意志民主共和国威廉·皮克总统阁下：
    值此德意志民主共和国成立两周年纪念之际，我代表中华人民共和国政府及人民，谨向阁下及德意志人民致衷心的庆贺，并祝德意志人民在争取德国的统一、民主、独立及维护欧洲与世界和平的事业中，获得更多的成就。中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东周总理贺电德意志民主共和国奥托·格罗提渥总理阁下：
    值此德意志民主共和国成立两周年纪念之际，请接受我对阁下、贵国政府及人民的热烈庆贺，谨祝中德两国人民的友好合作日益巩固与发展。
            中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日讯：约·维·斯大林致德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥的贺电如下：
    柏林德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥同志：
    欣逢德意志民主共和国建国二周年国庆节，总理同志，请接受我的祝贺。
    我祝德国人民、德国政府和你个人在建设统一、独立、民主和爱好和平的德国方面获得新的成就。约·维·斯大林（签字）
