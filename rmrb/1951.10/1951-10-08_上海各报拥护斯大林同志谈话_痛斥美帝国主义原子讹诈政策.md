Title: Shanghai Newspapers Support Comrade Stalin's Speech and Condemn US Imperialism's Atomic Blackmail Policy
Author:
Date: 1951-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 7th: Shanghai newspapers today all published Marshal Stalin's answer to the Pravda reporter's question on the atomic weapons issue on the front page in the headline position. Xinwen Ribao, Dagong Bao, and Wenhui Bao also published commentaries.

    Xinwen Ribao published a commentary entitled "The Bankruptcy of the U.S. Imperialist Atomic Blackmail Policy," saying: "This speech by the leader and teacher of the world's peace-loving people is a serious warning to the American aggressors: The era in which the United States monopolized atomic bombs is long gone! It is no longer possible for U.S. imperialism to use atomic weapons to intimidate and kill people." The editorial said: "The facts are very clear: the power of the peace-loving people is growing rapidly and infinitely. In socialist and new democratic countries, the people hold political power, master science and industry, and can also master the latest weapons to resist aggression and defend peace. If imperialism does not recognize this law of historical development and stubbornly opposes the people, the result will only be to accelerate its own demise." The editorial said: "We fully support Comrade Stalin's proposal to ban atomic weapons. Let us firmly grasp the weapons of defending peace and continue to strengthen the struggle to defend peace."

    Dagong Bao's editorial said: "This is a fatal blow to the aggressors and good news for the peace-loving people of the world." The editorial pointed out: "Let the American aggressors know that the power of the peace and democracy camp is great and invincible. Stalin's answers and the Soviet atomic bomb will greatly encourage the world peace movement and strengthen our confidence in the victory of peace over war."

    Wenhui Bao's commentary stated: "This speech shows that the Soviet Union's foreign policy is upright and highly vigilant. The Soviet Union's persevering foreign policy not only guarantees the inviolability of the Soviet Union, but also reflects the consolidation of the socialist peace camp and deepens the sense of security of the world's peace-loving people." The commentary said: "Today, we have another opportunity to solemnly warn the British and American imperialists: if they do not sincerely accept the control of atomic weapons, and if they still want to use atomic weapons to slaughter millions of peace-loving people in the world, then they will surely suffer the consequences. Please do not forget a clear saying: 'Bombs can be answered with bombs!'."

### 上海各报拥护斯大林同志谈话  痛斥美帝国主义原子讹诈政策

1951-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    上海各报拥护斯大林同志谈话
    痛斥美帝国主义原子讹诈政策
    【新华社上海七日电】上海各报今天都在首版头条地位刊登了斯大林大元帅关于原子武器问题的答真理报记者问。新闻日报、大公报、文汇报并发表评论。
    新闻日报发表题为“美帝原子讹诈政策的破产”的评论说：“这位全世界和平人民的领袖和导师的谈话，是对于美国侵略者的一个严重警告：美国专有原子弹的时代早已过去了！美帝国主义要想利用原子武器来吓人和杀人，已经不可能了。”社评说：“事实非常显明，和平人民的力量正在飞速地无限地增长起来。在社会主义和新民主主义的国家，人民掌握着政权，掌握着科学和工业，也就能掌握最新的武器，来反对侵略，保卫和平。帝国主义如果不承认这个历史发展的规律，顽固地与人民为敌，结果就只有加速它本身的死亡。”社评说：“我们完全拥护斯大林同志关于禁止原子武器的主张。让我们紧紧掌握着保卫和平的武器，再接再厉加强保卫和平的斗争。”
    大公报社评说：“这对侵略者是致命的打击，这是全世界爱好和平人民的福音。”社评指出：“让美国侵略者知道吧，和平民主阵营的力量是伟大的，不可战胜的。斯大林的答问和苏联的原子弹对世界和平运动将起巨大的鼓舞作用，将加强我们对于和平战胜战争的信心。”
    文汇报时评认为：“这一谈话说明了苏联的对外政策是正直的，警惕性很高的。苏联这种坚忍不拔的外交政策，不仅为苏联的不可被侵犯提供了保证，同时反映了社会主义和平阵营的巩固，加深了世界和平人民的安全感。”时评说：“今天，再有一次机会让我们正告英、美帝国主义者：如果不诚意接受管制原子武器，如果仍欲以原子武器来屠杀千千万万世界和平人民，那么，必将自食苦果。请它们不要忘记一句明言：‘炸弹是可以用炸弹来回敬的！’。”
