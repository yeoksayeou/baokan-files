Title: China Association for Science and Technology and CAST Host Soviet and Hungarian Scientific Workers
Author:
Date: 1951-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The China Association for Science and Technology, the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, and the Chinese Academy of Sciences jointly held a tea party this afternoon to host scientific workers from various countries' People's Observation Delegations who attended China's National Day. Among those invited were Oparin, Chairman of the Soviet Association for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge, Yunusov, Director of the Chemical Institute of the Uzbek Academy of Sciences of the Soviet Union, and István Rusznyák, President of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences. Those attending the reception included Liang Xi, Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology, Vice Chairman Mao Yisheng, Wu Youxun, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, Tao Menghe, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and more than 30 Chinese scientific workers.

Liang Xi first gave a warm welcome on behalf of the Chinese scientific workers to the Soviet and Hungarian scientific workers present, followed by a brief report on the popularization of science in China over the past two years.

Soviet representative Oparin rose to speak, giving a detailed account of the work and experience of the Soviet Association for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge in the four years since its establishment. Hungarian representative István Rusznyák, in his speech, introduced how Hungarian scientific workers, with the help of the Hungarian working class after liberation, restored scientific research and carried out the popularization of science. Soviet representative Yunusov vividly described the work of scientific workers in Uzbekistan. Finally, Soviet representative Oparin answered several questions raised by scientific workers attending the tea party regarding the popularization of science. After the tea party, a banquet was held; during the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted the unity and cooperation of Chinese, Soviet, and Hungarian scientific workers in jointly defending world peace.

### 我科学技术普及协会和科联招待苏匈科学工作者

1951-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我科学技术普及协会和科联
    招待苏匈科学工作者
    【新华社七日讯】中华全国科学技术普及协会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会及中国科学院今日下午联合举行茶会，招待参加我国国庆节的各国人民观礼代表团中的科学工作者。应邀出席的有苏联政治与科学知识普及协会主席奥巴林，苏联乌兹贝克科学院化学研究所所长尤努索夫，匈牙利科学院院长伊斯特万·鲁斯尼亚克三人。出席招待的，有中华全国科学技术普及协会主席梁希，副主席茅以升，中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会副主席吴有训，中国科学院副院长陶孟和及中国科学工作者三十余人。
    梁希首先代表中国科学工作者热烈欢迎到会的苏联及匈牙利科学工作者，接着作了关于二年来中国科学普及工作的简短报告。
    苏联代表奥巴林起立发言，详尽地介绍了苏联政治与科学知识普及协会成立四年来的工作及经验。匈牙利代表伊斯特万·鲁斯尼亚克在发言时，介绍了解放后的匈牙利科学工作者如何在匈牙利工人阶级的帮助下恢复了科学研究工作并进行普及科学的工作。苏联代表尤努索夫生动地描述了乌兹贝克的科学工作者工作的情况。最后，由苏联代表奥巴林回答了参加茶会的科学工作者所提出的有关科学普及工作的几个问题。茶会结束后并举行宴会；席间宾主一再为中苏、中匈的科学工作者的团结合作共同保卫世界和平而干杯。
