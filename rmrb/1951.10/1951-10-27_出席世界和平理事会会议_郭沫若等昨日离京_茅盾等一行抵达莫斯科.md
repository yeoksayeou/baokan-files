Title: Attending the World Peace Council Meeting, Guo Moruo and Others Departed Beijing Yesterday; Mao Dun and His Entourage Arrived in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1951-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 26th: Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation attending the Second Session of the World Peace Council, along with council members Ma Yinchu, Zhang Bojun, Wu Yaozong, specially invited Chinese farmer representative Liu Qingshan, and nine staff members of the delegation, departed Beijing by plane at 6:00 AM today, heading to Vienna via Moscow to attend the meeting. Those who saw them off at the airport included: Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Liu Ningyi, Situ Meitang, Peng Zemin, Ulanhu, Zhang Hanfu, Xu Baoju, Ai Qing, Wu Maosun, Liao Mengxing, Gong Pusheng, Ji Fang, Zhao Zhongchi, Cui Yueli, and more than forty others.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 26th: Part of the Chinese delegation led by Mao Dun to attend the Second Session of the World Peace Council arrived in Moscow by plane on the 25th. Representatives from the Soviet Peace Committee and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries welcomed them at the airport. Ge Baoquan, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, also welcomed them at the airport. The delegation members will soon transfer to Vienna, the capital of Austria, to attend the meeting.

### 出席世界和平理事会会议  郭沫若等昨日离京  茅盾等一行抵达莫斯科

1951-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会会议
    郭沫若等昨日离京
    茅盾等一行抵达莫斯科
    【新华社二十六日讯】出席世界和平理事会第二届会议的中国代表团团长郭沫若、理事马寅初、章伯钧、吴耀宗、特邀中国农民代表刘青山和代表团工作人员一行九人，在今日上午六时乘飞机离京赴莫斯科转赴维也纳参加会议。到机场欢送的有：彭真、陈叔通、刘宁一、司徒美堂、彭泽民、乌兰夫、章汉夫、许宝驹、艾青、吴茂荪、廖梦醒、龚普生、季方、赵仲池、崔月犁等四十多人。
    【新华社莫斯科二十六日电】由茅盾率领的出席世界和平理事会第二届会议的中国代表团部分人员，在二十五日乘飞机抵达莫斯科。到机场欢迎的有苏联拥护和平委员会和苏联对外文化协会的代表。我国驻苏联大使馆参赞戈宝权也到机场欢迎。代表团人员即将转赴奥地利首都维也纳参加会议。
