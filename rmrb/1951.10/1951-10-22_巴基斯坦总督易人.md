Title: Change of Governor-General of Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1951-10-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, October 19th, TASS dispatch: The King of England has approved the resignation of Pakistan's Governor-General Nazimuddin and appointed Finance Minister Ghulam Muhammad as his successor.

### 巴基斯坦总督易人

1951-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总督易人
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩十九日讯：英王已批准巴基斯坦总督纳齐姆丁辞职，并任命财政部长吴拉姆·穆罕默德继任。
