Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-10-10
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Under the education of the Communist Youth League, the patriotic labor enthusiasm of the League members has generally increased. This is Liu Qingfa, a member of the Communist Youth League in Liuzhuang Village, Zhengyang County, Henan Province. He actively participates in the autumn harvest and is very happy about this year's bumper harvest. Look how big the sorghum ears he is carrying are!

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新民主主义青年团的团员们，在团的教育下，爱国主义的劳动热情普遍高涨。这是河南正阳县刘庄的青年团员刘庆发。他积极地参加秋收，并且对今年的丰收表现非常高兴。你看他扛着的高粱穗子多么肥大！
