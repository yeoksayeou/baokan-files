Title: The Whole Country Universally Celebrates the First Anniversary of the Struggle to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea; People in Various Places Express Their Determination to Continue Strengthening Practical Actions to Support the Front Lines
Author:
Date: 1951-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: People across the country universally held celebration rallies on the anniversary of the struggle to resist US aggression and aid Korea, reviewing the achievements of the past year. People in various places unanimously expressed that they will continue to strengthen the work of resisting US aggression and aiding Korea in the future.

    Various factories, schools, and youth and women's organizations in Shanghai held celebration rallies. People from all walks of life also expressed their celebration through practical actions. Employees of enterprises such as the Shanghai Gas Company and Zhaofeng Yarn Factory donated one day's wages on the 25th to express their regards to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army. More than fifty automobile drivers formed a voluntary transportation team to go to Korea on the 25th. The business community held a collective donation rally on the 25th, and most industries had completed 80% of their donation plans. People from all walks of life sent letters of appreciation to the brave Chinese and Korean People's fighters on the Korean front. Fudan University students launched a "one person, one letter" campaign. On the eve of the anniversary, more than 2,000 representatives from all walks of life in East China and Shanghai held a grand commemorative meeting. Shu Tong, vice chairman of the East China General Branch of the Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea Association, and Liu Changsheng, chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea Association, spoke successively, reporting on the contributions of the people of East China and Shanghai in the Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea movement over the past year, and calling on the people of East China and Shanghai to make persistent efforts and continue to deepen this movement. Zhang Daosheng, a wounded soldier of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, spoke at the meeting, thanking the people of the motherland for their love and care. A total of twenty-three units, including the East China and Shanghai local organizations of various democratic parties and various people's organizations in East China and Shanghai, presented congratulatory speeches to the meeting. The meeting finally passed a telegram of respect to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and a telegram to the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council.

    More than 3,000 representatives from all walks of life in Nanjing held a grand celebration rally on the evening of the 24th. Korean writer Li Taijun, who was in Nanjing at the invitation to participate in China's National Day, and people's observation delegations from various Eastern European people's democratic countries, were also invited to attend. Jin Shanbao, chairman of the Nanjing Branch of the Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea Association, reported on the achievements made by the people of the city in the Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea movement over the past year, saying: More than 80% of the people in the city have signed patriotic covenants, and people from all walks of life have actually paid 25 billion yuan in donations, completing 80% of the donation plan.

    Representatives from all walks of life in Shenyang held a celebration rally on the 25th. A joint celebration for families of martyrs and families of military personnel was held on the same day. Starting on the 25th, employees of various factories held a four-day speech contest and broadcasting conference on increasing production and practicing economy.

    Representatives from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Xi'an also held a celebration rally on the 25th. The representatives unanimously expressed their determination to do a good job in resisting US aggression and aiding Korea and to strive to support the Korean front. At the celebration rally in Wuhan, the city's business community paid more than 18 billion yuan in donations on the spot. The people of Qingdao, Wuhu, Wuxi, Changsha, Zhangjiakou, Chengde, Lanzhou, Yinchuan and other cities also carried out enthusiastic celebration activities.

    People in various ethnic minority areas also excitedly commemorated the first anniversary of resisting US aggression and aiding Korea. The donations already paid by people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang Province (part of which are special products such as cattle, sheep, and horses) amounted to 30.5 billion yuan, completing more than 60% of the payment plan. Many Uyghur youths requested to join the Chinese People's Volunteer Army when commemorating this festival. Literary and artistic workers of Uyghur, Kazakh, and other ethnic groups have formed a cultural and artistic troupe and will soon go to the Korean front to express their regards to the brave Chinese and Korean People's fighters. Sangjiyueshi, chairman of the Tibetan Autonomous Region People's Government in Xikang Province, called on the Tibetan people to strengthen unity, step up production, and support the People's Liberation Army troops entering Tibet and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army at the celebration rally commemorating the first anniversary of resisting US aggression and aiding Korea.

### 全国普遍庆祝抗美援朝斗争一周年  各地人民表示要继续加强实际行动支援前线

1951-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    全国普遍庆祝抗美援朝斗争一周年
    各地人民表示要继续加强实际行动支援前线
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：全国各地人民在抗美援朝斗争一周年纪念日，普遍地举行了庆祝大会，检阅一年来抗美援朝斗争的成绩，各地人民并一致表示今后要继续加强抗美援朝的工作。
    上海市各工厂、学校和青年、妇女界分别举行了庆祝大会。各界人民并纷纷以实际行动来表示庆祝。上海煤气公司、兆丰纱厂等企业中的职工捐出了二十五日一天的工资，以慰劳中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军。有五十多个汽车司机在二十五日组成了志愿赴朝的运输队。工商界在二十五日举行了集体缴纳捐款大会，大部分的行业已经完成了他们捐献计划的百分之八十。各界人民纷纷给朝鲜前线英勇的中朝人民战士写慰问信，复旦大学学生已展开了一人一信运动。纪念日前夕，华东暨上海各界人民代表二千多人举行了盛大的纪念会。华东抗美援朝总分会副主席舒同、上海市抗美援朝分会主席刘长胜相继讲话，报告一年来华东和上海人民在抗美援朝运动中的贡献，并号召华东和上海人民再接再厉，继续深入开展这一运动。人民志愿军伤病员张道生在会上讲话，他感谢祖国人民对他们的热爱和慰问。各民主党派华东暨上海的地方组织和华东暨上海各人民团体共二十三个单位，向大会献词。大会最后通过了向中国人民志愿军的致敬电及致世界和平理事会执行局的电文。
    南京市各界人民代表三千多人在二十四日晚上举行了盛大的庆祝集会。正在南京的应邀参加我国国庆的朝鲜作家李泰俊和东欧各人民民主国家的人民观礼代表团，也都应邀出席。南京市抗美援朝分会主席金善宝在会上报告了一年来该市人民在抗美援朝运动中已经取得的成绩，他说：全市已有百分之八十以上的人民订立了爱国公约，各界人民已实缴捐款二百五十亿元，完成了捐献计划的百分之八十。
    沈阳市各界人民代表在二十五日举行了庆祝大会。同日举行了烈士家属和军人家属的联欢会。各工厂职工自二十五日起举行四天增产节约讲演比赛广播大会。
    西安市各族各界人民代表也在二十五日举行了庆祝大会，代表们一致表示要做好抗美援朝工作，努力支援朝鲜前线。在武汉市的庆祝大会上，该市工商界当场缴纳了捐款一百八十多亿元。青岛、芜湖、无锡、长沙、张家口、承德、兰州、银川等城市的人民也都进行了热烈的庆祝活动。
    各少数民族地区的人民也兴奋地纪念抗美援朝一周年。新疆省各民族人民已经缴纳的捐款（其中一部分为牛、羊、马等特产）达三百零五亿元，完成了缴款计划的百分之六十以上。有许多维吾尔族青年在纪念这个节日时，要求参加中国人民志愿军。维吾尔、哈萨克等各民族文艺工作者已组成文工团，即将到朝鲜前线慰问英勇的中朝人民战士。西康省藏族自治区人民政府主席桑吉悦希在庆祝抗美援朝一周年纪念的大会上，号召藏族人民加强团结，加紧生产，支援人民解放军入藏部队和中国人民志愿军。
