Title: Central and South China Nationalities Visiting Delegation Returns to Beijing; Delegation Received Warmly by People of All Nationalities in Visited Areas
Author:
Date: 1951-10-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Central People's Government Central and South China Nationalities Visiting Delegation, led by Head of Delegation Li Dequan, Deputy Heads Fei Xiaotong and Ma Jie, and other members, returned to Beijing by train from Hankou this morning. Representatives from various organizations and groups were at the station to welcome them, including Secretary-General of the State Council of the Central People's Government and Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Li Weihan, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council Qi Yanming, Vice Minister of Health He Cheng, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Liu Geping, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Deng Yingchao, Vice President of the Central Institute for Nationalities Liu Chun, and the Red Cross Society of China.

The delegation left Beijing on June 20th and divided into three sub-delegations, which visited the minority areas of Guangxi, Guangdong, and Hunan provinces. The delegation adopted methods such as mass meetings, symposiums, and individual interviews to promote the ethnic policies in the Common Program to the people of various ethnic groups, including the Li, Yao, Miao, Zhuang, Dong, and Hui, conveyed the concern of Chairman Mao and the leaders of the Central People's Government for the minority peoples, presented a large number of gifts on behalf of Chairman Mao to the minority peoples, and promoted ethnic policies through dramas, films, and exhibitions, thereby raising the political awareness of the minority peoples, greatly improving ethnic relations, and strengthening ethnic unity. In addition, the delegation also focused on gaining an in-depth understanding of the living conditions of the minority peoples in the visited areas, solicited their opinions and demands, and helped the local people's governments convene representative conferences of people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life, summarized the experience of ethnic work, and strengthened and expanded the united front. The delegation also assisted the minority peoples of Longsheng County, Guangxi, in establishing a national democratic joint government.

The delegation was warmly welcomed by the people of all ethnic groups in the areas visited. In order to express their love and gratitude to Chairman Mao, the people of all ethnic groups presented their precious items to the delegation to bring to Chairman Mao. The people of all ethnic groups competed to sign the banners dedicated to Chairman Mao, expressing their love and respect for Chairman Mao.

### 中央中南各民族访问团返京  访问团所到地区受到各族人民热烈欢迎

1951-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中央中南各民族访问团返京
    访问团所到地区受到各族人民热烈欢迎
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府中南各民族访问团团长李德全、副团长费孝通、马杰及团员等一行，已在今晨由汉口乘车返抵北京。赴车站欢迎的有中央人民政府政务院秘书长兼民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉、政务院副秘书长齐燕铭、卫生部副部长贺诚、民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平、全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、中央民族学院副院长刘春和中国红十字会总会等机关团体的代表多人。
    访问团于六月二十日离北京，分为三个分团，分赴广西、广东和湖南三省的少数民族地区进行了访问。该团采用群众大会、座谈会、个别访问等方式向黎族、瑶族、苗族、僮族、侗族、回族等各民族人民宣传了共同纲领中的民族政策，传达了毛主席和中央人民政府各首长对少数民族人民的关怀，代表毛主席赠送少数民族人民大批礼品，并通过戏剧、电影、展览等宣传了民族政策，提高了少数民族人民的政治认识，因而大大改善了民族关系，加强了民族团结。此外，该团在访问地区还有重点地深入了解了少数民族人民的生活情况，征询了他们的意见和要求，并帮助当地人民政府召开了各民族各界人民代表会议，总结了民族工作经验，加强和扩大了统一战线。访问团还协助广西龙胜县的少数民族人民建立了民族民主联合政府。
    访问团所到地区，受到各民族人民的热烈欢迎。各族人民为了表达他们对毛主席的热爱和感激，纷纷将自己的珍贵物品送给访问团带给毛主席。各民族人民都争先恐后地在献给毛主席的锦旗上签名，表示他们对毛主席的热爱和敬意。
