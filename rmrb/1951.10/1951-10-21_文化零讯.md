　　Cultural News Briefs
　　▲ The fourth volume, twelfth issue, of "Study" magazine was published on the 16th. This issue publishes Zheng Chang's essay, titled "‘Selected Works of Mao Zedong’ is the Most Complete Example of the Combination of Marxism and the Chinese Revolution." The author provides a brief introduction to several important issues, such as Chairman Mao's principles regarding the close and inseparable relationship between theory and practice, the struggle against "leftist" and right-leaning opportunist lines, and the methods and viewpoints of class analysis. This issue also includes a study note on Mao Zedong's works: Yan Su's "Some Experiences in Studying ‘On Practice’."
　　▲ The 20th marks the 28th anniversary of the founding of "China Youth." The society held a commemorative meeting on the evening of the 19th, attended by over twenty people from youth and cultural circles in the capital. The meeting began with a speech by the editor-in-chief of the society, Wei Junyi. Jiang Nanxiang, deputy secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, pointed out at the meeting that "China Youth" should carry forward its past fighting traditions, closely integrate with practical struggles, and systematically promote Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought. Yu Guangyuan, Hu Weide, Liu Nianqu, and others unanimously pointed out that "China Youth" has played a positive role in carrying out patriotic education over the past year, and should further strengthen its ideological content and combativeness in the future. Yang Shu, the president of the society, concluded in his speech that "China Youth" should strengthen propaganda on national defense construction and increasing production and practicing economy, strengthen ideological struggle, eliminate the reactionary thoughts of imperialism among young people, and criticize the erroneous thoughts of the bourgeoisie and petty bourgeoisie. Note: "China Youth" was founded on October 20, 1923. It was edited by Yun Daiying, an outstanding leader of the revolutionary youth movement at the time. It resumed publication in Yan'an during the Anti-Japanese War. Currently, the publication is an organ of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, with a circulation of 210,000 copies.

### 文化零讯

1951-10-21
第3版()
专栏：文化零讯

    文化零讯
    ▲“学习”杂志第四卷第十二期，已在十六日出版。这一期发表了郑昌的论文，题为“‘毛泽东选集’是马克思主义与中国革命相结合的最完全的典型”。作者对毛主席关于理论和实践密切不可分离的原则，反对“左”、右倾机会主义路线的斗争，阶级分析的方法、观点等几个重要问题，都作了扼要的介绍。这一期还刊载了一篇学习毛主席著作的笔记：阎素的“学习‘实践论’的一点体会”。
    ▲二十日为“中国青年”创刊二十八周年纪念日，该社在十九日晚召开纪念会，到会者有首都青年界文化界人士二十余人。会上首由该社总编辑韦君宜致词。青年团中央副书记蒋南翔在会上指出“中国青年”应发扬过去战斗传统，紧密结合实际斗争，系统地宣传马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想。于光远、胡韦德、刘念渠等一致指出：“中国青年”一年来在进行爱国主义教育等方面发挥了良好的作用，今后更应加强思想性与战斗性。该社社长杨述最后在发言中指出今后“中国青年”要加强国防建设与增产节约的宣传，加强思想斗争，在青年中肃清帝国主义的反动思想，并批判资产阶级、小资产阶级的错误思想。按：“中国青年”在一九二三年十月二十日创刊。由当时革命青年运动的杰出领导者恽代英主编。抗日战争时期在延安复刊。目前该刊为青年团中央机关刊物，销行达二十一万份。
