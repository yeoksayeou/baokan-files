Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Holds Banquet Welcoming Model Industrial Workers to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-10-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions held a banquet on the afternoon of the 2nd to welcome all the model industrial workers who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations. Li Lisan and Zhu Xuefan, vice-chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, as well as ministers of various departments and chairmen of the National Committees of various industrial trade unions, attended the reception.

    Before the banquet began, Li Lisan, vice-chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a welcome speech to all the model industrial workers. He said: Thanks to the efforts of all workers and masses, especially the leading role of the model workers, the industrial construction of the motherland has made great achievements in the past two years since the founding of the country. He called on all model workers to work hard in production in the future, not to be arrogant or complacent, to learn with humility, and to further develop the spirit of self-sacrifice and overcoming difficulties of the working class, to unite around Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, and to strive for greater achievements in national construction.

### 全国总工会举行宴会  欢迎来京工业劳动模范

1951-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    全国总工会举行宴会
    欢迎来京工业劳动模范
    【新华社三日讯】中华全国总工会二日下午举行宴会欢迎来北京参加国庆节观礼的全体工业劳动模范。全国总工会副主席李立三、朱学范和各部部长、各产业工会全国委员会主席都出席招待。
    宴会开始前，全国总工会副主席李立三向全体工业劳动模范致欢迎词。他说：建国两年来，由于全体工人群众的努力，特别是劳动模范的带头作用，祖国的工业建设已获得巨大的成就。他号召全体劳动模范今后努力生产，不骄傲，不自满，虚心学习，更进一步发扬工人阶级的自我牺牲、克服困难的精神，团结在毛主席和中国共产党的周围，为争取国家建设的更大成就而努力。
