    Our Great Motherland
    Oranges are one of Sichuan's specialties. Li Dewen, a poor peasant from Jiangbei, Sichuan, received nineteen orange trees during the land reform. He is picking the fruits of victory for the first time, and his face is filled with boundless joy.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    柑子是四川的特产之一。四川江北的贫农李德文在土地改革中分到了十九棵柑子树。他第一次采摘着斗争胜利的果实，脸上流露着无限的喜悦。
