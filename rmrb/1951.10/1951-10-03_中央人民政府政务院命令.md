Title: Order of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1951-10-03
Page: 1

The Decision on the Reform of the Education System, passed by the 97th Government Affairs Meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, is hereby ordered to be promulgated and implemented.

                Premier Zhou Enlai
                  October 1, 1951
