Title: Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic: Mission Chief Koenig Held a Reception in Beijing Yesterday; Vice Chairmen Zhu De and Soong Ching Ling and Over Two Hundred Others Were Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1951-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: To celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, Koenig, the Ambassador-rank Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to our country, held a reception in Beijing at 7:00 PM today. Over two hundred people were invited to attend.

Among those invited to attend the reception were: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De and Soong Ching Ling, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Lin Boqu, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Dong Biwu and Huang Yanpei, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Vice President Zhang Zhizhiang, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, Deputy Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Lan Gongwu, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, Deputy Mayor Wu Han, members of the Central People's Government Committee, members of the Government Administration Council, and heads of the various commissions, ministries, departments, academies, bureaus, and banks under the Government Administration Council, Xu Teli, Ma Xulun, Shen Yanbing, Chen Shutong, Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Fu Zuoyi, Zhang Bojun, Liu Yazi, Pan Zhenya, Luo Ruiqing, Ye Jizhuang, Zhu Xuefan, Liang Xi, etc., as well as Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League Feng Wenbin, and Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Deng Yingchao.

The diplomatic envoys of various countries to China who were invited to attend the reception included: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Vietnamese Ambassadorial Representative Hoang Van Hoan, Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Dotin, Hungarian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Fekete, and Korean Counselor Choi Young.

The entire delegation of the People's Observation Delegation from the German Democratic Republic, headed by Ditter, who participated in our National Day celebrations, as well as members of the People's Observation Delegations from the Soviet Union and other countries who participated in our National Day celebrations, also attended the reception.

The reception took place in a friendly atmosphere, and a film was shown afterwards. (Attached picture)

### 庆祝德意志民主共和国成立两周年纪念  柯尼希使团长昨在京举行招待会  朱德宋庆龄副主席等二百余人应邀参加

1951-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝德意志民主共和国成立两周年纪念
    柯尼希使团长昨在京举行招待会
    朱德宋庆龄副主席等二百余人应邀参加
    【新华社七日讯】德意志民主共和国驻我国外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希，为庆祝德意志民主共和国成立两周年纪念，特于今日下午七时在京举行招待会。应邀参加招待会的有二百余人。
    应邀参加招待会的，有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、宋庆龄，中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，副总理董必武、黄炎培，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒、副院长张志让，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，最高人民检察署副检察长蓝公武，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，北京市人民政府市长彭真、副市长吴晗，中央人民政府委员会委员、政务院政务委员、及政务院所属各委、部、会、院、署、行首长徐特立、马叙伦、沈雁冰、陈叔通、蔡廷锴、彭泽民、傅作义、章伯钧、柳亚子、潘震亚、罗瑞卿、叶季壮、朱学范、梁希等以及中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超。
    应邀参加招待会的各国驻华外交使节，有：苏联大使罗申，捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫，罗马尼亚大使鲁登科，蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗，保加利亚大使彼得科夫，越南大使衔代表黄文欢，波兰临时代办道定，匈牙利临时代办费凯尔特，朝鲜参赞崔英。
    以狄特为首的德意志民主共和国参加我国国庆典礼的人民观礼代表团的全体团员，以及苏联等国参加我国国庆典礼的人民观礼代表团的团员，也参加了招待会。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行，会后并放映电影。（附图片）
