Title: CYLC Holds Symposium to Welcome Young Agricultural Labor Models
Author:
Date: 1951-10-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League held a symposium on the evening of the 5th to welcome 46 young agricultural labor models, militia heroes, and model workers in the Huai River and other water conservancy projects from all over the country who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations. Li Chang, Standing Committee member of the Central Committee of the Youth League, delivered a welcome speech to the model workers and called on the labor models and militia heroes to study hard in the future national construction cause and be a strong assistant to the Party. At the meeting, Huai River governance model Gan Caihua, Wang Jinshan, militia hero of the Jerim League branch in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and Jiang Zaiqiu, agricultural labor model from Quan County, Guangxi Province, all spoke enthusiastically. They described in detail how they grew up under the education of Chairman Mao, the Communist Party, and the Youth League, as well as the construction achievements in their respective regions over the past two years.

### 青年团中央举行座谈会  欢迎青年农业劳动模范

1951-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团中央举行座谈会
    欢迎青年农业劳动模范
    【新华社六日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会在五日晚举行座谈会，欢迎全国各地来京参加国庆节观礼的青年农业劳动模范、民兵英雄、治淮和其他水利事业的模范等四十六人。青年团中央委员会常务委员李昌向模范们致欢迎词，并号召劳动模范和民兵英雄在今后国家建设事业中努力学习，做党有力的助手。会上治淮模范甘彩华、内蒙古自治区哲里木盟分区民兵英雄王金山、广西省全县农业劳动模范蒋在球等都纷纷发言。他们详细地叙述了自己在毛主席和共产党、青年团的教育下成长起来的情形，以及所在地区两年来的建设成就。
