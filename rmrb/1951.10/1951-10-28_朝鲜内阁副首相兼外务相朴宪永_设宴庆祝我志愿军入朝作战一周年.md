Title: DPRK Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Heon-yeong Hosts Banquet to Celebrate the First Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' Entry into the War in Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 27th: DPRK Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Heon-yeong held a grand celebration banquet on the evening of the 25th to celebrate the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' entry into the war in Korea. Deputy Prime Minister Hong Myung-hee, Vice Chairmen of the Supreme People's Assembly Kim Tal-hyun and Lee Young, and cabinet ministers attended the reception. Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Heo Ga-i and Park Jeong-ae, as well as heads of other democratic parties and social organizations, Deputy Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army Kim Il, senior generals of the People's Army, and combat heroes also participated in the banquet.

General Han Xianchu, representative of the Chinese People's Volunteers, commanders of various units of the People's Volunteers, combat heroes, and meritorious personnel were invited to the banquet. Also invited to the banquet were Ni Zhiliang, China's ambassador to North Korea, Razovayev, the Soviet ambassador, and representatives of embassies of various People's Democratic countries.

Deputy Prime Minister Park Heon-yeong delivered a congratulatory speech at the beginning of the banquet. On behalf of the Korean government and the Korean people, he expressed deep gratitude for the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers' great action of coming to the aid of the Korean people during the most difficult period of the Korean Fatherland Liberation War. He extended warm congratulations on the historically significant and brilliant victories achieved by the Chinese and North Korean People's forces fighting side by side on the Korean battlefield to strike at the American aggressors. He then proposed a toast to the health of the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, and to the greater victories of the Chinese and North Korean People's forces in the future.

General Han Xianchu, representative of the Chinese People's Volunteers, proposed a toast to the health of the wise leader of the Korean people and Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, General Kim Il-sung. Ambassador Ni Zhiliang proposed a toast to the health of the leader and teacher of the working people of the world, Marshal Stalin. Soviet Ambassador Razovayev proposed a toast to the great achievements of the outstanding commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, General Peng Dehuai, and the heroic troops under his command on the Korean battlefield.

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere filled with respect and love for the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers and confidence in the victory of the War to Resist America. Commanders of various units of the Volunteers, combat heroes, and meritorious personnel were continuously warmly congratulated by friends from various countries. The guests and hosts frequently toasted the fighting friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples and the growth of the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union.

After the banquet, the cultural troupes of various units of the Volunteers and the art troupe of the Korean National Art Theater jointly performed celebration programs.

### 朝鲜内阁副首相兼外务相朴宪永  设宴庆祝我志愿军入朝作战一周年

1951-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜内阁副首相兼外务相朴宪永
    设宴庆祝我志愿军入朝作战一周年
    【新华社平壤二十七日电】朝鲜内阁副首相兼外务相朴宪永为庆祝中国人民志愿军入朝作战一周年，在二十五日晚上举行盛大庆祝宴会。内阁副首相洪命熹，最高人民会议副议长金达铉、李英及内阁各相均出席招待。朝鲜劳动党中央委员会书记许嘉谊、朴正爱以及其他民主政党、社会团体的负责人，朝鲜人民军总政治局副局长金日以及人民军高级将领、战斗英雄都参加了宴会。
    中国人民志愿军代表韩先楚将军、人民志愿军各部队首长以及战斗英雄、功臣等应邀赴宴。应邀赴宴的还有我国驻朝鲜大使倪志亮、苏联大使拉佐瓦耶夫以及各人民民主国家大使馆代表。
    朴宪永副首相在宴会开始时致祝词。他代表朝鲜政府和朝鲜人民，对于英勇的中国人民志愿军在朝鲜祖国解放战争最困难的时期，前来援助朝鲜人民的伟大行动，表示深切的感谢；对于中朝人民部队在朝鲜战场并肩作战打击美国侵略者所取得的具有历史意义的光辉胜利，表示热烈的祝贺。接着，他提议为伟大的中国人民领袖毛泽东主席的健康和中朝人民部队今后更伟大的胜利干杯。
    中国人民志愿军代表韩先楚将军提议为朝鲜人民的英明领袖、朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成将军的健康干杯。倪志亮大使提议为全世界劳动人民的领袖和导师斯大林大元帅的健康干杯。苏联大使拉佐瓦耶夫提议为中国人民志愿军的卓越指挥员彭德怀将军及其所指挥的英雄部队在朝鲜战场上建立的伟大功勋干杯。
    宴会在充满着对英雄的中国人民志愿军的敬爱和对抗美战争的胜利信心的气氛中进行。志愿军各部队首长、战斗英雄和功臣不断受到各国友人的热烈祝贺。宾主频频为中朝人民的战斗友谊，为以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营的壮大而干杯。
    宴会毕，由志愿军各部队文工团和朝鲜国立艺术剧场艺术队联合演出庆祝节目。
