Title: The State Council Holds its 108th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-10-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 26th—The 108th meeting of the Central People's Government State Council was held this afternoon. The meeting, after listening to Vice Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, Zeng Zhaolun's explanation of the draft instructions on consolidating and developing secondary technical education, approved the aforementioned instructions. The meeting also approved several personnel appointments and removals and submitted several personnel appointments and removals to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一零八次会议

1951-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一零八次会议
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百零八次政务会议于今日下午举行。会议在听取了中央人民政府教育部曾昭抡副部长所作的关于整顿和发展中等技术教育指示草案的说明后，通过了上述指示。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
