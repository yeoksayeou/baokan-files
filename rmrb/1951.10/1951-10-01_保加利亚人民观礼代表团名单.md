Title: List of Bulgarian People's Delegation to National Day Observance

Author:

Date: 1951-10-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: The following is the list of the Bulgarian People's Delegation invited to attend our National Day celebrations:

    Head of Delegation: Georgi Karaslavov, Vice-Chairman of the Bulgarian Peace Committee.
    Member: Maria Kirilova, Secretary of the Central Council of Bulgarian Trade Unions.
    Dimiter Polyanov, writer, member of the Bulgarian Writers' Association, Dimitrov Prize winner.

### 保加利亚人民观礼代表团名单

1951-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚人民观礼代表团名单
    【新华社三十日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆典的保加利亚人民观礼代表团名单如下：
    团长：乔治·卡拉斯拉渥夫，保加利亚和平委员会副主席。
    团员：马丽亚·基利洛娃，保加利亚工会中央理事会秘书。
    季米特尔·波梁诺夫，作家，保加利亚作家协会委员，季米特洛夫奖金得奖者。
