Title: Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Victory of the Chinese and Korean People's Armies Fighting Together Against American Aggression. Korean People's Army Delegation in Kaesong Hosts Banquet for Volunteer Army Delegation. Unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Holds Meeting on the Front Lines to Commemorate the First Anniversary of Fighting Abroad
Author:
Date: 1951-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, October 26th. To celebrate the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's participation in the war, the Korean People's Army delegation participating in the Korean armistice negotiations in Kaesong hosted a banquet for the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation on the evening of the 25th. During the banquet, the Korean and Chinese armistice negotiation delegations warmly congratulated each other on the brilliant victory of the Korean and Chinese People's Armies fighting together against the American imperialist war of aggression for the past year. The banquet was held in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

	The people of Kaesong specially organized a comfort group to visit the Volunteer Army units. The comfort group brought gifts such as silk banners and ginseng to present to the commanders and fighters of the Volunteer Army.

	The Kaesong Municipal People's Committee hosted a banquet for representatives of the Volunteer Army's Kaesong garrison troops. During the banquet, everyone frequently raised their glasses to congratulate the great victory of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army in one year of fighting.

	The people in the liberated areas of Changdan and Kaepung counties near Kaesong also sent representatives to warmly visit the local Volunteer Army.

	Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, October 26th. A unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army held a meeting on the front lines on the anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's participation in the war to celebrate the glorious victory of the past year. The entire faculty and students of the Korean Patriotic Martyrs' Children's Vocational School and representatives of local residents also attended the meeting.

	At the meeting, the unit leader reported on the victorious experience of fighting abroad for the past year. The unit fought north and south in the five battles and made great achievements, and emerged heroic units such as the "Steel Company," "Ever-Victorious Company," and "Pioneer Company," and heroes such as Zhang Caishu, Zheng Qi, and Zhao Zilin. The unit leader pointed out that this victory was the result of the hard work of all commanders and fighters based on high patriotism and internationalism, with the strong support of the people of the motherland and the Korean people. He called on all commanders and fighters to continue to advance and strive for the realization of peace in Korea.

	Representatives of the Korean Patriotic Martyrs' Children's Vocational School and representatives of local Korean residents spoke successively. They unanimously expressed their deep gratitude and congratulations for the great victory achieved by the Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army fighting side by side, and expressed that they would redouble their efforts to assist the Volunteer Army in combat and strive for greater victories in the future.

	Zhang Caishu, a famous combat hero of the unit, said on behalf of all the commanders and fighters at the meeting: We will continue to carry forward the heroic, good-fighting, and hard-working style, and advance from victory to victory on the basis of victory.

### 祝贺中朝人民军队并肩反抗美国侵略一年的胜利  开城朝鲜人民军代表团欢宴志愿军代表团  志愿军某部在前线集会纪念出国作战一周年

1951-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺中朝人民军队并肩反抗美国侵略一年的胜利
    开城朝鲜人民军代表团欢宴志愿军代表团
    志愿军某部在前线集会纪念出国作战一周年
    【新华社开城二十六日电】为庆祝中国人民志愿军出国作战一周年纪念日，在开城参加朝鲜停战谈判的朝鲜人民军代表团，于二十五日晚间设宴招待我国人民志愿军代表团。席间朝中停战谈判代表团相互热烈祝贺朝中人民军队并肩反抗美帝国主义侵略战争的一年间的辉煌胜利。宴会在友好热情的气氛中进行。
    开城人民特组织慰劳团慰问志愿军部队。慰劳团携带锦旗、人参等礼品赠给志愿军指战员。
    开城市人民委员会宴请志愿军驻开城市警卫部队的代表。席间大家频频举杯祝贺中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军作战一年的伟大胜利。
    开城附近长湍、开丰两县解放区人民也派遣代表热烈慰问当地志愿军。
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十六日电】中国人民志愿军某部，于中国人民志愿军出国作战一周年纪念日在前线集会，欢庆一年来的光辉胜利。参加大会的还有朝鲜爱国烈士遗子女技艺学院的全体师生和当地居民代表。
    会上该部首长报告了出国作战一年的胜利的经历。该部在五次战役中转战南北，立下了巨大功绩，涌现出“钢铁连”、“常胜连”、“先锋连”等英雄单位，和张才树、郑起、赵子林等英雄人物。该部首长指出：这一胜利是在祖国人民和朝鲜人民的大力支援下，在全体指战员高度爱国主义与国际主义的思想基础上，艰苦奋斗的结果。他号召全体指战员继续前进，为争取实现朝鲜的和平而奋斗。
    朝鲜爱国烈士遗子女技艺学院代表和当地朝鲜居民代表相继讲话。他们一致对志愿军和朝鲜人民军并肩作战所取得的伟大胜利表示深切的感谢和祝贺，他们并表示今后要加倍援助志愿军作战，争取更大的胜利。
    该部著名战斗英雄张才树代表全体指战员在会上说：我们一定继续发扬英勇善战艰苦奋斗的作风，在胜利的基础上向胜利前进。
