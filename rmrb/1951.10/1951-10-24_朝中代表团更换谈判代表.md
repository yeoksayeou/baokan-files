Title: Korean-Chinese Delegation Changes Negotiating Representatives
Author:
Date: 1951-10-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 22nd. The headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers has decided to replace General Deng Hua with General Bian Zhangwu as the first negotiating representative. The headquarters of the Korean People's Army has decided to replace General Zhang Pingshan with General Zheng Doukun as the Korean People's Army negotiating representative.

### 朝中代表团更换谈判代表

1951-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    朝中代表团更换谈判代表
    【新华社平壤二十二日电】中国人民志愿军总部决定由边章五将军代替邓华将军为第一谈判代表。朝鲜人民军总部决定由郑斗困将军代替张平山将军为朝鲜人民军谈判代表。
