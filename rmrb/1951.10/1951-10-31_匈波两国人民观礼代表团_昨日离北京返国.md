Title: Hungarian and Polish People's Observation Delegations Depart Beijing Yesterday to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1951-10-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 30th: The Hungarian and Polish People's Observation Delegations, who were invited to attend our National Day celebrations, departed Beijing by plane at 6:00 AM today to return home. Those who came to the airport to see them off included: Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Liu Ziju, Director of the Culture and Education Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Zhao Zhongchi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, among others. Hungarian Ambassador Száfranek and Polish Embassy Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Doda also came to the airport to see them off.

### 匈波两国人民观礼代表团  昨日离北京返国

1951-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    匈波两国人民观礼代表团
    昨日离北京返国
    【新华社三十日讯】应邀参加我国国庆典礼的匈牙利人民观礼代表团和波兰人民观礼代表团，在今天上午六时乘飞机离北京返国。到机场欢送的有：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会副秘书长吴茂荪，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中华全国总工会文教部长刘子久，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会秘书长荣高棠，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中苏友好协会总会副总干事赵仲池和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等多人。匈牙利大使夏法朗柯和波兰大使馆临时代办道定也到机场欢送。
