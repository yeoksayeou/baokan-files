Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Yaqi, Vice Chairman, and Sandilye, Secretary of the All-India Forward Bloc

Author:

Date: 1951-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 1st -- Yaqi, Vice Chairman, and Sandilye, Secretary of the All-India Forward Bloc, sent a telegram on behalf of the All-India Forward Bloc to congratulate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. In their telegram, they extended "the most ardent revolutionary salute" to Chairman Mao Zedong and the People's Republic of China. The telegram stated: "This year's celebration of the second National Day of the People's Republic of China is a great encouragement to the millions of hungry people in India who have received food assistance and aid from the People's Republic of China."

### 全印前进集团副主席雅齐和书记桑迪利亚贺电

1951-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    全印前进集团副主席雅齐和书记桑迪利亚贺电
    【新华社新德里一日电】全印前进集团副主席雅齐和书记桑迪利亚，代表全印前进集团致电祝贺中华人民共和国成立二周年。他们在电文中向毛泽东主席和中华人民共和国致“最热诚的革命敬礼”。电文说：“今年庆祝中华人民共和国第二届国庆，对于受到中华人民共和国的粮食接济和援助的数百万印度饥民，是一个伟大的鼓舞。”
