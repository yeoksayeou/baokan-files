Title: Swedish Ambassador, Danish Envoy, and Swiss Envoy Send Letters of Congratulation on China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1951-10-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The Swedish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Torsten Hammarström, and the Danish Envoy Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Alex Mørch, sent letters to Mr. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, on September 29th. The Swiss Envoy Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. René Zoellner, also sent a letter to Minister Zhou Enlai on October 1st, congratulating China on its National Day.

### 瑞典大使及丹公使瑞士公使致函祝贺我国庆

1951-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典大使及丹公使瑞士公使致函祝贺我国庆
    【新华社一日讯】瑞典王国驻华特命全权大使赛德勃隆及丹麦王国驻华特命全权公使穆克曾于九月二十九日致函我中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长，瑞士驻华特命全权公使任佐立于十月一日亦致函周恩来部长，庆贺我国国庆。
