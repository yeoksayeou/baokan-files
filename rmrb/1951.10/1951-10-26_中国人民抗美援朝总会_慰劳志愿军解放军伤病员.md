Title: China People's Anti-American Aid Korea General Association Sends Condolences to Wounded and Sick Soldiers of the Volunteer Army and the People's Liberation Army

Author: 
Date: 1951-10-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 25th. As the people of the whole country warmly celebrate the first anniversary of the great victory of the Resist-America-Aid-Korea movement and the Chinese People's Volunteers in fighting abroad, the China People's Anti-American Aid Korea General Association decided on the 25th to send condolences to each of the Volunteer Army soldiers who were heroically fighting against the American aggressors on the Korean battlefield and the wounded People's Liberation Army soldiers who were gloriously wounded in suppressing bandits in China, with RMB 50,000 yuan per person, to express the love and care of the people of the whole country for them. This consolation money has been entrusted to the hospitals and revolutionary disabled soldiers' schools where the Volunteer Army and the People's Liberation Army wounded and sick soldiers are located for distribution.

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会  慰劳志愿军解放军伤病员

1951-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民抗美援朝总会
    慰劳志愿军解放军伤病员
    【新华社二十五日讯】在全国人民热烈庆祝抗美援朝运动与中国人民志愿军出国作战一周年所获伟大胜利之际，中国人民抗美援朝总会决定于二十五日慰劳在朝鲜战场英勇抗击美国侵略军而光荣负伤的志愿军和在国内剿匪光荣负伤的解放军伤病员每人人民币五万元，以表示全国人民对他们的热爱与关怀。此项慰问金已请志愿军和解放军伤病员所在各地医院和革命残废军人学校等机关代发。
