Title: Overseas Chinese Celebration of National Day Preparatory Committee Congratulatory Telegram from Port Louis, British Mauritius
Author:
Date: 1951-10-08
Page: 4

    [Our Newspaper] The Overseas Chinese Celebration of National Day Preparatory Committee in Port Louis, British Mauritius, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao, congratulating the motherland on National Day. The original telegram is as follows: To Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government:
    Under your wise leadership, the motherland is becoming increasingly bright and great. Now, on the occasion of the second National Day, we respectfully send this telegram and wish you good health. In order to build a new motherland, we hope that Taiwan will be liberated from its suffering as soon as possible.
    The Preparatory Committee for the National Day Celebration of All Overseas Chinese in Port Louis, Mauritius, September 30th

### 英属毛律休斯岛鲁易士港  华侨庆祝国庆筹委会贺电

1951-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    英属毛律休斯岛鲁易士港华侨庆祝国庆筹委会贺电
    【本报讯】英属毛律休斯岛鲁易士港华侨庆祝国庆筹委会致电毛主席，祝贺祖国国庆节。原电如下：中央人民政府毛主席：
    祖国在您的英明领导下日趋光明伟大，现值二届国庆，谨电致敬，并祝政躬康泰。为建设新祖国，更希早日出台湾于水火。
    毛律休斯岛鲁易士港
    全体华侨庆祝国庆筹备委员会九月三十日
