Title: Commemorating the First Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' Entry into Korea: Xikang Tibetan Autonomous Region Holds Representative Conference on Resisting America and Aiding Korea; Beijing Business Community Formulates Measures to Strengthen Practical Actions in Resisting America and Aiding Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-10-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Ya'an, 17th -- To welcome the anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' entry into Korea and to continue strengthening the work of resisting America and aiding Korea, the people of the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Province held the region's first representative conference on resisting America and aiding Korea on the 13th and 14th of this month, summarizing past work experience and determining the guidelines for future work.

More than 400 representatives from all walks of life, including Tibetans, Hans, Huis, and Yis from the region's twenty counties, attended the conference. All the representatives discussed the current situation and unanimously agreed that the people of all ethnic groups in the region must continue to strengthen the work of resisting America and aiding Korea.

The conference considered that the movement to resist America and aid Korea in the region had been successful. Most of the people in the region have received patriotic education on resisting America and aiding Korea; the region's plan to increase production and make donations was overfulfilled by September; and the people of all ethnic groups in the region wrote a large number of letters of appreciation to the Volunteers and donated more than 2,000 items of comfort goods. In the patriotic movement, the unity of all ethnic groups has also been strengthened, and all work has made significant progress.

The conference determined that the guidelines for the work of resisting America and aiding Korea in the region in the future are: to continue to popularize patriotic education in remote areas; to vigorously promote the patriotic pact movement in rural and pastoral areas; to gradually develop agricultural and animal husbandry production; to further strengthen the unity among all ethnic groups; and to resolutely eliminate bandits and special agents, and consolidate national defense and social security.
Xinhua News Agency, 18th -- To welcome the anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' entry into Korea, the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce convened a representative meeting of various industries and stallholders on the 17th, deciding to support the Volunteer heroes who are bravely fighting the American aggressors on the Korean front with practical actions such as thoroughly inspecting the implementation of the patriotic pact, and completing more than 75% of the payment task of the half-year donation plan. At the initiative of Fu Huating, chairman of the federation, all the representatives attending the meeting unanimously adopted the following commemorative measures: (1) Continue to strengthen patriotic education, and carry out a comprehensive and in-depth publicity campaign around the 25th, with various industries and markets holding forums and lectures to oppose the illegal separate peace treaty between the United States and Japan and the arming of Japan, and to oppose the United States' obstruction of the Korean negotiations and its occupation of our country's Taiwan; (2) Continue to strengthen the movement to increase production and donate aircraft and artillery, and complete the payment task of more than 75% of the total amount pledged by the industrial and commercial circles of the city before the 25th; (3) Continue to thoroughly inspect the implementation of the patriotic pact, and after the inspection, each grassroots unit that has signed the patriotic pact must combine it with its own business to study and formulate improvement measures; (4) Continue to strengthen the work of giving preferential treatment to the families of martyrs and military personnel, mobilize the industrial and commercial circles to write letters of appreciation to the families of the Volunteers and the honored soldiers who were bravely wounded in combat, and participate in the condolence activities organized by the local government; (5) Actively participate in the North China regional urban and rural materials exchange exhibition being held; (6) In conjunction with the above work, discover and commend model units, model individuals, and model deeds that have emerged in the movement to resist America and aid Korea.

### 迎接人民志愿军出国作战一周年纪念日  西康藏族自治区举行抗美援朝代表会议  北京市工商界订出办法加强抗美援朝的实际行动

1951-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    迎接人民志愿军出国作战一周年纪念日
    西康藏族自治区举行抗美援朝代表会议
    北京市工商界订出办法加强抗美援朝的实际行动
     【新华社雅安十七日电】西康省藏族自治区人民为迎接中国人民志愿军出国作战周年纪念日，继续加强抗美援朝工作，本月十三、十四两日举行全区首届抗美援朝代表会议，总结过去的工作经验，确定今后各项工作的方针。
    出席会议的包括该区二十个县的藏、汉、回、彝等四个民族的各界代表四百多人。全体代表讨论了目前时局，一致认为今后全区各族各界人民必须继续加强抗美援朝工作。
    会议认为该区抗美援朝运动是有成绩的。全区大部分人民已受到抗美援朝爱国主义教育；全区增产捐献计划在九月份以前即已超额完成；全区各族人民给志愿军写了大批的慰问信，并捐献慰劳品达两千余件。在爱国运动中，各族人民的团结也日益加强，各项工作都有显著进步。
    会议确定今后该区抗美援朝工作的方针是：继续向边远地区普及爱国主义教育；大力在农村牧场中推行爱国公约运动；逐步发展农牧业生产；进一步加强各民族间的团结；坚决肃清土匪特务，巩固国防和社会治安。【新华社十八日讯】为了迎接中国人民志愿军出国作战周年纪念日，北京市工商业联合会在十七日召开了各行业、各摊商代表大会，决定以深入检查爱国公约的执行情况、完成半年捐献计划百分之七十五以上的缴款任务等实际行动，支援正在朝鲜前线英勇打击美国侵略军的志愿军英雄们。在该会主任委员傅华亭的倡议下，全体与会代表一致通过下列纪念办法：（一）继续加强爱国主义教育，在二十五日前后进行一次普及深入的宣传，各行业、各市场分别召开座谈会和讲演会，反对美国非法单独对日媾和与武装日本，反对美国阻挠朝鲜谈判和霸占我国台湾；（二）继续加强增产捐献飞机大炮运动，在二十五日前，完成全市工商界认捐总数的百分之七十五以上的缴款任务；（三）继续深入检查爱国公约的执行情况，每个订立爱国公约的基层单位，在检查后，必须结合本身业务研究与订出改进的办法；（四）继续加强优待烈士家属、军人家属的工作，发动工商界向志愿军家属和在作战中英勇负伤的荣誉军人写慰问信，并参加当地区政府主持的慰问活动；（五）积极参加华北地区正在举行的城乡物资交流展览会；（六）结合上述工作，发现与表扬在抗美援朝运动中涌现的模范单位、模范人物、模范事迹。
