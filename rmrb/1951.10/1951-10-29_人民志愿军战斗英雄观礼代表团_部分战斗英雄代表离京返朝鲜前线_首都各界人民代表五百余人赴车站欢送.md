Title: People's Volunteer Army Combat Heroes Delegation: Some Combat Hero Representatives Leave Beijing to Return to the Korean Front; Representatives from All Circles in the Capital, Over 500 People, Go to the Station to See Them Off
Author:
Date: 1951-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Combat Heroes National Day Observation Delegation, which returned home to participate in the National Day celebrations, had some representatives go to various places to report to the people of the motherland about their heroic fighting on the front lines. The remaining 51 combat hero representatives, led by Meng Fang and Li Huafei, left Beijing on the afternoon of the 28th to return to the Korean front. Over 500 representatives from all democratic parties, people's organizations, and various circles in the capital went to the station to see them off. A farewell meeting was held at the station. Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association, delivered a farewell speech at the meeting. He said: The people of the motherland extremely admire and sincerely thank you for driving the American invading army from the Yalu River back to the vicinity of the 38th parallel, allowing the people of the motherland to live and work in peace and engage in the various construction projects of the New China. You are not only the meritorious servants of the Chinese people, but also the pillars of world peace and the Great Wall of peace-loving people all over the world. I hope that you will continue to carry forward your heroic fighting spirit and strive for the victorious conclusion of the Korean War. Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, then delivered a speech, saying: We have achieved great victories in the anti-aggression struggle over the past year, and the people of the motherland have your merits in mind. The people of the whole country are following Chairman Mao's instructions to continue to strengthen the Resist America, Aid Korea work, increase production, practice thrift, and support you with greater strength. Victory will surely belong to the Chinese and Korean peoples who are fighting for justice.

Combat hero representative Xu Changfu delivered a reply, saying: After we return to the front lines, we must report in detail to all comrades the booming Resist America, Aid Korea movement and the production and construction situation in the motherland, as well as the care and love of the people of the motherland for the Volunteer Army, so that the comrades will love our motherland even more and fight the enemy even more heroically. When all the comrades of our delegation return to the front lines, we must continue to work hard, perform more meritorious deeds, and strive for the greatest glory in order to answer the care and love of the people of the motherland for us.

### 人民志愿军战斗英雄观礼代表团  部分战斗英雄代表离京返朝鲜前线  首都各界人民代表五百余人赴车站欢送

1951-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    人民志愿军战斗英雄观礼代表团
    部分战斗英雄代表离京返朝鲜前线
    首都各界人民代表五百余人赴车站欢送
    【新华社二十八日讯】回国参加国庆典礼的中国人民志愿军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表团，除一部分代表去各地向祖国人民报告他们在前线英勇作战的情形外，其余五十一位战斗英雄代表由孟方、李化飞率领在二十八日下午离京返朝鲜前线。赴车站欢送的有首都各民主党派、各人民团体、各界人民代表五百余人。欢送者并在车站举行了欢送会。北京市抗美援朝分会主席张奚若在会上致欢送词，他说：祖国人民极端地钦佩和衷心地感谢你们，感谢你们把美国侵略军从鸭绿江边赶回三八线附近，使祖国人民得以安居乐业和从事新中国的各种建设工作。你们不但是中国人民的功臣，同时也是世界和平的支柱，是全世界爱好和平人民的长城。希望你们继续发扬英勇的战斗精神，争取朝鲜战争的胜利结束。继由抗美援朝总会秘书长刘宁一致词，他说：我们在一年来的反侵略斗争中已获得了伟大的胜利，祖国人民对你们的功绩是铭记在心的。全国人民都遵照毛主席的指示，继续加强抗美援朝工作，增加生产，厉行节约，以更大的力量来支援你们。胜利一定属于为正义而战的中朝两国人民的。
    战斗英雄代表徐长福致答词，他说：我们回到前线之后，一定要把祖国人民轰轰烈烈的抗美援朝运动、生产建设的情况，以及祖国人民对志愿军的关怀和热爱详细地报告给全体同志们，让同志们更加热爱我们的祖国和更英勇地与敌人作战。我们代表团全体同志们回到前线，一定要继续努力，更多地立功，争取最大的光荣，来回答祖国人民对我们的关怀和热爱。
