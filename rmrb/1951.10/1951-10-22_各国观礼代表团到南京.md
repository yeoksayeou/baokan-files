Title: Foreign Delegation Arrives in Nanjing to Attend National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1951-10-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, October 20th: The Indian Goodwill Delegation to China and people's observation delegations from Burma, Indonesia, Pakistan, and other countries, a total of twenty people, who were invited to attend our National Day celebrations, arrived in Nanjing on the 19th. Greeting them at the railway station were Jin Shanbao, Chairman of the Nanjing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Vice-Chairman Shi Ximin, Li Leping, Deputy Mayor of the Nanjing Municipal People's Government, and representatives of various people's organizations. That evening, 1,500 people's representatives from all walks of life in Nanjing held a welcome meeting. Representatives of the various delegations spoke at the meeting about their impressions of attending our National Day celebrations, unanimously praising the great achievements of New China in the two years since its founding, and expressing their determination to work with the Chinese people to strive for world peace.
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, October 21st: The people's observation delegations from Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Mongolia, and the German Democratic Republic, as well as the Korean writer Li Tae-joon, a total of thirty people, who were invited to attend our National Day celebrations, arrived in Nanjing from Hangzhou on the evening of the 21st. Jin Shanbao, Chairman of the Nanjing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Li Leping, Deputy Mayor of the Nanjing Municipal People's Government, and more than 30 representatives of various people's mass organizations welcomed them at the railway station.

### 各国观礼代表团到南京

1951-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    各国观礼代表团到南京
    【新华社南京二十日电】应邀参加我国国庆典礼的印度亲善访华团和缅甸、印尼、巴基斯坦等国人民观礼代表团一行二十人在十九日抵达南京。赴车站欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会南京分会主席金善宝、副主席石西民，南京市人民政府副市长李乐平和各人民团体代表等。当日晚，南京市各界人民代表一千五百人举行了欢迎大会，各代表团的代表们在会上畅谈了这次参加我国国庆典礼的观感，一致盛赞新中国成立两年来的伟大成就，表示决心和中国人民一起为争取世界和平而奋斗。
    【新华社南京二十一日电】应邀参加我国国庆典礼的保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古和德意志民主共和国人民观礼代表团及朝鲜作家李泰俊等一行三十人，在二十一日晚由杭州到南京。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会南京分会主席金善宝，南京市人民政府副市长李乐平及各人民群众团体代表等三十多人都到车站欢迎。
