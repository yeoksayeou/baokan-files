Title: Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission Hosts Banquet for Combat Hero Representatives of the People's Liberation Army and the Volunteer Army; Agricultural Labor Models Coming to Beijing to Visit Tianjin Materials Exchange Exhibition

Author:
Date: 1951-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission held a banquet at 6:00 PM today in Zhongshan Hall, Zhongshan Park, in honor of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Combat Hero National Day Observation Delegation and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Combat Hero National Day Observation Delegation. More than 170 people from the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Combat Hero National Day Observation Delegations were invited to the banquet. Also invited were the heads of the General Staff Department, the General Political Department, the General Cadre Management Department, and the General Logistics Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, as well as the heads of the Air Force, Navy, and various Special Forces units of the People's Liberation Army, totaling more than 30 people.

The banquet was hosted by Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department. After a brief speech by Deputy Director Xiao Hua, the banquet officially began. During the banquet, toasts were frequently raised to the health of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, to the great victory of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the Korean battlefield, to the significant progress of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in building a regularized and modernized national defense force, and to the health of the combat heroes. The banquet ended at 7:30 PM in a joyful atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The agricultural labor models who came to Beijing to observe National Day will go to Tianjin on the 9th to visit the North China Region Urban and Rural Materials Exchange Exhibition. The agricultural labor models who came to Beijing to observe National Day are extremely grateful for the hospitality of the Central People's Government. On the eve of National Day, Chairman Mao hosted a banquet for them in Huairen Hall. In recent days, they have successively participated in welcome receptions held by the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, National Day celebration parties, symposiums held by the Ministry of Agriculture, and visited the North China Agricultural Machinery General Factory, the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute, the National Railway Exhibition, the Resist America, Aid Korea Exhibition, the National Minority Cultural Relics Exhibition, and toured the capital's scenic spots.

The agricultural labor models were extremely excited when they met Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and Premier Zhou at the banquet. They frequently toasted to the health of Chairman Mao, and to the health of Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Premier Zhou, and other leaders. They said, "After we go back, we must tell everyone about Chairman Mao's concern for us peasants, and about Chairman Mao's health."

### 中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会  欢宴人民解放军和志愿军战斗英雄代表  来京农业劳动模范将往天津参观物资交流展览会

1951-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会
    欢宴人民解放军和志愿军战斗英雄代表
    来京农业劳动模范将往天津参观物资交流展览会
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会于今日下午六时假座中山公园中山堂欢宴中国人民解放军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表团与中国人民志愿军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表团。应邀赴宴的有中国人民解放军与中国人民志愿军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表全体共一百七十余人，应邀作陪的有人民革命军事委员会总参谋部、总政治部、总干部管理部、总后勤部各部首长，人民解放军空军、海军及各特种兵部队首长共三十余人。
    宴会由总政治部萧华副主任主持。在萧华副主任简短致词后，宴会即正式开始，席间频频为毛主席和朱总司令的健康，为中国人民志愿军在朝鲜战场的伟大胜利，为中国人民解放军在建设正规化、近代化国防军中的长足进步，为战斗英雄们的健康而干杯。宴会至七时半尽欢而散。
    【新华社七日讯】国庆节来北京观礼的农业劳动模范们，九日将前往天津，参观华北区城乡物资交流展览会。这次来北京观礼的农业劳动模范们，对中央人民政府的招待极为感谢。国庆节前夕，毛主席曾在怀仁堂宴请他们。连日来，他们曾先后参加中央人民政府农业部的欢迎会、国庆庆祝晚会、农业部召开的座谈会，参观华北农业机械总厂、华北农业科学研究所、全国铁道展览会、抗美援朝展览会、少数民族文物展览会，和游览首都名胜。
    农业劳动模范们在宴会上会见毛主席、朱总司令和周总理时极为兴奋，他们频频为毛主席的健康干杯，为朱总司令、周总理等首长的健康干杯。他们说：“我们回去后，一定要把毛主席对我们农民的关心，要把毛主席的健康告诉大家。”
