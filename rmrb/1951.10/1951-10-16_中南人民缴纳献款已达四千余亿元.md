Title: Central and Southern China People's Donations Reach Over 440 Billion Yuan

Author: 
Date: 1951-10-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 15th: As of the 13th, the amount of aircraft and artillery donations from the people of Central and Southern China has reached 444.1215 billion yuan, nearly half of the total amount pledged in the region. Hubei Province has completed more than 75.7 percent of its total pledged amount. To commemorate National Day, Henan Province launched a wave of collective payments throughout the province. By National Day, the business community of Yu County had completed 92 percent of its entire donation plan. As of the end of September, Ganzhou Prefecture in Jiangxi Province had completed its donation plan of 10.5 billion yuan for the entire prefecture, and over-donated 700 million yuan. Thanks to mutual assistance and cooperation, the Xie Yucheng group in Dama Township, Lanshan County, Hunan Province, increased rice production by more than 70 *shi* this year, and they paid off their planned donation of more than 500 *jin* of rice in one lump sum. On the eve of National Day, the people of Quan County, Guangxi Province, overfulfilled the donation plan for the "Quanzhou" fighter plane. Currently, people in various regions are continuing to actively make payments to welcome the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the war in Korea.

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 15th: In response to the call from the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea on the 13th of this month regarding commemorating the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the war in Korea and strengthening the work of resisting America and aiding Korea, the business community of Xi'an has decided to complete the plan to donate funds for ten fighter planes in half a year before the 25th of this month, and to donate an additional "Volunteer Army Hero" fighter plane to welcome the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the war in Korea. The Xi'an business community also wrote a letter to business communities across the country, expressing their willingness to continue to display a high degree of patriotism with them, complete the entire donation plan around this great anniversary, and support our "dearest people" who are fighting bravely against the enemy on the Korean front! The original letter is as follows:

    Fellow members of the business community across the country:

    In order to welcome the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the war in Korea, our business community in Xi'an is enthusiastically making donations, and has decided to complete the donation plan for ten fighter planes before the 25th, and to donate an additional "Volunteer Army Hero" fighter plane. We are willing to continue to demonstrate patriotism with our colleagues in the business community across the country, complete the donation plan around this great anniversary, and support our "dearest people" who are bravely fighting the enemy on the front lines in Korea!

### 中南人民缴纳献款已达四千余亿元

1951-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    迎接志愿军出国作战一周年纪念日
    中南人民缴纳献款已达四千余亿元
    西安市工商界决定缴纳全部献款增献飞机一架
    并函约全国工商界在纪念日前后完成捐献计划
    【新华社汉口十五日电】中南各地人民缴纳飞机大炮献款的数字，截至十三日止，已达四千四百十二亿一千五百万元，将近全区认捐总数的一半。湖北省已完成认捐总数的百分之七十五点七强。河南省为纪念国庆，曾在全省范围内掀起集体缴款的热潮，禹县工商界至国庆日止，已完成全部捐献计划的百分之九十二。江西省赣州专区至九月底止，已完成全专区一百零五亿元的捐献计划，并超额捐献了七亿元。湖南省蓝山县大麻乡谢玉臣小组，由于互助合作，今年增产稻谷七十多石，他们一次缴清了认捐稻谷五百多斤的计划。广西省全县各界人民在国庆节前夕，超额完成“全州号”战斗机的捐献计划。现在各地人民正继续以积极缴款来迎接志愿军出国作战一周年纪念。
    【新华社西安十五日电】西安市工商界响应中国人民抗美援朝总会本月十三日关于纪念中国人民志愿军出国作战一周年并加强抗美援朝工作的号召，决定在本月二十五日前完成半年捐献十架战斗机的计划，并增献“志愿军英雄号”战斗机一架，来迎接中国人民志愿军出国作战一周年。该市工商界还写信给全国各地工商界，愿意和他们一起继续发挥高度爱国精神，完成全部捐献计划。原信如下：全国各地工商界同仁：
    为着迎接我国人民志愿军出国作战一周年纪念日，我们西安市工商界正踊跃缴纳献款，决定在二十五日前完成十架战斗机的捐款计划，并增献“志愿军英雄号”战斗机一架，我们愿意和全国各地工商界的同仁，一起继续发挥爱国精神，在这伟大的纪念日前后，完成捐献计划，支援我们在朝鲜前线奋勇杀敌的“最亲爱的人”！
