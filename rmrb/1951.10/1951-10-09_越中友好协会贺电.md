Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Vietnam-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1951-10-09
Page: 4

[This dispatch comes from] this newspaper.

    Telegram of Congratulations from the Vietnam-China Friendship Association
    [This newspaper dispatch] To Chairman Mao:
    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we wish the Chinese people new achievements in their construction work and new victories in their "Resist America, Aid Korea" movement.
    We are determined to consolidate and develop the friendship between Vietnam and China in order to contribute to the struggle against imperialism and to defend world peace in Southeast Asia.
                        Vietnam-China Friendship Association

### 越中友好协会贺电

1951-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    越中友好协会贺电
    【本报讯】越中友好协会致电毛主席，祝贺我国国庆节，原电如下：中华人民共和国毛泽东主席：
    值此中华人民共和国成立二周年之际，我们预祝中国人民在他们的建设工作中获得新的成就，并在他们的“抗美援朝”运动中得到新的胜利。
    我们决心巩固和发展越中间的友谊，以有助于东南亚反对帝国主义和保卫世界和平的斗争。
                        越中友好协会
