Our Great Motherland

The magnificently scenic Xiang River is the major artery for water transportation and a renowned fish-producing area in Hunan Province. This is a freighter docked on the Xiang River near Changsha. They are preparing to load the year's abundant rice harvest for transport to various locations.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    风景壮丽的湘江是湖南省水上交通的大动脉和著名的产鱼区。这是停泊在长沙附近湘江上的货船。它们正准备装运今年丰收的稻米到各地去。
