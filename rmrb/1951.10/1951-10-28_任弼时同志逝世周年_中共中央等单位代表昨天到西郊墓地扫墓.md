Title: Comrade Ren Bishi's Death Anniversary: Representatives from the CPC Central Committee and Other Units Sweep the Tomb at the Western Suburbs Cemetery Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: Today marks the first anniversary of the death of Comrade Ren Bisi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Honorary Chairman of the All-China Youth League. Representatives from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central Committee of the All-China Youth League, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the Beijing Municipal People's Government, as well as family members of Comrade Ren Bishi, a total of more than 100 people, went to the tomb of Comrade Ren Bishi in the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery in the western suburbs of Beijing at 10:30 am to sweep the tomb.

### 任弼时同志逝世周年  中共中央等单位代表昨天到西郊墓地扫墓

1951-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    任弼时同志逝世周年
    中共中央等单位代表昨天到西郊墓地扫墓
    【新华社二十七日讯】今日为中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会名誉主席任弼时同志逝世一周年纪念日。中国共产党中央委员会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会及北京市人民政府等单位代表和任弼时同志家属等共百余人，于十时半前往北京西郊八宝山人民公墓任弼时同志墓地扫墓。
