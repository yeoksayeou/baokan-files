Title: World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary Louis Saillant cables Comrade Liu Shaoqi to congratulate China's second National Day
Author:
Date: 1951-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th:
World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary Louis Saillant sent a cable to Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, congratulating China's second National Day. The cable is as follows:
Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Beijing:
On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the Government of the People's Republic of China, we represent every worker's warm congratulations to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions for the great success and achievements it has achieved since 1949, and wish it rapid progress in the future. The efforts of Chinese workers under the leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong to build socialism and fight for peace against imperialism are an inspiration to workers all over the world.
World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary, Louis Saillant

### 世界工联总书记路易·赛扬电刘少奇同志祝贺我国第二届国庆

1951-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    世界工联总书记路易·赛扬电刘少奇同志祝贺我国第二届国庆
    【新华社六日讯】世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬致电我国全国总工会名誉主席刘少奇，祝贺我国第二届国庆。电文如下：北京刘少奇同志：
    欣逢中华人民共和国政府成立两周年纪念日，我们代表每一工人的热情祝贺中华全国总工会自一九四九年来所获得的巨大成功和成就，并预祝将来的迅速进步。中国工人在毛泽东同志领导下建设社会主义、争取和平反对帝国主义的努力是对全世界工人的鼓舞。世界工联总书记路易·赛扬
