Title: Chairman Mao's Reply Telegram

Author:

Date: 1951-10-27
Page: 1

Chairman Mao's Reply Telegram to Mr. Dalai Lama:

    I have received your telegram of October 24, 1951. I thank you for your efforts in implementing the Agreement on the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet, and extend my heartfelt congratulations.

    Mao Zedong
    October 26, 1951

### 毛主席复电

1951-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席复电达赖喇嘛先生：
    你于一九五一年十月二十四日的来电，已经收到了。我感谢你对实行和平解放西藏协议的努力，并致衷心的祝贺。
    毛泽东    一九五一年十月二十六日
