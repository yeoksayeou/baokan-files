    The employee welfare services in factories across our country are constantly improving. This is the nursery of the Zhejiang Hemp Textile Factory. The childcare workers here are taking the children to the garden in baby carriages to breathe fresh air.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国各地工厂的职工福利事业都在不断地改善着。这是浙江麻纺织厂的托儿所。这里的保育员正用摇篮车把小孩带到花园中呼吸新鲜空气。
