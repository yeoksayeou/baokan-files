Title: Indian Goodwill Mission and People's Delegations from Indonesia and Other Countries Arrive in Shenyang; Sundar Lal Sends Letter of Thanks to Premier Zhou and Guo Moruo Before Leaving Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-10-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 11th -- The Indian Goodwill Mission and people's observation delegations from Indonesia, Burma, Pakistan, and other countries, a total of twenty-three people, arrived in Shenyang from Beijing at 5:00 PM on the 11th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Liu Zhiming, Vice Chairman of the Northeast Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Jiao Ruoyu, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang; representatives of people's organizations in the Northeast and Shenyang; and representatives of various democratic parties, more than fifty people in total. That evening, the people's organizations jointly hosted a banquet to welcome them. In his speech, Sundar Lal, the head of the Indian Goodwill Mission, repeatedly congratulated the People's Republic of China on its thriving construction achievements under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong. After the banquet, the Northeast People's Art Theater's Youth Art Troupe held a song and dance evening.

Xinhua News Agency, 11th -- Sundar Lal, the head of the Indian Goodwill Mission, sent letters to Premier Zhou and Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, on October 10th before leaving Beijing. In his letter to Premier Zhou, Sundar Lal said: "After two memorable weeks, we are about to leave Beijing. On behalf of my delegation and myself, I would like to express my warmest thanks to you, and assure you of our friendship and India's permanent concern for the happiness of the Chinese people and the realization of the great common program that you have begun." In his letter to Chairman Guo Moruo, Sundar Lal said: "We are deeply impressed by the excellent spirit of your people, their efforts to achieve the goals of the Common Program, and their enthusiastic mobilization for peace. We once again assure you that we will wholeheartedly cooperate with you in the noble cause you are engaged in."

### 印度亲善访华团和印尼等国人民代表团抵沈阳  森德拉尔离京前函谢周总理和郭沫若

1951-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    印度亲善访华团和印尼等国人民代表团抵沈阳
    森德拉尔离京前函谢周总理和郭沫若
    【新华社沈阳十一日电】印度亲善访华团和印尼、缅甸、巴基斯坦等国人民观礼代表团一行二十三人，在十一日下午五时由北京抵沈阳。赴车站欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会东北总分会副主席刘芝明、沈阳市副市长焦若愚、东北和沈阳市各人民团体代表、各民主党派代表五十余人。当晚，各人民团体联合设宴欢迎，印度亲善访华团森德拉尔团长在致词中，一再祝贺中华人民共和国在毛泽东主席领导下建设事业蒸蒸日上的成就。晚宴后，东北人民艺术剧院少年艺术队并举行歌舞晚会。
    【新华社十一日讯】印度亲善访华团团长森德拉尔在十月十日离京前函谢周总理和中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会郭沫若主席。森德拉尔团长在给周总理的信中说：“经过了值得记忆的两星期，我们将要离开北京了。我谨代表我代表团与我个人，向你致以热烈的谢意，并向你保证我们的友谊及印度对中国人民的幸福与你们已开始获得的伟大共同纲领的实现之永久关切。”森德拉尔团长在给郭沫若主席的信中说：“贵国人民的优秀精神，他们为实现共同纲领目标的努力以及他们为争取和平的热烈动员，都给了我们极深刻的印象。我们再一次向你保证，在你们所从事的崇高事业中，我们将全心全意地和你们合作。”
