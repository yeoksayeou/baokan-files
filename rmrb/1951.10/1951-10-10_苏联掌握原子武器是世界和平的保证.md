Title: The Soviet Union's Possession of Atomic Weapons is a Guarantee of World Peace
Author: Ping Jun, An Donghua, Wang Xiupo, Chen Shixiu
Date: 1951-10-10
Page: 2

Reader's Letters:

    The Soviet Union's Possession of Atomic Weapons is a Guarantee of World Peace

To the Editor:
    We read Comrade Stalin's interview with a Pravda correspondent on atomic weapons three times in one go, and also carefully studied the People's Daily editorial. Comrade Stalin's remarks strengthened our confidence in national defense and safeguarding peace, and gave us infinite encouragement and strength.

    Comrade Stalin wisely instructed us: "Only when the people who advocate the atomic bomb see that they are no longer monopolists will they agree to ban atomic weapons." The People's Daily editorial also said: "Only by holding our own weapons tightly can we make the enemy lay down their weapons." Comrade Stalin's wise instructions are completely correct. Before the imperialists abandon their aggressive schemes, we must hold our weapons tightly and constantly strengthen our national defense.

    We will stand at our posts, work more actively, hold our weapons tightly, and forever defend the security of our great motherland!
            People's Navy Soldiers Ping Jun, An Donghua, Wang Xiupo, Chen Shixiu

### 苏联掌握原子武器是世界和平的保证
平均  安冬华  王秀坡  陈石秀
1951-10-10
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    苏联掌握原子武器是世界和平的保证编辑同志：
    斯大林同志关于原子武器答真理报记者问的谈话，我们一口气看了三遍，并且还仔细研读了人民日报的社论。斯大林同志的谈话，加强了我们对国防建设与保卫和平的信心，给予了我们无限的鼓舞与力量。
    斯大林同志英明地指示我们：“只有鼓吹原子弹的人看到他们已不再是垄断者的时候，他们才可能同意禁止原子武器。”人民日报的社论也说：“只有紧握着自己的武器，才能使敌人放下武器。”斯大林同志的英明指示是完全正确的。在帝国主义还没有放弃他们的侵略阴谋以前，我们必须紧握我们的武器，不断地加强我们的国防力量。
    我们将站在自己的岗位上，更加积极地工作，紧握着我们的武器，永远保卫着我们伟大祖国的安全！
            人民海军战士    平均  安冬华  王秀坡  陈石秀
