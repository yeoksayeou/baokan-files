Title: Liaison Officers Reach Agreement; Both Sides Agree to Sign Agreement Today; Date for Resumption of Delegation Meetings Set

Author:

Date: 1951-10-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 21st - On the morning of October 21st, liaison officers from both sides continued negotiations on the resumption of meetings of the delegations. At the meeting before the recess at 11:36 a.m., our liaison officer once again refuted the unreasonable demand of the other side that military aircraft could fly over the neutral zone. At the meeting that began at 2:00 p.m., the United Nations Command Delegation liaison officer finally agreed that, except in cases where climatic and technical conditions are uncontrollable, military aircraft from both sides shall not fly over the Panmunjom conference area, United Nations Command military aircraft shall not fly over the Kaesong area and the corridor area from Kaesong to the Panmunjom conference area, and Korean People's Army and Chinese People's Volunteer Army military aircraft shall not fly over the Munsan area and the corridor area from Munsan to the Panmunjom conference area. With this, the liaison officers of both sides have reached an agreement on all the issues on the agenda. The meeting ended at 2:40 p.m. The liaison officers of both sides agreed to sign the agreement on the morning of October 22nd and determined the date and time for the resumption of meetings of the delegations.

### 联络官会议达成协议  双方约定今天在协议上签字  同时确定代表团复会的日期

1951-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    联络官会议达成协议
    双方约定今天在协议上签字
    同时确定代表团复会的日期
    【新华社开城二十一日电】十月二十一日上午十时双方联络官继续协商双方代表团复会事宜。在上午十一时三十六分休会前的会上，我方联络官再度驳斥对方关于军用飞机可以飞入中立区上空的无理要求。下午二时开始的会上，联合国军代表团联络官最后同意除去气候条件与技术条件无法控制的情况下，双方的军用飞机均不得飞越板门店会场区上空，联合国军军用飞机不得飞越开城区及开城区至板门店会场区通道区域的上空，朝鲜人民军及中国人民志愿军的军用飞机不得飞越汶山区及汶山区至板门店会场区通道区域的上空。至此，双方联络官对议程上的各项问题均已达成协议。会议在下午二时四十分结束。双方联络官约定在十月二十二日上午在协议上签字，并确定双方代表团复会的日期与时间。
