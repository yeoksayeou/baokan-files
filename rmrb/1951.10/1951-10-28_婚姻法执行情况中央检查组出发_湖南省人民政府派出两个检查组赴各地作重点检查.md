Title: Central Inspection Group on Marriage Law Enforcement Sets Off; Hunan Provincial People's Government Sends Two Inspection Groups to Key Areas for Inspection
Author:
Date: 1951-10-28
Page: 3

    The Central Inspection Group on Marriage Law Enforcement, formed according to the resolution of the 103rd Political Affairs Conference of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, departed from Beijing on the 23rd and 24th to inspect the enforcement of the Marriage Law in various regions. The inspection group is composed of members from the Supreme People's Court, the Supreme People's Procuratorate, the People's Supervision Committee of the Central People's Government, the Legislative Affairs Commission of the Government Administration Council, the Discipline Inspection Commission of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the Ministry of Internal Affairs, the Ministry of Public Security, the Ministry of Justice, the Ministry of Education, the Ministry of Culture, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the People's Daily, Xinhua News Agency, and other agencies and organizations. Minister of Justice Shi Liang serves as the overall leader, with Vice Minister of Justice Li Mu'an, Member of the People's Supervision Committee Guo Renzhi, Director of the Service Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Cao Mengjun, and Head of the Criminal Court Trial Group of the Supreme People's Court Zhang Xiangqian as deputy leaders. The group has four subgroups: the North China subgroup is led by Committee Member Guo Renzhi, with Commissioner Ma Zhaosong of the Legislative Affairs Commission as deputy leader; the Central South subgroup is led by Deputy Director of the Third Department of the Central Ministry of Justice Wang Ruqi, with Senior Supervision Commissioner of the People's Supervision Committee Zhao Shisheng as deputy leader; the East China subgroup is led by Director Cao Mengjun, with Counselor of the People's Supervision Committee Xu Xingzhi as deputy leader; the Northwest subgroup is led by Zhang Xiangqian. (Xinhua News Agency)

    In order to inspect the implementation of the Marriage Law in various regions of the province, the Hunan Provincial People's Government has organized two inspection groups to conduct key inspections in various areas. The inspection groups are composed of representatives from the Provincial Political and Legal Affairs Committee, the People's Court, the People's Supervision Committee, the People's Procuratorate, the Preparatory Committee of the Democratic Women's Federation, the Preparatory Committee of the Federation of Trade Unions, and the Hunan Provincial Working Committee of the Communist Youth League. The inspection groups arrived in the Changde and Changsha Special Districts on the 19th of this month to conduct inspections. In addition to understanding the implementation of the Marriage Law in various regions, they will also cooperate with the local People's Courts to handle pending marriage cases. Certain serious illegal cases that were improperly judged in the past will be retried, and the handling of these cases will be used to educate cadres and the broad masses. At the same time, the Provincial People's Court has also dispatched three inspection groups to various regions to inspect the implementation of the Marriage Law by courts at all levels and to assist in handling backlog cases. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 婚姻法执行情况中央检查组出发  湖南省人民政府派出两个检查组赴各地作重点检查

1951-10-28
第3版()
专栏：

    婚姻法执行情况中央检查组出发
    湖南省人民政府派出两个检查组赴各地作重点检查
    根据中央人民政府政务院第一百零三次政务会议的决议组成的婚姻法执行情况中央检查组，已在二十三、二十四两日先后自北京出发，分赴各地检查婚姻法执行情况。该检查组由中央人民政府最高人民法院、最高人民检察署、人民监察委员会、政务院法制委员会、中共中央纪律检查委员会、中共中央华北局、中央人民政府内务部、公安部、司法部、教育部、文化部、全国民主妇联、青年团中央委员会、人民日报社、新华社等机关团体组成，由中央司法部部长史良任总组长，中央司法部副部长李木庵、人民监察委员会委员郭任之、全国民主妇联服务部部长曹孟君及最高人民法院刑庭审判组组长张向前任副组长。该组下设四个分组：华北分组由郭任之委员任组长，法制委员会马肇嵩专员任副组长；中南分组由中央司法部第三司副司长王汝琪任组长，人民监察委员会高级监察专员赵石生任副组长；华东分组由曹孟君部长任组长，人民监察委员会参事徐行之任副组长；西北分组由张向前任组长。（新华社）
    湖南省人民政府为检查该省各地婚姻法执行情况，已组织两个检查组，分赴各地作重点检查。检查组由该省政法委员会、人民法院、人民监察委员会、人民检察署、民主妇女联合会筹备委员会、总工会筹备委员会、青年团湖南省工作委员会等单位代表组成。该检查组已于本月十九日到常德、长沙两专区检查，除了解各地对婚姻法执行情况外，并将协同当地人民法院处理婚姻悬案，对某些严重违法案件，过去判决得不妥当的，将重新判处；并将通过这些案件的处理来教育干部和广大群众。与此同时，该省人民法院亦派出三个检查组分头到各地检查各级法院执行婚姻法的情况，并协助处理积案。（新华社）
