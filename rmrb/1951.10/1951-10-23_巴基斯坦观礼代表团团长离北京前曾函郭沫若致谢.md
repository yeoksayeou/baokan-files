Title: Pakistani Delegation Leader Sends Letter of Thanks to Guo Moruo Before Leaving Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-10-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 21st. Iftikharuddin, the leader of the Pakistani People's Delegation who participated in our National Day celebration, sent a letter to Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, before leaving Beijing on the 18th. The letter said: "Thank you for your warm and extremely generous hospitality during our stay in China. The Pakistani delegation is extremely grateful for your friendship and care towards us. I sincerely hope that the connection established between us will have a lasting effect on the friendship between our two peoples, and that the world peace desired by all rational people can be realized. Please accept again our gratitude for all your efforts."

### 巴基斯坦观礼代表团团长离北京前曾函郭沫若致谢

1951-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦观礼代表团团长离北京前曾函郭沫若致谢
    【新华社二十一日讯】参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的巴基斯坦人民观礼代表团团长伊夫提卡鲁丁在十八日离北京前，曾致函中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若。该函说：“感谢你对于我们在中国期间的热情和极其慷慨的招待。巴基斯坦代表团对于你们对我们的友好和关怀表示万分的感激。我衷心希望在我们之间所建立的联系将对我们两国人民的友谊有着恒久的作用，并望一切有理智的人们所希冀的世界和平能够实现。请再度接受我们对你们的种种费神的感谢。”
