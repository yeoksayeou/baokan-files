Title: Representatives of the Governments of Britain, Norway, and the Netherlands Send Letters to Congratulate China on its National Day
Author:
Date: 1951-10-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Mr. Hutchison, the British government's negotiating representative, sent a letter to Mr. Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our Central People's Government, on September 30. Mr. Golan, the Norwegian government's negotiating representative, and Mr. Houweimin, the Dutch government's negotiating representative, also sent letters to Deputy Minister Zhang Hanfu on September 29, congratulating China on its National Day.

### 英、挪、荷三国政府谈判代表致函祝贺我国庆

1951-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    英、挪、荷三国政府谈判代表致函祝贺我国庆
    【新华社一日讯】英国政府谈判代表蓝来讷曾于九月三十日致函我中央人民政府外交部章汉夫副部长，挪威政府谈判代表高兰及荷兰政府谈判代表侯卫民亦曾于九月二十九日致函章汉夫副部长，庆贺我国国庆。
