Date: 1951-10-24
Page: 2

Reader's Letters

    Protest Against the Crimes of British Imperialism in Suppressing the Egyptian People
    To the Editor:
    The crimes of British imperialism in frantically suppressing the Egyptian people have aroused great anger among the Chinese people. I am now writing a letter to the Egyptian people as follows: Dear Egyptian brothers and sisters:
  Your resolute struggle for independence and freedom has been met with suppression and slaughter by British imperialist troops, resulting in over a hundred casualties. This bloody tactic of British imperialism has aroused great anger and protest from us, the Chinese people.
    We, the Chinese people, under the leadership of the great Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, have already shattered the shackles imposed on us by imperialism for over a century, have been liberated, and are now continuing to struggle to strike at the imperialist aggressors.
    Dear Egyptian brothers and sisters, fight bravely! Peace-loving people all over the world are supporting you, and your just struggle will surely achieve final victory!
              Zhao Jingchen, Cadre School of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives

### 抗议英帝国主义镇压埃及人民的罪行
赵静尘
1951-10-24
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    抗议英帝国主义镇压埃及人民的罪行编辑同志：
    英帝国主义疯狂镇压埃及人民的罪行，使中国人民感到极大的愤怒。我现在写了一封给埃及人民的信如下：亲爱的埃及兄弟姊妹们：
  你们为争取独立自由而进行坚决的斗争，竟遭到英帝国主义军队的镇压与屠杀，因而伤亡了一百余人。英帝国主义这种血腥手段，激起了我们中国人民的极大愤怒和抗议。
    我们中国人民在伟大的毛主席和中国共产党的领导下，已经粉碎了帝国主义百余年来加在我们身上的枷锁，获得了解放，现在我们正在继续为打击帝国主义侵略者而斗争着。
    亲爱的埃及兄弟姊妹们，你们勇敢地斗争吧！全世界爱好和平的人们都在支持着你们，你们的正义斗争一定会获得最后的胜利！
              全国合作总社干部学校  赵静尘
