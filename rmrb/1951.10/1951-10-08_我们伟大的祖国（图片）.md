Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-10-08
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Our cotton farmers, educated in patriotism, are actively selling new cotton to the state. Cotton farmers in the Ninth Village of Nanyang Township, Xiaoshan County, Zhejiang Province, are writing letters of challenge here. They guarantee to sell all their cotton and hemp to the cooperative, abide by the pre-sale contracts, resolutely support government prices, and organize collective cotton sales.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国棉农受到爱国主义的教育，纷纷把新棉花卖给国家。浙江萧山县南阳乡第九村的棉农们正在这里写挑战书。他们保证把全部棉麻卖给合作社，遵守预售合同，坚决拥护政府价格，组织集体售棉。
