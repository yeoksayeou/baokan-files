Title: Developing Scientific Research in New China
Author:
Date: 1951-10-05
Page: 3

The Chinese Academy of Sciences, in order to strengthen and encourage scientific research and introduce the important academic contributions of modern Chinese scientists, has decided to compile and publish the "Modern Chinese Scientific Works Series" and, according to the instructions of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, to make this work one of the important tasks of the Academy this year.

In order to carry out this work concretely, the Chinese Academy of Sciences has drafted the "Measures for the Publication of the Modern Chinese Scientific Works Series by the Chinese Academy of Sciences" and the "Compilation and Review Plan for the Publication of the Modern Chinese Scientific Works Series by the Chinese Academy of Sciences," and has established compilation and review groups in various specialized disciplines to promote this work. According to the compilation and review plan, the focus of the series' editing in 1951 is on mathematics, physics, geology, physiology, and meteorology. Important papers published by modern Chinese scientists in their respective fields of research will be compiled into the series according to certain stages and eras of scientific development. The series will be published in both Chinese and foreign languages. Works originally published in foreign languages will be included in the original language, with a Chinese translation added. Works originally published in Chinese may be translated into Russian or English.

The compilation and publication of the "Modern Chinese Scientific Works Series" is a work of great significance for scientific research in New China. On the one hand, it can systematically introduce the important academic contributions of modern Chinese scientists and affirm the existing achievements of modern Chinese scientific research. On the other hand, it can promote the exchange of scientific research experience between China and the international community (especially the Soviet Union). We believe that, based on the existing modern Chinese scientific research and with more absorption of the experiences of the Soviet Union and other people's democratic countries in the exchange of international scientific research, New China's science will flourish with the development of New China's national defense, economy, and cultural construction.

### 发展新中国的科学研究工作

1951-10-05
第3版()
专栏：文化生活简评

    发展新中国的科学研究工作
    中国科学院为加强和鼓励科学研究工作，介绍现代中国科学家的重要学术贡献，决定编印“现代中国科学论著丛刊”，并根据政务院文化教育委员会的指示，把这个工作作为该院本年度重要工作任务之一。
    中国科学院为了具体进行这件工作，已拟订了关于“中国科学院刊行现代中国科学论著丛刊的办法”和“中国科学院刊行现代中国科学论著丛刊编审方案”，并成立了各专门学科的编审小组来推动这个工作。根据编审方案，丛刊一九五一年的编辑重点为数学、物理、地质、生理、气象五门。凡是中国现代的科学家，在其所研究的科目内所发表的重要论文，都将依照一定的科学发展的阶段和年代，汇编在丛刊内。丛刊分中文及外文两种，其论著原为外文发表者，除原著文字重版收入外，另加译中文，原为中文发表者，可译为俄文或英文。
    编印“现代中国科学论著丛刊”的工作，对于新中国的科学研究，是一项具有重大意义的工作。它一方面可以有系统地介绍现代中国科学家的重要学术贡献，肯定已有的中国现代科学研究的成果；另一方面，可以促进中国和国际（特别是苏联）间科学研究经验的交流。我们相信，在已有的现代中国科学研究的基础上，在与国际的科学研究的交流中更多地吸收苏联及其他人民民主国家的经验，新中国的科学，将随着新中国的国防、经济、文化建设的发展而蓬蓬勃勃地发展起来。
