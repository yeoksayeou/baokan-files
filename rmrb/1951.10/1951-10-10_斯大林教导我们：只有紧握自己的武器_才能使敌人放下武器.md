Title: Stalin Teaches Us: Only by Firmly Grasping Our Weapons Can We Make the Enemy Lay Down Their Weapons
Author: Lin Guanyi
Date: 1951-10-10
Page: 2

    Stalin Teaches Us:
    Only by Firmly Grasping Our Weapons
    Can We Make the Enemy Lay Down Their Weapons
To the Editor:
    After reading Comrade Stalin's interview with a Pravda reporter on the issue of atomic weapons in the newspaper, I was extremely excited. I read it again and again, deeply feeling that this is indeed "a great gospel of world peace."
    Stalin's talk teaches all peace-loving people around the world a truth: only by firmly grasping our own weapons can we make the enemy lay down their weapons.
    Stalin's talk greatly helped the people of the world to further recognize the true face of American atomic warmongers, and powerfully exposed the hypocritical lies of the United States' so-called "control" of atomic weapons.
    I believe that the Soviet Union's production of atomic weapons and the Chinese people's resistance against America and aid to Korea are for the same purpose of defending world peace. I am determined to contribute my strength to the movement to resist America and aid Korea, do a good job in donation and propaganda work, follow the path indicated by Comrade Stalin, and strive for lasting world peace!
                        Third Courtyard, Peking University Lin Guanyi

### 斯大林教导我们：只有紧握自己的武器  才能使敌人放下武器
林观仪
1951-10-10
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    斯大林教导我们：
    只有紧握自己的武器
    才能使敌人放下武器编辑同志：
    在报上看到斯大林同志关于原子武器问题答真理报记者问的谈话后，我兴奋极了。我一次又一次地阅读着它，深深地感到这的确是“世界和平的一个伟大福音”。
    斯大林的谈话教导全世界爱好和平的人民一个真理：只有紧握着自己的武器，才能使敌人放下武器。
    斯大林的谈话大大地帮助世界人民进一步认识美国原子黩武主义者的真面目，有力地揭穿了美国的所谓“管制”原子武器的伪善谎言。
    我相信苏联的生产原子武器和中国人民的抗美援朝，都是为了保卫世界和平的同一目的。我决心在抗美援朝运动中贡献出我的力量，做好捐献工作与宣传工作，遵循斯大林同志所指示的道路，为争取世界的持久和平而奋斗！
                        北京大学三院    林观仪
