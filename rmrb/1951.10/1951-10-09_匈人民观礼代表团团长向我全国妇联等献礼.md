Title: Head of Hungarian People's Delegation Presents Gifts to All-China Women's Federation, etc.
Author:
Date: 1951-10-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Mrs. Istvánné Vass, head of the Hungarian People's Delegation attending China's Second National Day celebration and Secretary-General of the Hungarian Democratic Women's League, presented gifts to the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation on the afternoon of the 8th. The gifts included a banner and other handicrafts. Li Dequan, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation, accepted the gifts on behalf of Chinese women, and Zhang Xiaomei, Chairman of the Beijing Women's Federation, accepted them on behalf of Beijing women. Li Dequan thanked Mrs. Vass and asked her to take the friendship of Chinese women back to Hungarian women. In her speech, Mrs. Vass said: Although China and Hungary are far apart, I feel as close as if I were back in my own home this time I came to China. She also reported to the staff of the Women's Federation on the past and present status of Hungarian women, the work of the Hungarian Women's Federation, and the overview of the Hungarian women's struggle for peace.

### 匈人民观礼代表团团长向我全国妇联等献礼

1951-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    匈人民观礼代表团团长
    向我全国妇联等献礼
   【新华社八日讯】参加我国第二届国庆典礼的匈牙利人民观礼代表团团长、匈牙利民主妇女联盟总书记伊斯特万尼·华斯夫人，于八日下午向中华全国民主妇女联合会和北京市民主妇女联合会献礼。礼品有锦旗及其他手工业品。全国妇联副主席李德全代表中国妇女、北京市妇联主席张晓梅代表北京市妇女受礼。李德全向华斯夫人致谢，并请她把中国妇女对匈牙利妇女的友爱带回匈牙利去。华斯夫人在致词中说：中国与匈牙利，虽然隔得很远，但我这次来到中国，就好像回到自己家里一样的亲切。她并向妇联工作人员报告了匈牙利妇女过去和现在的地位、匈牙利妇联的工作以及匈牙利妇女为和平而斗争的概况。
