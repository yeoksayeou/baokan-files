Title: Visiting Activities of Observation Delegations from India and Other Countries in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1951-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 13th. The Indian Goodwill Delegation to China and the People's Observation Delegations from Indonesia, Burma, Pakistan and other countries are conducting visiting activities in Shenyang. On the morning of the 12th, the delegation visited a factory in Shenyang and was warmly welcomed by the workers. The delegates congratulated the model workers who attended the reception at the factory. In the afternoon of the same day, the delegation visited a machine factory. On the 13th, the delegation visited the Northeast Regional Materials Exchange Exhibition. The head of the Indian Goodwill Delegation to China, Mr. Sundarlal, wrote in the book of impressions: "Through this remarkable exhibition, I am very happy to see the rapid development of China!"

### 印度等国观礼代表团在沈阳进行访问活动

1951-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

     印度等国观礼代表团在沈阳进行访问活动
    【新华社沈阳十三日电】印度亲善访华团和印尼、缅甸、巴基斯坦等国人民观礼代表团在沈阳进行访问活动。十二日上午，代表团参观了沈阳某工厂，受到职工的热烈欢迎。代表们向该厂出席招待会的劳动模范表示祝贺。当日下午，代表团参观了某机器厂。十三日，代表团参观了东北区物资交流展览大会。印度亲善访华团团长森德拉尔在感想簿上写着：“通过这个了不起的展览会，我十分高兴地看到中国的迅速发展！”
