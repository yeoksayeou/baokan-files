Title: South Vietnamese People's Army and Militia Eliminate Over a Thousand Enemies in Đồng Tháp Mười and Other Provinces
Author:
Date: 1951-10-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th: The South Vietnamese People's Army Command issued a communique stating that during May, June and July of this year, the People's Army and militia engaged in over a hundred battles in Đồng Tháp Mười, Long Châu Province, and Mỹ Tho Province, killing or wounding over 1,400 French soldiers and French mercenaries. During the same period, People's Army units captured thirteen enemy strongholds and sank twenty-one enemy river boats in the Đồng Tháp Mười reed marshes north of Saigon, seizing two mortars, eight machine guns, approximately two hundred rifles, and over three tons of ammunition and four radio stations.
    Further, according to late news from North Vietnam: Local People's Army units in Ninh Bình and Thái Bình Provinces, south of Hanoi, repelled several large-scale attacks by French aggressor troops in the latter half of August, eliminating over 400 enemy officers and soldiers and destroying a large quantity of enemy weapons.

### 南越人民军及民兵在同塔梅等省歼敌千余

1951-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    南越人民军及民兵在同塔梅等省歼敌千余
    【新华社六日讯】据越南通讯社消息：南越人民军司令部发表公报称：在今年五、六、七月间，人民军和民兵在同塔梅、龙朱省和米荻省作战一百多次，共毙伤法军和法国雇佣兵一千四百余名。在同一期间，人民军部队共拔除了十三个敌人据点，并在西贡以北的同塔梅芦苇沼泽地带击沉敌人二十一艘内河船只，缴获迫击炮两门、机关枪八挺、步枪约两百支以及三吨多弹药和四部无线电台。
    又据北越迟到消息：河内以南的宁平和太平两省的地方人民军部队曾在八月下半月击退法国侵略军几次大规模的攻击，并歼灭敌军官兵四百余名，破坏了敌军的大批武器。
