Title: Agreement on Methods for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet - The Dalai Lama Sends a Telegram to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1951-10-27
Page: 1

    Supporting the Agreement on Methods for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet
    The Dalai Lama Sends a Telegram to Chairman Mao
    [Xinhua News Agency, 26th] The Dalai Lama of the Tibetan local government sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong on October 24th. The text of the telegram is as follows: To Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government:
    This year, the Tibetan local government specially dispatched plenipotentiary representatives Kalon Ngapoi and four others, who arrived in Beijing at the end of April 1951 to hold peace talks with the plenipotentiary representatives designated by the Central People's Government. The representatives of both sides, on a friendly basis, signed the Agreement on Methods for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet on May 23, 1951. The Tibetan local government and the Tibetan monks and lay people unanimously support it, and under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, actively assist the People's Liberation Army troops entering Tibet, consolidate national defense, expel imperialist forces from Tibet, and protect the unification of the motherland's territorial sovereignty. I hereby send this telegram for your attention.
    Dalai Lama of the Tibetan Local Government
    October 24, 1951 (Gregorian calendar)
    The 24th day of the eighth month of the Iron Rabbit Year (Tibetan calendar)

### 拥护和平解放西藏办法的协议  达赖喇嘛致电毛主席

1951-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    拥护和平解放西藏办法的协议
    达赖喇嘛致电毛主席
    【新华社二十六日讯】西藏地方政府达赖喇嘛在十月二十四日致电毛泽东主席，电文如下：中央人民政府毛主席：
    今年西藏地方政府，特派全权代表噶伦阿沛等五人，于一九五一年四月底抵达北京，与中央人民政府指定的全权代表进行和谈。双方代表在友好基础上，已于一九五一年五月二十三日签订了关于和平解放西藏办法的协议。西藏地方政府及藏族僧、俗人民一致拥护，并在毛主席及中央人民政府领导下，积极协助人民解放军进藏部队，巩固国防，驱逐帝国主义势力出西藏，保护祖国领土主权的统一，谨电奉闻。西藏地方政府达赖喇嘛
    公历一九五一年十月二十四日
    藏历铁兔年八月二十四日呈
