Congratulations from the People's Committee of Brașov State, Romania
[This newspaper dispatch] The People's Committee of Brașov State, Romania, sent a telegram to the Central People's Government of our country to congratulate us on our National Day. The original telegram is as follows:

To the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

The People's Committee of Brașov State, Romania, warmly congratulates the People's Republic of China on its National Day anniversary. The working people of our state, with admiration, are watching the historic victories won by the great Chinese people under the leadership of the glorious Communist Party headed by Mao Zedong. We wish you new victories in the struggle for peace, consolidation of national independence, and the happiness of the working people in the mighty camp of peace and socialism headed by the invincible Soviet Union.

Long live the Chinese people, who are fighting selflessly for the freedom of all nations and for the liberation of the heroic Korean people from the clutches of the American devil! Long live the great Soviet Union and Comrade Stalin, the genius leader and the banner of the world's working people in the struggle for peace and freedom!

Chairman: Peter Annaki, October 1

### 罗马尼亚布拉索瓦州人民委员会来电祝贺

1951-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚布拉索瓦州人民委员会来电祝贺
    【本报讯】罗马尼亚布拉索瓦州人民委员会致电我中央人民政府，祝贺我国国庆，原电如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府：
    罗马尼亚布拉索瓦州人民委员会热烈祝贺中华人民共和国国庆纪念日，我州劳动人民怀着敬慕的心情，注视着伟大的中国人民在以毛泽东为首的光荣的共产党领导下所获得的历史性胜利。谨祝在以不可战胜的苏联为首的和平与社会主义的强大阵营中，获得争取和平、巩固民族独立和劳动人民的幸福的斗争的新胜利。
    为了各国人民的自由、为了从美国的魔爪中解放英勇的朝鲜人民而进行忘我战斗的中国人民万岁！伟大的苏联及天才领袖、世界劳动人民争取和平与自由的旗帜斯大林同志万岁！
            主席彼得·安那基十月一日
