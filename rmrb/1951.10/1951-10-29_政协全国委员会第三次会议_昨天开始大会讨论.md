Title: The Third Session of the CPPCC National Committee Begins Plenary Discussions Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-10-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: The Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference continued yesterday and today. Group discussions continued yesterday morning, afternoon, and this morning, and this afternoon entered the plenary discussion stage. Those who made special reports and speeches at today's meeting included: Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government; Ma Xulun, Chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Cheng Qian, Vice Chairman of the Central-South Military and Political Committee; Chen Jiageng, Member of the National Committee of the CPPCC; Fan Yong, Instructor of the Railway Brigade for Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea; Fu Zuoyi, Minister of the Ministry of Water Resources of the Central People's Government; Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government; Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the Northwest Military and Political Committee; Chen Shaokuan, Vice Chairman of the Fujian Provincial People's Government; Wazhamuji, representative of the Yi ethnic group; Peng Zemin, Chairman of the Central Supervisory Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party; Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Jiusan Society; and Chen Qiyou, Chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party. In their speeches, they unanimously supported the current tasks of the Chinese people as indicated by Chairman Mao Zedong in his opening remarks, and the reports of Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yun, Vice Premier Guo Moruo, and Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, and guaranteed to implement them in their work.
    Today's meeting adjourned at 6:00 PM and will continue tomorrow.

### 政协全国委员会第三次会议  昨天开始大会讨论

1951-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会第三次会议
    昨天开始大会讨论
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议于昨今两日继续进行。昨日上午、下午及今日上午继续分组讨论，今日下午进入大会讨论阶段。在今日的大会上作专题报告和发言的，有：中央人民政府最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，中国民主促进会主席马叙伦，中南军政委员会副主席程潜，人民政协全国委员会委员陈嘉庚，抗美援朝铁道大队指导员范永，中央人民政府水利部部长傅作义，东北人民政府副主席高崇民，西北军政委员会副主席张治中，福建省人民政府副主席陈绍宽，彝族代表瓦渣木基，中国农工民主党中央监察委员会主席彭泽民，九三学社主席许德珩，中国致公党主席陈其尤。他们在发言中，一致拥护毛泽东主席在开会辞中所指示的中国人民的当前任务和周恩来总理、陈云副总理、郭沫若副总理、彭真副主席的报告，并保证在工作中贯彻执行。
    今日会议于下午六时休会，明日将继续举行。
