Title: Our Friends Are All Over the World! – People from Various Countries Send Congratulatory Telegrams for Our National Day
Author:
Date: 1951-10-04
Page: 4

    Our Friends Are All Over the World!
    ——People from Various Countries Send Congratulatory Telegrams for Our National Day
    Telegram from Palmiro Togliatti,
    General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party:
    To Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong:
    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Italian Communists and democrats extend warm greetings to the great People's Republic of China and to you – the leader and founder of this republic.
    We wish the Chinese people and the heroic Chinese Communist Party ever-increasing success in the cause of building a new life, and in the struggle against imperialist aggression, defending the freedom, independence, and peace of Asia.
            Palmiro Togliatti
            General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party
    Telegram from Prahov,
    Chairman of the Bulgarian General Trade Union, to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions in Beijing:
    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Central Council of the Bulgarian General Trade Union, on behalf of the Bulgarian workers and staff, extends warm and militant greetings to the Chinese working class and all the Chinese working people. The Chinese working class and the Chinese people, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the wise mentor Mao Zedong, are building a new democratic China; and are fighting against the US and British aggression in Taiwan and Korea, against the rearmament of Japan, and striving to lay the foundation for peace in Asia and the world. The Bulgarian workers and staff wish the heroic Chinese working class and all the Chinese people greater achievements. Long live the world camp of peace, democracy, and socialism, headed by the great Soviet Union and the peace standard-bearer Stalin!
        Prahov, Chairman of the Central Council of the Bulgarian General Trade Union
    Telegram from Kołoszyński,
    Chairman of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions, to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions in Beijing:
    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the great Chinese people and their heroic People's Liberation Army's victory over the long-term oppression of imperialism and the domestic Kuomintang reactionaries, and the establishment of the people's government, the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions, on behalf of the Polish trade union members, expresses the most sincere friendship to the Chinese people, and extends warm and fraternal greetings to all the Chinese workers and comrades in the common struggle for peace and socialism. We hereby express our joy for all the economic and social achievements made by the Chinese working masses, and wish you more glorious achievements under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party headed by Mao Zedong.
    Long live the international solidarity and fraternal friendship of the working masses in the struggle for peace under the leadership of the great peace standard-bearer Stalin!
        Kołoszyński, Chairman of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions
    Telegram from Norovsambuu,
    Chairman of the Central Council of Mongolian Trade Unions, to Comrade Chen Yun, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions in Beijing:
    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Mongolian People's Republic and all trade union members, I extend to you, and through you, to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the friendly Chinese people, the most sincere congratulations. All trade union members and all the people of the Mongolian People's Republic are sincerely happy for the great achievements made by the great Chinese people under the leadership of the glorious Communist Party and the beloved leader Comrade Mao Zedong. On this memorable day for the Chinese people, Mongolian trade union members and the Mongolian people wholeheartedly congratulate the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Chinese people on new achievements in rebuilding the country, making the country prosperous, and wish them success in the struggle to strengthen the camp of peace headed by the great Soviet Union.
        Norovsambuu, Chairman of the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Mongolian People's Republic
    Telegram from Noss,
    Chairman of the Textile Workers' International, to the Chinese Textile Trade Union:
    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Italian textile workers extend friendly congratulations to you and hope you achieve the greatest success in peacefully building the People's China. The textile trade unions of various countries under the leadership of the Textile Workers' International will join you in strengthening the front of the struggle for peace.
    Noss, Chairman of the Textile Workers' International
            Secretary Fiette
    Telegram from Matti Sannel,
    Chairman of the Finnish Textile Workers' Union, to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions in Beijing:
    Dear Comrades, two years ago, on October 1st, your great work – liberating the people from imperialist oppression – succeeded. On the occasion of this great and solemn day, please accept our tribute to you and all the heroic Chinese people who are building a happy new life and defending lasting world peace.
            Matti Sannel, Chairman of the Finnish Textile Workers' Union

### 我们的朋友遍于全世界！——各国人民电贺我国国庆

1951-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我们的朋友遍于全世界！
    ——各国人民电贺我国国庆
    意大利共产党中央委员会
    总书记托里亚蒂贺电
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席：
    值此中华人民共和国成立二周年之际，意大利共产党人和民主人士谨向伟大的中华人民共和国和您——这个共和国的领袖和缔造者，致以热烈的敬礼。
    祝中国人民和英勇的中国共产党在建设新生活的事业中，和在反对帝国主义侵略、保卫亚洲的自由、独立、和平的斗争中，获得日新月异的成功。
            意大利共产党中央委员会总书记
              巴米罗·托里亚蒂
    保加利亚总工会主席
    普拉霍夫贺电北京中华全国总工会：
    欣逢中华人民共和国成立两周年纪念日，保加利亚总工会中央理事会代表保加利亚的工人职员向中国工人阶级及全体中国劳动人民致以热烈的战斗的敬礼。中国的工人阶级和中国人民正在中国共产党和英明的导师毛泽东领导下，建设着民主的新中国；并且正进行着斗争，反对美、英侵略台湾和朝鲜，反对重新武装日本，争取在亚洲和全世界奠定和平。保加利亚工人和职员谨预祝英勇的中国工人阶级及全体中国人民获得更大的成就。以伟大的苏联及和平旗手斯大林为首的世界和平、民主、社会主义阵营万岁！
        保加利亚总工会中央理事会主席普拉霍夫
    波兰工会中央理事会主席
    柯罗西维兹贺电北京中华全国总工会：
    欣逢伟大的中国人民及其英勇的人民解放军战胜长期的帝国主义和国内国民党反动派压迫、建立人民政权的两周年纪念日，波兰工会中央理事会代表波兰工会会员们向中国人民表示最诚挚的友谊，并向为和平为社会主义的共同斗争中的全体中国工人和同志们，致以热烈的兄弟的贺意。我们谨在此对中国劳动群众获得的一切经济和社会成就表示欢欣，并祝你们在以毛泽东为首的中国共产党领导下获得更多的光辉的成就。
    劳动群众在伟大的和平旗手斯大林领导下的和平斗争中的国际团结和兄弟友谊万岁！
        波兰工会中央理事会主席柯罗西维兹
    蒙古工会中央理事会主席
    诺罗夫萨布贺电北京中华全国总工会主席陈云同志：
    欣逢中华人民共和国建国两周年纪念日，谨代表蒙古人民共和国工会中央理事会及全体工会会员向您，通过您并向中华全国总工会及友好的中国人民致以最真诚的祝贺。蒙古人民共和国的全体工会会员和全体人民真诚地因为伟大的中国人民在光荣的共产党和敬爱的领袖毛泽东同志领导下所获得的重大成就而感到高兴。在中国人民的这个值得纪念的节日，蒙古工会会员和蒙古人民全心祝贺中华全国总工会及中国人民获得新的成就，以重建国家，使国家繁荣，并祝在加强以伟大苏联为首的和平阵营的斗争中获得成就。
        蒙古人民共和国工会中央理事会主席
                                    诺罗夫萨布
    纺织工会国际主席诺斯贺电中国纺织工会：
    际此中华人民共和国成立二周年纪念日，意大利纺织工人向你们致以友谊的祝贺，并望你们在和平建设人民中国中获得最大的成功。在纺织工会国际所领导下的各国纺织工会，将与你们一道加强和平斗争的阵线。
    纺织工会国际主席诺斯
            书记斐约特
    芬兰纺织工人工会主席
    马蒂·森乃尔贺电北京中华全国总工会：
    亲爱的同志们，两年以前的十月一日，你们的伟大工作——从帝国主义压迫下解放人民——成功了。欣逢这个伟大庄严的日子，请接受我们向你们以及全体英勇的正在建设幸福的新生活、保卫世界持久和平的中国人民致敬。
            芬兰纺织工人工会主席马蒂·森乃尔
