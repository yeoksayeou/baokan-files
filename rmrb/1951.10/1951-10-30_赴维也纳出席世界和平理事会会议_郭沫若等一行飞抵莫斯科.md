Title: Guo Moruo and Entourage Fly to Moscow En Route to Vienna for World Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-10-30
Page: 1

    Going to Vienna to attend the World Peace Council meeting
    Guo Moruo and Entourage Fly to Moscow
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 29th] Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation to the Vienna meeting of the World Peace Council, and council members Ma Yinchu, Zhang Bojun, Wu Yaozong, and others, a group of nine people, arrived in Moscow by plane on the 28th. Kotov, Executive Secretary of the Soviet Peace Committee, and committee members Kabalevsky and Rossisky were among those who welcomed them at the airport. Ge Baoquan, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 赴维也纳出席世界和平理事会会议  郭沫若等一行飞抵莫斯科

1951-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    赴维也纳出席世界和平理事会会议
    郭沫若等一行飞抵莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科二十九日电】出席世界和平理事会维也纳会议的中国代表团团长郭沫若、理事马寅初、章伯钧、吴耀宗等一行九人，在二十八日乘飞机抵达莫斯科。到机场欢迎的有苏联拥护和平委员会执行书记科托夫、委员卡巴列夫斯基和罗西斯基等人。我国驻苏大使馆参赞戈宝权也到机场欢迎。
