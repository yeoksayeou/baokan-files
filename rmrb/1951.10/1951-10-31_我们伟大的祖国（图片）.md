Our Great Motherland

The people of Shaoshan Village, Xiangtan, Hunan Province, the birthplace of Chairman Mao, carried out land reform last year, which greatly increased their enthusiasm for agricultural and handicraft production and resulted in good harvests. This is the scene of the bumper harvest of farmers in Shaoshan Village this year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    毛主席的故乡——湖南湘潭韶山村的人民，去年实行了土批改革，大大提高了在农业和手工业生产中的积极性，得到了良好的收获。这是韶山村农民今年丰收的情形。
