Title: Soviet People's Observation Delegation Leaves Beijing for Moscow Yesterday; Delegation Leader Solovyov Issues Statement
Author:
Date: 1951-10-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 20th: The Soviet People's Observation Delegation, invited to participate in our country's National Day celebrations, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to the Soviet Union. Those who saw them off at the airport included: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; Qian Junrui, Director-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Zhao Zhongchi, Deputy Director-General; Li Lisan and Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; and Ai Qing, representative of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles. Soviet Ambassador Roshchin also saw them off at the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency, October 20th: Before leaving Beijing to return to the Soviet Union, Solovyov, the leader of the Soviet People's Observation Delegation, issued the following written statement:
    "We are about to set off for home, and we will take back to our country the warmest brotherly feelings of the Chinese people towards the Soviet working people.
    We are convinced that the working people of the People's Republic of China are loyal to the Communist Party and infinitely love their long-tested leader, Comrade Mao Zedong. We are also convinced that the Chinese people have brotherly love for our Soviet people and for the great Stalin. For the sake of strengthening world peace, we wish that the brotherly friendship between the peoples of our two countries will be further strengthened and that our two countries will become increasingly prosperous.
    We warmly hope that the great Chinese people will achieve further success in the construction of a new democratic China."

### 苏联人民观礼团昨离京返莫斯科  团长索洛维耶夫发表谈话

1951-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联人民观礼团昨离京返莫斯科
    团长索洛维耶夫发表谈话
    【新华社二十日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的苏联人民观礼代表团，在今天上午乘飞机离北京返苏。到机场欢送的有：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若、秘书长刘贯一，中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞、副总干事赵仲池，中华全国总工会副主席李立三、刘宁一，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会秘书长荣高棠，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际联络部副部长廖梦醒，中华全国文学艺术界联合会代表艾青等人。苏联大使罗申也到机场欢送。
    【新华社二十日讯】苏联人民观礼代表团团长索洛维耶夫在离京返苏前夕发表书面谈话如下：
    “我们即将启程回国，我们将把中国人民对苏联劳动人民最热诚的兄弟感情带回国去。
    我们确信：中华人民共和国劳动人民忠于共产党，无限热爱自己久经考验的领袖毛泽东同志。我们也确信：中国人民对我们苏联人民、对伟大的斯大林怀着兄弟般的热爱。为了世界和平的加强，谨祝我们两国人民间兄弟友好的情谊进一步加强，我们两国日益繁昌。
    我们热烈希望伟大的中国人民在新的民主中国的建设事业中获得进一步的成就。”
