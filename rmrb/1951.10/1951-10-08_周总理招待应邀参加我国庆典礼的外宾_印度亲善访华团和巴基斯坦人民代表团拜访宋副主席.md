Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts Foreign Guests Invited to Attend China's National Day Celebrations; Indian Goodwill Mission and Pakistan People's Delegation Visit Vice-Chairman Song

Author:
Date: 1951-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government Administrative Council held a tea party today to host foreign guests invited to attend China's National Day celebrations. The foreign guests invited to attend were: specially invited foreign guests Soviet writer Ilya Ehrenburg, Chilean poet Pablo Neruda, the Soviet People's Observation Delegation headed by Soloviev, the Bulgarian People's Observation Delegation headed by Georgi Karaslavov, the Romanian People's Observation Delegation headed by Ileana Răceanu, the Hungarian People's Observation Delegation headed by István Vas, Li Tae-jun, a member of the Korean People's Observation Delegation remaining in Beijing, the Czechoslovak People's Observation Delegation headed by Gusta Fučíková, the Polish People's Observation Delegation headed by Jerzy Putrament, the Mongolian People's Observation Delegation headed by Tsendiin Damdinsüren, the German Democratic Republic People's Observation Delegation headed by Dietter, the Vietnamese People's Observation Delegation headed by Tôn Đức Thắng, the Indian Goodwill Mission to China headed by Sundarlal, the Burmese People's Observation Delegation headed by Deakin Kotomai, the Indonesian People's Observation Delegation headed by Tabrani, and the Pakistani People's Observation Delegation headed by Mian Iftikharuddin. Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government, Vice Premiers of the Administrative Council Dong Biwu and Guo Moruo, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Wang Jiaxiang, Zhang Hanfu, and Wu Xiuquan, Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government Qi Yanming, and Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies Yu Xinqing were present as companions. The tea party proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Indian Goodwill Mission to China and the Pakistan People's Observation Delegation, invited to attend China's National Day celebrations, paid respects to Vice-Chairman Song Qingling of the Central People's Government this afternoon. Vice-Chairman Song hosted a tea party.

### 周总理招待应邀参加我国庆典礼的外宾  印度亲善访华团和巴基斯坦人民代表团拜访宋副主席

1951-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理招待应邀参加我国庆典礼的外宾
    印度亲善访华团和巴基斯坦人民代表团拜访宋副主席
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理在今天举行茶会，招待应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的外宾。应邀出席的外宾有：特邀外宾苏联作家爱伦堡，智利诗人聂鲁达，以索洛维耶夫为首的苏联人民观礼代表团，以乔治·卡拉斯拉渥夫为首的保加利亚人民观礼代表团，以伊丽安娜·拉契亚努为首的罗马尼亚人民观礼代表团，以伊斯特万尼·华斯为首的匈牙利人民观礼代表团，留在北京的朝鲜人民观礼代表团团员李泰俊，以古丝妲·伏契科娃为首的捷克斯洛伐克人民观礼代表团，以耶齐·普特拉曼特为首的波兰人民观礼代表团，以策·达木丁苏隆为首的蒙古人民观礼代表团，以狄特为首的德意志民主共和国人民观礼代表团，以孙德胜为首的越南人民观礼代表团，以森德拉尔为首的印度亲善访华团，以德钦哥都迈为首的缅甸人民观礼代表团，以塔勃兰尼为首的印尼人民观礼代表团和米安·伊夫提卡鲁丁为首的巴基斯坦人民观礼代表团。出席作陪的有：中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，政务院副总理董必武、郭沫若，外交部副部长王稼祥、章汉夫、伍修权，中央人民政府办公厅主任齐燕铭，典礼局局长余心清。茶会在友好气氛中进行。
    【新华社七日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆典的印度亲善访华团和巴基斯坦人民观礼代表团，今天下午拜访中央人民政府宋庆龄副主席致敬意。宋副主席设茶会招待。
