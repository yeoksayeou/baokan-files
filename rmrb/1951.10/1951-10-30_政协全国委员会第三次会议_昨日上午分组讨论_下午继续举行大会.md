Title: The Third Session of the National Committee of the CPPCC Held Group Discussions Yesterday Morning; Continued General Assembly in the Afternoon

Author:
Date: 1951-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: The Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) continued its group discussions this morning and held a general assembly this afternoon. Those who made special reports and speeches at today's general assembly included Chen Yi, member of the East China Military and Political Committee; Du Ping, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army; Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government; Zhang Yun, Chairman of the East China Democratic Women's Federation; agricultural labor model Li Shunda; industrial labor model Guo Shude; Burhan (Uyghur), Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government; Cui Yuzhen, representative of old revolutionary base areas; steel engineer Wang Zhixi; Zhu Dehai (Korean), member of the National Committee of the CPPCC; Xiong Kewu, Vice Chairman of the Southwest Military and Political Committee; and Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the Central People's Government.

The meeting adjourned at 7:15 PM and will continue tomorrow.

### 政协全国委员会第三次会议  昨日上午分组讨论  下午继续举行大会

1951-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会第三次会议
    昨日上午分组讨论
    下午继续举行大会
    【新华社二十九日讯】中国人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议，今日上午继续分组讨论，下午举行大会。在今日的大会上作专题报告和发言的，有华东军政委员会委员陈毅，中国人民志愿军政治部副主任杜平，中央人民政府农业部部长李书城，华东民主妇女联合会主席章蕴，农业劳动模范李顺达，工业劳动模范郭树德，新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉（维吾尔族），老根据地代表崔玉振，钢铁工程师王之玺，人民政协全国委员会委员朱德海（朝鲜族），西南军政委员会副主席熊克武，中央人民政府卫生部部长李德全。
    会议于七时一刻休会，明日将继续进行。
