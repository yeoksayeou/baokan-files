Title: List of the Korean People's Delegation for the National Day Observance

Author:

Date: 1951-10-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The following is the list of the Korean People's Delegation invited to attend our National Day celebration:

    Head of the Delegation: Hyun Heun, Chairman of the Korean Professional Federation.
    Members: Cho Bok-rye, Vice-Chairman of the Korean Democratic Women's League.
    Lee Tae-joon, Vice-Chairman of the Korean Literary and Art General League.
    Jeong Seong-yeon, Standing Committee Member of the Korean National Committee for the Support of Peace.
    Kim Bong-ho (Youth Representative), Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
    Lim Seong-hak, Secretary of the Delegation, Chief of the Editorial Bureau of Democratic Korea Newspaper.

### 朝鲜人民观礼代表团名单

1951-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民观礼代表团名单
    【新华社三十日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的朝鲜人民观礼代表团名单如下：
    团长：玄勋，朝鲜职业总同盟委员长。
    团员：赵福礼，朝鲜民主妇女同盟副委员长。
    李泰俊，朝鲜文学艺术总同盟副委员长。
    郑圣彦，朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会常任委员。
    金凤浩（青年代表），朝鲜民主主义人民共和国英雄。
    林圣鹤，代表团秘书，民主朝鲜报编辑局长。
