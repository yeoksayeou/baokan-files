Title: Third Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between Korea and the Soviet Union: Stalin and Kim Il Sung Exchange Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1951-10-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th: According to TASS Moscow, 20th: October 12 marked the third anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic and economic cooperation relations between Korea and the Soviet Union. Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Stalin, and Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Il Sung, exchanged congratulatory telegrams.

    Marshal Stalin's telegram to Premier Kim Il Sung reads as follows: To Comrade Kim Il Sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Pyongyang. Comrade Premier:

    Please accept the thanks of the Soviet government and myself for your congratulations on the third anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between our two countries.

    I wish the brave Korean people victory in their heroic struggle to defend the freedom and independence of their motherland. Yo. Stalin.

    Premier Kim Il Sung's telegram to Marshal Stalin reads as follows: To Marshal Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, Moscow Kremlin. Respected Marshal Stalin:

    On this third anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic and economic relations between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, please allow me, on behalf of the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the entire Korean people, and myself, to extend warm congratulations and express deep respect to the government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, to the entire Soviet people, and to their brilliant leader - you, dear Joseph Vissarionovich.

    Since the establishment of diplomatic and economic relations between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, thanks to the friendly relations between our two countries and the selfless assistance of the Soviet Union, our young republic has developed and consolidated, becoming one of the countries fighting for its own freedom and independence, and for world peace.

    The close friendship between the peoples of the Soviet Union and Korea, and the assistance and support given to us by the Soviet Union, are the reliable guarantee of our victory in the just war against the American interventionists and the traitor Syngman Rhee, for the freedom and independence of our motherland.

    The Korean people have completely thwarted the US interventionists' aggressive plans in Korea, and today they are strengthening the friendship between the peoples of the Soviet Union and Korea, and are fully determined to safeguard the unification and independence of their motherland.

    For the benefit and happiness of all progressive mankind, and for lasting world peace, Marshal, dear Joseph Vissarionovich, I wish you longevity. Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Kim Il Sung

### 朝鲜和苏联建立外交关系三周年  斯大林、金日成互致贺电

1951-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜和苏联建立外交关系三周年
    斯大林、金日成互致贺电
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：十月十二日为朝鲜和苏联建立外交与经济合作关系三周年纪念日。苏联部长会议主席斯大林和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成互致贺电。
    斯大林大元帅致金日成首相的电文如下：平壤朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成同志。首相同志：
    请接受苏联政府和我本人的谢意，感谢你在我们两国建立外交关系三周年的祝贺。
    祝勇敢的朝鲜人民在保卫他们祖国的自由和独立的英勇斗争中得到胜利。约·斯大林
    金日成首相致斯大林大元帅的电文如下：莫斯科克里姆林苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林大元帅敬爱的斯大林大元帅：
    在这朝鲜民主主义人民共和国与苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟建立外交及经济关系三周年的时候，请允许我代表朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府、全体朝鲜人民以及我本人，向苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府，向全体苏联人民以及他们天才的领袖——您，亲爱的约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇，致热诚的祝贺，并表示深切的敬意。
    自朝鲜民主主义人民共和国与苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟建立外交和经济关系以后，由于我们两国间的友好关系以及苏联的无私的援助，我们年青的共和国获得了发展和巩固，成了为自身的自由与独立，为世界和平而斗争的国家之一。
    苏联和朝鲜两国人民间的亲密友谊，苏联给我们的援助和支持，是我们反对美国干涉者和李承晚卖国贼、为了我们祖国的自由和独立的正义战争取得胜利的可靠保证。
    朝鲜人民已经完全挫败了美国干涉者在朝鲜的侵略计划，他们今天加强苏联和朝鲜两国人民的友谊，并且完全有决心维护他们祖国的统一和独立。
    为了全体进步人类的利益和幸福，为了世界持久和平，大元帅、亲爱的约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇，我祝你万寿无疆。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相  金日成
