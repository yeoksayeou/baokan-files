Title: List of Czechoslovak People's Delegation for National Day Observation

Author:

Date: 1951-10-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The list of the Czechoslovak People's Delegation invited to participate in our National Day celebrations is as follows:

    Gustava Fučíková, representative of the Czechoslovak Peace Committee and the Czechoslovak Women's Federation.

    Svoboda, representative of the Czechoslovak Trade Union and Youth League.

### 捷克斯洛伐克人民观礼代表团名单

1951-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克人民观礼代表团名单
    【新华社三十日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆典的捷克斯洛伐克人民观礼代表团名单如下：
    古丝妲·伏契科娃，捷克斯洛伐克和平委员会及捷克斯洛伐克妇女联合会代表。
    斯沃波达，捷克斯洛伐克工会和青年联盟代表。
