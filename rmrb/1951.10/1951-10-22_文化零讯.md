Cultural Snippets
    ▲ The Capital and Dahua Cinemas in Beijing will hold a "Chinese People's Films Win International Awards Exhibition" from the 20th, for a total of eight days. The films include: "The Liberated China," "The White Haired Girl," "Steel Fighter," "A Million Soldiers Cross the Yangtze River," "Daughters of China," "The Three Years of Liberation War in Northeast China," "Zhao Yiman," "New Children of Heroes," "Triumphant Song of the Great Southwest," "Embarking on a Path of Life," "Great Unity of Chinese Nationalities," "Red Flag Sweeps the West Wind," "The Victory of the Chinese People," and thirteen other films.
    ▲ On June 18th of this year, the American Society of Civil Engineers sent a letter proposing to award its Mosseiff Award to Qian Lingxi, a Chinese civil engineering expert and professor at Zhejiang University. Professor Qian Lingxi replied on September 3rd, refusing to accept the award.

### 文化零讯

1951-10-22
第3版()
专栏：文化零讯

    文化零讯
    ▲北京市首都、大华两影院，自二十日起，举行“中国人民电影荣获国际奖影片展览”，映期共八天。影片有：“解放了的中国”、“白毛女”、“钢铁战士”、“百万雄师下江南”、“中华女儿”、“东北三年解放战争”、“赵一曼”、“新儿女英雄传”、“大西南凯歌”、“踏上生路”、“中国民族大团结”、“红旗漫卷西风”、“中国人民的胜利”等十三部。
    ▲今年六月十八日，美国土木学会来信，拟将该会的莫西夫奖金授予我国土木工程学专家、浙江大学教授钱令希。钱令希教授已于九月三日回信，拒绝接受该项奖金。
