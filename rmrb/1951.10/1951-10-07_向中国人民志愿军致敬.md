Title: Saluting the Chinese People's Volunteer Army
Author: 
Date: 1951-10-07
Page: 4

    Saluting the Chinese People's Volunteer Army
    The vanguard unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Tibet sent a telegram of salute to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army fighting on the Korean front during the Second National Day. The full text of the telegram is as follows: To all commanders and fighters of the heroic Chinese People's Volunteer Army:
    On the occasion of celebrating the second anniversary of the National Day, all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Lhasa first extend our highest respect to you heroes who are fighting hard against the American imperialist aggressor bandits on the Korean battlefield to defend the motherland and world peace, and have achieved great victories.
    Your heroic spirit of loving peace and the motherland has increased our courage and confidence in overcoming difficulties. Now we express our respect and support to you with practical actions to resolutely and thoroughly drive the forces of imperialist aggression out of Tibet and consolidate national defense.
    All commanders and fighters of the vanguard unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in Tibet

### 向中国人民志愿军致敬

1951-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    向中国人民志愿军致敬
    进驻西藏的中国人民解放军先遣部队于第二届国庆节致电在朝鲜前线作战的中国人民志愿军致敬，该电全文如下：英勇的中国人民志愿军全体指战员们：
    我们进驻拉萨的人民解放军全体指战员，当此庆祝国庆节二周年的时候，首先向你们为保卫祖国，保卫世界和平在朝鲜战场和美帝国主义侵略强盗作艰苦斗争，并取得伟大胜利的英雄们致以崇高的敬礼。
    你们热爱和平，热爱祖国的英雄气概，使我们增加了战胜困难的勇气与信心。现在我们以坚决完成彻底驱逐帝国主义侵略势力出西藏，巩固国防任务的实际行动来表示对你们的敬意与支援。
    中国人民解放军进藏部队先遣部队全体指战员
