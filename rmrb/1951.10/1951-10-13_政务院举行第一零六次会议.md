Title: Government Administration Council Holds 106th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-10-13
Page: 1

    (Xinhua News Agency, 12th) The 106th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 12th. The meeting listened to and approved the report on the 1951 flood prevention work made by Fu Zuoyi, Deputy Commander of the Central Flood Control Headquarters, and the work report of the Central Northeast Flood Disaster Relief Delegation made by Chen Qiyuan, Deputy Head of the delegation. The meeting also decided to organize a Central Chahar-Suiyuan Drought Area Relief Delegation. In addition, the meeting passed several cases of appointments and removals, and proposed cases of appointments for approval by the Central People's Government Committee.

### 政务院举行第一零六次会议

1951-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一零六次会议
    【新华社十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百零六次政务会议于十二日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央防汛总指挥部副总指挥傅作义所作的关于一九五一年防汛工作报告和中央东北水灾慰问团副团长陈其瑗所作的该团工作报告。会议并决定组织中央察绥旱灾区慰问团。此外，会议通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
