Title: At the Invitation of the President of Czechoslovakia, Pieck Visits Prague
Author: 
Date: 1951-10-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 25th: According to TASS, German Democratic Republic President Pieck arrived in Prague on the 23rd by special train for a visit at the invitation of Czechoslovak President Gottwald. Accompanying President Pieck were Korrens, Chairman of the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany, Nuschke, Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Dodinger, Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Stanhoff, Minister of the Interior, among others. The Chairman of the Czechoslovak National Assembly, John, Czechoslovak government personnel led by Prime Minister Zápotocký, representatives of Czechoslovak mass organizations, the Soviet Chargé d'Affaires in Czechoslovakia, Klikoton, and diplomatic representatives of the People's Democratic countries in Czechoslovakia were all at the station to welcome him. Tens of thousands of Prague citizens also crowded the streets and squares, warmly welcoming President Pieck. The whole city of Prague was like a festival, with Czechoslovak, German, and Soviet national flags hanging everywhere, portraits of the leaders of the Czechoslovak and German peoples, and slogans hailing the friendship and cooperation between the Czechoslovak and German peoples.
　　President Pieck and his party visited Czechoslovak President Gottwald on the same day they arrived in Prague.

### 应捷克斯洛伐克总统邀请  皮克赴布拉格访问

1951-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    应捷克斯洛伐克总统邀请
    皮克赴布拉格访问
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：德意志民主共和国总统皮克应捷克斯洛伐克总统哥特瓦尔德的邀请，在二十三日乘专车抵布拉格访问。和皮克总统同来的有民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席科伦斯、德意志民主共和国副总理努舍克、外交部长窦丁格尔、内务部长斯丹霍夫等人。捷国民议会主席约翰、以总理萨波托斯基为首的捷政府人员、捷群众团体代表、苏联驻捷代办克利柯顿和人民民主国家驻捷的外交代表都到车站欢迎。成千成万的布拉格市民也拥挤在街头和广场上，热烈欢迎皮克总统。布拉格全市就像过节日一样，到处悬挂着捷、德、苏三国的国旗，捷德两国人民领袖的肖像和欢呼捷德人民友谊和合作的标语。
    皮克总统等一行在抵布拉格的当天就访问了捷总统哥特瓦尔德。
