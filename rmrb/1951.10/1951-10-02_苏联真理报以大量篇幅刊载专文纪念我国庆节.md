Title: Soviet Pravda Devotes Extensive Coverage to Commemorating China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1951-10-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st. The Pravda newspaper today published on its front page in a prominent headline the congratulatory telegrams from Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, to Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and from Foreign Minister Vyshinsky to Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council, on the occasion of China's National Day. The second page of the newspaper published an editorial entitled "The Great Festival of the Chinese People," and the fourth page carried Dong Biwu's article entitled "Two Years of the People's Republic of China."

Pravda also published the news of Chairman Mao's banquet held on the evening of September 30 to celebrate National Day.

### 苏联真理报以大量篇幅刊载专文纪念我国庆节

1951-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联真理报以大量篇幅刊载专文纪念我国庆节
    【新华社莫斯科一日电】真理报今日以第一版头条地位，发表苏联部长会议主席斯大林致中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席，外交部长维辛斯基致政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来的祝贺我国国庆的电文。该报的第二版发表了题为《中国人民的伟大节日》的社论，第四版刊载了董必武的题为《中华人民共和国的两年》的论文。
    真理报并登载毛主席在九月三十日晚间举行宴会欢庆国庆节的消息。
