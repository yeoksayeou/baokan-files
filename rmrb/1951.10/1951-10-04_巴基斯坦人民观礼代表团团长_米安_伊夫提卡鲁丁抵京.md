Title: Head of the Pakistani People's Observation Delegation, Mian Iftikharuddin, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-10-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd:

    Mian Iftikharuddin, the head of the Pakistani People's Observation Delegation and a journalist, who was invited to attend our National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing this afternoon due to a delay en route. Those who welcomed him at the airport included: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General, Zeng Xianzhi, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department, Mao Dun, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Fan Changjiang, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the China News Publication and Printing Trade Union, Qian Sanqiang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, Cao Jinghua, Director of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government. Ibrahim Jalees, a member of the Pakistani observation delegation who had arrived in Beijing earlier, and Mahboob Ul Alam, Secretary of the Pakistani Embassy, also welcomed him at the airport.

### 巴基斯坦人民观礼代表团团长  米安·伊夫提卡鲁丁抵京

1951-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦人民观礼代表团团长
    米安·伊夫提卡鲁丁抵京
    【新华社三日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的巴基斯坦人民观礼代表团团长、新闻工作者米安·伊夫提卡鲁丁，因中途迟滞，在今日下午抵达北京。到机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若、副主席陈叔通、秘书长刘贯一，中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长曾宪植、国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席茅盾，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，中国新闻出版印刷工会筹备委员会主任委员范长江，中华全国民主青年联合总会副主席钱三强，中苏友好协会总会理事曹靖华，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等人。先期到达北京的巴基斯坦观礼代表伊布拉希姆·杰利斯和巴基斯坦大使馆秘书马哈伯·乌尔·阿兰也到机场欢迎。
