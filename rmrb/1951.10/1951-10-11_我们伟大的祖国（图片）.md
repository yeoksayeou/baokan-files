Author:
Date: 1951-10-11

    Our Great Motherland
    The railway staff of New China take great care of passengers. This is a model passenger train of the China Changchun Railway, running between Harbin and Dalian. The female train attendant of the "Guan Chuanbi Advanced Chartered Car Group" on this train is enthusiastically helping children get off the train.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新中国的铁路工作人员对旅客照顾得很周到。这是中国长春铁路在哈尔滨到大连之间行驶的模范旅客列车。该列车的“官传弼先进包车组”的女列车员正在热情地照顾儿童下车。
