Title: People of Gaya, India, Hold Celebration Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-10-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 6th: The people of Gaya, Bihar Province, held a mass meeting on the evening of October 1st to celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Participants included prominent figures from society, representatives from local farmers' associations, the Democratic Youth Federation, student unions, progressive writers' associations, workshop assistants' unions, lawyers' associations, as well as representatives from the Communist Party and the People's Party. The meeting passed a resolution stating: "On this second National Day of the new China, we extend our congratulations to the Chinese people and the great Asian leader Comrade Mao Zedong, and we pledge to strengthen the friendship between the people of China and India."

### 印度加雅人民举行庆祝大会

1951-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    印度加雅人民举行庆祝大会
     【新华社新德里六日电】比哈尔省加雅人民在十月一日晚上开群众大会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立两周年。参加大会的有社会名流，地方农会、民主青年联合会、学生联合会、进步作家协会、车间助理联合会、律师联合会的代表，以及共产党、人民党的代表。大会通过决议说：“在这新中国的第二届国庆节，我们谨向中国人民与伟大的亚洲领袖毛泽东同志致贺，我们保证加强中印两国人民间的友谊。”
