Title: Tianjin Has Already Paid Nearly 80 Billion Yuan in Donations; National Literary and Art Circles' Donations Exceed Original Plan by More Than Double

Author:
Date: 1951-10-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 30th - People from all walks of life in Tianjin are welcoming the second National Day since the founding of New China with patriotic actions of actively paying for aircraft and artillery donations. As of the evening of the 29th, donations from all walks of life amounted to 79,764,424,291 yuan. The amount paid has exceeded half of the donation amount for 100 aircraft proposed by all sectors of the city at the meeting commemorating the sixth anniversary of the victory of the "September 3rd" Anti-Japanese War this year.
    [This Newspaper] Since the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles issued the call to donate a "Lu Xun" aircraft on June 5th, literary and art federations and groups across the country have responded enthusiastically and have made donation plans one after another. As of September 25th, 3.6 billion yuan in donations had been paid, exceeding the planned 2.1 billion yuan; enough to buy more than two fighter planes. At present, the donation movement is still being carried out enthusiastically by literary and art circles across the country.

### 天津已缴献款近八百亿元  全国文艺界捐献超过原计划一倍多

1951-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    天津已缴献款近八百亿元
    全国文艺界捐献超过原计划一倍多
    【新华社天津三十日电】天津市各界以积极缴纳飞机大炮献款的爱国行动来迎接新中国诞生以来的第二个国庆节。截至二十九日晚止，各界缴纳献款达七百九十七亿六千四百二十四万零二百九十一元，所缴款额已超过本市各界在纪念“九三”抗日战争胜利六周年大会上提出的在今年内献飞机百架捐款的半数。
    【本报讯】中华全国文学艺术界联合会自六月五日发出了捐献“鲁迅号”飞机的号召以来，全国各地文联、文艺团体都热烈响应，并纷纷订出捐献计划。截至九月二十五日止，已缴纳捐款三十六亿元整，超额完成了二十一亿；共可买战斗机两架多。目前全国各地文艺界仍热烈开展这一捐献运动。
