Title: Mongolian and British People's Observation Delegation Depart Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1951-10-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 26th: The Mongolian People's Observation Delegation and British People's Observation Delegate, Mr. D. Pritt, who were invited to participate in our National Day celebration, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their countries. Those who saw them off at the airport included: Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department; Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Ai Qing, representative of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Zhao Zhongchi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Zhou Peiyuan, and many others. Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan also went to the airport to see them off.

On the evening of the 25th, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression and other people's organizations hosted a farewell banquet for the foreign guests.

### 蒙古和英国人民观礼代表离京返国

1951-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古和英国人民观礼代表离京返国
    【新华社二十六日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的蒙古人民观礼代表团和英国人民观礼代表德里邦，在今天上午乘飞机离北京返国。到机场欢送的有：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会副秘书长吴茂荪，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，国际联络部副部长金子明，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中华全国文学艺术界联合会代表艾青，中苏友好协会总会副总干事赵仲池，北京市人民政府市长彭真和周培源等多人。蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗也到机场送行。
    二十五日晚，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会等人民团体曾设宴为外宾饯别。
