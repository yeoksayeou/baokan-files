Title: Ehrenburg and Neruda Departed Beijing for Moscow Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-10-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 9th.

    Ehrenburg and Neruda Departed Beijing for Moscow Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, 9th] Ilya Ehrenburg, a Soviet writer, and Pablo Neruda, a Chilean poet, along with their wives, who represented the Stalin International Peace Prize Committee to present the award to Ms. Soong Ching Ling and participate in China's National Day celebration, left Beijing by plane for Moscow this morning. Those who saw them off at the airport included: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Vice-Chairman Chen Shutong, Committee Member Zhang Bojun, Secretary-General Liu Guanyi, Shao Lizi and Cao Jinghua, directors of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Fan Changjiang, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the China News Publishing and Printing Trade Union, Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, Zeng Xianzhi, Deputy Secretary-General, and Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Mao Dun and Zhou Yang, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, writers Ding Ling, Ai Qing, Shakov, and Wu Han, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and others. Soviet Ambassador Roschin also came to the airport to see them off.
    On the evening of the 8th, Guo Moruo hosted a farewell banquet for Ehrenburg and Neruda, accompanied by Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, Lao She, Ouyang Yuqian, Ding Ling, Yang Hansheng and others. The hosts and guests enjoyed a very pleasant time.

### 爱伦堡、聂鲁达昨日离京飞莫斯科

1951-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    爱伦堡、聂鲁达昨日离京飞莫斯科
    【新华社九日讯】代表“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会前来我国授奖给宋庆龄先生并参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的苏联作家爱伦堡和智利诗人聂鲁达、和爱伦堡夫人、聂鲁达夫人一行四人，今天上午乘飞机离北京赴莫斯科。到机场欢送的有：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若，副主席陈叔通，委员章伯钧，秘书长刘贯一，中苏友好协会总会理事邵力子、曹靖华，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中国新闻出版印刷工会筹备委员会主任委员范长江，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会秘书长荣高棠，中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长曾宪植和国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席茅盾、周扬，作家丁玲、艾青、沙可夫，和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等人。苏联大使罗申也到机场欢送。
    八日晚，郭沫若曾设宴为爱伦堡和聂鲁达饯别，由茅盾、周扬、老舍、欧阳予倩、丁玲、阳翰笙等人作陪，宾主极为欢洽。
