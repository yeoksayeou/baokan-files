Title: People from All Circles in East China and Shanghai Gather to Welcome Delegation of Observers from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1951-10-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency:

    People from all circles in East China and Shanghai, more than two thousand in number, gathered on the evening of the 12th at the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Auditorium to welcome the people's observer delegations from various countries who came to East China for visits after participating in the celebration of our National Day. Invited to attend were: the people's observer delegations from Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Mongolia, and the German Democratic Republic; Korean writer Li Taijun, members of the Indian Goodwill Delegation to China, Anand, Habib, and Majiebo, the head of the Pakistani People's Observer Delegation, Iftikharuddin, and the British People's Observer Delegate, Delibon, and others. The conference began with a welcoming speech by Ma Yinchu, Chairman of the East China General Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression. He extended a warm welcome on behalf of all the people of East China and Shanghai to the people's observer delegations from various countries who had traveled a long way to visit East China. He said: Today's gathering powerfully demonstrates that our friends are all over the world, and our close unity will shatter all the aggressive conspiracies of American imperialism. Next, the people's representatives from various countries who were invited to attend successively delivered speeches, unanimously congratulating the great achievements of New China in the two years since its founding, and expressing their gratitude for the welcome from the people of East China and Shanghai. The people's representatives from various countries stated in their speeches that the people of all countries are willing to stand closely with the Chinese people and jointly fight against the aggression of American imperialism and defend world peace. After the meeting, a song and dance evening was held.

    Also, various people's organizations in East China and Shanghai jointly hosted a banquet on the evening of the 13th to welcome the representatives of the people's observer delegations from various countries who came to East China for visits. The Consul General of Czechoslovakia in Shanghai, Mr. and Mrs. Slimak, and the Deputy Consul of India in Shanghai, Oberoi, were invited to accompany them. The heads of various people's organizations in East China and Shanghai attended the reception. After the meeting, a musical evening was held.

    On the morning of the 13th, the representatives from various countries visited the Shanghai Workers' Cultural Palace and the China Welfare Institute Nursery, and paid their respects at the Lu Xun Tomb in Hongqiao. In the afternoon, the representatives from various countries visited the former residence of Mr. Sun Yat-sen and the Lu Xun Memorial Hall.

### 华东和上海各界人民集会欢迎各国观礼代表团

1951-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    华东和上海各界人民集会欢迎各国观礼代表团
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：华东和上海各界人民二千余人于十二日晚在上海市人民政府大礼堂集会，欢迎参加我国国庆节庆典的各国人民观礼代表前来华东参观访问。应邀出席的有：保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古和德意志民主共和国人民观礼代表团，朝鲜作家李泰俊、印度亲善访华团团员安纳德、哈比伯和马杰伯，巴基斯坦人民观礼代表团团长伊夫提卡鲁丁及英国人民观礼代表德里邦等。大会首由中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会华东总分会主席马寅初致欢迎词。他代表全华东和上海人民对远路前来华东参观访问的各国人民观礼代表表示热烈的欢迎。他说：今天的集会，有力地说明我们的朋友遍于天下，我们紧密的团结将粉碎美帝国主义的一切侵略阴谋。接着，应邀出席的各国人民代表相继致词，一致祝贺新中国成立两年来的伟大成就，并对华东和上海人民的欢迎表示感谢。各国人民代表在致词中表示，各国人民愿紧密地和中国人民站在一起，共同为反对美帝国主义的侵略、为保卫世界和平而斗争。会后，举行了歌舞晚会。
    又讯，华东和上海各人民团体十三日晚联合欢宴前来华东访问参观的各国人民观礼代表团代表。捷克驻上海总领事斯立马克夫妇、印度驻上海副领事邬倍尔等被邀作陪。华东和上海各人民团体的负责人都出席招待。会后，举行了音乐晚会。
    十三日上午，各国代表曾参观上海工人文化宫、中国福利会托儿所，并往虹桥谒鲁迅墓。下午，各国代表参观了孙中山先生故居和鲁迅纪念馆。
