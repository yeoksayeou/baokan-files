Title: Bulgarian, Hungarian and Other Countries' Observance Delegations Depart for East China

Author: 

Date: 1951-10-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: The people's observation delegations from Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Mongolia, and the German Democratic Republic who were invited to participate in our country's National Day celebrations, the Korean writer Li Taijun, the Indian goodwill visit delegation members Anad, Habib, and Majib, the head of the Pakistan People's Observation Delegation Iftikharuddin, and the British People's Observation Representative Deliban, who arrived in the capital today, a total of thirty-five people, left Beijing tonight accompanied by Sha Kefu, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, to visit East China. Liu Guan Yi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others went to the station to see them off.

### 保罗匈等国观礼代表赴华东

1951-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

     保罗匈等国观礼代表赴华东
    【新华社十日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古和德意志民主共和国人民观礼代表团，朝鲜作家李泰俊、印度亲善访华团团员安纳德、哈比伯和马杰伯，巴基斯坦人民观礼代表团团长伊夫提卡鲁丁，及今天到达首都的英国人民观礼代表德里邦等一行三十五人，在今晚离开北京，由中华全国文学艺术界联合会沙可夫秘书长陪同前往华东参观访问。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会秘书长刘贯一、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一等人均往车站送行。
