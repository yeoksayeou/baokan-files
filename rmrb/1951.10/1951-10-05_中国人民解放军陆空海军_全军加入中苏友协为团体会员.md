Title: All Forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (Army, Navy, Air Force) Join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as Group Members
Author:
Date: 1951-10-05
Page: 1

    Chinese People's Liberation Army (Army, Navy, Air Force)
    All Forces Join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as Group Members
    [Xinhua News Agency, 4th] The five million commanders, fighters, and staff members of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (Army, Navy, Air Force) have, through the application of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and with the approval of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, joined the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as group members. In order to further enhance the deep friendship between the entire army and the Soviet people and army, the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission instructed the troops on June 25th of this year to vigorously carry out propaganda work on Sino-Soviet friendship and introduce the Soviet Union, and to cooperate with the local Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in conducting propaganda and education on Sino-Soviet friendship among the broad masses of the people. In accordance with this instruction, military units at all levels across the country will send representatives to participate in the councils of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations at the corresponding level or where they are stationed, and assign specific personnel to maintain regular contact with the Friendship Association to lead the work of the branches in their respective units.

### 中国人民解放军陆空海军  全军加入中苏友协为团体会员

1951-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民解放军陆空海军
    全军加入中苏友协为团体会员
    【新华社四日讯】中国人民解放军陆空海军全体指挥员战斗员和工作人员五百万人，已由人民革命军事委员会总政治部代表申请并经中苏友好协会批准加入中苏友好协会，为团体会员。人民革命军事委员会总政治部为了更加增强全军对苏联人民和军队的深厚友谊，曾于本年六月二十五日指示部队，大力进行中苏友好和介绍苏联的宣传工作，并协同所在地中苏友好协会在广大人民群众中进行中苏友好的宣传教育。依照该项指示，全国各级部队都将派员参加同级或驻在地中苏友好协会的干事会，并责成专人和友协保持经常联系，以领导各该部队的支会工作。
