Our Great Motherland
Sugarcane is one of the native products of Guangdong, China, and it is the best raw material for sugar production. This year, the People's Government has issued a large number of loans, which has encouraged the production enthusiasm of sugarcane farmers. This is a scene of farmers in Shunde County, Guangdong Province, bundling sugarcane.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    甘蔗为我国广东的土产之一，它是制糖最好的原料。今年人民政府大量发放贷款，鼓励了蔗农的生产情绪。这是广东顺德县的农民正在收捆甘蔗的情形。
