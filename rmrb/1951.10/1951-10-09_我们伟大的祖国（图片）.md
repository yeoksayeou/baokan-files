Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-10-09
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Farmers in the newly liberated areas are working hard in production after the land reform, and the output of agricultural products has greatly increased. This is Chen Shuihuo, a farmer from Qiandong Township, Jinhua County, Zhejiang Province. He cultivated 2.4 mu of land and used to harvest only 1,170 jin of grain, but this year he harvested 1,800 jin, an increase of 60%.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新解放区的农民，在土地改革后，努力生产，农产物的产量大大增加。这是浙江金华县乾东乡的农民陈水火。他种了二亩四分地，往年只收一千一百七十斤谷子，今年却收了一千八百斤，增加了六成产量。
