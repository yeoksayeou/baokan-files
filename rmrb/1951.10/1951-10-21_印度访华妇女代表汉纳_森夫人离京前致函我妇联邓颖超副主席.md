Title: Mrs. Hanna Sen, Indian Women's Representative to China, Sends a Letter to Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Before Leaving Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1951-10-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 19th. Mrs. Hanna Sen, Chairman of the All India Women's Conference and women's representative of the Indian Goodwill Delegation to China for our National Day celebrations, sent a letter to Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, before leaving Beijing on the 10th. The letter stated: "We, the members of the Indian delegation, sincerely wish you an early realization of your ideals. We will watch the development of your nation with deep and lasting interest. We hope that in the years to come, China and India will unite more closely and dedicate themselves to the great work of realizing world peace and international friendship."

### 印度访华妇女代表汉纳·森夫人离京前致函我妇联邓颖超副主席

1951-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    印度访华妇女代表汉纳·森夫人离京前致函我妇联邓颖超副主席
    【新华社十九日讯】参加我国国庆的印度亲善访华团妇女代表全印妇女大会主席汉纳·森夫人于十日离北京前，特致函中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超。该函称：“我们印度代表团的团员们，谨以至诚，祝您们早日实现您们的理想。我们将怀着深厚和持久不渝的兴趣，注视着您们民族的发展。我们希望：在未来的岁月中，中国和印度将更加紧密地团结起来，致力于实现世界和平和国际间友好的巨大工作。”
