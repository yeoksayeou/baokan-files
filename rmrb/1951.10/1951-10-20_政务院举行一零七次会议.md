Title: Government Administration Council Holds 107th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-10-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th—The 107th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 19th. The meeting listened to and approved the report on national epidemic prevention work made by Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the Central People's Government. The meeting also approved several personnel appointment and removal cases.

### 政务院举行一零七次会议

1951-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行一零七次会议
    【新华社十九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百零七次政务会议于十九日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府卫生部李德全部长所作的全国防疫工作报告。会议并通过了任免案多起。
