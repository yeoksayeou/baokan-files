Author:
Date: 1951-10-01

Our Great Motherland
Today is the second anniversary of the founding of the great People's Republic of China. In the past two years, Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government have led the people of the whole country in developing the cause of people's revolution and national construction, and have achieved brilliant achievements. This is the gate of the Central People's Government - the Xinhua Gate of Zhongnanhai in Beijing. The ranks of young people marching in front of the Central People's Government symbolize the infinite future of the new China.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是伟大的中华人民共和国成立的第二周年。两年来，毛主席和中央人民政府领导全国人民，发展人民革命和国家建设事业，获得了辉煌的成就。这是中央人民政府的大门——北京中南海的新华门。中中央人民政府门前行进着的青年的行列，象征着新中国的前途无限。
