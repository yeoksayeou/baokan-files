Title: National Railway Labor Model Representatives Conference Concludes
Author:
Date: 1951-10-04
Page: 2

    National Railway Labor Model Representatives Conference Concludes
    The National Railway Labor Model Representatives Conference concluded on September 29th. During the conference, 32 railway labor models, including Fan Yong, Wang Jikui, and Guo Shude, gave exemplary reports to the conference. The conference also heard reports on the current political situation, railway operations, and the development of the patriotic production increase and economization movement from Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council, Vice-Director Li Fuchun of the Financial and Economic Committee, Vice-Ministers of the Ministry of Railways Lü Zhengcao and Shi Zhiren, Director of the Political Department of the Ministry of Railways Lu Ping, and Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union Li Jiebo.
    Minister of Railways Teng Daiyuan delivered the closing speech at the conference. He pointed out that this conference concentrated and exchanged the rich experience created by railway workers across the country, especially the labor models, over the past few years. If these experiences can be well implemented among all employees, it will greatly improve railway work. He called on all representatives to convey Vice-Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai's instructions, "Consolidate existing achievements and strive for greater achievements," to the broad masses of workers and turn them into practical actions.
    In their speeches at the closing ceremony, labor model representatives from various units unanimously expressed their warm response to Minister Teng's call. Jiang Fayu, a labor model from the Railway Engineering Corps of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, said: "After we return to Korea, we will definitely complete the tasks assigned to us by the people of the whole country more bravely and resolutely, ensure smooth military transportation, and not return home until we defeat American imperialism!" Nie Weijin, a Soviet labor model from the China Changchun Railway, representing all Soviet employees working on the China Changchun Railway, guaranteed to teach the advanced experience of the Soviet Union to Chinese railway employees, so that the Chinese railway will become a strong transportation network for the national defense and economic construction of the new China.
    Finally, the conference unanimously passed a message of respect to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army, a message of respect to the railway workers fighting against the U.S. and aiding Korea and the commanders and fighters of the Railway Engineering Corps of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, a letter to the railway workers of the whole country, and resolved to support the heroic struggle of the Japanese railway workers in the Matsukawa incident.
            (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国铁路劳动模范代表大会闭幕

1951-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    全国铁路劳动模范代表大会闭幕
    全国铁路劳动模范代表大会，已在九月二十九日闭幕。在会议期间，铁路劳动模范范永、王吉奎、郭树德等三十二人曾向大会作了典型报告。大会还听取了政务院总理周恩来、财政经济委员会副主任李富春、铁道部副部长吕正操、石志仁、铁道部政治部主任陆平和中国铁路工会全国委员会主席李颉伯等关于目前政治形势、铁路业务和开展爱国增产节约运动等报告。
    大会由铁道部滕代远部长致闭幕词。他指出：这次大会集中了和交流了全国铁路职工——特别是劳动模范们几年来所创造的丰富经验。这些经验如果能很好地贯彻到全体职工中去，将会使铁路工作大大提高一步。他号召全体代表要把朱德副主席和周恩来总理的指示：“巩固已有的成绩，争取更大的成绩”，传达到广大职工中去，变成实际行动。
    各单位劳动模范代表在闭幕大会讲话中，一致表示热烈响应滕部长的号召。中国人民志愿军铁道兵团劳动模范姜发玉说：“我们回到朝鲜后，一定更勇敢更坚决地完成全国人民所交给的任务，保证军运畅通，不打败美帝国主义不回国！”中长铁路苏联籍劳动模范聂维金代表全体在中长铁路工作的苏联职工，保证把苏联的先进经验教会中国铁路员工，使中国铁路成为新中国国防建设和经济建设的强大交通网。
    大会最后一致通过向中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军致敬电、向志愿抗美援朝铁路职工和中国人民志愿军铁道兵团指战员致敬电、告全国铁路职工书，并决议声援松川事件日本铁路工人的英勇斗争。
            （新华社）
