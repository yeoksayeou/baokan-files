Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-10-29
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The successful completion of the first phase of the Huai River harnessing project has transformed the age-old water woes of the Huai River into permanent water conservancy, enabling over 40 million mu of land in the Huai River basin to increase its harvest by more than 50% each year. This year, some of the abundant grain harvests from Henan and northern Anhui were transported to Shanghai in exchange for industrial products. This is a small steamer sailing in the middle reaches of the Huai River.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    根治淮河第一期工程的胜利完成，把淮河的千年水患变成了永远的水利，使得淮河流域四千万亩以上的土地，每年增加百分之五十以上的收获。今年河南、皖北等地丰收的粮食有一部分运到上海换取工业品。这是航行在淮河中游的小轮船。
