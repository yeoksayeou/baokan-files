Title: Joint Office of Liaison Officers Established

Author: 
Date: 1951-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, October 28th. According to the agreement reached by the delegations of both sides on October 25th, the liaison officers of both sides established a joint office of liaison officers at 9:00 a.m. on October 28th to ensure that both sides strictly abide by the agreement so that violations of the agreement no longer occur. At the first meeting of the joint office of liaison officers of both sides on October 28th, detailed rules for security arrangements in the conference area were specifically stipulated, and administrative issues such as communication and liaison between the two sides were resolved. The meeting ended at 9:50 a.m.

### 双方联络官联合办事处成立

1951-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    双方联络官联合办事处成立
    【新华社开城二十八日电】十月二十八日上午九时，双方联络官根据双方代表团在十月二十五日所达成的协议，成立了双方联络官联合办事处，以保证双方严格遵守协议，使违反协议的事件不再发生。十月二十八日双方联络官联合办事处的第一次会议上，具体地规定了会场区内安全安排的细则，并解决了双方之间通讯联络等事务性问题。会议在九时五十分结束。
