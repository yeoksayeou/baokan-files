Title: Learn from Lu Xun, Persist in Ideological Struggle!
Author:
Date: 1951-10-19
Page: 1

    Learn from Lu Xun, Persist in Ideological Struggle!
    Today marks the fifteenth anniversary of the death of Mr. Lu Xun, the most outstanding cultural warrior in modern China, and not long ago, on September 25th, we also celebrated the seventieth anniversary of Mr. Lu Xun's birth. The fifteen years since Mr. Lu Xun's death have been fifteen years of great change for the Chinese people, from suffering to rebirth, from slaves to masters of the country. The "cursed era" that Mr. Lu Xun lamented during his lifetime is gone forever; the bright world he longed for and pursued has been established on our land. The victorious Chinese people cannot but remember and thank this great thinker and warrior for his immortal contributions to the Chinese revolution.
    We can learn many things from the spiritual legacy left by Mr. Lu Xun, but what has the most direct guiding significance for the revolutionary warriors in China's cultural and ideological circles today? It is Mr. Lu Xun's spirit of tenacious ideological struggle. During his lifetime, Mr. Lu Xun not only persisted in carrying out the most acute struggles against the reactionary ideas of imperialists, feudalists, comprador class, and other reactionaries, but also carried out merciless criticism of various erroneous ideas of the liberal bourgeoisie and petty bourgeoisie.
    People admire Mr. Lu Xun's perseverance and courage in ideological struggle, but what exactly is the power that makes him so persevering and so courageous? It is the thought of Marxism-Leninism. When Mr. Lu Xun was merely an evolutionist, his courage was sometimes accompanied by confusion about the future. But after accepting the truth of Marxism-Leninism, he became a resolute, thorough, and unwavering ideological warrior. In his speech at the founding meeting of the League of Left-Wing Writers on March 2, 1930, he pointed out: "The struggle against the old society and the old forces must be resolute, persistent, and continuous, and attention must be paid to strength"; "The battlefront should be expanded"; "A large group of new warriors should be created"; "The united front is conditional upon a common purpose...the purpose is to serve the workers and peasants." These valuable opinions of Mr. Lu Xun are completely consistent with the struggle program of the Chinese Communist Party on the cultural and ideological front. Mr. Lu Xun himself faithfully implemented these programs and marched boldly at the forefront of the battle line.
    The ideological struggle that Mr. Lu Xun insisted on has obtained the broadest possibilities for development since the founding of the People's Republic of China. In the article "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," Comrade Mao Zedong instructed us to "educate and transform ourselves on a nationwide and large-scale basis by democratic methods, so that we can break away from the influence of internal and external reactionaries (this influence is still very large and will exist for a long time and cannot be quickly eliminated), transform the bad habits and bad ideas we have acquired from the old society, prevent ourselves from going down the wrong path pointed out by the reactionaries, and continue to move forward, towards a socialist and communist society." This fully demonstrates the significance of our task of struggle on the cultural and ideological front today after the victory of the revolution. We do not have the difficulties that Mr. Lu Xun encountered; we can freely and fully use the weapons of Marxism-Leninism, freely and fully combine with the workers and peasants, and we can freely and fully expand our battle line, gather and cultivate new warriors. At such a time, it is completely unforgivable if we relax our ideological struggle.
    In order to complete the glorious task assigned to us by Comrade Mao Zedong, we must learn from Lu Xun's tenacious fighting spirit, strengthen and consolidate the leadership of Marxist-Leninist thought in cultural and ideological work, eliminate the ideological influence of imperialism and feudalism, and conduct serious criticism of various erroneous ideas of the liberal bourgeoisie and petty bourgeoisie. Let us unite as one, dedicate all our strength, and advance towards new victories!

### 学习鲁迅，坚持思想斗争！

1951-10-19
第1版()
专栏：社论

    学习鲁迅，坚持思想斗争！
    今天是近代中国最优秀的文化战士鲁迅先生逝世的十五周年，而不久以前的九月二十五日，又是鲁迅先生的七十诞辰。鲁迅先生逝世以来的十五年，是中国人民从苦难走到新生、从奴隶变成国家主人的伟大变化的十五年。鲁迅先生在生前所疾首痛心的“可诅咒的时代”已经一去不复返了；他所想望和追求的光明的世界已经在我们的土地上建立起来了。胜利了的中国人民，不能不追念和感谢这个伟大的思想家和战士在中国革命过程中的不朽的功绩。
    我们从鲁迅先生所留下的精神遗产中可以学习的东西很多，其中对于今天我国文化思想界的革命战士们最有直接的指导作用的是什么呢？这就是鲁迅先生坚韧的思想斗争的精神。鲁迅先生在生前，不但坚持不断地对帝国主义者、封建主义者、买办阶级和其他反动分子的反动思想进行了最尖锐的斗争，而且对于自由资产阶级和小资产阶级的各种错误的思想，也进行了无情的批判。
    人们敬佩鲁迅先生在思想斗争中的毅力和勇敢，但究竟是什么力量使他这样有毅力和这样勇敢呢？这是马克思列宁主义的思想。当鲁迅先生仅仅是一个进化论者的时候，他的勇敢曾经有时候伴着对于前途的迷惑。但在接受了马克思列宁主义的真理以后，他就成了坚决的彻底的毫不怀疑的思想的战士了。他在一九三○年三月二日左翼作家联盟成立大会上的讲话中指出：“对于旧社会和旧势力的斗争，必须坚决，持久不断，并且注意实力”；“战线应该扩大”；“应当造出大群的新的战士”；“联合战线是以共同目的为必要条件的，……目的都在工农大众”。鲁迅先生这些宝贵的意见，和中国共产党在文化思想战线上的斗争纲领是完全一致的。鲁迅先生自己忠实地执行了这些纲领，大踏步地走在战线的最前列。
    鲁迅先生所坚持的思想斗争，在中华人民共和国成立以后，得到了最广大的发展的可能性。在“论人民民主专政”一文中，毛泽东同志指示我们，要“在全国范围内和全体规模上，用民主的方法，教育自己和改造自己，使自己脱离内外反动派的影响
    （这个影响现在还是很大的，并将在长时期内存在着，不能很快地消灭），改造自己从旧社会得来的坏习惯和坏思想，不使自己走入反动派指引的错误路上去，并继续前进，向着社会主义社会和共产主义社会发展”。这就充分说明在革命胜利后的今天，我们在文化思想战线上的斗争任务的重大。我们今天没有鲁迅先生所遭遇的困难了，我们可以自由地充分地使用马克思列宁主义的武器，自由地充分地和工农大众相结合了，我们可以自由地充分地扩大我们的战线，集合和培养新的战士了；在这样的时候，我们如果放松思想斗争，就是完全不可以原谅的了。
    为着完成毛泽东同志所交给我们的光荣的任务，我们必须学习鲁迅坚韧的斗争精神，在文化思想工作上加强和巩固马克思列宁主义思想的领导，肃清帝国主义、封建主义的思想影响，并且对自由资产阶级和小资产阶级的各种错误思想进行严肃的批判。让我们团结一致，献出一切力量，向着新的胜利进军吧！
