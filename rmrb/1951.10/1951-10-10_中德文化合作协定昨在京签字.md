Title: Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement Signed in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-10-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, after friendly negotiations between the representatives of both sides, was signed in Beijing on October 9, 1951. The plenipotentiary representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China who signed the agreement was Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, and the plenipotentiary representative of the German Democratic Republic was Mr. Koenig, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China with the title of Ambassador. Those who attended the signing ceremony on our side were Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Shao Quanlin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council, Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of Culture, Xu Yixin, Deputy Director of the Soviet and East European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee, and Zhao Feng, Counselor of the Cultural and Educational Committee. On the German side were Maria Rentmeister, Robert Havemann, Gulte Maechtig, and others, members of the Cultural Delegation of the German Democratic Republic. (Attached photo)
    When the signing ceremony of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement was held, Shen Yanbing (right), Minister and Plenipotentiary Representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Ambassador Koenig (left), Head of the Delegation and Plenipotentiary Representative of the German Democratic Republic, signed the agreement.

### 中德文化合作协定昨在京签字

1951-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中德文化合作协定昨在京签字
    【新华社九日讯】中德文化合作协定，经过双方代表友好的谈判，已于一九五一年十月九日在北京签字。协定签字者，中华人民共和国中央人民政府全权代表为中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，德意志民主共和国全权代表为德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希。参加签字仪式者，我方有中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若，外交部副部长伍修权，政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长邵荃麟，文化部副部长周扬，外交部苏联东欧司副司长徐以新，文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，文化教育委员会参事赵沨等。德方有德意志民主共和国文化代表团团员马利亚·伦特迈斯特夫人，罗伯特·哈福曼，古尔特·麦契希等。（附图片）
    在举行中德文化合作协定签字仪式时，中华人民共和国中央人民政府全权代表沈雁冰部长（右）与德意志民主共和国全权代表柯尼希大使衔团长（左）在协定上签字。
