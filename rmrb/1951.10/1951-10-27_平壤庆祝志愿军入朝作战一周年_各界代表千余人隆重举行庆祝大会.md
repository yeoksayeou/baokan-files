Title: Pyongyang Celebrates the First Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's Entry into Korea; More Than 1,000 Representatives from All Walks of Life Hold Grand Celebration Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-10-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 26th: To commemorate the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's entry into Korea, Pyongyang held a grand celebration meeting here on the evening of the 24th. Participants included Korean Deputy Prime Ministers Park Heon-young and Hong Myung-hee, cabinet ministers, Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Secretary Park Jung-ae, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chondoist Chongu Party Kim Dal-hyun, and other leaders of the Republic's government and democratic parties, social organizations, as well as high-ranking generals of the People's Army, Heroes of the Republic, workers, farmers, staff, women, youth, cultural workers, and other representatives from all walks of life, totaling more than 1,000 people. Chinese Ambassador to Korea Ni Zhiliang, representatives of the Soviet Union and other People's Democratic countries' embassies in Korea, representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army headquarters, as well as leaders, combat heroes, and meritorious individuals from various units of the Volunteer Army were also invited to attend.

    On the front of the presidium hung huge portraits of Premier Kim Il-sung, Comrade Mao Zedong, and Marshal Stalin, along with the national flags of Korea, China, and the Soviet Union, and a banner that read, "Victory and Honor to the Heroic Chinese People's Volunteer Army." When the representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army headquarters and the leaders, combat heroes, and Ambassador Ni Zhiliang from various units were led to the presidium by Deputy Prime Minister Park Heon-young, the entire venue erupted in warm applause and cheers.

    General Kim Il, Deputy Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, delivered a report to the meeting commemorating the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's entry into Korea. On behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army, General Kim Il expressed gratitude and respect to all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, who have fought bravely and inflicted heavy blows on the American aggressor army and its accomplices over the past year, for their hard work and meritorious service. General Kim Il warmly praised the great victories achieved by the fraternal Chinese People's Volunteer Army in fighting alongside the Korean People's Army, and pointed out: At the most critical moment of the Korean people's just liberation war against American imperialist armed aggression, the fraternal Chinese People's Volunteer Army raised the banner of resisting America and aiding Korea to protect the homeland and defend the country and entered Korea to fight, effectively striking the ferocious American aggressors. This righteous act of assistance is a manifestation of the Chinese people's lofty patriotism and internationalism in resolutely fighting for their own country, for the Korean people, and for world peace under the leadership of the great Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party.

    Zhang Shunming, representative of the Korean Democratic Front for the Unification of the Fatherland, then delivered a congratulatory speech.

    The representative of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army headquarters stepped onto the stage amidst thunderous applause to speak. He first expressed gratitude on behalf of Commander Peng Dehuai and all commanders and fighters of the Volunteer Army to the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea for holding a grand celebration on the occasion of the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's participation in the war to resist America and aid Korea. He said: The Korean people have always been good friends of the Chinese people. The victory of the Chinese revolution is inseparable from the heroic struggle of the Korean revolutionary comrades. He then described the great victories achieved by the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in fighting alongside the Korean People's Army over the past year, as well as the reasons for achieving these victories. He concluded by saying: The Chinese and Korean people have always advocated peace. The Chinese People's Volunteer Army will fight alongside the Korean People's Army until the Korean issue is resolved fairly and reasonably.

    The meeting unanimously passed congratulatory telegrams to Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Kim Il-sung, and Commander Peng Dehuai of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. After the meeting, the Korean National Art Theater performed celebratory programs and screened films such as "The Victory of the Chinese People."

### 平壤庆祝志愿军入朝作战一周年  各界代表千余人隆重举行庆祝大会

1951-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤庆祝志愿军入朝作战一周年
    各界代表千余人隆重举行庆祝大会
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】平壤市各界为纪念中国人民志愿军入朝作战一周年，于二十四日晚在此间隆重地举行了庆祝大会。参加大会的，有朝鲜内阁副首相朴宪永、洪命熹、内阁各相、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会书记朴正爱、天道教青友党中央委员会委员长金达铉等共和国政府首长与各民主政党、社会团体负责人以及人民军高级将领、共和国英雄、工人、农民、职员、妇女、青年、文化工作者等各界代表共一千余人。我国驻朝大使倪志亮将军、苏联和各人民民主国家驻朝使馆代表、中国人民志愿军司令部代表以及志愿军各部队首长、战斗英雄、功臣亦应邀与会。
    大会主席台正面悬着金日成首相、毛泽东同志、斯大林大元帅的巨幅画像与朝、中、苏三国国旗，以及写着“胜利和荣誉归于英雄的中国人民志愿军”字样的横额。当中国人民志愿军司令部代表及各部队首长、战斗英雄和倪志亮大使由朴宪永副首相引上主席台时，全场响起了热烈的掌声和欢呼声。
    朝鲜人民军总政治局副局长金日将军向大会作了纪念中国人民志愿军入朝作战一周年的报告。金日将军代表朝鲜人民军全体将士，向一年来英勇作战、予美国侵略军及其帮凶军以沉重打击的劳苦功高的中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员致谢和致敬。金日将军热烈地赞扬中国人民志愿军在和朝鲜人民军并肩作战中所取得的伟大的胜利，并指出：兄弟般的中国人民志愿军在朝鲜人民反抗美帝国主义武装侵略的正义解放战争的最严重时期，高举抗美援朝保家卫国的旗帜入朝作战，有力地打击了凶恶的美国侵略者，这一正义的援助行动，是中国人民在伟大的毛泽东主席和中国共产党的领导下为自己祖国、为朝鲜人民、为世界和平而坚决斗争的崇高的爱国主义和国际主义精神的表现。
    朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线代表张顺明继起致祝辞。
    中国人民志愿军司令部代表在雷动的掌声中登台讲话。他首先代表彭德怀司令员和志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府在中国人民志愿军抗美援朝出国作战一周年纪念日举行盛大的庆祝，表示感谢。他说：朝鲜人民历来就是中国人民的好朋友。中国革命的胜利是与朝鲜革命同志的英勇斗争分不开的。他接着叙述了中国人民志愿军一年来和朝鲜人民军并肩作战所取得的伟大胜利，以及取得胜利的原因。他最后说：中、朝人民一向是主张和平的。中国人民志愿军将与朝鲜人民军并肩斗争直至朝鲜问题获得公平合理的解决为止。
    大会最后一致通过了给毛泽东主席、金日成首相和中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军的致敬电。会后由朝鲜国立艺术剧场演出庆祝节目，并放映“中国人民的胜利”等影片。
