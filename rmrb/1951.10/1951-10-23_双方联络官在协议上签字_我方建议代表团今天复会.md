Title: Liaison Officers of Both Sides Sign the Agreement; Our Side Suggests Delegation Resumes Meeting Today
Author:
Date: 1951-10-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, October 22nd: The liaison officers' meeting on the 22nd began at 10:00 AM. After the liaison officers of both sides signed the agreement on the resumption of the meeting of the delegations of both sides, our liaison officer suggested that the delegations of both sides resume their meeting at 11:00 AM on October 23rd. The opposing liaison officer agreed to report the specific date and time suggested by our side for the resumption of the delegation's meeting to his superiors, and agreed to give our side a reply on the afternoon of the 22nd. The meeting ended at 11:08 AM.

### 双方联络官在协议上签字  我方建议代表团今天复会

1951-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    双方联络官在协议上签字
    我方建议代表团今天复会
    【新华社开城二十二日电】二十二日的联络官会议，于上午十时开始。双方联络官在关于双方代表团复会事宜的协议上签字后，我方联络官建议双方代表团于十月二十三日上午十一时复会。对方联络官允将我方所提出的代表团复会的具体日期与时间的建议向其上级报告，并约定于二十二日下午给予我方答复。会议于十一时零八分结束。
