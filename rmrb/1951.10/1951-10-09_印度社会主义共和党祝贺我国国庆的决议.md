Title: Resolution of the Indian Socialist Republican Party Congratulating Our Country's National Day
Author:
Date: 1951-10-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 4th:
    Resolution of the Indian Socialist Republican Party Congratulating Our Country's National Day
    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Indian Socialist Republican Party extends warm congratulations to the Chinese government and people. The Party hopes that the people of China and India will jointly work to destroy the last remnants of imperialism in Asia and to build a socialist society.

### 印度社会主义共和党祝贺我国国庆的决议

1951-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    印度社会主义共和党祝贺我国国庆的决议
    【新华社四日讯】印度社会主义共和党祝贺我国国庆纪念的决议，全文如下：
    值此中华人民共和国二周年纪念之际，印度社会主义共和党谨向中国政府及人民致以热忱的祝贺。本党希望中国及印度人民将共同为摧毁帝国主义在亚洲的最后残余，并为建设社会主义社会而努力。
