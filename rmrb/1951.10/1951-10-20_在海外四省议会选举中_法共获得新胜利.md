Title: French Communist Party Achieves New Victory in Overseas Four Provinces Parliamentary Elections
Author:
Date: 1951-10-20
Page: 4

    In Overseas Four Provinces Parliamentary Elections
    French Communist Party Achieves New Victory
    [Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 18th] TASS, Paris, 17th: The French Ministry of Interior announced the results of the parliamentary elections in the four French overseas provinces, namely, Guadeloupe, French Guiana, Martinique, and Réunion. In these four provinces, a total of forty communists were elected as members of the provincial parliament. This means that the Communist Party gained three new seats, as it only held eleven seats in the previous provincial parliament. "L'Humanité" pointed out that the Communist Party has a high reputation among the residents of these provinces. In Martinique, the Communist Party candidates received 60% of the vote in the first round of voting.

### 在海外四省议会选举中  法共获得新胜利

1951-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    在海外四省议会选举中
    法共获得新胜利
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社巴黎十七日电：法国内政部公布法国海外四省，即瓜德罗普岛、法属圭亚那、马提尼克岛、留尼汪岛省议会选举的结果。在这四省中共产党有四十人当选为省议会议员。这就是说共产党获得了三个新的席位，因为在上届省议会中它只占十一席。《人道报》指出：共产党在这些省份的居民中有很高的威望。共产党候选人在马提尼克岛第一次投票中获得了百分之六十的选票。
