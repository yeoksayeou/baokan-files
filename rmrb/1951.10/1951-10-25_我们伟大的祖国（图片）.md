Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-10-25
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Today is the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers going abroad to fight. Over the past year, the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army have resisted the aggression of American imperialism and achieved great victories. This is a picture of the Chinese People's Volunteers crossing the Yalu River in October of last year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是中国人民志愿军出国作战一周年纪念日。一年来，中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军反抗美国帝国主义的侵略，取得了伟大的胜利。这是中国人民志愿军去年十月间渡过鸭绿江的情形。
