Title: The Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Continued Yesterday; Resolution on Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea Adopted; Unanimous Support for Chairman Mao's Instructions on the Current Tasks of the Chinese People; Approval of Premier Zhou's Report and Comrade Peng Zhen's Report on Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea
Author: 
Date: 1951-10-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 24th: The Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference continued today. In the morning, group discussions were held on Premier Zhou Enlai's political report. In the afternoon, a plenary meeting was held to hear the work report of Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and the report of Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea, on the movement to resist U.S. aggression, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country. Today's meeting unanimously adopted the draft resolution on the work of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, proposed by Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the National Committee, on behalf of the Standing Committee, based on Peng Zhen's report. It also unanimously adopted a congratulatory telegram to the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army, celebrating the first anniversary of the joint combat of the Chinese and Korean people's armies against American aggression. Before the adoption of the resolution and telegram, the following spoke: Li Yunhan, representative of combat heroes of the Chinese People's Volunteers; Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Zhang Bojun, Secretary-General of the China Democratic League; Huang Yanpei, convener of the China National Democratic Construction Association; Ma Yinchu, representative of non-party democratic人士 of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Han En, representative of agricultural labor models; and Wu Yaozong, representative of religious democratic人士. They unanimously expressed their support for Chairman Mao's instructions on the current tasks of the Chinese people in his opening address, and agreed with Premier Zhou Enlai's political report and the report of Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea, on resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea.
    The meeting adjourned at 6 p.m. and will continue tomorrow.

### 人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议昨继续举行  通过关于抗美援朝工作的决议  一致拥护毛主席对于中国人民当前任务的指示  同意周总理报告和彭真同志关于抗美援朝的报告

1951-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议昨继续举行
    通过关于抗美援朝工作的决议
    一致拥护毛主席对于中国人民当前任务的指示
    同意周总理报告和彭真同志关于抗美援朝的报告
    【新华社二十四日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第三次会议今日继续举行。上午分组讨论周恩来总理的政治报告，下午举行大会，听取中国人民政协全国委员会陈叔通副主席所作的该会常务委员会的工作报告和中国人民抗美援朝总会彭真副主席所作的关于抗美援朝保家卫国运动的报告。今天的会议一致通过了全国委员会郭沫若副主席代表常务委员会根据彭真的报告所提出的关于抗美援朝工作的决议草案，并一致通过了给中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军，庆祝中朝人民军队并肩作战抵抗美国侵略的一周年贺电。在通过决议和电文前发言的，有：中国人民志愿军战斗英雄代表李云汉，中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深，中国民主同盟秘书长章伯钧，民主建国会召集人黄炎培，中国人民政协无党派民主人士代表马寅初，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，农业劳动模范代表韩恩，宗教界民主人士代表吴耀宗。他们一致表示拥护毛主席在开会词中对于中国人民当前任务的指示，同意周恩来总理的政治报告和中国人民抗美援朝总会彭真副主席关于抗美援朝的报告。
    会议于下午六时休会，明日将继续进行。
