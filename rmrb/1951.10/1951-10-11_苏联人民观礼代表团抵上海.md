Title: Soviet People's Delegation Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1951-10-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 10th. The Soviet People's Delegation, invited to participate in our National Day celebrations and accompanied by Yan Baohang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, arrived in Shanghai at 9:00 AM on the 10th for a visit to East China. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Xia Yan, Director-General of the Shanghai Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Ye Yiqun, Deputy Director-General; Ma Yinchu, Chairman of the East China General Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Shu Tong, Vice Chairman; Liu Changsheng, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch; Zhang Qi, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions; Liu Daosheng, Deputy Secretary of the East China Working Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Lu Guang, Deputy Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Youth League; He Yuzhen, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Democratic Women's Federation; Ba Jin, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Wu Kejian, Secretary-General of the East China Military and Political Committee; Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government; Huang Hua, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office, and more than 30 others. V.S. Lilakhin, Deputy Consul of the Soviet Union in Shanghai, and others also went to the station to welcome them.

### 苏联人民观礼代表团抵上海

1951-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联人民观礼代表团抵上海
    【新华社上海十日电】应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的苏联人民观礼代表团，由中苏友好协会副总干事阎宝航陪同前来华东参观访问，已在十日上午九时抵达上海。前往车站欢迎的有：中苏友好协会上海分会总干事夏衍、副总干事叶以群，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会华东总分会主席马寅初、副主席舒同、上海分会主席刘长胜，上海总工会副主席张祺，中国新民主主义青年团华东工作委员会副书记刘导生，青年团上海市工作委员会副书记鲁光，上海市民主妇女联合会副主席何宇珍、上海市文学艺术界联合会副主席巴金，华东军政委员会秘书长吴克坚、上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年、外事处处长黄华等三十余人。苏联驻上海副领事魏立夏金等也到车站欢迎。
