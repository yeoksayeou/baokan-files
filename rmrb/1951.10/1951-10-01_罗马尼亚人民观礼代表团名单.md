Title: List of Romanian People's Observation Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1951-10-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The list of the Romanian People's Observation Delegation invited to attend our National Day celebration is as follows:
    Head of the delegation: Ileana Răceanu, Vice-President of the Romanian Democratic Women's Union.
    Members: Constantin Lehadatu, Vice-Chairman of the Romanian Standing Committee for the Defense of Peace.
    Traian Șelmaru, Secretary of the Writers' Union.
    Iosif Anghelko, Member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian General Confederation of Labor, Chairman of the Agricultural Workers' Union.
    Kornelia Firi Bess, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Labor Youth League.

### 罗马尼亚人民观礼代表团名单

1951-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚人民观礼代表团名单
    【新华社三十日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的罗马尼亚人民观礼代表团名单如下：
    团长：伊丽安娜·拉契亚努，罗马尼亚民主妇女联合会副主席。
    团员：康斯坦丁·列巴达土，罗马尼亚保卫和平常设委员会副主席。
    特拉扬·谢尔马鲁，作家联盟书记。
    约西夫·安节尔科，罗马尼亚总工会执行委员会委员、农业工人工会主席。
    考尔聂里亚·费里巴士，罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会政治局委员。
