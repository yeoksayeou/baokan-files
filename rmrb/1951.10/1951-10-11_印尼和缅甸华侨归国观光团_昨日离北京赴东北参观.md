Title: Indonesian and Burmese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group Leaves Beijing for Northeast China Tour Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-10-11
Page: 1

    Indonesian and Burmese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group
    Leaves Beijing for Northeast China Tour Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, October 10th] The Indonesian and Burmese Overseas Chinese visiting group left Beijing this afternoon to visit various construction projects in Northeast China. During their ten days in Beijing, the visiting group participated in the National Day celebrations, visited factories, nurseries, hospitals, schools and other cultural institutions in the capital, toured scenic spots and historical sites; and received a warm welcome and hospitality from relevant departments of the Central People's Government and various people's organizations.
    The visiting group members were deeply impressed by the great achievements of New China's construction in various fields over the past two years, and from this they saw the infinitely bright and splendid future of the motherland. Especially during the National Day ceremony, they saw the strong national defense power of the motherland, and further realized the greatness and prosperity of the motherland, and the glory of being a citizen of the People's Republic of China. They were particularly excited to see the parade of hundreds of thousands of people overflowing with enthusiasm for their beloved leader, warmly chanting "Long live Chairman Mao." A female member of the Burmese Overseas Chinese visiting group was moved to tears, saying, "Seeing such a scene, I feel that our generation is truly happy."

### 印尼和缅甸华侨归国观光团  昨日离北京赴东北参观

1951-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼和缅甸华侨归国观光团
    昨日离北京赴东北参观
    【新华社十日讯】印尼和缅甸华侨归国观光团于今日下午离北京赴东北参观各种建设。观光团在北京的十几天中，曾参加国庆节庆祝典礼，参观首都工厂、托儿所、医院、学校和其他文化机关，游览名胜古迹；并受到中央人民政府有关部门及各人民团体热烈的欢迎与招待。
    观光团团员对新中国两年来各方面建设的伟大成就，得到深刻的印象，并由此看到了祖国无限光明灿烂的远景。特别是在国庆节典礼上，他们看到了祖国强大的国防力量，进一步认识到祖国伟大强盛，和做一个中华人民共和国的人民的光荣。他们看到几十万人的游行队伍洋溢着敬爱领袖的热情，热烈欢呼“毛主席万岁”，尤其兴奋。缅甸华侨观光团的一个女团员感动得流泪，她说：“看得到这样的景象，我感到我们这一代真幸福。”
