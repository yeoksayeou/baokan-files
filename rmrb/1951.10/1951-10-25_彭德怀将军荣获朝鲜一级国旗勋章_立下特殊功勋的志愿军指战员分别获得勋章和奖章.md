Title: General Peng Dehuai Awarded the Korean First Class National Flag Order; Volunteer Army Commanders and Fighters Who Made Special Meritorious Service Separately Awarded Medals and Awards
Author:
Date: 1951-10-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 24th - The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea held a meeting on October 23rd to commemorate the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's entry into Korea. It was decided to award the First Class National Flag Order to General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, because in the Korean people's liberation war against the armed aggression of American imperialism, he used his outstanding command art to command the heroic Chinese People's Volunteer Army, giving a devastating blow to the American invading army, the common enemy of the Korean and Chinese people, and giving enormous help to the Korean People's Army.

The meeting also decided to award the First Class Order of Freedom and Independence, the Second Class National Flag Order, the Second Class Order of Freedom and Independence, the Third Class National Flag Order, the First and Second Class Soldier's Medal of Honor, and the Military Merit Medal to many commanders and combatants of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who made special meritorious services in the struggle to annihilate and mop up the invaders.

### 彭德怀将军荣获朝鲜一级国旗勋章  立下特殊功勋的志愿军指战员分别获得勋章和奖章

1951-10-25
第2版()
专栏：

    彭德怀将军荣获朝鲜一级国旗勋章
    立下特殊功勋的志愿军指战员分别获得勋章和奖章
    【新华社平壤二十四日电】朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会为纪念中国人民志愿军入朝作战一周年，在十月二十三日举行会议，决定以一级国旗勋章授予中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军，因为他在朝鲜人民反抗美帝国主义武装侵略的解放战争中，以卓越的指挥艺术指挥英雄的中国人民志愿军，予朝、中两国人民的共同敌人美国侵略军以歼灭性打击，给了朝鲜人民军以莫大的帮助。
    会议并决定对于在歼灭和扫荡侵略者的斗争中立下特殊功勋的中国人民志愿军的许多指挥员和战斗员，分别授予一级自由独立勋章、二级国旗勋章、二级自由独立勋章、三级国旗勋章、一级二级战士荣誉勋章以及军功奖章。
