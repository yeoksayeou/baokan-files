Title: Soviet People's Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-10-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Soviet People's Delegation, invited to participate in our National Day celebrations, returned to Beijing this morning after a visit to East China. They will stay in the capital for a short time before returning home. Cao Jinghua, Director of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Zhao Zhongchi, Deputy Secretary-General, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, and Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, were at the station to welcome them.

### 苏联人民观礼代表团返京

1951-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联人民观礼代表团返京
    【新华社十七日讯】应邀参加我国国庆庆祝典礼的苏联人民观礼代表团在到华东参观访问后，已在今天上午回到北京。他们将在首都稍事停留，然后归国。中苏友好协会总会理事曹靖华、副总干事赵仲池、中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会秘书长刘贯一、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一等人都到车站欢迎。
