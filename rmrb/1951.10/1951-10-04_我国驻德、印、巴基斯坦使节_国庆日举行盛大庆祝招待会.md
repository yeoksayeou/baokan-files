Title: Chinese Envoys to Germany, India, and Pakistan Hold Grand National Day Receptions
Author:
Date: 1951-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 3rd. Ji Pengfei, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Germany, held a reception on the evening of the 1st at the Ministry Council Building here to celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Among the distinguished guests attending the reception were Dieckmann, Acting President of the Democratic Republic of Germany and Speaker of the People's Chamber; Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Germany; Nuschke, Deputy Prime Minister; Dertinger, Foreign Minister; and other government officials, representatives of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, representatives of people's organizations, labor heroes, and figures from the cultural circles, totaling about 800 people. Heads of diplomatic missions of the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Hungary, Bulgaria, and other countries to the Democratic Republic of Germany, General Chuikov, Chairman of the Soviet Control Commission, and other responsible officials also attended the reception.

    During the banquet, Commander Ji Pengfei, Acting President Dieckmann, Prime Minister Grotewohl, and General Chuikov frequently raised their glasses to toast the friendship between China and Germany and between China, the Soviet Union, and Germany. The Chinese Youth Art Troupe performed artistic programs at the meeting, which were warmly welcomed. A dance party was held after the banquet.

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 3rd. To celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese Embassy in India held a grand celebration on the evening of October 1st, with over 1,000 people attending.

    Distinguished guests attending the celebration included Indian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Nehru, Home Minister Rajagopalachari, Food and Agriculture Minister Munshi, Railways and Transport Minister Ayyangar, Indian Army Chief of Staff and Commander-in-Chief General Cariappa, Chief Justice of India Kania, and several members of the Indian Parliament.

    Also present were the Soviet Ambassador to New Delhi, the Czechoslovak Chargé d'affaires, the Indonesian Ambassador, the Burmese Ambassador, the Pakistani High Commissioner, the Ceylonese High Commissioner, the Afghan Ambassador, the Nepalese Ambassador, the United Kingdom High Commissioner, and the envoys of Norway, Finland, and Switzerland, as well as the Chargé d'affaires of Sweden and other countries.

    Many famous figures and leaders of mass organizations also attended the celebration, including Dr. Saifuddin Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, former Indonesian Prime Minister Sjahrir, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, daughter of Prime Minister Nehru, representatives of the India-China Friendship Associations from all over India, representatives of overseas Chinese in India and Delhi, Chandra, Chairman of the All-India Students' Federation, famous Indian writers, artists, editors of newspapers and periodicals, representatives of mass organizations in New Delhi, and well-known entrepreneurs.

    Distinguished guests also came from provinces outside New Delhi, including writers, artists, professors, and cultural workers.

    At the meeting, guests frequently toasted Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China.

    Chinese fireworks were set off at the meeting, and the film "New Beijing" was shown, which was warmly welcomed by those present.

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 3rd. Karachi dispatch: The Chinese Embassy in Pakistan held a grand banquet at a luxury seaside hotel on the evening of October 1st to celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Among those attending the banquet were Pakistani Prime Minister Ali Khan and his wife, central ministers, the Governor of Sindh Province, ministers of Sindh Province, foreign diplomats stationed in Karachi, and prominent figures.

    After the meal, a Chinese documentary was shown to entertain the guests.

### 我国驻德、印、巴基斯坦使节  国庆日举行盛大庆祝招待会

1951-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我国驻德、印、巴基斯坦使节
    国庆日举行盛大庆祝招待会
    【新华社柏林三日电】中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国外交使团特命全权大使衔团长姬鹏飞将军一日晚在此间部长会议大厦举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立两周年。出席招待会的贵宾，有德意志民主共和国代理总统、人民议会议长狄克曼，德意志民主共和国政府总理格罗提渥、副总理努舍克、外交部长窦丁格尔及其他政府人员、德国统一社会党代表、人民团体代表、劳动英雄、文化界人士等约八百人。苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、保加利亚等国驻德意志民主共和国的外交使团团长，苏联管制委员会主席崔可夫将军及其他负责官员，都参加了招待会。
    在宴会中，姬鹏飞团长、狄克曼代理总统、格罗提渥总理和崔可夫将军频频举杯祝中德两国和中苏德三国友好。中国青年文艺工作团在会上演出文艺节目，受到热烈欢迎。宴会后举行舞会。
    【新华社新德里三日电】为庆祝中华人民共和国成立二周年，我国驻印度大使馆在十月一日晚举行盛大庆祝会，到会的有一千多人。
    参加庆祝会的贵宾有印度总理兼外交部长尼赫鲁、内政部长罗加哥巴拉卡里、粮食及农业部长穆希、铁道及运输部长阿阳加尔、印度陆军参谋总长兼陆军总司令卡里阿伯将军、印度首席法官坎尼亚、印度议会的几位议员等多人。
    到会的还有驻新德里的苏联大使、捷克斯洛伐克代办、印尼大使、缅甸大使、巴基斯坦高级专员、锡兰高级专员、阿富汗大使、尼泊尔大使、联合王国高级专员、挪威、芬兰和瑞士的使节、瑞典以及其他国家的代办。
    许多著名人物和群众团体领袖也参加了庆祝会，其中包括全印和平理事会主席赛福丁博士、前印尼总理沙利尔、尼赫鲁总理的女公子印地拉·甘地夫人、印度各地印中友好协会代表、印度和德里的华侨代表、全印学生联合会主席钱德拉、印度的著名作家、艺术家、报纸和期刊主笔、新德里的群众团体代表和著名企业家。
    贵宾也有来自新德里以外各省的作家、艺术家、教授、文化工作者等。
    会上，贵宾们频频为毛主席和中华人民共和国中央人民政府干杯。
    会上燃放了中国焰火，并放映“新北京”影片，受到到会的人的热烈欢迎。
    【新华社新德里三日电】喀喇蚩讯：中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆十月一日晚在海滨豪华旅馆举行盛大宴会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立二周年。参加宴会的有巴基斯坦总理阿里汗及其夫人、中央各部长、信德省省长、信德省各部长、各国驻喀喇蚩外交人员，以及著名人士。
    餐后放映中国纪录片招待来宾。
