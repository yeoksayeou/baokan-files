Author: 
Date: 1951-10-02
Page: 4

    "Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!"
    Publishes Comrade Dong Biwu's Essay
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 1st] The Cominform's organ, "Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!", published on September 28th, carried an essay by Comrade Dong Biwu entitled "The People's Republic of China's Two Years," and included three photographs illustrating the water conservancy projects on the Huai River, the training of new experts, and the fundamental changes in the lives of Chinese peasants after land reform.

### “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”  刊载董必武同志的论文

1951-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”
    刊载董必武同志的论文
    【新华社布拉格一日电】九月二十八日出版的共产党情报局机关刊“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”刊载了董必武同志所撰题为《中华人民共和国的两年》的论文，并登载三幅照片，说明淮河的水利工程、新专家的训练，以及土地改革后中国农民生活所起的根本变化。
