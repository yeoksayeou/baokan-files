Title: Pakistan People's Observation Delegation Sends Letter of Thanks to Premier Zhou Before Leaving Beijing; Democratic German Cultural Delegation Leaves Hangzhou for Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1951-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: Iftikharuddin, head of the Pakistan People's Observation Delegation, who was invited to attend our National Day celebrations, sent a letter to Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government before leaving Beijing on the 18th of this month. The letter said: "On behalf of the Pakistan delegation, I would like to express our most sincere gratitude for the warm hospitality of your people and for the concern shown to us by you and your people when we participated in the second anniversary of your great Republic." The letter concluded: "I leave with full confidence. The friendship between our two countries will be further consolidated by this visit. Pakistan and China will assist each other because they have many things in common and common problems."

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, October 23rd: The head of the Cultural Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, Konig, and three members of the delegation arrived in Nanjing from Hangzhou on the 23rd for a visit. When leaving Hangzhou, Liu Dan, Director of the Zhejiang Provincial Department of Culture and Education, and Chen Shouchuan, Deputy Director of the Hangzhou Municipal People's Government's Bureau of Culture, went to the station to see them off. Qi Jianqiu, Director of the Nanjing Municipal People's Government's Bureau of Culture and Education, Lai Shaoqi, Chairman of the Nanjing Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Pan Shu, President of National Nanjing University, and Zeng Zhaoju, Deputy Director of the Nanjing Museum, welcomed them at the Nanjing Railway Station.

### 巴基斯坦人民观礼代表团  离京前函谢周总理  民主德国文化代表团离杭赴南京

1951-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦人民观礼代表团
    离京前函谢周总理
    民主德国文化代表团离杭赴南京
    【新华社二十四日讯】应邀参加我国国庆典礼的巴基斯坦人民观礼代表团团长伊夫提卡鲁丁在本月十八日离北京前，曾致函我中央人民政府周恩来总理。信上说：“我谨代表巴基斯坦代表团，对贵国人民的殷勤招待以及当我们参加你们伟大的共和国第二届国庆节时您和贵国人民对于我们的关切，表示最诚挚的感谢。”该信最后说：“我带着充分的信心而离去。我们两国的友谊，将因此次的访问而益趋巩固。巴基斯坦和中国，因有许多共同点与共同的问题而将相互协助。”
    【新华社南京二十三日电】德意志民主共和国文化代表团团长柯尼希和团员三人在二十三日由杭州到南京访问。离杭时，浙江省文教厅厅长刘丹、杭州市人民政府文化局副局长陈守川等都到车站欢送。在南京车站欢迎的有南京市人民政府文教局局长齐剑秋、南京市文联主席赖少其、国立南京大学校长潘菽、南京博物院副院长曾昭橘。
