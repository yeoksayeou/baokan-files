Title: Representatives of East China and Shanghai Gather to Welcome Delegations from India and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1951-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 22nd: The Indian Goodwill Delegation to China and the people's observation delegations from Burma, Indonesia, Pakistan, and other countries, totaling twenty people, who were invited to participate in our National Day celebration, arrived in Shanghai on the afternoon of the 21st from Nanjing, accompanied by Hong Shen, Director of the Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles.  More than twenty people, including Xia Yan, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, heads of various people's organizations, Wu Kejian, Secretary-General of the East China Military and Political Committee, and Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, greeted them at the station. That evening, more than 2,000 representatives from all walks of life in East China and Shanghai gathered to welcome them. Xia Yan delivered a welcoming speech.  Afterwards, representatives from various countries delivered speeches one after another.  Sunderlal, the head of the Indian Goodwill Delegation to China, said: We firmly believe that the liberation of the Chinese people is an example for the people in all Asian and worldwide colonial and semi-colonial countries seeking liberation.  Your aspirations are the aspirations of the Indian people. We assure you that India and the Indian people will always stand with the Chinese people.  The friendship between China and India is lasting, until the liberation of all of Asia and the world, until imperialism, feudalism, and bureaucratic capitalism are completely eliminated.  Tekin Kohto Mai, the head of the Burmese People's Observation Delegation, said: We swear that the Burmese people will always cooperate with the Chinese people to oppose imperialist aggression. He said that the Burmese people resolutely oppose the American-made peace treaty with Japan.  Tabrani, the head of the Indonesian People's Observation Delegation, expressed his firm belief that under the leadership of Chairman Mao, the people of all Asia will unite closely, and the cause of defending world peace will surely achieve success and final victory.  Ibrahim Jalis, a member of the Pakistan People's Observation Delegation and a writer, said: The glorious achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteers will prevent all of Asia from being enslaved by Wall Street. We firmly believe that peace will surely triumph over war. Their speeches were constantly interrupted by applause.  After the meeting, a musical soiree was held.

### 华东和上海人民代表集会  欢迎印度等国代表团

1951-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    华东和上海人民代表集会
    欢迎印度等国代表团
   【新华社上海二十二日电】应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的印度亲善访华团和缅甸、印尼、巴基斯坦等国人民观礼代表团一行二十人，在中华全国文学艺术界联合会联络部部长洪深陪同下，于二十一日下午由南京到达上海。到车站欢迎的，有中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会上海分会副主席夏衍及各人民团体负责人和华东军政委员会秘书长吴克坚、上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年等二十多人。当晚，华东和上海各界人民代表二千多人集会欢迎。夏衍致欢迎词。接着，各国代表相继致词。印度亲善访华团团长森德拉尔说：我们深深相信：中国人民的解放，是全亚洲全世界殖民地和半殖民地国家中的人民求解放的榜样。你们的志愿就是印度人民的志愿。我们向你们保证：印度和印度人民永远和中国人民站在一起。中印间的友谊是持久的，直到全亚洲全世界的解放，直到帝国主义、封建主义、官僚资本主义被彻底消灭的时期。缅甸人民观礼代表团团长德钦哥都迈说：我们宣誓：缅甸人民永远与中国人民共同合作反对帝国主义的侵略。他说缅甸人民坚决反对美制对日和约。印尼人民观礼代表团团长塔勃兰尼表示坚信在毛主席的领导下，全亚洲人民紧密团结，保卫世界和平的事业，一定能获得成功和最后胜利。巴基斯坦人民观礼代表团团员作家伊布拉希姆·杰利斯说：中国人民志愿军的光辉战绩，将使全亚洲不再受华尔街的奴役，我们坚信：和平一定战胜战争。他们的发言不断为掌声打断。会后，举行了音乐晚会。
