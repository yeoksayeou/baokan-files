    The Brilliant Achievements of the New China in its Second Year
    (III) Land Reform Completed in Areas with Over 310 Million Agricultural Population Nationwide
    By Gu Yuan
    In the past year, land reform has been completed in areas with an agricultural population of over 150 million. Together with the previously completed areas, land reform has been completed in areas with an agricultural population of over 310 million.
    Land reform has greatly enhanced the political awareness and production enthusiasm of farmers, providing extremely important conditions for resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, suppressing counter-revolutionaries, and national construction.

### 新中国第二年的辉煌成就（图片）
古元
1951-10-04
第3版()
专栏：

    新中国第二年的辉煌成就
    （三）全国共有三亿一千万以上农业人口地区完成了土地改革
    古元  作
    一年来有一亿五千万以上农业人口地区完成土地改革，连以前完成的共有三亿一千万以上农业人口地区完成土地改革。
    土地改革使农民的政治觉悟和生产热情大大提高，为抗美援朝、镇压反革命及国家建设提供了极重要的条件。
