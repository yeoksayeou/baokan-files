Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-10-19
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Today is the 15th anniversary of the death of China's great writer, thinker, and revolutionary—Mr. Lu Xun. This is Mr. Lu Xun's former residence in Xisantiao, Gongmenkou, Beijing; after liberation, it has been renovated by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是中国伟大的文学家、思想家和革命家——鲁迅先生逝世十五周年纪念日。这是鲁迅先生生前在北京宫门口西三条的住宅；解放以后，已由中央人民政府文化部加以修葺。
