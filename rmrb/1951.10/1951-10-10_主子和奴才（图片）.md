Author: Fang Cheng Zhong Ling
Date: 1951-10-10

    Masters and Servants
    By Fang Cheng and Zhong Ling
    At the Washington and Ottawa conferences, the United States sought to pressure its Western European servants, who were already struggling, to accelerate their service to American aggression. Before the conferences, these servants had been begging the United States for handouts of dollars to maintain their precarious rule.
