Title: People's Liberation Army Delegation Attends Czechoslovak Army Day; Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Hosts Banquet; Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister Široký and Others Attend Upon Invitation
Author: 
Date: 1951-10-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 17th – Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, General Tan Xilin, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 15th to welcome the Chinese People's Liberation Army delegation attending Czechoslovak Army Day. Invited to the banquet were: Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Viliam Široký, Minister of National Defense Alexej Čepička, Minister of Culture and Information Václav Kopecký, Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of the General Staff Bohumír Procházka, as well as diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries, totaling approximately 200 people. The Chinese film "Frontier Warriors" was screened at the meeting.

    On the afternoon of the same day, Czechoslovak Deputy Minister of National Defense Procházka, on behalf of the President and Minister of National Defense of Czechoslovakia, once again presented gifts to the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese people. The gifts were received on behalf of the recipients by the Chinese People's Liberation Army delegation, including a brand new, top-of-the-line "Škoda" car for Chairman Mao, as well as two large film projectors and a large number of books.

### 人民解放军代表团参加捷陆军节  我驻捷大使设宴招待  捷副总理西罗基等应邀出席

1951-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    人民解放军代表团参加捷陆军节
    我驻捷大使设宴招待
    捷副总理西罗基等应邀出席
    【新华社布拉格十七日电】我驻捷大使谭希林将军在十五日晚设宴招待参加捷克斯洛伐克陆军节的中国人民解放军代表团。应邀出席宴会的并有：捷克斯洛伐克副总理兼外交部长西罗基，国防部长契皮兹卡，文化与宣传部长柯柏斯基，国防部副部长兼总参谋长普罗加兹卡，以及苏联和各人民民主国家外交使节等约二百人。会上曾放映我国影片《边疆战士》。
    同日下午，捷克斯洛伐克国防部副部长普罗加兹卡代表捷克斯洛伐克总统和国防部长再次向中国人民解放军和中国人民赠礼。礼物由我国人民解放军代表团代收，其中包括送给毛主席的最新式超等“斯科达”牌汽车一辆，以及大型电影放映机二座和大批书籍。
