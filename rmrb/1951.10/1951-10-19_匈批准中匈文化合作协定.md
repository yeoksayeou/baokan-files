Title: Hungary Approves Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1951-10-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 18th: The Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic approved the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, signed in Beijing on July 12th, on October 3rd.

### 匈批准中匈文化合作协定

1951-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    匈批准中匈文化合作协定
    【新华社十八日讯】布达佩斯讯：匈牙利人民共和国政府主席团已于十月三日批准七月十二日在北京签订的中匈文化合作协定。
