Title: Congratulations on the Second Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China; Marshal Stalin Cables Chairman Mao; Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky Cables Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai

Author: 
Date: 1951-10-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th: Marshal Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong, congratulating him on the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads as follows:

    Comrade Mao Zedong:

    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, please accept my friendly congratulations, Comrade Chairman. I sincerely hope that the great Chinese people, the government of the People's Republic of China, and you personally will achieve further success in the construction of a people's democratic China.

    May the great friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics—a strong guarantee of peace and security in the Far East—be forever consolidated.

            Yo. Stalin
        Moscow, September 28th

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th: Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky sent a telegram to Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the Central People's Government of China, congratulating him on China's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:

    Beijing, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China:

    Please accept my congratulations on the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and I sincerely hope that the great Chinese people and you personally will achieve further success in peaceful construction, in consolidating the indestructible friendship between our two peoples, and in the struggle for lasting peace in the world.

            A. Vyshinsky
      Moscow, September 28, 1951

### 祝贺中华人民共和国成立两周年  斯大林大元帅致电毛主席  苏联外交部长维辛斯基致电我政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来

1951-10-01
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺中华人民共和国成立两周年
    斯大林大元帅致电毛主席
    苏联外交部长维辛斯基致电我政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来
    【新华社三十日讯】苏联部长会议主席斯大林大元帅致电毛泽东主席，祝贺中华人民共和国成立两周年。电文如下：毛泽东同志：
    在中华人民共和国成立二周年纪念日，主席同志，请接受我友谊的祝贺。衷心地希望伟大的中国人民、中华人民共和国政府及您本人在人民民主中国的建设中获得进一步的成就。
    让中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟的伟大友谊——远东和平及安全的坚强保障永远巩固。
            约·斯大林
        九月二十八日于莫斯科
    【新华社三十日讯】苏联外交部长维辛斯基致电我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，祝贺我国国庆。电文如下：北京  中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来同志：
    值此中华人民共和国成立二周年之际，请接受我的祝贺，并衷心地希望伟大的中国人民和您本人在和平建设中，在巩固贵我两国人民间不可摧毁的友谊及争取全世界持久和平的斗争中获得进一步的成就。
            安·维辛斯基
      一九五一年九月二十八日于莫斯科
