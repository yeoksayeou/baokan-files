Title: Government Administration Council's Financial and Economic Committee Dispatches Basic Construction Inspection Teams
Author:
Date: 1951-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: In order to summarize the experiences and lessons of basic construction in state-owned enterprises, and to guide the future progress of basic construction projects, the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council has dispatched basic construction inspection teams to various regions to conduct inspections. The inspection teams are divided into six groups (two for railways) covering heavy industry, light industry, fuel industry, textile industry, and railways, comprising a total of 103 people. They are composed of personnel from the Financial and Economic Committee, the People's Supervisory Committee, the relevant industrial ministries, the North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Northeast Industrial Department, as well as the People's Daily and the Xinhua News Agency. The inspection is expected to last from one month to one and a half months.

The groups have departed successively since the 5th.

### 政务院财政经济委员会派出基本建设检查组

1951-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院财政经济委员会派出基本建设检查组
    【新华社九日讯】为了总结国营企业基本建设的经验教训，指导今后基本建设工程的进行，政务院财政经济委员会特派出基本建设检查组，分赴各地进行检查。检查组分重工业、轻工业、燃料工业、纺织工业、铁道等六个组（铁道两组），共一百零三人，由财政经济委员会、人民监察委员会、各主管工业部、中共中央华北局、东北工业部以及人民日报、新华通讯社等单位的人员组成。检查时间预计一月到一个半月。
    各组已自五日起陆续出发。
