Title: Representatives of Old Southern Revolutionary Base Areas Visit Tianjin; Representatives of Old Northern Revolutionary Base Areas and Agricultural and Water Conservancy Labor Models Depart Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1951-10-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: Representatives of the Old Southern Revolutionary Base Areas, invited by the Central People's Government to participate in the National Day celebrations in Beijing, left Beijing on the morning of the 14th to visit the North China Region Urban and Rural Materials Exchange Exhibition in Tianjin. Xie Juezai, Minister of the Interior Ministry of the Central People's Government, Vice Ministers Wu Xinyu and Chen Qiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Deputy Head of the Central People's Government Southern Old Revolutionary Base Areas Visiting Group, and more than 500 staff members of the agencies went to the station to see them off.
    During their stay in Beijing, the representatives participated in the National Day celebrations, toured the capital's scenic spots and historical sites, and visited the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, the North China Farm Implement Factory, and the North China Agricultural Machinery General Factory. After the visits, the representatives praised the great achievements of the motherland's production and construction in the past two years.
    The representatives also visited the Ethnic Minority Cultural Relics Exhibition, the Resist America, Aid Korea, and Defend Peace Exhibition, the History of the Communist Party of China Exhibition, and the Gift and Cultural Relics Exhibition of the Old Revolutionary Base Areas. The representatives were particularly excited and felt a sense of familiarity when visiting the Party History Exhibition and the Old Base Areas Gift and Cultural Relics Exhibition. They also provided many suggestions.
    During their stay in Beijing, the representatives exchanged reports with combat heroes of the People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, as well as industrial, agricultural, and water conservancy labor models; and held a get-together with combat heroes of the People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Beijing.
    The representatives were respected and loved by the Central People's Government and the people of all walks of life in the capital during their stay in Beijing. The representatives attended the National Day celebration banquet held by Chairman Mao on the evening of September 30th. The representatives participated in the banquet held by the Ministry of Interior of the Central People's Government and the reception jointly held by the North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the North China Military Region, and the North China Affairs Department. The representatives gave reports to staff and students of various agencies in Beijing. Their heroic deeds of struggle in the past were widely circulated throughout the capital.
     Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, October 15th: The representatives of the Old Northern Revolutionary Base Areas, national agricultural labor models, and labor models of Huai River harnessing and other water conservancy projects, who came to Tianjin to visit the North China Region Urban and Rural Materials Exchange Exhibition, left Tianjin on the 13th and 14th. The first batch of farmer representatives from Hebei, Shanxi, Pingyuan, Suiyuan, Chahar and other provinces who came to Tianjin for a visit also left Tianjin on the 11th and 12th.
    During their stay in Tianjin, the representatives and labor models, in addition to visiting the North China Region Urban and Rural Materials Exchange Exhibition, also visited more than ten public and private factories, including the State-owned Tianjin No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory, the Tianjin Automobile Assembly Factory, and the private Hengyuan Yarn Factory. The agricultural labor models and water conservancy labor models also visited the State-owned Zhangguizhuang Farm and the Haihe River Engineering Office respectively. During the few days of the visit, they saw the new face of the rapid recovery and development of industrial and agricultural production in North China in the past two years, and more deeply realized the greatness and loveliness of the motherland. They all said that they would continue to do a good job after returning, and strive for greater glory. Li Shunda, Qu Yaoli and other forty-four agricultural labor models, on the eve of their departure from Tianjin, also jointly signed a patriotic production increase pact, determined to lead the peasants to continue to deepen the Resist America, Aid Korea movement, increase the yield per unit area, and supply the country with more grain and cotton and other industrial raw materials.
    When the representatives and labor models were in Tianjin, they were warmly welcomed and entertained by the working committee of the North China Region Urban and Rural Materials Exchange Exhibition and workers, students, business circles and citizens of Tianjin. They participated in many get-togethers and forums. Hu Wenxiu, mother of martyr Liu Hulan, Dong Quanzhong, father of revolutionary martyr Dong Cunrui, Cui Yuzhen, a veteran Red Army soldier, and Lu Laotaitai, who sheltered martyr Zhao Yiman, were also invited to meet with more than 10,000 youth and women representatives in Tianjin and gave reports to everyone.

### 南方老根据地人民代表赴天津参观  北方老根据地人民代表和农业、水利劳动模范离津

1951-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    南方老根据地人民代表赴天津参观
    北方老根据地人民代表和农业、水利劳动模范离津
    【新华社十四日讯】应中央人民政府邀请来北京参加国庆典礼的南方老根据地人民代表在十四日上午离北京赴天津参观华北区城乡物资交流展览会。到车站欢送的有中央人民政府内务部部长谢觉哉，副部长武新宇、陈其瑗，中央人民政府南方老根据地访问团副团长朱学范及机关干部五百余人。
    代表们在北京期间，参加了国庆典礼，游览了首都的名胜古迹，参观了石景山钢铁厂、华北农具工厂、华北农业机械总厂。代表们参观后，纷纷赞扬两年来祖国生产建设上的伟大成就。
    代表们还参观了少数民族文物展览会、抗美援朝保卫和平展览会、中国共产党党史展览会、人民革命老根据地礼品文物展览会。代表们在参观党史展览和老根据地礼品文物展览时，感到特别兴奋和亲切。他们还提供了许多意见。
    代表们在北京期间，与解放军、志愿军的战斗英雄和工业、农业、水利劳动模范，互相作了报告；并与解放军、志愿军的战斗英雄和解放军驻北京部队举行了联欢。
    代表们在北京受到了中央人民政府和首都各界人民的尊敬和热爱。代表们出席了毛主席在九月三十日晚举行的欢庆国庆的宴会。代表们参加了中央人民政府内务部的宴会和中共中央华北局、华北军区和华北事务部联合举行的招待会。代表们在北京向各机关的工作人员和学生作了报告。他们过去英勇斗争的事迹，在首都到处传诵着。
     【新华社天津十五日电】到天津参观华北区城乡物资交流展览会的北方老根据地人民代表、全国农业劳动模范、治淮和其他水利事业的劳动模范已在十三日、十四日两天先后离开天津。河北、山西、平原、绥远、察哈尔等省第一批来天津参观的农民代表也已在十一日、十二日两天先后离天津。
    代表们和劳动模范们在天津期间，除参观了华北区城乡物资交流展览会外，还参观了国营天津棉纺二厂、天津汽车制配厂、私营恒源纱厂等十多家公私营工厂；农业劳动模范和水利劳动模范并分别参观了国营张贵庄农场和海河工程处。在几天的参观中，他们看到了两年来华北工、农业生产迅速恢复和发展的新面貌，更加深切地体会到祖国的伟大和可爱。他们一致表示：回去以后要继续做好工作，争取更大的光荣。李顺达、曲耀离等四十四位农业劳动模范，在离开天津的前夕，还共同订立了爱国增产公约，决心带动农民继续深入抗美援朝运动，增加单位面积产量，供给国家更多的粮食和棉花等工业原料。
    代表们和劳动模范们在天津的时候，受到了华北区城乡物资交流展览会工作委员会和天津市工人、学生、工商界以及市民的热烈欢迎和招待。他们参加了许多联欢会和座谈会。刘胡兰烈士的母亲胡文秀、革命先烈董存瑞的父亲董全忠、老红军战士崔玉振、掩护赵一曼烈士的吕老太太等，并应邀和天津市青年、妇女代表一万多人见面，向大家作了报告。
