Title: The Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Heard Reports on Financial and Economic and Cultural and Educational Work Yesterday; Passed the Proposal to Issue Medals for Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea to All Members of the Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1951-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: The Third Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference continued its group discussions this morning. In the afternoon, it held a plenary session to hear reports by Chen Yun, Vice Premier of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, on economic and financial work, and by Guo Moruo, Vice Premier, on cultural and educational work. The meeting unanimously passed the proposal by Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, and Peng Zhen: To commemorate the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's departure to fight abroad, to produce and distribute medals for Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea to all commanders, combatants, political workers, logistics workers, and transportation workers of our Volunteer Army.

Today's meeting adjourned at 6:00 PM. There will be a day of rest tomorrow.

### 人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议  昨听取财经及文教工作的报告  通过制发抗美援朝纪念章给志愿军全体人员的提案

1951-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议
    昨听取财经及文教工作的报告
    通过制发抗美援朝纪念章给志愿军全体人员的提案
    【新华社二十五日讯】人民政协第一届全国委员会第三次会议今日上午继续分组讨论，下午举行大会，听取中央人民政府政务院陈云副总理所作的关于经济工作和财政工作的报告，以及郭沫若副总理所作的关于文化教育工作的报告。会议一致通过了郭沫若、陈叔通、彭真的提案：为了纪念中国人民志愿军出国作战一周年，制发抗美援朝纪念章，送给我国志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员、政治工作人员、后勤工作人员和运输工作人员。
    今日会议于下午六时休会。明日将休息一天。
