Title: Grand Rallies Held in Berlin and Prague to Warmly Celebrate the Second Anniversary of Our Nation's Founding; Our Embassy in the Soviet Union Holds National Day Celebration Reception
Author:
Date: 1951-10-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 1st. The National Committee of the National Front of the German Democratic Republic held a celebration meeting in the Berlin State Opera House on the evening of September 29th to celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Chairman Dieckmann of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic attended the meeting on behalf of President Pieck. Also present were Foreign Minister Dodinger and other government leaders, social leaders, and members of various countries' diplomatic missions to Germany, totaling two thousand people. Ji Pengfei, head of the diplomatic mission of the People's Republic of China with the rank of ambassador, and all members of the mission also attended.

The meeting was opened by Professor Linser, member of the Presidium of the National Committee of the National Front and Chairman of the German People's Aid Korea Committee. Minister Ziller of the Ministry of Machine Building of the German Democratic Republic delivered a speech at the meeting. He listed many events in the past year that demonstrated the friendship between Germany and China, and pointed out: "The German people show deep friendship to the Chinese people because they know what role the Chinese people are playing in defending world peace. The Chinese People's Volunteers fighting side by side with the Korean People's Army against American aggression are particularly respected by the German people universally and without reservation." Ziller emphasized: "The victory of the Chinese people is due to the correct leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong. We German people have much to learn from China in the struggle for our motherland's national independence, democracy, and unity."

In his speech, Head Ji Pengfei thanked the National Front and the German people for their deep friendship to the Chinese people, and detailed the achievements of the People's Republic of China in politics, economy, culture, and military affairs in the two years since its founding. At the meeting, the literary and artistic troupe from Germany and China that participated in the World Youth and Student Peace Festival performed literary and artistic programs.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 30th. The Czech-Chinese Association held a celebration meeting in the Prague Industrial Hall on the 29th to celebrate our National Day. More than 4,000 representatives from all walks of life in Prague attended the meeting. Invited to attend were: Ion, Chairman of the National Assembly of Czechoslovakia, Siroky, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Sevcsik, Deputy Prime Minister, Nosik, Minister of Interior, Kopecky, Minister of Information, Jonáš, Minister of Light Industry, Procházka, Minister of Health, representatives of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Václavík, Mayor of Prague, Tan Xilin, Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, and diplomatic envoys from various friendly countries. On the rostrum of the meeting hall hung the national flags of China, Czechoslovakia, and the Soviet Union, and portraits of Chairman Mao Zedong, President Gottwald, and Marshal Stalin. At the top hung a long flag with the words: "Struggle with the Soviet Union and People's China for the victory of world peace."

The celebration meeting opened with the national anthems of Czechoslovakia, China, and the Soviet Union. First, President Pušik of the Czech-Chinese Association delivered the opening speech. Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister Siroky said in his speech: "These days, the powerful peace camp is celebrating the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China everywhere. The world significance of the Chinese people's victory further demonstrates that the People's Republic of China stands firmly in the world peace and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and is one of the powerful fortresses in the peace camp." When Siroky spoke of the great achievements of the Chinese people, the audience burst into cheers of "Long live Comrade Mao Zedong!"

Ambassador Tan Xilin then delivered a speech amidst warm applause. He spoke of the tremendous achievements made by the Chinese people in politics, economy, culture, and military affairs in the past two years.

The Chinese film "Steel Warrior," which won the "Peace Award" at the 6th International Film Festival recently held in Czechoslovakia, was shown at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st. To celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, our embassy in the Soviet Union held a commemorative reception on September 29th. Hundreds of people attended the commemorative meeting, including all members of our embassy, students studying in the Soviet Union, representatives of the Chinese youth student delegation in Moscow, and representatives of young pioneers instructors. Ambassador Zhang Wentian delivered a report at the meeting. In his report, he pointed out the great significance of the victory of the Chinese revolution, detailed the brilliant achievements of the People's Republic of China in military, political, economic, and cultural aspects in the past two years, and explained that this was the result of Chairman Mao's correct leadership, the efforts of the people of the whole country, the superiority of the people's democratic system, and the assistance of the people of the world, especially the Soviet Union. After the meeting, a film was shown and a dance party was held.

### 柏林、布拉格举行盛大集会  热烈庆祝我国建国二周年  我驻苏大使馆举行庆祝国庆纪念晚会

1951-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    柏林、布拉格举行盛大集会
    热烈庆祝我国建国二周年
    我驻苏大使馆举行庆祝国庆纪念晚会
    【新华社柏林一日电】民主德国共和国全国阵线全国委员会为庆祝中华人民共和国成立二周年，特于九月二十九日晚间在柏林国家歌剧院举行庆祝大会。德意志民主共和国人民议会主席狄克曼代表皮克总统出席大会。此外出席者有外交部长窦丁格尔及其他政府首长、社会领袖，以及各国驻德外交使团人员等两千人。中华人民共和国外交使团大使衔团长姬鹏飞及使团馆全体人员也都出席。
    大会由全国阵线全国委员会主席团委员、德国人民援朝委员会主席林塞教授致开幕词。德意志民主共和国机器制造部部长齐勒在会上发表演说，他列举一年来许多表明德中友谊的事件，并指出：“德国人民所以对中国人民表示深厚友谊，是因为他们知道中国人民在保卫世界和平方面起着怎样的作用。中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军并肩作战反对美国侵略，特别受到德国人民的普遍和毫无保留的尊敬。”齐勒强调说：“中国人民的胜利是由于毛泽东主席的正确领导得来的。我们德国人民在争取祖国的民族独立、民主与统一的斗争中，有许多要向中国学习。”
    姬鹏飞团长在致辞中感谢全国阵线和德国人民对中国人民的深厚友谊，并详细介绍了中华人民共和国成立两年来在政治、经济、文化、军事等方面的成就。会上由德国和中国参加世界青年与学生和平联欢节的文艺工作团表演文艺节目。
    【新华社布拉格三十日电】捷中协会为庆祝我国国庆，特于二十九日在布拉格工业大厅举行庆祝大会，到会者有布拉格各界代表四千多人。应邀出席的有：捷国民议会主席伊昂，副总理兼外交部长西罗基，副总理塞夫西克，内务部长诺西克，宣传部长柯柏斯基，轻工业部长裘那契，卫生部长普洛伊哈，捷共中央委员会的代表，布拉格市市长瓦西克，中国驻捷大使谭希林和各友好国家外交使节。会场主席台上悬挂着中、捷、苏三国国旗，和毛泽东主席、哥特瓦尔德总统、斯大林大元帅的画像，上端高悬着一幅长旗，上面写着：“与苏联和人民中国一道为世界和平的胜利而斗争”。
    庆祝大会在捷、中、苏三国国歌声中揭幕，首由捷中协会主席普实克致开会辞。捷副总理西罗基致辞说：“这些天来，强大的和平阵营到处都在庆祝中华人民共和国成立两周年纪念。中国人民胜利的世界意义更进一步地表明了，中华人民共和国屹立在以苏联为首的世界和平与社会主义阵营里，而且是和平阵营中的强大堡垒之一。”当西罗基谈到中国人民的伟大成就时，全场响起了“毛泽东同志万岁！”的欢呼声。
    谭希林大使接着在热烈的掌声中致辞。他谈述了中国人民两年来在政治、经济、文化、军事等方面的巨大成就。
    会上放映了最近在捷克举行的第六届国际电影赛会上荣获“和平奖”的我国影片“钢铁战士”。
    【新华社莫斯科一日电】为庆祝中华人民共和国建立两周年，我国驻苏联大使馆在九月二十九日举行纪念晚会。参加纪念会的有我国使馆全体人员、留苏学生、在莫斯科的中国青年学生代表团、少年儿童队辅导员代表团等数百人。会上张闻天大使作了报告。他在报告中指出了中国革命胜利的伟大意义，详述了中华人民共和国建立两年来在军事、政治、经济、文化各方面的辉煌成就，并说明这是毛主席领导的正确、全国人民的努力、人民民主制度的优越，以及全世界人民特别是苏联的援助的结果。会后放映电影并举行舞会。
