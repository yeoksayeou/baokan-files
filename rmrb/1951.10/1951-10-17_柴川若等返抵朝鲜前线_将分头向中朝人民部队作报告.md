Title: Chai Chuanruo and Others Return to the Korean Front; Will Separately Report to Chinese and Korean People's Forces

Author:
Date: 1951-10-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 16th -- The Chinese People's Volunteer Army returning delegation, including Chai Chuanruo, arrived back at the Korean front on the 13th. They departed from Beijing on September 22nd and gave over twenty reports to people from all walks of life in the Northeast along the way. After returning to the front, they were warmly welcomed by the commanders and fighters of the Volunteer Army. The delegates will separately report to various units of the Chinese and Korean People's forces about the great achievements of the Chinese people's movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, and the Chinese people's love and expectations for the Chinese and Korean People's forces.

### 柴川若等返抵朝鲜前线  将分头向中朝人民部队作报告

1951-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    柴川若等返抵朝鲜前线
    将分头向中朝人民部队作报告 【新华社朝鲜前线十六日电】中国人民志愿军归国代表柴川若等一行，已于十三日返抵朝鲜前线。他们于九月二十二日由北京启程，路过东北时曾向当地各界人民作了二十余次报告。他们返回前线后受到志愿军指战员们的热烈欢迎。代表们将分头到中朝人民部队各部报告我国人民抗美援朝爱国运动的伟大成就，及我国人民对中朝人民部队的热爱与期望。
