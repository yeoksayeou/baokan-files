Title: Canadian Labour Progressive Party Leader Writes Article in Commemoration
Author: 
Date: 1951-10-10
Page: 4

    [Xinhua News Agency Dispatch] To commemorate the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, progressive forces in Canada are calling on the Canadian government to recognize China and establish trade relations. "The Canadian Forum" published an editorial commemorating the second anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Labour-Progressive Party of Canada, wrote an article for the newspaper discussing the great role of Mao Zedong in the struggle for Chinese independence. Buck, on behalf of the Labour-Progressive Party, called for a broad popular movement to force the Canadian government and Parliament to establish diplomatic and trade relations with China. "The Canadian Forum" also published Buck's congratulatory telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

### 加劳工进步党领袖著文纪念

1951-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    加劳工进步党领袖著文纪念
     【本报讯】据新华社讯：为了纪念中华人民共和国成立两周年，加拿大的进步力量要求加拿大政府承认中国，并建立贸易关系。“加拿大论坛报”发表社论纪念中华人民共和国成立二周年。加拿大劳工进步党总书记布克为该报撰文论述毛泽东在争取中国独立的斗争中所起的伟大作用。布克代表劳工进步党号召展开广泛的人民运动，以迫使加拿大政府和国会跟中国建立外交和贸易关系。“加拿大论坛报”还刊登了布克致中国共产党中央委员会的贺电。
