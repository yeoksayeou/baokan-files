Title: Paul and Other Countries' Observation Delegations Depart for Hangzhou, Indian and Other Countries' Delegations Leave Tianjin for the South
Author:
Date: 1951-10-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 17th. The People's Observation Delegations from Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Mongolia, the German Democratic Republic, the Korean writer Li Tae-jun, and the British People's Observation Delegate Derrigan, who participated in our National Day celebrations, left Shanghai for Hangzhou City on the afternoon of the 17th after a six-day visit in Shanghai. Sha Kefu, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, also accompanied them. During their stay in Shanghai, the delegates from various countries visited Fudan University, Jiaotong University, and factories producing cotton textiles, dyeing and printing, and waterproof fabrics, as well as the Xinwen Ribao newspaper office, and toured the Huangpu River. The delegates from various countries received warm welcomes and receptions everywhere. Hang Peilan, a Shanghai model worker who attended the National Labor Model Conference, production models, meritorious workers from various factories, and study models from schools personally attended the reception for the visiting foreign guests. Korean writer Li Tae-jun said: "I will definitely write the deeds of the Chinese people's generous and heroic support for Korea in my book about my travels in China, to inspire our people's determination to resist American imperialism." Writers and artists among the delegates from various countries stated that after returning to their countries, they would use various artistic forms to tell their homeland's people what they had seen and heard. Tse. Damdin Süren, the head of the Mongolian People's Observation Delegation, said after visiting the factory: "The great Resist-America, Aid-Korea movement of the Chinese people demonstrates the invincible power of the Chinese people."
　　On the evening of the 16th, more than 2,000 people from Shanghai's women's and youth circles gathered to welcome Gusta Fučíková, head of the Czechoslovak People's Observation Delegation and Vice-Chairman of the Czechoslovak Anti-Fascist League, and invited Fučíková to report on the heroic struggle of Czech hero Julius Fučík.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Indian Goodwill Mission to China and the People's Observation Delegations from Indonesia, Burma, Pakistan, and other countries arrived in Tianjin on the 15th from Shenyang. After more than two days of visits, they left Tianjin for Nanjing on the 17th.
　　During their stay in Tianjin, they visited the North China Region Urban and Rural Materials Exchange Exhibition and some factories, and were warmly received by various people's organizations in Tianjin.
　　In addition: Members of the Indian Goodwill Mission to China, Mohammad Habib and Mohammad Majeeb, arrived in Guangzhou from Shanghai on the 16th and left Guangzhou on the 17th via Hong Kong to return to India.

### 保罗等国观礼代表团赴杭州  印度等国代表团离天津南下

1951-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    保罗等国观礼代表团赴杭州
    印度等国代表团离天津南下
    【新华社上海十七日电】参加我国庆节庆祝典礼的保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古、德意志民主共和国人民观礼代表团、朝鲜作家李泰俊和英国人民观礼代表德里邦等，在上海参观六天，已在十七日下午离上海赴杭州市。全国文学艺术界联合会秘书长沙可夫亦陪同前往。各国代表在上海期间曾分别访问了国立复旦、交通大学和参观了棉纺、印染、防雨布等工厂及新闻日报馆，并游览了黄浦江。各国代表到处受到热烈的欢迎和接待。曾出席全国劳动模范大会的上海市劳动模范杭佩兰、各工厂中的生产模范、功臣和学校中的学习模范都亲自出席招待远道来访的外宾。朝鲜作家李泰俊说：“我一定把中国人民慷慨英勇支援朝鲜的事迹，写在我的中国记行的著作中，以鼓舞我国人民抗击美帝国主义的决心。”各国代表中的作家和艺术家都表示在返国后，要把所见所闻，用各种的文艺形式告诉祖国的人民。蒙古人民观礼代表团团长策·达木丁苏隆在参观了工厂后说：“中国人民伟大的抗美援朝运动显示了中国人民不可战胜的力量。”
    十六日晚，上海妇女界、青年界二千余人集会欢迎捷克斯洛伐克人民观礼代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克反法西斯联盟副主席古丝妲·伏契科娃，并请伏契科娃报告捷克斯洛伐克人民英雄尤利斯·伏契克的英勇斗争事迹。
    【新华社十七日讯】印度亲善访华团和印尼、缅甸、巴基斯坦等国人民观礼代表团十五日由沈阳到天津，经过两天多访问已在十七日离天津去南京。
    他们在天津期间，先后参观了华北区城乡物资交流展览会和某些工厂，并受到天津市各人民团体的热烈招待。
    又讯：印度亲善访华团团员谟罕默德·哈比伯、谟罕默德·马杰伯二人已在十六日由上海抵达广州，并在十七日离广州经香港返印度。
