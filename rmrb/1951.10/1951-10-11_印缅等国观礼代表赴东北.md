Title: Observation Delegations from India, Burma, and Other Countries Depart for Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1951-10-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 10th—The Indian Goodwill Delegation to China, the Burmese and Indonesian People's Observation Delegations, and Mr. Jilais, a member of the Pakistani People's Observation Delegation, a total of twenty-three people, who were invited to participate in our country's National Day celebration, left Beijing today. They will be accompanied by Hong Shen, Director of the Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, to visit Northeast China. Those who saw them off at the station included: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman; Xu Deheng, Standing Committee Member; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; Zhu Xuefan and Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Fan Changjiang, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China News Publication and Printing Trade Union; Sha Qianli, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation; Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Deng Yingchao and Xu Guangping, Vice Chairwomen of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Zeng Xianzhi, Deputy Secretary-General; Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Affairs Department; Shao Lizi and Cao Jinghua, Directors of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

### 印缅等国观礼代表赴东北

1951-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    印缅等国观礼代表赴东北
    【新华社十日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的印度亲善访华团、缅甸和印尼的人民观礼代表团及巴基斯坦人民观礼代表团团员杰利斯等一行二十三人，在今天离开北京，由中华全国文学艺术界联合会联络部洪深部长陪同前往东北参观访问。到车站欢送的有：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若，副主席陈叔通，常务委员许德珩，秘书长刘贯一，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、刘宁一，中国新闻出版印刷工会筹备委员会主任委员范长江，中华全国民主青年联合总会副主席沙千里，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会秘书长荣高棠，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、许广平，副秘书长曾宪植、国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中苏友好协会总会理事邵力子、曹靖华，及北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等人。
