Title: Tehran Citizens Strike and Close Shops to Protest British Interference in Iranian Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1951-10-05
Page: 4

    Tehran Citizens Strike and Close Shops
    To Protest British Interference in Iranian Internal Affairs
    [Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 1st dispatch] TASS, citing a Tehran dispatch on October 1st, stated: On September 30th, Tehran citizens held strikes and closed shops to protest against British imperialists' interference in Iran's internal affairs. All factories, enterprises, and municipal organizations were closed. All the citizens of Tehran went to the streets, and traffic was completely halted.

### 德黑兰市民罢工罢市  抗议英国干涉伊内政

1951-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    德黑兰市民罢工罢市
    抗议英国干涉伊内政
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社巴黎一日电引德黑兰消息称：九月三十日，德黑兰市民举行罢工与罢市，以抗议英帝国主义者干涉伊朗内政。一切工厂、企业和市政机关都关上了门。德黑兰全体市民都走到街上，交通完全停顿。
