Title: Volunteer Army Combat Hero Delegation Presents Banner to the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association
Author:
Date: 1951-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The National Day observation delegation of combat heroes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army jointly invited Guo Moruo, chairman of the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association, on the morning of the 4th to report on the great achievements of the Resist America Aid Korea movement in the past year. After Chairman Guo Moruo's report, the delegation of combat heroes of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army presented a banner to the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association on behalf of the Volunteer Army commanders and fighters. The banner read: "Following the will of the people of the motherland, we resolutely stand on the front line against aggression and fight to defend the motherland and world peace." After the presentation, Du Ping, a representative of the delegation, delivered a speech, saying: Our People's Volunteer Army, fighting side by side with the Korean People's Army, has won the victory of five major campaigns and many counter-attacks in the past eleven months, driving the enemy from the Yalu River back to the 38th parallel, greatly demonstrating the greatness of the strength of our Chinese and Korean people. The achievement of this victory is inseparable from the leadership of the Resist America Aid Korea General Association and the enthusiastic support of the people of the motherland. The support of the people of the motherland has not only given us great encouragement, but also is the source of our infinite strength and the guarantee of our victory. Therefore, today the delegation, representing all comrades of the Volunteer Army, presents a banner to the Resist America Aid Korea General Association, which has made outstanding contributions to the cause of peace and democracy, to express our respect and to thank the General Association and the people of the whole country for their support.

### 志愿军战斗英雄代表团向抗美援朝总会献旗

1951-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军战斗英雄代表团向抗美援朝总会献旗
    【新华社四日讯】中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表团四日上午联合邀请中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若报告一年来抗美援朝运动的伟大成就。郭沫若主席报告后，中国人民志愿军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表团代表志愿军指挥员、战斗员向中国人民抗美援朝总会献旗。旗上写着：“遵照祖国人民的意志，我们坚决地站在反侵略的前线，为保卫祖国、保卫世界和平而奋斗。”献旗后代表团代表杜平致词，他说：我们人民志愿军，与朝鲜人民军并肩作战，十一个多月以来，已取得了五个大战役的胜利和多次反击战的胜利，把敌人从鸭绿江赶回到三八线，大大地显示了我中朝人民力量的伟大，这一胜利的取得与抗美援朝总会的领导和祖国人民热情的支援是分不开的。祖国人民的支援不仅给了我们极大的鼓舞，而且也是我们无限力量的源泉，是我们胜利的保障。因而今天代表团代表志愿军全体同志向对和平民主事业有着卓越贡献的抗美援朝总会敬献锦旗，是表示我们的敬意，并借以感谢总会和全国人民对我们的支援。
