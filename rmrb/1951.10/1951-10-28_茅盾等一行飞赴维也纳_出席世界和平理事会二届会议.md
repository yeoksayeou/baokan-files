Title: Mao Dun and Entourage Fly to Vienna to Attend Second Session of World Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1951-10-28
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 27th: The Chinese delegation to the World Peace Council, led by Mao Dun, and a party of four, flew from here this morning via Prague to Vienna to attend the second session of the World Peace Council. Another group of delegates, including Cai Tingkai and Mei Ru'ao, and a party of seven, took a train this morning via Hungary to Vienna.

### 茅盾等一行飞赴维也纳  出席世界和平理事会二届会议

1951-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    茅盾等一行飞赴维也纳
    出席世界和平理事会二届会议
    【新华社莫斯科二十七日电】我国出席世界和平理事会代表茅盾等一行四人今晨由此乘飞机经布拉格赴维也纳出席世界和平理事会第二届会议。另一部分代表蔡廷锴、梅汝璈等一行七人，今晨乘火车取道匈牙利赴维也纳。
