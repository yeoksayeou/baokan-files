Title: Delegation of People from Old Revolutionary Base Areas in Northern China Depart for Tianjin; Water Conservancy Representatives Depart Beijing for Tianjin on the Same Day
Author:
Date: 1951-10-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:

    Delegation of People from Old Revolutionary Base Areas in Northern China Depart for Tianjin
    Water Conservancy Representatives Depart Beijing for Tianjin on the Same Day

     [Report from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: The delegation of people from old revolutionary base areas in Northern China, who came to Beijing to observe the National Day celebrations, departed by special train on the 10th for Tianjin to visit the North China Regional Urban and Rural Materials Exchange Exhibition. Those who came to the station to see them off included Liu Lantao, Minister of the North China Affairs Department of the Central People's Government and Third Secretary of the North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Wu Xinyu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government; Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture, and Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister; Fu Zuoyi, Minister of Water Conservancy; Liu Jingfan, Deputy Director of the People's Supervisory Committee; Xu Guangping, Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government; representatives of the combat heroes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who observed the National Day celebrations; and representatives of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission and the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, totaling more than 600 people.
    During their stay in Beijing, the representatives of the people from the old revolutionary base areas in Northern China participated in the Second National Day celebration, and successively visited the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant and Power Plant, the North China Agricultural Machinery General Factory, the Exhibition of Cultural Relics of Ethnic Minorities, and the Exhibition of the History of the Communist Party of China, and toured the capital's famous historical sites.
    The representatives were particularly moved by the warm hospitality of the Central People's Government and various organizations. In particular, Chairman Mao and the central leaders had cordial meetings with them, which further increased the representatives' respect and love for their leaders. Li Wansheng, a representative of the people from the old revolutionary base area of Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia, said with great emotion: "We must work hard in production and work when we return, so that we can live up to our leader Chairman Mao and our motherland."

    [Xinhua News Agency, 10th] The water conservancy representatives who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations left Beijing for Tianjin on the 10th. Fu Zuoyi, Minister of Water Conservancy of the Central People's Government, and Vice Ministers Li Baohua and Zhang Hanying went to the station to see them off.
    During their stay in Beijing, the representatives visited historical sites and scenic spots in the capital, mechanized reclamation farms, the Agricultural Machinery General Factory, and the Railway Exhibition. The Huai River delegation was especially welcomed by people from all walks of life. They were invited to give reports to the troop observation delegation, Renmin University of China, college and middle school students in Beijing, and workers. The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the Central Academy of Drama, the Farmland Water Conservancy Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture and other units also invited some representatives to hold forums.

### 北方老根据地人民观礼代表团赴天津  水利代表同日离京赴津

1951-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

    北方老根据地人民观礼代表团赴天津
    水利代表同日离京赴津
     【本报讯】据新华社讯：北方老根据地人民国庆节观礼代表团十日乘专车赴天津参观华北区城乡物资交流展览会。到车站欢送的有中央人民政府华北事务部部长、中共中央华北局第三书记刘澜涛，中央人民政府内务部副部长武新宇，农业部部长李书城、副部长杨显东，水利部部长傅作义，人民监察委员会副主任委员刘景范，政务院副秘书长许广平，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军战斗英雄国庆节观礼代表团代表，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会和中共中央华北局机关代表等共六百余人。
    北方老根据地人民代表在留在北京的期间，参加了二届国庆大典，又先后参观了石景山钢铁厂和发电厂、华北农业机械总厂、少数民族文物展览、中国共产党党史展览等，并游览了首都的名胜古迹。
    代表们对中央人民政府及各机关团体的热忱招待，尤为感奋。特别是毛主席和中央各首长与他们作了亲切的会见，更使代表们增加了自己对领袖的崇敬与热爱。陕甘宁老区人民代表李万胜感动万分地说：“我们回去一定要努力生产和工作，这样才对得起我们的领袖毛主席和我们的祖国。”
    【新华社十日讯】来北京参加国庆节观礼的水利代表已于十日离北京赴天津。中央人民政府水利部傅作义部长、李葆华、张含英副部长均前往车站欢送。
    代表们在北京期间，曾参观首都古迹名胜、机垦农场、农业机械总厂和铁路展览会。淮河代表团特别受到各界人民的欢迎。他们先后应邀给部队观礼代表团、中国人民大学、北京市大中学生、工人做了报告。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中央戏剧学院、农业部农田水利局等单位，也曾邀请了部分代表举行了座谈会。
