Author: Shao Yu
Date: 1951-10-03

     Brilliant Achievements of New China in its Second Year
     (II) Vigorously Suppressing Counter-Revolutionaries
     By Shao Yu
     The suppression of counter-revolutionaries has been carried out thoroughly or relatively thoroughly in the vast majority of regions across the country, thereby mobilizing and educating the broad masses of the people and strengthening the people's democratic dictatorship.
    The suppression of counter-revolutionaries has consolidated the rear for resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, and guaranteed the progress of national construction.

### 新中国第二年的辉煌成就（图片）
邵宇
1951-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

     新中国第二年的辉煌成就
     （二）大张旗鼓镇压反革命
     邵宇作
     全国极大多数地区的镇压反革命运动，已进行得彻底，或比较彻底，从而发动与教育了广大人民群众，加强了人民民主专政。
    镇压反革命运动巩固了抗美援朝的後方，保障了国家建设的进行。
