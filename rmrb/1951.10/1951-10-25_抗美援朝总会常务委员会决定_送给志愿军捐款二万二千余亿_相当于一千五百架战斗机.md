Title: Standing Committee of the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association Decides to Donate Over 2.2 Trillion to the Volunteer Army, Equivalent to 1,500 Fighter Planes
Author:
Date: 1951-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: To celebrate the great victory of the Chinese people's resistance against U.S. aggression and aid to Korea, and the first anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's participation in the war, the Standing Committee of the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association decided today to donate the equivalent of 1,500 fighter planes (totaling 2,250 billion RMB) to the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, so that the Chinese People's Volunteer Army can procure weapons according to its needs.

### 抗美援朝总会常务委员会决定  送给志愿军捐款二万二千余亿  相当于一千五百架战斗机

1951-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    抗美援朝总会常务委员会决定
    送给志愿军捐款二万二千余亿
    相当于一千五百架战斗机
    【新华社二十四日讯】为庆祝中国人民抗美援朝及中国人民志愿军出国作战一周年的伟大胜利，中国人民抗美援朝总会常务委员会决定于本日送给中国人民志愿军司令部相当于一千五百架战斗机的捐款（合人民币二万二千五百亿元），以便中国人民志愿军按照需要备置武器。
