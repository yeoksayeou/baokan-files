Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly
Author:
Date: 1951-10-09
Page: 4

    [Reported by this newspaper] The Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly sent a telegram to Chairman Mao to congratulate our National Day. The original telegram is as follows: To His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we, on behalf of the Vietnamese National Assembly and the Vietnamese people, are honored to extend our warmest congratulations to you, the Political Consultative Conference, and the Chinese people. The great achievements of the Chinese people in construction and the brilliant victories won by the Chinese People's Volunteers in the Korean War in defending peace in the Far East and the world have greatly increased our enthusiasm. With the support of the people's democratic countries, especially the People's Republic of China, we are determined to defeat the imperialist aggressors in order to establish a people's democratic regime, thereby contributing to the defense of world peace and democracy.

        The Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam

### 越国民议会常设委员会贺电

1951-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    越国民议会常设委员会贺电
    【本报讯】越南国民议会常设委员会致电毛主席，祝贺我国国庆节，原电如下：中华人民共和国中国人民政治协商会议主席毛泽东阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国国庆日，我们代表越南国民议会及人民，荣幸地向您、政治协商会议及中国人民致以最热烈的祝贺。中国人民在建设工作中的伟大成就以及在朝鲜作战的中国人民志愿军为保卫远东及世界和平所获得的辉煌胜利大大提高了我们的热情。我们在人民民主国家、特别是中华人民共和国的支持下，决心打垮帝国主义侵略者，以建立人民民主政权，从而有助于保卫世界和平与民主。
        越南民主共和国国民议会常设委员会
