Title: Hua Dong and Shanghai People's Gathering Welcomes Soviet Delegation

Author:

Date: 1951-10-12
Page: 1

    Hua Dong and Shanghai People's Gathering
    Welcomes Soviet Delegation
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 11th] Over two thousand people from Hua Dong and Shanghai gathered on the evening of the 10th to welcome the Soviet People's Delegation. The conference began with a welcome speech by Liu Changsheng, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Shanghai Branch, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, and Chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions. On behalf of the people of all of Hua Dong and Shanghai, he expressed a warm welcome to the Soviet delegation and thanked the Soviet people for their long-term friendship and assistance to the Chinese people.
    Soviet delegation leader Solovyov then spoke, congratulating China on the great achievements made in the two years since its founding. Following this, Trofimenko, Chairman of the Soviet Union of Cultural and Educational Workers and a member of the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Soviet Union, and Zotelaeva, a member of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League and a member of the Central Committee of the Georgian Communist Youth League, spoke on behalf of Soviet women and youth respectively, congratulating Chinese women and youth on their achievements in the great cause of building their motherland. After the meeting, a musical evening was held.

### 华东暨上海各界人民集会  欢迎苏联观礼代表团

1951-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

    华东暨上海各界人民集会
    欢迎苏联观礼代表团
    【新华社上海十一日电】华东暨上海各界人民二千余人在十日晚集会欢迎苏联人民观礼代表团。大会首由中苏友好协会上海分会副会长、中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会上海分会主席、上海总工会主席刘长胜致欢迎词。他代表全华东及上海人民、向苏联代表团表示热烈欢迎，并感谢苏联人民长期给予中国人民的友谊援助。
    苏联代表团团长索洛维耶夫继起致词，祝贺我国成立两年来的巨大成就。接着苏联文化教育工作者联合会主席、全苏工会中央理事会理事特罗菲敏科，全苏列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会委员、乔治亚共产主义青年团中央委员会委员佐捷拉娃先后代表苏联妇女、青年祝贺中国妇女和青年在建设自己祖国的伟大事业中的成就。会后，举行音乐晚会。
