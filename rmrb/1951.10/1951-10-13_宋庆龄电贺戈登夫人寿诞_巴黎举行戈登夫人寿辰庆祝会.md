Title: Soong Ching Ling Sends Congratulations to Madame Gordon on Her Birthday; Madame Gordon's Birthday Celebration Held in Paris
Author:
Date: 1951-10-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Soong Ching Ling, Chairman of the China People's Relief Administration, sent a telegram on September 30th to Madame Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation and President of the Union of French Women, to congratulate her on her 70th birthday. The original telegram is as follows: Madame Cotton:
    On the occasion of your 70th birthday, I extend my warm congratulations from afar, and wish you even more vigor to strive for the world peace movement, in order to achieve greater success.
               Soong Ching Ling, September 30th.
Xinhua News Agency, 12th: According to TASS, Paris, 11th: The celebration assembly for the 70th birthday of Madame Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, President of the French Women's Union, and winner of the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize, was held on October 10th in the "Pleyel" Hall in Paris.
    Participating in the assembly were representatives from many people's organizations, workers in culture, science, and the arts, diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries stationed in France, and representatives from democratic women's organizations in Belgium, Switzerland, Spain, and other countries.
    Amidst warm applause, Madame Cotton, Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, Madame Vaillant-Couturier, Secretary-General of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Laurent Casanova, President of the French National Peace Council, Jeannette Vermeersch, Secretary-General of the French Women's Union, and Louis Aragon, a famous French poet, were elected to the presidium of the assembly.
    Jeannette Vermeersch, Madame Vaillant-Couturier, Laurent Casanova, Frédéric Joliot-Curie and many others spoke successively, praising Madame Cotton's activities and her contributions to the struggle for peace.
    Telegrams of congratulations to Madame Cotton from various parties were read at the meeting, including telegrams from the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and women's organizations from other countries, as well as telegrams from Jacques Duclos, Secretary of the French Communist Party, and Dolores Ibárruri, Secretary-General of the Spanish Communist Party.
    The people participating in the assembly expressed great indignation upon hearing that the Soviet women's congratulatory delegation could not attend because the French government had refused to issue them entry visas.
    After receiving gifts, Madame Cotton gave a speech. She thanked everyone for their love and concern for her, and spoke about the powerful force of women around the world striving for peace. She said, "Dear friends! On my seventieth birthday, I call on you to unite, regardless of your beliefs, to defend peace!" Madame Cotton finally pledged to do everything in her power to fight for international peace.

### 宋庆龄电贺戈登夫人寿诞  巴黎举行戈登夫人寿辰庆祝会

1951-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄电贺戈登夫人寿诞
    巴黎举行戈登夫人寿辰庆祝会
    【新华社十二日讯】中国人民救济总会宋庆龄主席于九月三十日电贺国际民主妇女联合会主席、法兰西民主妇女联盟主席戈登夫人七十寿辰。原电如下：戈登夫人：
    欣逢您七十大寿，谨遥致热烈的庆贺，并祝以更充沛的精神为世界和平运动努力，以期获得更大的成功。
               宋庆龄  九月三十日
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社巴黎十一日讯：国际民主妇女联合会主席、法兰西妇女联盟主席、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人戈登夫人七十寿辰庆祝大会，于十月十日在巴黎“普列叶尔”大厅举行。
    参加大会的，有许多人民团体的代表，文化、科学、艺术工作者，苏联和各人民民主国家驻法国的外交使节，比利时、瑞士、西班牙等国民主妇女团体的代表等等。
    在热烈的掌声中，戈登夫人、世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里、国际民主妇女联合会总书记瓦扬—古久里夫人、法国全国和平理事会主席法奇、法兰西妇女联盟总书记萧玛、法国名诗人阿拉贡等人被选为大会主席团。
    萧玛、瓦扬—古久里夫人、法奇和约里奥—居里等许多人相继发言，颂扬戈登夫人的活动和她在和平斗争中的贡献。
    会上宣读了各方致戈登夫人的贺电，其中有苏联妇女反法西斯委员会、中华全国民主妇女联合会及其他各国妇女团体的来电，也有法国共产党书记杜克洛，西班牙共产党总书记伊巴露丽等的来电。
    参加大会的人们听到苏联妇女祝寿代表团因法国政府拒发入境签证而不能到会，都表示十分愤慨。
    戈登夫人在接受礼物以后发表谈话。她感谢大家对她的热爱和关切，并且谈到全世界妇女争取和平的强大力量。她说：“亲爱的朋友们！在我的七十岁生日的时候，我号召你们不分信仰地团结起来，保卫和平！”戈登夫人最后保证将竭尽全力为争取国际和平而斗争。
