Title: Editor's Note
Author:
Date: 1951-10-14
Page: 4

    In this issue, we publish articles written for our publication by Aubelin, member of the Soviet Peace Committee and member of the World Peace Council, and Jerzy Putrament, Vice-Chairman of the Polish Peace Committee and member of the World Peace Council. We also publish letters to the Chinese People's Volunteers from Gusta Fučíková (wife of the protagonist of the famous book "Report from the Gallows"), representative of the Czech Peace Committee and the Czech Women's Federation participating in our National Day celebration; Karaslavov, Vice-Chairman of the Bulgarian Peace Committee; Bartel, representative of the Free German Youth League of the German Democratic Republic and a German writer; Professor Seitz, representative of the German Cultural Democratic Renewal League; and Lebădatu, Vice-Chairman of the Romanian Standing Committee for the Defense of Peace. In the previous issue, we published articles written for our publication by Hyun Heun, head of the Korean People's Delegation; Lê Đình Thám, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace; Tseveenii Damdinsüren, member of the Mongolian Committee for the Defense of World Peace and member of the World Peace Council; Sundarlal, member of the All-India Peace Council; Tekin Kodomai, head of the Burmese People's Delegation; Tekin Lun, Chairman of the Burmese Congress for the Defense of World Peace; and Tabrani, Chairman of the Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association. In addition, letters to the Chinese People's Volunteers written by Jerzy Putrament, Vice-Chairman of the Polish Peace Committee and member of the World Peace Council; articles written for our publication by Karaslavov, Vice-Chairman of the Bulgarian Peace Committee; and Lebădatu, Vice-Chairman of the Romanian Standing Committee for the Defense of Peace, will be published in the next issue. Due to the publication of these valuable articles and enthusiastic letters, other columns of the previous and current issues are suspended. We ask our readers to take note.

### 编者的话

1951-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    编者的话
    本刊这期发表了苏联保卫和平委员会委员、世界和平理事会理事奥巴林和波兰和平委员会副主席、世界和平理事会理事耶齐·普特拉曼特为本刊写的文章，并发表了捷克和平委员会及捷克妇女联合会参加我国国庆典礼代表古丝妲·伏契科娃（著名的“绞索套着脖子时的报告”一书主人公的妻子），保加利亚和平委员会副主席卡拉斯拉渥夫，德意志民主共和国自由德国青年联盟代表、德国作家巴特尔，德国文化民主复兴联盟代表赛兹教授和罗马尼亚保卫和平常设委员会副主席列巴达土写给中国人民志愿军的信。上期本刊已发表了朝鲜人民观礼代表团团长玄勋，越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎庭探，蒙古保卫世界和平委员会委员、世界和平理事会理事策·达木丁苏隆，印度全国和平理事会理事森德拉尔，缅甸人民观礼代表团团长德钦哥都迈，缅甸保卫世界和平大会主席德钦伦和印尼印中友好协会主席塔勃兰尼为本刊写的文章。此外，尚有波兰和平委员会副主席、世界和平理事会理事耶齐·普特拉曼特写给中国人民志愿军的信，保加利亚和平委员会副主席卡拉斯拉渥夫和罗马尼亚保卫和平常设委员会副主席列巴达土为本刊写的文章，将在本刊下期发表。因发表这些宝贵的文章和热情的信件，上期和这期本刊其他各栏暂停，请读者注意。
