Title: Soviet Newspapers Prominently Publish News of China's National Day

Author:
Date: 1951-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd. News, Labor, Komsomolskaya Pravda, Red Star, Red Navy, Socialist Agriculture, Moscow Truth, and other newspapers all prominently published on their front pages the telegrams of congratulation on China's National Day from Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Marshal Stalin, to Chairman Mao Zedong, and from Foreign Minister Vyshinsky to Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai. The newspapers also prominently published news of the National Day celebrations held by the people of Beijing, the grand reception held by the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, and the opening of the Chinese Film Week in Moscow.

### 苏报显著刊载我国国庆消息

1951-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏报显著刊载我国国庆消息
    【新华社莫斯科三日电】二日消息报、劳动报、共青真理报、红星报、红海军报、社会主义农业报、莫斯科真理报和其他报纸，都用头条地位登载苏联部长会议主席斯大林大元帅致毛泽东主席、外交部长维辛斯基致政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来的祝贺我国庆日的电文。各报并用显著地位登载北京人民举行国庆庆祝典礼、中国驻苏联大使馆举行盛大酒会和中国电影周在莫斯科揭幕的消息。
