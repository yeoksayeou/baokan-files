Title: Korean People's Delegation Leaves Beijing Yesterday to Return Home

Author:
Date: 1951-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Korean People's Delegation, invited to attend our National Day celebrations, left Beijing today under the leadership of head of delegation Hyun Heon to return home. Those who saw them off at the station included: Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; Zhu Xuefan and Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Li Jiebo, Executive Committee Member; Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation; Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Zeng Xianzhi, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Work Department; Sha Kefu, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Shao Lizi, Director of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government. Kim Kuk-bo, Counselor of the Korean Embassy, also went to the station to see them off.

### 朝鲜人民观礼代表团昨日离京启程返国

1951-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民观礼代表团昨日离京启程返国
    【新华社五日讯】应邀参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼的朝鲜人民观礼代表团在团长玄勋率领下，今天离北京启程返国。到车站欢送的有：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会副主席陈叔通，秘书长刘贯一，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、刘宁一，执行委员李颉伯，中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会秘书长荣高棠，中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长曾宪植，国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中华全国文学艺术界联合会秘书长沙可夫，中苏友好协会总会理事邵力子，及北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等人。朝鲜大使馆参赞金国辅也到车站欢送。
