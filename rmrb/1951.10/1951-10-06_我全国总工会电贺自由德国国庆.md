Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Congratulatory Message on National Day of Free Germany
Author:
Date: 1951-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th: On October 7th, the second anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Free German Trade Union Federation to express congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Free German Trade Union Federation:

On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, on behalf of the entire Chinese working people, we extend warm and militant greetings and sincere congratulations to the working class of your country. All the workers in China are watching with admiration all the efforts and achievements of the German working people in building their motherland, striving for the independence and unity of their motherland, opposing the arming of West Germany, and defending world peace. We wish the heroic German working class and all the people greater victories in this regard!

                            All-China Federation of Trade Unions
                                    October 4th

### 我全国总工会电贺自由德国国庆

1951-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国总工会电贺自由德国国庆
    【新华社五日讯】本月七日为德意志民主共和国成立两周年纪念日，中华全国总工会特致电自由德国工会联合会表示祝贺。电文如下：自由德国工会联合会：
    欣逢德意志民主共和国成立二周年纪念日，谨代表中国全体劳动人民向贵国工人阶级致以热烈的战斗的敬礼和诚挚的祝贺。全中国的工人都以钦佩的目光注视着德国劳动人民在建设祖国、争取祖国独立统一、反对武装西德及保卫世界和平的斗争中的一切努力和成就。谨祝英勇的德国工人阶级及全体人民在这方面获得更大的胜利！
                            中华全国总工会
                                    十月四日
