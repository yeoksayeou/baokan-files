Title: Let Us Firmly Grasp the Weapon of Defending Peace and Fight to Defeat Imperialist Aggression
Author: Mao Yu
Date: 1951-10-10
Page: 2

    Let Us Firmly Grasp the Weapon of Defending Peace
    And Fight to Defeat Imperialist Aggression

    To the Editor:
    Comrade Stalin's speech has greatly inspired the fighting confidence of peace-loving people around the world, severely hit the arrogant attempts of the American aggressor bandits, and caused the shameful bankruptcy of America's atomic blackmail policy!

    The Soviet Union has always been peace-loving. Today, the Soviet Union's possession of atomic weapons can effectively prevent imperialist aggression, thus powerfully guaranteeing world peace and security. This is worthy of jubilation for all peace-loving people around the world. Let us firmly grasp the weapon of defending world peace, follow the path indicated by Comrade Stalin, and strive to completely defeat imperialist aggression!

                                Reader Mao Yu

### 让我们紧握捍卫和平的武器  为打败帝国主义侵略而奋斗
毛芋

专栏：读者来信

    让我们紧握捍卫和平的武器
    为打败帝国主义侵略而奋斗编辑同志：
    斯大林同志的谈话大大地鼓舞了全世界爱好和平的人民的斗争信心，严重地打击了美国侵略强盗的狂妄企图，使美国的原子讹诈政策遭到了可耻的破产！
    苏联一向是爱好和平的。今天苏联掌握原子武器，就能有效地制止帝国主义的侵略，因而有力地保证了世界的和平与安全。这是值得全世界爱好和平的人民欢欣鼓舞的。让我们紧握着捍卫世界和平的武器，遵循着斯大林同志所指示的道路，为彻底打败帝国主义的侵略而奋斗吧！
                                读者    毛芋
