Title: Sino-German Postal and Telecommunications Agreement Signed in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-10-13
Page: 1

    Sino-German Postal and Telecommunications Agreement Signed in Beijing Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th] The Sino-German Postal and Telecommunications Agreement, after harmonious and friendly consultations between the representatives of both sides, was signed on October 12, 1951, in Beijing, the capital of the People's Republic of China. The plenipotentiary representative signing the agreement on our side was Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government, and the plenipotentiary representative on the German side was Mr. Koenig, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China with the rank of Ambassador. Those who attended the signing ceremony on our side included Lai Yali, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Xu Yixin, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Dong Xibai, Secretary-General of the Treaty Committee; Shen Guang, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications; Wang Zegang, Director of the Long-Distance Telephone Administration and concurrently Director of the International Relations Department; Su Younong, Director of the Postal Administration; and Li Qiang, Director of the Radio Administration; on the German side were Mr. Grutner, Counselor, Mr. Duerber, Counselor, and Mr. Rosemeier, Counselor, of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China.

### 中德两国通邮通电协定昨日在京签字

1951-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中德两国通邮通电协定昨日在京签字
    【新华社十二日讯】中德两国通邮通电协定，经过双方代表和谐友好的协商，已于一九五一年十月十二日在中华人民共和国首都北京签字。在协定上签字者，我方全权代表为中央人民政府邮电部部长朱学范，德方全权代表为德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希。参加签字仪式者，我方有外交部办公厅副主任赖亚力，外交部苏联东欧司副司长徐以新，条约委员会秘书主任董希白，邮电部办公厅主任申光，长途电话总局局长兼国际关系处处长王子纲，邮政总局局长苏幼农，无线电总局局长李强等；德方有德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团葛鲁特纳参赞，杜尔伯参赞，罗斯迈色参赞等。
