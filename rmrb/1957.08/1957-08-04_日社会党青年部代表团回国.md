Title: Japanese Socialist Party Youth Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-08-04
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 3rd: The Japanese Socialist Party Youth Delegation concluded its visit to China and left Guangzhou today to return home.
    Before leaving, the head of the delegation, Shuichiro Arase, told Xinhua reporters that the delegation had visited thirteen cities in China over the past fifty-two days. With the help of the All-China Youth Federation and youth from various regions, the purpose of this visit to China has been completely achieved.

### 日社会党青年部代表团回国

1957-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    日社会党青年部代表团回国
    新华社广州3日电　日本社会党青年部代表团结束了在中国的访问，今天离开广州回国。
    临行前，代表团团长荒濑修一郎向新华社记者说，代表团在过去五十二天里，访问了中国十三个城市，通过中华全国民主青年联合会和各地青年的帮助，这次来华访问的目的已经完满地达到。
