Title: Australian Construction Industry Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-08-30
Page: 4

Friendly Exchanges

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 29th. The Australian Construction Industry Union Delegation, led by Hancock, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They are visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Construction Workers' Union.
　　Since arriving in Guangzhou on August 21, the delegation has visited the Wuhan Iron and Steel Complex, the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, building materials factories, workers' clubs, canteens, and workers' family dormitories in Guangzhou, Wuhan, Zhengzhou, Xi'an, and other places.
　　Zhang Tianmin, Chairman of the Chinese Construction Workers' Union, Wang Yushan, Chairman of the Beijing Construction Workers' Union, and more than 20 union cadres and advanced producers welcomed them at the airport today.

### 澳建筑业工会代表团到京

1957-08-30
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

　　澳建筑业工会代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　以汉柯克为首的澳大利亚建筑业工会代表团一行四人，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中国建筑工会全国委员会的邀请来中国访问的。
    代表团自8月21日到达广州以后，曾先后在广州、武汉、郑州、西安等地参观了武汉钢铁联合企业、武汉长江大桥等建设工地和建筑材料工厂、职工俱乐部、食堂、职工家属宿舍等。
    今天到机场欢迎的有中国建筑工会主席张天民、北京市建筑工会主席王玉山，以及工会干部、先进生产者等二十多人。
