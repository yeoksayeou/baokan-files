Title: Syria Arrests Three Jordanian Spies
Author:
Date: 1957-08-26
Page: 6

　  Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 25th. TASS reported from Damascus on the 25th: According to newspapers, Syrian security agencies have arrested three Jordanian spies who infiltrated Syria to carry out terrorist activities.

### 叙逮捕三名约旦间谍

1957-08-26
第6版()
专栏：

　  叙逮捕三名约旦间谍
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社大马士革25日讯：据报纸报道，叙利亚保安机关逮捕了三名潜入叙利亚进行恐怖活动的约旦间谍。
