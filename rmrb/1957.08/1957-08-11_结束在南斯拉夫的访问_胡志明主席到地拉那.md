Title: Ending the Visit to Yugoslavia, Chairman Ho Chi Minh Arrives in Tirana
Author:
Date: 1957-08-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, August 10th: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and his entourage arrived in Tirana by plane on August 9th.

	Along the road from the city center of Tirana to the airport, banners written in both Albanian and Vietnamese, giant portraits of Chairman Ho Chi Minh, and the national flags of Albania and Vietnam were everywhere.

	After President Ho Chi Minh stepped off the plane, he received flowers from a group of children and warmly shook hands with Albanian People's Assembly Presidium Chairman Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Mehmet Shehu, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Labor Party of Albania Hysni Kapo, and others who came to welcome him. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Albania also came to the airport to welcome him.

	Accompanied by Chairman Lleshi, President Ho Chi Minh reviewed the guard of honor and then rode in an open-top car through the crowd, which stretched for several miles, to the "Guerrilla Palace" residence. The people on both sides cheered enthusiastically to the distinguished guests.

	On the day of arrival, President Ho Chi Minh paid visits to the Albanian government and Labor Party leaders Mehmet Shehu, Hysni Kapo, Beqir Balluku, and others, and paid a visit to People's Assembly Presidium Chairman Haxhi Lleshi.

	Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, August 10th: According to the Yugoslav News Agency, President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and his entourage left Pula by plane on August 9th, ending a five-day friendly visit to Yugoslavia.

### 结束在南斯拉夫的访问  胡志明主席到地拉那

1957-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

    结束在南斯拉夫的访问
    胡志明主席到地拉那
    据新华社地拉那10日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他的随行人员8月9日乘飞机到达地拉那。
    从地拉那市中心区通向机场的道路上，到处是用阿、越两国文字书写的欢迎的标语、胡志明主席的巨幅画像和阿、越两国国旗。
    胡志明主席走下飞机以后，接受了一群儿童的献花，并且同前来欢迎的阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席列希、部长会议主席谢胡、劳动党中央委员会书记处书记卡博等亲切握手。到机场欢迎的还有各国驻阿尔巴尼亚的外交使节。
    胡志明主席在列希主席的陪同下检阅了仪仗队，随后，乘敞篷汽车穿过长达数里的人群前往住所“游击队宫”。两旁的人们向贵宾热烈欢呼。
    在到达的当天，胡志明主席拜会了阿尔巴尼亚政府和劳动党的领导人谢胡、卡博、巴卢库等，拜会了人民议会主席团主席列希。
    据新华社贝尔格莱德10日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社讯：越南民主共和国主席胡志明和随行人员8月9日乘飞机离开普拉，结束了在南斯拉夫五天的友好访问。
