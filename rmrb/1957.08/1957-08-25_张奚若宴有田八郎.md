Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Hachiro Arita
Author:
Date: 1957-08-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet at noon today to welcome Hachiro Arita, advisor to the Japanese Socialist Party.
    Those present to accompany him included Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China; Lei Renmin and Li Zhuchen, Vice Chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Chen Hansheng and Qiao Guanhua, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General.
    Hachiro Arita arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 23rd.

### 张奚若宴有田八郎

1957-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    张奚若宴有田八郎
    新华社24日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今天中午举行宴会，欢迎日本社会党顾问有田八郎。
    出席作陪的有中国红十字会总会会长李德全，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、李烛尘，中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙、乔冠华，秘书长吴茂荪等人。
    有田八郎是在23日下午到达北京的。
