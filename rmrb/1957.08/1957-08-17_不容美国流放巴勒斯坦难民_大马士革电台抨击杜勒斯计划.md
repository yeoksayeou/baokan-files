Title: Do Not Allow the United States to Exile Palestinian Refugees - Damascus Radio Criticizes Dulles Plan
Author:
Date: 1957-08-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 16th. Damascus Radio said on the 15th that the United States government has taken Dulles' plan for resolving the Palestinian Arab refugee problem as the basis of its Middle East policy. This policy mainly uses Israel as a springboard to colonize the entire Middle East region politically or economically.

It is reported that the main contents of this American plan are: repatriating approximately 20,000 Arabs; paying compensation to the remaining refugees who are prepared to permanently reside in Arab countries, especially Iraq; merging the Gaza Strip with Israel; the United States providing financial assistance to Arab countries that will resettle refugees; and providing financial assistance to Israel to help it pay compensation to Arab refugees.

Damascus Radio commented: "The main purpose of the American plan is to force Arab refugees to settle in countries other than their homeland of Palestine. For this purpose, the United States has bribed the ruling groups of some countries with dollars. These people are willing to cooperate with the United States government to realize this plan."

### 不容美国流放巴勒斯坦难民  大马士革电台抨击杜勒斯计划

1957-08-17
第7版()
专栏：

    不容美国流放巴勒斯坦难民
    大马士革电台抨击杜勒斯计划
    新华社大马士革16日电  大马士革电台15日说，美国政府已经把杜勒斯关于解决巴勒斯坦的阿拉伯难民问题的计划当作它在中东政策的基础，这个政策主要是把以色列当作把整个中东地区从政治上或者经济上殖民地化的跳板。
    据报道，这个美国计划的主要内容是：遣返大约两万名阿拉伯人；对于准备永久住在阿拉伯国家特别是伊拉克的其余难民付给补偿；把加沙地带和以色列合并；美国对将有难民定居的阿拉伯国家给予财政援助；给以色列以财政援助，帮助它支付给阿拉伯难民的补偿费。
    大马士革电台评论说：“美国计划的主要目的是强迫阿拉伯难民在他们故乡巴勒斯坦以外的其他国家定居下来。为了这个目的，美国用美元贿赂了一些国家的统治集团，这些人是愿意同美国政府合作以实现这个计划的。”
