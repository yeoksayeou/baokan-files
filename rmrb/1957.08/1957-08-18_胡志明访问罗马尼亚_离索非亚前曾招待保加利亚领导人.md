Title: Ho Chi Minh Visits Romania; Hosted Bulgarian Leaders Before Leaving Sofia
Author:
Date: 1957-08-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 17th: President Ho Chi Minh and his entourage arrived in Bucharest by plane this morning.

At 12:30 PM, President Ho paid a visit to Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Romanian Grand National Assembly, and Pavelescu, Chairman of the Grand National Assembly, and had cordial talks with them.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, August 17th: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and his entourage left Sofia by plane on the morning of the 17th to visit Romania.

When President Ho Chi Minh left Sofia, Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, Yugov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, went to the airport to see him off.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, August 17th: According to reports, President Ho Chi Minh held a grand reception here on the 16th.

Among those present at the reception were Bulgarian party and government leaders Damyanov, Yugov, and Zhivkov, heads of the Agrarian Union and various mass, socio-political organizations, as well as diplomatic envoys from some countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, August 17th: According to the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency, representatives of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Vietnam Workers' Party held talks on August 16 to exchange views on certain issues of the international workers' movement and further strengthening ties between the two parties.

### 胡志明访问罗马尼亚  离索非亚前曾招待保加利亚领导人

1957-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

    胡志明访问罗马尼亚
    离索非亚前曾招待保加利亚领导人
    新华社布加勒斯特17日电  胡志明主席和他的随行人员今天上午乘飞机到达布加勒斯特。
    上午十二时半，胡主席前往拜会了罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团主席格罗查和大国民议会主席帕伏列斯库并且和他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    据新华社索非亚17日电  越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他的随行人员在17日早晨乘飞机离开索非亚前往罗马尼亚访问。
    在胡志明主席离开索非亚的时候，保加利亚国民议会主席团主席达米扬诺夫、部长会议主席于哥夫、保共中央第一书记日夫科夫等曾经到机场送行。
    据新华社索非亚17日电  胡志明主席16日在这里举行了盛大的招待会。
    出席招待会的有保加利亚党政领导人达米扬诺夫、于哥夫、日夫科夫，农民联盟和各群众、社会政治团体的负责人以及一些国家的外交使节等。
    据新华社索非亚17日电  据保加利亚通讯社报道，保加利亚共产党和越南劳动党的代表8月16日举行会谈，就国际工人运动的某些问题和进一步加强两党联系的问题交换了意见。
