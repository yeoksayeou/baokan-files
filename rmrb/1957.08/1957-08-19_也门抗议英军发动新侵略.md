Title: Yemen Protests New British Aggression
Author:
Date: 1957-08-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, August 17th (Tass) - The Yemeni Embassy in London issued a statement protesting the new aggression by British forces against Yemeni territory.

The statement said that British forces, equipped with cannons and machine guns, attacked several locations inside Yemen on August 12th, 13th and 14th. British jet aircraft dropped heavy bombs on Shukair.

The Yemeni Embassy demanded that the British government immediately cease the British military's acts of aggression against Yemeni territory.

According to Egypt's "Al-Ahram" newspaper, the Yemeni authorities have lodged thirty-eight protests in the past year against British aircraft infringing on Yemeni airspace and British aggression against Yemeni territory.

### 也门抗议英军发动新侵略

1957-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    也门抗议英军发动新侵略
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社伦敦17日讯，也门驻伦敦公使馆发表声明，抗议英国军队对也门领土进行新的侵略。
    声明说，配备着大炮和机关枪的英国军队，在8月12日、13日和14日袭击了也门境内的一些地点。英国喷气式飞机向舒克尔投掷了重磅炸弹。
    也门公使馆要求英国政府立即停止英国军队对也门领土的侵略行动。
    据埃及“金字塔”报报道，一年来，也门当局对英国飞机侵犯也门领空和英国侵略也门领土的行为已经提出了三十八次抗议。
