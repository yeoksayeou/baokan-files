    The Sprinkling Tree
    At the end of July, a redbud tree that sprayed water like a small fountain was discovered in Dallas, Texas, USA. The branches of the tree sprayed out lines of clear liquid.
    This tree grows in the yard of a Mr. Warner. Warner said that for two days, his family spent the whole day under this "cooling tree" to escape the summer heat.
    A local tree nursery owner believes that the tree absorbed "too much" water during the heavy rain in the spring and is therefore "eliminating excess moisture."
        (Xinhua News Agency)

### 喷水的树

1957-08-06
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    喷水的树
    7月底的时候，在美国得克萨斯州达拉斯发现一株喷出像小泉水那样的水的红蕾花树，这株树的树枝喷出一线线的明净的液体。
    这株树长在一个华纳先生的家园里。华纳说，在两天中，他家人整天都是在这株“冷却树”下避暑。
    当地一位植树园主认为，这株树是在春天大雨的时候吸进去的水“过多”，因此在“消除过多的水份”。
        （新华社）
