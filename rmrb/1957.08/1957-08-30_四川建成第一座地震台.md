Title: Sichuan Builds First Seismic Observatory

Author:

Date: 1957-08-30
Page: 4

Sichuan Builds First Seismic Observatory
    Chengdu Seismic Observatory, Sichuan's first seismic observatory built this year, has reported earthquake information more than ten times to the Institute of Geophysics, Chinese Academy of Sciences in Beijing.
    The Chengdu Seismic Observatory's current set of Soviet-made seismographs are the most advanced in the world and can observe earthquakes around the world and in the vicinity of Chengdu. It has already recorded an earthquake approximately 9,000 kilometers away from Chengdu. The data on earthquake conditions observed by this seismic observatory are of great significance to the economic construction of Sichuan and the Southwest region. (Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu)

### 四川建成第一座地震台

1957-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　四川建成第一座地震台
    今年建成的四川第一个地震台——成都地震台，已经向北京中国科学院地球物理研究所报告了十几次地震情况。
    成都地震台现有的一套苏联出产的地震仪，是目前世界上最新式的，能够观测世界各地和成都附近地区的地震。它已经收到了一次离成都约九千公里远的地震。这座地震台观测到的地震情况的资料对四川和西南一带的经济建设有很重要的意义。　（新华社成都电）
