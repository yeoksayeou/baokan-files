Title: Further Develop the Struggle to Ban Nuclear Weapons: Hiroshima Holds Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs
Author:
Date: 1957-08-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hiroshima, 7th Dispatch from TASS: The city of Hiroshima held a conference against atomic and hydrogen bombs on August 6th. Approximately three thousand people participated, including representatives from various social groups and political parties in Japan, cultural, scientific, and artistic workers, as well as representatives from India, the United States, Australia, and West Germany, who were attending the Third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament.

The Mayor of Hiroshima, Watanabe, the Governor of Hiroshima Prefecture, Ohara, and representatives from the Japanese Socialist Party and Communist Party, among others, spoke at the conference.

The Socialist Party representative, Kato, called for further strengthening the struggle for peace and opposing the conspiracies of reactionary forces in Japan and the United States.

Australian peace activist Goldbloom pointed out that the atomic bombing of Hiroshima was intended to intimidate the people of other countries. He said that the atomic attack only further aroused the worldwide wave of the peace movement. He stated that now, every country in the world is voicing demands for universal peace and a ban on nuclear weapons.

The U.S. representative, Delamarter, said at the conference that the movement for peace is expanding in the United States. He said that U.S. peace advocates oppose the plans for atomic war and demand the immediate declaration of atomic and hydrogen weapons as illegal.

The conference discussed ways to further develop the struggle to ban nuclear weapons and adopted a complete and detailed resolution on this issue.

### 进一步开展争取禁止核武器的斗争  广岛举行禁止原子弹和氢弹大会

1957-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

    进一步开展争取禁止核武器的斗争
    广岛举行禁止原子弹和氢弹大会
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社广岛讯：广岛市在8月6日举行了禁止原子弹和氢弹大会。参加大会的有日本各社会团体和政党的代表，文化、科学和艺术工作者，以及参加第三届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取裁军世界大会的印度、美国、澳大利亚和西德的代表共约三千人。
    广岛市长渡边、广岛县知事大原以及日本社会党和共产党的代表等人在会上讲了话。
    社会党代表加藤在会上呼吁进一步加强争取和平的斗争，反对日本和美国反动势力的阴谋。
    澳大利亚和平运动活动家果耳德布路姆指出，用原子弹轰炸广岛是为了恐吓其他国家的人民。他说，但是，原子弹的袭击只不过更加激起了全世界争取和平运动的浪潮。他说，现在，世界上每一个国家都发出了要求普遍和平和禁止核武器的呼声。
    美国代表德拉马尔特尔在会上说，争取和平的运动在美国日益扩大。他说，美国和平拥护者反对原子战争的计划，并且要求立即宣布原子武器和氢武器为非法。
    大会讨论了关于进一步开展争取禁止核武器斗争的途径问题，并且就这个问题通过了一项完全而详尽的决议。
