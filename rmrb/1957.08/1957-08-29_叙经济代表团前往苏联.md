Title: Syrian Economic Delegation Travels to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-08-29
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 28th: A Syrian delegation led by Hassan Keppar, Chairman of the Syrian Economic Development Committee, left Damascus on the morning of August 27th for Moscow. The delegation will discuss and finalize the details of the aid program recently signed with the Soviet Union.

### 叙经济代表团前往苏联

1957-08-29
第7版()
专栏：

　  叙经济代表团前往苏联
    新华社大马士革28日电　由叙利亚经济发展委员会主席哈桑·基卜拉率领的叙利亚代表团8月27日上午离开大马士革前往莫斯科。代表团将在那里讨论与确定最近同苏联签订的援助计划的细节。
