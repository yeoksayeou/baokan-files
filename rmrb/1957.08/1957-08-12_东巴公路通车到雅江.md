Title: Dongba Highway Opens to Yajiang
Author:
Date: 1957-08-12
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, August 10th: The Dong (E'eluo) Ba (Tang) Highway, the southern artery of the Ganzi Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture in Sichuan Province, has recently opened to Yajiang, an ancient city on the banks of the Yalong River, after five months of construction. This is the first city on the Dongba Highway.
    The 72-kilometer section from Dong'eluo on the Kangzang Highway to Yajiang passes through the 4,600-meter-high Ga'ersi Mountain and the 4,500-meter-high Jianziwan Mountain, as well as some narrow river valleys and virgin forests.
    The Dongba Highway starts from Dong'eluo on the Kangzang Highway and ends at Batang on the banks of the Jinsha River, with a total length of 400 kilometers. This year, it is planned to build 199 kilometers from Dong'eluo to Litang.

### 东巴公路通车到雅江

1957-08-12
第5版()
专栏：

    东巴公路通车到雅江
    新华社成都10日电　四川省甘孜藏族自治州南部的大动脉——东（俄洛）巴（塘）公路，经过五个月的修筑，最近通车到雅砻江岸的古城——雅江。这是东巴公路上经过的第一座城市。
    从康藏公路上的东俄洛到雅江，长七十二公里，沿线要经过拔海四千六百公尺的高尔寺山和四千五百公尺的剪子湾山，还要经过一些狭窄的河谷和原始森林。
    东巴公路的起点是康藏公路上的东俄洛，终点是金沙江岸的巴塘，全线四百公里。今年计划从东俄洛修到理塘共一百九十九公里。
