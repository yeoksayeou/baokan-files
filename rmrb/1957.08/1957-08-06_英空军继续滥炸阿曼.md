Title: British Air Force Continues to Indiscriminately Bomb Oman
Author:
Date: 1957-08-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: Manama (in Bahrain) dispatch: British authorities announced in Manama on August 4th that the British Air Force continued to launch attacks on Omani insurgents on August 3rd.

A British official spokesman admitted on the same day that a British "Shackleton" bomber was attacked by Omani insurgents' rifles in the tail when it attacked the skies over the area controlled by Omani insurgents on August 3rd. He said no one was injured.

### 英空军继续滥炸阿曼

1957-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    英空军继续滥炸阿曼
    据新华社5日讯　马纳马（在巴林岛）消息：英国当局8月4日在马纳马发表的一项公报说，英国空军在8月3日继续出动袭击阿曼起义者。
    英国官方发言人在同一天承认，英国一架“沙克莱顿式”轰炸机8月3日在阿曼起义者控制地区的上空进行袭击时，它的尾部被阿曼起义者的步枪击中。他说，没有人受伤。
