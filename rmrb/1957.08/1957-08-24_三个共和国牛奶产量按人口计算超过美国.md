Title: Milk Production Per Capita Exceeds US Levels in Three Republics
Author:
Date: 1957-08-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 23rd. According to per capita calculations, milk production in three Soviet republics has significantly surpassed that of the United States. These three republics are Latvia, Estonia, and Lithuania, located along the Baltic coast.

Last year, the average milk production per capita in the United States was 343 kilograms, while Latvia produced 522 kilograms, Estonia 540 kilograms, and Lithuania 402 kilograms. This year, the milk production in these three republics is expected to exceed that of the United States even further.

According to calculations, the average milk production per capita in the Ukrainian Republic of the Soviet Union will also surpass that of the United States this year. It is estimated that the average milk production per capita in the entire Soviet Union may catch up with that of the United States next year.

In the past two years, the total milk production in the Soviet Union has increased by 30%, representing an increase of 11 million tons. The task of milk storage for the first half of this year was completed 8% above target and ten days ahead of schedule.

### 三个共和国牛奶产量按人口计算超过美国

1957-08-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　三个共和国牛奶产量按人口计算超过美国
    新华社莫斯科23日电　按人口平均计算，苏联已经有三个共和国的牛奶产量大大地超过了美国。这三个共和国是波罗的海沿岸的拉脱维亚、爱沙尼亚和立陶宛。
    去年美国按人口平均的牛奶产量是三百四十三公斤，而拉脱维亚是五百二十二公斤，爱沙尼亚是五百四十公斤，立陶宛是四百零二公斤。今年这三个共和国的牛奶产量，还要进一步超过美国。
    根据计算，苏联的乌克兰共和国今年按人口平均的牛奶产量也将会超过美国。整个苏联按人口平均的牛奶产量预计在明年就有可能赶上美国。
    在最近两年内，苏联的牛奶总产量已经提高了30％，即增加了一千一百万吨。今年上半年的牛奶储存量任务已经提前十天超额8％完成。
