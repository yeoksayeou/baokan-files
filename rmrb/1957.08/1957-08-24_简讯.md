    Brief News
　  Chinese Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Sofia
　  The Chinese agricultural delegation led by Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan arrived in Sofia by plane on August 20 at the invitation of the Bulgarian Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry.
    North Korea and India Reach Trade Agreement
    Chen Banxiu, head of the trade delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Sandilya, manager of the Indian State Trading Corporation, exchanged letters in New Delhi on August 19 regarding the promotion of trade between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and India. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 简讯

1957-08-24
第6版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
　  中国农业代表团到索非亚
　  由农业部长廖鲁言率领的中国农业代表团应保加利亚农林部的邀请，8月20日乘飞机到达索非亚。
    朝鲜和印度达成贸易协议
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国贸易代表团团长陈班秀和印度国家贸易公司经理山迪利亚8月19日在新德里交换了关于促进朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和印度之间的贸易的信件。　（新华社）
