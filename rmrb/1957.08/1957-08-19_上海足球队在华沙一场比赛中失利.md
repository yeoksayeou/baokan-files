Title: Shanghai Football Team Loses Match in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1957-08-19
Page: 3
Sports News

    Shanghai Football Team Loses Match in Warsaw
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 18th. The Shanghai football team lost to the Warsaw football team by a score of 3-1 in a match on the 17th.
    The Shanghai team scored a goal fifteen minutes into the first half. The Warsaw team then scored a goal, tying the score. In the second half, although the Shanghai team worked hard on both offense and defense, they were scored on twice by the Warsaw team due to their poorer physical condition.
    In addition to the spectators in the stadium stands, many Warsaw citizens watched the game on television that day.
    The Shanghai football team visited Poland after participating in the World Youth Festival football competition in Moscow.

### 上海足球队在华沙一场比赛中失利

1957-08-19
第3版()
专栏：体育新闻

    上海足球队在华沙一场比赛中失利
    新华社华沙18日电　上海足球队17日在一场比赛中以三比一败给华沙足球队。
    在上半场开始十五分钟的时候，上海队踢入一球。华沙队接着也踢入一球，打成平局。下半场，上海队虽然奋力攻守，但是因体力较差而被华沙队攻入两球。
    这一天除了运动场看台上的观众外，很多华沙市民通过电视也看到了这场球赛。
    上海足球队是在参加了在莫斯科举行的世界青年联欢节足球比赛后到波兰访问的。
