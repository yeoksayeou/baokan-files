Title: Bucharest Holds Military Parade and Procession to Celebrate Romanian National Day
Author:
Date: 1957-08-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 24th: A grand military parade and procession were held in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, on the morning of August 23rd to celebrate National Day. Groza, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Stoica, and other leaders of the Romanian Workers' Party and the state reviewed the troops.

Before the start of the military parade, General Salajan, Minister of the Armed Forces of Romania, addressed the armed forces participating in the review and the marching contingents.

At 8:20 AM, the parade commenced. Leading the way were students from various military academies, followed by armored troops, paratroopers, artillery, and other units. Jet formations soared over the reviewing stand, and tanks rumbled past. Following closely behind were the workers' armed self-defense forces, dressed in blue uniforms, with orderly steps and vibrant spirit.

The mass procession then began. The marching crowds shouted, "August 23rd brought us freedom!" "The Workers' Party is our hope!" "The Soviet Union is a bastion of peace!" (Attached picture)

### 布加勒斯特举行阅兵和游行　庆祝罗马尼亚国庆

1957-08-25
第6版()
专栏：

　　布加勒斯特举行阅兵和游行
　　庆祝罗马尼亚国庆
    据新华社布加勒斯特24日电　罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特8月23日上午举行了盛大的阅兵和游行，庆祝国庆。格罗查、乔治乌—德治、斯托伊卡等罗马尼亚工人党和国家的领导人检阅了队伍。
    在开始阅兵以前，罗马尼亚武装部队部长萨拉扬上将向参加检阅的武装部队和游行队伍讲了话。
    八点二十分，阅兵分列式开始。走在最前面的是各军官学校的学生，后边是装甲兵、伞兵、炮兵等部队。喷气机群从检阅台上空掠过，坦克在检阅台前隆隆驶过。接踵而来的是身穿蓝制服、步伐整齐、精神焕发的工人武装自卫队。
    群众游行接着开始了。游行队伍高呼“八·二三给我们带来了自由！”“工人党是我们的希望！”“苏联是和平的堡垒！”（附图片）
