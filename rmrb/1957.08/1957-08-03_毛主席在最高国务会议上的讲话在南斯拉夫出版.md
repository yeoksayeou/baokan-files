Title: Mao Zedong's Speech at the Supreme State Conference Published in Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 5

    Belgrade, August 2nd (By Our Correspondent) - Chairman Mao Zedong's speech at the Supreme State Conference, "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People," has recently been translated into Macedonian and published in full by the Skopje Cultural Publishing House, the capital of the Macedonian Republic of Yugoslavia. At the same time, this publishing house also published Comrade Lu Dingyi's article "Let a Hundred Flowers Bloom, Let a Hundred Schools of Thought Contend" in full in Macedonian.

### 毛主席在最高国务会议上的讲话在南斯拉夫出版

1957-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席在最高国务会议上的讲话
    在南斯拉夫出版
    本报贝尔格莱德2日电　毛泽东主席在最高国务会议上的讲演“关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题”最近已由南斯拉夫马其顿共和国首府斯科普里文化出版社译成马其顿文全文出版。同时，这家出版社还用马其顿文全文出版了陆定一同志的“百花齐放、百家争鸣”一文。
