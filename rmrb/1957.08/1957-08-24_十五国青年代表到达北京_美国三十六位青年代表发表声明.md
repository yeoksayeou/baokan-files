Title: Youth Representatives from Fifteen Countries Arrive in Beijing; Thirty-Six American Youth Representatives Issue Statement
Author:
Date: 1957-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: Ninety-six youth representatives from thirteen countries arrived in Beijing by special train this evening. They are visiting China after attending the Sixth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival in Moscow.

These youth representatives are from India (6), Burma (5), Nepal (1), Ceylon (1), the Philippines (3), Syria (4), Lebanon (3), Morocco (3), West Africa (1), the United States (41), West Germany (6), Australia (20), and New Zealand (2). Twenty-four Indonesian youth representatives also arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They will return home after a short visit to China. One Canadian youth representative, who was invited to visit China after the festival, arrived in Beijing on the 22nd. The youth representatives from various countries will be hosted by the All-China Youth Federation.

At 6:15 PM, the train slowly entered the station to the music of the "March of Democratic Youth." More than 600 people from the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, and youth from all walks of life in Beijing, who were at the station to welcome the guests, enthusiastically cheered and applauded.

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: After arriving in Beijing to visit China following their participation in the Sixth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, thirty-six of the forty-one American youth representatives jointly issued a statement to Chinese and foreign reporters. The statement said:

"We wish to express our respect to our hosts, the All-China Youth Federation, and the Chinese people. We are delighted to have this opportunity to visit China. The world has long recognized China's long and rich cultural heritage, and we are particularly interested in learning how these ancient traditions are integrated with the many important new developments we have already learned about. However, we have come here not only to learn and bring our impressions of modern China back to the United States, but also to try to give the Chinese people some understanding of our country. We believe that mutual understanding is best promoted through all kinds of personal friendships. We look forward to the time when there will be free exchanges between China and the United States, and we believe that our visit is an important step in this direction." (Photo attached)

Our youth warmly welcomed young friends from various countries at the station with flowers, applause, and cheers. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Chengjian.

### 十五国青年代表到达北京　美国三十六位青年代表发表声明

1957-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　十五国青年代表到达北京
  　美国三十六位青年代表发表声明
    据新华社23日讯　十三个国家的九十六位青年代表今天晚上乘专车到达北京。他们是在莫斯科参加第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节后来中国访问的。
    这些青年代表是来自印度（六人）、缅甸（五人）、尼泊尔（一人）、锡兰（一人）、菲律宾（三人）、叙利亚（四人）、黎巴嫩（三人）、摩洛哥（三人）、西非（一人）、美国（四十一人）、西德（六人）、澳大利亚（二十人）和新西兰（二人）。印度尼西亚青年代表二十四人也已在今天下午乘飞机到达北京，他们将在中国进行短期访问后返国。在联欢节后应邀来中国访问的加拿大青年代表一人已在22日到达北京。各国青年代表们将受到中华全国民主青年联合会的接待。
    下午六时一刻，列车在民主青年进行曲的乐曲声中徐徐进站。在车站欢迎的中华全国民主青年联合会和中华全国学生联合会的负责人以及北京各界青年六百多人，向客人们热情地欢呼和鼓掌。
    新华社23日讯　在参加第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节后来中国访问的美国青年代表四十一人在到达北京后，其中有三十六人联名向中外记者发表了声明。声明说：
    我们希望向招待我们的主人中华全国民主青年联合会和中国人民表示敬意。我们很高兴能有这个机会访问中国。世界长久以来就承认了中国悠久的、丰富的文化财产，而我们特别感到兴趣的是学习这些古老的传统是如何同我们已经学习到的许多重要的新发展融合起来。但是，我们这次前来并不仅仅是为了学习以及把我们对现代中国的印象带回美国，我们同时也为了设法让中国人民对我们的国家有某些了解。我们认为，通过各种各样个人的友谊往来是最能够促进互相的了解的。我们期望着中国和美国之间自由交往的时期的到来，我们认为我们的访问是这方面的一个重要的步骤。（附图片）
　　我国青年在车站上用鲜花、掌声和欢呼来热烈欢迎各国青年朋友。　新华社记者　于澄建摄
