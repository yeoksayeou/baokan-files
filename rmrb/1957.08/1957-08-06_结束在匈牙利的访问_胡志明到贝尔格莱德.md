Title: End of Visit to Hungary: Ho Chi Minh Arrives in Belgrade
Author:
Date: 1957-08-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 5th. President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh, and his entourage, have left Budapest by plane today after a four-day visit to Hungary.
    Hungarian party and state leaders, including Kádár, Dobi, Münnich, and Marosán, were at the airport to see them off.
    Yesterday afternoon, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and General Secretary of the Workers' Party, Ho Chi Minh, held talks with the Prime Minister of the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party, János Kádár. Present at the meeting were Hoàng Văn Hoan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and Marosán, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party.
    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 5th. President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh, arrived in Belgrade by plane today from Budapest.
    Those at the airport to welcome him included: President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, Josip Broz Tito, President of the Federal People's Assembly, Petar Stambolić, and Vice Presidents of the Federal Executive Council, Edvard Kardelj, Aleksandar Ranković, and Svetozar Vukmanović-Tempo.
    President Ho Chi Minh delivered a speech at the airport. He expressed his belief that his meeting with President Tito will further strengthen the friendly relations between the peoples of Vietnam and Yugoslavia.

### 结束在匈牙利的访问  胡志明到贝尔格莱德

1957-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    结束在匈牙利的访问
    胡志明到贝尔格莱德
    据新华社布达佩斯5日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他的随行人员在匈牙利进行了四天的访问后，已经在今天乘飞机离开布达佩斯。
    到机场欢送的有卡达尔、道比、明尼赫、马罗山等匈牙利党和国家的领导人。
    昨天下午，越南民主共和国主席、劳动党总书记胡志明同匈牙利工农革命政府总理、社会主义工人党中央委员会第一书记卡达尔进行了谈话。在座的有：越南劳动党中央政治局委员黄文欢和匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员马罗山。
    新华社贝尔格莱德5日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明今天从布达佩斯乘飞机到达贝尔格莱德。
    到机场迎接的有：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托，联邦国民议会主席斯坦鲍利奇，联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔、兰科维奇、伏克曼诺维奇等。
    胡志明主席在机场发表了谈话。他表示相信他同铁托总统的会见将进一步加强越、南两国人民的友好关系。
