Title: Weightlifters Chang Guanqun and Xu Honglin Create New National Records in Snatch and Clean & Jerk
Author:
Date: 1957-08-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 26th. Forty-five-year-old heavyweight weightlifter Chang Guanqun, on the evening of the 25th in Shanghai, with a snatch of 103 kg and a clean & jerk of 150 kg, broke his own national records of 102.5 kg and 142.5 kg set last year.

　　Chang Guanqun's two new national records were achieved during a competition with the Indonesian weightlifting team. On the 25th, Chang Guanqun's third clean & jerk was 145.5 kg, which not only exceeded the national record but also surpassed the heavyweight weightlifter Wang Fuman's new heavyweight clean & jerk record (145 kg) created in April this year by half a kilogram. Subsequently, Chang Guanqun requested a fourth clean & jerk, lifting 150 kg.

　　The Indonesian weightlifting team visited China again after participating in the 3rd International Youth Friendship Games in Moscow. On the evening of the 25th, they held a friendly competition with Shanghai weightlifters. In the seven weight classes, Shanghai athletes won first place in five weight classes, and Indonesian athletes won first place in two weight classes.

　　The Indonesian weightlifting team arrived in Shanghai on the 23rd and is scheduled to leave Shanghai for Guangzhou on the 28th.

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 26th. The four Chinese youth teams (tennis, badminton, table tennis, and weightlifting) and the visiting group of the Chinese Youth Sports Delegation who participated in the 3rd International Youth Friendship Games have returned to Beijing from Moscow on the 21st, 22nd, and 26th, respectively.

　　Before returning to China, the Chinese Youth Weightlifting Team visited and competed in the city of Alma-Ata in the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic, where featherweight athlete Xu Honglin also broke the national record of 98 kg in the two-hand snatch with a result of 102.5 kg.

### 举重运动员常冠群、徐鸿林　创造抓举、挺举全国新纪录

1957-08-27
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

　  举重运动员常冠群、徐鸿林
　　创造抓举、挺举全国新纪录
    新华社上海26日电　四十五岁的重量级举重运动员常冠群，25日
  晚上在上海以抓举一百零三公斤和挺举一百五十公斤的成绩，打破了他去年创造的一百零二点五公斤和一百四十二点五公斤的全国纪录。
    常冠群的这两个全国新成绩，是他参加同印度尼西亚举重队比赛时创造的。25日，常冠群第三次挺举起的重量是一百四十五点五公斤，不但超过了全国纪录，而且已经比重量级举重运动员王富满在今年4月间创造的重量级挺举新成绩（一百四十五公斤）多了半公斤。接着，常冠群要求第四次挺举时，举起了一百五十公斤。
    印度尼西亚举重队是到莫斯科参加了第三届国际青年友谊运动会后，再次应邀到中国访问的。25日晚上他们同上海举重运动员举行了友谊比赛。在七个等级的比赛中，上海选手得到了五个等级的第一名，印度尼西亚选手得到两个等级的第一名。
    印度尼西亚举重队是23日到上海的，他们预定28日离上海去广州访问。
    新华社26日讯　参加第三届国际青年友谊运动会的中国青年网球、羽毛球、乒乓球、举重等四个队和中国青年体育代表团的参观组，已经在21日、22日和26日先后由莫斯科回到北京。
    中国青年举重队回国前在苏联哈萨克加盟共和国阿拉木图城市的访问比赛中，次轻量级运动员徐鸿林还以一百○二点五公斤的成绩，打破了双手抓举九十八公斤的全国纪录。
