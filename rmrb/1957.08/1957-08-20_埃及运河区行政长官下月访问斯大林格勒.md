Title: Egyptian Canal Zone Administrator to Visit Stalingrad Next Month
Author:
Date: 1957-08-20
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 19th: According to the Middle East News Agency, the Egyptian administrator of the Suez Canal region, General Abdel Aziz Safwat, and the chief engineer of the canal authority, have been invited to visit Stalingrad in September.

The Stalingrad municipal authorities hope to express their admiration for the heroic resistance of Port Said and the Suez Canal region cities against imperialist aggression.

### 埃及运河区行政长官下月访问斯大林格勒

1957-08-20
第7版()
专栏：

    埃及运河区行政长官下月访问斯大林格勒
    据新华社19日讯　开罗消息：据中东通讯社报道，苏伊士运河地区的埃及行政长官阿卜杜勒·阿齐兹·萨法特将军和运河机构的总工程师应邀将在9月间前往斯大林格勒访问。
    斯大林格勒市政当局希望借此表示对塞得港和苏伊士运河地区城市英勇抵抗帝国主义侵略的钦佩。
