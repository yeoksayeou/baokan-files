Title: Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theatre Personnel Arrive in Beijing; Opening Ceremony of Performance Tour to be Held in Shanghai on September 1st
Author:
Date: 1957-08-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th - The Soviet Novosibirsk State Opera and Ballet Theatre troupe, totaling one hundred and seventy people, have all arrived in Beijing as of today. They will depart for Shanghai on the 28th, and the opening ceremony for their performance tour of China will be held in Shanghai on September 1st.

The large artistic delegation is led by Ligachev, Deputy Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Soviet Novosibirsk Oblast Soviet, with Zelimanov, Director of the Novosibirsk State Opera and Ballet Theatre, as the deputy leader. This theatre is one of the top opera and ballet theatres in the Soviet Union. Among those who welcomed them at the airport today were Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan and nearly one hundred Chinese singers and dancers. (Attached)

### 新西伯利亚歌剧芭蕾舞剧院人员到京　9月1日将在上海举行访问演出开幕式

1957-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　新西伯利亚歌剧芭蕾舞剧院人员到京
　　9月1日将在上海举行访问演出开幕式
    据新华社27日讯　苏联新西伯利亚国立歌剧芭蕾舞剧院一行一百七十人，到今天为止已经全部到达北京。他们将在28日前往上海，9月1日将在上海举行访问中国演出的开幕式。
    这个大型艺术团由苏联新西伯利亚州苏维埃执行委员会副主席利加乔夫担任团长，副团长是新西伯利亚国立歌剧芭蕾舞剧院院长泽利马诺夫。这个剧院是苏联第一流的歌剧芭蕾舞剧院之一。今天到飞机场欢迎的有文化部副部长夏衍以及中国的歌舞演员等近一百人。（附）
　　苏联新西伯利亚国家歌舞剧院芭蕾舞蹈团团员在机场和欢迎者合影。
                                  本报记者　曹桂江摄
