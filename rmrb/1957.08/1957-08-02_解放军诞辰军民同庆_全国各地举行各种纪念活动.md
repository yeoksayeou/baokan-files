Title: Army and People Celebrate PLA's Anniversary: Various Commemorative Activities Held Across the Country
Author:
Date: 1957-08-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 1st: The vast number of officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed throughout the country, together with the people of the whole country, warmly celebrated the 30th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day.

The birthplace of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Nanchang, held a foundation laying ceremony for the "August 1st" Memorial Tower today, as well as a celebration rally with more than 3,000 people.

The headquarters directly subordinate organs, troops and military units stationed in Beijing of the People's Liberation Army held various activities to warmly celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the army.

In Shanghai, on this festive day, 3,500 citizens braved the rain to visit naval vessels at the Bund on the Huangpu River. They stood on the decks, listening quietly to naval soldiers recount the history and combat deeds of the ships. Military-civilian get-togethers were held everywhere in Shanghai's districts today. In the evening, a large-scale radio gala brought the entire city into a joyful climax. Yesterday evening, people from all walks of life in Shanghai held a celebration rally for the 30th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day.

In Guangzhou, an air force flight demonstration was held this morning, and 15,000 people, including representatives of the Guangdong Province, people from all walks of life in Guangzhou, overseas Chinese returning to China for visits, and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macau, watched the performance. This evening, Guangzhou held a garden party and get-together with more than 30,000 participants. The exhibition of military history pictures and weapon models for the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army also opened tonight. People from all walks of life in Guangzhou also held a celebration meeting last night for the 30th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day.

In Jinan, more than 25,000 people from all walks of life in Shandong Province and Jinan City held a celebration rally last night. Tan Qilong, Secretary of the Shandong Provincial Party Committee, and General Wang Xinting, head of the Jinan garrison, spoke at the meeting. A grand recreational event and dance party were held in the evening. Today, the military history exhibition sponsored by the Jinan garrison officially began receiving visitors. On this festive day, the navy stationed in Qingdao also held a maritime armed performance.

More than 4,500 party, government, military and civilian personnel from Liaoning Province and Shenyang City held a celebration rally last night. After the meeting, a rich and colorful military-government-civilian get-together was held.

In recent days, various districts in Shenyang City have also held military-government-civilian get-togethers or garden parties. Many factories, government agencies, schools, and street bulletin boards have published special issues celebrating "August 1st."

In Wuhan, a celebration rally was held before "August 1st." During the celebration of the "August 1st" Army Day, on the morning of July 31, representatives from the Wuhan garrison and all walks of life in Hubei Province and Wuhan City also made a special trip to the cemetery of the Soviet Air Force Volunteer Martyrs to lay wreaths and pay their respects.

People from all walks of life in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City held a celebration meeting on the eve of the festival. Hui Yuyu, Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Party Committee and Governor of Jiangsu Province, called on everyone to continue strengthening military-civilian unity and learn from the fine tradition of hard struggle of the People's Liberation Army.

In Lanzhou, about 20,000 people from more than ten ethnic groups, including Han, Hui, Tibetan, Uygur, Mongolian, and Dongxiang, gathered in the Five Springs Mountain Park yesterday afternoon to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day. After the meeting, a garden party was held, and more than a dozen troupes performed wonderful programs.

In various parts of Xinjiang, "August 1st" Army Day celebrations were also launched. Governments and people in Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture and Kashgar Prefecture in southern Xinjiang, among other places, sent condolences to the border troops before "August 1st." The Xinjiang Military Region also specially dispatched cultural troupes and acrobatic troupes to Kashgar and other places to perform for the border soldiers. In Urumqi, the Xinjiang Military Region organs and local troops have been holding get-togethers with factories, agricultural cooperatives, and schools such as the July 1st Cotton Textile Factory for the past two days.

A celebration rally with more than 1,000 participants was held today in Altay, a major town in the northwest border region of the motherland. A delegation from the Soviet Zaisan Border Guard in Kazakhstan, headed by Major Bukhanov, was also invited to attend the meeting.

Tonight, the Skills Team of the Cultural Troupe of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army will perform programs to entertain local troops and Soviet friends.

Troops stationed in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Tianjin, Chongqing, Xining, and other regions also held various celebration activities today or on the eve of the festival.

### 解放军诞辰军民同庆  全国各地举行各种纪念活动

1957-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    解放军诞辰军民同庆
    全国各地举行各种纪念活动
    据新华社1日讯　驻守全国各地的中国人民解放军各部队广大官兵，和全国人民一道，热烈庆祝“八一”建军节三十周年。
    中国人民解放军诞生地——南昌，今天举行了“八一”纪念塔奠基典礼以及三千多人的庆祝大会。
    人民解放军总部直属机关、部队和驻京各军事单位，举行各种活动热烈庆祝建军三十周年。
    在上海，节日这天，有三千五百多市民冒雨前往黄浦江外滩，参观了海军舰艇。他们立在甲板上，静听海军战士讲舰艇的历史和战斗事迹。上海各区今天到处都在举行军民联欢会。晚上，一个规模很大的广播联欢大会把全市卷入了欢乐的高潮。昨天晚上，上海市各界人民还举行了“八一”建军节三十周年庆祝大会。
    在广州，今天上午举行了空军飞行表演，广东省人民代表和广州市各界人民以及回国参观的华侨和港澳同胞一万五千多人参观了表演。今天晚上，广州市举行了有三万多人参加的游园联欢大会。中国人民解放军建军三十周年军史图片、兵器模型展览会，也在今晚开幕。广州市各界人民昨晚还举行了庆祝“八一”建军节三十周年大会。
    在济南，山东省和济南市各界人民二万五千多人昨晚举行了庆祝大会。中共山东省委书记处书记谭启龙和驻济南部队首长王新亭上将都在会上讲了话。晚上举行了盛大的游艺会和联欢舞会。今天，济南驻军主办的军史展览会开始正式接待参观的人群。节日这天，驻青岛海军还举行了海上武装表演。
    辽宁省、沈阳市党政军民四千五百多人昨晚举行了庆祝大会。会后，举行了丰富多采的军政民联欢大会。
    连日来，沈阳市各区还先后举行了军政民联欢会或游园会。许多工厂、机关、学校和街道的黑板报都出了庆祝“八一”的专刊。
    在武汉，“八一”前夕举行了庆祝大会。在庆祝“八一”建军节时，7月31日上午，驻武汉部队和湖北省、武汉市各界代表还特地前往苏联空军志愿队烈士墓献花圈致敬。
    江苏省和南京市各界人民在节日前夜举行庆祝会。中共江苏省委书记处书记、江苏省省长惠浴宇号召大家继续加强军民团结，学习人民解放军的艰苦奋斗的优良传统。
    在兰州，汉、回、藏、维吾尔、蒙古、东乡等十多个民族约二万人，昨天下午在五泉山公园集会庆祝“八一”建军节三十周年。会后，举行了游园，十几个剧团表演了精采节目。
    在新疆各地，也展开了“八一”建军节的庆祝活动。伊犁哈萨克族自治州和南疆喀什专区等地政府和人民，在“八一”前对边防军进行了慰问，新疆军区并特别派出了文工团、杂技团前去喀什等地为边防战士作慰问演出。在乌鲁木齐市，新疆军区机关和当地驻军两天来和七一棉纺织厂等工厂、农业社、学校进行联欢活动。
    祖国西北边疆重镇阿勒泰今天举行了一千多人参加的庆祝大会。以布卡诺夫少校为首的苏联驻哈萨克斯坦斋桑边防军代表团也应邀参加了这个大会。
    今晚中国人民解放军总政治部文工团技艺队演出节目，招待当地驻军和苏联友人。
    驻内蒙古自治区、天津、重庆、西宁以及其他地区的部队，也都在今天或节日前夕举行了各种庆祝活动。
