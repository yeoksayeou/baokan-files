Title: "August 1st" Men's Basketball Team Wins Again in Romania
Author:
Date: 1957-08-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 22nd. The Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Men's Basketball Team defeated the Romanian National Youth Mixed Team again on the 21st with a score of 78 to 57.

At the start of the game, the "August 1st" team once led 6-2, 8-2, and 12-7. Later, the Romanian team made more mid-range and long-range shots, tying the score several times. After the "August 1st" team adopted a fast-break tactic, they finally led by thirteen points in the first half.

In the second half, the Romanian team was eager for quick results and made continuous mistakes. The "August 1st" team took the opportunity to attack, resulting in a victory by twenty-one points more than the Romanian team.

### “八一”男子篮球队在罗马尼亚再次获胜

1957-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　“八一”男子篮球队在罗马尼亚再次获胜
    据新华社布加勒斯特22日电  中国人民解放军“八一”男子篮球队21日以七十八比五十七再次胜罗马尼亚国家青年混合队。
    比赛开始，“八一”队曾经以六比二、八比二、十二比七领先，以后罗马尼亚队中距和远距投篮命中较多，数次打成平局。“八一”队采取快攻战术后，上半场终于领先十三分。
    下半场罗马尼亚队急于求成，连续发生错误，“八一”队乘机进攻，结果比罗马尼亚队多二十一分获胜。
