Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Host Banquet for Chairman Ho Chi Minh
Author:
Date: 1957-08-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, and Chen Yun, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, hosted a banquet tonight for Chairman Ho Chi Minh.

Also present on the Chinese side were Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, and He Long, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; and Luo Guibo, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam.

Hoang Minh Giam, Vietnamese Minister of Culture; Hoang Van Hoan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party; Pham Ngoc Thach, Deputy Minister of Health and Economy of Vietnam; and Nguyen Khang, Vietnamese Ambassador to China, were also invited to attend.

### 中共中央领导人宴请胡志明主席

1957-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人宴请胡志明主席
    新华社29日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云今晚宴请了胡志明主席。
    中国方面出席的，还有中共中央政治局委员邓小平、彭真、贺龙，中共中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆，中国驻越南大使罗贵波。
    越南文化部长黄明鉴，越南劳动党中央政治局委员黄文欢，越南医济部副部长范玉石和越南驻中国大使阮康也应邀出席。
