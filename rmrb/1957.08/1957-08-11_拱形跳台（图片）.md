Title: Arched Diving Platform (Photo)
Author: Guo Weiqi
Date: 1957-08-11
Page: 4

    Arched Diving Platform
    The Guangzhou Sports Club's swimming pool has built a state-of-the-art arched diving platform. The platform has six diving boards ranging from three meters to ten meters high, accommodating six people diving at the same time.
    Photo by Guo Weiqi (Submitted by Southern Daily)

### 拱形跳台（图片）
郭伟祺
1957-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    拱形跳台
    广州市体育俱乐部的游泳池建有一座全国最新式的拱形跳台。跳台有从三公尺到十公尺高的六块跳板，可同时容纳六个人一齐跳水。
    郭伟祺摄（南方日报社稿）
