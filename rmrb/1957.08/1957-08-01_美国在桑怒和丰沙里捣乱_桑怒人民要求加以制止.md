Title: The United States stirs up trouble in Sam Neua and Phongsaly. The people of Sam Neua demand it be stopped.
Author:
Date: 1957-08-01
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 30th -- Vientiane dispatch: According to a recent report in the Laotian newspaper "Peace," people in many parts of Sam Neua province have recently held gatherings one after another to oppose the United States and Katay Don Sasorith and others sending troops, bandits, and special agents to Sam Neua and Phongsaly provinces to carry out harassment, making it impossible for people to live in peace.

The people of various places in Sam Neua have recently sent hundreds of letters with the signatures of more than 10,000 people to the Laotian National Assembly and the International Commission, requesting that necessary measures be taken to force the United States and others to immediately stop the above-mentioned actions. At the same time, they demanded that Laos be truly led onto the path of peace and neutrality, continue to negotiate and fulfill the various clauses of the agreement, and establish a coalition government with the participation of the Lao Fighting Forces.

### 美国在桑怒和丰沙里捣乱  桑怒人民要求加以制止

1957-08-01
第7版()
专栏：

    美国在桑怒和丰沙里捣乱
    桑怒人民要求加以制止
    新华社河内30日电　万象消息：据老挝报纸“和平”最近报道，桑怒省许多地方的民众近来陆续举行集会，反对美国和卡代·萨索里特等把军队、盗匪和特务派到桑怒和丰沙里两省进行骚扰，使人民生活不能安定。
    桑怒各地人民近来曾经寄了共有一万多人签名的上百封信给老挝国民议会和国际委员会，要求采取必要的措施，迫使美国等立即停止上述行动。同时，要求把老挝真正引上和平中立的道路，继续协商和履行协议的各项条款，成立有寮国战斗部队参加的联合政府。
