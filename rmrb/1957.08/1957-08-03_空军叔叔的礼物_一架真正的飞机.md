Title: Air Force Uncle's Gift - A Real Airplane
Author:
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 3

    Air Force Uncle's Gift
    ——A Real Airplane
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The Air Force Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army presented a silver-gray airplane to young aviation enthusiasts in Beijing to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day.
    Yesterday morning, a ceremony was held at the Beijing Youth Science and Technology Museum to present the aircraft. Colonel Liu Kejiang of the Air Force Headquarters said to more than one hundred young aviation enthusiasts: "In order to enable you to better learn aviation knowledge, serve the country's aviation cause in the future, become successors to the country's aviation cause, and heroic defenders of the country's sky, the Air Force Headquarters presents you with a La-9 fighter plane. This aircraft represents the warm care and hope of all officers and soldiers of the Air Force for you."

### 空军叔叔的礼物——一架真正的飞机

1957-08-03
第3版()
专栏：

    空军叔叔的礼物
    ——一架真正的飞机
    新华社1日讯　中国人民解放军空军司令部为了庆祝“八一”建军节三十周年，把一架银灰色的飞机赠送给北京市少年航空爱好者。
    昨天上午，在北京市少年科学技术馆举行了这架飞机的赠送仪式。空军司令部刘克江上校对一百多名少年航空爱好者说：“为了使你们更好地学习航空知识，将来为祖国的航空事业服务，成为祖国航空事业的接班人、祖国天空的英勇的保卫者，空军司令部赠送给你们一架拉9式战斗机。这架飞机代表着空军全体指战员对你们热情的关怀和希望。”
