Title: There Is No Such Thing as a "Clean" Hydrogen Bomb; American Lies Are Exposed
Author:
Date: 1957-08-31
Page: 6

　　There Is No Such Thing as a "Clean" Hydrogen Bomb
　　American Lies Are Exposed
　　Xinhua News Agency, August 30th: The U.S. Congressional Atomic Energy Committee reported on August 25th: "There is absolutely no such thing as a 'clean' (nuclear) weapon."
　　This report from the U.S. Congressional Atomic Energy Committee demolishes the notion of a so-called "clean" hydrogen bomb.

### 绝无“干净”的氢弹  美国的鬼话不攻自破

1957-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

　  绝无“干净”的氢弹
    美国的鬼话不攻自破
    据新华社30日讯　美国国会原子能委员会8月25日报告说：“根本没有绝对‘干净’的（核）武器这样的东西。”
    美国国会原子能委员会这个报告使所谓“干净”的氢弹的说法不攻自破。
