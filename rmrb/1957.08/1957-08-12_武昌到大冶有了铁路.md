Title: Railway from Wuchang to Daye Completed
Author:
Date: 1957-08-12
Page: 5

    The main line of the Wuda Railway from Wuchang to Daye Tieshan has been completely laid. On the morning of the 4th, a train full of equipment and railway employees pulled into Tieshan Station. Thousands of residents around Tieshan came to visit.
    The Wuda Railway's main line is 81.5 kilometers long and was built specifically for transporting iron ore from Tieshan to the Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation (WISCO).

### 武昌到大冶有了铁路

1957-08-12
第5版()
专栏：

    武昌到大冶有了铁路
    本报讯　从武昌到大冶铁山的武大铁路正线已经全部铺轨完毕。4日早晨，一列满载器材和铁路员工的火车开进了铁山车站，铁山周围成千的居民都赶来参观。
    武大铁路正线共长八十一点五公里，是专为武钢运输铁山的矿石而修的。
