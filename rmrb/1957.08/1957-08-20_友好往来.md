Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1957-08-20
Page: 4

    Friendly Exchanges
    Egyptian Ministry of Education Sports Group Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: The Egyptian Ministry of Education Sports Group delegation of three people, invited to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
    The delegation is led by Bahiyeh Abdel Nabi.
    Japanese Metal and Machinery Workers Delegation Invited to Visit China
    Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: The Japanese Metal and Machinery Workers Delegation, a group of seventeen people led by Chairman Kiyoshi Kawakami, arrived in Beijing by train this evening. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the China First Machine Building Trade Union.
    British Economist Mrs. Robinson Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, August 19th: Mrs. Robinson, Vice Chairman of the British Council for the Promotion of International Trade and Professor of Economics at Cambridge University, England, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

### 友好往来

1957-08-20
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    埃及教育部体育组代表团到京
    新华社18日讯　应邀来中国访问的埃及教育部体育组代表团一行三人，今天坐飞机到达北京。
    代表团由巴希叶·阿卜杜勒·纳比率领。
    日五金机械工人代表团应邀来我国访问
    据新华社18日讯　日本五金机械工人访华代表团一行十七人在团长川上清率领下，今天晚上乘火车到达北京。代表团是应中国第一机械工会邀请来中国参观访问的。
    英经济学家罗宾逊夫人到京
    据新华社19日讯　应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请来我国访问的英国国际贸易促进会副主席、英国剑桥大学经济学教授罗宾逊夫人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
