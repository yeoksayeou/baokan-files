Title: Tapping the Potential of Mountainous Areas (Picture)
Author: Sun Piyong
Date: 1957-08-17
Page: 5

    Tapping the Potential of Mountainous Areas
    Huanren County, Liaoning Province, has begun comprehensive planning of agricultural, forestry, and animal husbandry production in mountainous areas throughout the county. This is a picture of Wang Chengwu (center), the leader of the seventh team of the mountain production and construction planning team, and other comrades, investigating soil types, humidity, cohesion, and depth on a hillside.
                                          Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Piyong

### 挖掘山区的潜力（图片）
孙丕永
1957-08-17
第5版()
专栏：

    挖掘山区的潜力
    辽宁省桓仁县，开始在全县范围内全面规划山区农、林、牧生产工作。这是山区生产建设规化队第七组组长王成武（中间）等同志，在山坡上调查土壤种类、干湿度、结合度、深度等。
                                          新华社记者孙丕永摄
