　　The Infinite Superiority of the Socialist System
　　Achievements of Socialist Industrialization in European People's Democratic Countries (I)
    The socialist industrialization construction of the European People's Democratic countries has already achieved tremendous accomplishments. Although the starting points and tasks of socialist industrialization in various countries are different, the industrial production of the European People's Democratic countries has flourished in the ten or so years after the war, heavy industry has grown rapidly, and the industrial structure has undergone significant changes. At the same time, socialist components have also gained an absolute advantage in the entire industrial production of various countries. The speed of industrial development in European People's Democratic countries after the war is beyond the reach of various European capitalist countries. This fully demonstrates the superiority of the socialist system.
    Below is the first of a series of statistical infographics that we plan to publish successively. It illustrates the growth of industrial production in European People's Democratic countries after the war. The content of the other infographics is: the development of heavy industry; the growth of socialist components in industrial production; changes in the proportion of industrial output in total industrial and agricultural output; the increase in the proportion of production means in total industrial output; and the increase in the proportion of machinery manufacturing in total industrial output.

### 社会主义制度无比优越　欧洲人民民主国家社会主义工业化的成就（一）

1957-08-25
第6版()
专栏：数字与事实

　　社会主义制度无比优越
　　欧洲人民民主国家社会主义工业化的成就（一）
    欧洲人民民主国家的社会主义工业化建设已经取得了巨大的成就。虽然各国社会主义工业化的起点和任务不同，但是在战后十多年内，欧洲人民民主国家的工业生产都有了蓬勃发展，重工业都已迅速增长，工业结构都有了重大的变化。同时，社会主义成分在各国的整个工业生产中也都已占有绝对的优势。欧洲人民民主国家战后工业发展的速度，是欧洲各资本主义国家所望尘莫及的。这正充分地表现了社会主义制度的优越性。
    下面是我们准备陆续发表的一系列统计图解的第一幅。它说明了欧洲人民民主国家战后工业生产增长的情况。另外几幅图解的内容是：重工业的发展；社会主义成分在工业生产中的增长；工业产量在工农业总产量中所占比重的变化；生产资料生产在工业总产量中所占比重的增长；机器制造业在工业总产量中所占比重的增长。
