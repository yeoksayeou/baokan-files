Title: Indonesian Parliament Speaker Sartono Arrives in Nanning
Author:
Date: 1957-08-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, August 11th: At the invitation of Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Indonesian Parliament Speaker Sartono and his entourage arrived in Nanning by plane from Hanoi at 10:30 this morning.
    Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, made a special trip from Beijing to welcome the guests. Jiang Jinghe, Vice Governor of the Guangxi Provincial People's Committee, and Chen Feng, Mayor of Nanning, were also at the airport to greet them.
    After a short rest in Nanning, Speaker Sartono and his entourage flew to Hangzhou.

### 印度尼西亚议长沙多诺到南宁

1957-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚议长沙多诺到南宁
    新华社南宁11日电　应刘少奇委员长邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺和随行人员，今天上午十时半由河内乘飞机到达南宁。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长余心清特地由北京赶来欢迎客人。今天到机场欢迎的还有广西省人民委员会副秘书长江景河和南宁市市长陈枫等人。
    沙多诺议长和随行人员在南宁稍事休息后即飞往杭州。
