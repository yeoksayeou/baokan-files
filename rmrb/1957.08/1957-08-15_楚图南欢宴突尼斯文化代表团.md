Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet for Tunisian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-08-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 14th. Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet in Beijing tonight to welcome the Tunisian Cultural Delegation. In his speech, Chu Tunan expressed a heartfelt welcome to the cultural delegation, which is the first to visit China since the establishment of the Republic of Tunisia. The head of the delegation, Mr. Gouiga, also spoke, saying that he felt the warmth of friendship everywhere after arriving in China.

### 楚图南欢宴突尼斯文化代表团

1957-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

    楚图南欢宴突尼斯文化代表团
    新华社14日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南今天晚上在北京设宴欢迎突尼斯文化代表团。楚图南在致词中，对突尼斯共和国成立以后第一次来中国访问的这个文化代表团表示衷心的欢迎。团长古伊加也讲了话，他说到中国以后，处处都感受到友情的温暖。
