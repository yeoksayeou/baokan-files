Title: Central Committee of the China Democratic Party Forms Rectification Leadership Organization

Author:

Date: 1957-08-14
Page: 2

The Central Committee of the China Democratic Party
Formally Establishes Rectification Leadership Organization

(Reported in this paper) The Central Committee of the China Democratic Party, at its 30th Executive Bureau meeting held on the 12th, formally established the Central Rectification Leadership Committee and approved the list of members of the Central Executive Bureau Office Meeting.

The members of the Rectification Leadership Committee approved at this meeting include: Wang Rencheng, Fang Rongxin, Qiu Chen, Liu Chun, Liu Xingheng, Liu Shuxun, Yan Xinmin, He Shikun, Ji Fang, Zhou Gucheng, Zhou Taixuan, Zhou Yanjin, Fan Quan, Hu Yihe, Xu Binru, Tang Wuyuan, Xia Kangnong, Guo Zechen, Fu Xiaoxian, Yang Qingyuan, totaling twenty people. The conveners of this committee are: Ji Fang, Xu Binru, Guo Zechen, Yan Xinmin, Zhou Gucheng, Zhou Taixuan, and Liu Chun, totaling seven people.

In addition, the Central Rectification Leadership Committee of the China Democratic Party immediately held its first meeting after its establishment. The meeting discussed the division of labor among members and the establishment of working bodies, and in principle approved the "Instructions on Strengthening the Anti-Rightist Struggle and Rectification within the Party" issued by the Rectification Leadership Committee to local organizations. This instruction will be issued after some revisions in the next few days.

### 农工民主党中央  正式成立整风领导机构

1957-08-14
第2版()
专栏：

    农工民主党中央
    正式成立整风领导机构
    本报讯　中国农工民主党中央在12日召开的第三十次执行局会议上，正式成立中央整风领导委员会，并且通过了中央执行局办公会议的成员名单。
    在这次会议上通过的整风领导委员会的成员计有：王人程、方荣欣、丘辰、刘春、刘星垣、刘树勋、严信民、何世琨、季方、周谷城、周太玄、周延瑾、范权、胡一禾、徐彬如、唐午园、夏康农、郭则沉、傅肖先、杨清源等二十人。这个委员会的召集人是：季方、徐彬如、郭则沉、严信民、周谷城、周太玄、刘春等七人。
    又，农工民主党中央整风领导委员会成立后，立即举行了第一次会议。会议讨论了有关成员分工和设立工作机构的问题，并且原则上通过整风领导委员会向各地方组织发出的“关于加强反右派斗争和党内整风的指示”。这项指示，日内再作若干修改即可发出。
