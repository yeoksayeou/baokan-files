Title: Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov Sends Congratulatory Message on the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the PLA
Author:
Date: 1957-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov sent a telegram to our Defense Minister Marshal Peng Dehuai on the 30th, congratulating him on the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The telegram is as follows: To Comrade Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Marshal of the People's Republic of China:
    Respected Comrade Peng Dehuai:
    On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of the Soviet Armed Forces, I extend to you and all personnel of the Army and Navy a militant salute and heartfelt congratulations.
    The Chinese People's Liberation Army, founded and educated by the Chinese Communist Party, has gone through a glorious combat path.
    Our Army and Navy personnel and all the people of the Soviet Union unanimously give high praise to the courage, fearlessness, and heroism displayed by the Chinese People's Liberation Army in the struggle for the independence and freedom of their motherland.
    On this beautiful festival, we are particularly pleased to point out the militant friendship between the Chinese and Soviet armies, which is constantly developing and consolidating and reliably guarantees the peace and security of our two peoples.
    All personnel of the Soviet Armed Forces heartily congratulate our comrades-in-arms—the soldiers of the great People's China—on their new achievements in further consolidating the national defense might of their socialist motherland.
                      Soviet Defense Minister, Marshal of the Soviet Union Zhukov
  July 30, 1957

### 苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅  电贺解放军建军三十周年

1957-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅
    电贺解放军建军三十周年
    新华社31日讯　苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅30日打电报给我国国防部长彭德怀元帅祝贺中国人民解放军建军三十周年。电文如下：中华人民共和国国防部长中华人民共和国元帅彭德怀同志：敬爱的彭德怀同志：
    值此中华人民共和国英勇的中国人民解放军建军三十周年之际，谨以苏联武装力量的名义向您和陆海军全体人员致以战斗的敬礼和衷心的祝贺。
    由中国共产党创立和教育出来的中国人民解放军经历了光荣的战斗道路。
    我们的陆海军军人和苏联全体人民，一致对中国人民解放军在为争取自己祖国的独立和自由的战斗中所表现的勇敢无畏精神和英雄主义给予崇高的评价。
    在这美好的节日，我们特别高兴地指出中苏两国军队之间的战斗友谊，它正在不断地发展和巩固并且可靠地保障着我们两国人民的和平与安全。
    苏联武装力量全体人员衷心地祝贺我们的战友——伟大的人民中国的军人们——在进一步巩固自己的社会主义祖国的国防威力方面获得新的成就。
                      苏联国防部长苏联元帅　朱可夫
  1957年7月30日
