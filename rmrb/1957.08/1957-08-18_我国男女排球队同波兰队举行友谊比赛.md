Title: Chinese Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Hold Friendly Matches with Polish Teams
Author:
Date: 1957-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 17th: The Chinese men's and women's youth volleyball teams held friendly matches with the Polish Sparta men's and women's teams on the 17th. As a result of the matches, the Chinese men's team won by three games to two, while the women's team lost by five games to two.

    In the first two games of the women's team match, the Chinese women's team lost to the Polish team by twelve to fifteen and thirteen to fifteen. In the third and fourth games, they defeated the Polish team by fifteen to thirteen and fifteen to six. In the fifth game final, the Chinese team lost to the Polish team by twelve to fifteen.

    In the men's team match, the Polish team won the first game by fifteen to thirteen, and the Chinese team won the second and third games consecutively by fifteen to six and sixteen to fourteen.

### 我国男女排球队同波兰队举行友谊比赛

1957-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    我国男女排球队同波兰队举行友谊比赛
    据新华社华沙17日电  中国男女青年排球队17日同波兰斯巴达男女队进行了友谊比赛。比赛结果，中国男子队以三局两胜获胜，女子队以五局两胜受挫。
    在女子队比赛的前两局中，中国女子队以十二比十五和十三比十五败于波兰队，在第三、四两局中，以十五比十三和十五比六战胜波兰队两局。在第五局的决赛中，中国队以十二比十五败于波兰队。
    在男子队比赛中，第一局波兰队以十五比十三获胜，中国队以十五比六和十六比十四在第二、三两局中连获胜利。
