Title: Chairman Mao Receives Delegation from the Burmese Parliament
Author:
Date: 1957-08-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Qingdao, August 4th. Chairman Mao Zedong received the delegation from the Burmese Federal Parliament, headed by the Deputy Speaker of the Burmese Federal Chamber of Nationalities, Deqin Dengmao, in Qingdao on the evening of the 2nd.

Premier Zhou Enlai was also present at the reception.

Also present at the reception were Liu Guanyi, Wang Lifu, and the Mayor of Qingdao, Li Mu. (Attached picture)

Right picture: Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Zhou Enlai, and members of the Burmese Federal Parliament delegation in cordial conversation.
          Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo photographed

### 毛主席接见缅甸国会代表团

1957-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见缅甸国会代表团
    新华社青岛4日电　毛泽东主席2日晚在青岛接见了以缅甸联邦民族院副议长德钦登貌为首的缅甸联邦国会访华代表团。
    接见时，周恩来总理也在座。
    接见时，在座的还有刘贯一、王梨夫和青岛市长李慕。（附图片）
    右图：毛泽东主席、周恩来总理和缅甸联邦国会代表团人员亲切谈话。
          新华社记者　侯波摄
