Title: Small Bamboo Gun - Rural Children's Song
Author: Li Zuohua
Date: 1957-08-15
Page: 8

Small bamboo pole, a foot long,
Dig a hole, open a slit,
Split the bamboo to make a beam,
Assemble a small bamboo gun.
Tie a string, hang it on my chest,
Stick out my chest, stand by the door,
So spirited and generous.
Parents pass, I stand at attention;
Others pass, I stop them;
What are you doing? Tell me!
Explain clearly, then I'll let you pass.
If anyone asks me why?
I say I am standing guard.

### 小竹枪——农村儿歌
李作华
1957-08-15
第8版()
专栏：

    小竹枪——农村儿歌
    李作华小竹竿，尺把长，挖个眼，开个膛，劈竹劈做个梁安成一支小竹枪。拴上带，挂胸膛，挺着胸，站门旁，又神气来又大方。爸妈过，我立正；别人过，我阻挡；干什么？对我讲！说清楚，才能放。谁要问我为什么？我说我是站门岗。
