Title: Moscow Hosts Soviet-Afghan Friendship Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-08-01
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 31st - TASS reports from Moscow: On July 30th, representatives of the working people of Moscow held a Soviet-Afghan Friendship Conference in the Kremlin to welcome the King of Afghanistan and his entourage, who are in the Soviet Union for a friendly visit.
    The Soviet side attending the conference included Aristov, Belyayev, Brezhnev, Bulganin, Voroshilov, Zhukov, Kuusinen, Suslov, Furtseva, Khrushchev, Pospelov, and Kosygin, among others.
    At the conference, Barsukov, chairman of the trade union committee of the "Dynamo" factory, Tolstov, corresponding academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and physician Kruzhkov spoke on behalf of the working people of Moscow, extending their welcome to the King of Afghanistan.
    Subsequently, the King of Afghanistan, Mohammed Zahir Shah, delivered a speech. He stated that the relations between the Soviet Union and Afghanistan have always been friendly and sincere. The friendship between the two peoples is built on the best and most solid foundations. The Afghan people, adhering to their traditions and beliefs, hope to establish friendship with the people and countries of the world, and believe that friendship is the only means of maintaining peace and the only guarantee of human happiness.
    The Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, also spoke. He said that the Soviet Union is now and will always be Afghanistan's selfless, faithful friend and friendly neighbor. For the sake of friendship and peace, it is willing to share its experience in developing industry, agriculture, and culture with the Afghan people. The Afghan people will always find the Soviet people to be honest and reliable friends in their cause of protecting peace and human security.
    Voroshilov said that the recent friendly talks between the Soviet Union and Afghanistan in Moscow have achieved significant results, and both sides have agreed to jointly take measures to promote the further development of the Afghan economy. Voroshilov stated that the Soviet Union's relationship with Afghanistan is based on the Soviet Union's hope that its southern neighbor will become a free, strong, and prosperous independent country.

### 莫斯科举行苏联阿富汗友好大会

1957-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    莫斯科举行苏联阿富汗友好大会
    新华社31日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：7月30日，莫斯科劳动人民的代表在克里姆林宫举行了苏联和阿富汗友好大会，欢迎在苏联作友好访问的阿富汗国王和他的随行人员。
    苏联方面参加这次大会的有阿里斯托夫、别利亚耶夫、勃列日涅夫、布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、朱可夫、库西宁、苏斯洛夫、福尔采娃、赫鲁晓夫、波斯伯洛夫和柯西金等。
    在大会上，“狄纳莫”工厂工会委员会主席巴尔苏科夫、苏联科学院通讯院士托尔斯托夫和医师克鲁日科夫代表莫斯科的劳动人民讲话，对阿富汗国王表示欢迎。
    随后，阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿讲了话。他说，苏联和阿富汗的关系一向是友好而真诚的。两国人民的友谊是建立在最良好的、最巩固的基础上的。阿富汗人民遵循自己的传统和信念，希望同世界各国人民和各国建立友谊，并且认为友谊是维护和平的唯一手段，也是保证人类幸福的唯一手段。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫也讲了话。他说，苏联现在是、将来也是阿富汗的无私的忠实的朋友和友好的邻居。为了友谊和和平，它愿意向阿富汗人民介绍自己在发展工业、农业和文化方面的经验。阿富汗人民永远会觉得苏联人民是他们在保护和平和人类安全的事业中的诚实可靠的朋友。
    伏罗希洛夫说，苏联和阿富汗最近在莫斯科的友好会谈，取得了重大的成果，双方同意共同采取措施来促进阿富汗经济的进一步发展。伏罗希洛夫说，苏联对待阿富汗的这种关系是基于苏联希望自己的这位南部邻居，成为一个自由、强大而繁荣的独立国家。
