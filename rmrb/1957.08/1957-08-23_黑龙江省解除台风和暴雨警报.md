Title: Heilongjiang Province Lifts Typhoon and Rainstorm Alert
Author:
Date: 1957-08-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd. The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued the following typhoon bulletin at 7:00 PM on the 22nd:

    The typhoon that moved from Korea into Heilongjiang Province, China, was centered at 2:00 PM today in the middle reaches of the Ussuri River on the Sino-Soviet border, near 47 degrees north latitude and 134 degrees east longitude. The maximum wind force around the center is estimated to be below level 7, and the typhoon has gradually transformed into a temperate low-pressure system. The wind force and rainfall in eastern Heilongjiang Province have weakened. The typhoon alert and rainstorm alert have been lifted.

### 黑龙江省解除台风和暴雨警报

1957-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

    黑龙江省解除台风和暴雨警报
    新华社22日讯　中央气象科学研究所22日下午七时发布台风消息如下：
    从朝鲜移到我国黑龙江省的台风，今天下午二时中心位置在中苏边境上的乌苏里江中游，就是北纬四十七度东经一百三十四度附近。中心四周的最大风力，估计在七级以下，台风已渐变成温带低气压。黑龙江省东部的风力和雨量均已减弱，此次台风警报和暴雨警报解除。
