Title: Talks Between Leaders of Vietnam and Yugoslavia Discuss Development of Bilateral Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1957-08-09
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, August 8th: According to the Yugoslav News Agency, on August 7th, Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and his entourage held talks with Yugoslav leaders Tito and others in Brioni.

During the talks, the leaders of the two countries discussed the development of cooperation between Yugoslavia and Vietnam, as well as certain international political issues of interest to both countries.

At 11:00 a.m. that day, President Tito first met with President Ho Chi Minh at the Brioni Villa. He was there to pay a courtesy call on his distinguished guest.

The second friendly meeting between the representatives of the two countries took place on Vanga Island in the Brioni archipelago. President Ho Chi Minh took a brief walk on the island, accompanied by Hoang Van Hoan, member of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and member of the Central Political Bureau of the Lao Dong Party; Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Vice Presidents Kardelj and Ranković; President of the Macedonian National Assembly Kolishevski; Secretary-General for Foreign Affairs Popović; Vice President of the Croatian Executive Council Krajačić; and Secretary-General of the Yugoslav President Vilfan.

Subsequently, President Ho Chi Minh and others attended a luncheon hosted by President Tito and his wife on Vanga Island. During the luncheon, the friendly talks between the leaders of the two countries resumed.

In the evening, President Ho Chi Minh hosted a banquet in Brioni. Among those present at the banquet were Yugoslav leaders Tito, Kardelj, Ranković, Đurašković, Gošnjak, Popović, and others.

### 越南和南斯拉夫两国领导人会谈  讨论发展两国合作问题

1957-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    越南和南斯拉夫两国领导人会谈
    讨论发展两国合作问题
    新华社贝尔格莱德8日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社布里俄尼讯：越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他的随行人员8月7日在布里俄尼同南斯拉夫领导人铁托等举行了会谈。
    两国领导人在会谈中，讨论了发展南斯拉夫和越南之间的合作问题，以及两国感到兴趣的某些国际政治问题。
    这天上午十一时，铁托总统先在布里俄尼别墅同胡志明主席晤面。他是到这里对他的贵宾作礼节性拜访的。
    两国代表的第二次友好会谈是在布里俄尼群岛的万加岛上举行的。胡志明主席在岛上曾作片刻散步。陪同他的有越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会委员和劳动党中央政治局委员黄文欢，南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔和兰科维奇，马其顿国民议会主席科利谢夫斯基，外交国务秘书长波波维奇，克罗地亚执行委员会副主席克拉雅契奇，和南斯拉夫总统秘书长维尔范。
    接着，胡志明主席等出席了铁托总统和夫人在万加岛上设的午宴。午宴上，两国领导人的友好会谈又重新开始。
    晚间，胡志明主席在布里俄尼举行了宴会。出席宴会的有南斯拉夫领导人铁托、卡尔德、兰科维奇、乔拉柯维奇、戈什尼亚克、波波维奇等。
