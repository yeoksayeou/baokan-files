Title: Hachiro Arita Returns to His Country

Author: 
Date: 1957-08-30
Page: 4

    Hachiro Arita Returns to His Country
    Xinhua News Agency, 29th — Hachiro Arita, advisor to the Japanese Socialist Party, has returned to his country by plane today after visiting Beijing.
