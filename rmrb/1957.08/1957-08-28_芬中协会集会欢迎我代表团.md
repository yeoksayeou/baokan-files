Title: Finnish-Chinese Association Holds Gathering to Welcome Our Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, August 27th: The Finnish-Chinese Association held a mass gathering in the auditorium of the Helsinki School of Economics on August 26th to welcome the Chinese National People's Congress Delegation.

　　Chairman Kahonen of the Finnish-Chinese Association pointed out in his speech at the meeting that the visit of the Chinese National People's Congress Delegation to Finland will bring the two countries closer together. Saifuddin, head of the Chinese National People's Congress Delegation, expressed his gratitude in his speech to the Finnish Parliament, the government, and all sectors of Finnish society for their warm reception of the Chinese NPC delegation. He said that with the concerted efforts of the people of China and Finland, the friendship between the two peoples will flourish and contribute to world peace.

　　Before the meeting, Chairman Kahonen of the Finnish-Chinese Association presented gifts to the Chinese guests, and the Chinese guests reciprocated by presenting gifts to the Finnish hosts at the meeting.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, August 26th: After a five-day visit to various parts of Finland, the Chinese National People's Congress Delegation returned to Helsinki on the 25th from the Arctic Circle region in northern Finland.

### 芬中协会集会欢迎我代表团

1957-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　芬中协会集会欢迎我代表团
    据新华社赫尔辛基27日电　芬中协会8月26日在赫尔辛基高级商业学校的礼堂中举行群众集会，欢迎中国全国人民代表大会代表团。
    芬中协会主席卡赫宁在会上讲话中指出：中国全国人民代表大会代表团在芬兰的访问将使芬中两国更加接近。中国全国人民代表大会代表团团长赛福鼎在讲话中对芬兰议会、政府以及芬兰各界对中国人大代表团的热烈招待表示感谢。他说，在中芬两国人民一致的努力下，两国人民的友谊将欣欣向荣，并且有助于世界的和平。
    芬中协会主席卡赫宁在会前向中国客人赠送了礼品，中国客人在会上向芬兰主人回赠了礼品。
    据新华社赫尔辛基26日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团在芬兰各地作了五天的访问以后，在25日由芬兰北部北极圈地区返回赫尔辛基。
