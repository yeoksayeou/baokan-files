Title: Syria Expels Three U.S. Diplomats
Author: 
Date: 1957-08-15
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 14th: A Syrian Foreign Ministry spokesperson stated that the Syrian government declared three officials of the U.S. Embassy as persona non grata on the 13th, because they plotted a conspiracy to overthrow the current Syrian regime.

The three U.S. officials are: U.S. Embassy in Syria Military Attaché Colonel Robert Molloy, Embassy Second Secretary Howard Stone, and Vice-Consul Francis Geaton.

The Syrian Foreign Ministry spokesperson said that Syria has demanded that these three U.S. diplomatic officials leave Syrian territory within twenty-four hours. The U.S. Embassy Chargé d'Affaires agreed to this request.

### 叙驱逐三名美国外交官

1957-08-15
第7版()
专栏：

    叙驱逐三名美国外交官
    新华社大马士革14日电　叙利亚外交部发言人说：叙利亚政府已经在13日宣布美国大使馆的三名官员为不受欢迎的人，因为他们策划推翻叙利亚现政权的阴谋。
    这三名美国官员是：美国驻叙利亚大使馆武官罗伯特·莫洛伊上校，大使馆二等秘书霍华德·斯通和副领事弗兰西斯·杰顿。
    叙利亚外交部发言人说，叙利亚要求这三个美国外交官员在二十四小时内离开叙利亚领土。美国大使馆代办答应了这个要求。
