Title: Sichuan warlord Yang Sen: "Liu Xue'an! Did you ever sing the praises of my opium pipe and my twenty-eighth concubine?" (Picture)
Author: Hua Junwu
Date: 1957-08-19
Page: 2

(Liu Xue'an, a rightist ringleader in the music world, was a singer for the reactionary class. Before liberation, he not only composed such songs as "Draftee Joining the Army," "Air Force Song," "Navy Song," and "War Song" for the Kuomintang, but even composed a "Family Song" for the Sichuan warlord Yang Sen.)
						Hua Junwu

### 四川军阀杨森：“刘雪庵！我的烟枪和二十八姨太可曾歌颂？”（图片）
华君武
1957-08-19
第2版()
专栏：

    四川军阀杨森：“刘雪庵！我的烟枪和二十八姨太可曾歌颂？”
    （音乐界的右派头目刘雪庵，是反动阶级的歌手，解放前他不只为国民党作了什么“壮丁入伍歌”“空军之歌”“海军之歌”“战歌”，甚至为四川大军阀杨森作过“家歌”。）
                                        华君武
