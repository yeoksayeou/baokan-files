Title: Mongolian Government Delegation Visits Eastern European Brotherly Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-08-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, 13th August: The Mongolian government delegation, led by the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, Tsedenbal, left here by plane this morning for friendly visits to eight socialist countries in Eastern Europe: the German Democratic Republic, Albania, Yugoslavia, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and Poland.

The leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the government, Damba, Tsende, Dugersuren, and others, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia, saw them off at the airport.

### 蒙古政府代表团  赴东欧兄弟国家访问

1957-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古政府代表团
    赴东欧兄弟国家访问
    新华社乌兰巴托13日电　由蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔率领的蒙古政府代表团今天上午从这里坐飞机出发，前往东欧的德意志民主共和国、阿尔巴尼亚、南斯拉夫、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克和波兰八个社会主义国家进行友好访问。
    蒙古人民革命党和政府的负责人达姆巴、曾德、杜格苏伦等和各国驻蒙古外交使节到机场送行。
