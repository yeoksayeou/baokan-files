Title: Zhejiang Golden Thread Jujubes See Huge Production Increase This Year
Author: Zhuge Zifang
Date: 1957-08-26
Page: 5

　　Zhejiang Golden Thread Jujubes See Huge Production Increase This Year
    Lanxi, Zhejiang, is a jujube-producing area.

    After the Beginning of Autumn, before the night fades each day, the jujube farmers carry bamboo poles and baskets to pick large green jujubes in the jujube groves. This year, Lanxi's green jujubes have had an unprecedented bumper harvest. The county can harvest more than 600,000 jin, doubling the production of 1954.

    Lanxi jujubes are fresh, tender, plump, thin-skinned, fleshy, fragrant, and crisp. They are good materials for refining the famous "Golden Thread Jujubes" at home and abroad.

    (Zhuge Zifang)

### 浙江金丝蜜枣今年产量大增
诸葛子房
1957-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　浙江金丝蜜枣今年产量大增
    浙江兰溪是产枣的地方。
    立秋过后，每天夜色还未退尽，枣农们就背着竹竿，挑着筐箩，在枣树林里采摘大青枣。今年兰溪的青枣获得了空前的大丰收，全县可收获六十多万斤，比1954年增产一倍。
    兰溪枣鲜嫩肥硕、皮薄多肉、清香脆口，是精制国内外有名的“金丝蜜枣”的好材料。
    （诸葛子房）
