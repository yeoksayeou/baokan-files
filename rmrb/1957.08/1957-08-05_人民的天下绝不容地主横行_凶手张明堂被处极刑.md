Title: The People's World Will Never Allow Landlords to Run Rampant: Murderer Zhang Mingtang Executed

Author:
Date: 1957-08-05
Page: 2

    The People's World Will Never Allow Landlords to Run Rampant
    Murderer Zhang Mingtang Executed
    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, August 4th: Zhang Mingtang, an illegal landlord in Xiangyang County, Hubei Province, openly and brutally murdered peasants during the bourgeois rightists' frenzied attack on socialism. This counter-revolutionary element has now been sentenced to death.
    Illegal landlord Zhang Mingtang spread rumors and engaged in sabotage during various movements after liberation. He consistently harbored dissatisfaction with the People's Government and hostility towards cadres and the masses. Especially after the bourgeois rightists launched a frenzied attack on socialism, Zhang Mingtang became even more arrogant, applauded the rightists' speeches, openly counterattacked and settled scores with the peasants, and even dared to kill Zhang Xiaohao, the advanced agricultural cooperative production team leader of Shizili Township, and Tan Guixiang, a poor peasant member, in broad daylight. Zhang Mingtang was caught on the spot by the masses before he could escape after committing the murders.
    According to the demands of the local masses and in order to consolidate the people's democratic dictatorship and protect the safety of the people's lives and property, the Xiangyang County People's Court, with the approval of the Provincial Higher People's Court in early June, recently held a sentencing rally in Heilongji, Xiangyang County, sentencing illegal landlord Zhang Mingtang to death and confiscating his personal property to compensate the victims' families.

### 人民的天下绝不容地主横行  凶手张明堂被处极刑

1957-08-05
第2版()
专栏：

    人民的天下绝不容地主横行
    凶手张明堂被处极刑
    新华社武汉4日电　湖北省襄阳县不法地主张明堂，在资产阶级右派分子向社会主义猖狂进攻的时候，公然惨杀农民。现在，这个反革命分子已被判处死刑。
    不法地主张明堂，解放后在各项运动中造谣破坏，一贯对人民政府不满，敌视干部和群众，特别是当资产阶级右派分子向社会主义展开猖狂进攻以后，张明堂更加嚣张，对右派分子的言论拍手称快，公然向农民反攻倒算，竟敢在光天化日之下，杀死襄阳县史子李乡先进农业社生产队长张小好和贫农社员谭贵香。张明堂在行凶杀人后未及逃跑就被群众当场捉住。
    襄阳县人民法院根据当地群众的要求和为了巩固人民民主专政，保卫人民生命财产的安全，经呈请省高级人民法院在6月初批准，最近在襄阳县黑龙集举行了宣判大会，判处不法地主张明堂死刑，并没收其个人财产以抚恤被害者家属。
