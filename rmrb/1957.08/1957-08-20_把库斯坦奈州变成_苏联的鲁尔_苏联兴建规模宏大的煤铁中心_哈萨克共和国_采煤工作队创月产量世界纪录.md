Title: Transforming Kostanay Oblast into the "Soviet Ruhr": The Soviet Union Constructs a Grand Coal and Iron Center; Coal Mining Team in the Kazakh Republic Sets New World Record for Monthly Output
Author: 
Date: 1957-08-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 19th dispatch. TASS reports: The Minister of Geology and Mineral Resources Protection of the Kazakh Republic, Bogatyrev, said that all conditions are now in place to build the Kostanay Oblast of the Kazakh Republic into a "Soviet Ruhr" industrial zone. Moreover, this "Soviet Ruhr" will far surpass the famous German industrial base, the Ruhr, in terms of industrial production levels.

Kostanay Oblast has abundant iron ore, coal, and other mineral deposits. It has been proven that the iron ore reserves here exceed 10 billion tons, no less than the total iron ore reserves of France and Switzerland, famous European iron ore producing countries (these two countries' pig iron production accounts for two-thirds of the total pig iron production of European capitalist countries). Its magnetite reserves are greater than the famous Lake Superior iron ore region in the United States, and its phosphorus iron ore reserves far exceed the world-renowned Lorraine iron ore in Europe.

Bogatyrev said that large-scale mining industry construction has now begun in Kostanay Oblast. The production capacity of the Sokolov-Sarbay Mining and Processing Combine, currently under construction and the largest in the Soviet Union, is almost equal to the total production capacity of all mineral processing industrial enterprises in Iran, India, Canada, and Spain. In fifteen years, the entire Kostanay mining enterprise will produce 110 million tons of iron ore sand annually, which is 2.1 million tons more than the current annual iron ore sand production of the entire Soviet Union.

To adapt to the future development of the mining and metallurgical industries, Kostanay Oblast is also constructing many large-scale coal mines.

Xinhua News Agency, 19th dispatch. TASS reports: In the Karaganda coal region of the Kazakh Republic, a coal mining combined machine team achieved a monthly output of 30,524 tons per "Donbass" coal mining machine in July, setting a new world record for monthly output.

The achievement of this team from Mine No. 31 of the "Kirov Coal Industry Trust" is 2,499 tons more than the previous world record achieved by coal mining combined machine operators in the same mine.

### 把库斯坦奈州变成“苏联的鲁尔”  苏联兴建规模宏大的煤铁中心  哈萨克共和国—采煤工作队创月产量世界纪录

1957-08-20
第6版()
专栏：

    把库斯坦奈州变成“苏联的鲁尔”
    苏联兴建规模宏大的煤铁中心
    哈萨克共和国—采煤工作队创月产量世界纪录
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社报道：哈萨克共和国地质和矿藏保护部部长鲍加蒂廖夫说，现在已经具备一切条件，以便把哈萨克共和国的库斯坦奈州建成“苏联的鲁尔”工业区。而且，这个“苏联的鲁尔”在工业生产水平上，将远远超过有名的德国工业基地鲁尔。
    库斯坦奈州有着丰富的铁矿、煤矿和其他矿藏。已经探明，这里的铁矿储量超过一百亿吨以上，并不亚于欧洲有名的出产铁矿的国家法国和瑞士（这两个国家的生铁产量占欧洲资本主义各国生铁总产量的三分之二）的铁矿储量的总和。它的磁铁矿储量比美国著名的上湖铁矿区还多，磷铁矿的储量远远超过了驰名世界的欧洲的洛林铁矿。
    鲍加蒂廖夫说，现在已经开始在库斯坦奈州建设大规模的采矿工业。单是正在建设的苏联最大的索科洛夫—萨尔巴伊选矿联合工厂的生产能力，就几乎等于伊朗、印度、加拿大、西班牙所有选矿工业企业的生产能力的总和。而在十五年以后，整个库斯坦奈的采矿企业每年出产的铁矿砂将有一亿一千万吨，这比目前全苏联的铁矿砂年产量还要多二百一十万吨。
    为了适应今后采矿和冶金工业的发展，库斯坦奈州现在还在建设许多大型煤矿。
    据新华社19日讯　据塔斯社报道：在哈萨克共和国卡拉干达煤区，一个采煤联合机工作队的每台“顿巴斯”采煤机7月份月产量达到了三万零五百二十四吨，创造了月产量的最高世界纪录。
    这个属于“基洛夫煤业托辣斯”三十一号矿井的工作队的成绩，比同一个矿井的采煤联合机手以前所达到的世界纪录多二千四百九十九吨。
