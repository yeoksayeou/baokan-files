Title: Southern Night
Author: Han Xiao
Date: 1957-08-06
Page: 8

Column: Singing the Praises of the PLA

    Southern Night
  　Han Xiao
    Majestic palms kiss the moon
    Lights in the tree-lined shade like jewels
    Embedded in green window frames
    The hazy streets like a sleeping river
    The crisp footsteps of the patrolman
    Resound beneath the magnolia trees
    How quiet the night is
    How fragrant the wind is
