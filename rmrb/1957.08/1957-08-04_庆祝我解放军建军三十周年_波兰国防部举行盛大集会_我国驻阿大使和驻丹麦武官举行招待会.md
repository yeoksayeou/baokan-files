Title: Celebrating the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army: Polish Ministry of National Defense Holds Grand Gathering; Chinese Ambassador to Albania and Military Attaché to Denmark Hold Receptions
Author:
Date: 1957-08-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 3rd: To celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Polish Ministry of National Defense held a grand celebration at the National Opera House on August 2nd.

    Present at the celebration were leaders and representatives from the Polish United Workers' Party, the government, the People's Army, and various circles, as well as a large number of Warsaw citizens.

    On the presidium of the celebration were Morawski, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, and Jiang Yizhen, Military Attaché, as well as representatives from the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association and various parties and organizations.

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 3rd: Chinese Ambassador to Albania, Luo Shigao, held a reception in Tirana on the evening of August 1st to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 3rd: Copenhagen dispatch: To celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Colonel Ye Changqu, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Denmark, held a reception in Copenhagen on the evening of August 1st.

### 庆祝我解放军建军三十周年  波兰国防部举行盛大集会  我国驻阿大使和驻丹麦武官举行招待会

1957-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝我解放军建军三十周年
    波兰国防部举行盛大集会
    我国驻阿大使和驻丹麦武官举行招待会
    据新华社华沙3日电　为庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十周年，波兰国防部8月2日在国家歌剧院举行了盛大的庆祝会。
    出席庆祝会的有波兰统一工人党、政府、人民军和各界的领袖和代表，以及人数众多的华沙市民。
    在庆祝会主席团席位上的有波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员莫拉夫斯基，中国驻波兰大使王炳南和武官姜一震，以及波中友好协会和各党派、团体的代表等。
    据新华社地拉那3日电　中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗世高8月1日晚在地拉那举行酒会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十周年。
    据新华社柏林3日电　哥本哈根消息：为庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十周年，中国驻丹麦大使馆武官叶昌渠上校8月1日晚在哥本哈根举行了招待会。
