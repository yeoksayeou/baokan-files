Title: Gale Force 8-12 in the East China Sea and Eastern Yellow Sea Today
Author:
Date: 1957-08-20
Page: 4

    East China Sea and eastern Yellow Sea to have gale force 8-12 today
    (Reported by this newspaper) On August 19th at 8:00 PM, the Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon warning: The powerful typhoon center in the Pacific Ocean is currently moving in a north-northwesterly direction at a speed of 15 knots per hour. It is estimated that by 2:00 PM tomorrow, the typhoon center will move to the western coast of Korea. In the next 24 hours, the eastern part of the East China Sea and the eastern part of the Yellow Sea will experience gale force 8-12, while the western part of the Yellow Sea and the northwestern part of the East China Sea, the Shandong Peninsula, and the coastal areas of northern Jiangsu will experience northerly winds of force 7-9. From tomorrow morning, the northeasterly winds in the Bohai Strait will strengthen to force 7-8. Eighteen hours later, the typhoon center will gradually turn northeast and move towards the Sea of Japan. Thirty-six hours later, the wind force on the East China Sea will gradually weaken to around force 6. Forty-eight hours later, the wind force in the Yellow Sea and the Bohai Strait will also gradually weaken to around force 6.
    The weak typhoon that landed on Hainan Island is moving very slowly. The maximum wind force near the center is estimated to be force 6-8. In the next 24 hours, the western part of the South China Sea, the Qiongzhou Strait, and Beibu Gulf will experience gale force 6-8, and the wind force in the Leizhou Peninsula and Hainan Island will remain at force 5-7.

### 东海、黄海东部今天有八——十二级大风

1957-08-20
第4版()
专栏：

    东海、黄海东部今天有八——十二级大风
    本报讯　8月19日下午八时中央气象科学研究所发布台风警报：目前太平洋上的强大台风中心正以每小时十五浬的速度向西北偏北方向移动。估计明天下午二时台风中心将移到朝鲜西部沿海地区。未来二十四小时内，东海东部、黄海东部有八——十二级大风，黄海西部和东海西北部、山东半岛和苏北沿海地区有七——九级的偏北风。明天上午起渤海海峡的偏东北风将加强到七——八级。十八小时以后，台风中心将逐渐转向东北，向日本海方向移动。三十六小时以后，东海海面的风力将逐渐减弱到六级左右。四十八小时以后，黄海和渤海海峡的风力也将逐渐减弱到六级左右。
    在海南岛登陆的弱台风，中心移动很慢。中心附近最大风力估计为六——八级。未来二十四小时内，南海西部、琼州海峡北部湾有六——八级的大风，雷州半岛和海南岛的风力仍有五——七级。
