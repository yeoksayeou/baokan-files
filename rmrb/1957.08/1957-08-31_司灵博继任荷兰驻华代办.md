Title: Sybren succeeds as Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China
Author:
Date: 1957-08-31
Page: 1

　　Sybren succeeds as Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China
　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th. After the former Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. Sman, was recalled to his country, the Dutch government has obtained the Chinese government's agreement to appoint Mr. Barend Jan Sybren as the successor to the Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China.

### 司灵博继任荷兰驻华代办

1957-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　司灵博继任荷兰驻华代办
    新华社30日讯　荷兰前任驻华代办司曼奉调回国后，荷兰政府已取得中国政府的同意将派贝廉·扬·司灵博继任荷兰驻华代办。
