Title: Nakaminato City Council of Japan Decides to Investigate the Incident of American Plane Killing Japanese Woman
Author:
Date: 1957-08-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th: The Nakaminato City Council in Ibaraki Prefecture, Japan, decided on the 15th to thoroughly investigate the responsibility of the U.S. government for the incident of a U.S. military plane flying low and killing a Japanese woman, through the prefectural assembly and the Diet, and demanded that the U.S. military stop using the Maewatari Airfield in the area.

On August 2nd, a plane piloted by U.S. Air Force pilot John Gordon suddenly flew low near Nakaminato City, killing a Japanese woman, Haru Hojo, who was riding a bicycle, and injuring her son.

### 日本那珂凑市议会决定  追究美机撞死日本妇女事件

1957-08-17
第7版()
专栏：

    日本那珂凑市议会决定
    追究美机撞死日本妇女事件
    据新华社16日讯  东京消息：日本茨城县那珂凑市议会15日决定，要通过县议会和国会向美国政府彻底追究美军飞机低飞撞死日本妇女事件的责任，并且要求美军停止使用当地的前渡飞机场。
    8月2日，由美国空军驾驶员约翰·戈登驾驶的飞机在那珂凑市附近突然低飞，撞死了一位骑在自行车上的日本妇女北条春，并且撞伤了她的儿子。
