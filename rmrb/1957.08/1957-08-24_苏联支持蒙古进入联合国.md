Title: Soviet Union Supports Mongolia's Admission to the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1957-08-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 20th: The Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, in a reply, stated that it fully supports the request of the Mongolian People's Republic to be admitted as a member of the United Nations and guarantees to strive for the realization of this goal.

The reply pointed out that there is no reason, nor can there be, to deny the Mongolian People's Republic the legitimate right to be a member of the United Nations.

This reply was in response to the appeal letter passed by the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic on July 8th to the parliaments of all member states of the United Nations.

### 苏联支持蒙古进入联合国

1957-08-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏联支持蒙古进入联合国
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科20日讯：苏联最高苏维埃在一份复文中表示，它完全支持蒙古人民共和国提出的关于接受蒙古人民共和国为联合国会员国的请求，并且保证为实现这一目的而努力。
    复文指出，没有而且也不可能有理由不让蒙古人民共和国实现作为联合国会员国的合法权利。
    这个复文是答复蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔7月8日通过的致联合国各会员国议会的呼吁书的。
