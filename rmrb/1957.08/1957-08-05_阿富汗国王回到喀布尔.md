Title: Afghan King Returns to Kabul
Author:
Date: 1957-08-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 4th - Afghan King Zahir returned here this morning after a seventeen-day visit to the Soviet Union.

    He told the crowd who welcomed him at the airport that the progress the Soviet Union has made in all aspects of life is very interesting, and that this progress is the result of the hard work of the Soviet people.

### 阿富汗国王回到喀布尔

1957-08-05
第3版()
专栏：

    阿富汗国王回到喀布尔
    据新华社喀布尔4日电　阿富汗国王查希尔在苏联访问十七天以后于今天早晨回到这里。
    他告诉在飞机场上欢迎他的群众说，苏联在生活各方面取得的进步令人感到非常有趣，这些进步是苏联人民艰苦工作的结果。
