Title: Demanding the Government Free Itself from American Shackles; Japanese Business Circles Demand Expansion of Sino-Japanese Trade
Author:
Date: 1957-08-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 16th: Japanese business figures and public opinion are increasingly and resolutely demanding that the government change its pro-American position in trade and expand trade with the People's Republic of China.

Tokyo Radio commentator Tokuda pointed out that Japan's trade with the United States "frequently results in import surpluses." He said: "If the current situation continues, that is, if we still cannot import industrial raw materials and grain from Communist China or other countries, our economy will always be subordinate to the United States."

According to an Associated Press reporter in San Francisco, Mizuno, a representative of Japan's largest "Mitsui Shipbuilding Company," said when talking about Japan's current economic situation, "Japan's economy is standing on only one leg." Mizuno believes that Japan's economy "must base its other leg on China," otherwise it will not be able to stand firmly.

In a statement issued on August 15th, the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade pointed out that it demands that the government eliminate all obstacles to the development and expansion of Sino-Japanese trade, and conclude a Sino-Japanese trade agreement in the shortest possible time.

### 要求政府摆脱美国的束缚  日本商界要求扩大中日贸易

1957-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    要求政府摆脱美国的束缚
    日本商界要求扩大中日贸易
    据新华社17日讯　塔斯社东京16日讯：日本商界人士和舆论界越来越坚决地要求政府改变在贸易上从属美国的局面，要求扩大同中华人民共和国的贸易。
    东京电台评论员德田指出，日本同美国的贸易“经常有入超”。他说：“目前的状况要是继续保持下去，也就是说要是我们还不能从共产党中国或者其他国家输入工业原料和粮食，我们的经济就始终要从属美国。”
    据美联社驻旧金山记者报道，日本最大的“三井造船公司”的代表水野讲到日本目前的经济状况时说，“日本的经济只靠一条腿站着”。水野认为，日本经济的“另一条腿必须以中国为立足点”，否则就无法站得稳。
    日本国际贸易促进协会在8月15日发表的一项声明中指出，要求政府消除发展和扩大日中贸易的一切障碍。并且在最短期内缔结日中贸易协定。
