Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Arrives in Budapest, Hosts Banquet for East German Party and Government Leaders Before Leaving Berlin
Author:
Date: 1957-08-02
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, August 1st: President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh, and his entourage arrived in Budapest by plane from Berlin today. Accompanying the distinguished guests from Berlin to Hungary were Hungarian Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Sarka, and the Vietnamese Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to Hungary.

    Welcoming Chairman Ho Chi Minh at the airport were Hungarian Party and Government leaders such as Dobi, Münnich, Marosán, and Kish, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Hungary.

    At the airport, Chairman Ho Chi Minh was warmly welcomed by thousands of people and children.

    President Dobi and Chairman Ho Chi Minh delivered speeches at the airport.

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 1st: Chairman Ho Chi Minh and his entourage concluded their visit to the German Democratic Republic and left Berlin by special plane at 10 o'clock this morning for a visit to Hungary.

    Party and government leaders such as Grotewohl, Ulbricht, and Matern, people from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Germany went to the airport to see them off.

    Chairman Ho Chi Minh and his entourage arrived in Berlin on July 25 and visited the German Democratic Republic for a week. Chairman Ho Chi Minh will also visit Yugoslavia, Albania, Bulgaria, and Romania before returning home via Moscow and Beijing.

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 1st: Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, held a grand banquet in Berlin on the evening of July 31 to entertain leaders of the German Democratic Republic's Party and government and people from all walks of life.

    Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Dieckmann, Chairman of the People's Chamber acting as President, spoke at the banquet.

    Chairman Ho Chi Minh said: The government statement issued by Comrade Grotewohl a few days ago is a new contribution to the reunification of Germany and world peace. The Vietnam Workers' Party and the Vietnamese people fully support the just struggle of the German Democratic Republic government and wish you new achievements.

    Chairman Ho Chi Minh finally pointed out: We, the Vietnamese people, are doing our utmost to oppose the imperialists' interference in our internal affairs. Just as the unification of Germany is a matter for the German people themselves, the unification of Vietnam is also a matter for the Vietnamese people themselves. We resolutely oppose the conspiracies of US imperialism to invade both North and South Vietnam and the socialist camp.

    Dieckmann then made a speech, saying: Chairman Ho Chi Minh's visit shows the brotherly friendship and close unity between the people of Germany and Vietnam.

### 胡志明主席到达布达佩斯  离柏林前宴请民主德国党政领袖

1957-08-02
第7版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席到达布达佩斯
    离柏林前宴请民主德国党政领袖
    新华社布达佩斯1日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他的随行人员今天乘飞机从柏林到达布达佩斯。陪同贵宾从柏林来匈牙利的有匈牙利外交部副部长萨尔卡和越南驻匈牙利临时代办。
    在机场欢迎胡志明主席的有道比、明尼赫、马罗山、基什等匈牙利党和政府的领导人以及各国驻匈牙利外交使节。
    在机场上，胡志明主席受到了数千群众和儿童的热烈欢迎。
    道比主席和胡志明主席在机场上讲了话。
    新华社柏林1日电　胡志明主席和他的随行人员结束了在民主德国的访问，今晨十点乘专机离柏林前往匈牙利访问。
    前往机场送行的有格罗提渥、乌布利希、马特恩等党和政府领导人、各界人士以及各国驻德外交使节等。
    胡志明主席和他的随员是在7月25日到达柏林的，在民主德国访问了一个星期。胡志明主席还将要访问南斯拉夫、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚和罗马尼亚，然后经莫斯科、北京返国。
    据新华社柏林1日电　越南民主共和国胡志明主席7月31日晚间在柏林举行盛大宴会招待民主德国党和政府领导人和各界人士。
    胡志明主席和代行总统职务的人民议院主席狄克曼在宴会上先后讲了话。
    胡志明主席说：几天以前格罗提渥同志发表的政府声明是对德国的重新统一和世界和平的新的贡献。越南劳动党和越南人民完全支持德意志民主共和国政府所进行的正义斗争，并祝你们获得新的成就。
    胡志明主席最后指出：我们越南人民正在以全力反对帝国主义者对我们内政的干涉。正像统一德国是德国人民自己的事情一样，统一越南也是越南人民自己的事情。我们坚决反对美帝国主义侵略南北越和社会主义阵营的阴谋。
    狄克曼接着发表谈话说：胡志明主席的访问显示了德越两国人民的兄弟友谊和亲密团结。
