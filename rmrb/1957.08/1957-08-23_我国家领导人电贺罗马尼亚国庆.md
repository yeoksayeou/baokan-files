Title: Chinese Leaders Send Congratulatory Message on Romanian National Day
Author:
Date: 1957-08-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd -- Chinese state leaders sent a telegram to Romanian state leaders on the occasion of the Romanian National Day. The telegram is as follows:
To Comrade Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic:
To Comrade Chivu Stoica, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic:
To Comrade Ian Gheorghe Maurer, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Romanian People's Republic:

　　On the occasion of the 13th anniversary of the National Day of the Romanian People's Republic, we, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend our heartfelt and warm congratulations to you and, through you, to the government and people of the Romanian People's Republic.

　　The Chinese people are watching with joy the great achievements made by the Romanian people under the leadership of the Romanian Workers' Party and the government of the Romanian People's Republic in developing the socialist economy and culture, improving the people's living standards, and safeguarding world peace. We wish the fraternal Romanian people new and greater achievements in the struggle to build socialism and defend European and world peace, and we wish the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Romanian people further development and consolidation.

　　Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
　　Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
　　Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
　　August 21, 1957, Beijing

### 我国家领导人电贺罗马尼亚国庆

1957-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国家领导人电贺罗马尼亚国庆
    新华社22日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理兼外交部长21日联名致电罗马尼亚人民共和国国家领导人，祝贺罗马尼亚人民共和国国庆。电文如下：罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查同志：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席基·斯托伊卡同志：罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长扬·格·毛雷尔同志：
    当罗马尼亚人民共和国国庆十三周年纪念日的时候，我们谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向您们，并通过您们向罗马尼亚人民共和国政府和人民致以衷心的热烈的祝贺。
    中国人民怀着欢欣的心情注视着罗马尼亚人民在罗马尼亚工人党和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府的领导下，在发展社会主义经济和文化、提高人民的生活水平和维护世界和平的斗争中所取得的巨大成就。祝兄弟的罗马尼亚人民在建设社会主义和保卫欧洲和世界和平的斗争中获得新的、更大的成就，祝中罗两国人民间的兄弟友谊更加发展和巩固。
  中华人民共和国主席毛泽东
  中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长　刘少奇
  中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长　周恩来
                                        1957年8月21日于北京
