Title: Results and Rankings of the National Swimming and Diving Competition
Author:
Date: 1957-08-29
Page: 4

　　Sports News

　　Results and Rankings of the National Swimming and Diving Competition

　　This newspaper reports that at the closing ceremony of the 1957 National Swimming and Diving Championships yesterday, Xia Xiang, Chairman of the Organizing Committee, announced the results and rankings of the competition.

　　In this National Swimming and Diving Championships, two adult athletes reached the standard of Master of Sport, forty-nine reached the first-grade athlete standard, two hundred and twenty-seven reached the second-grade athlete standard, and one hundred and thirty-two reached the third-grade athlete standard.

　　In the youth group competition, three athletes reached the first-grade athlete standard, one hundred and eight reached the second-grade athlete standard, and one hundred and fifty-seven reached the third-grade athlete standard. In the youth group competition, eleven events, seventeen individuals, and two relay teams broke the records of the 1956 Fifteen-City Youth Swimming Competition.

　　In the men's adult Group A competition, the top finishers in total score were: Guangdong (128 points), second place, the People's Liberation Army (95 points), third place, Tianjin (40 points), fourth place, Shanghai (31 points), fifth place, Beijing (11 points), and sixth place, Sichuan (1 point). The rankings for women's adult Group A were: first place, the People's Liberation Army (66 points), second place, Guangdong (65 points), third place, Shanghai (46 points), fourth place, Fujian (21 points), and fifth place, Sichuan (4 points).

　　The rankings for men's adult Group B were: first place, Guangdong (83 points), second place, Guangxi (65 points), third place, Fujian (55 points), fourth place, Tianjin (38 points), fifth place, Shanghai (26 points), and sixth place, Beijing (16 points). The rankings for women's adult Group B were: first place, Guangdong (62 points), second place, Beijing (50 points), third place, Guangxi (47 points), fourth place, Liaoning (18 points), fifth place, Sichuan (15 points), and sixth place, Shanghai (14 points).

　　The rankings for the men's youth group were: first place, Guangzhou (54 points), second place, Shanghai (38 points), third place, Tianjin (34 points), fourth place, Fuzhou (24 points), fifth place, Nanning (17 points), and sixth place, Zhanjiang (6 points). The rankings for the women's youth group were: first place, Guangzhou (68 points), second place, Shanghai (52 points), third place, Beijing (17 points), fourth place, Xiamen (13 points), fifth place, Nanning (8 points), and sixth place, Luda (7 points).

### 全国游泳跳水比赛的成绩和名次

1957-08-29
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

　　全国游泳跳水比赛的成绩和名次
    本报讯　在昨天闭幕的1957年全国游泳、跳水锦标赛大会上，大会主任委员夏祥宣布了这次比赛的成绩和名次。
    在这次全国游泳、跳水锦标赛中，成人组有两名运动员达到了运动健将的标准，达到一级运动员标准的有四十九人次，达到二级运动员标准的有二百二十七人次，达到三级运动员标准的有一百三十二人次。
    在少年组比赛中，达到一级运动员标准的有三人，达到二级运动员标准的有一百零八人次，达到三级运动员标准的有一百五十七人次。在少年组比赛中，有十一个项目、十七人和两个接力队打破了1956年十五城市少年游泳比赛的成绩。
    在男子成人甲组比赛中，获得总分第一名的是广东（一百二十八分）、第二名解放军（九十五分）、第三名天津（四十分）、第四名上海（三十一分）、第五名北京（十一分）、第六名四川（一分）。女子成人甲组的名次是：第一名解放军（六十六分）、第二名广东（六十五分）、第三名上海（四十六分）、第四名福建（二十一分）、第五名四川（四分）。
    男子成人乙组的名次是：第一名广东（八十三分）、第二名广西（六十五分）、第三名福建（五十五分）、第四名天津（三十八分）、第五名上海（二十六分）、第六名北京（十六分）。女子成人乙组的名次是：第一名广东（六十二分）、第二名北京（五十分）、第三名广西（四十七分）、第四名辽宁（十八分）、第五名四川（十五分）、第六名上海（十四分）。
    男子少年组的名次是：第一名广州（五十四分）、第二名上海（三十八分）、第三名天津（三十四分）、第四名福州（二十四分）、第五名南宁（十七分）、第六名湛江（六分）。女子少年组的名次是第一名广州（六十八分）、第二名上海（五十二分）、第三名北京（十七分）、第四名厦门（十三分）、第五名南宁（八分）、第六名旅大（七分）。
