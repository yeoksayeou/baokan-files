Title: Indonesia-China Friendship Association Establishes National Headquarters in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1957-08-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 3rd. The Indonesia-China Friendship Associations from various regions of Indonesia decided at a representatives' meeting to establish a national headquarters for the Indonesia-China Friendship Association in Jakarta.

At the meeting, Professor Prijono, the Minister of Education and Culture, was elected as the chairman of the central council of the national headquarters. Professor Zeng Zhusen, a member of the National Council, and Mrs. Rasuna Said were elected as the first and second vice-chairpersons, respectively.

This national representatives' meeting of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association was held in Surabaya from July 29th to August 1st.

Since the establishment of the Jakarta Indonesia-China Friendship Association in 1955, the work of the Friendship Association has greatly developed. Now, friendship association organizations have been established in thirty-seven places from North Sumatra to West Irian. Members include government officials, religious figures, cultural and artistic figures, workers, farmers, youth, and women. Friendship Associations in various places often show new Chinese films, hold photo exhibitions, and lectures, and extensively carry out activities to promote friendship between the people of the two countries.

### 印度尼西亚—中国友协在雅加达设立总会

1957-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚—中国友协
    在雅加达设立总会
    据新华社雅加达3日电　印度尼西亚各地的印度尼西亚—中国友好协会代表会议决定在雅加达成立印度尼西亚—中国友好协会总会。
    会上选出了文教部长普里约诺教授担任总会中央理事会主席，还选出了民族委员会委员曾珠森教授和腊苏娜·赛义德夫人分别担任第一、第二副主席。
    这次印度尼西亚—中国友好协会的全国性代表会议是从7月29日到8月1日在泗水举行的。
    自从雅加达印度尼西亚—中国友好协会在1955年成立以来，友好协会的工作获得了很大的发展。现在从北苏门答腊到西伊里安的三十七个地方都成立了友好协会的组织。会员包括政府官员、宗教界和文化艺术界人士和工人、农民、青年、妇女等。各地友好协会常常放映新中国电影、举办图片展览会和演讲会，广泛开展了促进两国人民友好的活动。
