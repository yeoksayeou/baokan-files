Title: Japanese Mothers Oppose Nuclear Testing
Author:
Date: 1957-08-07
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 6th: The Third Japanese Mothers' Congress, which began in Tokyo on August 3rd, closed on August 5th with all participants singing "Song of Happiness."

    At the closing meeting, more than 4,000 representatives of women and mothers from all walks of life in Japan passed a resolution including 46 items. The main contents include launching a nationwide movement led by mothers to strive for increased national education budget; preventing the Japanese government's malicious attempt to revise the constitution, and defending the people's livelihood; and urging the Japanese government and the United Nations to strive for the early conclusion of an agreement prohibiting nuclear weapons testing.

    The declaration passed by the Congress stated that since the first Japanese Mothers' Congress was held, the "Mothers' Movement" has taken root throughout Japan. This fact gives mothers great courage and encouragement. The declaration stated that they will unite all the strength of mothers to carry out activities to realize the 46 resolutions.

    During the Congress, the head of the Women's International Democratic Federation, as well as representatives from various countries who came to Tokyo to participate in the Third World Congress against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament, addressed the meeting and congratulated the Congress on its success.

    Shi Ruzhang, the Chinese representative, also delivered a congratulatory message on behalf of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, saying that Japanese women have played a significant role in the movement to ban atomic and hydrogen bombs and in the movement to strive for better living conditions for themselves and their children. Chinese women are very familiar with these situations and cherish them very much. Shi Ruzhang wished that the seeds sown by the Mothers' Congress would quickly sprout, blossom, and bear many rich fruits.

### 日本母亲反对核试验

1957-08-07
第7版()
专栏：

    日本母亲反对核试验
    据新华社6日讯　东京消息：从8月3日起在东京举行的第三届日本母亲大会，8月5日在全体与会者欢唱“幸福之歌”声中闭幕。
    在闭幕会议上，四千多名日本各界妇女和母亲代表们通过了包括四十六项内容的决议，其中主要的内容有，展开以母亲们为前锋的全民性运动，争取增加国家教育预算；阻止日本政府恶劣地修改宪法的企图，保卫人民的生活；促使日本政府和联合国努力争取早日实现缔结禁止试验核武器的协定等。
    大会通过的宣言说，自从召开第一届日本母亲大会以后，“母亲运动”已经在日本全国各地生根。这个事实给予母亲们莫大的勇气和鼓励。宣言表示，她们将要团结母亲们所有的力量，为实现四十六项决议展开活动。
    大会进行期间，国际民主妇女联合会的负责人，以及到东京参加第三届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取裁军世界大会的各国代表曾经到会致词，祝贺大会成功。
    中国代表施如璋也受中华全国民主妇女联合会的委托，在会上致贺词说，日本妇女在禁止原子弹氢弹运动中，在为自己和孩子们争取更美好的生活条件的运动中起了很大的作用。中国妇女对这些情况都非常熟悉，并且非常关怀和珍视。施如璋预祝母亲大会所种下的种子，能够很快地发芽、开花，结出许许多多丰盛的果子来。
