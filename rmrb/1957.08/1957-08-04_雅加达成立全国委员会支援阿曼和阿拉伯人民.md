Title: Jakarta Establishes National Committee to Support the People of Oman and Arab Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-08-04
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 3rd: Yesterday, the National Committee in Jakarta for Supporting the Struggle of the People of Oman and Arab Countries was established. This committee was transformed from the previous Committee for Supporting the Struggle of the Egyptian People.

### 雅加达成立全国委员会支援阿曼和阿拉伯人民

1957-08-04
第6版()
专栏：

    雅加达成立全国委员会
    支援阿曼和阿拉伯人民
    据新华社雅加达3日电　昨天雅加达支持阿曼和阿拉伯人民斗争的全国委员会已经成立。这个委员会是由以前的支援埃及人民斗争委员会转变而成的。
