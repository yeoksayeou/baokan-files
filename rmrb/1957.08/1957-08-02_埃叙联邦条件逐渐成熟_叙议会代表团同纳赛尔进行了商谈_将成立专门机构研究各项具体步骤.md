Title: Conditions for Egyptian-Syrian Federation Gradually Mature; Syrian Parliamentary Delegation Held Talks with Nasser; Special Agency to Be Established to Study Specific Steps

Author:
Date: 1957-08-02
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 1st: The head of the Syrian parliamentary delegation, Ponndari, said on July 31 that the Syrian parliamentary delegation and Egyptian President Nasser discussed the issue of establishing a federation between Egypt and Syria. He said that in order to establish this federation, all that remains to be done is to take practical steps.

Ponndari said that the first step in establishing the federation is to establish an Egyptian-Syrian committee to study and formulate the details of the composition of the federal institutions, and to stipulate a transitional period for the establishment of the Federal Committee.

Ponndari said that the proposed Egyptian-Syrian committee will establish subcommittees to study the political, military, and economic aspects of the federation, and then prepare reports for the two governments for the formal signing of an agreement.

Ponndari supported President Nasser’s recent statement regarding the Egyptian-Syrian federation. Ponndari said that this federation will be the core of the entire Arab federation. The delegation left Cairo on July 31 to return to Damascus after attending the Egyptian National Assembly meeting.

### 埃叙联邦条件逐渐成熟  叙议会代表团同纳赛尔进行了商谈  将成立专门机构研究各项具体步骤

1957-08-02
第7版()
专栏：

    埃叙联邦条件逐渐成熟
    叙议会代表团同纳赛尔进行了商谈
    将成立专门机构研究各项具体步骤
    据新华社开罗1日电　叙利亚议会代表团团长庞达里7月31日说，叙利亚议会代表团和埃及总统纳赛尔讨论了埃及—叙利亚建立联邦的问题。他说，为了建立这个联邦，现在必须做的只是采取实际的步骤而已。
    庞达里说，建立联邦的第一个步骤是成立一个埃及—叙利亚委员会来研究和制订联邦机构组成的细节，并且规定一个过渡时期，以便成立联邦委员会。
    庞达里说，拟议中的埃及—叙利亚委员会将成立小组委员会来研究联邦的政治、军事和经济等方面，然后为两国政府准备报告以便正式签订协定。
    庞达里支持纳赛尔总统最近发表的关于埃及—叙利亚联邦的声明。庞达里说，这个联邦将是全阿拉伯联邦的核心。代表团在开罗参加了埃及国民议会会议以后已经在7月31日离开这里回大马士革。
