Title: Indonesian Speaker of Parliament Sartono Arrives in Hanoi for Visit
Author:
Date: 1957-08-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 5th: Indonesian Speaker of Parliament Sartono arrived in Hanoi by plane at noon today, accompanied by parliamentarians Siauw Giok Tjhan and Sunito. The Indonesian parliamentary delegation is visiting Vietnam at the invitation of Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

	Ton Duc Thang delivered a welcoming speech at the airport. He said that the common goal of the people of Vietnam and Indonesia is to oppose colonialism and seek national independence, sovereignty, and a happy life. The people of the two countries share a common aspiration: peaceful coexistence and struggle to defend world peace.

	Sartono said in his speech, "We have always been concerned about the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people." He believes that this visit will further strengthen the friendship between Indonesia and Vietnam.

### 印度尼西亚国会议长  沙多诺到达河内访问

1957-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会议长
    沙多诺到达河内访问
    据新华社河内5日电　印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺今天中午乘飞机到达河内，同来的有议员萧玉灿和桑尼托。印度尼西亚国会代表团是应越南民主共和国国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜的邀请到越南访问的。
    孙德胜在飞机场致欢迎词。他说，越南和印度尼西亚两国人民的共同的目的是反对殖民主义，谋求民族独立、主权和幸福生活。两国人民怀着共同的愿望，即和平共处，为保卫世界和平而斗争。
    沙多诺在致词时说，我们一向关心越南人民的英勇斗争。他相信这一次访问将更加紧密印度尼西亚和越南之间的友谊。
