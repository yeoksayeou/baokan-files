Title: "Bell Sounds Concert Troupe Active in Yanbian Autonomous Prefecture"
Author:
Date: 1957-08-16
Page: 4

Column: Summer Cultural Activities

    The "Bell Sounds Concert Troupe," organized by teachers in Yanji City, Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture, plans to tour various counties within the autonomous prefecture during the summer vacation to enrich the teachers' summer lives. The Korean people are inherently a nation of song and dance. Although the "Bell Sounds Concert Troupe" is an amateur artistic group, it possesses a certain level of artistic skill. Within a year, they have already given three public performances.

### 钟声协奏团活跃在延边自治州

1957-08-16
第4版()
专栏：暑期文娱生活

    钟声协奏团活跃在延边自治州
    延边朝鲜族自治州延吉市教师组织的“钟声协奏团”，暑期内准备在自治州各县巡回演出，以丰富教师们的暑假生活。朝鲜族本是个能歌善舞的民族，“钟声协奏团”虽是业余艺术团体，但有一定的艺术水平。一年间，他们已公开演出过三次。
