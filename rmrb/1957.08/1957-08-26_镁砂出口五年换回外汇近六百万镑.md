Title: Magnesite Exports Earned Nearly Six Million Pounds in Foreign Exchange Over Five Years
Author:
Date: 1957-08-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, August 25th: The Anshan Iron and Steel's Dashiqiao Magnesite Mine, famous in the Far East, has sold large quantities of high-quality magnesite and other products to the Soviet Union, Poland, Japan and other places in recent years. From 1952 to the present, Anshan Iron and Steel's magnesite products have earned nearly six million pounds in foreign exchange for the country.

Magnesite is an indispensable and valuable material for the metallurgical industry, and an important material for manufacturing aircraft and ship parts, as well as industrial materials such as manufacturing utensils.

### 镁砂出口五年换回外汇近六百万镑

1957-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    镁砂出口五年换回外汇近六百万镑
    据新华社鞍山25日电　闻名远东的鞍钢大石桥镁矿，几年来以大量优质镁砂等产品远销苏联、波兰、日本等地。从1952年到现在，鞍钢所出的镁矿产品已为国家换取了近六百万英镑的外汇。
    镁砂是冶金工业不可缺少的宝贵材料，是制造飞机、船舶零件及制造器皿等工业的重要材料。
