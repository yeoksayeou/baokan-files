    On August 14th, in the Sino-Soviet Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative in the suburbs of Beijing, thirty-two middle and primary school students who graduated this summer returned to the cooperative to participate in agricultural production. The picture shows Guo Zhen, the director of the cooperative (holding the grain ears in the center), introducing the cooperative's promising big white millet to the middle and primary school graduates who have returned to participate in agricultural production.
    Our reporter Gao Liang [photographed]

    8月14日，北京郊区中苏友好农业生产合作社有三十二名今年暑假毕业的中小学生回社参加农业生产。图为社主任郭振（中拿谷穗者）在向回社参加农业生产的中小学毕业生介绍本社丰收在望的大白谷。
