Title: Thirteenth Anniversary of the Surrender of German Troops: Paris Crowds Enthusiastically Commemorate

Author:
Date: 1957-08-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, August 26th. Several thousand Parisians participated in a rally held at the Montparnasse train station on August 25th to commemorate the thirteenth anniversary of the surrender of German troops.

On the rostrum of the conference were: Seine Department Councilors Duclos and Depreux, former commander of the French Forces Colonel Rol-Tanguy, Chairman of the Paris Municipal Council Levecque, and French General Confederation of Labor representative Mauvais.

Toreilles, chairman of the Paris Committee of the Liberation Movement, first delivered a speech, praising the spirit of unity shown by the patriots who participated in the resistance movement. Then, the Republican Guard band played the Marseillaise and other marches. The famous actor of the Comédie-Française, J. Bertaux, also enthusiastically recited two poems by Aragon.

Before the end of the commemorative meeting, representatives from all walks of life laid many flowers in front of the monument to the martyrs, and then the masses demonstrated, shouting "Speidel, go home!"

### 德国军队投降十三周年　巴黎群众热烈纪念

1957-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

    德国军队投降十三周年
　　巴黎群众热烈纪念
    新华社巴黎26日电　好几千巴黎人参加了8月25日在蒙巴那斯火车站举行的德国军队投降十三周年纪念大会。
    在大会主席台上的有：塞纳省议员杜克洛和德普勒、前法兰西部队指挥官罗耳汤纪上校、巴黎市政委员会主席勒韦克和法国总工会代表摩维。
    解放运动巴黎委员会主席托累首先致辞，他赞扬了参加抵抗运动的爱国者所表现的团结一致的精神。接着共和国保安队乐队奏了马赛曲和其他进行曲。法兰西喜剧院的著名演员日·贝尔多还热情洋溢地朗诵了阿拉贡的两首诗。
    在纪念大会结束以前，各界代表在烈士纪念碑前献了许多鲜花，然后群众进行了示威，高呼“斯派达尔滚回去”。
