Title: "Mao Zedong" Locomotive Safely Travels for Ten Years; Ministry of Railways Rewards Entire Crew

Author:
Date: 1957-08-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: The "Mao Zedong" locomotive crew, having safely traveled over one million kilometers, was rewarded today by the Ministry of Railways and the Beijing Railway Administration. The nine employees working on the locomotive received collective certificates of merit from the Ministry of Railways and the Beijing Railway Administration, as well as a bonus of 500 yuan from the Ministry of Railways. The thirty-five employees who had previously worked on the locomotive also received certificates of merit. In the evening, all the employees of the Fengtai Locomotive Depot of the Beijing Railway Administration held a celebration and award ceremony for them.

Xinhua News Agency, August 26th: The "Mao Zedong" locomotive crew, which set a new national record for freight locomotives safely traveling over one million kilometers, drove the locomotive, decorated with colorful flags, into the Fengtai Railway Station near Beijing at 4:53 pm today. Cai Lianxing, the train captain, stepped out of the driver's cab and warmly shook hands with Li Yong, the second driver of the locomotive and a renowned national model worker. Cai Lianxing happily told everyone on the platform: This locomotive has traveled one million and six hundred and fifty-two kilometers since it officially started running in January 1947. It has never had an accident in these ten years and seven months. He also said that the entire crew will ensure they strive for even higher goals.

During the First Five-Year Plan, this locomotive carried an extra 277,000 tons of cargo. In addition, it saved more than 1,040 tons of coal in the first four years and seven months of the First Five-Year Plan. (See attached photo)

### “毛泽东号”安全行驶十年  铁道部奖励包乘组全体成员

1957-08-28
第5版()
专栏：

　　“毛泽东号”安全行驶十年
    铁道部奖励包乘组全体成员
    据新华社27日讯　安全行车一百万公里以上的“毛泽东号”机车包乘组，今天受到了铁道部和北京铁路管理局的奖励。在机车上工作的九名职工得到了铁道部、北京铁路管理局的集体奖状和铁道部的五百元奖金。曾经在这个机车上工作过的三十五名职工也分别得到了奖状。晚上，北京铁路管理局丰台机务段的全体职工为他们举行了庆祝发奖大会。
    据新华社26日讯　创造货运机车安全行驶百万公里以上的全国新纪录的“毛泽东号”机车包乘组，在今天下午四时五十三分，驾驶着满扎彩旗的机车驶进了北京附近的丰台机车站。车长蔡连兴，从驾驶室里走下来，同这台机车的第二任司机长、全国著名的劳动模范李永热烈握手。蔡连兴在月台上高兴地告诉大家：这台机车从1947年1月正式行车到现在，已经行驶了一百万零六百五十二公里。在这十年零七个多月中从来没有发生过事故。他还说，他们包乘组的全体乘务人员要保证向更高的目标努力。
    这台机车在第一个五年计划期间，已经多拉了二十七万七千多吨货物。另外，还在第一个五年计划的前四年零七个月中，节约了一千零四十多吨煤。（附图片）
