Title: Powerful Typhoon to Strike Zhejiang Coast

Author: 
Date: 1957-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued the following typhoon emergency warning at 7:00 PM today:

    The center of a powerful typhoon in the Pacific Ocean was located at 21.2 degrees North latitude and 131.8 degrees East longitude at 2:00 PM today. The maximum wind force near the typhoon center is estimated at 50 meters per second, which is above force 12. Within 800 kilometers of the typhoon center, there are winds of force 6 or higher on the sea. The typhoon center is currently moving northwest at a speed of 7 to 9 knots per hour. It is expected that by 2:00 PM tomorrow (August 18th), the typhoon center will reach the vicinity of 24 degrees North latitude and 128 degrees East longitude. In the next 24 hours, there will be winds of force 8 to 10 in the southeastern part of the East China Sea, and winds of force 6 to 8 in the northwestern part of the East China Sea and the coastal waters south of the Yangtze River estuary. At 2:00 PM on August 19th, it is preliminarily estimated that the typhoon center will reach the sea area 350 kilometers northeast of Taiwan, and there will be winds of force 8 to 12 in the East China Sea. At 2:00 PM on August 20th, it is preliminarily estimated that the typhoon center will strike the Zhejiang coast. From the morning of the 20th, there will be winds of force 8 to 10 on the land from the Yangtze River estuary to the northern coast of Fujian, with a maximum wind force of up to force 12.
    The center of the typhoon in the South China Sea was located 200 kilometers directly east of Hainan Island at 2:00 PM today, with the maximum wind force near the center estimated at force 11. Within 350 kilometers of the typhoon center, there are winds of force 6. The typhoon center is currently moving in a northwest-by-west direction at a speed of 6 to 8 knots per hour, and is expected to make landfall near Qiongdong on Hainan Island between 2:00 AM and 6:00 AM tomorrow (August 18th). Tomorrow evening, the typhoon center will cross Hainan Island and enter the Beibu Gulf. In the next 36 hours, there will be winds of force 7 to 11 in the western part of the South China Sea, Hainan Island, the Leizhou Peninsula, and the Beibu Gulf, and there will be strong winds in the eastern part of the South China Sea, the coast of Guangdong, the Taiwan Strait, and the southern coast of Fujian.

### 强大台风将袭浙江沿海

1957-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    强大台风将袭浙江沿海
    据新华社17日讯  今天下午七时，中央气象科学研究所发布台风紧急警报如下：
    太平洋上的强大台风今天下午二时中心位置在北纬二十一点二度东经一百三十一点八度的地方，台风中心附近最大风力估计为每秒五十公尺，就是十二级以上。距台风中心八百公里以内的洋面上，有六级以上的大风。目前台风中心正以每小时七到九浬的速度向西北方向移动。预计明天（18日）下午二时，台风中心将达到北纬二十四度东经一百二十八度附近。未来二十四小时内，东海东南部将有八到十级的大风，东海西北部和长江口以南的沿海海面将有六到八级的大风。19日下午二时，台风中心将到达台湾东北三百五十公里的海面上，东海海面将有八到十二级的大风。20日下午二时，初步预计台风中心将侵袭浙江沿海。20日晨起，从长江口到福建北部沿海的陆地上将有八到十级的大风，最大风力达十二级。
    南海上的台风今天下午二时中心位置在海南岛正东二百公里的海面上，中心附近最大风力估计有十一级。距台风中心三百五十公里的海面上，有六级大风。目前台风中心正以每小时六到八浬的速度向西北偏西方向移动，预计明天（18日）二时到六时之间在海南岛的琼东附近登陆。明天傍晚，台风中心将越过海南岛进入北部湾。未来三十六小时内，南海西部、海南岛、雷州半岛和北部湾将有七到十一级的大风，南海东部、广东沿海、台湾海峡和福建南部沿海将有大风。
