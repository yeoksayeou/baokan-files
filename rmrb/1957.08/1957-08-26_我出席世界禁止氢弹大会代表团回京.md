Title: Delegation Attending World Conference Against Hydrogen Bombs Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-08-26
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 25th: The Chinese delegation to the Third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament, held in Tokyo, Japan, led by head of the delegation Cai Tingkai, returned to Beijing today.

### 我出席世界禁止氢弹大会代表团回京

1957-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　我出席世界禁止氢弹大会代表团回京
    新华社25日讯　出席在日本东京举行的第三届禁止原子弹、氢弹和争取裁军世界大会的中国代表团，在团长蔡廷锴率领下，已经在今天回到北京。
