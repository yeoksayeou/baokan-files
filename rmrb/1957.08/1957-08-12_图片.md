Title: Bumper Harvest
Author: Sheng Guo
Date: 1957-08-12
Page: 1

    After the socialist great debate, the political consciousness of the peasants in Gaocheng County, Hebei Province, has been greatly enhanced. They have realized the benefits of the unified purchase and sale of grain and are actively paying agricultural taxes and selling surplus grain. This is a large batch of grain being put into storage after purchase. Xinhua News Agency reporter Sheng Guo photographed.
