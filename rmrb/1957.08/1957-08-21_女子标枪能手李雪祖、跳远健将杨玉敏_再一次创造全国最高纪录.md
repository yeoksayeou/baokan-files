Title: Women's Javelin Expert Li Xuezhu, Long Jump Athlete Yang Yumin Once Again Create National Highest Records
Author:
Date: 1957-08-21
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 20th -- Women's javelin highest record holder Li Xuezhu, at the 1957 Shaanxi Province Track and Field, Weightlifting Games on the 19th, threw the javelin to a distance of 41.44 meters. This result is six centimeters higher than her own national highest record of 41.38 meters, which she created last year at the Sino-Soviet Track and Field Friendship Games in Kiev, Soviet Union. Another famous athlete, Yang Yumin, also created a new record of 5.46 meters in the women's long jump competition on the 18th. The national highest record in this event, also created by Yang Yumin herself, was 5.45 meters. Li Xuezhu and Yang Yumin are both athletes from the Track and Field Class of the Beijing Institute of Physical Education. They were invited to participate in the performance competitions of the 1957 Shaanxi Province Track and Field, Weightlifting Games.

### 女子标枪能手李雪祖、跳远健将杨玉敏　再一次创造全国最高纪录

1957-08-21
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

　　女子标枪能手李雪祖、跳远健将杨玉敏
　　再一次创造全国最高纪录
    据新华社西安20日电　全国女子标枪最高纪录保持者李雪祖，19日在1957年陕西省田径、举重运动会上，把标枪掷到了四十一公尺四十四公分远，这个成绩比她本人去年在苏联基辅中苏田径友谊赛中创造的四十一公尺三十八公分的全国最高纪录又提高了六公分。另一个著名运动员杨玉敏，也在18日女子跳远比赛中创造了五公尺四十六公分的新纪录。杨玉敏自己创造的这个项目的全国最高纪录是五公尺四十五公分。李雪祖和杨玉敏，都是北京体育学院田径班的运动员，她们是应邀来参加1957年陕西省田径、举重运动会的表演比赛的。
