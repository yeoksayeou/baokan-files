Title: Mongolian Delegation Arrives in Tirana
Author:
Date: 1957-08-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, August 24th: The government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Tsedenbal, arrived in Tirana by special plane from Berlin on the afternoon of August 23rd at the invitation of the government of the People's Republic of Albania.

Standing at the forefront of the welcoming crowd were Albanian Party and government leaders Shehu, Baluku, Mufti, Kabo, Marko, and others, as well as prominent social activists and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Tirana.

On the same day, the Mongolian government delegation visited Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, and then visited the leaders of the Council of Ministers of Albania and the Central Committee of the Labor Party.

### 蒙古代表团到达地拉那

1957-08-25
第6版()
专栏：

　　蒙古代表团到达地拉那
    据新华社地拉那24日电　以部长会议主席泽登巴尔为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团，应阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府的邀请，8月23日下午自柏林乘专机到达地拉那。
    站在欢迎人群最前列的有：阿尔巴尼亚党和政府领导人谢胡、巴卢库、穆夫蒂乌、卡博、马尔科等人以及著名的社会活动家和各国驻地拉那的外交使节。
    蒙古政府代表团当天访问了阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席列希，接着又访问了阿尔巴尼亚部长会议和劳动党中央的领导人。
