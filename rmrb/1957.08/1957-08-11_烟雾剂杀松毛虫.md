Title: Aerosol to Kill Pine Caterpillars
Author:
Date: 1957-08-11
Page: 5

    Last year, the Ministry of Forestry conducted a trial in Shangcheng County, Henan Province, using (26)111-A insecticidal aerosol to kill young pine caterpillars. In June and July this year, (6)111-A insecticidal aerosol was also tested in Taoyuan and Yiyang Counties in Hunan Province, and Yuhang County in Zhejiang Province to kill pine caterpillars and bamboo locusts. Experiments have shown that the aerosol has a very high killing power.
    This agent is easy to use and has high work efficiency, bringing great convenience to pest control work in forest areas. (Lin)

### 烟雾剂杀松毛虫

1957-08-11
第5版()
专栏：

    烟雾剂杀松毛虫
    林业部去年曾在河南省商城县进行了一次用（26）111—A杀虫烟雾剂毒杀幼龄松毛虫的试验，今年6、7月间分别在湖南省桃源、益阳，浙江省的余杭等县又试用了（6）111—A杀虫烟雾剂熏杀松毛虫和竹蝗。试验证明烟雾剂具有很高的杀伤力。
    这种药剂，使用方便，工作效率高，为林区的防虫工作带来很大方便。（林）
