Title: Tirana Volleyball Team Wins in Hangzhou Competition
Author:
Date: 1957-08-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, August 27th. The Albanian Tirana Men's Volleyball Team left Hangzhou this afternoon for a visit to Guangzhou.

    In a friendly match held in Hangzhou on the evening of the 26th, they defeated the Hangzhou Men's Volleyball Team with an overwhelming score of three to zero.

    The Tirana Volleyball Team arrived in Hangzhou on the 25th from Shanghai. They also visited scenic spots and historical sites such as West Lake in Hangzhou.

### 地拉那排球队在杭州比赛获胜

1957-08-28
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    地拉那排球队在杭州比赛获胜
    新华社杭州27日电　阿尔巴尼亚地拉那男子排球队今天下午离开杭州去广州访问。
    他们26日晚上在杭州举行的一场友谊比赛中，以三比零的压倒优势战胜了杭州市男子排球队。
    地拉那排球队是25日由上海来到杭州的。他们在杭州还游览了西湖等名胜古迹。
