Title: Finnish Speaker of Parliament and Prime Minister Receive Our Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-08-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, August 21st: Finnish Speaker of Parliament Fagerholm received the delegation from the Chinese National People's Congress on August 20th. During the meeting, the delegation presented the Constitution of the People's Republic of China to the Finnish Parliament.

　　The entire delegation then visited the Parliament Building and observed the ongoing parliamentary discussions.

　　Subsequently, Finnish Prime Minister Sukselainen received the entire delegation at his official residence. Prime Minister Sukselainen and delegation leader Saifuddin had a cordial and sincere conversation. Saifuddin said that the Chinese government and people highly appreciate the policy of peace and neutrality pursued by the Finnish government. The Chinese people cherish the friendship between China and Finland.

　　Sukselainen said that the Finnish people hope for peace and want to live in friendship and harmony with the people of all countries. He also spoke about his impressions of his visit to China last year and expressed his admiration for Chinese culture.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, August 21st: Saifuddin, the leader of the Chinese National People's Congress delegation, issued a written statement at the airport after arriving in Helsinki, the capital of Finland, on August 20th.

　　In his speech, he first thanked the Finnish Parliament for its kind invitation to visit Finland. He said: Our delegation has come to Finland for a ten-day visit with the sincere desire to seek peace and friendship, and to seek knowledge. We will do our utmost to strengthen the friendly ties between the Chinese and Finnish parliaments, increase mutual understanding and friendship between the people of China and Finland, and safeguard the world peace cause we share.

　　Saifuddin expressed the hope that the delegation's visit would achieve satisfactory results, just like the visit of the Finnish parliamentary delegation to China last year.

### 芬议长和总理接见我代表团

1957-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　芬议长和总理接见我代表团
    新华社赫尔辛基21日电　芬议会议长法格霍姆8月20日接见了中国全国人民代表大会代表团。在接见时，代表团把中华人民共和国宪法送给芬兰议会。
    代表团全体人员接着参观了议会大厦和正在进行的议会讨论。
    随后，芬兰总理苏克西莱宁在官邸接见了代表团全体人员。苏克西莱宁总理和赛福鼎团长作了亲切诚恳的交谈。赛福鼎说，中国政府和人民对芬兰政府奉行的和平中立政策有崇高的估价。中国人民非常珍视中芬两国的友谊。
    苏克西莱宁说，芬兰人民希望和平，希望生活在各国人民的友好和睦中。他还谈到他去年访问中国的印象，并且对中国的文化表示敬慕。
    新华社赫尔辛基21日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团团长赛福鼎8月20日到达芬兰首都赫尔辛基后，在机场上发表了书面讲话。
    他在讲话中首先感谢芬兰共和国议会邀请他访问芬兰的盛意。他说：我们代表团带着寻求和平和友谊、寻求知识的真诚愿望，到芬兰作十天的访问。我们将尽一切努力，加强中芬两国国会的友好联系，增加中芬两国人民的相互了解和友谊，维护我们共同关怀的世界和平事业。
    赛福鼎表示希望代表团的访问将同去年芬兰议会代表团在中国的访问一样，获致圆满的结果。
