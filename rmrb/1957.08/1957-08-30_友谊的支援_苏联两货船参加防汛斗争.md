Title: Friendship Support: Two Soviet Freighters Participate in Flood Control Efforts
Author:
Date: 1957-08-30
Page: 4

　　Friendship Support
　　Two Soviet Freighters Participate in Flood Control Efforts
　　Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 29th -- The two Soviet freighters CT-507 and CT-504, sent to support flood control efforts in the Heilongjiang province, arrived in Zhaoyuan County along the Songhua River on the 28th, laden with 702 tons of grain, 3,375 reed mats, and 60,000 straw sacks, at a time when the flood situation there was tense.
　　These two ships belong to the Soviet Heilongjiang Shipping Administration.
　　This friendly support has greatly encouraged the local flood control army.

### 友谊的支援  苏联两货船参加防汛斗争

1957-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　  友谊的支援
　  苏联两货船参加防汛斗争
    新华社哈尔滨29日电　苏联派来支援黑龙江防汛的货船CT五零七和CT五零四两条货船，满载着七百零二吨粮食、三千三百七十五张席子和六万条草包，在28日到了松花江沿岸汛情紧张的肇源县。
    这两只船都是属于苏联黑龙江航运管理局的。
    友谊的支援大大地鼓舞了当地的防汛大军。
