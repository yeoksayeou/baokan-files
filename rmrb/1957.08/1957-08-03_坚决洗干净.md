Title: Firmly Wash Clean
Author: Kuang Fei
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 8

Poetry Leaflet

    Firmly Wash Clean
    Kuang Fei
    The steel fortress
    Uses steel nails
    The steel army
    Must have steel soldiers
    The red warship
    Uses a red rudder
    The red warrior
    Must have a red heart
    The turncoat deserter
    Is the enemy's kin
    The Communist Youth League traitor
    Must be firmly washed clean
    (Selected from Peking University’s "Red Building")

### 坚决洗干净
匡非
1957-08-03
第8版()
专栏：诗传单

    坚决洗干净
    匡非
    钢铁的堡垒
    用钢铁的钉
    钢铁的军队
    要有钢铁的兵
    红色的战舰
    用红色的舵
    红色的战士
    要有红色的心
    变节的逃兵
    是敌人的亲
    共青团的叛徒
    要坚决洗干净
    （选自北京大学“红楼”）
