Date: 1957-08-09
Page: 5

    One Area Suffers Disaster, All Areas Support
    Beijing, Inner Mongolia, Shaanxi, Anhui and other places have currently allocated approximately 13 million jin of buckwheat, vegetable seeds and other late autumn crop seeds to support farmers in the flood-stricken areas of Heze and Linyi in Shandong Province to replant late autumn crops in a timely manner. This is a batch of buckwheat seeds that has arrived in Shandong and is about to be transferred to Shan County and other places in the Heze area. Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Jing

### 一地受灾各地支援（图片）
孙静
1957-08-09
第5版()
专栏：

    一地受灾各地支援
    北京、内蒙古、陕西和安徽等地，目前调拨了大约一千三百多万斤荞麦、蔬菜籽等晚秋作物种籽，支援山东省菏泽、临沂等水灾区农民及时补种晚秋作物。这是到达山东的一批荞麦种籽，即将转运菏泽专区的单县等地。　新华社记者　孙静摄
