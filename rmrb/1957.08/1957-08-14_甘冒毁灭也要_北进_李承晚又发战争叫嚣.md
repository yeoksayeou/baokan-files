Title: Risking Destruction to "March North" Lee Seung-man Again Utters War Cries
Author:
Date: 1957-08-14
Page: 7

　　Risking Destruction to "March North"
　　Lee Seung-man Again Utters War Cries
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 13th. Seoul dispatch: In a written statement to the United Press reporter on August 12th, Lee Seung-man again clamored for using war to "unify" the divided Korea. He said that he sometimes felt that, regardless of whether there are weapons or not, even at the risk of his own destruction, he should "march north." Lee Seung-man believes that unification without a "conflict" is impossible. Lee Seung-man also stated that on this issue, he "cannot wait forever."

### 甘冒毁灭也要“北进”  李承晚又发战争叫嚣

1957-08-14
第7版()
专栏：

    甘冒毁灭也要“北进”
    李承晚又发战争叫嚣
    新华社平壤13日电　汉城消息：李承晚在8月12日对合众社记者发表的一篇书面谈话中，再次叫嚣要以战争来“统一”分裂的朝鲜。他说，他有时觉得，不管有没有武器，甚至甘冒使自己遭到毁灭的危险，他也应当“北进”。李承晚认为，不经过一场“冲突”而实现统一是不可能的。李承晚还表示，在这个问题上，他“不能永远等待下去”。
