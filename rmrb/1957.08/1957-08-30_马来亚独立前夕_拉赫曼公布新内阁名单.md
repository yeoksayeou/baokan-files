Title: On the Eve of Malayan Independence, Rahman Announces New Cabinet List
Author:
Date: 1957-08-30
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kuala Lumpur, August 29th: On August 28th, Rahman, the Chief Minister of the Federation of Malaya, announced the list of the new cabinet of the soon-to-be-independent Federation of Malaya.

　　According to the list, the new cabinet will be headed by the current Chief Minister Rahman as Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Abdul Razak Hussein, Vice President of the United Malays National Organisation, will serve as Minister of Defense; and the current Minister of Finance, Lee Hau-Shik, will be re-elected.

　　The Federation of Malaya will declare its independence on August 31st.

### 马来亚独立前夕　拉赫曼公布新内阁名单

1957-08-30
第6版()
专栏：

    马来亚独立前夕
　　拉赫曼公布新内阁名单
    据新华社29日讯　吉隆坡消息：马来亚联合邦首席部长拉赫曼8月28日公布了即将宣告独立的马来亚联合邦的新内阁名单。
    根据这项名单，新内阁将由现任首席部长拉赫曼任总理兼外交部长；马来民族联合机构副主席阿卜杜勒·拉扎克·侯赛尼任国防部长；现任财政部长李孝式将获得连任。
    马来亚联合邦将在8月31日宣告独立。
