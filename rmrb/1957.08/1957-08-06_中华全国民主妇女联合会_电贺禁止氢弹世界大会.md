Title: All-China Women's Federation Cables Congratulations to the World Conference Against Hydrogen Bombs
Author:
Date: 1957-08-06
Page: 6

    All-China Women's Federation
    Cables Congratulations to the World Conference Against Hydrogen Bombs
    (Our Newspaper News) The All-China Women's Federation sent a congratulatory cable to the Third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs. The full text is as follows:
    On the occasion of the opening of the Third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament, Chinese women extend their highest respect to the people of all countries, especially the people and women of Japan, who are fighting against atomic weapons. We will fully support your just struggle against war and for the defense of peace. We wish the conference a success.
    All-China Women's Federation

### 中华全国民主妇女联合会  电贺禁止氢弹世界大会

1957-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主妇女联合会
    电贺禁止氢弹世界大会
    本报讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会给第三届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的贺电，全文如下：
    值此第三届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取裁军世界大会开幕之际，中国妇女谨向为反对原子武器而斗争的各国人民、特别是日本人民和妇女致以崇高的敬意，我们将全力支持你们反对战争、保卫和平的正义斗争。谨祝大会胜利。　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国民主妇女联合会
