Title: Portuguese Troops in Goa Attack Indian Patrol

Author:

Date: 1957-08-18
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 17th. According to a report from the Press Trust of India, Portuguese soldiers fired on an Indian patrol on the border between India and Goa on the night of August 14th.

It is reported that this is the fifth time Portuguese troops have violated the Indian border since the beginning of this year.

### 果阿葡兵袭击印巡逻队

1957-08-18
第7版()
专栏：

    果阿葡兵袭击印巡逻队
    新华社德里17日电  据印度报业托辣斯报道，葡萄牙士兵在8月14日夜间射击在印度和果阿边界上的印度巡逻队。
    据报道，这是今年初以来葡萄牙军队第五次侵犯印度边境。
