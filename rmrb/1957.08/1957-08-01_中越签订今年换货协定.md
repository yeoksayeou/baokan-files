Title: China and Vietnam Sign This Year's Barter Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-08-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 31st: The government trade delegations of the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam signed the "Agreement on Mutual Supply of Goods and Payments between China and Vietnam for 1957" this afternoon in Hanoi.

According to the provisions of the agreement, China will supply Vietnam with goods such as cotton cloth, cotton yarn, tires, steel, stationery, Chinese herbal medicine, and prepared medicines. Vietnam will supply China with agricultural products, forestry products, native products, mineral products, and handicrafts.

At the same time, according to the joint communiqué issued by the two governments on July 7, 1955, China and Vietnam signed a protocol on the People's Republic of China's assistance to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for 1957.

The protocol stipulates that in 1957, China will provide Vietnam with cotton, cloth, medicines, vehicles, steel, machinery, and other equipment needed for industrial production free of charge.

Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, signed the above documents on behalf of the People's Republic of China, and Phan Anh, Minister of Commerce, signed on behalf of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 中越签订今年换货协定

1957-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    中越签订今年换货协定
    新华社河内31日电　中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国的政府贸易代表团今天下午在河内签订了中越两国1957年度互相供应货物和付款协定。
    根据协定的规定，中国将供给越南棉布、棉纱、轮胎、钢材、文教用品、中药和成药等货物；越南将供应中国农产品、林产品、土产品、矿产品和手工艺品等货物。
    同时，中越双方根据两国政府1955年7月7日发表的联合公报，签订了关于1957年度中华人民共和国援助越南民主共和国的议定书。
    议定书规定，在1957年度，中国将无偿地供应越南棉花、布匹、药品、车辆、钢材、机器和工业生产所需要的其它器材。
    代表两国政府签署上述文件的，中华人民共和国方面是对外贸易部副部长林海云，越南民主共和国方面是商业部部长潘英。
