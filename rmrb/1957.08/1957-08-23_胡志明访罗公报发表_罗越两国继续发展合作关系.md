Title: Hồ Chí Minh's Visit to Romania Communiqué Published: Romania and Vietnam to Continue Developing Cooperative Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-08-23
Page: 6
　
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 22nd: Romanian newspapers today published the communiqué on the visit of President Hồ Chí Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to Romania.

The communiqué states that during his visit to Romania, President Hồ Chí Minh held talks with Romanian Workers' Party and government leaders Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Groza, and Stoica on issues of mutual concern.

The talks, conducted in a warm and friendly atmosphere, demonstrated the complete agreement of both sides on the struggle for peace, on strengthening the unity of the socialist camp led by the Soviet Union and the international workers' movement, and on other issues discussed.

During the talks, the representatives of both countries emphasized the necessity of promoting the relaxation of international tensions, strengthening cooperation between countries with different social systems, ceasing the arms race, and unconditionally prohibiting atomic and hydrogen weapons.

Both sides noted with satisfaction that political, economic, and cultural relations between Romania and Vietnam have developed and are developing in accordance with the interests of the two peoples and the interests of world peace.

The Romanian representatives pointed out that they fully support the position of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in its note of June 8, 1957, to the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference. In this note, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam requested effective measures to implement the provisions of the Geneva Accords regarding the peaceful reunification of Vietnam.

Both sides also agreed to continue developing the fraternal ties between the Romanian Workers' Party and the Vietnam Workers' Party, and to strengthen the work of exchanging experiences and information between the two parties.

### 胡志明访罗公报发表　罗越两国继续发展合作关系

1957-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

　　胡志明访罗公报发表
　　罗越两国继续发展合作关系
    新华社布加勒斯特22日电　罗马尼亚报纸今天发表了越南民主共和国主席胡志明访问罗马尼亚的公报。
    公报说，胡志明主席在访问罗马尼亚期间，曾经同罗马尼亚工人党和政府领导人乔治乌—德治、格罗查和斯托伊卡等就双方有关的问题进行了会谈。
    在热烈、友好气氛中所进行的会谈，证明了双方对为和平而斗争，对加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营和国际工人运动的团结，以及对其他一些讨论到的问题的看法完全一致。
    在会谈中，两国代表强调地指出了促进国际紧张局势的和缓、加强不同社会制度各国间的合作、停止军备竞赛、无条件地禁止原子武器和氢武器的必要性。
    双方满意地指出，罗越两国之间的政治、经济和文化关系已经得到了发展，并且正在按照两国人民的利益和世界和平的利益发展着。
    罗马尼亚代表指出，他们完全支持越南民主共和国在1957年6月8日给日内瓦会议两个主席的照会的立场。在这个照会中，越南民主共和国政府要求采取有效措施实现日内瓦协议中关于和平统一越南的规定。
    双方还同意继续发展罗马尼亚工人党和越南劳动党之间的兄弟联系，加强两党之间的交流经验和交换情报的工作。
