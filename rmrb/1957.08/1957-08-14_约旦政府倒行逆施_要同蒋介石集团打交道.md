Title: Jordanian Government Acts Perversely, To Deal with Chiang Kai-shek Clique
Author:
Date: 1957-08-14
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th, Amman Dispatch: Jordanian officials announced on August 12th that the Jordanian government has decided to "establish diplomatic relations" with the Chiang Kai-shek clique in Taiwan, which has been spurned by the Chinese people. King Hussein of Jordan approved this decision on August 12th.

Syria's "Al-Jamahir" newspaper published a commentary on August 10th on the Jordanian official's prematurely disclosed decision, stating that this is an action by a government without the support of the people to "recognize" another "government" without the support of the people, and it is a "recognition" of one U.S. imperialist agent by another U.S. imperialist agent.

The pro-Western government of Jordan came to power in April of this year after the United States orchestrated a coup.

### 约旦政府倒行逆施  要同蒋介石集团打交道

1957-08-14
第7版()
专栏：

    约旦政府倒行逆施
    要同蒋介石集团打交道
    新华社13日讯　安曼消息：约旦官方8月12日宣布，约旦政府已经决定同被中国人民唾弃的台湾蒋介石集团“建立外交关系”。约旦国王侯赛因在8月12日批准了这个决定。
    叙利亚“群众报”在8月10日就约旦官方预先透露的这项决定发表评论说，这是一个没有人民支持的政府“承认”另一个也没有人民支持的“政府”的行动，这是一个美帝国主义代理人对另一个美帝国主义代理人的“承认”。
    约旦的亲西方政府是在今年4月间美国策划了政变之后上台的。
