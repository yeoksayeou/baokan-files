Title: Former Indonesian Vice President Hatta to Visit China Next Month
Author:
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 2nd - Former Indonesian Vice President Hatta, at the invitation of Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai, is scheduled to visit China in September.

Hatta told an Indonesian news agency reporter on August 1 that when he visits China, he will focus on studying China's industrial and agricultural construction and the situation of the agricultural cooperative movement.

### 印度尼西亚前副总统  哈达下月访问我国

1957-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚前副总统
    哈达下月访问我国
    新华社雅加达2日电　印度尼西亚前副总统哈达应中国国务院总理周恩来的邀请，定于9月间到中国访问。
    哈达在8月1日对印度尼西亚新闻社记者说，他到中国访问的时候，将着重研究中国的工农业建设和农业合作化运动的情况。
