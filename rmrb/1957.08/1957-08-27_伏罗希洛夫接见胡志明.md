Title: Voroshilov Receives Ho Chi Minh
Author:
Date: 1957-08-27
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th - TASS reports: The Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, today received the President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh.
    President Ho Chi Minh arrived in Moscow this morning from Leningrad.

### 伏罗希洛夫接见胡志明

1957-08-27
第6版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫接见胡志明
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫今天接见了越南民主共和国主席胡志明。
    胡志明主席是在今天上午从列宁格勒到达莫斯科的。
