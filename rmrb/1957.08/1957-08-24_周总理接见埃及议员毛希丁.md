Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Egyptian Parliamentarian Mohieddin
Author: 
Date: 1957-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai received Khaled Mohieddin, member of the Egyptian National Assembly and editor-in-chief of "Al-Masaa," this evening. Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General. Also present were Egyptian Ambassador to China Ragab and his wife.

### 周总理接见埃及议员毛希丁

1957-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　  周总理接见埃及议员毛希丁
    新华社23日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了埃及国民议会议员、“晚报”总编辑哈立德·穆罕默德·毛希丁。接见时在座的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，秘书长吴茂荪。在座的还有埃及驻华大使拉加卜夫妇。
