Title: China Recognizes the Republic of Tunisia; Chairman Mao Sends Telegram to Congratulate Tunisian President Bourguiba
Author:
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd—The Tunisian Embassy in Egypt notified the Chinese Embassy in Egypt on July 30th that the Tunisian Constituent Assembly had decided on July 25th to abolish the Kingdom and establish a Republic, and that former Prime Minister Habib Bourguiba was elected President, and requested the Chinese Embassy in Egypt to forward the above decision to the Chinese government.

Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd—Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on August 2nd to congratulate Habib Bourguiba on his election as President of the Republic of Tunisia. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency Habib Bourguiba, President of the Republic of Tunisia:
	I am delighted to hear of the establishment of the Republic of Tunisia and Your Excellency's election as President of the Republic of Tunisia. I would like to express my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Tunisian people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name.

					Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
							August 2, 1957, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd—Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Mokaddem, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Tunisia, formally recognizing the Republic of Tunisia. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency Mokaddem, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Tunisia:
	I am honored to notify Your Excellency on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China that the People's Republic of China has officially decided to recognize the Republic of Tunisia. I wish the Republic of Tunisia prosperity and the Tunisian people happiness, and I wish the friendly relations between our two countries further development.

				Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
						August 2, 1957, in Beijing

### 我国承认突尼斯共和国  毛主席电贺突尼斯总统布尔吉巴

1957-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我国承认突尼斯共和国
    毛主席电贺突尼斯总统布尔吉巴
    新华社2日讯　突尼斯驻埃及使馆于7月30日照会中国驻埃及使馆，通知突尼斯制宪议会7月25日决议废除王国，成立共和国，原首相哈比布·布尔吉巴被选为总统，并请中国驻埃使馆将上述决议转告中国政府。
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东于8月2日致电祝贺布尔吉巴当选突尼斯共和国总统。电文如下：突尼斯共和国哈比布·布尔吉巴总统阁下：
    欣闻突尼斯共和国成立和阁下当选为突尼斯共和国总统，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义向阁下和突尼斯人民表示衷心的祝贺。
                              中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                        1957年8月2日于北京
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来致电突尼斯共和国外交部长莫卡德姆，正式承认突尼斯共和国。电文如下：突尼斯共和国外交部长莫卡德姆阁下：
    我荣幸地代表中华人民共和国政府通知阁下，中华人民共和国已正式决定承认突尼斯共和国。祝突尼斯共和国繁荣和突尼斯人民幸福，祝我们两国之间的友好关系获得进一步的发展。
                          中华人民共和国外交部长　周恩来
                                      1957年8月2日于北京
