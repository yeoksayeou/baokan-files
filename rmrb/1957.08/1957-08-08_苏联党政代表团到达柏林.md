Title: Soviet Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1957-08-08
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th - The Soviet Communist Party and government delegation arrived in Berlin, the capital of the German Democratic Republic, at noon today.
    The special train carrying the Soviet guests pulled into the platform of the East Station at 11:55 local time. The Soviet Party and government delegation was warmly welcomed by the leaders of the German Socialist Unity Party and the government of the German Democratic Republic, as well as leaders of other democratic parties and mass organizations of the German Democratic Republic.
    Groups of Berlin residents crowded the square next to the station, enthusiastically shouting slogans such as "Greetings to the Soviet Union" and "Long live the unbreakable fraternal friendship between Germany and the Soviet Union."
    At that time, a mass rally was held in this square. First Secretary of the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party, Ulbricht, and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, spoke at the meeting.

### 苏联党政代表团到达柏林

1957-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联党政代表团到达柏林
    新华社柏林7日电　苏联共产党和政府代表团今天中午到达德意志民主共和国的首都柏林。
    苏联客人乘坐的专车，在当地时间十一时五十五分开进了东车站的月台。苏联党和政府代表团受到德国统一社会党和德意志民主共和国政府的领导人以及德意志民主共和国其他民主党派和群众团体领导人的热烈欢迎。
    成群成群的柏林居民挤满车站旁广场，他们热烈欢呼“向苏联致敬”，“德苏两国牢不可破的兄弟友谊万岁”等口号。
    当时，就在这个广场上举行了群众大会。德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希和苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫在会上讲了话。
