Title: More Than 1,200 from Beijing Universities to Participate in Rural Socialist Debate
Author:
Date: 1957-08-25
Page: 4

    Beijing Higher Education Institutions to Have More Than 1,200 People
　　Participate in Rural Socialist Debate
    (Our Newspaper) News: More than 1,200 faculty, staff, and students from higher education institutions in Beijing will participate in the socialist debate in the countryside.
    Participants include faculty, staff, and students from more than twenty higher education institutions, including Peking University, Tsinghua University, Renmin University of China, Beijing Mining Institute, and Beijing Medical College. They will go to six districts, including Nanyuan, Changping, Fengtai, Shijingshan, and the Jingxi Mining Area, for a period of one and a half to two months. During this time, they will help farmers to raise their awareness through the great debate, criticize capitalist ideas in the countryside, and make the socialist system more consolidated.

### 北京高等学校将有一千二百多人　参加农村社会主义大辩论

1957-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    北京高等学校将有一千二百多人
　　参加农村社会主义大辩论
    本报讯　北京市高等院校将有教职员和学生一千二百多人，参加农村的社会主义大辩论。
    参加这一工作的有北京大学、清华大学、中国人民大学、北京矿业学院、北京医学院等二十余所高等院校的教职员和学生。他们将分别到南苑、昌平、丰台、石景山、京西矿区等六个区，住一个半月到两个月的时间。在这一段时间里，他们要帮助农民通过大辩论，提高觉悟，批判农村中资本主义思想，使社会主义制度更加巩固。
