Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Ceylonese Government Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-08-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Ceylonese Government Trade Delegation yesterday at noon. The delegation included the joint leader and Parliamentary Secretary of the Ministry of Finance, Mr. Wijesinghe; the joint leader, Parliamentary Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Personal Representative of the Prime Minister, Mr. Subasinghe; and the member and Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Commerce and Trade, Mr. Leggberg.

Present at the meeting was the Ceylonese Ambassador to China, Mr. Pereira. Also present were the Minister of Foreign Trade, Mr. Ye Jiuzhuang, and the Vice-Minister, Mr. Lei Renmin.

After the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a banquet for them.

### 周总理接见锡政府贸易代表团

1957-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见锡政府贸易代表团
    新华社16日讯  周恩来总理昨天午间接见了锡兰政府贸易代表团联合团长、财政部国会秘书维杰辛格，联合团长、外交部国会秘书、总理个人代表苏巴辛格及团员、商业贸易部常务秘书勒格贝格。
    接见时，锡兰驻华大使佩雷拉也在座。在座的还有我对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长雷任民。
    接见以后，周恩来总理并设宴招待他们。
