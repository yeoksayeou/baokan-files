Title: South Korean Progressive Party Leader Advocates Peaceful Reunification of Korea
Author:
Date: 1957-08-17
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th. Seoul dispatch: According to South Korea's Hapdong News Agency, Cho Bong-am, leader of South Korea's Progressive Party, said in a speech on the 12th anniversary of "August 15th" that all "democratic figures" should unite to strive for the peaceful reunification of Korea and the building of an independent, free, democratic, and prosperous country. He stated that the Progressive Party would stand at the forefront of this struggle. On the same day, South Korean Vice President and leader of the Democratic Party, Chang Myon, also delivered a speech, calling for the safeguarding of the South Korean constitution and emphasizing the need to stop police interference in elections. Chang Myon believes that eradicating corruption in the Syngman Rhee government is extremely important. He called for the reform of government institutions to "show political fairness."

### 南朝鲜进步党首领  赞成和平统一朝鲜

1957-08-17
第6版()
专栏：

    南朝鲜进步党首领
    赞成和平统一朝鲜
    新华社平壤16日电  汉城消息：据南朝鲜的合同通讯社报道，南朝鲜进步党首领曹奉岩在“八一五”十二周年发表的演说中说，所有的“民主人士”应该一致团结起来，为和平统一朝鲜，建成独立、自由、民主和繁荣的国家而斗争。他表示，进步党将站在这一斗争的最前面。同一天，南朝鲜副总统、民主党领袖张勉也发表了演说，要求维护南朝鲜宪法，强调必须停止警察对选举的干涉。张勉认为，肃清李承晚政府中的贪污极为重要。他要求革新政府机构，以“显示政治上的公平”。
