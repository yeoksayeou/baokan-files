Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts a Banquet for Japanese National Delegation Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs
Author:
Date: 1957-08-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd: Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet today to welcome the Japanese National Delegation Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs.

Those present included Xu Guangping, Liu Ningyi, Qian Sanqiang, and other responsible persons from relevant people's organizations.

Japanese Agricultural Technology Delegation Leaves Guangzhou
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 22nd: The Japanese Agricultural Technology Delegation, a group of twenty-five people led by the head of the delegation, Hisayoshi Muramatsu, left Guangzhou for Hong Kong this morning.

Zhang Qingtai, Director of the Cooperative Business Administration Bureau of our Ministry of Agriculture, accompanied the guests to Shenzhen.

The delegation arrived in Guangzhou on the evening of the 20th. On the 21st, they visited the Agricultural Science Institute and the Modiesha Agricultural Production Cooperative in the suburbs. On the evening of the same day, Ding Ying, Director of the Guangdong Branch of the Chinese Society of Agronomy, hosted a farewell banquet for the guests.

### 廖承志宴请日本禁止原子弹氢弹国民代表团

1957-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　廖承志宴请日本禁止原子弹氢弹国民代表团
    新华社22日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志今天设宴欢迎日本禁止原子弹、氢弹国民代表团。
    出席作陪的有有关人民团体负责人许广平、刘宁一、钱三强等人。日农业技术访华团离广州
    新华社广州22日电　日本农业技术访华团一行二十五人，在团长村松久义率领下，今晨离广州去香港。
    我农业部合作事业管理局局长张庆泰陪同客人到深圳。
    代表团是20日晚上到达广州的。21日，他们访问参观了这里的农业科学研究所和郊区的磨碟沙农业生产合作社。同日晚，中国农学会广东省分会主任丁颖为客人设宴送行。
