Title: Youth Representatives from Nineteen Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: Thirty-seven youth representatives from sixteen countries arrived in Beijing by plane this morning from Moscow, after attending the Sixth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation.

These youth representatives are from: Burma, Nepal, Ceylon, Syria, West Africa, Sudan, Mexico, Guatemala, Cuba, Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Ecuador, Venezuela, Uruguay, and Spain.

A thirty-two member Indian Youth Art Troupe, as well as forty-four Australian youth representatives and eight New Zealand youth representatives, also arrived in Beijing from Moscow yesterday and today.

### 十九个国家青年代表到京

1957-08-30
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

　　十九个国家青年代表到京
    据新华社29日讯　又有十六个国家的青年代表三十七人，在参加第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节后，于今天上午乘飞机从莫斯科到达北京。他们是应中华全国民主青年联合会的邀请来中国访问的。
    这些青年代表是来自：缅甸、尼泊尔、锡兰、叙利亚、西非、苏丹、墨西哥、危地马拉、古巴、阿根廷、巴西、智利、厄瓜多尔、委内瑞拉、乌拉圭和西班牙。
    印度青年艺术团一行三十二人和澳大利亚青年代表四十四人、新西兰青年代表八人，也先后在昨天和今天从莫斯科到达北京。
