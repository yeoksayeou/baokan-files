Title: Oman Imam Calls on Rebels to Continue Fighting, Appeals to Soviet and American Leaders to Intervene Against Britain, Arab League Asks Bandung Conference Nations to Stop British Aggression
Author:
Date: 1957-08-06
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 5th: The Imam of Oman, Ghalib bin Ali, has called on the Omani rebels to ignore British threats and continue fighting.
    Oman's representative in Cairo, Harsey, announced in Cairo on August 4th an order issued by Ghalib bin Ali to his troops on August 3rd. The order stated: "British aggressor planes today dropped threatening leaflets, demanding that we surrender to the tyrannical Sultan (of Muscat) supported by imperialist forces... But continue fighting. Their bombs cannot frighten us, because we are just, and Allah grants victory to those who stand on the side of justice."
    Harsey said on the same day that the Imam of Oman had sent appeals to the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and the President of the United States, Eisenhower, requesting them to intervene against Britain so that the people in the Imam's territory could seek a ceasefire and restore peace on the basis of independence.
    He said that the appeal reads: "We request you to intervene to achieve a ceasefire and hold negotiations to restore peace between us and the aggressors, thereby respecting the borders and independence and establishing our rights, while respecting the borders of the Sultanate of Muscat."
    Harsey also visited the US and Soviet embassies in Egypt today, requesting the Soviet and American governments to stop the British acts of aggression against Oman.
    Xinhua News Agency, August 5th: Cairo News: On August 4th, the Arab League condemned the British attack on Oman as a threat to peace in the Middle East and demanded that the Bandung Conference countries step forward to stop this aggression.
    In a memorandum sent to all Asian and African Bandung Conference countries, the Arab League said: "The British attack is an act that violates the principles of the United Nations, the Bandung Principles, and international principles."

### 阿曼教长号召起义者继续战斗  呼吁苏美两国领导人对英国进行干涉  阿拉伯联盟要求万隆会议国家制止英国侵略

1957-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    阿曼教长号召起义者继续战斗
    呼吁苏美两国领导人对英国进行干涉
    阿拉伯联盟要求万隆会议国家制止英国侵略
    据新华社开罗5日电　阿曼教长加勒布·本·阿里已经号召阿曼起义者不要理睬英国的威胁而继续战斗。
    阿曼驻开罗代表哈尔赛8月4日在开罗发表了加勒布·本·阿里在8月3日向他的军队发布的命令。这项命令说：“英国侵略飞机在今天投掷威胁性的传单，要我们向帝国主义军队所支持的暴虐的（马斯喀特）苏丹投降。……但是，继续战斗吧。他们的炸弹吓不倒我们，因为我们是正义的，真主给予站在正义一边的人以胜利。”
    哈尔赛同一天说，阿曼教长已经向苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和美国总统艾森豪威尔发出呼吁，请求他们对英国进行干涉，以便在教长所属地区的人在获得独立的基础上求得停火和恢复和平。
    他说，这封呼吁书写道：“我们请求你进行干预，以实现停火并举行谈判，以便在我们和侵略者之间恢复和平，从而在尊重我们的边界和独立并确立我们权利的条件下，尊重马斯喀特苏丹国的边界。”
    哈尔赛今天还先后访问了美国和苏联驻埃及的大使馆，要求苏联和美国的政府制止英国对阿曼的侵略行为。
    据新华社5日讯　开罗消息：阿拉伯联盟8月4日斥责英国进攻阿曼是对中东和平的威胁，并且要求万隆会议国家出面制止这种进攻。
    阿拉伯联盟在送给所有亚非万隆会议国家的备忘录中说：“英国的进攻是违反联合国原则、万隆原则和国际原则的行动。”
