Title: Chairman of the Icelandic United Socialist Party Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-08-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd August. Einar Olgeirsson, Chairman of the Icelandic United Socialist Party, his wife, and their two children, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to our country.

They were welcomed at the airport by Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee.

### 冰岛统一社会党主席到京

1957-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    冰岛统一社会党主席到京
    新华社2日讯　冰岛统一社会党主席埃纳尔·奥格尔森和他的夫人、子女一行四人前来我国访问，已经在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中共中央总书记邓小平，中共中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆和中央委员刘宁一等。
