Title: Syrian President Goes to Alexandria for Medical Treatment After Meeting with Nasser
Author:
Date: 1957-08-20
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 19th: Syrian President Kuwatli arrived in Alexandria by plane from Cairo on the afternoon of the 18th and immediately went to Mossad Hospital for medical treatment.

Kuwatli arrived in Cairo on the morning of the 18th. Before going to Alexandria, he held a lengthy meeting with Egyptian President Nasser. It is reported that they discussed issues related to "implementing a unified Arab policy" and the recent exposure of the American conspiracy to overthrow the current Syrian regime.

### 叙总统同纳赛尔会谈后到亚历山大港进行医疗

1957-08-20
第7版()
专栏：

    叙总统同纳赛尔会谈后到亚历山大港进行医疗
    据新华社19日讯　开罗消息：叙利亚总统库阿特利18日下午乘飞机从开罗到达亚历山大港，并立即到莫萨特医院去进行医疗。
    库阿特利是在18日上午到达开罗的。在前往亚历山大港以前，他同埃及总统纳赛尔进行了长时间的会谈。据报道，他们讨论了有关“执行统一的阿拉伯政策”问题和最近被揭露的企图推翻叙利亚现政权的美国阴谋。
