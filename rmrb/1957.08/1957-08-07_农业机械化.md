Title: Agricultural Mechanization
Author:
Date: 1957-08-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — China's first agricultural mechanization research institute—the Agricultural Mechanization Research Institute of the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences—was established in Beijing on the 5th. This institute currently has four research divisions: agricultural machinery use and repair, tractor power, agricultural machinery, and electrification. It is also arranging future research work according to the national scientific plan.

### 农业机械化

1957-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    农业机械化
    新华社6日讯　中国第一个农业机械化研究所——中国农业科学院农业机械化研究所5日在北京成立。这个研究所现在设立农业机械的使用修理、拖拉机动力、农业机械和电气化四个研究所，并且正在根据国家的科学规划安排今后的研究工作。
