Title: American Youth Representatives Visit Changchun

Author:
Date: 1957-08-30
Page: 4

　　Friendly Exchanges

    Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: The group of forty-one American youth representatives who came to China to visit after attending the Sixth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival left Beijing by train this morning to visit Changchun and other places. One of the representatives arrived in Beijing yesterday from Moscow.

    Two Burmese youth representatives also left Beijing by plane this morning and will return to their country via Kunming.

### 美青年代表去长春参观

1957-08-30
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

　　美青年代表去长春参观
    新华社29日讯　在参加第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节后来中国访问的美国青年代表四十一人，今天上午乘火车离开北京去长春等地参观。其中有一位代表是昨天从莫斯科到达北京的。
    缅甸青年代表二人也在今晨乘飞机离开北京，将经昆明回国。
