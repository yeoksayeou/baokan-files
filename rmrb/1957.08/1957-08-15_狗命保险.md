American Black Curtain

    Dog Life Insurance
    A new addition to the American way of life—now, in the United States, the lives of dogs can also be insured. A company for dog life insurance—"American Livestock Insurance Company"—has been established with the approval of the New York State authorities, with a capital of one million dollars.
    Any purebred dog between the ages of six months and nine years can be insured with this company by paying a certain annual premium. The maximum insurance amount for a dog is five thousand dollars.

### 狗命保险

1957-08-15
第7版()
专栏：美国黑幕

    狗命保险
    美国生活方式又增添了一个新的内容——现在在美国对狗的寿命也可以进行保险了。一个对狗命保险的公司——“美国牲畜保险公司”经纽约州当局的批准已经宣告成立，它的资本是一百万美元。
    凡是六个月到九岁之间的纯种狗都可以在这个公司里进行保险，每年付一定的保险金。最高的一条狗的保险额是五千美元。
