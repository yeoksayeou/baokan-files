Title: Asian and African Representatives Hold Symposium in Tokyo, Supporting the Holding of an Asian-African Solidarity Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-08-09
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 8th: Representatives of Asian and African countries attending the Third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament held a symposium on the evening of August 7th, unanimously expressing their desire to strengthen the unity of Asian and African countries and supporting the holding of an Asian-African Solidarity Conference in Cairo.

The symposium was hosted by the Japan Asian Solidarity Committee.

Indian representative Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru pointed out in her speech that it is likely that an Asian-African Solidarity Conference will be held in Cairo soon.

Chinese representative Zhao Puchu said in his speech that holding an Asian-African Solidarity Conference in Cairo is of great significance, as it will expand Asian solidarity into Asian-African solidarity.

Egyptian delegation leader El Din said in his speech that he first agreed to hold an Asian-African Solidarity Conference in Cairo, believing that doing so would further promote the Bandung Spirit.

### 亚非国家代表在东京座谈  支持举行亚非团结会议

1957-08-09
第7版()
专栏：

    亚非国家代表在东京座谈
    支持举行亚非团结会议
    据新华社东京8日电　参加第三届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取裁军世界大会的亚非国家代表在8月7日晚间举行的一次座谈会上，一致表示要加强亚非国家的团结，并且支持在开罗举行亚非团结会议。
    这次座谈会是由日本亚洲团结委员会主办的。
    印度代表拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人在发言中指出，最近有可能在开罗召开亚非团结会议。
    中国代表赵朴初在发言中认为，在开罗召开亚非团结会议是有重大意义的，它将使亚洲团结扩大为亚非团结。
    埃及代表团团长艾勒登在发言中首先表示赞成在开罗举行亚非团结会议，他认为这样做会更加发挥万隆精神。
