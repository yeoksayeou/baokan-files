Title: Riot in a Concentration Camp in Kenya
Author:
Date: 1957-08-24
Page: 7

　  Riot in a Concentration Camp in Kenya
    Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd, Nairobi dispatch: The Kenyan Ministry of Defence revealed on August 21st that a riot occurred on August 17th in a concentration camp holding members of the Kenyan patriotic armed organization Mau Mau. Two hundred prisoners clashed with seventy-five guards, resulting in eighteen prisoners injured and two guards injured and two dead.
    The British colonial authorities immediately dispatched a large number of police forces to suppress the riot, but it is said that they were not able to completely control the concentration camp.

### 怯尼亚一集中营骚动

1957-08-24
第7版()
专栏：

　  怯尼亚一集中营骚动
    新华社22日讯　内罗毕消息：怯尼亚国防部8月21日透露，囚禁怯尼亚爱国武装组织茅茅成员的一个集中营在8月17日发生骚动。营中二百个囚徒和七十五个看守冲突，结果囚徒有十八人受伤，看守有两人受伤，两人死亡。
    英国殖民当局马上调派大批警察部队去镇压骚动，但是据说并没有能够完全控制住这个集中营。
