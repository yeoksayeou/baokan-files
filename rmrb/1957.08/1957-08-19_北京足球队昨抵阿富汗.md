Title: Beijing Football Team Arrives in Afghanistan Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1957-08-19
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, August 18th: The Beijing Football Team, led by Huang Zhong, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation and Chairman of the Chinese Football Association, arrived in Kabul, the capital of Afghanistan, today.

Chinese guests were welcomed at the airport by Mohammad Farouk Saraj, President of the Afghan Olympic Federation.

The Beijing Football Team is invited to participate in the week-long celebration of the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, which begins on August 24th. Also invited are football teams from Iran and Turkey, table tennis teams from Iran and the United States, and a hockey team from India.

### 北京足球队昨抵阿富汗

1957-08-19
第3版()
专栏：体育新闻

    北京足球队昨抵阿富汗
    新华社喀布尔18日电　由中华全国体育总会副主席、中国足球协会主席黄中率领的北京足球队今天到达阿富汗首都喀布尔。
    中国客人们在机场受到阿富汗奥林匹克联合会主席穆罕默德·法鲁克·萨拉杰的欢迎。
    北京足球队是应邀参加8月24日开始的为期一周的阿富汗王国独立日庆祝活动的。同时受邀请的还有伊朗和土耳其的足球队，伊朗和美国的乒乓球队，以及印度的曲棍球队。
