Title: Syrian Prime Minister Welcomes Soviet Aid
Author:
Date: 1957-08-10
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 8th. Syrian Prime Minister al-Asali, commenting on the Soviet-Syrian communiqué on economic and technical cooperation on August 7, said: "The Syrian-Soviet joint communiqué includes everything Syria requested."

    Member of Parliament Husami (former People's Party member, now independent) said on August 7 that the Soviet-Syrian agreement "will help us complete our industrial plans and break free from the economic blockade imposed on our country by Western imperialism."

    The "Al-Nour" newspaper said in an editorial on August 8 that the agreement tells us how great a friend the Soviet Union is, because the Soviet Union is providing assistance precisely when all our construction plans are in dire need of funding.

### 叙总理欢迎苏联援助

1957-08-10
第7版()
专栏：

    叙总理欢迎苏联援助
    新华社大马士革8日电　叙利亚总理阿萨利8月7日在评论苏联和叙利亚关于发展经济技术合作的公报时说：“叙利亚—苏联联合公报包括了叙利亚要求的一切需要。”
    议员胡萨米（前人民党人，现在无党无派）8月7日说，苏联和叙利亚的协议“将帮助我们完成我们的工业计划，并且摆脱西方帝国主义对于我国所实施的经济封锁”。
    “光明报”在8月8日的社论中说，协议告诉我们，苏联是多么伟大的朋友，因为苏联正是在我们的一切建设计划极其需要经费的时候给予援助的。
