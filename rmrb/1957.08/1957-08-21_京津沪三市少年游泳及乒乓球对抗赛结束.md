Title: Beijing-Tianjin-Shanghai Junior Swimming and Table Tennis Competition Concludes
Author:
Date: 1957-08-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 19th. The 1957 Beijing, Tianjin, and Shanghai Junior Swimming and Table Tennis Competition, which began on the 17th in Beijing, concluded today. Tianjin's junior male and female swimmers won the team first place in the swimming competition. In the thirty-two events of the four strokes (freestyle, backstroke, butterfly, and breaststroke), they achieved seventeen first places. Second and third place went to the Shanghai and Beijing teams, respectively.

　　The three-city junior swimming and table tennis competition was held as a team event, except for the table tennis competition, which was a mixed competition of adults and juniors. The table tennis competition was very intense. The Shanghai team repeatedly gained the initiative in the competition with diverse serving tactics, winning the championship in both the men's and women's team events. Their scores against the Beijing and Tianjin men's teams were both five to two; their scores against the Beijing and Tianjin women's teams were three to two and three to one, respectively.

　　The Beijing team won second place in the three-city table tennis competition for men, and the Tianjin team came in third. The second and third places for women were the Tianjin and Beijing teams, respectively.

### 京津沪三市少年游泳及乒乓球对抗赛结束

1957-08-21
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

　  京津沪三市少年游泳及乒乓球对抗赛结束
    新华社19日讯　17日开始在北京举行的1957年北京、天津、上海三城市少年游泳及乒乓球对抗赛，今天全部结束。天津市少年男、女游泳运动员获得了游泳比赛的团体第一名。他们在四种姿式（自由泳、仰泳、蝶泳、蛙泳）三十二个项目的比赛中，取得了十七项第一名。第二名和第三名分别是上海队和北京队。
    三城市少年游泳及乒乓球对抗赛，都是举行团体比赛，唯乒乓球赛是由成人和少年混合在一起比赛的。乒乓球对抗赛打得十分激烈，上海队以多样化的发球战术多次赢得了比赛的主动权，而夺得了男子组和女子组团体赛的冠军。他们胜北京和天津男子队的比数都是五比二；胜北京和天津女子队的比数分别是三比二和三比一。
    获得三城市乒乓球对抗赛男子组第二名的是北京队，天津队得第三名。女子组第二名和第三名分别是天津队和北京队。
