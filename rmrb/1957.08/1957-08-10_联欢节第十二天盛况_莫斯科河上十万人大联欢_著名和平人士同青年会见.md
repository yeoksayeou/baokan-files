Title: Highlights of the Twelfth Day of the Youth Festival: 100,000 People Join the Celebration on the Moscow River; Prominent Peace Activists Meet with Youth

Author:

Date: 1957-08-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 9th: On the twelfth day of the festival (August 8th), youth from various countries held a "Grand River Carnival" on the Moscow River. The art competition held its closing ceremony and awards ceremony. The youth met with famous peace activists from various countries.

The Youth Festival art competition has ended. The awards ceremonies were held on the 8th in the Hall of Columns of the Trade Union Building, the Tchaikovsky Concert Hall, and the Conservatory Hall, respectively.

The Soviet Ministry of Culture held a reception on this day to entertain the distinguished guests of the festival. Attendees included people from the literary and artistic circles of various countries and members of the judging committee of the festival's art competition. Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov expressed his gratitude to them for their assistance to the festival.

On this day, famous peace activists from various countries, including Wen Youzhang, Tikhonov, Ehrenburg, Jacques Denis, Archbishop Nikolai, Han Xueye, Hikmet, and Xiao San, met with representatives of the festival at the International Meeting Club. Famous social figures from various countries issued a letter to the youth of the five continents, calling on young people to resolutely strive for an immediate halt to the production and testing of nuclear weapons, and to further unite to defend peace.

In recent days, youth from various countries have continuously visited the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum in Red Square. On this day, eight hundred French youth representatives from the French Communist Youth League, the French Women's Youth League, and the French Communist Student Union laid wreaths here. Youth from Vietnam, Syria, Bulgaria, Ghana, Israel, Mongolia, Indonesia, and many other countries also visited the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum.

Young physicists from various countries visited Dubna, the location of the International Atomic Nuclear Research Center near Moscow. They visited the world's largest particle accelerator—a 10 billion electron volt synchrophasotron, a cyclotron for accelerating high-charge ions under construction, and a one-kilometer-long neutron physics laboratory.

The three-day discussion among young physicians and medical students from various countries on issues of healthcare organization, medical treatment, and cadre training concluded on the 8th. (Photo attached)

Our award-winning artists cheerfully march on the streets of Moscow after receiving their awards.

### 联欢节第十二天盛况  莫斯科河上十万人大联欢  著名和平人士同青年会见

1957-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

    ·联欢节第十二天盛况·
    莫斯科河上十万人大联欢
    著名和平人士同青年会见
    据新华社莫斯科9日电　联欢节第十二天（8月8日），各国青年在莫斯科河上举行了“河上大联欢”，艺术竞赛举行了闭幕式和发奖大会，青年们同各国著名和平人士举行了会见。
    联欢节艺术竞赛已经结束。8日在工会大厦圆柱大厅、柴可夫斯基音乐厅和音乐院大厅分别举行了发奖大会。
    苏联文化部在这一天举行了招待会招待联欢节贵宾。出席的有各国文学艺术界人士和联欢节艺术竞赛评判委员会委员等。苏联文化部长米哈伊洛夫对他们给予联欢节的帮助表示了谢意。
    这一天各国著名和平人士文幼章、吉洪诺夫、爱伦堡、雅克·德尼、尼古拉大主教、韩雪野、希克梅特、萧三等在国际会见俱乐部同联欢节代表们举行了会见。各国著名社会人士发表了告五大洲青年书，号召青年们坚决争取立即停止核武器的生产和试验，进一步团结起来保卫和平。
    这些天来，各国青年陆续不断地前往红场谒列宁和斯大林陵墓。这一天法国青年八百人代表法兰西共产主义青年联盟、法兰西女青年联盟、法兰西共产主义学生联盟在这里献了花圈。越南、叙利亚、保加利亚、加纳、以色列、蒙古、印度尼西亚等许多国家的青年也来谒了列宁和斯大林陵墓。
    各国青年物理学家参观了国际原子核研究中心所在地——莫斯科近郊的杜布纳。他们参观了世界最大的粒子加速器——一○○亿电子伏特的同步稳相加速器，正在建设中的加速高电荷离子用的回旋加速器和长达一公里的中子物理学实验室。
    历时三天的各国青年医师和医科学生关于保健组织、医疗和干部培养问题的讨论会在8日闭幕。（附图片）
    我国获奖的艺术家们在领奖后愉快地在莫斯科大街上行进着。
                  新华社记者　吴化学摄（无线电传真照片）
