    Brief News Items

    US Construction Workers' Strike Ends Victoriously
    New York dispatch: The strike of 8,000 construction workers in California, USA, has ended. The strike lasted forty-seven days. The workers' demands for higher wages were met.

    South Vietnamese Authorities Intensify Persecution of Overseas Chinese
    According to Saigon newspapers, the Ngo Dinh Diem authorities in South Vietnam are intensifying their use of threats to ruin Overseas Chinese businesses, forcing Overseas Chinese merchants to adopt Vietnamese nationality.
    Ngo Dinh Diem's "Commissioner for Chinese Affairs," Nguyen Van Hoang, recently summoned the Overseas Chinese rice millers of Cho Lon, ordering them to cease operations by September 6. Nguyen Van Hoang also told these merchants that they could continue operations if they adopted Vietnamese nationality. (Xinhua News Agency)

专栏：简讯

    简讯
    美建筑工人罢工胜利结束
    纽约消息：美国加利福尼亚州八千名建筑工人的罢工已经结束。这次罢工继续了四十七天。工人提高工资的要求得到了满足。
    南越当局加紧迫害华侨
    据西贡报纸报道，南越吴庭艳当局又加紧用威胁摧残华侨企业的手段来迫使华侨商人加入越南籍。
    吴庭艳的“中华事务专员”阮文黄最近召见了堤岸的华侨碾米商人，限令他们在9月6日结束营业。阮文黄同时对这些商人说，如果加入越南籍就可继续营业。（新华社）
