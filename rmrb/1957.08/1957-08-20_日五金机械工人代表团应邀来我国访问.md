Title: Japanese Metal and Machinery Workers Delegation Arrives in China for a Visit at Invitation

Author: 
Date: 1957-08-20
Page: 4

    Friendly Exchanges

    Japanese Metal and Machinery Workers Delegation Arrives in China for a Visit at Invitation
    (Xinhua News Agency, 18th) The Japanese Metal and Machinery Workers Delegation, led by head of the delegation Kiyoshi Kawakami, arrived in Beijing by train tonight. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the China First Machine Building Trade Union.

### 日五金机械工人代表团应邀来我国访问

1957-08-20
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    日五金机械工人代表团应邀来我国访问
    据新华社18日讯　日本五金机械工人访华代表团一行十七人在团长川上清率领下，今天晚上乘火车到达北京。代表团是应中国第一机械工会邀请来中国参观访问的。
