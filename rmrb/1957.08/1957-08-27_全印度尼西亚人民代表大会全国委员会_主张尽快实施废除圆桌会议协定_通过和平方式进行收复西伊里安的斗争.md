Title: All-Indonesia People's Congress National Committee Advocates for the Prompt Implementation of the Abolition of the Round Table Conference Agreement and the Struggle to Recover West Irian Through Peaceful Means
Author:
Date: 1957-08-27
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 26th: The plenary session of the All-Indonesia People's Congress National Committee, which concluded today, passed a resolution requesting the government to promptly implement the abolition of the Round Table Conference Agreement in order to break free from the constraints of the old financial and economic policies.

The meeting also called on the National Council, the Parliament, and the government to conduct the struggle to recover West Irian through peaceful means and through the United Nations. The resolution stated that if the Netherlands is still unwilling to return West Irian, it should be declared an aggressor and bear all the consequences.

The All-Indonesia People's Congress National Committee also condemned the British aggression against Oman.

The meeting also requested the Indonesian government to take measures to alleviate the people's burden and demanded that workers' right to strike not be further restricted.

Prior to this, President Sukarno spoke at the opening ceremony of this session of the organization's National Committee. He advocated for internal reorganization of the organization, but without changing its purpose and objectives. In his speech, he emphasized that Indonesia must achieve one hundred percent freedom, liberation, and unification.

The All-Indonesia People's Congress National Committee has already decided at this plenary session to establish a reorganization committee to formulate a reorganization plan and prepare for the convening of the Second Congress on November 10 to discuss internal reorganization issues. Sukarno appointed six non-members to participate in the reorganization committee. According to National Committee Secretary-General Hanafi, the current situation in Indonesia requires the organization to decide on a new direction.

### 全印度尼西亚人民代表大会全国委员会  主张尽快实施废除圆桌会议协定  通过和平方式进行收复西伊里安的斗争

1957-08-27
第7版()
专栏：

　　全印度尼西亚人民代表大会全国委员会
　  主张尽快实施废除圆桌会议协定
    通过和平方式进行收复西伊里安的斗争
    新华社雅加达26日电　今天闭幕的全印度尼西亚人民代表大会全国委员会全体会议通过决议，要求政府尽快实施废除圆桌会议协定，以摆脱旧的财经政策的拖累。
    会议还要求民族委员会、国会和政府以和平方式、通过联合国进行收复西伊里安的斗争。决议说，如果荷兰还是不愿意归还西伊里安，就宣布荷兰为侵略者，并且要由它来担负一切后果。
    全印度尼西亚人民代表大会全国委员会还谴责了英国对阿曼的侵略行为。
    会议还要求印度尼西亚政府采取减轻人民负担的措施，并且要求不要再限制工人的罢工权利。
    在这以前，苏加诺总统在这个组织的这一次全国委员会全体会议开幕式上讲了话。他主张这个组织进行内部改组，但是，它的目的和宗旨不改变。他在讲话中强调印度尼西亚全国必须获得百分之百的自由解放和统一。
    全印度尼西亚人民代表大会全国委员会已经在这次全体会议上决定成立一个改组委员会来制定改组计划，并且进行筹备在11月10日召开第二届代表大会来讨论改组内部问题。苏加诺指定了六个会外人士参加改组委员会。据全国委员会秘书长哈纳菲说，印度尼西亚目前形势使得这个组织要决定新的方针。
