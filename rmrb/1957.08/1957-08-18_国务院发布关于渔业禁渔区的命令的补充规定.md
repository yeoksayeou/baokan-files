Title: The State Council Issues Supplementary Regulations Regarding the Order on Fishing Restricted Areas
Author:
Date: 1957-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 17th: The State Council of the People's Republic of China issued supplementary regulations on July 26th regarding the order on fishing restricted areas for motorized trawlers in the Bohai Sea, Yellow Sea, and East China Sea.

The regulations state that, recently, due to improved security in the sea area south of 29 degrees north latitude, fishery production has gradually recovered. In order to protect coastal aquatic resources and avoid disputes between motorized trawler fishing and fishermen's sailing vessel fishing, the coastal waters west of the connecting lines of the following three points are designated as a trawler fishing restricted area: the 17th base point (29 degrees north latitude, 122 degrees 45 minutes east longitude), the 18th base point (27 degrees 30 minutes north latitude, 121 degrees 30 minutes east longitude), and the 19th base point (27 degrees north latitude, 121 degrees 10 minutes east longitude).

### 国务院发布关于渔业禁渔区的命令的补充规定

1957-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院发布关于渔业禁渔区的命令的补充规定
    新华社17日讯  中华人民共和国国务院7月26日发布了关于渤海、黄海和东海机轮拖网渔业禁渔区的命令的补充规定。
    规定说，近来由于北纬二十九度以南海面的治安情况好转，渔业生产也逐渐恢复。为了保护沿海水产资源和避免机轮拖网渔业和渔民帆船渔业的纠纷，将第十七基点（北纬二十九度、东经一百二十二度四十五分）、第十八基点（北纬二十七度三十分、东经一百二十一度三十分）、第十九基点（北纬二十七度，东经一百二十一度十分）这三个基点的联结线以西的我国沿海划为拖网渔业禁渔区。
