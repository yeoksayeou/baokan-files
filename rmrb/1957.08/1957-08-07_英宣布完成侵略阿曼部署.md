Title: Britain Announces Completion of Deployment for Aggression Against Oman
Author:
Date: 1957-08-07
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Manama, 6th - A British official spokesman in Manama (on Bahrain Island) announced today that British ground forces have completed preparations to launch an attack on the Omani insurgents.

He said that Brigadier Robertson, commander of the British ground forces directing the attack, had established a logistics headquarters at the front. He said, "The assembly of British troops, including Cameroonian forces, Trucial Oman Scouts, and the Northern Frontier Regiment of the Muscat forces, has been completed. Almost all necessities, gasoline, rations, and ammunition have been airlifted to the administrative base at the front."

### 英宣布完成侵略阿曼部署

1957-08-07
第7版()
专栏：

    英宣布完成侵略阿曼部署
    据新华社6日讯　马纳马（在巴林岛）消息：英国官方发言人今天在马纳马宣布，英国地面部队已经完成对阿曼起义者发动攻击的准备工作。
    他说，指挥这次攻击的英国地面部队司令罗伯逊准将已经在前线设立了一个后勤总部。他说，“英国军队，包括喀麦隆人部队，特鲁西尔阿曼侦察兵以及马斯喀特部队的北部边疆团的集结工作已经完成。差不多所有的必需品、汽油、口粮和弹药都已经空运到前线的行政基地。”
