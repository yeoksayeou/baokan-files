Title: Delegations from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing to Participate in "Asian Film Week"
Author:
Date: 1957-08-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: Film delegations from Japan and Pakistan, invited to China to participate in "Asian Film Week," arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Members of film delegations from Indonesia, Syria, and Vietnam arrived in Beijing successively on the 25th and 26th.

The Japanese film delegation consists of three members, including Yuko Mochizuki, the female lead in the Japanese film "Rice." The head of the delegation, Kyohiko Ushihara, is one of Japan's oldest directors and had already arrived in Beijing earlier.

The Pakistani film delegation consists of five members. The head of the delegation, Ashfaq Malik, is the director of the Pakistani film "Rebellion." Members include Mussarat Nazir, the female lead in "Rebellion," and Sudhir, the male lead.

The Indonesian film delegation also consists of five members. Kotto Sukardi, who is leading the delegation, is the screenwriter and director of the Indonesian film "Chayaburana."

Khaled Hamada, a director, has already arrived in Beijing as part of the Syrian film delegation.

The Vietnamese film delegation consists of four members, led by Tran Nam Huong, the Acting Director of the Vietnam Central Film Bureau.

The Cambodian film delegation left Phnom Penh today for Beijing with four color documentaries. One documentary is about Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Cambodia, and another is about the coronation of Cambodian King Suramarit.

### 参加“亚洲电影周”　各国代表团陆续到京

1957-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　参加“亚洲电影周”
　　各国代表团陆续到京
    据新华社27日讯　应邀来中国参加“亚洲电影周”的日本和巴基斯坦的电影代表团，今天下午乘飞机来到北京。印度尼西亚、叙利亚和越南的电影代表团人员，已在25日和26日先后到达北京。
    日本电影代表团由三人组成，其中有日本影片“米”的女主角望月优子。代表团团长牛原虚彦是日本最老的导演之一，他已先期到京。
    巴基斯坦电影代表团共五人，代表团团长阿什法德·马利克是巴基斯坦影片“叛逆”的导演，团员中有影片“叛逆”的女主角穆萨拉特·纳齐尔和男主角苏齐尔。
    印度尼西亚电影代表团也由五人组成，率领代表团的科托·苏卡迪是印度尼西亚影片“查雅布拉纳”的编导。
    叙利亚电影代表团已经到达北京的是导演哈立德·哈马德。
    越南电影代表团共四人，由越南中央电影局代局长陈南馨率领。
    柬埔寨电影代表团今天携带四个彩色纪录片，离开金边前来北京。纪录片中一个是关于周恩来总理访问柬埔寨的情况，一部是柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特加冕的情况。
