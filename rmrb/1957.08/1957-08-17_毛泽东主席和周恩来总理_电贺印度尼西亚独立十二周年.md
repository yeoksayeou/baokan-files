Title: Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai Send Telegrams of Congratulations on the 12th Anniversary of Indonesian Independence
Author:
Date: 1957-08-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia on the 15th, congratulating him on the 12th anniversary of the Republic of Indonesia's independence. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia:

    On the occasion of the 12th anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Indonesia, I, on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Indonesian people.

    The profound friendship between China and Indonesia has been further consolidated and developed through Your Excellency's friendly visit to China last year. I wish the friendly relations between China and Indonesia will continue to strengthen, and wish Your Excellency and the Indonesian people greater achievements in the cause of safeguarding national sovereignty and strengthening national unity. I wish the Republic of Indonesia prosperity and its people happiness.
                                  Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                   August 15, 1957, in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Premier Juanda of the Republic of Indonesia on the 15th, congratulating him on the 12th anniversary of the Republic of Indonesia's independence. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Premier Juanda of the Republic of Indonesia:

    On the occasion of the 12th anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Indonesia, I, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name, extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the Government of the Republic of Indonesia.

    The Chinese people are deeply concerned and sympathetic to the struggle of the Indonesian people against colonialism, in defense of national sovereignty, and for the strengthening of national unity, and believe that the Indonesian people will achieve greater victory in this struggle.

    We are pleased to see the growing friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries. We sincerely hope that this friendly relationship will be increasingly consolidated and strengthened in the common effort to promote the Bandung Spirit, promote friendly cooperation between Asian and African countries, and safeguard world peace.
                            Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                          August 15, 1957, in Beijing

### 毛泽东主席和周恩来总理  电贺印度尼西亚独立十二周年

1957-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席和周恩来总理
    电贺印度尼西亚独立十二周年
    新华社16日讯  毛泽东主席15日致电印度尼西亚共和国苏加诺总统，祝贺印度尼西亚共和国独立十二周年。电文如下：印度尼西亚共和国苏加诺总统阁下：
    欣逢印度尼西亚共和国独立十二周年，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，向阁下和印度尼西亚人民致热诚的祝贺。
    中国和印度尼西亚两国间的深厚友谊，通过阁下去年对中国的友好访问，已经获得进一步的巩固和发展。祝中国和印度尼西亚两国间的友好关系不断增强，祝阁下和印度尼西亚人民在维护国家主权和加强民族团结的事业中获得更大的成就，祝印度尼西亚共和国国家昌盛和人民幸福。
                                  中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东
                                   1957年8月15日于北京
    新华社16日讯  周恩来总理15日致电印度尼西亚共和国朱安达总理，祝贺印度尼西亚共和国独立十二周年。电文如下：印度尼西亚共和国朱安达总理阁下：
    值此印度尼西亚共和国独立十二周年之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府并以我个人的名义，向阁下和印度尼西亚共和国政府致热烈的祝贺。
    中国人民十分关怀和同情印度尼西亚人民反对殖民主义、维护国家主权和加强民族团结的斗争，并且相信印度尼西亚人民在这一斗争中将获得更大的胜利。
    我们欣慰地看到我们两国之间的友好合作关系日益发展。我们衷心地希望这种友好关系，将在发扬万隆精神、促进亚非各国间的友好合作和维护世界和平的共同努力中，日益巩固和加强。
                            中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来
                                          1957年8月15日于北京
