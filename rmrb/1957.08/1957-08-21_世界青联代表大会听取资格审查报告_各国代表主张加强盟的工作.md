Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Congress Hears Credentials Report; Delegates from Various Countries Advocate Strengthening the Work of the Federation
Author:
Date: 1957-08-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Kiev, August 20th: The World Federation of Democratic Youth (WFDY) Congress heard the report of the Credentials Committee on the 19th. The representative of the committee, Guerrero, a Chilean youth representative, stated that this WFDY Congress is more representative than the previous one.

Guerrero said that participating in this Congress are representatives of organizations that implement national policies and oppose colonial policies, representatives from newly independent Afro-Asian colonial countries, representatives from socialist and communist organizations, as well as representatives from youth labor unions, student organizations, cultural, sports, travel, and children's organizations. He said that this proves that the policies implemented by the WFDY are in line with the interests of young people of all countries and all tendencies.

In addition to hearing the report of the Credentials Committee, the Congress continued to discuss the first and second items on the agenda.

Youth representatives from Bulgaria, Sudan, Greece, Japan, Spain, Jordan, Ireland, Madagascar, Czechoslovakia, Poland, East Germany, Brazil, and an observer from Yugoslavia also spoke at the meeting. In their speeches, they talked about the role and influence of the WFDY among young people in their respective countries, the active participation of young people in the movements for peace and national independence, and made some suggestions to the Congress.

### 世界青联代表大会听取资格审查报告　各国代表主张加强盟的工作

1957-08-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　世界青联代表大会听取资格审查报告
　　各国代表主张加强盟的工作
　  据新华社20日讯　据塔斯社基辅讯：世界民主青年联盟代表大会19日听取了大会资格审查委员会的报告。代表委员会作报告的是智利青年代表格雷罗。他说，这一届世界青联代表大会比上届代表大会更有代表性。
    格雷罗说，参加这届大会的有执行民族政策和反对殖民政策的组织的代表，有最近才宣布独立的亚非殖民地国家的代表，也有社会主义和共产主义组织的代表，以及青年工会、学生、文化、体育、旅行、儿童等组织的代表。他说，这证明世界民主青年联盟所执行的政策是符合各国和各种倾向的青年的利益的。
    除听取了资格审查委员会的报告外，大会继续讨论议程中的第一个和第二个问题。
    在会上发言的还有保加利亚、苏丹、希腊、日本、西班牙、约旦、爱尔兰、马达加斯加、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、民主德国、巴西等国的青年代表和南斯拉夫的观察员。他们在发言中谈到了世界青联在本国青年中的作用和影响，谈到了各国青年积极参加争取和平和民族独立的运动的情况，并且向大会提出了一些建议。
