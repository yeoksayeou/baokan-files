Title: Premier Zhou Meets Syrian Parliamentarian Joseph
Author:
Date: 1957-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai today received Syrian Parliamentarian Hassan Joseph, his wife, and their daughter.

Those accompanying the reception included Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee Liao Chengzhi, Standing Committee members Liu Ningyi, Xu Baoju, and President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Chu Tunan.

### 周总理接见叙议员约瑟夫

1957-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见叙议员约瑟夫
    新华社23日讯　周恩来总理今天接见了叙利亚国会议员哈森·约瑟夫夫妇及女儿等。
    陪同接见的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、常务委员刘宁一、许宝驹和中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南等人。
