Title: Beijing and Vladivostok Department Stores Set Conditions to Launch Socialist Competition
Author:
Date: 1957-08-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd: The entire staff of the Wangfujing Department Store of the Beijing Department Store Company accepted the proposal of the Vladivostok Department Store in the Soviet Union, and both sides launched a socialist friendship labor competition.

　　This morning, the store's administration, Party and Youth League branches, and grassroots trade union jointly held a general meeting of all employees to announce the start of the competition. Yao Yilin, Vice Minister of Commerce, Zhao Zhengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Commercial Trade Union, and Su Yifu, Deputy Director of the Financial and Trade Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, attended the meeting and delivered speeches, encouraging all employees to better learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union through this international competition, and wishing both sides significant achievements in the competition.

### 北京和海参崴两个百货公司订出条件开展社会主义竞赛

1957-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    北京和海参崴两个百货公司
    订出条件开展社会主义竞赛
    据新华社3日讯　北京市百货公司王府井百货商店全体职工，接受苏联海参崴百货商店的倡议，双方开展社会主义友谊劳动竞赛。
    今天上午，这个商店的行政、党和团的支部、基层工会联合召开全体职工大会，宣布这项竞赛开始。商业部副部长姚依林，中国商业工会副主席赵正之，中共北京市委财贸部副部长苏一夫都到会讲话，勉励全体职工通过这次国际间的竞赛，更好地学习苏联先进经验，并预祝双方竞赛获得重大成就。
