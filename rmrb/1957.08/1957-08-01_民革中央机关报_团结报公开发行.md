Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Central Organ Newspaper "Unity Daily" Publicly Distributed
Author:
Date: 1957-08-01
Page: 4

    Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Central Organ Newspaper
    "Unity Daily" Publicly Distributed
    (From Our Newspaper) The central organ newspaper of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, "Unity Daily," will be publicly distributed starting today. The "Unity Daily" primarily targets former Kuomintang members and those with historical connections to the former Kuomintang. The so-called people with historical connections include former military and political personnel and intellectuals from the late Qing Dynasty and the Beiyang Government era.

### 民革中央机关报  团结报公开发行

1957-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    民革中央机关报
    团结报公开发行
    本报讯　中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会的机关报——“团结报”，从今天起，公开发行。“团结报”以原国民党及与原国民党有历史关系的人士为主要对象；所谓有历史关系的人士，包括了晚清和北洋政府时代的旧军政人员、旧知识分子。
