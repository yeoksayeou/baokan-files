Date: 1957-08-26

　　Friendly Exchanges
　　Philippe Gérard Arrives in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, August 25th: French composer Philippe Gérard and his wife, photographer Victor Méranda, and others arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
　　Indian writer Balwant Gargi also arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th at the invitation of the Chinese Writers Association.
　　Mao Heydin Returns Home
　　Xinhua News Agency, August 25th: Khaled Muhammad Mao Heydin, member of the Egyptian National Assembly and editor-in-chief of "Al-Masaa", left Beijing this morning to return home.
　　Norman Leaves Guangzhou for Hong Kong
　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 25th: Norman, Secretary-General of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, left Guangzhou by special train yesterday afternoon for Shenzhen, from where he will proceed to Hong Kong.
　　Mrs. Norman plans to stay in Guangzhou for two days and is scheduled to fly to Beijing on the morning of the 27th before returning home.

### 友好往来

1957-08-26
第3版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
　  菲利普·热拉等到京
    新华社25日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的法国作曲家菲利普·热拉夫妇，摄影师维克多·默朗达等24日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    应中国作家协会邀请来中国访问的印度作家巴耳万特·加尔吉也在24日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    毛希丁回国
    新华社25日讯　埃及国民议会议员、“晚报”总编辑哈立德·穆罕默德·毛希丁今天早晨离开北京回国。
　  诺尔曼离广州赴香港
    新华社广州25日电　国际民主法律工作者协会总书记诺尔曼昨天下午乘专车离开广州前往深圳转赴香港。
    诺尔曼夫人准备在广州逗留两天，定27日晨乘飞机前往北京转道回国。
