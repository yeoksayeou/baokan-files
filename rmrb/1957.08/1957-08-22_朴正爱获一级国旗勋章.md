Title: Pak Chong-ae Awarded First Class Order of the National Flag
Author:
Date: 1957-08-22
Page: 6
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 21st - It was announced here today that the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea issued an order on August 3rd, awarding Comrade Pak Chong-ae, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, the First Class Order of the National Flag on the occasion of her fiftieth birthday.
    The decree stated that Comrade Pak Chong-ae has established special merits for the motherland and the people in the national liberation struggle against Japanese imperialism, in the cause of freedom and independence of the motherland after liberation, and in strengthening the democratic base of the republic. She has also contributed to the cause of world peace.

### 朴正爱获一级国旗勋章

1957-08-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　朴正爱获一级国旗勋章
    新华社平壤21日电　此间今天宣布，朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会在8月3日发布政令，在朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱同志五十岁寿辰的时候，授予她一级国旗勋章。
    政令说，朴正爱同志在反对日本帝国主义的民族解放斗争中，在解放后为了祖国的自由独立和加强共和国的民主基地的事业中，为祖国和人民建立了特殊的功勋。她并且对世界和平事业作出了贡献。
