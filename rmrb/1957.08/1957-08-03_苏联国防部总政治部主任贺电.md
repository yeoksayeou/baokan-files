Title: Telegram of Greetings from the Chief of the Main Political Department of the Soviet Ministry of Defense
Author:
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 3

Telegram of Greetings from the Chief of the Main Political Department of the Soviet Ministry of Defense

To Comrade General Tan Zheng, Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I extend to you, Comrade Tan Zheng, my warmest respect and congratulations on behalf of the Main Political Department of the Soviet Army!

Please allow me to wish you further achievements in the cause of educating all personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in the spirit of faithfully serving the people and with the thought of Marxism-Leninism!

A. Zheltev, Colonel General, Chief of the Main Political Department of the Soviet Ministry of Defense (signed)
July 30, 1957

### 苏联国防部总政治部主任贺电

1957-08-03
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部总政治部主任贺电
    中国人民解放军总政治部主任谭政大将同志：
    值此中国人民解放军三十周年建军节之际，谨代表苏军总政治部向您，谭政同志，致以最热烈的敬意和祝贺！
    请允许我祝您在以忠诚地为人民服务的精神和以马克思列宁主义的思想教育中国人民解放军全体人员的事业中获得进一步的成就！
          苏联国防部总政治部主任　A·热尔托夫上将（签字）
                                        1957年7月30日
