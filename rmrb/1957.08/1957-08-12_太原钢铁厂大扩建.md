Title: Large-scale Expansion of Taiyuan Iron and Steel Plant
Author:
Date: 1957-08-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, 11th August. The Taiyuan Iron and Steel Plant has begun an unprecedented expansion project. After this construction, the plant's steel production will increase by nearly two times compared to the present, and the production of high-quality steel will be equivalent to ten times the current output.

Currently, a bustling scene has emerged on more than 440 hectares of land north of the factory site. Large-scale primary rolling workshops, soaking pit workshops, and billet workshops will be successively started in the third quarter.

### 太原钢铁厂大扩建

1957-08-12
第5版()
专栏：

    太原钢铁厂大扩建
    据新华社太原11日电　太原钢铁厂开始了规模空前的扩建工程。经过这次建设，这个厂钢的产量将比现在提高近两倍，其中优质钢的产量将可相当于目前产量的十倍。
    现在，厂址北面的四百四十多公顷的土地上，已经出现了一片繁忙的景象。规模很大的初轧车间、均热炉车间和钢胚车间等，都将在第三季度内陆续开始新建。
