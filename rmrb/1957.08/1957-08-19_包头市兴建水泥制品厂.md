Title: Baotou City Builds Cement Products Factory
Author:
Date: 1957-08-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Baotou, 18th. A cement products factory began construction in Baotou City on the 16th. This factory is being built to support the construction of Baotou Iron and Steel (Baogang).

    This newly built cement products factory is scheduled to complete all factory construction work in ninety-five days. At that time, the factory will be able to produce more than 72,000 meters of cement pipes of various diameters each year, and cement poles sufficient to erect 150 kilometers of high-voltage power transmission lines.

### 包头市兴建水泥制品厂

1957-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

    包头市兴建水泥制品厂
    据新华社包头18日电　包头市在16日动工兴建一座水泥制品厂。这个工厂是为支援包钢建设而兴建的。
    这座新建的水泥制品厂，准备用九十五天的时间完成全部建厂工程。那时，这座工厂每年就可以生产各种不同口径的水泥管七万二千多公尺，和可以供架设一百五十公里的高压输电线的水泥电柱。
