Title: Shizuishan Coalfield (Picture)
Author: Huang Duidai
Date: 1957-08-26
Page: 4

    Shizuishan Coalfield
    The newly discovered Shizuishan Coalfield on the eastern foot of the Helan Mountains in Gansu is a large coalfield with abundant coal reserves, excellent coal quality, and suitable for coking. Various basic construction works are now underway in preparation for mining. This is a corner of the coalfield mining area.
      Photo by Huang Duidai (Manuscript from Gansu Daily)

### 石咀山煤田（图片）
黄对岱
1957-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    石咀山煤田
    甘肃贺兰山东麓新发现的石咀山煤田，是一个煤藏丰富，煤质优良，可供炼焦的大煤田。现正进行开采前的各项基本建设工作。这是煤田工矿区的一角。
      黄对岱摄（甘肃日报社稿）
