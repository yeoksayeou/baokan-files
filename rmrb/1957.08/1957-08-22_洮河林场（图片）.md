　　Tao River Forest Farm

　　The Tao River Basin in Gansu Province boasts lush forests. Here, there are billions of cubic meters of superior timber such as fir and spruce. Every year, the timber is transported via the winding Tao River for more than 2,000 li to Lanzhou, supplying the needs of Lanzhou's industrial construction.

　　Left Picture: A corner of the Tao River Forest Farm.

　　Middle Picture: Teams of rafts carrying timber on the Tao River.

　　Right Picture: Using tractors to transport timber greatly improves transportation efficiency and meets the needs of timber rafting.

　　Taken by Dai Yafu (Draft from Gansu Daily)

### 洮河林场（图片）
黛亚夫
1957-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

　　洮河林场
    甘肃省洮河流域有着茂盛的森林。这里有亿万的方杉、冷杉等上等木材，每年通过蜿蜒二千余华里的洮河放运到兰州，供应兰州工业建设的需要。
      左图：洮河林场的一角。
      中图：成队的木排放运在洮河上。
      右图：利用拖拉机运输木材，大大提高了运输效率，满足了编排木材的需要。
                                黛亚夫摄（甘肃日报社稿）
