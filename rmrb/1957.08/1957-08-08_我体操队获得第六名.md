Title: Our Gymnastics Team Achieves Sixth Place
Author:
Date: 1957-08-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th: The gymnastics competition of the International Youth Friendship Games began on August 6th. On this day, the individual and team competitions for men's floor exercise, pommel horse, and horizontal bar were held.

　　The Soviet team took first place in the overall team score for the three events with 171.2 points. Second place went to the Japanese team with 167.8 points, and third place went to the Czechoslovakian team with 164.35 points. The Chinese team came in sixth place with a total score of 159.05 points.

### 我体操队获得第六名

1957-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

    我体操队获得第六名
    据新华社莫斯科7日电　国际青年友谊运动会的体操比赛在8月6日开始举行，这一天举行了男子自由体操、鞍马和单杠三项的个人和队际比赛。
    苏联队以一七一点二分取得三项比赛的团体总分第一名，第二名是日本队，它得到一六七点八分，第三名是捷克斯洛伐克队，它得到一六四点三五分。中国队得第六名，总分是一五九点零五分。
