Title: Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet for Delegation from Yamaguchi, Tottori, and Shimane Prefectures, Japan
Author:
Date: 1957-08-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Japanese delegation from Yamaguchi, Tottori, and Shimane Prefectures, led by Kenji Futaki, Speaker of the Yamaguchi Prefectural Assembly.

Beijing Vice Mayor Zhang Youyu presided over tonight's banquet on behalf of Mayor Peng Zhen. In his welcoming speech, he pointed out that this is the fifth time Japanese friends have visited here at Mayor Peng Zhen's invitation. This shows that the mutual understanding and friendship between the people of China and Japan are increasing day by day, and will play a positive role in promoting the normalization of relations between the two countries. Zhang Youyu expressed his hope that the people of China and Japan will work together to strive for the normalization of relations between the two countries and develop economic and cultural exchanges.

In his speech, Kenji Futaki expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome and hospitality they had received. He pointed out that Japan and China have a long history of cultural and economic relations, and that Yamaguchi, Tottori, and Shimane Prefectures in Japan have particularly close ties with China. Futaki expressed his regret over the abnormal relations between Japan and China, and expressed his hope to further strengthen the understanding and friendship between the people of Japan and China, thereby contributing to the defense of peace in Asia and the world.

Also present at the banquet were Beijing Vice Mayors Wu Han and Yue Songsheng, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Ling Qijun, and heads of various departments of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee and various people's organizations.

### 彭真欢宴日山口县鸟取县岛根县代表团

1957-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    彭真欢宴日山口县鸟取县岛根县代表团
    新华社6日讯　北京市市长彭真今晚举行宴会，欢迎由山口县议会议长二木谦吾率领的日本山口县、鸟取县、岛根县访华代表团。
    北京副市长张友渔代表彭真市长主持今晚的宴会，他在欢迎词中指出，日本朋友们应彭真市长的邀请来到这里进行访问，这已经是第五次了。这说明中日两国人民之间的相互了解和友谊正在日益加强，对于促进中日两国关系的正常化将会起到良好的作用。张友渔希望中日两国人民共同努力争取两国关系的正常化，发展两国的经济和文化交流。
    二木谦吾在讲话中对他们所受到的热烈欢迎和招待表示感谢，他指出日中两国有着悠久的文化和经济关系，日本山口县、鸟取县、岛根县又同中国有着特别密切的关系。二木谦吾对日中两国关系的不正常表示遗憾，他希望进一步加强日中两国人民的了解和友谊，从而为保卫亚洲和世界的和平作出贡献。
    出席宴会作陪的，还有北京市副市长吴晗、乐松生，政协北京市委员会副主席凌其峻，北京市人民委员会各部门和各人民团体的负责人。
