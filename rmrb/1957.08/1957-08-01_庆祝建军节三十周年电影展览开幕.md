Title: Film Exhibition Opens to Celebrate the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Army
Author:
Date: 1957-08-01
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 31st - A film exhibition celebrating the 30th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day opened here tonight.

Wang Lanxi, Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, and Wei Chuantong, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army, both spoke at the meeting. After the opening ceremony, the color documentary "A Single Spark Can Start a Prairie Fire" was screened.

The film exhibition also includes the feature films "Growing Up in Battle," "The Eve of the Fierce Battle," "Battle in the Desert," and the color documentaries "Removing Mountains and Reclaiming Seas," and "On the Road of the Long March."

Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, also attended tonight's opening ceremony.

### 庆祝建军节三十周年电影展览开幕

1957-08-01
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝建军节三十周年
    电影展览开幕
    新华社31日讯　庆祝“八一”建军节三十周年电影展览，今天晚上在这里举行了开幕式。
    文化部电影事业管理局局长王阑西和解放军总政治部宣传部副部长魏传统都在会上讲了话。开幕式结束后，放映了彩色纪录片“星火燎原”。
    参加这次电影展览的还有故事片“战斗里成长”、“激战前夜”、“沙漠里的战斗”和彩色纪录片“移山填海”、“在长征的道路上”。
    文化部副部长夏衍也出席了今晚的开幕式。
