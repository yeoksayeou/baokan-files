　　International Friendship

　　Our Art Troupe Performs in Siberia

　　A portion of the Chinese Youth Art Troupe arrived in Tyumen, one of the oldest Russian cities in Siberia, on August 24th. This is the first time a Chinese art group has performed here.

　　The art troupe, consisting of forty-nine members, was warmly welcomed at the station by the mayor of Tyumen, the secretary of the city Party committee, and the secretary of the Communist Youth League committee.

　　On the evening of the 24th, members of the art troupe participated in the 50th anniversary celebration of the establishment of the Tyumen Oblast Trade Union and held their first performance.

　　The art troupe will perform in Tyumen until the 26th, and then continue to visit Omsk, Novosibirsk, Barnaul, and Chita.

　　Soviet and British Athletes Compete

　　The Soviet and British mixed track and field teams competed in London on August 23rd and 24th. The Soviet male athletes won with a score of 119 to 93, and the female athletes won with a score of 73 to 40. In the thirty-one events, the Soviet Union won twenty-two first places, and Britain won nine first places.

　　The participants in this competition were all first-class track and field athletes from the Soviet Union and Britain, including many world record holders. Several Soviet and British national records were broken in this competition, and many excellent results were achieved.

　　Indian Youth Actors Visit Mongolia

　　The Indian youth actors who participated in the World Youth Festival, at the invitation of the Mongolian cultural department, gave a brilliant performance at the National Theatre here on the evening of the 24th. The Indian songs and dances with strong national characteristics aroused great interest among the audience. They performed a total of fourteen song and dance programs.

　　The Indian actors will also perform once for the people of the Mongolian capital today, and then return to their country via China. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际友谊

1957-08-26
第6版()
专栏：国际友谊

　  国际友谊
  　我艺术团在西伯利亚演出
    中国青年艺术团的一部分团员8月24日到达西伯利亚最古老的俄国城市之一——第乌门。这是第一次有一个中国艺术团体在这里演出。
    艺术团人员一行四十九人在车站受到第乌门市长、市党委书记和共青团委书记的热烈欢迎。
    24日晚间，艺术团人员参加了第乌门州工会成立五十周年纪念会，举行了第一次演出。
    艺术团人员将在第乌门举行演奏到26日，然后继续访问鄂木斯克、新西伯利亚、巴尔瑙尔和赤塔。
　  苏英两国运动员比高低
    苏联田径混合队同英国田径混合队8月23日和24日在伦敦举行了比赛，苏联男运动员以一百一十九比九十三分获胜，女运动员以七十三比四十分获胜。在三十一个项目的比赛中，苏联得二十二个第一名，英国得九个第一名。
    参加这次比赛的都是苏联和英国第一流的田径运动员，其中有许多世界纪录保持者。在这次比赛中打破了好几项苏联和英国的全国纪录，出现了不少优秀成绩。
    印度青年演员访问蒙古
    参加世界青年联欢节的印度青年演员应蒙古文化部门的邀请，24日晚上在这里的国家剧院作了一场精采演出。具有浓厚民族色彩的印度歌舞引起观众很大兴趣。他们一共演出了十四个歌舞节目。
    印度演员们今天还要向蒙古首都人民演出一次，然后经过中国回国。　（新华社）
