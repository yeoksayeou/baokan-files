Title: "Warm" Hands
Author: Zhao Shuguang
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 8

Column: Poetry Handbill

    "Warm" Hands
    Zhao Shuguang
    "Warm" hands,
    Like two bottles of aged wine,
    Intoxicated his heart;
    "Warm" hands,
    Like sweet sleeping pills,
    Dragging him snoring into dreams.
    "A trivial matter, can't betray a friend,"
    He tightly hugs this black heart to his chest.
    Rightist elements nod in approval:
    "May God be with you, kind
  guard."

### “温情”的双手
赵曙光
1957-08-03
第8版()
专栏：诗传单

    “温情”的双手
    赵曙光
    “温情”的双手，
    好像两瓶陈年老酒，
    迷醉了他的心头；
    “温情”的双手，
    好像甜蜜的安眠药片，
    拖着他呼呼梦里走。
    “区区小事，不能出卖朋友” 
    他把这颗黑心紧搂在胸前。
    右派分子不住地点头称赞：
    “愿上帝与你同在，好心的
  警卫员”。
