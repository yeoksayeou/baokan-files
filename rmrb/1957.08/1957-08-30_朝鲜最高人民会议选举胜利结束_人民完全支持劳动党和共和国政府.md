Title: Korean Supreme People's Assembly Election Concludes Successfully: The People Fully Support the Workers' Party and the Republic's Government
Author:
Date: 1957-08-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency report: Korean Central Broadcasting Station broadcast the communique of the Central Election Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on the evening of the 29th regarding the results of the election of deputies to the Supreme People's Assembly held on August 27th. The communique stated that 99.99% of all voters participated in the election, and 99.92% of the voters voted in favor of the candidates registered with the election committees in each electoral district.

The communique stated that the election was held amidst the high political enthusiasm of all the people and in strict compliance with the Supreme People's Assembly Election Law. The candidates in all two hundred and fifteen electoral districts were elected as deputies to the Korean Supreme People's Assembly.

The communique published a list of the two hundred and fifteen elected deputies, including leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and the Republic's government such as Kim Il-sung, Kim Tu-bong, Choi Yong-kun, and Pak Chong-ae; leaders of various democratic parties and social organizations such as Kim Tal-hyon, Hong Ki-hwang, and Pak Yong-guk; cultural and artistic figures such as Han Sorya, Lee Ki-young, and Choi Seung-hee; as well as well-known labor heroes and model farmers.

Foreign Minister Nam Il said to the ambassadors of various countries and foreign journalists stationed in Korea on the evening of the 29th that the results of the Korean Supreme People's Assembly election held on August 27th demonstrate the strengthening of the political and ideological unity of the Korean people in order to peacefully reunify the fatherland and build socialism in northern Korea. At the same time, it demonstrates the full support of the Korean people for the Korean Workers' Party and the Republic's government.

Nam Il said that the political and ideological unity and unification of the Korean people, further consolidated in this election, is a powerful force for promoting the peaceful reunification of the fatherland and building socialism in northern Korea. This force will deal a decisive blow to the American imperialists and the Syngman Rhee clique who attempt to undermine the democratic construction of northern Korea.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议选举胜利结束  人民完全支持劳动党和共和国政府

1957-08-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜最高人民会议选举胜利结束
    人民完全支持劳动党和共和国政府
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：朝鲜中央广播电台29日晚上广播了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议中央选举委员会关于8月27日举行的最高人民会议议员选举的结果的公报。公报说，全体选民的99.99％参加了选举，其中有99.92％的选民投票赞成在各选区选举委员会登记的候选人。
    公报说，选举是在全体人民高涨的政治热情中、在严格遵守最高人民会议选举法的情况下举行的。二百一十五个选区的候选人全部当选为朝鲜最高人民会议议员。
    公报发表了当选的二百一十五位议员的名单，其中有朝鲜劳动党和共和国政府的领导人金日成、金枓奉、崔庸健、朴正爱等，有各民主政党、社会团体负责人金达铉、洪箕璜、朴容国等，有文化艺术界人士韩雪野、李箕永、崔承喜等，另外还有著名的劳动英雄、模范农民等。
    南日外务相29日晚上对驻朝鲜的各国大使和外国记者说，8月27日举行的朝鲜最高人民会议选举的结果，表明了朝鲜人民为了和平统一祖国和在朝鲜北部建设社会主义，在政治上和思想上的统一的加强，同时表明了朝鲜人民对朝鲜劳动党和共和国政府的全面支持。
    南日说，在这次选举中进一步巩固了的朝鲜人民在政治上思想上的团结统一，是促进祖国的和平统一和在朝鲜北部建设社会主义的雄厚力量，这种力量将给予企图破坏朝鲜北部民主建设的美帝国主义和李承晚集团以决定性的打击。
