Title: Wu Youxun of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Hosts a Banquet for Dr. Cheng Maolan
Author:
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. August - Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 2nd for Dr. Cheng Maolan, who had just returned from France. Cheng Maolan went to France at the age of twenty to work and study, worked in many factories in France, and engaged in scientific research in France for thirty-two years. He obtained a Master of Mathematics degree and a Doctorate in Astronomy, and was also a recipient of the French Academy of Sciences Prize (1945) and the French Knight of the Order of Academic Palms (1956).

Cheng Maolan specializes in stellar spectrophotometry, the study of night sky light and auroral spectra. He has published sixty-eight papers and is an internationally renowned scholar. Now he has returned to his motherland, which he left more than thirty years ago, and will participate in astronomical research.

### 中国科学院吴有训欢宴程茂兰博士

1957-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院吴有训欢宴程茂兰博士
    新华社2日讯　中国科学院吴有训副院长在2日晚欢宴留法刚回国的程茂兰博士。程茂兰二十岁时勤工俭学去法国，曾在法国许多工厂工作过，在法国从事科学研究有三十二年，曾得数学硕士、天文学博士，而且还是法国科学院奖金获得者（1945）和法国科学骑士勋章获得者（1956）。
    程茂兰专长恒星光谱光度学，夜天光和极光的光谱研究，曾发表过论文六十八篇，是国际知名的学者。现在他回到离开三十多年的祖国，将参加天文学的研究工作。
