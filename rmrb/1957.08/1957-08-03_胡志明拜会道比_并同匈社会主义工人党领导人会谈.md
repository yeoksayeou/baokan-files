Title: Ho Chi Minh Visits Dobi and Holds Talks with Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Leaders
Author:
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, August 1st. Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, paid a visit to Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, on the afternoon of August 1st upon his arrival in Budapest. The host and guest had a cordial conversation.

Subsequently, President Ho Chi Minh began talks with the leaders of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party.

In the evening, President Ho Chi Minh attended an opera performance.

The "People's Freedom Daily" and "People's Will Daily" both published President Ho Chi Minh's photo and the news of his visit on the front page, and also published editorials. The "People's Freedom Daily's" editorial stated that President Ho Chi Minh's visit to Hungary will be a new page in the history of the development of friendly relations between the Hungarian and Vietnamese peoples.

### 胡志明拜会道比  并同匈社会主义工人党领导人会谈

1957-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明拜会道比
    并同匈社会主义工人党领导人会谈
    新华社布达佩斯1日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明在8月1日到达布达佩斯的当天下午，拜会了匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比。宾主之间进行了亲切的谈话。
    随后，胡志明主席开始同匈牙利社会主义工人党的领导人进行会谈。
    晚间，胡志明主席参加了歌剧晚会。
    这一天的“人民自由报”和“人民意志报”都在第一版刊登了胡志明主席的照片和来访的消息，并且发表了社论。“人民自由报”的社论说，胡志明主席这次对匈牙利的访问将是匈越两国人民友好关系发展史中的新的一页。
