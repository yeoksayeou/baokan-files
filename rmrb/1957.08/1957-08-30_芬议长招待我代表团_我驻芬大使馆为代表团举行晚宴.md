Title: Finnish Speaker of Parliament Hosts Chinese Delegation; Chinese Embassy in Finland Holds Dinner for Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-08-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, August 29th. Finnish Speaker of Parliament, Karl-August Fagerholm, held a banquet here on the evening of the 27th to host the Chinese National People's Congress delegation.

　　At the banquet, Speaker Fagerholm and Prime Minister Sukselainen repeatedly raised their glasses to wish the Chinese leaders good health.

　　Delegation leader Saifudin also toasted the Finnish President and government leaders.

　　In his speech at the banquet, Fagerholm said that he hoped to further increase Sino-Finnish friendly ties and exchanges. He said: Finland hopes to live in peace and friendship with all countries. Therefore, we highly value all forms of friendship that China has shown us.

　　Saifudin said in reply: During the delegation's visit to Finland, we felt the Finnish people's sincere friendship towards the Chinese people everywhere. He said that China and Finland have always respected each other, and both countries have a common desire to safeguard world peace and promote friendly relations between countries, which constitutes a good foundation for friendship between the two countries. The Chinese National People's Congress actively advocates developing friendly exchanges between parliaments of various countries. We affirm that if the parliaments of various countries strengthen their friendly relations and take on the responsibility of promoting international cooperation and world peace, then we will make a significant contribution to the creative cause of human progress.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, August 28th. The Chinese National People's Congress delegation, which is currently visiting Finland, laid a wreath at the tomb of former Finnish President Paasikivi on the morning of the 28th to pay their respects.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, August 29th. The Chinese Embassy in Finland held a dinner on August 28th for the Chinese National People's Congress delegation's visit to Finland, hosting Finnish parliamentary and government leaders as well as people from all walks of life.

　　Those attending the dinner included the Speaker of the Finnish Parliament, the Prime Minister, and ministers from the Ministries of Foreign Affairs, Finance, Agriculture, Education, and Social Affairs, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Finland. More than a hundred guests gathered together, engaging in friendly conversation. A friendly atmosphere prevailed at the banquet.

### 芬议长招待我代表团  我驻芬大使馆为代表团举行晚宴

1957-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　芬议长招待我代表团
　  我驻芬大使馆为代表团举行晚宴
    新华社赫尔辛基29日电　芬兰议会议长法格霍姆27日晚上在这里举行宴会，招待中国全国人民代表大会代表团。
    在宴会上，法格霍姆议长和苏克西莱宁总理频频举杯遥祝中国领导人员健康。
    赛福鼎团长也为芬兰总统和政府领袖干杯。
    法格霍姆在宴会上致词说，他希望进一步增加中芬友谊联系和交往。他说：芬兰希望同一切国家共同生活在和平和友谊中。因此，我们非常珍视中国对我们表示的各种形式的友谊。
    赛福鼎致答词说：代表团在芬兰访问期间到处感到芬兰人民对中国人民的真诚友谊。他说，中芬两国一向相互尊重，同时两国都有维护世界和平和增进各国友好关系的共同愿望，这就构成了两国友好的良好基础。中国全国人民代表大会积极主张发展各国议会之间友好往来。我们确认，如果各国议会加强它们的友好关系，并把增进国际合作和世界和平的责任担当起来，那么我们就会对人类进步的创造事业作出重大贡献。
    新华社赫尔辛基28日电　正在芬兰访问的中国全国人民代表大会代表团28日上午在芬兰前总统巴锡基维墓前献花圈致敬。
    新华社赫尔辛基29日电　中国驻芬兰大使馆8月28日为中国全国人民代表大会代表团访问芬兰举行了晚宴，招待芬兰议会和政府领导人员以及各界人士。
    出席晚宴的有芬兰议会议长、总理和外交、财政、农业、教育、社会等部部长以及各国驻芬兰使节。宾主一百多人欢集一堂，亲切交谈。宴会上笼罩着一片友好气氛。
