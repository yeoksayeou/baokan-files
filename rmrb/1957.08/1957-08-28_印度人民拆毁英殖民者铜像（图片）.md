Title: Indian People Demolish Statue of British Colonialist (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1957-08-28
Page: 6

　　Indian People Demolish Statue of British Colonialist
    This year, India commemorates the centenary of the 1857 anti-British uprising nationwide. Recently, workers in Calcutta removed the statue of British General James Outram from the city center. He was the commander of the British forces who suppressed the Indian uprising a hundred years ago.
    (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 印度人民拆毁英殖民者铜像（图片）

1957-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　印度人民拆毁英殖民者铜像
    今年，印度全国纪念1857年反英起义一百周年。最近加尔各答的工人们把市中心的英国将官詹姆士·奥特兰的铜像拆去，他是一百年前镇压印度起义的英军司令官。
    （新华社稿）
