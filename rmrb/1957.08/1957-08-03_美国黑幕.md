Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 6

　　·American Black Curtain·
　　"The Silent Generation"
　　"New York Standard Post" published the speech of Phelps, a fourth-year student at Utica University, at a speech contest. He believes that the current generation in the United States is "the silent generation."

　　Phelps said: "We are a generation that obeys everything and considers its own safety everywhere, so we are all taciturn. Our characteristic is that we are indifferent to everything, lack vigor, and are obedient."

　　He also said: "Due to political reasons, we have become a silent generation. We dare not have any thoughts, nor dare to express any thoughts, because we are afraid that twenty-five years later, they can bring us to a parliamentary investigation committee, where we will be persecuted for the thoughts we formed in childhood. We all understand that such things have happened in the past, so we are unwilling to put ourselves in such a position again."

　　He finally said: "Our generation was cultivated by the previous generation. We will be the cultivators of the next generation. Since our generation is silent, the next generation will not only be silent, but will become deaf, mute, and blind."

　　Americans Don't Like to Read
　　According to the data recently published by the Gallup Poll, Americans are the least likely to read among civilized nations. Among the 103 million adult population in the United States (more than one-third of whom are high school graduates), if asked whether they read books or not (regardless of what books), only 17% can answer that they do. The same situation was 18% in 1952 and 21% in 1950. It can be seen that the tendency not to read is constantly increasing.

### 美国黑幕

1957-08-03
第6版()
专栏：

    ·美国黑幕·
    “沉默的一代”
    “纽约标准邮报”刊载了乌梯卡大学四年级学生菲尔浦斯在一次演说比赛会上的讲词。他认为目前美国的一代是“沉默的一代”。
    菲尔浦斯说：“我们是言听计从的一代，处处得顾虑到自己的安全，因而我们大家沉默寡言。我们的特点是，对任何事情都无动于衷，缺乏朝气和唯命是从”。
    他还说：“由于政治的原故，我们变成了沉默的一代人。我们不敢有任何思想，也不敢表达任何思想，因为我们怕，二十五年后，他们可以把我们带到一个议会调查委员会那里去，在那里我们因有在儿童时代所形成的思想而被受到迫害。我们大家明白，这样的事在过去发生过，所以我们不愿意使我们再处于这样的地位”。
    他最后说道：“我们这一代是前一代培养的。我们将是下一代的培养者，既然我们这一代默默无声，那么下一代不仅是沉默，而且将会成为聋子、哑吧和瞎子”。
    美国人不爱看书
    据美国戞勒波舆论研究所最近发表资料说：美国人是文明国民中最不爱看书的国民。在美国成人人口一亿零三百万人中（有三分之一以上是高中毕业生），如果问以看书不看（不管是什么书），能回答看看的仅占17％。同样的情况，在1952年为18％，在1950年为21％。可见不看书的倾向，正在不断增加。
