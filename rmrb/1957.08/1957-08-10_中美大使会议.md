Title: Sino-American Ambassadorial Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-08-10
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 8th. The Chinese and American ambassadors held their sixty-ninth meeting here from 10:00 AM to 11:55 AM today. The next meeting is scheduled for September 12th.

### 中美大使会议

1957-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使会议
    新华社日内瓦8日电　中美两国大使今天上午十时到十一时五十五分在这里举行了第六十九次会议。下次会议订于9月12日举行。
