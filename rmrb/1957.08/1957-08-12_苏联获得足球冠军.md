Title: The Soviet Union Wins Football Championship
Author:
Date: 1957-08-12
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th – The Soviet football team defeated the Hungarian team five to one at the Lenin Central Stadium in Moscow on the afternoon of the 10th, winning the championship of the Third International Youth Friendship Games football competition. The Hungarian team won the second place, and the third and fourth places were the Czechoslovak team and the Democratic German team respectively.

### 苏联获得足球冠军

1957-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联获得足球冠军
    新华社莫斯科11日电　苏联足球队10日下午在莫斯科列宁中央运动场以五比一打败了匈牙利队，取得了第三届国际青年友谊运动会足球比赛冠军。匈牙利队取得亚军，第三名和第四名分别是捷克斯洛伐克队和民主德国队。
