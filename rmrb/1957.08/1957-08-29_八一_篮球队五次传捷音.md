Title: "August First" Basketball Team Reports Five Consecutive Victories

Author:
Date: 1957-08-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 28th - The Chinese People's Liberation Army "August First" basketball team, already four for four in Romania, achieved its fifth victory on the 27th.

　　In the game against the Satu Mare City basketball team on that day, the "August First" team defeated the Satu Mare team with a score of 125 to 50. The "August First" basketball team had a high shooting percentage, with successful shots accounting for 70% of the total shots.

　　The game was conducted in an extremely friendly atmosphere throughout. The good sportsmanship and excellent skills of the Chinese athletes have been well received by the local audience and the press.

　　The "August First" team's final game in Romania will be held in Oradea City on the 29th.

### “八一”篮球队五次传捷音

1957-08-29
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

　　“八一”篮球队五次传捷音
    新华社布加勒斯特28日电　在罗马尼亚已经四战四捷的中国人民解放军“八一”篮球队，27日第五次获得了胜利。
      “八一”队在这天同萨土马勒城篮球代表队进行的比赛中，以一百二十五比五十战胜萨土马勒队。“八一”篮球队的投篮命中率较高，投进次数占总投篮次数的70％。
    比赛始终是在极友好的气氛中进行的。中国运动员的良好的体育道德和优秀的技术，得到了当地观众和新闻界的好评。
      “八一”队在罗马尼亚的最后一场比赛将在29日在俄拉第亚城举行。
