Title: Syrian Parliamentary Delegation to Visit Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-08-21
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 20th: Ishan Jabri, Chairman of the Syrian Parliament's Foreign Affairs Committee, announced on the 19th that the Syrian parliamentary delegation had accepted an invitation to visit the Soviet Union. He said that the date of the visit will be determined after the delegation returns from its visits to Bulgaria, Albania, Tunisia, and Libya next month.

### 叙议会代表团将访苏

1957-08-21
第6版()
专栏：

　  叙议会代表团将访苏
    新华社20日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚议会外交委员会主席伊山·贾布里19日宣布，叙利亚议会代表团已经接受了访问苏联的邀请。他说，访问日期将在代表团下月访问保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、突尼斯和利比亚回国以后确定。
