Title: President Ho Chi Minh Arrives in Sofia; Vietnam and Albania Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-08-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, August 14th: President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh, arrived in Sofia by plane at 5:00 PM on August 13th.

When President Ho Chi Minh stepped off the plane, he was warmly welcomed by Bulgarian Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Yugov, and Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly, Damyano. A group of Young Pioneers presented President Ho Chi Minh with flowers. The band played the national anthems of Vietnam and Bulgaria. Damyano and Ho Chi Minh spoke at the airport.

Afterward, accompanied by Yugov and Damyano, Ho Chi Minh traveled in an open-top car to "September 9th Square." The road and "September 9th Square" were decorated with thousands of Bulgarian and Vietnamese national flags, and countless banners written in both Bulgarian and Vietnamese expressing respect to President Ho Chi Minh and the Vietnamese people, and hailing the fraternal friendship between the Bulgarian and Vietnamese peoples. Tens of thousands of Sofia citizens welcoming President Ho Chi Minh lined the streets, showering him with flowers.

A mass rally of tens of thousands of people was held at "September 9th Square." Yugov and Ho Chi Minh delivered speeches at the meeting.

Subsequently, the honored guests, accompanied by their hosts, traveled by car to their residence to rest.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, August 14th: After a four-day visit to Albania, President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and his entourage left Tirana by special plane on the afternoon of August 13th to visit Bulgaria.

On the morning of that day, Leshi, on behalf of the Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly, awarded President Ho Chi Minh the first-class "Freedom Medal" and the first-class "Skanderbeg Medal."

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, August 14th: A cultural cooperation agreement between the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Albania was signed in Tirana on August 13th. Signing the agreement were Vietnamese Minister of Culture Hoang Minh Giam and Albanian Minister of Education and Culture Aliya.

### 胡志明主席到达索非亚  越阿两国签订文化合作协定

1957-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席到达索非亚
    越阿两国签订文化合作协定
    据新华社索非亚14日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明8月13日下午五时乘飞机到达索非亚。
    胡志明主席走下飞机的时候受到了保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫和国民议会主席团主席达米扬诺夫的热烈欢迎。一群少先队员向胡志明主席献了鲜花。乐队奏起越保两国国歌。达米扬诺夫和胡志明在机场上讲了话。
    接着，胡志明由于哥夫和达米扬诺夫陪同，乘敞篷汽车前往“九九广场”。路上和“九九广场”上装饰着几千面保越两国国旗，悬挂着无数幅用保越两国文字写的向胡志明主席和越南人民致敬以及欢呼保越人民兄弟友谊的标语。成千上万欢迎胡志明主席的索非亚市民排列在马路两旁，向他投掷鲜花。
    在“九九广场”上举行了数万群众的欢迎大会。于哥夫和胡志明在会上发表了演说。
    随后，贵宾在主人陪同下乘汽车到寓所休息。
    据新华社地拉那14日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他的随行人员在阿尔巴尼亚访问了四天以后，已经在8月13日下午乘专机离开地拉那前往保加利亚访问。
    在这一天的上午，列希曾经以阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团的名义，将一级“自由勋章”和一级“斯坎德培勋章”授给了胡志明主席。
    新华社地拉那14日电　越南民主共和国和阿尔巴尼亚两国间的文化合作协定8月13日在地拉那签字。在协定上签字的是越南文化部长黄明鉴和阿尔巴尼亚教育文化部长阿利雅。
