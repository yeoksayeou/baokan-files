Title: News Photography Team Goes Deep into Tibet
Author:
Date: 1957-08-26
Page: 3

Culture News Briefs

    News Photography Team Goes Deep into Tibet
    A photography team from the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio has arrived in Baima Gang in the Luoyu region of Tibet. They will work here for two or three months, capturing the beautiful scenery of the Luoyu region and the hardworking and simple life of the people on film.

### 新闻摄影队深入西藏

1957-08-26
第3版()
专栏：文化零讯

    新闻摄影队深入西藏
    中央新闻纪录电影制片厂的一个摄影队已到了西藏洛渝地区的白马岗。他们将在这里工作两三个月，把洛渝地区美丽的风光和人民勤劳朴实的生活都摄进影片里。
