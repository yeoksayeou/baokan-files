Title: National People's Congress Delegation Visits Finland at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1957-08-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th.
    National People's Congress delegation of the People's Republic of China left Beijing by plane this morning for a visit to the Republic of Finland at the invitation of the Finnish Parliament. The head of the delegation, Saifuddin, has already traveled to Moscow en route to Finland.
    Those who saw the delegation off at the airport today included Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, Vice Minister Zeng Yongquan, prominent figures from various circles, and the Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Sundström, among others.
    Last night, Ambassador Sundström held a reception to bid farewell to the delegation.
    The list of the National People's Congress delegation of the People's Republic of China visiting the Republic of Finland is as follows:
    Head of Delegation: Saifuddin, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chairman of the People's Committee of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region.
    Members (in order of stroke count of surnames):
    Tian Xiujuan (female), Deputy to the National People's Congress, Director of the Women and Children's Welfare Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.
    Li Shucheng, Member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.
    Chen Qiyuan, Member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the People's Republic of China.
    Qu Wu, Deputy to the National People's Congress, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.
    Gao Chongmin, Member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Member of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.
    Zhang Bangying, Member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.
    Tang Di Yin (female), Deputy to the National People's Congress, Manager of Shanghai Huafu Pen Factory.
    Yang Shixian, Deputy to the National People's Congress, President of Nankai University
    Secretary-General: Qu Wu (concurrent)

### 全国人民代表大会代表团  应邀赴芬兰访问

1957-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会代表团
    应邀赴芬兰访问
    新华社17日讯  中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会访问芬兰共和国代表团在今天早晨乘飞机离开北京，前往芬兰。代表团是应芬兰共和国国会的邀请访问芬兰的。代表团团长赛福鼎已先期前往莫斯科转芬兰。
    今天到机场欢送的有郭沫若副委员长、曾涌泉副部长和各界著名人士，芬兰驻华大使孙士敦等。
    昨晚，孙士敦大使曾举行酒会为代表团饯行。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会访问芬兰共和国代表团名单如下：
    团长  赛福鼎  全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、新疆维吾尔自治区人民委员会主席。
    团员（按姓名笔划排列）
