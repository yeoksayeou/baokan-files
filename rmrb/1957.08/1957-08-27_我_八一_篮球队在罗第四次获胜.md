Title: Our "August 1st" Basketball Team Wins Fourth Victory in Romania
Author:
Date: 1957-08-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 26th - After winning three consecutive victories in Romania, the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" basketball team defeated the Krosch City basketball team by a score of 95 to 61 on the morning of August 25th.

　　Krosch is the second largest city in Romania after the capital, a famous cultural center with a population of about 200,000.

　　Today's Romanian "People's Sports Daily" reported on the match, saying that although the score difference was large, the game was intense. The Chinese "August 1st" team moved quickly and played well under the basket, creating difficulties for the opponent's defense. The match aroused great interest among the audience.

### 我“八一”篮球队在罗第四次获胜

1957-08-27
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

　  我“八一”篮球队在罗第四次获胜
    新华社布加勒斯特26日电　中国人民解放军“八一”篮球队在罗马尼亚三战三捷以后，8月25日上午又以九十五比六十一战胜克罗什市篮球代表队。
    克罗什是罗马尼亚仅次于首都的第二大城市，著名的文化中心，居民约有二十万人。
    今天的罗马尼亚“人民体育报”报道这场比赛说，虽然双方比数相差较大，但是打得很紧张。中国“八一”队动作迅速，篮下球打得好，造成了对方防守的困难。比赛引起了观众很大的兴趣。
