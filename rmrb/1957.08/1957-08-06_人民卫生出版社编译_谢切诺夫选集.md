Title: People's Medical Publishing House compiles "Selected Works of Sechenov"
Author: 
Date: 1957-08-06
Page: 7
    Academic Trends

    People's Medical Publishing House
    Compiles "Selected Works of Sechenov"
    The Chinese version of "Selected Works of Sechenov" has recently been edited and published by the People's Medical Publishing House. Ivan Mikhailovich Sechenov (1829-1905), the father of Russian physiology, was a great thinker and scholar in the history of science. His main research work consists of two parts, one on the problem of the nerve center and the other on the problem of blood gases. Among them, especially the research on the inhibitory effect of the central nervous system, made him gain a glorious reputation among physiologists all over the world. He also used this discovery to explain complex psychophysiological phenomena, thus providing a thorough materialist explanation of psychological phenomena. Therefore, his work is an extremely important contribution not only to physiology, but also to psychology and philosophy.
    This anthology contains twelve important works by Sechenov, all of which are related to the central nervous system. In addition, the anthology also includes three articles written by the famous physiologists Pavlov and Vvedensky in memory of the author, which briefly and fairly introduce the author's life and career, which is of great help to readers in reading Sechenov's original works.
　　　　　　　　(Jing)

### 人民卫生出版社编译“谢切诺夫选集”

1957-08-06
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    人民卫生出版社
    编译“谢切诺夫选集”
    中文的“谢切诺夫选集”最近已由人民卫生出版社编辑和出版。俄罗斯生理学之父伊万·米哈依洛维奇·谢切诺夫（1829—1905），是科学史上一位伟大的思想家和学者。他主要的研究工作有两个部分，一是关于神经中枢问题的，二是关于血液气体问题的。其中特别是关于中枢神经系统抑制作用问题的研究，使他在全世界生理学家中间获得了光荣的声望。他并且用这一发现来解释复杂的心理生理现象，从而使心理现象有了彻底的唯物主义的解释。因此，他的作品不仅对于生理方面，而且对于心理和哲学方面，都是极其重要的贡献。
    这个选集一共收集了谢切诺夫的十二篇重要作品，这些作品都是关于中枢神经方面的。此外，选集还收集了著名生理学家巴甫洛夫和维金斯基为纪念作者而写的三篇文章，这些文章简明而公允地介绍了作者的生平及其事业，对于读者阅读谢切诺夫的原著有着很大的帮助。　　　
　　　　　　　　（静）
