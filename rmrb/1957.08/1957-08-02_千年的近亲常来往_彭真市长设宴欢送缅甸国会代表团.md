Title: Millennia-Old Kin Often Visit; Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to the Burmese Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-08-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to the entire Burmese Parliamentary Delegation to China, headed by Chairman Thakin Tin Maung. The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy in China, U Ba Maung, and his wife were also invited to the banquet.

In his speech at the banquet, Peng Zhen thanked the Burmese Parliamentary Delegation for bringing the friendship of the Burmese people. He said that our two countries are not only relatives and close neighbors, but the Five Principles formulated by the Prime Ministers of our two countries will continuously enhance the friendship and cooperation between our two countries. Peng Zhen asked the distinguished guests to take back to Burma the friendship of the people of Beijing and the 600 million people of the whole country.

In his speech, Chairman Thakin Tin Maung expressed his gratitude for the warm reception from the Chinese people. He said that the people of our two countries have had a friendly relationship like relatives for over a thousand years. Only the aggression of imperialism interrupted this relationship. Since our two countries became independent, we have re-established friendly relations. Thakin Tin Maung finally expressed his desire to further enhance the friendship between Burma and China through face-to-face talks, friendship, and sincerity.

Accompanying the banquet were Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Ji Pengfei, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association; Liu Ren, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Vice Mayors of Beijing; and people from the political, legal, cultural, educational, and religious circles of the capital.

### 千年的近亲常来往  彭真市长设宴欢送缅甸国会代表团

1957-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    千年的近亲常来往
    彭真市长设宴欢送缅甸国会代表团
    新华社1日讯　北京市市长彭真今晚设宴欢送以德钦登貌团长为首的缅甸联邦国会访华代表团全体人员。缅甸驻中国大使馆临时代办吴巴茂和夫人也应邀出席了宴会。
    彭真在宴会上的致词中，感谢缅甸联邦国会访华代表团带来了缅甸人民的友谊。他说，我们两国不但是亲戚和近邻，而且我们两国总理制订的五项原则将不断增进我们两国的友好和合作。彭真请贵宾们把北京市人民和全国六亿人民的友谊带回缅甸去。
    德钦登貌团长在讲话中，对中国人民的热情接待表示感谢。他说，我们两国人民有一千多年的亲戚般的友好关系。只是帝国主义的侵略才使这种关系中断了。自从我们两国独立以后，重新建立了友谊关系。德钦登貌最后表示要用面对面的交谈以及友情和忠诚来进一步增进缅中两国的友谊。
    出席宴会作陪的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中缅友好协会会长郑振铎，政协北京市委员会主席刘仁，北京市的副市长们以及首都政法、文化、教育、宗教界人士。
