Title: Tirana Holds Mass Rally to Welcome Chairman Ho Chi Minh
Author:
Date: 1957-08-14
Page: 6
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, August 13th: On August 12th, a mass rally of tens of thousands of people was held in Tirana, the capital of Albania, to welcome Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, and Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, attended the rally.

　　Shehu and Ho Chi Minh spoke at the mass rally.

　　In his speech, Shehu pointed out that the Albanian people cherish the friendship with the Soviet people and the people of other socialist countries as they protect their own eyes, because this friendship is a solid guarantee of Albania's future happiness and a guarantee of world peace.

　　In his speech, Chairman Ho Chi Minh pointed out that the unity of the great socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and China is unbreakable. He also pointed out that the talks between the Vietnamese and Albanian representatives showed that the two countries have completely identical views on all issues involved.

　　On the same evening, Chairman Ho Chi Minh held a grand reception. Lleshi, Shehu and other Albanian party and government leaders were invited to attend the reception.

### 地拉那举行群众大会欢迎胡志明主席

1957-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    地拉那举行群众大会欢迎胡志明主席
    新华社地拉那13日电　8月12日，在阿尔巴尼亚首都地拉那举行了数万人的群众大会，欢迎越南民主共和国主席胡志明。阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席列希和部长会议主席谢胡等参加了大会。
    谢胡和胡志明在群众大会上分别讲了话。
    谢胡在讲话中指出，阿尔巴尼亚人民像保护自己的眼珠一样保护同苏联人民和社会主义阵营各国人民的友谊，因为这种友谊是阿尔巴尼亚未来幸福的坚固保证，也是世界和平的保证。
    胡志明主席在讲话中指出，以苏联和中国为首的伟大的社会主义阵营的团结是牢不可破的。他还指出，越、阿两国代表之间的会谈表明两国对所涉及的一切问题的看法是完全一致的。
    同日晚间，胡志明主席举行了盛大招待会。列希、谢胡等阿尔巴尼亚的党政领导人应邀出席了招待会。
