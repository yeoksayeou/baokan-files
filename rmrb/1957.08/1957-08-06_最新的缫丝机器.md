Title: The Latest Silk Reeling Machine
Author:
Date: 1957-08-06
Page: 5

    The Latest Silk Reeling Machine

    Recently, scientific staff at the Uzbek Silk Industry Scientific Research Institute in the Soviet Union created a silk reeling machine that can replace human eye and hand labor. This silk reeling machine can automatically unwind the outer layer of silkworm cocoons and sort the silk filaments, and then automatically reel the silk. At the end of the silk reeling process, the controller sends a signal to a specialized mechanical device, which then immediately produces raw silk. In the old method of silk reeling, workers needed to unwind a certain number of cocoons by hand and sort the silk threads very evenly. Now these complex tasks can be replaced by machinery. The quality of the raw silk produced is also very high.

    Using this automatic machinery, each worker's output is two and a half times more than manual silk reeling. It completely eliminates heavy manual labor and reduces the impact of hot water and acid on workers' health.

    The Soviet Margilan Silk Textile United Factory has already installed this silk reeling machine and has begun using it for production. During the Sixth Five-Year Plan, the Soviet Union will install this automatic machinery in all silk textile enterprises.

### 最新的缫丝机器

1957-08-06
第5版()
专栏：

    最新的缫丝机器
    最近，苏联乌兹别克丝工业科学研究所的科学工作人员创造了一种缫丝机械，它能代替人的眼和手劳动。这种缫丝机械能自动地解开蚕茧的外层和整理丝头，然后又能自动地缫丝。在缫丝结束的时候，控制仪就会向专门的机械装置发出信号，接着立刻就制成生丝。用老法缫丝的时候，工人需要用手解开一定数量的蚕茧，还要把丝线整理得很均匀，现在这些复杂的工作可以用机械来代替了。它所生产的生丝质量也很高。
    使用这种自动机械，每个工人的生产量比人工缫丝多二倍半。它完全消灭了繁重的手工劳动，减少了热水和酸对工人健康的影响。
    现在苏联马尔格兰丝纺织联合工厂已装设这种缫丝机械，并且开始用它来进行生产。在第六个五年计划内，苏联将要在所有丝纺织企业中装置这种自动机械。
