Title: Buckwheat Seeds Descend from the Sky
Author:
Date: 1957-08-07
Page: 4

    Buckwheat Seeds Descend from the Sky
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th: The Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army dispatched 58 aircraft on August 3rd to drop over 150,000 kilograms of buckwheat seeds to the flood-stricken areas of Dingtao and Heze in western Shandong Province to support local farmers in rushing to plant late autumn crops.
    In carrying out this airdrop mission, the People's Air Force highly developed the spirit of serving the people, with many aircraft making four consecutive airdrops. The two-day airdrop mission was completed in one day.

### 荞麦种　从天降

1957-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    荞麦种　从天降
    新华社6日讯　中国人民解放军空军部队在8月3日出动飞机五十八架次，向山东西部定陶、菏泽等遭受水灾地区投置荞麦种子十五万多公斤，支援当地农民抢种晚秋作物。
    人民空军在执行这次空投任务中，高度的发扬了为人民服务的精神，许多飞机连续空投四次，预定两天的空投任务在一天内全部空投完毕。
