Harbin City's Military and Civilians Fight the Flood

    Top left: In the Jiangbei suburban area of Harbin, government employees, barefoot and wading through water, transport soil to repair farmland dikes.

    Top right: Female worker Yu Fuying (member of the Communist Youth League) participating in flood prevention and dike repair.

    Right: Local People's Liberation Army troops are digging and loading soil onto trucks at Guxiang Railway Station, actively participating in the flood control efforts.
