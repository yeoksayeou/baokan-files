Title: Belgian Communist Party Delegation Returns Home from Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-08-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The delegation of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party, which recently went to Moscow to hold talks with the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, has returned to Brussels. This delegation was led by the Secretary of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party, Gérard Van Moerkerke.

The delegation issued a statement after returning home saying that the characteristic of this meeting was that the two party delegations discussed all issues conducive to completing their mission with extreme frankness.

### 比共代表团离苏回国

1957-08-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　比共代表团离苏回国
    据新华社讯　最近到莫斯科同苏联共产党中央委员会代表团举行会谈的比利时共产党中央委员会代表团已经回到布鲁塞尔。这个代表团是由比共中央委员会书记格拉尔特·范穆克尔克率领的。
    代表团在回国后发表声明说，这次会谈的特点是：两党代表团极其坦率地讨论了一切有利于完成它们的使命的问题。
