Title: Good Results at the Youth Friendship Games
Author:
Date: 1957-08-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 31st: Soviet athletes won the championships in the 10,000-meter race, 50-kilometer walk, men's high jump, and women's long jump at the International Youth Friendship Games on July 30th.

On the same day, the men's shot put final was also held. Czechoslovakian athletes Skobla and Prlihal took the first and second places.

Our Team Has Wins and Losses
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 31st: The Chinese men's and women's basketball teams both won their preliminary basketball matches at the International Youth Friendship Games on July 30th. The men's team played against the Korean team, with a score of 108 to 50. The women's team played against Poland, with a score of 63 to 47.

In the tennis preliminaries held on the same day, Chinese player Pan Jiazhen lost to India's Kumar with a score of 0 to 3.

The Chinese women's basketball team defeated the Belgian team with a score of 89 to 51 on July 31st. On the same day, the Chinese women's volleyball team lost to the Romanian team with a score of 1 to 3. The men's volleyball team lost to the Bulgarian team with a score of 1 to 3.

Chinese tennis player Mei Fuji defeated Finnish player Nusonen with a score of 3 to 0 in the preliminaries on July 30th. Zhu Zhenhua defeated Uruguayan player Argon with a score of 3 to 2. Huang Daxuan and Pan Jiazhen defeated Finnish players Alberger and Hedman with a score of 3 to 2 in doubles.

Table tennis player Xu Yinsheng defeated Soviet player Leonov with a score of 3 to 0 on July 30th, and also defeated North Korean player Kim Dong-soo (transliteration) with a score of 3 to 0.

Lin Jinzhu Creates New Record
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 31st: Chinese swimmer Lin Jinzhu created a new record for the World Youth Games in the 100-meter freestyle preliminary round at the Third International Youth Friendship Games today, with a time of 57.2 seconds, breaking the Chinese national record, equivalent to the fourth-place result in the same event at the 16th Olympic Games.

### 青年友谊运动会上成绩好

1957-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    青年友谊运动会上成绩好
    苏联田径队获四项冠军
    据新华社莫斯科31日电　苏联运动员7月30日在国际青年友谊运动会上获得了一万公尺赛跑、五十公里竞走、男子跳高和女子跳远的冠军。
    在这一天还举行了男子铅球决赛。捷克斯洛伐克运动员斯科布拉和普利哈尔取得了头二名。
    我球队选手有胜有负
    据新华社莫斯科31日电　中国男女篮球队7月30日在国际青年友谊运动会上的篮球预赛中都取得了胜利。男子队对朝鲜队，结果是一○八比五十，女子队对波兰，结果是六十三比四十七。
    在同一天举行的网球预赛中，中国选手潘家震以○比三败于印度的库马尔。
    中国女子篮球队31日以八十九比五十一战胜了比利时队，在同一天，中国女子排球队以一比三输给罗马尼亚队。男子排球队以一比三输给了保加利亚队。
    中国网球选手梅福基30日在预赛中以三比○战胜了芬兰选手努森宁，朱振华以三比二战胜了乌拉圭选手阿尔贡，黄大暄和潘家震在双打中以三比二战胜了芬兰选手阿尔贝格和赫德曼。
    乒乓球选手徐寅生30日以三比零打败了苏联选手列昂诺夫，还以三比零打败了朝鲜选手金东秀（译音）。
    林锦珠创新纪录
    据新华社莫斯科31日电　中国游泳运动员林锦珠今天在第三届国际青年友谊运动会的一百公尺自由泳预赛中，以五十七秒二的成绩创造了世界青年运动会的新纪录，打破了中国全国纪录，等于第十六届奥林匹克运动会同类比赛中第四名的成绩。
