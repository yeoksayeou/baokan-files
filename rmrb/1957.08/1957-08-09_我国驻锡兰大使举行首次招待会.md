Title: Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon Holds First Reception
Author:
Date: 1957-08-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 7th. Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon, Zhang Canming, and his wife held their first reception at the Galle Face Hotel in Colombo on the evening of August 6th.

The Governor-General of Ceylon, Sir Oliver Goonetilleke, Prime Minister Bandaranaike, government ministers, foreign envoys, and prominent figures from all walks of life in Ceylon, totaling about seven hundred guests, attended the reception.

Ambassador Zhang Canming reviewed the history of the long-standing friendly relations between the peoples of China and Ceylon. He said that with the official establishment of diplomatic relations, the traditional friendship, economic cooperation, and cultural exchanges between China and Ceylon will be further developed and strengthened on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

Prime Minister Bandaranaike said: "China is not only the largest country in Asia, but also one of the most important countries in the world. It has stood up like a giant freed from shackles, and it is working for the benefit of its six hundred million people. At the same time, it plays a very important role not only in Asian affairs but also in world affairs."

Bandaranaike said that Ceylon has always pursued a foreign policy of peace and friendship towards all countries. He said that mutual understanding and the exchange of different opinions between countries are very important for achieving international peace and cooperation.

Referring to the rubber-rice agreement signed between China and Ceylon, Prime Minister Bandaranaike said that the agreement was helpful to Ceylon and consolidated the friendship between China and Ceylon. Now, Ceylon is in Beijing discussing a new five-year agreement with China.

### 我国驻锡兰大使举行首次招待会

1957-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    我国驻锡兰大使
    举行首次招待会
    新华社7日讯　科伦坡消息：中国驻锡兰大使张灿明和他的夫人8月6日晚上在科伦坡加耳费斯旅馆举行第一次招待会。
    锡兰总督古涅狄莱克爵士、总理班达拉奈克、政府部长们、外国使节们和锡兰各界知名人士共约七百位来宾出席了这次招待会。
    张灿明大使回顾了中锡两国人民间长期的友好关系的历史。他说，随着外交关系的正式建立，中锡两国传统的友谊、经济合作和文化交流将在五项原则的基础上进一步发展和加强。
    班达拉奈克总理说：“中国不仅是亚洲最大的国家，而且是世界上最重要的国家之一。它像一个摆脱了枷锁的巨人那样站起来了，它在为它的六亿人民的利益而努力。同时，它不仅在亚洲事务中而且也在世界事务中起着十分重要的作用。”
    班达拉奈克说，锡兰一直奉行着对一切国家和平友好的外交政策。他说，国家之间互相取得谅解和交换不同的意见，对于争取国际和平和合作是非常重要的。
    班达拉奈克总理在提到中国和锡兰签订的大米橡胶协定时说，这个协定对锡兰是有帮助的，并且巩固了中锡之间的友谊，现在，锡兰正在北京同中国讨论签订一个新的五年协定。
