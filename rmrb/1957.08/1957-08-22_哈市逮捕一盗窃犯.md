Title: Harbin Arrests a Thief

Author:

Date: 1957-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, August 21st. The Nangang Branch of the Harbin Municipal People's Procuratorate recently legally arrested the criminal element Yu Fengzhi for stealing and selling cloth coupons.

Yu Fengzhi was the chief cashier of the Harbin No. 7 Department Store. Following reports from the masses and subsequent investigations, it was confirmed that she had stolen 11,600 feet of civilian cloth coupons over a period of more than six months by altering purchase certificates for cloth from government organizations and groups and sold them to small vendors.

### 哈市逮捕一盗窃犯

1957-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　哈市逮捕一盗窃犯
    新华社哈尔滨21日电　哈尔滨市人民检察院南岗分院最近依法逮捕了盗窃和出售布票的犯罪分子于凤芝。
    于凤芝是哈尔滨市第七百货商店的总出纳员，经群众检举和调查证实，她以涂改机关、团体购布证等手段，在半年多时间内盗窃了一万一千六百尺民用布票，卖给了小商贩。
