Title: Omani Forces Advance Victoriously, Capture Sultan's Brother and Kill British Commander, Ghaleb Appeals to Bandung Conference Nations to Stop British Aggression
Author:
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 2nd: Omani Imam's representative in Cairo, Harithy, announced on the morning of August 1st that Omani insurgents' forces are advancing towards Ibri. Ibri, located between Nizwa and the Buraimi Oasis, is the main stronghold of British-commanded forces.

Harithy said that while advancing towards Ibri, Omani forces captured Tariq Taimur, brother of the Sultan of Muscat. Taimur commanded a battalion of approximately two thousand men. In another battle, a British commander named Hamed Shahat and seven of his soldiers were killed in a vehicle.

On the same day, the British Foreign Office denied the news that Tariq Taimur, brother of the Sultan of Muscat, had been captured by Omani insurgents.

Cairo dispatch from Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd: The Omani Imam Ghaleb's representative in Cairo, Harithy, announced on the evening of August 1st that the Omani Imam is appealing for "Asian and African nations to mediate and stop the aggression against Oman."

On the same day, the U.S. Ambassador to Egypt, Robert Hale, discussed the Oman issue with the Secretary-General of the Arab League, Hassouna. (Attached picture)

### 阿曼部队乘胜挺进  俘获苏丹的兄弟并击毙英指挥官  加勒布呼吁万隆会议国家制止英国侵略

1957-08-03
第6版()
专栏：

    阿曼部队乘胜挺进
    俘获苏丹的兄弟并击毙英指挥官
    加勒布呼吁万隆会议国家制止英国侵略
    据新华社开罗2日电　阿曼教长驻开罗代表哈尔赛1日上午宣布，阿曼起义者的部队正在向伊布里推进。伊布里在尼兹瓦和布赖米绿洲之间，是英国人指挥的部队的主要据点。
    哈尔赛说，阿曼部队在向伊布里推进时俘获了马斯喀特苏丹的兄弟塔里克·泰木尔。泰木尔指挥着大约有二千人的一个营。在另一次战斗中，一个名叫哈麦德·沙特的英国指挥官和他的七个士兵被击毙在车中。
    同一天，英国外交部否认马斯喀特苏丹兄弟塔里克·泰木尔被阿曼起义者俘虏的消息。
    据新华社2日讯　开罗消息：阿曼教长加勒布驻开罗代表哈尔赛1日晚间宣布，阿曼教长吁请“亚非国家出面调解以制止对阿曼的侵略”。
    同一天，美国驻埃及大使罗伯特·赫尔同阿拉伯联盟秘书长哈苏纳讨论了阿曼问题。（附图片）
