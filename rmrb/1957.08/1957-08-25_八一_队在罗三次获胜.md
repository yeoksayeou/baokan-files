Title: "August First" Team Wins Three Times in Romania
Author:
Date: 1957-08-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 24th - The Chinese People's Liberation Army "August First" Men's Basketball Team won for the third time in Romania on the 23rd.

　　This match was held in Timisoara, Romania. The "August First" team's opponent this time was the Timisoara City Basketball Team. As a result of the match, the "August First" team defeated the Timisoara team with a score of 88 to 55.

　　Since arriving in Romania, the "August First" team has won all three matches. They will play three more matches here. After all six matches are completed, the "August First" team will leave Romania for Czechoslovakia to compete.

### “八一”队在罗三次获胜

1957-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　“八一”队在罗三次获胜
    新华社布加勒斯特24日电　中国人民解放军“八一”男子篮球队23日在罗马尼亚比赛第三次获胜。
    这次比赛是在罗马尼亚底密索拉城进行的。“八一”队这次比赛的对手是底密索拉市篮球代表队。比赛结果，“八一”队以八十八比五十五战胜底密索拉队。
      “八一”队来罗马尼亚后，三战三捷。他们在这里将再进行三次比赛。全部六次比赛完毕以后，“八一”队即离开罗马尼亚赴捷克斯洛伐克比赛。
