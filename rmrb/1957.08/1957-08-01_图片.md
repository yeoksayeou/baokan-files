Title: Third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament to be Held in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1957-08-01
Page: 7

The Third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament is about to be held in Tokyo, and various places in Japan are making intensive preparations. This is a scene of young people in Tokyo collecting conference fees. (This situation exists all over the country.)
