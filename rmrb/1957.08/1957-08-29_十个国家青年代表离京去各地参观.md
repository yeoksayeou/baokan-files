Title: Youth Representatives from Ten Countries Leave Beijing to Visit Various Places

Author:

Date: 1957-08-29
Page: 4

Friendly Exchanges

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th: Youth representatives from various countries who participated in the Sixth World Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship Festival and then visited China, left Beijing in batches today to visit various places.

This afternoon, a group of seven youth representatives from Syria, Lebanon, Morocco, and West Africa left Beijing to visit Nanjing and other places; five youth representatives from West Germany left for Shanghai and other places.

A group of six youth representatives from Burma, Nepal, Ceylon, and India left Beijing this evening, and they will visit Anshan and other places. Three youth representatives from the Philippines also left Beijing this morning, and they will visit Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Guangzhou before returning home. Another five youth representatives from India left Beijing on the 27th at the invitation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. They will visit Vietnam and then continue their visit to China.

A total of one hundred and fifteen youth representatives from Indonesia, Australia, and New Zealand have left Beijing successively to return home. Among the Australian youth representatives, four continue to visit Beijing.

### 十个国家青年代表离京去各地参观

1957-08-29
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

　  十个国家青年代表离京去各地参观
    据新华社28日讯　参加第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节后来中国访问的各国青年代表，今天分批离开北京，去各地参观访问。
    在今天下午，叙利亚、黎巴嫩、摩洛哥和西非青年代表一行七人，离开北京去南京等地参观；西德青年代表五人，去上海等地参观。
    缅甸、尼泊尔、锡兰和印度青年代表一行六人，今天晚间离开北京，他们将到鞍山等地参观。菲律宾青年代表三人也在今天早晨离开北京，他们将在上海、杭州和广州等地参观后回国。另有印度青年代表五人已在27日离开北京，应邀去越南民主共和国访问，他们将在越南访问后，继续来中国访问。
    印度尼西亚、澳大利亚和新西兰青年代表共一百一十五人，已先后离开北京回国。澳大利亚青年代表中，有四人继续在北京访问。
