Title: Peace Conference Held in Kamakura, Japan; Zhao Puchu Conveys Condolences from the Chinese People to the Japanese People
Author:
Date: 1957-08-12
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 11th: More than five thousand people gathered today on the beach in Kamakura, a city about fifty kilometers from Tokyo, to hold a Peace Conference for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs. Twenty-six representatives from thirteen countries, including China, the United States, Britain, and Australia, who are participating in the Third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament, also attended the conference.

    After the chairman of the conference, Kamakura Mayor Toshio Isobe, delivered a welcoming speech, the conference, amidst waves of applause, passed a declaration.

    The conference declaration demanded the prohibition of nuclear weapons testing; opposed all preparations for launching an atomic war; and demanded that the Japanese government find ways to provide relief to atomic bomb victims.

    Zhao Puchu, deputy head of the Chinese delegation, on behalf of the Chinese people, extended cordial greetings and heartfelt condolences to the Japanese people who suffered from the atomic bomb disaster. He said that all the propositions and actions of the Japanese people for the prohibition of nuclear weapons and the defense of peace will be forever supported by the Chinese people.

    Representatives from Australia, the United States, India, France, Portugal, and Britain, as well as the head of the Kanagawa Prefecture delegation from Japan, Sagamihara Mayor Yojiro Kobayashi, who will participate in the World Conference tomorrow, also delivered speeches at the meeting. They unanimously called for the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen bombs and the striving for lasting world peace.

    After the conference, thousands of young men and women performed collective dances and other brilliant programs on the beach.

    The representatives from various countries attending the Peace Conference also attended a welcome reception held by the Mayor of Kamakura at noon.

### 日本鎌仓举行和平大会  赵朴初代表中国人民慰问日本人民

1957-08-12
第7版()
专栏：

    日本鎌仓举行和平大会
    赵朴初代表中国人民慰问日本人民
    据新华社东京11日电　在离开东京约五十公里的镰仓市海滨，今天有五千多人在那里举行了禁止原子弹氢弹和平大会。参加第三届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取裁军世界大会的中、美、英、澳等十三个国家的二十六个代表也出席了大会。
    大会主席、镰仓市长矶部利右卫门致词欢迎来宾以后，接着在一阵阵的掌声中通过了大会宣言。
    大会宣言要求禁止试验核武器；反对发动原子战争的一切准备；要求日本政府设法救济原子弹受害者。
    中国代表团副团长赵朴初代表中国人民，向受到原子弹灾害的日本人民致以亲切的问候和衷心的慰问。他说，日本人民为禁止核武器和保卫和平的一切主张和行动，都会受到中国人民永远的支持。
    澳大利亚、美国、印度、法国、葡萄牙和英国的代表，以及将在明天参加世界大会的日本神奈川县代表团团长、相模原市长小林与次右卫门等也都在会上致词。他们一致要求禁止原子弹、氢弹和争取世界持久和平。
    大会结束以后，成千的青年男女们在海滨上表演了集体舞蹈等精采节目。
    出席和平大会的各国代表，还在中午出席了镰仓市长举行的欢迎酒会。
