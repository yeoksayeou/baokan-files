Title: China's Women's Basketball and Men's Swimming Teams Advance to Finals After Winning Preliminary Rounds
Author: 
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd. The Chinese men's swimming team broke the Chinese national record of 1956 (9 minutes 5.1 seconds) with a time of 9 minutes 3.6 seconds in the 800-meter freestyle relay preliminary round of the International Youth Friendship Games today, securing a spot in the finals.

The Chinese team's result in the preliminary round was second only to the Soviet team. The Soviet team's result was 8 minutes 54.6 seconds. In addition to the Soviet Union and China, Romania, Poland, Hungary, East Germany, Scotland, and Czechoslovakia also qualified for the finals.

The Chinese women's basketball team lost to Bulgaria by a score of 52-63 in the preliminary round today. The Polish team defeated the Belgian team by a score of 64-30. Now, the second group preliminary round robin of women's basketball has concluded. The Belgian team lost all three games. China, Bulgaria, and Poland all won two games and lost one. Bulgaria and China have higher scores and have obtained the right to join the first group in the finals. The six countries in the first group will begin competing on August 5th in a round-robin format to determine the first to sixth place rankings.

### 我国女篮和男游泳两队在预赛中获胜进入决赛

1957-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    我国女篮和男游泳两队
    在预赛中获胜进入决赛
    新华社莫斯科2日电　中国男子游泳队今天在国际青年友谊运动会的八百公尺自由泳接力预赛中，以九分三秒六的成绩打破了中国1956年全国最高纪录（九分五秒一），取得了决赛权。
    中国队在预赛中的成绩仅次于苏联队。苏联队的成绩是八分五十四秒六。除苏联和中国队外，罗马尼亚、波兰、匈牙利、民主德国、苏格兰和捷克斯洛伐克也取得了决赛权。
    中国女子篮球队今天在预赛中以五十二比六十三败于保加利亚队。波兰队以六十四比三十胜比利时队。现在，女子篮球第二组预赛循环赛已经结束，比利时队负三场，中国、保加利亚和波兰都胜二场，负一场。保加利亚和中国队积分较多，都取得了加入第一组的决赛权利。第一组的六个国家将在8月5日开始比赛，以循环赛方式确定一至六名次。
