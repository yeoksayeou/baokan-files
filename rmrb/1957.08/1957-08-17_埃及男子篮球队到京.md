Title: Egyptian Men's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-08-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Egyptian Men's Basketball Team, a delegation of seventeen members invited to China by the All-China Sports Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They came here after participating in the Third International Youth Friendship Games in Moscow. After a few days of rest, they will first hold a friendly match with the Chinese Youth Men's Basketball Team in Beijing.

    Azaar Ishaq Guigis, leader of the Egyptian Men's Basketball Team and Secretary-General of the Egyptian Basketball Federation, said at the airport that they were very happy to be the first Egyptian sports team to visit China. Zhang Lianhua, Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, and Mu Zuoyun, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Basketball Association, and others welcomed them at the airport. Chinese basketball players who had played against the Egyptian basketball team at the Third International Youth Friendship Games not long ago also presented them with flowers.

    The Egyptian Men's Basketball Team has played against Chinese basketball teams three times. The first time was at the 12th World University Summer Games in 1954, where the Chinese team, after a hard battle, only won by one point against the Egyptian team. In 1955, the result of the match at the Second International Youth Friendship Games was 72-58, with the Chinese team winning. This time at the Third International Youth Friendship Games, the Chinese team won against the Egyptian team again with 77-61 in the competition for seventh to twelfth place. The final result this time was that the Chinese team came in seventh and the Egyptian team came in ninth.

    Among the Egyptian Men's Basketball Team visiting China this time, fourteen are athletes, four of whom are 1.90 meters or more in height. The basketball team also includes one international referee and one coach.

### 埃及男子篮球队到京

1957-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及男子篮球队到京
    新华社16日讯  应中华全国体育总会邀请来中国访问的埃及共和国男子篮球队一行十七人，今天坐飞机来到北京。他们是在莫斯科参加了第三届国际青年友谊运动会后来到这里的。经过几天休息以后，他们将首先在北京同中国青年男子篮球队举行第一场友谊比赛。
    埃及男子篮球队领队、埃及篮球联合会秘书长阿泽尔·伊沙克·古伊吉斯在飞机场上说，他们能作为埃及第一个来中国访问的体育运动队，感到非常高兴。中华全国体育总会秘书长张联华，中国篮球协会副主席牟作云等人都到机场欢迎他们。不久以前在第三届国际青年友谊运动会上同埃及篮球队举行过比赛的中国篮球运动员们，还向他们献了花。
  埃及男子篮球队曾经同中国的篮球队举行过三次比赛。1954年第一次在第十二届世界大学生夏季运动会上，中国队经过艰苦的战斗，只赢了埃及队一分。1955年在第二届国际青年友谊运动会上的比赛结果是七十二比五十八，中国队胜。这次在第三届国际青年友谊运动会上，中国队在争夺第七名到第十二名名次的比赛中，又以七十七比六十一胜埃及队。这次的最后比赛结果，中国队得第七名，埃及队得第九名。
    这次来中国访问的埃及男子篮球队中，有十四名是运动员，其中有四人身高达到或超过了一公尺九十公分。篮球队中还有国际裁判和教练各一人。
