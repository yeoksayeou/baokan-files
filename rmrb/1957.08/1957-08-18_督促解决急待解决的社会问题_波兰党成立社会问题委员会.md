Author:
Date: 1957-08-18
Page: 6

Urging the Resolution of Pressing Social Problems
Polish Party Establishes Social Issues Committee

Warsaw Dispatch: The Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party has established a Social Issues Committee, which was set up in accordance with the instructions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Party Central Committee.

The main tasks of this committee are: 1. To provide opinions on draft resolutions related to social issues. 2. To urge the Ministry of Health, the Ministry of Social Insurance and Labor, and relevant departments to resolve urgent social problems. 3. To provide information and assistance to United Workers' Party members of parliament, Party activists in relevant government departments, and relevant departments of the Party Central Committee.

Soon, Social Issues Committees will also be established within the provincial committees.

### 督促解决急待解决的社会问题  波兰党成立社会问题委员会

1957-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

    督促解决急待解决的社会问题
    波兰党成立社会问题委员会
    本报华沙17日电  波兰统一工人党中央委员会成立了社会问题委员会，这是根据党中央八中全会的指示而设立的。
    这个委员会的主要任务是：一、对有关社会问题的决议草案提出意见。二、督促卫生部、社会保险与劳动部和有关部门解决急迫的社会问题。三、向统一工人党的议员、有关政府部门的党积极分子以及党中央有关部门提供情况和帮助。
    不久，社会问题委员会也将在各省委会内成立。
