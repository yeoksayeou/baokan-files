Title: Chinese Athletes Win Third Place in International Water Motorcycle Competition
Author:
Date: 1957-08-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 11th. The water motorcycle competition of the Second International Water Multilateral Sports and Water Motorcycle Games concluded on the 9th. Chinese male and female athletes in the offshore motorboat competition scored 5,944 points, ranking third.

Athletes from Poland and Bulgaria also participated in the competition. Polish athletes won first place with 11,204 points. The athletes from Bulgaria's first team won second place, and the athletes from Bulgaria's second team won fourth place.

The International Offshore Multilateral Sports Games, held in Varna, on the Bulgarian Black Sea coast, are still continuing.

### 国际水上摩托运动比赛  我运动员获第三名

1957-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

    国际水上摩托运动比赛
    我运动员获第三名
    新华社索非亚11日电　第二届国际水上多项运动和水上摩托运动会的水上摩托比赛在9日结束，中国海上摩托艇男女运动员得五千九百四十四分，名列第三。
    参加这次比赛的还有波兰和保加利亚的运动员。波兰运动员得第一名，得一万一千二百零四分。保加利亚第一队的运动员得第二名，保加利亚第二队的运动员得第四名。
    在保加利亚黑海沿岸瓦尔那市举行的这次国际海上多项运动会还在继续举行。
