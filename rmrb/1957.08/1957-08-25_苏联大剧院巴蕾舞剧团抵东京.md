Title: Soviet Bolshoi Ballet Troupe Arrives in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1957-08-25
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Tokyo: The Soviet Moscow Bolshoi Ballet Troupe arrived in Tokyo by plane on the evening of August 23. They were warmly welcomed by thousands of representatives from all walks of life in Tokyo.

　　The troupe is led by Nazarov, Deputy Minister of Culture of the Soviet Union. It is preparing for a one-month tour in Japan.

### 苏联大剧院巴蕾舞剧团抵东京

1957-08-25
第6版()
专栏：

　  苏联大剧院巴蕾舞剧团抵东京
    据新华社讯　苏联莫斯科大剧院巴蕾舞剧团在8月23日晚上坐飞机到达东京。他们受到东京各界代表数千人的热烈欢迎。
    剧团由苏联文化部副部长纳扎罗夫率领。它准备在日本作为期一月的巡回演出。
