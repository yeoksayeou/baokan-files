Title: Telegram of Greetings from the Japanese Former Military Personnel Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 3

    Telegram of Greetings from the Japanese Former Military Personnel Delegation to China
    To the Minister of National Defense, Beijing:
    On the occasion of the 30th Anniversary of the founding of the Army, we wish your army growing strength. We sincerely thank you for the profound friendship you have shown us. Please give our regards to all the gentlemen.
                        Japanese Former Military Personnel Delegation
