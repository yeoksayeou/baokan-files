Title: Indian Vice President to Visit Our Country in Mid-September
Author:
Date: 1957-08-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th. Indian Republic Vice President Dr. Radhakrishnan, at the invitation of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, will visit our country in mid-September of this year.

### 印副总统九月中旬访问我国

1957-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    印副总统九月中旬访问我国
    新华社8日讯　印度共和国副总统拉达克里希南博士应中华人民共和国毛泽东主席邀请，将于今年9月中旬来我国访问。
