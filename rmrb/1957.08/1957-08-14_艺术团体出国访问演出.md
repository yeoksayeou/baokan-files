Title: Art Troupes Visit Foreign Countries for Performances
Author:
Date: 1957-08-14
Page: 4

    Art Troupes Visit Foreign Countries for Performances
    PLA Song and Dance Troupe to Mongolia
    Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe left for the Mongolian People's Republic this morning for a one-month performance visit. The troupe consists of seventy-seven people, led by Colonel Chen Yading, the head of the troupe. They are going at the invitation of the Mongolian side according to the 1957 Sino-Mongolian cultural cooperation plan.
    Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe to Syria
    Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: The Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe, in accordance with the implementation plan of the 1956 Sino-Syrian cultural cooperation agreement, departed from Beijing today for a friendly visit to Syria, led by Zhang Tiesheng, the head of the troupe, and Zhang Fei, the deputy head of the troupe.
    The Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe will perform China's traditional acrobatic arts for the Syrian people during the Damascus International Exposition in early September.
    After visiting Syria, the art troupe will proceed to Egypt and other countries for friendly visits.

### 艺术团体出国访问演出

1957-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    艺术团体出国访问演出
    人民解放军歌舞团赴蒙古
    新华社13日讯　中国人民解放军歌舞团于今天早晨赴蒙古人民共和国，作为期一个月的演出访问。这个团共有七十七人，由团长陈亚丁大校率领。他们是根据中蒙1957年文化合作计划，应蒙方的邀请前去的。
    中国杂技艺术团去叙利亚
    新华社13日讯　中国杂技艺术团根据1956年中叙文化合作协定执行计划规定，已于今日在团长张铁生、副团长张非率领下，从北京启程前往叙利亚作友好访问。
    中国杂技艺术团将在9月上旬大马士革国际博览会期间，向叙利亚人民演出我国民族传统的杂技艺术。
    艺术团访叙后，将赴埃及等国进行友好访问。
