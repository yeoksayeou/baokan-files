Title: Burmese Parliamentary Delegation Departs Beijing, Will Visit Qingdao and Other Places Before Visiting Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd: The Burmese Parliamentary Delegation, headed by Tekin Din Maung, and accompanied by Liu Guan-yi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit Qingdao. After visiting East China and South China, the delegation will visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
Those who saw them off at the airport included Vice Chairman Li Jishen and members of the Standing Committee, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, representatives of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, heads of various people's organizations, and people from all walks of life in the capital.
Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy, U Ba Maung, and a representative of the Vietnamese Embassy also saw them off at the airport.
Vice Chairman Li Jishen delivered a farewell speech, thanking the delegation for their work in promoting Sino-Burmese friendship during this visit, and asked the delegation to bring back the friendship of the Chinese people to Burma.
In his farewell speech, delegation leader Tekin Din Maung expressed his gratitude to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and various localities for the warm welcome and hospitality they had received. He said that the visit of the Burmese Parliamentary Delegation had deepened Sino-Burmese friendship, and he believed that Sino-Burmese friendship would be further developed in the future.

### 缅甸国会访华代表团离京  将在青岛等地参观后去越南访问

1957-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸国会访华代表团离京
    将在青岛等地参观后去越南访问
    据新华社2日讯　以德钦登貌为首的缅甸联邦国会访华代表团由全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长刘贯一陪同，今天早晨乘飞机离北京去青岛参观。代表团将在到华东和华南各地参观后去越南民主共和国进行访问。
    到机场送行的，有李济深副委员长和委员们，国务院秘书长习仲勋，中缅友好协会会长郑振铎，北京市副市长吴晗，外交部的代表和各人民团体负责人，以及首都各界人士。
    缅甸大使馆临时代办吴巴茂和越南大使馆的代表也到机场送行。
    李济深副委员长致欢送词，感谢代表团这次访问为中缅友好作了很多工作，并且请代表团把中国人民的友谊带回缅甸去。
    德钦登貌团长在告别词中，对全国人民代表大会常务委员会和各地给予他们的热烈欢迎和招待表示感谢。他说，缅甸国会代表团的这次访问加深了中缅友谊，他相信中缅友谊在今后还将获得进一步的发展。
