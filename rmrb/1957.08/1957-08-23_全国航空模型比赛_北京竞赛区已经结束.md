Title: National Aviation Model Competition Beijing Competition Area Concludes
Author: Bai Shigang
Date: 1957-08-23
Page: 4

　  National Aviation Model Competition
　  Beijing Competition Area Concludes
    (Our newspaper dispatch) The 1957 National Aviation Model Competition is being held in seven regions. The Beijing area competition has concluded yesterday.

    In the Class 1 free flight piston engine model airplane competition, Liu Mingdao from Xi'an Aviation Academy, Luo Sizhu from Beijing Aviation Academy, and Tang Jianzhuo from Nanjing Aviation Academy, all three of their model airplanes achieved perfect scores of 540 points in all three flight events.

    In the Class 2 and Class 3 tow glider and Class 2 and Class 3 rubber band glider competitions, the best results were achieved by the Xi'an and Nanjing Aviation Academies.

    In this competition, the Xi'an Aviation Academy team achieved first place with a total score of 2577 points (out of a perfect score of 2700 points). This score is an improvement of 123 points over the 2454 points they achieved at the National Aviation Model Competition last year. Nanjing Aviation Academy achieved second place with a total score of 2500 points; third place was awarded to Beijing Aviation Academy with a total score of 2102 points.
                      (Bai Shigang)

### 全国航空模型比赛　北京竞赛区已经结束
白世刚
1957-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　  全国航空模型比赛
　  北京竞赛区已经结束
    本报讯　1957年航空模型比赛分七个地区进行，北京区竞赛大会已于昨日结束。
    在一级自由飞活塞式发动模型飞机的比赛中，西安航空学院的刘明道，北京航空学院的罗四逐，南京航空学院的唐坚卓，他们三个人的模型飞机在三项飞行中，都达到了满分——540分。
    在二级、三级牵引模型滑翔机，二级、三级橡筋模型滑翔机的比赛中，成绩最好的是西安和南京两个航空学院。
    在这次比赛中，西安航空学院代表队以2577分（满分是2700分）的总成绩获得第一名。这个成绩比他们在去年全国航空模型竞赛大会上所创造的2454分提高了123分。获得第二名的是南京航空学院，总成绩是2500分；第三名被北京航空学院获得，总成绩是2102分。
                      （白世刚）
