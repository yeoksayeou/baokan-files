Title: Bringing Peace and Friendship to the Five Continents: Youth Representatives from Various Countries Depart the Soviet Union for Home

Author:

Date: 1957-08-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: According to TASS reports, representatives from various countries who participated in the Sixth World Festival of Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship are departing Moscow for home in succession. Before returning home, they will also visit some cities in the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 14th: The Chinese youth delegation participating in the World Youth Festival left Moscow on the 13th to return home. On the same day, part of the delegation, led by head of the delegation Hu Yaobang, flew to Kiev to attend the Fourth World Youth Congress. The Chinese Youth Art Troupe will travel to various parts of the Soviet Union in three groups to perform. One group will go to the Soviet Far East, one group will go to Riga and Minsk, and then tour the cities along the Volga River. The Acrobatic Troupe will perform in cities along the Black Sea. (Attached picture)

Egyptian youth representatives are about to return home, and their friends lift him up to express their reluctance to part. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 把和平和友谊带往五大洲  各国青年代表离苏回国

1957-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

    把和平和友谊带往五大洲
    各国青年代表离苏回国
    据新华社14日讯　据塔斯社报道：参加第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的各国代表纷纷动身离开莫斯科回国。回国前，他们还将参观苏联的一些城市。
    新华社莫斯科14日电　中国参加世界青年联欢节的青年代表团13日已经离开莫斯科返国。同日，代表团一部分团员在团长胡耀邦率领下乘飞机赴基辅参加第四届世界青年代表大会。中国青年艺术团将分三批到苏联各地演出。一队到苏维埃远东，一队到里加、明斯克，然后沿伏尔加河各城市作巡回演出。杂技团将到黑海沿岸各城市演出。（附图片）
    埃及青年代表要回国了，朋友们把他抬起来表示惜别。（新华社）
