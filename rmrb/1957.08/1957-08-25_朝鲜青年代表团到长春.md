Title: Korean Youth Delegation Arrives in Changchun
Author:
Date: 1957-08-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, August 24th: The Korean youth delegation, which participated in the World Youth Festival, arrived in Changchun City for a visit in two groups on the 20th and 21st, at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League during their return trip home. On the 23rd, they traveled by car to Anshan City in Liaoning Province to continue their tour.

In Changchun, the Korean youth delegation visited the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant, Changchun Film Studio, etc. On the 22nd, the delegation's art troupe held two wonderful performances for people from all walks of life in Jilin Province and Changchun City, as well as for the workers of the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant, and received warm praise. Before leaving Changchun on the 23rd, the guests also held a joint soiree with youth from all walks of life.

### 朝鲜青年代表团到长春

1957-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜青年代表团到长春
    新华社长春24日电　参加世界青年联欢节的朝鲜青年代表团在返国途中，应我国共青团中央委员会的邀请，在20、21日分两批来到长春市访问。23日他们乘车前往辽宁省鞍山市继续参观访问。
    朝鲜青年代表团在长春曾经参观了第一汽车制造厂、长春电影制片厂等。代表团的艺术团在22日曾经为吉林省、长春市各界和第一汽车制造厂的职工们举行了两场精采演出，受到热烈的赞扬。客人们在23日离开长春前，又和各界青年举行了联欢晚会。
