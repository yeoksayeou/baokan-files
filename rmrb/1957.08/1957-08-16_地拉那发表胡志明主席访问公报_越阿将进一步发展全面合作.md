Title: Tirana Publishes Communiqué on Ho Chi Minh's Visit: Vietnam and Albania to Further Develop Comprehensive Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1957-08-16
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, August 15th: The newspapers here published on August 14th the communiqué on the visit of President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to Albania. The communiqué stated that Albania fully supports the unremitting efforts of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to implement the Geneva Agreements on the peaceful reunification of Vietnam. It firmly condemns the attempts by imperialist countries, especially the United States and the South Vietnamese authorities, to obstruct the peaceful reunification of Vietnam.

The communiqué said that relations between Vietnam and Albania are developing smoothly. In the future, the two countries will further develop cooperation in political, economic, and cultural fields. At the same time, for the benefit of the people of both countries and for the cause of peace and socialism, the leaders of the two countries will continue to visit and meet with each other in the future.

The communiqué also pointed out the importance of maintaining and further strengthening the solidarity of socialist countries.

The two governments believe that the policy of peaceful coexistence and resolving all international issues, such as disarmament, through negotiation is the only correct policy that is in the interest of the people of all countries and the interests of world peace. They also stated that they will always stand closely united with the Soviet Union and other socialist countries, contributing their strength to easing international tensions, promoting cooperation among the people of all countries, and defending world peace.

### 地拉那发表胡志明主席访问公报  越阿将进一步发展全面合作

1957-08-16
第6版()
专栏：

    地拉那发表胡志明主席访问公报
    越阿将进一步发展全面合作
    新华社地拉那15日电  此间报纸在8月14日公布的越南民主共和国主席胡志明访问阿尔巴尼亚的公报说，阿尔巴尼亚完全支持越南民主共和国政府为实现关于和平统一越南的日内瓦协议所作的坚持不懈的努力。它坚决谴责帝国主义国家、特别是美国和南越当局阻挠和平统一越南的企图。
    公报说，越、阿两国之间的关系正在顺利地发展。今后，两国将进一步发展在政治经济和文化方面的合作关系。同时，为了两国人民的利益以及和平和社会主义事业的利益，两国领导人今后还将继续相互进行访问和会见。
    公报还指出维护和进一步加强社会主义阵营国家的团结的重要性。
    两国政府认为，和平共处和通过谈判的方式来解决裁减军备等所有国际问题的政策，是符合各国人民利益和世界和平利益的唯一正确的政策，并且表示将永远同苏联和社会主义阵营其他各国紧密团结在一起，为缓和国际紧张局势、增进各国人民之间的合作和保卫世界和平而贡献出自己的力量。
