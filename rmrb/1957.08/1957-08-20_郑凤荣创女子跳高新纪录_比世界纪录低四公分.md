Title: Zheng Fengrong Sets New National Women's High Jump Record; Four Centimeters Short of World Record
Author:
Date: 1957-08-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 19th. Chinese athlete Zheng Fengrong broke the Chinese national women's high jump record on August 18th in Berlin with an astonishing result of 1.72 meters. This record is only four centimeters short of the current world record, five centimeters higher than the second-place result in women's high jump at the 16th Olympic Games last year, and eleven centimeters higher than the Chinese national record in 1956.

Young athlete Zheng Fengrong is currently visiting the German Democratic Republic with the Chinese track and field team. She recently participated in the Third International Youth Friendship Games in Moscow, where she achieved fourth place with a result of 1.63 meters.

Zheng Fengrong is currently twenty years old, and this new result is one centimeter higher than her own height (1.71 meters). Her progress is very rapid. In 1953, when she first participated in a national competition (the Chinese National Athletics, Gymnastics, and Cycling Games), she could only jump 1.36 meters and finished fourth. By 1956, she had reached 1.61 meters, equalling the then-national record of China. In 1957, she broke the 1956 Chinese national record with a result of 1.65 meters at the China-Soviet (Dynamo) track and field meet held in Beijing. (Attached picture)

Above: In the track and field competition held in Berlin, German Democratic Republic, Chinese athlete Zheng Fengrong won first place in the high jump competition. The second place winner is Fu Xueyan (left).

### 郑凤荣创女子跳高新纪录  比世界纪录低四公分

1957-08-20
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    郑凤荣创女子跳高新纪录
    比世界纪录低四公分
    新华社柏林19日电  中国女运动员郑凤荣8月18日在柏林以一公尺七十二公分的惊人成绩打破了中国全国女子跳高纪录。这个纪录只比目前的世界纪录低四公分，比去年第十六届奥林匹克运动会女子跳高第二名的成绩高五公分，比1956年中国全国纪录高十一公分。
    青年选手郑凤荣目前正随中国田径队在民主德国访问。她最近在莫斯科参加第三届国际青年友谊运动会中，曾经以一公尺六十三公分的成绩获得第四名。
    郑凤荣现年二十岁，这次的新成绩比她本人的体高（一公尺七十一公分）还高一公分。她的进步非常迅速。1953年，她第一次参加全国性比赛（中国全国田径、体操、自行车运动大会）的时候，还只能跳一公尺三十六公分，获得第四名。到1956年，她就达到了一公尺六十一公分的成绩，与那时的全中国纪录相等。1957年，她在北京举行的中、苏（狄纳莫）田径对抗赛中，就以一公尺六十五公分的成绩打破了1956年全中国最高纪录。（附图片）
