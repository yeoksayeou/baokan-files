Title: Representatives from Various Countries Visit Hiroshima
Author:
Date: 1957-08-18
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 17th. Representatives from China and other countries attending the Third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament, accompanied by Mr. Kuroda, a permanent director of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, took a train to Hiroshima this evening to visit.
	Zhao Puchu, deputy head of the Chinese delegation, and delegation members Chen Yongwen and Zhu Hongyuan, are scheduled to participate in the anti-atomic and hydrogen bomb rally in Osaka on their way back to Tokyo from Hiroshima.

### 各国代表前往广岛参观

1957-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

    各国代表前往广岛参观
    新华社东京17日电  参加第三届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取裁军世界大会的中国和其他各国代表团今天晚间在日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会常任理事黑田先生的陪同下，乘火车前往广岛参观访问。
    中国代表团副团长赵朴初和团员陈用文、朱洪元三人预定在从广岛回东京的途中，到大阪参加反对原子弹氢弹大会。
