Title: Men's Basketball and Gymnastics Competitions Conclude; Our Team Both Ranked Seventh
Author:
Date: 1957-08-10
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow Dispatch: In the matches for seventh to twelfth place, the Chinese men's basketball team defeated the Egyptian team 77-61 on August 8th and the Belgian team 96-36 on August 9th, securing seventh place.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 9th: The men's gymnastics competition of the International Youth Friendship Games concluded on August 8th. The Chinese team scored a total of 321.4 points, ranking seventh. The top six were the Soviet Union, Japan, East Germany, Czechoslovakia, Poland, and Romania. Eighth place went to the Egyptian team.

### 男子篮球和体操比赛结束  我队均名列第七

1957-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

    男子篮球和体操比赛结束
    我队均名列第七
    据新华社莫斯科电　在争夺第七到第十二名名次的比赛中，中国男子篮球队8日以七十七比六十一战胜埃及队，9日以九十六比三十六胜比利时队，取得第七名。
    据新华社莫斯科9日电　国际青年友谊运动会的男子体操比赛已经在8月8日结束。中国队总分得三二一点四分，名列第七。前六名是苏联、日本、民主德国、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、罗马尼亚。第八名是埃及队。
