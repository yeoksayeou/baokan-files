Title: Touring Three Continents with an Amphibious Jeep
Author: Gong Yan
Date: 1957-08-04
Page: 6

	Touring Three Continents with an Amphibious Jeep
	In London, recently four young men and women, using a semi-new amphibious jeep, set off from London on a trip to Australia. Their route is: the first leg across the English Channel to Paris, then to Geneva, Milan, Venice and then Belgrade; the second leg starts in Istanbul, passing through Ankara, Middle Eastern countries and Pakistan to India, then from Calcutta, crossing the sea to Rangoon, Sumatra, Java and other places. The last leg of the journey is Melbourne and Sydney in Australia.
	(Gong Yan)

### 利用两用吉普游历三大洲
公岩
1957-08-04
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    利用两用吉普游历三大洲
    在伦敦，最近有四个男女青年利用了一辆半新旧的水陆两用吉普车，从伦敦出发，前往澳大利亚旅行。他们的旅行路线是：第一段渡过英吉利海峡到巴黎，再到日内瓦、米兰诺、威尼斯后贝尔格莱德；第二段以伊斯坦堡为起点，经过安卡拉、中东国家和巴基斯坦直到印度，然后由加尔各答，渡海到仰光、苏门答腊、爪哇等地。最后一段的行程是澳大利亚的墨尔本和悉尼。
          （公岩）
