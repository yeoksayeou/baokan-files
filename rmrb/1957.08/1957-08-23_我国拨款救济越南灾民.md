Title: China Allocates Funds to Aid Vietnamese Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1957-08-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 22nd: According to a Vietnam News Agency report today, the Chinese government has allocated 300,000 yuan in Renminbi to aid the disaster victims in Bac Ninh Province, Vietnam, who suffered from flooding due to the breach of the Thun River dike.

Since Bac Ninh Province suffered flooding in July, countries of the socialist camp have successively allocated funds to aid the 200,000 disaster victims there.

### 我国拨款救济越南灾民

1957-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

    我国拨款救济越南灾民
    据新华社河内22日电　据越南通讯社今天报道：中国政府已经拨款三十万人民币，救济越南北宁省由于敦河河堤决口而遭受水灾的灾民。
    北宁省在7月间遭受水灾以来，社会主义阵营各国纷纷拨出款项，救济这里的二十万灾民。
