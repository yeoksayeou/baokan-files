Title: Ógertsson Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-08-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: The Chairman of the Icelandic United Socialist Party, Einar Ógertsson, and his wife and two children, a party of four, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their country after a three-week visit to China.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; and Liu Ningyi, Member of the Central Committee.

### 奥格尔森回国

1957-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    奥格尔森回国
    新华社24日讯　冰岛统一社会党主席埃纳尔·奥格尔森和他的夫人、子女一行四人在我国进行了三个星期的参观访问后，已经在今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    前往机场欢送的有中共中央总书记邓小平，中共中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆和中央委员刘宁一等。
