Title: Yao Nationality Private Elementary School (Picture)
Author: Nan Sha
Date: 1957-08-27
Page: 4

　　Yao Nationality Private Elementary School
    In the remote mountainous area of Guizhou Province – Yaolu Township, Libo County, a new Yaolu Elementary School has been established. This township is inhabited by one hundred and ninety-nine Yao compatriots. In the past, few of them were literate. Now, they have established cooperatives, their lives have improved, and they have established their own school. Yao teacher Qin Tingsheng often shows picture books and tells stories to the children. The private elementary school, Yaolu Elementary School, is built on the scenic Yaolu Mountain.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Photo by Nan Sha (Xinhua News Agency)

### 瑶族民办小学（图片）
南沙
1957-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　瑶族民办小学
    在贵州省的边远山区——荔波县瑶麓乡，新办了一所瑶麓小学。这个乡里居住着一百九十九个瑶胞，从前他们很少有人识字，现在他们建立了合作社，生活有了改善，自己也办了学校。瑶族教员覃廷生经常给孩子们看画报、讲故事。建立在风景优美的瑶麓山上的民办小学——瑶麓小学。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　南沙摄（新华社稿）
