    Friendly Exchanges

    Japanese Agricultural Chemistry Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Zhu Kezhen Hosts Banquet to Welcome Distinguished Guests
    Xinhua News Agency, August 15th: The Japanese Agricultural Chemistry Delegation, invited to visit China by the Chinese Academy of Sciences, arrived in Beijing by train tonight. The delegation consists of nine members and is led by Professor Yuzuki Sumiki.

    Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Japanese Agricultural Chemistry Delegation to China, led by Professor Yuzuki Sumiki.

    Syrian Socialist Party Member of Parliament Youssef Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Mr. and Mrs. Hassan Youssef, a member of parliament of the Syrian Socialist Party, invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, have arrived in Beijing today for a visit to China.

    Beijing Football Team to Visit Afghanistan
    Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: The Beijing Football Team flew to Kabul today to participate in the week-long celebrations of the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, which begins on August 24th.

    The Beijing Football Team consists of twenty-one members. The leader of the team is Huang Zhong, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation and Chairman of the Chinese Football Association. The deputy leader is Ke Lun. The football team includes eighteen athletes: goalkeepers Xu Fusheng and Lin Zhongshu, defenders Sun Baorong and Sun Yuming, center backs Wang Xinsheng and Xie Hongjun, midfielders Chen Chengda, Ren Bin, Cheng Wenkuan, and Weng Chuanlong, and forwards Chen Jialiang, Liu Fulin, Chen Keliang, Pan Peigen, Sheng Jinrong, Xian Dixiong, Cui Hengxie, and Sun Fucheng.

    During the Independence Day celebrations of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, the Beijing Football Team will play matches with three football teams from the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

    Football teams from India, Iran, and Turkey have also been invited to participate in the friendly football matches during the Independence Day celebrations of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. They will also play matches in rotation with the three football teams from the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

    Chinese Seamen's Union Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: A seven-member delegation of the Chinese Seamen's Union left Beijing by train today for a friendly visit to the Soviet Union.

    The delegation was invited to visit the Soviet Union by the Central Committee of the Soviet Maritime and Inland Water Transport Workers' Union. The head of the delegation is Zhang Zifu, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union.

    British Foundry Workers' Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: A three-member delegation of the British Foundry Workers' Union, invited to visit China by the China First Machine Building Trade Union, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 友好往来

1957-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    日农艺化学代表团到京
    竺可桢设宴欢迎贵宾
    新华社15日讯  应中国科学院邀请来我国访问的日本农艺化学代表团一行九人，由团长住木谕介率领，已经在今天晚上乘火车到达北京。
    据新华社16日讯  中国科学院竺可桢副院长今晚设宴欢迎以住木谕介教授为首的日本农艺化学访华代表团。
    叙社会党议员约瑟夫到京
    新华社16日讯  应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来中国参观访问的叙利亚社会党议员哈森·约瑟夫夫妇，已经在今天到达北京。
    北京足球队去阿富汗访问
    新华社16日讯  北京足球队今天乘飞机赴喀布尔，他们是应邀前往参加8月24日开始的为时一周的阿富汗王国独立日庆祝活动的。
    北京足球队由二十一人组成，领队是中华全国体育总会副主席、中国足球协会主席黄中，副领队是柯轮。足球队中有运动员十八名。他们是：守门员徐福生、林仲淑，后卫孙宝荣、孙玉明，中卫王新生、谢鸿钧，前卫陈成达、任彬、成文宽、翁传龙，前锋陈家亮、刘福林、陈克亮、潘培根、盛金荣、冼迪雄、崔亨燮、孙福成。
    在阿富汗王国独立日庆祝活动期间，北京足球队将同阿富汗王国的三个足球队举行比赛。
    这次应邀参加阿富汗王国独立日庆祝活动足球友谊赛的还有印度、伊朗和土耳其的足球队。他们也将同阿富汗王国的三个足球队轮流举行比赛。
    我海员工会代表团访苏
    新华社16日讯  中国海员工会访苏代表团一行七人今天从北京乘火车去苏联进行友好访问。
    代表团是应苏联海上和内河航运工人工会中央委员会的邀请去苏联访问的。代表团团长是中国海员工会全国委员会副主席张子夫。
    英翻砂工人联合会代表团到京
    新华社16日讯  英国翻砂工人联合会访华代表团一行三人，应中国第一机械工会邀请来我国访问，已经在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
