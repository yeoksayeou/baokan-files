Title: Chilean Poet Neruda Leaves Beijing for Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-08-18
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, August 17th: Chilean poet Pablo Neruda and his wife left Beijing for Moscow by plane today.

Neruda and his wife arrived in China for the second time in July this year. During the past month, he traveled to Kunming, Wuhan and other places, and also went to the seaside resorts of Qingdao and Beidaihe for vacation and creation.

On August 15th, at the welcome poetry recital for Neruda in Beijing, he recited a poem he wrote during his trip to China, "Song of the Chinese Land," in which he once again expressed his love for China.

### 智利诗人聂鲁达离北京赴莫斯科

1957-08-18
第7版()
专栏：

    智利诗人聂鲁达离北京赴莫斯科
    据新华社17日讯  智利诗人巴勃罗·聂鲁达夫妇今天乘飞机离开北京赴莫斯科。
    聂鲁达和他的夫人是今年7月间第二次来到中国的。一个月来他曾到昆明、武汉等地旅行，还到青岛、北戴河的海滨休养，创作。
    8月15日，他在北京欢迎聂鲁达诗歌朗诵晚会上，朗诵了他在中国旅行期间所写的一首诗“中国大地之歌”，在这首诗里又一次表达了他对中国的热爱。
