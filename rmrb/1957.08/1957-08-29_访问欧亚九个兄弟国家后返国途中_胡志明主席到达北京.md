Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Arrives in Beijing After Visiting Nine Fraternal Countries in Europe and Asia

Author:

Date: 1957-08-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th. After concluding his visit to nine fraternal countries in Asia and Europe, Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and his entourage arrived in Beijing at 2:20 PM today by plane from Moscow on their way back home.

As Chairman Ho Chi Minh stepped off the plane, cheers erupted at the airport. He warmly shook hands, embraced, and kissed Vice Chairman Zhu De. At this moment, the welcoming crowd surged forward from all directions, cheering and presenting him with flowers.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh also shook hands and embraced the Chinese state leaders who came to welcome him. Those who came to the airport to welcome him included Vice Chairmen Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers Deng Xiaoping and He Long, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Vice Mayor of Beijing Zhang Youyu, Chinese Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Luo Guibo, and heads of various departments of Chinese state organs and generals of the People's Liberation Army.

Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China Nguyen Khang, other diplomatic envoys to China, and Vietnamese students also came to the airport to welcome him.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 28th. After visiting Czechoslovakia, Poland, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Yugoslavia, Albania, and Romania, Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam left Moscow on August 27th evening aboard a "Tu-104" special plane, heading home via Beijing.

The Supreme Soviet and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union sent special personnel to accompany Chairman Ho Chi Minh to the Soviet border station of Irkutsk.

Soviet leaders such as Suslov, Pospelov, and Kosygin, as well as envoys from many countries to the Soviet Union, went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

On this day, Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, hosted a banquet in honor of Chairman Ho Chi Minh. (Attached picture)

### 访问欧亚九个兄弟国家后返国途中　胡志明主席到达北京

1957-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　访问欧亚九个兄弟国家后返国途中
　　胡志明主席到达北京
    新华社28日讯　越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他的随行人员，结束了在亚洲和欧洲九个兄弟国家的访问之后，在返国途中于今天下午二时二十分乘飞机从莫斯科到达北京。
    胡志明主席走下飞机时，机场上响起了一片欢呼声。他同朱德副主席亲切地握手拥抱和接吻。这时欢迎的群众从四面拥上前来，向他欢呼和献花。
    胡志明主席又同前来欢迎他的中国国家领导人握手和拥抱。到机场欢迎的，有彭真、陈叔通副委员长，邓小平、贺龙副总理，外交部副部长张闻天，北京市副市长张友渔，中国驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波，以及中国国家领导机关各部门负责人和解放军将领等。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使阮康和其他各国驻中国使节以及越南留学生也到机场欢迎。
    新华社莫斯科28日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明在访问了捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、民主德国、匈牙利、南斯拉夫、阿尔巴尼亚和罗马尼亚等国以后，8月27日晚乘“图—104”专机离开莫斯科取道北京回国。
    苏联最高苏维埃和外交部派了专人陪送胡志明主席到苏联边境站伊尔库次克。
    前往机场欢送贵宾的有苏斯洛夫、波斯伯洛夫、柯西金等苏联领导人以及许多国家驻苏联使节。
    这一天，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫曾经设宴招待胡志明主席。（附图片）
