Title: Rongchang Coal Mine Put Into Production Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1957-08-19
Page: 4

    Rongchang Coal Mine Put Into Production Ahead of Schedule
    (Our Dispatch) The No. 1 inclined shaft of the Rongchang Coal Mine in Sichuan, with an annual output of 210,000 tons, was put into trial production on August 2nd. This new mine started construction on February 4, 1956. According to the original plan, it should have been handed over for production in the first quarter of 1958, but it is now put into production half a year ahead of schedule. It is estimated that 40,000 tons of raw coal can be produced this year, supplying the industrial and civilian coal needs of Chengdu, Chongqing, Neijiang and other places.

### 荣昌煤矿提前投入生产

1957-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

    荣昌煤矿提前投入生产
    本报讯　年产二十一万吨的四川荣昌煤矿一号斜井，已于8月2日投入试生产。这对新建矿井是从1956年2月4日开工的。按原计划应在1958年第一季度移交生产，现在却提前半年投入生产。预计今年可产原煤四万吨，供给成都、重庆、内江等工业和民用煤的需要。
