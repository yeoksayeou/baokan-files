Title: Love Socialism, Hate Militarism: East German Ten Thousand Youth Carnival
Author:
Date: 1957-08-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 6th: The "Magdeburg Ten Thousand Youth Festival" has already opened on August 3rd, according to the German News Agency. In addition to young people from all over East Germany, about 20,000 young men and women from West Germany and youth representatives from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Hungary, Austria and other countries are participating in this festival. The festival will last until August 11th.

When the festival opened at the Magdeburg Stadium, more than 5,000 German and foreign youth representatives held a grand entrance ceremony. In the procession, the young people expressed their hatred of German militarism and their love for socialism. They held up signs that read: "Down with the North Atlantic Treaty Organization!" "Youths, become advanced work teams in socialist construction!" The First Secretary of the Magdeburg District Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party, Pischner, addressed the youth participating in the festival and expressed a warm welcome to them. Finally, the young people held a wonderful sports performance.

### 热爱社会主义　憎恨军国主义  民主德国十万青年联欢

1957-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    热爱社会主义　憎恨军国主义
    民主德国十万青年联欢
    新华社柏林6日电　据德意志通讯社报道，“马格德堡十万青年联欢节”已经在8月3日开幕。参加这个联欢节的除了来自民主德国各地的青年外，还有大约两万名西德男女青年和来自苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、匈牙利、奥地利等国的青年代表。联欢节将举行到8月11日结束。
    联欢节在马格德堡大运动场开幕时，德国和外国青年代表五千多人举行了盛大的入场式。在行进中，青年们表示了他们对德国军国主义的憎恨和社会主义的热爱。他们举着标语牌的上面写道：“打倒北大西洋公约组织！”“青年们，成为社会主义建设中先进的工作队！”德国统一社会党马格德堡专区委员会第一书记皮斯尼克向参加联欢节的青年致辞，对他们表示热烈的欢迎。最后，青年们举行了精彩的体育表演。
