Title: Yugov to Visit Vietnam at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1957-08-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, August 18th:  President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, in a speech at the airport before leaving Sofia on the 17th, mentioned that Bulgarian Council of Ministers Chairman Yugov is about to visit Vietnam. President Ho Chi Minh said that the Vietnamese people will welcome this visit with the greatest joy.

### 于哥夫将应邀访问越南

1957-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    于哥夫将应邀访问越南
    据新华社索非亚18日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明在17日离开索非亚前在机场的讲话中提到，保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫即将到越南去访问。胡志明主席说，越南人民将怀着最愉快的心情来迎接这个访问。
