Title: Indonesian Cabinet Agrees to Promote the New Life Movement
Author:
Date: 1957-08-04
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 3rd. The Indonesian Cabinet meeting last night discussed the National Council's proposal to promote the New Life Movement nationwide and expressed its agreement. The Cabinet also organized a special committee headed by the First Deputy Prime Minister to be responsible for formulating the steps to implement this movement, in order to implement it from August 17th. The National Council will assist the government in promoting this movement.
    President Sukarno submitted this proposal from the National Council to the Cabinet in his capacity as Chairman of the National Council. Sukarno also participated in last night's cabinet meeting. The New Life Movement is a spiritual revolution movement initiated by President Sukarno.

### 印度尼西亚内阁同意推行新生活运动

1957-08-04
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚内阁
    同意推行新生活运动
    新华社雅加达3日电　印度尼西亚内阁会议昨天晚上讨论了民族委员会提出的在全国推行新生活运动的建议之后，表示同意这个建议。内阁并且组织了一个以第一副总理为首的特别委员会负责拟定推行这个运动的步骤，以便从8月17日起实行。民族委员会将协助政府推行这个运动。
    民族委员会的这个建议是由苏加诺总统以民族委员会主席身份提交内阁的。苏加诺并且参加了昨天晚上的内阁会议。新生活运动是苏加诺总统倡议的一种精神革命运动。
