Title: “August 1st” Men's Basketball Team Visits Romania and Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1957-08-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - The Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Men's Basketball Team left Beijing by plane today for Romania and Czechoslovakia. They are going to visit and play games at the invitation of these two countries.

This "August 1st" Men's Basketball Team consists of sixteen people, including the following basketball players: Yu Bangji, Wang Zhaoyu, Yu Yuanxu, Zhou Chunlin, Lu Lianhan, Wu Zibing, Cai Jijie, Zhang Dexin, Zheng Li, Wei Wenqiang, Jia Xiangqi, Zhong Jiwen.

### “八一”男子篮球队  前往罗捷两国访问

1957-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    “八一”男子篮球队
    前往罗捷两国访问
    据新华社12日讯　中国人民解放军“八一”男子篮球队今天从北京乘飞机前往罗马尼亚和捷克斯洛伐克。他们是应这两个国家的邀请前去作访问比赛的。
    这个“八一”男子篮球队共十六人，其中篮球运动员是：余邦基、王兆钰、俞元煦、周春霖、路廉翰、吴自秉、蔡集杰、张德鑫、郑礼、魏文强、贾祥麒、钟纪文。
