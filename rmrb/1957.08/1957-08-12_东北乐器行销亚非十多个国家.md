Title: Northeast Musical Instrument Sales Reach Over Ten Countries in Asia and Africa
Author:
Date: 1957-08-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, August 11th: More than ten types of "Dongfanghong" (East is Red) brand musical instruments produced by the Northeast Musical Instrument Factory, including pianos, violins, organs, harmonicas, and brass instruments, are now sold throughout more than ten countries in Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and Africa.

Recently, this factory is manufacturing a refined piano for Sweden. Soon, this musical instrument factory will also successfully trial-produce four new types of musical instruments: dulcimers, accordions, pianos, and harmonicas. Among them, the "twist harmonica" can change tones at will during playing, and can play beautiful semitones.

### 东北乐器行销亚非十多个国家

1957-08-12
第5版()
专栏：

    东北乐器行销亚非十多个国家
    据新华社沈阳11日电 东北乐器厂生产的钢琴、提琴、风琴、口琴及铜管乐器等十多种“东方红”牌乐器，现在遍销东南亚、中东及非洲十多个国家。
    最近，这个工厂正为瑞典制造一种精制的钢琴。不久，这个乐器厂还将试制成功新型打琴、手风琴、钢琴、口琴等四种乐器。其中“扭式口琴”，在吹奏的时候可以随意变换音调，能吹奏出优美的半音来。
