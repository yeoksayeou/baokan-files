Title: British Flag Lowered Dismally in Penang, Malaya Prepares to Celebrate Independence
Author:
Date: 1957-08-31
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 30th dispatch from Kuala Lumpur: On the eve of the Federation of Malaya's declaration of independence, Kuala Lumpur, the nation's capital, is preparing feverishly for celebrations. The whole city is filled with a festive atmosphere.

Tonight at midnight, mosques across the country will beat drums, and huge bonfires will be lit on many mountains, marking the start of the independence celebrations.

The British Queen's representative, the Duke of Gloucester, arrived in Kuala Lumpur yesterday. He brought the British documents transferring power, which he will hand over on the 31st to Abdul Rahman, the current Chief Minister who is about to take office as the first Prime Minister of the Federation of Malaya, thereby ending British colonial rule in this country.

In Penang, the Malayan island first occupied one hundred and seventy-one years ago, the last flag-lowering ceremony of the British flag was held today.

### 英国国旗在槟榔屿黯然扯落　马来亚筹备庆祝独立

1957-08-31
第7版()
专栏：

　　英国国旗在槟榔屿黯然扯落
　　马来亚筹备庆祝独立
    据新华社30日讯　吉隆坡消息：在马来亚联合邦宣告独立前夕，这个国家的首都吉隆坡正在紧张地筹备进行庆祝。全市充满节日气氛。
    在今天的子夜，全国的清真寺将击鼓，在许多山上将燃起巨大的篝火，表明庆祝独立的开始。
    英国女王的代表格罗斯特公爵昨天已经到达吉隆坡。他带来了英国的移交政权的文件，将于31日交给就要就任马来亚联合邦第一任总理的现任首席部长阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼，从而结束英国在这个国家的殖民统治。
    在一百七十一年前最早占领的马来亚岛屿——槟榔屿，今天举行了英国国旗最后一次的降旗礼。
