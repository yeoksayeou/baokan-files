Title: Australian Peace Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-08-28
Page: 4

　　Friendly Exchanges

　　Australian Peace Delegation Arrives in Beijing

　  Xinhua News Agency, August 27th—The Australian Peace Delegation, invited by the China Peace Committee, arrived in Beijing today.

    The Australian Peace Delegation is visiting China after participating in the Third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament in Japan.

### 澳大利亚和平代表团到京

1957-08-28
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

　　澳大利亚和平代表团到京
　  据新华社27日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的澳大利亚和平代表团今天到京。
    澳大利亚和平代表团是在日本参加了第三届反对原子弹氢弹和争取裁军世界大会后来我国访问的。
