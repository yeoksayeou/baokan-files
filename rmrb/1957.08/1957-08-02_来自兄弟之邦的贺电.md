Author: 
Date: 1957-08-02

Congratulatory Messages from Fraternal Countries

Congratulatory Message from Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the People's Army of Vietnam

To Comrade Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I, on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Vietnamese People's Army, and in my own name, extend fraternal congratulations to you, Comrade Minister, and through you to the soldiers and generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

We respectfully wish the Chinese People's Liberation Army, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and the Government of the People's Republic of China, will achieve even greater new successes in building the army, consolidating national defense, and safeguarding China's socialist construction, and make significant contributions to the cause of defending peace in Asia and the world.

　　Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army, General
　　August 1, 1957, Hanoi

Congratulatory Message from Dorj, Minister of Military and Public Security of Mongolia

To Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China:

Please allow me, on behalf of the Mongolian People's Army and in my own name, to extend my most heartfelt respect to you and all the personnel of the great Chinese People's Liberation Army under your leadership, and to wish the great Chinese people and all the personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army new and great achievements in building their country's socialism, consolidating friendship among nations, and promoting peace throughout the world!

　　Dorj, Minister of Military and Public Security of the Mongolian People's Republic, Lieutenant General, Ulaanbaatar

Congratulatory Message from Bochiwofsky, Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of General Staff of the People's Army of Poland

To Comrade Su Yu, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General:

On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Comrade General, please allow me to extend my heartfelt congratulations to you.

I wish you even greater achievements in strengthening the combat readiness of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. I wish you good health.

　　Deputy Minister of National Defense of the Polish People's Republic
　　and Chief of General Staff of the Polish People's Army, General Bochiwofsky

Congratulatory Message from Bo·Lomsky, Minister of National Defense of Czechoslovakia

To Comrade Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China

Respected Comrade Minister!

On the occasion of the Army Day of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, please accept the heartfelt congratulations and fraternal combat greetings of all Czechoslovak soldiers and myself to you and all members of the Chinese armed forces.

We unanimously recognize the great army of the Chinese people, with its outstanding military achievements, as our loyal and reliable comrade-in-arms, a strong and unbreakable pillar of socialism and peace, standing together with the Soviet Army.

A special friendship closely links us with the Chinese People's Liberation Army, a friendship born from the awareness that our two fraternal armies are defending the mighty socialist camp on different borders. Our army is proud to have the glorious Chinese People's Liberation Army, shoulder to shoulder with the Soviet Army, vigilantly watching the conspiracies of the enemies of socialism and peace on the distant Far Eastern border, on the shores of the Pacific Ocean and the South China Sea.

Therefore, we Czechoslovak soldiers congratulate the Chinese People's Liberation Army on achieving further successes in improving military technology and strengthening combat power.

May the fraternal combat friendship between the Chinese and Czech armies continue to be consolidated under the leadership of the two countries' Communist Parties!

　　Bo·Lomsky, Minister of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Republic, General (signature)
　　July 22, 1957, Prague

Congratulatory Message from Leontin Salajan, Minister of Armed Forces of Romania

To Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army, on behalf of all personnel of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic and in my own name, I extend warm and heartfelt congratulations to you, and wish all personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, who faithfully and bravely defend the independence and security of their socialist motherland—the People's Republic of China, new achievements in military and political training.

　　Leontin Salajan, Minister of Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic, General
　　Bucharest

Congratulatory Message from Baluku, Minister of National Defense of Albania

To Comrade Peng Dehuai, Marshal of the People's Republic of China, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the Army Day of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, on behalf of all personnel of the Albanian People's Army and in my own name, I extend my most sincere and fraternal congratulations to you and all the heroic soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army! The Albanian People's Army respectfully wishes the armed forces of the People's China further achievements in the cause of improving and consolidating combat training to ensure the great historical victories of the fraternal Chinese people and to safeguard peace in Asia and the world!

　　Be·Baluku, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Albania, General

### 来自兄弟之邦的贺电

1957-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    来自兄弟之邦的贺电
    越南国防部长兼人民军总司令武元甲贺电
    中华人民共和国国防部长敬爱的彭德怀元帅同志：
    值此中国人民解放军建军三十周年之际，我谨代表越南人民军全体干部和战士，并以我个人的名义向您、部长同志并通过您向中国人民解放军的战士和将领同志们致以兄弟的祝贺。
    我们敬祝中国人民解放军在中国共产党和中华人民共和国政府的领导下，在建设军队、巩固国防、保卫中国的社会主义建设事业中，将日益取得更多的新的成就，并且对保卫亚洲和世界的和平事业作出重大的贡献。
      越南民主共和国国防部长兼越南人民军总司令　武元甲大将
                                      1957年8月1日于河内
    蒙古军事和公安部长道尔吉贺电
    中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅：
    请允许我代表蒙古人民军并以我个人名义向您和在您所领导下的伟大中国的人民解放军的全体人员致以最衷心的敬意，并祝伟大的中国人民和中国人民解放军的全体人员为建设本国的社会主义、为巩固各民族间的友谊和全世界的和平而获得新的巨大成就！
      蒙古人民共和国军事和公安部长道尔吉中将　乌兰巴托
    波兰国防部副部长兼人民军总参谋长博齐沃夫斯基贺电
    中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕大将同志：
    值此中国人民解放军建军三十周年之际，大将同志，请允许我向您致以衷心的祝贺。
    祝您在加强中国人民解放军战斗准备的工作中取得更大的成就。祝您身体健康。
                        波兰人民共和国国防部副部长
                        兼波兰人民军总参谋长博齐沃夫斯基将军
    捷克斯洛伐克国防部长波·洛姆斯基贺电
    中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅同志
    敬爱的部长同志！
    值此中国人民解放军建军节之际，请您和中国武装力量的全体成员接受全体捷克斯洛伐克军人和我个人的衷心的祝贺及兄弟的战斗敬礼。
    我们一致认定具有卓越战功的伟大的中国人民的军队是我们忠实可靠的战友，是同苏军站在一起的、坚强的、牢不可破的社会主义与和平的支柱。
    一种特别的友情使我们同中国人民解放军紧密地联系在一起，这种友情是由于意识到我们两国兄弟军队在不同的边界上保卫着强大的社会主义阵营。我们军队以在千里遥遥的远东边界上，在太平洋岸和南海岸上，有着光辉的中国人民的军队肩并肩地同苏军一起，警惕地注视着社会主义与和平的敌人的阴谋而感到骄傲。
    因此，我们捷克斯洛伐克的军人们祝贺中国人民解放军在提高军事技术和加强战斗威力中获得进一步的成就。
    让捷中两国军队间的兄弟的战斗友谊在两国共产党的领导下不断巩固！
        捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部长　波·洛姆斯基上将（签名）
                              1957年7月22日　布拉格
    罗马尼亚武装部队部长利昂廷·萨拉扬贺电
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理、国防部长彭德怀元帅：
    值此英勇的中国人民解放军三十周年之际，谨代表罗马尼亚人民共和国武装力量的全体人员并以我个人名义，向您致以热烈的、衷心的祝贺，祝忠实和勇敢地保卫着自己的社会主义祖国——中华人民共和国的独立和安全的中国人民解放军的全体人员，在军事和政治训练中获得新的成就。
          罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队部长　利昂廷·萨拉扬上将
                                       于布加勒斯特
    阿尔巴尼亚国防部长巴卢库贺电
    中华人民共和国国防部长中华人民共和国元帅彭德怀同志：
    值此中国人民解放军建军节之际，谨代表阿尔巴尼亚人民军全体人员并以我个人名义向您和中国人民解放军全体英勇的战士们致以最衷心和兄弟般的祝贺！阿尔巴尼亚人民军敬祝人民中国的武装力量在为保障兄弟的中国人民的伟大历史胜利、保障亚洲和全世界和平而提高和巩固战斗训练的事业中，获得进一步的成就！
           阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国防部长　贝·巴卢库上将
