Title: Indonesian Cabinet Approves Dual Nationality Treaty with China
Author:
Date: 1957-08-14
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 13th: The Indonesian Cabinet, at its meeting on the evening of August 12th, approved the China-Indonesia treaty on the question of dual nationality. However, the date for the Parliament's discussion of this treaty has not yet been announced.
    The Cabinet meeting also agreed to appoint Professor Mohammed Yamin and Tumakara as members of the National Council, the former as a representative of intellectuals, and the latter as a representative of the Sulawesi region.

### 印度尼西亚内阁批准  同中国的双重国籍条约

1957-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚内阁批准
    同中国的双重国籍条约
    新华社雅加达13日电　印度尼西亚内阁在8月12日晚上举行的会议上批准了中国—印度尼西亚关于双重国籍问题的条约。但是，国会讨论这项条约的日期还未见公布。
    内阁会议还同意委任穆罕默德·雅明教授和土马卡拉为民族委员会委员，前者作为知识分子的代表，后者作为苏拉威西地方的代表。
