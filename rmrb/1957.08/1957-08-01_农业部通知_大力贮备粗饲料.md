Title: Ministry of Agriculture Notice: Vigorously Store Coarse Fodder
Author:
Date: 1957-08-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency report: The Ministry of Agriculture issued a notice on the 29th to agricultural and animal husbandry departments at all levels across the country, urging them to seize the current favorable opportunity and vigorously store the coarse fodder needed for livestock.

The notice stated that now is the time when wild grasses are growing vigorously or heading, and all localities must mobilize the masses to start early and store more.

### 农业部通知  大力贮备粗饲料

1957-08-01
第5版()
专栏：

    农业部通知  大力贮备粗饲料
    据新华社讯　农业部29日通知全国各地各级农牧业部门抓紧目前有利时机，大力贮备牲畜所需的粗饲料。
    通知中说，现在正是野草生长旺盛或抽穗的时候，各地必须发动群众，及早动手多打多贮。
