Title: Egyptian Basketball Team to Hold Friendly Match with Our Youth Team Today
Author:
Date: 1957-08-19
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: The Egyptian men's basketball team has been practicing intensely in Beijing for the past few days, preparing for their first friendly match in China, which will be held here on the 19th. Their opponent is the Chinese Youth Men's Basketball Team.

The Egyptian basketball team will hold three friendly matches in Beijing. The other two matches are: August 21st against the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Yellow Team; August 23rd against the Beijing Team.

The Egyptian basketball team will also visit Tianjin, Shenyang, and Shanghai in the future, and plans to hold seven friendly matches with seven Chinese men's basketball teams in these cities.

### 埃及篮球队今日和我青年队举行友谊赛

1957-08-19
第3版()
专栏：体育新闻

    埃及篮球队今日和我青年队举行友谊赛
    新华社18日讯　埃及共和国男子篮球队连日来在北京紧张练习，准备迎接19日在这里举行的来中国后的第一场友谊比赛。这场比赛的对手是中国青年男子篮球队。
    埃及篮球队在北京将举行三场友谊比赛。其他两场比赛是：8月21日对中国人民解放军“八一”黄队；8月23日对北京队。
    埃及篮球队以后还将去天津、沈阳和上海访问，并准备在这些城市同中国的七个男子篮球队举行七场友谊比赛。
