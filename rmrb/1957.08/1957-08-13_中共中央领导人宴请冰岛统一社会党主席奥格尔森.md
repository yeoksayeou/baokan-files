Title: CCP Central Leaders Host Banquet for Ólafsson, Chairman of the Icelandic United Socialist Party
Author:
Date: 1957-08-13
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, 12th - Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De, Vice-Chairmen of the CCP Central Committee, and Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, received and hosted a banquet today for Ólafsson, Chairman of the Icelandic United Socialist Party, along with his wife, son, and daughter.

Comrades Peng Zhen, Wang Jiaxiang, Yang Shangkun, and others also attended the reception and banquet.

### 中共中央领导人宴请冰岛统一社会党主席奥格尔森

1957-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人宴请冰岛统一社会党主席奥格尔森
    新华社12日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德，总书记邓小平，今日接见和宴请冰岛统一社会党主席奥格尔森及其夫人、儿女。
    参加接见和宴会的，还有彭真、王稼祥、杨尚昆等同志。
