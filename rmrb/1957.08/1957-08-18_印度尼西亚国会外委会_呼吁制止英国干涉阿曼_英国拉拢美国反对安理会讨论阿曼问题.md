Title: Indonesian Parliament's Foreign Affairs Committee Calls for an End to British Interference in Oman; Britain Coaxes the United States to Oppose Security Council Discussion of the Oman Issue
Author:
Date: 1957-08-18
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 17th: The Foreign Affairs Subcommittee of the Indonesian Parliament passed a resolution at its meeting on August 15th, calling on parliaments of all countries to take action to stop British interference in Oman.

The resolution also urged the Indonesian government and Asian-African countries to work together to swiftly halt this British intervention through the United Nations and to seek a peaceful resolution to the Oman issue.

Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 17th: The British delegation to the United Nations intensified its activities on August 16th, attempting to gather as many countries as possible in the Security Council to oppose the Arab countries' demand that the Security Council take action regarding Britain's armed aggression against Oman when the Security Council meets on August 20th to discuss the Oman issue.

Britain hopes that no more than two members of the Security Council – the Soviet Union and Iraq – will vote in favor of including the Oman issue on the agenda. In this regard, Britain is currently primarily trying to get the United States to vote against it, or at least abstain, as the attitude of the United States will influence other members of the Council.

### 印度尼西亚国会外委会  呼吁制止英国干涉阿曼  英国拉拢美国反对安理会讨论阿曼问题

1957-08-18
第7版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会外委会
    呼吁制止英国干涉阿曼
    英国拉拢美国反对安理会讨论阿曼问题
    新华社雅加达17日电  印度尼西亚国会外交小组委员会在8月15日举行的会议上通过了一项决议，呼吁各国议会采取行动制止英国对阿曼的干涉。
    决议还促请印度尼西亚政府和亚非国家一道，通过联合国迅速制止英国的这一干涉行动，并且设法以和平方式解决阿曼问题。
    据新华社17日讯  纽约消息：英国驻联合国代表团在8月16日加紧活动，设法在安全理事会8月20日开会讨论阿曼问题时纠合尽可能多的国家来反对阿拉伯国家提出的关于安全理事会对英国武装侵略阿曼一事采取行动的要求。
    英国希望，在安全理事会中，投票赞成把阿曼问题列入议程的不超过两个理事国——苏联和伊拉克。在这方面，目前英国主要是争取美国投反对票，或者至少是弃权，因为美国的态度会影响其他理事国。
