Title: Czechoslovak Premier to Visit India Early Next Year
Author: 
Date: 1957-08-19
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 18th: It was announced here that Czechoslovak Premier Široký, at the invitation of the Indian government, will visit India in the first half of January next year.

### 捷总理明年初将访印度

1957-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    捷总理明年初将访印度
    新华社布拉格18日电　此间宣布，捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基应印度政府的邀请，将在明年1月上半月访问印度。
