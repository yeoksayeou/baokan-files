Title: "August 1st" Basketball Team Wins All Games in Romania
Author:
Date: 1957-08-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 30th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Basketball Team achieved complete victory in its matches in Romania.

On the evening of August 29th, the "August 1st" Basketball Team played its final match in Romania in Oradea, defeating the Romanian team with a score of 51 to 34. This Romanian team was composed of six main players from the Romanian National Youth Team and excellent players from the Oradea city team, which ranks fourth in Romania.

With this, the "August 1st" team achieved six wins in six games in Romania.

The "August 1st" team will travel to Czechoslovakia on August 31st, where it will play seven games.

### “八一”篮球队在罗比赛获全胜

1957-08-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　“八一”篮球队在罗比赛获全胜
    新华社布加勒斯特30日电　中国人民解放军“八一”篮球队在罗马尼亚的比赛获得全胜。
    8月29日晚间，“八一”篮球队在俄拉第亚市进行了在罗马尼亚的最后一场比赛，以五十一比三十四战胜罗马尼亚队。这个队是由罗马尼亚国家青年队的六名主力和占罗马尼亚全国第四位的俄拉第亚市代表队的优秀队员组成的。
    至此，“八一”队在罗马尼亚获得了六战六捷的战果。
    “八一”队将在8月31日前往捷克斯洛伐克，在那里将进行七场比赛。
