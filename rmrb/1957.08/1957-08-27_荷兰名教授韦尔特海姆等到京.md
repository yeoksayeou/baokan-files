Title: Noted Dutch Professor Wertheim and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-08-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 26th. Noted Dutch Professor Wertheim and his wife arrived in Beijing yesterday afternoon at the invitation of the Sino-Foreign Cultural Association.

Manuel Duchesne Cuzán, a Cuban music conductor who participated as an art judge at the World Youth Festival, and composer Argelier Leon Perez also arrived in Beijing yesterday afternoon at the invitation of the Sino-Foreign Cultural Association.

### 荷兰名教授韦尔特海姆等到京

1957-08-27
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    荷兰名教授韦尔特海姆等到京
    新华社26日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的荷兰名教授韦尔特海姆夫妇在昨日下午到达北京。
    参加世界青年联欢节的艺术评判员古巴音乐指挥曼努埃尔·杜切斯内·库桑、作曲家阿尔赫列尔斯·莱昂·佩雷兹应对外文化协会邀请也在昨日下午到京。
