Title: National Junior Basketball Championships Begin
Author:
Date: 1957-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The first stage of the 1957 National Junior Basketball Championships, with fifty-six junior boys' and girls' basketball teams participating, has begun. The first stage of the competition is being held in five competition zones. The competition in the Kunming competition zone, in which ten junior boys' and girls' basketball teams participated, ended on the 8th. The junior boys' and girls' championships in this competition zone were both won by the Chongqing junior boys' and girls' basketball teams.
The competitions in the other four competition zones in the first stage will begin on the 11th, and will be held in Changchun, Taiyuan, Nanjing, and Zhengzhou, respectively. They are expected to end on August 20th, after which the boys' and girls' champion teams from each competition zone will gather in Tianjin to hold a single round-robin competition (final) to compete for the 1957 junior boys' and girls' basketball team championships.

### 全国少年篮球锦标赛开始

1957-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    全国少年篮球锦标赛开始
    新华社10日讯　有五十六个少年男、女篮球队参加的1957年全国少年篮球锦标赛的第一阶段比赛已经开始。第一阶段的比赛分五个竞赛区举行。其中有十个少年男、女篮球队参加的昆明竞赛区的比赛，已在8日结束。获得这个竞赛区男、女冠军的都是重庆市少年男、女篮球队。
    第一阶段其他四个竞赛区的比赛将从11日起，分别在长春、太原、南京、郑州举行，预计在8月20日结束，然后各个竞赛区所产生的男、女冠军队都汇集到天津，举行单循环比赛（决赛），争夺1957年度的少年男、女篮球队的冠军。
