Title: Mexican Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-08-27
Page: 4

Friendly Exchanges

    Mexican Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, August 26th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Mexican National Modern Ballet Dance Troupe, led by troupe leader Elana Noriega and Guillermina Parafu, arrived in Beijing by train this morning. The dance troupe consists of thirty-eight people and will perform in China for a month and a half. (Photo attached)
　　The photo shows the Mexican National Modern Ballet Dance Troupe arriving at the Beijing Railway Station.
                                          Our reporter Cao Guijiang

### 墨西哥舞蹈团到京

1957-08-27
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    墨西哥舞蹈团到京
    据新华社26日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问演出的墨西哥民族现代芭蕾舞蹈团，在团长爱兰娜·诺爱加和吉叶尔米娜·帕拉伏率领下，今天上午乘火车到达北京。舞蹈团共三十八人，将要在中国演出一个半月。（附图片）
　　图为到达北京车站时的墨西哥民族现代芭蕾舞蹈团。
                                          本报记者　曹桂江摄
