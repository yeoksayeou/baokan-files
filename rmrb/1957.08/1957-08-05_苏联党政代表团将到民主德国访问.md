Title: Soviet Party and Government Delegation to Visit Democratic Germany
Author: 
Date: 1957-08-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 3rd: TASS reported that a Soviet Communist Party and government delegation will depart from Moscow on August 7th to visit the German Democratic Republic.

The members of the delegation are: First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev; Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Mikoyan; Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Gromyko; Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, Kumykin; Secretary of the Leningrad City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Spiridonov; Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, Grishin; Deputy Chairman of the Moscow City Executive Committee, Mironova; Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, Semichastny; and Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, G.M. Pushkin.

### 苏联党政代表团将到民主德国访问

1957-08-05
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联党政代表团
    将到民主德国访问
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科3日讯：苏联共产党和政府代表团将在8月7日从莫斯科启程前往德意志民主共和国访问。
    代表团的成员是：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫、苏联部长会议副主席米高扬、苏联外交部长葛罗米柯、苏联对外贸易部副部长库米金、苏联共产党列宁格勒市委会书记斯波里多诺夫、全苏工会中央理事会主席格里申、莫斯科市执行委员会副主席米罗诺娃、苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记谢米恰斯内伊和苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使格·马·普希金。
