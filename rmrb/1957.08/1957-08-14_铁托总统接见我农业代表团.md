Title: President Tito Receives Chinese Agricultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-08-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Brioni, August 13th - President Tito of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia received the Chinese agricultural delegation headed by Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan at the presidential villa on Vanga Island, near Brioni Island, at 4 p.m. on the 12th.

President Tito had a cordial conversation with the delegation members and hosted a luncheon for them. After the meal, the President led the entire delegation to visit the island's orchards and wine cellars, and took out vintage wine stored since 1904 to share with the guests.

President Tito spent more than two hours with the Chinese guests. When the delegation said goodbye to him, he instructed the delegation to convey his regards to Chairman Mao Zedong upon their return home.

Accompanying the delegation to meet President Tito were Komar, a member of the Yugoslav Federal Executive Council, and Wu Xiuquan, the Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia.

### 铁托总统接见我农业代表团

1957-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    铁托总统接见我农业代表团
    新华社布里俄尼岛13日电　南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托，12日下午四时在布里俄尼岛附近望科岛上的总统别墅中接见了以农业部长廖鲁言为首的中国农业代表团。
    铁托总统同代表团人员进行了亲切的谈话，并且设便宴招待他们。饭后，总统领着代表团全体人员参观了岛上的果园和酒窖，并且取出1904年储存的陈酒同客人共饮。
    铁托总统同中国客人们在一起共度过了两个多小时，当代表团向他告别的时候，他曾嘱咐代表团回国后代表他向毛泽东主席问好。
    这次陪同代表团晋见铁托总统的有南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会委员科马尔和中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权。
