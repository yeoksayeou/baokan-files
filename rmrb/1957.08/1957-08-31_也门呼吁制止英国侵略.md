Title: Yemen Calls for an End to British Aggression
Author:
Date: 1957-08-31
Page: 7

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 30th — The Yemeni Minister to Cairo appealed on the 29th to peace-loving countries to exert moral pressure on Britain, forcing it to respect Yemen's sovereignty and to stop using force to resolve outstanding issues, as this violates the United Nations Charter and threatens world peace.
　　The Yemeni Minister received a telegram from the King of Yemen on August 29 stating that Britain is intensifying its aggression against Yemen, “British infantry, supported by cannons, tanks, planes and rockets, has invaded the Yemeni territories east of the Beda and Kataba regions, and the fighting has caused heavy casualties.”

### 也门呼吁制止英国侵略

1957-08-31
第7版()
专栏：

　  也门呼吁制止英国侵略
    据新华社开罗30日电　也门驻开罗公使29日呼吁爱好和平国家对英国施加道义压力，迫使它尊重也门的主权，并且停止使用武力来解决悬而未决的问题，因为这是违反联合国宪章和威胁世界和平的。
    也门公使8月29日收到的也门国王的电报说，英国加紧对也门进行侵略，“有大炮、坦克、飞机和火箭支持的英国步兵侵入也门领土贝达和卡塔巴地区以东，战斗造成很大的伤亡。”
