Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Achieves Brilliant Victories; Over 400 French Soldiers Killed in the Second Half of July
Author:
Date: 1957-08-05
Page: 3

　　According to a Middle East News Agency dispatch from Tunis on August 3, citing a communique from the Algerian National Liberation Army, in the second half of July, 414 French soldiers and 5 French officers were killed in Algeria. The National Liberation Army also wounded 63 French soldiers and 1 French officer.

### 阿民族解放军战绩辉煌  七月下半月打死法军四百多名

1957-08-05
第3版()
专栏：

    阿民族解放军战绩辉煌
    七月下半月打死法军四百多名
    据新华社4日讯　中东通讯社8月3日的突尼斯消息援引阿尔及利亚民族解放军的公报说，在7月下半月，有四百十四名法国士兵和五名法国军官在阿尔及利亚被打死。民族解放军还打伤了六十三名法国士兵和一名法国军官。
