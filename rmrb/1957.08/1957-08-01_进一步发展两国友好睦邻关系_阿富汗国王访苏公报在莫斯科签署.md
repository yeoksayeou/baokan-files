Title: To Further Develop the Friendly and Neighborly Relations Between the Two Countries: Joint Communiqué of Afghan King's Visit to the Soviet Union Signed in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-08-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 31st—According to TASS, the Afghan King, Mohammed Zahir Shah, and the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, signed a joint communiqué in Moscow on July 30 regarding the Afghan King's visit to the Soviet Union.

The communiqué stated that the Afghan King paid a friendly visit to the Soviet Union from July 17th.

During the visit, the Afghan King held talks with Chairman Voroshilov and other leaders of the Soviet government, exchanging views on issues related to the two countries.

The communiqué pointed out that the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Afghan King expressed mutual understanding of the policies of the two countries, reaffirmed the desire of the two governments for the development and prosperity of their countries, and highly valued the common desire of the two peoples to strengthen world peace and security.

The Afghan King explained Afghanistan's policy of neutrality, which stems from the desire for peace and the development of his own country.

Both sides fully agreed that strengthening friendship among the people and nations of the world is the foundation of world peace, and declared the necessity for further international cooperation and mutual assistance among the people and nations of the world.

Both sides hope that mankind will devote all its material and moral strength to eliminating international tensions and the unease caused by weapons of mass destruction in the world. They announced their willingness to use all scientific and technological means for peaceful purposes, to ensure the improvement of the material and spiritual well-being of the people of all countries.

Considering the good results of the consistently friendly and neighborly policy pursued by the Soviet Union and Afghanistan, both sides reaffirmed their willingness to continue this friendly relationship and hoped to further develop and strengthen it, thereby promoting peace, security, and the development of international cooperation and mutual assistance.

The communiqué announced that the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union accepted the invitation of the Afghan King to pay a friendly visit to Afghanistan.

### 进一步发展两国友好睦邻关系  阿富汗国王访苏公报在莫斯科签署

1957-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    进一步发展两国友好睦邻关系
    阿富汗国王访苏公报在莫斯科签署
    新华社31日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿和苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫7月30日在莫斯科签署了关于阿富汗国王访问苏联的公报。
    公报说，阿富汗国王从7月17日起到苏联作了友好访问。
    在访问期间，阿富汗国王同伏罗希洛夫主席和苏联政府其他领导人员举行了会谈，就两国有关的问题交换了意见。
    公报指出，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席和阿富汗国王对两国的政策表示相互谅解，他们重申了两国政府对发展和繁荣自己国家的愿望，并且对两国人民要求加强世界和平和安全的共同愿望给予崇高的评价。
    阿富汗国王对阿富汗的中立政策作了解释，这种政策的出发点是爱好和平和发展自己国家的愿望。
    双方完全同意，加强世界各国人民和各民族之间的友谊是世界和平的基础，并且声言世界各国人民和各民族有必要进一步实行国际合作和互助。
    双方希望人类以全部物质和道义力量致力于消除国际关系紧张和大规模毁灭性武器在世界上所造成的不安。他们宣布，愿意把一切科学技术手段用于和平目的，用于保证增进世界各国人民的物质和精神的幸福生活条件。
    考虑到苏联和阿富汗一贯奉行的友好睦邻政策的良好结果，双方重申愿意继续这种友好关系，并且希望进一步发展和加强这种关系，从而促进和平、安全，发展国际合作和互助。
    公报宣布，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席接受了阿富汗国王关于到阿富汗作友好访问的邀请。
