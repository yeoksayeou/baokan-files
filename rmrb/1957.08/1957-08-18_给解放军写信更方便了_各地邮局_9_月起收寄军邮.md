Title: It's Now Easier to Write Letters to the People's Liberation Army; Post Offices Nationwide to Accept Military Mail Starting in September
Author:
Date: 1957-08-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. To further facilitate communication between the people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, in consultation with the General Staff Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, has decided to resume accepting registered letters, parcels, and remittances addressed to the Chinese People's Liberation Army units and their personnel at post offices nationwide, starting from September 1st. The procedures and charges for mailing will be the same as the general regulations of the post office. However, the envelope must clearly state the detailed address and unit name or code of both the recipient and the sender to facilitate handling by the post office.

### 给解放军写信更方便了  各地邮局9月起收寄军邮

1957-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

    给解放军写信更方便了
    各地邮局9月起收寄军邮
    新华社17日讯  为了进一步便利人民群众同中国人民解放军之间的通信联系，邮电部同中国人民解放军总参谋部商定，自9月1日起在全国各地邮局开始恢复收寄寄交中国人民解放军部队及其所属人员的挂号函件、包裹和汇款等特种邮件。交寄手续和收费标准跟邮局一般规定相同，但邮件封面必须写明收件人及寄件人的详细地址和单位名称或代号以便于邮局处理。
