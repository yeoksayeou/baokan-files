Title: Ho Chi Minh Visits Crimea
Author:
Date: 1957-08-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Yalta, August 24th: According to TASS, Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and his entourage arrived in Yalta, a resort town on the southern coast of Crimea, on the evening of August 22nd.

They arrived in southern USSR after visiting Romania.

On the 23rd, President Ho Chi Minh and others took a yacht trip on the Black Sea, and then visited the Artek Young Pioneer Camp, where they met with children on vacation.

On the same day, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, met with President Ho Chi Minh in Yalta and had a sincere and cordial conversation.

### 胡志明访问克里米亚

1957-08-25
第6版()
专栏：

　　胡志明访问克里米亚
    据新华社24日讯　据塔斯社雅尔塔讯：越南民主共和国主席胡志明和他的随行人员8月22日晚间来到了克里米亚南岸的休养地雅尔塔。
    他们是在访问了罗马尼亚以后来到苏联南部的。
    23日，胡志明主席等乘游艇在黑海上游览，随后又参观了阿尔台克少年先锋营，同在那里休假的儿童们会面。
    同一天，苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫在雅尔塔会见了胡志明主席，并且进行了真诚亲切的谈话。
