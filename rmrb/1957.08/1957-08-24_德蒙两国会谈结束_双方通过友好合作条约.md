Title: German-Mongolian Talks Conclude: Both Sides Adopt Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1957-08-24
Page: 6
　　
　　German-Mongolian Talks Conclude
　　Both Sides Adopt Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 23rd: According to the German News Agency, the talks between the government delegations of the German Democratic Republic and the Mongolian People's Republic concluded on August 22nd. At the final meeting, the Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation between Germany and Mongolia, the Agreement on Cultural and Scientific Cooperation between Germany and Mongolia, and the Communiqué on the visit of the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic to the German Democratic Republic, which were unanimously adopted by both sides, were signed on the afternoon of the same day by Prime Ministers Grotewohl and Tsedenbal, representing their respective governments.
    After the signing, both Prime Ministers unanimously emphasized that the signing of these treaties and agreements would not only help to further consolidate and develop the friendship and cooperation between the German and Mongolian peoples, but would also further strengthen the strength and unity of the entire socialist camp.
    The talks between the two government delegations began on August 19th. The two sides held discussions in a very friendly atmosphere on major current international issues and many issues related to the two countries.

### 德蒙两国会谈结束  双方通过友好合作条约

1957-08-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　德蒙两国会谈结束
　  双方通过友好合作条约
    据新华社柏林23日电　据德意志通讯社报道，德意志民主共和国和蒙古人民共和国两国政府代表团的会谈已经在8月22日结束。在末次会议上双方一致通过的德蒙友好合作条约、德蒙文化科学合作协定和蒙古人民共和国政府代表团访问民主德国公报等三个文件，已经在当天下午由格罗提渥和泽登巴尔两位总理分别代表两国政府签字。
    两位总理在签字之后一致强调指出，这些条约和协定的签订不但有助于进一步巩固和发展德蒙人民间的友谊和合作，而且也将进一步加强整个社会主义阵营的力量和团结。
    两国政府代表团的会谈是从8月19日开始的。双方在十分友好的气氛里对当前的重大国际问题和同两国有关的许多问题进行了讨论。
