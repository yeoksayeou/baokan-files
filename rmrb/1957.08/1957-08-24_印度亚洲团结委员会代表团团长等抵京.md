Title: Head of Indian Asian Solidarity Committee Delegation Arrives in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1957-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, head of the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee delegation, Chairman of the Asian Solidarity Committee, and Chairman of the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee, and delegation members Mrs. Chaudhari and Mrs. Ghosh, who were invited by the Chinese Asian Solidarity Committee to visit China, arrived in Beijing today by plane from Guangzhou after attending the recent World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament in Tokyo. Other members of the delegation will arrive in Beijing in the coming days.

Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Asian Solidarity Committee, Chen Hansheng, Deputy Secretary-General, and Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, were among those who welcomed them at the airport.

Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 印度亚洲团结委员会代表团团长等抵京

1957-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度亚洲团结委员会代表团团长等抵京
    新华社23日讯　应中国亚洲团结委员会邀请来我国参观访问的印度亚洲团结委员会代表团团长、亚洲团结委员会主席、印度亚洲团结委员会主席拉·尼赫鲁夫人和代表团团员乔达里夫人、高士夫人，在参加最近在东京举行的禁止原子弹、氢弹和争取裁军世界大会后，今天由广州乘飞机到达北京。其他团员将于日内抵京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国亚洲团结委员会副主席李德全、副秘书长陈翰笙和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长吴茂荪等人。
    前往机场欢迎的还有印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁。
