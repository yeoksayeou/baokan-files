　　Gazing at the Ocean and Sighing
　　Fang Cheng
    Zhang Naiqi opposed the state's decree prohibiting the export of cultural relics. He said: "A ding can sell for seven or eight thousand US dollars in the United States, which gives me some comfort... I alone have more than twenty dings."
