Title: Tunisian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-08-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 12th—The Tunisian Cultural Delegation, invited by the Chinese People’s Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries for their first visit to China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

    The head of the delegation is Guiga, a professor of literature and chairman of the National Education Trade Union. The members include Khalifa, a professor of geography; Salah, a professor of philosophy; Djaïtly, head of the National Theater; Driss, a museum director; Bellagha, a painter and potter; Turki, an oil painter; and Triri, a representative of the Tunisian-Chinese Association.

    Xiao Xiangping and others, the vice-chairman of the National Committee of the Educational Workers Union of China, went to the airport to welcome them.

### 突尼斯文化代表团到京

1957-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    突尼斯文化代表团到京
    据新华社12日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请第一次来中国访问的突尼斯文化代表团一行八人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团团长是文学教授、全国教育工会主席古伊加。团员有地理教授哈勒法兰，哲学教授萨拉，国家剧团负责人迪亚齐里，博物馆长德里斯，画家、陶器家贝拉加，油画家图尔基，突中协会代表特里塔。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国教育工会全国委员会副主席萧项平等。
