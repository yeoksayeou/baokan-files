Title: Australian Building Industry Union Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 21st: The Australian Building Industry Union delegation, led by Hancock, Vice General Secretary of the Australian Builders Labourers' Federation, arrived in Guangzhou from Shenzhen this afternoon. Zhang Jin, Vice Chairman of the China Building Workers' Union, traveled from Beijing to Guangzhou to greet the guests.

### 澳大利亚建筑业工会代表团到广州

1957-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

　  澳大利亚建筑业工会代表团到广州
    据新华社广州21日电　以澳大利亚建筑工人产业工会副总书记汉柯克为首的澳大利亚建筑业工会代表团一行四人，今日下午自深圳到达广州。中国建筑工会副主席张进从北京赶到广州迎接客人。
