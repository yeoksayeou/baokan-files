Title: Preferential Measures for Overseas Chinese Investing in State-Owned Overseas Chinese Investment Companies
Author:
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 3

Order of the State Council

The "Preferential Measures for Overseas Chinese Investing in State-Owned Overseas Chinese Investment Companies," having been approved by the 78th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on August 1, 1957, are hereby promulgated.

                        Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, August 2, 1957

Preferential Measures for Overseas Chinese Investing in State-Owned Overseas Chinese Investment Companies

(Approved at the 78th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on August 1, 1957)

Article 1: These measures are formulated to meet the aspirations of overseas Chinese to invest and participate in the construction of the motherland and to protect the interests of overseas Chinese investments.

Article 2: Overseas Chinese who remit funds to invest in state-owned Overseas Chinese Investment Companies shall enjoy the following preferential treatment:

	(1) The shares of overseas Chinese invested in state-owned Overseas Chinese Investment Companies shall remain the property of the investors even after the completion of socialist construction. Investors who have invested for a full twelve years may recover their shares, which will be paid in Renminbi.

	(2) The dividend on overseas Chinese investment in state-owned Overseas Chinese Investment Companies is set at an annual interest rate of eight percent, payable in Renminbi.

	(3) Dividends obtained by overseas Chinese from investments in state-owned Overseas Chinese Investment Companies may be remitted abroad with the approval of the foreign exchange control authorities, but shall not exceed 50% of the current year's dividend.

	(4) Investors in state-owned Overseas Chinese Investment Companies who request employment may be given priority for employment based on the needs of the relevant enterprises of the company and the specific circumstances of the investors.

Article 3: These measures also apply to compatriots in Hong Kong and Macao who remit funds to invest in state-owned Overseas Chinese Investment Companies.

Article 4: These measures shall come into effect from the date of promulgation.

### 华侨投资于国营华侨投资公司的优待办法

1957-08-03
第3版()
专栏：

    国务院命令
    华侨投资于国营华侨投资公司的优待办法，业经全国人民代表大会常务委员会于1957年8月1日第七十八次会议批准，现在予以公布。
                        国务院总理　周恩来　1957年8月2日
    华侨投资于国营华侨投资公司的优待办法
    （一九五七年八月一日全国人民代表大会常务
    委员会第七十八次会议批准） 
    第一条　为了适应华侨投资参加祖国建设的愿望和保障华侨投资的利益，制定本办法。
    第二条　华侨汇款投资于国营华侨投资公司，可以享受下列优待：
    （1）华侨投资于国营华侨投资公司的股金，到社会主义建成后，仍为投资人所有。凡投资满十二年的，可以收回股金，以人民币支付。
    （2）华侨投资于国营华侨投资公司的股息定为年息八厘，以人民币支付。
    （3）华侨投资于国营华侨投资公司所得的股息，经过外汇主管机关批准后，可以汇往国外，但是不得超过本年股息的50％。
    （4）投资于国营华侨投资公司的投资人，如果要求工作，可以根据公司有关企业的需要和投资人的具体情况，优先录用。
    第三条　本办法也适用于港澳同胞汇款投资于国营华侨投资公司。
    第四条　本办法自公布日起施行。
