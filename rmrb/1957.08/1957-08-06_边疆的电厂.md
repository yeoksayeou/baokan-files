Title: Border Region Power Plant
Author:
Date: 1957-08-06
Page: 5

    Border Region Power Plant
    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 3rd - The largest thermal power plant in the Yunnan border region began transmitting electricity on the 1st.
    This newly built power plant is located in Mangshi, the seat of the People's Committee of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture in Yunnan Province. This power plant can not only supply electricity for lighting in the urban area and nearby rural areas, but also meet the production electricity needs of newly built new-style farm tool factories, vermicelli factories, printing factories, and other industrial and mining enterprises.
    Construction of the Mangshi Thermal Power Plant began in early last year and has a power generation capacity of 240 kilowatts.

### 边疆的电厂

1957-08-06
第5版()
专栏：

    边疆的电厂
    新华社昆明3日电　云南边疆一座规模最大的火力发电厂在1日开始送电。
    这座新建的发电厂在云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治州人民委员会所在地芒市。这个电厂不但能供应市区和附近农村的照明用电，并可满足新建的新式农具厂、粉丝厂、印刷厂等工矿的生产用电。
    芒市火力发电厂是去年初动工兴建的，发电量为二百四十瓩。
