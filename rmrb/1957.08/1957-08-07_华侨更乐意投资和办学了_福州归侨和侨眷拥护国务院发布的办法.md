Title: Overseas Chinese are More Willing to Invest and Establish Schools: Returned Overseas Chinese and Relatives of Overseas Chinese in Fuzhou Support the Measures Issued by the State Council
Author:
Date: 1957-08-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 6th - More than 30 representatives of returned overseas Chinese and relatives of overseas Chinese in Fuzhou held a forum on the 5th to discuss the "Measures for Overseas Chinese to Donate Funds to Establish Schools" and the "Preferential Measures for Overseas Chinese to Invest in State-Owned Overseas Chinese Investment Companies" promulgated by the State Council. At the meeting, everyone believed that these two measures conformed to the wishes of the vast number of overseas Chinese and compatriots in Hong Kong and Macao to enthusiastically participate in the construction of the motherland and their hometowns, and further protected the interests of overseas Chinese. They expressed their intention to repay the care and consideration of the People's Government with practical actions.
    Returned overseas Chinese, Ye Hongbao, said that the measures for overseas Chinese to donate funds to establish schools are reasonable and thoughtful. He believes that these measures can further stimulate the enthusiasm of overseas Chinese in running schools.
    Returned overseas Chinese, Guo Hongzhong, said that the preferential measures for overseas Chinese to invest in state-owned overseas Chinese investment companies promulgated by the State Council further demonstrate the government's meticulous care for overseas Chinese. It is beneficial to both the country and themselves for overseas Chinese to invest in the construction of the motherland.

### 华侨更乐意投资和办学了  福州归侨和侨眷拥护国务院发布的办法

1957-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    华侨更乐意投资和办学了
    福州归侨和侨眷拥护国务院发布的办法
    据新华社福州6日电　福州市三十多名归国华侨和侨眷代表5日举行座谈会，座谈国务院公布的“华侨捐资兴办学校办法”和“华侨投资于国营华侨投资公司的优待办法”。会上大家认为这两个办法符合广大华侨和港澳同胞热心参加祖国和家乡建设的愿望，进一步保障了华侨的利益。他们表示要以实际行动来答谢人民政府的关怀和照顾。
    归国华侨叶鸿宝说，华侨捐资兴办学校的办法定得合理而周到，相信这个办法能进一步激发华侨办学的积极性。
    归国华侨郭鸿忠说，国务院公布的华侨投资于国营华侨投资公司的优待办法，更加表明了政府对华侨的细心照顾。华侨投资祖国建设对国家对自己都有好处。
