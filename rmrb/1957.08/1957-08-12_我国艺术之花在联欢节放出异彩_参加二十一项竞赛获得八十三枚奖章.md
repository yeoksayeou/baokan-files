Title: The Flowers of Chinese Art Shine with Splendor at the Festival; Participating in Twenty-One Competitions and Winning Eighty-Three Medals
Author:
Date: 1957-08-12
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th: Chinese young artists shone with brilliant splendor on the stage of the festival, winning a total of twenty-seven gold medals, twenty-three silver medals, and sixteen bronze medals in the festival's art competition for group and individual programs. As some programs had several winners of medals, a total of thirty-four gold medals, thirty-one silver medals, and eighteen bronze medals were awarded.

　　In the twenty-one art competitions of the festival, China participated in fourteen items including mime, oriental classical dance, folk dance, oriental classical songs, classical songs, folk songs, popular songs, amateur chorus, national musical instrument performance, piano performance, wind instrument performance, string instrument performance, musical works, and acrobatics, with a total of eighty-eight programs.

　　The gold medal winners are:

　　One mime: Du Jinfang's "Picking up the Jade Bracelet";

　　Two oriental classical dances: Guan Sushuang and Zhang Yunbin's "Da Jiao Zan", and Li Xiaochun and twelve others' "Ne Zha Stirs up the Sea";

　　Three folk dances: Li Changsuo and others' "Dragon Dance", Zhang Yi and ten others' "Flower Drum Dance", and Cui Meishan and others' "Peacock Dance";

　　Two oriental classical songs: Hong Xiannu, Du Jinfang;

　　One classical song: Guo Shuzhen;

　　Four folk songs: Ma Yutao, Ju Xiufang, Li Yueqiu, Wei Xikui;

　　One amateur chorus: Beijing Amateur Youth Folk Song Chorus;

　　Eight national musical instrument performances: Peng Xiuwen and others' national orchestra, Hu Tianquan and others' folk wind instrument band, Lin Yunxi, Dong Jun and others' Chaozhou band, Tang Yubin, Lei Yusheng and Yin Qiying's trio, Hu Tianquan's sheng solo, Liu Mingyuan's banhu, Zhang Changcheng's banhu, Wang Fandi's pipa;

　　One piano performance: Gu Shengying;

　　One musical work: "Distant Guest, Please Stay Behind" arranged by Mai Ding;

　　Three acrobatics: Xia Juhua's "Bowl Balancing", Qiao Pinghai's "Plate Spinning", and Jin Yeqin and Jin Shuqin's "Vehicle Skills".

　　The silver medal winners are: One mime: "Fang Pei";

　　Two oriental classical dances: Du Jinfang and Yang Mingqing's "Chang'e Flying to the Moon", Liu Cuiyu, Ling Yun and others' "Weaving Maidens Embroidering Flowers";

　　Six folk dances: Yu Ying, Jia Shiming and others' "Flower Drum Lantern", Zhuo Wenrui and others' "Spring Comes to the Tea Mountain", Zohara and others' "Plate Dance", Mao Xiang and Bai Wenfen's "Double Peacock Dance", Zohara's "Hand Drum Dance", Amina's "Melon Planting Dance";

　　Three oriental classical songs: Li Shiji, Ma Changli, Hao Qinghai;

　　Two classical songs: Wen Kezheng, Zhang Lijuan;

　　One popular song: Fang Chushan;

　　Three musical works: Zheng Zhenyu's "Daughter's Song", Zhang Junqing's "Ah, How Happy!", Zheng Zhenyu's "Changbai Mountain Song" (choral piece);

　　Five acrobatics: Wang Guiying and Wang Shuying's "Chinese Yo-yo", Wang Qingyuan's "Flying Fork", Wang Qingyuan, Jin Yeqin and Zhang Hua's "Interlude Comedy", Meng Lingkuan's "Jars", Wei Guoyou, Zhang Jieming and Ren Fuzeng's "Leather Whip".

　　The bronze medal winners are:

　　One oriental classical dance: Shu Qiao and eight others' "Sword Dance";

　　Three folk dances: Ou Mi Jiacang and sixteen others' "Reba on the Grassland", Huhegerile and six others' "Milkmaid", Lu Yisheng and Wang Peiying's "Shepherd's Flute";

　　One classical song: Liang Meizhen;

　　Two wind instrument performances: Chao Xuan Guang's French horn, Liu Zhigang's bassoon;

　　Two string instrument performances: Yang Bingxun's violin, Wang Lei's cello;

　　Four musical works: Shi Yongkang's symphonic poem "The Story of the Yellow Crane", Liu Shouyi and Yang Jiwu's suona concerto "Celebrating Victory", Jiang Zuxin's piano solo suite "Temple Fair", Sang Tong's piano piece "East Mongolian Folk Song Suite";

　　Three acrobatics: Cheng Xiaolin, Wang Guiying and Wang Shuying's "Magic", Wei Guoyou and others' "Pole Climbing", Zhang Hua, Xia Yingwu and Xia Yingxia's "Shaking Board".

　　In addition, some programs received encouragement awards.

### 我国艺术之花在联欢节放出异彩  参加二十一项竞赛获得八十三枚奖章

1957-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

    我国艺术之花在联欢节放出异彩
    参加二十一项竞赛获得八十三枚奖章
    新华社莫斯科11日电　中国青年艺术家在联欢节舞台上放出了灿烂的异彩，共获得了联欢节艺术竞赛集体节目和个人节目金奖二十七个、银奖二十三个、铜奖十六个。由于有些节目有几人获奖章，因此共获得金质奖章三十四枚、银质奖章三十一枚、铜质奖章十八枚。
    在联欢节的二十一项艺术竞赛中，中国共参加了哑剧、东方古典舞蹈、民间舞、东方古典歌曲、古典歌曲、民歌、流行歌曲、业余合唱、民族乐器演奏、钢琴演奏、管乐器演奏、弦乐器演奏、音乐作品和杂技等十四项，参加节目共有八十八个。
    获金奖的有：
    哑剧一个：杜近芳的“拾玉镯”；
    东方古典舞蹈二个：关肃霜和张韵斌的“打焦赞”、李小春等十二人的“哪吒闹海”；
