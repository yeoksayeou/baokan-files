Brief News

    Saudi Ambassador to Syria Returns to Damascus
    Damascus dispatch: The Saudi Arabian Ambassador to Syria, Abdul Aziz Said, returned to Damascus on July 30th. He left in June when relations between Syria and Saudi Arabia were tense. His return to Damascus is seen as confirmation that the tension between the two countries has ended.

    Yemen and Ethiopia Sign Aviation Agreement
    Addis Ababa dispatch: Yemen and Ethiopia signed an aviation agreement in Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia, on July 30th. According to the agreement, Yemen will allow Ethiopian Airlines flights to fly into Yemen.

    U.S. to Export Radioactive Isotopes to the Soviet Union
    Washington dispatch: The U.S. Atomic Energy Commission revealed on July 30th that it has approved the shipment of a batch of radioactive isotopes to the Soviet Union for research on cancer treatment.

    Italian Lower House Approves Western European Common Market Treaty
    Rome dispatch: The Italian Chamber of Deputies approved the Western European "Common Market" and "Atomic Energy Community" treaties on the evening of July 30th with 311 votes in favor, 144 votes against, and 54 abstentions. All members of the Italian Communist Party voted against, and Socialist Party members abstained because they disagreed with certain clauses of the "Common Market" treaty.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 简讯

1957-08-01
第7版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    沙驻叙大使返抵大马士革
    大马士革消息：沙特阿拉伯驻叙利亚大使阿卜杜勒·阿齐兹·赛义德已经在7月30日返回大马士革。他是在6月间叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯两国关系紧张的时候离开的。他重新回到大马士革这件事，被认为已证实这两个国家之间的紧张关系已告结束。
    也门埃塞俄比亚签航空协定
    亚的斯亚贝巴消息：也门和埃塞俄比亚7月30日在埃塞俄比亚首都亚的斯亚贝巴签订了一项航空协定，根据这个协定，也门准许埃塞俄比亚的航空公司的班机飞入也门。
    美将向苏联输出放射性同位素
    华盛顿消息：美国原子能委员会7月30日透露，它已经批准向苏联运送一批放射性同位素，供研究治疗癌症使用。
    意下院通过西欧共同市场条约
    罗马消息：意大利下院7月30日晚间以三百一十一票对一百四十四票（五十四票弃权）批准西欧“共同市场”和“原子能联营”条约。意大利共产党议员全部投反对票，社会党议员由于不同意“共同市场”条约的某些条款而弃权。
                      （新华社）
