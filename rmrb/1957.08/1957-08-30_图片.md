Title: Picture

Author: 
Date: 1957-08-30
Page: 6

On the evening of the closing ceremony of the Third World Conference against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament (August 16th), workers in Tokyo's Bunkyo Ward held a demonstration demanding the speedy implementation of the tasks assigned by the conference. The picture shows printing workers at the forefront of the demonstration.
