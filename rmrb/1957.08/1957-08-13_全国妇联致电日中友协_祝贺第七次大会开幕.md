Title: ACWF Sends Telegram to Japan-China Friendship Association Congratulating the Opening of the Seventh Congress
Author:
Date: 1957-08-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 12th. The All-China Women's Federation (ACWF) today sent a telegram to the Japan-China Friendship Association's Seventh National Congress, expressing congratulations.

The telegram said, "Since its establishment, your association has made beneficial contributions to promoting friendship between the peoples and women of China and Japan. We hereby express our heartfelt gratitude and hope that your association will achieve greater success in strengthening economic and cultural exchanges between China and Japan and in promoting the normalization of relations between the two countries in the future."

### 全国妇联致电日中友协  祝贺第七次大会开幕

1957-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    全国妇联致电日中友协
    祝贺第七次大会开幕
    新华社12日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会今天致电日本日中友好协会第七次全国大会，表示祝贺。
    电报说，“贵会自从成立以来，对促进中日两国人民和妇女友好曾作出有益的贡献，我们在此谨表示衷心感谢，并望贵会今后在加强中日两国的经济文化交流，在促进两国邦交正常化的努力中取得更大的成就。”
