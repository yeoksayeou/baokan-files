Title: The Anti-Rightist Struggle Determines the Direction of China's Historical Development: Peng Zhen Gives Report to Professors and Students of Universities in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1957-08-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 5th. The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party held a report meeting tonight for professors and students of universities in the capital. Comrade Peng Zhen gave a report at the meeting, pointing out that the anti-Rightist struggle is a struggle that determines the direction of China's historical development. He called on intellectuals to arm themselves with Marxism-Leninism, closely integrate with the working class, and wholeheartedly serve socialism.

### 反右派斗争决定我国历史发展方向  彭真向首都大专学校教授和学生作报告

1957-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    反右派斗争决定我国历史发展方向
    彭真向首都大专学校教授和学生作报告
    据新华社5日讯　中共北京市委今天晚上召开了首都大专学校教授、学生的报告会。彭真同志在会上作了报告，指出反右派斗争是决定我国历史发展方向的斗争。他号召知识分子用马克思列宁主义武装起来，和工人阶级紧密结合，全心全意为社会主义服务。
