Title: Li Dequan, Liu Ningyi, Liao Chengzhi, and Others Host Banquet Welcoming the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1957-08-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 26th: Responsible persons from the Chinese Asian Solidarity Committee hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee delegation to China, led by Mrs. R. Nehru, Chairwoman of the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee, and including members: K. Khadikar, Member of Parliament; Ms. S. Choudhury, Professor of Medicine; Dr. D.L. Rao; N.J. Naidu, Entrepreneur; R.K. Agrawal, Secretary-General of the Delhi Branch of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association; Mrs. Ghosh, Secretary of the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee; Mrs. L.B. Khadikar, Child Educator; Mrs. M.L. Mehta, Social Worker; Dr. R.C. Adhikari; and Mrs. V. Tangaonkar, Secretary of the Bombay Branch of the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee.
    Attending the banquet were Li Dequan, Liu Ningyi, and Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairmen of the Chinese Asian Solidarity Committee, as well as responsible persons from relevant people's organizations, including Cai Tingkai, Xu Baoju, Zhao Puchu, Chen Hansheng, Fang Shishan, and Yang Shuo.

### 李德全、刘宁一、廖承志等设宴欢迎　印度亚洲团结委员会访华代表团

1957-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

　  李德全、刘宁一、廖承志等设宴欢迎
　　印度亚洲团结委员会访华代表团
    新华社26日讯　中国亚洲团结委员会负责人今晚设宴欢迎印度亚洲团结委员会访华代表团团长、印度亚洲团结委员会主席拉·尼赫鲁夫人和团员：国会议员卡·卡勒尔卡、医学教授苏·乔达里女士、医生杜·拉·拉奥、企业家纳·贾·奈都、印中友协德里分会秘书长拉·克·阿格瓦尔、印度亚洲团结委员会秘书高士夫人、儿童教育家勒·巴·卡列尔卡夫人、社会工作者曼·拉·梅达夫人、医生拉·钱·阿地卡利、印度亚洲团结委员会孟买分会秘书维·坦戈安卡尔夫人。
    出席宴会的有中国亚洲团结委员会副主席李德全、刘宁一、廖承志以及有关各人民团体负责人蔡廷锴、许宝驹、赵朴初、陈翰笙、方石珊、杨朔等人。
