Title: East German Textile Exports Surge
Author:
Date: 1957-08-29
Page: 6

　　East German Textile Exports Surge
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 28th. According to a report by the German News Agency on the 27th, East German Minister of Light Industry, Mr. Fredmann, said that since 1955, East German textile exports have doubled. Textile exports include rayon, synthetic fibers, and knitwear.

### 民主德国纺织品出口激增

1957-08-29
第6版()
专栏：

　　民主德国纺织品出口激增
    据新华社柏林28日电　据德意志通讯社27日消息：民主德国轻工业部部长费德曼说：1955年以来，民主德国纺织品的出口数额已经增加到两倍。纺织品出口货中包括人造丝、合成纤维和针织品等。
