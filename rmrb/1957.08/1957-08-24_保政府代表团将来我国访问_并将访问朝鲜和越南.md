Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation to Visit Our Country, Also to Visit North Korea and Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1957-08-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, August 23rd: Bulgarian newspapers today published news that the Bulgarian government delegation, at the invitation of the governments of China, North Korea, and Vietnam, will visit these countries in September and October of this year.
    The delegation will be led by the Chairman of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers, Yugov.

### 保政府代表团将来我国访问  并将访问朝鲜和越南

1957-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　保政府代表团将来我国访问
    并将访问朝鲜和越南
    新华社索非亚23日电　保加利亚各报今天发表消息说，保加利亚政府代表团应中国、朝鲜和越南三国政府的邀请，将在今年9、10月间前往这些国家访问。
    代表团将由保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫率领。
