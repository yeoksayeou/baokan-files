Title: The Fruit of Close Cooperation Between the Soviet Union and Romania: Romania's First Nuclear Reactor Goes Into Production
Author:
Date: 1957-08-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 12th: Romania's first nuclear reactor, which began test operations at the end of July this year, officially went into production on August 10th, reaching its designed capacity of 2,000 kilowatts.

This reactor was built in cooperation with the Soviet Union. With the reactor going into production, Romania will train a group of specialized personnel to apply atomic energy in the national economy and cultivate a group of personnel engaged in nuclear physics research.

### 苏罗两国亲密合作的结晶  罗第一座原子反应堆投入生产

1957-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    苏罗两国亲密合作的结晶
    罗第一座原子反应堆投入生产
    新华社布加勒斯特12日电　在今年7月底开始试运转的罗马尼亚第一座原子反应堆，8月10日已经正式投入生产，达到了预计的两千瓩的设计能力。
    这个反应堆是在同苏联的合作下建成的。随着反应堆的投入生产，罗马尼亚将训练出一批在国民经济中应用原子能的专门干部，并且培养出一批从事原子核物理研究的工作人员。
