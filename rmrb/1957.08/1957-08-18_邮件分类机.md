Title: Mail Sorting Machine
Author:
Date: 1957-08-18
Page: 7

    Mail Sorting Machine

    The Netherlands has invented a mail sorting machine. When a bag of mail is poured into this machine, it can automatically place each piece of mail in the correct location and stamp the postage. This machine can sort mail for three to four hundred different regions. The work of identifying printed addresses is achieved through optical and electronic systems.

### 邮件分类机

1957-08-18
第7版()
专栏：花絮

    邮件分类机
    荷兰发明了一部邮件分类机。把一袋邮件倒入这部机器里，它能自动地把每件邮件放在正确的位置上，在邮票上盖上戳。这部机器可以分出三百到四百个不同地区的邮件。识别印出来的地址的工作是通过光学和电子系统来实现的。
