Title: Thai Socialist United Front Suggests Government Shed Western Shackles
Author:
Date: 1957-08-15
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, August 14th, Bangkok dispatch: The Thai Socialist United Front submitted a five-point suggestion to Thai Prime Minister Pibul Songkram on August 8th, advocating that the Thai government should break free from all unequal treaty constraints imposed on Thailand by Western countries; abolish all monopolistic privileges of foreigners in Thai commerce; abolish "embargoes" and engage in free trade with all countries, regardless of their camp; accept unconditional economic aid from all countries; and implement a neutral foreign policy that adheres to peaceful coexistence.
    The suggestions stated that the various constraints imposed on Thailand by Western countries have placed Thailand in a position of economic oppression and exploitation, and have hindered Thailand's economic development.

### 泰国社会主义联合阵线建议政府摆脱西方束缚

1957-08-15
第7版()
专栏：

    泰国社会主义联合阵线建议政府摆脱西方束缚
    新华社14日讯　曼谷消息：泰国社会主义联合阵线8月8日在致泰国总理銮披汶的一项意见书中提出五项建议，主张泰国政府摆脱西方国家对泰国施加的一切不平等条约的束缚；废除外国对泰国商业的一切垄断特权；取消“禁运”，不分阵营地同各国展开自由贸易；接受各国的不附条件的经济援助；执行遵循和平共处的中立外交政策。
    意见书说，西方国家对泰国所施加的种种束缚，使泰国在经济上处于被压迫和被剥削的地位，并且阻碍了泰国经济的发展。
