Title: Soviet Union and Afghanistan Strengthen Cooperation; Foreign Ministers Meet and Sign Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st: According to a TASS report on the 30th, Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Naim met with Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko in Moscow in July to exchange views on issues of mutual concern and decided to further develop cooperation between the two countries.

    During the negotiations, the Soviet government agreed to provide Afghanistan with technical and material assistance for oil exploration in northern Afghanistan, without any political or other similar conditions attached. The Soviet government also promised to help Afghanistan train national technical personnel for economic development.

    The two sides reached a principled agreement on the signing of a treaty between the two governments on detailed regulations for the border between the two countries. The two sides will appoint relevant representatives to hold talks on this issue in the near future. The two sides also agreed to cooperate in developing the border waterways of the two countries.

### 苏联阿富汗加强合作  两国外长会谈并签订协定

1957-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联阿富汗加强合作
    两国外长会谈并签订协定
    据新华社1日讯　据塔斯社30日讯：今年7月间阿富汗副首相兼外交大臣纳伊姆同苏联外交部长葛罗米柯在莫斯科就双方有关的问题交换了意见，并且决定进一步发展两国的合作。
    在谈判中，苏联政府同意为在阿富汗北部进行石油勘探给予阿富汗以技术和物质援助，这种援助不附有任何政治性或其他类似的条件。苏联政府还答应帮助阿富汗为开发经济训练国家技术人员。
    双方就两国政府签订关于两国国界详细规定的条约问题取得了原则上的协议。双方将任命有关的代表在最近的将来就这个问题举行会谈。双方还同意将进行合作来开发两国的边境水道。
