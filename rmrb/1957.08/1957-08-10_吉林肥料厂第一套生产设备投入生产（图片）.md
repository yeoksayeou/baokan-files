Author: Liu Entai
Date: 1957-08-10
Page: 5

Jilin Fertilizer Plant's First Set of Production Equipment Put Into Operation
    Below: A panoramic view of Jilin Fertilizer Plant, one of the key projects of China's First Five-Year Plan.
    Right: Soviet experts, assisting our country in the construction of Jilin Fertilizer Plant, are working selflessly. The photo shows Soviet experts Pavlovets (left 1), Mikhailovich (left 3) and the director of the nitric acid workshop inspecting the production situation on the tall acid-base absorption tower. Behind them is the 62-meter-high granulation tower, where granular chemical fertilizer is produced.
