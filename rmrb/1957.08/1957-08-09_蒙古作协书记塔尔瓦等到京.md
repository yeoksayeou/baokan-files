Title: Mongolian Writers Association Secretary Tarva and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-08-09
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, August 8th. Mongolian writer Tarva, Secretary of the Mongolian Writers Association and editor of the Mongolian "Literary Gazette," and poet Bulevdaorji of the "Spark" magazine, arrived in Beijing by train on the 7th for a visit to China under the "1957 Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Plan."

### 蒙古作协书记塔尔瓦等到京

1957-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古作协书记塔尔瓦等到京
    新华社8日讯　根据“中蒙1957年文化合作计划”前来中国访问的蒙古作家塔尔瓦和诗人布列夫道尔吉，于7日乘火车到京。塔尔瓦是蒙古作家协会书记、蒙古“文学报”编辑；布列夫道尔吉是“星火”杂志社干事。
