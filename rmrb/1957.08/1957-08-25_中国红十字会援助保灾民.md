Title: Chinese Red Cross Aids Bulgarian Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1957-08-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: The Chinese Red Cross Society announced today that the four states of Plovdiv, Bulgaria, etc., suffered flooding at the end of June due to continuous heavy rains. The Bulgarian Red Cross sent a telegram to the Chinese Red Cross Society requesting assistance. The Chinese Red Cross Society immediately sent a telegram in reply expressing condolences, and donated a total of 100,000 yuan in Renminbi to the Bulgarian Red Cross Society in two installments at the end of July and mid-August to help the people of Bulgaria who were unfortunately affected by the floods. After receiving the donation, the Bulgarian Red Cross Society sent a telegram thanking them, saying: "Your condolences and great relief have further strengthened the friendship between Bulgaria and China."

### 中国红十字会援助保灾民

1957-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国红十字会援助保灾民
    新华社24日讯　中国红十字会今天宣布，保加利亚普罗夫迪夫等四个州在6月底由于连下暴雨，发生水灾。保加利亚红十字会打电报给中国红十字会要求援助。中国红十字会立即回电表示慰问，并且在7月底和8月中旬先后两次共捐给保加利亚红十字会人民币十万元，帮助保加利亚不幸遭受水灾的人民。保加利亚红十字会在收到捐款后来电感谢说：“你们的慰问和伟大的救济更进一步地加强了保中两国的友谊”。
