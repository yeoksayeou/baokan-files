Title: Abundant Fruit Harvest
Author:
Date: 1957-08-21
Page: 5

Fruitful Harvest

　　This newspaper reports that in Liaoning, Shandong, Guangdong, Zhejiang, Fujian, Shanxi, and other provinces, the fruit trees in many areas are showing a scene of abundant harvest.

　　Judging from the latest situation in the major producing areas, the output of citrus, apples, pears, etc. this year will be higher than last year. In terms of citrus: Zhejiang estimates an increase of more than 20% compared with last year, and Fujian about 10%; Guangdong can also increase by about 10%. In terms of apples: Liaoning Province, which accounts for 80% of the country's apple production, will increase by 10% compared with last year; Shandong will increase even more, about 70% more than last year. The output of pears will also increase compared with last year, with Liaoning increasing by about 40% compared with last year. Although this year is a "small year" for pear trees in Shanxi Province, the output may be close to the level of last year's "big year."

### 果品丰收

1957-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

　  果品丰收
    本报讯　辽宁、山东、广东、浙江、福建、山西等省，不少地区的果树，呈现出一片丰收的景象。
    从各主要产区最近情况看，今年柑桔、苹果、梨等都将比去年增产。柑桔方面：浙江估计可比去年增产20％以上，福建约可增产10％；广东也可增产10％左右。苹果方面：占全国苹果产量80％的辽宁省将比去年增产10％；山东增产更多，约比去年增加70％。梨的产量也将比去年增加，辽宁约比去年增产40％，山西省今年梨树虽逢小年，但是产量可能接近去年大年的水平。
