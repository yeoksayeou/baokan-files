Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Hachiro Arita

Author:

Date: 1957-08-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 25th: Premier Zhou Enlai received Hachiro Arita, advisor to the Japanese Socialist Party, at noon today and had lunch with him. Zhang Xiruo, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Qiao Guanhua and others were present.

### 周总理接见有田八郎

1957-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见有田八郎
    新华社25日讯　周恩来总理在今天中午接见了日本社会党顾问有田八郎，并共进午餐。在座的有张奚若、廖承志、刘宁一、乔冠华等人。
