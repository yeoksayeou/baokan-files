Title: Our Coal Miners' Union Delegation Visits Hungary
Author:
Date: 1957-08-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: A three-member delegation from the Chinese Coal Miners' Union, invited by the Hungarian Free Miners' Union to attend the Hungarian Miners' Day celebrations in Budapest, left Beijing by plane today.

### 我煤矿工会代表团赴匈

1957-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　我煤矿工会代表团赴匈
    新华社24日讯　应匈牙利自由矿工工会邀请前往布达佩斯参加匈牙利矿工节庆祝活动的中国煤矿工会代表团一行三人，已经在今天乘飞机离开北京。
