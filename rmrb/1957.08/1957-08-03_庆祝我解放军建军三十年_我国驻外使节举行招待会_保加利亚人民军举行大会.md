Title: Celebrating the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army: Our Ambassadors Abroad Hold Receptions, Bulgarian People's Army Holds Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-08-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency dispatches: Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, Lieutenant General Han Zhenji; Assistant Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Bulgaria, Wang Xiangsheng; Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, Mei Yongxi; Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, Colonel Song Denghua; Ambassador to Mongolia, He Ying; Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Yugoslavia, Senior Colonel Ding Ganru; Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, Senior Colonel Xing Rongjie; Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in India, Senior Colonel Zhang Tong; Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, Colonel Mao Chen; and Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, Colonel Jiang Keding, held receptions or cocktail parties on the evening of August 1st to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    Leaders of governments and armed forces of various countries attended the receptions. Many foreign envoys to these countries also attended.

    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 2nd: Officers and soldiers of the Bulgarian People's Army held a celebration in Sofia on August 1st to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    Major General Chernev, who recently visited China with the Bulgarian military delegation, gave a report at the meeting. He introduced the achievements of the Chinese people and the development path of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. After the report, the Chinese film "Plain Guerrillas" was shown.

    On the same day, all teams of the Bulgarian People's Army and all officers' clubs in the country held photo exhibitions commemorating the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Some officers' clubs also held celebrations.

    In the past two days, Bulgarian newspapers such as "People's Army," "Workers' Cause," "Fatherland Front," and "Labor" have published editorials, articles, and pictures commemorating the Chinese "August 1st" Army Day. The newspapers emphasized the important role played by the Chinese People's Liberation Army in defending world peace. Sofia Radio also broadcast special programs to celebrate the festival.

### 庆祝我解放军建军三十年  我国驻外使节举行招待会  保加利亚人民军举行大会

1957-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝我解放军建军三十年
    我国驻外使节举行招待会
    保加利亚人民军举行大会
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：我国驻苏联大使馆武官韩振纪中将、驻保加利亚大使馆助理武官王祥生、驻罗马尼亚大使馆武官梅永熙、驻民主德国大使馆武官宋登华上校、驻蒙古大使何英、驻南斯拉夫大使馆武官丁甘如大校、驻越南大使馆武官邢荣杰大校、驻印度大使馆武官张彤大校、驻印度尼西亚大使馆武官茅琛上校、驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定上校，在8月1日晚分别举行了招待会或酒会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十周年。
    出席招待会的有各国政府和武装部队领导人，许多国家驻这些国家的外交使节也都出席了招待会。
    新华社索非亚2日电　保加利亚人民军官兵8月1日在索非亚举行庆祝会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十周年。
    不久前随同保加利亚军事代表团到中国访问的契尔?夫少将在会上作了报告。他介绍了中国人民的成就和中国人民解放军的发展道路。报告之后放映了中国影片“平原游击队”。
    在同一天，保加利亚人民军各个团队和国内所有的军官之家都举办了中国人民解放军建军节的图片展览会。有些军官之家还举行了庆祝会。
    这两天来，保加利亚的“人民军报”、“工人事业报”、“祖国阵线报”、“劳动报”等都刊登了纪念中国“八一”建军节的社论、文章和图片。报纸着重指出了中国人民解放军在保卫世界和平方面所起的重要作用。索非亚广播电台也举办有庆祝这个节日的专门节目。
