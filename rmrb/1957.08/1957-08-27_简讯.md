　　Brief News
　  American soldiers kill again in Incheon
    Seoul News: A U.S. soldier, Mills, stationed in Incheon, shot and wounded a Korean civilian, Cao Fengji (transliteration), on August 25 while guarding a U.S. oil pipeline. Cao Fengji died of his injuries on the 26th.
    West Germany establishes Air Force
    Bonn News: The West German Air Force held its founding ceremony on August 24. The first transport squadron of the West German Air Force was declared established in Erding on this day.

### 简讯

1957-08-27
第7版()
专栏：简讯

　　简讯
　  美国兵又在仁川杀人
    汉城消息：驻在仁川的一名美国兵米尔斯在8月25日守卫美军输油管时，开枪打伤了一名朝鲜平民曹奉吉（译音）。曹奉吉已经在26日因伤重死去。
    西德建立空军
    波恩消息：西德空军在8月24日举行成立仪式。西德空军第一个运输中队这一天在埃尔丁宣告建成。
