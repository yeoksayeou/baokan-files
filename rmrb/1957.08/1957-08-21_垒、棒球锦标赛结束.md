Title: Baseball and Softball Championships Conclude
Author:
Date: 1957-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 20th - The 1957 National Fourteen-City Student Men's Softball and Eight-Unit Baseball Championships concluded today.

　　The Tianjin team, which won first place in the second group of the softball preliminaries, defeated the Beijing team, the first-place winner in the first group of the preliminaries, by a score of ten to one in the finals, thus winning the championship. The Shanghai team, which placed second in the second group of the preliminaries, also defeated the Beijing team by a score of eleven to seven in the finals, rising to second place, while the Beijing team took third place. The following rankings are: Guangzhou, Lüda, Xi'an, Qingdao, Lanzhou, Chongqing, Wuhan, Shenyang, Nanjing, Kaifeng, and Xining teams.

　　The baseball championship was won by the Shanghai team. The Beijing team, which was second in the second group of the preliminaries, surprisingly defeated the Yanbian team, the first-place winner in its group, by a score of ten to one in the finals, taking second place, while the Yanbian team fell to third place. The following rankings are: Tianjin, Guangzhou, Qiqihar, Lüda, and Shenyang teams.

### 垒、棒球锦标赛结束

1957-08-21
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

　　垒、棒球锦标赛结束
    新华社沈阳20日电　1957年全国十四城市学生男子垒球和八单位棒球锦标赛，今天全部结束。
    在垒球预赛第二组中获得第一名的天津队，在决赛中以十比一战胜了预赛第一组的第一名北京队，而取得冠军。预赛第二组的第二名上海队，在决赛中也以十一比七战胜了北京队，而跃居亚军，北京队得第三名。以下的名次是：广州、旅大、西安、青岛、兰州、重庆、武汉、沈阳、南京、开封和西宁队。
    棒球的冠军被上海队获得。预赛第二组第二名的北京队，在决赛中竟以十比一战胜了本组的第一名延边队，而取得亚军，延边队退居第三。以下的名次是：天津、广州、齐齐哈尔、旅大和沈阳队。
