Title: Soviet Union to Publish Small Encyclopedia

Author:
Date: 1957-08-24
Page: 6

　  Moscow, August 23rd (By our Correspondent) – The Soviet Union is preparing to publish a small encyclopedia. It will consist of ten volumes with 55,000 entries. Many prominent scholars are participating in its editing. The Great Soviet Encyclopedia Yearbook will be published soon. This year, the "Chronicle of Domestic Economic Life (1917 to 1957)" will be published. In the next two to three years, many encyclopedias and handbooks will be published: a four-volume Encyclopedia of Philosophy, an Industrial Economics Dictionary, a Practical Industrial Encyclopedia, a three-volume Geographical Encyclopedia, a two-volume Encyclopedia of Pharmacology, a Concise Physics Dictionary, a Chemical Encyclopedia Dictionary, and an Atomic Energy Dictionary. Some of these reference books are for the general readership. For example, the Medical Encyclopedia Dictionary will include knowledge of hygiene, rest, sports, disease prevention, etc.; the Small Home Encyclopedia will have chapters on daily life, diet, clothing, etc.

### 苏联将出版小百科全书

1957-08-24
第6版()
专栏：

　  苏联将出版小百科全书
    本报莫斯科23日电　苏联准备出版小百科全书。它总共十卷，五万五千条。许多著名学者参加它的编辑工作。大百科全书年鉴快要出版。今年将出版“国内经济生活大事记（1917年到1957年）”。在最近两三年内将出版许多百科全书和手册：四卷的哲学百科全书、工业经济辞典、实用工业百科全书、三卷的地理百科全书、两卷的药物学百科全书、简明物理学辞典、化学百科全书辞典、原子能辞典。这些参考书有的供给广大读者。如医学百科全书辞典将包括卫生、休息、体育、防疫等等知识；家庭小百科全书将有起居、饮食、衣着等等章节。
