Title: Japanese National Delegation Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-08-22
Page: 4

　  Xinhua News Agency, August 20th – The Japanese National Delegation Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane today from Moscow. The delegation is led by Akira Imamura, head of the delegation, Vice Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, and Vice Chairman of the Japan Teachers Union.

### 日禁止原子弹、氢弹国民代表团到京

1957-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

　  日禁止原子弹、氢弹国民代表团到京
    新华社20日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国参观访问的日本禁止原子弹、氢弹国民代表团，在团长、日本工会总评议会副议长、教职员工会副委员长今村彰率领下，今天由莫斯科乘飞机到达北京。
