Title: State Council Holds Fifty-Sixth Plenary Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-08-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The State Council held its fifty-sixth plenary meeting on the 9th.

    The meeting first heard a "Report on the Supply of Civilian Cloth in the Fourth Year of the Cotton Cloth Planned Supply" presented by Yao Yilin on behalf of the Ministry of Commerce. After discussion, the meeting approved this report.

    After hearing an explanation from Cheng Zihua, the meeting adopted the State Council's regulations prohibiting agricultural products that are subject to state planned purchase and unified purchase from entering the free market.

    After hearing an explanation from Shen Yanbing, the meeting adopted the Cultural Co-operation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia. This agreement will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and approval.

    The meeting deliberated on the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Communications' report requesting recognition of the 1930 International Convention on Load Lines for Ships, and decided that China should recognize and accede to this convention. This matter will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and approval.

    The meeting adopted the State Council's decision on abolishing the railway and waterway transportation courts. The decision pointed out that after the abolition of the railway and waterway transportation courts, general criminal cases related to transportation shall be handled by the local basic-level courts where the cases occurred; criminal cases that directly endanger transportation shall be handled by the intermediate people's courts in the locations of the transportation system's administration bureaus and sub-bureaus.

    The meeting adopted the State Council's Interim Regulations on Rewards and Punishments for State Administrative Organ Personnel, and decided to submit them to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and approval.

    The meeting also adopted the State Council's regulations on handling the length of service and wage issues of counter-revolutionaries and other bad elements found in the suppression of counter-revolutionaries movement in state organs, enterprises, and schools.

### 国务院举行第五十六次全体会议

1957-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第五十六次全体会议
    新华社11日讯　国务院全体会议第五十六次会议在9日举行。
    会议首先听取了姚依林代表商业部所作的“关于棉布计划供应第四年度中民用布供应问题的报告”。会议经过讨论批准了这个报告。
    会议在听取了程子华的说明后，通过了国务院关于由国家计划收购和统一收购的农产品不准进入自由市场的规定。
    会议在听了沈雁冰的说明后，通过了中华人民共和国政府和南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府文化合作协定。这个协定将提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议审议了外交部和交通部关于承认1930年国际船舶载重线公约的请示报告，决定我国承认并加入这一公约。这个问题将提交全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议通过了国务院关于撤销铁路、水上运输法院的决定。决定指出，铁路、水上运输法院撤销后，运输方面的一般刑事案件，分别由案件发生的地方基层法院处理；直接危害运输的刑事案件，由运输系统的管理局、分局所在地的中级人民法院处理。
    会议通过了国务院关于国家行政机关工作人员的奖惩暂行规定，并决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议还通过了国务院关于处理国家机关、企业、学校在肃反运动中查出的反革命分子和其他坏分子的工龄和工资问题的规定。
