Title: Syrian President Says After Visiting Egypt, Egypt and Syria Fear No Foreign Pressure; Syrian People Are Ready to Shed Blood to Defend Independence

Author:

Date: 1957-08-27
Page: 7

Comprehensive report from this newspaper: According to Xinhua News Agency: Syrian President al-Quwatli left Alexandria on the morning of August 25th to return to his country via Cairo after receiving medical treatment.

Egyptian President Nasser and the entire Egyptian cabinet went to Cairo Airport to see al-Quwatli off. Before leaving Cairo, al-Quwatli held talks with President Nasser on the situation in Syria.

Al-Quwatli made a statement to reporters before leaving Alexandria. He said, I am returning home "with health, strength, and determination, as well as confidence in the strength of Arab nationalism and the coming prosperity of the Arab future."

Al-Quwatli said that Syrians are ready "to shed blood to defend their independence."

Al-Quwatli said: "Although dangers surround us, our situation is very good, and the whole country has firmly united to resist the conspiracies of Western imperialists." In his speech, al-Quwatli expressed his firm belief that the Arab world will move towards unity, despite the imperialists' efforts to divide them.

Syrian President al-Quwatli said upon returning to Damascus on August 25th that his talks with President Nasser were very successful. He also said, "No matter what methods imperialism uses to exert pressure on Syria and Egypt, their governments and people will stop it."

Al-Quwatli was warmly welcomed at the airport by all cabinet ministers and senior army officers. Diplomatic envoys from many countries to Syria also went to the airport to welcome him, but the US embassy did not send a representative.

Al-Quwatli told reporters that his talks with Nasser involved various issues of concern to Syria and Egypt, as well as the entire Arab world. He said that the situation in Egypt is "good, strong, just like the situation in Syria." He also said that Syria and Egypt both believe that their current situation is consolidated, and they are determined to resist all pressure. (Attached picture)

### 叙总统访问埃及后表示  埃叙两国不怕外国压力  叙利亚人民已准备为保卫独立而流血

1957-08-27
第7版()
专栏：

    叙总统访问埃及后表示
    埃叙两国不怕外国压力
    叙利亚人民已准备为保卫独立而流血
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：叙利亚总统库阿特利在进行医疗以后，8月25日上午离开亚历山大港经开罗回国。
    埃及总统纳赛尔和全体埃及内阁人员都到开罗机场给库阿特利送行。库阿特利在离开罗前同纳赛尔总统会谈了叙利亚的局势。
    库阿特利在离开亚历山大以前向新闻记者发表了谈话。他说，我“带着健康、力量和决心以及对于阿拉伯民族主义的力量和对阿拉伯即将来临的繁荣前途的信心”回国去。
    库阿特利说，叙利亚人已经准备“为保卫他们的独立而流血”。
    库阿特利说：“虽然危险环绕着我们，但是我们的形势很好，全国已经坚决团结起来抵抗西方帝国主义者的阴谋。”库阿特利在谈话中表示深信，阿拉伯世界将走向团结一致，尽管帝国主义在竭力分裂它们。
    叙利亚总统库阿特利8月25日在回到大马士革时说，他和纳赛尔总统的会谈是很成功的。他还说，
  “不管帝国主义采取怎样的方式对叙利亚和埃及施加压力，它们的政府和人民一定会制止这样做的。”
    库阿特利在机场上受到所有内阁部长和高级陆军军官们的热烈欢迎。许多国家的驻叙利亚外交使节也到机场欢迎，但是美国大使馆没有派代表去。
    库阿特利对新闻记者说，他和纳赛尔的会谈涉及叙利亚和埃及，以及整个阿拉伯世界所关切的各种问题。他说，埃及的情形是“良好的，强有力的，正如叙利亚的情形一样”。他还说，叙利亚和埃及都相信他们现时的形势是巩固的，他们坚决反对一切的压力。（附图片）
