Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Meets with Ethnic Representatives
Author:
Date: 1957-08-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Vice Chairman Zhu De and Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Guangxi Province Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation, the Northwest Nationalities Institute Railway Class Visiting and Internship Team, and representatives attending the Second Congress of the China Islamic Cultural Association this afternoon.

Accompanying them were Liu Geping, Chairman of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, and Liu Chun, Gan Chunlei, and Xie Hechou, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China.

### 朱副主席接见民族代表

1957-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

    朱副主席接见民族代表
    新华社16日讯  朱德副主席和陈毅副总理今天下午接见广西省各民族参观团、西北民族学院铁路班参观实习队和出席中国回民文化协进会第二届代表会议的代表。
    陪同接见的有全国人民代表大会民族委员会主任委员刘格平、中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任刘春、甘春雷、谢鹤筹等。
