Title: China Delegation Arrives in Finland
Author:
Date: 1957-08-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 20th: Helsinki dispatch: The delegation of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, headed by Saifudin, arrived in Helsinki from Moscow by plane at noon today. The delegation was warmly welcomed by the Finnish Parliament and government.

　　Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Finnish Speaker of Parliament Fagerholm, Deputy Speakers Heljas and Kujala, Finnish Prime Minister Sukselainen, Chairman of the Finland-China Friendship Association Kahonen, and Finnish parliamentarians who visited China last year.

### 我国代表团抵芬兰

1957-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国代表团抵芬兰
    新华社莫斯科20日电　赫尔辛基消息：以赛福鼎为首的中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会代表团今天中午乘飞机从莫斯科到达赫尔辛基。代表团受到了芬兰议会和政府方面的热烈欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的有芬兰议会议长法格霍姆、副议长赫利雅斯和库雅拉、芬兰总理苏克西莱宁、芬中友好协会主席卡赫宁及去年访问中国的芬兰议员们。
