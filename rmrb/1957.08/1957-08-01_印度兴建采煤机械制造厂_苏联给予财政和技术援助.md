Title: India to Build Coal Mining Machinery Plant with Soviet Financial and Technical Assistance
Author:
Date: 1957-08-01
Page: 6

    India to Build Coal Mining Machinery Plant
    Soviet Union Provides Financial and Technical Assistance
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 31st: The "Indian Express" reported on the 30th that India, with the financial and technical assistance of the Soviet Union, will build a coal mining machinery manufacturing plant with a production capacity of 30,000 tons. The cost of building this factory is 132 million rupees, which will be drawn from the 600 million rupee long-term loan provided by the Soviet Union to India. The factory's machinery and equipment will also be supplied by the Soviet Union.

### 印度兴建采煤机械制造厂  苏联给予财政和技术援助

1957-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    印度兴建采煤机械制造厂
    苏联给予财政和技术援助
    据新华社新德里31日电　“印度快报”30日报道，印度在苏联的财政和技术援助下，将建立一座有三万吨生产能力的采煤机械制造厂。建造这个工厂所需的费用为一亿三千二百万卢比，这笔资金将取自苏联给印度的六亿卢比长期贷款。工厂的机器装备也将由苏联供应。
