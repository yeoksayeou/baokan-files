Title: Beijing, Shenyang, Jinan, Lanzhou, and Xinjiang Garrison Troops Solemnly Hold Awarding Ceremonies
Author:
Date: 1957-08-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, July 31st. Chinese People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Beijing, Shenyang, Lanzhou, Jinan, Xinjiang and other places, successively and solemnly held awarding ceremonies on the eve of "August 1st."

    At the awarding ceremony of the General Staff Department, General Su Yu, Chief of the General Staff, on behalf of Chairman Mao, awarded various medals to nine hundred and sixty-eight officers. At the joint awarding ceremony held by the General Political Department, the Military Court, and the Military Procuratorate, General Tan Zheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense and Director of the General Political Department, on behalf of Chairman Mao, awarded medals to five hundred and fifty-nine people including Lin Yueqin, among whom were twenty-two female comrades. At the Shenyang Military Region awarding ceremony, Naval Admiral Xiao Jingguang, Deputy Minister of National Defense, awarded medals to five hundred and forty-nine meritorious personnel.

    At the awarding ceremonies held separately in the Lanzhou and Xinjiang Military Regions, General Xiao Hua, Director of the General Cadre Department and Deputy Director of the General Political Department, awarded medals on behalf of Chairman Mao. At the Jinan Military Region awarding ceremony, General Wang Xinting, head of the Jinan Military Region, awarded medals on behalf of Chairman Mao.

    Local Party and government leaders participated in the awarding ceremonies of the various troops and expressed warm congratulations to the comrades who won medals at the meetings, encouraging them to make greater achievements in defending the motherland and building the army.

    The people who won medals attributed the glory to the Party, the country, and the people. They expressed their determination to make new contributions to liberating Taiwan, defending the socialist construction of the motherland, and defending world peace.

### 北京沈阳济南兰州新疆驻军  隆重举行授勋典礼

1957-08-01
第3版()
专栏：

    北京沈阳济南兰州新疆驻军
    隆重举行授勋典礼
    新华社31日讯　中国人民解放军驻北京、沈阳、兰州、济南、新疆等地部队，先后在“八一”前夕隆重举行了授勋典礼。
    在总参谋部的授勋典礼上，总参谋长粟裕大将代表毛主席把各种勋章授予九百六十八名军官。在总政治部、军事法院、军事检察院联合举行的授勋典礼上，由国防部副部长、总政治部主任谭政大将代表毛主席将勋章授予林月琴等五百五十九人，其中有女同志二十二人。在沈阳部队授勋典礼上，由国防部副部长萧劲光海军大将将勋章授予五百四十九名有功人员。
    在兰州、新疆部队分别举行的授勋典礼上，代表毛主席授勋的是总干部部部长兼总政治部副主任萧华上将。在济南部队授勋典礼上，代表毛主席授勋的是济南部队首长王新亭上将。
    各地党政负责人都分别参加了各部队的授勋典礼，并且在会上向荣获勋章的同志表示热烈的祝贺，勉励他们在保卫祖国和建军事业中取得更大的成就。
    荣获勋章的人们都把光荣归于党、国家和人民。他们表示，要为解放台湾、为保卫祖国社会主义建设、保卫世界和平作出新的贡献。
