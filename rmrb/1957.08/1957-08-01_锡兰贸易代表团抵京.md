Title: Ceylon Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-08-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 31st: An eight-member Ceylon government trade delegation, led by Ceylon's Parliamentary Secretary to the Minister of Finance, Wijesinghe, and Parliamentary Secretary to the Minister of External Affairs and Personal Representative of the Prime Minister, Subasinghe, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 锡兰贸易代表团抵京

1957-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    锡兰贸易代表团抵京
    据新华社31日讯　锡兰政府贸易代表团一行八人，在锡兰财政部国会秘书维杰辛格和外交部国会秘书、总理个人代表苏巴辛格率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
