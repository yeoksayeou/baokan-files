Title: Xuanzang Statue Discovered in Yunnan
Author:
Date: 1957-08-26
Page: 3
Culture News Briefs

　　Xuanzang Statue Discovered in Yunnan
    A statue of Xuanzang was recently discovered in Deyun Temple in Yao County, Yunnan Province. According to the Chinese Buddhist Association, this is currently the only statue of Xuanzang in China.

### 云南发现玄奘塑像

1957-08-26
第3版()
专栏：文化零讯

　  云南发现玄奘塑像
    最近在云南省大姚县德云寺发现了一座玄奘塑像，据中国佛教协会考证，认为这是目前中国唯一的一座玄奘塑像。
