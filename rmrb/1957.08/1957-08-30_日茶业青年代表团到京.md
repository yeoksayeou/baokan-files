Title: Japanese Tea Industry Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-08-30
Page: 4

　　Japanese Tea Industry Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, August 29th. The Japanese Tea Industry Youth Delegation, headed by Suzuki Juro, arrived in Beijing by train from Guangzhou last night. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation.

### 日茶业青年代表团到京

1957-08-30
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

　　日茶业青年代表团到京
    据新华社29日讯　以铃木重郎为首的日本茶业青年代表团在昨天晚间从广州乘火车到达北京。他们是应中华全国民主青年联合会的邀请来中国访问的。
