Title: Lu Yuwen Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1968-11-15
Edition: 4

	Lu Yuwen, a standing committee member and vice-secretary-general of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and a standing committee member and vice-secretary-general of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, passed away from heart disease at 6:30 PM on November 6 in Beijing. He was 68 years old.

### 卢郁文逝世

1968-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　卢郁文逝世
    新华社十四日讯　政协全国委员会常务委员、副秘书长，民革中央常务委员、副秘书长卢郁文患心脏病于十一月六日下午六时半在北京逝世，终年六十八岁。
