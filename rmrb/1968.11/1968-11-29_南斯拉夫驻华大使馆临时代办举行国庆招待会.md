Title: Yugoslav Charge d'Affaires Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1968-11-29
Edition: 5

Yugoslav Charge d'Affaires Holds National Day Reception

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, November 28 -- The Charge d'Affaires of the Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia's Embassy in China, Babic, held a reception today to celebrate the national day of Yugoslavia.

	Relevant officials from the Chinese Foreign Ministry and diplomats from various countries accredited to China were invited to attend the reception.

### 南斯拉夫驻华大使馆临时代办举行国庆招待会

1968-11-29
第5版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫驻华大使馆临时代办举行国庆招待会
    新华社二十八日讯　南斯拉夫社会主义联邦共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办巴比奇，今天为南斯拉夫国庆举行招待会。
    我国外交部有关方面负责人，各国驻中国使节，应邀出席了招待会。
