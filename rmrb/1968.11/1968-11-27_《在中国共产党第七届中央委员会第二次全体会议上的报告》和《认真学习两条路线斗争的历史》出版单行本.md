Title: The Publication of Single-Volume Editions of "Report to the Second Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China" and "Seriously Study the History of the Struggle Between the Two Lines"
Author:
Date: 1968-11-27
Edition: 1

Our great leader Chairman Mao's report to the Second Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on March 5, 1949, as well as the important editorial "Seriously Study the History of the Struggle Between the Two Lines," which conveys Chairman Mao's latest instructions, have been published as single-volume editions by the People's Publishing House. These two books will go on sale simultaneously at the Beijing Xinhua Bookstore starting November 28 and will be distributed nationwide in the near future.

### 《在中国共产党第七届中央委员会第二次全体会议上的报告》和《认真学习两条路线斗争的历史》出版单行本

1968-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

   《在中国共产党第七届中央委员会第二次全体会议上的报告》和《认真学习两条路线斗争的历史》出版单行本
    新华社二十六日讯　我们的伟大领袖毛主席一九四九年三月五日《在中国共产党第七届中央委员会第二次全体会议上的报告》以及传达了毛主席的最新指示的《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志、《解放军报》重要社论《认真学习两条路线斗争的历史》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，这两本书自十一月二十八日开始在北京新华书店同时发行，并将在全国各地陆续发行。
