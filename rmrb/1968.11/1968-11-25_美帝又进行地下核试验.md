Title: The U.S. Imperialists Conduct Another Underground Nuclear Test
Author:
Date: 1968-11-25
Edition: 5

	Washington, November 23 (Xinhua) - The U.S. Atomic Energy Commission announced that the U.S. imperialists conducted another underground nuclear weapons test on November 22 at the Nevada Test Site.

	In order to further push its nuclear blackmail policy, the U.S. imperialists are accelerating nuclear explosion tests. The test on November 22 was the third official announcement in eight days.

	The spokesman for the Atomic Energy Commission said that the United States has conducted 26 nuclear weapons tests this year. Since the U.S. imperialists colluded with the Soviet revisionists in 1963 to launch the Partial Nuclear Test Ban Treaty, the U.S. imperialists have conducted 144 underground nuclear tests.

### 美帝又进行地下核试验

1968-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    美帝又进行地下核试验
    新华社二十三日讯　华盛顿消息：据美国原子能委员会宣布，美帝国主义二十二日在内华达州核武器试验场又进行了一次地下核武器试验。
    美帝国主义为了进一步推行核讹诈政策，正在加紧核爆炸试验。二十二日的试验，是八天来官方宣布的第三次试验。
    原子能委员会的发言人说，今年以来美国已进行了二十六次核武器试验。自从一九六三年美帝同苏修勾结在一起，抛出部分禁止核试验条约的大骗局以来，美帝国主义已进行了一百四十四次地下核试验。
