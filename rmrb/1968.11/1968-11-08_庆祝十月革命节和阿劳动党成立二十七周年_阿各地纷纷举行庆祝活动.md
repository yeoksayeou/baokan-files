Title: Celebrating the October Revolution and the 27th Anniversary of the Albanian Labor Party
Author:
Date: 1968-11-08
Edition: 5

Albania's various regions, including industrial and mining enterprises, agricultural cooperatives, people's army units, government agencies, and schools, have been holding various activities in recent days to celebrate the 51st anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution and the 27th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian Labor Party (November 8).

In the capital, Tirana, many units organized lectures and discussions. Cadres of the Albanian Labor Party and veterans who participated in the Albanian National Liberation War spoke at the meetings, introducing the great historical significance of the October Revolution and the glorious path of the heroic Albanian Labor Party under the leadership of Comrade Enver Hoxha over the past 27 years. The National Liberation War Museum, the Lenin-Stalin Museum, and the Albanian Labor Party Founding Memorial Museum in Tirana also held various celebratory activities and discussions. Participants introduced the revolutionary achievements of Lenin and Stalin during their lifetimes, as well as the heroic struggle of the Albanian people against imperialism and revisionism under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party, and the brilliant victories achieved in the socialist revolution and socialist construction.

In Hoxha's hometown of Gjirokastra, people held various political and cultural activities, organized photo exhibitions, and enthusiastically celebrated the Labor Party's birthday.

In the Shkodra region, many workers, farmers, soldiers, and students visited the Labor Party Founding Memorial Museum and some major construction projects of the five-year plan. Workers' amateur performers also staged cultural performances at the Martyrs' Square.

In the Durres region, workers, farmers, and students met with old comrades of the Labor Party, inviting revolutionary veterans to recount the glorious history of the Labor Party over the past 27 years.

In the Mat region, the people, in their gathering to celebrate the October Revolution and the 27th anniversary of the Labor Party, spoke highly of the great role of the Albanian Labor Party and its great leader, Comrade Enver Hoxha, in the liberation war and the struggle for the victory of the people's revolution. They strongly exposed and condemned the treacherous crimes of the Khrushchev revisionists.

In the Elbasan region, a lecture titled "The Albanian Labor Party—The Leader and Organizer of All Victories of Our People" was held.

### 庆祝十月革命节和阿劳动党成立二十七周年  阿各地纷纷举行庆祝活动

1968-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月革命节和阿劳动党成立二十七周年
    阿各地纷纷举行庆祝活动
    新华社地拉那七日电　阿尔巴尼亚各地工矿企业、农业合作社、人民军部队、机关和学校，连日来纷纷举行各种活动，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命五十一周年和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立二十七周年（十一月八日）。
    在首都地拉那，许多单位举办了报告会和座谈会。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的一些干部和参加过阿尔巴尼亚民族解放战争的一些老战士在会上介绍了十月革命的伟大历史意义和以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的英雄的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党二十七年来所经历的光荣道路。地拉那的民族解放战争博物馆、列宁斯大林博物馆和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立纪念馆，也举办了各种庆祝活动和座谈会。与会者介绍了列宁、斯大林生前的革命业绩，以及阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党领导下所进行的反帝反修的英勇斗争和在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中所取得的辉煌胜利。
    在霍查同志的家乡吉诺卡斯特，人们举行各种政治和文艺活动，举办图片展览会，热烈庆祝劳动党的生日。
    斯库台区的许多工人、农民、人民军战士和学生参观了这个区的劳动党成立纪念馆和五年计划中的一些巨大建设项目等。工人业余演员还在烈士广场演出了文艺节目。
    都拉斯区的工人、农民和学生同劳动党的老战士举行会见，请革命老前辈讲述劳动党二十七年来的光荣历史。
    米尔迪塔区人民在庆祝十月革命节和劳动党成立二十七周年的集会上，畅谈了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖霍查同志在解放战争和争取人民革命胜利的斗争中的伟大作用，并强烈揭露和谴责了赫鲁晓夫修正主义者的叛卖罪行。
    爱尔巴桑区举办了题为《阿尔巴尼亚劳动党——我国人民一切胜利的领导者和组织者》的讲座。
