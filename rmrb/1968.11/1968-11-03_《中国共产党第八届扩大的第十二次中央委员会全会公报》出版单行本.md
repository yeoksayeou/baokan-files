Title: Publication of the Single Volume of the Communiqué of the Twelfth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1968-11-03
Edition: 2

The single volume of the "Communiqué of the Twelfth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China" has been published by the People's Publishing House. Starting from November 4, it will be available for purchase at the Xinhua Bookstore in Beijing and will gradually be available in Xinhua Bookstores across the country.

### 《中国共产党第八届扩大的第十二次中央委员会全会公报》出版单行本

1968-11-03
第2版()
专栏：

    《中国共产党第八届扩大的第十二次中央委员会全会公报》出版单行本
    新华社二日讯　《中国共产党第八届扩大的第十二次中央委员会全会公报》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，从十一月四日开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
