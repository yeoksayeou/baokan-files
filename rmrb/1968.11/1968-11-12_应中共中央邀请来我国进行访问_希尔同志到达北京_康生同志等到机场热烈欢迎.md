Title: Comrade Hill Arrives in Beijing at the Invitation of the CCP Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1968-11-12
Edition: 1

Comrade E. F. Hill, Chairman of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist), arrived in Beijing by plane this evening at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China for a visit to our country.

Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee, and responsible comrades from relevant departments, were at the airport to give him a warm welcome.

Above: Comrade Kang Sheng and Comrade Hill shake hands warmly at the airport. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency)

### 应中共中央邀请来我国进行访问　希尔同志到达北京　康生同志等到机场热烈欢迎

1968-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　应中共中央邀请来我国进行访问
　　希尔同志到达北京
　　康生同志等到机场热烈欢迎
    新华社十一日讯　澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）主席爱·弗·希尔同志，应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国进行访问，今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。
    中共中央政治局常委康生同志，以及有关部门的负责同志，到机场热烈欢迎。
    上图：康生同志和希尔同志在机场亲切握手。
