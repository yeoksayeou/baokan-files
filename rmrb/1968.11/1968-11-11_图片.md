Title: Paris Students Continue Their Just Struggle Against the Decaying Bourgeois Education System and Government Suppression
Author:
Date: 1968-11-11
Edition: 5

Paris students continue their just struggle against the decaying bourgeois education system and government suppression of the student movement. The image shows Paris students bravely fighting against the reactionary police and military forces that came to suppress them on the streets leading to the University of Paris.
