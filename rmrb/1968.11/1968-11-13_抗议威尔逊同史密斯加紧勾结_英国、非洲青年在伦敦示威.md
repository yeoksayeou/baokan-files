Title: Protest Wilson's Collusion with Smith
Author:
Date: 1968-11-13
Edition: 6

Many young workers and students from Zimbabwe, the West Indies, and some British universities held an all-night torchlight demonstration in London's Parliament Square on November 10. They protested against British Prime Minister Wilson and the head of the colonial regime in Southern Rhodesia, Smith, for tightening their collusion and suppressing the people of Zimbabwe. The demonstrators carried placards supporting the Zimbabwe people's struggle for national liberation, starting from the "Rhodesia House" and marching to Parliament Square. They condemned Wilson and Smith for their collusion and conspiracy to suppress the Zimbabwe people.

### 抗议威尔逊同史密斯加紧勾结　英国、非洲青年在伦敦示威

1968-11-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　抗议威尔逊同史密斯加紧勾结
　　英国、非洲青年在伦敦示威
    据新华社伦敦十一日电　许多来自津巴布韦、西印度群岛和英国一些大学的青年工人和学生，十日在伦敦议会广场举行通宵火炬示威，抗议英国首相威尔逊和南罗得西亚殖民当局头子史密斯加紧勾结，镇压津巴布韦人民的罪行。示威群众举着支持津巴布韦人民争取民族解放斗争的标语牌，从“罗得西亚大厅”出发，游行到议会广场，谴责威尔逊同史密斯加紧勾结，密谋镇压津巴布韦人民的罪行。
