Title: Widespread Famine in India
Author:
Date: 1968-11-19
Edition: 6

Under the brutal exploitation and oppression of the landlords and the bourgeoisie, famine is worsening in vast areas of India. Millions of impoverished peasants are struggling to survive on the brink of starvation, with the number of those dying from hunger increasing daily. Elderly Indians, with no one to rely on, are starving, their bodies emaciated.
