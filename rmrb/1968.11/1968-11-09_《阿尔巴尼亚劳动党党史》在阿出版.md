Title: The History of the Albanian Labour Party Published in Albania
Author:
Date: 1968-11-09
Edition: 5

The History of the Albanian Labour Party was published in Albania on November 8, the 27th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian Labour Party, and is being distributed throughout the country.

The History of the Albanian Labour Party was edited and published in accordance with a resolution of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labour Party.

### 《阿尔巴尼亚劳动党党史》在阿出版

1968-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    《阿尔巴尼亚劳动党党史》在阿出版
    新华社地拉那八日电　《阿尔巴尼亚劳动党党史》在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立二十七周年——十一月八日出版，并在阿尔巴尼亚全国各地发行。
    《阿尔巴尼亚劳动党党史》是根据阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会的决议编辑和出版的。
