Title: Summary of the Twelfth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1968-11-20
Edition: 3

The plenary session calls on all Party members and the people of the entire country to continue to carry out the great proletarian cultural revolution in depth, to thoroughly criticize and repudiate the counter-revolutionary revisionist thoughts of the handful of renegades, hidden traitors, and scabs within the Party, headed by Liu Shaoqi.
