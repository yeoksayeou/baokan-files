Title: Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants in Hubei's Xunyang County Follow Chairman Mao's Instructions to "Seize the Revolution and Promote Production"
Author:
Date: 1968-11-10
Edition: 3

    Left Image: In Hubei's Xunyang County, poor and lower-middle peasants follow Chairman Mao's instructions to "seize the revolution and promote production." After the bumper harvests of summer grain and early rice, they have achieved another bumper harvest of middle-season rice. This is a scene of community members from Waogou Commune in Xunyang County enthusiastically delivering their bumper harvest to the state.  Xinhua News Agency
