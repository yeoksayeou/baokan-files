Zambian Minister Inspects Chinese Survey and Design Team for Tanzam Railway
Chinese Aid Deeply Moves Zambian People

LUSAKA, Oct. 31 (Xinhua) -- Zambian Minister of Transport, Power and Communications Kalulu visited the Chinese survey and design team's camps at Mukushi and Mpika from October 25 to 27, accompanied by Charge d'Affaires Li Zhenhe of the Chinese Embassy in Zambia. He listened to reports on the progress of various preparatory works for the survey and design. He praised the hard work and spirit of overcoming difficulties shown by the Chinese engineering and technical personnel.

Minister Kalulu addressed the Chinese engineering and technical personnel and local residents, saying that China is the only country that has provided interest-free loans to Zambia. All Zambian people, young and old, are deeply moved by China's friendly actions. The minister called on local people and officials to fully cooperate with and assist the Chinese engineering and technical personnel, and to actively participate in the construction of the Tanzam Railway, which is the most important project in Zambia's history.

### 赞比亚部长视察我援建坦赞铁路勘测设计小组工作营  中国援助使赞比亚人民深深感动

1968-11-02
第6版()
专栏：

    赞比亚部长视察我援建坦赞铁路勘测设计小组工作营
    中国援助使赞比亚人民深深感动
    新华社三十一日讯　卢萨卡消息：赞比亚运输、电力和交通部长卡卢卢十月二十五日到二十七日，在中国驻赞比亚大使馆临时代办李振河陪同下，视察了帮助修建坦桑尼亚—赞比亚铁路的中国勘测设计小组在穆库希和姆皮卡工作营，并且听取了关于勘测设计的各项准备工作进度的汇报。他称赞中国工程技术人员工作努力和克服困难的精神。
    卡卢卢部长向中国工程技术人员和当地居民发表讲话说，中国是世界上唯一向赞比亚提供无息贷款的国家，全体赞比亚人民，无论老少，都被中国的这种友好行动所深深感动。这位部长号召当地的人民和官员给予中国工程技术人员以充分的合作和帮助，积极参加赞比亚历史上最重要的工程坦桑尼亚—赞比亚铁路的修建。
