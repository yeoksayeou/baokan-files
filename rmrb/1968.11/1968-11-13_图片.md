Title: She Shan People's Commune Establishes "May 7th" School
Author:
Date: 1968-11-13
Edition: 3

    The poor and lower-middle peasants of She Shan People's Commune, guided by Chairman Mao's thoughts on educational revolution, have established a new type of "May 7th" school. In less than a year, they have universalized junior middle school education in the countryside. This photo shows seventh-grade students of the "May 7th" school reciting the "Three Articles" by Chairman Mao. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)
