Title: Palestinian Guerrillas Score Major Victory
Author:
Date: 1968-11-26
Edition: 6

Palestinian guerrillas scored a major victory in mid-November, launching more than 60 attacks against Israeli occupation forces. They killed or wounded over 400 Israeli aggressors, including 26 Israeli officers.

According to a battle report issued by a Palestinian nationalist organization, during this period, the Palestinian guerrillas attacked the Israeli military headquarters in Rafah, Gaza Strip, and destroyed the Israeli military academy in Kfar Saba near the Israeli ruling center of Tel Aviv, as well as the Israeli military training ground in Beit Berel.

Additionally, the guerrillas attacked four Israeli military camps in the West Bank of the Jordan River, the Besan Valley, and the Golan Heights.

The guerrillas also attacked and blew up several important Israeli economic facilities, including the Israeli refinery in Haifa, a large Israeli factory in Jerusalem, the cable factory in Beit Maidit, and the wire factory in Ramat-Hakuvish near Tel Aviv. The refinery in Haifa caught fire and burned, with estimated damages exceeding 100,000 pounds.

The guerrillas also destroyed five Israeli observation posts, fourteen military outposts, removed seven ambush points, and attacked numerous Israeli patrol teams. On the 17th, they ambushed an Israeli military vehicle carrying officers from the barracks in Hima, Golan Heights, to the headquarters in Kfar Haruv. The vehicle was destroyed, and all 25 Israeli officers on board were killed.

On the 15th, Palestinian guerrillas shelled an Israeli military gathering point near the crossroads of Der Sharaf on the West Bank of the Jordan River, destroying two minesweepers, five military transport vehicles, fifteen half-track vehicles, and twelve machine guns. They also blew up a section of the railway east of Gaza City and two bridges in the Negev.

On the 8th and 9th, the "Storm" commando unit of "Fatah" (Palestine National Liberation Movement) blew up several sections of the railway in the Gaza Strip and the Negev Desert, causing traffic to come to a standstill for an extended period.

### 巴勒斯坦游击队又获重大战果  十一月中旬进行战斗六十多次，歼敌四百多袭击了以色列军总部，击毁敌人军事学院等

1968-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    巴勒斯坦游击队又获重大战果
    十一月中旬进行战斗六十多次，歼敌四百多袭击了以色列军总部，击毁敌人军事学院等
    新华社大马士革二十四日电　巴勒斯坦游击队十一月中旬对以色列占领军发动了六十多次袭击，取得重大战果，打死打伤四百多名以色列侵略者，其中包括二十六名以色列军官。
    据巴勒斯坦民族主义组织发表的战报，在这一期间，巴勒斯坦游击队袭击了加沙地带拉法的以色列军总部，并击毁了以色列统治中心特拉维夫附近的克法萨巴的以色列军事学院和在贝特贝雷勒的以色列军事训练场。
    此外，游击队还袭击了约旦河西岸、贝桑河谷和戈兰高地的四座以色列军营。
    游击队还袭击和爆破了一些以色列的重要经济设施，其中包括在海法的以色列炼油厂，在耶路撒冷的一座以色列大工厂，贝特迈迪特的电缆厂和在特拉维夫附近拉马特—哈库维奇的电线厂。海法的炼油厂着火燃烧，估计损失在十万英镑以上。
    游击队还摧毁了以色列的五个观察哨、十四个军事哨所，拔除了七个伏击点，袭击了许多以色列巡逻队。十七日，他们伏击了载送以色列军官从戈兰高地希马的宿舍前往克法哈里卜的总部的以色列军用汽车，汽车被击毁，车上的二十五名以色列军官全部被打死。
    十五日，巴勒斯坦游击队炮轰约旦河西岸德尔沙拉夫附近十字路口的以色列军队集结据点，毁伤两艘扫雷艇，五辆军用运输车，十五辆半履带车，还击毁了十二挺机枪。他们还爆炸了加沙市以东的一段铁路和内格夫的两座桥梁。
    八日和九日，“法塔赫”（巴勒斯坦民族解放运动）所属的“暴风”突击队还爆炸了加沙地带和内格夫沙漠的几段铁路，使交通长时间陷于停顿。
