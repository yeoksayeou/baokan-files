Title: Our Dearest Great Leader Chairman Mao Receives the Pakistani Armed Forces Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-11-11
Edition: 1

Our dearest great leader Chairman Mao received the Pakistani Armed Forces Friendship Delegation this evening. He shook hands warmly with General Agha Muhammad Yahya Khan and other Pakistani guests and had a friendly conversation with them.

Comrades Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Huang Yongsheng, Wu Faxian, and others accompanied Chairman Mao in receiving the delegation.

The members of the Pakistani Armed Forces Friendship Delegation who were received included Major General Muhammad Akbar Khan, Brigadier Muhammad Akram, Rear Admiral Muhammad Sharif, Air Vice Marshal Zafar Masud, and others.

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan and other diplomatic staff from the embassy were also present during the reception.

Our dearest great leader Chairman Mao received General Agha Muhammad Yahya Khan, the Commander-in-Chief of the Pakistani Army, and the entire Pakistani Armed Forces Friendship Delegation led by him on November 10.

1968-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席接见巴基斯坦武装部队友好代表团
　　同叶海亚·汗上将等巴基斯坦客人一一亲切握手，并进行了友好的谈话
　　周恩来、康　生、黄永胜、吴法宪等同志陪同接见
    新华社十日讯　我们最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席今晚接见了巴基斯坦陆军总司令阿格赫·穆罕默德·叶海亚·汗上将和由他率领的巴基斯坦武装部队友好代表团全体成员。
    毛主席同叶海亚·汗上将等巴基斯坦客人一一亲切握手，并且进行了友好的谈话。
    被接见的巴基斯坦武装部队友好代表团成员有：穆罕默德·阿克巴尔·汗少将、穆罕默德·阿克拉姆准将、穆罕默德·夏利夫海军准将、查法·马苏德空军准将等。
    陪同接见的，有周恩来、康生、黄永胜、吴法宪等同志。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗和大使馆外交官员接见时也在座。（附图片）
    我们最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席十一月十日接见了巴基斯坦陆军总司令阿格赫·穆罕默德·叶海亚·汗上将（前左三）和由他率领的巴基斯坦武装部队友好代表团全体成员。　新华社发
