Title: Cambodian Government Issues Statement Condemning New Aggression by the U.S. Against the Cambodian People
Author:
Date: 1968-11-23
Edition: 6

	Cambodian Government Issues Statement
	Condemning New Aggression by the U.S. Against the Cambodian People

	Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, November 22 — The Cambodian News Agency released a statement from the Royal Government of Cambodia on November 22, strongly condemning the new acts of aggression by U.S. armed forces against the Cambodian people.

	The statement pointed out that on November 16, three armed patrol boats flying the U.S. flag, escorted by three helicopters, opened fire on a group of Cambodian farmers working peacefully in the rice fields in the village of Ya Ban Lei in Pong Tuk district, Takeo province. The attack resulted in twelve deaths and six serious injuries.

	The statement noted that this U.S. aggression occurred just after the U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet forces attacked a Cambodian patrol in Chup province on November 15, killing three Cambodian soldiers.

	The statement emphasized, "The Royal Government will take all possible measures to protect the lives and property of the people." The statement demands that the U.S. government take full responsibility for the recent aggression and compensate Cambodia for the damages.

### 柬埔寨政府发表声明  谴责美国对柬人民犯下新的侵略罪行

1968-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨政府发表声明
    谴责美国对柬人民犯下新的侵略罪行
    新华社金边二十二日电　柬埔寨新闻社二十二日发表了柬埔寨王国政府的一项声明，愤怒谴责美国武装部队对柬埔寨人民犯下新的侵略罪行。
    声明指出，十一月十六日，在江旦河上航行的三艘挂有美国旗的武装巡逻艇，在三架直升飞机的掩护下，向唝吥省磅塔县已班力村正在稻田里从事和平劳动的一批柬埔寨农民开火，造成十二人死亡，六人受重伤。
    声明指出，美国的这次侵略是紧接着美国—南越伪军在十一月十五日袭击柬埔寨柴桢省的巡逻队，并打死三名柬埔寨战士之后发生的。
    声明强调说：“王国政府将采取一切力所能及的措施，以保护人民的生命和财产。”声明要求美国政府对最近的侵略承担全部责任，并给予柬埔寨赔偿损失。
