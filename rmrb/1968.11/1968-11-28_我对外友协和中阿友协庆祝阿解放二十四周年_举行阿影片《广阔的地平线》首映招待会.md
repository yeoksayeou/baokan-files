Title: The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Albania Friendship Association Celebrate the 24th Anniversary of Albania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1968-11-28
Edition: 4

The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Albania Friendship Association held a premiere screening and reception for the Albanian film "Wide Horizons" in Beijing today to celebrate the 24th anniversary of Albania's liberation.

The film "Wide Horizons" depicts the heroic Albanian working class, standing at the forefront of Europe's anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist struggle, under the leadership of Comrade Enver Hoxha and the Albanian Party of Labor. It shows their selfless labor in building and defending the socialist motherland during the revolutionary movement, as well as their boundless loyalty and love for the party and the people.

Albanian Ambassador to China, Natanaeli, and other diplomatic officials from the Albanian embassy, as well as Albanian comrades in Beijing, were invited to attend the reception.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian of the State Council, and relevant officials from the government departments, the People's Liberation Army, the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, and other relevant parties, including Xiao Jingguang, Ji Pengfei, Lin Haiyun, Xie Huaiyi, Ding Xilin, and Zhou Rongguo, attended the reception.

### 我对外友协和中阿友协庆祝阿解放二十四周年  举行阿影片《广阔的地平线》首映招待会

1968-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    我对外友协和中阿友协庆祝阿解放二十四周年
    举行阿影片《广阔的地平线》首映招待会
    新华社二十七日讯　中国人民对外文化友好协会和中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会，今天在北京举行阿尔巴尼亚影片《广阔的地平线》首映招待会，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十四周年。
    影片《广阔的地平线》表现了站在欧洲反帝反修最前线的英雄的阿尔巴尼亚工人阶级，在以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，在革命化运动中，建设和保卫社会主义祖国所进行的忘我劳动，以及他们对党对人民的无限忠诚和热爱。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利和大使馆其他外交官员，以及在北京的阿尔巴尼亚同志，应邀出席了招待会。
    国务院副总理李先念，政府有关部门、中国人民解放军、北京市革命委员会等有关方面负责人萧劲光、姬鹏飞、林海云、谢怀德、丁西林、周荣国等出席了招待会。
