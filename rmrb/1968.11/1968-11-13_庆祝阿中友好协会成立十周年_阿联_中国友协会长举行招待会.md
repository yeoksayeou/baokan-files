Title: Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the Egypt-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1968-11-13
Edition: 5

    The President of the Egypt-China Friendship Association, Ibrahim Tahawi, held a reception on the evening of November 11 to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the Egypt-China Friendship Association.

    The Egyptian Minister of Planning, Said Gaber, the Minister of Housing and Public Utilities, Hassan Mustafa, the Deputy Secretary-General of the Arab League, Said Nofal, and representatives from various sectors of Egyptian society attended the reception.

    Chinese Ambassador to Egypt, Huang Hua, was invited to attend the reception.

    Tahawi and Huang Hua both spoke at the reception.

### 庆祝阿中友好协会成立十周年　阿联—中国友协会长举行招待会

1968-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　庆祝阿中友好协会成立十周年
　　阿联—中国友协会长举行招待会
    新华社开罗十一日电　阿联—中国友好协会会长易卜拉欣·塔哈威十一日晚在这里举行招待会，庆祝阿联—中国友好协会成立十周年。
    阿联计划部长赛义德·加贝拉，住房和公用事业部长哈桑·穆斯塔法，阿拉伯联盟副秘书长赛义德·诺法勒以及阿联各界人士等出席了招待会。
    中国驻阿联大使黄华应邀出席了招待会。
    塔哈威和黄华在招待会上讲了话。
