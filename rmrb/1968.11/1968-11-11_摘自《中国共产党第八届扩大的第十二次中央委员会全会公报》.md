Title: Notice from the Enlarged Twelfth Plenum of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1968-11-11
Edition: 3

The Plenum calls upon all workers, poor and lower-middle peasants, soldiers and officers of the People's Liberation Army, revolutionary cadres, revolutionary Red Guards, and revolutionary intellectuals across the nation. It calls upon all revolutionary people of all nationalities to resolutely carry out Chairman Mao's directive that the working class must lead in all things. It calls for the establishment of the dictatorship of the proletariat in the superstructure, including all cultural domains, and the completion of the tasks of struggle, criticism, and transformation in each stage as proposed by Chairman Mao. The proletarian cultural revolution must be carried through to the end!
