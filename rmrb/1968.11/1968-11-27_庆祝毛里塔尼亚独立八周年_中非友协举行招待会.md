Title: Celebrating the 8th Anniversary of Mauritania's Independence
Author:
Date: 1968-11-27
Edition: 5

The China Africa Friendship Association held a reception in Beijing today to celebrate the 8th anniversary of the independence of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania.

Mauritanian Ambassador to China, Harsi, and diplomatic staff from the embassy were invited to attend the reception.

At the reception, Vice Chairman of the China Africa Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, and Ambassador Harsi raised their glasses to toast the ever-strengthening friendship between the peoples of China and Mauritania. Relevant officials from our country attended the reception.

### 庆祝毛里塔尼亚独立八周年  中非友协举行招待会

1968-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝毛里塔尼亚独立八周年
    中非友协举行招待会
    新华社二十六日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会，今天在北京举行招待会，庆祝毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国独立八周年。
    毛里塔尼亚驻中国大使哈尔希和大使馆外交官员，应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会上，中非友协副会长丁西林和哈尔希大使举杯共祝中国和毛里塔尼亚两国人民的友谊不断加强。我国有关方面负责人出席了招待会。
