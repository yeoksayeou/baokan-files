Title: The History of the Albanian Party of Labour to be Published with Comrade Hoxha Receiving Workers and Editors
Author:
Date: 1968-11-07
Edition: 5

The history of the Albanian Party of Labour, edited by the Marxist-Leninist Institute of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, will be published on the occasion of the 27th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian Party of Labour (November 8) and will be distributed nationwide. Recently, the great leader of the Albanian people, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, Comrade Enver Hoxha, received representatives of the workers of the "Mihal Durri" Printing House in Tirana, which printed the book, and representatives of the staff of the Marxist-Leninist Institute, which edited the book. The printing workers presented the first batch of printed copies of the "History of the Albanian Party of Labour" to Comrade Hoxha.

Those present at the reception included: Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Labour Party, Ramiz Alia, and Member of the Central Committee and Director of the Marxist-Leninist Institute, Neciimi Hoxha.

Comrade Enver Hoxha had a very cordial conversation with the workers of the printing house and the staff of the Marxist-Leninist Institute. He, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Party, praised their work and wished them new and greater successes in their work of supplying more Marxist-Leninist works to the working class, cooperative farmers, and all the people. Comrade Hoxha also spoke on the importance of publishing and studying the "History of the Albanian Party of Labour."

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党党史将出版霍查同志接见工人和编辑人员

1968-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党党史将出版霍查同志接见工人和编辑人员
    新华社地拉那六日电  据这里的报纸五日报道：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央马克思列宁主义研究所编辑的《阿尔巴尼亚劳动党党史》将于阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立二十七周年（十一月八日）的时候出版，并在全国各地发行。阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志最近接见了印刷这本书的地拉那“米哈尔·杜里”印刷厂的工人代表和编辑这本书的马克思列宁主义研究所的工作人员代表。印刷工人把刚刚出版的首批印出的《阿尔巴尼亚劳动党党史》献给了霍查同志。
    接见时在座的有：劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记拉米兹·阿利雅，中央委员、马克思列宁主义研究所所长涅奇米叶·霍查。
    恩维尔·霍查同志同印刷厂工人和马克思列宁主义研究所工作人员进行了十分亲切的谈话。他代表党中央赞扬他们所进行的工作，并祝他们在为工人阶级、合作社农民和全体人民供应更多的马克思列宁主义著作的工作中取得新的更大的成就。霍查同志还就出版和学习《阿尔巴尼亚劳动党党史》的重要意义作了讲话。
