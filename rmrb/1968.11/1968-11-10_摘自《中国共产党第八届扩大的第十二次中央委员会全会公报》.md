Title: Notice of the Twelfth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1968-11-10
Edition: 2

The plenary session calls on all Party members and the people of the entire nation to continue to carry out the great criticism of the revolution, thoroughly eliminating the reactionary revisionist ideology of the handful of top capitalist-roaders in the Party, such as Liu Shaoqi.
