Title: Burdened with the Deep Friendship of the Chinese Party, Government, and People, the Albanian Government Economic Delegation Returns to Tirana
Author:
Date: 1968-11-24
Edition: 5

Albanian Labor Party Politburo member and Deputy Prime Minister of the Albanian Council of Ministers Adil Charçani, along with the Albanian government economic delegation he led, returned to Tirana by plane on the 23rd after a friendly visit to China. They brought back with them the revolutionary and combative friendship of the Chinese Party, government, and people towards the Albanian Party, government, and people. They were greeted with extremely enthusiastic welcome by the Albanian Party and government leaders.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included: Albanian Labor Party Politburo member and Deputy Prime of the Council of Ministers Haki Toska, Labor Party Politburo member and Chairman of the State Planning Commission Spiro Koleka, Labor Party Politburo candidate member Abdyl Kellezi, Labor Party Politburo candidate member Pilo Peristeri, Labor Party Central Committee Secretariat member Kafëze Spasiu, and several ministers and Labor Party Central Committee members.

The acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Li Tingqian, and the commercial counselor Chen Guang, also went to the airport to warmly welcome the Albanian government economic delegation.

### 满载中国党、政府和人民的深情厚谊  阿政府经济代表团回到地拉那  在机场受到阿尔巴尼亚党政领导人极其热烈的欢迎

1968-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    满载中国党、政府和人民的深情厚谊
    阿政府经济代表团回到地拉那
    在机场受到阿尔巴尼亚党政领导人极其热烈的欢迎
    新华社地拉那二十三日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席阿迪尔·查尔查尼同志，以及由他率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府经济代表团，在中国进行了友好访问以后，满载着中国党、政府和人民对阿尔巴尼亚党、政府和人民的革命的战斗友谊，二十三日乘飞机回到地拉那，受到了阿尔巴尼亚党政领导人极其热烈的欢迎。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席哈基·托斯卡，劳动党中央政治局委员、国家计划委员会主席斯皮罗·科列加，劳动党中央政治局候补委员阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐，劳动党中央政治局候补委员皮洛·皮里斯特里，劳动党中央书记处书记查费尔·斯巴修，以及一些政府部长和劳动党中央委员等。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃、商务专员陈光等也到机场热烈欢迎阿尔巴尼亚政府经济代表团。
