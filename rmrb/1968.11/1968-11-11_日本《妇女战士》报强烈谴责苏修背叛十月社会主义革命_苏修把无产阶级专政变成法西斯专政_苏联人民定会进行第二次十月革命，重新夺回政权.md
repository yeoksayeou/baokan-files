Title: Japanese "Women Warriors" Newspaper Strongly Condemns Soviet Revisionists' Betrayal of the October Socialist Revolution
Author:
Date: 1968-11-11
Edition: 5

Tokyo, Nov. 10 (New China News Agency) — The Tokyo Committee of the Japanese Women's League recently published an editorial in its organ, "Women Warriors," strongly condemning the Soviet revisionist traitor clique for betraying Marxism-Leninism, abolishing the dictatorship of the proletariat in the Soviet Union, and fully restoring capitalism.

The editorial says: Fifty-one years ago, under the great leader Lenin, the Soviet people, through a violent revolution, established the first dictatorship of the proletariat in human history. Since then, under the leadership of Lenin and Stalin, the Soviet people have achieved great victories in building socialism.

However, due to the usurpation of power by the Soviet revisionist traitor clique, capitalism has been fully restored in the Soviet Union, and the dictatorship of the proletariat has been completely transformed into the dictatorship of the bourgeoisie and fascism.

The editorial angrily points out that the Soviet revisionist traitor clique "has pushed the Soviet working class back into the dark and miserable plight before the October Revolution," and "has carried out ruthless political oppression and economic exploitation against the Soviet people."

The editorial emphasizes that now, within the Soviet people, the struggle against the Soviet revisionist traitor clique has begun.

We believe that the Soviet people will definitely carry out a second October Revolution and seize power back into their own hands.

The editorial concludes: "The great leader Chairman Mao has summed up the experience and lessons of the restoration of capitalism in the Soviet Union, personally initiated and led the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China, thereby consolidating the dictatorship of the proletariat in China, preventing the restoration of capitalism, and opening up a brilliant path for the world's people to advance towards communism."

### 日本《妇女战士》报强烈谴责苏修背叛十月社会主义革命　苏修把无产阶级专政变成法西斯专政　苏联人民定会进行第二次十月革命，重新夺回政权

1968-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　日本《妇女战士》报强烈谴责苏修背叛十月社会主义革命
　　苏修把无产阶级专政变成法西斯专政
　　苏联人民定会进行第二次十月革命，重新夺回政权
    新华社十日讯　东京消息：日本妇女同盟东京委员会机关报《妇女战士》最近发表社论，强烈谴责苏联修正主义叛徒集团背叛马克思列宁主义，在苏联取消无产阶级专政，全面复辟资本主义的滔天罪行。
    社论说，五十一年前在无产阶级的伟大领袖列宁领导下，苏联人民通过暴力革命，建立了人类历史上第一个无产阶级专政的社会主义国家。在这以后，苏联人民又在列宁和斯大林的领导下，在建设社会主义方面取得了伟大的胜利。
    但是，由于苏修叛徒集团篡夺了领导权，在苏联全面复辟资本主义，把无产阶级专政完全变成了资产阶级专政和法西斯专政。
    社论愤怒地指出，苏修叛徒集团“把苏联的工人阶级推进了十月革命以前的黑暗的悲惨境地”，“对苏联人民实行了残酷的政治压迫和经济剥削”。
    社论强调说，现在，在苏联人民当中已经开始了反对苏修叛徒集团的斗争。
    我们相信，苏联人民一定会进行第二次十月革命，把政权重新夺回到自己的手里。
    社论最后说：“伟大领袖毛主席总结了苏联复辟资本主义的经验教训，亲自发动和领导了中国无产阶级文化大革命，从而巩固了中国的无产阶级专政，防止了资本主义复辟，为世界人民走向共产主义开辟了光辉灿烂的道路。”
