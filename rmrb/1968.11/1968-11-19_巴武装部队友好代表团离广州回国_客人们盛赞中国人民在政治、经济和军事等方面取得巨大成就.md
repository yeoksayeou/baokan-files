Title: Pakistani Armed Forces Friendship Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home
Author:
Date: 1968-11-19
Edition: 5

The Pakistani Armed Forces Friendship Delegation, led by General Agha Muhammad Yahya Khan, Commander-in-Chief of the Pakistani Army, concluded their friendly visit to China and departed from Guangzhou by train on the morning of the 17th to return home.

Deputy Minister of Defense Xiao Jinguang, responsible officials of the Guangzhou Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, and responsible officials of the Guangdong Revolutionary Committee, along with over a thousand soldiers, militia, and Red Guards, went to the station to give them a warm send-off.

Pakistani Military Attaché Colonel Kayum also came to see them off.

The Pakistani Armed Forces Friendship Delegation arrived in Guangzhou by special plane on the morning of the 15th. That evening, the responsible officials of the Guangzhou Military Region hosted a banquet in their honor.

During their stay in Guangzhou, the Pakistani guests visited the Guangzhou militia's military and cultural performances, the China Export Commodities Fair, and watched the revolutionary model opera, the modern ballet "The White-Haired Girl." The guests praised the great achievements of the Chinese people in politics, economy, military, and culture under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao.

### 巴武装部队友好代表团离广州回国　客人们盛赞中国人民在政治、经济和军事等方面取得巨大成就

1968-11-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　巴武装部队友好代表团离广州回国
　　客人们盛赞中国人民在政治、经济和军事等方面取得巨大成就
    新华社广州十八日电  巴基斯坦陆军总司令叶海亚·汗上将和由他率领的巴基斯坦武装部队友好代表团，结束了在我国的友好访问，十七日上午乘火车离开广州回国。
    国防部副部长萧劲光，人民解放军广州部队负责人和广东省革命委员会负责人，以及人民解放军指战员、民兵和红卫兵小将一千多人前往车站热烈欢送。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆武官卡尤姆上校也前往送行。
    巴基斯坦武装部队友好代表团是十五日上午乘专机到达广州的。当天晚上，广州部队负责人设宴招待了巴基斯坦客人。
    巴基斯坦客人在广州期间，先后参观了广州民兵军事、文艺表演和中国出口商品交易会，并观看了革命样板戏现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》。客人们参观后，盛赞我国人民在毛主席的英明领导下，政治、经济、军事和文化等方面所取得的巨大成就。
