Title: Laotian Patriots Achieve New Victories in Attacks on Enemy Airfields, Camps, and Sweeps
Author:
Date: 1968-11-22
Edition: 6

Laotian patriotic military and civilian forces have recently achieved new victories in their battles.

According to a report by the Pathet Lao News Agency, Laotian patriotic military and civilian forces have recently achieved new victories in their battles.

On November 2, patriotic armed forces in the province of Savannakhet attacked an enemy military unit and an airfield in the Phalan area, killing and wounding many enemy soldiers, and destroying one enemy military truck and one barracks.

On November 5, local forces in the province of Saravane inflicted heavy casualties on the enemy in Moungkhet. Prior to this, on October 31, the People's Liberation Army also shelled enemy positions in Ongpao, killing and wounding a number of enemy soldiers.

In early November, patriotic armed forces in the Dengdinh area of the province of Xiengkhouang repelled an enemy company's "creeping" attack, killing and wounding more than ten enemy soldiers on the spot, with the remaining enemy fleeing in disarray.

On October 31, patriotic armed forces in the province of Khammouane launched a fierce attack on enemy positions on Hill 1705 in the southern part of the province, killing and capturing 30 enemy soldiers, and seizing two mortars, six machine guns, and other military supplies.

According to a report by the Xinhua News Agency in Kheang Khay, the Pathet Lao News Agency reported that Laotian patriotic military and civilian forces achieved many new victories in October.

In October, local forces and guerrillas in the Nambo area of the province of Luang Prabang intercepted enemy "sweeps" and eliminated American-backed bandits, killing 19 enemy soldiers and wounding 26, while seizing a batch of weapons and military supplies.

In October, the People's Liberation Army and guerrillas in the province of Udomsai annihilated 104 enemy soldiers and seized 28 weapons of various types.

According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao Radio Station, patriotic military and civilian forces in the province of Phongsali bravely struck at bandits who infiltrated liberated areas to carry out sabotage activities in October, annihilating 96 enemy soldiers and seizing a batch of weapons and supplies.

On October 31, patriotic military and civilian forces in the province of Khammouane bravely attacked enemy forces in the Seno area, killing 23 enemy soldiers and wounding many others. Prior to this, on October 29, armed forces in this province continued to strike at enemy forces in the Monghong area, killing 14 enemy soldiers and seizing seven weapons.

From October 23 to October 30, guerrillas in the province of Vientiane bravely intercepted enemy "sweeps," annihilating 30 enemy soldiers.

### 袭击敌人机场　摧毁敌军营房　粉碎敌人“扫荡”  老挝爱国军民最近又获新战果

1968-11-22
第6版()
专栏：

    袭击敌人机场　摧毁敌军营房　粉碎敌人“扫荡”
    老挝爱国军民最近又获新战果
    新华社康开十八日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道：老挝爱国军民最近在战斗中又取得了新的战果。
    沙湾拿吉省的爱国武装力量十一月二日袭击了法兰地区的一支敌军部队和机场，打死打伤许多敌人，并且击毁敌人一辆军用卡车和一座兵营。
    沙拉湾省地方部队十一月五日重创了更嘉的守敌。在这之前，十月三十一日，人民解放军还炮击了敌军在翁巴老的阵地，打死打伤一批敌人。
    桑怒省登丁地区的爱国武装力量，十一月初击退了敌人一个连的“蚕食”进攻，当场打死打伤敌人十多名，残敌狼狈逃窜。
    川圹省爱国武装力量十月三十一日向这个省南部一七○五高地的敌军阵地发起了猛烈进攻，打死和俘虏敌军三十名，并且缴获迫击炮两门、机枪六挺和其它一些军用物资。
    据新华社康开电　据巴特寮通讯社报道：老挝爱国军民十月份取得许多新战果。
    琅勃拉邦省南博地区的地方部队和游击队上月在截击敌军“扫荡”和在剿灭美国豢养的匪徒的战斗中，打死敌人十九名，打伤二十六名，同时缴获一批武器和军用物资。
    乌多姆塞省的人民解放军和游击队在十月份共歼灭敌军一百零四名，缴获各类武器二十八件。
    另据巴特寮电台广播，丰沙里省爱国军民十月份多次英勇打击窜入解放区进行破坏活动的匪徒，共歼灭敌人九十六名，缴获一批武器和物资。
    川圹省爱国军民十月三十一日英勇袭击森佐地区的敌军，打死敌人二十三名，打伤许多敌人。在这以前，十月二十九日，这个省的爱国武装力量继续打击芒翁地区的敌军，打死敌人十四名，缴获武器七件。
    万象省游击队在从十月二十三日到十月三十日的七天内，英勇截击进行“扫荡”的敌军，歼灭敌人三十名。
