Title: Syria Forms New Government
Author:
Date: 1968-11-01
Edition: 6

	Syria Forms New Government

	Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, October 29th - According to the Arab Syrian News Agency, Syria's Head of State, Atassi, formed a new government on the 29th with himself as the Prime Minister.

	The new government consists of the Prime Minister and 22 ministers. Fifteen ministers from the previous government retain their original positions, including Defense Minister Asad and Finance Minister Shurbaji. In the new government, former Interior Minister Ashawi serves as the Foreign Minister, and former Commander of the People's Army, Tawil, serves as the Interior Minister.

	The previous government's Prime Minister, Zain, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mahus, and the ministers of five departments including Transportation and News, did not join the new government.

### 叙利亚组成新政府

1968-11-01
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚组成新政府
    新华社大马士革二十九日电　据阿拉伯叙利亚通讯社报道：叙利亚国家元首阿塔西二十九日组成了以他为总理的新政府。
    新政府由总理和二十二名部长组成。上届政府的十五名部长在新政府中留任原职，其中包括国防部长阿萨德和财政部长舒尔巴吉。在新政府中，前内政部长阿沙维担任外交部长，前人民军司令塔维勒担任内政部长。
    上届政府的总理扎因、副总理兼外交部长马胡斯以及交通、新闻等五个部的部长没有参加新政府。
