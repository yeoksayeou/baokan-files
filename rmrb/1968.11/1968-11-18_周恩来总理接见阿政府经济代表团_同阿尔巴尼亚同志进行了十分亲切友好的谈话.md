Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Albanian Government Economic Delegation and Holds a Very Cordial and Friendly Talk with Albanian Comrades
Author:
Date: 1968-11-18
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai received the Albanian Government Economic Delegation and held a very cordial and friendly talk with Albanian comrades.

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, November 17 -- Premier Zhou Enlai today received Comrade Adil Charçani, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania and Vice-Premier of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, and the entire delegation of the Albanian Government Economic Delegation led by him. He held a very cordial and friendly talk with them.

	The members of the Albanian Government Economic Delegation who were received include: Comrade Koço Theodhori, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania and Minister of Industry and Mines of Albania; Comrade Pupo Shyti, Vice-Chairman of the State Planning Commission of Albania; Comrade Rahman Hanku, Vice-Minister of Construction of Albania; Comrade Vasili Kati, Vice-Minister of Foreign Trade of Albania; Comrade Vasili Natanaeli, Albanian Ambassador to China; and Comrade Kozma, Commercial Counselor of the Albanian Embassy in China, and others.

	Present at the reception were Comrades Li Xiannian, Lin Haiyun, Li Qiang, Zhong Yuyi, Xie Huai-de, Qiao Guanhua, Zhou Ping, and Han Zongzheng.

### 周恩来总理接见阿政府经济代表团　同阿尔巴尼亚同志进行了十分亲切友好的谈话

1968-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理接见阿政府经济代表团
　　同阿尔巴尼亚同志进行了十分亲切友好的谈话
    新华社十七日讯　国务院总理周恩来同志，今天接见了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席阿迪尔·查尔查尼同志和由他率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府经济代表团全体成员，并且同他们进行了十分亲切友好的谈话。
    被接见的阿尔巴尼亚政府经济代表团团员有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局候补委员、阿尔巴尼亚工业和矿产部长科乔·蒂奥多西、阿尔巴尼亚国家计划委员会副主席普波·希提、阿尔巴尼亚建工部副部长拉赫曼·汉库、阿尔巴尼亚贸易部副部长瓦西里·卡蒂、阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利、阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆商务参赞考兹玛等同志。
    接见时在座的，有李先念、林海云、李强、钟羽一、谢怀德、乔冠华、周平、韩宗正等同志。
