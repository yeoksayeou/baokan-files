Title: The Italian Edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong (Version A)" Published in Italy
Author:
Date: 1968-11-22
Edition: 5

	Milan News: The Italian edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong (Version A)" has recently been published in Italy. This book was translated and published by the Oriental Publishing House in Milan. Earlier, this publishing house also published the second edition of "Mao Zedong on Literature and Art" in Italian.

	New China News Agency, November 21st:

### 《毛泽东著作选读（甲种本）》意大利文版在意大利出版

1968-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    《毛泽东著作选读（甲种本）》意大利文版在意大利出版
    新华社二十一日讯　米兰消息：《毛泽东著作选读（甲种本）》意大利文版最近在意大利出版。这本书是米兰的东方出版社翻译出版的。这家出版社在早些时候还出版了意大利文版《毛泽东论文学与艺术》的第二版。
