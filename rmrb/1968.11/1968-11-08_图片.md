Title: Soldiers of the People's Liberation Army Propaganda Team Enter the Countryside to Promote the Spirit of the Party's Eighth Plenary Session of the Twelfth Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1968-11-08
Edition: 3

Soldiers of the People's Liberation Army propaganda team enter the countryside to promote the spirit of the Party's Eighth Plenary Session of the Twelfth Central Committee. This is a soldier from the propaganda team enthusiastically explaining the spirit of the plenary session to an elderly woman whose family member is a revolutionary martyr.
