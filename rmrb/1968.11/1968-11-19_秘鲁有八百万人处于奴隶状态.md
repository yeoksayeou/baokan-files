Title: Eight Million Peruvians Live in Slavery
Author:
Date: 1968-11-19
Edition: 6

Currently, under U.S. imperialist plunder, eight million indigenous people and peasants in Peru live in a state of slavery. Their only means of subsistence is coca leaves. Due to high living costs and meager wages, residents suffer from malnutrition, with sixty percent afflicted by tuberculosis. The child mortality rate reaches 83.5 per thousand, making it one of the highest in the Americas.

### 秘鲁有八百万人处于奴隶状态

1968-11-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　秘鲁有八百万人处于奴隶状态
    目前，在美帝掠夺下，秘鲁土著居民和农民中有八百万人处于奴隶状态，他们得到的报酬是以古柯叶维生。由于生活费用高昂和工资微薄，居民营养不良，百分之六十的人患结核病。儿童死亡率达千分之八十三点五，是美洲最高之一。
