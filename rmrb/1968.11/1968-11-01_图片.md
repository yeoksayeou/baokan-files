Title: The Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants Manage the School
Author: Xiang Dongbing
Date: 1968-11-01
Edition: 2

After the poor and lower-middle peasants took over the management of the school in Songshu Brigade, Jianyi Commune, Liaoning Province's Yingkou County, the poor and lower-middle peasants stepped onto the podium. They used Mao Zedong Thought to educate the students and cultivate laborers who have socialist consciousness and are cultured. This is old poor peasant Li Shengwen sharing his experiences in applying Mao Zedong Thought in practice with the students.

	Xiang Dongbing, photographer
