Title: Protesting the Unjust Persecution of a Black Teacher's Racist Acts
Author:
Date: 1968-11-12
Edition: 6

		Protesting the Unjust Persecution of a Black Teacher's Racist Acts
		American Students Smash School's Reactionary Authorities

		Xinhua News Agency, November 10th: Washington reports: The strike by students at San Francisco State College in California to protest the school administration's persecution of a black lecturer has lasted three days as of the 9th. Black students have engaged in fierce struggles with the reactionary school authorities.

		On the 9th, many black youths attacked various offices in the college, throwing furniture out of the windows. They also set fires in several places to express their strong protest against the reactionary authorities.

		This strike began on the 6th. A black lecturer was dismissed by the school administration for defending the right of black students to self-defense against the oppression and discrimination of the racist school authorities. Following this, black students, with the support of many white students, initiated the strike and held large-scale demonstrations. On the 8th, a group of teachers also expressed support for the students' just strike. They announced that if the reactionary authorities did not reinstate the black lecturer by the 13th, they would also go on strike.

		Xinhua News Agency, November 7th: Washington reports: Students at San Francisco State College in California held a large-scale strike on the 6th to protest the school administration's unjust dismissal of a black lecturer.

		This strike was initiated by black students at the college, with many white students actively participating. Over 2,000 black and white students held a large-scale demonstration on the campus and clashed with the reactionary school authorities' police, who were called in from San Francisco to suppress the students.

### 抗议无理迫害黑人教师的种族主义行径　美国大学生怒砸学校反动当局

1968-11-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　抗议无理迫害黑人教师的种族主义行径
　　美国大学生怒砸学校反动当局
    新华社十日讯　华盛顿消息：美国加利福尼亚州州立旧金山学院学生为抗议校方迫害一名黑人讲师而举行的大罢课，到九日已经坚持了三天。黑人学生同反动学校当局进行了激烈的斗争。
    九日，许多黑人青年袭击了学院各处的办公室，把办公室里的家具从窗口扔出去。他们还在几处地方点着了火，表示对反动当局的强烈抗议。
    这次罢课是在六日开始的。一名黑人讲师因为维护黑人学生在种族主义学校当局的压迫和歧视面前有自卫的权利而被校方解除了职务。接着，黑人学生在许多白人学生的支持下发起罢课，并且举行了大规模的示威游行。八日，一批教师也表示支持学生们的正义罢课斗争。他们宣布，如果反动当局在十三日以前还不恢复这个黑人讲师的职务，他们也将举行罢教。
    新华社七日讯　华盛顿消息：美国加利福尼亚州州立旧金山学院的学生六日举行大罢课，抗议校方无理解除一名黑人讲师的职务。
    这次大罢课是这所学院的黑人学生发起的，很多白人学生积极参加了这一行列。二千多名黑人学生和白人学生还在校园内举行了大规模的示威游行，并和反动的学校当局从旧金山市召来镇压学生的大批警察进行了搏斗。
