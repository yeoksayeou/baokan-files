Comrade Shehu Receives the Chinese Party, Government, and Military Delegation

During the reception, Comrade Shehu had an extremely cordial and friendly conversation with Comrade Huang Yongsheng and all members of the delegation.

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, TIRANA, NOVEMBER 28 — On the afternoon of November 27, Comrade Muhammad Shehu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, received Comrade Huang Yongsheng, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the entire delegation of Chinese party, government, and military representatives led by him at the Council of Ministers building.

	Those who participated in the reception included: Comrade Beqir Balluku, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Minister of Defense; Comrade Adil Carcani, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Comrade Haki Toska, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor and Chairman of the State Planning Commission; Comrade Abdul Kelmendi, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor and Chairman of the Executive Committee of the People's Assembly of Tirana; Comrade Koci Todhri, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor and Minister of Industry and Mines; Comrade Petrit Dumo, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor and Chief of the General Staff of the People's Army; Comrade Hito Cako, member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor and Director of the Political Department of the People's Army; Ministers Nesti Nase, Kico Ngjela, and Shefqet Peçi; and Comrade Koci Prifti, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

	During the reception, Comrade Shehu had an extremely cordial and friendly conversation with Comrade Huang Yongsheng and all members of the delegation.

	Present at the reception were also Comrade Li Tingqian, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, and Military Attaché Li Zhongxuan.

### 谢胡同志接见中国党政军代表团  接见时，谢胡同志同黄永胜同志和代表团全体成员进行了极其亲切友好的谈话

1968-11-29
第2版()
专栏：

    谢胡同志接见中国党政军代表团
    接见时，谢胡同志同黄永胜同志和代表团全体成员进行了极其亲切友好的谈话
    新华社地拉那二十八日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡同志二十七日下午在部长会议大厦接见了中共中央委员、中国人民解放军总参谋长黄永胜同志和由他率领的中国党政军代表团全体成员。
    参加接见的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库，劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席阿迪尔·查尔查尼和哈基·托斯卡，劳动党中央政治局委员、国家计划委员会主席斯皮罗·科列加，劳动党中央政治局候补委员、地拉那区人民会议执行委员会主席阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐，劳动党中央政治局候补委员、工业和矿产部长科乔·蒂奥多西，劳动党中央政治局候补委员、人民军总参谋长彼得里特·杜米，劳动党中央委员、人民军政治部主任希托·恰科，政府部长奈斯蒂·纳赛、基乔·恩杰拉和谢夫切特·佩奇，以及外交部礼宾司司长科乔·普里夫蒂等同志。
    接见时，谢胡同志同黄永胜同志和代表团全体成员进行了极其亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃和武官李钟玄也在座。
