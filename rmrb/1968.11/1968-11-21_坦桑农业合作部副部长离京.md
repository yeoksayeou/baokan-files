Title: Tanzanian Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Cooperatives Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-11-21
Edition: 6

Tanzanian Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Cooperatives and Chairman of the Cotton Marketing Board, Kihambu, and his entourage concluded their visit to China and departed Beijing by plane this morning.

### 坦桑农业合作部副部长离京

1968-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

    坦桑农业合作部副部长离京
    新华社二十日讯　坦桑尼亚农业合作部副部长兼棉花销售局主席基哈姆巴等结束了在中国的访问，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京。
