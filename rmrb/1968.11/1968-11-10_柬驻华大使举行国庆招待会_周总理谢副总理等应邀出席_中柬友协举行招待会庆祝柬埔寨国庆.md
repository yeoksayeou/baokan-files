Title: Cambodian Ambassador Hosts National Day Reception; Zhou Enlai and Xie Fuzhi Attend
Author:
Date: 1968-11-10
Edition: 5

Cambodian Ambassador Hosts National Day Reception; Zhou Enlai and Xie Fuzhi Attend

	Beijing, November 9 (Xinhua) — Cambodian Ambassador to China, Zhang Gang, held a reception today to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the Kingdom of Cambodia's National Day.

	Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Xie Fuzhi, and relevant officials from the Chinese government, the People's Liberation Army, and the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, including Li Tianyou, Wu De, Han Nianlong, Ma Wenbo, Zhou Huamin, Xie Huai'de, and Ding Xilin, attended the reception upon invitation.

	Ambassadors from various countries to China also attended the reception upon invitation.

	Beijing, November 8 (Xinhua) — The China-Cambodia Friendship Association held a reception in Beijing today to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the Kingdom of Cambodia's National Day.

	Cambodian Ambassador to China, Zhang Gang, and other diplomatic staff from the Cambodian Embassy, as well as Cambodian friends in Beijing, attended the reception upon invitation.

	Vice Premier Li Xiannian and relevant officials, including Ji Pengfei, Ma Wenbo, Xie Huai'de, Zheng Weishan, and Ding Xilin, attended the reception.

	After the reception, the films "The Ship Needs a Helmsman" and "Piano Accompaniment to 'The Red Lantern'" were screened.

### 柬驻华大使举行国庆招待会  周总理谢副总理等应邀出席  中柬友协举行招待会庆祝柬埔寨国庆

1968-11-10
第5版()
专栏：

    柬驻华大使举行国庆招待会
    周总理谢副总理等应邀出席
    中柬友协举行招待会庆祝柬埔寨国庆
    新华社九日讯　柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗今天举行招待会，庆祝柬埔寨王国国庆十五周年。
    国务院总理周恩来，副总理谢富治，以及我国政府有关部门、中国人民解放军、北京市革命委员会等有关方面负责人李天佑、吴德、韩念龙、马文波、周化民、谢怀德、丁西林等应邀出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国使节也应邀出席了招待会。
    新华社八日讯　中国柬埔寨友好协会今天在北京举行招待会，庆祝柬埔寨王国国庆十五周年。
    柬埔寨王国驻中国大使张岗和大使馆其他外交官员以及在北京的柬埔寨朋友，应邀出席了招待会。
    国务院副总理李先念和有关方面负责人姬鹏飞、马文波、谢怀德、郑维山、丁西林出席了招待会。
    会后，放映了影片《大海航行靠舵手》和《钢琴伴唱〈红灯记〉》。
