Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with Albanian Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-11-18
Edition: 5

Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council received Albanian Labor Party Politburo member and Deputy Premier of the Albanian People's Republic, Adil Charçani, and the entire delegation of the Albanian government economic mission led by him on November 17. The photo shows a group picture taken during the reception.  Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
