Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-11-07
Edition: 1

	Establish revolutionary committees that combine cadres, representatives of the masses, and People's Liberation Army personnel. Engage in extensive criticism, clean up the class ranks, rectify the Party, streamline organizations, reform unreasonable regulations, transfer office staff to the grassroots, and carry out the struggle, criticism, and transformation in factories. Generally, these are the stages that will be experienced.

### 毛主席语录

1968-11-07

专栏：

    毛主席语录
