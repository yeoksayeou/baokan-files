Title: Red Shadow Soldier
Author:
Date: 1968-11-24
Edition: 4

After the publication of the communiqué of the 12th Plenary Session of the 8th Central Committee of the Party, the soldiers and officers of a company of an advanced unit of the Engineering Corps who actively study and apply Chairman Mao Zedong's thoughts, filled with deep hatred for the traitor, secret agent, and capitalist-roader Liu Shaoqi, immediately held a criticism meeting to severely criticize Liu Shaoqi's heinous crimes.
