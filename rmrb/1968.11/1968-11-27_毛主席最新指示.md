    Chairman Mao's Latest Directive

    Historical experience is worth noting. A line, a viewpoint, must be frequently and repeatedly expounded. It is not enough to expound it to a few people; it must be made known to the broad revolutionary masses.

### 毛主席最新指示

1968-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席最新指示
    历史的经验值得注意。一个路线，一种观点，要经常讲，反复讲。只给少数人讲不行，要使广大革命群众都知道。
