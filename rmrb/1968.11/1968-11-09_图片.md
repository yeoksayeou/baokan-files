Title: Sparks Fly, Furnace Glows Red, Steelworkers' Loyalty to Chairman Mao Burns Even Brighter!
Author:
Date: 1968-11-09
Edition: 2

Sparks fly, the furnace glows red, the steelworkers' loyalty to Chairman Mao burns even brighter! Workers at the Capital Steel Company, battling at the steel furnaces, have declared that they will resolutely fulfill all the combat tasks outlined in the communiqué.
