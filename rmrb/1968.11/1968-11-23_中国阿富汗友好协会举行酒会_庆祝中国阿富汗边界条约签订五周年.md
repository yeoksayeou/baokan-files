Title: China-Afghanistan Friendship Association Holds a Reception to Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of the Signing of the China-Afghanistan Border Treaty
Author:
Date: 1968-11-23
Edition: 6

The China-Afghanistan Friendship Association held a reception in Beijing today to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the China-Afghanistan Border Treaty.

Afghanistan's Ambassador to China, Suhail, and other diplomatic officials from the embassy were invited to attend the reception.

At the reception, Vice Chairman of the China International Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, and Ambassador Suhail raised their glasses to toast the ever-developing friendship between the peoples of China and Afghanistan.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Han Nianlong, and other relevant officials attended the reception.

### 中国阿富汗友好协会举行酒会  庆祝中国阿富汗边界条约签订五周年

1968-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    中国阿富汗友好协会举行酒会
    庆祝中国阿富汗边界条约签订五周年
    新华社二十二日讯　中国阿富汗友好协会今天在北京举行酒会，庆祝中国阿富汗边界条约签订五周年。
    阿富汗驻中国大使苏海尔和大使馆其他外交官员，应邀出席了酒会。
    酒会上，我对外友协副会长丁西林和苏海尔大使举杯共祝中国和阿富汗两国人民之间的友谊不断发展。
    外交部副部长韩念龙等有关方面负责人出席了酒会。
