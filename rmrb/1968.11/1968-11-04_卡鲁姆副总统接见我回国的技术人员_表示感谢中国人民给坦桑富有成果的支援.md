Title: Vice President Karume Receives Chinese Technical Personnel Returning Home
Author:
Date: 1968-11-04
Edition: 5

Vice President Karume of Tanzania received four Chinese technical personnel who had worked for two years on the island of Zanzibar and were about to return home. The meeting took place at the State House in Zanzibar on October 31.

During the cordial conversation with the Chinese technical personnel, Karume expressed his gratitude to the government and people of China, led by Chairman Mao Zedong, for their fruitful support to Zanzibar, Tanzania.

He said that the youth of Zanzibar had learned technical skills from the Chinese experts, and they would use these skills to benefit the people of Zanzibar.

He also said that the work done by the Chinese technical personnel for the people of this island would forever be appreciated by the people of Zanzibar.

Present at the reception were Sabit Komba, Minister of Trade and Industry of Zanzibar, and Rashid Abdulla, Minister of Agriculture and Land Reform of Zanzibar. Liu Qingyou, Deputy Consul of China in Zanzibar, was also present.

### 卡鲁姆副总统接见我回国的技术人员  表示感谢中国人民给坦桑富有成果的支援

1968-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    卡鲁姆副总统接见我回国的技术人员
    表示感谢中国人民给坦桑富有成果的支援
    新华社桑给巴尔一日电　坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆十月三十一日在这里的国家大厦接见了在桑给巴尔岛上工作了两年并即将回国的四名中国制革、制鞋和种植烟草方面的技术人员。
    卡鲁姆在同中国技术人员的亲切谈话中，表示感谢毛泽东主席领导下的中国政府和人民对坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔的富有成果的支援。
    他说，桑给巴尔青年从中国专家那里学到了技术知识，他们将用这些技术知识为桑给巴尔人民造福。
    他还说，中国技术人员为这个岛的人民所做的工作将永远受到桑给巴尔人民的赞赏。
    接见时在座的有：桑给巴尔贸易和工业部长萨比特·孔布和桑给巴尔农业和土改部长拉希德·阿卜杜拉。中国驻桑给巴尔副领事刘庆有也在座。
