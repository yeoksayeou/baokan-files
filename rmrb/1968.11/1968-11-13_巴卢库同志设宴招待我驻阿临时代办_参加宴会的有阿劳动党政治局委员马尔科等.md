Title: Comrade Baluku Hosts a Banquet for Our Temporary Chargé d'Affaires in Albania
Author:
Date: 1968-11-13
Edition: 5

Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Politburo Member and Albanian People's Republic Council of Ministers Deputy Chairman and Minister of Defense Bekir Baluku hosted a luncheon on November 11 to welcome the temporary chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Li Tingquan, and military attaché Li Zhongxuan.

Attending the luncheon were: Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Politburo Member and Labor Party First Secretary of the Durrës Region Rita Marko, Chief of the Political Department of the People's Army Shito Chako, Deputy Foreign Minister Rez Malile, and others. The banquet was held in a very friendly and cordial atmosphere.

### 巴卢库同志设宴招待我驻阿临时代办　参加宴会的有阿劳动党政治局委员马尔科等

1968-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　巴卢库同志设宴招待我驻阿临时代办
　　参加宴会的有阿劳动党政治局委员马尔科等
    新华社地拉那十二日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库同志，十一日举行午宴招待中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃和武官李钟玄。
    参加午宴的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、劳动党都拉斯区委员会第一书记里塔·马尔科，人民军政治部主任希托·恰科，外交部副部长雷兹·马利列等。宴会在十分亲切友好的气氛中进行。
