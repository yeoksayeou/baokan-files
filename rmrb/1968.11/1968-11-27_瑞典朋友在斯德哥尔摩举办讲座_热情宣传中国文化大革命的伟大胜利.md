Title: Swedish Friends Hold Lectures in Stockholm to Enthusiastically Promote the Great Victory of China's Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1968-11-27
Edition: 5

The Swedish China Association held lectures on November 23rd and 24th in Stockholm to enthusiastically introduce China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and the great victories achieved by the Chinese people on all fronts.

Friends from the Swedish China Association and the Swedish Marxist-Leninist Communist Union introduced the situation of the Cultural Revolution at the meeting, pointing out that the purpose of this great revolution is to consolidate the dictatorship of the proletariat, prevent the restoration of capitalism, and ensure that China remains forever red. Swedish friends at the meeting condemned the crimes committed by the traitor, spy, and capitalist-roader Liu Shaoqi.

Swedish friends also introduced the great achievements made by the Chinese people in industry, agriculture, education, and other fields.

Other speakers at the meeting exposed and refuted the malicious slanders and anti-China propaganda spread by imperialism and Soviet revisionism against the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

### 瑞典朋友在斯德哥尔摩举办讲座  热情宣传中国文化大革命的伟大胜利

1968-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    瑞典朋友在斯德哥尔摩举办讲座
    热情宣传中国文化大革命的伟大胜利
    新华社斯德哥尔摩二十五日电　瑞典中国协会二十三日和二十四日在斯德哥尔摩举办讲座，热情介绍中国无产阶级文化大革命和中国人民在各条战线上取得的伟大胜利。
    瑞典中国协会和瑞典马列主义者共产主义联盟的朋友在会上介绍中国文化大革命的情况，并指出，这次大革命的目的在于巩固无产阶级专政，防止资本主义复辟，使中国永远保持鲜红的颜色。瑞典朋友在会上谴责了叛徒、内奸、工贼刘少奇犯下的罪行。
    瑞典朋友还介绍了中国人民在工业、农业、教育和其他方面所取得的伟大成就。
    其他一些发言人在会上揭露和驳斥了帝国主义和苏修对中国无产阶级文化大革命的恶毒诬蔑和反华谰言。
