Title: Resolution of the Twelfth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1968-11-18
Edition: 2

The plenary session calls on all Party members and the people of the entire country to continue to carry out the great criticism and purge the counter-revolutionary revisionist thoughts of the handful of capitalist roaders within the Party, headed by Liu Shaoqi.
