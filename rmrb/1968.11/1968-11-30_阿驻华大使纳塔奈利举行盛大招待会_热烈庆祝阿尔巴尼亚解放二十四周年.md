Title: Albanian Ambassador Natanieli Hosts Grand Reception to Celebrate the 24th Anniversary of Albania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1968-11-30
Edition: 1

Albanian Ambassador Natanieli hosted a grand reception to celebrate the 24th anniversary of Albania's liberation. Comrades from China and Albania, along with revolutionary friends from various countries, frequently raised their glasses, toasting to the long life of Comrade Enver Hoxha and Chairman Mao!

Comrades Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Yao Wenyuan, Xie Fuzhi, Wu Faxian, and Wen Yucheng were invited to attend the reception. Ambassador Natanieli and Comrade Zhou Enlai delivered speeches filled with revolutionary camaraderie at the reception.

	New China News Agency, November 29th: Today marks the 24th anniversary of Albania's liberation. Albanian Ambassador to China, Natanieli, held a grand reception at the Beijing Hotel this evening to celebrate this significant national holiday for the Albanian people.

	Leading comrades from the Central Committee, the Central Cultural Revolution Group, and other sectors, including Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Yao Wenyuan, Xie Fuzhi, Wu Faxian, and Wen Yucheng, were invited to attend the reception. Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian and others were also invited to attend.

	Comrade E.F. Hill, Chairman of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist), and Comrade Yusuf Ajitorop, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party and head of the Indonesian Communist Party delegation, were invited to attend the reception.

	The reception was filled with the atmosphere of proletarian internationalist friendship. At the front of the venue, portraits of Enver Hoxha, the great leader of the Albanian people, and Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people, were prominently displayed side by side, along with the national flags of both countries.

	During the reception, comrades from China and Albania, along with revolutionary friends from other countries, frequently raised their glasses, toasting to the everlasting friendship between the two parties and peoples of China and Albania! To the continuous great victories in the struggle of the revolutionary peoples of all countries against imperialism led by the United States, against modern revisionism centered around the Soviet revisionist renegade clique, and against the reactionaries of all countries! To the long life of Comrade Enver Hoxha! To the long life of Chairman Mao!

	Ambassador Natanieli spoke first at the reception (full text on the second page).

	Comrade Zhou Enlai then spoke (full text on the second page).

	The speeches filled with revolutionary camaraderie by Ambassador Natanieli and Comrade Zhou Enlai were met with enthusiastic applause throughout the venue. After their speeches, the orchestra played the national anthems of China and Albania.

	Those invited to attend the reception also included leading comrades from various headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the National Defense Science and Technology Commission, various military branches, units, the Beijing Military Region, and the Beijing Garrison Command, such as Li Zuopeng, Qiu Huizuo, Liu Xianquan, Xiao Jinguang, Li Tianyou, Wang Xinting, Zhang Chuming, Wang Bingzhen, Luo Shunchu, Wang Hongkun, Wang Huijiu, Zheng Weishan, Chen Renqi, Yang Junsheng, Huang Zhiyong, Chen Shipeng, Zhang Yixiang, Huang Zuozhen, and Wu Zhong, as well as representatives of the Five Good Soldiers from the Beijing Military Region.

	Leading comrades from relevant departments, including Ji Pengfei, Ma Wenbo, Ren Yunzhong, Li Dongye, Lin Haiyun, Liu Dahu, Xie Huaiyi, Shen Jian, and Ding Xilin, were also invited to attend the reception.

	Ambassadors from various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 阿驻华大使纳塔奈利举行盛大招待会　热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十四周年

1968-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    阿驻华大使纳塔奈利举行盛大招待会
  　热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十四周年
    中阿两国战友和各国革命朋友频频举杯，敬祝霍查同志万寿无疆！敬祝毛主席万寿无疆！
    周恩来、康生、姚文元、谢富治、吴法宪、温玉成同志等应邀出席招待会
    纳塔奈利大使和周恩来同志在招待会上先后发表了充满革命情谊的讲话
    新华社二十九日讯  今天是阿尔巴尼亚解放二十四周年，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利今晚在北京饭店举行盛大招待会，热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民这一举国欢庆的光辉节日。
    中央、中央文革和其他方面的负责同志周恩来、康生、姚文元、谢富治、吴法宪、温玉成，应邀出席了招待会。应邀出席招待会的还有国务院副总理李先念等。
    正在北京的澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）主席爱·弗·希尔同志，印度尼西亚共产党中央政治局委员、印度尼西亚共产党中央代表团团长优素福·阿吉托罗普同志，应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会上洋溢着无产阶级国际主义友谊的气氛。会场正前方，并排高悬阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖恩维尔·霍查同志的画像和中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席的画像，挂着阿中两国国旗。
    在招待会进行过程中，中阿两国战友和其他各国革命朋友频频举杯，祝中阿两党、两国人民的伟大战斗友谊万古长青！祝各国革命人民反对以美国为首的帝国主义、反对以苏修叛徒集团为中心的现代修正主义、反对各国反动派的斗争不断取得更加伟大的胜利！敬祝恩维尔·霍查同志万寿无疆！敬祝毛主席万寿无疆！
    纳塔奈利大使在招待会上首先讲话（全文见第二版）。
    周恩来同志接着讲话（全文见第二版）。
    纳塔奈利大使和周恩来同志发表的充满革命情谊的讲话，在全场激起一阵阵热烈的掌声。他们讲话以后，乐队分别奏中国国歌和阿尔巴尼亚国歌。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有中国人民解放军各总部、国防科委、各军种、兵种、北京部队和北京卫戍区的负责同志李作鹏、邱会作、刘贤权、萧劲光、李天佑、王新亭、张池明、王秉璋、罗舜初、王宏坤、王辉球、郑维山、陈仁麒、杨俊生、黄志勇、陈士榘、张翼翔、黄作珍、吴忠，以及驻北京部队五好战士代表。
    有关部门负责同志姬鹏飞、马文波、任允中、李东野、林海云、刘大煜、谢怀德、申健、丁西林等，也应邀出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国使节应邀出席了招待会。
