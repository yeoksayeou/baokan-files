Title: The Albanian Youth Voice Newspaper Exposes the Soviet Revisionists' Use of the Komsomol as a Tool for Restoring Capitalism
Author:
Date: 1968-11-06
Edition: 6

The editorial board of the Albanian Youth Voice, the organ of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Youth League, published an article on the 2nd, which sternly exposes and condemns the crimes of the Soviet modern revisionists in betraying the revolutionary fighting traditions of the Komsomol, which was created by Lenin and Stalin, in corrupting the youth with bourgeois and revisionist ideology at home, and in sabotaging the revolutionary movement of the youth and undermining the anti-imperialist unity of struggle internationally.

The article, after recounting the glorious path of the Komsomol, says that after the traitorous Khrushchev clique seized the leadership of the Soviet party, the Komsomol fell into the hands of the modern revisionists. It lost all revolutionary characteristics and became an anti-Marxist organization, a tool for the revisionists to oppose socialism and restore capitalism. Now, the Brezhnev-Kosygin revisionist clique, through the Komsomol, is making every effort to corrupt Soviet youth with bourgeois morality, ideology, art, and culture. Bourgeois individualism is flourishing in various forms among Soviet youth. The Soviet Khrushchev revisionist clique poisons the youth with individualism and selfishness to make Soviet youth detach themselves from the revolutionary and communist cause.

The article says that the Soviet revisionist clique, in order to poison Soviet youth, has flooded the market with American-style decadent adventure novels, opened nightclubs nationwide, invited American jazz bands to visit the Soviet Union, and promoted decadent music among the youth, and held "fashion" competitions with American imperialists.

The article points out that Lenin and Stalin have always educated the youth with the spirit of proletarian internationalism. The Soviet revisionists, as traitors to the revolution and socialism, as servants of the bourgeoisie, have replaced proletarian internationalism with national egoism and great-power chauvinism. The Soviet revisionists have established a US-Soviet alliance, opposing the freedom and independence of the peoples of all countries. The Komsomol's revisionist leaders, following the reactionary policy of the Brezhnev-Kosygin revisionist clique, have become the most vicious splitters and saboteurs of the world youth's anti-imperialist and democratic movement.

The article goes on to say that the world revolutionary youth will never forget and forgive the Soviet revisionists' betrayal of the Vietnamese people and the Arab peoples, the savage occupation of Czechoslovakia, and the bloody suppression of the demonstrations by foreign students in Moscow against US imperialism's aggression in Vietnam. The world revolutionary youth will also never forget and forgive the Soviet revisionists' brutal persecution of Latin American students in Moscow who demonstrated in support of Mexican students.

The article points out that if there are revisionists in the ranks of the youth, there can be no anti-imperialist fighting unity. It is impossible to fight against imperialism without fighting against its agents.

The article says that the revolutionary movement of the world youth is developing. Under the leadership of various Marxist-Leninist revolutionary parties, the youth movement is closely linked with the workers' movement and will surely achieve victory.

The article concludes by saying that the revolutionary youth of the Soviet Union will unite and organize, stand together with the Soviet working class, and under the leadership of true Marxist-Leninists, raise high the banner of Lenin and Stalin, and fight to revive the great cause of the October Revolution. The Albanian youth, under the guidance of the Albanian Party of Labor and Comrade Enver Hoxha, will stand together with the Soviet youth and firmly believe that the day when the banner of the October Revolution will proudly fly and wave again in the Soviet Union is not far off.

### 阿《青年之声报》揭露苏修把苏共青团变为复辟资本主义工具  谴责苏修毒害青年破坏世界青年革命运动  苏联革命青年必将团结起来，同苏联工人阶级一起在真正的马列主义者领导下奋起战斗

1968-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

    阿《青年之声报》揭露苏修把苏共青团变为复辟资本主义工具
    谴责苏修毒害青年破坏世界青年革命运动
    苏联革命青年必将团结起来，同苏联工人阶级一起在真正的马列主义者领导下奋起战斗
    新华社地拉那三日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会机关报《青年之声报》二日发表编辑部文章，严正揭露和谴责苏联现代修正主义者背叛列宁和斯大林亲手缔造的苏联共青团的革命的战斗传统，在国内用资产阶级和修正主义思想腐蚀青年，在国际上破坏青年的革命运动和破坏反帝战斗团结的滔天罪行。
    文章在叙述了苏联共青团所经历的光荣道路后说，在赫鲁晓夫叛徒集团窃取了苏联党的领导以后，苏联共青团就落到了现代修正主义者的手里。它丧失了任何革命的特征，成了一个反马克思主义的组织，成了修正主义者反对社会主义和复辟资本主义的工具。现在，勃列日涅夫、柯西金修正主义集团通过苏联共青团，正在竭力用资产阶级的道德、思想、艺术和文化腐蚀苏联青年。资产阶级个人主义正在以各种形式在苏联青年中得到滋长。苏联赫鲁晓夫修正主义集团用个人主义、利己主义去毒害青年，为的是使苏联青年脱离革命和共产主义事业。
    文章说，苏修叛徒集团为了毒害苏联青年，还把美国式的道德败坏的惊险小说塞满市场，在全国开设夜总会，邀请美国爵士乐队访苏，在青年中宣传颓废音乐，同美帝国主义者举行“时装”比赛。
    文章指出，列宁和斯大林一直用无产阶级国际主义的精神教育青年。苏联修正主义者作为革命和社会主义的叛徒，作为资产阶级的仆从，用民族利己主义和大国沙文主义代替了无产阶级国际主义。苏修建立了美苏同盟，反对各国人民的自由和独立。苏联共青团的修正主义领导人追随勃列日涅夫、柯西金修正主义集团的这一反动政策，成了世界青年反帝和民主运动的最恶毒的分裂者和破坏者。
    文章接着说，世界革命青年决不会忘记和饶恕苏联修正主义者对越南人民和阿拉伯人民的背叛，对捷克斯洛伐克的野蛮占领，以及对在莫斯科的外国留学生反对美帝侵略越南的示威所进行的血腥镇压，也决不会忘记和饶恕苏联修正主义者对在莫斯科的拉丁美洲学生声援墨西哥大学生的示威所进行的粗暴迫害。
    文章指出，青年队伍里出了修正主义者，就不可能有反帝的战斗团结。不同帝国主义的代理人进行斗争，也就不可能同帝国主义进行斗争。
    文章说，世界青年的革命运动正在发展。在各个马克思列宁主义革命政党的领导下，青年运动紧密地同工人运动联系起来，就必将取得胜利。
    文章最后说，苏联革命青年必将团结起来，组织起来，同苏联工人阶级站在一起，在真正的马克思列宁主义者的领导下，高举列宁和斯大林的旗帜，奋起投入战斗，复兴伟大十月革命的事业。在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和霍查同志教导下的阿尔巴尼亚青年，将同苏联青年站在一起，并且深信，十月革命的旗帜重新在苏联骄傲地升起和飘扬的日子不远了。
