Title: Welcoming the Friendly Delegation of the Pakistani Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1968-11-10
Edition: 5

The Ministry of National Defense held an evening of performing arts tonight to warmly welcome the friendly delegation of the Pakistani Armed Forces, led by General Agha Muhammad Yahya Khan, Commander-in-Chief of the Pakistani Army.

Deputy Minister of National Defense Xiao Jinguang accompanied the Pakistani guests to watch the revolutionary modern ballet "The Red Detachment of Women" performed by the Workers', Peasants', and Soldiers' Ballet Troupe.

Air Force Attaché to the Pakistani Embassy in China, Air Commodore Haider, was also invited to attend the evening.

Attending the evening were: senior officials from the General Staff Department, General Logistics Department, various military branches, and corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including Li Tianyou, Zhang Xianyao, Wu Ruilin, and Tan Jieshu.

After the performance, Deputy Minister Xiao Jinguang and General Khan ascended the stage to shake hands with the performers, congratulating them on their successful performance.

### 欢迎巴基斯坦武装部队友好代表团  我国防部举行文艺晚会

1968-11-10
第5版()
专栏：

    欢迎巴基斯坦武装部队友好代表团
    我国防部举行文艺晚会
    新华社九日讯　我国防部今晚举行文艺晚会，热烈欢迎由巴基斯坦陆军总司令叶海亚·汗上将率领的巴基斯坦武装部队友好代表团。
    国防部副部长萧劲光陪同巴基斯坦客人观看了工农兵芭蕾舞剧团演出的革命现代芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆空军武官海达上校应邀出席了晚会。
    出席晚会的有：中国人民解放军总参谋部、总后勤部、各军种、兵种负责人李天佑、张贤约、吴瑞林、谭家述等。
    演出结束后，萧劲光副部长和叶海亚·汗上将等走上舞台和演员们握手，祝贺他们演出成功。
