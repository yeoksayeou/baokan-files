Title: West German Bonn Reactionary Government's "Emergency Law" Faces Strong Opposition
Author:
Date: 1968-11-15
Edition: 5

The reactionary government of West Germany in Bonn, in an attempt to strengthen its fascist rule, has enacted the "Emergency Law." This has met with strong opposition from the broad masses of the West German people. The image shows 15,000 workers and students in West Berlin bravely marching forward despite the reactionary police's water cannons.
