Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Peru Denounces the Savage Crimes of the Soviet Revisionists in Invading Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1968-11-10
Edition: 6

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Peru published an article in the 39th issue of the party's official newspaper, *El Diario*, denouncing the Soviet revisionist clique's invasion of Czechoslovakia.

The article states that the Soviet revisionist clique's despicable occupation of Czechoslovakia is the result of an evil scheme to divide the world into "spheres of influence" between American imperialism and the Soviet revisionist clique.

The article points out that the Brezhnev-Kosygin revisionist clique, representing the Soviet bourgeois privileged stratum, has restored the commodity economy, bourgeois dictatorship, and capitalist culture. They have abandoned proletarian internationalism and are dividing the world into "spheres of influence." Brezhnev even informed Johnson in advance of his plan to occupy Czechoslovakia.

The article says: "These serious events once again prove the universal truth of contemporary Marxism-Leninism—Mao Zedong Thought—that class struggle exists in socialist society. They also prove the great importance of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China in preventing capitalist restoration."

The article says that in the sharp and complex class struggle, the proletariat has lost power in the Soviet Union, Yugoslavia, and some other countries that had once established proletarian dictatorship. This is a heartbreaking tragedy and a severe lesson. However, this is only a temporary setback. The just struggles of the proletariat and youth in these countries clearly indicate a bright future for the struggle.

The article says: "Comrade Mao Zedong said: 'Although the leadership of the party and the state in the Soviet Union has now been usurped by the revisionists, I advise comrades to firmly believe that the broad masses of the people, the broad ranks of party members and cadres in the Soviet Union are good and want revolution. The rule of the revisionists will not last long.'"

The article further points out: "The traitorous clique that has defiled the world proletariat and social revolution will not hold onto power for long."

### 秘共中央发表文章谴责苏修侵捷的野蛮罪行  苏修叛徒集团的日子不会长久  苏修集团早已抛弃无产阶级国际主义，同美帝合伙重新瓜分世界“势力范围”

1968-11-10
第6版()
专栏：

    秘共中央发表文章谴责苏修侵捷的野蛮罪行
    苏修叛徒集团的日子不会长久
    苏修集团早已抛弃无产阶级国际主义，同美帝合伙重新瓜分世界“势力范围”
    新华社九日讯　利马消息：秘鲁共产党中央委员会机关报《红旗报》第三十九期发表秘鲁共产党中央委员会政治委员会的文章，谴责苏修叛徒集团侵略捷克斯洛伐克。
    文章说，苏修集团卑鄙地占领捷克斯洛伐克，是要把世界瓜分为美帝国主义同苏联修正主义集团“势力范围”的恶劣结果。
    文章指出，作为苏联的资产阶级特权阶层代表的勃列日涅夫—柯西金修正主义集团，恢复了资产阶级的商品经济、资产阶级专政和资本主义文化。他们抛弃了无产阶级国际主义，把世界瓜分为“势力范围”。勃列日涅夫甚至事先把他准备占领捷克斯洛伐克的事告诉了约翰逊。
    文章说：“这些严重事件再一次证明了当代马克思列宁主义——毛泽东思想关于在社会主义社会存在阶级和阶级斗争的普遍真理。也证明了（中国）无产阶级文化大革命对防止资本主义复辟的极大的重要性。”
    文章说，在尖锐复杂的阶级斗争中，无产阶级已在苏联、南斯拉夫和其他一些曾建立过无产阶级专政的国家失去了政权。这是一个痛心的悲剧，同时也是一个严厉的教训。但是，这不过是一种暂时的挫折。这些国家的无产阶级和青年的正义斗争，清楚地说明了斗争的光辉前景。
    文章说：“毛泽东同志说：‘虽然，苏联的党和国家的领导现在被修正主义者篡夺了，但是，我劝同志们坚决相信，苏联广大的人民、广大的党员和干部，是好的，是要革命的，修正主义的统治是不会长久的。’”
    文章接着指出：“玷污了世界无产阶级和社会革命的叛徒集团，把持政权是不会长久的。”
