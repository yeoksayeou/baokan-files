Title: Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Annihilate Large Numbers of Enemies in Rainy Season Battles
Author:
Date: 1968-11-14
Edition: 6

Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Annihilate Large Numbers of Enemies in Rainy Season Battles

	Four Hundred Sixty-Seven Battles in Five Months, Annihilating Over 3,600 Enemies

	Xinhua News Agency, Khamkeut, November 13th

	According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, the Lao patriotic military and civilians achieved great victories in the rainy season this year.

	The radio station reported that from June 1st to October 31st, over the course of five months in the rainy season, the Lao patriotic military and civilians continuously and bravely fought against the enemy, engaging in 467 large and small battles. They annihilated a total of 3,632 enemies, including 2,360 killed, 685 wounded, and 236 captured. Additionally, hundreds of soldiers from the right-wing Lao military defected to the people's side.

	During this period, the Lao patriotic military and civilians also destroyed five enemy vehicles, shot down 40 American planes, seized 746 various types of weapons, and destroyed a large number of enemy weapons and tens of tons of military supplies. According to another broadcast, the local forces and guerrilla units in the lower Laos provinces engaged in 70 large and small battles over four months in the rainy season, annihilating 240 enemies, seizing 45 various types of weapons, and shooting down three American planes.

	Xinhua News Agency, Khamkeut, November 11th

	According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, the Lao patriotic military and civilians annihilated over 1,100 American imperialists and their right-wing Lao military in October.

	The radio station reported that in October, the American imperialists and their lackeys fiercely attacked the liberated areas of Savannakhet Province, conducted "sweeping" operations and suppressed the people in Vientiane, Attapeu, and other provinces. They dispatched planes to madly bomb and strafe the liberated areas and sent large numbers of bandits to carry out sabotage activities in the liberated areas of Xam Neua and other provinces. However, all the enemy's evil activities were met with continuous strikes from the Lao patriotic military and civilians.

	The radio station reported that according to incomplete statistics, in October, the Lao patriotic military and civilians annihilated 1,114 enemies, shot down five American planes, seized 117 various types of weapons, and seized and destroyed a large number of enemy military supplies.

	The radio station reported that in October, the patriotic military and civilians in Xam Neua Province repelled the enemy's "eating away" attacks in Baty, Mangkhon, Nakhang, and other places, annihilating nearly 200 enemies. The patriotic military and civilians in Xieng Khouang Province annihilated more than 200 enemies in battles in the Mang Ong and Kham Mong An areas. The patriotic military and civilians in the middle and lower Laos annihilated 439 enemies in October, seizing a large number of weapons and military supplies. The patriotic military and civilians in Luang Prabang, Udomsai, Phongsali, and Luang Namtha provinces also actively eliminated bandits, spies, and special agents, bravely counterattacked the enemy's "sweeping" operations, and achieved many new victories.

	According to a report by the Xinhua News Agency, Khamkeut, the Pathet Lao News Agency reported: The patriotic armed forces in the middle and lower Laos took the initiative to launch 35 attacks in October, annihilating 381 enemies, including 235 killed, including two American "advisors." During this period, they also shot down one enemy plane and seized and destroyed a large number of weapons.

	The report said that on October 29th, the patriotic armed forces in Bolikhamsai Province attacked and captured the lair of the bandits raised by the Americans in the Nong Yang area, killing many enemies.

	According to another report, after the patriotic armed forces in Phongsali Province destroyed the bandit lairs raised by the Americans in the Bakao and Nong Hang areas, 53 bandits surrendered to the patriotic armed forces over the next two months.

### 老挝爱国军民雨季战斗歼灭大批敌人　五个月中进行四百六十余次战斗，共歼敌三千六百多名

1968-11-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　老挝爱国军民雨季战斗歼灭大批敌人
　　五个月中进行四百六十余次战斗，共歼敌三千六百多名
    新华社康开十三日电  据巴特寮电台广播，老挝爱国军民在今年雨季五个月内取得了巨大的胜利。
    电台说，从六月一日到十月三十一日的雨季五个月中，老挝爱国军民连续、勇猛打击敌人，进行大小战斗四百六十七次，共歼灭敌人三千六百三十二名，其中打死两千三百六十名，打伤六百八十五名，俘虏二百三十六名。另外还有成百的老挝右派军队的士兵投奔人民。
    在此期间，老挝爱国军民还击毁敌人军车五辆，击落美机四十架，缴获各类武器七百四十六件，击毁敌人大批武器和数十吨军用物资。另据广播，下寮各省地方部队和游击队在今年雨季的四个月内，进行大小战斗七十次，共歼灭敌人二百四十名，缴获各类武器四十五件，击落美机三架。
    新华社康开十一日电  据巴特寮电台广播，老挝爱国军民十月份共歼灭美帝国主义及其老挝右派军队一千一百多名。
    电台说，十月份里，美帝国主义及其走狗竭力进攻沙湾拿吉省解放区，“扫荡”和镇压万象、阿速坡等省的人民，出动飞机疯狂轰炸和扫射解放区，派遣大批匪徒在桑怒等省解放区进行破坏活动。但是敌人的一切罪恶活动，都遭到了老挝爱国军民的连续打击。
    电台说，据不完全统计，十月份老挝爱国军民共歼灭敌人一千一百一十四名，击落美国飞机五架，缴获各类武器一百一十七件，缴获和击毁敌人大批军用物资。
    电台说，十月份桑怒省爱国军民在巴提、芒咸、纳康等地粉碎了敌人的“蚕食”进攻，歼灭敌人近二百名。川圹省爱国军民在芒翁和高蒙安地区的战斗中，歼灭敌人二百多名。中下寮爱国军民十月份共歼灭敌人四百三十九名，缴获大批武器和军用物资。琅勃拉邦、乌多姆塞、丰沙里、琅南塔等省的爱国军民也大力剿灭土匪、间谍特务，英勇反击敌人“扫荡”，取得了许多新胜利。
    据新华社康开电　据巴特寮通讯社报道：老挝中下寮爱国武装力量十月份主动出击三十五次，共歼灭敌人三百八十一名，其中打死二百三十五名，包括两名美国“顾问”。在此期间还击落敌机一架，缴获和击毁大批武器。
    报道说，十月二十九日，波里坎塞省爱国武装力量攻克了美国豢养的匪徒在南阳地区的巢穴，打死许多敌人。
    另据报道，在丰沙里省爱国武装力量摧毁了美国在巴高和南杭地区豢养的土匪巢穴之后的两个月期间，共有五十三名匪徒向爱国武装力量投诚。
