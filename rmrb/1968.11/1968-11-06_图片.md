Title: Workers, Soldiers, and Intellectuals Strive Day and Night at the Nanjing Yangtze River Bridge Construction Site
Author:
Date: 1968-11-06
Edition: 3

Workers, People's Liberation Army soldiers, and revolutionary intellectuals who are toiling day and night at the Nanjing Yangtze River Bridge construction site enthusiastically celebrated the publication of the communiqué of the 12th Plenary Session of the 8th Central Committee of the Party. They resolutely declared: They will definitely hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, complete all the tasks set forth by the plenary session, and achieve outstanding results to contribute to the 9th National Congress of the Party.
