Title: Sino-Albanian Economic and Trade Agreement Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-11-23
Edition: 1

The People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Albania signed the "Agreement on China's Loan to Albania," the "Protocol on China's Provision of Technical Assistance and Supply of Complete Sets of Equipment to Albania," and the "Protocol on China's Supply of Single Items of Machinery and Equipment, Various Materials, and Ships to Albania" in Beijing on November 20.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the signing ceremony.

Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council, signed the agreement and protocols on behalf of China, and Adil Charchani, Head of the Albanian Government Economic Delegation and Deputy Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, signed on behalf of Albania.

On the same day, the two sides also signed the "Protocol on the Exchange of Goods and Payments in 1969" and the "Protocol on the Use of Chinese Loans by Albania in 1969." Lin Haiyun, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of China, and Koco Theodhosi, Minister of Industry and Mines of Albania, signed the protocols.

Other members of the Albanian Government Economic Delegation who attended the signing ceremony included Pupo Shyti, Deputy Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Rahman Hanku, Deputy Minister of Construction, Vasil Kati, Deputy Minister of Trade, Natanael, Albanian Ambassador to China, and Kouzma, Commercial Counselor of the Albanian Embassy in China.

From the Chinese side, those who attended the signing ceremony included relevant department heads such as Qiao Guanhua, Zhong Yiyi, Li Qiang, and Xie Huai.

After the signing, the comrades from both China and Albania applauded warmly, expressing their congratulations.

### 中阿经济贸易协定在京签字  周恩来总理参加了签字仪式  李先念副总理和查尔查尼团长在协定和议定书上签字

1968-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中阿经济贸易协定在京签字
    周恩来总理参加了签字仪式
    李先念副总理和查尔查尼团长在协定和议定书上签字
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国政府和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府，二十日在北京签订了“关于中国给予阿尔巴尼亚贷款的协定”，“关于中国向阿尔巴尼亚提供技术援助和供应成套设备的议定书”，“关于中国向阿尔巴尼亚供应单项机器设备、各种材料和船只的议定书”。
    国务院总理周恩来参加了签字仪式。
    在协定和议定书上签字的，中国方面是国务院副总理李先念，阿尔巴尼亚方面是阿尔巴尼亚政府经济代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席阿迪尔·查尔查尼。
    中阿双方二十日同时签订了“关于一九六九年交换货物和付款的议定书”，“关于阿尔巴尼亚一九六九年使用中国贷款的议定书”。在议定书上签字的是我国对外贸易部代理部长林海云，阿尔巴尼亚工业和矿产部长科乔·蒂奥多西。
    阿尔巴尼亚方面参加签字仪式的还有阿尔巴尼亚政府经济代表团其他成员：国家计划委员会副主席普波·希提，建工部副部长拉赫曼·汉库，贸易部副部长瓦西里·卡蒂，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利和阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆商务参赞考兹玛等。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的还有：各有关部门负责人乔冠华、钟羽一、李强、谢怀德等。
    签字后，中阿两国同志热烈鼓掌，表示祝贺。
