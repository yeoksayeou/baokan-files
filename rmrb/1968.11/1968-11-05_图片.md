Title: Workers in Silver City Mechanical Repair Factory Condemn Liu Shaoqi's Crimes
Author:
Date: 1968-11-05
Edition: 3

After the publication of the communiqué of the Twelfth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, workers at the Silver City Mechanical Repair Factory immediately wrote big-character posters, condemning Liu Shaoqi and his accomplices for their treasonous acts against the Party and the nation.

	New China News Agency (Telephoto)
