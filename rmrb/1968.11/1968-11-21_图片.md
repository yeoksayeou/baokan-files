Title: Western European Sailors in Chinese Ports Celebrate the Great Victory of the Cultural Revolution
Author: Biàn Sòngdōng
Date: 1968-11-21
Edition: 6

Western European sailors staying in Chinese ports, upon hearing the news of the publication of the communiqué of the 12th Plenary Session of the 8th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, enthusiastically held up signs reading "Down with Liu Shaoqi" and joined in parades, jubilantly celebrating the great, decisive victory achieved in China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. (Photo by Bian Songdong, Xinhua News Agency)
