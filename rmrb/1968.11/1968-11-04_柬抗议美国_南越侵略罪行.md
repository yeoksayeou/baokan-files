Title: Cambodia Protests U.S.-South Vietnamese Aggression
Author:
Date: 1968-11-04
Edition: 5

According to a report by the New China News Agency in Phnom Penh, the Cambodian News Agency reported on October 31 that the Royal Government of Cambodia's Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent separate notes to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime on October 31, strongly protesting the U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops' planes. After killing a Cambodian resident with machine guns in the northern part of Borei Meas district, Prey Veng province, the planes continued to invade Cambodian airspace 20 times from September 8 to October 9 in Borei Meas province, Takeo province, Pursat province, Kampot province, and Kampong Cham province.

### 柬抗议美国—南越侵略罪行

1968-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    柬抗议美国—南越侵略罪行
    据新华社金边一日电　据柬埔寨新闻社一日报道，柬埔寨王国政府外交部十月三十一日分别照会美国政府和西贡傀儡集团，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军的飞机在波萝勉省北略白县用机枪杀害一名柬埔寨居民之后，又在九月八日到十月九日期间连续二十一次侵袭波萝勉省、柴桢省、磅湛省、茶胶省和干丹省的柬埔寨领空。
