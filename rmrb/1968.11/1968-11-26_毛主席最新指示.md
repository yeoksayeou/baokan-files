Title: Chairman Mao's Latest Directive
Author:
Date: 1968-11-26
Edition: 1

	Chairman Mao's Latest Directive

	History's lessons are worth noting. A line, a viewpoint, must be frequently and repeatedly explained. It is not enough to explain it to a few people; it must be made known to the broad revolutionary masses.

    毛主席最新指示
