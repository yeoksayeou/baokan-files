Title: Jakarta Has Become a Colonized City
Author:
Date: 1968-11-12
Edition: 6

Jakarta, under the control of the traitorous Suharto-Nasution regime, has become a colonized city.

Today's Jakarta is marked everywhere by the signs of a colonial city. Goods from the imperialist powers of the United States, Britain, the Netherlands, and Japan flood the markets of Jakarta. Eye-catching advertisements for the opening of American banks and other exploitative institutions, large advertisements for the dumping of goods by Western imperialist countries, are plastered all over the streets and alleys of Jakarta, and fill the advertising columns of the reactionary newspapers within the city. American imperialist films are widely shown in the cinemas of Jakarta. American imperialist pornographic publications are rampant on the newsstands of Jakarta. Under the control of the traitorous Suharto-Nasution regime, the radio stations of Jakarta broadcast American and British reactionary and decadent music all day long.

In the heart of Jakarta is the location of Suharto's "presidential palace." Right across from it is the American invasion command center in Indonesia—the U.S. Embassy in Indonesia and the recently opened branches of the American Express Bank, Citibank, and the American Bank of Commerce in Jakarta. These American institutions, in collusion with the traitorous policies of the Suharto-Nasution fascist regime, are busily engaged in their plundering and pillaging of the Indonesian people. The lairs of the representatives of the monopolistic capitalist groups of the United States, Britain, and other countries—concentrated in the Indonesia Hotel—are also near Suharto's "presidential palace." A group of capitalists representing more than forty large companies of the United States, Britain, and other countries, led by an American capitalist, have just held a so-called "round table conference" there to discuss plans for further dividing up Indonesia.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 雅加达已成殖民地化城市

1968-11-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　雅加达已成殖民地化城市
    在苏哈托—纳苏蒂安卖国政权控制下的印度尼西亚首都雅加达，已经沦落成为一座殖民地化的城市。
    今天的雅加达，到处都打上了一个殖民地城市的标记。美国、英国、荷兰、日本等帝国主义的商品，充斥雅加达市场。一些美国银行和其他吃人黑店开张营业的醒目启事，西方帝国主义国家倾销商品的大幅广告，张贴在雅加达的街头巷尾，并且塞满了市内各家反动报纸的广告栏。美帝国主义的电影在雅加达各电影院大肆上映。美帝国主义的黄色刊物充斥雅加达的报摊。在苏哈托—纳苏蒂安卖国政权控制下的雅加达电台，整天播送着美、英反动颓废的音乐。
    在雅加达市的中心，是苏哈托的“总统府”所在地。紧靠在它对面的，就是美国侵略印尼的现场指挥部——美国驻印尼大使馆和最近开张营业的美洲银行、花旗银行、美国捷运公司银行的雅加达分行。这些美国黑店在苏哈托—纳苏蒂安法西斯政权的卖国政策的配合下，正在紧张地进行着侵略和掠夺印尼民族的强盗活动。美、英等国垄断资本集团的各路代表人物在雅加达集中的巢穴——印尼旅馆，也在苏哈托“总统府”的附近。代表美、英等国垄断资本集团四十几个大公司的一批资本家，在一个美国资本家的率领下，刚刚在那里举行了一个所谓“圆桌会议”，讨论了进一步瓜分印尼的方案。
                                          （据新华社讯）
