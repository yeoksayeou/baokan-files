Title: Delegation from the University of Tirana Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1968-11-10
Edition: 5

The delegation from the University of Tirana, led by Deputy President Vehbi Shehu, concluded their friendly visit to China and departed Beijing by plane today to return home.

On the evening of the 8th, the President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Guo Moruo, hosted a banquet to warmly send off the Albanian comrades.

Ambassador Nataneli of Albania to China and other comrades were invited to attend the banquet.

### 地拉那大学代表团离京回国

1968-11-10
第5版()
专栏：

    地拉那大学代表团离京回国
    新华社九日讯　由副校长弗赫米·谢胡同志率领的阿尔巴尼亚国立地拉那大学代表团，结束了在我国的友好访问，于今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    八日晚，中国科学院院长郭沫若举行宴会，热烈欢送阿尔巴尼亚战友。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利等同志应邀出席了宴会。
