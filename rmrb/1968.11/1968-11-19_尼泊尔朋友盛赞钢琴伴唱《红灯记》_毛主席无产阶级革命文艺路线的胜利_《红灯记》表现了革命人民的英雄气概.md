Title: Nepalese Friends Praise Piano Accompanied "The Red Lantern"
Author:
Date: 1968-11-19
Edition: 5

The Chinese color documentary film "Piano Accompanied 'The Red Lantern'" has recently been screened in Nepal, receiving enthusiastic praise from Nepalese friends.

One Nepalese student, after watching the film, said that Chinese artists, following the teachings of the great leader Chairman Mao, have successfully created the piano-accompanied version of "The Red Lantern," which vividly portrays the heroic spirit of the revolutionary people. This is a victory for Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line in literature and art. He said, "We extend our warm congratulations to the Chinese artists."

Another young person said that the piano-accompanied "The Red Lantern" reflects the struggles of the Chinese people. It is an inspiration to revolutionary people.

Another Nepalese friend said that the piano-accompanied "The Red Lantern" brings revolutionary art to the broad masses of laboring people. Therefore, it is welcomed by the broad masses of laboring people.

He said that this film reflects the heroic deeds of the Chinese people in their struggle against imperialism and reactionaries. This is a great victory for Mao Zedong Thought and a great victory for the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

### 尼泊尔朋友盛赞钢琴伴唱《红灯记》　毛主席无产阶级革命文艺路线的胜利　《红灯记》表现了革命人民的英雄气概

1968-11-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　尼泊尔朋友盛赞钢琴伴唱《红灯记》
　　毛主席无产阶级革命文艺路线的胜利
　　《红灯记》表现了革命人民的英雄气概
    新华社加德满都十四日电　中国彩色纪录影片《钢琴伴唱〈红灯记〉》最近在尼泊尔放映，受到尼泊尔朋友的热烈赞扬。
    一位尼泊尔学生在看了电影之后说，中国的文艺工作者遵循伟大领袖毛主席的教导，成功地创作了钢琴伴唱《红灯记》，表现了革命人民的英雄气概。这是毛主席的无产阶级革命文艺路线的胜利。他说：“我们向中国的文艺工作者表示热烈的祝贺。”
    另一位青年说，钢琴伴唱《红灯记》反映了中国人民的斗争。它对革命人民是一种鼓舞。
    另一位尼泊尔朋友说，钢琴伴唱《红灯记》把革命文艺带给了广大的劳动人民。因此它受到广大劳动人民的欢迎。
    他说，这部影片反映了中国人民在反对帝国主义和反动派的斗争中的英勇事迹。这是毛泽东思想的伟大胜利，是无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利。
