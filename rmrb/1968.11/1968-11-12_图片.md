Title: Chinese Technicians Aid in Building Mali-Segou United Textile Mill
Author:
Date: 1968-11-12
Edition: 5

Chinese technical personnel assisting in the construction of the Mali-Segou United Textile Mill repeatedly and seriously study the works of Chairman Mao, determined to take Dr. Norman Bethune, an internationalist warrior, as their example, and wholeheartedly serve the people of Mali. The photo shows the first generation of textile workers in Mali eagerly learning textile technology.
