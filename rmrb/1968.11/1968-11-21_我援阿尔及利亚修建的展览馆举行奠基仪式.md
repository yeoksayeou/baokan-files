Title: Groundbreaking Ceremony for the Exhibition Hall Built by China in Algeria
Author:
Date: 1968-11-21
Edition: 6

The groundbreaking ceremony for the exhibition hall in Algiers, built with Chinese aid, was held on the afternoon of November 19. Houari Boumedienne, Chairman of the Algerian National Revolutionary Council and Prime Minister, presided over the ceremony and personally placed the foundation stone.

Attending the ceremony were prominent Algerian officials, including Ahmed Kaid Ahmed, a leader of the National Liberation Front, and several government ministers.

Chen Jinlong, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy, and Chinese technical personnel were invited to attend the ceremony. Several diplomats from other countries stationed in Algeria also attended the groundbreaking ceremony.

### 我援阿尔及利亚修建的展览馆举行奠基仪式

1968-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

    我援阿尔及利亚修建的展览馆举行奠基仪式
    新华社阿尔及尔十九日电　中国援助阿尔及利亚修建的阿尔及尔展览馆十九日下午举行了奠基仪式。阿尔及利亚全国革命委员会主席兼总理布迈丁主持了奠基仪式，并亲自安放了奠基石。
    出席奠基仪式的有：阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线负责人卡伊德·艾哈迈德和许多政府部长等阿尔及利亚高级官员。
    中国大使馆临时代办陈晋龙和中国技术人员应邀出席了这个仪式。一些国家驻阿尔及利亚的使节也出席了奠基仪式。
