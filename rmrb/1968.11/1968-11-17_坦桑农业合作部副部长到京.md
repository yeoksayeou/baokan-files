Title: Deputy Minister of Tanzania's Ministry of Agriculture and Cooperatives Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-11-17
Edition: 6

Deputy Minister of Tanzania's Ministry of Agriculture and Cooperatives and Chairman of the Cotton Marketing Board, Kihamba, and his party arrived in Beijing by airplane this afternoon.

Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Zhou Huamin, and others went to the airport to welcome them. Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Mwaluko, and others also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 坦桑农业合作部副部长到京

1968-11-17
第6版()
专栏：

　　坦桑农业合作部副部长到京
    新华社十六日讯　坦桑尼亚农业合作部副部长兼棉花销售局主席基哈姆巴等于今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    对外贸易部副部长周化民等到机场迎接。坦桑尼亚驻华大使姆瓦卢科等也到机场迎接。
