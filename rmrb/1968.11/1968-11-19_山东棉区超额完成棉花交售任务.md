Title: Shandong Cotton Region Exceeds Cotton Delivery Targets
Author:
Date: 1968-11-19
Edition: 3

Inspired by the communiqué of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party, the poor and lower-middle peasants in the cotton-growing areas of Shandong Province have enthusiastically delivered new cotton to the state. By November 10, the province had completed 6.4% more than the cotton delivery target, an increase of 12.9% compared to the same period last year.

This year, Shandong experienced a severe drought. However, under the guidance of Chairman Mao's teachings on "grasp revolution, promote production" and "cotton must be prioritized," the broad masses of poor and lower-middle peasants in the cotton-growing areas of the entire province, taking Dazhai as their example, fought against nature, managed the fields meticulously, and achieved a good cotton harvest this year. After the new cotton matured, various places held Mao Zedong Thought study classes, closely linking them with the class struggle in their respective regions and units. They fiercely criticized the traitor, spy, and scoundrel Liu Shaoqi and his agents in various places who promoted various counter-revolutionary revisionist black goods, quickly launching a high tide of delivering new cotton. By the end of October, the Changwei region had already exceeded the state's procurement plan, with an increase of 32% compared to the same period last year.

Currently, the broad masses of poor and lower-middle peasants in the cotton-growing areas of Shandong are continuing to launch a high tide of late-season cotton picking and delivery, determined to make greater contributions to the comprehensive victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

### 山东棉区超额完成棉花交售任务

1968-11-19
第3版()
专栏：

    山东棉区超额完成棉花交售任务
    在党的八届扩大的十二中全会公报的鼓舞下，山东棉区的广大贫下中农踊跃向国家交售新棉。十一月十日止，全省已超额百分之六点四完成了棉花交售任务，比去年同期收购量增加百分之十二点九。
    山东今年遭到干旱，全省棉区广大贫下中农，在毛主席“抓革命，促生产”和“必须把棉花抓紧”的伟大教导指引下，以大寨为榜样，战天斗地，精心管理，夺得了今年棉花的好收成。新棉成熟后，各地纷纷举办毛泽东思想学习班，紧密结合本地区、本单位阶级斗争的实际，狠批了叛徒、内奸、工贼刘少奇及其在各地代理人推行的各种反革命修正主义黑货，迅速掀起了交售新棉的高潮。昌潍地区到十月底就已超额完成国家收购计划，比去年同期收购量增加百分之三十二。
    目前，山东棉区的广大贫下中农，正继续掀起棉花后期采摘和交售高潮，决心为夺取无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利做出更大贡献。
