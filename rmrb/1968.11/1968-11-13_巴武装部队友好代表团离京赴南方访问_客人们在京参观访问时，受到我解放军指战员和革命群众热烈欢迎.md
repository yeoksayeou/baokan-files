Title: Pakistani Military Friendship Delegation Leaves Beijing for Southern China
Author:
Date: 1968-11-13
Edition: 5

By General Yahya Khan, Commander-in-Chief of the Pakistani Army, leading a Pakistani military friendship delegation, accompanied by Vice Minister of National Defense Xiao Jinguang, departed Beijing by special plane this morning to visit southern China.

General Huang Yongsheng, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and responsible personnel from the General Staff Department, General Logistics Department, various military branches, and relevant departments, including Li Tianyou, Wu Ruilin, Tan Jieshu, Han Nianlong, Xiang Qian, and Ding Xilin, as well as more than a thousand soldiers, militia, and Red Guards from the PLA and the capital, gathered at the airport to enthusiastically see off the Pakistani guests.

Colonel Kayum, the military attaché of the Pakistani Embassy in China, accompanied the delegation to visit southern China.

Ambassador Sultan Muhammad Khan of Pakistan to China, Colonel Haida, the air attaché of the Pakistani Embassy, and military attachés from several countries' embassies in China also came to the airport to see them off.

The Pakistani military friendship delegation, led by General Yahya Khan, Commander-in-Chief of the Pakistani Army, accompanied by Vice Minister of National Defense Xiao Jinguang, visited and inspected the Beijing units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, certain units of the Air Force, and the Beijing Knitting Factory during their stay in Beijing. The guests were warmly welcomed by the soldiers and workers wherever they went.

Ambassador Sultan Muhammad Khan of Pakistan to China accompanied the delegation to visit the Beijing Knitting Factory. Colonel Haida and Colonel Kayum, military attachés of the Pakistani Embassy in China, also accompanied the delegation to visit the military units and factories.

The Pakistani military friendship delegation also visited and toured the Shisanling Reservoir and the Great Wall.

### 巴武装部队友好代表团离京赴南方访问　客人们在京参观访问时，受到我解放军指战员和革命群众热烈欢迎

1968-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　巴武装部队友好代表团离京赴南方访问
　　客人们在京参观访问时，受到我解放军指战员和革命群众热烈欢迎
    新华社十二日讯　由巴基斯坦陆军总司令叶海亚·汗上将率领的巴基斯坦武装部队友好代表团，在我国国防部副部长萧劲光陪同下，今天上午乘专机离开北京，前往我国南方参观访问。
    中国人民解放军总参谋长黄永胜，总参谋部、总后勤部、各军种、兵种和有关方面负责人李天佑、吴瑞林、谭家述、韩念龙、向前、丁西林等，以及中国人民解放军指战员、首都民兵、红卫兵小将一千多人到机场热烈欢送巴基斯坦客人。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆武官卡尤姆上校，随同代表团前往我国南方参观访问。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗，大使馆空军武官海达上校，也到机场送行。到机场送行的，还有一些国家驻我国大使馆武官。
    新华社十二日讯　巴基斯坦陆军总司令叶海亚·汗上将率领的巴基斯坦武装部队友好代表团，由我国国防部副部长萧劲光陪同，在北京先后参观访问了中国人民解放军北京部队某部、空军部队某部和北京针织总厂。客人们所到之处，受到了人民解放军指战员和工人群众的热烈欢迎。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗随同代表团到北京针织总厂参观。随同代表团到部队、工厂参观的还有巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆武官海达上校和卡尤姆上校。
    巴基斯坦武装部队友好代表团还参观游览了十三陵水库和长城。
