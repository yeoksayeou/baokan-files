Title: Notice from the Twelfth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1968-11-09
Edition: 1

The plenary session calls on all Party members and the people of the entire country to continue to carry out the great criticism in depth and thoroughly purge the reactionary revisionist ideology of the anti-revolutionary revisionist clique headed by Liu Shaoqi, who are the biggest group of capitalist roaders within the Party.
