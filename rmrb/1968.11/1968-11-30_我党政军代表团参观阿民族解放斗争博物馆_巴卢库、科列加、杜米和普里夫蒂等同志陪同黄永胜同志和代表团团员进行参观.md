Title: Chinese Party, Government, and Military Delegation Visits Albanian National Liberation Struggle Museum
Author:
Date: 1968-11-30
Edition: 4

Chinese Party, Government, and Military Delegation Visits Albanian National Liberation Struggle Museum

	Baluke, Kolega, Dumi, and Priviti Accompany Comrade Huang Yongsheng and Delegation Members on the Visit

	NEW YORK, November 28 (Xinhua) — A delegation from the Chinese Communist Party, government, and military, led by Comrade Huang Yongsheng, a member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army, visited the Albanian National Liberation Struggle Museum in Tirana on the morning of the 28th.

	Comrades Bekir Baluku, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Defense, Spiro Kolega, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Labor Party and Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Petrit Dumi, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Labor Party and Chief of the General Staff of the People's Army, and Koco Priviti, Director of the Protocol Department of the Albanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied Comrade Huang Yongsheng and the delegation members on the visit.

	Comrade Hito Cako, member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and Director of the Political Department of the People's Army, Fadil Pacrami, member of the Central Committee of the Labor Party and Secretary of the Tirana District Committee, Sabri Pirkati, Deputy Chairman of the Executive Committee of the People's Assembly of the Tirana District, and the Director of the Museum, Barkiç Haxhisejdini, warmly welcomed the Chinese Party, Government, and Military Delegation at the museum entrance.

	Many Albanian workers, officials, and residents gathered at the museum entrance, shouting slogans such as "Enver-Hoxha" and "Friendship is the guarantee," and enthusiastically applauding to express their warm welcome to the delegation.

	During the visit, the museum director gave a detailed account to the Chinese comrades of the glorious history of the Albanian people's national liberation struggle under the leadership of the glorious Albanian Communist Party (the predecessor of the Albanian Labor Party) and Comrade Hoxha.

	The Chinese Party, Government, and Military Delegation presented a bamboo curtain with Chairman Mao's poems to the museum comrades during the visit. The museum director, Barkiç Haxhisejdini, also presented gifts to the Chinese comrades.

	After the visit, the Chinese Party, Government, and Military Delegation wrote in the guest book: "Long live the heroic Albanian people and the glorious Albanian Labor Party!"

	Li Tingqian, Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, and Military Attache Li Zhongxuan also accompanied the delegation on the visit.

### 我党政军代表团参观阿民族解放斗争博物馆  巴卢库、科列加、杜米和普里夫蒂等同志陪同黄永胜同志和代表团团员进行参观

1968-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我党政军代表团参观阿民族解放斗争博物馆
    巴卢库、科列加、杜米和普里夫蒂等同志陪同黄永胜同志和代表团团员进行参观
    新华社地拉那二十八日电　由中国共产党中央委员、中国人民解放军总参谋长黄永胜同志率领的中国党政军代表团，二十八日上午在地拉那参观了阿尔巴尼亚民族解放斗争博物馆。
    陪同黄永胜同志和代表团团员进行参观的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库，劳动党中央政治局委员、国家计划委员会主席斯皮罗·科列加，劳动党中央政治局候补委员、人民军总参谋长彼得里特·杜米和阿尔巴尼亚外交部礼宾司司长科乔·普里夫蒂。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、人民军政治部主任希托·恰科，劳动党中央委员、地拉那区委书记法迪尔·帕奇拉米，地拉那区人民会议执行委员会副主席萨布里·皮尔卡蒂和博物馆馆长巴尔基兹·哈奇希赛尼在博物馆门前热情迎接中国党政军代表团。
    许多阿尔巴尼亚工人、机关干部和居民聚集在博物馆门前，高呼：“恩维尔—毛泽东”“友谊就是保证”等口号，并热烈鼓掌，向代表团表示热忱的欢迎。
    参观时，博物馆馆长向中国同志详细讲述了英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民在光荣的阿尔巴尼亚共产党
  （阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的前身）和霍查同志的领导下所进行的民族解放斗争的光辉历程。
    中国党政军代表团在参观时向博物馆的同志赠送了印有毛主席诗词的竹帘。博物馆馆长巴尔基兹·哈奇希赛尼也向中国同志赠送了礼物。
    参观后，中国党政军代表团在留言簿上题词：“英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民和光荣的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党万岁！”
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃和武官李钟玄也陪同代表团进行了参观。
