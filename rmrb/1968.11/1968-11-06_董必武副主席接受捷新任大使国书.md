Title: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu Accepts Credentials from New Czechoslovakian Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1968-11-06
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman Dong Biwu of the People's Republic of China today accepted the credentials from Stanislav Kohout, the new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic to China.

Vice Chairman Dong Biwu had a friendly conversation with Ambassador Kohout.

Present at the ceremony were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua and others.

### 董必武副主席接受捷新任大使国书

1968-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    董必武副主席接受捷新任大使国书
    新华社五日讯　中华人民共和国副主席董必武，今天接受了捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国新任驻中国特命全权大使斯坦尼斯拉夫·柯浩赛克递交的国书。
    董必武副主席同柯浩赛克大使进行了友好的谈话。
    接受国书时在场的有外交部副部长乔冠华等。
