Title: Youths from Liaoning's Xinjin County Double Tower Commune Learn from Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants
Author:
Date: 1968-11-02
Edition: 4

Youths from Dalian City who have gone to the countryside in Xinjin County's Double Tower Commune, West Tower Production Brigade, humbly learn from poor and lower-middle peasants. They carry with them class feelings to study and apply Chairman Mao's works. They humbly learn production knowledge from them and contribute their strength to building socialism.
