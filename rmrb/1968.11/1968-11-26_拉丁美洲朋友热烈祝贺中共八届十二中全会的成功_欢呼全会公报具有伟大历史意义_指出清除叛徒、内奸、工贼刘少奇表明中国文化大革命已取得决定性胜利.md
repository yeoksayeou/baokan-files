Title: Latin American Friends Warmly Congratulate the Success of the 12th Plenary Session of the 8th Central Committee of the CPC
Author:
Date: 1968-11-26
Edition: 6

Some Latin American friends have warmly praised the communiqué of the enlarged 12th Plenary Session of the 8th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China as a document of great historical significance.

A Latin American friend said: The convening of the enlarged 12th Plenary Session of the 8th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the publication of the communiqué by this session are of great historical significance. The communiqué formally announces the expulsion of the traitor, spy, and capitalist-roader Liu Shaoqi from the Party forever and the revocation of all his positions within and outside the Party. This indicates that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China has already achieved a decisive victory.

He said, the communiqué points out that in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China, “the focus of the struggle is the question of power, the question of who holds the leadership, whether it is in the hands of Marxists or revisionists.” The experience of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is not only of great significance to the Chinese revolution but also of great significance to the Latin American revolution. Therefore, we Latin American revolutionaries must seriously study and propagate the experience of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China.

A South American friend said: The communiqué of the enlarged 12th Plenary Session of the 8th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China marks the victory of Chairman Mao’s proletarian revolutionary line, marking the victory of the Chinese proletariat over the bourgeoisie and all other exploiting classes. He quoted Chairman Mao’s words: “The working class must lead everything.”

He said, this is of great significance for consolidating the dictatorship of the proletariat in China and preventing the restoration of capitalism.

A Portuguese friend residing in Cuba said: The communiqué issued by this plenary session provides a profound analysis of the current international situation, pointing out the direction and path for revolutionaries around the world. Therefore, revolutionaries of all countries should seriously study this document of great historical significance.

### 拉丁美洲朋友热烈祝贺中共八届十二中全会的成功  欢呼全会公报具有伟大历史意义  指出清除叛徒、内奸、工贼刘少奇表明中国文化大革命已取得决定性胜利

1968-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲朋友热烈祝贺中共八届十二中全会的成功
    欢呼全会公报具有伟大历史意义
    指出清除叛徒、内奸、工贼刘少奇表明中国文化大革命已取得决定性胜利
    新华社哈瓦那二十二日电　一些拉丁美洲朋友热烈赞扬中国共产党第八届扩大的第十二次中央委员会全会公报是一项具有伟大历史意义的文件。
    一位拉丁美洲朋友说：中国共产党第八届扩大的十二中全会的召开以及这次全会发表的公报，具有伟大的历史意义。公报正式宣布把埋藏在中国共产党内的叛徒、内奸、工贼刘少奇永远开除出党，并撤消其党内外的一切职务。这表明，中国无产阶级文化大革命已经取得了决定性的胜利。
    他说，公报指出，在中国无产阶级文化大革命中，“斗争的中心，是政权问题，是无产阶级和资产阶级争夺领导权问题，是党和国家的领导权掌握在马克思主义者或修正主义者手里的问题。”中国无产阶级文化大革命的经验不仅对中国革命具有重大意义，对于拉丁美洲革命也具有重大意义。因此，我们拉丁美洲革命者必须认真学习和宣传中国无产阶级文化大革命的经验。
    一位南美洲朋友说：中国共产党第八届扩大的十二中全会公报标志着毛主席的无产阶级革命路线的胜利，标志着中国无产阶级对资产阶级和其他一切剥削阶级的胜利。他引用了毛主席的话：“工人阶级必须领导一切”。
    他说，这对巩固中国无产阶级专政、防止资本主义复辟具有重大的意义。
    一位旅居古巴的葡萄牙朋友说：这次全会发表的公报对当前的国际形势进行了精辟的分析，为全世界的革命者指出了革命的道路和方向，因此，各国的革命者都应当认真学习这一具有伟大历史意义的文件。
