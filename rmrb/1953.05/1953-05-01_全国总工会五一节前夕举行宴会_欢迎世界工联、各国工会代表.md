Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Holds Banquet on Eve of May Day to Welcome WFTU and Trade Union Representatives from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1953-05-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 30th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions held a banquet on the eve of May Day to welcome the World Federation of Trade Unions delegation, trade union delegations and trade union representatives from various countries who came to China to participate in the May Day International Labor Day celebration and the Seventh National Congress of the Chinese Trade Union. Liu Shaoqi, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, attended the banquet.

Invited guests included: the World Federation of Trade Unions delegation headed by Louis Saillant, the Soviet Trade Union delegation headed by Goroshkin, the Korean Trade Union delegation headed by Fang Taiyu, the Vietnamese Trade Union delegation headed by Chen Bao, the Polish Trade Union delegation headed by Wojas, the Czechoslovak Trade Union delegation headed by Trojanova, the German Democratic Republic Trade Union delegation headed by Kommerle, the Romanian Trade Union delegation headed by Nicolae, the Hungarian Trade Union delegation headed by Zentor, the Bulgarian Trade Union delegation headed by Ivanov, Indian trade union representatives Yusuf, Sibnath Bannerjee, Sudin Kumar Pramanik and Guruswami, the Indonesian Trade Union delegation headed by Zhu Yiduo, the Burmese Trade Union delegation headed by Deqinla, the Ceylonese Trade Union delegation headed by Mandis, the Japanese Trade Union delegation headed by Kinuko Kenta, the Australian Trade Union delegation headed by Dawson, and French trade union representative Dufriche, and others.

The Burmese Government Labor Inspection Group, which is visiting our country, was invited to attend the banquet.

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions was represented by: Vice-Chairmen Zhu Xuefan and Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General Lai Ruoyu, executive members of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and heads of various departments and national industrial trade unions, Wu Yuzhang, Liu Changsheng, Chen Shaomin, and Li Jiebo.

Fifty delegates to the Seventh National Congress of the Chinese Trade Union in Beijing also attended the banquet.

Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a welcoming speech at the meeting: Dear guests! On the occasion of the great day of international solidarity of working people—the advent of the May Day Labor Day—please allow me, on behalf of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the men and women workers of the whole country, to extend a warm welcome and congratulations to all the representatives who have been invited to China to participate in the May Day Commemoration Conference and the Seventh National Congress of the Chinese Trade Union. I wish trade union workers and worker brothers in Asia, Australia, and all countries of the world greater victories in the cause of striving for improved living standards, peace, democracy, and national independence and liberation. Long live the great unity of the working class of all countries in the world! Long live May Day International Labor Day!

Louis Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions, then delivered a speech: We are very honored to be able to participate in the Chinese May Day International Labor Day celebration and the Seventh National Congress of the Chinese Trade Union. The whole world is rejoicing in China's achievements. China's achievements in national construction are a great contribution to the cause of defending world peace and are of great significance to the world situation. Under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and with the help of Soviet experience, the Chinese people will surely achieve further achievements. Let us toast the great victory of the Chinese people and the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong.

The banquet proceeded in a cordial and friendly atmosphere. During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted the great unity of the international working class.

After the meeting, the Chinese documentary "Be Sure to Harness the Huai River Well" was shown to entertain trade union representatives from various countries.

### 全国总工会五一节前夕举行宴会  欢迎世界工联、各国工会代表

1953-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    全国总工会五一节前夕举行宴会
    欢迎世界工联、各国工会代表
    【新华社三十日讯】中华全国总工会在“五一”节前夕举行宴会，欢迎来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝典礼和中国工会第七次全国代表大会的世界工会联合会代表团和各国工会代表团及工会代表。中华全国总工会名誉主席刘少奇参加了宴会。
    应邀出席的有：以路易·赛扬为首的世界工会联合会代表团，以戈罗希金为首的苏联工会代表团，以方泰郁为首的朝鲜工 团，以陈保为首的越南工会代表团，以沃雅斯为首的波兰工会代表团，以特罗雅诺娃为首的捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团，以孔默勒为首的德意志民主共和国工会代表团，以尼古拉为首的罗马尼亚工会代表团，以曾托尔为首的匈牙利工会代表团，以伊凡诺夫为首的保加利亚工会代表团，优素福、西布纳特·班纳吉、苏丁·库马尔·普拉马尼克和古鲁斯瓦米等印度工会代表，以朱义多为首的印尼工会代表团，以德钦拉最为首的缅甸工会代表团，以曼迪斯为首的锡兰工会代表团，以金子健太为首的日本工会代表团，以道逊为首的澳大利亚工会代表团，法国工会代表杜弗里希等。
    前来我国访问的缅甸政府劳动考察团应邀出席了宴会。
    中华全国总工会出席的有：副主席朱学范、刘宁一，秘书长赖若愚，中华全国总工会执行委员、各部门和各全国性产业工会负责人吴玉章、刘长胜、陈少敏、李颉伯等人。
    参加宴会的还有：来京出席中国工会第七次全国代表大会的代表五十人。
    中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一在会上致欢迎辞说：各位亲爱的来宾们！在此劳动人民国际团结的伟大日子——“五一”劳动节降临之际，请允许我代表中华全国总工会和全国的男女工人们，向各位应邀来到中国参加“五一”纪念大会和中国工会第七次全国代表大会的代表们致以热烈的欢迎与祝贺。谨祝亚洲澳洲以及全世界各国工会工作者和工人兄弟们，在争取生活改善、争取和平、民主与民族独立解放的事业中获得更大的胜利。全世界各国工人阶级大团结万岁！五一国际劳动节万岁！
    世界工联总书记路易·赛扬接着致辞说：我们能够参加中国“五一”国际劳动节庆祝典礼和中国工会第七次全国代表大会，感到很大的荣幸。全世界都为中国的成就而欢欣鼓舞。中国在国家建设方面的成就，对于保卫世界和平事业是一个伟大的贡献，对于世界形势具有伟大的意义。中国人民在毛泽东主席的英明领导下，在苏联经验的帮助下，必将获得进一步的成就。让我们为中国人民的伟大胜利、为中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席而干杯。
    宴会在亲切友好的空气中进行。席间宾主频频为国际工人阶级的伟大团结而干杯。
    会后并放映中国纪录片“一定要把淮河修好”招待各国工会代表。
