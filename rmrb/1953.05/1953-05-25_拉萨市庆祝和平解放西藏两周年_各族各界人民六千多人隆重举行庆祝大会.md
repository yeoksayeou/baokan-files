Title: Lhasa Celebrates the Second Anniversary of the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet; Over 6,000 People from All Ethnic Groups and All Walks of Life Grandly Hold Celebration Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-05-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 24th:

    The people of all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Lhasa city held a grand meeting at the Zhongji Linka on the banks of the Lhasa River on the afternoon of the 23rd to celebrate the second anniversary of the signing of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet. Over 6,000 people attended the celebration, including officials of the Tibetan local government, the Tibetan local army, the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Lhasa, and citizens of all ethnic groups in Lhasa.
    The meeting was presided over by Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, a Kalon of the Tibetan local government, who reported on the significance of the meeting. Subsequently, representatives from the Tibetan local government, the Panchen Kanpo Lija, the three major monasteries of Tibet, the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association, the preparatory committee of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Association, and the Lhasa Primary School presented Hada scarves and flowers to General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, and the representatives of the Tibetan local government who signed the peaceful agreement.
    General Zhang Jingwu and General Tan Guansan, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, in their speeches, outlined the great achievements made in various aspects of the implementation of the agreement over the past two years. They then called on all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Tibet and all monks and lay people of Tibet to continue their efforts on the basis of the achievements already made, to further strengthen the unity between the Tibetan and Han nationalities and within the Tibetan nationality, to strengthen the combination of internationalism and patriotism, to strictly guard against all kinds of conspiracies and sabotage by imperialism, and to strive for the gradual and complete realization of the agreement and achieve greater success and greater victory.
    Kalon Ranba Tubdain Goinkhyen, representative of the Tibetan local government, said in his speech: The signing of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet is a great victory for Chairman Mao and the ethnic equality policy of the Central People's Government, and also a victory for the Tibetan people. It has brought infinite light and a happy future to the Tibetan people. He said: Over the past two years, the monks and lay people of Tibet have fully felt the warmth of the big family of the motherland and have vigorously supported the People's Liberation Army in Tibet in consolidating and building the national defense of the motherland. In the future, they will work even harder to fully assist in the thorough implementation of the agreement.
    Then, Yutok-tsemon, representative of the Panchen Erdeni, spoke. He said: Chairman Mao's great policy of ethnic equality and the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet have rescued the Tibetan people from the suffering of imperialist enslavement and oppression. We should joyfully celebrate this great festival. Over the past two years, thanks to the correct leadership of Chairman Mao, the great leader of all ethnic groups, and the Central People's Government, and the close unity of the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni, Tibet's economic and cultural undertakings have developed rapidly. In the future, we, the Tibetan people, must unite more closely with the fraternal nationalities in the great family of the motherland and, under the leadership of Chairman Mao, build Tibet into a happy and beautiful paradise.
    Representatives from the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association and the preparatory committee of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Association also spoke at the meeting. The celebration meeting proceeded for three hours amidst warm applause and slogans. Finally, the meeting concluded amidst loud slogans in both Tibetan and Chinese languages. People stood up and shouted: "Build a prosperous and happy new Tibet, consolidate national defense!" "Long live Chairman Mao, the great leader of all nationalities in China!"

### 拉萨市庆祝和平解放西藏两周年  各族各界人民六千多人隆重举行庆祝大会

1953-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    拉萨市庆祝和平解放西藏两周年
    各族各界人民六千多人隆重举行庆祝大会
    【新华社拉萨二十四日电】拉萨市各族各界人民庆祝关于和平解放西藏办法的协议签订两周年纪念大会，二十三日下午在拉萨河畔的仲机林卡隆重举行。参加庆祝大会的有西藏地方政府官员、西藏地方军队、中国人民解放军驻拉萨部队和拉萨市各族市民共六千多人。
    大会由西藏地方政府噶伦阿沛·阿旺晋美任主席并报告开会意义。接着由西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅、西藏三大寺、拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会、拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会筹备委员会和拉萨市小学校的代表，分别向中央人民政府代表张经武将军和签订和平协议的西藏地方政府代表们敬献哈达和鲜花。
    张经武将军和中国人民解放军西藏军区政治委员谭冠三将军在讲话中概述了两年来执行协议在各方面获得的伟大成就后，号召人民解放军进藏部队的全体指挥员、战斗员和西藏全体僧俗人民，在既得成绩的基础上，继续努力，进一步加强藏、汉民族和西藏民族内部的团结，加强国际主义和爱国主义相结合的教育，严防帝国主义的各种阴谋破坏，为逐步和全部实现协议而努力，并取得更大的成就和更大的胜利。
    西藏地方政府代表噶伦然巴·图丹滚清在讲话中说：和平解放西藏办法的协议的签订，是毛主席和中央人民政府民族平等政策的伟大胜利，也是西藏民族的胜利，它给西藏人民带来了无限光明和幸福的前途。他说：两年来，西藏的僧俗人民已经充分体会到祖国大家庭的温暖，曾大力支援进藏人民解放军巩固和建设祖国国防，今后将更加努力全力协助，以求协议的彻底实现。
    继由班禅额尔德尼的代表有德先生讲话。他说：毛主席伟大的民族平等政策和和平解放西藏办法的协议，把西藏人民从帝国主义奴役、压迫的痛苦中解救出来了，我们应当欢乐地庆祝这个伟大的节日。两年来，由于各族人民伟大领袖毛主席和中央人民政府的正确领导，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的亲密团结，西藏的经济、文化事业都有了迅速的发展，今后我们西藏人民必须和祖国大家庭中的各兄弟民族，更加紧密地团结起来，在毛主席的领导下，把西藏建设成幸福美丽的乐园。
    拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会和拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会筹备委员会代表都在大会上讲了话。庆祝大会在热烈的掌声和口号声中进行了三个小时，最后，大会在藏、汉两种语言的雄亮口号声中结束，人们起立高呼：“建设繁荣幸福的新西藏，巩固祖国国防！”“中国各民族伟大领袖毛主席万岁！”
