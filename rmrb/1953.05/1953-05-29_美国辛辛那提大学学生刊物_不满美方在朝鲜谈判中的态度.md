Title: University of Cincinnati Student Publication Dissatisfied with U.S. Attitude in Korean Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1953-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 28th: The University of Cincinnati student publication "Cincinnati News Record" recently published an editorial criticizing the United States' attitude in the Korean armistice negotiations. The editorial stated, "Negotiations are... a process of mutual concession... We are not now negotiating from a position of strength. We are not victors laying down the law to our defeated and submissive enemies."

### 美国辛辛那提大学学生刊物  不满美方在朝鲜谈判中的态度

1953-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    美国辛辛那提大学学生刊物
    不满美方在朝鲜谈判中的态度
    【新华社布拉格二十八日电】美国辛辛那提大学学生刊物“辛辛那提新闻纪录报”最近发表社论，批评美国在朝鲜停战谈判中的态度。社论中说，“谈判是……彼此让步的过程……现在我们不是从有力的地位来进行谈判的。我们不是给我们的被击败而屈服了的敌人规定法律的胜利者。”
