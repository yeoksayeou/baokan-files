Title: Celebrating the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1953-05-08
Page: 4
 
    Telegram of Congratulations from the Central Committee of the Korean Federation of Trade Unions
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd] The Central Committee of the Korean Federation of Trade Unions sent the following telegram of congratulations to the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions: To the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions:
    The Central Committee of the Korean Federation of Trade Unions, on behalf of all Korean workers, technicians, and staff members, extends warm congratulations to the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, convened to further unite the fighting ranks of the Chinese workers and ensure the completion of the First Five-Year Plan.
    The Korean working class expresses heartfelt gratitude to the fraternal Chinese working class, who have held high the banner of resisting America and aiding Korea and warmly supported our great motherland's war of liberation with the movement of increasing production and practicing economy. The Korean working class is determined to unite more closely around its leader, the Korean Workers' Party, and the beloved leader Marshal Kim Il-sung, to defeat the armed aggression of American and British imperialism in response to the fraternal friendship and assistance of the Chinese working class. The Central Committee of the Korean Federation of Trade Unions respectfully salutes the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, which has historical significance in the history of the Chinese trade union movement, and wishes the congress success.
              The Central Committee of the Korean Federation of Trade Unions
                Pyongyang, May 1, 1953
    Telegram of Congratulations from the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 7th] Hoang Quoc Viet, Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, extended warm congratulations to the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions.
    The telegram stated: The Vietnamese working people are deeply moved by the great achievements made by the Chinese workers and people in the past few years. These achievements have promoted the construction of New China and also inspired the revolutionary movement of the Vietnamese people who are carrying out arduous resistance against the French aggressors and American interventionists.
    The telegram believes that: The convening of this congress, just as the people of China are striving to implement large-scale construction plans, eagerly anticipating the convening of the National People's Congress, and continuing the movement to resist America and aid Korea, is proof of the strength of the Chinese working class in building a new China and striving for world peace.
    This congress will not only provide valuable lessons for the Chinese labor movement but also provide valuable lessons for the labor movements of Vietnam and other countries in Asia and around the world.
    Telegram of Congratulations from the Central Council of Albanian Trade Unions
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 7th] The Central Council of Albanian Trade Unions sent a telegram to congratulate the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions. The telegram said:
    On behalf of the working class of the People's Republic of Albania, we extend warm greetings to the representatives of the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, and through you, extend warm greetings to all the Chinese worker brothers. We sincerely wish your conference a complete success. This congress will make a major contribution to the heroic Chinese workers' efforts to strengthen the construction of New Democracy in your great country, and to the struggle to win peace and destroy the plans of warmongers.
    Long live the great Chinese people under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party headed by Comrade Mao Zedong!
    Long live the powerful peace camp headed by the glorious and invincible Soviet people!
    Long live the unbreakable friendship between the peoples of our two countries!
            Central Council of Albanian Trade Unions
                    Chairman Pirišteli
    Telegram of Congratulations from the All India Postal and Telegraph Workers Union
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 7th] The All India Postal and Telegraph Workers Union sent a telegram to the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions:
    Congratulating the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions on its brilliant achievements, the Indian postal and telegraph workers extend their warmest greetings.
              All India Postal and Telegraph Workers Union Ramamurti
                        May 1st
    Telegram of Congratulations from the All-Indonesia Trade Union Federation
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 7th] The All-Indonesia Trade Union Federation sent a telegram to the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions: All-China Federation of Trade Unions:
    The All-Indonesia Trade Union Federation, representing 150,000 workers in approximately one hundred trade union organizations under its umbrella, is greatly concerned with the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions to be convened by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions this May.
        Central Committee of the All-Indonesia Trade Union Federation
                      First Secretary Fatah
    Telegram of Congratulations from the Surabaya Sugar Industry Workers Union Federation of Indonesia
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 7th] The Surabaya Sugar Industry Workers Union Federation of Indonesia sent a telegram to congratulate the convening of the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions. The telegram said:
    On behalf of 45,000 members, we extend our congratulations to the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions and extend warm greetings to the struggle of the Chinese workers and the Chinese people to build a new China and safeguard world peace.
            Surabaya Sugar Industry Workers Union Federation of Indonesia
            Secretary-General Haidi Siswojo

### 庆祝中国工会第七次全国代表大会

1953-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国工会第七次全国代表大会
    朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会的贺电
    【新华社二日讯】朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会给中国工会第七次全国代表大会的贺电如下：中国工会第七次全国代表大会：
    朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会，代表朝鲜全体工人、技术人员、职员，向为进一步团结中国工人的战斗队伍、保证第一个五年计划完成而召开的中国工会第七次全国代表大会，致以热烈的祝贺。
    朝鲜工人阶级对高举着抗美援朝的旗帜、以增产节约运动热诚地支援我们伟大祖国解放战争的、兄弟般的中国工人阶级，表示衷心的感谢。朝鲜工人阶级，决心更加紧密地团结在自己的领导者——朝鲜劳动党和敬爱的领袖金日成元帅周围，战胜美英帝国主义的武装侵略，以回答中国工人阶级对我们兄弟般的友谊和援助。朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会，谨向在中国工会运动史上具有历史意义的中国工会第七次全国代表大会致以崇高的敬礼，并祝大会成功。
              朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会
                一九五三年五月一日平壤
    越南总工会的贺电
    【新华社七日讯】越南总工会主席黄国越向中国工会第七次全国代表大会表示热烈的祝贺。
    贺电中说：越南劳动人民对于中国的工人和人民在过去几年中获得的伟大成就非常感动。这些成就促进了新中国的建设，同时也鼓舞了对法国侵略者和美国干涉者进行艰苦抗战的越南人民的革命运动。
    贺电认为：正当中国全国人民努力执行大规模建设计划，热情地期待全国人民代表大会的召开，继续进行抗美援朝运动的时候，举行了这次大会，这是中国工人阶级在建设新中国和争取世界和平斗争中壮大的证明。
    这次大会不仅会给中国的工人运动提供宝贵的教训，而且也会给越南以及亚洲和全世界的其他国家的工人运动提供宝贵的教训。
    阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会的贺电
    【新华社七日讯】阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会来电致贺中国工会第七次全国代表大会，贺电说：
    我们代表阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的工人阶级向中国工会第七次全国代表大会的代表致以热烈的敬礼，并通过你们向全中国的工人兄弟致以热烈的敬礼。我们诚恳地祝贺你们大会的工作获得圆满的成功。这次大会将对英勇的中国工人在你们的伟大国家里加强建设新民主主义的努力，以及争取和平与摧毁战争贩子的计划的斗争，起重大的贡献。
    在以毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党的英明领导下的伟大的中国人民万岁！
    以光荣的不可战胜的苏联人民为首的强大和平阵营万岁！
    我们两国人民之间牢不可破的友谊万岁！
            阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会
                    主席皮里斯特利

              印度邮电工人工会拉马穆蒂

    全印尼工会联合会的贺电
