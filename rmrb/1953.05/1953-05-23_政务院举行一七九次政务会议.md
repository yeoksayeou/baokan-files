Title: Government Administration Council Holds 179th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-05-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: The 179th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 22nd. The meeting heard and approved the work report on eliminating illiteracy made by Chu Tunan, Director of the Central People's Government Illiteracy Elimination Work Committee. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal cases and cases submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行一七九次政务会议

1953-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行一七九次政务会议
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百七十九次政务会议在二十二日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府扫除文盲工作委员会楚图南主任委员所作的关于扫除文盲的工作报告，会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
