Title: Hungarian National Assembly Election Concludes; Rakosi-led People's Independent Front Achieves Complete Victory
Author:
Date: 1953-05-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th: Budapest News: The election of the National Assembly of the Hungarian People's Republic was held on the 17th. The candidates of the Hungarian People's Independent Front, led by Mátyás Rákosi, General Secretary of the Hungarian Working People's Party, achieved a complete victory. According to the election results announced on the 18th by the Central Election Commission of the Hungarian People's Republic, out of 6,501,869 registered voters, 6,370,519 people participated in the election, accounting for 98 percent of the voters. 6,256,653 people voted for the candidates of the Hungarian People's Independent Front, accounting for 98.2 percent of the total number of voters. A total of 298 members of the National Assembly and 155 alternate members were elected from 20 electoral districts nationwide.
    On the day of the election, cities and villages throughout Hungary were filled with festive joy, and voters dressed in beautiful clothes and went to the polls with joy to exercise their civil rights. In the capital Budapest, hundreds of thousands of people from all walks of life—workers, professors, students, writers, and artists—flocked to the polling stations to vote from early morning. Young workers and old workers voted together to elect the most outstanding sons and daughters of the Hungarian people to the National Assembly. Comrade Rakosi, the leader of the Hungarian people, went to the polling station to vote at 9 am and was warmly welcomed by citizens and Young Pioneers along the way. In Stalin City, which was newly built in the four years since the last election, people could be seen singing and dancing everywhere as they walked to the polling station. By 10 am, 95 percent of the city's voters had cast their ballots. Hungarian sailors sailing far away at sea also voted on their ships.

### 匈牙利国民议会选举结束  拉科西为首的人民独立阵线获得完全胜利

1953-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利国民议会选举结束
    拉科西为首的人民独立阵线获得完全胜利
    【新华社二十日讯】布达佩斯消息：匈牙利人民共和国国民议会的选举已在十七日举行。以匈牙利劳动人民党总书记马·拉科西为首的匈牙利人民独立阵线候选人，获得了完全的胜利。根据匈牙利人民共和国中央选举委员会十八日宣布的选举结果，在六百五十万一千八百六十九个登记的选民中，有六百三十七万零五百一十九人，即百分之九十八的选民参加了选举。投票选举匈牙利人民独立阵线候选人的，有六百二十五万六千六百五十三人，占投票人总数的百分之九十八点二。全国二十个选区共选出国民议会议员二百九十八人，候补议员一百五十五人。
    在选举这一天，匈牙利全国各城市和乡村都充满着节日的欢乐，选民们穿着漂亮的衣服，欢欣鼓舞地到投票站去执行他们的公民权利。在首都布达佩斯，工人、教授、学生、作家和艺术家——各行各业的几十万人民，从清晨起就络绎不绝地到投票站投票。年轻的工人和年老的工人一道投票选举匈牙利人民最优秀的儿女到国民议会中。匈牙利人民领袖拉科西同志在上午九时到投票站去投票，沿途受到市民和少年先锋队队员的热烈欢迎。在上届选举后的四年中新建成的斯大林城，到处可以看见人们载歌载舞，走到投票站。到上午十时，该城就有百分之九十五的选民投了票。在远离祖国航行在海上的匈牙利海员们，也在他们的海船上投了票。
