Title: Uruguayan People Oppose U.S.-Uruguay Military Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1953-05-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, May 24th: According to TASS, a mass rally was held in Montevideo on the evening of May 22nd to protest the military agreement concluded with the United States. This agreement is currently being discussed in the Uruguayan House of Representatives.

    The central slogans of the rally were to resolutely protest this agreement, defend national independence and democratic rights, and oppose the government's war policy and police suppression.

    All speakers exposed the aggressive and enslaving nature of this military agreement, severely condemned the policies of U.S. imperialism, and declared that if this agreement were approved, the people would not recognize it and would prevent its implementation.

    Following the rally, a demonstration was held to protest the military agreement.

### 乌拉圭人民反对乌美军事协定

1953-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭人民反对乌美军事协定
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社蒙得维的亚二十四日讯：五月二十二日晚上，蒙得维的亚举行群众大会抗议和美国订立军事协定。这个协定目前正在乌拉圭议会众议院讨论。
    大会的中心口号是坚决抗议这个协定，保卫民族独立和民主权利，反对政府的战争政策和警察镇压。
    所有发言的人都揭露了这个军事协定的侵略性质和奴役性质，严厉谴责美帝国主义的政策，并宣称这个协定如获批准，人民将不承认它并阻止它实行。
    在大会之后，随即举行了抗议军事协定的示威游行。
