Title: CPPCC National Committee Overseas Chinese Affairs Group Invites Indonesian Chinese Soccer Team for Discussion

Author: 
Date: 1953-05-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: The Overseas Chinese Affairs Group of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) invited the entire Indonesian Chinese soccer team to a discussion meeting on the afternoon of the 28th.  Attending the discussion were Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the CPPCC National Committee, and members Cai Tingkai, Shao Lizi, and Zhuang Mingli and Fei Zhendong from the Overseas Chinese Affairs Group. At the meeting, Vice-Chairman Chen Shutong spoke first, extending a warm welcome to the Indonesian Chinese soccer team on behalf of the CPPCC National Committee. Subsequently, Li Jishen, Cai Tingkai, and Shao Lizi also spoke. Cai Tingkai specifically pointed out the importance of sports in his speech. Finally, Fang Dingxun, the leader of the Indonesian Chinese soccer team, spoke to express his deep gratitude.

### 政协全国委员会华侨事务组邀请印尼华侨回国球队座谈

1953-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会华侨事务组邀请印尼华侨回国球队座谈
    【新华社二十八日讯】二十八日下午，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会华侨事务组邀请印尼华侨回国球队全体队员举行座谈会。出席座谈会的有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李济深、陈叔通和委员蔡廷锴、邵力子以及华侨事务组庄明理、费振东等。会上，陈叔通副主席首先讲话，他代表中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会对印尼华侨回国球队表示热烈的欢迎。接着，李济深、蔡廷锴、邵力子都相继讲话。蔡廷锴在讲话中特别指出体育运动的重要性。最后印尼华侨回国球队领队方定壎讲话表示深切的感谢。
