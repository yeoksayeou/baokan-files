Title: French National Assembly Foreign Affairs Committee Requests Four-Power Conference
Author:
Date: 1953-05-16
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 15th: The French National Assembly Foreign Affairs Committee unanimously passed a resolution on the 13th, requesting the government to quickly suggest holding a great power conference as soon as possible. The resolution stated: "The Foreign Affairs Committee hopes that the French government will reiterate its desire to hold a four-power summit as soon as possible. The committee hopes that the government will put forward concrete proposals to pave the way for such a conference." At the same time, the French National Assembly Foreign Affairs Committee rejected the suggestion to immediately discuss and approve the "European Army" agreement.

### 法国民议会外交委员会要求举行四国会议

1953-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    法国民议会外交委员会要求举行四国会议
    【新华社十五日讯】巴黎消息：法国国民议会外交委员会十三日一致通过决议，要求政府赶快建议尽早举行大国会议。决议说：“外交委员会希望法国政府重申它的愿望，就是尽早举行四国的最高级会议。委员会希望政府为此提出确实的建议，以便为这样的一个会议铺平道路。”同时，法国国民议会外交委员会否决了要求立即讨论批准“欧洲军”协定的建议。
