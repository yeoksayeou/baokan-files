Title: British "Times" Publishes Editorial Criticizing U.S. Attempt to Sever British-Chinese Trade
Author:
Date: 1953-05-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, May 7th: British newspapers reflect the eagerness of business circles to increase foreign trade.
    The "Times" strongly criticized McCarthy for attempting to completely prohibit trade between Britain and China. The "Times" editorial said: "The British government does not wish or intend to completely sever trade with China. Trade and shipping are the lifeblood of the British Empire."

### 英国“泰晤士报”发表社论  抨击美国企图断绝英中贸易

1953-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    英国“泰晤士报”发表社论
    抨击美国企图断绝英中贸易
   【新华社九日讯】塔斯社伦敦七日讯：英国报纸反映出商业界人士热切希望增加对外贸易的心情。
    “泰晤士报”强烈地抨击麦卡锡，因为他企图完全禁止英中之间的贸易。“泰晤士报”社论说：“英国政府并不希望或打算完全断绝与中国进行贸易。贸易与航运乃是英帝国活命的血液。”
