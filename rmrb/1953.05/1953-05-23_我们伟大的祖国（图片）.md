Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-05-23
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Today is the second anniversary of the signing of the agreement between the Central People's Government and the Tibetan local government on the peaceful liberation of Tibet. In the past two years, the lives of the Tibetan people have been full of new vitality. The herds of cattle and sheep of the Tibetan herdsmen are more numerous than in the past.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是中央人民政府和西藏地方政府签订和平解放西藏办法的协议两周年。两年来西藏人民的生活充满着新气象。西藏牧民们的牛群和羊群比过去更多了。
