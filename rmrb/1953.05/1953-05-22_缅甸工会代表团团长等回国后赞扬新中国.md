Title: Burmese Trade Union Delegation Leader and Others Praise New China After Returning Home
Author:
Date: 1953-05-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, 21st - The head of the Burmese trade union delegation, Tekin La Zui, and the General Secretary of the All Burma Trade Union Congress, Tekin Qing Su, who were invited to participate in the "May Day" celebration and the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions in our country, and who also attended the Vienna International Social Security Conference and came to our country to participate in the celebrations and congress, returned to Yangon on the 16th. They were warmly welcomed by more than 500 representatives of various trade union organizations upon their arrival at the airport. In their talks with reporters, they unanimously praised the speed of New China's construction and the development of the Chinese working class's strength. They excitedly described the grand scene of the "May Day" celebration in Beijing attended by more than 500,000 people and the grand occasion of the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions. Tekin Qing Su said that he had been to China twice before, and this time he saw many new constructions, and New China has made rapid progress. They also described what they learned from visiting Chinese worker and peasant representatives during the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions. They said: China's implementation of the Five-Year Plan for economic construction is not only training a large number of new forces of workers, but also greatly improving the lives of workers, and New China will become more prosperous and strong. Finally, the two representatives unanimously pointed out that the powerful Chinese people are peace-loving, and therefore they also have sufficient strength to defend peace.

### 缅甸工会代表团团长等回国后赞扬新中国

1953-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸工会代表团团长等回国后赞扬新中国
    【新华社二十一日讯】仰光消息：应邀来我国参加“五一”节庆祝典礼和中国工会第七次全国代表大会后回国的缅甸工会代表团团长德钦拉最和出席维也纳国际社会保险会议后顺道亦来我国参加典礼和大会的全缅工会大会总书记德钦庆素二人，在十六日回到仰光。他们抵达飞机场时即受到各职工团体代表五百余人的热烈欢迎。他们对记者发表谈话时，一致赞扬新中国建设的速度和中国工人阶级力量的发展。他们非常兴奋地叙述在北京举行的五十多万人参加的“五一”节庆祝典礼的伟大场面和中国工会第七次全国代表大会举行的盛况。德钦庆素说，他曾先后两次到过中国，这次又见到许多新的建设，新中国又有了突飞猛进的发展。他们并叙述了他们在中国工会第七次全国代表大会开会期间访问中国的工人、农民代表时所了解的情况。他们说：中国开始实施五年经济建设计划，不但大批地培养着工人的新的力量，而且也正在大大地改善工人的生活，新中国将更加富强起来。最后，两位代表一致指出：强大的中国人民是酷爱和平的，因此他们也有足够的力量来保卫和平。
