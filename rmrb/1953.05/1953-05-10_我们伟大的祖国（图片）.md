    Our Great Motherland
    With the widespread development of the labor emulation drive, new production achievements are constantly emerging. Workers in the converter workshop of the Shanghai Iron and Steel Company No. 1 Plant are striving to improve the quality of their products in the competition, exceeding the standards set by the Iron and Steel Bureau. This is a scene of workers engaged in production.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    随着劳动竞赛的普遍展开，新的生产成就不断地涌现出来。上海钢铁公司第一厂转炉车间工人，在竞赛中努力提高产品的质量，超过钢铁局规定的标准。这是工人正在生产的情形。
