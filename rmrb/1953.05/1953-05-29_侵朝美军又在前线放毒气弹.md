Title: U.S. Troops Invading Korea Again Use Poison Gas Bombs on the Front Lines
Author:
Date: 1953-05-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 28th – The invading U.S. troops in Korea recently used poison gas bombs again on our army's positions on the front lines. At 7:00 PM on May 25th, enemy troops bombarded our army's position near Kimhwa with 155mm howitzers, dropping more than 50 shells on one of our army's hilltop positions, six of which were poison gas bombs. Three poison gas bombs also landed on another hilltop position of our army. After inspection and verification, these were all suffocating and toxic poison gas bombs.

### 侵朝美军又在前线放毒气弹

1953-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    侵朝美军又在前线放毒气弹
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十八日电】侵朝美军最近又在朝鲜前线向我军阵地施放毒气弹。五月二十五日下午七时，敌军以一五五公厘口径的榴弹炮轰击金城附近我军阵地，在我军一个山头阵地上落弹五十多发，内有六发是毒气弹；在我军另外一个山头阵地上，也落下了三发毒气弹。经检验证明，这些都是窒息性和中毒性毒气弹。
