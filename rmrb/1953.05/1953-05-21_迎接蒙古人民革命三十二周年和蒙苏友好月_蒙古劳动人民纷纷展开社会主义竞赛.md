Title: To Greet the 32nd Anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution and the Month of Mongolian-Soviet Friendship, Mongolian Working People Vigorously Launch Socialist Emulation Drives
Author:
Date: 1953-05-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, May 18th: As the 32nd anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution (July 11th) and the Month of Mongolian-Soviet Friendship, beginning June 4th, approach, the working people of the Mongolian People's Republic, in order to properly celebrate these significant events, have launched socialist emulation drives in enterprises, state farms, and livestock production cooperatives, establishing concrete covenants.

    The "Unen" newspaper reported that the Livestock Production Cooperatives have called on all livestock cooperatives in Mongolia to launch a socialist emulation drive to develop the public economy. The Livestock Production Cooperatives pledge to overfulfill the annual plan to increase the total number of livestock by 27 percent, pay taxes to the state ten days ahead of schedule, establish a long-term feed base, build new livestock pens, increase land yields, organize animal husbandry technology and agricultural technology groups and training classes, and widely apply the experience of Soviet collective farms in animal husbandry and agriculture.

    The newspaper also published a letter of challenge from all the staff of the No. 10 mine of the Nalaikh Coal Mine, calling on all workers, engineers, and technicians in industrial enterprises, transportation enterprises, construction enterprises, and handicraft enterprises to launch an emulation drive to complete and overfulfill the 1953 production plan. The workers of the No. 10 mine of the Nalaikh Coal Mine pledge to fulfill 102.6 percent of the May coal mining plan, 106.6 percent of the June coal mining plan, and 104 percent of the half-year coal mining plan, striving to ensure that every miner fulfills at least 110 percent of their quota.

### 迎接蒙古人民革命三十二周年和蒙苏友好月  蒙古劳动人民纷纷展开社会主义竞赛

1953-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    迎接蒙古人民革命三十二周年和蒙苏友好月
    蒙古劳动人民纷纷展开社会主义竞赛
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托十七日讯：蒙古人民革命三十二周年纪念（七月十一日）和从六月四日开始的蒙苏友好月快要来到了。蒙古人民共和国的劳动人民，为了好好地庆祝这些意义重大的事件，在企业中、在国营农场中和畜牧业生产合作社中，展开了社会主义竞赛，订出了具体公约。
    蒙古“真理报”报道，畜牧业生产合作社号召蒙古所有畜牧业合作社展开发展公有经济的社会主义竞赛。畜牧业生产合作社保证超额完成增加牲畜总头数百分之二十七的全年计划，在规定期限十天之前向国家缴纳赋税，建立长久的饲料基地，修筑一些新的牲畜栏，提高田地的收获量，组织动物饲养技术和农业技术小组和训练班，广泛地运用苏联集体农庄在畜牧业和农业方面的经验。
    报纸还刊载了那莱哈煤矿第十号矿井全体工作人员的挑战书，号召工业企业、运输企业、建筑企业和手工业企业的全体工人和工程技术人员，展开完成和超额完成一九五三年生产计划的竞赛。那莱哈煤矿第十号矿井的工人保证完成五月份采煤计划的百分之一百零二点六，完成六月份采煤计划的百分之一百零六点六，完成半年采煤计划的百分之一百零四，竭力使每一个矿工至少完成自己的定额百分之一百一十。
