Title: Celebrating the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1953-05-09
Page: 4

International Trade Union of Leather, Fur, and Leather Products’ Congratulatory Telegram
[Xinhua News Agency, 8th] The International Trade Union of Leather, Fur, and Leather Products sent a congratulatory telegram to the Presidium of the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions:
To the Presidium of the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions and all Chinese workers, we extend warm fraternal congratulations. We wish the Congress success.
International Trade Union of Leather, Fur, and Leather Products
(Industrial Department of the World Federation of Trade Unions)
May 5

Congratulatory Telegram from the Pakistan Federation of Labour
[Xinhua News Agency, 8th] Muhammad Ibrahim, Chairman of the Pakistan Federation of Labour, sent a letter congratulating the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions. The letter stated:
On behalf of the Pakistan Federation of Labour and the workers of Pakistan, I extend warm congratulations to the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions.
The people of Pakistan have great love and concern for the people of China, not only because we are neighbors, but also because we realize that your standing up against imperialist exploiters has given us Asian people a new place in the world today.
The Chinese people, under the leadership of their working class, have become a great bastion of peace, progress, and democracy in the world today. You have laid the foundation for China's path towards a prosperous and happy future. These great victories and achievements have greatly inspired us.
Currently, with American aggression in Korea, the struggle for peace continues. You will begin new construction plans to transform your economy from primarily agricultural to primarily industrial. You will formulate a new constitution to consolidate the foundations of your People's Government. We firmly believe that you will successfully complete the great tasks you have undertaken.
I wish your Congress success.
Long live the friendship between the people of China and Pakistan!
Glory to the Chinese working class and its powerful Communist Party!
Long live world peace!

Congratulatory Telegram from the All-Burma Trade Union Congress
[Xinhua News Agency, 7th] The All-Burma Trade Union Congress sent a telegram congratulating the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions. The telegram stated: The great victory achieved by the Chinese people is a great and living example for the oppressed working people of Asia who are bravely and tirelessly struggling for bread, democracy, independence, and peace. The opportunity for representatives of the Burmese working class to participate in this historic Congress will further strengthen the unity and friendship between Chinese and Burmese workers.
The Burmese workers, who are engaged in a vigorous struggle for liberation under the iron heels of foreign imperialism and feudalism, warmly congratulate the success of the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions and wish for the completion and over-fulfillment of the first national construction five-year plan.
Long live the unity and friendship of the Chinese and Burmese working classes!
Long live the vanguard of the Chinese proletariat—the Communist Party of China!
Long live the All-China Federation of Trade Unions!
Long live the World Federation of Trade Unions—the fighting organization of workers around the world!
Long live the great and glorious cause of peace!
Your fraternal All-Burma Trade Union Congress
Chairman: Tekin Lwin
General Secretary: Myo Lwin

Congratulatory Telegram from the Japanese Liaison Office of the World Federation of Trade Unions
[Xinhua News Agency, 6th] The Japanese Liaison Office of the World Federation of Trade Unions sent a congratulatory telegram to the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions.
All-China Federation of Trade Unions:
Long live the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions! We are willing to work with you for peace!
Japanese Liaison Office of the World Federation of Trade Unions
April 30

Congratulatory Telegram from the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan
[Xinhua News Agency, 6th] The General Council of Trade Unions of Japan sent a congratulatory telegram to the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions.
All-China Federation of Trade Unions:
We, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, on behalf of all Japanese workers, congratulate your great Congress. We pledge to work with you for the peace and friendship of the Chinese and Japanese people, for free trade, and for improving living standards!
Minoru Takano, General Council of Trade Unions of Japan
April 29

Congratulatory Telegram from the Arab Workers' Congress in Israel
[Xinhua News Agency, 8th] The Arab Workers' Congress in Israel sent a congratulatory telegram to the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions:
World Federation of Trade Unions, transfer to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:
On the occasion of "May Day" and the convening of your Seventh Congress, we send you fraternal congratulations.
Arab Workers' Congress in Israel
Secretary: Salim Qasim
April 23

Congratulatory Telegram from the United Electrical, Radio and Machine Workers of America
[Xinhua News Agency, 7th] The United Electrical, Radio and Machine Workers of America sent a telegram congratulating the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions. The telegram stated:
On the occasion of the convening of the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, on behalf of the peace-loving American working people, we send greetings to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Chinese people.
The noble tasks undertaken by the great Chinese people—consolidating the people's democratic dictatorship, industrializing China, striving for a socialist future, and uniting the workers of the world to strive for world peace—will undoubtedly be successful. We wish you great achievements in completing and overfulfilling your five-year plan.
In the struggle for world peace, freedom, and improving the material lives of the working class in China and around the world, we stand in solidarity with you.
Long live the All-China Federation of Trade Unions!
Long live the Chinese people!
Long live world peace!
United Electrical, Radio and Machine Workers of America
Louis Whiton

Congratulatory Telegram from the Britain-China Friendship Association
[Xinhua News Agency, 8th] The Britain-China Friendship Association sent a congratulatory telegram to the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions:
All-China Federation of Trade Unions:
On the occasion of the opening of the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, we extend our warmest congratulations to the Chinese workers. We deeply admire your achievements and rapid growth.
Britain-China Friendship Association

### 庆祝中国工会第七次全国代表大会

1953-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国工会第七次全国代表大会
    皮革皮毛皮货工人工会国际的贺电
    【新华社八日讯】皮革皮毛皮货工人工会国际给中国工会第七次全国代表大会的贺电：中国工会第七次全国代表大会主席团：
    谨向中国工会第七次全国代表大会及中国全体工人致热烈的兄弟的祝贺。敬祝代表大会成功。
              皮革皮毛皮货工人工会国际
                （世界工联产业部）
                        五月五日
    巴基斯坦总工会的贺电
    【新华社八日讯】巴基斯坦总工会主席穆罕默德·易卜拉欣来信祝贺中国工会第七次全国代表大会，来信说：
    我谨代表巴基斯坦总工会和巴基斯坦工人，向中国工会第七次全国代表大会致以热烈的祝贺。
    巴基斯坦人民对中国人民非常地热爱与关怀，不但因为我们是邻国，也因为我们体会到你们站起来反对帝国主义剥削者这一事实，使我们亚洲人民会在今天世界上有了新的地位。
    中国人民在他们工人阶级的领导下，成为今天世界上和平、进步与民主的伟大堡垒。为中国走向繁荣幸福未来的道路奠定了基础，这些伟大胜利和成就大大地鼓舞了我们。
    目前，在朝鲜有着美国的侵略，争取和平的斗争仍在继续，你们将开始新的建设计划，使你们以农业为主的经济改变为以工业为主的经济，你们将制定新宪法，以巩固你们人民政府的基础。我们深信你们将胜利地完成你们自己所担负的伟大任务。
    谨祝你们的代表大会成功。
    中巴两国人民的友谊万岁！
    光荣归于中国工人阶级及其强大的共产党！
    世界和平万岁！
    全缅工会大会的贺电
    【新华社七日讯】全缅工会大会来电祝贺中国工会第七次全国代表大会，贺电说：中国人民所获得的伟大胜利，是那些正在英勇不倦地为面包、民主、独立与和平而斗争的亚洲被压迫的劳动人民伟大的活生生的榜样。缅甸工人阶级的代表有机会参加这个有历史意义的代表大会，将促使中缅两国工人的团结和友谊更趋紧密。
    在外国帝国主义和封建主义铁蹄下为争取解放，正在进行着有力斗争的缅甸工人热烈地祝贺中国工会第七次全国代表大会成功，并祝第一个国家建设的五年计划的完成和超额完成。
    中缅工人阶级团结和友谊万岁！
    中国无产阶级先锋队——中国共产党万岁！
    中华全国总工会万岁！
    全世界工人战斗的团结的组织——世界工联万岁！
    伟大的和光荣的和平事业万岁！
        你们的兄弟般的全缅工会大会
                      主　席　德钦伦
                    执行总书记谬柳

    美国联合电气工人工会的贺电
    【新华社七日讯】美国联合电气工人工会来电祝贺中国工会第七次全国代表大会，贺电说：
    值此中国工会第七次全国代表大会召开之际，谨代表爱好和平的美国劳动人民向中华全国总工会及中国人民致贺。
    伟大的中国人民所担负的崇高任务：巩固人民民主专政，使中国工业化，争取社会主义的前途，团结全世界的工人们争取世界和平，无疑地都将获得成功。我们预祝你们在完成和超额完成你们的五年计划中获得巨大的成就。
    在争取世界和平、自由、争取中国及全世界工人阶级的物质生活改善的斗争中，我们与你们团结一致。
    中华全国总工会万岁！
    中国人民万岁！
    世界和平万岁！
            美国联合电气工人工会
                      路易斯·惠吞
