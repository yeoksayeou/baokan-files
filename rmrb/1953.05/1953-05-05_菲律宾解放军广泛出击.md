Title: Philippine Liberation Army Launches Extensive Offensives
Author:
Date: 1953-05-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 4th: Manila dispatch: The Philippine People's Liberation Army is continuously launching extensive offensives, dealing powerful blows to the Philippine Quirino puppet government. The Quirino authorities admit that the Philippine People's Liberation Army launched 124 offensives in April alone.

On April 30, the Philippine People's Liberation Army defeated a unit of Quirino puppet troops 40 miles north of Manila. According to figures released by the Philippine puppet government's Ministry of National Defense, which clearly underestimate the numbers, the People's Liberation Army killed 13 puppet soldiers and wounded two in the battle.

### 菲律宾解放军广泛出击

1953-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾解放军广泛出击
   【新华社四日讯】马尼拉消息：菲律宾人民解放军不断广泛出击，给菲律宾季里诺傀儡政府以有力的打击。季里诺当局承认：菲律宾人民解放军仅在四月份就出击一百二十四次。
    四月三十日，菲律宾人民解放军在马尼拉北方四十英里打败了一支季里诺傀儡军。据菲律宾傀儡政府国防部所公布的显然缩小了的数字，人民解放军在这一场战斗中打死傀儡军十三人，打伤二人。
