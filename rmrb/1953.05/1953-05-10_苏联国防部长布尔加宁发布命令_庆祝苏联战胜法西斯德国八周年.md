Title: Soviet Defense Minister Bulganin Issues Order Celebrating the Eighth Anniversary of the Soviet Victory Over Fascist Germany
Author:
Date: 1953-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th: TASS Moscow, May 9th: The Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union N.A. Bulganin, issued the following order commemorating the eighth anniversary of the victory over Fascist Germany:
    Comrade soldiers and sailors! Comrade non-commissioned officers and junior officers! Comrade officers, comrade generals!
    Eight years ago, the Soviet Union's Great Patriotic War against German imperialists ended with our complete victory. Under the leadership of our glorious Communist Party, the Soviet people and their armed forces won this epoch-making victory, defended the glory and independence of their socialist motherland, and liberated the peoples of Europe from the threat of fascist enslavement. On this Victory Day, I extend my congratulations to the personnel of the Army and Navy.
    In commemoration of Victory Day, I order: Today, May 9th, in our motherland's capital Moscow, in the capitals of all the union republics, and in the hero cities of Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa, a thirty-gun salute will be fired.
    Long live our socialist motherland!
    Long live the heroic Soviet people and their glorious Army and Navy!
    Long live the Soviet government!
    Long live the great Communist Party of the Soviet Union!
    Eternal glory to the heroes who sacrificed themselves in the battle to defend the freedom and independence of our motherland!
    Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union N.A. Bulganin (signed)

### 苏联国防部长布尔加宁发布命令  庆祝苏联战胜法西斯德国八周年

1953-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部长布尔加宁发布命令
    庆祝苏联战胜法西斯德国八周年
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅尼·亚·布尔加宁发布庆祝战胜法西斯德国八周年纪念日的命令如下：陆海军战士同志们！士官和尉官同志们！校官同志们，将军同志们！
    八年以前，苏联对德国帝国主义者的伟大卫国战争，以我们的完全胜利结束了。苏联人民和他们的武装部队在我们光荣的共产党的领导下，获得了这个划时代的胜利，保卫了他们社会主义祖国的光荣和独立，并且把欧洲各国人民从法西斯奴役的威胁下解放了出来。值此胜利日，我谨向陆军和海军人员祝贺。
    为了纪念胜利日，我命令：今天，五月九日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科，在各加盟共和国的首都，在列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、塞瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨诸英雄城市，一律鸣放礼炮三十响。
    我们的社会主义祖国万岁！
    英雄的苏联人民和他们光荣的陆军和海军万岁！
    苏联政府万岁！
    伟大的苏联共产党万岁！
    在保卫我们祖国的自由和独立的战斗中牺牲的英雄们永垂不朽！
    苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅尼·亚·布尔加宁（签字）
