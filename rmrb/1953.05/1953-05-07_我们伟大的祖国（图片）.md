    Our Great Motherland
    Workers at the New Machinery Factory of the Dalian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Manufacturing Plant, in order to support Anshan Iron and Steel, have manufactured two 15-ton steam cranes. The workers are using the cranes to send the newly made cold steel wheels to the workshop for assembly.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    大连机车车辆制造厂新机器厂的工人，为了支援鞍钢，制好了两台十五吨的蒸气吊车。工人们正把新制好的冷钢轮，用吊车送往车间去装配。
