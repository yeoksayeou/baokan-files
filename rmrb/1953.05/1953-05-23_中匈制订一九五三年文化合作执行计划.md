Title: China and Hungary Formulate 1953 Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1953-05-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: The 1953 China-Hungary Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan, formulated according to the China-Hungary Cultural Cooperation Agreement, was exchanged through notes on the 19th of this month after consultation and agreement between the two sides. The exchange was conducted by Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, and Sándor Szabó, Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, representing the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Hungarian People's Republic, respectively.

### 中匈制订一九五三年文化合作执行计划

1953-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈制订一九五三年文化合作执行计划
    【新华社二十二日讯】根据中匈文化合作协定制订的中匈一九五三年文化合作执行计划，经双方商谈协议后，已在本月十九日，由我中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权和匈牙利人民共和国驻我国大使夏法朗柯，分别代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府和匈牙利人民共和国换文。
