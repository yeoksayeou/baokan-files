Title: The Seventh Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Holds its First Meeting: Comrade Liu Shaoqi is Invited to Continue Serving as Honorary Chairman, and the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Presidium, and Secretariat are Elected.
Author:
Date: 1953-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 12th: The Seventh Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions held its first meeting on the morning of the 12th. In accordance with the Constitution of the Chinese Trade Unions passed by the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Presidium, and Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions were elected.

The first item on the meeting's agenda was the election.

The committee members present first warmly applauded and unanimously approved the list of candidates for Chairman and Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions proposed by Rao Shushi, the list of candidates for members of the Presidium jointly proposed by Zhang Weizhen on behalf of the national trade union delegations, and the list of candidates for secretaries of the Secretariat proposed by Lai Ruoyu.

All committee members present warmly applauded Liu Ningyi's suggestion and unanimously agreed to invite Comrade Liu Shaoqi to continue serving as Honorary Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

Then, the meeting proceeded with the election by secret ballot.

The election results are as follows:

Lai Ruoyu was elected as Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

Liu Ningyi, Liu Changsheng, and Zhu Xuefan were elected as Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

Kong Xiangzhen, Wang Weigang, Qiu Jin, Zhu Xuefan, Du Yanqing, Li Jiebo, Jin Zhifu, Xi Zhanyuan, Chen Shaomin, Li Zaiwen, Xu Zhizhen, Kang Yonghe, Zhang Weizhen, Zhang Canming, Zhang Qi, Yang Zhihua, Yang Jue, Dong Xin, Zhao Guoqiang, Liu Ziju, Liu Changsheng, Liu Ningyi, Cai Shufan, and Lai Ruoyu, a total of twenty-four people, were elected as members of the Presidium of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

Lai Ruoyu, Liu Ningyi, Xu Zhizhen, Chen Shaomin, Li Jiebo, Liu Ziju, Li Zaiwen, and Dong Xin, a total of eight people, were elected as secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

At the end of the election, the entire audience stood up and applauded warmly to celebrate the formal establishment of the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Presidium, and Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

The second item on the meeting's agenda was the issue of future work.

The meeting believes that this meeting is the first executive committee meeting held immediately after the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions. The guiding principles, tasks, organizational principles, and organizational forms for future work have been clearly stipulated at the National Congress, so there is no need for this meeting to discuss them again. The meeting submitted the question of how to carry out future work and launch various activities in accordance with the spirit of the resolutions of the National Congress to the Presidium for discussion.

Sixty-two executive committee members and twenty-seven alternate executive committee members attended the meeting.

The meeting began at 9:40 am and ended at 11:15 am.

Xinhua News Agency, May 12th: The Seventh Finance Review Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions held its first meeting on the morning of the 12th.

The meeting was presided over by Du Daozhou.

The meeting unanimously elected Wang Wenxing as Chairman of the Seventh Finance Review Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

The meeting approved Jin Ziming's proposal and unanimously elected Du Daozhou, Xiang Minghua, and Gao Zongzhi to draft the working regulations of the Finance Review Committee.

### 中华全国总工会第七届执委会举行第一次会议  请刘少奇同志继续担任名誉主席  并选出主席、副主席、主席团和书记处

1953-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会第七届执委会举行第一次会议
    请刘少奇同志继续担任名誉主席
    并选出主席、副主席、主席团和书记处
    【新华社十二日讯】中华全国总工会第七届执行委员会于十二日上午举行第一次会议，根据中国工会第七次全国代表大会通过的中国工会章程，选出了中华全国总工会主席、副主席、主席团和书记处。
    会议的第一个议题是选举。
    到会委员首先热烈鼓掌赞同并一致通过了饶漱石提议的中华全国总工会主席、副主席的候选人名单，张维桢代表全国各工会代表团联名提议的主席团委员候选人名单，和赖若愚提议的书记处书记候选人名单。
    到会全体委员热烈鼓掌接受刘宁一的建议，并一致通过，请刘少奇同志继续担任中华全国总工会名誉主席。
    接着，会议以无记名投票方式进行选举。
    选举结果如下：
    赖若愚当选为中华全国总工会主席。
    刘宁一、刘长胜、朱学范当选为中华全国总工会副主席。
    孔祥桢、王维纲、丘金、朱学范、杜延庆、李颉伯、金直夫、郗占元、陈少敏、栗再温、许之桢、康永和、张维桢、张灿明、张祺、杨之华、杨珏、董昕、赵国强、刘子久、刘长胜、刘宁一、蔡树藩、赖若愚等二十四人当选为中华全国总工会主席团委员。
    赖若愚、刘宁一、许之桢、陈少敏、李颉伯、刘子久、栗再温、董昕等八人当选为中华全国总工会书记处书记。
    选举结束时，全场起立热烈鼓掌，庆祝中华全国总工会主席、副主席、主席团和书记处的正式产生。
    会议的第二个议题是关于今后工作问题。
    会议认为，这次会议是紧接在中国工会第七次全国代表大会之后召开的第一次执行委员会会议，关于今后工作方针、任务、组织原则和组织形式等问题已在全国代表大会上作了明确的规定，所以这次会议无须再来讨论。关于如何根据全国代表大会决议的精神进行今后工作，展开各方面活动的问题，会议提交主席团讨论。
    出席这次会议的，有执行委员六十二人，候补执行委员二十七人。
    会议于上午九时四十分开始，十一时十五分结束。
     【新华社十二日讯】中华全国总工会第七届经费审查委员会于十二日上午举行第一次会议。
    会议由杜道周主持。
    会议一致选出了王文兴为中华全国总工会第七届经费审查委员会主席。
    会议通过金子明的提议并一致推选杜道周、向明华、高宗智三人起草经费审查委员会工作条例。
