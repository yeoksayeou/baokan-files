Title: Albanian Government and People of Canada and Other Countries Express Support for the Proposal to Conclude a Five-Power Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1953-05-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 13th - According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency: Enver Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Albania, wrote to Joliot-Curie, Nenni, and Wirth, expressing his agreement with the letter from the International Committee of the World Peace Congress requesting the Albanian government to support the proposal to conclude a five-power peace pact.

In his letter, Hoxha said: The People's Republic of Albania fully supports the World Peace Congress's letter to the governments of the five major powers and agrees with the proposal to conclude a peace pact among the five major powers, because this is fully consistent with the Albanian government's peace policy, namely a policy of friendly cooperation among all the peoples of the world based on the equality of all nations and respect for the national independence and sovereignty of all countries in the world.

The Albanian people have consistently demonstrated through their peaceful construction labor that they advocate maintaining and consolidating world peace. The Albanian government, representing the sentiments and aspirations of all the Albanian people, believes that consultation and the conclusion of a peace pact among the five major powers will eliminate the current international tension and open up prospects for a peaceful resolution of the issues that remain outstanding.

865,885 citizens of the People's Republic of Albania have signed the World Peace Council's declaration calling for the conclusion of a peace pact, and the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania passed the Law on Defending Peace on January 10, 1951. This clearly demonstrates the will of the Albanian people and their fervent hope for peace. The government of the People's Republic of Albania fully supports this desire and is prepared to resolutely defend peace to the end.

Xinhua News Agency, 14th - According to TASS: Peace advocates in Canada, Uruguay, and Brazil are calling for the conclusion of a five-power peace pact and an end to the Korean War.

Recently, a conference of Canadian peace advocates, attended by more than 700 representatives of trade union and church workers, passed a resolution calling for an immediate end to the Korean War and the implementation of the principles of national independence and non-interference in the internal affairs of other countries. At the conclusion of the conference, a mass rally was held, passing a proposal put forward by Dr. James Endicott, chairman of the Canadian Peace Congress, urging the Canadian government to respond satisfactorily to the World Peace Congress's request for the conclusion of a five-power peace pact.

The Uruguayan National Peace Council held a special meeting in Montevideo, attended by representatives who passed a resolution opposing the conclusion of the "U.S.-Uruguay Military Agreement" and calling for the swift conclusion of a five-power peace pact.

In Brazil, General Buxbaum, a member of the International Committee of the World Peace Congress, delivered a speech at the Brazilian Press Association in Rio de Janeiro. After explaining the great significance of the five-power peace pact in easing international tension, he called on the Brazilian people to urge the government to support this peace initiative.

### 阿尔巴尼亚政府和加拿大等国人民  表示支持缔结五大国和平公约的建议

1953-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚政府和加拿大等国人民
    表示支持缔结五大国和平公约的建议
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社地拉那十三日讯：阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席兼外交部部长恩维尔·霍查写信给约里奥—居里、南尼和维尔特，表示同意世界人民和平大会国际委员会要求阿尔巴尼亚政府支持缔结五大国和平公约建议的来信。
    霍查在信中说：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国完全支持世界人民和平大会的致五大国政府书，并且同意要求五大国缔结和平公约的建议，因为这和阿尔巴尼亚政府的和平政策、即以各国人民平等和尊重世界所有国家的民族独立与主权为基础的全世界人民友好合作的政策是完全一致的。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民一贯以他们和平建设的劳动证明，他们是主张维护和巩固世界和平的。阿尔巴尼亚政府代表着全体阿尔巴尼亚人民的感情与愿望，认为五大国进行协商并缔结和平公约，将会消除现在的国际紧张局势，并展开和平解决目前仍然悬而未决的问题的前景。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国有八十六万五千八百八十五名公民在世界和平理事会要求缔结和平公约的宣言上签了名，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会在一九五一年一月十日通过了保卫和平法案，这就最清楚地表明了阿尔巴尼亚人民的意愿和对和平的热切希望。阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府全力支持这个愿望，并准备坚决地保卫和平到底。
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社讯：加拿大、乌拉圭、巴西的和平拥护者要求缔结五大国和平公约和结束朝鲜战争。
    最近，加拿大的和平拥护者召开的工会与教会工作者代表七百多人参加的会议，通过决议要求立即停止朝鲜战争，执行民族独立和不干涉他国内政的原则。会议结束时并举行了群众大会，通过加拿大和平大会主席文幼章博士提出的提案，敦促加拿大政府圆满答复世界人民和平大会关于缔结五大国和平公约的要求。
    乌拉圭全国和平理事会曾在蒙得维的亚召开了一个特别会议，参加会议的代表通过决议，反对缔结“乌美军事协定”，并要求迅速缔结五大国和平公约。
    在巴西，世界人民和平大会国际委员会委员布希鲍姆将军在里约热内卢巴西记者协会上发表演说，他在说明五大国和平公约对缓和国际紧张局势的重大意义后，要求巴西人民敦促政府支持这个和平的倡议。
