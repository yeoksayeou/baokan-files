Title: International Broadcasting Organization Holds 12th General Assembly, Re-elects Leadership; Mei Yi, Director of China's Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, Elected Chairman
Author:
Date: 1953-05-10
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 1st dispatch) (Delayed News): The International Broadcasting Organization held its 12th General Assembly, the 23rd session of the Administrative Council, and meetings of the Technical Committee in Prague from April 14th to 28th. Member organizations from eighteen countries participated in the meetings.

The General Assembly approved the re-election of the leadership of the International Broadcasting Organization as passed by the previous Administrative Council meeting. Mei Yi, Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau of the People's Republic of China, was elected as Chairman, and Josef Vrabitz, head of the Czechoslovak Broadcasting Committee, and I. Hadi, Director of the Hungarian Radio Broadcasting Bureau, were elected as Vice-Chairmen. The General Assembly also accepted the Broadcasting Committee of the Mongolian People's Republic as a member of the International Broadcasting Organization.

The General Assembly heard the work report of the International Broadcasting Organization from April 1952 to April 1953. During this period, member organizations of the International Broadcasting Organization cooperated with 120 institutions in 50 countries. Responding to the call of the Administrative Council last September, member organizations have broadcast the resolutions of the Vienna World People's Peace Congress in 45 different languages.

In the discussions at the meeting, many speakers specifically called for strengthening the publishing and distribution work of the International Broadcasting Organization, in order to disseminate the International Broadcasting Organization's experience in broadcasting technology, and to exchange radio programs to promote cultural exchange and friendship among member organizations. The resolutions passed at the meeting indicated that all member organizations have an unwavering determination to strive to expand international cooperation in broadcasting and to abide by international agreements, while also expanding and strengthening the ranks of peace fighters.

### 国际广播组织举行第十二届全体大会  改选领导机构，我广播事业局局长梅益当选为主席

1953-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    国际广播组织举行第十二届全体大会
    改选领导机构，我广播事业局局长梅益当选为主席 【新华社布拉格一日电】（迟到消息）：国际广播组织从四月十四日至二十八日在布拉格召开了第十二届全体大会、行政理事会第二十三届会议和技术委员会会议。有十八个国家的会员组织参加会议。
    全体大会批准了上届行政理事会会议通过的关于国际广播组织领导机构的改选；中华人民共和国广播事业局局长梅益当选为主席、捷克斯洛伐克广播委员会负责人约瑟夫·夫拉比茨和匈牙利无线电广播局局长伊·哈迪当选为副主席。大会并接受蒙古人民共和国广播委员会为国际广播组织的会员。
    全体大会听取了关于国际广播组织从一九五二年四月到一九五三年四月之间的工作报告。在这个期间，国际广播组织各会员组织曾经和五十个国家的一百二十个机构合作。各会员组织响应行政理事会去年九月的号召，已用四十五种不同的语言传播了维也纳世界人民和平大会的决议。
    在会议的讨论中，许多发言者特别号召加强国际广播组织的出版发行工作，以传播国际广播组织在广播技术上的经验，并为促进文化交流和会员组织之间的友好而交换广播节目。会议通过的决议表明：所有会员组织都有坚定不移的决心争取在广播事业方面扩大国际合作并遵守国际协定，同时，还要扩大与加强和平战士的队伍。
