Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Delegation to the Soviet Union Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-05-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency:

    The delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) to the Soviet Union, headed by Cai Shufan, to attend the "May Day" Labor Day celebrations, returned to Beijing on the afternoon of the 15th. Xu Zhizhen, Yang Zhihua, Cai Xin and other heads of departments of the ACFTU were at the airport to greet them.

### 全国总工会赴苏代表团回京

1953-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

    全国总工会赴苏代表团回京
    【新华社讯】中华全国总工会去苏联参加“五一”劳动节观礼代表团团长蔡树藩等一行，已在十五日下午回到北京。中华全国总工会各部门负责人许之桢、杨之华、蔡昕等到机场迎接。
