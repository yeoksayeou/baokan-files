Title: The US Side Murders Another Prisoner of War
Author:
Date: 1953-05-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, May 8th - On May 6th, another of our captured personnel was murdered by the US side in the US-run No. 1 Prisoner of War Camp on Geoje Island. As usual, the US side lied, saying that the prisoner of war "committed suicide."

### 美方又谋害战俘一名

1953-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

    美方又谋害战俘一名
    【新华社开城八日电】五月六日在巨济岛美方第一号战俘营中又有我方被俘人员一名被美方谋害。美方照例撒谎说这名战俘“是自杀的”。
