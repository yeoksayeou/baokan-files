Author:
Date: 1953-05-20

    Our Great Motherland
    The temporary bridge of the Yellow River Bridge, one of the important projects of the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway, has been opened to traffic. Now, various construction materials transported from all over the country are being transported to the construction site through the temporary bridge, which will greatly accelerate the construction of the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    兰新路的重要工程之一——黄河大桥便桥，已经通车了。现在，各地运来的各种建筑器材正源源地通过便桥运到工地去，这将大大地加速兰新路修建工程的速度。
