Title: British Co-operative Party Issues Statement Demanding British Government Not Be Dictated to by the United States
Author: 
Date: 1953-05-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, May 25th: According to Agence France-Presse, London: The British Co-operative Party issued a statement on the 23rd, demanding that the British government "immediately change its policy of being dictated to by the United States," and strongly advocated admitting the People's Republic of China to the United Nations Security Council. The statement also said that increasing trade with People's China is extremely beneficial.

### 英国合作党发表声明  要求英国政府不受美国指使

1953-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    英国合作党发表声明
    要求英国政府不受美国指使
    【新华社二十五日讯】据法新社伦敦讯：英国合作党二十三日发表声明，要求英国政府“立即改变其受美国指使的政策”，并力主接纳中华人民共和国参加联合国安全理事会。声明又说，增加对人民中国的贸易是极为有利的。
