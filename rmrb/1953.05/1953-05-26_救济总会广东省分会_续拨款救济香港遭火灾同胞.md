Title: Relief Society Guangdong Provincial Branch Continues to Allocate Funds to Aid Hong Kong Compatriots Affected by Fire
Author:
Date: 1953-05-26
Page: 1

    Relief Society Guangdong Provincial Branch
    Continues to Allocate Funds to Aid Hong Kong Compatriots Affected by Fire
    Xinhua News Agency: The Guangdong Provincial Branch of the China People's Relief Society has continued to allocate funds to aid our compatriots in Hong Kong's Kowloon Walled City, Tai Kok Tsui, and Wo Tsai Village in Sham Shui Po who have been affected by fire. This allocation totals 195 million yuan and was remitted to the Hong Kong Chinese General Chamber of Commerce on the 21st to be distributed on its behalf. While allocating funds for relief, the Guangdong Provincial Branch of the China People's Relief Society also wrote letters to these disaster-stricken compatriots, expressing sincere condolences.

### 救济总会广东省分会  续拨款救济香港遭火灾同胞

1953-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    救济总会广东省分会
    续拨款救济香港遭火灾同胞
    据新华社讯：中国人民救济总会广东省分会，继续拨款救济香港九龙城砦、大角嘴和深水埗窝仔村等地遭受火灾的我国同胞。这次拨款共一亿九千五百万元，已在二十一日汇交香港华商总会代为发放。中国人民救济总会广东省分会在拨款救济的同时，还写信给这些受灾同胞，表示亲切的慰问。
