Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1953-05-04
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The state-owned Central China Iron and Steel Company is engaged in both production and capital construction. This is the overhead crane of the company's steelmaking plant. It is loading the refined steel ingots onto a train to be transported to the rolling mill. In the distance is a new type of factory under construction.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营华中钢铁公司正在一面生产，一面进行基本建设。这是该公司炼钢厂的天车。它正把炼好的钢锭装入火车运到轧钢厂去。远处是一座正在建筑中的新型工厂。
