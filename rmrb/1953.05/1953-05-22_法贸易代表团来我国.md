Title: French Trade Delegation Arrives in China
Author:
Date: 1953-05-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 21st: A French trade delegation, led by Mr. de Bras, Chairman of the French International Trade Promotion Committee, and composed of thirteen individuals from industry, commerce, banking, and shipping, arrived here in Berlin on the 19th from Paris, en route to China. Shi Zhiang, representative of the China National Import and Export Corporation, welcomed and saw them off at the airport.
    The French trade delegation is prepared to discuss with relevant Chinese parties the issue of further developing trade relations between the two countries.

### 法贸易代表团来我国

1953-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    法贸易代表团来我国
    【新华社柏林二十一日电】以法国国际贸易促进委员会主席德·勃拉斯为首的、由工业、商业、银行及航运业人士十三人所组成的法国贸易代表团十九日从巴黎抵此，途经此间前往中国。在机场欢迎和欢送他们的有中国进出口公司代表石志昂。
    法国贸易代表团准备和中国各有关方面讨论进一步发展两国贸易关系的问题。
