Title: Central Flood Control Headquarters Established
Author:
Date: 1953-05-08
Page: 1

    Central Flood Control Headquarters Established
    (Xinhua News Agency, 7th) The Central Flood Control Headquarters was established in Beijing on April 7th and has begun operations. The State Council of the Central People's Government appointed Vice Premier Dong Biwu to concurrently serve as Director, and Minister of Water Resources of the Central People's Government Fu Zuoyi, and Minister of the Operations Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission Zhang Zhen to concurrently serve as Deputy Directors; the Ministry of Water Resources of the Central People's Government serves as the daily operating agency.

### 中央防汛总指挥部成立

1953-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中央防汛总指挥部成立
    【新华社七日讯】中央防汛总指挥部已于四月七日在北京成立，并已开始办公。中央人民政府政务院任命副总理董必武兼任主任，中央人民政府水利部部长傅作义、中国人民革命军事委员会作战部部长张震兼任副主任；并以中央人民政府水利部为日常办事机构。
