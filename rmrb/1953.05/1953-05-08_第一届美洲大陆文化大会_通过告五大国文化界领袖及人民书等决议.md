Title: First Inter-American Cultural Congress Passes Resolutions Such as "Letter to Cultural Leaders and Peoples of the Five Great Powers"
Author:
Date: 1953-05-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, 7th: The First Inter-American Cultural Congress passed a letter to the cultural leaders and peoples of the United States, the Soviet Union, Great Britain, the People's Republic of China, and France, as well as resolutions on cultural exchange between countries and the protection of the culture and the rights of cultural workers in Latin American countries.

The letter to the cultural leaders and peoples of the United States, the Soviet Union, Great Britain, the People's Republic of China, and France points out the importance of an agreement between the governments of the five major powers. The resolution on cultural exchange between countries urges cultural workers to promote cultural exchange between Latin American countries, as well as between these countries and countries on other continents. The resolution on protecting the culture and the rights of cultural workers in Latin American countries urges intellectuals in Latin American countries to unite and intensify their struggle to protect their economic and political rights. This congress is a powerful demonstration of Latin American intellectuals striving for peace, national independence, and democratic freedom in Latin American countries. Intellectuals from Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Colombia, Costa Rica, Cuba, Chile, the United States, Guatemala, Haiti, Mexico, Paraguay, Peru, Uruguay, and Venezuela attended the congress.

### 第一届美洲大陆文化大会  通过告五大国文化界领袖及人民书等决议

1953-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

    第一届美洲大陆文化大会
    通过告五大国文化界领袖及人民书等决议
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社蒙得维的亚六日讯：第一届美洲大陆文化大会通过了一个告美国、苏联、英国、中华人民共和国和法国的文化界领袖和人民书以及关于各国间文化交流和保卫拉丁美洲国家的文化与文化工作者权利的决议。
    告美国、苏联、英国、中华人民共和国和法国文化界领袖及人民书指出五大国政府达成协议的重要性。关于各国间文化交流的决议敦促文化工作者促进拉丁美洲国家之间以及这些国家和其他大陆国家之间的文化交流。保卫拉丁美洲国家的文化与文化工作者权利的决议敦促拉丁美洲国家的知识分子团结起来，加紧为保卫他们经济与政治的权利而斗争。这个大会是拉丁美洲的知识分子争取拉丁美洲国家的和平、民族独立与民主自由的一个强大的示威。出席大会的有阿根廷、玻利维亚、巴西、哥伦比亚、哥斯达黎加、古巴、智利、美国、危地马拉、海地、墨西哥、巴拉圭、秘鲁、乌拉圭和委内瑞拉等国的知识分子。
