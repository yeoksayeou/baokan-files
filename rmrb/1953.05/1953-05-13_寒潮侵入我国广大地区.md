Title: Cold Wave Invades Vast Areas of China
Author:
Date: 1953-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a cold wave message at 21:00 on the 12th: The front of the cold wave invading our country from Mongolia has reached the Yangtze River coast at 20:00 tonight. Tomorrow morning, the temperature in Inner Mongolia, Northeast China, North China, and eastern Gansu will drop significantly; the minimum temperature in northern Inner Mongolia, northwestern Northeast China, and eastern Gansu will drop below 0 degrees Celsius; the minimum temperature in southern Inner Mongolia, Shanxi, and northern Shaanxi may drop to around 0 degrees Celsius. The wind force in the Yangtze River basin will generally increase to level 6 or 7, and strong winds in Dongting Lake, Poyang Lake, the East China Sea, and the Taiwan Strait will reach level 7 or 8. The cold air will continue to move southward and may reach the vicinity of the Nanling Mountains on the afternoon of the 13th.

### 寒潮侵入我国广大地区

1953-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    寒潮侵入我国广大地区
    【新华社十二日讯】中央气象台在十二日二十一时发布寒潮消息：由蒙古向我国侵入的寒潮，它的前方已在今晚二十点到达长江沿岸。我国内蒙古、东北、华北和甘肃东部地区，明晨气温将显著下降；内蒙古北部、东北西北部和甘肃东部最低气温将降到摄氏零度以下；内蒙古南部和山西、陕西北部一带最低气温可降到摄氏零度左右。长江流域风力普遍可增至六、七级，洞庭湖、鄱阳湖和东海、台湾海峡，大风将达七、八级左右。冷空气继续南进，十三日下午可到南岭附近。
