Title: Soviet Union Abolishes the Soviet Control Commission in Germany; German People Demand Peaceful Resolution of the German Question
Author:
Date: 1953-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 29th: TASS Moscow, May 28th: The Soviet Council of Ministers has resolved to abolish the Soviet Control Commission in Germany and to relieve the Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Forces of his control duties in the German Democratic Republic. Henceforth, his activities will be limited to commanding the Soviet Forces stationed in Germany.

The Council of Ministers decided to establish a Soviet High Commissioner for Germany, whose office will be located in Berlin. The High Commissioner is tasked with representing Soviet interests in Germany and supervising the activities of the authorities of the German Democratic Republic to ensure that it fulfills the obligations stipulated in the Allied Potsdam Agreement regarding Germany. The High Commissioner is also instructed to maintain appropriate contact with representatives of the occupation authorities of the United States, Britain, and France on matters of all-German nature arising from the Four-Power decisions on Germany.

Ambassador V.S. Semyonov has been appointed as the Soviet High Commissioner for Germany.

Xinhua News Agency, May 29th: German News Agency, May 28th: The German Child Protection Association issued a declaration for International Children's Day on June 1st. The declaration states: "On this International Children's Day, we demand the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany. We Germans feel a special responsibility to fight for the rapid implementation of the Soviet government's new proposals for a peaceful settlement of the German question."

### 苏联撤消苏联对德管制委员会  德国人民要求和平解决德国问题

1953-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联撤消苏联对德管制委员会
    德国人民要求和平解决德国问题
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十八日讯：苏联部长会议决定，撤消苏联对德管制委员会，解除苏军总司令在德意志民主共和国内的管制职务，以后他的活动限于指挥驻德苏军。
    部长会议决定设立苏联驻德高级专员，高级专员设办公处在柏林，奉命代表苏联在德国的利益，并监督德意志民主共和国权力机构的活动，使它履行盟国关于德国的波茨坦决定中所规定的义务。高级专员并奉命与美、英、法三国占领当局的代表，在由四国关于德国所协议的决定产生的、全德性质的问题上，保持相应的接触。
    维·斯·谢麦诺夫大使已被任命为苏联驻德高级专员。
    【新华社二十九日讯】德意志通讯社二十八日讯：德国保护儿童协会为“六一”儿童节发表宣言。宣言说：“在这国际儿童节的时候，我们要求缔结对德和约。我们德国人感到有特殊的责任，要为迅速实现苏联政府关于和平解决德国问题的新建议而斗争。”
