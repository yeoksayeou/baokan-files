Title: From the Motherland - Welcome to the Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea to Console Art Troupes
Author: Xiao Sheng
Date: 1953-05-10
Page: 3

From the Motherland
——Welcome to the Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea to Console Art Troupes
Chinese People's Volunteer Army Unit, Xiao Sheng
　　The hills of Korea are covered with "Kimilsungia" [Note],
　　The art troupe from the motherland has arrived at our front lines,
　　Bringing the dances and songs of our hometown,
　　The songs are filled with the deep affection of the people of the motherland!
　　Smiling red faces,
　　The faces of long-separated loved ones appear before our eyes.
　　We clap our hands until they are sore, still feeling it's not enough,
　　The cheers from the audience and the stage merge into one.
　　Even a single word, we listen carefully,
　　Every pair of eyes shines brightly.
　　Staring at your every movement,
　　It's like seeing the fields and mountains of the motherland.
　　Everyone wants to get closer to the front,
　　In order to see your faces even more clearly.
　　After one performance ends, we welcome another,
　　Never tiring of watching—because all of this comes from the motherland.
　　Tears of excitement float in our eyes,
　　Everyone's chest is like a boiling pot.
　　Motherland, we vow to defend everything you have,
　　We firmly believe that glory and victory will always be ours.
　　[Note] The Korean name for a flower.

### 祖国来的——欢迎中国人民赴朝慰问文艺工作团
箫笙
1953-05-10
第3版()
专栏：

    祖国来的
    ——欢迎中国人民赴朝慰问文艺工作团
    中国人民志愿军某部　箫笙
    朝鲜的山坡开遍“金达莉”〔注〕，
    祖国来的文工团到了我们阵地，
    带来的是故乡的舞姿和歌声，
    歌声里藏着祖国人民的深情蜜意！
    一个个红红的脸庞露着微笑，
    久别的亲人们的面影出现在眼前。
    把手掌拍痛了还恨不够响啊，
    台下和台上的欢呼连成一片。
    那怕是一个字，我们也听个仔细，
    一双双眼睛都光芒闪闪。
    凝视着你们的每一个动作，
    就像看见了祖国的田园山川。
    谁的身子都想再挨近一些前面，
    为的是想把你们的模样儿看得更加明显。
    一个完了，再欢迎来一个，
    百看不厌——都为啊，这一切是祖国来的。
    激动的泪水在眼眶里浮动，
    谁的胸口都像一锅沸水翻腾。
    祖国啊，我们宣誓要捍卫你的一切，
    我们坚信光荣和胜利永远是我们的。
    〔注〕朝鲜的花名。
