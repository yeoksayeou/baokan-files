Title: Korean Publication of the First Volume, First Edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong"

Author:

Date: 1953-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th – According to a report in "Nodong Sinmun," the Korean translation of the first part of the first volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" has been published by the Korean Workers' Party Publishing House. The publishing house has decided to divide each volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" into several fascicles for translation and publication. "Nodong Sinmun," in its report, briefly introduced the contents of the first part of the first volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" and commented that: Comrade Mao Zedong's works are a history of the Chinese people's arduous and tortuous struggle under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party to finally achieve a historic victory, a compass guiding the Chinese people in the political direction, a fighting program of the Chinese people, and a force inspiring the Chinese people's struggle. In dealing with specific Chinese issues in his works, Comrade Mao Zedong steadfastly followed the teachings of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, and especially remained infinitely faithful to Comrade Stalin's instructions on the Eastern revolution.

"Nodong Sinmun" said: Comrade Mao Zedong's works are revolutionary documents of great significance. Therefore, countries all over the world are translating and publishing "Selected Works of Mao Zedong." "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" has been widely welcomed by readers in various countries. The publication of the Korean translation of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" is of great significance to the Korean people.

### 朝鲜出版毛泽东选集第一卷上册译本

1953-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜出版毛泽东选集第一卷上册译本
    【新华社平壤九日电】据“劳动新闻”报道，“毛泽东选集”第一卷上册的朝鲜文译本已由朝鲜劳动党出版社出版。出版社决定把“毛泽东选集”各卷分为几个分册翻译出版。“劳动新闻”在报道中扼要地介绍了“毛泽东选集”第一卷上册的内容，并评论说：毛泽东同志的著作，是中国人民在中国共产党领导下经过艰难曲折的斗争终于取得历史性胜利的历史，是给中国人民指引政治方向的指南针，是中国人民的斗争纲领，也是鼓舞中国人民斗争的力量。毛泽东同志在他的著作中处理中国具体问题时，坚定地遵循马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的学说，尤其无限忠实于斯大林同志关于东方革命的指示。
    “劳动新闻”说：毛泽东同志的著作是具有重大意义的革命文献。因此，世界各国都在翻译和出版“毛泽东选集”。“毛泽东选集”受到了各国读者的广泛欢迎。“毛泽东选集”朝鲜文译本的出版，对于朝鲜人民是有重大意义的。
