Title: Burmese Government Labor Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-05-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd—The Burmese Government Labor Delegation, a group of six who came to visit our country, has departed Beijing by plane this morning, May 22nd, after visiting and touring Tianjin, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Shenyang and other places. Li Wenyi, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government, Xu Zhizhen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Lang Xinkang, Deputy Section Chief of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others went to the airport to see them off.
    U Hla Maung, the Burmese Ambassador to China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 缅甸政府劳动考察团离京

1953-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府劳动考察团离京
    【新华社二十二日讯】前来我国访问的缅甸政府劳动考察团一行六人，在天津、上海、杭州、沈阳等地参观访问后，已在二十二日晨搭机离京。前往机场送行的有中央人民政府劳动部办公厅副主任李文宜，中华全国总工会书记处书记许之桢，外交部交际处副科长郎新康等人。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂也赴机场送行。
