    Our Great Motherland
    Workers in the fitter workshop of the Lushun-Dalian Machinery Plant No. 7, while celebrating "May Day," proposed the slogan of manufacturing a greater number of cement mixers to meet the needs of various basic construction departments, thus promoting production. This is the cement mixer that the plant is about to complete.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    旅大机械—七厂钳工车间工人，在庆祝“五一”的时候，提出制造数量更多的水泥搅拌机供给各基本建设部门需要的口号，推动了生产。这是该厂即将制造完成的水泥搅拌机。
