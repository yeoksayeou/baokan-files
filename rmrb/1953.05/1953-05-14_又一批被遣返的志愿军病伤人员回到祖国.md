Title: Another Group of Repatriated Volunteer Army Sick and Wounded Personnel Return to the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1953-05-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, An Dong, 13th. Another group of 244 sick and wounded personnel of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who were repatriated returned to the motherland on the 13th.

　　The sick and wounded personnel were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in An Dong City. When they passed through the central streets of An Dong City, the masses waved and cheered from the roadside, vying to shake hands with them. The sick and wounded personnel were deeply moved in the vehicles, shouting: "Long live the Chinese Communist Party!" "Long live the People's Republic of China!" "Long live Chairman Mao!" They sang loudly "Without the Communist Party, there would be no New China" and "Unity is Strength" and other songs. When people threw flowers to them, the sick and wounded personnel couldn't help but burst into tears. When the sick and wounded personnel arrived at the hospital, nearby workers and students ran to warmly welcome them. The repatriated sick and wounded personnel who returned to the country on the 7th of this month also stood in front of the hospital to welcome them.

### 又一批被遣返的志愿军病伤人员回到祖国

1953-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

    又一批被遣返的志愿军病伤人员回到祖国
    【新华社安东十三日电】被遣返的中国人民志愿军病伤人员又一批二百四十四人，在十三日回到祖国。
    病伤人员在安东市受到各界人民的热情欢迎，他们经过安东市中心街道时，群众在路旁招手欢呼，争相前去和他们握手致意。病伤人员在车上感奋万分，高呼：“中国共产党万岁！”“中华人民共和国万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”他们高声唱着“没有共产党就没有新中国”和“团结就是力量”等歌曲。当人们把鲜花投给他们时，病伤人员不禁热泪夺眶而出。病伤人员到达医院时，附近的工人、学生都跑来热情地欢迎他们。本月七日归国的被遣返的病伤人员也站在医院门前欢迎。
