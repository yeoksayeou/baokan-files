Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation to the Soviet Union Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd: In order to further strengthen the work of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters has sent a delegation to the Soviet Union for study. The delegation departed from Beijing on the 23rd. The delegation consists of 40 members and 2 interpreters, headed by Li Furen, with Liao Jingtian and Li Zhanwu as deputy heads, and Wen Jianping as secretary-general.

Those who went to the station to see them off included: Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters; Li Zhigan, Secretary-General of the North China Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Gao Zhendong, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee; Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director; and Tieleev, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in Beijing.

### 中苏友好协会总会赴苏参观团离京

1953-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会总会赴苏参观团离京
    【新华社二十三日讯】中苏友好协会总会为了进一步加强中苏友好协会的工作，特派遣参观团到苏联学习，该团于二十三日启程离京。参观团共有团员四十人，翻译二人，团长李敷仁，副团长廖经天，李沾吾，秘书长温建平。
    前往车站欢送的有：中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞，华北中苏友好协会总干事李之乾，北京市中苏友好协会副总干事高振东，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、副局长陈忠经，此外还有苏联对外文化协会驻京代表季列也夫等。
