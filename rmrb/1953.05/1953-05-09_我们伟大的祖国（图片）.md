Our Great Motherland
In order to more widely and deeply study the advanced experience of the Soviet Union in sowing rice, the State-run Lutai Farm, at the beginning of the spring ploughing this year, had Comrade Li Junlun, the instructor of the farm's tractor driving training class and a national model worker, teach the trainees the method of deep ploughing and close planting. This is the scene of Comrade Li Junlun explaining.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营芦台农场为了更广泛、更深入地学习苏联播种水稻的先进经验，在今年春耕开始的时候，由该场拖拉机驾驶训练班教员、全国劳动模范李俊伦同志教导学员们进行深耕密植的方法。这是李俊伦同志正在讲解的情形。
