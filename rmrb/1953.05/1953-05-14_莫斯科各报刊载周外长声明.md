Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1953-05-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 13th: Moscow newspapers on the 13th all published the news of American aircraft violating the airspace of the People's Republic of China and killing and wounding Chinese people, and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's protest against this incident.

### 莫斯科各报刊载周外长声明

    莫斯科各报刊载周外长声明
