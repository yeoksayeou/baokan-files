Title: Swedish National Peace Committee Issues Statement Supporting Great Power Negotiations to Ease Tensions
Author:
Date: 1953-05-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, May 26th: The Swedish National Peace Committee has issued a statement supporting the communiqué released by the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council following its meeting in Stockholm on May 5th and 6th, which advocated for negotiations between the major powers.
The statement said: The Peace Committee welcomes these recent measures aimed at easing international tensions. More and more people from all walks of life in Sweden are supporting the demand for consultations between the major powers.

### 瑞典全国和平委员会发表声明  支持大国协商缓和紧张局势

1953-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典全国和平委员会发表声明
    支持大国协商缓和紧张局势
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩二十六日讯：瑞典全国和平委员会发表声明，支持世界和平理事会常务委员会于五月五日和六日在斯德哥尔摩举行会议后所发表的支持大国协商的公报。
    声明说：和平委员会欢迎这些最近的措施，这些措施的目的是缓和国际紧张局势。愈来愈多的瑞典各界人民都支持大国间举行商谈的要求。
