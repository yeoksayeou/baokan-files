Title: Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble Holds Performance Gala; Chairman Mao Attends, Watches, and Receives Ensemble Members; Premier Zhou Hosts Reception for Entire Ensemble

Author:
Date: 1953-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: The Polish People's Republic Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble held a music and dance performance gala at Huairen Hall in Beijing on the evening of the 9th. The gala was hosted by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government.

Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government attended the gala and, before the start of the program, received the head of the song and dance ensemble, Pannufnik; the deputy head, Donbarovsky; the secretary, Shatziewska; the dance instructor, Paparinski; and the Silver Cross Medal winner, actor Mieczyslaw. Accompanying the meeting was the Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, Kirillok.

Also attending the gala were: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Huang Yanpei, Deng Xiaoping, as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and famous writers, musicians, dancers, artists, and actors from the capital.

Foreign guests attending the performance included Kirillok, Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, Kuznetsov, Ambassador of the Soviet Union to China, and diplomatic personnel from the Polish and Soviet embassies.

The Polish People's Republic Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble performed many wonderful programs full of national characteristics and folk styles at the gala. These programs, full of youthful vitality and joyful mood, were warmly welcomed by the attendees.

Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government held a reception on the night of the 9th to entertain all members of the Polish People's Republic Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble, who were invited to perform in China. Kirillok, Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, was also invited to attend the reception.

Those attending the reception included Chen Shutong, Xi Zhongxun, Ma Yinchu, Qian Junrui, Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, Zhou Yang, Ding Xilin, as well as famous figures from the literary, film, art, music, drama, dance, and opera circles.

At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai and the head of the song and dance ensemble, Pannufnik, delivered speeches successively. At the meeting, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to the friendship and cooperation between China and Poland, to the health of the people's leader of the Polish People's Republic, President Beirut, to the health of the Chinese people's leader, Chairman Mao, and to the health of the Soviet people's leaders headed by Chairman Malenkov.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

According to Xinhua News Agency: On the 7th and 8th, 140 young dance workers from various art and literary units in the capital learned various excellent Polish folk art programs from the Polish People's Republic Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble. In two days, the young dance workers from various units participating in the study had learned seven Polish folk dances. After the Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble arrived in Beijing, the art and literary units of the capital selected twelve young dance workers to form a study group to regularly observe and learn various programs of the song and dance ensemble. This dance study for each unit was based on the members of the study group. In addition to actively participating in dance studies, the young dance workers in the study group also learned many beautiful Polish folk songs.

### 波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团举行表演晚会  毛主席出席观看并接见该团人员  周总理举行招待会招待歌舞团全体人员

1953-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团举行表演晚会
    毛主席出席观看并接见该团人员
    周总理举行招待会招待歌舞团全体人员
   【新华社九日讯】波兰人民共和国玛佐夫舍歌舞团于九日晚在北京怀仁堂举行音乐、舞蹈表演晚会，主持这一晚会的是中央人民政府文化部。
    中央人民政府毛泽东主席出席了晚会，并在节目开始前，接见了歌舞团团长潘努夫尼克，副团长顿巴鲁夫斯基，秘书长沙杰夫斯卡，舞蹈指导巴帕林斯基，银十字奖章获得者、演员米齐斯拉夫。陪同进见的有波兰人民共和国驻我国大使基里洛克。
    出席晚会的还有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深、秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理周恩来，副总理黄炎培、邓小平，以及中央人民政府各部门首长和首都的著名作家、音乐家、舞蹈家、美术家、演员等。
    出席观看表演的外宾，有波兰人民共和国驻我国大使基里洛克，苏联驻我国大使库兹涅佐夫及波、苏两国大使馆的外交人员。
    波兰人民共和国玛佐夫舍歌舞团在晚会上演出了许多富有民族色彩与民间风格的精采节目。这些充满着青春的活力与欢乐情调的节目，受到了与会者的热烈欢迎。
   【新华社九日讯】中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理在九日夜举行招待会，招待应邀前来我国表演的波兰人民共和国玛佐夫舍歌舞团的全体人员。波兰人民共和国驻我国大使基里洛克也应邀参加了招待会。
    出席招待会的，有陈叔通、习仲勋、马寅初、钱俊瑞、章汉夫、伍修权、周扬、丁西林，以及文学、电影、美术、音乐、戏剧、舞蹈、戏曲界的著名人士。
    在招待会上，周恩来总理、歌舞团团长潘努夫尼克曾相继致词。会上宾主频频举杯为中波两国的友好和合作，为波兰人民共和国人民领袖贝鲁特主席的健康，为中国人民领袖毛主席的健康，为以马林科夫主席为首的苏联人民领袖们的健康干杯。
    招待会在热烈、友好的空气中进行。
    据新华社讯：七日和八日，首都各文艺工作单位的青年舞蹈工作者一百四十人向波兰人民共和国玛佐夫舍歌舞团学习了各种优秀的波兰民间艺术节目。在两天内，各单位参加学习的青年舞蹈工作者已经分别学会了七种波兰的民间舞蹈。在玛佐夫舍歌舞团到达北京后，首都各文艺工作单位就选派了十二个青年舞蹈工作者，组成学习小组，对歌舞团的各种节目进行经常的观摩、学习。这次各单位的舞蹈学习就以学习小组的人作骨干。学习小组的青年舞蹈工作者们除了积极参加舞蹈学习外，还学会了多支优美的波兰民歌。
