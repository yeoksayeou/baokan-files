Title: Czechoslovak Republic Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. In accordance with the provisions of the Sino-Czechoslovak Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement, the four-member delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic to the first meeting of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation arrived in Beijing by plane on the 6th.  Those who went to the airport to welcome them were: Jia Tuofu, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation and Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council; Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the Central People's Government, and Li Zhuozhi, Commercial Counselor of our Embassy in the Czechoslovak Republic, who are members of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation. Also at the airport to welcome them were I. Sochorek, head of the Czechoslovak Trade Delegation, and Šar, Commercial Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in our country.
    Delegation list:
    Head: František Vlasák, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Group of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation, Deputy Director of the Czechoslovak State Planning Office
    Members: Ferdinand Fenzl, Member of the Czechoslovak Group of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation, Deputy Minister of the Czechoslovak Construction Industry Karel Hatzmas
  Miroslava Jefremová, Member of the Czechoslovak Group of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation, Secretary of the Czechoslovak Group of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation

### 参加中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会会议  捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团到达北京

1953-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    参加中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会会议
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团到达北京
    【新华社六日讯】根据中捷科学与技术合作协定的规定，捷克斯洛伐克共和国参加中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会第一届会议代表团一行四人，六日乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有：中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会中国组主席、中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任贾拓夫，中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会中国组委员：中央人民政府第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵、我国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使馆商务参赞李琢之等多人。到机场欢迎的还有捷克斯洛伐克商务代表团团长伊·索契克及捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆商务参赞沙尔等。
    代表团名单：
    团长：弗朗齐歇克·伏拉萨克  中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会捷克斯洛伐克组主席、捷克斯洛伐克国家计划局副局长
    团员：斐迪南德·芬茨尔  中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会捷克斯洛伐克组委员、捷克斯洛伐克建筑工业部副部长卡列尔·哈茨马斯
  中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会捷克斯洛伐克组委员米罗斯拉瓦·叶弗列罗娃中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会捷克斯洛伐克组秘书
