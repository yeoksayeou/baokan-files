Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Issues Announcement Acknowledging Congratulations on the Chinese Trade Union Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 14th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions today issued an announcement expressing gratitude to all parties for their congratulations on the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions. The announcement reads as follows:

    The Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions received 201 congratulatory gifts and 1,639 congratulatory telegrams and letters from trade union organizations of various countries, trade union delegations of various countries, Party committees at all levels of the Chinese Communist Party, various democratic parties, various people's organizations, administrative departments of various industrial and mining enterprises, trade union organizations at all levels, and individual workers, engineers, technicians, and staff members.

    The All-China Federation of Trade Unions hereby expresses its deep gratitude to the above-mentioned organizations and individuals who sent congratulatory gifts, telegrams, and letters.

### 中华全国总工会发表公告  答谢各方对中国工会代表大会的祝贺

1953-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会发表公告
    答谢各方对中国工会代表大会的祝贺
    【新华社十四日讯】中华全国总工会今日发表公告，向各方对中国工会第七次全国代表大会的祝贺，表示谢意。公告文如下：
    中国工会第七次全国代表大会，收到了各国工会团体、各国工会代表团、中国共产党各级党委、各民主党派、各人民团体、各工矿企业行政、各级工会组织及工人、工程技术人员、职员个人对大会的贺礼二百零一件、贺电、贺信一千六百三十九件。
    中华全国总工会谨向上述致送贺礼、贺电、贺信的组织与个人，表示深切的谢意。
