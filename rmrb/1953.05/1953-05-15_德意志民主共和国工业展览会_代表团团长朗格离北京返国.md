Title: Head of the German Democratic Republic Industrial Exhibition Delegation, Lange, Departs Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1953-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 14th: Lange, the head of the German Democratic Republic Industrial Exhibition Delegation, and one member of the delegation, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 14th to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Chen Ming, Minister of the Exhibition Department, Xie Xiao Nai, Minister of the Liaison Department, and Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government.

Also at the airport to see them off were Dürber, Commercial Counselor of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic in China and Deputy Head of the Industrial Exhibition Delegation, and some members of the delegation remaining in Beijing.

### 德意志民主共和国工业展览会  代表团团长朗格离北京返国

1953-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国工业展览会
    代表团团长朗格离北京返国
    【新华社十四日讯】德意志民主共和国工业展览会代表团团长朗格及团员一人于十四日晨乘飞机离北京返国。
    到机场欢送的有：中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎、展览部部长陈明、联络部部长谢筱迺及中央人民政府对外贸易部交际处处长杨勉等。
    到机场欢送的还有：德意志民主共和国驻我国外交使团商务参赞、工业展览会代表团副团长杜尔伯及代表团部分留京团员。
