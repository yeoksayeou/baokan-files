Title: Soviet Army Private Bludanov stationed in Lushunkou writes to thank the Chinese Art Troupe for their慰问演出
Author:
Date: 1953-05-21
Page: 3

Dear Editor:
    Not long ago, the Art Troupe of the Delegation of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to慰问 the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area held a concert for the Soviet soldiers here.

    This wonderful concert made us feel very happy. It encouraged us to strive for new achievements in military studies, and made us burn even more in our hearts with love and friendship for the brotherly Chinese people and their great leader Comrade Mao Zedong. After participating in this concert, we despise and hate even more the bloody provocateurs of a new war—the American imperialists. They are barbarously destroying the peaceful cities and villages of North Korea and carrying out robber-style provocative attacks on North Korean territory.

    I request that your newspaper convey my personal and my comrades’ heartfelt thanks and deep respect to all the actors of the Art Troupe for this wonderful concert performance. We also wish them greater creative achievements.

    The Chinese government, like the Soviet government, pays special attention to the consolidation of Sino-Soviet friendship. On the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army, the Chinese government awarded the Soviet soldiers commemorative medals of the People's Republic of China, which is a clear expression of the Chinese people's love and deep respect for the heroic Soviet Army and, through them, for the entire Soviet people.

    I am very honored that some of my outstanding comrades in military and political training—Sergeant Samsudov, Soldier Masanikov, and others (including myself)—have won high-level medals.

    I wish the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union will grow and be consolidated.

    Soviet Army Private Alexey Bludanov, Lushunkou
        April 4th

### 驻旅顺口苏军下士布留兰诺夫来信感谢我国文艺工作团为他们作慰问演出

1953-05-21
第3版()
专栏：

    驻旅顺口苏军下士布留兰诺夫来信感谢我国文艺工作团为他们作慰问演出亲爱的编辑同志：
    不久以前，中华人民共和国中央人民政府慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团的文艺工作团曾为我们这里的苏军战士举行了音乐会。
    这次精彩的音乐会使我们感到非常欣慰。它鼓舞我们在军事学习方面争取新的成绩，使我们更加在心里燃起对兄弟中国的人民及其伟大领袖毛泽东同志的热爱和友谊。在参加这次音乐会以后，我们更加鄙视和憎恨新战争的血腥挑拨者——美帝国主义。他们野蛮地毁灭着和平的北朝鲜的城市和乡村，对北朝鲜的领土进行了强盗式的挑衅的袭击。
    我要求你报把我个人和我的同志们为这次美好的音乐会的演出所表示的衷心感谢和深的敬意转达给文艺工作团的全体演员。我们并预祝他们取得更伟大的创造性的成绩。
    中华人民共和国政府也正像苏联政府一样，对于苏中友谊的巩固给予特别的关怀。在苏联建军三十五周年时，中国政府授给苏军战士以中华人民共和国的纪念章，便是中国人民对英勇的苏军，和通过他们，对全体苏联人民表示友爱和深刻敬重的明显表示。
    由于我的一些在军事和政治修养方面的优秀同志—中士萨姆苏道夫、士兵马萨尼柯夫和其他一些人（其中也包括我个人）荣获高级奖章，我感到很荣幸。
    祝苏中两国人民牢不可破的友谊增长和巩固起来。
    旅顺口苏军下士亚历克赛·布留兰诺夫
        四月四日
