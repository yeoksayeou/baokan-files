Title: Chinese Trade Union National Congress Held Group Meetings on the 8th
Author:
Date: 1953-05-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions held group meetings on the 8th, and discussed in groups the draft Constitution of the Trade Union of the People's Republic of China and the nomination of candidates for members of the Seventh Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

The speeches of representatives from various regions and industrial trade unions at the plenary meeting have come to an end. A total of thirty-eight representatives spoke at the conference on the 5th, 6th, and 7th. In their speeches, they reported on the great achievements of workers in various regions and industries in the national economic recovery and construction, pointed out the achievements, shortcomings, and errors in the work of trade unions in various regions and industries, and based on Comrade Liu Shaoqi's instructions on the general direction of trade union work in the future, put forward the specific efforts of trade unions in various regions and industries in the future. They unanimously agreed with the reports of Lai Ruoyu, Xu Zhizhen, and Li Zaiwen, and expressed that they would strive to implement the trade union work guidelines and tasks formulated by the conference during the national construction period and to complete and overfulfill the national construction plan.

### 中国工会全国代表大会八日举行小组会

1953-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中国工会全国代表大会八日举行小组会
    【新华社八日讯】中国工会第七次全国代表大会在八日举行小组会，分组讨论了中华人民共和国工会章程草案及中华全国总工会第七届执行委员会委员的候选人提名问题。
    各地区、各产业工会代表在全体大会上的发言已告一结束。在五日、六日、七日三天大会上发言的代表共三十八人，他们在发言中分别报告了各地区、各产业的工人在国家经济恢复和建设中的伟大成就，指出了各地区、各产业工会工作中的成绩、缺点和错误，并根据刘少奇同志所指示的今后工会工作总方针，提出了各地区、各产业工会今后的具体努力方向。他们一致同意赖若愚、许之桢和栗再温的报告，并表示将为执行大会所制定的国家建设时期的工会工作方针和任务、为完成和超额完成国家建设计划而奋斗。
