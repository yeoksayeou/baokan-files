Title: US, Britain, and France to Hold Summit Conference
Author:
Date: 1953-05-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 22nd: TASS, Paris, May 22nd—It is officially announced here that the heads of government of the United States, Britain, and France will hold talks in mid-June. French Prime Minister Mayer (note: who has resigned due to a government crisis) announced in the National Assembly on May 21st that this meeting would be held.

Washington and London have also issued similar statements regarding this meeting, which will likely be held in Bermuda. According to these sources, this "Big Three Summit" was initiated by President Eisenhower.

According to Mayer's statement, the purpose of this meeting of the heads of government of the United States, Britain, and France is to "enable the three governments to reach a consensus on the issues to be discussed at the Four Powers Conference."

### 美英法将举行三国首脑会议

1953-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    美英法将举行三国首脑会议
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社巴黎二十二日讯：此间正式宣布美国、英国和法国政府首脑将于六月中举行会谈。法国总理迈耶（按：已因政府发生危机辞职）于五月二十一日在国民议会宣布将举行这个会谈。
    华盛顿和伦敦方面亦已发表了关于这个会谈的同样的声明，这个会谈很可能在百慕大举行。据这些消息说，这个“三巨头会谈”是艾森豪威尔总统倡议的。
    据迈耶的声明说，美、英、法三国政府首脑这个会谈的目的是“使三国政府对于四大国会议所将要商谈的问题取得一致的意见”。
