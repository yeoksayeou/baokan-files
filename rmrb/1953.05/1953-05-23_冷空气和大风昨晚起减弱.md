Title: Cold Air and Strong Winds Weakening Since Last Night
Author:
Date: 1953-05-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd—The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a gale warning at 10 PM on May 22nd: The cold air that had reached the Yangtze River basin from Gansu and the strong winds caused by this cold air mass have both weakened as of the evening of the 22nd. On the 23rd, except for the western, central, and southern parts of Northeast China which will continue to experience strong winds of around force six, and the Bohai Sea, Yellow Sea, South China Sea, and Taiwan Strait where winds may still reach force six or seven, the impact of strong winds is weakening in other parts of the country.

### 冷空气和大风昨晚起减弱

1953-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    冷空气和大风昨晚起减弱
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央气象台五月二十二日晚十时发布大风消息：自甘肃一带进抵长江流域的冷空气和受此冷空气影响而形成的大风，二十二日晚均已减弱。二十三日，我国除东北的西部、中部及南部地区仍继续有六级左右大风，渤海、黄海、南海及台湾海峡风力仍可达六、七级外，其他各地所受大风影响均在减弱中。
