Title: May Day in the Capital (Pictures)
Author: 
Date: 1953-05-03
Page: 5

    May Day in the Capital
    A parade of 500,000 people in the capital passes through Tiananmen Square. (Special Correspondent for this newspaper, Hou Bo, photo)
    People from all walks of life in the capital warmly celebrate "May 1st" International Labor Day. Chairman Mao frequently waves to the parading masses and guests from the Tiananmen rostrum. (Special Correspondent for this newspaper, Hou Bo, photo)
    Representatives of the Beijing Municipal Young Pioneers present flowers to Chairman Mao on behalf of the people of the capital. (Special Correspondent for this newspaper, Hou Bo, photo)
    Countless balloons rise from the ranks of the Young Pioneers marching towards the sky above Tiananmen.
                (Reporter for this newspaper, Li Yu, photo)
    A ceremonial guard of more than ten workers marches majestically at the front of the parade. (Reporter for this newspaper, Zhu Bo, photo)
    Representatives of the visiting Railway Art Troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea present flowers to Chairman Mao.
　(Xinhua News Agency reporter, Qi Guanshan, photo)
    Railway workers carry a model of a locomotive forward. (Xinhua News Agency reporter, Chen Zhengqing, photo)
    The parading masses march forward, holding high the banner of peace. (Reporter for this newspaper, Li Yu, photo)
    Representatives of the Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe present flowers to Chairman Mao.
            (Special Correspondent for this newspaper, Hou Bo, photo)
    Representatives of the Polish People's Republic Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble present flowers to Chairman Mao.
            (Special Correspondent for this newspaper, Hou Bo, photo)
    The mighty ranks of workers are reviewed by the beloved leader Chairman Mao on this great day. (Reporter for this newspaper, Zhu Bo, photo)

### 五一劳动节在首都（图片）

1953-05-03
第5版()
专栏：

    五一劳动节在首都
    首都五十万人组成的游行队伍通过天安门广场。　（本报特约记者　侯　波摄）
    首都各界人民热烈庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。毛主席在天安门主席台上向游行群众和来宾们频频招手。　（本报特约记者　侯　波摄）
    北京市少年儿童队代表首都人民向毛主席献花。     （本报特约记者　侯　波摄）
    无数气球从行进中的少年儿童队队伍中升向天安门上空。
                （本报记者　李　棫摄）
    由一十多工人组成的仪仗队，雄壮地走在游行队伍的最前面。　（本报记者　朱　波摄）
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团的代表向毛主席献花。
　（新华社记者　齐观山摄）
    铁路工人抬着机车的模型前进。　（新华社记者　陈正青摄）
    游行的群众高举着和平的旗帜前进。      （本报记者　李　棫摄）
    蒙古人民共和国艺术团的代表向毛主席献花。
            （本报特约记者　侯　波摄）
    波兰人民共和国玛佐夫舍歌舞团的代表向毛主席献花。
            （本报特约记者　侯　波摄）
    浩浩荡荡的工人队伍，在这伟大的日子里接受着敬爱的领袖毛主席的检阅。　（本报记者　朱　波摄）
