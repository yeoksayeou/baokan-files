Title: African Trade Union Delegation Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1953-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The African Trade Union Delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and the Seventh National Congress of the Chinese Trade Union, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 3rd. Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Jin Zhifu, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Coal Miners' Union, and Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, were among those who went to the airport to welcome them. The list of the African Trade Union Delegation is as follows:
	Head of the Delegation: Ali Gujji Muhammad, Secretary of the Algerian Agricultural and Forestry Workers' Union, Executive Member of the Algerian Trade Union Liaison Committee.
	Members: Maki-Mokhtar Abbas, Member of the Sidi Bel Abbes Regional Trade Union Council of Algeria; Mevoua Bek, Secretary of the Liaison Committee of Trade Unions of French Equatorial Africa and Cameroon; Diallo Seydou, General Secretary of the Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union of French West Africa.

### 非洲工会代表团昨天到达北京

1953-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    非洲工会代表团昨天到达北京
    【新华社三日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请，前来参加“五一”国际劳动节和中国工会第七次全国代表大会的非洲工会代表团，在三日下午乘飞机抵达北京。前往机场欢迎的有：中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，中国煤矿工会全国委员会主席金直夫和中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明等人。非洲工会代表团名单：团长：阿里·古杰·穆罕默德　阿尔及利亚农林工
    人工会书记，阿尔及利亚工会联络委员会执
    行委员。团员：麦基—莫赫塔·阿巴斯　阿尔及利亚西第·
    培尔·阿比斯地区工会理事会委员梅沃阿·贝克法属赤道非洲与喀麦隆工会联络委员会书记。迪亚洛·赛杜法属西非达加邮电工会总书记。
