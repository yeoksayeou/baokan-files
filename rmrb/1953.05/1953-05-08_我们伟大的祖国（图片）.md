    Our Great Motherland
    The newly built Hubei Province Paper Mill with modern equipment in our country has been completed. All the equipment of this paper mill is manufactured and installed by our own country. This is the situation of the workers in the steaming ball room carrying out machine maintenance.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国新建的近代化设备的湖北省造纸厂，已经建设完成。这个造纸厂的全部设备，都是由我国自己制造和安装的。这是蒸球间工人正在进行机器保养工作的情形。
