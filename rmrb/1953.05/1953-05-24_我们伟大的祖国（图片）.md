    Our Great Motherland
    The Chinese People's Volunteers maintain an eternally un-bombable steel transportation line from the rear to the front lines in Korea. It supports the war and guarantees victory. This anti-aircraft sentry of the Volunteers is using various prescribed signals to direct traffic, ensuring the safety of transport vehicles.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国人民志愿军保持着从朝鲜的后方到前线的一条永远炸不断的钢铁的运输线。它支援了战争，保证了胜利。这个志愿军的防空哨兵，正在运用各种规定的信号指挥交通，保证了运输车辆的安全。
