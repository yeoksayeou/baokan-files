Title: Representatives of the Military Authorities of the Soviet Union, the United States, the United Kingdom, and France Hold the Fourth Meeting on Air Safety Issues
Author:
Date: 1953-05-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 6th: According to TASS, on May 5, 1953, representatives of the military authorities of France, the Soviet Union, the United States, and the United Kingdom held the fourth meeting on air safety issues at the French military government in Berlin.
    The representatives of France, the United States, and the United Kingdom put forward some counter-proposals to the suggestions made by the Soviet representative on April 17 regarding the safety of the air corridors between Berlin and West Germany and the prevention of air incidents.
    The next meeting will be held at the location of the Soviet Control Commission for Germany in Berlin, with the date and time to be determined later.

### 苏、美、英、法四国军事当局代表  举行第四次空中安全问题会议

1953-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏、美、英、法四国军事当局代表
    举行第四次空中安全问题会议
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社柏林六日讯：一九五三年五月五日，法国、苏联、美国和英国军事当局代表在柏林法国军政府举行了第四次空中安全问题的会议。
    法、美、英三国代表就四月十七日苏联代表提出的关于柏林与西德之间的空中走廊中的安全问题和防止空中事件问题的建议提出了一些反建议。
    下次会议将在柏林苏联对德管制委员会所在地举行，日期与时间另定。
