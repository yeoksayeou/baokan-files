Title: Meeting of Foreign Ministers of Eight Arab Nations Issues Statement Rejecting Imperialist "Middle East Command" Plan
Author:
Date: 1953-05-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 12th. TASS, Cairo, 11th: The meeting of Foreign Ministers of the eight Arab League countries closed on the 10th. According to newspaper reports, the meeting reiterated its rejection of the so-called "Middle East Command" plan proposed by Western countries, and expressed "full support" for Egypt's demand for the unconditional withdrawal of British troops from the Suez Canal Zone.

### 八个阿拉伯国家外交部长会议发表声明  拒绝参加帝国主义的“中东司令部”计划

1953-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    八个阿拉伯国家外交部长会议发表声明
    拒绝参加帝国主义的“中东司令部”计划
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社开罗十一日讯：阿拉伯联盟的八个阿拉伯国家外交部长会议在十日闭幕。据报纸报道，会议再次声明拒绝西方国家提出的所谓“中东司令部”计划，并且表示“完全支持”埃及提出的要英国军队从苏彝士运河区无条件撤退的要求。
