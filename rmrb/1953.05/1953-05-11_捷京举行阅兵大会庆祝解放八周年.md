Title: Prague Holds Military Parade to Celebrate the Eighth Anniversary of Liberation
Author:
Date: 1953-05-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 10th: According to TASS, a military parade was held on Letná Square in Prague, the capital of the Republic, on the 9th to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia by the Soviet Army. Taking part in the parade were the Supreme Commander of the Czechoslovak Armed Forces, President of the Republic Antonín Zápotocký, and government officials headed by Prime Minister Viliam Široký. Also participating in the parade were Soviet Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Yevgeny Yegorovich Bogomolov, the Chinese government delegation headed by Li Fuchun, the head of the Mongolian government delegation Demchigiin Adilbish, and diplomatic representatives from friendly countries.
    After the ceremony began, the Minister of National Defence of Czechoslovakia, A. Čepička, delivered a speech. He congratulated the armed forces neatly lined up on the square on the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia. Then Zápotocký delivered a speech. He pointed out the world-historical significance of the Soviet army's victory over fascism eight years ago, pointing out that it was this victory that brought freedom and independence to Czechoslovakia. Zápotocký said: The alliance between Czechoslovakia and the Soviet Union is based on mutual trust, economic assistance, and the joint efforts of the two countries to strive to maximize the ever-increasing material and cultural needs of their people. We will always be with the Soviet Union! After describing the construction achievements of the Czechoslovak people over the past eight years, Zápotocký said: In order to fully develop the economy and culture, we need peace. The task of our people's army is to guarantee our peace. After Zápotocký finished his speech, a grand military parade was held in the square.
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 10th: TASS reports: Czechoslovak Prime Minister Viliam Široký and Minister of National Defence General A. Čepička held a reception in Prague on the 9th to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia.
    Attending the reception were: President of the Republic Antonín Zápotocký, Government members, Chairman of the National Assembly Oldřich John, Soviet Ambassador to the Czech Republic Ya. Bogomolov, the Chinese government delegation headed by Li Fuchun, and Chinese Ambassador to the Czech Republic Tan Xilin, Mongolian government representative D. Adilbish, diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Czech Republic and many other guests.

### 捷京举行阅兵大会庆祝解放八周年

1953-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    捷京举行阅兵大会庆祝解放八周年
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：为了庆祝苏联军队解放捷克斯洛伐克八周年，共和国首都布拉格的勒提尼广场上九日举行了阅兵大会。参加大会的有捷克斯洛伐克武装部队最高统帅、共和国总统安·萨波托斯基和以威·西罗基总理为首的政府人员。参加大会的还有：苏联驻捷克斯洛伐克大使亚·叶·波戈莫洛夫，以李富春为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团，蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长德·阿迪尔毕希，各友好国家的外交代表等。
    大会开始后，捷克斯洛伐克国防部部长阿·契皮茨卡首先致词。他向整齐地排列在广场上的武装部队祝贺捷克斯洛伐克解放八周年。接着萨波托斯基发表演说。他指出了八年前苏联军队战胜法西斯主义的世界历史意义，指出正是这个胜利给捷克斯洛伐克带来了自由和独立。萨波托斯基说：捷克斯洛伐克和苏联的联盟，是建立在互相信任、经济上互相帮助和两国共同努力争取最大限度地满足自己人民的不断增长的物质和文化要求这种基础之上的。我们要永远和苏联在一起！萨波托斯基在叙述了捷克斯洛伐克人民八年来的建设成就之后说：为了全面地发展经济和文化，我们需要和平。我们人民军队的任务就是保证我们的和平。萨波托斯基演说完毕后，在广场上举行了隆重的阅兵式。
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理威·西罗基和国防部部长阿·契皮茨卡大将为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放八周年，九日在布拉格举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有：共和国总统安·萨波托斯基、政府委员、国民议会主席奥·约翰，苏联驻捷大使亚·波戈莫洛夫，以李富春为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团和中国驻捷大使谭希林，蒙古人民共和国政府代表德·阿迪尔毕希，各国驻捷外交使节及来宾多人。
