Title: Shvernik Hosts Banquet for Chinese Trade Union Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-05-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 14th: According to TASS, Moscow, May 13th, the Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, N.M. Shvernik, hosted a banquet on the 12th to welcome the delegation from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, who were in the Soviet Union to participate in the May Day celebrations. Responsible individuals from the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions attended the reception.
    At the banquet, Shvernik extended warm congratulations to the Chinese representatives and wished the All-China Federation of Trade Unions great success in the construction of the new China. Cai Shufan, head of the delegation, delivered a reply, thanking the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions for giving the delegation the opportunity to study the life and work of the Soviet people. Cai Shufan said: "We have learned a lot from meeting with staff of Soviet enterprises and cultural institutions. We will never forget the enthusiasm expressed by the working people of Stalingrad and Rostov when they met us. We toured the Lenin Volga-Don Canal and saw how magnificent this project, which could only be completed in a socialist country, is."
    The reception was filled with a cordial and friendly atmosphere.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: The Chinese trade union delegation, invited by the Soviet trade unions to participate in the May Day celebrations in the Soviet Union, left Moscow by plane on the afternoon of the 13th to return to Beijing after visiting the Lenin Canal, Stalingrad, and Rostov.

### 什维尔尼克举行宴会招待我工会代表团

1953-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    什维尔尼克举行宴会招待我工会代表团
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十三日讯：全苏工会中央理事会主席尼·米·什维尔尼克曾在十二日设宴招待到苏联参加“五一”劳动节观礼的中华全国总工会代表团。全苏工会中央理事会的负责人都出席了招待会。
    会上，什维尔尼克向中国代表们表示了热烈的祝贺，并祝中华全国总工会在新中国的建设工作中获得巨大的胜利。代表团团长蔡树藩致答词，感谢全苏工会中央理事会给代表团以考察苏联人民生活和工作的机会。蔡树藩说：我们在与苏联企业部门和文化机关的工作人员会面时，学到了许多东西。我们将永久不会忘记斯大林格勒和罗斯托夫劳动人民和我们会见时所表示的热情。我们游历了列宁伏尔加河—顿河运河，看到了这个只有在社会主义国家才能完成的工程，它的规模是多么的宏大。
    招待会上充满着亲切友好的气氛。
    据新华社莫斯科讯：应苏联工会邀请前来苏联参加“五一”观礼的中国工会代表团，在参观了列宁运河、斯大林格勒和罗斯托夫后，十三日下午乘飞机离莫斯科返京。
