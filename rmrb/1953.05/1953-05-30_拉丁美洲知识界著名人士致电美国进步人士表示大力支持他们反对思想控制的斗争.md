Title: Prominent Figures in Latin American Intellectual Circles Send Telegram to American Progressives, Expressing Strong Support for Their Struggle Against Thought Control
Author:
Date: 1953-05-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New York, May 27th: TASS reported that a group of prominent figures in Latin American intellectual circles sent a telegram to American progressives, pledging their utmost support for their struggle against thought control. This action was taken to show solidarity with Jerome, the president of International Publishers, Alexander Trachtenberg, Stephen Nelson, Benjamin Davis, Henry Winston, and many others who were framed by the so-called "Un-American Activities Investigation Committee."

The statement said: "If we want the Americas to be a truly free continent, these persecutions that are poisoning the atmosphere of the Americas should be stopped; the ugly phenomenon of hostility towards black people should be eradicated; the sinful McCarthy group and its daily clamoring to provoke hatred must be stopped.

"Movies, radio and television must never be used as tools of war and killing. An end must be put to the situation in which despicable atmosphere of fear is created by various methods and people are forbidden to express their true feelings for peace and freedom. We, the intellectuals of the Americas from Mexico to Chile, are signing here for all of the above." Those who signed the statement include Chilean poet Pablo Neruda; Brazilian novelist Jorge Amado; Cuban poet Guillén; Cuban prose writer Juan Marinello; Costa Rican novelist Joaquín Gutiérrez; Colombian prose writer Luis Vidales; Peruvian poet Juan Ríos; Argentine painter Antonio Berni; Brazilian judge Irina Joffily; Haitian poet René Depestre; Mexican novelist José Mancisidor, and others.

### 拉丁美洲知识界著名人士致电美国进步人士表示大力支持他们反对思想控制的斗争

1953-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲知识界著名人士致电美国进步人士
    表示大力支持他们反对思想控制的斗争
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社纽约二十七日讯：拉丁美洲知识界的一批名流致电美国进步人士，保证以最大的力量支持他们反对思想控制的斗争。他们的这一行动，是为了声援国际出版社社长季洛姆、亚历山大·特拉腾堡、斯蒂芬·纳尔逊、本杰明·戴维斯、亨利·温斯顿和其他许多被所谓“非美活动调查委员会”陷害的人。
    声明说：“如果我们希望美洲是一个真正自由的大陆，这种正在毒害美洲气氛的迫害行为就应该停止；丑恶的敌视黑人的现象就应该根除；罪恶的麦卡锡集团及其每天叫嚣挑拨仇恨的伎俩，必须予以制止。
    “决不许把电影、无线电和电视当作战争和杀人的工具。必须结束这种以各种方法制造可鄙的恐惧气氛和禁止人民表示他们对于和平与自由的真正情绪的情形。我们从墨西哥到智利的美洲知识分子为了上面这一切而在这里签名。”在声明上签名的人有智利诗人巴勃罗·聂鲁达；巴西小说家若热·亚马多；古巴诗人纪廉；古巴散文家胡安·马里内略；哥斯达黎加小说家豪金·古特雷斯；哥伦比亚散文家路易斯·维达勒斯；秘鲁诗人胡安·里奥斯；阿根廷画家安东尼奥·贝尔尼；巴西法官伊里纳·若菲利；海地诗人雷纳·德佩斯特；墨西哥小说家何塞·曼西斯多及其他一些人。
