Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Departs to Attend World Women's Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-05-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 19th: The Chinese women's delegation, which will attend the World Women's Congress to be held in Denmark on June 5th, left Beijing by train on the 19th. The delegation consists of 24 representatives and 18 staff members, led by Cao Mengjun, the deputy secretary-general of the delegation. Cai Chang, the head of the delegation, Li Dequan, the deputy head of the delegation, and some staff members are already abroad. Zhang Yun, another deputy head of the delegation, and Lu Cui, the secretary-general, will depart a few days later due to other matters. Those who came to the station to see them off included: Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General; Chen Shaomin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Yang Zhihua, Deputy Director of the Women Workers Department; Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Shi Liang, Zhang Yun, Wei Que, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Protection of Children; and representatives from the Chinese Women's Federation, the Chinese Women's Temperance Association, the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, the Beijing YWCA, and other organizations.

### 出席世界妇女大会  中国妇女代表团启程

1953-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界妇女大会
    中国妇女代表团启程
    【新华社十九日讯】出席即将于六月五日在丹麦举行的世界妇女大会的中国妇女代表团已于十九日乘火车离北京。代表团有代表二十四人，工作人员十八人，由该团副秘书长曹孟君率领。代表团团长蔡畅、副团长李德全及一部分工作人员已在国外，该团另一副团长章蕴和秘书长陆璀因事须稍迟几天启程。到车站欢送的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一、副秘书长吴茂荪，中华全国总工会书记处书记陈少敏、女工部副部长杨之华，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，史良、章蕴，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会副主席韦悫和中国妇女联谊会、中华妇女节制协会、北京市民主妇女联合会、北京市基督教女青年会及其他机关团体的代表。
