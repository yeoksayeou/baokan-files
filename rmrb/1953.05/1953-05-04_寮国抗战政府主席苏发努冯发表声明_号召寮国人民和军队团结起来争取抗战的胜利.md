Title: Laotian Resistance Government Chairman Souphanouvong Issues Statement, Calling on the Laotian People and Army to Unite and Strive for Victory in the War of Resistance
Author:
Date: 1953-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vientiane: Laotian Resistance Government Chairman Souphanouvong issued a statement on April 19th, saying: The liberation of Sam Neua is a great victory in the Laotian people's war of resistance and is the result of the Laotian people's long-term resistance against colonialist aggressors. Chairman Souphanouvong said: On behalf of the Laotian people and the Laotian Resistance Government, I declare that the Laotian Resistance Government elected by the Laotian National Assembly is the only legitimate government of Laos. This government represents the legitimate rights of the Laotian people. It is leading the Laotian people in their struggle for Laos's true freedom and independence. The statement called on the people and army to strive to expand the victory of Sam Neua, unite around the Resistance Government and the Lao Free National United Front, and strive for victory in the war of resistance. Chairman Souphanouvong also issued eight orders, stipulating the protection of the lives and property of the people and foreigners, respect for temples, churches, and people's customs, and lenient treatment of prisoners of war.

At the same time, Vice Chairman of the Lao Free National United Front, Singkapo, representing the Central Executive Committee of the Lao Free National United Front, issued a letter to the people of Laos, calling on the Laotian people to unite closely around the Resistance Government, unite in the Free National United Front, fully support the Lao Liberation Army, and work hard to increase production, in order to lead the war of resistance to final victory.

### 寮国抗战政府主席苏发努冯发表声明  号召寮国人民和军队团结起来争取抗战的胜利

1953-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    寮国抗战政府主席苏发努冯发表声明
    号召寮国人民和军队团结起来争取抗战的胜利
   【新华社三十日讯】据越南通讯社寮国讯：寮国抗战政府主席苏发努冯四月十九日发表声明说：桑怒的解放是寮国人民抗战中的一个伟大胜利，是寮国人民长时期以来反抗殖民主义侵略者的结果。苏发努冯主席说：我代表寮国人民和寮国抗战政府宣布：由寮国国民议会选举出来的寮国抗战政府是寮国唯一的合法政府。这个政府是代表寮国全国人民的正当权利的。它正在领导寮国人民为争取寮国的真正自由和独立而斗争。声明号召全国人民和军队努力扩大桑怒的胜利，团结在抗战政府的周围和寮国自由民族统一战线中，以争取抗战的胜利。苏发努冯主席并发布八项命令，规定保护人民和外侨的生命财产，尊重庙宇、教堂和人民风俗习惯，宽待俘虏等。
    同时，寮国自由民族统一战线副主席辛卡波代表寮国自由民族统一战线中央执行委员会发表告寮国全国人民书，号召寮国人民紧密团结在抗战政府周围，团结在自由民族统一战线中，全力支持寮国解放军，努力增加生产，以便把抗战引到最后胜利。
