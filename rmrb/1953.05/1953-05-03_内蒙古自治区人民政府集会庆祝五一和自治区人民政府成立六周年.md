Title: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government Holds Meeting to Celebrate May Day and the Sixth Anniversary of the Founding of the Autonomous Region

Author: 
Date: 1953-05-03
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency

    The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government Holds Meeting to Celebrate May Day and the Sixth Anniversary of the Founding of the Autonomous Region
    The North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and the North China Administrative Committee Send Congratulatory Telegrams on the Sixth Anniversary of the Founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government
    The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government solemnly held a meeting on April 30 to celebrate the "May Day" Labor Day and the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. More than 1,100 people attended the meeting, including cadres from various agencies of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, leading officials of the Suiyuan Province and Guisui City Party, government, and military, and representatives of the North China Region minority ethnic group visiting delegation visiting Inner Mongolia and Suiyuan Province.
    At the meeting, Ulanhu, Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government, spoke, explaining that due to the implementation of Chairman Mao's great ethnic policy, Inner Mongolia has achieved remarkable results in politics, economy, culture, and other aspects in the past six years. He said: Over the past six years, people of all ethnic groups in Inner Mongolia have completely shaken off their past poverty and are living happy and prosperous lives. This year, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region will also conduct a general election, so that people of all ethnic groups will be more closely united, and under the leadership of the Communist Party and Chairman Mao, Inner Mongolia will be built more prosperously and beautifully.
    The North China Region minority ethnic group visiting delegation was warmly welcomed by all attendees at the meeting. Ping Jiesan, the head of the delegation, gave a congratulatory speech, saying that Inner Mongolia's brilliant achievements in various tasks are the result of earnestly implementing Chairman Mao's great ethnic policy. He hoped that people of all ethnic groups in Inner Mongolia would be more united in the future to jointly build a beautiful Inner Mongolia, so that Inner Mongolia will always maintain the glorious title of a good example of ethnic regional autonomy.
    After the meeting, the Autonomous Region People's Government hosted a banquet to entertain representatives of all ethnic groups and held an evening party to celebrate. (Xinhua News Agency)
    Congratulatory telegram from the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee to Chairman Ulanhu of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government:
    On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government, we would like to extend our heartfelt congratulations to the people of all ethnic groups in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.
    In the past six years, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has achieved great achievements in social reform and political, economic, and cultural construction, which fully proves the greatness and correctness of Chairman Mao's ethnic policy. We firmly believe that you will definitely build on the existing achievements, further implement Chairman Mao's policy of ethnic regional autonomy, and unite the people of all ethnic groups under the unified leadership of the Party and the unified plan of the country to achieve new and greater achievements.
                The North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    Congratulatory telegram from Liu Lantao, Chairman of the North China Administrative Committee, to Chairman Ulanhu of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government:
    On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government, I would like to extend my warm congratulations! I wish you greater achievements in further strengthening the various construction tasks of the autonomous region and in striving to "always become a good example of ethnic minority regional autonomy"!
              Liu Lantao, Chairman of the North China Administrative Committee

### 内蒙古自治区人民政府集会庆祝五一和自治区人民政府成立六周年

1953-05-03
第3版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区人民政府集会庆祝五一和自治区人民政府成立六周年
    中共中央华北局和华北行政委员会致电祝贺内蒙古自治区人民政府成立六周年纪念
    内蒙古自治区人民政府四月三十日隆重举行庆祝“五一”劳动节和内蒙古自治区人民政府成立六周年大会。到会的有内蒙古自治区各机关干部、绥远省和归绥市党、政、军首长和访问内蒙古和绥远省的华北区少数民族访问团代表等，共一千一百多人。
    会上，内蒙古自治区人民政府主席乌兰夫讲话，说明了内蒙古六年来由于贯彻执行了毛主席伟大的民族政策，在政治、经济、文化等各方面都取得显著成绩。他说：六年来内蒙古境内各族人民已完全摆脱了过去的贫苦生活，过着幸福美好的日子。今年内蒙古自治区也将进行普选，这样，各族人民将更加紧密团结，在共产党和毛主席领导下，内蒙古将会建设得更繁荣和美丽。
    华北区少数民族访问团在会上受到全体到会者的热烈欢迎。该团团长平杰三致祝词说：内蒙古在各项工作中获得如此光辉的成绩，是认真贯彻执行毛主席伟大民族政策的结果。他希望内蒙古各族人民今后更加团结一致，共同建设美丽的内蒙古，使内蒙古永远保持民族区域自治良好榜样的光荣称号。
    会后自治区人民政府除设宴招待各民族代表外，并举行晚会庆祝。　（新华社）
    中共中央华北局的贺电内蒙古自治区人民政府乌兰夫主席：
    值此内蒙古自治区人民政府成立六周年纪念日之际，特向内蒙古自治区各族人民致以衷心的祝贺。
    六年来，内蒙古自治区在社会改革及政治、经济、文化建设等方面均获得巨大成就，完全证明了毛主席的民族政策的伟大、正确。我们深信你们一定会在已有成绩的基础上，进一步贯彻毛主席的民族区域自治政策，团结各族人民在党的统一领导和国家的统一计划下，获得新的更大的成就。
                中国共产党中央华北局
    华北行政委员会主席刘澜涛的贺电内蒙古自治区人民政府乌兰夫主席：
    值此内蒙古自治区人民政府成立六周年之际，谨致热烈的祝贺！祝你们在进一步加强自治区的各项建设工作、为实现“永远成为少数民族区域自治的良好榜样”的努力中，获得更大的成就！
              华北行政委员会主席  刘澜涛
