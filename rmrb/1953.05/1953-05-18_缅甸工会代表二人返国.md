Title: Two Burmese Trade Union Representatives Return Home
Author: 
Date: 1953-05-18
Page: 4
　
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 15th: Deqin La Zui, head of the Burmese trade union delegation invited to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day and the Seventh National Congress of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Deqin Qing Su, Secretary-General of the All-Burma Trade Union Congress who passed through China after attending the Vienna International Social Security Conference, arrived in Guangzhou on the evening of the 12th, and left Guangzhou by train on the morning of the 14th via Hong Kong to return home. Wu Jiemin, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and Zhang Yiming, Deputy Director of the Organization Department, went to the station to see them off.

### 缅甸工会代表二人返国

1953-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸工会代表二人返国
    【新华社广州十五日电】应邀前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节和中国工会第七次全国代表大会的缅甸工会代表团团长德钦拉最和出席维也纳国际社会保险会议后途经我国的全缅工会大会总书记德钦庆素二人，在十二日晚到达广州，十四日晨乘火车离广州取道香港返国。前往车站欢送的有广州市总工会副主席吴介民、组织部副部长张逸鸣等。
