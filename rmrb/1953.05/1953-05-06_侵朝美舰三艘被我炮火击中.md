Title: Three invading US warships hit by our artillery fire
Author:
Date: 1953-05-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 5th: The United States Navy has admitted that the American destroyers "USS Orleck" and "USS McGowen" were hit by artillery fire from the Korean and Chinese People's Army in the waters off Wonsan on the 2nd. The U.S. Navy had previously admitted that the American destroyer "USS Cassin Young" was also hit outside Wonsan Harbor on April 19th.

### 侵朝美舰三艘被我炮火击中

1953-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    侵朝美舰三艘被我炮火击中
    【新华社五日讯】东京消息：美国海军方面承认，美国驱逐舰“奥文号”和“麦道格斯号”二日在元山海面被朝中人民军队炮火击中。美国海军方面以前还承认，美国驱逐舰“开斯号”四月十九日也在元山港外被击中。
