Title: Czechoslovak Film Art Workers Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1953-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. After a month's visit to our country, the Czechoslovak Republic Film Art Workers Delegation left Beijing on the morning of the 12th to return home. Those who went to the station to see them off included: Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Government Administration Council's Culture and Education Committee, Zhao Feng, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture, Wang Lanxi, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, Luo Guangda, General Manager of the China Film Management Company, Wu Qing, Specialist of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and film artists Cai Chusheng, Shi Dongshan, Tian Fang, Shi Lianxing, and others, totaling more than 100 people.
Also at the station to see them off were Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Kousal, and his wife, the embassy's cultural attache, Mr. Hedlicka, and other embassy staff. On the evening of the 10th, the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government hosted a farewell banquet for the delegation.

### 捷电影艺术工作者代表团返国

1953-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    捷电影艺术工作者代表团返国
    【新华社十二日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国电影艺术工作者代表团在我国访问了一个月后，十二日晨离京首途返国。到车站欢送的有：中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、文化部办公厅主任赵沨、文化部电影局副局长王阑西、中国影片经理公司总经理罗光达、外交部苏联东欧司专员吴青及电影艺术界人士蔡楚生、史东山、田方、石联星等共一百多人。
    到车站欢送的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使康萨拉及其夫人、大使馆文化专员赫德利奇卡及使馆其他人员。十日晚，中央人民政府文化部曾设宴为代表团饯行。
