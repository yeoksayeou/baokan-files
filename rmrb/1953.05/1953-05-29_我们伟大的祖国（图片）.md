    Our Great Motherland
    All the workers of the Tianjin Wire Manufacturing Factory are supporting the country's economic construction by increasing production and improving technology. This is a scene of intense production in the factory's weaving workshop. The production rate of wires and cables here has increased by 123% compared to last year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    天津电线制造厂的全体职工正以提高产量和改良技术支援着国家的经济建设。这是该厂编织车间紧张生产的情景。这里电线和电缆的生产率比去年提高了百分之一百二十三。
