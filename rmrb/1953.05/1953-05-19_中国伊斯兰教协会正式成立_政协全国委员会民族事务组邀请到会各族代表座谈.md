Title: Formal Establishment of the China Islamic Association; The National Committee of the CPPCC's Ethnic Affairs Group Invites Representatives of All Ethnic Groups to a Forum

Author:
Date: 1953-05-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The inaugural meeting of the China Islamic Association was held in Beijing from May 9th to 11th, formally establishing the China Islamic Association. A total of 111 representatives attended the meeting, all peace-loving and democratic individuals from Islamic circles across the country. Among them were many prominent Ahongs and Mullahs, representing ten ethnic groups including Hui, Uyghur, Kazakh, Uzbek, Tatar, Kyrgyz, Tajik, Dongxiang, Salar, and Baoan.

The meeting opened with a speech by Grand Ahong Da Pousheng. Burhan, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Islamic Association, reported to the assembly on the work of the Preparatory Committee of the China Islamic Association and proposed suggestions regarding the tasks of the China Islamic Association. After full discussion, the assembly unanimously approved Director Burhan's report, adopted the charter of the China Islamic Association, and determined the future tasks of the China Islamic Association. The meeting elected Burhan (Uyghur) as Chairman of the China Islamic Association Committee, Yang Jingren (Hui), Ma Yuhuai (Hui), Da Pousheng (Hui), Ma Zhenwu (Hui), and Imin Mahsum (Uyghur) as Vice-Chairmen, and Zhang Yuzhen, Ma Jian, Pang Shiqian and eighty-three others as committee members.

The assembly invited Liu Geping, Vice-Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, to give a report on ethnic and religious policies, and Vice-Chairman Wang Feng to give a report on current affairs. Chen Qiyuan, Vice-Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, also spoke. After full discussion of these reports and speeches, the representatives from various regions who attended the meeting enhanced their understanding of the ethnic and religious policies of the Central People's Government and the great achievements of the motherland over the past three years. They spoke at the assembly, focusing on the development of various ethnic groups in China who believe in Islam in terms of politics, economy, and culture, and the respect and care given to their religious beliefs and customs under the brilliant light of Chairman Mao's ethnic policy. The representatives unanimously stated that after returning, they would, in the spirit of this meeting, assist the People's Government in continuing to implement Chairman Mao's policy of ethnic equality and freedom of religious belief, and unite with the people of all ethnic groups to strive for the defense and construction of the great motherland and the defense of world peace.

The meeting concluded successfully after passing a letter of respect to Chairman Mao and a telegram of respect to the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers.

In addition: The Ethnic Affairs Group of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) invited representatives attending the inaugural meeting of the China Islamic Association to a forum on May 12th.

At the meeting, Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, congratulated the representatives on the establishment of the China Islamic Association on behalf of the National Committee. He hoped that after returning to their respective regions, the representatives would widely publicize the great construction achievements and bright future of the motherland to the local people of all ethnic groups, assist the People's Government in continuing to implement ethnic and religious policies, strengthen unity between and within ethnic groups, and jointly create a prosperous and happy life for the people of all ethnic groups.

Burhan, Chairman of the China Islamic Association, and Vice-Chairmen Da Pousheng and Imin Mahsum, spoke successively at the meeting, explaining the correctness and greatness of Chairman Mao's ethnic and religious policies, enabling all ethnic minorities across the country to fully enjoy the rights of ethnic equality and freedom of religious belief. Burhan said: We sincerely thank the central leaders for their concern and love for our ethnic minorities. In the future, we people of all ethnic groups who believe in Islam will unite more closely with other brotherly ethnic groups to strive to complete our country's economic construction tasks!

### 中国伊斯兰教协会正式成立  政协全国委员会民族事务组邀请到会各族代表座谈

1953-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中国伊斯兰教协会正式成立
    政协全国委员会民族事务组邀请到会各族代表座谈
    【新华社十六日讯】中国伊斯兰教协会成立会议于五月九日到十一日在北京举行，正式成立了中国伊斯兰教协会。出席这次会议的代表共一百一十一人，他们是来自全国各地的伊斯兰教中的和平民主人士，其中有许多是著名的阿訇、毛拉，包括回族、维吾尔族、哈萨克族、乌兹别克族、塔塔尔族、柯尔克兹族、塔吉克族、东乡族、撒拉族、保安族等十个民族的成分。
    会议由达浦生大阿訇致开幕词。中国伊斯兰教协会筹备委员会鲍尔汉主任向大会报告了中国伊斯兰教协会筹备委员会的工作，并提出关于中国伊斯兰教协会任务的建议。大会经过充分讨论后，一致同意了鲍尔汉主任的报告，并通过中国伊斯兰教协会简章、确定了中国伊斯兰教协会今后的任务。会议选出鲍尔汉（维吾尔族）为中国伊斯兰教协会委员会主任，杨静仁（回族）、马玉槐（回族）、达浦生（回族）、马震武（回族）、依明·马合苏木
    （维吾尔族）为副主任，张玉珍、马坚、庞士谦等八十三人为委员。
    大会邀请中央人民政府民族事务委员会刘格平副主任委员作了关于民族政策和宗教政策的报告，汪锋副主任委员作了关于时事的报告，并请中央人民政府内务部陈其瑗副部长讲话。各地出席会议的代表对这些报告和讲话进行充分讨论之后，提高了对中央人民政府民族政策、宗教政策和祖国三年来伟大成就的认识。他们在大会上纷纷发言，着重地介绍了中国信仰伊斯兰教的各民族，在毛主席的民族政策的光辉照耀下，政治、经济、文化各方面的发展以及宗教信仰、风俗习惯受到尊重和照顾的情况。与会代表一致表示：回去后要本着这次会议的精神，协助人民政府继续贯彻毛主席的民族平等政策和宗教信仰自由政策，和各民族人民团结一致，为保卫和建设伟大祖国、保卫世界和平而奋斗。
    会议在通过向毛主席致敬信和向中国人民解放军、中国人民志愿军致敬电后，圆满闭幕。
    又讯：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会民族事务组，在五月十二日邀请出席中国伊斯兰教协会成立会议的代表举行座谈会。
    会上，政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通代表全国委员会向各位代表祝贺中国伊斯兰教协会的成立。他希望各位代表回到各地后，向当地各族人民广泛宣传祖国伟大的建设成就和未来的光明前途，协助人民政府继续贯彻民族政策和宗教政策，加强民族之间和民族内部的团结，共同缔造各族人民繁荣幸福的生活。
    中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉，副主任达浦生、依明·马合苏木在会上相继发言，说明了毛主席的民族政策和宗教政策的正确与伟大，使全国各少数民族充分享受到民族平等和宗教信仰自由的权利。鲍尔汉说：我们衷心感激中央各位首长对我们各少数民族的关怀和热爱，今后，我们信仰伊斯兰教的各族人民，要更加亲密地团结其他各兄弟民族，为完成我们国家的经济建设任务而奋斗！
