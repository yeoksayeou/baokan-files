Title: Vice Minister Mao Qihua of the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government Hosts a Banquet to Welcome the Burmese Government Labor Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-05-04
Page: 4

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 30th] Mao Qihua, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government, hosted a banquet at noon on April 30th to welcome the Burmese Government Labor Delegation headed by Bo Maung. The Burmese Ambassador to China, Wu La Mao, was also invited to attend.

　　Those attending the banquet included: Li Wenyi, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Labor; Chen Huazheng, Director of the Labor Force Allocation Department; Di Zuozhi, Deputy Director of the Labor Protection Department; Dong Chuping, Director of the Labor Policy Research Office, and others.

　　Also invited to attend and accompany were He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Wang Zhijie, Secretary-General of the National Committee of the China Railway Trade Union; Zhang Beihua, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Machine Manufacturing Trade Union, and many others.

　　Vice Minister Mao Qihua delivered a speech at the banquet, extending a warm welcome to the Burmese Government Labor Delegation. Delegation leader Bo Maung then delivered a speech in reply. During the banquet, the guests frequently toasted to the continuous enhancement of friendship between the Chinese and Burmese peoples and to the common cause of defending peace in Asia and the world.

　　The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 我中央人民政府劳动部毛齐华副部长  设宴欢迎缅甸政府劳动考察团

1953-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我中央人民政府劳动部毛齐华副部长
    设宴欢迎缅甸政府劳动考察团
   【新华社三十日讯】中央人民政府劳动部毛齐华副部长于四月三十日中午设宴欢迎以波木昂为首的缅甸政府劳动考察团。缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂也应邀参加。
    参加宴会的有：劳动部办公厅副主任李文宜，劳动力调配司司长陈化争，劳动保护司副司长邸作之，劳动政策研究室主任董耡平等人。
    应邀出席作陪的有中央人民政府外交部亚洲司副司长何英，中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明，中国铁路工会全国委员会秘书长王志杰，中国机器制造业工会筹备委员会主任张北华等多人。
    毛齐华副部长在宴会上致词，对缅甸政府劳动考察团的到来表示热忱的欢迎。考察团团长波木昂随后致词答谢。席间宾主频频为中缅两国人民友谊的不断增进和为保卫亚洲与世界和平的共同事业而干杯。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。
