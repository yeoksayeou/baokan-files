Title: 1953 National Basketball, Volleyball Competition and Tennis, Badminton Exhibition Tournament Opens in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1953-05-04
Page: 3

The 1953 National Basketball, Volleyball Tournament and Tennis, Badminton Exhibition Tournament opened on the afternoon of May 2nd at the Tianjin Second People's Stadium.

Present at the opening ceremony were Wu De, Chairman of the Tournament Preparatory Committee, and Vice Chairmen Ma John, Huang Zhong, and committee member Dong Shouyi. Also attending the meeting were Huang Huoqing, Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Deputy Mayor Zhou Shutao.

The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Sports Visiting Group and the Volunteer Army Railway Corps Sports Visiting Group from the Korean front also attended the opening ceremony. The stands around the stadium were filled with more than seven thousand spectators.

Soviet Deputy Consul in Tianjin, Basman, and Polish Consul in Tianjin, Abramovich, were also invited to attend the opening ceremony.

The weather that day was clear and mild. The stadium, meticulously renovated by construction workers, looked magnificent and beautiful. Bright red flags fluttered around the venue. In the center of the main rostrum of the conference hung a huge portrait of Chairman Mao and Chairman Mao's inscription "Develop sports and improve the people's physique" and Commander-in-Chief Zhu's inscription "Popularize people's sports, serve production and national defense".

After the announcement of the opening of the conference, the athletes held the entrance ceremony to the majestic song of the "March of the Chinese People's Liberation Army" played by the band. Leading the way was the Chinese People's Liberation Army team. As the athletes strode into the venue with vigorous and heroic steps, the audience applauded warmly. After the Chinese Railway Team entered the venue, followed by the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region team from the Inner Mongolia grasslands, the Southwest Region team crossing the Yangtze River and over the mountains, and the Northwest team composed of athletes of Han, Uygur, Kazakh, Hui, Uzbek, Xibe, and Tatar nationalities, followed by the teams from Central South, East China, Northeast and North China regions. The athletes' strong physique and brightly colored and tidy sportswear left a deep impression on the audience.

After the athletes entered the venue, they formed a colorful and orderly team. In the solemn sound of the national anthem, the national flag of the People's Republic of China was raised over the venue. Comrade Wu De, Chairman of the Committee, delivered an opening speech at the meeting. He congratulated the opening of the conference. He said: This conference is the second national basketball and volleyball sports conference since liberation. At this conference, we will review the achievements of my country's ball sports in recent years, exchange advanced experiences from various places, and move basketball, volleyball and other ball sports forward, and develop it into a broader mass movement. He pointed out that my country's basketball movement has a broad mass base, and improving sports technology is an important task in the future. Six-person volleyball movement still needs vigorous publicity and continued popularization and in-depth development, and improvement on the basis of popularization. Finally, he called on the athletes participating in the conference to carry forward the spirit of unity, friendship, and learning with an open mind, to carry out revolutionary competitions, and to make the conference a complete success.

After the meeting, the competition began in two places. These wonderful games were warmly welcomed by the entire audience.
(Xinhua News Agency)

### 一九五三年全国篮、排球比赛和网球、羽毛球表演赛大会在天津开幕

1953-05-04
第3版()
专栏：

    一九五三年全国篮、排球比赛和网球、羽毛球表演赛大会在天津开幕
    一九五三年全国篮球、排球比赛大会和网球、羽毛球表演赛二日下午在天津市第二人民体育场举行开幕典礼。
    出席开幕典礼的有大会筹备委员会主任委员吴德、副主任委员马约翰、黄中，委员董守义等。出席大会的还有中共天津市委书记黄火青、副市长周叔弢等。
    出席开幕式的还有来自朝鲜前线的中国人民志愿军体育参观团和志愿军铁道兵团体育参观团。大会四周的看台上坐满了七千多观众。
    苏联驻津副领事巴思曼、波兰驻津领事阿布拉莫维奇等也应邀出席了开幕典礼。
    那日天气清和。体育场经过建筑工人们的精心修建，显得壮观美丽。会场四周飘扬着鲜艳的红旗。大会的主席台正中悬挂着毛主席的巨像和毛主席的“发展体育运动，增强人民体质”和朱总司令的“普及人民体育运动，为生产和国防服务”的巨幅题字。
    大会宣布开始后，运动员在乐队奏着“中国人民解放军进行曲”的雄壮歌声中，举行了入场式。走在最前列的是中国人民解放军代表队。当着运动员迈着雄赳、英武的步伐进入会场时，全场观众都报以热烈的掌声。中国铁路代表队进入会场后，接着是来自内蒙古草原的内蒙古自治区代表队，跨过长江、越过峻岭的西南区代表队，和有汉族、维吾尔族、哈萨克族、回族、乌兹别克族、锡伯族和塔塔尔族的运动员共同组成的西北代表队，接着是中南、华东、东北和华北区的代表队。运动员们的健强体格和穿着鲜艳整齐的运动衣服，给观众留下深刻的印象。
    运动员进入会场后，排成了五彩缤纷的整齐队伍。在庄严的国歌声中，会场上升起了中华人民共和国国旗。吴德主任委员在会上致开幕词。他祝贺大会的开幕。他说：这次大会是解放后第二次全国篮、排球运动大会，在这次大会上将检阅几年来我国球类运动的成就，交流各地先进经验，并把篮、排等球类运动向前推动一步，开展成更广泛的群众性的运动。他指出：我国的篮球运动已有广泛的群众基础，今后提高运动技术是一个重要任务。六人排球运动还需大力宣传及继续普及和深入，并在普及的基础上加以提高。最后他号召：参加大会的运动员发扬团结友爱，虚心学习的精神，展开革命竞赛，使大会获得圆满的成功。
    会后就分两处开始了比赛，这些精彩的比赛，受到全体观众热烈的欢迎。
                          （新华社）
