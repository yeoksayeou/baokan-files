Title: Former French Prime Minister Daladier Gives Speech Advocating a Four-Power Conference
Author:
Date: 1953-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 26th (TASS dispatch): "Le Monde" published a speech by former Prime Minister Édouard Daladier to representatives of the French Veterans Union Congress at the Avignon Municipal Council. Daladier said: "One of the issues most concerning to you veterans is the issue of arming Germany. Arming Germany is a danger to France and would eliminate any hope of establishing a truly democratic state in Germany... We must realistically assess the actual situation and strive for a Four-Power conference with the firm intention of holding negotiations."

### 法国前总理达拉第发表谈话  主张举行四大国会议

1953-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    法国前总理达拉第发表谈话
    主张举行四大国会议
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社巴黎二十六日讯：“世界报”刊载了前总理爱德华·达拉第在亚威农市政委员会对法国退伍军人联盟代表大会的代表的谈话。达拉第说：“你们退伍军人最关心的一个问题，是武装德国的问题。武装德国对于法国是个危险，并且会使得在德国建立真正民主的希望消灭净尽……。我们必须实事求是地估计事情的实际情况，并且本着要举行谈判的坚定意图来努力争取举行四大国会议。”
