Title: Czechoslovak Government Invites Korean Orphans and Students to Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1953-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 27th - According to a report by the Korean Central News Agency on the 26th, Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, recently received a letter from Viliam Široký, Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic, dated May 8th. In his letter, Široký expressed that the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic is filled with deep sympathy and love for the heroic Korean people, and has decided to invite 700 Korean orphans aged twelve to sixteen, 300 specialized students, and 200 university students to live and study in Czechoslovakia from 1953-1954.

### 捷克斯洛伐克政府  邀请朝鲜孤儿和学生赴捷

1953-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克政府
    邀请朝鲜孤儿和学生赴捷
    【新华社平壤二十七日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社二十六日报道，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成，最近接到捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基五月八日的来信。西罗基在来信中表示，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府对英雄的朝鲜人民充满着深切的同情和热爱，决定从一九五三年—一九五四年起，邀请七百名从十二岁到十六岁的朝鲜孤儿，三百名专科学生和二百名大学生，到捷克斯洛伐克居住和留学。
