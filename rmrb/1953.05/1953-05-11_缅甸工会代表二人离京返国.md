Title: Two Burmese Trade Union Representatives Leave Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1953-05-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Te Kin La Tsui, head of the Burmese trade union delegation invited to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day and the Seventh National Congress of the Chinese Trade Union, and Te Kin Qing Su, General Secretary of the All Burma Trade Union Congress, who was passing through China after attending the Vienna International Social Security Conference, left Beijing by train this morning to return to their country. Li Jiebo, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, Sundon, Chen Yu, Zhang Xihua and others from the World Federation of Trade Unions Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau went to the station to see them off.

### 缅甸工会代表二人离京返国

1953-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸工会代表二人离京返国
    【新华社十日讯】应邀前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节和中国工会第七次全国代表大会的缅甸工会代表团团长德钦拉最及出席维也纳国际社会保险会议后途经我国的全缅工会大会总书记德钦庆素二人，在十日上午乘火车离京返国。前往车站欢送的有中国铁路工会全国委员会主席李颉伯，世界工联亚澳联络局的桑顿、陈宇、张西华等人。
