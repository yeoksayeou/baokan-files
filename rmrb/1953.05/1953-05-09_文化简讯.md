    The CCP Xinjiang Sub-bureau Cadre School Adds Theory Study Class
    The Cadre School of the Xinjiang Sub-bureau of the Chinese Communist Party has added a theory study class. The class has a total of 89 students, including members of the Uyghur, Kazakh, Han, Uzbek, Mongolian, and Russian ethnic groups. These students come from all parts of Xinjiang and various units, including battalion and company-level commanders and political workers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army from various ethnic groups, government workers from various ethnic groups, and outstanding educated youth from various ethnic groups.
    The students in this class have completed their study of political common sense. They will now continue to study "World Modern History," "Political Economy," "A Short History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks)," "Thirty Years of the Communist Party of China," and the theories and policies of Lenin, Stalin, and Mao Zedong on the national question, so that after two years, all students will have a general basic knowledge of Marxism-Leninism.
    Xinjiang's People's Film Industry is Developing Rapidly
    Xinjiang Province's people's film industry is developing rapidly. At present, 40 cinemas have been established in various parts of Xinjiang, and 16 film projection teams have been formed. Projection teams have begun to show films in remote areas. At present, whether it is farmers of all ethnic groups at the foot of the Kunlun Mountains and on the banks of the Ili River, or herders of all ethnic groups in the Pamir Plateau and the Altai Mountains, they all have the opportunity to receive film education. Whenever films are shown in various places, men and women, old and young, of all ethnic groups ride horses from dozens or even hundreds of miles away to watch. According to statistics, more than 5,729,800 people of all ethnic groups in the province watched movies last year.

### 文化简讯

第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    文化简讯
    中共新疆分局干校增设理论学习班
    中共中央新疆分局干部学校增设理论学习班。该学习班，共有学员八十九人。包括维吾尔、哈萨克、汉、乌兹别克、蒙古、俄罗斯等民族成份。这些学员来自新疆各地和各个单位，有中国人民解放军的各族营、连级指挥员和政治工作人员，有政府部门的各族工作人员，还有各族的优秀知识青年。
    该班学员们已学完了政治常识。现将继续学习：“世界近代史”、“政治经济学”、“联共（布）党史简明教程”、“中国共产党的三十年”和列宁、斯大林、毛泽东关于民族问题的理论、政策，使学员们在两年之后，都能具有马克思列宁主义的一般基础知识。
    新疆人民电影事业发展很快
    新疆省人民电影事业发展得很快。现在新疆各地已设立了四十座电影院和成立了十六个放映队。放映队已开始在边远地区放映电影。目前无论是在昆仑山下和伊犁河畔的各族农民，或是帕米尔高原和阿尔泰山区的各族牧民，都有机会受到电影教育。各地每当放映电影时，各族男女老少都从几十里甚至百里以外骑马赶来观看。去年全省看过电影的各族人民，据统计有五百七十二万九千八百多人次。
