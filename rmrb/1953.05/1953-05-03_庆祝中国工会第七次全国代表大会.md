Title: Celebrating the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1953-05-03
Page: 2

The message of congratulation from the China Peace Committee: To the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions:

    On the occasion of the opening of the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, we extend our warm congratulations to the congress and our lofty respect to all the delegates present.

    The Chinese working class has played a significant role in the great movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, protect our homes and defend our country, and has made brilliant contributions to the cause of economic recovery and construction in our country, which is also a great contribution to defending world peace.

    We believe that the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions will certainly mobilize the Chinese working class and all working people to exert greater labor enthusiasm and greater creativity, so as to better complete the national construction plan, improve people's lives, and enhance the strength to defend world peace.
            China Peace Committee
                May 2, 1953

The message of congratulation from the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League: To the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, dear comrades:

    With extremely excited and joyful feelings, we warmly congratulate the opening of the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions.

    Since the birth of New China, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and our great leader Chairman Mao, the Chinese working class has made tremendous efforts to complete social reforms, support the resistance against U.S. aggression and aid Korea, and restore and develop production. With high patriotic enthusiasm and great contributions to the motherland, the Chinese working class has proved itself to be the great vanguard of China's construction cause. At this moment when the country is entering the first year of a new era of great economic construction, we are deeply convinced that the convening of the 7th National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions will further mobilize the working class and the people of the whole country to work together to complete and overfulfill the national construction plan, and to strive for the industrialization of the country and the socialist communist future.

    The members of the China New Democratic Youth League and the youth of the whole country, especially the Youth League members and youth in factories, mines, and enterprises, will fully support and implement the resolutions of the Congress. Let our great motherland become more prosperous and beautiful through our joint efforts.

    We wish the Congress a complete success.
          Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
                May 1, 1953

### 庆祝中国工会第七次全国代表大会

1953-05-03
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国工会第七次全国代表大会
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的贺电中国工会第七次全国代表大会：
    值此中国工会第七次全国代表大会开幕之际，我们谨向大会表示热烈的祝贺，并向与会的全体代表致以崇高的敬意。
    中国工人阶级，在伟大的抗美援朝保家卫国运动中，起了重大的作用，并在我国经济恢复和建设的事业中，有着辉煌的贡献，这也就是对于保卫世界和平的巨大贡献。
    我们相信：中国工会第七次全国代表大会，定能动员我国工人阶级和全体劳动人民，发挥更高的劳动热情和更大的创造能力，来更好地完成国家建设计划，改进人民生活，增强保卫世界和平的力量。
            中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
                一九五三年五月二日
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的贺电中国工会第七次全国代表大会亲爱的全体代表同志们：
    我们以极其兴奋和愉快的心情，热烈祝贺中国工会第七次全国代表大会的开幕。
    新中国诞生以来，中国工人阶级在中国共产党和我们伟大领袖毛主席的领导下，为完成社会改革，支援抗美援朝，和恢复、发展生产已经作了巨大努力。中国工人阶级以高度爱国主义热忱和对祖国伟大贡献，证明了自己是中国建设事业的伟大先锋。当此全国正进入新的经济建设的伟大时期的头一年，我们深信中国工会第七次全国代表大会的召开，必将会更进一步地动员全国工人阶级和全国人民一道，为完成和超额完成国家的建设计划，为实现国家工业化和社会主义共产主义前途而奋斗。
    中国新民主主义青年团团员和全国青年，特别是厂、矿、企业中的青年团员和青年们，定将全力支持和贯彻大会的决议。让我们的伟大祖国，在我们共同努力下，变得更加富强和美丽。
    谨祝大会胜利成功。
          中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
                一九五三年五月一日
