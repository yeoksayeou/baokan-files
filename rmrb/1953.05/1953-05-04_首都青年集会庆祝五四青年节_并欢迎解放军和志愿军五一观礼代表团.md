Title: Capital Youth Rally Celebrates May Fourth Youth Day and Welcomes May Day Delegation of the People's Liberation Army and the Volunteer Army

Author:

Date: 1953-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd—A rally of the capital's youth commemorating the "May Fourth" Youth Day and welcoming the May Day Delegation of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the May Day Returning Delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers was held this afternoon at the Beijing Concert Hall. Over 4,500 young people from all walks of life in Beijing attended the meeting.

Zhang Dazhong, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, spoke first, expressing, on behalf of the capital's youth, his high respect to the representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army from the frontier of national defense and the representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers from the Korean front. He called on the youth of the city to learn from the People's Liberation Army and the Volunteer Army, to do a good job in production, to complete their study tasks, and to commemorate "May Fourth" with practical actions.

Zuo Qi, representative of the May Day Delegation of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, briefly reported on the significant role played by Chinese youth in national defense construction. Then, Feng Zhenye, Deputy Political Commissar of a certain regiment of the Chinese People's Volunteers, in which heroes such as Huang Jiguang, Sun Zhanyuan, Yi Caixue, and Chen Zhiguo emerged in the Shangganling campaign, reported on the battle course and many heroic deeds of the Shangganling campaign. His speech was often interrupted by applause and cheers. Ma Xinnian, a second-class combat hero of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and Lu Min, a first-class combat hero of the Chinese People's Volunteers Air Force and an expert in shooting down saber-jet enemy planes, also reported on their combat deeds at the meeting.

Jin Zhaoying, a female mechanical worker and industrial labor model in Beijing, and Li Yujin, a student from the Affiliated Women's Middle School of Beijing Normal University, representing the city's young workers and students, expressed their determination to better learn the great patriotism and internationalism of the People's Liberation Army and the Volunteer Army, to further strengthen their ties with them, and to strive for greater victories on the production and defense fronts.

During the meeting, a group of Young Pioneers presented their beloved red scarves and flowers to the heroes. Zhou Jiaxi, a student from the Russian Department of Renmin University of China, entrusted a representative to present to Feng Zhenye, the representative of the heroes on the Shangganling front, a handkerchief with a Tiananmen pattern and the words "To the Most Beloved People" meticulously embroidered by her while ill, along with a letter of tribute.

### 首都青年集会庆祝五四青年节  并欢迎解放军和志愿军五一观礼代表团

1953-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    首都青年集会庆祝五四青年节
    并欢迎解放军和志愿军五一观礼代表团
    【新华社三日讯】首都青年纪念“五四”青年节、欢迎中国人民解放军“五一”观礼代表团和中国人民志愿军“五一”归国观礼代表团大会，三日下午在北京市音乐堂举行，到会的有北京市各界青年四千五百多人。
    中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会书记张大中首先讲话，他代表首都青年向来自国防前哨的中国人民解放军代表和来自朝鲜前线的中国人民志愿军的代表们表示崇高的敬意。他号召全市青年向解放军和志愿军学习，搞好生产，完成学习任务，以实际行动纪念“五四”。
    中国人民解放军“五一”观礼代表团代表左齐在会上扼要地报告了中国青年在国防建设中所起的重大作用。接着，由在上甘岭战役中出现黄继光、孙占元、易才学、陈治国等英雄人物的中国人民志愿军某团副政治委员冯振业报告上甘岭战役的战斗经过和许多英雄事迹。他的讲话常常为掌声和欢呼声所打断。中国人民志愿军二级战斗英雄马新年，中国人民志愿军空军一级战斗英雄、打佩刀式敌机的能手鲁珉，也都在会上报告了他们的战斗事迹。
    北京市工业劳动模范女机械工金兆瑛和北京师范大学附属女子中学学生李玉瑾，代表全市青年工人和学生表示决心更好地学习解放军和志愿军伟大的爱国主义和国际主义精神，进一步加强和他们的联系，争取在生产战线和国防战线上的更大胜利。
    会议中，一群少年儿童队员向英雄们献上自己心爱的红领巾和鲜花，中国人民大学俄文系学生周家玺把她在病中精心绣制的有着天安门图案和“送给最可爱的人”字样的手帕并致敬信一封，托代表献给上甘岭前线的英雄的代表冯振业。
