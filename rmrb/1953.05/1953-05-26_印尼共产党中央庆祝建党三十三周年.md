Title: Indonesian Communist Party Central Committee Celebrates 33rd Anniversary of Founding

Author:

Date: 1953-05-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta: The Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party (PKI) convened a meeting in Jakarta on May 22 to celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the party's founding. Local labor unions, youth groups, and women's organizations participated. A total of more than 700 people attended the conference.

    The General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, D.N. Aidit, delivered a lengthy report at the meeting entitled "The Indonesian People's Struggle for Full National Independence and for the Establishment of a New Indonesia." He reported on the history of the Indonesian people's struggle for national independence and the path taken by the Indonesian Communist Party.

    The conference also announced congratulatory telegrams from the fraternal communist parties of the Netherlands, Australia, India, and the People's Republic of China.

### 印尼共产党中央庆祝建党三十三周年

1953-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼共产党中央庆祝建党三十三周年
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社雅加达讯：印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会于五月二十二日在雅加达集会庆祝印尼共产党成立三十三周年，参加的有当地的工会、青年与妇女等团体。共有七百多人参加了大会。
    印尼共产党中央委员会总书记艾地在会上发表了“印尼人民为争取充分的民族独立、为争取建立一个新的印尼而斗争”的长篇报告。他报告了印尼人民为争取民族独立而斗争的历史以及印尼共产党所走过的道路。
    大会上并宣读了荷兰、澳大利亚、印度和中华人民共和国各兄弟共产党的贺电。
