Title: Youth Across the Country Joyfully Celebrate May Fourth Youth Day
Author:
Date: 1953-05-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency report: Youth in cities such as Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Xi'an, Qingdao, Harbin, Hangzhou, and Gui Sui, like youth across the country, happily celebrated their holiday - the "May Fourth" Youth Day.

    On this day, the atmosphere of youth pervaded various cities, with young people singing and engaging in joyous celebrations. The youth of all circles in the capital celebrated the "May Fourth" Youth Day with joy and excitement. Various commemorative activities were enthusiastically launched on the eve of the festival. Scenic spots such as Beihai Park, the Working People's Cultural Palace, the Temple of Heaven, and the Summer Palace were all open to the city's youth. On the 3rd and 4th alone, more than 100,000 young people participated in garden parties. The performance of wonderful programs by the Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble at the Xianongtan on the afternoon of the 4th further enhanced the festive joy of the young people. In Tianjin, more than 50,000 young people held garden parties in the Water Park and People's Park before the festival. In Shenyang, young people and working people organized more than 20 celebration groups to spend their holiday happily in the green forests of pines and cypresses. Youth League organizations in Beijing, Wuhan, and Gui Sui also held oath-taking ceremonies for admitting outstanding youth into the Youth League.

    Many city theaters and cinemas have prepared wonderful dramas and films for young people, including the color film "Youth Sports Festival."

    Reports and lectures attracted tens of thousands of young people. On May 2nd, Wu Xian, Secretary of the Harbin Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, made a report to more than 1,300 cadres of youth league organizations at all levels in the Working People's Cultural Palace, using vivid facts to illustrate the importance of young people strengthening their learning and mastering scientific knowledge and technology in the new economic construction that began this year. A group of students from the Department of Chemistry at Chongqing University also specially invited experienced veteran technicians to give reports. From these reports, young people deeply realized that they must intensify their learning and strive to improve their technical level.

    Young people held informal talks and symposiums to exchange their achievements and experiences in various fields.

    During the festival, many places also held sports meets. All sports fields across the country are open to young people today, allowing these young male and female athletes to show their talents here!

### 全国各地青年欢度五四青年节

1953-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    全国各地青年欢度五四青年节
    据新华社讯：北京、天津、沈阳、武汉、广州、重庆、西安、青岛、哈尔滨、杭州、归绥市等城市的青年，和全国各地青年一样，愉快地度过了自己的节日——“五四”青年节。
    这一天，各个城市洋溢着青春的气氛，青年人纵情地歌唱和进行联欢活动。首都各界青年怀着欢欣鼓舞的心情度过“五四”青年节。各种纪念活动，在节日的前夕，就热烈地展开。北海公园、劳动人民文化宫、天坛、颐和园等风景优美的游览场所，都为全市青年开放。三、四两日，参加游园的青年即有十万人以上。波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团四日下午在先农坛为首都青年演出精彩节目，更增加了青年们节日的欢欣。天津市五万多青年，在节日前就分别在水上公园、人民公园举行了游园大会。沈阳市青年们和劳动人民组织了二十多个联欢人群在苍松翠柏的绿林中，欢度自己的节日。北京、武汉和归绥等市的青年团组织，还举行了吸收优秀青年加入青年团的入团宣誓大会。
    许多城市的剧场与影院，都为青年们准备了精彩的剧目和影片，其中包括五彩影片“青年运动节”。
    报告会和讲演会，吸引了成千成万的青年。五月二日，中国新民主主义青年团哈尔滨市委书记吴限，在劳动人民文化宫向全市各级青年团组织的一千三百多干部作了报告，他用生动的事实说明了，在今年开始的新的经济建设中，青年们必须加强学习，掌握科学知识和技术的重要意义。重庆大学化学系有一个小组的学生，还特别邀请了富有经验的老技术工人作了报告。从这些报告中，青年们深深地体会到：必须加紧学习，努力提高自己的技术水平。
    青年们分别举行了不拘形式的漫谈和座谈会，交换自己在各个方面所获得的成就和经验。
    在节日里，不少地方还举办了运动会。各地所有的运动场今天都为青年人开放着，让这些青年男女运动员们在这里大显身手！
