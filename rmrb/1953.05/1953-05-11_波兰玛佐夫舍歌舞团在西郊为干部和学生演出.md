Title: The Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble Performs for Cadres and Students in the Western Suburbs

Author: 
Date: 1953-05-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: The Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble of the People's Republic of Poland held an evening of music and dance performances in the open-air auditorium of the North China Bureau Party School of the CPC Central Committee in the western suburbs this evening. More than 5,600 cadres and students from units such as the North China Administrative Committee, the North China Bureau Party School of the CPC Central Committee, the Marxist-Leninist Institute, Renmin University, and Beijing Foreign Languages School attended the evening. They enthusiastically appreciated the wonderful programs performed by these international friends for them.

　　The song and dance ensemble performed more than 20 music and dance programs for the audience within two hours. These programs demonstrated the outstanding achievements of the Polish people in song and dance art. Each program won continuous applause and enthusiastic cheers from the audience. Among them, the Polish folk song "How Difficult!" and the Mazurka dance were performed a second time at the audience's repeated requests.

　　At the end of the performance, dozens of male and female representatives from the Marxist-Leninist Institute, the North China Bureau Party School, and Beijing Foreign Languages School went on stage to present flags and flowers to all members of the ensemble. All the audience members lined up to bid farewell to the ensemble members as they returned to the city.

### 波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团在西郊为干部和学生演出

1953-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团在西郊为干部和学生演出
    【新华社十日讯】波兰人民共和国玛佐夫舍歌舞团十日晚在西郊中共中央华北局党校露天礼堂举行音乐、舞蹈表演晚会。参加晚会的有华北行政委员会，中共中央华北局党校，马克思列宁学院，人民大学及北京外国语学校等单位五千六百多个干部和学生。他们以极大的热情，欣赏了这些国际友人们为他们所演出的精彩节目。
    歌舞团在两小时内为观众演出了二十多个音乐和舞蹈节目。这些节目表现了波兰人民在歌舞艺术上的卓越成就。每个节目都博得了观众不断的鼓掌和热情的欢呼。其中波兰民歌“好难哪！”及玛茹卡舞，在观众的再三要求下都作了第二次表演。
    表演结束时，马克思列宁学院、华北局党校及北京外国语学校的代表男女数十人上台向全体团员献旗献花。全体观众并列队欢送团员们回城。
