Title: Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League Congratulates China on Youth Day
Author:
Date: 1953-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd: The Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China, congratulating China on Youth Day and the fourth anniversary of the founding of the New Democratic Youth League of China. The telegram reads as follows: Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China:

    On the occasion of China's Youth Day and the fourth anniversary of the founding of the New Democratic Youth League of China, on behalf of the members of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League and the Soviet youth, I extend fraternal greetings to the heroic Chinese youth! We sincerely wish the youth of the People's Republic of China new achievements in the cause of building a strong people's democratic China.
    Long live the permanent and unbreakable friendship between the youth of the Soviet Union and China!
    Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League
    April 29th

### 苏联青年团中央委员会祝贺我国青年节

1953-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联青年团中央委员会祝贺我国青年节
    【新华社二日讯】苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会致电中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会，祝贺中国青年节和中国新民主主义青年团成立四周年。电文如下：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会：
    值此中国青年节及中国新民主主义青年团成立四周年之际，谨代表苏联列宁共产主义青年团团员及苏联青年向英勇的中国青年致兄弟般的敬礼！我们谨祝中华人民共和国的青年在建设强大的人民民主中国的事业中获得新的成就。
    苏中两国青年永久的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会
    四月二十九日
