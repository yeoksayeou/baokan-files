Title: Volunteer Army May Day Observation Delegation Leaves Beijing to Return to Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd - The Chinese People's Volunteer Army's May Day observation delegation left Beijing on the afternoon of the 23rd to return to the Korean front.

Those who saw them off at the station included Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Resist America, Aid Korea General Association; Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General; Zeng Zhaosheng, Director of the General Office; Mo Yichang and Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Directors of the Organization Department; Hu Zhongchi, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department; Yu Zhenying and Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Directors of the Liaison Department; Wu Han, Vice Chairman of the North China Resist America, Aid Korea General Branch; Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Resist America, Aid Korea Branch; and more than 400 representatives from various organs and people's organizations in the capital.

Also seeing them off at the station were Xu Xingxi, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China; Kim Kuk-bo, Counselor; and many embassy staff.

During its stay in Beijing, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army May Day observation delegation was invited to give dozens of reports to people from all walks of life in the capital, providing a profound education in patriotism and internationalism to the broad masses of the people.

During their stay in Beijing, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army May Day observation delegation also visited the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, the Shijingshan Power Plant, and the Shuangqiao Farm.

### 志愿军五一节观礼代表团离京返朝鲜

1953-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军五一节观礼代表团离京返朝鲜
    【新华社二十三日讯】中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团在二十三日下午离开北京返回朝鲜前线。
    到车站欢送的有中国人民抗美援朝总会秘书长刘贯一、副秘书长吴茂荪、办公厅主任曾昭生、组织部副部长莫艺昌、王明远、宣传部副部长胡仲持、联络部副部长于振瀛、唐明照，华北抗美援朝总分会副主席吴晗，北京市抗美援朝分会主席张奚若及首都各机关、各人民团体代表等四百余人。
    到车站欢送的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办徐兴锡、参赞金国辅及使馆人员多人。
    中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团在京期间，曾应邀向首都各界人民作了数十场报告，使广大人民受到爱国主义和国际主义的深刻教育。
    中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团在京还参观了石景山钢铁厂、石景山发电厂和双桥农场。
