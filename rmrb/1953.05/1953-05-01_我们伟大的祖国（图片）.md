Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-05-01
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Workers at the Anshan Iron and Steel Company's steelmaking plant have pledged to produce high-quality steel for May Day as a gift to Chairman Mao. This is a scene of the plant's workers working hard. During this rapid steelmaking process, they add materials while quickly stirring the molten steel in the furnace to ensure uniform smelting and excellent quality.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍山钢铁公司炼钢厂的工人提出保证，在五一节炼好优质钢，作为向毛主席的献礼。这是该厂工人正在紧张工作的情形。他们在这快速炼钢的过程中，一面加料，一面迅速搅动炉中的钢水，以求熔炼均匀、质量优良。
