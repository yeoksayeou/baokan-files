Title: Celebrating the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1953-05-07
Page: 2

    Congratulations to All Representatives of the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation:
    On the occasion of the opening of the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, we, on behalf of women across the country, extend our warmest congratulations.
    Over the past four years, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese working class has played a great pioneering and exemplary role, completed the country's economic recovery, and prepared the necessary conditions for the country to enter economic construction!
    We are fully confident that after this congress, workers across the country will unite more closely around Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, dedicate greater efforts to over fulfilling the country's great economic construction plan, and further defend the cause of world peace and democracy!
    We wish the congress a complete success!
              All-China Democratic Women's Federation
                  May 3, 1953
    Congratulatory Telegram from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association:
    To all representatives of the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions:
    The Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions has opened. Since the birth of New China, the Chinese working class has made significant contributions to the recovery and development of the national economy. We extend to you our highest respect and warmest congratulations.
    Now, our country is facing a new historical task, which is to realize national industrialization and gradually carry out socialist transformation. The Chinese working class will play a glorious pioneering role in completing this task. We firmly believe that the convocation of the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions will help further promote labor emulation among the Chinese working class, give full play to the working class's high labor enthusiasm and creativity, learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, and complete and over fulfill the first five-year construction plan of our great motherland.
    We wish the congress a complete success.
                Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
                            May 3

### 庆祝中国工会第七次全国代表大会

1953-05-07
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国工会第七次全国代表大会
    中华全国民主妇女联合会的贺电中国工会第七次全国代表大会全体代表：
    值此中国工会第七次全国代表大会开幕之际，我们谨代表全国妇女表示最热烈的庆贺。
    过去四年来，中国工人阶级在毛主席和中国共产党的领导下，发挥了伟大的先锋与模范作用，完成了国家的经济恢复工作，为国家进入经济建设准备了必要的条件！
    我们完全相信，经过此次大会，全国工人将更加紧密地团结在毛主席和中国共产党的周围，为超额完成祖国伟大的经济建设计划，并为进一步保卫世界和平民主事业而献出更大的力量！
    谨祝大会胜利成功！
              中华全国民主妇女联合会
                  一九五三年五月三日
    中苏友好协会总会的贺电中国工会第七次全国代表大会全体代表同志们：
    中国工会第七次全国代表大会开幕了。新中国诞生以来，中国工人阶级对国民经济的恢复与发展，曾作了重大的贡献。我们谨向你们表示崇高的敬意和热烈的祝贺。
    现在，我们的国家正面临着一个新的历史任务，这就是实现国家工业化逐步进行社会主义改造。中国工人阶级在完成这一任务中，将起光辉的先锋作用。我们深信中国工会第七次全国代表大会的召开，必将有助于在中国工人阶级中进一步开展劳动竞赛，发挥工人阶级高度的劳动热情和创造精神，学习苏联的先进经验，来完成和超额完成我们伟大祖国第一个五年建设计划。
    谨祝大会胜利成功。
                中苏友好协会总会
                            五月三日
