Title: The State Council Holds Its 177th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-05-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The 177th meeting of the Central People's Government's State Council was held on the afternoon of the 8th. The meeting heard a report by Minister of Justice Shi Liang of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Justice on the situation of the Second National Judicial Conference, and approved Minister Shi Liang's "Report on Strengthening the Construction of People's Judicial Work" and the "Resolution of the Second National Judicial Conference" made at the Second National Judicial Conference. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal cases and appointment and removal cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一七七次会议

1953-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一七七次会议
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百七十七次政务会议在八日下午举行。会议听取了中央人民政府司法部史良部长所作的关于第二届全国司法会议情况的报告，批准了史良部长在第二届全国司法会议上所作的“关于加强人民司法工作建设的报告”及“第二届全国司法会议决议”。会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
