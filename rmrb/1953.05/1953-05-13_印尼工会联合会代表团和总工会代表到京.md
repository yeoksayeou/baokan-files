Title: Delegation of Indonesian Trade Union Federation and Representatives of the General Confederation of Trade Unions Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 11th: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the delegation of the Indonesian Trade Union Federation and the representatives of the Indonesian General Confederation of Trade Unions, a group of four people who came to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day and the Seventh National Congress of the Trade Union, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 11th due to travel delays. Li Jiebo, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Education Trade Union, Zhang Jin, Director of the Construction Trade Union Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Wang Xianzhou, Deputy Director of the Financial Trade Union Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Chen Xiwu, Deputy Director of the Workers' Publishing House, and others welcomed them at the station. The representative of the Indonesian General Confederation of Trade Unions now in Beijing also came to the station to welcome them.
    The list of the Delegation of the Indonesian Trade Union Federation:
    Head of the Delegation: Datu, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Public Works Trade Union
    Members: Suyono, Chairman of the Indonesian Teachers' Union; Agung Sudarti, Second Secretary of the Indonesian People's Bank Employees Union
    Member of the Delegation of the Indonesian General Confederation of Trade Unions:
        Suporato, Representative of the Indonesian Farm Workers' Union

### 印尼工会联合会代表团和总工会代表到京

1953-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼工会联合会代表团和总工会代表到京
     【新华社十一日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请，前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节和中国工会第七次全国代表大会的印尼工会联合会代表团及印尼总工会代表一行四人，因旅途迟滞，在十一日晚间乘火车抵达北京。到车站欢迎的有中国铁路工会全国委员会主席李颉伯、中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明、中华全国总工会建筑工会工作委员会主任张进、中华全国总工会金融工会工作委员会副主任王显周、工人出版社副社长陈希文等人。现在北京的印尼总工会代表也到车站欢迎。
    印尼工会联合会代表团名单：
    团长：达都公共工程工会中央委员会主席
    团员：苏约诺印尼教师公会主席  阿贡·苏达第印尼人民银行职工会第二书记
    印尼总工会代表团团员：
        苏波拉托  代表印尼农场工会
