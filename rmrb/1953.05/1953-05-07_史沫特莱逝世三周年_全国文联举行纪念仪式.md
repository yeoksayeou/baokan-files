Title: Commemorating the Third Anniversary of Agnes Smedley's Death: National Federation of Literary and Art Circles Holds Memorial Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1953-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles held a memorial ceremony on the 6th, the third anniversary of the death of Agnes Smedley, an American revolutionary writer and friend of the Chinese people, at Smedley's tomb in the Cemetery of Revolutionary Martyrs in Babaoshan.

Attending the memorial ceremony were: Zhou Yang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and literary and art figures from the capital and friends of Smedley during her lifetime, including Sha Kefu, Kang Keqing, Liao Mengxing, Zhao Shuli, Cao Jinghua, Chen Hansheng, Louis Wheaton, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and his wife, Louis Ayre, a member of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, British peace activist Ms. Blake, and journalists Starobin and Epstein.

Zhou Yang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, spoke at the memorial ceremony. He said: Ms. Smedley was a faithful friend of the Chinese people, and her friendship with the Chinese people was established during the difficult period of the Chinese revolution, when she fought alongside the Chinese people through arduous struggles. She had deep sympathy and concern for the Chinese people's revolution, and in her works, she passionately depicted and praised the Chinese revolution. She was always full of confidence in the victory of the Chinese revolution. Although she was persecuted to death by the American reactionaries, she ultimately witnessed the victory of the Chinese people. Smedley dedicated her life to the cause of justice and won the respect and remembrance of not only the Chinese people but also the people of all countries.

At the memorial ceremony, Chen Hansheng, Louis Ayre, Xin Dun, and Ms. Blake also spoke. Zhou Yang also presented a wreath on behalf of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the All-China Association of Literary Workers.

### 史沫特莱逝世三周年  全国文联举行纪念仪式

1953-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    史沫特莱逝世三周年
    全国文联举行纪念仪式
    【新华社六日讯】六日为中国人民之友美国革命作家史沫特莱女士逝世三周年纪念日，中华全国文学艺术界联合会特在八宝山革命烈士公墓史氏墓前举行纪念仪式。参加纪念仪式的有：中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席周扬和首都文学艺术界人士及史氏生前友好沙可夫、康克清、廖梦醒、赵树理、曹靖华、陈翰笙，亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长路易斯·惠吞及夫人，亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会委员路易·艾黎，英国和平人士布雷克女士，和新闻界人士斯塔罗宾、爱泼斯坦等。中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席周扬在纪念仪式上讲话。他说：史沫特莱女士是中国人民的忠实朋友，她和中国人民的友谊是在中国革命困难时期与中国人民一同进行艰苦斗争的当中建立起来的。她对中国人民革命给予了深切的同情和关怀，在她的作品中，热情洋溢地描绘和歌颂了中国革命，她对中国革命是始终充满着胜利信心的。虽然她被美国反动派迫害至死，但她终究看到了中国人民的胜利。史沫特莱毕生为正义事业而奋斗，博得了不仅中国人民，而且各国人民的尊敬和怀念。在纪念仪式上，陈翰笙、路易·艾黎、辛敦、布雷克女士都讲了话。周扬并代表中华全国文学艺术界联合会和中华全国文学工作者协会献花圈。
