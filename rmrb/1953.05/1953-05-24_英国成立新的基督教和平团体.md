Title: Britain Establishes New Christian Peace Group
Author:
Date: 1953-05-24
Page: 4
    TASS, London, May 21st: A new Christian peace group has been established in Britain. The chairman of this organization is Reverend Mervyn Stockwood, a clergyman of the Church of England; the secretary is Mrs. Katherine Williamson, who has twice served as the Mayor of Canterbury and participated in the Vienna World People's Peace Congress; the treasurer is Mrs. Jessie Street, a member of the World Peace Council and a well-known progressive.

    The stated aims of this group are: to end the wars currently being waged, to prevent the occurrence of a third world war, and to abolish war as a means of resolving disputes.

### 英国成立新的基督教和平团体

1953-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    英国成立新的基督教和平团体
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十一日讯：英国成立了一个新的基督教和平团体。这个组织的主席是英格兰教会的牧师麦尔塞·威尔逊教士；书记是凯塞琳·威廉逊夫人，她曾经两度任坎特伯雷的市长，并且曾参加维也纳世界人民和平大会；负责财务的是杰西·斯崔特夫人，她是世界和平理事会理事，是一位著名的进步人士。
    这个团体所宣布的宗旨是：结束现在正在进行的战争，防止第三次世界大战的发生和取消以战争作为解决纠纷的手段。
