Title: Guo Moruo and Others Leave Beijing for Stockholm to Attend World Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee and Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, and committee member Mao Dun, left Beijing on the 30th for Stockholm, the capital of Sweden, to attend the Standing Committee meeting of the World Peace Council to be held on May 5th and 6th.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Standing Committee members Luo Longji, committee members Zhu Kezhen, Tao Menghe, Hong Shen, and Secretary-General Liu Ningyi, Liu Guanyi, and Deputy Secretary-General Wu Maosun, totaling more than 30 people.

### 郭沫若等离京赴斯德哥尔摩  出席世界和平理事会会议

1953-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若等离京赴斯德哥尔摩
    出席世界和平理事会会议
    【新华社三十日讯】　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、世界和平理事会常务委员会副主席郭沫若与委员茅盾，于三十日离京赴瑞典首都斯德哥尔摩出席五月五日、六日召开的世界和平理事会常务委员会会议。
    到机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，常务委员罗隆基、委员竺可桢、陶孟和、洪深及秘书长刘宁一、刘贯一、副秘书长吴茂荪等共三十余人。
