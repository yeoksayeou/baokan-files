Title: Volunteer Army "May Day" Homecoming Delegation Leader Yuan Shengping Invited to Report to the National Committee of the CPPCC
Author:
Date: 1953-05-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency Report: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a report meeting on the 15th, inviting Yuan Shengping, head of the "May Day" Homecoming Delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, to give a report. The report meeting was presided over by Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC.
    In his report, Yuan Shengping first paid tribute on behalf of all the Volunteer Army comrades to the great leader Chairman Mao, to all the comrades of the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and to all the fathers, elders, brothers, and sisters of all circles in the motherland. Yuan Shengping then described the great achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in the past six months in the three stages of the Shangganling Campaign, the Winter Offensive, and this year's Spring Offensive, and cited many vivid examples to illustrate the Volunteer Army soldiers' strong will to love the motherland and defend peace, as well as their fighting spirit to overcome difficulties and bravely kill the enemy. The heroic deeds of the Volunteer Army's infantry, artillery, air force, railway troops, engineers, and other services, as introduced by Yuan Shengping, deeply moved all the attendees, and the venue was filled with warm applause. Yuan Shengping finally stated that all comrades of the Volunteer Army deeply appreciate Chairman Mao's meticulous care and the tremendous support of the people of the motherland.
    Following this, Ma Xinnian, representative of the "May Day" Homecoming Delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and recipient of the title of "Second-Class Combat Hero" in the Shangganling Campaign, gave a report. He vividly described his heroic deeds of skillfully using various weapons to kill and wound more than eighty enemies in the Shangganling Campaign. All the attendees repeatedly stood up and applauded to pay tribute to the hero.
    On the 12th and 14th, some representatives of the "May Day" Homecoming Delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army gave reports to the staff of the directly affiliated organizations of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the State Council of the Central People's Government, respectively.

### 志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团  袁升平团长应邀向政协全国委员会作报告

1953-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团
    袁升平团长应邀向政协全国委员会作报告
    据新华社讯：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会于十五日举行报告会，邀请中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团团长袁升平作报告。报告会由中国人民政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通主持。
    袁升平在报告中首先代表志愿军全体同志向伟大的领袖毛主席，向中国人民解放军陆、海、空军全体同志们，向祖国各界父老、兄弟、姐妹们致敬。袁升平接着叙述了半年来中国人民志愿军在上甘岭战役、冬季作战以及今年春季作战等三个阶段中的伟大战绩，并引述了许多生动事例，说明志愿军战士们热爱祖国、保卫和平的坚强意志，以及他们克服困难、英勇杀敌的战斗精神。袁升平所介绍的志愿军步兵、炮兵、空军、铁道部队、工兵等兵种中许多可歌可泣的英雄事迹，深深感动了到会的全体人员，会场上不断响起热烈的掌声。袁升平最后表示：志愿军全体同志深深感谢毛主席的无微不至的关怀，感谢祖国人民的巨大支援。
    接着由中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团代表、上甘岭战役中荣获“二级战斗英雄”称号的马新年作报告。他生动地叙述了在上甘岭战役中熟练地使用各种武器杀伤敌人八十多名的英勇事迹，到会的全体人员一再起立鼓掌，向英雄致敬。
    十二、十四两日，中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团的部分代表，曾分别向中共中央直属机关、中央人民政府政务院的工作人员作报告。
