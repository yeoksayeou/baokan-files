Title: China and Various Countries Exchange Greetings for May Day International Labor Day
Author:
Date: 1953-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: On the eve of May Day International Labor Day, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions wrote to the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, the Mongolian Trade Union, the All-India Trade Union Congress, the Indian Labor Association, the United Trades Union Congress of India, the Pakistan Federation of Labour, the All-Indonesia Central Labor Organization, the Trade Union Congress of Burma, and the Ceylon Federation of Labour, extending cordial greetings to the working classes of various countries and wishing them closer unity to jointly fight against war and defend Asian and world peace.
    
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: On May Day International Labor Day, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions received congratulatory telegrams or letters from the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions of the Soviet Union, the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Poland, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia, the Free German Trade Union Federation, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Romania, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Hungary, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Bulgaria, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Albania, the Pakistan Federation of Labour, the All-Burma Trade Union Congress, the Japan Liaison Office of the World Federation of Trade Unions, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the Italian General Confederation of Labour, and the Latin American Workers' Confederation, representing the working classes of their respective countries, extending warm fraternal greetings to the workers of our country and wishing the workers of our country greater achievements in national construction. The Britain-China Friendship Association telegraphed the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to congratulate the workers of our country on May Day International Labor Day.

### 我国和各国工人互贺五一国际劳动节

1953-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我国和各国工人互贺五一国际劳动节
    据新华社讯：中华全国总工会于五一国际劳动节前夕，写信给越南总工会、蒙古工会、全印总工会、印度劳工协会、印度工会统一工会大会、巴基斯坦工会联合会、全印尼中央职工会、缅甸工会大会、锡兰工会联合会，向各国工人阶级致以亲切的祝贺，并祝贺更加紧密地团结起来，为反对战争，保卫亚洲及世界和平而共同奋斗。
    据新华社讯：在五一国际劳动节，中华全国总工会收到全苏工会中央理事会、越南总工会、波兰工会中央理事会、捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会、自由德国工会联合会、罗马尼亚总工会中央理事会、匈牙利工会中央理事会、保加利亚总工会中央理事会、阿尔巴尼亚总工会中央理事会、巴基斯坦工会联合会、全缅工会大会、世界工联日本联络事务局、日本工会总评议会、意大利总工会、拉丁美洲工人联合会等工会组织的贺电或贺信，分别代表各该国的工人阶级向我国工人致以热烈的兄弟的敬礼，并祝贺我国工人在国家建设中取得更大的成就。英中友协致电我国人民外交学会，向我国工人祝贺五一国际劳动节。
