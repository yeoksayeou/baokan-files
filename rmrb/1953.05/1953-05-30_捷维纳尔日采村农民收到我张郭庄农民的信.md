Title: Farmers of Řehenice Village Receive Letter from Farmers of Zhangguozhuang
Author:
Date: 1953-05-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 29th: The Unified Agricultural Cooperative of Řehenice Village in Prague Oblast, Czechoslovakia, received a letter from farmers of Zhangguozhuang Village near Beijing, China, who celebrated Friendship Day with the fraternal farmers of the village in late April. The full text of this letter was published in the newspaper Red Rights on the 28th.

The farmers of Zhangguozhuang expressed their gratitude in the letter to their Czechoslovakian friends for giving them agricultural machinery, believing that these gifts were a manifestation of the profound friendship and brotherly unity between the peoples of China and Czechoslovakia. They expressed their heartfelt gratitude for the great concern and assistance that the late President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Klement Gottwald, had shown to the Chinese farmers during his lifetime. The letter from the farmers of Zhangguozhuang narrated their achievements in agricultural production and guaranteed that they would use the new machines to obtain higher yields. The letter concluded by expressing the hope to exchange experiences and actively learn from Soviet experience; to work together to increase production, to build a happier and better life, and to defend world peace.

### 捷维纳尔日采村农民收到我张郭庄农民的信

1953-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    捷维纳尔日采村农民收到我张郭庄农民的信
    【新华社布拉格二十九日电】捷克斯洛伐克共和国布拉格州维纳尔日采村统一农业合作社，接到了四月下旬与该村兄弟农民同时庆祝友谊日的我国北京附近张郭庄农民的信。这封信的全文已刊载在二十八日的红色权利报上。
    张郭庄的农民在信里表示感谢捷克斯洛伐克友人送给他们农业机器，认为这些礼物是中国和捷克斯洛伐克两国人民深厚友谊和兄弟般团结的表现。他们对于捷克斯洛伐克共和国故总统克·哥特瓦尔德生前对中国农民的极大关怀和援助，表示衷心的感谢。张郭庄农民的信叙述了他们在农业生产方面的成就，并且保证要用新机器获得更高的收获量。信中最后表示希望交流经验和积极学习苏联经验；为提高生产量、为建设更幸福和更美好的生活并为保卫世界和平而共同努力。
