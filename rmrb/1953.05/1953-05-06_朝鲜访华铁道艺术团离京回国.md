Title: Korean Railway Art Troupe Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1953-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    The Korean Democratic People's Republic Visiting Chinese Railway Art Troupe, invited by the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government to visit and perform in China, concluded its visit and performances in fifteen cities including Beijing, Tianjin, Jinan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Xi'an, and Shenyang. It departed Beijing for Korea by special train on the afternoon of the 5th.

    Among those who saw the troupe off at the station were Lü Zhengcao, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, Gui Peng, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Ministry of Railways, Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Xie Shuangqiu, Specialist of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, Xiao Hongqi, Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union, and more than 1,000 workers from the Ministry of Railways and the Beijing Railway Administration.

    Xu Xingxi, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Korean Democratic People's Republic in China, and embassy staff members also went to the station to see them off.

    A grand send-off rally was held at the station by those seeing them off.

### 朝鲜访华铁道艺术团离京回国

1953-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华铁道艺术团离京回国
    据新华社讯：应中央人民政府铁道部邀请前来我国访问、演出的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团在北京、天津、济南、南京、上海、杭州、广州、武汉、西安、沈阳等十五个城市访问演出结束后，五日下午乘专车离北京回朝鲜。
    到车站送行的有中央人民政府铁道部副部长吕正操、铁道部政治部副主任桂蓬、中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、中央人民政府外交部亚洲司专员谢爽秋、交际处副处长沈平、中国铁路工会全国委员会秘书长萧洪启以及铁道部和北京铁路管理局的职工共一千多人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办徐兴锡和使馆人员也到车站欢送。
    欢送人员在车站上举行了隆重的欢送大会。
