Title: The Soviet Marx-Engels-Lenin Institute Houses a Rich Collection of Valuable Documents on the Communist Movement
Author:
Date: 1953-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: The Soviet Marx-Engels-Lenin Institute is the institution with the largest collection of valuable historical documents on Marxism, the international workers' movement, and the communist movement in the world. Its library now holds a total of one million rare books, newspapers, and magazine compilations that were first published or are out of print. The Marx-Engels-Lenin Institute was established in 1931, through the merger of the original K. Marx Institute, F. Engels Institute, and U.I. Lenin Institute. This institute is a scientific research institution of the Communist Party. Its task is to collect and preserve the documents of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, publish their works, and study the life and activities of the classic authors of Marxism-Leninism. The library of the institute contains rare editions of Marx's and Engels' works published during their lifetimes, translations of their various works published in the languages of the world, and compilations of newspapers and magazines they personally edited. It also contains the most complete German editions and translations of "The Communist Manifesto" and "Das Kapital" in other languages. The library holds almost all the books secretly published in Russia before the revolution, compilations of various Bolshevik underground newspapers, and all of Lenin's and Stalin's works, as well as the newspapers, leaflets, and pamphlets they edited and wrote. The institute's archives also contain the manuscripts of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, all the main documents and materials related to the history of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and the historical documents of the Communist League, the First International, and the Paris Commune. Studying in the reading room of the library are scientific staff, doctors, graduate students from the Soviet Academy of Sciences, the Academies of Sciences of the various Union Republics, and various research institutes, lecturers of the "Fundamentals of Marxism-Leninism" course, and scientific staff specially sent from various regions. The Marx-Engels-Lenin Institute has also established branch institutes—Institutes of Party History—in various Union Republics of the Soviet Union and some cities of the Russian Federation. The task of these institutes is to collect documents for the study and compilation of the history of local Communist Party organizations, and to translate the classic works of Marxism-Leninism into the languages of the various nationalities of the Soviet Union for publication.

### 苏联马克思——恩格斯——列宁学院  藏有极丰富的共产主义运动珍贵文献

1953-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联马克思——恩格斯——列宁学院藏有极丰富的共产主义运动珍贵文献
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联的马克思—恩格斯—列宁学院，是世界上收藏马克思主义、国际工人运动和共产主义运动珍贵的历史文献最多的一个机关。它的图书馆现在共保存有一百万部最早出版或绝版了的珍本书籍、报纸和杂志的合订本。马—恩—列学院建立于一九三一年，由原来的卡·马克思学院、弗·恩格斯学院和乌·伊·列宁学院合并而成。这个学院是共产党的科学研究机关。它的任务是收集和保藏马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的文献，出版他们的著作，研究马克思—列宁主义经典著作人的生平及活动。学院的图书馆中藏有马克思、恩格斯本人在世时出版的著作珍本、用世界各国文字出版的他们的各种著作的译本，以及他们亲自主办的报纸和杂志的合订本，还藏有最齐全的“共产党宣言”和“资本论”的德文本及其他文字的译本。图书馆几乎藏有全部革命前在俄罗斯秘密出版的书籍，各种布尔什维克地下报纸的合订本，以及列宁和斯大林的全部著作，他们所编辑和撰写的报纸、传单和小册子。在学院的文件库中还藏有马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的著作手稿，有关苏联共产党党史的全部主要文件和材料，以及共产主义者同盟、第一国际和巴黎公社的历史文件。在图书馆的阅览室中学习的有苏联科学院、各加盟共和国科学院、各研究所的科学工作人员、博士、研究生，“马克思—列宁主义基础”课程的讲师，和各地专门派来的科学工作人员。马—恩—列学院还在苏联各加盟共和国和俄罗斯联邦的一些城市中设立了分院——党史研究所。这些研究所的任务是收藏供研究和编纂各地共产党组织历史之用的文件，把马克思—列宁主义的经典著作译成苏联各民族的文字出版。
