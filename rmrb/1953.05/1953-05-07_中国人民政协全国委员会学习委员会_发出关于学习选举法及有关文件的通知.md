Title: The Learning Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Issues a Notice on Studying the Electoral Law and Relevant Documents
Author:
Date: 1953-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Learning Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference recently issued a supplementary notice on learning issues to the learning committees of provincial and municipal CPPCC committees, requiring the organization of people from all walks of life to study the "Electoral Law of the National People's Congress and Local People's Congresses of the People's Republic of China" and related documents. The full text of the notice is as follows: To the learning committees of provincial and municipal CPPCC committees:

In our committee's March 10th notice to the learning committees of provincial and municipal CPPCC committees regarding the organization of the study of documents from the Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and other relevant documents, due to the upcoming nationwide general election movement in our country, it is now stipulated that the study of the electoral law and related documents will begin in May.

1.  The purpose of the study is to understand the superiority of our country's People's Congress system and the significance of implementing general elections and convening people's congresses at all levels for our country's national construction through the study of the electoral law and related documents; and through the understanding of the above issues, to enable people from all walks of life to more correctly and actively participate in the publicity work of the electoral law and participate in the general election movement.

2.  The documents stipulated for study are as follows, of which the first four are required reading:

    (1) Resolution of the Central People's Government Committee on Convening the National People's Congress and Local People's Congresses at All Levels

    (2) Electoral Law of the National People's Congress and Local People's Congresses of the People's Republic of China

    (3) Deng Xiaoping: Explanation on the Draft of the "Electoral Law of the National People's Congress and Local People's Congresses of the People's Republic of China"

    (4) Central Election Committee: Instructions on Basic-Level Election Work

    (5) Central Election Committee: Answers to Several Questions Regarding Voter Qualifications

    (6) Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government: Instructions on Conducting a National Population Census and Registration in Preparation for the General Election

    (7) Welcome the General Election and Implement the People's Congress System (January 15th People's Daily Editorial)

    (8) Support the Electoral Law and Launch the General Election Movement (March 4th People's Daily Editorial)

3.  Study time: Estimated at two months, but localities can shorten or extend it according to actual conditions.

4.  The main methods of study are reading documents and organizing discussions, and inviting responsible comrades to make reports and answer questions.

The Learning Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference

April 26th

### 中国人民政协全国委员会学习委员会  发出关于学习选举法及有关文件的通知

1953-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民政协全国委员会学习委员会
    发出关于学习选举法及有关文件的通知
    【新华社六日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会学习委员会最近向各省、市协商委员会学习委员会发出关于学习问题的补充通知，要求在各界人士中组织学习“中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会及地方各级人民代表大会选举法”及有关文件。通知全文如下：各省、市协商委员会学习委员会：
    本会三月十日通知各省、市协商委员会学习委员会组织学习中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议文件及其他有关文件，由于我国普选运动即将展开，现在规定自五月份开始学习选举法及有关文件。
    一、学习目的是通过选举法及有关文件的学习，认识我国人民代表大会制度的优越性和实行普选、召开各级人民代表大会对我国国家建设的意义；并通过对上述问题的认识，使各界人士更加正确地积极参加选举法的宣传工作和参加普选运动。
    二、规定学习的文件如后，其中前四篇是必读的文件：
    （一）中央人民政府委员会关于召开全国人民代表大会及地方各级人民代表大会的决议
    （二）中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会及地方各级人民代表大会选举法
    （三）邓小平：关于“中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会及地方各级人民代表大会选举法”草案的说明
    （四）中央选举委员会：关于基层选举工作的指示
    （五）中央选举委员会：关于选民资格若干问题的解答
    （六）中央人民政府政务院：为准备普选进行全国人口调查登记的指示
    （七）迎接普选，实行人民代表大会制度（一月十五日人民日报社论）
    （八）拥护选举法，开展普选运动（三月四日人民日报社论）
    三、学习时间：预计两个月，各地可根据实际情况缩短或延长之。
    四、学习方式主要是阅读文件和组织座谈，并请负责同志做报告和解答问题。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会学习委员会
    四月二十六日
