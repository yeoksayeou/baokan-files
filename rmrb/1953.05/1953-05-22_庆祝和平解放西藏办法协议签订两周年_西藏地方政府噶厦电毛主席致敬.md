Title: Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet, the Kashag of the Tibetan Local Government Sends a Telegram to Chairman Mao Expressing Respect

Author:
Date: 1953-05-22
Page: 1

    Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet
    The Kashag of the Tibetan Local Government Sends a Telegram to Chairman Mao Expressing Respect
    【Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 21st】 The Kashag of the Tibetan Local Government sent a telegram to Chairman Mao expressing respect on the occasion of the second anniversary of the signing of the "Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet". To Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government:
    On May 23, 1953, the second anniversary of the signing of the "Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet," the Tibetan local government and the People's Liberation Army stationed in Tibet will jointly hold a meeting in Lhasa and major cities and towns in Tibet to celebrate warmly. Under the correct leadership of the Central People's Government, the Dalai Lama of the Tibetan local government is actively and responsibly leading the monks and laypeople of Tibet to fully support the People's Liberation Army stationed in Tibet, strengthen the unity between the Han and Tibetan nationalities and the internal unity of the Tibetan nationality, and strive to gradually implement the agreement, consolidate national defense, and build a new Tibet.
    Respectfully wishing Chairman Mao a long life!
    Respectfully presented by the Kashag of the Tibetan Local Government
    May 20, 1953, Tibetan Water Snake Year, April 5th

### 庆祝和平解放西藏办法协议签订两周年  西藏地方政府噶厦电毛主席致敬

1953-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝和平解放西藏办法协议签订两周年
    西藏地方政府噶厦电毛主席致敬
    【新华社拉萨二十一日电】西藏地方政府噶厦为庆祝和平解放西藏办法的协议签订两周年给毛主席的致敬电。中央人民政府毛主席：
    一九五三年五月二十三日为“关于和平解放西藏办法的协议”签订两周年，西藏地方政府和驻藏中国人民解放军将在拉萨及西藏各大城镇共同召开大会，热烈庆祝。西藏地方政府达赖喇嘛正积极负责地领导西藏僧俗人民，在中央人民政府的正确领导下，全力支援驻藏人民解放军，加强汉藏民族团结和西藏民族内部团结，为逐步实现协议、巩固国防、建设新西藏而努力。
    敬祝毛主席万寿无疆
    西藏地方政府噶厦敬呈
   一九五三年五月二十日藏历水蛇年四月五日
