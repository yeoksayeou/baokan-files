Title: TASS Authorized to Deny Rumors
Author: 
Date: 1953-05-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 9th: TASS, Moscow, May 9th: According to information from Kuomintang agent Gu Weijun in the United States, the New York "Herald Tribune" published a report on May 7th, falsely claiming that the People's Republic of China, the Soviet Union, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam signed an agreement last November. According to this agreement, the People's Republic of China promised to send 300,000 troops to Indochina when needed, and the Soviet Union promised to supply the Democratic Republic of Vietnam with the armaments for five divisions of troops.

TASS is authorized to issue a statement: This report is a complete fabrication aimed at misleading world public opinion.

### 塔斯社奉命辟谣

1953-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    塔斯社奉命辟谣
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：纽约的“先驱论坛报”根据国民党在美国的代理人顾维钧的消息，在五月七日刊登了一项报道，硬说中华人民共和国、苏联和越南民主共和国三国已在去年十
    一月间签订了一项协定，根据这项协定，中华人民共和国答应在需要时派遣三十万军队到印度支那去，苏联则答应供给越南民主共和国五个师军队的武装。
    塔斯社奉命发表声明：这项报道完全是一种捏造，其目的是想迷惑世界的舆论。
