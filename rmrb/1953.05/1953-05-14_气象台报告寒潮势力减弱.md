Title: Meteorological Observatory Reports Cold Wave Weakening
Author:
Date: 1953-05-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency report: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a cold wave message: The cold wave reached the South China coast on the evening of the 13th and its force is weakening. On the 14th, except for the northeast, Bohai Sea, Yellow Sea, Shandong Peninsula, and parts of Fujian, Guangdong, and Guangxi, where there are still strong winds of force 5 or 6, the wind force in other areas has tended to weaken.

### 气象台报告寒潮势力减弱

1953-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台报告寒潮势力减弱
    据新华社讯：中央气象台发布寒潮消息：寒潮十三日晚到达华南沿海，势力在减弱中。十四日除东北、渤海、黄海、山东半岛和福建、广东、广西的局部地方还有五、六级大风外，其余各地风力已趋减弱。
