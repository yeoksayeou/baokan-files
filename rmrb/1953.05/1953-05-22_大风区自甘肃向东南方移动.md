Title: Strong Wind Zone Moving Southeastward from Gansu
Author:
Date: 1953-05-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 21st: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a strong wind advisory on the evening of the 21st: Starting on the evening of the 21st, a strong wind zone will gradually move southeastward from the Gansu area of ​​China. It is expected that starting from the morning of the 22nd, wind force will reach level 6 to 7 in southern Shaanxi, southern Shanxi, all of Henan, and northern Hubei, and level 5 to 6 in the Sichuan area. From the evening of the 22nd, wind force in southern Hubei and the Dongting Lake area will also reach around level 6 to 7, with northerly winds.

The Yellow Sea and East China Sea in China will continue to experience strong winds of around level 7 on the 22nd, with southerly winds.

### 大风区自甘肃向东南方移动

1953-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    大风区自甘肃向东南方移动
    【新华社二十一日讯】中央气象台二十一日晚发布大风消息：二十一日晚起，大风区自我国的甘肃一带陆续向东南方向移动，预计自二十二日晨起，陕西南部、山西南部、河南全省、湖北的北部，风力达六至七级，四川一带风力达五至六级。二十二日晚起，湖北的南部与洞庭湖一带，风力亦将达六至七级左右，风向偏北。
    我国黄海与东海二十二日仍将继续有七级左右大风，风向偏南。
