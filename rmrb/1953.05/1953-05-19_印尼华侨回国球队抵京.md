Title: Indonesian Chinese Returned Athletes Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-05-19
Page: 3

The Indonesian Chinese Returned Athletes Team, a group of fifty people led by team leaders Fang Dingxun and Ding Zicai, arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 16th.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Li Tiemin, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government; Commissioners Fei Zhendong, Zhuang Mingli, Wang Jiyuan, and Hong Sisi, Deputy Director of the General Office Chen Manyun, as well as representatives from the All-China Sports Federation and overseas Chinese students in Beijing. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 印尼华侨回国球队抵京

1953-05-19
第3版()
专栏：

    印尼华侨回国球队抵京
    印尼华侨回国球队一行五十人，在领队方定熏、丁子才率领下，于十六日晚到北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府华侨事务委员会副主任委员李铁民；委员费振东、庄明理、王纪元、洪丝丝，办公厅副主任陈曼云，及中华全国体育总会代表和在京华侨学生等。  （新华社）
