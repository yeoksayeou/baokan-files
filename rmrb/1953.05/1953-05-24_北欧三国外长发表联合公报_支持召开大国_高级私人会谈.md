Title: Nordic Foreign Ministers Issue Joint Communiqué Supporting High-Level "Private Talks" Between Major Powers
Author: 
Date: 1953-05-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th TASS dispatch: Reuters, Oslo, reports that the Foreign Ministers of Sweden, Norway, and Denmark issued a joint communiqué in Oslo on the 20th, expressing support for steps to convene high-level "private talks" between major powers. The Foreign Ministers of Denmark, Norway, Sweden, and the Icelandic Minister to Oslo exchanged views on Scandinavian and foreign policies at the regular meeting of the four Foreign Ministers Council. The communiqué stated that the ministers were satisfied with the recent meeting of the United Nations Economic Commission for Europe in Geneva regarding the development of East-West trade. "The ministers agreed to support this effort, which aims to maximize all existing possibilities to achieve closer contact and negotiations between major powers (such as high-level private talks) on other international issues (including the Korean issue)."

### 北欧三国外长发表联合公报  支持召开大国“高级私人会谈”

1953-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    北欧三国外长发表联合公报
    支持召开大国“高级私人会谈”
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：路透社奥斯陆消息，瑞典、挪威和丹麦外交部长二十日在奥斯陆发表联合公报，表示支持召开大国“高级私人会谈”的步骤。丹、挪、瑞三国外长及冰岛驻奥斯陆公使在四国外长理事会例会上就斯堪的纳维亚的政策及外交政策交换了意见。公报说，外长们对最近在日内瓦举行的联合国欧洲经济委员会会议关心发展东西贸易一事表示满意。“外长们同意支持这种努力，它的目的在最大限度地利用现有一切可能性，以在其他国际问题（朝鲜问题在内）上达到大国间更密切的接触与谈判（譬如高级私人会谈）。”
