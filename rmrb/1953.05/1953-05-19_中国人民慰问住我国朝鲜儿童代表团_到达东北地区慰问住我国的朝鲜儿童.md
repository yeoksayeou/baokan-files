Title: Chinese People's Delegation to Comfort Korean Children Residing in China Arrives in Northeast China to Comfort Korean Children Residing in China
Author:
Date: 1953-05-19
Page: 3

    The Chinese People's Delegation to Comfort Korean Children Residing in China, organized by eight national people's organizations, departed from Beijing on the 15th to conduct comfort activities in the Northeast region. These eight organizations are: the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defence of Children, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the China People's Relief Association, the Red Cross Society of China, and the Chinese Medical Association. The delegation is headed by Kang Keqing, with Gu Jinxin, Tan Tiwu, and Liu Zurong as deputy heads.
    The delegation brought many educational, recreational, sports, and medical supplies, as well as candies and other gifts, preparing to present these gifts from the Chinese people to the children living in various parts of Northeast China who have been displaced from their homes due to war on "June 1st" International Children's Day. (Xinhua News Agency)

    The Chinese People's Delegation to Comfort Korean Children Residing in China, a group of nineteen people led by head Kang Keqing, arrived in Shenyang on the afternoon of the 16th from Beijing. Among those who went to the station to welcome them were responsible persons from relevant organizations and people's organizations at the Northeast and Shenyang city levels, Kim Gwi-nam, Director of the Korean Embassy in China's Shenyang Liaison Office, and Zhang Zemin, Director of the Korean Children's Education Office in Northeast China.
    The entire delegation is scheduled to travel to Jilin Province on May 19th, together with representatives from people's organizations in the Northeast and a film projection team, to comfort the Korean children residing in seventeen elementary schools and nurturing homes in Jilin Province. Around "June 1st" International Children's Day, the delegation will be divided into groups to comfort the Korean children residing in Liaodong, Liaoxi, and Songjiang areas. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国人民慰问住我国朝鲜儿童代表团  到达东北地区慰问住我国的朝鲜儿童

1953-05-19
第3版()
专栏：

    中国人民慰问住我国朝鲜儿童代表团
    到达东北地区慰问住我国的朝鲜儿童
    中国人民慰问住我国朝鲜儿童代表团，十五日由北京出发前往东北地区进行慰问。代表团是由八个全国性的人民团体发起组成的。这八个团体是：中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国新民主主义青年团、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中国人民救济总会、中国红十字会总会和中华医学会。代表团由康克清任团长，顾锦心、谭惕吾、刘祖荣任副团长。
    代表团带了很多教育、文娱、体育和医药用品以及糖果等慰问品，准备在“六一”国际儿童节把这些中国人民的礼物送给住在东北各地的因遭受战争灾害而远离家乡的孩子们。    　（新华社）
    中国人民慰问住我国朝鲜儿童代表团一行十九人，在团长康克清率领下于十六日下午由北京到达沈阳。前往车站欢迎的有东北和沈阳市一级有关机关和人民团体的负责人，朝鲜驻华大使馆沈阳连络所所长金贵南，朝鲜驻中国东北朝鲜儿童教育处处长张泽民等。
    代表团全体团员，定于五月十九日会同东北各人民团体代表和电影放映队前往吉林省，慰问住在吉林省的十七个初等学院和爱育院中的朝鲜儿童。“六一”国际儿童节前后，代表团将分组慰问住在辽东、辽西和松江等地的朝鲜儿童。（新华社）
