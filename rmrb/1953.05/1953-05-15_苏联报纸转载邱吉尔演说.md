Title: Soviet Newspapers Reprint Churchill's Speech
Author:
Date: 1953-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 14th: The newspapers *Pravda*, *Izvestia*, and *Red Star* on the 14th reprinted a lengthy article containing British Prime Minister Churchill's speech in the British House of Commons, as broadcast by Reuters. They also published a TASS dispatch concerning the debate on foreign policy in the British House of Commons.

### 苏联报纸转载邱吉尔演说

1953-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联报纸转载邱吉尔演说
    【新华社莫斯科十四日电】十四日真理报、消息报、红星报转载路透社广播的英国首相邱吉尔在英下院演说长文，并登载塔斯社发表的英下院关于对外政策的辩论的消息。
