Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Grand Reception to Fete Delegates to Trade Union Congress and Trade Union Representatives from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1953-05-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government held a grand reception at Huairentang at 7 p.m. on the 10th to fete all the delegates to the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions and the World Federation of Trade Unions delegation invited to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebration and the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, representatives of the Asia-Australia Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions, the Soviet Trade Union Delegation, and trade union delegations and trade union representatives from Vietnam, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, India, Australia, Ceylon, North Korea, Romania, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Burma, Poland, Indonesia, France, Japan, Mongolia, Africa, and other places.

Those attending as hosts included: Gao Gang, Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government; Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council; Rao Shushi, Minister of the Organization Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Bo Yibo and Ma Yinchu, Deputy Directors of the Financial and Economic Commission of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee; He Long, Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission; Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; and heads of various commissions, ministries, committees, academies, bureaus, and banks under the State Council.

At the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech on behalf of the Central People's Government to welcome the guests. General Secretary Saillant of the World Federation of Trade Unions and the head of the Soviet Trade Union Delegation, Goroshkin, also delivered speeches successively.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests raised their glasses to congratulate the success of the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, to congratulate the victory of the great cause of the Chinese working class, and to congratulate the unity and friendship of the world's working class. The hosts and guests frequently toasted Chairman Mao's health, the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, and the health of all the delegates to the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions and the representatives of trade unions from various countries.

The banquet was filled with an atmosphere of close friendship and amity.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions held a workers' art performance in the evening on the 10th, performing music and dance programs to entertain all the delegates to the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, the World Federation of Trade Unions delegation, and trade union representatives from various countries.

Feng Peizhi, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a speech before the performance, extending a warm welcome to the delegates and guests attending the evening, and pointed out: The programs performed at the evening are the programs that the comrades of the Beijing staff and workers love in their daily cultural life, and they reflect that the staff and workers are victoriously advancing step by step along the road of having a high degree of cultural accomplishment.

A total of fifteen programs were performed at the evening, and each program received continuous applause from the delegates and guests. (Attached picture)

### 周恩来总理昨天举行盛大酒会  欢宴工会代表大会代表和各国工会代表

1953-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理昨天举行盛大酒会
    欢宴工会代表大会代表和各国工会代表
    【新华社十日讯】中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理于十日下午七时在怀仁堂举行盛大酒会，欢宴中国工会第七次全国代表大会的全体代表和应邀前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝大会和参加中国工会第七次全国代表大会的世界工会联合会代表团、世界工会联合会亚澳联络局的代表、苏联工会代表团以及越南、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、印度、澳大利亚、锡兰、朝鲜、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、缅甸、波兰、印尼、法国、日本、蒙古、非洲等地的工会代表团和工会代表。
    出席作陪的有：中央人民政府国家计划委员会主席高岗，政务院副总理邓小平，中国共产党中央组织部部长饶漱石，政务院财政经济委员会副主任薄一波、马寅初，文教委员会副主任习仲勋，体育运动委员会主任贺龙，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊以及政务院所属各委、部、会、院、署、行的负责人。
    会上，周恩来总理代表中央人民政府致词欢迎。世界工会联合会总书记赛扬和苏联工会代表团团长戈罗希金也相继致词。
    席间，宾主举杯，祝贺中国工会第七次全国代表大会的成功，祝贺中国工人阶级的伟大事业的胜利，祝贺世界工人阶级的团结和友谊。宾主频频为毛主席的健康干杯，为中苏两国人民的友谊干杯，为参加中国工会第七次全国代表大会的全体代表和各国工会的代表们的健康干杯。
    宴会洋溢着亲密友爱的空气。
    【新华社十日讯】北京市总工会于十日举行工人文艺演出晚会，演出音乐和舞蹈节目，招待出席中国工会第七次全国代表大会的全体代表、世界工联代表团和各国工会代表。
    北京市总工会秘书长冯佩之在演出前致词，他对参加晚会的代表们和来宾们表示热烈欢迎，他指出：晚会演出的节目是北京市职工同志们在日常文艺生活中所喜爱的节目，它们反映了职工群众正在沿着具有高度文化修养的道路一步一步地胜利前进。
    晚会一共演出了十五个节目，每一个节目都得到代表和来宾们不断的掌声。（附图片）
    周恩来总理在酒会上致词。左为世界工联总书记路易·赛扬，右为苏联工会代表团团长戈罗希金。  新华社稿陈勃摄
