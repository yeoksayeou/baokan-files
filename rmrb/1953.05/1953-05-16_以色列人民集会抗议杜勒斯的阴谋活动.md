Title: Israeli People Rally to Protest Dulles' Conspiracy Activities
Author:
Date: 1953-05-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: Tel Aviv dispatch: People from all walks of life in Israel are fiercely opposing the conspiratorial activities of U.S. Secretary of State Dulles in Israel.

Dulles, who is "traveling" in the Middle East, arrived in Tel Aviv, the capital of Israel, on the 13th after two days of activities in Egypt. The Israeli Ben-Gurion government attempted to suppress the Israeli people's angry sentiments against Dulles' activities. It had preemptively ordered a ban on demonstrations and gatherings and taken "unprecedented security measures," dispatching thousands of police and police dogs, cavalry, armored vehicles, and tanks to all key points in Tel Aviv. However, the people of Tel Aviv, disregarding the government's ban, held a mass rally in Allenby Square on the 13th under the auspices of the Israeli Peace Council, strongly protesting Dulles' activities in Israel. Speakers at the meeting unanimously pointed out that Dulles is attempting to make the Israeli people fight for others.

### 以色列人民集会抗议杜勒斯的阴谋活动

1953-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    以色列人民集会抗议杜勒斯的阴谋活动
    【新华社十五日讯】特拉维夫消息：以色列各阶层人民激烈反对美国国务卿杜勒斯到以色列进行阴谋活动。
    正在中东“旅行”的杜勒斯，在埃及活动两天后于十三日到达以色列首都特拉维夫。以色列本·古里安政府企图镇压以色列人民反对杜勒斯活动的愤怒情绪，曾预先下令禁止举行示威集会，并采取了“空前的安全措施”，派遣成千的警察和警犬、马队、装甲车、坦克车等布满特拉维夫全城各要点。但是，特拉维夫人民不顾政府的禁令，在以色列和平理事会主持下，于十三日在亚冷比广场举行了群众集会，强烈抗议杜勒斯到以色列活动。在会上发言的人们一致指出，杜勒斯正企图使以色列人民为别人作战。
