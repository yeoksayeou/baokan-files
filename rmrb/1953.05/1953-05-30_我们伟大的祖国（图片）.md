Author:
Date: 1953-05-30
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This is an experimental construction site where the Southwest Construction Bureau is promoting advanced experience. Here, a new-style weaving factory with complete equipment is being built. Workers are using parallel assembly line methods to cover the roof. The workers in front are binding steel bars, and the workers behind are laying concrete.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是西南建筑管理局推广先进经验的一个实验工地。在这里，一座设备完善的新式织布厂正在建造起来。工人们采用平行流水作业法铺盖屋面，前面的工人扎钢筋，后面的工人铺混凝土。
