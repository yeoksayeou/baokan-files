Title: Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee Hosts Banquet for Volunteer Army May Day Observation Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-05-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a reception on the 15th to welcome the representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army "May Day" homecoming observation delegation. The banquet was presided over by Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee. Those present were Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, Standing Committee members and members Jiang Guangnai, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Naiqi, Ma Xulun, Zhang Xiruo, Liang Xi, Shao Lizi, Lin Boqu, Xu Guangping, Huang Qixiang, Xu Deheng, Shi Liang, Mei Gongbin, Zhou Xinmin, Yi Lirong, and Zhu Zaoguan, as well as Deputy Secretary-General Xing Xiping and others.

At the meeting, Li Jishen, on behalf of the CPPCC National Committee, Lin Boqu, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Zhang Bojun, on behalf of the headquarters of the China Democratic League, delivered speeches respectively, extending their respects to the representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army "May Day" homecoming observation delegation and to all the Chinese People's Volunteer Army comrades who are bravely fighting on the Korean front. Yuan Shengping, head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army "May Day" homecoming observation delegation, then delivered a speech, thanking the people of the whole country for their enormous support to the Volunteer Army.

### 中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会  欢宴志愿军五一节观礼代表团

1953-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
    欢宴志愿军五一节观礼代表团
    【新华社十五日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在十五日举行酒会，欢宴中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团的代表。宴会由中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李济深主持。出席作陪的有中国人民政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通，常务委员和委员蒋光鼐、章伯钧、章乃器、马叙伦、张奚若、梁希、邵力子、林伯渠、许广平、黄琪翔、许德珩、史良、梅龚彬、周新民、易礼容、朱早观，副秘书长邢西萍等。
    会上，李济深代表中国人民政协全国委员会，林伯渠代表中国共产党中央委员会，章伯钧代表中国民主同盟总部分别致词，向到会的中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团代表以及在朝鲜前线英勇善战的中国人民志愿军全体同志致敬。中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团团长袁升平接着致词，感谢全国人民对志愿军的巨大支援。
