Title: Beijing Held Celebration Meeting for the Eighth Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia; Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Konsal, Held a Reception
Author:
Date: 1953-05-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: Beijing held a celebration meeting at the Beijing Youth Palace yesterday, organized by the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia. Among those attending the celebration meeting were Ma Xulun, Minister of Higher Education of the Central People's Government, Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education, Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of Culture, Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau. Wu Yuzhang, President of the Renmin University of China, and representatives from various organizations, schools, and people's groups in Beijing, totaling over a thousand people, also attended the celebration meeting. Invited to attend the celebration meeting were Konsal, the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, the delegation of Czechoslovak Republic film artists currently in Beijing, the delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic government for commercial affairs, and the delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic attending the first meeting of the Sino-Czech Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation. Diplomatic envoys from various People's Democratic countries to China were also invited to attend the celebration meeting.

    The celebration meeting opened with the national anthems of Czechoslovakia, China, and the Soviet Union. Minister Ma Xulun delivered a speech first, expressing warm congratulations on the achievements made by the Czechoslovak people in various fields over the past eight years since liberation. Ambassador Konsal then delivered a speech, recounting the great achievements made by the Czechoslovak people under the leadership of the Communist Party and with the assistance of the Soviet Union over the past eight years, and pointed out that the strengthening of friendship between the Chinese and Czech people is a major contribution to the cause of world peace and progress.

    Wei Que, the head of the Chinese cultural and educational delegation that recently returned from a visit to the Czechoslovak Republic and other People's Democratic countries in Eastern Europe, also spoke at the meeting. He spoke about the profound impressions the delegation gained during its visit to the Czechoslovak Republic, and focused on the achievements of Czechoslovakia in education and culture. He also used vivid examples to illustrate the deep friendship shown by the Czechoslovak people to the delegation and the Chinese people.

    After the meeting, the Central Song and Dance Troupe, the China Peking Opera Troupe, and others performed artistic programs.
Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: May 9th is the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia by the Soviet army. Konsal, Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, held a reception at the embassy on the afternoon of the 9th. Invited to attend were: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, Zhang Zhizhiang, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court, Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, heads of various people's organizations, combat heroes, model workers, and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and others, totaling more than four hundred people. Diplomatic envoys and diplomats from various countries stationed in China were also invited to the meeting.

    Ambassador Konsal and Vice Chairman Zhu De delivered speeches at the meeting successively, mutually wishing that the friendship between the Chinese and Czech people would develop day by day, that the people of both countries would achieve greater victories in their new lives of construction, and that the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union would become increasingly strong.

    The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests frequently toasted to the friendship between the Chinese and Czech people and to the health of the leaders of the two countries.

    On the same day, the Czechoslovak Embassy in China also held a film reception in Beijing.

    In addition, Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government Administration Council, sent congratulatory telegrams to Antonín Zápotocký, President of Czechoslovakia, and Viliam Široký, Prime Minister, respectively.

### 北京昨日举行捷解放八周年庆祝会  捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使康萨拉特举行招待会

1953-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    北京昨日举行捷解放八周年庆祝会
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使康萨拉特举行招待会
   【新华社九日讯】为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放八周年，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局，九日在北京青年宫举行庆祝会。参加庆祝会的有中央人民政府高等教育部部长马叙伦，教育部副部长韦悫，文化部副部长丁西林，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，对外文化联络事务局局长洪深。参加庆祝会的还有中国人民大学校长吴玉章和北京各机关、学校和人民团体的代表共一千余人。应邀参加庆祝会的有捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使康萨拉和正在北京的捷克斯洛伐克共和国电影艺术工作者代表团、捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府商务代表团及捷克斯洛伐克共和国参加中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会第一届会议代表团。各人民民主国家驻中国外交使节也应邀参加了庆祝会。
    庆祝会在捷、中、苏三国国歌声中开幕后，由马叙伦部长首先致辞。他对于捷克斯洛伐克人民解放八年来在各方面所获得的成就表示了热烈的祝贺。康萨拉大使接着致辞，他叙述了捷克斯洛伐克人民八年来在共产党的领导和苏联的援助下所取得的巨大成就，指出中捷两国人民友谊的加强是对于世界和平进步事业的一大贡献。
    最近刚从捷克斯洛伐克共和国和东欧其他人民民主国家访问归来的我国文化教育考察团团长韦悫，也在会上讲话。他谈到考察团在访问捷克斯洛伐克共和国期间所获得的深刻印象，并着重叙述了捷克斯洛伐克在教育和文化方面的成就。他还以生动的事例来说明捷克斯洛伐克人民对考察团和中国人民所表现的深厚友谊。
    会后，由中央歌舞团、中国京剧团等演出文艺节目。
   【新华社九日讯】五月九日是苏联军队解放捷克斯洛伐克八周年纪念日。捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使康萨拉特于九日下午在大使馆举行招待会。 应邀出席的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德，最高人民法院副院长张志让，人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，外交部副部长伍修权及中央人民政府各部门首长、各人民团体负责人、战斗英雄、劳动模范和北京市副市长吴晗等四百多人。各国驻华使节和外交人员也应邀到会。
    康萨拉大使和朱德副主席相继在会上致词，互祝中捷两国人民的友谊日益发展，两国人民在建设新生活中取得更大的胜利，以苏联为首的和平民主阵营日益强大。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。宾主频频为中捷两国人民的友谊和两国人民的领袖的健康而干杯。
    同日，捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆还在北京举行了电影招待会。
    又讯，我中央人民政府主席毛泽东和政务院总理周恩来曾分别致电捷克斯洛伐克总统安·萨波托斯基和总理威·西罗基，表示祝贺。
