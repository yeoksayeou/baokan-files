Title: Mongolian Trade Representative Bator Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-05-04
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st—Mongolian People's Republic Trade Representative, Deputy Director of the Mongolian Cooperative General Society Bator, and Mongolian Foreign Trade Wholesale Department Director Qiaokesumu arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 1st.
    Those who went to greet them were: Bai Xiangyin, Deputy Director of the First Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Zheng Tuobin, Deputy Chief of the Export Section of the First Bureau, and Zhou Liecheng, Chief of the Liaison Section.
    Also present to greet them were: Mongolian People's Republic Ambassador to China Jalkhasaikhan, First Secretary Nima, and Second Secretary Ailingqingsuonuomu, and others.

### 蒙古贸易代表巴托尔等到京

1953-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古贸易代表巴托尔等到京
    【新华社一日讯】蒙古人民共和国贸易代表、蒙古合作总社副社长巴托尔和蒙古对外贸易批发处处长乔克苏木两人在一日晨乘火车抵京。
    前往迎接的有：中央人民政府对外贸易部第一局副局长白向银、第一局出口科副科长郑拓彬和交际处科长周烈承。
    前往迎接的还有：蒙古人民共和国驻华大使贾尔卡赛汗、一等秘书尼玛、二等秘书艾林清苏诺木等人。
