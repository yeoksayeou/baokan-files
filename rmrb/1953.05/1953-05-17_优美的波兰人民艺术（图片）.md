Beautiful Polish People's Art
Welcome the Mazowsze Polish Folk Song and Dance Ensemble to Perform in Our Country

Starting from May 3rd, the Mazowsze Polish Folk Song and Dance Ensemble performed in Beijing for workers, the People's Liberation Army, government cadres, young students, farmers, and city residents. Every program was warmly welcomed by the audience. (Photo by Zhang Li, our newspaper)

Many folk song and dance programs performed by the Mazowsze Polish Folk Song and Dance Ensemble powerfully expressed the happy life of the Polish working people. This is a scene from the folk dance "Polka" performed by the troupe. (Photo by Zhang Li, our newspaper)

The Mazowsze Polish Folk Song and Dance Ensemble performs the "Żuraw" dance. (Photo by Zhang Li, our newspaper)

The Mazowsze Polish Folk Song and Dance Ensemble performs the "Olien Die Er" dance. (Photo by Zhang Li, our newspaper)

Members of the Children's Troupe of the China Youth Art Theatre and members of the Mazowsze Polish Folk Song and Dance Ensemble observe, study, and learn from each other's performances. (Photo by Sheng Jirun, Xinhua News Agency)

The Mazowsze Polish Folk Song and Dance Ensemble sang many beautiful folk songs. This is the scene when they sang the folk song "Valley." (Photo by Zhang Li, our newspaper)

On May 4th, Beijing youth representatives presented gifts to the Mazowsze Polish Folk Song and Dance Ensemble to thank them for their outstanding performances. (Photo by Zhang Li, our newspaper)

The Mazowsze Polish Folk Song and Dance Ensemble, invited by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government to visit and perform in our country, arrived in Beijing on April 28th, led by the head of the troupe, Andrzej Panufnik. This is a scene of Vice Minister of Culture Zhou Yang giving a welcoming speech at the Beijing Railway Station welcoming ceremony. (Photo by Zhu Bo, our newspaper)

On April 28th, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, held a banquet to welcome all members of the Mazowsze Polish Folk Song and Dance Ensemble. This is a scene of Minister Shen Yanbing talking with Andrzej Panufnik, head of the Mazowsze Polish Folk Song and Dance Ensemble, and others. (Photo by Sheng Jirun, Xinhua News Agency)

This is a scene from May 3rd when the Mazowsze Polish Folk Song and Dance Ensemble performed for over 40,000 workers at the Beijing Xian Nong Tan. (Photo by Qi Guanshan, Xinhua News Agency)

### 优美的波兰人民艺术（图片）

1953-05-17
第5版()
专栏：

    优美的波兰人民艺术
    欢迎波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团在我国演出
    五月三日起，波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团在北京曾分别为工人、人民解放军、机关干部、青年学生、农民和市民等演出，每个节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎。  （本报张力摄）
    波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团演出的许多富有民族色彩的歌舞节目，有力地表现了波兰劳动人民的幸福生活。这是歌舞团演出民间舞蹈“波尔卡舞”时的一个场面。（本报张力摄）
    波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团演出“茹拉夫舞”。（本报张力摄）
    波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团演出“奥连捷尔舞”。（本报张力摄）
    中国青年艺术剧院所属少年儿童剧团团员和波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团团员互相观摩、研究和学习双方演出的节目。（新华社记者盛继润摄）
    波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团演唱了许多优美的民歌，这是演唱民歌“山谷”时的情形。    　（本报张力摄）
    五月四日，北京市青年代表向波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团献礼，感谢他们卓越的演出。    （本报张力摄）
    应中央人民政府文化部邀请前来我国访问和演出的波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团，由团长安德烈·潘努夫尼克率领于四月二十八日到达北京。这是文化部副部长周扬在北京车站欢迎大会上致欢迎词的情形。
　（本报记者朱波摄）
    四月二十八日，中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰举行宴会，欢迎波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团全体人员。这是沈雁冰部长和波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团团长潘努夫尼克等谈话的情形。    （新华社记者盛继润摄）
    五月三日波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团在北京先农坛为四万多工人演出时的一个场面。（新华社记者齐观山摄）
