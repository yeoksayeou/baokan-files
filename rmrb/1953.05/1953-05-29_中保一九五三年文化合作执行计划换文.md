Title: Exchange of Notes on the 1953 Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1953-05-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The 1953 Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan, formulated based on the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, was agreed upon after negotiations between the two parties. On May 22nd, it was exchanged through notes by Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, and Tsa Erfu Lanov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, representing the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, respectively.

### 中保一九五三年文化合作执行计划换文

1953-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中保一九五三年文化合作执行计划换文
    【新华社二十八日讯】根据中保文化合作协定制订的中保一九五三年文化合作执行计划，经双方商谈协议后，已在五月二十二日，由我中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权和保加利亚人民共和国驻我国临时代办察尔伏拉诺夫，分别代表中华人民共和国政府和保加利亚人民共和国政府换文。
