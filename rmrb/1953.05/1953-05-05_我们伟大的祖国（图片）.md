Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-05-05
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    On the 135th anniversary of Marx's birth, the Chinese translation of Marx's great work "Das Kapital" is beginning to be available to readers in major cities across the country. These are the young female workers binding "Das Kapital." They are working hard and meticulously on this glorious task.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在马克思诞辰一百三十五周年的今天，马克思的伟大著作“资本论”的中译本，开始在全国各大城市和广大读者见面。这是装订“资本论”的青年女工。她们正在紧张而细致地进行着这个光荣的工作。
