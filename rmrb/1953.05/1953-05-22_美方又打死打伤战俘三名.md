Title: U.S. Side Again Kills and Injures Three POWs
Author:
Date: 1953-05-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, May 21st. The U.S. side has again killed and injured three of our captured personnel. According to the U.S. side, on May 16th, in the Koje Island POW camp, a prisoner was shot and killed by a U.S. guard without any reason. On May 17th, in the Pongam Island POW camp, two prisoners were shot and injured by U.S. guards, one of whom was seriously injured. According to the U.S. side, these two prisoners were so-called "detained civilians." As everyone knows, horrific massacres of prisoners have occurred continuously on Pongam Island. For example, on December 14th of last year, the U.S. side killed and injured 202 prisoners on Pongam Island. On March 24th of this year, the U.S. side attacked the prisoners on Pongam Island with chemical weapons, causing 80 percent of the prisoners to be injured.

### 美方又打死打伤战俘三名

1953-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    美方又打死打伤战俘三名
    【新华社开城二十一日电】美方又打死打伤我方被俘人员三名。据美方宣布，五月十六日，在巨济岛战俘营中有一名战俘被美方卫兵毫无理由地开枪打死。五月十七日，在蜂岩岛战俘营中，有两名战俘被美方卫兵打伤，其中一名伤势严重。据美方声称，这两名战俘是所谓“被拘平民”。大家知道，在蜂岩岛上，曾经接连不断地发生过骇人听闻的屠杀战俘的事件。例如，在去年十二月十四日，美方在蜂岩岛上打死打伤战俘二百零二人。在今年三月二十四日，美方用化学武器进攻蜂岩岛上的战俘，以致百分之八十的战俘都受到伤害。
