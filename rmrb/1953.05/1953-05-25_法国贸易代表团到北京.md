Title: French Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-05-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd:

    The French trade delegation, led by Mr. Depra, Chairman of the French International Trade Promotion Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 23rd. Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Ji Chao-ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Xie Xiaonai, Director of the Liaison Department; Lu Xuzhang, Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation; Cao Zhongshu, Deputy Manager; Zhan Wu, Deputy General Manager of the Bank of China, and others.
    The list of the delegation is as follows:
    Head of Delegation: Depra (Chairman of the French International Trade Promotion Committee)
    Deputy Head of Delegation: Dument (Vice-Chairman of the French International Trade Promotion Committee)
    Members:
    	Ilson (Manager of Banque Commerciale pour l'Europe du Nord)
    	Rossier (Representative of Etablissements Yong Hing)
    	Aubeau (Representative of Saint-Gobain)
    	Bouvier (Representative of Berthiez Machine Tool Factory)
    	Lachaux (Representative of the oilseed and oil industry)
    	Margoulan (Manager of Europe Import Export Company)
    	Picard (Representative of Société Générale de Surveillance)
    	Demolaine (Representative of Schuif Machine Tool Factory)
    	Demondant (Representative of Société Métallurgique de Normandie)
    	Fourcade (Representative of Motte Grall Import Export Company)
    	Bourquin (Representative of the hand tool manufacturing industry)
    	Moreau (Not yet arrived)
    	Félix (Not yet arrived)
    Another member, Froloff (Manager of Paris Industrial Electric Motor Factory), also arrived in Beijing by train on the same day.

### 法国贸易代表团到北京

1953-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    法国贸易代表团到北京
    【新华社二十三日讯】法国贸易代表团在法国国际贸易促进委员会主席德普拉率领下，在二十三日飞抵北京。到机场欢迎的有我国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎、联络部部长谢筱乃，中国进出口公司经理卢绪章、副经理曹中枢，中国银行副总经理詹武等人。
    代表团名单如下：
    团长：德普拉（法国国际贸易促进委员会主席）
    副团长：杜孟（法国国际贸易促进委员会副主席）
    团员：伊尔逊（北欧商业银行经理）
    罗西埃（永兴洋行代表）
    奥布拉（圣哥斑公司代表）
    布维埃（贝尔蒂兹工作母机工厂代表）
    拉萧（油籽油料业代表）
    马尔古朗（欧洲进出口公司经理）
    皮卡（索瑞司商品检验机构代表）
    德摩兰（舒尔富工作母机工厂代表）
    德蒙丹当（诺曼底冶金公司代表）
    弗尔卡德（莫特格拉进出口公司代表）
    布尔坎（手工具制造业代表）
    摩兰（尚未到达）
    费利克斯（尚未到达）
    另一团员佛罗浩夫（巴黎工业电机厂经理）也在同日乘火车到达北京。
