Title: U.S. Reactionary Rulers Eager to Execute the Rosenbergs, Supreme Court Rejects Request to Delay Execution
Author:
Date: 1953-05-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, May 27th: After the U.S. Supreme Court rejected the Rosenbergs' appeal for the third time on the 25th and rescinded the decision to delay the execution of the sentence during the appeal, the Rosenbergs' defense lawyers, Block and Finerty, submitted an application to Chief Justice Vincent of the U.S. Supreme Court on the 26th, requesting a stay of execution so that the Rosenbergs could file another appeal. The application cited irrefutable facts proving that the U.S. judicial authorities fabricated the so-called "crimes" of the Rosenbergs throughout the entire case.

However, the rulers of the United States are eager to execute the Rosenbergs. After receiving the application on the 26th, Chief Justice Vincent of the U.S. Supreme Court simply wrote "rejected" on the application the same day.

News from U.S. news agencies indicates that the U.S. government is currently directing New York Federal Judge Kaufman to expedite the execution of the death sentence against the Rosenbergs.

### 美国反动统治者急于杀害罗森堡夫妇  最高法院竟拒绝对罗森堡夫妇延缓执行判决的要求

1953-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    美国反动统治者急于杀害罗森堡夫妇
    最高法院竟拒绝对罗森堡夫妇延缓执行判决的要求
    【新华社二十七日讯】华盛顿消息：在美国最高法院二十五日第三次拒绝罗森堡夫妇的上诉并撤消在上诉期间延缓执行判决的决定后，罗森堡夫妇的辩护律师布洛克与芬奈蒂二十六日向美国最高法院首席法官文生提出申请书，要求缓期执行死刑，以便罗森堡夫妇能再度提出上诉。申请书举出了无可辩驳的事实，证明美国司法当局在罗森堡夫妇整个案件的处理过程中一手捏造罗森堡夫妇的所谓“罪状”。
    但是美国的统治者急于杀害罗森堡夫妇。美国最高法院首席法官文生在二十六日收到申请书后，当天便在申请书上简单地批了“驳回”两个字。
    美国通讯社的消息表明：美国政府目前正指使纽约联邦法官考夫曼从速执行对罗森堡夫妇的死刑判决。
