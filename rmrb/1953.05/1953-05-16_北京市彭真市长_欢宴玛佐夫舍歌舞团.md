Title: Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen Fetes the Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1953-05-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen of the Beijing Municipal People's Government hosted a banquet on the afternoon of the 14th for all members of the Polish People's Republic Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble. Kirilo, Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, was also invited to attend.

Those present at the reception included Zhang Xiruo, Fan Changjiang, Wu Han, and well-known figures in the literary and art circles such as Tian Han, Hong Shen, Cao Yu, Wang Chaowen, Ma Yanxiang, and Xu Beihong.

At the beginning of the banquet, Mayor Peng Zhen delivered a speech, followed by speeches by Pannufnik, head of the song and dance troupe, Donbalufsky, deputy head of the troupe, and Shajefska, secretary, who unanimously spoke of the unforgettable impression given to them by the friendly reception they received during their performances in Beijing.

During the banquet, Zhang Xiruo, chairman of the Beijing branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association, Fan Changjiang, deputy secretary-general of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, Cao Yu, president of the Beijing People's Art Theater, female writer Feng Zi, and veteran artist Hao Shouchen successively gave speeches. The hosts and guests frequently toasted the friendly cooperation between China and Poland and the health of the leaders of the two countries.

### 北京市彭真市长  欢宴玛佐夫舍歌舞团

1953-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市彭真市长
    欢宴玛佐夫舍歌舞团
    【新华社十四日讯】北京市人民政府彭真市长十四日下午欢宴波兰人民共和国玛佐夫舍歌舞团的全体人员。波兰人民共和国驻我国大使基里洛克也应邀参加。
    出席招待会的有张奚若、范长江、吴晗和文学艺术界著名人士田汉、洪深、曹禺、王朝闻、马彦祥、徐悲鸿等。
    宴会开始，彭真市长讲话后，接着歌舞团团长潘努夫尼克、副团长顿巴鲁夫斯基、秘书长沙杰夫斯卡先后讲话，他们一致谈到这次在北京演出期间受到的友谊的招待所给予他们的难忘的印象。
    宴会进行中，北京市抗美援朝分会主席张奚若、政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长范长江、北京市人民艺术剧院院长曹禺、女作家凤子、老艺人郝寿臣等相继致词。宾主频频为中波两国的友好合作，为两国人民的领袖的健康而干杯。
