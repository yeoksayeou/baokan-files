Title: Indian Trade Union Representative Gan Da Rajkar Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-05-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 8th: Gan Da Rajkar, the Indian trade union representative and chairman of the Northern Bombay Secondary School Teachers Association of India, who was invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day and the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 8th due to travel delays. Those who welcomed him at the station included: Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Educational Workers' Union, Qiu Jin, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union, and Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 印度工会代表甘·达·拉杰卡尔到达北京

1953-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    印度工会代表甘·达·拉杰卡尔到达北京
    【新华社八日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请，前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节和中国工会第七次全国代表大会的印度工会代表、印度孟买北部中学教师协会主席甘·达·拉杰卡尔因旅途迟滞，在八日晚间乘火车抵达北京。到车站欢迎的有：中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明，中国海员工会筹备委员会副主任丘金和中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明等人。
