Title: People of the German Democratic Republic Widely Commemorate Marx's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1953-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 6th: According to a report from Deutsches Nachrichten-Agentur (DPA) Berlin on the 4th: To commemorate the 135th anniversary of the birth of Karl Marx, the great thinker of the German nation and the founder of scientific socialism, the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic has decided to establish the Karl Marx Prize. This prize will reward any individual, collective, institution, factory, or organization that has made outstanding contributions to the planned socialist construction of Germany in the political, economic, and cultural fields, as well as in the struggle for the peaceful reunification of Germany. The Council of Ministers has also decided to establish the Karl Marx Scholarship as the highest award for students who excel in the study of Marxism-Leninism. The Council of Ministers has further decided that the city of Chemnitz will be renamed Karl-Marx-Stadt, the Chemnitz District will be renamed Karl-Marx-District, and the University of Leipzig will be renamed Karl Marx University.

On May 2nd, the Karl Marx exhibition, sponsored by the German Historical Museum, opened in Berlin.

Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, heads of various departments of the Republic's government, members of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party, representatives of various parties and people's organizations, National Prize winners, and other prominent figures attended the opening ceremony. Representatives of the Soviet Control Commission in Germany and members of the Soviet diplomatic mission in Germany also attended the ceremony. The exhibition will be held until the end of 1953—the year designated by the government of the German Democratic Republic as the Karl Marx Year in commemoration of Marx.

Xinhua News Agency, May 6th: TASS Berlin, May 5th: The working people of the German Democratic Republic widely commemorate the 135th anniversary of the birth of Karl Marx. Newspapers in the democratic sector of Berlin have published many articles about Marx.

"Neues Deutschland" published the specific regulations of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany on forever commemorating Marx under the title "Commemorating the Great Son of the German People." Various newspapers also published an article by Premier Grotewohl entitled "Marx, Engels, and the Struggle for the Establishment of a Unified and Democratic Germany," an article by Hermann Matern, Vice-Chairman of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, entitled "Marx and Engels' Struggle to Establish a Revolutionary Workers' Party in Germany," and other articles and documents about Marx's life and career.

### 德意志民主共和国人民广泛纪念马克思诞辰

1953-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国人民广泛纪念马克思诞辰
    【新华社六日讯】据德意志通讯社柏林四日讯：为了纪念德国民族伟大的思想家、科学的社会主义的创始人卡尔·马克思诞生一百三十五周年，德意志民主共和国部长会议决定设置卡尔·马克思奖金，奖励一切对于在德国有计划的社会主义建设中政治、经济、文化各方面以及在争取德国的和平统一的斗争方面有卓越贡献的个人、集体、机关、工厂或组织；部长会议并决定设置卡尔·马克思奖学金，作为对于学习马克思列宁主义成绩优秀的学生的最高奖励；部长会议还决定：开姆尼斯城命名为卡尔·马克思城，开姆尼斯区命名为卡尔·马克思区，来比锡大学命名为卡尔·马克思大学。
    五月二日，德国历史馆主办的介绍卡尔·马克思的展览会在柏林开幕。
    参加开幕典礼的有德意志民主共和国政府总理、德国统一社会党中央委员会主席格罗提渥，共和国政府各部门的首长和统一社会党中央委员会的委员们，各党派与人民团体的代表们、国家奖金获得者和其他方面的著名人士。参加典礼的还有苏联对德管制委员会的代表们和苏联驻德外交使团的团员们。展览会将举行到一九五三年——德意志民主共和国政府为纪念马克思，定今年为卡尔·马克思年——年底为止。
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社柏林五日讯：德意志民主共和国的劳动人民广泛地纪念卡尔·马克思诞生一百三十五周年。柏林民主区的报纸刊载了许多关于马克思的文章。
    “新德意志报”以“纪念德国人民伟大的儿子”为题，刊载了德国统一社会党中央委员会关于永远纪念马克思的具体办法的规定。各报并刊载了格罗提渥总理所写的题为“马克思、恩格斯与争取建立统一、民主的德国的斗争”的文章，德意志民主共和国人民议院副主席赫尔曼·马特恩所写的“马克思与恩格斯争取在德国建立革命的工人党的斗争”的文章以及其他关于马克思的生平与事业的文章与文件。
