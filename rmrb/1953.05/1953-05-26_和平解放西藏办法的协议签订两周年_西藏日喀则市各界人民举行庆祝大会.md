Title: Second Anniversary of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet: People from All Walks of Life in Shigatse, Tibet, Hold Celebration Rally
Author:
Date: 1953-05-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 25th: People from all walks of life in Shigatse City, Tibet, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army units, joyously celebrated the second anniversary of the signing of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet on the 23rd. On that day, Shigatse City was filled with a festive atmosphere. In the early morning, five-star red flags fluttered in the city, and the lamas of Tashilhunpo Monastery chanted longevity scriptures. At noon, monks and lay officials, men and women, lamas, and farmers from the suburbs, all changed into summer clothes and happily rushed to participate in the celebration rally. The rally was chaired by Jimee, the Zasa of the Panchen Kanpo Lija, who reported on the achievements made in various aspects since the liberation of Tibet two years ago. Then, Cheoka, the secretary of the Panchen Erdeni, read the Panchen Erdeni's congratulatory message to the assembly. The congratulatory message said: "May 23rd is the second anniversary of the signing of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet, a joyous day for the Tibetan people and monks and lay people. The facts of the past two years have proven that the great Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party's ethnic policies have enabled the Tibetan people to enjoy the warmth and happiness of the motherland's big family." Responsible lamas from the Shigatse Jigzong and Tashilhunpo Monastery spoke successively. After describing the benefits that the Tibetan monks and lay people have received since the signing of the agreement, they unanimously expressed that in the future, under the leadership of Chairman Mao, the Chinese Communist Party, and the Central People's Government, and under the united leadership of the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni, they will faithfully implement the agreement and further strive to build a new Tibet and consolidate national defense. The person in charge of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shigatse also delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting.

### 和平解放西藏办法的协议签订两周年  西藏日喀则市各界人民举行庆祝大会

1953-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    和平解放西藏办法的协议签订两周年
    西藏日喀则市各界人民举行庆祝大会
    【新华社拉萨二十五日电】日喀则市各界人民和中国人民解放军部队，在二十三日欢庆和平解放西藏办法的协议签订两周年纪念日。当天，日喀则市一片节日气象。清晨，市内飘扬着五星国旗，扎什伦布寺的喇嘛们念起长寿经。中午，换了夏装的僧俗官员、男女市民、喇嘛和市郊的农民都欢欢喜喜赶来参加庆祝大会。大会由班禅堪布会议厅扎萨计晋美任主席，他报告了西藏解放两年来在各方面所得到的成就。接着，班禅额尔德尼的秘书却卡向大会宣读了班禅额尔德尼的贺词。贺词中说：“五月二十三日是和平解放西藏办法的协议签订两周年纪念日，是西藏民族和僧俗人民的大喜日，两年来的事实已经证明：伟大的毛主席和中国共产党的民族政策，已经使西藏民族享受到了祖国大家庭的温暖和幸福。”日喀则机宗和扎什伦布寺的负责喇嘛相继讲话。他们叙述了协议签订后西藏僧俗人民得到的好处以后，一致表示，今后要坚决在毛主席、中国共产党、中央人民政府领导下，在达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼团结率领下，忠实地执行协议，进一步为建设新西藏，巩固祖国国防而努力。驻日喀则的中国人民解放军负责人也在会上致贺词。
