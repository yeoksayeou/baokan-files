Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru Presents an Elephant to Chinese Children; Presentation Ceremony Held Yesterday at Beijing Western Suburban Park
Author:
Date: 1953-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 30th: An elephant presented by Indian Prime Minister Nehru to the children of China arrived in Beijing on May 27th. The presentation ceremony was held at the Beijing Western Suburban Park on the afternoon of the 30th. Indian Ambassador to China, N. Raghavan, made the presentation on behalf of Prime Minister Nehru, and Vice Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government and President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, accepted the gift on behalf of the Chinese children. Those attending the ceremony included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Zhang Hanfu; Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yan Baohang; Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, Hong Shen; Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Jiakang; Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Zhuoru; Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Chen Hansheng; Director of the Children's Welfare Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Kang Keqing; Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Ma Zhenwu; Deputy Director of the Beijing Parks Administration Committee, Fan Dongshen; and more than twenty children.
    Staff members of the Indian Embassy in China also attended the ceremony.
    At the presentation ceremony, Ambassador N. Raghavan delivered a speech, and Vice Minister Ding Xilin delivered a speech of thanks on behalf of the Chinese children. After the ceremony, the elephant performed various acts in its newly built elephant house.
    The elephant, named "Asa," will be open to the public at the Western Suburban Park starting May 31st.

### 印度总理尼赫鲁赠给我国儿童一只大象  受礼仪式昨日在北京西郊公园举行

1953-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁赠给我国儿童一只大象
    受礼仪式昨日在北京西郊公园举行
    【新华社三十日讯】印度总理尼赫鲁赠送给我国儿童的一只大象已于五月二十七日运抵北京，受礼仪式于三十日下午在北京西郊公园举行。由印度驻华大使赖嘉文代表赠礼，中央人民政府文化部副部长、中印友好协会会长丁西林代表接受。参加受礼仪式的有中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，外交部亚洲司司长陈家康，外交部交际处处长王倬如，中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙，中华全国民主妇女联合会儿童福利部部长康克清，北京市人民政府外事处处长马振武，北京市公园管理委员会副主任范栋申以及二十多位少年儿童。
    印度驻华大使馆馆员也参加了受礼仪式。
    在受礼仪式上，首先由赖嘉文大使致词，并由丁西林副部长代表中国儿童致谢词。仪式结束后，这只大象在新盖的象房中进行了各种表演。
    这只大象名叫“阿萨”，将从五月三十一日起在西郊公园公开展览。
