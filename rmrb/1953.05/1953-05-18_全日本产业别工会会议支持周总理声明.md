Title: All-Japan Federation of Industrial Unions Supports Premier Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1953-05-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. According to the Japanese newspaper "Akahata": In order to support Premier Zhou Enlai's statement on the Korean armistice negotiations, the All-Japan Federation of Industrial Unions (abbreviated as "Sanbetsu") issued an appeal to the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan on April 11, calling for a joint struggle to comprehensively transform Japan's munitions industry into a peaceful industry.

    "Sanbetsu" also put forward the following struggle program: (1) Oppose the munitions industry and demand a comprehensive transformation into a peaceful industry; (2) Establish normal trade relations between Japan and other countries; (3) Establish a reasonable minimum wage system; (4) Ensure the freedom of trade union activities and the freedom of political activities; (5) Oppose unemployment and completely establish a social insurance system; (6) Unite the labor front, establish a worker-peasant alliance, and unite the peace-loving people.

### 全日本产业别工会会议支持周总理声明

1953-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

    全日本产业别工会会议支持周总理声明
    【新华社十五日讯】据日本“赤旗报”消息：全日本产业别工会会议（简称“产别”）为了支持周恩来总理关于朝鲜停战谈判的声明，在四月十一日向日本工会总评议会发出号召，要求进行联合斗争，把日本的军需工业全面地转变成和平工业。
    “产别”同时提出了下列的斗争纲领：（一）反对军需工业，要求全面地转变为和平工业；（二）确立日本和各国的正常的通商关系；（三）确定合理的最低工资制；（四）确保工会活动的自由和政治活动的自由；（五）反对失业，完全地建立社会保险制度；（六）统一劳工战线，建立工农联盟，团结和平人民。
