Title: Invading American Warship Struck by Our Army
Author:
Date: 1953-05-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: Washington dispatch: The US Navy authorities admitted on the 18th that the American destroyer "USS Brush" was hit by artillery fire from the Korean-Chinese naval defense forces near Wonsan on the east coast of Korea on the 15th. Nine of the eleven US ship gunners were injured, six of them seriously.

### 侵朝美舰一艘被我军击中

1953-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    侵朝美舰一艘被我军击中
    【新华社二十日讯】华盛顿消息：美国海军当局十八日承认，美国驱逐舰“布拉希号”十五日在朝鲜东海岸元山附近，被朝中海防部队的大炮炮火击中，美舰炮手十一人中有九人受伤，其中六人受重伤。
