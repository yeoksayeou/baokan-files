Title: Our Government Delegation Visits Prague to Celebrate the Eighth Anniversary of Czechoslovakia's Liberation; Czechoslovak President Zápotocký Receives Our Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 8th: To celebrate the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia, the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has specially dispatched a government delegation composed of Li Fuchun, Vice-Chairman of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council, Tan Xilin, our country's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Czechoslovakia, and Lao She, member of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council and writer, to visit Czechoslovakia to offer congratulations. Head of Delegation Li Fuchun arrived in Prague from Moscow by plane on the 7th. Also arriving on the same plane was D. Adilbish, the Mongolian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, representing the Mongolian People's Republic to offer congratulations.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included: Czechoslovak Republic Deputy Prime Minister V. Kopecký, Minister of Foreign Affairs V. David, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs G. Sekaninová-Čakrtová, Chairman of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries J. Uher, etc. Members of our government delegation, our country's ambassador to Czechoslovakia Tan Xilin, and writer Lao She, who is currently visiting Czechoslovakia, as well as personnel from our embassy in Czechoslovakia, also went to welcome them. After Head of Delegation Li Fuchun and Ambassador D. Adilbish disembarked from the plane, they were accompanied by Deputy Prime Minister V. Kopecký to review the guard of honor.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 9th: The President of the Czechoslovak Republic, A. Zápotocký, received Li Fuchun, the head of the government delegation of the People's Republic of China, and members Tan Xilin, our country's ambassador to Czechoslovakia, and writer Lao She at the Presidential Palace on the afternoon of the 7th, who were participating in the celebration of the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia by the Soviet Army. Those present at the reception included Czechoslovak Minister of Foreign Affairs V. David and others.

Head of Delegation Li Fuchun presented a gift from Chairman Mao Zedong to President Zápotocký on behalf of Chairman Mao Zedong. President Zápotocký and members of the delegation had a long and pleasant conversation.

On the morning of the 8th, the Chinese delegation participated in the ceremony commemorating the Soviet heroes who sacrificed themselves in the war to liberate Czechoslovakia, and laid a wreath in front of the tomb of the Red Army soldiers here. Also participating in the ceremony were Soviet Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Ya. Ye. Bogomolov and embassy personnel, as well as the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic.

The Chinese government delegation also laid a wreath at the National Liberation Memorial Hall where the late President of the Czechoslovak Republic, K. Gottwald, and other revolutionary heroes are buried.

Over the past two days, the Chinese government delegation has also met with Prime Minister V. Široký and Minister of Foreign Affairs V. David respectively.

On the evening of the 7th, the Chinese and Mongolian government delegations also attended a reception hosted by the head of the diplomatic mission of the German Democratic Republic in Czechoslovakia, Ben Koenen, to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the liberation of Germany.

### 我政府代表团赴布拉格祝贺捷解放八周年  捷总统萨波托斯基接见我政府代表团

1953-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团赴布拉格祝贺捷解放八周年
    捷总统萨波托斯基接见我政府代表团
   【新华社布拉格八日电】为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放八周年，中华人民共和国中央人民政府特派遣由政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春、我国驻捷特命全权大使谭希林及政务院文化教育委员会委员、作家老舍组成的政府代表团，赴捷祝贺。李富春团长已于七日自莫斯科飞抵布拉格。同机抵达的有代表蒙古人民共和国前往祝贺的蒙古驻苏联大使德·阿迪尔毕希。
    到机场欢迎的有：捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理瓦·柯别茨基，外交部部长瓦·戴维，外交部副部长格·塞卡尼诺娃—查克尔托娃，对外文化联络委员会主席约·乌尔本等。我国政府代表团团员、我国驻捷大使谭希林及正在捷克斯洛伐克访问的作家老舍，以及我驻捷大使馆人员也前往欢迎。李富春团长及德·阿迪尔毕希大使下飞机后，由瓦·柯别茨基副总理陪同检阅了仪仗队。
   【新华社布拉格九日电】捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安·萨波托斯基，七日下午在总统府接见参加苏军解放捷克斯洛伐克八周年庆祝大会的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长李富春和团员、我国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使谭希林及作家老舍。接见时在座的有捷克斯洛伐克外交部部长瓦·戴维等人。
    李富春团长代表毛泽东主席向萨波托斯基总统赠送礼物。萨波托斯基总统和代表团人员欢谈甚久。
    八日上午，中国代表团参加了纪念在解放捷克斯洛伐克的战争中牺牲的苏联英雄的典礼，在此间红军战士墓前献了花圈。参加典礼的还有苏联驻捷大使亚·叶·波戈莫洛夫和大使馆人员，以及蒙古人民共和国政府代表团。
    中国政府代表团还在安葬捷克斯洛伐克共和国故总统克·哥特瓦尔德和其他革命英雄的民族解放纪念堂献了花圈。
    两日来，中国政府代表团还分别会见了威·西罗基总理和瓦·戴维外交部长。
    七日晚，中国和蒙古政府代表团还出席了德意志民主共和国驻捷外交使团团长本·科恩为庆祝德国解放八周年而举行的招待会。
