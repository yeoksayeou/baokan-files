Title: French People Demand Peaceful Resolution of International Issues
Author:
Date: 1953-05-30
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 26th: In France, a growing number of people are demanding a peaceful resolution of the German question, a cessation of the war in Vietnam, and a conference of the great powers to resolve all outstanding international issues.

    Peace committees from various districts of Paris and cities throughout the country are sending delegations to the Presidential Palace, demanding the establishment of a government in France that supports international peace negotiations.

    The Human Rights League is demanding an immediate end to the war in Vietnam. The organization passed a resolution stating that peace negotiations are the only way to resolve the war in Vietnam.

    The Eighth National Congress of Former Prisoners of War also denounced the wars currently being waged in Korea and Vietnam, and demanded "a peaceful resolution of the German question through negotiations between the four occupying powers."

### 法国人民要求和平解决国际问题

1953-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    法国人民要求和平解决国际问题
    【新华社二十九日讯】据塔斯社巴黎二十六日讯：在法国，越来越多的人要求和平解决德国问题、停止越南战争并且召开大国会议以解决一切悬而未决的国际问题。
    巴黎各区及全国各城市的和平委员会纷纷派代表团到总统府去，要求在法国建立一个支持国际和平谈判的政府。
    人权联盟要求立即停止越南战争。这个组织通过决议说：和平谈判是解决越南战争的唯一方法。
    前战俘第八届全国代表大会也痛斥现在在朝鲜和越南进行的战争，并且要求“通过四占领国间的谈判来和平解决德国问题”。
