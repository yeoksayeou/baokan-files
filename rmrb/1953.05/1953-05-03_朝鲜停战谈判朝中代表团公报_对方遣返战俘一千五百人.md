Title: Korean Armistice Negotiations: Korean-Chinese Delegation Communiqué - The Other Side Repatriates 1,500 POWs
Author:
Date: 1953-05-03
Page: 4

    Korean Armistice Negotiations: Korean-Chinese Delegation Communiqué
    The Other Side Repatriates 1,500 POWs
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 30th] The Korean Armistice Negotiations Korean-Chinese Delegation issued the following communiqué on April 30th:
    On April 30th, the other side repatriated 500 sick and wounded captured personnel of the Korean People's Army to our side at Panmunjom.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 1st] The Korean Armistice Negotiations Korean-Chinese Delegation issued the following communiqué on May 1st:
    On May 1st, the other side repatriated 250 sick and wounded captured personnel of the Korean People's Army and 250 sick and wounded captured personnel of the Chinese People's Volunteers to our side at Panmunjom.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 2nd] The Korean Armistice Negotiations Korean-Chinese Delegation issued the following communiqué on May 2nd:
    On May 2nd, the other side repatriated 500 sick and wounded captured personnel of the Korean People's Army to our side at Panmunjom.

### 朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团公报  对方遣返战俘一千五百人

1953-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团公报
    对方遣返战俘一千五百人
    【新华社开城三十日电】　朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团四月三十日发表公报如下：
    四月三十日对方在板门店遣返给我方朝鲜人民军病伤被俘人员五百人。
    【新华社开城一日电】　朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团五月一日发表公报如下：
    五月一日对方在板门店遣返给我方朝鲜人民军病伤被俘人员二百五十人，中国人民志愿军病伤被俘人员二百五十人。
    【新华社开城二日电】　朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团五月二日发表公报如下：
    五月二日对方在板门店遣返给我方朝鲜人民军病伤被俘人员五百人。
