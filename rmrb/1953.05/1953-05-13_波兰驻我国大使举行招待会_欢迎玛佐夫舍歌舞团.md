Title: Polish Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Welcome the Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble

Author: 
Date: 1953-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    The Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, Kirylok, and his wife, held a reception at the Beijing Hotel on the 11th to welcome the Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble of the Polish People's Republic.

    The head of the Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble of the Polish People's Republic, Pannuwnik, and all 150 members of the ensemble attended the reception.

    Chinese guests invited to the reception included Vice Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government Zhou Yang, Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Education Wei Que, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Chen Yi, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yan Baohang, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee on Cultural and Educational Affairs Hong Shen, as well as figures from the capital's literary and art circles.

    At the reception, Ambassador Kirylok delivered a speech, pointing out that the Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble's visit to China is of great significance to the friendly cooperation between the two peoples, and it will promote the increasing development of cultural exchanges between the two countries. Vice Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government Zhou Yang spoke at the meeting, praising the ensemble's outstanding performances in the capital. He said that these performances have brought us the brilliance of youth and provided us with valuable experience in organizing folk art.

    The reception was filled with a friendly atmosphere, and a dance party was held afterwards.

### 波兰驻我国大使举行招待会  欢迎玛佐夫舍歌舞团

1953-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰驻我国大使举行招待会
    欢迎玛佐夫舍歌舞团
    据新华社讯：波兰人民共和国驻我国特命全权大使基里洛克和大使夫人为欢迎波兰人民共和国玛佐夫舍歌舞团，十一日在北京饭店举行招待会。
    波兰人民共和国玛佐夫舍歌舞团团长潘努夫尼克和全团人员一百五十多人都出席了招待会。
    我国方面应邀出席招待会的有中央人民政府文化部副部长周扬、丁西林，教育部副部长韦悫，人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，以及首都文艺界人士。
    在招待会上，基里洛克大使致词，指出玛佐夫舍歌舞团的来华对两国人民的友好合作具有重要意义，它将推动两国的文化交流越来越发展。中央人民政府文化部副部长周扬在会上讲话，赞扬歌舞团在首都所作的卓越的演出。他说这些演出给我们带来了青春的光彩，并给我们提供了整理民间艺术的宝贵的经验。
    招待会充满了友好的空气，会后还举行了舞会。
