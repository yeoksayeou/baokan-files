Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1953-05-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 28th. The majority of the members of the Chinese Women's Delegation attending the World Women's Congress arrived in Moscow on the 28th. The deputy head of the delegation, Zhang Yun, and the secretary-general, Lu Cui, had already arrived earlier. The head of the delegation, Cai Chang, and the deputy head of the delegation, Li Dequan, are already here. The delegation will leave here on the 30th via Berlin for Denmark.

### 中国妇女代表团抵莫斯科

1953-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中国妇女代表团抵莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科二十八日电】出席世界妇女大会的中国妇女代表团的大部分人员二十八日抵达莫斯科，副团长章蕴和秘书长陆璀已先到，团长蔡畅和副团长李德全已先在此。代表团将于三十日离此经柏林赴丹麦。
