Title: The Korean Ministry of Foreign Affairs Issues a Statement Releasing Seven American Civilians
Author:
Date: 1953-05-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 3rd. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued the following announcement on April 30th:
    On April 6th of this year, the Soviet government conveyed to the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea the request of the United States government regarding the release and repatriation of American civilians detained in North Korea. The government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea examined the aforementioned request and made a positive decision on the matter. Therefore, on April 30th, all seven detained American civilians were handed over to representatives of the Soviet Union in Andong City, People's Republic of China, to allow them to return to their home country.
    Note: These seven American civilians are: Nellie Dyer, former American missionary in Seoul; Helen Rosser; William Booth; Lawrence Zellers; Bertha Smith; Anders K. Jensen; and Louis L. Lens, former American businessman in Seoul.

### 朝鲜外务省发表公告释放七名美国平民

1953-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜外务省发表公告释放七名美国平民
    【新华社平壤三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省在四月三十日发表公告如下：
    今年四月六日，苏联政府把美国政府关于释放被扣留在北朝鲜的美国平民并使他们归还本国的要求转达给朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府审查了上述要求，通过了对于这个问题的肯定的决定。因此，已于四月三十日在中华人民共和国安东市把所有被扣留的七名美国平民移交给苏联代表，使他们得以返回本国。
    按：这七个美国平民是：前美国在汉城传教士奈利·戴尔，海伦·罗赛，威廉·布斯，劳伦斯·泽勒斯，伯萨·史密斯，安德斯·克·詹逊和前美国在汉城的商人路易斯·勒·丹斯。
