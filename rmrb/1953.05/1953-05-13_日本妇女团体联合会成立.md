Title: Establishment of the Japan Women's Organization Federation
Author:
Date: 1953-05-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report:

    Japan's women's movement is progressing towards the common goal of opposing war and defending the rights of women and children.

    On April 5th, representatives of thirty women's organizations in Japan, representing a wide range of social strata, convened a meeting in Tokyo and resolved to establish the Women's Organization Federation. The purpose of the Federation is to prevent war and seek happiness for women and children. The meeting elected Raicho Hiratsuka as the president of the Women's Organization Federation, Tomi Kora as the vice president, and Ito Hamada as the director of the secretariat.

    The current main task of the Women's Organization Federation is to actively prepare to participate in the World Women's Congress to be held in Denmark in June.

### 日本妇女团体联合会成立

1953-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    日本妇女团体联合会成立
    据新华社讯：日本的妇女运动正在反对战争、保卫妇女儿童权利的共同目标下向前发展。
    四月五日，代表广泛阶层的日本的三十个妇女团体的代表在东京召开会议，决议建立妇女团体联合会。联合会的宗旨是防止战争、为妇女儿童谋幸福。会议选出了平冢雷鸟为妇女团体联合会会长，高良富为副会长，滨田糸卫为事务局局长。
    妇女团体联合会当前的主要工作是积极筹备参加将于六月间在丹麦举行的世界妇女大会。
