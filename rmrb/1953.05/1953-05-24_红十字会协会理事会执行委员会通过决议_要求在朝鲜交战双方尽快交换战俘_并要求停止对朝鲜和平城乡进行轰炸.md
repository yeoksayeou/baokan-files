Title: Red Cross Society Council Executive Committee Passes Resolution Demanding Prompt Exchange of Prisoners of War Between Warring Parties in Korea and Demanding Cessation of Bombing of Peaceful Towns and Villages in Korea

Author:
Date: 1953-05-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 23rd: TASS Geneva, May 23rd: The twenty-second session of the Executive Committee of the Council of the League of Red Cross Societies concluded its work here on May 22nd. Representatives from thirty-one countries attended the meeting, including those from the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China.
    The meeting unanimously adopted the motion put forward by the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union, demanding that the bombing of peaceful towns and villages in Korea cease. This motion had been amended by the British representative, who requested that hostilities in Korea cease immediately, without waiting for a ceasefire agreement to be reached.
    The meeting unanimously adopted a motion put forward by the representative of the Chinese Red Cross, demanding that the warring parties in Korea find a basis acceptable to both sides in order to smoothly resolve the issue of the prompt exchange of prisoners of war in Korea in accordance with the spirit of the Geneva Conventions.

### 红十字会协会理事会执行委员会通过决议  要求在朝鲜交战双方尽快交换战俘  并要求停止对朝鲜和平城乡进行轰炸

1953-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    红十字会协会理事会执行委员会通过决议
    要求在朝鲜交战双方尽快交换战俘
    并要求停止对朝鲜和平城乡进行轰炸
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社日内瓦二十三日讯：红十字会协会第二十二届理事会执行委员会会议于五月二十二日在此地结束了它的工作。出席会议的有三十一个国家的代表，其中包括苏联和中华人民共和国的代表。
    会议一致通过了苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会所提出的议案，要求停止对朝鲜和平城镇与村庄进行轰炸。这个议案曾经经过英国代表的修正，英国代表要求，不要等待达成停战协议，现在就立即停止在朝鲜的敌对行动。
    会议一致通过了中国红十字会代表所提出的一项议案，要求在朝鲜的交战双方寻找一个可以为双方接受的基础，以便按照日内瓦公约的精神顺利地解决尽快交换在朝鲜的战俘问题。
