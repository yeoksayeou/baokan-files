Title: Soviets, Americans, British, and French Continue Talks on Air Safety Issues
Author:
Date: 1953-05-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 21st: The representatives of the military authorities of the Soviet Union, the United States, the United Kingdom, and France continued discussions on air safety issues at the headquarters of the Soviet Control Commission for Germany in Berlin on May 19th.

    The Soviet representative expressed his views on the counter-proposals put forward by the French, American, and British delegations at the meeting on May 5th in response to the Soviet proposal, and put forward additional suggestions on the issue of flight safety between Berlin and West Germany. He also answered some questions raised by the representatives of the United States, Britain, and France at the meeting.
    The meeting will continue.

### 讨论空中安全问题  苏美英法继续举行会谈

1953-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    讨论空中安全问题
    苏美英法继续举行会谈
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社柏林二十日讯：五月十九日，苏联、美国、英国及法国四国军事当局代表在柏林苏联对德管制委员会总部继续讨论空中安全问题。
    苏联代表就今年五月五日的会议上法、美、英三国代表团为答复苏联建议所提出的反建议发表了意见，并就柏林和西德之间飞行安全的问题提出了附加建议。他并且回答了美、英、法三国代表在会上提出的一些问题。
    会议将继续举行。
