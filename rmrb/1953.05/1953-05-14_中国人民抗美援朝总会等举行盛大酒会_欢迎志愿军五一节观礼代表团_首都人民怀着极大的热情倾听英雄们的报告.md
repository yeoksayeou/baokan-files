Title: China Peace Committee and Other Organizations Hold Grand Reception Welcoming the Delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to the May Day Celebrations; The People of the Capital Listen to the Heroes' Reports with Great Enthusiasm
Author:
Date: 1953-05-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th - The China Peace Committee, the North China Peace Committee Branch, and the Beijing Peace Committee Branch jointly held a grand reception at the Beijing Hotel at 5:00 PM on the 13th to welcome the entire delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army returning to the country for the "May Day" celebrations.

The banquet was presided over by Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee.

Those present included Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; Wu Maosun, Cui Yueli, Xu Baoju, Minister of Organization; Luo Longji, Minister of Propaganda; Xu Deheng, Minister of Liaison; Wu Han, Vice Chairman of the North China Peace Committee Branch; Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Peace Committee Branch, and others.

Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, delivered a speech at the meeting. On behalf of the China Peace Committee, the North China Peace Committee Branch, the Beijing Peace Committee Branch, and the people of the whole country, he extended deep gratitude and respect to all the delegates of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army returning to the country for the "May Day" celebrations and to all the comrades of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who are bravely fighting on the Korean front. Subsequently, Yuan Shengping, the head of the delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army returning to the country for the "May Day" celebrations, also spoke at the meeting, thanking the people of the whole country for their enormous support to the Volunteer Army.

According to Xinhua News Agency: Since the 12th, representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army returning to the country for the "May Day" celebrations have been giving reports to various organizations, groups, and people from all walks of life in the capital. There were eleven reports given on the 12th, with a total audience of over 32,000 people. Among those giving reports were Lu Min, a first-class combat hero of the Volunteer Army Air Force and an expert at shooting down enemy planes with "佩刀式" (Peidaoshi) [saber-style], special-class merit recipients Yang Zhenyu, Feng Zhenye, Deputy Political Commissar of a certain Volunteer Army unit, Chen Yucai, Deputy Regimental Commander, special-class merit recipients Ma Xinnian, Cai Xinghai, Wang Gengzheng, second-class demolition hero Jiang Yongde, first-class merit recipients Yu Houcheng, Yang Taizhong, second-class merit recipients, female nurses Wang Ying, Deng Wanqin, and others.

Workers, government employees, students, and people from all walks of life in the capital listened to the reports of the heroes from the Korean front with great excitement and enthusiasm. At the construction site of the Beijing Second Construction Engineering Company's 3219 project, more than 5,700 workers sat next to the scaffolding and listened attentively to the heroes' reports, braving the swirling sand and dust. More than 5,000 street women activists gathered at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall, feeling extremely excited by the reports of female nurses Wang Ying, Deng Wanqin, and others. After Deng Wanqin's report, an old woman rushed up to her, embraced her warmly, and kissed her, saying, "You are such a good child!" Air Force hero Lu Min, who gave a report to more than 4,500 middle school students in the Xidan district of Beijing, was enthusiastically welcomed by the students. The young students lifted Lu Min high on their shoulders. The students also made many gifts by hand for the heroes. When listening to the report, middle school students in the Xisi district submitted notes to the platform, writing their names and classes, and requesting to be close friends with the heroes.

The heroes' reports deeply moved the workers, government employees, women, students, and children in the capital. After listening to the reports, many people expressed that they must intensify their work and study, strive to do a good job in production, complete and overfulfill the state's various construction plans with a fighting spirit, and support the front with greater strength, and strengthen the strength to resist America and aid Korea.

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会等举行盛大酒会  欢迎志愿军五一节观礼代表团  首都人民怀着极大的热情倾听英雄们的报告

1953-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民抗美援朝总会等举行盛大酒会
    欢迎志愿军五一节观礼代表团
    首都人民怀着极大的热情倾听英雄们的报告
    【新华社十三日讯】中国人民抗美援朝总会、华北抗美援朝总分会、北京市抗美援朝分会于十三日下午五时在北京饭店联合举行盛大酒会，欢宴中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团全体代表。
    宴会由中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席彭真主持。
    出席作陪的有：中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通、秘书长刘贯一、副秘书长吴茂荪、崔月犁、组织部长许宝驹、宣传部长罗隆基、联络部长许德珩，华北抗美援朝总分会副主席吴晗，北京市抗美援朝分会主席张奚若等。
    中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席彭真在会上致词。他代表中国人民抗美援朝总会、华北抗美援朝总分会、北京市抗美援朝分会及全国人民向到会的中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团全体代表及英勇战斗在朝鲜前线的中国人民志愿军全体同志，致以深切的谢意和敬意。接着，中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团团长袁升平也在会上讲话，感谢全国人民对志愿军的巨大支援。
    据新华社讯：中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团的代表们，自十二日起分别向首都各机关、团体和各界人民作报告。十二日报告共十一场，听众约三万二千多人。作报告的有志愿军空军一级战斗英雄、打佩刀式敌机的能手鲁珉，特等功臣杨振玉，志愿军某部副政治委员冯振业，副团长陈育才，特等功臣马新年、蔡兴海、王更正，二级爆破英雄蒋永德，一等功臣于厚诚、杨太忠，二等功臣、女护士王颖、邓万芹等。
    工人、机关干部、学生和首都各界人民，都以极大的兴奋和热情来听来自朝鲜前线的英雄们的报告。在北京市第二建筑工程公司三二一九工地，五千七百多个工人，冒着漫天风沙坐在工地的建筑架旁聚精会神地听英雄们的报告。聚集在中山公园音乐堂的五千多个街道妇女积极分子，对女护士王颖、邓万芹等的报告感到极大的兴奋。一个老太太在邓万芹报告后，抢上去和她热烈地拥抱并吻着她说：“真是好孩子！”向北京西单区四千五百多个中学生作报告的空军英雄鲁珉，受到学生们的狂热欢迎。青年学生们把鲁珉高高举在肩上。同学们还亲手做很多礼物送给英雄们。西四区的中学生在听报告时，纷纷向主席台递条子，条子上写着自己的姓名、班级，要求和英雄们作亲密的朋友。
    英雄们的报告深深地感动了首都的工人、机关干部、妇女、学生和儿童。不少人在听了报告后，都表示一定要加紧工作和学习，努力搞好生产，用战斗的精神，完成和超额完成国家各项建设计划，以更大的力量支援前线，加强抗美援朝的力量。
