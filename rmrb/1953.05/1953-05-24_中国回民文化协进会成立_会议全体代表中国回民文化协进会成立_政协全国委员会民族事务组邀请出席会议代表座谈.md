Title: Founding of the China Hui Muslim Culture Promotion Association; All Representatives at the Meeting Establish the China Hui Muslim Culture Promotion Association; The CPPCC National Committee's Ethnic Affairs Group Invites Representatives Attending the Meeting to a Symposium
Author:
Date: 1953-05-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd: The founding meeting of the China Hui Muslim Culture Promotion Association was held in Beijing from May 15th to 19th. The 143 representatives who attended the meeting were all well-known and representative figures from the Hui ethnic group, including workers, farmers, cultural and educational workers, religious figures, and relevant cadres. They came from all regions of the country.
    Bai Shouyi, one of the initiators of the China Hui Muslim Culture Promotion Association, delivered the opening speech. He pointed out that thanks to the wise leadership of the great Communist Party and Chairman Mao, the Hui people are now living happily in the great family of equality, friendship, unity, and cooperation of all ethnic groups in the People's Republic of China. With the development of the country's construction, the Hui people will fundamentally and gradually change the poverty and backwardness left over from history. Liu Geping, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, gave a report on ethnic issues at the meeting. After comprehensively summarizing the rapid development of the Hui people in political, economic, cultural, and educational aspects since liberation, he said: These achievements have greatly strengthened the great unity between the Hui and Han peoples, fundamentally changed the historical phenomena of mutual discrimination and distrust, and established a new relationship of equality, mutual assistance, friendship, and cooperation, and this relationship is being consolidated and developed day by day. He called on the Hui people to further strengthen their close unity with the Han people and other fraternal ethnic groups and actively participate in the various undertakings of the motherland. The meeting also invited Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, to give a current affairs report.
    Gan Chunlei, another initiator of the China Hui Muslim Culture Promotion Association, gave an explanation on the draft constitution of the China Hui Muslim Culture Promotion Association at the meeting. He emphasized that the China Hui Muslim Culture Promotion Association would assist the People's Government in researching and developing the cultural undertakings of the Hui people in accordance with the basic spirit of the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and conduct patriotic education among the Hui people. After thorough discussion, the representatives unanimously adopted the constitution and elected Liu Geping as the Director of the China Hui Muslim Culture Promotion Association, Wang Xingrang, Wu Hongbin, Ma Teng'ai, Zhang Bochuan, Zhao Zhongqi, Bai Shouyi, and Ma Songting as Deputy Directors, and Ding Zhende and 90 others as committee members. (The list is published on the third page.)
    Representatives from various regions attending the meeting spoke enthusiastically at the conference. They shared their happy lives since liberation and introduced the situations of the Hui people in various regions, and exchanged experiences in the work related to the Hui people. All speeches fully expressed the Hui people's love for Chairman Mao, the Chinese Communist Party, and the Central People's Government, their enthusiasm for building the motherland, and their confidence in developing the cultural and educational undertakings of the Hui people. After the meeting unanimously passed a letter of respect to Chairman Mao and a telegram of respect to the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, Ma Teng'ai delivered the closing speech.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Ethnic Affairs Group of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference invited more than 100 representatives who attended the founding meeting of the China Hui Muslim Culture Promotion Association to a symposium on May 22nd. At the meeting, Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, on behalf of the National Committee of the CPPCC, warmly congratulated the founding of the China Hui Muslim Culture Promotion Association. He proposed that representatives, after returning to their respective regions, assist the People's Government in researching and developing the cultural and educational undertakings of the Hui people and strive for the construction of the great motherland and the defense of world peace. The representatives unanimously stated that they would follow the instructions of the central leaders and work hard to implement the various tasks decided by the China Hui Muslim Culture Promotion Association.

### 中国回民文化协进会成立  会议全体代表中国回民文化协进会成立  政协全国委员会民族事务组邀请出席会议代表座谈

1953-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中国回民文化协进会成立
    会议全体代表中国回民文化协进会成立
    政协全国委员会民族事务组邀请出席会议代表座谈
    【新华社二十三日讯】中国回民文化协进会成立会议于五月十五日到十九日在北京举行。参加会议的一百四十三个代表，都是回族中知名的和有代表性的人士，包括工人、农民、文化教育工作者、宗教界人士和有关的干部等。他们来自全国各个地区。
    会议由中国回民文化协进会发起人之一白寿彝致开幕词。他指出：由于伟大的共产党和毛主席的英明领导，回族人民已经幸福地生活在中华人民共和国各民族平等、友爱、团结、合作的大家庭中，随着国家建设事业的发展，回族人民将从根本上逐步改变历史上遗留下来的贫困落后的状态。中央人民政府民族事务委员会刘格平副主任委员在会上作了关于民族问题的报告。他全面地总结了回族人民解放以来在政治、经济和文化教育各方面迅速发展的情况后说：这些成就已大大加强了回、汉人民的大团结，从根本上改变了历史上遗留下来的互相歧视和不信任的现象，建立了平等互助、友爱合作的新关系，而且这种关系正在日益巩固和发展着。他号召回族人民进一步加强与汉族人民和其他兄弟民族人民的亲密团结，积极参加祖国的各种事业。会议并邀请中央人民政府民族事务委员会刘春副主任委员作时事报告。
    中国回民文化协进会的另一发起人甘春雷在会上作了关于中国回民文化协进会组织简章草案的说明。他着重说明中国回民文化协进会将根据中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领的基本精神，协助人民政府研究和发展回民的文化事业，并在回民中进行爱国主义的教育。代表们经过充分讨论后，一致通过了组织简章，并选出刘格平为中国回民文化协进会主任，王兴让、吴鸿宾、马腾霭、张勃川、赵钟奇、白寿彝、马松亭为副主任，丁振德等九十七人为委员。（名单刊载第三版）
    各地出席会议的代表在大会上热烈发言，他们纷纷叙述各地回民解放以来的幸福生活，并介绍了各地回民情况，交流了回民工作的经验。所有发言都充分表现了回族人民对毛主席、中国共产党和中央人民政府的爱戴，对建设祖国的热情，对发展回民文化教育事业的信心。会议一致通过向毛主席致敬信和向中国人民解放军、中国人民志愿军致敬电以后，由马腾霭致闭会词。
    据新华社讯：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会民族事务组，在五月二十二日邀请参加中国回民文化协进会成立会议的全体代表一百多人举行座谈会。会上，政协全国委员会李济深副主席代表政协全国委员会热烈祝贺中国回民文化协进会的成立。他提出：希望代表们回到各地后，协助人民政府研究和发展回族人民的文化教育事业，为建设伟大祖国和保卫世界和平而奋斗。代表们一致表示：要遵照中央各位首长的指示，为贯彻执行中国回民文化协进会所决定的各项任务而努力。
