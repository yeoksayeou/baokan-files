Title: US Planes Indiscriminately Bomb Pyongyang and Other Areas
Author:
Date: 1953-05-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang: U.S. Air Force has recently intensified its indiscriminate bombing of Pyongyang and other residential areas in northern Korea. On May 1st, under the cover of high altitude and clouds, U.S. planes secretly flew to Pyongyang and attacked the peaceful residents of Pyongyang who were celebrating International Labor Day. U.S. planes dropped fragmentation bombs on Pyongyang's residential areas and strafed the streets. On the same day, U.S. planes also bombed other places in northern Korea.
    On the evening of May 3rd, Pyongyang was again subjected to a frenzied attack by the U.S. Air Force. In an attack that lasted about half an hour, U.S. planes dropped nearly 350 bombs on Pyongyang's residential areas.

### 美机滥炸平壤等地

1953-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

    美机滥炸平壤等地
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社平壤讯：美国空军最近加紧滥炸平壤和朝鲜北部其他居民地区。五月一日，美机在高空和云层的掩盖下，偷偷地飞到平壤，袭击正在庆祝五一劳动节的平壤和平居民。美机把杀伤弹投在平壤住宅区，并对街道进行扫射。同日，美机还轰炸了朝鲜北部其他地方。
    五月三日晚上，平壤又受到美国空军的疯狂袭击。美机在继续了约半小时的袭击中，向平壤的居民地区投了将近三百五十枚炸弹。
