Title: Several French Provincial Assemblies and Municipal Councils Support the Conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1953-05-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 21st: "L'Humanité" reports: The Aube Provincial Assembly of France, composed of members of the Communist Party, the Socialist Party, the Radical Socialist Party, the Popular Republican Movement, and others, unanimously adopted a resolution urging the French government to give a positive response to the letter from the International Committee of the World People's Peace Congress suggesting the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact. The Provincial Assembly also passed another resolution stating: The German question must be resolved peacefully, and a ceasefire must be implemented immediately in Korea.
　　The provincial assemblies of Alpes-Maritimes and Aude, as well as the municipal councils of several towns, have passed similar resolutions.

### 法国若干省议会和市政委员会  支持缔结五大国和平公约

1953-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    法国若干省议会和市政委员会
    支持缔结五大国和平公约
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社巴黎二十一日讯：“人道报”报道：包括共产党、社会党、激进社会党、天主教人民共和党党员以及其他人士的法国瑷茵省省议会一致通过决议，敦促法国政府对世界人民和平大会国际委员会建议缔结五大国和平公约的来信给予肯定的答复。省议会还通过另一个决议说：必须和平解决德国问题，必须立即在朝鲜停火。
    阿尔卑斯海滨省和奥德省的省议会以及若干城镇的市政委员会通过了类似的决议。
