Title: Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau Holds Evening Party to Celebrate German Liberation Day
Author:
Date: 1953-05-08
Page: 4

    Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau Holds Evening Party to Celebrate German Liberation Day
    [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] To celebrate the Liberation Day of the German Democratic Republic, the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government held a film evening party on the 7th. The evening party was presided over by Director Hong Shen, and the German Democratic Republic film "Fighting Village" was screened. Those attending the celebration included Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and more than 1,000 representatives from government departments and schools. Konrad König, head of the diplomatic mission of the German Democratic Republic to China, as well as other foreign envoys and embassy staff in China, were also invited to attend.

### 我对外文化联络事务局  举行晚会庆祝德国解放日

1953-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我对外文化联络事务局
    举行晚会庆祝德国解放日
    【新华社七日讯】为庆祝德意志民主共和国解放日，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局在七日举行电影晚会。晚会由洪深局长主持，放映了德意志民主共和国影片“战斗的乡村”。出席庆祝会的有中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航及机关、学校代表等一千多人。德意志民主共和国驻我国外交使团团长柯尼希及其他各国驻华使节和使馆人员也应邀出席。
