Title: China's Delegation Attends World Medical Conference in Austria
Author:
Date: 1953-05-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 19th: The World Medical Conference, initiated by prominent pro-peace medical professionals from various countries, will be held in Austria in late May. The purpose of this conference is to unite pro-peace medical professionals worldwide to jointly oppose aggressive wars, safeguard world peace, and protect human health. The Chinese delegation attending this conference is composed of domestic medical experts and flew to Austria on May 13th.
The list of the Chinese delegation is as follows:
Head: Fang Shishan (Internal Medicine Expert, Secretary-General of the Chinese Medical Association)
First Deputy Head and Secretary-General: Gong Naiquan (Surgery Expert, President of Shanghai Medical College)
Second Deputy Head: Bai Xiqing (Pathology Expert, Vice President of China Medical University in Shenyang)
Deputy Secretary-General: Ke Lin (Internal Medicine Expert, President of Zhongshan University Medical College in Guangzhou), Ji Zhongpu (Physiology Expert, President of Harbin Medical University)
Members (in order of the number of strokes in their surnames):
Lin Qiaozhi (Obstetrics and Gynecology Expert, Director and Professor of Obstetrics and Gynecology at Peking Union Medical College)
Wu Huanxing (Radiology Professor, Director of Shanghai Radium Treatment Hospital)
Ji Suhua (Surgery Expert, Vice President of Beijing Hospital)
Liang Boqiang (Pathology Expert, Director and Professor of the Department of Pathology, Zhongshan University Medical College in Guangzhou)
Huang Jiasi (Thoracic Surgery Expert, Director and Professor of Surgery, Shanghai Medical College)
Zhu Futang (Pediatrics Expert, President of Beijing Children's Hospital)
Zhong Huilan (Tropical Medicine Expert, President of Beijing Central People's Hospital)
Xue Yu (Pharmacy Expert, Director and Professor of the Department of Pharmacy, Beijing Medical College)
Secretary: Zhu Shouhe (Physician), Zhang Tiemin (Physician)
Translator: Yu Qian (Physician)

### 出席世界医学会议  我国代表团赴奥地利

1953-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界医学会议
    我国代表团赴奥地利
    【新华社十九日讯】由世界各国爱好和平的著名医学界人士所发起的世界医学会议五月下旬将在奥地利举行。这个会议的目的是团结世界爱好和平的医学界人士，共同起来反对侵略战争，保卫世界和平，保障人类的健康。出席这次会议的我国代表团是由国内的医学专家组成的，代表团已在五月十三日飞赴奥地利。
    我国代表团的名单如下：
    团长　方石珊（内科学专家、中华医学会总会秘书长）

    第二副团长　白希清（病理学专家、沈阳中国医科大学副校长）
    副秘书长　柯麟（内科学专家、广州中山大学医学院院长）季钟朴（生理学专家、哈尔滨医科大学校长）
