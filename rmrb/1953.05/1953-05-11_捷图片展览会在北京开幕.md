Title: Czechoslovakian Photo Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-05-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 10th: To celebrate the eighth anniversary of Czechoslovakia's liberation, the "Czechoslovak Republic Photo Exhibition" sponsored by the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, opened on the 10th at the Water Pavilion in Zhongshan Park, Beijing. Many people went to visit.

The exhibition features many photos about production and construction and cultural activities, showing the high enthusiasm of the Czechoslovak working people in striving to build socialism and their brilliant achievements in various fields. Some photos show the warm welcome our literary and art delegation received during their performances in various parts of Czechoslovakia last year. The exhibition also displays Czechoslovak film photos and folk art.

### 捷图片展览会在北京开幕

1953-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    捷图片展览会在北京开幕
    【新华社十日讯】为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放八周年，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局主办的“捷克斯洛伐克共和国图片展览会”，已于十日在北京中山公园水榭开幕。前往参观的人很多。
    展览会中悬挂着许多关于生产建设和文化活动的图片，显示捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民努力建设社会主义的高度热情和他们在各方面取得的辉煌成就。有些照片表现着我国文艺工作团去年在捷各地演出时备受欢迎的情况。展览会中还陈列着捷克斯洛伐克的电影照片和民间艺术品。
