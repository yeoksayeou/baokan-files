Title: China Peace Committee Cables Congratulations on the Opening of the British Peace Action Conference
Author:
Date: 1953-05-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: The China Peace Committee cabled congratulations on the opening of the British Peace Action Conference. The full text is as follows: To the British Peace Action Conference:

    Having learned that the British Peace Action Conference will be held on the 16th and 17th of this month, the China Peace Committee, on behalf of the peace-loving Chinese people, extends its sincere congratulations to the conference. We believe that this conference will unite the broad masses of the British people more closely and strive to safeguard world peace and ease international tensions. At the same time, we believe that the friendship between the people of China and Britain and the peace-loving people of the world will grow and be consolidated in the common struggle against war and for the defense of peace. We wish your conference success.

    China Peace Committee May 15th

### 我国人民保卫世界和平委员会  致电祝贺英国和平行动大会的召开

1953-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    致电祝贺英国和平行动大会的召开
    【新华社十五日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会致电祝贺英国和平行动大会召开。全文如下：英国和平行动大会：
    获悉英国和平行动大会于本月十六日和十七日两日召开，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会谨代表爱好和平的全中国人民向大会致热诚的祝贺。我们相信：此次大会将使英国广大人民更紧密地团结起来，为维护世界和平、缓和国际紧张局势而努力。我们同时相信中、英两国人民及全世界爱好和平人民之间的友谊，将在反对战争、保卫和平的共同奋斗中日益发展和巩固。谨祝你们大会的成功。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  五月十五日
