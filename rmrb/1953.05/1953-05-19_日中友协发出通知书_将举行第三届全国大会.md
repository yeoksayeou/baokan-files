Title: Sino-Japanese Friendship Association Issues Notice: Third National Congress to be Held
Author:
Date: 1953-05-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 18th: The Japan-China Friendship Association is scheduled to hold its Third National Congress in Tokyo on May 30th and 31st. The notice issued by the Japan-China Friendship Association for the convening of this congress points out: "Now, Sino-Japanese friendship has become the fervent call of the people in every corner of the country. The Third National Congress, convened under this situation, bears the historical task of vigorously promoting this movement forward."

The notice announces the agenda of the congress as follows: Work report; issues concerning work guidelines and organizational guidelines; thematic discussions on promoting Sino-Japanese trade, promoting cultural exchange, the issue of repatriation of Japanese nationals, fishery issues, commemorating the victims of the Hanaoka Incident and returning the remains of wartime Chinese prisoners who died to China, and other matters.

### 日中友协发出通知书  将举行第三届全国大会

1953-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友协发出通知书
    将举行第三届全国大会
    【新华社十八日讯】东京消息：日中友好协会定于五月三十日和三十一日在东京举行第三届全国大会。日中友好协会为召开这次大会而发出的通知书指出：“现在，日中友好已在全国每一个角落成为人民的热烈呼声。在这种形势之下召开的第三届全国大会负有使这个运动蓬勃向前发展的历史任务。”
    通知书宣布大会的议程为：工作报告；关于工作方针和组织方针问题；关于促进日中贸易、促进文化交流、日侨归国问题、渔业问题、追悼花冈惨案等战时中国俘虏殉难者并把他们的遗骨送回中国等专题讨论以及其他事项。
