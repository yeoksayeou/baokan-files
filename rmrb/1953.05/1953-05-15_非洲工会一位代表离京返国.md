Title: An African Trade Union Representative Departs Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1953-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 12th: Diallo Seydou, Secretary-General of the French West Africa Dakar Postal and Telecommunications Union, who came to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day and the Seventh National Congress of the Chinese Trade Union, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 12th to return home. Li Jinghan, Acting Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, Jin Zhifu, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union, and Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others went to the airport to see him off.

### 非洲工会一位代表离京返国

1953-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    非洲工会一位代表离京返国
    【新华社十二日讯】来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节和中国工会第七次全国代表大会的法属西非达加邮电工会总书记迪亚洛·赛杜，于十二日晨乘飞机离京返国。前往飞机场欢送的有：中国邮电工会全国委员会代理主席李景韩、中国煤矿工会全国委员会主席金直夫和中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明等人。
