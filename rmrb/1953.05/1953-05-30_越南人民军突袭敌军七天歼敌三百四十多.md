Title: Vietnamese People's Army Smashes Enemy in Seven-Day Surprise Attack, Annihilating Over 340

Author:

Date: 1953-05-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 28th: From May 11th to 17th, the Vietnamese People's Army conducted 20 surprise attacks and counter-"sweeping" operations in the plains of North Vietnam, eliminating 346 enemy soldiers.

    During these seven days, the People's Army raided enemy strongholds in Ninh Binh city and Binh An Nhan, attacked An Vei near Phu Ly city, and removed the Dong Cu stronghold near Thai Binh city and the Qua Wa stronghold in Vinh Phuc province. In the counter-"sweeping" operations conducted by the People's Army in the Van Tri area of Vinh Phuc province, a total of 150 enemy soldiers were killed and three tanks were destroyed. In these seven days of fighting, the People's Army seized more than 270 weapons, destroyed four tanks, 21 military vehicles, one warship, one airplane, and one gasoline depot.

    In central and southern Vietnam, the People's Army shattered an enemy "sweep" of the Tran Luong and Ben Ke areas, wiping out one enemy company and seizing many guns.

### 越南人民军突袭敌军七天歼敌三百四十多

1953-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军突袭敌军七天歼敌三百四十多
    【新华社二十九日讯】据越南通讯社二十八日讯：从五月十一日到十七日，越南人民军部队在北越平原进行了大小二十次突袭和反“扫荡”战，歼灭敌军三百四十六人。
    在这七天中，人民军曾突袭宁平城和贫安仁的敌军据点，进攻府里城附近的安卫，拔除太平城附近的东居和永福省的夸瓦据点。人民军在永福省文治地区进行的反“扫荡”战，共打死敌军一百五十人，击毁坦克三辆。人民军在这七天的战斗中，共缴获二百七十多件武器，击毁坦克四辆、军用车辆二十一辆，舰艇一艘、飞机一架和汽油库一座。
    在中越南部，人民军部队粉碎了敌军对真伦和奔克地区的一次“扫荡”，歼灭敌军一个连，缴获许多枪枝。
