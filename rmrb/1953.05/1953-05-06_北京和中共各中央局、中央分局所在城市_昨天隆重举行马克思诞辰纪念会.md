Title: Beijing and Cities Where CCP Central Bureaus and Central Sub-bureaus are Located Solemnly Hold Meetings to Commemorate Marx's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1953-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: On May 5th, Beijing, the capital of our country, and major cities where the Central Bureaus and Central Sub-bureaus of the Communist Party of China are located, solemnly held various activities to commemorate the 135th anniversary of the birth of Karl Marx.

    The Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, in order to commemorate the leader and mentor of the world proletariat, held a memorial meeting on the morning of the 5th, inviting Yang Xianzhen, Vice President of the Marx-Lenin Institute, to give a report to all the delegates entitled "Marxism - The Banner of the Liberation of the Chinese Working Class." (Full text is on page 3 of today's newspaper)

    The Chinese Academy of Sciences held a grand memorial meeting on the afternoon of the 5th. Chen Boda, Tao Menghe, and Zhu Kezhen, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, well-known scientists in our country, and staff members of the Beijing area of the Chinese Academy of Sciences attended the meeting. Chen Boda, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, gave a report at the meeting, focusing on the relationship between Marxism and natural scientists.

    The Marx-Lenin Institute, Renmin University of China, Peking University, Beijing Normal University and other famous universities held memorial meetings on the 5th. Those who gave reports included Yang Xianzhen and Hu Sheng of the Marx-Lenin Institute, and He Xilin, Vice President of Beijing Normal University.

    The exhibition commemorating the 135th anniversary of the birth of Karl Marx, held by the Beijing Library, officially opened on the afternoon of the 5th. Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, attended the opening ceremony and cut the ribbon. Personnel from various foreign embassies in China were also invited to attend the opening ceremony. The various photos, books, and charts displayed at the exhibition vividly demonstrated the emergence, development, and great victory of Marxism.

    The Committee of Organs Directly Under the Northwest Bureau of the CPC held a cadre meeting of more than 1,300 people on the afternoon of the 5th. Zhao Boping, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Northwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, introduced Marx's life and the significance of Marx's great theory to the world revolution, and mobilized cadres to earnestly implement the "Instructions of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on Cadre Theoretical Education in 1953-1954" and strive to study Marxism-Leninism. The Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee held a Marx birthday commemoration meeting on the afternoon of the 5th, with Liu Zizai, Deputy Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Northeast Bureau, giving a report. The Committee of Organs Directly Under the Central and Southern Bureau of the CPC Central Committee convened more than 1,000 cadres from the organs directly under the Central and Southern Bureau on the 5th to hold a memorial meeting, with Pan Zinian reporting on Marx's life and his theory, encouraging cadres to further study Marxism and improve their theoretical cultivation.

    The exhibition commemorating the 135th anniversary of the birth of Marx, jointly organized by the East China Region and Shanghai Municipality, opened on the afternoon of the 5th. Leading comrades of the CPC committees and governments of East China Region and Shanghai Municipality, the People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, representatives of various democratic parties, and diplomatic personnel from the Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia in Shanghai, more than 400 people, first visited the exhibition. The exhibition displayed the classic literature of Marxism, as well as the brilliant and creative works of the great revolutionary teachers Engels, Lenin, and Stalin on Marxism. In order to illustrate the great victory that Marxism has achieved in China, the exhibition also displayed the main works of the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, relevant historical documents, and the museum model of the site of the Shanghai Revolutionary History Memorial Hall and other important materials.

    More than 1,000 cadres from various agencies and organizations in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Suiyuan Province, and Guisui City held a memorial meeting at the Guisui City Workers' Cultural Palace on the 5th. Huang Jingtao of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee Inner Mongolia-Suiyuan Sub-bureau gave a report. More than 100 professors from Zhongshan University in Guangzhou held a symposium on the 5th, at which the memorial article written by the school's Marxism-Leninism Basic Teaching Group was read.

### 北京和中共各中央局、中央分局所在城市  昨天隆重举行马克思诞辰纪念会

1953-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    北京和中共各中央局、中央分局所在城市
    昨天隆重举行马克思诞辰纪念会
    据新华社讯：五月五日我国首都北京和中国共产党各中央局、中央分局所在的大城市都隆重举行纪念卡尔·马克思一百三十五周年诞辰的各项活动。
    中国工会第七次全国代表大会为了纪念全世界无产阶级的领袖和导师，特在五日上午举行纪念会，邀请马克思列宁学院副院长杨献珍向大会全体代表作了题为“马克思主义——中国工人阶级解放的旗帜”的报告。（全文见今天本报第三版）
    中国科学院在五日下午举行盛大纪念会，参加纪念会的有中国科学院副院长陈伯达、陶孟和、竺可桢，我国著名的科学家及中国科学院北京区的工作人员。中国科学院副院长陈伯达在会上作了报告，着重说明马克思主义与自然科学家的关系。
    马克思列宁学院、中国人民大学、北京大学和北京师范大学等著名学府，五日都举行了纪念会，作报告的人有马克思列宁学院的杨献珍、胡绳和北京师范大学的副校长何锡麟等。
    北京图书馆举办的纪念卡尔·马克思诞生一百三十五周年展览会，在五日下午正式开幕。中央人民政府文化部副部长周扬到会剪彩。各国驻华大使馆人员也应邀参加开幕典礼。展览会上陈列的各种照片、书籍、图表，生动地展示了马克思主义的发生、发展和伟大的胜利。
    中共中央西北局直属机关委员会五日下午举行一千三百多人的干部大会，由中共中央西北局宣传部部长赵伯平介绍马克思的生平及马克思伟大学说对于世界革命的意义，动员干部切实执行“中国共产党中央委员会关于一九五三年—一九五四年干部理论教育的指示”，努力学习马克思列宁主义。中共中央东北局在五日下午举行马克思诞辰纪念大会，由东北局宣传部副部长刘子载作报告。中共中央中南局直属机关委员会，五日召集中南局直属机关干部一千多人举行纪念会，由潘梓年报告马克思生平及其学说，鼓舞干部进一步学习马克思主义，提高理论修养。
    华东区和上海市举办的马克思诞生一百三十五周年展览会在五日下午开幕，华东区和上海市的中共党委、政府、人民解放军驻上海部队的负责人，各民主党派代表及苏联、捷克斯洛伐克在上海的外交人员等四百多人首先前往参观。展览会陈列了马克思主义的经典文献，以及伟大革命导师恩格斯、列宁、斯大林对于马克思主义的天才的创造性的著作。为了说明马克思主义在中国所获得的伟大胜利，又展览出了中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东主席的主要著作、有关历史性文件及上海革命历史纪念馆的馆址模型等重要材料。
    内蒙古自治区、绥远省和归绥市各机关团体干部一千多人五日在归绥市工人文化宫举行纪念会。中共中央蒙绥分局宣传部黄静涛作了报告。广州中山大学的教授一百多人五日举行了座谈会，会上宣读了该校马克思列宁主义基础教学小组所写的纪念文章。
