Title: Soviet Newspapers Publish Nehru's Statement
Author:
Date: 1953-05-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 17th: The Pravda, Izvestia, and Red Star newspapers published on the 17th a statement by Prime Minister Nehru on foreign policy, as broadcast by the Indian News Service.
