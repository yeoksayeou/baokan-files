Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-05-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    A large-scale sugar refinery, designed and constructed by our own workers, officially began construction in the sugarcane-producing area of Guangdong this January. This is the refinery's pressing workshop. Workers are installing large steam engines here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    由我国职工自己设计和施工的一座大型糖厂，已于今年一月在广东产蔗区正式施工。这是该厂的压榨车间。工人们正在这里安装大型的蒸气机。
