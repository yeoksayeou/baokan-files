Title: Guatemalan Government Rejects U.S. Interference in Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1953-05-07
Page: 4

    Guatemalan Government
    Rejects U.S. Interference in Internal Affairs
    (Xinhua News Agency, New York, 6th) TASS, New York, May 5th: According to American newspapers, Guatemalan Foreign Minister Raúl Osegueda declared:
    The government of the Republic of Guatemala has rejected the U.S. note demanding immediate compensation for the properties of the United Fruit Company that were expropriated. Osegueda said that the U.S. note concerned matters related to Guatemala's internal affairs.

### 危地马拉政府  拒绝美国干涉内政

1953-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    危地马拉政府
    拒绝美国干涉内政
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社纽约五日讯：据美国报纸消息，危地马拉外交部长劳尔·奥塞格达宣称：
    危地马拉共和国政府拒绝了美国要求立即赔偿被征用了的联合果品公司财产的照会。奥塞格达说，美国照会涉及关系危地马拉内政的问题。
