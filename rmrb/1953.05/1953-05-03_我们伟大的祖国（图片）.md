    Our Great Motherland
    On the first "May Day" International Labor Day night of the new economic construction in the motherland, the lights and fireworks on Tiananmen Square appeared even more magnificent and splendid. This happy light brought boundless joy and enthusiasm to the people, encouraging us to be more courageous and hardworking in the struggle to build our motherland.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在祖国开始新的经济建设第一年的“五一”国际劳动节的天安门之夜，广场上的灯光和焰火显得更加辉煌瑰丽。这幸福的光给人们带来了无限的欢乐和热情，鼓舞着我们更加勇敢而勤劳地去为建设我们的祖国而奋斗。
