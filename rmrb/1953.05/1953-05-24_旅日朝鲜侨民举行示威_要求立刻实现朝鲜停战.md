Title: Pro-North Korean Residents in Japan Demonstrate, Demanding Immediate Korean Armistice
Author: 
Date: 1953-05-24
Page: 4

    Pro-North Korean Residents in Japan Demonstrate
    Demanding Immediate Korean Armistice
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 22nd] Tokyo dispatch: Three thousand pro-North Korean residents in Japan held a general mobilization rally of Koreans in the Kanto region in Tokyo on the 21st to promote the Korean armistice, demanding an immediate armistice in Korea. The rally adopted resolutions such as "Immediate Armistice," "Crush the Japan-South Korea Talks," and "Oppose Dispatching Security Forces to Fight in Korea," and held a demonstration parade in the afternoon.

### 旅日朝鲜侨民举行示威  要求立刻实现朝鲜停战

1953-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    旅日朝鲜侨民举行示威
    要求立刻实现朝鲜停战
    【新华社二十二日讯】东京消息：旅日朝鲜侨民三千人于二十一日在东京举行促进朝鲜停战关东朝鲜人总奋起大会，要求立刻实现朝鲜停战。大会作出了“立刻停战”、“粉碎日韩会谈”、“反对派遣保安队到朝鲜作战”等决议，并在下午举行了示威游行。
