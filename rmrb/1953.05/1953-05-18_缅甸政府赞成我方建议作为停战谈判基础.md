Title: Burmese Government Endorses Our Proposal as Basis for Armistice Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1953-05-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, May 16th: According to a France-Presse report from Rangoon on the 16th, a Burmese government spokesperson stated that the Burmese government endorses the armistice proposal put forward by the Chinese and North Korean delegations in Panmunjom. He further stated that this proposal should serve as the basis for negotiations in order to achieve a swift resolution for the benefit of world peace.

### 缅甸政府赞成我方建议作为停战谈判基础

1953-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府赞成我方建议作为停战谈判基础
    【新华社十七日讯】据法新社仰光十六日消息：缅甸政府发言人说，缅甸政府赞成中国和北朝鲜代表团在板门店提出的停战建议。他又说，这个建议应成为谈判的基础，以便为了世界和平的利益求得迅速解决。
