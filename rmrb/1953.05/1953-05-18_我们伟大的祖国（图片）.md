    Our Great Motherland
    The newly built East China Workers' Taihu Sanatorium is located on Dushan Island in Taihu Lake, Wuxi, surrounded by water on three sides, making it a great place for recuperation. More than one hundred and fifty worker comrades have spent their vacation here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新建的华东工人太湖疗养所，在无锡太湖中独山上，三面环水，是一个疗养的好地方。有一百五十几位工人同志在这里度过了休养的假期。
