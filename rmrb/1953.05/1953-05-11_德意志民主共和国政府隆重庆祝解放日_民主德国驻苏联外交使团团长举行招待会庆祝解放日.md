Title: The Government of the German Democratic Republic Solemnly Celebrates Liberation Day; Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union Holds Reception to Celebrate Liberation Day
Author:
Date: 1953-05-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 10th: The government and people of the German Democratic Republic solemnly commemorate the eighth anniversary of Germany's liberation from Fascism.

According to TASS, Berlin: The government of the German Democratic Republic held a celebration meeting in Berlin on the 8th. The meeting was attended by Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Ulbricht, Deputy Prime Minister, Dieckmann, Chairman of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, heads of various departments of the Republic's government, and members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. Representatives of the Soviet Control Commission in Germany and diplomatic envoys from various countries to the German Democratic Republic also attended the celebration meeting.

Louis Leopold Stiedl, Minister of Health of the German Democratic Republic, delivered a report at the meeting. Stiedl emphasized the tremendous help the Soviet Union has given to the German people in their struggle for a peaceful solution to the German problem and for the establishment of a unified, democratic, independent, and peace-loving Germany. Stiedl said: All German patriots are striving to strengthen their friendship with the Soviet people and protect this great friendship as they would protect the apple of their eye.

At the meeting, it was also announced that the Karl Marx Order would be awarded to Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, and Ulbricht, General Secretary of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic.

According to the German News Agency: On the morning of the 8th, government leaders headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl, General Secretary of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany Ulbricht, members of the Politburo of the Party Central Committee, and Dieckmann, Chairman of the People's Chamber and representing President Pieck, along with members of parliament, laid wreaths at the Soviet Army Memorial in Treptow Park in Berlin. Behind them was a long line of people from various districts of Berlin.

Thousands of West Berlin residents, disregarding police obstruction, also laid wreaths at the Soviet Army Memorial in the Berlin Zoo (British sector).

Xinhua News Agency, May 10th: TASS, Moscow, May 9th: Rudolf Appelt, Ambassador-level Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union, held a reception in Moscow on the 8th to celebrate the German Liberation Day.

Those present at the reception included: V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, A.I. Mikoyan, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Domestic and Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, M.P. Tarasov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, V.A. Malyshev, Minister of Transport and Heavy Machine Building Industry, A.F. Tretyakov, Minister of Health of the Soviet Union, N.M. Pegov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union S.M. Budyonny, I.A. Kairov, Minister of Education of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, M.A. Yasnov, Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet, Air Force Marshal S.F. Zhavoronkov, Admiral G.I. Levchenko, senior officials from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Domestic and Foreign Trade, and the Ministry of Defense, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Moscow.

### 德意志民主共和国政府隆重庆祝解放日  民主德国驻苏联外交使团团长举行招待会庆祝解放日

1953-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国政府隆重庆祝解放日
    民主德国驻苏联外交使团团长举行招待会庆祝解放日
    【新华社十日讯】德意志民主共和国政府与人民隆重纪念德国从法西斯主义下解放八周年。
    据塔斯社柏林讯：德意志民主共和国政府八日在柏林举行了庆祝大会。出席大会的有德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥、副总理乌布利希、德意志民主共和国人民议院主席狄克曼、共和国政府各部门首长和德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员们。苏联对德管制委员会的代表和各国驻德意志民主共和国的外交使节也参加了庆祝大会。
    德意志民主共和国卫生部长路易波德·史泰德尔在大会上作了报告。史泰德尔着重指出在德国人民争取和平解决德国问题，争取建立统一、民主、独立与爱好和平的德国的斗争中，苏联给予德国人民巨大的帮助。史泰德尔说：德国一切爱国者正努力加强与苏联人民的友谊，并且像保卫眼珠一样来保卫这种伟大的友谊。
    会上并宣布以卡尔·马克思勋章授给德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克，德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥和德国统一社会党总书记、德意志民主共和国副总理乌布利希。
    据德意志通讯社报道：以格罗提渥总理为首的政府首长们，德国统一社会党总书记乌布利希及党中央委员会政治局的委员们和代表皮克总统的人民议院主席狄克曼及议员们，在八日早晨到柏林特莱普托夫公园中的苏军纪念碑前献花圈。他们的后面是一长列来自柏林各区的人民。
    成千的西柏林居民不顾警察的阻拦，也向柏林动物园（英占区）中的苏军纪念碑献花圈。
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：德意志民主共和国驻苏联外交使团大使衔团长鲁道夫·阿佩尔特八日在莫斯科举行招待会，庆祝德国解放日。
    出席招待会的有：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫，苏联部长会议副主席兼苏联国内和对外贸易部部长阿·伊·米高扬，俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席麦·普·塔拉索夫，运输和重型机器制造工业部部长维·亚·马利歇夫，苏联卫生部部长安·费·特雷季雅科夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团书记纳·麦·别哥夫，苏联元帅谢·米·布琼尼，俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国教育部部长伊·阿·凯罗夫，莫斯科市苏维埃主席米·阿·雅斯诺夫，空军元帅斯·弗·热沃隆科夫，海军上将格·伊·列夫钦科，苏联外交部、国内和对外贸易部和国防部的高级官员以及各国驻莫斯科的使节。
