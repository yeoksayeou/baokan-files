Title: Invited to Attend the May Day Celebration and the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions - List of Trade Union Representatives from Poland, India, Indonesia, France, Mongolia, and Japan
Author:
Date: 1953-05-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Polish Trade Union Delegation, the All-India Trade Union Congress Delegation, the Indonesian Central Trade Union Federation Delegation, the French General Confederation of Labour representatives, and the Mongolian Trade Union Delegation, who came to participate in the "May 1st" International Labor Day and the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, have arrived in Beijing successively in recent days.
    Polish Trade Union Delegation List:
    Head: Pa. Wojtas - Vice Chairman of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions
    Member: He. Kowalski - Vice Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Polish Foundry Workers' Union
    Translator: Ke. Dengbikaya
    All-India Trade Union Congress Delegation:
    Head: Yusuf - Vice President of the All-India Trade Union Congress
    Member: Vital Rao - Chairman of the Hyderabad Postal and Telegraph Workers' Union of India, one of the secretaries of the Singareni Coal Mine Workers' Union of India
    Indonesian Central Trade Union Federation Delegation:
    Head: Zhu Yiduo - First Secretary of the Organization Department of the All-Indonesia Central Trade Union Federation, Member of Parliament
    Deputy Head: Suharjo - Secretary of the Culture and Sports Department of the Indonesian Central Trade Union Federation
    Member: Dadu - Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Indonesian Central Trade Union Federation
       Haryo - First Secretary of the Indonesian Regional Trade Union
       Bardi - First Secretary of the Surabaya Branch of the Indonesian Central Trade Union Federation
       Hanabi - Chairman of the Indonesian Aviation Union
    Representative of the French General Confederation of Labour: Dufriche - Administrative Committee Member of the French General Confederation of Labour
    Mongolian Trade Union Delegation List:
    Head: Norovsambuu - Chairman of the Central Council of Mongolian Trade Unions
    Member: Balgan - Technician of the Ulaanbaatar Wood Factory, National Shock Team Member
    Translator: Khosbayar
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions invited Japanese trade union representatives Kaneko Kenta, Ozawa Kiyoshi, Kojima Hiroki, and Ogura Kinkichi to participate in this year's "May 1st" International Labor Day and the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions in Beijing. Kaneko Kenta is the Secretary-General of the Japan Liaison Office of the World Federation of Trade Unions and the representative of the Japanese Trade Union to the Asia-Pacific Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions. Ozawa Kiyoshi is a Central Committee Member of the Tokyo Civil Engineering and Construction General Union. Kojima Hiroki is a Central Committee Member of the National Procurement Agency Employees' Union of the Government Employees' Union Council. Ogura Kinkichi is a metalworker. They formed the Japanese Trade Union Delegation headed by Kaneko Kenta and were invited to attend the "May 1st" Labor Day and the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions.

    据新华社讯：中华全国总工会邀请日本工会代表金子健太、小泽清、儿岛博基、小仓金吉四人，参加今年在北京举行的“五一”国际劳动节和中国工会第七次全国代表大会。金子健太是世界工会联合会日本联络事务局干事长，日本工会驻世界工联亚澳联络局代表；小泽清为东京土木建筑一般工会中央委员；儿岛博基为官厅工会协议会全国调达厅职工会中央委员；小仓金吉为五金工人。他们组成以金子健太为首的日本工会代表团，应邀出席“五一”劳动节和中国工会第七次全国代表大会。
