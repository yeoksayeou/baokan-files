Title: China Red Cross Delegation to Geneva
Author:
Date: 1953-05-15
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, May 13th: The 22nd Session of the Board of Governors Executive Committee of the League of Red Cross Societies is scheduled to be held in Geneva, Switzerland on the 19th of this month. The Chinese Red Cross delegation, led by its President Li Dequan, departed from Beijing by plane on May 8th for Geneva to attend the meeting.

### 我国红十字会代表团赴日内瓦

1953-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我国红十字会代表团赴日内瓦
    【新华社十三日讯】红十字会协会第二十二届理事会执行委员会本年度第一次常会定于本月十九日在瑞士日内瓦召开。中国红十字会代表团由该会李德全会长率领于五月八日自北京乘飞机启程前往日内瓦出席会议。
