Title: The U.S. Side Has Again Murdered One of Our Captured Personnel

Author:

Date: 1953-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 23rd. On May 20th, in the U.S. side's Koje Island prisoner-of-war camp, another captured soldier of the Korean People's Army was murdered by the U.S. side. This prisoner of war was hanged. As usual, the U.S. side's communique attributed this atrocity to the prisoners of war.

### 美方又谋害我方被俘人员一名

1953-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    美方又谋害我方被俘人员一名
    【新华社开城二十三日电】五月二十日在美方巨济岛战俘营中又有朝鲜人民军被俘人员一名被美方谋害。这名战俘是被绞死的。美方的公报照例把这一暴行推说是战俘干的。
