Title: Communiqué of the Korean Armistice Negotiation Delegation of the Korean and Chinese Side
Author:
Date: 1953-05-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, May 9th. The Korean and Chinese side delegation to the Korean armistice negotiations issued the following communiqué on May 9th:
    At the plenary meeting of both delegations on May 9th, the other side raised several questions about the new proposal put forward by our side on May 7th, requesting our side to explain. The other side raised several questions regarding our side's proposal that the handling of prisoners of war not repatriated after being taken over by the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission for four months be decided through consultation by a higher-level political conference, and the specific arrangements for the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission in performing its legal duties and responsibilities.
    Our chief representative, General Nam Il, stated at the meeting that our side had taken note of the questions raised by the other side and suggested that the plenary meeting of both delegations adjourn until 11:00 a.m. on May 10th to continue. The other side agreed.

### 朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团发表公报

1953-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团发表公报
    【新华社开城九日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团五月九日发表公报如下：
    五月九日的双方代表团大会上，对方对我方在五月七日提出的新方案提出了若干问题，要求我方加以说明。对方对于经中立国遣返委员会收管四个月后，尚未被遣返之战俘的处理办法交由高一级政治会议协商决定的我方建议，及中立国遣返委员会在执行其合法职务和责任中的具体安排问题，提出若干询问。
    我方首席代表南日大将在会上表示我方已注意到对方所提出的问题，建议双方代表团大会休会至五月十日上午十一时继续举行。对方表示同意。
