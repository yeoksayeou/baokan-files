Title: Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions Continues

Author: 
Date: 1953-05-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 4th: The Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions continued on the 4th. All the delegates attending the conference held group discussions on the congratulatory message from Liu Shaoqi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, to the congress, the report on the work of the Chinese Trade Unions by Lai Ruoyu, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the report on the revision of the Constitution of the Chinese Trade Unions by Xu Zhizhen, Minister of the Organization Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and the report on the financial work of the trade unions by Li Zaiwen, Director of the General Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
    In the evening, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions hosted a performance by the China Acrobatic Troupe for the delegates.
    The conference will continue on the 5th.

### 中国工会第七次全国代表大会继续进行

1953-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中国工会第七次全国代表大会继续进行
    【新华社四日讯】中国工会第七次全国代表大会四日继续进行。出席大会的全体代表分组讨论了中国共产党中央委员会书记刘少奇在这次大会上的祝词、中华全国总工会秘书长赖若愚的关于中国工会工作的报告、中华全国总工会组织部部长许之桢的关于修改中国工会章程的报告和中华全国总工会办公厅主任栗再温的关于工会财务工作的报告。
    晚间，中华全国总工会招待代表们观看中国杂技团表演。
    大会将于五日继续开会。
