Title: Celebrating the Eighth Anniversary of Germany's Liberation: German Diplomatic Mission Chief in China, Koenigs, Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1953-05-09
Page: 1
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, May 8th: Koenigs, head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China, and his wife, held a reception in Beijing on the afternoon of May 8th to celebrate the German Liberation Day. Invited to attend were: Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture; Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; responsible persons from various departments of the Central People's Government; responsible persons from various people's organizations; combat heroes; labor models; and figures from the cultural circles of the capital, totaling more than 200 people. Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries stationed in China. Lange, head of the delegation of the Industrial Exhibition of the German Democratic Republic, and members of the delegation also attended the reception. The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. To celebrate the eighth anniversary of the German Liberation Day, Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, sent a telegram to Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic, expressing congratulations.

### 庆祝德国解放八周年  德驻华外交使团团长柯尼希举行招待会

1953-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝德国解放八周年
    德驻华外交使团团长柯尼希举行招待会
    【新华社八日讯】德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团团长柯尼希及夫人于五月八日下午在北京举行招待会，庆祝德国解放日。应邀出席的有：中央人民政府政务院副总理董必武，外交部副部长伍修权，文化部副部长丁西林，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，人民革命军事委员会办公厅主任萧向荣，中央人民政府各部门负责人，各人民团体负责人，战斗英雄，劳动模范和首都文化界人士共二百多人。应邀出席的还有各国驻我国外交使节和使馆人员。德意志民主共和国工业展览会代表团团长朗格和团员也出席了招待会。招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。为庆祝德国解放八周年纪念日，中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来特致电德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥表示祝贺。
