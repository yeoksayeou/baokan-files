Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-12-19
Edition: 1

	The parties and peoples of China and Albania have forged a deep revolutionary friendship in the course of their socialist revolutions and socialist construction, as well as in their struggle against imperialism and modern revisionism. This friendship is founded on the principles of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, and it is eternal and unbreakable.

		In a telegram to Comrade Enver Hoxha on the occasion of the 22nd anniversary of Albania's liberation

### 毛主席语录

1968-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    中阿两党、两国人民，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设事业中，在反对帝国主义和现代修正主义的斗争中，结成了深厚的革命友谊。这种友谊是建立在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义原则基础上的，是永恒的、牢不可破的。
              在阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年时给霍查同志的贺电
