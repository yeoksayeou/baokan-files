Palestinian Guerrillas Frequently Attack Enemies
Over 400 Israeli Invaders Annihilated in First Ten Days of December

According to a report from the New China News Agency in Damascus on the 14th, the "Fatah" (Palestine National Liberation Movement) and other Palestinian nationalist organizations issued a battle report here stating that in the first ten days of December, Palestinian guerrillas launched over 30 attacks against Israeli invaders, killing and wounding over 400 Israeli soldiers.

On the 10th, the "Storm" assault team of "Fatah" blew up a section of the Kafr Qasim-Tulkarm railway on the West Bank of the Jordan River. At that time, an Israeli military train carrying Israeli soldiers and ammunition was passing through, and seven carriages were destroyed, while the other carriages derailed. Palestinian assault teams ambushed on both sides of the railway immediately attacked the Israeli soldiers with machine guns and hand grenades, killing and wounding about 100 Israeli soldiers, setting fire to all the carriages, and then safely returning to their base.

During this period, Palestinian assault teams annihilated Israeli ambushes, ambushed Israeli patrols, destroyed some Israeli military camps and observation posts, and blew up some bridges. Palestinian assault teams also destroyed an Israeli tank, twenty military vehicles, and an Israeli factory.

Palestinian guerrillas have heavily struck the U.S. imperialists and their tool of aggression, Israel, achieving one victory after another.

### 巴勒斯坦游击队频繁袭击敌人  十二月头十天歼灭以侵略军四百多

1968-12-21
第6版()
专栏：

    巴勒斯坦游击队频繁袭击敌人
    十二月头十天歼灭以侵略军四百多
    据新华社大马士革十四日电　据“法塔赫”（巴勒斯坦民族解放运动）和其他巴勒斯坦民族主义组织在这里发表的战报，巴勒斯坦游击队在十二月份的头十天里对以色列侵略者发动了三十多次袭击，打死打伤四百多名以色列侵略军。
    十日，“法塔赫”所属的“暴风”突击队炸毁了约旦河西岸卡库恩—图尔卡姆铁路的一段，当时正有运载以色列军队和弹药的以色列军用列车经过，七节车厢被炸毁，其它车厢出轨。埋伏在铁路两旁的巴勒斯坦突击队立即用机枪和手榴弹袭击以色列军队，打死打伤大约一百名以色列官兵，放火烧了所有的车厢，然后安全返回基地。
    在这期间，巴勒斯坦突击队歼灭了以色列的伏兵，伏击了以色列巡逻队，摧毁了以色列的一些军营和观察哨，炸毁了一些桥梁。巴勒斯坦突击队还击毁了以色列的一辆坦克、二十辆军车以及一座以色列工厂。（附图片）
    巴勒斯坦游击队沉重地打击了美帝及其侵略工具以色列，取得了一个又一个的胜利。
                                新华社发
