Title: Comrade Guo Yonghuai Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1968-12-13
Edition: 4

Comrade Guo Yonghuai, a member of the Communist Party of China and a representative of the National People's Congress, died in an unfortunate accident at the age of 59.

Comrade Guo Yonghuai made significant contributions in the field of science and technology.

### 郭永怀同志逝世

1968-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    郭永怀同志逝世
    新华社十一日讯　中国共产党党员、全国人民代表大会代表郭永怀同志，因不幸事故牺牲，终年五十九岁。
    郭永怀同志在从事科学技术工作中，做出了贡献。
