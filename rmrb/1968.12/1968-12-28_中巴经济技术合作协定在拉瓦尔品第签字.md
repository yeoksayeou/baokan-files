Title: Sino-Pakistan Economic and Technical Cooperation Agreement Signed in Rawalpindi
Author:
Date: 1968-12-28
Edition: 4

The economic and technical cooperation agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan was signed in Rawalpindi on the 26th. The agreement stipulates that the Chinese government will provide the Pakistani government with an interest-free and unconditional loan of 100 million yuan to supply complete sets of equipment, individual items of equipment, general goods, and technical assistance.

The agreement was signed by: Lin Haiyun, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Chinese government, and I. A. Khan, Secretary of the Pakistani government.

Those present at the signing ceremony included Rashid, Deputy Secretary of the Economic Affairs Bureau of the Pakistani Presidential Secretariat, and others. Xu Ying, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan, and members of the Chinese government trade delegation also attended the signing ceremony. After the agreement was signed, the representatives of the two governments shook hands warmly, congratulating each other on the further growth of Sino-Pakistani friendship and cooperation.

After the ceremony, I. A. Khan hosted a luncheon for the Chinese government trade delegation led by Lin Haiyun.

On the evening of the 24th, Pakistani Commerce Minister Abdul Ghaffar Khan held a banquet to welcome the Chinese government trade delegation.

Ghaffar Khan said in his speech, "The Pakistani people greatly admire the talented and industrious Chinese people and praise the tremendous progress made by the Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong."

He said that the relationship between China and Pakistan, built on the existing deep friendship, will become increasingly solid in the future, which will be beneficial to the people of the two countries and the cause of world peace.

At the end of his speech, he wished Chairman Mao, the great leader of the Chinese people, long life.

The Chinese government trade delegation conducted visits on the 24th and 25th and was warmly welcomed by the Pakistani people.

### 中巴经济技术合作协定在拉瓦尔品第签字

1968-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    中巴经济技术合作协定在拉瓦尔品第签字
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十六日电  中华人民共和国政府和巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国政府经济技术合作协定，二十六日在拉瓦尔品第签字。协定规定，中国政府将给予巴基斯坦政府以无息的和不附带任何条件的贷款人民币一亿元，以提供成套设备、单项设备、一般商品和技术援助。
    在协定上签字的是：中国政府全权代表、对外贸易部代理部长林海云，巴基斯坦政府全权代表、巴基斯坦政府秘书伊·阿·汗。
    出席签字仪式的有巴基斯坦总统秘书厅经济事务局副秘书拉希德等。中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英和中国政府贸易代表团团员也出席了签字仪式。在签订协定后，两国政府代表热烈握手，祝贺中巴两国友谊和合作的进一步增长。
    在举行仪式后，伊·阿·汗为林海云率领的中国政府贸易代表团举行午宴。
    二十四日晚上，巴基斯坦商业部长阿卜杜勒·加福尔汗举行宴会，招待中国政府贸易代表团。
    加福尔汗在宴会上讲话说：“巴基斯坦人民非常钦佩天才的、勤劳的中国人民，赞扬中国人民在毛泽东主席领导下所取得的巨大进步。”
    他说，中国和巴基斯坦“两国之间在现有的深厚友谊的联系的基础上建立的关系，今后将越来越巩固，这将有利于我们两国人民和世界和平事业。”
    他在讲话结束时祝中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆。
    中国政府贸易代表团二十四日和二十五日进行了参观活动，他们受到巴基斯坦人民的热烈欢迎。
