Title: Comrade Baluku and Comrade Huang Yongsheng Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1968-12-04
Edition: 1

	Comrade Bekir Balluku, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Defense of the People's Republic of Albania, and Comrade Huang Yongsheng, Head of the Chinese Party, Government, and Military Delegation and Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, held talks in Tirana on December 2.

	Those who participated in the talks from the Albanian side included: General Staff Chief of the Albanian People's Army Petrit Dumit, Director of the Political Department of the People's Army Xhit Çako, responsible comrades of the Ministry of Defense, and Military Attaché of the Albanian People's Republic Embassy in China, Afim Hakan.

	Those who participated in the talks from the Chinese side included: Vice Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy Wu Ruilin, Vice Commander of the Air Force Qian Renong, and Military Attaché of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Albania, Li Zhongxuan.

	The talks were conducted in an extremely friendly and cordial atmosphere.

### 巴卢库同志和黄永胜同志举行会谈

1968-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    巴卢库同志和黄永胜同志举行会谈
    新华社地拉那二日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库同志和中国党政军代表团团长、中国人民解放军总参谋长黄永胜同志，十二月二日在地拉那举行了会谈。
    阿尔巴尼亚方面参加会谈的有：阿尔巴尼亚人民军总参谋长彼得里特·杜米，人民军政治部主任希托·恰科，国防部负责同志和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻中国大使馆武官阿弗尼·哈卡尼等同志。
    中国方面参加会谈的有：中国人民解放军海军副司令员吴瑞林，空军副司令员邝任农和中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆武官李钟玄等同志。会谈是在极其亲切友好的气氛中进行的。
