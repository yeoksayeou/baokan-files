Title: Liaoyuan Mining Bureau Completes Annual Coal Production Plan Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1968-12-23
Edition: 4

Inspired by the communiqué of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the broad masses of workers at the Liaoyuan Mining Bureau in Jilin Province have deeply studied the history of the struggle between the two lines, continuously raised their awareness of class struggle and the struggle between the two lines, earnestly implemented Chairman Mao's great policy of "grasp revolution, promote production," and promoted the continuous development of production. The entire bureau has completed the annual original coal production plan 15 days ahead of schedule and the annual tunneling plan 19 days ahead of schedule.

### 辽源矿务局提前完成全年产煤计划

1968-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

  　辽源矿务局提前完成全年产煤计划
    在党的八届扩大的十二中全会公报的鼓舞下，吉林省辽源矿务局广大职工，深入学习两条路线斗争的历史，不断提高阶级斗争和两条路线斗争觉悟，认真贯彻毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针，促进生产不断发展，全局已提前十五天超额完成了全年原煤生产计划，提前十九天完成了全年掘进计划。
