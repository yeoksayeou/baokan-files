Title: “We Also Have Two Hands, and We Won’t Eat Idly in the Cities!”
Author:
Date: 1968-12-25
Edition: 1

The single-volume edition of “We Also Have Two Hands, and We Won’t Eat Idly in the Cities!” has been published. The New China News Agency reported on the 24th that the important editorial note from the People’s Daily, which conveyed the latest instructions of our great leader Chairman Mao, and the report on the urban residents of Haining County, Gansu Province, rushing to the front lines of agricultural production have been published as a single-volume edition by the People’s Publishing House. The book, titled “We Also Have Two Hands, and We Won’t Eat Idly in the Cities!”, will begin circulating in the Beijing New China Bookstore starting December 24th and will be distributed nationwide in the near future.

### 《“我们也有两只手，不在城市里吃闲饭！”》出版单行本

1968-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    《“我们也有两只手，不在城市里吃闲饭！”》出版单行本
    新华社二十四日讯　传达了伟大领袖毛主席最新指示的《人民日报》重要编者按和关于甘肃省会宁县部分城镇居民奔赴农业生产第一线的报道，已由人民出版社出版单行本，书名为《“我们也有两只手，不在城市里吃闲饭！”》。这本书自十二月二十四日开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地陆续发行。
