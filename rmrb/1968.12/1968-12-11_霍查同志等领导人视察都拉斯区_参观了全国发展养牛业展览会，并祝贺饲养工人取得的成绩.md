Title: Comrade Enver Hoxha and Other Leaders Inspect the Durres Region and Visit the National Livestock Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1968-12-11
Edition: 5

Tirana, Dec. 9 (New China News Agency) — According to a report by the Albanian News Agency on Dec. 7, the great leader of the Albanian people, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour Enver Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Mehmet Shehu, and member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour Hysni Kapo, accompanied by the First Secretary of the Durres Regional Party Committee Rita Marko, inspected the Durres region.

The leaders of the Albanian Party and state first visited the national livestock exhibition held in Shijak. The exhibition clearly demonstrated Albania's rich experience and achievements in livestock breeding, as well as the great potential for developing this sector.

Among the high-yielding cows on display, a cow from the "Georgi Dimitrov" state farm produced 9,550 liters of milk annually, and a cow from the "Kryeministri" state farm produced 9,800 liters of milk annually.

The Party and state leaders congratulated the breeders on their achievements and wished for more cows to reach such high yields. The leaders also expressed concern about the mechanization of the operational processes.

Comrade Enver Hoxha, along with Shehu, Kapo, and others, then visited the livestock farm in the central area of the "November 8" state farm in Sukth and the dairy farm in the Sarmane-Jafzo region of the Shijak agricultural cooperative. They also inspected the workers' dormitories of this cooperative.

The Albanian Party and state leaders congratulated the outstanding work of these advanced units to the workers and managers.

Accompanying the Albanian Party and state leaders on the inspection were Minister of Agriculture Dodi Biba, Chairman of the Durres Regional People's Assembly Executive Committee Reka, and other comrades.

### 霍查同志等领导人视察都拉斯区  参观了全国发展养牛业展览会，并祝贺饲养工人取得的成绩

1968-12-11
第5版()
专栏：

    霍查同志等领导人视察都拉斯区
    参观了全国发展养牛业展览会，并祝贺饲养工人取得的成绩
    新华社地拉那九日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社七日报道，阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志，以及部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡和劳动党中央政治局委员、书记处书记希斯尼·卡博等同志，在劳动党都拉斯区委员会第一书记里塔·马尔科同志的陪同下，视察了都拉斯区。
    阿尔巴尼亚党和国家领导人首先参观了正在希雅克举行的全国发展养牛业展览会。这个展览会清楚地显示了阿尔巴尼亚在养牛业方面所取得的丰富经验和成就，以及发展养牛业的巨大潜力。
    在展览会展出的高产奶牛中，“格奥尔基·季米特洛夫”国营农场的一头奶牛年产奶九千五百五十升，“克利里米”国营农场的一头奶牛，年产奶九千八百升。
    党和国家领导人在参观时祝贺饲养工人所取得的成绩，并祝他们使更多的牛也能达到这种高产纪录。党和国家领导人还对操作过程机械化等方面表示了关切。
    霍查同志以及谢胡、卡博等同志接着参观了苏克西“十一月八日”国营农场中心地区的牲畜场和希雅克农业合作社萨尔莫内—贾夫佐太地区的奶牛场，并参观了这个合作社的工人宿舍。
    阿尔巴尼亚党和国家领导人为这些先进单位出色的工作向工人和管理人员表示了祝贺。
    陪同阿尔巴尼亚党和国家领导人进行视察的，还有农业部长多德比巴，都拉斯区人民会议执行委员会主席雷卡等同志。
