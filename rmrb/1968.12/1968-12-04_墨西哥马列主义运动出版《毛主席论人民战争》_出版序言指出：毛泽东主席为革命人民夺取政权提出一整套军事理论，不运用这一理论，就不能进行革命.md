Title: The Mexican Marxist-Leninist Movement Publishes "Mao Zedong on People's War"
Author:
Date: 1968-12-04
Edition: 6

The Mexican Marxist-Leninist Movement recently published and distributed the Spanish-language edition of "Mao Zedong on People's War."

The Mexican Marxist-Leninist Movement wrote a preface for the publication of this book. The preface states: The current national contradictions and class contradictions in Mexico can only be resolved through a new type of revolution. The preface states: This revolution will "establish a people's democratic government led by the working class, laying the foundation for the development of socialism. This revolution, as proposed by the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time, Mao Zedong, is a new democratic revolution."

The preface states: "This great movement also indicates the strategy to be adopted in the new revolutionary process, namely, it is necessary to build a revolutionary party guided by contemporary Marxism-Leninism—Mao Zedong Thought, to build a broad national united front of the people under the leadership of the proletariat, and to wage a people's war."

The preface continues: "Victorious armed struggle cannot be carried out in cities where the enemy is much stronger, but must establish a solid base in the countryside, strike the enemy in places where his rule is weakest, rely on revolutionary peasants to build a powerful people's army, carry out land revolution, wage guerrilla warfare, first seize the countryside, encircle the cities, and then take the cities."

The preface emphasizes: "The recent experience of armed struggle in our country and many other Latin American countries proves that it is not enough for a small group of honest, resolute, and brave people to take up arms in the mountains to achieve revolutionary victory, but it is necessary to implement such Marxist-Leninist principles: The people are the creators of history; weapons are important factors, but the decisive factor is people; revolutionary war is a war of the masses, and only by mobilizing the masses can war be waged, and only by relying on the masses can war be waged."

The preface states: "Mao Zedong has put forward a complete set of military theories for the revolutionary people to seize political power; without applying this theory, one cannot carry out a revolution. Without Marxism-Leninism, Mao Zedong Thought, there will be no socialism in any country, now or in the future."

### 墨西哥马列主义运动出版《毛主席论人民战争》  出版序言指出：毛泽东主席为革命人民夺取政权提出一整套军事理论，不运用这一理论，就不能进行革命

1968-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

    墨西哥马列主义运动出版《毛主席论人民战争》
    出版序言指出：毛泽东主席为革命人民夺取政权提出一整套军事理论，不运用这一理论，就不能进行革命
    新华社三日讯　墨西哥城消息：墨西哥马克思列宁主义运动不久前出版发行了《毛主席论人民战争》西班牙文版。
    墨西哥马克思列宁主义运动为出版这本书写了序言。序言说：墨西哥当前存在的民族矛盾和国内阶级矛盾，只有通过一场新型的革命才能解决。序言说：这一革命将“建立一个由工人阶级领导的人民民主政府，为以后发展社会主义奠定基础。这个革命，正象当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者毛泽东主席所提出的那样，是一个新民主主义革命。”
    序言说：“这一伟大运动还表明了在新的革命过程中所应采取的战略，即必须建立一个以当代马克思列宁主义——毛泽东思想为指导的革命党，必须建立一个在无产阶级领导之下的全国人民的广泛的统一战线，必须进行人民战争。”
    序言接着说：“胜利的武装斗争不能在敌人远为强大的城市开展，而必须在农村建立牢固的根据地，在敌人统治最薄弱的地方打击敌人，必须依靠革命农民建立一支强大的人民军队，进行土地革命，开展游击战争，首先占领农村，包围城市，然后夺取城市。”
    序言强调指出：“我国和拉丁美洲其他许多国家武装斗争最近的经验证明，光是一小股正直的、坚决勇敢的人在山上拿起武器，不足以取得革命的胜利，而必须执行这样一些马克思列宁主义原则：人民是创造历史的动力；武器是重要的因素，但决定的因素是人；革命战争是群众的战争，只有动员群众才能进行战争，只有依靠群众才能进行战争。”
    序言说：“毛泽东主席为革命人民夺取政权提出了一整套军事理论；不运用这一理论，就不能进行革命。没有马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想，在任何国家，现在和将来都不会有社会主义。”（附图片）
