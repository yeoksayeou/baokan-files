Title: Standing on the Snowy Mountains Looking at Beijing, a Red Sun Rises in the Heart
Author:
Date: 1968-12-19
Edition: 6

	“Standing on the snowy mountains looking at Beijing, a red sun rises in the heart.” The soldiers and officers of the 10th Company of a certain engineering unit of the People's Liberation Army, who are toiling away in the high-altitude snowy plateau, use their rest time during construction to diligently study the works of Chairman Mao.

		Photograph by our correspondent
