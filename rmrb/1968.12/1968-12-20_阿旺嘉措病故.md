Title: Awang Qigiao Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1968-12-20
Edition: 3

National People's Congress Representative and National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Standing Committee Member Awang Qigiao passed away at 8:45 AM on December 16 in Beijing due to heart disease. Awang Qigiao, a native of Garzê, Sichuan, of Tibetan ethnicity, was 74 years old.

### 阿旺嘉措病故

1968-12-20
第3版()
专栏：

    阿旺嘉措病故
    全国人民代表大会代表、全国政协常务委员阿旺嘉措患心脏病于十二月十六日八时四十五分在北京逝世。阿旺嘉措，四川甘孜人，藏族，终年七十四岁。
