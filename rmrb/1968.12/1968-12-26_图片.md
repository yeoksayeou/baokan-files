Title: Youth from Shaanxi's Weibei Coal Mine School for Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers Study Chairman Mao's Latest Instructions
Author:
Date: 1968-12-26
Edition: 4

Youth from the Shaanxi Weibei Coal Mine School for Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers, with the help of workers, are further implementing Chairman Mao's latest instructions and resolutely taking the revolutionary path of integrating with the workers and peasants. The photo shows knowledge youth who have settled in Jin Suo Commune studying Chairman Mao's quotations together with an old poor peasant.
