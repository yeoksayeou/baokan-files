Title: Mozambique Guerrillas Strike Hard at the Enemy
Author:
Date: 1968-12-10
Edition: 6

Mozambique guerrillas have dealt heavy blows to the enemy.

New Xinhua News Agency, Dar es Salaam, December 8th - According to a battle report issued by the Patriotic Armed Forces of Mozambique on the 7th, from early September to mid-October, the Mozambique guerrillas fought the Portuguese colonial army eleven times in the provinces of Delgado and Niassa. They killed seventy-one Portuguese soldiers, wounded many, and destroyed five enemy military vehicles. The report states that during this period, the Mozambique guerrillas intensified their attacks on the Portuguese colonial army. They launched a series of mine wars, ambushes, and assaults on enemy positions.

Mozambique guerrilla fighters are actively engaged in military training to more effectively strike and eliminate the enemy.

New Xinhua News Agency Report

### 埋地雷　打伏击　袭阵地  莫三鼻给游击队狠狠打击敌人

1968-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

    埋地雷　打伏击　袭阵地
    莫三鼻给游击队狠狠打击敌人
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆八日电　据莫三鼻给爱国武装力量七日在这里发表的战报，莫三鼻给游击队从九月初到十月中旬，在德尔加多角省和尼亚萨省同葡萄牙殖民军战斗十一次，共打死七十一名葡萄牙士兵，打伤多人，击毁五辆敌人军用车。战报说，在此期间，莫三鼻给游击队加强了对葡萄牙殖民军的袭击。他们发动了一系列的地雷战、伏击战，并袭击了敌人的阵地。（附图片）
    莫三鼻给游击战士为了更有效地打击敌人和消灭敌人，积极进行军事训练。
      新华社稿
