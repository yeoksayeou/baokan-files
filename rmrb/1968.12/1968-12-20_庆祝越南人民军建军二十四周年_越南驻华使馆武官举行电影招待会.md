Title: Celebrating the 24th Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnam People's Army
Author:
Date: 1968-12-20
Edition: 5

Vietnamese Ambassador to China's Military Attaché Holds Film Screening

New China News Agency, December 19th: Colonel Chen Wenpeng, Military Attaché of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's Embassy in China, held a film screening tonight to celebrate the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Vietnam People's Army. Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wang Xinting, Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department, Han Zhenji, and other responsible officials from various military branches and relevant departments were invited to attend the film screening.

Nguyen Van Quang, Head of the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, was also invited to attend the film screening.

The screening featured a documentary film depicting the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese military and people against American aggressors.

Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Vu Minh Quan, also attended the screening.

December 19th, the capital held a grand meeting to celebrate the 8th anniversary of the founding of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam.

### 庆祝越南人民军建军二十四周年  越南驻华使馆武官举行电影招待会

1968-12-20
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南人民军建军二十四周年
    越南驻华使馆武官举行电影招待会
    新华社十九日讯　越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆武官陈文彭上校，为庆祝越南人民军建军二十四周年，今晚在这里举行电影招待会。中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭、总后勤部副部长韩振纪以及各军种和有关方面负责人，应邀出席了电影招待会。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广也应邀出席了电影招待会。
    招待会上，放映了反映越南军民英勇抗击美国侵略者的纪录影片。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾也出席了招待会。（附图片）
    十二月十九日首都庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立八周年大会会场。　　　新华社记者摄
