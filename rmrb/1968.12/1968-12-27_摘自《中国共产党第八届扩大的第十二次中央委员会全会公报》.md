Title: Resolution of the Enlarged Twelfth Plenum of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1968-12-27
Edition: 6

Under the leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong, the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people adhere to the principles and policies of proletarian internationalism. They stand firmly with the genuine Marxist-Leninists of all countries, resolutely support the heroic Albanian people in their struggle against imperialism and revisionism, resolutely support the heroic Vietnamese people in carrying the war of resistance against American aggression and for national salvation to the end, and resolutely support the just struggles of the peoples of all countries against imperialism, revisionism, and the reactionaries of their own countries.
