Title: Celebrating the 8th Anniversary of the Establishment of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-12-19
Edition: 5

The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Vietnam Friendship Association held a film screening event in Beijing today to celebrate the 8th anniversary of the establishment of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam. The event featured a film from South Vietnam that showcased the brave resistance of the South Vietnamese people against American aggressors.

The head of the permanent representative office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, Nguyen Van Quang, the Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Vu Minh Quan, and other Vietnamese comrades in Beijing were invited to attend the film screening.

Relevant officials from our country, including Han Nianlong, Ding Xilin, Pan Zhenwu, and leaders from the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, along with over a thousand revolutionary masses from the capital, attended the film screening.

### 庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立八周年  我对外友协和中越友协举行电影招待会

1968-12-19
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立八周年
    我对外友协和中越友协举行电影招待会
    新华社十八日讯　对外友协和中越友协今天在北京举行电影招待会，庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立八周年。招待会上放映了反映越南南方人民英勇抗击美国侵略强盗的越南南方影片。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广，越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾，以及在北京的其他越南同志，应邀出席了电影招待会。
    我国有关方面负责人韩念龙、丁西林、潘振武，北京市革命委员会的负责人和首都革命群众共一千多人，出席了电影招待会。
