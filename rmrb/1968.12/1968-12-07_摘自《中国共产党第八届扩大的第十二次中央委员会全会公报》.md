Title: Excerpt from the Communiqué of the Twelfth Enlarged Plenum of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1968-12-07
Edition: 5

The plenum believes that, as Comrade Mao Zedong has said, the world revolution has entered a great new era. The revolutionary movements of the peoples of all countries in the world are developing vigorously.

### 摘自《中国共产党第八届扩大的第十二次中央委员会全会公报》

1968-12-07

专栏：

    全会认为，正如毛泽东同志所说，世界革命进入了一个伟大的新时代。全世界各国人民的革命运动正在蓬蓬勃勃地发展。
    摘自《中国共产党第八届扩大的第十二次中央委员会全会公报》
