Title: Ambush, Attack, Disrupt Traffic, Sink Enemy Ships: Patriotic Armed Forces of Guinea-Bissau Report More Victories
Author:
Date: 1968-12-07
Edition: 5

According to a battle report released by the patriotic armed forces of Guinea-Bissau on December 3, the patriotic armed forces of Guinea-Bissau continued to launch a series of attacks and assaults on Portuguese colonial military positions in November, inflicting heavy casualties and material losses on the enemy.

On November 1, in the southern region, the patriotic armed forces ambushed a company of Portuguese colonial troops that had just left the Bubá base. The enemy suffered six fatalities, several wounded, and abandoned a large amount of military supplies, retreating in disarray back to their base. On the same day, the patriotic armed forces launched a surprise attack on two Portuguese colonial military transport convoys heading from Catió and Quful to the strategic transportation hub of Cabaceira. They killed 13 enemy soldiers and wounded more than ten, destroying six enemy trucks.

On the night of November 2, the artillery units of the patriotic armed forces heavily bombarded the enemy positions at Camacunde, Gandul, and Cadamael with heavy mortars, causing significant casualties to the enemy.

On November 3, the patriotic armed forces took military action against the enemy airbase in Buda, annihilating 16 Portuguese colonial troops. On the same day, the patriotic armed forces' artillery sank a Portuguese colonial military ship on the Farim River near Farim Port, killing many enemy soldiers.

On November 11, the patriotic armed forces used rocket launchers to attack a Portuguese colonial naval unit on the Kombidja River, inflicting heavy losses on it.

On November 19, the patriotic armed forces sank an enemy ship on the Gabu River, killing all the enemy personnel on board.

In the northern region, the patriotic armed forces ambushed the enemy twice on the Buda-Binar road on November 11 and 19, killing seven enemy soldiers and wounding many more.

In the eastern region, the patriotic armed forces ambushed the enemy twice on the Sitole-Mançambudu-Saltanhô road on November 1 and 19, killing nine Portuguese colonial troops and wounding many more.

### 打伏击　攻阵地　断交通　沉敌船  几内亚（比绍）爱国武装力量再传捷报  十一月爱国武装多次袭击葡殖民军使敌人遭重大伤亡

1968-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    打伏击　攻阵地　断交通　沉敌船
    几内亚（比绍）爱国武装力量再传捷报
    十一月爱国武装多次袭击葡殖民军使敌人遭重大伤亡
    新华社科纳克里四日电　据几内亚（比绍）爱国武装力量三日发表的一项战报，几内亚（比绍）爱国武装力量在十一月份继续对葡萄牙殖民军的阵地进行了一系列的袭击和进攻，使敌人遭到重大的伤亡和物质损失。
    十一月一日，在南部地区，爱国武装力量伏击了刚从布巴阵地出发的一连葡萄牙殖民军，打得敌人丢下六具尸体、一些伤兵和大量军需物资，狼狈逃回阵地。同一天，爱国武装力量突然袭击了从卡提奥和库法尔出发向交通要道卡巴塞拉集结的两支葡萄牙殖民军运输车队，打死殖民军十三人、打伤十多人，击毁敌军卡车六辆。
    二日深夜，爱国武装力量的炮兵部队用重型迫击炮猛烈地轰击了卡梅孔德、甘杜尔和加达马埃尔的敌军阵地，使敌军遭到重大的伤亡。
    三日，爱国武装力量对布达的敌人空军驻地采取了军事行动，歼灭葡萄牙殖民军十六人。同一天，爱国武装力量的炮兵击沉了法林河上法查港的葡萄牙殖民军的一艘船只，打死敌军多人。
    十一日，爱国武装力量用火箭筒袭击了孔比贾河上的葡萄牙殖民军的一支海军部队，使它遭到重大损失。
    十九日，爱国武装力量在加巴河上击沉了敌军的一艘船只，打死船上的全部敌人。
    在北部地区，爱国武装力量十一日和十九日在布达—比纳尔公路上的两次伏击中，打死敌军七人，打伤多人。
    在东部地区，爱国武装力量一日和十九日在锡托莱—曼桑布杜—萨尔坦霍公路上的两次伏击中，打死葡萄牙殖民军九人，打伤多人。
