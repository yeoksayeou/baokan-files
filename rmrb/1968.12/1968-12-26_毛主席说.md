Title: Chairman Mao Says
Author:
Date: 1968-12-26
Edition: 6

Chairman Mao said: “All the people of the world must have the courage to fight, dare to struggle, fear no difficulties, and press on bravely. Then, the whole world will certainly belong to the people. All devils will be vanquished.”

### 毛主席说
