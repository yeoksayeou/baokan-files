Title: Excerpts from the Communiqué of the Twelfth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1968-12-13
Edition: 5

The plenary session believes, as Comrade Mao Zedong has said, that the world revolution has entered a great new era. The revolutionary movements of the peoples of all countries in the world are developing vigorously.
