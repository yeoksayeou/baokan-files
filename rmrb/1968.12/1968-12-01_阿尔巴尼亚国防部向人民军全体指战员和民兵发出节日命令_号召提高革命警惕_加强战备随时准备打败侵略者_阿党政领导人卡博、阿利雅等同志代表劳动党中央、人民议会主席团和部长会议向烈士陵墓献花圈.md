Title: Albanian Ministry of National Defense Issues a Holiday Order to All Soldiers and Militia of the People's Army
Author:
Date: 1968-12-01
Edition: 5

	Albanian Ministry of National Defense Issues a Holiday Order to All Soldiers and Militia of the People's Army
	Calls for Heightened Revolutionary Vigilance, Strengthened War Preparedness, and Readiness to Defeat Invaders
	Party and Government Leaders Kabo and Alia, Among Others, Lay Wreaths at the Martyrs' Cemetery on Behalf of the Central Committee of the Party of Labor, the Presidium of the People's Assembly, and the Council of Ministers

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, TIRANA, NOVEMBER 29 - On the occasion of the 24th anniversary of Albania's liberation, the Albanian Ministry of National Defense issued an order on November 29, calling on all soldiers and militia of the Albanian People's Army to heighten revolutionary vigilance, strengthen war preparedness, and be ready to defeat any invaders who dare to attack Albania.

	The order states that today, the working class, cooperative farmers, intellectuals, and military personnel are celebrating the 24th anniversary of Albania's liberation with high political enthusiasm and unprecedented revolutionary fervor.

	The order states that in 1968, the Albanian people and their armed forces, steadfastly and consciously, have advanced under the guidance of the Party's resolutions and the teachings of Comrade Enver Hoxha, achieving great successes in deepening the cultural revolution, promoting mass initiative, fulfilling state plans, and strengthening the national defense of our socialist motherland.

	The order states that the United States and the Soviet Union are currently collaborating to divide spheres of influence and dominate the world, and that the Soviet revisionists and their lackeys have turned the Warsaw Pact into a fascist tool of aggression.

	The order emphasizes that the task of the soldiers, cadres, militia, workers, and experts of the Albanian armed forces is to continuously improve the combat effectiveness of the troops and strengthen war preparedness, raise political and military-technical levels, and heighten revolutionary vigilance, so as to defeat any invaders and invader alliances that dare to attack our national borders, as our Party has taught.

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, TIRANA, NOVEMBER 29 - On the occasion of the 24th anniversary of Albania's liberation, party and government leaders of Albania laid wreaths at the Martyrs' Cemetery in Tirana on the morning of November 29.

	Albanian party and government leaders Hysni Kapo, Ramiz Alia, Muslin Peza, Spiro Moisiu, Adil Carcani, and Spiro Koleka, among others, laid wreaths on behalf of the Central Committee of the Party of Labor of Albania, the Presidium of the People's Assembly, and the Council of Ministers.

	Wreaths were also laid by the National Committee of the Democratic Front of Albania, the National Committee of the Association of Old Fighters of Albania, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Albania, the Central Council of the Women's Union of Albania, the Central Committee of the Union of Albanian Labor Youth, the Ministry of National Defense of Albania, the Tirana District Committee of the Party of Labor of Albania, and the Executive Committee of the Tirana District People's Assembly.

### 阿尔巴尼亚国防部向人民军全体指战员和民兵发出节日命令  号召提高革命警惕　加强战备随时准备打败侵略者  阿党政领导人卡博、阿利雅等同志代表劳动党中央、人民议会主席团和部长会议向烈士陵墓献花圈

1968-12-01
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚国防部向人民军全体指战员和民兵发出节日命令
    号召提高革命警惕　加强战备随时准备打败侵略者
    阿党政领导人卡博、阿利雅等同志代表劳动党中央、人民议会主席团和部长会议向烈士陵墓献花圈
    新华社地拉那二十九日电　在庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十四周年的时候，阿尔巴尼亚国防部，二十九日发布命令，号召阿尔巴尼亚人民军全体指战员、民兵提高革命警惕，加强战备，随时准备打败胆敢侵犯阿尔巴尼亚的侵略者。
    命令说，今天，工人阶级、合作社农民、人民知识分子和武装部队指战员在高度的政治热情和空前的革命干劲的气氛中，怀着喜悦的心情和革命豪情，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十四周年。
    命令说，一九六八年，阿尔巴尼亚人民及其武装力量坚定不移地、高度自觉地为贯彻党的决议和恩维尔·霍查同志的教导而奋勇前进，在进一步深入开展思想文化革命方面，在发扬群众的首创精神方面，在完成国家计划和全面加强我们社会主义祖国的国防力量方面，都取得了巨大的成就。
    命令说，现在，美苏正在加紧勾结，共同策划划分势力范围和主宰世界的阴谋，苏修及其走狗把华沙条约变成了法西斯式的侵略工具。
    命令强调指出：阿尔巴尼亚武装部队的战士、干部、民兵、工人和专家的任务是：象我们党所教导的那样，不断提高部队的战斗力和加强战备，提高思想水平和军事技术水平，提高革命警惕性，以便打败任何敢于侵犯我们祖国边疆的侵略者和侵略者联盟。
    新华社地拉那二十九日电　在阿尔巴尼亚解放二十四周年的时候，阿尔巴尼亚党政领导人二十九日上午在地拉那向阿尔巴尼亚民族解放战争烈士陵墓敬献花圈。
    阿尔巴尼亚党政领导人希斯尼·卡博、拉米兹·阿利雅、穆斯林·佩扎、斯皮罗·莫伊修、阿迪尔·查尔查尼、斯皮罗·科列加等同志分别代表阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央、人民议会主席团和部长会议向烈士陵墓献花圈。
    献花圈的还有：阿尔巴尼亚民主阵线全国委员会，阿尔巴尼亚老战士协会全国委员会，阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会，阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会中央理事会，阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会，阿尔巴尼亚国防部，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党地拉那区委员会和地拉那区人民会议执行委员会等。
