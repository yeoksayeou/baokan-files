Title: The Armed Forces of the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola Bravely Fight and Annihilate a Batch of Portuguese Colonial Troops
Author:
Date: 1968-12-23
Edition: 5

The armed forces of the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola have bravely fought and annihilated a batch of Portuguese colonial troops.

According to a recent military communiqué issued by the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola, from September 27 to October 19, the armed forces of the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola carried out a series of military actions against the Portuguese colonial troops in the eastern region of Angola, annihilating more than sixty enemy soldiers and seizing a large quantity of weapons and ammunition.

The communiqué said that on September 27, the armed forces of the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola ambushed a Portuguese convoy on the road from Mongue to Luau—Pengue in Moxico Province, killing several Portuguese colonial troops and seizing a large amount of ammunition. On September 29, the Angolan guerrillas, in repulsing an attack by the Portuguese colonial troops, killed four enemy soldiers and wounded six.

The communiqué said that on October 12, the Angolan guerrillas attacked a Portuguese barracks in the Kumba area, killing five enemy soldiers and wounding ten. The next day, the guerrillas completely annihilated a fifteen-man Portuguese reconnaissance team.

On October 19, when a Portuguese colonial troop invaded an Angolan guerrilla camp in Luau—Pengue, the guerrillas counterattacked, killing five Portuguese colonial troops and wounding seventeen.

The communiqué said that during this period, the armed forces of the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola seized from the Portuguese colonial troops one submachine gun, six Mauser rifles, three hundred rounds of ammunition, and twelve American-made hand grenades.

### “争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟”的武装部队  英勇战斗歼灭一批葡萄牙殖民军

1968-12-23
第5版()
专栏：

    “争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟”的武装部队
    英勇战斗歼灭一批葡萄牙殖民军
    新华社二十二日讯　据争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟最近发表的一项军事公报说，从九月二十七日到十月十九日，争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟的武装部队在安哥拉东部地区对葡萄牙殖民军采取了一系列军事行动，歼灭敌人六十多名，并缴获一大批武器弹药。
    公报说，九月二十七日，争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟的武装部队在莫西哥州的蒙盖通往卢瓦古埃—朋古的公路上，伏击了一支葡萄牙车队，打死葡萄牙殖民军好几个人，缴获大量弹药。二十九日，安哥拉游击队在反击葡萄牙殖民军的一次进攻中，打死敌军四人，打伤六人。
    公报说，十月十二日，安哥拉游击队在昆巴地区袭击了葡萄牙的一个兵营，打死敌人五人，打伤十人。第二天，游击队又全部歼灭了一支由十五人组成的葡萄牙侦察队。
    十月十九日，当一支葡萄牙殖民军侵犯安哥拉游击队在卢瓦古埃—朋古的一个营地时，游击队奋起反击，打死葡萄牙殖民军五人，打伤十七人。
    公报说，在这期间，争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟的武装部队缴获葡萄牙殖民军一支冲锋枪、六支毛瑟枪、三百发子弹和十二颗美国制手榴弹。
