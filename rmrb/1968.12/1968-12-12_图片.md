Title: Workers of Beijing North Suburbs Timber Factory Criticize Liu Shaoqi's "Expert Management" Line
Author:
Date: 1968-12-12
Edition: 4

Workers of the Beijing North Suburbs Timber Factory, under the guidance of the invincible thought of Mao Zedong, have raised high the banner of revolutionary criticism. They are vigorously criticizing the bourgeois reactionary line of "expert management" advocated by the big capitalist-roader Liu Shaoqi.
