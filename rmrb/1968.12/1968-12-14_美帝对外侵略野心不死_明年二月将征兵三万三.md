Title: The U.S. Imperialists' Ambitions of External Aggression Remain Unabated
Author:
Date: 1968-12-14
Edition: 6

The U.S. imperialists' ambitions of external aggression remain unabated. In February of next year, they will conscript 33,700 troops.

New China News Agency, December 13th: Washington reports that the U.S. Department of Defense announced on the 12th that to meet the needs of the U.S. imperialists' policy of external aggression, the U.S. will conscript 33,700 troops in February of next year. This number is 7,000 more than the previously announced conscription number for January of next year, and it is the highest conscription number since May of this year.

At the same time, according to a revelation by U.S. Secretary of Defense Clifford on the 10th, the U.S. will again increase the budget for the Vietnam War once the new Congress convenes in January of next year.

### 美帝对外侵略野心不死  明年二月将征兵三万三

1968-12-14
第6版()
专栏：

    美帝对外侵略野心不死
    明年二月将征兵三万三
    新华社十三日讯　华盛顿消息：据美国国防部十二日宣布，为了适应美帝对外侵略政策的需要，明年二月份美国将征兵三万三千七百人。这个数字比早先宣布的明年一月份的征兵数字多七千人，是今年五月以来征兵最多的一个月。
    在这同时，据美国国防部长克利福德十日透露，明年一月美国新国会一开张，美国将再次增加侵越战争的拨款。
