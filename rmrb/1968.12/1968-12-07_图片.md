Title: Medical Staff of the Anti-Imperialist Mine Clinic in Shaanxi's Weibei Coal Mine Receive Education from the Working Class
Author:
Date: 1968-12-07
Edition: 3

The medical and nursing staff of the Anti-Imperialist Mine Clinic in Shaanxi's Weibei Coal Mine have received profound education from the working class, establishing a mindset of serving the people. They have implemented a system of mobile medical services in the mines, worker residential areas, and rural villages, which is highly welcomed by workers and poor and lower-middle peasants. This is a scene of clinic staff inquiring about the health of workers underground.
