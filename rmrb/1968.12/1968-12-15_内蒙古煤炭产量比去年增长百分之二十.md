Title: Inner Mongolia's Coal Production Increases by 20% Compared to Last Year
Author:
Date: 1968-12-15
Edition: 4

In the process of implementing the communiqué of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee and deeply studying the history of the struggle between the two lines, the revolutionary and production situation on the coal industry front in Inner Mongolia is getting better and better. By the end of November, eighteen mines in the entire region had completed this year's annual original coal production plan ahead of schedule and in excess of the target. Among them, six key mines completed their annual production plans one to three months ahead of schedule and in excess of the target. This year, the coal production in the entire region has increased by 20% compared to last year.

Currently, the broad revolutionary workers on the coal front in Inner Mongolia are seizing the last half month of this year, focusing on the revolution, vigorously promoting production, and are determined to achieve even better results to welcome the new leap in revolution and production next year.

### 内蒙古煤炭产量比去年增长百分之二十

1968-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    内蒙古煤炭产量比去年增长百分之二十
    在落实党的八届十二中全会公报和深入学习两条路线斗争史的革命群众运动中，内蒙古煤炭工业战线的革命和生产形势越来越好。到十一月底，全区已有十八个煤矿提前超额完成今年全年的原煤生产计划，其中有六个重点煤矿提前一个月到三个月超额完成了全年生产计划。今年全区煤炭产量已经比去年的产量增长百分之二十。
    现在，内蒙古煤炭战线的广大革命职工，正抓紧今年的最后半个多月，狠抓革命，猛促生产，决心以更优异的成绩迎接明年革命和生产的新跃进。
