Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Visits Pakistan and Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1968-12-22
Edition: 6

A trade delegation led by Lin Haiyun, acting minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, left Beijing today for Pakistan and Ceylon at the invitation of the governments of Pakistan and Ceylon.

	Officials from the Ministry of Foreign Trade, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and the State Commission for Foreign Economic Relations were at the airport to see them off.

	The acting charge d'affaires of the Pakistani Embassy in China, Chaman, and the acting charge d'affaires of the Ceylonese Embassy in China, Dusilvami, were also at the airport to see them off.

### 我国政府贸易代表团赴巴基斯坦和锡兰访问

1968-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

    我国政府贸易代表团赴巴基斯坦和锡兰访问
    新华社二十一日讯　由对外贸易部代理部长林海云率领的中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团，应巴基斯坦政府和锡兰政府的邀请，今天离开北京，前往巴基斯坦和锡兰进行友好访问。
    到机场送行的，有对外贸易部、外交部和对外经委的负责人。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆临时代办查曼和锡兰驻中国大使馆临时代办都来丝瓦米等也到机场欢送。
