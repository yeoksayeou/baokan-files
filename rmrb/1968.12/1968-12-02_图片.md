Title: Members of the Revolutionary Committee and Revolutionary Workers of Shijiazhuang Cotton Mill No. 3 Study and Discuss Chairman Mao's Latest Instructions and Important Report at the Second Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1968-12-02
Edition: 2

Members of the Revolutionary Committee and revolutionary workers of Shijiazhuang Cotton Mill No. 3 have been deeply engaged in studying and discussing Chairman Mao's latest instructions and his important report delivered at the Second Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee. They are arming their minds with Chairman Mao's theory of continuing the revolution under the dictatorship of the proletariat, further enhancing their consciousness in carrying out Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line.
