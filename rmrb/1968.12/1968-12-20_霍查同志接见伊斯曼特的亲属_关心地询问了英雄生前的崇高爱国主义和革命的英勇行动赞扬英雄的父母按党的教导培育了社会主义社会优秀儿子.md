Comrade Enver Hoxha Meets Relatives of Ismet Brucaj
Cares about the heroic deeds of the hero's patriotic and revolutionary bravery in his lifetime, praising the hero's parents for raising an outstanding son of socialist society according to the party's teachings

	NEW DELHI, Dec. 18 (Xinhua) -- According to reports from local newspapers here, the great leader of the Albanian people, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania Enver Hoxha, on the 17th received at the Party Central Committee building the father of "Hero of Socialist Labour" Ismet Brucaj, who died in the line of duty, Shali Brucaj, Ismet's brother Islam, who took over Ismet's military service, young teacher Baholli Piri, who took over Ismet's teaching work in the village of Likë, and the principal of Ismet's alma mater, Shejnaze Ykë High School, Lytfi Hoxha.

	Comrade Hoxha had a very cordial conversation with them, caring about Ismet's life before his death and his lofty patriotism and revolutionary deeds. Comrade Hoxha praised Ismet's parents for following the party's teachings to cultivate and educate an outstanding son of socialist society; praised Islam and Baholli, the two young people, for their high revolutionary spirit under the party's education, taking over Ismet's military service and teaching work in the village of Likë respectively. Comrade Hoxha also commended the youth and teachers of Shejnaze Ykë High School for their firm stance in loyalty to the party and the motherland, and their determination to overcome difficulties and be ready to complete various tasks gloriously at any time.

	Those present at the reception included Ramiz Alia, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania and Secretary of the Central Secretariat.

专栏：

    霍查同志接见伊斯曼特的亲属
    关心地询问了英雄生前的崇高爱国主义和革命的英勇行动赞扬英雄的父母按党的教导培育了社会主义社会优秀儿子
    新华社地拉那十八日电　据这里的报纸报道：阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志，十七日在党中央大厦接见了因公牺牲的“社会主义劳动英雄”伊斯曼特·勃鲁察伊的父亲沙利·勃鲁察伊、到部队接替伊斯曼特服役的伊斯曼特的弟弟伊斯拉姆、到利卡村接替伊斯曼特教学工作的青年教师巴霍利·皮里和伊斯曼特的母校谢伊纳泽·尤卡师范学校的校长利特菲·霍查。
    霍查同志同他们进行了十分亲切的谈话，关心地询问了伊斯曼特生前的生活和他的崇高的爱国主义和革命的行动。霍查同志赞扬了伊斯曼特的父母遵照党的教导，培养和教育了社会主义社会的优秀儿子；赞扬了伊斯拉姆和巴霍利这两个青年在党的教育下，以高度的革命精神，分别代替伊斯曼特到部队去服役和到利卡村担任教师。霍查同志还表扬了谢伊纳泽·尤卡师范学校的青年和教员忠于党和祖国的坚定立场和他们不怕困难、随时准备光荣地完成各项任务的决心。
    参加接见的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记拉米兹·阿利雅。
