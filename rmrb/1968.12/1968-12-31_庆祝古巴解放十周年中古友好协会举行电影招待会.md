Title: Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of Cuba's Liberation: The China-Cuba Friendship Association Holds a Film Screening
Author:
Date: 1968-12-31
Edition: 6

The China-Cuba Friendship Association held a film screening today to celebrate the 10th anniversary of Cuba's liberation.

The Chargé d'affaires of the Cuban Embassy in China, Mora, and the deputy head of the Cuban government trade delegation, Ismail Beyio Rios, along with the entire delegation, were invited to attend the screening.

At the screening, the president of the China-Cuba Friendship Association, Shen Jian, and relevant officials such as Ji Pengfei and Li Qiang, raised their glasses with Mora and other Cuban friends, toasting to the continuous development of the friendship between the peoples of China and Cuba. The film "The Red Detachment of Women" with piano accompaniment was shown at the screening.

### 庆祝古巴解放十周年中古友好协会举行电影招待会

1968-12-31
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝古巴解放十周年中古友好协会举行电影招待会
    新华社三十日讯　中国古巴友好协会今天举行电影招待会，庆祝古巴解放十周年。
    古巴驻中国大使馆临时代办莫拉，正在北京的古巴政府贸易代表团副团长伊斯迈尔·贝约·里奥斯和代表团全体成员，应邀出席了电影招待会。
    在招待会上，中古友协会长申健和有关方面负责人姬鹏飞、李强等，同莫拉临时代办等古巴朋友举杯，祝中古两国人民的友谊不断发展。招待会上，放映了彩色影片《钢琴伴唱〈红灯记〉》。
