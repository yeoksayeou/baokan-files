Title: Comrade Enver Hoxha Receives Chinese Party, Government, and Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-12-03
Edition: 1

Comrade Enver Hoxha received the Chinese Party, Government, and Military Delegation and had a very cordial and friendly conversation with all the members of the delegation. Those present at the reception included Comrades Bekir Balluku, Hysni Kapo, Manush Myftiu, Mehmet Shehu, Ramiz Alia, and others.

NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, TIRANA, Dec. 2 -- The great leader of the Albanian people and the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Comrade Enver Hoxha, received the Chinese Party, Government, and Military Delegation, led by Comrade Huang Yongsheng, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, at the Central Committee building of the Albanian Labor Party at noon on Dec. 2.

Those present at the reception included the Political Bureau members of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party: Bekir Balluku, Hysni Kapo, Manush Myftiu, Mehmet Shehu, and Ramiz Alia; the Alternate Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee: Abdyl Kellezi and Petrit Dum; the Secretary of the Central Committee, Qafez Spasho; the Central Committee members: Behar Shtylla, Hito Cako, and Nestor Nas; the Director of the International Department of the Central Committee, Piro Bita; and the Military Attache of the Albanian Embassy in China, Afim Hakan.

During the reception, Comrade Enver Hoxha had a very cordial and friendly conversation with all the members of the delegation.

The Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Li Tingqian, and the Military Attache, Li Zhongxuan, were also present at the reception.

The reception was conducted in a very cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 霍查同志接见中国党政军代表团  同代表团全体成员进行了极为亲切友好的谈话  接见时，在座的有：巴卢库、卡博、穆夫蒂乌、谢胡、阿利雅等同志

1968-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    霍查同志接见中国党政军代表团
    同代表团全体成员进行了极为亲切友好的谈话
    接见时，在座的有：巴卢库、卡博、穆夫蒂乌、谢胡、阿利雅等同志
    新华社地拉那二日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志二日中午在劳动党中央委员会大厦接见了由中共中央委员、中国人民解放军总参谋长黄永胜同志率领的中国党政军代表团全体成员。
    接见时，在座的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员：贝基尔·巴卢库、希斯尼·卡博、马努什·穆夫蒂乌、穆罕默德·谢胡、拉米兹·阿利雅，党中央政治局候补委员：阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐、彼得里特·杜米，党中央书记查费尔·斯巴修，党中央委员：贝哈尔·什图拉、希托·恰科和奈斯蒂·纳赛，党中央国际部长皮罗·比塔和阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆武官阿弗尼·哈卡尼。
    接见时，恩维尔·霍查同志同代表团全体成员进行了极为亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃和武官李钟玄也在座。
    接见是在十分亲切友好的气氛中进行的。（附图片）
