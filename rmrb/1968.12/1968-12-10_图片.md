Title: A Wave of Learning, Propaganda, and Implementation of the Communiqué of the 12th Plenary Session of the 8th Central Committee Sweeps Through Guiyang Bearing Factory
Author:
Date: 1968-12-10
Edition: 4

A wave of learning, propaganda, and implementation of the communiqué of the 12th Plenary Session of the 8th Central Committee is sweeping through the Guiyang Bearing Factory. Broad masses of revolutionary workers are determined to further grasp the revolution and promote production on the basis of victory, resolutely completing all the battle tasks proposed in the communiqué of the 12th Plenary Session of the 8th Central Committee. The picture shows workers racing against time in their struggle.
