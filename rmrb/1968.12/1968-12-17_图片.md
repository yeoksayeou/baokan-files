Title: The Old Poor Peasant Li Xiangqing Gives a Class Education to the Knowledgeable Youth
Author: Shí Guāndá
Date: 1968-12-17
Edition: 4

	In the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, Wuzhong County, Dongfanghong Commune, Wulangqu Brigade, old poor peasant Li Xiangqing was forced to be a long-term laborer for the landlord in the old society, living a life worse than that of cattle and horses. This is the dilapidated and low house in front of which he used to live in the old society. He is giving a class education to the knowledgeable youth who have settled down in the countryside. (Photo by Shi Guandao, Xinhua News Agency)
