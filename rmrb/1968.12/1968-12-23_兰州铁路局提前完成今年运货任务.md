Title: Lanzhou Railway Bureau Completes This Year's Freight Transport Task Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1968-12-23
Edition: 4

The revolutionary workers of the Lanzhou Railway Bureau, under the brilliant guidance of the great leader Chairman Mao's latest instructions and inspired by the great encouragement of the Communist Party's Eighth Plenum of the Twelfth Central Committee, have been vigorously promoting the revolution and vigorously advancing production. The entire bureau has completed this year's annual freight transport task 23 days ahead of schedule.

### 兰州铁路局提前完成今年运货任务

1968-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    兰州铁路局提前完成今年运货任务
    兰州铁路局广大革命职工，在伟大领袖毛主席一系列最新指示的光辉照耀下，在党的八届十二中全会公报的巨大鼓舞下，狠抓革命，猛促生产，全局已提前二十三天完成今年全年货物运输任务。
