Title: Finnish Ambassador Holds Reception for Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1968-12-07
Edition: 5

Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Heileinous, held a reception in Beijing today to celebrate Finland's Independence Day.

Mr. Heileinous and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Han Nianlong, raised their glasses at the reception, toasting to the ever-deepening friendship between the peoples of China and Finland.

Relevant officials from our country, including Lin Haiyun, as well as diplomats from various countries stationed in China, were invited to attend the reception.

### 芬兰大使为独立日举行招待会

1968-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    芬兰大使为独立日举行招待会
    新华社六日讯　芬兰驻中国大使海莱尼乌斯，今天在北京为芬兰独立日举行招待会。
    海莱尼乌斯大使和我外交部副部长韩念龙，在招待会上举杯，共祝中芬两国人民的友谊不断发展。
    我国有关方面负责人林海云，以及各国驻中国使节，应邀出席了招待会。
