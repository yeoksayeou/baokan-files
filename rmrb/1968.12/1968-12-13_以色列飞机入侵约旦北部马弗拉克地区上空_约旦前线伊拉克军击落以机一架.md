Title: Israeli Aircraft Invade Northern Jordan's Mafraq Region
Author:
Date: 1968-12-13
Edition: 6

Israeli aircraft invaded the skies over the northern Jordanian region of Mafraq. Iraqi forces stationed on the Jordanian front shot down one Israeli plane.

	BEIJING, Dec. 11 (Xinhua) — Following the downing of seven Israeli aggressor aircraft on Dec. 4, Iraqi forces stationed on the Jordanian front shot down another Israeli plane on Dec. 10. According to reports from local newspapers here on Dec. 11, an Iraqi military spokesman stated that on Dec. 10, a group of Israeli aircraft invaded the skies over northern Jordan's Mafraq region. The Iraqi military's anti-aircraft artillery shot down one of the planes, forcing the rest to retreat.

### 以色列飞机入侵约旦北部马弗拉克地区上空  约旦前线伊拉克军击落以机一架

1968-12-13
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列飞机入侵约旦北部马弗拉克地区上空
    约旦前线伊拉克军击落以机一架
    新华社巴格达十一日电　继四日打下美帝走狗以色列侵略者飞机七架以后，驻扎在约旦前线的伊拉克军队十日又打下以色列飞机一架。据这里的报纸十一日报道，伊拉克军事发言人十日说，一队以色列飞机十日入侵约旦北部马弗拉克地区上空，伊拉克军队的高射炮击落其中一架，迫使其余的逃走了。
