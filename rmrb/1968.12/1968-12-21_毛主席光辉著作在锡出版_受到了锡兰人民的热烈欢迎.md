Title: Chairman Mao's Brilliant Works Published in Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1968-12-21
Edition: 5

The brilliant works of Chairman Mao, "On the Coalition Government" and "The Question of Independence and Initiative Within the United Front," have recently been translated into Sinhala and Tamil respectively, and published by the Colombo Workers' Publishing House and the Colombo People's Publishing House.

These two brilliant works have been warmly welcomed by the people of Ceylon.

### 毛主席光辉著作在锡出版  受到了锡兰人民的热烈欢迎

1968-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席光辉著作在锡出版
    受到了锡兰人民的热烈欢迎
    新华社科伦坡十七日电　伟大领袖毛主席的光辉著作《论联合政府》最近已经译成僧伽罗文，由科伦坡工人出版社出版。
    在这以前，毛主席的另一篇光辉著作《统一战线中的独立自主问题》已经译成泰米尔文，由科伦坡人民出版社出版。
    这两篇光辉著作受到锡兰人民的热烈欢迎。
