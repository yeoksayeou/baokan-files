Title: U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Forces Repeatedly Invade Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1968-12-16
Edition: 5

	New York Times, Phnom Penh, December 15 — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on December 14, the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a note to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime on December 12, strongly protesting the U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet forces' invasion of Cambodia.

	The note stated that the U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet forces invaded Cambodia's Takeo, Kandal, Pursat, Kampong Cham, Kampong Speu, and Kampong Chhnang provinces on November 19, 22, 23, 24, 25, 26, 27, and 29. They attacked civilians, security personnel, and military personnel of the Royal Cambodian Armed Forces in these provinces. The note said that these actions are increasingly dangerous provocations. The Royal Government demands that the U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet forces immediately stop these provocations and compensate Cambodia for the damages caused by these attacks.

	The note stated: These attacks injured six Cambodian civilians. Additionally, the enemy also kidnapped three residents within Cambodian territory.

### 美国—南越伪军一再侵犯柬埔寨  柬外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军一再侵犯柬埔寨
    柬外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边十五日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十四日报道：柬埔寨外交部十二日照会美国政府和西贡傀儡集团，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军侵犯柬埔寨。
    照会说，美国—南越伪军在十一月十九日、二十二日、二十三日、二十四日、二十五日、二十六日、二十七日和二十九日，多次侵犯柬埔寨柴桢省、干丹省、磅湛省、唝吥省、桔井省和波萝勉省，并袭击这些省的平民、保安人员和柬埔寨王家武装部队军事人员。照会说，这是越来越危险的挑衅行径，王国政府要求美国—南越伪军立即停止这种挑衅行径，并赔偿由于这些袭击给柬埔寨所造成的损失。
    照会说：这些袭击共使六个柬埔寨人受伤。另外，敌人还在柬埔寨境内劫走了三个居民。
