Title: Celebrating the 8th Anniversary of the Establishment of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association and the 8th Anniversary of the Signing of the China-Cambodia Friendship and Non-Aggression Treaty
Author:
Date: 1968-12-19
Edition: 5

The China-Cambodia Friendship Association held a reception today to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the establishment of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association and the eighth anniversary of the signing of the China-Cambodia Friendship and Non-Aggression Treaty.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Qichen, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and Standing Committee Member of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association Ding Xilin, and other leaders from the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee and relevant departments attended the reception. They joined Cambodian Charge d'Affaires Du Kouvuth and other Cambodian friends in Beijing in raising their glasses to toast the ever-deepening friendship between the two countries and their peoples.

### 庆祝中柬友协成立八周年和中柬友好条约签订八周年  中国柬埔寨友好协会举行酒会

1968-12-19
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中柬友协成立八周年和中柬友好条约签订八周年
    中国柬埔寨友好协会举行酒会
    新华社十八日讯　中国柬埔寨友好协会今天举行酒会，庆祝中柬友协成立八周年及中柬友好和互不侵犯条约签订八周年。
    李先念副总理，外交部副部长韩念龙，中国人民对外文化友好协会副会长、中柬友协常务理事丁西林，北京市革命委员会和其他有关方面负责人，同柬埔寨驻中国大使馆临时代办杜克武提和在北京的其他柬埔寨朋友，出席了酒会。宾主在酒会上举杯，共祝中柬两国和两国人民的友谊日益发展。
