Title: The People's Armed Struggle in Mozambique Advances
Author:
Date: 1968-12-07
Edition: 5

The people's armed struggle in Mozambique is advancing. Revolutionary masses in Mozambique have organized transport teams to deliver military supplies to the guerrilla forces, continuing the armed struggle.
