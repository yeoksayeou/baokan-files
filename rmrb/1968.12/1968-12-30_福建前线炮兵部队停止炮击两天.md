Title: Fuzhou Front Artillery Units Cease Fire for Two Days
Author:
Date: 1968-12-30
Edition: 3

The spokesperson for the command of the People's Liberation Army's Fuzhou Front announced that, to allow the compatriots and Nationalist soldiers on the islands of Jinmen, Xiaojinmen, Dadan, and Erdan to celebrate the New Year with the rest of the nation, the PLA's Fuzhou Front artillery units have been ordered to cease fire on December 31, 1968, and January 1, 1969, as a gesture of concern.

### 福建前线炮兵部队停止炮击两天

1968-12-30
第3版()
专栏：

    福建前线炮兵部队停止炮击两天
    新华社福建前线三十日电　中国人民解放军福建前线部队司令部发言人宣布，为了让大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的祖国同胞和国民党官兵们同全国人民一道欢度新年，中国人民解放军福建前线炮兵部队奉命在十二月三十一日和一九六九年元月一日两天停止炮击，以示关怀。
