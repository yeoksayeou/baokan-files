Title: Anshan Steel's Seamless Pipe Plant Exceeds Annual Target
Author:
Date: 1968-12-10
Edition: 4

Anshan Steel's Seamless Pipe Plant has exceeded its annual production target. The revolutionary workers of the plant, holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, have fully played the leading role of the working class. Over the past year, they have successfully developed 96 new products with advanced standards for the country and implemented 26 major technological innovations. Since the second half of this year, production indicators have been continuously broken, far surpassing the highest historical levels of 1966 for the same period. The plant has also completed this year's production plan 32 days ahead of schedule.

### 鞍钢无缝钢管厂超额完成全年任务

1968-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    鞍钢无缝钢管厂超额完成全年任务
    鞍钢无缝钢管厂广大革命职工，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，充分发挥工人阶级的领导作用，一年来，为国家试制成功九十六项具有先进水平的新产品，实现二十六项重大的技术革新。自今年下半年以来，生产指标月月创新，远远超过历史最高纪录的一九六六年的同期水平，并提前三十二天完成了今年生产计划。
