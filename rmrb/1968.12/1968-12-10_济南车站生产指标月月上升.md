Title: Production Indices at Jinan Station Rise Monthly
Author:
Date: 1968-12-10
Edition: 4

Jinan Station's revolutionary workers, under the great leader Chairman Mao's guidance that "the working class must lead everything," have armed themselves with Mao Zedong Thought. They have vigorously reformed all unreasonable rules and regulations, carried out extensive technological innovations and technological revolutions. As a result, the station's production indices have risen monthly, production tasks have been overfulfilled month after month. In the third quarter, the number of vehicles handled reached an all-time high. The tonnage of goods sent and the number of cars loaded completed the annual production tasks 60 days and 41 days ahead of schedule, respectively. Other indices have also been completed excellently.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 济南车站生产指标月月上升

1968-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    济南车站生产指标月月上升
    济南车站广大革命职工在伟大领袖毛主席“工人阶级必须领导一切”的教导下，以毛泽东思想武装自己的头脑，大力改革一切不合理的规章制度，大搞技术革新和技术革命，全站生产指标月月上升，生产任务月月超额完成，第三季度办理车辆的数目创造了历史上最高水平，货物发送吨数、装车数分别提前六十天、四十一天完成全年生产任务，其他指标也完成得很出色。
                                            （据新华社）
