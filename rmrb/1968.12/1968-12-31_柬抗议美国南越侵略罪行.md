Title: Cambodia Protests U.S.-South Vietnamese Aggression
Author:
Date: 1968-12-31
Edition: 6

Cambodia Protests U.S.-South Vietnamese Aggression

	According to a report from the Cambodian News Agency, the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a note on December 26 to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime, strongly protesting the aggressive acts committed by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops who attacked Cambodia's Takeo Province on December 19 and 22, injuring seven Cambodian civilians.

### 柬抗议美国南越侵略罪行

1968-12-31
第6版()
专栏：

    柬抗议美国南越侵略罪行
    新华社金边电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨外交部十二月二十六日照会美国政府和西贡傀儡集团，强烈抗议美国—南越傀儡军队在十二月十九日和二十二日两次袭击柬埔寨柴桢省，打伤七名柬居民的侵略罪行。
