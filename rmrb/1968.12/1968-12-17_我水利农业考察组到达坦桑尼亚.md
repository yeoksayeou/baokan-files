Title: Chinese Water and Agriculture Survey Team Arrives in Tanzania
Author:
Date: 1968-12-17
Edition: 6

The Chinese water and agriculture survey team led by Lu De arrived in Dar es Salaam, the capital of Tanzania, by plane on December 14.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the survey team included Tanzania's Minister of Lands, Settlement, and Water Development, Babu, and others.

Also present at the airport to welcome the team were Zhou Boqing, the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Tanzania, and Jiang Da, the Chinese economic representative in Tanzania.

### 我水利农业考察组到达坦桑尼亚

1968-12-17
第6版()
专栏：

    我水利农业考察组到达坦桑尼亚
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆十五日电　由卢德率领的中国水利农业考察组十四日乘飞机抵达坦桑尼亚首都达累斯萨拉姆。
    前往机场欢迎这个考察组的有：坦桑尼亚土地、定居和水利开发部长巴布等。
    到机场欢迎的还有：中国驻坦桑尼亚大使馆临时代办周伯萍和中国驻坦桑尼亚经济代表江达。
