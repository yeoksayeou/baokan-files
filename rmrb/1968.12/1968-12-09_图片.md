Title: Workers at Renault Automobile Factory in France Stage Strike
Author:
Date: 1968-12-09
Edition: 6

Workers at the Renault Automobile Factory in France have recently gone on strike to protest against the French government's reactionary policies of exploiting the working people. The image shows the masses of workers holding a demonstration march on the streets. (Xinhua News Agency, Telegraphic Photo)
