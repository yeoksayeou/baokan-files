Title: Worker Idah Daan Overcomes Challenges to Create Water Spray Dust Removal Method
Author:
Date: 1968-12-30
Edition: 3

Under the guidance of Chairman Mao's latest instructions, worker Idah Daan of the Beijing Steel Plant's Fourth Workshop, with a fearless revolutionary spirit, repeatedly conducted scientific experiments. Overcoming numerous difficulties, he created the water spray dust removal method, reducing dust to below the national standard, making a significant contribution to the prevention of silicosis. The image shows worker Idah Daan in operation. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
