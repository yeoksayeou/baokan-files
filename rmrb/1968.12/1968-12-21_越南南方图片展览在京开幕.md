Title: Photo Exhibition of South Vietnam Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-12-21
Edition: 5

	Photo Exhibition of South Vietnam Opens in Beijing

	Xinhua News Agency, December 20th – The "Photo Exhibition of the South Vietnamese People's Anti-American National Salvation War" opened today at the Beijing Exhibition Hall. Over 500 workers from the capital, soldiers and officers of the People's Liberation Army, and Red Guards attended the opening ceremony, expressing their warm congratulations on the eighth anniversary of the founding of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam.

	Today, the flags of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the People's Republic of China fluttered in the wind in front of the Beijing Exhibition Hall. On the curtain at the front of the exhibition hall, there were portraits of the great leader of the Vietnamese people, Chairman Ho Chi Minh, and Chairman of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, Nguyen Huu Tho. There was also a portrait of the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao. Inside the hall, there were prominent slogans: "Vietnam will win! The U.S. imperialists will be defeated! Firmly support the Vietnamese people in carrying the anti-American national salvation war to the end!"

	At the entrance of the hall, there stood a large board with quotations, on which was written the full text of the congratulatory telegram sent by our great leader Chairman Mao, his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao, and Premier Zhou Enlai to Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam on the occasion of the eighth anniversary of its founding.

	The exhibition features more than 200 photographs. These photographs expose the heinous crimes committed by the American aggressors in Vietnam; vividly reflect the South Vietnamese military and civilians' perseverance in people's war and the glorious victories achieved in the anti-American national salvation war. Many photographs also depict the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Vietnam.

	Nguyen Van Quang, Head of the Permanent Mission of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China Vu Minh Quan, and other Vietnamese comrades in Beijing attended the opening ceremony.

	The exhibition is organized by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Vietnam Friendship Association to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the founding of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam. Relevant officials Ding Xilin, Han Nianlong, Xie Huaiyi, Pan Zhenwu, and Xiang Qian attended the opening ceremony.

### 越南南方图片展览在京开幕

1968-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

    越南南方图片展览在京开幕
    新华社二十日讯  “越南南方人民抗美救国战争图片展览”今天在北京展览馆开幕。首都工人、中国人民解放军指战员和红卫兵小将共五百多人参加了开幕式，表示热烈祝贺越南南方民族解放阵线成立八周年。
    今天，越南南方民族解放阵线的旗帜、越南民主共和国国旗和中华人民共和国国旗，在北京展览馆前迎风飘扬。展览大厅正面帷幕上，挂着越南人民的伟大领袖胡志明主席的画像，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团阮友寿主席的画像。在帷幕上，还挂着中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席的画像。厅内还挂着一幅幅醒目的标语：“越南必胜！”“美帝必败！”“坚决支持越南人民把抗美救国战争进行到底！”
    大厅入口处，直立一座大型语录牌，上面写着我们伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席以及周恩来总理，为越南南方民族解放阵线成立八周年，致越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团阮友寿主席贺电的全文。
    展出的图片共有二百多幅。这些图片揭露了美国侵略强盗在越南犯下的滔天罪行；生动地反映了越南南方军民坚持人民战争，在抗美救国战争中取得的辉煌胜利。不少图片还表现了中越两国人民的战斗友谊。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广，越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾，在北京的其他越南同志，出席了开幕式。
    展览会是由中国人民对外文化友好协会和中国越南友好协会，为庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立八周年而举行的。有关方面负责人丁西林、韩念龙、谢怀德、潘振武、向前等，出席了开幕式。
