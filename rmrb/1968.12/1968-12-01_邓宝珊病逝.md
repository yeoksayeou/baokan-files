Title: Deng Baoshan Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1968-12-01
Edition: 2

National People's Congress representative, National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference member, and Vice Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Deng Baoshan passed away from heart disease at 3:30 PM on November 27 in Beijing. He was 75 years old.

### 邓宝珊病逝

1968-12-01
第2版()
专栏：

    邓宝珊病逝
    新华社三十日讯　全国人民代表大会代表、全国政协常务委员、中国国民党革命委员会副主席邓宝珊患心脏病于十一月二十七日下午三时三十分在北京逝世，终年七十五岁。
