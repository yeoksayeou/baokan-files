Title: Indian Liberation Monthly Denounces the Soviet Revisionist Clique for Betraying the Path of the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1968-12-05
Edition: 6

New Delhi, Dec. 4 (Xinhua) — The Indian monthly *Liberation* published an article in its November issue denouncing the Soviet revisionist clique for betraying the path of the October Revolution. The article also condemned the anti-revolutionary crimes of the Dange clique and the Indian Communist Party's revisionists.

The article states: "The Soviet revisionist clique, led by Khrushchev and his successors Kosygin and Brezhnev, has restored capitalism in the Soviet Union using the state apparatus and is pursuing neo-colonialism."

The article points out that the Soviet revisionist clique's invasion of Czechoslovakia clearly shows that this clique has degenerated into social fascism and social imperialism.

The article says: "This modern revisionist clique is not only the sworn enemy of the Soviet working people but also the sworn enemy of all workers and oppressed nations around the world, including the Indian people."

The article condemns the Dange clique and the Indian Communist Party's revisionists for their efforts to oppose the armed revolutionary path of Naxalbari. The article says that after the 20th Congress of the CPSU, they openly abandoned the path of the October Revolution, that is, the path of violent revolution, and attempted to lead the Indian revolutionary people onto the wrong path of "parliamentary struggle."

The article says that the Dange clique and the Indian Communist Party's revisionists have openly sided with the domestic reactionaries, the Soviet revisionist clique, and the American imperialists. They have become lackeys of American imperialism and Soviet revisionism, and servants of the Indian landlords and bourgeoisie. When the Indian revolution reaches its climax, it will sweep them away like garbage, no matter how fiercely they now oppose the peasant struggle and how fiercely they now shout against socialist China.

The article points out that although the path of the October Revolution has been betrayed on Soviet soil, it is achieving new victories in various countries. People around the world are spitting on the revisionist goods of "peaceful transition to socialism," denouncing them as poison. They are recognizing that the path of the October Revolution is the only path to national liberation, socialism, and world peace. As Chairman Mao has said: "The broad masses of the Soviet people, the broad ranks of the Party and the cadres, are good and want revolution. The rule of revisionism will not last long."

The article says that under the leadership of Marxism-Leninism, the Soviet people will soon return to the path of the October Revolution and overthrow the new tsars of the Kremlin. The whole world will be red.

The article concludes with the following slogans:

	"Long live the October Revolution!"
	"Long live Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought!"
	"Workers of the world and oppressed nations, unite!"

### 印度《解放》月刊谴责苏修集团背叛十月革命道路  苏修集团是世界人民不共戴天的敌人  指出丹吉集团和印共修正主义分子已公开站在国内反动派和美帝苏修一边

1968-12-05
第6版()
专栏：

    印度《解放》月刊谴责苏修集团背叛十月革命道路
    苏修集团是世界人民不共戴天的敌人
    指出丹吉集团和印共修正主义分子已公开站在国内反动派和美帝苏修一边
    新华社四日讯　新德里消息：印度《解放》月刊在十一月出版的一期上刊登一篇文章，谴责苏修叛徒集团背叛了十月革命的道路。文章同时谴责了印度丹吉叛徒集团和印共修正主义分子的反革命罪行。
    文章说：“以赫鲁晓夫和他的继任者柯西金和勃列日涅夫为首的苏修叛徒集团，利用国家机器在苏联复辟了资本主义，推行新殖民主义。”
    文章指出，苏修叛徒集团“对捷克斯洛伐克的入侵，清楚地证明了这个集团已经堕落到社会法西斯主义和社会帝国主义的地步。”
    文章说：“这个现代修正主义集团不仅是苏联劳动人民的，也是世界所有工人和各被压迫民族，包括印度人民在内的不共戴天的敌人。”
    文章谴责了丹吉叛徒集团和印共修正主义分子极力反对纳萨尔巴里武装革命的道路。文章说，在苏共二十大之后，他们便公开抛弃了十月革命的道路，即暴力革命的道路，并且妄图把印度革命人民引到“议会道路”的邪路上去。
    文章说，丹吉叛徒集团和印共修正主义分子已经公开站在国内反动派、苏修叛徒集团和美帝国主义者一边。他们已经堕落成为美帝国主义和苏联修正主义的走狗、印度大地主和资产阶级的仆从。当印度革命走向高潮的时候，它将象扫除垃圾一样把他们扫掉，不管他们现在怎样疯狂地反对农民斗争和对社会主义中国怎样疯狂地叫嚣。
    文章指出，十月革命的道路虽然已在苏联国土上被背叛，它在各国却在不断取得新的胜利。全世界人民都在唾弃“和平过渡到社会主义”的修正主义货色，把它斥为毒素。他们正在认识到十月革命的道路是通向民族解放、社会主义和世界和平的唯一道路。正如毛主席所说过的：“苏联广大的人民、广大的党员和干部，是好的，是要革命的，修正主义的统治是不会长久的。”
    文章说，苏联人民在马克思列宁主义者的领导下将在不久的将来回到十月革命的道路上来，并推翻克里姆林宫的新沙皇的专政。全世界将会是一片红。
    文章最后高呼：
      “十月革命万岁！”
    “马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想万岁！”
      “全世界的工人和被压迫民族团结起来！”
