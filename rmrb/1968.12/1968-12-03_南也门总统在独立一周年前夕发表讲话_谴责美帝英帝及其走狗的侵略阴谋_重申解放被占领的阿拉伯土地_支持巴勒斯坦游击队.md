Title: South Yemen President Delivers Speech on the Eve of Independence Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1968-12-03
Edition: 4

On the eve of South Yemen's first independence anniversary, South Yemen President Qahtan al-Shaabi delivered a speech on November 29 in the evening over the radio and television stations in Aden, denouncing the subversive and destructive plots of American and British imperialism and their lackeys.

Shaabi said: Our independence was won by our great people under the leadership of the National Front after four years of armed struggle. Our people drove out the colonialist troops and established the People's Republic of South Yemen.

He said, after independence, we must rely on our own efforts to develop industry and agriculture and build a good national economy. At the same time, we must smash the plots of imperialism, colonialism, and the reactionary forces of Saudi Arabia.

Shaabi reiterated that South Yemen will stand shoulder to shoulder with other Arab countries to liberate the occupied Arab territories and Palestine. He said: We support the guerrilla activities of the Palestinians. We will, together with other Arab countries, make active contributions to the struggle against the plots of world imperialism led by the United States. The United States providing attack planes and other weapons to Israel is an expression of hostility towards the Arab people.

Shaabi, in denouncing the plots of American and British imperialism to subvert the South Yemen government, said, we can defeat all plots. Reactionary forces, imperialism, and colonialism are doomed to failure. They will be driven out of the Arabian Peninsula forever.

### 南也门总统在独立一周年前夕发表讲话  谴责美帝英帝及其走狗的侵略阴谋  重申解放被占领的阿拉伯土地　支持巴勒斯坦游击队

1968-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    南也门总统在独立一周年前夕发表讲话
    谴责美帝英帝及其走狗的侵略阴谋
    重申解放被占领的阿拉伯土地　支持巴勒斯坦游击队
    新华社亚丁一日电　在南也门独立一周年前夕，南也门总统卡坦·沙比十一月二十九日晚在亚丁广播和电视台向全国人民发表讲话，谴责美英帝国主义及其走狗的颠覆和破坏阴谋。
    沙比说：我们的独立是我们伟大的人民在民族阵线领导下经过四年的武装斗争夺得的。我们的人民赶走了殖民主义军队，建立了南也门人民共和国。
    他说，在独立以后，我们应当靠自己的努力来发展工农业，建立良好的民族经济。同时，我们还要粉碎帝国主义、殖民主义和沙特阿拉伯反动派的侵略阴谋。
    沙比重申，南也门将同其他阿拉伯国家并肩战斗，以解放被占领的阿拉伯领土以及被占领的巴勒斯坦。他说：我们支持巴勒斯坦的游击队活动。我们要同其他阿拉伯国家一道，对于反对以美国为首的世界帝国主义策划的阴谋的斗争，作出积极的贡献。美国向以色列提供攻击机和其他武器是敌视阿拉伯人民的意愿的。
    沙比在谴责美英帝国主义阴谋颠覆南也门政权时说，我们能击溃一切阴谋。反动势力、帝国主义和殖民主义注定要失败。他们将永远被赶出阿拉伯半岛。
