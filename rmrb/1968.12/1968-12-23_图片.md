Title: The People of Mozambique Continue to Grow Stronger in Their Armed Struggle for Patriotism
Author:
Date: 1968-12-23
Edition: 5

The armed patriotic forces of Mozambique continue to grow stronger in their struggle, annihilating large numbers of the enemy, liberating vast areas of land and people, and achieving one victory after another. The image depicts the Mozambican guerrilla forces growing stronger in their struggle.
