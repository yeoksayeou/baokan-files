Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-12-21
Edition: 1

	The Vietnamese people in South Vietnam and all Vietnamese people can be assured that your struggle is our struggle. The 700 million Chinese people are the steadfast rear base of the Vietnamese people, and the vast territory of China is the reliable rear area of the Vietnamese people.

### 毛主席语录

1968-12-21

专栏：

    毛主席语录
