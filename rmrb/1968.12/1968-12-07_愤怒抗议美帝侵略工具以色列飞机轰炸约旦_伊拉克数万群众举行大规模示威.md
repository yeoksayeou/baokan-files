Title: Angry Protest Against U.S. Imperialist Tool Israel's Airstrikes on Jordan
Author:
Date: 1968-12-07
Edition: 6

Tens of thousands of Iraqi workers, peasants, teachers, students, and people from all walks of life held a massive demonstration in Baghdad on December 5 to angrily protest the U.S. imperialist tool Israel's large-scale air raids on Jordan on December 4, and to celebrate the glorious victory of the Iraqi army stationed on the Jordanian front in shooting down seven invading Israeli planes.

The demonstrators held hundreds of placards reading: "Down with the common enemy of the people of the world, the American imperialists!" "Smash the imperialist-Zionist conspiracy against the Arab nation!" "Glory to the warriors on the front!" "No negotiations, no peace talks, only people's revolution and armed struggle!" "We stand with the Palestinian commando fighters!" Angry demonstrators raised their fists and shouted: "We reject the United Nations Security Council's resolution on the Palestinian question!" "People's liberation war is the only way to recover the plundered land!" "Give us weapons, we will fight, fight, fight!" "Kill the American imperialists!"

The demonstrators declared that they would carry on the armed struggle against U.S. imperialism and Israeli Zionism to the end, and strongly support the Palestinian guerrillas fighting for national liberation on the occupied land.

Iraqi President Ahmed Hassan al-Bakr addressed the demonstrators, saying, "We will use all weapons and fight the Zionist enemy on all fronts. 'We will not give up an inch of our land.' 'We will not withdraw a single soldier from the battlefield.'"

President al-Bakr condemned the U.S. conspiracies against Iraq and its sabotage activities.

All Iraqi newspapers on December 5 published editorials celebrating the glorious victory of the Iraqi army on the Jordanian front in shooting down seven Israeli planes, and strongly condemned the U.S. imperialists for instigating and supporting the Israeli aggressors in repeatedly invading Arab countries.

### 愤怒抗议美帝侵略工具以色列飞机轰炸约旦  伊拉克数万群众举行大规模示威

1968-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

    愤怒抗议美帝侵略工具以色列飞机轰炸约旦
    伊拉克数万群众举行大规模示威
    据新华社巴格达六日电　数万名伊拉克工人、农民、教师、学生和其他各界人民五日在巴格达举行大规模的示威，愤怒抗议美帝侵略工具以色列四日大规模空袭约旦的暴行，热烈欢呼驻扎在约旦前线的伊拉克军队击落入侵的以色列飞机七架的辉煌胜利。
    示威者举着数以百计的标语牌，上面写着：“打倒世界人民的共同敌人美国佬！”“粉碎帝国主义—犹太复国主义对阿拉伯民族的阴谋！”“光荣属于前线的战士！”“不要和谈、不要谈判、不要和平解决，要人民革命和武装斗争！”“我们站在巴勒斯坦突击队战士一边！”愤怒的示威者举起拳头不断高呼：“我们拒绝联合国安全理事会关于巴勒斯坦问题的决议！”“人民解放战争是收复被掠夺的土地的唯一道路！”“给我们武器，我们要战斗、战斗、战斗！”“打死美国佬！”
    示威者表示，他们要把反对美帝和以色列犹太复国主义的武装斗争进行到底，并大力支持在被占领的土地上的巴勒斯坦游击队员为争取民族解放而进行的英勇斗争。
    伊拉克总统贝克尔向示威群众发表了讲话，他说，我们将使用一切武器和在各条战线上同犹太复国主义这个敌人战斗。“我们连一平方英尺国土也不放弃。”“我们连一个士兵也不会从战场上撤出来。”
    贝克尔总统谴责美国对伊拉克的阴谋和对伊拉克进行的破坏活动。
    伊拉克所有的报纸五日都发表评论，热烈欢呼驻约旦前线的伊拉克军队击落以色列飞机七架的辉煌胜利，并且强烈谴责美帝国主义唆使和支持以色列侵略者一再侵略阿拉伯国家的罪行。
