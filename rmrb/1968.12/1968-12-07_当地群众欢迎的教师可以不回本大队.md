Title: Teachers Welcomed by Local Residents May Not Need to Return to Their Home Brigades
Author: Tao Jianxian Song Shoudong
Date: 1968-12-07
Edition: 2

Some teachers have worked in one place for a long time, are familiar with the class struggle situation there, have good relationships with the local poor and lower-middle peasants, and are known to the masses. If the masses welcome them to stay and the teachers themselves do not wish to be transferred, we believe that they do not need to return to their home brigades.

An Hui Province Wuhu County Baodou Commune Mawan Primary School Teachers Tao Jianxian Song Shoudong

### 当地群众欢迎的教师可以不回本大队
陶见贤　宋守东
1968-12-07
第2版()
专栏：建议和问题

    当地群众欢迎的教师可以不回本大队
    有些教师在一个地方工作时间很长，对那里的阶级斗争情况比较熟悉，同当地的贫下中农的关系又比较好，群众对他们也是了解的。这样的教师，如果群众欢迎留下，教师本人也不愿调动，我们认为，可以不回本大队。
          安徽省芜湖县保斗公社马湾小学教师　陶见贤　宋守东
