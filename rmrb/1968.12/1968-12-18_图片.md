Title: The Driver Jianghai
Author: Chēfū Jiānghǎi
Date: 1968-12-18
Edition: 1

The Revolutionary Committee of Dongfeng County in Jilin Province has made it a key measure to revolutionize the committee by having cadres participate in collective labor. This is the vice chairman of the county revolutionary committee, Shen Zhaoding (standing), leading the way in criticizing selfishness and revisionism at a study session on Chairman Mao's works.
