Title: Mauritania Celebrates Eight Years of Independence
Author:
Date: 1968-12-01
Edition: 5

Mauritanian military and civilians held a parade in the capital, Nouakchott, on the 28th to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the independence of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania. President Daddah of Mauritania reviewed the parade.

The parade participants carried placards with slogans such as "U.S. out of Vietnam, Vietnam belongs to the Vietnamese people," "People's war is the only way to liberate Arab Palestine," and "Fully support the struggle of the brotherly Arab peoples against imperialism and Zionism."

After the parade, President Daddah addressed the National Assembly, reviewing the achievements of the Mauritania people in the past year in developing the national economy, consolidating national independence, and other areas.

In discussing Mauritania's foreign policy, he pointed out that as long as we stand united, we can more effectively fight against colonialism and neo-colonialism that enslave our African brothers.

President Daddah praised the friendly cooperative relations between Mauritania and China. He said, "Our cooperation with the People's Republic of China has entered an active and fruitful stage."

President Daddah also condemned U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam and the imperialist product, Zionism, for occupying Arab lands. He expressed that the Mauritania people support the just struggle of the Palestinian and Arab peoples against imperialism and colonialism.

### 毛里塔尼亚人民欢庆独立八周年

1968-12-01
第5版()
专栏：

    毛里塔尼亚人民欢庆独立八周年
    新华社努瓦克肖特二十八日电　毛里塔尼亚军民二十八日在首都努瓦克肖特举行游行，热烈庆祝毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国独立八周年。毛里塔尼亚总统达达赫检阅了游行队伍。
    游行群众举着标语牌，上面写着：“美国从越南滚出去，越南是属于越南人民的”、“人民战争是解放阿拉伯巴勒斯坦的唯一道路”、“全力支持兄弟的阿拉伯各国人民反对帝国主义和犹太复国主义”等口号。
    游行结束后，达达赫总统在国民议会会议上发表讲话，回顾了过去一年里毛里塔尼亚人民在发展民族经济、巩固民族独立和其他方面所取得的成就。
    他在谈到毛里塔尼亚的对外政策时指出，只要我们团结一致，就能更有效地同对我们非洲兄弟进行奴役的殖民主义和新殖民主义进行斗争。
    达达赫总统称赞毛里塔尼亚和中国两国之间的友好合作关系。他说：“我们同中华人民共和国的合作已进入了一个积极的和富有成果的阶段。”
    达达赫总统还谴责美帝国主义在越南进行侵略战争，谴责帝国主义的产物以色列犹太复国主义侵占阿拉伯土地，并表示毛里塔尼亚人民支持巴勒斯坦人民和阿拉伯人民反帝反殖的正义斗争。
