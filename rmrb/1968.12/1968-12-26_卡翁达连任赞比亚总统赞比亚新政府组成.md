Title: Kaunda Re-elected as Zambian President and New Government Formed
Author:
Date: 1968-12-26
Edition: 5

Zambian President Kaunda was re-elected in a recent general election and took the oath of office on the 21st.

On December 19th, Zambia held its first general election since declaring independence in October 1964.

Zambia is located in central Africa, with an area of over 750,000 square kilometers and a population of approximately four million.

Lusaka, December 25th: Zambian President Kaunda announced the formation of a new government on the 23rd.

The new government, excluding the president, consists of 17 ministers. Among them, Vice President Kapwepwe also serves as the Minister of Finance, former Foreign Minister Kamanaga is appointed as the new Minister of Rural Development, and former Minister of Finance Mudenda is reassigned as the Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Kaunda announced that eight ministers will each be stationed in one of the eight provinces across the country.

### 卡翁达连任赞比亚总统赞比亚新政府组成

1968-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

    卡翁达连任赞比亚总统赞比亚新政府组成
    新华社二十二日讯  卢萨卡消息：赞比亚总统卡翁达在最近一次大选中重新当选总统，并于二十一日宣誓就职。
    十二月十九日，赞比亚举行了自一九六四年十月宣布独立以来的第一次大选。
    赞比亚位于非洲中部，面积七十五万多平方公里，人口约四百万。
    新华社二十五日讯卢萨卡消息：赞比亚总统卡翁达二十三日宣布组成新的政府。
    新政府除总统外，共有十七名部长。其中，副总统卡普韦普韦兼财政部长，前外交部长卡曼加任新成立的农村发展部长，前财政部长穆登达改任外交部长。
    卡翁达宣布，有八名部长将分别驻在全国八个省工作。
