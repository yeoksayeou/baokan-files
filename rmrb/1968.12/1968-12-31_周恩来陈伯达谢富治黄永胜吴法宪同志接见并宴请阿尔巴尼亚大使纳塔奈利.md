Title: Premier Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Xie Fuzhi, Huang Yongsheng, and Wu Faxian Receive and Dine with Albanian Ambassador Nataneli
Author:
Date: 1968-12-31
Edition: 1

The CPC Central Committee, the Central Cultural Revolution Group, and other leaders Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Xie Fuzhi, Huang Yongsheng, and Wu Faxian received Albanian Ambassador to China Nataneli, who is about to complete his term and return home, this evening. They had a warm and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were also Albanian Charge d'Affaires to China, Minister Plenipotentiary Akoglu.

After the reception, a banquet was held to see off Ambassador Nataneli and his wife. The hosts and guests, in a friendly and warm atmosphere, repeatedly toasted to the fighting friendship between the two parties, the two countries, and the peoples of China and Albania, to the health of the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha, and to the health of the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao.

Albanian Charge d'Affaires to China Akoglu, Commercial Counselor Kouzma, Military Attache Hakani, and other diplomatic personnel, as well as their wives, also attended the banquet.

Those who participated in the reception and banquet included Deputy Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee Wu De, relevant department heads, and heads of the Great Alliance Commission Ji Pengfei, Ma Wenbo, Chen Dehe, Li Qiang, Li Dongye, Song Zhenxian, Fang Yi, Liu Dayu, Zhang Wensheng, and others.

### 周恩来陈伯达谢富治黄永胜吴法宪同志接见并宴请阿尔巴尼亚大使纳塔奈利

1968-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来陈伯达谢富治黄永胜吴法宪同志接见并宴请阿尔巴尼亚大使纳塔奈利
    新华社三十日讯  中共中央、中央文革和其他方面领导同志周恩来、陈伯达、谢富治、黄永胜、吴法宪，今晚接见了即将离任返国的阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，还有阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆公使衔参赞阿果利。
    接见后，举行了宴会，为纳塔奈利大使和夫人饯行。宾主在友好热烈的气氛中，一再为中阿两党、两国和两国人民的战斗友谊，为阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖恩维尔·霍查同志的健康，为中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席的健康，干杯！
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆参赞阿果利、商务参赞考兹玛、武官哈卡尼和其他外交人员，以及他们的夫人们，也出席了宴会。
    参加接见和宴会的，有北京市革命委员会副主任吴德同志，有关方面负责同志和大联委负责人姬鹏飞、马文波、陈德和、李强、李东野、宋振贤、方毅、刘大煜、张文敏等。
