Title: Comrade Hill Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1968-12-02
Edition: 2

Comrade Hill, Chairman of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist), concluded his visit to China and departed from Beijing by plane on November 30 to return home.

During his visit, our most beloved and great leader Chairman Mao Zedong met with Comrade Hill and had a very cordial conversation with him.

Comrade Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, and Kang Sheng, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, held a warm welcoming banquet for Comrade Hill. Comrade Chen Boda and Kang Sheng, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, had a cordial talk with Comrade Hill.

While in Beijing, Comrade Hill visited the Beijing New China Printing Plant, Beijing Knitting General Factory, Tsinghua University, and the exhibition on the exemplary service of the medical team of a certain unit of the Beijing Military Region, where he was warmly welcomed by the revolutionary workers, students, and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army. Comrade Hill also watched the revolutionary modern Beijing opera "The Harbor," the revolutionary modern ballet "Red Detachment of Women," and the color film "Piano Accompaniment to 'The Red Lantern'."

When Comrade Hill left Beijing, Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and other relevant responsible comrades, went to the airport to give him a warm send-off.

### 希尔同志离京回国  康生同志等到机场热烈欢送

1968-12-02
第2版()
专栏：

    希尔同志离京回国
    康生同志等到机场热烈欢送
    新华社一日讯　澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）主席爱·弗·希尔同志，结束了在中国的访问，十一月三十日乘飞机离开北京回国。
    在访问期间，我们最敬爱的伟大领袖毛泽东主席，会见了希尔同志，同他进行了极为亲切的谈话。
    中共中央政治局常委周恩来、陈伯达、康生同志曾设宴热烈欢迎希尔同志。中共中央政治局常委陈伯达、康生同志，同希尔同志进行了亲切的会谈。
    希尔同志在北京期间，参观了北京新华印刷厂、北京针织总厂、清华大学和北京部队某部先进卫生科全心全意为人民服务事迹展览，受到了广大革命职工、革命师生和人民解放军指战员的热烈欢迎。希尔同志在北京还观看了革命现代京剧《海港》、革命现代芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》，彩色影片《钢琴伴唱〈红灯记〉》。
    希尔同志离开北京的时候，中共中央政治局常委康生同志，以及有关方面负责同志，到机场热烈欢送。
