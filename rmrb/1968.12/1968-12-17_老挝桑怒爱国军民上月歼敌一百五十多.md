Title: Patriotic Military and Civilians of Xam Nuea Province, Laos, Annihilated Over 150 Enemies Last Month
Author:
Date: 1968-12-17
Edition: 6

Patriotic military and civilians of Xam Nuea Province, Laos, repelled the enemy's "creeping" attacks on this province last month. According to the radio broadcast of the Pathet Lao, the U.S. imperialists and their lackeys mobilized a large number of troops and bandits to launch "creeping" attacks on the liberated areas of Xam Nuea Province in November. The patriotic military and civilians of this province bravely fought back against the enemy and achieved new victories. According to incomplete statistics, the patriotic military and civilians engaged in seventeen major battles with the enemy, annihilating 155 enemy troops and seizing nearly 30 pieces of various weapons.

### 老挝桑怒爱国军民上月歼敌一百五十多

1968-12-17
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝桑怒爱国军民上月歼敌一百五十多
    据新华社康开十六日电　据巴特寮电台广播，老挝桑怒省爱国军民十一月份击败了敌人对这个省进行的“蚕食”进攻。电台说，在十一月份，美帝国主义及其走狗，出动大批军队和匪徒，对桑怒省解放区进行“蚕食”进攻。这个省的爱国军民英勇打击敌人，取得了新胜利。据不完全统计，爱国军民同敌人进行大的战斗就达十七次，共歼敌一百五十五名，缴获各种武器近三十件。
