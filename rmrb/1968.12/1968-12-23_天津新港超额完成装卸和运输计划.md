Title: Tianjin New Port Exceeds Loading, Unloading, and Transportation Plans
Author:
Date: 1968-12-23
Edition: 4

The broad revolutionary workers of Tianjin New Port, the largest international trade port in northern China, under the assistance of the PLA's Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team stationed at the port, resolutely implemented Chairman Mao's great policy of "grasp revolution, promote production." They completed the annual national loading, unloading, and transportation plans ahead of schedule by 18 days.

### 天津新港超额完成装卸和运输计划

1968-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    天津新港超额完成装卸和运输计划
    我国北方最大的国际贸易港口——天津新港的广大革命职工，在驻港解放军毛泽东思想宣传队帮助下，坚决贯彻执行毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针，提前十八天超额完成了全年的国家装卸、运输计划。　　　　　　　　　（据新华社讯）
