Title: Nepalese People Protest Against Indian Reactionaries' Atrocities
Author:
Date: 1968-12-10
Edition: 5

Thousands of Nepalese people held a demonstration in Lalitpur, near Kathmandu, on the afternoon of December 4 to protest against the recent outrage by Indian reactionaries who openly invaded Nepalese territory and abducted four Nepalese citizens.

Demonstrators marched through the streets of Lalitpur, shouting angrily, "Down with Indian expansionists!" "We are ready to fight and sacrifice for our sovereignty and independence at any time!" The demonstrators carried placards denouncing Indian aggression and opposing India's expansionist policies.

### 尼泊尔人民示威抗议印度反动派暴行

1968-12-10
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔人民示威抗议印度反动派暴行
    据新华社加德满都电　尼泊尔人民几千人四日下午在加德满都附近的拉利特普尔市举行示威，抗议印度反动派前些时候公然侵入尼泊尔境内劫走四个尼泊尔人的暴行。
    示威者在拉利特普尔市的马路上游行，愤怒高呼：“打倒印度扩张主义者！”“我们随时准备为我们的主权和独立而战斗和牺牲！”示威者举着反对印度侵略和反对印度的扩张主义政策的标语牌。
