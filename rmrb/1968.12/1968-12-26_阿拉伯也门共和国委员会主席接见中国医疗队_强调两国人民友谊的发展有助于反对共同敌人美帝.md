Title: Chairman of the Yemen Arab Republic Revolutionary Council Receives Chinese Medical Team
Author:
Date: 1968-12-26
Edition: 5

Chairman of the Yemen Arab Republic Revolutionary Council, Eryani, received the entire Chinese medical team working in the city of Taiz on December 23 at the Republican Palace in Taiz. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Eryani said that the aid provided by the Chinese people to Yemen, whether it be the medical team, roads, or textile factories, is truly selfless and unconditional. The Yemeni people and government are deeply grateful for China's assistance. He also praised the Chinese medical team members and the Chinese engineers and technicians working in Yemen for following the teachings of Chairman Mao, working together with the Yemeni people, and diligently and wholeheartedly serving the Yemeni people.

He emphasized that the development and strengthening of friendship between the peoples of Yemen and China are beneficial for opposing our common enemy, American imperialism.

Present at the reception were Yemeni Deputy Prime Minister Sabra and several other officials.

Chinese embassy officials in Yemen were also present.

### 阿拉伯也门共和国委员会主席接见中国医疗队  强调两国人民友谊的发展有助于反对共同敌人美帝

1968-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯也门共和国委员会主席接见中国医疗队
    强调两国人民友谊的发展有助于反对共同敌人美帝
    新华社萨那二十四日电　阿拉伯也门共和国委员会主席埃里亚尼，二十三日在塔伊兹市共和国宫接见了正在这个城市工作的中国医疗队全体队员，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    埃里亚尼说，中国人民给予也门的援助，无论是医疗队，公路，还是纺织厂，都是真正无私的，是不附带任何条件的，也门人民和政府非常感谢中国的援助。他还赞扬中国医疗队队员和在也门工作的中国工程技术人员遵照毛主席的教导，和也门人民一起劳动，勤劳地、全心全意地为也门人民服务。
    他强调说，也中两国人民友谊的发展和加强，有利于反对我们的共同敌人美帝国主义。
    接见时在座的有也门副总理萨布拉和一些其他官员。
    中国驻也门大使馆官员也在座。
