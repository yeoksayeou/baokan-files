Title: Cambodian Foreign Ministry Protests U.S., South Vietnam, and Thailand's Infringement of Cambodian Territory
Author:
Date: 1968-12-04
Edition: 6

Cambodian Foreign Ministry Protests U.S., South Vietnam, and Thailand's Infringement of Cambodian Territory

	Newspaper: Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh
	According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on November 27, the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a note to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime on November 26, strongly protesting the U.S.-South Vietnamese armed forces' deliberate shooting and inflicting injuries on one Cambodian citizen and causing other significant material damage in the provinces of Takeo, Kampong Cham, and Kampot on November 10, 12, and 13. The note demands that the U.S. and the Saigon puppet regime cease such actions and compensate the victims.

	The Cambodian News Agency also reported on November 27 that the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued another note on November 26. The note strongly protested the Thai puppet army's planting of landmines in Cambodian territory in Battambang Province on November 13 and 16, which resulted in the deaths and injuries of two Cambodian citizens each. The note demands that the Thai puppet regime immediately cease such actions.

	Newspaper: Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh
	According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency, the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent notes to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime, as well as the Thai puppet authorities, on November 27 and 28, respectively. The notes strongly protested the new crimes of the U.S.-South Vietnamese armed forces and Thai armed vessels invading Cambodian territory and killing Cambodian citizens. The notes demand that the U.S. government, the Saigon, and Thai puppet regimes immediately cease their criminal actions of infringing on Cambodian territory and compensate the families of the victims.

### 柬外交部抗议美国南越泰国侵犯柬领土

1968-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

    柬外交部抗议美国南越泰国侵犯柬领土
    新华社金边电　据柬埔寨新闻社十一月二十七日报道，柬埔寨外交部二十六日向美国政府和西贡傀儡集团发出一份照会，强烈抗议美国—南越武装部队十一月十日、十二日和十三日侵犯柬埔寨柴桢、干丹和茶胶三省并蓄意进行射击，打伤一名柬埔寨人并造成其它重大物质损失的罪行。照会要求美国和西贡傀儡集团停止这种行径，并对受害者进行赔偿。
    柬埔寨新闻社二十七日还发表了柬埔寨外交部二十六日发出的另一份照会。照会强烈抗议泰国傀儡军队十一月十三日和十六日在柬埔寨马德望省领土上埋设地雷，炸死炸伤柬埔寨人各两名的罪行，并要求泰国傀儡集团立即停止这种行径。
    新华社金边电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨外交部十一月二十七日和二十八日分别向美国政府和西贡傀儡集团以及泰国傀儡当局发出照会，强烈抗议美国—南越武装部队和泰国武装船只侵犯柬埔寨并打死柬埔寨居民的新的罪行。照会要求美国政府、西贡和泰国傀儡集团立即停止侵犯柬埔寨的罪恶行径，并对受害者的家属进行赔偿。
