Title: Carry Out Heart-to-Heart Talks
Author: Hong Xuan Bing, Liao Shan Hong
Date: 1968-12-08
Edition: 3

The workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team stationed at the Health School in Chaoyang, Liaoning Province, follows Chairman Mao's instruction to "carry out heart-to-heart talks." They have gone deep into the student body and extensively conducted heart-to-heart talks, effectively promoting the school's struggle, criticism, and reform.
