The U.S. Empire, Cornered and Desperate, Frantically Develops Nuclear Weapons
Brazenly Conducts Its Largest Underground Nuclear Test to Date

BEIJING, Dec. 20 (Xinhua) -- Washington -- The U.S. imperialists, cornered and desperate, are frantically developing nuclear weapons in an attempt to further blackmail the revolutionary peoples of the world with nuclear threats.

According to the U.S. Atomic Energy Commission, the U.S. government conducted its largest underground nuclear test to date at the Nevada nuclear test site on December 19.

The U.S. imperialists carried out this nuclear explosion test despite widespread protests from local residents.

The fact that the U.S. has been frequently conducting underground nuclear tests exposes the fraud of the partial nuclear test ban treaty that the U.S. and the Soviet Union jointly concocted in 1963.

### 美帝穷途末路疯狂发展核武器  悍然进行迄今为止最大地下核试验

1968-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

    美帝穷途末路疯狂发展核武器
    悍然进行迄今为止最大地下核试验
    新华社二十日讯　华盛顿消息：穷途末路的美帝国主义，正在加紧发展核武器，妄图进一步对世界革命人民进行核讹诈。
    据美国原子能委员会宣布，美国政府十九日在内华达核武器试验场进行了一次迄今为止爆炸力最大的地下核武器试验。
    美帝国主义的这次核爆炸试验，是在不顾当地居民广泛抗议的情况下进行的。
    美帝一直在频繁地进行地下核试验的事实，有力地戳穿了美苏在一九六三年合伙炮制的部分禁止核试验条约这个大骗局。
