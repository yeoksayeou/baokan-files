Title: Workers and Soldiers of the Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team at the Northeast Institute of Technology
Author: Liao Jun, Song Dong
Date: 1968-12-05
Edition: 5

The workers and soldiers of the Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team stationed at the Northeast Institute of Technology are actively promoting the Party's three great styles of work. They frequently take the initiative to go among the people, solicit opinions, and carry out criticism and self-criticism, thereby strengthening their ties with the masses. This is a scene of the propaganda team members listening to the opinions of the students.
