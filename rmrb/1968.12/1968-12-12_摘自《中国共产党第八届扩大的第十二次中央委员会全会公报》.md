Title: Excerpts from the Communiqué of the Twelfth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1968-12-12
Edition: 3

The Plenum calls on all Party members and the people of the entire country to continue to carry out in-depth revolutionary criticism and struggle, thoroughly eliminating the reactionary revisionist ideology of the handful of top capitalist-roaders within the Party, such as Liu Shaoqi.
