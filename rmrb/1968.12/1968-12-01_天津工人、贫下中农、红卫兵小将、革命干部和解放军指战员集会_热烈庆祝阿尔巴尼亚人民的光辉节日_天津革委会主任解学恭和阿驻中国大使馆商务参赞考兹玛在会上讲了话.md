Title: Tianjin Workers, Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants, Red Guards, Revolutionary Cadres, and PLA Soldiers Celebrate Albania's Glorious Holiday
Author:
Date: 1968-12-01
Edition: 3

Tianjin workers, poor and lower-middle peasants, Red Guards, revolutionary cadres, and PLA soldiers held a gathering to enthusiastically celebrate the 24th anniversary of Albania's liberation.

The head of the Tianjin Revolutionary Committee, Xue Xuegong, and the commercial counselor of the Albanian Embassy in China, Qiaozima, both spoke at the meeting. In his speech, Comrade Xue Xuegong enthusiastically praised the Albanian people for hoisting the banner of Marxism-Leninism under the leadership of Comrade Enver Hoxha and the Albanian Labor Party, for taking up arms and tools, for self-reliance, and for bravely fighting to achieve brilliant successes in the socialist revolution and socialist construction. He praised the Albanian people for their unyielding struggle against imperialism and modern revisionism, which has powerfully supported the revolutionary cause of the people of all countries and set a brilliant example for the revolutionary people of the whole world.

Qiaozima, who came specially from Beijing to participate in the celebration, said in his speech that over the past 24 years, our party, under the leadership of Comrade Enver Hoxha, has consistently been loyal to Marxism-Leninism. In the socialist revolution and socialist construction, a series of revolutionary measures have been taken, greatly enhancing the people's enthusiasm for labor and revolutionary spirit, enhancing the revolutionary vigilance to defend the motherland, strengthening the proletarian dictatorship, and effectively blocking the road to the emergence of revisionism and the restoration of capitalism. He also introduced the Albanian people's struggle against imperialism led by the United States and modern revisionism centered on the Soviet revisionists.

Comrade Qiaozima enthusiastically praised the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China as a great event of great significance for the Chinese people and the socialist cause of the whole world. He wished the Chinese people great victories in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and socialist construction, and highly praised the achievements of the people of Tianjin in holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and production.

Those attending today's meeting included Tianjin military responsible comrades Wang Yi and Zhu Biao, representatives of the Tianjin Workers' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team, representatives of the Tianjin Workers' Congress, Peasants' Congress, higher education Red Guards Congress, and middle school Red Guards Congress.

After the meeting, the Albanian film "Broad Horizons" was screened.

### 天津工人、贫下中农、红卫兵小将、革命干部和解放军指战员集会  热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民的光辉节日  天津革委会主任解学恭和阿驻中国大使馆商务参赞考兹玛在会上讲了话

1968-12-01
第3版()
专栏：

    天津工人、贫下中农、红卫兵小将、革命干部和解放军指战员集会
    热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民的光辉节日
    天津革委会主任解学恭和阿驻中国大使馆商务参赞考兹玛在会上讲了话
    新华社天津三十日电  天津市工人、贫下中农、红卫兵小将、革命干部和人民解放军指战员一千多人，今天举行集会，热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十四周年。
    天津市革委会主任解学恭和阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆商务参赞考兹玛分别在会上讲了话。解学恭同志在讲话中热情地赞扬阿尔巴尼亚人民在以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，高举马克思列宁主义的大旗，一手拿枪，一手拿镐，自力更生，英勇奋战，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中取得了光辉的成就；赞扬阿尔巴尼亚人民对帝国主义、现代修正主义进行了不屈不挠的斗争，有力地支持了世界各国人民的革命事业，为全世界革命人民树立了光辉的榜样。
    专程从北京前来参加庆祝大会的阿尔巴尼亚驻我国大使馆商务参赞考兹玛在讲话中说，二十四年来，我们党在恩维尔·霍查同志的领导下，一贯忠于马克思列宁主义，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中采取了一系列的革命措施，大大地提高了人民的劳动热情和革命精神，提高了保卫祖国的革命警惕性，使我国的无产阶级专政得到了加强，并有把握地堵塞了产生修正主义和复辟资本主义的道路。他还介绍了阿尔巴尼亚人民在同以美国为首的帝国主义和以苏修为中心的现代修正主义所进行的针锋相对的斗争。
    考兹玛同志热情赞颂中国共产党第八届扩大的十二中全会对于中国人民和全世界的社会主义事业，都是具有伟大意义的事件。他祝中国人民在无产阶级文化大革命和社会主义建设中取得伟大的胜利，并且高度赞扬了天津市人民高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，在无产阶级文化大革命和生产中所取得的成就。
    出席今天大会的，有天津驻军负责人王一、朱彪和天津市工人毛泽东思想宣传队的代表，天津市工代会、农代会、大专院校红代会、中学红代会的代表。
    大会结束后，放映了阿尔巴尼亚影片《广阔的地平线》。
