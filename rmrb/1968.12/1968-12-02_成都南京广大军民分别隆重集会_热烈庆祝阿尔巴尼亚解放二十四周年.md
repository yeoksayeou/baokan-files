Title: Massive Military and Civilian Gatherings in Chengdu and Nanjing Celebrate the 24th Anniversary of Albania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1968-12-02
Edition: 2

Massive military and civilian gatherings in Chengdu and Nanjing celebrate the 24th anniversary of Albania's liberation.

BEIJING, Dec. 1 (Xinhua) — The revolutionary masses of Sichuan Province's Chengdu City and Jiangsu Province's Nanjing City, along with the People's Liberation Army's soldiers and officers, held grand meetings with over a thousand participants each to celebrate the 24th anniversary of Albania's liberation.

Albania's Ambassador to China, Counselor Aqili and his wife, and Albania's representative to China, Naki, traveled to Nanjing and Chengdu respectively to participate in the celebrations and were warmly received.

In Chengdu, Vice Chairman Li Dazhang of the Sichuan Revolutionary Committee and Chairman Sun Hongdao of the Chengdu Revolutionary Committee, and in Nanjing, leaders of the Nanjing Revolutionary Committee, delivered speeches at the respective celebrations. They praised the Albanian people, who have a glorious revolutionary tradition, for their self-reliance, hard work, and steadfastness in following the socialist path under the leadership of Comrade Enver Hoxha and the Albanian Party of Labor. They highlighted the remarkable achievements in socialist revolution and construction, as well as in the struggle against imperialism and revisionism over the past 24 years. Li Dazhang noted that today, Albania, this great socialist beacon in Europe, shines brighter than ever. He stated that the 70 million military and civilian people of Sichuan Province will, as instructed by the great leader Chairman Mao, "stand firmly with the Albanian people in all circumstances," fight together, and achieve victory together. The Nanjing Revolutionary Committee leaders expressed that the revolutionary people of Nanjing, along with the people of the entire nation, will stand shoulder to shoulder with the heroic Albanian people in the joint struggle against U.S. imperialism, Soviet revisionism, and their lackeys.

Aqili and Naki detailed Albania's achievements under the leadership of Comrade Enver Hoxha and the Albanian Party of Labor in socialist revolution and construction, and in the struggle against imperialism, modern revisionism, and reactionaries worldwide. Aqili emphasized that the resolution passed at the enlarged 12th Plenum of the 8th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, chaired by Chairman Mao, is a historic testament to the complete and brilliant victory of Mao Zedong's revolutionary line. This resolution has buried the reactionary bourgeois headquarters of Liu Shaoqi and ensured that the great People's Republic of China, led by Chairman Mao, remains the greatest banner of the world proletarian revolution and Marxism-Leninism. He declared, "We assure you, as Comrade Mao Zedong said, we will forever stand together, fight together, and achieve victory together until the cause of the world proletarian revolution is completely victorious." Naki praised Sichuan Province's revolutionary people for their contributions in the struggle against U.S. imperialism and the Chiang Kai-shek bandits, and their great victories in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution led by Chairman Mao. Their speeches were met with enthusiastic applause.

Chairman Zhang Guohua of the Sichuan Revolutionary Committee attended the celebration in Chengdu. Attendees at the celebrations in Chengdu and Nanjing included local Revolutionary Committee leaders, People's Liberation Army commanders, representatives from workers' Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams, representatives from the army's "Four Good" platoons and "Five Good" soldiers, and representatives from local workers', peasants', and red guards' congresses.

### 成都南京广大军民分别隆重集会  热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十四周年

1968-12-02
第2版()
专栏：

    成都南京广大军民分别隆重集会
    热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十四周年
    新华社一日讯　四川省成都市和江苏省南京市的革命群众以及人民解放军指战员，今晚分别隆重举行有一千多人参加的大会，热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十四周年。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆公使衔参赞阿果利和他的夫人，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆代表纳启，分别专程到南京和成都参加庆祝大会，受到了热烈的欢迎。
    四川省革委会副主任李大章、成都市革委会主任孙洪道，以及南京市革委会负责人，分别在成都和南京市的庆祝大会上讲了话。他们热情赞扬具有光荣革命传统的阿尔巴尼亚人民，二十四年来在以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的英明领导下，自力更生，艰苦奋斗，坚持走社会主义道路，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中，在反帝、反修的斗争中取得了辉煌的胜利，热烈赞扬阿尔巴尼亚革命化运动的伟大成就。李大章同志在讲话中指出，今天，阿尔巴尼亚这盏欧洲的伟大的社会主义的明灯放射出的光芒，比以往任何时候都更加灿烂辉煌。他说，四川省七千万军民，坚决遵循伟大领袖毛主席的教导：“在任何时候、任何情况下，都将始终如一地站在兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民一边”，同英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民团结在一起，战斗在一起，胜利在一起。南京市革委会负责人在讲话中表示，南京市革命人民和全国人民一道，同英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民在反对美帝、苏修及其走狗的共同斗争中，互相鼓舞，互相支持，并肩前进。
    阿果利同志和纳启同志在讲话中详细地介绍了阿尔巴尼亚人民在恩维尔·霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党领导下，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中，在反对帝国主义、反对现代修正主义和各国反动派的斗争中所取得的伟大胜利。阿果利同志说，在中国人民伟大领袖毛主席亲自主持下召开的中国共产党八届扩大的十二中全会所通过的具有历史意义的决议，是毛泽东主席革命路线取得彻底的、光辉的胜利的集中体现。这一决议永远地埋葬了刘少奇的反动的资产阶级司令部，并保证了毛主席领导的伟大的人民中国这个世界无产阶级革命和马克思列宁主义的最伟大的旗手永不变色。他说：我们向你们保证，就象毛泽东同志所说的那样，我们将永远团结在一起，战斗在一起，胜利在一起，直到世界无产阶级革命事业取得彻底胜利。纳启同志在讲话中赞扬了四川省革命人民在反对美帝国主义和它的奴才蒋介石匪帮的斗争中作出的贡献，以及在毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命中所取得的伟大胜利。他们的讲话受到了热烈的欢迎。
    四川省革命委员会主任张国华参加了成都市的庆祝大会。参加成都、南京两地庆祝大会的，还有当地革委会的负责人，人民解放军驻军的负责人，工人毛泽东思想宣传队的代表，人民解放军四好连队、五好战士的代表，以及当地工代会、农代会、红代会的代表。
