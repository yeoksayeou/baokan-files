Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-12-25
Edition: 1

	It is very necessary for educated youth to go to the countryside to receive re-education from the poor and lower-middle peasants. We must persuade cadres and other people in the cities to send their children who have finished junior high, senior high, or college to the countryside. We must mobilize them for this purpose. The comrades in the countryside in various places should welcome them.

### 毛主席语录

1968-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    知识青年到农村去，接受贫下中农的再教育，很有必要。要说服城里干部和其他人，把自己初中、高中、大学毕业的子女，送到乡下去，来一个动员。各地农村的同志应当欢迎他们去。
