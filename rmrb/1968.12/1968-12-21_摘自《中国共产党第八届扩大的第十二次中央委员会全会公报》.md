Title: Report of the Twelfth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1968-12-21
Edition: 6

The plenary session holds that, as Comrade Mao Zedong has pointed out, the world revolution has entered a great new era. The revolutionary movements of the people of all countries in the world are developing vigorously.
