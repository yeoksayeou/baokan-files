Title: Celebrating the Seventh Anniversary of Tanganyika's Independence
Author:
Date: 1968-12-11
Edition: 6

Tanzania's people celebrated the seventh anniversary of Tanganyika's independence on December 9.

President Nyerere, accompanied by First Vice President Karume and Second Vice President Kawawa, presided over the celebration held at the National Stadium in the capital, Dar es Salaam.

President Nyerere inspected the guard of honor composed of members of the Tanzania People's Defense Force, the police, and the National Service Corps. He also presented awards to farmers from various provinces who had achieved outstanding results in agricultural production.

More than a thousand people attended the celebration, including government ministers, leaders of the Tanganyika African National Union and the African-Socialist Party, diplomats from various countries, and representatives of various African national liberation movements stationed in Dar es Salaam.

In the evening, President Nyerere delivered a radio broadcast to the nation.

### 发庆祝坦桑尼亚（大陆部分）独立七周年  达累斯萨拉姆举行庆祝活动

1968-12-11
第6版()
专栏：

    发庆祝坦桑尼亚（大陆部分）独立七周年
    达累斯萨拉姆举行庆祝活动
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆十日电  坦桑尼亚人民九日庆祝坦桑尼亚（大陆部分）独立七周年。
    尼雷尔总统在第一副总统卡鲁姆和第二副总统卡瓦瓦的陪同下，主持了在首都达累斯萨拉姆国家运动场举行的庆祝活动。
    尼雷尔总统检阅了由坦桑尼亚人民国防军、警察和全国服务队队员组成的仪仗队，并向各省选出的在农业生产上做出成绩的农民颁发奖杯和奖状。
    出席庆祝活动的有一千多人，其中有政府部长、坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟和非洲—设拉子党的负责人、各国外交使节以及驻在达累斯萨拉姆的各个非洲民族解放运动的代表。
    当晚，尼雷尔总统通过电台向全国发表了广播讲话。
