Title: Nepalese Students Protest Against Indian Reactionaries
Author:
Date: 1968-12-05
Edition: 6

Hundreds of Nepalese students held a demonstration in Kathmandu on December 2 to protest against the Indian reactionaries' abduction of Nepalese people. The demonstrators angrily shouted, "Down with Indian expansionists!"

According to a report by Nepal's _Deshantar_ newspaper on November 23, some Indian officials recently intruded into Nepal's territory and seized four Nepalese people in the Sushta area of Nepal's Parashi District, detaining them in the Betia prison in India's Bihar State.

The _Deshantar_ newspaper reported on December 2 that Nepal's Foreign Minister, Jyoti Bahadur Rajbhandari, summoned the Indian ambassador to Nepal to express opposition to this action by the Indian authorities and to demand the immediate release of these Nepalese individuals.

In recent days, Nepalese newspapers have published editorials condemning the atrocities of the Indian reactionary government.

### 尼泊尔学生示威反对印度反动派  示威者抗议印度反动派劫走尼泊尔人的暴行

1968-12-05
第6版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔学生示威反对印度反动派
    示威者抗议印度反动派劫走尼泊尔人的暴行
    新华社加德满都四日电　几百名尼泊尔学生二日在加德满都举行示威，抗议印度反动派劫走尼泊尔人民的暴行。示威者愤怒地高呼：“打倒印度扩张主义者！”
    据尼泊尔《祖国报》十一月二十三日报道，一些印度官员不久以前侵入尼泊尔境内，在尼泊尔帕尔希县的苏斯塔地方，抓走了四个尼泊尔人，并把他们关在印度比哈尔邦的贝提亚监狱。
    《祖国报》二日报道，尼泊尔外交大臣杰亨德拉·巴哈杜尔·拉杰班达里召见了印度驻尼泊尔大使，表示反对印度当局的这一行动，同时要求立即释放这几名尼泊尔人。
    尼泊尔报纸过去几天纷纷发表社论，谴责印度反动政府的暴行。
