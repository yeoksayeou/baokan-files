Title: U.S. Aircraft and Vessels Intrude into Our Airspace and Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1967-07-20
Edition: 5

U.S. aircraft and vessels intrude into our airspace and territorial waters

We issue the 439th serious warning

	New China News Agency, July 19th

	On July 19th, from 9:46 to 10:02, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace of Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 10:38 to 12:50, one U.S. military vessel intruded into the territorial waters southeast of Pinghai in Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 11:53 to 18:10, one U.S. military vessel intruded into the territorial waters east of the Minjiang River mouth in Fujian Province.

	Regarding the military provocations by U.S. military aircraft and vessels, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on instructions, issues the 439th serious warning.

### 美机美舰侵入我领空领海  我提出第四百三十九次严重警告

1967-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    美机美舰侵入我领空领海
    我提出第四百三十九次严重警告
    新华社十九日讯  七月十九日九时四十六分至十时零二分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛地区领空。同日十时三十八分至十二时五十分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我广东省平海东南领海。同日十一时五十三分至十八时十分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省闽江口以东领海。
    对于美国军用飞机和军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百三十九次严重警告。
