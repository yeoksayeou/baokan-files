Title: Delegation of the Union of Black African Students in France Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-07-29
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation, a delegation from the Union of Black African Students in France arrived in Beijing on July 25. The delegation is led by its chairman and the union's external affairs secretary, Ovide Laugo.

### 留法黑非洲学联代表团到京

1967-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    留法黑非洲学联代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　应全国学联邀请来我国访问的留法黑非洲学生联合会代表团，在团长、留法黑非学联外事书记奥维德劳戈率领下，于二十五日到达北京。
