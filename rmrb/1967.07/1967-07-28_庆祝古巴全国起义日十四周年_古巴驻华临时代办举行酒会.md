Title: Celebrating the 14th Anniversary of the Cuban National Uprising
Author:
Date: 1967-07-28
Edition: 5

The Charge d'Affaires of the Cuban Embassy in China, Garcia, held a reception today to celebrate the 14th anniversary of the "July 26" Cuban National Uprising.

Relevant officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and other departments were invited to attend the reception.

### 庆祝古巴全国起义日十四周年  古巴驻华临时代办举行酒会

1967-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝古巴全国起义日十四周年
    古巴驻华临时代办举行酒会
    新华社二十六日讯　古巴共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办加西亚今天举行酒会，庆祝“七·二六”古巴全国起义日十四周年。
    外交部等有关方面负责人应邀出席了酒会。
