Title: Yemen's Vice Premier Meets Chinese Chargé d'Affaires
Author:
Date: 1967-07-29
Edition: 5

Yemen's Vice Premier Abdullah al-Ghazali met with the Chinese chargé d'affaires in Yemen, Zheng Kangping, and the head of the Chinese expert group assisting at the Sana'a textile factory, Cui Junxian, on the 23rd. They had a friendly conversation.

Al-Ghazali highly praised the Sana'a textile factory, which is being built with Chinese assistance, and commended the hard work of the Chinese experts.

### 也门副总理接见我临时代办

1967-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    也门副总理接见我临时代办
    新华社萨那电　阿拉伯也门共和国副总理阿卜杜拉·加齐兰二十三日接见了中国驻也门大使馆临时代办郑康平和在萨那纺织厂帮助工作的中国专家组长崔俊贤，并同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    加齐兰在谈到由中国帮助也门建设的萨那纺织厂时，给予很高的评价，并赞扬了中国专家们的辛勤劳动。
