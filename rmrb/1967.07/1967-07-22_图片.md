Title: Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants and Revolutionary Cadres of Li Village Brigade in Fengying Commune, Xingfen County, Shanxi, Raise the Red Flag of Criticizing the Revisionism of China's Khrushchev
Author: Qiǒu Quánshòu
Date: 1967-07-22
Edition: 2

Poor and lower-middle peasants and revolutionary cadres of Li Village Brigade in Fengying Commune, Xingfen County, Shanxi, raised high the red flag of criticizing the revisionism of China's Khrushchev. They held a criticism meeting on the threshing floor, severely criticizing the crimes of China's Khrushchev.
