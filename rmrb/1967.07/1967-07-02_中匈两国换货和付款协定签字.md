Title: China and Hungary Sign Agreement on Barter and Payment
Author:
Date: 1967-07-02
Edition: 5

	China and Hungary Sign Agreement on Barter and Payment

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Budapest: The 1967 barter and payment agreement between China and Hungary was signed in Budapest on June 22. The Chinese side was represented by Han Kexua, head of the Chinese government trade delegation and Chinese ambassador to Hungary, while the Hungarian side was represented by Sarai Belo, head of the Hungarian government trade delegation and deputy minister of foreign trade.

### 中匈两国换货和付款协定签字

1967-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

　　中匈两国换货和付款协定签字
    据新华社布达佩斯电　中匈两国一九六七年交换货物和付款协定于六月二十二日在布达佩斯签字。在协定上代表中国方面签字的是中国政府贸易代表团团长、我国驻匈牙利大使韩克华，代表匈牙利方面的是匈牙利政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长萨拉伊·贝洛。
