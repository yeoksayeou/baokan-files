	Chairman Mao's Quotations

	The American monopolistic capitalist groups, if they insist on pursuing their policy of aggression and war, will inevitably one day be sentenced to death by the people of the whole world. The same will be true of other accomplices of the United States. September 8, 1958, speech at the highest state council meeting.
