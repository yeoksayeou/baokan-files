Title: Follow Chairman Mao's Words, Resume Classes and Make Revolution
Author: Wáng Dōng
Date: 1967-07-23
Edition: 6

Young revolutionaries of Shanghai's Kongjiang Middle School, filled with hatred for China's Khrushchev, launched a school-wide revolutionary criticism campaign. The image shows young revolutionaries using the sharp weapon of Mao Zedong Thought to denounce China's Khrushchev.

	Recently, several universities in Beijing have joined forces to denounce the biggest capitalist-roaders within the Party, determined to push the revolutionary criticism to a new high, and to criticize and expose him politically, ideologically, and theoretically.

	Revolutionary teachers and students of Shijingshan Middle School, taking Chairman Mao's "May 7th Directive" as their highest guiding principle, have carried forward the revolutionary spirit of the proletariat, thoroughly criticized the harm caused by the counter-revolutionary revisionist educational line, and blazed a trail for educational revolution. Photographed by Liu Zhenxiang of this newspaper.

	Beijing Institute of Aeronautics, taking Chairman Mao's "May 7th Directive" as their highest guiding principle, has made learning industry, agriculture, military affairs, and some professional knowledge the content of their classes for resuming classes and making revolution. This shows Beijing Institute of Aeronautics students listening to workers' lectures in an on-campus factory.

	Beijing Institute of Posts and Telecommunications, during the resumption of classes and making revolution, held an exhibition on the two-line struggle in the educational field to promote the school's struggle, criticism, and reform, and to launch a new high tide of revolutionary criticism. Revolutionary teachers and students are determined to turn the school into a red Mao Zedong Thought school.

	Resuming classes and making revolution means resuming the classes of Mao Zedong Thought. With the help of the PLA, Shijingshan Middle School in Beijing has achieved revolutionary great unity and resumed classes and made revolution. Photographed by Wang Dong of this newspaper.

### 听毛主席的话，复课闹革命（图片）
王东
1967-07-23
第6版()
专栏：

    听毛主席的话，复课闹革命
    上海市控江中学的革命小将，怀着对中国的赫鲁晓夫无比痛恨，展开了全校的革命大批判运动。图为革命小将用毛泽东思想的锐利武器，痛斥中国的赫鲁晓夫。
                      本报记者　王景仁摄
    最近北京的几所大学联合在一起，痛斥党内最大的走资本主义道路当权派，决心把革命的大批判推向一个新高潮，从政治上、思想上、理论上把他批倒、批臭。	                          本报记者　王东摄
    石景山中学的革命师生以毛主席的“五·七”指示为最高奋斗纲领，发扬无产阶级革命精神，彻底批判反革命修正主义教育路线对他们的毒害，闯出一条教育革命的道路来。本报记者　刘振祥摄
    北京航空学院以毛主席的“五·七”指示为最高奋斗纲领，把学工、学农，学军，学一些专业知识作为复课闹革命的教学内容。这是北京航空学院学生在校内实习工厂，听工人讲课。
                          新华社记者　顾德华摄
    北京邮电学院在复课闹革命中，举办了教育战线上的两条路线斗争展览，以促进学校的斗、批、改，掀起革命大批判的新高潮。革命师生决心把学校办成红彤彤的毛泽东思想大学校。
                    新华社记者　顾德华摄
    复课闹革命，就是要复毛泽东思想的课。北京石景山中学在解放军的帮助下，全校实现了革命大联合和复课闹革命。
                      本报记者　王东摄
