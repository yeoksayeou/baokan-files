Title: The Revolutionary Masses of Qinghai's First Machine Tool Factory
Author: Lǐ Zǐqīng
Date: 1967-07-29
Edition: 3

The revolutionary masses of Qinghai's First Machine Tool Factory, with the assistance of the People's Liberation Army, have correctly handled the relationships among revolutionary mass organizations, between revolutionary masses and those who have been misled, and between revolutionary masses and cadres, using Mao Zedong Thought as their weapon.
