Title: Indonesian Youth Voice Warmly Hails Red Flag Editorial Greatly Encourages Indonesian Revolutionary People
Author:
Date: 1967-07-27
Edition: 5

The Indonesian Youth Voice on July 17 published an editorial warmly hailing the editorial titled "Indonesian People Unite to Overthrow the Fascist Regime" published in the 11th issue of the Red Flag Magazine. The editorial emphasized: "Based on the great and invincible Mao Zedong Thought, especially Comrade Mao Zedong's thesis that 'political power grows out of the barrel of a gun,' Indonesian revolutionaries and Indonesian Marxist-Leninists must take the path of armed revolution against armed counter-revolution."

The editorial said, "The editorial in the Red Flag Magazine has greatly encouraged the Indonesian revolutionary people and dealt a heavy blow to U.S. imperialism, Soviet modern revisionism, and their running dogs, the Suharto-Nasution fascist military regime."

The editorial said, "We, the Indonesian revolutionary people and youth, express our deepest gratitude to the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time, the great leader of the world's revolutionary people, Comrade Mao Zedong, to the great, glorious, and correct Communist Party of China, and to the Chinese people for their great support for the Indonesian people's revolutionary struggle to overthrow the Suharto-Nasution fascist military regime."

The editorial said, "The experience of the Chinese people and the people of other countries shows that once revolutionary masses are armed with Mao Zedong Thought, they gain immense strength to defeat the enemy." "The experience of armed struggles by the people of Burma, India, Thailand, Malaya, and other countries has proven the brilliance of the great and invincible Mao Zedong Thought."

The editorial said, "Chairman Mao teaches us: 'The central task and highest form of revolution is to seize political power through armed struggle, to solve problems through war. This Marxist-Leninist revolutionary principle is universally applicable, whether in China or abroad.'"

The editorial said, "Currently, the main task facing the Indonesian Communist Party is to regroup forces, especially the scattered people's armed forces, to establish revolutionary base areas in the countryside, and to carry out armed peasant land revolutions under proletarian leadership."

The editorial said, "In Indonesia, although the people's armed forces are still small, their future is bright. Chairman Mao once said in 1930: 'China is full of dry grass all over the country, and it will soon burn into a raging fire. The phrase 'spark can start a prairie fire' is an apt description of the current situation.' The current situation in Indonesia is the same; the broad masses of Indonesian revolutionary people demand revolution; the economic situation of the Suharto-Nasution fascist military regime is inevitably heading towards bankruptcy, and its regime is fragile and rotten." The editorial said, "Now is the time for revolutionaries to shift the focus of their work to the countryside, to organize forces in the countryside, to surround the cities from the countryside, and to further seize the cities."

The editorial stressed, "Mao Zedong Thought has shown the Indonesian people the path to liberation. We are determined to forever follow the path of Mao Zedong and act according to the instructions of Comrade Mao Zedong."

### 《印尼青年之声》热烈欢呼我《红旗》社论极大鼓舞印尼革命人民  印尼革命人民决心永远走毛主席的道路  革命群众一旦用毛泽东思想武装起来就能战胜敌人

1967-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    《印尼青年之声》热烈欢呼我《红旗》社论极大鼓舞印尼革命人民
    印尼革命人民决心永远走毛主席的道路
    革命群众一旦用毛泽东思想武装起来就能战胜敌人
    新华社二十六日讯　《印尼青年之声》十七日发表社论，热烈欢呼《红旗》杂志第十一期发表题为《印度尼西亚人民团结起来，为推翻法西斯政权而斗争》的社论，并且强调指出：“根据伟大的、战无不胜的毛泽东思想，特别是毛泽东同志关于‘枪杆子里面出政权’的论断，印尼革命者和印尼马克思列宁主义者必须走武装的革命反对武装的反革命的道路。”
    社论说，《红旗》杂志的这篇社论，“极大地鼓舞了印尼革命人民，沉重地打击了美帝国主义、苏联现代修正主义和它们的走狗苏哈托—纳苏蒂安法西斯军人政权”。
    社论说：“我们印尼革命人民和青年向当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者、世界革命人民的伟大领袖毛泽东同志，向伟大的、光荣的和正确的中国共产党，向中国人民表示最大的感谢，感谢他们对印尼人民为推翻苏哈托—纳苏蒂安法西斯军人政权而进行的革命斗争的巨大支持。”
    社论说：“中国人民和各国革命人民的经验表明，革命群众一旦用毛泽东思想武装起来，他们就获得无比巨大的力量来战胜敌人”，“缅甸、印度、泰国、马来亚和其它国家的人民武装斗争的经验证明了伟大的、战无不胜的毛泽东思想的光辉”。
    社论说：“毛泽东主席教导我们：‘革命的中心任务和最高形式是武装夺取政权，是战争解决问题。这个马克思列宁主义的革命原则是普遍地对的，不论在中国在外国，一概都是对的。’”
    社论说，当前，摆在印尼共产党面前的主要任务是，重新集结力量，特别是还处于分散状态的人民武装力量，在农村建立革命根据地，开展无产阶级领导下的武装的农民土地革命。
    社论说，在印尼，虽然人民武装力量仍然很小，但是它的前途是光明的。毛泽东主席在一九三○年曾经说过：“中国是全国都布满了干柴，很快就会燃成烈火。‘星火燎原’的话，正是时局发展的适当的描写。”目前印尼的局势也是这样，广大的印尼革命人民要求革命；苏哈托—纳苏蒂安法西斯军人政权的经济不可避免地要破产，它的政权是脆弱的和腐朽的。社论说：“现在已经是革命者把工作的重点移到农村，在农村组织力量，从农村包围城市，并进一步夺取城市的时候了。”
    社论强调说：“毛泽东思想给印尼人民指明了取得解放的道路。我们决心永远走毛泽东的路，按着毛泽东同志的指示办事。”
