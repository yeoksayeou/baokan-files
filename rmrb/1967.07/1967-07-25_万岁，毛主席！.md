Title: Long Live Chairman Mao!
Author:
Date: 1967-07-25
Edition: 5

    Long Live Chairman Mao!
    An American reader wrote a passionate poem, praising Chairman Mao: It is you, our commander, who gathers the wisdom and strength of the masses into a vibrant ocean, accumulating into towering mountains symbolizing the future... It is you, the great teacher of the world's people, who point out the revolutionary direction for us to change the world. Valleys, plains, mountains, and oceans will forever praise you and sing for you. Long Live Chairman Mao! Wish you eternal life!

### 万岁，毛主席！

1967-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    万岁，毛主席！
    一位美国读者写了一首热情洋溢的诗，歌颂毛主席：是您，我们的统帅，把群众的智慧和力量汇集成生气蓬勃的海洋，积聚为象征未来的巍峨群山……是您，世界人民的伟大导师，为我们改造世界指出了革命的方向。溪谷原野、群山海洋，将永远赞颂您，为您歌唱。万岁，毛主席！祝您万寿无疆！
