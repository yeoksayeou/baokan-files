Title: Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Achieve New Victories in Brave Struggles
Author:
Date: 1967-07-28
Edition: 5

Lao patriotic military and civilians have achieved new victories in their brave struggles. People from all walks of life in Khamkeut braved the rain to hold a rally, strongly condemning the heinous crimes committed by U.S. imperialism in its aggression against Vietnam and Laos.

	KHAMKEUT, July 26 (Xinhua) — According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio on July 25, the military and civilians of Xieng Khouang Province launched a brave attack on the enemy forces stationed in Ban Fon on the night of July 19. They destroyed the enemy's stronghold, annihilated 92 enemy soldiers, and seized 40 weapons along with a large quantity of ammunition and military supplies.

	The Supreme Command of the Lao People's Liberation Army, led by Kaysone Phomvihane, sent a telegram on July 21 to the military and civilians of Xieng Khouang Province, enthusiastically commending them for their brilliant victory. The telegram stated that this victory demonstrates the progress made by the Lao military and civilians in their struggle against the U.S. aggressors and their lackeys. It also serves as a warning to U.S. imperialism and its lackeys: the more crimes they commit, the heavier the blows they will receive from the Lao military and civilians.

	KHAMKEUT, July 27 (Xinhua) — According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio, the military and civilians of the Nam Bo and Nam Tu areas of Luang Prabang Province fought bravely on July 11 and 12, achieving new victories. According to statistics, during the battle, the patriotic military and civilians killed 37 enemy soldiers, captured 4, and seized a batch of weapons and military supplies, including one recoilless gun and six machine guns.

	The broadcast also reported that the patriotic military and civilians of Xam Neua Province attacked the enemy command post in the Tad Tong area on July 11 and 17, and repulsed the enemy's advance in the Phou Sou area. In these battles, they killed 32 enemy soldiers, wounded many, and seized and destroyed a large number of weapons, ammunition, and military supplies.

	KHAMKEUT, July 26 (Xinhua) — More than 2,000 people from all walks of life in Khamkeut braved the rain to hold a rally on July 26, firmly supporting the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation, and strongly condemning the heinous crimes committed by U.S. imperialism against the Vietnamese and Lao peoples.

	The rally unanimously passed a resolution, severely condemning the Johnson administration for escalating the war on one hand and playing the "peace talk" trick on the other to cover up its evil scheme of expanding the aggression in Vietnam.

	The General Secretary of the Lao Patriotic Front, Nouhak Phoumsavanh, and the head of the Vietnamese Economic and Cultural Delegation in Laos spoke at the rally.

	The head of the Chinese Economic and Cultural Delegation in Laos was also invited to attend the rally.

### 老挝爱国军民英勇奋战取得新胜利  康开各界人民冒雨集会强烈谴责美帝侵略越南老挝的滔天罪行

1967-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民英勇奋战取得新胜利
    康开各界人民冒雨集会强烈谴责美帝侵略越南老挝的滔天罪行
    新华社康开二十六日电　据巴特寮电台二十五日广播：老挝东方省军民十九日夜间英勇袭击盘踞在班丰的敌军，摧毁敌军据点，全歼敌军九十二人，缴获武器四十件以及大批弹药和军用物资。
    老挝人民解放军最高指挥部总指挥坎代·西番顿二十一日打电报给东方省军民，热烈表彰他们取得的这一辉煌战果。电报说，这一胜利表明，老挝军民在战胜美国强盗及其走狗的战斗中有了新的进步，同时这也是对美帝国主义及其走狗的一个警告：它们越犯下罪行，就越遭到老挝军民的沉重打击。
    新华社康开二十七日电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝琅勃拉邦省南博和南吐地区军民，在七月十一日和十二日连续英勇奋战，又取得新的胜利。据统计，在战斗中，爱国军民打死了敌人三十七名，俘虏四名，缴获无后座力炮一门、机枪六挺以及其它武器和军用物资一批。
    另据广播，川圹省爱国军民在七月十一日和十七日袭击塔通地区的敌人指挥所，并且抗击了富苏地区敌人的进攻，在这些战斗中，共打死敌人三十二名，打伤多名，缴获和击毁许多武器、弹药和军用物资。
    据新华社康开二十六日电　老挝康开各界人民两千多人二十六日冒雨举行集会，坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国斗争，强烈谴责美帝国主义对越南人民和老挝人民犯下的滔天罪行。
    集会一致通过决议，严厉谴责约翰逊政府一面加紧战争“升级”，一面玩弄“和谈”骗局来掩盖它扩大侵越战争的罪恶阴谋。
    老挝爱国战线党中央委员会总书记富米·冯维希、越南驻老挝经济文化代表团的负责人在会上讲了话。
    中国驻老挝经济文化代表团的负责人也应邀出席了这次集会。
