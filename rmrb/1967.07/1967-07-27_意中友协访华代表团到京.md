Title: Delegation from the Italy-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-07-27
Edition: 5

A delegation from the Italy-China Cultural Friendship Association, led by Enrico Lambardi, a member of the association's executive committee, arrived in Beijing on July 25.

The delegation members include: Mario Gemona, a member of the executive committee, Carmelo Fragomeni, Floriano Balestrieri, and Rino Perico, all members of the association's national committee.

### 意中友协访华代表团到京

1967-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    意中友协访华代表团到京
    新华社二十六日讯　意中文化友好关系协会访华代表团，由团长、协会主席团成员恩里克·拉姆巴尔蒂率领，二十五日到达北京。
    代表团团员有：协会主席团成员马里奥·杰莫纳特，协会全国委员会成员卡尔梅罗·弗拉戈梅尼，弗洛里阿诺·巴莱斯特里，里诺·佩里柯。
