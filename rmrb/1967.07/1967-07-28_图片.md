Title: Capital Red Guards Alliance Central Institute of Nationalities Antaeus Commune
Author:
Date: 1967-07-28
Edition: 6

The Capital Red Guards Alliance, along with the Central Institute of Nationalities Antaeus Commune, held a grand rally to celebrate the great victory of the Cultural Revolution. Thousands of revolutionary masses gathered at the institute to express their firm support for Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line.

Comrade Wang, a representative of the Central Institute of Nationalities Antaeus Commune, delivered an impassioned speech. He emphasized that the Cultural Revolution is a great revolution that touches on every aspect of superstructure and every sphere of the superstructure. It is a profound revolution that touches the soul and is a revolution of the masses. He called on all revolutionary comrades to unite more closely around Chairman Mao and the Party Central Committee, to grasp the revolution and promote production, and to create even greater achievements in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

The rally was filled with a strong sense of revolutionary fervor. The revolutionary masses waved the Little Red Book and shouted slogans in unison, expressing their boundless loyalty to Chairman Mao and their determination to carry the Cultural Revolution through to the end.

The rally concluded with a march through the streets, where the revolutionary masses continued to chant slogans and express their support for the Cultural Revolution. The atmosphere was electric, and the spirit of revolution was palpable. This rally is a powerful demonstration of the unity and determination of the revolutionary masses in the Capital, and it marks a new stage in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.
