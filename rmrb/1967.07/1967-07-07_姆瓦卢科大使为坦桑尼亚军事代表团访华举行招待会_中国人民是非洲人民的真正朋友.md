Title: Tanzanian Ambassador Hosts Reception for Military Delegation Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1967-07-07
Edition: 5

Tanzanian Ambassador Hosts Reception for Military Delegation Visiting China
The Chinese People Are the True Friends of the African People

According to Xinhua News Agency, July 6th: Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Mwaluko, hosted a reception for the Tanzanian military delegation led by Colonel Kashmiri, who is visiting China.

Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Peng Shaohui, and Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department, Tang Jiji, as well as relevant officials, were invited to attend.

All members of the Tanzanian military delegation attended the reception.

Ambassador Mwaluko expressed gratitude for the warm reception given to the Tanzanian military delegation by the Chinese people and military. He praised, "In the midst of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China, we have seen everywhere how the Chinese people are diligently studying and applying the works of your beloved great teacher, great leader, great supreme commander, and great helmsman, Chairman Mao Zedong. Your national economy has undoubtedly developed. We must learn from your people's spirit of self-reliance and striving for progress."

In discussing the international situation, Ambassador Mwaluko pointed out, "In Tanzania, and throughout Africa, the situation is very good. The struggle of the African people against colonial rule is increasingly developing. He also praised the Chinese people as true friends who support the African people's struggle against imperialism and colonialism."

Peng Shaohui said in his speech, "The respect of our Tanzanian friends for our great leader Chairman Mao, their love for the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, their enthusiastic praise for the achievements of our socialist construction and national defense construction, and for our Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, deeply moves us."

He praised the Tanzanian government and people for their resolute opposition to imperialism and old and new colonialism, for building their own country through self-reliance and strengthening their national defense forces, and for actively supporting the anti-imperialist struggle of the oppressed peoples of Africa.

Peng Shaohui pointed out, "In the struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism led by the United States, the friendship between the peoples of China and Tanzania will certainly be further consolidated and developed."

The reception was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere throughout.

### 姆瓦卢科大使为坦桑尼亚军事代表团访华举行招待会　中国人民是非洲人民的真正朋友

1967-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

　　姆瓦卢科大使为坦桑尼亚军事代表团访华举行招待会
　　中国人民是非洲人民的真正朋友
    据新华社六日讯　坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科为由卡什米里中校率领的坦桑尼亚军事代表团访问中国，今晚举行招待会。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉、总后勤部副部长唐天际，以及有关方面负责人应邀出席。
    坦桑尼亚军事代表团全体成员出席了招待会。
    姆瓦卢科大使对中国人民和军队给予坦桑尼亚军事代表团的热情接待，表示感谢。他赞扬说，在中国无产阶级文化大革命运动中，使我们到处亲眼看到中国人民在勤奋地学习和运用你们敬爱的伟大导师、伟大领袖、伟大统帅、伟大舵手毛泽东主席的著作。你们的国民经济肯定地得到了发展。我们要学习你们人民自力更生、奋发图强的精神。
    姆瓦卢科大使在谈到国际形势时指出，在坦桑尼亚，在整个非洲，都是一片大好形势。非洲人民反对殖民主义统治的斗争正在日益发展。他还赞扬中国人民是支持非洲人民反对帝国主义和殖民主义斗争的真正朋友。
    彭绍辉在讲话中说，坦桑尼亚朋友对我们伟大领袖毛主席的尊敬，对战无不胜的毛泽东思想的热爱，对我国社会主义建设和国防建设的成就、对我国无产阶级文化大革命的热情赞扬，使我们深受感动。
    他赞扬坦桑尼亚政府和人民坚决反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义，自力更生地建设自己的国家和加强自己的国防力量，并积极支持非洲被压迫民族的反帝斗争。
    彭绍辉指出，在反对以美国为首的帝国主义、新老殖民主义的斗争中，中国和坦桑尼亚两国人民之间的友谊，必将获得进一步的巩固和发展。
    招待会始终充满着热烈友好的气氛。
