Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Seven Enemy Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1967-07-03
Edition: 6

North Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down seven enemy aircraft.

	New China News Agency Hanoi July 1 - It is reported from here that the military and civilians of Thanh Hoa Province and the military and civilians of Vinh City in Nghe An Province shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft each on June 30.

	It is also reported that the military and civilians of Me Island in Thanh Hoa Province shot down one U.S. aircraft on June 18.

	New China News Agency Hanoi July 2 - It is reported from here that the military and civilians of Hai Duong Province and the military and civilians of Nam Ha Province shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft each this morning.

	The military and civilians of Ninh Binh Province and the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province also shot down one enemy aircraft each in the afternoon of June 30.

### 越南北方军民击落敌机七架

1967-07-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　越南北方军民击落敌机七架
    新华社河内一日电　据这里公布，越南北方清化省军民和义安省荣市军民六月三十日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机各一架。据补充报道，清化省湄岛军民六月十八日击落美国飞机一架。
    新华社河内二日电　据这里公布，越南北方海阳省和南河省军民一日上午击落入侵的美国强盗飞机各一架。
    宁平省和河静省军民在六月三十日下午也击落敌机各一架。
