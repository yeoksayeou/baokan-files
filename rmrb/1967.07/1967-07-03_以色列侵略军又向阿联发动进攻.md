Title: Israeli Aggressors Launch Another Attack on the UAR
Author:
Date: 1967-07-03
Edition: 6

Israeli Aggressors Launch Another Attack on the UAR

	BEIJING, July 2 (Xinhua) -- According to a communiqué released by the Supreme Command of the Armed Forces of the United Arab Republic (UAR) last night, Israeli aggressors launched another attack on the UAR last night.

	The communiqué stated: At 8:30 PM Cairo time, the enemy attempted to advance from Kantara east of the Suez Canal towards Port Fuad. Our forces stationed at Ash Shatt east of the Suez Canal engaged them in battle. According to a communiqué issued by the UAR military on the morning of the 2nd, the battle continued until the early hours of the 2nd. The UAR military destroyed three Israeli tanks and three armored vehicles.

	Meanwhile, the Israeli aggressors brazenly threatened to continue their aggression. Israeli Defense Minister Dayan said in a statement to the press on the 1st that if the Arab countries refused to "achieve peace" on Israel's terms, "war in the Middle East may break out again."

### 以色列侵略军又向阿联发动进攻

1967-07-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　以色列侵略军又向阿联发动进攻
    新华社开罗二日电　据阿联武装部队最高司令部一日晚发表的一项公报宣布，以色列侵略军在一日晚再度进攻阿联。
    公报说：敌军在开罗时间二十时三十分企图从苏伊士运河以东的坎塔拉向福德港方向推进。我们驻扎在苏伊士运河以东的艾什角的军队同他们进行了战斗。据阿联军方二日上午发表的一项公报说，一日战斗一直持续到二日凌晨。阿联军队共击毁三辆以色列坦克和三辆装甲车。
    同时，以色列侵略者猖狂地发出继续进行侵略战争的叫嚣。以色列国防部长达扬一日在对新闻记者发表谈话时，公然叫嚷，如果阿拉伯国家拒绝按照以色列的条件“实现和平”的话，“中东的战争可能再爆发”。
