    Brief News
    Ceylonese People Condemn U.S. Aggression Against Vietnam
    On July 20, hundreds of people in Colombo held a demonstration in support of the Vietnamese people's heroic struggle against U.S. aggression, condemning U.S.-backed Israeli aggression against Arab countries, and denouncing the Soviet revisionists as accomplices of U.S. imperialism. This demonstration was organized by the Ceylon Communist Party, the Ceylon Trade Union Federation, and the Ceylon Youth League. The demonstrators burned effigies of Johnson and Kosygin. A public meeting was held after the demonstration.

    Saudi Arabian Workers Beat Back U.S. Bullies
    Recently, in the port of Tanura on the eastern coast of Saudi Arabia, dock workers refused to load oil onto an American ship. The Americans, revealing their true colors, personally intervened, using guns to force Saudi Arabian workers to work for them. They even shot and injured two workers. The dock workers fought back, beating up the American bullies, injuring four of them, and driving the rest out of the dock.

    Malaysian Students Protest Against U.S.-British Puppets
    On July 22, more than a thousand students at the University of Malaya held a demonstration in the campus, protesting against the U.S.-British imperialist puppets Rahman-Lee Kuan Yew clique's deprivation of the right of progressive Malaysian youth to enter school, and demanding the abolition of the so-called "certificate of eligibility for admission."

    The participating students wore black armbands, held up large banners reading "We oppose the certificate of eligibility for admission," "Down with the Internal Security (Amendment) Act," and chanted slogans. When the demonstrating students reached the "Ministry of Education" of the Rahman-Lee Kuan Yew clique, they posted banners opposing the Rahman-Lee Kuan Yew clique.

    简明新闻
    锡兰人民谴责美帝侵略越南
    七月二十日，科伦坡数百人举行示威，支持越南人民抗击美国侵略的英勇斗争，谴责美国支持的以色列侵略阿拉伯国家，并痛斥苏修充当美帝的帮凶。这次示威是由锡兰共产党、锡兰工会联合会和锡兰青年联盟联合会组织的。示威者焚烧了约翰逊和柯西金的模拟人像。示威后举行了公众集会。
    沙特阿拉伯工人狠揍美国鬼子
    最近，在沙特阿拉伯东海岸的塔努拉角港，码头工人拒绝给开到那里的美国油船装石油。美国人凶相毕露，竟亲自出马，拿枪强迫沙特阿拉伯工人替他们工作。他们甚至开枪打伤了两名工人。码头工人愤起回击，狠揍行凶的美国鬼子，打伤他们四人，并把其余的人都赶出了码头。
    马来亚学生示威反对美英傀儡
    马来亚大学学生一千多人，二十二日在校园内举行了示威游行，抗议美英帝国主义傀儡拉赫曼—李光耀集团剥夺马来亚进步青年入学的权利，要求废除所谓“入学合格准证”。
    参加游行的学生臂上缠着黑纱，高举着“我们反对入学合格准证”、“打倒内部安全（修正）法令”等巨幅标语牌并高呼口号。示威学生到达拉赫曼—李光耀的“教育部”时，张贴了反对拉赫曼—李光耀集团的标语。
                   （据新华社讯）
