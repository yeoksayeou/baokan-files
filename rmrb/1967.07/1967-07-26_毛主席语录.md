Title: Chairman Mao's Quotations
Author:
Date: 1967-07-26
Edition: 1

What is a genuine copper wall and iron wall? It is the masses, the tens of millions of people who genuinely and sincerely support the revolution. This is a genuine copper wall and iron wall, one that no force can break, that can absolutely not be broken. The counter-revolution cannot break us, but we must break the counter-revolution.

		_Concern for the People's Livelihood, Attention to Work Methods_

### 毛主席语录

1967-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    真正的铜墙铁壁是什么？是群众，是千百万真心实意地拥护革命的群众。这是真正的铜墙铁壁，什么力量也打不破的，完全打不破的。反革命打不破我们，我们却要打破反革命。
                              《关心群众生活，注意工作方法》
