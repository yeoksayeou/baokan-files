Title: Guanghua Timber Mill Excels in Completing the First Half of the Year's Plan
Author:
Date: 1967-07-03
Edition: 3

	Guanghua Timber Mill in Beijing has sent news that is heartening to the people: they have completed and exceeded the national plan for the first half of this year ahead of schedule. Among the achievements, the total output value exceeded the national plan by fourteen percent, nearly doubling the growth rate of the same period last year; the production of main products exceeded the national plan by 0.2 percent to 60 percent, and the quality of all products met the requirements. The revolutionary workers of the entire mill are jubilant, declaring this to be a great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, a brilliant crystallization of the great leader Chairman Mao's brilliant thought of "grasp revolution, promote production."

### 光华木材厂出色完成上半年计划

1967-07-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　光华木材厂出色完成上半年计划
    北京光华木材厂传来振奋人心的消息：他们提前超额完成了今年上半年的国家计划。其中，总产值超过国家计划百分之十四，比去年同时期增长将近两成；主要产品产量超过国家计划的百分之零点二到百分之六十，质量全部合乎要求。全厂革命职工欢呼，这是无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利，是伟大领袖毛主席“抓革命，促生产”光辉思想的灿烂结晶。
