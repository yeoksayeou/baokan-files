Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into Our Airspace
Author:
Date: 1967-07-11
Edition: 5

U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace. We issue the 438th serious warning.

New China News Agency, July 9th: On July 9th, from 11:03 to 11:14, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above the sea near the northeastern region of Wenchang County, Hainan Island, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 9:13 to 9:21, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace of Dong Island, Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on instructions, issues the 438th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我领空  我提出第四百三十八次严重警告

1967-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我领空
    我提出第四百三十八次严重警告
    新华社九日讯　七月九日十一时零三分至十一时十四分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省海南岛文昌县东北地区领海上空。同日九时十三分至九时二十一分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛东岛地区领空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百三十八次严重警告。
