Title: Vietnamese Charge d'Affaires Holds Film Screening Event
Author:
Date: 1967-07-19
Edition: 6

The Vietnamese Charge d'Affaires to China, Nguyen Quang Huy, held a film screening event today.

The film shown at the event introduced the heroic deeds and glorious victories of the Vietnamese military and people in their struggle against American aggression for national salvation.

At the beginning of the event, Nguyen Quang Huy, in his speech, introduced the remarkable achievements of the Vietnamese military and people over the past year, inspired by Chairman Ho Chi Minh's call to arms on July 17, 1966.

Representatives from relevant departments of our country and Deputy Head of the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China, Le Thong Son, attended the event.

### 越驻华临时代办举行电影招待会

1967-07-19
第6版()
专栏：

    越驻华临时代办举行电影招待会
    据新华社十八日讯　越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办阮光辉今天举行了电影招待会。
    招待会上放映的影片，介绍了越南军民抗美救国斗争的英雄事迹和他们所取得的辉煌胜利。
    招待会开始时，阮光辉在致词中介绍了越南军民在胡志明主席一九六六年七月十七日号召书的鼓舞下，一年来所建立的显赫战功。
    我国各有关部门的负责人，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长黎松山出席了招待会。
