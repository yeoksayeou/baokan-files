Chairman Mao Receives New Zealand Comrades

New China News Agency, July 2nd: Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the great leader of the Chinese people, received today in the afternoon New Zealand Communist Party Central Committee member Hugh McLeod and Wellington District Committee member Pat Kelly.

Chairman Mao had a very cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Kang Sheng, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Ningyi, member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, and Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the Central Committee. (Photo attached)

Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the great leader of the Chinese people, received on July 2nd New Zealand Communist Party Central Committee member Hugh McLeod (third from the left) and Wellington District Committee member Pat Kelly (third from the right).

### 毛主席接见新西兰同志

1967-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见新西兰同志
　　新华社二日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席、中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东同志，今天下午接见了新西兰共产党全国委员会委员休·麦克劳德同志和新西兰共产党惠灵顿地区委员会委员帕·凯利同志。
　　毛主席同他们进行了极为亲切的谈话。
　　参加接见的，有中共中央政治局常委康生同志，中央书记处书记刘宁一同志，中央候补委员赵毅敏同志。（附图片）
　　中国共产党中央委员会主席、中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东同志，七月二日接见新西兰共产党全国委员会委员休·麦克劳德同志（左三）和新西兰共产党惠灵顿地区委员会委员帕·凯利同志（右三）。　
　　新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
