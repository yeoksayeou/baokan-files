Title: Overthrow the Advocates of "Three Selfs and One Package"
Author: Xue Yu
Date: 1967-07-29
Edition: 2

Mom,

I received your letter and learned that the situation of the Cultural Revolution in our village is very good, and I am very happy about it.

Chairman Mao teaches us: "Only socialism can save China." However, the biggest capitalist-roader in power within the Party has thrown out the poisonous arrow of "Three Selfs and One Package," using every means to sabotage socialism. Mom, do you remember? At that time, the wind of individual farming blew through the countryside. Our family had no labor force and could not farm the land. This was clearly forcing us to be exploited and oppressed again. This was clearly against us, the poor and lower-middle peasants. The poor and lower-middle peasants and the biggest capitalist-roader in power within the Party are irreconcilable. We must resolutely overthrow the advocates of "Three Selfs and One Package."

Mom, we must remember Chairman Mao's teachings: "You should pay attention to state affairs and carry the Cultural Revolution through to the end!" We must completely defeat the handful of capitalist-roaders in power within the Party, making them fall flat on their faces and leaving them with nothing, and throw "Three Selfs and One Package" into the dustbin of history.

Xue Yu

### 打倒“三自一包”鼓吹者
学禹
1967-07-29
第2版()
专栏：革命群众来信

    打倒“三自一包”鼓吹者妈妈：
    收到你的来信，知道咱村无产阶级文化大革命形势很好，我很高兴。
    毛主席教导我们：“只有社会主义能够救中国。”可是，党内最大的走资本主义道路当权派，却抛出“三自一包”这支大毒箭，千方百计拆社会主义的台。妈妈，你还记得吧，当时农村刮起了单干风，咱们家没有劳力，无法种地，这不是明明硬逼咱重受剥削、重受压迫吗？这不是明明跟咱们贫下中农作对吗？贫下中农和党内最大的走资本主义道路当权派是势不两立的。我们要坚决打倒这个“三自一包”的鼓吹者。
    妈妈：我们要牢记毛主席的教导：“你们要关心国家大事，要把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！”把党内最大的一小撮走资本主义道路当权派杀他个人仰马翻、片甲不留，把“三自一包”抛到历史垃圾堆里去。
                                                    学禹
