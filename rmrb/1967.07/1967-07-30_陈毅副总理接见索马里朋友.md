Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Somali Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-07-30
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Somali Art Troupe delegation led by Jama Harar and Deputy Leader Yusuf Ali Harun this evening and had a friendly conversation with them. After the meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet for the entire delegation.

The Somali Art Troupe will depart Beijing by train this evening to visit and perform in other parts of the country.

### 陈毅副总理接见索马里朋友

1967-07-30
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见索马里朋友
    新华社二十九日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今晚接见了索马里艺术代表团团长贾马·哈拉夫、副团长优素福·阿里·哈伦，同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见后，陈毅副总理设宴招待艺术代表团全体成员。
    索马里艺术代表团今晚乘火车离开北京，到外地访问演出。
