Title: Vice Premier Li Qingyi and the Vietnamese Delegation Watch "Sha Jianhu"
Author:
Date: 1967-07-23
Edition: 2

Vice Premier Li Qingyi and the Vietnamese delegation, led by him, watched the revolutionary modern Peking opera "Sha Jianhu" tonight. Vice Premier Li Xiannian and relevant officials Zhang Ziyi, Ding Xilin, Li Qiang, and Yang Lin accompanied them to watch the performance. After the performance ended, Vice Premier Li Qingyi and others went on stage to shake hands with the performers, congratulating them on their successful performance. The audience applauded enthusiastically and sang "The Ship Sails with the Helmsman." Tonight's event was organized by the China International Friendship Promotion Association and the China-Vietnam Friendship Association.

### 黎清毅副总理和越代表团同志观看《沙家浜》  李先念副总理等陪同观看演出

1967-07-23
第2版()
专栏：

    黎清毅副总理和越代表团同志观看《沙家浜》
    李先念副总理等陪同观看演出
    新华社二十二日讯　黎清毅副总理和由他率领的越南政府经济代表团的同志们，今晚观看了革命现代京剧《沙家浜》。李先念副总理和有关方面负责人张奚若、丁西林、李强、杨琳等陪同观看了演出。演出结束后，黎清毅副总理等登上舞台和演员们亲切握手，祝贺他们演出成功。全场热烈鼓掌，高唱《大海航行靠舵手》。今天的晚会是由对外文委和中越友协主办的。
