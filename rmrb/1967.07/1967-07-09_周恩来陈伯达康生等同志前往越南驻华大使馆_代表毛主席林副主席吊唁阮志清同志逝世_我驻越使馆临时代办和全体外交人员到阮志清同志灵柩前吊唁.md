Title: Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, and Others Visit the Vietnamese Embassy in China to Mourn the Passing of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh
Author:
Date: 1967-07-09
Edition: 1

	Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, and other comrades visited the Vietnamese Embassy in China to mourn the passing of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh on behalf of Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin. The acting head of our embassy in Vietnam and all diplomatic personnel paid their respects at Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh's coffin.

	BEIJING, July 8 (Xinhua) — Members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and State Council Premier Zhou Enlai, Political Bureau member and head of the Central Cultural Revolution Group Chen Boda, Political Bureau member and advisor to the Central Cultural Revolution Group, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Kang Sheng, and other comrades went to the Vietnamese Embassy in China this morning to mourn the passing of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, Secretary of the Central Secretariat, member of the National Defense Council of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and General of the Vietnamese People's Army.

	Comrade Zhou Enlai told the acting head of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, Nguyen Quang Huy, "We represent Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Lin, the CPC Central Committee, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, the responsible comrades of the Central Cultural Revolution Group, and other department heads in expressing our condolences to the Vietnamese Embassy for the passing of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh. Please convey our sentiments to Chairman Ho Chi Minh, the Vietnamese Labor Party, the Vietnamese government, and the family of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh."

	Comrade Zhou Enlai said, "The passing of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh is not only a loss to the Vietnamese people and the Vietnamese Labor Party but also a loss to the Chinese people, who are comrades-in-arms and the rear base of the Vietnamese people, and a loss to the revolutionary people of the world. We know that Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh is an outstanding communist in both politics and military affairs, a brave communist warrior. Under the leadership of Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the Vietnamese Labor Party, in the struggle against American imperialism, in the struggle against French imperialism, and in the struggle against modern revisionism, he has made significant contributions."

	Comrade Zhou Enlai said, "We must carry forward Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh's spirit of thorough revolution and fight to the end for the cause of human communism."

	Accompanying Comrades Zhou Enlai and others to the Vietnamese Embassy in China to pay their respects were Political Bureau member and Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, Political Bureau member and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Ye Jianying, Secretary of the Central Secretariat and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Ningyi, Central Committee member and Deputy Minister of Defense Wang Shusheng, Central Committee member and Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Xiao Hua, Alternate Central Committee member and Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Yang Chengwu, Central Committee member Wei Guoqing, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, among others.

	HA NOI, July 8 (Xinhua) — The acting head of the Chinese Embassy in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Lu Weizhao, and all diplomatic personnel from the embassy, the Chinese Economic Representative Office in Vietnam, and other units, visited the Military Club on the afternoon of July 7 to pay their respects to the coffin of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh with deep sorrow and participated in his funeral.

	The Chinese Embassy, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and the Ministry of National Defense, presented a wreath to the coffin of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh. The Chinese Embassy, the Chinese Economic Representative Office in Vietnam, and the Military Attaché's Office of the Chinese Embassy also presented wreaths. (Photo attached)

	Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, and other leaders paid their respects to the passing of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh at the Vietnamese Embassy in China.

### 周恩来陈伯达康生等同志前往越南驻华大使馆　代表毛主席林副主席吊唁阮志清同志逝世　我驻越使馆临时代办和全体外交人员到阮志清同志灵柩前吊唁

1967-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来陈伯达康生等同志前往越南驻华大使馆
　　代表毛主席林副主席吊唁阮志清同志逝世
　　我驻越使馆临时代办和全体外交人员到阮志清同志灵柩前吊唁
    新华社八日讯　中共中央政治局常委、国务院总理周恩来，中共中央政治局常委、中央文革小组组长陈伯达，中共中央政治局常委、中央文革小组顾问、人大常委会副委员长康生等同志，今天上午前往越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆，吊唁越南劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记、越南民主共和国国防委员会委员、越南人民军大将阮志清同志的逝世。
    周恩来同志对越南驻中国大使馆临时代办阮光辉说，我们代表毛主席、林彪副主席，代表中共中央、国务院、中央军委、中央文革小组负责同志和其他部门负责同志，对阮志清同志的逝世，向越南驻中国大使馆的同志表示哀悼，并请大使馆同志把我们的心意转给胡志明主席、越南劳动党、越南政府和阮志清同志的家属。
    周恩来同志说，阮志清同志的逝世，不仅是越南人民、越南劳动党的损失，也是作为越南人民的战友和越南大后方的中国人民的损失，也是全世界革命人民的损失。我们知道，阮志清同志在政治上、军事上都是杰出的共产党员，是英勇的共产主义战士，他在胡志明主席和越南劳动党的领导下，在抗美救国斗争中，在反对法帝国主义的斗争中，在反对现代修正主义的斗争中，都作出了重大贡献。
    周恩来同志说，我们要发扬阮志清同志彻底革命的精神，为人类共产主义事业奋斗到底。
    同周恩来等同志一起前往越南驻中国大使馆吊唁的，还有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，中共中央政治局委员、国防委员会副主席叶剑英，中共中央书记处书记、人大常委会副委员长刘宁一，中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声，中共中央委员、中国人民解放军总政治部主任萧华，中共中央候补委员、中国人民解放军代理总参谋长杨成武，中共中央委员韦国清，外交部副部长韩念龙等同志。
    新华社河内八日电 中国驻越南民主共和国大使馆临时代办陆维钊和大使馆、中国驻越南经济代表处等全体外交人员，七日下午怀着无比悲痛的心情前往停放阮志清同志灵柩的军人俱乐部吊唁，并参加了阮志清同志的葬礼。
    中国大使馆代表中国共产党中央委员会、中华人民共和国国务院和国防部，向阮志清同志的灵柩献了花圈。中国大使馆、中国驻越南经济代表处、中国大使馆武官处等单位也献了花圈。（附图片）
