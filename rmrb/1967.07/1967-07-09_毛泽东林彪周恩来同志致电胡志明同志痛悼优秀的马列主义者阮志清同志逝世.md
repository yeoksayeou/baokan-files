Title: Mao Zedong, Lin Biao, and Zhou Enlai Send Telegram to Commemorate the Passing of the Outstanding Marxist-Leninist Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh
Author:
Date: 1967-07-09
Edition: 1

	Mao Zedong, Lin Biao, and Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Comrade Ho Chi Minh to mourn the passing of the outstanding Marxist-Leninist Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh.
	The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Central Military Commission sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the Central Military Commission to express condolences.
	Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh is an outstanding communist warrior of the Vietnam Workers' Party, a good son of the Vietnamese people and nation, and a remarkable hero nurtured under the leadership of Comrade Ho Chi Minh. He made outstanding contributions in the Vietnamese people's war of resistance against France and the current struggle against American aggression for national salvation. His brilliant example and influence will never fade away and will continue to be carried forward.

	Xinhua News Agency, July 8th: Today, Comrade Mao Zedong, Comrade Lin Biao, and Comrade Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Comrade Ho Chi Minh to mourn the passing of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh.

	The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To Chairman Ho Chi Minh:

	We were shocked to hear of the sudden passing of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh due to illness, and we are deeply grieved. Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh is a good comrade and good comrade-in-arms to you and to us, as well as to the revolutionary people and oppressed nations of the world. He made outstanding contributions to the revolutionary cause of Vietnam, and to the unity and joint struggle of the two countries, two parties, and two armies of China and Vietnam. His passing is a great loss to our common revolutionary cause. However, his brilliant example and influence will never fade away and will continue to be carried forward. We hope you will take care of your health and overcome your grief.

	Mao Zedong
	Lin Biao
	Zhou Enlai

	July 8, 1967

	Xinhua News Agency, July 8th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Central Military Commission sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the Central Military Commission to mourn the passing of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh.

	The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the Central Military Commission:

	We were shocked to hear of the sudden passing of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh on July 6 due to illness, and we are deeply grieved. Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh is an outstanding Marxist-Leninist, a distinguished communist warrior of the Vietnam Workers' Party, a good son of the Vietnamese people and nation, and a remarkable hero nurtured under the leadership of Comrade Ho Chi Minh. He made outstanding contributions in the Vietnamese people's war of resistance against France and the current struggle against American aggression for national salvation. His passing is not only a great loss to the Vietnamese people, party, and army, but also a loss to the Chinese and revolutionary people of the world.

	Please convey our deepest condolences to the family of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh.

	Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
	Central Military Commission

	July 8, 1967

### 毛泽东林彪周恩来同志致电胡志明同志痛悼优秀的马列主义者阮志清同志逝世

1967-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛泽东林彪周恩来同志致电胡志明同志痛悼优秀的马列主义者阮志清同志逝世
　　中共中央和中央军委致电越南劳动党中央和中央军委表示哀悼
    阮志清同志是越南劳动党杰出的共产主义战士，是越南人民和越南民族的好儿子，是在胡志明同志领导下的越南劳动党培养起来的一位了不起的英雄人物。他在越南人民的抗法战争和当前的抗美救国事业中作出了卓越的贡献。他的光辉的榜样和影响是永远不会消逝的，是会继续发扬光大的。
    新华社八日讯　毛泽东同志、林彪同志、周恩来同志今天打电报给胡志明同志，悼念阮志清同志逝世。
    唁电全文如下：胡志明主席同志：
    惊悉阮志清同志突然因病逝世，至为哀痛。阮志清同志是您的、也是我们的好同志、好战友，也是世界革命人民和被压迫民族的好战友。他为越南的革命事业，为中越两国、两党、两军的团结战斗友谊，作出了卓越的贡献。他的逝世，是我们共同的革命事业的巨大损失。但是，他的光辉的榜样和影响是永远不会消逝的，是会继续发扬光大的。务望节哀，保重您的身体健康。
  　毛泽东　林彪　周恩来
                                  一九六七年七月八日
    新华社八日讯　中国共产党中央委员会、中央军事委员会今天打电报给越南劳动党中央委员会、中央军事委员会，悼念阮志清同志逝世。
    唁电全文如下：越南劳动党中央委员会，中央军事委员会：
    惊悉阮志清同志七月六日突然因病逝世，至为痛悼。阮志清同志是一个优秀的马克思列宁主义者，是越南劳动党杰出的共产主义战士，是越南人民和越南民族的好儿子，是在胡志明同志领导下的越南劳动党培养起来的一位了不起的英雄人物。他在越南人民的抗法战争和当前的抗美救国事业中作出了卓越的贡献。他的逝世不但是越南人民、党和军队的巨大损失，也是中国和全世界革命人民的损失。
    请代向阮志清同志家属表示我们的深切慰问。
                                中国共产党中央委员会
                                中央军事委员会
                                    一九六七年七月八日
