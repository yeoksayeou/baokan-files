Title: A March by Patriotic Youth Students in Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1967-07-26
Edition: 4

A procession composed of patriotic youth students marched through the North Point district of Hong Kong, holding high portraits of Chairman Mao, quotations from Chairman Mao, and large banners. They strongly protested against the savage crimes committed by the British authorities in Hong Kong, who were brutally persecuting Hong Kong's patriotic compatriots. (Xinhua News Agency)
