Title: Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Receive Delegates of the Military Training Conference
Author:
Date: 1967-07-08
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Receive Delegates of the Military Training Conference
Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, and others participate in the reception

Chairman Mao, full of vigor, walked briskly into the reception hall and warmly waved to the crowd. The delegates shouted for a long time: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Chairman Mao lives forever!" We wish our dearest red sun, Chairman Mao, eternal life!

New China News Agency, July 7th: Our most beloved and great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao, along with other central leaders and responsible comrades from various relevant departments Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Jiang Qing, Xiao Hua, Yang Chengwu, Su Yu, Zhang Chunqiao, Guan Feng, Chen Boda, Li Tianyou, Wu Faxian, Ye Qun, Wang Dongxing, today in the afternoon received the delegates attending the Chinese People's Liberation Army Military Training Conference.

Chairman Mao, full of vigor, walked briskly into the reception hall. At this most joyful moment, the delegates were extremely excited, waving their red "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and shouting for a long time: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Chairman Mao lives forever!"

Chairman Mao, facing the joyful smiles of the delegates, warmly waved to everyone and posed for a group photo with the delegates.

When Chairman Mao left the reception hall, the delegates shouted: "Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!" and sang "The East is Red," wishing our dearest red sun, Chairman Mao, eternal life! Eternal life!

1967-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席林副主席接见军训会议代表
　　周恩来陈伯达康生等同志参加接见
    毛主席神采奕奕，健步走进接见大厅，向大家亲切招手。代表们长时间高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”祝我们心中最红最红的红太阳毛主席万寿无疆！
　　新华社七日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，中央其他领导同志和各有关方面的负责同志周恩来、陈伯达、康生、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英、江青、萧华、杨成武、粟裕、张春桥、关锋、戚本禹、李天佑、吴法宪、叶群、汪东兴，今天下午接见了出席中国人民解放军军事训练会议的代表。
　　毛主席神采奕奕，健步走进接见大厅。在这最最幸福的时刻，代表们激动万分，挥动着红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，长时间地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”
　　毛主席迎着代表们一张张幸福的笑脸，向大家亲切招手，并和代表们一起合影。
　　毛主席离开接见大厅的时候，代表们高呼：“战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！”并且齐唱《大海航行靠舵手》，祝我们心中最红最红的红太阳毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！（附图片）
　　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，以及周恩来等同志，接见出席中国人民解放军军事训练会议的代表。
　　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，以及周恩来、陈伯达、康生等同志，和出席中国人民解放军军事训练会议的代表合影。
                                          本报记者　吕相友摄
