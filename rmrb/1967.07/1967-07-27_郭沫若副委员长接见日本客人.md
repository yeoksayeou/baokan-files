Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-07-27
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Guo Moruo today received and dined with Japanese historian Ando Hachitaro and his wife. The host and guests engaged in cordial and friendly conversation.

During the reception and dinner, Japanese friends Nishisonji Koichi and Asakawa Kenji were also present.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1967-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社二十六日讯　人大常委会副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若，今天接见并宴请了日本历史学家安藤彦太郎和夫人，宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见和宴请时，日本朋友西园寺公一、浅川谦次也在座。
