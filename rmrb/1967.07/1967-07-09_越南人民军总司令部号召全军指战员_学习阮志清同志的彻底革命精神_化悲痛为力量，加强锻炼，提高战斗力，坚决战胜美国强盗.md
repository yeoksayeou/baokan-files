Title: The General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army Calls on All Soldiers and Officers to Learn from Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh's Spirit of Thorough Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-07-09
Edition: 1

The General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army calls on all soldiers and officers to learn from Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh's spirit of thorough revolution, to turn grief into strength, to strengthen training, to enhance combat effectiveness, and to decisively defeat the American aggressors.

		New China News Agency, Hanoi, July 8 — According to a report from Vietnam's "People's Daily," on July 7, the General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army issued a directive to the entire army, calling on all soldiers and officers of the Vietnam People's Armed Forces to learn from Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh's spirit of thorough revolution. The full text of the directive is as follows:

		The passing of Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party and a general, is a tremendous loss to our party, our army, and our people.

		The Party Central Committee and the government have decided to hold a funeral ceremony in accordance with military rites for the outstanding leader who made great contributions to the revolutionary cause of our party and people and to the cause of building our people's armed forces — General Nguyen Chi Thanh.

		The funeral was solemnly held in Hanoi on July 7, 1967.

		The General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army directs all units of the Vietnam People's Armed Forces to hold memorial services to mourn General Nguyen Chi Thanh of the Vietnam People's Army and to hold discussions on his biography and revolutionary achievements.

		Our army should strive to learn from the lofty revolutionary ethics of General Nguyen Chi Thanh of the Vietnam People's Army, learn from his spirit of thorough revolution of unceasingly attacking the enemy, and his modest and humble style. All cadres and soldiers should turn grief into strength, strengthen training, enhance the combat effectiveness of the troops, and resolutely defeat the American aggressors on all battlefields, liberate the South, defend the North, and ultimately unite the country.

### 越南人民军总司令部号召全军指战员　学习阮志清同志的彻底革命精神　化悲痛为力量，加强锻炼，提高战斗力，坚决战胜美国强盗

1967-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南人民军总司令部号召全军指战员
　　学习阮志清同志的彻底革命精神
　　化悲痛为力量，加强锻炼，提高战斗力，坚决战胜美国强盗
    新华社河内八日电  据越南《人民报》报道，越南人民军总司令部七日向全军发出指示，号召越南人民军全体指战员学习阮志清同志彻底革命的精神。指示全文如下：
    越南劳动党中央政治局委员、大将阮志清同志的逝世，是我党、我军和我国人民的一个巨大的损失。
    党中央和政府决定按照军队的仪式，给在党和人民的革命事业中，在建设我国人民武装力量事业中立下巨大功勋的出色的领导者——大将阮志清同志举行葬礼。
    葬礼已经于一九六七年七月七日在河内隆重举行。
    越南人民军总司令部指示越南人民武装力量各单位举行追悼会，悼念越南人民军大将阮志清同志并举行关于阮志清同志的简历和革命功勋的座谈会。
    我军应努力学习越南人民军大将阮志清同志崇高的革命道德，学习他的不断向敌人进攻的彻底革命精神和朴素谦逊的作风。全体干部和战士应化悲痛为力量，加强锻炼，提高部队的战斗力，坚决在各个战场上战胜美国侵略强盗，解放南方，保卫北方，进而统一祖国。
