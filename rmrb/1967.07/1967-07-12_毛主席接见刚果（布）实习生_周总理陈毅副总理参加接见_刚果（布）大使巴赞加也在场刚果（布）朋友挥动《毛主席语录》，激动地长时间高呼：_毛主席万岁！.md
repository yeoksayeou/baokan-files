Title: Chairman Mao Receives Congolese (Brazzaville) Trainees
Author:
Date: 1967-07-12
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao, our most beloved and respected great leader, the red sun in the hearts of the world's revolutionary people, received the Congolese (Brazzaville) trainees who have completed their training in our country and are about to return home this afternoon.

The moment the Congolese friends had been eagerly awaiting finally arrived! In the afternoon, at around 5 o'clock, Chairman Mao came to the reception hall and warmly greeted the Congolese friends. At this moment, the hall was filled with joy. The Congolese friends waved their red "Quotations from Chairman Mao," excitedly shouting "Long live Chairman Mao!" for a long time.

Chairman Mao, facing the Congolese friends' happy smiles, warmly applauded and greeted them, and took a group photo with them. After the photo, Chairman Mao shook hands with each of the Congolese friends. The Congolese friends gathered around Chairman Mao, singing "The East Is Red" in unison.

Those who received the Congolese friends with Chairman Mao included Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi.

The Congolese trainees who were received are: Eugine Basga Daydou, Busugu Joseph, Mbamba Bernard, Kungu Simon, Myambra Roger, Masamba Jean-Baptiste, Ozipi Adolf, Bayinga Albert, Maponso Louis, Bagna George, Piemi Clement, Nagoy Albert, Maguaya Bernard, Blou Medard, Eranga Andre, Nagoma Ki Charles, Ibala Lambert, Gombamba Roma, Madami Antoinette Kuka, Kumba Simone, Ikisiba David, Masamba Albert, and Bawanda Jean.

The Congolese Ambassador to China, Bazinga, was also present at the reception.

第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见刚果（布）实习生
    周总理陈毅副总理参加接见　刚果（布）大使巴赞加也在场刚果（布）朋友挥动《毛主席语录》，激动地长时间高呼：“毛主席万岁！”
    新华社十一日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖、世界革命人民心中的红太阳毛主席，今天下午接见了在我国实习期满即将回国的刚果（布）实习生。
    刚果（布）朋友日夜盼望的幸福时刻终于来到了！下午五时多，毛主席来到接见大厅，同刚果（布）朋友亲切见面。这时，大厅里一片欢腾。刚果（布）朋友们挥动着红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，激动地长时间地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”
    毛主席迎着刚果（布）朋友一张张幸福的笑脸，亲切地向他们鼓掌致意，并同他们一起合影。合影以后，毛主席和刚果（布）朋友们一一握手。刚果（布）朋友们簇拥在毛主席周围，齐声高唱《大海航行靠舵手》。
    同毛主席一起接见刚果（布）朋友的，有国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈毅等同志。
    被接见的刚果（布）实习生是：欧金巴·巴斯加·戴道尔，布苏古·约瑟夫，姆邦巴·贝纳尔，昆古·西蒙，米昂布拉·罗歇，马桑巴·让·巴底斯脱，奥西皮·阿道尔夫，巴依尼加·阿勒贝尔，马蓬索·路易，巴格那·乔治，皮埃米·克累芒，纳高由·阿勒贝尔，马古阿亚·贝纳尔，布卢·美达，埃朗加·安德烈，纳高马·基·查理，依巴拉·朗贝尔，果邦巴·罗马，马达米·安燕妮·库卡夫人，昆巴·西蒙娜，伊基西巴·达维，马桑巴·阿勒贝尔，巴翁达·让。
    接见时，刚果（布）驻中国大使巴赞加也在场。
