Title: African Freedom Fighters in Battle
Author:
Date: 1967-07-13
Edition: 6

Angolan patriotic armed forces annihilated more than 340 enemy troops. The Angolan people's armed forces achieved brilliant results in April this year, killing 343 Portuguese colonial troops, including eight officers. In addition, they wounded another 100 Portuguese soldiers. The people's armed forces in the southeastern part of Angola in the Luena region shot down a Portuguese bomber and destroyed four Portuguese military vehicles and six ships.

Portuguese Guinea fighters attack Portuguese military camp

The patriotic armed forces of "Portuguese" Guinea from May 6 to 25 this year killed 67 Portuguese colonial troops, destroying 70% of the Portuguese military facilities in Bissau, Cacheu, and Santo Domingo, as well as the river port of Binta in the north. On May 25, the patriotic armed forces attacked the Portuguese military camp in Quelfar, destroying most of the military facilities there.

Mozambican guerrillas kill and wound 50 Portuguese troops

Guerrilla fighters led by the Mozambique Revolutionary Committee recently engaged in a fierce battle in the northwestern Muza valley region of Mozambique, killing 33 Portuguese colonial troops and wounding 17. In this battle, which lasted more than two hours, three enemy heavy military vehicles transporting reinforcements were destroyed by landmines. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 非洲自由战士在战斗

1967-07-13
第6版()
专栏：

    非洲自由战士在战斗
    安哥拉爱国武装歼敌三百四十多
    安哥拉人民武装在今年四月间取得辉煌战果，打死了葡萄牙殖民军三百四十三人，其中包括八名军官。此外，还打伤了另外一百名葡萄牙士兵。人民武装在安哥拉东南部的卢埃纳地区击落了葡萄牙的一架轰炸机，还击毁了四辆葡萄牙军车和六艘船。
    葡属几内亚战士袭击葡军兵营
    “葡属”几内亚爱国武装力量在今年五月六日到二十五日，共打死了葡萄牙殖民军六十七人，摧毁比热内、纪热和圣多明戈的葡萄牙军营的百分之七十的军事设施以及北部的宾塔的河港。五月二十五日，爱国武装力量袭击了库法里埃卡奇尔的葡萄牙军营，摧毁了那里的大部分军事设施。
    莫三鼻给游击队毙伤葡军五十名
    由莫三鼻给革命委员会领导的游击战士不久前在莫三鼻给西北部穆泽河谷地区进行的一场激战中，打死三十三名葡萄牙殖民军，打伤十七人。在这场历时两个多小时的战斗中，敌人的三辆运送增援部队的重型军车被地雷炸毁了。　（据新华社讯）
