Title: With the Unlimited Love and Admiration of the Albanian People and Youth for Chairman Mao, the Chinese Red Guards Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-07-13
Edition: 1

With the unlimited love and admiration of the Albanian people and youth for Chairman Mao, the Chinese Red Guards delegation returned to Beijing. Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Liu Ningyi, Jiang Qing, and Ambassador Natanaeli welcomed them at the airport.

	New China News Agency, July 12th: Led by Comrade Yao Wenyuan, the Chinese Red Guards delegation, invited to participate in the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Labor Youth League and to conduct a friendly visit to Albania, returned to Beijing today, carrying with them the unlimited love and admiration of the Albanian people and youth for the red sun in the hearts of the world's revolutionary people, Chairman Mao, and the deep revolutionary friendship of the Albanian people and youth.

	Central leaders and responsible comrades from various relevant departments, including Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Liu Ningyi, Jiang Qing, Xiao Hua, Yang Chengwu, Zhang Chunqiao, Guan Feng, Chen Biyu, Zhao Yimin, Ye Qing, and Wang Dongxing, welcomed them at the airport.

	Today, the airport was adorned with red flags and filled with joy. Over 2,000 Red Guards from the capital held high portraits of Chairman Mao and Comrade Hoxha, forming neat rows to welcome the victorious return of the Chinese Red Guards delegation, celebrating the new developments in the fighting friendship and revolutionary unity between the peoples and youth of China and Albania.

	At 6 PM, Comrade Yao Wenyuan and delegation members Tan Houlan, Liu Xichang, Song Yimin, Chen Ganfeng, Cheng Jinhua, and Gao Jinghui, waving red copies of the revolutionary treasure "Quotations from Chairman Mao," shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!" as they stepped off the plane. At this moment, Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and others warmly shook hands and embraced Comrade Yao Wenyuan and others, welcoming their victorious return.

	Albanian Ambassador to China Natanaeli, who came to welcome them, shook hands with Comrade Yao Wenyuan and said enthusiastically, "Thank you for your friendly visit to our country. Your visit has greatly inspired the Albanian people and youth." Comrade Yao Wenyuan thanked the Albanian people and youth for their warm welcome and hospitality. He said, "This visit has strengthened the fighting friendship between the peoples and youth of China and Albania. We have learned a lot in heroic Albania and will definitely pass on these valuable experiences to the Chinese people and youth."

	Subsequently, the Red Guards delegation members, along with Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and others, as well as Ambassador Natanaeli, walked around the field to greet the Red Guards who came to welcome them. At this moment, the airport was filled with a joyous atmosphere. The Red Guards, filled with boundless excitement, beat drums, waved red copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," and continuously shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao! Long live Comrade Hoxha! Long live Sino-Albanian friendship! Sino-Albanian friendship forever! Long live the unity of the youth of China and Albania! Carry the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat to the end! Long live the victory of the revolutionary line of the proletariat represented by Chairman Mao!"

	Today, those who came to the airport to welcome the delegation included responsible members and representatives from the Beijing Revolutionary Committee, the Capital Workers' Congress, the Capital Peasants' Congress, the Red Guards Congress of Higher Education Institutions, and the Red Guards Congress of Middle Schools. Among them were Wu De, Zheng Weishan, Fu Chongbi, Zhou Jingfang, Nie Yuanzi, Ding Guanyi, Wang Laomin, Lu Wenge, Deng Wantian, Kuai Dafu, Han Aijing, Wang Dabin, and Li Dongmin.

	When the Red Guards delegation returned to Shanghai on the 11th, over 1,000 responsible members of the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee, responsible personnel and soldiers of the PLA Shanghai Garrison, responsible persons of various revolutionary mass organizations in Shanghai, and Red Guards enthusiastically welcomed them at the airport. (Accompanying photos)

	The Chinese Red Guards delegation and Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Liu Ningyi, Jiang Qing, and others who came to the airport to welcome them took a group photo at the airport.

		By reporters Wang Dong and Lü Xiangyou

### 带着阿尔巴尼亚人民和青年对毛主席的无限热爱和敬仰  中国红卫兵代表团回到北京  周恩来陈伯达康生刘宁一江青等同志和纳塔奈利大使到机场迎接

1967-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    带着阿尔巴尼亚人民和青年对毛主席的无限热爱和敬仰
    中国红卫兵代表团回到北京
    周恩来陈伯达康生刘宁一江青等同志和纳塔奈利大使到机场迎接
    新华社十二日讯　由姚文元同志率领的中国红卫兵代表团，应邀参加了阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟第五次代表大会和在阿尔巴尼亚进行友好访问以后，带着阿尔巴尼亚人民和青年对世界革命人民心中的红太阳毛主席的无限热爱和敬仰，带着阿尔巴尼亚人民和青年的深厚的革命友情，今天下午乘专机回到北京。
    中央领导同志和各有关方面的负责同志周恩来、陈伯达、康生、刘宁一、江青、萧华、杨成武、张春桥、关锋、戚本禹、赵毅敏、叶群、汪东兴等，到机场迎接。
    今天，飞机场上红旗招展、喜气洋洋，两千多名首都红卫兵高举毛主席像和霍查同志像，排成整齐的行列迎接中国红卫兵代表团胜利归来，热烈祝贺中阿两国人民和青年的战斗友谊和革命团结获得新发展。
    下午六点钟，姚文元同志和代表团成员谭厚兰、刘锡昌、宋义民、陈敢峰、程金香、高静慧等同志，挥动红彤彤的革命宝书《毛主席语录》，高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”走下飞机。这时，周恩来、陈伯达、康生、江青等领导同志，同姚文元等同志亲切握手，热烈拥抱，欢迎他们胜利归来。
    前来欢迎的阿尔巴尼亚驻我国大使纳塔奈利握着姚文元同志的手热情地说，感谢你们对我们国家的友好访问，你们的访问对阿尔巴尼亚人民和青年是个很大的鼓舞。姚文元同志感谢阿尔巴尼亚人民和青年对中国红卫兵代表团的热烈欢迎和亲切接待。他说，我们这次访问加强了中阿两国人民和青年的战斗友谊，我们在英雄的阿尔巴尼亚学习了很多东西，我们一定要把这些宝贵经验转告中国人民和青年。
    接着，红卫兵代表团同志们同周恩来、陈伯达、康生、江青等同志以及纳塔奈利大使一起，绕场一周，向前来欢迎的红卫兵战友们致意。这时，机场上呈现一片欢腾景象。红卫兵小将们怀着无比兴奋的心情，敲锣打鼓，挥动着红光闪闪的《毛主席语录》，不断地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“霍查同志万岁！”“中阿友谊万岁！”“中阿友谊万古长青！”“中阿青年团结万岁！”“把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！”“以毛主席为代表的无产阶级革命路线胜利万岁！”
    今天，到机场欢迎的还有北京市革命委员会、首都工代会、农代会、大专院校红代会、中学红代会的负责成员和代表。他们当中有吴德、郑维山、傅崇碧、周景方、聂元梓、丁国钰、王老民、鲁文阁、邓万田、蒯大富、韩爱晶、王大宾、李冬民等。
    红卫兵代表团在十一日回到上海的时候，上海市革命委员会负责成员、人民解放军驻上海部队负责人和指战员、上海各革命群众组织的负责人以及红卫兵小将共一千多人，到机场热烈欢迎他们。（附图片）
    中国红卫兵代表团和前来机场欢迎的周恩来、陈伯达、康生、刘宁一、江青等同志在机场合影。
