Title: Vietnamese People on the Frontline of Anti-American Struggle Love Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-07-25
Edition: 5

Standing at the forefront of the anti-American struggle, the Vietnamese people love Chairman Mao. The image shows a vendor in Hanoi writing in both Chinese and Vietnamese: "Long live Chairman Mao."

Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
