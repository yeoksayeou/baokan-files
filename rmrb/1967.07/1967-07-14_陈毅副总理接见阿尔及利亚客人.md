Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Algerian Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-07-14
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi today received Algerian Minister of Veterans Affairs Ben Allem Ben Hamouda and the members of the Algerian delegation he led.

Accompanying the reception were Minister of Public Security Zeng Shan.

Algerian Ambassador to China Sahli was also present at the reception.

The Algerian delegation arrived in Beijing on July 10 for a friendly visit.

### 陈毅副总理接见阿尔及利亚客人

1967-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见阿尔及利亚客人
    新华社十二日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见阿尔及利亚复员军人部部长本阿勒姆·本·哈穆达和他率领的阿尔及利亚代表团成员。
    陪同接见的有内务部部长曾山。
    接见时，阿尔及利亚驻华大使萨赫利也在座。
    阿尔及利亚代表团是在本月十日到达北京进行友好访问的。
