Title: Celebrating the Sixth Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-North Korean Friendship Treaty
Author:
Date: 1967-07-12
Edition: 5

China's Chargé d'Affaires in North Korea Holds a Reception
North Korean Ambassador in China Holds a Banquet

	New China News Agency, Pyongyang, July 11 -- The Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, Wang Peng, held a cold buffet and movie reception on the evening of July 10 to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-North Korean Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance Treaty.

	North Korean Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Pak Song-chol, Vice Premier Chong Chun-jae, Director of the Foreign Economic Bureau Kim Kyong-ryol, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ho Tan, and Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, Lieutenant General Yun Tae-hong, attended the reception.

	Chargé d'Affaires Wang Peng and Vice Premier Pak Song-chol both spoke at the reception.

	Two films, "Chairman Mao is the Red Sun in Our Hearts" and "The Great Victory of Mao Zedong Thought," were shown at the reception.

	New China News Agency, July 11 -- The Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Hyon Jun-gye, held a banquet today to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-North Korean Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance Treaty.

	Vice Premier Chen Yi and relevant officials from the Chinese government, as well as leaders from the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including Han Nianlong, Peng Shaohui, Chu Tu-nan, Yang Lin, and Zhou Huamin, were invited to attend the banquet.

	Ambassador Hyon Jun-gye and Vice Premier Chen Yi both spoke at the banquet.

### 庆祝中朝友好条约签订六周年  我驻朝临时代办举行招待会  朝鲜驻我国大使举行宴会

1967-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中朝友好条约签订六周年
    我驻朝临时代办举行招待会
    朝鲜驻我国大使举行宴会
    新华社平壤十一日电  中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办王彭，十日晚举行冷餐电影招待会，庆祝中朝友好合作互助条约签订六周年。
    朝鲜内阁副首相兼外务相朴成哲、内阁副首相郑准泽、内阁对外经济总局局长金炅炼、外务省副相许锬、朝鲜人民军副总参谋长尹泰洪中将等出席了招待会。
    王彭临时代办和朴成哲副首相先后在招待会上讲了话。
    招待会上，放映了《毛主席是我们心中的红太阳》、《毛泽东思想的伟大胜利》两部影片。
    新华社十一日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使玄峻极今天举行宴会，庆祝中朝友好合作互助条约签订六周年。
    国务院副总理陈毅和政府有关部门、中国人民解放军负责人韩念龙、彭绍辉、楚图南、杨琳、周化民等应邀出席了宴会。
    玄峻极大使和陈毅副总理先后在宴会上讲了话。
