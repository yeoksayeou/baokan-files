Title: Long Live Chairman Mao Forever!
Author: Ní Liánghuá
Date: 1967-07-01
Edition: 8

The nation is in turmoil, the world is singing.

Look: The red flags flutter within and beyond the Great Wall, and the great waves surge above and below the rivers.

Listen: The mountains and seas sing with us, and the voices resonate across the five continents and four seas.

Sing: Our great leader, Chairman Mao, the red sun rising in the East.

Shining with golden rays, radiant with boundless light!

Who in the world is as happy as we are!

Who has the glory we have!

With all the ink and paper in the world,

We cannot express our ardent longing for our great leader.

With all the paper in the world,

We cannot describe our boundless admiration for our great commander, Chairman Mao.

From Shaoshan to Beijing, our great teacher,

Through countless hardships, striving for China's liberation.

It is he who founded our great party,

It is he who established our revolutionary army.

He led us to overthrow the three great mountains, making socialist China stand forever in the East!

From Jinggangshan to Zhongnanhai, our great leader

Wrote volumes of brilliant articles, paved the way for the Chinese revolution, guided the revolutionary people to complete liberation, destroyed imperialist colonizers, smashed revisionism and all reactionaries, and buried the old world completely!

Chairman Mao is our great helmsman, holding the helm of revolution.

Navigating past countless reefs and shoals, breaking through layer after layer of mist and waves.

Steering the giant wheel of human history, galloping on the road to communism.

Developing Marxism-Leninism to a new stage, making the future of humanity even more glorious!

Chairman Mao is our great commander,

Leading the cultural revolution of the proletariat, fiercely attacking the capitalist roaders in power, smashing the evil nest of revisionism, shattering the illusions of the bourgeoisie, and completely burying the old culture, old thoughts, old customs, and old habits.

Establishing the new culture, new thoughts, new customs, and new habits of the proletariat,

Let them flourish and radiate brilliant light.

Mao Zedong Thought

Is the truth of revolution,

Is the unsetting sun.

It is our lifeblood,

It is our spiritual sustenance.

Its sunshine and rain nourish our growth,

Its cultivation and education forge us into steel.

Without it,

We cannot take a single step.

Lacking it,

The country and the party will perish.

With it,

Our path becomes broader!

Relying on it,

Our cause prospers!

Mao Zedong Thought is the guiding principle for all our work. We must strive to learn it,

Etch it in our hearts!

We must resolutely carry it out,

Use it to guide and navigate!

We must enthusiastically propagate it,

Make it known to every household!

We must bravely defend it,

Make it shine forever!

Head may be cut off, blood may flow,

But we will never forget Chairman Mao's teachings!

The sea may dry up, the rocks may crumble,

But we will forever be loyal to Mao Zedong Thought!

Sing: All rivers flow into the sea, sunflowers turn towards the sun, we turn towards Chairman Mao, we are loyal to Mao Zedong Thought!

Even with countless difficulties,

Following Chairman Mao, we dare to venture!

No matter the mountains of knives and seas of fire,

Following Chairman Mao, we dare to climb!

Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao,

We will forever follow you!

Paths where no one has walked before,

We dare to venture!

Peaks where no one has climbed before,

We dare to climb!

Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao,

We will forever and ever follow you,

Holding our heads high, riding the winds and breaking the waves,

Forever and ever galloping on the road to communism!

Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao,

Thinking of you stirs a storm in our hearts,

Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao,

Thinking of you sets our chests ablaze with waves,

Thousands of words surge in our hearts.

All roads lead to Beijing,

All rivers flow into the ocean.

Thousands of words boil down to one sentence:

"Long live Chairman Mao forever!"

　　女领：它是我们的命根子，
　　男领：它是我们的精神食粮。
