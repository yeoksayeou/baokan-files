Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-07-11
Edition: 1

	Without a firm and correct political direction, it is impossible to foster a spirit of hard work and self-reliance; without a spirit of hard work and self-reliance, it is also impossible to carry out a firm and correct political direction.		*Speech at the Celebration of May Day International Labor Day in Yan'an*
