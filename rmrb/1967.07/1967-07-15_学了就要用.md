Title: Put What You Learn into Practice
Author: Ling Yun
Date: 1967-07-15
Edition: 4

	To correctly handle contradictions among the people, one cannot merely stop at theoretical understanding or mere verbal slogans; instead, one must apply it in practice and implement it in action. What you learn, you must use—you must truly act according to Chairman Mao's instructions.

	"Regarding Marxist theory, one must master it and apply it. The purpose of mastery is entirely for application."

	After learning Chairman Mao's instructions on correctly handling contradictions among the people, when encountering specific issues, one still forgets Chairman Mao's words and instead uses bourgeois and petty-bourgeois thinking to guide actions, using emotions to replace policy. This attitude towards learning itself is a violation of Mao Zedong Thought.

### 学了就要用
凌云
1967-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    学了就要用
    西安交大  凌云
    正确处理人民内部矛盾，不能停留在理论上认识，口头上喊叫；而是要在实践中运用，行动上贯彻。学了就要用，就要切切实实按毛主席的教导办事。
    “对于马克思主义的理论，要能够精通它、应用它，精通的目的全在于应用。”
    学了毛主席关于正确处理人民内部矛盾问题的指示，一碰到具体问题，却把毛主席的话丢在脑后，还是用自己的资产阶级、小资产阶级思想指导行动，还是用感情代替政策。这种学习态度，本身就是违背毛泽东思想的。
