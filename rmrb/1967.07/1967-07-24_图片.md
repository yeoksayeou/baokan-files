Title: A Patriotic Student Art Troupe Performs Anti-British, Anti-Violence Skits
Author:
Date: 1967-07-24
Edition: 4

A patriotic student art troupe, holding high a large banner reading "Patriotism is no crime, resistance to violence is justified," performed anti-British, anti-violence skits near the Wanchai Neck Market in Hong Kong. The performance attracted a large crowd of onlookers.
