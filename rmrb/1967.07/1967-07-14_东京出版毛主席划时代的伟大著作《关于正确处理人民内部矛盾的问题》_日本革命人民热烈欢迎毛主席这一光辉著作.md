Title: Tokyo Publishes Chairman Mao's Milestone Work "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People"
Author:
Date: 1967-07-14
Edition: 5

In Tokyo, as Japanese revolutionary people enthusiastically celebrate the tenth anniversary of the publication of Chairman Mao's milestone work "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People," the Tokyo Chinese Bookstore recently published a single-volume Japanese edition of this great work, which has been warmly welcomed by a wide readership.

The publisher, in recommending this brilliant work, pointed out: "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People" is a beacon for the great proletarian cultural revolution that is shaking the world. In this brilliant work, Chairman Mao, with genius and creativity, comprehensively developed Marxism-Leninism's dialectical materialism and the theory of class struggle and proletarian dictatorship. For the first time, he analyzed the contradictions in socialist society in the deepest, most comprehensive, and most correct manner, proposing the great theory of correctly distinguishing and handling the two types of contradictions. This laid the theoretical foundation for continuing the revolution under the dictatorship of the proletariat, advancing Marxism-Leninism to an entirely new stage—the stage of Mao Zedong Thought.

The cover of the Japanese edition of "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People" features a red flag waving in the wind, with Chairman Mao in military uniform and a red star cap adorned with five points. It symbolizes that the Japanese revolutionary people are advancing victoriously under the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought.

### 东京出版毛主席划时代的伟大著作《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》  日本革命人民热烈欢迎毛主席这一光辉著作

1967-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

    东京出版毛主席划时代的伟大著作
    《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》
    日本革命人民热烈欢迎毛主席这一光辉著作
    新华社东京十三日电　在日本革命人民热烈庆祝毛主席划时代的光辉著作《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》发表十周年的时候，东京中华书店最近出版了这部伟大著作的日文版单行本，受到广大读者的热烈欢迎。
    出版者在推荐这部光辉著作的谈话中指出：《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》这部伟大著作是震撼全世界的中国无产阶级文化大革命的指路明灯。毛主席在这部光辉著作中，天才地、创造性地、全面地发展了马克思列宁主义的辩证唯物主义以及阶级斗争和无产阶级专政的理论，第一次最深刻地、最全面地、最正确地分析了社会主义社会的矛盾，提出了正确区分和处理两类不同性质的矛盾的伟大学说，奠定了在无产阶级专政条件下，必须继续进行革命的理论基础，把马克思列宁主义发展到一个完全崭新的阶段，即毛泽东思想的阶段。
    日文版《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》单行本封面图案是一面迎风招展的红旗，上面是身穿军装、头戴闪烁着五角红星军帽的毛主席像。它象征着日本革命人民正在高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗胜利前进。
