Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets Tanzanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-07-11
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council met with the entire delegation of the Tanzanian military delegation led by Colonel Kashmiri this afternoon and had a warm and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Peng Shaohui, Tang Tianji, Pan Zhenwu, and others.

Tanzanian Ambassador to China Mwaluko was also present.

### 陈毅副总理接见坦桑客人

1967-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见坦桑客人
    新华社九日讯　国务院副总理陈毅，今天下午接见了由卡什米里中校率领的坦桑尼亚军事代表团全体成员，同他们进行了热情友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有彭绍辉、唐天际、潘振武等。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科也在座。
