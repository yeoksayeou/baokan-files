Title: Comrade Lin Biao Cables Comrade Balluku to Extend Warmest Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Albanian Army
Author:
Date: 1967-07-10
Edition: 1

Comrade Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, cabled Comrade Bekir Balluku, Vice Premier of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Albania, on July 8 to extend the warmest congratulations on the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian People's Army. The full text of the cable is as follows:

To Comrade Bekir Balluku, Vice Premier of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Albania:

Dear Comrade Bekir Balluku,

On the occasion of the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian People's Army, I, on behalf of the Chinese people and all the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and in my personal capacity, extend the warmest congratulations to you and, through you, to the heroic Albanian people and all the officers and soldiers of the Albanian People's Army.

Under the wise leadership of the Albanian Labor Party, which is headed by Comrade Enver Hoxha and has stood the test of time and is glorious, the Albanian people and the Albanian People's Army have firmly held high the red banner of Marxism-Leninism and are advancing courageously along the road to victory in the face of the encirclement by imperialism and modern revisionism. Over the past year, the heroic comrades-in-arms of the Albanian People's Army have actively responded to the call of the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Labor Party and Comrade Hoxha, and have achieved great success in further emphasizing proletarian politics and strengthening revolutionary construction; in implementing a series of major revolutionary measures such as adhering to the mass line, strengthening ideological education, opposing bureaucratism, and having cadres participate in labor, they have achieved tremendous success. The entire army is more vigorous and full of fighting spirit. This will have a profound impact on promoting the construction of the Albanian People's Army in all aspects and further strengthening the dictatorship of the proletariat and the socialist system in Albania. Your victories and achievements are a great encouragement to the Chinese people who are carrying out the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and to the revolutionary people of all countries; they are a heavy blow to imperialism and modern revisionism.

The friendship and unity between the peoples and armies of China and Albania, which are based on the principles of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, have developed further and achieved greater progress over the past year. Comrade Mao Zedong said: "The peoples of China and Albania are separated by thousands of mountains and rivers, but our hearts are united. We are your true friends and comrades. You are also our true friends and comrades. We are not those false friends who are honey-tongued but dagger-hearted, nor are we two-faced. Our revolutionary and fighting friendship has withstood the test of storms and tempests." We are deeply convinced that this great friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Albania will be further consolidated and developed in the struggle against imperialism headed by the United States, against modern revisionism centered on the leadership of the CPSU, and against the reactionaries of all countries, in the course of carrying out the socialist revolution and socialist construction, and promoting the progress of mankind. It will continue to exert its invincible and great fighting power!

May the heroic Albania—the great socialist beacon of Europe—shine with even greater brilliance!

May the Albanian People's Army, in strengthening revolutionary construction, continuously improving political quality and combat effectiveness, and in the struggle to defend the socialist motherland and safeguard the peace of Europe and the world, achieve new and greater successes!

May the eternal, indestructible fighting friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Albania flourish forever!

Lin Biao
Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense
Beijing, July 8, 1967

专栏：

　　林彪同志致电巴卢库同志最热烈祝贺阿建军节
    祝兄弟的阿人民军在加强革命化建设，不断提高政治素质和战斗威力，保卫祖国，保卫和平的斗争中取得新的更大成就
    新华社九日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪同志八日打电报给阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库同志，最热烈地祝贺阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军二十四周年。电报全文如下：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国防部长亲爱的贝基尔·巴卢库同志：
    在兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军二十四周年的时候，我代表中国人民和中国人民解放军全体指战员，并以我个人的名义，向你并通过你，向英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民和阿尔巴尼亚人民军全体指战员，致以最热烈的祝贺。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民和阿尔巴尼亚人民军，在以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的、久经考验的、光荣的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的英明领导下，在帝国主义和现代修正主义的重重包围之中，坚定地高举马克思列宁主义的革命红旗，正沿着胜利的道路奋勇前进。一年来，英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民军战友们，积极响应了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会和霍查同志的号召，在进一步突出无产阶级政治，加强革命化建设方面，取得了重大的成就；在贯彻群众路线、加强思想教育、反对官僚主义、干部参加劳动等一系列重大革命化措施方面，获得了巨大的成功。全军更加朝气蓬勃、斗志昂扬。这对于推动阿尔巴尼亚人民军各方面的建设，进一步加强阿尔巴尼亚的无产阶级专政和社会主义制度，必将产生深远的影响。你们的胜利和成就，对于正在胜利进行无产阶级文化大革命的中国人民，对于各国革命人民，都是一个巨大的鼓舞；对于帝国主义和现代修正主义，则是一个沉重的打击。
    建立在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义原则基础之上的中阿两国人民和两国军队之间的友谊和团结，一年来又有了新的、更大的发展。毛泽东同志说：“中阿两国远隔千山万水，我们的心是连在一起的。我们是你们真正的朋友和同志。你们也是我们真正的朋友和同志。我们和你们都不是那种口蜜腹剑的假朋友，不是那种两面派。我们之间的革命的战斗的友谊，经历过急风暴雨的考验。”我们深信，中阿两国人民和两国军队之间的这种伟大友谊，在今后反对以美国为首的帝国主义、反对以苏共领导集团为中心的现代修正主义、反对各国反动派的斗争中，在进行社会主义革命和社会主义建设、促进人类进步的伟大事业中，必将获得不断的巩固和发展，必将继续发挥它无坚不摧的巨大战斗威力！
    祝英雄的阿尔巴尼亚——欧洲的伟大的社会主义明灯放射出更加灿烂的光辉！
    祝兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民军在加强革命化建设，不断提高政治素质和战斗威力，保卫社会主义祖国、保卫欧洲与世界和平的斗争中，取得新的更大成就。
    祝中阿两国人民和两国军队之间的永恒的牢不可破的战斗友谊万古长青！
　　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长  林彪
　　一九六七年七月八日于北京
