Title: A Great Joy for the People of the World – Warmly Celebrate the Widespread Circulation of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" Around the World
Author:
Date: 1967-07-02
Edition: 1

The widespread circulation of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" in more than a hundred countries and regions has been met with the warmest welcome and praise from hundreds of millions of revolutionary people. This is a great joy for the people of the world, and another resounding victory song of the unprecedented Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China.

The widespread circulation of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" around the world is a major event in the history of the international communist movement. Mao Zedong Thought is the historical locomotive of people's revolution. The widespread dissemination of Mao Zedong Thought around the world, when grasped by the revolutionary people of the world, will become an inexhaustible force, a spiritual atomic bomb of immense power, and will have a great and profound impact on the world revolution.

The widespread circulation of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" around the world has brought the invincible Mao Zedong Thought to the world's revolutionary people, promoting the deep development of the mass movement of studying and applying Mao Zedong Thought. The world's people love to read Chairman Mao's books because they are the highest level of Marxism-Leninism, universal truths applicable everywhere, and the sharpest weapons against imperialism, modern revisionism, and reactionaries of all countries.

The widespread circulation of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" around the world has brought the revolutionary people of the world a guide for revolutionary struggle. The world's revolutionary people call "Quotations from Chairman Mao" the "Little Red Book." Reading this red book clarifies who the enemies and friends are. Reading this treasure book makes the direction clear and the confidence strong, teaching how to revolutionize and how to struggle. The world's revolutionary people are overjoyed to have this red book, like a drought-stricken land receiving sweet rain, or a ship in the fog seeing a lighthouse. They are determined to study and apply it in their struggles, lives, studies, and work, and to contribute to the liberation cause of their own nation and all humanity.

The widespread circulation of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" around the world greatly boosts the morale of the proletariat and strikes fear into the hearts of imperialists, revisionists, and reactionaries. "Quotations from Chairman Mao" is like a blazing spark, igniting the flames of rebellion in the hearts of oppressed people around the world. The revolutionary people of the world, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, dare to climb mountains of knives and seas of fire, dare to smash the shackles of slavery, dare to shatter the evil old world, and strive to become the masters of the new era; while all the ghosts and snakes, upon seeing Mao Zedong Thought, are like bats seeing the light, losing their souls and trembling with fear.

The widespread circulation of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" around the world will make Mao Zedong Thought increasingly penetrate people's hearts, thereby rousing the broad masses of laborers around the world to form a mighty revolutionary army, launch a powerful offensive against the old world, and strive for the ultimate victory of the world proletarian revolution!

The great red flag of the invincible Mao Zedong Thought will surely be planted all over the globe!

### 世界人民的大喜事——热烈欢呼《毛主席语录》在全世界广泛发行
新华社
1967-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界人民的大喜事
　　——热烈欢呼《毛主席语录》在全世界广泛发行
　　新华社短评
    《毛主席语录》在一百多个国家和地区广泛发行，受到了亿万革命人民最热烈的欢迎和颂扬。这是世界人民的大喜事，是史无前例的中国无产阶级文化大革命的又一曲响彻云霄的凯歌。
    《毛主席语录》在全世界广泛发行，是国际共产主义运动史上的一个重大事件。毛泽东思想是人民革命的历史火车头。毛泽东思想在全世界的广泛传播，为世界革命人民所掌握，就会变成无穷无尽的力量，变成威力无比的精神原子弹，对世界革命产生巨大而深远的影响。
    《毛主席语录》在全世界广泛发行，给世界革命人民带来了战无不胜的毛泽东思想，促进世界革命人民活学活用毛泽东思想的群众运动的深入发展。世界人民最爱读毛主席的书，因为它是最高水平的马克思列宁主义，是放之四海而皆准的普遍真理，是反对帝国主义、现代修正主义和各国反动派的最锐利的武器。
    《毛主席语录》在全世界广泛发行，给世界革命人民带来了进行革命斗争的指针。世界革命人民把《毛主席语录》称为“红色宝书”。读了这红书，心里明，眼中亮，认清谁是敌人，谁是朋友。读了这宝书，方向明，信心足，学会怎样革命，怎样斗争。世界革命人民喜得这红书，就象久旱逢甘霖，雾航见灯塔。他们决心在斗争中、生活中、学习中和工作中活学活用，并且为本民族和全人类的解放事业作出贡献。
    《毛主席语录》在全世界广泛发行，大长无产阶级的志气，大灭帝、修、反的威风。《毛主席语录》象炽烈的火种，正在全世界被压迫人民心中点燃起反抗的怒火。世界革命人民用毛泽东思想武装起来，就刀山敢上，火海敢闯，敢于打碎奴隶的枷锁，敢于砸烂万恶的旧世界，争做新时代的主人；而一切牛鬼蛇神见了毛泽东思想，就象蝙蝠见了阳光，失魂落魄，胆战心惊。
    《毛主席语录》在全世界广泛发行，将使毛泽东思想越来越深入人心，从而唤起世界广大劳动群众，组成一支浩浩荡荡的革命大军，向旧世界发动声势凌厉的总攻击，争取无产阶级世界革命的彻底胜利！
    战无不胜的毛泽东思想的伟大红旗必将插遍全球！
