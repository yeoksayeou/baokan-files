Title: The Second Ocean-Going Ship of the Sino-Tanzanian Joint Shipping Company is Put into Use; Nyerere Receives a Warm Welcome During His Visit to the Cargo Ship
Author:
Date: 1967-07-28
Edition: 5

The second ocean-going ship of the Sino-Tanzanian Joint Shipping Company, the 12,000-ton cargo ship "Asia-Africa," flying the Chinese national flag, began operations in the port of Dar es Salaam on July 26.

Tanzanian President Nyerere visited the "Asia-Africa" cargo ship in the afternoon of July 26 and was warmly welcomed. Accompanied by the Chinese captain, President Nyerere praised the joint shipping company established by Tanzania and China, saying it "has had a very good beginning."

On the same day at noon, the company's co-directors held a reception on board the ship. Tanzanian Vice President Kawawa and others, as well as the Chinese chargé d'affaires and commercial representative at the Chinese Embassy in Dar es Salaam, attended the reception.

### 中坦联合航运公司第二艘远洋轮船启用  尼雷尔参观货轮时受到热烈欢迎

1967-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中坦联合航运公司第二艘远洋轮船启用
    尼雷尔参观货轮时受到热烈欢迎
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆二十六日电　高悬着中国国旗的中国—坦桑尼亚联合航运公司的第二艘远洋轮船——一万二千吨货轮“亚非号”，二十六日在达累斯萨拉姆港开始使用。
    坦桑尼亚总统尼雷尔二十六日下午参观了“亚非号”货轮，受到热烈欢迎。尼雷尔总统在中国船长陪同下参观时，称赞坦桑尼亚和中国共同创建的联合航运公司“有了很良好的开端”。
    同一天中午，公司的双方总经理在船上举行招待会。坦桑尼亚第二副总统卡瓦瓦等及中国驻达累斯萨拉姆大使馆临时代办和商务代表出席招待会。
