Title: Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1967-07-31
Edition: 4

The General Staff Department and the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held an evening of performing arts on the 30th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The event was attended by diplomats and military attachés from various countries stationed in China, along with their spouses.

The venue was adorned with large banners reading "Long Live the Great Communist Party of China" and "Long Live the Great Leader Chairman Mao." Before the performance began, both the stage and the audience chorused, "Long live our dearest and most beloved Chairman Mao! Long live Chairman Mao!"

The evening started with the stirring songs "The East Is Red," "Father and Mother Are Dearer Than Chairman Mao," and a song based on Chairman Mao's quotes, "Our Literature and Art Are for the People." This was followed by a grand revolutionary modern ballet, "The Red Detachment of Women," which was enthusiastically received.

Attending the event were Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Wang Xinting, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Yuan Ziqin, Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department Zhang Xianyu, as well as leaders from various military branches and the Beijing Military Region. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong also attended the event.

The evening concluded with the song "Count on the Helmsman to Navigate the Seas."

### 庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十周年  总参总政举行文艺晚会招待各国使节和武官

1967-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十周年
    总参总政举行文艺晚会招待各国使节和武官
    新华社三十日讯　中国人民解放军总参谋部、总政治部为庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十周年今晚举行文艺晚会，招待各国驻中国的使节、武官和他们的夫人。
    会场内高挂着“伟大的中国共产党万岁”“伟大的领袖毛主席万岁”的巨幅标语和毛主席语录。
    晚会开始前，台上、台下齐声欢呼敬祝我们心中最红最红的红太阳毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！
    文艺晚会在雄壮的《东方红》、《爹亲娘亲不如毛主席亲》和毛主席语录歌《我们的文学艺术都是为人民大众的》歌声中开始。接着演出了大型革命现代芭蕾舞剧——《红色娘子军》。演出受到了热烈的欢迎。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭、总政治部副主任袁子钦、总后勤部副部长张贤约，以及各军种、兵种和北京部队负责人出席晚会陪同观看。外交部副部长韩念龙也出席了晚会。
    晚会在《大海航行靠舵手》的歌声中结束。
