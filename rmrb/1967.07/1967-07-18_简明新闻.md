	Brief News

	Japan-China Friendship Association Kyoto Prefecture (Orthodox) Meeting
	The Japan-China Friendship Association Kyoto Prefecture (Orthodox) held a meeting with over 500 participants on the evening of the 12th in Kyoto. The attendees expressed their determination to uphold the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, to safeguard and develop genuine Japan-China friendship, and to fight against the common enemy of the peoples of Japan and China—American imperialism, Soviet modern revisionism, and Japanese militarism.
	Notable historian and Kyoto University professor Jianqing, and Japanese revolutionary friend Renbao, both delivered speeches at the meeting.

	Arif Denounces U.S. Involvement in Israeli Aggression
	Iraqi President Arif delivered a statement on the 13th, strongly condemning the United States for supporting and directly participating in Israel's military aggression against Arab countries.
	Arif pointed out that Israel is a hound acting under the command of the United States.

	Egal Forms New Government of Somali Republic
	According to a broadcast by the Mogadishu radio station on the 15th, the Somali Republic has formed a new government led by Prime Minister Muhammad Haji Ibrahim Egal.
	The new government consists of 18 ministers. Prime Minister Egal also serves as the Foreign Minister, Deputy Prime Minister Haji Farah Ali Omar also serves as the Finance Minister, Yasin Nur Hassan is the Interior Minister, and Yusuf Iman Gulleid is the Defense Minister.

	Thai Puppet Regime Again Armily Invades Cambodia
	On the night of July 12th to the early morning of the 13th, Thai armed forces invaded Cambodian territory, destroying a 60-meter section of railway in the southeast of Poupeu County, Battambang Province.
	According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 16th, the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a note of protest to the Thai government on the 13th through the Indonesian Embassy in Cambodia, strongly condemning the new acts of aggression by Thai armed forces in invading Cambodian territory and territorial waters on June 15th, 16th, and 17th.

	Suharto Appoints Old Right-Wing Figure as "Advisor"
	The fascist military clique led by Suharto-Nasution recently invited the notorious old right-wing figure from Indonesia, the reactionary economic "expert" Sumitro, who had been in exile in "Malaysia" for many years, back to the country to serve as Suharto's "economic advisor." In the so-called "revolutionary government" established after the counter-revolutionary rebellion in Sumatra in 1957, Sumitro served as the Minister of Commerce and concurrently as the Minister of Transportation and Communications.

    简明新闻
    日中友协京都府（正统）总部集会
    日中友好协会京都府（正统）总部十二日晚上在京都举行了有五百多人参加的集会。与会者表示决心高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，维护和发展真正的日中友好事业，为反对日中两国人民的共同敌人美帝国主义、苏联现代修正主义、日本军国主义而奋斗。
    著名历史学家、京都大学教授井上清，日本革命朋友仁保都在大会上发表了讲话。
    阿里夫谴责美帝参与以色列侵略
    伊拉克总统阿里夫十三日发表谈话，强烈谴责美国支持并直接参与以色列对阿拉伯国家的军事侵略。
    阿里夫指出，以色列是在美国指挥下活动的一条猎狗。
    埃加勒组成索马里共和国新政府
    据摩加迪沙电台十五日广播，索马里共和国已组成以新总理穆罕默德·哈吉·易卜拉欣·埃加勒为首的新政府。
    新政府由十八名部长组成，总理埃加勒兼任外交部长，副总理哈吉·法拉赫·阿里·奥马尔兼任财政部长，雅辛·努尔·哈桑任内政部长，优素福·伊曼·古勒特任国防部长。
    泰国傀儡当局再次武装侵犯柬埔寨
    七月十二日晚上到十三日凌晨，泰国武装分子侵入柬埔寨领土，在马德望省普瓦佩县东南炸毁了六十米长的一段铁路。
    另据柬埔寨新闻社十六日报道，柬埔寨外交部十三日通过印度尼西亚驻柬埔寨大使馆，交给泰国政府一份照会，强烈抗议泰国武装部队在六月十五、十六和十七日五次侵犯柬埔寨领土和领海的新的侵略罪行。
    苏哈托让老右派头子充当“顾问”
    以苏哈托—纳苏蒂安为首的印度尼西亚法西斯军人集团，最近把多年逃亡在“马来西亚”的臭名昭彰的印尼老右派头子、反动经济“权威”苏米特罗请回国内，充当苏哈托的“经济顾问”。一九五七年在苏门答腊反革命叛乱后成立的所谓“革命政府”中，苏米特罗任商业部长兼交通和航运部长。
                        （据新华社讯）
