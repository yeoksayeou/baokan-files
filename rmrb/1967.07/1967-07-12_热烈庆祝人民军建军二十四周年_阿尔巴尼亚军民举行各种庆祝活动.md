Title: Warmly Celebrate the 24th Anniversary of the People's Army
Author:
Date: 1967-07-12
Edition: 5

Albanian military and civilians held various activities to warmly celebrate the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian People's Army.

New China News Agency, Tirana, July 11th — The Albanian military and civilians held various activities to warmly celebrate the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian People's Army.

The Albanian Ministry of National Defense held a celebration meeting on the 9th for the Army Day. The Chief of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army, Qako, and the commanders and soldiers of the Tirana Military Region, as well as representatives from various sectors of the people, attended the meeting. The meeting unanimously passed a letter of congratulations to the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party.

On the morning of the 10th, the representative of the Albanian Ministry of National Defense laid a wreath at the martyrs' tomb.

On the evening of the 10th, the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe held a grand outdoor performance at the "Skanderbeg" Square in Tirana to warmly celebrate the Army Day.

The military and people in areas such as Elbasan and Lezhe also held meetings to celebrate the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian People's Army.

### 热烈庆祝人民军建军二十四周年  阿尔巴尼亚军民举行各种庆祝活动

1967-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

    热烈庆祝人民军建军二十四周年
    阿尔巴尼亚军民举行各种庆祝活动
    新华社地拉那十一日电　阿尔巴尼亚军民举行各种活动，热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军二十四周年。
    阿尔巴尼亚国防部在九日为建军节举行了庆祝集会。阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部主任恰科和地拉那军区的指战员、各界劳动人民代表出席了集会。集会一致通过了给阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会的贺信。
    十日上午，阿尔巴尼亚国防部代表向烈士墓敬献了花圈。
    十日晚，阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团在地拉那“斯坎德培”广场举行了盛大露天演出，热烈庆祝建军节。
    爱尔巴桑、莱希等区驻军和人民也举行了庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军二十四周年的集会。
