Title: Soldiers and Local Residents Criticize the Crimes of the Chinese Khrushchev
Author: Yang Changzhong
Date: 1967-07-27
Edition: 3

Soldiers and officers of a unit of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing, together with members of the local commune, used their rest time in the fields to fiercely denounce the heinous crimes of the Chinese Khrushchev in attempting to restore capitalism. They worked to eliminate his pernicious influence in the countryside.
