Indian students in Calcutta hold a demonstration

Indian students in Calcutta held a large-scale demonstration on the 20th, strongly protesting against U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam and the Soviet revisionist leadership's collusion with U.S. imperialism to betray the Vietnamese people's just struggle.

According to reports, the demonstrators held up placards reading "Oppose U.S. atrocities in Vietnam" and burned an effigy of U.S. war criminal Johnson in front of the U.S. Information Agency library. They also burned an effigy of Soviet revisionist leader Kosygin in front of the Soviet consulate, shouting slogans against the Soviet revisionist leadership's betrayal of the Vietnamese people's just struggle, and pasted protest letters on the consulate's walls.

Nearly 500,000 workers in Uruguay go on strike

Nearly 500,000 workers in Uruguay went on a 24-hour strike on the 20th, opposing the economic policies imposed by the U.S.-controlled International Monetary Fund that harm the country's national economy and the interests of the working people, and demanding that the Uruguayan government sever ties with the International Monetary Fund.

Workers from factories, railways, buses, telephone and power companies, as well as hospitals participated in the strike. (Xinhua News Agency)

专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    印度加尔各答学生举行游行示威
    在加尔各答的印度学生二十日举行大规模的游行示威，强烈抗议美帝国主义侵略越南和苏修领导集团勾结美帝出卖越南人民的正义斗争。
    据报道，示威学生高举“反对美国在越南的残暴行径”的标语牌，在美国新闻处图书馆门前焚烧了美国战争贩子约翰逊的模拟人像。他们又在苏联领事馆门前焚烧了苏修头目柯西金的模拟人像，高呼反对苏修领导集团出卖越南人民正义斗争的口号，并且把抗议书贴在领事馆的墙上。
    乌拉圭近五十万职工举行大罢工
    乌拉圭近五十万名职工二十日举行二十四小时罢工，反对美国控制的国际货币基金组织强加给乌拉圭的损害这个国家的民族经济和劳动人民利益的经济政策，并且要求乌拉圭政府同国际货币基金组织断绝关系。
    参加这次罢工的有工厂、铁路，公共汽车、电话和电力公司以及医院的职工。　（新华社）
