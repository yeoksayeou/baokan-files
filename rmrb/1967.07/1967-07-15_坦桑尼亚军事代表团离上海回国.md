Title: Tanzanian Military Delegation Leaves Shanghai to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1967-07-15
Edition: 5

A Tanzanian military delegation led by Colonel Kashmiri concluded its friendly visit to China and departed from Shanghai this evening by plane to return home. The delegation was seen off at the airport by Tang Tianji, Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as responsible personnel from the Shanghai garrison and relevant departments.

Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Mwaluko, was also at the airport to see them off.

During their stay in Shanghai, the Tanzanian friends visited factories, suburban people's communes, the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition, and the East Sea Fleet of the People's Liberation Army Navy. They also gained an understanding of the excellent situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in the Shanghai region.

### 坦桑尼亚军事代表团离上海回国

1967-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚军事代表团离上海回国
    新华社上海十三日电  由卡什米里中校率领的坦桑尼亚联合共和国军事代表团结束了在我国的友好访问，今晚乘飞机离上海回国。中国人民解放军总后勤部副部长唐天际，以及驻上海部队和有关部门负责人到机场热情欢送。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科，也到机场送行。
    坦桑尼亚朋友在上海期间，参观访问了工厂、郊区人民公社、上海工业展览会、人民解放军海军东海舰队，并且了解了上海地区的无产阶级文化大革命的大好形势。
