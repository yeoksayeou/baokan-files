Title: French Ambassador Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1967-07-16
Edition: 6

French Ambassador to China, Mr. Peyre, held a reception today to celebrate France's National Day. Relevant officials from our side, including Zhang Siyu, Luo Guibao, and Yang Haolu, were invited to attend the reception. Ambassadors from various countries stationed in China also attended the reception.

### 法国大使举行国庆招待会

1967-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

    法国大使举行国庆招待会
    新华社十四日讯　法国驻中国大使佩耶今天举行招待会，庆祝法国国庆日。我有关方面负责人张奚若、罗贵波、杨浩庐等应邀出席了招待会。各国驻中国使节也出席招待会。
