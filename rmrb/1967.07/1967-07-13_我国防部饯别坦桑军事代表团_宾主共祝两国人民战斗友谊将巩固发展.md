Title: The Chinese Ministry of National Defense Sees Off the Tanzanian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-07-13
Edition: 5

The Ministry of National Defense held a banquet in Shanghai this evening to warmly send off the military delegation from the United Republic of Tanzania.

At the banquet, filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere, Tang Tianji, Deputy Minister of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Kashmiri, the head of the Tanzanian military delegation and Director of the Engineering Department of the Tanzanian People's Defense Forces General Staff, both gave speeches.

Tang Tianji, on behalf of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, warmly congratulated the Tanzanian military delegation on their successful visit to China. He said that the visit by the Tanzanian friends will further enhance the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Tanzania in their opposition to imperialism and old and new colonialism, and will consolidate and develop the friendly cooperative relations between the two countries.

Kashmiri, the head of the delegation, enthusiastically praised the Chinese people and the People's Liberation Army for solving practical problems with Mao Zedong Thought, and for achieving tremendous progress in industrial and agricultural production.

He said, "We will return home carrying the deep friendship of the Chinese people and the People's Liberation Army. With the help of a country like China, we can overcome our difficulties."

The Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Mwaluko, attended the farewell banquet.

Senior officials from the Shanghai-based units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and relevant departments attended the banquet as hosts.

### 我国防部饯别坦桑军事代表团  宾主共祝两国人民战斗友谊将巩固发展

1967-07-13
第5版()
专栏：

    我国防部饯别坦桑军事代表团
    宾主共祝两国人民战斗友谊将巩固发展
    据新华社上海十二日电  国防部今晚在上海举行宴会，热烈欢送坦桑尼亚联合共和国军事代表团。
    在洋溢着热情友好气氛的宴会上，中国人民解放军总后勤部副部长唐天际和坦桑尼亚军事代表团团长、坦桑尼亚人民国防军总司令部后勤工程部部长卡什米里中校先后讲话。
    唐天际代表我国国防部热烈祝贺坦桑尼亚军事代表团在我国访问成功。他说，坦桑尼亚朋友这次访问，将进一步增进中坦两国人民反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的战斗友谊，巩固和发展两国的友好合作关系。
    卡什米里团长热情地赞扬中国人民和人民解放军用毛泽东思想解决实际问题，工农业生产取得巨大发展。
    他说，我们将带着中国人民和人民解放军的深厚友谊回国。我们有象中国这样的援助，能够克服我们的困难。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科出席了欢送宴会。
    中国人民解放军驻上海部队负责人和驻上海部队有关部门负责人出席作陪。
