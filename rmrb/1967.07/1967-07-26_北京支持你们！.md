Title: Beijing Supports You!
Author:
Date: 1967-07-26
Edition: 1

Beijing Supports You!

Yesterday afternoon, in our great capital, a million proletarian revolutionaries and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army gathered in Tiananmen Square to warmly welcome Comrades Xie Fuzhi and Wang Li back to Beijing in victory and glory. They resolutely supported the proletarian revolutionaries in the Wuhan area.

Our great leader Chairman Mao's close comrade-in-arms Comrade Lin Biao, as well as responsible comrades from the Party Central Committee and the Central Cultural Revolution Group, attended this meeting.

This meeting reflected the greatest concern of our beloved leader Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Lin, the Party Central Committee, and the Central Cultural Revolution Group for the proletarian revolutionaries in the Wuhan area. It demonstrated the support of Beijing and proletarian revolutionaries from all over the country for their comrades in Wuhan.

This meeting greatly boosted the morale of the proletarian revolutionaries and struck a heavy blow to the arrogance of the handful of power-holders taking the capitalist road within the Party and the army. Comrades in the Wuhan area, your struggle is not isolated. The proletarian revolutionaries across the country support you! Our great leader Chairman Mao supports you! The Party Central Committee supports you! The Central Cultural Revolution Group supports you! The proletarian revolutionaries in Beijing support you!

The revolutionary people armed with Mao Zedong Thought are invincible, they are the true iron wall. Although the enemies of the proletarian revolution are still playing tricks and plotting conspiracies, using brutal means to struggle desperately, they are ultimately doomed to failure. The handful of bad leaders in the "Million Heroes" of the Wuhan area who insist on the bourgeois reactionary line have been extremely isolated, and their failure is inevitable.

The enemy will not surrender, so we must annihilate it!

Victory will definitely belong to the proletarian revolutionaries in the Wuhan area, and to Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line!

### 北京支持你们！

1967-07-26
第1版()
专栏：社论

    北京支持你们！
    昨天下午，在我们伟大的祖国首都，百万无产阶级革命派和人民解放军三军指战员，在天安门广场隆重集会，最热烈地欢迎谢富治、王力同志胜利地、光荣地回到北京，最坚决地声援武汉地区的无产阶级革命派。
    我们伟大领袖毛主席的亲密战友林彪同志，以及党中央、中央文革小组的负责同志，参加了这次大会。
    这次大会，体现了我们敬爱的领袖毛主席、林副主席、党中央、中央文革小组对武汉地区无产阶级革命派最大的关怀，显示了北京和全国各地的无产阶级革命派对武汉地区战友们的最大的支持。
    这次大会，大长了无产阶级革命派的志气，大灭了党内、军内一小撮走资本主义道路当权派的威风。武汉地区的战友们，你们的斗争决不是孤立的。全国无产阶级革命派支持你们！我们伟大的领袖毛主席支持你们！党中央支持你们！中央文革小组支持你们！北京无产阶级革命派支持你们！
    用毛泽东思想武装起来的革命人民，是天下无敌的，是真正的铜墙铁壁。尽管无产阶级革命的敌人还在耍出种种花招，玩弄种种阴谋，用残暴的手段进行垂死挣扎，但是他们终究逃不脱失败的命运。武汉地区“百万雄师”中坚持资产阶级反动路线的一小撮坏头头，已经极端孤立了，他们的失败是完全注定了的。
    敌人不投降，就叫它灭亡！
    胜利一定属于武汉地区的无产阶级革命派，属于毛主席的无产阶级革命路线！
