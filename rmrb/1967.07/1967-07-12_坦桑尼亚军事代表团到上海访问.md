Title: Tanzanian Military Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1967-07-12
Edition: 5

Tanzanian military delegation visits Shanghai

A Xinhua News Agency Shanghai July 10th dispatch: The Tanzanian military delegation, led by the head of the delegation and the Director of the Logistics Engineering Department of the Tanzanian People's Defense Forces, Colonel Kashmiri, arrived in Shanghai by plane from Beijing this morning. The delegation was accompanied by Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Tang Tianji.

The Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Mwaluko, also arrived at the same time.

Relevant leaders from the Shanghai garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army warmly welcomed the Tanzanian friends at the airport.

In the evening, the Shanghai Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army hosted a banquet to welcome the Tanzanian military delegation.

### 坦桑尼亚军事代表团到上海访问

1967-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚军事代表团到上海访问
    新华社上海十日电　坦桑尼亚联合共和国军事代表团在团长、坦桑尼亚人民国防军总司令部后勤工程部部长卡什米里中校率领下，由中国人民解放军总后勤部副部长唐天际陪同，今天上午从北京乘飞机到达上海。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科也同时到达。
    中国人民解放军驻上海部队有关方面负责人到机场热烈欢迎坦桑尼亚朋友。
    晚间，中国人民解放军上海警备区设便宴招待坦桑尼亚联合共和国军事代表团。
