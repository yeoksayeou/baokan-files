	Chairman Mao's Quotations
	People of the whole world, unite and defeat the American aggressors and all their lackeys! People of the whole world, dare to fight, do not fear sacrifice, and advance wave upon wave. Then the whole world will belong to the people. All the reactionaries are paper tigers. Their end is foreseeable. They will be routed.
