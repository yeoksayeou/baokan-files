Title: China and Poland Sign Agreement on Goods Exchange and Payment
Author:
Date: 1967-07-02
Edition: 5

According to Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 1: The 1967 goods exchange and payment agreement between China and Poland was signed in Warsaw on June 30.

Signing on behalf of China was Jia Shi, head of the Chinese government trade delegation and deputy minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade. Signing on behalf of Poland was Demokhovsky, head of the Polish government trade delegation and deputy minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

### 中波两国换货和付款协定签字

1967-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

　　中波两国换货和付款协定签字
    据新华社华沙一日电　中波两国一九六七年交换货物和付款协定于六月三十日在华沙签订。
    代表中国方面在协定上签字的是中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长贾石；代表波兰方面签字的是波兰政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长德莫霍夫斯基。
