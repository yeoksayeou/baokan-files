Title: Inner Mongolia Achieves Bumper Harvest in Livestock Industry This Year
Author:
Date: 1967-07-25
Edition: 4

	The broad masses of revolutionary herdsmen and revolutionary cadres on the livestock front in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, have achieved another bumper harvest in the livestock industry in the triumphant songs of the great victory of the Cultural Revolution.

	According to statistics from relevant departments of the autonomous region, during the 1967 livestock production year from July of last year to June of this year, the entire region bred and raised over 9.4 million young livestock of various kinds, an increase of more than 20 percent over the previous year. The total number of livestock has increased by over 1.3 million heads on the basis of last year. In this year, herdsmen have also provided the state with over 3.2 million draft and meat animals. The wool and hides delivered to the state have also increased significantly.

### 内蒙古今年畜牧业丰收

1967-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

    内蒙古今年畜牧业丰收
    据新华社呼和浩特电　内蒙古自治区畜牧业战线广大革命牧民和革命干部，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，在文化大革命取得伟大胜利的凯歌声中，又夺得了今年畜牧业的丰收。
    据自治区有关部门统计，从去年七月到今年六月的一九六七年畜牧业生产年度里，全区接育大小仔畜九百四十多万头，比上年度增产百分之二十多。牲畜总头数已在去年的基础上净增一百三十多万头。在这一年里，牧民还向国家提供了三百二十多万头耕畜和肉畜。交售的绒毛和皮张也都有较大幅度的增长。
