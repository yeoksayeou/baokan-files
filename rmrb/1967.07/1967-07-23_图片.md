Title: Students in Calcutta Protest Soviet Revisionists' Betrayal of Vietnamese People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1967-07-23
Edition: 5

On July 20, students in Calcutta, India, held a large-scale demonstration to strongly protest the Soviet revisionist clique's collusion with U.S. imperialism in betraying the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression. The photo shows demonstrators burning an effigy of Soviet revisionist leader Kosygin near the Soviet consulate. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency)
