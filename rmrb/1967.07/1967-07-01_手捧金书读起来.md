Title: Holding the Golden Book and Reading It
Author: Zheng Mingdong
Date: 1967-07-01
Edition: 8

	Holding the golden book and reading it,
	Every word and sentence shines brightly,
	One sentence is worth ten thousand,
	Revolutionary people hold it in the highest regard.
	Holding the golden book and reading it,
	Arming the mind and warming the heart,
	With a red heart, bright eyes, and a strong spine,
	We tread upon the great winds and waves.
	Holding the golden book and reading it,
	With courage and ambition throughout the body,
	We fear no great difficulties,
	We take up the heavy burden of revolution.
	Holding the golden book and reading it,
	Mao Zedong Thought will be passed down for generations,
	With an unchanging red heart,
	We will forever be thorough revolutionaries.

### 手捧金书读起来
郑明东
1967-07-01
第8版()
专栏：

　　手捧金书读起来
　　解放军某部战士　郑明东
　　手捧金书读起来，
    字字句句放光彩，
    一句能顶一万句，
    革命人民最崇拜。
    手捧金书读起来，
    武装头脑暖心怀，
    心红眼亮骨头硬，
    大风大浪脚底踩。
    手捧金书读起来，
    浑身是胆志豪迈，
    天大困难咱不怕，
    革命重担挑起来。
    手捧金书读起来，
    毛泽东思想传万代，
    一颗红心不变色，
    永做彻底革命派。
