Title: "Not a Single Soldier to Colonial Wars!" Dutch People Demonstrate and March

Author:
Date: 1949-03-31
Page: 3

　　"Not a Single Soldier to Colonial Wars!"
    Dutch People Demonstrate and March
    [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, March 30th] The Hague dispatch: According to a report by the Dutch Communist Party's newspaper "Truth" on the 29th, when a large number of Dutch wounded soldiers were recently transported from Indonesia to the port of Amsterdam, Dutch people held demonstrations and marches on the streets of the port, chanting: "Withdraw Dutch troops from Indonesia," "Not a single soldier to colonial wars," "Down with the North Atlantic Military Treaty." The newspaper also published on the same day the conversations of many returning wounded soldiers, one of whom said: If the Indonesian people had sufficient arms, they would throw all the Dutch troops into the sea.

### “不出一卒参加殖民地战争！”　荷民众示威游行

1949-03-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　“不出一卒参加殖民地战争！”
    荷民众示威游行
    【新华社北平三十日电】海牙讯：据荷共真理报二十九日报导：当最近大批荷兰伤兵从印尼运抵阿姆斯丹港时，荷兰民众在该港大街上举行示威游行，高呼：“从印尼撤退荷军”，“不出一兵一卒参加殖民地战争”，“打倒北大西洋军事协定。”该报同日登载许多归国伤兵的谈话，其中有一个伤兵的说：如果印尼人民有充分的武装，他们会把全部荷军投入大海中。
