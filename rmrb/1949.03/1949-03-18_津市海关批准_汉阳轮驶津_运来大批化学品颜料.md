Title: Tianjin Customs Approves Hanyang Steamer to Tianjin Carrying Large Quantities of Chemicals and Pigments
Author:
Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 16th: The Hanyang steamer of the China Navigation Company arrived in Dagu on the 14th from Hong Kong, carrying 550 tons of chemical raw materials, machinery, and vehicle tires. The Tianjin Customs has approved the vessel's customs declaration for import. The vessel is represented in Tianjin by the Tanggu Foreign Firm. In addition, the Tianjin and Longsheng No. 1 steamers, represented by the Fushen Firm, arrived in Tianjin from Dalian on the afternoon of the 14th. The Tianjin carried 45,000 kilograms of roasted glue, glass, chemicals, and salted fish.

### 津市海关批准　汉阳轮驶津　运来大批化学品颜料

1949-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　津市海关批准 　 汉阳轮驶津
  　运来大批化学品颜料
    【新华社天津十六日电】中国航业公司的汉阳轮，于十四日由香港驶抵大沽，运来化学品原料、机器及车带等五百五十吨。津市海关已批准该轮报关入口。该轮在津由塘沽洋行代理。另由复申行代理的天津号和隆盛一号两轮，于十四日下午由大连驶抵天津。天津号运来烤胶、玻璃、化学品及咸鱼四万五千公斤。
