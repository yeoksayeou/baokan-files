Title: Burmese Striking Workers Stage Another Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1949-03-05
Page: 3
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, North Shaanxi, March 3rd: Rangoon dispatch: The strike by Burmese civil servants, protesting against salary reductions and layoffs, has persisted for more than three weeks. According to an Associated Press dispatch from February 28th, the striking workers staged another demonstration against the Thakin Nu government on the 28th, protesting against the authorities' trick of trying to win over Indian civil servants. Demonstrators filled the streets, blocking traffic. They gathered around government offices, calling on all employees to join the strike. According to the Associated Press, under official deception, Indian civil servants have already returned to work.

### 缅甸坚持罢工职工再次示威游行

1949-03-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　缅甸坚持罢工职工再次示威游行
    【新华社陕北三日电】仰光讯：缅甸公务员为抗议减薪裁员而举行的罢工，已坚持了三周以上。据美联社二月二十八日讯，罢工中的职工，二十八日又举行反对达钦努政府的示威游行，抗议当局采取笼络印籍公务员的诡计。示威者挤满街道，交通为之阻塞。他们齐集政府机关四周，号召全体职工出来参加罢工。据美联社称，在官方欺骗下，印籍公务员业已复工。
