Title: Workers are Busy
Author: Li Jincai
Date: 1949-03-23
Page: 4

Workers are busy
(Worker Li Jincai of the Pinghan Railway)

　　Workers are busy, workers are busy,
　　United we have strength.
　　The liberation of the whole country is not far away,
　　Developing transportation requires even more effort.
　　Thinking of the reactionaries back then,
　　One can't help but shed tears.
　　The reactionaries, devoid of conscience,
　　Don't care whether the workers live or die,
　　Cut wages by seventy or eighty percent.
　　Workers don't even have enough to eat and wear,
　　While corrupt officials buy gold by the tael.

　　×　　　×　　　×

　　Comrades, how happy,
　　It was so hard to finally be liberated!
　　We are now masters of our own house,
　　We should work hard, no matter how busy.
　　Repair railways, build bridges,
　　Work hard to repair vehicles.
　　Comrades, full of joy,
　　Happily go to work.

### 工人忙
李进才
1949-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　工人忙
    （平汉铁路工人李进才）
    工人忙，工人忙，
    团结起来有力量。
    解放全国已不远，
    发展交通更要忙。
    想起当年反动派，
    不由得人泪汪汪。
    反动派，丧天良，
    不管工人死与活，
    七折八扣发薪饷。
    工人吃穿都不够，
    贪官一买黄金多少两。
　　×　　　×　　　×
    工友们，好喜欢，
    好容易才解放！
    自己当了家，
    再忙也应当。
    修铁路，架桥梁，
    努力加工修车辆。
    工友们，喜洋洋，
    欢欢喜喜把工上。
