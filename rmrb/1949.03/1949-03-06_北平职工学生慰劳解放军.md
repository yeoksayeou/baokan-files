Title: Beiping Workers and Students Comfort the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1949-03-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, March 5th. The comfort groups formed by worker and student organizations in Beiping Municipality, carrying banners, gifts, and comfort letters, have recently been visiting various units of the Northeast Field Army stationed in and around Beiping to offer their regards. The comfort groups and the various units held various get-togethers and symposiums. At the meetings, all the soldiers expressed that they would respond to the people's regards by accelerating the completion of training tasks. Worker and student representatives told the People's Liberation Army that they were determined to take practical actions to actively increase production, study hard, and support the front lines.

### 北平职工学生慰劳解放军

1949-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平职工学生慰劳解放军
    【新华社北平五日电】平市职工、学生团体所组成的慰问团，携带锦旗及慰劳品、慰劳信，近日纷纷前往驻防北平及附近的东北野战军各部进行慰问。慰问团和各部队举行了各种联欢会、座谈会。会上，全体战士表示将以加紧完成整训任务来回答人民的慰问。职工学生代表向解放军表示，决以实际行动积极增加生产，努力学习，支援前线。
