Title: National Student Congress Opens in Beiping; CCP Central Committee Representative General Ye Jianying Attends and Offers Congratulations

Author:
Date: 1949-03-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 2nd. The 14th National Student Congress of the All-China Students' Federation solemnly opened at 1:30 PM on the 1st in Beiping, the birthplace of the Chinese student movement. A total of 204 student representatives from various regions attended this congress, representing 1,020,000 students from the Chinese Liberated Areas and the Kuomintang-controlled areas. Five representatives from the Liberated Areas Youth Federation attended as observers. Guests invited to the meeting included CCP Central Committee representative General Ye Jianying, Chinese People's Liberation Army representative General Luo Ronghuan, CCP Beiping Municipal Committee representative Zhao Yimin, All-China Federation of Trade Unions representative Xu Zhifu, Preparatory Committee of the All-China Women's Federation representative Li Dequan, China Liberated Areas Youth Federation representative Feng Wenbin, and democratic figures such as Li Jishen, Shen Junru (represented by Sha Qianli), Ma Xulun, Guo Moruo, Xu Deheng, and others. The guests expressed warm congratulations and encouragement on the opening of the congress in their speeches. When the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China was read, the audience erupted in cheers, shouting slogans such as "Long live the Communist Party of China!" and "Wishing Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu good health!" which lasted for several minutes. After the presentation of messages from student representatives from Beiping, North China, Northeast, Northwest, East China, Central Plains, and Kuomintang-controlled areas such as Shanghai, Hangzhou, Suzhou, Nanjing, Guangzhou, and Wuhan, the congress adjourned. Afterwards, the China Liberated Areas Youth Federation hosted a banquet for all student representatives and held an entertainment evening.

### 全国学代大会在平揭幕  中共中央代表叶剑英将军亲临致贺

1949-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国学代大会在平揭幕
    中共中央代表叶剑英将军亲临致贺
    【新华社北平二日电】中华全国学生第十四届代表大会，于一日下午一时半在中国学生运动发祥地的北平隆重揭幕。出席这次大会的各地学生代表共有二百零四人，他们代表了中国解放区和国民党统治区的一百零二万学生。列席大会的有解放区青年联合会的代表五人。被邀到会的来宾中有中共中央代表叶剑英将军、中国人民解放军代表罗荣桓将军、中共北平市委代表赵毅敏、中华全国总工会代表许之福、中华全国妇女联合会筹委会代表李德全、中国解放区青年联合会代表冯文彬、各方民主人士李济深、沈钧儒（沙千里代）、马叙伦、郭沫若、许德珩等。来宾在讲话中对大会的开幕都表示了热烈的祝贺和鼓励。大会在宣读中国共产党中央委员会的贺电时，全场欢声雷动高呼“中国共产党万岁！”“祝毛主席、朱总司令健康！”等口号，延续数分钟之久。大会在北平、华北、东北、西北、华东、中原和国民党统治区上海、杭州、苏州、南京、广州、武汉等地区的学生代表献辞后，宣告休会。会后，中国解放区青年联合会设宴招待全体学生代表并举行娱乐晚会。
