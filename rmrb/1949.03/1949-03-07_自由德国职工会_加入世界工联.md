Title: Free German Trade Union Federation Joins World Federation of Trade Unions
Author: 
Date: 1949-03-07
Page: 3

　　Free German Trade Union Federation
  　Joins World Federation of Trade Unions
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 5th] TASS Berlin: The Executive Committee of the Free German Trade Union Federation in the Soviet-occupied zone of Germany announced on February 27th that the Federation had received a notification from World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary Saillant stating that the World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Committee had decided to accept the German trade union as a member of the World Federation of Trade Unions from January 1, 1949. Meetings have been held in various enterprises in Berlin and the Soviet-occupied zone of Germany for this purpose. The working people warmly welcome the decision of the World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Committee to accept the German trade union into the World Federation of Trade Unions organization.

### 自由德国职工会　加入世界工联

1949-03-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　自由德国职工会
  　加入世界工联
    【新华社陕北五日电】塔斯社柏林讯：德境苏占区自由德国职工会执行委员会于二月二十七日宣布，该会已接获世界职工联盟总书记赛扬的通知称，世界工联执行委员会已决定从一九四九年一月一日起，接受德国的职工会为世界工联会员。柏林与德境苏占区的各企业，已为此举行会议。劳动人民热烈地欢迎世界工联执委会接受德国的职工会加入世界职工会组织的决定。
