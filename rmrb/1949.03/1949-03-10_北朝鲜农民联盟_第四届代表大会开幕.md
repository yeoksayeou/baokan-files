Title: North Korean Farmers' Alliance Fourth Congress Opens
Author:
Date: 1949-03-10
Page: 3

　　North Korean Farmers' Alliance
  　Fourth Congress Opens
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 8th] TASS, Pyongyang, 5th: The Fourth Congress of the North Korean Farmers' Alliance opened at the Pyongyang National Theater on March 5th, with 300 representatives attending. The congress will summarize the achievements of the past three years since the implementation of land reform in North Korea, and will also revise the charter of the Farmers' Alliance.

### 北朝鲜农民联盟　第四届代表大会开幕

1949-03-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜农民联盟
  　第四届代表大会开幕
    【新华社陕北八日电】塔斯社平壤五日讯；北朝鲜农民联盟第四届代表大会于三月五日在平壤国立剧院开幕，到会代表三百人。大会将总结北朝鲜实行土地改革以来三年中的成就，并将修改农民联盟的会章。
