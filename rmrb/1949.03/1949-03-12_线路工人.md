Title: Linemen
Author: Meng Guanchao
Date: 1949-03-12
Page: 4

　　Worker's Creative Writing

　　Linemen
 　　Shijiazhuang Electric Light Company Meng Guanchao
　　The violent wind howls and roars!
 　　The workers, with their tools, harness the vehicle,
 　　　　Not afraid of how strong the wind is,
 　　It cannot stop their high morale for work.
 　　The power supply section urgently sets up the lines,
 　　The laboratory prepares transformers to hang outside,
 　　Guaranteeing that the whole city can use electricity,
 　　Everyone strives to be a model.
 　　They all say: "In the past, we were treated like beasts of burden,
 　　Now we work for ourselves,
 　　Wholeheartedly following the Communist Party,
 　　The future is bright and there will be no mistake!"

### 线路工人
孟观潮
1949-03-12
第4版()
专栏：工人习作

　　线路工人
    石家庄电灯公司  孟观潮
    狂风刮的呼呼号！
     工人们带着工具把车套，
      不怕风有多么大，
     挡不住工作情绪高。
     供电科急忙把线架，
     试验室备好变压器往外挂，
     保证全市能用电，
     个个争先做模范。
     都说：“过去给人当牛马，
     现在工作为自家，
     一心跟着共产党，
     前途光明不会差！”
