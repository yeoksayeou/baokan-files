Title: Tianjin Women from All Walks of Life Actively Prepare to Commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day
Author:
Date: 1949-03-05
Page: 2

　　【Tianjin Electric News】Women from all walks of life in Tianjin are actively preparing to commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day. The Tianjin Municipal Committee for Commemorating "March 8th" International Women's Day and Preparing for the Election of Tianjin Delegates to the National Democratic Women's Congress has issued a notice deciding that on this day, factories, schools, government offices, organizations, and working women in various districts may organize women's mass meetings or forums separately or jointly on a voluntary basis, according to their specific circumstances. The committee will also hold forums for democratic women from all sectors and formulate methods for electing representatives to the Tianjin Municipal Democratic Women's Congress based on the opinions of all parties. Women worker representatives and family member representatives from various units of the Pingjin Railway Bureau, as well as female students from Nankai University, Hebei Provincial Normal School, Hebei Provincial Baoding Industrial and Commercial School, Furlong Middle School, and other schools, are holding meetings separately to discuss celebration methods.

### 天津市各界妇女　积极筹备纪念“三八”节

1949-03-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　天津市各界妇女
  　积极筹备纪念“三八”节
    【天津电】天津市各界妇女正积极筹备纪念“三八”节。天津市纪念“三八”国际妇女节及筹备选举天津市出席全国民主妇女代表大会筹委会，现已发出通知，决定是日各工厂、学校、机关、团体及各区劳动妇女，可根据具体情况，分别组织或自愿联合组织妇女群众会议或座谈会。该会并分别举行各界民主妇女座谈会，根据各方意见，制定选举天津市民主妇女代表会议之代表办法。平津铁路局各单位的妇女职工代表、家属代表及南开大学、河北省立师范、河北省立保定工商学校、扶轮中学等校女同学，正分别开会，筹商庆祝办法。
