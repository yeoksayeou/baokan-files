Title: Arrears of Flour Can Be Mediated
Author: Li Mingjun
Date: 1949-03-23
Page: 4

　　Regarding the matter of a certain grain store owing you flour, and now refusing to acknowledge or pay it, it is best to resolve this through mediation by the district government. If litigation is absolutely necessary, you can then file a lawsuit with the Beijing People's Court.

### 拖欠面粉可以调解

1949-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　拖欠面粉可以调解李铭君：
    关于某粮店欠你面粉，现在不予承认与支付一事，最好经区政府采取调解方式解决。如必须涉及诉讼，可再到北平市人民法院，提起诉讼。
