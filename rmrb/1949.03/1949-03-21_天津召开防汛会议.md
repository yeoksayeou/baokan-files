Title: Tianjin Holds Flood Prevention Meeting
Author:
Date: 1949-03-21
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 19th. The Tianjin Municipal People's Government's Bureau of Public Works convened a flood prevention meeting on the 7th. Representatives from the Military Control Commission's Water Conservancy Takeover Office and its affiliated units attended. The meeting determined six measures for flood prevention and river dredging. The Bureau of Public Works, the North China Water Conservancy General Bureau, and the former Hebei Provincial Water Conservancy Bureau will be responsible for carrying out all flood prevention and dredging projects, and it is stipulated that they be completed before the flood season in July and August this year.

### 天津召开防汛会议

1949-03-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　天津召开防汛会议
    【新华社天津十九日电】天津市人民政府工务局七日召开防汛会议。军事管制委员会水利接管处和所属各有关单位都派人参加。会议中决定了六项防汛和疏浚河道的办法，所有防汛疏浚工程由工务局、华北水利总局和前河北省水利局分别负责进行，并规定在今年七、八月间汛期以前完成。
