Title: Long-Distance Calls Between Beiping City and Various Places in Eastern Hebei

Author:

Date: 1949-03-23
Page: 1

　　Long-Distance Calls Between Beiping City and Various Places in Eastern Hebei

　　[This Newspaper] To facilitate communication for the people, the Beiping Telecommunications Bureau announces that long-distance telephone calls between Beiping and various places in the Eastern Hebei region have been restored since 00:00 on the 16th of this month. These places include Lutai, Xugezhuang, Kaiping, Luanxian, Beidaihe, Anshan, Qinhuangdao, Beidaihe Seaside, and Shanhaiguan, a total of nine locations.

### 平市与冀东各地长途通话

1949-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　平市与冀东各地
    长途通话
    【本报讯】北平电信局为便利人民通讯起见，北平与冀东区各地长途电话自本月十六日零时起，均已恢复通话。计有芦台、胥各庄、开平、滦县、北戴河、安山、秦皇岛、北戴河海滨、山海关等九处。
