Title: Over 20,000 Women Gather in Beiping to Commemorate International Women's Day and Celebrate Liberation; Women in Factories and Schools in Tianjin Hold Separate Symposia
Author:
Date: 1949-03-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, March 9th: Over 20,000 women from all walks of life in Beiping City held a grand meeting yesterday in front of the Hall of Supreme Harmony in the Forbidden City to commemorate International Women's Day. More than sixty units, including female worker and student groups in the city, as well as laboring women's groups from Changxindian, Mentougou, and Qinghezhen in the suburbs, gathered at the assembly hall in the Forbidden City via the Meridian Gate, East Prosperity Gate, and West Prosperity Gate starting at 7:00 AM. The rostrum was set up in the Hall of Supreme Harmony. A huge oil portrait of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu hung high on the platform, and fourteen large red flags fluttered in front of the platform. The square on both sides of the Imperial River was filled with over 20,000 liberated women. The commemoration meeting began at 11:00 AM. After Chairman Zhang Xiaomei delivered the opening remarks, the presidium read out congratulatory messages to the meeting from the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee and the Beiping Student Union. This was followed by speeches from guests and representatives of women from all walks of life. Tao Zhu, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the People's Liberation Army on the Pingjin Front; Ye Jianying, Director of the Beiping Military Control Commission and Mayor of the city; Xiao Xiangrong, Minister of Propaganda of the Political Department of the People's Liberation Army on the Pingjin Front; and Zhao Yimin, representative of the Beiping Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke. Subsequently, Zhang Xiuyan, representative of the Women's Work Committee of the Beiping Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Li Dequan, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Wang Yongping, representative of female workers in Beiping; and Lin Yueqin, representative of revolutionary military families, also gave speeches. After presenting flags to the People's Liberation Army and the Beiping Municipal People's Government, the conference adopted telegrams to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, women compatriots in the liberated areas, and women compatriots in the Kuomintang-controlled areas. In the evening, various cultural and art troupes of the People's Liberation Army performed programs such as "Liberation of Peasant Women" in six theaters to entertain women from all walks of life.

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, March 9th: When women in Tianjin are commemorating the first International Women's Day after liberation, they are also attending the opening of the Tianjin Women's Congress. At the meeting, attended by 189 women representatives, Mayor Huang Jing, Li Jiebo, Director of the Tianjin Office of the Preparatory Committee of the North China Federation of Trade Unions, Yang Gang, member of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and representatives of female workers from the Telecommunications Bureau, No. 4 Cotton Mill, and China Match Factory, as well as representatives of female students from various universities in Jingu, spoke. On the same day, many female workers, female students, female staff, and female citizens also held celebrations and symposiums separately. Female workers from the Pingjin Clothing Factory and the Woolen Fabric Factory traveled from twenty miles away to participate in the celebration meeting of the twenty-one factories under the Tianjin Factory Management Office of the Pingjin Clothing Factory, which was attended by 1,800 female workers. From the chairman of the conference to the discipline team, all were female workers. Female workers from the No. 2 Cotton Mill performed songs, dramas, and Yangko dances. More than 1,500 female students from more than 30 schools, including Nankai University, Yaohua, and Guangdong, held a joint evening party. In addition, female staff of the Pingjin Railway Bureau and the Jibei Electric Power Company also gathered to celebrate. The symposium convened by the No. 9 District People's Education Center to commemorate "March 8th" was attended by more than 100 women, many of whom recounted their suffering under the rule of the Kuomintang.

### 北平二万余妇女集会　纪念三八节庆祝解放  津各工厂学校妇女分别举行座谈会

1949-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平二万余妇女集会
  　纪念三八节庆祝解放
    津各工厂学校妇女分别举行座谈会
    【新华社北平九日电】北平市各界妇女二万余人，昨日在故宫太和殿前，举行盛大的纪念“三八”节大会。市内女工、女学生团体，及市郊长辛店、门头沟、清河镇等地的劳动妇女团体共六十余单位，自清晨七时起，即分别由午门、东华门、西华门集队进入紫禁城内的大会会场。主席台设在太和殿上。台上高悬着毛主席、朱总司令的巨大油彩像，台前飘扬着十四面大红旗。御河南北两侧的广场上站满了二万余获得了解放的妇女，纪念大会于上午十一时开始。主席张晓梅致开会词后，由主席团宣读了苏联妇女反法西斯委员会及北平学生联合会等致大会的贺电。接着是来宾及各界妇女代表讲话。平津前线解放军政治部副主任陶铸，北平市军管会主任兼市长叶剑英，平津前线解放军政治部宣传部长萧向荣，中国共产党北平市委会代表赵毅敏讲话以后，中国共产党北平市妇女工作委员会代表张秀岩，中华全国民主妇女联合会筹委会副主任李德全，平市女工代表王永萍，革命军人家属代表林月琴等相继讲话。大会在向人民解放军，北平市人民政府献旗后，通过了给毛主席、朱总司令，给解放区妇女同胞，给国民党统治区妇女同胞电文。当晚解放军各文工团分别在六个剧院演出“农妇解放”等节目，招待各界妇女。
    【新华社天津九日电】天津市妇女在纪念解放后的第一个“三八”节的时候，正逢天津市各界妇女代表大会的开幕。在有一百八十九个妇女代表出席的会议上，市长黄敬、华北总工会筹委会天津办事处主任李颉伯、全国民主妇联筹委会委员杨刚，和电讯局、中纺四厂、中华火柴厂等女工代表、津沽各大学女生代表等讲了话。在同一天，许多女工、女学生、女职员、女市民们还分别举行了庆祝会和座谈会。平津被服厂制呢厂女工们，从二十里以外赶去参加平津被服厂天津工厂管理处所属二十一个工厂女工的庆祝会，这个会上到有女工一千八百人。从大会主席到纠察队都由女工担任。中纺二厂女工们表演了歌咏、话剧和秧歌。南开大学、耀华、广东等三十余学校一千五百余女生举行了联欢晚会。其他，平津铁路局和冀北电力公司的女职员也分别集合庆祝。九区民教馆为纪念“三八”召集的座谈会，到有妇女百余人，许多人诉说了她们在国民党统治时代的痛苦。
