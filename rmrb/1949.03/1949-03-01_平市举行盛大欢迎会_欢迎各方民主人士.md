Title: Grand Welcome Rally Held in Beiping to Welcome Democratic Personages from All Quarters
Author:
Date: 1949-03-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 27th -- A grand rally to welcome democratic personages from all quarters was held in Beiping on the 26th. Democratic personages who came to Beiping via the Northeast, Tianjin, and Shijiazhuang, as well as those originally in Beiping, received a warm welcome. The welcomers were the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Pingjin Front, the Beiping Military Control Commission, the Beiping Municipal People's Government, and the Beiping Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party. This unprecedented grand meeting was held in the Zhongnanhai Auditorium, beginning at 2:00 PM and lasting for four and a half hours. First, Commander Lin Biao of the Pingjin Front and Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beiping Municipal Committee of the CPC, delivered welcoming speeches, followed by speeches from guests Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Ma Xulun, Guo Moruo, Tan Pingshan, Li Dequan, Zhang Bojun, Zhou Jianren, Zhu Xuejun, Zhu Huaqin, Zhang Naiqi, Zhang Xiruo, Xu Deheng, and Xiao Caiyu, a total of fourteen people. Finally, Dong Biwu, Chairman of the North China People's Government, and Ye Jianying, Director of the Beiping Military Control Commission, spoke. After the meeting, a grand banquet and entertainment were held to add to the festivities.

### 平市举行盛大欢迎会　欢迎各方民主人士

1949-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　平市举行盛大欢迎会
  　欢迎各方民主人士
    【新华社北平廿七日电】北平市二十六日举行了盛大的欢迎各方民主人士大会。经由东北、天津、石家庄来平及原在北平的民主人士，受到了热烈欢迎。主持欢迎者为中国人民解放军平津前线司令部和北平市军事管制委员会、北平市人民政府、中国共产党北平市委员会。这个空前的盛会举行于中南海大会堂，自下午二时开始，历时达四小时半。首由平津前线司令员林彪及中共北平市委员会书记彭真致欢迎词，继由来宾李济深、沈钧儒、马叙伦、郭沫若、谭平山、李德全、章伯钧、周建人、朱学筠、朱骅钦、张乃器、张奚若、许德珩、萧采瑜等十四人，相继发表演说。最后由华北人民政府主席董必武及北平市军事管制委员会主任叶剑英讲话。会后，并有盛大宴会及娱乐助兴。
