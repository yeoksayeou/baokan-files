Title: US May Day Joint Committee Proposes General Strike
Author:
Date: 1949-03-31
Page: 3

　　US May Day Joint Committee
    Proposes General Strike
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, March 30th] TASS, New York: The May Day Joint Committee conference, composed of nearly a thousand representatives (including 426 members of the AFL and CIO), agreed to emphasize slogans such as peace, economic security, and democratic rights during the upcoming May Day celebrations. The conference proposed taking protest action on April 4th against the North Atlantic Treaty and urged the CIO and AFL to call for a nationwide one-day strike to abolish the Taft-Hartley Act.

### 美五一节联合委员会　提议举行大罢工

1949-03-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　美五一节联合委员会
    提议举行大罢工
    【新华社北平三十日电】塔斯社纽约讯：将近一千代表（其中包括劳联与产联会员四百二十六人）组成的五一节联合委员会大会，同意在庆祝即将到来的五一节日会上强调和平、经济安全和民主权利等口号。大会提议于四月四日采取抗议行动，反对北大西洋公约，并敦促产联与劳联为取消塔夫脱——哈特莱法案，号召举行全国性的一日罢工。
