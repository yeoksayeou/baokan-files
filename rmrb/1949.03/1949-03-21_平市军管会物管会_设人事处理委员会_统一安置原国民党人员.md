Title: The Military Control Commission and Property Management Committee of Ping City Establish Personnel Handling Committee to Uniformly Resettle Former Kuomintang Personnel
Author:
Date: 1949-03-21
Page: 1

The Military Control Commission and Property Management Committee of Ping City

Establish Personnel Handling Committee

To Uniformly Resettle Former Kuomintang Personnel

[Reported by this newspaper] The Military Control Commission and Property Management Committee of Ping City, in order to uniformly resettle former Kuomintang personnel in various taken-over units, specially established a Personnel Handling Committee yesterday (the 20th), with Rong Zihe, the deputy director of the committee, and Tong Lusheng, the minister of the Military and Political Department, serving as the director and deputy director, respectively. The Personnel Handling Committee has officially started working. The committee and the "Beiping Joint Office" plan to quickly complete the resettlement of all former Kuomintang personnel in the various taken-over units of the committee before the end of this month. The committee has now notified all subordinate taken-over units to quickly compile a roster of former Kuomintang personnel, and, based on the committee's handling principles, put forward resettlement opinions as appropriate, and report them to the committee for review and then submit them to the "Beiping Joint Office" for management.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Zhu)

### 平市军管会物管会  设人事处理委员会  统一安置原国民党人员

1949-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　平市军管会物管会
    设人事处理委员会
  　统一安置原国民党人员
    【本报讯】平市军管会物管会为统一安置各接管单位的原国民党人员，特于昨日（二十日）成立人事处理委员会，由该会戎子和副主任及军政部童陆生部长分任正副主任。人事处理委员会业已正式办公。该会及“北平联合办事处”拟于本月底以前迅速将该会各接管单位原国民党人员全部安置完毕。现该会已通知所属各接管单位迅速具造原国民党人员名册，根据该会处理方针，酌情提出安置意见，报告该会审查后转报“北平联合办事处”管理。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（朱）
