Title: In Czech Economy, Socialist Sector Continuously Develops, Capitalist Sector Increasingly Weakens
Author:
Date: 1949-03-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 7th.
  
　　Czech Economy
    Socialist Sector Continuously Develops
    Capitalist Sector Increasingly Weakens
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 7th] Electroneews Agency Prague: The "Struggle" weekly recently published statistics illustrating the significance of Gottwald's speech at the Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee meeting in November 1948. At that time, Gottwald said: The socialist sector in the Czech economy is continuously developing, while the capitalist sector is increasingly weakening. The statistics show that before February 1948, the socialist sector employed 1,635,000 workers (35.4% of the total number of workers in the country), and after February, it employed 1,970,000 (40.3% of the total number of workers in the country); before February 1948, the socialist sector accounted for 50.3% of the national income, and after February, it accounted for 61.4%. The capitalist sector (excluding small businesses employing two people or fewer and farms smaller than 20 hectares): after February 1948, it employed 822,000 workers, while before February, it employed 1,096,000 workers (23.8% of the total number of workers in the country). In national income, the capitalist sector decreased from 24.7% to 13.5%. On the other hand, the statistics also show that the small business sector (including farms smaller than 20 hectares) is in a state of stagnation. Small businesses employ 40% to 41% of the total labor force, and their income accounts for 25% of the national income.

### 捷克经济中　社会主义部分不断发展  资本主义部分日益削弱

1949-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷克经济中
  　社会主义部分不断发展
    资本主义部分日益削弱
    【新华社陕北七日电】电通社布拉格讯：“斗争”周刊顷发表统计数字，说明一九四八年十一月哥特瓦尔德在捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会会议上所作演讲的意义。当时，哥特瓦尔德说：捷克经济中的社会主义部分正在不断地发展，而资本主义部分则日益削弱。统计数字表明：在一九四八年二月之前，社会主义部分雇用的工人为一百六十三万五千人（占全国工人总数的百分之三十五点四），二月之后，为一百九十七万人（占全国工人总数的百分之四十点三）；一九四八年二月以前，国民收入中的社会主义部分为百分之五十点三，而二月以后，为百分之六十一点四。资本主义部分（雇用二人以下的小企业与二十公顷以下的农场不包括在内）：一九四八年二月以后雇用工人数目为八十二万二千，而二月以前雇用的工人为一百零九万六千人（占全国工人总数百分之二十三点八）。国民收入中，资本主义部分，从百分之二十四点七减到百分之十三点五。另一方面，该统计数字又表明小企业部分（包括二十公顷以下的农场）处于停滞状态。小企业雇用工人占全体劳动人民总数的百分之四十到四十一，其收入占国民收入的百分之二十五。
