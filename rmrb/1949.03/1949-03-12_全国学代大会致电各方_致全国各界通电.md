Title: National Student Congress Sends Telegrams to All Parties: Telegram to All Sectors of the Country
Author:
Date: 1949-03-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th. The full text of the telegram from the 14th National Student Congress of the All-China Students' Federation to the whole country is as follows:

To the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, all democratic parties and people's organizations across the country, teachers across the country, and students across the country:

The 14th National Student Congress of the All-China Students' Federation opened in Beiping on March 1st and has now concluded. This is the first great gathering of students from all over China in more than 20 years, and it is the first completely free gathering in history. At this meeting, we decided on the tasks that our Chinese students should undertake in today's Chinese people's liberation struggle, and established a unified organization of all Chinese students including students in the liberated areas and the Kuomintang-controlled areas—the All-China Students' Federation, as the leading organ of the national student movement in the future.

When we realized this long-cherished wish, when we saw the surging tide of the people's democratic revolution developing vigorously across the country and approaching complete victory, we could not but express our respect and gratitude to the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong who have always led us forward, and we cannot but express our respect and gratitude to the victorious Chinese People's Liberation Army, we cannot but express our respect and gratitude to all democratic parties, all people's organizations, the teachers who have taught and cared for us, and the revolutionary students who are still fighting in the Kuomintang-controlled areas. We earnestly hope that you will give us more guidance and help in the future so that we can better contribute our share in the cause of the liberation of the Chinese people and the construction of the new democratic country.

The 14th National Student Congress of the All-China Students' Federation.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　March 5, 1949.

### 全国学代大会致电各方　致全国各界通电

1949-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国学代大会致电各方
  　致全国各界通电
    【新华社北平十日电】中华全国学生第十四届代表大会通电全国全文如下：
    中国共产党，中国人民解放军，全国各民主党派、各人民团体，全国的师长，全国的同学公鉴：
    中华全国学生第十四届代表大会于三月一日在北平开幕，现在已经结束了。这是二十多年来全中国学生第一次的大汇合，并且是历史上第一次完全自由的大汇合。我们在这个会上决定了我们中国学生在今天中国的人民解放斗争中应尽的任务，并且成立了包括解放区和国民党统治区内学生们的全中国学生的统一的组织——中华全国学生联合会，作为今后全国学生运动的领导机关。
    当我们实现了我们这个多年的愿望的时候，当我们看到人民民主革命高潮在全国蓬勃发展，并已迫近完全胜利的时候，我们不能不向始终领导我们前进的中国共产党和毛泽东主席表示我们的敬意和谢意，不能不向百战百胜的中国人民解放军表示我们的敬意和谢意，不能不向全国各民主党派，向全国各人民团体，向教诲我们和爱护我们的师长以及还在国民党统治区奋斗着的革命同学，表示我们的敬意和谢意。我们热烈地希望你们能够在今后给予我们更多的指导和帮助，使我们在中国人民解放事业和新民主主义国家建设事业中，能够更好地献出我们的一份力量。
    中华全国学生第十四届代表大会。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年三月五日。
