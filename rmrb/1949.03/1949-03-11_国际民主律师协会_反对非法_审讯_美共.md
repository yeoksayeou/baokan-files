Title: International Association of Democratic Lawyers Opposes Illegal "Trial" of the American Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1949-03-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 9th, Electone News Agency, New York: On the 6th, on the eve of the New York Federal Court's decision to "formally try" the leaders of the American Communist Party, a delegation from the International Association of Democratic Lawyers visited the United Nations Secretariat and requested that the proposal to "try" the American Communist Party as a violation of the Declaration of Human Rights be included in the agenda of the upcoming United Nations General Assembly. United Nations Deputy Secretary-General Lansa told the delegation that he intended to submit this request to the Human Rights Commission, which formulated the United Nations Declaration of Human Rights.

### 国际民主律师协会　反对非法“审讯”美共

1949-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际民主律师协会
  　反对非法“审讯”美共
    【新华社陕北九日电】电通社纽约讯：国际民主律师协会代表团在纽约联邦法院决定“正式审讯”美共领袖的前夕，于六日往访联合国秘书处，要求把“审讯”美共系违反人权宣言的提案，列入行将举行的联合国大会议程。联合国副秘书长朗瑟告代表团称：他拟将此项要求提交制定联合国人权宣言的人权委员会。
