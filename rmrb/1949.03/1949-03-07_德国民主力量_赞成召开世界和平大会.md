Title: Democratic Forces in Germany Support Convening a World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-03-07
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 5th—TASS, Berlin: German democratic forces welcome the suggestion made by the International Liaison Committee of the World Congress of Cultural Workers for Peace to convene a World Peace Congress in April. Konen, Secretary-General of the German People's Council, issued a statement saying: The German People's Congress supports this proposal. The Free German Trade Union's newspaper, *Forum*, welcomed the suggestion to convene a World Peace Congress, saying: The purpose of this congress is to unite the active forces of the people of all countries to defend peace.

### 德国民主力量　赞成召开世界和平大会

1949-03-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　德国民主力量
  　赞成召开世界和平大会
    【新华社陕北五日电】塔斯社柏林讯：对于世界文化工作者保卫和平大会国际联络委员会所提在四月间召开世界和平拥护大会的建议，德国民主力量表示欢迎。德国人民委员会总书记科南顷发表声明称：德国人民代表大会赞成这个建议。自由德国职工会的报纸论坛报，欢迎召开世界和平拥护者大会的建议说：这个大会的目的，是把一切国家人民的积极力量联合起来，保卫和平。
