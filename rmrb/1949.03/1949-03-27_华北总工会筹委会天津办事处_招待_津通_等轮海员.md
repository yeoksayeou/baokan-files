Title: Tianjin Office of the Preparatory Committee of the North China Federation of Trade Unions Hosts Seafarers of Vessels such as "Jin Tong"

Author:
Date: 1949-03-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 26th: The Tianjin Office of the Preparatory Committee of the North China Federation of Trade Unions held a tea party at the Municipal Government Reception Room at around 5 p.m. on the 25th to host Chinese seafarer comrades from vessels such as the "Jin Tong" that arrived in Tianjin. More than 80 seafarers attended. Huang Kecheng, Director of the Tianjin Military Control Commission, Mayor Huang Jing, Li Jiebo, Director of the Tianjin Office of the Preparatory Committee of the North China Federation of Trade Unions, Qiu Jin, Deputy Director, and heads of the organization, propaganda, welfare and other departments of the office were present. At the meeting, Mayor Huang Jing introduced the situation of Tianjin and the liberated areas, and then Director Li Jiebo delivered a speech, expressing welcome and regards to the seafarers who attended.

### 华北总工会筹委会天津办事处  招待“津通”等轮海员

1949-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北总工会筹委会天津办事处
    招待“津通”等轮海员
    【新华社天津二十六日电】二十五日下午五时许，华北总工会筹委会天津办事处假市府交际处举行茶会，招待抵津之“津通”等轮中国海员工友。到会海员八十余人，本市军管会主任黄克诚、市长黄敬、华北总工会筹委会天津办事处主任李颉伯、副主任丘金及该处组织、宣传、福利等部负责人均与会。会上黄敬市长介绍津市和解放区情况后即由李颉伯主任致词，对到会海员表示欢迎与慰问。
