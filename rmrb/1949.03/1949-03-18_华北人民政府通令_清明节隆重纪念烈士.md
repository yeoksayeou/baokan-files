Title: Notice from the North China People's Government: Solemnly Commemorate the Martyrs on Qingming Festival
Author: 
Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 1

　　North China People's Government Notice
    Solemnly Commemorate the Martyrs on Qingming Festival
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th. In order to commemorate the martyrs who heroically sacrificed themselves for the people's liberation cause in various regions, the North China People's Government issued a notice to the provincial governments, administrative offices, and directly-administered municipal governments on March 7th, informing all regions to hold martyrs' memorial meetings on Qingming Festival (i.e., April 5th), conduct tomb sweeping, add the names of the martyrs to the martyrs' monuments, and plant more trees in the martyrs' cemeteries. The notice also stated: This is the first martyrs' memorial festival after the basic liberation of North China, and all regions should solemnly commemorate it to raise the political consciousness of the people and learn from the revolutionary spirit of the martyrs.

### 华北人民政府通令  清明节隆重纪念烈士

1949-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府通令
    清明节隆重纪念烈士
    【新华社北平十六日电】为纪念各地为人民解放事业而英勇牺牲的烈士，华北人民政府于三月七日向各省政府行政公署及直辖市市政府发出通令，通知各地于清明节（即四月五日）举行烈士纪念会，进行扫墓，并在烈士碑上加刻烈士姓名，各烈士陵园中增植树木。通令并称：此为华北基本上已解放后的第一个烈士纪念节，各地应郑重纪念，以提高人民群众的政治觉悟，学习烈士的革命精神。
