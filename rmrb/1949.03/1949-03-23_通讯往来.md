Title: Correspondence

Author:

Date: 1949-03-23
Page: 1

　　Correspondence

　　(1) To responsible comrades of youth and women's mass organizations and student unions of major universities and middle schools in Beiping:

　　Please promptly organize reports on your recent work and the preparations for establishing the New Democratic Youth League, as well as the dynamics of youth and women's work. We urge you to supervise your original correspondents and correspondence groups to quickly contact our agency and help establish new correspondence organizations.

　　(2) To our correspondents in various regions: If you have been transferred from your original work posts to cities such as Beiping, Tianjin, Zhangjiakou, Baoding, Tangshan, or other nearby areas, please immediately inform us of your current mailing address so that we can contact you. Also, please report on your recent work situation.

　　(3) To all factory trade unions, correspondents, and workers:

　　In recent days, our agency has received a number of materials regarding worker training classes, spare-time schools, study groups, and other worker learning activities. These valuable materials have been of great help to us. Therefore, we hope you will quickly and more widely send us such materials to promote worker learning among others.

　　In your reports, please pay attention to reporting on the experiences in learning, as well as the changes in workers' work and thinking after learning. Please organize specific reports.

　　To all correspondents:

　　From now on, please do not affix postage stamps to manuscripts, mimeographed materials, etc., sent to our agency. Please see the notice on page one of this newspaper on the 21st and 22nd for specific regulations. When sending manuscripts in the future, simply write "Manuscript" on the envelope. Please correspond with us more often.

　　Comrade He Qifang: We have a letter for you. Please come and collect it.

　　To Comrade Yu Zeren of the Correspondence Section of our agency:

　　We have received the manuscript you sent. Some issues need to be studied. Please come to our newspaper for a face-to-face discussion between 3:00 PM and 5:00 PM today (the 23rd).

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　People's Daily Editorial Office

### 通讯往来

1949-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　通讯往来
            （一）北平市各青年、妇女等群众团体及各大中学校学生会负责同志：
    请将你处最近工作情况及筹备建立新民主主义青年团之情形、青年妇女工作动态等，抓紧组织报导。更希望督促你处原有之本社通讯员同志及通讯小组，迅速和本社联系，并帮助建立新的通讯组织。
    　　　　（二）本报各地通讯员同志们：你们有从原来的工作岗位调到平、津、张、保、唐等城市，或其他附近地区来的，希望立即来信告知你们现在的通讯地址，以便进行联系；并请将你处最近的工作情况进行报导。
    　　　　（三）各工厂工会、通讯员及全体工人同志们：
    本社近几天一连接到不少的职工训练班、业余学校、学习小组等职工学习的材料，这些珍贵的材料给本社帮助很大，因此，希望你们迅速而且更广泛的寄给我们这样的材料，以便推动其他职工学习。
    在报导中要注意学习中经验的报导，及经过学习后，职工在工作中、思想上的变化，请具体的组织报导。全体通讯员同志：
    今后寄本社的稿件，油印品等，均不贴邮票，具体规定请看本报二十一、二十二日一版上的通知，今后寄稿时在信封上写“稿件”二字即可，请多来信联系。何其芳同志：本科存有你信一封，请来取。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本社通联科余则人君：
    你寄来的稿件收到，有些问题需要研究一下，请于今日（二十三日）下午三时至五时到本报面叙为盼。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　人民日报总编室
