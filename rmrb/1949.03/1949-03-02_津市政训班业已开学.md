Title: Tianjin Municipal Political Training Class Has Already Begun
Author:
Date: 1949-03-02
Page: 1

　　Tianjin Municipal Political Training Class Has Already Begun
    [Tianjin Electric] In order to cultivate a large number of cadres, the political training class organized by the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China has started on the 20th.
