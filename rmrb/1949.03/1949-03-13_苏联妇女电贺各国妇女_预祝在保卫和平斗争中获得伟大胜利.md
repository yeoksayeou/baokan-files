Title: Soviet Women Send Greetings to Women of All Countries, Wishing Them Great Victory in the Struggle to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1949-03-13
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 10th: TASS, Moscow: The Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee sent a congratulatory telegram to the Women's International Democratic Federation on International Women's Day, stating: "On this International Women's Day, we extend our sincere greetings to all democratic women around the world through you. We wish you great victory in the struggle to win world peace and oppose the instigators of new wars. Long live the Women's International Democratic Federation - the motivator and organizer of the struggle for peace and democracy by progressive women!"
    The Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee also sent congratulatory messages to democratic women's organizations in various countries. The telegram to the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation stated: The Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, on behalf of millions of Soviet women, extends its congratulations to the democratic women of China and hopes for their victory in the struggle to win the happiness of the Chinese people. The committee also sent a telegram to the Korean Women's Democratic League, extending congratulations to all women in North and South Korea, and hoping for their victory in the struggle to consolidate the democratic reforms of Korea, in the struggle against reactionary forces and against foreign imperialists. The telegram to the Mongolian People's Republic Labor Women's Organization Committee expressed a keen expectation for the victorious completion and exceeding of the Five-Year Plan for the economic and cultural development of the Mongolian People's Republic. The telegram to Vietnamese women stated: "Soviet women are eagerly hoping for your victory in the struggle to establish a free and independent country, in the struggle for a peaceful, secure, and happy new life." The Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee also sent telegrams of congratulations to female members of the All-India Trade Union Congress, the All-Burma Women's Conference, and women's organizations in Africa, Australia, and the Americas.

### 苏联妇女电贺各国妇女  预祝在保卫和平斗争中获得伟大胜利

1949-03-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联妇女电贺各国妇女
    预祝在保卫和平斗争中获得伟大胜利
    【新华社陕北十日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联妇女反法西斯委员会，在国际妇女节向国际民主妇联致电祝贺称：“际此国际妇女节日，我们经由你们向全世界一切民主妇女，致热诚的贺忱。祝你们在争取全世界和平与反对新战争挑拨者的斗争中，获得伟大的胜利。国际民主妇联——进步妇女争取和平民主斗争的鼓动者与组织者万岁！”
    苏联妇女反法西斯委员会同时又分别致电各国民主妇女团体致祝贺之忱。致中国全国民主妇女联合会筹委会的贺电中略称：苏联妇女反法西斯委员会特代表苏联千百万妇女向中国民主妇女致贺，并期望她们在争取中国人民的幸福的斗争中胜利。该委员会并致电朝鲜妇女民主联盟，向北朝鲜及南朝鲜全体妇女致贺，并期望她们在巩固朝鲜民主改革的斗争中、在反对反动势力及反对外国帝国主义者的斗争中胜利。致蒙古共和国劳动妇女组织委员会的贺电中，表示极热切地期望胜利完成并超过蒙古共和国经济及文化发展五年计划。致越南妇女的贺电中称：“苏联妇女极热切地期望你们在争取建立自由与独立国家的斗争中，在争取和平与安全而愉快的新生活的斗争中胜利。”苏联妇女反法西斯委员会并致电祝贺印度全国职工大会女会员、缅甸全国妇女大会、非洲、澳洲及美洲各国妇女团体祝贺。
