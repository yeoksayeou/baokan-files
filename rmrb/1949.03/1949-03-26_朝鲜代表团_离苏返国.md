Title: Korean Delegation Departs Soviet Union for Home

Author:

Date: 1949-03-26
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 23rd: TASS Moscow dispatch: On March 20th, the Korean government delegation headed by Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, left Moscow for Leningrad, and then returned home. Those who went to the station to see them off included Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, Foreign Minister Vyshinsky, and Minister of Foreign Trade, Menshikov. Personnel from the Korean Embassy in the Soviet Union, headed by Korean Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Zhu Ninghe, as well as embassy staff and delegations from several countries stationed in Moscow, also went to the station to see them off. An honor guard was lined up at the station, the national anthems of Korea and the Soviet Union were played, and the Korean flag was displayed.

### 朝鲜代表团　离苏返国

1949-03-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜代表团
  　离苏返国
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：三月二十日，以朝鲜人民共和国总理金日成为首的朝鲜政府代表团离开莫斯科赴列宁格勒，然后转道返国。赴车站欢送者有苏联部长会议副主席米高扬、外交部长维辛斯基、对外贸易部长孟希科夫等。以朝鲜驻苏特命全权大使朱宁河，为首的朝鲜驻苏大使馆人员与驻莫斯科之若干国家大使馆及代表团亦到车站欢送。车站上排列仪仗队，演奏朝鲜与苏联两国国歌并悬挂朝鲜国旗。
