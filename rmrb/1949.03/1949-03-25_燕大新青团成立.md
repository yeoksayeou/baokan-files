Title: Yenching University New Youth League Established
Author:
Date: 1949-03-25
Page: 2

　　Yenching University New Youth League Established
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Yenching University General Branch of the New Democracy Youth League of China held its founding meeting last night (the 24th). One hundred and thirty-two students, staff, and workers gloriously swore to join the League. Representatives from the Teaching Assistant Association, the Teaching Assistant and Staff Women's Federation, the Trade Union, the Student Self-Government Association, and the Tsinghua University General Branch of the New Youth League all offered their congratulations and encouraged the League members to fulfill their great future tasks. The Yenching Teaching Assistant Association, the Trade Union, the Staff and Workers School Self-Government Association, and the Student Self-Government Association all presented flags to the meeting. One hundred and thirty-two League members swore to serve the working people loyally.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Yenching University Correspondent Group)

### 燕大新青团成立

1949-03-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　燕大新青团成立
    【本报讯】中关新民主主义青年团燕京大学总支部昨（二十四日）晚举行成立大会。一百三十二位同学、职员、工友光荣的宣誓入团。讲助械、讲助职妇女联合会、工会、学生自治会、新青团清华总支部代表，均一致祝贺，并勉励团员完成未来的伟大任务。燕京讲助职联、工会、职工学校自治会、学生自治会并分别向大会献旗。一百三十二位团员宣誓为劳动人民忠诚服务。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（燕京大学通讯小组）
