Title: Preparatory Committee of the Ping Mail Workers' Union Established, Actively Seeking Welfare for Workers
Author:
Date: 1949-03-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 26th: The work of postal workers in Ping City is unfolding. The Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Municipal Federation of Trade Unions has designated 15 committee members (nine of whom are postal workers) to establish a new preparatory committee for the postal workers' union to carry out organizational, cultural and educational, and labor insurance work. To help postal workers study, the library has added many new books and newspapers and magazines and established a reading room. At the request of the workers, rotating study classes have been set up, with 120 people in each class, for a period of seven days. The third session has now begun. The workers have published their own newspaper - the New Postal Worker Small Newspaper, and organized a Yangko team, a chorus team, and a postal worker theater troupe. The Labor Insurance Department assisted the workers in establishing a cooperative, purchasing more than 60,000 jin of white flour, more than 10,000 jin of corn, more than 10,000 jin of sorghum, and more than 10,000 jin of millet for all employees within three days, ensuring the actual wages of the employees. The workers have put forward many suggestions for reforming all the unreasonable old systems left over. The preparatory committee has received more than 200 letters of suggestion.

### 平邮政工会筹委会成立　积极为工人谋福利

1949-03-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　平邮政工会筹委会成立
  　积极为工人谋福利
    【新华社北平二十六日电】平市邮政职工工作正在展开。北平市总工会筹委会已指定筹委十五人（内有九个是邮政工人）成立新的邮政工会筹委会，进行组织、文教、劳动保险等工作。为帮助邮工学习，图书馆已添买许多新的书报杂志，并成立了阅览室。在职工的要求下，轮流学习班等已设立，每班一百二十人，七天为一期，现已开始第三期。工人们已出版了自己的报纸——新邮工小报，组织了秧歌队、歌咏队和邮工剧团。劳动保险部则协助职工们成立了合作社，三天内替全体职工买进了六万多斤白面，一万多斤玉米，一万多斤高粱和一万多斤小米，保证了职工实际工资的所得。职工们对遗留下来的一切不合理的旧制度提出许多改革的意见。筹委会已收到二百多封建议信。
