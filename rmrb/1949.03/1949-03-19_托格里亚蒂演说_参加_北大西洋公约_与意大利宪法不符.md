Title: Togliatti Speech: Participation in the "North Atlantic Treaty" is Incompatible with the Italian Constitution
Author:
Date: 1949-03-19
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 18th. TASS Rome dispatch: Italian Communist Party leader Togliatti delivered a speech in parliament on the 15th, stating that participation in the Atlantic Pact is incompatible with the Italian constitution, which prohibits all military alliances. During the election held on April 18th last year, the Christian Democratic Party solemnly assured voters that it would not impose military terms on Italy and would not involve the country in military blocs. Togliatti said to the government: "You should know that we do not recognize the measures you intend to take. We will tell the Italian people that they have the right to reject this decision." After pointing out that millions of Italian people will refuse to wage an anti-Soviet war, Togliatti said: "The task of the Communist Party today is to avoid that war and save the world! We will see how those who abandon your war policies will unite into a great peace front. It is for this reason that we say to the North Atlantic Treaty: 'We oppose it!' To the international conspiracy we say: 'We oppose it!' To the Italian government's policy of aggression against the Soviet Union we say: 'We oppose it!'"

### 托格里亚蒂演说  参加“北大西洋公约”与意大利宪法不符

1949-03-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　托格里亚蒂演说
    参加“北大西洋公约”与意大利宪法不符
    【新华社陕北十八日电】塔斯社罗马讯：意大利共产党领袖托格里亚蒂十五日在议会中发表演说称：参加大西洋公约是和禁止一切军事联盟的意大利宪法不相符合的。在去年四月十八日举行选举的时候，基督教民主党曾庄严地向选民保证过：不把军事条款强加于意大利，不把国家卷入军事集团。托格里亚蒂向政府说：“你们应该知道，你们所打算采取的措施，我们是不承认的。我们将告诉意大利人民说，他们有权抛弃这个决定。”托里格亚蒂在指出意大利千百万人民将拒绝进行反苏战争以后说：“共产党今天的任务就是避免那个战争和挽救世界！我们将要看见，抛弃你们的战争政策的人们怎样团结成伟大的和平阵线。就是由于这种理由，我们向北大西洋公约说：‘我们反对！’向国际阴谋说：‘我们反对！’向意大利政府对苏联进行侵略的政策说：‘我们反对！’”
