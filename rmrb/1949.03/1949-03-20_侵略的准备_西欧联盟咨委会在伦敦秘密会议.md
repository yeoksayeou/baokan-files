Title: Preparations for Aggression: Western European Union Consultative Council Holds Secret Meeting in London
Author:
Date: 1949-03-20
Page: 3

　　Preparations for Aggression
  　Western European Union Consultative Council Holds Secret Meeting in London
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 18th—Reports from various sources: The supreme organ of the Western European Union—the Western European Union Consultative Council, the aggressive tool of the US-British bloc in Europe—held a secret meeting in Lancaster House, London, from the 14th to the 15th. The foreign ministers, defense ministers, and finance ministers of the five countries of Britain, France, the Netherlands, Belgium, and Luxembourg participated in the meeting. According to Agence France-Presse, the council issued a communiqué after two days of deliberation, stating that the meeting discussed the recent developments of the North Atlantic Treaty: discussed all aspects of the so-called "joint defense issues" of the five countries; discussed further measures "in terms of military supplies and equipment production and funding"; and discussed the issue of the so-called "European Committee." The organization of this European Committee was proposed at the January meeting of the Western European Union Consultative Council; the purpose of this organization is to serve as a political concoction of the Western European Union. The Western European Union Consultative Council agreed this time: It is recommended that the diplomatic representatives of the ten countries of Britain, France, the Netherlands, Belgium, Luxembourg, Italy, Norway, Denmark, Sweden, and Ireland hold a meeting in London on March 28, followed by a ministerial meeting of the ten countries in April, to "draft and approve the final articles of the European Committee's constitution."

### 侵略的准备　西欧联盟咨委会在伦敦秘密会议

1949-03-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　侵略的准备
  　西欧联盟咨委会在伦敦秘密会议
    【新华社陕北十八日电】各方报道：美英集团在欧洲的侵略工具西欧联盟的最高机关—西欧联盟咨询委员会的秘密会议，于十四至十五日在伦敦兰开斯特娣厦举行。参加此次会议者，有英、法、荷、比、卢五国的外交部长、国防部长及财政部长。据法新社消息，该会在集议两日之后发表公报称：会议曾讨论了北大西洋公约最近的发展：讨论了五国所谓“联防问题”的各个方面；讨论了“在军事供应和装备的生产与经费方面”之进一步的措施；并讨论了所谓“欧洲委员会”的问题。此欧洲委员会之组织，系西欧联盟咨询委员会一月会会议上所建议；这个组织的目的，系作为西欧联盟在政治上的酌合物。本次西欧联盟咨询委员会协议：建议英、法、荷、比、卢、意、挪、丹、瑞典、爱尔兰十个国家的外交代表，先后三月二十八日在伦敦举行会议，再于四月份举行十个国家的部长会议，以“草拟和批准欧洲委员会章程的最后条文”。
