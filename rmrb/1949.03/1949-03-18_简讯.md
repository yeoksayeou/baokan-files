Title: Brief News

Author:

Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 2

　　Brief News
    [Taiyue Telegram] Cadres and support staff of the Taiyue Military Region are using their spare time to engage in production to earn money to buy stationery. Some sprout bean sprouts, reclaim wasteland to plant garlic, and some carry coal. In addition to making tofu, the cooking squad also does small carpentry work such as nailing tubs, earning 300 yuan at a time. Now everyone's school supplies have been resolved. Li Xinhai and others bought five fountain pens, and the cooking squad also collectively subscribed to a newspaper for active learning.

### 简讯

1949-03-18
第2版()
专栏：简讯

　　简讯
    【太岳电】太岳军区机关干部和勤杂人员，抽业余时间，进行生产，赚钱买文具。有的生豆芽、开荒种蒜，有的担炭，炊事班除了做豆腐，还做钉缸等小木匠活，一次就赚人民币三百元。现在各人学习用品已经解决。李新孩等买了水笔五支，炊事班还集体订了一份报纸，积极学习。
