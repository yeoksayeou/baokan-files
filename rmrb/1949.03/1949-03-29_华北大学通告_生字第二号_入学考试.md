North China University Notice
　　Student Character No. 2
　　--Entrance Examination--
The location and time of the entrance examination for the second batch of new students in the second phase, parts one and two, of our university, and the registration numbers of those taking the examination, are announced below:
　　I. Examination Date: March 31st
　　Time: 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM Written Examination　12:00 PM to 5:00 PM Oral Examination
　　II. Examination Location: Fourth Courtyard of Peking University on Guohui Street
　　III. Registration Numbers of Examination Participants:
　　　　Political Character 501——1000　Numerical Character 181——250
　　　　(Those who registered at Qianmian Hutong)
　　　　A Character 501——1000　B Character 251——350
　　　　(Those who originally registered at North China College)　　　　　　　March 29th
