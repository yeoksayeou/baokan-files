Title: Proclamation No. 8 of the Beiping Military Control Commission of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1949-03-28
Page: 4

　　Chinese People's Liberation Army
    Beiping Military Control Commission Proclamation
    Proclamation No. 8
    In order to facilitate legitimate trade for merchants and civilians, it is decided that, with the exception of contraband and military supplies, all other goods shall be freely transported into and out of the city.
    All military supplies (ordnance, ammunition, telecommunications, military industrial equipment, etc.) must have a certificate from a military organ of the People's Liberation Army in order to be transported out of the city.

    　　　　　　　　　Director:　　　Ye Jianying
    　　　　　　　　　Deputy Director:　　　Tan Zheng
    　　　　　　　　　March 27th, Thirty-eighth year of the Republic of China

### 中国人民解放军  北平市军事管制委员会布告　布字第八号

1949-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国人民解放军
    北平市军事管制委员会布告
    布字第八号
    为便利商民进行正当贸易决定除违禁品，军用品外其他商品均一律准予自由运入市内与运出市外。
    所有军用品（军械、弹药、电讯、军工器材等）须持有人民解放军军事机关之证明文件始得运出市外。
    　　　　　　　　　主任　　　叶剑英
    　　　　　　　　　副主任　　谭政
    　　　　　　　中华民国三十八年三月二十七日
