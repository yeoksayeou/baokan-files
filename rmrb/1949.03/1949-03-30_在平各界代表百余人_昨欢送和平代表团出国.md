Title: Over a Hundred Representatives From All Circles in Beiping Gave a Fond Farewell to the Peace Delegation Departing Abroad Yesterday
Author: Zhu
Date: 1949-03-30
Page: 1
　　
　　Over a Hundred Representatives From All Circles in Beiping
  　Yesterday Gave a Fond Farewell to the Peace Delegation Departing Abroad
    [Our Newspaper] The Chinese delegation to the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, including Guo Moruo, Liu Ningyi, and Ma Yinchu (excluding Lu Cui and Wu Qing, who are now in Paris; Ding Ling and Gu Yuan, who are in the Northeast Liberated Area; and Zhao Shuli, who is in the Taihang Liberated Area), a total of thirty-nine people, departed from Beiping at 1:00 PM yesterday (the 29th). Li Weihan, Zhou Yang, Deng Yingchao representing the National Women's Federation, Feng Wenbin representing the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Youth Federation, and democratic figures such as Li Jishen, Shen Junru, and Liu Yazi, along with over a hundred representatives from all walks of life, went to the station to see them off. Before his departure, Mr. Guo Moruo of the delegation declared: We will unite the forces of democracy and peace around the world, and with a strong fighting spirit, eliminate the aggressive wars of the imperialist bloc. (Zhu)

### 在平各界代表百余人　昨欢送和平代表团出国
朱
1949-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　在平各界代表百余人
  　昨欢送和平代表团出国
    【本报讯】我国出席世界拥护和平大会代表团郭沫若、刘宁一、马寅初等（不包括现在巴黎之陆璀、吴青、在东北解放区之丁玲、古元及在太行解放区之赵树理）一行三十九人，已于昨（二十九）日下午一时由平起程出国，李维汉、周扬、全国妇联会代表邓颖超、全国青联筹委会代表冯文彬及李济深、沈钧儒、柳亚子等民主人士各界代表百余人到车站欢送。代表团郭沫若先生行前宣称：我们将团结全世界民主和平的力量，以坚强的斗志，消灭帝国主义集团的侵略战争。（朱）
