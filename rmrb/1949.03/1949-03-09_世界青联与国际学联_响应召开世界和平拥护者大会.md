Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth and International Union of Students Respond to the Call for a World Congress of Partisans of Peace
Author:
Date: 1949-03-09
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 7th. TASS reports: The World Federation of Democratic Youth and the International Union of Students have issued communiques and statements responding to the call from the International Liaison Committee of the International Congress of Intellectuals in Defence of Peace, the Women’s International Democratic Federation, intellectuals, and political leaders, endorsing the convocation of the World Congress of Partisans of Peace. The International Union of Students’ statement calls on all students worldwide to fight against the instigators of new wars who will bring new disasters to humanity.

### 世界青联与国际学联  响应召开世界和平拥护者大会

1949-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　世界青联与国际学联
    响应召开世界和平拥护者大会
    【新华社陕北七日电】塔斯社讯：世界民主青年联盟与国际学联顷发表公报与声明，响应国际文化工作者保卫和平大会国际联络委员会、国际民主妇女联盟、文化工作者与政治领袖们的号召，拥护召开世界和平拥护者大会。国际学联的声明，号召全世界的一切学生向将给人类带来新灾难的新战争挑拨者进行斗争。
