Title: The "March 8th" International Women's Day is Coming, Please Write Articles Quickly
Author:
Date: 1949-03-04
Page: 1

To Women's Federations of all cities and counties, and female workers in factories:

    The "March 8th" International Women's Day is approaching, and this newspaper hopes that you will all work hard to write articles to commemorate this festival.
    (1) Reports on women's lives, especially the living conditions of female workers and rural women.
    (2) Works by female comrades: thoughts, diaries, and various creations.
    (3) Research and discussion on women's work.

 	 	 	 	 	 	 	 	 	 	 	 	 	 	 	 	 This Newspaper Liaison Department

### “三八”妇女节来了请大家快来写稿

1949-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　“三八”妇女节来了请大家快来写稿各市、县妇联会及工厂女工同志们：
    眼看“三八”妇女节就到了，本报希望你们大家努力动手写稿纪念这个节日。
    （一）妇女生活报导，特别是女工，农村妇女的生活情形。
    （二）妇女同志的作品：感想、日记及各种创作。
    （三）妇女工作研究和讨论。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
