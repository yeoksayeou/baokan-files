Title: Democratic Figures Zeng Zhaolun and Others Arrived in Beiping Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-03-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, North China, 30th—Democratic figures Zeng Zhaolun, Liu Wang Liming, Zhou Jingwen, Li Boqiu, Feng Sutao, Yang Ziheng, Luo Ziwei, Huang Dingchen, overseas Chinese democratic figures Fei Zhendong, Dai Ziliang, Wang Jinding, cultural figures Yang Hansheng, Shi Dongshan, Yu Ling, Zang Kejia, Hu Zhongchi, Feng Naichao, Di Chaoyue, professors Yan Jici, Zhu Zhixian, and women representatives Du Junhui, Zhang Qifan, youth representative Luo Fucai, arrived in Beiping yesterday. In addition, Zhou Xinmin and Zheng Kunlian also arrived in Beiping the day before yesterday.

### 民主人士　曾昭抡等昨日抵平

1949-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　民主人士
    曾昭抡等昨日抵平
    【新华社华北三十日电】民主人士曾昭抡、刘王立明、周鲸文、李伯球、冯素陶、杨子恒、罗子为、黄鼎臣、华侨民主人士费振东、戴子良、汪金丁、文化界阳翰笙、史东山、于伶、臧克家、胡仲持、冯乃超、狄超曰、教授严济慈、朱智贤、及妇女代表杜君慧、张启繁、青年代表罗俘材等于昨日抵平。另周新民、郑坤廉亦于前日抵平。
