Title: Liu Yazi and Others Arrive in East China Liberated Area
Author:
Date: 1949-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 14th—Democratic figures Chen Shutong, Liu Yazi, Ma Yinchu, Bao Dasan, Zhang Jiongbo, Zheng Zhenduo, Ye Shengtao, Zhang Zhirang, Cao Yu, Deng Yuzhi, and over twenty others have safely arrived in the East China Liberated Area. The CCP Central Committee East China Bureau, Shandong Branch Bureau, the People's Liberation Army East China Military Region, Shandong Military Region, and the Shandong Provincial People's Government jointly hosted a banquet on the 11th to welcome them, followed by a welcome party that evening. At the meeting, Shu Tong, Director of the Political Department of the East China Military Region, and Xu Shiyou, Commander of the Shandong Military Region, delivered speeches on behalf of their respective organizations, expressing their welcome and extending their regards.

### 柳亚子等抵华东解放区

1949-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　柳亚子等抵华东解放区
    【新华社华东十四日电】民主人士陈叔通、柳亚子、马寅初、包达三、张炯伯、郑振铎、叶圣陶、张志让、曹禺、邓裕志等一行二十余人，安抵华东解放区。中共中央华东局、山东分局、人民解放军华东军区、山东军区、山东省人民政府等特于十一日联合设宴招待，并于当晚举行欢迎晚会。会上华东军区政治部主任舒同、山东军区司令员许世友先后代表致词表示欢迎和慰问。
