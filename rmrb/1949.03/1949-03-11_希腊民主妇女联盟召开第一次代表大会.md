Title: Greek Democratic Women's League Convenes First Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-03-11
Page: 3

　　Greek Democratic Women's League Convenes First Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 9th] Free Greece Radio reports: The First National Congress of the All-Greek Democratic Women's League was convened on March 1st in Free Greece. Participants included 325 representatives from all over the country, including female soldiers of the Democratic Army, women from the liberated areas and areas under the rule of the royalist fascists, representatives of the Greek Communist Party, a delegation from the Greek Provisional Democratic Government, and representatives from the Farmers' Party, the National Liberation Front, and the All-Greek Agricultural Cooperatives League.

### 希腊民主妇女联盟召开第一次代表大会

1949-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　希腊民主妇女联盟召开第一次代表大会
    【新华社陕北九日电】自由希腊电台报道：全希腊民主妇女联盟第一次全国代表大会，于三月一日在自由希腊召开。与会者有来自全国各地的三百二十五位代表，其中有民主军女战士、解放区与保皇法西斯统治区的妇女、希腊共产党代表、希腊临时民主政府代表团、农民党、民族解放阵线与全希腊农业合作社联盟的代表。
