Author:
Date: 1949-03-28
Page: 1

　　Mrs. Gordon of Paris Calls
　　Inviting Our Country to Attend the Peace Conference
　　Our Delegation Replies, Setting Departure for the 29th and Nominating Guo Moruo as Vice-Chairman of the Preparatory Committee
　　[Our Newspaper's News] Mrs. Gordon, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Congress Preparatory Committee, recently sent a telegram to Comrade Cai Chang, formally inviting our country to organize a delegation to attend the Paris World Peace Congress and requesting our country to nominate a representative to serve as Vice-Chairman of the Congress Preparatory Committee. The original telegram is as follows:
Ms. Cai Chang:
　　The World Peace Congress, initiated by the World Intellectuals Liaison Department, the World Democratic Women's Federation, and other organizations, will be held in Paris on April 20th. We sincerely invite Chinese democratic groups and democratic figures to organize a delegation to attend and send a letter or telegram to the congress. Please immediately decide on one Chinese Vice-Chairman to participate in the Preparatory Committee and notify us.
　　[Another News] The reply telegram from the Chinese Delegation to the World Peace Congress is as follows:
　　Mrs. Gordon and the Presidium of the World Peace Congress Preparatory Committee: We are pleased to receive Mrs. Gordon's telegram, forwarded by Ms. Cai Chang, inviting Chinese democrats to send a delegation to the congress. After learning of the decision made by the Congress Preparatory Committee on March 18th, all democratic groups in China, namely the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the North China Literary and Art Circles Association, the Chinese Association of Scientific Workers, the Chinese Association of Academic Workers, the Shanghai People's Organizations Joint Committee, as well as democratic professors and representatives from religious circles, have jointly elected a delegation of forty representatives on March 24th to formally form the Chinese Delegation to the World Peace Congress. Guo Moruo was appointed as the head of the delegation, Liu Ningyi and Ma Yinchu as the deputy heads, and Qian Junrui as the secretary-general. It was decided that the delegation would depart from Beiping on the 29th of this month for Paris to attend the congress. We are willing to express to the congress the firm determination and victorious confidence of the Chinese people in opposing wars of aggression and striving for people's democracy and lasting peace. We are also willing to unite with all democratic and peaceful forces in the world, headed by the Soviet Union, to fight against war instigators and overcome new war dangers. We also nominate Mr. Guo Moruo, the head of our delegation, to participate in the Presidium of the Congress Preparatory Committee as Vice-Chairman. We wish the congress success. Long live the victory of people's democracy and lasting peace for the people of the world!
　　　　　The Chinese Delegation to the World Peace Congress, March 27th, from Beiping

### 巴黎戈登夫人来电　邀请我国参加和平大会  我代表团复电定廿九日启程并推郭沫若任筹委会副主席

1949-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　巴黎戈登夫人来电
  　邀请我国参加和平大会
    我代表团复电定廿九日启程并推郭沫若任筹委会副主席
    【本报讯】世界拥护和平大会筹委会副主席戈登夫人，顷电蔡畅同志，正式邀请我国组织代表团出席巴黎拥护世界和平大会，并请我国推派代表担任大会筹委会副主席。原电如下：蔡畅女士：
    由世界知识分子联络部，世界民主妇女联合会及其他团体所发起之世界和平大会，将于四月二十日在巴黎举行。我们恳切邀请中国民主团体及民主人士组织代表团前来参加，并向大会致书致电。请立即决定参加筹委会之中国副主席一名，并通知我们。
    【又讯】中国出席世界和平大会代表团复电如下：
    戈登夫人并世界和平大会筹委会主席团：顷由蔡畅女士处转来戈登夫人邀请中国民主人士派遣代表团出席大会的电报，欣悉。中国各民主团体于获悉大会筹委会在三月十八日所作的决定后，即由中华全国总工会，中华全国民主妇女联合会，中国青年联合会筹备会，中华全国学生联合会，中华全国文艺协会，华北文艺界协会，中国科学工作者协会，中国学术工作者协会，上海人民团体联合会及民主教授，宗教界各方面推选代表共四十人，于三月二十四日正式组成出席世界和平大会中国代表团，推定郭沫若为代表团的团长，刘宁一和马寅初为副团长，钱俊瑞为秘书长，并决定于本月二十九日由北平前往巴黎出席大会。我们愿意将中国人民反对侵略战争，争取人民民主与持久和平的坚强决心与胜利信心，向大会申述，并愿联合全世界以苏联为首的一切民主和平力量，为反对战争挑拨者、克服新的战争危险而斗争。我们并推定本代表团团长郭沫若先生，参加大会筹备会的主席团担任副主席的职任。预祝大会的成功。全世界人民民主与持久和平胜利万岁！
    　　　　　出席世界和平大会中国代表团三月二十七日自北平
