Title: Korean Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1949-03-09
Page: 3

　　Korean Delegation Arrives in Moscow
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 7th -- TASS Moscow: The government delegation of the Korean People's Democratic Republic, led by Premier Kim Il-sung, arrived here on March 3rd. Upon arriving at the Moscow train station, Premier Kim Il-sung stated: "The Korean people will never forget the extensive and selfless assistance provided by the Soviet Union in helping us to rebuild and develop our national economy and culture, and in helping us to build a unified, independent, and democratic nation. The Korean people understand that the friendship of the powerful Soviet people is the primary guarantee of their freedom, independence, and national sovereignty. I am confident that the Korean government delegation's visit to Moscow at this time will serve the cause of universal peace and security, and of expanding and strengthening the political, economic, and cultural ties between the Soviet Union and Korea."

### 朝鲜代表团到达莫斯科

1949-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜代表团到达莫斯科
    【新华社陕北七日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：朝鲜人民民主共和国政府代表团，由总理金日成率领，于三月三日抵达此间。在到达莫斯科车站时，金日成总理发表谈话称：“朝鲜人民将永远不会忘记苏联给予的大量的无私的援助，援助我们重建与发展国民经济及文化，援助我们建设统一的独立的民主国家。朝鲜人民了解到强大的苏联人民的友谊是他们的自由、独立与国家主权的主要保证。我确信：此刻访问莫斯科的朝鲜政府代表团，将为普遍和平与安全，为扩大与加强苏联与朝鲜两国间之政治、经济、文化联系的事业而服务。”
