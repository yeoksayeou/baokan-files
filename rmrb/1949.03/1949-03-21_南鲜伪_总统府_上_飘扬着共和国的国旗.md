Title: The National Flag of the Republic Flying Over the South Korean Puppet "Presidential Palace"
Author:
Date: 1949-03-21
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th. A broadcast from Pyongyang Radio stated: On March 4th, the national flag of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea flew over the puppet "Presidential Palace" in Seoul, South Korea, for the entire day. An unknown person very cleverly hung this national flag on the flagpole of the puppet "Presidential Palace," and the "Presidential Palace" servants were unable to take down the national flag for a long time. The residents of Seoul joyfully welcomed the appearance of the Republic's national flag.

### 南鲜伪“总统府”上  飘扬着共和国的国旗

1949-03-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　南鲜伪“总统府”上
    飘扬着共和国的国旗
    【新华社陕北二十日电】平壤电台广播称：三月四日，朝鲜人民民主共和国的国旗在南鲜汉城伪“总统府”上飘扬竟日。一位不知名人士非常巧妙的把这个国旗悬在伪“总统府”的旗杆上，而“总统府”的公役们久久不能把这面国旗取下。汉城居民欣悦的欢迎共和国国旗的出现。
