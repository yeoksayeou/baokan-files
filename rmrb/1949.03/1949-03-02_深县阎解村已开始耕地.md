Title: Yan Jie Village in Shen County Begins Plowing
Author:
Date: 1949-03-02
Page: 2

　　【News Dispatch】

　　Yan Jie Village in Shen County Begins Plowing
    [Further Dispatch] Yan Jie Village in Shen County is a disaster-stricken village. Thanks to the good development of sideline occupations, every household in the village has enough to eat. All the villagers are actively preparing for large-scale production. A large part of the manure has been delivered a few days ago, and some have begun plowing.

### 深县阎解村已开始耕地

1949-03-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　深县阎解村已开始耕地
    【又讯】深县阎解村是个受灾村，由于副业搞得好，全村各户都够吃的了，全村人们正积极准备大生产，日前已大部送完了粪，有的即开始耕地。
