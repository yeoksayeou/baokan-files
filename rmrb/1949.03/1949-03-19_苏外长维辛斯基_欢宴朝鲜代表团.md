Title: Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky Hosts Banquet for Korean Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-03-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 18th. TASS, Moscow, March 15th: On March 15th, Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky, on behalf of the government, hosted a banquet for the delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Among those attending the banquet were Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Korean Republic; Park Heon-young, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister; Hong Myung-hee, Vice Premier; Chung Joon-taek, Chairman of the State Planning Commission; Jang Shi-woo, Minister of Commerce; Baek Nam-woon, Minister of Education; Kim Yeon-joo, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, and other officials. The ambassadors to the Soviet Union from Bulgaria, Poland, Romania, Mongolia, Albania, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and Yugoslavia were also invited to attend the banquet. The banquet was held in an atmosphere of warmth and friendship.

### 苏外长维辛斯基　欢宴朝鲜代表团

1949-03-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏外长维辛斯基
  　欢宴朝鲜代表团
    【新华社陕北十八日电】塔斯社莫斯科十五日电：三月十五日，苏联外长维辛斯基代表政府，欢宴朝鲜人民民主共和国代表团，赴宴者有朝鲜共和国总理金日成、副总理兼外长朴宪永、副总理洪命熹、国家计划委员会主席郑准泽、商业部长张时雨、教育部长白南云、邮电部长金延柱及其他官员。保加利亚、波兰、罗马尼亚、蒙古、阿尔巴尼亚、匈牙利、捷克与南斯拉夫等国驻苏联的使节，亦被邀参加宴会。宴会在热忱与友谊的空气中进行。
