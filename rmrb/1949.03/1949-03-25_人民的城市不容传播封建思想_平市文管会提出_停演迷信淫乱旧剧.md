Title: People's City Does Not Tolerate the Spread of Feudal Ideas; Ping City Cultural Management Committee Proposes Suspension of Superstitious and Obscene Old Operas
Author:
Date: 1949-03-25
Page: 2

　　People's City Does Not Tolerate the Spread of Feudal Ideas
    Ping City Cultural Management Committee Proposes
    Suspension of Superstitious and Obscene Old Operas
    [This newspaper report] Since the liberation of Peiping, various circles have had many opinions on the performance of old operas. The phenomenon of staging feudal, superstitious, erotic, and anti-people dramas in the people's new city should be corrected immediately. The Cultural Takeover Committee recently proposed a temporary suspension of 55 operas, and the National Opera Association sent letters to the theaters and troupes to inform them. According to the general reflection of the old opera circles, they also believe that these operas should no longer be performed, but there are many similar operas, and it is necessary to widely absorb opinions from all parties and gradually carry out reforms. The temporarily suspended operas are listed below:
    1. Those that promote gods, monsters, and superstition: *You Liu Dian* [Tour of the Sixth Hall], *Hua You Shan* [Slippery Oil Mountain], *Pi Shan Jiu Mu* (Houbu of *Baolian Deng*) [Splitting the Mountain to Save Mother (Later part of the Lotus Lantern)], *Tan Yin Shan (Zha Pan Guan)* [Visiting the Underworld Mountain (Beheading the Judge)], *Hei Lv Gao Zhuang (Da Gun Chu Xiang Houbu)* [Black Donkey's Accusation (Later part of Beating the Stick and Exposing the Box)], *Qi Yuan Bao (Wu Pen Ji)* [Strange Injustice Reported (Black Pottery Basin)], *Ba Xian De Dao* [The Eight Immortals Attain Taoism], *Huo Zhuo San Lang (Wulong Yuan Houbu)* [Capturing the Third Son Alive (Later part of Wulong Courtyard)], *San Xi Bai Mu Dan* [Three Teases of White Peony], *Dao Hun Ling* [Stealing the Soul Bell], *Yin Yang He* [Yin Yang River], *Shi Ba Luo Han Shou Da Peng* [Eighteen Arhats Subdue the Great Peng Bird], *Da Jin Zhuan Houbu (Er Shi Ba Xiu Gui Wei)* [Beating the Golden Brick Later Part (Twenty-Eight Constellations Return to Their Positions)], *Tang Ming Huang You Yue Gong* [Emperor Tang Ming's Visit to the Moon Palace], *Liu Quan Jin Gua* [Liu Quan Offers Melons], *Kun Lun Jian Xia Zhuan* [The Biography of the Kunlun Swordsman], *Qing Cheng Shi Jiu Xia* [Nineteen Heroes of Qingcheng], *Feng Shen Bang* (Lian Tai Ben Xi) [Investiture of the Gods (Serial Opera)], *Quan Bu Zhuangzi* [All of Zhuangzi], *Fei Jian Zhan Bai Long* [Flying Sword Slays the White Dragon], *Quan Bu Zhong Kui* [All of Zhong Kui], *Fan Yan'an* [Oppose Yan'an], *Yan Fen Ji* [Rouge Powder Plot].
    2. All those that promote obscene and lewd thoughts: *Hong Niang* [The Matchmaker], *Da Pi Guan (Hu Die Meng)* [Splitting the Coffin (Butterfly Dream)], *Hai Hui Si (Ma Xiang Da, Shuang Ling Ji)* [Haihui Temple (Ma Xiangda, Double Bell Record)], *Shuang Ding Ji* [Double Nail Record], *Ye Shi Qi* [Also Qi], *Yi Cui Hua* [Lost Cuihua], *Gui Fei Zui Jiu* [The Drunken Concubine], *Sha Zi Bao* [Killing the Son for Revenge], *Yan Zhi Pan* [Rouge Verdict], *Pan Si Dong* [Spider Cave], *Shuang Yao Hui* [Double Shake Meeting], *Guan Wang Miao* [Guan Wang Temple], *Piao Yuan (Yu Tang Chun Qianbu)* [Whorehouse (First Part of Yu Tang Chun)].
    3. Those that promote national betrayal and the ideology of foreign invasion: *Si Lang Tan Mu* [Fourth Son Visits His Mother], *Tie Guan Tu* [Iron Crown Chart], *Tie Gong Ji* [Iron Rooster], *Ba Ben Yan Men Guan* [Eight Volumes of Yanmen Pass].
    4. Those that glorify slave morality: *Jiu Geng Tian (Ma Yi Jiu Zhu)* [Nine Watches of the Night (Ma Yi Saves His Master)], *Nan Tian Men* [South Heavenly Gate], *Bian Guan Gao* (Dan “Ji Fang Xun” Ke Yan) [Changing Official Edicts (But "Ji Fang Xun" Can Be Performed)].
    5. Those that promote feudal oppression: *Zhan Jing Tang (Wu Han Sha Qi)* [Beheading the Sutra Hall (Wu Han Kills His Wife)], *You Long Xi Feng (Mei Long Zhen)* [Dragon and Phoenix Play (Mei Long Town)], *Cui Ping Shan* [Cui Ping Mountain], *Hong Mei Ge* [Red Plum Pavilion], *Ku Zu Miao* [Crying at the Ancestral Temple].
    6. Some extremely boring or without fixed scripts: *Fang Mian Hua* [Spinning Cotton], *Xi Mi Jia Ting* [Opera Fan Family], *Xi Mi Xiao Jie* [Opera Fan Miss], *Shi Huang Jin* [Picking Up Gold], *Shi Ba Che* [Eighteen Pulls], *Bian Pa Lou* [Changing Fear of the Building], *Xia Le Guang Deng* [Blindly Strolling with Lanterns].

### 人民的城市不容传播封建思想  平市文管会提出  停演迷信淫乱旧剧

1949-03-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　人民的城市不容传播封建思想
    平市文管会提出
    停演迷信淫乱旧剧
    【本报讯】北平解放后，各界对旧剧的演出，曾有不少意见，在人民的新城市里，依然搬演着封建迷信以及色情和反人民的戏剧的现象，应即改正。文化接管委员会日前特提出暂时停演剧目五十五出，由国剧公会分函通知各剧院暨各班社，据旧剧界一般反映，也认为这些戏不应再演，但与这些戏类似的尚多，还需要广泛吸收各方意见，逐步进行改革。兹将暂时停演剧目分列于下：
    一、属于提倡神怪迷信的：游六殿、滑油山、劈山救母（宝莲灯后部）、探阴山（铡判官）、黑驴告状（打棍出箱后部）、奇冤报（乌盆计）、八仙得道、活捉三郎（乌龙院后部）、三戏白牡丹、盗魂铃、阴阳河、十八罗汉收大鹏、打金砖后部（二十八宿归位）、唐明皇游月宫、刘全进瓜、昆仑剑侠传、青城十九侠、封神榜（连台本戏）、全部庄子、飞剑斩白龙、全部钟馗、反延安、胭粉计。
    二凡属于提倡淫乱思想的：红娘、大劈棺（蝴蝶梦）、海慧寺（马想达、双铃记）、双钉记、也是齐、遗翠花、贵妃醉酒、杀子报、胭脂判、盘丝洞、双摇会、关王庙、嫖院（玉堂春前部）。
    三、属于提倡民族失节及异族侵略思想的：四郎探母、铁冠图、铁公鸡、八本雁门关。
    四、属于歌颂奴隶道德的：九更天（马义救主）、南天门、变官诰（但“机房训”可演）。
    五、属于表扬封建压迫的：斩经堂（吴汉杀妻）、游龙戏凤（梅龙镇）、翠屏山、红梅阁、哭祖庙。
    六、一些极无聊或无固定剧本的：纺棉花、戏迷家庭、戏迷小姐、拾黄金、十八扯、变怕蒌、瞎了逛灯。
