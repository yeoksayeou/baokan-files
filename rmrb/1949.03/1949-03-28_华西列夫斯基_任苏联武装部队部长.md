Title: Vasilevsky Appointed Minister of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-03-28
Page: 3

　　Vasilevsky
　　Appointed Minister of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Union

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 26th, TASS Moscow, March 24th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has relieved Bulganin, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, of his post as Minister of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Union. The Presidium has appointed Vasilevsky as Minister of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Union. Vasilevsky was originally Deputy Minister of the Department and Chief of the General Staff of the Soviet Army. When the Soviet Union declared war on Japan in August 1945, he was the Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Far East, commanding the Soviet army to eliminate the Kwantung Army of fascist Japan, liberate the Northeast from 14 years of Japanese enslavement, and force Japan to surrender.

### 华西列夫斯基　任苏联武装部队部长

1949-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　华西列夫斯基
  　任苏联武装部队部长
    【新华社北平二十六日电】塔斯社莫斯科二十四日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团解除苏联部长会议副主席布尔加寗苏联武装部队部部长的职务。主席团已任命华西列夫斯基为苏联武装部队部部长。按华氏原任该部副部长兼苏军参谋总长。一九四五年八月苏联对日宣战时任远东苏联总司令，指挥苏军消灭法西斯日本的关东军，从日本十四年奴役中解放东北，迫使日本投降。
