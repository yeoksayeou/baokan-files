Title: Qinggong of the Pingjin Railway Bureau Begins Preparations for Establishing the Youth League
Author: Chen Ji
Date: 1949-03-22
Page: 2

The Qinggong of the Pingjin Railway Bureau
    Begins Preparations for Establishing the Youth League

    [This Newspaper's News] The Pingjin Railway Administration's young workers officially established the Preparatory Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League yesterday (21st), beginning preparations for establishing the Youth League. The "Celebration of Liberation Joint Association," "Vocational Youth," and other progressive groups established by the Pingjin Railway Bureau of the Xiagongfu, the Pinghan Railway Bureau, and the railway workers of the Qianmen District before and after liberation, in order to further organize young workers, serve the broad masses of the people, and build a new democratic New China, specially selected 35 preparatory committee members for the establishment of the new Youth League. A full meeting of the preparatory committee was held at the Pingjin Railway Bureau of the Xiagongfu at 7:00 p.m. on the 21st, electing Xu Yujing and others as standing committee members, and appointing Hou Lin and Xu Yujing as the director and deputy director, actively launching the work of establishing the Youth League in the bureau.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Chen Ji)

### 平津铁路局青工  开始筹备建团
陈迹
1949-03-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　平津铁路局青工
    开始筹备建团
    【本报讯】平津铁路管理局青年职工于昨日（廿一）正式成立中国新民主主义青年团筹备委员会，开始筹备建团。该局霞公府平津铁路局、平汉铁路局及前门区铁路职工在解放前后所成立之“庆祝解放联合会”、“职业青年”等进步团体，为了进一步组织青年职工，为广大人民服务，建设新民主主义新中国，特推出建立新青团筹备委员三十五人，于二十一日晚七时在霞公府平津铁路局召开全体筹委会议，选出许玉景等为常委，并推定侯麟定，许玉景为正副主任，积极展开该局建团工作。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（陈迹）
