Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Officially Begins Work in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-03-02
Page: 1

　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions
  　Officially Begins Work in Beiping
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 28th] The All-China Federation of Trade Unions moved from Shenyang to Beiping on the 25th. Starting March 1st, it will officially begin work at No. 1 Fuqian Street, the former site of the puppet Beiping Municipal Council. Among the first group of personnel to arrive in Beiping are Li Lisan, First Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhu Xuefan, Second Vice-Chairman, Standing Committee members Xu Zizhen, Lin Qiangyun, Zhu Junxin, Executive Committee member Ouyang Zurun, and more than sixty staff members of the Federation. After arriving in Beiping, the Federation has sent some staff to conduct investigations and research in Beiping's mines and factories to understand the situation of the workers' movement in Beiping and its suburbs. The Federation will also hold an enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee in the near future. Various trade union organizations in Beiping are preparing a grand welcome.

### 全国总工会　迁平正式办公

1949-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国总工会
  　迁平正式办公
    【新华社北平二十八日电】中华全国总工会已于二十五日由沈阳迁抵北平，三月一日起，即在府前街一号原伪北平市参议会旧址开始正式办公。首批来平人员中，有中华全国总工会第一副主席李立三，第二副主席朱学范，常委许子桢、林锵云、朱俊欣，执委欧阳祖润等十二人及该会工作人员六十余人。该会在抵达平市后，已派出一部分工作人员前往平市矿山、工厂进行调查研究工作，以了解北平市内及四郊的职工运动情况。该会并将于最近召开常委会扩大会议。北平市各职工团体现正准备盛大欢迎。
