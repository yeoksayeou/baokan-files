Title: Yoshida Reactionary Government Cuts Paper Allocation to Japanese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th—The Yoshida government of Japan is restricting the expansion of the Japanese Communist Party's influence by drastically reducing the amount of paper allocated to JCP publications. According to a TASS dispatch on the 14th, the Japanese Communist Party issued a statement saying that the Yoshida Cabinet's move is intended to suppress democratic forces and encourage further activities by fascist forces. Communist Party representatives visited the Paper Allocation Committee and requested an increase in the amount of paper allocated to Communist Party publications.

### 吉田反动政府削减分配日共纸张

1949-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　吉田反动政府削减分配日共纸张
    【新华社陕北十七日电】日本吉田政府正以大量削减日共出版物所需用的纸张，来限制日共影响的扩大。据塔斯社十四日电，日本共产党为此发表声明称：吉田内阁采取此项步骤意在镇压民主力量并鼓励法西斯势力进一步活动。共产党代表曾造访纸张分配委员会，要求增加配给共产党出版物的纸张数量。
