Title: National Women's Congress Exhibition Notice
Author:
Date: 1949-03-30
Page: 1

　　National Women's Congress
  　Exhibition Notice
    This association has designated April 3rd as a visiting day for the Congress delegates. On this day, public viewing will be suspended. The exhibition will resume on the 4th.
