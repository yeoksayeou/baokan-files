Title: YU NAN WEI KE ZHENG YANG - Defeated Bandit Troops Flee West from Zhengyang
Author:
Date: 1949-03-24
Page: 1

　　Yu Nan We Conquer Zhengyang
    Defeated Bandit Troops Flee West
    【Xinhua News Agency Central Plains 23rd dispatch】 Late news: The Central Plains People's Liberation Army recovered the city of Zhengyang in southern Henan province on the 12th of this month. Part of the Seventh Army of the Kuomintang bandit army defending the city fled westward.

### 豫南我克正阳  守城匪军向西逃窜

1949-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　豫南我克正阳
    守城匪军向西逃窜
    【新华社中原二十三日电】迟到消息：中原人民解放军本月十二日收复河南南部的正阳县城，守城国民党匪军第七军一部向西逃窜。
