Title: Supporting the CCP's Eight Peace Conditions
Author:
Date: 1949-03-25
Page: 3

　　Supporting the CCP's Eight Peace Conditions
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 23rd: According to the Hong Kong-based "Hua Shang Bao" on March 17th, the "French Overseas Chinese Peace Promotion Association" organized by overseas Chinese in France, issued a declaration supporting the eight peace conditions of the Chinese Communist Party, and advocating: 1. The reactionary rule of the Kuomintang in Nanjing should be overthrown to eradicate the civil war. 2. The United States and other countries should immediately cease aiding Chiang Kai-shek and any form of intervention in China. 3. All democratic parties should consult as soon as possible to establish a people's coalition government.

### 拥护中共八项和平条件

1949-03-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　拥护中共八项和平条件
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】据香港三月十七日华商报称，旅法华侨所组织之“旅法和平促进会”，发表宣言，拥护中国共产党八项和平条件，并主张：一、南京国民党反动统治应予推翻，以根绝内战。二、美国及其他国家应立即停止援蒋，及对中国实行任何方式之干涉。三、各民主党派应早日协商，成立人民的联合政府。
