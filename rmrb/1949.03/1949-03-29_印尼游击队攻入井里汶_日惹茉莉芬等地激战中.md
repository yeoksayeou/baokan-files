Title: Indonesian Guerrillas Attack Cirebon, Fierce Fighting in Yogyakarta, Madiun and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1949-03-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 27th. TASS reports: The Dutch News Agency admitted that Indonesian guerrillas launched an attack on the Dutch naval base of Cirebon, Java, and engaged in two hours of fierce fighting. Only after the Dutch army obtained reinforcements of tanks and artillery did the guerrillas withdraw from the city. A reporter for the Dutch Communist newspaper De Waarheid reported that fierce fighting is still taking place in Yogyakarta, Madiun, and Sumatra. Dutch airborne troops have been reinforced in areas south of Yogyakarta, and Dutch aircraft are bombing and strafing peaceful villages in Java and Sumatra. The Dutch ship "Volendam" left Rotterdam on the 25th, carrying another batch of invading troops to Indonesia.

### 印尼游击队攻入井里汶　日惹茉莉芬等地激战中

1949-03-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　印尼游击队攻入井里汶
    日惹茉莉芬等地激战中
    【新华社北平二十七日电】据塔斯社讯：荷兰通讯社承认，印尼游击队对荷兰海军基地爪哇的井里汶发动进攻，曾进行两小时的激烈＠战。又有当荷军取得坦克与炮兵的增援之后，游击队才退出城外。荷共真理报记者报道，激战仍在日惹、茉莉芬及苏门答腊进行。荷兰空运部队增援日惹以南地区，荷机轰炸再扫射爪哇与苏门答腊的和平乡村。荷轮“沃伦丹”号于二十五日又运载一批侵略军离监特丹驶往印尼。
