Title: Wallace Speaks at New York Peace Conference: Millions Concerned About World Peace, US Imperialist Deception Will Surely Fail
Author:
Date: 1949-03-30
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 29th: Telecommunications Agency, New York Dispatch: Wallace declared in a radio address on the evening of the 27th that the State Department is attempting to destroy the United Nations and replace it with military aggressive alliances like the North Atlantic War Pact. Wallace said: The United Nations – the symbol and hope of peace for all nations – has become the first victim of the aggressive North Atlantic Pact. Because the Soviet Union will never be an aggressor, undoubtedly, establishing military groups around the Soviet Union to encircle it is an act of aggression by the United States.

Wallace spoke at the meeting of the Scientific and Cultural Circles for the Defense of World Peace Conference here on the 26th, saying that the US State Department used the trick of refusing entry visas to prevent almost all non-Communist representatives from Western Europe from attending the meeting. The invention of this dishonest tactic is to create the impression in the United States that this peace conference is only a matter for the Communists. But in reality, the cause of peace is based in the hearts of millions of people, who will fight together and defend peace together with all those who sincerely maintain peace.

### 华莱士在纽约和平大会演说  千百万人关心世界和平美帝骗人手法必将失败

1949-03-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　华莱士在纽约和平大会演说
    千百万人关心世界和平美帝骗人手法必将失败
    【新华社北平二十九日电】电通社纽约讯：华莱士二十七日晚在广播演说中宣称：国务院企图毁弃联合国，并以象北大西洋战争公约这一类的军事侵略同盟来代表联合国。华莱士说：联合国——一切国家和平的象征与希望——成为侵略性的北大西洋公约的第一个牺牲品。因为苏联绝不会成为侵略者，无疑地，在苏联周围成立军事集团以包围苏联的行动，是美国的侵略行动。
    华莱士二十六日在此间科学文化界保卫世界和平大会的会议上讲说称：美国国务院用拒发入境护照的诡计，使西欧的几乎一切非共产党的代表不得参加会议。发明这种不老实的手法，是为了在美国造成一种印象，即这个和平大会只是共产党人的事情。但实际上，和平事业是奠基在千百万人的心上的，他们将与一切真心保持和平的人共同奋斗，共同保卫和平。
