Brief Explanation of Terms

　　　　"Making up the deficit"
    Making up the deficit – A financial shortfall, usually written in red ink in accounting books. Therefore, this shortfall amount is habitually called a deficit, and making up this shortfall is called making up the deficit.
    　　　　Currency constantly returning to the bank
    Currency constantly returning to the bank – "Currency" refers to money. "Returning to the bank" means being recovered. When the bank systematically and gradually recovers the currency it has put into circulation, it is called currency constantly returning to the bank.

### 名词浅释

1949-03-30
第4版()
专栏：名词浅释

　　名词浅释
            “弥补赤字”
    弥补赤字——财政上的亏空，通常在帐簿上是用红颜色写的，所以把这个亏空数额，习惯上就称为赤字，弥补这个亏空，就叫做弥补赤字。
    　　　　筹码不断回笼
    筹码不断回笼——筹码是指货币说的，回笼就是收回的意思，银行把投放出去的货币有计划的逐步的收回来，就叫筹码不断回笼。
