Title: Peiping Municipal Government Holds District Chiefs' Meeting to Discuss Ways to Support the Troops

Author:

Date: 1949-03-25
Page: 2

　　Peiping Municipal Government Holds District Chiefs' Meeting
　　Discussing Ways to Support the Troops
　　[By Our Own Correspondent] In order to promote the movement to support the troops, the Peiping Municipal People's Government convened a meeting of the district chiefs of the twelve urban districts on the 22nd to discuss methods of providing comfort. The meeting decided on the following: (1) Material comfort: mobilize all sectors to send comfort items, which will be centrally distributed by each district in a unified manner. (2) Spiritual comfort: hold military-civilian get-togethers in elementary schools, write letters of appreciation, and present banners. All tasks are scheduled to be completed before the end of this month.

### 平市府开区长会议  研究劳军办法

1949-03-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市府开区长会议
    研究劳军办法
    【本报讯】北平市人民政府为推广劳军运动，于二十二日召集城区十二个区区长开会，研讨慰劳办法，会上决定：（一）物质慰劳，发动各界送慰劳品，由各区集中统一分配。（二）精神慰劳，举行小学的军民联欢会，写慰劳信，送锦旗等。各项工作，并定本月底以前完成。
