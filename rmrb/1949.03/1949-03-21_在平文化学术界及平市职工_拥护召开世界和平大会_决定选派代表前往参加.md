Title: Intellectual and Academic Circles in Beiping and Workers of Beiping City Support the Convening of the World Peace Congress and Decide to Send Representatives to Participate
Author:
Date: 1949-03-21
Page: 1

　　In Beiping, national cultural groups, university professors, writers, scientists, and journalists exchanged views on the issue of responding to the call for and supporting the World Peace Congress at the Beijing Hotel yesterday (the 20th). Attendees included Guo Moruo, Liu Yazi, Ma Xulun, Ma Yinchu, Mao Dun, Zhang Xiruo, Xu Deheng, Deng Chumin, Zheng Zhenduo, Ye Shengtao, Tian Han, Hong Shen, Cao Yu, Xiao San, Qian Junrui, Hu Yuzhi, Zhang Zhizhang, Qian Sanqiang, Ding Zan, Yuan Hanqing, Lu Yudao, Cao Jinghua, Xu Guangping, Zhou Yang, Zhou Jianren, Jian Bozan, Hou Wailu, Wu Han, Xia Kangnong, Song Yunbin, Fu Binran, Shakoff, Ai Qing, Ye Qianyu, Shen Zhiyuan, Shi Fuliang, Qian Jiaju, Yuan Muzhi, Ge Baoquan, Fan Changjiang, and others, totaling sixty-four people. The speeches at the meeting were very enthusiastic, and it was unanimously decided to issue a declaration in response to the world peace movement, opposing aggression. Representatives will be nominated by units such as the China Association of Scientists, the China Association of Academic Workers, the All-China Literary Association, the North China Literary and Art Circles Association, and the Preparatory Committee of Journalists in Liberated Areas to participate in the World Congress for the Defense of Peace to be held in Paris in April.
    [本报讯] More than 1,200 activists from major state-owned factories and mines in Beiping held a warm meeting yesterday (the 20th) at the auditorium of the Pinghan Railway Administration Bureau, responding to the call for the World Peace Congress. First, Xiao Ming, director of the preparatory committee of the Municipal Trade Union Council, read the Xinhua News Agency editorial "Democratic Forces Around the World Mobilize to Crush the Conspiracy of Warmongers." He expressed that the power of the working class and the people of the world is confident in overcoming the new danger of war, calling on all workers to participate in the anti-aggression struggle with practical actions. The meeting decided to prepare to send its own representatives to attend the upcoming World Congress for the Defense of Peace.

### 在平文化学术界及平市职工  拥护召开世界和平大会  决定选派代表前往参加

1949-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　在平文化学术界及平市职工
    拥护召开世界和平大会
    决定选派代表前往参加
    【本报讯】在北平的全国文化团体及大学教授、作家、科学家、新闻记者，于昨（二十）日在北京饭店就响应召开拥护世界和平大会问题交换意见，到会者有郭沫若、柳亚子、马叙伦、马寅初、茅盾、张奚若、许德珩、邓初民、郑振铎、叶圣陶、田汉、洪深、曹禺、萧三、钱俊瑞、胡愈之、张志让、钱三强、丁瓒、袁翰青、卢于道、曹靖华、许广平、周扬、周建人、翦伯赞、侯外庐、吴晗、夏康农、宋云彬、傅彬然、沙可夫、艾青、叶浅予、沈志远、施复亮、千家驹、袁牧之、戈宝权、范长江等六十四人。会上发言十分热烈，一致决定发表宜言，响应保卫世界和平运动，反对侵略，将由中国科学者协会、中国学术工作者协会、中华全国文艺协会、华北文艺界协会、解放区新闻记者筹备会等单位推派代表，参加行将于四月在巴黎召开之世界拥护和平大会。
    【本报讯】北平全市各主要公营工厂矿厂职工积极分子一千二百余人于昨日（二十日）在平汉铁路管理局礼堂热烈集会，响应召开世界拥护和平大会。首由市职工会筹备会主任萧明朗读了新华社“全世界民主力量动员起来，粉碎战争挑拨者阴谋”的社论。他表示工人阶级及全世界人民的力量有把握把新的战争危险克服下去。号召全体职工以实际行动参加反侵略斗争。会上决定准备派遣自己的代表出席即将召开的世界拥护和平大会。
