Title: North China People's Revolutionary University New Student Admission Notice
Author:
Date: 1949-03-26
Page: 1

North China People's Revolutionary University
　  New Student Admission Notice
　　The fourth and fifth groups of newly admitted students of our university are scheduled to register and enroll at the university's main campus in Xiyuan within four days from March 27th to 30th, bringing luggage and daily necessities. This is hereby announced.

　　　　　　　　　　March 25th, 38th Year of the Republic of China

### 华北人民革命大学新生入学通告

1949-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民革命大学
　  新生入学通告
    本校第四次及第五次录取新生决定于三月二十七日至三十日四天内携带行李及日用品到西苑本校注册入学。特此通告。
    　　　　　　　　　　中华民国三十八年三月二十五日
