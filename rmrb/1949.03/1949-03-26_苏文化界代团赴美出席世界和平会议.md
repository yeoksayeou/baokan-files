Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation Travels to the US to Attend World Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-03-26
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 23rd—TASS, Moscow: At the invitation of Shapley, the host of the American Arts, Sciences and Professions for Peace Conference, the Soviet cultural circles have formed a delegation and departed for the United States on the 23rd to attend the conference. It is reported that the delegation includes: Fadeyev, Secretary-General of the Soviet Writers' Union (head of the delegation), Obalil, a member of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Professor Shostakovich, a distinguished Master of Arts, the famous writer Pavlenko, and the People's Artists of the Soviet Union, Chiaulari, Gerasimov, and the delegation secretary Rozkonsky.

### 苏文化界代团赴美出席世界和平会议

1949-03-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏文化界代团赴美出席世界和平会议
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联文化界应美国艺术、科学、自由职业者保卫和平大会主持人沙普莱之邀请，已组成代表团于二十三日首途赴美，出席大会。据悉代表团中包括：苏联作家协会总书记法捷也夫（代表团团长），苏联科学院会员奥巴里尔，卓越的艺术硕士萧斯塔科维奇教授，著名作家巴甫连科，苏联人民艺术家齐阿乌拉里、格拉锡莫夫及代表团秘书罗兹康斯基。
