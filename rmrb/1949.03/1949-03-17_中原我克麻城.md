Title: Central Plains Our Army Captures Macheng
Author:
Date: 1949-03-17
Page: 1

　　Central Plains Our Army Captures Macheng
    [Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 15th] The People's Liberation Army of the Central Plains recovered Macheng in eastern Hubei on the 9th. The defending enemy fled to the southwest.

### 中原我克麻城

1949-03-17

专栏：

　　中原我克麻城
    【新华社中原十五日电】中原人民解放军九日收复湖北东部的麻城，守敌自西南逃窜。
