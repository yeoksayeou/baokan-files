Title: People's Organizations in Germany, Hungary, Poland, and Czechoslovakia Support the Convening of a Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-03-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 23rd: People's organizations in Germany, Hungary, Poland, and Czechoslovakia have announced their support for the convening of the World Congress for Peace. The German People's Committee meeting held in Berlin on March 18th and 19th passed a resolution endorsing the declaration of the International Liaison Committee of the World Congress of Intellectuals in Defense of Peace and the Women's International Democratic Federation regarding the convening of the World Peace Congress. The committee called on all democratic, peace-loving, and progressive forces in Germany to join the global people's peace front. The Hungarian Writers' Union recently sent a telegram to the International Liaison Committee of the World Congress of Intellectuals in Defense of Peace, supporting the proposal to convene the World Congress for Peace. The Hungarian National Council of Intellectuals also held an extraordinary meeting and passed a declaration announcing its participation in the World Congress for Peace. The Executive Committee of the Polish Journalists Association passed a resolution on the 20th, responding to the call to convene the World Congress for Peace, declaring that "it is fully prepared to further engage in the tireless struggle against the criminal conspiracies of arms merchants, so that Poland and all mankind may obtain a consolidated peace." The research societies of Warsaw University, the Krakow Agricultural Specialized School, and the Federation of Polish Student Groups also passed resolutions supporting the declaration of the International Liaison Committee of the World Congress of Intellectuals in Defense of Peace and the Women's International Democratic Federation. All churches within Czechoslovakia, with the exception of the Catholic Church, have issued statements announcing their support for the convening of the World Congress for Peace.

### 德匈波捷人民团体拥护召开和平大会

1949-03-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　德匈波捷人民团体拥护召开和平大会
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】德、匈、波、捷各国又有一批人民团体宣告它们拥护召开世界拥护和平大会。三月十八日及十九日在柏林举行的德国人民委员会会议通过决议，拥护世界文化工作者保卫和平大会国际联络委员会和国际民主妇女联盟关于召开世界和平大会的宣言。委员会号召德国一切民主、爱好和平与进步的力量参加全球人民的和平阵线。匈牙利作家联盟顷致电世界文化工作者保卫和平大会国际联络委员会，支持召开世界拥护和平大会的建议。匈牙利全国知识分子委员会亦召开非常会议，通过了宣告参加世界拥护和平大会的宣言。波兰新闻记者协会执行委员会于二十日通过决定，响应召开世界拥护和平大会的号召，宣告“它充分准备进一步从事不疲倦的斗争，反对军火商人的犯罪阴谋，使波兰和全人类获得巩固的和平”。华沙大学各研究会，克拉科农业专门学校和波兰学生团体联合会也通过决议，支持世界文化工作者保卫和平大会国际联络委员会和国际民主妇联的宣言。捷克斯洛伐克城境内的一切教会，除天主教外，均发表声明，宣布支持召开世界拥护和平大会。
