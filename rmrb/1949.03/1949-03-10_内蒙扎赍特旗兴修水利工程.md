Title: Water Conservancy Project in Zhaqi Special Banner, Inner Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1949-03-10
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Ulanhot, 6th -- The Water Conservancy Committee of the People's Government of Zhaqi Special Banner, Inner Mongolia, mobilized over 70,000 laborers and over 12,000 carts in early February to repair the Tao'er River water conservancy project, a tributary of the Nen River. The river dike project, including both diversion and damming, is a total of 10,000 meters in length and is expected to be completed by the end of May. Nine thousand hectares (1 hectare = 10 mu) of fertile land in Zhaqi Special Banner, Tailai, and Zhenqi counties/banners will be protected from flooding.

### 内蒙扎赍特旗兴修水利工程

1949-03-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　内蒙扎赍特旗兴修水利工程
    【新华社乌兰浩特六日电】内蒙古札赍特旗人民政府水利委员会，已于二月上旬动员七万余民工和一万二千余辆大车修嫩江支流洮儿河水利工程。该河堤工程包括顺水、堵口共长一万米突，预期于五月底竣工。扎赍特旗、泰来、镇赍等五县旗九万垧（每垧十亩）良田将可免除水患。
