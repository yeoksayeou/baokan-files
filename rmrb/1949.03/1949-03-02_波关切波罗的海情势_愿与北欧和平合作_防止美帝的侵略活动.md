Title: Poland Concerned About Baltic Situation, Willing to Cooperate Peacefully with Nordic Countries to Prevent US Imperialist Aggression

Author:

Date: 1949-03-02
Page: 3

　　Poland Concerned About Baltic Situation
  　Willing to Cooperate Peacefully with Nordic Countries
    Prevent US Imperialist Aggression
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th: TASS, Warsaw, 23rd: The Polish Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a communique stating that a conference of Polish ambassadors to Norway, Denmark, Sweden, and Finland, chaired by Polish Foreign Minister Modzelewski, was recently held here. The main topic of discussion was the activities of the imperialist bloc, which is attempting to drag the Scandinavian countries into the aggressive North Atlantic Treaty. The communique emphasized that Poland pays the closest attention to ensuring that the Baltic Sea does not become a base for imperialist conspiracies (which threaten the peaceful cooperation of the Baltic countries). The communique concluded that the meeting between the Foreign Minister and the Polish ambassadors to the Scandinavian countries also discussed the issue of establishing peaceful and constructive good-neighborly cooperation with these countries.

### 波关切波罗的海情势　愿与北欧和平合作  防止美帝的侵略活动

1949-03-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　波关切波罗的海情势
  　愿与北欧和平合作
    防止美帝的侵略活动
    【新华社陕北廿八日电】塔斯社华沙二十三日讯：波兰外交部发表公报称：波外长莫塞莱夫斯基主持的波兰驻挪、丹、瑞（典）、芬四国公使会议，最近曾在此间举行。会议讨论的主题为帝国主义集团的活动，帝国主义集团正企图把斯堪的纳维亚各国拉入侵略性的北大西洋公约的范围中。该公报力称：波兰对于使波罗的海不致成为帝国主义阴谋（这威胁着波罗的海各国的和平合作）的基地一事予以最关切的注意。该公报结语称：在外交部长与波兰共和国驻斯堪的纳维亚各国公使的会议上，还讨论了与这些国家建立和平的、建设性的睦邻合作的问题。
