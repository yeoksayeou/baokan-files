Title: Pinggong Mining Takeover Concludes, Construction Phase to Begin Soon
Author: Zhong
Date: 1949-03-27
Page: 2

　　Pinggong Mining Takeover Concludes
  　Construction Phase to Begin Soon
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] The takeover of industrial and mining enterprises in the Beiping area has come to an end, and the operational construction phase will begin soon. The Jibei Electric Power Company and Shijingshan Power Plant, the North China Iron and Steel Company and Shijingshan Iron Works, the North China Cement Company and Liulihe Cement Plant, the 70th Arsenal, and the Civilian Weapons Control and Distribution Committee and its subordinate repair shops, all major enterprises taken over by the Enterprise Department of the Beiping Military Control Commission's Materials Takeover Committee, have been transferred to the leadership of the Public Enterprises Department of the North China People's Government. However, the original military representative system will continue to be retained.
　　　　　　　(Zhong)

### 平工矿业接管告一段落　即将进入建设阶段
仲
1949-03-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　平工矿业接管告一段落
  　即将进入建设阶段
    【本报讯】北平区工矿业接管工作已告一段落，今后即开始进入经营建设阶段。北平军管会物资接管委员会企业处接管之冀北电力公司及石景山发电厂、华北钢铁公司及石景山制铁厂、华北水泥公司及琉璃河水泥厂、七十兵工厂、民间武器管制分配委员会及其所属各修械所等大企业机构，均已移交华北人民政府公营企业部领导。但原来之军事代表制仍继续保留。　　　　　　　（仲）
