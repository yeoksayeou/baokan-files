Title: The Vietnamese People Subscribe to Huge Public Bonds as Costs for the General Offensive
Author:
Date: 1949-03-21
Page: 3

　　The Vietnamese People
    Subscribe to Huge Public Bonds as Costs for the General Offensive
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 19th] Diantong News Agency, Bangkok, 16th: According to news from Vietnam, the Vietnamese people are subscribing to huge "Resistance Public Bonds" from the government as costs for a general counter-offensive against French imperialism. The Vietnamese General Staff Headquarters has announced that the general counter-offensive will be launched this year. In areas that have suffered from French rule, the subscription to public bonds is particularly enthusiastic. In the Lotte (transliteration) area, which is still partially occupied by French troops, the subscription amount has reached 180,000 Vietnamese currency.

### 越南人民  认购巨额公债作为总攻费用

1949-03-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　越南人民
    认购巨额公债作为总攻费用
    【新华社陕北十九日电】电通社曼谷十六日电：据越南来讯称，越南人民正在向政府认购巨额“抵抗公债”，作为对法帝国主义进行总反攻的费用，越南参谋总部已宣布，总反攻将在今年发动，在遭受过法国统治之害的地区，认购公债特别踊跃，在仍然被法军部分占领的洛得（译音）区，认购额达十八万越币。
