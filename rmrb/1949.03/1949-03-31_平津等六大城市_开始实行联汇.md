Title: Six Major Cities Including Pingjin to Implement Inter-Bank Exchange
Author: Yu
Date: 1949-03-31
Page: 1

　　Six Major Cities Including Pingjin
    Begin to Implement Inter-Bank Exchange
    [Our Dispatch] In order to facilitate the exchange of goods within the region and develop industry and commerce, the Head Office of the People's Bank of China has decided that the six branch offices in Ping, Jin, Zhang, Shi, Bao, and Tang will begin implementing inter-bank exchange on April 1st. The types of exchange are currently limited to mail transfer and draft transfer. Electronic transfer will also be launched between Pingjin in the near future. The exchange rate is temporarily set at 10 to 50 per mille, which will be determined by each branch office based on guidelines and specific circumstances.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Yu)

### 平津等六大城市　开始实行联汇
宇
1949-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　平津等六大城市
    开始实行联汇
    【本报讯】中国人民银行总行，为交流本区物资，发展工商业，决定平、津、张、石、保、唐六分行于四月一日起开始实行联汇。汇兑种类，目前暂限信汇、票汇两种。平津间于短期内并开办电汇。汇率暂规定为千分之十至千分之五十，由各分行根据方针及具体情况掌握订定。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（宇）
