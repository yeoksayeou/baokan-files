Title: British Ship "Gouvalia" Returns to Hong Kong Loaded with Cargo from Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1949-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 13th: The British ship "Gouvalia," which arrived in Tanggu on the 24th of last month from Hong Kong, sailed back to Hong Kong at 1:00 PM on the 11th. It carried a portion of medicinal materials, wool, and local products.

### 英轮“高华利”号由津载货返港

1949-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　英轮“高华利”号由津载货返港
    【新华社天津十三日电】上月二十四日由香港驶抵塘沽的英轮“高华利”号已于十一日下午一时驶回香港。载去药材、羊毛及土产品一部。
