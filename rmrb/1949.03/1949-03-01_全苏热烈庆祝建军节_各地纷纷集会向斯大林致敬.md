Title: All-Union Warmly Celebrates Army Day; Gatherings Held Across the Country to Pay Respects to Stalin
Author:
Date: 1949-03-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 27th: TASS reports: On February 23rd, the working people of the entire Soviet Union warmly celebrated the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Armed Forces.

　　In the capital, Moscow, mass meetings were held in factories, workshops, agencies, and schools. Theaters specially performed the best dramas thematically focused on current life for Soviet officers and soldiers. Moscow's finest actors and musicians went to the capital garrison troops to perform. Tens of thousands of people visited the Soviet Army Museum and the Museum of the Revolution. Celebratory meetings were held in all districts of Moscow. Participants in the meetings enthusiastically adopted letters and telegrams paying tribute to Stalin. In the evening, tens of thousands of people thronged Red Square, the streets, and the banks of the Moscow Canal. Thousands of fireworks lit up the sky, and a twenty-gun salute was fired, representing the Soviet capital's tribute to the heroic officers and soldiers of the Soviet Army and Navy.

　　In the heroic city of Leningrad, workers and soldiers joyfully celebrated this glorious festival. Military academies and the Leningrad Military District and the Baltic Sea Fleet held celebratory meetings.

　　In Minsk, the capital of the Belorussian Republic, workers and soldiers also held celebratory meetings. Army and Navy soldiers and officers visited historical battlefields. Soldiers of the Minsk garrison also joyfully gathered with former Belarusian guerrillas.

　　In Kiev, the capital of the Ukrainian Republic, buildings and streets were decorated with slogans, flags, and portraits of Marshal Stalin, the creator of the Soviet Army. Outstanding people from industry and agriculture in the Ukrainian Republic, along with the Soviet Army, held a grand meeting in the Kiev garrison.

　　The capitals of the Soviet republics, Kaliningrad, Rovno, Khabarovsk, Vladivostok, Port Lushun, Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa, all fired salutes to celebrate the festival.

　　It has become a tradition for the Soviet people to commemorate Army Day with new labor achievements. For example, the industrial plans for the first two months of this year have been completed ahead of schedule in Kharkov, in the Moscow mines and the Donbass coalfields, and in Ivanovo and Kuzbass.

### 全苏热烈庆祝建军节　各地纷纷集会向斯大林致敬

1949-03-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　全苏热烈庆祝建军节
  　各地纷纷集会向斯大林致敬
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】塔斯社讯：二月二十三日，全苏劳动人民热烈庆祝苏联武装部队建军三十一周年。
    在首都莫斯科，各工厂、作坊、机关、学校都举行了大会。各戏院特别为苏军官兵演出了以当前生活为主题的最好的戏剧。莫斯科最优秀的演员与乐师会到首都卫戍部队中去演奏。成千成万的人都去参观苏军博物馆和革命博物馆。莫斯科各个区都举行了庆祝大会。参加这次大会的人都热烈地通过向斯大林致敬的函电。到了晚间，成千成万的人又簇拥到红场上、大街上和莫斯科运河的堤岸上。几千个焰火把天空照得通明，礼炮齐鸣二十响，代表苏联首都向苏联陆海军的英勇官兵们致敬。
    在英雄之城列宁格勒，工人与士兵欢乐地庆祝这个光荣的节日。军事学校与列宁格勒军区及波维的海部队，都举行了庆祝大会。
    在白俄罗斯共和国的首都明斯克，工人和军人也举行了庆祝大会。陆海军士兵与军官们都去参观历史上的战场。明斯克卫戍部队的军人并与白俄罗斯的前游击队员欢聚。
    在乌克兰共和国的首都基辅，建筑物与大街上装饰着标语、旗帜及苏军的创造者斯大林大元帅的肖像。乌克兰共和国工农业中杰出的人民与苏军一同在基辅卫戍部队中举行了大会。
    苏联各加盟共和国首都、加里宁格勒、罗夫、伯力、海参岁、旅顺港、列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、悉瓦斯托波尔及敖德萨，都鸣放礼炮庆祝节日。
    苏联人民用新的劳动成绩来纪念苏军节，已经成了他们的传统。例如，今年开头两个月的工业计划，已在哈尔科夫，在莫斯科矿坑及顿巴斯煤田，在伊凡诺夫和库兹巴斯等地提前完成了。
