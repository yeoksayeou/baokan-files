Title: Jinpu Railway Jinan-Dezhou Section Connected North of Pingyuan

Author:
Date: 1949-03-28
Page: 1

　　Jinpu Railway Jinan-Dezhou Section
  　Connected North of Pingyuan
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 26th] The Dezhou-Jinan section of the Jinpu Railway was connected on the 24th north of Pingyuan County (approximately 30 kilometers from Dezhou).  With this, the northern section of the Jinpu Railway has been repaired and opened to traffic. Furthermore, passenger and freight trains between Tianjin and Cangxian will resume service starting on the 28th.

### 津浦路济南德县段　在平原以北接轨

1949-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　津浦路济南德县段
  　在平原以北接轨
    【新华社天津二十六日电】津浦铁路德县、济南段已于二十四日在平原县以北（距德县三十公里左右）接轨。至此，津浦路北段业已修通。又天津与沧县间的客货车将自二十八日起恢复。
