　　Xinhua News Agency Important Correction

　　The list of principal officials of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government issued by this agency on February 24 contained several important errors and omissions. It is reissued as follows: List of principal officials of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government: Acting Chairman of the Border Region Government: Liu Jingfan, Vice Chairman Yang Mingxuan, Secretary-General Wang Dacheng, Deputy Secretary-General Chang Lifu; Director of the Department of Civil Affairs Wang Ziyi, Deputy Director Zhang Yibo; Director of the Department of Finance Bai Rubing, Deputy Director Liu Yongru; Director of the Department of Education He Liancheng, Deputy Directors Jiang Longji, Zhao Zhongchi; Director of the Department of Agriculture Huo Zile, Deputy Directors Hui Zhongquan, Ding Zhongwen; Director of the Department of Public Security Zhou Xing, Deputy Directors Zhao Cangbi, Li Qiming; Director of the Department of Industry and Commerce Yu Jie, Deputy Directors Shi Weiran, Liu Zhuofu; Director of the Department of Transportation Gao Shiyi, Deputy Director Gao Dengbang; Director of the Department of Enterprise Jiang Chong＠, Deputy Directors Wu Shengxiu, Chen Zhiyuan; Manager of the Northwest Branch of the People's Bank of China Huang Yaguang, Deputy Managers Wang Lei, Zhang Dingfan; Chairman of the Northwest Financial and Economic Branch Committee He Long, Vice Chairman Jia Tuofu, Members Xi Zhongxun, Liu Jingfan, Chen Xiyun, Bai Rubing, Yu Jie, Huang Yaguang, Jiang Chong＠, Hui Zhongquan, Gao Shiyi, Fan Ziwen; Chairman of the People's Supervisory Committee Liu Jingfan, Members Ma Wenrui, Wang Ziyi, Ma Xiwu, Tan Bin, Zhao Cangbi, Fang Wenli; Chairman of the Ethnic Minority Affairs Committee Yang Mingxuan, Members Qi Tian (Mongolian), Zhao Cheng (Mongolian), Yang Jingren (Hui), Tian Bao (Tibetan), Cai Dengxiao (Hui); Chairman of the Comfort Committee Wang Ziyi, Vice Chairman Liu Yaozhi; The Border Region Higher Court is renamed the Border Region People's Court, with Ma Xiwu as the President and Qiao Songshan as the Vice President.
