Title: Shaanxi Central PLA Advances into Northwest Sanyuan, Annihilating One Division and One Regiment of the Enemy

Author:
Date: 1949-03-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 2nd. The People's Liberation Army, advancing into central Shaanxi, annihilated the 20th Division of the Kuomintang bandit army's 76th Army and the remnants of the 40th Regiment of the 14th Division in the areas of Koutou Town, Sanli Village, and Jiulong Mountain, 40 li northwest of Sanyuan, on the 1st. The enemy's 20th Division Commander Chu Jingya, Chief of Staff Zhang Qinghan, and Acting Regiment Commander of the 40th Regiment, Li Guangjun, were captured. The Kuomintang army's 76th Army has been annihilated by the People's Liberation Army three times, and this is the fourth time its 20th Division has been annihilated.

### 陕中我军挺进三原西北　歼敌一师一团

1949-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕中我军挺进三原西北
  　歼敌一师一团
    【新华社陕北二日电】向陕西中部进军的人民解放军，一日在三原西北四十里的口头镇、三里村、九龙山地区，歼灭国民党匪军七十六军的二十师和十四师的四十团残部。俘敌二十师师长褚静亚、参谋长张清汉和四十团代理团长李光军。国民党军七十六军已被解放军歼灭过三次，这次它的二十师是第四次被歼灭。
