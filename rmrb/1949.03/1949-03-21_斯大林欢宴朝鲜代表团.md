Title: Stalin Hosts North Korean Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-03-21
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 20th: TASS, Moscow: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Stalin, hosted a banquet for the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on March 17th in the Kremlin. Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Il-sung, Deputy Premier and Foreign Minister Park Hon-yong, and Deputy Premier Hong Myung-hee, among others, attended. The Soviet side was represented by Molotov, Malenkov, Beria, Voroshilov, Mikoyan, Kaganovich, Khrushchev, Kosygin, Vyshinsky, and Menshikov, among others. The hosts and guests enjoyed themselves immensely.
    On March 16th, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union, Doo Ying-hap, held a reception to welcome the North Korean government delegation currently in Moscow. Members of the North Korean delegation, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, Soviet officials including Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Mikoyan, representatives of the Soviet press, and ambassadors and ministers from several countries attended the reception.

### 斯大林欢宴朝鲜代表团

1949-03-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　斯大林欢宴朝鲜代表团
    【新华社陕北二十日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席斯大林于三月十七日在克里姆林宫欢宴朝鲜人民民主共和国政府代表团。朝鲜人民民主共和国总理金日成，副总理兼外长朴宪永及副总理洪命熹等均赴宴。苏方与宴者有：莫洛托夫、马林柯夫、贝利亚、伏罗希洛夫、米高扬、卡夫诺维奇、赫鲁什彻夫、科西金、维辛斯基及孟希科夫等。宾主尽欢而散。
    又三月十六日，朝鲜人民民主共和国驻苏联全权大使杜英合举行欢迎现在莫斯科的朝鲜政府代表团的招待会。出席招待会的有以总理金日成为首的朝鲜代表团团员，苏联部长会议副主席米高扬等苏联官员，苏联新闻界的代表，以及若干国家的大使与公使。
