Title: Tianjin Recruits Members for Southward Work Team; First Group of Over 400 Join

Author:
Date: 1949-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 18th -- The People's Liberation Army Work Team is recruiting members in Tianjin for the Southward Work Team. The first group of over 400 recruits joined the team yesterday. The second group of over 2,000 applicants took a written exam on the 16th. The original deadline for applications was the 15th, but on that day, over 900 people applied. The recruitment office decided to extend the deadline by one week. Those who apply during the extension will form the third group and will join the team at the same time as the second group.

### 天津招考南下工作团团员  第一批四百余人入团

1949-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　天津招考南下工作团团员
    第一批四百余人入团
    【新华社天津十八日电】人民解放军工作团在天津招考团员，第一批录取之四百余人已于昨日入团；第二批投考者二千余名亦已于十六日举行笔试。原定十五日为招收团员的最后一天，但是日报名者达九百余人，招生处乃决定延期一周。延期中报名者为第三批，将与第二批同时入团。
