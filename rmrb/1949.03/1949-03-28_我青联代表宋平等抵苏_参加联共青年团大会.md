Title: China Youth Federation Representatives Song Ping and Others Arrive in Soviet Union to Attend All-Union Leninist Young Communist League Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-03-28
Page: 3

　　China Youth Federation Representatives Song Ping and Others Arrive in Soviet Union to Attend All-Union Leninist Young Communist League Congress

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, March 27th: Moscow dispatch: Song Ping and Chen Shunyao, representatives of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation from the Chinese Liberated Areas, have arrived here to attend the 11th Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League. Delegations from the Democratic Youth League of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League have also arrived. Delegations from democratic youth organizations in Poland, Romania, Norway, and other countries will arrive on the 27th.

### 我青联代表宋平等抵苏　参加联共青年团大会

1949-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　我青联代表宋平等抵苏
    参加联共青年团大会
    【新华社北平二十七日电】莫斯科讯：中国解放区民主青年联合会代表宋平与陈舜瑶已抵达此间，参加苏联共产主义青年团第十一届代表大会。朝鲜人民民主共和国民主青年联盟，蒙古革命青年联盟，瑞典共产主义青年联合会代表团，亦已到达。波兰，罗马尼亚，挪威及其他国家民主青年的代表团，将于二十七日抵此。
