Title: National Student Congress Successfully Concludes; All-China Students' Federation Established; Leading Party, Government, and Military Organs of North China Host Banquet for All Delegates
Author:
Date: 1949-03-09
Page: 1

　　National Student Congress Successfully Concludes
  　All-China Students' Federation Established
    Leading Party, Government, and Military Organs of North China Host Banquet for All Delegates
    [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 6th] The Fourteenth National Student Congress, which opened on March 1st, successfully concluded today. The All-China Students' Federation, produced by the congress, was simultaneously announced as established. Over the past six days, student representatives from various regions attending the congress earnestly summarized the achievements and experiences of the Chinese student movement over the past thirty years; determined the future direction and tasks of the Chinese student movement; formulated the constitution of the All-China Students' Federation, and established a unified national student organization including both liberated areas and Kuomintang-controlled areas, and its leading body – the All-China Students' Federation. Guests attending the closing ceremony included Ye Jianying, Director of the Peiping Military Control Commission and Mayor; Zhou Yang, Director of the Propaganda Department of the North China Central Bureau of the CCP; Jiang Nanxiang, representative of the China Liberated Areas Youth Federation; Shen Junru, responsible person of the Democratic League; Zhang Xiruo, professor at Tsinghua University, and many others. At the meeting, the Northeast student delegation presented a flag to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, which was accepted by General Ye Jianying on their behalf. After the closing of the congress, the North China Central Bureau of the CCP, the North China People's Government, and the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army specially hosted a banquet for all student representatives at the Peking Hotel. Tonight, more than 20,000 university and middle school students in Peiping held a bonfire rally at the Democracy Square of Peking University to celebrate the establishment of the All-China Students' Federation.

### 全国学生代表大会胜利闭幕　全国学联成立  华北党政军首脑机关宴请全体代表

1949-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国学生代表大会胜利闭幕
  　全国学联成立
    华北党政军首脑机关宴请全体代表
    【新华社北平六日电】三月一日开幕的中华全国学生第十四届代表大会，已于今日闭幕。由大会产生的中华全国学生联合会于同时宣告成立。六天来，出席大会的各地学生代表，认真地总结了三十年来中国学生运动的成绩和经验；确定了今后中国学生运动的方针、任务；制订了中华全国学生联合会的会章，建立了包括解放区和国民党统治区的统一的全国学生组织和它的领导机构——中华全国学生联合会。参加大会闭幕式的来宾，有北平市军管会主任兼市长叶剑英，中共华北中央局宣传部长周扬，中国解放区青年联合会代表蒋南翔，民主同盟负责人沈钧儒，清华大学教授张奚若等多人。会上有东北学生代表团向毛主席和朱总司令献旗，当由叶剑英将军代表接受。大会闭幕后，中共华北中央局、华北人民政府和中国人民解放军华北军区特在北京饭店设宴招待全体学生代表。北平市二万多名大、中学生，今晚在北京大学民主广场，举行营火大会，庆祝全国学联的成立。
