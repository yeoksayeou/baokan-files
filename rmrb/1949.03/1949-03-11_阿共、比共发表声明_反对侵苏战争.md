Title: Argentine Communist Party and Belgian Communist Party Issue Statements Opposing War of Aggression Against the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-03-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 9th: According to a United Press report from Buenos Aires on the 5th: The Argentine Communist Party has issued a statement saying that if the United States and Britain launch a war of aggression against the Soviet Union, the Argentine Communist Party will do everything in its power to help the Soviet Union achieve victory.

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 9th: According to Agence France-Presse, Brussels, March 6th: The Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party passed a resolution stating: "If the servants of American imperialism, who now rule our country, drag Belgium into a war of aggression against the Soviet Union, the Communist Party and the vast majority of Belgian workers will refuse to participate in this shameful action and will fight by all means until the end of the most vicious enemies exposed before the Belgian people and humanity."

### 阿共、比共发表声明　反对侵苏战争

1949-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　阿共、比共发表声明
  　反对侵苏战争
    【新华社陕北九日电】据合众社布宜诺斯艾利斯五日讯：阿根廷共产党顷发表声明称：如果美英发动反苏的侵略战争，阿根廷共产党将竭全力使阿根廷人民帮助苏联取得胜利。
    【新华社陕北九日电】据法新社布鲁塞尔六日讯：比利时共产党中央委员会通过决议称：“假如现在统治着我国的美帝国主义的奴仆们把比利时拖入侵苏战争，共产党和极大多数的比利时工人，将拒绝参加这一可耻的行动，并将以一切方法进行战斗，直到那些被暴露在比利时人民和人类面前的最凶恶的敌人的末日为止。”
