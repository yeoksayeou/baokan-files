Title: People of Britain, Belgium, and Other Countries Enthusiastically Respond to the Call for a World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-03-24
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 22nd. Reports from various sources: The call for the World Peace Congress by the international liaison committee and other groups of the World Congress of Intellectuals for Peace has received enthusiastic responses from the people of Britain, Belgium, Hungary, Australia, and other countries.
    The British Cultural Workers' Peace Committee is organizing a delegation of several hundred people to attend the World Peace Congress in Paris. The committee issued a statement calling on all groups and individuals to send representatives or telegrams to support the congress. Signatories to the statement include Professor Bernal, Professor Heden, Archbishop of Canterbury Johnson, independent Labour Party MP Prisson, MPs Russell, Stapledon, Wood, physicist Worsster, and others.
    The Central Executive Committee of the Belgian Miners' Union passed a resolution welcoming the proposal to convene the World Peace Congress. Workers in Antwerp also passed similar proposals.
    In Eastern Europe, the Presidium of the Hungarian Trade Union Council passed a resolution on behalf of Hungary's 1.6 million organized laborers, welcoming the World Peace Congress. The resolution stated: The Hungarian working people want peace. The horrors of the two world wars have proven the value of peace. We lost 600,000 people in the war. Our country has 300,000 widows and orphans. We do not want to see our homes, which we have just rebuilt, turned into ruins again. We will work with the working people of the world to do everything possible to fight against those who declare war and threaten the whole world. We will defend the great achievements of our labor and construction, and defend our future. We represent the organized working people of Hungary in the struggle to defend peace and oppose new wars.
    In Australia, workers at a railway machinery factory near Sydney held a conference on the 18th, attended by Sharkey, General Secretary of the Australian Communist Party, who spoke. The conference passed a resolution declaring full support for the struggle to maintain world peace.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 22nd. London Dispatch: The British-Soviet Friendship Society issued a statement on the 19th, describing the North Atlantic Treaty as the most dangerous war preparation step taken by the US and British ruling groups. The association called on the British people to respond to Stalin's proposal for a peace pact.

### 对召开世界和平大会  英比等国人民热烈响应

1949-03-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　对召开世界和平大会
    英比等国人民热烈响应
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】各方报道：世界文化工作者保卫和平委员会国际联络委员会等团体召开的世界拥护和平大会的呼吁，获得英、比、匈、澳等国人民热烈响应。
    英国文化界保卫和平委员会正在组织人数百数的代表团，出席巴黎世界拥护和平大会。该委员会发表声明号召各团体与个人派代表或发电文支持大会。在声明上签名的有：伯纳尔教授、海登教授、坎特伯雷大主教约翰逊、独立的工党议员普立逊、国会议员罗素、斯达普列顿、伍德、物理学家伍斯特尔等。
    比利时矿工联合会中央执行委员会通过决议，表示欢迎召开世界拥护和平大会的倡议。安特卫普的工人也通过类似的建议。
    在东欧，匈牙利工会委员会主席团通过决议，代表匈牙利一百六十万有组织的劳动人民表示欢迎世界拥护和平大会。决议称：匈牙利劳动人民要和平。两次世界大战的恐怖，证明了和平的可贵。我们在战争中丧失了六十万人。我们国家有三十万寡妇和孤儿。我们不想使我们刚才重建起来的家室再次变成废墟。我们要和全世界劳动人民一起，尽一切力量进行斗争，反对那些宣布战争，威胁整个世界的人们。我们将保卫我们劳动建设的伟大成果，保卫我们的前途。我们代表匈牙利有组织的劳动人民参加保卫和平的斗争，反对新战争。
    在澳洲，悉尼附近铁路机器厂工人十八日举行大会，澳共总书记夏基出席讲话。大会通过决议。声明全力支持争取保持世界和平的斗争。
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】伦敦讯：英苏友好协会十九日发表声明，描述北大西洋公约为美英统治集团所采取的最危险的准备战争步骤。协会号召英国人民响应斯大林关于缔结和平公约的建议。
