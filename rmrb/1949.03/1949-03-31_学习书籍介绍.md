Author:
Date: 1949-03-31
Page: 4

Column: Introduction to Study Books

To Comrade Liu Jianxuan:

We are unable to answer your question because it is too general. We are introducing the following books: "Introduction to Dialectical Materialism" (by Hu Sheng), "Outline of New Philosophy" (by Cai Weiji et al., translated by Ai Siqi and Zheng Yili), "Dialectical and Historical Materialism" (by Stalin), "Dialectics of Nature," "Anti-Dühring" (by Engels), "Materialism and Empirio-Criticism" (by Lenin). Please read them carefully in order, from the shallow to the deep, and you will benefit more.

Social Service Group

### 学习书籍介绍

1949-03-31
第4版()
专栏：学习书籍介绍

　　学习书籍介绍刘剑轩同学：
    你所提的问题因过于笼统，我们无法作复。兹特介绍以下几册书籍：“辩证法唯物论入门”（胡绳著），“新哲学大纲”（蔡维基等著，艾思奇、郑易里合译），“辩证唯物论与历史唯物论”（斯大林著），“自然辩证法”，“反杜林论”（恩格斯著），“唯物论与经验批判论”（列宁）。请顺次精读，由浅入深，得益更大。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　社会服务组
