Title: Railway Workers' Representative Conference of Beiping District Formally Established on the 12th

Author:

Date: 1949-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 13th. The Railway Workers' Representative Conference of Beiping District was formally established on the 12th. The conference is composed of one representative each from the preparatory committees of the workers' unions of six units: Changxindian, Fengtai, Qianmen, Xizhimen, the former Pingjin Railway Bureau, and the Pinghan Railway Bureau. Li Lianshan, representative of Changxindian, was appointed as the director of the conference.

### 北平区铁路职工代表会十二日正式成立

1949-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平区铁路职工代表会十二日正式成立
    【新华社北平十三日电】北平区铁路职工代表会已于十二日正式成立。该会由长辛店、丰台、前门、西直门、前平津路局及平汉路局六个单位职工会筹备会各派代表一人组成，并由长辛店代表李连山任该会主任。
