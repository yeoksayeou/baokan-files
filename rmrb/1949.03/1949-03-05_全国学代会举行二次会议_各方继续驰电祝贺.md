Title: National Student Congress Holds Second Meeting, Congratulatory Telegrams Continue to Arrive from All Sides
Author:
Date: 1949-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, March 3rd. The second meeting of the 14th National Student Congress of the All-China Students' Federation continued in Peiping at 10:00 AM on the 2nd. Liu Aifeng, Deputy Minister of Education of the North China People's Government, was specially dispatched to the meeting to offer congratulations. Feng Wenbin, representative of the China Liberated Areas Youth Federation, delivered a report at the meeting entitled: "Combine with the Workers and Peasants, Serve the Workers and Peasants." Shi Jitao, Huang Hezhen, and Li Xiuzhen, three students from the Congress Presidium, gave reports on "Draft of the Current Tasks of the Primary and Secondary School Student Movement," "Student Movement in the Kuomintang-controlled Areas," and "Student Movement in the Liberated Areas," respectively, on behalf of the Congress Preparatory Committee. When the congratulatory letter, one hundred boxes of canned goods, four hundred and fifty towels, and four hundred and fifty pairs of socks from the Political Department of the Northeast Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army arrived at the congress, cheers resounded throughout the venue. The congress adjourned at 2:30 PM. In the evening, various delegations conducted group discussions.

All-China Federation of Trade Unions Congratulatory Telegram

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, March 3rd. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a congratulatory telegram to the 14th National Student Congress of the All-China Students' Federation:

Dear Representatives: Your convening of the 14th National Congress of the All-China Students' Federation at a time when the Chinese people's liberation struggle is developing victoriously is of great historical significance. Since the "May Fourth" Movement, Chinese intellectual youth have played a tremendous role in the entire Chinese revolutionary movement. From now on, we should call on workers and student youth across the country to unite more closely, strive to thoroughly realize the people's genuine peace and democracy, strive to intensify production to vigorously support the people's liberation struggle, and strive to rapidly liberate all of China. We wish your congress success.

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions

North China Government, North China Bureau, and Military Region Congratulatory Telegram

Beiping Telegram: The North China People's Government, the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sent the following full text of a congratulatory telegram to the entire body of representatives of the National Student Congress:

Comrades, representatives of the National Student Congress: Warmly celebrate the opening of your congress. Chinese students have always been an important force in the great revolutionary ranks of the Chinese people. At a time when Tianjin has been liberated and the victory of the national revolution is about to be realized, we believe that you will be more united and exert a more magnificent force in your studies and work. All the soldiers and civilians in North China are willing to work together with students and people across the country to completely eliminate the reactionary forces and build an independent, democratic, and prosperous New China. We respectfully wish the congress success.

The North China People's Government
The North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee
The North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army

### 全国学代会举行二次会议  各方继续驰电祝贺

1949-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国学代会举行二次会议
    各方继续驰电祝贺
    【新华社北平三日电】中华全国学生第十四届代表大会第二次会议，二日上午十时在平继续举行。华北人民政府特派教育部副部长刘皑风到会祝贺。中国解放区青年联合会代表冯文彬，在会上作了题为：“与工农群众结合，为工农群众服务”的报告。大会主席团史继陶、黄鹤桢、李秀真三同学，则代表大会筹委会分别作了关于“中小学生运动的当前任务草案”、“国民党统治区学生运动”和“解放区学生运动”的报告。当中国人民解放军东北野战军政治部送给全体代表的贺信，一百箱罐头，四百五十条毛巾，四百五十双袜子到达大会时，欢呼之声，响澈会场。大会至下午二时半休会。入晚，各代表团进行小组讨论。
    全国总工会贺电
    【新华社北平三日电】中华全国总工会致中华全国学生第十四届代表大会贺电：
    亲爱的代表们：正当中国人民解放斗争胜利地向前发展的时候，你们召开着全国学联第十四届代表大会，具有伟大的历史意义。中国知识青年自“五四”运动以来，在整个中国革命运动中是起了巨大的作用。今后我们应该号召全国工人与青年学生更密切的结合起来，为彻底实现人民的真正和平、民主而奋斗，为加紧生产大力支援人民解放斗争，为迅速地解放全中国而奋斗，祝你们大会的成功。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国总工会
    华北政府、华北局暨军区贺电
    【北平电】华北人民政府、中共中央华北局、中国人民解放军华北军区致中华全国学生第十四届代表大会贺电全文：
    全国学生代表大会全体代表同志：热烈庆祝你们代表大会的开幕。中国学生历来是中国人民伟大革命队伍的重要力量之一。当此平津已获解放，全国革命胜利就要实现的时候，相信你们将更加团结一致，在学习工作上发挥更雄伟的力量。华北全体军民愿与全国学生及全国人民在一起，为彻底消灭反动势力，建设独立、民主、富强的新中国而奋斗。敬祝大会成功。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　华北人民政府
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中共中央华北局
    　　　　　　　　　　　　中国人民解放军华北军区
