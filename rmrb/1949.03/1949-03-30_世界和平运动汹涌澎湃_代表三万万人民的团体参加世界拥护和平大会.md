Title: World Peace Movement Surges Forward: Groups Representing 300 Million People Participate in the World Congress of Supporters of Peace
Author:
Date: 1949-03-30
Page: 3
　　
　　World Peace Movement Surges Forward
    Groups Representing 300 Million People Participate in the World Congress of Supporters of Peace
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 29th] Paris dispatch: Peace-loving organizations from various countries are increasingly expressing their desire to participate in the World Congress of Supporters of Peace. As of the 25th, groups representing 300 million people have expressed this desire. News on the 27th stated that the Italian General Confederation of Labor, with 7 million members, the Italian Agricultural Workers' League, with 2 million members, as well as the Italian National Association of Victims of Fascism, the National Guerrilla Association, the Florence branch of the Human Rights League, and the National Committee of the Workers' Management Factory Movement have declared their willingness to participate in the World Congress of Supporters of Peace. The Argentine Women's League, the Women's Cultural League, the Youth Committee, the Youth Patriotic League, the Young Women's Patriotic League, the Human Rights League, and the Women's Department of the Argentine Communist Party have all issued similar statements. In addition, the following have also issued statements: the World Assembly of Youth, the University Labor Federation, and the Communist Youth League of Great Britain; the Democratic Women's League, the Arts and Culture League, and the Society for Cultural Relations with the Soviet Union of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; the Austrian Democratic Women's League; the Albanian Writers' Union; many French organizations; and the Moroccan Teachers' Union. Grand rallies in support of peace have been held in Brno, Bratislava, and other cities in Czechoslovakia.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 27th] TASS dispatch: Peace groups in Copenhagen, Denmark, organized a demonstration of thousands of people in front of the Danish Parliament on the 24th. Demonstrators shouted slogans such as "Down with the North Atlantic Treaty!", "Support Peace!", and "We don't want an alliance with the United States!" Demonstrators cheered for Communist leader Larsen. A delegation appointed by the demonstrators submitted a proposal to the Danish Prime Minister, demanding that the issue of Denmark's participation in the North Atlantic Treaty be discussed in Parliament. Copenhagen newspapers pointed out that the movement against participating in the North Atlantic Treaty has made the government very uneasy, and the police guarding the Parliament have been increased. It is reported that Danish Foreign Minister Rasmussen recently went to the United States to negotiate with the US government on Denmark's participation in the North Atlantic Treaty.

### 世界和平运动汹涌澎湃  代表三万万人民的团体参加世界拥护和平大会

1949-03-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　世界和平运动汹涌澎湃
    代表三万万人民的团体参加世界拥护和平大会
    【新华社北平二十九日电】巴黎讯：世界各国爱好和平的团体，表示愿望参加世界拥护和平大会者，日益增多。截至二十五日止，已有代表着三万万人民的团体作此表示。二十七日消息，又有拥有七百万会员的意大利总工会、拥有二百万会员的意大利农业工人联盟、以及意大利的全国法西斯受难者协会、全国游击队协会、人权同盟佛罗伦萨分会、工人管理工厂运动全国委员会声明愿参加世界拥护和平大会。阿根廷妇女联盟、妇女文化同盟、青年委员会、青年爱国者同盟、女青年爱国者联盟、人权同盟、阿根廷共产党妇女部都发表了同样声明。此外，发表声明的还有：英国的世界青年会议、大学劳动联盟和共产主义青年团，朝鲜人民民主共和国的民主妇女联盟、艺术文化同盟、对苏文化关系协会，奥地利民主妇女联盟，阿尔巴尼亚作家联盟，法国许多团体和摩洛哥教师联合会。在捷克斯洛伐克的布尔诺、布拉的斯拉发及其他城市，都举行盛大的拥护和平大会。
    【新华社北平二十七日电】塔斯社讯：丹京哥本哈根的和平团体，二十四日在丹麦议会之前组织数千人的示威。示威者高呼：“打倒北大西洋公约！”“拥护和平！”“我们不要和美国联盟！”等口号。示威者向共产党领袖拉荪欢呼。示威者委派的代表团，向丹麦总理提出建议，要求在议会中讨论丹麦参加北大西洋公约的问题。哥本哈根报纸指出，人民反对参加北大西洋公约的运动，使政府非常不安，守卫议会的警察已经增加。按丹麦外长拉斯穆荪最近曾赴美，与美政府谈判丹麦参加北大西洋公约的问题。
