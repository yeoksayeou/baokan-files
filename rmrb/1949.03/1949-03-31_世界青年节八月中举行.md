Title: World Youth Festival to be Held in Mid-August
Author:
Date: 1949-03-31
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 29th: Diantong News Agency reports: The Preparatory Committee for the World Youth Festival, jointly organized by the World Federation of Democratic Youth and the International Union of Students, announced on the 28th that the World Youth Festival will commence on August 14th this year with a gathering at the Leninváros Stadium in Budapest. The 10th World University Games will be inaugurated the following day by the President of Hungary, Szakasits Árpád. The program includes a parade of working youth, a solidarity day for world youth armies and the fighting youth of China, Greece, and Spain – Colonial Youth Day, and a peace demonstration will also be held.

### 世界青年节八月中举行

1949-03-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　世界青年节八月中举行
    【新华社北平二十九日电】电通社讯：世界民主青年联盟与国际学联共同组织的世界青年节筹备委员会二十八日宣布：世界青年节于今年八月十四日开始时，将在布达佩斯的伦那渥县体育场举行集会。第十次世界大学运动会将于第二日由匈牙利总统斯查卡西兹揭幕。日程中有劳动青年的游行，有世界青军与中国、希腊、西班牙战斗青年的团结日——殖民地青年日，并将举行和平示威。
