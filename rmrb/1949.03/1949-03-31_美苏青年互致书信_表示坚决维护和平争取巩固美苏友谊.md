Title: US and Soviet Youth Exchange Letters, Expressing Firm Determination to Safeguard Peace and Strive to Consolidate US-Soviet Friendship
Author:
Date: 1949-03-31
Page: 3

　　US and Soviet Youth Exchange Letters
    Expressing Firm Determination to Safeguard Peace and Strive to Consolidate US-Soviet Friendship
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 29th】Moscow Dispatch: The Philadelphia branch of the American Progressive Youth Association recently wrote an open letter to the youth of Leningrad, expressing the desire of American progressive youth to uphold peace and US-Soviet friendship. The youth of Leningrad convened a congress of two thousand representatives on the 26th to discuss this open letter, and unanimously passed a reply letter to the Philadelphia branch of the American Progressive Youth Association, which stated: "Dear friends: The young men and women of our heroic city of Leningrad have read your letter and extend warm greetings to you, the progressive youth of Philadelphia. We wholeheartedly support your desire to strive for peace and to consolidate friendship with Soviet youth. The youth of Leningrad, like all Soviet youth, support the cause of peace, democracy, and progress."

### 美苏青年互致书信  表示坚决维护和平争取巩固美苏友谊

1949-03-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　美苏青年互致书信
    表示坚决维护和平争取巩固美苏友谊
    【新华社北平二十九日电】莫斯科讯：美国进步青年协会费城分会最近写了一封公开信给列宁格勒的青年，表示美国进步青年拥护和平与美苏友谊的愿望。列宁格勒青年在二十六日召集了二千人的代表大会，讨论了这封公开信，并一致通过给美国进步青年协会费城分会的复信，内称：“亲爱的朋友们：我们英雄的城市列宁格勒的男女青年读了你们的来信，谨向你们费城进步青年致热烈的敬礼。我们全心全意支持你们争取和平，争取巩固对苏联青年友谊的愿望。列宁格勒青年和全体苏联青年同样支持和平、民主与进步的事业。”
