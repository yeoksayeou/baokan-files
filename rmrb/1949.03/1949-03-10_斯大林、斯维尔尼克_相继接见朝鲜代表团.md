Title: Stalin and Shvernik Receive Korean Delegation in Succession
Author:
Date: 1949-03-10
Page: 3
　　
　　Stalin and Shvernik
  　Receive Korean Delegation in Succession

    【Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 8th】TASS, Moscow: On March 5th, Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, received the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, including Premier Kim Il-sung, Vice-Premier Park Hon-yong, Hong Myung-hui, Chairman of the State Planning Commission Chung Joon-taek, Minister of Commerce Chang Shi-woo, Minister of Education Baek Nam-un, and Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Kim Jung-joo. Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky, Soviet Ambassador to Korea Shtykov, and Korean Ambassador to the Soviet Union Doo Young-hap (transliteration) were present. On the same day, Nikolai Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, received the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Gromyko, Soviet Ambassador to Korea Shtykov, and Korean Ambassador to the Soviet Union Doo Young-hap (transliteration) were present.

### 斯大林、斯维尔尼克　相继接见朝鲜代表团

1949-03-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　斯大林、斯维尔尼克
  　相继接见朝鲜代表团
    【新华社陕北八日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：三月五日，苏联部长会议主席斯大林接见朝鲜人民民主共和国政府代表团，该代表团包括总理金日成、副总理朴宪永及洪仑熹、国家计划委员会主席郑准泽、商业部长张时雨、教育部长白南云及邮电部长金廷柱。苏外长维辛斯基与苏驻朝鲜大使西地科夫与朝鲜驻苏大使杜英合（译音）均列席。同日，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席斯维尔尼克接见朝鲜人民民主共和国政府代表团。苏外交部副部长葛罗米柯与苏驻朝鲜大使西地科夫、朝鲜驻苏大使杜英合（译音）均列席。
