Title: All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions Issues Decision Calling for Expansion of Socialist Emulation
Author:
Date: 1949-03-08
Page: 3

　　All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions Issues Decision
　　Calling for Expansion of Socialist Emulation
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 6th: TASS reports: The Presidium of the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions issued a decision on the 3rd, calling on all male and female workers, foremen, technicians, engineers, and staff to fully support the initiative of the most advanced enterprises in the country and to further expand socialist emulation, using new labor victories to welcome the 10th All-Soviet Congress of Trade Unions scheduled to open on April 19th. The decision pointed out: The decision of the Soviet Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union to "reduce the prices of consumer goods in state-owned retail trade again from March 1, 1949" has been warmly welcomed throughout the country and will undoubtedly stimulate a further surge in national productivity and socialist emulation.

### 全苏职工会发布决定　号召扩大社会主义竞赛

1949-03-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　全苏职工会发布决定
  　号召扩大社会主义竞赛
    【新华社陕北六日电】塔斯社讯：全苏职工会中央委员会主席团于三日发布决定，号召全体男女工人、领班、技师、工程师及职员，全力支持全国最先进企业的倡议，更进一步地扩大社会主义竞赛，以新的劳动胜利来迎接定于四月十九日开幕的全苏职工会第十届代表大会。该决定指出：苏联部长会议与苏联共产党中央“自一九四九年三月一日起再次减低国营零售贸易中消费品价格”的决定，已受到全国热烈的欢迎，并且无疑地将激起全国生产力和社会主义竞赛的进一步高涨。
