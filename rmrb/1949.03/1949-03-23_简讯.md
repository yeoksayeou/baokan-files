　　Brief News
    [Zhangjiakou Electric Dispatch] The Zhangjiakou City Sixth District Trade Union Preparatory Office, in order to raise the awareness of the working class and implement the labor-capital policy, has run two training classes for workers in private flour mills. The first session, with 59 participating workers (including 5 well workers), and the second session, with 53 participating workers, totaling 112 people, were held from February 18th to February 28th. Each session lasted five days, with one hour of lectures and one hour of discussion each day, using the workers' off-work time from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM. The workers' enthusiasm for learning was very high.

### 简讯

1949-03-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　简讯
    【张垣电】张家口市六区工会筹备办事处为提高工人阶级觉悟，贯彻劳资政策，开办了两期私营面粉厂工人训练班。于二月十八日开始至二十八日结束，参加受训工人第一期五十九人（内有五个水井工人）第二期五十三人，共一百一十二人。每期五天，每天一小时的讲授，一小时的讨论，均利用晚上六时至八时工人歇班的时间进行，工人的学习情绪很高。
