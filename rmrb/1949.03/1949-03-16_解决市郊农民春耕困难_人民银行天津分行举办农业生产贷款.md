Title: Solving the Spring Planting Difficulties of Suburban Farmers: People's Bank of China Tianjin Branch Offers Agricultural Production Loans
Author:
Date: 1949-03-16
Page: 2

　　To solve the difficulties faced by poor farmers in the Tianjin suburbs regarding seeds, fertilizer, and agricultural tools during spring planting, the Cooperation Department of the People's Bank of China Tianjin Branch will provide agricultural production loans. The department has dispatched personnel to conduct key investigations to formulate specific measures accordingly.
    [Tianjin Electric]

### 解决市郊农民春耕困难  人民银行天津分行举办农业生产贷款

1949-03-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　解决市郊农民春耕困难
    人民银行天津分行举办农业生产贷款
    【天津电】人民银行天津分行合作部，为解决市郊贫苦农民春耕时种籽、肥料及农具的困难，将举办农业生产贷款。该部已派出业务人员作重点调查，据以制定具体办法。
