Title: Austrian Communist Party Statement: The Austrian People Stand on the Side of the Soviet Union and Will Never Shed Blood for the Dollar

Author:
Date: 1949-03-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 6th: The Vienna-based "People's Opinion," the central organ of the Austrian Communist Party, published a statement saying: The Austrian people will not oppose their socialist motherland, nor will they shed blood for the dollar. The statement continued: "If Austria is dragged into a war against the Soviet Union and the people's democracies by the instigators of a new imperialist war, then the Austrian people, first and foremost the Austrian working class, will stand on the side of the Soviet Union against the aggressors." The statement condemned the Austrian government's coalition of the Christian Democratic Party and the Socialist Party for attempting to make Austria part of the Western European war camp.

### 奥共声明：奥人民站在苏联一边  决不会去为美元流血

1949-03-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　奥共声明：
    奥人民站在苏联一边  决不会去为美元流血
    【新华社陕北六日电】电通社维也纳三日讯：奥地利共产党中央机关报“人民舆论”报刊登声明，内称：奥地利人民不会反对社会主义祖国，他们也不会去为美元流血。声明继称：“如果奥地利被新的帝国主义战争的挑拨者拖入一个反对苏联和人民民主国家的战争中，那么，奥地利人民，首先是奥地利的工人阶级，将站在苏联一边，反对侵略者。”声明斥责奥国政府党天主教党——社会党联盟企图使奥地利成为西欧战争阵营的一部分。
