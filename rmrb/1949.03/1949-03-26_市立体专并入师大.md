Title: Municipal Physical Education College Merged into Normal University
Author:
Date: 1949-03-26
Page: 1

　　Municipal Physical Education College Merged into Normal University
    [Report from this newspaper] On the 24th, three representatives from the Peiping Military Control Commission, along with a group of seven from the Military Control Liaison Group stationed at the Normal University, went to the Municipal Physical Education College to carry out the takeover. At the same time, it was announced that after the takeover, the Physical Education College would be merged into the Physical Education Department of the Normal University. Both schools have agreed that, with more concentrated human and material resources in the future, they hope to make contributions to new physical education.

### 市立体专并入师大

1949-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　市立体专并入师大
    【本报讯】二十四日北平军管会代表三人，偕驻师大军管联络组一行七人，赴市立体育专科学校履行接管。同时，宣布体专接管之后，合并于师大体育系。两校并已研究同意，今后在人力物力更加集中的条件下，希望对于新的体育教育有所供献。
