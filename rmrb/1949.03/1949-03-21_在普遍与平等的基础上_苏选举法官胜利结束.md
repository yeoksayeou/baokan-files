Title: Soviet Election of Judges Victoriously Concluded on the Basis of Universality and Equality
Author:
Date: 1949-03-21
Page: 3

　　On the Basis of Universality and Equality
    Soviet Election of Judges Victoriously Concluded
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th] TASS reports: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR recently received reports from the Presidiums of the Supreme Soviets of the various constituent republics on the results of the election of people's judges and people's assessors throughout the Soviet Union from December of last year to February of this year. The vast majority of working people participated in the election. All of the people's judges and people's assessors elected were candidates nominated by the alliance of party members and non-party people. Among those elected, women accounted for 39.2 percent, members and probationary members of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union accounted for 47 percent, and non-party people accounted for 53 percent. Izvestia pointed out: The election of people's courts, conducted through secret ballot on the basis of universal, direct, and equal suffrage, constitutes a new victory for Soviet socialist democracy. The elections in various regions were conducted in an atmosphere of high political enthusiasm among the working people, and this election constitutes a new and vivid example of the indestructible spiritual and political unity of the Soviet people, and the unity of the Soviet people with the Bolshevik Party and the great Stalin.

### 在普遍与平等的基础上  苏选举法官胜利结束

1949-03-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　在普遍与平等的基础上
    苏选举法官胜利结束
    【新华社陕北二十日电】据塔斯社讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团最近接到各加盟共和国最高苏维埃主席团的报告，报告全苏自去年十二月至今年二月间选举人民法官和人民陪审官的结果。全部选民工人多都参加了选举。选出的人民法官与人民陪审官全部都是党与非党人民联盟所提出的候选人。当选者中，妇女占百分之三十九点二，苏联共产党党员与候补党员占百分之四十七，非党人民占百分之五十三。消息报指出：在普遍、直接与平等选举的基础上以秘密投票进行的人民法庭选举，构成了苏维埃社会主义民主的新的胜利。各地的选举都是在劳动人民的高度政治积极性的空气中进行的，这个选举构成了苏联人民的不可摧毁的精神上与政治上的团结，以及苏联人民和布尔什维克党及伟大的斯大林团结的新的生动实例。
