Title: Tianjin Railway Bureau Establishes Communist Youth League Branch

Author:

Date: 1949-03-11
Page: 2

　　Tianjin Electric Telegram: The Tianjin New Democratic Youth League Railway Bureau branch has been formally established. At the first general meeting of all League members held by the League on the 15th, it was decided that: at present, without hindering work, the branch should organize young workers for study, cultural entertainment and other activities; extensively carry out League propaganda to enable young people to understand the organizational nature of the League and its relationship with young people; recruit outstanding young workers into the League, investigate and study the lives of young workers, commend models, improve production morale, and call on all young workers to play an exemplary leading role in their work.

### 津市铁道处建立青年团支部

1949-03-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　津市铁道处建立青年团支部
    【天津电】天津市新民主主义青年团铁道处团支部已正式成立。在该团十五日所召开的首次全体团员会议上，决定：目前要在不妨碍业务下，组织青工学习、文化娱乐及其他活动；普遍的展开团的宣传，使青年了解团的组织性质及与青年的关系；吸收优秀青年工人入团，调查研究青工生活，表扬模范、提高生产情绪，号召全体职工青年在工作中起模范带头作用。
