Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the Opening of the National Women's Congress, Hoping to Unite Women Nationwide to Step Up Production and Support the Front

Author:
Date: 1949-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 23rd: The CPC Central Committee today sent a telegram to congratulate the opening of the First National Women's Congress of the People's Republic of China in Beiping on the 24th. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To the representatives of the First National Women's Congress of China:

At this moment when the Chinese people's revolution is about to achieve its final victory throughout the country, it is a matter of great significance that you are holding the First Chinese Women's Congress in Beiping. For a long time, Chinese women have suffered greatly under the multiple oppressions of feudalism, imperialism, and bureaucratic capitalism. Since the May Fourth Movement in 1919, for thirty years, the working women and revolutionary intellectual women of China have devoted themselves in large numbers to the great struggle for national independence, democratic freedom, and the liberation of women themselves. In these struggles, Chinese women have become increasingly awakened and increasingly united, becoming a powerful component of the Chinese people's revolutionary front. Now that the Chinese revolution is about to achieve a national victory, and large-scale construction work in the new China is either underway or about to begin, the Chinese women, who account for half of China's population, have greater responsibilities in the current and future great struggles, whether in the production and exchange of industry, handicrafts, and agriculture, or in science, technology, education, literature and art, hygiene, childcare, and other construction work, than at any time in the past. It is hoped that your congress can summarize the experience of the past women's movement and formulate new women's work plans, so as to more closely unite all Chinese women, especially women on the industrial front, the agricultural front, and the cultural front, in the struggle for the liberation of all China, for the construction of the new China, for true peace in China, and for lasting peace in the world, and strive for the final victory. We wish your congress success.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　  March 23, 1949

### 中共中央委员会  电贺全国妇代大会开幕  望团结全国妇女加紧生产支前

1949-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央委员会
    电贺全国妇代大会开幕
    望团结全国妇女加紧生产支前
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】中共中央本日致电贺二十四日在北平举行的中华全国妇女第一届代表大会。电文如下：
    中华全国妇女第一届代表大会诸位代表：
    当此中国人民革命即将在全国范围内获得最后胜利的时候，你们在北平举行第一届中国妇女代表大会，这是一件具有伟大意义的事。中国妇女长久以来在封建主义、帝国主义和官僚资本主义的重重压迫下，受苦特甚。自一九一九年“五四”运动以来，三十年间，中国的劳动妇女和革命的知识妇女即大批地投入为民族独立、民主自由及为妇女自身解放的伟大的斗争。在这些斗争中，中国妇女已日益觉醒，日益团结，成为中国人民革命战线有力的组成部分。现在中国革命即将获得全国胜利，新中国大规模的建设工作，或已在进行，或即将开始，数达中国人口一半的中国妇女，在目前和未来的伟大斗争中，无论在工业、手工业和农业的生产和交换方面，在科学、技术、教育、文艺、卫生、保育和其他建设工作方面，中国妇女所巳负和将负的责任，均比过去任何时期为大。希望你们的大会，能够总结过去妇女运动的经验，制定新的妇女工作方案，以便更加紧密地团结全中国妇女，尤其是工业战线、农业战线、文化战线上的妇女，在为全中国的解放，为新中国的建设，为中国的真正和平及世界的持久和平的斗争中，争取最后的胜利。祝你们的大会成功。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国共产党中央委员会
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　  一九四九年三月二十三日
