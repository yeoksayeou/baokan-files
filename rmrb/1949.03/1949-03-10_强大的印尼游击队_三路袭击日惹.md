Title: Powerful Indonesian Guerrillas Launch Three-pronged Attack on Yogyakarta
Author:
Date: 1949-03-10
Page: 3

　　Powerful Indonesian Guerrillas
　　Three-pronged Attack on Yogyakarta
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 8th: Batavia dispatch: Strong Indonesian guerrillas launched a three-pronged attack on Yogyakarta, the capital of the Indonesian Republic occupied by Dutch troops, before dawn on the 1st of this month, and engaged in fierce fighting with the Dutch troops for two days. The guerrillas carried rifles, machine guns, and mortars, and the targets of the attack were Dutch barracks in the city center. Dutch troops suffered heavy casualties from intense fire from the jungles surrounding Yogyakarta. The Dutch authorities refused to disclose detailed information about the battle and casualties.

### 强大的印尼游击队　三路袭击日惹

1949-03-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　强大的印尼游击队
    三路袭击日惹
    【新华社陕北八日电】巴达维亚讯：强大的印尼游击队于本月一日拂晓前对荷军占领的印尼共和国首都日惹，发动三路袭击，并与荷军激战两日。游击队携有步枪、机枪与迫击炮，攻击目标为市中心的荷军兵营。荷军遭到来自日惹周围丛林的密集炮火的杀伤。荷兰当局拒绝透露关于战事与伤亡的详细情况。
