Title: Norwegian Government Rejects Soviet Proposal, Decides to Join Aggressive Alliance Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1949-03-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 7th -- Reports from various sources: The Norwegian Labor Party (Social Democratic Party) government, disregarding the opposition of the Norwegian Communist Party members of parliament, officially rejected on March 3rd the Soviet Union's proposal to conclude a treaty of mutual non-aggression between the Soviet Union and Norway, and decided to participate in the "North Atlantic Treaty" negotiations. Norwegian Ambassador to the United States, Morgenstierne, issued a statement in Washington on the 3rd, stating that he would attend the first formal meeting of the "North Atlantic Treaty" on the 4th. Morgenstierne revealed that when the Norwegian Parliament passed the aforementioned two decisions of the Labor Party government in a secret meeting on the evening of the 3rd, all eleven Norwegian Communist Party members of parliament voted against them.

### 挪政府竟拒绝苏联建议　决定参加侵略联盟谈判

1949-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　挪政府竟拒绝苏联建议
  　决定参加侵略联盟谈判
    【新华社陕北七日电】各方报道：挪威劳工党（社会民主党）政府，不顾挪威共产党国会议员的反对，已于三月三日正式拒绝苏联所提苏挪两国订立互不侵犯协定的建议，并已决定参加“北大西洋公约”的谈判。挪威驻美大使摩根斯端于三日在华盛顿发表声明称，他将于四日首次出席“北大西洋公约”的正式会议。摩根斯端透露，当挪威议会在三日晚秘密会议中通过劳工党政府前述两项决定时，挪威共产党议员十一人均投反对票。
