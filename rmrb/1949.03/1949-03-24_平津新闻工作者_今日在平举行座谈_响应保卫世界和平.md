Title: Tianjin-Beijing Journalists Hold Forum Today in Beijing to Respond to the Defense of World Peace
Author:
Date: 1949-03-24
Page: 1

　　Tianjin-Beijing Journalists
    Hold Forum Today in Beijing
    Responding to the Defense of World Peace
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Liberated Areas Journalists Association and the China Youth Journalists Society, in order to support the world peace movement, have jointly initiated an invitation to journalists in Tianjin and Beijing to hold a forum at the Hua'an Hotel in Beijing on the 24th to discuss specific measures to respond to the World Peace Congress.

### 平津新闻工作者  今日在平举行座谈  响应保卫世界和平

1949-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　平津新闻工作者
    今日在平举行座谈
    响应保卫世界和平
    【本报讯】解放区新闻记者联合会和中国青年记者学会，为拥护世界和平运动，特联名发起邀请在平津两地的新闻工作者于二十四日假北平华安饭店举行座谈，讨论响应世界拥护和平大会的具体办法。
