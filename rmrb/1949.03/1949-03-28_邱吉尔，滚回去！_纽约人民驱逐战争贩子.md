Title: Churchill, Get Out! New York People Expel Warmonger
Author:
Date: 1949-03-28
Page: 3

　　Churchill, Get Out!
    New York People Expel Warmonger
    [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 26th] The warmonger Churchill has been angrily expelled by the American people in the United States. He was invited to Washington by Truman. When he passed through New York on the 23rd, hundreds of demonstrators, holding signs demanding peace, shouted around him: "Churchill, go back to England!" The US government dispatched many motorcycle police to protect Churchill to the hotel, but the people of New York still shouted behind him: "Churchill, get out! Our warmongers will follow you out soon!" Churchill prepared to deliver a speech at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. After receiving this news, the United Electrical, Radio and Machine Workers of America (UE), the Fur and Leather Workers Union, public servants, and other progressive organizations in Boston, Massachusetts, jointly issued a statement opposing Churchill's visit.

### 邱吉尔，滚回去！　纽约人民驱逐战争贩子

1949-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　邱吉尔，滚回去！
    纽约人民驱逐战争贩子
    【新华社北平二十六日电】战争贩子邱吉尔在美遭到了美国人民愤怒的斥逐。他系应杜鲁门的邀请赴华盛顿。当他在二十三日经过纽约时，数百示威者手执要求和平的标语，在他周围高呼：“邱吉尔，滚回英国去！”美国政府出动了许多乘摩托车的警察保护邱吉尔往旅馆去，但纽约人民仍在他背后大喊：“邱吉尔，滚出去！我们的战争贩子不久也会跟着你滚出去！”邱吉尔准备往麻萨诸塞州理工学院发表演说，麻州波士顿产联用具工人工会及毛皮工业工人，公务员和其他进步组织，得到了这个消息后，即联合发表声明反对邱吉尔的访问。
