Title: Democratic Youth Delegations from Various Countries Arrive in Moscow One After Another to Attend the Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League

Author:
Date: 1949-03-29
Page: 3

　　Democratic Youth Delegations from Various Countries
    Arrive in Moscow One After Another
    To Attend the Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 28th】TASS, Moscow, March 27th: Democratic youth delegations from various countries have successively arrived in Moscow to attend the 11th Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League. The representatives who arrived here on March 26th include: Grozdev, Chairman of the Bulgarian People's Youth League; Stasia, Secretary of the Polish Youth League; Foconio, Chairman of the Romanian Labor Youth League; Glejzler, Chairman of the Czech Youth League, and Sikora, Chairman of the Slovak Youth League; Alia, General Secretary of the Albanian People's Youth Organization; Galigo, leader of the Republican Spanish Democratic Youth League; Berlinguer, Chairman of the Italian "Youth Alliance"; Figet, General Secretary of the French Republican Youth League; Brooks, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of Great Britain; Honick and Warning, Chairmen of the "Free German Youth" League; Kunisen and Larsen, activists of the Norwegian Communist Youth League, etc. When the above-mentioned representatives arrived, members of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, and the Capital Communist Youth League sent representatives to welcome them.

### 各国民主青年代表团  陆续抵达莫斯科　出席联共青年团代表大会

1949-03-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　各国民主青年代表团
    陆续抵达莫斯科
    出席联共青年团代表大会
    【新华社北平二十八日电】塔斯社莫斯科二十七日讯：各国民主青年代表团陆续抵达莫斯科，出席苏联共产主义青年团第十一届代表大会。三月二十六日抵达此间的代表有保加利亚青年人民联盟主席格罗杰兹夫，波兰青年联盟书记史塔西亚，罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟主席佛科尼奥，捷克青年联盟主席格莱兹勒与斯洛伐克青年联盟主席西科拉，阿尔巴尼亚人民青年组织总书记阿里亚，共和西班牙民主青年联盟领导加利哥，意大利“青年同盟”主席伯林格尔，法兰西共和青年联盟总书记菲格特，英国共产主义青年团中央委员会书记布鲁克斯，“自由德国青年”联盟主席荷尼克与瓦尔宁，挪威共产主义青年联盟积极分子库尼森与拉尔森等。全苏共产主义青年团中央委员，苏联青年反法西斯委员会与首都共产主义青年团于上述代表抵达时，曾派代表前往欢迎。
