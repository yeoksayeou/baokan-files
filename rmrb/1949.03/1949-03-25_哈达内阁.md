　　The Hatta Cabinet
    --Hatta is an Indonesian Muslim, a major capitalist, and the leader of the Nationalist Party, representing the interests of princes, nobles, the big bourgeoisie, and big landlords. After the reactionary upper echelons of Indonesia accepted the humiliating Renville Agreement on January 17, 1948, U.S. imperialism selected Hatta to form a cabinet. This cabinet excluded the Communist Party, the Socialist Youth League, the Labor Party, and other left-wing revolutionary parties, and was composed of three Nationalist Party members, four Muslim Party members (representing the interests of feudal groups), one Christian Party member, one Catholic Party member, and seven "non-party" right-wing elements. After coming to power, it pursued a reactionary policy of selling out national interests to the United States and the Netherlands, opposing the Soviet Union, and suppressing the domestic people's revolutionary movement, becoming a public enemy of the Indonesian people.

### 哈达内阁

1949-03-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　哈达内阁
    ——哈达是印尼的回教徒、大资本家，代表王公贵族、大资产阶级、大地主利益的国民党的首领。一九四八年一月十七日印尼反动的上层份子接受了屈辱的伦维尔协定以后，美帝国主义选定了哈达组织内阁，这个内阁排斥了共产党、社会主义青年党、劳工党以及其他左翼革命党派，由三个国民党人、四个回教党人（代表封建集团利益）、一个基督教党人、一个天主教党人、七个“无党派”右翼分子组成。上台后执行着出卖国家利益给美国、荷兰，反对苏联，镇压国内人民革命运动的反动政策，成为印尼人民的公敌。
