Title: U.S. Imperialism's Other Tool of Aggression: Mediterranean Pact in the Works
Author:
Date: 1949-03-25
Page: 3

　　U.S. Imperialism's Other Tool of Aggression
    Mediterranean Pact in the Works
    (Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 22nd) Following the announcement of the North Atlantic Pact, the main tool of U.S. imperialist aggression, the organization of another tool of aggression, the Mediterranean Pact, is in preparation. According to Agence France-Presse, Damascus, 19th: Britain, Turkey, and Syria are currently discussing the conclusion of the Mediterranean Pact.

### 美帝另一侵略工具  地中海公约在准备中

1949-03-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　美帝另一侵略工具
    地中海公约在准备中
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】美帝国主义侵略政策主要工具北大西洋公约公布之后，另一个侵略工具地中海公约的组织正在准备中。据法新社大马士革十九日讯：英国、土耳其、叙利亚现正讨论缔结地中海公约。
