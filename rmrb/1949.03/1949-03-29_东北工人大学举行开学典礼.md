Title: Northeast Workers' University Holds Opening Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1949-03-29
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 25th. The Northeast Workers' Political University held its opening ceremony on the 21st. Gao Gang,负责人 of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Zhu Qiwen, Mayor of Shenyang Special Municipality, and Tang Yunchao, Director of the Labor Bureau, all came to congratulate them, unanimously encouraging everyone to study actively and serve the people.

### 东北工人大学举行开学典礼

1949-03-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　东北工人大学举行开学典礼
    【新华社沈阳二十五日电】东北工人政治大学二十一日举行开学典礼。中共中央东北局负责人高岗，沈阳特别市市长朱其文，劳动总局局长唐韵超等，都往致贺，一致勉励大家积极学习，为人民服务。
