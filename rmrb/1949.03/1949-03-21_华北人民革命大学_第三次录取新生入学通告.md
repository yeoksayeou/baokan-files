Date: 1949-03-21

North China People's Revolutionary University Third Enrollment Notice

    This university has decided to enroll new students for the third time. Registration will take place at the university campus in Xiyuan, Western Suburbs, over four days from the 23rd to the 26th of this month. It is hoped that newly enrolled students will bring their personal bedding and supplies and register on time.

    March 21st

### 华北人民革命大学　第三次录取新生入学通告

1949-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民革命大学　第三次录取新生入学通告
    本校决定第三次录取新生于本月二十三日至二十六日四天内，在西郊西苑本校注册入学，希录取新生携带随身被服用品等届时入学为要。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　三月二十一日
