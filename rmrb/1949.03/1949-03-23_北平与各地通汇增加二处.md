Title: Two More Places Added for Remittances Between Beiping and Other Locations

Author:
Date: 1949-03-23
Page: 1

Beiping Adds Two Locations for Remittances with Other Places
[This Newspaper's Report] The Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China has added two more remittance locations, Hejian and Xuanhua, starting from the 21st. In addition, the bank has also established contact with seven locations including Huailai, Ding County, Huolu, Yi County, Wangdu, Lixian, and Shenze, and remittances will be available within days. (Yu)

### 北平与各地通汇增加二处

1949-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平与各地通汇
    增加二处
    【本报讯】人民银行北平分行通汇地点，自廿一日起又增加河间、宣化二处。此外该行并已与怀来、定县、获鹿、易县、望都、蠡县深泽等七地取得联系，日内即可通汇。（宇）
