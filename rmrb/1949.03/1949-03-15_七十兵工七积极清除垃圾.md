Title: Seventy Ordnance Factory Seven Actively Clears Garbage
Author:
Date: 1949-03-15
Page: 2

　　Seventy Ordnance Factory Seven Actively Clears Garbage
　　[This Dispatch] Workers at the Seventy Ordnance Factory, in response to the government's call to clear garbage in the city, have organized a cleaning committee, preparing to remove all the garbage stored inside the factory and dormitories.
　(Chi)

### 七十兵工七积极清除垃圾

1949-03-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　七十兵工七积极清除垃圾
    【本报讯】七十兵工厂的工人们为了响应政府号召，清除市内垃圾，已经组织了清洁委员会，预备把厂内和宿舍内所存的垃圾全部清出去。
　（炽）
