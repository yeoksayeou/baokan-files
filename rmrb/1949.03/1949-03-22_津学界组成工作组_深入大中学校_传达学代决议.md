Title: Tianjin Academic Circles Form Working Group to Go Deep into Universities and Middle Schools to Convey Student Congress Resolutions
Author:
Date: 1949-03-22
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, March 21st: The preparatory committee of the Tianjin Student Union's representative assembly decided on the 18th to invite Tianjin representatives attending the National Student Congress, representatives of the Tianjin Student Union preparatory committee, and representatives of the Democratic Youth Federation preparatory committee to jointly form a working group. The working group will go to various secondary and higher education schools to convey the resolutions of the National Student Congress and introduce the situation of the Congress. After the communication, discussions will be launched based on school societies or department levels.

### 津学界组成工作组  深入大中学校  传达学代决议

1949-03-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　津学界组成工作组
    深入大中学校
    传达学代决议
    【新华社天津二十一日电】天津学生联合会筹备会代表大会，十八日决定请出席全国学代大会天津代表、天津学联筹备会代表并民主青年联合会筹备会代表，共同组成工作组，分别到各中等以上学校，传达全国学生代表大会决议及介绍大会的情况。传达后即以各校社团或系级为基础展开讨论。
