Title: Telephone Lines Between Beiping, Tianjin, and Shijiazhuang are Now Open
Author:
Date: 1949-03-01
Page: 1

　　Pingjin-Shizhuang
　　Telephone Lines are Now Open
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 27th] The "carrier wave telephone" between Beiping and Tianjin was repaired on the 19th and can accommodate fifty people speaking simultaneously. More than twenty workers from the Beiping Telecommunications Bureau participated in the repairs, completing the work three days ahead of schedule. In addition, long-distance telephone lines from Beiping to Tianjin, Tongxian, and Shijiazhuang have all been repaired and are now in operation.

### 平津石庄间　电话已畅通

1949-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　平津石庄间
  　电话已畅通
    【新华社北平二十七日电】平津间的“载波电话”，已于十九日修好，可同时有五十人讲话。参加修理者为北平电讯局的二十多个工人，较预定计划提早三天完工。此外由北平至天津、通县、石家庄的长途电话亦已全部修复通话。
