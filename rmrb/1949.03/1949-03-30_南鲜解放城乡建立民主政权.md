Title: South Korean Liberated Cities and Villages Establish Democratic Regime
Author:
Date: 1949-03-30
Page: 3

　　South Korean Liberated Cities and Villages Establish Democratic Regime
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 29th: Pyongyang Radio broadcast: South Korean guerrillas have achieved victory in the struggle against the American puppet regime. In the liberated towns and villages, democratic regimes have been established.

### 南鲜解放城乡建立民主政权

1949-03-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　南鲜解放城乡建立民主政权
    【新华社北平二十九日电】平壤电台广播：南朝鲜游击队在反对美国傀儡政权的战斗中获得胜利。在已解放的城镇与村庄里，民主政权已告建立。
