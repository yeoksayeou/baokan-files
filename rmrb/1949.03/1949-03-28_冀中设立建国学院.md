Title: Jizhong Establishes Jian Guo College
Author:
Date: 1949-03-28
Page: 2

(Jizhong Electric) The Jizhong Administrative Office has recently established the Jizhong Jian Guo College, with an initial intake of 886 male and female students. The college is divided into seven classes: industry and commerce, finance, taxation, trade, banking, cooperation, and law. The duration of study is generally set at five months. Classes began on the 15th of this month.

### 冀中设立建国学院

1949-03-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀中设立建国学院
    【冀中电】冀中行署近建立冀中建国学院，第一期共收男女生八百八十六人。该校计分工商、财政、税务、贸易、银行、合作、司法等七个班。修业期限一般规定为五个月。已于本月十五日开学。
