Title: Tianjin Grain Company Formally Established
Author:
Date: 1949-03-30
Page: 2

    Tianjin Grain Company Formally Established
    [Tianjin Electric] The Tianjin Grain Company, under the Tianjin Trading Company, was formally established yesterday, the 28th. Its main business is to strengthen the grain supply in Tianjin and stabilize grain prices in the market.

### 津市粮食公司正式成立

1949-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　津市粮食公司正式成立
    【天津电】天津市贸易公司所属粮食公司已于昨二十八日正式成立，其主要业务为加强津市粮食供应及稳定市场粮价。
