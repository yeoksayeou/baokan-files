　　Near East News Snippets

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 14th] TASS, Beirut dispatch: The ministers of the United States, Britain, Turkey, and Greece to Syria visited the Syrian Prime Minister in Damascus on March 7th. Press circles pointed out that the visit discussed the issue of establishing a Mediterranean bloc.

### 近东零讯

1949-03-15
第3版()
专栏：近东零讯

　　近东零讯
    【新华社陕北十四日电】塔斯社贝鲁特讯：美国、英国、土耳其、希腊诸国驻叙利亚公使，于三月七日在大马士革访问叙利亚总理。报界人士指出：此次拜访曾讨论了成立地中海集团的问题。
