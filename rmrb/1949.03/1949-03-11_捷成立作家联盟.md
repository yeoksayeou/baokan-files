Title: Czechoslovak Writers Union Established
Author:
Date: 1949-03-11
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 9th: TASS, Prague: The first Czechoslovak Writers Union held its inaugural meeting in Prague on March 4th. The conference was declared open by the famous female writer Maria Majerová. The representatives attending the meeting cordially welcomed the delegation from the Soviet Writers Union, guests from the People's Democratic countries, and progressive writers from France, Latin America, and other countries. The Chairman of the National Assembly, David, addressed the meeting, calling on Czechoslovak writers to more actively help the people build the Republic on a new socialist foundation. The conference unanimously decided to organize a unified Czechoslovak Writers Union and adopted the union's charter. Ján Drda was elected as Chairman. Pavel Bojar was elected as General Secretary. The conference elected the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Writers Union, consisting of twenty-three members.

### 捷成立作家联盟

1949-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷成立作家联盟
    【新华社陕北九日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：第一届捷克斯洛伐克作家联盟于三月四日在布拉格举行成立大会。大会由著名女作家玛丽亚·梅叶洛娃宣布揭幕。出席大会的代表，衷心地欢迎苏联作家联盟的代表团、人民民主国家的来宾、以及从法国、拉丁美洲及其他国家来的进步作家。国民议会主席戴维特向大会演说，号召捷克斯洛伐克作家更加积极地帮助人民在新的社会主义的基础上建立共和国。大会一致决定组织单一的捷克斯洛伐克作家联盟，并通过联盟章程。吉安·得尔达当选为主席。帕魏尔·保雅尔当选为总书记。大会选出包括二—三名委员的捷克斯洛伐克作家联盟中央委员会。
