Title: Zhongda to Hold Grand Event to See Off Southbound Students
Author:
Date: 1949-03-16
Page: 2

　　[Ping City News] In order to bid farewell to students going south to work and those applying to the three universities (Military and Political University, North China University, and People's Revolutionary University), China University will hold a literary and art evening at 6:00 PM on the 17th of this month in the school's Yixian Hall. Mr. Tian Han, Ai Qing, An E, Feng Zhi, and Xu Guangping have been specially invited to give speeches, and there will be poetry recitations and other programs. A grand occasion is expected.
　　Furthermore, at 10:00 AM on the 18th, the school will invite democrat Mr. Zhang Naiqi to give a speech entitled: "Impressions of the Northeast Liberated Area". Location: Yixian Hall.

### 中大将有盛会欢送南下同学

1949-03-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　中大将有盛会欢送南下同学
    【平市讯】中国大学为欢送南下工作同学及投考三个大学（军政大学、华北大学、人民革命大学）的同学，将于本月十七日下午六时，在该校逸仙堂举行文艺晚会，特请田汉、艾青、安娥、冯至、许广平诸先生讲演，并有诗歌朗颂等节目。届时定有一番盛况。
    又十八日上午十时该校将请民主人士章乃器先生演讲，讲题为：“东北解放区观感”。地点逸仙堂。
