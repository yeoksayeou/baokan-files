Title: Tianjin Municipality Sends 5,000 Refugees Back Home to Resume Production; Post Office Restores Fast Mail and Parcel Delivery
Author:
Date: 1949-03-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 28th—The Tianjin Municipal People's Government, from February 10th to 22nd, over a period of thirteen days, sent more than 5,500 refugees back to their hometowns to resume production. Those who were verified by the district governments as being truly impoverished were issued free train tickets by the municipal government or were given travel subsidies as appropriate. The general refugees expressed deep gratitude to the People's Government for this measure.

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 28th—The Tianjin Municipal Postal Administration has resumed the acceptance and delivery of ordinary express mail, express letters, and parcels. In addition to the post offices within the Tianjin Military Control Zone, fast mail can be delivered to and ordinary express mail can be mutually exchanged with Beijing, Zhangjiakou, Baoding, Zhuoxian, and the post offices along the railway lines between Tianjin and Beijing and Zhangjiakou. The scope of parcel acceptance is temporarily limited to the Tianjin Military Control Zone and various liberated areas in North China.

### 天津市遣送难民五千人还乡生产  邮局恢复快信包裹寄递

1949-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　天津市遣送难民五千人还乡生产
    邮局恢复快信包裹寄递
    【新华社天津二十八日电】津市人民政府，自二月十日至二十二日十三天内，遣送难民五千五百余人返乡生产。其中经各区政府审查确系贫寒者，由市府发给免费乘坐火车证，或酌予补助旅费。一般难民对人民政府此一措施，深表感激。
    【新华社天津二十八日电】天津市邮政管理局已恢复平快、快递信件及包裹收寄。快信寄达地区及平快互寄范围除天津市军管区内之各邮局外，并有北平、张家口、保定、涿县及天津至北平及张家口间铁路沿线各邮局。包裹收寄范围，暂定天津军管区及华北解放区各地。
