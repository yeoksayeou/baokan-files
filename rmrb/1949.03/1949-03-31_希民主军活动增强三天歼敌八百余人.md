Title: Greek Democratic Army Increases Activities, Annihilating Over 800 Enemy Troops in Three Days
Author:
Date: 1949-03-31
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 29th: The Free Greece Radio broadcasted on the 28th: The Greek Democratic Army's offensive activities on the front lines are visibly increasing. In the Valtos region, the royalist fascist army suffered 850 casualties in three days. On the 22nd, a unit of the Democratic Army attacked the enemy, killing and wounding 158 enemy soldiers. Five battalions of the royalist army's counter-attack were repelled, with 404 killed and wounded. In the Chrissoniikia Peninsula, the Sixth Division of the Democratic Army penetrated deep into the peninsula and attacked the city of Konkia.

### 希民主军活动增强三天歼敌八百余人

1949-03-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　希民主军活动增强三天歼敌八百余人
    【新华社北平二十九日电】自由希腊电台二十八日广播：希腊民主军在前线的攻击活动日见增强。在瓦尔托斯地区，保皇法西斯军三天中伤亡八百五十人。二十二日，民主军某部攻击敌军，曾毙伤敌军一百五十八人。保皇军五个营的反攻都被击退，并死伤四百零四人。在克里逊尼基亚半岛，民主军第六师曾深入半岛，进攻康基亚城。
