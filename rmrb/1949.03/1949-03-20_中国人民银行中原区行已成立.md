Title: The Central Plains Branch of the People's Bank of China Has Been Established
Author:
Date: 1949-03-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 18th. The Central Plains Branch of the People's Bank of China was established on the 10th of this month. The branch stipulates that the People's Bank currency and the Zhongzhou currency will be mixed and used throughout the Central Plains region at a ratio of one to three. The head office of the Zhongzhou Farmers Bank will concurrently handle the business of the Central Plains Branch of the People's Bank of China. This measure will greatly benefit the promotion of trade between the Central Plains Liberated Area and the North China and East China Liberated Areas, the development of production, and the prosperity of the economy.

### 中国人民银行中原区行已成立

1949-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民银行中原区行已成立
    【新华社中原十八日电】中国人民银行中原区行已于本月十日成立。该行规定人民票与中州票以一比三的比例，在中原全区混合流通使用。中州农民银行总行将兼理中国人民银行中原区行业务。此举对于推进中原解放区与华北、华东解放区之间的贸易，发展生产，繁荣经济，将有很大裨益。
