Title: Correspondence

Author:

Date: 1949-03-06
Page: 1

　　Correspondence to responsible comrades of local prefectural committees, county committees, and joint offices:
    Recently, this newspaper has continuously received spring plowing production plans and materials from this year's production meetings from Jingxing, Quyang County, Taihang First Special District Office, Wuxiang, Neiqiu, Tunliu, and other places. These materials are all very good. We hope that you can also send us a copy of your local production plan for this year and the details of the production meeting so that the newspaper can use and refer to them. Also, please pay attention to promptly organizing reports on the actual progress.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　This newspaper's Correspondence Section

### 通讯往来

1949-03-06
第1版()
专栏：通讯往来

　　通讯往来各地委、县委并联合办公室负责同志：
    最近本报连收到井陉、曲阳县、太行一专署、武乡、内邱、屯留等地春耕生产计划，及今年生产会议的材料。这些材料都很好，希望将你们那里今年的生产计划，生产会议的情形亦能惠赐本报一份，以便供报纸采用和参考，并且请注意将实际进行的情形，及时的组织报导。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
