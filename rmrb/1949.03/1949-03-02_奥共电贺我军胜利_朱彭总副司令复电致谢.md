Title: Austrian Communists Congratulate Our Army on Victory; Zhu De and Peng Dehuai, Commander-in-Chief and Deputy Commander-in-Chief, Reply with Thanks

Author: 
Date: 1949-03-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th -- The Fourteenth Congress of the Austrian Communist Party, held in Vienna last November, sent a congratulatory telegram to the Chinese People's Liberation Army, celebrating the liberation of Northeast China. Due to transportation difficulties, it was only recently delivered to the headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the headquarters of the Northeast Field Army. Zhu De and Peng Dehuai, Commander-in-Chief and Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the People's Liberation Army, replied with a telegram of thanks. The telegrams are as follows:

Telegram from the Fourteenth Congress of the Austrian Communist Party to the Chinese People's Liberation Army:
To the heroic soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

The Fourteenth Congress of the Austrian Communist Party extends warm fighting greetings and congratulations to you for liberating Shenyang from the rule of the Kuomintang and thus liberating Manchuria. We know that the blood shed by your heroes in the struggle against the running dogs of American imperialism is also shed for the cause of the Austrian proletariat. We, the Austrian Communists, are fighting the same enemy in the struggle to win freedom for our motherland.

The Fourteenth Congress of the Austrian Communist Party expresses its unwavering confidence that the prestigious Chinese democratic army, which has already liberated vast areas of their country, will soon liberate all of China from the shackles of American imperialism.

We salute the Chinese Communist Party and its leader Mao Zedong, and we salute the great Chinese people and their just and victorious struggle.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Fourteenth Congress of the Austrian Communist Party
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Vienna, November 4, 1948

Reply from Zhu De and Peng Dehuai, Commander-in-Chief and Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, to the Austrian Communist Party Central Committee:

The telegram sent by the Fourteenth Congress of the Austrian Communist Party to the Chinese People's Liberation Army on November 4th of last year was only recently received due to transportation issues. On behalf of all the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, we thank you for your congratulations. The common struggle of the people of all countries against imperialism is achieving more and more victories every day. This is the result of the internationalist solidarity of the people of all countries, especially the working class of all countries. We wish the Austrian Communist Party and the Austrian people great victories in the near future in their struggle for democracy and independence of their motherland.

　　　　　　　　　　Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, Deputy Commander-in-Chief Peng Dehuai of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　February 27, 1949

### 奥共电贺我军胜利　朱彭总副司令复电致谢

1949-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　奥共电贺我军胜利
  　朱彭总副司令复电致谢
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】去年十一月在维也纳举行的奥国共产党第十四次代表大会，曾向中国人民解放军发出贺电一件，庆祝东北解放。因交通不便，最近才送达中国人民解放军总部及东北野战军司令部。人民解放军朱彭总副司令当予复电致谢。来电与复电如下：
    奥国共产党第十四次代表大会致中国人民解放军电：致中国人民解放军英勇的战士们：
    奥国共产党第十四次代表大会，为了你们从国民党统治下解放沈阳，从此解放满洲，特向你们致热烈的战斗的敬礼和祝贺。我们知道，你们的英雄们在反对美国帝国主义走狗的斗争中所流的血，也是为了奥国无产阶级的事业而流的。我们奥国共产党人，在争取我们祖国自由的斗争中，和你们反对着同样的敌人。
    奥国共产党第十四次代表大会，表示屹立不摇的信心，相信已经将他们国家的广大区域解放出来了的素负盛望的中国民主军队，在不久的将来，就要把全中国从美国帝国主义的束缚中解放出来。
    我们向中国共产党及其领袖毛泽东致敬，我们向伟大的中国人民及其正义的胜利的斗争致敬。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　奥国共产党第十四次代表大会
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四八年十一月四日维也纳
    中国人民解放军朱彭总副司令复奥国共产党电奥国共产党中央委员会：
    奥国共产党第十四次代表大会去年十一月四日致中国解放军的电报，因交通关系最近始收到。我们代表中国人民解放军全体将士感谢你们的祝贺，世界各国人民反对帝国主义的共同斗争，正在一天天取得更多的胜利，这是各国人民首先是各国工人阶级的国际主义团结的结果。我们预祝奥国共产党和奥国人民在为自己祖国的民主和独立的斗争中，将在最近的将来获得伟大的胜利。
    　　　　　　　　　　中国人民解放军总司令朱德副总司令彭德怀
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年二月二十七日
