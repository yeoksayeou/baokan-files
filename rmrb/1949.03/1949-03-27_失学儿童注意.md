Title: Attention, Out-of-School Children

Author:
Date: 1949-03-27
Page: 2

　　Attention, Out-of-School Children
    [This newspaper] In the spirit of serving the public, the faculty and staff of the First Affiliated Elementary School of the Teachers College have established a remedial class for out-of-school children in their spare time. The registration dates are from the 28th to the 31st of this month. Classes will begin on April 6th.

### 失学儿童注意

1949-03-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　失学儿童注意
    【本报讯】师大第一附小教职员本服务大众精神，在课余成立失学儿童补习班，报名日期为本月二十八日至三十一日。四月六日开始上课。
