Title: Former Kuomintang Central Epidemic Prevention Bureau Resumes Work After Takeover
Author:
Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 15th: The former Kuomintang Central Epidemic Prevention Bureau has resumed work after being taken over. The penicillin output of the bureau's pharmaceutical factory was 100 million units per month in the past, but has now increased to 400 million units. North China, East China, and the Central Plains Liberated Areas have all placed orders with the factory for various products.

### 原国民党中央防疫处接管后已恢复工作

1949-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　原国民党中央防疫处接管后已恢复工作
    【新华社北平十五日电】原国民党的中央防疫处，经接管后，已恢复工作。该处制药厂盘尼西林的产量过去一个月是一万万个单位，现已增至四万万个单位。刻华北、华东、中原等解放区均向该厂订购各种出品。
