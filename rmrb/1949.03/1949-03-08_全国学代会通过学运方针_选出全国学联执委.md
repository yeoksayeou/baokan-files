Title: National Student Congress Passes Student Movement Guidelines, Elects National Student Federation Executive Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-03-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, March 5th: The 14th National Student Congress of the All-China Students' Federation today passed the "Current Tasks of the Chinese Student Movement" resolution and the "Constitution of the All-China Students' Federation." In accordance with the election law and based on the population ratio of each region, the executive committee of the National Student Federation was elected. The congress approved the All-China Students' Federation joining the International Union of Students and participating in the preparatory work for the National Youth Congress scheduled to be held on May 4th of this year. The congress passed telegrams to the Chinese Communist Party, the People's Liberation Army, all democratic parties, all people's organizations, teachers, and students across the country, as well as telegrams to the International Union of Students and the World Federation of Democratic Youth. The list of executive committee members of the All-China Students' Federation elected by the congress is as follows: (1) Northwest Region: Cheng Hao, Yang Guangdou, Nie Weiqing, Wu Jianfu. (2) North China Region: Shi Buwei, Li Guirong, Yan Fumin. (3) Northeast Region: Liu Lianru, Feng Yinfu, Yang Jianbo, Xu Jingwu, Guo Yongze. (4) East China Region: Fan Jun (female), Tan Mingjia, Jiang Kuisheng (two seats remain vacant). (5) Central Plains Region: Ding Li, Li Gongtian, Wang Yunxiang, Cao Tingzhi. (6) Beiping Region: Liu Xisheng, Huang Zumin, Jiang Peifang (female). (7) Tianjin Region: Feng Binbin (female), Wu Gongjun. (8) Shanghai Region: Zhou Shouchang, He Changcheng, Chen Zhenzhong, Huang Zhensheng, Liu Xiuzheng (female). (9) Nanjing Region: Huang Hezhen, Zhao Shumin (one seat remains vacant). (10) Wuhan Region: Wang Yuncong (female) (one seat remains vacant). (11) Guangzhou Region: He Xiquan. (12) Shenyang Region: Zhang Guiyun (female). (13) Xi'an Region: Wang Tianyuan. (14) Zhejiang Region: Li Jingxian (one seat remains vacant). (The above executive committee members must be confirmed by the original regional student federations, and the original regional student federations have the right to change the assignments.)

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, March 6th: The All-China Student Congress has just received a congratulatory telegram from the International Union of Students. The telegram stated: "In the victory of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, we respectfully salute the National Student Congress of China. We wish you success in building a new democratic China and in the struggle for world peace and democracy." The Union of Chinese Students in Britain also sent a telegram congratulating the congress.

### 全国学代会通过学运方针　选出全国学联执委

1949-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国学代会通过学运方针
  　选出全国学联执委
    【新华社北平五日电】中华全国学生第十四届代表大会，今日通过“中国学生运动的当前任务”的决议和“中华全国学生联合会会章”；并依照选举法，按地区人数比率，选出了全国学联的执行委员。大会批准了全国学联加入国际学生联合会，并参加定于今年五月四日召开的全国青年代表大会的筹备工作。大会通过了给中国共产党，人民解放军，全国各民主党派，各人民团体，全国各师长，同学的通电，以及给国际学生联合会和世界民主青年联盟的电文。大会选出的中华全国学生联合会执行委员。名单如下：（一）西北区：程浩、杨光斗、聂维庆、吴剑夫。（二）华北区：施不为，李桂荣、晏福民。（三）东北区：刘廉儒、冯阴复、杨健伯、徐净武、郭永泽。（四）华东区：范均（女）、谭明甲、蒋奎生（尚保留二席）。（五）中原区：丁力、李公天、王云祥、曹廷芝。（六）北平区：刘希圣、黄祖民、姜培芳（女）。（七）天津区：冯彬彬（女）、吴功俊。（八）上海区：周寿昌、何长城、陈震中、黄振声、刘修正（女）。（九）南京区：黄鹤桢、赵树民（尚保留一席）。（十）武汉区：王云从（女）（保留一席）。（十一）广州区：何锡权。（十二）沈阳区：张桂云（女）。（十三）西安区：王天元。（十四）浙江区：李景先（尚保留一席）。（以上各执行委员，须经各原地区学联追认，各原地区学联并有改派权。）
    【新华社北平六日电】中华全国学生代表大会顷收到国际学生联合会的贺电，电文内称：“在中国人民解放军的胜利中，我们谨向中国全国学生代表大会致敬。敬祝你们在建立一个新民主的中国和为世界和平与民主而奋斗中成功”。又留英中国学生联合会亦电贺大会。
