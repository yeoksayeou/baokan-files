Title: Chairman Yun Ze of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Government Arrives in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-03-20
Page: 1

　　Chairman Yun Ze of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Government Arrives in Beiping
    [Our Dispatch] Chairman Yun Ze of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Government and Minister Gao Boze cloth of the Agricultural and Animal Husbandry Department of the government arrived in Beiping on the 18th of this month. Representatives of Mongolians staying in Beiping went to welcome them.
　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　(Zhu)

### 内蒙自治政府云泽主席抵平

1949-03-20

专栏：

　　内蒙自治政府云泽主席抵平
