Title: Soviet Union and Korean People's Republic Sign Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Further Strengthening Economic and Cultural Relations Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1949-03-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 23rd: TASS, Moscow: The Soviet government and the government of the Korean People's Democratic Republic simultaneously issued a communique on the 20th, announcing that the Soviet Union and the Korean People's Democratic Republic had signed a ten-year economic and cultural cooperation agreement and three other agreements: namely, the Soviet Union and the Korean People's Democratic Republic's Goods Delivery and Payment Agreement, "stipulating a significant increase in the volume of trade between the two countries in 1949 and 1950," a Soviet loan agreement to Korea "to pay for the goods delivered by the Soviet Union to Korea exceeding the goods delivery agreement," and a Soviet agreement to provide technical assistance to Korea.

These agreements are the result of talks between the Soviet government and the Korean government delegation, headed by Korean People's Democratic Republic Premier Kim Il-sung, since its three-day visit to Moscow. Soviet government Chairman of the Council of Ministers Stalin, Deputy Chairmen Molotov and Mikoyan, Foreign Minister Vyshinsky, and Foreign Trade Minister Menshikov all participated in the meeting. Soviet Ambassador to Korea Sidorov and Korean Ambassador to the Soviet Union Zhu Ninghe were also ordered to participate in the talks. The negotiations were conducted in an atmosphere of amity and friendship.

### 苏联与朝鲜人民共和国  签订经济文化合作协定  进一步加强两国间经济文化关系

1949-03-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联与朝鲜人民共和国
    签订经济文化合作协定
    进一步加强两国间经济文化关系
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府和朝鲜人民民主共和国政府于二十日同时发表公报，宣布苏联和朝鲜人民民主共和国之间已签定十年的经济与文化合作协定和另外三个协定：即苏联和朝鲜人民民主共和国交货与支付协定“规定大大地增加一九四九年与一九五零年两国间的贸易量”，苏联对朝鲜贷款协定“用以支付苏联超过交货协定所运交给朝鲜之货物的费用”，苏联予朝鲜以技术援助协定。
    这些协定，是以朝鲜人民民主共和国总理金日成为首的朝鲜政府代表团，自接连三日访问莫斯科以来，与苏联政府会谈的结果。苏联政府部长会议主席斯大林、副主席莫洛托夫与米高扬、外交部长维辛斯基及对外贸易部长孟希科夫均曾参加会议。苏联驻朝鲜大使西地科夫及朝鲜驻苏大使朱宁河亦奉命参加会谈。谈判是在亲睦友好的空气下进行的。
