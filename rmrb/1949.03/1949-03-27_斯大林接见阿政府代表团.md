Title: Stalin Receives Albanian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1949-03-27
Page: 3

　　Stalin Receives
　　Albanian Government Delegation
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th] TASS, Moscow: Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, received the Albanian government delegation headed by General Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Albania, on the 23rd. Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky, Soviet Minister to Albania, Mevasin, and Albanian Minister to the Soviet Union, Jin Qifti, also attended. On the 24th, Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, received the Albanian delegation.

### 斯大林接见阿政府代表团

1949-03-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　斯大林接见
  　阿政府代表团
    【新华社北平二十六日电】塔斯社莫斯科电：苏联部长会议主席斯大林二十三日接见阿尔巴尼亚人民民主共和国部长会议主席兼外交部长霍查上将为首的阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团，苏外交部长维辛斯基、苏驻阿公使楣瓦辛、阿驻苏公使晋萋夫蒂均参加。二十四日，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克接见阿尔巴尼亚代表团。
