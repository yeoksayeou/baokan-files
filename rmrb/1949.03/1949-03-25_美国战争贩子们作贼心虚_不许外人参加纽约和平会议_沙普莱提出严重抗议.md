Title: American Warmongers Act With a Guilty Conscience, Barring Outsiders from Attending New York Peace Conference; Shapley Lodges Strong Protest
Author: 
Date: 1949-03-25
Page: 3

    American Warmongers Act With a Guilty Conscience
    Barring Outsiders from Attending New York Peace Conference
    Shapley Lodges Strong Protest
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 24th: The Electrical Communication Press, New York: Shapley, the host of the upcoming American Arts, Sciences, and Professions for Peace conference in New York, protested to U.S. Secretary of State Acheson on the 22nd against the State Department's refusal to issue entry visas to twelve delegates attending the peace conference. These twelve delegates include four representatives from Britain, three from France, four from Italy, and the famous Brazilian writer, Portinari. This measure by the American government demonstrates that the American warmongers fear that the New York peace conference will increasingly isolate them in the face of the American public.

### 美国战争贩子们作贼心虚  不许外人参加纽约和平会议  沙普莱提出严重抗议

1949-03-25
第3版()
专栏：

    美国战争贩子们作贼心虚
    不许外人参加纽约和平会议
    沙普莱提出严重抗议
    【新华社陕北二十四日电】电通社纽约讯：即将在纽约召开之美国艺术、科学与自由职业者保卫和平大会主持人沙普莱，于二十二日向美国务卿艾奇逊提出抗议，反对国务院拒绝发给十二名出席和平会议的代表以入境护照。这十二名代表中包括英国代表四名，法国代表三名，意大利代表四名以及巴西名作家波特那。美国政府这一措施，表明了美国战争贩子们，害怕纽约和平会议将使他们在美国公众面前日益孤立。
