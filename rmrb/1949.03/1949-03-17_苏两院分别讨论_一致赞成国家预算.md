Title: Soviet Two Houses Discuss and Unanimously Approve the National Budget
Author:
Date: 1949-03-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 16th. Moscow dispatch: After the joint session of the two chambers of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union heard the report on the national budget by Finance Minister Zverev, the two chambers held separate meetings on the 11th and 12th to hear the reports of the Budget Committees and conduct debates. Kornetz, chairman of the Budget Committee of the Federal Council, stated in his report that he fully supported the national budget because it fully conformed to the interests of further consolidating the Soviet state and the people. Khokhlov, chairman of the Budget Committee of the Council of Nationalities, proposed in his report that the budget revenue should be increased by 830.3 million rubles. During the discussions in the two houses, the speakers unanimously approved the national budget proposed by the Soviet government.

### 苏两院分别讨论　一致赞成国家预算

1949-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏两院分别讨论
  　一致赞成国家预算
    【新华社陕北十六日电】莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃两院联席会议听取财长兹维列夫关于国家预算的报告之后，两院于十一日、十二日分别举行会议，听取预算委员会的报告并进行辩论。联邦院预算委员会主席柯尔涅茨在报告中表示完全拥护国家预算，因为该预算完全符合进一步巩固苏维埃国家和人民的利益。民族院预算委员会主席霍赫洛夫在报告中提议预算岁入应增加八亿三百三十万卢布。在两院讨论时，发言人一致赞成苏联政府所提出的国家预算。
