Title: Dredging Being Carried Out at Tanggu New Port
Author: 
Date: 1949-03-30
Page: 2

　　【Tianjin Electric Dispatch】Preliminary dredging work at Tanggu New Port began on the 19th of this month. The "Xigu" grab dredger is dredging approximately 120 cubic meters of silt per day in the channel in front of the shipyard. The "Tanggu No. 2" suction dredger has been test-run with good results and will be put into operation after the discharge pipe is repaired.

### 塘沽新港进行挖泥

1949-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　塘沽新港进行挖泥
    【天津电】塘沽新港初步挖泥工作，已于本月十九日开始。“西沽”号抓泥船，在船身工厂前航道中，每天约可抓出一百二十立方公尺的淤泥。“塘沽”二号吸泥船已试车，成绩良好，待排泥管修竣后，即可工作。
