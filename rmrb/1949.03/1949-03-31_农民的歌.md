Title: Farmers' Song
Author: Zhu Ziqi
Date: 1949-03-31
Page: 4

Farmers' Song

　　Listening to the yellow ox call

　　Listening to the yellow ox call,
　　Spring plowing has arrived!
　　Poplar and willow trees sprout green buds,
　　Under the trees, calves run all over.

　　Feed the ox well!
　　Wipe the plow clean!
　　The spring plowing movement is in full swing,
　　The whole village of farmers laughs heartily.

　　Listening to the yellow ox call,
　　Calling people to be diligent.
　　Dad goes out to the village with the plow,
　　Older brother goes to the fields to deliver manure.

　　Mom washes the rice,
　　I'll light the fire.
　　Everyone is busy participating in production,
　　The village is bustling with activity.

　　Magpie

　　In March, the weather is fine,
　　The magpie is busy reporting good news,
　　Reporting good news to my home.
　　Celebrating the liberation of Peiping and Tientsin,
　　Younger brother raises his hand to salute,
　　The whole family smiles happily.

　　Eldest sister makes a suggestion,
　　Second sister and I agree,
　　Supporting the front through production for victory,
　　Striving to be a model worker.
　　Magpie, please,
　　Please take the news to other homes.

### 农民的歌
朱子奇
1949-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　农民的歌
            耳听黄牛叫
　
    耳听黄牛叫，
    春耕来到了！
    杨树柳树绿芽冒，
    树下小牛满地跑。
　
    牛儿快喂饱！
    犁儿快擦好！
    春耕运动大展开，
    全村农民哈哈笑。
　
    耳听黄牛叫，
    叫人多勤劳。
    爸爸扶犁出村去，
    哥哥送粪上地了。
　
    妈妈把米淘，
    我来把火烧。
    参加生产人人忙，
    村里村外好热闹。
　
            花喜鹊
　
    三月里，好天气，
    花喜鹊儿忙报喜，
    一报报到我家里。
    庆祝平津得解放，
    弟弟举手敬个礼，
    全家大小笑嘻嘻。
　
    大姊姊，来提议，
    二姊和我也同意，
    生产支前为胜利，
    争取模范多努力。
    花喜鹊，劳驾你，
    请把消息捎到别家去。
