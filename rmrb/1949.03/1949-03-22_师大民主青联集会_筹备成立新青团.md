Title: Normal University Democratic Youth League Holds Meeting to Prepare for the Establishment of the New Youth League
Author:
Date: 1949-03-22
Page: 2

　　Normal University Democratic Youth League Holds Meeting to Prepare for the Establishment of the New Youth League
    [Ping Municipality News] In response to the call of the Preparatory Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of Beiping Municipality to establish the League, the China Democratic Youth League of National Beiping Normal University, after several days of deliberation, convened a League members' meeting on the afternoon of the 20th. More than 140 League members attended. The meeting included a work summary report from the Chinese Communist Party's Normal University branch, and a report from the representative of the Preparatory Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of Beiping Municipality on the establishment of the League at Normal University. Wu Ruizhang and eight others were elected to formally establish the Preparatory Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of Normal University.

### 师大民主青联集会  筹备成立新青团

1949-03-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　师大民主青联集会
    筹备成立新青团
    【平市讯】国立北平师范大学中国民主青年联盟为响应北平市新民主主义青年团筹委会建团的号召，经过多日酝酿，于二十日下午召开了盟员大会，共到盟员一百四十余人。会上有中国共产党师范大学支部的工作总结报告，北平市新民主主义青年团筹委会代表关于在师大建团的报告。并推举吴瑞章等九人正式成立新民主主义青年团师大筹委会。
