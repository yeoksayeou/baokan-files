Title: Tianjin Establishes Grain Stores Everywhere: Citizens Can Buy Grain at Low Prices
Author:
Date: 1949-03-17
Page: 2

　　Tianjin Establishes Grain Stores Everywhere
  　Citizens Can Buy Grain at Low Prices
    [Tianjin, 15th Dispatch] The Grain and Oil Department of the Tianjin Military Control Commission's Trade Office has established more than 480 entrusted grain sales outlets in the city, enabling the general public to exchange for essential food grains at low prices at any time. Therefore, it has been universally supported by the citizens. The department has now decided to continue to establish more such sales outlets.

### 津市普设粮食店　市民得低价买粮

1949-03-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　津市普设粮食店
  　市民得低价买粮
    【天津十五日电】天津市军管会贸易处粮油部已在市内设立委托粮食代售店四百八十余处，使广大市民能以低价随时换得必需食粮。因此受到市民普遍拥护。现该部决继续增设此项代售店。
