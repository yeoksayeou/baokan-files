Title: Notice from the Head Office of the People's Bank of China, General Dispatch No. 5
Author:
Date: 1949-03-28
Page: 3

　　Notice from the Head Office of the People's Bank of China
　　General Dispatch No. 5
　　March 20th, Year 38 of the Republic of China

　　The Bank will begin issuing a 50-yuan note, two types of 100-yuan notes, and one type of 200-yuan note in March. The designs are hereby announced as follows:

　　50-Yuan Note: The front features a scene of the Qianmen Railway Station. Above is printed "中国人民银行" [People's Bank of China]. On the left and right are "伍拾元" [Fifty Yuan]. At the bottom is "中华民国三十八年" [Year 38 of the Republic of China]. The back is chestnut-colored, with "中国人民银行" [People's Bank of China] at the top, the Arabic numeral (50) on the left and right, and "1949" at the bottom.

　　Type C 100-Yuan Note: The front has "中国人民银行" [People's Bank of China] at the top, "壹百元" [One Hundred Yuan] in the center, "中华民国三十八年" [Year 38 of the Republic of China] at the bottom, and "factory scenes" on the left and right sides. The back is brown, with "中国人民银行" [People's Bank of China] at the top and the Arabic numeral "100" on the left and right ends.

　　Type D 100-Yuan Note: The front has "中国人民银行" [People's Bank of China] at the top, a black and white "Beihai" landscape of Beijing on the left and right, and "中华民国三十八年印" [Printed in the Year 38 of the Republic of China] at the bottom. The back has "中国人民银行" [People's Bank of China] at the top, "1949" at the bottom, and the Arabic numeral "100" in the center and at the four corners.

　　200-Yuan Note: The front has "中国人民银行" [People's Bank of China] at the top, "贰百元" [Two Hundred Yuan] in the center, a "blue Summer Palace landscape" on the left and right sides, and "中华民国三十八年" [Year 38 of the Republic of China] at the bottom. The back is purple, with "中国人民银行" [People's Bank of China] at the top, and the Arabic numeral "200" in the center and on the left and right ends.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　For general information. This announcement is hereby made.

### 中国人民银行总行通告　总发字第五号  民国卅八年三月廿日

1949-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国人民银行总行通告
    总发字第五号　
    民国卅八年三月廿日
    本行于三月间开始发行伍拾元券一种，壹百元券两种，贰百元券一种。兹将式样通告于下：
    伍拾元券：正面有前门车站图景、正上方有“中国人民银行”六字，左右两方有“伍拾元”三字，底边有“中华民国三十八年”八字，背面为粟色，正上方有“中国人民银行”六字，左右两方均有阿拉伯字（５０）二字，底边有“１９１９”四字。
    丙种百元券：正面，正上方有“中国人民银行”六字，中央有“壹百元”三字，底边有“中华民国三十八年”八字，左右两旁有“工厂图景”。背面棕色，正上方有“中国人民银行”六字，左右两端有阿拉伯“１００”三字。
    丁种百元券：正面，正上方有“中国人民银行”六字，左右为黑色北平“北海”风景，底边有“中华民国三十八年印”九字。背面，正上方有“中国人民银行”六字，底边有“１９４９”四字，中央及四角均有阿拉伯字“１００”三字。
    贰百元券：正面，正上方有“中国人民银行”六字，中央有“贰百元”三字，左右两边有“蓝色颐和园风景”底边有“中华民国三十八年”八字。背面：紫色，正上方有“中国人民银行”六字，中央及左右两端有阿拉伯“２００”三字。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　恐未周知特此通告
