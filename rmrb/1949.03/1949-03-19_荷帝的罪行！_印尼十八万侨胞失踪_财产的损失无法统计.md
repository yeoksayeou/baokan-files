Title: The Crimes of the Dutch and Indonesian Reactionaries! 180,000 Overseas Chinese in Indonesia Missing; Property Losses Incalculable
Author:
Date: 1949-03-19
Page: 3

　　The Crimes of the Dutch and Indonesian Reactionaries!
  　180,000 Overseas Chinese in Indonesia Missing; Property Losses Incalculable
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 18th] According to late news from Batavia: The Chinese community in Batavia disclosed in February that the losses suffered by overseas Chinese in Indonesia during the recent new invasion launched by the Dutch army were unprecedented. The whereabouts of 160,000 overseas Chinese on Java Island are unknown, and the whereabouts of approximately 20,000 overseas Chinese in Sumatra are unknown. The property losses of the overseas Chinese are incalculable.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 18th] How is British imperialism mistreating the people and overseas Chinese in Malaya? The "Malayan Message," published in London, cited statistics published by the Federation of Malaya on January 1, 1949, to answer this question. The statistics pointed out that at that time, 5,705 people had been detained in concentration camps, mainly overseas Chinese. Since last October, 2,056 overseas Chinese have been deported.

### 荷帝的罪行！　印尼十八万侨胞失踪  财产的损失无法统计

1949-03-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　荷帝的罪行！
  　印尼十八万侨胞失踪  财产的损失无法统计
    【新华社陕北十八日电】据巴达维亚迟到消息：马达维亚华侨方面二月间披露，在荷军此次发动的新侵略行动中，印尼华侨损失空前。爪哇岛华侨下落不明者达十六万名，苏门答腊华侨下落不明者约两万名。华侨的财产损失则无法统计。
    【新华社陕北十八日电】英帝国主义正在怎样虐待马来亚人民和华侨？伦敦出版的“马来亚箴言报”援引马来亚联邦一九四九年一月一日发表的统计数字，回答了这个问题。该统计指出：当时已有五千七百零五人被拘禁在集中营里，其中主要是华侨。自去年十月以来，被驱逐出境的华侨已达二千零五十六人。
