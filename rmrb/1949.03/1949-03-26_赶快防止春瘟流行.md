Act Quickly to Prevent the Spread of Spring Plague

Due to the recent fluctuating weather, carelessness in food and clothing, a spring plague (including minor typhoid, blood typhoid, cholera, etc.) has become prevalent around the mining area. The symptoms are initial headaches and dizziness, which intensify sharply after one or two days, leading to unconsciousness. There are already five or six cases of this disease in Wangfeng, Jiaozuo. It is suggested that local governments, doctors, health departments, and medical personnel of various organizations strengthen prevention efforts. Otherwise, if it spreads widely, it will have a significant impact on the safety of people's lives.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Jiaozuo Yang Qiu

### 赶快防止春瘟流行
阳秋
1949-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　赶快防止春瘟流行
    由于近来气候忽冷忽热，衣食不慎，矿区周围流行了一种春瘟（小伤寒、血伤寒霍乱不等），病状是起初头痛发晕，一二日后骤然加重，不省人事，焦作王封现已有五六人得此疾病，建议各地政府、医生、卫生部门、各机关医务所同志，加紧防预，不然，如果大量流行起来，对于人民生命安全上是会有很大影响的。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　焦作阳秋
