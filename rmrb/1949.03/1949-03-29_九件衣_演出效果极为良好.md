Title: "Nine Pieces of Clothing" Performance Achieves Excellent Results
Author: Li Ji
Date: 1949-03-29
Page: 2

　　"Nine Pieces of Clothing" Performance Achieves Excellent Results
    [This Newspaper] The entire troupe of alumni from the Beiping Opera School, after three weeks of hard work, gave the first performance of the new revolutionary Peking Opera "Nine Pieces of Clothing" at the Meiqi Theater on East Chang'an Street in Beiping on the previous two evenings, with excellent results. The play offers a profound depiction of the viciousness and insidiousness of the feudal bully Hua Zifang and the corrupt officials Qiao Zidong, and is rich in class education significance. The performance of "Nine Pieces of Clothing" will have a certain impact on Peking Opera, namely that Peking Opera, both in content and form, must be given the necessary gradual transformation so as to achieve the purpose of serving workers, peasants, and soldiers.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Li Ji)

### “九件衣”演出效果极为良好

1949-03-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　“九件衣”演出效果极为良好
    【本报讯】北平戏曲学校校友剧团全体团员，经过三周的努力，已于前昨两晚，假北平东长安街美琪戏院，首次演出新的革命平剧“九件衣”，效果良好。该剧对封建恶霸花自芳与贪官污吏乔子侗毒辣、阴险，均有深刻的刻画，极富阶级教育意义。“九件衣”的演出，对平剧将发生一定影响的，即平剧无论在内容与形式上，必须予以必要的逐步的改造，使之达到为工、农、兵服务之目的。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（李冀）
