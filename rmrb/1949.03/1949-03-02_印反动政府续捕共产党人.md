Title: Indian Reactionary Government Continues to Arrest Communists
Author:
Date: 1949-03-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th. According to a TASS dispatch from Delhi on the 24th: The Indian government continues to carry out large-scale suppression of the Communist Party and other democratic groups. The "Patrika" newspaper reported that more than 1,600 communists have been arrested in various industrial centers.

### 印反动政府续捕共产党人

1949-03-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　印反动政府续捕共产党人
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】据塔斯社德里二十四日讯：印度各地继续对共产党及其他民主团体实行大规模镇压，“派特利卡”报称：各工业中心地区所逮捕的共产党人，已达一千六百名以上。
