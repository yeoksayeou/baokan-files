Title: Preparatory Committee of the Ping City Women's Federation Promotes Election of National Representatives by Women's Organizations
Author: Bai
Date: 1949-03-16
Page: 1

[Report from this newspaper] The Preparatory Committee of the Beiping People's Women's Federation is actively promoting the election of representatives to the First National Congress of Chinese Women by various women's organizations in the city. All representatives will be elected before the 20th of this month. Beiping should elect eleven representatives. Representatives of workers and staff are produced through a two-level election. The election principles are roughly based on integrity, ability to connect with the masses, enthusiasm for women's work, and proficiency in business. In addition, the Preparatory Committee is currently actively collecting historical materials, photographs, and materials on the lives and production of female workers related to the women's movement in Beiping. Furthermore, the additional five representatives of the Beiping Branch of the Chinese Women's Federation for this conference have been formally elected recently, namely Liu Qingyang, Qi Xiaoqing, Shen Cuizhen, Pu Jiexian, and Zhu Ying.

### 平市妇联筹备会推动妇女团体选举全国代表

1949-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　平市妇联筹备会推动妇女团体选举全国代表
    【本报讯】北平人民妇女联合会筹委会，现正积极推动本市各妇女团体，选举出席中国妇女第一次全国代表大会的代表，此项代表，将于本月二十日以前全部选出。北平应选代表十一人。职工代表以二级选举方式产生，选举原则大致以作风正派，能连系群众，热心妇女工作，精通业务为标准。此外筹委会目前正积极搜集有关北平妇女运动史料、照片及女工生活、生产的资料。又中国妇女联谊会北平分会出席此次大会的额外五名代表，已于日前正式选出，为刘清扬、齐笑庆、沈粹缜、浦洁修、朱颖五人。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　（柏）
