Title: Director of the Ping City Tax Bureau Officially Takes Office

Author:
Date: 1949-03-15
Page: 2

　　The Ping City Tax Bureau Director Officially Takes Office
　　[This newspaper's dispatch] Zhao Zishang, the director of the Beiping Tax Bureau and concurrently the director of the Ping City Tax Bureau, and Deputy Director Peng Tao, took office on the 12th.
