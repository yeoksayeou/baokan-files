Title: People's Bank of China Headquarters Revises Silver Dollar Exchange Rate
Author:
Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 2

The People's Bank of China Headquarters revised the exchange rates for pure silver and silver dollars in various parts of North China, effective yesterday (March 17th), as follows: In Peiping, Tianjin, Zhangjiakou, Tangshan, and the entire Jidong area, pure silver is exchanged at 230 yuan in People's Bank notes per tael, silver dollars (big head) at 230 yuan in People's Bank notes per dollar, and small head and miscellaneous foreign coins at 190 yuan in People's Bank notes per dollar. In Baoding and other administrative regions outside the aforementioned areas, pure silver is exchanged at 200 yuan in People's Bank notes per tael, silver dollars (big head) at 200 yuan in People's Bank notes per dollar, and small head and miscellaneous foreign coins at 170 yuan in People's Bank notes per dollar. (Yu).

### 人民银行总行重订银元牌价

1949-03-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　人民银行总行重订银元牌价
    【本报讯】人民银行总行于昨日（十七日）起重订华北各地纯银，银元牌价如下：北平、天津、张家口、唐山以及全冀东区，纯银每两兑人民券二三○元、银元大头一元兑人民券二三○元、小头及杂洋一元兑人民券一九○元。保定及前项地区以外之各行署区，纯银每两兑人民券二○○元、银元大头一元兑人民券二○○元、小头及杂洋一元兑人民券一七○元。（宇）。
