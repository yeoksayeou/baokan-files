Title: Between the Luo River and the Yellow River in Central Shaanxi, Our Army Liberates Dali and Chaoyi Counties, and Pingmin Township
Author:
Date: 1949-03-08
Page: 1

　　From Central Shaanxi, between the Luo River and the Yellow River
  　Our Army Liberates Dali and Chaoyi Counties, and Pingmin Township
    [Xinhua News Agency, Central Shaanxi Front, 6th] The People's Liberation Army on the Central Shaanxi Front successively liberated Dali and Chaoyi counties, and Pingmin township, located between the Luo River and the Yellow River, on the 4th. When liberating Dali city, some enemy troops were captured. On the 3rd and 4th, the People's Liberation Army liberated the area east of Gaoling County town and north of the Wei River, the Liyang town under the jurisdiction of Lintong County, and Jiaokou town, annihilating some enemy troops.

### 陕中洛水与黄河之间　我克大荔朝邑平民

1949-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕中洛水与黄河之间
  　我克大荔朝邑平民
    【新华社陕中前线六日电】陕中前线解放军于四日连续解放洛水与黄河间之大荔、朝邑及平民三县城，解放大荔城时，俘敌一部。解放军于三、四两日解放渭河北岸高陵县城以东、临潼县属之栎阳镇及交口镇，歼敌一部。
