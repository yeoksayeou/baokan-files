Title: Kuomintang-Controlled Areas: Representatives Attending the Women's Congress Hold Symposium on Congress Work Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 1

　　Kuomintang-Controlled Areas
　　Representatives Attending the Women's Congress Hold Symposium on Congress Work Yesterday
    [This newspaper reports] Representatives from Kuomintang-controlled areas attending the First National Women's Congress of China held a symposium at the Beijing Hotel in Ping City on the morning of the 17th to discuss matters concerning the Women's Congress, which is scheduled to open in Ping on the 24th of this month. Attendees included representatives from various locations such as Nanjing-Shanghai, Hong Kong-Kowloon, Kunming, Chongqing, Qingdao, and Taiwan: Tang Guifen, Xie Xuehong, Yu Yunshan, Zhao Ruzhi, Cao Mengjun, Li Dequan, Xu Guangping, Hu Ziying, Shen Ziji, Li Wenyi, Liu Qingyang, Qu Yuan, Huang Wei, and over forty others. In order to strengthen the organizational and propaganda work of the delegation from Kuomintang-controlled areas, attendees unanimously decided to establish a delegation affairs committee and a propaganda committee, and elected Li Dequan, Tang Guifen, and Zhao Ruzhi to be responsible for promoting this (Bai).

### 国民党统治区　出席妇代大会代表　昨日座谈大会工作

1949-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　国民党统治区
  　出席妇代大会代表　昨日座谈大会工作
    【本报讯】出席中国第一次全国妇女代表大会的国民党统治区代表，于十七日上午在平市北京饭店举行座谈会，讨论关于本月二十四日即将在平召开之妇代大会事项。到会者有京沪、港九、昆、重、青岛、台湾各地代表：汤桂芬、谢雪红、余韵珊、赵茹知、曹孟君、李德全、许广平、胡子婴、沈兹九、李文宜、刘清扬、屈沅、黄薇等四十余人。为加强国民党统治区代表团的组织和宣传工作；到会者一致决定成立团务委员会及宣传委员会，并推定李德全、汤桂芬、赵茹知三人负责推动（柏）
