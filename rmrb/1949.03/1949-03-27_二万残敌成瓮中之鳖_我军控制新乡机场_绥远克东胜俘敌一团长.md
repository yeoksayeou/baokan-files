Title: Twenty Thousand Remnant Enemies Become Turtles in a Jar; Our Army Controls Xinxiang Airport; Eastern Victory in Suiyuan Captures Enemy Regiment Commander
Author:
Date: 1949-03-27
Page: 1

　　Twenty Thousand Remnant Enemies Become Turtles in a Jar
    Our Army Controls Xinxiang Airport
  　Eastern Victory in Suiyuan Captures Enemy Regiment Commander
    [Taihang, 23rd Dispatch] (Delayed) The People's Liberation Army on the豫北 (Yu Bei) front, on the night of the 10th of this month at 9 o'clock, with highly alert and composed actions, advanced into the suburbs of Xinxiang in one fell swoop, completely controlling Xinxiang Airport (located about two li west of the city), cutting off the only escape route for the isolated remnant bandits in Xinxiang who were airlifting south. The Kuomintang's 40th Army under Li Zhenqing and the bandits Wang Sanzhu and Zhang Jingzhong of northern Henan, totaling nearly 20,000 men, have become turtles in a jar.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 24th Dispatch] A unit of the People's Liberation Army successively liberated Dongsheng County in Suiyuan Province and Kangjiaqu and Erzihao to its east from the 18th to the 20th, wiping out a portion of the 11th Regiment of the puppet security division and capturing enemy Deputy Regiment Commander Li Wenhuan alive.

### 二万残敌成瓮中之鳖  我军控制新乡机场　绥远克东胜俘敌一团长

1949-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　二万残敌成瓮中之鳖
    我军控制新乡机场
  　绥远克东胜俘敌一团长
    【太行二十三日电】（迟到）豫北前线人民解放军，于本月十日夜九时，以高度机敏沉着的动作，一举挺进新乡城郊，完全控制新乡飞机场（位于城西约二里），斩断正在空运南逃的新乡孤岛残匪的唯一逃路。国民党四十军李振清部及豫北贯匪王三祝、张敬忠等约近两万余人已成瓮中之鳖。
    【新华社延安二十四日电】人民解放军某部于十八日至二十日连续解放绥远省东胜县及其以东之康家渠、耳字濠等地，歼灭伪保安师十一团之一部，生俘敌副团长李温环。
