Title: Demanding Wage Increases: Hundreds of Thousands of Workers in Tokyo Strike
Author:
Date: 1949-03-21
Page: 3

　　Demanding Wage Increases
    Hundreds of Thousands of Workers in Tokyo Strike
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 19th. According to a Reuters dispatch from Tokyo on the 16th: Hundreds of thousands of workers in Tokyo and its suburbs participated in a three-day strike by Eastern Railway Company workers demanding wage increases. The strikers temporarily resumed work on the 15th pending the outcome of renewed negotiations between labor and management. Furthermore, the All-Japan Seamen's Union began a series of "short-term" strikes on the night of the 15th to protest proposed layoffs of seamen and the management's refusal to grant wage increases. The union stated that the strikes would take place in areas such as Tokyo, Kobe, Hokkaido, and Kyushu.

### 要求增加工资  东京数十万职工罢工

1949-03-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　要求增加工资
    东京数十万职工罢工
    【新华社陕北十九日电】据路透社东京十六日电：东京市及其近郊的数十万职工，曾参加历时三天的东部铁道公司职工要求增加工资的罢工。罢工者于十五日衣暂时复工以待劳资两方重开的谈判之结果。又全日海员工会已于十五日夜开始一连串的“短期”罢工，以抗议拟议中的解雇海员与资方的拒绝增资要求。工会方面称：罢工将在东京、神户、北海道与九州等地区进行。
