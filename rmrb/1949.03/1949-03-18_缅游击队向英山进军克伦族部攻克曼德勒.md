Title: Burmese Guerrillas Advance on Insein; Karen Troops Capture Mandalay
Author:
Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th. According to an Associated Press report from Rangoon on the 10th, a guerrilla force led by the Burmese Communist Party has moved north from the Irrawaddy Delta and is advancing towards Insein, ten miles north of Rangoon. Furthermore, according to Agence France-Presse, London, 14th, the Karen troops that entered Mandalay on the 12th have now occupied the city.

### 缅游击队向英山进军克伦族部攻克曼德勒

1949-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　缅游击队向英山进军克伦族部攻克曼德勒
    【新华社陕北十七日电】据美联社十日由仰光报导，缅甸共产党领导下的一支游击队，已由伊洛瓦底江三角洲北上，并正向仰光以北十哩的英山前进。另据法新社伦敦十四日讯，十二日攻入曼德勒的克伦族部队业已占领该城。
