Title: Under Li Zongren's Orders, Bandit Hu Re-Invades Central Shaanxi
Author:
Date: 1949-03-17
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Shaanxi Central Front, 15th—Due to the orders of Li Zongren, the bandit troops of Hu Zongnan, who were originally preparing to retreat into Sichuan, have now re-invaded central Shaanxi. From the 10th to the 12th, they have successively occupied our Ningping, Chunhua, and Yaoxian cities, as well as many surrounding villages and towns. Eight regiments of Ma Bufang's bandit troops from Qinghai also moved eastward from Pingliang, Jingchuan, and Lingtai areas in eastern Gansu at the same time, occupying Chunhua and the nearby Tongrun and Tiewang areas on the 10th.

### 在李宗仁的命令下　胡匪又向陕中回犯

1949-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　在李宗仁的命令下
  　胡匪又向陕中回犯
    【新华社陕中前线十五日电】由于李宗仁的命令，本来准备退入四川的胡宗南匪军，现又向陕中回犯，自十日至十二日已先后侵占我宁平、淳化、耀县三城及其周围许多村镇。青海马步芳的匪军八个团，亦于同时由陇东平凉、泾川、灵台一带向东出动，十日占领淳化及其附近的通润、铁王等地。
