Title: Yangqu Militia Eliminates 19 Yan Bandits
Author: Ji He, Bao Fu
Date: 1949-03-27
Page: 2

　　Yangqu Militia
  　Eliminates 19 Yan Bandits
    [Jinzhong News] The Yangqu militia eliminated 19 Yan bandits. On the 2nd of this month, a detachment of the 9th Work Team of the Armed Special Service of the 39th Division of the Yan bandits, consisting of 23 men led by the pseudo-Captain Ge Zhengwen, ambushed the Qizi Mountain area on the border of the eighth and second districts of Yangqu, attempting to attack the Second District Office. Yang Quanxi, a member of the detachment, seized the opportunity to defect to our side with a submachine gun to inform us. Our Second District militia, in coordination with a certain squad of the "Zhengzhou Unit," surrounded the bandit special agents, eliminating 19 including Ge Zhengwen, seizing 3 submachine guns, 4 rifles, 1 pistol, and a portion of other military supplies.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Ji He, Bao Fu)

### 阳曲民兵　歼阎匪十九名
计和　保富
1949-03-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　阳曲民兵
  　歼阎匪十九名
    【晋中讯】阳曲我民兵歼阎匪十九名。本月二日，阎匪三十九师武装特务第九工作队一部二十三名，伪装我军，由伪队长葛政文带领，设伏阳曲八区与二区交界处之琪子山一带，妄图袭击我二区区公所。该队队员杨全喜乘隙携冲锋枪一支投奔我方告密。我二区民兵配合“郑州部”某班，当将该部匪特包围，歼葛政文以下十九名，获冲锋枪三支，步枪四支，手枪一支，其他军用品一部。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（计和、保富）
