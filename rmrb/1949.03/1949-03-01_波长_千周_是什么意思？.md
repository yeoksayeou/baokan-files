Author: Liu Wei
Date: 1949-03-01
Page: 4

Column: Inquiry and Reply

　　What do "Wavelength" and "Kilocycle" mean?

Editor:
    What does "wavelength 353 meters, 850 kilocycles" of the Peiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station mean? Then what do "390 meters, 770 kilocycles" mean, with two meters and kilocycles linked together? Please explain!
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Respectfully,
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Liu Wei, February 10th

Comrade Liu Wei:
    A brief answer to your inquiry is as follows:

    Radio stations rely on radio waves to transmit sound, allowing listeners to tune into broadcasts on specific wavelengths. Therefore, the "wavelength" explained here refers to the length of the radio wave. The speed of radio waves is the same as the speed of light, which is 300 million meters per second. "Frequency" is the number of radio waves generated per second. The wavelength of the Peiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station is 353 meters, and if you divide 300 million meters by it, you get 850 kilocycles, which means the wavelength is 353 meters, and there are 850,000 radio waves (one thousand is one kilocycle). Because this number is too large, it is customary to use a kilocycle as a unit, hence the abbreviation 850 kilocycles.

    As for the two wavelengths and kilocycles, it is because the station is currently using two transmitters with different wavelengths to broadcast the same program simultaneously, that is: one transmitter uses a wavelength of 353 meters, and the other uses 390 meters.

### “波长”“千周”是什么意思？
刘伟
1949-03-01
第4版()
专栏：询问与答复

　　“波长”“千周”是什么意思？编者同志：
    北平新华广播电台“波长三五三公尺，八五零千周”是什么意思？接着下面又一个“三九零公尺，七七零千周”，两个公尺千周连在一起，又是什么意思？请解答！敬礼
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　刘伟　二月十日刘伟同志：
    来函所询简答如下：
    广播电台是依靠电波传达声音，使听众能在一定的波长上收听播送的节目。因此，这里解说的“波长”，是说电波之长。无线电波速度与光速相同，即每秒钟三万万公尺，“周率”是每秒钟产生的电波数目。北平新华广播电台的波长是三五三公尺，用它来除三万万公尺，则是八五零千周，就是三五三公尺的波长，而电波是八十五万个（一千个即是一千周）因这数字太多，习惯以一子周为单位，故合成为八五零千周。
    至于有两个波长和千周是因为该台现在用两具不同波长的发射机同时播送同样节目的原因，即：一个发射机使用的波长是三五三公尺，另一个是三九零公尺。
