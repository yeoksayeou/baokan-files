Title: Albanian Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1949-03-25
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Shanhua, 24th. TASS, Moscow: The government delegation of the People's Republic of Albania flew to Moscow on the 21st. The delegation includes: Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Hoxha; Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Koleka; Vice Chairman, Theodosi; and responsible personnel from the Ministries of Commerce, Industry, and Public Works, as well as Deputy Chief of Staff, Colonel Shefqet Peçi. Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Vyshinsky, and others went to the airport to welcome them. The Soviet Ambassador to Albania, Zuevakin, also returned to Moscow at the same time. After disembarking, Hoxha delivered a speech, stating that the purpose of the trip was to further strengthen the economic relations between Albania and the Soviet Union, and expressed heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet government and people for the assistance they have given to the Albanian people in the past and present.

### 阿尔巴尼亚代表团  飞抵莫斯科

1949-03-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚代表团
    飞抵莫斯科
    【新华社陕化二十四日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团于二十一日飞抵莫斯科。代表团包括：部长会议主席霍查，国家计划委员会主席柯列科，副主席西奥迪希以及商务、工业、公共工程各部负责人员与副参谋总长实查尼上校。苏联部长会议副主席米高扬、外交部长维辛斯基等均赴机场欢迎。苏驻阿公使朱瓦金亦同时返抵莫斯科。霍查下机后当即发表演说，表明此行是为了进一步加强阿尔巴尼亚与苏联的经济关系，并对苏联政府与人民过去和现在给予阿尔巴尼亚人民的援助，表示衷心感谢。
