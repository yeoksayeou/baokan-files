Title: Opening of the Provisional People's Congress of the Central Plains Liberated Area
Author:
Date: 1949-03-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 10th. The Provisional People's Congress of the Central Plains Liberated Area opened at 10:00 AM on the 3rd of this month. Eighty-one people's representatives from all walks of life in the Central Plains attended the meeting, and Deng Zihui, Liu Zihui, Pan Zinian and other seventeen people were elected as the presidium of the congress. The congress began preparations at the beginning of this year, and the people's representatives attending the congress were elected separately by region and group.

### 中原解放区临时人代会开幕

1949-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　中原解放区临时人代会开幕
    【新华社中原十日讯】中原解放区临时人民代表大会，已于本月三日上午十时开幕。出席大会的有中原各地各界人民代表八十一人，邓子恢、刘子久、潘梓年等十七人被推选为大会主席团。按该大会于本年年初即开始筹备，出席大会的人民代表系按区域和团体为单位分别推选产生。
