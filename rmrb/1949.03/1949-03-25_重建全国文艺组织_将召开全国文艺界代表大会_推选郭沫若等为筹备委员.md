Title: Reconstructing National Literary and Art Organizations; National Congress of Literary and Art Circles to be Convened; Guo Moruo and Others Elected as Preparatory Committee Members
Author: Jin Feng
Date: 1949-03-25
Page: 1

　　Reconstructing National Literary and Art Organizations
    National Congress of Literary and Art Circles to be Convened
    Guo Moruo and Others Elected as Preparatory Committee Members
    [Our Dispatch] In order to adapt to the needs of the national revolutionary situation and revolutionary tasks, to unite all progressive literary and art forces in the liberated areas and the Kuomintang-controlled areas, and to establish new national literary and art organizations, the General Council of the All-China Literary Association in Peiping and the directors of the North China Literary Association held a joint meeting at the Beijing Hotel on the 22nd, deciding to convene the All-China Congress of Literary and Art Workers. A preparatory committee was elected on the spot, and the first meeting of the preparatory committee was held on the 24th. The elected members of the preparatory committee are: Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, Tian Han, Hong Shen, Zheng Zhenduo, Ye Shengtao, Zhou Yang, Xiao San, Shakov, Ding Ling, Cao Jinghua, Cao Yu, Xu Beihong, Liu Yazi, Yu Pingbo, Hu Feng, He Luting, Cheng Yanqiu, Li Guangtian, Ye Qianyu, Zhao Shuli, Ke Zhongping, Lü Ji, Gu Yuan, Yuan Muzhi, Ai Qing, Ouyang Shan, Huang Mo, Li Bozhao, Ma Yanxiang, Song Zhi's, Liu Baiyu, Sheng Jialun and other thirty-seven people, and Guo Moruo was elected as the director of the preparatory committee, Mao Dun and Zhou Yang as deputy directors. Regarding the issue of the generation of representatives for the congress, it was decided at the meeting that the directors of the literary associations of the five major liberated areas of North China, Northeast China, Northwest China, East China, and Central Plains, as well as the directors and supervisors of the General Council of the All-China Literary Association and its branches, would be the natural representatives of the congress. In addition, the preparatory committee would invite a number of literary and art figures to participate in the congress as appropriate.
    [Another Dispatch] The directors and supervisors of the General Council of the All-China Literary Association in Peiping, including Guo Moruo, Ma Xulun, Liu Yazi, Tian Han, Mao Dun, Zheng Zhenduo, Cao Yu, Ye Shengtao, Zhou Jianren, Hong Shen, Xu Guangping, Ge Yihong, Zhang Ximan, and Ge Baoquan, and other nineteen people, held a meeting on the 22nd and resolved that the General Council of the Literary Association, originally in Shanghai, would move to Beiping for office starting from today, and would work with the North China Literary Association to prepare for the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers, so as to produce new national literary and art organizations.
    [Another Dispatch] At the joint meeting of the directors and supervisors of the General Council of the All-China Literary Association in Peiping and the directors of the North China Literary Association on the 22nd, Guo Moruo, Zheng Zhenduo, Tian Han, Hong Shen, Cao Yu, Xiao San, Cao Jinghua, Zhao Shuli, Gu Yuan, Xu Beihong, Dai Ailian, Cheng Yanqiu and other twelve people were elected to go abroad to participate in the World Peace Congress to be held in Paris on the 20th of next month, and are now preparing various literary and art materials to bring to the conference.
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Jin Feng)

### 重建全国文艺组织  将召开全国文艺界代表大会  推选郭沫若等为筹备委员
金凤
1949-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　重建全国文艺组织
    将召开全国文艺界代表大会     
    推选郭沫若等为筹备委员
    【本报讯】为适应全国革命形势与革命任务的需要，团结解放区与国民党统治区一切进步文艺力量，建立新的全国性的文艺组织，中华全国文艺协会在平的总会理监事及华北文协理事，特于二十二日在北京饭店举行联席会议，决定召开中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会，当场推选筹委会，并于二十四日举行第一次筹委会议。当选筹委为：郭沫若、茅盾、田汉、洪深、郑振铎、叶圣陶、周扬、萧三、沙可夫、丁玲、曹靖华、曹禺、徐悲鸿、柳亚子、俞平伯、胡风、贺绿汀、程砚秋、李广田、叶浅予、赵树理、柯仲平、吕骥、古元、袁牧之、艾青、欧阳山、荒煤、李伯钊、马彦祥、宋之的、刘白羽、盛家伦等三十七人，并选出郭沫若为筹委会主任，茅盾、周扬为副主任。关于代表大会代表产生问题，会上决定华北、东北、西北、华东、中原五大解放区文协理事及中华全国文协总会及各分会理监事为代表大会当然代表，此外由筹委会酌情邀请若干文艺界人士参加代表大会。
    【又讯】在平的全国文协总会理监事郭沫若、马叙伦、柳亚子、田汉、茅盾、郑振铎、曹禺、叶圣陶、周建人、洪深、许广平、葛一虹、张西曼、戈宝权等十九人，二十二日曾开会议决，原在上海之文协总会，即日起移至北平办公，并会同华北文协筹备全国文学艺术工作者代表大会，以便产生新的全国性的文艺界组织。
    【又讯】二十二日中华全国文艺协会在平的总会理监事与华北文协理事联席会上，推选出郭沫若、郑振铎、田汉、洪深、曹禺、萧三、曹靖华、赵树理、古元、徐悲鸿、戴爱莲、程砚秋等十二人出国参加下月二十日在巴黎召开的世界和平大会，现正准备各种文艺材料，携往大会。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（金凤）
