Title: To Adapt to the Needs of the Developing Situation, the Jidong Area is Placed Under the Jurisdiction of North China
Author:
Date: 1949-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, North China, 9th. In order to adapt to the needs of the developing situation, the North China People's Government and the Northeast Administrative Committee have agreed to place the Jidong Administrative Region under the jurisdiction of the North China People's Government. The director of the administrative office will be succeeded by Li Gengtao, the deputy director of the office. The former director of the administrative office has resigned with the approval of the Northeast Administrative Committee. The Jidong Administrative Region governs the eastern part of Hebei Province, the area east of the Pinggu Road (Beiping to Gubeikou) and the Pingjin section of the Beining Railway.

### 适应形势发展需要　冀东区划归华北

1949-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　适应形势发展需要
  　冀东区划归华北
    【新华社华北九日电】为适应形势发展的需要，华北人民政府与东北行政委员会商定，将冀东行政区划归华北人民政府管辖。行署主任由该署副主任李耕涛接任，前行署主任已呈经东北行政委员会辞职。按冀东行政区辖河北省东部，平古路（北平至古北口）及北宁路平津段以东地区。
