Title: Tianjin Postal Administration Receives and Sends Mail to Jiangnan

Author: 
Date: 1949-03-16
Page: 1

　　Tianjin Postal Administration Receives and Sends Mail to Jiangnan
    [Tianjin Electric Dispatch] The Tianjin Postal Administration officially began receiving and sending mail to Jiangnan yesterday. The first batch of mail transported by the "Greater Shanghai" vessel has been mostly sent out.

### 天津邮管局收发江南邮件

1949-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　天津邮管局收发江南邮件
    【天津电】天津邮政管理局昨日起已正式收发寄往江南邮件，前由“大上海”轮运来的第一批邮件已大部发出。
