Title: Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Convenes; Joint Session of the Two Chambers Hears Report on This Year's National Budget; Yefremov Promoted to Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1949-03-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th: TASS, Moscow—The Fifth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union opened here on the 10th. The Fifth Session of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR opened at 2 p.m. in the Kremlin, presided over by Ivan Vaviloff, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union. Malenkov, Voroshilov, Shvernik, Kaganovich, Beria, Popov, Ponomarenko, and Suslov were warmly welcomed by Soviet deputies and guests when they arrived.

The Fifth Session of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR opened at 4 p.m. in the Kremlin meeting hall, presided over by Vasily Kuznetsov, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities. Mikoyan, Shvernik, Baysetin, Ponomarenko, Suslov, and Shkiryatov were greeted with cheers and applause from Soviet deputies and guests as they took their seats on the government dais.

The first meeting of the two chambers adopted the same agenda: to approve the Soviet Union's national budget for 1949, to hear a report on the implementation of the 1947 national budget, and to approve decrees already issued by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. The two chambers also decided that a report on the 1949 national budget would be heard at a joint session of the two chambers; the respective reports of the budget committees of the two chambers, budget discussions and approvals, and the approval of decrees of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet would be conducted separately in the sessions of the two chambers.

The joint session of the Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities was held at 7 p.m. in the Kremlin under the chairmanship of Vasily Kuznetsov, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities. Stalin, Molotov, Beria, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Shvernik, Bulganin, Kosygin, Suslov, Popov, and Ponomarenko were greeted with prolonged cheers from Soviet deputies as they took their seats on the government dais. Soviet Minister of Finance and Soviet Deputy Arseny Zverev delivered a report on the Soviet Union's national budget for 1949 at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th: TASS, Moscow, 8th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed Yefremov as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and relieved Yefremov of his post as Minister of the Machine Tool Manufacturing Industry. Kostousov will succeed him as Minister of the Machine Tool Manufacturing Industry.

### 苏联最高苏维埃会议开幕  两院联席会听取今年国家预算报告  叶非列莫夫晋任部长会议副主席

1949-03-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃会议开幕
    两院联席会听取今年国家预算报告
    叶非列莫夫晋任部长会议副主席
    【新华社陕北十二日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃第五次会议于十日在此揭幕。苏联最高苏维埃联邦院第五次会议在联邦院主席伊凡·巴非洛夫主持下，于下午二时在克里姆林宫开幕。马林科夫、伏罗希洛夫、斯维尔尼克、卡冈诺维奇、贝利亚、波波夫、波诺玛林科、苏斯洛夫到会时，受到苏维埃代表们与来宾的热烈欢迎。
    苏联最高苏维埃民族院第五次会议，则在该院主席瓦西里·库兹涅佐夫主持下，于下午四时在克里姆林宫会议厅开幕。米高扬、斯维尔尼克、拜西金、波诺玛林科、苏斯洛夫、什基里雄托夫就政府席时，受到苏维埃代表们与来宾的欢呼鼓掌。
    两院第一次会通过相同的会议日程：通过苏联一九四九年的国家预算，听取一九四七年国家预算执行情形的报告，通过苏联最高苏维埃主席团所已发出的命令。两院会议并同样决定在两院联席会上听取关于一九四九年国家预算的报告；两院预算委员会各自的报告，预算的讨论以及通过，最高苏维埃主席团命令的通过，均将在两院会议上分别进行。
    联邦院与民族院联席会议在民族院主席瓦西里·库兹涅佐夫主持下，于同日下午七时在克里姆林宫开会。斯大林、莫洛托夫、贝利亚、马林科夫、米高扬、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、斯维尔尼克、布尔加宁、科西金、苏斯洛夫、波波夫及波诺玛林科就坐政府席时，苏维埃代表们报以经久不绝的欢呼。苏联财政部长、苏维埃代表阿塞尼·兹维列夫在会上作了关于苏联一九四九年国家预算的报告。
    【新华社陕北十二日电】塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团顷任叶非列莫夫为苏联部长会议副主席，并解除叶氏的工作母机制造部部长的职务。由科斯托梭夫继任工作母机制造部部长。
