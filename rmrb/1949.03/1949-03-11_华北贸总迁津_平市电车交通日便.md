Title: North China Trade Corporation Moves to Tianjin; Beijing Municipal Tram Transportation Becomes More Convenient

Author:
Date: 1949-03-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 9th: The North China Trade Corporation moved from Shijiazhuang to Tianjin a few days ago and started operating on the 7th.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th: Tram transportation in Beijing is becoming increasingly convenient. In the middle of last month, trams dispatched from the factory numbered 57 per day, but this has increased to 66 to 72 at the beginning of this month. On the 7th of this month, Vice Mayor Xu Bing invited 18 tram company workers to a discussion. At the meeting, the workers put forward many constructive suggestions for the development of urban tram transportation and workers' welfare. Vice Mayor Xu encouraged the workers to study politics and culture diligently, improve their professional skills, and serve the construction of the new China. After the meeting, they had dinner together.

### 华北贸总迁津　平市电车交通日便

1949-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北贸总迁津
  　平市电车交通日便
    【新华社天津九日电】华北贸易总公司已于日前由石家庄迁津，七日起开始办公。
    【新华社北平九日电】北平市内电车交通日益便利。上月中旬，电车每日出厂五十七辆，本月初已增至六十六辆至七十二辆。本月七日，徐冰副市长邀集电车公司工友十八人，举行谈话会。会上，工友们对发展市内电车交通和工人福利事业，提了许多建设性意见。徐副市长勉励工人努力学习政治和文化，提高业务技术，为新中国的建设效力。会后并共进晚餐。
