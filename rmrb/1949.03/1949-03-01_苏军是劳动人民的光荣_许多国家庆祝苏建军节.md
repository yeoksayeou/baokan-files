Title: The Soviet Army is the Glory of the Working People; Many Countries Celebrate Soviet Army Day
Author:
Date: 1949-03-01
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 27th -- TASS reports: People in many countries around the world are enthusiastically celebrating the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

	On the 22nd, social organizations in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, held a celebration meeting. The Czechoslovak Minister of National Defense, General Svoboda, delivered a speech at the meeting. When he mentioned the name of the Soviet Army leader Marshal Stalin, thunderous cheers erupted.
	On the same day, a celebration meeting was also held in Warsaw, the capital of Poland. Polish President Bolesław Bierut, Prime Minister Józef Cyrankiewicz, and Soviet Marshal Sokolovsky, specially invited by the Polish government, attended the meeting. Polish Deputy Minister of National Defense Spychalski pointed out in his speech: Under the leadership of Grand Marshal Stalin, the Soviet Army rescued mankind from Hitler's brutal rule. He said: The Soviet Army is the glory of the Soviet people and the working people of the world. Sokolovsky delivered a reply.
	In Bucharest, the capital of Romania, factories and organizations held meetings on the 23rd. In Cluj, the capital of Transylvania, the Romanian-Soviet Friendship Association also held a Soviet Army exhibition.
	In Budapest, the capital of Hungary, the Hungarian Army and the Hungarian-Soviet Cultural Association jointly hosted a celebration meeting. President Szakasits of the Republic, Speaker of the Parliament Révai, Minister of National Defense Farkas, and Soviet Ambassador to Hungary Pushkin attended the meeting. Lieutenant General Nógrádi thanked the Soviet Army for liberating Hungary from Hitler's enslavement in his report.
	In Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, the Presidium of the Little Khural of the Republic, the Central Committee of the People's Revolutionary Party, and representatives from various agencies and organizations in Ulaanbaatar and the garrison troops of the People's Revolutionary Army held a celebration meeting on the 23rd. Those present included Marshal Choibalsan and Soviet Plenipotentiary Minister to Mongolia, Brikhedov. Choibalsan also sent a telegram to Minister of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, to express congratulations.
	A celebration meeting was also held in Pyongyang, where the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea is located. Among those present were members of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly, headed by Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly, members of the Supreme People's Assembly, and government officials. The meeting passed a congratulatory telegram to Grand Marshal Stalin amidst cheers.
	In Germany, SED leaders Wilhelm Pieck and Otto Grotewohl sent a telegram to the Soviet military government in the Soviet occupation zone of Germany to express their respects on behalf of the Central Committee of the SED. The Berlin City Council and various democratic organizations in the city also congratulated Major General Kotikov, the Soviet commander.
	In the capitals of France, Japan, and Sweden, the respective Soviet-Friendship Associations hosted celebration meetings.
	Meanwhile, Soviet diplomatic envoys stationed in France, the United States, Canada, Japan, Austria, Finland, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, and other countries held grand receptions on the 23rd. French Communist Party leaders Maurice Thorez, Jacques Duclos, Florimond Bonte, and Auguste Lecœur were invited to attend the reception at the Soviet Embassy in France. The Central Committee of the French Communist Party also sent a telegram to express its respect to the Soviet Army.

### 苏军是劳动人民的光荣　许多国家庆祝苏建军节

1949-03-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏军是劳动人民的光荣
  　许多国家庆祝苏建军节
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】塔斯社讯：世界许多国家的人民，都热烈庆祝苏军的建军三十一周年。
    二十二日，捷克斯洛伐克首都布拉格的社会团体，举行了庆祝大会，捷国防部长斯沃波达将军在会上发表了演讲，当他提到苏军领袖斯大林元帅的名字时，欢呼雷动。
    同日，波兰首都华沙也举行庆祝会，波兰共和国总统贝鲁特、总理西伦凯维兹、及波兰政府特别邀请的苏联元帅索科洛夫斯基等，均出席大会。波国防部副部长斯比查尔斯基在演说中指出：苏军在斯大林大元帅的领导下，把人类从希特勒野蛮统治下拯救了出来。他说：苏军是苏联人民及全世界劳动人民的光荣。索科洛夫斯基曾致答辞。
    在罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特，各工厂、机关于二十三日都举行了大会。在外西尔瓦尼亚的首府克鲁依，罗苏友好协会并举办了苏军展览会。
    在匈牙利首都布达佩斯，匈军与匈苏文化协会共同主持了庆祝大会。共和国总统斯查卡西兹、议会主席奈奇、国防部长法卡斯及苏驻匈大使普士金等都出席了大会诺格拉第中将在作报告时，感谢苏军把匈牙利从希特勒的奴役下解放出来。
    在蒙古人民共和国首都库伦，共和国小呼拉尔主席团、人民革命党中央委员会及库伦市各机关团体与人民革命军卫戍部队的代表，于二十三日举行了庆祝大会。出席者有却伊巴桑元帅、苏联驻蒙古特命全权公使布利克贺多夫等人。却伊巴桑并电苏联武装部队部部长布尔加宁致贺。
    朝鲜人民民主共和国政府所在地的平壤，也举行了庆祝大会。出席的有以朝鲜最高人民议会主席团主席金科凤为首的议会主席团委员，最高人民议会议员及政府官员。大会在欢呼声中通过向斯大林大元帅致敬电。
    在德国，统一社会党领袖皮克及格罗提渥，代表统一社会党中央委员会致电德境苏占区军政府致敬。柏林市议会与该市各民主组织，亦向苏军司令柯夫少将祝贺。
    在法、日、瑞典三国的首都，各该国的对苏友好协会都主持了庆祝大会。
    同时，苏联驻法、美、加、日、奥、芬、瑞典、挪、丹等国的外交使节，都在二十三日分别举行盛大招待会。法共领袖多列士、杜克洛、佛拉商、加善等都应邀出席了苏驻法大使馆的招待会。法共中央并曾致电向苏军致敬。
