Title: Zhangjiakou: 40,000 People Vaccinated
Author:
Date: 1949-03-31
Page: 2

Zhangjiakou
　　40,000 People Vaccinated
    [Zhangyuan News] The Zhangjiakou Municipal Government organized doctors to provide free smallpox vaccinations. From March 2nd to 15th, more than 40,880 people were vaccinated. In the seventh district, the number of children vaccinated accounted for 90 percent of the total number of children, which was 4,045.

### 张家口　四万人种痘

1949-03-31
第2版()
专栏：

　　张家口
    四万人种痘
    【张垣讯】张家口市政府组织医生免费施种牛痘，从三月二日至十五日，已有四万零八百八十余人种了牛痘。七区儿童种牛痘的占儿童总数四千零四十五名的百分之九十。
