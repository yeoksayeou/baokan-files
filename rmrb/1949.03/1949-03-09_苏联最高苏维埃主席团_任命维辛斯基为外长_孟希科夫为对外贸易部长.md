Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Appoints Vyshinsky as Foreign Minister, Menshikov as Minister of Foreign Trade
Author:
Date: 1949-03-09
Page: 3

　　The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
    Appoints Vyshinsky as Foreign Minister
    Menshikov as Minister of Foreign Trade
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 6th] TASS reported: On the 4th, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR relieved Molotov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, of his concurrent post as Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, and appointed Vyshinsky, the former First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, as Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR. On the 5th, the Council of Ministers of the USSR appointed Gromyko as First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 6th] TASS reported: On the 4th, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR relieved Mikoyan, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, of his concurrent post as Minister of Foreign Trade of the USSR, and appointed Menshikov as Minister of Foreign Trade of the USSR.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团  任命维辛斯基为外长  孟希科夫为对外贸易部长

1949-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃主席团
    任命维辛斯基为外长
    孟希科夫为对外贸易部长
    【新华社陕北六日电】塔斯社讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团于四日解除苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫所兼任的苏联外交部长职务，任命原第一副外长维辛斯基为苏联外交部长。苏联部长会议于五日任命葛罗米柯为苏联外交部第一副外长。
    【新华社陕北六日电】塔斯社讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团于四日解除苏联部长会议副主席米高扬所兼任的苏联对外贸易部长的职务，任命孟希科夫为苏联对外贸易部长。
