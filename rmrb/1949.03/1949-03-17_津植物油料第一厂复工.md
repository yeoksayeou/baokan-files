Title: Tianjin No. 1 Vegetable Oil Factory Resumes Production
Author:
Date: 1949-03-17
Page: 2

Tianjin Dispatch: The No. 1 Factory of the China Vegetable Oil Factory, the largest state-owned oil and fat industrial enterprise in the Tianjin-Beijing area, resumed production on the 7th of this month. Before the liberation of Tianjin, the factory had been shut down for several months due to a shortage of raw materials. The factory has a daily capacity of 110 tons of cottonseed oil, 800 boxes of soap, 400 tons of cottonseed cake, and 3 tons of vulcanized green.

### 津植物油料第一厂复工

1949-03-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　津植物油料第一厂复工
    【天津电】平津一带油料工业中规模最大的公营中国植物油料厂第一厂已于本月七日复工。津市解放前，该厂因原料缺乏已停工数月。按该厂每日可生棉子油一百一十吨、肥皂八百箱、棉饼四百吨、硫化青三吨。
