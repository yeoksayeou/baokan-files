Title: North China Military and Political University, People's Revolutionary University Complete Third Examination

Author:
Date: 1949-03-15
Page: 1

　　North China Military and Political University People's Revolutionary University Complete Third Examination
    [This newspaper's report] The answer sheets for the third new student examination of North China Military and Political University and North China People's Revolutionary University have been graded. North China Military and Political University has accepted 725 regular students and 34 alternate students. The university has issued a notice that admitted students must register on the 16th and 17th, and enroll on the 18th. North China People's Revolutionary University has admitted 4,443 students.

### 华北军政大学  人民革命大学第三次考试完竣

1949-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北军政大学  人民革命大学第三次考试完竣
    【本报讯】华北军政大学和华北人民革命大学第三次新生考试试卷业已评阅完毕，华北军政大学正取生共七百二十五名，备取生三十四名，该校发出通告，录取各生须于十六、十七两日报到，十八日入学。华北人民革命大学录取学生四千四百四十三名。
