Title: U.S. Imperialism Prepares New Tools of War: North Atlantic Treaty Draft Negotiation Ends
Author: 
Date: 1949-03-19
Page: 3

　　U.S. Imperialism Prepares New Tools of War
  　North Atlantic Treaty
    Draft Negotiation Ends
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 18th: The formal negotiations between U.S. Secretary of State Acheson and representatives from Britain, Canada, France, the Netherlands, Belgium, Luxembourg, Norway, and other countries regarding the North Atlantic Treaty draft, which is aimed at instigating a new war, concluded on the night of the 15th. The formal negotiations between the eight countries began on February 25th, lasting a total of 19 days. According to a statement issued by the U.S. State Department on the 11th, the full text of the draft North Atlantic Treaty is expected to be published today; the signing of the treaty will take place in Washington during the first week of April.

### 美帝准备新战争工具　北大西洋公约  草案谈判结束

1949-03-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　美帝准备新战争工具
  　北大西洋公约
    草案谈判结束
    【新华社陕北十八日电】美国务卿艾奇逊与英、加、法、菏、比、卢、挪等国代表关于挑拨新战争的北大西洋公约草案的正式谈判，已于十五日夜结束。八国之间的正式谈判开始于二月二十五日，前后共历时十九天。据美国务院十一日发表的声明称：北大西洋公约草约的全文，预期于今日公布；条约之签字，将于四月份第一周在华盛顿举行。
