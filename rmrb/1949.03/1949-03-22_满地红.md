Title: All Red
Author: Liu Yazi
Date: 1949-03-22
Page: 4

　　All Red
    Liu Yazi  
    The sun comes out, all red
    We have a Mao Zedong
    The people suffered for three thousand years
    Today, they turn over and are infinitely happy
