Date: 1949-03-07

Letters to the Editor Editor:

　　The article "Yangquan Yugong Coal Mine Workers Overcome Wind, Snow, and Severe Cold to Transport Coal, Hauling Over 180,000 Tons in Thirteen Days," reprinted on page two of the People's Daily on February 21st, contains an error. The number "over 180,000 tons" should be "over 1,800 tons." Due to the carelessness of our newspaper editors, the decimal point in the original manuscript was misread, leading to such a large error. When the author sent a letter requesting correction, we did not carefully examine the original manuscript or humbly review ourselves. Instead, we subjectively assumed that the author had misplaced the decimal point in the original manuscript. Therefore, in our correction letter to the Shijiazhuang Daily, we shifted the responsibility for this error to the author. It was not until the leadership urged a thorough investigation of the original manuscript that we discovered the responsibility for the error should be borne by our newspaper editors. We are now issuing a correction in our own newspaper, apologizing to the author, and requesting you to issue a correction on our behalf.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　          Yangquan Workers' Newspaper
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　February 22nd

### 来函照登

1949-03-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　来函照登编辑同志：
    人民日报二月二十一日第二版转载的“阳泉裕工煤矿工人战胜风雪严寒运炭，十三天运炭十八万余吨”一文，其中数目字的“十八万余吨”，应该是一千八百多吨，因为本报编辑的不细心，把原稿数目的小数点看错了，致造成这样一个大错误。当作者来信请求更正时，我们又没有认真检查原稿，虚心检讨自己，而主观的以为是作者原稿把小数点点错了，因此给石家庄日报的更正信，把这错误的责任推给了作者。直到领导上督促清查原稿时，才发现错误的责任应由本报编辑负，现除在本报更正，向作者道歉外，并请你们代为更正。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　          阳泉职工报社
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　二月二十二日
