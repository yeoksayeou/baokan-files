Title: "China Youth" Moves Publication to Beiping, Fifth Issue to be Published at the End of This Month
Author:
Date: 1949-03-26
Page: 1

　　"China Youth" Moves Publication to Beiping
　　Fifth Issue to be Published at the End of This Month
　　[This Newspaper's Report] "China Youth" has now moved its publication to Beiping. The address of the publishing house is 17 East Chang'an Avenue in this city. The fifth issue has been changed to a 16-page format and will be published at the end of this month. This issue contains the resolutions of the 14th National Congress of the All-China Students' Federation, Comrade Feng Wenbin's report at the congress, and Comrade Luo Qiong's article on organizing women's production, etc.

### “中国青年”移平出版　第五期本月底出刊

1949-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　“中国青年”移平出版
  　第五期本月底出刊
    【本报讯】“中国青年”现已移平出版，该社社址设本市东长安街十七号。第五期改为十六开本并将于本月底出刊。本期载有中华全国学生第十四届代表大会决议，冯文彬同志在大会上的报告，罗琼同志关于组织妇女生产的文章等。
