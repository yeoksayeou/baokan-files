Title: Protesting the Promotion of New War Policies: French People Condemn Truman
Author:
Date: 1949-03-19
Page: 3

　　Protesting the Promotion of New War Policies
    French People Condemn Truman
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 18th: According to a TASS report from Paris on March 15th: The peace movement of the French people against instigators of new wars is developing across the country. L'Humanité, the organ of the French Communist Party, reported, "The 'Fighters for Peace and Freedom Society' has distributed more than 200,000 letters to Truman nationwide, seeking signatures. Tens of thousands of male and female workers, political leaders, scientists, writers, and people with various political or religious beliefs have already signed. Many signatory groups and individuals have attached petitions to the letters, representing their members in protesting the policy of promoting new wars. Among those submitting such petitions are the National Railway Workers' Federation, representing 20,000 people, metalworkers in Livarot, and teachers in the Seine-et-Marne department."

### 抗议推进新战争政策  法国人民谴责杜鲁门

1949-03-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　抗议推进新战争政策
    法国人民谴责杜鲁门
    【新华社陕北十八日电】据塔斯社巴黎十五日讯：法国全国反对新战争挑拨者的和平运动在全国各地日益展开。法国共产党机关报人道报报导，“和平与自由战士社在全国散发二十余万份致杜鲁门的信征求签名，已有成万男女工人、政治领袖、科学家、作家和具有各种政治信仰或宗教信仰的人签字。许多签名的团体和个人在信内附上请愿书，代表他们的成员抗议推进新战争的政策，提出这种请愿书的有代表二万人的全国铁路工人联合会，利瓦洛冶金属工人及塞纳瓦斯省的教师等。”
