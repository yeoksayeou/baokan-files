Title: Taiyue Directly Subordinate Organs Welcome Labor Congress Representatives
Author:
Date: 1949-03-04
Page: 2

　　Taiyue Directly Subordinate Organs Welcome Labor Congress Representatives
　　[Taiyue Electric] Comrades Li Xian and Zhao Futang, the worker representatives from the Taiyue area who attended the Sixth National Labor Congress, have recently returned to the area. The Taiyue District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Administrative Office, the Military Region, cadres from various organs and organizations, service personnel, and representatives from the Xinhua Printing Factory Trade Union and other groups, more than 2,000 people in total, held a welcome meeting on the 8th and 9th of last month. Comrade Li Xian reported on the proceedings and achievements of the National Labor Congress, as well as the prosperity of industrial construction in the Northeast Liberated Areas. He also proposed that, in accordance with the spirit of the Labor Congress, past work should be reviewed in order to implement the spirit of the congress.

### 太岳直属机关欢迎劳大代表

1949-03-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　太岳直属机关欢迎劳大代表
    【太岳电】太岳区出席全国第六次劳动大会的职工代表李仙、赵福堂同志，最近已返回本区。中共太岳区党委，行政公署、军区以及各机关团体干部、勤杂人员和新华印刷厂工会等各团体代表两千多人，在上月八、九两日召开欢迎大会。李仙同志报告了全国劳动大会的经过和成就，以及东北解放区工业建设的繁荣情况，并提出应根据劳大精神，检讨过去工作，以便把大会的精神贯彻下去。
