Title: After Adjustment of Pure Silver Purchase Price in Beiping, Citizens Enthusiastically Exchange for Local Currency
Author:
Date: 1949-03-19
Page: 2
Following the adjustment of the purchase price of pure silver by the Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China on the 17th, due to the reasonable pricing, citizens are extremely enthusiastic to exchange. According to the bank's statistics, as of 6:00 PM on the 18th, a total of 1,987 taels of pure silver have been purchased in the past two days.

[Another News] Since the Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China began posting the purchase price of silver dollars on March 5th, until the 17th, a total of 761 people went to exchange silver dollars in 13 days. The total number of silver dollars exchanged was 12,378 and a half (50-cent silver coins), for a total exchange of 2,300,6480 yuan in local currency. (Yu)

### 平市收兑纯银牌价调整后　市民踊跃兑换本币

1949-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市收兑纯银牌价调整后
  　市民踊跃兑换本币
    【本报讯】中国人民银行北平分行自十七日调整收兑纯银牌价后，因订价合理，前往兑换者极为踊跃，据该行统计，截至十八日下午六时，两日来共收兑进纯银一千九百八十七市两。
    【又讯】中国人民银行北平分行，自三月五日开始挂牌收兑银元，至十七日止，在十三天内计有七百六十一人前往兑换，兑入银元总数一万二千三百七十八枚半（五角银元），共兑出本币二百三十万零六千四百八十元。（宇）
