Title: Comrade Xia Zhenqiu, Head of the First Division of the Department of Civil Affairs of the North China People's Government, Passes Away

Author:
Date: 1949-03-22
Page: 2

　　Comrade Xia Zhenqiu, Head of the First Division of the Department of Civil Affairs of the North China People's Government, Passes Away
    [This newspaper reports] Comrade Xia Zhenqiu, Head of the First Division of the Department of Civil Affairs of the North China People's Government, unfortunately passed away at Peace Hospital in Shijiazhuang on February 22nd due to illness. Comrade Xia was from Shandong, 49 years old this year, a member of the Chinese Communist Party, and came from a poor background. He successively served as commissioner and president of the Construction College in the Hebei-Shandong-Henan region. In 1946, he was elected as a senator of the Jin-Hebei-Shandong-Henan Border Region and served as the Deputy Director of the Department of Civil Affairs of the former Jin-Hebei-Shandong-Henan Border Region Government. Last year, he personally went to southern Hebei to participate in the land reform work team for more than half a year. He fell ill from overwork, leading to his untimely death. Upon receiving the sad news, all staff members of the North China People's Government expressed their condolences.

### 华北人民政府民政部第一处长  夏振秋同志病逝

1949-03-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府民政部第一处长
    夏振秋同志病逝
    【本报讯】华北人民政府民政部第一处处长夏振秋同志不幸于二月二十二日在石家庄和平医院病逝。夏同志山东人，现年四十九岁，系中国共产党党员，出身贫寒。曾在冀鲁豫历任专员及建设学院院长等职，一九四六年被选为晋冀鲁豫边区参议员，任前晋冀鲁豫边区政府民政厅副厅长。去年亲赴冀南参加土地改革工作团工作，为时半年余，积劳成疾，以至不治，噩耗传来，华北人民政府全体工作人员，均表哀悼。
