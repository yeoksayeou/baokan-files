Title: Korean Delegation Tours Leningrad
Author:
Date: 1949-03-28
Page: 3

　　Korean Delegation
  　Tours Leningrad
    [Beijing, 27th] TASS reports: The Korean government delegation, led by Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, left Moscow on March 20 for a five-day sightseeing tour of Leningrad. With warm hospitality from the local government and people, the delegation visited various historical sites, cultural centers, industrial centers, shops, theaters, and the battlefields where German fascist invaders were blocked during the war. They left Leningrad on the evening of the 25th to return home.

### 朝鲜代表团　游览列宁格勒

1949-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜代表团
  　游览列宁格勒
    【北平二十七日电】塔斯社讯：由朝鲜人民共和国总理金日成率领的朝鲜政府代表团于三月二十日离莫斯科赴列宁格勒作历时五日的观光旅行，在当地政府与人民热烈招待下，参观了各处名胜古迹、文化中心、工业中心、商店、剧、以及战时阻击德国法西斯侵略者的战场，于二十五日晚间离列宁格勒返国。
