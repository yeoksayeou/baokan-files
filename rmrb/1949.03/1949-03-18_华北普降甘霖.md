Title: Abundant Rain Falls in North China
Author:
Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 1

　　Abundant Rain Falls in North China
    [This newspaper dispatch] Just as spring plowing is about to begin, abundant rain has fallen throughout North China, greatly benefiting this year's spring plowing and wheat harvest. According to a report from the Beijing Meteorological Observatory: In the vicinity of Beijing, from 8:00 AM on the 16th to 6:30 PM (with intermittent breaks), it rained (mixed with snowflakes) for five hours and fifty-eight minutes, with a precipitation of 2.8 millimeters. The rainfall area is extremely broad, extending west to Ningxia and Shaanxi, and east to the coast, covering the Yangtze and Yellow River basins. Since the beginning of spring this year, the amount of precipitation in various parts of North China has been minimal, and there have been signs of drought in various places. This spring rain is timely.

### 华北普降甘霖

1949-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北普降甘霖
    【本报讯】值此春耕开始前，华北各地普降甘霖，对今年春耕及麦收裨益极大。据北平气象台报告：北平附近自十六日晨八时起，至下午六时三十分止（中间有间歇时间），降雨（杂有雪花）达五小时又五十八分，降水量为二点八公厘。降水区域极广，西至宁夏、陕西，东至沿海，普及长江、黄河流域。今年入春以来，华北各地降水量微少，各地已有干旱现象。这场春雨适逢其时。
