Title: Hanyang Steamer Departs Dagu for Hong Kong with Passengers
Author:
Date: 1949-03-21
Page: 1

　　[Tianjin Electric] The Hanyang steamer, which arrived in Dagu on the 15th of this month from Hong Kong, is scheduled to return to Hong Kong today, carrying the first batch of 26 passengers, including 15 foreign nationals.

### 汉阳轮载客由大沽返港

1949-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　汉阳轮载客由大沽返港
    【天津电】本月十五日由香港驶抵大沽的汉阳轮订于今日驶返香港，载走第一批旅客二十六人，其中十五人为外籍侨民。
