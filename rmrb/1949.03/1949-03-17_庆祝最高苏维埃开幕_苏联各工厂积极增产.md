Title: Celebrating the Opening of the Supreme Soviet, Soviet Factories Actively Increase Production
Author:
Date: 1949-03-17
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 15th. TASS Moscow dispatch: An atmosphere of great labor enthusiasm permeates factories and manufacturing plants throughout the Soviet land. To celebrate the opening of the Fifth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, millions of workers, engineers, and technicians have undertaken Stakhanovite tasks. The first news of their work achievements has been continuously transmitted from all corners of the Soviet Union. And the workers of Moscow enterprises have once again taken the lead. The blacksmith team at the Moscow "Red Gliding Car" factory exceeds its daily target by 155 percent. The majority of workers at the "Stankolit" factory exceed their quotas by more than 100 percent. In Leningrad, all enterprises in the city and region have participated in the movement to increase labor productivity and improve product quality. Workers in many large departments of the Lenin Factory have undertaken Stakhanovite tasks. The workers of a department led by foreman Lukovsky all exceeded their quotas on March 11th. Many Stakhanovite workers even produced double their quotas. Many reports from Kiev, Voroshilovgrad Oblast, and the Donets Basin tell of the great labor enthusiasm that is spreading in Ukrainian enterprises.

### 庆祝最高苏维埃开幕　苏联各工厂积极增产

1949-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　庆祝最高苏维埃开幕
  　苏联各工厂积极增产
    【新华社陕北十五日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：伟大的劳动热情的气氛弥漫在苏维埃国土上的各个工厂和制造厂中。为了庆祝苏联最高苏维埃第五次会议的开幕，千百万工人、工程师和技师担负起了斯塔哈诺夫的任务。关于他们工作成果的第一批消息，已由苏联各个角落陆续传来。而莫斯科企业的工人们已再度占先。在莫斯科“红色滑车”工厂的铁匠工作队每日超过目标百分之一百五十五。“斯坦科里特”工厂的大多数工人均超过定额百分之百以上。在列宁格勒，该城和该州的一切企业全部参加了争取增加劳动生产率及改进产品质量的运动，列宁工厂的许多大部门的工人们已承担了斯塔哈诺夫任务。以领工卢科夫斯基为首的一个部门的工人全部在三月十一日超过了他们的定额。许多斯塔哈诺夫工人甚至生产了双份定额。来自基辅、伏罗希洛夫格勒州与顿尼兹盆地的很多消息，都讲述在乌克兰各企业中正弥漫着伟大的劳动热情。
