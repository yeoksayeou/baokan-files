Title: Expansion of Farmers' Movement in Madras Province, India
Author:
Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, North Shaanxi, March 17th. TASS, Delhi, March 14th: Due to the expansion of the farmers' movement, the government of Madras Province, India, has announced the implementation of martial law in five counties of the province.

### 印度马德拉斯省农民运动扩展

1949-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　印度马德拉斯省农民运动扩展
    【新华社陕北十七日电】塔斯社德里十四日讯：由于农民运动扩展，印度马德拉斯省政府已宣布在该省五县实施戒严法。
