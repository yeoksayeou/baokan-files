Title: All-China Students' Federation Urges Dutch Students to Oppose Dutch Military's Atrocities in Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1949-03-29
Page: 3

　　All-China Students' Federation Urges Dutch Students
    Oppose Dutch Military's Atrocities in Indonesia
    [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 28th] TASS dispatch: The International Students' Union published an open letter to the Dutch Students' Committee on the 26th, urging it to take a firm stand against the Dutch government's atrocities in Indonesia. The letter stated: It is well known that the Netherlands uses the most cruel atrocities and terror to deal with Indonesian residents. Students all over the world are deeply angered after learning that sixty Indonesian students and members of the Socialist Youth League in Samarinda were killed.

    According to a poster, a small team of Dutch troops in Surabaya broke into a hospital, expelled patients and medical staff, and opened fire on three medical staff and seven patients, seriously injuring six patients.

### 国际学联敦促荷兰学生　反对荷军在印尼的暴行

1949-03-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际学联敦促荷兰学生
    反对荷军在印尼的暴行
    【新华社北平二十八日电】塔斯社讯：国际学联于二十六日发表致荷兰学生委员会的公开信，敦促它采取坚决的立场，反对荷兰政府在印尼的暴行。信上说：尽人皆知，荷兰使用最残忍的暴行与恐怖手段来对付印尼居民。全世界学生在知道六十个印尼学生和社会主义青年党党员萨马森被杀害的消息后，深表愤怒。
    另据海报载，在泗水的荷军一小队曾闯入医院，逐出病人和医务人员，并向三个医务人员和七个病人开枪，致使六个病人受重伤。
