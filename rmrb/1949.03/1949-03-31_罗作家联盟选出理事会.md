Title: Romanian Writers Union Elects Council
Author:
Date: 1949-03-31
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 30th: TASS, Bucharest dispatch: The Romanian Writers Conference concluded on the 29th. The conference established the Romanian People's Republic Writers Union and elected a council including outstanding poets, writers, and playwrights.

### 罗作家联盟选出理事会

1949-03-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　罗作家联盟选出理事会
    【新华社北平三十日电】塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚作家会议已于二十九日结束。会议成立了罗马尼亚人民共和国作家联盟和选出了包括卓越的诗人、作家与剧作家的理事会。
