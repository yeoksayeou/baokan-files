Title: More Than Eighty Enemies from Datong Surrender
Author:
Date: 1949-03-06
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Xingxian, 2nd: More than eighty enemy soldiers from the "Zuoyun Development Work Team," who had fled to Datong, surrendered to the People's Liberation Army on February 3rd, led by detachment leader Wang Shiying. The surrendered officers and soldiers totaled more than eighty, carrying sixty-three rifles and pistols, five small cannons, three light machine guns, and a portion of shells and bullets.

### 大同敌八十余投诚

1949-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　大同敌八十余投诚
    【新华社兴县二日电】逃亡大同之敌“左云开展工作队”在支队长王世英率领下，于二月三日向解放军投诚。投诚官兵共八十余，携有长短枪六十三支，小炮五门、轻机枪三挺及炮弹子弹一部。
