Title: Our Army in Central Shaanxi Annihilates Over a Thousand Enemies; Bandit Division Commander Ma Desheng Killed

Author:
Date: 1949-03-19
Page: 1

Xinhua Broadcasting Station, 18th Broadcast: In operations in the northwest region of Yaoxian on the 11th, the Northwest People's Liberation Army killed and wounded over 1,000 officers and soldiers, including the commander of the 190th Division of Ma Jiyuan's bandit army (killed), Ma Desheng, and captured a portion of the Kuomintang bandit army. Earlier this month, bandit Ma Desheng led four cavalry regiments eastward from the Longdong region. After invading Chunhua on the 10th, they continued to flee to Liulin Village, northwest of the city, where they were dealt a heavy blow by the People's Liberation Army on the 11th.

### 陕中我军歼敌千余　击毙匪师长马得胜

1949-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕中我军歼敌千余
  　击毙匪师长马得胜
    【新华广播电台十八日播音】西北人民解放军十一日在耀县西北地区作战中，打死打伤青海国民党匪军马继援部一百九十师师长马得胜（打死）以下官兵一千多人，活捉国民党匪军一部。本月初马匪得胜带领四个骑兵团从陇东地区向东触犯，在十日侵占淳化以后，又继续向该城西北的柳林村窜犯，在十一日遭到人民解放军的痛击。
