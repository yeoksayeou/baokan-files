Title: Tianjin Opens Telegraph Service: Sending and Receiving Domestic and International Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1949-03-09
Page: 1

　　Tianjin Opens Telegraph Service
  　Sending and Receiving Domestic and International Telegrams
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 7th] Telegraph communications between Tianjin and major cities across the country, including Shanghai, Nanjing, and Wuhan, were fully opened on the 5th. International telegrams will also be temporarily accepted by the Tianjin Telecommunications Bureau starting from the same day and forwarded via Beiping. The Tianjin Telecommunications Bureau and its business offices began operations on the 5th. As of 9:00 PM that day, 379 domestic telegrams were sent and received, including those to Shanghai, Nanjing, Zhenjiang, Hankou, Guangzhou, Ningbo, Fuzhou, Taipei, Qingdao, Taiyuan, Xi'an, and other places, as well as 28 international telegrams, including those to the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, France, Canada, and other countries.

### 天津开放电报交通　收发国内国外电报

1949-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　天津开放电报交通
  　收发国内国外电报
    【新华社天津七日电】天津市与沪、宁、汉等全国各大城市间的电报交通已于五日全部开放。国际电报亦于同日起由津市电信局暂时代收，交由北平台转发。津市电信局及各营业所已于五日开始营业。是日截止晚九时收发国内电报三百七十九件，包括上海、南京、镇江、汉口、广州、宁波、福州、台北、青岛、太原、西安等地，国际电报二十八份，包括苏、美、英、法、加等国。
