　　Mail Forwarding
    Mr. Xi Ming: Your letter of March 11th has been received. We hope you can tell us your address and telephone number. The problem with your relative's cloth shop is currently under study and we will inform you soon!
　　　　　　　　Social Services Group of This Newspaper
