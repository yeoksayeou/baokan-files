Title: Kaifeng Workers' School Second Class Graduates
Author:
Date: 1949-03-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 26th—The second class of over four hundred students from the Kaifeng Workers' School graduated on the 20th. Among them, three hundred and forty-one requested to join the People's Liberation Army work teams, and the school has agreed to two hundred and forty of these requests.

### 开封职工学校　第二期学员毕业

1949-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　开封职工学校
  　第二期学员毕业
    【新华社开封二十六日电】开封市职工学校第二期学员四百余人，已于二十日毕业，其中有三百四十一人要求参加人民解放军工作团，已有二百四十余人得到校方同意。
