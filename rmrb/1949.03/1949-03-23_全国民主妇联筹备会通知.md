Title: Notice from the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
Author:
Date: 1949-03-23
Page: 1

　　Notice from the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
    The exhibition of the First National Congress of Chinese Women of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Preparatory Committee has been prepared and is scheduled to open on the 24th of this month. In order to facilitate visits, all troops, factories, institutions, schools, groups, etc. that wish to visit the exhibition, please send a letter to the Secretariat of this Committee in advance stating the number of people, so that we can arrange the date and order of visits and then announce them. Individual visits are completely free. This is a special notice.　March 21st

### 全国民主妇联筹备会通知

1949-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国民主妇联筹备会通知
    全国民主妇女联合会筹备委员会中国妇女第一次全国代表大会展览会，业经筹备就绪，拟于本月二十四日开始展览，兹为便利参观起见，凡欲参观展览会之各部队工厂机关学校团体等，请即将人数，预先函告本会秘书处，以便排定参观日期次序，再行公布，至于个人参观，完全自由，特此通知。　三月二十一日
