Title: Tianjin Does Not Restrict the Entry and Exit of Goods, Facilitating Material Exchange with Liberated Areas
Author:
Date: 1949-03-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 6th—The Tianjin Municipal Bureau of Industry and Commerce, in order to solve the problem of merchants going to neighboring friendly areas and cities during the military control period to purchase and sell goods, and to facilitate material exchange between urban and rural areas and between cities, has decided that the entry and exit of all commercial goods (except military火arms, radio stations and their parts, gold and silver, and drugs) into Tianjin will not be restricted. It is also stipulated that all institutions, troops, production departments, state-owned enterprises, and cooperatives above the county level in friendly neighboring areas must register with the Tianjin Trust Company for entrusted purchases when purchasing goods in Tianjin, and must also obtain the company's approval when transporting goods out of the city.

### 天津不限制商货出入　便利解放区物资交流

1949-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　天津不限制商货出入
  　便利解放区物资交流
    【新华社天津六日电】天津市工商局为解决商民到友邻区及军管时期之城市购销货物，便利城乡间及各城市间物资交流，决定凡商民运销商品货物（军火、电台及其零件、金银毒品除外）出入津市一律不加限制。并规定各友邻区之机关部队、生产部门、公营企业及县以上之合作社，凡在津市购买商品货物，必须向天津市信托公司登记委托代购，运销商品出境时，亦必须经过该公司批准。
