Title: Beiping Military Control Commission Takes Over the Sino-French Education Fund Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-03-20
Page: 1

　　Beiping Military Control Commission Takes Over the Sino-French Education Fund Committee

　　[This newspaper dispatch] The Sino-French Education Fund Committee, which managed Boxer Indemnity funds, was officially taken over yesterday (the 19th) by comrades Hao Renchu and Li Junfu, representatives dispatched by the Military Control Commission. It was immediately announced that the committee would suspend its activities from that day forward, and the persons in charge of the committee were instructed to prepare an inventory of materials to await auditing.

### 北平军管会接管中法教育基金委员会

1949-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平军管会接管中法教育基金委员会
    【本报讯】支配庚子赔款的中法教育基金委员会已于昨日（十九日）由军管会派遣代表郝人初、李俊甫同志正式接管。当即宣布该会自即日起暂停会务活动，并指示该会负责人准备物资清册，听候清点。
