Title: Indian Communist Party's Organ Forced to Suspend Publication
Author:
Date: 1949-03-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 11th: According to a report from Diantong News Agency, Bombay, dated the 4th, the Nehru government has ordered the suspension of seven weekly publications issued by the Communist Party of India in Bombay Province, including the CPI's organ, "People's Age." The All-India Student Federation's organ, "Student," has also been ordered to cease publication at the same time.

### 印共机关报被迫停刊

1949-03-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　印共机关报被迫停刊
    【新华社陕北十一日电】据电通社孟买四日讯：印度共产党在孟买省发行的七种周刊，包括印共机关报“人民世纪”在内，已被尼赫鲁政府勒令停刊。全印学联机关报“学生”，亦被同时勒令停刊。
