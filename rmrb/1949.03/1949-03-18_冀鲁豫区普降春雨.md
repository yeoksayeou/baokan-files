Title: Spring Rain Generally Falls in the Hebei-Shandong-Henan Area
Author:
Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 2
(Hebei-Shandong-Henan Telegram) On the 13th and 14th, the Hebei-Shandong-Henan area generally experienced spring rain, lasting for ten hours. In sixteen counties including Juancheng, Yuncheng, Heze, and Dingtao in Henan, and Fanxian, Puxian, and Guancheng in Hebei, rainfall reached three to four finger widths. This spring rain is extremely beneficial to spring plowing and wheat seedlings throughout the area.

### 冀鲁豫区普降春雨

1949-03-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀鲁豫区普降春雨
    【冀鲁豫电】十三、四两日，冀鲁豫区普降春雨，为时达十小时。河南鄄城、郓城、菏泽、定陶、河北范县、濮县、观城等十六县，均落三、四指不等。此次春雨，对全区春耕与麦苗均极有利。
