North China Trading Company
    Beiping City Company Announcement
    Trade Investigation No. 4
    March 25th

    The company previously entrusted some grain shops to sell grain on commission. The quality of these shops varied, so we have re-examined them. We have now selected over thirty of the better grain shops to continue selling on commission, starting today.
