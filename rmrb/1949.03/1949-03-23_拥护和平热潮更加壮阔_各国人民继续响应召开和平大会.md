Title: The Tide of Support for Peace Swells Even More Mightily, People of All Countries Continue to Respond to the Call for a Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-03-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 21st – The world's people's tide of support for peace is swelling mightily day by day, and workers, intellectuals, and women of all countries are enthusiastically responding to the call for convening a World Peace Congress.

　　In Romania, the Central Committee of the National League of Former Anti-Fascist Political Prisoners has sent a telegram to the International Liaison Committee of the World Congress of Intellectuals for Peace, declaring its support for convening the World Peace Congress. The telegram stated that the imperialist bloc still imprisons tens of thousands of anti-fascist fighters to this day. They threaten to once again plunge humanity into the horrors of war and fascist disaster. The World Peace Congress will powerfully strike at this conspiracy of theirs. At the same time, the Romanian Union of Actors, Writers, and Journalists also supports convening the World Peace Congress.
　　
    The Central Committee of Cooperatives of Czechoslovakia, representing more than 4.5 million cooperative members, issued a statement supporting the proposal to convene the World Peace Congress.
　　
    The Central Committee of Cooperatives of Poland also announced its support for the World Peace Congress on behalf of five million members. Supporting the convening of the congress were also the Presidium of the Executive Committee of the Polish Miners' Union, the Society of the Silesian Institute of Technology, the Varockfin University and Technical College, and the Krakow Mining College.
　　
    The Bulgarian Women's League, representing 500,000 women, also declared its support for convening the World Peace Congress.
　　
    In Germany, initiated by the German Cultural Association (in the Soviet zone), a German Liaison Committee has been organized to prepare for participation in the World Peace Congress. The Free German Trade Union Federation has also joined this liaison committee and will send its own representatives to the congress. The trade unions in the Soviet zone will widely explain the significance of the World Peace Congress at workers' rallies. The Federation declared: German workers will commemorate this year's May Day under the slogan of defending peace.
　　
    The Organizing Committee of the Italian Intellectuals' Peace Movement issued an appeal to the Italian people, welcoming the initiative to convene the World Peace Congress. It stated: "The Italian people must resolutely exert themselves for this international anti-war congress." The book called: "All loyal Italians, all organized and unorganized men and women, all who care about the fate of their children, the fate of culture and civilization, support the World Peace Congress and send representatives to Paris."

### 拥护和平热潮更加壮阔  各国人民继续响应召开和平大会

1949-03-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　拥护和平热潮更加壮阔
    各国人民继续响应召开和平大会
    【新华社陕北二十一日电】世界人民拥护和平的热潮，正一天天地更加壮阔，各国的工人、知识分子和妇女，都热烈地响应召开世界拥护和平大会的号召。
    在罗马尼亚，前反法西斯政治犯全国联盟中央委员会已致电世界文化工作者保卫和平大会国际联络委员会，声明赞成召开世界拥护和平大会。电文说，帝国主义集团直到现在还囚禁着成千成万的反法西斯战士。他们威胁着使人类再一次惨遭战争恐怖与法西斯灾难。世界拥护和平大会将有力地打击他们这一阴谋。同时，罗马尼亚的演员、作家与记者联合会也支持召开世界拥护和平大会。
    捷克斯洛伐克的合作社中央委员会代表四百五十余万名合作社社员发表声明，支持召开世界拥护和平大会的建议。
    波兰合作社中央委员会也代表五百万社员，宣告支持世界拥护和平大会。声明支持召开该大会的还有波兰矿工工会执委会主席团、西里西亚技术研究院的学会、瓦罗克芬大学和技术学院、克拉科采矿学院等。
    保加利亚妇女联盟代表五十万妇女也声明拥护召开世界和平大会。
    在德国，由德国文化界联合会（在苏占区）发起，已组织了一个德国联络委员会，进行参加世界拥护和平大会的准备工作。自由德国职工会联合会也参加了这一联络委员会，并将派它自己的代表出席大会。苏占区的职工会将在工人集会上广泛解释世界拥护和平大会的意义。该联合会宣称：德国工人将在保卫和平的口号下，纪念今年的五一节。
    意大利知识分子和平运动组织委员会发表告意大利人民书，欢迎召开世界拥护和平大会的创议。内称：“意大利人民必须果断地为这一国际反战大会尽力。”该书号召：“一切忠实的意大利人，一切有组织与无组织的男女们，一切关怀他们子孙的命运、关怀文化与文明之命运的人们支持世界拥护和平大会，并选派代表到巴黎去。”
