Title: Strengthening the Solidarity of Students Worldwide: International Union of Students to Commemorate World Student Week
Author:
Date: 1949-03-15
Page: 3

　　Strengthening the Solidarity of Students Worldwide
  　International Union of Students to Commemorate World Student Week
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 14th: Prague dispatch: The International Union of Students (IUS) has issued a statement stating that the annual World Student Week will hold its seventh commemoration from March 21st to 28th. This will be another step in defending the IUS and the World Federation of Democratic Youth (WFDY) movement. The statement, referring to the World Youth Congress to be held in Budapest this year, declares that this is "the best opportunity to practically realize a closer unity between students and other young people."
    Another dispatch: The Secretariat of the IUS, located in Prague, received a report from the annual meeting of American medical students recently held in Philadelphia. The annual meeting condemned the racial discrimination practiced by American universities and supported the IUS's actions to defend peace, national independence of all peoples, and democratization of education. The conference passed resolutions to step up aid to democratic students in China, Puerto Rico, and other countries.

### 加强全世界学生的团结　国际学联将纪念世界学生周

1949-03-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　加强全世界学生的团结
  　国际学联将纪念世界学生周
    【新华社陕北十四日电】布拉格讯：国际学联发表声明称：一年一度的世界学生周将于三月二十一至二十八日举行其第七次纪念。这将是保卫国际学联与世界青联运动中的另一步骤。该声明在提到将于今年在布达佩斯举行的世界青年代表大会时宣称：这是“在实际上实现学生与其他青年之间更紧密团结的最好机会。”
    另讯：设在布拉格的国际学联秘书处接到美国医科大学学生最近在费城召开年会的报告。该年会谴责美国大学所实行的种族歧视，并赞助国际学联保卫和平、保卫各族人民的民族独立和教育民主化的行动。大会通过加紧援助中国，玻多黎各和其他各国民主学生的决议。
