Title: Women's Circles in Beiping and Other Places Prepare to Commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day; Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Democratic Women's Federation Established
Author:
Date: 1949-03-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, March 4th: The preparatory committee of the Beiping Municipal Democratic Women's Federation was formally established a few days ago. More than 40 representatives from 19 organizations including female workers, female students, and female teachers attended the preparatory meeting. The meeting elected the standing committee of the preparatory committee, including eleven units such as the No. 1 and No. 3 sewing departments of the Pingjin Clothing Factory, Tsinghua and Peking University student associations, the Pingjin Railway Bureau Women's Federation, the women's department of the Preparatory Committee of the Telecommunications Bureau Trade Union, the Middle School Affiliated to Beijing Normal University, the Preparatory Committee of the Joint Association of Female Teachers in Primary and Secondary Schools, and the Beiping Women's Friendship Association. The conference also enthusiastically discussed the future work of women in Ping City and preparations for commemorating "March 8th" International Women's Day.

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, March 4th: The Preparatory Committee of the Central Plains Women's Federation held a standing committee meeting on February 24th to discuss the "Notice" issued by the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation on commemorating "March 8th" International Women's Day. According to the instructions in the notice regarding focusing on cities and newly liberated areas for commemorating "March 8th" International Women's Day, and the specific conditions in the Central Plains, the preparatory committee drafted supplementary instructions, stipulating that: In cities, in addition to holding large commemorative meetings, symposiums for women from all walks of life can be held separately, such as symposiums for poor working women, female students, female teachers, and female working cadres. In rural areas with a working foundation, key "March 8th" commemorative meetings can be held to convey the Central Plains women's movement's principles, tasks, and various issues. In general areas, women's representative meetings should be held, and representatives should conduct propaganda activities among the women masses. At the end of the meeting, He Ming and Du Yumei were elected as representatives to the Central Plains Provisional People's Representative Conference.

### 北平等地妇女界  筹备纪念“三八”节　北平民主妇联筹委会成立

1949-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平等地妇女界
    筹备纪念“三八”节
  　北平民主妇联筹委会成立
    【新华社北平四日电】北平市民主妇女联合会筹备委员会已于日前正式成立。出席筹备会议的有女工、女学生、女教职员等十九个团体的代表四十余人。会上选出了筹备委员会的常务委员会，包括平津被服厂第一和第三缝纫部、清华、北大同学会、平津铁路局妇女会、电讯局职工会筹委会妇女部、师大女附中、中小学女教职员联合会筹委会、北平妇女联谊会等十一个单位。大会并热烈讨论了今后平市妇女工作及筹备纪念“三八”节等问题。
    【新华社中原四日电】中原妇女联合会筹备委员会于二月二十四日举行常委会议，讨论全国民主妇女联合会筹备委员会所发纪念“三八”节的通告，根据该通告中关于以城市和新解放区为纪念“三八”节的重点之指示以及中原的具体情况，筹委会拟出补充指示，规定：在城市中除举行大纪念会外，可分别召开各界妇女座谈会，如贫民劳动妇女座谈会，女学生、女教员和妇女工作干部等座谈会。农村中有工作基础的地区，可有重点的举行“三八”节纪念大会，传达中原妇女运动方针，任务及各种问题。一般地区则召开妇女代表会，通过代表到妇女群众中进行宣传活动。会议最后推举出何铭、杜雨梅为出席中原临时人民代表会议的代表。
