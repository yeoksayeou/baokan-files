Title: Chiang Bandit Suppresses Local Peace Movement; Puppet Chairman of Zhejiang Province Chen Yi Arrested
Author:
Date: 1949-03-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 8th. The bandit chief Chiang Kai-shek, continuing to exercise power in Fenghua, has arrested Chen Yi, the puppet chairman of Zhejiang Province, who was deemed to be disobeying his orders, in order to suppress the local peace movement. It is reported that Chen is currently being held by Chiang in Quzhou, western Zhejiang. Chen was dismissed from his post by the puppet Executive Yuan in Guangzhou on the 16th of last month. Chen Cheng's direct descendant and die-hard element, Zhou, the former commander of the Sixth Pacification Zone of the Kuomintang, succeeded Chen as the puppet chairman of Zhejiang Province. Zhang Dulun, the puppet chairman of Hubei Province, was also dismissed at the same time as Chen. Zhu Dingqing, the former commander of the Ninth Supplementary Compilation Zone of the puppet Ministry of National Defense, succeeded Zhang. Both Chen Yi and Zhang Dulun belonged to the Kuomintang Political Science Clique.

### 蒋贼镇压局部和平运动　浙省伪主席陈仪被捕

1949-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒋贼镇压局部和平运动
  　浙省伪主席陈仪被捕
    【新华社陕北八日电】在奉化继续行使权力的匪首蒋介石，为了镇压局部和平运动，已将被认为不听其指挥的浙江省伪主席陈仪逮捕。据息，陈现被蒋囚禁于浙西衢州。陈在上月十六日被广州伪行政院撤职，继陈为浙江省伪主席的是陈诚嫡系死硬派原国民党第六绥靖区司令周。与陈同时被免职的尚有湖北省伪主席张笃伦，继张职位的是原伪国防部第九补编区司令朱鼎卿。陈仪与张笃伦都属于国民党政学系。
