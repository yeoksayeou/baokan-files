Title: Song of Liberation
Author: Shi De
Date: 1949-03-24
Page: 4

　　Song of Liberation
    Shi De, a worker from Tangshan
    When I think of the past, my heart aches,
    I can't help but shed tears!
    The reactionaries, what bastards,
    They treated people like dogs.
    If it weren't for the Communist Party,
    Who would help us turn ourselves around!
    Now I work and have a factory,
    Now you farm and have land.
    I work, you farm,
    Let's all engage in production.
    I raise my hammer and put more strength into it,
    You wield your hoe with joy.
    Not afraid of fatigue, not afraid of difficulties,
    Let's all work together with one heart and mind.
    Support the front lines, eliminate the reactionaries,
    Only then can true peace be realized.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　March 15th

### 翻身曲
史德
1949-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　翻身曲
    唐山工人  史德
    想起从前真心酸，
    不由人不泪涟涟！
    反动派，真混蛋，
    拿人当做狗来看。
    要是没有共产党，
    谁来帮我们把身翻！
    我现在作工有了工厂，
    你现在种地有了田。
    我作工、你种田，
    咱们大家搞生产。
    我举铁锤多加力，
    你使锄头耍的欢。
    不怕累，不畏难，
    大家齐心努力干。
    支援前线，消灭反动派，
    真正和平才能实现。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　三·十五·
