Title: North China and Jiangnan Begin Postal Service; Remittance Services Are Being Planned

Author:

Date: 1949-03-14
Page: 1

　　North China and Jiangnan Begin Postal Service
　　Remittance Services Are Being Planned
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 10th] Starting from the 9th of this month, postal services have begun between the North China Liberated Area and areas south of the Yangtze River. Ordinary letters, registered letters, newspapers, printed matter, business circulars, samples, trade contracts, and ordinary parcels can all be mailed. Postage and stamps will all be based on the current system in the North China region. Remittance services between the North China Liberated Area and areas south of the Yangtze River are also being planned. Starting from the 9th, the Beiping Post Office and its affiliated branches have officially begun accepting the aforementioned mail. Furthermore, 4,069 bags of mail sent from Shanghai to North China have all arrived in Tianjin, of which 1,125 bags destined for Beiping arrived in Beiping on March 8th. Mail originally accumulated in Beiping and Tianjin destined for various parts of South China has also been sent to Shanghai in recent days.

### 华北江南开始通邮　汇兑业务正在筹办

1949-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北江南开始通邮
  　汇兑业务正在筹办
    【新华社北平十日电】自本月九日起，华北解放区与长江以南各地已开始通邮。凡平信、挂号信、新闻纸、印刷品、商务传单、货样、贸易契约、普通包裹等均可邮寄。邮资、邮票等一律以华北区现行制度为准。至有关华北解放区与长江以南地区的汇兑业务亦正筹办中。自九日起北平邮局及所属各支局已正式收寄以上邮件。又由上海发来华北的四千零六十九袋邮件已全部抵达天津，其中发至北平的一千一百二十五袋邮件，已于三月八日到平。原积存在平津的发往华南各地的邮件亦已于日前发往上海。
