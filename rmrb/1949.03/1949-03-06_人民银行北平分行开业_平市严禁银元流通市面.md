Title: People's Bank of China, Beiping Branch Opens; Beiping Municipality Strictly Prohibits Silver Dollars from Circulating in the Market

Author:

Date: 1949-03-06
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, February 2nd: The Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China officially opened for business on the 1st, managing treasury, deposit, loan, remittance, and other services. Remittances between Beiping-Tianjin and Beiping-Baoding have already begun, and the work of establishing remittances with Zhangjiakou, Shijiazhuang, Cangxian, and major cities in central Hebei is also actively underway.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, February 2nd: In order to protect the interests of the people, stabilize finance, and stabilize prices, the Beiping Municipal Military Control Commission issued a proclamation on the 28th of last month, strictly prohibiting silver dollars from circulating, being bought or sold, or being priced in silver dollars in the market. It is stipulated that all public and private organizations and individuals holding silver dollars may exchange them at the People's Bank and its designated exchange offices at the listed price.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, February 2nd: In order to help develop industry, mining, transportation, and trade, absorb floating capital, and develop cooperative undertakings, the Tianjin Branch of the People's Bank of China has established four new departments: Industry, Foreign Exchange, Savings, and Cooperation, which began operations on the 1st of this month.

### 人民银行北平分行开业　平市严禁银元流通市面

1949-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民银行北平分行开业
  　平市严禁银元流通市面
    【新华社北平二日电】中国人民银行北平分行，于一日起正式营业，经理金库、存款、放款、汇兑等业务。现平津、平保（定）间已开始通汇，其与张家口、石家庄、沧县及河北中部各大城镇的通汇工作亦正积极筹划中。
    【新华社北平二日电】北平市军事管制委员会为保障人民利益，安定金融，稳定物价，特于上月二十八日发出布告，严禁银元在市面流通、买卖或以银元计价。并规定凡公私团体与个人所持银元，得持向人民银行及其所指定之兑换所，按牌价兑换。
    【新华社天津二日电】中国人民银行天津分行为帮助发展工矿交通贸易，吸收游资，并开展合作事业起见，特增辟实业、外汇、储蓄、合作四部，于本月一日起开始办公。
