Title: Indonesian Guerrillas Wound U.S. and British Observers
Author:
Date: 1949-03-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 21st: According to a Agence France-Presse dispatch from Batavia: Two military observers of the United Nations Indonesian Commission under U.S. control, U.S. Major Simmons and British Lieutenant Colonel Carpling, were wounded by Indonesian guerrillas near Medan, northern Sumatra, on the 18th. A Dutch officer accompanying them was also seriously injured. The white jeep they were traveling in was destroyed by the guerrillas.

### 印尼游击队  击伤美英观察员

1949-03-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　印尼游击队
    击伤美英观察员
    【新华社陕北二十一日电】据法新社巴达维亚讯：美国控制下的联合国印尼委员会的军事观察员美人希蒙斯少校和英人卡普林中校等两人，十八日在苏门答的北部棉兰附近被印尼游击队击伤。随行的一名荷兰军官亦负重伤。他们所乘白色吉普车已被游击队击毁。
