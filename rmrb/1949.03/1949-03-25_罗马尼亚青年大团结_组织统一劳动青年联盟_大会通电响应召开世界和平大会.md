Title: Romanian Youth Unite, Organize United Labor Youth League, Congress Telegraphs Response to Call for World Peace Congress
Author: 
Date: 1949-03-25
Page: 3

　　Romanian Youth Unite
    Organize United Labor Youth League
    Congress Telegraphs Response to Call for World Peace Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 24th] The youth of all circles in Romania held a solidarity congress in Bucharest from the 19th to the 21st, resolved to establish the Romanian Labor Youth League, a unified Marxist-Leninist labor youth organization in Romania, and telegraphed a response to the call for the World Congress in Defense of Peace. After the meeting, the youth held a grand peace demonstration.

### 罗马尼亚青年大团结  组织统一劳动青年联盟  大会通电响应召开世界和平大会

1949-03-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚青年大团结
    组织统一劳动青年联盟
    大会通电响应召开世界和平大会
    【新华社陕北二十四日电】罗马尼亚各界青年于十九至二十一日在布加勒斯特举行团结大会，决议成立罗国统一的马克思列宁主义的劳动青年组织罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟，并通电响应召开世界拥护和平大会。会议结束后，青年们曾举行了盛大的和平示威。
