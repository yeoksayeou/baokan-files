Title: What is "Cup-of-Water-ism"?
Author:
Date: 1949-03-25
Page: 4

Name Explanation

　　What is "Cup-of-Water-ism"?

Editor Comrade:

　　In the article "Some Opinions on Amending the Marriage Law" on the fourth page of your newspaper on February 23rd, there is a term "Cup-of-Water-ism". I don't know what it means. Please explain.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Wang Yi, March 7th

Comrade Wang Yi:

　　"Cup-of-Water-ism" refers to abnormal male-female sexual relations. During the implementation of the New Economic Policy (1921-1925) in the Soviet Union, some young students engaged in licentious behavior of casual divorce and sexual promiscuity. They denied love, believing it to be a manifestation of petty-bourgeois idealism. They advocated that male-female relationships were like drinking a cup of water when thirsty, just to quench the thirst for a moment. The implementation of this idea had adverse effects on the work, life, health, and spirit of young men and women. Lenin strongly opposed this behavior. He pointed out: "Drinking a cup of water is a matter for one person, but sexual relations are a matter for two people, and also a matter for society, which has an impact on revolutionary work..." The meaning of "Cup-of-Water-ism" in the "Some Opinions on Amending the Marriage Law" you mentioned is to urge us to correctly understand the meaning of freedom of marriage and to be vigilant that after the victory of the revolution and the removal of the shackles of feudal marriage, young men and women still repeat the abnormal tendencies on marriage issues that have long been criticized in the past.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Social Service Group

### 什么叫“杯水主义”？

1949-03-25
第4版()
专栏：名词解答

　　什么叫“杯水主义”？编者同志：
    贵报二月二十三日第四版“关于修改婚姻法的一点意见”一文中有“杯水主义”一语，不知是什么意思？请解释。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　王一　三月七日王一同志：
    “杯水主义”是指男女性生活不正常的意思。在苏联新经济政策（一九二一年至一九二五年）开始实行的时候，某些青年学生中间曾发生了一种放荡无悸的随便离婚与性乱的行为。他们否认爱情，以为这是小资产阶级的唯心主义的表现；他们主张男女性关系只是像口渴了喝一杯水一样，只要能解一时的渴就行。这种主张的实行，对于青年男女的工作上、生活上、健康上和精神上，都发生了恶劣的影响。对于这种行为，列宁曾极力反对。他指出：“喝一杯水只是一个人的事，然而性的关系却是两个人的事，同时也是社会的事，是对革命工作有影响的………。”你所提的“关于修改婚姻法的一点意见”一文中“杯水主义”的意思，就是要我们正确地理解婚姻自由的意义，警惕在革命胜利与解除封建社会婚姻束缚后，男女青年仍去重复过去早已被批判了的对婚姻问题上不正常的倾向。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　社会服务组
