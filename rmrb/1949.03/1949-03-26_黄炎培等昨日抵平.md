Title: Huang Yanpei and Others Arrived in Beiping Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-03-26
Page: 1
　
　　Huang Yanpei and Others Arrived in Beiping Yesterday
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] Democratic figures Huang Yanpei, Sheng Pihua, and Yu Huanzeng arrived in Beiping from Tianjin yesterday (the 25th) at 11:00 AM. Dong Biwu, Chairman of the North China People's Government, and others went to the station to welcome them. In the afternoon, democratic figures such as Chang Renxia also arrived in Beiping.

### 黄炎培等昨日抵平

1949-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　黄炎培等昨日抵平
    【本报讯】民主人士黄炎培、盛丕华、俞寰澄等于昨日（二十五）上午十一时由津抵平，华北人民政府董必武主席等前往车站欢迎。是日下午续有常任侠等民主人士抵平。
