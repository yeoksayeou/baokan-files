Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Telegram to Congratulate Opening of National Student Congress; Hopes to Call on Students Nationwide to Participate in the People's Liberation Struggle
Author: 
Date: 1949-03-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 28th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to congratulate the opening of the National Student Congress. The original telegram is as follows: To all representatives of the All-China Student Congress:

    We celebrate the opening of your congress. Chinese students have made glorious contributions in the modern revolutionary history of China. Now, the first goal of the long-term struggle of Chinese students and the Chinese people, namely, to overthrow the counter-revolutionary rule of imperialism, feudalism, and bureaucrat-capitalism, is approaching complete realization. Chinese students have the opportunity to freely combine with the working people, and freely serve the great revolutionary and construction undertakings of the People's Republic. We hope that your congress will call on students across the country to make persistent efforts, actively participate in and assist the Chinese people's liberation struggle, so that this struggle will quickly achieve final victory. At the same time, we hope students will study hard, constantly improve their awareness, continuously strengthen their ties with the working people, and continuously master scientific knowledge, so as to complete new historical tasks in the great cause of building a new, people's democratic China.

                                                 The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                                         February 28, 1949

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 28th. The Preparatory Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League sent a telegram to congratulate the opening of the National Student Congress. The original text is as follows:

                                       Preparatory Committee of the New Democratic Youth League
               Telegram of Congratulations to the Student Congress; To the beloved comrades of the National Student Congress:

    We are delighted to hear that the National Student Congress is being held in Peiping, the birthplace of the Chinese youth movement. We send a telegram of congratulations and hope to join hands and work together for the complete victory of the New Democratic Revolution.

                                               Preparatory Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
                                                          February 28, 1949

### 中共中央委员会　电贺全国学代会开幕  望号召全国学生参加人民解放斗争

1949-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央委员会
  　电贺全国学代会开幕
    望号召全国学生参加人民解放斗争
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】中国共产党中央委员会电贺全国学生代表大会开幕。原电如下：中华全国学生代表大会的全体代表们：
    庆祝你们的大会的开幕。中国学生在中国近代革命历史上有过光辉的贡献。现在中国学生和中国人民长期奋斗的第一步目标，即推翻帝国主义、封建主义、官僚资本主义的反革命统治，正在接近于完全实现，中国学生已经有可能自由地与劳动人民相结合，自由地为人民共和国的伟大的革命事业和建设事业服务了。希望你们的大会号召全国学生再接再厉，积极地参加和援助中国人民解放斗争，使这个斗争迅速地获得最后的胜利，同时努力学习，不断地提高自己的觉悟，不断地加强与劳动人民的联系，不断地掌握科学知识，以便在建设人民民主的新中国的伟业中完成新的历史任务。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　    中国共产党中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年二月二十八日
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】中国新民主主义青年团筹备委员会电贺全国学生代表大会开幕。原文如下：
    　　　　新民主主义青年团筹委会
    　　致电祝贺学代大会全国学生代表大会亲爱的同志们：
    欣闻全国学生代表大会在中国青年运动发祥地北平举行，特电致贺，并望携手并进，同为新民主主义革命的彻底胜利而奋斗。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国新民主主义青年团筹备委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年二月二十八日
