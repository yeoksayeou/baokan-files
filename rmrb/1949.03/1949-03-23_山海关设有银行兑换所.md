Title: Shanhaiguan Establishes Bank Exchange

Author: Gao Hengjun
Date: 1949-03-23
Page: 4

　　Shanhaiguan Establishes Bank Exchange:
    You must apply for and obtain a travel permit from the Public Security Bureau to visit relatives in the Northeast. When you arrive in Shanhaiguan, the bank will have an exchange office. The exchange rate between the Renminbi and the Northeast Currency remains at one Renminbi yuan to one thousand Northeast currency. As for carrying items, as long as they are not prohibited, you can carry them freely.

### 山海关设有银行兑换所

1949-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　山海关设有银行兑换所高横君：
    你赴东北探亲，必须赴公安局申请领取通行证，到山海关时，银行设有兑换所，人民券与东北币之比值仍为人民券一元兑东北币一千元。至于携带物品，只要不在违禁之列，当可自由携带。
