Title: The reactionary Siamese government arrests our overseas Chinese compatriots again
Author:
Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 3

　　The reactionary Siamese government arrests our overseas Chinese compatriots again
    【Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th】Bangkok dispatch: The Siamese authorities continue their anti-Chinese atrocities. The police of the Luang Phibunsongkhram fascist government extensively searched Chinese-owned hotels and various overseas Chinese schools in Bangkok on the 12th and 13th, arresting dozens of overseas Chinese, including hotel owners, faculty, and other overseas Chinese compatriots. Earlier this month, the Siamese government had already unreasonably arrested more than twenty overseas Chinese.

### 暹罗反动政府又逮捕我侨胞

1949-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　暹罗反动政府又逮捕我侨胞
    【新华社陕北十七日电】曼谷讯：暹罗当局继续排华暴行。銮披汶法西斯政府的警察，于十二、十三两日大肆搜索曼谷的华侨旅馆与各华侨学校，逮捕华侨数十名，其中有旅馆主人、教职员及其他侨胞。按本月初旬，暹政府已无理逮捕华侨二十余名。
