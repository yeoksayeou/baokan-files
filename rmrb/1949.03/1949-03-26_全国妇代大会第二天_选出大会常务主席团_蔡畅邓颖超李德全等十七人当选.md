Title: The Second Day of the National Women's Congress: The Presidium Standing Committee Elected; Cai Chang, Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan and 14 Others Elected
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-03-26
Page: 1

　　The Second Day of the National Women's Congress
    The Presidium Standing Committee Elected
    Cai Chang, Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan and 14 Others Elected
    [Report from this newspaper] The first National Congress of Chinese Women held its second day of sessions (March 25th). The Presidium held a full meeting, chaired by Secretary-General Zhang Qinqiu. Cai Chang, Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, Zhang Qinqiu, Luo Shuzhang, Yang Yunyu, Xu Guangping, Ou Mengjue, Li Zhen, Zhang Yun, Qi Yuande, Zhang Jinbao, Liu Yaxiong, Li Wenyi, Li Xiuzhen, Li Jianzhen, and Liu Ying were elected to form the Standing Committee of the Presidium. Zhao Shinan and Feng Qiuming were elected as alternates. The entire assembly unanimously elected Comrade Cai Chang as the General Chair of the Presidium. The meeting also decided that the Congress would send representatives to present flags and pay respects to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, conduct publicity to respond to and support the World Peace Congress, send off delegates going abroad, and organize mutual learning between delegates from the Kuomintang-controlled areas and delegates from the liberated areas, and increase labor listening, among other matters. (Bai Sheng)

### 全国妇代大会第二天  选出大会常务主席团  蔡畅邓颖超李德全等十七人当选
柏生
1949-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国妇代大会第二天
    选出大会常务主席团         
    蔡畅邓颖超李德全等十七人当选
    【本报讯】中国妇女第一次全国代表大会第二天（二十五日），举行全体主席团会议，由大会秘书长张琴秋主席。当场选出蔡畅、邓颖超、李德全、张琴秋、罗叔章、杨蕴玉、许广平、区梦觉、李桢、章蕴、戚元德、张金保、刘亚雄、李文宜、李秀镇、李坚贞、刘英十七人组成常务主席团。赵世楠、冯秋明为候补。全场并一致推出蔡畅同志为主席团总主席。会中并决定由大会选派代表向毛主席、朱总司令献旗致敬，进行响应拥护世界和平大会的宣传，欢送出国代表，以及组织国统区代表与解放区代表的互相学习，增加劳听等事项。（柏生）
