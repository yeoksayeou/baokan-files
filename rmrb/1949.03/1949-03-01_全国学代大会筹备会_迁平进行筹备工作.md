Title: National Student Congress Preparatory Committee Moves to Beiping to Carry Out Preparatory Work
Author:
Date: 1949-03-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 27th—The Preparatory Committee for the National Student Congress has moved its office to Beiping a few days ago and is now actively carrying out various preparatory tasks. After several drafts and discussions by the preparatory committee members, all draft proposals concerning the future direction and tasks of the student movement, the organizational charter of the All-China Student Federation, the regulations for the eligibility of representatives to the National Student Congress, the rules of procedure for the congress, the rules of the meeting place, and the agenda, which are to be submitted to the congress for discussion, are now ready. As of the 23rd, in addition to the student representatives from Southern Hebei and Shanghai who have already arrived in Beiping to register, student representatives from the Central Plains Liberated Area and Suzhou and Hangzhou in the Kuomintang-controlled areas have also registered with the Congress Preparatory Committee. The delegation of representatives from Beiping has also been carefully selected at the Beiping Student Congress. These include student representatives from fifteen units, including Peking University, Tsinghua University, Yenching University, Teachers College, and Peiman Girls' High School, and the Mongolian-Tibetan School.

### 全国学代大会筹备会　迁平进行筹备工作

1949-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国学代大会筹备会
  　迁平进行筹备工作
    【新华社北平二十七日电】中华全国学生代表大会筹备会已于日前迁平办公，现正积极进行各项筹备工作。连日经各筹备委员分别起草和多次讨论后，所有准备提交大会讨论的今后学生运动方针、任务的议案与全国学生联合会组织章程，全国学生代表大会代表资格审查条例，大会议事规则，会场规则和议事日程等草案，均已准备就绪。截至二十三日止，除冀南和上海的学生代表已抵平报到外，续有中原解放区和国民党统治区苏州、杭州等地的学生代表向大会筹备会报到。北平市出席大会的代表团，也已在北平市学生代表大会中慎重选出。其中包括北京、清华、燕京、师范学院等大学和贝满女中、蒙藏学校等十五个单位的学生代表。
