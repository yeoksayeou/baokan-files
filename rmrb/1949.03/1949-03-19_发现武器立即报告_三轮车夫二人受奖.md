Title: Discovering Weapons Promptly Reported: Two Trishaw Drivers Rewarded
Author: Yan Zhanjiang
Date: 1949-03-19
Page: 2

　　Discovering Weapons Promptly Reported
  　Two Trishaw Drivers Rewarded
    [Ping City News] On the morning of the 8th of this month, trishaw driver Ma Yongnian, while defecating inside a cave in the city wall near South Shunchang Street in the Inner Second District, discovered two handguns. Ma rushed home and found a companion, one kept watch, and the other reported to the Ninth Work Group of the Inner Second District People's Government. The work group immediately dispatched personnel to seize the firearms and gave Ma and his companion commendations.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Yan Zhanjiang)

### 发现武器立即报告　三轮车夫二人受奖
阎占江
1949-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　发现武器立即报告
  　三轮车夫二人受奖
    【平市讯】本月八日上午，三轮车夫马永年，于内二区南顺城街附近城根洞内大便，发现有手枪二枝，马急回家又找一同伴来，一人看守，一人到内二区人民政府第九工作组报告。该组即派人将枪支检获，并予马于二人以褒奖。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（阎占江）
