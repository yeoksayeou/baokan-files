Title: Eastern European Laboring People Unanimously Protest Against the Athens Bandits' Murder of Bebelis

Author:

Date: 1949-03-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 2nd. Reports from various sources: Laboring people in Eastern European countries protest against the atrocities of the Greek royalist fascists, the running dogs of American imperialism, who murdered Bebelis, the General Secretary of the Greek Labor General Confederation.

　　On the evening of February 25, Bulgarian workers held a rally at the Central Trade Union Hall in Sofia. Thousands of workers unanimously condemned this new crime of the Athens executioners. Tashev, secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Trade Union, representing the laboring people of Bulgaria, expressed their indignation at the crimes of the Athens rulers and their imperialist masters, and demanded an investigation into the circumstances of Bebelis's death. Dimitriadis, a well-known Greek trade union activist, declared at the meeting that the Greek people would unite more closely around the Democratic Army and the Communist Party to respond to the new crimes of the Athens executioners.

　　The Central Committee of the Polish Trade Union, representing 3.5 million members, demanded on February 25 that the United Nations immediately investigate Bebelis's death "in order to punish the perpetrators of this crime."

　　The Central Committee of the Albanian Trade Union, the Central Committee of the Romanian People's Republic Youth League, and the Hungarian Youth League all protested against the fascist bandits' despicable murder of Bebelis.

### 对雅典匪徒杀害贝贝利加斯  东欧劳动人民一致抗议

1949-03-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　对雅典匪徒杀害贝贝利加斯
    东欧劳动人民一致抗议
    【新华社陕北二日电】各方报导：东欧各国劳动人民抗议美国帝国主义的走狗——希腊保皇法西斯杀害希腊劳工总同盟总书记贝贝利加斯的暴行。
    保加利亚工人于二月二十五日晚在索非亚中央职工会会场举行大会。数千职工一致痛斥雅典刽子手的这一新罪行。保加利亚职工会中央委员会书记塔舍夫代表保国劳动人民表示了他们对雅典统治者及其帝国主义主子的罪行的义愤，并要求调查贝贝利加斯遇害的情况。著名希腊职工运动者地米特利亚地斯在会议上宣称：希腊人民将更紧密地团结在民主军与共产党的周围，来回答雅典刽子手的新罪行。
    代表三百五十万会员的波兰职工会中央委员会，于二月二十五日，要求联合国立刻调查贝贝利加斯遇害的情况，“以便惩办这一罪行的凶手”。
    阿尔巴尼亚职工会中央委员会，罗马尼亚人民共和国青年联盟中央委员会、匈牙利青年联盟，均对法西斯匪帮卑鄙地杀害贝贝利加斯表示抗议。
