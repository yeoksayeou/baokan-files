Title: Famous French Democratic Women Leaders Call on French Women to Defend Peace: "If France says 'no', there will be no war!"
Author:
Date: 1949-03-11
Page: 3
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, March 9th: TASS, Paris dispatch: L'Humanité published on March 5th an open letter from prominent leaders of the French Democratic Women's Movement to all French women. The letter pointed out: After struggle and suffering, French women have already obtained political and social rights, and these rights have also given us sacred responsibilities. Our strength is extraordinary, because we account for more than half of the population. We live, and we must defend our lives. If we let our country become a battlefield, if we fail to unite to prevent the road to war, we will be guilty of great sins. If France says "no," there will be no war. The letter calls on all French women to unite and fight to defend peace. The signatories of the letter are Magrim Charleblock, Ms. Braun, Mrs. General Batty, Mrs. General Brague, Mrs. Romain Rolland, Elsa Triolet, Marie Claude, Weiland Sitteril, Jeanette, Vermissu, etc.

### 法国著名民主妇女领袖　号召法国妇女保卫和平  “假如法兰西说‘不’，就不会有战争！”

1949-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　法国著名民主妇女领袖
  　号召法国妇女保卫和平
    “假如法兰西说‘不’，就不会有战争！”
    【新华社陕北九日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：人道报于三月五日发表法国民主妇女运动各著名领袖告全法国妇女书。该书指出：经过斗争与苦难，法国妇女们业已获得了政治和社会的权利，而这些权利也把神圣的责任加给了我们。我们的力量是异常巨大的，因为我们占人口的一半以上。我们在生活，我们必须保卫我们的生活。假如我们让我们的国家变成战场，假如我们未能团结起来以杜绝战争的道路，我们就将蒙有极大的罪过。假如法兰西说“不”，就不会有战争。该书号召法国一切妇女团结起来，为保卫和平而斗争。该书签名者有马格里姆·查理布洛克，布劳恩女士，白蒂将军夫人，布拉古将军夫人，罗曼罗兰夫人，埃尔莎·特里奥勒，玛丽·克劳，威兰·席特里尔，真尼特，维米徐等。
