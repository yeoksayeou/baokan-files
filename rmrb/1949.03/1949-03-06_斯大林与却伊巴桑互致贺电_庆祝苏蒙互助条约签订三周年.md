Title: Stalin and Choibalsan Exchange Congratulatory Telegrams Celebrating the Third Anniversary of the Sino-Mongolian Treaty of Mutual Assistance
Author:
Date: 1949-03-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th: TASS Moscow: Marshal Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, received a telegram on February 28 from Marshal Choibalsan, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, stating: "On the occasion of the third anniversary of the signing of the Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Assistance between the Soviet Union and Mongolia and the Agreement on Economic and Cultural Cooperation between the Soviet Union and Mongolia, I would like to ask Your Excellency and the Soviet government to accept the most sincere gratitude from all the Mongolian people, the government of the Mongolian People's Republic, and myself for the immeasurable, selfless, and brotherly help and care you have given to our country's prosperity and development. On behalf of the Mongolian people, the government of the Mongolian People's Republic, and myself, I hope that the fraternal Soviet people will achieve further victories in their great creative endeavors and in the struggle for world peace and democracy; and I express my firm belief that the friendship and cooperation between our two peoples will continue to develop and strengthen for the benefit of the progress and prosperity of both countries."

Marshal Stalin replied in a telegram: "I express my gratitude for your and the Mongolian People's Republic government's friendly congratulations on the third anniversary of the Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Assistance signed between our two countries. I am convinced that this treaty and the further development of extensive cooperation between the two countries will be conducive to the consolidation of the friendship between the two peoples and the improvement of their welfare."

### 斯大林与却伊巴桑互致贺电  庆祝苏蒙互助条约签订三周年

1949-03-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　斯大林与却伊巴桑互致贺电
    庆祝苏蒙互助条约签订三周年
    【新华社陕北四日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席斯大林大元帅于二月二十八日接获蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席却伊巴桑元帅的来电称：值此苏、蒙签订友好互助条约及苏、蒙经济与文化合作协定三周年纪念日，我请阁下和苏联政府接受全体蒙古人民、蒙古人民共和国政府和我个人对你们所致的最热忱的谢意，因为你们曾给予我国的繁荣与发展以无法衡量的、无私的、兄弟般的帮助与关心。我代表蒙古人民与蒙古人民共和国政府和我自己，希望兄弟般的苏联人民在其伟大的创造性的努力与争取全世界和平与民主的斗争中，取得进一步胜利；并表示深信我两国人民的友谊与合作将继续发展和加强，以利两国的进步与繁荣。
    斯大林大元帅复电称：我对阁下和蒙古人民共和国政府对贵国两国所签订的友好互助条约三周年纪念致友好的祝贺，谨表感谢。我深信这一条约与两国间广泛合作的进一步发展，将有利于两国人民友谊的巩固与他们的福利的提高。
