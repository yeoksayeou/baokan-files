Title: USSR Supreme Soviet Presidium Appoints Volin as President of the Supreme Court
Author:
Date: 1949-03-21
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th -- Moscow Dispatch: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has relieved Voznesensky, member of the Politburo of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks), of his positions as Chairman of the State Planning Committee and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers. Saburov, one of the Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, has been appointed as the Chairman of the State Planning Committee. At the same time, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR decided that Volin will replace Golyakov as the President of the Supreme Court of the USSR.

### 苏最高苏维埃主席团  任命伏林为最高法院院长

1949-03-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏最高苏维埃主席团
    任命伏林为最高法院院长
    【新华社陕北二十日电】莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团已解除联共中央政治局委员伏兹涅森斯基所担任的国家计划委员会主席与部长会议副主席两职。国家计划委员会主席一职，由部长会议副主席之一的萨布罗夫继任。同时苏联最高苏维埃主席团决定，高拉科夫所担任全苏联最高法院院长一职，由伏林担任。
