Title: British Communist Party Politburo Passes Declaration, Calling on People to Reject the Atlantic Pact and Oppose Britain's Subservience to American Plutocrats
Author:
Date: 1949-03-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th: TASS, London, 25th: The British Communist Party Politburo collectively passed a declaration last night, calling on the British people to reject the Atlantic Pact, which is preparing for a war of aggression. The declaration stated: This pact is a direct continuation of Churchill's Fulton speech and the Truman Doctrine, openly aimed at establishing a war front against the Soviet Union and the Eastern European People's Democracies. It directly violates the United Nations Charter and betrays the principle of collective security. It is a return to the old method of divided military alliances. This road is the road to war. It is the revival of the anti-communist pact of Hitler, Tojo, and Mussolini in our time, a replay of the Munich collusion of Chamberlain, Daladier, Hitler, and Mussolini. Although the initiators of these pacts claim they are "defensive," they are trying to establish a Western bloc against the Soviet Union.

　　The Atlantic Pact is against the freedom and national independence of all European countries. It has the right to carry out armed intervention in any country to suppress the working class and democratic movements, as in Greece.

　　The declaration further pointed out: This pact makes Britain closely subordinate to American plutocrats and warlords in opposition to European socialism. Britain is already under American military occupation. The policy of the Atlantic Pact is Britain's suicide. It is imposing a heavy burden on our country through rearmament and maintaining an unprecedented peacetime armed force. These burdens are undermining our domestic social construction. Because of this war policy, the people are being destroyed under the crushing weight of heavy taxes and high prices. Continuing this policy means economic disaster for Britain. To save peace, we must fight and reject this Atlantic Pact.

　　The Politburo called for: Organizing mass demonstrations against the Atlantic Pact, demanding every member of Parliament vote against ratifying the Atlantic Pact. Unite to support the Paris World Congress of Defenders of Peace.

　　The Politburo further warned the government: British workers will never allow themselves to be used as tools by Wall Street to wage war against the socialist Soviet Union and the People's Democracies. The declaration concluded: The united strength of the people of the world will shatter the Atlantic Pact's war plans.

### 英共政治局通过声明　号召人民摒弃大西洋公约反对英国作美国富豪附庸

1949-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　英共政治局通过声明
  　号召人民摒弃大西洋公约反对英国作美国富豪附庸
    【新华社北平二十六日电】塔斯社伦敦二十五日电：英国共产党政治局昨晚集体通过声明，号召英国人民摒弃准备侵略战争的大西洋公约。声明说：这一公约是邱吉尔富尔敦演说的政策和杜鲁门主义的直接延续，是公然以建立战争阵线反对苏联与东欧人民民主国家为目的的。它直接违反联合国宪章，背叛集体安全的原则。它是回复到各个分裂的军事同盟的老办法。这一道路就是走向战争的道路。它是希特勒、东条和墨索里尼反共公约在我们这一时代的复活，是张伯伦、达拉第、希特勒与墨索里尼慕尼黑勾当的重演。这些公约的倡导人虽然扬言它们是“防御性”的，但却力图建立反苏的西方国家集团。
    大西洋公约是反对欧洲一切国家的自由与民族独立的。它有权对任何国家进行武装干涉以镇压工人阶级与民主运动如象在希腊一样。
    声明更指出：这一公约使英国紧紧附属于美国富豪和军阀以反对欧洲的社会主义。英国业已处于美国的军事占领之下。大西洋公约的政策是英国的自杀。它正以重整军备与保持和平时期空前规模的武装部队的重负加于我国。这些负担正在破坏我们国内的社会建设。由于这种战争政策，人民正在把人压坍的重税与高昂物价的重压之下趋于毁灭。继续这一政策，就是英国经济的灾难。为了拯救和平，我们必须进行战斗，摒弃这个大西洋公约。
    政治局号召：组织群众示威反对大西洋公约，向每一个议员要求投票反对批准大西洋公约。团结起来，拥护巴黎的世界拥护和平大会。
    政治局更警告政府：英国工人决不容许被用作华尔街进行反对社会主义苏联和人民民主国家的战争的工具。声明最后说：全世界人民的联合力量将粉碎大西洋公约的战争计划。
