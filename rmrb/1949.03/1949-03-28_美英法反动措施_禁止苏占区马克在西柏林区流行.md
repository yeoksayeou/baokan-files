Title: US, British, and French Reactionary Measures: Prohibition of Soviet Zone Marks in West Berlin

Author:

Date: 1949-03-28
Page: 3

　　US, British, and French Reactionary Measures
  　Prohibition of Soviet Zone Marks in West Berlin

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 27th -- Following the "currency reform" on June 18th of last year, the United States, Britain, and France in West Germany carried out another round of plunder against the German people on the 21st. According to the Associated Press, the Western occupying authorities prohibited the circulation of German marks (Soviet Zone marks) in West Berlin on the 21st. "Deutsche Nachrichten" exposed that this new "currency reform" by the Western occupying authorities is a "political plunder of the currency held by the people."

### 美英法反动措施　禁止苏占区马克在西柏林区流行

1949-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　美英法反动措施
  　禁止苏占区马克在西柏林区流行
    【新华社北平二十七日电】西德美英法三国继去年六月十八日“币改”以后，二十一日实行对德国人民的又一次掠夺。据合众社称：西部占领当局于二十一日禁止德国马克（苏占区马克）在柏林西部流通。“德意志新闻”揭穿西方占领当局的此次新的“币改”，是对“人民所持货币的政治掠夺”。
