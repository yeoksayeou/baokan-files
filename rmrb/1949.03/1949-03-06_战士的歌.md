Title: Soldier's Songs
Author: 
Date: 1949-03-06
Page: 4

　　A Song for Soldiers
　　Unite and Fight the Enemy
　　Xing Shuyuan, First Company, Construction Unit

　　New comrade, arrives at the platoon,
　　Every comrade smiles constantly.
　　Feng Ming gives him a military jacket,
　　Gu Kai gives him a shirt to wear,
　　New and old soldiers are like brothers,
　　Helping each other should come first.
　　You help me, I help you,
　　Culture and technology must be practiced more,
　　Practice good technology to go to the battlefield,
　　Calmly and bravely to the front lines,
　　Kill more enemies and seize more guns.

　　Redeem Sins by Performing Meritorious Service
　　Wang Jinsheng, First Company, Successful Unit

　　In nineteen forty-seven,
　　June in the dog days,
　　Jiang bandit and his traitorous lackeys,
　　Came to my home,
　　Without any explanation upon entering the door,
　　Tied me up with rope,
　　Bound me to the brigade headquarters,
　　Detained me for seventeen days,
　　Forcing me to be a bandit soldier,
　　Serving as a clerk.
　　The bandits are truly fierce,
　　Robbing rice and flour,
　　一心害人民 (single-mindedly harm the people)
　　Their heinous crimes reach the sky.
　　With the sound of a cannon,
　　I was liberated,
　　Generous and preferential treatment,
　　Strong education for me,
　　Walked on the bright road,
　　Life is joyful,
　　Ideological transformation is good,
　　Join the army and go to the battlefield,
　　Not afraid to cross great waters,
　　Not afraid to climb high mountains,
　　Redeem sins by performing meritorious service,
　　Capture the bandit Chiang alive.
　　The whole country is liberated,
　　The people are safe and healthy.

　　The Stretcher is Seven Feet Long
　　Li Lanting, Construction Unit

　　The stretcher is seven feet long,
　　Go to the battlefield with me.
　　Run up in one breath,
　　And snatch the wounded soldiers.
　　Put the wounded soldiers on top,
　　Carry them to our rear,
　　Although not a weapon,
　　Its role on the battlefield is strong.
　　In addition to rescuing the wounded,
　　Its uses are also very broad.
　　Can be used as a ladder to get on the roof,
　　Can be used as a bed for sleeping.
　　Can be used as a bridge to cross the river,
　　Helps me everywhere.

### 战士的歌

1949-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　战士的歌
    团结打敌人
    建功部机一连邢书元
　
    新同志，来到班，
    各个同志笑连连。
    凤鸣给他军装褂，
    谷凯给他衬衣穿，
    新老战士如兄弟，
    互相帮助要当先。
    你帮我，我帮你，
    文化技术要多练，
    练好技术上战场，
    沉着勇敢到阵前，
    多杀敌人多缴枪。
　
    立功把罪赎
    成功部一连王金盛
　
    一九四七年，
    六月三伏天，
    蒋贼众汉奸，
    来到我家园，
    进门别不讲，
    用绳把我拴，
    绑到大队部，
    押我十七天，
    逼我当匪军，
    担任办事员。
    匪徒真凶狠，
    抢米又抢面，
    一心害人民，
    万恶罪滔天。
    大炮一声响，
    把我来解放，
    宽大又优待，
    对我教育强，
    走上光明路，
    生活乐洋洋，
    思想转变好，
    参军上战场，
    不怕过大水，
    不怕爬高山，
    立功把罪赎，
    活捉贼老蒋。
    全国得解放，
    人民得安康。
　
    担架七尺长
    建功部李兰亭
　
    担架七尺长，
    同我上战场。
    一气跑上去，
    就把伤员抢。
    伤员放上边，
    抬至我后方，
    虽不是武器，
    战场作用强。
    除救伤员外，
    用处也很广。
    上房可做梯，
    睡觉可当床。
    过河可架桥，
    处处把我帮。
