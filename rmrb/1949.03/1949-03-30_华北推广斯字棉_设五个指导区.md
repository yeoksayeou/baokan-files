Title: North China Promotes "S" Cotton; Establishes Five Guidance Zones
Author: Wei Jin
Date: 1949-03-30
Page: 2

　　North China Promotes "S" Cotton
    Establishes Five Guidance Zones
    [This newspaper reports] The Cotton Production Improvement Division of the North China People's Government's Agricultural Department will promote 2 million *jin* of "S" cotton (mainly No. 2 and No. 4) throughout the region, which can be planted on 200,000 *mu*. The scope of promotion focuses on the following major cotton-producing areas: Beiping Guidance Zone, 50,000 *mu*; Tianjin Guidance Zone, 65,000 *mu* (including the Langfang area); Baoding Guidance Zone, 20,000 *mu*; Shijiazhuang Guidance Zone, 15,000 *mu*; Linqing Guidance Zone, 30,000 *mu*; Handan Guidance Zone, 20,000 *mu* (including the Hebei-Shandong-Henan area). The cotton seeds have now all been transported from Beiping to the various guidance zones. The more than 160 technical personnel from the Cotton Production Improvement Division are scheduled to go to the various guidance zones in early April to carry out organizational promotion work and technical guidance.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　(Wei Jin)

### 华北推广斯字棉　设五个指导区
维进
1949-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北推广斯字棉
    设五个指导区
    【本报讯】华北人民政府农业部棉产改进处在全区推广斯字棉（二号、四号为主）种二百万斤，可植二十万亩。推广范围着重下列主要产棉区，计：北平指导区五万亩，天津指导区六万五千亩（廊坊区在内），保定指导区二万亩，石门指导区一万五千亩，临清指导区三万亩，邯郸指导区二万亩（冀鲁豫区在内）。现棉籽已自平市全部运到各指导区。棉产改进处的一百六十余名技术人员，定于四月初分赴各指导区进行组织推广工作和技术指导。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　（维进）
