Title: World Peace Congress Preparatory Committee Notifies Democratic Mass Organizations in Various Regions to Hold a Meeting in Paris in the Second Half of April
Author:
Date: 1949-03-20
Page: 1

　　World Peace Congress Preparatory Committee
    Notifies Democratic Mass Organizations in Various Regions to Hold a Meeting in Paris in the Second Half of April
    【Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 19th】Agence Telegraphique Parisienne reports: The Preparatory Committee of the World Peace Congress notified the democratic mass organizations of the Peace Congress in various regions on the 17th that the World Peace Congress will be held in Paris in the second half of April. The notice was signed by the famous French poet Aragon, on behalf of the Preparatory Committee. The main purpose is to gather the strength of intellectuals and democratic mass organizations to defend peace, which is increasingly threatened. The best representatives of intellectuals around the world will unite with representatives of millions of men and women who hate war and oppose the resumption of the war path by all means. The Preparatory Committee called on democratic mass organizations to promote the Peace Congress to the people in their countries, and to nominate representatives as soon as possible to assist the Preparatory Committee in carrying out various specific tasks of organizing the World Peace Congress.

### 世界拥护和平大会筹委会  通知各地民主群众团体四月下半月在巴黎开会

1949-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界拥护和平大会筹委会
    通知各地民主群众团体四月下半月在巴黎开会
    【新华社陕北十九日电】电通社巴黎讯：世界拥护和平大会筹备委员会于十七日通知各地拥护和平大会的民主群众团体：世界拥护和平大会将于四月下半月在巴黎举行。该通知由发起人法国著名诗人阿拉贡代表筹备委员会签字，大的目的是集合知识分子和民主群众团体的力量，以保卫和平，这和平正日益感受威胁。全世界知识分子的最优秀的代表将与千百万痛恨战争和以一切方法反对恢复战争路线的男女人们的代表联合起来。筹委会号召各民主群众团体在他们国内向人民宣传和平大会，尽速推举代表协助筹委会进行组织世界拥护和平大会的各项具体工作。
