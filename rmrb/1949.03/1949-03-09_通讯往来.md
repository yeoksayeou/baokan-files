Correspondence

To responsible comrades of various farms in North China, and Comrade Xi Fengzhou of Xingtai Farm:

The Spring Festival has passed, and farmers in various places are actively transporting manure in preparation for spring plowing and sowing. To ensure that this year's various crops are not affected or are less affected by various insect pests and diseases, and to further increase production, we hope that you will provide this newspaper with the following materials after seeing this report:

一、 Methods for selecting seeds for various crops. How to select good seeds? What issues should be noted during seed selection? How to soak seeds?

二、 How to prevent diseases such as yellow dwarf and black rust in wheat?

三、 What insect pests are various crops prone to? How to prevent them in advance? How to eliminate them after they occur?

四、 Please provide more information on the experience of cultivating nurseries and planting trees for afforestation.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Correspondence Section of this Newspaper

### 通讯往来

1949-03-09
第1版()
专栏：通讯往来

　　通讯往来华北各农场负责同志、邢台农场席凤洲同志：
    春节已过，各地农民正积极送粪准备春耕下种等工作。为使今年各种农作物不受或少受各种虫害病灾，使生产提高一步，望见报后，赐给本报以下材料。
    一、各种农作物的选种方法，怎样选择那些好种籽？在选种中应注意哪些问题？怎样浸种？
    二、怎样防止麦子的黄、黑疸等病害？
    三、各种农作物容易发生哪些虫害？预先怎样防御？发生后怎样消灭？
    四、多介绍培植苗圃及植树造林的经验。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
