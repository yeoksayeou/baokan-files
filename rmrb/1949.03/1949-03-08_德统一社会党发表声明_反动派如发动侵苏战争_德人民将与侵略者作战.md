Title: SED Issues Statement: If Reactionaries Launch a War of Aggression Against the Soviet Union, the German People Will Fight the Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1949-03-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 6th: TASS, Berlin, 2nd: The Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany (SED) issued a statement endorsing the statement made by Thorez, Togliatti, and Pollitt representing the people of France, Italy, and England. The SED Politburo's statement said: The German people feel that they share weal and woe with all people who do not want a new war and who oppose war propaganda against the Soviet Union. The Soviet Union is not an aggressor, but the most powerful peace-loving country in the world. The Politburo calls on the German nation to resolutely oppose the Western countries' war propaganda and war preparations against the Soviet Union. If aggression occurs, the German people must fight the aggressors and help the Soviet army achieve peace. The statement was signed by Wilhelm Pieck and Otto Grotewohl, Chairmen of the Socialist Unity Party.

### 德统一社会党发表声明  反动派如发动侵苏战争　德人民将与侵略者作战

1949-03-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　德统一社会党发表声明
    反动派如发动侵苏战争
  　德人民将与侵略者作战
    【新华社陕北六日电】塔斯社柏林二日讯：德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局发表声明，赞成多列士、托格里亚蒂和波立特代表法、意、英人民的声明。德国统一社会党政治局的声明说：德国人民感觉它自己和一切不要新战争、反对针对苏联的战争宣传的人民，都是休戚相关的。苏联不是侵略者，而是世界上最强大的爱好和平的国家。政治局要求德国民族坚决反对西方国家针对苏联的战争宣传和战争准备。如果发生侵略，德国人民必须与侵略者作战，帮助苏联军队达到和平。声明由统一社会党主席威廉·皮克与奥托·格罗提渥签字。
