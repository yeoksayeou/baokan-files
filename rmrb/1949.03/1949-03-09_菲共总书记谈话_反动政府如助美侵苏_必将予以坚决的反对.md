Title: Philippine Communist Party General Secretary's Remarks: If the Reactionary Government Aids the US in Invading the Soviet Union, It Will Be Resolutely Opposed
Author:
Date: 1949-03-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 7th. According to reports from Manila by the Associated Press and United Press, the General Secretary of the Philippine Communist Party, Balgos, told a Manila Times reporter on the 3rd that if US imperialism dares to launch a heinous anti-Soviet war, the Philippine Communist Party will support the Soviet Union; if the Philippine government participates in the US side's operations, the Philippine Communist Party will oppose its own government. Balgos declared that the Philippine Communist Party's immediate goals are to achieve true independence, improve the national economy, and implement land reform. The Philippine Communist Party hopes to establish a united government composed of representatives of workers, peasants, intellectuals, middle peasants, and true nationalists.

### 菲共总书记谈话　反动政府如助美侵苏  必将予以坚决的反对

1949-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　菲共总书记谈话
  　反动政府如助美侵苏  必将予以坚决的反对
    【新华社陕北七日电】据美联社与合众社自马尼拉报道：菲律宾共产党总书记巴尔哥斯于三日告马尼拉时报记者称，如果美帝国主义胆敢发动罪恶的反苏战争，则菲律宾共产党将支持苏联；如果菲律宾政府参加美方作战，则菲共将反对本国政府。巴尔哥斯声称：菲律宾共产党眼前的目标，是获得真正的独立、改善国家经济与实行土地改革。菲共希望建立一个由工人、农民、知识分子、中农阶级与真正民族主义者的代表组成的联合政府。
