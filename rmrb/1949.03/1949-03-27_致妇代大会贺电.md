Title: Congratulatory Telegram to the Women's Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-03-27
Page: 1

Column: The Third Day of the National Women's Congress

　　Congratulatory Telegram to the Women's Congress
        (1) To all the representatives of the National Women's Congress:
    We warmly celebrate the convening of the First National Women's Congress in the ancient capital of Beiping. Chinese women have demonstrated great strength in previous revolutionary movements. We believe that this conference will further unite women across the country to cooperate with the People's Liberation Army, eliminate reactionary forces as soon as possible, and strive for the complete victory of the people and women of the whole country.
    We wish the conference a smooth success!
    Respectfully, Lin Biao, Commander, and Luo Ronghuan, Political Commissar, of the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, leading all commanders and soldiers.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　March 24th
    　　　　(2)
    At the time of the great victory of the People's Army, especially at the time of Chiang Kai-shek's surrender, our association hereby expresses the fighting salute to the heroic Chinese sisters on behalf of the millions of women of Hungary! Your brilliant example encourages us. We are convinced that all forces striving for peace in the world, united together, will undoubtedly eliminate the imperialists who incite war and their running dogs.
    Long live the heroic Chinese women!
    Long live Cai Chang, the model of all democratic women in the world!
    Long live democratic China!
    Long live the world's peace forces!
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hungarian Women's Democratic League
    　　　　(3)
    To the National Women's Congress of China: Dear friends! The Central Committee of the Laboring Women's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic extends sincere and warm respect to the Presidium of the Chinese Women's Congress and all the representatives. The laboring women of the Mongolian People's Republic point out with joy that the convening of this National Women's Congress, initiated by the Women's Federation of the Chinese Liberated Areas, representing more than 200 million Chinese women, is a great historical achievement of the Chinese democratic women's movement. This conference unites all the laboring women of China, regardless of nationality, social status, cultural level, party affiliation, or religious belief. We firmly believe that the result of this conference, under the slogan of solid political unity, will be of great significance. It will strengthen the unity of the entire Chinese people to fight for the complete victory over the counter-revolutionary Kuomintang rule, for the independence of their nation, for the realization of people's democracy, for the free and happy life of China, and for the happiness of Chinese motherhood and children. We wish you greater achievements in your efforts for the happiness of the freedom-loving Chinese people!
    　　　　　Sukhbaatarin and Yanchima, Chairpersons of the Central Committee of the Mongolian Laboring Women

### 致妇代大会贺电

1949-03-27
第1版()
专栏：中国妇代大会第三天

　　致妇代大会贺电
            （一）全国妇女代表大会全体代表同志：
    热烈的庆祝你们在古都北平召开第一次全国妇女代表大会，中国妇女在历次革命运动中，均已显示了伟大的力量，我们相信此次大会必将更进一步的团结全国妇女，配合人民解放军，早日消灭反动势力，争取全国人民、全国妇女的彻底胜利。
    预祝大会的顺利成功！
    中国人民解放军第四野战军司令员林彪、政治委员罗荣桓率全体指战员敬贺
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　三月二十四日
    　　　　（二）
    在人民军伟大胜利之际，尤在蒋介石投降之际，敝会兹代表匈牙利千万的妇女向英勇的中国姊妹致战斗的敬礼！你们的光辉榜样鼓励着我们，我们确信世界上一切争和平的力量团结起来，将无疑问的消灭煽动战争的帝国主义及其走狗。
    英勇的中国妇女万岁！
    全世界一切民主妇女的模范蔡畅万岁！
    民主的中国万岁！
    世界和平力量万岁！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　匈牙利妇女民主联盟
    　　　　（三）
    中国全国妇女代表大会：亲爱的朋友们！蒙古人民共和国劳动妇女呼拉尔中央委员会，向中国妇女代表大会主席团和全体代表们致诚恳热烈的敬意。蒙古人民共和国的劳动妇女，以愉快的心情指出，由中国解放区妇联所发起的这一次代表二万万余中国妇女的全国妇女代表大会的召开，是中国民主妇女运动的一个伟大的历史成就。这个大会将全中国的劳动妇女，不问民族、社会地位，文化水准，所属党派、宗教信仰等等的不同，都团结在一起了。我们深深相信，这次大会的结果，在巩固的政治团结的口号下，必将具有极大的意义。它将加强全中国人民的团结，去为彻底战胜反革命的国民党统治，为自己民族的独立、为实现人民的民主、为中国的自由幸福的生活，以及为中国母性与儿童的幸运而斗争。谨祝你们为爱好自由的中国人民的幸福而努力！今后将获得更大的成就！
    　　　　　蒙古劳动妇女中央委员会主席苏希巴多林、杨齐玛
