Title: All-China Women's Federation Preparatory Committee Director and Deputy Director Cai Chang and Deng Yingchao Arrive in Beiping; More Than 100 Women's Congress Representatives Go to the Train Station to Welcome Them

Author:
Date: 1949-03-19
Page: 1

　　All-China Women's Federation Preparatory Committee Director and Deputy Director
  　Cai Chang and Deng Yingchao Arrive in Beiping
    More Than 100 Women's Congress Representatives Go to the Train Station to Welcome Them
    [This newspaper reports] Cai Chang, Deng Yingchao, Director and Deputy Director of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Preparatory Committee, and leaders of women and children's work in various liberated areas, including Chen Shaomin, Yang Zhihua, Kang Keqing, and Qu Mengjue, arrived in Beiping yesterday (18th) at 10:30 AM. Li Dequan, Deputy Director of the Women's Federation Preparatory Committee, Zhang Qinqiu, Secretary-General, Zeng Xianzhi, Luo Shuzhang, Preparatory Committee members Xu Guangping, Cao Mengjun, Liu Qingyang, Hu Ziying, and more than 100 representatives from various regions attending the First National Congress of Chinese Women all went to the Beiping East Railway Station to welcome them. As the special train pulled into the first platform, shouts of "Welcome Comrade Cai Chang!" "Welcome Comrade Deng Yingchao!" "Welcome Comrade Chen Shaomin!" resounded. The welcoming crowd surged towards the train doors, and Comrade Cai Chang and Comrade Deng Yingchao smiled and shook hands tightly with everyone.

### 全国妇联筹委会正副主任　蔡畅邓颖超抵平  妇代会代表百余人赴车站欢迎

1949-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国妇联筹委会正副主任
  　蔡畅邓颖超抵平
    妇代会代表百余人赴车站欢迎
    【本报讯】全国民主妇女联合会筹备委员会正副主任蔡畅、邓颖超、及解放区各地妇女儿童工作领导者陈少敏、杨芝华、康克清、区梦觉等同志，已于昨日（十八）上午十时半抵平。妇联筹委会副主任李德全，秘书长张琴秋、曾宪植、罗叔章，筹委许广平、曹孟君、刘清扬、胡子婴等及出席中国妇女第一次全国代表大会的各地代表百余人，齐赴北平东车站欢迎。当专车驰进第一月台时，“欢迎蔡畅同志！”“欢迎邓颖超同志！”“欢迎陈少敏同志！”的口号声四起，欢迎的行列，一齐拥向车门，蔡畅同志和邓颖超同志含笑和大家紧紧握手。
