Title: "March 8th" International Women's Day Celebrated Across the Soviet Union: Moscow Women Write Letter to Stalin to Express Respect
Author:
Date: 1949-03-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th. TASS, Moscow, 8th: The Moscow City Communist Party organization, the City Soviet, and mass organizations, together with representatives of the Soviet Army, held a meeting in the "Grand Theatre" to commemorate the March 8th International Women's Day. Among those present were prominent women from Moscow and the Moscow region - female representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, women who have won Stalin Prizes, Stakhanovite female workers from Moscow enterprises, female engineers, female teachers, female doctors, female actors, female Communist Party workers, female scientists, collective farm women, and Heroine Mothers. S.M. Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, also attended the meeting. N. V. Popova, Chairman of the All-Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, delivered a report on International Women's Day at the meeting. The meeting adopted a letter of respect to Stalin in an extremely warm atmosphere.

Leningrad held a meeting to commemorate International Women's Day. Many outstanding women wore medals and badges, which were proof of their merits for the motherland.

The working women of Tbilisi, the capital of the Georgian Republic, commemorated Women's Day with new production achievements. Silk factory workers Ekaterina Mekharadze, Tamara Badbridze and others completed 170 to 180 percent of their production quotas on March 8, and the quality was excellent. Prizes and certificates were awarded to the most outstanding female industrial workers at various conferences.

The commemoration meeting in the Mari Autonomous Republic (located in the middle reaches of the Volga River within the Russian Federal Republic) summarized the cultural progress of women in the republic. Before the October Revolution, only 2 percent of women in the area were literate, but now female students account for more than half of all students in universities and specialized colleges in the republic.

### 苏联各地纪念“三八”节  莫斯科妇女写信给斯大林致敬

1949-03-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联各地纪念“三八”节
    莫斯科妇女写信给斯大林致敬
    【新华社陕北十二日电】塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：莫斯科市共产党组织、市苏维埃及群众团体会同苏军代表在“大戏院”举行纪念三八国际妇女节大会。到会的有莫斯科市与莫斯科州的妇女界名人——苏联最高苏维埃与俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃中的女代表、获得斯大林奖金的妇女、莫斯科各企业斯塔哈诺夫女工、女工程师、女教师、女医生、女演员、共产党女工作人员、女科学家、集体农妇及母亲英雄等。苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席斯维尔尼克也出席了大会。全苏妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃在会上作国际妇女节报告。会议在极热烈的气氛中通过向斯大林致敬的信。
    列宁格勒举行了国际妇女节纪念会。许多优秀的妇女都佩带着勋章与奖章，这是她们为祖国所建功勋的证明。
    乔治亚共和国首都第佛利斯的劳动妇女，以新的生产成绩纪念妇女节。丝织厂女工叶卡特利娜·美卡拉杰，塔马拉·巴德布里杰及其他等人，在三月八日完成了生产定额的百分之一百七十到一百八十，并且品质优良。各处大会上并颁发奖金与奖状给最优秀的产业女工。
    马利自治共和国（在俄罗斯联邦共和国内，位于伏尔加河中游）的纪念会总结了该共和国妇女在文化上的进步，十月革命前该地只有百分之二的妇女识字，而现在该共和国高等学校与专科学院的全体学生中，女学生已占一半以上。
