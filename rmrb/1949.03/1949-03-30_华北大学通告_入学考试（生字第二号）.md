Author:
Date: 1949-03-30
Page: 1

　　North China University Notice
  　Entrance Examination (Student Character No. 2)
    The location, time, and candidate numbers for the second new student entrance examination for the first and second departments of our school's second term are announced below:
    1. Examination Date: March 31st Time: 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM Written Test 12:00 PM to 5:00 PM Oral Test
    2. Examination Location: Fourth Courtyard of Peking University, Guohui Street
    3. Registration Numbers of Examination Participants:
    Political Character 501 – 1000 Educational Character 181 – 250 (Those who registered at Qianmian Hutong)
    A 501 – 1000 B 251 – 350 (Those who originally registered at North China College)
    　　　　　　　　　March 29th
