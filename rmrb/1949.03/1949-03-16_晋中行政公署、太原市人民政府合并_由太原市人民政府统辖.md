Title: Jinzhong Administrative Office and Taiyuan Municipal People's Government Merged, Under the Jurisdiction of Taiyuan Municipal People's Government
Author:
Date: 1949-03-16
Page: 1

　　Jinzhong Administrative Office and Taiyuan Municipal People's Government Merged
	Under the Jurisdiction of Taiyuan Municipal People's Government
	[Our Special Dispatch] In order to facilitate centralized leadership, strengthen work, and more forcefully support the front-line war, the North China People's Government has decided to merge the Jinzhong Administrative Office and the Taiyuan Municipal People's Government. The Jinzhong Administrative Office will be closed, and its original jurisdiction will be temporarily under the jurisdiction of the Taiyuan Municipal People's Government. Pei Lisheng has been appointed as the mayor of the city.

### 晋中行政公署、太原市人民政府合并  由太原市人民政府统辖

1949-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　晋中行政公署、太原市人民政府合并
    由太原市人民政府统辖
    【本报特讯】华北人民政府为便利集中领导与加强工作而更有力的支援前线战争，特决定晋中行政公署与太原市人民政府合并，晋中行政公署即办理结束，原辖区暂行划归太原市人民政府管辖，并任命裴丽生为该市市长。
