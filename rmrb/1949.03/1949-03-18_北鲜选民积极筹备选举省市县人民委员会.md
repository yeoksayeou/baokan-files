Title: North Korean Voters Actively Prepare for Elections of Provincial, City, and County People's Committees
Author:
Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th. TASS, Pyongyang, 13th: The elections for the North Korean provincial, city, and county People's Committees, in accordance with the decision of the Second Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, are scheduled to be held on March 30th. Voters from all walks of life are actively participating in the preparations for the election campaign. Provinces have begun to nominate candidates for representatives to the People's Committees. Mass meetings are being held in factories, government offices, military units, and rural areas, at which advanced workers, peasants, and intellectuals are nominated as candidates. A meeting of workers, engineers, and staff of the Jin Nan Coal Mine nominated Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, as a candidate for representative to the People's Committee in the 37th electoral district of South Pyongyang Province, and has received unanimous support from all voters in the district. A meeting of workers and staff of the "Sado E. Boheng" factory nominated Kim Du-bong, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Chairman of the Workers' Party, as a candidate for the 77th electoral district. The workers' meeting at the "Jin Nan" factory also nominated Wang Ji Kai, a worker who has worked at the factory for fourteen years and has made many new contributions to the production process, as a candidate for representative to the Provincial People's Committee. The 5th electoral district nominated Kim Yi Se, a worker who has consistently exceeded his individual production quota for more than two years, as a candidate.

### 北鲜选民积极筹备选举省市县人民委员会

1949-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　北鲜选民积极筹备选举省市县人民委员会
    【新华社陕北十七日电】塔斯社平壤十三日电：北朝鲜盛市、县人民委员会的选举，遵照朝鲜人民民主共和国最高人民议会第二次大会的决定，定于三月三十日举行。各阶层选民正积极参加选举运动的准备工作。各省已开始提出人民委员会代表候选人。各工厂、机关、部队及农村纷纷举行群众大会，会上前进的工人、农民和知识分子被推举为候选人。金南（译音）煤矿的工人、工程师和职员大会推举朝鲜人民民主共和国总理金日成为南平壤省第三十七选区人民委员会代表的候选人，已获得该区全体选民的一致支持。“沙多·额·波亨”（译音）工厂的工人与职员大会，推举朝鲜人民民主共和国最高人民议会主席团主席、劳动党主席金＠凤为第七十七选区的候选人。“金南”工厂的工人大会并将在该厂工作十四年，对生产过程有许多新建树的工人王基开（译音）推举为省人民委员会代表的候选人。第五选区推举了两年余一贯地超过个人生产定额的工人金贻塞（译音）为候选人。
