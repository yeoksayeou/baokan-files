Title: Fourth Congress of North Korean Farmers' Alliance Concludes
Author:
Date: 1949-03-18
Page: 3

　　North Korean Farmers' Alliance Fourth Congress Concludes
　　(Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 14th) Pyongyang Radio broadcast on the 10th that the Fourth Congress of the North Korean Farmers' Alliance, which opened on the 5th of this month, concluded on the 10th. The representatives attending the congress listened to and discussed the work report of the Farmers' Alliance. Speakers pointed out that thanks to the active support of the North Korean people, the measures implemented by the Kim Il-sung government have achieved great success. Before closing, the congress passed a resolution paying tribute to Premier Kim Il-sung and to the workers and peasants who are currently fighting against the Syngman Rhee puppet government.

### 北鲜农民联盟四代大会闭幕

1949-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　北鲜农民联盟四代大会闭幕
    【新华社陕北十四日电】平壤电台十日广播称：本月五日开幕的北朝鲜农民联盟第四届代表大会，已于十日闭幕。出席大会的各代表听取并讨论了农民联盟的工作报告，各发言者指出，由于北朝鲜人民的积极支持，金日成政府所实施的措施已获得巨大的成就。大会于闭幕前通过决议，向总理金日成致敬，并向现正进行反对李承晚伪政府的工人农民致敬。
