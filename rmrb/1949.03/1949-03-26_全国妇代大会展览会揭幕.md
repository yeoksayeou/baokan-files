Title: National Women's Congress Exhibition Opens
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-03-26
Page: 1

　　National Women's Congress Exhibition Opens
    [Our Dispatch] The exhibition of the First National Congress of Chinese Women officially opened today (the 26th) at Sun Yat-sen Hall in Zhongshan Park. This is an exhibition of the great gains and achievements of the broad masses of women in China's five major liberated areas, especially working women. The exhibition is arranged in two large exhibition rooms. The exhibits include more than 2,000 photographs of women in the liberated areas engaged in various forms of production, supporting the front lines, and participating in land reform, as well as thousands of industrial, agricultural, and sideline products. From the opening of the congress on the 24th to the two days of the exhibition preview yesterday, there were 3,231 visitors. (Bai Sheng)

### 全国妇代大会展览会揭幕
柏生
1949-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国妇代大会展览会揭幕
    【本报讯】中国妇女第一次全国代表大会的展览会，今天（二十六）正式在中山公园中山堂揭幕。这是包括着中国五大解放区广大妇女群众，特别是劳动妇女的伟大收获和成果的展览。展览会是分两个大展览室布置的。展览品中包括了解放区妇女的各种生产和支援前线、参加土改的照片二千余幅，以及工业、农业、副业等的各种生产品数千件。从二十四日大会开幕后到昨天展览会举行预展的两天中，就有了三千二百三十一个观众。（柏生）
