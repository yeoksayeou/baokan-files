Title: Osaka Workers Demonstrate, Demanding Yoshida Get Out; MacArthur Actually Limits Union Activities
Author:
Date: 1949-03-29
Page: 3

　　Osaka Workers Demonstrate
    Demanding Yoshida Get Out
    MacArthur Actually Limits Union Activities
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th: Tokyo dispatch: 35,000 Japanese workers held a demonstration in Osaka on the 27th, demanding the resignation of the Yoshida reactionary cabinet. The demonstrating masses were suppressed by 2,000 police, with 20 injured and 20 arrested.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 27th: Dentsu News Agency, Tokyo dispatch: MacArthur Headquarters has ordered the Japanese Ministry of Labor to take emergency measures to "restrict union activities in Japan." According to this order, workers who actively engage in union work will be fired. This measure by the American occupation authorities aims to deprive workers of their rights, so they won't receive support and protection from the unions.

### 大阪工人示威  要求吉田滚开　麦克阿瑟竟限止工会活动

1949-03-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　大阪工人示威
    要求吉田滚开
    麦克阿瑟竟限止工会活动
    【新华社北平二十八日电】东京讯：三万五千日本工人于二十七日在大阪举行示威，要求吉田反动内阁辞职。示威群众遭受二千警察的镇压，二十名受伤，二十名被捕。
    【新华社北平二十七日电】电通社东京讯：麦克阿瑟总部已下令日本劳工省采取紧急措施，“限制日本的工会活动”。按照这个命令，凡积极从事工会工作的工人，都要被解雇。美国占领军当局这一措施，目的在于剥夺工人的权利，使他们受不到工会的支持与保护。
