Title: Tsinghua's Nation-Building Work Unfolds; Founding Meeting to be Held Tomorrow
Author: Jin Feng
Date: 1949-03-19
Page: 1

　　Tsinghua's Nation-Building Work Unfolds
  　Founding Meeting to be Held Tomorrow
    [Our Newspaper] After the establishment of the preparatory committee of the New Democratic Youth League of Tsinghua University in Ping City, the work of establishing the League has achieved initial results. On the 17th and 18th of this month, the preparatory committee approved the transfer of 138 members of the original revolutionary youth organization "New Democratic Youth Alliance" of Tsinghua, including Gao Zhonghua and Xu Naiming, to become members of the New Democratic Youth League. It is expected that more members of the Alliance will be reviewed and approved for membership. At the same time, the preparatory committee will also review the applications for membership introduced by various system organizations or individual applications, and decided to hold a grand founding meeting of the New Democratic Youth League at 1:00 PM on the 20th.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Jin Feng)

### 清华建国工作开展　明日举行成立大会
金凤
1949-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　清华建国工作开展
  　明日举行成立大会
    【本报讯】平市清华大学新民主主义青年团筹委会成立之后，建团工作已获初步成绩。本月十七、十八两日，筹委会批准清华原有之革命青年组织“新民主主义青年联盟”盟员高忠华、徐乃明等一百三十八人转为新民主主义青年团团员。预计将有更多盟员将被审核批准入团。同时，筹委会并将审核由各系统社团所介绍或个别申请之入团请求，并决定于二十日下午一时举行隆重之新青团成立大会。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（金凤）
