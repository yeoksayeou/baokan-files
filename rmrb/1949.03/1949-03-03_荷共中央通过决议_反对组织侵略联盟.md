Title: Dutch Communist Party Central Committee Passes Resolution Opposing Organization of Aggression Alliance
Author: 
Date: 1949-03-03
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 1st: TASS, The Hague, February 24th: The Central Committee of the Dutch Communist Party held a meeting in Amsterdam to discuss domestic and foreign policies. The resolution passed by the Central Committee of the Dutch Communist Party stated: Since the United States rejected Stalin's peace proposals, Britain and other countries of the "Western European Group," including the Netherlands, have begun to intensify armament and organize new aggressive alliances. The Central Committee of the Communist Party hereby calls on the Dutch nation to strongly protest against the signing of the North Atlantic Treaty, against the instigators of new wars, and to strive for lasting peace.

### 荷共中央通过决议　反对组织侵略联盟

1949-03-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　荷共中央通过决议
  　反对组织侵略联盟
    【新华社陕北一日电】塔斯社海牙二月二十四日讯：荷兰共产党中央委员会在阿姆斯特丹举行会议，讨论了内政和外交政策的问题。荷共中央委员会通过的决议称：自从美国拒绝了斯大林的和平建议以来，英国和其他“西欧集团”的国家，包括荷兰在内，就开始加紧武装和组织新的侵略联盟。共产党中央委员会为此特号召荷兰民族提出强力抗议，反对签订北大西洋公约，反对新战争挑拨者，争取持久的和平。
