Title: French "Left-Wing Radical Party and Resistance Movement Participants" Plenary Session Opposes French Participation in Aggression Plan
Author:
Date: 1949-03-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 7th. TASS, Paris, 4th: The National Committee of the "Left-Wing Radical Party and Resistance Movement Participants" (note: a group that split from the Radical Party) has recently announced its resolution regarding Queuille's remarks to a United Press reporter. The resolution states that in this baseless and previously unapproved-by-the-French-Parliament conversation, Queuille condemned an allied nation for intending to "seize control of Europe" and requested the assistance of the United States armed forces. Queuille is using this method to drag France into a joint group that violates the spirit and letter of France's international obligations. He is attempting to incite preemptive intervention by the United States military. Queuille revealed the atmosphere of the ongoing North Atlantic Treaty negotiations, which caused us such concern that this diplomatic document was drafted to enable the use of France in the event of an anti-Soviet war of aggression. The National Committee of the "Left-Wing Radical Party and Resistance Movement Participants" believes that this policy followed by the leaders of the French government seriously violates the government's obligation to protect national interests. The committee reiterates the willingness expressed by those members of parliament and leaders of the association who are loyal to the principles of the French Constitution and the principles of the United Nations Charter: to oppose France's participation in any aggression plan. The resolution was signed by committee members Pierre Cot, Albert Bayet, Justin Godart, and Mrs. Zeyer.

### 法“左翼激进党及抵抗运动参加者”全会  反对法国参加侵略计划

1949-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　法“左翼激进党及抵抗运动参加者”全会
    反对法国参加侵略计划
    【新华社陕北七日电】塔斯社巴黎四日讯：“左翼激进党及抵抗运动参加者”全国委员会（按系自激进党中分裂出来的团体），顷公布其关于葛义向合众社记者所发表的谈话的决议。该决议宣称，葛义在这一毫无根据而且事先丝毫未经法国议会认可的谈话中，曾谴责某一盟国意欲“攫取对欧洲的控制权”，并要求美国武装部队的援助。葛义正在用这种方式把法国拖入一种联合集团中，这种联合集团是与法国所承担的国际义务的精神及文字相违背的。他正在试图招致美国军队先发制人的干涉。葛义透露了正在进行的北大西洋公约谈判的气氛，他因而引起了我们如此的忧虑，即这一外交文件之拟成，乃是旨在使进行反苏侵略战争时有利用法国的可能。“左翼激进党及抵抗运动参加者”全国委员会认为，法国政府领袖所遵循的这一政策严重地违背了政府应该担负的保护民族利益的义务。委员会重申那些忠于法国宪法原则及联合国宪章原则的该会议员及该会领袖所表示的这一意愿，即：反对法国参加任何侵略计划。该决议由委员会委员彼埃尔·科特，阿尔伯特·贝叶，杰斯丁·哥达及札耶夫人等签名。
