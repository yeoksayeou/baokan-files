Title: Soviet Tea Harvest Bumper Crop
Author:
Date: 1959-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: According to TASS, the tea harvest in Soviet Georgia has been another bumper crop this year. Tea farmers have already picked 140,000 tons of fresh leaves and sent them to factories for processing. This figure is 7,000 tons more than in 1957, which was considered an unprecedented bumper crop in the history of Soviet tea cultivation.

Tea crops occupy a major position in the production of collective farms and state farms in this republic. Tea produced in this republic accounts for 97% of the total tea production in the Soviet Union.

### 苏联茶叶丰收

1959-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联茶叶丰收
    新华社讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联格鲁吉亚的茶叶今年又获得了大丰收。茶农们已经采了十四万吨鲜叶，并已送交工厂加工。这个数字被认为是苏联种茶史上空前大丰收的1957年还多七千吨。
    茶叶作物在这个共和国的集体农庄和国营农场的生产中占着主要地位。这个共和国出产的茶叶占苏联全国产茶量的97％。
