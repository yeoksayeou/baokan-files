Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China: Promulgating the Organizational Regulations for the People's Congress and People's Committee of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1959-11-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th.
    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    Promulgating the Organizational Regulations for the People's Congress and People's Committee of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region
    Xinhua News Agency, 27th - The Organizational Regulations for the People's Congress and People's Committee of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region were approved at the 11th meeting of the Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on November 27, 1959, and are hereby promulgated.
                             Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
			    November 27, 1959

### 中华人民共和国主席令  公布宁夏回族自治区人代会和人民委员会组织条例

1959-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    公布宁夏回族自治区人代会和人民委员会组织条例
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国主席令
    宁夏回族自治区人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例已由中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于一九五九年十一月二十七日第十一次会议批准，现予公布。
                             中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇			    一九五九年十一月二十七日
