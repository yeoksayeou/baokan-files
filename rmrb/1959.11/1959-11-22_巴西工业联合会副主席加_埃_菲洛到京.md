Title: Vice President of the Brazilian National Confederation of Industry, Mr. G. E. Filho, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 21st November. At the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Mr. Gabriel Hermes Filho, Vice President of the Brazilian National Confederation of Industry, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 20th for a visit to China. Ji Chao-ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, was at the airport to welcome him. Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet in the evening to welcome the guest.

### 巴西工业联合会副主席加·埃·菲洛到京

1959-11-22
第3版()
专栏：

    巴西工业联合会副主席加·埃·菲洛到京
    新华社21日讯　应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请来我国访问的巴西全国工业联合会副主席加布里埃耳·埃尔梅斯·菲洛20日下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎。中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸当晚设宴欢迎客人。
