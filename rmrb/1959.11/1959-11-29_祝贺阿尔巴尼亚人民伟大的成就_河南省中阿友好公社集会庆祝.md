Title: Congratulations on the Great Achievements of the Albanian People: Henan Province China-Albania Friendship Commune Holds Rally to Celebrate
Author:
Date: 1959-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 28th: More than 1,000 representatives of commune members from the China-Albania Friendship People's Commune in Lushi County, Henan Province, solemnly gathered on the 24th to celebrate the 15th anniversary of Albania's liberation.

Routfe Muhamed and Sotiri Naci, representatives from the Albanian Embassy in China, who came from Beijing, were invited to attend the celebration.

Lu Ming, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhao Ren, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of Henan Province, and Wang Fuguo, First Secretary of the Lushi County Party Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, also attended the celebration.

Zhang Shaoqu, Secretary of the Party Committee of the China-Albania Friendship Commune, extended cordial congratulations to the distinguished Albanian guests on behalf of the 27,000 commune members at the meeting. He said that we have established friendly relations with the Albanian people since 1955. The Albanian people often send letters of greetings and gifts. The noble friendship of the hardworking and brave Albanian people has greatly encouraged all members of the China-Albania Friendship Commune. The commune had a bumper harvest in agricultural production last year, and this year it continues to leap forward. He pointed out in his speech that the Albanian people, under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party, have also achieved brilliant achievements in socialist construction. All members of the China-Albania Friendship Commune regard this as their own achievement and are proud of it. At the end of his speech, he shouted: Long live the friendship between the fraternal Chinese and Albanian people!

Routfe Muhamed, representative of the Albanian Embassy in China, gave a detailed introduction in his speech to the great achievements made by the Albanian people under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party and with the encouragement and help of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union over the past fifteen years. He finally said, we are forever faithful friends to you. The common cause of building socialism unites us together. No force in the world can destroy our fraternal, great, and unbreakable friendship.

His speech was constantly interrupted by warm applause.

The conference read out the congratulatory telegram from the Lushi County Party Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Lushi County People's Committee to the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, and the congratulatory telegram from the China-Albania Friendship People's Commune to the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative.

### 祝贺阿尔巴尼亚人民伟大的成就  河南省中阿友好公社集会庆祝

1959-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺阿尔巴尼亚人民伟大的成就
    河南省中阿友好公社集会庆祝
    据新华社郑州28日电　河南省卢氏县中国阿尔巴尼亚友好人民公社社员代表一千多人，24日隆重集会，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年。
    由北京赶来的阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使馆代表鲁脱费·穆罕默德和索蒂里·纳奇应邀参加了庆祝会。
    对外文化联络委员会副司长鲁明、河南省外事办公室副主任赵仁、中共卢氏县委第一书记王富国等也参加了庆祝大会。
    中阿友好公社党委书记张绍渠，在会上代表全公社二万七千名社员向远道而来的阿尔巴尼亚贵宾致亲切的祝贺。他说，从1955年起，我们就和阿尔巴尼亚人民建立了友谊联系。阿尔巴尼亚人民经常寄来问候信和礼物。勤劳、勇敢的阿尔巴尼亚人民的崇高友谊，大大鼓舞了中阿友好公社全体社员的干劲。去年公社农业生产大丰收，今年又继续跃进。他在讲话中指出，阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，在社会主义建设中，同样也取得了辉煌的成就。中阿友好公社的全体社员，都把它看作是自己的成就，并为此而感到自豪。他在结束讲话的时候高呼：兄弟般的中阿两国人民友谊万岁！
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使馆代表鲁脱费·穆罕默德，在讲话中详细介绍了阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，在以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的鼓舞和帮助下，十五年来所取得的巨大成就。他最后说，我们和您们是永远忠实的朋友。建设社会主义的共同事业，把我们联结在一起。世界上没有任何力量能够破坏我们兄弟般的、伟大的、牢不可破的友谊。
    他的讲话不断为热烈的掌声所打断。
    大会宣读了中共卢氏县委、卢氏县人民委员会致阿中友好协会的贺电，中阿友好人民公社致阿中友好农业生产合作社的贺电。
