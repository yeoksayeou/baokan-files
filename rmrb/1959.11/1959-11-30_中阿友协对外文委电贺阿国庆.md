Title: Sino-Albanian Friendship Association, Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee Send Congratulations on Albanian National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-11-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Jiang Nanxiang, President of the China-Albania Friendship Association, and the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee of the People's Republic of China, sent telegrams to Kellezi, Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, and the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, respectively, on the 28th, to congratulate them on the 15th anniversary of Albania's liberation.
    The congratulatory messages congratulated the Albanian people on the brilliant achievements they have made under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party in the past fifteen years, and wished them greater victories in the future socialist construction.
    The congratulatory messages wished the fraternal friendship between the people of China and Albania further consolidation and development.

### 中阿友协对外文委电贺阿国庆

1959-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    中阿友协对外文委电贺阿国庆
    新华社28日讯　中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会会长蒋南翔、中华人民共和国对外文化联络委员会，28日分别致电阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会主席凯列齐、阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会，祝贺阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年。
    贺电祝贺十五年来阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下所取得的辉煌成就，并祝他们在今后的社会主义建设事业中取得更大的胜利。
    贺电祝中阿两国人民的兄弟友谊进一步巩固和发展。
