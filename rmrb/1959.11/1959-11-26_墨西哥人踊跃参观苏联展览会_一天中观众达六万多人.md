Title: Mexicans Eagerly Visit Soviet Exhibition; More Than 60,000 Visitors in One Day
Author:
Date: 1959-11-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, November 23rd (TASS dispatch): The Soviet Exhibition of Achievements in Science, Technology and Culture has been warmly welcomed by the Mexican people.

The exhibition began receiving visitors at eleven o'clock in the morning on the 22nd, and a continuous stream of people poured into the exhibition site—the building of the National Assembly. More than 60,000 people visited the exhibition in one day, an unprecedented scene for exhibitions held in Mexico City.

### 墨西哥人踊跃参观苏联展览会  一天中观众达六万多人

1959-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    墨西哥人踊跃参观苏联展览会
    一天中观众达六万多人
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社墨西哥城23日讯：苏联科学、技术和文化成就展览会受到墨西哥人的热烈欢迎。
    展览会在22日上午十一时开始接待广大观众，人群络绎不绝地涌向展览会所在地——国民大会的大厦。在一天中参观展览会的就有六万多人，这是在墨西哥城举行的展览会从未有过的盛况。
