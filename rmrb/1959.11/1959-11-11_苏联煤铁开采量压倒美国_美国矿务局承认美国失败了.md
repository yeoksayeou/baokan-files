Title: Soviet Coal and Iron Ore Production Surpasses the United States; U.S. Bureau of Mines Admits American Failure
Author:
Date: 1959-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 10th November. According to TASS, New York, 8th November: The U.S. Bureau of Mines reported that in 1958, the Soviet Union caught up with the United States in coal production and became the world's largest coal producer.

The report went on to say that in 1958, the Soviet Union also surpassed the United States in iron ore production.

The report pointed out that between 1953 and 1958, the Soviet Union's share of world steel production increased from 16.2% to 20.3%, while the United States' share decreased from 43.1% to 28.6% during the same period.

The report continued, noting that socialist countries now account for half of the world's coal production, compared to only 38.1% in 1953. In 1958, these countries mined 31.6% of the world's iron ore, compared to only 21.2% in 1953.

The report emphasized that "Communist countries have also made achievements in many other sectors, including oil and non-ferrous metals."

The report also highlighted the significant achievements made by the People's Republic of China in "developing vast reserves of coal and iron ore."

### 苏联煤铁开采量压倒美国  美国矿务局承认美国失败了

1959-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联煤铁开采量压倒美国
    美国矿务局承认美国失败了
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社纽约8日讯：据美国矿务局发表的报告说，1958年，苏联在煤的开采量方面赶上了美国而成为世界上最大的产煤国。
    报告接着说，1958年，苏联在铁矿的开采量方面也超过了美国。
    这个报告指出，在1953到1958年之间，苏联在世界钢产量中的比重从16.2％增加到20.3％；而美国的比重在这期间从43.1％降低到28.6％。
    报告继续指出，在社会主义国家中，现在煤的开采量为世界开采量的一半，而1953年只占38.1％。1958年，这些国家开采的铁矿为世界开采量的31.6％，而1953年只占21.2％。
    报告强调指出，“共产党国家在许多其他的部门，其中包括在石油和有色金属方面也取得了成就。”
    报告还指出了中华人民共和国在“开发蕴藏量巨大的煤矿和铁矿方面”所取得的重大成就。
