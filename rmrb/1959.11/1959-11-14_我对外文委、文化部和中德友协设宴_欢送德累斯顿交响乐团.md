Title: Foreign Cultural Commission, Ministry of Culture, and Sino-German Friendship Association Host Banquet to See Off Dresden Symphony Orchestra
Author:
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 13th – The Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Ministry of Culture, and the Sino-German Friendship Association held a banquet tonight to warmly bid farewell to all the musicians of the Dresden Symphony Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic, who are about to leave China.

Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Foreign Cultural Commission, and Professor Borngeatz, head of the Symphony Orchestra, spoke at the banquet successively, warmly congratulating the increasing cultural cooperation between China and Germany, and congratulating the unbreakable friendship between the two peoples, which is being consolidated and developed.

Wang Guodong, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, was invited to attend the banquet. Zhu Kezhen, President of the Sino-German Friendship Association, Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Commission, Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, and other well-known figures from the capital's music circles, attended the banquet as guests.

The Dresden Symphony Orchestra visited and performed in Beijing, Xi'an, Chongqing, Nanjing, Shanghai and other places for more than a month, giving outstanding performances to 150,000 Chinese audience members. At today's banquet, responsible persons from Chinese cultural and musical circles repeatedly raised their glasses to warmly congratulate the German musicians on the success of their visit and performances, and to thank them for bringing unforgettable artistic enjoyment and the precious friendship of the German people to the Chinese people.

### 我对外文委、文化部和中德友协设宴  欢送德累斯顿交响乐团

1959-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    我对外文委、文化部和中德友协设宴
    欢送德累斯顿交响乐团
    新华社13日讯　对外文化联络委员会、文化部和中国德意志民主共和国友好协会今晚举行宴会，热烈欢送即将离开我国的德意志民主共和国德累斯顿交响乐团全体音乐家。
    对外文委主任张奚若和交响乐团团长波恩格茨教授先后在宴会上讲话，热烈祝贺中德两国文化合作日益加强，祝贺两国人民牢不可破的友谊日益巩固和发展。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔应邀出席了宴会。中德友好协会会长竺可桢，对外文委副主任屈武，中国音乐家协会主席吕骥等首都音乐界著名人士，都出席宴会作陪。
    德累斯顿交响乐团这次在北京、西安、重庆、南京、上海等地访问演出了一个多月，先后为十五万中国听众作了卓越的演出。在今天的宴会上，中国文化界和音乐界的负责人士都不断举杯，热烈祝贺德国音乐家这次访问演出的成功，感谢他们给中国人民带来了难忘的艺术享受和德国人民的珍贵友谊。
