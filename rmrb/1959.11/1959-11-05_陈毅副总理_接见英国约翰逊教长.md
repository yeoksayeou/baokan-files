Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives British Dean Johnson
Author:
Date: 1959-11-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, November 4th. Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, who is currently inspecting work in Sichuan Province, received Dr. Johnson, a renowned British peace activist and Dean of Canterbury, and his wife in Chengdu this morning. They had a cordial and friendly conversation. Present at the meeting were Kang Nao'er, Vice Chairman of the Sichuan Provincial Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Ma Shitu, Secretary-General; Bishop Ding Guangxun, President of Nanjing Jinling Union Theological Seminary; Cai Fuchu, Bishop of the East Sichuan and West Sichuan Dioceses of the Chinese Holy Catholic Church; and Zhong Guoying, General Secretary of the Chengdu YWCA and Executive Committee Member of the Municipal Women's Federation.

After the meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi had lunch with Mr. and Mrs. Johnson.

Mr. and Mrs. Johnson arrived in Chengdu on October 30th. During their stay in Chengdu, they visited the Jinniu People's Commune in the suburbs of Chengdu, the Xinmin People's Commune in Xinfan County, and the Hongguang People's Commune in Pixian County. They also visited the Sichuan Provincial Arts and Crafts Exhibition Hall and toured the scenic spots and historical sites of Du Fu Thatched Cottage and Nanjiao Park.

Mr. and Mrs. Johnson have already left Chengdu by car for Chongqing tonight.

### 陈毅副总理  接见英国约翰逊教长

1959-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理
    接见英国约翰逊教长
    新华社成都4日电　正在四川省视察工作的国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，今天上午在成都接见了英国著名和平人士、英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊博士和他的夫人，进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会四川省分会副主席康乃尔、秘书长马识途，南京金陵协和神学院院长丁光训主教，中华圣公会东川、西川教区主教蔡复初，成都市基督教女青年会总干事、市妇联执行委员钟国英。
    接见后，陈毅副总理和约翰逊夫妇共进午餐。
    约翰逊夫妇是10月30日到达成都的。在成都期间，他们访问了成都市郊区金牛人民公社、新繁县新民人民公社、郫县红光人民公社、参观了四川省工艺美术展览馆，游览了杜甫草堂和南郊公园的名胜古迹。
    约翰逊夫妇已经在今晚乘车离开成都去重庆。
