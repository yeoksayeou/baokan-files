Title: Ambassador Parifti Points Out Brilliant Achievements in Albania's Construction
Author:
Date: 1959-11-22
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 21st — November 29th is the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Albania. Albanian Ambassador to China, Parifti, held a press conference this afternoon, introducing the brilliant achievements Albania has made in its socialist construction.
    At the press conference, Ambassador Parifti introduced that since the liberation of Albania fifteen years ago, due to the correct leadership of the Labor Party and the hard work of the people, Albania has today become an advanced agricultural-industrial country, and the people's material and cultural living standards have been continuously improved.
    Ambassador Parifti also emphatically talked about the profound friendship between the Albanian people and the people of the Soviet Union, China, and other socialist countries, and talked about Albania's consistently unwavering peaceful foreign policy.

### 帕里夫蒂大使在记者招待会上指出  阿尔巴尼亚建设成就辉煌

1959-11-22
第3版()
专栏：

    帕里夫蒂大使在记者招待会上指出
    阿尔巴尼亚建设成就辉煌
    新华社21日讯　11月29日是阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年纪念日，阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使帕里夫蒂今天下午举行记者招待会，介绍了阿尔巴尼亚在社会主义建设事业中取得的辉煌成就。
    帕里夫蒂大使在招待会上介绍了阿尔巴尼亚解放十五年以来，由于劳动党的正确领导和人民的努力劳动，今天已经变成了一个先进的农业—工业国，和人民的物质、文化生活水平不断提高的情况。
    帕里夫蒂大使还着重谈到了阿尔巴尼亚人民同苏联、中国以及其它社会主义国家人民之间的深厚友谊，谈到了阿尔巴尼亚始终坚持不渝的和平外交政策。
