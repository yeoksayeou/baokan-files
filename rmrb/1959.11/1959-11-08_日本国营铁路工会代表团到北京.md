Title: Japanese National Railway Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, November 6th: A ten-member delegation from the Japanese National Railway Union, led by Chairman Harugo Hori, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union.
    Xiao Hongqi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 日本国营铁路工会代表团到北京

1959-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    日本国营铁路工会代表团到北京
    新华社6日讯　日本国营铁路工会代表团一行十人，在团长堀春乡率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应中国铁路工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席萧洪启等。
