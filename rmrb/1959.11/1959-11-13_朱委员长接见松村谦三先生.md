Title: Chairman Zhu De Receives Mr. Kenzo Matsumura

Author:

Date: 1959-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - Chairman Zhu De received Mr. Kenzo Matsumura of Japan, along with his entourage Yutaro Takeyama, Ichitaro Ide, Yoshimi Furui, and Masakichi Tabayashi this morning. Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Su, and Lian Guan.

### 朱委员长接见松村谦三先生

1959-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    朱委员长接见松村谦三先生
    新华社12日讯　朱德委员长今天上午接见了日本松村谦三先生和他的随行人员竹山佑太郎、井出一太郎、古井喜实和田林政吉。接见时在座的有：廖承志、张苏、连贯。
