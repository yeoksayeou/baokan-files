Title: Rockefeller is a Big Missile Boss

Author:

Date: 1959-11-28
Page: 6

Foreign News Digest

    Rockefeller is a Big Missile Boss
    Editor's Note: The "Worker's Weekly" published an article on November 22 entitled "Candidate Rockefeller - Missile Profiteer." Excerpts are as follows:

    What are Governor Rockefeller's financial stakes in the Cold War?

    Oil contracts and missile contracts give us part of the answer. The Rockefeller empire receives millions of dollars in net profit each year from the goods it sells to the Department of Defense. The governor's family is one of the largest missile investors.

    Hydrogen bombs are carried by missiles. Governor Rockefeller is running for president on a hydrogen bomb platform. He is calling for a significant increase in the military budget, with an emphasis on missiles.

    The missile business is a new El Dorado for war profiteers. It did not occupy a prominent position in the budget before the 1950s. But it now costs billions of dollars a year. The Rockefeller brothers were among the first to rush in when this gold rush began.

    The Rockefeller family today holds shares in a dozen or more companies that are making a fortune on missiles. And these companies receive special preferential treatment in obtaining this Pentagon business, because all the doors of the government are open to the grandsons of the number one billionaire, John Rockefeller.

    Governor Rockefeller's family today profits from American Telecom's massive missile contracts. These contracts amount to hundreds of millions of dollars annually. As long as "Titan," "Nike-Zeus," "Nike-Hercules," "Rascal," or "Nike-Ajax" missiles are sold, money falls into Rockefeller's pockets.

    The Rockefellers, together with the Mellon Foundation, control the giant Westinghouse Corporation. Westinghouse is a major missile contracting company.

    Governor Rockefeller's family is also deeply involved in International Nickel. His brother, Lawrence, is a director of this company. Nickel is a big money-making project in missile contracts.

    The huge Martin Company of Baltimore (formerly Martin Bomber Company) has made a lot of money for the Rockefeller family. The first artificial satellite launched by the United States in December 1957 was manufactured by the Martin Company.

    The Rockefellers also reap huge missile profits from Olin Mathieson Chemical Corporation. This company produces missile fuel and missile parts, as well as many other materials needed by the Pentagon. Lawrence is a director on the company's board.

    Rockefeller's closest partner in this company is Executive Vice President William Foster. He is said to have stated that a "preemptive war" against the Soviet Union would be appropriate. Foster is said to believe that the United States should strike the Soviet Union first, rather than wait to be attacked.

    Thus, the question arises: if Governor Rockefeller enters the White House in January 1961, how will he handle those missiles?

### 洛克菲勒是导弹大老板

1959-11-28
第6版()
专栏：外论摘要

    洛克菲勒是导弹大老板
    编者按　11月22日的“工人周报”发表了题为“候选人洛克菲勒——导弹谋利者”的文章，摘译如下：
    冷战对洛克菲勒州长在金融上的利害关系是什么？
    石油合同和导弹合同给了我们部分的回答。洛克菲勒帝国每年从它出售给国防部的货物中得到数百万美元的纯利。这位州长的家族是最大的导弹投资者之一。
    氢弹是由导弹携带的。洛克菲勒州长正在以一个氢弹纲领来竞选总统。他要求大大增加军事预算，而且把重点放在导弹上。
    导弹企业是战争谋利者的一个新的黄金国。它在1950年代以前在预算中不占显著地位。但是，它现在一年要耗费几十亿美元。洛克菲勒兄弟是在这种采金狂潮开始时首先拥进去的几个。
    洛克菲勒家族今天在导弹上大赚其钱的一打或更多的公司中有股份。而这些公司在获得这种五角大楼的生意方面受到特殊的优待。因为政府所有的大门都是向第一名亿万富翁约翰·洛克菲勒的孙子们开着的。
    洛克菲勒州长的家族今天从美国电讯公司的大量导弹合同中获得利润。这些合同的金额每年达几亿美元。只要售出了“大力神式”、“奈克—宙斯式”、“奈克—赫尔克里斯式”、“恶棍式”或“奈克—阿贾克斯式”导弹，钱就落到了洛克菲勒的腰包中。
    洛克菲勒们同梅隆财团一起控制着巨大的威斯汀豪斯公司。威斯汀豪斯公司是一个重要的承包导弹的公司。
    洛克菲勒州长的家族也深入了国际镍公司。他的兄弟劳伦斯是这个公司的董事。镍在导弹合同方面是一个大赚钱的项目。
    巴尔的摩的巨大的马丁公司（前马丁轰炸机公司）为洛克菲勒家族赚了大量的钱。美国1957年12月发射的第一个人造卫星是马丁公司制造的。
    洛克菲勒们还从奥林马锡森化学公司获得巨大的导弹利润。这个公司是生产导弹燃料和导弹零件以及供五角大楼所需的许多其他材料的。劳伦斯是这个公司的董事会董事。
    洛克菲勒在这个公司最亲密的伙伴是执行副总经理威廉·福斯特。据说，他曾表示对苏联进行“先发制人的战争”是恰当的。据说福斯特认为，美国应该先打击苏联，而不要等着挨攻击。
    于是产生了这样的问题：如果洛克菲勒州长在1961年1月踏进白宫的话，他将怎样处置那些导弹。
