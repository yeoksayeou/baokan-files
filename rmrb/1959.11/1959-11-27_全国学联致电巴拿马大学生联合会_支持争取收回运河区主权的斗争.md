Title: All-China Students' Federation Sends a Telegram to the Panamanian Federation of University Students Supporting the Struggle to Recover Sovereignty over the Canal Zone

Author:
Date: 1959-11-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th November. The All-China Students’ Federation today sent a telegram to the Panamanian Federation of University Students, expressing firm support for the just struggle of the Panamanian people and students to recover sovereignty over the Panama Canal Zone from the United States.
  The telegram stated that the Latin American people's movement against U.S. imperialism and for the attainment and safeguarding of national independence is now surging to unprecedented heights, and the forces of world peace and democracy greatly outweigh the imperialist forces of war. With the support of peace-loving and righteous people and students all over the world, as long as they unite and persist in their struggle, the Panamanian people and students will surely achieve final victory.

### 全国学联致电巴拿马大学生联合会  支持争取收回运河区主权的斗争

1959-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    全国学联致电巴拿马大学生联合会
    支持争取收回运河区主权的斗争
    新华社26日讯　中华全国学生联合会今天致电巴拿马大学生联合会，表示坚决支持巴拿马人民和学生争取从美国手中收回巴拿马运河区主权的正义斗争。
  电文说，目前拉丁美洲人民反对美帝国主义、争取和维护民族独立运动空前高涨，世界和平、民主力量大大超过帝国主义战争力量。在全世界爱好和平、正义的人民和学生的支持下，只要团结一致，坚持斗争，巴拿马人民和学生就一定能取得最后胜利。
