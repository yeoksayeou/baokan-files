Title: Yugoslav Delegation Arrives in India
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-02
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 1st: The Yugoslav delegation, headed by Milentije Popović, a member of the Yugoslav Federal Executive Council, arrived in Delhi last night for a four-week visit to India, according to the Press Trust of India.

The delegation will have lunch with Nehru on the 2nd. They will also attend the annual meeting of the Indian People's Socialist Party.

### 南斯拉夫代表团到印度

1959-11-02
第6版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫代表团到印度
    新华社新德里1日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道，以南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会委员米伦蒂耶·波波维奇为首的南斯拉夫代表团一行三人昨晚到达德里。他们将在印度作为期四周的访问。
    代表团2日将同尼赫鲁共进午餐。他们还将参加印度人民社会党的年会。
