Title: Jeong Jong-yeo, Standing Committee Member of the Korean Fine Arts Artists Alliance, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th - Jeong Jong-yeo, Standing Committee Member of the Korean Fine Arts Artists Alliance, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon, in accordance with the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement, to assist in the preparation of the Korean Fine Arts Exhibition in our country.

### 朝鲜美术家同盟常委郑钟汝到京

1959-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜美术家同盟常委郑钟汝到京
    新华社29日讯　根据中朝文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国协助筹办朝鲜美术展览的朝鲜美术家同盟常务委员郑钟汝，今天下午乘火车到达北京。
