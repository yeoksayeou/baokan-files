Title: Lin Feng Receives Galdo
Author:
Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - Lin Feng, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received German Quiroga Galdo, former Speaker of the House of Representatives of Bolivia and current member of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the House of Representatives, this morning. The host and guest had a friendly conversation.

Present at the meeting were Zhang Su, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 林枫接见加尔多

1959-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    林枫接见加尔多
    新华社6日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫今天上午接见玻利维亚前众议院议长、现任众议院外交委员会委员赫尔曼·基罗加·加尔多，宾主进行了友好交谈。
    接见时在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员张苏、副秘书长连贯，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪，外交部美澳司司长申健。
