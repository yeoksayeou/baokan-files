Title: Chinese Photo Exhibition Opens in Colombo
Author:
Date: 1959-11-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, November 13th: A Chinese photo exhibition opened today at the Colombo Art Gallery. The opening ceremony was presided over by Ceylon's Minister of Health, Jaya Suriya.

At the meeting, Jaya Suriya said that he was very happy to see the great achievements made by China under the leadership of the new government in Ceylon, although these achievements were only displayed through pictures. He said that there is no doubt that this exhibition will help the people of Ceylon better understand China and help develop friendship between the two countries.

The President of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association and former Ambassador to China, Pereira, spoke at the opening ceremony. Chinese Ambassador Zhang Canming briefly described China's achievements in socialist construction over the past decade.

This exhibition was jointly sponsored by the Ceylon-China Friendship Association and the Chinese Embassy in Ceylon.

### 我图片展览会在科伦坡开幕

1959-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    我图片展览会在科伦坡开幕
    据新华社科伦坡13日电　中华人民共和国图片展览会今天在科伦坡艺术展览馆开幕。锡兰卫生部长贾亚苏里亚主持了开幕式。
    贾亚苏里亚在会上说，他很高兴在锡兰能看到中国在新政府领导下所取得的伟大成就，虽然这些成就只是通过图片显示出来的。他说，毫无疑问，这次展览会将帮助锡兰人民更好地了解中国，并且有助于发展两国的友谊。
    锡中友好协会主席、前驻华大使佩雷拉在开幕式上讲了话。中国大使张灿明简短地叙述了中国在过去十年社会主义建设中的成就。
    这个展览会是由锡中友好协会和中国驻锡兰大使馆联合举办的。
