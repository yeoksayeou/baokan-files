Title: First Tractor Manufacturing Plant's Engineering Quality is Excellent; National Acceptance Committee Approves Production Launch Today
Author:
Date: 1959-11-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Luoyang, 31st - The National Acceptance Committee inspected the First Tractor Manufacturing Plant this morning and held a signing ceremony for the acceptance certificate. The National Acceptance Committee announced that the First Tractor Manufacturing Plant has been completed, the engineering quality is excellent, and approved its official commissioning for production from November 1, 1959.
    The signing ceremony of the acceptance certificate was presided over by Wu Zhipu, Director of the National Acceptance Committee of the First Tractor Manufacturing Plant, First Secretary of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Governor of Henan Province. Participants in the signing ceremony included Li Yu, Deputy Director of the National Acceptance Committee of the First Tractor Manufacturing Plant and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agricultural Machinery; Bai Jian, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building; Liu Yumin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Construction Engineering; Chai Shufan, Deputy Director of the State Capital Construction Commission; Shao Ziyan, Chairman of the National Committee of the First Machinery Trade Union; and all members of the Acceptance Committee.
    Zhang Fengshi, member of the National Acceptance Committee and Director of the Sixth Bureau of the First Ministry of Machine Building, read out the acceptance certificate for the construction project of the First Tractor Manufacturing Plant on behalf of the National Acceptance Committee. Subsequently, the First Tractor Manufacturing Plant and all members of the National Acceptance Committee signed the acceptance certificate.

### 第一拖拉机制造厂工程质量优良  国家验收委员会批准今日投入生产

1959-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    第一拖拉机制造厂工程质量优良
    国家验收委员会批准今日投入生产
    据新华社洛阳31日电　国家验收委员会今天上午验收了第一拖拉机制造厂，并且举行了验收鉴定书签字仪式。国家验收委员会宣布：第一拖拉机制造厂已经建成，工程质量优良，批准自1959年11月1日起正式动用投入生产。
    验收鉴定书签字仪式，由第一拖拉机制造厂国家验收委员会主任、中共河南省委第一书记、河南省省长吴芝圃主持。参加签字仪式的，有第一拖拉机制造厂国家验收委员会副主任、农业机械部副部长黎玉，第一机械工业部副部长白坚，建筑工程部副部长刘裕民，国家基本建设委员会副主任柴树藩，第一机械工会全国委员会主席邵子言，以及验收委员会的全体委员。
    国家验收委员会委员、第一机械工业部第六局局长张逢时，代表国家验收委员会宣读了第一拖拉机制造厂建设工程验收鉴定书。接着，第一拖拉机制造厂和国家验收委员会全体委员，分别在验收鉴定书上签字。
