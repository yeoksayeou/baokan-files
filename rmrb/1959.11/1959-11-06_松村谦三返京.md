Title: Matsumura Kenzō Returns to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th – After a ten-day visit to Lanzhou, Xi'an, Sanmenxia, Luoyang and other places, Mr. Matsumura Kenzō of Japan and his entourage returned to Beijing by train this morning.

### 松村谦三返京

1959-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

    松村谦三返京
    新华社5日讯　日本松村谦三先生和他的随行人员，在兰州、西安、三门峡、洛阳等地进行了十天的参观访问后，于今天上午乘火车回到北京。
