Title: Water Conservancy and Fertilizer First
Author:
Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 3

　　Trees need strong roots to grow tall,
　　Houses need good foundations to be built,
　　If you want a bumper harvest next year,
　　Water conservancy and fertilizer come first.
