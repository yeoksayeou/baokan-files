Title: Marxism Classics Being Published in Mexico
Author:
Date: 1959-11-13
Page: 7

Academic and Cultural Trends

    Mexico is Publishing Marxist Classics

    The Mexican magazine "El Observador" reports that some Mexican publishers are publishing Marxist classics in Spanish, promoting Marxist works and other progressive books. Among them, Atlante Publishing House has published some of Lenin's works and is publishing Plekhanov's "Letters Without Address" and "Art and Social Life." Grijalbo Publishing House has published some works by Soviet authors on socio-economic issues, such as "History of the Soviet Union" (Socialist Section), "Historical Materialism" (F. Konstantinov), "Textbook of Political Economy," etc. These books are quite popular among Mexican readers. In addition, in Mexico, the famous work by American John Reed, "Ten Days That Shook the World," and the book about José Martí, "Martí, Writer of America," written by the Cuban progressive writer Juan Marinello, are also very popular.

### 墨西哥正在出版马克思主义经典著作

1959-11-13
第7版()
专栏：学术文化动态

    墨西哥正在出版马克思主义经典著作
    墨西哥“观察者”杂志报道，墨西哥的一些出版社正在用西班牙文出版马克思主义的经典著作，宣传马克思主义的著作以及其他进步书籍，其中，阿特兰特出版社已出版列宁的一些著作，正在出版普列汉诺夫的“没有地址的信札”和“艺术和社会生活”。格里哈尔波出版社出版了苏联作者关于社会经济问题的一些著作，如“苏联历史”（社会主义部分），“历史唯物主义”（弗·康斯坦丁诺夫），“政治经济学教科书”等。这些书籍颇受墨西哥读者的欢迎。此外，在墨西哥，美国约翰·李德的名著“震撼世界的十天”，古巴进步作家胡安·马里内略所写的关于何塞·马蒂的“马蒂，美洲的作家”也风行一时。
