Title: Chairman Liu, Premier Zhou, and Foreign Minister Chen Send Congratulatory Telegrams on Yemen Kingdom's National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd November: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram on the 1st to the King of Yemen, Imam Ahmad, congratulating him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Yemen. The text of the telegram is as follows:

    To His Majesty Imam Ahmad Ibn Yahya Hamidaddin Nasser Lidinillah, King of the Mutawakkilite Kingdom of Yemen:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Yemen, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of Yemen on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Kingdom of Yemen prosperity and its people happiness. I wish Your Majesty good health and longevity.
        Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                      November 1, 1959
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd November: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram on the 1st to Crown Prince Badr, Deputy Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Yemen, congratulating him on Yemen's National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

    To His Royal Highness Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, Crown Prince and Deputy Prime Minister of the Mutawakkilite Kingdom of Yemen:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Yemen, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Royal Highness and the government of the Kingdom of Yemen on behalf of the Chinese government and in my own name.
    In recent years, the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Yemen have developed significantly. This year, on the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the government of the Kingdom of Yemen sent a cultural delegation headed by His Excellency Sayyid Ali al-Muayyad to our country to participate in the tenth anniversary National Day celebrations and to pay a friendly visit. The Chinese government and people regard this as an expression by the government of the Kingdom of Yemen of its friendship towards the Chinese people.
    The Chinese government and people have always sympathized with and supported Yemen's just struggle against imperialist aggression and to defend national sovereignty and national independence, and firmly believe that Yemen's struggle will surely be victorious.
    Your Royal Highness, I sincerely wish you and the people of Yemen new achievements in building your own country, promoting the solidarity of Asian and African countries, and safeguarding world peace.
                      Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                            November 1, 1959

### 刘主席　周总理　陈外长电贺也门王国国庆

1959-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席　周总理　陈外长
    电贺也门王国国庆
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇1日打电报给也门国王伊马姆·艾哈迈德祝贺也门王国国庆。电文如下：也门穆塔瓦基利亚王国国王伊马姆·艾哈迈德·伊本·叶海亚·哈米德丁·纳赛尔·里迪尼拉陛下：
    值此也门王国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义向陛下和也门人民表示衷心的祝贺。祝也门王国繁荣，人民幸福。祝陛下健康长寿。
        中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                      1959年11月1日
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来1日打电报给也门王国副首相巴德尔王太子祝贺也门国庆。电文如下：
    也门穆塔瓦基利亚王国副首相塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔王太子殿下：
    值此也门王国国庆之际，我谨代表中国政府并且以我个人的名义向殿下和也门王国政府表示热烈的祝贺。
    近年来，中国和也门两国之间的友好合作关系有了显著的发展。今年，当中华人民共和国成立十周年的时候，也门王国政府派遣了以赛义德·阿里·穆安亚德阁下为首的文化代表团来我国参加十周年国庆大典和进行友好访问，中国政府和人民把这看作是也门王国政府对中国人民友好的表示。
    中国政府和人民一向同情和支持也门反对帝国主义侵略、保卫国家主权和民族独立的正义斗争，并且深信也门的斗争一定能够胜利。
    殿下，我衷心地祝你和也门人民在建设自己的国家、促进亚非各国的团结和维护世界和平的事业中，获得更新的成就。
                      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                            1959年11月1日
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅1日打电报给也门王国副首相兼外交大臣巴德尔王太子祝贺也门国庆。电文如下：
    也门穆塔瓦基利亚王国副首相兼外交大臣塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔王太子殿下：
    欣逢也门王国国庆，我谨向殿下表示热烈的祝贺。祝中国和也门两国之间的友好合作关系日益发展。祝贵国在维护亚洲和世界和平的崇高事业中作出新的贡献。
          中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长　陈毅
                                            1959年11月1日
