Title: Typhoon Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-11-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th - The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a typhoon warning at 7 p.m. on the 17th, stating:

    The typhoon in the Southwest Pacific was centered over the northeastern part of Luzon Island at 4 p.m. today, at approximately 18.1 degrees north latitude and 121.9 degrees east longitude, with a maximum wind force of 12 near the center. The center of the typhoon has now turned to a more northerly direction. In the future, it will turn to a northeasterly direction at a speed of 10-12 knots per hour. In the next 36 hours, the wind force in the Taiwan Strait, the eastern part of the South China Sea, the southern part of the East China Sea, and the central and southern coastal areas of Fujian will increase to level 8-10. The wind force in the eastern coastal areas of Guangdong and the nearby sea, the northern coastal areas of Fujian, the coastal areas of Zhejiang, the southern coastal areas of Jiangsu, the northwestern part of the East China Sea, and the southeastern part of the Yellow Sea will increase to level 6-9. After 36 hours, the typhoon will move towards Japan.

    In addition, cold air is moving southeastward from Inner Mongolia. In the next 24 hours, the Bohai Sea, the northern part of the Yellow Sea, and the Shandong Peninsula will experience southerly winds of level 5-6, which will turn into northerly winds of level 5-6.

### 台风警报

1959-11-18
第2版()
专栏：

    台风警报
    新华社17日讯　中央气象科学研究所17日下午七时发布台风警报说：
    西南太平洋上的台风，今天下午四时，中心位置在吕宋岛的东北部，就是北纬十八点一度、东经一百二十一点九度附近，中心附近最大风力有十二级。目前台风中心已转向偏北方向移动。未来将以每小时十——十二浬的速度转向东北方向移动。未来三十六小时内，台湾海峡、南海东部、东海南部、福建中部和南部沿海风力将加大到八——十级，广东东部沿海及附近海面、福建北部沿海、浙江沿海、江苏南部沿海、东海西北部、黄海东南部风力将加大到六——九级。三十六小时以后，台风将移向日本。
    另外，有冷空气自内蒙古向东南方移动。未来二十四小时内，渤海、黄海北部、山东半岛将有五、六级偏南风转为五、六级的偏北风。
