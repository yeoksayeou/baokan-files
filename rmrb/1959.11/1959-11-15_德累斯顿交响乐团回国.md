Title: Dresden Symphony Orchestra Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-11-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 14th. The Dresden Symphony Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic, led by Professor Bongartz, concluded its performance tour in our country and left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhu Ming, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Ministry of Culture, Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, and Yan Liangkun and Li Delun, conductors of the Central Philharmonic Orchestra, as well as many music workers and representatives from the music community in the capital.

Wandel, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and First Secretary Brie were also at the airport to see them off.

### 德累斯顿交响乐团回国

1959-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    德累斯顿交响乐团回国
    新华社14日讯　结束了在我国访问演出的德意志民主共和国德累斯顿交响乐团，在团长波恩格茨教授率领下，今晨乘飞机离开北京返国。
    前往机场欢送的，有对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武、文化部联络司副司长朱明、中国音乐家协会主席吕骥和中央乐团指挥严良坤、李德伦以及首都的许多音乐工作者和音乐界代表。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔和一等秘书布里也到机场送行。
