Title: Czechoslovak Symphony Orchestra Leaves China
Author:
Date: 1959-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou. The Czechoslovak Republic Symphony Orchestra, a group of one hundred and twenty-one people led by conductor Pavel, left China in two groups on the evening of the 25th and the 26th to visit other countries. During its visit to China, the Czech Symphony Orchestra held five symphony concerts and one violin and piano recital in the three major cities of Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou. This orchestra, with its more than one hundred outstanding musicians, captivated audiences with its unique style and high artistic charm at every performance.

### 捷克斯洛伐克交响乐团离开我国

1959-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克交响乐团离开我国
    据新华社广州电　捷克斯洛伐克共和国交响乐团一行一百二十一人，在团长巴维尔率领下，25日晚上和26日分两批离开我国到其他国家访问。捷克交响乐团在我国访问期间，曾先后在北京、上海、广州三大城市举行了五场交响乐演奏会和一场小提琴、钢琴独奏音乐会。这个拥有一百多位杰出音乐家的乐团，每次演出都以独特的风格和高度的艺术魅力倾倒了观众。
