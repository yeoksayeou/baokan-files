Title: Delegation from the Polish Council of Ministers' Small Industry Committee Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The five-member delegation from the Polish Council of Ministers' Small Industry Committee, led by Mieczysław Hoffman, Vice Chairman of the Polish Small Industry Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Wang Xinyuan, Vice Minister of Light Industry, Ma Yuzhang, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives, Xie Bangxuan, Deputy Director of the Beijing Municipal Light Industry Bureau, and others.

Dembnicki and Szymanski, counselors of the Polish Embassy in China, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 波兰部长会议小型工业委员会代表团抵京

1959-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰部长会议小型工业委员会代表团抵京
    新华社17日讯　波兰部长会议小型工业委员会代表团一行五人，在波兰小型工业委员会副主席米奇斯瓦夫·霍夫曼的率领下，已于今天下午乘飞机到北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有轻工业部副部长王新元，中华全国手工业合作总社副主任马豫章、北京市轻工业局副局长谢邦选等多人。
    波兰驻中国大使馆参赞邓布尼茨基和席曼斯基等也到机场欢迎。
