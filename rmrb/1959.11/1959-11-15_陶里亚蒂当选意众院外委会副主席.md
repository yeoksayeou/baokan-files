Title: Togliatti Elected Vice-Chairman of Italian Chamber of Deputies Foreign Affairs Committee
Author:
Date: 1959-11-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, November 14th: According to TASS, the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Italian Chamber of Deputies elected its vice-chairmen on the 13th. As a result of the vote, the General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, Togliatti, was elected.

### 陶里亚蒂当选意众院外委会副主席

1959-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    陶里亚蒂当选意众院外委会副主席
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社罗马讯：意大利众议院外交委员会13日选举这个委员会的副主席。投票结果，意大利共产党总书记陶里亚蒂当选。
