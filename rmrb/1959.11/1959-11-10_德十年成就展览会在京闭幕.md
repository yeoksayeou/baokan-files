Title: German Ten-Year Achievements Exhibition Closes in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The German Democratic Republic's Ten-Year Achievements Exhibition has closed in Beijing. The photo section of the exhibition will also tour Xi'an, Chongqing, Shanghai and other places.

During the month the exhibition was held in Beijing, it was visited by more than 75,000 people. Representatives from the National Conference of Heroes attended the exhibition. Factories and enterprises from many provinces and cities also sent representatives to visit.

Audiences were very interested in the content and artistic design of the exhibition, and believed that the exhibition in Beijing played a great role in deepening the understanding and friendship between the people of China and Germany. Many visitors stated in the guest book that they were overjoyed by the great achievements made by the fraternal people of the German Democratic Republic in the past ten years.

### 德十年成就展览会在京闭幕

1959-11-10
第5版()
专栏：

    德十年成就展览会在京闭幕
    新华社9日讯　德意志民主共和国十年成就展览会已经在北京闭幕。展出的图片部分，还将在西安、重庆、上海等地巡回展出。
    展览会在北京展出的一个月期间，观众达七万五千多人次。全国群英会的代表们参观了这个展览会。不少省、市的工厂企业也派了代表来参观。
    观众对展览会的内容和艺术设计，都感到很大兴趣，认为这个展览会在北京的展出，对于加深中德两国人民之间的了解和友谊起了很大的作用。许多观众在留言簿上表示，他们为兄弟的德意志民主共和国人民在十年间所取得的巨大成就而欢欣鼓舞。
