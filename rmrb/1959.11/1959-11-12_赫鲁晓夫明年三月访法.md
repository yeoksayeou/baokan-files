Title: Khrushchev to Visit France Next March
Author:
Date: 1959-11-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 11th: TASS Moscow, November 10th dispatch: According to an agreement reached between the Soviet Union and France, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, will visit France on March 15, 1960. Recently, French President de Gaulle and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, exchanged views on the visit. Both sides pointed out that the visit may have very important significance for both countries and for the cause of peace. Khrushchev expressed to President de Gaulle that he believed the meeting and exchange of views would help further improve Soviet-French relations and the overall international situation. For this reason, he believes that this visit to France is of great significance.

### 赫鲁晓夫明年三月访法

1959-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫明年三月访法
    据新华社莫斯科11日电　据塔斯社莫斯科10日讯：根据苏联和法国双方达成的协议，苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫将于1960年3月15日访问法国。最近法国总统戴高乐和苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫就这次访问的问题交换了意见，双方指出，这次访问可能对两国，对和平事业具有十分重大的意义。赫鲁晓夫向戴高乐总统表示，他相信这次会晤和交换意见将有助于苏法关系以及整个国际局势的进一步改善。正因为如此，他认为这次访问法国具有重要意义。
