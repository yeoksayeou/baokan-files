Title: Construction Speed of Large Blast Furnaces
Author:
Date: 1959-11-11
Page: 3

Column: Numbers and Facts

    The construction speed of large blast furnaces in our country is surpassing one another. This marks a significant improvement in the technical level and construction experience of our country's steel industry construction teams.

    In 1952, Anshan Iron and Steel's No. 8 blast furnace was completed in thirteen months.
    In 1953, Anshan Iron and Steel's No. 7 blast furnace was completed in eleven months.
    In 1954, Anshan Iron and Steel's No. 6 blast furnace was completed in seven months.
    In 1955, Anshan Iron and Steel's No. 5 blast furnace was completed in seven months.
    In 1957, Anshan Iron and Steel's No. 3 blast furnace was completed in six months.
    In 1958, Anshan Iron and Steel's No. 10 blast furnace was completed in four months.
    In 1959, Wuhan Iron and Steel's No. 2 blast furnace was completed in four months.
