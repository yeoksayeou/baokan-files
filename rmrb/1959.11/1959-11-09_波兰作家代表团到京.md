Title: Polish Writers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 8th - A Polish writers' delegation of three people, led by the head of the delegation, Stanisław Wigocki, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China under the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 波兰作家代表团到京

1959-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰作家代表团到京
    新华社8日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国访问的波兰作家代表团一行三人，在团长斯旦尼斯瓦夫·维果茨基率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
