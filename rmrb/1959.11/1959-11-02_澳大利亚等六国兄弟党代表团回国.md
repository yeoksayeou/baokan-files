Title: Delegation of Fraternal Parties from Six Countries, Including Australia, Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-02
Page: 5
 
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st November – The Australian Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Lance Louis Sharkey, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Australian Communist Party, the Canadian Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Nelson Clarke, Member of the National Executive Committee of the Canadian Communist Party, the Belgian Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade F. Vandenbranden, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party, the Uruguayan Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Enrique Rodríguez, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Uruguayan Communist Party, the Nepalese Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Keshar Jung Rayamajhi, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Nepalese Communist Party, and the Nicaraguan Socialist Party delegation, after participating in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting various parts of our country, left our country today on the same plane.
    Those who were at the airport to see them off included Peng Zhen, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the CPC and Secretary of the Central Secretariat, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Central Secretariat, and Liu Ningyi, Member of the Central Committee.

### 澳大利亚等六国兄弟党代表团回国

1959-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚等六国兄弟党代表团回国
    新华社1日讯　以澳大利亚共产党中央总书记兰斯·路易斯·夏基同志为首的澳大利亚共产党代表团，以加拿大共产党全国执行委员会委员纳尔逊·克拉克同志为首的加拿大共产党代表团，以比利时共产党中央政治局委员弗·范登布兰敦同志为首的比利时共产党代表团，以乌拉圭共产党中央书记处书记恩里克·罗德里格斯同志为首的乌拉圭共产党代表团，以尼泊尔共产党中央总书记克沙尔·忠格·腊伊马吉同志为首的尼泊尔共产党代表团，尼加拉瓜社会主义党代表团，在参加了中华人民共和国建国十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地参观访问之后，于今日同乘一架飞机离开我国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记彭真，中央书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员刘宁一。
