Title: Soviet Youth Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 13th: The Soviet National Youth Men's and Women's Basketball Teams, a group of twenty-nine tall and strong athletes led by the Secretary-General of the Soviet Basketball Association, Nikolai Beshkaryov, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by the Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Basketball Association, Mu Zuoyun, and members of the Chinese Youth Men's and Women's Basketball Teams.

The Soviet National Youth Men's and Women's Basketball Teams each consist of eleven male and female athletes, selected from the best players in this year's All-Union Games. The average height of the players is 1.935 meters for the men's team and 1.764 meters for the women's team.

The Chinese Youth Men's Basketball Team, which will play its first friendly match against the visiting team tomorrow night, has defeated the European runner-up Czechoslovakia, the fourth-place Hungary, and the fifth-place Bulgaria this year. The Chinese Youth Women's Basketball Team, which will also face the visiting team, has drawn twice with last year's European champion Bulgaria this year. It is expected that tomorrow night's friendly matches will be very intense.

The Soviet National Youth Men's and Women's Basketball Teams will hold nine friendly matches in Beijing, Tianjin, Nanjing, and Shanghai, totaling eighteen games.

### 苏联青年男女篮球队到京

1959-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联青年男女篮球队到京
    据新华社13日讯　队员身高体壮的苏联国家青年男、女篮球队一行二十九人，今天由苏联篮球协会秘书长尼古拉·别什卡廖夫率领乘飞机到达北京。他们在飞机场上受到了中国篮球协会副主席牟作云和中国青年男、女篮球队队员们的热情欢迎。
    苏联国家青年男、女篮球队中，各有男女运动员十一名，他们是由今年全苏运动会中选拔出来的好手。队员的平均身高，男子队是一米九十三点五，女子队一米七十六点四。
    明晚将和客队举行第一次友谊比赛的中国青年男子篮球队，今年以来曾经战胜过欧洲亚军捷克斯洛伐克队、第四名匈牙利队、第五名保加利亚队。迎战客队的中国青年女子篮球队，今年曾经两次同去年欧洲冠军保加利亚队打成平局。预料明晚的友谊比赛将十分激烈。
    苏联国家青年男、女篮球队这次将在北京、天津、南京、上海举行九次十八场友谊比赛。
