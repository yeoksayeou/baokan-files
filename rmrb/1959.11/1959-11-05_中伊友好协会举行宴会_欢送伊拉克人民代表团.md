Title: China-Iraq Friendship Association Holds Banquet to See Off Iraqi People's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-11-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The China-Iraq Friendship Association held a banquet tonight to bid farewell to the Iraqi Republic People's Delegation, which is about to return home.

Deputy Head of the Delegation and Prosecutor General of the Iraqi Ministry of Justice, Abdul Amir Ockley, and all members of the delegation attended the banquet.

At the banquet, Luo Jun, Vice President of the China-Iraq Friendship Association, warmly expressed his farewell to the distinguished guests and wished the friendship between the people of China and Iraq would be further consolidated and developed.

Deputy Head Ockley said at the banquet that the warm welcome we received in China proved that the people of Iraq and China have a sincere friendship. Imperialism has tried to undermine the friendship between the people of Iraq and China in the past and still does now, but they cannot succeed. No matter what methods they use, they can never cut off this road of friendship between the people of Iraq and China.

Among those present at the banquet were Bao Erhan, Director of the China Islamic Association, Dapusheng, Vice Director, Zhang Zhirang, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court, Ma Xiwu, Mao Yisheng, Vice Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology, Sa Kongliao, Director of the China-Iraq Friendship Association, and other people from all walks of life. Jalal Jaff, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Iraqi Embassy in China, and his wife were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 中伊友好协会举行宴会  欢送伊拉克人民代表团

1959-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    中伊友好协会举行宴会
    欢送伊拉克人民代表团
    据新华社4日讯　中国伊拉克友好协会今晚举行宴会，欢送即将回国的伊拉克共和国人民代表团。
    代表团副团长、伊拉克司法部检察长阿卜杜勒·阿密尔·奥克里及全体团员出席了宴会。
    在宴会上，中伊友好协会副会长罗俊向贵宾们热烈表示欢送惜别之意，并祝中伊两国人民的友谊进一步巩固和发展。
    奥克里副团长在宴会上说，我们在中国所受到的热烈欢迎，证明了伊中两国人民有着真诚的友谊。帝国主义过去和现在一直想破坏伊中两国人民的友谊，但是他们不可能成功，不管他们用什么方法，永远也不能切断伊中两国人民这条友谊的道路。
    出席今晚宴会作陪的有中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉、副主任达浦生，最高人民法院副院长张志让、马锡五，科学技术协会副主席茅以升，中伊友好协会理事萨空了等各界人士。应邀出席宴会的还有伊拉克驻华大使馆临时代办贾拉尔·贾夫及夫人。
