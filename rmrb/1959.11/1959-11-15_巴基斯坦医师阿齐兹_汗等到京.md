Title: Pakistani Physicians Aziz Khan and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 14th: Aziz Khan, President of the Pakistan Cancer Institute, surgeon, and pediatrician Hamid Ali Khan, along with a delegation of five people, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 13th at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association.

### 巴基斯坦医师阿齐兹·汗等到京

1959-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦医师阿齐兹·汗等到京
    新华社14日讯　应中华医学会邀请来我国访问的巴基斯坦癌症研究院院长、外科医师阿齐兹·汗和小儿科医师哈米德·阿里汗等一行五人，已经在13日下午到达北京。
