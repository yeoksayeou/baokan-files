Title: Our Table Tennis Team B Defeats England Again
Author:
Date: 1959-11-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, November 25th: The Chinese Table Tennis Team B defeated the English team again in Birmingham on the evening of the 24th with a score of five to three. This is the third victory for the young Chinese table tennis players in England.

Xinhua News Agency, London, November 26th: The Chinese Table Tennis Team B held its fifth match with the English team on the evening of the 25th, winning with a score of six to two. Chinese male players achieved complete victory in all five matches.

### 我乒乓球乙队  再胜英格兰队

1959-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    我乒乓球乙队  再胜英格兰队
    据新华社伦敦25日电　中国乒乓球队乙队24日晚上在伯明翰再次以五比三击败了英格兰队。这是中国青年乒乓球选手在英国的第三次胜利。
    据新华社伦敦26日电　中国乒乓球队乙队25日晚上同英格兰队举行第五场比赛，以六比二取得胜利。中国男子选手在五盘比赛中取得全部胜利。
