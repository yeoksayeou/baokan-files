International Briefs

    Italian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister to Visit Britain
    London dispatch: The British Foreign Office announced on the 2nd that Italian Prime Minister Antonio Segni and Foreign Minister Giuseppe Pella have accepted the invitation of British Prime Minister Macmillan to make a three-day official visit to Britain on December 1st. Diplomatic circles believe that this visit is part of their discussions before the Western heads of government conference.

    Kishi Government to Build Missile Base on New Island
    Tokyo dispatch: The Kishi Nobusuke government's "Defense Agency" has decided to build the first missile launch site on Niijima in Tokyo Bay to accelerate the implementation of nuclear armament.
    The "Defense Agency" has formally issued a notice to the local area to forcibly requisition 650,000 square meters of land. Local youth and women strongly oppose the Kishi Nobusuke government's plan.

### 国际简讯

1959-11-05
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    意总理及外长将访英
    伦敦讯　英国外交部2日宣布：意大利总理安托尼奥·塞尼和外交部长朱塞佩·贝拉接受了英国首相麦克米伦的邀请，将于12月1日到英国作三天正式访问。外交界人士认为，这次访问是西方政府首脑会议前他们之间的讨论的一部分。
    岸政府在新岛建导弹基地
    京东讯　岸信介政府的“防卫厅”已经决定要在东京湾的新岛建立第一个导弹发射场，来加速实行核武装。
    “防卫厅”已经正式向当地发出了强征六十五万平方米的土地的通知。当地青年和妇女强烈反对岸信介政府的计划。
