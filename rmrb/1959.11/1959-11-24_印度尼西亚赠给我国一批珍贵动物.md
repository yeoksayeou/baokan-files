Title: Indonesia Presents China with a Batch of Precious Animals
Author:
Date: 1959-11-24
Page: 4

    Indonesia Presents China with a Batch of Precious Animals

    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: A batch of rare animals presented to China by Indonesia, escorted by Cui Zhanping, director of the Beijing Zoo, arrived safely in Beijing on the 22nd. This batch of animals includes many precious animals exchanged between the Surabaya, Yogyakarta, and Bandung zoos and the Beijing Zoo, as well as an orangutan given to Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen by the Mayor of Jakarta. There are also precious birds and animals presented to the Beijing Zoo by the Indonesia-China Friendship Association and the Indonesian National Association.

### 印度尼西亚赠给我国一批珍贵动物

1959-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚赠给我国一批珍贵动物
    据新华社22日讯　印度尼西亚赠送给我国的一批稀有动物，由北京动物园主任崔占平携运，已于22日安全抵达北京。这批动物包括泗水、日惹、万隆等动物园同北京动物园交换的许多珍贵动物，以及雅加达市长送给北京市彭真市长的一头猩猩。还有印（尼）中友协、印度尼西亚国籍协商会赠送给北京动物园的珍贵鸟兽。
