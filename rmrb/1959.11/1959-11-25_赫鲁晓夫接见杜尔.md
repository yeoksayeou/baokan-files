Title: Khrushchev Receives Touré
Author:
Date: 1959-11-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Gagala (Caucasus), November 24th. TASS reported: On November 23rd, Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, received Sékou Touré, President of the Republic of Guinea, who is on an official visit to the Soviet Union, as well as Diallo Saifoulaye, Speaker of the Guinean National Assembly, Fodéba Keita, Minister of the Interior and Security, and Gounté Seydou, Ambassador of the Republic of Guinea to the Soviet Union, who accompanied him.

During the friendly and mutually understanding conversation, Khrushchev and Sékou Touré exchanged views on certain issues in the relations between the Soviet Union and Guinea, as well as current international issues.

Khrushchev asked the President to convey his warm greetings to the heroic people of Guinea, and wished them new and great achievements in the noble struggle to consolidate the independence of the young Republic of Guinea.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见杜尔

1959-11-25
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见杜尔
    新华社莫斯科24日电　据塔斯社加格拉（高加索）23日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫23日接见了正在苏联作正式访问的几内亚共和国政府总统塞古·杜尔以及陪同他的几内亚国民议会议长迪阿洛·塞福拉耶、内政和公安部长福代巴·凯塔和几内亚共和国驻苏大使贡特·赛杜。
    在友好和互相谅解的谈话中，赫鲁晓夫和塞古·杜尔就苏联和几内亚两国关系的某些问题以及当前国际问题交换了意见。
    赫鲁晓夫请总统向英勇的几内亚人民转达他的热烈的问候，并祝在争取巩固年轻的几内亚共和国的独立的崇高斗争中取得新的巨大成就。
