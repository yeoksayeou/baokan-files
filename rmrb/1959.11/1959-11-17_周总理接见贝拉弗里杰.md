Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Ahmed Balafrej
Author:
Date: 1959-11-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th November. Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with former Moroccan Prime Minister Ahmed Balafrej and the former Director of the Prime Minister's Office, Rahalki.

Present at the meeting were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见贝拉弗里杰

1959-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见贝拉弗里杰
    新华社16日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了摩洛哥前首相贝拉弗里杰和前首相办公室主任拉哈基。
    接见时在座的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷，中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达等人。
