Title: The Soviet Union Sends Notes to Various Countries, Forwarding the Appeal to the Parliaments of All Countries Regarding Disarmament

Author:

Date: 1959-11-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 5th, TASS: The Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs handed to the embassies and legations of various countries in Moscow a note, to which was attached the appeal to the parliaments of all countries of the world adopted by the recent Third Session of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union on the issue of complete and thorough disarmament.

The note requested them to forward the appeal of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to the parliaments of their respective countries. At the same time, the Permanent Mission of the Soviet Union to the United Nations also distributed the same document to the representatives of those countries that do not have embassies and legations in Moscow.

### 苏联向各国发出照会  转交关于裁军的告各国议会书

1959-11-09
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联向各国发出照会
    转交关于裁军的告各国议会书
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社莫斯科5日讯：苏联外交部交给各国驻莫斯科大使馆和公使馆一份照会，照会中附有最近举行的苏联最高苏维埃第三次会议就全面和彻底裁军问题通过的告世界各国议会书。
    照会要求它们把苏联最高苏维埃的告世界各国议会书转交各该国的议会。同时，苏联常驻联合国的代表处也把同样的文件散发给了那些在莫斯科没有大使馆和公使馆的国家的代表。
