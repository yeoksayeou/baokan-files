Title: Vietnamese Labor Party Organ and "Democratic Korea" Support Premier Zhou Enlai's Peaceful Proposals, Condemn Imperialism for Spreading Rumors and Sowing Discord in Sino-Indian Relations
Author:
Date: 1959-11-27
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, November 26th, Hanoi: The Vietnamese Labor Party's official newspaper published a commentary on the 23rd, supporting Premier Zhou Enlai's proposals in his letter to Prime Minister Nehru on November 7th, and expressed a sincere hope that the Chinese and Indian governments would conduct negotiations on the Sino-Indian border issue as soon as possible.

    The commentary strongly condemned imperialism for its efforts to slander China, distort China's correct propositions and policies, and spread rumors to sow discord in Sino-Indian relations on the Sino-Indian border issue.

    The commentary emphasized that the imperialist conspiracy to undermine Sino-Indian friendly relations is inseparable from their conspiracy to undermine the Asian people's movement to unite and fight for independence and peace and to poison the improved international atmosphere.

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 26th: "Democratic Korea" published a commentary on the 25th, demanding that the Indian government adopt a positive attitude towards the proposals put forward and the sincere efforts made by the Chinese government for a peaceful resolution of the Sino-Indian border issue, and to bravely smash the imperialists' aggressive conspiracy to exploit the Sino-Indian border dispute.

    The commentary said that Premier Zhou Enlai's letter to Prime Minister Nehru on November 7th regarding the Sino-Indian border issue has received the support of people all over the world, including the Korean people. The proposals put forward by Premier Zhou Enlai in his letter embody the peaceful diplomatic policy consistently implemented since the founding of the People's Republic of China. It is another expression of efforts to create a peaceful and stable atmosphere in the Sino-Indian border region and has become a reasonable and practical solution to resolve the current Sino-Indian border dispute.

    The commentary pointed out that the Sino-Indian border incident was caused by Indian acts of aggression. The commentary said that the enemies of peace are viciously carrying out conspiracies, using the Sino-Indian border dispute to slander the People's Republic of China, while undermining the friendly relations between China and India.

    The commentary said that all disputes between the two friendly countries of China and India can be resolved smoothly through talks.

### 越南劳动党机关报和“民主朝鲜报”  支持周恩来总理的和平建议  谴责帝国主义制造谣言挑拨中印关系

1959-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党机关报和“民主朝鲜报”
    支持周恩来总理的和平建议
    谴责帝国主义制造谣言挑拨中印关系
    新华社26日讯　河内消息：越南劳动党机关报23日发表评论，支持周恩来总理11月7日给尼赫鲁总理信中所提出的建议，并且表示真诚地希望中印两国政府早日进行关于中印边界问题的谈判。
    评论强烈谴责帝国主义在中印边界问题上极力污蔑中国、歪曲中国正确的主张和政策，并且制造谣言挑拨中印关系。
    评论强调指出，帝国主义破坏中印友好关系的阴谋是同它们破坏亚洲人民团结起来为独立与和平而斗争的运动和毒化已经得到改善的国际气氛的阴谋分不开的。
    新华社平壤26日电　“民主朝鲜报”25日发表评论，要求印度政府以积极的态度对待中国政府为和平解决中印边界问题而提出的建议和进行的有诚意的努力，并且勇敢地粉碎帝国主义者企图利用中印边界争端的侵略阴谋。
    评论说，周恩来总理在11月7日就中印边界问题给尼赫鲁总理的信件受到了包括朝鲜人民在内的全世界人民的支持。周恩来总理在信中所提出的建议，体现了中华人民共和国成立以来一贯执行的和平外交政策，是为了在中印边界地区造成和平安定的气氛进行努力的又一表示，并且成为解决当前中印边界争端的合理的切实的方案。
    评论指出中印边界事件是由于印度方面的侵犯行为所引起的。评论说，和平的敌人正在恶毒地进行阴谋活动，利用中印边界争端来中伤中华人民共和国，同时破坏中印两国的友好关系。
    评论说，中印两个友好国家之间的一切争端没有不能通过会谈来顺利解决的。
