Title: In Response to Invitation to Attend the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Congress: Chinese Communist Party Delegation Leaves for Budapest
Author:
Date: 1959-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th - In response to an invitation to attend the Seventh Congress of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, the head of the Chinese Communist Party delegation, Tan Zhenlin, along with delegation member Wang Congwu and five accompanying staff, left Beijing by plane today for Budapest. Another delegation member, Hao Deqing, is already in Hungary.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee, and others.

Szigeti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and Bernok, Counselor, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 应邀参加匈社会主义工人党代表大会  中共代表团前往布达佩斯

1959-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加匈社会主义工人党代表大会
    中共代表团前往布达佩斯
    新华社26日讯　应邀参加匈牙利社会主义工人党第七次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长谭震林，团员王从吾及随员共五人，今天乘飞机离开北京前往布达佩斯。另一团员郝德青已经在匈牙利。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真，中央委员伍修权等。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂、参赞白诺克等也到机场欢送。
