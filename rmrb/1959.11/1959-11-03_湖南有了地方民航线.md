Title: Hunan Opens Local Civil Aviation Route
Author:
Date: 1959-11-03
Page: 3
Hunan Province's first local civil aviation route—the Changsha-Shaoyang line—was officially opened to air traffic recently. Currently, An-2 type passenger and cargo planes are operating on this route.

### 湖南有了地方民航线

1959-11-03
第3版()
专栏：

    湖南有了地方民航线
    湖南省第一条地方民用航空线——长（沙）邵（阳）线，在最近正式通航。目前在这条航线上航行的是安二型客货机。
