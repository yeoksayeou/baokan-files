Title: Chairman Zhu De Receives Quiroga
Author:
Date: 1959-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th - Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Germán Quiroga Galdo, former Speaker of the House of Representatives of Bolivia and current member of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the House of Representatives, who is currently visiting our country, this afternoon. The host and guest had a friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Zhang Su, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 朱德委员长接见基罗加

1959-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德委员长接见基罗加
    新华社28日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，今天下午接见正在我国访问的玻利维亚前众议院议长、现任众议院外交委员会委员赫尔曼·基罗加·加尔多，宾主进行了友好谈话。
    接见时在座的，有全国人民代表大会常委会委员张苏、副秘书长连贯，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪，外交部美澳司司长申健。
