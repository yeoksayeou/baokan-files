Title: Zhang Zhixiang to Visit Bulgaria
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, left by plane on the 13th to visit Bulgaria at the invitation of Mrs. Avramova, Chairman of the Committee for Friendly and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and to negotiate the 1960 Executive Plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 张致祥去保加利亚访问

1959-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    张致祥去保加利亚访问
    新华社13日讯　对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，应保加利亚人民共和国对外友好和文化联络委员会主席阿弗拉莫娃的邀请，于13日乘飞机赴保加利亚访问并商订中保文化合作协定1960年执行计划。
