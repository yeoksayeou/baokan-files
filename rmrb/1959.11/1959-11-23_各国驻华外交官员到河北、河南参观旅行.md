Title: Diplomatic Officials from Various Countries Stationed in China Visit Hebei and Henan

Author:

Date: 1959-11-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. From the 12th to the 21st of this month, ambassadors, chargés d'affaires, other diplomatic officials and their wives, stationed in China, visited Hebei and Henan provinces accompanied by Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. They visited people's communes and various factories in Shijiazhuang, Zhengzhou, Luoyang, Sanmenxia and other places; visited the Yellow River water conservancy project at Sanmenxia and the Gangnan Reservoir near Shijiazhuang; and toured famous historical sites such as Longmen, Guanlin, and the White Horse Temple in Luoyang.

Through this visit, the diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China have gained a further understanding of the situation of China's socialist construction, especially the new leap forward situation in all aspects since the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The ambassadors, diplomatic officials and their wives who participated in the visit returned to Beijing on the 21st. They were warmly received by the responsible persons of the Party and government of the relevant provinces and cities during their visits.

### 各国驻华外交官员到河北、河南参观旅行

1959-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    各国驻华外交官员到河北、河南参观旅行
    新华社22日讯　本月12日到21日，各国驻华使节、代办、其他外交官和他们的夫人们，在我外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文等人的陪同下，到河北、河南两省参观旅行。他们在石家庄、郑州、洛阳、三门峡等地，参观了人民公社和各种工厂；参观了三门峡的黄河水利枢纽工程和石家庄附近的岗南水库；游览了洛阳龙门、关林、白马寺等名胜古迹。
    各国驻华外交官员们经过这次参观旅行，对我国社会主义建设的情况，特别是中国共产党八届八中全会以后在各方面出现的新的跃进形势，有了进一步的了解。参观旅行的各国使节、外交官和他们的夫人们已于21日返抵北京。他们在各地参观游览时，受到了有关省市党、政负责人的热情接待。
