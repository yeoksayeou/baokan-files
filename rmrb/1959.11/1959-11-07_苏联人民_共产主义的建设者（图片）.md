Title: Soviet People—Builders of Communism (Pictures)
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-07
Page: 6

    Soviet People—Builders of Communism
    1. The large steel plant "Kazakhstan's Magnitogorsk," under construction in the Kazakhstan steppe. This plant is an important component of the Soviet Union's third steel base.
    2. In the Gorky Collective Farm in the Odessa Oblast, the team led by Brazovsky (right), who has twice been awarded the title of Hero of Socialist Labor, is harvesting corn. The corn they cultivate can yield more than 60 quintals of corn grain per hectare.
    3. The work team led by Hero of Socialist Labor Gaganova.
    4. The builders of the Bratsk Hydroelectric Power Station are competing to complete the construction project two years ahead of schedule. The Communist Labor Brigade led by Berezyteko (first from the right) is completing two quotas per day in the competition.
    5. The "Pavlodar" State Farm in the Pavlodar Oblast has raised over 100,000 Peking ducks this year and plans to increase the number to one million by 1961.

### 苏联人民—共产主义的建设者（图片）

1959-11-07
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联人民—共产主义的建设者
    1、在哈萨克斯坦草原上兴建中的大钢铁厂“哈萨克斯坦的马格尼托哥尔斯克”。这个厂是苏联第三钢铁基地的重要组成部分。
    2、敖德萨州高尔基集体农庄以两次获得社会主义劳动英雄称号的布拉日夫斯基（右）为首的工作队正在收割玉蜀黍，他们培植的玉蜀黍每公顷可收六十余公担玉蜀黍粒。
    3、社会主义劳动英雄加干诺娃所领导的工作队。
    4、布拉茨克水电站的建设者们正在为提前两年完成建设工程展开竞赛，以别列捷科（右起第一人）为首的共产主义劳动队在竞赛中每天完成两个定额。
    5、帕夫罗达尔州“帕夫罗达尔”国营农场今年饲养了十几万只北京鸭，计划到1961年发展到一百万只。
