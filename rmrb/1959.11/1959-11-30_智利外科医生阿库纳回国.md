Title: Chilean Surgeon Acuña Returns Home

Author:

Date: 1959-11-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Justo Ulloa Acuña, a Chilean surgeon who visited our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, returned home by plane this morning. He arrived in our country on October 27 and visited Beijing, Hefei, Shanghai, Wuxi, Wuhan and other places.

### 智利外科医生阿库纳回国

1959-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    智利外科医生阿库纳回国
    新华社29日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的智利外科医生胡斯多·乌利奥阿·阿库纳，今天上午乘飞机回国。他是在10月27日来到我国的，先后访问了北京、合肥、上海、无锡、武汉等地。
