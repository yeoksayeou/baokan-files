Title: President of the Indian-Soviet Cultural Society, Baliga, and his wife return home

Author: 
Date: 1959-11-11
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Baliga, President of the Indian-Soviet Cultural Society of India, and his wife, left Beijing by plane today to return home.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included the heads of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the Chinese Medical Association, as well as Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association.

### 印苏文协主席巴利加夫妇回国

1959-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    印苏文协主席巴利加夫妇回国
    新华社10日讯　印度印苏文化协会主席巴利加和他的夫人，今日乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中国人民外交学会和中华医学会负责人以及中印友好协会会长丁西林等。
