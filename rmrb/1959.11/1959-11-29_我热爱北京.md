Title: I Love Beijing
Author: Alek Çaçi
Date: 1959-11-29
Page: 8

Dirana—Beijing

    I Love Beijing
   [Albanian Poet] Alek Çaçi
    I love Beijing.
    In the blink of an eye,
    How much it has changed!
    What did I see yesterday,
    What do I see today?
    Where have the low houses gone?
    Where has the impoverished life gone?
    Palaces have risen from the ground,
    Even people's eyes have changed.
    The Summer Palace secretly hides in a corner,
    As if still doubting
    These changes...
    How could the emperor have imagined
    The people striding forward, breaking through everything.
    I stroll on the wide streets,
    People dressed neatly
    Know where they are going,
    Therefore their steps are firm...
    Molten steel flows like a great river.
    How clean it is here,
    Look!
    Chrysanthemums are blooming everywhere!
    Beijing speaks,
    All of China is building.
    Beijing speaks,
    All of Asia is listening.
    Tirana is so happy too!
    I love Beijing
    How warm it is here!
    The heart of New China is beating,
    I see the future from here.
            [Translated by Li Ninglai]

### 我热爱北京
阿列克斯·恰奇
1959-11-29
第8版()
专栏：地拉那—北京

    我热爱北京
   〔阿尔巴尼亚诗人〕阿列克斯·恰奇
    我热爱北京。
    转瞬之间
    它有多么大的变化！
    昨天我看到了什么，
    今天我又看到了什么？
    低矮的房屋哪里去了？
    贫困的生活哪里去了？
    平地升起了一座座的宫殿，
    连人们的目光也都已经改变。
    颐和园偷偷躲在角落里，
    好像还在怀疑
    这些变化……
    皇帝怎么能想到
    人民大踏步地冲破了一切。
    我漫步在宽阔的街道上，
    穿着整洁的人们
    了解他们要到哪里去，
    因此步伐坚定。……
    钢水像大江似地滚流。
    这里是多么清洁，
    看吧！
    到处开遍了菊花！
    北京发言了，
    全中国正在建设。
    北京发言了，
    全亚洲都在倾听。
    地拉那也是那样高兴！
    我热爱北京
    这里多么温暖！
    新中国的心脏在跳动，
    我从这里看到了未来。
            〔李宁来译〕
