Title: Soviet Ice Hockey Team Wins Big Against US Champion Team
Author:
Date: 1959-11-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th November: According to TASS, the Soviet ice hockey champion team – the Central Sports Club of the Ministry of Defence – overwhelmingly defeated the US champion team – the Brockton team – by twelve goals to three in a match in Moscow on the 15th. The scores for the three periods were three to two, six to one, and three to zero. Fifteen thousand spectators watched the game at the Lenin Central Stadium.

Within the first five minutes of the game, the Soviet team lost two goals in a row. However, the well-trained Soviet ice hockey players quickly counterattacked and scored three consecutive goals, leaving the American team unable to parry.

### 苏联冰球队大胜美国冠军队

1959-11-17
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联冰球队大胜美国冠军队
    新华社莫斯科16日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联冰球冠军队——国防部中央体育俱乐部代表队，15日在莫斯科的一场比赛中，以十二比三的绝对优势战胜美国冠军队——布罗克顿队。三节的比分是三比二，六比一和三比零。一万五千名观众在列宁中央运动场上观看了这场比赛。
    在球赛开始后的五分钟内，苏联队连失两球。但是，训练有素的苏联冰球队员快速反攻，连续打进了三球，使美国队无法招架。
