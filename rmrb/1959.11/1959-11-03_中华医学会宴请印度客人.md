Title: Chinese Medical Association Hosts Banquet for Indian Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-11-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Vice President of the Chinese Medical Association, Cui Yitian, held a reception tonight to welcome Dr. Baliga, Chairman of the India-Soviet Cultural Association, and his wife, who are visiting China.

More than 40 well-known figures from the Chinese medical community attended the reception.

### 中华医学会宴请印度客人

1959-11-03
第6版()
专栏：

    中华医学会宴请印度客人
    新华社2日讯　中华医学会副会长崔义田今晚举行酒会，欢迎来我国访问的印度印苏文化协会主席巴利加医生和他的夫人。
    出席酒会的有中国医学界知名人士四十多人。
