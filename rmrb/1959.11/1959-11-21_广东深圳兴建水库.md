Title: Guangdong Shenzhen to Build Reservoir
Author:
Date: 1959-11-21
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency report: The Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Guangdong Provincial People's Council have decided that the state will invest in the construction of a reservoir in Shenzhen, Bao'an. This reservoir project officially commenced on the 15th.

This reservoir is a comprehensive project that integrates water supply, irrigation, power generation, and flood control. After the reservoir is completed, it will not only supply water to residents and industries in Shenzhen and play a significant role in flood control, but also, when necessary, provide water to people in the Shenzhen area to the south, who frequently experience water shortages, including residents of Hong Kong and Kowloon.

### 广东深圳兴建水库

1959-11-21
第2版()
专栏：

    广东深圳兴建水库
    新华社讯　中共广东省委和广东省人民委员会决定由国家投资在宝安深圳兴建一座水库。这个水库工程已在15日正式动工。
    这个水库是供水、灌溉、发电、防洪综合利用的工程。水库完工以后，不仅可供应深圳居民和工业用水，对防洪也将起重大作用，并且必要时可供应经常缺乏用水的深圳以南地区的人民，包括香港九龙地区的居民在内。
