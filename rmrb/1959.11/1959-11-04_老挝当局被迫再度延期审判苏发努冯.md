Title: Lao Authorities Forced to Postpone Trial of Souphanouvong Again
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 3rd: Vientiane dispatch: The Lao Ministry of Justice announced on the 2nd that the trial of Prince Souphanouvong and other leaders of the Lao Patriotic Front, originally scheduled for November 2nd, has been postponed for two to four weeks.

### 老挝当局被迫再度延期审判苏发努冯

1959-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝当局被迫再度延期审判苏发努冯
    据新华社河内3日电　万象消息：老挝司法部在2日宣布，原定在11月2日举行的对苏发努冯亲王和老挝爱国战线党其他领导人的审判延期两个星期到四个星期。
