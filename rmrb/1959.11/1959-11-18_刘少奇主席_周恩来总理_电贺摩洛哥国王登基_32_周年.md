Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai Send Telegrams to Congratulate the King of Morocco on the 32nd Anniversary of His Accession to the Throne
Author:
Date: 1959-11-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram today to the King of Morocco, Mohammed V, congratulating him on the thirty-second anniversary of his accession to the throne. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Majesty Mohammed V, King of the Kingdom of Morocco, Rabat:
	On the occasion of the thirty-second anniversary of Your Majesty's accession to the throne, I, on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, extend warm congratulations and best wishes to Your Majesty and the Moroccan people. I wish your people happiness and Your Majesty good health.
				Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
						November 17, 1959

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram today to the Prime Minister of Morocco, Abdallah Ibrahim, congratulating King Mohammed V of Morocco on the thirty-second anniversary of his accession to the throne. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency Abdallah Ibrahim, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Morocco, Rabat:
	On the occasion of the thirty-second anniversary of the accession of His Majesty King Mohammed V of Morocco to the throne, I, on behalf of the Chinese government and in my own name, extend sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the government of your country.
	In the past year, with the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Morocco, the friendly relations between our two countries have achieved new development. I wish the profound friendship between the people of China and Morocco will be continuously enhanced and strengthened. I wish your government and the Moroccan people greater achievements in the cause of safeguarding national independence, promoting the solidarity of Asian and African countries, and promoting world peace.
			Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
					November 17, 1959

### 刘少奇主席　周恩来总理  电贺摩洛哥国王登基32周年

1959-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席　周恩来总理
    电贺摩洛哥国王登基32周年
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给摩洛哥国王穆罕默德五世祝贺他登基三十二周年。电文如下：拉巴特摩洛哥王国国王穆罕默德五世陛下：
    欣逢陛下登基三十二周年纪念日，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向陛下和摩洛哥人民表示热烈的祝贺和最良好的祝愿。祝贵国人民幸福，祝陛下身体健康。
                  中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                          一九五九年十一月十七日
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给摩洛哥首相阿卜杜拉·易卜拉欣，祝贺摩洛哥国王穆罕默德五世登基三十二周年。电文如下：拉巴特摩洛哥王国首相阿卜杜拉·易卜拉欣阁下：
    在摩洛哥国王穆罕默德五世陛下登基三十二周年纪念日的时候，我谨代表中国政府，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和贵国政府致以热诚的祝贺。
    一年来，随着中摩两国外交关系的建立，我们两国间的友好关系得到了新的发展。祝中摩两国人民的深厚友谊日益增进和加强。祝贵国政府和摩洛哥人民在维护民族独立、促进亚非各国团结和世界和平的事业中获得更大的成就。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                        一九五九年十一月十七日
