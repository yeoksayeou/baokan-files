Title: Soviet Leninist Komsomol Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 19th: The Soviet Leninist Young Communist League delegation of ten people, led by Chairman Murtazaev, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League for a visit to our country.

Those who greeted the delegation at the airport included Liu Xiyuan and Liang Buting, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, Wang Zhaohua, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and more than fifty representatives of youth from all walks of life.

In the evening, the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League hosted a banquet to welcome the delegation.

### 苏联列宁共青团代表团到京

1959-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联列宁共青团代表团到京
    新华社19日讯　应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会邀请来我国访问的苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团一行十人，在团长穆尔塔扎耶夫率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国共产主义青年团中央书记处书记刘西元、梁步庭，共青团北京市委第一书记王照华及各界青年代表五十多人。
    晚上，中国共产主义青年团中央书记处设宴招待代表团。
