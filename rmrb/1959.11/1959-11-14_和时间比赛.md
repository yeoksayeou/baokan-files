Title: Racing Against Time
Author: Li Xingwen
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 3

    The leaping call to race against time has shaken the coal sea, and production records are like ripples on the water. This one has just spread, and that one closely follows. The miners are racing against time, making the clock angry enough to stop!
              Li Xingwen
