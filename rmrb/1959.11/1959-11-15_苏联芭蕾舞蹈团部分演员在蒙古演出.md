Title: Soviet Ballet Troupe Members Perform in Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1959-11-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 13th: A group of artists from the Soviet National Grand Theatre Ballet Troupe, led by Lebedinskaya, gave their first performance in Mongolia at the Mongolian National Theatre this evening, receiving a warm welcome from the audience.

Mongolian Party and state leaders Tsedenbal, Sambuu, Tsend and others, as well as foreign diplomatic envoys, watched the performance.

The artists performed more than twenty numbers, including a Chinese folk dance. The Soviet artists arrived in Ulaanbaatar on November 12th at the invitation of Mongolia, following a month-and-a-half-long performance tour in China. They will stay here until November 20th.

### 苏联芭蕾舞蹈团部分演员在蒙古演出

1959-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联芭蕾舞蹈团部分演员在蒙古演出
    新华社乌兰巴托13日电　以列别辛斯卡娅为首的苏联国家大剧院芭蕾舞蹈团的一部分艺术家，今天晚上在蒙古国家剧院举行首次访问演出，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    蒙古党和国家领导人泽登巴尔、桑布、曾德等人和各国外交使节观看了演出。
    艺术家们演出了二十多个节目，他们还演出了一个中国民间舞蹈。苏联艺术家们是在中国进行了为时一个半月的访问演出后，应邀在12日到达乌兰巴托演出的。他们将在这里逗留到11月20日。
