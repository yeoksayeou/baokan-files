Title: U.S. Satellite Container Disappears Again
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-27
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, November 26th: The U.S. Air Force launched the "Discoverer VIII" satellite on the 20th, which orbits the Earth's poles, and again attempted to send a container from the satellite back to the ground from outer space. However, as with previous attempts, the container fell into the vast expanse of space and its whereabouts are unknown.
    The U.S. Air Force originally planned to have the container return to Earth on the satellite's seventeenth orbit, but this attempt was unsuccessful. Since the U.S. launched the "Discoverer" satellite, this is the sixth experiment of this nature and the sixth failure.

### 美国卫星容器又失踪

1959-11-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美国卫星容器又失踪
    据新华社26日讯　华盛顿消息：美国空军在20日发射环绕地球两极运行的人造卫星“发现者八号”，又企图把卫星上的一个容器从宇宙空间送回地面，但是同前几次一样，这个容器掉在茫茫的太空中不知去向。
    美国空军原计划在卫星环绕地球运行第十七圈时让容器回到地面，但是这个企图没有成功。自美国发射“发现者”卫星以来，这是第六次作这样性质的试验，也是第六次失败。
