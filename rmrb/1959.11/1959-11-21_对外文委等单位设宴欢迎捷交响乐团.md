Title: Foreign Cultural Commission and Other Units Host Banquet Welcoming Czech Symphony Orchestra
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-21
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 20th - The Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Ministry of Culture, and the Sino-Czech Friendship Association held a banquet this evening to warmly welcome all the musicians of the Czech Symphony Orchestra of the Czechoslovak Republic on their visit to China for performances.
    Qu Wu, Vice-Chairman of the Foreign Cultural Commission, and Pavel, head of the orchestra, spoke warmly at the banquet, congratulating the increasing development and strengthening of cultural and artistic exchanges and the friendship between the people of China and Czechoslovakia.

### 对外文委等单位设宴欢迎捷交响乐团

1959-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    对外文委等单位设宴欢迎捷交响乐团
    据新华社20日讯　对外文化联络委员会、文化部和中捷友好协会今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎捷克斯洛伐克共和国捷克交响乐团全体音乐家到中国访问演出。
    对外文委副主任屈武和乐团团长巴维尔等都在宴会上热情地讲了话，祝贺中捷两国文化艺术交流和人民友谊的日益发展和加强。
