Title: Czech Symphony Orchestra Arrives in China for Performance Tour; All Members Arrive in Guangzhou and Receive a Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1959-11-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 15th: The Czech Symphony Orchestra of the Czechoslovak Republic, led by conductor Pavel and consisting of 51 members, arrived in Guangzhou by train from Shenzhen this afternoon, as part of the 1959 executive plan for the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement, to tour China for performances. As of now, all 122 members of the Czech Symphony Orchestra have arrived in Guangzhou and are scheduled to hold a large-scale symphony concert at the Guangzhou Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall on the 16th.

### 捷交响乐团来我国访问演出  全部团员到达广州受到热烈欢迎

1959-11-16
第5版()
专栏：

    捷交响乐团来我国访问演出
    全部团员到达广州受到热烈欢迎
    据新华社广州15日电　根据中捷文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国访问演出的捷克斯洛伐克共和国捷克交响乐团团长巴维尔和团员五十一人，今天下午乘火车由深圳到达广州。至此，捷克交响乐团的一百二十二名团员已经全部到达广州，定于16日在广州中山纪念堂举行大型交响乐演奏会。
