Title: Song Qingling Sends Greetings to the Soviet People
Author:
Date: 1959-11-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Song Qingling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, sent a telegram to Popova, Chairman of the Council of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, warmly congratulating them on the 42nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

The telegram stated that the October Revolution opened a new era in which mankind transitions from capitalism to socialism and communism. Over the past forty-two years, under the leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Soviet people have achieved brilliant accomplishments in the cause of socialist and communist construction. Now, the Soviet Union has entered a new historical period of fully developing the construction of a communist society. We express our warm congratulations on these great achievements of the Soviet people!

The telegram continued, for the past ten years, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese people have made tremendous achievements in all aspects of socialist revolution and socialist construction. In the process of our country's construction, we have received sincere and comprehensive assistance from the Soviet people. We express our heartfelt gratitude for this!

As the people of the world celebrate the 42nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, we are infinitely pleased that the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people is flourishing and the unity between China and the Soviet Union is becoming increasingly consolidated.

Let the glory of the October Revolution forever illuminate us! Let us raise the red flag of the October Revolution even higher and advance victoriously together!

### 宋庆龄会长向苏联人民致贺

1959-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄会长向苏联人民致贺
    新华社6日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄，打电报给苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会理事会主席波波娃和苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫，热烈祝贺伟大的十月社会主义革命四十二周年。
    贺电说，十月革命开辟了人类由资本主义过渡到社会主义和共产主义的新时代。四十二年来，苏联人民在苏联共产党领导下，在社会主义和共产主义建设事业中，取得了光辉灿烂的成就。现在苏联又开始进入了全面展开共产主义社会建设的新的历史时期。我们对于苏联人民获得的这些伟大成就，表示热烈的祝贺！
    贺电接着说，十年来，中国人民在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设的各个方面都取得了巨大的成就。在我国建设过程中，得到了苏联人民真诚的全面的援助。我们对此表示衷心的感谢！
    当此全世界人民欢庆伟大十月社会主义革命四十二周年的时候，我们感到无限高兴的是，中苏两国人民的伟大友谊蓬勃发展，中苏两国的团结日益巩固。
    让十月革命的光辉永远照耀着我们！让我们更高地举起十月革命的红旗，胜利地共同前进！
