Title: Macmillan to Visit Africa Early Next Year; Cairo "Evening News" Says African People Want British Imperialism to Get Out

Author:

Date: 1959-11-21
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, London, 18th - British officials announced today that Prime Minister Macmillan will visit Africa in January next year.

　　The places Macmillan will visit include Ghana, Northern Rhodesia, Southern Rhodesia, Nyasaland, Nigeria and the Union of South Africa.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 19th - Cairo's "Evening News" published a commentary today on British Prime Minister Macmillan's upcoming visit to Africa, saying that Macmillan will not receive a warm welcome from the fighting African people. He will see with his own eyes that the African people demand that British imperialism get out of their country.

　　The commentary also said that the African people are fighting for freedom. In Africa, the myth of the "white god" has gone bankrupt, and the African people have awakened. They demand their rights and insist on having a voice in world affairs.

### 麦克米伦明年初去非洲活动  开罗“晚报”说非洲人民要英帝国滚出去

1959-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

    麦克米伦明年初去非洲活动
    开罗“晚报”说非洲人民要英帝国滚出去
    新华社伦敦18日电　英国官方今天宣布，首相麦克米伦将在明年1月访问非洲。
    麦克米伦将访问的地方包括加纳、北罗得西亚、南罗得西亚、尼亚萨兰、尼日利亚和南非联邦。
    新华社开罗19日电　开罗“晚报”今天就英国首相麦克米伦即将到非洲活动一事发表评论说，麦克米伦不会得到从事战斗的非洲人民的热情接待。他将亲眼看到，非洲人民要求英国帝国主义滚出他们的国家。
    评论还说，非洲人民正在为争取自由而战斗。在非洲，“白种人上帝”的神话已经破产，非洲人民已经觉醒，他们要求自己的权利，并且坚持在世界事务中要有发言权。
