Title: Soviet "Tomsk People" Football Team Arrives in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1959-11-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 3rd: The Soviet "Tomsk People" football team, consisting of twenty people led by Klyukova, Chairman of the Tomsk Oblast Sports Association and Sports Organizations Federation of the Soviet Union, arrived in Tianjin today on the Moscow-Beijing international train at the invitation of our country.

After their visit and matches in Tianjin, the "Tomsk People" football team will visit and play matches in Beijing, Wuhan, Guangzhou, and other places.

### 苏联“托木斯克人”足球队抵天津

1959-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联“托木斯克人”足球队抵天津
    新华社天津3日电　应邀来我国访问的苏联“托木斯克人”足球队一行二十人，今天由苏联托木斯克州体育协会与体育组织联合会主席克柳科娃率领，乘莫斯科—北京国际列车到达天津市。
    “托木斯克人”足球队在天津访问比赛结束后，将到北京、武汉、广州等地进行访问比赛。
