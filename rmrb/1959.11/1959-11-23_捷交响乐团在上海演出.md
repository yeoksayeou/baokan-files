Title: Czech Symphony Orchestra Performs in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-11-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 22nd: The Czechoslovak Republic Symphony Orchestra gave its first performance in Shanghai tonight. Every piece performed by the musicians was warmly welcomed by the audience.

Tonight, the symphony orchestra performed programs including Tchaikovsky's "Romeo and Juliet" Overture, Dvořák's "Symphony No. 8 in G Major," and Shostakovich's "Symphony No. 1."

Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and other Shanghai municipal party and government leaders attended tonight's concert. At the end of the performance, Shanghai Vice Mayor Niu Shucai, Director of the Bureau of Culture Xu Pingyu, Deputy Director Zhong Wangyang, Council Member of the Sino-Czech Friendship Association Bai Yan, and famous figures from the music community went on stage to congratulate the orchestra on its successful performance.

The Czechoslovak Symphony Orchestra arrived in Shanghai this morning by special train from Beijing. At the station, they were warmly welcomed by Xu Pingyu, Zhong Wangyang, Bai Yan, and figures from the music community. The Czechoslovak Consul General in Shanghai, Mr. Ferlinger, also greeted them at the station.

### 捷交响乐团在上海演出

1959-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    捷交响乐团在上海演出
    新华社上海22日电　捷克斯洛伐克共和国交响乐团，今天晚上在上海首次演出。音乐家们演奏的每一个节目，都博得了听众的热烈欢迎。
    今晚，交响乐团演奏的节目，有柴可夫斯基的“罗密欧和朱丽叶”序曲、德沃夏克的“G大调交响乐”和萧斯塔科维奇的“第一交响乐”等。
    中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显等上海市党政负责人，出席了今晚的音乐会。演出结束时，上海市副市长牛树才，文化局局长徐平羽、副局长钟望阳，中捷友好协会理事白彦及音乐界著名人士等上台祝贺演出的成功。
    捷克斯洛伐克交响乐团是在今天上午由北京乘专车到达上海的。在车站上，受到徐平羽、钟望阳、白彦和音乐界人士的热烈欢迎。捷克斯洛伐克驻上海总领事费林格也到车站迎接。
