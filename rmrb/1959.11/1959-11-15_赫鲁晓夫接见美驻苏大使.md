Title: Khrushchev Receives U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1959-11-15
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 14th. TASS Moscow dispatch: At the request of U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union Thompson, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Khrushchev received him on the 13th.
    Present at the meeting was Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Semyonov.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见美驻苏大使

1959-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见美驻苏大使
    新华社莫斯科14日电　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫应美国驻苏联大使汤普森的请求，于13日接见了他。
    接见时在座的有苏联外交部副部长谢苗诺夫。
