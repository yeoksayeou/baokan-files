Title: Soviet Ambassador to China Holds Film Reception
Author:
Date: 1959-11-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Soviet Ambassador to China, Chervonenko, held a film reception on the evening of the 18th, screening the color documentary "N.S. Khrushchev's Visit to the United States."

    Among those who attended the film reception were: Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Vice-Chairmen Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, and Lin Feng; Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun; President of the Supreme People's Court Xie Juezai; Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Zeng Yongquan; as well as heads of various government departments and people's organizations such as the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.
    Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries in China were also invited to attend the film reception.

### 苏联驻华大使举行电影晚会

1959-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联驻华大使举行电影晚会
    新华社19日讯　苏联驻华大使契尔沃年科于18日晚举行电影晚会，放映彩色纪录片“尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫访问美国”。
    出席电影晚会的有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，副委员长沈钧儒、陈叔通、林枫，国务院副总理习仲勋，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，外交部副部长章汉夫、曾涌泉，以及政府各部门和中苏友好协会等人民团体的负责人。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也应邀出席了电影晚会。
