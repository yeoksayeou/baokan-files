Title: Japanese Coal Miners Hold a General Strike
Author:
Date: 1959-11-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 20th: Workers at fourteen major Japanese coal mining companies today launched a nationwide strike at mines across the country to oppose the capitalists' "enterprise rationalization" plans involving mass layoffs and to demand year-end bonuses.

　　Among these fourteen major companies, workers at the Mitsui and Mitsubishi companies held a 24-hour strike, workers at the Furukawa company held a 42-hour strike, and workers at the Nitetsu Coal Mine's Nise coal mine have been holding a 72-hour strike since yesterday.

　　Workers at the remaining ten major companies held scheduled strikes lasting nearly two hours.

### 日本煤矿工人举行大罢工

1959-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

    日本煤矿工人举行大罢工
    新华社20日讯　东京消息：日本十四家大煤矿公司的工人今天在全国各地矿山一齐举行罢工，反对资方实行大批解雇工人的“企业合理化”计划，并且要求发给年终津贴。
    在这十四家大公司中，三井和三菱两大公司的工人举行了二十四小时罢工，古河公司的工人举行四十二小时罢工，日铁煤矿公司二濑煤矿的工人从昨天起举行了七十二小时罢工。
    其余十家大公司的工人举行了将近两小时的定时罢工。
