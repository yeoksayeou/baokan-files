Title: Soviet Embassy Sends Greetings to All Circles in Our Country, Thanking Them for Their Welcome to the Soviet Party and Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-11-13
Page: 1

    Soviet Embassy Sends Greetings to All Circles in Our Country
    Thanks for the Welcome to the Soviet Party and Government Delegation
    (Our Newspaper dispatch) The Soviet Embassy, entrusted by the Soviet Party and Government Delegation headed by Comrade Khrushchev, sent a letter to this newspaper on the 12th to express its gratitude for the large number of enthusiastic welcoming letters received from people all over China before and after attending our country's 10th anniversary celebration. The letter is as follows: To the Editorial Department of the People's Daily:
    During the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the People's Republic of China in Beijing, and after the delegation returned to its country, the Soviet Embassy in China received a large number of enthusiastic welcoming letters to Comrade Khrushchev and the delegation from many organizations, workers, farmers, intellectual representatives, university and middle school students from all over China.
    These letters expressed gratitude to the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Soviet government, and Comrade Khrushchev personally for their unremitting and fruitful activities aimed at further developing and consolidating the socialist system, ensuring peace, and easing international tensions. They also expressed satisfaction with the results of Comrade Khrushchev's visit to the United States and conveyed feelings of fraternal friendship and respect for the Soviet people.
    Entrusted by the Soviet Party and Government Delegation, the Soviet Embassy in China expresses its heartfelt gratitude to all the organizations and Chinese comrades in the People's Republic of China who sent these friendly wishes.
    Soviet Embassy in the People's Republic of China

### 苏联大使馆来函向我国各界致意  感谢对苏党政代表团的欢迎

1959-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联大使馆来函向我国各界致意
    感谢对苏党政代表团的欢迎
    本报讯　以赫鲁晓夫同志为首的苏联党政代表团，在参加我国建国十周年典礼前后，曾收到我国各地人民大批热情欢迎的信件。苏联驻华大使馆受代表团的委托，12日致函本报表示感谢。来函如下：人民日报编辑部：
    在以赫鲁晓夫同志为首的苏联党政代表团于北京参加中华人民共和国十周年庆典时，以及在代表团回国之后，苏联驻华大使馆收到了中国各地许多机关、工人、农民、知识分子代表、大学生和中小学生写给赫鲁晓夫同志和代表团的大批热情欢迎的信件。
    在这些信件中，对苏联共产党、苏联政府和赫鲁晓夫同志本人的旨在进一步发展和巩固社会主义体系，旨在保证和平，缓和国际紧张局势的不懈的、卓有成效的活动表示感谢，对赫鲁晓夫同志访美的结果表示满意，表达了对苏联人民的兄弟友谊和尊敬的感情。
    苏联驻华大使馆受苏联党政代表团的委托，对寄来这些友好祝愿的中华人民共和国的一切机关和中国同志表示衷心的感谢。
    苏联驻中华人民共和国大使馆
