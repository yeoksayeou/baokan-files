Title: Number of Czechoslovak Agricultural Cooperatives Continues to Increase, Cooperative Farmland Reaches 64%
Author:
Date: 1959-11-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague - In the first nine months of this year, the number of Unified Agricultural Cooperatives in Czechoslovakia increased by 368, and the cooperative farmland area increased by more than 401,000 hectares.

Currently, there are more than 12,500 agricultural cooperatives in Czechoslovakia, with more than 964,000 members. The land they cultivate accounts for 64% of the country's agricultural land. Including the agricultural land occupied by state-owned farms, school farms, and other state-owned enterprises, the socialist component in Czechoslovak agriculture accounts for 82.6% of the country's cultivated land area. This percentage has increased by 5.6% compared to last year.

At present, agricultural cooperatives have been established in 82.9% of the rural areas in Czechoslovakia.

(Attached picture)
	The agricultural cooperativization movement in the German Democratic Republic has made great progress. So far, there are more than 9,600 agricultural production cooperatives nationwide, with a cultivated area accounting for 40.9% of the total cultivated area of the country; together with 700 state-owned farms, nearly 50% of the country's cultivated land belongs to the socialist component. ### 捷农业社数目不断增加  合作化耕地面积达64％

1959-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    捷农业社数目不断增加
    合作化耕地面积达64％
    新华社布拉格电　今年头九个月，捷克斯洛伐克统一农业合作社的数目增加了三百六十八个，合作社的耕地面积增加了四十万一千多公顷。
    现在，捷克斯洛伐克一共有一万二千五百多个农业合作社，这些合作社拥有社员九十六万四千多人，他们耕种的土地占全国农业土地的64％。加上国营农场、学校农场和其他国营企业所占的农业土地，捷克斯洛伐克农业中的社会主义成份占全国耕地面积的82.6％。这个百分比比去年增加了5.6％。
    目前在捷克斯洛伐克的82.9％的农村中都成立了农业合作社。
    （附图片）
    德意志民主共和国农业合作化运动已取得巨大的进展。到目前为止，全国已有九千六百多个农业生产合作社，耕地面积共占全国耕地总面积的40.9％；连同七百个国营农场在一起，则全国已有差不多50％的耕地属于社会主义成份。图为机器拖拉机站的各种机器在田间为农业合作社工作。
