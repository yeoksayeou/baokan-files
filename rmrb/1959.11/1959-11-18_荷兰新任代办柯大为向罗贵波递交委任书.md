Title: Dutch New Chargé d'Affaires C. H. A. Plug Presented Credentials to Luo Guibo
Author:
Date: 1959-11-18
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: The newly appointed Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China, C. H. A. Plug, presented his credentials to Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, this morning. Present at the presentation of credentials were Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Jin, specialist of the Department of West European Affairs.

### 荷兰新任代办柯大为向罗贵波递交委任书

1959-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    荷兰新任代办柯大为向罗贵波递交委任书
    新华社17日讯　荷兰新任驻华代办柯大为今天上午向我国外交部副部长罗贵波递交委任书。递交委任书时在座的，有外交部礼宾司副司长马振武、西欧司专员王进。
