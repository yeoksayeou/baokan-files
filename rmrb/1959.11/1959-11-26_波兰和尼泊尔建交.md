Title: Poland and Nepal Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1959-11-26
Page: 6

    Poland and Nepal Establish Diplomatic Relations
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 24th: According to a report by the Polish Press Agency, the government of the Polish People's Republic and the government of the Kingdom of Nepal have decided to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic representatives at the ambassadorial level.

### 波兰和尼泊尔建交

1959-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    波兰和尼泊尔建交
    新华社华沙24日电　据波兰通讯社报道：波兰人民共和国政府和尼泊尔王国政府决定建立外交关系，并互派大使级外交代表。
