Title: Vice Premier Li Fuchun Receives Mongolian Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 27th: Vice Premier of the State Council Li Fuchun received Tumenbayar Alagachaa, First Deputy Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the Mongolian People's Republic, and all members of the Mongolian economic delegation led by him, this evening. The host and guests had a cordial conversation in a friendly atmosphere.

Present at the reception was the Mongolian Ambassador to China, Sharav.

Also present were Wang Guangwei, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission of our country, Gao Fengqi, Director of the General Office, and Li Qiangfen, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Attached picture)

### 李富春副总理接见蒙古经济考察团

1959-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    李富春副总理
    接见蒙古经济考察团
    新华社27日讯　国务院副总理李富春今晚接见了蒙古人民共和国国家计划委员会第一副主席图门巴雅尔·阿拉嘎查和由他率领的蒙古经济考察团的全体团员，宾主在友好的气氛中进行了亲切交谈。
    接见时蒙古驻华大使沙拉布在座。
  在座的还有我国国家计划委员会副主任王光伟、办公厅主任高凤歧，外交部第二亚洲司副司长李强奋等。（附图片）
