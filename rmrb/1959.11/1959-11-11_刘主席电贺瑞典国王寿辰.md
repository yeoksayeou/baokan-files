Title: Chairman Liu Sends Congratulations on Swedish King's Birthday
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi today sent a telegram to King Gustaf VI Adolf of Sweden, congratulating him on his birthday. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows:
To His Majesty King Gustaf VI Adolf of the Kingdom of Sweden:

On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday, I would like to express my congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of Sweden on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish prosperity to the Kingdom of Sweden and happiness to its people.

                Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, November 10, 1959

### 刘主席电贺瑞典国王寿辰

1959-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席电贺瑞典国王寿辰
    新华社10日讯　刘少奇主席今天打电报给瑞典国王古斯塔夫·阿道尔夫六世，祝贺他的寿辰。贺电全文如下：瑞典王国国王古斯塔夫·阿道尔夫六世陛下：
    在陛下寿辰的时候，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义，向陛下和瑞典人民表示祝贺。祝瑞典国家繁荣，人民幸福。
                中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇　1959年11月10日
