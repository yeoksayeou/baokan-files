Title: Mrs. Goonewardene, Vice-Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th. Mrs. Goonewardene, Vice-Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a short visit to our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
    Greeting her at the airport was Lin Lin, Deputy Secretary-General of the CPAFC.

### 锡中友协副主席古纳瓦达纳夫人到京

1959-11-10
第5版()
专栏：

    锡中友协副主席古纳瓦达纳夫人到京
    新华社9日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国作短期访问的锡兰—中国友好协会副主席古纳瓦达纳夫人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有对外文协副秘书长林林。
