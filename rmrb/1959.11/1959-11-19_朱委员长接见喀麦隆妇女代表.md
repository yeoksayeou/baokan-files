Title: Chairman Zhu Receives Cameroonian Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-11-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhu De, received Madame Carmen Monique, head of the Cameroonian Women's Delegation, and delegation members Madame Marta Moumié and Madame Goï Marguerite this morning. The hosts and guests had a friendly conversation.

Present at the meeting were Zhang Su, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Shi Liang, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation, Wu Quanheng, Secretary of the Secretariat, and Shen Yuanhui, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department.

### 朱委员长接见喀麦隆妇女代表

1959-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    朱委员长接见喀麦隆妇女代表
    新华社18日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，今天上午接见了喀麦隆妇女代表团团长卡门·莫尼克夫人，代表团团员：玛塔·穆米埃夫人、戈伊·玛格丽特夫人，宾主进行了友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有全国人民代表大会常委会委员张苏，全国妇联副主席史良、书记处书记吴全衡、国际联络部副部长沈元辉。
