Title: Party and State Leaders Receive All Representatives of the National Small Coal Mine Work Conference and the Agricultural Machinery Science and Technology Conference, and Receive Coal Mine Employee Basketball and Volleyball Athletes
Author:
Date: 1959-11-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 25th — Party and state leaders Zhu De, He Long, Tan Zhenlin, Bo Yibo, and Nie Rongzhen, this afternoon received all representatives attending the Second National Small Coal Mine Work Conference and the First National Agricultural Machinery Science and Technology Work Conference, as well as all athletes participating in the National Coal Mine Employee Basketball and Volleyball Games.

### 党和国家领导人  接见全国小型煤矿工作会议农业机械科学技术会议全体代表  并接见煤矿职工篮排球运动员

1959-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    党和国家领导人
    接见全国小型煤矿工作会议农业机械科学技术会议全体代表
    并接见煤矿职工篮排球运动员
    新华社25日讯　党和国家的领导人朱德、贺龙、谭震林、薄一波、聂荣臻，今天下午接见了出席全国第二次小型煤矿工作会议和全国第一次农业机械科学技术工作会议的全体代表，以及参加全国煤矿职工篮排球运动会的全体运动员。
