Title: New Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 14th – Mr. C.G. Stelle, the new Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China, arrived in Beijing by train today. Han Xu, a specialist from the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the station to welcome him.

### 荷兰新任代办到京

1959-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    荷兰新任代办到京
    新华社14日讯　荷兰新任驻我国代办柯大为先生今天乘火车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有外交部礼宾司专员韩叙。
