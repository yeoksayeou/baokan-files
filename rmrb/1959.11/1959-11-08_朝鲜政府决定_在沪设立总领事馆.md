Title: Korean Government Decides to Establish Consulate General in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, with the consent of the Chinese government, has decided to establish a Consulate General in Shanghai, and has appointed Li Kuanyong as Consul General.

### 朝鲜政府决定  在沪设立总领事馆

1959-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府决定
    在沪设立总领事馆
    新华社7日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府征得中国政府同意，决定在上海设立总领事馆，并任命李宽镛为总领事。
