Title: Further Strengthening Scientific Cooperation Between the Two Countries: China and Hungary Sign Scientific Cooperation Plan for Next Year
Author:
Date: 1959-11-11
Page: 5

　　Further Strengthening Scientific Cooperation Between the Two Countries
　　China and Hungary Sign Scientific Cooperation Plan for Next Year
　　Xinhua News Agency, 10th. After friendly discussions, representatives of the Chinese and Hungarian Academies of Sciences have reached an agreement on the 1960 implementation plan for scientific cooperation between the two Academies, and a signing ceremony was held this afternoon. Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Major Máté, head of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences delegation, signed the implementation plan on behalf of the two Academies.
　　Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Nográdi, the Hungarian Ambassador to China, also attended the signing ceremony.
　　Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet at noon today to bid farewell to the Hungarian Academy of Sciences delegation. The delegation will leave Beijing for Moscow on the 11th.

　　Mongolia Builds Automated Shoe Factory with Czechoslovak Assistance
　　Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 10th. An automated leather shoe factory in Mongolia has been completed and officially put into production today. According to equipment capacity, this factory can produce 1.2 million pairs of various leather shoes annually, which can basically meet the needs of the Mongolian people for leather shoes. This leather shoe factory is part of the Mongolian Industrial United Factory and was built with economic and technical assistance from Czechoslovakia.
　　At noon today, workers from the Mongolian Industrial United Factory held a rally to celebrate the commissioning of the leather shoe factory. Mongolian party and government leaders Tsedenbal, Tsende, and others attended the celebration.

### 进一步加强两国科学合作  中匈签订明年科学合作计划

1959-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步加强两国科学合作
    中匈签订明年科学合作计划
    新华社10日讯　中国和匈牙利两国科学院的代表经过友好的商谈，已就两国科学院科学合作1960年执行计划达成协议，并且在今天下午举行了签字仪式。代表两国科学院在执行计划上签字的是中国科学院副院长吴有训和匈牙利科学院代表团团长马约尔·马泰。
    中国科学院院长郭沫若和匈牙利驻华大使诺格拉第也出席了签字仪式。
    中国科学院副院长吴有训今天中午设宴欢送匈牙利科学院代表团。代表团将于11日离开北京去莫斯科。
  在捷克斯洛伐克帮助下蒙建成自动化鞋厂
    新华社乌兰巴托10日电　蒙古一个自动化皮鞋制造厂已经建成，并在今天正式投入生产。根据设备能力，这个工厂每年可以生产各种皮鞋一百二十万双，可以基本上满足蒙古人民对皮鞋的需要。这个皮鞋制造厂是蒙古工业联合工厂的组成部分，它是在捷克斯洛伐克的经济和技术援助下建成的。
    今天中午，蒙古工业联合工厂职工举行集会，庆祝皮鞋制造厂投入生产。蒙古党和政府领导人泽登巴尔、曾德等人出席了庆祝会。
