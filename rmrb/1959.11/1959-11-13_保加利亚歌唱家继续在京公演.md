Title: Bulgarian Singers Continue Performances in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — The five Bulgarian singers and one pianist continued their performances at the Tianqiao Theater tonight. The singers sang more than twenty songs as solos and in ensembles tonight, and were warmly welcomed by more than 1,500 listeners.

Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhou Weizhi, Director of the Art Bureau of the Ministry of Culture; Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association; Zhao Feng, Executive Director, and other figures from the music circles; as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries' embassies in China, attended the concert.

### 保加利亚歌唱家继续在京公演

1959-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

    保加利亚歌唱家继续在京公演
    据新华社12日讯　保加利亚五位歌唱家和一位钢琴家今晚在天桥剧场继续公演。歌唱家们今晚独唱、重唱了二十多首歌曲，受到一千五百多听众的热烈欢迎。
    对外文委副主任屈武，文化部艺术局局长周巍峙，中国音乐家协会主席吕骥、常务理事赵沨等音乐界人士，以及各国驻华使节与外交官员，出席了音乐会。
