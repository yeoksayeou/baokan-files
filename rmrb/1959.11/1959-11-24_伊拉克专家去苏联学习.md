Title: Iraqi Experts to Study in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1959-11-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 23rd - TASS Moscow, 21st: Nearly one hundred Iraqi experts have arrived in the Soviet Union to undertake production technology internships at Soviet industrial enterprises. Another thirty-six Iraqi engineers and technicians will arrive soon.

According to a contract signed in October between Iraq and the Soviet Union, Iraqi experts will undertake production internships in the Soviet Union.

After interning for about a year, the engineers and technicians will return to Iraq to work in factories being built with Soviet assistance.

### 伊拉克专家去苏联学习

1959-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克专家去苏联学习
    据新华社莫斯科23日电　据塔斯社莫斯科21日讯：到苏联工业企业里进行生产技术实习的将近一百名伊拉克专家已经来到苏联。另有三十六名伊拉克工程师和技术员最近即将前来。
    按照伊拉克和苏联有关方面今年10月签订的合同，伊拉克专家将在苏联进行生产实习。
    在大约实习一年以后，工程师和技术员们将回到伊拉克，在苏联帮助建设的工厂中工作。
