Title: Active Transactions at Autumn Chinese Export Commodities Fair
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 4th: Since the opening of the 1959 Autumn Chinese Export Commodities Fair, jointly sponsored by China's foreign trade corporations, on November 1st, a wide range of Chinese export commodities have been traded at the fair. These include traditional export goods such as silk, soybeans, ceramics, preserved fruits, fresh fruits, aquatic products, and fishery products, as well as industrial products like cotton cloth, newsprint, cement, and canned goods. Various agricultural and sideline products, local specialties, machinery, instruments, and daily necessities have also seen significant transaction volumes. Sales cover regions across five continents.

As of November 3rd, over 1,000 traders from 17 countries and regions in Asia, Africa, Latin America, Europe, and Australia, as well as Hong Kong and Macao, have attended the fair. The majority are returning clients who have participated in previous Chinese Export Commodities Fairs, with a significant number of new clients also present. It is understood that many other traders from various countries who have been invited to attend the fair are currently en route to China.

To facilitate the viewing and purchasing of goods and the negotiation of deals for traders from various regions, the newly completed Chinese Export Commodities Exhibition Hall showcases an even richer and more diverse array of export commodity samples. The total number of exhibits exceeds 20,000, with approximately 2,000 new items compared to the previous fair.

### 秋季中国出口商品交易会交易活跃

1959-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

    秋季中国出口商品交易会交易活跃
    据新华社广州4日电　中国各对外贸易公司联合主办的1959年秋季中国出口商品交易会，从11月1日开幕以来，交易会上成交的我国出口商品很广泛，主要有丝绸、大豆、陶瓷、京果、鲜果、水产、渔产等传统出口商品，以及棉布、新闻纸、水泥、罐头等工业产品，其他各种农副土特产品和各种机械、仪器、日用百货等，也有相当数额的成交；销售地区遍于世界五大洲。
    截至11月3日止，前来参加这次交易会的，有亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲、欧洲和澳洲的十七个国家和地区以及香港、澳门的贸易界人士，共一千多人。他们大多数是过去参加过中国出口商品交易会的老客户，也有不少是新客户。据悉，目前还有不少应邀前来参加交易会的各国贸易界人士，正在来中国途中。
    为了便于各地贸易界人士参观选购、看样成交，新落成的中国出口商品陈列馆展出了比以往更加丰富多采的出口商品样品，全部展品共两万多种，其中新展品比上届交易会增加约两千种。
