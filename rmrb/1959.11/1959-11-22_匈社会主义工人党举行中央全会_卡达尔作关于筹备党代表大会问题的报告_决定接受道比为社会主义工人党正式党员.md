Title: Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Holds Central Committee Plenary Session; Kádár Delivers Report on Preparations for Party Congress; Decision Made to Accept Dobi as Formal Member of Socialist Workers' Party
Author:
Date: 1959-11-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, November 21st. According to today's report in the Hungarian newspaper "People's Freedom," the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party held a plenary session on the 20th, listened to and unanimously approved the report made by First Secretary of the Central Committee, János Kádár, on issues related to preparations for the Party Congress.

The Seventh Congress of the Socialist Workers' Party will be held on November 30th.

The Central Committee also discussed the application of István Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Republic, to join the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party. The meeting held that, due to Comrade Dobi's decades of self-sacrificing struggle, his loyalty to the people, and his diligent work for the People's Republic, it was decided to accept him as a formal member (waiving the probationary period).

"People's Freedom" also published a letter written by Comrade Dobi to the Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party in October, requesting to join the Party.

### 匈社会主义工人党举行中央全会  卡达尔作关于筹备党代表大会问题的报告  决定接受道比为社会主义工人党正式党员

1959-11-22
第3版()
专栏：

    匈社会主义工人党举行中央全会
    卡达尔作关于筹备党代表大会问题的报告
    决定接受道比为社会主义工人党正式党员
    新华社布达佩斯21日电　据匈牙利“人民自由报”今天报道，匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会20日举行了全体会议，听取并一致通过了中央委员会第一书记卡达尔所作的关于筹备党代表大会的一些问题的报告。
    社会主义工人党第七次代表大会将在11月30日召开。
    中央委员会还讨论了人民共和国主席团主席道比要求加入匈牙利社会主义工人党的申请书。会议认为，由于道比同志几十年来进行自我牺牲的斗争活动，由于他对人民的忠诚和对人民共和国的辛勤劳动，决定接受他为正式党员（免去预备期）。
    “人民自由报”还刊登了道比同志10月间写给社会主义工人党中央委员会的请求入党的一封信。
