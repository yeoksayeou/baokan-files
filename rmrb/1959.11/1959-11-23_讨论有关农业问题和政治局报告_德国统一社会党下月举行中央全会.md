Title: Discussing Agricultural Issues and Politburo Report: German Socialist Unity Party to Hold Central Committee Plenary Session Next Month
Author:
Date: 1959-11-23
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 21st. According to a Deutsche Presse-Agentur report on the evening of the 20th: The Politburo of the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party has decided to hold the Seventh Plenary Session of the Central Committee in December. The main agenda of the meeting will be to discuss agricultural issues related to the seven-year plan and a report from the Politburo.

### 讨论有关农业问题和政治局报告  德国统一社会党下月举行中央全会

1959-11-23
第5版()
专栏：

    讨论有关农业问题和政治局报告
    德国统一社会党下月举行中央全会
    新华社柏林21日电　据德意志通讯社20日晚消息：德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局决定在12月举行第七次中央委员会全体会议。会议主要议程是讨论七年计划中有关农业的问题和政治局的一个报告。
