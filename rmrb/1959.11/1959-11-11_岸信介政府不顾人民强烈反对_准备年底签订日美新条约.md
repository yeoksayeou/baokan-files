Title: Kishida Nobusuke Government, Disregarding Strong Public Opposition, Prepares to Sign New Japan-U.S. Treaty at the End of the Year
Author:
Date: 1959-11-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 10th dispatch: Foreign Minister Aiichiro Fujiyama of Japan stated at a press conference today that negotiations to revise the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty" may conclude within this month.

He hopes to sign this Japan-U.S. military alliance treaty, which is resolutely opposed by the Japanese public, in mid-December.

Due to the strong demands of the opposition parties, Aiichiro Fujiyama today made a report in the Diet on the revision of the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty." However, he did not disclose the true situation of the Japan-U.S. negotiations according to the public's demands, but tried his best to conceal the essence of the new treaty in his report.

### 岸信介政府不顾人民强烈反对  准备年底签订日美新条约

1959-11-11
第6版()
专栏：

    岸信介政府不顾人民强烈反对
    准备年底签订日美新条约
    新华社10日讯　东京消息：日本外务相藤山爱一郎今天在记者招待会上说，修改日美“安全条约”的谈判可能在这个月内结束。
    他期望在12月中旬能够签订这个遭到日本公众坚决反对的日美军事同盟条约。
    由于反对党的强烈要求，藤山爱一郎今天在国会作了关于修改日美“安全条约”的报告。但是，他并没有根据公众的要求，公开日美谈判的真实情况，却在报告中竭力掩饰新条约的本质。
