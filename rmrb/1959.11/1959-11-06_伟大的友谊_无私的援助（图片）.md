Great Friendship, Selfless Assistance

    Top right picture: To express gratitude for the Soviet Union's selfless assistance to China's agricultural production, the "Great Northern Wilderness" Friendship Farm built a monument to "Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship."　　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Hu Wei photographed
    Top left picture: Soviet scientist Yang Ke (right), who teaches at Peking University, is studying inorganic chemistry with Ren Wenzhong (left), director of the Chemistry Department of Peking University, and Zhang Qinglian (center), director of the Inorganic Teaching and Research Section of the Chemistry Department. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Fu Jun photographed
    Middle left picture: Soviet expert Tikhonov lectures to Chinese forestry cadres.
              Cheng Xiaochun photographed (Xinhua News Agency manuscript)
    Middle right picture: In the Luoyang No. 1 Tractor Manufacturing Plant, chassis workshop process expert Kavalev (left) and chief process expert Daochinko (third from left) are guiding production on the tractor assembly line.
              Xinhua News Agency Reporter Yang Zhenhe photographed
    Bottom left picture: Liuda, a Soviet student studying at Peking University, is learning folk dance from Chinese students.
              Xinhua News Agency Reporter Fu Jun photographed

### 伟大的友谊　无私的援助（图片）

1959-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    伟大的友谊　无私的援助
    上右图：为了感谢苏联对我国农业生产的无私援助，“北大荒”友谊农场建筑了一座“中苏友谊万岁”纪念碑。　　新华社记者　胡伟摄
    上左图：在北京大学任教的苏联科学家杨柯（右）和北京大学化学系主任任文重（左）、化学系无机教研室主任张青莲（中）在一起研究无机化学方面的问题。新华社记者　傅军摄
    中左图：苏联专家吉洪诺夫为我国林业干部讲课。
              程晓春摄（新华社稿）
    中右图：在洛阳第一拖拉机制造厂里，底盘车间工艺专家卡瓦廖夫（左）和总工艺专家道钦科（左三）正在拖拉机总装配线上指导生产。
              新华社记者　杨震河摄
    下左图：在北京大学学习的苏联同学柳达向中国同学学习民间舞蹈。
              新华社记者　傅军摄
