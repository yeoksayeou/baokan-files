Title: Weak Typhoon Alert
Author:
Date: 1959-11-15
Page: 2

    Weak Typhoon Alert
    Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a weak typhoon alert at 7:00 PM on the 14th, stating:
    The weak typhoon over the South China Sea was centered at 2:00 PM today approximately 650 kilometers southeast of Hainan Island, near 15 degrees North latitude and 115.3 degrees East longitude. The maximum wind force near the center is estimated to be around force 10. Currently, the typhoon center is moving in a west-leaning direction at a speed of 7-9 knots per hour and will later turn to a west-northwest direction. It is expected that within the next 48 hours, the wind force in the northern part of the South China Sea, Hainan Island, and Beibu Gulf will gradually increase to force 7-10, and the wind force along the western coast of Guangdong will increase to around force 6.

### 弱台风警报

1959-11-15
第2版()
专栏：

    弱台风警报
    新华社14日讯　中央气象科学研究所14日下午七时发布弱台风警报说：
    南海上的弱台风，今天下午二时中心位置在海南岛东南方六百五十公里左右的海面上，就是北纬十五度、东经一百一十五点三度附近，中心附近最大风力估计有十级左右。目前台风中心正以每小时七浬——九浬的速度向偏西方向移动，以后将转为西偏北方向移动。预计未来四十八小时内，南海北部、海南岛、北部湾的风力将逐渐加大到七级——十级，广东西部沿海的风力将加大到六级左右。
