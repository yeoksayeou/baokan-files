Title: China and Mongolia Hold Film Receptions, Screening Jointly Produced "Ulaanbaatar – Beijing"
Author:
Date: 1959-11-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 25th: Today, on the eve of the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Republic, the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China jointly held a film reception for the Sino-Mongolian co-produced feature-length documentary "Ulaanbaatar – Beijing". The documentary "Ulaanbaatar – Beijing" was jointly produced by the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio of the People's Republic of China and the Film Studio of the Mongolian People's Republic. It is the first film co-produced by Chinese and Mongolian filmmakers. This film documents the friendly exchanges between China and Mongolia since the founding of the People's Republic of China and the mutual support in construction. In the film, one can see the vast grasslands of the Mongolian People's Republic, newly built factories, and the rich and beautiful mountains and rivers. One can also see how the hardworking and brave Mongolian people, under the leadership of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the government, have embarked on the road to a prosperous and wealthy socialist society.

Among those attending the film reception were Vice Minister of Culture Hu Yuzhi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Qu Wu, Vice President of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association Mao Qihua, and more than a thousand people from the capital's cultural and artistic circles. The Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Sharab, and diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to China attended today's film reception.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 24th: The Mongolian Ministry of Foreign Affairs held a film reception tonight, screening the feature-length documentary "Ulaanbaatar – Beijing," which was co-produced by Chinese and Mongolian filmmakers.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia were invited to attend the reception.

### 中蒙分别举行电影招待会  放映两国合拍的“乌兰巴托——北京”

1959-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    中蒙分别举行电影招待会
    放映两国合拍的“乌兰巴托——北京”
    新华社25日讯　今天，在蒙古人民共和国建立三十五周年前夕，中华人民共和国文化部、对外文化联络委员会和蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆共同举行了中蒙合拍大型纪录片“乌兰巴托——北京”电影招待会。纪录片“乌兰巴托——北京”是中华人民共和国中央新闻电影制片厂和蒙古人民共和国电影制片厂联合摄制的，它是中蒙电影工作者合作拍摄的第一部影片。这部影片，纪录了中华人民共和国建国以来，中蒙两国的友谊往来和在建设中的互相支援。影片中可以看到蒙古人民共和国辽阔的草原、新建的工厂和富饶美丽的山河，可以看到勤劳勇敢的蒙古人民如何在蒙古人民革命党和政府的领导下，走上了繁荣、富裕的社会主义道路。
    出席电影招待会的有文化部副部长胡愈之、外交部副部长姬鹏飞、对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武、中蒙友好协会副会长毛齐华及首都文化艺术界人士千余人。蒙古人民共和国驻华大使沙拉布和各国驻华使节及外交官员参加了今天的电影招待会。
    新华社乌兰巴托24日电　蒙古外交部今天晚上举行电影招待会，放映了由中蒙两国电影工作者合作拍摄的大型纪录片“乌兰巴托—北京”。
    各国驻蒙古的外交使节应邀出席了招待会。
