Title: Continue the Effort to Produce More Steel: Benxi Iron and Steel Company's Two Large Blast Furnaces Both Set New Records
Author:
Date: 1959-11-21
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 19th -- The Dongfeng (East Wind) and Yuejin (Leap Forward) large blast furnaces of Benxi Iron and Steel Company have both set new records.

	The utilization coefficient of the Dongfeng blast furnace averaged 2.428 in the first half of November, surpassing Anshan Iron and Steel's No. 9 blast furnace by 0.193 and continuing to lead among the nation's large blast furnaces. On the 15th, the utilization coefficient of the Dongfeng blast furnace reached 2.801, breaking the national record of 2.705 set by Anshan Iron and Steel's No. 3 blast furnace on October 27th. On the same day, the Yuejin blast furnace also achieved a score of 2.46, setting a new record since the furnace was put into operation. Since these two blast furnaces launched a joint technical performance competition on November 11th, their production level has risen sharply, with the average daily output of pig iron increasing by more than 400 tons compared to the first ten days of the month. From November 11th to 15th, the two blast furnaces overproduced a total of 1,439 tons of high-quality iron.

### 再接再厉增产更多的钢铁  本溪钢铁公司两座大型高炉双双创造新纪录

1959-11-21
第2版()
专栏：

    再接再厉增产更多的钢铁
    本溪钢铁公司两座大型高炉双双创造新纪录
    新华社沈阳19日电　本溪钢铁公司的东风号、跃进号两座大型高炉，双双创造新纪录。
    东风号高炉11月上半月的利用系数平均达到二点四二八，超过鞍钢九号高炉零点一九三，继续在全国大型高炉中领先。15日这天，东风号高炉利用系数达到二点八零一，打破了鞍钢三号高炉10月27日创造的二点七零五的全国最高纪录；在同一天里，跃进号高炉也以二点四六的成绩，创造了这座高炉开炉以来的最高纪录。这两座高炉从11月11日开展联合技术大表演赛以来，生产水平猛烈上升，生铁的平均日产量比上旬增长四百多吨，11日到15日两座高炉共超产优质铁一千四百三十九吨。
