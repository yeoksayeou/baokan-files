Title: Lieutenant General Wang Daobang's Memorial Service Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The memorial service for Lieutenant General Wang Daobang, Commander of the Hebei Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, was held yesterday at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery in Beijing. Wreaths from Marshal Lin Biao, Peng Zhen, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Marshal Xu Xiangqian, Lin Tie, the Ministry of National Defense, and the General Headquarters of the People's Liberation Army were placed before Wang Daobang's coffin.

Attending the memorial service were Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Grand General Luo Ruiqing, Grand General Tan Zheng, Senior General Yang Chengwu, Senior General Xiao Hua, Senior General Li Juikui, Senior General Li Zhimin, Senior General Zhu Liangcai, Senior General Chen Bojun, Senior General Huang Yongsheng, First Secretary of the Hebei Provincial Party Committee Lin Tie, Governor of Hebei Province Liu Zihou, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Zhang Su, responsible comrades from party and government organizations in Beijing, Hebei Province, and Tianjin, and representatives from various services and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, totaling more than 500 people.

Chief mourner Marshal Nie Rongzhen presented a wreath to the martyr's coffin. Yuan Shengping, Deputy Political Commissar of the Beijing Military Region, delivered a eulogy. The remains of Lieutenant General Wang Daobang were buried in the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery.

### 王道邦中将追悼会在京举行

1959-11-17
第7版()
专栏：

    王道邦中将追悼会在京举行
    据新华社16日讯　中国人民解放军河北军区司令员王道邦中将的追悼大会，昨天在北京八宝山殡仪馆举行。王道邦的灵柩前，陈放着林彪元帅、彭真、聂荣臻元帅、徐向前元帅、林铁等和国防部、人民解放军总部送的花圈。
    参加追悼会的，有聂荣臻元帅、罗瑞卿大将、谭政大将、杨成武上将、萧华上将、李聚奎上将、李志民上将、朱良才上将、陈伯钧上将、黄永胜上将，中共河北省委第一书记林铁、河北省省长刘子厚、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏和北京市、河北省、天津市党政机关负责同志，以及中国人民解放军各军种、兵种的代表等五百多人。
    主祭人聂荣臻元帅向烈士灵柩献花圈后，北京部队副政治委员袁升平致悼词。王道邦中将的遗体葬于八宝山烈士公墓。
