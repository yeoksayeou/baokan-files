Title: Literary Circles in the Capital Commemorate Zhang Jinyi
Author:
Date: 1959-11-26
Page: 4

    Literary Circles in the Capital Commemorate Zhang Jinyi
    (Our dispatch) Literary circles in the capital gathered on the 25th of this month to commemorate Zhang Jinyi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Writers Association. Over a hundred people attended, including Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, Lao She, Shao Quanlin, Xia Yan, Yang Hansheng, Ye Shengtao, Cao Yu, Zhou Erfu, and Chen Baichen. Mao Dun delivered a memorial speech at the meeting, and Chen Baichen introduced Zhang Jinyi's deeds in striving for the revolutionary literary cause for nearly thirty years. The memorial service concluded after speeches by many of Zhang Jinyi's friends.

### 首都文学界追悼章靳以

1959-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    首都文学界追悼章靳以
    本报讯　首都文学界于本月25日集会，追悼中国作家协会书记处书记章靳以。到会的有郭沫若、茅盾、老舍、邵荃麟、夏衍、阳翰笙、叶圣陶、曹禺、周而复、陈白尘等一百余人。茅盾在会上致悼词，陈白尘介绍了靳以为革命文学事业奋斗近三十年的事迹。追悼会在靳以生前友好多人致词后结束。
