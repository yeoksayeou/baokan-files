Title: Fuzhou No. 2 Chemical Plant
Author:
Date: 1959-11-20
Page: 2
Column: Chemical Industry New Force

    Fuzhou No. 2 Chemical Plant
    The first phase of the Fuzhou No. 2 Chemical Plant has been completed ahead of schedule and began trial production on the 7th.

    This factory is a comprehensive chemical plant mainly producing basic chemical raw materials. The main products include caustic soda, polyvinyl chloride, chloroprene rubber, pesticides, calcium carbide, bleaching powder, hydrochloric acid and more than ten other types. After the completion of the first phase of the project, it will be able to use the abundant sea salt in Fujian Province as the main raw material to produce 7,500 tons of caustic soda, 6,000 tons of 666 crude powder, 4,000 tons of hydrochloric acid, 4,000 tons of bleaching powder and other chemical products for the country every year, supporting industrial and agricultural production inside and outside the province.

### 福州第二化工厂

1959-11-20
第2版()
专栏：化工新军

    福州第二化工厂
    福州第二化工厂首期工程已经提前完成，并从7日起开始试车生产。
    这个工厂是以生产基本化工原料为主的综合化工厂，主要产品有烧碱、聚氯乙烯、氯丁橡胶、农药、电石、漂白粉、盐酸等十多种。第一期工程建成后，就可以利用福建省富饶的海盐作主要原料，每年为国家生产七千五百吨烧碱、六千吨六六六原粉、四千吨盐酸、四千吨漂白粉以及其他化工产品，支援省内外的工农业生产。
