Title: Under Broad Public Condemnation, Laos Postpones Trial of Souphanouvong for the Third Time
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-20
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 19th: Vientiane dispatch: The Lao Kingdom's Ministry of Justice issued a public statement today announcing another postponement of the "trial" of Prince Souphanouvong and other leaders of the Lao Patriotic Front. The stated reason is the need "to conduct further investigations." Today's postponement is the third. The political persecution plot of the Sananikone clique has been widely and strongly condemned by the Lao people and fair-minded public opinion around the world from the very beginning.

### 在公众舆论的广泛谴责下  老挝第三次延期审讯苏发努冯

1959-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    在公众舆论的广泛谴责下
    老挝第三次延期审讯苏发努冯
    据新华社河内19日电　万象消息：老挝王国司法部今天发表公报，宣布再一次延期“审判”苏发努冯亲王和老挝爱国战线党的其他领导人。据宣布的理由是：需要“进行进一步的调查”。今天这一次延期是第三次。萨纳尼空集团的政治迫害阴谋自始就受到老挝人民和世界正直舆论的广泛和强烈的谴责。
