Title: Capital Celebrates 42nd Anniversary of October Revolution with Grand Evening Party; Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, and Other Party and State Leaders Attend
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 6th: Tonight, people from all walks of life in the capital held a grand evening party in the Great Hall of the People to celebrate the 42nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. Artists from the Soviet State Bolshoi Theatre Ballet performed wonderful programs at the party.

Attending the party tonight were Party and state leaders Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Dong Biwu, Lin Boqu, Peng Zhen, He Long, and Lin Feng. Also attending were Bao Erhan, Liao Chengzhi, Shen Yanbing, Shao Lizi, Li Zhuchen, Zhang Xiruo, and Zeng Yongquan, responsible persons from the CPPCC National Committee, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and other organizations. Soviet Ambassador to China Chervonenko and responsible staff members of the embassy, experts from the Soviet Union and other socialist countries in Beijing, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China attended the party.

Tonight's party was jointly sponsored by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Ministry of Culture, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Soviet Embassy in China.

At the party, Soviet artists performed with great success the one-act ballet "Les Sylphides," as well as excerpts from the ballets "Sleeping Beauty," "Swan Lake," and "The Stone Flower," and excerpts from the operas "Faust" and "Ivan Susanin." At the party, Soviet artists also performed the Chinese folk dance "Carrying Pole Dance." Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, Professor Durova, played "Lullaby" and "Waltz" on the harp. Two actors from the Political Department Song and Dance Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force performed "Stalingraders" in cooperation with Soviet artists. The performances and playing of the Soviet artists continuously won the audience's stormy applause. After Soviet People's Artist Lebedinskaya performed the "Waltz," she had to repeat it once again under the audience's prolonged applause and welcome.

After the entire performance ended, Comrades Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, and Dong Biwu, together with Ambassador Chervonenko, went on stage and cordially shook hands with the actors and the conductor of the orchestra, Yu. F. Fayer, congratulating them on the success of their performance and playing. At this moment, a group of young women presented flowers to the Soviet artists, and the applause on the scene lasted for a long time.

After the performance, Party and state leaders Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, and others took a group photo with the actors of the Soviet Ballet. This newspaper reporter, Lu Xiangyou.

### 庆祝十月革命四十二周年首都各界举行联欢晚会  周恩来、朱德等党和国家领导人出席

1959-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月革命四十二周年首都各界举行联欢晚会
    周恩来、朱德等党和国家领导人出席
    新华社6日讯　今天晚上，首都各界人民在人民大会堂举行盛大联欢晚会，庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十二周年，苏联国家大剧院芭蕾舞团的艺术家们在晚会上表演了精彩节目。
    出席今天晚会的，有党和国家领导人周恩来、朱德、董必武、林伯渠、彭真、贺龙、林枫。出席晚会的还有政协全国委员会和中苏友好协会总会等各方面的负责人包尔汉、廖承志、沈雁冰、邵力子、李烛尘、张奚若、曾涌泉等。苏联驻华大使契尔沃年科和大使馆的负责工作人员、在北京的苏联和其他社会主义国家的专家及各国驻华使节出席了晚会。
    今天的晚会是由对外文化联络委员会、文化部、中苏友好协会总会和苏联驻华大使馆联合主办的。
    晚会上，苏联艺术家们极其成功地演出了独幕舞剧“仙女们”，以及“睡美人”、“天鹅湖”、“宝石花”等舞剧的片断，“浮士德”、“伊凡·苏萨宁”等歌剧的片断。晚会上，苏联艺术家们还表演了中国民间舞“扁担舞”，俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国功勋艺术家杜洛娃教授用竖琴演奏了“摇篮曲”和“圆舞曲”，中国人民解放军空军政治部文工团的两位演员，同苏联艺术家们合作演出了“斯大林格勒人”。苏联艺术家们的表演和演奏，不断博得观众们暴风雨般的掌声，苏联人民演员列别申斯卡娅演出“华尔兹”舞后，她在观众们长久鼓掌欢迎下不得不重演了一遍。
    演出全部结束后，周恩来、朱德、董必武等同志和契尔沃年科大使一同上台，和演员们及乐队指挥尤·菲·法耶尔等亲切握手，祝贺他们演出和演奏的成功。这时，一群女青年向苏联艺术家们献鲜花，场上的掌声长久不息。
