Title: Central CCP Leaders Receive Visiting Groups from Hebei and Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 5th: Zhu De, Vice-Chairman of the CCP Central Committee; Lin Boqu, Peng Zhen, and He Long, members of the Political Bureau; and Lu Dingyi, Chen Boda, and Kang Sheng, alternate members of the Political Bureau, today received all members of the Hebei Province Minority Nationalities Visiting Group and the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Education Visiting Group.

Present at the reception were Liu Chun and Sa Kongliao, vice-chairmen of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Lin Liru, vice-minister of the Ministry of Education.

### 中共中央领导人  接见河北和新疆两个参观团

1959-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人
    接见河北和新疆两个参观团
    新华社5日讯　中共中央副主席朱德，政治局委员林伯渠、彭真、贺龙，政治局候补委员陆定一、陈伯达、康生，今天接见了河北省少数民族参观团和新疆维吾尔自治区教育参观团的全体人员。
    接见时在场的有，民族事务委员会副主任刘春、萨空了，教育部副部长林砺儒。
