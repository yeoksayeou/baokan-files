Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Bolivian Guests
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 1st: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet tonight to welcome German Quiroga Galdo, former Speaker of the Bolivian House of Representatives and current member of the House Foreign Affairs Committee, who is visiting our country.

Among those present at the banquet were: Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General, Qiao Guanhua, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Shen Jian, Director, and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General.

German Quiroga Galdo arrived in Beijing on October 30th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 张奚若欢宴玻利维亚客人

1959-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    张奚若欢宴玻利维亚客人
    新华社1日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚举行宴会，欢迎来我国访问的玻利维亚前众议院议长、现众议院外交委员会委员赫尔曼·基罗加·加尔多。
    出席宴会的有：全国人民代表大会常委会副秘书长张苏，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、秘书长周而复，中国人民外交学会副会长乔冠华、理事申健、秘书长吴茂荪等。
    赫尔曼·基罗加·加尔多是应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问，于10月30日到达北京的。
