Title: Soviet Dancers Visit Beijing Dance School
Author:
Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 7th — Teachers, students, and staff of the Beijing Dance School warmly welcomed artists from the Soviet State Grand Theatre Ballet to visit the school this afternoon and spend a festive evening together.

　　When the Soviet ballet artists arrived at the school, music and firecrackers resounded throughout the area. Principal Dai Ailian and Vice Principal Chen Jinqing led the teachers, students, and staff to greet the guests at the school gate. Young Pioneers presented the artists with "?" flowers made of rose-colored velvet.

　　At the reception, the entire school faculty and students held a warm and cordial meeting with the Soviet artists and Soviet experts from the school. The school presented the guests with a banner inscribed with "The Pinnacle of World Ballet Art" and gifts. The Soviet artists presented the school with ballet shoes and vases. Some artists also enthusiastically signed their names on the soles of the ballet shoes as souvenirs.

　　In their speeches at the reception, Principal Dai Ailian and Vice Principal Chen Jinqing warmly congratulated the Soviet ballet artists on the success of their performances in China and thanked the guests for their guidance to the school and the Soviet experts for their assistance. They expressed their determination to continuously learn from the Soviet artists. Soviet artists Bogolyubskaya, Falmanyants, and Soviet expert Gusev also gave enthusiastic speeches at the meeting.

### 苏联舞蹈家参观北京舞蹈学校

1959-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联舞蹈家参观北京舞蹈学校
    据新华社7日讯　北京舞蹈学校师生和工作人员，今天下午热情地欢迎苏联国家大剧院芭蕾舞团艺术家们到学校参观，并一起欢度节日夜晚。
    苏联芭蕾舞艺术家们到达学校的时候，乐曲和鞭炮声响成一片，校长戴爱莲、副校长陈锦清带领师生和工作人员在校门口迎接客人，学校的少先队员向艺术家们献了用玫瑰色绒做成的“?”花。
    招待会上，全校师生同苏联艺术家、这个学校的苏联专家举行了热烈亲切的会见。学校向客人们赠送了写着“世界芭蕾舞艺术的高峰”的锦旗和礼品，苏联艺术家向学校赠送了芭蕾舞鞋和花瓶，有些艺术家还在芭蕾舞鞋底上热情地签字留念。
    校长戴爱莲和副校长陈锦清在招待会上的讲话中，热烈祝贺苏联芭蕾舞艺术家们在我国演出的成功，感谢客人们来校指导，感谢苏联专家的帮助，她们表示将不断地向苏联艺术家们学习。苏联艺术家包格柳勃斯卡娅、法尔玛尼扬茨、苏联专家古谢夫也都在会上热情洋溢地讲了话。
