	International Briefs

	Yemen Expels an American Spy
	Taz News Agency: The Yemeni government recently ordered the expulsion of an American named Abdullah Rahman Conde, because he was engaged in espionage activities in Yemen. Conde was originally an intelligence officer stationed with the U.S. military in Japan. In order to facilitate his espionage activities in Yemen, he obtained Yemeni citizenship after coming to Yemen in 1956, pretended to be a Muslim, and changed his original name, Burl Bond Conde, to Abdullah Rahman Conde. Now his Yemeni citizenship has been revoked by the Yemeni government.

	French Peace Fighter and Famous Actor Philippe Dies
	Paris News Agency: The famous French film actor Gérard Philipe died of liver cancer on the 25th at the age of thirty-six. Gérard Philipe and his wife were both members of the French National Peace Council. As a member, he participated in many activities and recently helped prepare for the tenth anniversary of the peace movement. Philipe once visited China, and films in which he starred, such as "The Red and the Black" and "The Adventures of Till Eulenspiegel," were shown in China.

### 国际简讯

1959-11-29
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    也门驱逐一名美国间谍出境
    塔兹讯　也门政府最近下令把一个叫阿卜杜拉赫曼·康德的美国人驱逐出境，因为他在也门进行间谍活动。康德原来是驻日美军的情报官员，为了在也门进行间谍活动方便起见，他在1956年来到也门后取得了也门国籍，伪装信奉伊斯兰教，并且把他原来的姓名布尔邦·康德改为阿卜杜拉赫曼·康德。现在他的也门国籍已被也门政府取消。
    法和平战士名演员菲利普逝世
    巴黎讯　法国著名电影演员热拉尔·菲利普25日因肝癌逝世，年三十六岁。热拉尔·菲利普和他的夫人，都是法国全国和平理事会的理事，他作为理事参加了许多活动，前不久还帮助筹备和平运动的十周年纪念。菲利普曾经访问中国，他所主演的影片如“红与黑”、“勇士的奇遇”等曾在中国上演。
