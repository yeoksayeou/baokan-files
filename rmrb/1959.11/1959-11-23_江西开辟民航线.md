Title: Jiangxi Opens Civil Aviation Route
Author:
Date: 1959-11-23
Page: 2

Jiangxi Opens Civil Aviation Route

    The Nanchang—Ji'an—Ganzhou civil aviation route in Jiangxi Province was officially opened to traffic recently. This is the first civil aviation route within Jiangxi Province, with a total length of 320 kilometers.

    江西开辟民航线
