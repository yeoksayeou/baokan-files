Title: Our Women's Delegation to the Soviet Union Leaves for Moscow
Author:
Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th: The Chinese Women's Delegation to the Soviet Union, led by Qian Zhengying, consisting of six members, left Beijing by plane this afternoon for Moscow. The delegation was invited by the Soviet Women's Committee to participate in the celebration of the 42nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution and to visit the Soviet Union. Among those who saw them off at the airport were Shen Zijiu, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and others.

Tiedelin, First Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, and others also saw them off at the airport.

### 我妇女访苏代表团去莫斯科

1959-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

    我妇女访苏代表团去莫斯科
    新华社5日讯　中国妇女访苏代表团一行六人在团长钱正英率领下，今天下午乘飞机离开北京去莫斯科。代表团是应苏联妇女委员会邀请。赴苏参加伟大十月社会主义革命四十二周年庆祝典礼并参观访问的。到机场欢送的有全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九等人。
    苏联驻华大使馆一等秘书铁迭林等也到机场欢送。
