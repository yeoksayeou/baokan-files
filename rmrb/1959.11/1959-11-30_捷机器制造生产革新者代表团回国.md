Title: Czechoslovak Machine Manufacturing Production Innovators Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Czechoslovak Machine Manufacturing Production Innovators Delegation, led by Antonín Malík, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Machine Manufacturing Workers' Union, returned home by plane on the 24th, after a five-week visit to China. Qi Zhanyuan, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Shao Ziyan, Chairman of the National Committee of the First Machine Workers' Union of China, were among those who welcomed them at the airport.

### 捷机器制造生产革新者代表团回国

1959-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    捷机器制造生产革新者代表团回国
    据新华社讯　由捷克斯洛伐克机器制造业工人工会中央委员会主席安托宁·马利克率领的捷克斯洛伐克机器制造生产革新者代表团一行六人，在我国访问五周以后，已经在24日乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有中华全国总工会书记处书记郄占元、中国第一机械工会全国委员会主席邵子言等。
