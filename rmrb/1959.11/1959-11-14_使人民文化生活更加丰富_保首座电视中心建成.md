Title: Enrich the People's Cultural Life; Ensure the Completion of the First Television Center
Author:
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 13th: Bulgaria's first television center has recently been completed in Sofia and has already begun test broadcasts.

　　The television center is a thirteen-story building, seventy-two meters high, with its antenna extending to one hundred and seven meters above the ground.

　　Currently, television programs including movies, concerts, dramas, and sports games can be received within a hundred kilometers of the television center. When some television relay stations are completed, television programs will be viewable throughout the country. In a few years, television programs from countries such as Poland, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and the German Democratic Republic will also be viewable in Sofia.

### 使人民文化生活更加丰富  保首座电视中心建成

1959-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    使人民文化生活更加丰富
    保首座电视中心建成
    新华社索非亚13日电　保加利亚第一座电视中心最近在索非亚建成，并且已经开始试播节目。
    这座电视中心是一座十三层的建筑物，高达七十二米，它的天线的顶端距地面为一百零七米。
    目前，在电视中心周围一百公里以内，可以收到包括电影、音乐会、戏剧、球赛等电视节目。当一些电视转播站建成后，全国各地将都能看到电视节目。几年之后，在索非亚还将能看到波兰、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国等国家的电视节目。
