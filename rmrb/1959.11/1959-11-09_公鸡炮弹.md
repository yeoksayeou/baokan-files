    Rooster Cannonball
    Wang Xieshan
    Do you know why bullets can penetrate walls, pierce steel plates...? When the explosive force of gunpowder pushes the bullet through the gun barrel, the gunpowder's power gives the bullet extremely high speed. When a high-speed bullet encounters an obstacle and crashes into it fiercely, its speed changes dramatically at the moment of impact, creating an enormous instantaneous force between it and the obstacle. It is this instantaneous force that pierces the obstacle.
    If we reverse the above process, when an obstacle rushes towards a bullet at the same high speed, there is no need for a gun barrel at all. People only need to gently throw the bullet at the rushing object, and the same result will occur. In this case, even if what is thrown is not a bullet or projectile-like weapon, but a "peaceful" object like a pear or an apple, it will still have a destructive effect. Therefore, one must never throw things at oncoming speeding cars or trains, otherwise, it will have quite unpleasant consequences for the car or train and the passengers on board. Someone has calculated that if a four-kilogram watermelon is thrown at an oncoming car traveling at 120 kilometers per hour, its effect is almost equivalent to a ten-gram rifle bullet fired at a stationary object!
    High above the white clouds, eagles are often soaring, and occasionally the graceful figures of swans or cranes flit by.... But these high-flying birds are headaches for jet planes, unwelcome "visitors." Jet planes always try to avoid the "greeting" from these uninvited guests. We can imagine the regrettable accident caused when a swan weighing several kilograms slams into the cockpit of a plane traveling at 700 kilometers per hour, shattering the glass window! In this case, the jet plane is no different from being hit by a stray bullet. For this reason, the modern aviation industry has been manufacturing cabin glass that can withstand bird cannonballs. When testing the strength of this glass, in order to approximate the real situation, people thought of asking roosters for help. ...People wrapped the rooster in a silk bag and then placed it on the loading ram of a specially made cannon. When the rooster cannon fired with a loud bang, the brave and fierce rooster, subjected to twelve atmospheres of pressure, rushed towards the glass target at a speed of 725 kilometers per hour. Under the bombardment of the rooster cannonball test, people found a shatterproof glass that could withstand bird strikes. This glass has five layers and is as thick as 28.5 millimeters.

### 公鸡炮弹
王燮山
1959-11-09
第8版()
专栏：知识小品

    公鸡炮弹
    王燮山
    你知道子弹为什么能够透过墙壁、打穿钢板……吗？当火药的爆炸力把子弹推枪筒的时候，火药的力量使得子弹获得极高的速度。当高速度飞行的子弹遇到障碍，恶狠狠地向着障碍物撞去的时候，它的速度在碰撞的一瞬间，发生了很大的变化，使得它和障碍物之间产生极大的瞬时作用力，就是这个瞬时作用力把障碍物打穿。
    如果把上面的过程倒过来，障碍物以子弹那样高的速度朝着子弹跑过来的时候，这时根本就不需要枪筒，人们只要把子弹轻轻地朝着飞跑过来的物体一掷，就会产生同样的结果。在这种情况下，即使掷的不是子弹和弹丸之类的武器，而是像梨和苹果之类的“和平”物体，也同样会发生破坏的作用。因此切不可以向迎面疾驰的汽车或火车投掷东西，否则对于汽车或火车以及在这上面的旅客，都会产生相当不愉快的后果。有人计算过，如果向迎面开来的以每小时一百二十公里速度奔驰的汽车，投掷一个四公斤重的西瓜，那它的作用几乎相当于一颗向静止物体发射的十克重的步枪子弹！
    在高高的白云上面，时常有苍鹰在翱翔，有时还偶而掠过天鹅或仙鹤的倩影……。可是这些鸟类中的高飞者，却是喷气飞机头疼的“拜访者”，喷气飞机总是竭力避免这些不速之客的“碰头礼”。我们可以设想一下，当一只数公斤重的天鹅一头撞到速度为每小时七百公里的飞机的驾驶舱上，把玻璃窗撞得四分五裂，而引起的遗憾的事故吧！在这种情况，喷气飞机不啻于中了一颗飞来的流弹。为了这个缘故，近代航空工业一直在制造能够良好地抵挡飞禽炮弹的机舱玻璃。在试验这种玻璃的强度时，为了接近真实的情况，人们想到了请公鸡来帮忙。……人们把公鸡包装在一个用绸做的袋内，然后把它放在一架特制大炮的送弹塞上。当公鸡大炮一声巨响，好勇狠斗的公鸡受到十二个大气压力以每小时七百二十五公里的速度向着玻璃靶子冲去。在公鸡炮弹轰击的试验下，人们找到了一种能够抵挡飞禽撞击的防碎玻璃，这种玻璃有五层，厚度竟达二十八点五毫米。
