Title: Chinese Women's Delegation to the Soviet Union Returns to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 24th—The Chinese Women's Delegation to the Soviet Union, consisting of six members led by Qian Zhengying, returned to Beijing by plane this morning. The delegation went to the Soviet Union to attend the celebration of the 42nd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution and to conduct visits.

### 我妇女访苏代表团回京

1959-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    我妇女访苏代表团回京
    新华社24日讯　中国妇女访苏代表团一行六人在团长钱正英率领下，今天上午乘飞机回到北京。代表团是去苏联参加伟大的十月社会主义革命四十二周年庆祝典礼和进行参观访问的。
