Title: Two Writers from the German Democratic Republic Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, November 24th.  Manfred Haekel, Ph.D., and Erich Köhler, writers from the German Democratic Republic, who are visiting China according to the 1959 implementation plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 德意志民主共和国两位作家到京

1959-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国两位作家到京
    新华社24日讯　根据中德文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国访问的德意志民主共和国作家曼弗里特·海克尔博士和艾里希·柯勒，已于今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
