Title: Governor of the State Bank of Vietnam, Le Viet Luong, Visits Shanghai and Other Places
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 8th—At the invitation of Cao Juru, President of the People's Bank of China, Le Viet Luong, Governor of the State Bank of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, who is visiting China, left Beijing by plane today for Shanghai after a three-day visit to Beijing. He will visit Shanghai, Hangzhou, Wuhan and other places before returning home.

Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian met with Governor Le Viet Luong and had a cordial conversation with him.
The General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea Issues a Call to Be a Chollima Rider Under the Leadership of the Party, Speeding Towards a New Peak in Socialist Construction

This newspaper comprehensively reports that, according to Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang: The five-day Third National Congress of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea closed on the morning of the 6th.

At the meeting on the 6th, the new Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions, including 125 central committee members and 55 alternate central committee members, was elected. Lee Hyo-soon was elected as the chairman of the Central Committee, and five vice-chairmen were also elected.

After the election, Lee Hyo-soon delivered a closing speech. He said that the process of the congress proved the fact that the Korean working class is firmly united around the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and the congress will become a good beginning for improving and developing the work of the trade union in the future. He expressed his gratitude to the World Federation of Trade Unions and the trade union delegations of brotherly countries for attending the congress.

After all the representatives stood up and sang the Internationale in unison, the congress announced its closing.

On the 5th, the congress passed a resolution on the summary report of the work of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea and the new constitution of the Korean Trade Union.

The resolution adopted by the congress on the summary report made by Lee Hyo-soon, Chairman of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, stated that the congress approved the work carried out by the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea in the twelve years since the Second National Congress, and supported and approved the tasks put forward in the report.

The resolution emphasized that the "Chollima Work Team Movement" carried out by the working masses under the slogan of "working, studying and living in the spirit of communism" is of great significance for promoting socialist construction and helping workers prepare for communism. Trade union organizations at all levels should actively support, assist and further expand and develop this movement, making it a broad mass movement.

The resolution pointed out that conducting communist education for the working masses is the primary and most central task of the trade union at present; in addition, efforts should be made to improve the technical and cultural level of employees, carry out a movement to master new technologies and be proficient in technology, and implement the policy put forward by the Labor Party to enable all workers to master more than one technology, so as to improve the knowledge level of all employees to above primary or junior high school graduation level in the near future.

At the meeting on the 5th, Park Sang-hong, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, made a report on the revision of the trade union constitution. This report and the adopted revised new constitution emphasized that the lines, policies and constant leadership of the Korean Workers' Party are the unwavering guide for trade union activities. Therefore, all activities of the trade union must be subject to the implementation and execution of the Party's policies.

At the meeting on the 5th, a letter from the Congress to the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party was also passed, in which it was guaranteed that the Korean working class would be loyal to the Party's instructions, act as "Chollima" riders, and sprint towards a new peak in socialist construction.

In addition, a letter to the workers of South Korea was also passed, calling on the workers of South Korea to launch a struggle to force the US troops to withdraw from South Korea and realize the peaceful reunification of the motherland.

At the meetings on the 3rd and 4th, the heads of trade union delegations from Czechoslovakia, Mongolia, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Poland, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania and other countries delivered congratulatory messages to the congress. They expressed warm congratulations to the congress on behalf of the trade union organizations of their respective countries and expressed their willingness to strengthen friendship with Korean trade union organizations and the working class.

### 越南国家银行行长  黎日亮去上海等地访问

1959-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    越南国家银行行长
    黎日亮去上海等地访问
    据新华社8日讯　应中国人民银行行长曹菊如邀请来我国进行友好访问的越南民主共和国国家银行行长黎日亮，在北京进行三天访问后，今天乘飞机离开北京去上海。他将在上海、杭州、武汉等地访问后回国。
    国务院副总理李先念接见了黎日亮行长，并且同他进行了亲切的谈话。朝鲜职业总同盟代表大会发出号召在党的领导下做千里马骑手向社会主义建设新高峰奔驰
    本报综合报道　据新华社平壤消息：朝鲜职业总同盟历时五天的第三次全国代表大会在6日上午闭幕。
    在6日的会议上，选出了包括一百二十五名中央委员和五十五名候补中央委员的新的职业总同盟中央委员会，李孝淳被选为中央委员会委员长，另外选出了五名副委员长。
    选举结束后，李孝淳致闭幕词。他说，大会举行的过程证明，朝鲜工人阶级牢固地团结在朝鲜劳动党中央委员会周围的这一事实，大会将成为今后改进和发展职业同盟工作的良好开端。他对前来参加大会的世界工联和各兄弟国家的工会代表团表示感谢。
    在全体代表起立齐唱国际歌以后，大会宣告闭幕。
    5日，代表大会通过了关于朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会工作总结报告的决议和朝鲜职业同盟新章程。
    大会就朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会委员长李孝淳所作的总结报告通过的决议说，大会批准朝鲜职业总同盟自第二次全国代表大会以后十二年来所进行的工作，并且支持和赞成报告中提出的各项任务。
    决议强调说，劳动群众在“以共产主义的精神工作、学习和生活”的口号下所展开的“千里马作业班运动”，对促进社会主义建设、帮助职工为共产主义进行准备具有巨大意义。职业同盟各级组织要积极支持、帮助和进一步扩大和发展这一运动，使它成为广泛的群众运动。
    决议指出，对工人群众进行共产主义教育，是职业同盟当前首要的最中心的任务；另外，要努力提高职工的技术和文化水平，开展掌握新技术、精通技术的运动，贯彻劳动党提出的使所有工人掌握一种以上的技术、以便在不久的将来把全体职工的知识水平提高到小学或初中毕业程度以上的方针。
    在5日的会议上，朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会副委员长朴相洪作了关于修改职业同盟章程的报告，这个报告和通过的修改后的新章程强调说，朝鲜劳动党的路线、政策和经常的领导，是职业同盟活动的坚定不移的指南，因此，必须使职业同盟的一切活动服从于贯彻和执行党的政策。
    在5日的会议上还通过了大会致朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的信，信中保证，朝鲜工人阶级要忠实于党的指示，充当“千里马”骑手，向着社会主义建设的新高峰奔驰。
    此外，还通过了告南朝鲜工人书，号召南朝鲜工人展开斗争，迫使美军撤出南朝鲜，实现祖国的和平统一。
    在3日和4日的会议上，有捷克斯洛伐克、蒙古、越南民主共和国、波兰、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚等国的工会代表团团长向大会致祝词，他们代表各自国家的工会组织向大会热烈祝贺，并且表示要加强同朝鲜工会组织和工人阶级的友谊。
