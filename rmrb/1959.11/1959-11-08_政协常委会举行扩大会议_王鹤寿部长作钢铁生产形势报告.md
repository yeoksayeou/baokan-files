Title: CPPCC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting; Minister Wang Heshou Reports on Steel Production Situation
Author:
Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held its fourth enlarged meeting this morning at the CPPCC Auditorium in Beijing. Minister of Metallurgical Industry Wang Heshou delivered a report at the meeting on the current steel production situation. This report was conducted to coordinate CPPCC members, democratic parties, and non-party democratic figures in studying the documents of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC.

Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, presided over today's meeting. CPPCC members in Beijing, members of the central committees of various democratic parties, members of the Beijing Municipal CPPCC, and others attended the meeting. All members of the Henan Provincial CPPCC and Tangshan Municipal CPPCC visiting groups also attended the meeting to listen to the report.

### 政协常委会举行扩大会议  王鹤寿部长作钢铁生产形势报告

1959-11-08
第2版()
专栏：

    政协常委会举行扩大会议
    王鹤寿部长作钢铁生产形势报告
    新华社7日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会，今天上午在北京政协礼堂举行第四次扩大会议。冶金工业部部长王鹤寿在会议上作了目前钢铁生产形势的报告。这个报告是为了配合政协委员、各民主党派和无党派民主人士学习中共八届八中全会文件而进行的。
    政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通主持今天的会议，在京的政协委员、各民主党派中央委员、北京市政协委员等列席了会议。河南省政协、唐山市政协等参观团全体团员也到会听报告。
