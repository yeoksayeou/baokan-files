Title: The Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee and Council of Ministers Make Resolution: Scientific Research Should Be Closely Linked to Economic Development
Author:
Date: 1959-11-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, November 10th: The Bulgarian Telegraph Agency announced today the resolution of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee and the Bulgarian Council of Ministers on further developing science and raising the role of science in socialist construction.

    The resolution pointed out that the main and direct task facing Bulgarian science is to deeply explore major issues in socialist construction, the resolution of which will determine the accelerated development of the national economy. The resolution emphasized that the subject of scientific research should be closely linked to the development of industry and agriculture.

    In order to accomplish these tasks, the resolution stipulated a series of practical measures, including the merger of some branches of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences and the establishment of scientific research institutes in large industrial enterprises. The resolution also pointed out the main development directions of various scientific departments.

    The resolution emphasized that it is necessary to enhance the role of Party organizations in the life and work of scientific research institutions. The resolution believes that Party organizations should pay attention to improving the ideological and theoretical level of scientific research work, so as to concentrate the strength of scientific workers to comprehensively study the most important scientific problems and improve the ideological, political and professional level of scientific workers.

    This reporter learned from the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences that in the years since the establishment of the people's regime, Bulgaria has achieved great success in developing scientific undertakings and has created good conditions for its further development. Now, the number of scientific research institutes in Bulgaria has increased from twelve in 1944 to ninety-three. During the same period, the number of scientific workers in Bulgaria increased from one hundred and ninety-nine to more than five thousand, an increase of more than twenty times.

### 保共中央和部长会议作出决议  科学研究应密切联系经济发展

1959-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    保共中央和部长会议作出决议
    科学研究应密切联系经济发展
    新华社索非亚10日电　保加利亚通讯社今天发布了保加利亚共产党中央委员会和保加利亚部长会议关于进一步发展科学和提高科学在社会主义建设中的作用的决议。
    决议指出，保加利亚科学面临的主要的、直接的任务是，深入探讨社会主义建设中这样一些重大问题，这些问题的解决会决定国家经济的加速发展。决议强调指出，科学研究工作的主题应当与工农业的发展密切联系起来。
    为了完成这些任务，决议规定了一系列实际措施，其中包括合并保加利亚科学院的一些分院，在大工业企业中建立科学研究所等。决议还指出了各科学部门的主要发展方向。
    决议强调指出，必须提高党组织在科学研究机构的生活和工作中的作用。决议认为，党组织应当注意提高科学研究工作的思想水平和理论水平，以便集中科学工作者的力量来综合研究最重要的科学问题，提高科学工作者的思想、政治水平和业务水平。
    本社记者从保加利亚科学院获悉，在人民政权建立后的年代里，保加利亚在发展科学事业方面已经获得了很大成绩，并为它的进一步发展创造了良好条件。现在，保加利亚的科学研究所已从1944年的十二个增加到九十三个。在同一时期，保加利亚的科学工作者从一百九十九人增加到五千多人，即增加了二十多倍。
