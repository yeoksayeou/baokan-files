Title: Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation Head Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd - Mr. Kozol, head of the Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation and Vice Minister of the Czechoslovak Foreign Trade Ministry, and Mr. Nietolitcki, deputy head of the delegation and Director of the Asian Democratic Countries Department of the Czechoslovak Foreign Trade Ministry, who are here to discuss trade issues between China and Czechoslovakia for 1960, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon.

Those who went to welcome them included Mr. Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Mr. Jia Shi, Director of the Second Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

Mr. Ševčík, Commercial Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and others also went to the station to greet them.

### 捷政府贸易代表团团长到京

1959-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    捷政府贸易代表团团长到京
    新华社22日讯　前来我国商谈1960年中捷两国贸易问题的捷克斯洛伐克政府贸易代表团团长、捷对外贸易部副部长科佐尔和副团长、捷对外贸易部亚洲民主国家司司长涅托利茨基，已于今天下午乘火车抵达北京。
    前往欢迎的，有我对外贸易部副部长林海云，第二局局长贾石等人。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华商务参赞舍伏契克等也去车站迎接。
