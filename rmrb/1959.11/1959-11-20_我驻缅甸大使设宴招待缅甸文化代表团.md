Title: Chinese Ambassador to Burma Hosts Banquet for Burmese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-11-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 18th – Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Li Yimang, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of Burmese Minister of Culture, U Chit Thoung, and the members of the Burmese Cultural Friendship Delegation he led, upon their return from a recent visit to China.

Among those attending the banquet were James Barrington, Permanent Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, senior officials from the Ministry of Culture, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ministry of Information, Dr. Htin Aung, Chairman of the Burma-China Friendship Association, prominent figures from the Burmese cultural circles, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Rangoon.

At the banquet, Ambassador Li Yimang, on behalf of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, presented two Chinese paintings to the Burmese Ministry of Culture. Burmese Minister of Culture, U Chit Thoung, accepted the paintings and expressed his gratitude on behalf of the Burmese government.

The banquet was held in a warm and cordial atmosphere.

### 我驻缅甸大使设宴招待缅甸文化代表团

1959-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    我驻缅甸大使设宴招待缅甸文化代表团
    新华社仰光18日电　中国驻缅甸大使李一氓今晚举行宴会，招待最近访问中国后归国的缅甸文化部长吴漆东和由他率领的缅甸文化友好代表团的团员。
    参加宴会的有缅甸外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿，文化部、外交部和宣传部的高级官员，缅中友好协会主席信波博士，缅甸文化界著名人士和一些国家驻仰光的外交使节。
    在宴会上，李一氓大使代表中国人民对外文化协会向缅甸文化部赠送了两幅中国画。缅甸文化部长吴漆东接受了图画，并且代表缅甸政府表示感谢。
    宴会是在烈热融洽的气氛中进行的。
