Title: Small Rolling Mills Display Their Power

Author: 
Date: 1959-11-10
Page: 3

Small Rolling Mills Display Their Power

    The three rolling workshops of the Guangzhou City Jiehe Steel Plant have recently all achieved rolling mill encirclement, enabling small rolling mills to exert greater power, doubling output, and saving more than half of the labor force.
    This year, this factory has honorably undertaken more than half of the steel production tasks in Guangzhou.

### 小轧钢机发挥威力

1959-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

    小轧钢机发挥威力
    广州市捷和钢铁厂的三个轧钢车间最近全部实现了轧机围盘化，使小轧钢机发挥出更大威力，产量成倍地增长，劳动力节省一半以上。
    今年，这个厂已经光荣地担负起广州市一半以上的钢材生产任务。
