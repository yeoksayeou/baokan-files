Title: Inspire Fighting Will, Encourage Construction Enthusiasm: Soviet Film Week Grandly Opens
Author:
Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, November 5th:

    To celebrate the forty-second anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the Soviet Film Week grandly opened in Beijing at 3 p.m. today.

    The Soviet Film Week will begin on November 6th in twenty-nine large and medium-sized cities across the country, featuring the latest translated Soviet films "Song of the Sea," "In the Days of October," "I Know Him," "Volunteers," "Youth," "The Fate of a Man," "Kiev Girl" (Parts 1 and 2), "The Sky is Calling," and an anthology of five animated films.

    The Soviet Film Week is jointly sponsored by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Attending today's opening ceremony were Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premier of the State Council; Antonov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China; Sudarikov, Cultural Counselor; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Shao Lizi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Gao Ge, Director of the Beijing Film Bureau, and more than 1,200 people from the cultural circles of the capital. Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to China also attended the opening ceremony.

    At today's opening ceremony, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing said in his speech: "We Chinese people celebrate the great and glorious festival of the October Socialist Revolution with great excitement, enthusiasm, and inspiration every year because it has opened a new era in the development of human history, and it is the greatest festival in human history. The brilliant victories and great achievements of our socialist construction are inseparable from the selfless assistance given to the Chinese people by our great ally, the Soviet Union."

    He said that Soviet films are always familiar to the Chinese people. Especially since the founding of New China, Soviet films have become our good teachers and helpful friends, deeply loved by the Chinese people. Soviet films have played a significant role in increasing our knowledge, inspiring our fighting will, and encouraging our enthusiasm for construction. We firmly believe that the screening of this Soviet Film Week will have a tremendous impact on the Chinese people in learning from the valuable experience of the Soviet people in building socialism, on developing cultural exchanges between our two countries, and on enhancing the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Soviet peoples.

    Soviet Embassy Cultural Counselor Sudarikov said at the meeting: The Soviet Film Week, which begins today, is one of the many important cultural activities organized by Chinese friends and is a politically significant event proving the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the Soviet Union and China. He also emphasized the excellent results of the continuously developing Soviet-Chinese cultural cooperation. In the last two years alone, five large films have been produced through the joint efforts of Soviet and Chinese film workers. Finally, he congratulated the increasing consolidation and development of Soviet-Chinese cultural cooperation and the unbreakable unity and friendship between the peoples of the Soviet Union and China.

    After the meeting, the film "In the Days of October," which is participating in the Soviet Film Week, was shown.

### 激发战斗意志  鼓舞建设热情  苏联电影周隆重开幕

1959-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

    激发战斗意志  鼓舞建设热情
    苏联电影周隆重开幕
    新华社5日讯　为庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十二周年举办的苏联电影周，今天下午三时在北京举行了隆重的开幕式。
    苏联电影周将从11月6日开始在全国二十九个大中城市上演最新译制的苏联影片“海之歌”、“在十月的日子里”、“我了解他”、“志愿者”、“青年时代”、“一个人的遭遇”、“基辅姑娘”（上下集）、“天空在召唤”和由五部美术片组成的美术片集锦。
    苏联电影周由中华人民共和国文化部、对外文化联络委员会和中苏友好协会总会联合举办。参加今天开幕式的有，国务院副总理习仲勋，苏联驻华大使馆临时代办安东诺夫、文化参赞苏达利柯夫，文化部部长沈雁冰、对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若、中苏友好协会总会副会长邵力子、外交部副部长曾涌泉、北京市电影局局长高戈和首都文化界人士一千二百多人。各国驻华使节和外交官员也参加了开幕式。
    在今天的开幕式上，文化部部长沈雁冰致词说：“我们中国人民，年年都怀着十分激动、兴奋、鼓舞的心情庆祝十月社会主义革命这个伟大光荣的节日，因为它开辟了人类历史发展的新时代，它是人类历史上一个最伟大的节日。我国的社会主义建设能够取得如此光辉的胜利和巨大成就，是同我们伟大的盟邦苏联所给予中国人民的无私援助分不开的。”
    他说，苏联电影是中国人民一向感到亲切的，特别是从新中国建立以来，苏联电影已经成了我们的良师益友，深为中国人民所喜爱。苏联电影起了增进我们的知识、激发我们战斗意志、鼓舞我们建设热情的重大作用。我们深信，通过这次苏联电影周的映出，对中国人民学习苏联人民建设社会主义的宝贵经验，对发展我们两国的文化交流，对增进中苏两国人民的友谊团结将起巨大的作用。
    苏联驻华大使馆文化参赞苏达利柯夫在会上讲话说：今天开始的苏联电影周，是中国朋友所组织的许多重要文化活动之一，也是证明苏中两国牢不可破的友谊和合作的具有重要政治意义的事件。他还着重指出逐年不断发展的苏中文化合作的优秀成果，仅最近两年，在苏中电影工作人员合作努力之下，就产生了五部大型电影片。最后他祝贺苏中文化合作的日益巩固、发展和苏中两国人民牢不可破的团结和友谊。
    会后，放映了参加苏联电影周的影片“在十月的日子里”。
