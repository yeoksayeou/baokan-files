Title: Burmese Cultural Friendship Delegation Leaves Kunming for Home
Author:
Date: 1959-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 10th. The Burmese Cultural Friendship Delegation of five people led by U Chit Thoung, the Burmese Government Representative, Minister of Industry, Mines, Culture, and Labour, concluded their visit to our country and left Kunming by plane this morning to return home. Liu Zhuofu, Deputy Governor of Yunnan Province, and people from cultural circles in Yunnan Province and Kunming City saw them off at the airport. Also at the airport to see them off were Wu La Thon, Deputy Consul of the Burmese Consulate General in Kunming, and Wu Wenxiang, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming.

On the evening of the 9th, Yu Yichuan, Governor of Yunnan Province, hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the guests. At the banquet, head of the delegation U Chit Thoung once again expressed his belief that the visit of the Cultural Friendship Delegation will further consolidate and develop the friendship between the people of Burma and China. He said: Your construction work has left a deep impression on me.

### 缅甸文化友好代表团离昆明回国

1959-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化友好代表团离昆明回国
    新华社昆明10日电　由缅甸政府代表、缅甸工业、矿务、文化和劳工部长吴漆东率领的缅甸文化友好代表团一行五人，结束了在我国的访问，今日上午乘飞机离开昆明回国。云南省副省长刘卓甫及云南省、昆明市文化界人士到机场送行。到机场送行的还有缅甸驻昆明总领事馆副领事吴拉吞和越南民主共和国驻昆明领事武文享。
    9日晚，云南省省长于一川曾设宴为客人们饯行。在宴会上，吴漆东团长再一次表示，相信通过文化友好代表团的访问，将使缅中两国人民之间的友谊进一步得到巩固和发展。他说：你们的建设事业给我留下了深刻的印象。
