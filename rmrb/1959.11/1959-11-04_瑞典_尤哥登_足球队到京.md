Title: Swedish "Djurgården" Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd—At the invitation of the All-China Sports Federation, the first group of eighteen members of the Swedish football champion of this year, the Swedish "Djurgården" football team, led by Sigvard Berg, director of the football department of the "Djurgården" Sports Club, arrived in Beijing by plane today. Tomorrow they will play their first friendly match in China with the Tianjin football team in Beijing.

Li Fenglou, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Football Association, and Chinese football players welcomed this first Swedish sports team invited to visit our country at the airport. Shortly after arriving today, the "Djurgården" football team members practiced at the Xianongtan Stadium. The second group of four members will arrive within the next few days.

The Swedish "Djurgården" football team will also play a friendly match with the Beijing football team in Beijing on the 5th. After that, they will visit and compete in Shanghai and Guangzhou.

### 瑞典“尤哥登”足球队到京

1959-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    瑞典“尤哥登”足球队到京
    据新华社3日讯　应中华全国体育总会邀请来我国访问的瑞典今年的足球冠军——瑞典“尤哥登”足球队第一批人员十八人，今天由“尤哥登”体育俱乐部足球部主任西格瓦德·贝尔格率领乘飞机到达北京。他们明天将在北京同天津足球队举行访华第一场友谊比赛。
    中国足球协会副主席李凤楼和我国足球运动员们到飞机场欢迎了这批第一次应邀来我国访问的瑞典体育运动队伍。“尤哥登”足球队队员今天到达后不久，就到先农坛体育场进行了练习。他们的第二批人员四人将在日内到达。
  瑞典“尤哥登”足球队5日还将在北京同北京足球队举行友谊比赛。以后，他们将到上海、广州访问比赛。
