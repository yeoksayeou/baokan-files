Title: Chinese Singer Zheng Xingli Sings for Australian Workers
Author:
Date: 1959-11-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 19th dispatch from Sydney: Australian dockworkers in Sydney crowded into their union hall today to enjoy the performance of Chinese singer Zheng Xingli and Indian dancer Indrani Rahman. The two artists are in Sydney to attend the Australian and New Zealand International Cooperation and Disarmament Conference.

The dockworkers rushed from various parts of Sydney Harbor during their lunch break to attend the concert.

Zheng Xingli told the Australian workers that she brought greetings from the Chinese workers.

### 我国歌唱家郑兴丽  为澳大利亚工人演唱

1959-11-21
第5版()
专栏：

    我国歌唱家郑兴丽
    为澳大利亚工人演唱
    新华社19日讯　悉尼消息：澳大利亚悉尼码头工人今天满满地拥挤在他们的工会大厅里，欣赏中国歌唱家郑兴丽的演唱和印度舞蹈家印德拉尼·拉赫曼的表演。这两位艺术家是到悉尼参加澳大利亚、新西兰国际合作和裁军会议的。
    码头工人们在午饭时分从悉尼港口的各个部分赶去参加音乐会。
    郑兴丽向澳大利亚工人们说，她带来了中国工人的问候。
