Title: Vice Premier Lu Dingyi Receives Chilean News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th: Vice Premier Lu Dingyi received this morning the Chilean News Workers Delegation, headed by Juan Emilio Pacull, and delegation members: Genaro Medina Vera, Adriana Zearral, and Max Nolff.

Present at the reception were Wang Yi, Standing Director of the All-China Journalists Association, Li Bingquan, Deputy Secretary-General, and Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 陆定一副总理  接见智利新闻代表团

1959-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

    陆定一副总理
    接见智利新闻代表团
    新华社5日讯　陆定一副总理今天上午接见了智利新闻工作者代表团团长胡安·埃米略·帕库耳，代表团团员：赫纳罗·梅迪纳·贝拉，阿德里亚娜·塞亚尔莱，马克斯·诺耳夫。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会常务理事王揖、副秘书长李炳泉，外交部新闻司副司长康矛召。
