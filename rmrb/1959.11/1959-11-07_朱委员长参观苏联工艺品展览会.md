Title: Chairman Zhu De Visits the Soviet Arts and Crafts Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1959-11-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Chairman Zhu De visited the Soviet National Applied Art and Folk Arts and Crafts Exhibition at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace this morning. The exhibition opened on the 3rd. The exhibition features more than 700 pieces of art and crafts, which were sent by the Soviet government to our country for exhibition to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country. The Soviet government has presented these exhibits to our country as gifts.

### 朱委员长参观苏联工艺品展览会

1959-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    朱委员长参观苏联工艺品展览会
    新华社6日讯　朱德委员长今天上午到北京市劳动人民文化宫参观了苏联各民族实用艺术和民间工艺品展览会。这个展览会是3日开幕的。展览会上展出的七百多件艺术品和工艺品，是苏联政府为庆祝我国建国十周年送来我国展览的。苏联政府已将这批展品作为礼物赠送我国。
