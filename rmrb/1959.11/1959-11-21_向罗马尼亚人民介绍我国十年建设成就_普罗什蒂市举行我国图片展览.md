Title: Introducing Our Country's Ten Years of Construction Achievements to the Romanian People: Our Country's Photo Exhibition Held in Ploiești City
Author:
Date: 1959-11-21
Page: 5

    Introducing Our Country's Ten Years of Construction Achievements to the Romanian People
    Our Country's Photo Exhibition Held in Ploiești City
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 20th - The "China's Ten Years of Construction Achievements Photo Exhibition" opened on the 19th in Ploiești, Romania's famous oil city.
    The Vice Chairman of the Ploiești City People's Council Executive Committee, Vladescu, spoke at the opening ceremony, warmly praising our country's achievements in the Great Leap Forward of 1958 and the continued leap forward this year.
    Wang Luming, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of our country's embassy in Romania, also attended the opening ceremony.

### 向罗马尼亚人民介绍我国十年建设成就  普罗什蒂市举行我国图片展览

1959-11-21
第5版()
专栏：

    向罗马尼亚人民介绍我国十年建设成就
    普罗什蒂市举行我国图片展览
    新华社布加勒斯特20日电  “中国十年建设成就图片展览会”19日在罗马尼亚著名的石油城普罗什蒂开幕。
    普罗什蒂市人民会议执行委员会副主席弗拉德斯库在开幕式上讲话，热烈赞扬了我国1958年大跃进和今年继续跃进的成就。
    我国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办王鲁明也出席了开幕式。
