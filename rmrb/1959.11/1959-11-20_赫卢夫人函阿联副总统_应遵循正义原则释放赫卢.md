Title: Mrs. Helou Writes to UAR Vice President: Should Follow Principles of Justice to Release Helou
Author:
Date: 1959-11-20
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, November 17th: According to a report in the Lebanese "Call" newspaper today, Virginie Helou, the wife of Lebanese Communist leader Helou, wrote a letter to UAR Vice President Amer, requesting the release of her husband.

According to reports, Mrs. Helou requested in the letter that Amer follow the principles of justice in this matter.

The letter reviewed the circumstances of Helou's arrest by UAR authorities in Damascus on June 25th of this year. The letter pointed out that there is a person named Assaf Mansour in a prison in the Syrian region, which has not been denied, and this person is precisely her husband, Helou.

### 赫卢夫人函阿联副总统  应遵循正义原则释放赫卢

1959-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    赫卢夫人函阿联副总统
    应遵循正义原则释放赫卢
    新华社贝鲁特17日电　据黎巴嫩“呼声报”今天报道，黎巴嫩共产党领袖赫卢夫人维尔吉妮·赫卢写信给阿联副总统阿密尔，要求释放她的丈夫。
    据报道，赫卢夫人在信中要求阿密尔在这个问题上遵循正义原则。
    信中回顾了赫卢在今年6月25日在大马士革被阿联当局逮捕的情况。信中指出，在叙利亚地区的监狱中有一个名叫阿萨夫·曼苏尔的人，这件事并没有遭到否认，这个人正是她的丈夫赫卢。
