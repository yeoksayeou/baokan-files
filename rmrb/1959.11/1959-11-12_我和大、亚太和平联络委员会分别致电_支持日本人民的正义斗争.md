Title: I and the All-China and Asia-Pacific Peace Liaison Committee Sent Telegrams Respectively Supporting the Just Struggle of the Japanese People
Author:
Date: 1959-11-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 10th. The China Peace Committee sent a telegram to the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs on November 9th, congratulating them on the convening of the central rally against the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty." The text of the telegram is as follows: Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs:

    On the occasion of your council's central rally against the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty," we extend our warm congratulations and express our heartfelt respect to the Japanese people who are persevering in their struggle for independence, democracy, peace, and neutrality.

    U.S. imperialism and Japanese reactionaries, in order to strengthen the U.S.-Japan military alliance and accelerate the militarization of Japan, turning Japan into a U.S. military base for nuclear war, are intensifying their activities to revise the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty" in order to further carry out economic expansion and war aggression schemes in Asia. This not only puts the Japanese people in danger of being dragged into war again and suffering profound disasters but also directly threatens peace in Asia and the world.

    At present, the struggle of the people from all walks of life in Japan demanding the abolition of the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty" and the cessation of the revival of Japanese militarism is developing more and more widely. The Chinese people fully sympathize with and firmly support this just struggle of the Japanese people. We are ready to unite with the Japanese people and strive together to safeguard peace in Asia and the world. We firmly believe that the struggle of the Japanese people, through continuous efforts, will surely achieve final victory. We wish the conference success!
    
    China Peace Committee
            November 9, 1959
    
Xinhua News Agency, November 10th. The Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee today sent a telegram to the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, congratulating them on the convening of the central rally against the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty." The text of the telegram is as follows:

    Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs:

    The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee wholeheartedly congratulates the convening of the central rally against the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty" and expresses its respect to your council and the Japanese people.

    The people of all countries in the Asian and Pacific regions and peace-loving people all over the world strongly demand the elimination of the Cold War, oppose the threat of war, and safeguard world peace. However, the U.S. and Japanese governments are now stepping up the revision of the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty" to intensify international tensions and carry out aggressive activities, seriously threatening peace in Asia and the world. We support the just struggle of the Japanese people against the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty" and in defense of peace, and wish your council great success.

    The Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee
              November 10, 1959

### 我和大、亚太和平联络委员会分别致电  支持日本人民的正义斗争

1959-11-12
第6版()
专栏：

    我和大、亚太和平联络委员会分别致电
    支持日本人民的正义斗争
    新华社10日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会9日打电报给日本禁止原子弹氢弹协议会，祝贺反对日美“安全条约”中央集会的召开。电文如下：日本禁止原子弹氢弹协议会：
    值此贵协议会召开反对日美“安全条约”中央集会之际，谨致热烈的祝贺，并向为独立、民主、和平、中立而坚持奋斗的日本人民表示衷心的敬意。
    美帝国主义和日本反动势力为了加强美日军事同盟，加速日本军国主义化。变日本为美国进行核战争的军事基地，以进一步在亚洲进行经济扩张和战争侵略阴谋，正加紧进行修改日美“安全条约”的活动。这不仅使日本人民面临被再一次拖入战争和遭受深重灾难的危险，而且也直接威胁着亚洲和世界的和平。
    现在，日本各界人民要求废除日美“安全条约”和制止复活日本军国主义的斗争，正日益广泛和深入地发展。中国人民完全同情和坚决支持日本人民这一正义斗争。我们愿同日本人民团结一起，为保卫亚洲和世界和平共同奋斗。我们深信，日本人民的斗争经过不断的努力，必将获得最后的胜利。敬祝大会成功！
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
            1959年11月9日
    新华社10日讯　亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处今天打电报给日本禁止原子弹氢弹协议会，祝贺反对日美“安全条约”中央集会的召开。电文如下：
    日本禁止原子弹氢弹协议会：
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会衷心祝贺反对日美“安全条约”中央集会的召开，并向贵协议会和日本人民表示敬意。
    亚洲及太平洋区域各国人民和全世界爱好和平的人民，强烈要求消除冷战，反对战争威胁和维护世界和平。但是，美国和日本政府现正在加紧修改美日“安全条约”，以加剧国际紧张局势和进行侵略活动，严重威胁亚洲和世界和平。我们支持日本人民反对日美“安全条约”和保卫和平的正义斗争，并祝贵协议会获得重大成功。
    亚洲及太平洋区域
    和平联络委员会秘书处
              1959年11月10日
