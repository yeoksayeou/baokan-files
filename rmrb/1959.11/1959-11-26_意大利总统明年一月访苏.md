Title: Italian President to Visit Soviet Union Next January
Author:
Date: 1959-11-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 24th: TASS reported on the 24th that the President of the Italian Republic, Giovanni Gronchi, has gladly accepted the invitation of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and will arrive in Moscow on January 8, 1960, for an official visit of about one week.

In addition to meeting with Soviet state leaders in Moscow, the President will also visit Leningrad.

Foreign Minister Giuseppe Pella will accompany President Gronchi on his visit to the Soviet Union.

### 意大利总统明年一月访苏

1959-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    意大利总统明年一月访苏
    新华社莫斯科24日电　据塔斯社24日讯：意大利共和国总统乔瓦尼·格隆基欣然接受了苏联最高苏维埃主席团的邀请，将于1960年1月8日抵达莫斯科作为期一星期左右的正式访问。
    总统除了在莫斯科和苏联国家领导人会晤以外，还将访问列宁格勒。
    陪同格隆基总统访问苏联的将有外交部部长朱塞佩·贝拉。
