Title: Soviet Defense Minister Issues Order: Maintain High Vigilance, Defend Peaceful Labor
Author:
Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 7th. TASS reports: Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Malinovsky issued an order on the occasion of the 42nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, stating that the Soviet working people are celebrating this festival amidst the comprehensive development of communist construction in the Soviet Union and the further consolidation of the strength of the world socialist system.

The order points out that Khrushchev's historic visit to the United States is justly regarded by world public opinion as the most important step in easing international tension, eliminating the "Cold War," and consolidating universal peace.

The order says that, at the same time, it cannot be ignored that there are still imperialist forces in the world that oppose the normalization of the international situation. They are unwilling to give up the arms race and active war preparations against the Soviet Union and other socialist countries. This requires the Soviet armed forces to maintain a high degree of vigilance and constant readiness in the future to defend the peaceful and creative labor of the Soviet people and the national interests of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联国防部长发布命令  保持高度警惕　捍卫和平劳动

1959-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部长发布命令
    保持高度警惕　捍卫和平劳动
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科7日讯：苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅在庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十二周年的时候发布命令说，苏联劳动人民是在苏联全面展开共产主义建设和世界社会主义体系的实力进一步巩固的情况下来庆祝这个节日的。
    命令指出，赫鲁晓夫的具有历史意义的访美之行被世界公众公正地认为是缓和国际紧张局势、消除“冷战”、巩固普遍和平的最重要的步骤。
    命令说，同时，不能不注意到，在世界上还存在着反对使国际局势正常化的帝国主义势力，它们不肯放弃军备竞赛和反对苏联以及其他社会主义国家的积极备战勾当。这就使苏联的武装力量在今后仍然必须保持高度的警惕性和经常的戒备，以保卫苏联人民和平的创造性的劳动和苏联的国家利益。
