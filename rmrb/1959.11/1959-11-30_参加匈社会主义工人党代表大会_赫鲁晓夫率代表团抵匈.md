Title: Khrushchev Leads Delegation to Hungary to Attend Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-11-30
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, November 29th: The Soviet Communist Party delegation, led by Khrushchev, arrived in Budapest this afternoon at the invitation to attend the Seventh Congress of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party.
    The delegation members include: Kirilenko, Alternate Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and First Secretary of the Sverdlovsk Oblast Committee; Demichev, First Secretary of the Moscow Oblast Committee; Kazanets, First Secretary of the Stalino Oblast Committee; Naumova, Second Secretary of the Leningrad Oblast Committee; and Shtikov, Soviet Ambassador to Hungary.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party leaders Kádár, Münnich, Marosán, Fock, and Kállai, among others.

### 参加匈社会主义工人党代表大会  赫鲁晓夫率代表团抵匈

1959-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    参加匈社会主义工人党代表大会
    赫鲁晓夫率代表团抵匈
    新华社布达佩斯29日电　应邀前来参加匈牙利社会主义工人党第七次代表大会的苏联共产党代表团，在赫鲁晓夫的率领下，今天下午到达布达佩斯。
    代表团团员有：苏共中央主席团候补委员、斯维德洛夫斯克州委员会第一书记基里连科，莫斯科州委员会第一书记杰米契夫，斯大林诺州委员会第一书记卡扎涅次，列宁格勒州委员会第二书记瑙莫娃，苏联驻匈牙利大使什蒂科夫。
    到机场欢迎的有匈牙利社会主义工人党领导人卡达尔、明尼赫、马罗山、福克和卡拉伊等。
