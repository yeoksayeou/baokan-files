Title: Panchen Lama, etc., Depart from Baotou for Beijing After Concluding Their Visit to Inner Mongolia

Author:
Date: 1959-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baotou, November 29th: Panchen Erdeni Choskyi Gyaltsen, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Acting Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Pabala Gelleg Namgyal, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, accompanied by Wang Feng, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, and Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, concluded their visit to Hohhot and Baotou in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. They departed from Baotou by special train at 5:30 PM today for Beijing.

Su Qianyi, Secretary of the CPC Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee and First Secretary of the CPC Baotou Municipal Committee, Li Zhi, Secretary of the CPC Baotou Municipal Committee and Mayor of Baotou, An Enda, Vice-Chairman of the Baotou Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, Chen Shouzhong, Secretary of the Baogang Party Committee, and other leaders, along with more than 200 people from all ethnic groups and various sectors, saw them off at the station.

Panchen Erdeni and Pabala and their entourage arrived in Hohhot on the 23rd and visited the Hohhot No. 1 Wool Textile Factory, the Inner Mongolia College of Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Medicine, Inner Mongolia University, and the Hohhot Xinsheng Steel Plant. Panchen Erdeni also attended a Dharma assembly and received worship from Buddhist believers. After arriving in Baotou on the 26th, they visited the Baogang No. 1 Blast Furnace, the Coking Plant, the Bayan Obo Mining Area, and a nearby people's commune in a pastoral area. Panchen Erdeni and Pabala also attended banquets held by the Party and government leading organs in Hohhot and Baotou. At the banquet held by the Party and government leading organs of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Panchen Erdeni said, "In Hohhot, we personally witnessed the unity of the Mongolian people and other ethnic groups in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, fighting shoulder to shoulder under the wise leadership of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, and under the brilliant light of the Party's ethnic policies and the General Line, achieving brilliant achievements in industry, agriculture, animal husbandry, culture, education, health and other fields. From these great achievements of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, we also see the bright future of the Tibetan people."

### 结束了在内蒙古的参观访问  班禅等离包头回北京

1959-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    结束了在内蒙古的参观访问
    班禅等离包头回北京
    新华社包头29日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和政协全国委员会副主席帕巴拉·卓列朗杰，在中共中央统战部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋和中共西藏工委副书记周仁山陪同下，结束了在内蒙古自治区呼和浩特和包头市的参观访问，今天下午五时三十分由包头乘专车去北京。
    中共内蒙古自治区委员会书记、中共包头市委第一书记苏谦益，中共包头市委书记、包头市市长李质，政协包头市委员会副主席安恩达，包钢党委书记陈守中等负责人和各族各界二百多人到车站欢送。
    班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉等一行在23日到达呼和浩特，先后参观访问了呼和浩特第一毛纺织厂、内蒙古畜牧兽医学院、内蒙古大学和呼和浩特新生钢铁厂。班禅额尔德尼并出席了一次法会，接受了佛教信徒的朝拜。他们在26日到达包头后，参观访问了包钢一号高炉、焦化厂、白云鄂博矿山及附近的一个牧区人民公社。班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉等在呼和浩特和包头期间，还出席了两地党政领导机关举行的宴会。班禅额尔德尼在内蒙古自治区党政领导机关举行的宴会上讲话说，“我们在呼和浩特亲眼看到内蒙古自治区的蒙古族人民和其他各族人民团结得像一个人一样，在党中央和毛主席的英明领导下，在党的民族政策和总路线的光辉照耀下，肩并肩地战斗，在工业、农业、牧业、文教卫生事业等各方面都取得了辉煌的成就。我们从内蒙古自治区的这些伟大成就中，也看到了西藏人民的美好前景。”
