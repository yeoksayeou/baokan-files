Title: In Praise of the Public Canteen
Author: Tang Yushu
Date: 1959-11-21
Page: 8

    In Praise of the Public Canteen
    Tang Yushu, Shanghai Liberation Commune

    Holding up my bowl, I'm filled with joy,
    Each grain of rice, a fragrant pearl,
    Warm soup, warm rice, warms the heart,
    My heart is filled with gratitude to the Communist Party.

### 赞公共食堂
唐玉书
1959-11-21
第8版()
专栏：

    赞公共食堂
    上海解放公社　唐玉书
    捧起饭碗喜洋洋，
    粒粒珍珠粒粒香，
    暖汤暖饭暖人心，
