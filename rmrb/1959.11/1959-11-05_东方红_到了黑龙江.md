Title: “Dongfanghong” Arrives in Heilongjiang
Author:
Date: 1959-11-05
Page: 3

    “Dongfanghong” Arrives in Heilongjiang
    Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, November 4th. The first batch of thirteen domestically produced "Dongfanghong" brand tractors has arrived in Heilongjiang Province and will soon be transported to rural areas to participate in autumn plowing operations.
    The news of the completion of the First Tractor Manufacturing Plant and the arrival of the first batch of domestically produced tractors in Heilongjiang Province had already spread in the rural areas of Heilongjiang Province. It gave great encouragement to the farmers of the province. The farmers said happily: There are now better conditions for achieving mechanization. While carrying out the intense autumn harvest, the people's communes are actively raising funds, training machine耕人才 [jigeng rencai - mechanized farming personnel], repairing bridges and roads, planning land, and expanding the area of ​​autumn plowing in preparation for further developing agricultural mechanization next year.

### “东方红”到了黑龙江

1959-11-05
第3版()
专栏：

    “东方红”到了黑龙江
    新华社哈尔滨4日电　首批十三台国产“东方红”牌拖拉机已经到达黑龙江省，即将运往农村参加秋耕作业。
    第一拖拉机制造厂建成和首批国产拖拉机运到黑龙江省的消息早就在黑龙江省农村中传开。它给予全省农民很大的鼓舞。农民们兴高采烈地说：实现机械化更有条件了。在紧张秋收的同时，各人民公社正在积极筹备资金、训练机耕人材、修桥补路、规划土地、扩大秋翻地面积，为明年进一步发展农业机械化做好准备。
