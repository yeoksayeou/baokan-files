Title: Mongolian Economic Delegation Departs Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1959-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 28th: The Mongolian People's Republic economic delegation, led by Tumenbayar Alagach, First Deputy Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the Mongolian People's Republic, concluded its visit to China and left Beijing by train this afternoon for home.

    Those who saw them off at the station included Gu Zhuoxin and Wang Guangwei, Vice Chairmen of the State Planning Committee, Deng Jie, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Light Industry, Li Qiangfen, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and leading members of other relevant departments of the Central Government.

    Sharab, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, and staff members of the Embassy also went to the station to see them off.

### 蒙古经济考察团离京回国

1959-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古经济考察团离京回国
    据新华社28日讯　由蒙古人民共和国国家计划委员会第一副主席图门巴雅尔·阿拉嘎查率领的蒙古人民共和国经济考察团，结束了在中国的参观访问，今天下午乘火车离开北京回国。
    到车站欢送的，有国家计划委员会副主任顾卓新、王光伟，轻工业部副部长邓洁，外交部第二亚洲司副司长李强奋，以及中央其他有关部门的负责人。
    蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使沙拉布和大使馆人员也到车站送行。
