Title: The CPSU Central Committee to Convene Plenary Session Next Month to Discuss Further Development of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry
Author:
Date: 1959-11-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 11th: TASS, Moscow, November 10th, reported: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union passed a resolution to convene a regular plenary session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on December 22, 1959.

The plenary session will discuss the following issues: measures regarding the implementation of the resolutions of the 21st Congress of the CPSU and the December 1958 Plenary Session of the CPSU Central Committee on developing agriculture and increasing the production of grain and other agricultural products; measures regarding the resolution on developing public animal husbandry; measures to further mechanize agriculture, consolidate the collectively owned economy of collective farms, and improve the material welfare of collective farmers, etc.

The CPSU Central Committee decided that the plenary session will hear reports on these issues from the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation and the Central Committees of the Communist Parties of Ukraine, Kazakhstan, Belarus, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, and Azerbaijan.

### 讨论进一步发展农牧业等问题  苏共中央下月召开全会

1959-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    讨论进一步发展农牧业等问题
    苏共中央下月召开全会
    新华社莫斯科11日电　据塔斯社莫斯科10日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会通过决议，决定在1959年12月22日召开苏联共产党中央委员会例行全体会议。
    全会将讨论下列问题：关于执行苏共第二十一次代表大会和苏共中央1958年12月全会有关发展农业和增加谷物以及其他农产品产量的决议的措施问题，关于发展公共畜牧业的决议的措施问题，关于进一步使农业机械化、巩固集体农庄公有经济和提高集体农民物质福利的措施问题等。
    苏共中央决定，全会将听取俄罗斯联邦部长会议和乌克兰、哈萨克、白俄罗斯、乌兹别克、塔吉克、阿塞拜疆共产党中央委员会就这些问题作的报告。
