    Speeding Up the Construction of the Beijing-Chengde Railway

    The Beijing-Chengde Railway, connecting Beijing and Chengde, will soon be open to traffic along its entire length. Currently, the railway construction workers are laying tracks and building bridges on the final thirty-plus kilometers of the line between Miyun and Xinglong.

专栏：

    赶修京承铁路
    连接北京—承德的京承铁路，不久将全线通车。目前，筑路职工们正在这条铁路的密云、兴隆间最后三十多公里的线路上铺轨架桥。
