Title: Amateur School Established
Author: Li Cheng
Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 4

    Amateur schools are established,
    Cultural revolution flowers bloom,
    Amateur schools are established,
    Cadres are nurtured everywhere,
    Both red and expert, with talent in both civil and military affairs.
        (Li Cheng, Fuyang Township, Guangdong)

### 业余学校建起来
李成
1959-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    业余学校建起来
    文化革命鲜花开，
    业余学校建起来，
    干部人人得培养，
    又红又专文武才。
        （广东浮洋乡　李成）
