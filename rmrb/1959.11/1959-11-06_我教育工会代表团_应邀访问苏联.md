Title: Chinese Education Union Delegation Visits the Soviet Union at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. A Chinese Education Union delegation led by Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Education Union, left Beijing by plane today for Moscow. The delegation is visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Soviet Union's Higher Education and Scientific Institution Workers' Union.

### 我教育工会代表团  应邀访问苏联

1959-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

    我教育工会代表团
    应邀访问苏联
    新华社5日讯　中国教育工会代表团在中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京去莫斯科。代表团是应苏联高等学校和科学机关工作者工会中央委员会的邀请去苏联访问的。
