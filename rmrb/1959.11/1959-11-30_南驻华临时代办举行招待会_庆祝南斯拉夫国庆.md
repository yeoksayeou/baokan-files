Title: Yugoslav Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Holds Reception Celebrating Yugoslav National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-11-30
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The Yugoslav Chargé d'Affaires ad interim in China, Mr. Šestan, held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the National Day of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

    Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs, attended the reception. Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China also attended the reception.

### 南驻华临时代办举行招待会  庆祝南斯拉夫国庆

1959-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    南驻华临时代办举行招待会
    庆祝南斯拉夫国庆
    新华社29日讯　南斯拉夫驻中国大使馆临时代办赛斯坦今天下午举行招待会，庆祝南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国国庆。
    外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文、苏联东欧司副司长余湛等人出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有各国驻中国使节和外交官员。
