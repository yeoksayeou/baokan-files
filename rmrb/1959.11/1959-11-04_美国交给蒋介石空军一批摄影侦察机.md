Title: US Hands Over Batch of Photo Reconnaissance Aircraft to Chiang Kai-shek Air Force
Author:
Date: 1959-11-04
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd (Dispatch from Taipei) – US Military Assistance Advisory Group chief in Taiwan, Bowen, announced on October 29th that the United States is handing over a batch of RF-101 Voodoo jet reconnaissance aircraft equipped with photographic equipment to the Chiang Kai-shek clique. United Press International quoted US military officers in Taipei as saying that the United States is supplying these aircraft to the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force so that they can take pictures of mainland China.

### 美国交给蒋介石空军一批摄影侦察机

1959-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

    美国交给蒋介石空军一批摄影侦察机
    新华社3日讯　台北消息：美国驻台湾军事援助顾问团团长宝恩在10月29日宣布，美国正在把一批装有照相设备的RF—101优都式喷气侦察机交给蒋介石集团。合众国际社引述在台北的美军军官的谈话说，美国把这批飞机供给蒋介石空军，是为了使它们拍摄中国大陆的照片。
