International Briefs

    India's Largest Zoo Opens
    New Delhi dispatch: India's largest zoo, the Delhi Zoo, opened on November 1, presided over by Agriculture Minister Deshmukh. The zoo covers an area of ​​250 acres. Five hundred and twenty-four animals have been moved into their permanent new homes.
    Dignitaries from all walks of life and diplomatic envoys to India from various countries, including Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili, attended the opening ceremony. When Deshmukh arrived, an elephant in the zoo presented him with a garland and played the harmonica for him.
    United States Launches an Artificial Satellite
    Washington dispatch: The United States Air Force launched an artificial satellite, "Discoverer VII," into a polar orbit around the Earth at 21:45 Universal Time on November 7.
    "Discoverer VII" weighs 1,700 pounds (771 kilograms) and was launched using a "Thor" intermediate-range ballistic missile. The satellite's orbit is 550 miles (885 kilometers) from the ground at its farthest point and 104 miles (167 kilometers) from the ground at its closest point. The satellite orbits the Earth in 95 minutes.
    "Discoverer VII" carries a sealed container weighing more than 300 pounds (more than 136 kilograms). The sealed container has a parachute and contains no living organisms. It is said that the purpose of launching this satellite is to send the sealed container back to Earth from outer space through the atmosphere when it completes its seventeenth orbit around the Earth.

    国际简讯
    印度最大的动物园开幕
    新德里讯　印度最大的动物园——德里动物园1日在农业部长德希穆克主持下开幕。这个动物园的面积为二百五十英亩。五百二十四只动物已经迁入它们的固定的新舍
    出席开幕仪式的有各界知名人士和包括中国驻印度大使潘自力在内的各国驻印度外交使节。当德希穆克到达时，动物园内的一只象向他献花环，并且为他演奏口琴。
    美国发射一个人造卫星
    华盛顿讯　美国空军在7日国际标准时间二十一点四十五分发射了一个环绕地球两极运行的人造卫星“发现者七号”。
    “发现者七号”重一千七百磅（七百七十一公斤），是用“雷神式”中程弹道导弹发射的。卫星的运行轨道离地面最远时为五百五十英里（八百八十五公里），离地面最近时为一百零四英里（一百六十七公里）。卫星绕地球运行一周时间为九十五分钟。
    “发现者七号”上面带有一个三百多磅（一百三十六公斤多）重的密封容器。密封容器带有降落伞，内部没有生物。据说，发射这个卫星的目的是要在它环绕地球运行第十七圈时把密封容器从宇宙空间通过大气层送回地面。
