Title: Building a Canal Beneath the Daming Mountain
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-23
Page: 2

Beneath the Daming Mountain in the eastern part of the Nanning Special Zone of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, construction has begun on the Dalongdong Canal, a 380-li long waterway.

The Dalongdong Canal runs through Shanglin and Binyang counties. Once completed, it will be navigable for 15-ton steamers.

### 大明山下修运河

1959-11-23
第2版()
专栏：

    大明山下修运河
    广西僮族自治区南宁专区东部大明山下，开始修建一条长达三百八十华里的运河——大龙洞运河。
    大龙洞运河贯通上林、宾阳两县，建成后，可以航行十五吨的汽船。
