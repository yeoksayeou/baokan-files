Title: Former General Secretary of the Nepalese Communist Party, Man Mohan Adhikari, Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. Comrade Man Mohan Adhikari, former General Secretary of the Nepalese Communist Party, who had been receiving medical treatment in our country, has recovered his health after a long period of treatment and recuperation, and left our country on November 26th to return home. Before returning home, Comrade Adhikari participated in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country in Guangzhou, and also visited Hunan, Wuhan, Shanghai, Beijing, the Northeast and other places.

### 尼共前总书记曼摩汉·阿迪卡利回国

1959-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    尼共前总书记曼摩汉·阿迪卡利回国
    新华社27日讯　在我国养病的尼泊尔共产党前总书记曼摩汉·阿迪卡利同志，经过长期治疗和休养，已经恢复健康，于11月26日离开我国回国。在回国前，阿迪卡利同志在广州参加了我国建国十周年庆祝典礼，并且到湖南、武汉、上海、北京、东北等地进行了参观游览。
