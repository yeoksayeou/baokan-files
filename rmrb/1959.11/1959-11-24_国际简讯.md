International Briefs

    Sihanouk Leaves the UAR for Yugoslavia
    Cairo Dispatch: After a two-week official visit to the United Arab Republic, Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Sihanouk left Cairo on the morning of the 23rd for Yugoslavia. During his visit, Sihanouk held talks with Nasser and issued a joint statement, signing a cultural agreement between the two countries.

    Iraqi General Federation of Trade Unions
    Elects Provisional Executive Committee
    Baghdad Dispatch: According to Baghdad Radio, the members of the Preparatory Committee of the Iraqi General Federation of Trade Unions and the twenty-eight chairmen of various trade unions held their first meeting on the 20th. The meeting elected the provisional executive committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions. Ali Shukr, Chairman of the Railway Workers' Union, was elected as Chairman; Ala Hajadur, Chairman of the Oil Workers' Union, was elected as Vice-Chairman; and Kadir Ayashi, Chairman of the Printing Workers' Union, was elected as Secretary-General.

    Iceland Forms New Government
    Reykjavik Dispatch: The Icelandic State Council approved the list of the new Icelandic government on the 20th. The new government is a coalition of the Independence Party (also known as the Conservative Party) and the Social Democratic Party. Ólafur Thors (Independence Party) is the Prime Minister; Guðmundur Guðmundsson (Social Democratic Party) is the Minister of Foreign Affairs.

    Primary School Teachers in a Brazilian State End Strike
    Rio de Janeiro Dispatch: The strike of 25,000 primary school teachers in the Brazilian state of Minas Gerais ended on the evening of the 20th. The governor of the state is about to send a letter to the state legislative assembly, suggesting that the teachers' demands for increased wages be met.

    国际简讯
    西哈努克离开阿联往南斯拉夫
    开罗讯　柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王，在阿拉伯联合共和国作了两个星期的正式访问之后，于23日晨离开开罗前往南斯拉夫。西哈努克在访问期间同纳赛尔进行了会谈，并且发表了一项联合声明，签订了两国之间的文化协定。
    伊拉克工会总联合会
    选出临时执行委员会
    巴格达讯　据巴格达电台广播，伊拉克工会总联合会筹备委员会的委员们和各工会的二十八位主席，曾在20日举行了第一次会议。会议选出了工会总联合会的临时执行委员会。铁路工人工会主席阿里·舒克尔当选为主席，石油工人工会主席阿拉·哈贾杜尔当选为副主席，印刷工人工会主席卡迪尔·阿亚希当选为总书记。
      冰岛组成新政府
    雷克雅未克讯　冰岛国务委员会20日批准了冰岛新政府的名单。新政府是由独立党（又称保守党）和社会民主党联合组成的。由奥拉夫·托尔斯（独立党）任总理；格德门德尔·格德门森（社会民主党）任外交部长。
    巴西一个州小学教师结束罢教
    里约热内卢讯　巴西米纳斯吉拉斯州二万五千名小学教师的罢教于20日晚间结束。这个州的州长即将致函州立法议会，建议满足教师们提出的增加工资的要求。
