Title: U.S. Pacific Area Forces Commander Strives to Create War Atmosphere in Asia, Cries that Small-Scale Wars Could Happen in Asia at Any Time
Author:
Date: 1959-11-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th, Honolulu News: The Commander of the U.S. Pacific Area Armed Forces, Felt, issued bellicose cries in Honolulu on the 23rd.

Felt told reporters, "In Asia, small-scale wars could happen at any time." Felt stated that the United States' "established policy of continuing to control Okinawa as a 'defense fortress in the Far East' remains unchanged." He said: Okinawa, now under American administration, is a "critical link in the chain" for the United States in the Pacific to deal with China, and if "there is no Okinawa, the entire chain will collapse."

In order to conceal the U.S. conspiracy to occupy Okinawa Island and carry out aggression in Asia, Felt tried his best to spread the lie of the so-called "Communist threat," and he incited Asian countries to oppose Communism. He said: "If any Asian country shows weakness, there will immediately be the danger of another Communist-launched aggression." He further threatened, "The final step is nuclear war."

Felt is engaging in activities in the Far East based on a proposal from the U.S. Department of Defense. He will "visit" Japan, South Korea, Okinawa Island, the Philippines, Hong Kong, South Vietnam, Thailand, and Taiwan.

### 美国太平洋地区部队总司令  竭力制造亚洲的战争气氛  叫嚷亚洲随时可能发生小规模战争

1959-11-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美国太平洋地区部队总司令
    竭力制造亚洲的战争气氛
    叫嚷亚洲随时可能发生小规模战争
    新华社25日讯　檀香山消息：美国太平洋地区武装部队总司令费耳特23日在檀香山发出好战的叫嚣。
    费耳特向新闻记者说：“在亚洲，任何时候都可能发生小规模战争”。费耳特表示，美国继续控制冲绳作为“远东的防御堡垒”的“既定政策不变”。他说：现在在美国行政统治下的冲绳是美国在太平洋一带对付中国的“锁链的重要环节”，如果“没有冲绳，整个锁链就会垮台”。
    为了掩饰美国霸占冲绳岛，在亚洲进行侵略的阴谋，费耳特竭力散布所谓“共产党威胁”的谎言，他煽动亚洲国家反共。他说：“如果任何亚洲国家表示软弱的话，马上就会有共产党发动的另一次侵略的危险”。他进一步恐吓说，“最后的步骤是核战争”。
    费耳特是根据美国国防部的提议到远东活动的。他将“访问”日本、南朝鲜、冲绳岛、菲律宾、香港、南越、泰国和台湾。
