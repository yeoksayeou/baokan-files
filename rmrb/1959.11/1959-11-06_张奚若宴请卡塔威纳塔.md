Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts a Banquet for Kartawinata
Author:
Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 5th November: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and his wife hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Arudji Kartawinata, Second Deputy Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament and Chairman of the Indonesian Islamic Union Party, and his wife. Zhang Xiruo delivered a welcome speech at the banquet, saying: "This visit to China by our old Indonesian friend will further promote the development of friendship between the people of China and Indonesia." Deputy Speaker Arudji Kartawinata also said at the meeting that he would take deep friendship back with him on this visit to China.
    Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wijopranoto, and his wife were invited to attend the banquet.
    Among those present at the banquet were Burhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association and Director of the China Islamic Association; Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the National People's Congress Standing Committee; Hu Yuzhi and Qiao Guanhua, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Shen Zijiu, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation; and Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

### 张奚若宴请卡塔威纳塔

1959-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

    张奚若宴请卡塔威纳塔
    新华社5日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若和他的夫人，今天晚上设宴欢迎印度尼西亚国会第二副议长、印度尼西亚伊斯兰联盟党主席阿鲁季·卡塔威纳塔和他的夫人。张奚若在宴会上致词欢迎，他说：我们的印度尼西亚老朋友这次来中国访问，将会进一步促进中国和印度尼西亚两国人民友谊的发展。阿鲁季·卡塔威纳塔副议长也在会上说，他这次访问中国将要带着深厚的友谊回去。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托和他的夫人应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的，有中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长、中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉，人大常委会副秘书长张苏，中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、乔冠华，全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九，外交部第一亚洲司副司长沈平等。
