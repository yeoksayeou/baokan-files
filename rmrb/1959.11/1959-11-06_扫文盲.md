Title: Eradicating Illiteracy
Author: Rui Ling
Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 4

    Eradicating Illiteracy
    The great leap forward of society,
    Illiteracy still obstructs the way,
    Setting up cultural posts,
    Establishing cultural sentry posts.
    The illiteracy hat is firmly worn,
    Our determination is high,
    Aiming at the illiteracy hat,
    Firing relentless cannons.
      (Collected by Rui Ling, Nanle, Henan)

### 扫文盲
瑞龄

专栏：

    扫文盲
    社会大飞跃，
    文盲还拦道，
    设起文化岗，
    布起文化哨。
    文盲帽子牢，
    我们决心高，
    对准文盲帽，
    开起绝情炮。
      （河南南乐　瑞龄搜集）
