Title: Hungarian Telecommunications Expert Barta Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: Barta István, Academician of Communications of Hungary, a telecommunications expert who is visiting China according to the 1959 execution plan of Sino-Hungarian Academy of Sciences scientific cooperation, arrived in Beijing by plane on November 16th.

### 匈牙利电讯学家巴尔塔到京

1959-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利电讯学家巴尔塔到京
    新华社17日讯　根据中匈两国科学院科学合作1959年执行计划来我国访问的匈牙利电讯学家巴尔塔·伊斯特万通讯院士，于11月16日乘飞机到达北京。
