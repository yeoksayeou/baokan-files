Title: Master and Apprentice (Picture)
Author: Yang Pu Tao, Chen Tao
Date: 1959-11-27
Page: 8

Column:

    Master and Apprentice
    Photography by Yang Pu Tao, Poem by Chen Tao
    Master and apprentice at the furnace,
    Together watching the steel flowers,
    The master diligently instructs,
    The apprentice learns from experience.
    Two people with one heart,
    Four eyes on one line:
    Support major construction,
    Smelt more good steel.

### 师徒（图片）
杨浦滔  陈涛
1959-11-27
第8版()
专栏：

    师徒
    杨浦滔摄影  陈涛配诗
    师徒在炉前，
    共把钢花看，
    师傅勤指点，
    徒弟学经验。
    两人一条心，
    四目一条线：
    支援大建设，
    多把好钢炼。
