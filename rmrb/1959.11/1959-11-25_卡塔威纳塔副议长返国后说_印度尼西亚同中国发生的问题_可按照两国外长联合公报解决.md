Title: Deputy Speaker Katawinata Says After Returning Home: Problems Between Indonesia and China Can Be Resolved According to the Joint Communiqué of the Two Foreign Ministers
Author:
Date: 1959-11-25
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 24th: Indonesian Parliament Second Deputy Speaker Katawinata and his wife returned to Jakarta by plane last night after a 20-day visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen and his wife, Head of the Indonesian Parliament's Protocol Department Sunito, and Katawinata's family were at the airport to welcome them.

According to Indonesian News Agency, when answering questions at the airport, Katawinata expressed his personal opinion, saying, "The recent problems between Indonesia and People's China can be resolved in accordance with the letter and spirit of the Subandrio-Chen Yi Joint Communiqué."

### 卡塔威纳塔副议长返国后说  印度尼西亚同中国发生的问题  可按照两国外长联合公报解决

1959-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    卡塔威纳塔副议长返国后说
    印度尼西亚同中国发生的问题  可按照两国外长联合公报解决
    新华社雅加达24日电　印度尼西亚议会第二副议长卡塔威纳塔及其夫人应中国人民外交学会的邀请在中国作二十天访问以后，昨晚乘飞机回到雅加达。到机场欢迎的有中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇及其夫人、印度尼西亚国会交际处处长苏尼托和卡塔威纳塔的家属。
    据印度尼西亚新闻社说，卡塔威纳塔在机场答复问题时发表他的个人意见说：“印度尼西亚同人民中国最近发生的问题可以按照苏班德里约—陈毅联合公报的文字和精神来解决。”
