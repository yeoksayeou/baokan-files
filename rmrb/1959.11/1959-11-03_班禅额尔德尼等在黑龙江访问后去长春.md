Title: Panchen Erdeni and Others Visit Heilongjiang, Then Go to Changchun
Author:
Date: 1959-11-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, November 2nd. Panchen Erdeni Chökyi Gyaltsen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and their entourage, concluded their tour of Heilongjiang Province and left Harbin by special train this morning for Changchun.

They arrived in Harbin on October 27th, accompanied by Wang Feng, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee. During their stay in Heilongjiang Province, Panchen Erdeni and Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal and others visited the Harbin Boiler Factory, the Steam Turbine Factory, and the Xinfa People's Commune in Harbin City, and visited factories in Qiqihar City. This morning, Panchen Erdeni and Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal met with the responsible persons of the Party and government of Heilongjiang Province and Harbin City, and had cordial talks with the responsible comrades of the province and city.

When Panchen Erdeni and his party arrived and departed from Harbin, Ouyang Qin, First Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the CPC, Qiang Xiaochu, Secretary of the Secretariat, Wang Yilun and Yang Yichen, Secretaries of the Secretariat and Vice Governors, and Fan Ziwu, Vice Governor, went to the station to welcome or see them off.

### 班禅额尔德尼等在黑龙江访问后去长春

1959-11-03
第2版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼等在黑龙江访问后去长春
    新华社哈尔滨2日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞、政协全国委员会副主席帕巴拉·卓列朗杰和随行人员，结束了在黑龙江省的参观活动，今天上午乘专车离开哈尔滨去长春。
    他们是10月27日在中共中央统战部副部长汪锋、中共西藏工委副书记周仁山的陪同下来到哈尔滨的。班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉·卓列朗杰等在黑龙江省期间，参观了哈尔滨市的锅炉厂、汽轮机厂、新发人民公社，并到齐齐哈尔市参观了工厂。今天上午，班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉·卓列朗杰会见了黑龙江省和哈尔滨市党和政府的负责人，并且和省市负责同志进行了亲切的交谈。
    班禅额尔德尼等一行到达和离开哈尔滨的时候，中共黑龙江省委第一书记欧阳钦、书记处书记强晓初，书记处书记、副省长王一伦、杨易辰、副省长范子文等曾到车站欢迎或欢送。
