Title: Fraternal Countries' Economists Gather to Criticize Modern Bourgeois Economic Theories
Author:
Date: 1959-11-13
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 11th: An international conference on the Marxist critique of modern bourgeois reformist and revisionist economic theories was held in Smolenice, Slovakia, and closed on November 11th. Prominent economists from the Soviet Union, the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, Hungary, Poland, and Czechoslovakia participated in the conference. The purpose of the conference was to summarize research in this area and strengthen cooperation among economists from socialist countries. The conference formulated and adopted the agenda for the next international conference to discuss new phenomena in modern bourgeois political economy. The next conference will be held in Berlin in October next year.

### 兄弟国家经济学家集会  批判现代资产阶级经济理论

1959-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

    兄弟国家经济学家集会
    批判现代资产阶级经济理论
    新华社布拉格11日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：对现代资产阶级改良主义和修正主义的经济理论进行马克思主义批判的国际会议于11月9日在斯洛伐克的斯莫列尼策举行，11日闭幕。参加会议的有苏联、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、匈牙利、波兰和捷克斯洛伐克等国的著名经济学家。这次会议的目的是，总结在这方面的研究情况、加强社会主义国家经济学家之间的合作。会议制定和通过了下一次国际会议讨论现代资产阶级政治经济学中的新现象的会议日程。下次会议将于明年10月在柏林举行。
