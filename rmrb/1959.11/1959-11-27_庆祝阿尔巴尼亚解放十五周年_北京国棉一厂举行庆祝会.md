Title: Beijing No. 1 Cotton Mill Holds Celebration to Celebrate the 15th Anniversary of Albania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1959-11-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th November. Beijing No. 1 Cotton Mill held an evening party tonight to celebrate the 15th anniversary of Albania's liberation. More than 1,200 workers participated in this joyous celebration.

The wife of the Albanian Ambassador to China, Comrade Prifti, and embassy staff attended the celebration. Lin Qiaozhi, director of the Sino-Albanian Friendship Association, also attended the celebration.

Li Zhao, the director of the factory, warmly congratulated the Albanian people on the great achievements they have made in the cause of socialist construction in his speech at the celebration.

The wife of the Albanian Ambassador to China, Comrade Prifti, introduced the construction achievements in the economic and cultural fields in the 15 years since Albania's liberation and the new life of Albanian women after liberation. On behalf of the Albanian people and Albanian women, she congratulated the factory on completing the annual national production plan ahead of schedule.

The Ambassador's wife's speech, filled with fraternal friendship, was warmly welcomed by the workers.

A congratulatory letter from all the factory workers to all the workers in Albania was read out at the meeting. In the congratulatory letter, the workers wished the brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Albanian people would last forever.

The factory workers' amateur cultural troupe finally sang Albanian songs at the evening party and showed the Albanian film "Great Warrior".

### 庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年  北京国棉一厂举行庆祝会

1959-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年
    北京国棉一厂举行庆祝会
    据新华社26日讯　北京第一棉纺织厂今天晚上举行晚会，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年。有一千二百多名职工参加了这个喜气洋洋的庆祝会。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使帕里夫蒂的夫人和大使馆工作人员出席了庆祝会。中阿友好协会理事林巧稚也参加了庆祝会。
    这个厂的厂长李昭在庆祝会上的致词中热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚人民在社会主义建设事业中取得的伟大成就。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使帕里夫蒂的夫人在会上介绍了阿尔巴尼亚解放十五年来在经济、文化方面的建设成就和阿尔巴尼亚妇女解放后的新生活。她代表阿尔巴尼亚人民和阿尔巴尼亚妇女，祝贺工厂提前完成全年的国家生产计划。
    大使夫人充满着兄弟友情的讲话受到了工人们的热烈欢迎。
    会上宣读了全厂职工致阿尔巴尼亚全国职工的贺信，职工们在贺信中祝中阿两国人民兄弟般的友谊万古常青。
    工厂工人的业余文工团最后在晚会上演唱了阿尔巴尼亚歌曲，并放映了阿尔巴尼亚电影“伟大的战士”。
