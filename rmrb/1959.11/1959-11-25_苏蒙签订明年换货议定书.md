Title: Soviet-Mongolian Agreement Signed on Exchange of Goods for Next Year
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-25
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 23rd: According to TASS, a protocol on the exchange of goods between the Soviet Union and the Mongolian People's Republic for 1960 was signed in Moscow today. According to the protocol, the Soviet Union will provide Mongolia with various industrial equipment, agricultural machinery, tractors, automobiles, petroleum products, building materials and other commodities.

    Mongolia will provide the Soviet Union with meat livestock, butter, wool, camel hair, and leather products.

### 苏蒙签订明年换货议定书

1959-11-25
第6版()
专栏：

    苏蒙签订明年换货议定书
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科23日讯：苏联和蒙古人民共和国1960年换货议定书今天在莫斯科签字。根据议定书，苏联将向蒙古提供各种工业设备、农业机器、拖拉机、汽车、石油产品、建筑材料和其它商品。
    蒙古将向苏联提供肉用牲畜、奶油、羊毛、骆驼毛和皮革制品。
