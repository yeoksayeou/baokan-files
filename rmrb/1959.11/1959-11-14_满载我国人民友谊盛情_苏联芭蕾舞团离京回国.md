Title: Soviet Ballet Troupe Departs Beijing for Home, Fully Loaded with Profound Friendship of Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The Soviet Bolshoi Theatre Ballet Troupe, led by its director Aretchinko, concluded its performance tour in various parts of China and departed Beijing by plane today, fully loaded with the profound friendship of the Chinese people, returning home.

Those who saw them off at the airport today included Ding Xilin, Qu Wu, and Luo Jun, Vice-Chairmen of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture; Tian Han, Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association; Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and many art workers and representatives of art groups from the capital.

Soviet Ambassador to China, Chervonenko, and Counselor of the Embassy, Sudarikov, also went to the airport to see them off. Today, at the Capital Airport, the profound friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people and the artists of the two countries once again resonated in everyone's hearts. When the Ballet Troupe Director Aretchinko and the ballet troupe artists arrived at the airport, the capital's male and female art workers presented them with flowers. At the moment of parting, hosts and guests were reluctant to part, shaking hands repeatedly, expressing their feelings of farewell. Colin, the artistic director of the ballet troupe, said excitedly: "Although we are leaving, our hearts are staying in China."

### 满载我国人民友谊盛情  苏联芭蕾舞团离京回国

1959-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    满载我国人民友谊盛情
    苏联芭蕾舞团离京回国
    新华社13日讯　结束了在我国各地访问演出的苏联国家大剧院芭蕾舞团，在团长阿列钦科率领下，满载着中国人民的友谊盛情，于今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    今天到机场欢送的，有对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林、屈武、罗俊，文化部副部长夏衍，中国戏剧家协会主席田汉，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权，首都的许多艺术工作者和艺术团体的代表。
    苏联驻华大使契尔沃年科和大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫也到机场送行。今天在首都机场上，中苏两国人民和两国艺术家的深厚友情再一次在每个人的心里激荡。当芭蕾舞团团长阿列钦科和芭蕾舞团的艺术家们来到机场后，首都男女艺术工作者们向他们献鲜花。在告别的时刻，宾主依依不舍，一再握手，互相表达惜别的情意。芭蕾舞团艺术指导柯林激动地说：“我们人虽然走了，可是心却留在中国了。”
