Title: Premier Zhou Receives Indian-Soviet Cultural Association President Baliga and His Wife
Author:
Date: 1959-11-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Premier Zhou Enlai received Baliga, the President of the Indian-Soviet Cultural Association, and his wife this afternoon. Present at the reception were He Ying, Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见印苏文协主席巴利加夫妇

1959-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印苏文协主席巴利加夫妇
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了印度印苏文化协会主席巴利加和他的夫人。接见时在座的有外交部第一亚洲司副司长何英，中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达。
