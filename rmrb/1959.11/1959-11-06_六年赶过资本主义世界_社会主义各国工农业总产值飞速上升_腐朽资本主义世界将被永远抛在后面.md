Title: Overtake the Capitalist World in Six Years: Total Industrial and Agricultural Output of Socialist Countries Rises Rapidly, Decaying Capitalist World Will Be Left Behind Forever
Author:
Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 5th: According to Soviet press reports: The economies of the socialist camp countries, led by the Soviet Union, are developing at a high speed. As a result, by 1965, the socialist world system will be able to catch up with and surpass the capitalist world system in terms of total industrial and agricultural output.

According to materials from the Soviet press, if the current total industrial output of the socialist world system is taken as 100, then that of the capitalist world system is 180 to 185. Because the economic development of socialist countries is accelerating faster than that of capitalist countries, it is estimated that by 1965, the total industrial output of the socialist world system will increase to 220 to 230; while the corresponding indicator for the capitalist world system will increase to approximately 198 to 222. This means that in terms of total industrial output, the socialist world system will then firmly gain the upper hand. Considering that the population of the socialist world system is about 60% of the population of the capitalist world system, by that time, it will leave the decaying capitalist world even further behind in terms of industrial output per capita.

In terms of agricultural production, by 1965, the socialist camp will also surpass the capitalist world in terms of total agricultural output.

In the economic competition, the fundamental condition for determining victory or defeat is the speed of economic development. And the advantage in this respect has always belonged, and will always belong, to the socialist camp countries.

Taking the situation since the end of World War II as an example. In the entire post-war period, the growth rate of industrial production in socialist countries, on the whole, has been about one and a half times faster than that of capitalist countries. In the five years from 1954 to 1958, the industrial production of the socialist camp countries increased by an average of 11% per year, while the entire capitalist world increased by an average of less than 3% per year, which means that the former was more than two and a half times faster than the latter. In the future, the advantage of socialist countries in this regard will be further strengthened. According to the Soviet magazine "International Life": Based on the past economic development of the capitalist world and the economic development prospects of socialist countries, in the seven years starting this year, on the whole, the industrial output of the socialist camp will develop at a rate five to ten times faster than that of the capitalist world, and the total industrial output will increase by at least 1.2 to 1.3 times. During the same period, the industrial production of the capitalist world will only increase by about 10% to 20%. This gives the socialist camp the confidence to catch up with and surpass the capitalist world in terms of total industrial output in the next few years.

Currently, the capitalist world is facing numerous economic difficulties, which stands in stark contrast to the fact that the economies of socialist countries have entered a new stage of development. In this stage, their economies will achieve more rapid and more comprehensive upsurges. This development trend is clearly reflected in the economic development prospects of various socialist countries.

According to Soviet press reports, after such major development, by 1965, the steel output of the European People's Democracies alone will increase from 17 million tons last year to 29 million tons, the power generation will increase from 94 billion kilowatt-hours to about 170 billion kilowatt-hours, and the coal output will increase from 450 million tons to 570 million to 600 million tons. By that time, the total industrial potential of these countries will roughly be equivalent to the sum of the current industrial potentials of West Germany, France, and Italy.

In the economic competition between the two world systems, the implementation of the Soviet Seven-Year Plan (1959-1965) will play a particularly important role. According to statistics, last year, the total industrial output of the Soviet Union accounted for more than three-fifths of the total industrial output of the socialist camp countries, and about one-fifth of the world's total industrial output. The Seven-Year Plan stipulates that the Soviet Union's total industrial output will increase by about 80%. The completion and over-completion of this task means an unprecedented strengthening of the economic strength of the entire socialist camp. For example, during these seven years, the absolute increase in Soviet oil will be 117 million tons to 127 million tons, which is almost equivalent to the total oil output of all socialist countries (including the Soviet Union) last year. It can be seen from this that the large-scale development of the Soviet economy will play a decisive role in the socialist world system's final defeat of the capitalist world system in the economic competition.

### 六年赶过资本主义世界  社会主义各国工农业总产值飞速上升  腐朽资本主义世界将被永远抛在后面

1959-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

    六年赶过资本主义世界
    社会主义各国工农业总产值飞速上升
    腐朽资本主义世界将被永远抛在后面
    据新华社莫斯科5日电　据苏联报刊报道：以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国的经济正在高速度地发展。由于这样，到1965年，社会主义世界体系就可以在工业和农业的总产值方面赶上和超过资本主义世界体系。
    根据苏联报刊的材料，如果以目前社会主义世界体系的工业总产值为一百，那么资本主义世界体系则为一百八十到一百八十五。由于社会主义国家经济发展速度越来越比资本主义国家快，预计到1965年，社会主义世界体系的工业总产值将增长为二百二十到二百三十；而资本主义世界体系的这方面的指标大致将增加到一百九十八到二百二十二。这就是说，在工业总产值方面，那时社会主义世界体系将稳占上风。如果考虑到社会主义世界体系的人口约为资本主义世界体系人口的60％，到那个时候，它在按人口平均计算的工业产量方面就将把腐朽的资本主义世界更加远远地抛到后面。
    在农业生产方面，到1965年，社会主义阵营也将在农业总产值方面超过资本主义世界。
    在经济竞赛中，决定胜负的根本条件是经济的发展速度。而这方面的优势，不论在过去、现在或将来都永远属于社会主义阵营国家。
    以第二次世界大战结束以来的情况为例。在整个战后时期中，社会主义国家的工业生产增长速度，总的说来，大约比资本主义世界各国快一倍半。在从1954年到1958年的五年间，社会主义阵营国家工业生产每年平均增加11％，而整个资本主义世界每年平均还增加不到3％，这就是说前者要比后者快两倍半以上。今后，社会主义国家在这方面的优势还将进一步加强。据苏联“国际生活”杂志报道：根据过去一个时期资本主义世界经济的发展状况，根据社会主义国家的经济发展远景计划，在从今年开始的往后七年间，整个说来，社会主义阵营的工业产量将以比资本主义世界快五倍到十多倍的速度发展，工业总产量至少将增加一点二倍到一点三倍。而在同一时期内，资本主义世界的工业生产大致只会增加10％到20％。这就使得社会主义阵营有把握在今后几年间在工业总产值方面赶上和超过资本主义世界。
    目前，资本主义世界的经济困难重重，和这成鲜明对比的是，社会主义国家的经济却进入了新的发展阶段。在这个阶段里，它们的经济将获得更加迅速、更加全面的高涨。这种发展趋势明显地反映在各个社会主义国家的经济发展远景计划里。
    据苏联报刊报道，经过这样的大发展，到1965年，单是欧洲各人民民主国家的钢产量就将从去年的一千七百万吨增加到二千九百万吨，发电量将从九百四十亿度增加到约一千七百亿度，煤产量将从四万五千万吨提高到五万七千万吨到六千万吨。到那时候，这些国家的工业总潜力将大致相当于西德、法国和意大利三国目前工业潜力的总和。
    在两个世界体系的经济竞赛中，苏联的七年计划（1959—1965年）的实现将起特别重要的作用。据统计，去年苏联的工业总产值占社会主义阵营各国工业产值总和的五分之三以上，约占世界工业总产值的五分之一左右。七年计划规定苏联工业总产值将增加约80％，这个任务的完成和超额完成意味着整个社会主义阵营的经济力量的空前增强。例如，在这七年间，苏联石油的绝对增长量将为一万一千七百万吨到一万二千七百万吨，这几乎相当于所有社会主义国家（包括苏联）去年的石油产量的总和。人们从这里可以看出，苏联经济的大规模发展，对社会主义世界体系在经济竞赛中最后击败资本主义世界体系，将起决定性的作用。
