Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and All-China Women's Federation Send Greetings to Albania on National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-11-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, November 28th: Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China, and the All-China Women's Federation, sent telegrams to Nush, Chairman of the Central Council of Albanian Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Youth League, and the Albanian Women's Federation, warmly congratulating them on the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Albania.

The congratulatory telegrams congratulated the Albanian people on the brilliant achievements they have made in the cause of socialist construction under the correct leadership of the Albanian Labor Party over the past fifteen years; and congratulated them on the important contributions they have made in defending world peace, consolidating the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, safeguarding the purity of Marxism-Leninism, and fighting the lofty struggle against modern revisionism.

The congratulatory telegrams also said that the unbreakable brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Albanian peoples will last forever.

### 我全总、团中央和妇联  电贺阿尔巴尼亚国庆

1959-11-29
第3版()
专栏：

    我全总、团中央和妇联
    电贺阿尔巴尼亚国庆
    新华社28日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一、中国共产主义青年团中央委员会、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会，分别致电阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会主席努什、阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会、阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会，热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年。
    贺电祝贺十五年来阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的正确领导下，在社会主义建设事业中取得的光辉的成就；祝贺他们在保卫世界和平、巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结、捍卫马克思列宁主义的纯洁性、反对现代修正主义的崇高斗争中所作出的重要贡献。
    贺电并说中阿两国人民牢不可破的兄弟友谊万古长青。
