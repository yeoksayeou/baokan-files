Title: Chinese Journalists Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1959-11-28
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 27th: The Chinese Journalists Delegation, invited by the Central Committee of the Korean Journalists Union to visit Korea, arrived in Pyongyang by train today. The delegation is headed by Hu Jiwei, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the "People's Daily," and consists of nine members.

    Hyun Pil-hoon, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Journalists Union, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the delegation. Those attending the banquet included: Ha Ang-cheon, Chairman of the Korea-China Friendship Association; Kim Do-man, Director of the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Park Kwang-seon, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Park Rip-yang, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Writers' Union; responsible cadres from various newspapers, news agencies, and radio stations in Pyongyang; Cao Keqiang, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Korea; and Mao Dafeng, Cultural Attaché.

    In his speech at the banquet, Hyun Pil-hoon extended a warm welcome to the delegation. He said that the delegation's visit to Korea would contribute to strengthening the friendship and solidarity between the people of Korea and China.

    In his speech at the banquet, Hu Jiwei said that the delegation would introduce the valuable experiences gained by the Korean people in the Chollima Movement to the Chinese people.

### 我新闻工作者代表团抵平壤

1959-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    我新闻工作者代表团抵平壤
    新华社平壤27日电　应朝鲜记者同盟中央委员会的邀请来朝鲜访问的中国新闻工作者代表团，在“人民日报”副总编辑胡绩伟率领下，今天乘火车到达平壤。代表团由九人组成。
    朝鲜记者同盟中央委员会委员长玄弼勋今天晚上设宴欢迎代表团。参加宴会的有：朝中友好协会委员长河仰天、朝鲜劳动党中央宣传鼓动部长金道万、外务省副相朴光善、作家同盟中央委员会副委员长朴入阳和平壤各报社、通讯社、广播电台负责干部，中国驻朝鲜大使馆参赞曹克强、文化专员毛大风等。
    玄弼勋在宴会上致词时，对代表团表示热烈欢迎。他说，代表团访问朝鲜将对加强朝中两国人民间的友谊团结作出贡献。
    胡绩伟在宴会上致词时表示，代表团要把朝鲜人民在千里马运动中取得的宝贵经验，介绍给中国人民。
