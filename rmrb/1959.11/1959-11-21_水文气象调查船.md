Title: Hydrological and Meteorological Survey Vessel
Author:
Date: 1959-11-21
Page: 2

    Hydrological and Meteorological Survey Vessel
    China's first self-designed and manufactured hydrological and meteorological survey vessel has been launched in Shanghai.
    This type of ship is for marine hydrological and meteorological surveys and for marine chemistry, marine biology, marine geology, and other survey and research work. It can navigate in winds and waves of typhoon level 8 or 9.

### 水文气象调查船

1959-11-21
第2版()
专栏：

    水文气象调查船
    我国自行设计制造的第一艘水文气象调查船，已在上海下水。
    这种船是供海洋水文气象调查和供海洋化学、海洋生物、海洋地质等调查研究工作用的。能在八、九级台风的风浪里行驶。
