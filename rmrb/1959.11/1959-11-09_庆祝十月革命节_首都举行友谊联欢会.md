Title: Celebrating the October Revolution Day, Friendship Gathering Held in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1959-11-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 8th November: This evening, the State Council's Bureau of Foreign Experts and the Soviet Expert Organization jointly held a friendship gathering to celebrate the 42nd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.

	The friendship gatherings were held separately at the Friendship Hotel and the International Hotel. At the gatherings, responsible personnel from relevant departments of our country, together with Soviet experts and experts from other socialist brother countries, joyfully gathered together like family members, extending festive greetings and expressing brotherly friendship. Everyone was extraordinarily happy at the party, some enjoying song and dance acrobatics performances, and others participating in various interesting games. As the music played, people danced continuously. The friendly conversation continued until late at night.

### 庆祝十月革命节  首都举行友谊联欢会

1959-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月革命节
    首都举行友谊联欢会
    新华社8日讯　今天晚间，国务院外国专家局和苏联专家组织联合举办友谊联欢晚会，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十二周年。
    联欢晚会分别在友谊宾馆和国际饭店举行。在联欢会上，我国有关部门的负责工作人员同苏联专家以及其他社会主义兄弟国家的专家欢聚一起，大家喜气洋洋，亲如家人，互致节日的祝贺和表达兄弟的友好情谊。大家在晚会上快乐非凡，有的欣尝歌舞杂技表演，有的参加各种有趣的游戏。乐曲响处，人们不断翩翩起舞。友谊的欢叙，一直持续到深夜。
