Title: Souphanouvong Sends Letter to Lao Prime Minister and Speaker of Parliament Requesting Release of Lao Patriotic Leaders
Author:
Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 4th. According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency today, the Lao "National Assembly Gazette" published a letter from Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Lao Patriotic Front, to the Speaker of the Lao National Assembly and the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos. In the letter, Prince Souphanouvong requested the release of Lao Patriotic Front leaders, including himself, who were imprisoned by the Lao authorities, in order to create conditions for a peaceful resolution of the Lao issue.

The "Lao Patriotic News" reported this news on October 30th.

The Lao "National Assembly Gazette" also published a joint letter from the wives of the eight imprisoned Lao Patriotic Front leaders. The letter requested the release of these eight leaders. Among those who signed the letter was Madame Khampha Boupha, a member of the Lao Patriotic Front and Laos' only female parliamentarian.

### 苏发努冯致函老挝首相和议会议长  要求释放老挝爱国领袖

1959-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

    苏发努冯致函老挝首相和议会议长
    要求释放老挝爱国领袖
    新华社河内4日电　据越南通讯社今天报道，老挝“国民议会公报”，发表了老挝爱国战线党主席苏发努冯亲王写给老挝国民议会议长和老挝王国首相的一封信。苏发努冯亲王在信中要求释放被老挝当局监禁的包括他在内的老挝爱国战线党领导人，以便为和平解决老挝问题创造条件。
    “老挝爱国报”在10月30日报道了这个消息。
    老挝“国民议会公报”还发表了被监禁的八位老挝爱国战线党领导人的妻子的联名信件。信中要求释放这八位领导人。在信上签名的人中包括老挝爱国战线党党员、老挝唯一的女议员坎法·布法夫人。
