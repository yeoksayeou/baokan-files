Title: Premier Zhou Receives Algerian Trade Union Leaders
Author:
Date: 1959-11-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 17th — Premier Zhou Enlai received Dekkar Ramane, Secretary of the Secretariat of the General Union of Algerian Workers, Mohammed Bernou, Executive Member of the General Union of Algerian Workers, and Pierre Mame, Member of the Agricultural Work Committee of the General Union of Algerian Workers, this afternoon and had a friendly conversation. Liu Changsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, was present at the reception.

    (Attached picture)
    The picture shows Premier Zhou receiving Dekkar Ramane (second from left), Secretary of the Secretariat of the General Union of Algerian Workers, Mohammed Bernou (fourth from left), Executive Member of the General Union of Algerian Workers, and Pierre Mame (third from left), Member of the Agricultural Work Committee of the General Union of Algerian Workers. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Liu Qingrui.

### 周总理接见阿尔及利亚工会领袖

1959-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理接见阿尔及利亚工会领袖
    新华社17日讯　周恩来总理在今天下午接见了阿尔及利亚工人总联合会书记处书记德卡尔·拉孟、阿尔及利亚工人总联合会执行委员穆罕默德·贝尔努和阿尔及利亚工人总联合会农业工作委员会成员彼埃尔·玛梅，进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜。
    （附图片）
    图为周总理接见阿尔及利亚工人总联合会书记处书记德卡尔·拉孟（左第二人），阿尔及利亚工人总联合会执行委员穆罕默德·贝尔努（左第四人）和阿尔及利亚工人总联合会农业工作委员会成员彼埃尔·玛梅（左第三人）。　新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄
