Title: Beijing University Holds a Joint Celebration to Commemorate the 15th Anniversary of the Liberation of Albania
Author:
Date: 1959-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th. To celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Albania, 1,200 teachers and students of Peking University held a joint celebration this evening together with Albanian students studying at Peking University.

    Mrs. Parifti, wife of the Albanian Ambassador to China, and embassy staff members attended the evening party and were warmly welcomed by the teachers and students.

    Zhou Peiyuan, Vice President of Peking University, delivered a speech at the party, congratulating the fraternal Albanian people on achieving even greater victories on the road to socialism, and wishing the friendship between the Chinese and Albanian peoples would last forever.

    Niko Midhi, a representative of the Albanian students and a fifth-year student in the History Department, also spoke at the meeting, saying, "We and China are brothers, and we have found the closest friends here."

    Students from the Soviet Union and more than 30 other countries studying at Peking University also attended the party to congratulate their Albanian classmates.

    Chinese and Albanian students both performed programs at the party, and jointly performed folk dances from Tirana.

### 庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年  北京大学举行联欢晚会

1959-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年
    北京大学举行联欢晚会
    新华社25日讯　北京大学的一千二百名师生，今晚和在北京大学学习的阿尔巴尼亚留学生一起举行联欢晚会，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使帕里夫蒂的夫人和使馆人员参加了晚会，受到师生们的热烈欢迎。
    北京大学副校长周培源在晚会上致词，他祝贺兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民在向社会主义前进的道路上取得更伟大的胜利，祝中阿两国人民的友谊万古长青。
    阿尔巴尼亚留学生代表、历史系五年级学生尼柯·米德喜也在会上讲话，他说：“我们和中国是兄弟，我们在这里找到了最亲密的朋友”。
    苏联和其他三十多个国家在北大的留学生也参加了晚会，向他们的阿尔巴尼亚同学表示祝贺。
    中国和阿尔巴尼亚学生都在晚会上演出节目，并合作演出了地拉那的民间舞蹈。
