Title: Nepal-China Friendship Association Delegation Returns Home After Concluding Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1959-11-22
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Nepal-China Friendship Association delegation, led by Chairman P. Bahadur, which came to China at the invitation of the China-Nepal Friendship Association to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the National Day and to visit, left Beijing by train on the morning of the 17th via Guangzhou to return home. Among those who saw them off at the station was Zhou Shujia, Vice President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association.
    Before their departure, Zhou Shujia, Vice President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association, held a reception to bid farewell to the delegation.
    During the delegation's stay in Beijing, Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, met with the delegation members of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, including Jialun, Vice Chairman of the Nepal Committee for the Defense of World Peace.

### 结束在我国访问  尼中友协代表团回国

1959-11-22
第3版()
专栏：

    结束在我国访问
    尼中友协代表团回国
    新华社讯　应中国尼泊尔友好协会邀请来我国参加国庆十周年庆祝典礼和访问的尼中友协代表团，在团长普·巴哈杜尔率领下，已于17日上午乘火车离京经广州回国。到车站欢送的有中国尼泊尔友好协会副会长周叔迦等。
    临行前，中尼友协副会长周叔迦举行酒会欢送代表团。
    代表团在京期间，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若接见了尼中友协代表团团员、尼泊尔保卫世界和平委员会副主席嘉拉恩。
