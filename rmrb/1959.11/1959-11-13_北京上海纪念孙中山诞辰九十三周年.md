Title: Beijing and Shanghai Commemorate the 93rd Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth
Author:
Date: 1959-11-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 12th - Beijing: He Xiangning, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Zhizhong, vice chairmen of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and more than 40 members of the Standing Committee and Central Committee of the RevComm in Beijing, visited the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall in Biyun Temple in the Western Hills of Beijing this morning.

    A memorial ceremony was held in the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall. He Xiangning presented flowers to the portrait of Sun Yat-sen, and all participants stood in silent tribute.

    After the visit, He Xiangning addressed the RevComm members who attended the ceremony, encouraging them to learn from Sun Yat-sen's fearless spirit of fighting for the revolution throughout his life and to engage in the glorious socialist construction cause together under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong.

    Xinhua News Agency, November 12th - Shanghai: This morning, Chen Pixian and Wei Wenbo, Secretaries of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, Niu Shucai, Song Jiwen, and Sheng Pihua, Vice Mayors of Shanghai, Liu Jingji, Shen Tilan, Hu Juewen, and Shu Xincheng, Vice Chairmen of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, as well as responsible persons from the Shanghai Municipal organizations of various democratic parties and Shanghai's people's organizations, paid their respects at Sun Yat-sen's former residence to commemorate the 93rd anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth. Personnel from the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, and the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the RevComm presented flower baskets at Sun Yat-sen's former residence.

### 北京上海纪念孙中山诞辰九十三周年

1959-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    北京上海纪念孙中山诞辰九十三周年
    新华社12日讯　今天是孙中山先生诞生九十三周年纪念日。中国国民党革命委员会副主席何香凝、蔡廷锴、张治中及在北京的民革常务委员和中央委员等四十多人，上午前往京郊西山碧云寺参谒孙中山纪念堂。
    在孙中山纪念堂举行了参谒仪式。何香凝向孙中山先生遗像献花，全体参谒的人肃立致敬。
    参谒完毕后，何香凝向参加仪式的民革成员讲话，勉励他们学习孙中山先生一生为革命奋斗的大无畏精神，要在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，共同来从事光辉灿烂的社会主义建设事业。
    新华社上海12日电　今天上午，中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显、魏文伯，上海市副市长牛树才、宋季文、盛丕华，政协上海市委员会副主席刘靖基、沈体兰、胡厥文、舒新城，以及各民主党派上海市地方组织和上海市各人民团体的负责人瞻仰中山故居，纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十三周年。中共上海市委、上海市人民委员会、政协上海市委员会、民革上海市委员会等单位的人员都到中山故居敬献了花篮。
