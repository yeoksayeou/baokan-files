Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-11-10
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, November 9th. Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with the Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation headed by the Speaker of the House of Representatives, Francisco Rodriguez Camusso, and the delegation members: Dr. Angel Maria Gianola, Semala Micelia, Juan Rodriguez Correa, Francisco Mario Ubillos, and Martinez Moreno.

    Present at the meeting were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理会见乌拉圭议会代表团

1959-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理会见乌拉圭议会代表团
    新华社9日讯　周恩来总理今天下午会见了乌拉圭议会代表团团长、众议院议长弗朗西斯科·罗德里格斯·卡穆索和代表团团员：安赫耳·玛丽亚·希亚诺拉博士、塞马拉·米切利亚、胡安·罗德里格斯·科雷亚、弗朗西斯科·马里奥·乌比略斯、马丁内斯·莫雷诺。
    接见时在座的有：中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、外交部美澳司司长申健、中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达。
