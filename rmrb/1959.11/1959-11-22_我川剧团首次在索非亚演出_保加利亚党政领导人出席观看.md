Title: Our Sichuan Opera Troupe Performs for the First Time in Sofia; Bulgarian Party and Government Leaders Attend
Author:
Date: 1959-11-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, November 21st – The Chinese Sichuan Opera Troupe gave its first performance at the Sofia People's Opera House on the evening of the 20th, and was warmly welcomed by the audience.

Bulgarian Communist Party and government leaders Damyanov, Traykov, Chervenkov, and others watched the performance.

The Sichuan Opera Troupe performed "Stopping the Horse," "Hua Rong Shoots the Eagle," "Autumn River," "Affinity in the Cabinet," and "The Flooding of Jinshan Temple." After each performance, the more than one thousand audience members in the hall applauded enthusiastically for a long time, and the actors took repeated curtain calls.

After the performance, Boshnakov, Deputy Director of the People's Opera House, delivered a speech on behalf of the Bulgarian Ministry of Education and Culture, the Committee for Friendly Relations and Cultural Ties with Foreign Countries, and all cultural figures in Sofia, warmly welcoming the Sichuan Opera Troupe. Ming Lan, the head of the Sichuan Opera Troupe, delivered a reply.

The Sichuan Opera Troupe arrived here on the 19th at the invitation of the Bulgarian Committee for Friendly Relations and Cultural Ties with Foreign Countries for a performance tour. In addition to Sofia, they will also perform in Plovdiv, Pleven, Dimitrov, and five other cities for more than ten days. On the morning of the 19th, the Bulgarian Committee for Friendly Relations and Cultural Ties with Foreign Countries held a press conference for the Sichuan Opera Troupe's visit and performance.

### 我川剧团首次在索非亚演出  保加利亚党政领导人出席观看

1959-11-22
第3版()
专栏：

    我川剧团首次在索非亚演出
    保加利亚党政领导人出席观看
    新华社索非亚21日电　中国川剧团20日晚在索非亚人民歌剧院举行了首次演出，受到观众热烈欢迎。
    保加利亚共产党和政府领导人达米扬诺夫、特莱科夫、契尔文科夫等观看了演出。
    川剧团表演了“拦马”、“花荣射雕”、“秋江”、“柜中缘”和“水漫金山寺”等剧目。每个节目演完时，大厅里一千多名观众都长时间地热烈鼓掌，演员们一再谢幕。
    人民歌剧院副院长鲍什纳科夫在演出结束后，代表保加利亚教育和文化部、对外友好和文化联络委员会和索非亚市全体文化界人士致词，对川剧团表示热烈欢迎。川剧团团长明蓝致了答词。
    川剧团是应保加利亚对外友好和文化联络委员会的邀请于19日到达这里进行访问演出的。除在索非亚市外，还将到普罗夫迪夫、普勒文、季米特洛夫等八个城市进行十多天的演出。19日上午，保加利亚对外友好和文化联络委员会曾为川剧团的访问演出举行记者招待会。
