Title: Head of Finnish Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The head of the Finnish Government Trade Delegation, Mr. Han Caranta, Deputy Director of the Commercial Department of the Finnish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Beijing today. The deputy head of the delegation, Mr. Neva Leinen, and four members including Mr. Kong Nara, had arrived in Beijing earlier. Mr. Nu Dilla, Commercial Counselor of the Finnish Embassy in China, is also a member of the delegation. Mr. Chen Ming, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and others welcomed them at the airport today. Mr. War Wanni, the Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Neva Leinen, the deputy head of the delegation, and others also went to greet them.

### 芬兰政府贸易代表团团长到京

1959-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    芬兰政府贸易代表团团长到京
    新华社13日讯　芬兰政府贸易代表团团长、芬兰外交部商务司副司长杭卡兰塔已经在今天到达北京。代表团副团长耐瓦莱宁和团员孔纳拉等四人已先期到达北京。芬兰驻华大使馆商务参赞努狄拉也是代表团的成员。今天到机场欢迎的有对外贸易部第三局副局长陈明等人。芬兰驻华大使瓦尔万尼、副团长耐瓦莱宁等也去迎接。
