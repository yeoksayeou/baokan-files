Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Félix-Roland Moumié, President of the Union of the Peoples of Cameroon
Author:
Date: 1959-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th -- Premier Zhou Enlai received Félix-Roland Moumié, President of the Union of the Peoples of Cameroon, who is currently visiting China, this afternoon, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him. Present at the reception was Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity.
