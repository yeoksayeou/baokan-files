Title: Kenzo Matsumura Departs Beijing for Kunming Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, November 13th: Mr. Kenzo Matsumura of Japan and his entourage left Beijing by plane this morning to visit Kunming. They will visit Kunming and other cities before returning to their country.

    Among those who saw them off at the airport were: Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Xiruo, Chu Tunan, Cai Tingkai, Xu Guangping, Wang Kunlun, Zhao Puchu, Wu Maosun, Xie Nanguang, Zhao Anbo, Peng Yan, and others.

    Koichi Saiyonji, a Japanese peace activist in Beijing, also saw them off at the airport.

### 松村谦三离京赴昆明访问

1959-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    松村谦三离京赴昆明访问
    新华社13日讯　日本松村谦三先生和他的随行人员今天上午乘飞机离开北京，前往昆明参观访问。他们将在昆明等城市参观访问以后回国。
    到机场送行的有各方面的人士：廖承志、张奚若、楚图南、蔡廷锴、许广平、王昆仑、赵朴初、吴茂荪、谢南光、赵安博、彭炎等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也到机场送行。
