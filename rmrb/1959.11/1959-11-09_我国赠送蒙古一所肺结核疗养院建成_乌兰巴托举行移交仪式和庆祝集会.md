Title: China Donates Tuberculosis Sanatorium to Mongolia; Handover Ceremony and Celebration Rally Held in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1959-11-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar: A tuberculosis sanatorium donated by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to the Central Council of Mongolian Trade Unions has been completed, and a handover ceremony and mass celebration rally were held in Ulaanbaatar on the afternoon of November 5th.

    At the mass rally, the Presidium of the Great People's Hural and the Council of Ministers of Mongolia awarded medals, badges, and certificates of merit to the Chinese construction workers.

    On the morning of November 5th, Mongolian party and state leaders Tsedenbal, Sambuu, Tsende, and others, along with Xie Fusheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, visited the sanatorium.

### 我国赠送蒙古一所肺结核疗养院建成  乌兰巴托举行移交仪式和庆祝集会

1959-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我国赠送蒙古一所肺结核疗养院建成
    乌兰巴托举行移交仪式和庆祝集会
    新华社乌兰巴托电　中华全国总工会赠送给蒙古工会中央理事会的一所肺结核疗养院已经建成并于5日下午在乌兰巴托举行移交仪式和群众庆祝集会。
    在群众集会上，蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团和部长会议授予中国建筑职工以勋章、奖章和奖状。
    5日上午，蒙古党和国家领导人泽登巴尔、桑布、曾德等人和中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生参观了疗养院。
