Title: Gale and Cooling Alert
Author:
Date: 1959-11-24
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd. The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a gale and cooling alert at 8:00 PM on November 23rd, saying: A cold air mass is moving into our country. The front of the cold air has reached the Yellow River basin at noon today and will continue to move southeastward. It is expected that within the next 48 hours, the Bohai Sea, Yellow Sea, East China Sea, Taiwan Strait, and northeastern part of the South China Sea will experience northerly winds of force six to eight from north to south; the northwestern part of the South China Sea and the Beibu Gulf will have northerly winds of force five to seven; the Huai River basin and the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River provinces will have northerly winds of force five to six. The wind force on the river and lake surfaces in the Huai River basin and the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River provinces may reach force six to seven; Sichuan, Guizhou, northeastern Yunnan, Guangdong, and Guangxi will have northerly winds of force four to five. It is expected that within 48 hours after the cold air front passes, the minimum temperature in the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River provinces and the Huai River basin will drop by six to eight degrees Celsius; the minimum temperature in Sichuan, Guizhou, northeastern Yunnan, Guangdong, and Guangxi will drop by three to five degrees Celsius. The Huai River basin and the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River, Sichuan, and Guizhou provinces will also experience one to two days of cloudy and rainy weather.

### 大风和降温警报

1959-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    大风和降温警报
    新华社23日讯　11月23日下午八时，中央气象科学研究所发布大风和降温警报说：有冷空气移入我国，今天中午冷空气前锋已到达黄河流域，冷空气将继续向东南方向移动。预计未来四十八小时内，渤海、黄海、东海、台湾海峡、南海东北部，将自北向南先后有六到八级偏北风；南海西北部、北部湾将有五到七级的偏北风；淮河流域、长江流域中下游各省，将有五到六级的偏北风，淮河流域和长江流域中下游各省的江面、湖面风力，可达六到七级；四川、贵州、云南东北部、广东、广西将有四到五级的偏北风。预计冷空气前锋过后的四十八小时内，长江流域中下游各省，淮河流域各地的最低气温将下降摄氏六到八度；四川、贵州、云南东北部、广东、广西各地的最低气温将下降三到五度。淮河流域和长江流域中下游，四川、贵州各省，并将维持一到两天的阴雨天气。
