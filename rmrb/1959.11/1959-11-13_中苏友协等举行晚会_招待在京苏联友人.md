Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and others hold soiree to entertain Soviet friends in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-13
Page: 5

    Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and others hold soiree
    To entertain Soviet friends in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th November. To commemorate the 42nd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the Chinese Dramatists Association jointly held a soiree tonight. The Harbin Repertory Theatre, which is in Beijing for performances, performed the Soviet play "An Optimistic Tragedy" to entertain Soviet friends in Beijing.
    Those attending the performance included Fu Ming, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and other diplomatic officials of the Embassy, some Soviet experts in Beijing, and Soviet scholars currently visiting China. People from all walks of life who attended the performance included Shao Lizi, Li Zhuchen, Chen Shutong, Shi Liang, Hu Weide, Ge Baoquan, Lao She, Xiao San, Shao Quanlin, Cao Yu, Shu Xiuwen, and others.
    After the performance, the wives of Soviet experts presented flowers to the actors.

### 中苏友协等举行晚会  招待在京苏联友人

1959-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

    中苏友协等举行晚会
    招待在京苏联友人
    新华社12日讯　为纪念伟大的十月社会主义革命四十二周年，中苏友好协会总会、中国文学艺术界联合会和中国戏剧家协会今晚联合举行晚会，由来北京演出的哈尔滨话剧院演出苏联话剧“乐观的悲剧”，招待在北京的苏联友人。
  出席观看演出的有苏联驻华大使馆参赞符明和大使馆其他外交官员，部分在京的苏联专家以及正在我国访问的苏联学者。观看演出的各界人士有邵力子、李烛尘、陈叔通、史良、胡韦德、戈宝权、老舍、萧三、邵荃麟、曹禺、舒绣文等。
    演出后，苏联专家夫人向演员们献了花。
