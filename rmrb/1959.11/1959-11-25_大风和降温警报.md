Title: Gale and Temperature Drop Alert
Author:
Date: 1959-11-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, November 24th. The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a gale and temperature drop alert at 12:00 noon on the 24th:
　　This morning, the cold air front has reached the areas of Zhejiang, the Nanling Mountains, and Guizhou. Tomorrow, the cold air front will enter the South China Sea. It is expected that in the next thirty-six hours, the East China Sea, the Taiwan Strait, and the northeastern part of the South China Sea will experience northerly winds of force six to eight. The northwestern part of the South China Sea and the Beibu Gulf will experience northerly winds of force six to seven. Guangdong and Guangxi will experience northerly winds of force four to five. Tomorrow or the morning after, the temperature in areas north of the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River will drop to 0 degrees Celsius or below. The temperature in northern Hunan, northern Jiangxi, northern Zhejiang, southern Jiangsu, and southern Anhui will drop to 0 to 3 degrees Celsius. The minimum temperature in southern Hunan, southern Jiangxi, and other places will drop to 2 to 5 degrees Celsius. Frost will appear in the aforementioned areas on clear nights or mornings after the wind stops.

### 大风和降温警报

1959-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    大风和降温警报
    新华社24日讯　中央气象科学研究所24日中午十二时发布大风和降温警报：
    今天上午，冷空气前锋已经到达浙江、南岭、贵州一带。明天冷空气前锋将进入南海。预计未来三十六小时内，东海、台湾海峡、南海东北部将有六到八级偏北风。南海西北部、北部湾将有六到七级偏北风。广东、广西将有四到五级偏北风。明天或后天早晨，长江中下游以北地区，气温将下降到摄氏零度或零度以下。湖南北部、江西北部、浙江北部、江苏南部、安徽南部等地的气温将下降到摄氏零度到三度。湖南南部、江西南部等地的最低气温将下降到摄氏二到五度。上述地区，在风停天晴的夜晚或早晨将有霜冻出现。
