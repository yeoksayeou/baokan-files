Title: Gale and Temperature Drop Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-11-07
Page: 4

    Gale and Temperature Drop Warning
    The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a gale and temperature drop warning at 12:00 noon on the 6th:
    Cold air from Siberia will move southeastward. It is expected that in the next 48 hours, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, northern Shanxi, northwestern Hebei, and the Northeast Plain will experience northerly winds of level 6 or 7. Eastern Gansu, Shaanxi, southern Shanxi, and southern Hebei will experience northerly winds of level 5 or 6. The Bohai Sea will experience northerly winds of level 7 or 8. In the above areas, the temperature will drop by 7 to 10 degrees Celsius within 24 hours after the cold air front passes.

### 大风和降温警报

1959-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

    大风和降温警报
    中央气象科学研究所6日中午十二时发布大风和降温警报：
    西伯利亚的冷气将向东南方移动。预计未来四十八小时内，内蒙古自治区、山西北部、河北西北部、东北平原有六、七级偏北风，甘肃东部、陕西、山西南部、河北南部将有五、六级偏北风，渤海海面将有七、八级偏北风。以上地区在冷空气前锋过后二十四小时内，气温将下降摄氏七至十度。
