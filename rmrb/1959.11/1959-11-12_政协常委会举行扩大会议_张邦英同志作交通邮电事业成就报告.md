Title: CPPCC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting; Comrade Zhang Bangying Delivers Report on Achievements in Transportation and Postal Communications

Author:

Date: 1959-11-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held its fifth enlarged meeting this afternoon at the CPPCC Auditorium. Zhang Bangying, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered a report on the achievements in transportation and postal communications.

Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, presided over the meeting. CPPCC members in Beijing, members of the central committees of various democratic parties, and members of the Beijing Municipal CPPCC attended the meeting. Visiting delegations from the CPPCC of Hohhot and Qiqihar also attended the report.

### 政协常委会举行扩大会议  张邦英同志作交通邮电事业成就报告

1959-11-12
第2版()
专栏：

    政协常委会举行扩大会议
    张邦英同志作交通邮电事业成就报告
    新华社11日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会今天下午在政协礼堂举行第五次扩大会议，中共中央交通工作部副部长张邦英在会上作了关于交通邮电事业的成就的报告。
    政协全国委员会副主席沈钧儒主持会议。在京的政协委员、各民主党派的中央委员和北京市政协委员等列席了今天的会议。呼和浩特市和齐齐哈尔市政协参观团也到会听了报告。
