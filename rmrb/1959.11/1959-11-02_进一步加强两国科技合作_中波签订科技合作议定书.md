Title: Further Strengthen Sino-Polish Scientific and Technological Cooperation: China and Poland Sign Scientific and Technological Cooperation Protocol
Author:
Date: 1959-11-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 1st: Communiqué of the Sixth Session of the Sino-Polish Joint Standing Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Poland.

The Sixth Session of the Sino-Polish Joint Standing Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Poland was held in Beijing from October 20 to November 1, 1959. The meeting was conducted in an atmosphere of sincere friendship and warmth. During the meeting, both sides held discussions on issues related to further strengthening scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries and direct contacts between scientific research institutions, and signed a protocol on November 1.

The protocol stipulates that the People's Republic of China will provide the People's Republic of Poland with technical data and research materials related to the chemical, metallurgical, food, and coal industries, and receive Polish experts to China to conduct inspections on the use of electrical equipment and the production of calcium carbide; the People's Republic of Poland will provide the People's Republic of China with technical data and research materials related to the coal and chemical industries, as well as agriculture, forestry, and construction engineering, and receive Chinese experts to Poland to conduct inspections on beet seed production.

The protocol was signed by Zhong Ziyun, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry, and Z. Koi, Chairman of the Polish Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of the Polish Ministry of Heavy Industry.

Attending the signing ceremony of the protocol were all members of the committee and Liu Xiyao, Deputy Director of the Scientific and Technological Commission of the People's Republic of China, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. F. Lato, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the People's Republic of Poland in China, also attended the signing ceremony.

### 进一步加强两国科技合作  中波签订科技合作议定书

1959-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步加强两国科技合作
    中波签订科技合作议定书
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国和波兰人民共和国科学技术合作联合常任委员会第六届会议公报。
    中华人民共和国和波兰人民共和国科学技术合作联合常任委员会第六届会议自1959年10月20日至11月1日在北京举行。会议是在真诚友好和热烈的气氛中进行的。在会议上，双方曾就有关进一步加强两国科学技术合作和科学研究机构之间的直接联系问题进行了讨论，并在11月1日签订了议定书。
    议定书规定，中华人民共和国将供给波兰人民共和国有关化学、冶金、食品和煤炭工业等方面的技术资料和研究材料，接待波兰专家来华在电器器材使用和电石生产方面进行考察；波兰人民共和国将供给中华人民共和国有关煤炭和化学工业，以及农业、林业和建筑工程等方面的技术资料和研究材料，接待中国专家赴波兰在甜菜种子生产等方面进行考察。
    在议定书上签字的是：委员会中国组主席、煤炭工业部副部长钟子云，委员会波兰组主席、波兰重工业部副部长兹·科依。
    出席会议议定书签字仪式的有委员会全体委员和中华人民共和国科学技术委员会副主任刘西尧、外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清。波兰人民共和国驻华临时代办傅拉托也参加了签字仪式。
