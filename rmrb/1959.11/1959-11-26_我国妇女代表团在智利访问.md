Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Visits Chile
Author:
Date: 1959-11-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, November 24th: The Chinese women's delegation, invited to attend the Latin American Women's Congress, today visited the city of Rancagua together with women representatives from Bulgaria and the Soviet Union. They were invited to visit by Baltasar Castro, Director of the World Peace Council and writer. Rancagua is a city about 51 miles from Santiago with a population of about 40,000.
    The Chinese women's delegation will attend the opening ceremony of the Chinese Art Exhibition held in the Chilean capital on December 3.

### 我国妇女代表团在智利访问

1959-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    我国妇女代表团在智利访问
    新华社圣地亚哥24日电　应邀参加拉丁美洲妇女代表大会的中国妇女代表今天与保加利亚和苏联的妇女代表一起访问了兰加瓜城。她们是受世界和平理事会理事、作家巴耳塔萨·卡斯特罗的邀请去访问的。兰加瓜是距圣地亚哥五十一哩、约有四万人口的一个城市。
    中国妇女代表团将在12月3日参加在智利首都举行的中国艺术展览会的开幕式。
