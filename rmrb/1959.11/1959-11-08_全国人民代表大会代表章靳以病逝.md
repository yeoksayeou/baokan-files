Title: National People's Congress Deputy Zhang Jin Yi Passes Away Due to Illness
Author:
Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 7th. Zhang Jin Yi, Deputy to the National People's Congress, Deputy to the Shanghai Municipal People's Congress, Member of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Writers Association, and Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese Writers Association, passed away in Shanghai at 0:16 on November 7th due to rheumatic heart disease and acute heart failure, despite medical treatment. He was 50 years old.

The funeral committee for Comrade Zhang Jin Yi was established on the 7th.

### 全国人民代表大会代表章靳以病逝

1959-11-08
第2版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会代表章靳以病逝
    新华社上海7日电　全国人民代表大会代表、上海市人民代表大会代表、上海市人民委员会委员、中国作家协会书记处书记、中国作家协会上海分会副主席章靳以同志因患风湿性心脏病、急性心力衰竭症，诊治无效，于11月7日零时十六分在上海逝世，享年五十岁。
    章靳以同志的治丧委员会已于7日成立。
