Title: Soviet Leaders Send a Telegram to Our Country's Leaders, Thanking Them for Their Congratulations on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1959-11-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 25th:

    Soviet Party and state leaders sent a telegram on the 24th to the Chinese Party and state leaders, expressing heartfelt thanks for their congratulations on the 42nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China;
Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China;
Comrade Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China;
Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China;
Dear Comrades!

    Please allow us, on behalf of the Soviet people, the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and the Soviet government, to extend our heartfelt thanks to you, and through you to the entire Chinese people, the Communist Party of China, and the Chinese government, for the warm congratulations and good wishes on the 42nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.
    The continuous growth and consolidation of the world socialist system concretely demonstrates the invincible vitality of Marxist-Leninist doctrine, and the ideas of the Great October Revolution. The victory of the great people's Revolution in China is one of the most vivid examples of this. Not long ago, the Soviet people, the people of socialist countries, and all progressive humanity, together with the fraternal Chinese people, celebrated the brilliant 10th anniversary of the victory of the Chinese Revolution. The historically significant achievements made by the Chinese people under the long-tested leadership of the Communist Party of China in socialist construction have transformed the People's Republic of China into a powerful world power that plays an increasingly important role in international relations.
    The Soviet people are sincerely pleased with China's rapid progress on the road to socialism, and wish the fraternal Chinese people new and greater achievements in the development of the national economy, science, and culture. Recently, thanks to the strengthening of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and other countries of the world socialist system, as well as all peace-loving forces, the tense situation in international relations has been eased, and the international situation has improved. The peace-loving forces of the whole world are waging a resolute struggle for the realization of the proposal for complete and thorough disarmament and for the peaceful coexistence of countries with different social systems. The People's Republic of China's active support for the Soviet Union's proposal for complete and thorough disarmament is a major contribution to this noble struggle. For ten years, the close unity, friendship, and fraternal cooperation between the Soviet and Chinese peoples have been more consolidated and unbreakable than ever before, because the great ideas of Marxism-Leninism and the common goals of our two peoples have made this unity, friendship, and cooperation even more sublime. The Soviet people will continue to do everything possible to further consolidate and develop the fraternal friendship and close cooperation between the Soviet and Chinese peoples, to strengthen the consolidation and unity of the world socialist system, and to ensure lasting peace and the security of all peoples.
    We extend fraternal greetings to the great Chinese people who are building socialism!
    May the unbreakable friendship and co-operation between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China, the mighty bulwark of world peace, last forever!
    Long live the rock-solid unity of the countries of the world socialist system!

Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union
Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union

Moscow, the Kremlin, November 24, 1959

### 苏联领导人致电我国领导人  感谢对十月革命节的祝贺

1959-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联领导人致电我国领导人
    感谢对十月革命节的祝贺
    新华社25日讯　苏联共产党和国家领导人在24日打电报给中国共产党和国家领导人，衷心感谢对于伟大的十月社会主义革命四十二周年的祝贺。电报全文如下：北京中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同志中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务
  委员会委员长朱德同志中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志亲爱的同志们！
    在伟大的十月社会主义革命四十二周年的时候，承致热烈的祝贺和良好的祝愿，请允许我们代表苏联人民、苏联共产党和苏联政府向你们，并且通过你们向全中国人民、中国共产党和中国政府表示衷心的感谢。
    世界社会主义体系的不断壮大和巩固，具体地表现着马克思列宁主义学说和伟大十月革命思想的不可战胜的生命力。伟大的人民革命在中国的胜利，就是这方面最鲜明的例证之一。不久以前，苏联人民、社会主义各国人民和全体进步人类，同兄弟的中国人民一道，庆祝了中国革命胜利的光辉的十周年。中国人民在中国共产党的久经考验的领导下在社会主义建设中所获得的具有历史意义的成就，已经把中华人民共和国变成在国际关系中起着日益重要作用的强大的世界大国。
    苏联人民对于中国在社会主义道路上的飞速前进感到由衷的高兴，并祝兄弟的中国人民在发展国民经济、科学和文化的事业中获得新的更大的成就。最近，由于苏联、中华人民共和国和世界社会主义体系其他国家，以及一切爱好和平的力量的壮大，使国际关系中的紧张局势得到了缓和，国际形势得到了改善。全世界爱好和平的力量为了实现全面、彻底裁军建议和不同社会制度的国家的和平共处展开了坚决的斗争，中华人民共和国对苏联关于全面、彻底裁军建议的积极支持是对这一崇高的斗争的重大贡献。十年来，苏中两国人民的亲密团结、友谊和兄弟般的合作比过去任何时候都更加巩固，更加不可摧毁，因为马克思列宁主义的伟大思想和我们两国人民一致的目标使得这种团结、友谊和合作变得愈益崇高。苏联人民今后将为进一步巩固和发展苏中两国人民间的兄弟友谊和密切合作，为加强世界社会主义体系的巩固和团结，为保证持久和平和各国人民的安全竭尽一切努力。
    向正在建设社会主义的伟大的中国人民致以兄弟的敬意！
    愿世界和平强大的堡垒——苏联和中华人民共和国的牢不可破的友谊和合作万古常青！
    世界社会主义体系各国的坚如磐石的团结万岁！
    苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记苏联部长会议  主席尼·赫鲁晓夫
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席　克·伏罗希洛夫
                    1959年11月24日于莫斯科
                                    克里姆林宫
