Title: Large-scale Cotton Textile Mill Under Construction in Baotou
Author:
Date: 1959-11-30
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Baotou, November 29th: A modern, large-scale cotton textile mill is under construction in Baotou, a newly emerging iron and steel industrial city in China.

This cotton textile mill is the first of its kind in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. It will be equipped with 100,000 spindles and 2,000 looms. Once fully operational, it will be able to produce 60 million meters of khaki, gabardine, twill, plain cloth, coarse cloth, and other fabrics annually. Currently, the structural engineering of the main factory building, covering an area of over 60,000 square meters, has been more than half completed.

### 包头兴建大型棉纺织厂

1959-11-30
第2版()
专栏：

    包头兴建大型棉纺织厂
    据新华社包头二十九日电  我国新兴的钢铁工业城市——包头，正在兴建一座现代化大型棉纺织厂。
    这座棉纺织厂是内蒙古自治区第一座棉纺织厂，将安装十万枚纺锭，两千台布机，全部投入生产以后，每年可生产卡叽、华达呢、斜纹、平纹、粗布等六千万米。现在，占地达六万多平方米的主厂房结构工程，已完成了二分之一以上。
