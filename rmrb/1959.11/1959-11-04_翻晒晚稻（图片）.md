Title: Sunning Late Rice (Picture)
Author: Wang Ping
Date: 1959-11-04
Page: 4

    Sunning Late Rice
    After overcoming a severe drought, Yiyang County, Hunan Province, reaped a bumper harvest of single-season late rice, with an average yield per mu increasing by 27% compared to last year. The picture shows members of the Shatou People's Commune sunning the abundant late rice.

                                 Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Ping photographed.

王平

    翻晒晚稻
