Title: Picking Cotton
Author: Xing Caiying
Date: 1959-11-04
Page: 1

    Picking Cotton
    A group of girls picks cotton,
    Chatting and laughing merrily,
    Cotton like clouds hangs on the trees,
    Each hand takes a white cloud,
    You grab a handful, I grab a handful,
    Bringing home silver mountains, row on row.
    (An'guo County Commune Member Xing Caiying)
          (Originally published in Hebei Daily)

### 摘棉花
邢彩英
1959-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    摘棉花
    一群姑娘摘棉花，
    说说闹闹笑哈哈，
    棉花如云挂树上，
    朵朵白云手中拿，
    你一把来我一把，
    座座银山拉回家。
    （安国县社员  邢彩英）
          （原载河北日报）
