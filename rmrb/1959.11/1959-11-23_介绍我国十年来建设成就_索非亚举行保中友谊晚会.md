Title: Introducing China's Ten Years of Construction Achievements; Sofia Holds Bulgarian-Chinese Friendship Evening

Author:

Date: 1959-11-23
Page: 5

    Introducing China's Ten Years of Construction Achievements
    Sofia Holds Bulgarian-Chinese Friendship Evening
    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, November 21st. The Bulgarian National Committee for the Defense of Peace and the Sofia City Committee held a Bulgarian-Chinese friendship evening on the 20th at the Cultural Workers Club. Pilinski, the Vice-Chairman of the Bulgarian National Committee for the Defense of Peace, who attended the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China in Beijing, introduced his profound impressions of the new China at the evening.

### 介绍我国十年来建设成就  索非亚举行保中友谊晚会

1959-11-23
第5版()
专栏：

    介绍我国十年来建设成就
    索非亚举行保中友谊晚会
    新华社索非亚21日电　保加利亚保卫和平全国委员会和索非亚市委员会，20日晚在文化工作者俱乐部举行保中友谊晚会。曾到北京参加中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝大典的保加利亚保卫和平全国委员会副主席皮林斯基，在晚会上介绍了他对新中国的深刻的印象。
