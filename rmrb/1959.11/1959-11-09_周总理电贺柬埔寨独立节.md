Title: Premier Zhou Sends Congratulatory Telegram for Cambodian Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1959-11-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 8th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram on November 7th to Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Norodom Sihanouk, congratulating him on the Kingdom of Cambodia's Independence Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia:

On the occasion of the Kingdom of Cambodia's Independence Day, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Highness, the Cambodian government, and the Cambodian people on behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people, and in my own name.
Since the establishment of formal diplomatic relations between China and Cambodia, the long-standing friendship between our two peoples has been further developed. I believe that, through our joint and sincere efforts, the friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries will be further enhanced and strengthened on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

I wish Your Highness, the Cambodian government, and the Cambodian people new achievements in the cause of safeguarding national independence and developing the national economy, and I wish your country prosperity and strength.

Zhou Enlai
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
November 7, 1959

### 周总理电贺柬埔寨独立节

1959-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺柬埔寨独立节
    新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来7日打电报给柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王祝贺柬埔寨王国独立节。电文如下：柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下：
    在柬埔寨王国独立节的时候，我谨代表中国政府和中国人民并且以我个人的名义向殿下、柬埔寨政府和柬埔寨人民致以热诚的祝贺。
    自从中柬两国建立了正常的外交关系以来，我们两国人民之间久已存在的友谊得到了新的发展。我相信，通过我们共同的和真诚的努力，我们两国间的友好合作关系，将在和平共处五项原则的基础上，获得进一步的增进和加强。
    祝殿下、柬埔寨政府和柬埔寨人民在维护民族独立和发展民族经济的事业中取得新的成就，祝贵国繁荣昌盛。
              中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                      1959年11月7日
