Title: All-China Women's Federation Sends Telegram to Congolese Women, Supporting the Struggle of the Congolese People
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, November 25th: The All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China today sent a telegram to Congolese women, supporting the Congolese people's struggle for independence.
　　The telegram said, "On behalf of hundreds of millions of Chinese women, we express our highest respect and firm support to the Congolese people and Congolese sisters who are heroically fighting for national independence."
　　The telegram also said, "Your heroic struggle has inspired the fighting spirit of all African people who are striving for national independence and has shaken the colonialist rule in Africa. We firmly believe that the staunch struggle of the Congolese people and women, with the support of socialist countries, Asian and African countries, and all people of justice in the world, will surely achieve final victory."

### 全国妇联致电刚果妇女 　支持刚果人民的斗争

1959-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    全国妇联致电刚果妇女
　  支持刚果人民的斗争
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会今天打电报给刚果妇女，支持刚果人民争取独立的斗争。
    电报说，“我们代表亿万中国妇女向正在为争取民族独立而英勇斗争的刚果人民和刚果姊妹们表示崇高的敬意和坚决的支持。”
    电报又说，“你们的英勇斗争，鼓舞了所有正在争取民族独立的非洲人民的斗志，动摇了殖民主义在非洲的统治。我们深信刚果人民和妇女的坚强斗争，在社会主义各国和亚非各国人民以及全世界正义人士的支持下，必将取得最后胜利。”
