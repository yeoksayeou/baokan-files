Title: ROK Army Pilot Defects

Author: 
Date: 1959-11-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 24th - Pyongyang newspapers reported today that First Lieutenant Pilot Wen Yongxi of the flight squadron of the 25th Division of the 6th Army Corps of the Rhee Syngman puppet army defected on the 22nd, flying an L19A aircraft to northern Korea.

### 李伪军一飞行员起义

1959-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    李伪军一飞行员起义
    据新华社平壤24日电　平壤报纸今天报道，李承晚伪军第六军团第二十五师团飞行队中尉飞行员文永熙22日驾驶L19A型飞机起义，飞到朝鲜北部。
