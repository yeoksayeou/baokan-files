Title: Chinese Art Exhibition Opens in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1959-11-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 9th. A Chinese Art Exhibition opened at the National Museum in Warsaw at noon on the 9th. This exhibition was jointly organized by the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association and the National Museum to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Among those who attended the opening ceremony of the exhibition today were the Chairman of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association, Indrikhovschi, the Polish Minister of Culture and Art, Galinski, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Rapacki, people from all walks of life in Warsaw, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Poland.

Wang Bingnan, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, also attended the opening ceremony of the exhibition.

### 我艺术展览会在华沙开幕

1959-11-10
第5版()
专栏：

    我艺术展览会在华沙开幕
    新华社华沙9日电　中国艺术展览会9日中午在华沙国家博物馆开幕。这个展览会是波中友好协会和国家博物馆为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年而联合举办的。
    今天参加展览会开幕式的有波中友好协会主席英德里霍夫斯基、波兰文化艺术部长加林斯基、外交部长腊帕茨基、华沙各界人士以及各国驻波兰外交使节。
    中国驻波兰大使王炳南也出席了展览会开幕式。
