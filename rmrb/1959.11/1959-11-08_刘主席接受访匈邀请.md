Title: Chairman Liu Accepts Invitation to Visit Hungary
Author:
Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th — During the visit of István Dobi, Chairman of the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic, to the People's Republic of China, he invited Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, to visit the Hungarian People's Republic at an appropriate time. Chairman Liu Shaoqi accepted the invitation.

### 刘主席接受访匈邀请

1959-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接受访匈邀请
    新华社7日讯　匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比·伊斯特万同志在中华人民共和国访问期间，曾经邀请中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同志在适当时机访问匈牙利人民共和国。刘少奇主席表示接受这个邀请。
