Title: Soviet Youth Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Win Double Victories Against Jiangsu Team
Author:
Date: 1959-11-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, November 26th. The Soviet National Youth Men's and Women's Basketball Teams won against the Jiangsu Men's and Women's Basketball Teams in friendly matches held in Nanjing tonight. The score for the men's team was 88 to 65, and for the women's team, it was 62 to 36.

The visiting men's team changed its lineup multiple times today, but it consistently maintained an advantage in height, leading 47 to 28 in the first half. The Jiangsu women's basketball team played relatively tight defense in the first half, tying with the visiting team six times during the game. However, after reaching 18 points each, due to continuous passing errors, the visiting team gained a 26 to 18 advantage by the end of the first half. In the second half, the visiting team played more aggressively, and the score gap widened.

### 苏联青年男女篮球队双胜江苏队

1959-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联青年男女篮球队双胜江苏队
    新华社南京26日电　苏联国家青年男、女篮球队今晚在南京举行的友谊赛中，双双胜江苏男、女篮球队。男子队的比数是八十八比六十五，女子队是六十二比三十六。
    客队男子队今天多次变换阵容，但在身材高度上始终占着优势，上半场以四十七比二十八领先。江苏女子篮球队上半场防守的比较严，比赛进行中曾六次同客队打成平局。但打到十八平以后，因连续传球失误，上半场结束时客队取得了二十六比十八的优势。下半场客队越打越猛，比分距离越来越大。
