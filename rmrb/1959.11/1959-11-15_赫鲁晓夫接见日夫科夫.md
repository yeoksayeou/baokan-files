Title: Khrushchev Receives Zhivkov
Author:
Date: 1959-11-15
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 14th: TASS Moscow, November 13th: Khrushchev received Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, who was visiting the Soviet Union, on November 12th and had a conversation with him. The conversation took place in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.
    On the 13th, Suslov, Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU, received Zhivkov.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见日夫科夫

1959-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见日夫科夫
    新华社莫斯科14日电　据塔斯社莫斯科13日讯：赫鲁晓夫于11月12日接待了访问苏联的保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫，并且和他谈了话，谈话是在亲切友好的气氛中进行的。
    13日，苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏共中央书记苏斯洛夫接待了日夫科夫。
