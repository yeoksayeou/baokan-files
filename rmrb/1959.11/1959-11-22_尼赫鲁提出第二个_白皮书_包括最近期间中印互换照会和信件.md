Title: Nehru Presents Second "White Paper" Including Recent Exchanges of Notes and Letters Between China and India
Author:
Date: 1959-11-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 21st: According to the Indian Information Service, Indian Prime Minister Nehru presented a "White Paper" to the Lok Sabha (Lower House) on the 16th, which includes forty notes, memoranda, and letters exchanged between India and China from September to November 1959. It also includes a political map of the Sino-Indian border drawn by the Indian side and a "description of the historical background of the Indian Himalayan border."

On September 7th of this year, Nehru had presented a "White Paper" to the parliament, which included some documents exchanged between India and China during the period of 1954-1959.

The second "White Paper" presented on the 16th contains notes exchanged between the two governments on border issues and border incidents, and letters exchanged between the two prime ministers. In addition, there are documents relating to Indian violations of China's territorial waters and airspace, Indian police officers intruding into the Chinese Embassy in India, the "propaganda activities" of the Chinese Embassy in India, and "the treatment of Indian representatives and nationals in Tibet."

The Lok Sabha winter session, which opened on the 16th and will last for five weeks, will begin on the 25th and use this "White Paper" and other documents to conduct a special debate on the Sino-Indian border issue.

Delhi newspapers published most of the Indian documents included in this "White Paper" on the 17th, with only a passing mention of the Chinese documents.

### 尼赫鲁提出第二个“白皮书”  包括最近期间中印互换照会和信件

1959-11-22
第3版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁提出第二个“白皮书”
    包括最近期间中印互换照会和信件
    新华社新德里21日电　据印度新闻处报道，印度总理尼赫鲁16日向人民院（下院）提出了一个“白皮书”，其中包括1959年9月到11月期间印度和中国互换的四十个照会、备忘录和信件。它还包括印度方面绘制的中印边界的政治地图和“关于印度喜马拉雅山边界的历史背景的说明”。
    在今年9月7日，尼赫鲁曾经向议会提出过一个“白皮书”，其中包括1954—1959年期间印度和中国互换的一些文件。
    在16日提出的第二个“白皮书”中包含两国政府就边界问题和边境事件交换的照会和两国总理交换的信件。此外还有内容涉及到印度方面侵犯中国领海和领空、印度警官侵入中国驻印度大使馆、中国驻印度大使馆的“宣传活动”和“印度在西藏的代表和国民所受待遇”等问题的文件。
    在16日开幕并将举行五个星期的人民院冬季会议，将从25日开始，利用这个“白皮书”和其他文件就中印边界问题进行特别辩论。
    德里各报在17日刊登了这个“白皮书”中所包括的大多数印度方面的文件，而只是附带提了一下中国方面的文件。
