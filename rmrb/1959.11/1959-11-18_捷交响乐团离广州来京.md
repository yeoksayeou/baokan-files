Title: Czech Symphony Orchestra Leaves Guangzhou for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 17th: After performing in Guangzhou, the Czech Symphony Orchestra of the Czechoslovak Republic left Guangzhou by special train today for Beijing. Mr. Lehman, Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the Czechoslovak Republic in China, who came from Beijing to welcome the Symphony Orchestra, traveled on the same train. Those who came to the station to see them off included Du Ai, Director of the Guangdong Provincial Bureau of Culture and Council Member of the Sino-Czech Friendship Association, Hua Jia, Director of the Guangzhou Municipal Bureau of Culture, and Zhou Guojin, Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese Musicians Association.

### 捷交响乐团离广州来京

1959-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    捷交响乐团离广州来京
    据新华社广州17日电　捷克斯洛伐克共和国捷克交响乐团在广州演出后，于今天乘专车离开广州前往北京。从北京来迎接交响乐团的捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使馆文化参赞雷曼同车前往。到车站欢送的有中捷友好协会理事、广东省文化局局长杜埃，广州市文化局局长华嘉，中国音乐家协会广州分会主席周国瑾等。
