Title: Chinese Gymnastics Team Visits Burma
Author:
Date: 1959-11-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 27th. The Chinese men's and women's gymnastics team, led by Zhao Bin, Vice President of the Beijing Institute of Physical Education, departed from Beijing on the 26th for a visit and performance in Burma.
    The female gymnasts on the team are: Men Xiaomin, Hou Boyu, Chen Guide, and Ni Muxian. The male gymnasts are: Qiao Zhenying, Yang Jianmin, Cai Geng, and Liang Zhigang.

### 中国体操队去缅甸访问

1959-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    中国体操队去缅甸访问
    新华社27日讯　中国男、女体操队在北京体育学院副院长赵斌率领下，26日从北京动身前往缅甸访问和表演。
    体操队女子运动员有门晓敏、侯伯玉、陈贵德、倪慕贤四人，男子运动员是：乔振英、杨健民、蔡庚、梁志刚。
