Title: The Provisional Government of Algeria's Minister of Transportation and Liaison Delivers a Speech: Algerian Liberation Army Will Persist in Struggle
Author:
Date: 1959-11-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 25th -- The Minister of Transportation and Liaison for the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Mr. Boussouf, told reporters from the Maghreb Arab Press Agency today, "The Algerian government and the Algerian National Liberation Army are prepared to continue the struggle until victory." He also stated, "Our people have already experienced five years of struggle and have accumulated rich experience, enabling them to wage even more serious struggles."

Boussouf refuted the assertion that "the Algerian revolution has declined." He said that the Algerian people are not isolated in their struggle for independence. They have the support of the entire Arab world and various democratic countries.

Referring to a series of statements issued by the French authorities, he said, "These statements are contradictory."

He continued, "If France agrees to negotiate with the representatives we appoint, we will be able to achieve peace quickly, but if they refuse, it will prove that they do not want peace. France should be held responsible for the continuation of the war."

Regarding his talks with Moroccan Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Ibrahim, he said that during the talks, they discussed issues related to the Arab Maghreb, particularly the Algerian question. We reached complete agreement.

Boussouf left Rabat this afternoon for Oujda in order to make contact with Algerian refugees there.

### 阿临时政府交通联络部长发表谈话  阿尔及利亚解放军将坚持斗争

1959-11-27
第6版()
专栏：

    阿临时政府交通联络部长发表谈话
    阿尔及利亚解放军将坚持斗争
    新华社拉巴特25日电　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府交通联络部长布苏夫今天向马格里布阿拉伯新闻社记者发表谈话时说，“阿尔及利亚政府和阿尔及利亚民族解放军准备继续斗争，直到胜利。”他还说，“我国人民已经经历了五年的斗争，掌握了丰富的经验，能进行更严重的斗争”。
    布苏夫驳斥了这样一种说法：“阿尔及利亚革命衰退了”。他说，阿尔及利亚人民在为争取独立而进行的斗争中，并不是孤立的。他们得到整个阿拉伯世界和各民主国家的支持。
    在谈到法国当局所发表的一系列声明时，他说，“这些声明是互相矛盾的。”
    他又说，“如果法国同意和我们所指派的代表进行谈判，我们将能很快地实现和平，但是如果他们拒绝，那就证明他们不要和平。法国应对战争的继续负责。”
    关于他同摩洛哥首相兼外交大臣易卜拉欣的会谈，他说，在这次会谈中，他们谈了有关马格里布阿拉伯的一些问题，特别是阿尔及利亚问题。我们取得了完全一致的意见。
    布苏夫在今天下午离开拉巴特前往欧奇达，以便同那里的阿尔及利亚难民接触。
