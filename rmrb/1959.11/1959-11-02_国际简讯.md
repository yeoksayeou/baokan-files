International Briefs

    Japanese Coal Miners Strike
    Tokyo Dispatch: 29,000 coal miners from Japan's Mitsui Mining Company began a 24-hour strike on the morning of October 30th to protest layoffs.
    At the company's Miike Mine in Kyushu, a procession of forty bicycles and pedestrians also held a demonstration in Omuta City, the center of the coal mining industry.

    U.S. Assistant Secretary of State Jones Meets with Pakistani President
    Karachi Dispatch: According to newspaper reports on October 31st, U.S. Assistant Secretary of State Jones held a two-and-a-half-hour meeting with Pakistani President Ayub Khan in Rawalpindi on the 30th.
    According to newspaper reports, Jones discussed the issues of the so-called "increasing Russian influence in Afghanistan" and the "current Chinese threat to India and the Pakistan subcontinent" with Ayub Khan.
    Jones will be active in Pakistan for seven days. He will "visit" Lahore and Karachi. After his activities in India, he will also "visit" East Pakistan.

    Laotian Regent Savang Vatthana to Ascend Throne as King
    Hanoi Dispatch: According to a broadcast from Vientiane Radio on November 1st, Laotian Kingdom Regent Savang Vatthana will ascend the throne, replacing King Sisavang Vong, who passed away on October 29th, and will be styled as King Savang Vatthana.
    Savang Vatthana is the Laotian Crown Prince and assumed the regency in early September of this year.

### 国际简讯

1959-11-02
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    日本煤矿工人罢工
    东京讯　日本三井矿山公司二万九千名煤矿工人从10月30日早晨开始举行二十四小时罢工，反对解雇工人。
    在这个公司的九州的三池煤矿，四十辆自行车和徒步游行队伍，也在煤矿工业中心大牟田市市内举行了示威游行。
    美助理国务卿琼斯同巴总统会谈
    卡拉奇讯　据报纸10月31日报道，美国助理国务卿琼斯30日在拉瓦尔品第同巴基斯坦总统阿尤布汗举行了两小时半的会谈。
    据报纸报道，琼斯同阿尤布汗谈到所谓“俄国在阿富汗的影响日益增多”和“目前中国对印度和巴基斯坦次大陆的威胁”的问题。
    琼斯将在巴基斯坦活动七天。他将“访问”拉合尔和卡拉奇。他在去印度活动以后，还将“访问”东巴基斯坦。
    老挝摄政萨旺·瓦达纳将即位任国王
    河内讯　据万象电台1日广播，老挝王国摄政萨旺·瓦达纳将即位，代替10月29日逝世的西萨旺·冯国王，称号为萨旺·瓦达纳国王。
    萨旺·瓦达纳是老挝王储，今年9月上旬取得了摄政权力。
