Title: Korean Federation of Trade Unions Congress Continues; World Federation of Trade Unions and Soviet and Chinese Trade Union Delegations Deliver Congratulatory Messages
Author:
Date: 1959-11-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 3rd: The Third National Congress of the Korean Federation of Trade Unions continued today. The World Federation of Trade Unions and the Soviet and Chinese trade union delegations delivered congratulatory messages to the congress at the morning session today.
    The head of the World Federation of Trade Unions delegation, Su Jili, Secretary of the Secretariat of the World Federation of Trade Unions, first delivered warm, fraternal congratulations to the congress on behalf of the 95 million members of the World Federation of Trade Unions, amidst warm applause and cheers. He enthusiastically praised the decisive victories achieved by the Korean working people in the reconstruction of their country and the cause of socialist construction.
    The head of the Soviet trade union delegation, Musakhanov, member of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, extended warm congratulations to the congress on behalf of the Soviet trade union organizations and all Soviet workers. He warmly praised the brilliant achievements made by the Korean people in the cause of socialist construction.
    After his speech, Musakhanov presented a banner to the congress on behalf of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions. At this time, all the delegates stood up and applauded warmly, shouting "Long live Soviet-Korean friendship!"
    The head of the Chinese trade union delegation, Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting. He also presented a gift to the congress—a pair of carved lacquer vases made by Beijing handicraft workers. As Liu Changsheng spoke, all the delegates applauded enthusiastically, and finally, the delegates stood up and shouted "Long live Chinese-Korean friendship!"
    At the afternoon session yesterday and the morning session today, congress delegates Han Sang-doo, Minister of the Metal Industry, Kim Hoe-il, Minister of Transportation, and several team leaders of the "Chollima Work Team" spoke successively. The worker representatives unanimously supported Li Hyo-soon's summary report to the congress and introduced the experiences and achievements of Korean workers in various aspects such as carrying out the "Chollima Work Team" movement, technological innovation, and helping backward workers.

### 朝鲜职业总同盟代表大会继续举行  世界工联和苏中工会代表团致祝词

1959-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜职业总同盟代表大会继续举行
    世界工联和苏中工会代表团致祝词
    据新华社平壤3日电　朝鲜职业总同盟第三次全国代表大会今天继续举行。世界工会联合会和苏、中两国工会代表团在今天上午的会议上向大会致了祝词。
    世界工会联合会代表团团长、世界工会联合会书记处书记苏吉里首先在热烈的掌声和欢呼声中，以世界工会联合会九千五百万会员的名义，向大会致热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。他热情赞扬了朝鲜劳动人民在重建祖国和社会主义建设事业中取得的决定性胜利。
    苏联工会代表团团长、全苏工会中央理事会理事穆萨哈诺夫代表苏联的工会组织和全体苏联工人向大会表示热烈的祝贺。他热烈赞扬了朝鲜人民在社会主义建设事业中取得的辉煌成就。
    穆萨哈诺夫在致词后代表全苏工会中央理事会向大会献锦旗一面。这时，代表们全体起立，热烈鼓掌，并高呼“朝苏友谊万岁！”
    中国工会代表团团长、中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜在会上致了祝词。他并且向大会献了礼物——北京手工艺工人制作的雕漆花瓶一对。在刘长胜致词时，全体代表不断地热烈鼓掌，最后，代表们起立高呼“朝中友谊万岁！”
    在昨天下午和今天上午的会议上，大会代表、金属工业相韩相斗，交通相金会一以及“千里马作业班”的几位班长先后发了言。工人代表们一致支持李孝淳在大会上所作的总结报告，并且介绍了朝鲜工人在开展“千里马作业班”运动、革新技术、帮助落后工人等各方面取得的经验和成就。
