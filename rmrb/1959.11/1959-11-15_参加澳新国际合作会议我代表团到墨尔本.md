Title: Chinese Delegation Arrives in Melbourne to Attend Australia-New Zealand International Cooperation Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-11-15
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, November 14th: The three-member Chinese delegation to the Australia-New Zealand International Cooperation and Disarmament Conference arrived in Melbourne on the 12th.

### 参加澳新国际合作会议我代表团到墨尔本

1959-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    参加澳新国际合作会议我代表团到墨尔本
    新华社14日讯　悉尼消息：参加澳大利亚、新西兰国际合作和裁军会议的由三人组成的中国代表团，在12日到达墨尔本。
