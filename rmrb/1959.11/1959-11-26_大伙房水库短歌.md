Title: A Short Song of the Dahuofang Reservoir
Author: Li Zhezhe, Li Daisheng
Date: 1959-11-26
Page: 8

    A Short Song of the Dahuofang Reservoir
    Li Zhezhe, Li Daisheng
    Blue sky high, white clouds drift,
    Gentle breeze stirs the willow tips,
    Passenger ships run on the reservoir lake;
    Startling carp to spit white bubbles in their wake.

    A sheet of lake water ripples silver bright,
    Motorboats flit across the light,
    Horns sing songs anew,
    The reservoir connects to a road of happiness true.

    Gazing at the waves, thoughts rise and flow,
    Floods once swept, houses brought low,
    Fertile fields became sandy ground,
    Victims slept in the desolate surrounding.

    The reservoir is built to store pure water,
    Clear water irrigates fields, nourishing rice shoots younger,
    Engines stir the water, electricity is generated,
    Silver lamps light up the remote mountain hollows elated.

    Blue sky high, red flags wave,
    Green hills and clear waters all smile and rave,
    Sitting and watching, standing to gaze,
    Looking at the reservoir, we smile in a daze.

### 大伙房水库短歌
李振哲  李代生
1959-11-26
第8版()
专栏：

    大伙房水库短歌
    李振哲  李代生
    蓝天高，白云飘，
    微风吹动杨柳梢，
    水库湖面客轮跑；
    惊出鲤鱼吐白泡。
    一片湖水泛银波，
    艘艘汽艇上面飘，
    声声汽笛唱新歌，
    水库连成幸福道。
    望着浪花翻思潮，
    洪水卷过房屋倒，
    良田一片黄沙地，
    灾民露宿在荒郊。
    水库建成贮清水，
    清水灌田浇稻苗，
    机轮搅水发了电，
    银灯照亮荒山坳。
    蓝天高，红旗飘，
    青山绿水都含笑，
    坐着看，站着瞧，
    望着水库又笑了。
