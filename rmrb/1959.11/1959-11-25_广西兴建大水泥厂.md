Title: Large Cement Plant Under Construction in Guangxi
Author:
Date: 1959-11-25
Page: 2

    Guangxi is Building a Large Cement Plant
    The main building of the first large-scale modern cement plant in the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region recently began construction in Liuzhou. After the first phase of the plant is fully put into production, it will produce 600,000 tons of silicate cement and slag cement annually. The complete set of equipment for the Liuzhou Cement Plant is supplied by the Czechoslovak Republic.

### 广西兴建大水泥厂

1959-11-25
第2版()
专栏：

    广西兴建大水泥厂
    广西僮族自治区第一座大型现代化水泥厂的主厂房，最近在柳州动工兴建。这个厂的第一期工程全部投入生产后，每年将生产矽酸盐水泥和矿渣水泥六十万吨。柳州水泥厂的成套设备是由捷克斯洛伐克共和国供给的。
