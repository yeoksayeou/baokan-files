Title: Mongolian Education Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: A three-member Mongolian education workers delegation, led by Kherloo, First Deputy Minister of Education of the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing by train today for a study tour of China, as invited in accordance with the 1959 implementation plan of the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Lin Liru, Vice Minister of Education, and others were at the station to greet them.

### 蒙古教育工作者代表团到京

1959-11-17
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古教育工作者代表团到京
    新华社16日讯　根据中蒙文化合作协定1959年执行计划应邀来我国考察的蒙古教育工作者代表团一行三人，在蒙古人民共和国教育部第一副部长赫尔洛率领下，于今天乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站迎接的有教育部副部长林砺儒等。
