Title: Mongolian Handicraft Cooperatives Hold Representative Congress; Decide to Fulfill Three-Year Plan Ahead of Schedule; Current Handicraft Output Value Accounts for One-Fifth of Total Industrial Output Value
Author:
Date: 1959-11-21
Page: 5

Comprehensive report from our newspaper: The Fifth Representative Congress of the Mongolian Handicraft Cooperatives was held in Ulaanbaatar from the 16th to the 18th.

Demchig, Chairman of the Central Council of the Mongolian Handicraft Cooperatives, delivered a report at the congress. He pointed out that in 1958, the total output value of handicraft production in Mongolia increased by 91.4% compared to 1954. Currently, the output value of handicraft products nationwide accounts for more than 20% of Mongolia's total industrial output value.

In his report, Demchig pointed out that since the Fourth Representative Congress of Handicraft Cooperatives was held in 1954, handicraft cooperatives have fulfilled their total output value and commodity production plans with results of 103%-114% each year, with an average annual increase in production of 13.4%. During this period, the organization of handicraft cooperatives has also continued to expand.

In a congratulatory message to the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Council of Ministers, the congress stated that the handicraft cooperatives have decided to fulfill the production tasks of the Three-Year Plan (1958-1960) seventy days ahead of schedule, and to increase labor productivity by 17%, reduce production costs by 12.6%, and accumulate funds of more than 36 million Tugriks (Mongolian currency).

The congress also decided to continue to increase the production of daily necessities for the people and improve product quality. By 1965, product output will increase by 40%.

Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Mongolian Great People's Hural, spoke at the closing ceremony. He pointed out that the handicraft cooperatives, as an important economic sector of Mongolia, have made great contributions to Mongolia's socialist construction. He called on the workers to further unleash their enthusiasm for production and to successfully complete the tasks assigned by the Party.

At the closing meeting, the heads of the Central Council were elected. The Central Council elected Demchig as Chairman. (Attached picture)

### 蒙古手工业合作社举行代表大会　决定提前完成三年计划  目前手工业产值占工业总产值五分之一

1959-11-21
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古手工业合作社举行代表大会
  　决定提前完成三年计划
    目前手工业产值占工业总产值五分之一
    本报综合报道　蒙古手工业合作社第五次代表大会于16日至18日在乌兰巴托举行。
    蒙古手工业合作社中央理事会主席德木其格在代表大会上作了报告。他指出，1958年蒙古的手工业生产总值比1954年增加了91.4％，目前全国手工业产品的产值占蒙古工业总产值的20％以上。
    德木其格在报告中指出，自从在1954年召开手工业合作社第四次代表大会以来，手工业合作社每年以103％—114％的成绩完成总产值和商品生产计划，生产量每年平均增加13.4％。在这期间，手工业合作社的组织也不断扩大。
    大会在给蒙古人民革命党中央委员会和部长会议的致敬电中提出：手工业合作社决定提前七十天完成三年计划（1958—1960）的生产任务，并提高劳动生产率17％，降低生产成本12.6％，积累资金三千六百多万图格里克（蒙币）。
    大会还决定继续增加人民日用品的生产，提高产品质量，到1965年产品产量将增加40％。
    蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布在闭幕式上讲了话。他指出，作为蒙古重要经济部门之一的手工业合作社，对蒙古的社会主义建设作出了巨大贡献。他要求职工们进一步发挥生产积极性，很好的完成党交给的任务。
    在闭幕会上选出了中央理事会的负责人。中央理事会选举德木其格为主席。（附图片）
