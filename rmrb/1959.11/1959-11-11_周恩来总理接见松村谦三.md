Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Matsumura Kenzō
Author:
Date: 1959-11-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th — Premier Zhou Enlai received Mr. Matsumura Kenzō today and held a conversation. The conversation proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 周恩来总理接见松村谦三

1959-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见松村谦三
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理在今天接见了松村谦三先生，并进行了交谈。谈话在友好的气氛中进行。
