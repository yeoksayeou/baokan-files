Title: Greek Authorities' New Conspiracy of Persecution Fails; Acknowledge "Dawn Daily" and Glezos' Innocence
Author:
Date: 1959-11-11
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Athens, 10th -- A new conspiracy of persecution by the Greek authorities against "Dawn Daily," the organ of the Greek United Democratic Left, and its president, Glezos, was declared bankrupt yesterday. After several days of interrogation, the Athens Criminal Court had to declare Glezos innocent.

The new trial of "Dawn Daily" and Glezos began on November 3. The charge against "Dawn Daily" and Glezos this time was so-called "insulting the government," because "Dawn Daily" published articles on November 5, 1958, and May 20, 1959, exposing the Greek authorities' crimes of persecuting democrats.

Glezos solemnly pointed out in court on the 6th that "Dawn Daily" speaks on behalf of the Greek people. The Greek authorities' trial of this newspaper and Greek democrats because of "Dawn Daily's" firm political stance violates the Greek constitution.

Under Glezos' stern condemnation and the pressure of protests from Greek and world public opinion, the Athens Criminal Court had to admit that Glezos and "Dawn Daily" were innocent and stop the trial.

Glezos was already sentenced to a heavy sentence by the Athens Military Court in July this year on trumped-up charges.

### 希腊当局新的迫害阴谋失败  承认“黎明日报”和格列索斯无罪

1959-11-11
第6版()
专栏：

    希腊当局新的迫害阴谋失败
    承认“黎明日报”和格列索斯无罪
    新华社10日讯　雅典消息：希腊当局对希腊统一民主左翼党机关报“黎明日报”和它的社长格列索斯的一次新的迫害阴谋，在昨天宣告破产了。在经过几天的审讯后，雅典刑事法庭不得不宣布格列索斯无罪。
    对“黎明日报”和格列索斯的这次新的审讯，是在11月3日开始的。这次审讯“黎明日报”和格列索斯时提出的罪名是所谓“污辱政府”，因为“黎明日报”曾在1958年11月5日和1959年5月20日发表文章，揭露了希腊当局迫害民主人士的罪行。
    格列索斯6日在法庭上曾经严正指出，“黎明日报”是代表希腊人民说话的。希腊当局因为“黎明日报”坚定的政治主张而对这家报纸进行的审讯以及对希腊民主人士进行的审讯，是违反希腊宪法的。
    在格列索斯的严词谴责以及希腊和世界舆论抗议的压力下，雅典刑事法庭不得不承认格列索斯和“黎明日报”无罪，并且停止这次审讯。
    格列索斯在今年7月间已经被雅典军事法庭以莫须有的罪名判过重刑。
