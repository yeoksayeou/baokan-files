Title: Delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Returns from the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1959-11-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 28th – The delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, led by Deputy Head Liu Shuzhou, returned to Beijing by plane this morning from Moscow after attending the celebrations for the 42nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Union of Societies of Soviet Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association.
    Greeting them at the airport were Shao Lizi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General, and others. Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Razdukhov, Second Secretary, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 中苏友协代表团由苏联回国

1959-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中苏友协代表团由苏联回国
    据新华社28日讯　应苏联对外友好和文化联系协会联合会、苏中友好协会的邀请，去苏联参加伟大十月社会主义革命四十二周年庆祝活动的中苏友好协会代表团，在副团长刘述周率领下，于今天上午乘飞机由莫斯科回到北京。
    到机场迎接的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长邵力子、副秘书长戈宝权等人。苏联驻中国大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫和二等秘书拉兹杜霍夫，也到机场欢迎。
