Title: Cold Wave Alert
Author:
Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 7th – The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a cold wave alert at 7:00 PM on the 7th. The cold wave front had already moved to the Yellow River basin by 2:00 PM this afternoon and will continue to move south. It is expected that in the next 24 hours, Shandong, Jiangsu, Anhui, Henan, northern Sichuan, Zhejiang, northern Fujian, Jiangxi, and Hunan will experience northerly winds of force 5 to 7, with the Two Lakes Basin experiencing northerly winds of force 6 to 8. The Sichuan Basin and Guizhou will experience northerly winds of force 4 to 5, and the Yellow Sea, Shandong Peninsula, East China Sea, and the rivers and lakes of the Jianghuai region will experience northerly winds of force 7 to 9.

In the next 48 hours, Guangdong and Guangxi will experience northerly winds of force 5 to 6, the Taiwan Strait will experience northeasterly winds of force 7 to 9, and the northern part of the South China Sea will experience northeasterly winds of force 6 to 8.

In the above areas, the temperature will drop by 8 to 12 degrees Celsius within 24 hours after the cold wave front passes. On the morning of the 9th or 10th, there will be widespread frost in areas north of the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River, and on the morning of the 10th or 11th, there will be frost in most areas north of the Nanling Mountains.

### 寒潮警报

1959-11-08
第3版()
专栏：

    寒潮警报
    新华社7日讯　中央气象科学研究所7日下午七时发布寒潮警报。寒潮前锋今天下午二时已经移到黄河流域，未来将继续向南移动。预计未来二十四小时内，山东、江苏、安徽、河南、四川北部、浙江、福建北部、江西、湖南将有五至七级偏北风，其中两湖盆地有六至八级偏北风。四川盆地和贵州将有四至五级偏北风，黄海、山东半岛、东海、江淮流域的江面湖面上将有七至九级的偏北风。
    未来四十八小时内，广东、广西将有五至六级的偏北风，台湾海峡将有七至九级的东北风，南海北部将有六至八级东北风。
    以上地区在寒潮前锋过后二十四小时内，气温将下降八至十二度。9日或10日早晨长江中下游以北地区，将普遍有霜冻，10日或11日早晨，南岭山地以北的大部地区将有霜冻。
