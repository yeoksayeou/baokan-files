Title:  RCCK Central Committee Holds Meeting to Commemorate Sun Yat-sen's Birthday
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-12
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: The Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang (RCCK) held a meeting tonight to commemorate the 93rd anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.
    The meeting was presided over by Cai Tingkai, Vice Chairman of the RCCK. Vice Chairman Xiong Kewu spoke at the meeting. He said, "November 12th is the 93rd anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, the great democratic revolutionary. As we gather here to commemorate Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birthday, our country is in a new upsurge of the mass movement of opposing right-leaning tendencies, boosting morale, and vigorously increasing production and practicing economy. The people of the whole country actively respond to the great call of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and are working hard to realize the continued leap forward of the national economy this year. ... We must, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, continue to strengthen our fundamental self-transformation, and together with the people of the whole country, devote ourselves to the movement of opposing right-leaning tendencies, boosting morale, and vigorously increasing production and practicing economy; devote ourselves to the struggle to liberate Taiwan and realize the reunification of the motherland; and devote ourselves to the cause of safeguarding world peace and striving for human progress. This is the unshirkable political responsibility of every RCCK member, and it is also the best way to commemorate the great revolutionary Mr. Sun Yat-sen."
    Also present at tonight's memorial meeting were members of the Standing Committee of the RCCK Central Committee, including Lu Han, Lu Yuwen, Zhu Yunshan, Shao Lizi, Chen Cisheng, Chen Shaoxian, Weng Wenhao, Mei Gongbin, and more than 500 members of the RCCK Central Committee organs and the RCCK Beijing Municipal Committee.

### 民革中央集会纪念孙中山先生诞辰

1959-11-12
第2版()
专栏：

    民革中央集会纪念孙中山先生诞辰
    据新华社11日讯　中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会今晚举行大会，纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十三周年。
    大会由民革副主席蔡廷锴主持。熊克武副主席在会上讲了话。他说，“11月12日是中国伟大的民主主义革命家孙中山先生诞生九十三周年纪念日。当我们在这里集会纪念孙中山先生诞辰的时候，我们的国家正处在反右倾，鼓干劲，厉行增产节约的轰轰烈烈的群众运动的新高潮中。全国人民积极响应中国共产党八届八中全会的伟大号召，正在为实现今年国民经济的继续跃进而努力。……我们必须在中国共产党和毛主席的领导下，继续加强自我根本改造，和全国人民一道，投身于反右倾、鼓干劲、厉行增产节约的运动中去；投身于解放台湾、实现祖国统一的斗争中去；投身于维护世界和平和争取人类进步的事业中去。这是我们每个民革成员义不容辞的政治责任，也是对于伟大的革命者孙中山先生最好的纪念”。
    出席今晚纪念会的还有民革中央常务委员卢汉、卢郁文、朱蕴山、邵力子、陈此生、陈劭先、翁文灏、梅龚彬等以及民革中央机关和民革北京市委员会成员共五百多人。
