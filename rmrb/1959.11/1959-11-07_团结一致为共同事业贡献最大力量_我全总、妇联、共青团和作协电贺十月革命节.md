Title: Unite and Contribute the Greatest Strength to the Common Cause: All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Women's Federation, Communist Youth League, and Writers Association Send Cables to Congratulate the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1959-11-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 6th. Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, sent a telegram to Grishin, Chairman of the Central Council of All-Union Trade Unions, to congratulate the 42nd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

The telegram enthusiastically hailed the brilliant achievements of the Soviet people in all aspects this year, and wished them new and greater victories in the struggle to build communism and safeguard world peace.

The telegram stated that for ten years, the Chinese people have achieved great victories in socialist revolution and socialist construction along the path opened up by the October Revolution. This is inseparable from the selfless assistance of socialist camp countries, especially the comprehensive and enormous assistance given to our country by the Soviet Union. On behalf of all Chinese workers, I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet people for this profound proletarian internationalist friendship!

The telegram emphasized that the Chinese people cherish the great friendship and unity between the Chinese and Soviet people like the pupils of their eyes. All attempts by imperialist reactionaries to sow discord can only end in shameful failure.

Xinhua News Agency, November 6th. The All-China Women's Federation sent a telegram to the Soviet Women's Committee, warmly congratulating the 42nd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.

The congratulatory telegram said, "We heartily congratulate the Soviet people and women for their great contributions to the construction of communism; and congratulate you on the brilliant achievements you have made in conquering space. Your achievements have made outstanding contributions to the cause of communism and world peace."

The congratulatory telegram said that Chinese women have taken, are taking, and will take Soviet women as their glorious example. May the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people and women last forever.

Xinhua News Agency, November 6th. The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, congratulating the 42nd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

The telegram stated that the Chinese people and youth will always regard the Soviet Union as a great example and source of encouragement, and under the leadership of the great Chinese Communist Party, will unswervingly advance victoriously along the path of the October Socialist Revolution.

The telegram emphasized that the achievements of China's socialist construction are inseparable from the assistance of the Soviet Union. The Chinese youth express their heartfelt gratitude for this.

The telegram said that Chinese youth love the great Soviet Union. Chinese youth will always follow the teachings of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, be loyal to the principles of proletarian internationalism, and unswervingly strive to strengthen the great friendship and unity between the Chinese and Soviet people and youth, and to consolidate the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

This newspaper reports: Mao Dun, Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, sent a telegram to Fedin, Chairman of the Soviet Writers Association, on the 5th, warmly congratulating the 42nd anniversary of the October Revolution. The text of the telegram is as follows: Dear Comrade Fedin of the Soviet Writers Association:

On the occasion of the 42nd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, which is jointly celebrated by the working people of the world, please allow me, on behalf of all Chinese writers, to extend fraternal congratulations to you and all Soviet writers. Today, progressive writers all over the world are watching the development of Soviet literature with respectful eyes. Soviet literature for more than 40 years has created valuable wealth for progressive literature and accumulated rich experience in artistic creation. We express our heartfelt admiration and respect for your brilliant achievements and your creative efforts to build communism. We believe that Soviet writers will continue to make greater contributions to the cause of progress of the Soviet people and mankind all over the world. Let us always unite closely and contribute the greatest strength to our common cause.

\t\t\t\t\t\t\tChairman of the Chinese Writers Association, Mao Dun
\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t1959, 11, 5, Beijing

### 团结一致为共同事业贡献最大力量  我全总、妇联、共青团和作协电贺十月革命节

1959-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    团结一致为共同事业贡献最大力量
    我全总、妇联、共青团和作协电贺十月革命节
    新华社6日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一打电报给全苏工会中央理事会主席格里申，祝贺十月社会主义革命四十二周年。
    电报中热情欢呼苏联人民今年来在各方面所取得的辉煌成就，并祝他们在建设共产主义和维护世界和平的斗争中取得新的更巨大的胜利。
    电报中说，十年来，中国人民沿着十月革命所开辟的道路，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中取得了伟大的胜利，这是和社会主义阵营国家的无私援助，特别是苏联给予我国全面的、巨大的援助分不开的。我谨代表中国全体职工衷心感谢苏联人民这种无产阶级国际主义的深厚友情！
    电报中强调说，中国人民像爱护眼珠那样爱护中苏两国人民的伟大友谊和团结。帝国主义反动派的一切挑拨离间只能遭到可耻的失败。
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会打电报给苏联妇女委员会，热烈祝贺伟大的十月社会主义革命四十二周年。
    贺电说，我们衷心祝贺苏联人民和妇女对建设共产主义所作的巨大贡献；祝贺你们在征服宇宙空间方面所获得的辉煌成就。你们的成就对共产主义事业，对世界和平事业作出了卓越的贡献。
    贺电说，中国妇女过去、现在和将来都以苏联妇女作为自己的光辉榜样。祝中苏两国人民和妇女的友谊万古长青。
    新华社6日讯　中国共产主义青年团中央委员会打电报给苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会，祝贺十月社会主义革命四十二周年。
    电报中说，中国人民和青年永远把苏联看成为伟大的榜样和鼓舞的力量，在伟大的中国共产党的领导下，坚定不移地沿着十月社会主义革命的道路胜利前进。
    电报中强调指出，中国社会主义建设的成就是和苏联的援助分不开的。中国青年对此表示衷心的感谢。
    电报说，中国青年热爱伟大的苏联。中国青年永远遵循中国共产党和毛泽东主席的教导，忠实于无产阶级国际主义原则，坚定不移地为加强中苏两国人民和青年的伟大友谊和团结，为巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结而努力。
    本报讯　中国作家协会主席茅盾于5日打电报给苏联作家协会主席费定，热情祝贺十月革命四十二周年。电文如下：苏联作家协会亲爱的费定同志：
    值此全世界劳动人民共庆十月社会主义革命四十二周年之际，请允许我代表全体中国作家，向您及全体苏联作家致以兄弟般的祝贺。今天，全世界的进步作家都以尊敬的目光注视着苏联文学的发展。四十多年来的苏联文学已为进步文学创造出了珍贵的财富，积累了丰富的艺术创造经验。我们对于您们已经取得的辉煌成就，以及您们正在为建设共产主义而进行的创造性的努力，表示衷心的钦佩和敬意。我们相信苏联作家们将会继续为苏联人民和全世界人类的进步事业作出更伟大的贡献，让我们彼此永远紧密地团结在一起，为我们共同的事业贡献出最大的力量。
                                中国作家协会主席　茅盾
                                        1959，11，5，北京
