    International Briefs
    Sihanouk Visits India and Other Countries
    Phnom Penh dispatch: Prince Sihanouk, Prime Minister of Cambodia, left here on the 5th for official visits to India, the United Arab Republic, and Yugoslavia.
    Bourguiba Re-elected as President of Tunisia
    Tunis dispatch: It has been announced that the current President of Tunisia, Bourguiba, was elected as the next President of Tunisia in the election held on November 8th. Bourguiba was the only candidate participating in this presidential election. The Neo Destour party of Tunisia, led by Bourguiba, won all ninety seats in the National Assembly.

### 国际简讯

1959-11-10
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    西哈努克访问印度等国
    金边讯　柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王5日离开这里前往印度、阿联和南斯拉夫作正式访问。
    布尔吉巴连任突尼斯总统
    突尼斯讯　据宣布，突尼斯现任总统布尔吉巴，在11月8日举行的大选中，当选为突尼斯下届总统。布尔吉巴是参加这次总统竞选的唯一候选人。布尔吉巴所领导的突尼斯新宪政党获得了国民议会全部九十个议席。
