Title: National Winter Track and Field Games Open, Four National Records Broken on First Day
Author:
Date: 1959-11-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 15th—The 1959 National Winter Track and Field Games commenced today in Wuhan. In the first day of competition, athletes achieved outstanding results: four national records were broken in thirteen events, and one national record was tied. More than 25,000 spectators watched today's competition at the Wuhan Stadium.

This morning, in the men's 100-meter semi-final, Sichuan athlete Chen Jiaquan broke the national record of 10.6 seconds with a time of 10.5 seconds. The final results of this event were: Chen Jiaquan finished first with a time of 10.6 seconds (tying the national record), while Hubei's Li Bihua and Shanxi's Chen Lixiang finished second and third, respectively, both with times of 10.7 seconds.

In the men's 110-meter hurdles preliminary round, Hebei athlete Gao Jiqiao broke the national record of 14.6 seconds set by Zhou Lianli in 1958 with a time of 14.4 seconds, tying the record of the First National Games. In the final, Gao Jiqiao broke the record of 14.4 seconds set by Guangdong athlete Zhou Yuguang at the First National Games with a time of 14.3 seconds, tying the new results created by Zhou Lianli and Zhou Yuguang successively in August this year.

Inner Mongolia long-distance runner Zheng Zhaoxin and Henan long-distance runner Huo Tianshun competed fiercely in the men's 10,000-meter final. In the last 20 meters, Zheng Zhaoxin's courageous sprint brought him to the finish line first with a time of 31 minutes 16.6 seconds. Huo Tianshun finished second, 0.2 seconds behind. Both of them broke the national record of 31 minutes 22.4 seconds set by Su Wenren last year, but they did not exceed the record of 30 minutes 49.4 seconds set by Huang Zhiyong of the People's Liberation Army at the National Games. Liaoning's Guo Jinfeng finished third.

The rising Jiangsu female athlete Chong Xiuyun broke the national record of 13.62 meters set by Hebei athlete Xian Shaomei last year with a throw of 13.71 meters in the women's shot put final, but she did not exceed her own record of 14.39 meters created in August this year and her own record of 13.97 meters created at the First National Games.

### 全国冬季田径运动会揭幕  第一天打破四项全国纪录

1959-11-16
第5版()
专栏：

    全国冬季田径运动会揭幕
    第一天打破四项全国纪录
    据新华社武汉15日电　1959年全国冬季田径运动会今天开始在武汉举行。在第一天的比赛中，运动员创造了出色的成绩：十三个项目中有四个项目打破了全国纪录，一个项目平全国纪录。有二万五千多观众观看了今天在武汉体育场举行的比赛。
    今天上午，男子一百米复赛中，四川运动员陈家全以十秒五的成绩，第一个打破了十秒六的全国纪录，这个项目的决赛结果：陈家全以十秒六的成绩第一个跑完全程（平全国纪录），湖北选手李必华、山西选手陈立祥分别获得第二、第三名，成绩都是十秒七。
    在男子一百一十米高栏预赛中，河北选手高济桥以十四秒四的成绩打破1958年周连立创造的十四秒六的全国最高纪录，平第一届全国运动会的纪录。在决赛时，高济桥又以十四秒三的成绩打破了第一届全运会上广东选手周裕光创造的十四秒四的纪录，平周连立、周裕光今年8月先后创造的新成绩。
    内蒙古长跑健将郑昭信和河南长跑健将霍天顺，在男子一万米决赛中，相争甚为激烈，到最后二十米处，郑昭信勇猛的冲刺，以三十一分十六秒六的成绩第一个到达了终点。霍天顺以○·二秒之差获得第二，以上两人均打破去年苏文仁创造的三十一分二十二秒四的全国最高纪录，但没有能超过全运会解放军选手黄志勇创造的三十分四十九秒四的纪录。获得第三名的是辽宁选手郭金凤。
    新起的江苏女选手崇秀云，在女子铅球决赛中，以十三·七一米的成绩，打破了去年河北选手冼少梅创造的十三·六二米的全国纪录，但未超过她今年8月创造的十四米三九和第一届全运会上她自己创造的十三·九七米的成绩。
