Title: Indonesian Core Cabinet Insists on Twelve Nautical Mile Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1959-11-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 14th -- The Indonesian core cabinet meeting last night approved the bill on Indonesian territorial waters proposed by the Chief of Naval Staff. This bill insists on the decision that the scope of Indonesian territorial waters is twelve nautical miles.

The Indonesian government announced on December 13, 1957, that the scope of Indonesian territorial waters was expanded from the original three nautical miles to twelve nautical miles in order to deal with the activities of imperialists who were then aiding Indonesian rebel groups. At the international conference on territorial waters held in Geneva in February 1958, the Indonesian government representative also insisted on the Indonesian government's decision.

### 印度尼西亚核心内阁坚持领海为十二浬

1959-11-17
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚核心内阁坚持领海为十二浬
    新华社雅加达14日电　印度尼西亚核心内阁会议昨晚通过了海军参谋长提出的关于印度尼西亚领海的法案。这个法案坚持了印度尼西亚领海范围为十二海里的决定。
    印度尼西亚政府在1957年12月13日宣布印度尼西亚的领海范围从原来的三海里，扩大到十二海里，以对付帝国主义者当时援助印度尼西亚叛乱集团的活动。1958年2月在日内瓦举行讨论领海问题的国际会议上，印度尼西亚政府代表也坚持印度尼西亚政府的决定。
