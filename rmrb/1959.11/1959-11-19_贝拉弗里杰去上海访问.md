Title: Bella Frej goes to Shanghai for a visit
Author:
Date: 1959-11-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Former Moroccan Prime Minister Bella Frej and former Chief of the Prime Minister's Office, Rahalki, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou and other places. They will leave China after visiting these cities.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Hu Yuzhi, Vice President, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Mei Gongbin, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Gong Dafei, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 贝拉弗里杰去上海访问

1959-11-19
第5版()
专栏：

    贝拉弗里杰去上海访问
    新华社18日讯　摩洛哥前首相贝拉弗里杰和前首相办公室主任拉哈基，今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往上海、武汉、广州等地访问。他们将在访问这些城市后离开中国。
    到机场欢送的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长胡愈之，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、政协全国委员会副秘书长梅龚彬，外交部西亚非洲司副司长宫达非等。
