Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee and Council of Ministers Pass Resolution Reducing Prices of Forty-Two Items
Author:
Date: 1959-11-02
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 31st - According to reports in the newspapers here today, the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee and the Council of Ministers have passed a resolution on reducing the prices of some items.
    The resolution stated that, starting from November 1st this year, the retail prices of forty-two items, including high-grade wool fabrics, rayon fabrics, paints and coatings, and medicines, will be reduced by 5% to 60%.
    The resolution pointed out that the reason for reducing the prices of these items is that departments such as the textile and chemical industries have made great achievements in reducing product costs. The purpose of reducing prices is to further improve the material welfare of the people.

### 保共中央和部长会议通过决议  降低四十二种物品价格

1959-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    保共中央和部长会议通过决议
    降低四十二种物品价格
    新华社索非亚31日电　据此间报纸今天报道，保加利亚共产党中央委员会和部长会议通过了关于减低部分物品价格的决议。
    决议说，从今年11月1日起，高级毛织品、人造纤维织品、油漆涂料、药品等四十二种物品的零售价格将降低5％到60％。
    决议指出，减低这些物品的价格的原因在于，纺织与化学工业等部门在降低产品成本方面取得了很大成就。减低物价的目的是为了进一步增进人民的物质福利。
