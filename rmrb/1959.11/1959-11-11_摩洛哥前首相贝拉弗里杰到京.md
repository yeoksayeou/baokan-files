Title: Former Moroccan Prime Minister Bela Frej Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Former Moroccan Prime Minister Ahmed Balafrej, who was invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou this afternoon. Ahmed Raki, the former Director of the Office of the Moroccan Prime Minister, arrived on the same visit.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Mei Gongbin, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Deputy Secretary-General of CPPCC National Committee; Gong Dafei, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General; and Wen Shizhen, head of the Chinese Exhibition Group who went to Morocco to participate in the International Exposition.

The guests arrived in Guangzhou yesterday. Lin Qiangyun, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, hosted a casual banquet to welcome them.

### 摩洛哥前首相贝拉弗里杰到京

1959-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥前首相贝拉弗里杰到京
    新华社10日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的摩洛哥前首相艾哈迈德·贝拉弗里杰，今日下午由广州乘飞机到达北京。同来访问的有摩洛哥前首相办公室主任艾哈迈德·拉哈基。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、政协全国委员会副秘书长梅龚彬，外交部西亚非洲司副司长宫达非，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪，副秘书长吴晓达，前赴摩洛哥参加国际博览会的中国展览团团长文士祯等。
    客人们是在昨日抵达广州的。广东省副省长林锵云曾设便宴欢迎。
