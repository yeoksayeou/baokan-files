Title: US Company Allocates Funds to Support Indonesian Rebels
Author:
Date: 1959-11-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 29th: "Sunday News" published a report today indicating that the United States is providing financial assistance to the Indonesian rebel groups.

The newspaper stated that Dick International, a company headquartered in New York with branches in almost every country in the world, suggested in March of this year that the company provide a $10 million loan to the Indonesian Permesta group.

The newspaper said that, for this purpose, the company sent Dilake Brink, the manager of its Los Angeles branch, to Hong Kong, Singapore, and Indonesia.

According to the newspaper, the activities of this American company are being carried out under the direction of "important officials" in the United States.

### 美国一家公司拨款资助印度尼西亚叛匪

1959-11-30
第6版()
专栏：

    美国一家公司拨款资助印度尼西亚叛匪
    新华社雅加达29日电　“星期日新闻”今天刊载的一条消息表明，美国在经济上帮助印度尼西亚的叛乱集团。
    这家报纸说，总办事处设在纽约的几乎在全世界设有分公司的迪克国际公司今年3月建议由公司给予印度尼西亚全面斗争约章运动集团一千万美元的贷款。
    这家报纸说，为此，该公司派洛杉矶分公司经理迪腊克·布林克到香港、新加坡和印度尼西亚。
    据这家报纸指出，这个美国公司的活动是在美国“重要官员”指使下进行的。
