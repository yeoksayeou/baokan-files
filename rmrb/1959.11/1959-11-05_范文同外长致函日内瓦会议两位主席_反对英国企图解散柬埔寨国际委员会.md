Title: Phan Van Dong Sends Letter to Co-Chairmen of Geneva Conference Opposing British Attempt to Dissolve International Commission for Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1959-11-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 3rd—It was announced here today that Phan Van Dong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, wrote to Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko and British Foreign Secretary Lloyd, the two chairmen of the 1954 Geneva Conference on the problem of Indochina, on October 26, expressing opposition to the British government’s attempt to terminate the activities of the International Commission for Cambodia. Phan Van Dong expressed the hope that the two chairmen would take appropriate measures, for the sake of peace in Cambodia and Indochina, to allow the International Commission for Cambodia to continue to exist and carry out its normal activities, and at the same time take measures to restore the activities of the International Commission for Laos.

In his letter, Phan Van Dong pointed out: "The current situation in Indochina is such that, when the political problems of Laos and Vietnam have not yet been completely resolved, and the peace and neutrality of Cambodia are still threatened, terminating the activities of the International Supervisory and Control Commission for Cambodia will certainly endanger the respect for and thorough implementation of the Geneva Agreements on Cambodia, Vietnam, and Laos."

The letter said, "The attempt to dissolve the International Commission for Cambodia is clearly aimed at abolishing an international body designed to prevent foreign interference in the countries of Indochina. It is part of a plan to abolish the Geneva Agreements on the problem of Indochina, seriously endangering peace in the region."

### 范文同外长致函日内瓦会议两位主席  反对英国企图解散柬埔寨国际委员会

1959-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    范文同外长致函日内瓦会议两位主席
    反对英国企图解散柬埔寨国际委员会
    新华社河内3日电　此间今天公布：越南民主共和国外交部长范文同10月26日曾经写信给1954年关于印度支那问题日内瓦会议两位主席苏联外交部长葛罗米柯和英国外交大臣劳埃德，表示反对英国政府关于停止柬埔寨国际委员会的活动的企图。范文同表示希望两位主席为了柬埔寨和印度支那的和平，采取适当措施使柬埔寨国际委员会存在下去，并进行正常活动，同时采取措施使老挝国际委员会恢复活动。
    范文同在信中指出：“印度支那目前的情况是，当老挝和越南政治问题还未完全获得解决、柬埔寨的和平中立仍然受到威胁的时候，停止柬埔寨的国际监察和监督委员会的活动，一定将危害到对关于柬埔寨、越南和老挝的日内瓦协议的尊重和彻底履行。”
    信中说，“解散柬埔寨国际委员会的企图，显然是为了取消目的在于阻止外国对印度支那各国进行干涉的一个国际机构。它是取消关于印度支那问题的日内瓦协议的计划中的一部分，严重地危害着这一地区的和平。”
