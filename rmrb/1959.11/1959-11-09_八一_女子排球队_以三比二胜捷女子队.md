Title: “August 1st” Women's Volleyball Team Defeats Czech Women's Team Three to Two

Author:
Date: 1959-11-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The "August 1st" Women's Volleyball Team of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, after more than two hours of tenacious fighting tonight at the Beijing Gymnasium, defeated the Czechoslovakian National Women's Volleyball Team with a score of three to two.

### “八一”女子排球队  以三比二胜捷女子队

1959-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    “八一”女子排球队
    以三比二胜捷女子队
    据新华社8日讯　中国人民解放军“八一”女子排球队，今晚在北京体育馆比赛馆经过两个多小时的顽强奋战，以三比二战胜了捷克斯洛伐克国家女子排球队。
