Metallurgical Industry Design Capability

In old China, the design work of the metallurgical industry was a blank sheet of paper. Now, our country is capable of designing a joint iron and steel enterprise with an annual output of 1.5 million tons, a ferrous metal concentrator with an annual output of 7 million tons, a 1,150 mm primary rolling mill, an electrolytic copper plant with an annual output of 60,000 tons, and an electrolytic aluminum plant with an annual output of 120,000 tons.

### 冶金工业设计能力

1959-11-11
第3版()
专栏：数字与事实

    冶金工业设计能力
    旧中国，冶金工业的设计工作是一张白纸。现在，我国自己已经能够设计年产一百五十万吨的钢铁联合企业，年产七百万吨的黑色金属选矿厂，一千一百五十毫米的初轧车，年产六万吨的电解铜厂，年产十二万吨的电解铝厂。
