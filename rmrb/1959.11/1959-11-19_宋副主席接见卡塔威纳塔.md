Title: Vice Chairman Song Receives Kartawinata
Author:
Date: 1959-11-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 18th:  Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Song Qingling, met this afternoon in Shanghai with Arudji Kartawinta, Second Deputy Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament and Chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the Indonesian Muslim League Party, and his wife.

Present at the meeting were Shanghai Vice Mayor Niu Shucai, Secretary-General of the Municipal People's Committee Zhang Suping, Liang Yu-fan, Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Shen Cuizhen, Deputy Director of the Municipal Women's Federation.

Arudji Kartawinata and his wife left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 16th for Shanghai. He will visit Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou and other places before returning home.

### 宋副主席接见卡塔威纳塔

1959-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    宋副主席接见卡塔威纳塔
    新华社上海18日电　中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄今天下午在上海接见了印度尼西亚国会第二副议长、印度尼西亚伊斯兰联盟党中央执行委员会主席阿鲁季·卡塔威纳塔和他的夫人。
    接见时在座的有上海市副市长牛树才、市人民委员会秘书长张苏平，中国人民外交学会理事梁于藩、市妇联副主任沈粹缜。
    阿鲁季·卡塔威纳塔和他的夫人是16日上午乘飞机离开北京去上海的。他将在上海、武汉、广州等地参观以后回国。
