Title: Osvaldo Pugliese Orchestra's Last Performance in Beijing; Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with Orchestra Leaders; Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Holds Welcome Banquet
Author:
Date: 1959-11-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 24th. The Argentine Osvaldo Pugliese Orchestra held its last performance in Beijing tonight at the Tianqiao Theater. The orchestra is scheduled to leave Beijing on the morning of the 25th to visit and perform in Zhengzhou and other places.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended tonight's concert and met with the orchestra leaders Pugliese and Bonifadi during the intermission. Also attending the concert were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, other famous musicians from the capital, and foreign experts in Beijing.

In addition to performing Argentine tango music and folk songs, the orchestra actors sang the Chinese folk song "In That Faraway Place" tonight.

After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai and others went on stage to shake hands warmly with the musicians and congratulate them on their successful performance. Literary and art workers from the capital presented them with flowers.

This afternoon, the Chinese Musicians Association held a tea party to entertain the Argentine musicians and exchange experiences in music work with them. Orchestra leader Pugliese said at the tea party: "We came to China not only to play music, but also to bring friendship."

Xinhua News Agency, November 24th. The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries held a banquet at noon today to welcome the Argentine Osvaldo Pugliese Orchestra to visit and perform in China.

Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, delivered a congratulatory speech at the banquet, congratulating the orchestra on its successful performance in China. He also expressed his heartfelt gratitude on behalf of the Chinese cultural figures and artists who had visited Argentina, to the Argentine cultural figures, artists, and the Argentine people who had warmly received and helped the Chinese delegation.

Orchestra leaders Pugliese and Bonifadi also spoke at the banquet one after another. Pugliese said in his speech that they would convey the Chinese people's kind reception and friendship to the Argentine people. (Attached picture)

### 奥斯瓦尔多·普格利赛乐队在京末次演出  周总理接见乐队领导人  对外文化协会举行欢迎宴会

1959-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    奥斯瓦尔多·普格利赛乐队在京末次演出
    周总理接见乐队领导人
    对外文化协会举行欢迎宴会
    新华社24日讯　阿根廷奥斯瓦尔多·普格利赛乐队今晚在天桥剧场举行在北京的最后一场演出。乐队定于25日上午离开北京去郑州等地访问演出。
    周恩来总理出席了今晚的音乐会，并且在休息时间接见了乐队领导人普格利赛和波尼法迪。出席音乐会的，还有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，文化部副部长夏衍，中国音乐家协会主席吕骥，首都其他著名音乐家，以及在北京的外国专家。
    乐队演员今晚除演出了阿根廷探戈乐曲和民间歌曲以外，还演唱了中国民歌“在那遥远的地方”。
    演出结束后，周恩来总理等登上舞台同音乐家们热烈握手，祝贺他们演出成功。首都文艺工作者向他们献了鲜花。
    今天下午，中国音乐家协会举行茶会招待阿根廷音乐家，同他们交流音乐工作的经验。乐队领导人普格利赛在茶会上说：“我们到中国来不仅是演奏音乐，并且带来了友谊”。
    新华社24日讯　中国人民对外文化协会今天中午举行宴会，欢迎阿根廷奥斯瓦尔多·普格利赛乐队来中国访问演出。
    对外文化协会会长楚图南在宴会上致祝词，祝贺乐队在中国演出成功。他并且代表曾经访问过阿根廷的中国文化界人士和艺术家，对热情接待和帮助过中国代表团的阿根廷文化界人士、艺术家和阿根廷人民，表示衷心的感谢。
    乐队领导人普格利赛和波尼法迪也先后在宴会上讲话。普格利赛在讲话中表示，他们将把中国人民对他们的亲切接待和友谊，转达给阿根廷人民。（附图片）
