Title: Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: The Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation, consisting of ten members, led by the head of the delegation, Zsuffa Mihály, Deputy Minister of Metallurgy and Machine Industry of Hungary and Chairman of the Hungarian Group of the Committee, arrived in Beijing today by plane from Wuhan to attend the Fifth Session of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee.

### 匈科技合作代表团到京

1959-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    匈科技合作代表团到京
    据新华社4日讯　来我国参加中匈科学技术合作委员会第五届会议的匈牙利科学技术合作代表团一行十人，在团长、委员会匈牙利组主席、匈牙利冶金和机械工业部副部长索菲?茨·米哈伊率领下，今天从武汉乘飞机到达北京。
