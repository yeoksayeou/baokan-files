Title: Lin Biao, He Long, and Nie Rongzhen Receive Representatives of the Model Workers' Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-11-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th. Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council Marshal He Long, and Marshal Nie Rongzhen today received representatives of the People's Liberation Army troops who were invited to attend the National Model Workers' Conference.

　　Marshal He Long spoke during the reception. He encouraged the troop representatives attending the National Model Workers' Conference to achieve new and greater successes in the future socialist construction!

　　Also present at the reception were General Luo Ruiqing, General Tan Zheng, General Yang Chengwu, General Zhang Aiping, General Xiao Hua, General Gan Siqi, General Li Ju Kui, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Qiu Huizuo, Lieutenant General Li Shouxuan, and Lieutenant General Cui Tianmin.

### 林彪贺龙聂荣臻接见群英会的部队代表

1959-11-12
第2版()
专栏：

    林彪贺龙聂荣臻接见群英会的部队代表
    新华社11日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅和国务院副总理贺龙元帅、聂荣臻元帅今天接见了应邀出席全国群英会的人民解放军部队代表。
    贺龙元帅在接见时讲了话。他鼓励出席全国“群英会”的部队代表在今后社会主义建设事业中获得新的、更大的成就！
    接见时在座的还有罗瑞卿大将、谭政大将、杨成武上将、张爱萍上将、萧华上将、甘泗淇上将、李聚奎上将、萧向荣中将、丘会作中将、李寿轩中将、崔田民中将。
