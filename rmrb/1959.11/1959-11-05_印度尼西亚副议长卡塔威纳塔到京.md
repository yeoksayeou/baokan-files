Title: Indonesian Deputy Speaker Kartawinata Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: Mr. Arudji Kartawinata, Second Deputy Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament and Chairman of the Central Executive Committee for the Indonesian Islamic Union Party, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

Those who went to the airport to greet them included Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Bao Erhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association and Director of the China Islamic Association; Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Shen Zijiu, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation; and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 印度尼西亚副议长卡塔威纳塔到京

1959-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚副议长卡塔威纳塔到京
    新华社4日讯　印度尼西亚第二副议长、印度尼西亚伊斯兰联盟党中央执行委员会主席阿鲁季·卡塔威纳塔和他的夫人，应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长、中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪等。
