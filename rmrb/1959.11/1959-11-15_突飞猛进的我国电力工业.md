Title: The Rapidly Advancing Chinese Electric Power Industry
Author:
Date: 1959-11-15
Page: 4

    The Rapidly Advancing Chinese Electric Power Industry

    In the past ten years, especially since the Great Leap Forward, China's electric power industry has developed by leaps and bounds. Electricity generation has risen rapidly, the construction period of power stations has been repeatedly shortened, and engineering costs have continuously decreased. China's position in the world's electric power industry has also undergone significant changes.

### 突飞猛进的我国电力工业

1959-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    突飞猛进的我国电力工业
    十年来，特别是大跃进以来，我国电力工业以飞跃的速度向前发展。发电量迅速上升，电站建设工期一再缩短，工程造价不断降低。我国电力工业在世界上的地位也发生了重大的变化。
