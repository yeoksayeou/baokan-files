Date: 1959-11-07
Page: 12

Inside and Outside the National Conference of Heroes

    Representing nearly 300,000 advanced collectives and more than 3,000,000 advanced producers, over 6,000 delegates gathered in the Great Hall of the People to report on the achievements of the Great Leap Forward to the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, and to accept new combat tasks. China Youth Daily reporter, Shu Ye, photographed.

    Heroes from all walks of life enter the assembly hall. Xinhua News Agency reporter, Li Qing.

    Heroes of all ethnic groups gather to chat freely. Our reporter, Lü Xiangyou, photographed.

    Delegates attending the National Conference of Heroes discuss Comrade Li Fuchun's report in a group meeting. Hu Zhanfang (second from the right), a representative of the advanced collective Yang Lian Di Company of the Railway Corps, speaks. Xinhua News Agency reporter, Li Xuezeng, photographed.

    Party and state leaders such as Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Zhou Enlai, and Deng Xiaoping receive members of the presidium of the National Conference of Heroes. Comrade Liu Shaoqi is shaking hands with Meng Tai. The second person on the left is Li Shaokui. Our reporter, Lü Xiangyou, photographed.

    The workers of Anshan Iron and Steel's No. 16 Open Hearth Furnace are working hard, braving the high temperatures to hit the gas injection port, ensuring high-temperature smelting, and striving for fast steelmaking in every furnace. Xinhua News Agency reporter, Jia Chengbin, photographed.

    Steel heroes from "small-scale foreign" enterprises enthusiastically discuss the great achievements of large-scale steel production. From the left, the first person is Zhao Zhengquan, the smelting foreman of the Shaanxi Construction Machinery Factory; the second is Jie Xiangzhen, a representative of the Fujian Miaoqian Iron Factory; the third is Liu Zhendong, deputy director of the workshop of the Jiaonan Iron and Steel Plant in Jiaozuo City, Henan; and the last one is Song Jianming, an accountant at the Minggang Iron and Steel Plant in Xinyang, Henan. Xinhua News Agency reporter, Qi Guanshan, photographed.

    The 887 contracted crew group of the Zhakou Locomotive Depot of the Hangzhou Railway Bureau, active on the Shanghai, Jinhua, and Ningbo lines, sent its representative to attend the National Conference of Heroes. This group protects the locomotives as if they were their own eyes. Since New Year's Day 1950, the trains they have contracted have traveled 1,585,000 kilometers without any accidents, and have also saved fuel and oil worth more than 37,000 yuan. Zhou Kunquan photographed (Xinhua News Agency draft).

    Kailuan Coal Mine, a nationally renowned high-yield coal mine with large areas of high and abundant production, wins every battle. This is Yang Guichun (right), an advanced young coal miner, and others, from the Fourth Coal Mining Area of the Tangjiazhuang Mine of Kailuan Coal Mine, tunneling. Fang Yi photographed (Xinhua News Agency draft).

    The nationally famous "Tianqiao" Department Store is creating new achievements with renewed vigor. Our reporter, Lü Xiangyou, photographed.

    The Li Xueyi Concrete Team of Harbin City, which has maintained the red flag of high quality, high output, and safety for seven years, has set a record of pouring ten concrete beams in one day. They are pouring concrete beams for the expansion project of the Harbin Electric Machinery Factory. Xinhua News Agency reporter, Zong Baiyi, photographed.

    The Yan Chunhui group of Shanghai Jiangnan Shipyard assembles ocean-going vessels with a fighting spirit. Xinhua News Agency reporter, Zhao Cheng, photographed.

### 群英会内外（图片）

1959-11-07
第12版()
专栏：

    群英会内外
    代表着近三十万个先进集体和三百万多个先进生产者的六千多位代表，聚集在人民大会堂里，向党中央和毛主席汇报大跃进的成就，接受新的战斗任务。　中国青年报记者　舒野摄
    各路英雄走进大会场。新华社记者　李青
    各族英雄欢聚畅谈。　本报记者　吕相友摄
    出席全国群英大会的代表在小组会上讨论李富春同志的报告，铁道兵的先进集体杨连第连的代表胡占芳（右起第二人）在发言。
                          新华社记者　李学增摄
    刘少奇、朱德、周恩来、邓小平等党和国家领导人，接见全国群英会主席团成员。刘少奇同志正同孟泰握手。左第二人是李绍奎。
    本报记者  吕相友摄
    鞍钢十六号平炉的工人们干的真猛，冒着高温打煤气喷出口，确保高温熔炼，力争炉炉快速炼钢。　新华社记者　贾承滨摄
    来自“小洋群”企业的钢铁英雄们，热烈地谈论着大办钢铁的伟大成就。左起第一人赵正泉，是陕西建筑机械厂的冶炼工长；第二人揭象祯，福建庙前铁厂的代表；第三人是河南焦作市焦南钢铁厂车间副主任刘振东；最后边那位是宋鉴铭，河南信阳明港钢铁厂的核算员。
                                  新华社记者　齐观山摄
    活跃在上海、金华、宁波三线的杭州铁路局闸口机务段887包乘组，派出了自己的代表出席全国群英大会。这个小组像爱护自己的眼珠一样保护机车，自1950年元旦以来，包乘的火车行驶了一百五十八万五千公里没出任何事故，还节约了价值三万七千多元的燃料和油脂。
              周坤全摄（新华社稿）
    全国大面积高额丰产煤矿——开滦煤矿，连战皆捷。这是开滦煤矿唐家庄矿第四采煤区先进青年采煤工杨贵春（右）等在掘进。
        方艺摄（新华社稿）
