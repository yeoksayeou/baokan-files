International Briefs

    Pakistan to Build Jet Runway at Karachi Airport
    Karachi dispatch: Pakistani officials announced on the 19th that the Pakistani government has decided to build a new runway at Karachi Airport for jet aircraft to take off and land. It was announced that the 48 million rupee cost of this project, designed by American engineering experts, will be allocated by the United States Development Loan Fund.

    Moroccan National News Agency Begins Publishing
    Rabat dispatch: The Maghreb Arab News Agency began supplying Arabic news here on the 18th. This is Morocco's first national news agency. It is a private enterprise.

### 国际简讯

1959-11-26
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    巴基斯坦在卡拉奇机场
    建喷气式飞机跑道
    卡拉奇讯　巴基斯坦官方19日宣布，巴基斯坦政府已经决定在卡拉奇机场新建一条供喷气式飞机起落的跑道。据宣布，这项由美国工程专家设计的工程所需要的四千八百万卢比的费用将由美国开发贷款基金会拨给。
    摩洛哥本国通讯社开始发稿
    拉巴特讯　马格里布阿拉伯新闻社18日在这里开始供应阿拉伯文消息。这是摩洛哥的第一个本国通讯社。它是一个私人企业。
