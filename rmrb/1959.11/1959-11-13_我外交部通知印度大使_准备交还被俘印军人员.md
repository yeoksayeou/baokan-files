Title: Chinese Foreign Ministry Notifies Indian Ambassador of Readiness to Return Captured Indian Military Personnel
Author:
Date: 1959-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Chinese Foreign Ministry notified the Indian Ambassador to China, Mr. Parthasarathi, on the 12th that the Chinese border troops are prepared to hand over the ten Indian military personnel detained and captured in the border incident south of Kongka Pass on October 20th and 21st, as well as the nine Indian military corpses, to the Indian personnel sent to receive them during the period from the 13th to the 15th. The Indian military weapons, ammunition, etc., seized by the Chinese border troops will also be returned at the same time. The specific handover time and method will be negotiated on the spot by representatives of the Chinese border troops and representatives of the Indian side.

### 我外交部通知印度大使  准备交还被俘印军人员

1959-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我外交部通知印度大使
    准备交还被俘印军人员
    新华社12日讯　我国外交部于12日通知印度驻华大使帕塔萨拉蒂：我国边防部队准备于13日至15日期间将10月20日、21日在空喀山口以南边境事件中扣押的和俘获的十名印军人员以及九具印军尸体交给印度方面派来接收的人员领回。中国边防部队所缴获的印军武器、弹药等，也同时一并发还。关于具体交接的时间和办法，将由中国边防部队代表和印方代表就地商定。
