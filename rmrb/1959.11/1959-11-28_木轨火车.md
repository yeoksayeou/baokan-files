Title: Wooden Rail Train
Author: Chen Wenjian
Date: 1959-11-28
Page: 1

    Wooden rail trains bloom red,
    Wooden rail trains show wondrous ability,
    They carry all of the thousands of mountains and ridges,
    A crystal palace appears on the plain.
      (Yuzhong Work Area of the Water Diversion Project)

### 木轨火车

1959-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    木轨火车
    跃进花开朵朵红，
    木轨火车显奇能，
    千山万岭它装尽，
    平地出现水晶宫。
      （引洮工程榆中工区陈文俭）
