Title: Typhoon News
Author:
Date: 1959-11-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Central Meteorological Research Institute released a typhoon bulletin on the afternoon of the 10th, stating:

    The center of the typhoon over the Southwest Pacific Ocean was located approximately 900 kilometers east of the Philippines at 2:00 PM today, near 15.8 degrees North latitude and 130 degrees East longitude, with a maximum wind force of force 12 near the center. This typhoon will move westward at a speed of about 10 nautical miles per hour. In the next 72 hours, under the combined influence of the typhoon and cold air, the Taiwan Strait will experience northeasterly winds of force 7-9, and the northern part of the South China Sea, the Beibu Gulf, and the eastern and southern parts of Hainan Island will experience northeasterly winds of force 6-8.

### 台风消息

1959-11-11
第2版()
专栏：气象台

    台风消息
    新华社10日讯　中央气象科学研究所10日下午发布台风消息说：
    西南太平洋上的台风，今天下午二时中心位置在菲律宾以东九百公里左右的洋面上，就是北纬十五点八度、东经一百三十度附近，中心附近最大风力有十二级。未来这个台风将以每小时十海里左右的速度向偏西方向移动。未来七十二小时内，受台风和冷空气的共同影响，台湾海峡将有七级—九级东北风，南海北部、北部湾、海南岛东部和南部将有六级—八级东北风。
