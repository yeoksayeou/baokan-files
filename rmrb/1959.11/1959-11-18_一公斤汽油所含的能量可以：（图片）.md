Title: The energy contained in one kilogram of gasoline can: (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1959-11-18
Page: 3

    The energy contained in one kilogram of gasoline can: melt 30 kilograms of pig iron, roll 200 kilograms of steel, extract 140 kilograms of petroleum, harvest 15 mu of grain with a combine harvester, power a small car to travel more than 10 kilometers, shear the wool of 45 sheep, produce 20 meters of textiles, press 70 aluminum pressure cookers, or screen 12 full-length movies.
