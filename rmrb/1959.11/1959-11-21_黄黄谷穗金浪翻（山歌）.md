Title: Golden Waves of Yellow Millet Ears (Folk Song)
Author:
Date: 1959-11-21
Page: 3

    Autumn wind blows across the high-yield fields, millet stalks bowing their waists one by one;
    Not beaten down by wind and rain, but heavy millet ears creating golden waves.
      (Luowan Commune, Guiyang City)
    Mountain upon mountain, slope upon slope, this year the millet is bearing in clusters;
    The people's commune has great strength, granary upon granary, basket upon basket.
      (Muyang Commune, Suiyang County)

### 黄黄谷穗金浪翻（山歌）

1959-11-21
第3版()
专栏：

    黄黄谷穗金浪翻（山歌）
    秋风吹过丰产田，谷秆棵棵把腰弯；
    并非风雨把它打，沉沉谷穗金浪翻。
      （贵阳市洛湾公社）
    山重山来坡重坡，今年谷子结成坨；
    人民公社力量大，仓叠仓来箩叠箩。
      （绥阳县募阳公社）
