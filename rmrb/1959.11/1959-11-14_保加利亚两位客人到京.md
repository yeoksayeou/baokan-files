Title: Two Bulgarian Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 13th: According to the 1959 implementation plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Kirkin Ganchev Dirkinov, Director of the Marxism Teaching and Research Office of the Sofia Georgi Dimitrov Higher Institute of Agricultural Economics, and Gertruda Karlova Lukarova, a Bulgarian playwright, arrived in Beijing by plane on the morning of the 13th for a visit to China.

### 保加利亚两位客人到京

1959-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    保加利亚两位客人到京
    新华社13日讯　根据中保文化合作协定1959年执行计划，来中国访问的索非亚格奥尔基·季米特洛夫高等农业经济学院马列主义教研室主任基尔金·干切夫·季尔金诺夫和保加利亚戏剧家格尔特鲁达·卡尔洛娃·芦卡洛娃，于13日上午乘飞机到达北京。
