Title: Tirana Holds Opening Ceremony of Film Exhibition Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of the Motherland's Liberation
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-26
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, November 25th: Activities celebrating the 15th anniversary of Albania's liberation have begun in the Albanian capital, Tirana. On the evening of the 24th, the grand opening ceremony of the Albanian Film Exhibition was held here. The newly produced Albanian color films "Storm" and "Prosperous Albania" were screened for the first time.

    Albanian Labor Party and government leaders Hoxha, Shehu and others attended the opening ceremony.

    "Storm" is an artistic feature film made to commemorate the Albanian Liberation War, and it praises the guerrillas who fought heroically against the German and Italian fascists for the freedom and independence of their motherland. "Prosperous Albania" is a large-scale documentary that depicts Albania's beautiful rivers and mountains, the people's enthusiasm for building socialism, and their happy and prosperous lives.

    On the same afternoon, the Albanian Fine Arts Exhibition also grandly opened. The exhibition displays Albania's finest paintings, sculptures, and other works since liberation.

### 庆祝祖国解放十五周年  地拉那举行电影展览开幕式

1959-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝祖国解放十五周年
    地拉那举行电影展览开幕式
    新华社地拉那25日电阿尔巴尼亚首都地拉那庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年的活动已经开始。24日晚，这里隆重举行了阿尔巴尼亚电影展览开幕式，首次上映了阿尔巴尼亚新近摄制的五彩影片“风暴”和“繁荣的阿尔巴尼亚”。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和政府领导人霍查、谢胡等出席了开幕式。
      “风暴”是为纪念阿尔巴尼亚解放战争而拍摄的一部艺术故事片，它歌颂了为祖国的自由和独立而对德、意法西斯进行英勇斗争的游击队员们。“繁荣的阿尔巴尼亚”是一部大型的纪录片，它描绘了阿尔巴尼亚的锦绣河山、人民建设社会主义的热情和幸福美好的生活。
    同一天下午，阿尔巴尼亚造型艺术展览会也隆重揭幕。展览会上展出了解放以来阿尔巴尼亚最优秀的绘画和雕塑等作品。
