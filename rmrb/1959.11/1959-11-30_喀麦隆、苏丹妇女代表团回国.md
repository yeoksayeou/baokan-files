Title: Cameroon, Sudan Women's Delegations Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The Cameroon Women's Delegation, headed by Mrs. Carmen Monique, left Beijing by plane today to return home. Prior to this, they had visited Zhengzhou, Luoyang, Wuhan, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places, and also visited the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The Sudanese Women's Delegation, headed by Mrs. Salma Muhammad Suleiman, returned home on the 27th after visiting Zhengzhou, Luoyang, Wuhan, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places.

### 喀麦隆、苏丹妇女代表团回国

1959-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    喀麦隆、苏丹妇女代表团回国
    新华社29日讯　以卡门·莫尼克夫人为首的喀麦隆妇女代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。在此以前，她们曾到郑州、洛阳、武汉、上海、杭州等地参观，又曾去越南民主共和国访问。
    以萨勒玛·穆罕默德·苏莱曼夫人为首的苏丹妇女代表团，在参观了郑州、洛阳、武汉、上海、杭州等地以后，也已经在27日回国。
