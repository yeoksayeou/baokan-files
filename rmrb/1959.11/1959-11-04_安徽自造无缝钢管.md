Title: Anhui Produces Seamless Steel Pipes

Author: 
Date: 1959-11-04
Page: 3

    Anhui Produces Seamless Steel Pipes
    On the 1st, the seamless steel pipe workshop of the Hefei Iron and Steel Plant was officially put into production. This is the first workshop in Anhui Province to produce seamless steel pipes, and its planned capacity is 10,000 to 15,000 tons of various specifications of seamless steel pipes per year.

### 安徽自造无缝钢管

1959-11-04
第3版()
专栏：

    安徽自造无缝钢管
    1日，合肥钢铁厂无缝钢管车间正式投入生产。这是安徽省第一座生产无缝钢管的车间，它的计划能力为年产各种不同规格的无缝钢管一万到一万五千吨。
