Title: Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Holds Reception to Celebrate October Revolution Day, Congratulates Soviet People on Their Brilliant Achievements in Construction
Author:
Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 4th: Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, held a reception at the embassy this evening to celebrate the 42nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. Invited to attend the reception were Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Pushkin, as well as leading comrades from departments of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministry of Culture, Union of Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association.

In his speech at the reception, Ambassador Liu Xiao, on behalf of all the staff of the embassy, warmly congratulated the Soviet people on their great festival. He said that we are celebrating the 42nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution with immense joy. The brilliant achievements made by the Soviet people over the past 42 years are unprecedented, and we express our heartfelt joy at this. He pointed out that the first year of the Seven-Year Plan has already achieved great success, and the Chinese people are deeply convinced that the Soviet people, under the leadership of the Party, will surely be able to successfully complete the basic economic tasks of communist construction.

Deputy Minister Pushkin also spoke at the reception. He said that the friendly cooperation between the Soviet Union and China is becoming deeper and wider day by day, and this cooperation fully conforms to the interests of the two countries, the international communist movement, and world peace. The Soviet people have been, are now, and will always be the loyal friends of the Chinese people. He pointed out that this friendship and unity is an important factor in safeguarding world peace.

The reception proceeded in a very cordial and friendly atmosphere. The Chinese film "Lotus Lantern" was also screened at the reception, and a dance was held. This afternoon, Patchkalyov, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, was invited to give a report at our embassy in the Soviet Union on the 42nd anniversary of the October Revolution.

### 我驻苏大使举行晚会庆祝十月革命节  祝贺苏联人民建设的辉煌成就

1959-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻苏大使举行晚会庆祝十月革命节
    祝贺苏联人民建设的辉煌成就
    据新华社莫斯科4日电　中国驻苏联大使刘晓今晚在大使馆举行晚会，庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十二周年。应邀参加晚会的有苏联外交部副部长普希金以及苏联外交部、文化部、对外友好和文化协会联合会、苏中友协等部门负责同志。
    刘晓大使在晚会上讲话时代表使馆全体人员向苏联人民热烈祝贺伟大的节日。他说，我们怀着无比欢乐的心情庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十二周年。苏联人民四十二年来所取得的辉煌成就是史无前例的，我们对此表示衷心的高兴。他指出，七年计划的第一年已取得了巨大成绩，中国人民深信苏联人民在党的领导下一定能胜利完成共产主义建设的基本经济任务。
    普希金副部长也在晚会上讲了话。他说，苏中两国的友好合作正在一天比一天深刻和广泛，这种合作完全符合两国、国际共产主义运动以及世界和平的利益。苏联人民过去、现在和将来永远是中国人民的忠实朋友。他指出，这种友谊和团结是维护世界和平的重要因素。
    晚会在十分亲切友好的气氛中进行。会上还放映了中国电影“宝莲灯”，举行了舞会。今天下午，苏联共产党莫斯科市委书记帕奇卡廖夫应邀在我驻苏使馆作了关于十月革命四十二周年的报告。
