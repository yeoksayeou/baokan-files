Title: Brazilian President Criticizes U.S. Policy
Author:
Date: 1959-11-15
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, November 13th (TASS): According to today's newspapers, Brazilian President Kubitschek, in a speech at the Sao Paulo Chamber of Commerce, criticized the policies pursued by the United States towards Latin America.
    Kubitschek said that anti-American sentiment in Latin America has been growing in the past two years because the United States is unwilling to consider the legitimate demands of Latin American countries in developing their economies. Anti-American sentiment was particularly strong during U.S. Vice President Nixon's visit to these countries in 1958.

### 巴西总统批评美国政策

1959-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    巴西总统批评美国政策
    新华社14日讯　塔斯社纽约13日讯：据今天的报纸报道，巴西总统库比契克在美国圣保罗商会的演说中批评了美国对拉丁美洲所奉行的政策。
    库比契克说，由于美国不愿意考虑拉丁美洲国家在发展经济方面的正当要求，最近两年来拉丁美洲的反美情绪日见激昂。1958年，美国副总统尼克松访问这些国家期间，反美情绪表现得特别强烈。
