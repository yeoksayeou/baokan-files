Title: Ding Xilin Hosts Banquet to Welcome Indonesian Ministry of Information Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1959-11-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 25th - Ding Xilin, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the delegation from the Ministry of Information of the Republic of Indonesia. Suleiman, Counselor of the Indonesian Embassy in China, also attended the banquet.

### 丁西林设宴欢迎印度尼西亚情报部代表团

1959-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    丁西林设宴欢迎印度尼西亚情报部代表团
  新华社25日讯　对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林今天晚上设宴欢迎印度尼西亚共和国情报部代表团。印度尼西亚驻华大使馆参赞苏雷曼也出席了宴会。
