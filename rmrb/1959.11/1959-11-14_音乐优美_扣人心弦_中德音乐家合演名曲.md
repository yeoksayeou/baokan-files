Title: Beautiful Music, Riveting Performance: Chinese and German Musicians Perform Famous Compositions
Author:
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 13th – The performance of Beethoven's Ninth Symphony tonight by musicians from the German Democratic Republic and China deeply moved the hearts of over ten thousand listeners in the magnificent Great Hall of the People.

	This was a grand musical evening involving over 330 musicians and also the final performance of the Dresden Symphony Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic in China. German musicians had invited Chinese musicians to participate in every public performance in the capital. Tonight, together with the Central Philharmonic Orchestra, the Central Philharmonic Orchestra Chorus, the Central Broadcasting Orchestra Chorus, and the Central Conservatory of Music Student Choir, they left unforgettable memories for the audience in the capital with their exquisite playing skills and perfect cooperation.

	Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Bao Erhan, President of the Supreme People's Court Xie Juezai, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Xiruo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, President of the Sino-German Friendship Association Zhu Kezhen, and Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China Wang Daiel all attended and enjoyed the wonderful performance of the musicians from both countries. Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to China also attended the concert.

	Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, and others received Professor Borngetz, head of the Dresden Symphony Orchestra, before the performance.

	The Ninth Symphony is the culmination of Beethoven's entire creative life and the peak of European classical music. In tonight's performance, the first three movements of this great musical work were conducted by Professor Borngetz, winner of the German Democratic Republic National Prize and the Silver Fatherland Order, and the fourth movement was conducted by Yan Liangkun, conductor of the Central Philharmonic Orchestra. Under their direction, the musicians of the two countries profoundly embodied the creative theme of Beethoven's Ninth Symphony in their performance. When the joyous singing of "Millions of people are forever united as brothers" and the fast, passionate final movement resounded on the stage, the entire Great Hall was filled with an atmosphere of unparalleled jubilation. More than ten thousand listeners stood up from their seats and applauded warmly for a long time. Professor Borngetz and conductor Yan Liangkun shook hands and embraced warmly, repeatedly holding hands and bowing to the audience. The Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Ministry of Culture, the Sino-German Friendship Association, and the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic presented flower baskets to the musicians of both countries.

### 音乐优美　扣人心弦  中德音乐家合演名曲

1959-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    音乐优美　扣人心弦
    中德音乐家合演名曲
    新华社13日讯　德意志民主共和国和中国音乐家今晚合作演出的贝多芬第九交响乐，在宏伟的人民大会堂深深地打动了一万多首都听众的心弦。
    这是一次有三百三十多位音乐家参加的规模盛大的音乐晚会，也是德意志民主共和国德累斯顿交响乐团在中国的最后一场演出。德国音乐家在首都的每一场公演都曾邀请中国音乐家参加，今晚，他们又同中央乐团、中央乐团合唱队、中央广播乐团合唱队和中央音乐学院学生合唱队一起，以精湛的演奏技巧和完美的合作，给首都听众留下了难忘的记忆。
    国务院副总理聂荣臻，人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，文化部副部长夏衍，中德友好协会会长竺可桢和德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔，都出席欣尝了两国音乐家的精彩演奏。各国驻华使节和外交官员也出席了音乐会。
    聂荣臻副总理、郭沫若副委员长等在演出前接见了德累斯顿交响乐团团长波恩格茨教授。
    第九交响乐是贝多芬整个创作生活的总结和欧洲古典音乐的高峰。今晚演出中，这个伟大音乐作品的前三个乐章由德意志民主共和国国家奖金和银质祖国勋章获得者波恩格茨教授担任指挥，第四乐章的指挥由中央乐团指挥严良堃担任。在他们的指挥下，两国音乐家在演奏中深刻地体现了贝多芬第九交响乐的创作主题。当舞台上最后传出“亿万人民永远团结成兄弟”的欢乐歌声和快速热烈的终曲声时，整个大会堂洋溢着一片无比欢腾的气氛，一万多听众都从座位上站立起来，长时间地热烈鼓掌。波恩格茨教授和严良堃指挥热烈握手拥抱，两人一再地手拉着手向听众谢幕。对外文委、文化部、中德友好协会和德意志民主共和国大使馆，分别向两国音乐家献花篮。
