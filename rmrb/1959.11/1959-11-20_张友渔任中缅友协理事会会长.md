Title: Zhang Youyu Appointed President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association

Author:

Date: 1959-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th — The China-Burma Friendship Association held a council meeting today to re-elect the president, vice presidents, and council members of the council. The meeting elected Zhang Youyu as president, Jian Bozan and Zhao Puchu as vice presidents, and twenty new council members were elected.

### 张友渔任中缅友协理事会会长

1959-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    张友渔任中缅友协理事会会长
    新华社19日讯　中国缅甸友好协会今天召开理事会会议，改选理事会的会长、副会长和理事。会议选出张友渔任会长，翦伯赞、赵朴初任副会长，选出的新任理事有二十人。
