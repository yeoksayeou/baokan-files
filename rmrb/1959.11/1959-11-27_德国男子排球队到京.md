Title: German Men's Volleyball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 26th—A 16-member German Democratic Republic national men's volleyball team, led by Horst Hipp, Vice-Chairman of the German Democratic Republic Volleyball Association, arrived in Beijing this afternoon. The German Democratic Republic national men's volleyball team is visiting China at the invitation after visiting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. They will compete against the Beijing volleyball team and the Shanghai volleyball team in Beijing.

### 德国男子排球队到京

1959-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    德国男子排球队到京
    新华社26日讯　德意志民主共和国国家男子排球队一行十六人，在德意志民主共和国排球协会副主席霍斯特·希普率领下，今天下午到达北京。德意志民主共和国国家男子排球队是在访问朝鲜民主主义人民共和国之后，应邀到中国访问的。他们将在北京同北京排球队和上海排球队比赛。
