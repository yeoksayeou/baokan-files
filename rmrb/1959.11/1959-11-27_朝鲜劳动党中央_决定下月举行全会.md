Title: Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Decides to Hold Plenary Session Next Month
Author:
Date: 1959-11-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th: Pyongyang newspapers today published a communiqué from the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party announcing that the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party will hold a plenary session starting on December 1st.

According to the announcement, the following issues will be discussed at this meeting: 1. On improving and strengthening the work of local government organs; 2. On the 1960 national economic plan; 3. On carrying out a nationwide movement to plant economic forests.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央  决定下月举行全会

1959-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央
    决定下月举行全会
    新华社平壤26日电　平壤报纸今天刊载的朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的公报宣布：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会将从12月1日起举行全体会议。
    据宣布，在这次会议上将讨论下列问题：一、关于改善和加强地方政权机关的工作；二、关于1960年度的人民经济计划；三、关于开展全民植造经济林的运动。
