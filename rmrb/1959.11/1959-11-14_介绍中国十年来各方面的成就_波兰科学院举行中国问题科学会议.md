Title: Introducing China's Achievements in Various Fields Over the Past Decade; Polish Academy of Sciences Holds Scientific Conference on Chinese Issues
Author:
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 12th. A scientific conference celebrating the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China was held at the Polish Academy of Sciences this morning. The conference was organized by the Department of Social Sciences of the Polish Academy of Sciences.

Five reports on China were presented at the meeting: The Founding of the People's Republic of China; The Chinese Economy; New Policies in Chinese Higher Education; Chinese Literature; Sinology in Poland.

More than fifty people from Polish scientific circles and teachers and students of the Chinese Department of the University of Warsaw attended the meeting.

The cultural attache of the Chinese Embassy in Poland also attended the meeting.

### 介绍中国十年来各方面的成就  波兰科学院举行中国问题科学会议

1959-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    介绍中国十年来各方面的成就
    波兰科学院举行中国问题科学会议
    新华社华沙12日电　今天上午在波兰科学院举行了庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年的科学会议。会议是由波兰科学院社会科学部组织的。
    会上作了五个有关中国的报告：中华人民共和国的成立；中国经济；中国高等学校工作的新方针；中国文学；汉学在波兰。
    参加会议的有波兰科学界人士和华沙大学汉语系师生等五十多人。
    中国驻波兰大使馆文化专员也出席了会议。
