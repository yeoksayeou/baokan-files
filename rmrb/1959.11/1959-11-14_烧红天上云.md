Title: Burning Red Clouds in the Sky
Author: Jin Ge
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 3

    Look how fast the raging wind is! But the miners are even faster than the wind! Listen to how loud the whistle sounds, but the miners' voices scare the whistle. What is burning red the sunset glow? The coal mined by the miners, burning red all the clouds in the sky.
              Jin Ge

### 烧红天上云
金戈
1959-11-14
第3版()
专栏：

    烧红天上云你看狂风的速度够多快！可矿工们速度比风快！你听汽笛的声音够多高，可矿工们的声音把汽笛吓坏。是什么烧红了天边的彩霞？矿工们采出的煤呀，烧红了天上所有的云彩。
              金戈
