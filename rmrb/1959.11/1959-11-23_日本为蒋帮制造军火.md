Title: Japan Manufactures Arms for the Chiang Gang
Author:
Date: 1959-11-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 21st: The "Akahata" revealed today that the Yokohama Toyo Chemical Company's munitions factory, where an explosion occurred on the 20th, is manufacturing munitions for the Chiang Kai-shek clique in Taiwan.

The markings on the wooden box fragments found at the scene of the accident indicate that the consignee of this batch of munitions was the Navy of the Chiang Kai-shek clique.

The "Akahata" also pointed out that this munitions factory specializes in remanufacturing munitions using various old ammunition sold by the U.S. military.

According to a report in today's "Asahi Shimbun," residents of the Kanazawa district of Yokohama City, who suffered tragic losses due to the major explosion at this munitions factory, have united to demand compensation from Toyo Chemical Company.

It is reported that residents in the area are also brewing a movement to oppose the factory resuming production. However, the chairman of Toyo Chemical Company stated that despite the explosion, they will continue to manufacture munitions there.

### 日本为蒋帮制造军火

1959-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    日本为蒋帮制造军火
    新华社21日讯　东京消息：据“赤旗报”今天揭露，20日发生爆炸的横滨东洋化工公司军火工厂正在为台湾蒋介石集团制造军火。
    在肇事地点发现的木箱碎片上的标志说明，这批军火的收货人是蒋介石集团的海军。
    “赤旗报”还指出，这家军火工厂专门利用美军出售的各种旧弹药重制军火。
    据“朝日新闻”今天报道，因为这家军火工厂发生大爆炸而受到悲惨损失的横滨市金泽地区居民，已经团结起来要求东洋化工公司给予赔偿。
    据报道，这一带的居民还酝酿着展开反对这家工厂恢复生产的运动。但是东洋化工公司董事长表明，虽然发生了这次爆炸事件，还是要在那里继续制造军火。
