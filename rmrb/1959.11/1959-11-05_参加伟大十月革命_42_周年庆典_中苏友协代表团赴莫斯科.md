Title: Chinese-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation Visits Moscow to Participate in the 42nd Anniversary Celebration of the Great October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1959-11-05
Page: 5

    To Participate in the 42nd Anniversary Celebration of the Great October Revolution
    Chinese-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation Visits Moscow
    Xinhua News Agency, November 4th. At the invitation of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, the Chinese-Soviet Friendship Association delegation of fourteen people, led by Qian Junrui, head of the delegation, and Liu Shuzhou, deputy head of the delegation, left Beijing by plane this morning for Moscow to participate in the 42nd anniversary celebration of the Great October Socialist Revolution.
    Among those who saw them off at the airport were Shao Lizi, Vice President of the China-Soviet Friendship Association, directors Shi Liang and Ding Xilin, Deputy Secretary-General Ge Baoquan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Cao Jinghua, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Department of the Ministry of Culture Zhu Ming, and Deputy Director of the Soviet Union Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Junqing, and others.
    Counsellor Sudarikov of the Soviet Embassy in China and others also saw them off at the airport.

### 参加伟大十月革命42周年庆典  中苏友协代表团赴莫斯科

1959-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    参加伟大十月革命42周年庆典
    中苏友协代表团赴莫斯科
    新华社4日讯　应苏联对外友好和文化联系协会联合会与苏中友好协会的邀请，到苏联参加伟大十月社会主义革命四十二周年庆祝典礼的中苏友好协会代表团一行十四人，在团长钱俊瑞和副团长刘述周率领下，于今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往莫斯科。
    到机场欢送的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长邵力子，理事史良、丁西林，副秘书长戈宝权，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，北京市中苏友好协会副会长曹靖华，文化部对外文化联络司副司长朱明，外交部苏联司副司长陈俊清等。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫等也到机场欢送。
