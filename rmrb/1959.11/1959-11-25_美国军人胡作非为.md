Title: American Soldiers' Misdeeds

Author: 
Date: 1959-11-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd dispatch from Moscow: "Soviet Navy News" quoted an official material saying that in four years, American soldiers in Turkey committed nearly three hundred major criminal cases. In these years, they beat to death twenty-three Turks, raped three hundred and twenty-eight women, robbed one hundred and sixty-four shop owners and car drivers, committed one thousand two hundred and twelve cases of assault and hooliganism in public places, and arbitrarily insulted the Turkish flag eighteen times.

### 美国军人胡作非为

1959-11-25
第5版()
专栏：

    美国军人胡作非为
    据新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：“苏联海军报”引用一项官方材料说，在四年中美国军人在土耳其发生的重大刑事犯罪案件将近三百起。这几年中，他们打死土耳其人二十三人、强奸妇女三百二十八人，劫掠商店主和汽车司机一百六十四人，在公共场所发生打人和流氓行为一千二百一十二起，任意侮辱土耳其国旗的行为十八起。
