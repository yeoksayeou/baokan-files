Title: Hangzhou Veterinary Drug Factory
Author:
Date: 1959-11-20
Page: 2

Column: New Force in Chemical Industry

    Hangzhou Veterinary Drug Factory
    Zhejiang's first veterinary drug factory, the Hangzhou Veterinary Drug Factory, has been basically completed and put into production. Currently, the factory's main products include six varieties such as swine erysipelas vaccine, swine fever vaccine, fowl cholera vaccine, and piglet paratyphoid vaccine. Recently, they will also trial-produce fowlpox, chicken bacillary white diarrhea, and bovine hemorrhagic septicemia vaccines, and are preparing to put them into mass production in the first quarter of next year.

### 杭州兽用药厂

1959-11-20
第2版()
专栏：化工新军

    杭州兽用药厂
    浙江第一座兽药厂——杭州兽用药厂已经基本建成并投入生产。目前，这个厂的主要产品有猪丹毒疫苗、猪肺疫疫苗、家禽出血性败血病疫苗、小猪副伤寒疫苗等六个品种。最近，他们还要试制鸡痘鸡白痢、牛出血性败血病疫苗等，准备明年第一季度大量投入生产。
