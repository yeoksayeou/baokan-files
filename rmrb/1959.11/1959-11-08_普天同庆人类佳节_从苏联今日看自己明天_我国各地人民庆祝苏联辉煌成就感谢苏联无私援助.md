Title: Celebrating the Great Holiday of Humanity; Learning from the Soviet Union Today to See Our Own Tomorrow; People Across Our Country Celebrate the Glorious Achievements of the Soviet Union and Thank the Soviet Union for Its Selfless Assistance
Author:
Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: People across our country are warmly celebrating the 42nd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

As of the 7th, messages have been received from eighteen provinces and cities, including Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Xi'an, Chongqing, Harbin, Anshan, Chengdu, Zhengzhou, Hohhot, Lhasa, Urumqi, Guiyang, Lanzhou, Yinchuan, and Baotou. These provinces and cities held celebration meetings on the 6th and 7th, and held Soviet construction achievement report meetings, photo exhibitions, music appreciation meetings, and festive evenings before and after the holiday to celebrate this great holiday of mankind.

Various branches of the People's Liberation Army also held celebrations.

Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, who is currently in Chengdu, and Minister of Public Health and Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Li Dequan, who is currently in Henan, also attended local celebrations.

At the celebration meetings, the heads of local Party and government organizations, representatives from all walks of life, and representatives of Soviet experts all spoke. Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC and Vice President of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Wang Kangzhi, Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the CPC; Wang Shucheng, Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the CPC; Chen Yu, member of the Central Committee of the CPC, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC, and President of the Guangdong Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Zhang Ce, Secretary of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the CPC and First Secretary of the Xi'an Municipal Committee; Du Xinyuan, Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the CPC and President of the Sichuan Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Li Tangbin, Secretary of the Chongqing Municipal Committee of the CPC and President of the Chongqing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Yang Shijie, First Secretary of the Anshan Municipal Committee of the CPC and President of the Anshan Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Ren Zhongyi, Standing Committee Member of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the CPC and First Secretary of the Harbin Municipal Committee; Jiao Ruoyu, First Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the CPC and President of the Shenyang Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Hui Yuyu, Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the CPC and Governor of Jiangsu Province; Zeng Di, Secretary of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Committee of the CPC; Zhang Guohua, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Work Committee of the CPC and Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region; Apei Awang Jinmei, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region; Zhandong Jigme, Standing Committee Member of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region; and Hafuna, President of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, all spoke at local celebrations. They all elaborated in detail on the world significance of the October Socialist Revolution and the great achievements made by the Soviet Union in the past forty-two years, as well as the great contributions made by the Soviet Union to striving for world peace and easing international tensions. They all hailed the fraternal unity between the Chinese and Soviet peoples as solid as a rock and thanked the Soviet people and government for their tremendous help in China's socialist construction.

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Progressive people around the world are jubilantly celebrating the 42nd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.

Socialist countries are filled with a joyful festive atmosphere. The Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Mongolia, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, and Albania all held grand celebration meetings on the 6th. Party and government leaders of various countries attended the meetings and spoke. They congratulated the Soviet people on the great achievements they have made in building communism, especially the epoch-making victory recently in conquering outer space. In their speeches, they congratulated the unbreakable unity of the socialist camp and unanimously supported the Soviet government's proposal for complete and thorough disarmament, believing that this proposal is a reliable guarantee for eliminating the "Cold War" and easing international tensions.

Progressive people in other countries have also held gatherings to celebrate the 42nd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. Residents of Baghdad held a gathering; in Paris, the French democratic forces held a grand gathering at the initiative of the French Communist Party; in Colombo, the Ceylon-Soviet Friendship Association held a report meeting; the Belgian Communist Party and the Swedish Communist Party also held celebrations.

### 普天同庆人类佳节  从苏联今日看自己明天  我国各地人民庆祝苏联辉煌成就感谢苏联无私援助

1959-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    普天同庆人类佳节
    从苏联今日看自己明天
    我国各地人民庆祝苏联辉煌成就感谢苏联无私援助
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：我国各地人民热烈庆祝十月社会主义革命四十二周年。
    截至7日止，已有上海、天津、沈阳、武汉、广州、西安、重庆、哈尔滨、鞍山、成都、郑州、呼和浩特、拉萨、乌鲁木齐、贵阳、兰州、银川、包头等十八个省、市发来了消息。这些省市，都在6日和7日举行了庆祝大会，并在节日前后举行了苏联建设成就报告会、图片展览、音乐欣赏会和联欢晚会等活动，庆祝这个人类伟大的节日。
    人民解放军的各兵种部队，也都举行了庆祝活动。
    正在成都的国务院副总理陈毅和正在河南的卫生部部长、中苏友协总会副会长李德全，也都出席了当地的庆祝会。
    在庆祝大会上，各地的党和政府的负责人、各界代表和苏联专家的代表，都讲了话。中共上海市委书记、上海市中苏友协副会长陈丕显，中共天津市委书记王亢之，中共湖北省委书记王树成，中共中央委员、中共广东省委书记、中苏友协广东分会会长陈郁，中共陕西省委书记兼西安市委第一书记张策，中共四川省委书记、中苏友协四川分会会长杜心源，中共重庆市委书记、中苏友协重庆分会会长李唐彬，中共鞍山市委第一书记、中苏友协鞍山分会会长杨士杰，中共黑龙江省委常务委员、中共哈尔滨市委第一书记任仲夷，中共沈阳市委第一书记、中苏友协沈阳分会会长焦若愚，中共江苏省委书记、江苏省省长惠浴宇，中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会书记曾涤，中共西藏工委副书记、西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员张国华，西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员兼秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美，西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员詹东·计晋美，内蒙古自治区中苏友好协会会长哈丰阿等都分别在当地的庆祝会上讲了话。他们都详细地阐述了十月社会主义革命的世界意义和苏联四十二年来取得的伟大成就，以及苏联为争取世界和平和缓和国际紧张局势所作的巨大贡献。他们都欢呼中苏两国人民兄弟般的团结坚如磐石，并感谢苏联人民和政府对我国社会主义建设的巨大帮助。
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：全世界进步人类欢欣鼓舞庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十二周年。
    社会主义各国充满了欢乐的节日气氛。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南民主共和国、蒙古、德意志民主共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚和阿尔巴尼亚等国在6日都举行了盛大的庆祝大会。各国党和政府领导人出席大会并讲了话。他们祝贺苏联人民在建设共产主义事业中取得的巨大成就，特别是最近在征服宇宙空间方面的划时代的胜利。他们在发言中祝贺社会主义阵营各国牢不可破的团结一致，并一致支持苏联政府关于全面彻底裁军的建议，认为这一建议是消除“冷战”和缓和国际紧张局势的可靠的保证。
    其他国家的进步人士也纷纷举行集会庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十二周年。巴格达市民举行了集会；在巴黎，由法共倡议，法国民主力量举行了盛大集会；在科伦坡，锡苏友协举行报告大会；比利时共产党和瑞典共产党也分别举行了庆祝会。
