Title: Czechoslovakian National Women's Volleyball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd - The Czechoslovakian National Women's Volleyball Team, a group of seventeen people led by Josef Abraham, Vice Chairman of the Czechoslovakian Sports Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane today at our invitation for a visit and matches in our country. They will play against the Chinese women's team on the 5th, the Beijing women's team on the 7th, and the "August 1st" women's team on the 8th.

### 捷国家女子排球队到京

1959-11-03
第6版()
专栏：

    捷国家女子排球队到京
    据新华社2日讯　应邀来我国访问比赛的捷克斯洛伐克国家女子排球队一行十七人，今天由捷克斯洛伐克体育联合会副主席约瑟夫·亚伯拉罕率领乘飞机到达北京。她们将在5日对中国女子队，7日对北京女子队，8日对“八一”女子队。
