Title: Nalivkin Leads Soviet Delegation to Beijing to Attend China Stratigraphy Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-11-15
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, 14th. At the invitation of the Chinese National Stratigraphy Conference, the Soviet Academy of Sciences, the Soviet Ministry of Geology and Mineral Resources Protection delegation of six people, has arrived in Beijing. The head of the delegation, Academician Nalivkin, Chairman of the Soviet Stratigraphic Committee, and one member of the delegation arrived by plane today. The remaining four members of the delegation arrived in Beijing yesterday.
　　Li Siguang, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Minister of Geology, and many Chinese stratigraphy and paleontology experts, were at the airport to greet them.

### 应邀参加我国地层会议  纳利夫金率苏联代表团抵京

1959-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀参加我国地层会议
    纳利夫金率苏联代表团抵京
    新华社14日讯　应邀来参加中国全国地层会议的苏联科学院、苏联地质和矿藏保护部代表团一行六人，已经到达北京。团长、苏联地层委员会主席纳利夫金院士和一名团员是今天乘飞机到达的，其余四名团员已于昨天抵北京。
    到机场迎接的，有中国科学院副院长、地质部部长李四光和我国地层古生物学家多人。
