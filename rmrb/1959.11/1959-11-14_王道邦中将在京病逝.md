Title: Lieutenant General Wang Daobang Passes Away in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Lieutenant General Wang Daobang, Commander of the Hebei Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, passed away in Beijing on the 12th at the age of forty-eight.

Lieutenant General Wang Daobang was from Yongxin County, Jiangxi Province. He joined the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in 1930 and the Communist Party of China in the same year. He successively served as squad leader, platoon leader, instructor, political commissar of the regiment, brigade commander and political commissar, army commander and political commissar, etc. In the long-term revolutionary struggle, he was consistently loyal, worked hard, and fought heroically for the liberation of the Chinese people throughout his life. He was an excellent cadre of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Before Lieutenant General Wang Daobang passed away, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, General Yang Chengwu, and Lin Tie, First Secretary of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, visited him. After his death, Nie Rongzhen, Xu Xiangqian, Tan Zheng, and seventy-one other comrades formed a funeral committee.

### 王道邦中将在京病逝

1959-11-14
第2版()
专栏：

    王道邦中将在京病逝
    新华社13日讯　中国人民解放军河北军区司令员王道邦中将，12日在北京病逝，今年四十八岁。
    王道邦中将江西永新县人，1930年参加中国工农红军，同年参加中国共产党，历任班长、排长、指导员、团政委、旅长兼政委、军长兼政委等职。他在长期革命斗争中，一贯忠心耿耿，艰苦奋斗，为中国人民解放事业英勇地战斗了一生，是中国共产党和中国人民解放军的优秀干部。
    王道邦中将临终前，聂荣臻元帅、杨成武上将和中共河北省委第一书记林铁等曾亲往探望。病逝后，聂荣臻、徐向前、谭政等七十四位同志组成治丧委员会。
