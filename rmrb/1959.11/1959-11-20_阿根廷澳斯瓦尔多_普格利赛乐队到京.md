Title: Argentine Osvaldo Pugliese Orchestra Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-20
Page: 4

    Argentine Osvaldo Pugliese Orchestra Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, November 19th—The Argentine Osvaldo Pugliese Orchestra, a group of twenty people led by Osvaldo Pugliese, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. The orchestra is visiting China for performances at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
    Greeting them at the airport were Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General, and others.

### 阿根廷澳斯瓦尔多·普格利赛乐队到京

1959-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷澳斯瓦尔多·普格利赛乐队到京
    新华社19日讯　阿根廷澳斯瓦尔多·普格利赛乐队一行二十人，在澳斯瓦尔多·普格利赛率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。这个乐队是应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问演出的。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会秘书长周而复、副秘书长朱伯深等。
