Title: Four Friendship Associations Jointly Hold Council Meeting; Listen to Reports on Visits to Vietnam, North Korea, Mongolia, and East Germany; Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and Other Units Host Ambassadors from Vietnam, North Korea, Mongolia, East Germany, and Poland

Author:
Date: 1959-11-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 21st - The delegations from the China-Vietnam, China-North Korea, China-Mongolia, and China-East Germany Friendship Associations, which recently returned from visits to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Mongolian People's Republic, and the German Democratic Republic, respectively, reported on their visits to the aforementioned fraternal countries at a joint council meeting held by the four friendship associations this afternoon.

　　These four delegations were all invited to the above four fraternal countries to participate in their celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The China-East Germany Friendship Association delegation also participated in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic. Today, Yang Xiufeng, head of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association delegation, Mao Qihua, head of the China-Mongolia Friendship Association delegation, Huang Luofeng, a member of the China-North Korea Friendship Association delegation, and Wang Yuanfang, a member of the China-East Germany Friendship Association delegation, successively reported in detail at the meeting on the enthusiastic celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country in the four fraternal countries, vividly narrating the incomparably deep friendship of the people of these countries towards the Chinese people, as well as the prosperous scenes of the vigorous development of various socialist construction undertakings in the four fraternal countries.

　　Tonight, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the China-Vietnam, China-North Korea, China-Mongolia, China-East Germany, and China-Poland Friendship Associations jointly held a reception and film evening to entertain the ambassadors and diplomatic officials of the five fraternal countries of Vietnam, North Korea, Mongolia, the German Democratic Republic, and Poland, in gratitude for the warm reception of the delegations of the various friendship associations of our country by these five fraternal countries. The China-Poland Friendship Association delegation went to participate in the celebration of the 15th anniversary of the Polish National Day and conducted a friendly visit in July this year.

### 我四个友协代表团联合举行理事会议  听取访问越朝蒙德四国报告  对外文委等单位招待越朝蒙德波驻华使节

1959-11-22
第3版()
专栏：

    我四个友协代表团联合举行理事会议
    听取访问越朝蒙德四国报告
    对外文委等单位招待越朝蒙德波驻华使节
    新华社21日讯　不久前分别从越南民主共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、蒙古人民共和国、德意志民主共和国访问归来的中越、中朝、中蒙、中德四个友好协会代表团，今天下午在这四个友好协会联合举行的理事会议上，报告了他们访问上述四个兄弟国家的经过。
    这四个代表团都是不久前应邀到以上四个兄弟国家参加他们庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年活动的。中德友好协会代表团并且参加了德意志民主共和国成立十周年的庆祝活动。今天，中越友好协会代表团团长杨秀峰，中蒙友好协会代表团团长毛齐华，中朝友好协会代表团团员黄洛峰，中德友好协会代表团团员王元方，先后在会上详细报告了四个兄弟国家热烈庆祝我国建国十周年的情况，生动地叙述了这些国家的人民对中国人民无比深厚的友谊，以及四个兄弟国家各项社会主义建设事业蓬勃发展的繁荣景象。
    今天晚间，对外文化联络委员会和中越、中朝、中蒙、中德、中波等五个友好协会，联合举行酒会和电影晚会，招待越南、朝鲜、蒙古、德意志民主共和国、波兰等五个兄弟国家的驻华使节和外交官员，答谢这五个兄弟国家对我国各友好协会代表团的热情接待。中波友好协会代表团是今年7月前往参加波兰国庆十五周年庆祝活动和进行友好访问的。
