Title: Central Leaders Receive Visiting Group from Five Provinces and Regions and Receive All Representatives of the Conference on Scientific Research Work in Universities and the National Conference on Advanced Experiences in Finance

Author:
Date: 1959-11-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd: Party and state leaders Zhu De, Lin Boqu, Li Fuchun, He Long, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, Nie Rongzhen, and Guo Moruo received this afternoon the entire visiting group from five provinces and autonomous regions, including representatives of various ethnic groups, religious figures, and overseas Chinese from Henan, Hubei, Guizhou, Heilongjiang, and Inner Mongolia, as well as all representatives of the National Conference on Advanced Collectives and Advanced Workers in Finance.

Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd: Party and state leaders Zhu De, Lin Boqu, Li Fuchun, He Long, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, and Nie Rongzhen received this afternoon all the representatives attending the Conference on Scientific Research Work in Universities.

### 中央领导人接见五省区参观团  并接见高等学校科学研究工作会议  全国金融先进经验交流大会全体代表

1959-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    中央领导人接见五省区参观团
    并接见高等学校科学研究工作会议  全国金融先进经验交流大会全体代表
    新华社22日讯　党和国家的领导人朱德、林伯渠、李富春、贺龙、陆定一、康生、聂荣臻、郭沫若，今天下午接见了河南、湖北、贵州、黑龙江、内蒙古等五个省和自治区、包括各兄弟民族代表和宗教、华侨人士在内的参观团全体人员，以及全国金融工作先进集体和先进工作者经验交流大会的全体代表。
    新华社22日讯　党和国家的领导人朱德、林伯渠、李富春、贺龙、陆定一、康生、聂荣臻，今天下午接见了出席高等学校科学研究工作会议的全体代表。
