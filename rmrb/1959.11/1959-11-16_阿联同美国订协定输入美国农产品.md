Title: 阿联(Ālián)同(tóng)美(měi)国(guó)订(dìng)协定(xiédìng)输入(shūrù)美(měi)国(guó)农产品(nóngchǎnpǐn) (The United Arab Republic Signs Agreement with the United States to Import American Agricultural Products)
Author:
Date: 1959-11-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 14th. The Minister of Central Economy of the United Arab Republic, Keshouqi, and the US Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, Hale, signed an agreement in Cairo today stipulating that the United States will provide the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic with agricultural products with a market value of 9.6 million US dollars, including 75,000 tons of barley and 75,000 tons of wheat.

### 阿联同美国订协定输入美国农产品

1959-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联同美国订协定输入美国农产品
    新华社开罗14日电　阿联中央经济部长凯舒尼和美国驻阿联大使黑尔今天在开罗签订了一项协定，规定美国将给阿联的叙利亚地区提供市场价值九百六十万美元的农产品，其中包括大麦和小麦各七万五千吨。
