Title: Chinese Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Budapest to Attend Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-11-28
Page: 5

    To Attend the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Congress
    Chinese Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Budapest
    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, November 27th. The Chinese Communist Party delegation, invited to attend the Seventh Congress of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, consisting of delegation leader Tan Zhenlin and member Wang Congwu, arrived in Budapest by plane this afternoon.
    Those present at the airport to welcome them included: Marosan and Kish, members of the Political Bureau and Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, Atser, member of the Central Committee, and Szilagyi, Director of the International Liaison Department of the Party Central Committee, among others. Hao Deqing, member of the delegation and China's Ambassador to Hungary, as well as embassy staff, were also at the airport to welcome them.

### 参加匈社会主义工人党代表大会  中共代表团抵布达佩斯

1959-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    参加匈社会主义工人党代表大会
    中共代表团抵布达佩斯
    新华社布达佩斯27日电　应邀前来参加匈牙利社会主义工人党第七次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长谭震林、团员王从吾，今天下午乘飞机抵达布达佩斯。
    到机场欢迎的有：匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员、书记处书记马罗山、基什，中央委员阿策耳，党中央国际联络部部长西拉吉等人。代表团团员、我国驻匈牙利大使郝德青以及使馆工作人员也到机场迎接。
