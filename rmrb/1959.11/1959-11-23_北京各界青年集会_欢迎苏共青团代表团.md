Title: Beijing Youth Rally Welcomes Soviet Komsomol Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-11-23
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd November. 1,500 young people from all walks of life in Beijing gathered tonight to welcome the Soviet Leninist Young Communist League delegation headed by Murtazaev.
    Liu Xiyuan and Liang Buting, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and other comrades attended the welcoming meeting.
    Wang Zhaohua, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, delivered a welcoming speech at the meeting. He praised the Soviet people and youth for their great achievements under the leadership of the Soviet Communist Party, with high political enthusiasm and creative labor, in realizing the magnificent program of the Seven-Year Plan. He said that the unparalleled heroism and pioneering spirit shown by the Soviet youth in communist construction are examples for our youth to learn from. He expressed that the advanced experience and friendship brought by the Soviet Leninist Young Communist League delegation will surely encourage the youth of Beijing to make greater contributions to socialist construction.
    Comrade Murtazaev, the head of the delegation, then spoke. He said that the heroic labor shown by the young men and women of China in socialist construction makes them proud. He congratulated the Chinese youth on their greater achievements under the guidance of the Party's general line of going all out, aiming high, and building socialism with greater, faster, and more economical results. After describing the brilliant achievements of the Soviet youth in the cause of communist construction, he said that the great cause of socialist and communist construction lies before the youth of our two countries. Let us further consolidate the great friendship between the youth of our two countries, because this is the guarantee of our achievements.
    After the speech, Wang Zhaohua, on behalf of the Central Committee and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, presented the "Opening Mountains and Diverting Water" sculpture, symbolizing the great will of the Chinese people, and a "Beijing Album" to the Soviet Leninist Young Communist League delegation. The Soviet Leninist Young Communist League delegation presented a Lenin statue, a model of Lenin's works, and an album of Lenin's life to the Central Committee and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League.
    After the meeting, the hosts and guests watched Peking Opera together.

### 北京各界青年集会  欢迎苏共青团代表团

1959-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    北京各界青年集会
    欢迎苏共青团代表团
    新华社22日讯　北京各界青年一千五百多人，今晚集会欢迎以穆尔塔扎耶夫为首的苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团。
    中国共产主义青年团中央书记处书记刘西元、梁步庭等同志参加了欢迎大会。
    中国共产主义青年团北京市委员会第一书记王照华在大会上致欢迎词。他盛赞苏联人民和青年在苏联共产党领导下，以高度的政治热情和创造性的劳动，为实现七年计划这一宏伟纲领所取得的巨大成就。他说，苏联青年在共产主义建设中所表现出来的无比英雄气概和首创精神，是我国青年学习的榜样。他表示，这次苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团带来的先进经验和友谊，一定会鼓舞北京市的青年在社会主义建设中做出更大的贡献。
    穆尔塔扎耶夫团长接着讲话。他说，中国青年男女在社会主义建设中所表现出来的英勇劳动，使他们感到骄傲。他祝贺中国青年在党的鼓足干劲、力争上游、多快好省地建设社会主义的总路线的指导下，获得更大的成就。他在叙述了苏联青年在共产主义建设事业中的光辉成绩后说，在我们两国青年面前摆着伟大的社会主义和共产主义的建设事业。让我们进一步巩固我们两国青年之间的伟大友谊，因为这是我们取得成绩的保障。
    讲话结束后，王照华代表中国共产主义青年团中央委员会和北京市委员会，把象征中国人民伟大意志的“开山引水”雕塑和一本“北京画册”赠给苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团。苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团，把列宁铜像、列宁著作的模型和一本列宁生平画册，赠给中国共产主义青年团中央委员会和北京市委员会。
    会后，宾主一起观看了京戏。
