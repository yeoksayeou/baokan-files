Title: Our Gymnastics Team Arrives in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1959-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, November 27th: The Chinese men's and women's gymnastics team, consisting of twelve members and led by Zhao Bin, Vice President of the Beijing Institute of Physical Education, arrived here by plane from Kunming on the 27th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the Chinese guests included U Suwe, President of the Burmese National Fitness Committee, Assistant Secretary U Kyaw Myint, and U Than Tun, Secretary of the Burmese Amateur Gymnastics Federation. Cheng Zhiping, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 我体操队到达仰光

1959-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我体操队到达仰光
    新华社仰光27日电　由十二人组成的中国男女体操队在北京体育学院副院长赵斌率领下，27日由昆明乘飞机到达这里。
    前往机场欢迎中国客人的，有缅甸国民健身委员会会长吴素韦、助理秘书吴觉密，缅甸业余体操联合会秘书吴丹吞。中国驻缅甸大使馆文化参赞程之平也到机场欢迎。
