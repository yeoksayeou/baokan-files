Title: Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-11-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th November: The Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation, led by the head of the delegation, Sofinyecz Mihaly, Chairman of the Hungarian Group of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, and Deputy Minister of Hungarian Metallurgical and Machine Industry, left Beijing by train today for Korea.
    Among those who saw them off at the station were Wang Xinyuan, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Deputy Minister of Light Industry, and others.

### 匈科学技术合作代表团离京

1959-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    匈科学技术合作代表团离京
    新华社19日讯　匈牙利科学技术合作代表团，在团长、中匈科学技术合作委员会匈牙利组主席、匈牙利冶金和机械工业部副部长索菲涅茨·米哈伊率领下，今天乘火车离开北京去朝鲜。
    到车站欢送的有委员会中国组主席、轻工业部副部长王新元等人。
