Title: Iraqi People's Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th. The Iraqi People's Delegation, led by the Deputy Head of the Delegation and Prosecutor General of the Iraqi Ministry of Justice, Abdul Amir Okli, concluded its visit to our country and left Beijing by plane this afternoon to return home.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Luo Jun, Vice President of the Chinese-Iraqi Friendship Association, Bao Erhan, Director of the China Islamic Association, Zhang Jie, Deputy Director, and Ma Xiwu, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court.

### 伊拉克人民代表团离京回国

1959-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克人民代表团离京回国
    新华社5日讯　伊拉克人民代表团结束了在我国的参观访问，今天下午在副团长、伊拉克司法部检察长阿卜杜勒·阿密尔·奥克里率领下乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中国伊拉克友好协会副会长罗俊，中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉、副主任张杰，最高人民法院副院长马锡五等。
