Title: A Joint Celebration Held by Children from China and the Soviet Union in the Capital

Author:

Date: 1959-11-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, November 5th - More than 900 Soviet and Chinese children held a joint celebration at the Friendship Hotel in the capital today to celebrate the 42nd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

	The celebration began with music. The Young Pioneers of the Soviet middle school held a flag presentation ceremony. Then, the principal of the Soviet middle school, Kalmykov, introduced the great achievements of Soviet science and technology to the children. He encouraged the children to study hard, saying that no force in the world could destroy the friendship between the Soviet Union and China. Chinese children's representatives also spoke at the meeting, and presented Young Pioneer flags, drums and other souvenirs to the Soviet children on behalf of the Young Pioneers of Beijing. Wang Tongqi, Deputy Director of the Beijing Municipal Education Bureau, also spoke at the meeting.

	The children of the two countries performed wonderful songs and dances at the meeting.

### 首都中苏儿童举行联欢会

1959-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

    首都中苏儿童举行联欢会
    新华社5日讯　九百多名苏联和中国儿童今天在首都友谊宾馆举行了庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十二周年的苏中儿童联欢会。
    联欢会在乐曲声中开始。苏联中学的少先队员举行了出旗仪式，接着苏联中学校长卡尔梅可夫向小朋友们介绍了苏联科学技术的伟大成就。他勉励孩子们好好学习，他说，世界上没有任何力量能够破坏苏中两国的友谊。中国儿童代表也在会上讲话，并且代表北京市少先队员向苏联小朋友赠送了少先队旗和乐鼓等纪念品。北京市教育局副局长汪通祺也在会上讲了话。
    两国的儿童在会上演出了歌曲、舞蹈等精彩节目。
