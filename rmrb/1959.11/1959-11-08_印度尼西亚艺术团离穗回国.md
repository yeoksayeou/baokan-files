Title: Indonesian Art Troupe Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author: 

Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 6th: The Indonesian Art Troupe, after performing two shows in Guangzhou on the 1st and 2nd, concluded its visit and performances in our country and left Guangzhou by train this morning via Hong Kong to return home.

On the evening of the 5th, Du Ai, Vice President of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet to bid farewell to all members of the Indonesian Art Troupe. When the Indonesian guests left Guangzhou, Ren Bosheng, Secretary-General of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, and others went to the station to see them off.

### 印度尼西亚艺术团离穗回国

1959-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚艺术团离穗回国
    据新华社广州6日电　印度尼西亚艺术团1日、2日在广州演出两场后，结束了在我国的访问演出，在今天早上乘火车离开广州取道香港回国。
    5日晚上，中国人民对外文化协会广州分会副会长杜埃设宴欢送印度尼西亚艺术团全体团员。印度尼西亚客人离开广州的时候，中国印度尼西亚友好协会总干事任泊生等到车站欢送。
