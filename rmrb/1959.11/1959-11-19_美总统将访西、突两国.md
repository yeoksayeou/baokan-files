Title: US President to Visit Spain and Tunisia

Author: 
Date: 1959-11-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington dispatch: The White House announced on the 11th that US President Eisenhower will make a brief visit to Madrid on December 21 to hold talks with the Spanish dictator Franco. In addition, Eisenhower will also meet with Tunisian President Bourguiba on an American cruiser on December 17.

### 美总统将访西、突两国

1959-11-19
第6版()
专栏：

    美总统将访西、突两国
    据新华社讯华盛顿消息：据白宫11日宣布，美国总统艾森豪威尔将在12月21日到马德里作一次短暂访问，同西班牙的独裁者佛朗哥举行会谈。此外，艾森豪威尔还将在12月17日同突尼斯总统布尔吉巴在一艘美国巡洋舰上会谈。
