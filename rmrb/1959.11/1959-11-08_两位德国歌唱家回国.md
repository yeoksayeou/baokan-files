Title: Two German Singers Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. According to the 1959 implementation plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the singers Christa Maria Ziese and Wilfried Krug, who came to China with the German Democratic Republic's Dresden Symphony Orchestra for a performance tour, returned home by plane today after visiting Beijing, Chongqing, Nanjing, Shanghai, and other places.

Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Yu Yixuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Musicians Association and female singer, and Wei Mingquan, male singer, and others.

### 两位德国歌唱家回国

1959-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    两位德国歌唱家回国
    新华社7日讯　根据中德文化合作协定1959年执行计划，随德意志民主共和国德累斯顿交响乐团来我国访问演出的歌唱家克莱斯特·玛丽娅·齐塞和威尔弗雷德·克鲁格，在访问了北京、重庆、南京、上海等地以后，今天乘飞机回国。
    前往机场欢送的有中国音乐家协会书记处书记、女歌唱家喻宜萱和男歌唱家魏鸣泉等人。
