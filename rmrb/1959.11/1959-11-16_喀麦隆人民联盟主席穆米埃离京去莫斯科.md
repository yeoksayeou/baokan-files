Title: President Moumié of the Cameroon People's Union Leaves Beijing for Moscow
Author:
Date: 1959-11-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 15th November: Félix-Roland Moumié, President of the Cameroon People's Union, who visited our country at the invitation of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, left Beijing by plane this morning for Moscow. Those who went to the airport to see him off included Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Bao Erhan, Vice-Chairman, and Zhu Ziqi, Secretary-General.

### 喀麦隆人民联盟主席穆米埃离京去莫斯科

1959-11-16
第5版()
专栏：

    喀麦隆人民联盟主席穆米埃离京去莫斯科
    新华社15日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会邀请来我国访问的喀麦隆人民联盟主席费利克斯·罗兰·穆米埃，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京去莫斯科。前往机场欢送的有中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志、副主席包尔汉、秘书长朱子奇。
