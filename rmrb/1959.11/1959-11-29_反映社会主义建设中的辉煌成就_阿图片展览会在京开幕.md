Title: Albania Photo Exhibition Opens in Beijing, Reflecting Brilliant Achievements in Socialist Construction
Author:
Date: 1959-11-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The "Photo Exhibition of the 15-Year Achievements of the People's Republic of Albania," jointly organized by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Sino-Albanian Friendship Association, opened this morning in Zhongshan Park.

Those who attended the exhibition today included Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zeng Yongquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Li Chao, Secretary-General of the Sino-Albanian Friendship Association, Le Songsheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, other responsible persons from relevant central departments and Beijing, as well as diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China. Albanian Ambassador to China, Parifti, accompanied them on their tour.

The large number of photos and statistical charts on display fully demonstrate the brilliant achievements Albania has made in socialist construction over the past 15 years since its liberation. Albania has transformed from a backward agricultural country into a country with modernized industry and cooperative agriculture. The exhibition detailed the great achievements the Albanian people have made under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party in various aspects such as industry, agriculture, cultural education, and continuous improvement of people's lives. The exhibition is scheduled to end on December 19th. (Attached Photo)

### 反映社会主义建设中的辉煌成就  阿图片展览会在京开幕

1959-11-29
第2版()
专栏：

    反映社会主义建设中的辉煌成就
    阿图片展览会在京开幕
    新华社28日讯　对外文化联络委员会和中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会联合举办的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国十五年成就图片展览会，今天上午在中山公园开幕。
    今天出席观看展览会的，有对外文委副主任屈武，外交部副部长曾涌泉，中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会秘书长李超，北京市副市长乐松生，中央有关部门和北京市的其他负责人，以及各国驻华使节和外交官员。阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使帕里夫蒂，陪同他们一起参观。
    展出的大批照片和统计图表，充分显示阿尔巴尼亚解放十五年来在社会主义建设中取得了辉煌成就。阿尔巴尼亚已经由落后的农业国家变成一个拥有现代化的工业和合作化的农业的国家。展览会详细地介绍了阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，在工业、农业、文化教育和不断提高人民生活等各方面取得的巨大成绩。展览会预定在12月19日结束。（附图片）
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国十五年成就图片展览会开幕，群众在参观展览会。　新华社记者　塔吉古勒摄
