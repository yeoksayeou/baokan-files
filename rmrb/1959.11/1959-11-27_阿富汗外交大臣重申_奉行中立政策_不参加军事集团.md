Title: Afghan Foreign Minister Reiterates Policy of Neutrality; Will Not Participate in Military Blocs
Author:
Date: 1959-11-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, November 23rd, TASS Dispatch: Afghan Foreign Minister Mohammed Naim Khan stated, "We do not want to participate in military blocs. The purpose of Afghanistan's neutral policy is to strengthen peace, trust, and friendship among all nations." He made the aforementioned statement to reporters via Kabul Radio in response to Pakistani President Ayub Khan's expression of hope at the Tehran meeting of Central Treaty Organization member states that Afghanistan would join the Central Treaty Organization.

The Afghan Foreign Minister also said that his country has long desired to resolve the existing differences between Afghanistan and Pakistan on a friendly basis, and to establish a realistic foundation for friendship between the two countries.

### 阿富汗外交大臣重申  奉行中立政策　不参加军事集团

1959-11-27
第6版()
专栏：

    阿富汗外交大臣重申
    奉行中立政策　不参加军事集团
    新华社24日讯　塔斯社喀布尔23日讯：阿富汗外交大臣穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗说，“我们不想参加军事集团。阿富汗的中立政策的目的是加强各国人民之间的和平、信任和友谊。”他是针对巴基斯坦总统阿尤布汗在中央条约组织成员国德黑兰会议上表示希望阿富汗参加中央条约组织一事，通过喀布尔电台向记者们发表了上述声明的。
    阿富汗外交大臣还说，他的国家早就渴望在友好基础上解决阿富汗和巴基斯坦两国之间现存的分歧，并且为两国的友好建立现实基础。
