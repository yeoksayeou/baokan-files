Title: Sino-Polish Gymnastics Friendship Competition Concludes
Author:
Date: 1959-11-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd. The Sino-Polish Gymnastics Friendship Competition concluded completely on the evening of November 1st. In the men's competition held that evening, the Chinese team won with a score of 280.85 against 279.90 (the total score of the best five athletes from each side). Chinese athlete Yu Lie-feng won the all-around championship with a total score of 56.55 (out of a possible 60). The runner-up was China's Bao Nai-jian (56.45); the third place went to Polish athlete Konopka (56.30).

The Polish men's and women's gymnastics teams will leave Beijing on the 4th and will visit Xi'an, Chongqing, Wuhan and other places for performances.

### 中波体操友谊赛结束

1959-11-03
第6版()
专栏：

    中波体操友谊赛结束
    据新华社2日讯　中、波体操友谊比赛1日晚全部结束。在这天晚上举行的男子组的比赛中，中国队以二百八十点八五比二百七十九点九零分（双方各计五名成绩最好选手得分总和）获胜。中国选手于烈烽获得全能冠军，他的总分是五十六点五五分，（满分六十分），亚军是中国队的鲍乃健（五十六点四五分）；第三名是波兰队的运动健将科诺普卡（五十六点三分）。
    波兰男女体操队将在4日离北京，先后去西安、重庆、武汉等地表演。
