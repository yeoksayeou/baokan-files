Title: The Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association Hosted a Banquet for Bulgarian Singers

Author: 
Date: 1959-11-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 13th - The Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association jointly hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Bulgarian singers.

	Qu Wu, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Cai Ruohong, Vice-President of the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association, Zhao Feng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Musicians Association, Yu Yixuan, and others attended the banquet. Tatyev, Counselor of the Bulgarian Embassy in China, was also invited to attend the banquet.

	At the banquet, Qu Wu raised his glass to congratulate the Bulgarian singers on their successful performances in China and toasted the friendship between the people of China and Bulgaria.

### 我对外文委和中保友协宴请保加利亚歌唱家

1959-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    我对外文委和中保友协宴请保加利亚歌唱家
    新华社13日讯　对外文化联络委员会和中国保加利亚友好协会今晚联合设宴欢迎保加利亚歌唱家。
    出席宴会作陪的有对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武，中保友好协会副会长蔡若虹，中国音乐家协会书记处书记赵沨、喻宜萱等。保加利亚驻华大使馆参赞塔泰夫也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会上，屈武举杯祝贺保加利亚歌唱家们在中国演出成功，并为中保两国人民的友谊干杯。
