Title: High-Grade Paper as Thin as a Cicada's Wing, Beautiful as Brocade
Author:
Date: 1959-11-17
Page: 2

    Six-Micron Capacitor Paper
    The six-micron capacitor paper recently successfully manufactured by the Minfeng Paper Mill in Jiaxing, Zhejiang, is currently the most technically complex and demanding type of paper in China's papermaking industry. This paper is "as thin as a cicada's wing," with the thinnest being only one-sixth the thickness of a human hair. It is one of the insulating dielectric materials for capacitors used in national defense communications. The ignition coil of the Dongfeng sedan also uses this capacitor paper.

    Plastic Laminate Paper
    Plastic laminate paper is a new material for decorating walls. It is made from bleached sulfite wood pulp as the base paper for lamination and then chemically processed. This paper has various beautiful patterns on its surface and can be used for decorating wooden appliances and building interiors, making them both beautiful and lightweight. The interior walls of the Great Hall of the People in Beijing used this plastic laminate paper produced by the Shanghai Guofeng Paper Mill.

    Metallized Paper
    Metallized paper is a necessary paper for manufacturing communication cables. This paper has a very thin layer of aluminum foil attached to one side of the cable paper, making it resistant to high temperatures, even at 150 degrees Celsius, the aluminum foil will not peel off. Currently, only a few countries in the world can produce it. Workers at the Shanghai Huali Factory successfully trial-produced this high-grade paper this year.

### 高级纸薄如蝉翼美似锦

1959-11-17
第2版()
专栏：

    高级纸薄如蝉翼美似锦
    六微米电容器纸
    最近浙江嘉兴民丰造纸厂制造成功的六微米电容器纸，是目前我国造纸工业中技术最复杂、要求最高的一种纸。这种纸“薄如蝉翼”，最薄的只有头发丝厚度的六分之一。它是国防通讯用的电容器的绝缘介质材料之一。东风牌小轿车的发火线圈，用的也是这种电容器纸。  
    塑料贴面纸
    塑料贴面纸是装饰墙壁的一种新材料。它是用漂白亚硫酸木浆制作贴面原纸，再经过化学加工后制成的。这种纸的纸面上有各种美丽的花纹，可供木材器具与建筑物内壁装饰用，既美观又轻巧。北京人民大会堂的内壁就采用了上海国丰造纸厂生产的这种塑料贴面纸。
    金属化纸
    金属化纸是制造通讯电缆必要的纸张。这种纸是在电缆纸的一面贴上一层很薄的铝箔，能耐高温，即使在摄氏150度，铝箔也不会脱落。目前世界上只有很少几个国家能够生产。上海华丽厂职工今年试制成功了这种高级纸。
