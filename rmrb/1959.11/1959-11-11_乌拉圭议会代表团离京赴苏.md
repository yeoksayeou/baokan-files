Title: Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation Leaves Beijing for Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1959-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 10th -- The Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation, led by Francisco Rodriguez Camusso, Speaker of the House of Representatives of Uruguay, left Beijing by plane today for Moscow after visiting Beijing, Shenyang, Anshan, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other cities.

Wu Maosun and Wu Xiaoda, responsible persons of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, went to the airport to see them off today.

### 乌拉圭议会代表团离京赴苏

1959-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭议会代表团离京赴苏
    新华社10日讯　由乌拉圭众议院议长弗朗西斯科·罗德里格斯·卡穆索率领的乌拉圭议会代表团，在访问了北京、沈阳、鞍山、上海、杭州等城市以后，于今天乘飞机离开北京前往莫斯科访问。
    中国人民外交学会负责人吴茂荪、吴晓达今天去机场欢送。
