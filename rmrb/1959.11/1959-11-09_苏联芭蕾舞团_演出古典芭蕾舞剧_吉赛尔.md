Title: Soviet Ballet Troupe Performs Classical Ballet "Giselle"
Author:
Date: 1959-11-09
Page: 5

    Soviet Ballet Troupe
    Performs Classical Ballet "Giselle"
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th—The Soviet State Grand Theatre Ballet Troupe performed the famous classical ballet "Giselle" tonight. The newly arrived Soviet People's Artist R.S. Struchkova, who is visiting and performing in our country, played Giselle in the play. She expressed the inner feelings of the heroine with extremely beautiful and moving dance.
    The male protagonist in the play was played by Fadeyechev, an Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Socialist Republic. Honored Artists Levashov and Baglyubovskaya also participated in the performance tonight.
    The superb performances of the Soviet artists were warmly welcomed by more than 1,500 spectators.

### 苏联芭蕾舞团  演出古典芭蕾舞剧“吉赛尔”

1959-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联芭蕾舞团
    演出古典芭蕾舞剧“吉赛尔”
    据新华社8日讯　苏联国家大剧院芭蕾舞团今晚演出著名的古典芭蕾舞剧“吉赛尔”。新来我国访问演出的苏联人民演员拉·斯·斯特路齐柯娃，扮演剧中的吉赛尔，她以极其优美动人的舞蹈，细腻地表达出女主人公的内心感受。
    剧中的男主角由俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国功勋演员法捷耶切夫扮演。功勋演员列瓦绍夫、包格柳勃斯卡娅，今晚也参加了演出。
    苏联艺术家的精湛表演，受到一千五百多观众的热烈欢迎。
