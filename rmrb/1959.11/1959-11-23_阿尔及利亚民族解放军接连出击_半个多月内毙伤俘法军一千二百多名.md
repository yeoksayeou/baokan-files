Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Launches Successive Attacks, Killing, Wounding, and Capturing Over 1,200 French Soldiers in Just Over Half a Month

Author: 
Date: 1959-11-23
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency Comprehensive Report: According to military communiques recently released by the Supreme Command of the Algerian National Liberation Army, from November 1st to 18th, the Algerian National Liberation Army attacked sixty positions held by the French invading forces, engaged in fifty-seven battles, and carried out twenty-five ambushes against the enemy.
    In this period of just over half a month, the Algerian National Liberation Army killed 851 French invading soldiers, wounded 418, and captured four French soldiers; nine French soldiers defected. During this period, they destroyed 60 French military vehicles, 7 tanks, 6 armored vehicles, and 2 trains. They seized 72 enemy weapons and a large quantity of military supplies and ammunition.
    The communique pointed out that Algerian National Liberation Army assault teams, armed with grenades and automatic weapons, launched eighteen attacks on French military and police strongholds in seventeen Algerian towns. They also attacked French military recreational centers.
    In addition to the regular armed forces, Algerian nationalists also participated in the fighting, cutting off some French transportation lines, seizing 38 enemy weapons, destroying 19 enemy military vehicles, and blowing up a train.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放军接连出击  半个多月内毙伤俘法军一千二百多名

1959-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚民族解放军接连出击
    半个多月内毙伤俘法军一千二百多名
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：阿尔及利亚民族解放军最高司令部最近陆续发表的军事公报记载，从11月1日到18日，阿尔及利亚民族解放军对法国侵略军六十个阵地进攻，曾经进行五十七次战斗，并且向敌军进行过二十五次伏击战。
    在这半个多月的时间内，阿尔及利亚民族解放军共打死法国侵略军士兵八百五十一名，打伤四百一十八名，此外，俘获了四名法军，有九名法兵投诚。在此期间，击毁法军军用车辆六十辆、坦克七辆、装甲车六辆、火车两列。缴获敌人七十二件武器和大量军火弹药。
    公报指出，由手榴弹和自动武器武装的阿尔及利亚民族解放军突击队曾在阿尔及利亚十七个城镇对法国军警据点进行了十八次袭击。也袭击到了法国军队的娱乐中心。
    除了正规武装之外，阿尔及利亚民族主义者也参加了战斗，切断法军一些交通线，缴获敌人三十八件武器，击毁敌人十九辆军用车，炸毁一列火车。
