Title: Norwegian Labor Party Member Finn Moe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Norwegian Labor Party member, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs and Constitutional Committee of the Parliament, Finn Moe and his wife arrived in Beijing this afternoon for a visit to our country.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Zhou Gengsheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, Wu Kejian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and others.
    Norwegian Ambassador to China, Mr. Klog-Hansen, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 挪威工党议员芬·穆到达北京

1958-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威工党议员芬·穆到达北京
    新华社25日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的挪威工党议员，议会外交、宪法委员会主席芬·穆和夫人，今天下午到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、秘书长吴茂荪、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长吴克坚等。
    挪威驻华大使克洛格—亨生也前往机场迎接。
