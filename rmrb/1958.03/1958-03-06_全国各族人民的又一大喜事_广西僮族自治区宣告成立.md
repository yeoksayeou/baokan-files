Title: Another Great Happy Event for the People of All Nationalities Across the Country: The Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region is Proclaimed Established

Author: 
Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 5th. The Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region was formally established today. The first session of the First People's Congress of the Autonomous Region solemnly opened in Nanning today.

A total of 412 representatives of various ethnic groups attended the meeting. Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, representing the CPC Central Committee, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao, came to congratulate the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, participated in today's meeting, and delivered a speech at the meeting. Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Xie Fumin, Deputy Chairman of the National People's Congress Nationalities Committee, attended the meeting. Representatives from the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, and various provinces and cities also attended the meeting.

Old comrades who participated in the arduous struggle in Guangxi during the domestic revolutionary war and famous people from Guangxi working in other places also attended today's meeting.

The delegation of people of all ethnic groups from the Viet Bac Autonomous Region of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Chu Van Tan, with Phan Van Tinh and Duong Cong Ho as deputy heads, and all its members, also attended the meeting. Chu Van Tan also spoke at the meeting.

The conference began with music and firecrackers. Wei Guoqing delivered the opening speech on behalf of the presidium of the congress. When he announced that "the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region is formally established," prolonged applause erupted from the audience, and representatives and guests rejoiced together at the birth of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. Wei Guoqing said that this meeting will produce the People's Committee of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, elect members of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Region, and discuss the various tasks of socialist construction in the Autonomous Region. At present, a vigorous upsurge in industrial and agricultural production is unfolding throughout the Autonomous Region, and a situation of a comprehensive great leap forward in socialist construction has emerged. The establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region will surely more powerfully promote this upsurge to new victories.

Next, Vice Premier He Long spoke, first extending warm congratulations to the people of all ethnic groups in Guangxi. He pointed out: After the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, the people of all ethnic groups in the Autonomous Region must further consolidate and strengthen the unity and cooperation of all ethnic groups, and on the basis of continuing to complete the rectification movement and opposing bourgeois rightists, mobilize the people of all ethnic groups in Guangxi to actively participate in the socialist construction of the motherland with unparalleled enthusiasm. Everyone should "go all out, aim high," and strive to complete the revised draft of the National Program for Agricultural Development ahead of schedule in the Autonomous Region, and strive for the local industrial output value of the Autonomous Region to catch up with the agricultural output value in a short period of time, strive to change the face of the Autonomous Region in the shortest possible time, and further improve the people's material and cultural life.

Chu Van Tan, head of the delegation of people of all ethnic groups from the Viet Bac Autonomous Region of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, addressed the conference. He said: The establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region is a glorious and historically significant day for the people of all ethnic groups in the Autonomous Region, and a happy event for all the Chinese people; at the same time, we also regard it as a happy event for the Vietnamese people. We warmly congratulate the great victory of the fraternal Chinese people and congratulate the achievements made by the people of all ethnic groups in Guangxi in launching a competition in socialist construction to welcome the establishment of the Autonomous Region.

Amid warm applause, the presidium of the congress read out congratulatory telegrams from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress、theState Council, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and accepted congratulatory banners from the Nationalities Affairs Commission, the Nationalities Affairs Committee of the National People's Congress, and the delegation of people of all ethnic groups from the Viet Bac Autonomous Region of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The meeting will continue tomorrow.

### 全国各族人民的又一大喜事  广西僮族自治区宣告成立

1958-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    全国各族人民的又一大喜事
    广西僮族自治区宣告成立
    据新华社南宁5日电　广西僮族自治区在今天正式成立。自治区第一届人民代表大会第一次会议今天在南宁隆重开幕。
    出席会议的各族代表共四百一十二人。代表中共中央、中央人民政府和毛主席前来祝贺广西僮族自治区成立的国务院副总理贺龙，参加了今天的会议，并在会上讲了话。民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、全国人民代表大会民族委员会副主任委员谢扶民出席了会议。参加会议的还有内蒙古自治区、新疆维吾尔自治区、西藏自治区筹备委员会以及各省市的代表们。
    国内革命战争时期在广西参加艰苦斗争的老同志、广西在外地工作的著名人士，也参加了今天的会议。
    参加会议的还有越南民主共和国越北自治区各族人民代表团团长朱文晋，副团长潘文省、杨公活和全体团员，朱文晋并在会上讲了话。
    大会在乐声和鞭炮声中开始。韦国清代表大会主席团致开幕词。当他宣布“广西僮族自治区宣告正式成立了”的时候，全场发出了经久不息的掌声，代表们和来宾们共同为广西僮族自治区的诞生而欢欣。韦国清说，这次会议将产生广西僮族自治区人民委员会，选举自治区人民委员会组成人员，并讨论自治区社会主义建设的各项任务。目前，全自治区的工农业生产高潮正在波澜壮阔地开展着，社会主义建设全面大跃进的形势已经出现。广西僮族自治区的成立，必将更有力地推动这个高潮走向新的胜利。
    接着，贺龙副总理讲话，他首先向广西各族人民表示热烈的祝贺。他指出：广西僮族自治区成立后，自治区各族人民要更进一步巩固和加强各民族的团结合作，在继续完成整风运动和反对资产阶级右派的基础上，动员广西各族人民以无比的热情积极地参加祖国的社会主义建设。人人都应该“鼓起干劲，力争上游”，为在自治区提前完成农业发展纲要修正草案、为自治区的地方工业产值能够在不长的时间内赶上农业的产值而努力奋斗，争取在最短期间改变自治区的面貌，并且更加改善人民的物质文化生活。
    越南民主共和国越北自治区各族人民代表团团长朱文晋向大会致词。他说：广西僮族自治区的成立，是自治区各族人民光荣的有历史意义的日子，是中国全体人民的喜事；同时我们也把它当作越南人民的喜事。我们热烈祝贺兄弟般的中国人民的伟大胜利，祝贺广西各族人民为迎接自治区成立在社会主义建设事业中展开竞赛所获得的成就。
    大会主席团在热烈的掌声中，宣读了全国人民代表大会常务委员会、国务院、中华全国总工会、共青团中央委员会的贺电，并且接受了民族事务委员会、全国人民代表大会民族委员会和越南民主共和国越北自治区各族人民代表团的贺幛。
    会议明天继续进行。
