Title: Faisal Concurrently Serves as Commander of Saudi Arabian Army
Author:
Date: 1958-03-29
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 26th (TASS): Some newspapers, quoting Radio Mecca, reported that Saudi Arabian Crown Prince and Prime Minister Faisal, in addition to being granted authority over internal affairs, foreign affairs, and finance by the King, has now also been appointed as the Commander-in-Chief of the Saudi Arabian Army.

### 费萨尔兼任沙军队统帅

1958-03-29
第6版()
专栏：

    费萨尔兼任沙军队统帅
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社开罗26日讯：有些报纸引用麦加电台的消息说，沙特阿拉伯王储、首相费萨尔除了被国王授予内政、外交和财政的权力以外，现在又被任命为沙特阿拉伯军队的总司令。
