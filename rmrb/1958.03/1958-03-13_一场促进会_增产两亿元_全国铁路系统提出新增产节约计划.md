Title: A Promotion Meeting Increases Production by 200 Million Yuan; National Railway System Proposes New Production Increase and Savings Plan
Author:
Date: 1958-03-13
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

    A Promotion Meeting Increases Production by 200 Million Yuan
    National Railway System Proposes New Production Increase and Savings Plan
    (Xinhua News Agency dispatch) The Ministry of Railways recently convened a meeting of the heads of national railway administrations in Shanghai. Leading cadres from the seventeen national railway administrations participating in the meeting all put forward their own bureaus' production increase and savings targets at this meeting. According to statistics, the national railway system can increase production and save more than 670 million yuan this year, an increase of more than 220 million yuan compared to the Ministry of Railways' estimated figure.

### 一场促进会　增产两亿元  全国铁路系统提出新增产节约计划

1958-03-13
第3版()
专栏：

    一场促进会　增产两亿元
    全国铁路系统提出新增产节约计划
    据新华社讯　铁道部最近在上海召开了全国铁路管理局局长会议。参加会议的全国十七个铁路管理局的领导干部，在这次会议上都提出了自己局的增产节约指标。据统计，今年一年，全国铁路系统可以增产节约六亿七千多万元，比铁道部预计数增加二亿二千多万元。
