Title: Philippine Senior Politician Supplies Arms to Rebel Group
Author:
Date: 1958-03-27
Page: 6

    Philippine Senior Politician
    Supplies Arms to Rebel Group
    Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 26th: According to the Philippine News Agency on the 25th, "smugglers" in the Sulu Island of the southern Philippines are supplying arms to the rebel group in Indonesia. The news agency said that according to "unconfirmed reports," a senior Philippine politician "disguised military arms as ordinary goods and transported them by merchant ship to provinces in northern Indonesia." The son-in-law of this senior politician is in charge of this "business."
    Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, 26th: Indonesian Consul General in Singapore, Achmad Natanegara, stated on the 24th that the Indonesian government is dissatisfied that Singapore authorities have not taken active measures to deal with rebels engaging in anti-Indonesian government activities in Singapore.

### 菲律宾高级政客  向叛乱集团运送军火

1958-03-27
第6版()
专栏：

    菲律宾高级政客
    向叛乱集团运送军火
    据新华社26日讯　马尼拉消息：据菲律宾通讯社25日报道，菲律宾南部苏禄岛的“走私者”正在把军火供给印度尼西亚的叛乱集团。这家通讯社说，据“未证实的消息”，一个菲律宾高级政客“把军火假装成普通货物用商船运送到印度尼西亚北部的省份去”。负责这种“生意”的是这个高级政客的女婿。
    据新华社26日讯　新加坡消息：印度尼西亚驻新加坡总领事阿末诺丹那克拉在24日发表谈话说，印度尼西亚政府对新加坡当局没有采取积极行动来对付在新加坡进行反对印度尼西亚政府活动的叛乱分子这一点感到不满。
