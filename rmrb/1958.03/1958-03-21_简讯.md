    Brief News
    Czechoslovakia and Britain Sign This Year's Trade Protocol
    Prague Dispatch: Czechoslovakia and Britain signed a trade protocol for 1958 here on the 19th.
    The protocol stipulates that Czechoslovakia will export machine tools, furniture, glassware, timber, textiles, food, etc., to Britain, and Britain will export machinery, machine equipment, industrial raw materials, chemicals, textiles, etc., to Czechoslovakia.
    China and Bulgaria Sign This Year's Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan
    Sofia Dispatch: The 1958 implementation plan of the China-Bulgaria Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed here on the afternoon of the 19th.
    The plan stipulates that cooperation between China and Bulgaria in science, education, literature, and art will be further strengthened and developed.

### 简讯

专栏：简讯

    简讯
    捷英签订今年换货议定书
    布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克和英国19日在这里签订了1958年换货议定书。
    议定书规定，捷克斯洛伐克将向英国输出工作母机、家具、玻璃器皿、木材、纺织品、食品等，英国将向捷克斯洛伐克输出机器、机器设备、工业原料、化学品、纺织品等。
    中保签订今年文化合作执行计划
    索非亚讯：中国和保加利亚文化合作协定1958年执行计划19日下午在这里签字。
    计划规定，中保两国在科学、教育、文学、艺术各方面的合作将得到进一步的加强和发展。
