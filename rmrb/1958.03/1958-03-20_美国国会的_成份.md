Author: Ābùlā mò fū
Date: 1958-03-20
Page: 5

    The "Composition" of the US Congress
    Soviet Union  Abramov
    Of the five hundred and thirty-one members of the US Congress, not one is a worker or a farmer. More than half are lawyers serving monopoly capital, and a quarter are business owners and bankers. The United States' seventeen and a half million Black people, accounting for 10.4 percent of the total population, are represented by only three Black members of Congress, accounting for 0.56 percent.

### 美国国会的“成份”
阿布拉莫夫
1958-03-20
第5版()
专栏：

    美国国会的“成份”
    苏联  阿布拉莫夫
    美国国会的五百三十一名议员中，没有一个工人，也没有一个农民，半数以上是为垄断资本服务的律师，四分之一是企业主和银行家。美国的一千七百五十万黑人，占人口总数的百分之十点四，但国会议员中却只有三名黑人，占百分之零点五六。
