Title: Zhangjiakou Prepares to Build a Steel Plant
Author:
Date: 1958-03-03
Page: 3

Column: Flowers of Construction

    Zhangjiakou Prepares to Build a Steel Plant
    Zhangjiakou City is preparing to build a small-scale iron and steel plant in Xuanhua with an annual output of 70,000 tons of pig iron, 50,000 tons of steel, and 43,000 tons of steel products.
    This steel plant will produce iron this year and strive to produce steel in the same year. The entire plant construction project will be completed in 1959.

### 张家口筹建钢铁厂

1958-03-03
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    张家口筹建钢铁厂
    张家口市正在宣化筹建一座年产生铁七万吨、钢五万吨、钢材四万三千吨的小型钢铁厂。
    这座钢铁厂今年将出铁，同时争取今年出钢。全部建厂工程将于1959年完成。
