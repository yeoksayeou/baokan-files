Author:
Date: 1958-03-06

    Brief News

    Improving Saline-Alkali Land
    It is estimated that about 30% of the cultivated land in western Jilin is saline-alkali land. In order to transform these low-yielding fields into high-yielding fields, Jilin Province has recently determined the guidelines, methods, and action plans for improving saline-alkali soil in the future.

### 改良盐碱地

1958-03-06
第2版()
专栏：简讯

    改良盐碱地
    在吉林西部估计约有30％的耕地是盐碱地。为了使这些低产田变为高产田，吉林省最近已确定了今后改良盐碱土的方针、办法与行动计划。
