Title: Japanese Matsuyama Ballet Troupe Concludes Performances in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: The Japanese Matsuyama Ballet Troupe gave its final performance in Beijing tonight. Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Culture, and Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, went backstage during the intermission to congratulate the Japanese artists on the success of their performances in the capital. Qian Junrui said that the performances of the Matsuyama Ballet Troupe have provided the Chinese art community with many things worth learning and have made valuable contributions to strengthening Sino-Japanese cultural exchange and the friendship between the two peoples.
    Matsuyama Mikiko, the deputy head and principal dancer of the ballet troupe, told Qian Junrui that the warm welcome of the audience in the capital to the Japanese artists had deeply moved them. She expressed her commitment to working even harder to strengthen cultural exchange between the two countries.
    In addition to its main program, "The White-Haired Girl," the Matsuyama Ballet Troupe also performed excerpts from the classical ballet "Fountain of Bakhchisarai" and six other pieces tonight. Each of their performances was met with prolonged applause from the audience, and the performers had to take multiple curtain calls.
    The Japanese artists will leave Beijing in the coming days to perform in the Southwest and other regions.

### 日本松山芭蕾舞团在京演出结束

1958-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    日本松山芭蕾舞团在京演出结束
    据新华社23日讯　日本松山芭蕾舞团今晚在北京举行了最后一次演出。文化部副部长钱俊瑞和对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武，在演出休息时特地到后台祝贺日本艺术家们在首都演出的成功。钱俊瑞说，松山芭蕾舞团的演出，为中国艺术界提供了许多值得学习的地方，并且为加强中日文化交流和两国人民友谊都作出了可贵的贡献。
    芭蕾舞团副团长、主要演员松山树子对钱俊瑞说，首都观众对日本艺术家的热情欢迎，使他们非常感动。她表示要更好地为加强两国文化交流而努力。
    松山芭蕾舞团今夜除演出了主要节目“白毛女”外，还演出了古典芭蕾舞剧“泪泉”的片断等六个节目。他们演出的每个节目都博得全场长时间的掌声，演员们不得不多次谢幕。
    日本艺术家们将在日内离开北京去西南等地演出。
