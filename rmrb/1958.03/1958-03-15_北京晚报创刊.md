Title: Beijing Evening News Begins Publication
Author:
Date: 1958-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: "Beijing Evening News" is scheduled to begin publication on the 15th. The inaugural issue's editorial states that "Beijing Evening News" is primarily intended for city residents. Its mission is to publicize the Party and government's guidelines and policies, conduct socialist education, reflect the masses' production, work, life, and thoughts, and the layout should be lively, engaging, and rich in illustrations.

"Beijing Evening News" will be published daily in a four-folio, four-page format, with two pages of news and two pages of supplements.

### 北京晚报创刊

1958-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    北京晚报创刊
    新华社14日讯　“北京晚报”定15日创刊。创刊号的发刊词说明：“北京晚报”主要是给城市居民看的。它的任务是宣传党和政府的方针政策，进行社会主义教育，反映群众生产、工作、生活和思想情况，版面要求做到生动活泼、图文并茂。
    “北京晚报”每天出四开四版，有两版新闻和两版副刊。
