Title: Wishing the Returning Volunteers a Safe Journey: Korean Party and Government Leaders Hold Farewell Receptions Separately
Author:
Date: 1958-03-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 12th: Premier Kim Il-sung and other Korean Party and Government leaders, including General Kim Kwang-hyup, Chairman Li Jong-ok, and Minister of Transportation Kim Hoe-il, accompanied by General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and Qiao Xiaoguang, China's Ambassador to Korea, personally visited and bid farewell to officers and soldiers of a unit of the first group of Chinese People's Volunteers withdrawing from Korea this afternoon. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the officers and soldiers of this unit.

After arriving at the headquarters of this unit, Premier Kim Il-sung received Li Ying-jae, a Second-Grade Hero of the unit, and Jie Fazheng, as well as Zhang Lichun, a First-Class Merit recipient who shot down or damaged seven enemy aircraft during the Korean War, and Huang Zhifu, hailed as the "Living Luo Shengjiao" by the Korean people. He shook hands with them warmly and took group photos.

Subsequently, Premier Kim Il-sung and others held a farewell meeting at this unit.

At the meeting, Premier Kim Il-sung delivered a farewell speech. He said that the Chinese People's Volunteers were withdrawing from Korea to promote the peaceful reunification of our country. The comrades have left behind glorious achievements that will shine forever in history, leaving boundless friendship in the hearts of our people. The fighting friendship and unity forged in blood between the Korean and Chinese peoples, and between the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, will be eternal and will be further developed.

Premier Kim Il-sung said, "I can assure you that the Korean people, with the active support and solidarity of the people of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and other socialist countries, will be able to firmly defend the outpost of peace and socialism in the East."

In his speech, Premier Kim Il-sung hoped that the Volunteers would convey the Korean people's profound love and respect for the Volunteers and the entire Chinese people upon their return to China. He also encouraged the Volunteers to make greater contributions to the construction and defense of socialism after returning home.

Premier Kim Il-sung's speech was repeatedly interrupted by storms of applause.

Subsequently, Chairman Li Jong-ok and Minister of Transportation Kim Hoe-il, representing the Korean Workers' Party and state organs, and General Kim Kwang-hyup, representing the Korean People's Army, presented flags to the leadership of this unit of the Volunteers. Premier Kim Il-sung also awarded the officers and soldiers of this unit the Fatherland Liberation War Medal and the Korean-Chinese Friendship Medal, personally pinning the medals on the chests of the unit's commanders, including Major General Wu Ruishan, Senior Colonel Xie Fulin, and Senior Colonel Dang Wanhe. At this moment, the slogans "Thank you, Premier Kim Il-sung, the Korean Workers' Party, the Republic's Government, and the Korean people for your care for us!" and "Long live the fighting friendship forged in blood between the Chinese and Korean people!" and cheers resounded across the venue.

Wu Ruishan delivered a speech of thanks at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 12th: Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Kim Chang-man, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, who came with Premier Kim Il-sung to the first group of Chinese People's Volunteers withdrawing from Korea, were accompanied separately today by General Wang Ping, Political Commissar of the Volunteers, and Lieutenant General Zhang Tianyun, Commander of the first group of troops withdrawing from Korea, to bid farewell to two units of the Volunteers.

When Chairman Choi Yong-kun, Vice-Chairman Kim Chang-man, and others arrived at the locations of these two units of the Volunteers, they were welcomed by the neatly arrayed officers and soldiers of the Volunteers lining the streets.

At 2:30 PM, Chairman Choi Yong-kun held a farewell rally at the location of a Volunteer unit. At the meeting, Chairman Choi Yong-kun delivered a farewell speech, wishing all officers and soldiers of the first group of Chinese People's Volunteers withdrawing from Korea a safe journey home and good health, and wishing them new achievements in participating in the socialist construction of their motherland after returning home. Minister of Justice Xu Zhenshu and Major General Park Chang-lim, representing the leadership organs of the Korean Party, government, and army, presented two silk banners to this unit. Chairman Choi Yong-kun and others also presented Korean-Chinese Friendship Medals to representatives of the unit's officers.

Before the farewell rally, Chairman Choi Yong-kun and other leaders had cordial conversations with representatives of the Volunteer unit.

Vice-Chairman Kim Chang-man and others received the heroes and model workers of another Volunteer unit at their location and held a farewell meeting and presented flags and medals.

The commanders of these two Volunteer units delivered speeches of thanks at the meetings, thanking the Korean Workers' Party, the Republic's Government, and the entire Korean people for their long-term, meticulous care, love, and support for the Volunteers.

### 祝归国志愿军一路平安  朝鲜党政领导人分头举行送别会

1958-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    祝归国志愿军一路平安
    朝鲜党政领导人分头举行送别会
    据新华社平壤12日电　金日成首相和朝鲜党政领导人金光侠大将、李锺玉委员长、金会一交通相等，今天下午由志愿军司令员杨勇上将和我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光陪同，亲到中国人民志愿军首批撤出朝鲜部队某部看望和送别即将归国的志愿军官兵。贵宾们受到志愿军这个部队官兵们的热烈欢迎。
    金日成首相在到达志愿军这个部队的领导机关后，接见了这个部队的二级英雄李英财和解法正、曾经在朝鲜战争中击落击伤敌机七架的一等功臣张立春和被朝鲜人民称为“活罗盛教”的黄治富，并且同他们亲切握手，一起合影留念。
    接着，金日成首相等在这个部队举行了送别大会。
    会上，金日成首相作了送别讲话。他说，为了促进我国的和平统一，中国人民志愿军就要撤出我国了。同志们留下了在历史上永远是光芒万丈的丰功伟绩，把无限友爱的情谊留在我国人民的心坎里。朝鲜人民和中国人民之间，朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军之间，用鲜血凝结成的战斗的友谊团结，将是永恒不灭的，并将得到进一步的发展。
    金日成首相说，我可以向同志们表示：得到苏联、中华人民共和国和社会主义阵营各国人民的积极支持和声援的朝鲜人民，一定能够牢固地捍卫东方的和平和社会主义前哨。
    金日成首相在讲话中，希望志愿军官兵归国以后，向中国人民转达朝鲜人民对志愿军和全体中国人民的深厚的友爱和尊敬，并且勉励志愿军官兵归国后，在建设和保卫社会主义中作出更大的贡献。
    金日成首相的讲话，不断被暴风雨般的掌声所打断。
    接着，李锺玉委员长、金会一交通相代表朝鲜劳动党和国家机关，金光侠大将代表朝鲜人民军，向志愿军这个部队的领导机关授旗。金日成首相还授予志愿军这个部队官兵以祖国解放战争纪念章和朝中友谊纪念章，并且亲自把纪念章佩在这个部队的首长吴瑞山少将、谢福林大校、党万合大校等人的胸前。这时，会场上响起了“感谢金日成首相、朝鲜劳动党、共和国政府和朝鲜人民对我们的关怀！”“中朝人民鲜血结成的战斗友谊万岁！”的口号声和欢呼声。
    吴瑞山少将在会上致了答谢辞。
    新华社平壤12日电　和金日成首相一起来到志愿军首批撤出朝鲜部队的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长金昌满等，今天分别由志愿军政治委员王平上将、首批撤出朝鲜部队首长张天云中将陪同，到志愿军某两个部队送别。
    崔庸健委员长、金昌满副委员长等分别到达志愿军这两个部队的驻地时，受到军容整齐的志愿军官兵们夹道欢迎。
    下午二时半，崔庸健委员长在志愿军某部驻地举行欢送大会。会上，崔庸健委员长作了送别讲话，祝志愿军首批撤出朝鲜部队全体官兵在归国途中一路平安、身体健康，并祝他们在回国后参加祖国社会主义建设事业中取得新的成就。许贞琡司法相和朴昌林少将分别代表朝鲜党政军领导机关，授予这个部队两面锦旗。崔庸健委员长等还分别给部队军官代表佩上朝中友谊纪念章。
    欢送大会前，崔庸健委员长等领导人还和志愿军部队的代表进行亲切的交谈。
    金昌满副委员长等在志愿军另一个部队的驻地接见了这个部队的英雄和功臣，并且举行了送别大会和授旗、授纪念章。
    志愿军这两个部队的首长，都分别在大会上致感谢辞，感谢朝鲜劳动党、共和国政府和全体朝鲜人民对志愿军长期的无微不至的关怀、爱护和支援。
