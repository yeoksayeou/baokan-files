Title: Japanese Socialist Party Congress Resolution: Demands the Resignation of the Kishi Cabinet; Opposes U.S. Military Bases
Author:
Date: 1958-03-01
Page: 6

    Japanese Socialist Party Congress Resolution
    Demands the Resignation of the Kishi Cabinet; Opposes U.S. Military Bases
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 27th— The three-day 14th Congress of the Japanese Socialist Party concluded on the 26th. The congress passed a resolution calling on the Japanese people to launch a resolute struggle to demand the resignation of the Kishi Nobusuke cabinet. The resolution passed by the congress also expressed opposition to U.S. military bases and supported the movement to restore diplomatic relations with China.
    The congress elected forty members to the next Central Executive Committee. According to Kyodo News, 23 left-wing members were elected and 17 right-wing members were elected. The left-wing gained one additional member. The congress also passed the re-election of Suzuki Mosaburo as chairman and Asanuma Inejirō as secretary-general.
    The congress also passed action guidelines, organizational activity guidelines, foreign policy guidelines, guidelines for struggle in the Diet, and guidelines for overthrowing the Kishi Nobusuke cabinet, strengthening preparations for the general election and the struggle to overthrow the Kishi Nobusuke cabinet. The congress also passed a resolution on supporting the Okinawa Socialist Party in participating in the Okinawa Legislative Assembly election.

### 日本社会党代表大会决议  要求岸内阁辞职　反对美军事基地

1958-03-01
第6版()
专栏：

    日本社会党代表大会决议
    要求岸内阁辞职　反对美军事基地
    据新华社27日讯　东京消息：历时三天的日本社会党第十四次代表大会已在26日闭幕。大会通过决议，号召日本人民展开坚决的斗争，要求岸信介内阁辞职。大会通过的决议还表示反对美国的军事基地，并且支持同中国恢复外交关系的运动。
    大会选出了下一届中央执行委员四十人。据共同社报道，其中左派有二十三人当选，右派有十七人当选，左派增加了一人。大会并且通过了铃木茂三郎连任委员长，浅沼稻次郎连任书记长。
    大会还通过了运动方针、组织活动方针、外交政策方针、在国会中进行斗争的方针和打倒岸信介内阁的方针，加强了迎接大选的准备工作和打倒岸信介内阁的斗争。大会还通过了关于支援冲绳社会党参加冲绳立法院大选的决议。
