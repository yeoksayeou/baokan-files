Title: Mongolian Embassy in China Holds Song and Dance Reception

Author:
Date: 1958-03-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st – The Mongolian People's Republic Embassy in China held a song and dance reception this evening, entertaining people from all walks of life in the capital and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China.

At the reception, artists from the Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe performed wonderful song and dance programs. The Art Troupe had previously visited and performed in India and Vietnam and is now passing through Beijing on its way back home.

Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, and others attended the evening's reception.

### 蒙古驻华大使馆举行歌舞招待晚会

1958-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古驻华大使馆举行歌舞招待晚会
    据新华社21日讯　蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆今晚举行歌舞晚会，招待首都各界人士和各国驻华外交官员。
    晚会上，蒙古人民共和国艺术团的艺术家们演出了精采的歌舞节目。艺术团在这以前曾在印度、越南访问演出，现在途经北京回国。
    对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长夏衍等人出席了今天的晚会。
