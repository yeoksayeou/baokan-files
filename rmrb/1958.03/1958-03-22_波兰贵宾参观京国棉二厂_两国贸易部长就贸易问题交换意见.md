Title: Polish Guests Visit Beijing No. 2 State-owned Cotton Mill; Trade Ministers of Two Countries Exchange Views on Trade Issues
Author:
Date: 1958-03-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st: The Polish People's Republic Government Delegation, headed by Vice-Chairman Jaroszewicz, visited the State-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Mill this morning.

The distinguished guests walked through the factory's spacious workshops and visited each production process with great interest.

After the visit, Vice-Chairman Jaroszewicz said that the factory had left him with a very good impression and wished all the factory workers to overfulfill this year's production plan and create more wealth for the country.

Accompanying the distinguished guests on the visit were Qian Zhiguang, Vice Minister of Textile Industry, and Kirylok, the Polish Ambassador to China.

At 3:00 p.m., Yang Yingjie, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission, and Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, went to the guesthouse to introduce the basic situation of China's economic development to Vice-Chairman Jaroszewicz and other Polish guests.

At 1:30 p.m., Polish Minister of Foreign Trade, Trampczynski, and Vice Minister of Heavy Industry, Koi, and others called on Minister Ye Jizhuang of the Ministry of Foreign Trade to exchange preliminary views on trade issues between China and Poland. Vice Minister Li Zheren was present during the meeting.

### 波兰贵宾参观京国棉二厂  两国贸易部长就贸易问题交换意见

1958-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰贵宾参观京国棉二厂
    两国贸易部长就贸易问题交换意见
    据新华社21日讯　以雅罗谢维奇副主席为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团，今天上午参观了国营北京第二棉纺织厂。
    贵宾们走过工厂的一个个宽大的车间，很有兴趣地参观了各个生产工序。
    雅罗谢维奇副主席在参观以后说，这个工厂给他留下了极为良好的印象，祝全厂职工超额完成今年的生产计划，为国家创造更多的财富。
    陪同贵宾们参观的，有我国纺织工业部副部长钱之光和波兰驻我国大使基里洛克。
    下午三时，我国国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰和对外贸易部副部长李哲人前往宾馆，向雅罗谢维奇副主席和其他波兰贵宾介绍了我国经济发展的基本情况。
    下午一时半，波兰对外贸易部部长特朗普钦斯基和重工业部副部长科依等，到我国对外贸易部拜会了叶季壮部长，就中波两国的贸易问题初步交换了意见。拜会时，李哲人副部长在座。
