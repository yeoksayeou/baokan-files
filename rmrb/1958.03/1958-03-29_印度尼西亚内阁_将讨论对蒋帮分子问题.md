Title: Indonesian Cabinet to Discuss Issue of Chiang Kai-shek Clique Elements
Author:
Date: 1958-03-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 28th: Indonesian Minister of Information Sudibjo said on the 27th, in response to questions from reporters, that the Indonesian Cabinet meeting will discuss measures to deal with elements of the Taiwan Chiang Kai-shek clique. He also said that the Indonesian government will take measures within the scope of national vigilance.

Sudibjo made these remarks after a meeting of the National Security Council.

The Jakarta Garrison Command of Indonesia and the Attorney General jointly ordered on the 28th to prohibit elements of the Chiang Kai-shek clique from holding so-called "Youth Day" activities in Indonesia on the 29th.

### 印度尼西亚内阁  将讨论对蒋帮分子问题

1958-03-29
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚内阁
    将讨论对蒋帮分子问题
    据新华社雅加达28日电  印度尼西亚情报部长苏迪比约27日在回答新闻记者提出的问题时说，印度尼西亚内阁会议将讨论对付台湾蒋介石集团分子的措施问题。他还说，印度尼西亚政府将在民族警惕的范围内采取措施。
    苏迪比约是在国家安全委员会举行会议以后说这番话的。
    印度尼西亚雅加达卫戍司令部和检察总长28日联合下令禁止蒋介石集团分子29日在印度尼西亚举行所谓“青年日”的活动。
