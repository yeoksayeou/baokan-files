Title: Exchange Advanced Experiences, Accelerate the Leap Forward: Inner Mongolia Holds Advanced Producers' Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-03-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, March 18th. The Second Advanced Producers' Representative Conference of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region was held in Hohhot from the 10th to the 16th. This conference greatly encouraged cadres and workers of all ethnic groups both inside and outside the conference through telephone connections and radio broadcasts.

The more than 880 advanced producers (workers) representatives of all nationalities who attended the conference, in addition to discussing and revising the various planned targets of the autonomous region in the spirit of rectification, also summarized and exchanged various advanced experiences. During the conference, 24 representatives spoke at the radio conference, and 16 representatives delivered radio speeches to the entire region. Employees of factories, mines, enterprises, and departments in various parts of the autonomous region launched heated discussions on the leap forward targets and measures proposed by the conference, and they sent proposals, challenges, guarantees, and letters of determination to the conference via telephone, telegram, and letter.

The workers building Baotou expressed their determination to work hard for four years to basically complete the Baotou Iron and Steel Industrial Base. Other leap forward targets proposed by the conference were: local industry should surpass the total output value of agriculture, animal husbandry, and sideline industries during the Second Five-Year Plan period, and make 24 kinds of products reach domestic and international advanced levels in terms of quality and cost; the rural areas of the entire region should basically realize water conservancy within three years; basically realize agricultural mechanization and afforestation of the entire region within five years; grain yield should reach 400 jin per mu; pastoral areas should eliminate waterless grasslands within five years, vigorously develop feed bases, basically achieve settled nomadism, and increase the number of livestock to 40 million head.

Yang Zhilin, secretary of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, spoke at the meeting. He said: Popularizing the advanced levels of production and work in all aspects and continuously improving them to higher levels, and putting these proposals, challenges, and guarantees into practice, is the goal of the advanced producers' movement for a certain period in the future.

### 交流先进经验　加快跃进速度  内蒙古举行先进生产者代表会

1958-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    交流先进经验　加快跃进速度
    内蒙古举行先进生产者代表会
    据新华社呼和浩特18日电  内蒙古自治区第二届先进生产者代表大会，从10日到16日在呼和浩特市举行。这次大会通过电话联系和广播大会等形式，使会内外各族职工都受到很大鼓舞。
    参加大会的八百八十多名各民族先进生产者（工作者）代表，除以整风精神讨论和修订了自治区各项计划指标外，还总结交流了各种先进经验。大会期间，有二十四名代表在广播大会上发了言，有十六名代表向全区发表了广播讲话。自治区各地厂矿企业和部门的职工，对大会提出的跃进指标和措施展开了热烈讨论，并纷纷用电话、电报和书信向大会提出倡议书、挑战书、保证书和决心书。
    建设包头的职工们表示决心苦干四年，基本建成包头钢铁工业基地。大会提出的其他跃进指标是：地方工业要在第二个五年计划期间赶过农牧副业总产值，并使二十四种产品在质量和成本上，赶上国内和国际先进水平；全区农村要在三年内基本实现水利化；五年内基本实现农业机械化和全区绿化；粮食每亩达到四百斤；牧业区五年内消灭无水草原，大力发展饲料基地，基本实现定居游牧，使牲畜头数达到四千万头。
    中共内蒙古自治区委员会书记杨植霖在会上讲了话。他说：把各方面先进的生产和工作水平推广普及，并且不断地提高到更高的水平，把这些倡议书、挑战书和保证书付诸实现，就是今后一定时期内先进生产者运动的奋斗目标。
