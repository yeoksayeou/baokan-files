Title: Corn Yield of 2,500 Jin Per Mu

Author: 
Date: 1958-03-04
Page: 2

Column: Agricultural High Yield Aspirations

    Editor's Note: On the agricultural, forestry, and animal husbandry production fronts, what new high-yield records have the working people created? We plan to introduce some of these in this column to help everyone understand the new achievements in our country's production development.

    The Le Lou Production Team of the Le Lou Agricultural Cooperative in Paosha Township, Cheng County, Gansu Province, planted a total of 69.25 mu of corn in 1957. Of this, 11.12 mu of corn averaged 1,236.3 jin per mu, an increase of 48.7% over 1956. Among them, the highest yielding land, 1.67 mu, averaged 2,520 jin per mu, exceeding the original plan by 26%. In 1956, this cooperative created a national record of 1,975 jin per mu on just over one mu of corn.

    The natural conditions of this cooperative are not superior. It is located in the Chuanchuan River valley area, more than 950 meters above sea level, with a frost-free period of about 190 days throughout the year.

    The main reasons they have continuously achieved high corn yields are: the use of the "Golden Queen" improved variety, the implementation of deep plowing and early plowing, reasonable close planting (planting about 3,100 plants per mu), and artificial pollination.

### 玉米亩产二千五百斤

1958-03-04
第2版()
专栏：农业高产志

    玉米亩产二千五百斤
    编者按：在农、林、牧生产战线上，劳动人民创造了哪些高产新纪录？我们准备在这一栏里陆续作一些介绍，帮助大家了解我国生产发展的新成就。
    甘肃省成县抛沙乡乐楼农业社乐楼生产队，1957年共种植玉米六十九亩二分五厘，有十一亩一分二厘地玉米每亩平均产一千二百三十六点三斤，比1956年增加48.7％。其中最高丰产地一亩六分七厘，平均亩产二千五百二十斤，超过原计划的26％。这个社1956年就有一亩多玉米创造了亩产一千九百七十五斤的全国纪录。
    这个社的自然条件并不优越。位于沿川河谷地区，海拔九百五十公尺以上，全年无霜期一百九十天左右。
    他们连续获得玉米高额丰产的主要原因是：采用“金皇后”良种，实行深耕早耕、合理密植（每亩种三千一百株左右），和人工授粉等。
