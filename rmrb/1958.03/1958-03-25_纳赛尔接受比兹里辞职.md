Title: Nasser Accepts Bizri's Resignation
Author:
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 23rd: According to Cairo newspapers today, the President of the United Arab Republic, Nasser, has accepted the resignation of Bizri, the commander of the First Army of the United Arab Republic. Before the establishment of the United Arab Republic, Bizri was the commander-in-chief of the Syrian armed forces. He was appointed by President Nasser as the commander of the First Army on March 12th.

The reason for Bizri's resignation has not been announced.

Bizri came to Cairo on March 20th to participate in the rally organized by the National Union on that day to celebrate the establishment of the United Arab Republic and the Arab Federation, and to listen to Nasser's speech about his trip to Syria.

### 纳赛尔接受比兹里辞职

1958-03-25
第5版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔接受比兹里辞职
    新华社开罗23日电　据开罗报纸今天报道，阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔已经接受阿拉伯联合共和国第一军军长比兹里的辞职。在阿拉伯联合共和国成立以前，比兹里是叙利亚武装部队的总司令，他是在3月12日被纳赛尔总统任命为第一军军长的。
    比兹里辞职的原因没有宣布。
    比兹里是在3月20日来到开罗，参加在当天由民族联盟组织的庆祝阿拉伯联合共和国和阿拉伯联合邦的成立大会，听取纳赛尔关于他的叙利亚之行的讲演的。
