Title: Yemen Accuses British Troops of Invading; Omani Commandos Resist Aggressors on All Fronts
Author:
Date: 1958-03-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 28th: New York dispatch: Yemen's Permanent Representative to the United Nations, Kamel Abdul Rahim, in a letter delivered to the President of the United Nations Security Council, Sobolev, and the United Nations Secretary-General, Hammarskjöld, on the 27th, accused Britain of invading the Yemeni border. The Yemeni representative warned Britain in the letter that if it did not immediately cease its aggression, Yemen would request the Security Council to take action.

    The Yemeni representative accused Britain in the letter of carrying out planned attacks on the Yemeni border. He said that British troops attacked nine regions on the Yemeni border from January 21st to February 20th, causing serious casualties and extensive property damage, and constituting a serious threat to peace and security in the region.
    The letter pointed out that Britain's invasion of the Yemeni border was an attempt to establish military bases there and seize the oil there.
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 27th: The Omani Imam's office in Cairo announced today that a group of Omani commandos unexpectedly attacked British troops near Green Mountain the other night, killing fifteen British soldiers. Omani nationalists also seized a large number of weapons.
    The office also said that fighting between Omani nationalists and British aggressors is continuing on all fronts.

### 也门控诉英军犯境  阿曼突击队全线抗击侵略军

1958-03-01
第6版()
专栏：

    也门控诉英军犯境
    阿曼突击队全线抗击侵略军
    新华社28日讯　纽约消息：也门常驻联合国代表卡姆尔·阿卜杜勒·拉希姆27日在交给联合国安全理事会主席索波列夫和联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德的一封信中控诉英国侵略也门边境。也门代表在信里警告英国：假如不立即停止侵略，也门将要求安全理事会采取行动。
    也门代表在信里指责英国对也门边境进行有计划的进攻。他说，英国军队在1月21日到2月20日攻击了也门边境上的九个地区，造成严重的死伤和大量的财产损失，并且构成了对这个地区的和平和安全的严重威胁。
    信中指出英国侵略也门边境是企图在那里建立军事基地和攫取那里的石油。
    新华社开罗27日电　阿曼教长驻开罗办事处今天宣布，一批阿曼突击队在最近一个晚上出其不意地袭击绿山附近的英国部队，打死了英军十五名。阿曼民族主义者还夺取了大量武器。
    办事处还说，阿曼民族主义者和英国侵略军的战斗正在所有战线上继续进行。
