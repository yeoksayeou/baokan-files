Title: Doubling the Effort with Half the Time
Author:
Date: 1958-03-01
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 27th: Liu Jianhua, a national railway advanced producer and lathe operator from the wheel workshop of Zhuzhou Locomotive and Rolling Stock Repair Factory, recently formulated a plan to complete the Second Five-Year Plan one and a half years ahead of schedule, after completing the First Five-Year Plan two and a half years ahead of schedule.
    During the First Five-Year Plan, Communist Party member Liu Jianhua completed seven and a half years' worth of work, while also maintaining eight consecutive years without producing any defective products or mechanical damage accidents.
    Liu Jianhua's Second Five-Year Plan also guarantees no mechanical damage accidents or defective products within five years.

### 时半功倍

1958-03-01
第3版()
专栏：

    时半功倍
    据新华社长沙27日电  全国铁路先进生产者——株洲机车车辆修理厂车轮车间旋工刘建华，在提前两年半完成第一个五年计划以后，最近订出提前一年半完成第二个五年计划的规划。
    共产党员刘建华在第一个五年计划期间共完成七年半的工作量，同时连续八年没有出过废品，没有出过械损事故。
    刘建华订出的第二个五年计划的规划中还保证五年内不出械损事故和废品。
