Title: Ceylon's Governor-General and Prime Minister Receive Chinese Merchandise Exhibition Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, March 8th: Ceylon's Governor-General, Sir Oliver Goonetilleke, received Ma Yimin, the head of the Chinese Merchandise Exhibition Delegation, and Yu Ping, the deputy head, this morning. The Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon, Zhang Canming, was also present at the meeting.
    During the reception, Governor-General Goonetilleke said that he was proud of the friendship between Ceylon and China. He expressed his hope to further expand trade between the two countries.

### 锡兰总督和总理接见我商品展览团

1958-03-10
第6版()
专栏：

    锡兰总督和总理接见我商品展览团
    据新华社科伦坡8日电　锡兰总督古?狄莱克今天上午接见了中国商品展览会代表团团长马一民和副团长余萍。接见时，中国驻锡兰大使张灿明也在场。
