    A Poem and a Painting
    Gao Mi

    Poem: Dong Xiwen's Painting

    Kites fly and butterflies dance with joy,
    Near and far, controlled by a single thread.
    Such a time, such a place,
    The spring breeze sends you soaring into the sky. (Attached image)
