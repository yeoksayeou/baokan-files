Title: The Chinese People's Delegation to Korea Concludes its慰问 (consolation) visit to the Kaesong area

Author: 
Date: 1958-03-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, March 21st – The Chinese People's Delegation to Korea has concluded its work in the Kaesong area today.

Since arriving in Kaesong on the 15th, the delegation has, through慰问 (consolation) meetings and visits, extended their regards to the Korean People's Army and Chinese People's Volunteer Army officers and soldiers working at the Military Armistice Commission, Kaesong municipal party and government cadres, and the local Korean people. At the慰问 (consolation) meeting, Wang Yifu, the head of the delegation, on behalf of the Chinese people, expressed deep gratitude to the representatives of the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission for their significant contributions to maintaining the Korean Armistice Agreement, and to the people of Kaesong for their tremendous help to the Volunteer Army troops over the years. He also presented silk banners to the Korean-Chinese side's delegation and the Kaesong People's Committee.

On the 16th, representatives of the delegation, accompanied by Major General Jiang Shanghao, the chief representative of the Korean-Chinese side, and the heads of the Kaesong municipal party and government organs, laid wreaths at the cemeteries of the martyrs of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

The Chinese People's Liberation Army Naval Political Department Song and Dance Troupe, which accompanied the delegation, has been giving慰问 (consolation) performances in the Kaesong area for several consecutive days, and has been warmly welcomed by more than 40,000 spectators.

### 中国人民赴朝慰问团总团  结束开城地区的慰问

1958-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民赴朝慰问团总团
    结束开城地区的慰问
    新华社开城21日电　中国人民赴朝慰问团总团在开城地区的慰问工作，今天全部结束。
    慰问团总团于15日到达开城以来，先后通过慰问大会和访问等活动，慰问了在军事停战委员会工作的朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军官兵，开城市党政干部，以及当地的朝鲜人民。在慰问大会上，王一夫总团长代表中国人民，分别向为维护朝鲜停战协定作出重大贡献的军事停战委员会朝中方面代表和几年来给予志愿军部队以巨大帮助的开城人民，表示了深切的感谢，并分别向朝中方面代表团和开城市人民委员会献了锦旗。
    16日，慰问团代表在朝中方面首席代表姜尚昊少将和开城市党政机关负责人陪同下，分别到朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军烈士陵园献了花圈。
    随同慰问团前来的中国人民解放军海军政治部文工团，连日在开城地区进行慰问演出，受到四万多观众的热烈欢迎。
