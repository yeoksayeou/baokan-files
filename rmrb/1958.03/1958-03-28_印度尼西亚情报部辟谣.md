Title: Indonesian Ministry of Information Denies Rumors

Author: 
Date: 1958-03-28
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 27th: The Indonesian Ministry of Information on the evening of March 26th denounced the rebels for spreading rumors that Soviet ships had brought weapons to Indonesia. The Ministry of Information broadcast a statement quoting Minister of Maritime Affairs Nazir as saying that the ten Soviet ships purchased by the government arrived in Indonesia last week empty.
    The statement also said that Nazir had stated that these ships would not be used to transport troops in the fight against the rebels, but only to replace the previous Dutch ships for inter-island shipping.

### 印度尼西亚情报部辟谣

1958-03-28
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚情报部辟谣
    新华社雅加达27日电　印度尼西亚情报部3月26日晚指责叛乱分子制造苏联船只给印度尼西亚运来武器的谣言。情报部广播一项声明引述海运部长纳齐尔的话说，政府所购买的十艘苏联船只上星期到达印度尼西亚时是空着的。
    声明还说，纳齐尔已经说过，不会在对叛军作战中使用这些船只来运兵，而只是用来代替以前的荷兰船只从事岛际航运。
