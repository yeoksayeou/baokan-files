    Performing Meritorious Service for Socialism

    "Everyone strives to be a meritorious worker in building socialism!" This is the bold statement of 150,000 people in the porcelain capital, and it is also the bold statement of 600 million people.

    In the next fifteen years, the task of the Chinese people is to build a poor and blank country into a strong socialist country with modern industry, modern agriculture, and modern science and culture. This is a very arduous task, and everyone has a part to play. To be a meritorious worker in building socialism, one must complete tasks more, faster, better, and more economically than others. Carrying out the socialist meritorious service movement will make people more concerned about collective undertakings, correct their attitude towards labor, and regard the amount of contribution to socialism as the dividing line between glory and shame. Some porcelain factory workers in Jingdezhen have proposed in the meritorious service movement: "Rather not have wages, but also ensure product quality!" This is a manifestation of the improvement of socialist consciousness.

    We wish the 150,000 people of the porcelain capital to become meritorious workers in building socialism! May the people of the whole country perform meritorious service for socialism!

### 为社会主义立功

1958-03-05
第3版()
专栏：编后随笔

    为社会主义立功
    “人人争做建设社会主义的功臣！”这是十五万瓷都人民的豪语，也是六亿人民的豪语。
    在今后十五年内，我国人民的任务，就是把又穷又白的国家建设成具有现代工业、现代农业和现代科学文化的社会主义强国。这是个非常艰巨的任务，每一个人都有一份。要做建设社会主义的功臣，就要比别人多、快、好、省地完成任务。开展社会主义立功运动，将使人们更加关心集体事业，端正劳动态度，把对社会主义的贡献多寡作为光荣和耻辱的分界。景德镇有些瓷厂工人在立功运动中提出：“宁可不要工资，也要保证产品质量！”就是社会主义觉悟提高的表现。
    祝瓷都十五万人民都成为建设社会主义的功臣！愿全国人民都为社会主义立功！
