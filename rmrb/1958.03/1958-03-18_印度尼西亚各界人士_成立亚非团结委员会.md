Title: Indonesian Figures from All Walks of Life Establish Asian-African Solidarity Committee
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-18
Page: 5

    Indonesian Figures from All Walks of Life
    Establish Asian-African Solidarity Committee
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 17th: According to a report in today's Indonesian "People's Daily," people of various political tendencies in Indonesia established the Indonesian Asian-African Solidarity Committee on March 14th.
    It is reported that this committee includes figures from many political parties and the parliament. The chairman of the committee is Anwar Tjokroaminoto, chairman of the Islamic League Party, the vice chairman is Siradjuddin Abbas, chairman of the Perti Islamic Party, and the members include Supeni of the Indonesian Nationalist Party, who is also the vice chairman of the Parliamentary Foreign Affairs Committee, Wahib Wahab of the Islamic Teachers Association, and Yudo of the Indonesian Communist Party.

### 印度尼西亚各界人士  成立亚非团结委员会

1958-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚各界人士
    成立亚非团结委员会
    据新华社雅加达17日电  据印度尼西亚“人民日报”今天报道，印度尼西亚各种不同政治倾向的人士，已经在3月14日成立了印度尼西亚亚非团结委员会。
    据悉，这个委员会包括许多政党和国会等方面人士。委员会的主席是伊斯兰联盟党主席安瓦尔·佐克罗阿米诺托，副主席是白尔蒂伊斯兰党主席西拉米汀·阿巴斯，成员中有国会外交委员会副主席、印度尼西亚国民党的苏佩妮，伊斯兰教师联合会的瓦希布·瓦哈布和印度尼西亚共产党的约多。
