Small Data

When did the Chinese People's Volunteers enter Korea?
	October 25, 1950.
When did the Korean War end?
	July 27, 1953.
What great achievements did the Chinese and Korean people achieve?
	From June 25, 1950, to the end of the war, the Korean and Chinese People's Armies annihilated, wounded, and captured more than 1,090,000 enemy troops, including more than 390,000 American soldiers. They shot down and damaged more than 12,200 enemy aircraft and sank and damaged 257 enemy warships of various types. As for the destruction and capture of other enemy combat materials, it is impossible to count. During this period, the enemy transported more than 73 million tons of combat materials to Korea. If each freight car carried 30 tons, then 73 million tons of materials could fill 2.43 million freight cars. These freight cars, lined up, would stretch for 26,730 kilometers! The direct war cost exceeded 20 billion US dollars.
How many heroes, models, and meritorious individuals emerged from the Chinese People's Volunteers?
	From October 25, 1950, to the end of August 1953, more than 300,000 third-class or higher meritorious individuals and more than 6,100 units with third-class or higher collective merits emerged from the Chinese People's Volunteers. Among them, there were two special-class heroes and special-class meritorious individuals, namely Huang Jiguang and Yang Gensi, who are most familiar to us; forty-six first-class heroes and special-class meritorious individuals; four first-class models and special-class meritorious individuals; four first-class heroes and first-class meritorious individuals; seventy-two second-class heroes and special-class meritorious individuals; four second-class models and special-class meritorious individuals; one hundred and five second-class heroes and first-class meritorious individuals; forty-five second-class models and first-class meritorious individuals; eighty-nine special-class meritorious individuals. Among the more than 300,000 heroes, models, and meritorious individuals of the Chinese People's Volunteers, 231,190 people received various medals and awards from the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 小资料

1958-03-17
第2版()
专栏：小资料

    小资料
    志愿军何时出国？
    1950年10月25日。
    朝鲜何时停战？
    1953年7月27日。
    中朝人民取得多大战果？
    从1950年6月25日算起，到停战时止，朝中人民军队共毙、伤、俘敌军一百零九万多人，其中有美军三十九万多人。击落击伤敌机一万二千二百多架，击沉击伤敌军各种舰艇二百五十七艘。至于击毁和缴获敌军其他各种作战物资，就无法统计了。在这一期间内，敌人运往朝鲜的作战物资达七千三百万吨以上，若以每个车皮装三十吨计算，七千三百万吨物资就可装二百四十三万个车皮。这些车皮排起来，长达两万六千七百三十公里！直接战费的消耗达二百亿美元以上。
    志愿军出了多少英雄、模范和功臣？
    从1950年10月25日起，到1953年8月止，志愿军中共涌现三等以上功臣三十多万名，集体立三等功以上的单位六千一百多个。其中，特级英雄、特等功臣两名，这就是我们最熟悉的黄继光和杨根思；一级英雄、特等功臣四十六名；一级模范、特等功臣四名；一级英雄、一等功臣四名；二级英雄、特等功臣七十二名；二级模范、特等功臣四名；二级英雄、一等功臣一百零五名；二级模范、一等功臣四十五名；特等功臣八十九名。在三十多万志愿军的英雄、模范和功臣中，有二十三万一千一百九十人，获得了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会颁发的各种勋章和奖章。
