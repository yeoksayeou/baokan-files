Title: Congratulations on the First Anniversary of Ghana's Independence, Premier Zhou Cables Prime Minister Nkrumah
Author:
Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Ghanaian Prime Minister Kwame Nkrumah on the 4th, congratulating him on the first anniversary of Ghana's independence. The telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Kwame Nkrumah, Prime Minister of Ghana:

    On the occasion of the first anniversary of Ghana's independence, I would like to express my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of your country on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China.

    In the past year, the people of Ghana have continuously achieved new victories in the cause of consolidating national independence and safeguarding national sovereignty. The Chinese people are very happy about the victories achieved by the people of Ghana.

    Since your country's independence, mutual understanding and friendly relations between China and Ghana have been enhanced. I wish the friendly relations between China and Ghana will develop day by day. I wish Your Excellency and the people of Ghana continuous new achievements in the cause of building your beautiful country and safeguarding world peace.

                            Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                            March 4, 1958, in Beijing

### 祝贺加纳独立一周年  周总理电恩克鲁玛总理

1958-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺加纳独立一周年
    周总理电恩克鲁玛总理
    新华社5日讯　周恩来总理4日致电加纳总理克瓦米·恩克鲁玛，祝贺加纳独立一周年。电文如下：加纳总理克瓦米·恩克鲁玛阁下阁下：
    值此加纳独立一周年之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向阁下和贵国人民表示热烈的祝贺。
    一年来，加纳人民在巩固民族独立、维护国家主权的事业中不断取得新的胜利，中国人民对加纳人民所取得的胜利感到非常高兴。
    自从贵国独立以来，中国和加纳之间的相互了解和友好关系得到了增进。祝中加两国之间的这种友好关系日益发展。祝阁下和加纳人民在建设自己美好国家和维护世界和平的事业中不断地取得新的成就。
                            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                        一九五八年三月四日于北京
