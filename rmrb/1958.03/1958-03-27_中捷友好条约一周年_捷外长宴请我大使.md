Title: China-Czechoslovakia Friendship Treaty Anniversary: Czech Foreign Minister Hosts Banquet for Chinese Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1958-03-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 26th: To celebrate the first anniversary of the signing of the China-Czechoslovakia Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation, Czechoslovak Foreign Minister David held a luncheon at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs today to entertain Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Cao Ying and the embassy's principal diplomatic personnel.

### 中捷友好条约一周年  捷外长宴请我大使

1958-03-27
第5版()
专栏：

    中捷友好条约一周年
    捷外长宴请我大使
    据新华社布拉格26日电为庆祝中捷友好合作条约签订一周年，捷克斯洛伐克外交部长戴维今天在外交部举行午宴，招待中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛和使馆主要外交人员。
