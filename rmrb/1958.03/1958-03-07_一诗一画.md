Title: A Poem and a Painting
Author: Deng Tuo, Hua Junwu
Date: 1958-03-07
Page: 8

    A Poem and a Painting
    Deng Tuo's Poem
    Hua Junwu's Painting
    Beating drums on Golden Mountain celebrate the victory, sending letters of challenge for a three-year contest.
    Eager to see whose art is more advanced, a high tide sweeping the four seas connects to the sky. (Attached image)
