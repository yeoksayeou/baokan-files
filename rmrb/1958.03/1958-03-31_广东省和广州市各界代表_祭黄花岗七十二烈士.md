Title: Representatives from Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City Pay Respects to the Seventy-Two Martyrs of Huanghuagang
Author:
Date: 1958-03-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 29th: Today is the anniversary of the martyrdom of the Seventy-Two Martyrs of Huanghuagang. Chen Ru-tang, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, Li Lang-ru, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou City, Zhang Bo-cun, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Yao Yu-ping, Director of the Guangdong Provincial Office of Councillors, and dozens of representatives from various circles in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, went to Huanghuagang to lay flowers and pay their respects.

### 广东省和广州市各界代表  祭黄花岗七十二烈士

1958-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    广东省和广州市各界代表
    祭黄花岗七十二烈士
    新华社广州29日电　今天是黄花岗七十二烈士殉难纪念日。广东省副省长陈汝棠、广州市副市长李朗如、政协广东省委员会副主席张?村、广东省参事室主任姚雨平及广东省、广州市各界代表数十人，前往黄花岗献花致祭。
