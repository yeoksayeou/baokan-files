Western Press Clippings

    Cannot Deceive People
    The achievements of Russia and the testimony of American scientists regarding Russia's scientific power stand in stark contrast to the subjective conjectures of the House Un-American Activities Committee. This committee still insists that every Russian achievement is obtained by "stealing" American secrets. …
    But everyone is eager to know, if Russia's scientific achievements mainly rely on theft, then how can they defeat us in the space race and be ahead of us in the development of intercontinental missiles!
      — American "Milwaukee Journal"

    Who is Afraid of Peaceful Competition?
    The real threat to American (and Western) superiority is that Russia not only has some great scientists, mathematicians, and engineers to guide the direction but also has a large number of ordinary technicians and engineers to develop the genius creations of these scientists.
　　——Canadian “Toronto Star”

### 西方报刊拾零

1958-03-08
第6版()
专栏：西方报刊拾零

    西方报刊拾零
    骗不了人
    俄国的成就以及美国科学家对俄国科学力量的作证，都同众议院“非美活动调查委员会”的主观臆测恰恰相反。这个委员会现在还继续认为，俄国的每一件成就，都是“偷窃”美国的秘密取得的。……
    但是，大家都很想知道，如果俄国的科学成就主要依靠偷窃，那么，他们怎能在空间竞赛中击败我们，并且在发展洲际导弹方面也走在我们前面！
      ——美国“密尔瓦基日报”
    谁害怕和平竞赛？
    对美国（和西方）优势真正的威胁是：俄国不仅有一些伟大的科学家、数学家和工程师来指导方向，并且还有大量普通的技术人员和工程师把这些科学家的天才创造加以发挥。　——加拿大“多伦多星报”
