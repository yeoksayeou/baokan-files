Title: Our Higher Education Delegation Leaves Berlin, Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1958-03-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: After a twelve-day study visit to the German Democratic Republic, the Chinese Higher Education Delegation, headed by Minister of Education Yang Xiufeng, left Berlin by plane on the 8th to return home.

The delegation visited the German Democratic Republic to implement the 1958 cultural cooperation plan of the Sino-German Cultural Agreement.

On its way back to China, the Chinese Higher Education Delegation arrived in Moscow by plane on the 8th.

### 我高等教育考察团离柏林抵莫斯科

1958-03-10
第6版()
专栏：

    我高等教育考察团离柏林抵莫斯科
    据新华社讯　以教育部长杨秀峰为首的中国高等教育考察团在民主德国进行了十二天的考察访问之后，8日乘飞机离柏林回国。
    考察团是为执行中德文化协定1958年文化合作计划而来民主德国访问的。
    中国高等教育考察团在返国途中8日乘飞机到达莫斯科。
