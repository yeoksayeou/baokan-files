Author: Xi Yi
Date: 1958-03-23
Page: 4

Did you know?
    Where on Earth is the same object lightest? Of course, it is on the equator.
    A train weighing sixteen hundred tons in Moscow, when it arrives in Arkhangelsk, will lose one hundred and sixty kilograms, and the same when it arrives in Odessa.
    Who stole the train's weight? The thief is mainly centrifugal force. Due to centrifugal force, the weight of an object at the equator is reduced by 1/290 compared to the Earth's poles. Moreover, because the Earth's equatorial part is slightly bulging and further away from the Earth's center, the further away from the equator, the less weight is lost. Therefore, the total weight of an object on the equator is reduced by 1/200 compared to its weight at the poles. (Xi Yi)

### 你知道吗？
熙译
1958-03-23
第4版()
专栏：医生的答复

    你知道吗？
    同样的物体，在地球上什么地方为最轻呢？当然是在赤道上。
    在莫斯科一千六百吨重的列车，开到了阿尔汉格尔斯克，就减轻了一百六十公斤，开到敖德萨也是这样。
    谁偷走了这列车的重量呢？偷走它的，主要是离心力。由于离心力，使物体在赤道的重量比在地球的两极减轻二百九十分之一。又因为地球的赤道部分稍为膨胀，离地球中心比较远一些，所以离赤道越远，重量减轻得越少。因此，物体在赤道上的重量总计比在两极的重量减轻二百分之一。　（熙译）
