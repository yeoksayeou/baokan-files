Title: Drilling the Three Gorges
Author: Li Jiuling
Date: 1958-03-27
Page: 3

    Drilling the Three Gorges
    The Three Gorges Geological Team of the Ministry of Geology is conducting offshore drilling in the Three Gorges to collect geological data for the comprehensive development of the Yangtze River. This is a drilling ship drilling near Nanjin Pass in Yichang.　Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Jiuling photo

### 钻探三峡
李九龄
1958-03-27
第3版()
专栏：

    钻探三峡
    地质部三峡地质队正在三峡进行水上钻探，为综合开发长江收集地质资料。这是钻探船正在宜昌南津关附近钻探。　新华社记者　李九龄摄
