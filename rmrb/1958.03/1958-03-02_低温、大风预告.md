Title: Low Temperature and Strong Wind Forecast
Author:
Date: 1958-03-02
Page: 2

    Low Temperature and Strong Wind Forecast
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st - The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a low temperature and strong wind forecast at 8 PM on the 1st, stating:
    Affected by cold air, it is expected that the minimum temperature in the Huai River basin and southern Jiangsu will drop to -5 to -8 degrees Celsius on the morning of the 3rd; southern Hubei, northern Hunan, northern Jiangxi, southern Anhui, and northern Zhejiang will drop to -1 to -4 degrees Celsius; southern Zhejiang, southern Hunan, and southern Jiangxi will drop to around 0 degrees Celsius. In the next 24 hours, there will be northerly winds of level 5 or 6 in the Huai River basin and the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River; the southern Yellow Sea and the East China Sea will have northerly winds of level 6 or 7.

### 低温、大风预告

1958-03-02
第2版()
专栏：气象台

    低温、大风预告
    新华社1日讯　中央气象科学研究所1日下午八时发布低温和大风预告说：
    受冷空气影响，预计3日早晨淮河流域、江苏南部的最低气温将下降到摄氏零下五度到零下八度；湖北南部、湖南北部、江西北部、安徽南部、浙江北部将下降到摄氏零下一度到零下四度；浙江南部、湖南南部、江西南部将下降到摄氏零度左右。未来二十四小时内，淮河流域、长江流域中下游将有五、六级的偏北风，黄海南部和东海将有六、七级的偏北风。
