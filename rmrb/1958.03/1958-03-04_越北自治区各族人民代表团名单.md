Title: List of Delegates of the People's Representative Assembly of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1958-03-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, March 3rd. List of Delegates of the People's Representative Assembly of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

    Head of Delegation: Chu Wenjin (Nong nationality, member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Secretary of the Party Committee of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region, Chairman of the Administrative Committee of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region)

    Deputy Head of Delegation: Phan Van Tinh (Kinh nationality, member of the Party Committee of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region, President of the Procuratorate of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region)

    Yang Gonghuo (Tu nationality, representative of the People's Representative Assembly of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region)

    Members: Chao Wenjing (Man nationality, Director of the Supply and Marketing Cooperative of Bac Kan Province)

    Pan Zhihan (Man nationality, member of the Administrative Committee of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region)

    Nguyen Khanh (Kinh nationality, Secretary to the Chairman of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region)

    Chao Thi Lan (female, Man nationality, cadre of the Women's Federation of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region)

    Bi Zhenbian (Nong nationality, Chairman of the Administrative Committee of Lang Son Province)

    Hoang Hong Cao (Man nationality, representative of the People's Representative Assembly of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region)

    Yang Gao Li (Hmong nationality, representative of the People's Representative Assembly of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region, member of the Administrative Committee of Cao Bang Province)

    Nong Guozhen (Tu nationality, poet, Vice Chairman of the Vietnam National Federation of Literary and Art Associations)

    Qiu Shi Tao (female, Chinese nationality, cadre of the Women's Federation of Bac Kan Province)

    Ho Thi Kim (female, Nong nationality, member of the Administrative Committee of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region, member of the Vietnam Central Women's Federation)

    Bui Kim Thanh (female, Kinh nationality, Chairman of the Women's Federation of Thai Nguyen Province)

    Le Dinh Nham (Tu nationality, President of the People's Court of Thai Nguyen Province)

    Phuong Thi Van Anh (female, Tu nationality, Chairman of the Women's Federation of Hoa An County, Cao Bang Province)

    Hoang Tuan Son (Tu nationality, President of the Procuratorate of Cao Bang Province)

    La Van Lien (Gaolan nationality, member of the Administrative Committee of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region)

    Ly Van Lu (Tu nationality, representative of the Tu nationality of Lang Son Province)

    Bui Xuan Su (Kinh nationality, translator)
