Title: To Further Strengthen the Friendly Cooperation Between China and Poland - Polish Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-21
Page: 1
    
    Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: Polish People's Republic Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Jaroszewicz, said upon arriving in Beijing this afternoon that the Polish government delegation will jointly discuss some economic issues of mutual concern with the Chinese government in Beijing, and is confident that this friendly meeting will help expand and deepen the economic and technological cooperation between the two countries based on mutual benefit, and promote the rapid development of the two countries' economies.
    The delegation arrived in Beijing at 3:30 this afternoon by jet plane from Moscow. In his speech at the airport, Deputy Chairman Jaroszewicz also said that our talks will help further strengthen the sincere friendship and brotherly relations. It is this sincere friendship and brotherly relationship that connects Poland and China and unites all countries of the great socialist camp.
    Deputy Chairman Jaroszewicz said that it is difficult to express in a few words the great friendly and brotherly feelings of the Polish workers, peasants, and intellectuals towards the Chinese people, the Chinese Communist Party, and the Chinese government. The newly built Beijing East Suburb Airport was flying the national flags of China and Poland today. When the jet plane carrying the Polish government delegation slowly landed, Vice Premiers Chen Yi, He Long, Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Guo Moruo, Deputy Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and others who were waiting for the distinguished guests at the airport, stepped forward to warmly shake hands with the guests in front of the plane.
    Before Deputy Chairman Jaroszewicz's speech, Vice Premier Chen Yi, in his speech, warmly welcomed the Polish guests who had come from afar on behalf of the Chinese government and people. He said that although China and Poland are separated by thousands of mountains and rivers, the common goals of struggle closely link the people of our two countries together. In recent years, the friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries have been continuously consolidated and developed. Vice Premier Chen Yi said that this friendly visit by the Polish government delegation will further strengthen the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Poland, thereby promoting the socialist construction of our two countries.
    Before the speeches of the Deputy Chairman and Vice Premier, the band played the national anthems of China and Poland, and Deputy Chairman Jaroszewicz, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, reviewed the guard of honor. Young Pioneers of the capital presented flowers to the Polish guests. Many heads of various departments of our government, people's organizations, and democratic parties also came to the airport to welcome the guests. The Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilok, and embassy staff also came to the airport to welcome them.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi, He Long, and Nie Rongzhen received all the distinguished guests of the Polish People's Republic government delegation led by Deputy Chairman Jaroszewicz this evening.
    Present were heads of relevant departments Ye Jizhuang, Yang Yingjie, Cao Xiangren, Zeng Yongquan, Li Zheren, etc., and Polish Ambassador to China Kirilok.
    After the meeting, the hosts held a banquet to welcome the guests. (Attached picture)

### 进一步加强中波两国友好合作  波兰政府代表团到达北京

1958-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    进一步加强中波两国友好合作
    波兰政府代表团到达北京
    陈毅、贺龙、聂荣臻副总理接见波兰贵宾
    据新华社20日讯　波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇在今天下午到达北京时说，波兰政府代表团将在北京同中国政府共同讨论双方相互关心的一些经济问题，并且深信这次友好会谈将有助于两国基于互利基础的经济和技术合作的扩大与加深，促进两国经济的迅速发展。
    代表团今天下午三时半乘喷气式专机由莫斯科到达北京。雅罗谢维奇副主席在机场上发表的讲话中还说，我们的会谈会有助于真诚的友谊和兄弟般关系的进一步加强。也就是这种真诚友谊和兄弟般的关系，把波兰和中国联系了起来，把伟大的社会主义阵营的所有国家团结起来了。
    雅罗谢维奇副主席说，波兰工人、农民和知识分子对中国人民、中国共产党和中国政府那种友好和兄弟般的伟大感情，是很难用几句话来表达的。新建的北京东郊机场今天迎风飘扬着中波两国国旗。当波兰政府代表团乘坐的喷气式专机徐徐着陆时，在机场上迎候贵宾的副总理陈毅、贺龙、聂荣臻、副委员长、政协全国委员会副主席郭沫若，国防委员会副主席张治中，外交部副部长曾涌泉等人，一齐迎上前去，在飞机前同贵宾们热烈握手。
    在雅罗谢维奇副主席讲话前，陈毅副总理在讲话中代表中国政府和人民热烈欢迎远道而来的波兰贵宾。他说，中波两国虽然隔着千山万水，但是共同的奋斗目标却把我们两国人民紧密地联结在一起。近年来，我们两国的友好合作关系不断地获得巩固和发展。陈毅副总理说，波兰政府代表团这次的友好访问必将进一步加强中波两国的友好合作关系，从而促进我们两国的社会主义建设事业。
    副主席和副总理讲话前，乐队高奏中波两国国歌，雅罗谢维奇副主席由陈毅副总理陪同检阅了仪仗队。首都少先队员向波兰贵宾们献了鲜花。到机场欢迎贵宾的，还有我国政府各部门以及人民团体和民主党派的负责人多人。波兰驻华大使基里洛克和大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    新华社20日讯　国务院副总理陈毅、贺龙、聂荣臻，在今天晚间接见了由雅罗谢维奇副主席率领的波兰人民共和国政府代表团全体贵宾。
    在座的，有各有关部门负责人叶季壮、杨英杰、曹祥仁、曾涌泉、李哲人等，以及波兰驻中国大使基里洛克。
    在接见以后，主人们举行便宴为贵宾们洗尘。（附图片）
