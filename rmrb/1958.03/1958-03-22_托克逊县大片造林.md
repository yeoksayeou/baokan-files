Title: Large-scale Afforestation in Toksun County
Author:
Date: 1958-03-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, 21st: The Uyghur people of Toksun County in the Turpan Basin have launched an unprecedented struggle against sandstorms. They are determined to fight hard for three years to create complete farmland shelterbelts throughout the county, green the entire county in five years, and eliminate sandstorm disasters in ten years. As of the 16th, they have afforested 2,516 mu this year, exceeding the original target of 2,000 mu. They are now striving to complete a new target of 3,770 mu of afforestation and 200,000 scattered tree plantings for the entire year, with a survival rate of 95%.

### 托克逊县大片造林

1958-03-22
第2版()
专栏：

    托克逊县大片造林
    新华社乌鲁木齐21日专电  吐鲁番盆地托克逊县的维吾尔族人民，和风沙灾害展开了史无前例的斗争。他们决心苦战三年，造完全县农田防护林，五年绿化全县，十年消灭风沙灾害。截至16日，今年已造林二千五百一十六亩，超过了原订二千亩的指标。现在他们正在争取完成全年造林三千七百七十亩，零星植树二十万株，成活率达95％的新指标。
