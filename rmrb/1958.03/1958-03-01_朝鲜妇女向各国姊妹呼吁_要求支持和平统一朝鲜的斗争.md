Title: Korean Women Appeal to Sisters of All Countries, Demanding Support for the Struggle for Peaceful Reunification of Korea
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-01
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 27th - The National Women Activists' Rally of Korea, held in Pyongyang tonight, passed an appeal to women all over the world, calling on women all over the world to support the struggle of Korean women demanding the withdrawal of the US military and all foreign troops from South Korea and the peaceful reunification of Korea.

The appeal stated that our struggle is closely linked to your struggle for peace and against the US war policy. We are confident that women's organizations and women all over the world will actively support our struggle as they have done in the past. The appeal called on women all over the world to take all possible measures to ensure that the United States and the countries that have troops stationed in South Korea quickly withdraw their troops from South Korea and force the United States to withdraw its aggressive claws from Korea.

The National Women Activists' Rally of Korea was held in support of the February 5th statement of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the joint statement of the governments of Korea and China. More than 800 women representatives, including Kim Young-soo and Ahn Shin-ho, vice-chairpersons of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Women's League, and heads of women's organizations from northern Korea, attended the rally.

Zhu Bingxian, a female weaver and labor hero from the Pyongyang Textile Factory, said at the meeting, "We unanimously support the statement of the Republic's government and the joint statement of the governments of Korea and China. We are determined to fight for its realization." She said, "The Chinese People's Volunteers have decided to withdraw from Korea within this year. The US military has no excuse to continue to stay in South Korea. I resolutely demand that the US military get out of South Korea." Female agricultural cooperative members, female teachers, and female Christians also spoke at the meeting. The atmosphere of the rally was extremely enthusiastic, and the attendees constantly shouted slogans: "The Korean issue should be resolved by the Korean people themselves," "US troops get out of Korea," "Long live the friendship between Korea and China."

### 朝鲜妇女向各国姊妹呼吁  要求支持和平统一朝鲜的斗争

1958-03-01
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜妇女向各国姊妹呼吁
    要求支持和平统一朝鲜的斗争
    新华社平壤27日电　今天晚上在平壤举行的朝鲜全国妇女积极分子奋起大会通过了一项给全世界妇女的呼吁书，呼吁全世界妇女支持朝鲜妇女要求美军和一切外国军队撤出南朝鲜和和平统一朝鲜的斗争。
    呼吁书说，我们的斗争是和你们争取和平、反对美国战争政策的斗争密切联系在一起的。我们确信，全世界的妇女组织和全世界的妇女将同以往一样积极支持我们的斗争。呼吁书呼吁全世界妇女采取一切可能的措施，使美国和派军队驻在南朝鲜的国家迅速从南朝鲜撤出自己的军队，迫使美国缩回它侵略朝鲜的魔掌。
    朝鲜全国妇女积极分子奋起大会是为了支持朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府2月5日的声明和朝中两国政府的联合声明而举行的。朝鲜民主妇女同盟中央委员会副委员长金英秀、安信浩和来自朝鲜北部各地的妇女组织的负责人等八百多名妇女代表参加了这次大会。
    平壤纺织工厂女织布工、劳动英雄朱炳仙在会上说，我们一致拥护共和国政府的声明和朝中两国政府的联合声明。我们决定为它的实现而奋斗。她说，中国人民志愿军已经决定在今年内撤出朝鲜，美军没有任何借口继续留在南朝鲜，我坚决要求美军从南朝鲜滚出去。接着还有女农业社社员、女教员、女基督教徒等在会上发了言。大会气氛极为激昂，到会者不断地高呼口号：“朝鲜问题由朝鲜人民自己来解决”“美军滚出朝鲜去”“朝中友谊万岁”。
