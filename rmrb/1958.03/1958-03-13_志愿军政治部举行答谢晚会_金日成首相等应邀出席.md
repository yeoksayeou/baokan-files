Title: Volunteer Army Political Department Holds Thank You Reception; Premier Kim Il-sung and Others Attend Upon Invitation
Author:
Date: 1958-03-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 12th. The Cultural Troupe of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Cultural Troupe of a unit of the first group of troops withdrawn from Korea jointly held a thank you reception tonight to express their gratitude to Premier Kim Il-sung and other leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and the state for their warm farewell to the first group of Chinese People's Volunteer Army troops withdrawing from Korea.

Premier Kim Il-sung, Chairman Choe Yong-gon, Vice-Chairman Kim Chang-man, and other party and state leaders, accompanied by General Yang Yong, General Wang Ping, Lieutenant General Liang Biye, and Lieutenant General Zhang Tianyun of the Volunteer Army, watched the evening's performance.

Also accompanying Premier Kim Il-sung and others to watch the performance was Qiao Xiaoguang, China's ambassador to North Korea.

After the performance, Premier Kim Il-sung, Chairman Choe Yong-gon, and Vice-Chairman Kim Chang-man went on stage, presented flower baskets to the performers, and warmly shook hands with the performers, taking pictures together as a memento.

### 志愿军政治部举行答谢晚会  金日成首相等应邀出席

1958-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军政治部举行答谢晚会
    金日成首相等应邀出席
    新华社平壤12日电　为答谢金日成首相等朝鲜劳动党和国家领导人对中国人民志愿军首批撤出朝鲜部队的亲切欢送，志愿军政治部文工团和首批撤出朝鲜部队某部文工团今晚联合举行了答谢晚会。
    金日成首相、崔庸健委员长、金昌满副委员长等党和国家领导人，在志愿军杨勇上将、王平上将、梁必业中将、张天云中将陪同下，观看了晚会的演出。
    陪同金日成首相等观看演出的还有我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光。
    演出结束后，金日成首相、崔庸健委员长、金昌满副委员长走上舞台，向演员们赠送了花篮，并且和演员们亲切握手，合影留念。
