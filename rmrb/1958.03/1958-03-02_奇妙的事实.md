    Amazing Facts
    △The smallest fish in the world is the "Pandaka pygmaea" fish, which grows in the Philippine Islands and is nearly one centimeter long. The largest fish is the "whale shark," which is up to fifteen meters long.
    △The fastest-moving creature in the world is a small insect that lives in northern England, which can fly one thousand three hundred kilometers per hour.

### 奇妙的事实

1958-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    奇妙的事实
    △世界上最小的鱼是生长在菲律宾群岛的“杜父”鱼，它的长度将近一糎。最大的鱼是“鲨鲸”，它长达十五公尺。
    △世界上游动最快的生物是栖居在英国北部的一种小昆虫，它每小时能飞一千三百公里。
