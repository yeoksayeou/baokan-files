Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet to Welcome Japanese Matsuyama Ballet Troupe
Author:
Date: 1958-03-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a banquet tonight to welcome all members of the Japanese Matsuyama Ballet Troupe.

　　At tonight's banquet, Matsuyama Kiyoko, the deputy head of the Matsuyama Ballet Troupe and the actress who plays Xi'er in the ballet "The White Haired Girl," and other Japanese artists met with He Jingzhi and Ding Yi, the playwrights of the Chinese opera "The White Haired Girl," and Wang Kun, the actress who once played Xi'er. They repeatedly congratulated each other on achieving new artistic successes.

### 楚图南设宴欢迎日本松山芭蕾舞团

1958-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    楚图南设宴欢迎日本松山芭蕾舞团
    据新华社12日讯  中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南今晚举行宴会，欢迎日本松山芭蕾舞团的全体人员。
   在今晚的宴会上，松山芭蕾舞团副团长、在舞剧“白毛女”中饰演喜儿的松山树子和其他日本艺术家们，会见了中国歌剧“白毛女”的编剧贺敬之、丁毅和曾经扮演喜儿的演员王昆，他们一再地互祝在艺术上获得新的成就。
