Title: English-Language "Beijing Review" Launched

Author:
Date: 1958-03-04
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd: The English-language "Beijing Review" was launched on the 4th.

The task of this publication is to introduce to English-speaking readers around the world the achievements of our country's political, economic, and cultural construction, and the development of foreign relations.

The inaugural issue contains special articles such as "The Chinese People's Major Peaceful Actions," "Economic Construction in 1958," and "Cooperativization and Agricultural Production," and also reports on the construction upsurge of comparing with the advanced, learning from the advanced, and catching up with the advanced on our country's industrial and agricultural fronts.

In the three special columns of "Review of the Week's Major Events," "China and Foreign Countries," and "Excerpts from Current Commentary," it reports on the new developments in our country's domestic life and foreign political, economic, and cultural relations, and our country's public opinion on current international issues.

"Beijing Review" is published every Tuesday.

### 英文版“北京周报”创刊

1958-03-04
第7版()
专栏：

    英文版“北京周报”创刊
    据新华社3日讯　用英文出版的“北京周报”4日创刊。
    这个刊物的任务是向世界各地的英语读者介绍我国政治、经济、文化建设的成就和对外关系的发展。
    创刊号载有“中国人民的重大和平行动”、“1958年的经济建设”、“合作化与农业生产”等专文，同时报道了我国工农业战线上比先进、学先进、赶先进的建设热潮。
    在“一周大事述评”、“中外关系”、“时论摘录”等三个专栏中，报道了我国国内生活和对外政治、经济、文化关系的新发展和我国舆论对当前国际问题的看法。
    “北京周报”每逢星期二出版。
