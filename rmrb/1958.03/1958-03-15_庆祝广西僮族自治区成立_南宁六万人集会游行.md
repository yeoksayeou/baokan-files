Title: Celebrating the Establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, 60,000 People Gather in Nanning for a Parade
Author:
Date: 1958-03-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, March 14th: Over 60,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Nanning City held a grand rally and parade today to celebrate the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. The evergreen city of Nanning was decorated with a riot of colors today, with national flags hanging all over the city, and red couplets newly pasted on the doors of many homes.

Those attending the celebration included Liu Jianxun, First Secretary of the CPC Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Committee, Wei Guoqing, Chairman of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, Vice Chairmen He Ximing, Li Renren, Qin Yingji, Mo Naiqun, Lu Shaowu, and members of the People's Committee.

Wang Feng, Vice Chairman of the National Ethnic Affairs Commission, Xie Fumin, Vice Chairman of the National People's Congress Ethnic Affairs Committee, representatives from various autonomous regions, provinces, and cities, and specially invited representatives such as General Zhang Yunyi also attended the celebration.

Also attending the celebration were Zhu Wenjin, head of the delegation of people from all ethnic groups in the Viet Bac Autonomous Region of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, deputy heads Phan Van Tỉnh, Yang Gong Huo, and all members of the delegation.

Tonight, over 17,000 people of all ethnic groups held a garden party and get-together at the Wangshanpo People's Park in Nanning City, singing and dancing.

### 庆祝广西僮族自治区成立  南宁六万人集会游行

1958-03-15
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝广西僮族自治区成立
    南宁六万人集会游行
    据新华社南宁14日电　南宁市各族各界六万多人今天举行盛大的集会和游行，庆祝广西僮族自治区成立。四季长青的南宁市今天被装饰得万紫千红，全市遍悬国旗，许多人家的门上新贴了红色的门联。
    今天参加庆祝大会的有中共广西僮族自治区委员会第一书记刘建勋，广西僮族自治区主席韦国清，副主席贺希明、李任仁、覃应机、莫乃群、卢绍武和人民委员会的委员们。
    民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、全国人民代表大会民族委员会副主任委员谢扶民和各自治区、省、市的代表以及张云逸大将等特邀代表都参加了庆祝大会。
    参加庆祝大会的还有越南民主共和国越北自治区各族人民代表团团长朱文晋，副团长潘文省、杨公活和全体团员。
    今晚在南宁市内望山坡人民公园，各族人民一万七千多人载歌载舞地举行了游园联欢晚会。
