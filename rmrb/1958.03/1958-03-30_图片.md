Author: Li Xuan

	This year, the double-cropping rice planting time in various counties of the western Sichuan Plain is earlier than in previous years, and the area is also larger. This is a scene of members of the Youyi Agricultural Cooperative in Xicheng Township, Chengdu suburbs, sowing seeds.
	The members of the Youyi Cooperative are selecting seeds with mud water by the field. (Draft from Sichuan Daily) Photo by Li Xuan
