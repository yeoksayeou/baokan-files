Title: People Universally Demand a Stop to Nuclear Testing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 5

Small Statistics

    People Universally Demand a Stop to Nuclear Testing

    According to a report in the "New York Herald Tribune," the "World Opinion Poll" recently conducted a survey in thirteen countries, in which two-thirds to one-half of those surveyed agreed to accept the Soviet Union's proposal to halt nuclear testing for two years.

    The percentage of people in each country who agreed to accept the Soviet proposal is as follows:

    France 76%.
    Austria 70%.
    West Germany 70%.
    Japan 67%.
    Sweden 66%.
    Norway 63%.
    Belgium 62%.
    Netherlands 61%.
    United Kingdom 59%.
    Denmark 54%.
    Australia 50%.
    Italy 48%.
    Brazil 45%.

### 人民普遍要求停止核试验

1958-03-08
第5版()
专栏：小统计

    人民普遍要求停止核试验
    据“纽约先驱论坛报”报道，最近“世界民意测验所”在十三个国家进行了一次调查，被调查的人中有三分之二到二分之一的人同意接受苏联提出的停止核试验两年的建议。
    各国同意接受苏联建议的人数比例如下：
    法国76％。
    奥地利70％。
    西德70％。
    日本67％。
    瑞典66％。
    挪威63％。
    比利时62％。
    荷兰61％。
    英国59％。
    丹麦54％。
    澳大利亚50％。
    意大利48％。
    巴西45％。
