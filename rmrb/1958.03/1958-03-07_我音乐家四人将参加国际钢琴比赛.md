Title: Four Chinese Musicians to Participate in International Piano Competition
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 6th. Four young Chinese musicians, Gu Shengying, Liu Shikun, Yang Bingsun, and Lin Kehan, will participate in the grand International Piano and Violin Competition in memory of Tchaikovsky, which will be held here from March 18th to April 14th. They are currently practicing in Moscow.

### 我音乐家四人将参加国际钢琴比赛

1958-03-07
第6版()
专栏：

    我音乐家四人将参加国际钢琴比赛
    据新华社莫斯科6日电　中国将有四个青年音乐家顾圣婴、刘诗昆、杨秉荪和林克汉参加3月18日到4月14日在这里举行的纪念柴可夫斯基的国际钢琴和小提琴盛大比赛。目前，他们已经在莫斯科进行练习。
