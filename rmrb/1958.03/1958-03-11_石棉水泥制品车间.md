Title: Asbestos Cement Products Workshop
Author: Ye Lǜsù (叶绿素)
Date: 1958-03-11
Page: 3

Construction Flowers

    Asbestos Cement Products Workshop
    Our country's first mechanized asbestos cement products workshop has begun construction in Huangshi City, Hubei Province. Following the principle of "spending less money and doing more work," the Ministry of Building Materials Industry has changed the original plan to build this workshop as a separate factory and instead attached it to the Huaxin Cement Factory. This will save two-thirds of the investment and one-half of the personnel.
    After the completion of this workshop, it can produce 1.3 million meters of asbestos cement pressure pipes annually. This kind of asbestos cement pressure pipe can replace metal pipes and can be used for water and sewage pipes, gas pipes, low-pressure oil pipelines, cable conduits, alkali water conveying pipes, hot spring conveying pipes, and other industrial pipelines.
  (Ye Lǜsù)

### 石棉水泥制品车间
叶绿素
1958-03-11
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    石棉水泥制品车间
    我国第一座机械化石棉水泥制品车间，在湖北省黄石市动工兴建。根据“少花钱、多办事”的方针，建筑材料工业部将这个车间由原计划单独建厂改为附设在华新水泥厂内，这就可以节省投资三分之二，节省人员二分之一。
    这个车间建成后，每年可生产石棉水泥加压管一百三十万公尺。这种石棉水泥加压管可以代替金属管道，用于上下水道瓦斯管、低压输油管、电缆套管、碱水输送管、温泉输送管和其他工业管道。
  （叶绿素）
