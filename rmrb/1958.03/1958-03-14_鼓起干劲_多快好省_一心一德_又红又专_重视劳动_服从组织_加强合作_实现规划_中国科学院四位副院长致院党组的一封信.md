Title: Exert Effort, Achieve More, Faster, Better, and More Economically; One Heart, One Mind; Both Red and Expert; Value Labor; Obey Organization; Strengthen Cooperation; Realize the Plan: A Letter from Four Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences to the Academy's Party Group
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-14
Page: 7

    Our report The People's Daily dispatch: Li Siguang, Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, and Wu Youxun, vice presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, sent a letter to the Academy's Party Group on March 6th. The full text of the letter is as follows: Chinese Academy of Sciences Party Group:
    These days, the nationwide vigorous socialist leap forward has made us feel extremely excited and greatly encouraged. We are determined to thoroughly transform ourselves and, following the workers and peasants, contribute all our strength to the cause of socialist construction. We are determined to act according to the spirit of
    Exert Effort, Achieve More, Faster, Better, and More Economically; One Heart, One Mind; Both Red and Expert
    Value Labor, Obey Organization, Strengthen Cooperation, Realize the Plan
    and to achieve the following:
    1. Serve the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party wholeheartedly, give our hearts to the Party, and resolutely follow the socialist road.
    2. Seriously study Marxism-Leninism. Strive to learn from the advanced experience of Soviet science, integrate it with China's reality, and rapidly promote the people's scientific cause.
    3. Work hard to transform ourselves and strive to become intellectuals of the working class, serving the people wholeheartedly.
            Li Siguang, Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun

### 鼓起干劲  多快好省  一心一德  又红又专  重视劳动  服从组织  加强合作  实现规划  中国科学院四位副院长致院党组的一封信

1958-03-14
第7版()
专栏：

    鼓起干劲  多快好省  一心一德  又红又专
    重视劳动  服从组织  加强合作  实现规划
    中国科学院四位副院长致院党组的一封信
    本报讯  中国科学院副院长李四光、陶孟和、竺可桢、吴有训于3月6日致科学院党组的一封信，信的全文如下：中国科学院党组：
    这些日子全国人民轰轰烈烈的社会主义大跃进，使我们感到无比的兴奋和莫大的鼓舞，我们决心彻底改造自己，跟着工人农民，把自己所有的力量贡献给社会主义建设事业。我们决心根据
    鼓起干劲多快好省一心一德又红又专
    重视劳动服从组织加强合作实现规划的精神，做到
    一、全心全意服从中国共产党的领导，把心交给党，坚决走社会主义道路。
    二、认真学习马克思列宁主义。努力学习苏联科学先进经验，结合中国实际，迅速推进人民的科学事业。
    三、努力改造自己，争取成为工人阶级知识分子，全心全意为人民服务。
            李四光  陶孟和  竺可桢  吴有训
