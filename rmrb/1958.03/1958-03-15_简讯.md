    Brief News

    China and Bulgaria Sign This Year's Goods Exchange Protocol
    Sofia Dispatch: China and Bulgaria signed the 1958 Goods Exchange and Payment Protocol in Sofia on the 13th.
    The protocol stipulates that China will supply Bulgaria with tin, silk, tea, asbestos, tires, spinning machines, chemicals, etc. Bulgaria will supply China with lead, zinc, ammonium nitrate, tobacco, penicillin, cotton cloth, etc.

    China and Albania Sign This Year's Goods Exchange Protocol
    Tirana Dispatch: Representatives of the Chinese and Albanian governments, after friendly consultations, signed the 1958 Goods Exchange and Payment Protocol in Tirana on the 12th.
    The protocol stipulates that this year's trade volume and the variety of goods exchanged between the two sides will increase compared to last year. Albania will export petroleum, copper, cotton cloth and other commodities to China, and China will export pure tin, silk, woollen cloth, paper, tea, tires and other industrial products to Albania.

### 简讯

专栏：简讯

    简讯
    中保签订今年换货议定书
    索非亚讯：中国和保加利亚1958年交换货物和付款议定书13日在索非亚签字。
    议定书规定：中国将供给保加利亚以锡、丝绸、茶叶、石棉、轮胎、纺纱机、化工品等。保加利亚将供给中国以铅、锌、硝酸铵、烟草、青霉素、棉布等。
    中阿签订今年换货议定书
    地拉那讯：中国和阿尔巴尼亚两国政府代表经过友好洽商，12日在地拉那签订了1958年交换货物和付款议定书。
    议定书规定，今年双方的贸易额和交换货物的品种都比去年有所增加。阿尔巴尼亚将向中国输出石油、铜和棉布等商品，中国将向阿尔巴尼亚输出纯锡、丝绸、呢绒、纸张、茶叶、轮胎和其他工业产品。
