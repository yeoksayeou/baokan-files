    Amazing Facts
    △ The Austrian, Wenzell Mayer, is the tallest man in the world. His height is 2.78 meters. Second is the Alsatian, Claudius, who is 2.75 meters tall. There is a man in England named Aubry, who is 2.68 meters tall.
    △ The hottest place in the world is Tripoli, Libya, Africa. The highest temperature reaches +58°.
    △ Gold is an extremely ductile and malleable metal. From one gram of gold, a thin wire as long as three kilometers and four hundred and twenty meters can be drawn.

### 奇妙的事实

1958-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    奇妙的事实
    △奥地利人文采里麦耶尔是世界上最高的人。他的身长有二公尺七十八公分。其次是阿尔萨斯人克拉乌，他有二公尺七十五公分高。英国有一个叫奥勃利克的人，身长二公尺六十八公分。
    △世界上最热的地方是非洲利比亚的特黎波里亚。最高温度达＋58°。
    △金，是一种极富有韧性的可塑金属。从一克重的金内，可以拉出一根长达三公里四百二十公尺的细丝。
