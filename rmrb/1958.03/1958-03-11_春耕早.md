Title: Early Spring Plowing
Author: Guo Ke
Date: 1958-03-11
Page: 8

    Early Spring Plowing
    Guo Ke
    People surge like tides across the mountains and fields,
    This year's spring plowing is early;
    Songs resound in the winter water paddies,
    Red flags flutter everywhere.

    春耕早
