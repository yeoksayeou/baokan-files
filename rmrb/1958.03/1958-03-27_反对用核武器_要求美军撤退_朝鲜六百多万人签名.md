Title: Oppose the Use of Nuclear Weapons, Demand the Withdrawal of American Troops: More Than Six Million Koreans Sign Petition
Author:
Date: 1958-03-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th. The Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland Front issued a communiqué today stating that a total of 6,458,961 people in North Korea have participated in the signature campaign demanding the prohibition of the production, testing, and use of atomic and hydrogen weapons, opposing the United States turning South Korea into an atomic base, and demanding the withdrawal of American troops from Korea.

This nationwide signature campaign was initiated by the Second Congress of the Korean Fatherland Front. The communiqué said that from December 27th last year to February this year, more than 20,000 mass rallies were held in factories, rural areas, schools, and streets in North Korea for the signature campaign. Workers, farmers, students, figures in science and culture, industrialists and businessmen, religious figures, and others participated in the signature campaign.

The communiqué said that this nationwide signature campaign once again demonstrated the invincible strength of the Korean people united around the Korean Workers' Party and the Republic's government, and the righteousness of the Korean people's struggle for the peaceful reunification of the fatherland.

### 反对用核武器  要求美军撤退  朝鲜六百多万人签名

1958-03-27
第5版()
专栏：

    反对用核武器  要求美军撤退
    朝鲜六百多万人签名
    新华社平壤26日电　朝鲜祖国战线中央委员会书记局今天发表公报说，朝鲜北部共有六百四十五万八千九百六十一人参加了要求禁止生产试验和使用原子武器和氢武器、反对美国把南朝鲜变为原子基地、要求美军撤出朝鲜的签名运动。
    这次全民性的签名运动是由朝鲜祖国战线第二次大会发起的。公报说，从去年12月27日到今年2月，在朝鲜北部各地的工厂、农村、学校、街道为签名运动共举行了两万多次群众集会。工人、农民、学生、科学文化界人士、工商业者、宗教界人士等都参加了签名运动。
    公报说这个全民性的签名运动再次显示了团结在朝鲜劳动党和共和国政府周围的朝鲜人民的不可战胜的力量和朝鲜人民和平统一祖国的斗争的正义性。
