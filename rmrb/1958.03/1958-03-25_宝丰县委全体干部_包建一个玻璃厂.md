Title: The Entire Cadre of the Baofeng County Committee Builds a Glass Factory Through Contracting
Author: Zhang Daosheng, Zhao Changli
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 3

    The Entire Cadre of the Baofeng County Committee
    Builds a Glass Factory Through Contracting
    All the staff members of the Baofeng County Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in Henan Province, actively responded to the County Committee’s call for cadres to invest in the development of local industries, and unanimously proposed: to get rid of bureaucratic airs with the spirit of three years of hard work, to turn extravagance into machinery, and to turn ostentation into factories; to support the great leap forward of local industries with practical actions of thrift and frugality. In one morning, sixty-eight comrades reported a total of 3,569 yuan. They are determined to contract and build a glass factory with an annual output of 400,000 glass bottles to promote the great leap forward of local industries. (Zhang Daosheng, Zhao Changli)

### 宝丰县委全体干部  包建一个玻璃厂
张道生  赵长立
1958-03-25
第3版()
专栏：

    宝丰县委全体干部
    包建一个玻璃厂
    中共河南省宝丰县委会全体工作人员，积极响应县委提出的干部投资发展地方工业的号召，一致提出：以苦战三年的精神，打掉官气，变阔气为机器，变排场为工厂；以实际行动节衣缩食，支持地方工业大跃进。在一个早晨，有六十八个同志共报款三千五百六十九元。他们决心包建一个年产四十万个玻璃瓶的玻璃厂，促进地方工业大跃进。  （张道生、赵长立）
