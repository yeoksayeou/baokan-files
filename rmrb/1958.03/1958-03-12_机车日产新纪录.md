Title: Locomotive Daily Production Sets New Record

Author:

Date: 1958-03-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 8th. The youth contracted locomotive team of the Sujiatun Locomotive Depot reached a daily production of 2,341,000 ton-kilometers on the 7th, setting a new national record for daily locomotive production.

　　On that day, they were driving a "Construction" type locomotive manufactured in China. The new record was created on the Sujiatun-Dashiqiao line. Their driving speed reached 47.6 kilometers per hour, and each train carried 500 to 1,000 tons more cargo than in the past.

　　This contracted team has now put forward a new goal: to strive for a daily locomotive production of 2.5 million ton-kilometers in the next ten days.

### 机车日产新纪录

1958-03-12
第3版()
专栏：

    机车日产新纪录
    新华社沈阳8日电  苏家屯机务段青年包车组7日达到日产二百三十四万一千吨公里，创造了机车日产量全国最高纪录。
    这天，他们驾驶的是我国制造的建设型机车。新纪录是在苏家屯大石桥线路上创造的。他们每小时的行车速度达到了四十七点六公里，每列车比过去多拉货物五百到一千吨。
    这个包车组现在又提出新的奋斗目标：在最近十天内争取机车日产量达到二百五十万吨公里。
