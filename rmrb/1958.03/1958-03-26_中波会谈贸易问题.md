Title: Sino-Polish Talks on Trade Issues
Author:
Date: 1958-03-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 25th. Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, Jaroszewicz, held talks on trade issues between China and Poland this afternoon. The talks took place in an extremely friendly atmosphere.

    On the Chinese side participating in the talks were Minister of Foreign Trade, Ye Ji-zhuang; Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, Yang Yingjie; Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Li Zheren; Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Cao Xiangren; and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zeng Yongquan.

    On the Polish side participating in the talks were Minister of Foreign Trade, Trampczynski; Vice Chairman of the Planning Commission, Jaszczuk; Director of the Economic Department of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Olszewski; Vice Minister of Finance, Kolej; Vice Minister of Heavy Industry, Koi; and Polish Ambassador to China, Kiryluk.

### 中波会谈贸易问题

1958-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中波会谈贸易问题
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅和波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇，在今天下午就中波两国的贸易问题举行了会谈。会谈是在极为友好的空气中进行的。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有对外贸易部部长叶季壮，国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰，对外贸易部副部长李哲人，第一机械工业部副部长曹祥仁，外交部副部长曾涌泉。
    波兰方面参加会谈的，有对外贸易部部长特朗普钦斯基，计划委员会副主席雅希楚克，波兰统一工人党中央经济部部长奥尔谢夫斯基，财政部副部长科莱，重工业部副部长科依，波兰驻中国大使基里洛克。
