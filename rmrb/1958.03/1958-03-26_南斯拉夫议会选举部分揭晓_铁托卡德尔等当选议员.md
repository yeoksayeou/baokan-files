Title: Partial Results of Yugoslav Parliamentary Elections Announced: Tito, Kardelj, and Others Elected as Deputies
Author:
Date: 1958-03-26
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, March 24th. According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency, the Yugoslav Federal Election Commission announced the first batch of official results of the election of deputies to the Federal Chamber of the Federal People's Assembly on the morning of the 24th. President Tito was elected in the electoral district where he was nominated as a candidate. He received all the valid votes, that is, 99.3% of the total votes cast. In this electoral district, the number of voters accounted for 95.5% of the total number of voters.

Other elected Yugoslav leaders include Federal Executive Council Vice Presidents Kardelj, Ranković, Vukmanović, and Djurašković, and State Secretary for Foreign Affairs Popović, among others.

The election results of the deputies to the Republican Chamber of the Republican People's Assemblies of each republic have also begun to be successively provided by the election committees of each republic.

### 南斯拉夫议会选举部分揭晓  铁托卡德尔等当选议员

1958-03-26
第6版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫议会选举部分揭晓
    铁托卡德尔等当选议员
    新华社贝尔格莱德24日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫联邦选举委员会24日早晨宣布了联邦国民议会联邦院议员选举的第一批正式结果。铁托总统在他被提名为候选人的选区当选。他获得了全部有效票数，即投票总数的99.3％。在这个选区，投票人数占选民总数的95.5％。
    其他当选的南斯拉夫领导人有联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔、兰科维奇、伏克曼诺维奇、乔拉柯维奇，外交国务秘书长波波维奇等。
    各共和国国民议会共和国院议员的选举结果也已经开始由各共和国选举委员会陆续提供。
