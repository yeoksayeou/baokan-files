Title: Nasser Appoints Leaders of the United Arab Republic

Author:
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, March 7th. United Arab Republic President Nasser issued an order on the 6th, appointing Boghdadi, Amer, Hourani, and Asali as Vice Presidents of the United Arab Republic. Boghdadi is the former Speaker of the Egyptian National Assembly, Amer is the Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of the United Arab Republic, Hourani is the former Speaker of the Syrian National Assembly, and Asali is the former Prime Minister of Syria.

President Nasser also appointed the ministers of the various departments of the Cabinet of the United Arab Republic, namely: Foreign Minister Fawzi, Defense Minister Amer, Deputy Defense Minister Rizk, Industry Minister Sidky, Education Minister Hosseini, Minister of National Guidance Radwan, Minister of Religious Endowments Bakouri, Minister of State in charge of Presidential Affairs Sabri, and Minister of State in charge of Arab Affairs Bitar. Bitar is the former Syrian Foreign Minister, while the other ministers and deputy ministers held the same positions in the former Egyptian government.

### 纳赛尔任命阿联领导人

1958-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔任命阿联领导人
    新华社大马士革7日电  阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔在6日颁布命令，任命波格达迪、阿密尔、胡拉尼和阿萨利为阿拉伯联合共和国副总统。波格达迪是前埃及国民议会议长，阿密尔是阿拉伯联合共和国武装部队总司令，胡拉尼是前叙利亚国民议会议长，阿萨利是前叙利亚总理。
    纳赛尔总统还任命了阿拉伯联合共和国内阁的各部部长，这就是：外交部长法齐、国防部长阿密尔、国防部副部长里兹克、工业部长西德基、教育部长侯赛尼、国家指导部长拉德温、宗教基金部长巴库里、负责总统事务的国务部长萨布里、负责阿拉伯事务的国务部长比塔尔。其中比塔尔是前叙利亚外交部长，其他各部部长和副部长在前埃及政府中都担任相同的职务。
