Title: Soviet "Il-18" Aircraft Flies Directly from Moscow to Irkutsk
Author:
Date: 1958-03-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd: TASS reports: The new "Il-18" passenger plane with seventy-five seats flew non-stop from Moscow to Irkutsk for the first time on the 21st, taking six hours and forty-five minutes.

In the past, planes flying this route usually had to land once or twice.

### 苏联“伊尔—18式”机从莫斯科直飞伊尔库次克

1958-03-23
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联“伊尔—18式”机
    从莫斯科直飞伊尔库次克
    据新华社22日讯　据塔斯社报道：有七十五个座位的“伊尔—18式”新型客机，21日第一次由莫斯科不着陆飞到伊尔库次克，历时六小时四十五分。
    过去，飞行在这条航线上的飞机通常要着陆一两次以上。
