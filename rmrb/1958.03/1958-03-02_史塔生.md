    Stassen
    Stassen was born in April 1907. A Republican, he is a rather influential figure in American politics. He worked as a lawyer, wore a military uniform, taught, and especially spent a long time in officialdom. He has been a prominent figure in the United States since 1938. From 1938 to 1943, he served as Governor of Minnesota. From 1943 to 1945, he served as Assistant Chief of Staff of the U.S. Third Fleet. In 1945, he was a U.S. representative at the San Francisco Conference. In 1948, he became President of the University of Pennsylvania. In 1953, he became Director of the "U.S. Foreign Aid Administration." In March 1955, he became disarmament advisor to U.S. President Dwight Eisenhower. During his time as a disarmament advisor, he had long-standing disagreements with Dulles and others, and finally resigned recently.

### 史塔生

1958-03-02
第3版()
专栏：

    史塔生
    史塔生出生于1907年4月，共和党人，是美国政界一个颇有影响的人物。他当过律师，穿过军装，执过教鞭，而尤其长期跑过官场。打从1938年起，他便成了美国的风云人物。1938—1943年间，他任明尼苏达州州长。1943—1945年间，任美国第三舰队助理参谋长。1945年任旧金山会议美国代表。1948年任宾夕法尼亚大学校长。1953年出任“美国援外事务管理署”署长。1955年3月就任美国总统艾森豪威尔裁军问题顾问。在担任裁军顾问期间，他同杜勒斯等人曾经长期存在分歧，终于在最近辞职。
