Title: Burmese Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate the 13th Anniversary of the Founding of the Burmese Army
Author:
Date: 1958-03-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: Burmese Military Attaché to China, Colonel Liu Dang, and his wife, held a reception in Beijing this evening to celebrate the 13th anniversary of the founding of the Burmese Army.

    Among those invited to the reception were Senior General Zhang Zongxun, Senior General Gan Siqi, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice Admiral Luo Shunchu, Lieutenant General Liu Shaowen, Lieutenant General Cheng Jun, and other senior generals and officers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing.

    Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, and Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, were also invited to the reception.

    Colonel Liu Dang said at the reception that the Union of Burma and the People's Republic of China are neighbors connected by rivers and lands. In order to seek more friendly relations, the two countries have repeatedly sent friendly delegations and military friendly delegations headed by the leaders of the two governments to each other's countries. Through these visits, the relationship between the armed forces of the two countries has become more friendly, closer, and developed with more confidence. He congratulated the Union of Burma and the People's Republic of China on their eternal friendship, peace, and prosperity.

    On behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, extended heartfelt and warm congratulations to the armed forces of the Union of Burma at the reception. Senior General Zhang Zongxun said that the armies of China and Burma both shoulder the glorious task of defending their motherlands and safeguarding peace. We firmly believe that, with our joint efforts in the future, the friendship between the people and armed forces of China and Burma will be further consolidated and developed, and will flow endlessly like the Irrawaddy River.

    U Hla Maw, Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, attended this evening's reception.

    Heads of diplomatic missions and military attachés from various countries to China were also invited to the reception.

### 庆祝缅甸建军十三周年  缅甸驻华武官举行酒会

1958-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝缅甸建军十三周年
    缅甸驻华武官举行酒会
    新华社27日讯　缅甸联邦驻华武官柳当上校和夫人，今天晚上在北京举行酒会，庆祝缅甸建军十三周年。
    应邀参加庆祝酒会的有张宗逊上将、甘泗淇上将、萧向荣中将、罗舜初海军中将、刘少文中将、成钧空军中将等中国人民解放军高级将领和驻京陆海空军军官。
    我国外交部副部长章汉夫，中缅友好协会会长郑振铎，也应邀参加了酒会。
    柳当上校在酒会上讲话说，缅甸联邦和中华人民共和国水连地接，为了谋求更友好的关系，两国曾屡次互派以两国政府领导人为首的友好代表团和军事友好代表团。通过这些访问，两国武装部队间的关系更加友好了，更加密切了，更满怀信心地发展了。他祝贺缅甸联邦和中华人民共和国永恒友好、和平与繁荣。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将在酒会上代表中国人民解放军全体官兵向缅甸联邦的武装部队表示衷心的热烈的祝贺。张宗逊上将说，中缅两国军队都肩负着保卫自己的祖国和维护和平的光荣任务。我们深信，今后在我们共同努力下，中缅两国人民和武装部队之间的友谊，一定会进一步巩固和发展，将像伊洛瓦底江一样长流不息。
    缅甸联邦驻我国大使吴拉茂出席了今晚的酒会。
    各国驻华使节和武官也应邀参加了酒会。
