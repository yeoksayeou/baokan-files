Title: "Lu Ban" Master Goes to the Countryside
Author:
Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 3

    "Lu Ban" Master Goes to the Countryside
    In order to realize the "thousand-jin county" plan, more than two thousand iron and woodworking handicraft workers in Pingjiang County, Hunan Province, organized more than one thousand carpentry teams and itinerant iron furnaces to go to the countryside to help farmers innovate agricultural tools. The county's six pot-casting factories were also temporarily converted into raw material manufacturing plants for manufacturing new-style water plows.

### “鲁班”师傅下乡

1958-03-06
第3版()
专栏：

    “鲁班”师傅下乡
    为了实现千斤县的计划，湖南平江县两千多个铁、木手工业工人，组织了一千多个木工组和巡回铁炉下乡帮助农民革新农具，县里六个铸锅厂也暂时改成了制造新式水犁的原料制造厂。
