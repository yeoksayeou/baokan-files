Title: Representatives Going to Congratulate the Establishment of the Zhuang Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 5th - Representatives who have come to Nanning to congratulate the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region have arrived one after another. The list is as follows:
    He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council
    Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China
    Xie Fumin (Zhuang), Deputy Chairman of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress
    Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region: Wang Zaitian (Mongolian, Vice Chairman of the Autonomous Region), Han Shijin (Mongolian, Director of the Industrial Department of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Party Committee of the Chinese Communist Party), Zhang Hui (Secretary of the Chinese Communist Party Committee of the Inner Mongolia Medical College).
    Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region: Sima Yi Yasinuofu (Uygur, Director of the Autonomous Region Forestry Department), Mai Hesude Tiyifu (Uygur, Director of the Autonomous Region Communications Department), Yusufu Han (Kazak, Deputy Governor of the Ili Kazak Autonomous Prefecture), Zaina Pu Han (Uygur, Chairman of the Urumqi Women's Federation).
    Tibet: Liu Xia Tudeng Taba (Tibetan, Standing Committee Member of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region), Ji Jinmei (Tibetan, Member of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region), Bangda Duoji (Tibetan, Member of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region), Huorkang Zhazha (Tibetan, Lama).
    Jilin Province: Zhu Dehai (Korean, Deputy Governor), Zheng Jianxuan (Zhuang, Professor at Northeast People's University).
    Qinghai Province: Xi Rao Jiacuo (Tibetan, Deputy Governor), Ma Letian (Hui, Director of the Department of Civil Affairs).
    Gansu Province: Huang Zhengqing (Tibetan, Deputy Governor), Zhou Xiangchu (Vice Chairman of the Provincial Political Consultative Conference), Du Ruilan (Deputy Director of the Education Department), Ma Wenqing (Hui, Deputy Secretary of the Chinese Communist Party Linxia Hui Autonomous Prefecture Committee).
    Sichuan Province: Ren Jinglong (Director of the Provincial Nationalities Affairs Commission), Guoji Mugu (Yi, Vice Chairman of the Provincial Political Consultative Conference), Awang Jiacuo (Tibetan, Deputy Governor of the Ganzi Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture), Su Xin (Qiang, Deputy Governor of the Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture).
    Yunnan Province: Zhang Chong (Yi, Deputy Governor), Dao Jingban (Dai, Governor of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture), Dao Youliang (Dai, Deputy Governor of the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Prefecture).
    Guizhou Province: Yang Hanxian (Miao, Deputy Director of the Provincial Nationalities Affairs Commission), Wei Maowen (Buyi, Governor of the Qiannan Buyi and Miao Autonomous Prefecture), Wang Jiaxian (Buyi, Director of the Qiannan Buyi and Miao Autonomous Prefecture Women's Federation), Wang Yaolun (Miao, Deputy Governor of the Qiandongnan Miao and Dong Autonomous Prefecture), Wang Donglan (Dong, Deputy Director of the Supervision Office of the People's Committee of the Qiandongnan Miao and Dong Autonomous Prefecture).
    Hunan Province: Long Jiwu (Miao, Director of the Provincial Nationalities Affairs Commission), Peng Zugui (Tujia, Deputy Governor of the Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture), Yang Wenhui (Miao, Deputy County Chief of Chengbu Miao Autonomous County), Yang Wenxian (Dong, Deputy County Chief of Tongdao Dong Autonomous County), Long Xiuzhen (Miao, Director of the Jishou County Women's Federation), Li Xingsheng (Yao, Deputy County Chief of Jianghua Yao Autonomous County).
    Guangdong Province: Lin Qiangyun (Deputy Governor), Luo Ming (Director of the Provincial Nationalities Affairs Commission), Lin Yuechuan (Li, Deputy Governor of the Hainan Li and Miao Autonomous Prefecture), Ma Jianhuan (Zhuang), Ma Chengchu.
    Hubei Province: Xie Wei (Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Chinese Communist Party Hubei Provincial Committee), Yu Jintang (Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Chinese Communist Party Wuhan Municipal Committee), Li Shouxian (Hui, President of the Central South University for Nationalities).
    Beijing Municipality: Li Shu (Hui, Deputy Director of the Municipal Civil Affairs Bureau and President of the Hui Nationality College).
    Shanghai Municipality: Song Richang (Deputy Mayor).
    Guangzhou Municipality: Lu Zhengpin (Director of the Municipal Civil Affairs Bureau).
