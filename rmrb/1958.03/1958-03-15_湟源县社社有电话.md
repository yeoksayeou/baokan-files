Title: Huangyuan County Communes Have Telephones

Author:
Date: 1958-03-15
Page: 2

    Huangyuan County Communes Have Telephones
    Qinghai Province's Huangyuan County has established a rural telephone network. All 102 agricultural cooperatives in the county's twelve townships have had telephones installed. In addition, more than ten production teams have also had telephones installed.
    Now, the various urgent notices of the CCP Huangyuan County Committee can be quickly transmitted to all agricultural cooperatives. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 湟源县社社有电话

1958-03-15
第2版()
专栏：

    湟源县社社有电话
    青海省湟源县已经建成了农村电话网。全县十二个乡的一百零二个农业社里都安装了电话。另外还有十多个生产队也都安装了电话。
    现在，中共湟源县委的各项紧急通知，都能迅速地传达到各个农业社。（据新华社消息）
