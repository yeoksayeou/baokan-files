Title: Rebel Representative Pantouw Goes to Manila Again to Seek Help
Author:
Date: 1958-03-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 13th, Manila Dispatch: While the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Council meeting in Manila is plotting further interference in Indonesia's internal affairs, Pantouw, the adjutant general of Soemual, the rebellious former commander of the Seventh Military District of Indonesia, arrived in Manila from the United States on the 12th. It is reported that this Indonesian rebel went to Manila as an "envoy" of the Central Sumatra rebel "government" to carry out conspiratorial activities.

Pantouw had previously accompanied Soemual to Manila in February to carry out activities, and then went to Japan, some European countries, and the United States. This rebel said that he had tried to "win financial assistance" for the Central Sumatra rebel "government" in these countries.

### 叛乱分子代表潘托伍  再到马尼拉求救

1958-03-14
第5版()
专栏：

    叛乱分子代表潘托伍
    再到马尼拉求救
    新华社13日讯  马尼拉消息：当正在马尼拉举行的东南亚条约组织理事会会议在策划进一步干涉印度尼西亚内政的阴谋的时候，印度尼西亚叛变的前第七军区司令苏穆阿尔的副官长潘托伍在12日从美国到达马尼拉。有消息说，印度尼西亚的这个叛乱分子是作为中苏门答腊叛乱“政府”的“使节”到马尼拉进行阴谋活动的。
    潘托伍在2月间曾经随苏穆阿尔到马尼拉进行了活动，随后前往日本、欧洲的一些国家和美国。这个叛乱分子说，他在这些国家曾经设法为中苏门答腊的叛乱“政府”“赢得财政帮助”。
