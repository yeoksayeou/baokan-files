Title: A Batch of Results Obtained from Observations of Two Satellites
Author:
Date: 1958-03-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 27th: Soviet scientists have obtained the first batch of scientific results from observations of the two artificial satellites, according to TASS. At the annual meeting of the Soviet Academy of Sciences on the 25th, the Academic Secretary-General of the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Topchiev, in addition to discussing the experiment on the dog Laika and the calculation of satellite orbit parameters using the Doppler effect, also reported the preliminary conclusions obtained by Soviet scientists based on observation materials in determining atmospheric density, ionospheric electron concentration, and cosmic ray intensity.

Topchiev said that these preliminary results are sufficient to show that artificial satellites are a powerful tool for scientific research. In addition to satellites, the Soviet Union also widely uses various rockets for geophysical exploration. If satellites provide extremely valuable conditions for long-term measurement of certain parameters in different regions of the Earth, then high-altitude rockets enable us to obtain the entire vertical profile of the atmosphere from the bottom to the top.

Topchiev concluded by saying that the achievements of our country's jet technology enable us to send containers weighing hundreds of kilograms, equipped with scientific instruments, into high-altitude orbits. Utilizing these achievements, our scientists are now able to propose various tasks in the study of the upper atmosphere and the part of the cosmic space close to the Earth. It is equally clear that only by manufacturing satellites of enormous weight can we solve the problem of long-range flights into outer space and reaching other planets.

### 对两个卫星的观测已获得一批成果

1958-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    对两个卫星的观测已获得一批成果
    据新华社27日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联科学家已经从对两个人造卫星的观测中获得了第一批科学成果。苏联科学院主席团学术秘书长托普切夫25日在苏联科学院年会上除了谈到关于小狗莱伊卡的试验和利用多普勒效应计算卫星轨道参数等问题外，还报告了苏联科学家根据观测材料在确定大气密度、电离层电子浓度、宇宙线强度等方面获得的初步结论。
    托普切夫说，这些初步结果足以说明人造卫星是科学研究的一项有力工具。除了卫星以外，苏联还广泛使用各种火箭来进行地球物理考察。如果说卫星为长期测量地球不同地区某些参数提供了极为宝贵的条件，那么高空火箭就使我们能够获得大气从底部到高层的整个垂直面的情况。
    托普切夫最后说，我国喷气技术的成就使我们能够把装有科学仪器的数百公斤重容器送到高空的轨道上去。利用这些成就，我们的科学家目前已经可能在研究大气高层和接近地球的部分宇宙空间方面提出各种任务。同样清楚的是，只有制造重量巨大的卫星，才能解决向宇宙空间从事远程飞行和到达其他行星的问题。
