Title: All Foreign Troops Should Withdraw from Korea: The All-China Women's Federation Supports the Appeal of Korean Women

Author:

Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 4th -- The All-China Women's Federation sent a telegram to the Korean Democratic Women's Union on the 4th, stating: "Chinese women express great concern and full support for the recent appeal made by our Korean sisters to women around the world. We warmly endorse the joint statement of the Chinese and Korean governments and fully agree with the decision of the Chinese People's Volunteers to withdraw from Korea."

The National Women Activists' Congress held recently in Pyongyang appealed to women around the world to support the struggle of Korean women demanding the withdrawal of US troops and all foreign troops from South Korea and the peaceful reunification of Korea.

The All-China Women's Federation's telegram stated that the demands of Korean women are not only related to the happiness of the people but also closely related to the peace of the Far East and the world. The telegram said: "We believe that your righteous call will surely receive the active support of women around the world."

The All-China Women's Federation said in the telegram: "The friendship between Chinese and Korean women has been established and developed in the common struggle. Your struggle is our struggle, and your victory is our victory. We will always stand shoulder to shoulder with you to defend peace in the Far East and the world and strive to promote the peaceful reunification of Korea."

### 一切外国军队应撤出朝鲜  我妇联支持朝鲜妇女呼吁

1958-03-06
第6版()
专栏：

    一切外国军队应撤出朝鲜
    我妇联支持朝鲜妇女呼吁
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会4日打电报给朝鲜民主妇女同盟说：“中国妇女对朝鲜姊妹们最近向全世界妇女发出的呼吁，表示极大的关怀和全力的支持。我们热烈拥护中朝两国政府的联合声明，并且完全同意中国人民志愿军撤出朝鲜的决定。”
    最近在平壤举行的朝鲜全国妇女积极分子奋起大会，曾经呼吁全世界妇女支持朝鲜妇女要求美军和一切外国军队撤出南朝鲜和和平统一朝鲜的斗争。
    全国妇联的电报说，朝鲜妇女的要求不仅有关人民的幸福，也密切关连到远东和世界的和平。电报说：“我们相信，你们正义的呼声，一定会获得全世界妇女的积极支持。”
    全国妇联在电报中说：“中朝妇女之间的友谊是在共同的战斗中建立和发展起来的，你们的斗争就是我们的斗争，你们的胜利就是我们的胜利，我们将永远和你们肩并肩地保卫远东和世界和平，并为促进朝鲜的和平统一而努力。”
