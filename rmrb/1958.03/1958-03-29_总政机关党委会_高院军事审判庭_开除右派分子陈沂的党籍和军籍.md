Title: General Political Department Organs Party Committee, Supreme People's Court Military Tribunal: Expulsion of Rightist Chen Yi from the Party and Military

Author:

Date: 1958-03-29
Page: 4

    The General Political Department Organs Party Committee decided, and it has been approved by the General Political Department and the Party Central Committee, to expel the rightist Chen Yi from the Party.
    Furthermore, the Military Tribunal of the Supreme People's Court of the People's Republic of China, based on accusations, believes that the rightist Chen Yi's crimes of opposing the Party and socialism are serious and that he has completely lost the morality that a revolutionary soldier should possess. On March 6th, it was sentenced to strip Chen Yi of the rank of Major General of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and expel him from the military.

### 总政机关党委会　高院军事审判庭  开除右派分子陈沂的党籍和军籍

1958-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    总政机关党委会　高院军事审判庭
    开除右派分子陈沂的党籍和军籍
    本报讯　总政治部机关党委会决定并已经总政治部和党中央批准，将党内右派分子陈沂开除出党。
    又讯　中华人民共和国最高人民法院军事审判庭根据控告，认为右派分子陈沂反党反社会主义罪行严重，完全丧失革命军人应有的品德，已于3月6日判决，剥夺陈沂中国人民解放军少将军衔，并开除军籍。
