Title: Fang Ming to Attend the Annual Conference of the National Union of Teachers in Britain
Author:
Date: 1958-03-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th: Fang Ming, representative and Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China, left Beijing by plane today for Britain at the invitation of the National Union of Teachers of Britain to attend the annual conference of the British Teachers' Union held in Scarborough from April 5th to 10th.

### 方明赴英参加英国全国教师工会年会

1958-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    方明赴英参加英国全国教师工会年会
    新华社28日讯　中国教育工会全国委员会代表、副主席方明，应英国全国教师工会的邀请，今天乘飞机离开北京去英国，参加英国教师工会4月5日至10日在斯卡巴罗举行的年会。
