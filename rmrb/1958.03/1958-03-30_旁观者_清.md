Foreign Press Clippings

    The Bystander Sees Clearly
    The fanatical party members of the South African Federation National Party firmly believe that they can rely on violence to suppress the resistance of Africans.
    In the short term, they can do this. However, the ruthless suppression of the will of the majority by a minority will eventually backfire. In the long run, the power of Africans is strong. The result of this police state persecuting Africans is bound to arouse their fierce resistance.
        ——British "News Chronicle"

### “旁观者”清

1958-03-30
第3版()
专栏：外国报刊拾零

    “旁观者”清
    南非联邦国民党的狂热的党徒们，深信他们可以依靠暴力来镇压非洲人的反抗。
    短时期内他们可以这样做。不过，少数人对大多数人的意志采取无情镇压手段，最后必定会自食其果。从长远来看，非洲人的力量是强大的。这个警察国家迫害非洲人的结果，势必激起他们的猛烈反抗。
        ——英国“新闻纪事报”
