Title: Beijing Cotton Textile United Factory Established
Author:
Date: 1958-03-29
Page: 3

    Beijing Cotton Textile United Factory Established
    (By our newspaper) The Beijing Cotton Textile United Factory was established on March 28th. This factory was formed by merging the original state-owned First, Second, and Third Cotton Textile Factories, with a total of more than 13,000 employees. The factory equipment includes more than 234,000 spindles and more than 7,200 looms. These equipment capacities can produce 170,000 bales of yarn and 210 million meters of cloth annually. After the establishment of the united factory, it can save a lot of manpower, materials, and financial resources for the country, and better implement the principle of more, faster, better, and more economical.

### 北京棉纺织联合厂成立

1958-03-29
第3版()
专栏：

    北京棉纺织联合厂成立
    本报讯　北京棉纺织联合工厂在3月28日成立。这个工厂是由原来国营第一、第二、第三三个棉纺织工厂合并成的，共有职工一万三千多人。工厂设备有二十三万四千多枚纱锭，七千二百多台织布机器。这些设备能力每年能够生产十七万件纱，二亿一千万公尺布。联合工厂成立后，可以为国家节省大量的人力、物力、财力，更好地贯彻多快好省的方针。
