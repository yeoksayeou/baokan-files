Title: Bulgaria Successfully Completes Second Five-Year Plan; Industrial Output Increases by 76 Percent; Agricultural Cooperativization Basically Completed
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-17
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, March 15th: The Central Statistical Bureau of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers published a communique on the results of the implementation of Bulgaria's Second Five-Year Plan (1953-1957) on March 15th. The communique stated that, compared with 1952, Bulgaria's pig iron production increased 5.5 times, steel production increased 10 times, and lead production increased 6 times in 1957, at the end of the Five-Year Plan. The output of electricity, coal, and other industrial products also greatly increased.

The communique pointed out that during the Second Five-Year Plan, Bulgaria's total investment in capital construction in various sectors of the national economy increased by 80% compared with the First Five-Year Plan.

The task of increasing the total industrial output of the republic, local areas, and cooperatives, put forward by the Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party, was greatly overfulfilled. In 1957, their total industrial output value (calculated according to the new prices of 1956) increased by 76% compared with 1952, and tripled compared with 1948.

During the Second Five-Year Plan, the power generation capacity of power stations belonging to the Bulgarian Ministry of Electricity and Water Resources nearly doubled.

During the Second Five-Year Plan, Bulgaria began oil extraction. Compared with 1952, the mining volume of lead-zinc ore increased by 1.7 times in 1957, and copper ore increased by 2.8 times.

The communique also mentioned the production achievements of machine manufacturing, metal processing, chemical, food, and daily necessities industries.

When talking about the development achievements in agriculture, the communique pointed out that Bulgaria had basically completed agricultural cooperativization during the Second Five-Year Plan. By the end of last year, agricultural production cooperatives had possessed four-fifths of the country's cultivated land and nearly six-fifths of the sown area. Thus, in Bulgaria, socialism has achieved victory not only in the cities but also in the countryside. In 1957, Bulgaria's foreign trade volume more than doubled compared with 1952.

The communique said that, according to preliminary statistics, Bulgaria's national income increased by 46% in 1957 compared with 1952, and the actual income of workers increased by almost 55% on average.

### 保胜利完成第二个五年计划  工业产值增加百分之七十六  农业合作化已经基本上完成

1958-03-17
第5版()
专栏：

    保胜利完成第二个五年计划
    工业产值增加百分之七十六
    农业合作化已经基本上完成
    据新华社索非亚15日电  保加利亚部长会议中央统计局15日发表了保加利亚第二个五年（1953—1957年）计划执行结果的公报。公报说，和1952年比较，在五年计划结束时的1957年，保加利亚的生铁产量增加了五点五倍，钢材产量增加了十倍，铅产量增加了六倍。电力、煤和其他工业品的产量也大大增加了。
    公报指出，在第二个五年计划期间，保加利亚用在国民经济各部门的基本建设投资总额比第一个五年计划增加了80％。
    保加利亚共产党第六次代表大会所提出的增加共和国、地方和合作社的工业总产量的任务大大超额完成了。1957年，它们的工业总产值（根据1956年的新价格计算）比1952年增加了76％，比1948年增加了三倍。
    在第二个五年计划期间，保加利亚电力和水利部所属的发电站的发电能力增加了将近一倍。
    在第二个五年计划期间，保加利亚开始进行了石油的开采工作。和1952年比较，1957年铅—锌矿的开采量增加了一点七倍，铜矿的开采量增加了二点八倍。
    公报还谈到了机器制造、金属加工、化学、食品和日用品工业的生产成就。
    公报在谈到农业的发展成就时指出，在第二个五年计划期间，保加利亚已经基本上完成了农业合作化。到去年年底，农业生产合作社已经拥有全国五分之四的耕地和将近六分之五的播种面积。这样，在保加利亚，社会主义不仅在城市而且在农村也取得了胜利。1957年，保加利亚的对外贸易额比1952年增加了一倍多。
    公报说，据初步统计，和1952年相比，1957年保加利亚的国民收入增加了46％，工人的实际收入差不多平均增加了55％。
