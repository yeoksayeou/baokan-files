Title: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej to Visit Burma

Author:
Date: 1958-03-26
Page: 6

    Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej
    To Visit Burma
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 25th: At the invitation of the government of the Union of Burma, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, will arrive in Rangoon on March 28th. He will pay a three-day friendly visit to Burma.
    During his stay in Burma, Chairman Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej will visit Rangoon and Taunggyi, the capital of Shan State.

### 基伏·斯托伊卡将访问缅甸

1958-03-26
第6版()
专栏：

    基伏·斯托伊卡
    将访问缅甸
    新华社仰光25日电　罗马尼亚部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡应缅甸联邦政府的邀请，将在3月28日到达仰光。他将在缅甸作为时三天的友好访问。
    基伏·斯托伊卡主席在缅甸停留期间，将访问仰光和掸邦首府东枝。
