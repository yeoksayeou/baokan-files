Title: Algerian National Liberation Front Representative Ghaffar Departs for Our Country
Author:
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 5

    Algerian National Liberation Front Representative
    Ghaffar Departs for Our Country
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 23rd. Ibrahim Ghaffar, a representative of the Algerian National Liberation Front, departed from Cairo on the evening of the 22nd to go to China to participate in the preparatory work for Algerian Day, which will be held on March 30th.
    This is the first visit to China by a member of the Algerian National Liberation Front.

### 阿尔及利亚解放阵线代表  加法启程来我国

1958-03-25
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚解放阵线代表
    加法启程来我国
    新华社开罗23日讯　阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线的一位代表易卜拉欣·加法22日晚间从开罗启程前往中国参加将在3月30日举行的阿尔及利亚日的筹备工作。
    这是阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线的成员第一次访问中国。
