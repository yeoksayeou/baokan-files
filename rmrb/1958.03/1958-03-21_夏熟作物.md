Lexical Explanations

    Summer Harvest Crops
    Summer harvest crops generally include: wheat, barley, rapeseed, broad beans, peas, etc. Summer harvest crops are also called: summer crops, spring flowering crops, mid-season crops, and minor spring crops.

专栏：名词解释

    夏熟作物
    夏熟作物一般包括：小麦、大麦、油菜、蚕豆、豌豆等。夏熟作物又叫：夏收作物、春花作物、午季作物、小春作物。
