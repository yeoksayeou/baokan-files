Title: Resolutely Oppose Further Nuclear Weapons Testing: People of Soviet Union, Vietnam, and Other Countries Determined to Intensify Struggle
Author:
Date: 1958-03-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 2nd - The Soviet Peace Committee and the Soviet Asian Countries Solidarity Committee held a meeting on the evening of March 1st at the Central House of Art Workers in Moscow. The meeting adopted a resolution expressing support for the Soviet government's efforts to ban atomic and hydrogen weapons and to halt nuclear testing, and called on the people of all countries to intensify their struggle to ban such weapons. Participants in the meeting also called on the US and British governments to agree to the Soviet Union's humanitarian proposal to halt nuclear testing.
	The meeting was held in response to the call of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference held in Cairo last year to designate March 1 as a common day of action for the people of all countries to strive for the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons. Hundreds of representatives from all walks of life in Moscow attended the meeting.
	The meeting unanimously adopted a resolution calling on the people of all countries to unite and strive for the prohibition of atomic weapons and for lasting peace. The meeting also adopted a telegram to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, hoping that the United Nations would do everything possible to promote the realization of the prohibition of atomic weapons.
	On March 1, mass rallies were also held in Leningrad and Frunze, the capital of the Georgian Republic, resolutely opposing the continued testing of thermonuclear weapons and demanding an immediate and unconditional halt to the production of such weapons.
	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 2nd - The people of all strata in Hanoi adopted a resolution at a grand rally held at the Hanoi Grand Theater on the evening of March 1, demanding the prohibition of nuclear weapons testing by the United States on Eniwetok Atoll.
	Nguyen Xuan Thuy, head of the Vietnamese delegation to the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference, spoke at the meeting. In his speech, Nguyen Xuan Thuy pointed out the enormous harm of atomic warfare and criticized the United States for rejecting the peace initiatives recently proposed by the Soviet Union, Poland, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and China.
	Prominent social figures, women's representatives, and political leaders from Hanoi also spoke at the meeting.
	On March 1 - the day the Vietnamese people demanded a struggle to ban nuclear tests on Eniwetok Atoll, report meetings with the participation of people from all walks of life were also held at the Hanoi Solidarity Club and the Labor Club. Mass rallies and report meetings were also held in major cities in northern Vietnam.
	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 1st - Vietnamese newspapers published on March 1 a joint statement issued by twenty-six people's organizations and political parties across Vietnam, condemning the United States for planning to conduct nuclear weapons tests on Eniwetok Atoll.
	Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, March 1st - People from all walks of life in Ulaanbaatar held a rally on the evening of March 1, supporting the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference's decision to oppose the testing and use of nuclear weapons, and supporting the World People's Conference for Disarmament and Peaceful Coexistence to be held this year.
	Jargal, Secretary-General of the Mongolian Peace Protection Committee, said at the meeting that the people of the world are waging a struggle against the use of nuclear weapons. US imperialism is building nuclear weapons bases everywhere, which seriously threatens world peace. The people of the world will never forget the tragedy caused by the US imperialism dropping atomic bombs on Hiroshima, Japan.
	Xinhua News Agency, March 2nd - According to TASS, Beirut, March 1st: The Lebanese Peace Protection Committee issued a statement on holding a day to stop the production and testing of nuclear weapons.
	The statement said that Lebanese peace advocates support the resolution of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference against the production and testing of nuclear weapons. The statement said that these resolutions are in line with the aspirations of the people of the Near East countries.
	The statement emphasized that Lebanese peace advocates have repeatedly pointed out the serious danger of the arms race and the establishment of atomic weapons and rocket bases to the country. The statement said that Lebanese peace advocates call on all citizens, regardless of faction, to take joint action to prevent the establishment of rocket bases and nuclear weapons depots in Lebanon, thereby eliminating the danger of atomic war and realizing the beautiful future for all mankind - the desire of all nations for peace, security and happiness. ### 坚决反对继续试验核武器  苏越等国人民决心加紧斗争

1958-03-03
第5版()
专栏：

    坚决反对继续试验核武器
    苏越等国人民决心加紧斗争
    苏联
    新华社莫斯科2日电　苏联保卫和平委员会和苏联亚洲国家团结委员会1日晚在莫斯科中央艺术工作者之家举行了会议。会议通过决议，表示支持苏联政府为争取禁止原子武器和氢武器及停止核试验所作的努力，并且呼吁各国人民加紧斗争，争取禁止这种武器。大会参加者还呼吁美国和英国政府同意苏联关于停止核试验的人道主义建议。
    这次会议是为了响应去年在开罗举行的亚非人民团结大会关于决定3月1日为各国人民争取禁止原子武器和氢武器共同行动日的号召而举行的。参加会议的有莫斯科各界代表数百人。
    会议一致通过决议，号召各国人民一致起来争取禁止原子武器和争取持久和平。会议还通过致联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德的电报，希望联合国尽一切可能促进禁止原子武器的实现。
    3月1日，列宁格勒和格鲁吉亚共和国首都伏龙芝也举行了群众集会，坚决反对继续试验热核武器，并且要求立即无条件地停止生产这种武器。
    越南
    据新华社河内2日电　河内各阶层人民1日晚间在河内大戏院举行的盛大集会上通过决议，要求禁止美国在恩纳威托克岛进行核武器试验。
    越南出席亚非人民团结大会代表团团长阮春水在会上讲了话。阮春水在讲话中指出原子战争的巨大危害，并且指责美国拒绝苏联、波兰、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和中国在最近提出的和平倡议。
    河内的社会著名人士、妇女代表和政界领袖也在大会上讲了话。
    在3月1日——越南人民要求禁止在恩纳威托克岛进行核试验斗争日这天，在河内团结俱乐部和劳动俱乐部还举行了有各界人民参加的报告会。越南北方各大城市也举行了群众大会和报告会。
    新华社河内1日电　越南各报1日刊载了越南全国二十六个人民团体和政党发表的联合声明，谴责美国要在恩纳威托克岛进行核武器试验。
    蒙古
    新华社乌兰巴托1日电　乌兰巴托市各界人民1日晚上举行集会，支持亚非人民团结大会关于反对试验和使用核武器的决定，并且支持今年召开争取裁军与和平共处的世界人民大会。
    蒙古保卫和平委员会秘书长扎格德在会上说，世界人民正进行着反对使用核武器的斗争。美帝国主义竟在各地建立核武器基地，这严重地威胁着世界和平。世界人民永远忘不了美帝国主义在日本广岛投下原子弹所造成的惨剧。
    黎巴嫩
    据新华社2日讯　据塔斯社贝鲁特1日讯：黎巴嫩保卫和平委员会就举行停止生产和试验核武器日发表了一项声明。
    声明说，黎巴嫩和平拥护者拥护亚非人民团结大会关于反对生产和试验核武器的决议。声明说，这些决议是符合近东各国人民的愿望的。
    声明着重指出，黎巴嫩和平拥护者一再指出了军备竞赛和建立原子武器和火箭基地对于国家的严重危险。声明说，黎巴嫩和平拥护者呼吁全体公民不分派别，采取共同行动，不允许在黎巴嫩建立火箭基地和核武器仓库，从而消除爆发原子战争的危险，实现全人类对美好的将来——各国人民的和平、安全和幸福的愿望。（附图片）
