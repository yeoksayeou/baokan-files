Title: Polish Guests Return to Beijing from Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1958-03-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, March 28th. The government delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, headed by Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz, accompanied by Yang Yingjie, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission of China, and Kiryluk, the Polish Ambassador to China, left Shenyang by special plane at 8:30 this morning to return to Beijing.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included Qiu Youwen, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province, and others.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th. The government delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, headed by Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz, returned to Beijing this morning.
    Those who welcomed them at the airport included Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, and Li Zheren, Vice Minister, as well as Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    Members of the Polish government delegation: Trumpczynski, Minister of Foreign Trade, and Koi, Vice Minister of Heavy Industry, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 波兰贵宾由沈阳回到北京

1958-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰贵宾由沈阳回到北京
    新华社沈阳28日电　以雅罗谢维奇副主席为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团，在我国国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰、波兰驻中国大使基里洛克陪同下，今天早晨八点三十分乘专机离开沈阳回北京。
    到机场送行的，有辽宁省副省长仇友文等。
    新华社28日讯　以雅罗谢维奇副主席为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团已经在今天上午回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长李哲人，外交部副部长曾涌泉。
    波兰政府代表团团员：对外贸易部部长特朗普钦斯基和重工业部副部长科依，也到机场欢迎。
