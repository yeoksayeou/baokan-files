Title: Soviet Communist Party Delegation Leaves Italy for Home

Author: 
Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Rome, March 5th: According to TASS, the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, headed by P.N Pospelov, Alternate Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Secretary of the Central Committee, left Rome on the 4th to return home after a visit of nearly three weeks in Italy.
    The delegation's visit to Italy, which began on February 13, was a return visit to the Italian Communist Party delegation that visited the Soviet Union last summer.

### 苏共代表团离意返国

1958-03-06
第6版()
专栏：

    苏共代表团离意返国
    据新华社5日讯　据塔斯社罗马讯：以苏联共产党中央委员会主席团候补委员、中央委员会书记波斯伯洛夫为首的苏联共产党中央委员会代表团，在意大利进行将近三个星期的访问后，在4日离开罗马回国。
    代表团从2月13日起在意大利的访问，是对去年夏季访问苏联的意大利共产党代表团的回访。
