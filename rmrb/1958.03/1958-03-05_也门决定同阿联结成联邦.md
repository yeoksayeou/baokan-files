Title: Yemen Decides to Form a Federation with the United Arab Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-03-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, March 4th: It was announced in Damascus that Arab United Republic President Nasser and Yemen Deputy Prime Minister Crown Prince Badr announced on the 2nd in Damascus, that Yemen has decided to form a federation with the newly established Arab United Republic.
　　
They announced this decision together from the balcony of the Syrian Guest Palace to several thousand people. During his speech, Nasser expressed his welcome to Yemen joining the Arab United Republic on a federal basis. During his speech, Badr said, "This union of Egypt and Syria, and this federation of the Arab United Republic and Yemen, is the result of a long struggle against imperialism."
　　
Badr later told reporters, "The agreement to establish the federation will be signed as soon as the cabinet of the Arab United Republic is formed."

### 也门决定同阿联结成联邦

1958-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    也门决定同阿联结成联邦
    新华社4日讯　大马士革消息：阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔和也门副首相巴德尔王太子2日在大马士革宣布，也门已经决定同新成立的阿拉伯联合共和国结成联邦。
    他们是一起在叙利亚迎宾宫的阳台上向几千名群众讲话时，先后宣布这个决定的。纳赛尔在讲话时，表示欢迎也门在联邦基础上加入阿拉伯联合共和国。巴德尔在讲话时说，“埃及和叙利亚的这种联合，以及阿拉伯联合共和国和也门的这种联邦，是反对帝国主义的长期斗争的结果”。
    巴德尔后来对新闻记者说，“等到阿拉伯联合共和国的内阁一成立，就将签订建立联邦的协定”。
