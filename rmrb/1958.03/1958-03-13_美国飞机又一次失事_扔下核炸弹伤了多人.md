Title: U.S. Plane Crashes Again, Dropping Nuclear Bomb, Injuring Many
Author:
Date: 1958-03-13
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, March 12th, Omaha dispatch: The U.S. Strategic Air Command headquarters announced that a U.S. B-47 bomber carrying nuclear weapons, which took off from Hunter Air Force Base in Georgia, dropped an atomic bomb on the evening of the 11th due to a malfunction of the bomb bay door device.

    According to officials from the Strategic Air Command, the bomb landed on a house five miles east of Florence, South Carolina. It is now known that five people were injured as a result.

    After the incident, the U.S. Strategic Air Command has ordered "surveillance" of the area, prohibiting anyone from entering, and ordered explosive ordnance experts to the scene.

    It is revealed that this is the second accident in a month involving a B-47 bomber carrying nuclear weapons from Hunter Air Force Base in Georgia. On February 5 this year, a bomber also dropped a nuclear weapon into the sea off Savannah, Georgia, after a mid-air collision.

### 美国飞机又一次失事  扔下核炸弹伤了多人

1958-03-13
第5版()
专栏：

    美国飞机又一次失事
    扔下核炸弹伤了多人
    新华社12日讯  内布拉斯加州奥马哈消息：美国战略空军司令部总部宣布，从佐治亚州亨特尔空军基地起飞的一架载有核武器的美国B—47型轰炸机由于炸弹闸门装置失灵，在11日傍晚扔下了一颗原子核炸弹。
    据战略空军司令部官员说，这颗炸弹落在南卡罗来纳州弗洛伦斯以东五英里的一所房屋上。现在已经知道有五个人因此受了伤。
    事情发生后，美国战略空军司令部已经下令对这个地区进行“监视”，不许任何人入内，并且命令爆炸军械专家到现场去。
    据透露，这已经是美国亨特尔空军基地载有核武器的B—47型轰炸机在一个月内第二次失事了。今年2月5日，一架轰炸机在空中发生碰撞以后也曾经把一个核武器投到佐治亚州萨瓦纳的海面上。
