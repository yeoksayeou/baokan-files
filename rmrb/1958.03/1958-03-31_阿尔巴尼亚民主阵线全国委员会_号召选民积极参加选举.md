Title: Albanian Democratic Front National Committee Calls on Voters to Actively Participate in Elections
Author:
Date: 1958-03-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, March 29th. The Albanian Democratic Front National Committee held a meeting on the 29th, presided over by Enver Hoxha, Chairman of the Democratic Front. The meeting adopted a call to all voters regarding the People's Assembly election to be held on June 1st this year.

The call pointed out the construction achievements made in various fields in Albania since liberation and in the four years since the last election, as well as the prospects for future development. The call stated that industrial production in 1957 increased by 14.6 times compared to 1938, and agricultural production has also greatly improved. Four years ago, the cultivated area of collectivized agriculture accounted for only 8.3 percent of the total area, but now it has increased to over 58.1 percent. By the end of the Second Five-Year Plan in 1960, the national income will increase by 73% compared to 1955, the actual wages of workers and staff will increase by 35%, and the income of farmers will increase by 45%. The call also talked about Albania's peaceful foreign policy.

The call finally called on the people to actively participate in the election and vote for the candidates of the Democratic Front.

### 阿尔巴尼亚民主阵线全国委员会  号召选民积极参加选举

1958-03-31
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚民主阵线全国委员会
    号召选民积极参加选举
    新华社地拉那29日电　阿尔巴尼亚民主阵线全国委员会29日在民主阵线主席恩·霍查主持下举行了会议。会议就今年6月1日将要举行的人民议会选举通过了给全体选民的号召书。
    号召书指出了阿尔巴尼亚解放以来和自上次选举后四年来在各方面所取得的建设成就以及今后的发展前景。号召书说，1957年工业生产比1938年增加了十四点六倍，农业生产也大大提高了。四年前集体化农业的耕地面积只占总面积的百分之八点三，现在已经增加到百分之五十八点一强。到第二个五年计划末的1960年，国民收入将比1955年增加73％，职工实际工资将提高35％，农民收入将增加45％。号召书还谈到了阿尔巴尼亚的和平外交政策。
    号召书最后号召人民积极参加选举和投民主阵线候选人的票。
