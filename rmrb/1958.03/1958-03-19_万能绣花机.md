Title: Versatile Embroidery Machine
Author:

Page: 3

New Products

    Versatile Embroidery Machine

    A machine that can embroider beautiful patterns like a girl's skillful hands – the versatile embroidery machine – has been trial-manufactured by the Guangzhou Public-Private Joint Venture Huanan Sewing Machine Factory and is about to go into production.

    This embroidery machine has an automatic embroidering device installed at the tail end of a versatile sewing machine. This embroidering device is equipped with adjustable discs for embroidering various patterns. Each time a disc is changed, more than thirty different shapes of patterns, such as semi-circles, dog-tooth shapes, and olive-shaped patterns, can be embroidered, and they are neat and beautiful.
                  (Xinhua News Agency Dispatch)

### 万能绣花机

1958-03-19
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    万能绣花机
    一种像姑娘的巧手能刺绣出漂亮花样的机器——万能绣花机，已经由广州公私合营华南缝纫机制造厂试制出来，并且即将投入生产。
    这种绣花机是在万能缝纫机的尾部装上一个自动绣花器，在这个绣花器上装有刺绣各种花纹的调节饼，每换一饼，就可以刺绣出不同形状的图案花纹如半圆形、狗牙形、榄核形等共三十多种，而且整齐美观。
                  （新华社专稿）
