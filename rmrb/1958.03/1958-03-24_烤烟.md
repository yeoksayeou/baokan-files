Flue-cured tobacco
    Flue-cured tobacco is tobacco leaf that is artificially baked. It is golden in color, has a strong aroma, and is the main raw material of the cigarette industry. Tobacco leaf used for smoking in dry pipes is sun-dried and is called sun-cured tobacco. (Attached picture)

### 烤烟

1958-03-24
第2版()
专栏：名词解释

    烤烟
    烤烟是用人工烘烤而成的烟叶，色金黄，香味浓，是卷烟工业的主要原料。用作旱烟吸用的烟叶是利用太阳光晒制的，叫晒烟。（附图片）
