Title: Needle-Free Injection: Soviet Union Develops Needle-Free Injector
Author:
Date: 1958-03-26
Page: 6

    Needle-Free Injection
    Soviet Union Develops Needle-Free Injector
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 25th – Soviet scientists have developed a "needle-free injector." When using this injector, the person being injected will not feel the unpleasant stinging sensation produced by ordinary injections. One "needle-free injector" can complete two thousand injections in 24 hours.
    This injector was created by Trusov and Ippolitov, staff members of the Moscow Experimental Surgical Instrument and Tool Research Institute. The medicinal liquid in the device is forced through a small opening on the injector into the human body under enormous pressure. The injection process is very fast. Simply place the small opening of the injector against the skin, press the button, and a stream of medicinal liquid will seep under the skin in an instant. Because the medicinal liquid enters the body quickly, the person being injected will not feel a stinging sensation.
    Since this injector does not have the needle that is essential for general injectors, it eliminates the process of boiling the needle for disinfection during use. Therefore, the possibility of spreading infectious diseases due to incomplete disinfection is greatly reduced. (Attached picture)
    This is the Soviet Union's new four-seat helicopter "flying car." This helicopter can transport passengers and mail on short-distance routes and help prevent and control plant diseases and insect pests in agriculture. The helicopter's engine is 250 horsepower, and the cruising speed is 120 kilometers per hour.

### 注射不用针  苏联制成无针注射器

1958-03-26
第6版()
专栏：

    注射不用针
    苏联制成无针注射器
    新华社莫斯科25日电　苏联科学家制成了一种“无针注射器”，用这种注射器注射时，受注射的人不会感到普通注射时所产生的不愉快的刺痛，一个“无针注射器”一昼夜可以完成两千次注射。
    这种注射器是莫斯科实验外科器械和工具研究所工作人员特鲁索夫和伊波利托夫制成的。器械中的药液在巨大压力下经过注射器上的一个小口渗入人体，注射过程十分快。只要把注射器的小口贴在皮肤上，按一下按纽，一股药液就会在一瞬间渗入皮下。因为药液进入人体很快，受注射的人就不会感到刺痛。
    由于这种注射器没有一般注射器所不可少的针头，在使用时就省去了把针头煮沸消毒的过程，因此，由于消毒不彻底而传布传染病的可能性也大大减少了。（附图片）
    这是苏联新式四座位直升飞机“飞行汽车”。这种直升飞机可在短程航线上运送旅客和邮件，在农业上帮助防治病虫害。这种直升飞机的发动机是二百五十马力，巡航速度每小时是一百二十公里。
