New steelmaking technology
    The Tianjin Public-Private Joint Venture Xinxing Steel Plant adopted the alkaline hot-charging three-furnace steelmaking method and trial-produced the first furnace of high-quality steel.
    The alkaline hot-charging three-furnace steelmaking method is to first melt pig iron in a cupola furnace, then pour the molten iron into a converter, and then pour the refined steel into an electric furnace to refine high-quality steel. This steelmaking method shortens the melting time by half compared with using cold-charging electric furnaces, and electricity can also be saved by one-third. (Attached picture)
    Guan Ping (Xinhua News Agency special manuscript)

### 炼钢的新技术

1958-03-25
第3版()
专栏：

    炼钢的新技术
    天津市公私合营新兴钢厂采用碱性热装三联炼钢法，试炼出第一炉优质钢。
    碱性热装三联炼钢法，就是先把生铁放到化铁炉里熔化，再把化好的铁水倒到转炉里，炼好的钢水再倒在电炉里炼优质钢。这种炼钢法熔炼时间比用冷装电炉熔炼时间缩短一半，电力也可节省三分之一。（附图片）
    关平摄（新华社专稿）
