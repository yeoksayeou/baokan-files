Title: Ninety-Nine Point Ninety-Seven Percent of Soviet Voters Participate in Election; Communist and Non-Party Alliance Achieves Complete Victory
Author:
Date: 1958-03-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 17th (TASS dispatch): The Central Election Committee of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union issued a communique stating that, according to preliminary statistics, the Communist and Non-Party alliance achieved a complete victory in the elections held on March 16th.

The communique stated that the election on March 16th was carried out in a political high tide in which the people demonstrated a high degree of political enthusiasm and organization. According to preliminary statistics, 133,594,561 voters, or 99.97% of the total number of voters, participated in the election. In the electoral districts of the Union Council, 99.57% of the voters participated in the election; in the electoral districts of the Council of Nationalities, 99.72% participated, all voting for the candidates for deputies to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union nominated by the Party and Non-Party alliance.

The Central Election Committee will announce the final results of the election on March 19th.

### 苏联百分之九十九点九七选民参加了选举  共产党和非党联盟获全胜

1958-03-19
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联百分之九十九点九七选民参加了选举
    共产党和非党联盟获全胜
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科17日讯：苏联最高苏维埃中央选举委员会发表公报说，根据初步统计，在3月16日举行的选举中，共产党和非党联盟取得了全胜。
    公报说，3月16日的选举是在人民表现了高度的政治积极性和组织性的政治高潮中进行的。据初步统计，有一亿三千三百五十九万四千五百六十一个选民、即选民总数的99.97％参加了选举。联盟院各选区参加选举的选民有99.57％、民族院各选区有99.72％，都投票选举了党和非党联盟提出的苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人。
    中央选举委员会将在3月19日公布选举最后结果。
