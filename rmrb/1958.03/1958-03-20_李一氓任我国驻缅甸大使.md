Title: Li Yimang Appointed as China's Ambassador to Burma

Author: 
Date: 1958-03-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th — Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Li Yimang as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Union of Burma.
    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has relieved Yao Zhongming of his position as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Union of Burma.

### 李一氓任我国驻缅甸大使

1958-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    李一氓任我国驻缅甸大使
    新华社19日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命李一氓为中华人民共和国驻缅甸联邦特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去姚仲明的中华人民共和国驻缅甸联邦特命全权大使的职务。
