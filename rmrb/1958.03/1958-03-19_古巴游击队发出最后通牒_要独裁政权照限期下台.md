Title: Cuban Guerrillas Issue Ultimatum, Demanding Dictatorial Regime Step Down by Deadline
Author:
Date: 1958-03-19
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 18th: Cuban rebel leader Castro issued an ultimatum to the Batista dictatorial regime on the 17th, demanding that Batista step down before April 5th, otherwise the rebel forces will launch a full-scale offensive.

Castro also called on the army, navy, and air force personnel under the Batista government to stage an uprising.

### 古巴游击队发出最后通牒  要独裁政权照限期下台

1958-03-19
第7版()
专栏：

    古巴游击队发出最后通牒
    要独裁政权照限期下台
    据新华社18日讯　哈瓦那消息：古巴起义部队领袖卡斯特罗17日向巴蒂斯塔独裁政权提出最后通牒，要求巴蒂斯塔在4月5日以前下台，否则，起义部队将展开全面进攻。
    卡斯特罗还号召巴蒂斯塔政府下的陆、海、空军人员举行起义。
