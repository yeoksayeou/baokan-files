Title: Yangtze River Delta Power Grid
Author: Bao Qiwei
Date: 1958-03-19
Page: 3

    Construction Blossoms

    Yangtze River Delta Power Grid
    As of the end of last year, the construction project of the Jiangnan Power Grid had completed 398 kilometers of high-voltage transmission lines (353 kilometers at 110 kV and 45 kilometers at 35 kV). The high-voltage transmission lines between Shanghai, Wangting, Nanjing, Ma'anshan, and Tongguanshan have been connected. Power plants in Shanghai, Wangting, Nanjing, Ma'anshan, and other places along this line have been preliminarily unified for dispatch, and electricity consumption between Shanghai and Nanjing has been mutually balanced and adjusted.

    This year, ten high-voltage transmission lines, with a total length of 827 kilometers (291 kilometers at 220 kV and 536 kilometers at 110 kV), will be completed and begin construction.

    The first high-voltage transmission line in the Jiangnan area will soon be constructed between Shanghai and Wangting. At the same time, ultra-high-voltage overhead lines crossing the Yangtze River will be planned and constructed in the central and southern Anhui regions. The designed iron towers will be even taller than the 24-story International Hotel in Shanghai. Once this line is completed, the Jiangnan power grid will be connected to the Huainan power grid, composed of thermal power plants and hydroelectric power stations in Huainan, Foziling, and Meishan, forming a powerful Yangtze River Delta power grid.
      (Bao Qiwei)

### 长江三角洲电力网

1958-03-19
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    长江三角洲电力网
    江南电力网的建设工程，到去年底为止，已经建成了高压输电线路三百九十八公里（十一万伏三百五十三公里，三万五千伏四十五公里），已把上海、望亭、南京、马鞍山和铜官山间的高压送电线路连接起来。在这条线路上的上海、望亭、南京、马鞍山等地的电厂已经初步统一调度，沪宁间的用电已经互相平衡和互相调剂。
    今年要建成的和开始建设的高压输电线路有十条，全长八百二十七公里（二十二万伏二百九十一公里，十一万伏五百三十六公里）。
    江南地区第一条高压输电线路即将在上海—望亭之间施工。与此同时，将在皖中、皖南地区筹建横跨长江的超高压过江架空线，设计中的铁塔比上海二十四层楼国际饭店还要高。这条线路建成以后，江南地区的电力网将和淮南、佛子岭、梅山等地火力发电厂、水电站组成的淮南电力网连接起来，组成强大的长江三角洲大电力网了。
      （包启卫）
