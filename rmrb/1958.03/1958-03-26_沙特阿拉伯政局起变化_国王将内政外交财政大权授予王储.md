Title: Political Situation Changes in Saudi Arabia: King Grants Crown Prince Power Over Internal Affairs, Diplomacy, and Finance
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-26
Page: 5

　　Saudi Arabia's Political Situation Changes
　　King Grants Crown Prince Power Over Internal Affairs, Diplomacy, and Finance

Xinhua News Agency, 25th dispatch from Jiddah: Saudi Arabian King Saud issued a decree on the 23rd, announcing the grant of full authority to Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Faisal to formulate national domestic, foreign, and financial policies, and to supervise the implementation of these policies. Faisal is the Crown Prince of Saudi Arabia and the younger brother of King Saud. He went to the United States and Europe for medical treatment last June and only returned to the country at the end of January this year. Before returning to the country, he visited Egypt for a month and held several talks with Nasser.

### 沙特阿拉伯政局起变化  国王将内政外交财政大权授予王储

1958-03-26
第5版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯政局起变化
    国王将内政外交财政大权授予王储
    新华社25日讯　吉达消息：沙特阿拉伯国王沙特23日颁布敕令，宣布把制订国家内政、外交、财政政策和监督这些政策执行的全权授予首相兼外交大臣费萨尔。费萨尔是沙特阿拉伯王储，国王沙特的弟弟。他曾经在去年6月去美国和欧洲就医，今年1月底才回国。在回国以前，他在埃及访问了一个月，同纳赛尔会谈了几次。
