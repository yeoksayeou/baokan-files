Title: Low Temperature Forecast
Author:
Date: 1958-03-28
Page: 7

    Low Temperature Forecast
    (This newspaper report) The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a low temperature forecast at 7:00 PM on March 27th:
    It is expected that on the morning of the 28th, the minimum temperature in the Huai River basin will drop to -1 to -4 degrees Celsius, the lower reaches of the Yangtze River will drop to around 0 degrees Celsius, the middle reaches of the Yangtze River and Zhejiang will drop to 1 to 3 degrees Celsius, southern Hunan, southern Jiangxi, and northern Fujian will drop to 3 to 6 degrees Celsius, and northern Guangdong, northern Guangxi, and southern Fujian will drop to around 10 degrees Celsius. On the morning of the 29th, the minimum temperatures in the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River and areas south of it will continue to drop somewhat. The minimum temperature in the middle reaches of the Yangtze River and Zhejiang will drop to around 0 degrees Celsius. Southern Hunan, southern Jiangxi, and northern Fujian will drop to 1 to 3 degrees Celsius. Northern Guangdong, northern Guangxi, and southern Fujian will drop to 5 to 7 degrees Celsius. Southern Guangdong and southern Guangxi will drop to around 10 degrees Celsius. From the 28th to the 30th, the temperature in areas north of the Yangtze River will gradually rise, while the temperature in areas south of the Yangtze River will not change significantly.
    In addition, over the next 24 hours, there will still be northeasterly winds of force 6 to 8 in the northeastern part of the South China Sea and the Taiwan Strait, and northeasterly winds of about force 6 in the northwestern part of the South China Sea. Strong winds in other areas will gradually weaken.

### 低温预报

1958-03-28
第7版()
专栏：气象台

    低温预报
    本报讯　中央气象科学研究所3月27日下午七时发布低温预报：
    预计28日早晨，淮河流域的最低气温将下降到摄氏零下一度到零下四度，长江下游将下降到摄氏零度左右，长江中游和浙江将下降到摄氏一度到三度，湖南南部、江西南部和福建北部将下降到摄氏三度到六度，广东北部、广西北部和福建南部将下降到摄氏十度左右。29日早晨，长江中下游及其以南各地的最低气温，仍将继续下降一些，长江中游和浙江的最低气温将下降到摄氏零度左右。湖南南部、江西南部和福建北部将下降到摄氏一度到三度。广东北部、广西北部和福建南部将下降到摄氏五度到七度。广东南部和广西南部将下降到摄氏十度左右。28日到30日，长江以北各地区的气温将逐渐回升，长江以南各地的气温将无大变化。
    另外未来二十四小时内，南海东北部和台湾海峡，仍将有六到八级的东北风，南海西北部仍将有六级左右的东北风，其他各地的大风将逐渐减弱。
