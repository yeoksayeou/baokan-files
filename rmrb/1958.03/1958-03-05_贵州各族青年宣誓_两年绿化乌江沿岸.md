Title: Guizhou Ethnic Youth Pledge to Green the Wujiang River Banks in Two Years
Author:
Date: 1958-03-05
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Guiyang, March 3rd: The Fourth Plenary Session (Enlarged) of the Guizhou Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League decided on the 1st to mobilize the youth of all ethnic groups in the province to exert revolutionary zeal and strive to green the banks of the Wujiang River within two years. On the same day, more than 300 youth representatives from the province and youth from along the Wujiang River held a rally by the Wujiang River to pledge their determination.
    Prior to this, the Zunyi County Committee of the Communist Youth League had organized more than 10,000 young people from February 11th to 14th to green more than 48,000 mu of barren hillside suitable for afforestation on the north bank of the Wujiang River.
    The Wujiang River is located in the north of Guizhou Province, flowing through 35 counties in the Bijie, Anshun, Tongren and other regions. It is 838 kilometers long within the province (1,018 kilometers in total) and is the largest river in Guizhou Province. There are more than one million mu of barren mountains suitable for afforestation on both sides of the river.

### 贵州各族青年宣誓  两年绿化乌江沿岸

1958-03-05
第2版()
专栏：

    贵州各族青年宣誓
    两年绿化乌江沿岸
    据新华社贵阳3日电　正在共青团贵州省委员会第四次全体委员扩大会议，在1日决定动员全省各族青年鼓足革命干劲，力争两年内绿化乌江沿岸。同一天，全省青年代表和乌江沿岸的青年三百多人，在乌江边举行誓师大会。
    在这以前，共青团遵义县委已组织了一万多名青年从2月11日到14日在乌江北岸绿化了宜林荒山坡地四万八千多亩。
    乌江位于贵州省北部，流经毕节、安顺、铜仁等专区的三十五个县，在省境内共长八百三十八公里（全长一千零十八公里），是贵州省第一条大河。河流两岸有宜林荒山一百多万亩。
