Title: Pakistan Celebrates National Day
Author:
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, March 23rd: Pakistan celebrated the second anniversary of the founding of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan nationwide on the 23rd. Parades were held in the capital, Karachi, and other cities.

President Mirza delivered a short congratulatory message to the army, navy, and air force this morning and reviewed the troops participating in the parade.

### 巴基斯坦庆祝国庆

1958-03-25
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦庆祝国庆
    据新华社卡拉奇23日电巴基斯坦全国23日庆祝巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立两周年。在首都卡拉奇和其他一些城市都举行了阅兵典礼。
    米尔扎总统这天上午向陆、海、空三军发表简短祝词，并且检阅了参加阅兵式的部队。
