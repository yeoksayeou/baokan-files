Title: Albanian Labor Party Organizes Study Group to Study CPSU Experience
Author:
Date: 1958-03-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 22nd: According to the Albanian "People's Voice" newspaper, in order to further study the experience of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Albanian Labor Party organized a study group that left for the Soviet Union on the 18th.

The study group is led by K. Kabo, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, and its members include staff from various departments of the Central Committee of the Party and local party organizations.

### 阿劳动党组织学习组到苏联学习苏共经验

1958-03-24
第6版()
专栏：

    阿劳动党组织学习组
    到苏联学习苏共经验
    据新华社地拉那22日电　据阿尔巴尼亚“人民之声报”报道：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党为进一步学习苏联共产党的经验，组织了一个学习组于18日前往苏联。
  学习组由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会书记卡博率领，成员有党中央各部门和地方党组织的工作人员。
