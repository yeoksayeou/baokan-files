Title: Albanian Parliament Meets
Author:
Date: 1958-03-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, March 17th. The Seventh Session of the Albanian People's Assembly opened in Tirana on the 17th. The meeting will last for three days.

    The agenda of the meeting includes discussion and adoption of reports on the current international situation and Albania's foreign policy, reports on Albania's economic development plan for 1958 and the national budget for 1958, reports on the revision of the People's Assembly election law and the civil procedure law, and adoption of orders issued by the Presidium of the People's Assembly, etc.
    At the meeting held on the 17th, a report on the revision of Articles 44 and 46 of the Constitution was adopted. According to the amended Constitution, the number of representatives to the People's Assembly will be changed from one representative for every 10,000 residents as previously stipulated to one representative for every 8,000 residents. The number of vice-chairmen of the People's Assembly will be changed from one to two as previously stipulated.

### 阿尔巴尼亚议会开会

1958-03-19
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚议会开会
    新华社地拉那17日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会第七次会议17日在地拉那开幕。会议将进行三天。
    会议议程包括讨论和通过当前国际形势和阿尔巴尼亚外交政策的报告、1958年阿尔巴尼亚经济发展计划和1958年国家预算的报告、关于修正人民议会选举法和民事诉讼法的报告以及通过人民议会主席团所发布的命令等。
    在17日举行的会议上，通过了关于修正宪法第四十四条和第四十六条的报告。根据修正后的宪法，人民议会的代表将从过去所规定的每一万居民中选出一名改为每八千居民中选出一名。人民议会的副主席将从过去所规定的一人改为二人。
