Title: Published by the Nationalities Publishing House: Pictorial Album of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1958-03-07
Page: 7

Publishing News

Published by the Nationalities Publishing House
Pictorial Album of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region

This newspaper reports: To welcome the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, the Nationalities Publishing House has published the "Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region" pictorial album. This pictorial album introduces the achievements of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region in political, economic, and cultural construction since liberation, the new life of the people of all ethnic groups in the autonomous region, and the efforts of the people of all ethnic groups to build socialism. The book is divided into sections such as: beautiful scenery, a long history and glorious revolutionary tradition, rich agricultural and forestry resources, emerging industry and mining, and comprehensive transportation, as well as developing trade, cultural and educational undertakings, and folk arts.

### 民族出版社出版  广西僮族自治区画集

1958-03-07
第7版()
专栏：出版消息

    民族出版社出版
    广西僮族自治区画集
    本报讯　为了迎接广西僮族自治区的成立，民族出版社出版了“广西僮族自治区”画集。这个画集介绍了解放以来广西僮族自治区在政治、经济、文化方面建设的成就，和自治区各民族人民的新的生活面貌以及各族人民为建设社会主义而努力的情况。全书分美丽的风光、悠久的历史和光荣的革命传统、富饶的农业和森林资源、新兴的工矿业和四通八达的交通运输，以及发展中的贸易、文化教育事业、民间文艺等辑。
