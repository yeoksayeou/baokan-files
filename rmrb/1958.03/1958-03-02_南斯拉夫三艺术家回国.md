Title: Three Yugoslav Artists Return Home
Author:
Date: 1958-03-02
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Three Yugoslav artists, Marijan Lipovšek, Karol Ljupe, and Bruno Brun, who came to China for a visit under the 1957 executive plan of the Sino-Yugoslav Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by plane on March 1st to return to their country.
    Vice Minister of Culture Zhang Zhixiang and others went to the airport to see them off.

### 南斯拉夫三艺术家回国

1958-03-02
第2版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫三艺术家回国
    新华社1日讯　根据中南文化合作协定1957年执行计划来中国访问的南斯拉夫艺术家马立扬·里波夫赛克、卡罗·卢佩尔、布鲁洛·布鲁恩等三人，在3月1日乘飞机离北京回国。
    文化部副部长张致祥等曾到机场欢送。
