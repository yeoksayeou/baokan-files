Title: Chairman Mao Cables President Bourguiba to Congratulate Tunisia on its Second Anniversary of Independence
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to Tunisian President Habib Bourguiba, congratulating Tunisia on its second anniversary of independence. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Habib Bourguiba, President of Tunisia:

    On the occasion of the second anniversary of Tunisia's independence, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of your country on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Tunisian people new victories in their struggle to safeguard national sovereignty and oppose imperialism. May the friendship between the Chinese and Tunisian peoples grow stronger day by day. I wish the Republic of Tunisia prosperity and its people happiness.

                          Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                            March 19, 1958

### 毛主席电布尔吉巴总统  祝贺突尼斯独立二周年

1958-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电布尔吉巴总统
    祝贺突尼斯独立二周年
    新华社19日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电突尼斯总统哈比布·布尔吉巴，祝贺突尼斯独立二周年。贺电如下：突尼斯总统哈比布·布尔吉巴阁下阁下：
    欣逢突尼斯独立二周年纪念日，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和贵国人民表示热烈的祝贺。祝突尼斯人民在维护国家主权、反对帝国主义的斗争中获得新的胜利。祝中、突两国人民的友谊日益增进。祝突尼斯共和国繁荣、人民幸福。
                          中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                            1958年3月19日
