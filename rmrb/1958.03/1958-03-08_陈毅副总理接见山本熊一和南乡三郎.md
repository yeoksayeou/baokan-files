Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Yamamoto Kumao and Nango Saburo
Author:
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council received Yamamoto Kumao, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and Nango Saburo, Chairman of the Japan-China Export and Import Association, this afternoon. During the meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi spoke about the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement, and said that the future of Sino-Japanese trade is broad. Liao Chengzhi and Lei Renmin were present at the reception.

### 陈毅副总理接见山本熊一和南乡三郎

1958-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见山本熊一和南乡三郎
    新华社7日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天下午接见了日本国际贸易促进协会会长山本熊一、日本日中输出入组合理事长南乡三郎。陈毅副总理在接见时谈到了中日第四次贸易协定，并且说，中日贸易的前途是广阔的。接见时在座的有廖承志、雷任民。
