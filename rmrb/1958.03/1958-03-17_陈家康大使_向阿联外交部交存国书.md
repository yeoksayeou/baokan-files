Title: Ambassador Chen Jiakang Presents Credentials to the UAR Foreign Ministry
Author:
Date: 1958-03-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 15th: Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, Chen Jiakang, presented his credentials to the Chief of Protocol of the Foreign Ministry of the United Arab Republic on the 15th.

After presenting his new credentials, Ambassador Chen Jiakang also visited the Presidential Palace to offer his congratulations.

### 陈家康大使  向阿联外交部交存国书

1958-03-17
第5版()
专栏：

    陈家康大使
    向阿联外交部交存国书
    据新华社开罗15日电　中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康15日向阿拉伯联合共和国外交部交际处处长交存了新国书。
    在交存新国书以后，陈家康大使还拜访了总统府。表示祝贺。
