Title: Soviet Ambassador to the US Visits the US President
Author:
Date: 1958-03-05
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 4th. TASS Washington dispatch: Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Menshikov, called on US President Eisenhower on the 3rd and had a long talk with him. Secretary of State Dulles was also present during the conversation.

### 苏驻美大使访美国总统

1958-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    苏驻美大使访美国总统
    新华社4日讯　塔斯社华盛顿讯：苏联驻美国大使缅希科夫3日拜会了美国总统艾森豪威尔，并且和他作了长谈。谈话时在座的还有国务卿杜勒斯。
