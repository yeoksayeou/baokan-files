    Yue Ye Completes "Friends and Enemies"
    Following "Sharing Joys and Sorrows," playwright Yue Ye has completed another new play, "Friends and Enemies." This is a drama praising the great friendship between China and the Soviet Union. It expresses the deep friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union through the flesh-and-blood relationship between a Soviet officer and a Chinese worker. The play depicts this pair of friends, who are closer than brothers, working side-by-side, overcoming many difficulties and defeating the sabotage of hidden enemies in socialist construction.
    The Experimental Theatre of the Central Academy of Drama is rushing to rehearse this play, preparing for its premiere in the capital soon. The director of this play is Sun Weishi, and the main actors include Zhang Ping, Yu Lan, Wang Yizhi, Zhuang Zejing, and others. (Wen Jin)

### 岳野完成“友与敌”
闻津
1958-03-26
第7版()
专栏：文艺列车

    岳野完成“友与敌”
    剧作家岳野继“同甘共苦”后，又完成一个新剧“友与敌”。这是歌颂中苏伟大友谊的戏剧。它通过一个苏联军官和一个中国工人的血肉关系，来表现中苏两国人民深厚的友谊；剧中描绘了这一对生死朋友并肩工作，在社会主义建设中，一起克服了许多困难，战胜了暗藏敌人的破坏。
    中央戏剧学院实验话剧院正在赶排这个戏，准备最近在首都公演。这出戏的导演是孙维世，主要演员有张平、于兰、王一之、庄则敬等人。　（闻津）
