Title: Chen Yi, Guo Moruo Receive All Members of the Matsuyama Ballet Troupe
Author:
Date: 1958-03-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Guo Moruo, received all members of the Japanese Matsuyama Ballet Troupe during the intermission of their premiere performance this evening.

    During the reception, Chen Yi and Guo Moruo shook hands with the Japanese artists one by one, congratulating them on the success of their performance. Present at the reception were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Vice Presidents Ding Xilin and Yang Hansheng.

### 陈毅、郭沫若接见松山芭蕾舞团全体人员

1958-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅、郭沫若接见松山芭蕾舞团全体人员
    据新华社13日讯  陈毅副总理、郭沫若副委员长今晚在日本松山芭蕾舞团首次演出休息时，接见了芭蕾舞团的全体人员。
    陈毅和郭沫若在接见时同日本艺术家们一一握手，祝贺他们演出的成功。陪同接见的，有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，副会长丁西林和阳翰笙。
