Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Condolences on the Death of Diaz, General Secretary of the Chilean Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1958-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China today sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Chilean Communist Party, expressing deep condolences on the death of Comrade Carlos González Videla, General Secretary of the Chilean Communist Party. The telegram reads as follows:
To the Central Committee of the Chilean Communist Party:

	We are shocked to hear the sad news of the passing of Comrade Carlos González Videla, General Secretary of the Chilean Communist Party. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, on behalf of all members of the Communist Party of China, expresses our deep condolences to the Central Committee of the Chilean Communist Party.

		The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, March 20, 1958

### 中共中央电唁智共总书记迪亚斯逝世

1958-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电唁智共总书记迪亚斯逝世
    新华社20日讯　中国共产党中央委员会今天致电智利共产党中央委员会，对智利共产党总书记加洛·冈萨雷斯·迪亚斯的逝世表示沉痛哀悼。电文如下：智利共产党中央委员会：
    惊悉智利共产党总书记加洛·冈萨雷斯·迪亚斯同志逝世噩耗，中国共产党中央委员会谨代表中国共产党全体党员，向智利共产党中央委员会表示沉痛的哀悼。
                  中国共产党中央委员会　一九五八年三月二十日
