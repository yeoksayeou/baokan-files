Title: Polevoy Awarded Order of the Red Banner of Labor
Author:
Date: 1958-03-19
Page: 6

　　Polevoy
　　Awarded Order of the Red Banner of Labor
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th - TASS reports: The famous Soviet writer, author of the novel "A Real Man," "We are Soviet People," Boris Polevoy, was awarded the Order of the Red Banner of Labor on his fiftieth birthday for his great contribution to the development of Soviet literature.
　　Polevoy is one of the most prestigious writers in the Soviet Union. His first collection of features, written at the age of nineteen, was a success and received high praise from Gorky. In 1939, he published his first novella, "Boiling Shop," depicting the life of metallurgical workers. During World War II, Polevoy served as a war correspondent for "Pravda." After the war, he published the famous novel "A Real Man," the collection of short stories "We are Soviet People," the novella "Return," and the novel "Gold," among others.

### 波列伏依  荣获劳动红旗勋章

1958-03-19
第6版()
专栏：

    波列伏依
    荣获劳动红旗勋章
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联著名作家、小说“真正的人”、“我们是苏维埃人”的作者鲍里斯·波列伏依在五十寿辰的时候由于对苏联文学发展的巨大贡献而获得劳动红旗勋章。
    波列伏依是苏联最有声望的作家之一。他在十九岁时写的第一本特写集就已获得了成功，得到高尔基很好的评价。1939年，他发表了第一本中篇小说、描写冶金工人生活的作品“沸腾的车间”。第二次世界大战时，波列伏依担任了“真理报”战地记者。战后，他发表了著名小说“真正的人”、短篇小说集“我们是苏维埃人”、中篇小说“归来”和长篇小说“金子”等。
