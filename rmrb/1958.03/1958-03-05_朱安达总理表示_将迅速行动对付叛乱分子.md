Title: Prime Minister Djuanda Says He Will Act Quickly to Deal with the Rebels
Author:
Date: 1958-03-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 4th. According to Antara News Agency, Indonesian Prime Minister Djuanda said today that there is no other way but to take swift action against the rebels. He said that the government will not hold negotiations with the rebels in Central Sumatra. He believes that the central government will soon be able to regain control of the domestic situation.

Prime Minister Djuanda made these remarks this morning to a delegation of veteran youth soldiers who visited him.

Prime Minister Djuanda said that when the government takes action, it will receive the support of the Navy, Army, Air Force, and police, as well as the support of the people in the rebel areas and the people throughout Indonesia.

### 朱安达总理表示  将迅速行动对付叛乱分子

1958-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    朱安达总理表示
    将迅速行动对付叛乱分子
    据新华社雅加达4日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚总理朱安达今天说，除了对叛乱分子采取迅速行动以外没有别的办法。他说，政府决不同中苏门答腊叛乱分子举行谈判。他相信中央政府不久一定能够重新控制国内的局势。
    朱安达总理是在今天上午对拜访他的退伍青年军人组织的代表团说这番话的。
    朱安达总理说，在政府采取行动时，将会得到海、陆、空军和警察的支持，也会得到叛乱地区的人民和印度尼西亚全国人民的支持。
