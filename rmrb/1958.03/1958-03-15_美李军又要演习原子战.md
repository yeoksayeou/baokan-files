Title: US-Li Clique to Stage Another Nuclear War Exercise
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 14th: Seoul dispatch: The US military in Korea and the Syngman Rhee army will hold their first joint amphibious landing exercise “under hypothetical atomic warfare conditions” in early May. It is reported that this will be the largest nuclear war exercise since the Korean armistice.

According to the South Korean naval authorities, more than seventy warships and more than fifty aircraft from the United States and Syngman Rhee, as well as a reinforced company of Marines, will participate in the exercise.

### 美李军又要演习原子战

1958-03-15
第5版()
专栏：

    美李军又要演习原子战
    据新华社平壤14日电　汉城消息：侵朝美军和李承晚军队又要在5月初举行“在假想原子战争条件下”的第一次联合两栖登陆演习。据报道，这将是朝鲜停战以来规模最大的原子战争演习。
    据南朝鲜海军当局宣布，美国和李承晚的七十多艘军舰和五十多架飞机，以及海军陆战队的一个加强连将参加这次演习。
