Title: USSR Publishes Lenin's Collection "Against Revisionism"
Author:
Date: 1958-03-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 29th: A collection of Lenin's works, "Against Revisionism," compiled by the Institute of Marxism-Leninism of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the State Political Book Publishing Bureau, has been published.

The works included in the collection reflect Lenin's consistent and uncompromising struggle against revisionism, opportunism, and dogmatism in the Russian and international workers' movements.

In these works, Lenin exposed the essence of revisionism, its economic basis and social roots, pointed out its international character and its manifestations in different historical conditions and different countries, and revealed the harm of revisionism to the world workers' movement. Lenin wrote: "Revisionism, or 'revision' of Marxism, is, if not the most important, then at least one of the main manifestations of the bourgeoisie's influence on the proletariat and the bourgeois degeneration of the proletarians."

The works in the collection show that Lenin also opposed dogmatism and opposed turning Marxism into a rigid collection of formulas divorced from life.

### 苏联出版列宁的文集“反对修正主义”

1958-03-31
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联出版列宁的文集
    “反对修正主义”
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科29日讯：苏共中央马克思列宁主义研究院和国家政治书籍出版局编纂的列宁的文集“反对修正主义”已经出版了。
    收入文集中的著作反映了列宁对俄国和国际工人运动中的修正主义、机会主义和教条主义所一贯进行的毫不妥协的斗争。
    列宁在这些著作里揭露了修正主义的实质以及它的经济基础和社会根源，指出了它的国际性和在不同历史条件下、不同国家里的表现形式，揭示了修正主义对于世界工人运动的危害。列宁写道：“修正主义或者对马克思主义的‘修正’即使不是目前资产阶级对无产阶级的影响和无产者的资产阶级蜕化的最主要的表现，那末至少也是主要表现之一”。
    文集中的著作表明，列宁也反对教条主义，反对把马克思主义变成脱离生活的僵化的公式汇集。
