Title: Sukarno Inspects Military Affairs in Various Regions
Author:
Date: 1958-03-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 11th March: Indonesian President Sukarno left Jakarta today to inspect military affairs in various regions. According to the officially announced schedule, Sukarno will visit Malang, Makassar, Batang, Surabaya, and Bandung.

### 苏加诺到各地视察军情

1958-03-12
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺到各地视察军情
    据新华社雅加达11日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺今天离开了雅加达，前往各地视察军事情况。据官方宣布的日程，苏加诺将到玛琅、望加锡、巴塘、泗水和万隆。
