Title: Burmese News Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, March 30th. The Burmese news delegation, invited by the All-China Journalists Association to visit China, arrived in Beijing this afternoon by plane from Kunming.

The members of the Burmese news delegation are: leader U Ba Than, secretary U Maung Maung Gyi, members U Thein Lin, U Min O, Daw Yin Yin, Daw Ma La, U Sein Win, U Maung Maung and others, eight people in total.

Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and many people from the capital's press circles went to the airport to welcome them. Tran Thanh, the press attache of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 缅甸新闻代表团到北京

1958-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸新闻代表团到北京
    新华社30日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会的邀请到中国访问的缅甸新闻工作者代表团，今天下午由昆明乘飞机到达北京。
    缅甸新闻工作者代表团成员有团长吴巴丹，秘书吴貌貌支，团员吴登林、吴敏吴、杜因因、杜马拉、吴盛温、吴貌貌等八人。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长王芸生，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃以及首都新闻界人士多人前往机场欢迎。越南驻华大使馆新闻专员陈青也到机场欢迎。
