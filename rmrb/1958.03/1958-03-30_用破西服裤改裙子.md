Title: Remaking a Skirt from Worn-Out Western-Style Trousers
Author:
Date: 1958-03-30
Page: 4

A skirt can be made from Western-style trousers that are torn in the back. Below is a Western-style skirt remade from worn-out trousers with a length of 3.1 *shi chi*, a waist of 2.4 *shi chi*, a crotch width of 2.22 *shi chi*, and a hem width of 0.75 *shi chi*. The finished skirt has a length of 2.1 *shi chi*, a waist of 2.17 *shi chi*, and a hip measurement of 3.1 *shi chi*.

	5. Pocket Flap 6. Gusset Material (with picture)
