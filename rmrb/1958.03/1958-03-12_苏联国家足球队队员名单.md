Title: List of Players for the Soviet National Football Team
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-12
Page: 7

    The Soviet National Football Team brought a total of twenty-eight players this time. They are: Goalkeepers: Yashin, Pilyayev, Maslachenko; Defenders: Okonnikov, Kesarev, Kryzhevsky, Maslenkin, Kuznetsov, Ostrovsky, Yeloshin; Midfielders: Netto, Voynov, Tsarev, Derkachov; Forwards: Tatushin, Isayev, Ilyin, Metreveli, Ivanov, Gusarov, Fedosov, Buzunov, Semyonov, P. Dzerzhnik, Bubukin, Apukhtin, Kovalev, Korsunov.
