Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet to Welcome Norwegian Labour Party MP Finn Moe
Author:
Date: 1958-03-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 26th — Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and his wife hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Finn Moe, a member of the Norwegian Labour Party and Chairman of the Parliamentary Foreign Affairs and Constitutional Committee, and his wife.

Among those present at the banquet were Zhou Gengsheng and Qiao Guanhua, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Qu Wu, Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Meng Yongqian, Director of the Institute of International Relations of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. The Norwegian Ambassador to China, Mr. Kloug-Hansen, also attended the banquet.

### 张奚若设宴欢迎挪威工党议员芬·穆

1958-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

     张奚若设宴欢迎挪威工党议员芬·穆
    新华社26日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若和夫人，今晚举行宴会欢迎挪威工党议员，议会外交、宪法委员会主席芬·穆和夫人。
    出席宴会的有中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、乔冠华，全国人民代表大会常务委员会秘书长屈武，中国科学院国际关系研究所所长孟用潜等。挪威驻华大使克洛格—亨生也出席了宴会。
