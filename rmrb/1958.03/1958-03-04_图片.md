Title: In Local Newspapers

Author:

Date: 1958-03-04
Page: 4

On March 1st, the front page of the Shanxi Daily devoted an entire page to prominently reporting on the mass production of tubers and the transformation of tubers from supplementary food to staple food. This is the layout of that day's page.
