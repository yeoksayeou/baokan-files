Title: Achieving “Three Cleans” and “Six Nos”: Xiaoying Lane Residents Honored

Author:

Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, March 23rd. The Xiaoying Lane residential area in Hangzhou, which initiated a friendship competition among urban residential areas nationwide to eliminate the four pests and promote hygiene, has basically achieved the competition conditions of "Three Cleans" and "Six Nos" ahead of schedule. This was announced by Peng Xiaocun, the district head of Shangcheng District in Hangzhou, at a mass meeting held on the 22nd. According to the inspection and appraisal results of more than 300 representatives from various residential areas in Shangcheng District, all 467 households in the Xiaoying Lane residential area have become clean households, achieving "Three Cleans" (clean indoors, clean environment, clean kitchen and toilet); the number of households with no mosquitoes, no flies, no rats, no cockroaches, and no bedbugs all exceeded 95%.

Since Chairman Mao inspected the sanitation work in the Xiaoying Lane residential area, all the residents have been particularly enthusiastic about eliminating the four pests and promoting hygiene. Men, women, old, and young all took action to launch a siege against the "six pests." After two and a half months of hard work, they achieved the initial goal of the eliminating the four pests and promoting hygiene campaign.

### 实现“三洁”“六无”  小营巷居民光荣

1958-03-25
第7版()
专栏：

    实现“三洁”“六无”
    小营巷居民光荣
    新华社杭州23日电　向全国城市居民区发起除四害、讲卫生友谊竞赛的杭州市小营巷居民区，已经提前基本上实现了“三洁”、“六无”等竞赛条件，这是杭州市上城区区长彭孝存在22日召开的群众大会上宣布的。据上城区各居民区三百多名代表检查鉴定结果，小营巷居民区四百六十七户居民已全部成为整洁户，做到了“三洁”（室内洁、环境洁、厨房厕所洁）；无蚊、无蝇、无鼠、无蟑螂、无臭虫的户数都在95％以上。
    从毛主席到小营巷居民区查看卫生工作以后，全体居民除四害、讲卫生的劲头特别大，男女老少人人动手，向“六害”展开围剿，经过两个半月的苦战，实现了除四害、讲卫生运动的初步目标。
