Title: Abundant Coal Resources Discovered on Fujian Beaches
Author: Sun Xizhu
Date: 1958-03-27
Page: 3
 
    Abundant Coal Resources Discovered on Fujian Beaches

    (Our newspaper reports) A large amount of coal has been discovered on the largest sandy beach, Dasha Pu (covering an area of 30,000 mu), in Zhangpu County, Fujian Province. Preliminary findings indicate that coal exists along the beach stretching over ten li from Jiazi Village in Beijiang Township to Xincuo Village at the foot of Duren Mountain. Among these, the coal discovered beneath the beach stretching over five li from Jiazi Village to Xiacai Village is of the highest quality.
    This discovery is of great significance in solving the fuel problem for coastal fishermen.

    (Sun Xizhu) (Attached photo)

### 福建海滩煤炭丰富
孙喜柱
1958-03-27
第3版()
专栏：

    福建海滩煤炭丰富
    本报讯　福建省漳浦县的沿海最大沙滩——大沙埔（面积三万亩），发现有大量煤炭，据初步发现，从北江乡的甲仔村至杜任山东的新厝村十多华里长的沙滩都有煤炭，其中从甲仔村到下蔡村有五华里多长的沙滩下，发现的煤炭质量最高。
    这一发现，对于解决沿海渔民的燃料问题有很大的作用。　
    （孙喜柱）（附图片）
