    Fresh Oysters Yield of 1984 Jin Per Mu
    Last winter, the Xiangzhou Aquatic Production Cooperative in Zhuhai County, Guangdong Province, harvested 2,560 dan of fresh oysters from 129 mu of pond bottom, averaging over 1,984 jin per mu, setting the highest unit yield record for aquatic products in the country. This year, in order to further expand production, the cooperative plans to increase the oyster farming area to 6,000 mu. (Yao)
