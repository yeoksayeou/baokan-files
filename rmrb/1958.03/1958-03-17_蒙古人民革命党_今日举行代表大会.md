Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Holds Congress Today
Author:
Date: 1958-03-17
Page: 5

    Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party
    Holds Congress Today
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, March 16th. The 13th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party decided to be held on March 17th in the capital, Ulaanbaatar.
    At this congress, the delegates will hear and discuss the summary report of the Central Committee given by Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee; the work report of the Inspection Committee given by Bimba, Chairman of the Central Inspection Committee; and the report on the directives for the three-year plan for the development of the Mongolian national economy and culture given by Tsendenbal, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee. The congress will also discuss the revision of the Party Constitution and the election of the Party's central organs.
    The 12th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party was held in November 1954.

### 蒙古人民革命党  今日举行代表大会

1958-03-17
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党
    今日举行代表大会
    新华社乌兰巴托16日电  蒙古人民革命党第十三次代表大会决定3月17日在首都乌兰巴托举行。
    这次大会将要听取和讨论中央委员会第一书记达姆巴所作的中央委员会总结报告，中央检查委员会主席宾巴的检查委员会工作报告，以及中央政治局委员泽登巴尔作的关于发展蒙古国民经济和文化的三年计划的指示的报告。大会还要讨论修改党章的问题和选举党的中央机构。
    蒙古人民革命党第十二次代表大会是在1954年11月举行的。
