Title: Vo Nguyen Giap Sends Letter to International Commission Suggesting Negotiations with South Vietnam on Liaison Issues
Author:
Date: 1958-03-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 27th - Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army, recently wrote to the International Commission suggesting that negotiations be held with the South Vietnamese authorities as soon as possible to resolve the issue of liaison delegations to the International Commission.

Vo Nguyen Giap reiterated that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is willing to allow the South Vietnamese regime to establish a liaison delegation at the International Commission's office in Hanoi.

Vo Nguyen Giap emphasized that the Vietnamese People's Army must have its own delegation stationed at the Saigon International Commission, especially now that the International Commission's main office has been relocated to Saigon. Vo Nguyen Giap requested that the International Commission relay these opinions to the South Vietnamese authorities and hoped to be informed of the opinions of the South Vietnamese authorities and the Commission to the General Headquarters of the People's Army.

Vo Nguyen Giap hoped that before the two sides held meetings, the International Commission would intervene with the South Vietnamese authorities as necessary to have them lift the restrictions on the freedom of movement of the Vietnamese People's Army's liaison delegation stationed in Saigon.

### 武元甲致函国际委员会  建议同南越协商联络问题

1958-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    武元甲致函国际委员会
    建议同南越协商联络问题
    新华社河内27日电　越南人民军总司令武元甲最近写信给国际委员会，建议同南越当局早日举行协商来解决双方驻国际委员会的联络代表团问题。
    武元甲再一次表示，越南民主共和国政府方面愿意让南越政权在国际委员会河内办事处设立一个联络代表团。
    武元甲强调说，越南人民军方面必须有自己的代表团留驻在西贡国际委员会，特别是在目前国际委员会总办事处已经迁驻西贡的时候。武元甲要求国际委员会把这些意见转告南越当局，并且希望把南越当局和委员会的意见通知人民军总司令部。
    武元甲希望国际委员会在双方举行会议之前，对南越当局进行必要的干涉，使南越当局取消对越南人民军驻西贡联络代表团的来往自由的限制。
