Title: Moroccan Communist Party Central Committee Plenary Session Points Out: Foreign Troops Must Withdraw from Morocco
Author:
Date: 1958-03-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 1st (TASS dispatch) - The Central Committee of the Moroccan Communist Party held a plenary session in Casablanca to discuss the situation in North Africa. During the discussion on the issue of the French military presence in Morocco and the retention of military bases by the United States in Morocco, the plenary session emphasized that foreign armed forces must withdraw from Morocco. The Central Committee advocated the establishment of a permanent advisory conference composed of representatives from the three North African countries (including representatives from the Algerian National Liberation Front).

### 摩洛哥共产党中央全会指出  外国军队必须撤出摩洛哥

1958-03-03
第5版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥共产党中央全会指出
    外国军队必须撤出摩洛哥
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社巴黎1日讯：摩洛哥共产党中央委员会在卡萨布兰卡举行了全体会议，讨论了北非局势。全会在讨论法国在摩洛哥驻军和美国在摩洛哥保有军事基地问题时强调指出，外国武装部队必须撤出摩洛哥。中央委员会主张建立由北非三国代表（包括阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表）组成的常设咨询会议。
