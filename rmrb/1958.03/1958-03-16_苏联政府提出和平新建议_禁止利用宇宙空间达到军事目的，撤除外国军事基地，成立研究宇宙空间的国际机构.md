Title: The Soviet Government Proposes New Peace Proposals: Ban the Use of Outer Space for Military Purposes, Remove Foreign Military Bases, Establish an International Organization to Study Outer Space
Author:
Date: 1958-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 15th: The Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs held a press conference on the 15th, announcing the Soviet government's proposals regarding the prohibition of using outer space for military purposes, the removal of foreign military bases on the territory of various countries, and international cooperation in the study of outer space.

    The Soviet government proposes banning the use of outer space for military purposes. All countries should undertake the obligation to launch rockets into outer space only according to jointly agreed international plans.
    The Soviet government proposes the removal of foreign military bases on the territory of various countries, primarily foreign military bases on the territory of European, Middle Eastern, and North African countries.
    Corresponding international supervision should be established within the framework of the United Nations to supervise the fulfillment of the above obligations.
    The Soviet government proposes that the United Nations establish an international cooperation organization for the study of outer space. The Soviet government's proposal states that this organization could formulate an international plan for the joint launching of intercontinental rockets and space rockets to study outer space, and supervise the implementation of this plan. In addition, this organization could regularly engage in outer space research work (which is currently being carried out within the scope of the International Geophysical Year) and become a global center for collecting, exchanging, and disseminating information about outer space research. This organization could coordinate the scientific research plans of various countries in the study of outer space and endeavor to help and promote the realization of these plans.
    The Soviet government suggests discussing this issue at a summit meeting of heads of government, with the aim of reaching an agreement on this issue, even if it is only an agreement in principle.
    In order to draft a universal international agreement in which all countries can participate, the Soviet government is simultaneously submitting the above issues for discussion at the Thirteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 苏联政府提出和平新建议  禁止利用宇宙空间达到军事目的，撤除外国军事基地，成立研究宇宙空间的国际机构

1958-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联政府提出和平新建议
    禁止利用宇宙空间达到军事目的，撤除外国军事基地，成立研究宇宙空间的国际机构
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部15日举行记者招待会，会上宣布了苏联政府提出的关于禁止利用宇宙空间达到军事目的、关于撤除各国领土上的外国军事基地、关于在研究宇宙空间方面进行国际合作的建议。
    苏联政府建议禁止利用宇宙空间达到军事目的。各国应该承担这样义务：只根据共同议定的国际计划向宇宙空间发射火箭。
    苏联政府建议撤除各国领土上的外国军事基地，首先是欧洲、中近东和北非国家领土上的外国军事基地。
    应该在联合国范围内建立监督履行上述义务的相应的国际监督。
    苏联政府建议联合国成立一个研究宇宙空间的国际合作机构。苏联政府的建议中说，这个机构可以制订一个发射洲际火箭和宇宙火箭以研究宇宙空间的共同协议的国际计划，并且监督实现这个计划。此外，这个机构还可以经常从事宇宙空间的研究工作（这个工作现在是在国际地球物理年的范围内进行的）并且成为世界上收集、交换和传播关于宇宙研究情报的中心站。这个机构可以使各国在研究宇宙空间方面的科学研究计划相互配合，并且尽力帮助和促进这些计划的实现。
    苏联政府建议在政府首脑会议上讨论这个问题，目的是希望对这个问题达成协议，那怕是在原则上达成协议也好。
    为了草拟一个各国都能参加的普遍性的国际协定，苏联政府同时把上述问题提交联合国大会第十三届会议讨论。
