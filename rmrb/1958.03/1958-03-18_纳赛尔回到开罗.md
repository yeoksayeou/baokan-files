Title: Nasser Returns to Cairo
Author:
Date: 1958-03-18
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 17th: President Nasser of the United Arab Republic returned to Cairo by plane this morning from Aleppo, a famous city in the northern part of the Syrian region. Vice President Boghdadi and Marshal Amer also returned to Cairo.
    Nasser arrived in Damascus on February 24 for a visit. Two days before his departure for Cairo, he visited Aleppo on March 15. When delivering a speech to the residents of Aleppo on the 16th, he reiterated his determination to fight imperialism and pursue a peaceful and independent policy.

### 纳赛尔回到开罗

1958-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔回到开罗
    新华社开罗17日电　阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔今天上午从叙利亚地区北部著名城市阿勒颇乘飞机回到开罗。副总统波格达迪和阿密尔元帅也一同回到开罗。
    纳赛尔是在2月24日到达大马士革访问的。他在动身回开罗之前两天，在3月15日曾经到阿勒颇访问。他在16日向阿勒颇居民发表演说时，重申了同帝国主义斗争和奉行和平独立政策的决心。
