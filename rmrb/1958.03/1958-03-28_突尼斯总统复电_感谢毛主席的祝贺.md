Title: Tunisian President Replies to Chairman Mao, Thanking Him for Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1958-03-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: Tunisian President Bourguiba sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong on the 26th, thanking him for his congratulations to him and the Tunisian people on the second anniversary of Tunisia's independence.

President Bourguiba's telegram is as follows: To His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

I sincerely thank Your Excellency for your kind telegram of congratulations to me on the second anniversary of Tunisia's Independence Day. I am deeply moved by Your Excellency's friendly sentiments towards Tunisia. Please accept my best wishes for your personal health and for the happiness and prosperity of the Chinese people.

Tunisian Republic President Bourguiba
March 26, Tunis

### 突尼斯总统复电  感谢毛主席的祝贺

1958-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    突尼斯总统复电
    感谢毛主席的祝贺
    新华社27日讯　突尼斯总统布尔吉巴26日致电毛泽东主席，感谢毛主席在突尼斯独立二周年纪念日的时候对他和突尼斯人民的祝贺。
    布尔吉巴总统的来电如下：北京中华人民共和国主席毛泽东阁下：
    我衷心感谢阁下善意地在突尼斯独立日二周年的时候给我的贺电。我深为阁下对突尼斯表示的友好盛情所感动。请你接受我对你个人的健康以及对中国人民的幸福和繁荣的良好愿望。
                                  突尼斯共和国总统　布尔吉巴
                                        3月26日于突尼斯
