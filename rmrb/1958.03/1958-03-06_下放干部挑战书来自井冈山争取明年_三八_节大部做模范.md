Title: Female Cadres Transferred Downward from Jinggang Mountain Issue Challenge, Striving for Most to Become Models by Next "March 8th" International Women's Day

Author:

Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jinggang Mountain, March 5th: All the female cadres transferred downward to the Jinggang Mountain Comprehensive Agricultural, Forestry, and Animal Husbandry Reclamation Farm in Jiangxi Province recently issued a proposal to female cadres across the country who have been transferred downward to participate in agricultural production, calling for the launch of a "March 8th" labor competition.

In their proposal, these female cadres proposed that in 1958, they should work eight hours a day and strive to earn eight work points; be able to carry eighty catties; guarantee full attendance for 240 days throughout the year; and strive to have 80% of them become labor models by next "March 8th" International Women's Day.

### 下放干部挑战书来自井冈山争取明年“三八”节大部做模范

1958-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    下放干部挑战书来自井冈山争取明年“三八”节大部做模范
    新华社井冈山5日电　江西省下放到井冈山林牧农综合垦殖场的全体女干部，最近向全国下放参加农业生产的女干部提出了开展“三八”劳动竞赛的倡议。
    这些女干部在倡议书中提出在1958年内要做到每天劳动八小时，争取做八个工分；能挑能背八十斤；保证全年出勤二百四十天；并且要争取明年“三八”节有80％的人做劳动模范。
