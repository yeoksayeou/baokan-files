Title: Marosán Says in Berlin: German-Hungarian Talks Will Promote Friendship Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-03-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 24th: According to TASS, the Berlin City Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany (SED) convened a meeting of activists here on the 24th. Members of the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation, which is currently visiting the German Democratic Republic (GDR), along with Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the SED Central Committee, Otto Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the GDR, and other leaders of the SED, attended the meeting.

    Mátyás Marosán, Member of the Political Bureau of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Central Committee, spoke. He introduced the Hungarian people's victorious struggle against the counter-revolutionary rebellion. He pointed out that the Hungarian People's Republic is now stronger than ever. Marosán emphasized that the negotiations between the Party and Government Delegations of the German Democratic Republic and the Hungarian People's Republic will promote the further consolidation of friendly relations between the two parties and the two countries. On behalf of the Hungarian working class and members of the Socialist Workers' Party, he wished the working people of the GDR new achievements in socialist construction and in the struggle to establish a unified, democratic, and peace-loving Germany.

### 马罗山在柏林说  德匈会谈将促进两国友好

1958-03-26
第6版()
专栏：

    马罗山在柏林说
    德匈会谈将促进两国友好
    据新华社25日讯　据塔斯社柏林24日讯：德国统一社会党大柏林市委员会24日在这里召开了积极分子会议。正在访问民主德国的匈牙利党政代表团成员和德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希、德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥以及德国统一社会党的其他领导人都出席了这次会议。
    匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员马罗山讲了话。他介绍了匈牙利人民对反革命叛乱的胜利斗争。他指出，目前匈牙利人民共和国比任何时候都更强大了。马罗山强调说，德意志民主共和国和匈牙利人民共和国党政代表团之间的谈判将会促进两党和两国间友好关系的进一步巩固。他代表匈牙利工人阶级和社会主义工人党党员祝民主德国劳动人民在社会主义建设和争取建立一个统一、民主和爱好和平的德国的斗争中取得新的成就。
