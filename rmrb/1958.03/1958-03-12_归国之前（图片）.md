Title: Before Returning Home (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1958-03-12
Page: 4

    Before Returning Home
    The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Civil Police unit guarding the Maliangshan position transfers defense duties to the Korean People's Army Civil Police unit. Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Xianquan photo
    Li Renshan is a Korean girl who lives in Xidun Village, Tieyuan County. She had been suffering from pleuro-adhesion for several years. Last year, Xu Wentian, a military doctor from the Volunteer Army's health battalion, cured her disease with acupuncture. This is Dr. Xu Wentian's last visit to Li Renshan before the Volunteer Army returns home. Lin Zhichang and Yu Fenglou photo.
          (Xinhua News Agency manuscript)
    Before leaving Korea, Volunteer Army soldiers grab a handful of soil or pick up a piece of shrapnel from the heroic position where they shed blood, taking it back to their motherland as a permanent souvenir. Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao photo

### 归国之前（图片）

1958-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    归国之前
    警卫在马良山阵地上的中国人民志愿军民警部队，向朝鲜人民军民警部队移交防务。新华社记者　孟宪全摄
    李仁善，是居住在铁原郡席顿里的朝鲜姑娘，她几年来患着胸膜粘联病。去年，志愿军某部卫生营军医徐文田，用针灸把她的病治好了。这是在志愿军回国前，徐文田医生最后一次来探望李仁善。林志常、俞凤楼摄
          （新华社稿）
    离开朝鲜之前，志愿军战士在洒过鲜血的英雄阵地上，抓起一把土或拣起一块弹片，带回祖国作为永久纪念。新华社记者
  孟庆彪摄
