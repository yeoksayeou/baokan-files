Title: World Peace Council Standing Committee to Meet in Delhi in Late March
Author:
Date: 1958-03-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, March 14th, TASS Paris, March 13th: The secretariat of Professor Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, announced that the meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council will be held in Delhi from March 22nd to 24th. The meeting will discuss preparations for the Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference. The conference is scheduled to be held from July 16th to 22nd this year.

### 世界和平理事会常委会  三月下旬在德里开会

1958-03-15
第6版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常委会
    三月下旬在德里开会
    新华社14日讯　塔斯社巴黎13日讯：世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里教授的秘书处宣布，世界和平理事会常务委员会会议将从3月22日到24 日在德里举行。会议将讨论裁军和国际合作大会筹备问题。这次大会预定在今年7月16日—22日举行。
