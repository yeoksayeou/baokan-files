Title: Comrade Jaroszewicz's Biography
Author:
Date: 1958-03-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency

    Comrade Piotr Jaroszewicz, who is leading the Polish government delegation on a visit to our country, is the Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland and a member of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party.

    Comrade Jaroszewicz was born in Nieswiez in 1909. His father was a teacher from a poor peasant family. After graduating from normal school, Comrade Jaroszewicz worked as a primary school teacher and principal from 1929. During his time as a teacher, he was enthusiastic about education and social work. In September 1939, he went to the Soviet Union. From 1943, he participated in the Polish army organized by Polish patriots residing in the Soviet Union with Soviet assistance, and fought heroically on the front lines against the Hitlerite bandits. He fought all the way to Berlin with the Polish First Division named after the Polish national hero Kosciuszko. During the war, he was promoted to the position of Deputy Commander of the First Army. After the war ended in 1945, he was appointed Deputy Minister of National Defense.

    Comrade Jaroszewicz joined the Polish Workers' Party on the front lines in 1944. In 1945, he was elected as a delegate to the National Congress of the Polish Workers' Party. After the merger of the Polish Workers' Party and the Polish Socialist Party into the Polish United Workers' Party, he was elected as a member of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party. From 1947, he was elected as a deputy to all sessions of the Polish People's Republic Parliament. From the end of 1950 to the end of 1952, he was the Vice-Chairman of the Polish State Economic Committee. From 1952, he served as the Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, mainly responsible for national economic affairs. (Xinhua News Agency) (Attached picture)

### 雅罗谢维奇同志的简历

1958-03-21
第6版()
专栏：

    雅罗谢维奇同志的简历
    率领波兰政府代表团来我国访问的彼奥特·雅罗谢维奇同志是波兰部长会议副主席和波兰统一工人党中央委员会委员。
    雅罗谢维奇同志于1909年生于聂斯维兹。他的父亲是出身于贫农家庭的教员。雅罗谢维奇同志在师范学校毕业后，从1929年起先后担任小学教员和小学校长。任教期间，他热心从事教育和社会工作。1939年9月他到了苏联。1943年起，他参加了在苏联帮助下由侨居苏联的波兰爱国者组织起来的波兰军队，在反希特勒匪帮的战线上英勇战斗。他和以波兰民族英雄科斯久什科为名的波兰第一师在一起，一直打到了柏林。他在战争中被擢升到第一军副军长的职务。1945年战争结束后，他被任命为国防部副部长。
    1944年雅罗谢维奇同志在前线时加入了波兰工人党。1945年被选为波兰工人党全国代表大会代表。波兰工人党和波兰社会党两党合并为波兰统一工人党后，他当选为波兰统一工人党中央委员会委员。1947年起他被选为历届波兰人民共和国议会的议员。1950年末至1952年末，他是波兰国家经济委员会副主席。1952年起任波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席，主要负责国家经济方面的事务。（新华社）（附图片）
