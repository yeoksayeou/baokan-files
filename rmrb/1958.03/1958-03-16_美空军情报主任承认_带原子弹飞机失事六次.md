Title: U.S. Air Force Intelligence Chief Admits Six Accidents Involving Planes Carrying Atomic Bombs
Author:
Date: 1958-03-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 15th—U.S. Air Force Intelligence Chief Major General Arno H. Luehman said in a radio interview on NBC on the evening of the 13th that, to date, the United States has had "six" accidents involving Air Force planes carrying atomic bombs.

The U.S. government has only disclosed three accidents. In the most recent incident, an Air Force plane dropped a bomb on Florence, South Carolina. In the previous two incidents, planes dropped bombs into the Atlantic Ocean off the coasts of Florida and Georgia.

### 美空军情报主任承认  带原子弹飞机失事六次

1958-03-16
第3版()
专栏：

    美空军情报主任承认
    带原子弹飞机失事六次
    新华社15日讯　华盛顿消息：美国空军情报主任阿诺·卢曼少将13日晚在全国广播公司的广播谈话中说，到现在为止，美国已经发生了“六次”带有原子弹的空军飞机失事的事件。
    美国官方只公布过三次失事事件，在最近的一次事件中，空军飞机把炸弹扔在南卡罗来纳州弗洛伦斯。前两次飞机把炸弹抛在佛罗里达州和佐治亚州沿岸的大西洋中。
