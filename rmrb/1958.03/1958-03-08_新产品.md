    New Products
    Automatic Loading and Unloading Vehicle
    The Harbin Jiancheng Machinery Factory has successfully trial-produced the Model 04 automatic loading and unloading vehicle and has begun mass production. This automatic loading and unloading vehicle can use its unique long "chicken beak" to scoop up whole crates or block-shaped objects weighing one and a half tons from the ground and skillfully transport them elsewhere.
    Sewing Machine Without Thread
    The Shanghai No. 1 Sewing Machine Factory recently manufactured a high-frequency plastic cloth welding machine. This welding machine is used to sew plastic raincoats and toothbrush bags and other items. Xinhua News Agency reporter Xia Daoling photo

### 新产品

1958-03-08
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    新产品
    自动装卸车
    哈尔滨建成机械厂试制成功04型自动装卸车，并开始成批生产。这种自动装卸车，可以用它特有长鸡嘴把一吨半重的整箱物件或块状物件从地上铲起来，灵巧地搬运到别处去。
    不用线的缝衣机
    上海第一缝纫机厂最近制成一台高频率塑料胶布焊接机。这种焊接机是用来缝制塑料雨衣和牙具袋等用品的。新华社记者　夏道陵摄
