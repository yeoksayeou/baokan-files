    This is the "Old Ladies' Chorus" of the Nanshi District of Shenyang City. The members of the chorus are all old ladies in their fifties and sixties. They often go to construction sites, rural areas, military units, and schools to perform. Tonight, from 21:35 to 21:55, they will perform for the national audience on the Central People's Broadcasting Station.
