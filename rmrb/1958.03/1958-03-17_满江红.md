    Man Jiang Hong
    Welcoming the Triumphant Return of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to Aid Korea

    At the Yalu River's edge,
    Triumphant songs resound through mountain passes beneath the moon.
    Remember the seven-year Long March,
    Blood and flesh intertwined, emotions intense.
    Resisting America and Aiding Korea upholds justice,
    Protecting the home and defending the nation displays fierce valor.
    Consider the Imjin River and the heroic feats of Shangganling,
    All sing their praises.

    Wishing for peace, people unite their hearts.
    Easing the situation,
    Troops are withdrawn.
    See how the enemy chieftain's claws,
    Can extend and retract?
    Hundreds of millions joyfully welcome the brave soldiers,
    Red flags fly, the east wind is victorious.
    Let us look forward to, building the nation and establishing extraordinary merits,
    Together exerting our strength!

### 满江红
李济深
1958-03-17
第2版()
专栏：

    满江红
    欢迎援朝志愿军凯旋　李济深
    鸭绿江头，
    铙歌唱彻关山月。
    记七载长征，
    血肉交凝情切。
    抗美援朝申正义，
    保家卫国扬威烈。
    筭临津、致果上甘功，
    同称说。
    和平愿人心协。
    缓局势，
    将兵撤，
    看敌酋魔爪，
    如何伸屈？
    亿兆腾欢迎壮士，
    红旗飞舞东风捷。
    更相期、建国树奇勋，
    齐宣力！
