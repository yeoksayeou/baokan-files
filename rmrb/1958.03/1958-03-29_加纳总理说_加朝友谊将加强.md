Title: Ghanaian Prime Minister Says Ghana-Korea Friendship Will Strengthen
Author:
Date: 1958-03-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Ghanaian Prime Minister Kwame Nkrumah recently stated in a telegram to Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, his firm belief that the friendly relations between Ghana and Korea will continue to strengthen and will be beneficial to the common interests of the people of both countries.

### 加纳总理说  加朝友谊将加强

1958-03-29
第5版()
专栏：

    加纳总理说
    加朝友谊将加强
    据新华社平壤讯　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：加纳总理恩克鲁玛最近给朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首相金日成的电报中，表示深信加纳和朝鲜两国之间的友好关系将会继续加强，并且对两国人民的共同利益有利。
