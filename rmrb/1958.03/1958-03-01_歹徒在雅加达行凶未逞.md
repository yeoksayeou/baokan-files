Title: Thugs' Attempted Attack in Jakarta Foiled
Author:
Date: 1958-03-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 28th—Near the Capital Cinema in Jakarta, on the evening of the 27th, not long after a movie had ended, someone threw a grenade in front of a hotel next to the cinema. The grenade did not explode. On the morning of the same day, an unexploded grenade was also found at the Sene Station near the old market in Jakarta.

The Indonesian military authorities have immediately launched an investigation into the grenade incidents.

### 歹徒在雅加达行凶未逞

1958-03-01
第6版()
专栏：

    歹徒在雅加达行凶未逞
    新华社雅加达28日电　雅加达首都电影院附近27日傍晚在一场电影散场不久，有人向电影院隔壁的酒店前面扔了一枚手榴弹。这枚手榴弹没有爆炸。同一天上午，在雅加达旧市场附近的塞内火车站也发现了一枚没有爆炸的手榴弹。
    印度尼西亚军事当局已经立即就手榴弹事件进行调查。
