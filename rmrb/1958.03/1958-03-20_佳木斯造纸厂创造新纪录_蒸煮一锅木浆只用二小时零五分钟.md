Title: Jiamusi Paper Mill Creates New Record: Cooking a Pot of Wood Pulp in Only Two Hours and Five Minutes
Author: Zhou Zuojun, Hua Yue
Date: 1958-03-20
Page: 3

Leap Forward List, Who Leads Today?

    Jiamusi Paper Mill Creates New Record
    Cooking a Pot of Wood Pulp in Only Two Hours and Five Minutes
    Our newspaper, Jiamusi, 19th dispatch: The state-owned Jiamusi Integrated Pulp and Paper Mill, on the 18th, spent two hours and five minutes cooking a pot of 110 cubic meter capacity sulfate wood pulp, greatly shortening the designed five hours to cook a pot of wood pulp.
    The national new record for cooking a pot of unbleached wood pulp created by Shixian Paper Mill on March 8 was three hours and forty-eight minutes. (Zhou Zuojun, Hua Yue)

### 佳木斯造纸厂创造新纪录  蒸煮一锅木浆只用二小时零五分钟
周作君  华岳
1958-03-20
第3版()
专栏：跃进榜上，今日谁家领先？

    佳木斯造纸厂创造新纪录
    蒸煮一锅木浆只用二小时零五分钟
    本报佳木斯19日电　国营佳木斯综合制浆造纸厂在18日以二时五分的时间蒸煮出一锅容量一百一十立方公尺的硫酸盐木浆，比设计能力规定的五小时蒸煮一锅木浆的时间大为缩短。
    石岘造纸厂3月8日创造的蒸煮一锅本色木浆的全国新纪录是三时四十八分。　（周作君、华岳）
