Title: Peking Opera Artist Cheng Yanqiu Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1958-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th — Comrade Cheng Yanqiu, representative of the National People's Congress, member of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, executive director of the Chinese Dramatists Association, vice president of the Chinese Academy of Traditional Opera, famous Peking Opera artist, and member of the Communist Party of China, passed away at Beijing Hospital at 8:20 PM on March 9, 1958, due to myocardial embolism and pneumonia, at the age of 54.

Comrade Cheng Yanqiu's coffin has been moved to Jiaxing Temple, and a public memorial service will be held at 10:00 AM on March 13.

The list of members of the Comrade Cheng Yanqiu Funeral Committee is as follows:

    Director: Guo Moruo

    Members (in order of stroke count of surnames): Ding Xilin, Wang Kunlun, Tian Han, Liu Zhiming, Qi Yanming, Shen Yanbing, Ma Xulun, Ma Shaobo, Ma Yanxiang, Zhou Enlai, Zhou Yang, Zhou Xinfang, Zhou Weizhi, Shang Xiaoyun, Chen Shutong, Ouyang Yuqian, Zheng Zhenduo, Xia Yan, Luo Heru, Xun Huisheng, Kang Sheng, Gai Jiaotian, Chu Tunan, Yan Yong, Yang Hansheng, Zhang Geng, Zhang Menggeng, Peng Zhen, He Long, Mei Lanfang, Cai Chusheng, Xiao Changhua, Qian Junrui (with photo)

### 京剧艺术家程砚秋病逝

1958-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    京剧艺术家程砚秋病逝
    新华社10日讯　全国人民代表大会代表、中国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会委员、中国戏剧家协会常务理事、中国戏曲研究院副院长、著名京剧艺术家、中国共产党党员程砚秋同志因患心肌栓塞症和肺炎不治，于1958年3月9日下午八时二十分在北京医院逝世，享年五十四岁。
    程砚秋同志灵柩已移至嘉兴寺，定于3月13日上午十时举行公祭。
    程砚秋同志治丧委员会名单如下：
    主任　郭沫若
