Western Press Clippings

　　Flowery Words

　　The army's air defense experts, in secret testimony before a House committee, admitted that even with the best "Nike" missiles to deal with enemy jet bombers or missiles, at most 30% to 50% would be effective. However, the same experts say in public that America's air defense missiles are 100% effective.
　　　　——American "Newsweek"

　　Much Ado About Nothing

　　The governor of California, Knight (Republican), said that he keeps a batch of emergency food rations in his official car at all times, in case the United States is bombed by the enemy. He also advised the people of California to do the same.
　　　　——American Los Angeles "Mirror"

　　Empty Promise

　　Despite President Eisenhower's many optimistic predictions, the American economy shows no sign of improvement... About three weeks ago, Eisenhower also made a statement that the economic recession was about to end and things were about to improve.
　　Now, the US government has to put aside the outer space issue in order to deal with the most worrying problem of unemployment relief.
　　　　——French "Le Monde"

### 西方报刊拾零

1958-03-24
第5版()
专栏：西方报刊拾零

    西方报刊拾零
    花言巧语
    陆军的防空专家们，在众议院的一个委员会上秘密作证时承认，即使采用最优良的“奈克”式导弹来对付敌人的喷气轰炸机或导弹，顶多30％到50％能够有效。然而，同样是这一批专家，他们在公开场合时说，美国的防空导弹百分之百有效。
          ——美国“新闻周刊”
    庸人自扰
    加利福尼亚州的州长来特（共和党人）说，他在他乘坐的那辆公家的轿车里随时存放一批应急的食粮，以备万一美国受到敌人轰炸时食用。他并且劝告加利福尼亚州的人们也做同样的准备。
        ——美国洛杉矶“镜报”
    空头支票
    尽管艾森豪威尔总统作了许多乐观的预言，但是，美国的经济情况丝毫没有露出好转的迹象……。大约三个星期以前，艾森豪威尔还发表了经济衰退即将结束，情况好转就要到来的谈话。
    现在，美国政府不得不放下外层空间的问题，以便应付当前最令人担心的失业者的救济问题。
            ——法国“世界报”
