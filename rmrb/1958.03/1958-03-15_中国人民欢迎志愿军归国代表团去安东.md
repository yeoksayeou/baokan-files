Title: The Chinese People Welcome the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Homecoming Delegation to Andong
Author:
Date: 1958-03-15
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, March 14th. The Chinese People's Delegation to Welcome the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Homecoming, jointly formed by the Communist Party of China, various people's organizations, and various democratic parties, led by Chen Shutong, head of the delegation, and Wang Weizhou, Shao Lizi, Gao Chongmin, and Li Zhuchen, deputy heads of the delegation, left Beijing by train tonight for Andong.

    Mun Jae-soo, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of North Korea in China, also went to Andong on the same train to welcome the returning troops of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

    Those who saw them off at the station were: Liao Chengzhi, Liu Xiyuan, Wang Shao'ao, Mei Gongbin, Hu Yuzhi, Sun Xiaocun, Yan Xichun and other leaders of the Communist Party of China, various people's organizations, and various democratic parties, as well as senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Zhang Zongxun, General Gan Siqi, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Tang Tianji, and Lieutenant General Tan Xilin. Kim Soon-ho, First Secretary of the Embassy of North Korea in China, was also at the station to see them off.

### 中国人民欢迎志愿军归国代表团去安东

1958-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民欢迎志愿军归国代表团去安东
    新华社14日讯　由中国共产党、各人民团体、各民主党派联合组成的中国人民欢迎志愿军归国代表团，在团长陈叔通、副团长王维舟、邵力子、高崇民、李烛尘等率领下，今天晚上乘火车离开北京前往安东。
    朝鲜驻我国大使馆临时代办文在洙，也同车去安东欢迎志愿军归国部队。
    到车站送行的有：中国共产党、各人民团体、各民主党派负责人廖承志、刘西元、王绍鏊、梅龚彬、胡愈之、孙晓村、严希纯等和中国人民解放军高级将领张宗逊上将、甘泗淇上将、萧向荣中将、唐天际中将、谭希林中将等。到车站送行的还有朝鲜驻我国大使馆一等秘书金淳湖。
