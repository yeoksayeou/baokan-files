Title: Celebrating the Establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, the State Ethnic Affairs Commission Holds a Grand Gathering in Beijing; Congratulatory Telegrams Pour in from All Over the Country

Author:

Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 5th – The State Ethnic Affairs Commission held a grand gathering at the Central Institute for Nationalities tonight to celebrate the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. More than 1,000 people attended the conference, including Zhuang people and people from all ethnic groups, as well as students and cadres, all based in Beijing.

The conference was presided over by Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission.

Yang Jingren, Deputy Director of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, warmly congratulated the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region at the meeting. He said that from this major political event, people can more clearly see that the Chinese Communist Party has always adhered to the principle of equality among all ethnic groups and has unswervingly served the interests of the people of all ethnic groups wholeheartedly.

Yang Jingren said that the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region will surely further enhance the socialist consciousness of the people of all ethnic groups throughout the country, inspire the revolutionary enthusiasm of all ethnic groups, and be conducive to further strengthening unity and mutual assistance among all ethnic groups. He wished the Zhuang and all ethnic groups in Guangxi, under the leadership of the Communist Party, great development and a great leap forward in socialist economic and cultural construction in a short period of time.

After the meeting, a film evening was held.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, March 5th – Congratulatory telegrams have poured in from all over the country to celebrate the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. Up to now, the First Session of the First People's Congress of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region has received congratulatory telegrams the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the State Council, the All-China Federation of Labor Unions, and the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League.

The congratulatory telegrams received by the meeting also included: the People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Committee of the Chinese Communist Party of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, the People's Committee of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, the People's Committee of the Diqing Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture of Yunnan Province, the People's Committee of the Dali Bai Autonomous Prefecture, the People's Committee of the Wenshan Zhuang and Miao Autonomous Prefecture, the People's Committee of the Honghe Hani and Yi Autonomous Prefecture, the People's Committee of the Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Prefecture, the People's Committee of the Qian Dongnan Miao and Dong Autonomous Prefecture of Guizhou Province, the People's Committee of the Qiannan Buyi and Miao Autonomous Prefecture, the Committee of the Chinese Communist Party of the Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture and the People's Committee of the Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture; the People's Committees of Heilongjiang, Qinghai, Gansu, Guizhou, Shaanxi, Hebei, Anhui, Zhejiang, Fujian, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Guangdong, Beijing, Wuhan and other provinces and cities, as well as the Central Institute for Nationalities, etc.

The congratulatory telegrams sent by the autonomous regions of various ethnic groups and various provinces and cities all expressed their determination to further strengthen the unity and mutual assistance of all fraternal ethnic groups, go all out, and build a great socialist motherland.

### 庆祝广西僮族自治区成立  民委在京举行盛大集会  全国各地纷纷致电祝贺

1958-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝广西僮族自治区成立
    民委在京举行盛大集会
    全国各地纷纷致电祝贺
    新华社5日讯　民族事务委员会今晚在中央民族学院举行盛大集会，庆祝广西僮族自治区成立。参加大会的有在北京的僮族和各民族的各界人士、学生和干部，共一千多人。
    大会由民族事务委员会副主任刘春主持。
    民族事务委员会副主任杨静仁在会上热烈祝贺广西僮族自治区的成立。他说，从这件重大的政治事件中，人们可以更清楚地看到中国共产党一贯坚持各民族平等的原则，始终不渝地全心全意为各民族人民的利益服务。
    杨静仁说，广西僮族自治区的成立，必将更加提高全国各民族人民的社会主义觉悟，鼓舞各族人民的革命干劲，有利于进一步加强各民族间的团结和互助。他预祝广西僮族和各民族人民在共产党的领导下，在短时期内获得社会主义经济文化建设的大发展和大跃进。
    会后，举行了电影晚会。
    新华社南宁5日电　全国各地纷纷致电祝贺广西僮族自治区成立。到目前为止，广西僮族自治区第一届人民代表大会第一次会议已经收到了全国人民代表大会常务委员会、国务院、中华全国总工会、共青团中央委员会的贺电。
    会议收到的贺电还有：内蒙古自治区人民委员会、中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会和新疆维吾尔自治区人民委员会、云南省迪庆藏族自治州人民委员会、大理白族自治州人民委员会、文山僮族苗族自治州人民委员会、红河哈尼族彝族自治州人民委员会、怒江傈僳族自治州人民委员会、贵州省黔东南苗族侗族自治州人民委员会、黔南布依族苗族自治州人民委员会、中共湘西土家族苗族自治州委员会和湘西土家族苗族自治州人民委员会；黑龙江、青海、甘肃、贵州、陕西、河北、安徽、浙江、福建、湖北、湖南、江西、广东、北京、武汉等省市人民委员会以及中央民族学院等。
    各民族自治地方和各省、市发来的贺电，都表示要进一步加强各兄弟民族的团结互助，鼓足干劲，建设伟大的社会主义祖国。
