Title: Vice Chairman Lin Boqu Receives Norwegian Parliamentarian Finn Moe
Author:
Date: 1958-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 29th: Lin Boqu, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received today Finn Moe, a member of the Norwegian Labour Party and Chairman of the Parliamentary Foreign Affairs and Constitutional Committee, and his wife, and had lunch with them.

Present at the reception were Zhang Su and Xu Guangping, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General.

Norwegian Ambassador to China, Mr. Krog-Hansen, was also present.

### 林伯渠副委员长接见挪威议员芬·穆

1958-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    林伯渠副委员长接见挪威议员芬·穆
    新华社29日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠今天接见了挪威工党议员，议会外交、宪法委员会主席芬·穆和夫人，并共进午餐。
    接见时在座的有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员张苏、许广平，外交部西欧司司长黄华，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪。
    挪威驻华大使克洛格—亨生也在座。
