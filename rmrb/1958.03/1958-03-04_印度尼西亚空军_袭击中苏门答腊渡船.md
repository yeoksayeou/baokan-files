Title: Indonesian Air Force Attacks Ferry in Central Sumatra
Author:
Date: 1958-03-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 3rd: The Indonesian Air Force press office announced that from February 27th to March 1st, Indonesian Air Force planes attacked ferries in several places in Central Sumatra, thereby more thoroughly cutting off the connections between Padang, seized by the Hussein rebel group, and other places.

Jakarta's "Youth Daily" said today that many residents of Padang and Bukittinggi have fled the main areas and military strongholds seized by the Hussein rebel group, and the armed forces of the rebels have also begun to withdraw to other areas. The families of the rebel group leaders have also evacuated these two cities.

### 印度尼西亚空军  袭击中苏门答腊渡船

1958-03-04
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚空军
    袭击中苏门答腊渡船
    新华社雅加达3日电　印度尼西亚空军新闻处宣布，从2月27日到3月1日，印度尼西亚空军飞机袭击了中苏门答腊几个地方的渡船，从而更加彻底地切断了侯赛尼叛乱集团窃据的巴东同其他地方的联系。
    雅加达“青年报”今天说，巴东和武吉丁宜地方的许多居民已经逃离侯赛尼叛乱集团所窃据的主要地区和军事据点，叛乱分子的武装部队也开始撤往其他地区。叛乱集团头目们的家属也撤离了这两个城市。
