Title: The True Popular Nature of Soviet Democracy: Two-Thirds of the Representatives are Workers and Peasants; The First Session of the Fifth Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to be Held at the End of the Month
Author:
Date: 1958-03-21
Page: 6

    The True Popular Nature of Soviet Democracy
    Two-Thirds of the Representatives are Workers and Peasants
    The First Session of the Fifth Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to be Held at the End of the Month
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 20th—According to TASS, Moscow, March 19th: The Central Election Commission of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union held its final meeting in Moscow on the 19th. At the meeting, the committee signed the document regarding the final results of the election of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. Grishin, Chairman of the Central Election Commission, pointed out in his speech at the meeting that this election and its results once again eloquently demonstrated the true popular nature of Soviet democracy.
    Almost half of the newly elected representatives to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union are workers and collective farm members directly engaged in industrial and agricultural production. According to social status, two-thirds of the representatives are workers and peasants. The rest are representatives of the people's intellectuals. Women representatives account for over 26%.
    All nationalities, Union Republics, Autonomous Republics, Autonomous Regions, and National Districts of the Soviet Union have their own representatives in the Supreme Soviet.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 20th—TASS, Moscow, March 20th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union decided to convene the first session of the Fifth Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union in Moscow on March 27th.

### 苏维埃民主的真正人民性  三分之二的代表是工农  第五届苏联最高苏维埃首次会议月底举行

1958-03-21
第6版()
专栏：

    苏维埃民主的真正人民性
    三分之二的代表是工农
    第五届苏联最高苏维埃首次会议月底举行
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科19日讯：苏联最高苏维埃中央选举委员会19日在莫斯科举行了最后一次会议。会上签署了委员会关于苏联最高苏维埃选举最后结果的文件。中央选举委员会主席格里申在会上发言指出，这次选举和选举的结果又一次雄辩地证明了苏维埃民主的真正人民性。
    新选出的苏联最高苏维埃代表中几乎有一半是直接参加工农业生产的工人和集体农庄的庄员。按社会地位划分，有三分之二的代表是工人和农民。其余的是人民知识分子的代表。妇女代表占26％强。
    苏联各民族、各加盟共和国和自治共和国、各自治州和民族专区，在最高苏维埃里都有自己的代表。
    新华社20日讯　塔斯社莫斯科20日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团决定于3月27日在莫斯科召开第五届苏联最高苏维埃第一次会议。
