Title: Hungarian Party and Government Delegation Visits Democratic Germany
Author:
Date: 1958-03-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 19th: A Hungarian Party and Government delegation headed by Münnich left Budapest on the afternoon of the 19th to visit the German Democratic Republic.

### 匈党政代表团赴民主德国访问

1958-03-21
第6版()
专栏：

    匈党政代表团
    赴民主德国访问
    新华社布达佩斯19日电以明尼赫为首的匈牙利党和政府代表团，19日下午离开布达佩斯前往德意志民主共和国访问。
