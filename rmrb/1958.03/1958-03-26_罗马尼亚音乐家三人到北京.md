Title: Three Romanian Musicians Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 25th. Three Romanian musicians arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to conduct a friendly visit and performance in China.

The female singer S. Simonetti and flute player V. Jiannu, who arrived in Beijing today, are Honored Artists and National Prize winners of the Romanian People's Republic, and pianist R. Negreanu is a professor and music critic at the Bucharest Conservatory of Music. They will soon hold performances in Beijing.

### 罗马尼亚音乐家三人到北京

1958-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚音乐家三人到北京
    新华社25日讯　应我国对外文化联络委员会的邀请前来中国进行友好访问演出的罗马尼亚音乐家三人在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    今天到北京的女歌唱家斯·西蒙乃迪和横笛演奏家瓦·日安努是罗马尼亚人民共和国的功勋演员和国家奖金获得者，钢琴家拉·奈格连努是布加勒斯特音乐学院教授和音乐评论家。他们即将在北京举行演出。
