Title: World Peace Council Standing Committee Holds Meeting to Prepare for Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 24th. The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council continued its meeting yesterday and today at the Science Palace here. A press release issued by the Standing Committee said that the meeting on March 23 studied the organizational issues of the Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference to be held from July 16 to 22, based on the current situation.

    Tikhonov of the Soviet Union described the various activities carried out by the Soviet people to strive for peace, and then put forward some suggestions on the organization of the Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference.

    Sahib Singh Sokhey of India requested the Standing Committee to pay special attention to the Algerian issue and the Indonesian issue.

    Dr. Francis of the Algerian National Liberation Front was invited to speak on the situation in Algeria and introduced how the Algerian people are carrying out the struggle for independence under difficult conditions.

    Earlier, the Standing Committee appointed a committee to draft a report on the Algerian issue. Another committee was also appointed to prepare a report on the Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference.

### 世界和平理事会常委会举行会议  筹备裁军和国际合作大会

1958-03-25
第5版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常委会举行会议
    筹备裁军和国际合作大会
    据新华社新德里24日电　世界和平理事会常务委员会昨天和今天在这里的科学宫继续举行会议。常务委员会发表的新闻公报说，3月23日的会议根据目前的形势研究了将在7月16日到22日举行的裁军和国际合作大会的组织问题。
    苏联的吉洪诺夫叙述了苏联人民为了争取和平所进行的种种活动，然后就裁军和国际合作大会的组织问题提出了一些建议。
    印度的萨希布·辛格·索克要求常务委员会特别注意阿尔及利亚问题和印度尼西亚问题。
    阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线的弗朗西斯博士应邀就阿尔及利亚的情况发表讲话，并且介绍了阿尔及利亚人民怎样在艰苦情况下进行着争取独立的斗争。
    早些时候，常务委员会指定一个委员会起草关于阿尔及利亚问题的报告。还指定了另外一个委员会来准备关于裁军和国际合作大会的报告。
