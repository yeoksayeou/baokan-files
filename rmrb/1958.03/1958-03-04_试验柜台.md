Title: Experimental Counter

Author: 
Date: 1958-03-04
Page: 3

    Experimental Counter
    Jinan No. 1 Department Store opened a new experimental counter today—a cosmetics specialty counter. Seventeen leading cadres, including the store's Communist Party branch secretary and manager, will serve as salespersons. This experimental group, composed of these leading cadres, will be fully responsible for the purchase and sale business of the experimental counter. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 试验柜台

1958-03-04
第3版()
专栏：

    试验柜台
    济南市第一百货零售商店今天新辟了一个试验柜台——化妆品专业柜台，由这个店的中共支部书记、经理等十七个领导干部担任营业员。这些领导干部组成的试验小组，将对试验柜台的购销业务全部负责到底。　（据新华社讯）
