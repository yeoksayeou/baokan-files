Date: 1958-03-05
Page: 7

    "China Government Delegation's Visit to North Korea" Documentary Shown in Beijing

    (Our newspaper dispatch) The news documentary "China Government Delegation's Visit to North Korea" met with audiences in Beijing on the 4th.

    The film recorded the grand welcome received by the Chinese government delegation led by Premier Zhou in North Korea; the talks held between the Chinese and North Korean delegations; the signing ceremony of the Sino-Korean Joint Statement; and the delegation's visits to North Korean factories, rural areas, and other scenes.

### “中国政府代表团访问朝鲜”记录片在北京上映

1958-03-05
第7版()
专栏：

    “中国政府代表团访问朝鲜”记录片在北京上映
    本报讯　“中国政府代表团访问朝鲜”的新闻纪录影片4日和北京观众见面。
    影片纪录了周总理率领的我国政府代表团在朝鲜受到欢迎的盛况；中朝两国代表团举行会谈；中朝联合声明签字仪式；以及代表团参观朝鲜工厂、农村等情景。
