Title: Before Returning Home (Photos)
Author: Meng Qingbiao, Meng Xianquan
Date: 1958-03-15
Page: 4

Column:

    Before Returning Home
    Before returning home, officers and soldiers of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteers deeply mourned the martyrs who sacrificed their precious lives on this land.
                          Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao photo
    Before leaving Korea, soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers planted pine trees, symbolizing evergreen friendship, in front of their positions and barracks.
                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Xianquan photo
    Before leaving Korea, the Second Class Merit Citation Company, which attacked Dingzi Mountain, held a get-together with a certain unit of the Korean People's Army. Soldiers from China and Korea warmly embraced at the gathering. Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao photo
    Before leaving Korea, the Chinese People's Volunteers wrote letters to thank the Korean Workers' Party, the Korean government, and the Korean people for their strong support during the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea. This is a picture of the soldiers signing the letters of thanks.
                   Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Xianquan photo

专栏：

    归国之前
    志愿军某部官兵在归国之前，向在这块土地上献出自己宝贵生命的烈士们致以深沉的悼念。
                          新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
    中国人民志愿军的战士们在离开朝鲜前，把象征友谊长青的松树种植在阵地营房前。
                        新华社记者　孟宪全摄
    攻打丁字山的二等功臣连在离朝前，和朝鲜人民军某部联欢。中朝两国战士在联欢会上热烈拥抱。　新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
    中国人民志愿军在离开朝鲜之前写信感谢朝鲜劳动党、朝鲜政府和朝鲜人民在抗美援朝战争中的大力支援。这是战士们在感谢信上签名。
                   新华社记者　孟宪全摄
