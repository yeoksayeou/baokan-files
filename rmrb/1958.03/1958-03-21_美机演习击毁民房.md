Title: US Aircraft Exercise Destroys Civilian Housing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-21
Page: 5

    US Aircraft Exercise Destroys Civilian Housing
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 20th (TASS): Another dangerous incident has occurred in Wisconsin, USA.
    On the evening of March 17, a "B-47" jet bomber participating in shooting practice near Sheboygan Falls "mistakenly" fired upon two towns. The bomber's bullets hit houses and streets in the towns of Sheboygan Falls and Westburg. Some houses in both towns were damaged.
    The "Chicago Tribune's" Sheboygan Falls correspondent said, "Citizens felt very panicked."
    Representative Pellt, elected from Wisconsin to the House of Representatives, issued a statement regarding the incident, demanding that measures be taken to prevent similar incidents from happening in the future.

### 美机演习击毁民房

1958-03-21
第5版()
专栏：

    美机演习击毁民房
    据新华社讯　据塔斯社纽约20日讯：美国威斯康星州又发生一椿危险事件。
    3月17日傍晚，在设博伊干瀑布市附近参加射击练习的“B—47”喷气轰炸机“错误地”对两个市镇射击。轰炸机的子弹射到设博伊干瀑布市和威斯特堡这两个市镇的房屋上和街道上。两个市镇都有一些房屋遭到破坏。
    “芝加哥论坛报”驻设博伊干瀑布市记者说“市民感到非常惶恐。”
    从威斯康星州选出的众议院议员佩耳特针对这个事件发表了一个声明，要求采取措施来防止以后发生类似的事件。
