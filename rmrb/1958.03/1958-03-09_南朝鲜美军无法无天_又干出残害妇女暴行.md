Title: U.S. Troops in South Korea Run Rampant, Commit Atrocities Against Women Again
Author:
Date: 1958-03-09
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 7th: Seoul dispatch: According to a report by the South Korean World News Agency, on the evening of the 4th, another tragedy occurred in which U.S. troops stationed in South Korea harmed Korean women.
    The report said that near the base of the U.S. 7th Division, a group of American soldiers forcibly pulled seven Korean women passing by onto their trucks. As the truck sped away, three of the Korean women broke free from the U.S. soldiers and jumped from the truck. One of the women, a 24-year-old named Jin Jinshun, died on the spot after hitting her head when she jumped from the truck, and the other two were seriously injured.

### 南朝鲜美军无法无天  又干出残害妇女暴行

1958-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

    南朝鲜美军无法无天
    又干出残害妇女暴行
    新华社平壤7日电　汉城消息：据南朝鲜世界通讯社报道，在4日晚上，又发生一件驻在南朝鲜的美军残害朝鲜妇女的惨案。
    消息说，在美军第七师的驻地附近，有一批美国军人把路过的七个朝鲜妇女强拉上他们的卡车。在卡车飞驰的时候，有三个朝鲜妇女从美军手中挣脱以后就从车上跳下来，其中一个二十四岁名叫金今顺的朝鲜妇女从车上跳下后因脑袋碰碎当场死亡，另外两个受重伤。
