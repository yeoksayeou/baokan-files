Author:
Date: 1958-03-27
Page: 2

    Double-Wheel Double-Tube Waterwheel
    Created by Shang Benli, deputy director of the Qianjin No. 8 Cooperative in Changge County, Henan Province. Its main components are a wooden frame, sprockets, and a large wooden swing wheel, utilizing the pipes and chains from Liberation-style waterwheels. This type of waterwheel has a high speed and fast water output, allowing two people to irrigate eight to ten mu of land per day.

### 双轮双管水车

1958-03-27
第2版()
专栏：

    双轮双管水车
    河南长葛县前进八社副社长尚本立创造。其主要部分是木架、链轮和大木甩轮，利用解放式水车的管子和链子。这种水车速度猛，出水快，两个人每天可浇地八到十亩。
