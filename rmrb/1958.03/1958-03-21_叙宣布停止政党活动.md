Title: Syria Announces Cessation of Party Activities
Author:
Date: 1958-03-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus - Syrian Regional Interior Minister Sarraj announced on the 18th that, according to President Nasser's recent decision to dissolve political parties in the Syrian region, all political parties and organizations are considered to have ended.

He also said that after the publication of this announcement, no political party may hold gatherings, and those who oppose it will be punished.

### 叙宣布停止政党活动

1958-03-21
第5版()
专栏：

    叙宣布停止政党活动
    据新华社大马士革电　叙利亚地区内政部长萨拉杰18日宣布，根据纳赛尔总统最近宣布的关于解散叙利亚地区的政党的决定，一切政党和政治组织都被认为已经结束。
    他又说，在这个公告发表之后，任何政党都不得举行集会，反对的一定要受到惩罚。
