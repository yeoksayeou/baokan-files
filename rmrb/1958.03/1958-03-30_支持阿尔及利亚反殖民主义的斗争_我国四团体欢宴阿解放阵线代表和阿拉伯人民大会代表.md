Title: Supporting Algeria's Anti-Colonialist Struggle: Four Chinese Organizations Host Banquet for Algerian Liberation Front Representatives and Arab People's Congress Representatives
Author:
Date: 1958-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 29th. The Chinese Asian Solidarity Committee, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the China Islamic Association, and the Sino-Arab Friendship Association hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Brahim Ghafa, representative of the Algerian National Liberation Front, and Adef Danial, representative of the Arab People's Congress.

Mao Dun, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Asian Solidarity Committee, delivered a speech at the banquet. He said that the Chinese people, who have deeply suffered from colonialism, admire and feel very close to the Algerian people and the Arab people who are fighting heroically against colonialism and constantly achieving victories. Mao Dun also said: "The two gentlemen bring the friendship of the Algerian and Arab people to China, and I hope that the two gentlemen will bring back the friendship of the Chinese people." Mao Dun proposed a toast to the friendship between China and the Algerian and Arab people, to world peace, and to the ultimate and complete victory of the Algerian people in their struggle against colonialism.

Brahim Ghafa then spoke. He said: The warm reception I have received in China has increased my confidence in the victory of the struggle. Our friendship in the struggle shows that the whole world is united for peace. What we want is freedom, independence and peace. The Chinese people's helping hand to us is a major contribution to world peace.

Adef Danial also spoke, wishing that the friendship between the Chinese and Arab peoples would continue to expand and strengthen.

Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Zhang Jie, Vice-President of the Sino-Arab Friendship Association, also spoke at the meeting. They unanimously expressed their firm support for the Algerian people's just struggle against colonialism and wished the friendship between China and the Algerian and Arab peoples to continue to expand and strengthen.

Also present at the banquet tonight were Da Punsheng, Xu Guangping, Ji Chaoding and many others.

### 支持阿尔及利亚反殖民主义的斗争  我国四团体欢宴阿解放阵线代表和阿拉伯人民大会代表

1958-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    支持阿尔及利亚反殖民主义的斗争
    我国四团体欢宴阿解放阵线代表和阿拉伯人民大会代表
    新华社29日讯　中国亚洲团结委员会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国伊斯兰教协会、中阿友好协会今日晚间设宴，欢迎阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表卜拉欣·加法和阿拉伯人民大会代表阿德夫·达尼尔。
    中国亚洲团结委员会副主席茅盾在宴会上首先致词。他说，曾经深受殖民主义压迫的中国人民，对于正为反对殖民主义进行英勇斗争并不断取得胜利的阿尔及利亚人民和阿拉伯人民是十分钦佩的，是感到很亲切的。茅盾还说：“两位先生带着阿尔及利亚和阿拉伯人民的友谊到中国来，我希望两位先生再带着中国人民的友谊回去。”茅盾建议，为中国同阿尔及利亚和阿拉伯人民的友谊，为世界和平，为阿尔及利亚人民在反对殖民主义的斗争中取得最后的彻底的胜利干杯。
    卜拉欣·加法接着讲话。他说：我在中国受到的热情款待使我增加了对斗争胜利的信心。我们在斗争中的友谊，说明全世界为了和平是团结的。我们要的是自由、独立与和平。中国人民向我们伸出支援的手，也就是对世界和平的重大贡献。
    阿德夫·达尼尔也讲了话，他祝中国和阿拉伯人民的友谊不断扩大和增强。
    全国妇联副主席李德全、中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕、中阿友协副会长张杰也先后在会上讲话，他们一致表示坚决支持阿尔及利亚人民反对殖民主义的正义斗争，并祝中国同阿尔及利亚和阿拉伯人民的友谊，不断扩大增强。
    出席今晚宴会的，还有达浦生、许广平、冀朝鼎等多人。
