Title: To the Arctic to Perform! (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-03-11
Page: 6

    To the Arctic to Perform!
    Some actors from Moscow, the capital of the Soviet Union, recently set off to perform in the Arctic. They will bring Moscow's song and dance to the coldest places, bringing the cultural life of the capital of the socialist country to the remote north. In addition to performing themselves, these actors will also help local amateur song and dance troupes.

### 到北极去演戏！（图片）

1958-03-11
第6版()
专栏：

    到北极去演戏！
    苏联首都莫斯科的一些演员们，最近出发到北极去演出。他们将把莫斯科的歌舞搬到最寒冷的地方，把社会主义国家首都的文化生活带到边远的北方。这些演员们除自己上演外，还要帮助当地的业余歌舞剧团。
