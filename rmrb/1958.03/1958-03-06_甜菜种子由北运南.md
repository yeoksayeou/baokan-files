Mutual Support, Close as Brothers

    Sugar Beet Seeds Transported from North to South
    (Reported by this newspaper) Heilongjiang Province is using a large quantity of sugar beet seeds to support the needs of Enshi, Yichang, and Xiangyang Special Zones in Hubei Province for developing sugar beet production. These seeds were transferred from Suihua and other places in Heilongjiang in late February and are expected to arrive in Hankou in early March.

### 甜菜种子由北运南

1958-03-06
第2版()
专栏：互相支援  亲如兄弟

    甜菜种子由北运南
    本报讯　黑龙江省用大量的甜菜种子支援湖北省恩施、宜昌、襄阳三个专区发展甜菜生产的需要。这些种子在2月下旬由黑龙江绥化等地调出，预计3月上旬可以全部到汉口。
