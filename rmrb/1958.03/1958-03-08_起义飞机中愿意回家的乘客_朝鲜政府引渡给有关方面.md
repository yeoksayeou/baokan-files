Title: Passengers on the Uprising Plane Who Wished to Return Home: Korean Government Extradited to Relevant Parties
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th - According to the Korean Central News Agency's Panmunjom dispatch, the representative of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea handed over at 7:30 PM today in Panmunjom twenty-two Koreans, two Germans, and two Americans from among the people who arrived in northern Korea on the uprising South Korean civil aircraft, who wished to return home, along with their belongings, to representatives of the South Korean Red Cross, representatives of the Government of the Federal Republic of Germany, and representatives of the U.S. government, respectively.

On the certificate regarding the extradition and reception of the aforementioned personnel, the signatory as the extraditing party was Kim Joon-kyung, Colonel of the Korean People's Army and representative of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the signatories as the receiving parties were Kim Ho-jin, representative of the South Korean Red Cross, Mr. Weil, Counselor of the U.S. "Embassy" in Seoul, and Mr. Hertz, "Minister" of the Federal Republic of Germany to Seoul.

The aforementioned extradited personnel received preferential treatment and protection during their stay in the northern part of the Republic, and their health was good.

### 起义飞机中愿意回家的乘客  朝鲜政府引渡给有关方面

1958-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    起义飞机中愿意回家的乘客
    朝鲜政府引渡给有关方面
    新华社平壤6日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社板门店讯，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的代表今天下午七时三十分在板门店把乘着起义的南朝鲜民航机到达朝鲜北部的人中，希望回家的朝鲜人二十二名、德国人两名和美国人两名连同他们所携带的物品，分别引渡给南朝鲜红十字会代表、德意志联邦共和国政府代表和美国政府代表。
    在关于引渡和接收上述人员的证明书上，作为引渡一方签字的是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表、朝鲜人民军上校金骏经，作为接收一方签字的是南朝鲜红十字会代表金浩镇、美国驻汉城“大使馆”参赞威尔和德意志联邦共和国驻汉城“公使”赫尔兹。
    被引渡的上述人员在共和国北部期间都受到了优待和保护，他们的健康状况良好。
