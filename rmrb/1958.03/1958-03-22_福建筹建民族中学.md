Title: Fujian Prepares to Establish a National Middle School
Author:
Date: 1958-03-22
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 21st - Fujian Province's first middle school for minority nationalities will be established in Fu'an County this year. Currently, the construction of the school buildings is underway. The establishment of this middle school will greatly facilitate the enrollment of minority nationality primary school graduates into higher education. During the first Five-Year Plan period, the Fu'an Special District specifically established ninety-nine minority nationality primary schools, with two thousand five hundred and thirty students. Furthermore, one hundred and sixty-one primary schools also enrolled one thousand seven hundred and eighty-six minority nationality students.

### 福建筹建民族中学

1958-03-22
第7版()
专栏：

    福建筹建民族中学
    新华社福州21日电　福建省第一所少数民族中学今年将在福安县建立。目前，校舍筹建工作正在进行。这所中学的建立，将为少数民族小学毕业生升学带来很大方便。在第一个五年计划期间，福安专区专门设立了少数民族小学九十九所，学生二千五百三十人，并有一百六十一所小学兼收少数民族学生一千七百八十六人。
