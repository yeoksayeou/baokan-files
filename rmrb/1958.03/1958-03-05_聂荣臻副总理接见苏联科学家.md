Title: Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Receives Soviet Scientists
Author:
Date: 1958-03-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the Science Planning Commission, Nie Rongzhen, received Academician V.S. Nemchinov, member of the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and Chairman of the Council for the Study of Productive Forces, and fourteen other Soviet scientists who came to China to participate in the second meeting of the Sino-Soviet Heilongjiang (Amur River) Joint Academic Committee, on the evening of the 4th.
    After the reception, Guo Moruo hosted a banquet to welcome the Soviet scientists headed by V.S. Nemchinov.

    接见后，郭沫若设宴欢迎以?姆钦诺夫为首的苏联科学家。
