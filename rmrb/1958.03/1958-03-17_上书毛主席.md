Title: Letter to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1958-03-17
Page: 1

    Letter to Our Respected Chairman Mao:
    Under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and yourself, our country has achieved great victories in the socialist revolution on the political and ideological fronts, and a new situation of a great leap forward has emerged in all aspects of the country. Inspired by the new situation in the motherland, our democratic parties and non-party democratic人士 have improved their socialist consciousness. Now, fundamentally transforming ourselves, breaking with the capitalist stance, establishing the socialist stance, and strengthening ideological remolding have become our unanimous and urgent demands.
    Today, with fiery enthusiasm, we gathered in front of Tiananmen Square in the capital and unanimously adopted the Socialist Self-Remolding Covenant.
    We assure you that under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and yourself, we will conscientiously transform our political stance and ideology, improve our work style, offer our hearts, resolutely take the socialist road, and contribute all our knowledge and strength to promote the socialist cause.
          With the highest revolutionary salute!
          Socialist Self-Remolding Promotion Conference of
            Democratic Parties and Non-Party Democratic人士, March 16, 1958

### 上书毛主席

1958-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    上书毛主席敬爱的毛主席：
    在中国共产党和您的英明领导下，我们国家在政治战线上和思想战线上的社会主义革命取得了伟大的胜利，全国各方面出现了大跃进的新形势。我们各民主党派和无党派民主人士在祖国的新形势的鼓舞下，社会主义觉悟有所提高。现在，进行根本的自我改造、破资本主义立场、立社会主义立场、加强思想改造，已经成为我们一致的、迫切的要求。
    今天，我们以火一般的热情，在首都天安门前集会，一致通过了社会主义自我改造公约。
    我们向您保证：我们一定要在中国共产党和您的领导下，认真改造政治立场和思想意识，改进作风，交出心来，坚决走社会主义道路，贡献出全部知识和力量，为促进社会主义事业而奋斗。
          谨致崇高的革命敬礼！
          民主党派和无党派民主人士社会主义
            自我改造促进大会　1958年3月16日
