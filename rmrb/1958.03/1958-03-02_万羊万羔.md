Title: "Ten Thousand Sheep, Ten Thousand Lambs"
Author:
Date: 1958-03-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency:

    "Ten Thousand Sheep, Ten Thousand Lambs"
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Xinjiang Military Region Production and Construction Corps rewarded the 28th Ranch, which had reached the "Ten Thousand Sheep, Ten Thousand Lambs" target, at the Advanced Producers Conference. Last year, this ranch grazed 9,146 ewes and bred 9,851 lambs. By the end of last year, a total of 9,280 lambs had been raised, with a breeding rate of 101.46%, creating a record of "Ten Thousand Sheep, Ten Thousand Lambs."

### “万羊万羔”

1958-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    “万羊万羔”
    据新华社讯　新疆军区建设兵团在先进生产者会议上，奖励了达到“万羊万羔”指标的第二十八牧场。这个牧场去年放牧九千一百四十六只母绵羊，繁殖了九千八百五十一只羊羔。到去年底，共育成羔羊九千二百八十只，繁育率达到101.46％，创造了“万羊万羔”的纪录。
