Title: Dredging the Middle Reaches of the Jialing River Channel
Author:
Date: 1958-03-20
Page: 3

    Dredging the Middle Reaches of the Jialing River Channel
    (Xinhua News Agency) A large-scale channel dredging project has recently begun in the middle reaches of the Jialing River.
    More than ten thousand workers are working hard on the 260-kilometer stretch from Nanchong to Hechuan, blasting reefs, building dams to scour channels, and excavating new waterways. The Jialing River is a major waterway running through the Sichuan Basin.

### 整治嘉陵江中游航道

1958-03-20
第3版()
专栏：

    整治嘉陵江中游航道
    据新华社讯　大规模的整治航道工作最近在嘉陵江中游展开。
    嘉陵江中游从南充到合川之间二百六十多公里的工地上，万多工人正在紧张地爆破暗礁、筑坝淘漕和开凿新航道。嘉陵江是贯穿四川盆地的一条主要航道。
