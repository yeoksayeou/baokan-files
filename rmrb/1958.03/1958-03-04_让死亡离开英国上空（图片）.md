Title: Let Death Leave the Skies Over Britain (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-03-04
Page: 5

    Let Death Leave the Skies Over Britain
    These are two placards used by the people of the Chelsea and Fulham districts of London during their march on March 1st. The upper placard reads "Let Death Leave the Skies Over Britain" on the left, and "End the American Hydrogen Bomb Threat" on the right. The lower placard reads "Maintain Britain's Integrity" and "Abolish American Bases."
　　(Xinhua News Agency Draft)

### 让死亡离开英国上空（图片）

1958-03-04
第5版()
专栏：

    让死亡离开英国上空
    这是伦敦切尔西和富尔罕区人民在3月1日游行时用的两块标语牌。上面标语的左边是“让死亡离开英国上空”，右边是“结束美国的氢弹威胁”。下面标语是“维护英国的完整”和“废弃美国的基地”。
　　（新华社稿）
