Title: Semiconductor Radio

Author: 
Date: 1958-03-13
Page: 3

    Semiconductor Radio
    China's first semiconductor radio was recently born in Shanghai.
    This radio, powered by three dry cell batteries used in flashlights, can continuously operate for four to five hundred hours.
    It was collaboratively trial-produced by nine factories including Hongyin Wireless Equipment Factory, Tianhe Electrochemical Factory, and the Shanghai Radio Technology Research Institute, and it only took forty days. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 半导体收音机

1958-03-13
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    半导体收音机
    我国第一架半导体收音机最近在上海诞生。
    这种收音机以三节手电筒里用的干电池作电源，就可以连续收音四、五百个小时。
    它是宏音无线电器材厂、天和电化厂等九个工厂以及上海无线电技术研究所协作试制出来的，只花了四十天时间。（据新华社讯）
