Title: East German Youth Organization Invites Djamila Bouhired to Study in Germany

Author:

Date: 1958-03-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 11th. According to the German News Agency, Berlin, 10th: The Free German Youth League organization of the State Secretariat for Higher Education of the German Democratic Republic sent a telegram to Djamila Bouhired, the imprisoned Algerian patriotic young woman, inviting her to study at schools in the German Democratic Republic.

The Free German Youth League organization of the University of Halle also expressed in another telegram to Bouhired its willingness to sponsor her studies in the German Democratic Republic.

On the 10th, people from all walks of life in the German Democratic Republic sent telegrams to French President Coty, demanding the revocation of the death sentence against Bouhired.

### 民主德国青年组织  邀布伊海德去德国求学

1958-03-12
第6版()
专栏：

    民主德国青年组织
    邀布伊海德去德国求学
    新华社11日讯　据德意志通讯社柏林10日讯：德意志民主共和国高等教育国务秘书处的自由德国青年联盟组织打电报给在狱中的阿尔及利亚爱国女青年布伊海德，邀请她到民主德国的学校去学习。
    哈雷大学的自由德国青年联盟组织在打给布伊海德的另一个电报中也表示愿意资助她在民主德国学习。
    民主德国各界人士10日纷纷打电报给法国总统戈蒂，要求撤销对布伊海德的死刑判决。
