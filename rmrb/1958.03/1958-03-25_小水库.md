Small Reservoir (Rhyme)

Having built a small reservoir,
It is like raising a dragon.
It can irrigate in dry weather,
And store floodwater in times of flooding.
All grains are harvested abundantly,
Forever free from poverty.

### 小水库

1958-03-25
第2版()
专栏：

    小水库（歌谣）修起小水库，如养一条龙。旱天能灌溉，涝天能蓄洪。五谷都丰收，永远不受穷。
