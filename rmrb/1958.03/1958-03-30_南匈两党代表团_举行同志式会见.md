Title: South-Hungarian Party Delegations Hold Comradely Meeting
Author:
Date: 1958-03-30
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, March 29th.
According to the Yugoslav News Agency, on March 27th and 28th, the delegation of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia and the delegation of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party held a comradely meeting in a certain place in Vojvodina Province, and exchanged opinions on issues of mutual concern in a friendly atmosphere.
    The representatives of the Yugoslav side included Tito, Kardelj, Ranković, etc.
    The representatives of the Hungarian side included Kádár, Kállai, Fock, etc.

### 南匈两党代表团  举行同志式会见

1958-03-30
第3版()
专栏：

    南匈两党代表团
    举行同志式会见
    新华社贝尔格莱德29日电
  据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，3月27日和28日，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会代表团和匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会代表团，在伏伊伏丁那省某地举行了同志式的会见，并且在友好气氛中就双方关心的问题交换了意见。
    南斯拉夫方面的代表有铁托、卡德尔、兰科维奇等。
    匈牙利方面的代表有卡达尔、卡拉伊、福克等。
