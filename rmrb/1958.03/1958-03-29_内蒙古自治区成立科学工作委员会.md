Title: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Establishes Science Work Committee
Author:
Date: 1958-03-29
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, March 27th. The Science Work Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region was established in Hohhot City on the 26th. The committee is headed by Ha Feng'a (Mongolian), Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Committee; Hu Zhaoheng, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Li Jidong, Vice President of Inner Mongolia University, and Hu Erqin Bilige (Mongolian), Director of the Autonomous Region Health Department, serve as deputy directors; Keli Geng (Mongolian), Director of the Autonomous Region Industry Department, and twenty-two others are members.

The committee's main tasks this year are to formulate a science plan; to organize scientific and technological forces to solve scientific and technological problems in the Great Leap Forward in production; and to formulate a plan to cultivate and strengthen Inner Mongolia's scientific workforce. In addition, this year it will also publish "Science Briefs," prepare to establish a night university in Hohhot, and strengthen popular science work.

### 内蒙古自治区成立科学工作委员会

1958-03-29
第7版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区成立科学工作委员会
    新华社呼和浩特27日电　内蒙古自治区科学工作委员会二十六日在呼和浩特市成立。委员会由内蒙古自治区人民委员会副主席哈丰阿（蒙古族）担任主任；中共内蒙古自治区委员会宣传部部长胡昭衡、内蒙古大学副校长李继侗、自治区卫生厅厅长胡尔钦毕力格（蒙古族）担任副主任；自治区工业厅厅长克力更（蒙古族）等二十二人为委员。
    这个委员会今年的主要工作是制定科学规划；组织科学技术力量来解决生产大跃进中的科学技术问题；制定培养和壮大内蒙古科学工作队伍的规划。另外，今年还要出版“科学简报”，在呼和浩特市筹建夜大学和加强科学技术普及工作。
