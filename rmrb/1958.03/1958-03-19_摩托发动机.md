    New Products

    Motorcycle Engine
    The Qunli Machinery Factory in Harbin has successfully trial-produced a "Rocket Brand Motorcycle Engine." Its top speed is 30 kilometers per hour, and it consumes only a little over one kilogram of gasoline per 100 kilometers. It is not only suitable for converting ordinary bicycles into motorcycles, but also suitable for various small agricultural machinery, such as engines for sprayers, small air compressors, small generators, small movie projectors, small motorboats, and water bicycles.

### 摩托发动机

1958-03-19
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    摩托发动机
    哈尔滨群力机械厂试制成功了一种“火箭牌摩托车发动机”，时速最高为三十公里，每百里仅用汽油一公斤多。它不仅适合于改普通自行车为摩托车用，而且还适合于各种小型农业机械，如作为喷雾器，小型空气压缩机、小型发电机、小型电影机、小汽船以及水上自行车等发动机用。
