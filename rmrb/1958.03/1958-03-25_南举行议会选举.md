Title: Yugoslavia Holds Parliamentary Elections
Author:
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, March 24th: The Yugoslav News Agency reported that Yugoslavia held elections for members of the Federal Chamber of the Federal People's Assembly and the Republican Chambers of the National Assemblies of the six republics on the 23rd.

    The total number of voters in Yugoslavia is 11,337,000. This election will elect 301 Federal Chamber members and 810 Republican Chamber members from various republics. Preparations for the election lasted approximately two and a half months, with about ten million voters participating in various pre-election rallies. Voting began at 7:00 AM on the 23rd. Many voters cast their ballots within the first few hours of the morning. In many places, voting ended at noon. Some Yugoslav voters actually began voting on March 16th, including military personnel and embassy staff stationed abroad. Elections for the Producers' Chamber of the Federal People's Assembly and the Producers' Chambers of the National Assemblies of various republics will be held on March 26th, 27th, and 28th.

### 南举行议会选举

1958-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

    南举行议会选举
    新华社贝尔格莱德24日电  据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫在23日举行了联邦国民议会联邦院和六个共和国国民议会共和国院议员的选举。
    南斯拉夫的选民总数是一千一百三十三万七千人。这次选举将选出三百零一名联邦院议员和八百一十名各共和国院的议员。选举前的准备工作进行了约两个半月，有一千万左右的选民参加了各种选举前的集会。投票在23日上午七时开始。很多选民在上午头几小时内就投了票。许多地方的投票在中午就结束了。部分南斯拉夫选民实际上在3月16日就已经开始投票了，这些人包括军队中的人员和驻外使馆的人员等。联邦国民议会生产者院和各共和国国民议会生产者院的选举，将在3月26日、27日和28日举行。
