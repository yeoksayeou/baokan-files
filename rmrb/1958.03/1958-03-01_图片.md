Title: He Aiqun Chen Jie
Author: He Aiqun, Chen Jie
Date: 1958-03-01
Page: 3

    Old worker Li Hongru of the Xi'an Tianxiang Instrument Factory has maintained the glorious title of advanced producer for four consecutive years. Since he came to work at this factory in 1954, he has never been absent for four years, and his workload has been completed ahead of schedule every year. Now he has proposed to complete the annual production quota in the third quarter of this year. This is him installing optical instruments.
                              Xinhua News Agency reporter He Aiqun, Chen Jie photographed

专栏：

    西安市天祥仪器厂老工人李鸿儒连续四年保持着先进生产者的光荣称号。他从1954年来到这个厂工作以来，四年时间从未缺勤，每年的工作量都提前完成。现在他又提出，在今年的第三季度内完成全年的生产定额工作量。这是他正在安装光学仪器。
