Title: Enhance Friendship Between China and the United Arab Republic: Sino-Arab Friendship Association Established
Author:
Date: 1958-03-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: In order to develop friendly relations between China and the United Arab Republic, the China-Egypt Friendship Association and the China-Syria Friendship Association jointly held a meeting on the afternoon of the 28th and unanimously decided to merge the two associations and form the People's Republic of China - Arab United Republic Friendship Association (referred to as the Sino-Arab Friendship Association).

The meeting determined that the purpose of the Sino-Arab Friendship Association is to enhance mutual understanding between the people of the People's Republic of China and the Arab United Republic, develop the brotherly friendship between the two countries, and promote cultural exchange between the two countries. The meeting also determined that supporting the just struggle of the people of the Arab United Republic to safeguard national independence is one of the main tasks of the Sino-Arab Friendship Association.

The meeting unanimously elected Burhan as President, Da Pongsheng, Yang Jingren, Zhang Tiesheng, and Zhang Jie as Vice Presidents, and Yang Huilin and Yue Daiheng as Director-General and Deputy Director-General respectively. The meeting also passed the list of sixty members of the Council of the Sino-Arab Friendship Association:

### 增进中国和阿拉伯联合共和国的友谊  中阿友好协会组成

1958-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    增进中国和阿拉伯联合共和国的友谊
    中阿友好协会组成
    新华社28日讯　为发展中国同阿拉伯联合共和国的友好关系，中国埃及友好协会和中国叙利亚友好协会在28日下午联合举行会议，一致决定两会合并并组成中华人民共和国——阿拉伯联合共和国友好协会（简称中阿友协）。
    会议确定中阿友协的宗旨是，增进中华人民共和国和阿拉伯联合共和国人民的相互了解，发展两国兄弟般的友好关系，促进两国的文化交流。会议还确定，支持阿拉伯联合共和国人民维护民族独立的正义的斗争，是中阿友协的主要工作之一。
    会议一致通过包尔汉为会长，达浦生、杨静仁、张铁生、张杰为副会长，杨慧琳、岳岱衡分任正、副总干事。并通过丁毅民
