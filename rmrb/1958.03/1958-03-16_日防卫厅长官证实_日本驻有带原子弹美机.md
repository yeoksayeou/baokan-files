Title: Japanese Defense Agency Director Confirms: U.S. Planes Stationed in Japan Carry Atomic Bombs
Author:
Date: 1958-03-16
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, March 14th, Tokyo Dispatch: Japanese Defense Agency Director Juichi Tsushima admitted on the 13th that the United States has transported "Skyhawk" bombers capable of carrying atomic bombs into Japan.

	Juichi Tsushima made the above admission on the 13th in response to a question from Socialist Party member Yuzo Tani in a meeting of the House of Representatives Cabinet Committee, but he said, "These planes have not transported nuclear weapons into Japan."

	U.S. military officials in Manila admitted on the 12th that the U.S. "Skyhawk" bombers currently flying in the Far East belong to the 1st Air Wing of the U.S. Marine Corps stationed at the Iwakuni base in Hiroshima Prefecture, Japan.

### 日防卫厅长官证实  日本驻有带原子弹美机

1958-03-16
第3版()
专栏：

    日防卫厅长官证实
    日本驻有带原子弹美机
    新华社14日讯　东京消息：日本防卫厅长官津岛寿一在13日承认，美国已经把能够携带原子弹的“空中之鹰”式轰炸机运进日本。
    津岛寿一13日在众议院内阁委员会会议上答复社会党议员谈谷悠藏的质询时承认了上述事实，但是他说“这些飞机没有把核武器运进日本”。
    马尼拉美国军方人士在12日承认，目前在远东地区飞行的美国“空中之鹰”式轰炸机隶属于驻在日本广岛县岩国基地的美国海军陆战队的空军第一联队。
