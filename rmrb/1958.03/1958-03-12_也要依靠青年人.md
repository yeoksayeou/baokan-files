    Column: Editor's Notes

    We Must Also Rely on Young People
    In the previous period, everyone emphasized that we should mainly rely on the old workers in enterprises, and pointed out the shortcomings of some young people, requesting party organizations in enterprises to strengthen education for young people. This was originally correct. However, some people with one-sided ways of thinking have gone to the other extreme, thinking that it is completely unnecessary and that we should not rely on young people at all.
    This kind of thinking is not good. Engels said: "Our party is the party that controls the future, and the future belongs to the youth. Our party is the party of innovators, and the youth always follow the innovators most enthusiastically. Our party is the party that fights selflessly against the old and corrupt phenomena, and the youth are always the first to fight selflessly."
    Today, reading the news of Beijing's advanced young workers initiating an increase in production and thrift in an iron and steel plant, it further prompts people to remember this passage from Engels.
    Let me say one more thing: We must rely on the old workers, and we must also rely on the young people.

### 也要依靠青年人

1958-03-12
第3版()
专栏：编后随笔

    也要依靠青年人
    前一个时候，大家都强调在企业里主要要依靠老工人，并且指出一些青年人身上的毛病，要求企业的党组织加强对青年人的教育。这原本是对的。但是，有些思想方法片面的人，又偏到了一边去，以为完全不需要、也不应该依靠青年人了。
    这种想法可不好。恩格斯讲过：“我们党是掌握未来的党，而未来是属于青年的。我们党是革新者的党，而最热心地跟随着革新者走的总是青年。我们党是同陈旧腐败现象舍身奋斗的党，而首先去舍身奋斗的总是青年。”
    今天读到北京先进青年职工创议增产节约一座钢铁厂的消息，更促使人记起了恩格斯的这段话。
    再说一句：老工人我们要依靠，青年人我们也要依靠。
