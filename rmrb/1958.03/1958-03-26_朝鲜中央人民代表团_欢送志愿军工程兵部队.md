Title: Korean Central People's Delegation Bids Farewell to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Engineering Corps

Author:
Date: 1958-03-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 25th. The Korean Central People's Delegation, led by its head, Minister of Local Management Jeong Song-yeon, braved the rain today to rush to a certain location to bid farewell to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Engineering Corps, which is about to board trains to return home.

The delegation was warmly welcomed upon arriving at the headquarters of the CPVA Engineering Corps.

This afternoon, the Korean Central People's Delegation held a farewell meeting. Head of the delegation Jeong Song-yeon delivered a farewell speech, thanking the CPVA Engineering Corps for the selfless assistance they have given to the Korean people over the past few years. At the meeting, on behalf of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Supreme People's Assembly, and the Republic's government, Jeong Song-yeon presented silk banners and Sino-Korean Friendship Commemorative Medals to the Engineering Corps. Major General Choe Hong-geuk presented gifts on behalf of the People's Army Headquarters.

In his reply speech, Senior Colonel Yang Bin, head of the Engineering Corps, said that the Korean government and people had given them great help and encouragement both during the war and after the armistice. The Korean people's noble qualities of diligence and courage, and their lofty internationalist spirit, will always be a model for them to learn from. On behalf of the Engineering Corps, Senior Colonel Yang Bin presented the delegation with gifts and Peace Forever Commemorative Medals. (Attached pictures)
    When the first batch of CPVA troops passed through Pyongyang on their way home, they were warmly seen off by Korean Party and government leaders and the masses. Korean People's Army soldiers lifted up CPVA soldiers, cheering for peace and friendship.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao photo.

    At the Sinmak Station in Korea, Korean woman Li Mingzi bids farewell to Liang Shuhua, a member of the CPVA cultural troupe returning home. They hugged for a long time, reluctant to part.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao photo.

### 朝鲜中央人民代表团  欢送志愿军工程兵部队

1958-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜中央人民代表团
    欢送志愿军工程兵部队
    据新华社平壤25日电　朝鲜中央人民代表团在团长、地方管理相郑圣彦的率领下，今天冒雨赶到某地，欢送即将登车返国的志愿军工程兵部队。
    代表团到达志愿军工程兵领导机关驻地时，受到热烈欢迎。
    今天下午，朝鲜中央人民代表团举行了欢送大会。郑圣彦团长在会上致送别词，他感谢志愿军工程兵部队几年来给予朝鲜人民的无私帮助。会上，郑圣彦团长代表朝鲜劳动党、最高人民会议和共和国政府，崔弘极少将代表人民军总部，向工程兵部队赠送了锦旗和朝中友谊纪念章。
    工程兵部队首长杨滨上校在致答谢词中说，在战争时期和停战以后，朝鲜政府和人民都给了他们极大的帮助和鼓励，朝鲜人民勤劳勇敢的高贵品质和崇高的国际主义精神，永远是他们学习的榜样。杨滨上校代表工程兵部队向代表团赠送了礼品和和平万岁纪念章。（附图片）
    志愿军首批归国部队路经平壤时，受到朝鲜党、政领导人和群众热烈欢送。朝鲜人民军战士抬起志愿军战士，他们欢呼和平和友谊。
    新华社记者　孟庆彪摄在朝鲜新幕车站上，朝鲜妇女李明子送别归国的志愿军文工团员梁漱华。她们久久地拥抱着，惜别依依。
    新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
