Title: America's "Prosperity": Applications for Unemployment Benefits Increase by More Than 400,000 in One Week
Author:
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 23rd: Materials released by the U.S. Department of Labor prove that in the week of March 9th to 15th, another 410,500 people in the United States filed applications for unemployment benefits. The Labor Department also said that as of March 8th, the number of people receiving unemployment benefits remained at 3,274,000. This means that among the more than five million unemployed workers officially announced, more than two million unemployed workers still have not received benefits.

### 美国的“繁荣”  申请失业补助金人数一周间增加四十多万

1958-03-25
第5版()
专栏：

    美国的“繁荣”
    申请失业补助金人数
    一周间增加四十多万
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社纽约23日讯：美国劳工部发表的材料证明，在3月9日到15日这一周，美国又有四十一万零五百人提出要求发给失业补助金的申请。劳工部同时说，截至3月8日，领取失业补助金的人数仍旧停留在三百二十七万四千人这样一个水平上。这就是说，在官方公布的五百多万失业工人中仍有二百多万失业工人没有领到补助金。
