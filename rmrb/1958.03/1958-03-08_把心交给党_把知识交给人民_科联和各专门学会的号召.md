Title: Dedicate Your Heart to the Party, Dedicate Your Knowledge to the People: Appeal from the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies and Specialized Societies

Author:

Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 7th - Hou Debang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Specialized Societies, speaking today on behalf of the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies and Specialized Societies at the Fifth Meeting of the State Council's Scientific Planning Commission, called on all members of the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies and its member societies to strive to be both Red and expert, and to achieve the following points: (1) Support the leadership of the Communist Party, resolutely follow the path of socialism, dedicate your heart to the Party, and dedicate your knowledge to the people. (2) Work hard to study Marxism-Leninism, strengthen labor training, carry out criticism and self-criticism, accelerate self-transformation, and strive to transform into scientific workers of the working class in a short period of time. (3) Promote the spirit of collective cooperation and coordination among scientific and technological workers, oppose working alone, and oppose departmentalism. (4) Sincerely learn from the advanced science and technology of the Soviet Union, and learn from the work attitude, style, and methods of Soviet scientists; strengthen Sino-Soviet scientific and technological cooperation. (5) In all scientific and technological work, conscientiously implement the policy of combining scientific research with production practice. (6) Vigorously train scientific and technological cadres and strengthen the scientific ranks. (7) Actively implement the policy of running science diligently and thriftily, oppose waste, oppose conservatism, and ensure the completion of research tasks and production tasks in a greater, faster, better, and more economical way. Raise revolutionary vigor, work hard for ten years, and strive to realize the twelve-year national scientific and technological development long-term plan, and strive to promote a great leap forward in science and technology.
    The appeal of the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies and its member societies was first warmly responded to by the scientists attending the meeting.

### 把心交给党  把知识交给人民  科联和各专门学会的号召

1958-03-08
第7版()
专栏：

    把心交给党  把知识交给人民
    科联和各专门学会的号召
    新华社7日讯　中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会副主席侯德榜今日代表全国科联和各专门学会在国务院科学规划委员会第五次会议上的发言中，号召全国科联各学会会员努力争取又红又专，做到下列各点：（一）拥护共产党的领导，坚决走社会主义的道路，把心交给党，把知识交给人民。（二）努力学习马克思列宁主义，加强劳动锻炼，开展批评和自我批评，加速自我改造，争取在短期内改造成为工人阶级的科学工作者。（三）发扬科学技术工作者的集体合作和协调精神，反对单干，反对本位主义。（四）诚心诚意地学习苏联先进科学技术，学习苏联科学家的工作态度、作风和方法；加强中苏两国科学技术合作。（五）在各项科学技术工作中，认真贯彻科学研究与生产实践相结合的方针。（六）大力培养科学技术干部，壮大科学队伍。（七）积极执行勤俭办科学的方针，反对浪费、反对保守，保证多、快、好、省地完成研究任务和生产任务。鼓起革命干劲，苦干十年，努力实现十二年全国科学技术发展远景规划，为促进科学技术大跃进而奋斗。
    全国科联和各专门学会的号召首先得到出席这次会议的科学家的热烈响应。
