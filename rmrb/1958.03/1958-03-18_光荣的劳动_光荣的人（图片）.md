Title: Glorious Labor, Glorious People (Picture)
Author: Gao Liang
Date: 1958-03-18
Page: 3

    Glorious Labor, Glorious People
    Hou Nianxi, an electrician in the repair shop of the North China Metal Structure Factory, was originally a soldier in the Volunteer Army who earned three Third-Class Merits and one Second-Class Merit in Korea. Now he is a skilled Grade 5 worker.
    The left picture shows him teaching female apprentices who were sent to work at the grassroots level how to install electric motors.
    Our reporter Gao Liang, photographed
