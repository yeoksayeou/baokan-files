Title: Leading Organ of the First Group of Chinese People's Volunteers Withdrawing from Korea Holds Banquet Honoring Kim Il-sung and Other Korean Leaders
Author:
Date: 1958-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th -- The leading organ of the first group of Chinese People's Volunteers withdrawing from Korea held a grand banquet tonight in honor of Premier Kim Il-sung and other leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and the state.

Attending as guests were General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, General Wang Ping, Political Commissar, Lieutenant General Liang Biye, Deputy Political Commissar, and senior military officers of the first group of withdrawing troops.

Also present at the banquet was Qiao Xiaoguang, China's Ambassador to North Korea.

Lieutenant General Zhang Tianyun, head of the first group of withdrawing troops, delivered a speech at the banquet first. Premier Kim Il-sung then spoke at the banquet. Commander Yang Yong and Ambassador Qiao Xiaoguang also spoke at the banquet.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests bid farewell to each other and frequently toasted to the health of Premier Kim Il-sung and Chairman Mao Zedong, and to the friendly unity of the Chinese and Korean peoples.

### 志愿军首批撤出朝鲜部队领导机关  欢宴金日成等朝鲜领导人

1958-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军首批撤出朝鲜部队领导机关
    欢宴金日成等朝鲜领导人
    新华社平壤11日电　中国人民志愿军首批撤出朝鲜部队领导机关今晚举行盛大宴会，欢宴金日成首相等朝鲜劳动党和国家的领导人。
    出席作陪的有：志愿军司令员杨勇上将、政治委员王平上将、副政治委员梁必业中将以及志愿军首批撤出部队的高级军官等。
    我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光也出席了宴会。
    志愿军首批撤出部队首长张天云中将首先在宴会上致辞。金日成首相接着在宴会上讲了话。杨勇司令员和乔晓光大使也在宴会上讲了话。
    席间，宾主间依依话别，并且频频为金日成首相和毛泽东主席的健康，为中朝两国人民的友好团结而干杯。
