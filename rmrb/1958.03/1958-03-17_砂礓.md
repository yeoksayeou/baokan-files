    Sand Conglomerate
    Also known as sandy conglomerate soil, in some places it is called "Jiangmi Shi" (礓米石). It has the characteristics of being impermeable and as hard as stone.

专栏：名词解释

    砂礓
    又叫砂礓土，有的地方称礓米石，具有不透水、和石料一样坚硬的特性。
