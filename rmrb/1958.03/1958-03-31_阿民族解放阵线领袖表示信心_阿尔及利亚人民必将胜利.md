Title: Algerian National Liberation Front Leader Expresses Confidence: The Algerian People Will Surely Win
Author:
Date: 1958-03-31
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 30th: TASS reported from Cairo on March 30th: Abbas, one of the leaders of the Algerian National Liberation Front, made a speech on Cairo Radio on the "Day of Solidarity with Algeria." He said that holding the "Day of Solidarity with Algeria" is proof of the support of the people of all countries who have shaken off the shackles of colonialism and racial oppression for the Algerian people. Abbas emphasized, "We are no longer alone, we are no longer living in 1830 (the year France occupied Algeria)." The "Fundamental Law" imposed by France on Algeria cannot end the colonial war. "If France wants to end the war in Algeria, the only feasible way is negotiation."
    Abbas finally said, "The Algerian people are fighting for independence, and they will surely win, because they are full of confidence in their victory."

### 阿民族解放阵线领袖表示信心  阿尔及利亚人民必将胜利

1958-03-31
第5版()
专栏：

    阿民族解放阵线领袖表示信心
    阿尔及利亚人民必将胜利
    据新华社30日讯　塔斯社开罗30日讯：阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线领袖之一阿巴斯在开罗电台就“声援阿尔及利亚日”发表了谈话。他说，举行“声援阿尔及利亚日”是摆脱了殖民主义桎梏和种族压迫的各国人民支持阿尔及利亚人民的证明。阿巴斯强调说，“我们再不是孤单的了，我们已经不是生活在1830年（法国侵占阿尔及利亚的一年）了。”法国强加给阿尔及利亚的“根本法”不能结束殖民主义战争。“如果法国想结束阿尔及利亚的战争，唯一可行的道路就是谈判。”
    阿巴斯最后说，“阿尔及利亚人民是为独立而斗争，他们必将胜利。因为他们对自己的胜利充满了信心。”
