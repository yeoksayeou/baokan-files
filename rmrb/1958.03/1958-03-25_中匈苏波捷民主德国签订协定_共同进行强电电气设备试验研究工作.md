Title: China, Hungary, Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and East Germany Sign Agreement to Jointly Conduct Testing and Research on High-Voltage Electrical Equipment
Author:
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 24th. Government delegations from China, the Soviet Union, Hungary, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and the German Democratic Republic recently held a meeting in Beijing regarding testing and research on high-voltage electrical equipment for humid tropical regions. At the meeting, they signed the "Agreement between the People's Republic of China, the Hungarian People's Republic, the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, the Polish People's Republic, the Czechoslovak Republic, and the German Democratic Republic on Jointly Conducting Testing and Research on High-Voltage Electrical Equipment under Humid Tropical Climatic Conditions in the People's Republic of China," and negotiated the common conditions for implementing this agreement.

According to this agreement, the contracting countries will send experts and transport high-voltage electrical samples, as well as related chemical, petroleum, and other products to China, to jointly conduct scientific research work and sample testing. The research results will be shared by all contracting countries. The agreement also stipulates that other socialist countries that have not participated in the meeting may also join this agreement after endorsing it and obtaining the consent of the Chinese government. The signing of this agreement is another example of close cooperation in science and technology among socialist countries.

During the meeting, Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen received the representatives of various countries.

The meeting was conducted in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 中匈苏波捷民主德国签订协定  共同进行强电电气设备试验研究工作

1958-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈苏波捷民主德国签订协定
    共同进行强电电气设备试验研究工作
    新华社24日讯　中、苏、匈、波、捷和德意志民主共和国六个国家的政府代表团最近在北京举行了有关湿热带强电电气设备试验研究工作的会议，会上签订了“中华人民共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟、波兰人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克共和国和德意志民主共和国关于在中华人民共和国湿热带气候条件下共同进行强电电气设备试验研究工作的协定”，并协商了执行这一协定的共同条件。
    根据这一协定，各缔约国家将派遣专家和运送强电电工样品以及与其有关的化工、石油等产品到中国，共同进行科学研究工作和样品试验工作。研究成果归各缔约国共同享有。协定中并规定，没有参加会议的其他各社会主义国家在赞同这一协定和征得中国政府同意后，也可以参加这一协定。这一协定的签订是社会主义国家在科学技术上亲密合作的又一例证。
    会议期间，国务院副总理聂荣臻接见了各国代表。
    会议是在热诚、友好的气氛中进行的。
