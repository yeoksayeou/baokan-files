Title: Why Not Use Old Equipment?
Author: Wang Zhen
Date: 1958-03-05
Page: 3

Reader's Letter

    Why Not Use Old Equipment?
    A reconstruction project at a factory in Harbin was designed by the former Electrical Machinery Manufacturing Industry Ministry Design Institute. To save investment, the design unit planned to use seventeen old F-5 type switch cabinets in the electrical part of the design. The design unit also proposed a solution to the problem of insufficient inter-phase air gap that occurs when using this type of switch cabinet. This could save 68,000 yuan in investment. However, the Harbin Electric Power Bureau disagreed and insisted on using new high-voltage switch cabinets.
    We believe that not utilizing old equipment when it does not affect the quality of the project is a violation of the principle of building the country through diligence and frugality. We hope that the Harbin Electric Power Bureau will reconsider the design unit's opinion.
                        (Wang Zhen)

### 为何不用旧设备？
王震
1958-03-05
第3版()
专栏：读者来信

    为何不用旧设备？
    哈尔滨有一个工厂的改建工程，是由原电机制造工业部设计院设计的。为了节省投资，在电气部分设计中，设计单位准备利用十七台F—5型旧的开关柜。用这种开关柜所发生的相间空气间隙不够的问题，设计单位也提出了解决的办法。这样可以节省投资六万八千元。但是哈尔滨电业局不同意，一定要用新高压开关柜。
    我们认为在不影响工程质量的情况下，有旧设备不利用，是违反勤俭建国的原则的。希望哈尔滨电业局重新考虑设计单位意见。
                        （王震）
