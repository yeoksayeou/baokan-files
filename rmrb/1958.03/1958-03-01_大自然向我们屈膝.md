Title: Nature Bows to Us
Author: You Mo
Date: 1958-03-01
Page: 8

　　Shoulder carrying a pickaxe, bathed in moonlight, going to the construction site, a mighty throng; the first ray of dawn pouring from the horizon, quietly flowing into the earth basket on the shoulder. Song rises, shocking the valley, the pickaxe dances, turning over the sleeping frozen earth. Thunder from blasting rolls over the mountain, eclipsing the sun, nature bows to us.

### 大自然向我们屈膝
游默
1958-03-01
第8版()
专栏：

    大自然向我们屈膝
    游默肩扛镐头，身披月光，上工地去，浩浩荡荡；天边初泻的第一道霞光，悄悄地流入肩上的土筐。歌声扬起，震惊山谷，镐头飞舞，翻起沉睡的冻土。山头滚过爆破的春雷，使太阳失色，大自然向我们屈膝。
