Title: Czechoslovak Ambassador Holds Reception to Welcome Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th March. The Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušiak and his wife, held a reception in Beijing this afternoon to welcome the Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation headed by Mr. Kožol, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade. Mr. Li Zheren, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of our country, as well as commercial counselors from various embassies in China, were invited to attend the reception.

The Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation arrived in Beijing on February 27th. Mr. Li Zheren, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, received the delegation on the 28th. Currently, the negotiations between the People's Republic of China and the Czechoslovak Republic regarding trade for 1958 are proceeding in a very friendly and harmonious atmosphere.

### 捷大使举行酒会欢迎捷政府贸易代表团

1958-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    捷大使举行酒会欢迎捷政府贸易代表团
    新华社7日讯　捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克和夫人今天下午在北京举行酒会，欢迎以捷对外贸易部第一副部长科佐尔为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府贸易代表团。我国对外贸易部副部长李哲人以及各国驻华大使馆商务参赞等应邀参加了酒会。
    捷政府贸易代表团是在2月27日到达北京的。对外贸易部副部长李哲人在28日接见了代表团。现在，中华人民共和国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国之间关于1958年贸易的谈判，正在十分友好融洽的气氛中进行。
