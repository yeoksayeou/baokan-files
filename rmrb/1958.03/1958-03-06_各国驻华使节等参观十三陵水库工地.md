Title: Foreign Envoys and Others Visit the Construction Site of the Ming Tombs Reservoir
Author:
Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Foreign Envoys of various countries to China and some diplomatic officials, accompanied by Vice Foreign Ministers Qiang Wentian, Luo Guibo and others, visited the construction site of the Ming Tombs Reservoir today. Before the visit, Zhang Junshi, the Secretary of the CCP Changping District Committee in Beijing Municipality where the Ming Tombs Reservoir is located, introduced the construction situation of the reservoir to them.

### 各国驻华使节等参观十三陵水库工地

1958-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    各国驻华使节等参观十三陵水库工地
    新华社5日讯　各国驻华使节和部分外交官员，今天由外交部副部长强闻天、罗贵波等陪同，参观了十三陵水库工地。参观前，十三陵水库所在地——北京市昌平区的中共区委书记张俊士向他们介绍了水库的建设情况。
