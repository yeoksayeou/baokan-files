Title: Soviet National Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-10
Page: 7

    Soviet National Football Team Arrives in Beijing
    (Our Newspaper) The Soviet National Football Team, invited to visit our country, arrived in Beijing yesterday from Shanghai.
    The Soviet National Football Team arrived in our country on February 3rd. Before coming to Beijing, they visited Guangzhou and Shanghai, where they played eight friendly matches with our football team, achieving a record of six wins and two draws.
    This time, in Beijing, the Soviet football team will play two friendly matches with the Beijing team and the "August 1st" team on March 12th and 13th.

### 苏联国家足球队到北京

1958-03-10
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联国家足球队到北京
    本报讯　应邀来我国访问的苏联国家足球队昨日从上海到达北京。
    苏联国家足球队是2月3日到达我国的。在来北京以前，他们访问了广州和上海，在那里同我国足球队作了八场友谊比赛，取得了六胜二和的战绩。
    这次，苏联足球队在北京将在3月12日和13日同北京队和“八一”队作两场友谊比赛。
