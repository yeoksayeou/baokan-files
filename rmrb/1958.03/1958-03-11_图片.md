Title: Yuan Ruxun
Author: Yuan Ruxun
Date: 1958-03-11
Page: 1

    The newly built iron factory in Hohhot, Inner Mongolia, has completed a small blast furnace with a daily output of 6.25 tons of pig iron. The first molten iron flowed out on March 3. It is estimated that 2,000 tons of pig iron will be produced this year. This small blast furnace requires little investment and produces a lot of iron. The outer skin of the small blast furnace is made of ordinary bricks instead of steel plates, saving a lot of funds for the country. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ruxun photographed.

专栏：

    内蒙古呼和浩特市新生铁工厂建成了一座日产六点二五吨生铁的小高炉，3月3日流出了第一炉铁水。预计今年生产二千吨生铁。这座小高炉投资少，出铁多。小高炉的外皮是用普通砖代替钢板砌成的，为国家节约了很多资金。  新华社记者  袁汝逊摄
