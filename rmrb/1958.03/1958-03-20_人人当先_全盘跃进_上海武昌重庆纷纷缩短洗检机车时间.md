Title: Everyone Takes the Lead, Overall Leap Forward: Shanghai, Wuhan, and Chongqing Successively Shorten Locomotive Washing and Inspection Time

Author:

Date: 1958-03-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, March 17th: Workers at the Jiulongpo Locomotive Depot today washed and repaired a locomotive in two hours and thirty-nine minutes, breaking the latest national record of three hours and seven minutes for locomotive washing and repair downtime held by the Lanzhou and Longxi Locomotive Depots, with a quality rating of excellent.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, March 19th: The Wuhan Locomotive Depot washed and inspected a MKY model locomotive in two hours and thirty-three minutes on the 18th, breaking the national record of two hours and thirty-nine minutes set by the Chengdu Jiulongpo Locomotive Depot on the 17th. After inspection, the quality was fully qualified.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 19th: Today, workers at the Shanghai Locomotive Depot washed and inspected a locomotive in two hours and ten minutes, breaking the national record of two hours and thirty-three minutes set by the Wuhan Locomotive Depot yesterday.

### 人人当先　全盘跃进  上海武昌重庆纷纷缩短洗检机车时间

1958-03-20
第3版()
专栏：跃进榜上，今日谁家领先？

    人人当先　全盘跃进
    上海武昌重庆纷纷缩短洗检机车时间
    新华社重庆17日电　九龙坡机务段的工人，今天又以两小时三十九分钟的时间洗修好了一台机车，突破了兰州和陇西两机务段三小时零七分钟的机车洗修停时全国最新纪录，质量评定优良。
    据新华社武汉19日电　武昌机务段18日以两点三十三分的时间洗检好一台ㄇㄎ一型号机车，刷新了成都九龙坡机务段在17日创造的两小时三十九分的全国纪录。经过检验，质量完全合格。
    据新华社上海19日电　今天，上海机务段工人以两小时十分钟的时间洗检了一台机车，又刷新了昨天武昌机务段创造的两小时三十三分的全国纪录。
