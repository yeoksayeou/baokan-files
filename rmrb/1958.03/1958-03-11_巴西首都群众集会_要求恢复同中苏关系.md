Title: Mass Rally in Brazilian Capital Demands Restoration of Relations with China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1958-03-11
Page: 6

    Mass Rally in Brazilian Capital
    Demands Restoration of Relations with China and the Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: TASS, Montevideo, March 8th: According to newspaper reports, a mass rally was held in Rio de Janeiro, the capital of Brazil. Attendees demanded that the government immediately restore relations with all countries, first and foremost with the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China.

### 巴西首都群众集会  要求恢复同中苏关系

1958-03-11
第6版()
专栏：

    巴西首都群众集会
    要求恢复同中苏关系
    据新华社8日讯　塔斯社蒙得维的亚8日讯：据报纸报道，巴西首都里约热内卢举行了一个群众大会，到会者要求政府立即恢复同各国的关系，首先是同苏联和中华人民共和国的关系。
