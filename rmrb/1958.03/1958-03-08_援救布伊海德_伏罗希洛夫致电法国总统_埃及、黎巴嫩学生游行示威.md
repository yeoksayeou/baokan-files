Title: Rescue Djamila Bouhired; Voroshilov Sends Telegram to French President; Egyptian and Lebanese Students Demonstrate
Author:
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 7th, Dateline Moscow, March 6th: TASS reported that Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, sent a telegram to French President Coty on March 6th, hoping that he would take measures to save the life of Algerian young woman Djamila Bouhired.
The telegram reads: Mr. President: I, personally, and the Soviet government have received many letters from Soviet organizations, Soviet citizens, and abroad, requesting assistance in saving the life of Algerian female college student Djamila Bouhired, who has been sentenced to death. In view of this, purely out of humanitarian considerations, I would like to informally notify you of this situation and hope that you can take measures to preserve Djamila Bouhired's life.
Xinhua News Agency reported: The movement in Egypt to rescue Djamila has reached a climax. News reports indicate that the French colonial authorities are preparing to execute this Algerian patriotic young woman on March 7th.
Many Egyptian women held a demonstration in front of the United Nations Information Center in Cairo on the 4th, demanding a halt to the execution. They chanted slogans to rescue Djamila.
Arab League Secretary-General Hassouna received a reply from Hammarskjöld on the 4th. Hammarskjöld said that he had already contacted the French representative to the United Nations regarding Djamila's case.
Another report from Beirut: Five thousand students in Beirut, the capital of Lebanon, held a demonstration on the 6th, demanding the abolition of the death sentence for Djamila Bouhired. They shouted slogans condemning French colonialism.

### 援救布伊海德  伏罗希洛夫致电法国总统  埃及、黎巴嫩学生游行示威

1958-03-08
第5版()
专栏：

    援救布伊海德
    伏罗希洛夫致电法国总统
    埃及、黎巴嫩学生游行示威
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科6日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫6日致电法国总统戈蒂，希望他采取措施保全阿尔及利亚女青年布伊海德的生命。
    电文中说，总统先生：我个人和苏联政府收到苏联团体、苏联公民和国外发来的许多书信，请求协助挽救被判处死刑的阿尔及利亚女大学生布伊海德的生命。有鉴于此，我纯然出于人道考虑，想非正式地把这一情况通知您，并希望您能采取措施保全布伊海德的生命。
    据新华社讯　埃及援救加米拉的运动进入了高潮。有消息说，法国殖民当局准备在3月7日对这个阿尔及利亚爱国女青年执行死刑。
    许多埃及妇女4日在设在开罗的联合国新闻处前面举行了示威游行，要求停止执行死刑。她们高呼援救加米拉的口号。
    阿拉伯联盟秘书长哈苏纳4日接到了哈马舍尔德的回电。哈马舍尔德说，他已经就加米拉的事向法国驻联合国代表交涉。
    另据贝鲁特消息：黎巴嫩首都贝鲁特学生五千人6日举行示威游行，要求取销对布伊海德的死刑。他们高呼谴责法国殖民主义的口号。
