Title: Joyful Rain and Snowfall
Author:
Date: 1958-03-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Since 8:00 AM on the 5th, most parts of the country have joyfully welcomed rain and snow. Gansu, northern Shaanxi, central Inner Mongolia, and western Shandong have successively experienced widespread snowfall, with snowfall amounts of approximately 2 to 4 millimeters. Northern Shanxi and northern Hebei experienced snowfall amounts of 3 to 5 millimeters.

During the same period, the Huai River basin, the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River, and most parts of the provinces of Zhejiang, Guangxi, Guangdong, and Fujian also experienced rainfall successively. Southern Jiangsu, southern Anhui, northern Zhejiang, and eastern Guangxi experienced the heaviest rainfall, with 10 to 20 millimeters, while other areas generally received 5 to 10 millimeters.

The above-mentioned snowfall and rainfall have ceased successively by 2:00 PM today.

According to relevant departments, this spring snow and spring rain are of significant importance for the growth of crops such as wheat and rapeseed, the upcoming spring plowing, and alleviating drought in some provinces in the Yellow River basin.

### 喜降雨雪

1958-03-07
第2版()
专栏：

    喜降雨雪
    新华社6日讯　从5日上午八时开始，全国大部分地区喜降雨雪。甘肃、陕西北部、内蒙古中部和山东西部先后普遍下雪，降雪量大约有二到四公厘。山西、河北北部降雪量有三到五公厘。
    同一期间，淮河流域、长江中下游各省和浙江、广西、广东、福建等省大部分地区，也先后下了雨，其中以江苏南部、安徽南部、浙江北部和广西东部降雨量较大，有十到二十公厘，其他地区一般也有五到十公厘。
    上述地区的降雪、降雨情况，已在今日下午二时先后停止。
    据有关部门谈，这一场春雪和春雨对小麦、油菜等作物的生长和即将开始的春耕以及减轻黄河流域某些省的干旱，有重要的意义。
