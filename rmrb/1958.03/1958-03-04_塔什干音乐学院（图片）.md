    Tashkent Conservatory
    The Tashkent Conservatory is the highest musical institution in the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic. It has a history of more than 20 years and has trained more than 500 Uzbek national music cadres. The Conservatory also has a ten-year music school attached to it. This is the gate of the Conservatory.

### 塔什干音乐学院（图片）

1958-03-04
第6版()
专栏：

    塔什干音乐学院
    塔什干音乐学院是苏联乌兹别克共和国一所最高音乐学府，它已有二十多年的历史，已培养出五百多名乌兹别克的民族音乐干部。学院并附设有一所十年制的音乐学校。这是音乐学院的大门。
