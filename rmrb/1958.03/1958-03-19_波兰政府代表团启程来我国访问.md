Title: Polish Government Delegation Departs for Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1958-03-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 18th: The Polish government delegation, led by Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Jaroszewicz, left Warsaw by train this morning for a visit to China.

Those who saw them off at the station included ministers from various Polish government departments and Wang Bingnan, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, and others.

### 波兰政府代表团启程来我国访问

1958-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰政府代表团启程来我国访问
    新华社华沙18日电　由部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇率领的波兰政府代表团，今天上午乘火车离开华沙前往中国访问。
    到车站送行的有波兰政府各部部长和中国驻波兰大使王炳南等。
