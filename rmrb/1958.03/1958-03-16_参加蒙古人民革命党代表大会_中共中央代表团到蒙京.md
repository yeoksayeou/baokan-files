Title: Chinese Communist Party Central Committee Delegation Arrives in Mongolian Capital to Attend Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1958-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 15th. The Chinese Communist Party delegation invited to attend the 13th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, led by Ulanhu (alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee) and including Ma Mingfang (member of the Central Committee), arrived in the Mongolian capital, Ulaanbaatar, this afternoon by special train.

Damba, First Secretary of the Central Political Bureau of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and Political Bureau members Damdin, Dugersuren, Sambu, Surenjab, Tsedenbal, and Tsend, alternate members of the Political Bureau Jambaldorj and Molomjamts, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of Mongolia Ochirbat, and Mongolian Ambassador to China, Lubsan, who is currently here, welcomed the delegation at the train station.

Also present at the station to welcome the delegation were He Ying, the current Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, and embassy staff, Soviet Ambassador to Mongolia Molotov, and other foreign envoys to Mongolia.

The 13th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party is scheduled to open on March 17th. Delegations from fraternal parties of the Soviet Union, Poland, Hungary, Bulgaria, North Korea, Vietnam, Yugoslavia, Indonesia, Albania, and Czechoslovakia have already arrived here.

### 参加蒙古人民革命党代表大会  中共中央代表团到蒙京

1958-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    参加蒙古人民革命党代表大会
    中共中央代表团到蒙京
    新华社乌兰巴托15日电  应邀参加蒙古人民革命党第十三次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长乌兰夫（中央政治局候补委员）和团员马明方（中央委员），今天下午乘专车到达蒙古首都乌兰巴托。
    蒙古人民革命党中央政治局委员、第一书记达姆巴、政治局委员达姆丁、杜格苏伦、桑布、苏伦扎布、泽登巴尔、曾德，政治局候补委员占巴道尔吉、莫洛姆扎木茨，蒙古外交部代理部长奥其尔巴特，以及现在在这里的蒙古驻中国大使鲁布桑等到火车站欢迎代表团。
    现在在蒙古的代表团团员、中国驻蒙古大使何英和使馆人员、苏联驻蒙古大使莫洛托夫，以及其他国家驻蒙古使节也到车站欢迎。
    蒙古人民革命党第十三次代表大会预定在3月17日开幕。苏联、波兰、匈牙利、保加利亚、朝鲜、越南、南斯拉夫、印度尼西亚、阿尔巴尼亚和捷克斯洛伐克等兄弟党的代表团已经先后到达这里。
