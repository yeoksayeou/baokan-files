Title: Our Determination - Shen Junru's Opening Speech
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-17
Page: 4

Comrades!

The Socialist Self-Remolding Promotion Conference has opened.

Participating in today's conference are people from various democratic parties, non-party democrats, intellectuals, and industrialists and businessmen. With one heart and one mind, we, united as one, express our greatest determination to Chairman Mao, to the Communist Party, and to the people of the whole country here in front of Tiananmen Square in the capital.

We are determined to follow the Communist Party.

We are determined to take the socialist road.

We are determined to learn from the workers and peasants.

We are determined to transform ourselves—to transform our political stand and ideological consciousness.

We are determined to contribute our knowledge, exert our strength, give our hearts, and give everything we have to our beloved motherland, to the people, and to the Party!

Comrades! Under the new situation of the great leap forward in the motherland, our hearts are leaping, and our blood is boiling!

First of all, let us cheer:

Long live the Motherland!

Long live Socialism!

Long live the Communist Party!

Long live Chairman Mao!

### 我们的决心  沈钧儒的开幕词

1958-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我们的决心
    沈钧儒的开幕词
    同志们！
    社会主义自我改造促进大会开会了。
    参加今天大会的有各民主党派人士，无党派民主人士；有知识分子和工商界。我们万众一心，在首都天安门前，向毛主席、向共产党、向全国人民表示我们最大的决心。
    我们决心跟共产党走。
    我们决心走社会主义道路。
    我们决心向工农学习。
    我们决心改造自己——改造我们的政治立场和思想意识。
    我们决心把知识拿出来，把力量使出来，把心交出来，把一切都交出来，贡献给我们可爱的祖国，贡献给人民，贡献给党！
    同志们！在祖国大跃进的新形势之下，我们的心在跳跃，我们的血在沸腾！
    首先让我们欢呼：
    祖国万岁！
    社会主义万岁！
    共产党万岁！
    毛主席万岁！
