Title: Spring of World Music Arrives: Tchaikovsky International Piano and Violin Competition Opens
Author:
Date: 1958-03-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 18th: The grand Tchaikovsky International Piano and Violin Competition opened on the 18th at the Tchaikovsky Conservatory in Moscow. This competition is the first large-scale international music competition held in the Soviet Union, and a major event in international musical life, hailed as the "Spring of World Music." Eighty musicians from twenty-three countries are participating in the competition. They are all young people between the ages of eighteen and thirty. Fifty of them are preparing to participate in the piano competition, and thirty are participating in the violin competition. Chinese young pianists Gu Shengying and Liu Shikun, and violinists Yang Bingsun and Lin Kehan are also participating in the competition.

This musical event will be held from March 18 to April 14.

A large number of foreign travelers have made a special trip to attend the competition concerts. Many foreign celebrities will come as guests. The Queen of Belgium will also come as a guest at the invitation of Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

### 世界音乐之春到人间  柴可夫斯基钢琴和小提琴国际比赛开幕

1958-03-19
第6版()
专栏：

    世界音乐之春到人间
    柴可夫斯基钢琴和小提琴国际比赛开幕
    据新华社莫斯科18日电　规模盛大的柴可夫斯基钢琴和小提琴国际比赛18日在莫斯科的柴可夫斯基音乐院开幕。这次比赛是在苏联举行的第一次大规模的国际音乐比赛，是国际音乐生活中的一件大事，被誉为“世界音乐之春”。参加比赛的有二十三个国家的八十位音乐家。他们都是十八岁到三十岁的青年，准备参加钢琴比赛的有五十人，参加小提琴比赛的有三十人。中国青年钢琴家顾圣婴、刘诗昆，小提琴家杨秉荪和林克汉都参加了这次比赛。
    这次音乐盛会将从3月18日举行到4月14日。
    大批外国旅行者特地赶来参加比赛音乐会。许多外国社会名流将来作客。比利时女王也将应苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫的邀请前来作客。
