Agricultural Proverbs

　　To grow sweet potatoes well,
　　Start nurturing seedlings early.
　　The sweet potato field must be deeply ploughed,
　　So the sweet potatoes will grow big.
　　Sweet potatoes meeting wet soil,
　　Grow a layer of skin every day.
　　Winter and spring rain is as precious as oil,
　　Don't let a drop flow away.
　　Repairing ponds is like repairing granaries,
　　Storing water is like hoarding grain.

### 农谚

1958-03-14
第2版()
专栏：

    农谚
    白薯要长好，
    养苗得赶早。
    薯地要深挖，
    薯块长得大。
    白薯见湿泥，
    一天长一皮。
    冬春雨水贵如油，
    不让一滴往外流。
    修塘如修仓，
    蓄水如囤粮。
