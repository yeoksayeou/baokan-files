Title: Chang Guanqun Sets New National Weightlifting Record in Heavyweight Division
Author:
Date: 1958-03-04
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 3rd: Chang Guanqun, the national record holder in heavyweight weightlifting, set new national records in the snatch and total score at the 1958 Shanghai Weightlifting Competition on the evening of the 2nd. He improved the national snatch record of 103 kg to 105 kg and the national total score record of 377.5 kg to 380 kg.

This is the first time this year that a national weightlifter has broken a national record.

### 常冠群创重量级举重新纪录

1958-03-04
第7版()
专栏：

    常冠群创重量级举重新纪录
    新华社上海3日电　重量级举重全国纪录保持者常冠群，2日晚在1958年上海市举重比赛中创造了重量级抓举和总成绩两项新成绩，他把双手抓举一○三公斤的全国纪录提高到一○五公斤，把总成绩三七七．五公斤的全国纪录提高到了三八○公斤。
    这是今年全国举重运动员第一次打破全国纪录。
