Title: China Begins Using Atomic Energy to Treat Illnesses (Photo)
Author: Fu Jun
Date: 1958-03-19
Page: 5

    China Begins Using Atomic Energy to Treat Illnesses
    China's first specialized tumor hospital—the Tumor Hospital of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences—has been completed and began accepting patients on March 15. It will use atomic energy to treat tumor patients.
    This hospital is a hospital for comprehensive treatment of drugs, surgery and radiation. Treatment and research are equally important. In terms of treatment, in addition to radioactive cobalt, there is also radium.
    This is the staff preparing to use the radioactive cobalt-60 therapy machine presented to our country by the Soviet Union to treat patients. Xinhua News Agency reporter Fu Jun photographed.

### 我国开始用原子能治病（图片）
傅军
1958-03-19
第5版()
专栏：

    我国开始用原子能治病
    我国第一个专科性的肿瘤医院——中国医学科学院肿瘤医院已经建成，并于3月15日开始接受病人，它将用原子能来治疗肿瘤病人。
    这个医院是药物、手术和放射线综合治疗的医院。治疗和研究并重。在治疗上除了放射性钴以外，还有镭。
    这是工作人员正在准备用苏联赠送我国的放射性钴——六十治疗机给病人治疗。  新华社记者  傅军摄
