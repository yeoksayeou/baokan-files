Title: Yangzhou Stevedore Wang Zhengxi Breaks National Marathon Record
Author:
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 7

    Yangzhou Stevedore Wang Zhengxi
    Breaks National Marathon Record
      (Our Dispatch) Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing: On the afternoon of the 23rd, seven units in Jiangsu Province held a marathon road race competition on the highway from Changshu to Wuxi. Wang Zhengxi, an athlete and stevedore from Yangzhou, broke the national record of 2 hours, 52 minutes, and 34.6 seconds set by Zhang Liangyou, an athlete and miner from Anhui Province, in December last year, with a time of 2 hours, 50 minutes, and 55.8 seconds, and won the first place in the competition.
    A total of thirteen athletes from seven units, including the six cities of Xuzhou, Nanjing, Xin Hailian, Yangzhou, Wuxi, Changshu, and a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army, participated in the competition that day. The entire race was forty-two kilometers and one hundred and ninety-five meters.

### 扬州市搬运工人王正喜  破马拉松赛跑全国纪录

1958-03-25
第7版()
专栏：

    扬州市搬运工人王正喜
    破马拉松赛跑全国纪录
      本报讯　据新华社南京电：23日下午，江苏省有七个单位在从常熟到无锡的公路上举行马拉松长跑对抗赛。扬州市运动员搬运工人王正喜以二小时五十分五十五秒八的成绩打破了安徽省运动员、矿工张亮友在去年12月所创造的二小时五十二分三十四秒六的全国纪录，获得对抗赛的第一名。
    参加这天比赛的有徐州、南京、新海连、扬州、无锡、常熟六个市和解放军某部队等七个单位的运动员共十三人。比赛全程四十二公里一百九十五公尺。
