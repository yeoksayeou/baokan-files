Glorious Labor, Glorious People

    Chen Jingren, an old sand casting worker at the Harbin Farm Tool Factory, created a multi-box continuous operation method that can increase the output of casting paddy field plow baffles tenfold, with a quality reaching 100%. This is Chen Jingren (left) using the multi-box continuous operation method to turn out the model. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Hu Wei photo)
