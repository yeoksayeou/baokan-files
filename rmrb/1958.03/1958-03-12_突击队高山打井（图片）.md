Title: Commando Team Digs Wells in the High Mountains (Picture)
Author: XU Zhicheng
Date: 1958-03-12
Page: 2

    Commando Team Digs Wells in the High Mountains
    The Youth High Mountain Well-Digging Commando Team of the Lingxia Agricultural Cooperative in Baicheng County, Jilin Province, after more than two months of arduous struggle, has dug 25 wells on the hills, generally to a depth of four or five zhang. Nine of the wells have already produced clear water.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter XU Zhicheng photo

### 突击队高山打井（图片）
胥志成
1958-03-12
第2版()
专栏：

    突击队高山打井
    吉林省白城县岭下农业社的青年高山打井突击队，经过两个多月的艰苦奋斗，已在山岗上打了二十五眼井，一般都打到四、五丈深，有九眼井已经冒出了清水。
    新华社记者　胥志成摄
