Title: Chemical Plant to be Built in Zhuzhou
Author:
Date: 1958-03-03
Page: 3
Column: Flowers of Construction

    Zhuzhou to Build a Chemical Plant
    A chemical plant under construction in the suburbs of Zhuzhou City is currently undergoing active construction. It is expected that the first phase of the project can be officially put into production in the third quarter of 1959.
    After the completion of this factory, it will be able to produce more than ten kinds of products such as phosphate fertilizer, pesticides, and caustic soda.
  　(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 株洲兴建化工厂

1958-03-03
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    株洲兴建化工厂
    在株洲市郊兴建的一座化工厂，目前正在积极施工。预计第一期工程在1959年第三季度就可以正式投入生产。
    这个工厂建成以后，将能生产磷肥、农药、烧碱等十多种产品。
  　（据新华社讯）
