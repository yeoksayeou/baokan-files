New Songs from the Henan Countryside

　　Outraging the Old Dragon King
　　Building reservoirs in the valleys,
　　Building ponds on the plains,
　　With clear water in drought,
　　Outraging the Old Dragon King.

　　Water Control
　　Voices boiling, songs bright,
　　Grasping the moon as the sun,
　　Controlling water, not caring for day or night,
　　Rice seedlings planted on the mountain ridges.

　　Investment
　　Having money, not buying cloth,
　　Rather wearing tattered clothes;
　　Must invest,
　　Early happiness in life.

### 河南农村新歌

1958-03-13
第2版()
专栏：

    河南农村新歌
    气死老龙王
    山谷修水库，
    平地修水塘，
    干旱有清水，
    气死老龙王。
    治水
    人声沸腾歌声亮，
    抓住月亮当太阳，
    治水那管昼和夜，
    稻秧插在山岗上。
    投资
    有钱不买布，
    宁穿破衣服；
    一定要投资，
    生活早幸福。
