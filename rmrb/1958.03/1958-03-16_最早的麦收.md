Title: The Earliest Wheat Harvest
Author:
Date: 1958-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, March 15th: More than 3.8 million mu of barley and wheat in Fujian Province are beginning to ripen. In Jinjiang, Longxi, and other areas where planting began earlier, the early-planted barley and wheat have already started to be harvested. In counties like Fuqing, Putian, Nan'an, Jinjiang, and Tong'an, the yield of the more than 57,000 mu of barley and wheat that have already been harvested has increased by more than 10% compared to last year. The wheat in other unharvested areas is also growing well. Agricultural cooperatives are strengthening field management on the one hand, and preparing to harvest as soon as the crops are ripe on the other.

### 最早的麦收

1958-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    最早的麦收
    新华社福州15日电　福建省三百八十多万亩大麦、小麦开始黄熟。季节较早的晋江、龙溪等地区早种的大小麦已经开始收割。福清、莆田、南安、晋江、同安等县已收割的五万七千多亩大小麦，产量都比去年增产一成以上。其他未收割地区的麦子也都生长良好，农业社正一面加强田间管理，一面准备做到随熟随割。
