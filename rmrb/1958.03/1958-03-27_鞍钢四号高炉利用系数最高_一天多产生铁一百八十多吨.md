Title: Anshan Iron and Steel's No. 4 Blast Furnace Achieves Highest Utilization Rate - Producing Over 180 Tons More Iron Per Day
Author:
Date: 1958-03-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, 26th. The No. 4 blast furnace at Anshan Iron and Steel Works created a new national record for blast furnace utilization coefficient on the 24th, using only 0.583 cubic meters of effective furnace volume to produce one ton of pig iron. The coke consumption was only 637 kilograms per ton of iron produced, and the quality acceptance rate of pig iron reached 100%.

The blast furnace utilization coefficient refers to the number of cubic meters of effective furnace volume occupied to produce one ton of iron. The smaller the effective furnace volume utilization coefficient, the greater the output of the blast furnace. Due to the reduction of the utilization coefficient, the No. 4 blast furnace produced more than 180 tons of iron than the state plan on the 24th alone.

Currently, the workers of the No. 4 blast furnace are determined to exert revolutionary vigor, continue their efforts, offer suggestions and advice, and make even greater contributions.

### 鞍钢四号高炉利用系数最高  一天多产生铁一百八十多吨

1958-03-27
第3版()
专栏：

    鞍钢四号高炉利用系数最高
    一天多产生铁一百八十多吨
    新华社鞍山26日电　鞍钢炼铁厂四号高炉在24日创造了全国高炉利用系数的最新纪录，仅用零点五八三立方公尺的高炉有效容积炼出了一吨生铁；每炼一吨铁只消耗六百三十七公斤焦炭；生铁的合格率达到百分之百。
    高炉利用系数，是指每炼一吨铁占用若干立方公尺的高炉有效容积，高炉有效容积利用系数越小，高炉的产量就越大。四号高炉由于降低了利用系数，在24日一天内就比国家计划多产了一百八十多吨铁。
    目前，四号高炉的职工决心鼓足革命干劲，再接再厉，献计献策，作出更大的贡献。
