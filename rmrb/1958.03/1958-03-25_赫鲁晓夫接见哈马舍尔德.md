Title: Khrushchev Receives Hammarskjöld
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 24th—TASS, Moscow, March 24th: The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the Secretary-General of the United Nations, Hammarskjöld, today and spoke with him. Hammarskjöld arrived in Moscow on the 23rd.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见哈马舍尔德

1958-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫
    接见哈马舍尔德
    据新华社24日讯　塔斯社莫斯科24日电：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫今天接见了联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，并且同他谈了话。哈马舍尔德是在23日到达莫斯科的。
