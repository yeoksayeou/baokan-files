Title: Blood Witness
Author:
Date: 1958-03-03
Page: 5

    Blood Witness
    On February 8th, the French Air Force, using seventeen American-made aircraft, barbarically bombed and strafed the peaceful Tunisian village of Sakiet Sidi Youssef, massacring and injuring about two hundred innocent residents, mostly women and children. French government authorities are trying to evade responsibility with the absurd excuse of so-called "legitimate self-defense." But isn't this Tunisian child, with his belly blown open and bleeding profusely, a blood witness? (Attached picture)

### 血的见证

1958-03-03
第5版()
专栏：

    血的见证
    法国空军2月8日驾着十七架美制飞机对突尼斯的和平村庄萨基埃特·西迪·优素福进行了野蛮的轰炸扫射，屠杀和伤害了约二百个无辜居民，其中大多数为妇女和儿童。法国的政府当局却力图用所谓“正当的自卫行动”这样荒谬的借口来推脱罪责。但是这个被炸开了肚子而鲜血直流的突尼斯孩子，不就是血的见证吗？（附图片）
