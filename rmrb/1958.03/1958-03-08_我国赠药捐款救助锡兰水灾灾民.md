Title: China Donates Medicine and Funds to Aid Ceylonese Flood Victims
Author:
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th -- The Chinese government, in response to the request of the Ceylonese government, has agreed to donate 15 metric tons (gross weight) of medicines and medical equipment to Ceylon to aid the flood victims.

Ceylon experienced severe flooding in December of last year. Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Ceylonese Prime Minister Bandaranaike on January 1st of this year to express his condolences and, on behalf of the Chinese government, donated RMB 80,000 yuan for relief efforts.

### 我国赠药捐款救助锡兰水灾灾民

1958-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我国赠药捐款救助锡兰水灾灾民
    新华社7日讯　我国政府根据锡兰政府的请求，已同意赠予锡兰毛重十五公吨的药品和医疗卫生器材，作为对锡兰遭受水灾的灾民的援助。
    锡兰在去年12月发生严重水灾，我国周恩来总理曾于今年1月1日致电锡兰班达拉奈克总理表示慰问，并代表我国政府捐助人民币八万元作为救济。
