Title: Khrushchev Receives Manager of American Newspaper
Author:
Date: 1958-03-24
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 22nd: According to TASS, Moscow, on the 22nd, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, at the request of Eric Liddell, General Manager and Publisher of the American "Journal of Commerce," and editor Heinz Luedicke, received them on the 22nd and talked with them.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见美国一报纸经理

1958-03-24
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫
    接见美国一报纸经理
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科22日讯：苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫应美国“商务日报”总经理兼发行人埃里克·李德尔和编辑海因茨·卢迪克的请求，在22日接见了他们，并且同他们谈了话。
