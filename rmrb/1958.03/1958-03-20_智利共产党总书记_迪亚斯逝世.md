Title: General Secretary of the Communist Party of Chile, Díaz, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1958-03-20
Page: 5

    General Secretary of the Communist Party of Chile
    Díaz Passes Away
    Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: According to a report in Berlin's "Neues Deutschland," the General Secretary of the Communist Party of Chile, Galo González Díaz, passed away on March 8th at the age of sixty-four. He had persisted in underground struggle since the Communist Party of Chile was banned by the authorities ten years ago.

### 智利共产党总书记  迪亚斯逝世

1958-03-20
第5版()
专栏：

    智利共产党总书记
    迪亚斯逝世
    新华社19日讯　据柏林“新德意志报”报道：智利共产党总书记加洛·冈萨雷斯·迪亚斯在3月8日逝世，享年六十四岁。自从智利共产党十年前被当局禁止以来，他一直坚持地下斗争。
