Title: China and Yugoslavia Sign Trade Protocol for This Year in Belgrade
Author:
Date: 1958-03-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 28th—Following negotiations between representatives of the Chinese and Yugoslav governments, a trade protocol for 1958 was signed here today.

According to the protocol, the total value of trade between the two countries this year will reach approximately seven million pounds. China will export to Yugoslavia tin, asbestos, chemical industrial products, pharmaceuticals, various mineral products and metals, tires, raw silk, silk products, hides, tea, fruit juice, and other agricultural products. Yugoslavia will export to China chemical industrial products, pharmaceuticals, ferrous metal products, non-ferrous metals and semi-finished products, machinery, tobacco, and cotton cloth.

### 中南今年换货议定书  在贝尔格莱德签字

1958-03-03
第6版()
专栏：

    中南今年换货议定书
    在贝尔格莱德签字
    据新华社贝尔格莱德28日电  中国和南斯拉夫政府的代表经过谈判后，今天在这里签订了1958年换货议定书。
    根据议定书规定，两国今年的换货总值将约达七百万英镑。中国将向南斯拉夫输出锡、石棉、化学工业产品、医药品、各种矿产品和金属、轮胎、生丝、丝制品、皮张、茶、果汁和其他农产品等。南斯拉夫将向中国输出化学工业产品、医药品、黑色金属制品、有色金属及半制品、机器、烟草和棉布等。
