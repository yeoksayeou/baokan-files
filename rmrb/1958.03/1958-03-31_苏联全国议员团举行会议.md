Title: Soviet National Parliamentary Group Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1958-03-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow. TASS reports: The Soviet National Parliamentary Group held a plenary meeting in the Kremlin on the 29th. About nine hundred representatives of the Union Council and the National Council of the Fifth Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union attended the meeting. Chairman of the Parliamentary Group, Kuusinen, delivered a report on the work of the Parliamentary Group Committee for 1957. The representatives unanimously approved the activities of the committee. The meeting elected new members of the Soviet National Parliamentary Group Committee. Kuusinen was elected as Chairman, and Volkov and Kairov were elected as Vice-Chairmen. Twenty-six representatives from various Soviet republics were elected as members of the committee.

### 苏联全国议员团举行会议

1958-03-31
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联全国议员团举行会议
    据新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联全国议员团29日在克里姆林宫举行全体会议。第五届苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院的代表约有九百人出席了这次会议。议员团主席库西宁作了关于议员团委员会1957年度的工作报告。代表们一致赞成委员会的活动。会议选出了苏联全国议员团委员会的新成员。库西宁当选为主席，沃尔科夫和凯罗夫当选为副主席。苏联各共和国的二十六名代表当选为委员会委员。
