Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Telegrams to Congratulate the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 5th: Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to King Norodom Suramarit of the Kingdom of Cambodia, congratulating him on his birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom. The telegram reads as follows:
Your Majesty King Norodom Suramarit of the Kingdom of Cambodia:
    On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of your country on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Kingdom of Cambodia prosperity and its people happiness. I wish Your Majesty good health.
                                  Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong
                             March 5, 1958, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, March 5th: Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to Prince Norodom Sihanouk of the Kingdom of Cambodia, congratulating him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia. The telegram reads as follows:
Your Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk of the Kingdom of Cambodia:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Royal Highness and the Cambodian people.
    We are pleased to see that the traditional friendship between China and Cambodia has been further developed on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. I sincerely hope that the friendly relations between China and Cambodia will be increasingly consolidated and developed. I wish Your Royal Highness and the Cambodian people new achievements in the cause of building their own country.
                      Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
                March 5, 1958, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, March 5th: Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to Prince Penn Nouth, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, congratulating him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia. The telegram reads as follows:
Your Excellency Prince Penn Nouth, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Government and Your Excellency on behalf of the Chinese Government and in my own name.
    Over the past year, with the strengthening of economic and cultural ties between China and Cambodia, the friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries have developed. I sincerely hope that the traditional friendship between our two countries will be further enhanced through joint efforts.
              Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
                    March 5, 1958, in Beijing

### 毛主席和周总理电贺柬埔寨王国国庆

1958-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和周总理电贺柬埔寨王国国庆
    新华社5日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电柬埔寨王国诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特国王，祝贺国王寿辰和王国国庆。贺电如下：柬埔寨王国诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特国王陛下：陛下：
    值此陛下寿辰和柬埔寨王国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人名义向陛下和贵国人民表示热诚的祝贺。祝柬埔寨王国繁荣，人民幸福。祝陛下健康。
                                  中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                             一九五八年三月五日于北京
    新华社5日讯　周恩来总理今天致电柬埔寨王国诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王，祝贺柬埔寨王国国庆。贺电如下：柬埔寨王国诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下殿下：
    欣逢柬埔寨王国国庆，谨向殿下和柬埔寨人民表示热烈的祝贺。
    我们高兴地看到，中柬两国间的传统友谊在和平共处五项原则的基础上已经有了新的发展。我热诚地希望，中柬两国的友好关系将日益巩固和发展。祝殿下和柬埔寨人民在建设自己国家的事业中获得新的成就。
                      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                一九五八年三月五日于北京
    新华社5日讯　周恩来总理今天致电柬埔寨王国首相宾努亲王，祝贺柬埔寨王国国庆。贺电如下：柬埔寨王国首相宾努亲王阁下阁下：
    值此柬埔寨王国国庆之际，我谨代表中国政府并且以我个人名义向贵国政府和阁下致以热诚的祝贺。
    一年来，随着中柬两国在经济和文化方面联系的加强，我们两国间的友好合作关系已有发展。我衷心地希望，我们两国间的传统友谊将在共同努力下获得进一步的增长。
              中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                    一九五八年三月五日于北京
