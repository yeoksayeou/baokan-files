Title: Compete in Enthusiasm, Compete in Work: Launch a Comprehensive Inspection

Author: 
Date: 1958-03-05
Page: 2

    Compete in Enthusiasm, Compete in Work
    Launch a Comprehensive Inspection
    Guangdong
    (Xinhua News Agency dispatch) The Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has decided to immediately launch a comprehensive inspection of spring plowing preparations to further promote a production upsurge. From water conservancy, fertilizer accumulation, sowing, and afforestation to all other aspects, from the county to the township, and from the township to the commune, inspections will be conducted at each level according to plans and targets, mobilizing the masses for a comprehensive check. Through inspection, commend those who have targets, measures, great enthusiasm, and quick action, criticize the bad, and especially expose and criticize those units that only talk empty words and falsely report achievements.

### 比干劲　比工作  开展全面检查

1958-03-05
第2版()
专栏：

    比干劲　比工作
    开展全面检查
    广东
    本报讯　中共广东省委决定立即开展备耕工作大检查，进一步促进生产高潮，从水利、积肥、播种、造林等各个方面，由县到乡、乡到社，层层按计划、按指标，发动群众全面检查。通过检查，表扬那些有指标、有措施、干劲大、行动快的好人好事，批评坏的，特别要揭露批评那些只讲空话，虚报成绩的单位。　（新华社专稿）
