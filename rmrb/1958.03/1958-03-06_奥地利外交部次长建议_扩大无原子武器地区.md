Title: Austrian Undersecretary of State for Foreign Affairs Suggests Expanding Nuclear-Weapon-Free Zone
Author:
Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, March 5th: Austrian Undersecretary of State for Foreign Affairs, Bruno Kreisky, suggested on the evening of March 4th, while delivering a speech to the Socialist students of the University of Vienna, that the nuclear-weapon-free zone proposed by the Polish government for Central Europe be expanded to include not only Poland, Czechoslovakia, and East and West Germany, but also Austria, Sweden, Switzerland, and the countries of the Middle East.

He also advocated prohibiting the transportation of nuclear weapons and conventional weapons into the Middle East.

### 奥地利外交部次长建议  扩大无原子武器地区

1958-03-06
第6版()
专栏：

    奥地利外交部次长建议
    扩大无原子武器地区
    新华社5日讯　维也纳消息：奥地利外交部次长布伦诺·克赖斯基4日晚间在对维也纳大学的社会党学生发表演说时建议，扩大波兰政府建议设立的中欧无原子武器地区，使这一地区不仅包括波兰、捷克斯洛伐克和东西德，而且包括奥地利、瑞典、瑞士和中东各国。
    他还主张禁止把原子武器和常规武器运入中东地区。
