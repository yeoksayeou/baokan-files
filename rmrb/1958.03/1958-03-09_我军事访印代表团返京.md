Title: Our Military Delegation to India Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: Our military goodwill delegation to India returned to Beijing by special plane this afternoon.

    Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation today included: generals from the various headquarters, services, and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; the Indian Embassy's Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Bahadur Singh, and embassy officials. Military attaches from various countries stationed in China also went to welcome them.

### 我军事访印代表团返京

1958-03-09
第2版()
专栏：

    我军事访印代表团返京
    新华社8日讯　我国军事友好访印代表团今天下午乘专机返回北京。
    今天去机场欢迎代表团的有：中国人民解放军各总部、军种、兵种将领；印度驻华大使馆临时代办巴哈杜尔·辛格和大使馆官员。各国驻华武官也前往欢迎。
