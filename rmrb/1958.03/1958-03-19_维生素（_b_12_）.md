Column: New Products

    Vitamin (B12)
    Shanghai Third Pharmaceutical Factory has recently successfully trial-produced a valuable medicine – “Vitamin (B12)” – which can treat pernicious anemia and promote animal growth. It officially started production on the 17th.
    "Vitamin (B12)" is extracted from the waste liquid of chlortetracycline. Now, this factory is expanding the production of chlortetracycline, and the production of "Vitamin (B12)" will also increase accordingly.
              (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 维生素（B12）

1958-03-19
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    维生素（B12）
    一种能够医治恶性贫血症和促进动物生长的贵重药品——“维生素（B12）”，最近在上海第三制药厂试制成功。17日起，已经开始正式生产。
    “维生素（B12）”是从金霉素的废液中提取出来的。现在，这个厂正在扩大金霉素的产量，“维生素（B12）”的产量也将随之增长。
              （据新华社讯）
