　　Lenin was the Editor and Organizer of the Party Newspaper

　　"Lenin was the Editor and Organizer of the Party Newspaper" is a work by Lenin's wife, Krupskaya, which was translated and published by the Shanghai People's Publishing House in February. In this book, Krupskaya describes how Lenin organized and edited the Party newspaper; the struggle Lenin waged in this fighting post for twenty years; Lenin's assessment of the writing ability of workers and peasants. Finally, the author describes how we should learn. This book is valuable material for us to study Lenin's newspaper management line and Lenin's life and career. (Wu)

### 列宁是党报的编辑和组织者

1958-03-01
第7版()
专栏：出版消息

    列宁是党报的编辑和组织者
    “列宁是党报的编辑和组织者”这本书是列宁夫人克鲁普斯卡娅的著作，在2月份由上海人民出版社翻译出版。克鲁普斯卡娅在这本书内叙述了列宁是如何组织和编辑党报的；列宁在这个战斗的岗位上二十年来所进行的斗争；列宁对工农群众写稿能力的估计。最后作者叙述了我们如何进行学习的问题。这本书是我们学习列宁的办报路线和列宁生平事业的珍贵的材料。（梧）
