Title: American Intercontinental Rocket Still Can't Fly
Author:
Date: 1958-03-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 29th -- According to the U.S. Air Force authorities, the test of an "Atlas" intercontinental missile originally scheduled for launch on the 28th at Cape Canaveral, Florida, has been postponed due to technical difficulties. The Air Force authorities did not specify when the next test would be held.

### 美洲际火箭还是飞不出去

1958-03-30
第3版()
专栏：

    美洲际火箭还是飞不出去
    新华社29日讯　华盛顿消息：据美国空军当局宣布，它原订28日在佛罗里达州卡纳维拉尔角发射一枚“阿特拉斯”式洲际导弹的试验已经延期，因为在技术上发生困难。空军当局没有说明下次试验在什么时候举行。
