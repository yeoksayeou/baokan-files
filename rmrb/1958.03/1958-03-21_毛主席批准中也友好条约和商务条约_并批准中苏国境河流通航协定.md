Title: Chairman Mao Approves China-Yemen Friendship Treaty and Commerce Treaty, and Approves China-Soviet Border River Navigation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, in accordance with the decision of the 95th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on March 19, 1958, approved on March 20, 1958, the Treaty of Friendship, the Treaty of Commerce, and the Agreement on Scientific, Technical and Cultural Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Mutawakkilite Kingdom of Yemen.

Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, in accordance with the decision of the 94th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on March 7, 1958, approved on March 18, 1958, the Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics on Navigation by Merchant Ships on Border Rivers, their Tributaries and Lakes.

### 毛主席批准中也友好条约和商务条约  并批准中苏国境河流通航协定

1958-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席批准中也友好条约和商务条约
    并批准中苏国境河流通航协定
    新华社20日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会1958年3月19日第九十五次会议的决定，在1958年3月20日批准了中华人民共和国和也门穆塔瓦基利亚王国友好条约，商务条约，科学、技术和文化合作协定。
    新华社20日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会1958年3月7日第九十四次会议的决定，在1958年3月18日批准了中华人民共和国政府和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府关于国境及其相通河流和湖泊的商船通航协定。
