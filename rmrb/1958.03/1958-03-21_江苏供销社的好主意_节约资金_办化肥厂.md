Title: Jiangsu Supply and Marketing Cooperative's Good Idea: Saving Funds to Build Fertilizer Plants
Author:
Date: 1958-03-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency report: The Jiangsu Provincial Supply and Marketing Cooperative is improving its operational management and extracting 78 million yuan in funds to help counties and cities build chemical fertilizer and other factories.

The Jiangsu Provincial Supply and Marketing Cooperative system plans to reduce commodity circulation costs by 25% compared to last year. Capital turnover will accelerate from 4.19 times last year to 5.19 times. These two items alone will save 150 million yuan in funds for the entire year.

### 江苏供销社的好主意  节约资金　办化肥厂

1958-03-21
第3版()
专栏：

    江苏供销社的好主意
    节约资金　办化肥厂
    据新华社讯　江苏省供销社改善经营管理，抽出资金七千八百万元，帮助县、市兴建化学肥料等工厂。
    江苏省供销社系统今年计划把商品流转费用在去年的基础上降低25％；资金周转由去年的四点一九次加快到五点一九次，只此两项，全年可节省资金一亿五千万元。
