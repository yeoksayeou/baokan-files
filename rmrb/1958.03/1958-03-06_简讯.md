Brief News
    Ulbricht Receives British Parliamentarians
    According to a report by the German News Agency: Walter Ulbricht, First Deputy Premier of the German Democratic Republic, received British Members of Parliament Davies (Liberal), Drayson (Conservative), Lewis (Labour), Montgomery-Hyde (Conservative), and Orbach (Labour), as well as some British businessmen participating in the Leipzig Fair, in Leipzig on the 2nd, and held a two-hour conversation with them.
    Czechoslovak Parliamentary Delegation Visits Poland
    According to a report by the Polish News Agency: The Czechoslovak National Assembly delegation, headed by National Assembly Chairman Fierlinger, arrived in Warsaw by train on the 4th at the invitation of the Polish Parliament. Polish Parliament Speaker Wicek and others were at the station to welcome them.

### 简讯

1958-03-06
第6版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    乌布利希接见英国议员
    据德意志通讯社报道：德意志民主共和国第一副总理乌布利希2日在来比锡接见了英国下院议员戴维斯（自由党）、德雷逊（保守党）、刘易斯（工党）蒙戈麦里—海德（保守党）、奥巴赫（工党）和一些参加来比锡博览会的英国商人，并且同他们进行了两个小时的交谈。
    捷议会代表团到波兰访问
    据波兰通讯社报道：以国民议会主席费林格为首的捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团应波兰议会的邀请，在4日乘火车抵达华沙。前往车站欢迎的有波兰议会议长维策赫等人。
