Title: French Colonialists Are Bloodthirsty and Kill a Group of Algerian Patriots
Author:
Date: 1958-03-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th, dispatch from TASS, Paris, March 7th: French authorities continue to kill Algerian patriots participating in the national liberation movement in Algeria.

    According to a report in the Paris newspaper "L'Humanité," the French military tribunal in Philippeville sentenced fifteen Algerians to death on March 6th; sentenced seven people to life imprisonment with hard labor, sentenced two people to twenty years of hard labor, and another nineteen people were sentenced to imprisonment, and more than twenty people were sentenced to death *in absentia*.

    The newspaper also reported that five Algerian patriots were sentenced to death in Constantine.

### 法国殖民者嗜杀成性  残害一批阿尔及利亚爱国者

1958-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

    法国殖民者嗜杀成性
    残害一批阿尔及利亚爱国者
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社巴黎7日讯：法国当局继续在阿尔及利亚杀害参加民族解放运动的阿尔及利亚爱国者。
    据巴黎“人道报”报道，菲利普维尔市法国军事法庭3月6日判处十五名阿尔及利亚人死刑；判处七人终生苦役，判处两人二十年苦役，另外还有十九人被判处徒刑，二十多人被缺席判处死刑。
    报纸还报道，在君士坦丁有五名阿尔及利亚爱国者被判处死刑。
