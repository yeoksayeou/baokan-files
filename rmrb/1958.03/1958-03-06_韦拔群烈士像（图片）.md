Title: Statue of Martyr Wei Baqun (Picture)
Author: Huang Jian
Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 4

    Statue of Martyr Wei Baqun (Sculpture) by Huang Jian
    Martyr Wei Baqun was a loyal son of the Zhuang people and an excellent member of the Chinese Communist Party. He was born in Donglan, Guangxi in 1900, and participated in revolutionary activities in 1921. He was a student of Chairman Mao's Peasant Movement Training Institute in Guangzhou. In 1925, he led the Zhuang and Han farmers of Guangxi in a vigorous uprising. He joined the Chinese Communist Party in 1929 and served as the commander of the 21st Division of the Seventh Army of the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army and a member of the Youjiang Soviet Government. In 1930, the Seventh Red Army was ordered to move north, and the Party left Comrade Wei Baqun to continue the revolutionary struggle in Youjiang. He died gloriously in October 1932.

### 韦拔群烈士像（图片）
黄俭
1958-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    韦拔群烈士像（雕塑）　黄俭作
    韦拔群烈士是僮族人民的忠诚子弟、中国共产党的优秀党员。他于1900年生于广西东兰，1921年参加革命活动，曾是毛主席在广州举办的农民运动讲习所的学生。1925年领导广西僮、汉各族农民举行轰轰烈烈的暴动。1929年参加中国共产党，曾任中国工农红军第七军第二十一师师长和右江苏维埃政府委员。1930年红七军奉令北调，党留下韦拔群同志继续坚持右江的革命斗争，1932年10月光荣牺牲。
