Title: Vice Premier He Long Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th: He Long, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 10th after representing the CPC Central Committee, the State Council, and Chairman Mao in Guangxi to congratulate the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region.

    Xirao Gyatso, Deputy Governor of Qinghai Province, arrived in Beijing on the same plane.

    Greeting them at the airport were Qi Yanming, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, and Yang Jingren, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Tian Fuda and other members of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress.

### 贺龙副总理返京

1958-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理返京
    新华社10日讯　中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理贺龙代表中共中央、国务院和毛主席到广西祝贺广西僮族自治区的成立后，已在10日下午乘飞机回到北京。
    同机到京的有青海省副省长喜饶嘉错。
    到机场迎接的，有国务院副秘书长齐燕铭，民族事务委员会副主任刘春、萨空了、杨静仁，全国人民代表大会民族委员会委员田富达等人。
