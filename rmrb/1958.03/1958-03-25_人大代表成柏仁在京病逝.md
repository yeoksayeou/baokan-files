Title: NPC Delegate Cheng Boren Passes Away in Beijing Due to Illness
Author:
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 24th: Cheng Boren, delegate to the National People's Congress, Vice Governor of Shaanxi Province, member of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, and Deputy Chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the China Democratic League, passed away in Beijing at 12:38 noon on March 24, 1958, due to leukemia and complications from pneumonia, despite medical treatment. He was 70 years old. A public memorial service will be held at Jiaxing Temple at 10:00 am on the 27th. After the public memorial service, the remains will be moved to the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery for burial.

The funeral committee for Mr. Cheng Boren was established on March 24th, with the following members:

沈钧儒 (Shen Junru), 习仲勋 (Xi Zhongxun), 邓初民 (Deng Chumin), 孙蔚如 (Sun Weiru), 汪锋 (Wang Feng), 辛志超 (Xin Zhichao), 屈武 (Qu Wu), 胡愈之 (Hu Yuzhi), 赵寿山 (Zhao Shoushan), 赵伯平 (Zhao Boping), 马明方 (Ma Mingfang), 马文瑞 (Ma Wenrui), 高崇民 (Gao Chongmin), 高登榜 (Gao Dengbang), 张治中 (Zhang Zhizhong), 张德生 (Zhang Desheng), 张奚若 (Zhang Xiruo), 张苏 (Zhang Su), 张凤翽 (Zhang Fenghui), 张邦英 (Zhang Bangying), 郭则沉 (Guo Zechen), 杨明轩 (Yang Mingxuan), 杨子廉 (Yang Zilian), 杨玉亭 (Yang Yuting), 贾拓夫 (Jia Tuofu), 韩望尘 (Han Wangchen).

### 人大代表成柏仁在京病逝

1958-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    人大代表成柏仁在京病逝
    新华社24日讯　全国人民代表大会代表、陕西省副省长、中国民主同盟中央委员、中国民主同盟陕西省委员会副主任委员成柏仁，因患白血症和并发肺炎，医治无效，于1958年3月24日中午十二时三十八分在北京逝世，享年七十岁。定于27日上午十时在嘉兴寺举行公祭，公祭毕移灵八宝山革命公墓安葬。
