Title: Motherland North and South (Picture)
Author: Xu Zhicheng
Date: 1958-03-04
Page: 2

    Motherland North and South
    Now is the season of spring returning to the earth in the South of the motherland. Farmers on Hainan Island are already engaged in spring plowing production in the sunny and warm fields (bottom picture). However, the lumberjacks in Changbai Mountain are still working in the snowy forest area (left picture).
                         Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Zhicheng photo

### 祖国南北（图片）
胥志成
1958-03-04
第2版()
专栏：

    祖国南北
    现在祖国的南方已是大地回春的季节，海南岛的农民已在阳光和煦的田间进行春耕生产（下图）。而长白山的伐木工人郤还在白雪皑皑的林区工作（左图）。
                         新华社记者　胥志成摄
