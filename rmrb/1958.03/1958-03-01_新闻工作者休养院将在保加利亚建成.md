Title: Sanatorium for Journalists to be Built in Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1958-03-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: A sanatorium for journalists from various countries is about to appear in Golden Sands, a resort on the Bulgarian Black Sea coast. It is located in a beautiful beach area surrounded by peach orchards, vineyards, and shady beech and oak forests. This sanatorium will begin receiving its first guests next year.

Currently, the engineering design for this international sanatorium for journalists has been completed. The architecture of the sanatorium will be completely modern, but will also have the traditional ethnic style of Bulgarian Black Sea coastal cities. The sanatorium can accommodate 200 people. If necessary, it can accommodate dozens more with minor arrangements.

The construction of this sanatorium has received the active support of journalists' associations from many countries, including capitalist countries.

### 新闻工作者休养院将在保加利亚建成

1958-03-01
第5版()
专栏：

    新闻工作者休养院将在保加利亚建成
    据新华社讯　在保加利亚黑海之滨的疗养胜地金沙镇上，在一个前面是一片优美的海滨浴场、周围有许多桃园、葡萄园、绿叶成荫的山毛榉和橡树林环绕的地方，即将出现一座可供各国新闻工作者休养的休养院。这个休养院明年就将开始接纳第一批前来休养的客人。
    现在，这个新闻工作者国际休养院的工程设计已经完成。休养院的建筑将是完全现代化的，但也带有保加利亚黑海海滨城市建筑所具有的传统民族风格。休养院可容纳二百人。必要时稍经布置还可多容纳数十人。
    这个休养院的建造得到了许多国家包括资本主义国家的新闻工作者协会的积极支持。
