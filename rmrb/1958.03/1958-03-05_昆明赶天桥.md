Title: Kunming Catches Up with Tianqiao

Author:

Date: 1958-03-05
Page: 3

Kunming, March 4th. More than 5,000 employees in Kunming's financial and trade system, through the anti-waste and anti-conservatism movement, have launched a high tide of "learning from Tianqiao, catching up with Tianqiao, surpassing Tianqiao, and striving for the upstream" in commercial work. Recently, they also passed a proposal for "Five Nos" (no errors, no accidents, no losses or waste, no embezzlement or theft, no mildew or spoilage) and "Four Goods" (good service, good study, good cleanliness and hygiene, and good completion of tasks).

### 昆明赶天桥

1958-03-05
第3版()
专栏：

    昆明赶天桥
    本报昆明4日电　昆明市财贸系统的五千多名职工，通过反浪费反保守运动，掀起了“学天桥、赶天桥、过天桥、争上游”的商业工作大跃进高潮。最近，并通过了“五无”（无差错、无事故、无损失浪费，无贪污盗窃、无霉坏变质）“四好”（服务好、学习好、清洁卫生好、完成任务好）的倡议。
