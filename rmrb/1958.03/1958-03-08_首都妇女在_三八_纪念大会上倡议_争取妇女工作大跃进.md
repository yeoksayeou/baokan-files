Title: Capital Women Advocate for a Great Leap Forward in Women's Work at "March 8th" Commemoration Meeting
Author:
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 7th. A gathering of 1,500 women from all walks of life in the capital took place this afternoon at the Tianqiao Theater to solemnly commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day. At the meeting, the Beijing Women's Federation proposed a friendly competition to the women's federations of seven major cities, including Shanghai.

Zhang Xiaomei, Chairman of the Beijing Women's Federation, delivered a speech first. She cited many examples to illustrate that women in all production and work positions in Beijing are advancing shoulder to shoulder with men, and have achieved outstanding results in the upsurge of socialist construction, having become an indispensable force in the construction upsurge.

Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, spoke at the meeting. She pointed out that the current task of women across the country is to summon greater revolutionary vigor and strive for a thorough victory in the 1958 mass rectification movement and the realization of a comprehensive Great Leap Forward in socialist construction. Cai Chang said that in order to realize the above tasks, women across the country must strive to raise their socialist consciousness, establish socialist labor views and collectivist ideology; go all out in actively participating in the increase production and practice economy movement and the socialist emulation drive, and strive to learn technology; women cadres must go to the masses and into practice; women's federations at all levels must change their conservative and procrastinating work style to ensure a Great Leap Forward in women's work as well.

At today's meeting, women representatives from various production and work positions in the capital expressed their determination to throw themselves into the tide of the socialist construction Great Leap Forward with practical actions. This was a feature of today's meeting. After Wang Chunping, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Women's Federation, read out the Beijing Women's Federation's proposal to launch a friendly competition with the women's federations of seven major cities including Shanghai, Tianjin, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Shenyang, Chongqing and Xi'an, Zhang Xingdi, a female worker from the No. 2 State Cotton Factory, Zhao Weixin, a female sales clerk from the Tianqiao Department Store, Xu Shuqin, a female member of the Beijing Pick-up and Repair Cooperative, and Sun Ruizhi, a representative of the family members of workers from the Agricultural Machinery General Factory and the Beifengwo Railway Bureau, also spoke at the meeting one after another, representing the women of their respective units in challenging the textile workers, female sales clerks, female handicraft workers and family members of workers in the seven major cities and Shantou and Qingdao. They loudly proposed: to launch a great competition that compares who has more vigor, who has higher ideological awareness, who has more advanced production, who has a better work attitude, who is more diligent and thrifty in managing the household, and who is more hardworking and simple. Through the competition, let production make a Great Leap Forward and ideology achieve a great harvest. The entire venue welcomed and supported their proposals with warm applause.

The wives of foreign envoys to China attending the meeting presented flower baskets to the meeting.

One hundred and fifty strong and lively young pioneers, both male and female, entered the venue in two teams, playing drums and blowing bugles, to present congratulatory messages and flower baskets to the meeting. The women representatives present responded to the children's affectionate greetings with the warmest applause.

Also attending today's meeting were Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of Beijing, Liu Qingyang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and heads of various people's organizations in Beijing. Some foreign female experts and wives of experts in Beijing were also invited to attend the meeting.

### 首都妇女在“三八”纪念大会上倡议  争取妇女工作大跃进

1958-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    首都妇女在“三八”纪念大会上倡议
    争取妇女工作大跃进
    新华社7日讯　首都各界妇女一千五百人今天下午在天桥剧场集会，隆重纪念“三八”国际妇女节。北京市妇联在会上向上海等七大城市的妇联倡议开展友谊竞赛。
    北京市妇联主席张晓梅首先致词。她列举许多事例，说明北京市各个生产和工作岗位上的妇女同男子肩并肩前进，在社会主义建设高潮中取得了出色的成绩，已经成为建设高潮中一支不可缺少的生力军。
    全国妇联主席蔡畅在会上讲了话。她指出，当前全国妇女的任务是，鼓起更大的革命干劲，为争取1958年全民整风运动的彻底胜利和实现社会主义建设事业的全面大跃进而努力奋斗。蔡畅说，全国妇女要实现上述任务，就必须努力提高社会主义觉悟，树立社会主义的劳动观点和集体主义思想；在积极参加增产节约运动和社会主义竞赛运动中鼓足干劲，并努力学习技术；妇女干部要到群众中去，到实际中去；各级妇联组织必须改变保守和拖拉的工作作风，保证妇女工作也来个大跃进。
    在今天的大会上，来自首都各个生产和工作岗位上的妇女代表纷纷表示决心，要以实际行动投入社会主义建设大跃进的浪潮中去。这是今天大会的特点。在北京市妇联副主席王春平宣读了北京市妇联向上海、天津、武汉、广州、沈阳、重庆和西安等七大城市妇联开展友谊竞赛的倡议书后，国棉二厂女工章杏娣、天桥百货商店女售货员赵维新、北京市挑补联社女社员徐淑琴、农业机器总厂和铁路局北蜂窝职工家属代表孙瑞芝也先后在会上讲话，代表本单位妇女分别向七大城市和汕头、青岛的纺织女工、女售货员、女手工业者和职工家属提出挑战，她们响亮地提出：要展开一个比干劲足，比思想觉悟高，比生产先进，比劳动态度好，比勤俭持家，比艰苦朴素的大竞赛。通过竞赛让生产大跃进，思想大丰收。全场以热烈的掌声，欢迎和支持了她们的倡议。
    参加大会的各国驻华使节夫人，向大会赠送了花篮。
    一百五十名健壮活泼的男女少年先锋队员今天打着小鼓，吹着号角，分两队进入会场，向大会献词和献花篮。到会的妇女代表对孩子们亲热的祝词，报以最热烈的掌声。
    参加今天大会的还有北京市副市长张友渔、全国妇联副主席刘清扬以及北京市各人民团体的负责人。在京的一些各国女专家和专家夫人也应邀出席大会。
