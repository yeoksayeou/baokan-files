Title: Peking University Faculty and Students Welcome Algerian National Liberation Front Representatives, Brahim Ghafa, and Others Who Spoke at the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1958-03-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, March 29th. More than 1,300 faculty and students of Peking University today shouted slogans and raised their arms together at the welcome meeting for Brahim Ghafa, representative of the Algerian National Liberation Front, and Adef Danil, representative of the Arab People's Congress: "We support Algeria!"

Brahim Ghafa and Adef Danil were invited to give speeches to the faculty and students of Peking University this afternoon. In the center of Peking University Auditorium, which served as the venue for the meeting, a banner written in Chinese and Arabic was hung high, reading: "Salute to the heroic Algerian people!"

At 2:00 p.m., when the two distinguished guests, accompanied by Ma Yinchu, President of Peking University, entered the venue, warm applause broke out in the hall, and the university students repeatedly shouted "We support Algeria" in Arabic.

Ma Yinchu first delivered a welcome speech on behalf of all faculty, students, and staff of Peking University. He said that the faculty and students of Peking University, like all the Chinese people, are concerned about and sympathize with the just struggle of the Algerian people for national independence, and they regard the struggle and victory of the Algerian people as their own struggle and victory.

Subsequently, Brahim Ghafa and Adef Danil delivered speeches one after another. In his speech, Brahim Ghafa conveyed the wishes and gratitude of the Algerian people to the Chinese people. He denounced the various barbaric crimes committed by French imperialism in Algeria with the support of American and British imperialism. He said that the Algerian people are waging a heroic struggle, and the Algerian people will never yield until the final victory. In his speech, Adef Danil pointed out that the people of Arab countries and the Algerian people are united, and the people of the world sympathize with and support the struggle of the Algerian people. He believes that the imperialist aggressor forces will be completely crushed in the face of this force. In his speech, he also praised the consistent friendship of the Chinese people towards the Arab people. Their speeches constantly won warm applause from the audience.

Lu Zhangyi, a student of Peking University, spoke on behalf of more than 8,000 students, resolutely supporting the Algerian people's struggle for national independence.

Ya Xin, a student from the United Arab Republic studying at Peking University, also spoke, believing that the heroic struggle of the Algerian people will surely achieve final victory.

After the speech meeting, student representatives also handed over more than 70 letters written by students expressing their condolences to Algerian youth and students to Brahim Ghafa.

### 北大师生欢迎阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表  卜拉欣·加法等在会上演讲

1958-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

    北大师生欢迎阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表
    卜拉欣·加法等在会上演讲
    新华社29日讯　北京大学一千三百多名师生今天下午在欢迎阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表卜拉欣·加法和阿拉伯人民大会的代表阿德夫·达尼尔的时候，一齐振臂高呼：“我们支持阿尔及利亚！”
    卜拉欣·加法和阿德夫·达尼尔今天下午应邀向北京大学的师生作了演讲。在作为会场的北京大学礼堂的正中，高悬着用中国、阿拉伯文写的标语：“向英勇的阿尔及利亚人民致敬！”
    下午二时，两位贵宾由北京大学校长马寅初陪同进入会场时，会场上响起了热烈的掌声，大学生们用阿拉伯语连续高呼“我们支持阿尔及利亚”。
    马寅初首先代表北京大学全体师生员工致欢迎词。他说，北京大学的师生同全中国人民一样，都在关心和同情阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立的正义斗争，他们把阿尔及利亚人民的斗争和胜利都看做是自己的斗争和胜利。
    接着，卜拉欣·加法和阿德夫·达尼尔先后发表了演讲。卜拉欣·加法在演讲中转达了阿尔及利亚人民对中国人民的祝愿和谢意。他控诉了法国帝国主义在美、英帝国主义支持下在阿尔及利亚干下的种种野蛮罪行。他说，阿尔及利亚人民正在进行着英勇的斗争，阿尔及利亚人民决不屈服，一直到最后胜利。阿德夫·达尼尔在演讲中指出，阿拉伯各国人民和阿尔及利亚人民是团结一致的，全世界人民都同情和支持阿尔及利亚人民的斗争。他相信帝国主义侵略势力在这种力量面前一定会被彻底粉碎。他在演讲中还赞扬了中国人民对阿拉伯人民始终一贯的友谊。他们的演讲不断博得全场热烈的掌声。
    北京大学学生卢章谊代表八千多名学生讲话，坚决支持阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立的斗争。
    在北京大学学习的阿拉伯联合共和国学生雅兴也讲了话，他相信阿尔及利亚人民的英勇斗争一定能够取得最后胜利。
    演讲会结束后，学生代表还把学生们写的慰问阿尔及利亚青年和学生的七十多封信，交给了卜拉欣·加法。
