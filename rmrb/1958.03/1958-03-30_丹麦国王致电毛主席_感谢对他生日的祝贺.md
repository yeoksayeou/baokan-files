Title: Danish King Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao Thanking Him for Birthday Greetings
Author:
Date: 1958-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 29th: Danish King Frederik sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong on the 28th, thanking him for his birthday greetings. King Frederik's telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

Please accept my sincere thanks for your telegram of congratulations on my birthday. I share your good wishes for friendly relations between Denmark and China, and extend my best regards to yourself and the Chinese people.

King Frederik of Denmark
Copenhagen, March 28, 1958

### 丹麦国王致电毛主席  感谢对他生日的祝贺

1958-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    丹麦国王致电毛主席
    感谢对他生日的祝贺
    新华社29日讯　丹麦国王斐德烈28日致电毛泽东主席，感谢毛主席对他生日的祝贺。斐德烈国王的来电如下：北京中华人民共和国主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    在我生日的时候，承蒙阁下来电祝贺，请接受我的诚挚的谢意。我和你同样对丹麦和中国之间的友好关系表示良好的愿望，并向你本人和中国人民致最良好的问候。
                                丹麦国王　斐德烈
                                  1958年3月28日　哥本哈根
