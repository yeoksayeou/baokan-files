Title: International Weightlifting Competition in Preparation
Author:
Date: 1958-03-05
Page: 6

    International Weightlifting Competition in Preparation
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The 1958 Moscow Prize International Individual Weightlifting Competition is being actively prepared.
    The Chinese weightlifting team's athletes, including world record holder Chen Jingkai, have already arrived in Moscow.

### 国际举重比赛在准备中

1958-03-05
第6版()
专栏：

    国际举重比赛在准备中
    据新华社讯　1958年莫斯科奖国际个人举重比赛正在积极准备中。
    中国举重队的选手包括世界纪录保持者陈镜开，已经到达莫斯科。
