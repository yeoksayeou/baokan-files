Title: Chairman Mao Sends Telegram to King of Denmark Congratulating Him on His Birthday and Danish National Day
Author:
Date: 1958-03-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 10th: Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to King Frederik IX of the Kingdom of Denmark, congratulating the King on his birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Denmark. The telegram reads as follows:
His Majesty King Frederik IX of the Kingdom of Denmark:

    On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Denmark, I, on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, extend my sincere congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of your country. I wish the friendly relations between China and Denmark will be further developed and consolidated. I wish prosperity to the Kingdom of Denmark, happiness to the Danish people, and good health to Your Majesty.

                                 Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                 Beijing, March 10, 1958

### 毛主席致电丹麦国王  祝贺他的寿辰和丹麦国庆

1958-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席致电丹麦国王
    祝贺他的寿辰和丹麦国庆
    新华社10日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电丹麦王国国王斐德烈九世，祝贺国王寿辰和丹麦王国国庆。电文如下：丹麦王国国王斐德烈九世陛下：
    值此陛下寿辰和丹麦王国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人名义，向陛下和贵国人民表示热诚的祝贺。祝中丹两国间的友好关系日益发展和巩固。祝丹麦王国繁荣，丹麦人民幸福和陛下健康。
                                 中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                 一九五八年三月十日于北京
