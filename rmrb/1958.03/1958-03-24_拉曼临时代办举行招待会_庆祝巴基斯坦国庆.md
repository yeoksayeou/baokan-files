Title: Rahman, Chargé d'Affaires, Holds Reception Celebrating Pakistan's National Day
Author:
Date: 1958-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd. Rahman, Chargé d'Affaires of the Pakistan Embassy in China, held a reception this evening to celebrate the National Day of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan.

Vice Premier Chen Yi raised his glass at the reception to wish His Excellency Iskander Mirza, President of Pakistan, good health, and to wish the Islamic Republic of Pakistan prosperity and strength. Chen Yi also proposed a toast to the friendship between the Chinese and Pakistani peoples and to the unity and friendship of the peoples of Asia and the world.

Chargé d'Affaires Rahman delivered a speech at the beginning of the reception. He asked his guests—people from all walks of life in China and envoys from various countries to China—to toast to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and the happiness of the Chinese people.

This evening, Vice Premier He Long, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen and Cheng Qian, and Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu attended the reception.

At the reception, the hosts and guests also enjoyed musical and dance performances from both Pakistan and China performed by the Chinese art troupe that had visited Pakistan.

### 拉曼临时代办举行招待会  庆祝巴基斯坦国庆

1958-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    拉曼临时代办举行招待会
    庆祝巴基斯坦国庆
    新华社23日讯　巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆临时代办拉曼今晚举行招待会，庆祝巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国国庆日。
    陈毅副总理在招待会上举杯，祝巴基斯坦总统伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎阁下的身体健康，祝巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国的繁荣富强。陈毅还提议为中巴两国人民间的友谊、为亚洲和世界各国人民的团结友好干杯。
    拉曼临时代办在招待会开始的时候致了词。他请他的客人——中国各方面的人士和各国驻中国的使节，为毛泽东主席的健康、为中国人民的幸福干杯。
    今晚，贺龙副总理、李济深、程潜副委员长和章汉夫副部长都出席了招待会。
    招待会上，宾主还一起欣赏了曾经访问过巴基斯坦的中国艺术团表演的巴中两国的音乐歌舞节目。
