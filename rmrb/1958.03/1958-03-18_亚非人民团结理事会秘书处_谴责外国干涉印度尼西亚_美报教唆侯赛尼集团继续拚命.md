Title: Secretariat of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council Condemns Foreign Interference in Indonesia; American Newspaper Instigates the Hussein Group to Continue Desperate Struggle
Author:
Date: 1958-03-18
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 16th -- The Permanent Secretariat of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council sent a telegram to the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization on the 16th, condemning foreign interference in the internal affairs of Indonesia and other Afro-Asian countries.
    Xinhua News Agency, 17th -- New York News: Some American newspapers are blatantly supporting various Indonesian rebel groups.
    The "New York Times" published a lengthy editorial on the 16th, actually describing the rebellion of the Hussein group in Central Sumatra as an action with "justification". The editorial openly instigated the rebel group to wage a "protracted and arduous guerrilla war" in the face of the government forces' successful operations, and "never tolerate the manipulation of Java." The newspaper stated that the rebels "must fight on, and we dare say they will do so."
    The "Daily News" published an editorial on the 17th, demanding that the United States recognize the so-called "Republic of South Maluku" of another rebel group, the "Islamic Army". The editorial also launched a personal attack on President Sukarno, falsely accusing him of being a "communist agent".

### 亚非人民团结理事会秘书处  谴责外国干涉印度尼西亚  美报教唆侯赛尼集团继续拚命

1958-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非人民团结理事会秘书处
    谴责外国干涉印度尼西亚
    美报教唆侯赛尼集团继续拚命
    新华社开罗16日电　亚非人民团结理事会常设秘书处16日打电报给东南亚条约组织，谴责外国干涉印度尼西亚和亚非国家的内政。
    新华社17日讯　纽约消息：一些美国报纸露骨地支持形形色色的印度尼西亚叛乱集团。
    “纽约时报”16日发表长篇社论，竟把中苏门答腊侯赛尼集团的叛乱活动说成是“师出有名”的行动。这篇社论公开教唆叛乱集团在政府军顺利行动的情况下，进行一场“持久和艰苦的游击战争”，而“绝不能容忍爪哇的摆布”。报纸说，叛乱分子“必须战斗下去，我们敢说，他们会这样做”。
    “每日新闻”在17日发表的社论里要求美国承认另一个叛乱集团“伊斯兰教军”的所谓“南马鲁古共和国”。这篇社论还对苏加诺总统进行人身攻击，诬蔑他是“共产党代理人”。
