Title: Chinese Communist Party Delegation Meets Mongolian Workers, Encourages Chinese Employees to Modestly Learn from Mongolian Comrades
Author:
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, March 24th. The Chinese Communist Party delegation participating in the 13th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party met today with Mongolian construction workers and Chinese construction workers assisting in construction in Mongolia at the Ulaanbaatar Trade Union Building.

　　The Party Secretary of the Mongolian State Construction Affairs Committee, Pulebuzhabu, spoke on behalf of the construction workers at the meeting, warmly welcoming the Chinese Communist Party delegation.

　　Ulanhu conveyed greetings from the Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese working class to the Mongolian workers. He congratulated the Mongolian workers for their selfless labor under the leadership of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the completion of the basic construction tasks of the Second Five-Year Plan. He said that the Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese people highly value the great achievements made by the fraternal Mongolian people and regard them as their own achievements.

　　In his speech, Ulanhu encouraged the Chinese employees who are assisting in construction in Mongolian construction enterprises to modestly learn from their Mongolian comrades in their work, abide by Mongolian laws and social customs, promote labor enthusiasm, and successfully complete the production tasks of the enterprises.

　　Subsequently, Maidar, Deputy Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers and Chairman of the State Construction Affairs Committee, presented the Chinese Communist Party delegation with commemorative badges and Mongolian chess as gifts on behalf of the Construction Affairs Committee.

　　At the meeting, construction workers also performed songs and dances to welcome the Chinese guests.

### 中共代表团会见蒙古工人  勉励中国员工虚心向蒙古同志学习

1958-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

    中共代表团会见蒙古工人
    勉励中国员工虚心向蒙古同志学习
    新华社乌兰巴托24日电
    参加蒙古人民革命党第十三次代表大会的中国共产党代表团今天在乌兰巴托工会大厦和蒙古建筑工人以及在蒙古帮助建设的中国建筑工人会见。
    蒙古国家建筑事务委员会党委书记普列布扎布代表建筑工人在会上讲话，表示热烈欢迎中国共产党代表团。
    乌兰夫代表中国共产党和中国工人阶级向蒙古工人致意。他祝贺蒙古工人在蒙古人民革命党的领导下进行着忘我的劳动，完成了第二个五年计划基本建设任务。他说，中国共产党和中国人民非常重视亲如手足的蒙古人民取得的巨大成就，并且把它看成像自己的成就一样。
    乌兰夫在致词中勉励在蒙古建筑企业中帮助建设的中国员工，要他们在劳动中虚心向蒙古同志学习，遵守蒙古的法律制度和社会风俗习惯，发扬劳动积极性，很好地完成企业的生产任务。
    接着，蒙古部长会议副主席兼国家建筑事务委员会主席迈达尔代表建筑事务委员会向中国共产党代表团赠送了纪念章和蒙古象棋等礼品。
    会上，建筑工人们还表演了歌舞节目来欢迎中国客人。
