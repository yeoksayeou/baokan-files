Title: Zhang Xiruo Holds Reception for Foreign Envoys to China
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 13th—Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a reception on the afternoon of the 13th to entertain foreign envoys and cultural and diplomatic officials stationed in China.

    At the reception, Zhang Xiruo expressed his hope that he could strengthen contact with foreign embassies in China in the future and strive to develop cultural exchanges between China and other countries and enhance friendly relations.

### 张奚若举行酒会招待各国驻华使节

1958-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    张奚若举行酒会招待各国驻华使节
    据新华社13日讯  对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若13日下午举行酒会招待各国驻华使节和文化外交官员。  
    张奚若在会上表示希望今后能加强同各国驻华使馆的联系，为发展中国与各国之间的文化交流，增进友好关系而努力。
