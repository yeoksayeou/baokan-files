Title: New Type of Gas Engine
Author:
Date: 1958-03-28
Page: 3

    New Type of Gas Engine
    A model 1105, 7.5 horsepower gas engine was successfully trial-produced at the Tianjin Automobile Repair Factory on the 25th. This gas engine weighs over 280 kilograms, which is almost half the weight of the larger horsepower gas engine produced by the factory last year. The number of revolutions per minute is 900 more than the large horsepower gas engine.
      (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 新型煤气机

1958-03-28
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    新型煤气机
    一台1105型七点五马力的煤气机，25日在天津市汽车修配厂试制成功。这种煤气机重二百八十多公斤，比这个厂去年生产的大马力煤气机体重减轻将近一半，每分钟转数比大马力煤气机多九百转。
      （据新华社讯）
