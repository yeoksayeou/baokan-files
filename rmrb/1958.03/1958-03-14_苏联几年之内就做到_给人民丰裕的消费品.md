Title: The Soviet Union Will Achieve Abundant Consumer Goods for the People Within a Few Years
Author:
Date: 1958-03-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: TASS reported on the 12th that Kozlov, a member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation, who was nominated as a candidate for deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, said in a speech to voters in the Smolny District of Leningrad on the 12th that the economic development of the Soviet Union has now reached a level that allows us to fully guarantee the supply of abundant consumer goods to residents within the next five to seven years.

Kirichenko, a member of the Presidium and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, pointed out in a speech in the Kiev-Lenin constituency that in the next fifteen years, the Soviet Union will increase the output of decisive industrial sectors by two to three times. After the realization of this task, the Soviet people will push the industrial power of the Soviet Union to a higher level and will catch up with and surpass the United States in the per capita output of many important products.

### 苏联几年之内就做到  给人民丰裕的消费品

1958-03-14
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联几年之内就做到
    给人民丰裕的消费品
    据新华社13日讯  据塔斯社12日报道：被提名为苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人的苏共中央主席团委员、俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席科兹洛夫12日向列宁格勒市斯莫尔尼区选民发表演说时说，现在，苏联的经济发展已经达到这样的水平，使我们完全能够在最近五年到七年内保证供应居民丰裕的消费品。
    苏共中央主席团委员和中央书记基里钦科在基辅—列宁选区发表演说时指出，在最近十五年内，苏联将把决定性的工业部门的产量提高二到三倍。在这个任务实现以后，苏联人民将把苏联的工业威力推向更高的水平，并且将有许多重要产品在按人口平均的产量方面赶上和超过美国。
