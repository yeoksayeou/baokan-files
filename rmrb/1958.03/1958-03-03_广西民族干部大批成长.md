Title: A Large Number of Ethnic Minority Cadres are Growing in Guangxi

Author:

Date: 1958-03-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 2nd - In the Zhuang, Yao, Miao, Dong and other ethnic groups in Guangxi Province, there are already more than 51,000 ethnic minority cadres, of which 1,441 are cadres holding leadership positions at or above the county level.

Most of these ethnic minority cadres are activists who emerged after liberation in various movements, and some have honorably joined the Chinese Communist Party or the Communist Youth League.

### 广西民族干部大批成长

1958-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    广西民族干部大批成长
    据新华社南宁2日电　在广西省僮、瑶、苗、侗等民族中，已有民族干部五万一千多人，其中担任县级以上领导工作的干部有一千四百四十一人。
    这些民族干部大部分是解放后在历次运动中涌现出来的积极分子，有些已经光荣地参加了中国共产党或共青团。
