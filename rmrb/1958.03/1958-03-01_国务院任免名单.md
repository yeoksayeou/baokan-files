Title: State Council Appointment and Dismissal List
Author:
Date: 1958-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The State Council, at its 71st plenary meeting held on February 12th, approved the appointment and removal of a number of personnel from foreign embassies, state organs, and institutions of higher learning. The list is as follows:

    Appointed Li Zewang as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics;
    Liang Guobin as Vice Minister of Public Security;
    Gao Qingchun as Vice President of the Beijing Institute of Technology;
    Wang Taijun as Vice President of Henan Medical College.
    Removed Zhou Xing from the post of Vice Minister of Public Security;
    Gao Qingchun from the post of Vice President of Northwest Institute of Technology;
    Huang Wenmin and Xu Sidu from the posts of Vice President of Chengdu Institute of Radio Engineering.

### 国务院任免名单

1958-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社28日讯　国务院在2月12日举行的第七十一次全体会议上通过任免一批驻外使馆、国家机关和高等学校工作人员，名单如下：
    任命李则望为中华人民共和国驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟大使馆参赞；
    梁国斌为公安部副部长；
    高庆春为北京工业学院副院长；
    王太钧为河南医学院副院长。
    免去周兴的公安部副部长职务；
    高庆春的西北工学院副院长职务；
    黄文民、徐思铎的成都电讯工程学院副院长职务。
