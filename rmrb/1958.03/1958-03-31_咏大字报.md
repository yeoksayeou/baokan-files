Title: Ode to the Big-Character Poster
Author: Lou Shiyi
Date: 1958-03-31
Page: 8

　　Ode to the Big-Character Poster
　　Lou Shiyi
　　Like a primeval forest, boundless and vast; like white-hot flames, in buildings, in courtyards, in workshops, in alleyways,
  burning everywhere. A vivid and lively scene appears here. It is a red line, connecting people's hearts together; it is a bright mirror, allowing thoughts,
  to meet with thoughts.
      1957.3, Shanghai.

### 咏大字报
楼适夷
1958-03-31
第8版()
专栏：

    咏大字报
　　楼适夷像原始的森林，无际无边；像白热的火焰，在大楼、在庭院、在车间、在里弄，
  到处烧遍。一个生动的、活泼的局面，在这里出现。是红线，把人和人的心结成一串；是明镜，让思想，
  和思想见面。
      1957、3，上海。
