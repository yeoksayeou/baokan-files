Title: Large Numbers of Draught Animals Support Various Regions
Author:
Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 2

Mutual Support, Brotherly Affection

    Large Numbers of Draught Animals Support Various Regions
    (Our Newspaper reports) Farmers and herdsmen of all ethnic groups in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region will use 250,000 head (or units) of strong draught animals to support farmers in Hebei, Shandong, Shanxi, Anhui, and Jiangsu provinces in achieving a great leap forward in agricultural production. The first batch of over 72,000 head (or units) of strong oxen, horses, and donkeys is now being transferred and shipped.
    Hebei Province, which has always lacked draught animals, will receive 150,000 head (or units) of draught animals supported by Inner Mongolia this year. Soon, 11,500 Mongolian horses will appear in Henan, Shandong, and Anhui.

### 大批耕畜支援各地

1958-03-06
第2版()
专栏：互相支援  亲如兄弟

    大批耕畜支援各地
    本报讯　内蒙古自治区各族农牧民将以二十五万头（匹）精壮耕畜，支援河北、山东、山西、安徽和江苏等省农民实现农业生产大跃进。首批精壮的耕牛、马、驴七万二千多头（匹）现正向外交接启运。
    耕畜一向缺乏的河北省，今年将得到由内蒙古支援的十五万头（匹）耕畜。河南、山东、安徽不久就有一万一千五百匹蒙古马出现。
