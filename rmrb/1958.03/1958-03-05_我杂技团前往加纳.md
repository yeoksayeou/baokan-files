Title: Our Acrobatic Troupe Departs for Ghana
Author:
Date: 1958-03-05
Page: 6

    Our Acrobatic Troupe Departs for Ghana
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo: The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, which is currently on a performance tour of the Middle East and African countries, left Morocco for Ghana on February 23. Since mid-January, the acrobatic troupe has performed forty-one shows in cities such as Casablanca, Rabat, and Fez, and has been warmly welcomed by the Moroccan government and people.
    The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe was specially invited to perform at the Moroccan Royal Palace on February 4.

### 我杂技团前往加纳

1958-03-05
第6版()
专栏：

    我杂技团前往加纳
    据新华社讯　开罗讯：正在中东和非洲国家进行访问演出的中国杂技团2月23日离开摩洛哥前往加纳。杂技团从1月中旬以来在卡萨布兰卡、拉巴特、费斯等城市演出了四十一场，受到摩洛哥政府和人民的热烈欢迎。
    中国杂技团2月4日曾经被特地邀请到摩洛哥王宫演出。
