Title: State Council Holds Plenary Meeting, Approves China-Yemen Friendship Treaty
Author:
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th March: The State Council held its 72nd plenary meeting this morning. Vice Premier Bo Yibo reported on the great leap forward in national industrial and agricultural production.

    The meeting adopted a decision: In order to meet the needs of the development of socialist economic construction in the Beijing and Hebei regions, the State Council has decided to transfer the five counties of Tongxian, Shunyi, Daxing, Liangxiang, and Fangshan, originally under the jurisdiction of Hebei Province, and Tongzhou City to the leadership of Beijing Municipality.

    The meeting discussed and approved the "Provisional Regulations on Financial Management in National Autonomous Areas (Draft)" and decided to submit it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and approval.

    The meeting approved the Treaty of Friendship between China and Yemen, the Treaty of Commerce, and the Agreement on Scientific, Technical and Cultural Cooperation, and decided to submit these treaties and agreements to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and approval.

    The meeting approved the China-Mongolia Air Transport Agreement, the Minutes of the China-Mongolia Civil Aviation Bureau, and the China-Mongolia Civil Aviation Technical Cooperation Agreement. The meeting has also approved the "Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic on Cooperation in Health Affairs."

    Finally, the meeting approved a list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行全体会议  通过中也友好条约

1958-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议
    通过中也友好条约
    新华社7日讯　国务院在今天上午举行了第七十二次全体会议。薄一波副总理在会上报告了全国工农业生产大跃进的情况。
    会议通过了一项决定：为了适应北京和河北两个地区社会主义经济建设的发展的需要，国务院决定将原属河北省的通县、顺义、大兴、良乡、房山五个县和通州市划归北京市领导。
    会议讨论通过了“民族自治地方财政管理暂行办法（草案）”，并决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议通过了中国和也门友好条约，商务条约以及科学、技术和文化合作协定，并且决定把这些条约和协定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议核准了中蒙航空交通协定、中蒙民航局议定书和中蒙民航技术合作协定。会议还核准了“中华人民共和国政府和德意志民主共和国政府关于卫生事业合作的协定”。
    会议最后通过了任免名单。
