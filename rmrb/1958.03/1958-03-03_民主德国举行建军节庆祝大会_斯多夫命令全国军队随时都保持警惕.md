Title: GDR Holds Celebration Rally for Army Day, Stoph Orders National Army to Remain Vigilant at All Times
Author:
Date: 1958-03-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd - According to a report by the German News Agency: On February 28th, on the eve of the German Democratic Republic's Army Day, a grand celebration was held in Berlin.

    GDR Minister of National Defense Stoph spoke at the celebration, condemning the transformation of West Germany into a center of provocation and a major base for preparing aggressive war. He said that in the face of the conspiracies of imperialists and warmongers, we must heighten our vigilance and strengthen our national defense to defend the socialist construction of the GDR and peace in Europe.

    Stoph finally reiterated to the Socialist Unity Party, the Republic's government, and the people his determination to defend the socialist motherland from invasion by any enemy. He said: "Standing with us are loyal and great friendly nations like the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China, as well as all socialist countries, and the armed forces of the socialist camp united on the basis of the Warsaw Pact. Therefore, we are invincible."

### 民主德国举行建军节庆祝大会  斯多夫命令全国军队随时都保持警惕

1958-03-03
第6版()
专栏：

    民主德国举行建军节庆祝大会
    斯多夫命令全国军队随时都保持警惕
    据新华社2日讯　据德意志通讯社报道：2月28日，德意志民主共和国建军节的前夕，在柏林举行了盛大的庆祝会。
    德意志民主共和国国防部长斯多夫在庆祝会上讲话，谴责把西德变成挑衅的中心和准备侵略战争的主要基地。他说，面对着帝国主义者和战争贩子的阴谋，我们必须提高警惕和加强国防力量来保卫民主德国的社会主义建设和欧洲的和平。
    斯多夫最后向统一社会党、共和国政府和人民重申了保卫社会主义祖国不受任何敌人侵犯的决心。他说：“同我们站在一边的有像苏联、中华人民共和国这样忠实和伟大的友邦，以及所有社会主义国家，有在华沙条约基础上团结在一起的社会主义阵营的武装部队，因此，我们是不可战胜的。”
