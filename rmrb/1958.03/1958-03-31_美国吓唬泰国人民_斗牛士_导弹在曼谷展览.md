Title: US Scares the Thai People: "Matador" Missile on Display in Bangkok
Author:
Date: 1958-03-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 29th, Bangkok dispatch: The United States handed over eighty-three American aircraft of various types to Thailand as "military aid" on March 27th. At the same time, the American "Matador" missile was exhibited in Bangkok. These missiles were specially shipped from Taiwan to Thailand.

It is reported that since the US-Thai "Military Aid" Agreement was signed in 1950, the United States has transferred a total of one hundred and fifty-five aircraft to Thailand.

### 美国吓唬泰国人民  “斗牛士”导弹在曼谷展览

1958-03-31
第5版()
专栏：

    美国吓唬泰国人民
    “斗牛士”导弹在曼谷展览
    据新华社29日讯　曼谷消息：美国在3月27日把八十三架不同类型的美国飞机作为“军事援助”移交给泰国，同时在曼谷展出了美国的“斗牛士”式导弹。这些导弹是特地从台湾运到泰国的。
    据悉：自从美泰“军事援助”协定在1950年签订以来，美国一共移交给泰国一百五十五架飞机。
