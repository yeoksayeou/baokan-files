Title: The City of Heroes Welcomes the Returning Heroes (Picture)
Author: Gao Guoquan
Date: 1958-03-16
Page: 2

    The City of Heroes Welcomes the Returning Heroes
    In the past few days, Andong City has been filled with the sound of gongs and drums, and decorated with lanterns and colored streamers. The people of the entire city are preparing to welcome the most beloved people with great excitement.
    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Gao Guoquan photographed
    A huge red triumphal arch has been solemnly erected at the Yalu River Bridge, ready to welcome the most beloved people back. The amateur propaganda team of Andong City is rehearsing programs to welcome the Chinese People's Volunteers.

### 英雄的城市迎接英雄回国（图片）
高国权
1958-03-16
第2版()
专栏：

    英雄的城市迎接英雄回国
    安东市几天来，到处都是锣鼓喧天，悬灯结彩，全市的人民都以万分兴奋的心情准备迎接最可爱的人
    新华社记者  高国权摄
    一座红色巨大的凯旋门，已经庄严地矗立在鸭绿江桥头，准备迎接最可爱的人归来。安东市业余宣传队正在排演欢迎志愿军的节目。
