Title: Li Zhouyuan Appointed Vice Premier of North Korea
Author:
Date: 1958-03-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th -- The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea appointed Li Zhouyuan as Vice Premier of the Cabinet on the 8th.

The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly relieved Park Ui-wan of his post as Vice Premier of the Cabinet.

### 李周渊任朝鲜副首相

1958-03-11
第6版()
专栏：

    李周渊任朝鲜副首相
    据新华社平壤10日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会在8日任命李周渊为内阁副首相。
    最高人民会议常任委员会解除了朴义琓的内阁副首相职务。
