Title: World Peace Council Standing Committee Meets in New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1958-03-24
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 22nd: The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council began its meeting this morning at Vigyan Bhavan here. The meeting will last three days. This is the first meeting of the World Peace Council to be held in India.

    The meeting was presided over by Dr. Kitchlu, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council and Chairman of the All-India Peace Council.

    Mr. Sundarlal, Vice-Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, addressed the meeting, welcoming the members of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council and requesting support for the national independence movements of Asian and African countries.

    Mrs. Isabelle Blume, Secretary of the Secretariat of the World Peace Council, gave a report on the international situation and the peace movement on behalf of the Standing Committee.

    Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, President of the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee, as well as Kaka Kalelkar, Sahib Singh Soakhey, Dr. Gian Chand, A.K. Gopalan, and other Indian peace movement leaders were also invited to attend the meeting.

### 世界和平理事会常委会在新德里举行会议

1958-03-24
第6版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常委会
    在新德里举行会议
    据新华社新德里22日电世界和平理事会常务委员会今天上午在这里的科学宫开始举行会议。会议将举行三天。这是世界和平理事会第一次在印度举行的会议。
    会议由世界和平理事会副主席、全印和平理事会主席克其鲁博士主持。
    全印和平理事会副主席森德拉尔在会上致词，对世界和平理事会常务委员会的委员们表示欢迎，并且要求支持亚非各国的民族独立运动。
    世界和平理事会书记处书记伊莎贝丽·布伦姆夫人代表常务委员会作了关于国际局势和和平运动的报告。
    印度亚洲团结委员会主席拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人，以及卡卡·卡勒尔卡、萨希布·辛格·索克、吉安·昌德博士、阿·库·高普兰和其他印度和平运动领袖也应邀参加了这次会议。
