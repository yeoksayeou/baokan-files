Title: Premier Zhou Meets Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 29th: Premier Zhou Enlai received this morning the Government Delegation of the Polish People's Republic, headed by Jaroszewicz, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, and delegation members Trąmpczyński, Jaszczuk, Olszewski, Kolę, and Koi.

Present at the meeting were Chen Yi, Vice-Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Yang Yingjie, Vice-Chairman of the State Planning Commission; and Li Zheren, Vice-Minister of Foreign Trade.

Also present at the meeting was the Polish Ambassador to China, Kirylok.

During the meeting, Vice-Chairman Jaroszewicz presented gifts to Premier Zhou.

### 周总理接见贵宾

1958-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见贵宾
    新华社29日讯　周恩来总理在今天上午接见了波兰人民共和国政府代表团团长、波兰部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇和代表团团员特朗普钦斯基、雅希楚克、奥尔谢夫斯基、科莱、科依。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰，对外贸易部副部长李哲人。
    接见时，波兰驻中国大使基里洛克也在座。
    接见的时候，雅罗谢维奇副主席向周总理赠送了礼品。
