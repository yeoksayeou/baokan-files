Title: Soviet Artificial Satellite to Pass Over China Tomorrow

Author:

Date: 1958-03-21
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, March 20th. The Zijinshan Astronomical Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences announced: On the morning of March 22nd, the Soviet Union's second artificial satellite will pass over China from northwest to southeast at the following times: 4:47 AM over Baotou, Yinchuan, Xining, and Lanzhou; 4:48 AM over Taiyuan, Xi'an, and Zhengzhou; 4:49 AM over Chongqing, Wuhan, Changsha, and Nanchang; 4:50 AM over Hengyang, Guilin, Ganzhou, Wuzhou, Guangzhou, and Shantou. The satellite will be visible to the naked eye in areas near the above routes.

### 苏联人造卫星明日经过我国时间

1958-03-21
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联人造卫星
    明日经过我国时间
    新华社南京20日电　中国科学院紫金山天文台发布：3月22日晨，苏联第二颗人造卫星从西北到东南经过我国时间如下：四时四十七分经过包头、银川、西宁、兰州，四时四十八分过太原、西安、郑州，四时四十九分过重庆、武汉、长沙、南昌，四时五十分过衡阳、桂林、赣州、梧州、广州、汕头。在上述路线附近的地区，都可以用肉眼看到。
