Author: Gao Liang
Date: 1958-03-22
Page: 7

Five White-Haired Girls
Yesterday, at the "Welcome Reception for the Japanese Matsuyama Ballet Troupe" held at the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles building, five actresses who have already performed and are currently performing as "Xi'er" in Beijing met. The photographer took a special photo of them. From left to right are Zhao Yanxia, the Peking Opera White-Haired Girl actress, Wang Kun, the opera White-Haired Girl actress, Matsuyama Mikiko, the ballet White-Haired Girl actress, Du Jinfang, the Peking Opera White-Haired Girl actress, and Wang Qingfu, the opera White-Haired Girl actress. Photo by Gao Liang, our reporter

### 五个白毛女（图片）
高粮
1958-03-22
第7版()
专栏：

    五个白毛女
    昨天在中国文联大楼举行的“欢迎日本松山芭蕾舞团联欢会”上，五位现在北京已经演出和正在演出的“白毛女”中扮演喜儿的演员见了面。摄影记者专为她们拍了照。左起是京剧白毛女演员赵燕侠，歌剧白毛女演员王昆，芭蕾舞剧白毛女演员松山树子，京剧白毛女演员杜近芳，歌剧白毛女演员王庆芙　本报记者　高粮摄
