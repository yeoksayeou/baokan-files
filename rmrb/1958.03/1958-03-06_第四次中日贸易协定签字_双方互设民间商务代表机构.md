Title: Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement Signed; Both Sides to Establish Non-Governmental Commercial Representative Offices
Author:
Date: 1958-03-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 5th. The China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, together with the Diet Members' League to Promote Japan-China Trade, the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and the Japan Export-Import Association, signed the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement in Beijing at noon today. The agreement stipulates that the total value of exports and imports between China and Japan will be 35 million pounds each, for the year commencing today.

This agreement includes provisions not found in the Third Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement signed in Tokyo in 1955, such as the mutual establishment of permanent non-governmental commercial representative offices in each other's countries; both parties agreeing to strive to promote and strengthen technical exchanges and cooperation between the two countries; and the establishment of guaranteed long-term supply relationships for important materials needed by both parties, based on needs and possibilities.

Signing the agreement on behalf of their respective sides were: Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Vice Chairmen Lei Renmin, Li Zhuchen, and Ji Chaoding; Chinese negotiators Li Xinnong, Xiao Fangzhou, Zhan Wu, Sun Chun, and Shu Ziqing; Chief Representative of the Diet Members' League to Promote Japan-China Trade, Masanosuke Ikeda; Representatives Koziro Ueki, Seiichi Katsumada, Einosuke Maeda, Yoshie Morita, and Kanichi Kawakami; Representatives of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, Kumaichi Yamamoto and Den Kawakatsu; and Representatives of the Japan Export-Import Association, Saburo Nango and Hiroshi Nida.

After the signing, Masanosuke Ikeda spoke, congratulating on the signing of the agreement. He said that the signing and implementation of the agreement would play a significant role in developing trade and friendly relations between the two countries.

Nan Hanchen said in his speech that the signing of this agreement was a matter of great celebration in Sino-Japanese trade relations. He particularly emphasized that the mutual establishment of commercial representative offices according to the agreement would undoubtedly facilitate the conduct of trade between the two countries and achieve broader development. He said that this was an important achievement in the continuous development of trade between the two countries.

Li Dequan, Zhang Xiruo, Liao Chengzhi, Hou Debang, and Qiao Guanhua, among others, attended the signing ceremony on the Chinese side.

In addition to the entire members of the Japanese trade mission, Vice Chairman of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, Imaichi Ito; Head of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Group, Eiichi Shukutani; Executive Director of the Japan-China Trade Association, Kazuo Suzuki; and Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee in Beijing, Kinkazu Saionji, and others attended the signing ceremony on the Japanese side.

### 第四次中日贸易协定签字  双方互设民间商务代表机构

1958-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    第四次中日贸易协定签字
    双方互设民间商务代表机构
    新华社5日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会同日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟、日本国际贸易促进协会和日本输出入组合，今天中午在北京签订了第四次中日贸易协定。协定规定从今天开始的一年内，中日双方输出和输入的总额各为三千五百万英镑。
    这次协定规定了双方相互在对方国家设置常驻的民间商务代表机构；双方同意努力促进和加强两国间的技术交流和技术合作；根据需要和可能就双方所需要的重要物资建立有保证的长期供应关系等条款，这些都是1955年双方在东京签订的第三次中日贸易协定所没有的内容。
    分别代表双方在协定上签字的是：中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸，副主席雷任民、李烛尘、冀朝鼎，中国方面谈判代表李新农、萧方洲、詹武、孙纯和舒自清；日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟首席代表池田正之辅，代表植木庚子郎、胜间田清一、前田荣之助、森田义卫、川上贯一，日本国际贸易促进协会代表山本熊一、川胜传，日本日中输出入组合代表南乡三郎和似田博。
    签字后，池田正之辅讲话祝贺协定的签订。他说，协定的签订和执行，对发展两国的贸易和友好关系将起很大的作用。
    南汉宸在讲话中说，这次协定的签订是中日贸易关系中一件值得庆贺的大事。他特别强调双方根据协定互设商务代表机构以后，必将使两国贸易的进行更加顺利，获得更广阔的发展。他说，这是两国贸易不断发展中的一个重要成就。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有李德全、张奚若、廖承志、侯德榜和乔冠华等人。
    日本方面参加签字仪式的，除日本通商使节团的全体人员外，还有日本国际贸易促进协会副会长伊藤今朝市，日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一，日本中日贸易会专务理事铃木一雄和在北京的亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长西园寺公一等人。
