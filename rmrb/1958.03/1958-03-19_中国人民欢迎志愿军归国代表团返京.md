Title: The Chinese People Welcome the Return of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Chinese people welcomed the Chinese People's Volunteer Army (CPVA) homecoming delegation, which returned to Beijing by train from Andong this morning.

Mun Jae-ju, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy in China, who went to Andong to welcome the first group of returning CPVA troops, also returned to Beijing on the same train.

### 中国人民欢迎志愿军归国代表团返京

1958-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民欢迎志愿军归国代表团返京
    新华社18日讯　中国人民欢迎志愿军归国代表团今天上午乘车从安东返回北京。
    前往安东欢迎志愿军首批归国部队的朝鲜驻我国大使馆临时代办文在洙，也同车回到北京。
