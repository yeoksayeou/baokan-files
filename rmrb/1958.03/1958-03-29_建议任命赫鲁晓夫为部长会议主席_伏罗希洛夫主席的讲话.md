Title: Suggesting Khrushchev as Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Chairman Voroshilov's Speech
Author:
Date: 1958-03-29
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 27th: TASS reported from Moscow on the 27th that Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, entrusted by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the heads of delegations to the Supreme Soviet, proposed at the joint session of the Union Council and the Council of Nationalities held on the 27th: Appoint N.S. Khrushchev as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, and instruct him to submit a list of government members for approval by the Supreme Soviet.

	Voroshilov said that Khrushchev is an outstanding activist of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet state, and a glorious son of the Soviet working class. Voroshilov pointed out that for more than forty years, Khrushchev has faithfully served the interests of the working people and the cause of socialism and communism.

	Voroshilov talked about the many tasks the Soviet people have undertaken in recent years under the leadership of the Communist Party to realize Lenin's idea of building communism in the Soviet Union. He emphasized that in this whole creative undertaking, Khrushchev, with his extraordinary creative genius, his endless tireless perseverance, and his pioneering spirit, plays an outstanding role.

	Voroshilov said that Khrushchev's name is inextricably linked with the remarkable achievements in implementing the Soviet Union's peaceful foreign policy and in the Soviet people's struggle for lasting world peace. Voroshilov said that it is no exaggeration to say that people all over the world now know Khrushchev as the strongest, most principled, and tireless defender of peace, and a fighter against the threat of new wars and for peaceful cooperation among all countries in the world. Voroshilov went on to say that we are proud that the person leading our party is carrying out this great work for all mankind so tenaciously, so competently, and with such魄力. His tireless work in this area, his visits to Eastern and Western countries, and his talks with politicians and journalists from all over the world have greatly increased the number of friends of the Soviet Union and greatly enhanced the Soviet Union's prestige on the international stage.

	At the same time, Voroshilov also pointed out Khrushchev's highly fruitful work as the First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee. Voroshilov said that according to the decision of the Party Central Committee, Khrushchev will continue to serve as the First Secretary of the Central Committee. Voroshilov expressed his belief that after leading the Soviet government, Khrushchev will undoubtedly implement Lenin's foreign policy and the policy of peace and security for all peoples with greater perseverance.

### 建议任命赫鲁晓夫为部长会议主席  伏罗希洛夫主席的讲话

1958-03-29
第5版()
专栏：

    建议任命赫鲁晓夫为部长会议主席
    伏罗希洛夫主席的讲话
    新华社28日讯　塔斯社莫斯科27日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克利门特·伏罗希洛夫受苏联共产党中央委员会和最高苏维埃代表团团长会议的委托，在27日举行的联盟院和民族院的联席会议上提出一项建议：任命尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫为苏联部长会议主席，并且责成他提出政府成员名单交最高苏维埃批准。
    伏罗希洛夫说，赫鲁晓夫是苏联共产党和苏维埃国家的杰出活动家，是苏联工人阶级的光荣的儿子。伏罗希洛夫指出，赫鲁晓夫四十多年来一直忠实地为劳动人民的利益、为社会主义和共产主义事业服务。
    伏罗希洛夫谈到苏联人民在共产党的领导下，最近一些年来为实现列宁的在苏联建设共产主义这一思想而进行的许多工作。他着重指出，在这整个创造性的事业中，赫鲁晓夫、他的非凡的创造天才、无穷无尽的孜孜不倦的毅力和首创精神，起着卓越的作用。
    伏罗希洛夫说，赫鲁晓夫的名字同在执行苏联的和平外交政策和在苏联人民争取世界持久和平斗争中的卓越成就不可分地联系在一起。伏罗希洛夫说，可以毫不夸张地说，全世界人民现在都知道赫鲁晓夫是一个最坚强、最有原则性和不疲倦的和平捍卫者，为反对新战争威胁、为世界一切国家和平合作而奋斗的战士。伏罗希洛夫接着说，我们感到自豪的是，领导我们党的这个人是这样顽强地、这样干练地、这样有魄力地进行着这项巨大的全人类的工作。他在这方面的不倦的工作、对东西方国家的访问、他同各国政界人士和世界各国报界人士的谈话，大大地增加了苏联的朋友，大大提高了苏联在国际舞台上的威望。
    同时伏罗希洛夫还指出赫鲁晓夫在担任苏共中央第一书记职务方面的极富成果的工作。伏罗希洛夫说，根据党中央委员会的决定，赫鲁晓夫仍然继续担任中央委员会第一书记的职务。伏罗希洛夫表示相信，赫鲁晓夫在领导苏联政府以后，无疑地将以更大的毅力不懈地执行列宁主义的外交政策，执行和平和各国人民安全的政策。
