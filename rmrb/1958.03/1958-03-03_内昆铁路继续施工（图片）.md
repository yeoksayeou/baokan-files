Title: Neijiang-Kunming Railway Construction Continues (Picture)
Author: Jin Xuqi
Date: 1958-03-03

    Neijiang-Kunming Railway Construction Continues
    The construction of the Neijiang-Kunming Railway is continuing southward from Zigong and Shuping in Sichuan. The above picture shows the track-laying and bridge-building team laying the Aizi River Bridge in Fushun County on the Neijiang-Yibin section.
