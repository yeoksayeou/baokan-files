Title: French Fascists Are Stirring; De Gaulle Group Demands Establishment of Dictatorial Government
Author:
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, March 24th: The National Committee of the French Social Republican Party (De Gaulle Group) passed a resolution on the 23rd, demanding the establishment of a "National Salvation Institution" with broad powers to reorganize the French political system. According to Western news agencies, this resolution is, in effect, a demand for the establishment of a government headed by the fascist ringleader De Gaulle, wielding supreme power.

The Social Republican Party's resolution, aimed at the frequent instability of the French political situation and the contradictions and weakness within the ruling group, advocates the fascistization of the French political system. It calls for increasing the administrative power of the French president, making him "a true national arbiter." It states that this is the "key issue" in changing the current political system. The resolution also openly states that within the current parliamentary and governmental system, it is impossible to achieve the kind of "reform" that the Social Republicans expect.

### 法国法西斯分子蠢蠢欲动  戴高乐集团要求建立独裁政府

1958-03-25
第5版()
专栏：

    法国法西斯分子蠢蠢欲动
    戴高乐集团要求建立独裁政府
    据新华社24日讯巴黎消息：法国社会共和党（戴高乐集团）全国委员会23日通过决议，要求成立一个具有广泛权力的“国民援救机构”来改组法国的政治制度。据西方通讯社报道，这个决议事实上是要求成立一个以法西斯头目戴高乐为首的总揽大权的政府。
    社会共和党的这个决议针对着法国政局的经常动荡不定和统治集团内部矛盾和软弱的情况，鼓吹实行法国政体的法西斯化。它要求增加法国总统的行政权力，使他成为“一个真正的全国仲裁者”。它说，这是改变目前政治制度中的“关键问题”。这个决议还公开表示，在目前的议会和政府的体制以内，不可能实现社会共和党人所期待的那种“改革”。
