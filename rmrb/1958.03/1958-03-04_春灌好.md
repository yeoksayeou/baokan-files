Title: Spring Irrigation is Good

Author: 
Date: 1958-03-04
Page: 2

New Folk Rhymes

    Spring Irrigation is Good
    Spring irrigation, spring irrigation, one mu yields one and a half dan of grain.
                    (Huayin County, Henan)
    Spring irrigation following winter irrigation, grain and cotton production doubles.
                  (Jinghui Irrigation Area, Henan)

### 春灌好

1958-03-04
第2版()
专栏：新农谚

    春灌好
    春灌，春灌，一亩打粮一担半。
                    （河南华阴县）
    春灌接冬灌，粮棉翻一翻。
                  （河南泾惠灌区）
