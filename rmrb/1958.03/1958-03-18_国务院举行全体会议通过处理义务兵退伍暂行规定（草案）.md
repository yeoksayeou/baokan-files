Title: State Council Holds Plenary Session to Approve Provisional Regulations (Draft) on Handling the Discharge of Conscripted Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1958-03-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th — The State Council held its seventy-third plenary session on the morning of the 17th, and approved the State Council's Provisional Regulations (Draft) on Handling the Discharge of Conscripted Soldiers. This regulation will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and approval.
    The meeting also approved the Provisional Rules of the People's Republic of China on Maritime Safety Navigation for Non-Powered Vessels and passed a list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行全体会议通过处理义务兵退伍暂行规定（草案）

1958-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议通过处理义务兵退伍暂行规定（草案）
    新华社17日讯　国务院在17日上午举行第七十三次全体会议，通过了国务院关于处理义务兵退伍的暂行规定（草案）。这个规定将提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议还批准了中华人民共和国非机动船海上安全航行暂行规则，并通过了任免的名单。
