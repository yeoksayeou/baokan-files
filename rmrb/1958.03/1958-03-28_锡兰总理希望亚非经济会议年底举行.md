Title: Ceylon Prime Minister Hopes for Afro-Asian Economic Conference to be Held at Year's End
Author:
Date: 1958-03-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, March 27th: According to the Press Trust of India Colombo dispatch: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike said today at the Ceylon Chamber of Commerce that if other countries agree, he hopes that the Afro-Asian Economic Conference can be held in Ceylon before the end of this year to discuss issues of common concern regarding trade and economic cooperation.

He said that this conference would promote a certain degree of cooperation among agricultural countries in the Afro-Asian region, which is very valuable. The conference will also help to discuss important issues such as food production and distribution, as well as food transportation.

### 锡兰总理希望亚非经济会议年底举行

1958-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理希望
    亚非经济会议年底举行
    据新华社27日讯　据印度报业托辣斯科伦坡消息：锡兰总理班达拉奈克今天在锡兰商会上说，如果其他国家同意的话，他希望亚非国家经济会议能在今年底以前在锡兰举行，以讨论共同有关的贸易和经济合作问题。
    他说，这个会议会促进亚非地区农产国家一定程度的合作，这种合作是很有价值的。会议也会有助于讨论粮食生产和分配以及粮食运输之类的重要问题。
