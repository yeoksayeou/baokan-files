Title: One Ox Can Pull Two Waterwheels
Author:
Date: 1958-03-29
Page: 2

Brief News

    One Ox Can Pull Two Waterwheels
    The "East is Red" Agricultural Cooperative in Fenghua, Zhejiang Province, has successfully modified old-style waterwheels, enabling one ox to pull two waterwheels. This not only saves animal power and increases irrigation efficiency, but also keeps the modification costs low.

### 一牛能拖两部水车

1958-03-29
第2版()
专栏：简讯

    一牛能拖两部水车
    浙江奉化东方红农业社把旧式水车改装，使一牛拖两部水车的尝试已经成功。不仅节省了畜力，提高了灌溉效率，而且改装的成本也不高。
