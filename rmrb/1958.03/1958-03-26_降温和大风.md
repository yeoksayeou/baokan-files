    Cooling Temperatures and Strong Winds
    (Our dispatch) The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a cooling temperature and strong wind forecast at 8:00 PM on March 25th:
    It is predicted that on the morning of the 26th, the minimum temperature in northern Shanxi, northern Hebei, northern Shandong, and the entire province of Liaoning will drop below 0 degrees Celsius. Northern Shaanxi, southern Shanxi, southern Hebei, southern Shandong, and northeastern Henan will drop to between 0 and 3 degrees Celsius. On the morning of the 27th, the temperature in southern Shanxi, southern Hebei, southern Shandong, northern Henan, and northern Shaanxi will drop below 0 degrees Celsius. The temperature in northern Jiangsu, northern Anhui, southern Henan, and northern Hubei will drop to between 0 and 4 degrees Celsius, possibly with frost. Southern Hubei, southern Anhui, and southern Jiangsu will drop to between 3 and 6 degrees Celsius. In the next thirty-six hours, in the Bohai Sea, Yellow Sea, Shandong Peninsula, East China Sea, and Taiwan Strait, winds will gradually increase from north to south to about force 7. In the middle and lower reaches of the Yellow River, the Huai River basin, and the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River, winds will gradually increase to force 5 or 6, with a northerly direction.

### 降温和大风

1958-03-26
第7版()
专栏：气象台

    降温和大风
    本报讯　中央气象科学研究所3月25日下午8时发布降温和大风预告：
    预计26日早晨，山西北部，河北北部，山东北部，辽宁全省的最低气温将下降到摄氏零度以下。陕西北部，山西南部，河北南部，山东南部，河南东北部将下降到摄氏零度到三度。27日早晨，山西南部，河北南部，山东南部，河南北部，陕西北部的气温将下降到摄氏零度以下。江苏北部，安徽北部，河南南部，湖北北部的气温将下降到摄氏零度到四度，可能出现霜冻。湖北南部，安徽南部，江苏南部将下降到摄氏三度到六度。未来三十六小时内，渤海、黄海，山东半岛，东海，台湾海峡，风力将自北向南逐渐加强到七级左右，黄河中下游，淮河流域，长江流域中下游，风力将逐渐加强到五、六级，风向偏北。
