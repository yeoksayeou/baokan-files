Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Receives Shen Ke
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received Shen Ke, President of the West German "German-Chinese Association", on the afternoon of February 28th. Those present at the reception were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ji Chao Ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Song Zhiguang, Deputy Director of the Department of West European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理接见沈克

1958-03-02
第2版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见沈克
    新华社1日讯　国务院副总理陈毅在2月28日下午接见了西德“德中协会”会长沈克。接见时在座的有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎、外交部西欧司副司长宋之光。
