Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Congress Continues; Representatives of Fraternal Parties Deliver Congratulatory Speeches
Author:
Date: 1958-03-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, March 18th: The 13th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party began its general debate today. Delegations from the Soviet Union, China, and other fraternal parties delivered congratulatory speeches to the congress.

Representatives of party organizations at all levels in Mongolia who spoke at the meeting unanimously expressed their agreement with the summary report of the Central Committee made by Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee.

Ignatov, head of the delegation of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting and read out the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. He congratulated the Mongolian people on the significant achievements they had made in socialist construction. He said that the achievements of the Mongolian people are of great significance to the people of Asia and Africa who are fighting for national independence. The friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and Mongolia is being strengthened day by day in socialist construction and the struggle against common enemies. He also said that the recent peace initiatives put forward by the Soviet Union and the statement by the Chinese and Korean governments on the withdrawal of the Chinese People's Volunteers are important steps in easing international tensions. He pointed out that the Mongolian people have made concrete contributions to the cause of defending world peace.

Ulanfu, head of the delegation of the Communist Party of China, congratulated the congress on behalf of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people, wishing it a complete and brilliant success.

Ulanfu read out the congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the congress, and on behalf of the CPC Central Committee, presented the congress with a banner with the inscription: "We wish your party new victories in leading the Mongolian people in the cause of building socialism."

The heads of the delegations of the Albanian Labor Party and the Bulgarian Communist Party also delivered congratulatory speeches at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, March 18th: Ulanfu, head of the Chinese Communist Party delegation attending the 13th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and delegation members Ma Mingfang and He Ying, laid a wreath at the Sukhe Bator - Choibalsan Mausoleum in Ulan Bator, the capital of Mongolia, this afternoon.

### 蒙古人民革命党代表大会继续举行  各兄弟党代表致词祝贺

1958-03-19
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党代表大会继续举行
    各兄弟党代表致词祝贺
    新华社乌兰巴托18日电　蒙古人民革命党第十三次代表大会今天开始大会发言。苏联、中国和其他兄弟党的代表团向大会致祝词。
    在会上发言的蒙古各级党组织的代表一致表示同意中央委员会第一书记达姆巴所作的中央委员会的总结报告。
    苏联共产党代表团团长伊格纳托夫在会上致祝词，并且宣读了苏联共产党中央委员会的贺词。他祝贺蒙古人民在社会主义建设中取得的重大成就。他说，蒙古人民的成就对为民族独立而进行斗争的亚非人民有很大意义。苏蒙两国人民的友谊在社会主义建设和反对共同敌人的斗争中日益巩固。他还说，苏联最近提出的和平倡议，中朝两国政府关于撤出中国人民志愿军的声明，是缓和国际紧张局势的重要步骤。他指出，蒙古人民在保卫世界和平的事业中作了具体的贡献。
    中国共产党代表团团长乌兰夫代表中国共产党和中国人民祝大会获得圆满的光辉的成就。
    乌兰夫宣读了中国共产党中央委员会给大会的贺电，并且代表中共中央向大会献了一面锦旗，旗上面写着：“祝你们的党在领导蒙古人民建设社会主义的事业中取得新的胜利”。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、保加利亚共产党代表团团长也分别在会上致词祝贺。
    新华社乌兰巴托18日电参加蒙古人民革命党第十三次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长乌兰夫和团员马明方、何英今天下午在蒙古首都乌兰巴托向苏赫巴托尔——乔巴山陵墓献了花圈。
