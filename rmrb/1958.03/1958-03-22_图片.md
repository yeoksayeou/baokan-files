Title: "The White-Haired Girl" by the China Peking Opera Troupe
Author:
Date: 1958-03-22
Page: 8

The China Peking Opera Troupe's "The White-Haired Girl" held a dress rehearsal on the evening of the 20th and invited people from the literary and art circles. It will officially premiere tonight. The audience who watched the rehearsal expressed their welcome, believing that this is a good start to further promote the development of Peking Opera art. The picture shows a scene from the rehearsal: Li Shaochun as Yang Bailao, Du Jinfang as Xi'er.
