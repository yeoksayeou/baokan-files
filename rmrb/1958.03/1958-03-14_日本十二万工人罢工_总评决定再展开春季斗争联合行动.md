Title: 120,000 Japanese Workers Strike; General Council of Trade Unions of Japan Decides to Launch Further Joint Action in Spring Struggle
Author:
Date: 1958-03-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency report: The General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo) launched its second joint action of the spring struggle nationwide on the 13th. Coal miners, railway workers, postal and telecommunications workers, and others held strikes or rallies across the country, demanding higher wages. Workers who are members of the "Mitsui," "Mitsubishi," "Sumitomo," "Furukawa," and 14 other major coal mining companies began their first 24-hour strike on the 13th. In the Hokkaido and Kyushu regions alone, 140,000 miners participated in the strike. As of the 13th, workers at more than 130 small and medium-sized private railway companies had gone on strike. Members of the All-Japan Electric Communication Workers' Union, the All-Communications Employees' Union, and the All-Monopoly Corporation Workers' Union also held rallies on the 13th, demanding wage increases. The National Railway Workers' Union and the Japan National Railway Locomotive Engineers' Union also began their third round of legal struggles on the 13th. On the 11th, 120,000 employees of 122 private railway companies across Japan went on strike.

### 日本十二万工人罢工  总评决定再展开春季斗争联合行动

1958-03-14
第6版()
专栏：

    日本十二万工人罢工
    总评决定再展开春季斗争联合行动
    本报综合报道  据新华社消息：日本工会总评议会13日在全国范围内展开了春季斗争的第二次联合行动。煤矿、铁道和邮电工人等分别在全国各地举行罢工或大会，要求提高工资。“三井”、“三菱”、“住友”、“古河”等十四家大煤矿公司的工人会员13日开始举行第一次二十四小时的罢工。仅北海道和九州两个地区参加罢工的矿工就有十四万人。到13日已有一百三十多家中小私营铁道公司的工人举行了罢工。全国电气通讯工会、全递信从业人员工会和全专卖工会的会员13日也分别举行了要求增加工资的大会。国营铁道工会和日本国营铁道机车工会13日也开始举行第三次的合法斗争。11日日本全国已有一百二十二家私营铁道公司的十二万员工举行罢工。
