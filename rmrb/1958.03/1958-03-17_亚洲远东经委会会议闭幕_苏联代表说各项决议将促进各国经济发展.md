Title: ECAFE Meeting Concludes; Soviet Representative Says Resolutions Will Promote Economic Development of All Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-03-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Report: The 14th session of the United Nations Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East (ECAFE), which opened on March 5 in Kuala Lumpur, the capital of the Federation of Malaya, closed on the 15th. Delegations from twenty-three countries, including India, Burma, Indonesia, the Soviet Union, Pakistan, the United States, the United Kingdom, and France, participated in the meeting. Some specialized agencies of the United Nations, various international organizations, and certain non-member states of the commission sent observers to the meeting.

During the meeting, discussions were held on the report on the economic situation in Asia, issues related to technical assistance to countries in the ECAFE region, and other matters.

On the final day of the meeting, March 15, the committee adopted a report to the United Nations Economic and Social Council, as well as the ECAFE's work plan for 1958.

The report included a series of suggestions made by the Soviet delegation during the meeting, such as drafting principles for adjusting economic relations among ECAFE member states and establishing an agency for the peaceful use of atomic energy under the ECAFE. The report stated that the committee was pleased to see that some industrialized countries, including the Soviet Union, were willing to provide long-term loans for economic development to countries in the region.

The report emphasized that the Soviet representative had expressed willingness to assist in the design and construction work related to the development of the lower Mekong River.

The head of the Soviet delegation, Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Firyubin, emphasized in his closing remarks that the series of suggestions adopted by the meeting would undoubtedly actively promote the economic development of countries in the ECAFE region. The spirit of cooperation and mutual understanding among the member states of the commission, demonstrated during the meeting, was an important achievement of this session.

The next (fifteenth) session of the ECAFE is scheduled to be held in Australia in March 1959.

### 亚洲远东经委会会议闭幕  苏联代表说各项决议将促进各国经济发展

1958-03-17
第5版()
专栏：

    亚洲远东经委会会议闭幕
    苏联代表说各项决议将促进各国经济发展
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：3月5日在马来亚联合邦首都吉隆坡开幕的联合国亚洲及远东经济委员会第十四次会议，已于15日闭幕。参加这次会议的有印度、缅甸、印度尼西亚、苏联、巴基斯坦、美国、英国、法国等二十三个国家的代表团。联合国的一些专门机构、各种国际组织和某些不是这个委员会的成员国都派有观察员出席会议。
    会议期间，讨论了关于亚洲经济状况的报告、关于对亚洲及远东经济委员会地区国家的技术援助问题和其他问题。
    在15日最后一天会议上通过了委员会向联合国经济及社会理事会的报告以及亚洲及远东经济委员会1958年的工作计划。
    报告中包括了苏联代表团在会议过程中提出的一系列建议，例如拟订调整亚洲及远东经济委员会成员国之间的经济关系的原则以及在亚洲及远东经济委员会之下设立一个和平利用原子能的机构等。报告中说，委员会很满意地看到，有些工业国，包括苏联在内，愿意对这个地区的国家提供发展经济用的长期贷款。
    报告中强调指出，苏联代表曾经表示愿意协助进行有关开发湄公河下游的设计和施工工作。
    苏联代表团团长、苏联外交部副部长费留宾在闭幕会上发言时强调指出，会议通过的一系列建议毫无疑问会积极促进亚洲及远东经济委员会所属地区国家的经济发展。在会议过程中所表现的委员会成员国之间的合作和互相谅解的精神是这次会议的重要成绩。
    亚洲及远东经济委员会下一次（第十五次）会议定于1959年3月在澳大利亚举行。
