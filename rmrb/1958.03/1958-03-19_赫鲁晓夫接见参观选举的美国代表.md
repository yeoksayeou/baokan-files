Title: Khrushchev Receives American Representatives Visiting the Election
Author:
Date: 1958-03-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 18th: TASS reports: Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, received three American representatives on March 17th at their request, who came to the Soviet Union to understand the situation of the Soviet Supreme Soviet election. They were Professor Skymon, Professor Black, and "Fortune" magazine editor Donovan. Khrushchev had a conversation with them. (See picture above)

### 赫鲁晓夫接见参观选举的美国代表

1958-03-19
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见参观选举的美国代表
    据新华社18日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫在3月17日应请接见了到苏联来了解苏联最高苏维埃选举情况的三名美国代表，他们是：斯凯蒙教授、布莱克教授和“幸福”杂志编辑杜诺万。赫鲁晓夫同他们进行了谈话。（见上图）
