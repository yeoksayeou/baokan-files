Title: Romanian Government Delegation Visits Asia; Indian Capital Enthusiastically Prepares to Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-03-07
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 6th—The Romanian government delegation, headed by Minister Council Chairman Chivu Stoica, departed on a friendly visit to Asia at 8:00 AM today on a “Tu-104” special plane, upon invitation.

The delegation will first visit India, followed by China, Burma, Democratic Vietnam, Democratic Korea, and Mongolia.

Members of the delegation include Romanian Minister Council Vice Chairman Emil Bodnăraş and Foreign Minister Avram Bunaciu.

Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee, Maurer, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly, and members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, Central Committee members, government ministers, and representatives from all sectors, all saw the delegation off at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 6th—The Indian capital is undergoing final decorations and arrangements in preparation for welcoming Romanian Minister Council Chairman Chivu Stoica, who will arrive here tomorrow for a fifteen-day state visit.

The eight-mile road from the airport to the Presidential Palace has been decorated with the national flags of India and Romania, as well as many colorful flags.

Meanwhile, a Romanian folk art exhibition opened on the evening of the 5th at the All-India Fine Arts and Crafts Society building. Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan inaugurated the exhibition.

### 罗政府代表团来亚洲访问  印度首都热烈筹备迎贵宾

1958-03-07
第6版()
专栏：

    罗政府代表团来亚洲访问
    印度首都热烈筹备迎贵宾
    据新华社布加勒斯特6日电  以部长会议主席斯托伊卡为首的罗马尼亚政府代表团，应邀在今天上午八时乘“图—104”专机前往亚洲作友好访问。
    代表团首先到印度进行访问，随后将访问中国、缅甸、民主越南、民主朝鲜和蒙古。
    代表团团员有罗马尼亚部长会议副主席埃米尔·波德纳拉希，外交部长阿夫拉姆·布纳丘。
    罗马尼亚工人党中央第一书记乔治乌—德治，大国民议会主席团主席毛雷尔，以及工人党中央政治局委员、中央委员、政府部长和各界代表，都到机场送行。
    据新华社新德里6日电　印度首都正在进行最后的装饰和安排，准备欢迎明天到达这里进行十五天国事访问的罗马尼亚部长会议主席斯托伊卡。
    从飞机场到总统府八英里长的路上已经装饰了印度和罗马尼亚的国旗和许多彩旗。
    同时，罗马尼亚民间艺术展览会5日晚上在全印美术工艺协会大厦开幕。印度副总统拉达克里希南为展览会揭幕。
