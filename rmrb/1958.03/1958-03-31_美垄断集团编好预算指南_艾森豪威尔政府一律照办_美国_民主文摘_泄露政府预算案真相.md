Title: US Monopoly Group Compiles Budget Guide, Eisenhower Government Follows Suit – US "Democratic Digest" Reveals Truth About Government Budget

Author:
Date: 1958-03-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, March 30th: An article in the February issue of the American magazine "Democratic Digest," titled: "How the People Lose Out in the 1959 Budget," with the subtitle: "Eisenhower's Budget Follows the National Association of Manufacturers' Line to the Letter," states in full:

The (US government's) 1959 fiscal year budget plans three main ways to neuter programs disliked by the "corporate cabinet" that has dominated the federal government for the past five years. They neuter a program in one of three ways: (1) Cancel it; (2) Reduce it; (3) Transfer responsibility to the states.

This is the fate of many health, welfare, education, soil conservation, and urban construction programs in Eisenhower's 1959 fiscal year budget.

The blueprint for this plan was carefully laid out as early as September 1957 in a publication of the National Association of Manufacturers, giving Eisenhower's budget planners plenty of time to use it as a guide. This publication is titled "A Guide to Controlling and Reducing the 1959 Federal Budget."

    美垄断集团编好预算指南
    艾森豪威尔政府一律照办
    ——美国“民主文摘”泄露政府预算案真相
    新华社30日讯　华盛顿消息：美国“民主文摘”杂志二月号刊载一篇文章，正题是：“人民在1959年的预算案中怎样遭到损失”，副题是：“艾森豪威尔的预算案分毫不差地遵循全国制造商协会的方针”。全文如下：
    （美国政府）1959财政年度预算案中策划了三种主要的办法，来阉割为过去五年来一直支配着联邦政府的“企业内阁”所讨厌的那些计划。他们阉割一项计划不出如下三途：（一）勾销；（二）削减；
  （三）转嫁责任于各州。
    这就是在艾森豪威尔1959财政年度预算案中卫生、福利、教育、土壤保持及城市修建方面许多计划的命运。
    这个计划的蓝图早在1957年9月出版的一项全国制造商协会刊物中就仔细地提出来了，所以有充分的时间可以做艾森豪威尔的预算计划者的指南。这本刊物名为“控制和削减1959年联邦预算的指南”。
