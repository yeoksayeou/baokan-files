Title: Indonesian Government Forces Recapture Medan, Occupy Another Important Area in Central Sumatra
Author:
Date: 1958-03-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 17th. A spokesperson for the Indonesian Army Headquarters announced on the evening of March 17th that Indonesian government forces had regained control of Medan, the capital of North Sumatra (including the Polonia Airport and Belawan Port), at 7:00 AM on March 17th.

　　Troops taking illegal actions have fled the city. There are no rebel troops in or around Medan.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 17th. The Indonesian Army Headquarters announced today that on the morning of March 16th, illegal military activities occurred in Medan, the capital of North Sumatra. Major Nainggolan and his gang used their soldiers to usurp the authority of their superiors in order to achieve their political goals.

　　Nainggolan is the Deputy Chief of Staff of the 1st Military Region of North Sumatra.

　　Army Chief of Staff Major General Nasution said in an open letter to army personnel in the Medan area today that the Commander of the 1st Military Region and his headquarters, the 2nd Infantry Regiment, the Medan Garrison Commander, and the local naval and air force units are in constant contact with him and are carrying out the tasks of the government forces in and around Medan as usual.

　　According to foreign news reports, the illegal military actions in Medan were aimed at preventing government forces from further pressuring the Hussein group in Central Sumatra.

　　Reports say that the rebel troops under Hussein's control are preparing to flee from Padang and Bukittinggi in Central Sumatra to the southern part of Tapannuli in North Sumatra.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 17th. According to a report by Antara News Agency, Indonesian Prime Minister Djuanda today denied that Polonia Airport in Medan, the capital of North Sumatra, had been occupied by non-government forces.

　　Western news agencies on March 16th quoted this news spread by the Padang radio station controlled by the rebels.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 17th. According to a report by Antara News Agency, Indonesian government troops occupied the important oil-producing area of ​​Sorek-Seri-Indrapura in Central Sumatra on March 14th and purged the local rebels.

　　Army spokesman Lieutenant Colonel Piengadie said on March 15th that after the government troops occupied the Sorek-Seri-Indrapura area, they completely controlled the logistics supply line between Pekanbaru and the east coast of Central Sumatra. The Sorek-Seri-Indrapura area is located on the banks of the Sorek River.

　　According to Antara News Agency, after the government troops occupied Pekanbaru, Dumai, Rumbai, Sungai Pakning, Bengkalis, Sorek-Seri-Indrapura and other places, the local Caltex Oil Company will be able to resume normal operations soon.

　　At the same time, news from Central Sumatra said that the rebel group in Padang is currently very short of gasoline. This is the result of the government forces actually recovering the entire oil region of Central Sumatra.

　　A street on Wangkali Island outside the eastern port of Sumatra, Indonesia. Indonesian rebels led by Hussein and Syafruddin once occupied the island. It has now been recovered by government forces. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 印度尼西亚政府军收复棉兰  并在中苏门答腊占领又一重要地区

1958-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府军收复棉兰
    并在中苏门答腊占领又一重要地区
    新华社雅加达17日电　印度尼西亚陆军总部新闻处负责人17日晚上宣布，印度尼西亚政府军已经在17日早晨七点钟重新控制北苏门答腊首府棉兰（包括浮罗岭飞机场和勿拉湾港口）。
    采取非法行动的军队已经逃出城外。在棉兰市内和周围已经没有叛乱军队。
    新华社雅加达17日电　印度尼西亚陆军总部新闻处今天宣布，在16日早上四点钟，北苏门答腊首府棉兰发生了非法的军事活动。奈恩戈蓝少校和他的一帮人利用他们的兵士篡夺了他们的上级的权力，以便达到他们的政治目的。
    奈恩戈蓝是北苏门答腊第一军区的副参谋长。
    陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安少将今天在他给棉兰地区陆军人员的公开信中说，第一军区司令和他的司令部、第二步兵团、棉兰卫戍司令和当地的海军和空军部队都同他经常保持联系，他们照常在棉兰市内外执行政府部队的任务。
    据外国报纸消息说，在棉兰发生的非法的军事行动，目的是要阻止政府军对中苏门答腊侯赛尼集团进一步施加压力。
    有消息说，在侯赛尼控制下的叛乱部队准备从中苏门答腊的巴东和武吉丁宜逃到北苏门答腊打板奴里的南部去。
    新华社雅加达17日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚总理朱安达今天否认北苏门答腊首府棉兰的浮罗岭飞机场已经被非政府军队所占领。
    西方通讯社在16日引述了由叛乱分子掌握的巴东电台散布的这一消息。
    新华社雅加达17日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚政府军队已经在3月14日占领了中苏门答腊的重要石油产地硕顶—斯里—英德拉普拉地区，并且肃清了当地的叛乱分子。
    陆军发言人皮恩加迪中校15日说，政府军队占领了硕顶—斯里—英德拉普拉地区以后，就完全控制了北干巴鲁和中苏门答腊东海岸之间的后勤供应线。硕顶—斯里—英德拉普拉地区位于硕顶河畔。
    据安塔拉通讯社从其他方面获悉，政府军队占领了北干巴鲁、杜迈、伦拜、素涯帕宁、望加丽、硕顶—斯里—英德拉普拉等地以后，将使当地的德士古石油公司能够很快恢复正常的经营。
    同时，来自中苏门答腊的消息说，巴东的叛乱集团目前十分缺乏汽油。这是政府军队实际上收复了中苏门答腊的整个油区的结果。
    印度尼西亚苏门答腊东部港口外的望加丽岛上的一条街。以侯赛尼和沙弗鲁丁为首的印度尼西亚叛乱分子曾占据该岛。现已为政府军所收复。　（新华社）
