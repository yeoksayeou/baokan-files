Title: Another New Tractor Factory
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-14
Page: 3

Another New Tractor Factory

    This newspaper reports that in order to vigorously meet the needs of agricultural development in agricultural machinery, the state-owned Nanchang Diesel Engine Factory will be expanded into a modern tractor manufacturing factory with an annual output of 50,000 tractors. The entire capital construction project is expected to be completed in 1959.

    This year, the factory will have new workshops for engines, cast chassis, and other components built and put into production. Hundreds of new machine tool equipment will also be completely installed. By next year, more than five large workshops, including stamping dies, gearboxes, and piston rings, will also be built and put into production successively.

    This year, in addition to continuing to produce a large number of diesel engines, the factory will also produce 360 tractors. It is expected to increase to 5,000 units next year, and gradually expand to an annual output of 50,000 units after 1962.

### 又一个新拖拉机厂

1958-03-14
第3版()
专栏：

    又一个新拖拉机厂
    本报讯  为了在农业机械上大力满足农业发展的需要，国营南昌柴油机厂将扩建成为一座年产五万台拖拉机的现代化拖拉机制造厂。预计全部基建工程将于1959年完成。
    今年，这个厂将有新型的发动机、铸造底盘等车间建成并投入生产。还将有数百台新式机床设备，全部安装完毕。到明年，冲模、变速箱、活塞环等五个以上的大型车间，也将相继建成并投入生产。
    这个厂今年除继续生产大批柴油机外，同时将生产三百六十台拖拉机，到明年预计可增加到五千台，到1962年后将逐渐扩展到年产五万台。
