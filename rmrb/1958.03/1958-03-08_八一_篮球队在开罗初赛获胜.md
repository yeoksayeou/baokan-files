Title: "August 1st" Basketball Team Wins Preliminary Round in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 7th. The Chinese "August 1st" Basketball Team, currently visiting Egypt, played its first match against the United Arab Republic team in Cairo on the 6th, winning with a score of 78 to 57. The home team was composed of Egyptian and Syrian players, including seven Egyptian players who visited China last year after participating in the Moscow World Youth Festival.

### “八一”篮球队在开罗初赛获胜

1958-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    “八一”篮球队在开罗初赛获胜
    据新华社开罗7日电　现在埃及访问的中国“八一”篮球队6日在开罗同阿拉伯联合共和国队举行第一次比赛，结果以七十八对五十七获胜。主队是由埃及队员和叙利亚队员组成的，其中包括去年在参加莫斯科世界青年联欢节以后到中国访问过的七名埃及队员。
