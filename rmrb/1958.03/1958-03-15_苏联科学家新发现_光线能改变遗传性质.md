Title: Soviet Scientists' New Discovery: Light Can Change Hereditary Properties
Author:
Date: 1958-03-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 12th (TASS): Soviet scientists have for the first time successfully used light to create recombination within and between chromosomes inside the cell nucleus, giving living cells new hereditary properties, according to Dubinin, Corresponding Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and one of the founders of Soviet radiation genetics, in an interview with a TASS reporter.

Professor Dubinin pointed out that, in addition to the cell's own vital activities, the Earth's natural radiation and cosmic rays, and temperature variations, ordinary visible light is also a fundamental factor causing genetic changes. These factors combined constitute the genetic and evolutionary basis of the natural selection process discovered by Darwin.

Dubinin emphasized that the fact that visible light can cause the same chromosomal changes as radioactive radiation is of inestimable significance. It has been confirmed that the energy carried by photons can activate photosensitive substances, which in turn stimulate the appearance of strong oxidants (oxygen-containing chemical groups) in the cell. Under the action of these oxidants, chromosomes split into several parts, which then combine in various ways, giving living cells new hereditary properties. Professor Dubinin believes that there are now sufficient grounds to believe that, although the energy of photons in visible light is very small, only two or three electron volts, which is only one-thousandth of the particle energy of radioactive radiation, photons can still cause the same chromosomal changes as radioactive radiation.

### 苏联科学家新发现  光线能改变遗传性质

1958-03-15
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联科学家新发现
    光线能改变遗传性质
    据新华社14日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科12日讯：苏联科学家第一次成功地用光来造成了细胞核内的染色体内部和染色体相互间的重新组合，使活细胞具有了新的遗传性质，这是苏联辐射遗传学创始人之一、苏联科学院通讯院士杜比宁对塔斯社记者说的。
    杜比宁教授指出，造成遗传改变的基本因素，除细胞本身生机活动结果、地壳的天然辐射和宇宙线、温度的变动三点以外，普通的可见光线也是一项基本因素。这些因素加在一起就构成了达尔文所发现的自然选择过程的遗传进化学基础。
    杜比宁强调指出，可见光线能够造成放射性辐射所能造成的染色体变化的现象，具有难以估计的意义。已经证实，光子所带的能可以使感光物质活跃，感光物质转过来刺激细胞中强烈的氧化剂（含氧的化学物质群）的出现。染色体在这些氧化剂的作用下，分裂成若干部分，然后，这些部分再按各种各样方式结合起来，就使活细胞具有了新的遗传性质。杜比宁教授认为，现在可以有充分根据认为，尽管可见光线中的光子的能很小，总共只有二、三电子伏特，只有放射性辐射所具有的粒子能的千分之一，但是放射性辐射所能造成的染色体变化，光子也能造成。
