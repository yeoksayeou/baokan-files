Title: Indian National Congress Parliamentary Committee Studies Internal Discord

Author: 
Date: 1958-03-31
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 29th – According to a report in the "Times of India" on the 29th, the Congress Parliamentary Committee held a meeting here to "study a series of internal crises across the country."
    According to the newspaper, the escalating internal strife within the Congress parliamentary parties in Mysore, Andhra Pradesh, Orissa, West Bengal, Uttar Pradesh, and Punjab has become a matter of great concern for Congress leaders and may be discussed at the Congress Parliamentary Committee meeting. The meeting will also discuss the setbacks suffered by the Congress party in the recent elections for the Delhi municipal institutions. Out of the eighty seats in the Delhi municipal institutions, the Congress party only won thirty-one seats.

### 印国大党议会委员会研究党内不和问题

1958-03-31
第5版()
专栏：

    印国大党议会委员会
    研究党内不和问题
    据新华社新德里29日电　据“印度时报”29日报道，国大党议会委员会在这里举行会议，“研究全国各地一连串的党内危机”问题。
    据报纸报道，迈索尔邦、安得拉邦、奥里萨邦、西孟加拉、北方邦和旁遮普各地的国大党议会党团内部日益严重的倾轧不和已经成为国大党领袖们非常关心的事情，可能在国大党议会委员会会议中加以讨论。会议还将讨论国大党在德里市政机构最近的选举中遭受挫折的问题。在德里市政机构的八十个席位中，国大党只得到了三十一席。
