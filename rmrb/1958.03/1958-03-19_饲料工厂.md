Construction Blossoms

    Feed Factory
    Hengyang City is building a factory to produce pig feed.
    The raw materials used are local wild plants and chicken and duck eggshells, etc. After these things are ground into fine powder, they can be formulated into more than 20 kinds of mixed feed. Scientific tests have proven that these feeds contain relatively rich protein, fat, carbohydrates, and vitamins, and the pigs love to eat them. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 饲料工厂

1958-03-19
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    饲料工厂
    衡阳市兴建一座生产猪饲料的工厂。
    它所用的原料都是本地的野生植物和鸡、鸭蛋壳等，把这些东西碾成细末以后可以配制成二十多种混合饲料。经过科学检验证明，这些饲料中含有比较丰富的蛋白质、脂肪、碳水化合物和维生素等养份，而且猪很喜欢吃。　（据新华社讯）
