Title: Cooling Temperatures and Strong Winds
Author:
Date: 1958-03-27
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 26th, 8 PM: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a cooling temperature and strong wind forecast:

　　It is expected that on the morning of the 27th, the minimum temperatures in Hebei, Shandong, northern Henan, Shanxi, and northern Shaanxi will drop below 0 degrees Celsius, with frost. The Huai River basin will drop to minus 3 degrees Celsius, with frost. The middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River provinces will drop to between 3 and 8 degrees Celsius. On the morning of the 28th, the minimum temperatures in the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River provinces will continue to drop by about 2 degrees Celsius, and the minimum temperatures in Guangdong and Guangxi will drop to about 10 degrees Celsius.

　　It is expected that in the next 24 hours, there will still be northerly winds of around force 6 in the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River provinces, and northerly winds of force 6 to 8 in the East China Sea and the Taiwan Strait. It is expected that from the morning of the 27th, there will be northerly winds of force 5 to 6 in Guangdong and Guangxi. It is expected that from noon on the 27th, the wind force in the northern part of the South China Sea will gradually increase to force 6 to 7, with a northerly direction.

### 降温和大风

1958-03-27
第7版()
专栏：气象台

    降温和大风
  新华社讯　26日下午八时中央气象科学研究所发布降温和大风预报：
    预计27日早晨河北、山东、河南北部、山西、陕西北部的最低气温将下降到摄氏零度以下，并有冰冻。淮河流域将下降到摄氏零下三度，并有霜冻。长江流域中下游各省将下降到摄氏三度到八度。28日早晨长江流域中下游各省最低气温将继续下降摄氏二度左右，广东、广西的最低气温将下降到摄氏十度左右。
    预计未来二十四小时内，长江流域中下游各省仍有六级左右的偏北风，东海、台湾海峡将有六到八级的偏北风。预计27日早晨起，广东、广西将有五、六级的偏北风。预计27日中午起，南海北部的风力也将逐渐加强到六、七级，风向偏北。
