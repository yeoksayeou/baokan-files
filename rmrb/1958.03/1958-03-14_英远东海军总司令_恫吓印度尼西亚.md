Title: British Far East Naval Commander Threatens Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1958-03-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, March 13th: British Far East Naval Commander Gladstone stated in Sydney on March 12th, in a threatening manner, that the joint fleet of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization countries is capable of dealing with "any possible enemy" in the Southeast Asian waters.

At a press conference, he announced that an aircraft carrier will soon be permanently stationed in Singapore, so that his forces can be strengthened; and after that, another aircraft carrier called an "assault aircraft carrier" will also be stationed in Singapore.

Gladstone said that this aircraft carrier carries helicopters and "assault troops."

### 英远东海军总司令  恫吓印度尼西亚

1958-03-14
第5版()
专栏：

    英远东海军总司令
    恫吓印度尼西亚
    新华社13日讯  悉尼消息：英国远东海军总司令格拉德斯通3月12日在悉尼发表恫吓性谈话说，东南亚条约组织国家的联合舰队能够对付东南亚海面上的“任何可能的敌人”。
    他在记者招待会上宣布，不久将有一艘航空母舰常驻新加坡，这样他的部队可以加强；而在这以后，另外一艘称为“突击航空母舰”的航空母舰也将驻扎在新加坡。
    格拉德斯通说，这艘航空母舰上载有直升飞机；还有“突击部队”。
