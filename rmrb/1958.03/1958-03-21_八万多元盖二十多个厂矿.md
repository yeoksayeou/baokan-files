Title: Over 20 Factories and Mines Built for Over 80,000 Yuan
Author: 
Date: 1958-03-21
Page: 3

    This newspaper reports: The Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Prefecture in Yunnan Province has built 22 small factories and mines, including gold mines, mica mines, coal plants, iron plants, printing plants, soap factories, and sugar factories, with a total investment of only 86,500 yuan. Most of the newly built factories and mines will be put into production by the end of April this year. The total output value this year is expected to reach 700,150 yuan, an increase of 410% over last year's total output value.

### 八万多元盖二十多个厂矿

1958-03-21
第3版()
专栏：

    八万多元盖二十多个厂矿
    本报讯　云南省怒江傈僳族自治州兴建金矿、云母矿、煤厂、铁厂、印刷厂、肥皂厂、糖厂等小型厂矿二十二个，总投资仅八万六千五百元。今年4月底，大部分新建厂矿将投入生产。今年的总产值，预计可达到七十万零一千五百元，比去年总产值增长410％。
