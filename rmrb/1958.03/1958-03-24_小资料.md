Small Data
    
    China's main flue-cured tobacco producing areas include: Henan, Shandong, Anhui, Guizhou, Yunnan, Liaoning, Jilin and other provinces, among which Henan and Shandong have the highest output, and Yunnan, Shandong, and Henan provinces produce the best quality.
    
    At present, China's total tobacco leaf output ranks second in the world. China exports about 800,000 to 1,000,000 dan of tobacco leaves every year. Exporting 200,000 dan can be exchanged for a complete set of equipment for a seamless steel pipe plant like the Anshan Iron and Steel Plant.

### 小资料

1958-03-24
第2版()
专栏：小资料

    小资料
    我国主要烤烟产区有：河南、山东、安徽、贵州、云南、辽宁、吉林等省，其中河南、山东产量最高，云南、山东、河南等省所产的品质最好。
    目前我国烟叶总产量居世界第二位。我国每年出口烟叶大约八十万担到一百万担。出口二十万担就可以换回一个像鞍山钢铁厂的无缝钢管厂的全套设备。
