　　New Products
　　Pocket Phonograph
　　A portable, lightweight pocket phonograph, about the size of a large diary, has been manufactured by the Shanghai Hardware United Cooperative. When not in use, this phonograph can be disassembled and packed into a case. Each time the spring is fully wound, this phonograph can play both sides of a 25 cm record.
　　　　(Tang Hongxiang)
　　Travel Gramophone
　　The Shanghai No. 1 Hardware Tool Factory has successfully trial-produced the "Red Flag" brand travel gramophone and has already put it into production.
　　The style of this gramophone is novel and beautiful, and all parts are manufactured with domestic raw materials.
　　The gramophone is very small, only one-eighth the size of a regular gramophone, weighs less than three kilograms, and is easy to carry and use. Each one sells for about 30 yuan.
　　　　(Lu Qiang)

### 新产品

1958-03-14
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    新产品
    袖珍唱机
    一种携带方便、式样轻巧、体积相当于一本大日记本子的袖珍唱机已由上海五金联社制成。这种唱机在不使用的时候可以拆卸装成一合。这种唱机每摇足一次发条可唱二十五公分唱片二面。
    （唐鸿祥）
    旅行留声机
    上海第一五金工具制造厂试制红旗牌旅行留声机成功，并且已经投入生产。
    这种留声机的式样新颖、美观，所有零件都是国产原料制造。
    留声机的体积很小，只等于一般留声机的八分之一，全部重量不到三公斤，携带方便，使用简单。每只售价三十元左右。
              （鲁强）
