Title: Indonesian National Council Supports Government's Nation-Saving Measures
Author:
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 7th: The Indonesian National Council, in a press release issued on the 6th, stated its agreement with the measures taken by the Juanda government to address the current situation in Indonesia.

The press release stated that the central government's approach to dealing with the current situation is correct. The press release believes that all difficulties can be overcome in a short period.

The press release also stated that the government is determined to adopt a firm and resolute approach to resolve the current difficulties. The entire Indonesian people stand on the side of the government, helping the government and the armed forces to take nation-saving steps.

The Indonesian National Council is an institution established according to President Sukarno's plan, with representatives from all sectors of society. Under the chairmanship of President Sukarno, it held its first meeting of the year from March 3rd to 5th to discuss the current situation.

### 印度尼西亚民族委员会  支持政府救国步骤

1958-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚民族委员会
    支持政府救国步骤
    新华社雅加达7日电　印度尼西亚民族委员会在6日发表的新闻公报中表示同意朱安达政府对印度尼西亚目前局势所采取的措施。
    公报说，中央政府所采取的对付目前局势的方针是正确的。公报认为，各种困难都可以在短时期内克服。
    公报还说，政府已经决心采取坚定的、斩钉截铁的方针来解决目前的困难。印度尼西亚全国人民都站在政府这一边，帮助政府和武装部队采取救国的步骤。
    印度尼西亚民族委员会是按照苏加诺总统方案成立的、有社会各界人士代表参加的机构。它在苏加诺总统的主持下，从3月3日到5日举行了今年的第一次会议，讨论了目前的局势。
