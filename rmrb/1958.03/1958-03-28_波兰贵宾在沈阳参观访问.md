Title: Polish Guests Visit and Tour Shenyang

Author:

Date: 1958-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, March 27th. The government delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, headed by Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz, visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall in Shenyang this afternoon.

　　Accompanied by Xu Zhi, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, the distinguished guests visited the main industrial products in the exhibition hall. The guests were very interested in the rich content of the exhibition hall and inquired in detail about the performance, specifications, and production development of various products. The person in charge of the exhibition hall also presented the distinguished guests with commemorative albums and badges.

　　Afterwards, the distinguished guests took a car to visit the industrial area and the city of Shenyang.

　　This morning, the distinguished guests also visited two factories in Shenyang.

　　In the evening, the distinguished guests watched wonderful programs such as "Golden Leopard" performed by the Shenyang Peking Opera Troupe at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Palace.

### 波兰贵宾在沈阳参观访问

1958-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰贵宾在沈阳参观访问
    新华社沈阳27日电　以雅罗谢维奇副主席为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团，今天下午在沈阳参观了东北工业陈列馆。
    贵宾们由沈阳市副市长徐志陪同参观了陈列馆里的各种工业的主要产品。贵宾们对陈列馆里的丰富内容很感兴趣，详细地询问了各种产品的性能、规格以及生产发展情况。陈列馆负责人还向贵宾们赠送了纪念画册和纪念章。
    随后，贵宾们驱车参观了沈阳市工业区和市容。
    今天上午，贵宾们还参观了沈阳市的两个工厂。
    晚间，贵宾们在中苏友谊宫观看了沈阳市京剧团演出的“金钱豹”等精采节目。
