Title: Ten-Thousand Mu Afforestation Township
Author:
Date: 1958-03-22
Page: 2

    Ten-Thousand Mu Afforestation Township
    (Our dispatch) The Qiaotang, Dingcheng, and Mingxin agricultural cooperatives in Qiaotang Township, Nan'an County, Fujian Province, mobilized their members to launch a crash afforestation campaign. In twenty-eight days, they planted more than 8.4 million trees, greening 14,370 mu of barren mountains, creating a model for large-scale afforestation by agricultural cooperatives and becoming the first "Ten-Thousand Mu Afforestation Township" in Fujian Province this year.

### 万亩林乡

1958-03-22
第2版()
专栏：

    万亩林乡
    本报讯　福建南安县谯塘乡的谯塘、鼎诚、明新等三个农业社，发动社员开展突击造林运动，二十八天种树八百四十多万株，绿化荒山一万四千三百七十亩，创造了农业社大面积造林的范例，成为福建省今年的第一个“万亩林乡”。
