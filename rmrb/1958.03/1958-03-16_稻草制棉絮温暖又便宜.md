Title: Straw-made Cotton Wadding: Warm and Cheap

Author:
Date: 1958-03-16
Page: 1

The Third Cotton Production Cooperative of Xi'an City has successfully trial-produced artificial cotton using straw. This artificial cotton has the same whiteness as natural cotton and can be used for spinning, weaving, and making cotton wadding. The cost of this artificial cotton is only one-third that of natural cotton. This year, the cooperative plans to produce more than 500,000 jin of artificial cotton.

(Attached picture)
   The quilt shown below is made of 60% artificial cotton and 40% natural cotton.
    Yang Tingliang (Xinhua News Agency)

### 稻草制棉絮温暖又便宜

1958-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    稻草制棉絮温暖又便宜
    西安市第三熟棉生产合作社用稻草试制人造棉成功。这种人造棉和天然棉的白度一样，可以纺纱、织布、做棉絮。这种人造棉的成本只等于天然棉的三分之一。今年，这个社计划生产五十多万斤人造棉。
    （附图片）
   下面这件被絮就是用60％的人造棉和40％的天然棉制成的。
    杨廷良摄（新华社稿）
