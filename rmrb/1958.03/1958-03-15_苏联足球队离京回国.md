Title: Soviet Football Team Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1958-03-15
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th — The Soviet national football team left Beijing by plane on the 14th to return home. During their thirty-eight-day visit to our country, they held ten friendly matches with eight of our first-division football teams, achieving a record of eight wins and two draws.

### 苏联足球队离京回国

1958-03-15
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联足球队离京回国
    据新华社1 4日讯　苏联国家足球队14日乘飞机离北京回国。他们在访问我国的三十八天中，同我国八个甲级足球队举行十场友谊比赛，取得了八胜二平的战绩。
