Title: Attending the "Support Algeria Day" Activities: Representatives of the Algerian National Liberation Front Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th. Brahim Ghafa, a representative of the Algerian National Liberation Front, invited to China to participate in the "Support Algeria Day" activities on March 30th, arrived in Beijing by plane today. Those who went to the airport to welcome him included responsible persons from the Chinese Asian Solidarity Committee, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the China Islamic Association, and the Sino-Algerian Friendship Association.

    Adeef Daniel, a representative of the Arab People's Congress who came to China to visit and participate in the "Support Algeria Day" activities, arrived in Beijing on the 23rd.

### 参加“声援阿尔及利亚日”活动  阿民族解放阵线代表到京

1958-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    参加“声援阿尔及利亚日”活动
    阿民族解放阵线代表到京
    新华社28日讯　应邀来我国参加3月30日“声援阿尔及利亚日”活动的阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表卜拉欣·加法已经在今天乘飞机到达北京。前往机场迎接的有中国亚洲团结委员会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国伊斯兰教协会和中阿友好协会的负责人。
    前来我国访问和参加“声援阿尔及利亚日”活动的阿拉伯人民大会的代表阿德夫·达尼尔，已经在23日到达北京。
