Title: Southern Jiangxi Advances Spring Plowing

Author:

Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 2

Southern Jiangxi Advances Spring Plowing

(Our dispatch) This year's spring plowing in the southern Jiangxi region of Jiangxi Province has started two solar terms earlier than in previous years. Currently, millions of farmers throughout the region have been mobilized into spring plowing production, with an attendance rate of over 90%.

The quality of spring plowing production in southern Jiangxi this year is also better than in previous years.

### 赣南提早春耕

1958-03-25
第2版()
专栏：

    赣南提早春耕
    本报讯　江西省赣南区今年的春耕比往年提早了两个节气，目前全区已有数百万农民卷入春耕生产，出勤率高达90％以上。
    赣南区今年春耕生产的质量也比往年好。
