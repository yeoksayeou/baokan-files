Title: Creating a World Record for Rocket Altitude: Soviet Union Launches Rocket to 1,000 Li High

Author:
Date: 1958-03-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 27th: The Soviet Committee for the International Geophysical Year announced that the Soviet Union successfully launched a single-stage rocket with 1,520 kilograms of equipment to an altitude of 473 kilometers (equivalent to 946 li), setting a world record for the altitude reached by a single-stage rocket.

This geophysical rocket was launched on February 21, 1958, at 11:42 AM Moscow time in the mid-latitude region of the European part of the Soviet Union.

By utilizing special devices, the rocket remained stable throughout its entire flight, including the period of inertial flight, without rotating around the vertical or horizontal axis. This greatly increased the accuracy and value of the scientific investigations being conducted. The rocket flew in a predetermined direction at a small angle to the vertical and accurately landed in the designated area.

The rocket was equipped with the following geophysical instruments: 1. An ultra-shortwave scattering radio interferometer to measure the concentration of free electrons in the ionosphere; 2. Instruments to measure the ionic composition of the atmosphere; 3. Instruments to study the concentration of positive ions in the atmosphere; 4. Instruments to measure electron temperature; 5. Ionization and magnetic pressure gauges to measure air pressure; 6. Instruments to record micrometeorite particle impacts; 7. A solar spectrograph to record ultraviolet spectral ranges.

Preliminary examination of the obtained materials indicates that the instruments functioned well during the flight.

As a result of this rocket launch, the distribution of free electron concentration in the ionosphere up to an altitude of 473 kilometers was obtained for the first time, and the distribution of air pressure up to an altitude of 260 kilometers, as well as the impact of the rocket with micrometeorites, were recorded. The obtained materials are being processed.

### 创火箭上升高度世界纪录  苏联发射火箭到千里高空

1958-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    创火箭上升高度世界纪录
    苏联发射火箭到千里高空
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联国际地球物理年委员会宣布，苏联成功地把一个带有一千五百二十公斤重的装备的一级火箭发射到了四百七十三公里（合九百四十六华里）的高空，创造了一级火箭上升高度的世界纪录。
    这个地球物理火箭，是1958年2月21日莫斯科时间十一点四十二分在苏联欧洲部分中纬地区发射的。
    由于利用了特殊装置，火箭在整个飞行期间，包括靠惯性飞行的期间，都是稳定的，没有发生围绕垂直轴或水平轴自转的现象。这种情形大大增加了所进行的科学考察的准确性和价值。火箭按着同垂直线成不大的角度的预定方向飞行，以后准确地落在预定的场地上。
    火箭上装有下列地球物理仪器：一、测量电离层自由电子浓度的超短波散射无线电干涉仪；二、测量大气电离成分的仪器；三、研究大气中正离子浓度的仪器；四、测量电子温度的仪器；五、测量空气压力的电离和磁力压力计；六、纪录微陨星粒子撞击的仪器；七、纪录紫外线光谱段的太阳摄谱仪。
    对所获材料初步检查表明，仪器在飞行期间工作情况良好。
    这次火箭发射结果，第一次获得了电离层中高达四百七十三公里的高空中自由电子浓度分布的情况，纪录了高达二百六十公里高空空气压力分布情况和火箭同微陨星撞击的情况等。获得的材料正在整理中。
