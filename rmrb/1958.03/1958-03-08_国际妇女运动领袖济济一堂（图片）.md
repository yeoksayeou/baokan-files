Title: International Women's Movement Leaders Gather Together (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 5

International Women's Movement Leaders Gather Together (Oil Painting)
    Sitting from right to left are Mrs. Eugénie Cotton (France), President of the Women's International Democratic Federation (WIDF), and Vice President Vaillant-Couturier (France). Standing are N. Popova (USSR), Vice President of the WIDF, Maria Rosi, President of the Italian Women's Union, Ran Gutti (Nigeria), Vice President of the WIDF, Soong Ching Ling (China), Ibarruri (Spain), P. Bose (India).
