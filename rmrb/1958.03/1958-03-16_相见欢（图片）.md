Title: Meeting Joy (Picture)
Author: Gao Liang, Deng Tuo
Date: 1958-03-16
Page: 4

    Meeting Joy
    Deng Tuo
    Two hearts, one stream connects,
    Intention so strong,
    Suddenly remember Xi'er's plight, endless hate.
    The White Haired Girl,
    Turning over dance,
    Celebrating reunion,
    Wish human world be drunk in joy with the east wind.
    Wang Kun (right) and Matsuyama Kiyoko (left), two actresses from China and Japan who played Xi'er in "The White Haired Girl," embrace affectionately.　Our reporter Gao Liang photographed.

    相见欢
    邓拓

    忽忆喜儿遭际恨无穷。

    但愿人间欢乐醉东风。
