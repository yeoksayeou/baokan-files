Title: Gheorghe Stoica Says at Hanoi Welcome Rally Romania Supports Vietnam's Struggle for Unification and Accuses Imperialism of Interfering in Eastern Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-03-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 24th - A grand civic welcome rally was held this afternoon in Hanoi's People's Theater to welcome the Romanian government delegation headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Gheorghe Stoica. Seven thousand people from all walks of life attending the rally enthusiastically cheered, "Long live the unbreakable unity and friendship between the socialist camp countries headed by the Soviet Union!"

　　Amid the cheers, Gheorghe Stoica delivered a speech, stating that the Romanian government and people fully support the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's steadfast efforts in the peaceful reunification of the motherland. He said that one day, all Vietnamese people will live in a unified, peaceful, independent, and democratic motherland family.

　　Gheorghe Stoica accused imperialism of interfering in some Eastern countries and expressed support for all initiatives conducive to banning atomic warfare and defending peace. He said that the Romanian government and people fully support the Indonesian people's struggle to eradicate the rebellion instigated by foreign forces. He said that today, the era of imperialism lording it over colonies has come to an end, and the colonial system must disintegrate.

　　Gheorghe Stoica expressed firm support for convening a meeting of leaders of Eastern and Western governments in the shortest possible time. He said that if an Asian collective security pact could replace the Southeast Asia aggressive bloc, it would make a significant contribution to the tranquility of Asian nations and world peace.

　　Gheorghe Stoica emphasized the necessity of the great unity of the socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union. He said that this is the guarantee of the progress of socialist countries and the strong pillar of world peace. He pointed out that no matter how imperialism carries out sabotage, the unity of the socialist camp countries is indivisible.

　　He finally shouted in Vietnamese: "Long live unified Vietnam!" He then firmly shook the hand of Prime Minister Pham Van Dong and raised his hand high, at which point the entire venue erupted in applause. Prior to this, Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Hanoi Administrative Committee, delivered a welcome speech at the rally. He thanked the Romanian people for their assistance to the Vietnamese people.

　　On the morning of the 24th, government leaders of Vietnam and Romania began formal talks in Hanoi to exchange views on important current international issues and on the current situation in Vietnam and Romania and issues of friendly cooperation between the two countries.

　　On the Vietnamese side, those participating in the talks included Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister Phan Ke Toai, Minister of the Prime Minister's Office Pham Hung, Minister of Culture Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Education Nguyen Van Huyen, Minister of Justice Vu Dinh Hoe, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ung Van Khiem, and Deputy Minister of Commerce Dang Viet Chau.

　　On the Romanian side were Chairman of the Council of Ministers Gheorghe Stoica, Vice Chairman Emil Bodnaras, Minister of Foreign Affairs Avram Bunaciu, and Romanian Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Teodor Rudenco.

### 基伏·斯托伊卡在河内欢迎大会上说  罗马尼亚支持越南争取统一的斗争  并指责帝国主义对东方国家的干涉

1958-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

    基伏·斯托伊卡在河内欢迎大会上说
    罗马尼亚支持越南争取统一的斗争
    并指责帝国主义对东方国家的干涉
    新华社河内24日电　河内今天下午在人民剧场举行盛大的市民欢迎大会，欢迎以部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡为首的罗马尼亚政府代表团。到会的各阶层人士七千人热烈欢呼“以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国之间牢不可破的团结友谊万岁！”
    基伏·斯托伊卡在欢呼声中发表讲话说，罗马尼亚政府和人民完全支持越南民主共和国在和平统一祖国方面所作的坚毅的努力。他说，终有一天，全体越南人民将会生活在一个统一、和平、独立和民主的祖国大家庭里。
    基伏·斯托伊卡指责帝国主义对东方一些国家所施加的干涉，并且表示拥护有利于禁止原子战争和保卫和平的一切倡议。他说，罗马尼亚政府和人民完全支持印度尼西亚人民为肃清由外国势力所唆使的叛乱而进行的斗争。他说，今天，帝国主义在殖民地作威作福的时代已告终了，殖民体系一定要瓦解。
    基伏·斯托伊卡表示坚决拥护在最短期间召开东西方政府领导人的会议。他说，如果能够以亚洲集体安全公约来代替东南亚侵略集团，这将对亚洲各民族的安宁和世界和平作出重大的贡献。
    基伏·斯托伊卡强调指出以苏联为首的社会主义各国大团结的必要性。他说，这是社会主义各国进步的保证和世界和平的坚强支柱。他指出，不管帝国主义怎样进行破坏，社会主义阵营各国的团结是不可分裂的。
    他最后用越南语高呼：“越南统一万岁！”他接着紧紧握住范文同总理的手，并且把手高高地举起来，这时全场掌声雷动。在这以前，河内行政委员会主席陈维兴在会上致了欢迎词。他感谢罗马尼亚人民对越南人民的援助。
    24日上午，越南和罗马尼亚的政府领导人在河内开始举行正式的会谈，就目前国际重要问题和有关越罗两国当前局势和两国之间的友好合作问题交换了意见。
    越南方面参加会谈的有总理范文同、副总理潘继遂、总理府部长范雄、文化部长黄明鉴、教育部长阮文煊、司法部长武廷槐、外交部副部长雍文谦和商业部副部长邓曰州。
    罗马尼亚方面有部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡、副主席埃米尔·波德纳拉希、外交部长阿夫拉姆·布纳丘和罗马尼亚驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使特奥多·鲁登科。
