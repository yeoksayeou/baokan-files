Title: State Council Appointment and Dismissal List
Author:
Date: 1958-03-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 26th: The 73rd plenary meeting of the State Council held on March 17th, approved the appointment and dismissal of a number of personnel from state organs and universities. The list is as follows:
    Appointed Ding Xilin, Ding Hua, Bao Erhan, Liu Geping, Wu Yunfu, Yang Hansheng, Da Pusheng, He Chengxiang, Xiao San, Wu Lengxi, Chen Zhongjing, Zhou Jianren, Zheng Zhenduo, Qu Wu, Rong Gaotang, Yuan Chaojun, Xia Yan, Cui Yitian, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Zhixiang, Zhang Hanfu, Mei Yi, Feng Jiping, Zou Dapeng, Chu Tunan, Xie Xinhe, Wei Chuantong as members of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations;
    Li Sichi as President of Chengdu College of Traditional Chinese Medicine, Zhang Hua as Vice President.
    Removed Shen Bochun from the post of Deputy Director of the Bureau of Government Offices Administration of the State Council; Su Li from the post of Vice President of Sichuan Normal College.

### 国务院任免名单

1958-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社26日讯　国务院在3月17日举行的第七十三次全体会议，通过任免一批国家机关和高等学校工作人员，名单如下：
    任命丁西林、丁华、包尔汉、刘格平、伍云甫、阳翰笙、达浦生、何成湘、萧　三、吴冷西、陈忠经、周建人、郑振铎、屈　武、荣高棠、袁超俊、夏　衍、崔义田、张奚若、张致祥、章汉夫、梅　益、冯基平、邹大鹏、楚图南、谢鑫鹤、魏传统为对外文化联络委员会委员；
    李斯炽为成都中医学院院长，张华为副院长。
    免去申伯纯的国务院机关事务管理局副局长职务；苏藜的四川师范学院副院长职务。
