Title: Japanese Socialist Party Decides to Strive for the Realization of the Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-03-12
Page: 6

    Japanese Socialist Party Decides
    to Strive for the Realization of the Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 11th -- The Japanese Socialist Party decided on the 11th to use the full strength of the party to promote the implementation of the newly signed Sino-Japanese trade agreement.
    The Central Executive Committee of the Socialist Party held a meeting on the 11th and listened to a report on the negotiation of the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement from Seichi Katsumada and Einosuke Maeda, who participated in the signing. The Socialist Party subsequently made the above decision at the meeting.

### 日本社会党决定  力争中日贸易协定的实现

1958-03-12
第6版()
专栏：

    日本社会党决定
    力争中日贸易协定的实现
    新华社11日讯　东京消息：日本社会党11日决定以全党的力量来促使新签订的日中贸易协定付诸实施。
    社会党中央执行委员会在11日举行会议，听取了参加签订第四次日中贸易协定的胜间田清一和前田荣之助两人关于日中贸易协定谈判情况的报告。社会党随后在会上作出了上述决定。
