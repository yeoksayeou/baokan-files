Title: Zhou Xiaoyan and Others' Performances in Bulgaria Greeted with Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1958-03-12
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 8th -- China's famous singer Zhou Xiaoyan and pianist Wu Leyi have given five performances in the Bulgarian cities of Tyrnovo, Gabrovo, Vratsa, Sofia, and Varna, receiving enthusiastic praise from the audience.
    Zhou Xiaoyan and Wu Leyi have concluded their performance activities in Bulgaria and are now investigating music education in Bulgaria and exchanging experiences with Bulgarian musicians.

### 周小燕等在保演出  受到热烈欢迎

1958-03-12
第6版()
专栏：

    周小燕等在保演出
    受到热烈欢迎
    据新华社索非亚8日电　我国著名的歌唱家周小燕和钢琴家吴乐懿在保加利亚的第尔诺夫、加布罗沃、弗拉查、索非亚和瓦尔那等城市先后举行了五次演出，受到观众的热烈赞扬。
    周小燕和吴乐懿在保加利亚的演出活动已经结束，现在正在考察保加利亚的音乐教育，同保加利亚音乐界人士交流经验。
