Date: 1958-03-13

    "Yankee Go Home" Song

    Recently, when the youth of Yorkshire, England, protested against the US establishing a base in Elvington, they sang a "Yankee Go Home" march, the lyrics of which are:

    Go home Yankee, Yankee go home,
    We don't want you.
    The lifestyle you peddle doesn't suit our taste,
    Don't think we'll die for you.

    Everywhere in Elvington, near Yorkshire, people are talking
    About the Yankees building a bomber base there,
    But we must save humanity,
    Don't think we'll die for you!

    Chorus: Go home Yankee, Yankee go home...

    If we don't stop them, they will destroy our little land,
    Yes, they want to build a base in Elvington;
    Shouldn't we say: Yankee, get out of here right away,
    Go back to Washington, chew your gum, and stir up trouble.

    Chorus: Go home Yankee, Yankee go home...

    The day will come when we will finally be victorious,
    There will never be any more Yankee bombers;
    As long as you guarantee that you will no longer bring us war,
    We will be happy to say: "You are welcome anytime."

### “美国佬滚回去”之歌

1958-03-13
第5版()
专栏：

    “美国佬滚回去”之歌
    最近，英国约克郡的青年反对美国在爱尔温顿建立基地举行游行示威时，唱了一支“美国佬滚回去”的进行曲，歌词是：
    滚回去美国佬，美国佬滚回去，
    我们不要你。
    你们贩卖的生活方式不合我们胃口，
    别想我们会为你们去送死。
    在约克郡附近的爱尔温顿到处街谈巷议，
    说美国佬要在那儿搞轰炸基地，
    但是我们一定要拯救人类，
    别想我们会为你们去送死！
    合唱：滚回去美国佬，美国佬滚回去……
    如果我们不予制止，他们就会毁灭我们小小的土地，
    是的，他们要在爱尔温顿把基地建起；
    难道我们不该说：美国佬，你们马上滚回去，
    回到华盛顿去嚼你们的口香糖，惹事生非。
    合唱：滚回去美国佬，美国佬滚回去……
    那一天终会来到，我们最后一定会得到胜利，
    永远不会再有美国佬的轰炸机；
    只要你们保证不再给我们带来战争，
    我们就会高兴地说：“随时都欢迎你”。
