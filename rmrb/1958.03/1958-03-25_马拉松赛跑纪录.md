    Marathon Records
    Olympic Games highest record: Two hours, twenty-three minutes, three and two-tenths seconds;
    European Championship highest record: Two hours, twenty-four minutes, fifty-one and six-tenths seconds;
    China's highest record: Two hours, fifty minutes, fifty-five and eight-tenths seconds;
    China's marathon athlete standard: Two hours, forty-five minutes.

### 马拉松赛跑纪录

1958-03-25
第7版()
专栏：

    马拉松赛跑纪录
    奥林匹克运动会最高纪录：二时二十三分零三秒二；
    欧洲锦标赛最高纪录：二时二十四分五十一秒六；
    我国最高纪录：二时五十分五十五秒八；
    我国马拉松赛跑运动健将标准：二时四十五分。
