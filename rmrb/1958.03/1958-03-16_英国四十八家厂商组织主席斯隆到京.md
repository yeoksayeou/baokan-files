Title: Chairman Sloane of the British "48 Group of Traders" Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 15th. Sydney Gordon Sloane, Chairman of the British "48 Group of Traders", arrived in Beijing on the 14th at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

Ji Chao-ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, was present at the airport to welcome him, along with Roland Berger, advisor to the British "48 Group of Traders", who had arrived in Beijing earlier.

### 英国四十八家厂商组织主席斯隆到京

1958-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    英国四十八家厂商组织主席斯隆到京
    新华社15日讯　英国四十八家厂商组织主席悉尼·戈登·斯隆，应中国国际贸易促进委员会的邀请，在14日到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎；还有先期到达北京的英国四十八家厂商组织顾问罗兰·伯格。
