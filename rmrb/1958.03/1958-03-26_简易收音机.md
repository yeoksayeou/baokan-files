Title: Simple Radio

Author:

Date: 1958-03-26
Page: 3

New Products

    Simple Radio

    The Changchun City First Bureau of Industry has recently trial-produced three types of simple radios for rural use: crystal radios, single-tube radios, and double-tube radios. All three types of radios can receive broadcasts from the central and provincial radio stations.

    Some of these radios do not use electricity or vacuum tubes; others use very little electricity and only one or two vacuum tubes, so the cost is very low and the price is cheap. The cost of each crystal speaker radio is only 8.50 yuan; single-tube and double-tube radios only cost about 15 yuan and 25 yuan respectively.

              (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 简易收音机

1958-03-26
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    简易收音机
    长春市第一工业局最近试制成矿石、单管、双管等三种农村用的简易收音机。这三种收音机都可以收听中央和本省电台的广播。
    这些收音机有的不用电和真空管；有的用电量很少和只用一、二个真空管，因此成本很低，价格便宜。矿石喇叭收音机每个成本只需八元五角；单管、双管收音机也只需十五元、二十五元左右。
              （据新华社讯）
