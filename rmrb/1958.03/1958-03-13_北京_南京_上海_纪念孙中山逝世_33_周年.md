Title: Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai Commemorate the 33rd Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Death
Author:
Date: 1958-03-13
Page: 4

    Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai
    Commemorate the 33rd Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Death
    Xinhua News Agency, March 12th—Today is the 33rd anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's death. Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and Vice Chairmen He Xiangning, Zhang Zhizhong, and Xiong Kewu, as well as Zhu Yunshan, Chen Shaoxian, Shao Lizi, Weng Wenhao, and more than 20 other members of the Central Standing Committee, went to the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall in Biyun Temple, Fragrant Hills, in the suburbs of Beijing to pay their respects to Mr. Sun Yat-sen, and presented wreaths to Mr. Sun Yat-sen's portrait.
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, March 12th—More than 30 people from all walks of life in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City braved the rain this morning to visit the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum to commemorate the 33rd anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's death.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 12th—Heads of the Party, government, democratic parties, and people's organizations in Shanghai visited Sun Yat-sen's former residence on Xiangshan Road today to commemorate the 33rd anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's death.

### 北京　南京　上海  纪念孙中山逝世33周年

1958-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    北京　南京　上海
    纪念孙中山逝世33周年
    新华社12日讯　今天是孙中山先生逝世三十三周年纪念日。中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深，副主席何香凝、张治中、熊克武，中央常务委员朱蕴山、陈劭先、邵力子、翁文灏等二十多人，前往京郊香山碧云寺晋谒孙中山先生纪念堂，并向孙中山先生的遗像献花圈致敬。
    据新华社南京12日电　江苏省和南京市各界人士三十多人今天上午冒雨参谒中山陵，纪念孙中山先生逝世三十三周年。
    据新华社上海12日电　上海市党、政府和各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，今天到香山路瞻谒中山故居，纪念孙中山先生逝世三十三周年。
