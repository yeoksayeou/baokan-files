Title: Sudanese Foreign Minister States They Will Implement a Neutral Policy
Author:
Date: 1958-03-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 29th: Khartoum News: Sudanese Prime Minister Khalil announced the list of Sudan's new coalition cabinet on the 27th. The National Party holds ten ministerial positions in the new cabinet, including Defense, Foreign Affairs, and Finance, while the People's Democratic Party holds six ministerial positions, including Internal Affairs, Agriculture, and Commerce.

The new cabinet's Foreign Minister, Mahgoub, told reporters from the Middle East News Agency in Khartoum on the 29th that Sudan's foreign policy would be based on the following four points: non-alignment with the East or West; maintaining a neutral attitude among various Arab groups; accepting any unconditional aid from the East or West; and not participating in any foreign treaties unless for the defense of Sudan.

### 苏丹外长表示  将执行中立政策

1958-03-31
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹外长表示
    将执行中立政策
    据新华社开罗29日电　喀土穆消息：苏丹总理哈利勒27日宣布了苏丹新的联合内阁的名单。国民党在新内阁中占有国防、外交、财政等十个部长职位，人民民主党占有内政、农业和商业等六个部长职位。
    新内阁的外交部长马哈古卜29日在喀土穆对中东通讯社记者说，苏丹的外交政策将以下述四点为依据：不依附于东方或西方；在各个阿拉伯集团之间保持中立态度；接受来自东方或西方的任何不附带条件的援助；不参加任何外国条约，除非为了保卫苏丹。
