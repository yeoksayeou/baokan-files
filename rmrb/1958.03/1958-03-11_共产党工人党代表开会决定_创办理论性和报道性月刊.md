Title: Communist and Workers' Parties Representatives Meeting Decides to Establish Theoretical and Reporting Monthly Journal
Author:
Date: 1958-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 11th -- "Communique on the Publication of a Theoretical Journal of Communist and Workers' Parties," full text as follows:

    At the Moscow meeting in November 1957, representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties of various countries expressed the hope to publish an international journal. Based on this desire, from March 7th to 8th this year, representatives of some Communist and Workers' Parties held a meeting in Prague.

    The meeting unanimously decided that, after some time, a theoretical and reporting monthly journal would be launched in Prague.

    The representatives of the countries' parties founding the journal declared that all fraternal parties that did not participate in this meeting could participate in the founding of the journal on an equal basis, or participate in the publication of the journal in any way they deem appropriate.

### 共产党工人党代表开会决定  创办理论性和报道性月刊

1958-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    共产党工人党代表开会决定
    创办理论性和报道性月刊
    新华社布拉格11日电　“关于出版共产党和工人党的理论性刊物的公报”，全文如下：
    在1957年11月莫斯科会议上，各国共产党和工人党代表曾经表示希望出版一种国际性的刊物。根据这种愿望，今年3月7日到8日，一些共产党和工人党的代表在布拉格举行了会议。
    会议一致决定，经过若干时间以后，在布拉格开始出版一份理论性和报道性的月刊。
    创办刊物的各国党的代表声明，所有未参加这次会议的兄弟党可以在平等的基础上参加创办刊物，或者通过任何它们认为合适的方式参加刊物的出版工作。
