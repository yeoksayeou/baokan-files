Title: National Football League A and B Leagues to be Held at the End of March
Author:
Date: 1958-03-22
Page: 7
Sports News:

    National Football League A and B Leagues to be Held at the End of March

    The 1958 National Football League A and B Leagues are scheduled to begin on March 30th.

    The twelve League A football teams will hold the first stage of the first round robin in Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing and other places.

    This year's National Football League A League will still hold two round robin tournaments. Each round robin will be divided into three stages. The first round robin will last until June, and the second round robin will run from September to November. In each round robin, each team will play against the other eleven teams. The twelve teams will play a total of one hundred and thirty-two games throughout the year.

    This year's League B football league will be held in fourteen locations in eight competition zones. The number of teams participating in the competition has increased from thirty-one last year to sixty-seven. The competition will adopt a zonal or group single round robin system, divided into two stages. All games will be completed by the end of October. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国足球甲乙级联赛将在三月底举行

1958-03-22
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    全国足球甲乙级联赛将在三月底举行
    1958年全国足球甲级队联赛和乙级队联赛，定3月30日开始分别举行。
    十二个甲级足球队将分别在北京、天津、上海、南京等地举行第一次循环赛的第一阶段比赛。
    今年的全国足球甲级队联赛仍要举行两次循环赛，每次循环赛都分三个阶段进行，第一次循环赛将进行到6月，第二次循环赛将从9月进行到11月，在每次循环赛中，每个队都要同其他十一个队交锋。十二个队全年共将举行一百三十二场比赛。
    今年的乙级足球队联赛，分别在八个竞赛区的十四个地方举行。参加比赛的队数由去年的三十一个增加到六十七个。比赛将采用分区或分组单循环制的方法，分两个阶段进行，全部比赛将进行到10月下旬结束。　（据新华社讯）
