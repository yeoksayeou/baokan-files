    Brief News
    
    Sino-Yugoslav Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan Signed This Year
    Belgrade dispatch: The 1958 implementation plan for the Sino-Yugoslav Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed here on the morning of the 29th.
    According to the plan, China will send artists, sculptors, museum experts and cultural workers to Yugoslavia for visits this year. Yugoslavia will send university professors, art historians and artists to visit China. Yugoslavia will exhibit Chinese Dunhuang murals, and China will exhibit Yugoslavian art photographs.
    
    Soviet Party and Government Delegation to Visit Hungary
    According to TASS: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the Workers and Peasants Revolutionary Government, a Soviet Party and Government delegation will visit Hungary soon. The delegation will participate in the celebration of the 13th anniversary of Hungary's liberation.

    简讯
    中南文化合作协定今年执行计划签字
    贝尔格莱德讯：中国和南斯拉夫文化合作协定1958年执行计划29日上午在这里签字。
    根据计划，今年中国将派艺术家、雕塑家、博物馆专家和文化工作者到南斯拉夫访问。南斯拉夫将派大学教授、艺术史家和美术家到中国访问。南斯拉夫将展出中国敦煌壁画，中国将展出南斯拉夫的艺术照片。
    苏党政代表团将访匈
    据塔斯社讯：苏联党和政府代表团应匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会和工农革命政府的邀请，最近将访问匈牙利。代表团将参加匈牙利解放十三周年的庆祝活动。
