Title: Soviet Children Will Dress Better: CPC Central Committee and Council of Ministers Decide to Increase Production of Children's Clothing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 19th (TASS dispatch): Moscow announced the resolution of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union on increasing the production of children's clothing and shoes, expanding the variety of these commodities, and improving their quality. The resolution pointed out that Soviet industry has the potential to fully meet residents' consumption of children's clothing and shoes in the next two years, greatly expand the variety, and improve the quality of these commodities to meet residents' needs.

The CPC Central Committee and the Council of Ministers decided to produce at least 149.5 million pairs of children's leather shoes in 1958 (27.3% more than the actual output in 1957), 310 million pairs of children's socks, 130 million knitted shirts, and 55 million knitted outerwear. The children's clothing manufacturing industry will increase production by 1 billion rubles more than originally planned.

The resolution stipulates that the construction and reconstruction of 22 sewing enterprises, 18 knitting enterprises, and 34 leather shoe-making enterprises will be accelerated from 1958 to 1959, and the total investment in the construction and equipment of these industrial enterprises in 1958 will increase by 195 million rubles. The resolution also stipulates the implementation of many measures to improve the quality of children's clothing and expand its variety, including adding clothing cooperatives, sewing factories, and specialized children's stores.

### 苏联儿童将穿得更好  苏共中央和部长会议决议增产童装

1958-03-21
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联儿童将穿得更好
    苏共中央和部长会议决议增产童装
    新华社20日讯　塔斯社莫斯科19日讯：莫斯科公布了苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议关于增加童装和童鞋的生产、扩充这些商品的品种和改进它们的质量的决议。决议指出，苏联工业有可能在今后两年内使童装和童鞋的生产完全满足居民的消费，大大扩充品种和改进这些商品的质量以适应居民的需求。
    苏共中央和部长会议决定在1958年至少生产儿童皮鞋一亿四千九百五十万双（比1957年实际产量多27.3％），童袜三亿一千万双、针织衬衣一亿三千万件、针织外衣五千五百万件。童装制造业要比原定计划多增产十亿卢布的产品。
    决议规定在1958年到1959年加速建设和改建二十二个缝纫企业、十八个针织企业和三十四个制革制鞋企业，1958年对这些工业企业的建设装备的投资总额将增加一亿九千五百万卢布。决议还规定实行许多改进儿童服装质量和扩充品种的措施，其中包括增添服装社、缝纫厂和专业化的儿童商店。
