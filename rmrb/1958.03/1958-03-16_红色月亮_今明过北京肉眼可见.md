Title: Red Moon Visible to the Naked Eye in Beijing Today and Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1958-03-16
Page: 3

    Red Moon
    Visible to the Naked Eye in Beijing Today and Tomorrow
    Xinhua News Agency, March 15th: TASS reports: As of 6:00 AM Moscow time on March 15th, the Soviet Union's second artificial satellite had orbited the Earth 1,901 times.

    On the 16th, the satellite will pass over Changchun, Beijing, Shanghai, Urumqi, Lanzhou, and Chengdu.

    On the 17th, at 5:04 AM Beijing time, the satellite will pass over Changchun and Beijing; at 5:07 AM, over Shanghai; at 6:38 AM, over Urumqi; at 6:42 AM, over Lanzhou; at 6:43 AM, over Chengdu; at 6:44 AM, over Kunming; and at 6:45 AM, it will reach Hanoi, Vietnam.

    When the weather is clear, the satellite may be visible to the naked eye in all of the above regions.

### 红色月亮  今明过北京肉眼可见

1958-03-16
第3版()
专栏：

    红色月亮
    今明过北京肉眼可见
    据新华社15日讯　据塔斯社讯：到3月15日莫斯科时间早晨六点钟为止，苏联第二个人造卫星围绕地球转了一千九百零一圈。
    16日卫星经过长春、北京、上海、乌鲁木齐、兰州、成都。
    17日北京时间五点四分，卫星过长春、北京，五点七分过上海；六点三十八分过乌鲁木齐，六点四十二分过兰州，六点四十三分过成都，六点四十四分过昆明，六点四十五分到越南的河内。
    天气晴朗时，以上地区都有可能用肉眼看到这个卫星。
