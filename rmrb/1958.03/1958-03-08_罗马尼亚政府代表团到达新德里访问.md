Title: Romanian Government Delegation Arrives in New Delhi for Visit
Author:
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 7th. The Romanian government delegation, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Chivu Stoica, arrived in New Delhi today at noon on a "Tu-104" special plane. The delegation is visiting India for a period of fifteen days at the invitation of the Indian government. Members of the delegation include Romanian Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Alexandru Bârlădeanu, and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Grigore Preoteasa.

Welcoming the Romanian government delegation at the airport were Indian Prime Minister Nehru, Defense Minister Menon, Minister of Steel, Mines and Fuel Swaran Singh, other ministers, senior officers of the Indian armed forces, members of parliament, and diplomatic envoys to India from various countries, including Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili.

Chairman Stoica reviewed the guard of honor at the airport and accepted wreaths presented by representatives of the Indo-Romanian Friendship Association and Romanian children.

Prime Minister Nehru, in his welcoming address on behalf of the Indian government and people, expressed his hope that Chairman Stoica's visit would further strengthen cooperation and understanding between India and Romania.

Chairman Stoica said amidst cheers from the crowd that the Romanian government and people fully endorse the joint statement on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence issued by Prime Minister Nehru and Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai.

Subsequently, Chairman Stoica and Prime Minister Nehru rode in an open car to the Presidential Palace, and were cheered by many Delhi citizens along the way.

### 罗马尼亚政府代表团到达新德里访问

1958-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚政府代表团到达新德里访问
    新华社新德里7日电　以部长会议主席斯托伊卡为首的罗马尼亚政府代表团今天中午乘“图—104”专机到达新德里。代表团是应印度政府的邀请，来印度作为期十五天的访问的。代表团团员中包括罗马尼亚部长会议副主席波德纳拉希和外交部长布纳丘。
    到机场欢迎罗马尼亚政府代表团的有印度总理尼赫鲁，国防部长梅农，钢铁、矿业和燃料部长斯·辛格和其他部长以及印度武装部队的高级军官、国会议员们和包括中国驻印度大使潘自力在内的各国驻印度外交使节等。
    斯托伊卡主席在机场上检阅了仪仗队，并且接受了印罗友好协会的代表和罗马尼亚儿童所献的花环。
    尼赫鲁总理在代表印度政府和人民致欢迎词时，表示希望斯托伊卡主席的这次访问将进一步加强印度和罗马尼亚两国的合作和了解。
    斯托伊卡主席在群众的欢呼声中说，罗马尼亚政府和人民完全赞成尼赫鲁总理和中国总理周恩来所发表的关于和平共处的五项原则的联合声明。
    随后，斯托伊卡主席和尼赫鲁总理同乘敞篷汽车前往总统府，沿途受到许多德里市民的欢呼。
