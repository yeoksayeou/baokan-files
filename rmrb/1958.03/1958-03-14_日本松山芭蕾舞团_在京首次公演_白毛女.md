Title: Japanese Matsuyama Ballet Troupe Premieres "The White Haired Girl" in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1958-03-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: The ballet "The White Haired Girl," adapted and performed by the Japanese Matsuyama Ballet Troupe, met with Chinese audiences for the first time today at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing. Over 1,500 spectators from the capital congratulated the performance's success with prolonged and enthusiastic applause, forcing the Japanese artists to take repeated curtain calls.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, Minister Shen Yanbing, and others attended tonight's performance.

Before the performance began, troupe leader Masao Shimizu delivered a speech, saying that if their performance could strengthen Sino-Japanese friendship and thereby contribute to world peace, it would be the greatest honor for them.

In the one-and-a-half-hour, three-act ballet, the Japanese artists expressed the theme of the Chinese opera "The White Haired Girl" with great precision and completeness. Many audience members praised it as a successful creation using ballet to portray modern life.

Wang Kun, who first played Xi'er in the Chinese opera "The White Haired Girl," went on stage after the performance to present flowers to the Japanese artists and warmly kissed Mikiko Matsuyama.

"The White Haired Girl" was the last of three programs performed by the Matsuyama Ballet Troupe tonight. They also performed the fairytale ballet "Peter and the Wolf" and excerpts from the classical ballet "Les Sylphides." Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, and others also stepped onto the stage at the end of the performance to shake hands and congratulate the Japanese artists.

### 日本松山芭蕾舞团  在京首次公演“白毛女”

1958-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    日本松山芭蕾舞团
    在京首次公演“白毛女”
    据新华社13日讯  由日本松山芭蕾舞团改编演出的芭蕾舞剧“白毛女”，今天在北京天桥剧场第一次同中国观众见面。一千五百多首都观众以长时间的热烈鼓掌祝贺这次演出的成功，日本艺术家们不得不连续谢幕多次。
    陈毅副总理、郭沫若副委员长、沈雁冰部长等人出席观看了今晚的演出。
    清水正夫团长在演出开始前致词，他说，如果他们的演出能够加强日中友谊，并从而对世界和平有所贡献，将是他们感到最光荣的事情。
    日本艺术家们在一小时半的三幕舞剧中，非常精练而又完满地表达了中国歌剧“白毛女”的主题。许多观众在演出结束时都交口称赞这是一次用芭蕾舞来表现现代生活的成功创造。
    第一次在中国歌剧“白毛女”中表演喜儿的王昆，在演出结束后上台向日本艺术家们献花，同松山树子热烈亲吻。
    “白毛女”是今晚松山芭蕾舞团表演的三个节目中的最后一个，他们还演出了童话舞剧“彼得和狼”，以及古典舞剧“仙女们”的片断。陈毅副总理、郭沫若副委员长等人，在演出结束时也步上舞台，向日本艺术家们握手道贺。
