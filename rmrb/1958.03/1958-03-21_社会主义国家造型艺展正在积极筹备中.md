Title: Socialist Countries' Fine Arts Exhibition is Under Active Preparation
Author:
Date: 1958-03-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th: According to TASS, representatives from the art workers of eleven socialist countries began a meeting in Moscow on the 19th to discuss the issue of holding the first fine arts exhibition of socialist countries. These countries are: Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, Democratic Germany, China, Democratic Korea, Mongolia, Poland, Romania, Czechoslovakia, and the Soviet Union.

Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov said at the meeting that everyone expressed the hope to hold a fine arts exhibition of socialist countries during preliminary talks.

A committee for preparing the first fine arts exhibition of socialist countries has been established.

The meeting is continuing.

### 社会主义国家造型艺展正在积极筹备中

1958-03-21
第6版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家造型艺展
    正在积极筹备中
    据新华社20日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：十一个社会主义国家的艺术工作者代表团19日在莫斯科开始举行会议，讨论举办社会主义国家第一届造型艺术展览会的问题。这些国家是：阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利、民主德国、中国、民主朝鲜、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克和苏联。
    苏联文化部长米哈伊洛夫在会上说，初步会谈时大家都表示希望举办社会主义国家造型艺术展览会。
    筹备社会主义国家第一届造型艺术展览会事宜的委员会已经成立。
    会议在继续进行中。
