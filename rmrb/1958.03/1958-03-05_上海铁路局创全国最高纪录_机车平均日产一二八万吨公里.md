Title: Shanghai Railway Bureau Sets National Record: Average Locomotive Daily Output 1.28 Million Ton-Kilometers
Author:
Date: 1958-03-05
Page: 1

    Shanghai Railway Bureau Sets National Record
    Locomotive Average Daily Output 1.28 Million Ton-Kilometers
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 4th. The Shanghai Railway Bureau today created a new national record of an average daily output of 1,289,000 ton-kilometers per locomotive.
    This achievement comes on the first day after the Shanghai Railway Bureau implemented a new operating schedule guaranteeing a daily output of one million ton-kilometers per locomotive, in competition with the Beijing Railway Bureau.
    Today, the average daily output per locomotive at the Shanghai Railway Bureau increased by 440,000 ton-kilometers compared to yesterday. The daily mileage of locomotives increased from 429.1 kilometers yesterday to 640 kilometers. Passenger trains also ran on time according to the accelerated new schedule.
    On this day, locomotive "ムㄌ7—502" from Hangzhou to Shanghai hauled 4,219 tons in excess of its axle load.

### 上海铁路局创全国最高纪录  机车平均日产一二八万吨公里

1958-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    上海铁路局创全国最高纪录
    机车平均日产一二八万吨公里
    新华社上海4日电　上海铁路管理局，今天创造了平均每台机车日产一百二十八万九千吨公里的全国最高纪录。
    这是在和北京铁路管理局竞赛中，上海铁路管理局实行保证机车日产百万吨公里的新运行图后第一天的成绩。
    今天，上海铁路管理局每台机车平均日产量比昨天增加了四十四万吨公里，机车的日行里程从昨天的四百二十九点一公里提高到六百四十公里；客车也全部按加快后的新时刻表准点开行。
    在这一天，从杭州到上海的“ムㄌ7—502号”机车超轴牵引了四千二百十九吨。
