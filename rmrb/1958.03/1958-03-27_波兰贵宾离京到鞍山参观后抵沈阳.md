Title: Polish Guests Left Beijing to Visit Anshan and Arrived in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1958-03-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 26th: The Government Delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, headed by Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz, left Beijing by special plane today to visit Anshan, Shenyang, and other places.

Yang Yingjie, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission of our country, and Jerzy Knothe, the Polish Ambassador to China, accompanied the distinguished guests on their visits.

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, March 26th: The members of the Polish government delegation led by Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz arrived in Anshan by special plane at 11:00 a.m. today.

After listening to Yuan Zhen, General Manager of Anshan Iron and Steel, introduce the production and construction of Anshan Iron and Steel, the distinguished guests visited the Anshan Iron and Steel Ironmaking Plant, Seamless Steel Tube Plant, Second Sheet Mill, Second Steelmaking Plant, and Large Rolling Mill in the afternoon. In front of the open hearth furnace of the Second Steelmaking Plant, Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz warmly shook hands with the steelworkers, saying, "I bring you greetings from Poland." After inquiring in detail about the steel production, he satisfactorily encouraged the workers to produce more and better steel.

At 4:30 p.m., the distinguished guests took a special plane to Shenyang. In the evening, Qiu Youwen hosted a banquet at the Liaoning Hotel to welcome the Polish guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, March 26th: The government delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, headed by Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz, arrived in Shenyang by special plane from Anshan at 5:10 p.m. today.

Qiu Youwen, Vice Governor of Liaoning Province, and Li Huang, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and others welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport.

### 波兰贵宾离京到鞍山参观后抵沈阳

1958-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰贵宾离京到鞍山参观后抵沈阳
    据新华社26日讯　以雅罗谢维奇副主席为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团，今天乘专机离开北京去鞍山、沈阳等地参观访问。
    我国国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰和波兰驻中国大使基里洛克，陪同贵宾到各地参观访问。
    据新华社鞍山26日电　雅罗谢维奇副主席率领的波兰政府代表团部分团员，今天上午十一时乘专机来鞍山参观。
    贵宾们在听了鞍钢总经理袁振对鞍钢生产建设情况的介绍后，下午参观了鞍钢炼铁厂、无缝钢管厂、第二薄板厂、第二炼钢厂、大型轧钢厂。在第二炼钢厂的平炉前，雅罗谢维奇副主席和炼钢工人们亲切地握手，他说：“我从波兰给你们带来了问候”。他详细地询问了钢的生产情况以后，满意地勉励工人们生产更多更好的钢铁。
    午后四点三十分贵宾们乘专机去沈阳，晚上，仇友文在辽宁宾馆设宴欢迎波兰贵宾。
    据新华社沈阳26日电　以雅罗谢维奇副主席为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团，今天下午五点十分乘专机由鞍山到达沈阳。
    辽宁省副省长仇友文、中共辽宁省委书记李荒等在机场上欢迎贵宾。
