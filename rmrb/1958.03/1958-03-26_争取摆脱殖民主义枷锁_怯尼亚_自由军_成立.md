Title: Striving to Break Free from the Shackles of Colonialism: Kenyan "Freedom Army" Established
Author:
Date: 1958-03-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 25th. TASS, London, March 24th: According to a report in the British "News Chronicle," the "Freedom Army" organization was established at a mass rally of thousands of people held in Nairobi, the capital of Kenya, on March 23. Its purpose is to fight for Kenya to break free from the shackles of colonialism.

### 争取摆脱殖民主义枷锁  怯尼亚“自由军”成立

1958-03-26
第5版()
专栏：

    争取摆脱殖民主义枷锁
    怯尼亚“自由军”成立
    据新华社25日讯　塔斯社伦敦24日讯：据英国“新闻纪事报”报道，3月23日在怯尼亚首都内罗毕市举行的成千上万人的群众大会上成立了“自由军”组织，它的目的是为怯尼亚摆脱殖民主义枷锁而斗争。
