Title: Japanese Matsuyama Ballet Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: The Japanese Matsuyama Ballet Troupe, which first adapted the Chinese opera "The White Haired Girl" into a ballet, arrived in Beijing tonight, led by Kiyoshi Masao, cultural committee member of the Japan-China Friendship Association headquarters, and his wife, Mikiko Matsuyama.

This professional dance troupe, led by Japanese progressives, will perform "The White Haired Girl" as its main program during a month and a half of visits and performances in Beijing, Chongqing, Wuhan, and Shanghai. Since 1955, they have performed "The White Haired Girl" more than 40 times in various parts of Japan, earning high praise from the vast Japanese audience.

The Japanese artists were warmly welcomed at the Beijing Railway Station by Yang Hansheng, Ouyang Yuqian, Tian Han, Ouyang Shanzun, Ma Shaobo, Dai Ailian, and many others.

### 日本松山芭蕾舞团到北京

1958-03-09
第2版()
专栏：

    日本松山芭蕾舞团到北京
    据新华社8日讯　第一次把中国歌剧“白毛女”改编成芭蕾舞剧的日本松山芭蕾舞团，今晚由日中友好协会总会文化委员清水正夫和他的夫人松山树子率领到达北京。
    这个由日本进步人士领导的职业舞蹈团将以“白毛女”作为主要节目，在北京、重庆、武汉和上海等地访问演出一个半月。他们从1955年开始，已在日本国内各地演出“白毛女”四十多次，获得了日本广大观众的好评。
    日本艺术家们在北京车站受到了阳翰笙、欧阳予倩、田汉、欧阳山尊、马少波、戴爱莲等多人的热烈欢迎。
