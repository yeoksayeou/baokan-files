Title: Production Begins Before Project Completion
Author:
Date: 1958-03-14
Page: 3

    Production Begins Before Project Completion
    The newly built, modern Luoyang Bearing Factory in our country has begun to supply a large quantity of high-quality bearings produced during its trial production to the First Automobile Works and various machinery factories across the country. Currently, it has received orders from more than 270 units.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 工程未完  先出产品

1958-03-14
第3版()
专栏：

    工程未完  先出产品
    我国新建的现代化洛阳轴承厂在试生产中制造的大批优良的轴承，开始源源向第一汽车制造厂和各地机械厂供应。现在，它所接受的订货单位已经有二百七十多家。
                      （新华社）
