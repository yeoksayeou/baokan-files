Title: Li Juikui Appointed Political Commissar of the PLA General Logistics Department
Author:
Date: 1958-03-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th - Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, in accordance with the decision of the 94th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, appointed Li Jukui as Political Commissar of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 李聚奎任解放军总后勤部政治委员

1958-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    李聚奎任解放军总后勤部政治委员
    新华社7日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九十四次会议的决定，任命李聚奎为中国人民解放军总后方勤务部政治委员。
