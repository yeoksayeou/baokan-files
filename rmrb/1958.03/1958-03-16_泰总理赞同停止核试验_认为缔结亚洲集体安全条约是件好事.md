Title: Thai Prime Minister Agrees to Halt Nuclear Tests; Believes Concluding an Asian Collective Security Treaty is a Good Thing
Author:
Date: 1958-03-16
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, March 15th: Thai Prime Minister Nai Thanom stated to the press on the 12th regarding the issue of concluding an Asian Collective Security Treaty: "I think this is a good thing. I would very much like everyone to not invade each other."

　　Nai Thanom expressed that Thailand is willing to support anything that is conducive to peace in the Asian region.

　　Nai Thanom said: "I myself do not want to fight with anyone."

　　Nai Thanom also said: "I personally am in favor of all parties stopping nuclear weapons testing, because testing nuclear weapons will bring great danger to mankind."

　　At the same time, Nai Thanom did not express a positive opinion on the suggestion of establishing a nuclear-free zone in Asia.

### 泰总理赞同停止核试验  认为缔结亚洲集体安全条约是件好事

1958-03-16
第3版()
专栏：

    泰总理赞同停止核试验
    认为缔结亚洲集体安全条约是件好事
    新华社15日讯　曼谷消息：泰总理乃他侬12日就缔结亚洲集体安全条约问题向报界发表谈话说：“我认为这是一件好事，我是很喜欢大家互不侵犯的”。
    乃他侬表示，凡有利于亚洲地区和平的，泰国都愿意支持。
    乃他侬说：“我本身不想跟谁打仗。”
    乃他侬还说：“我本人是赞成任何方面都应该停止试验核武器的，因为试验核武器将有给人类带来很大的危险”。
    同时，乃他侬对于在亚洲建立无原子地区的建议没有表示正面意见。
