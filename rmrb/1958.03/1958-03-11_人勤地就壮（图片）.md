Title: Diligent People Make the Land Flourish (Picture)
Author: Wang Li
Date: 1958-03-11
Page: 4

    Diligent People Make the Land Flourish
    (Quick Sketch of Life for Downward Transferred Cadres)
    The nursing baby doesn't leave its mother,
    Crops lack fertilizer and refuse to grow tall.
    Manure is more precious than gold,
    Diligently accumulate fertilizer for a bountiful harvest.
    New Commune Member of Yongxing Cooperative, Mapeng Township, Gaoyou, Jiangsu
          Wang Li
