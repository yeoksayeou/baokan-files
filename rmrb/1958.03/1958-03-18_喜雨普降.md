Title: Joyful Rain Widely Falls
Author:
Date: 1958-03-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th: From the 11th to the 17th, most parts of the country experienced widespread joyful rain. This is the largest rainfall since the beginning of spring, which is extremely beneficial to the growth of spring crops, spring planting, and the relief of drought in some areas.

    This rainfall started in the Yangtze River basin. From the 11th to the 17th, rain (or snow) fell in Anhui, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, western Sichuan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Fujian, northern Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, eastern Gansu, Shaanxi, Shanxi, Hebei, Henan, Shandong, southern Liaoning, northern Heilongjiang Province, and eastern Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

    As of 8:00 AM on the 17th, the rainfall in northern Jiangxi and southern Zhejiang was mostly above 100 millimeters, such as 162 millimeters in Jingdezhen, Jiangxi, and 120 millimeters in Longquan, Zhejiang. Other provinces in the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River basin generally had 20 to 40 millimeters; western Sichuan, southern Shaanxi, southern Henan, northern Anhui, and northern Jiangsu had 10 to 20 millimeters; southern Hebei, northern Henan, Shandong, and southern Shanxi had 20 to 30 millimeters, including 39 millimeters in Kaifeng, Henan, 34 millimeters in Zhengzhou, and 35 millimeters in Luoyang; northern Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, southern Liaoning, northern Heilongjiang, and eastern Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region generally had 5 to 8 millimeters.

    Currently, the rain and snow in provinces north of the Yangtze River have gradually stopped, and the rainfall zone south of the Yangtze River is also moving southward and gradually shrinking.

### 喜雨普降

1958-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    喜雨普降
    新华社17日讯　从11日起到17日止，全国大部地区普降喜雨。这是入春以来最大的一次，对春季农作物的生长、春耕播种以及解除某些地区的干旱，有极大的好处。
    这次降雨是从长江流域开始的。从11日到17日，下过雨（雪）的地方，有安徽、江苏、浙江、四川西部、湖北、湖南、江西、福建、新疆维吾尔自治区北部，甘肃东部、陕西、山西、河北、河南、山东、辽宁南部、黑龙江省北部、内蒙古自治区东部等省和自治区。
    到17日早八时止，江西北部、浙江南部雨量大部分在一百公厘以上，如江西景德镇为一百六十二公厘，浙江龙泉为一百二十公厘；长江流域中下游其他各省，一般也有二十到四十公厘；四川西部、陕西南部、河南南部、安徽北部、江苏北部，有十到二十公厘；河北南部、河南北部、山东、山西南部有二十到三十公厘，其中如河南开封为三十九公厘、郑州三十四公厘、洛阳三十五公厘；新疆维吾尔自治区北部、辽宁南部、黑龙江北部、内蒙古自治区东部，一般也有五到八公厘。
    目前，长江以北各省的雨雪已先后停止，长江以南的降雨地带也正在南移并逐渐缩小。
