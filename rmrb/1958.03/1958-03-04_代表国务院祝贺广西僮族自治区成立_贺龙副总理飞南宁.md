Title: Vice Premier He Long Flies to Nanning to Represent the State Council in Congratulating the Establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1958-03-04
Page: 4

    Representing the State Council to Congratulate the Establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region
    Vice Premier He Long Flies to Nanning
    Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd: Vice Premier He Long flew from Beijing to Nanning this morning to represent the State Council in congratulating the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region.
    The Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region will be formally established on the 5th.
    Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, accompanied Vice Premier He Long to Nanning.
    Those who saw him off at the airport included Xi Zhongxun, Liu Yalou, Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, and Yang Jingren.
    Prior to this, Xie Fumin, Deputy Chairman of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, had already left for Nanning.

### 代表国务院祝贺广西僮族自治区成立  贺龙副总理飞南宁

1958-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    代表国务院祝贺广西僮族自治区成立
    贺龙副总理飞南宁
    新华社3日讯　贺龙副总理今天上午由北京乘飞机去南宁，代表国务院前往祝贺广西僮族自治区的成立。
    广西僮族自治区将在5日正式成立。
    随同贺龙副总理一起去南宁的有民族事务委员会副主任汪锋。
    到机场送行的，有习仲勋、刘亚楼、刘春、萨空了、杨静仁等人。
    在这以前，全国人民代表大会民族委员会副主任委员谢扶民已动身去南宁。
