Title: Linxia Hui Autonomous Prefecture is Preparing to Build 2155 Livestock and Horticultural Farms
Author: Guo Dong
Date: 1958-03-14
Page: 2

    Linxia Hui Autonomous Prefecture is Preparing to Build
    2155 Livestock and Horticultural Farms
    (Our report) The farmers of all ethnic groups in the Linxia Hui Autonomous Prefecture, Gansu Province, have recently formulated a plan to greatly develop diversified economy and animal husbandry production this year, and build 2,155 new farms.
    These projects include: 126 livestock breeding farms, developing 6,300 head of fine breeds of livestock; 280 sheep farms, developing 42,000 sheep; 94 chicken and duck farms, breeding 18,800 chickens and ducks; 872 rabbit farms, breeding 130,800 fine breed rabbits; 231 pig farms, developing 2,390 pigs; 191 bee farms (including 40 Italian bee farms and 151 Chinese bee farms), raising 3,020 boxes of bees; 8 fish farms; and 243 horticultural farms, covering an area of 23,400 mu. In addition, 110 bamboo and willow weaving farms will be established in areas near the mountains. Some of these farms will be established before the spring plowing.
    In order to solve the problem of fine breed sources, train technical personnel, and ensure the development of animal husbandry in the entire prefecture, the Linxia Hui Autonomous Prefecture is preparing to build a large-scale breeding and promotion farm, specializing in the introduction and breeding of fine breeds to serve agricultural cooperatives.
                        (Guo Dong)

### 临夏回族自治州正在筹建  2155个畜牧场园艺场
郭栋
1958-03-14
第2版()
专栏：

    临夏回族自治州正在筹建
    2155个畜牧场园艺场
    本报讯  甘肃省临夏回族自治州各族农民，最近订出计划，将在今年大量发展多种经济和畜牧业生产，新建二千一百五十五个场。
    在这些项目里，包括有：牲畜繁殖场一百二十六处，发展优良种畜六千三百头；羊场二百八十处，发展羊只四万二千只；鸡鸭场九十四处，繁殖鸡鸭一万八千八百只；兔场八百七十二处，繁殖良种兔一十三万零八百只；猪场二百三十一处，发展猪二千三百九十口；蜂场一百九十一处（其中意大利种蜂场四十处，中国种蜂场一百五十一处），养蜂三千零二十箱；养鱼场八处；园艺场二百四十三处，面积达二万三千四百亩。此外，在沿山附近地带，建立竹柳编制场一百一十处。这些场，一部分将在春耕前建立。
    为了解决优良种源问题，培养技术人员，保证全州养畜事业的发展，临夏回族自治州正在筹建一处大型的种畜繁殖推广场，专作引进繁殖良种工作，为农业社服务。
                        （郭栋）
