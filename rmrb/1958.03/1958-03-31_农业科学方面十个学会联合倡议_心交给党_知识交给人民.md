Title: Joint Initiative of Ten Academic Societies in Agricultural Science: Dedicate Our Hearts to the Party, Dedicate Our Knowledge to the People
Author:
Date: 1958-03-31
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, March 30th: Ten national academic societies in agricultural science today jointly issued an initiative to agricultural scientific and technical workers nationwide, proposing to transform themselves into both red and expert working-class intellectuals as soon as possible and to better serve production.

This initiative was adopted by the ten societies at a joint meeting held from the 26th to the 30th to convey the work report of the Chinese Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union. The ten societies are: the Chinese Society of Agronomy, the Chinese Society for Horticultural Science, the Chinese Society of Agricultural Machinery, the Entomological Society of China, the Soil Science Society of China, the Chinese Society of Forestry, the Chinese Society of Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Medicine, the Hydraulic Engineering Society of China, the Chinese Society for Plant Pathology, and the Chinese Society of Oceanology and Limnology.

The initiative proposes to resolutely support the leadership of the Party, follow the socialist road, dedicate our hearts to the Party, and dedicate our knowledge to the people; to orient ourselves towards production and practice, use scientific methods to summarize the rich experience of the masses in achieving high yields, use advanced theories and technologies of modern science to study and solve practical problems in production, and catch up with or surpass international scientific levels as soon as possible; to resolutely implement the principle of diligence and thrift in running science, personally conduct experiments, and promote major innovations in agricultural technology; to continue criticizing bourgeois academic thought and launch a struggle between the two roads; to rapidly cultivate a scientific and technical workforce; to further study advanced Soviet scientific theories and technologies, strengthen Sino-Soviet cooperation, and contribute to the realization of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Agreement; to promote contact and cooperation between fraternal societies and conduct comprehensive research; and to improve the working style and methods of the societies, advocate holding academic activities on-site, and mobilize members to solve important agricultural production problems in the region. More than 200 representatives from the ten societies and their local branches who attended the meeting listened to the report of the Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union on agricultural science work, as well as reports from the Ministry of Agriculture, the Ministry of State Farms and Land Reclamation, and responsible persons from relevant departments on the current situation of the great leap forward in agricultural production in our country, and held discussions. After the meeting, the representatives will go to the Tianjin Special District to visit and investigate the situation of lowland transformation.

### 农业科学方面十个学会联合倡议  心交给党　知识交给人民

1958-03-31
第7版()
专栏：

    农业科学方面十个学会联合倡议
    心交给党　知识交给人民
    新华社30日讯　全国农业科学方面的十个学会，今天联合向全国农业科学技术工作者发出倡议，提出要尽早地使他们改造成为又红又专的工人阶级知识分子，并更好地为生产服务。
    这个倡议是十个学会在26日到30日联合召开的中国访苏科学技术代表团工作报告传达会议上通过的。这十个学会是：中国农学会、中国园艺学会、中国农业机械学会、中国昆虫学会、中国土壤学会、中国林学会、中国畜牧兽医学会、中国水利学会、中国植物病理学会、中国海洋湖沼学会。
    这个倡议书提出，要坚决拥护党的领导，走社会主义的道路，把心交给党，把知识交给人民；面向生产，面向实际，用科学的方法总结群众丰产经验，用近代科学的先进理论与技术，研究与解决生产实际问题，尽早地赶上或超过国际科学水平；坚决实行勤俭办科学，亲自动手做试验，促进农业技术大革新；继续批判资产阶级学术思想，展开两条道路的斗争；迅速培养科学技术队伍；进一步学习苏联先进科学理论和技术，加强中苏合作，为实现中苏科学技术协定贡献力量；促进兄弟学会间的联系与协作，进行综合性研究；改进各学会的工作作风和方法，提倡到现场去举行学术活动和发动会员为该地区解决农业生产上的重要问题。出席会议的十个学会和各地分会的二百多名代表，听取了访苏科学技术代表团关于农业科学工作方面的传达报告，以及农业部、农垦部和有关部门负责人关于目前我国农业生产大跃进的形势的报告，并进行了讨论。会议结束后，代表们将去天津专区参观和考察洼地改造的情形。
