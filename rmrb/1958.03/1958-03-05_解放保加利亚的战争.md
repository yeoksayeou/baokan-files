Title: The War of Liberation of Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1958-03-05
Page: 6

Background Information:

    In the early 1870s, most of the Balkan Peninsula was under the feudal rule of Turkey. In April 1876, Bulgaria launched an uprising against Turkish feudal lords and Bulgarian wealthy peasant usurers, but it was suppressed by the Turkish army. Turkey's barbaric behavior aroused extreme indignation among the Russian people.

    In 1876, Russia demanded that Turkey cease its military actions against Serbia by means of an ultimatum. Because Turkey refused to make any concessions to the Slavic people, coupled with Britain's instigation, this triggered a war between Russia and Turkey. In April 1877, Russia declared war on Turkey. The following year, the Russian army drove the Turkish army out of Bulgaria and advanced to the city of Constantinople, ultimately forcing Turkey to sign a peace treaty.

### 解放保加利亚的战争

1958-03-05
第6版()
专栏：小资料

    解放保加利亚的战争
    在十九世纪的七十年代初，巴尔干半岛的大部分受着土耳其的封建统治。1876年4月，保加利亚发动了反对土耳其封建主和保加利亚富农高利贷者的起义，但是被土耳其军队镇压下去了。土耳其的野蛮行为引起俄罗斯人民的极度愤慨。
    1876年，俄国曾以最后通牒的方式要求土耳其停止对塞尔维亚的军事行动。由于土耳其拒绝对斯拉夫族人民作任何让步，加上英国的挑拨，引起了俄国同土耳其的战争。1877年4月，俄国向土耳其宣战，翌年俄军把土耳其军队驱逐出保加利亚，并进抵君士坦丁堡城下，最后迫使土耳其签订了和约。
