Title: Voroshilov Receives Hammarskjöld
Author:
Date: 1958-03-27
Page: 5

    Voroshilov
    Receives Hammarskjöld
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 26th – TASS Moscow, March 26th: The Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, received the Secretary-General of the United Nations, Hammarskjöld, today and spoke with him.

### 伏罗希洛夫接见哈马舍尔德

1958-03-27
第5版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫
    接见哈马舍尔德
    据新华社26日讯　塔斯社莫斯科26日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫今天接见了联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，并且同他谈了话。
