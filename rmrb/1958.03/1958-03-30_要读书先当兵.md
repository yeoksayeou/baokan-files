Title: To Study, First Enlist

Author:

Date: 1958-03-30
Page: 3

Foreign Press Digest:

    To Study, First Enlist
    The military has currently adopted various material incentives to enable military technicians who want to retire to find higher-paying jobs to continue serving in the military.
    Active duty soldiers can get a free four-year university education. However, to obtain a free year of university study, one needs to serve an additional three years.
    ——"New York Herald Tribune"

### 要读书先当兵

1958-03-30
第3版()
专栏：外国报刊拾零

    要读书先当兵
    军队目前已经采取各种物质奖励措施，以使那些想退伍去寻找赚钱更多的工作的军事技术人员，能够继续留在军队服务。
    凡是现役的军人，可以得到免费读四年大学的机会，不过，要取得每一年的大学免费学习，就需要再服役三年。
    ——美国“纽约先驱论坛报”
