Title: Nasser Orders Dissolution of Syrian Political Parties and Groups
Author:
Date: 1958-03-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, March 13th: United Arab Republic President Nasser signed an order on the evening of March 12th, stipulating that "all political parties and groups in the Syrian region should be dissolved, and the establishment of any new or old political parties or groups is prohibited."

The order announced that "all members of the dissolved political parties and groups are prohibited from engaging in any kind of partisan activities, and it is forbidden to provide any assistance to members engaged in such activities."

The order also stipulates that the money and property of the dissolved political parties and groups must be handed over to the National Union.

### 纳赛尔下令  解散叙利亚政党团体

1958-03-14
第5版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔下令
    解散叙利亚政党团体
    据新华社大马士革13日电  阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔12日晚间签署了一项命令，规定“叙利亚地区的一切政党和团体都应予解散，并且禁止成立任何新旧政党或团体”。
    这项命令宣布“禁止被解散的政党和团体的一切成员进行任何种类的党派活动，并且禁止给予从事这种活动的成员以任何帮助”。
    命令还规定，被解散的政党和团体的钱财必须交给民族联盟。
