Title: Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Send Greetings to the Soviet National Day; Comrade Stalin Sends a Reply of Thanks
Author:
Date: 1947-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 1st—On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the October Revolution in the Soviet Union, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Commander-in-Chief of the People's Liberation Army Zhu De sent a congratulatory telegram. We have now received a reply telegram from Comrade Stalin expressing his gratitude. The original telegram stated:

To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Zhu De: We sincerely thank you for your congratulations on the 30th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution!

　　Joseph Stalin (signed)

### 毛主席朱总司令电贺苏联国庆  斯大林同志复电感谢

1947-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席朱总司令电贺苏联国庆
     斯大林同志复电感谢
    【新华社陕北一日电】苏联十月革命三十周年纪念日，中共中央委员会主席毛泽东同志及人民解放军总司令朱德同志曾致电祝贺，顷接斯大林同志复电，表示感谢。原电称：
    中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东、中国人民解放军总司令朱德：衷心感谢你们对伟大的十月社会主义革命三十周年的祝贺！
    　约瑟夫·斯大林（签字）
