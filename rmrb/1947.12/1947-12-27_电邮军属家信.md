Title: Radio Broadcast of Family Letters to Military Dependents

Author:

Date: 1947-12-27
Page: 1

Radio Broadcast of Family Letters to Military Dependents

　　To further serve the southward advancing counter-offensive army, this station will broadcast family letters on behalf of military dependents starting from January 1, 1948. Please send your written family letters (please specify the recipient's name and unit number, as well as the sender's name and address) to the People's Daily, to be forwarded to the editorial department of this station.
　　　　　　　　　　Editorial Department of the Handan Xinhua Broadcasting Station

### 电邮军属家信

1947-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　电邮军属家信
    本台为了进一步为南下反攻大军服务，从一九四八年一月一日起，代替军属们播送家信，请各军属们把写好的家信（请写明收信人姓名及部队番号，发信人姓名及住址），寄人民日报社转本台编辑部即可。
    　　　　　　　　　　邯郸新华广播电台编辑部
