Title: Border Region Government Announces Three-Day New Year Holiday
Author:
Date: 1947-12-30
Page: 1

　　Border Region Government Announces Three-Day New Year Holiday
　　[Our Newspaper's Report] With New Year's Day 1948 approaching, the Border Region Government has notified all Party, government, military, and civilian organizations throughout the region that they will be closed for three days starting from New Year's Day. Industrial and mining production organizations may set their own schedules based on work needs.

### 边区政府通知新年放假三天

1947-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　边区政府通知新年放假三天
    【本报讯】一九四八年元旦即届，边府通知全区党政军民各机关自元旦起放假三天，工矿等各生产机关根据工作需要，可自行规定。
