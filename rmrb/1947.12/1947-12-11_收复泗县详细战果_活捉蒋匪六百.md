Title: Detailed Victory of Recovering Si County: Captured 600 Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Alive
Author: 
Date: 1947-12-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, East China, December 8th: Late news: The local armed forces of Huaibei recovered Si County with the following detailed results: a total of 636 enemies were captured, including acting county magistrate Wang Yongkui, Public Security Bureau Chief Chu Chenghuang, Deputy Commander of the County Security Corps Sun Miao, Second Captain Yan Zhensheng, and Deputy Battalion Commander Xu Hu, among others. Over 40 enemies were killed, including First Battalion Commander Zhang Jingbo, and the following items were seized: 25 light machine guns, 1 grenade launcher, 580 rifles and pistols, over 34,000 rounds of ammunition, and many other military supplies.

Xinhua News Agency, East China, December 8th: The Binhai Military Region recently announced its comprehensive combat results for November: (1) 25 battles of varying sizes (including two offensive battles: the mopping-up operations in eastern Linyi and along the Longhai Railway). (2) 182 enemies were killed, 252 wounded, 302 local bandits were killed, 217 wounded, and 3,402 enemy personnel were captured alive, including the deputy leader of the puppet commando. (3) One light machine gun, one hand cannon, 360 rifles and pistols, over 10,000 rounds of various types of ammunition, and some other military materials were seized.

### 收复泗县详细战果  活捉蒋匪六百

1947-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    收复泗县详细战果
    活捉蒋匪六百
    【新华社华东八日电】迟到：淮北地武收复泗县详细战果：计俘代理县长汪永奎、公安局长储城黄、县保安总队副孙淼、第二队长阎振声、大队副许浒以下六百三十六名，毙敌第一大队长张镜波以下四十余名，缴轻机枪二十五挺，掷弹筒一个，长短枪五百八十枝，子弹三万四千余发，及其他军用品甚多。
    【新华社华东八日电】滨海军区顷公布十一月份综合战绩：（一）大小战斗二十五次（内临沂东及陇海路扫荡，攻势战役两次。）（二）毙敌一百八十二名、伤二百五十二名、毙土顽三百零二名、伤二百一十七名、生俘伪突击大队长以下三千四百零二名。（三）缴获轻机枪一挺，手炮一门，长短枪三百六十枝，各种子弹万余发，及其他军用物资一部。
