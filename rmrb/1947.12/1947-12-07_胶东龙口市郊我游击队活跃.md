Title: Our Guerrilla Forces Active in the Suburbs of Longkou City, Jiaodong
Author:
Date: 1947-12-07
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, East China, 5th. The people's guerrilla forces in the suburbs of Longkou City, Jiaodong are active. From September 21st, when the Chiang bandits invaded and occupied the city, to November 21st, in two months, they killed, wounded, and captured 221 enemies, seized some weapons, and destroyed a vehicle. The Chiang bandits in the city are extremely panicked, and eighteen people have surrendered to us, and there have been multiple escapes.

### 胶东龙口市郊我游击队活跃

1947-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　胶东龙口市郊我游击队活跃
    【新华社华东五日电】胶东龙口市郊人民游击队活跃，自九月二十一日蒋匪侵踞该市至十一月二十一日两月中，计毙伤俘敌二百二十一名，缴武器一部，毁汽车一辆。市内蒋匪极为恐慌，已有十八人向我投诚，逃跑者亦有多起。
