Title: Southern Shaanxi Recaptures Shanyang, Songxian Guerrilla Corps Annihilates 400 Bandits
Author:
Date: 1947-12-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Frontline of Henan, Shaanxi, and Hubei, 9th: A unit of the Southern Shaanxi People's Liberation Army, on the 5th, for the second time, captured Shanyang County, south of the Dan River. The defending enemy fled north.

    Xinhua News Agency, Frontline of Henan, Shaanxi, and Hubei, 9th: On the eastern front, our guerrilla corps, on the 2nd, cleared out bandits and diehards in the Pingdingshan area, 20 li southeast of Songxian County, annihilating 398 bandits and seizing 34 rifles and pistols, and over 5,000 rounds of ammunition.

### 陕南再克山阳  嵩县游击兵团歼匪四百

1947-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    陕南再克山阳
    嵩县游击兵团歼匪四百
    【新华社豫陕鄂前线九日电】陕南解放军一部，五日第二次攻克丹江南岸山阳县城，守敌北逃。
    【新华社豫陕鄂前线九日电】东线我游击兵团二日在嵩县东南二十里之平顶山地区清剿匪顽，歼匪三百九十八名，缴长短枪三十四枝，子弹五千余发。
