Title: Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia and Jin-Sui Banks Merge to Form Northwest Farmers Bank
Author:
Date: 1947-12-21
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 16th. In order to advance financial and economic work in the Northwest Liberated Area, the Northwest Central Bureau of the Chinese Communist Party has decided to merge the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Bank with the Jin-Sui Farmers Bank to form the Northwest Farmers Bank. The Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Trading Company will also merge with the Jin-Sui Trading Company to form the Northwest Trading Company. The two are unified in organizational leadership, with Yu Jie appointed as President and Manager, Liu Zhuofu as Vice President and Deputy Manager, and Shi Weiran as Supervisor and Director of the Political Department. The presidents and managers of the various branches and companies have also been formally appointed. The Northwest Bureau has also determined that the agricultural currency issued by the Northwest Farmers Bank will be the standard currency of the Northwest Liberated Area, while the circulating notes issued by the former Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Trading Company will be the auxiliary currency, with an exchange rate of one yuan of currency notes for one yuan of agricultural currency.

### 陕甘宁、晋绥银行合并　成立西北农民银行

1947-12-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　陕甘宁、晋绥银行合并
  　成立西北农民银行
    【新华社西北十六日电】为推进西北解放区财经工作，中共西北中央局，顷决定将陕甘宁边区银行与晋绥农民银行合并，成立西北农民银行。陕甘宁贸易公司亦与晋绥贸易公司合并，成立西北贸易公司。二者在组织领导上合而为一，任喻杰为行长兼经理，刘卓甫为副行长兼副经理，史唯然为监委兼政治处主任，各分行、公司、正副行长兼经理，亦经明令委任，西北局并确定以西北农民银行发行之农币，作为西北解放区之本位币，而以前陕甘宁贸易公司发行之流通券为辅币，其比价券币一元换农币一元。
