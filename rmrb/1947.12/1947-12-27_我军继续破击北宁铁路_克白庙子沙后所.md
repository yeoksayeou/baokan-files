Title: Our Army Continues to Destroy the Beining Railway, Captures Baimiaozi and Shahousuo
Author:
Date: 1947-12-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 25th. In the Beining Railway destruction campaign on the 9th, our Jidong troops captured the important stations of Baimiaozi and Shahousuo between Suizhong and Xingcheng, capturing 17 enemies. On the night of the 18th, another unit of our army advanced to the Changli-Beidaihe section of the railway, destroying 18 Jiang bandit blockhouses and 13 bunkers in one go, killing or wounding more than 30 enemies and capturing more than 50 puppet police; seized one heavy machine gun, 14 long and short guns, and two boxes of 60mm mortar shells; and destroyed more than 240 roadbeds.

### 我军继续破击北宁铁路　克白庙子沙后所

1947-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军继续破击北宁铁路
  　克白庙子沙后所
    【新华社东北二十五日电】冀东我军于九日北宁路破击战中，攻克绥中、兴城间重要车站白庙子、沙后所两处，俘敌十七名。十八日夜我军另部挺进昌黎、北戴河段铁路，一气破毁蒋匪炮楼十八座，地堡十三个，毙伤敌三十余名，俘伪警队五十余名；缴获重机枪一挺，长短枪十四支，六零炮弹两箱；毁路基二百四十余处。
