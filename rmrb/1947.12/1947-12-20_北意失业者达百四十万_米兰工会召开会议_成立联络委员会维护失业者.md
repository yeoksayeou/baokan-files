Title: Northern Italy Unemployment Reaches 1.4 Million; Milan Trade Union Holds Meeting; Establishes Liaison Committee to Protect Unemployed Workers
Author:
Date: 1947-12-20
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 15th: Rome dispatch: The unemployment problem in Italy is becoming increasingly serious. In Rome alone, 70,000 workers are unemployed, and the total number of unemployed in Northern Italy has surged to 1.4 million. Recently, the Milan Trade Union held a meeting of provincial committees to protect the unemployed in Northern Italy, resolving to "establish a liaison committee to protect the unemployed in Northern Italy" and, on behalf of the 1.4 million unemployed in Northern Italy, sent a telegram to the Gasperi government, protesting the government's indifference to the unemployment problem and vehemently denouncing the reactionary government's abolition of the decree prohibiting employers from firing workers, as the abolition of this decree has made the unemployment problem even more serious.

### 北意失业者达百四十万  米兰工会召开会议  成立联络委员会维护失业者

1947-12-20
第3版()
专栏：

    北意失业者达百四十万
    米兰工会召开会议
    成立联络委员会维护失业者
    【新华社陕北十五日电】罗马讯：意大利的失业问题已日趋严重，仅罗马一地即有七万工人失业，整个北意失业者已突增至一百四十万。最近米兰工会曾举行保障北意失业者的各省委员会会议，决议“成立联络委员会，以维护北意失业者”，并代表北意一百四十万失业者致电加斯贝利政府，抗议政府对失业问题漠不关心，大声斥责反动政府废除禁止雇主辞退工人的法令，因该法令的废除，使失业问题更趋严重。
