Title: Workers of the World Unite: Romanian Laboring People Support French Workers
Author:
Date: 1947-12-10
Page: 3
　　
　　World Workers United as One
    Romanian Laboring People Support French Workers
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th] Dispatches from various sources: The French working class's strike struggle continues to receive sympathy and support from workers in other countries. On the 2nd, the Romanian General Confederation of Labor telegraphed the French General Confederation of Labor, announcing: The Romanian laboring people express their solidarity with the French working class in their struggle against the reactionary forces and government of France that serve capitalism and Anglo-American imperialism. Also: The sailors of the American Liberty ship "Hulda L. Hulton," which was stranded in the port of Bordeaux due to the French sailors' strike, declared on November 27th that they were in solidarity with the French sailors' strike. Note: This righteous act by American sailors has happened for the second time. On November 18th, the American sailors of the American merchant ship "Henry Costine" expressed the same support for the striking workers in Marseille.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 8th] South New Agency reported on the 7th: The Yugoslav working class has expressed complete solidarity with the French workers' strike movement. The Central Committee of the Yugoslav Miners' Union sent a telegram to the French miners expressing their respect. The Belgrade Trade Union sent a telegram to the Paris Trade Union stating: "The laboring people of Belgrade are deeply impressed and closely follow the efforts of the working class of Paris. Under the leadership of the Communist Party, the working class of the French capital is defending the Republic and fighting for the democracy and future of France." The Yugoslav Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union also sent a telegram to the French Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union expressing their respect.

### 世界工人阶级一条心  罗南劳动人民声援法国工人

1947-12-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　世界工人阶级一条心
    罗南劳动人民声援法国工人
    【新华社陕北四日电】各方讯：法国工人阶级的罢工斗争，继续得到其他国家工人的同情声援。二日，罗马尼亚总工会致电法国总工会，宣布：罗国劳动人民对于法国工人阶级，在反对为资本主义和美英帝国主义服务的法国反动势力和政府的斗争中，表示休戚相关之意。又：因法国海员罢工而被阻滞于波尔多港内的美国自由轮胡尔顿·克利号海员于十一月二十七日声明，他们和法国海员罢工团结一致。按：美国海员此种义举已属第二次。十一月十八日美国商船亨利。考斯丁号的美国水手对马赛罢工工人即曾表示同样的支援。
    【新华社陕北八日电】南新社七日讯：南斯拉夫工人阶级顷对法国工人的罢工运动，表示完全的休戚相关。南国矿工联合会中委会致电法国矿工致敬。贝尔格莱德职工会致电巴黎职工会称：“贝尔格莱德劳动人民感佩万分，并密切注意着巴黎工人阶级的努力，法国首都的工人阶级在共产党的领导下，正在保卫共和国，为着法国的民主与将来而奋斗。”南国邮电职工会联合会亦电法国邮电职工联合会致敬。
