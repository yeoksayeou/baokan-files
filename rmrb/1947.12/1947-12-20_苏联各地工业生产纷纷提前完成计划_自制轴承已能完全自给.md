Title: Industrial Production in Various Regions of the Soviet Union Pre-Schedule Completion of Plans; Domestically Produced Bearings Now Fully Self-Sufficient
Author:
Date: 1947-12-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, December 18th: Moscow dispatch: Various industries in various regions of the Soviet Union have completed their annual production plans ahead of schedule. The entire industry of the Estonian Republic, celebrating the third anniversary of its liberation on November 24th, had completed its annual plan, an increase of 42.8% over the entire production volume of last year. The entire industry of the Georgian Republic had completed and exceeded this year's plan by 7.5% on December 1st. The entire industry of the Kazakh Republic has also completed this year's plan. The Soviet printing industry and the bearing industry completed this year's plan in late November, and will exceed the plan by more than 30% by the end of the year. The Soviet Union is now capable of manufacturing all kinds of bearings, from those as large as millstones to those as small as needles, and can supply all the needs of various industries. On December 7th, the entire Soviet food industry also completed its annual plan.

### 苏联各地工业生产纷纷提前完成计划  自制轴承已能完全自给

1947-12-20
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联各地工业生产纷纷提前完成计划
    自制轴承已能完全自给
    【新华社陕北十八日电】莫斯科讯：苏联各地各工业纷纷完成本年生产计划。爱沙尼亚共和国全部工业于十一月二十四日庆祝解放三周年纪念时，已完成本年计划，比去年全年生产量增加百分之四十二点八。乔治亚共和国全部工业在十二月一日已完成并超过本年计划百分之七点五。卡萨克斯坦共和国全部工业亦已完成本年计划。全苏印刷工业及轴承工业于十一月下旬即完成本年计划，到年底将超过计划百分之三十以上。苏联刻已能自制大如巨磨小如针尖之各种轴承，能供应各种工业之全部需要。十二月七日，全苏食品工业也已完成全年计划。
