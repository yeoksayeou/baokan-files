Title: Shijiazhuang Municipal Government Calls on Residents to Join Forces to Rebuild the People's City
Author:
Date: 1947-12-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 3rd. After the establishment of the Shijiazhuang Democratic Municipal Government, it actively carried out rehabilitation and relief work, and issued a proclamation calling on all citizens to mobilize and quickly build the people's city of Shijiazhuang. The proclamation of the Municipal Government and the Garrison Headquarters stated: (1) Workers and the poor must rise up to defend their factories and public buildings, and organize pickets to maintain law and order. (2) Peasants must rise up to implement "land to the tiller." (3) People from all walks of life in industry, commerce, and academia should quickly resume work, business, and schooling. (4) All personnel of the Chiang Kai-shek puppet organs must not leave their posts and flee, but immediately register with the Democratic Municipal Government to await processing. All responsible persons of Chiang Kai-shek puppet organs, groups, warehouses, and public enterprises must be responsible for safekeeping and await processing. Those who flee, harbor, sabotage, or steal will be punished. (5) All military supplies, ammunition, and military materials must be handed over to the Democratic Government.

### 石庄市府号召居民协力重建人民城市

1947-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　石庄市府号召居民协力重建人民城市
    【新华社晋察冀三日电】石庄民主市政府成立后，积极进行恢复救抚工作，并发出布告，号召全市市民动员起来，迅速建设人民城市石家庄。市政府、卫戍司令部布告称：（一）工人、贫民要起来保卫自己的工厂和公共建筑，组织纠察队维持治安。（二）农民们要起来实行耕者有其田。（三）工商学各界迅速复工、复业、复学。（四）一切蒋伪机关人员不得离职逃匿，立即到民主市府登记听候处理。一切蒋伪机关、团体、仓库及公有企业主管人员须负责保管，听候处理，如有逃匿、窝藏、破坏、盗窃等行为，必予惩处。（五）一切军火、弹药、军事物资一律交给民主政府。
