Title: Our Army Active West of Hankou, South of Dahong Mountain; Jianghan Military Region Captures Four Cities in Succession; Takes Important Stronghold of Zaoshi on the Hankou-Yichang Highway
Author:
Date: 1947-12-29
Page: 1

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Eyuwan Front, 27th】 The People's Liberation Army, active in the Jianghan Military Region, liberated four county towns in Hubei Province from the 20th to the 22nd, namely Tianmen, Jingshan, Zhongxiang, and Qianjiang, as well as Zaoshi (northwest of Tianmen), an important stronghold on the highway from Hankou to Yichang. (1) A unit of the People's Liberation Army captured the cities of Tianmen and Jingshan on the 20th, and also took Zaoshi on the same day, seizing some personnel, weapons, and ammunition. Detailed battle results are still being verified. (2) Another unit of the People's Liberation Army launched a long-range attack on Zhongxiang County on the 22nd. After several hours of fighting, they completely annihilated the four battalions of the Kuomintang bandit Hubei Provincial Security Second General Team guarding the city. In addition to killing and wounding more than 100, they also captured more than 1,100 enemy officers and soldiers, seized three mortars, more than 20 small cannons, 36 light and heavy machine guns, more than 600 rifles, a radio station, and a large amount of ammunition. The People's Liberation Army only suffered a dozen or so casualties. (3) Another unit of the People's Liberation Army liberated Qianjiang County on the 22nd. The defending enemy fled in panic. The People's Liberation Army has now established democratic order in this city.
　　【Xinhua News Agency, Eyuwan Front, 27th】 A unit of the People's Liberation Army of the Jianghan Military Region advanced to the Zhiluhe area south of Qianjiang on the 24th and encountered local KMT bandits. After a courageous pursuit by the People's Liberation Army, this group of enemies was wiped out, with a total of more than 50 killed and wounded, 85 captured alive, and three light machine guns, 60 rifles and short guns, more than 20,000 rounds of ammunition, and some other spoils of war seized. ### 我军活跃汉口以西大洪山南　江汉军区连克四城　汉宜公路上打下重要据点皂市

1947-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军活跃汉口以西大洪山南
  　江汉军区连克四城
  　汉宜公路上打下重要据点皂市
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十七日电】活动在江汉军区的解放军，从二十日到二十二日三天里，接连着解放了湖北的天门、京山、钟祥、潜江等四座县城，和从汉口到宜昌的公路上的重要据点皂市（在天门西北）。（一）解放军某部于二十日攻克天门京山两城，同日又打下皂市，缴获人枪和弹药一部，详细战果正清查中。（二）解放军另一部，于廿二日，远程袭击钟祥县城，经过几个钟头的战斗，把守城的蒋匪湖北省保安第二总队四个大队全部歼灭，除打死打伤一百多外，还活捉敌官兵一千一百余名，缴获迫击炮三门，小炮二十余门，轻重机枪三十六挺，步枪六百余支，电台一部和很多弹药。解放军只伤亡了十几个人。（三）解放军另一部于二十二日解放潜江县城，守敌闻风逃窜，现在解放军已在这个城里建立民主秩序。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十七日电】江汉军区解放军某部，二十四日进到潜江以南的直路河地区，和当地土蒋匪遭遇，经解放军勇猛追击，把这股敌人消灭，打死打伤共五十余名，活捉了八十五名，缴获轻机枪三挺，长短枪六十支，子弹两万多发，和别的一些胜利品。（附图片）
