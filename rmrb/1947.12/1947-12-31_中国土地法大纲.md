　　Outline Land Law of China
    (Adopted by the National Land Conference of the Communist Party of China on September 13, 1947)
    This is the land law formulated by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China according to the demands of all Chinese farmers. It is the basic policy of the Chinese revolution and a good policy for farmers to free themselves. All cadres and farmers must carefully study this land law and use it to fight against landlords and rich peasants.

### 中国土地法大纲

1947-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国土地法大纲
    （中国共产党全国土地会议一九四七年九月十三日通过）
    这是中共中央根据全中国农民的要求，制定的土地法。它是中国革命的基本政策，也是农民翻身斗争的好政策。无论干部与农民切须好好研究这个土地法，用来同地主富农做斗争。
    　　　　　　　　　　　华北新华书店发行
