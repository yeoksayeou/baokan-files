Title: Our Army Captures Seven Strongholds on the Outskirts of Baoding, Liberates Matou Town Southeast of Beiping
Author:
Date: 1947-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jin察冀 (Jin-Cha-Ji) 1st—A unit of the People's Liberation Army launched an offensive against the Jiang bandit strongholds on the outskirts of Baoding starting on the 17th of last month. On the 17th, they defeated more than 300 Jiang bandits who ventured out from Da Xiao Fu Village, more than 20 *li* south of Baoding. From the 18th to the 25th, they followed up their victory by capturing seven Jiang bandit strongholds including Xiao Jin Zhuang, three *li* northeast of Baoding, Dong Shi Qiao, fifteen *li* southeast of Baoding, and Da Ji Dian, Nan Yan Tong, Xiao Zhu Zhai, Fu Guan Tun, and Si Ping Zhuang within twenty *li* southwest of Baoding. Our army, active on the south bank of the Yongding River, captured Matou Town, more than eighty *li* southeast of Beiping, in one fell swoop on the 22nd. In all of the above battles, more than 220 Jiang bandit soldiers were killed or captured, and five machine guns and 150 rifles were seized.

### 保定外围我军攻克据点七处  北平东南解放马头镇

1947-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　保定外围我军攻克据点七处
    北平东南解放马头镇
    【新华社晋察冀一日电】解放军一部自上月十七日起向保定外围蒋匪据点展开攻势，十七日曾于保定南二十余里之大小福村击溃出犯之蒋匪三百余人，十八日至二十五日并乘胜攻克保定东北三里之小金庄、保定东南十五里之东石桥及保定西南二十里内之大汲店、南阎同、小祝寨、富官屯、四平庄等蒋匪据点七处。活跃于永定河南岸之我军，于二十二日一举攻克北平东南八十余里之马头镇。以上各次战斗共毙俘蒋匪军二百二十余人，缴机枪五挺，步枪一百五十枝。
