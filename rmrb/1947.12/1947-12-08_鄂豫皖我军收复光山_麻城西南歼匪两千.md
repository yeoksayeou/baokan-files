Title: Our Army in Hubei-Henan-Anhui Liberates Guangshan, Annihilates 2,000 Bandits Southwest of Macheng
Author:
Date: 1947-12-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hubei-Henan-Anhui Front, 6th: Belated news: After our troops gave the enemy's Reorganized 10th Division a severe blow at Zhanghudian, west of Guangshan, on the 27th of last month, the Chiang bandit security regiment and part of the Reorganized 10th Division entrenched in Guangshan abandoned the city and fled west on the 28th, and the city was recovered by us.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hubei-Henan-Anhui Front, 6th: On the 3rd, the People's Liberation Army completely annihilated two large detachments and eight companies of bandit puppet security forces, totaling more than 2,000 men, at Songbu, 50 li southwest of Macheng. After we recovered Macheng on the 27th of last month, the enemy, led by the puppet county magistrate, fled to defend Songbu. On the 3rd, we launched a general offensive against Songbu, and completely annihilated the enemy in two hours. The puppet county magistrate of Macheng was captured alive, and two vehicles were seized. Detailed battle results are still being investigated.

### 鄂豫皖我军收复光山　麻城西南歼匪两千

1947-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　鄂豫皖我军收复光山
  　麻城西南歼匪两千
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线六日电】迟到消息：上月二十七日我某部于光山以西张胡店给敌整十师痛击后，盘踞光山之蒋匪保安团及整十师一部于二十八日弃城向西逃窜，该城即为我收复。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线六日电】人民解放军三日于麻城西南五十里之宋埠全部歼灭匪伪保安队两个大队、八个中队共两千余人。上月二十七日我收复麻城后，该敌由伪县长率领窜守宋埠，三日我向宋埠发动总攻，两小时即全部歼灭该敌，麻城伪县长被活捉，并缴获汽车两辆，详细战果正清查中。
