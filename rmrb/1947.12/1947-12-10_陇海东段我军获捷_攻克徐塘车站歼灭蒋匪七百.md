Title: Our Army Achieves Victory in the Eastern Section of the Longhai Railway; Captures Xutang Station and Annihilates 700 Chiang Kai-shek Bandits
Author:
Date: 1947-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 8th: The Xia Ren Regiment of the Binhai Military Sub-district, in coordination with the Special Service Battalion of the Sub-district and the armed forces of the Donghai area, captured Xutang Station on the Longhai Railway and 64 surrounding important strongholds on the morning of November 28th, liberating over a hundred villages. Now, the entire Longhai Railway, from Baitabu in the east to Xin'anzhen in the west, is under our control, except for A'hu Station. In less than half a day of offensive operations, a total of 699 enemies and puppet district and township personnel were annihilated, including 392 officers and soldiers below the rank of township head of the puppet Donghai Eighth District, who were captured alive. Furthermore, we repelled the reinforcements of four divisions and one battalion of Chiang Kai-shek bandits from Xin'anzhen, killing over 30 of them.

### 陇海东段我军获捷  攻克徐塘车站歼灭蒋匪七百

1947-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　陇海东段我军获捷
    攻克徐塘车站歼灭蒋匪七百
    【新华社华东八日电】滨海军分区夏任团配合分区特务营、东海地区武装于十一月二十八日上午连克陇海路徐塘车站及周围重要据点六十四处，解放村庄百余，现陇海路东起白塔埠西至新安镇，除阿湖车站外，已全部被我控制。攻势不到半天，共歼敌及伪区乡人员六百九十九名，内生俘伪东海八区区乡镇长以下官兵三百九十二名，并击退新安镇蒋匪四个师一个营兵力之增援、毙其三十余人。
