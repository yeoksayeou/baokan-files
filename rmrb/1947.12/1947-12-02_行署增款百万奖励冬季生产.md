Title: Administrative Office Increases Funds by One Million to Reward Winter Production
Author:
Date: 1947-12-02
Page: 2
　　
　　The Administrative Office Increases Funds by One Million to Reward Winter Production
　　[This newspaper's Taiyue dispatch, 30th] The Administrative Office, in order to reward winter production, has, in addition to the previously decided winter production bonus of one million yuan, recently added another one million five hundred thousand yuan; and instructed each special administrative office to distribute it with emphasis according to the different situations of each county. At the same time, in order to more effectively promote the role of the bonus and achieve timely rewards, the medals and certificates of merit are entrusted by the Administrative Office to each special administrative office for production, and used for summary rewards.

### 行署增款百万奖励冬季生产

1947-12-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　行署增款百万奖励冬季生产
    【本报太岳三十日电】行署为奖励冬季生产，除前已决定之冬季生产奖金一百万元外，顷又增加一百五十万元；并指令各专署根据各县不同情况，有重点的进行分配。同时为更有效的发扬奖金的作用，作到及时奖励，因此奖章与奖状由行署委托各专署代制，作为总结奖励之用。
