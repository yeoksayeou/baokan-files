Title: South of the Jidong Beining Railway: We Capture the Important Town of Benchang
Author:
Date: 1947-12-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jidong, 18th. Our local armed forces in Jidong launched a large-scale sabotage campaign on various sections of the Beining Railway. Following the recovery of Leting, on the morning of the 12th, they also recovered Benchang Town (20 kilometers northwest of Leting) south of the Beining Railway, as well as the three strongholds of Tinghe and Shigezhuang on the Luan (County) - Leting Highway. The Chiang-Kai Shek-puppet bandits entrenched in the above three locations, together with the remnant bandits in Leting City, fled to Luancheng.

### 冀东北宁路南  我克奔城重镇

1947-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    冀东北宁路南
    我克奔城重镇
    【新华社冀东十八日电】冀东我地方武装向北宁路各段展开大破击，继收复乐亭后，十二日上午又收复北宁路南之奔城镇（乐亭西北二十公里）及滦（城）乐（亭）公路上之汀河、石各庄等三据点。盘踞上述三地之蒋伪匪，与乐亭城残匪同时向滦城逃窜。
