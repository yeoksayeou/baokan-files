Title: Strengthening Unified Command of All Forces: Inner Mongolia People's Self-Defense Army Officially Establishes Headquarters
Author:
Date: 1947-12-12
Page: 1

    Strengthening Unified Command of All Forces
    Inner Mongolia People's Self-Defense Army Officially Establishes Headquarters

    [Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 10th] To strengthen the unified command and political work of all units of the Inner Mongolia People's Self-Defense Army, the Inner Mongolia Communist Party Working Committee decided on November 26th to formally establish the Inner Mongolia People's Self-Defense Army Headquarters and Political Department. Yun Ze, Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Government, will serve as Commander and Political Commissar, Asigen and Wang Zaitian will serve as Deputy Commanders, and Fang Zhida will serve as Director of the Political Department. The former An Army Division and the Military Department of the Autonomous Government have been decided to be abolished. All their business and work will be incorporated into the Inner Mongolia People's Self-Defense Army Headquarters and Political Department.

### 加强各部队的统一指挥  内蒙人民自卫军正式成立司令部

1947-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    加强各部队的统一指挥
    内蒙人民自卫军正式成立司令部
    【新华社东北十日电】内蒙古共产党工作委员会为加强内蒙人民自卫军各部队之统一指挥与政治工作，于十一月二十六日决定正式成立内蒙古人民自卫军司令部及政治部。由内蒙自治政府主席云泽任司令兼政治委员，阿思根、王再天任副司令员，方知达任政治部主任。前与安军区及自治政府之军事部已决定取消。其一切业务与工作均并入内蒙古人民自卫军司令部及政治部。
