Title: The People's Liberation Army on Hainan Island Has Now Grown to 30,000

Author:

Date: 1947-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 1st. The strength of the People's Liberation Army under General Feng Baiju on Hainan Island is growing daily. According to the Tianjin Yishi Daily on November 16, the number of troops has now reached 30,000 (according to the Shanghai "Wen Cong", it was more than 16,000 in April this year), and 2.5 million people are living under the democratic regime. With the exception of cities, which are besieged by Chiang Kai-shek's bandits, the vast rural areas are controlled by the People's Liberation Army. Also: The people's armed forces active in the Dongjiang and Beijiang areas of Guangdong Province now possess various weapons such as "thunder boats", "long dragon speedboats", machine guns, and steel cannons. Their momentum is huge. Since the 14th of last month, Chiang Kai-shek's Dongjiang and Beijiang ships have been forced to stop operations under the attack of the people's armed forces.

### 海南岛人民解放军现已发展到三万人

1947-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　海南岛人民解放军现已发展到三万人
    【新华社山东一日电】海南岛冯白驹将军所部之人民解放军实力日益壮大。据十一月十六日天津益世报载称：该部人数目前已达三万人（按前据上海“文丛”载称，今年四月间为一万六千余人），在民主政权下生活之人民已有二百五十万人。蒋匪除困守城市外，广大农村均为人民解放军所控制。又息：广东境内活跃于东江、北江沿岸之人民武装，现拥有“雷艇”、“长龙快艇”、机枪、钢炮等各种武器，声势浩大，自上月十四日起，蒋匪东江、北江之轮船，已在人民武装之袭击下被迫停驶。
