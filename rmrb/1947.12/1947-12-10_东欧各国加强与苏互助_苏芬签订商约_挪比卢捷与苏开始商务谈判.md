Title: Eastern European Countries Strengthen Cooperation with the Soviet Union; Soviet-Finnish Trade Agreement Signed; Norway, Belgium, Luxembourg, and Czechoslovakia Begin Trade Negotiations with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1947-12-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 5th: Moscow dispatch: The Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade and the Finnish trade delegation, following recent negotiations, signed a trade agreement in Moscow on the 1st. Both sides agreed to vigorously develop and strengthen trade relations between the two countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit. The two countries also signed a barter agreement at the same time, stipulating the types and quantities of goods to be exchanged in 1948, as well as the exchange of certain goods in 1949. The Soviet Union has agreed to Finland's request for food, feed, metals, fertilizers, and other goods, in exchange for timber, building materials, fiber paper, and other items from Finland. Another report: Trade delegations from Norway, Belgium, Luxembourg, and Czechoslovakia have recently arrived in Moscow one after another and begun trade negotiations with the Soviet authorities. The Czechoslovak government announced in Prague on the 2nd that Stalin, at the request of Czech Prime Minister Gottwald, has agreed to provide Czechoslovakia with another 200,000 tons of wheat before April next year.

### 东欧各国加强与苏互助　苏芬签订商约  挪比卢捷与苏开始商务谈判

1947-12-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　东欧各国加强与苏互助
  　苏芬签订商约
    挪比卢捷与苏开始商务谈判
    【新华社陕北五日电】莫斯科讯：苏联对外贸易部与芬兰贸易代表团，最近谈判结果，已于一日在莫斯科签订商务条约。双方同意在平等互惠之基础上，大力发展并加强两国贸易关系。两国并于同时签订换货协定，规定一九四八年之换货种类与数量，以及一九四九年若干货物之交换。苏联已同意芬兰要求予以粮食、饲料、金属、肥料及其他货物，而由芬兰以木材、建筑材料、纤维纸张等与之交换。另讯：挪威、比利时、卢森堡、捷克等国商务代表团最近相继抵达莫斯科，与苏当局开始商务谈判。捷克政府二日则于布拉格宣布：斯大林应捷总理哥特瓦尔德之请，已允于明年四月前再行援助捷克小麦二十万吨。
