Title: Guerrilla Corps on Various Lines in Western Henan Annihilate Over 700 Bandits; Our Army in Eastern Hubei Recovers Macheng
Author:
Date: 1947-12-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Front Lines of Henan, Hubei and Shaanxi, December 5th: In the middle of last month, guerrilla corps on various lines annihilated more than 770 bandits. Guerrilla units active in the Luanchuan area, 100 li southeast of Lushi, annihilated the remnants of the Kuomintang Lushi County government on the 21st of last month, killing and capturing 50 each. Furthermore, the East Line Guerrilla Corps repelled an invasion by a battalion of the 3rd Brigade of the Kuomintang's 3rd Reorganized Division twice on the 12th of last month at Hongshishan, 35 li southeast of Baofeng, killing and wounding over 50 enemies. On the 14th, another unit captured over 30 remnant bandits with their weapons in the suburbs of Linru. On the 17th, they captured Xiadian Town, 50 li north of Linru, killing and wounding over 90 Kuomintang bandits from Linru under the command of Wang Wenzheng, and capturing over 500 alive.

Xinhua News Agency, Front Lines of Hubei, Henan and Anhui, December 5th: Late news: The People's Liberation Army recovered Macheng for the third time on the 27th of last month. The defending enemy, a regiment of the 85th Division and local bandits, abandoned the city and fled southwest. We seized a portion of military supplies.

### 豫西各线游击兵团  歼匪七百余名  鄂东我军收复麻城

1947-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　豫西各线游击兵团
    歼匪七百余名
    鄂东我军收复麻城
    【新华社豫鄂陕前线五日电】上月中旬各线游击兵团歼匪七百七十余名。活跃于卢氏东南一百里滦川地区游击队，上月二十一日歼蒋卢氏县府残顽，毙俘各五十名。又东线游击兵团上月十二日在宝丰东南三十五里红石山，两次击退蒋匪整三师第三旅一个营的进犯，毙伤敌五十余名。十四日另一部在临汝城郊捕获残匪人枪三十余。十七日攻克临汝城北五十里夏店镇，毙伤临汝蒋匪司令王文正以下九十余人，生俘五百余名。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线五日电】迟到消息：解放军于上月二十七日三次收复麻城，守敌八五师一个团及土顽弃城向西南逃窜，我缴获军用品一部。
