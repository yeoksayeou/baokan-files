Radio Broadcasts Family Letters to Military Dependents

    To further serve the southward counter-offensive army, this station will broadcast family letters on behalf of military dependents starting January 1, 1948. Military dependents are requested to send their written letters (please specify the recipient's name and military unit number, as well as the sender's name and address) to the People's Daily, addressed to the editorial department of this station.

    　　　　　　　　　　Editorial Department of Handan Xinhua Broadcasting Station

### 电邮军属家信
邯郸新华广播电台编辑部
1947-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　电邮军属家信
    本台为了进一步为南下反攻大军服务，从一九四八年一月一日起，代替军属们播送家信，请各军属们把写好的家信（请写明收信人姓名及部队番号，发信人姓名及住址），寄人民日报社转本台编辑部即可。
    　　　　　　　　　　邯郸新华广播电台编辑部
