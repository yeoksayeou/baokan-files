Title: Inner Mongolia People's Self-Defense Army Captures Tongjiangkou, Annihilating Over a Hundred Bandits
Author:
Date: 1947-12-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 14th: Belated news: On November 29th, the Inner Mongolia People's Self-Defense Army, in coordination with the Northeast Democratic Allied Army, captured Tongjiangkou, northwest of Tieling. They killed, wounded, and captured over 150 Chiang bandit New Sixth Army 22nd Division soldiers, including Company Commander Liu Zhimin, and seized a portion of military supplies.

### 内蒙人民自卫军  克通江口歼匪百余

1947-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    内蒙人民自卫军
    克通江口歼匪百余
    【新华社东北十四日电】迟到消息：内蒙人民自卫军于十一月二十九日配合东北民主军作战中，攻克铁岭西北之通江口，毙伤俘蒋匪新六军二十二师连长刘志敏以下一百五十余名，缴获军用品一部。
