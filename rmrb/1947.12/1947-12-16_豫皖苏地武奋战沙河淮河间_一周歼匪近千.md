Title: Local Armed Forces of Henan-Anhui-Jiangsu Fight Valiantly Between the Shahe and Huaihe Rivers: Nearly 1,000 Bandits Annihilated in One Week
Author:
Date: 1947-12-16
Page: 1

    Local Armed Forces of Henan-Anhui-Jiangsu Fight Valiantly Between the Shahe and Huaihe Rivers
    Nearly 1,000 Bandits Annihilated in One Week

    [Special Dispatch from This Newspaper] The local armed forces of Henan-Anhui-Jiangsu have achieved remarkable results in bandit suppression between the Shahe and Huaihe rivers. The Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Military Region has issued a special commendation. The Chiang bandits are organizing puppet bandits everywhere in the area between the Shahe and Huaihe rivers in the Henan-Anhui-Jiangsu region. In the Linquan and Xincai areas alone, there are more than 5,000 men with guns. Our Henan-Anhui-Jiangsu Military Region's armed forces launched a bandit suppression campaign against the puppet bandits from November 23rd to 29th. In one week, they fought five battles, killing 42 enemies, wounding more than 50, capturing 841 prisoners, seizing 2 light machine guns, 965 rifles, more than 50 short guns, 62 head of livestock, more than 4,000 rounds of ammunition, 12 bicycles, and more than 6.9 million in legal tender.

### 豫皖苏地武奋战沙河淮河间  一周歼匪近千

1947-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    豫皖苏地武奋战沙河淮河间
    一周歼匪近千
    【本报特讯】豫皖苏我地方部队于沙河淮河间剿匪战绩卓著，晋冀鲁豫军区特通令表扬。蒋匪在豫皖苏区沙河与淮河之间地区到处组织伪匪，仅临泉、新蔡地区即有人枪五千余，我豫皖苏军区武装于十一月廿三日至二十九日一周间向伪匪清剿，共作战五次，毙敌四十二人，击伤五十余人，俘虏八百四十一人，缴轻机枪二挺，步枪九百六十五支，短枪五十余支，牲口六十二头，子弹四千余发，自行车十二辆，法币六百九十余万元。
