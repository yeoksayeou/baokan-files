Title: Our Army Annihilates Over a Thousand Bandits in Luoshan, Tongbai, and Other Areas; Local Armed Forces in Hubei and Henan Recover Yingshan

Author:

Date: 1947-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hubei-Henan-Anhui Front, 28th: On the 13th, west of Liu'an at Yaomudian, the Liberation Army repelled three attacks by Chiang Kai-shek's 46th Division, killing and wounding over 50 enemy soldiers. Furthermore, a unit of our main force, on the 14th, in the area east of Beixiangdian, southeast of Luoshan, struck the invading Chiang Kai-shek's 11th Division, killing over 100 enemy soldiers. On the 16th, our army again repelled several attacks by Chiang Kai-shek's 48th Division in the Renheji area, southeast of Huangchuan, killing and wounding over 300 enemy soldiers.

Xinhua News Agency, Hubei-Henan-Anhui Front, 28th: Delayed news: A unit of the Liberation Army active in the Tongbai Military Region completely annihilated over 700 enemy soldiers, local despots, and bandits defending the Tongbai County seat on the 17th. Over 80 were killed, and over 600, including the Chiang Kai-shek county magistrate and the security regiment commander, were captured alive. Nine light and heavy machine guns, over 500 rifles, and a portion of other military supplies were seized.

Xinhua News Agency, Hubei-Henan-Anhui Front, 28th: Delayed news: On the 16th, the Hubei-Henan Military Region troops captured Yingshan County seat for the fourth time, and the defending enemy fled in panic.

### 罗山桐柏等地我军歼匪千余　鄂豫地武收复英山

1947-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　罗山桐柏等地我军歼匪千余
  　鄂豫地武收复英山
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十八日电】解放军十三日，在六安以西之姚木店，击退蒋匪四六师之三次进攻，毙伤敌五十余名，另我主力一部，十四日在罗山东南之北向店以东地区，打击东犯之蒋匪十一师，毙敌百余名，十六日我军复在潢川东南任和集地区击退蒋匪四十八师数次进攻，毙伤敌三百余名。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十八日电】迟到消息：活动于桐柏军区之解放军某部，于十七日攻克桐柏县城时，把守敌土蒋匪七百余人全部歼灭。打死八十余人，活捉蒋匪县长保安团长以下六百余人，缴获轻重机枪九挺，长短枪五百余支，其他军用品一部。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十八日电】迟到消息：鄂豫军区部队，十六日第四次攻克英山县城，守敌狼狈逃窜。
