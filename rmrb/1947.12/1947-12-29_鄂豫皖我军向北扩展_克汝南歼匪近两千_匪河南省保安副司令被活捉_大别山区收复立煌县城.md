Title: Our Eyuwan Army Expands Northward: Captures Runan, Eliminates Nearly 2,000 Bandits; Bandit Henan Province Deputy Commander of Security Captured Alive; Dabie Mountain Area Recovers Lihuang County Seat
Author:
Date: 1947-12-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Eyuwan Front, 27th: The People's Liberation Army captured Runan city on the 24th, and the enemy defending the city was completely wiped out. More than 100 members of Chiang Kai-shek's Henan provincial troops were killed or wounded, and 1,725 people, including Chiang Kai-shek's Henan Province Deputy Commander of Security Wen Hanqing, were captured alive. Our army seized 9 mortars, 34 light and heavy machine guns, 2,624 rifles and pistols, 3 radio stations, 6 cars, and hundreds of thousands of rounds of ammunition.

Xinhua News Agency, Eyuwan Front, 26th: Belated news: The People's Liberation Army recovered Lihuang County on the 18th, and the enemy defending it fled. Furthermore, another unit of our army defeated four squads of enemy security regiments near Yingshan from the 15th to the 18th, capturing more than 60 people and weapons.

### 鄂豫皖我军向北扩展　克汝南歼匪近两千　匪河南省保安副司令被活捉　大别山区收复立煌县城

1947-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　鄂豫皖我军向北扩展
  　克汝南歼匪近两千
  　匪河南省保安副司令被活捉
  　大别山区收复立煌县城
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十七日电】解放军二十四日攻克汝南城，守城的敌人被消灭净光。蒋匪河南省团队一百多人，被打死或打伤，蒋匪河南省保安副司令温汉卿以下一千七百二十五名被活捉，我军缴获迫击炮九门，轻重机枪三十四挺，长短枪两千六百二十四支，电台三部，汽车六辆，子弹十几万发。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十六日电】迟到消息：人民解放军于十八日收复立煌县城，守敌逃窜。又我军另部于十五日至十八日在英山附近击溃敌保安团四个小队，俘获人枪六十余。
