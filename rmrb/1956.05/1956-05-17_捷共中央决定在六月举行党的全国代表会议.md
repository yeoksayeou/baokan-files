Title: CPC Central Committee of Czechoslovakia Decides to Hold National Congress of the Party in June
Author:
Date: 1956-05-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th. According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia has decided to hold the National Congress of the Party on June 11th.

### 捷共中央决定在六月举行党的全国代表会议

1956-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共中央决定在六月举行党的全国代表会议
    新华社布拉格16日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会决定，在六月十一日举行党的全国代表会议。
