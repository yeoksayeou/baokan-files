Title: Japanese Government Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-05-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow Dispatch: According to TASS, the Japanese government delegation arrived in Moscow on April 27th. The delegation will participate in negotiations between the Soviet Union and Japan on fishing in the northwest Pacific and on rescuing ships and personnel in distress at sea.
　　The delegation is led by Japanese Minister of Agriculture and Forestry Kono and Japanese Ambassador to Canada, Matsudaira.

### 日本政府代表团到莫斯科

1956-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    日本政府代表团到莫斯科
    新华社讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：日本政府代表团在四月二十七日到达莫斯科。代表团将参加苏日两国关于在太平洋西北海面捕鱼以及援救海上遇难船只和人员问题的谈判。
    率领代表团的是日本农林相河野和日本驻加拿大大使松平。
