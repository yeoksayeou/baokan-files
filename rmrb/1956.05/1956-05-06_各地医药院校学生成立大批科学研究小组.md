Title: Large Numbers of Scientific Research Groups Established by Medical and Pharmaceutical College Students Across the Country
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-06
Page: 3

The fourth-year students of the Pharmaceutical Analysis Department of the East China Pharmaceutical College in Nanjing recently designed an improved analytical method for the proprietary medicine "You San Tong" (analgesic) in their scientific research activities. They provided valuable technical data for the Nanjing Municipal Health Bureau's drug inspection office in this testing work.

Currently, more than ten higher medical and pharmaceutical colleges in Beijing, Shanghai, Hebei, and other places have established a total of ninety-four student scientific research groups, with more than one thousand four hundred students participating in research activities.

The research methods of student scientific research groups generally involve making comprehensive scientific reports after reading literature, as well as conducting special studies in conjunction with course content. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 各地医药院校学生成立大批科学研究小组

1956-05-06
第3版()
专栏：

    各地医药院校学生成立大批科学研究小组
    设在南京的华东药学院分鉴系四年级学生最近在科学研究活动中设计出一个解决成药“优散痛”（止痛剂）的较好的分析方法，给南京市卫生局药品检验所在这项检验工作中提供了有参考价值的技术资料。
    现在北京、上海、河北等地十多个高等医、药院校建立的学生科学研究小组共有九十四个，参加研究活动的学生共有一千四百多人。
    学生科学研究小组的研究方式，一般是通过阅读文献后作综合性的科学报告，以及结合课程内容进行专题研究。　（据新华社讯）
