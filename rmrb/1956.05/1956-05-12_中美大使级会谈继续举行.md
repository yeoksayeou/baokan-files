Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1956-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 11th - The Sino-US ambassadorial talks held their forty-sixth meeting at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 am on May 11th. The meeting adjourned at 11:25 am. The next meeting will be held on Thursday, May 17th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦11日电　中美两国大使级会谈五月十一日上午十时在国联大厦举行第四十六次会议，会议在十一时二十五分休会，下次会议将在五月十七日（星期四）举行。
