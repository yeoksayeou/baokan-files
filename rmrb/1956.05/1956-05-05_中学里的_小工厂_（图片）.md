Page: 3

　　The "Small Factory" in Middle School
    The No. 1 Middle School in Neijiang City, Sichuan Province, has established a "small factory." This "small factory" operates under the help and guidance of the school administration and the Young Pioneers organization. The small factory has four workshops: motor, chemistry, model, and gliding, which are specifically for young people to carry out various scientific activities in their spare time. This is a picture of the "little workers" in the model workshop making models of trains and steamboats.
                                  Photo by Li Xuan (Sichuan Daily)

### 中学里的“小工厂”（图片）
李煊
1956-05-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　中学里的“小工厂”
    四川省内江市第一中学成立了一个“小工厂”，这个“小工厂”是在学校行政和少年先锋队组织的帮助和指导下进行工作的。小工厂内设有电机、化学、模型、滑翔等四个车间，专供青少年们在课余时间进行各种科学活动。这是模型车间的“小工人”们正在制造火车和汽船的模型。
                                  李煊摄（四川日报社稿）
