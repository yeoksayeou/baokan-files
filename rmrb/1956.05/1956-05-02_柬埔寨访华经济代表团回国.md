Title: Cambodian Economic Delegation to China Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-05-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 1st. The four-member economic delegation from the Kingdom of Cambodia to China, led by Head of Delegation Hu Sen'a, left Guangzhou this morning via Shenzhen to return to their country via Hong Kong. Before coming to Guangzhou, the economic delegation from the Kingdom of Cambodia to China visited Shanghai, Hangzhou, Wuhan, and other cities.

### 柬埔寨访华经济代表团回国

1956-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨访华经济代表团回国
    据新华社广州1日电　柬埔寨王国访华经济代表团四人，由团长胡森阿率领，一日早晨离广州经深圳取道香港返国。柬埔寨王国访华经济代表团在来广州之前曾经先后到上海、杭州和武汉等城市参观游览。
