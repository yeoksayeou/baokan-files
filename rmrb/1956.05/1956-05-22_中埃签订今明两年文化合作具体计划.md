Title: China and Egypt Sign Specific Plans for Cultural Cooperation for the Next Two Years
Author:
Date: 1956-05-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 21st. China and Egypt signed specific plans for cultural cooperation for 1956 and 1957 in Cairo on May 20th. This plan was formulated to implement the recently concluded cultural cooperation agreement between China and Egypt. The plan stipulates the items concerning cultural exchange and cooperation that both parties should implement during this period.

### 中埃签订今明两年文化合作具体计划

1956-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中埃签订今明两年文化合作具体计划
    新华社开罗21日电　中国和埃及五月二十日在开罗签订了1956年和1957年的文化合作具体计划。这个计划是为执行中国和埃及之间新近缔结的文化合作协定而拟订的。计划规定了双方应当在这个时期执行的关于文化交流和合作的项目。
