Title: Dehler Advocates East-West German Discussions on a Pan-German Referendum to Find Ways to Eliminate Germany's Division
Author:
Date: 1956-05-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 30th: Bonn dispatch: The chairman of the West German Free Democratic Party, Dehler, advocated holding a pan-German referendum on the issue of a reunified Germany joining a European security system. He pointed out: This security system must include the Soviet Union.

　　Dehler made these remarks in response to questions from a "Frankfurter Rundschau" reporter. The newspaper published Dehler's remarks on May 29th.

　　Dehler also said: It is necessary to discuss the issue of holding this pan-German referendum with the German Democratic Republic. "On the issue of reunification, all German people should have a say."

　　Dehler said that a way must be found to eliminate Germany's division.

　　If the West German government cannot do this, then any Bonn parliamentarian with a sense of responsibility must participate in the research to clarify this situation.

### 戴勒主张东西德讨论全德公民投票问题  找出办法来消除德国的分裂

1956-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    戴勒主张东西德讨论全德公民投票问题
    找出办法来消除德国的分裂
    新华社柏林30日电　波恩消息：西德自由民主党主席戴勒主张就一个重新统一起来的德国加入欧洲安全体系问题举行全德公民投票。他指出：这个安全体系必须把苏联包括在内。
    戴勒是在回答“法兰克福评论”记者的问题的时候这样说的。这家报纸在五月二十九日发表了戴勒的谈话。
    戴勒还说：必须同德意志民主共和国一起讨论举行这个全德公民投票的问题。“在重新统一的问题上，全体德国人民都应该有发言权。”
    戴勒说，一定要找出一个办法来消除德国的分裂。
    如果西德政府做不到这一点，那么任何一个具有一点责任感的波恩议员必须参加研究来弄清这种局势。
