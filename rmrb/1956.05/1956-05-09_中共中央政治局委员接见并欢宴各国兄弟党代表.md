Title: Members of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau Meet and Host Banquet for Representatives of Fraternal Parties from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-05-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: Members of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Kang Sheng, Peng Zhen, Dong Biwu, Peng Dehuai, and Deng Xiaoping, at 6 PM on the 8th, met with and hosted a banquet for the representatives of fraternal parties from various countries who, after attending the Third Congress of the Korean Workers' Party, were making a short visit in Beijing on their way home. These included: Haouchowski, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Polish United Workers' Party; Federovski, Minister of the Machine Manufacturing Industry of the People's Republic of Poland; Fazekas, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party; Constantin Lazarescu, staff member of the Central Committee; Vig, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party; Cobol, Member of the Central Committee; Wünsche, Member of the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party; Hanneck, Member of the Central Committee; Plaku, Director of the Political Bureau of the Albanian People's Army; Damdinjav, Member of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; and Lamjav, Alternate Member of the Central Committee.

Present at the reception were the Ambassadors to China from Romania, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, Albania, and Mongolia, Gogu, Skalaidan, Gipner, Nase, and Ochirbat, as well as the Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, Majman.

Also present at the reception were members of the CPC Central Committee, Tan Zhenlin and Wang Jiaxiang, and responsible staff members of the CPC Central Committee, Zhao Yimin, Li Chuli, Li Qixin, and Xiong Fu.

Also present at the reception were the Chinese Ambassadors to Romania, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, and Mongolia, Ke Bonian, Hao Deqing, Zeng Yongquan, and He Ying, as well as Yu Zhan, Counselor of the Embassy in Poland, and Wang Dong, First Secretary of the Embassy in Albania.

### 中共中央政治局委员接见并欢宴各国兄弟党代表

1956-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央政治局委员接见并欢宴各国兄弟党代表
    新华社8日讯  中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、康生、彭真、董必武、彭德怀、邓小平，在八日下午六时，接见并设宴招待了参加朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会以后经北京回国作短期访问的波兰统一工人党政治局候补委员海乌霍夫斯基、波兰人民共和国机器制造工业部部长费德尔斯基，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会书记法泽卡斯、中央委员会工作人员康斯坦丁·拉扎雷斯库，匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会书记维格、中央委员科博尔，德国统一社会党中央委员温舍、中央委员汉奈克，阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治局长普拉库，蒙古人民革命党中央委员达姆京扎布、候补中央委员拉姆扎布。
    接见时在座的有罗马尼亚、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、阿尔巴尼亚和蒙古的驻中国大使郭佐文、斯克拉丹、纪普纳、纳赛、奥其尔巴特和波兰临时代办马伊曼。
    接见时在座的还有中共中央委员谭震林、王稼祥和中共中央负责工作人员赵毅敏、李初梨、李启新、熊复。
    接见时在座的还有中国驻罗马尼亚、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、蒙古等国的大使柯柏年、郝德清、曾涌泉、何英和驻波兰大使馆参赞余湛、驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆一等秘书王栋。
