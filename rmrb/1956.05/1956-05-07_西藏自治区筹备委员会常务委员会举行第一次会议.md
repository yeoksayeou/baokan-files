Title: Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region Holds First Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 6th - The Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region held its first meeting this afternoon. The meeting was presided over by the Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, the Dalai Lama, the First Vice Chairman, Bainqen Erdini, and the Second Vice Chairman, Zhang Guohua.

At the beginning of the meeting, according to the traditional customs of the Tibetan people, the standing committee members attending the meeting and the responsible cadres of the departments under the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, who attended the meeting as nonvoting delegates, all ate butter tea and ginseng fruit rice, which symbolize auspiciousness.

Then, the Dalai Lama made a report on the proposals for the first meeting. His report covered the following six issues: 1. In order to expand the publicity of the great significance of the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and the arrival of the central delegation in Tibet, and to strengthen the relationship between the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and the people of all social strata, the resolutions of the inaugural meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region should be widely and thoroughly publicized and conveyed. 2. Establish and improve the administrative organs of various departments of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region. The Tibet Local Government, the Panchen Kampotse Conference Hall Committee, and the Qamdo Area People's Liberation Committee should immediately submit the list of cadres for each administrative organ, and request the central government to send cadres to assist in the work. 3. Complete some of the basic construction of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region itself within this year. Survey six new highway routes in various parts of Tibet. Give strong assistance to various construction projects undertaken by the central government in Tibet and resource exploration. 4. In order to adapt to the growing needs of various tasks in Tibet, it is necessary to rapidly and vigorously train cadres and train the original monastic and secular officials and cadres. It is planned to train 2,000 cadres in the Tibet Local Cadre School this year. 5. In order to improve the lives of the Tibetan people, after the Kangzang and Qingzang highways are opened to traffic and inland materials are supplied to Tibet, appropriate measures should be studied and taken to reduce prices, so as to facilitate the construction of various tasks in Tibet. 6. The Tibet Local Government, the Panchen Kampotse Conference Hall Committee, the Qamdo Area People's Liberation Committee, and the various departments, committees, and offices of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region should immediately submit plans and programs based on their own business work and submit them to the Standing Committee for approval and implementation.

The standing committee members attending the meeting unanimously supported the Dalai Lama's report. Many people put forward active supplementary opinions at the meeting.

At today's meeting, the standing committee members of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region raised their hands and passed the six proposals put forward by the Dalai Lama.

### 西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员会举行第一次会议

1956-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员会举行第一次会议
    新华社拉萨6日电　西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员会今天下午举行第一次会议。这次会议是由西藏自治区筹备委员会主任委员达赖喇嘛、第一副主任委员班禅额尔德尼、第二副主任委员张国华主持的。
    会议开始时，按照西藏民族的传统习惯，出席会议的常务委员和列席会议的西藏自治区筹备委员会所属各部门负责干部，都吃了象征吉祥的酥油茶和人参果米饭。
    接着，达赖喇嘛就第一次会议的议案作了报告，他的报告共谈了以下六个问题：一、为扩大西藏自治区筹备委员会成立及中央代表团来藏的重大意义的宣传，密切西藏自治区筹备委员会与各阶层人民的关系，应将西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会的决议进行普遍的、深入的宣传和传达。二、建立和健全西藏自治区筹备委员会各部门的办事机构。西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会和昌都地区人民解放委员会应即提出各办事机构的干部名单，并请中央酌派帮助工作的干部。三、在今年内完成部分西藏自治区筹备委员会本身的基本建设。在西藏各地勘测六条新的公路线路。对中央在西藏举办的各项建设和进行资源勘探等工作，应予大力协助。四、为适应西藏各项工作日益发展的需要，必须迅速、大力的培养干部及训练原有的僧俗官员干部，计划今年在西藏地方干部学校内训练干部两千名。五、为使西藏人民生活得到改善，在康藏、青藏公路通车后，内地物资源源供应西藏的情况下，应研究并采取适当措施，争取降低物价，以利西藏各项工作的建设。六、西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会、昌都地区人民解放委员会和西藏自治区筹备委员会的各厅、委、处立即根据本身业务工作，提出计划和方案，交常务委员会批准执行。
    出席这次会议的常务委员一致拥护达赖喇嘛的报告。许多人在会议上提出了积极的补充意见。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员们，在今天的会议上举手通过了达赖喇嘛提出的六项议案。
