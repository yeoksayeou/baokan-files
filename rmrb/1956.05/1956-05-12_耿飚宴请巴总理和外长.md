Title: Geng Biao Hosts a Banquet for Pakistani Prime Minister and Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1956-05-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, May 11th: Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Geng Biao hosted a banquet on the evening of May 10th in honor of Pakistani Prime Minister Muhammad Ali, Foreign Minister Chaudhri, and their entourage, who are about to visit China at the invitation of the Chinese government.

In addition to Prime Minister Ali and Foreign Minister Chaudhri, the banquet was attended by ministers of various departments, members of the National Assembly, high-ranking officials, diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Karachi, and prominent figures from all walks of life in Pakistan. The atmosphere of the banquet was warm and friendly.

### 耿飚宴请巴总理和外长

1956-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    耿飚宴请巴总理和外长
    新华社喀喇蚩11日电　中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚五月十日晚上设宴招待即将应中国政府的邀请去中国访问的巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里、外交部长乔德里和他们的随行人员。
    出席宴会的除了阿里总理和乔德里外交部长以外，还有各部部长、国民议会议员、高级官员、各国驻喀喇蚩外交使节以及巴基斯坦各界名流。宴会的气氛是热烈友好的。
