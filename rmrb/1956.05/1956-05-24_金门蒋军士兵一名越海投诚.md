Title: A Kuomintang Soldier from Kinmen Defects by Swimming Across the Sea

Author: 
Date: 1956-05-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, May 23rd: On the morning of the 22nd, Kuomintang soldier Rong Huaji escaped from Kinmen Island by swimming to the coast of a certain location in Jinjiang County, Fujian Province, and surrendered to the Chinese People's Liberation Army coastal defense troops.

Rong Huaji is a Private First Class in the 4th Company, 2nd Battalion, 76th Regiment, 26th Division of the Kuomintang army, and is from Anhui Province.

### 金门蒋军士兵一名越海投诚

1956-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    金门蒋军士兵一名越海投诚
    新华社福建前线23日电　二十二日早晨，蒋军士兵戎华吉逃出金门岛泅水到达福建省晋江县某地海岸，向中国人民解放军海防部队投诚。
    戎华吉是蒋军二十六师七十六团二营四连的上等兵，安徽省人。
