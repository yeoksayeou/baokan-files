Title: Ceylon Prime Minister Says He Will Visit China and the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 18th: Colombo dispatch: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike said on May 17th that he had received invitations to visit China and the Soviet Union. He will visit China and the Soviet Union after he has largely relieved the various pressing domestic problems he is currently facing.

### 锡兰总理表示将访问中国和苏联

1956-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理表示将访问中国和苏联
    新华社新德里18日电　科伦坡消息：锡兰总理班达拉奈克五月十七日说，他已经接到访问中国和苏联的邀请。他将在相当程度上摆脱了他目前所面临的各种迫切的国内问题以后访问中国和苏联。
