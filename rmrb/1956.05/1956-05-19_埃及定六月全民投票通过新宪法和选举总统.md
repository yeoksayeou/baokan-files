Title: Egypt to Hold National Referendum in June to Approve New Constitution and Elect President
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 17th (TASS): According to Egyptian newspapers, the Egyptian cabinet meeting decided to hold a national referendum on June 23rd to approve the new constitution of the Egyptian Republic and elect a president.

### 埃及定六月全民投票通过新宪法和选举总统

1956-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及定六月全民投票通过新宪法和选举总统
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社开罗十七日讯：据埃及报纸报道，埃及内阁会议决定在六月二十三日举行全民投票，通过埃及共和国新宪法和选举总统。
