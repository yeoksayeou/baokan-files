Title: Dulles Is Unfit to Be Secretary of State: Resolution of a US Labor Union Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th, Washington: The Amalgamated Clothing Workers of America, a union with 400,000 members, passed a resolution on the 26th stating that Dulles is "unfit" to continue serving as Secretary of State.

The resolution from this congress, held in Washington, stated that Dulles, "through repeated major errors," has proven that "he is unfit to hold a position that makes decisions about the fate of our nation and the fate of the world."

The congress, in a resolution on foreign policy, stated that the United States must not demand that independent countries like India express "obedience."

### 杜勒斯不配再当国务卿  美国一个工会代表大会的决议

1956-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯不配再当国务卿
    美国一个工会代表大会的决议
    新华社28日讯　华盛顿消息：拥有四十万会员的美国混合成衣工人工会代表大会二十六日通过决议，认为杜勒斯“不适宜于”继续担任国务卿职务。
    在华盛顿举行的这个大会的决议里说，杜勒斯“通过屡次的重大错误”证明，“他是不适宜于担任要作出关于我们国家的命运和世界命运的决定的职务的”。
    大会在关于外交政策的决议里说，美国不得要求像印度之类的独立国家表示“顺从”。
