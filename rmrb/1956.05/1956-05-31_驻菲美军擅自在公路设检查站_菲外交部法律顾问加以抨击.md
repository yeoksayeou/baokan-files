Title: U.S. Troops Stationed in the Philippines Set Up Checkpoints on Highways Without Authorization; Legal Advisor to the Philippine Foreign Ministry Criticizes
Author:
Date: 1956-05-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Manila: According to a France-Presse dispatch from Manila, Alegra Querrerdo, Legal Advisor to the Philippine Foreign Ministry, criticized on May 26th the American military authorities for setting up military checkpoints on some Philippine state-owned highways that cross American military bases without obtaining the consent of the Philippine authorities.

The Foreign Ministry official said: The establishment of such checkpoints by the American military authorities is illegal. He said that the establishment of these checkpoints also violates the military base agreement between the United States and the Philippines.

### 驻菲美军擅自在公路设检查站  菲外交部法律顾问加以抨击

1956-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    驻菲美军擅自在公路设检查站
    菲外交部法律顾问加以抨击
    据新华社讯　据法新社马尼拉消息：菲律宾外交部法律顾问阿雷格拉多五月二十六日抨击美国军事当局没有取得菲律宾当局的同意就在一些穿越美国军事基地的菲律宾国有公路上设立军事检查站。
    这位外交部官员说：美国军事当局设立这种检查站是非法的。他说，设立这种检查站也违反了美国和菲律宾之间的军事基地协定。
