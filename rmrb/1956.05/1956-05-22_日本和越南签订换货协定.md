Title: Japan and Vietnam Sign Barter Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-05-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 20th - According to today's report in the Vietnamese "People's Daily," the Vietnam Democratic Republic Import and Export Corporation and the Japanese trade delegation, after discussions, signed an agreement on exchanging goods on May 12th. The Japanese trade delegation consisted of representatives from major trading companies in Tokyo, who arrived in Hanoi in April.

Both sides believe that the negotiations have achieved good results and guarantee that they will work hard to overcome difficulties to correctly implement the various terms signed, and contribute to the broad development of trade relations in the future.

### 日本和越南签订换货协定

1956-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    日本和越南签订换货协定
    新华社河内20日电　据越南“人民报”今天报道：越南民主共和国进出口总公司和日本贸易代表团经过商谈以后，在五月十二日签订了一项交换货物的协定。日本贸易代表团由东京一些大贸易公司的代表组成，他们是在四月间到达河内的。
    双方都认为这次商谈已经取得良好结果，并且保证将要努力克服困难来正确实现所签订的各项条款，为今后广泛发展贸易关系作出贡献。
