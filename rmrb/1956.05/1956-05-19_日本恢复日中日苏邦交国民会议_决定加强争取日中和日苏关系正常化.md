Title: Japan's National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations Decides to Strengthen Efforts to Achieve Normalization of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-05-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 18th -- The Japan National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations held a meeting on the 17th to discuss its work policy following the signing of the Japan-Soviet Fisheries Treaty. The meeting decided that future efforts should focus on achieving the normalization of diplomatic relations between Japan and China.

The meeting also decided that, in order to break down various obstacles and realize the normalization of diplomatic relations between Japan and China as soon as possible, a joint meeting including people from a wide range of fields such as economy and culture, as well as representatives from various organizations, will be held soon to determine a unified work plan.

The meeting also decided to organize, concentrate, and mobilize the strength of the Japanese people and public opinion to urge the Japanese government to normalize diplomatic relations between Japan and the Soviet Union as soon as possible.

### 日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议  决定加强争取日中和日苏关系正常化

1956-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议
    决定加强争取日中和日苏关系正常化
    新华社18日讯　东京消息：日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议十七日召开会议，讨论日苏渔业条约签订后的工作方针。会议决定今后要着重努力争取日中两国外交关系的正常化。
    会议并且决定，为了打破种种障碍，早日实现日中两国邦交的正常化，将在最近召开一个包括经济、文化等广泛阶层人士和各种团体代表参加的联席会议，来确定统一工作的计划。
    会议还决定有组织地集中和动员日本人民和舆论的力量，敦促日本政府使日苏两国外交关系早日正常化。
