Title: Poland Builds First Television Broadcast Station
Author:
Date: 1956-05-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 2nd - Poland's first television broadcasting station opened on April 30th in the Palace of Culture and Science in Warsaw.

　　This broadcasting station allows viewers within a hundred kilometers to watch its programs, and it can also transmit performances and gatherings held in the main halls of the Palace of Culture and Science to television viewers.

　　In addition, television broadcasting stations are also under construction in Łódź and Stalinogród.

### 波兰建成第一座电视播送台

1956-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰建成第一座电视播送台
    据新华社华沙2日电　波兰第一座电视播送台四月三十日在华沙文化科学宫开幕了。
    这个播送台可以使一百公里以内的观众看到它的节目，还可以把在文化科学宫各个主要大厅里举行的表演和集会传送到电视观众眼前。
    另外，罗兹和斯大林诺格罗德也正在建设电视播送台。
