Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1956-05-08
Page: 4
    △ A comprehensive expedition team composed of zoologists, botanists, and geographers from the Soviet Academy of Sciences arrived in Beijing on the 6th. They will cooperate with scientists from the Chinese Academy of Sciences to conduct research work. This work is of great significance for studying the history of the formation of fauna and flora.
    △ A group of scientists from the German Democratic Republic, who came to cooperate with Chinese scientists in the investigation of cultivated plants and wild plants, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 7th.
    This group of scientists came to China in accordance with the 1956 implementation plan of the Sino-German cultural cooperation agreement.

### 友好往来

1956-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    △一个由苏联科学院的动物学家、植物学家和地理学家组成的综合考察队，六日到达北京。他们即将同中国科学院的科学家合作进行考察工作。这项工作对研究动植物区系形成史有重大作用。
    △前来同中国科学家合作考察栽培植物和野生植物的德意志民主共和国科学家小组，在七日乘飞机到达北京。
    这个科学家小组是根据中德文化合作协定1956年执行计划前来中国的。
