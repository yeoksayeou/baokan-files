Title: Liaoning Peking Opera Troupe Rehearses "Fifteen Strings of Cash"
Author:
Date: 1956-05-22
Page: 3

    Liaoning Peking Opera Troupe Rehearses "Fifteen Strings of Cash"
    The Peking Opera Troupe of the Liaoning Opera Theatre is adapting the Kunqu Opera "Fifteen Strings of Cash" into a Peking Opera version and began rehearsing the first scene on the 18th. Xu Juhua, the head of the Peking Opera Troupe, is directing the play, and all the main actors of the troupe are participating in the rehearsal.
    The adapted Peking Opera "Fifteen Strings of Cash" has ten scenes and is expected to be formally staged at the end of May.
                        (Xinhua News Agency)

### 辽宁京剧团排演“十五贯”

1956-05-22
第3版()
专栏：

    辽宁京剧团排演“十五贯”
    辽宁戏曲剧院京剧团把昆剧“十五贯”改编成京剧，并在十八日开始排演第一场。这个戏由京剧团团长徐菊华担任导演，该团主要演员都参加了排演工作。
    改编的“十五贯”京剧有十场，预计在五月底可以正式演出。
                        （新华社）
