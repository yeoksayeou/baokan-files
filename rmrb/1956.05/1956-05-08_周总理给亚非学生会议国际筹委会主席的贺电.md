Title: Premier Zhou's Telegram to the Chairman of the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian-African Students Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-05-08
Page: 1

　　Premier Zhou's Telegram to the Chairman of the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian-African Students Conference
　　Mr. Djolotom, Chairman of the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian-African Students Conference:
    Thank you for your letter. I welcome the convening of the Asian-African Students Conference. The Asian-African Conference held in Bandung last year expressed the common aspirations of the people of Asian and African countries to oppose colonialism, safeguard world peace, and demand friendly cooperation. I believe that the young generation of Asian and African countries will be able to better understand, learn from, and help each other. I hope that this Asian-African Students Conference will be able to make contributions in strengthening the unity and mutual assistance of students from Asian and African countries, promoting academic and cultural exchanges, and promoting the cause of peace in Asia and the world.
    I wish the conference success.
    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China
                    May 6, 1956

### 周总理给亚非学生会议国际筹委会主席的贺电

1956-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理给亚非学生会议国际筹委会主席的贺电
　　亚非学生会议国际筹备委员会主席乔尔托姆先生：
    感谢你给我的来信。我欢迎亚非学生会议的召开。去年在万隆召开的亚非会议，表达了亚非各国人民反对殖民主义、维护世界和平和要求友好合作的共同愿望。我相信，亚非各国青年的一代，一定能够更好地互相了解，互相学习和互相帮助。我希望，这次亚非学生会议，在加强亚非各国学生的团结互助、推动学术文化交流和促进亚洲和世界的和平事业等方面，将能作出贡献。
    祝贺会议的成功。
    中华人民共和国总理  周恩来
                    1956年5月6日
