Title: Twenty-Five Chiang Kai-shek Soldiers Fleeing Burma Return to Yunnan, Motherland, after Deserting Chiang's Army
Author:
Date: 1956-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 18th -- Twenty-five Chiang Kai-shek soldiers fleeing Burma, including Li Zhaosheng and Li Xiaozhuang, returned to a certain area on the Yunnan border of the motherland on May 8th. They were warmly received by the local people's government and the People's Liberation Army troops.

Li Zhaosheng and others belonged to the 1st Company, 1st Battalion, 37th Regiment of the 13th Division of Li Mi's remnant Chiang army. They deserted Chiang's army from Mengta in Burma (south of Jingdong) and returned to the motherland after more than a month. They carried with them one light and heavy machine gun, two carbines, twenty various rifles, three hand grenades, and more than 1,800 rounds of various ammunition.

On May 14th, a welcome meeting was held in a certain border area for them. The leading organs of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, in accordance with the policies and reward measures for Chiang's army uprising and surrender personnel promulgated by the Ministry of National Defense, issued bonuses to them at the meeting.

### 逃缅蒋军人员二十五人  脱离蒋军回到祖国云南

1956-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    逃缅蒋军人员二十五人
    脱离蒋军回到祖国云南
    新华社昆明18日电　逃缅蒋军人员李召生、李小壮等二十五人，在五月八日回到祖国云南边境某地。他们受到当地人民政府和解放军部队的热情接待。
    李召生等属于蒋军李弥残部十三师三十七团一营一连。他们自缅甸的勐塔（在景东以南）脱离蒋军，经过一个多月的时间返回祖国的。他们携带有轻重两用机枪一挺，卡宾枪二支，各种步枪二十支，手榴弹三枚，各种子弹一千八百多发。
    五月十四日，边境某地举行了欢迎他们的大会。中国人民解放军某部领导机关根据国防部颁布的对蒋军起义、投诚人员的政策及奖励办法，在会上发给他们奖金。
