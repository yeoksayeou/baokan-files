Sowing Double-Season Rice
　　According to the spirit of the three agricultural reforms, the Shiren Agricultural Production Cooperative of Baofeng Township, Wuhu County, Anhui Province, has changed the past situation of planting only one season of intermediate rice and expanded the planting area of double-season rice. This is a picture of the members sowing seeds.

### 播种双季稻（图片）

1956-05-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　播种双季稻
    安徽省芜湖县宝丰乡石人农业生产合作社根据农业三项改革的精神，改变过去只种一季中稻的情况，扩大了双季稻的播种面积。这是社员们正在播种。
