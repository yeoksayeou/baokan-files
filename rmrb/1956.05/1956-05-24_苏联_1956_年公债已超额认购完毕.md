Title: Soviet Union's 1956 Public Bonds Oversubscribed
Author:
Date: 1956-05-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 22nd: According to TASS, the Soviet working people enthusiastically subscribed to the 1956 National Economic Development State Bonds. As of May 21st, the Soviet people had subscribed 34,305,323,000 rubles, while the originally stipulated total issuance was 32,000,000,000 rubles.

According to the instructions of the USSR Council of Ministers, the USSR Ministry of Finance has ordered the cessation of subscriptions.

### 苏联1956年公债已超额认购完毕

1956-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联1956年公债已超额认购完毕
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科22日讯：苏联劳动人民踊跃认购1956年发展国民经济国家公债。到五月二十一日为止，苏联人民已经认购了三百四十三亿零五百三十二万三千卢布，而原来规定的发行总额是三百二十亿卢布。
    根据苏联部长会议的指示，苏联财政部已经下令停止认购。
