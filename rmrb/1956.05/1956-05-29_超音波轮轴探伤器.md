Title: Ultrasonic Wheel Axle Flaw Detector
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 2

    Ultrasonic Wheel Axle Flaw Detector
    An ultrasonic wheel axle flaw detector has begun trial use at the Jinzhou Wheel Factory.

    This ultrasonic wheel axle flaw detector was recently successfully trial-produced by the China Academy of Railway Sciences. It uses electrical indicator lines to display various abnormalities in the wheel axle, and can accurately and meticulously detect the depth and length of cracks in the wheel axle.

    The use of this inspection equipment will effectively prevent serious train accidents caused by wheel axle fractures and ensure traffic safety.
                        (Xinhua News Agency)

### 超音波轮轴探伤器

1956-05-29
第2版()
专栏：

    超音波轮轴探伤器
    一架超音波轮轴探伤器开始在锦州车轮厂试用。
    这架超音波轮轴探伤器，是由中国铁道科学研究院新近试制成功的。它是用电力指示线来显示出轮轴各种异状，能够正确细致地探出轮轴的裂纹深度和长度。
    使用这种检查器械以后，将有效地防止因轮轴折断而造成的严重列车事故，保证行车的安全。
                        （新华社）
