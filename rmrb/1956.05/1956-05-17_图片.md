The All-China Federation of Trade Unions mountaineering team, consisting of 28 athletes and 7 staff members, climbed to the highest peak of Taibai Mountain in Shaanxi Province, at an altitude of 4,113 meters, on April 25th. This mountaineering expedition is the first of its kind in China.

专栏：

    由二十八名运动员和七名工作人员组成的中华全国总工会爬山运动队，四月二十五日攀登到海拔4，113公尺的陕西省境内的太白山最高峰。这次爬山运动在中国还是第一次。
