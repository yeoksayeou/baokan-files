Title: CCP Celebrates 35th Anniversary of Founding

Author: 
Date: 1956-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 14th: The Czechoslovak Communist Party celebrated its 35th anniversary on May 14th. The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party held a celebration meeting on the 13th.

    In addition to the members of the Central Committee, veteran party members, meritorious party fighters, and representatives of party organizations from enterprises, cities, and rural areas attended the celebration meeting.

    First Secretary of the Central Committee, Novotny, delivered the opening speech. Subsequently, Political Bureau member Kopecki made a report on the glorious path of the Czechoslovak Communist Party's struggle over the past 35 years.

    On the evening of the 12th, participants in the celebration meeting watched the ballet "The Flames of Paris," performed at the Prague National Theatre in celebration of the Party's birthday.

### 捷共庆祝建党三十五周年

1956-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共庆祝建党三十五周年
    新华社布拉格14日电　五月十四日是捷克斯洛伐克共产党成立三十五周年。捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会在十三日举行了庆祝会。
    出席这个庆祝会的除了中央委员会的委员外，还有老党员、有功勋的党的战士以及企业、城市和农村的党组织的代表。
    中央委员会第一书记诺沃提尼致开会词。接着，政治局委员柯别茨基作了关于捷克斯洛伐克共产党三十五年来斗争的光荣道路的报告。
    十二日晚间，庆祝会的参加者在布拉格民族剧院观看了为庆祝党的生日而演出的巴蕾舞剧“巴黎的火焰”。
