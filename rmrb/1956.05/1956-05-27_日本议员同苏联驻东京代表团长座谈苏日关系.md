Title: Japanese Diet Members Meet with Head of Soviet Delegation in Tokyo to Discuss Soviet-Japanese Relations
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 26th: According to a report by Kyodo News, Japanese Diet members held a welcome and send-off meeting in Tokyo on the 25th with the new head of the Soviet delegation in Tokyo, Tikhvinsky, and his predecessor, Domnitsky. At the meeting, they exchanged views on issues such as Soviet-Japanese relations, focusing on the Soviet-Japanese negotiations.

Tikhvinsky stated at the meeting that the Soviet Union fully supports the restoration of peaceful relations between Japan and the Soviet Union and hopes to establish diplomatic relations between Japan and the Soviet Union as soon as possible. He pointed out that the signing of the Soviet-Japanese Fisheries Treaty and the Maritime Rescue Agreement will accelerate the improvement of Soviet-Japanese negotiations.

Diet members who have visited the Soviet Union, such as Tokutaro Kitamura and Masaru Nozoe, and relevant Diet members, attended the meeting. They expressed their hope at the meeting that they could lay the foundation for peace between Japan and the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, May 26th, Tokyo Dispatch: On May 25th, a mass rally of approximately 3,000 people was held in Hakodate, Hokkaido. The rally passed a resolution calling for the early restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and the Soviet Union.

On the previous day, mass rallies were also held in Otaru and Kushiro, Hokkaido, demanding the establishment of normal relations with the Soviet Union as soon as possible.

### 日本议员同苏联驻东京代表团长座谈苏日关系

1956-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    日本议员同苏联驻东京代表团长座谈苏日关系
    新华社26日讯　据共同社报道，日本国会议员二十五日在东京同新任苏联驻东京代表团团长季契温斯基和他的前任多穆尼茨基举行欢迎欢送座谈会。他们在座谈会上就以日苏谈判为主的两国关系问题等交换了意见。
    季契温斯基在座谈会上表示，苏联完全赞成日苏恢复和平关系，希望早日建立日苏邦交。他指出，日苏渔业条约和海上营救协定的签订，将会加速改善日苏谈判。
    参加座谈会的有北村德太郎、野沟胜等访问过苏联的国会议员和有关的国会议员。他们在座谈会上表示希望能够奠定日苏之间的和平。
    新华社26日讯　东京消息：五月二十五日，北海道函馆举行了大约有三千人参加的群众大会，大会通过决议，要求及早恢复日本同苏联的外交关系。
    在前一天，北海道的小樽和钏路也分别举行了群众大会，要求尽快同苏联建立正常关系。
