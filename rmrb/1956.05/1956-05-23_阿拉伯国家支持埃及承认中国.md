Title: Arab Countries Support Egypt's Recognition of China
Author:
Date: 1956-05-23
Page: 4

    Arab Countries Support Egypt's Recognition of China
    Sudanese Foreign Minister Expresses Support
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 21st: "News" published a message stating: The Sudanese Foreign Minister, Mubarak Zarrouk, commented on Egypt's recognition of the People's Republic of China, saying: "We Sudanese support the actions approved by Arab countries."
    Former Jordanian Prime Minister Rifai Advocates
    All Arab Countries Should Recognize China
    Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: According to TASS, Beirut, May 22nd: Former Jordanian Prime Minister Rifai told a reporter from the Lebanese "Telegraph" that all Arab countries should recognize the People's Republic of China.
    When commenting on the news of Egypt's recognition of the People's Republic of China, Rifai said: "Egypt's act of recognizing the People's Republic of China is the act of a truly independent and autonomous country. I believe that all the achievements Egypt has made in its foreign policy are also the achievements of the entire Arab world."
    Arab League Political Committee Passes Recommendation
    Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: According to TASS, Beirut, May 21st: A reporter from the Lebanese "Neighbor" newspaper reported that the Arab League Political Committee passed a recommendation: giving all Arab countries full freedom of action on the issue of recognizing China.
    The reporter then wrote, based on the words of a certain Syrian minister, that Syria and Saudi Arabia will also recognize China soon.
    The reporter then said that the US Embassy in Damascus is now taking all measures to delay the Syrian government's recognition of China.

### 阿拉伯国家支持埃及承认中国

1956-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯国家支持埃及承认中国
    苏丹外交部长表示支持
    新华社开罗21日电　“新闻报”刊载的一条消息说：苏丹外交部部长穆巴拉克·查鲁克就埃及承认中华人民共和国一事发表评论说：“我们苏丹人支持阿拉伯国家所赞成的行动。”
    约旦前首相里法伊主张
    阿拉伯国家都应承认中国
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社贝鲁特二十二日讯：约旦前首相里法伊向黎巴嫩“电讯报”记者说，所有的阿拉伯国家都应当承认中华人民共和国。
    里法伊就埃及承认中华人民共和国这一消息发表谈话的时候说：“埃及承认中华人民共和国的行为，是像任何一个真正独立和自主的国家的行为。我认为，埃及在外交政策方面所获得的一切成就，也就是整个阿拉伯世界的成就。”
    阿拉伯联盟政委会通过建议
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社贝鲁特二十一日讯：据黎巴嫩“邻人报”记者报道，阿拉伯联盟政治委员会通过一项建议：给各阿拉伯国家在承认中国的问题上以充分的行动自由。
    记者接着根据叙利亚某一部长的话写道，叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯最近也将承认中国。
    记者接着说，美国驻大马士革大使馆现在正在采取一切办法来延缓叙利亚政府承认中国。
