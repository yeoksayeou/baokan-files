Title: Ceylonese Prime Minister Hopes Conference Will Succeed
Author:
Date: 1956-05-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 6th: According to an Antara news agency report on May 5th, Ceylonese Prime Minister Bandaranaike expressed his hope that the Asian-African Students Conference to be held in Bandung will be successful.

Bandaranaike, in his telegram to the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian-African Students Conference, also extended a warm welcome to the preparatory committee.

### 锡兰总理希望会议成功

1956-05-07
第4版()
专栏：亚非学生会议新闻

    锡兰总理希望会议成功
    新华社雅加达6日电　据安塔拉通讯社五月五日报道：锡兰总理班达拉奈克表示希望即将在万隆举行的亚非学生会议获得成功。
    班达拉奈克在他打给亚非学生会议国际筹备委员会的电报中，还对这个筹备委员会表示热烈欢迎。
