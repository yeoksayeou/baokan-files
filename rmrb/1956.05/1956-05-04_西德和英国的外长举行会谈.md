Title: West German and British Foreign Ministers Hold Talks
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch from London: West German Foreign Minister von Brentano visited London from April 30th to May 3rd, and held talks with British Foreign Secretary Lloyd, Chancellor of the Exchequer Macmillan, and other government officials. The communiqué issued on May 2nd stated that both sides discussed the Anglo-Soviet talks, the German question, the disarmament question, and economic issues related to both countries during the talks.

According to Agence France-Presse, the differences between the two sides on the thorny issue of who should pay for the expenses of British troops stationed in West Germany were not resolved.

### 西德和英国的外长举行会谈

1956-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    西德和英国的外长举行会谈
    据新华社3日讯　伦敦消息：西德外交部长勃伦塔诺四月三十日到五月三日访问了伦敦，同英国外交大臣劳埃德，财政大臣麦克米伦及其他政府官员举行了会谈。五月二日发表的会谈公报表明，双方在会谈里就英苏会谈、德国问题、裁军问题、同两国有关的经济问题等进行了讨论。
    据法新社消息说，双方在关于英国驻西德军队的经费由谁支付这个棘手问题上的分歧意见没有能够得到解决。
