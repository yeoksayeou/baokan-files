Title: Volunteer Army Holds First Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-05-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 20th: The first congress of the Chinese Communist Party of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army concluded on the 19th. The congress began on the 10th.

    At this congress, Li Zhimin, on behalf of the Chinese Communist Party Committee of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, made a summary report on the Party's work since the Korean armistice. After full discussion, the congress passed a resolution, unanimously agreeing with the report's assessment of the work of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army since the Korean armistice and the basic tasks outlined for the future, and resolved to carry out unremitting struggle to realize the glorious tasks of the Volunteer Army in the future.

    The congress finally elected the new CPC Volunteer Army Committee and Supervisory Committee, and elected delegates and alternate delegates to attend the Eighth National Congress of the CPC.

    During the congress, all the delegates presented wreaths to the Cemetery of the Volunteer Army Martyrs.

### 志愿军举行第一次党代表大会

1956-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军举行第一次党代表大会
    新华社平壤20日电　中国共产党中国人民志愿军第一次代表大会于十九日闭幕。代表大会是从十日开始举行的。
    在这次代表会上，李志民代表中国共产党中国人民志愿军委员会作了自朝鲜停战以来党的工作的总结报告。大会在经过充分讨论后，通过决议，一致同意报告中对于朝鲜停战以来中国人民志愿军工作情况的估计和所指出的今后的基本任务，并且决心为实现志愿军今后的光荣任务而进行不懈的斗争。
    大会最后选出了新的中共志愿军委员会和监察委员会，并选出出席中共第八次全国代表大会的代表和候补代表。
    大会期间，全体代表曾到志愿军烈士陵园敬献花圈。
