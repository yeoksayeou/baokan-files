Title: Marshal Chen Yi Inspects the Tibet Military Region and Local Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-05-02
Page: 2

　　Marshal Chen Yi Inspects the Tibet Military Region and Local Troops
　　On the 1st, Marshal Chen Yi inspected the troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region and the Tibetan local troops in Lhasa, and extended cordial greetings to them.
　　On the square in front of the Potala Palace, the PLA soldiers in their grass-green summer uniforms, and the Tibetan local troops officers and soldiers in their dark brown wide-sleeved, large-collared "Chuba" and felt hats, were neatly and majestically lined up, waiting to be inspected.
　　At 10:00 AM, Marshal Chen Yi, accompanied by Lieutenant General Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Tibet Military Region, inspected the troops in an open-top car. Marshal Chen Yi raised his hand and shouted: "Long live the great unity of the Tibetan people!" "Long live the People's Republic of China!" "Long live Chairman Mao!" The officers and soldiers being inspected shouted "Long live!" in unison.
　　After the parade, Marshal Chen Yi addressed all the troops who had been inspected. He said: "In the past few years, the PLA troops stationed in Tibet have resolutely implemented the central government's ethnic and religious policies while safeguarding the motherland's borders and consolidating national defense, and have protected the safety and interests of the people of all strata in Tibet. In the past few years, all officers and soldiers of the Tibetan local troops have made great contributions in implementing the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet, cooperating with the People's Liberation Army in guarding the motherland's borders, and maintaining local public security. For your contribution to the motherland and the people, I would like to express my deep condolences and gratitude to you here." Marshal Chen Yi also encouraged all officers and soldiers to guard against arrogance and rashness, make persistent efforts, safeguard the happy lives of the people of all nationalities in the motherland's big family, and safeguard world peace, continuously improve their military, political, and cultural level, master modern military science and technology, and strive to build a modern and regular national defense force.
　　Attending the parade on the reviewing stand were also Lieutenant General Tan Guansan, Lieutenant General Ape A-wang Jinmei, Lieutenant General Duo Ge Pengcuo Raojie, Major General Chen Mingyi, Major General Jin Shaoshan, Major General Fan Ming of the Tibet Military Region, and Zhang Jingwu and Wang Feng, deputy heads of the central delegation, and others. Representatives attending the inaugural meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region participated in the ceremony. (Reported by our reporter)

### 陈毅元帅检阅西藏军区和地方部队

1956-05-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　陈毅元帅检阅西藏军区和地方部队
    陈毅元帅一日在拉萨检阅了中国人民解放军西藏军区部队和西藏地方部队，并且向他们进行了亲切的慰问。
    在布达拉宫前面的广场上，穿着草绿色夏季新装的解放军战士，和穿着深褐色宽袖大领的“楚巴”、带着呢帽的西藏地方部队官兵，整齐威武地排列着，等候受检阅。
    上午十时，陈毅元帅由西藏军区司令员张国华中将陪同，乘着敞篷汽车检阅队伍。陈毅元帅举手高呼：“西藏人民大团结万岁！”“中华人民共和国万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”受检阅的官兵一起随声高呼“万岁！”
    阅兵后，陈毅元帅向全体接受检阅的部队讲了话。他说：“中国人民解放军驻藏部队几年来在保卫祖国边疆，巩固国防的同时，坚持不渝地严格执行了中央的民族政策和宗教政策，保护了西藏各阶层人民的安全和利益。西藏地方部队的全体官兵，几年来在执行和平解放西藏办法的协议，配合人民解放军守卫祖国边疆，维护地方治安等方面，有很大的功绩。为了你们对祖国和人民所作的这种贡献，我在这里向你们表示深切的慰问和感谢。”陈毅元帅并勉励全体官兵戒骄戒躁，再接再厉，保卫祖国大家庭各民族人民的幸福生活和保卫世界和平，不断地提高军事、政治、文化水平，掌握现代化军事科学技术，为建设现代化、正规化的国防军而奋斗。
    在检阅台上参加阅兵的，还有西藏军区谭冠三中将、阿沛·阿旺晋美中将、朵喀·彭措饶杰中将、陈明义少将、金绍山少将、范明少将，中央代表团副团长张经武、汪锋等人。出席西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会的代表们参加了观礼。（本报记者电）
