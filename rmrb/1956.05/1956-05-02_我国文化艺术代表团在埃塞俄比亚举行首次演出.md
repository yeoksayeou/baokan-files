Title: Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation Holds First Performance in Ethiopia
Author:
Date: 1956-05-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Addis Ababa, May 1st. The Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation held its first performance in Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia, on April 29th.

More than 1,200 people attended the performance, including the speakers and deputy speakers of the Senate and House of Representatives, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, the Minister of Commerce and Industry, the Minister of Justice, the Minister of Public Works, the Commander of the Imperial Guard, and other senior officials, as well as prominent figures from all walks of life in Ethiopia.

Diplomatic envoys from the United Kingdom, Czechoslovakia, Switzerland, the Netherlands, and Japan to Ethiopia also attended the performance.

### 我国文化艺术代表团在埃塞俄比亚举行首次演出

1956-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    我国文化艺术代表团在埃塞俄比亚举行首次演出
    新华社亚的斯亚贝巴1日电　中国文化艺术代表团四月二十九日在埃塞俄比亚首都亚的斯亚贝巴举行了第一次演出。
    出席观看表演的有一千二百多人，其中有贵族院和下议院的议长和副议长、外交大臣、工商大臣、司法大臣、公共工程大臣、禁卫军司令和其他高级官员，以及埃塞俄比亚各界著名人士。
    出席观看演出的还有英国、捷克斯洛伐克、瑞士、荷兰、日本驻埃塞俄比亚的外交使节。
