Title: Yunnan Establishes Seven New Tropical Crop Farms

Author: 
Date: 1956-05-20
Page: 2

Yunnan Province's seven newly established state-owned tropical crop farms have recently been put into production, one after another. Each farm is currently developing its land, with five farms having already cultivated more than 3,000 mu. This year, they plan to cultivate a total of 12,000 mu for planting and cultivating tropical cash crops such as coffee, kapok, sisal hemp, citronella grass, and cinchona.

These seven farms are distributed in subtropical areas including the Mengzi Special District, the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Prefecture, and the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture, possessing a total of more than 200,000 mu of land. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 云南新建七个热带作物垦殖场

1956-05-20
第2版()
专栏：

    云南新建七个热带作物垦殖场
    云南省新建的七个国营热带作物垦殖场，最近已经先后投入生产。各场都在开垦场地，其中五个场已经垦地三千多亩。今年共计划垦地一万二千亩，种植和培育咖啡、木棉、剑麻、香茅草、金鸡纳等热带经济作物。
    这七个垦殖场分布在蒙自专区、西双版纳傣族自治州和德宏傣族景颇族自治州等亚热带地区内，共拥有二十多万亩土地。（据新华社讯）
