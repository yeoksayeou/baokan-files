Title: Afghan Ambassador Samad Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1956-05-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 27th: Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad, held a reception in Beijing this afternoon to celebrate the National Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

At the joyous reception, Ambassador Samad proposed a toast to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and all the Chinese people. He also proposed a toast to Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and the two hundred Chinese and foreign guests present at today's reception.

Premier Zhou Enlai then proposed a toast to the National Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, to the friendship between China and Afghanistan, and to the health of the King of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. Premier Zhou Enlai also raised his glass to the health of the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan and the Ambassador himself.

Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai engaged in frequent interactions and conversations with the envoys of various countries to China today. They both spoke with the Egyptian trade representatives, congratulating them on the new development of friendship between China and Egypt.

Vice Chairman Zhu De spent the longest time conversing with Ambassador Samad, the host of today's banquet, and they took a picture together.

At the request of Sharma, a famous Indian photojournalist, Premier Zhou Enlai took a group photo with Ambassador Samad and the ambassadors of the Soviet Union, India, Burma, Finland, and other countries.

Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian also attended today's reception.

Marshal Peng Dehuai had the most enthusiastic conversations with the military attachés of the various embassies in China today. He spoke with the Egyptian trade representative about the Egyptian government military delegation that will soon visit China, saying that we warmly welcome them to visit China and hope they will spend more time visiting China.

The reception lasted for approximately two hours.

### 阿富汗大使萨马德举行国庆招待会

1956-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗大使萨马德举行国庆招待会
    新华社27日讯　阿富汗驻华大使萨马德今天下午在北京举行招待会，庆祝阿富汗王国国庆日。
    萨马德大使在这个欢乐的招待会上，提议为中华人民共和国毛泽东主席和全体中国人民的健康干杯。他还提议为出席今天招待会的朱德副主席、周恩来总理和二百位中外来宾干杯。
    周恩来总理紧接着提议，为阿富汗王国的国庆、为中国和阿富汗两国间的友谊、为阿富汗王国国王和他的健康干杯。周恩来总理还举杯祝阿富汗王国首相和大使本人的健康。
    朱德副主席和周恩来总理今天同各国驻中国的使节们进行了频繁的接触和谈话。他们都先后同埃及的商务代表谈了话，祝贺中埃两国友谊的新的发展。
    朱德副主席同今天宴会的主人萨马德大使交谈时间最长，他们并且在一起照了像。
    周恩来总理应印度著名摄影记者夏尔马的请求，同萨马德大使及苏联、印度、缅甸、芬兰等国的大使在一起合影。
    副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅和外交部副部长张闻天也出席了今天的招待会。
    彭德怀元帅今天同各国驻华使馆的武官交谈得最热烈。他同埃及商务代表畅谈着不久将到中国访问的埃及政府军事代表团，他说，我们热烈的欢迎他们来中国访问，并且希望他们多在中国访问一些日子。
    招待会进行了约两小时。
