Title: Chinese Peking Opera Troupe to Visit Japan Departs
Author:
Date: 1956-05-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Chinese Peking Opera Delegation to Japan, led by the famous performing artist Mei Lanfang, left Beijing by train on the 16th. The delegation is visiting Japan for performances at the invitation of the "Asahi Shimbun" company.

The Peking Opera Troupe is headed by the famous dramatist Ouyang Yuqian as the first deputy head and general director. The deputy heads are the dramatist Ma Shaobo (also secretary-general) and Liu Jia, and Sun Pinghua. The deputy secretary-general is the dramatist Ouyang Shanzun. The members include famous Peking Opera actors Li Shaochun, Yuan Shihai, Jiang Miaoxiang, Li Hezeng, Jiang Xinrong, Mei Baojiu, Mei Baoyue, Lou Zhenkui, Hou Yulan, etc. The delegation is taking more than twenty wonderful programs to Japan, including various dramas of Wen, Wu, Kun, Luan and Sheng, Dan, Jing, Chou, and Mo. Mei Lanfang is preparing to perform his masterpieces "Drunken Concubine," "Farewell My Concubine," "Broken Bridge," and "A Stroll in the Garden" in Japan. The delegation will also perform some other excellent classical Chinese dramas.

Today, those who went to the station to see off the delegation included Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Vice Presidents Zhao Yimin and Yang Hansheng, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association Tian Han, as well as literary and art figures from the capital and Japanese people currently in Beijing.

### 中国访日京剧团启程

1956-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中国访日京剧团启程
    新华社16日讯  由著名表演艺术家梅兰芳率领的中国访日京剧代表团已在十六日乘火车离开北京。代表团是应“朝日新闻”社的邀请前往日本进行访问演出。
    这个京剧团由著名戏剧家欧阳予倩担任第一副团长和总导演，副团长有戏剧家马少波（兼秘书长）和刘佳、孙平化，担任副秘书长的是戏剧家欧阳山尊，团员中包括京剧名演员李少春、袁世海、姜妙香、李和曾、江新蓉、梅葆玖、梅葆玥、娄振奎、侯玉兰等。代表团带往日本的共有二十几个精采节目，其中包括了文、武、昆、乱和生、旦、净、丑、末的各种剧目。梅兰芳准备在日本演出的有他的杰作“醉酒”、“霸王别姬”、“断桥”、“游园惊梦”等节目，代表团将要演出的有另外一些优秀的中国古典剧目。
    今天到车站欢送代表团的，有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，副会长赵毅敏、阳翰笙，文化部副部长夏衍，中国戏剧家协会主席田汉，以及首都文艺界人士和现在北京的日本人士。
