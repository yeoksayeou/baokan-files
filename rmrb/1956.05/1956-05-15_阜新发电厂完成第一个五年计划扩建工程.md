Title: Fuxin Power Plant Completes First Five-Year Plan Expansion Project
Author:
Date: 1956-05-15
Page: 2

    Fuxin Power Plant Completes First Five-Year Plan Expansion Project
    The construction personnel of the Fuxin Power Plant handed over another newly installed steam turbine generator to the production unit on the 12th, completing all the expansion projects of the power plant's first five-year plan three months ahead of schedule. The fourth power generation unit was handed over for production this time, and the first three power generation units were put into production successively before last year.
    At the signing handover ceremony, the members of the start-up committee unanimously expressed satisfaction with the recent trial operation of the equipment. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 阜新发电厂完成第一个五年计划扩建工程

1956-05-15
第2版()
专栏：

    阜新发电厂完成第一个五年计划扩建工程
    阜新发电厂的建设人员，在十二日又把一部新安装的汽轮发电机移交给了生产单位，提前三个月结束了这座发电厂第一个五年计划的全部扩建工程。这次移交生产的是第四部发电设备，前三部发电设备早在去年以前就陆续投入了生产。
    在签字移交仪式上，起动委员会的委员们对这台设备在最近试运转的情况一致表示满意。（据新华社讯）
