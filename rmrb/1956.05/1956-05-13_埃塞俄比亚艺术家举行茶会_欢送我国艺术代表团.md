Title: Ethiopian Artists Hold Tea Party to Bid Farewell to Chinese Art Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-05-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Addis Ababa, May 12th: Ethiopian artists held a tea party on the afternoon of May 10th to bid farewell to the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, performing many programs with rich national flavors that won the appreciation and warm applause of the Chinese artists.

Ma Hanbing, Deputy Head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, and Ethiopian artist Tasfaye Tassama delivered speeches at the meeting. Both of them expressed their hope to develop and strengthen cultural exchanges between the two countries.

Ethiopian artists presented the delegation with classical Ethiopian musical instruments at the meeting. The delegation reciprocated with several famous Chinese embroidered screens.

When the Chinese guests left, the hosts saw them off at the door, warmly saying "Goodbye" in Chinese.

### 埃塞俄比亚艺术家举行茶会  欢送我国艺术代表团

1956-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    埃塞俄比亚艺术家举行茶会
    欢送我国艺术代表团
    新华社亚的斯亚贝巴12日电　在五月十日下午举行的一次茶会上，埃塞俄比亚艺术家们表演了许多富有民族风趣的节目来欢送中国文化艺术代表团，这些节目博得了中国艺术家们的赞赏和热烈掌声。
    中国文化艺术代表团副团长马寒冰和埃塞俄比亚艺术家塔斯法伊·塔萨马在会上相互致词。他们两人都希望能够发展和加强两国之间的文化交流。
    埃塞俄比亚的艺术家们在会上向代表团赠送了埃塞俄比亚的古典乐器。代表团回赠了几幅著名的中国绣屏。
    在中国客人离开的时候，主人们都到门口欢送，用中国话热情地说：“再见”。
