Title: Syrian Prime Minister Returns Visit to Leaders of Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation; Damascus Audience Warmly Welcomes Performance by Our Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-05-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, May 27th. Syrian Prime Minister Ghazi paid a return visit to the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation's leaders, delegation head Bao Erhan and deputy heads Ma Yuhuai and Ma Hanbing, at their hotel on May 26th. Prime Minister Ghazi had a friendly conversation with them. On the morning of the same day, Bao Erhan visited the Syrian Minister of Economy.

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, May 27th. More than 2,300 spectators in the Syrian capital, Damascus, watched the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation's first performance in Syria on May 25th.

The performance took place in the beautiful open-air theater of the Damascus Fair. Chinese and Syrian flags were hung on the stage.

Among those who watched the performance were Syrian Prime Minister Ghazi, the Minister of the Army, the Minister of Economy, the Minister of Agriculture, the Minister of Public Works, the Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces and Chief of the General Staff, and other senior officials, as well as the General Secretary of the Syrian Communist Party, Parliamentarian Bakdash.

Also watching the performance were diplomatic representatives from various countries to Syria, and famous figures from Damascus's cultural, artistic, educational, financial, and journalistic circles.

In addition to Chinese songs, dances, and Peking opera, the delegation also performed Syrian songs and dances. The performance was warmly welcomed by the audience. After the performance, the Syrian Ministry of Education, the Ministry of Broadcasting and Propaganda, and the Syrian-Chinese Friendship Association presented flowers to the delegation in gratitude.

### 叙总理回拜我国文化艺术代表团领导人  大马士革观众热烈欢迎我代表团演出

1956-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    叙总理回拜我国文化艺术代表团领导人
    大马士革观众热烈欢迎我代表团演出
    新华社大马士革27日电　叙利亚总理加齐五月二十六日到中国文化艺术代表团所住的旅馆回拜代表团团长鲍尔汉和副团长马玉槐、马寒冰。加齐总理同他们作了友好的交谈。同一天上午，鲍尔汉拜会了叙利亚经济部长。
    新华社大马士革27日电　叙利亚首都大马士革二千三百多个观众五月二十五日观看了中国文化艺术代表团在叙利亚的首次演出。
    演出是在环境优美的大马士革博览会的露天剧场中举行的。舞台上悬挂着中国和叙利亚的国旗。
    观看演出的有叙利亚总理加齐、陆军部长、经济部长、农业部长、公共工程部长、武装部队总司令兼总参谋长和其他高级官员，以及叙利亚共产党总书记、议员巴克达斯。
    观看演出的还有各国驻叙利亚的外交代表，大马士革文化界、艺术界、教育界、金融界和报界的著名人士。
    除了中国歌舞和京剧以外，代表团还演出了叙利亚的歌舞。演出受到了观众的热烈欢迎。演出结束后，叙利亚教育部、广播和宣传部和叙中友好协会向代表团献花表示感谢。
