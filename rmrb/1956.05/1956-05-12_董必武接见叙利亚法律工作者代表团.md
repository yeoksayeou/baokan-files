Title: Dong Biwu Receives Syrian Legal Workers Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court, received the Syrian legal workers delegation headed by Adnan al-Kwatly this morning. Gao Kelin, Zhang Zhizhang, and Ma Xiwu were also present at the reception.

This morning, the Syrian legal workers delegation also paid a visit to Shi Liang, Minister of Justice. In the afternoon, they visited the Law Department and the Department of Oriental Languages of Peking University, and met with Chen Shouyi, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Lawyers Association, and others.

### 董必武接见叙利亚法律工作者代表团

1956-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    董必武接见叙利亚法律工作者代表团
    新华社11日讯　最高人民法院院长董必武今天上午接见了以阿德南·库阿特利为首的叙利亚法律工作者代表团。接见的时候，高克林、张志让、马锡五也在座。
    在今天上午，叙利亚法律工作者代表团还拜会了司法部部长史良。下午，他们访问了北京大学法律系和东语系，并且同北京市律师协会筹备委员会主任委员陈守一等会见。
