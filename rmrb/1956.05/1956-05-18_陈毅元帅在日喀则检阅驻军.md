Title: Marshal Chen Yi Inspects Troops in Xigazê
Author:
Date: 1956-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xigazê, 17th - Marshal Chen Yi, head of the Central Delegation and Vice Premier of the State Council, inspected all officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Xigazê, the Tibetan local troops stationed in Xigazê, and the Panchen Guard Battalion at the Xigazê People's Square this morning. Accompanying Marshal Chen Yi during the inspection were Zhang Jingwu, Wang Feng, and Huang Qixiang, deputy heads of the Central Delegation; Lieutenant General Zhang Guohua, Commander of the PLA Tibet Military Region; Lieutenant General Dorje Puncog Raoje, Second Deputy Commander; and Jaindong Jigme, Director of the Panchen Kanpo Lhentsok Committee.

After inspecting the troops, Marshal Chen Yi ascended the reviewing stand and addressed all officers and soldiers. He encouraged all officers and soldiers to build the Tibetan Plateau into a socialist paradise. Zhang Jingwu, on behalf of the Central Delegation, expressed his regards to all officers and soldiers. Lieutenant General Zhang Guohua, Lieutenant General Dorje Puncog Raoje, and Jaindong Jigme also addressed the officers and soldiers.

### 陈毅元帅在日喀则检阅驻军

1956-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅元帅在日喀则检阅驻军
    新华社日喀则17日电　中央代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅元帅今天上午在日喀则人民广场检阅了中国人民解放军驻日喀则部队、西藏地方部队驻日喀则部队和班禅警卫营的全体官兵。陪同陈毅元帅阅兵的有中央代表团副团长张经武、汪锋、黄琪翔，中国人民解放军西藏军区司令员张国华中将、第二副司令员朵喀·彭措饶杰中将，班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任詹东·计晋美。
    陈毅元帅检阅部队后，登上阅兵台向全体官兵讲了话。他勉励全体官兵把西藏高原建设成社会主义的乐园。张经武代表中央代表团向全体官兵表示慰问。张国华中将、朵喀·彭措饶杰中将、詹东·计晋美也都向官兵们讲了话。
