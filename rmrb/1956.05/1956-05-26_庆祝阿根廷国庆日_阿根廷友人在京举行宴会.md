Title: Argentinian Friends in Beijing Hold Banquet to Celebrate Argentina's National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-05-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 25th. The Argentine medical delegation and the Argentine cultural delegation, which are currently visiting China, celebrated Argentina's National Day in Beijing today. In the evening, the two delegations hosted a banquet for people from the medical and cultural circles in Beijing.

After the banquet, members of the delegation performed cultural programs and held a pleasant dance party with their Chinese friends.

### 庆祝阿根廷国庆日  阿根廷友人在京举行宴会

1956-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝阿根廷国庆日
    阿根廷友人在京举行宴会
    新华社25日讯　正在中国访问的阿根廷医学代表团和阿根廷文化代表团今天在北京庆祝阿根廷国庆日。晚上，两个代表团设宴招待北京的医学界和文化界人士。
    宴会以后，代表团的团员们表演了文艺节目，并且同他们的中国朋友们举行了一个愉快的舞会。
