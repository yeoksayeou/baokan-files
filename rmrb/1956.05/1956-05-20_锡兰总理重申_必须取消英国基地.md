Title: Ceylonese Prime Minister Reiterates: British Bases Must Be Removed
Author:
Date: 1956-05-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 18th: Colombo dispatch: Ceylonese Prime Minister Bandaranaike reiterated on May 17th that British bases in Ceylon must be removed.

At his weekly press conference, he said: As a dominion, Ceylon has the absolute right to have its views accepted on this issue. As a member of the Commonwealth, Ceylon is not bound by any Commonwealth joint defense plan.

### 锡兰总理重申  必须取消英国基地

1956-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理重申
    必须取消英国基地
    新华社新德里18日电　科伦坡消息：锡兰总理班达拉奈克五月十七日重申必须取消在锡兰的英国基地。
    他在每周例行的记者招待会上说：锡兰作为自治领，有绝对的权利在这个问题上要别人接受它的看法。作为一个英联邦成员国家，并没有使锡兰受到任何英联邦共同防御计划的约束。
