Title: Romania and Iceland Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-05-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 19th. According to a report by the Romanian News Agency on the 18th, the Romanian government and the Icelandic government have reached an agreement to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic representatives at the level of minister.

### 罗马尼亚和冰岛建交

1956-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚和冰岛建交
    新华社布拉格19日电　据罗马尼亚通讯社十八日报道，罗马尼亚政府和冰岛政府已经达成协议，决定两国间建立外交关系并且互派公使衔的外交代表。
