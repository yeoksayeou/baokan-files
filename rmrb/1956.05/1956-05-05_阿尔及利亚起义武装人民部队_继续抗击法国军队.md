Title: Algerian Armed People's Forces Continue to Fight French Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-05-05
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, 4th: According to a French communiqué published on May 2nd, French troops continued to engage in fierce battles with the Algerian armed people's forces throughout the Constantine province and the Aurès and Nementcha mountains in eastern Algeria.

    On May 2nd, the French authorities also announced a curfew in eight towns near Algiers. Other towns near Algiers and Algiers itself have already been under curfew for more than a month.

    French reinforcements have been continuing to arrive in Algeria recently.

### 阿尔及利亚起义武装人民部队  继续抗击法国军队

1956-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚起义武装人民部队
    继续抗击法国军队
    新华社4日讯　阿尔及尔消息：据法国方面五月二日发表的公报说，在整个君士坦丁省和阿尔及利亚东部的奥雷斯山区和内门特查山区，法国军队同起义武装人民部队继续发生激战。
    法国当局还在五月二日宣布阿尔及尔附近的八个城镇晚上戒严，阿尔及尔附近的其他城镇和阿尔及尔一个多月前已经实行戒严。
    法国的增援军队最近正在继续到达阿尔及利亚。
