Title: Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, May 29th. The Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Delegation, led by N.M. Silyanov, Chairman of the Soviet Group of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee and Deputy Chairman of the Soviet State Economic Committee, arrived in Beijing today to participate in the fourth session of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee.

### 苏联科学技术合作代表团到京

1956-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联科学技术合作代表团到京
    据新华社29日讯　来中国参加中苏科学技术合作委员会第四届会议的苏联科学技术合作代表团，在委员会苏联组主席、苏联国家经济委员会副主席恩·姆·西卢雅诺夫率领下，今天到达北京。
