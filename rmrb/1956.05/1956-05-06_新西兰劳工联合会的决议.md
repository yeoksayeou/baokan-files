Title: Resolution of the New Zealand Federation of Labour
Author:
Date: 1956-05-06
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, May 5th, Vienna (TASS): According to a communiqué from the Secretariat of the World Peace Council, the National Committee of the New Zealand Federation of Labour recently opposed the testing of atomic weapons. The resolution passed by the National Committee stated: "We resolutely oppose preparations for war, and therefore also oppose the testing of atomic weapons. We request the New Zealand government to call on all governments not to conduct atomic and hydrogen weapon tests."

### 新西兰劳工联合会的决议

1956-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    新西兰劳工联合会的决议
    新华社5日讯　塔斯社维也纳五日讯：据世界和平理事会书记处公报报道，新西兰劳工联合会全国委员会最近反对试验原子武器。在全国委员会所通过的决议中说：“我们坚决反对准备战争，因此也反对试验原子武器。我们请求新西兰政府呼吁各国政府不进行原子武器和氢武器的试验。”
