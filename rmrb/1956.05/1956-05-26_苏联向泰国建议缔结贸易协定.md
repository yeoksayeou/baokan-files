Title: The Soviet Union Suggests Trade Agreement with Thailand
Author:
Date: 1956-05-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 25th: According to a report from Bangkok by the Indian Press Trust on the 24th, the Thai Ministry of Economic Affairs stated that the Soviet Union has suggested concluding a barter trade agreement with Thailand.

Officials from the Ministry of Economic Affairs said that the Thai government is considering this suggestion.

### 苏联向泰国建议缔结贸易协定

1956-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联向泰国建议缔结贸易协定
    据新华社新德里25日电　据印度报业托辣斯二十四日从曼谷报道，泰国经济部说，苏联已经建议同泰国缔结一项换货贸易协定。
    经济部人士说，泰国政府正在考虑这项建议。
