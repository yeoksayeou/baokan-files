Title: 5,000 People March in Paris Demanding Restoration of Peace in Algeria; French Government Prepares to Launch General Offensive in Algeria
Author:
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: Paris dispatch: On May 27th, five thousand people in Paris held a demonstration march, demanding the restoration of peace in Algeria and calling for unified action in the struggle for peace in Algeria. Demonstrators marched in formation past the "Wall of the Communards," the place where the martyrs of the Paris Commune sacrificed themselves.

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: According to a United Press International Cairo dispatch: Representatives of the Algerian National Liberation Front said at a press conference in Cairo on the 26th: According to information from the Algerian National Liberation Army, the French government is preparing to launch a general offensive against the Algerian people from June 1st to 15th.

Ahmed Medani, a representative of the Algerian National Liberation Front and president of the Algerian Association of Ulema, pointed out at the press conference that Mendès-France's resignation shows that the French government's policy of violent suppression in Algeria has aroused dissatisfaction among the domestic public. This confirms our repeatedly expressed view that violence cannot solve the Algerian problem.

He said, "We welcome all mediation efforts to bring about a peaceful solution to the problem."

Representatives of the Algerian National Liberation Front said that if France launches a general offensive, the Algerian National Liberation Army will launch a forceful counterattack.

### 巴黎五千人游行示威  要求在阿尔及利亚恢复和平  法国政府准备在阿尔及利亚发动总攻

1956-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    巴黎五千人游行示威
    要求在阿尔及利亚恢复和平
    法国政府准备在阿尔及利亚发动总攻
    新华社28日讯　巴黎消息：五月二十七日，巴黎有五千人举行示威游行，要求恢复阿尔及利亚的和平和号召在争取阿尔及利亚和平中一致行动。示威者列队走过巴黎公社烈士牺牲的地方——“公社社员墙”。
    新华社28日讯　据合众社开罗消息：阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线的代表二十六日在开罗举行的记者招待会上说：根据阿尔及利亚民族解放军的消息，法国政府准备从六月一日到十五日对阿尔及利亚人民发动总攻。
    阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线的一位代表、阿尔及利亚阿訇协会主席艾哈迈德·梅达尼在记者招待会上指出，孟戴斯—弗朗斯辞职表明法国政府对阿尔及利亚的暴力镇压政策引起了国内公众的不满。这证实了我们一再表示的暴力不能解决阿尔及利亚问题的看法。
    他说，我们欢迎为使问题和平解决而作的一切调解努力。
    阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线的代表说，如果法国发动总攻，阿尔及利亚民族解放军将进行有力的反击。
