Title: Japan International Trade Promotion Association Decides to Exchange Visiting Delegations with China
Author:
Date: 1956-05-10
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 9th. The Japan International Trade Promotion Association decided on the 8th to send a Japanese business and industry delegation to visit China. At the same time, the association decided to formally invite a Chinese economic delegation to visit Japan in May.
    The Japanese business and industry delegation to China will visit in two groups. The first group, headed by Ito Kesashi, managing director of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and with Yamanashi Prefecture Governor Hisashi Amano as advisor, will include about 40 representatives from Japanese trading companies and the textile industry, and will depart in May. The second group will be composed of representatives from the steel, machinery, shipbuilding, and chemical industries, as well as related companies, and will depart in June.
    The association also approved its business plan for this year. The plan stipulates: continuing to promote the implementation of the Third Japan-China Trade Agreement and the signing of the Fourth Japan-China Trade Agreement; striving to remove the embargo restrictions of the Coordinating Committee for Export Control (COCOM); promoting economic exchanges and the establishment of commercial representative offices with the Soviet Union and other democratic countries, and promoting the realization of the Asian Economic Conference to be held in Tokyo next spring.

### 日本国际贸易促进协会决定同中国互派访问团

1956-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本国际贸易促进协会决定同中国互派访问团
    新华社9日讯  东京消息：日本国际贸易促进协会八日决定派遣日本商工代表团访问中国。协会同时决定在五月间正式邀请中国经济代表团访问日本。
    日本商工访华代表团将分两批访问中国。第一批以日本国际贸易促进协会常务委员伊藤今朝市为团长，以山梨县知事天野久为顾问，包括日本商行和纺织界代表约四十人，将在五月间启程。第二批以钢铁、机械、造船、化学界和有关公司代表组成，将在六月间启程。
    协会还通过了它的今年度事业计划。这个计划规定：继续促进执行第三次日中贸易协定和签订第四次日中贸易协定；努力撤销巴黎统筹委员会的禁运限制；促进同苏联和其他民主国家的经济交流和互设商务代表机构，促进实现明年春天在东京举行亚洲经济会议。
