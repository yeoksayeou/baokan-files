Title: Paris Exposition Opens, Visitors Highly Appreciate the Chinese Pavilion
Author:
Date: 1956-05-07
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 6th. A Paris dispatch: The Paris Exposition opened on May 5th. 13,000 units from twenty-seven countries, including France, China, the United States, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and Egypt, are participating in this exposition.

　　Among the exhibition halls, the Chinese Pavilion is one of the most lively. Representatives from French labor unions and youth organizations visited the Chinese Pavilion and congratulated the Chinese exhibition delegation. The Paris Exposition authorities expressed their appreciation for the efforts of the Chinese Pavilion, and visitors generally praised the exhibits in the Chinese Pavilion. A Frenchman working in the machinery sector, after watching the performance of Chinese workers operating machines, praised the sophistication of Chinese machinery. Women lingered in front of the beautiful Chinese silks and embroideries. Many visitors left messages in the visitors' book, praising the beautiful national style of the Chinese Pavilion's arrangement and the various exhibits reflecting the rapid rise of China's national economy and culture.

### 巴黎博览会开幕，观众非常赞赏中国馆

1956-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴黎博览会开幕，观众非常赞赏中国馆
    新华社布拉格6日电　巴黎消息：巴黎博览会在五月五日开幕。参加这次博览会的，有法国、中国、美国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、埃及等二十七个国家的一万三千个单位。
    在博览会中，中国馆是最热闹的展览馆之一。法国工会和青年方面的代表都来到中国馆参观，并且向中国展览团道贺。巴黎博览会当局对中国馆的努力表示赞赏，观众也普遍赞扬中国馆的展出。一个在机械部门工作的法国人观看了中国工人操作机器的表演以后，赞叹中国机器的精良。妇女们在美丽的中国丝绸和刺绣面前留连不去。许多参观者都在观众意见本上留言，称赞中国馆那种美丽的民族风格的布置以及反映出中国国民经济和文化迅速高涨的各种展览品。
