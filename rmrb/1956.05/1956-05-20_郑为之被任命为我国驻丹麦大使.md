Title: Zheng Weizhi Appointed as China's Ambassador to Denmark
Author:
Date: 1956-05-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th - Zheng Weizhi has been appointed by Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Denmark.
    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has relieved Chai Chengwen of his post as Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Denmark.

### 郑为之被任命为我国驻丹麦大使

1956-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

    郑为之被任命为我国驻丹麦大使
    新华社19日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命郑为之为中华人民共和国驻丹麦王国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去柴成文的中华人民共和国驻丹麦王国特命全权公使的职务。
