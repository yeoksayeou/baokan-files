Date: 1956-05-12

    Friendly Exchanges
    △ At the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society, the Italian representative Griva and three others, part of a group of legal workers from Western European countries visiting China, left Beijing by plane on the 10th, via Guangzhou, to return to their country. Mrs. Neumann, Secretary of the Secretariat of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, from the German Democratic Republic, and eight legal worker representatives from Britain, France, and Belgium, had already returned to their countries on the 3rd.

### 友好往来

1956-05-12
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    △应中国政治法律学会邀请来华访问的西欧国家一批法律工作者中的意大利代表格里瓦等四人，已在十日乘飞机离开北京经广州回国。国际民主法律工作者协会书记处书记、德意志民主共和国的诺伊曼夫人和英、法、比的法律工作者代表八人已在三日先行返国。
