Title: Stoica Discusses the Development of Romanian-Yugoslav Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-05-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 23rd -- The Romanian News Agency reported on the 22nd that the Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers, Stoica, stated in an interview with Belgrade Radio: We are pleased to see that the relations between Romania and Yugoslavia are developing in many aspects along the path of friendly and fruitful cooperation, which is in line with the interests of the people of both countries. The foundation of this friendly relationship is the common goal of the people and governments of our two countries: the desire to build socialism, to avoid a new war, and to strive for the victory of the policy of peaceful coexistence between countries with different social systems. Therefore, we are convinced that nothing can hinder the further development of the fraternal relations between the people of our two countries.

Stoica spoke about several agreements recently concluded between the two countries. He pointed out that the Romanian-Yugoslav economic agreement established beneficial trade between the two countries and restored economic relations between the two countries. In this regard, there is undoubtedly great potential for further development. He also said that the number of reciprocal visits by representatives of public organizations of the two countries is increasing.

Referring to the upcoming visit of the Yugoslav parliamentary delegation to Romania at the invitation of the Romanian National Assembly, Stoica said that this visit will undoubtedly make a new and valuable contribution to the cause of friendship and cooperation between our two countries.

Stoica also answered questions raised by Belgrade Radio regarding Romania's domestic construction and the significance of the resolutions of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

### 斯托伊卡  谈罗南关系的发展

1956-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    斯托伊卡
    谈罗南关系的发展
    新华社布拉格23日电　据罗马尼亚通讯社二十二日报道：罗马尼亚部长会议主席斯托伊卡，对贝尔格莱德电台发表谈话说：我们高兴地看到，罗马尼亚和南斯拉夫之间的关系正在沿着符合两国人民利益的友好和有成效的合作的道路多方面地发展着。这种友好关系的基础是我们两国人民和政府的共同目的：建设社会主义的愿望，为避免发生新战争和为社会制度不同的国家和平共处的政策的胜利而斗争。因此，我们确信，任何东西都不能阻碍我们两国人民之间的兄弟关系的进一步发展。
    斯托伊卡谈到了最近两国之间缔结的一些协定。他指出罗南经济协定建立了两国间有益的贸易，恢复了两国之间的经济关系。在这方面，毫无疑问有着进一步发展的巨大可能性。他还说，两国公众团体的代表相互进行访问的次数正在增加中。
    在谈到南斯拉夫议会代表团将应罗马尼亚国民议会的邀请前来访问的时候，斯托伊卡说，这次访问无疑地将对我们两国之间的友好和合作的事业作出新的有价值的贡献。
    斯托伊卡还回答了贝尔格莱德电台提出的有关罗马尼亚国内建设和苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的决议的意义等问题。
