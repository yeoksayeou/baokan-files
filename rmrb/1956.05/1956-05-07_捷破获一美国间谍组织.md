Title: Czechoslovakia Smashes an American Spy Organization
Author:
Date: 1956-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 6th. According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency on the 5th: The security organs of the Czechoslovak Ministry of the Interior have smashed a spy organization within the country. All the criminals have been apprehended.

This spy organization was led by American agent B. Lorenz, a former textile factory owner. Its members had collected important military, economic, and political intelligence for American intelligence agencies.

### 捷破获一美国间谍组织

1956-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    捷破获一美国间谍组织
    新华社布拉格6日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社五日报道：捷克斯洛伐克内务部的保安机关在境内破获了一个间谍组织。全部人犯已经落网。
    这个间谍组织是由美国特务、过去的一个纺织工厂厂主贝·洛伦茨领导的。它的成员曾经为美国间谍机关搜集有关军事、经济和政治的重要情报。
