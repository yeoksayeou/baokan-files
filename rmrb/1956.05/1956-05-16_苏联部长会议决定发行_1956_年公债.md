Title: Soviet Council of Ministers Decides to Issue 1956 Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1956-05-16
Page: 4

Moscow dispatch from our newspaper on the 15th. The Soviet Council of Ministers announced on the 14th a decision on the issuance of the 1956 National Public Bonds for the Development of the National Economy.

The Moscow newspapers all published this decision on the 15th. The total amount of bonds issued is 32 billion rubles, to be repaid in twenty years. The purpose of issuing the bonds is to attract residents' funds to further develop the Soviet national economy. Bond principal and prizes are exempt from state and local taxes.

On the same day, the Soviet people began to enthusiastically subscribe.

### 苏联部长会议决定发行1956年公债

1956-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联部长会议决定发行1956年公债
    本报莫斯科15日电　苏联部长会议十四日公布了关于发行1956年发展国民经济国家公债的决定。
    莫斯科各报于十五日都刊载了这一决定。公债发行总数是三百二十亿卢布，分二十年还清。发行公债的目的是吸收居民资金来进一步发展苏联国民经济。公债债券和奖金免于征收国家和地方的赋税。
    当天，苏联人民就开始踊跃地认购了。
