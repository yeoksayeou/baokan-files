Title: Bao Erhan Visits Syrian Prime Minister and Minister of Education
Author:
Date: 1956-05-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, May 25th: Bao Erhan, head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, and deputy heads Ma Yuhuai and Ma Hanbing visited Syrian Prime Minister Ghazi on the morning of May 24th.

On the same day, they also visited Minister of Education Kazberi.

During these two meetings, both China and Syria expressed a common desire to promote cultural exchanges between the two countries and hoped to restore the friendly relations that once existed in the history of the two countries, a friendly relationship that was only interrupted by the influence of imperialism.

### 鲍尔汉访叙总理和教育部长

1956-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    鲍尔汉访叙总理和教育部长
    新华社大马士革25日电　中国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉、副团长马玉槐和马寒冰五月二十四日上午访问了叙利亚总理加齐。
    他们在同一天还访问了教育部长卡兹贝里。
    在这两次会见中，中叙双方都表示了促进两国文化交流的共同愿望，并且希望恢复两国历史上曾经存在过的友好关系，这种友好关系只是由于帝国主义的影响才遭到中断的。
