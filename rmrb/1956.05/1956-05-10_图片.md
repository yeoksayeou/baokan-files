Title: Young Worker Li Jingyun Creates a Rotary Tool Fixture, Increasing Work Efficiency by More Than Eight Times
Author:
Date: 1956-05-10
Page: 1

Young worker Li Jingyun has created a rotary tool fixture, increasing work efficiency by more than eight times. This is Li Jingyun (right) and apprentice technician Tong Wenxuan assembling the chain plates on the rotary tool fixture.
