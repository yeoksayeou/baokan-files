Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1956-05-09
Page: 4
    △ Mrs. Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 8th to return to her country. She came to Beijing to attend the Beijing meeting of the Council of the Women's International Democratic Federation. Ms. Gilette Van Nieuwenborg, Secretary of the Belgian Women's Union, who also attended the meeting, left Beijing by plane with her to return to her country.
    △ The Romanian national men's and women's table tennis team, after completing its visit to China, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 8th, with all eight members returning to their country.
    △ Mr. Asrarudin, Secretary-General of the Indonesian Labour Party, and his wife, who came to China for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, returned to their country via Guangzhou on the 7th.

### 友好往来

1956-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    △国际民主妇女联合会主席欧仁妮·戈登夫人八日上午乘飞机离京回国。她是来北京参加国际民主妇联理事会北京会议的。和她一起乘飞机离开北京回国的有参加会议的比利时妇女联合会书记居第勒·范涅温博格。
    △罗马尼亚国家男女乒乓球队结束了在中国的访问以后，全体人员八人在八日早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    △应中国人民外交学会邀请前来中国访问的印度尼西亚工党秘书长阿斯拉鲁丁及夫人在七日经广州返国。
