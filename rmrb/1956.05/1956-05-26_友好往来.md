Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1956-05-26
Page: 4

·Friendly Exchanges·
    △ At the invitation of the Chinese Preparatory Committee for the 1956 Commemoration of World Cultural Celebrities, German writers Ernst Schumacher and Hans Cibulka, and Soviet writers Vadim Sobko (head of the delegation), Vera Ketlinskaya, and Sabit Mukanov have arrived in Beijing successively to participate in the "Commemorative Meeting for World Cultural Celebrities Kalidasa, Heine, and Dostoevsky" held in Beijing on May 26.
    △ The Soviet weightlifting team, a group of twelve, led by team leader P. Repiev, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th.
    The Soviet weightlifting team is visiting China and participating in friendly matches at the invitation of the Chinese. Their first friendly match with Chinese weightlifters will be held on May 27 at the Beijing Gymnasium. In addition to the team leader, the Soviet weightlifting team includes four Soviet Honored Athletes, five Soviet Master Athletes, and two coaches.
    △ Ulaanbaatar News: The Chinese Inner Mongolia Cultural Workers Delegation, led by delegation leader Meng He, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by plane on May 25. The delegation is visiting the Mongolian People's Republic according to the 1956 Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Plan.

### 友好往来

1956-05-26
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    ·友好往来·
    △应中国1956年纪念世界文化名人筹备委员会的邀请，前来参加五月二十六日在北京举行的“世界文化名人迦梨陀娑、海?、陀思妥也夫斯基纪念会”的德国作家恩斯特·舒玛赫和汉斯·齐布尔卡二人，苏联作家瓦吉姆·索布柯（团长）、薇拉·凯特玲斯卡娅和萨比特·穆卡诺夫三人已先后到达北京。
    △苏联举重队一行十二人，在领队彼·雷皮耶夫率领下，二十四日下午坐飞机到达北京。
    苏联举重队是应邀来中国访问和进行友谊比赛的。他们同中国举重运动员的第一次友谊比赛，将在五月二十七日在北京体育馆中举行。苏联举重队中除领队外，有四名苏联功勋运动员、五名苏联运动健将和两名教练。
    △乌兰巴托消息：中国内蒙古文艺工作者代表团在团长孟和率领下，五月二十五日乘飞机到达乌兰巴托。代表团是根据中蒙1956年文化合作计划到蒙古人民共和国进行访问的。
