Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1956-05-03
Page: 4

    △ At the invitation of the Chinese Red Cross Society, the delegation of the Bulgarian Red Cross Society, led by Georgi Gospodinov, Vice Chairman of the Bulgarian Red Cross Society, arrived in Beijing on May 1st to attend the "May Day" celebrations and visit China. The members of the delegation are Rumen Damyanov and Ivan Baulongsuzov, members of the Central Council of the Bulgarian Red Cross Society.
    △ The Italian General Confederation of Labour delegation headed by Fernando Montagnani, one representative of the Iranian trade union, and one representative of the Indonesian trade union arrived in Beijing on May 1st. They were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and visit China.
    △ A thirteen-member Belgian cultural delegation headed by Georges Smets arrived in Beijing by plane on the 1st. They were invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China. The delegation is composed of well-known figures from the Belgian cultural circles. In addition to sightseeing and visiting, they will also have contact with Chinese academics.
    △ The Chinese film delegation, invited to participate in the Cannes Film Festival in France, left Beijing for France by plane on April 28. The head of the delegation is Cai Chusheng, and the members are Situ Huimin, Zhang Shuihua, Lin Yi, and Zhang Kefen (currently abroad).

### 友好往来

1956-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    △应中国红十字会邀请来我国参加“五一”节观礼和访问的保加利亚红十字会代表团于五月一日到达北京。代表团由保加利亚红十字会副主席格奥尔基·戈斯波季诺夫率领。团员是保加利亚红十字会中央理事会理事鲁缅·达米扬诺夫和伊凡·鲍隆苏佐夫。
    △以费尔南多·蒙塔尼亚尼为首的意大利总工会代表团和伊朗工会代表一人、印度尼西亚工会代表一人，在五月一日到达北京。他们是应中华全国总工会邀请来中国参加“五一”节观礼和访问的。
    △以乔治·斯麦茨为首的比利时文化代表团十三人一日乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请前来中国访问的。代表团由比利时文化界著名人士组成。他们除参观、访问外，并将和中国学术界人士进行接触。
    △应邀参加在法国举行的戛纳电影节的中国电影代表团，已经在四月二十八日乘飞机离北京去法国。代表团团长是蔡楚生，团员是司徒慧敏、张水华、林艺和张可奋（现在国外）。
