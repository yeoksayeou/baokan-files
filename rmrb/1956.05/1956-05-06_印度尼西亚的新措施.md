Title: New Measures in Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1956-05-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 4th — The Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament, Sardono, today sent a telegram to the United Nations Secretary-General, Hammarskjöld, informing him of the resolution passed by the Indonesian Parliament condemning nuclear weapons testing.
    This resolution was unanimously passed by the Indonesian Parliament on April 21st. The resolution specifically opposes such tests near Indonesia and the Asia-Africa continents.

### 印度尼西亚的新措施

1956-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚的新措施
    新华社雅加达4日电　印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺今天打电报给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，把印度尼西亚国会通过的谴责核子武器试验的决议通知了他。
    这个决议是印度尼西亚国会在四月二十一日一致通过的。决议特别反对在印度尼西亚附近和亚非两洲进行这种试验。
