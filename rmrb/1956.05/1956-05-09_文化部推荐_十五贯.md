Title: Ministry of Culture Recommends "Fifteen Strings of Cash"
Author:
Date: 1956-05-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    Ministry of Culture Recommends "Fifteen Strings of Cash"
    The Ministry of Culture recently issued a notice hoping that local opera troupes can perform the opera "Fifteen Strings of Cash," a drama with profound educational significance.
    The Zhejiang Kunqu Opera Troupe's performance script of "Fifteen Strings of Cash" will be published in the June issue of "Script" monthly, and will also be published as a separate volume by People's Literature Publishing House.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 文化部推荐“十五贯”

1956-05-09
第3版()
专栏：

    文化部推荐“十五贯”
    文化部在最近发出通知，希望各地戏曲剧团都能演出有着深刻教育意义的剧目“十五贯”。
    浙江省昆苏剧团的“十五贯”演出本将在“剧本”月刊六月号上刊登；并且将由人民文学出版社出版单印本。
                      （新华社）
