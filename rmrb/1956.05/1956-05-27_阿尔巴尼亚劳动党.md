Author: (Hua)

    Albanian Labour Party
    The predecessor of the Albanian Labour Party was the Albanian Communist Party, which was founded on November 8, 1941. After its establishment, the Party led the Albanian people in the struggle against German and Italian fascism for freedom and independence, and gradually grew and developed in this struggle.
    In September 1942, under the leadership of the Party, the National Liberation Front (later renamed the Democratic Front) was established, uniting all anti-fascist democratic forces in Albania. In July 1943, thanks to the Party's leading role, the Albanian People's Liberation Army was formed on the basis of guerrilla units. This army later developed rapidly in combat and inflicted severe blows on the fascist invaders in Albania.
    On May 24, 1944, under the leadership of the Party, the first National Liberation Council Congress in Albanian history—the famous Congress of Përmet—was held. At this congress, a provisional people's government was established, laying an important foundation for the establishment of Albania's people's democratic system.
    After the liberation of Albania on November 29, 1944, the Albanian Communist Party became the leading force in the new Albania, leading the people to complete the restoration of the national economy and establish and consolidate the people's democratic system.
    In November 1948, the Albanian Communist Party held its First Congress, adopted the Party Constitution, and resolved to change the name of the Albanian Communist Party to the Albanian Labour Party.
    In March and April 1952, the Albanian Labour Party held its Second Congress and adopted Albania's first Five-Year Plan (1951-1955). The purpose of this plan was to build the foundations of socialism in Albania, transform Albania from a backward agricultural country into an industrial-agricultural country, further consolidate the worker-peasant alliance under the leadership of the working class, and improve the material and cultural living standards of the people.
    The first Five-Year Plan has now been successfully completed, with industrial output in 1955 increasing by 179% compared to 1950, equivalent to ten and a half times that of 1938.
    The Third Congress of the Albanian Labour Party, which is currently being held, will adopt the summary reports of the Central Committee and the Central Inspection Committee, the summary report on the First Five-Year Plan, and the report on the guidelines for the Second Five-Year Plan, and will revise the Party Constitution and elect the Party's central organs. This congress is of great significance for the further development of socialist construction in Albania.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党

1956-05-27
第4版()
专栏：资料

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的前身是阿尔巴尼亚共产党，它是在1941年11月8日成立的。成立以后，党就领导阿尔巴尼亚人民进行反对德、意法西斯、争取自由独立的斗争，并在这个斗争中逐渐成长发展起来。
    1942年9月，在党的领导下建立了民族解放阵线（后改名为民主阵线），把阿尔巴尼亚的一切反法西斯民主力量团结在一起。1943年7月，由于党的领导作用，在游击队的基础上成立了阿尔巴尼亚人民解放军。这支军队后来在战斗中得到了迅速的发展，曾给予阿尔巴尼亚境内的法西斯侵略者以严重的打击。
    1944年5月24日，在党的领导下举行了阿尔巴尼亚历史上第一次民族解放委员会代表大会——著名的波列梅特代表大会。在这次大会上建立了临时的人民政权，为阿尔巴尼亚人民民主制度的确立奠定了重要的基础。
    1944年11月29日阿尔巴尼亚解放以后，阿尔巴尼亚共产党成为新阿尔巴尼亚的领导力量，它领导人民完成了国民经济的恢复工作，建立和巩固了人民民主制度。
    1948年11月，阿尔巴尼亚共产党举行了第一次代表大会，通过了党章，并决议把阿尔巴尼亚共产党改称为阿尔巴尼亚劳动党。
    1952年三、四月间，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党举行第二次代表大会，通过了阿尔巴尼亚第一个五年计划（1951—1955年）。这个计划的目的是在阿尔巴尼亚建设社会主义基础，使阿尔巴尼亚从落后的农业国家改变成为一个工业—农业国，并进一步巩固在工人阶级领导下的工农联盟，提高人民的物质和文化生活水平。
    现在第一个五年计划已经胜利完成，1955年工业产值比1950年增加了179％，等于1938年的十倍半。
    目前正在举行的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第三次代表大会，将通过中央委员会和中央检查委员会的总结报告、关于第一个五年计划的总结报告和关于第二个五年计划的指示的报告，并修改党章，选举党的中央机关。这次代表大会对阿尔巴尼亚社会主义建设的进一步发展具有重大的意义。
                              （华）
