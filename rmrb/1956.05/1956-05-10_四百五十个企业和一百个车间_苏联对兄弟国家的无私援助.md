Title: Four Hundred and Fifty Enterprises and One Hundred Workshops: The Soviet Union's Unselfish Assistance to Fraternal Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th Dispatch from TASS: The Soviet Union is currently assisting people's democracies in building four hundred and fifty enterprises and nearly one hundred separate workshops.

	Smelov, Deputy Director of the Soviet Central Economic Relations Bureau, told a reporter from "Moscow Truth": After these enterprises are put into production, China, Poland, Bulgaria, and other people's democracies can increase annual production by about ten million tons of steel, nearly seventy thousand tons of copper, and mine an additional twenty-five million tons of coal.

	The Soviet people are very satisfied with the achievements made by China in socialist industrialization. The Soviet Union is helping China with the design of factories and providing equipment for the construction and renovation of more than two hundred industrial enterprises in China.

	The workers of two hundred large factories in Moscow and the Moscow region are working hard to produce orders for the people's democracies, including the Podolsk Heavy Machinery Manufacturing Plant, the Moscow "Red Proletarian" Tractor Manufacturing Plant, and so on. These factories produce machine tools, automatic production lines, pumps, boilers, and many other equipments for them.

### 四百五十个企业和一百个车间  苏联对兄弟国家的无私援助

1956-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　四百五十个企业和一百个车间
    苏联对兄弟国家的无私援助
    新华社9日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：苏联现在正在帮助人民民主国家建设四百五十个企业和将近一百个单独的车间。
    苏联中央经济关系局副局长斯麦洛夫对“莫斯科真理报”记者说：这些企业投入生产以后，中国、波兰、保加利亚和其他人民民主国家每年可以增产约一千万吨钢，将近七万吨铜，多开采两千五百万吨煤。
    苏联人对中国在社会主义工业化方面所取得的成就感到非常满意。苏联正在帮助中国进行工厂的设计工作，并且向中国两百多个工业企业提供新建和改建用的设备。
    莫斯科市和莫斯科州有两百个大工厂的职工在紧张地替人民民主国家生产订货，其中包括波多尔重型机器制造厂、莫斯科“红色无产者”拖拉机制造厂等等。这些工厂为它们生产机床、自动作业线、抽水机、锅炉和很多其它设备。
