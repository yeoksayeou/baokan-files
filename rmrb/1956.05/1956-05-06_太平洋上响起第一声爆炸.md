Title: The First Explosion Sounds in the Pacific Ocean
Author:
Date: 1956-05-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. According to reports from American news agencies, the United States government, disregarding the protests of public opinion from all countries, began a series of nuclear weapon tests in the Pacific Trust Territory on May 5th. This morning, an explosion was carried out on an island in the Eniwetok Atoll, and several more will be carried out in the future.

### 太平洋上响起第一声爆炸

1956-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    太平洋上响起第一声爆炸
    新华社5日讯　据美国通讯社报道，美国政府不顾世界各国公众的抗议，五月五日开始在太平洋托管地区举行一系列的核子武器试验。今天早上，已经在恩纳威托克珊瑚岛中的一个岛上进行了一次爆炸，以后还要继续进行若干次。
