Title: Pakistan's Prime Minister Postpones Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1956-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 12th. Pakistan's Prime Minister of the Islamic Republic, Muhammad Ali, and Foreign Minister Hamidul Huq Choudhury, who were originally scheduled to depart for a friendly visit to China on May 15th at the invitation of the Chinese government, have decided to postpone their visit to China until June 2nd after consulting with the Chinese government.

### 巴基斯坦总理延期来我国访问

1956-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理延期来我国访问
    新华社12日讯　应中国政府邀请原定五月十五日动身来中国作友好访问的巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总理穆罕默德·阿里和外交部长哈米杜尔·哈克·乔德里，经商得中国政府的同意后，决定延迟到六月二日来中国访问。
