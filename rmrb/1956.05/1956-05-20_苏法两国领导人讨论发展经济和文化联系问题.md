Title: Soviet and French Leaders Discuss Developing Economic and Cultural Ties
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 19th: The Soviet government delegation and the French government delegation continued their talks in a friendly and mutually understanding atmosphere in the Kremlin on May 18th.

    Participating in today's talks were Bulganin, Khrushchev, and Molotov (on one side) and French Prime Minister Mollet and Foreign Minister Pineau (on the other side).

    During the talks, opinions were exchanged on the development of economic and cultural ties between the Soviet Union and France. The representatives of the two governments held consistent views on many aspects of these issues. It has now been decided to entrust experts from both sides to draft corresponding proposals based on the exchange of views, so that these proposals can be further discussed.

    At the meeting, the representatives of both sides were also entrusted with drafting a final statement of the negotiations between the Soviet government delegation and the French government delegation.

### 苏法两国领导人讨论发展经济和文化联系问题

1956-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏法两国领导人讨论发展经济和文化联系问题
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府代表团和法国政府代表团五月十八日继续在克里姆林宫在友好的和互相谅解的气氛中进行会谈。
    参加今天会谈的有布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫、莫洛托夫（一方）同法国总理摩勒和外交部长比诺（另一方）。
    在会谈时就发展苏联和法国的经济联系和文化联系的问题交换了意见。两国政府的代表在这些问题的许多方面所持的观点是一致的。现在已经决定委托双方的专家根据所交换的意见拟定相应的建议，以便就这些建议作进一步的讨论。
    在会议上还委托双方代表草拟苏联政府代表团和法国政府代表团谈判的最后声明草案。
