Title: Burma Selects Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, May 5th. A Burmese student delegation of seven members will attend the Asian-African Student Conference to be held in Bandung, Indonesia on May 26th. The members of the delegation were elected on May 4th at a joint meeting of the Rangoon University Students' Union, the All-Burma Students' Union, and the All-Rangoon Students' Union.

### 缅甸选出代表团

1956-05-07
第4版()
专栏：亚非学生会议新闻

    缅甸选出代表团
    新华社仰光5日电　一个由七人组成的缅甸学生代表团将出席五月二十六日在印度尼西亚万隆举行的亚非学生会议。代表团的团员是五月四日在仰光大学生联盟、全缅学生联合会和全仰光学生联盟的联席会议上选出来的。
