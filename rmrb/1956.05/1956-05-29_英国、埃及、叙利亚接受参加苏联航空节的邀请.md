Title: Britain, Egypt, Syria Accept Invitations to Attend Soviet Aviation Day

Author:
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency

    Britain, Egypt, Syria Accept Invitations to Attend Soviet Aviation Day
    According to a Reuters dispatch from London: The British Air Ministry announced on the 26th that British Air Minister Birch had accepted the invitation from Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Bulganin to visit the Soviet Union and participate in the Soviet Aviation Day on June 24th.
    According to a Xinhua dispatch from Cairo: Egyptian Minister of National Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, Amer, said on May 27th that Egypt had accepted the Soviet invitation and would send a delegation to participate in the Soviet Aviation Day on June 24th.
    According to a broadcast from Beirut Radio, Syria has also accepted the same invitation.

### 英国、埃及、叙利亚接受参加苏联航空节的邀请

1956-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    英国、埃及、叙利亚接受参加苏联航空节的邀请
    据新华社讯　据路透社伦敦消息：英国空军部二十六日宣布，英国空军大臣伯奇接受了苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁的邀请，将到苏联去参加在六月二十四日举行的苏联航空节。
    据新华社讯　开罗消息：埃及国防部长兼武装部队总司令阿密尔五月二十七日说，埃及已经接受了苏联的邀请，将派遣一个代表团去参加在六月二十四日举行的苏联航空节。
    另据贝鲁特电台广播，叙利亚也已经接受了同样的邀请。
