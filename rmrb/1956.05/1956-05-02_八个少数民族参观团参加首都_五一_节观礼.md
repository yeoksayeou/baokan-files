Title: Eight Minority Nationalities' Visiting Groups Participate in National Day Observances in the Capital

Author:

Date: 1956-05-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 1st. Eight visiting groups from minority nationalities from provinces such as Yunnan, Gansu, Qinghai, Jilin, Guangxi, Sichuan, and the Tibet region arrived in Beijing successively at the end of April and participated in the May Day observances in the capital today. The visiting groups comprised more than 580 people, including more than 20 nationalities such as Mongolian, Hui, Tibetan, Miao, Yi, Zhuang, Korean, and Dai.

The eight visiting groups are: the Yunnan Province Dai Buddhist Visiting Group headed by Huba Meng (Dai), the Gansu Province Pastoral Area Minority Nationalities Visiting Group headed by Daji (Tibetan), the Qinghai Province Minority Nationalities Visiting Group headed by Wulan (Mongolian), the Jilin Province Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture Nationalities Visiting Group headed by Cui Cai (Korean), the Yunnan Province Nationalities Visiting Group headed by Zhang Min, the Guangxi Province Nationalities Visiting Group headed by Mo Jin (Zhuang), the Tibet Youth Sports Observation Group headed by Suokang Jinmei (Tibetan), and the Sichuan Province Nationalities Youth Visiting Group headed by Da Pengzhi (Hui).

### 八个少数民族参观团参加首都“五一”节观礼

1956-05-02
第1版()
专栏：

    八个少数民族参观团参加首都“五一”节观礼
    新华社1日讯　来自云南、甘肃、青海、吉林、广西、四川等省和西藏地区的八个少数民族参观团，在四月底先后到达北京，今天参加了首都五一节观礼。各参观团共有五百八十多人，包括蒙古、回、藏、苗、彝、僮、朝鲜、傣等二十多个民族。
    这八个参观团是：以祜巴勐（傣）为首的云南省傣族佛教参观团，以达吉（藏）为首的甘肃省牧区少数民族参观团，以乌兰（蒙古）为首的青海省少数民族参观团，以崔采（朝鲜）为首的吉林省延边朝鲜族自治州各民族参观团，以张敏为首的云南省各民族参观团，以莫矜（僮）为首的广西省各民族参观团，以索康晋美（藏）为首的西藏青年体育观摩团，以达鹏志（回）为首的四川省各民族青年参观团。
