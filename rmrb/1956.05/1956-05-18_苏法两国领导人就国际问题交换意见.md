Title: Soviet and French Leaders Exchange Views on International Issues
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 17th: According to TASS, the first meeting between Bulganin, Khrushchev, and Molotov, and French Prime Minister Mollet and Foreign Minister Pineau, on the morning of May 16th, took place in a sincere and friendly atmosphere.

The two sides agreed that, during the talks, they would extensively exchange views on issues concerning Franco-Soviet relations and general international issues of concern to both countries. During the first meeting, the two sides held general discussions on a number of issues and frankly exchanged views on the issue of disarmament.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 17th: TASS Moscow, May 17th: The Soviet government delegation and the French government delegation continued their talks at the Kremlin at 10:00 AM (Moscow time) on May 17th. The talks took place in a sincere and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 17th: TASS Moscow, May 17th: The Soviet and French government delegations continued their talks at the Kremlin at 4:00 PM (Moscow time) on May 17th.

### 苏法两国领导人就国际问题交换意见

1956-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

    苏法两国领导人就国际问题交换意见
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：五月十六日上午十时，布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫和莫洛托夫同法国总理摩勒和外交部长比诺举行的第一次会谈，是在诚恳和友好的气氛中进行的。
    双方一致同意，在会谈期间就法苏关系问题和两国所关心的一般国际问题广泛地交换意见。在第一次会谈中，双方就一些问题进行了一般性的讨论，并且就裁军问题坦率地交换了意见。
    新华社17日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：苏联政府代表团和法国政府代表团，五月十七日上午十时（莫斯科时间）在克里姆林宫继续会谈。会谈是在真诚友好的气氛中进行的。
    新华社17日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：苏联和法国两国政府代表团，十七日下午四时（莫斯科时间）在克里姆林宫继续会谈。
