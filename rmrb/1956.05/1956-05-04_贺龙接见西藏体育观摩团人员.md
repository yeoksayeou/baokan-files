Title: He Long Receives Personnel from the Tibetan Sports Observation Group
Author:
Date: 1956-05-04
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, He Long, received all members of the Tibetan Sports Observation Group visiting Beijing this afternoon - the head of the group, Soikang Jigme, the deputy head of the group, Qulo Basang, and fifty-two members. After the reception, all members of the observation group visited the training hall and swimming pool of the Beijing Gymnasium.
　　The Tibetan Sports Observation Group came to Beijing from Tianjin on April 27. They participated in the May 1st International Labor Day observation ceremony in Beijing. The men's basketball team in the observation group held a friendly match with the men's basketball team of the Central Institute for Nationalities on May 2nd, and they defeated the Central Institute for Nationalities team by a score of 91 to 78.

### 贺龙接见西藏体育观摩团人员

1956-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　贺龙接见西藏体育观摩团人员
    新华社3日讯　国务院副总理兼体育运动委员会主任贺龙，今天下午接见了正在北京访问的西藏体育观摩团全体人员——团长索康计美，副团长曲洛巴桑和五十二名团员。接见以后，观摩团全体人员参观了北京体育馆的练习馆和游泳馆。
    西藏体育观摩团是四月二十七日由天津来北京的。他们在北京参加了“五一”国际劳动节观礼。观摩团中的男子篮球队五月二日同中央民族学院的男子篮球队举行了一场友谊比赛，他们以九十一比七十八胜中央民族学院队。
