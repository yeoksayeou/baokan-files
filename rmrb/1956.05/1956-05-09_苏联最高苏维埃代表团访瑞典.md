Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Visits Sweden
Author:
Date: 1956-05-09
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, 8th dispatch: According to TASS, the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation arrived in Stockholm on May 7th and was warmly welcomed by the Speakers of both the Upper and Lower Houses of the Swedish Parliament and representatives of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The delegation is led by Volkov, Chairman of the Union Council of the Supreme Soviet. They are visiting in return at the invitation of the Swedish Parliament. The Swedish parliamentary delegation visited the Soviet Union last year at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团访瑞典

1956-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃代表团访瑞典
    新华社8日讯  据塔斯社讯：苏联最高苏维埃代表团五月七日到达斯德哥尔摩，受到瑞典议会上、下两院议长和外交部代表的热烈欢迎。这个代表团由最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫率领，他们是应瑞典议会的邀请前去进行回访的。瑞典议会代表团在去年应邀访问了苏联。
