Title: Liao Luyan Hosts Banquet for Czechoslovak Experts
Author:
Date: 1956-05-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 21st: Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the seven Czechoslovak experts headed by Nadernaski. The Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Grigor, was also invited to attend the banquet.

Nadernaski and the other experts arrived in Beijing on the 19th. They are here to help China install a complete set of equipment for a state-owned farm, which was a gift from the Czechoslovak government to China when Vice Chairman Zhu De visited the Czechoslovak Republic.

Tonight, Minister of State Farms and Land Reclamation Wang Zhen, Vice Minister of Agriculture Zhang Linchi, and others attended the banquet as guests.

Before the banquet, Liao Luyan met with the experts from Czechoslovakia.

The equipment given to China by the Czechoslovak government will be used to establish a state-owned farm in Huanghua County, Hebei Province. The farm will be named the "Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Farm."

### 廖鲁言欢宴捷克斯洛伐克专家

1956-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    廖鲁言欢宴捷克斯洛伐克专家
    新华社21日讯　农业部长廖鲁言今晚设宴欢迎以那德尔那斯基为首的七位捷克斯洛伐克专家。捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使格里哥尔也应邀出席了宴会。
    那德尔那斯基等专家在十九日到达北京，他们是前来帮助中国安装一个国营农场的全套设备，这套设备是朱德副主席访问捷克斯洛伐克共和国的时候，捷克斯洛伐克政府送给中国的。
    今晚，农垦部长王震、农业部副部长张林池等都出席宴会作陪。
    在宴会以前，廖鲁言曾接见了来自捷克斯洛伐克的专家们。
    捷克斯洛伐克政府送给中国的设备，将用来在河北省黄骅县建立一座国营农场，农场将命名为“中捷友谊农场”。
