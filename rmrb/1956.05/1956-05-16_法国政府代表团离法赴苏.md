Title: French Government Delegation Leaves France for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-05-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 15th. TASS, Paris dispatch: The French government delegation, composed of Prime Minister Mollet, Foreign Minister Pineau, and a group of experts and advisors, accompanied by Soviet Ambassador to France Vinogradov, left Paris by special plane for Moscow at 8:15 AM on May 15th, Paris time.

Those who saw them off at the airport included cabinet ministers such as Gazier and Gilbert Jules, General Petit, President of the France-USSR Association, as well as representatives of the public and the press.

### 法国政府代表团离法赴苏

1956-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    法国政府代表团离法赴苏
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社巴黎讯：由摩勒总理、外交部长比诺和一批专家及顾问组成的法国政府代表团在苏联驻法国大使维诺格拉多夫的陪同下，在五月十五日巴黎时间八点十五分乘专机飞往莫斯科。
    到机场送行的有加齐埃、希伯尔·儒勒等内阁部长，法苏协会主席柏蒂将军以及公众代表和新闻界的代表。
