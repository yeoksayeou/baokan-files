Title: Soviet-Vietnamese Sign Exchange of Goods Protocol
Author:
Date: 1956-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th Dispatch: According to TASS, the Soviet Union and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam signed a 1956 exchange of goods protocol in Moscow on May 5th.

The protocol stipulates that the trade volume between the two countries this year will greatly increase compared to last year. The Soviet Union will supply Vietnam with various machinery equipment, tractors, agricultural machines, automobiles, petroleum products, cable products, and other items. Vietnam will supply the Soviet Union with precious timber, furniture, tea, spices, citrus fruits, handicrafts, etc.

### 苏越签订换货议定书

1956-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏越签订换货议定书
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联和越南民主共和国1956年换货议定书五月五日在莫斯科签字。
    议定书规定，两国今年的贸易额将比去年大大增加。苏联将供给越南各种机器装备、拖拉机、农业机器、汽车、石油产品、电缆制品和其他物品。越南将供给苏联贵重木材、家具、茶叶、香料、柑橘和手工艺品等。
