Title: All-China Journalists Association Hosts Reception for Journalists from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd — The All-China Journalists Association held a reception on the 3rd to entertain journalists from various countries currently visiting China.

More than 60 reporters from over 20 countries currently in Beijing and more than 200 people from Beijing's press circles attended the reception. The hosts and guests engaged in cordial conversations in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 我国新闻工作者联谊会举行酒会招待各国新闻工作者

1956-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我国新闻工作者联谊会举行酒会招待各国新闻工作者
    据新华社3日讯　中国新闻工作者联谊会三日举行酒会，招待现在中国访问的各国新闻工作者。
    出席酒会的有现在北京的二十多个国家的记者六十多人和北京新闻界人士共两百多人。宾主在热烈友好的气氛中，进行了亲切的交谈。
