Title: Cambodia's Neutral Policy Earns it More and More Friends - Sihanouk's Letter to the Cambodian People Before Visiting Europe
Author:
Date: 1956-05-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 22nd (via radio dispatch from Phnom Penh): Prince Sihanouk, in a letter to the Cambodian people before visiting European countries, said: We firmly believe that this visit will be beneficial to Cambodia and to the civilization of the whole world. It will make the whole world more aware of and cherish us. It will further consolidate our neutrality and peace, because we will increase many friends, including those in the "Free World" and the "Communist World."
    Sihanouk said: "Spain and Poland are two countries with completely different systems. Our visit to these two countries proves that our neutrality does not favor any side, that is, we do not agree to join any camp. Our attitude makes us the only country in Southeast Asia welcomed by any camp."
    Sihanouk added: "People's China recently decided to give us huge assistance within two years. This assistance does not carry any binding conditions."
    He also said that the Soviet Union, China, and Poland have formally recognized Cambodia's full independent sovereignty. The United States, Thailand, and South Vietnam have agreed to open the borders with Cambodia.

### 柬埔寨执行中立政策使它得到越来越多的友人  西哈努克在赴欧访问前写给柬埔寨人民的信

1956-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨执行中立政策使它得到越来越多的友人
    ·西哈努克在赴欧访问前写给柬埔寨人民的信·
    新华社河内22日电　据金边电台广播：西哈努克亲王在赴欧洲各国访问以前写给柬埔寨人民的信中说：我们深信这一次的访问将有利于柬埔寨，同时有利于全世界的文明。将会使全世界更加了解和爱护我们。会更加使得我们的中立和和平得到进一步的巩固，因为我们会增加许多包括“自由世界”和“共产世界”的朋友。
    西哈努克说：“西班牙和波兰是两个具有截然不同制度的国家。我们前往这两个国家，这证明我们的中立不偏向于任何一方，那就是不赞成加入某一个阵营。我们的态度使我们成为东南亚的唯一的受到任何阵营欢迎的国家。”
    西哈努克又说：“人民中国最近决定在两年内给我们巨大的援助。这个援助并没有带有任何约束的条件。”
    他还说，苏联、中国和波兰已经正式承认柬埔寨有充分的独立主权。美国、泰国和南越业已同意开放和柬埔寨之间的边境。
