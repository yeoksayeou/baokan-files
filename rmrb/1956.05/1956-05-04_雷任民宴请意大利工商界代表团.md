Title: Lei Renmin Hosts Banquet for Italian Business Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-05-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 3rd to entertain the entire Italian business delegation.
    Those present included Li Zhuchen, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Xiao Fangzhou, Deputy Secretary-General, and the heads of various import and export corporations of China.

### 雷任民宴请意大利工商界代表团

1956-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　雷任民宴请意大利工商界代表团
    据新华社3日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民三日晚设宴招待意大利工商界代表团全体人员。
    出席作陪的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席李烛尘，副秘书长萧方洲和中国进出口各专业公司负责人。
