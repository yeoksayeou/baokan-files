Title: Nie Rongzhen Leaves North Korea for Home

Author:

Date: 1956-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th - Nie Rongzhen, head of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee delegation participating in the Third Congress of the Korean Workers' Party, and delegation member Wang Congwu, left North Korea by car today to return home.

During their stay in Pyongyang, and on their way back home from Pyongyang on the 4th, the delegation visited some factories and schools in Korea.

### 聂荣臻离朝鲜回国

1956-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻离朝鲜回国
    新华社平壤11日电　参加朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会的中国共产党中央委员会代表团团长聂荣臻和团员王从吾今日乘车离开朝鲜回国。
    代表团一行在平壤逗留期间和在四日离平壤回国途中，曾经参观了朝鲜的一些工厂和学校。
