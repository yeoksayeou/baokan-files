Title: Polish Embassy Holds Reception to Celebrate Polish Education, Book and Publication Festival
Author:
Date: 1956-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 29th: The Polish Embassy in China held a reception this evening to celebrate the Polish Education, Book and Publication Festival.

Ding Xilin and Xia Yan, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, and heads of various news and publishing organizations, as well as more than sixty Chinese and foreign journalists in Beijing, attended today's reception.

At the reception, people saw many beautifully printed books, newspapers, and magazines published in Poland, including Polish versions of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," "Selected Works of Guo Moruo," "The Hurricane," and "The White-Haired Girl" and other Chinese books.

Poland has celebrated this festival annually since 1946, and this year's festival is being held from May 19th to June 3rd.

### 庆祝波兰教育、书籍和出版节  波兰大使馆举行酒会

1956-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝波兰教育、书籍和出版节
    波兰大使馆举行酒会
    新华社29日讯　波兰驻中国大使馆今天晚上举行酒会，庆祝波兰教育、书籍和出版节。
    中华人民共和国文化部副部长丁西林、夏衍和各新闻出版机关的负责人及在北京的中外记者六十多人，出席了今天的酒会。
    人们在酒会上看到了波兰出版的许多印刷精美的书报和杂志，其中有波文版的“毛泽东选集”、“郭沫若选集”、“暴风骤雨”、“白毛女”等中国书籍。
    波兰从1946年以来都一年一度地庆祝这个节日，今年的节日在五月十九日到六月三日举行。
