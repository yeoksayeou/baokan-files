Title: Ho Chi Minh Receives Women's Delegations from China, Germany, Korea, and Others
Author:
Date: 1956-05-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 25th: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam received all members of the women's delegations from China, the German Democratic Republic, and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, as well as representatives from the Women's International Democratic Federation at 7:00 AM today. They were all invited to attend the Second National Women's Congress of Vietnam.

    The Second National Women's Congress of Vietnam, originally scheduled to open on May 25th, has now been rescheduled to begin on the afternoon of May 26th.

    In recent days, the women's delegations from various countries have been visiting Hanoi and touring the city's famous historical sites.

### 胡志明接见中德朝等国妇女代表团

1956-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明接见中德朝等国妇女代表团
    新华社河内25日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明在今天上午七时，接见了中国、德意志民主共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的妇女代表团全体人员以及国际民主妇女联合会的代表们。她们都是应邀来参加越南全国妇女第二届代表大会的。
    越南全国妇女第二届代表大会原定在五月二十五日开幕，现在已经决定改期在五月二十六日下午举行。
    连日来，各国妇女代表们在河内进行了参观访问，并且游览了河内的名胜古迹。
