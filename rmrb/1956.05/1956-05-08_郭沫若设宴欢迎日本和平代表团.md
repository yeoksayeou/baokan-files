Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet to Welcome Japanese Peace Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet today to welcome the head of the Japanese Peace Delegation, Kyoshi Ishikawa, and all its members, who are visiting China at the invitation of the China Peace Committee. Japanese peace advocate Ryusuke Miyazaki and his wife also attended the banquet.

Among those present were Chen Shutong and Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairmen of the China Peace Committee, and well-known figures from various circles, including Chen Hansheng, Zhou Peiyuan, Wei Lihuang, Fang Shishan, Hou Jingru, and Fang Ming.

### 郭沫若设宴欢迎日本和平代表团

1956-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若设宴欢迎日本和平代表团
    新华社7日讯  中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天设宴欢迎应邀来中国访问的日本和平代表团团长石河京市和全体团员。参加宴会的还有日本和平人士宫崎龙介和夫人。
    出席作陪的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通、廖承志和各界著名人士陈翰笙、周培源、卫立煌、方石珊、侯镜如、方明等人。
