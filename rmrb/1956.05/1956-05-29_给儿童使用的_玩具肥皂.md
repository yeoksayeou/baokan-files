Title: "Toy Soap" for Children

Author:

Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 3

    "Toy Soap" for Children
    A special "toy soap" for children will meet them on International Children's Day, June 1st. This was specially designed and manufactured by the state-owned Shanghai Soap Factory for children. The "toy soap" has a beautiful appearance, with six styles including a dove of peace, a little white rabbit, a goldfish, a frog, and an elephant, and four colors including red, yellow, emerald green, etc. This soap can arouse children's interest in using it. (Our correspondent in Shanghai)

### 给儿童使用的“玩具肥皂”

1956-05-29
第3版()
专栏：

    给儿童使用的“玩具肥皂”
    一种专门给儿童使用的“玩具肥皂”将在“六一”国际儿童节和孩子们见面。这是国营上海制皂厂专门为儿童设计制造的。“玩具肥皂”的外形美观，有和平鸽、小白兔、金鱼、青蛙、大象等六种式样，颜色分红黄翠绿等四种。这种肥皂可以引起儿童使用兴趣。　（本报驻上海记者）
