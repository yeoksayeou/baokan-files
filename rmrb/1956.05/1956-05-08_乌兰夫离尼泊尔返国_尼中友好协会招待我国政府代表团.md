Title: Ulanhu Departs Nepal for Return to China; Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association Hosts Chinese Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-05-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, May 7th: The Chinese government delegation participating in the coronation ceremony of the King of Nepal, led by Special Envoy Ulanhu, left Kathmandu by plane today to return to China.

Prime Minister Acharya of Nepal, along with other Nepalese government officials, saw them off at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, May 7th: Ulanhu, the Chinese special envoy and Vice Premier who came to participate in the coronation ceremony of the King of Nepal, delivered a farewell speech on the Kathmandu Nepal Broadcasting Station on May 6th.

In his speech, Ulanhu expressed his gratitude to the Nepalese government and people for the warm hospitality the Chinese delegation received in Kathmandu, and he hoped that the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Nepal would be further consolidated and developed.

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, May 7th: The Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association held a reception in Kathmandu on the evening of May 6th to host the Chinese government delegation headed by Vice Premier Ulanhu, which came to participate in the coronation ceremony of the King of Nepal.

Ulanhu spoke at the reception. After mentioning the long history of friendship between China and Nepal, he said: The friendly and cooperative relations between China and Nepal, established in the course of history, have been further developed on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, especially since the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Nepal last year.

Ulanhu said: The Chinese people are now engaged in large-scale national construction. There are many areas where we can learn from each other, and we can help each other in many ways. The Chinese people are very willing to further promote economic cooperation, mutual assistance, and cultural exchanges between our two countries in accordance with the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and in accordance with the Bandung spirit.

Ulanhu concluded by saying that the Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association has done much useful work in further promoting friendly cooperation between the people of Nepal and China, and he believes that the association will make more contributions in the future.

Chairman Purni Bahadur of the Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association welcomed the Chinese guests, saying that diplomatic relations between Nepal and China have been restored at the highest level, and that economic, commercial, and cultural contacts will help strengthen friendly relations. He said that the Chinese delegation headed by Ulanhu, which came to participate in the coronation ceremony of the King of Nepal, also served as a goodwill mission, paving the way for further promoting cooperation between the two countries in all aspects.

Former Nepalese Prime Minister P. Koirala also delivered a brief speech at the reception.

Representatives of mass organizations, famous artists, and poets attended the reception. Also present was Asta Mangal, Speaker of the Kathmandu City Council. A seventy-five-year-old Nepalese doctor also attended the reception. He had visited China during the Qing Dynasty when he was only nineteen years old. He still remembers some Chinese words.

Chairman Bahadur of the Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association presented gifts to the Chinese delegation on behalf of the association. A cultural program was performed at the reception, and the famous Nepalese poet Chitadhar recited his poem welcoming the Chinese guests.

### 乌兰夫离尼泊尔返国  尼中友好协会招待我国政府代表团

1956-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　乌兰夫离尼泊尔返国
    尼中友好协会招待我国政府代表团
    新华社加德满都7日电  参加尼泊尔国王加冕典礼的中国政府代表团，在乌兰夫特使的率领下，今天乘飞机离开加德满都回国。
    尼泊尔首相阿查里雅偕同其他尼泊尔政府官员到机场送行。
    新华社加德满都7日电  前来参加尼泊尔国王加冕典礼的中国特使、副总理乌兰夫五月六日在加德满都尼泊尔广播电台发表了告别演说。
    乌兰夫在演说中对中国代表团在加德满都期间所受到的热情招待向尼泊尔政府和人民表示感谢，并且希望中国和尼泊尔的友好合作关系日益巩固和发展。
    新华社加德满都7日电  尼中友好协会五月六日晚上在加德满都举行招待会，招待前来参加尼泊尔国王加冕典礼的以乌兰夫副总理为首的中国政府代表团。
    乌兰夫在会上讲了话。他在谈到中国和尼泊尔两国有悠久历史的友谊以后说：在历史的年代中建立起来的中尼两国之间的友好合作关系，已经在和平共处的五项原则的基础上得到了进一步的发展，特别是自从去年中尼两国建立了外交关系以来。
    乌兰夫说：中国人民现在正在大规模从事他们的国家建设。有很多地方我们可以相互学习，而且在许多方面可以彼此帮助。中国人民非常愿意根据和平共处的五项原则和遵照万隆精神在我们两国之间进一步促进经济合作和相互帮助以及文化交流。
    乌兰夫最后说，尼中友好协会在进一步促进尼泊尔和中国人民之间的友好合作方面已经作了许多有益的工作，他相信这个协会今后将作出更多的贡献。
    尼中友好协会主席普尔尼·巴哈杜尔对中国客人们表示欢迎，他说，尼泊尔和中国之间的外交关系已经在最高级水平上恢复了，经济、商务以及文化接触将有助于加强友好关系。他说，以乌兰夫为首的前来参加尼泊尔国王加冕典礼的中国代表团也作为一个亲善的使团，为进一步促进两国在各方面的合作铺平了道路。
    出席招待会的尼泊尔前首相普·柯伊拉腊在会上也发表了简短的讲话。
    出席招待会的有群众团体的代表、著名艺术家和诗人。出席招待会的还有加德满都市议会议长阿斯塔·曼加尔。一位七十五岁的尼泊尔医生也出席了招待会。他在清朝年代曾访问过中国，那时他才十九岁。他依然记得一些中国话。
    尼中友好协会主席巴哈杜尔代表协会把礼物赠给了中国代表团。招待会上演出了文艺节目，尼泊尔著名诗人奇塔达尔朗诵了他的欢迎中国客人的诗。
