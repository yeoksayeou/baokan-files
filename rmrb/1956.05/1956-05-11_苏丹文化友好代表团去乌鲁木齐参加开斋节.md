Title: Sudanese Cultural Friendship Delegation Goes to Urumqi to Participate in Eid al-Fitr
Author:
Date: 1956-05-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Izz al-Din Ali Amer, head of the Sudanese Cultural Friendship Delegation, and seven members of the delegation, left Beijing by plane this morning to go to Urumqi to participate in the Islamic Eid al-Fitr. Two other members of the delegation remain in Beijing for visits.

After arriving in Beijing, Izz al-Din Ali Amer and others visited the Yongding River diversion project site and toured the Forbidden City.

### 苏丹文化友好代表团去乌鲁木齐参加开斋节

1956-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    苏丹文化友好代表团去乌鲁木齐参加开斋节
    新华社10日讯  苏丹文化友好代表团团长伊兹尔丁·阿里·阿密尔和团员等七人，今天上午乘飞机离开北京，前往乌鲁木齐参加伊斯兰教的开斋节。代表团还有团员二人留在北京进行参观访问。
    伊兹尔丁·阿里·阿密尔等到达北京后，曾参观了永定河引水工程工地，游览了故宫。
