Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Sends Congratulations on Norwegian National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-05-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to King Haakon VII of Norway on the 15th to congratulate him on Norwegian National Day. The telegram is as follows:
His Majesty King Haakon VII of Norway:
On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Norway, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the Norwegian people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, wishing the Kingdom of Norway prosperity and the Norwegian people happiness.
Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, May 15, 1956

### 毛泽东主席致电祝贺挪威国庆

1956-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席致电祝贺挪威国庆
    新华社16日讯　毛泽东主席十五日致电挪威王国国王哈康七世，祝贺挪威国庆。电文如下：挪威王国国王哈康七世陛下：
    当挪威王国国庆的时候，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向陛下和挪威人民致热烈的祝贺，祝挪威国家繁荣，挪威人民幸福。
             中华人民共和国主席毛泽东　1956年5月15日
