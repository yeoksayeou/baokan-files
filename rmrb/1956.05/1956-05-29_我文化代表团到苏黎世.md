Title: Our Cultural Delegation Arrives in Zurich
Author:
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 26th: The Chinese Cultural Delegation, which is visiting Switzerland, arrived in Zurich on the 24th.

    On the same day, Zhang Yunxiao, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Switzerland, held a reception to introduce Dr. Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, to Swiss business leaders. More than 150 people attended. At the meeting, they discussed the possibilities of developing trade between China and Switzerland.
    On the same day, Deputy Head of the Delegation Ji Chaoding and Mao Yisheng also paid a visit to Mr. Stahling, Director of the Swiss Office for the Expansion of Foreign Trade.

### 我文化代表团到苏黎世

1956-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团到苏黎世
    据新华社布拉格26日电　在瑞士访问的中国文化代表团二十四日到达苏黎世。
    同一天，中国驻瑞士大使馆商务参赞张云啸举行招待会，介绍中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎博士同瑞士工商界人士见面。到会的有一百五十多人。会上交谈了发展中瑞两国贸易的可能性。
    这一天，副团长冀朝鼎和茅以升还拜会了瑞士对外贸易扩张处主任斯塔赫林。
