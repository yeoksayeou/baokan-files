Title: Chiang Kai-shek's Troops Shell Fujian Coastal Areas; Our Artillery Units Retaliate Fiercely
Author:
Date: 1956-05-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 23rd: On the morning and afternoon of the 22nd, Chiang Kai-shek's artillery on Kinmen Island shelled Dadeng Island twice, killing and wounding one resident each.

Since May, Chiang Kai-shek's artillery on Kinmen and Matsu Islands has been shelling our areas of Xiamen, Lianhe, Dadeng, Xiaodeng, Jiaoyu, and Huangqi every day. Up to the 22nd, Chiang Kai-shek's artillery had killed a total of four residents in coastal villages and towns, wounded eight residents, destroyed fifteen civilian houses, destroyed two civilian boats, and killed seven head of livestock such as cattle.

 The frontline artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have carried out many fierce retaliatory attacks against Chiang Kai-shek's artillery.

### 蒋军炮击福建沿海地区  我军炮兵进行猛烈还击

1956-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋军炮击福建沿海地区
    我军炮兵进行猛烈还击
    新华社福建前线23日电　二十二日早晨和下午，金门岛上蒋军炮兵向我大嶝岛炮击两次，打死、打伤居民各一人。
    自五月以来，金门、马祖岛上的蒋军炮兵每天都向我厦门、莲河、大嶝、小嶝、角屿和黄岐等地区开炮，到二十二日止，蒋军炮兵共计打死沿海村镇居民四人，打伤居民八人，打毁民房十五所，打毁民船两只，打死耕牛等牲畜七头。
      中国人民解放军前线炮兵部队对蒋军炮兵进行过多次猛烈还击。
