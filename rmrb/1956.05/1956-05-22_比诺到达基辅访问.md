Title: Bino Arrives in Kiev for Visit
Author:
Date: 1956-05-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 21st, Leningrad: According to TASS, French Foreign Minister Pineau, who is currently visiting the Soviet Union, left Leningrad for Kiev on the morning of May 21st. He was accompanied by his wife and other staff members.

Pineau gave a speech at the airport, expressing his gratitude for the warm welcome from the people of Leningrad.

Xinhua News Agency, May 21st, Kiev: According to TASS, French Foreign Minister Pineau and his entourage arrived in Kiev on the 21st. He was welcomed at the airport by the Foreign Minister of the Ukrainian Republic, Palamarchuk.

In his speech, Pineau pointed out that his visit to Kiev was to further consolidate the friendly ties between the French and Soviet peoples.

### 比诺到达基辅访问

1956-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    比诺到达基辅访问
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社列宁格勒讯：正在苏联访问的法国外交部长比诺在五月二十一日晨离开列宁格勒前往基辅。和他同行的有他的夫人和其他随员。
    比诺在机场发表了一篇谈话，对列宁格勒人的热诚欢迎表示感谢。
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社基辅讯：法国外交部长比诺和随行人员二十一日到达基辅。他在机场上受到乌克兰共和国外交部长帕拉马尔楚克的欢迎。
    比诺在讲话中指出，他到基辅来访问是为了进一步巩固法苏两国人民之间的友好联系。
