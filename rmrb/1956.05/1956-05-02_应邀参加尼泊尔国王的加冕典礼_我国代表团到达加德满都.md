Title: Our Delegation Arrives in Kathmandu to Attend the Coronation Ceremony of the King of Nepal
Author:
Date: 1956-05-02
Page: 3

　　Invited to Attend the Coronation Ceremony of the King of Nepal
　　Our Delegation Arrives in Kathmandu
　　Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, May 1st. The Special Envoy of the People's Republic of China, Vice Premier of the State Council Ulanhu, leading the delegation of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, by plane on the morning of April 30th.
　　The delegation of the People's Republic of China is invited by the Nepalese government to attend the coronation ceremony of the King of Nepal.
　　Nepalese Foreign Minister Sharma, Director of Ceremonies Singh, representatives of the Nepalese government's reception committee, and Indian Ambassador to Nepal, Sahay, and others welcomed the delegation of the People's Republic of China at the airport.
　　Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to Nepal and a member of the delegation of the People's Republic of China, also greeted them at the airport.
　　Those who came to welcome Ulanhu placed many garlands around his neck. Male and female students chanted "Nepalese and Chinese are brothers" at the airport.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, April 30th. Tanka Prasad Acharya, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, received Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, on the afternoon of April 29th.

### 应邀参加尼泊尔国王的加冕典礼  我国代表团到达加德满都

1956-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    应邀参加尼泊尔国王的加冕典礼
    我国代表团到达加德满都
    新华社加德满都1日电　中华人民共和国特使、国务院副总理乌兰夫率领的中华人民共和国代表团四月三十日上午乘飞机到达尼泊尔首都加德满都。
    中华人民共和国代表团是应尼泊尔政府的邀请来参加尼泊尔国王的加冕典礼的。
    尼泊尔外交大臣夏尔马、典礼局长辛格、尼泊尔政府接待委员会的代表们以及印度驻尼泊尔大使萨海等到机场上欢迎中华人民共和国代表团。
    中华人民共和国代表团团员、中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力也在机场上迎接。
    来欢迎的人们给乌兰夫戴上了许多花环。男女学生们在机场上欢呼“尼泊尔人和中国人是兄弟”。
    新华社加德满都30日电　尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉沙德·阿查里雅在四月二十九日下午接见了中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力。
