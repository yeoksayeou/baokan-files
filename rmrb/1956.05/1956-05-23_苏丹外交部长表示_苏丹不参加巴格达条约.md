Title: Sudanese Foreign Minister States Sudan Will Not Participate in the Baghdad Pact
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-23
Page: 4

    Sudanese Foreign Minister States
    Sudan Will Not Participate in the Baghdad Pact
    Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, May 22nd (TASS dispatch): According to Damascus Radio, Sudanese Foreign Minister Mubarak Zarrouk told reporters from the Beirut-based Arab News Agency on May 21st that Sudan will definitely not join the Baghdad Pact or any other treaty. He said that on this issue, all political parties in Sudan are completely unified.

### 苏丹外交部长表示  苏丹不参加巴格达条约

1956-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    苏丹外交部长表示
    苏丹不参加巴格达条约
    新华社22日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特二十二日讯：据大马士革电台广播，苏丹外交部长穆巴拉克·查鲁克五月二十一日对贝鲁特的阿拉伯新闻社记者表示，苏丹一定不加入巴格达条约和任何其他条约。他说，在这个问题上，苏丹的各个政党是完全一致的。
