Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Defense Minister Peng Dehuai Invite the Egyptian Prime Minister and Military Delegation to Visit China; The Egyptian Government Has Decided to Accept the Two Invitations from Our Government

Author:

Date: 1956-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China and Defense Minister Peng Dehuai of the People's Republic of China, on May 18th, sent letters of invitation to Egyptian Republic Prime Minister Nasser and Minister of the Army and Navy Amer respectively. The full text of the two letters is as follows:

His Excellency Gamal Abdel Nasser, Prime Minister of the Egyptian Republic:

On behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, I hereby invite you, Mr. Prime Minister, to visit China as a representative of the Egyptian government. I firmly believe that your visit to China will not only help further develop the friendly relations between the two countries, but will also be of great significance in promoting friendly cooperation between Asian and African countries and maintaining world peace.

If Your Excellency can accept the invitation from the Chinese government, the Chinese people will be extremely honored. We are very willing to arrange the date and schedule of your visit to China according to your wishes. I eagerly await your reply.

I extend to you, Mr. Prime Minister, my highest respect.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China (signed) May 18, 1956, in Beijing

His Excellency General Abdel Hakim Amer, Minister of the Army and Navy of the Egyptian Republic:

On behalf of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, I hereby invite a military delegation from your government to visit our country. We sincerely hope that you can accept this invitation. Please determine the date of the visit to China by your military delegation at your convenience. I firmly believe that the visit of your military delegation will further strengthen the friendship between the peoples and armies of our two countries and will help maintain world peace.

Your Excellency, please accept my highest respect.

Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China (signed) May 18, 1956, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, May 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of the Chinese government, sent a letter to Egyptian Republic Prime Minister Nasser on May 18th, inviting him to visit China. At the same time, Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, also sent a letter to Egyptian Republic Minister of the Army and Navy Amer on May 18th, inviting a military delegation from the Egyptian government to visit our country.

It is reported that the Commercial Representative of the Egyptian Republic to our country has notified our Ministry of Foreign Affairs that the government of the Egyptian Republic has decided to accept the two invitations from the Chinese government.

### 周恩来总理和国防部长彭德怀  邀请埃及总理和军事代表团来我国访问  埃及政府已决定接受我国政府的两项邀请

1956-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理和国防部长彭德怀
    邀请埃及总理和军事代表团来我国访问
    埃及政府已决定接受我国政府的两项邀请
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国总理周恩来和中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀，在五月十八日分别向埃及共和国总理纳赛尔和陆海军部长阿密尔发出了邀请信，两信全文如下：埃及共和国总理加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔阁下：
    我谨代表中华人民共和国政府邀请您，总理阁下，代表埃及政府前来中国访问。我深信，您的访问中国，不仅将有助于进一步发展两国的友好关系，而且对于促进亚非国家的友好合作和维护世界的和平，也将有重大的意义。
    如果阁下能够接受中国政府的邀请，中国人民将至感荣幸。我们极愿按照您的意愿来安排您访问中国的日期和日程。我殷切地等候您的回音。
    顺向您，总理阁下，致崇高的敬意。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来（签署）　1956年5月18日于北京
    埃及共和国陆海军部长阿卜杜勒·哈基姆·阿密尔将军阁下：
    我谨代表中华人民共和国政府国防部邀请贵国政府军事代表团前来我国访问。我们诚恳地希望你们能接受这一邀请。贵国军事代表团来中国访问的日期，请按你们的方便确定。我深信，贵国军事代表团的访问将进一步密切我们两国人民和军队的友谊，并且将有助于维护世界和平。
    阁下，请接受我崇高的敬意。
    中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀（签署）　1956年5月18日于北京
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国总理周恩来代表中国政府于五月十八日去信给埃及共和国总理纳赛尔，邀请他前来中国访问。同时，中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀也在五月十八日去信给埃及共和国陆海军部长阿密尔，邀请埃及政府军事代表团来我国访问。
    据悉，埃及共和国政府驻我国商务代表已通知我国外交部，埃及共和国政府已决定接受中国政府的两项邀请。
