Title: "Prague Spring" International Music Festival: Chinese Musicians Hold First Concert
Author:
Date: 1956-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 14th - The Chinese delegation participating in the 11th "Prague Spring" International Music Festival held its first concert on May 13th.

The concert began with a horn solo of Mozart's Concerto in E-flat Major. Chinese female pianist Wu Leyi performed Xinjiang Dance music and pieces by Chopin and Liszt. At the audience's request, she added a piece by Czech musician Suk. Yang Bide's bass solo of "Ode to the Yellow River" and a section from Tchaikovsky's opera "Eugene Onegin" were particularly warmly received by the audience. At the audience's request, he sang two encores.

The audience warmly welcomed Bao Yindelege and Fan Yulun, winners of the first prize for folk song performance at the 5th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival. Dong Ailin, a third-prize winner at the 4th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, sang "Yellow River Lament," and female singer Chen Yu sang a section from Dvořák's "Rusalka" in Czech, which also received a warm welcome. These singers all performed one encore at the audience's request.

Other programs performed by the Chinese delegation included: Ma Yinuoer, an eighteen-year-old Uyghur female singer, singing Uyghur and Uzbek folk songs; Li Xuequan playing the flute; and solos on Chinese folk instruments such as the guzheng and the Inner Mongolian sihu.

At the end of the performance, the entire audience stood up and applauded enthusiastically.

### “布拉格之春”国际音乐节  我国音乐家举行第一次音乐会

1956-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    “布拉格之春”国际音乐节
    我国音乐家举行第一次音乐会
    新华社布拉格14日电　参加第十一届“布拉格之春”国际音乐节的中国代表团，五月十三日举行了第一次音乐会。
    音乐会以圆号独奏莫扎特的降E大调协奏曲开始。中国女钢琴家吴乐懿演奏了新疆舞曲和萧邦、李斯特的曲子。在听众的鼓掌要求下，她加奏了一支捷克斯洛伐克音乐家苏克的曲子。男低音杨彼得唱的“黄河颂”和柴可夫斯基的歌剧“叶夫根尼·奥涅金”的一段，受到听众特别热烈的欢迎。在听众的要求下，他加唱了两次。
    听众热烈欢迎第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节民歌演唱一等奖获得者宝音德勒格和范裕纶的演唱。第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节三等奖获得者董爱琳唱的“黄河怨”和女歌唱家陈瑜用捷克语演唱的德沃夏克的“水仙”中的一段，也受到了热烈的欢迎。这几位演唱者应听众的要求都加唱了一次。
    中国代表团演出的其它节目是：十八岁的维吾尔族女歌唱家马依诺尔演唱维吾尔和乌兹别克民歌、李学全吹奏长笛，还有中国民间乐器古筝、内蒙古四胡独奏等。
    在演出结束的时候，全场起立热烈鼓掌。
