Title: Chen Yi Hosts the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni
Author:
Date: 1956-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 6th. Vice Premier Chen Yi, head of the Central Delegation and member of the State Council, held a banquet at the Norbulingka in Lhasa today to host the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni.

At the banquet, Chen Yi said that the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region has achieved great success and united the entire Tibet. First of all, we must thank the two masters, the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni, for their efforts and their spirit of serving the people. Chen Yi finally raised his glass and congratulated: "May our unity be as evergreen as the Tanggula Mountains and the Himalayas; may our friendship be as long-lasting as the Tongtian River and the Yarlung Zangbo River." The Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni then spoke one after another. They expressed their gratitude to the Central Delegation headed by Chen Yi for the help they gave during the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and unanimously stated that they would continue to strengthen the internal unity of Tibet and the unity of all ethnic groups in the motherland, and strive to promote progress in all aspects of Tibet.

Among those present at the banquet were the main responsible cadres of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China and the sub-working committees in various regions, the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, as well as the deputy heads and some members of the Central Delegation.

After the banquet, the art troupe that accompanied the Central Delegation to Tibet performed wonderful programs for them.

### 陈毅招待达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼

1956-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈毅招待达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼
    新华社拉萨6日电　中央代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅，今天在拉萨的尧西林卡举行宴会招待达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼。
    陈毅在宴会上讲话说，西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会得到了很大的成就，使整个西藏团结起来了，这首先要感谢达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼两位大师的努力和为人民服务的精神。陈毅最后举杯祝贺说：“愿我们的团结和唐古拉山、喜马拉雅山一样的青山不老；愿我们的友谊像通天河、雅鲁藏布江一样的绿水长流。”达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼接着先后讲话。他们对以陈毅为首的中央代表团在西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会期间所给予的帮助表示感谢，并一致表示今后要继续加强西藏内部的团结和祖国各族人民的团结，努力促进西藏各方面的进步。
    今天出席宴会作陪的有中共西藏工委和各地区分工委、中国人民解放军西藏军区、西藏自治区筹备委员会的主要负责干部，以及中央代表团的副团长和部分团员。
    宴会后，随中央代表团赴西藏的文艺工作队为他们演出了精采的节目。
