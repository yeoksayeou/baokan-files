Title: Eighteen Thousand Youths in Shanxi Join the Youth League
Author:
Date: 1956-05-11
Page: 3

    Eighteen Thousand Youths in Shanxi Join the Youth League
    In the upsurge of the socialist transformation of agriculture, handicrafts, and capitalist industry and commerce, nearly 500,000 young activists have emerged on all fronts in Shanxi Province, and 180,000 of them have joined the New Democratic Youth League. To date, the province has a total of 536,623 Youth League members.
     (Shanxi Youth Daily Editorial Department)

### 山西十八万青年加入青年团

1956-05-11
第3版()
专栏：

    山西十八万青年加入青年团
    在农业、手工业和资本主义工商业的社会主义改造的高潮中，山西省各个战线上涌现出近五十万青年积极分子，其中有十八万人已经加入了新民主主义青年团。到目前为止，全省共有团员536，623人。
     （山西青年报编辑部）
