Title: Renmin University of China to Hold Sixth Scientific Discussion Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-05-24
Page: 3

Renmin University of China
Will Hold Sixth Scientific Discussion Conference

    Renmin University of China will hold its sixth scientific discussion conference from May 26 to June 2. This conference will feature both plenary and group discussions. There are a total of forty topics for discussion. Among them are: Hu Xikui's "On the Peaceful Transformation of Capitalist Industry and Commerce and Class Struggle in Our Country," Soviet expert Birman's "On Several Issues Concerning the Profitability of Socialist Enterprises," Qi Yi's "Lu Xun's Socio-Political and Ethical Views," Hu Zhaoliang's "On the Differences of Opinion in Economic Geography," Chen Xueyi's "The Basis and Characteristics of the Alliance and Struggle between the Working Class and the National Bourgeoisie in Our Country's Transition Period," Fang Hua and Yin Ming's "The Interrelationship Between Formal Logic and Dialectical Logic in the Process of Cognition," Dai Yi's "The Periodization of Modern Chinese History," and so on.
    In addition to professors, associate professors, lecturers, and graduate students from Renmin University of China, experts and scholars from the Institute of History and the Institute of Economics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, universities in Beijing, and relevant government departments will also be invited to participate. The conference will conclude with a summary of the scientific discussions.

### 中国人民大学  将召开第六次科学讨论会

1956-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

    中国人民大学
    将召开第六次科学讨论会
    中国人民大学将在五月二十六日到六月二日召开第六次科学讨论会。这次讨论会，将进行全组和分组讨论。参加讨论的题目共有四十个。其中，有胡锡奎的“关于我国对资本主义工商业的和平改造与阶级斗争”，苏联专家毕尔曼的“关于社会主义企业赢利的若干问题”，齐一的“鲁迅的社会政治观点和伦理观点”，胡兆量的“论经济地理学界意见的分歧”，陈学易的“我国过渡时期工人阶级与民族资产阶级的联盟和斗争的基础与特点”，方华、尹明的“形式逻辑与辩证逻辑在认识过程中的相互关系”，戴逸的“中国近代史分期问题”等等。
    参加这次会议的除中国人民大学的教授、副教授、讲师、研究生外，中国科学院历史研究所、经济研究所、北京各高等学校及政府有关部门的专家及学者也将被邀参加。会议最后将作科学讨论会的总结。
