Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives French Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-05-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 29th - Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, received the French cultural delegation headed by Madame Malaterre-Sellier on the afternoon of the 29th.
    Present at the reception were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mei Yi, Director of the Broadcasting Bureau, and He Chengxiang, Director of the Bureau of Religious Affairs of the State Council.

### 周恩来总理接见法国文化代表团

1956-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见法国文化代表团
    新华社29日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在二十九日下午接见了以马拉特尔—塞利埃夫人为首的法国文化代表团。
    接见的时候，在座的有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华，广播事业局局长梅益，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘。
