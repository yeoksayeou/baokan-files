Title: Draft of Pakistan's First Five-Year Plan Submitted to Cabinet for Discussion
Author:
Date: 1956-05-10
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 7th. According to Pakistan newspaper reports, the draft of Pakistan's First Five-Year Plan was submitted to the Pakistan Cabinet on April 27th for preliminary discussion. According to the draft, the plan will begin to be implemented in April 1955. The total investment of this five-year plan is estimated to be over 5 billion rupees, of which approximately 2.67 billion rupees will be used for water conservancy and power generation. Half of the total investment will rely on foreign aid and foreign debt.

### 巴基斯坦第一个五年计划草案已提交内阁讨论

1956-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦第一个五年计划草案已提交内阁讨论
    新华社7日讯  据巴基斯坦报纸消息，巴基斯坦的第一个五年计划草案已在四月二十七日提交巴基斯坦内阁作初步的讨论。按照草案，这个计划开始执行的时间是1955年4月。这个五年计划的投资总额大约是五十多亿卢比，其中用于水利和发电的约占二十六亿七千万卢比。总投资的半数将依靠外援和外债。
