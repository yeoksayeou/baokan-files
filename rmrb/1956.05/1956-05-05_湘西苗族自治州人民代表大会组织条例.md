Title: Regulations on the Organization of the People's Congress of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture
Author:
Date: 1956-05-05
Page: 1

　　Regulations on the Organization of the People's Congress of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture
　　(Approved at the 36th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on May 4, 1956)
　　Article 1　These Regulations on the Organization of the People's Congress of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture (hereinafter referred to as the Autonomous Prefecture) are formulated in accordance with Section 5, Chapter II of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China.
　　Article 2　The People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture is the organ of self-government of the Autonomous Prefecture and the local state power organ.
　　Article 3　Representatives to the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall be elected by the People's Congresses of the counties under the jurisdiction of the Autonomous Prefecture. The number of representatives to the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture and the methods for their election shall be determined separately in accordance with the provisions of the Electoral Law.
　　In the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture, each ethnic group shall have an appropriate number of representatives.
　　Article 4　The term of office of each People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture is two years.
　　Article 5　The People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall exercise the following functions and powers within the Autonomous Prefecture:
　　(1) Ensuring the observance and implementation of laws, decrees, and resolutions of higher-level People's Congresses;
　　(2) Formulating regulations on autonomy and separate regulations for the Autonomous Prefecture in accordance with the limits of authority prescribed by the Constitution and in light of the characteristics of the Autonomous Prefecture, and submitting them to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for approval;
　　(3) Adopting and promulgating resolutions within the scope of its authority;
　　(4) Planning economic construction, cultural construction, public services, preferential treatment for soldiers' families, and relief work;
　　(5) Examining and approving budgets and final accounts in accordance with the financial powers of the Autonomous Prefecture as stipulated by law;
　　(6) Deciding on the organization of public security forces in the Autonomous Prefecture in accordance with the national military system;
　　(7) Electing the Governor and Deputy Governors of the Autonomous Prefecture and members of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture;
　　(8) Electing the President of the Intermediate People's Court of the Autonomous Prefecture;
　　(9) Electing representatives to the Provincial People's Congress;
　　(10) Hearing and reviewing the work reports of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture and the Intermediate People's Court of the Autonomous Prefecture;
　　(11) Changing or revoking inappropriate resolutions and orders of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture;
　　(12) Changing or revoking inappropriate resolutions of the People's Congresses of the counties under its jurisdiction and inappropriate resolutions and orders of the People's Committees of the counties;
　　(13) Protecting public property, maintaining public order, and safeguarding citizens' rights;
　　(14) Safeguarding the equal rights of all ethnic groups, ensuring the implementation of ethnic policies, and strengthening unity and cooperation among ethnic groups.
　　Article 6　The People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture has the power to remove members of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture and the President of the Intermediate People's Court of the Autonomous Prefecture.
　　Article 7　Meetings of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall be convened by the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture.
　　The first meeting of each People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall be convened by the outgoing People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture after the election of the representatives to the new People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture has been completed.
　　Article 8　When representatives to each People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture attend the meeting of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture for the first time, they shall submit their certificates of election as representatives to the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture, which shall be examined by the Credentials Committee. The People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall, based on the report submitted by the Credentials Committee, confirm the qualifications of the representatives or declare the election of individual representatives invalid.
　　Article 9　Meetings of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall be held twice a year.
　　If the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture deems it necessary or if one-fifth of the representatives propose it, a meeting of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture may be convened temporarily.
　　Article 10　When the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture holds a meeting, it shall elect a presidium to preside over the meeting.
　　The meeting of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall have one secretary-general and one to two deputy secretaries-general. The candidates for secretary-general shall be nominated by the presidium and approved by the meeting of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture; the candidates for deputy secretary-general shall be determined by the presidium.
　　The meeting of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall establish a secretariat to carry out its work under the leadership of the secretary-general.
　　Article 11　When the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture holds a meeting, it may establish a Credentials Committee, a Motions Examination Committee, a Budget Committee, and other committees that need to be established, and carry out its work under the leadership of the presidium.
　　Article 12　When the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture holds a meeting, representatives and the presidium, and the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture may submit motions.
　　Motions submitted to the meeting of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall be submitted by the presidium to the meeting of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture for discussion, or submitted to the meeting of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture for discussion after being examined by the Motions Examination Committee.
　　Article 13　Resolutions of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall be adopted by a majority of all representatives.
　　Article 14　The candidates for members of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture and the President of the Intermediate People's Court of the Autonomous Prefecture shall be jointly or individually nominated by the representatives of the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture.
　　The People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall elect the members of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture and the President of the Intermediate People's Court of the Autonomous Prefecture by secret ballot.
　　Article 15　The People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall use one or several languages and scripts commonly used within the Autonomous Prefecture when performing its duties.
　　Article 16　When the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture holds a meeting, the responsible persons of the working departments of the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture, the President of the Intermediate People's Court of the Autonomous Prefecture, and the Procurator of the People's Procuratorate of the Autonomous Prefecture may attend as observers.
　　Article 17　When the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture holds a meeting, questions raised by the representatives to the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture or its working departments shall be submitted to the questioned organ by the presidium. The questioned organ must be responsible for answering at the meeting.
　　Article 18　Representatives to the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall not be arrested or brought to trial during the period of attending the meeting of the People's Congress without the consent of the presidium. If they are detained because they are caught in the act of committing a crime, the organ executing the detention must immediately report to the presidium for approval.
　　Article 19　During the period when representatives to the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture attend the meeting of the People's Congress, the state shall provide round-trip travel expenses and necessary material conveniences as needed.
　　Article 20　Representatives to the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall maintain close contact with their original electoral units, publicize laws, decrees, and policies, assist the People's Committee of the Autonomous Prefecture in promoting its work, and reflect the opinions and demands of the masses to the People's Congress and the People's Committee.
　　Representatives to the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture may attend the meetings of the People's Congress of their original electoral units as observers.
　　Article 21　Representatives to the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture shall be supervised by their original electoral units.
　　The electoral units of representatives to the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture have the right to remove the representatives they elected at any time. The removal of a representative must be approved by a majority of all representatives of the original electoral unit.
　　Article 22　When a representative to the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture is unable to serve as a representative due to unforeseen circumstances, a by-election shall be held by the original electoral unit.
　　Article 23　These Regulations shall be implemented after being adopted by the People's Congress of the Autonomous Prefecture and submitted to the People's Committee of Hunan Province for forwarding to the State Council for submission to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for approval.

### 湘西苗族自治州人民代表大会组织条例

1956-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　湘西苗族自治州人民代表大会组织条例
　　（1956年5月4日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第三十六次会议批准）
　　第一条　湘西苗族自治州（以下简称自治州）人民代表大会组织条例，根据中华人民共和国宪法第二章第五节制定。
　　第二条　自治州人民代表大会是自治州的自治机关，是地方国家权力机关。
　　第三条　自治州人民代表大会代表由自治州所属各县人民代表大会选举。自治州人民代表大会代表名额和代表产生办法根据选举法的规定另定。
    自治州人民代表大会中，各民族都应当有适当名额的代表。
　　第四条　自治州人民代表大会每届任期两年。
　　第五条　自治州人民代表大会在自治州内行使下列职权：
    （一）保证法律、法令和上级人民代表大会决议的遵守和执行；
    （二）根据宪法规定的权限，依照自治州的特点，制定自治州的自治条例和单行条例，报请全国人民代表大会常务委员会批准；
　　（三）在职权范围内通过和发布决议；
　　（四）规划经济建设、文化建设、公共事业、优抚工作和救济工作；
　　（五）依照法律规定的自治州财政权限，审查和批准预算和决算；
　　（六）依照国家的军事制度，决定组织自治州的公安部队；
　　（七）选举自治州州长、副州长和自治州人民委员会委员；
　　（八）选举自治州中级人民法院院长；
　　（九）选举省人民代表大会代表；
　　（十）听取和审查自治州人民委员会和自治州中级人民法院的工作报告；
　　（十一）改变或者撤销自治州人民委员会的不适当的决议和命令；
　　（十二）改变或者撤销所属各县人民代表大会的不适当的决议和各县人民委员会的不适当的决议和命令；
　　（十三）保护公共财产，维护公共秩序，保障公民权利；
　　（十四）保障各民族的平等权利，保证民族政策的贯彻执行，加强民族间的团结与合作。
　　第六条　自治州人民代表大会有权罢免自治州人民委员会的组成人员和自治州中级人民法院院长。
　　第七条　自治州人民代表大会会议由自治州人民委员会召集。
    每届自治州人民代表大会第一次会议，在本届自治州人民代表大会代表选举完成后，由上届自治州人民委员会召集。
　　第八条　每届自治州人民代表大会代表在第一次出席自治州人民代表大会会议的时候，向自治州人民代表大会提出代表当选证书，由代表资格审查委员会进行审查。自治州人民代表大会根据代表资格审查委员会提出的报告，确认代表的资格或者宣布个别代表的当选无效。
　　第九条　自治州人民代表大会会议每年举行两次。
    自治州人民委员会如果认为必要或者有五分之一的代表提议，可以临时召集自治州人民代表大会会议。
　　第十条　自治州人民代表大会举行会议的时候，选举主席团主持会议。
    自治州人民代表大会会议设秘书长一人，副秘书长一人至二人。秘书长的人选由主席团提名，由自治州人民代表大会会议通过；副秘书长的人选由主席团决定。
    自治州人民代表大会会议设立秘书处，在秘书长领导下进行工作。
　　第十一条　自治州人民代表大会举行会议的时候，可以设立代表资格审查委员会、议案审查委员会、预算委员会和其他需要设立的委员会，在主席团领导下进行工作。
　　第十二条　自治州人民代表大会举行会议的时候，代表和主席团，自治州人民委员会，都可以提出议案。
    向自治州人民代表大会会议提出的议案，由主席团提请自治州人民代表大会会议讨论，或者交付议案审查委员会审查后提请自治州人民代表大会会议讨论。
　　第十三条　自治州人民代表大会的决议，以全体代表的过半数通过。
　　第十四条　自治州人民委员会组成人员和自治州中级人民法院院长的人选，由自治州人民代表大会代表联合提名或者单独提名。
    自治州人民代表大会选举自治州人民委员会组成人员和自治州中级人民法院院长，采用无记名投票方式。
　　第十五条　自治州人民代表大会在执行职务的时候，使用自治州内通用的一种或者几种语言文字。
　　第十六条　自治州人民代表大会举行会议的时候，自治州人民委员会所属各工作部门负责人员和自治州中级人民法院院长、自治州人民检察院检察长可以列席。
　　第十七条　自治州人民代表大会举行会议的时候，代表向自治州人民委员会或者自治州人民委员会所属各工作部门提出的质问，经过主席团提交受质问的机关。受质问的机关必须在会议中负责答复。
　　第十八条　自治州人民代表大会代表在出席人民代表大会会议的期间，非经主席团同意不受逮捕或者审判，如果因为是现行犯被拘留，执行拘留的机关必须立即报请主席团批准。
　　第十九条　自治州人民代表大会代表在出席人民代表大会会议的期间，国家根据需要给以往返的旅费和必要的物质上的便利。
　　第二十条　自治州人民代表大会代表应当和原选举单位保持密切联系，宣传法律、法令和政策，协助自治州人民委员会推行工作，并且向人民代表大会和人民委员会反映群众的意见和要求。
    自治州人民代表大会代表可以列席原选举单位的人民代表大会会议。
　　第二十一条　自治州人民代表大会代表受原选举单位的监督。
    自治州人民代表大会代表的选举单位有权随时撤换自己选出的代表。代表的撤换必须由原选举单位以全体代表的过半数通过。
　　第二十二条　自治州人民代表大会代表因故不能担任代表职务的时候，由原选举单位补选。
　　第二十三条　本条例经自治州人民代表大会通过，报请湖南省人民委员会转报国务院提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会批准后实行。
