Title: Feng Xuan Appointed as China's Ambassador to the Swiss Confederation
Author:
Date: 1956-05-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Feng Xuan as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Swiss Confederation.

### 冯铉被任命为我国驻瑞士联邦大使

1956-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    冯铉被任命为我国驻瑞士联邦大使
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命冯铉为中华人民共和国驻瑞士联邦特命全权大使。
