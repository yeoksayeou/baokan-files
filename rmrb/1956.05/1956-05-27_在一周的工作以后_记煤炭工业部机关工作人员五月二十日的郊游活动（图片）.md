Title: After a Week of Work...—A Record of the Outing of Staff Members of the Ministry of Coal Industry on May 20 (Photos)
Author: Yuan Ying
Date: 1956-05-27
Page: 5

　　After a Week of Work...
　　—A Record of the Outing of Staff Members of the Ministry of Coal Industry on May 20
　　Poem by Yuan Ying
　　After a week of work,
　　Who doesn't want to go to the countryside to eliminate fatigue?
　　If you only spend spring at your desk,
　t you afraid of being laughed at by the beautiful lakes and mountains?
　　Let the gentle ripples on Kunming Lake,
　　Remember our joyful songs;
　　Let the winding mountain paths of the Eight Great Sites in the Western Hills,
　　Leave behind our sturdy footprints.
　　Needless to say, the young people are in high spirits,
　　The middle-aged are not willing to be left behind either;
　　Although they have to breathe a little heavily when climbing,
　　How can it be called climbing if you don't breathe heavily?
　　Only those who know how to rest can work,
　　Only those who force themselves to age don't leave their homes;
　　The bright spring scenery is boundless,
　　The mountains and rivers of the motherland are waiting for us everywhere.
　　Our reporters Cang Shi and Yuan Yiping, photographed

　　Ironing clothes…
　　Off to the outing.
　　Boating on Kunming Lake.
　　"Don't move, quick, take a picture!"
　　Amateur painter.
　　"This is so much fun!"—Jin Rongguang and Chai Beibei caught a small frog.
　　Comparing who is the winner.
　　Jump and sing to your heart's content. Rest is for better work.
　　Look, how happy they are talking!
　　See who climbs up first.

### 在一周的工作以后……——记煤炭工业部机关工作人员五月二十日的郊游活动（图片）
袁鹰
1956-05-27
第5版()
专栏：

    在一周的工作以后……
    ——记煤炭工业部机关工作人员五月二十日的郊游活动
    袁鹰作诗
    在一周的工作以后，
    谁不想到野外去消除疲劳？
    如果你只在办公桌上度过春天，
    难道不怕大好湖山耻笑？
    让昆明湖上柔软的波纹，
    记住了我们欢乐的歌声；
    让西山八大处曲折的山道，
    留下了我们结实的脚印。
    年青人兴高彩烈不用说，
    中年人也不甘落在后边；
    虽说爬山的时候要微微喘气，
    可是不喘气又怎么能算爬山？
    只有会休息的人才会工作，
    只有强迫自己衰老的人才不离家门；
    灿烂的春光一望无边，
    祖国的山河到处都在等着我们。
    本报记者　苍石、袁毅平摄影
    把衣裳烫好…………
    野游去。
    荡漾在昆明湖上。
    “都别动，快照啦！”
    业余画家。
    “真好玩啊！”——金荣光和柴贝贝捉到了一只小青蛙。
    比一比胜负。
    尽情地跳啊、唱啊。休息是为了更好地工作。
    看，他们谈得多么高兴！
    看谁先爬上来。
