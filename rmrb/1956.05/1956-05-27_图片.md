After the Qianfeng Agricultural Production Cooperative in Guangxing Township, Shehong County, Sichuan Province, implemented the system of contracting work and output, the enthusiasm of its members for labor greatly increased. The total output of spring crops this year increased by about 33% compared to last year. The average yield of wheat planted by the cooperative is expected to be 270 jin per mu.
