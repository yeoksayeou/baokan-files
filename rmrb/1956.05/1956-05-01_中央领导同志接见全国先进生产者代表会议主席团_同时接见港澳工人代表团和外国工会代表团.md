Title: Central Leading Comrades Receive Presidium of National Advanced Producers Representative Conference, Simultaneously Receive Hong Kong and Macau Workers Delegation and Foreign Trade Union Delegations
Author:
Date: 1956-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and members of the Political Bureau Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Peng Zhen, Dong Biwu, Zhang Wentian, Peng Dehuai, Kang Sheng, and Deng Xiaoping, this afternoon received the Presidium of the National Advanced Producers Representative Conference, the Hong Kong and Macau workers' "May Day" sightseeing delegation to Beijing, and delegations of advanced producers from the Soviet Union coal mining industry, Soviet Union machine manufacturing industry, Soviet Union transportation and heavy machinery manufacturing industry, Soviet Union heavy industry, Soviet Union textile industry, Soviet Union construction industry, Soviet Union postal and telecommunications, Soviet Union agriculture, Soviet Union highway transportation, Soviet Union maritime and inland river transportation, Soviet Union forest industry, the Soviet Union Trade Union delegation, the Mongolian Trade Union delegation, the Korean Trade Union delegation, the Romanian Trade Union delegation, the Vietnamese Trade Union delegation, the Federation of Free German Trade Unions delegation, the Czechoslovak Trade Union delegation, the Polish Trade Union delegation, the Hungarian Trade Union delegation, and the Albanian Trade Union delegation who were invited to participate in the National Advanced Producers Representative Conference.

### 中央领导同志接见全国先进生产者代表会议主席团  同时接见港澳工人代表团和外国工会代表团

1956-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    中央领导同志接见全国先进生产者代表会议主席团
    同时接见港澳工人代表团和外国工会代表团
    新华社30日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东和政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、彭真、董必武、张闻天、彭德怀、康生、邓小平，今天下午接见了全国先进生产者代表会议主席团，港澳工人“五一”劳动节赴京观光团和应邀前来参加全国先进生产者代表会议的苏联煤矿工业、苏联机器制造业、苏联运输和重型机器制造业、苏联重工业、苏联纺织工业、苏联建筑业、苏联邮电、苏联农业、苏联公路运输、苏联海上和内河运输、苏联森林工业等先进生产者代表团人员，苏联工会代表团，蒙古工会代表团，朝鲜工会代表团，罗马尼亚工会代表团，越南工会代表团，自由德国工会联合会代表团，捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团，波兰工会代表团，匈牙利工会代表团和阿尔巴尼亚工会代表团。
