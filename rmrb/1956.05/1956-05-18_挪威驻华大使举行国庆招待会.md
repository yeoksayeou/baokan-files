Title: Norwegian Ambassador to China Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1956-05-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 17th: Premier Zhou Enlai attended the National Day reception given by the Norwegian Ambassador to China, Mr. Krog-Hansen, this evening.

    At the reception, Ambassador Krog-Hansen proposed a toast to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, and to the prosperity and happiness of the Chinese people.

    Premier Zhou Enlai proposed a toast to the National Day of the Kingdom of Norway, to the prosperity of the Kingdom of Norway and the happiness of the Norwegian people, to the friendship between China and Norway, and to the good health of His Majesty King Haakon VII of the Kingdom of Norway.

    The reception was held in a charming small garden at the embassy. A gentle early summer evening breeze blew, and the sounds of conversation and laughter between hosts and guests could be heard throughout the garden. The pleasant and harmonious reception lasted for an hour and a half.

    Among the more than one hundred guests were Vice-Chairman Guo Moruo, Vice-Premier Li Xiannian, Vice-Chairman Zhang Bojun of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, responsible persons from relevant departments, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

### 挪威驻华大使举行国庆招待会

1956-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    挪威驻华大使举行国庆招待会
    新华社17日讯　周恩来总理在今晚出席了挪威驻华大使克洛格—亨生举行的国庆招待会。
    招待会上，克洛格—亨生大使建议大家举杯为中华人民共和国毛泽东主席的健康，为中国人民的繁荣和幸福干杯。
    周恩来总理提议为挪威王国国庆，为挪威王国国家的繁荣和挪威人民的幸福，为中国和挪威两国间的友谊，为挪威王国国王哈康七世陛下的身体健康干杯。
    招待会是在大使馆的一座雅致的小花园中举行的。初夏的晚风轻拂，花园中不断传出宾主的谈笑声。愉快融洽的招待会进行了一个半小时。
    在一百多位客人中，有郭沫若副委员长、李先念副总理、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧、外交部副部长姬鹏飞和有关部门的负责人，以及各国驻华使节和外交官员。
