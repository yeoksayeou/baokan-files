Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Says Time is Ripe for Establishing Friendly Relations Between Vietnam and Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1956-05-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 4th: According to Vietnam News Agency, Premier and Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, said during a meeting with journalist Wilfred Burchett on May 2nd, that the time has come for the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Cambodia to establish friendly relations in all aspects based on the five principles of peaceful coexistence. The two countries can exchange goodwill delegations. In the economic and trade fields, it is hoped that fruitful exchanges can be carried out.

Pham Van Dong said that Cambodia's neutral policy is in line with the Geneva Accords. At the same time, it is in line with the Cambodian people's desire for the Kingdom to achieve peace, independence, and prosperity. It will help consolidate peace in Asia and give Cambodia an important international status in Southeast Asia. The Democratic Republic of Vietnam, like all peace-loving governments and people, warmly welcomes this correct and far-sighted policy.

### 范文同总理说  越柬建立友好关系时机已到

1956-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    范文同总理说
    越柬建立友好关系时机已到
    新华社河内4日电　据越南通讯社讯：越南民主共和国总理兼外交部长范文同在五月二日接见新闻记者贝却敌的时候说，越南民主共和国同柬埔寨在共处的五项原则的基础上在一切方面建立友好关系的时机已经来到。两国可以互派亲善代表团。在经济和贸易方面，希望可以进行有成果的交流。
    范文同说，柬埔寨的中立政策是符合日内瓦协议的政策。同时，它是符合柬埔寨人民要求王国得到和平、独立和繁荣的愿望的。它将有助于巩固亚洲的和平，并且使柬埔寨在东南亚得到一个重要的国际地位。越南民主共和国同所有爱好和平的政府和人民一样，热烈欢迎这种正确的和有远见的政策。
