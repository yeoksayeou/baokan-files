Title: Czechoslovakia Holds Military Parade to Celebrate 11th Anniversary of Liberation
Author:
Date: 1956-05-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 10th: A military parade was held in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, on May 9th to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia.

The parade was held on Letná Square. Široký, Novotný, and other leaders of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and government reviewed the parade on the rostrum.

Czechoslovak Minister of National Defense, General Lomský, delivered a speech. He spoke of the great achievements made by the Czechoslovak people in socialist construction in the eleven years since liberation.

After the start of the parade, students from military academies and military schools marched across the square first. Subsequently, approximately two hundred twin-engine Ilyushin Il-28 jet aircraft, appearing in the parade for the first time, and other jet aircraft flew over the square. Artillery, tanks, and units of other military branches successively marched across the square. Rocket artillery also appeared in the parade for the first time.

After the parade, the masses in the square surged towards the front of the rostrum, enthusiastically waving to the leaders of the Party and the government and shouting "Long live the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia!"

In the evening, Czechoslovak Foreign Minister David held a reception for the National Day.

### 捷克斯洛伐克举行阅兵式庆祝解放十一周年

1956-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克举行阅兵式庆祝解放十一周年
    据新华社布拉格10日电  捷克斯洛伐克首都布拉格在五月九日举行了阅兵式，庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十一周年。
    阅兵式是在列特纳广场上举行的，在主席台上阅兵的有西罗基、诺沃提尼和捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府的其他领导人。
    捷克斯洛伐克国防部长洛姆斯基上将发表了演说。他讲到了捷克斯洛伐克人民在解放以来十一年中，在社会主义建设方面所获得的巨大成就。
    阅兵式开始以后，军事学院和军事学校的学员首先走过广场。随后，第一次在阅兵中出现的双引擎“伊柳辛—28”式喷气飞机和其他喷气机共约两百架掠过广场上空。炮兵、坦克和其他一些兵种的部队相继走过广场。火箭炮也是第一次在阅兵中出现。
    阅兵完毕，广场上的群众涌向主席台前，向党和政府的领导人热情地挥手，并且高呼“捷克斯洛伐克共产党万岁！”
    晚间，捷克斯洛伐克外交部长戴维为国庆节举行了招待会。
