Title: Eleven-Nation Delegation from China, the Soviet Union, and Others Holds Meeting in Moscow to Exchange Standardization Experience in Science and Technology
Author:
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th: An international scientific and technological conference on the exchange of standardization experiences was held in Moscow from May 22nd to 26th. Delegations from Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, China, Korea, Mongolia, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, and Czechoslovakia participated in the meeting.

    At the meeting, the heads of standardization departments of various countries reported on the progress of standardization work. The meeting discussed the issue of further developing cooperation and exchanging experiences in this area, and also heard reports from many experts.

    The meeting passed resolutions on expanding the exchange of experiences in organizational and technical aspects of standardization, and on participating more actively in international standardization organizations and other organizations.

### 中苏等十一国代表团在莫斯科举行会议  交流科学技术方面的标准化经验

1956-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏等十一国代表团在莫斯科举行会议
    交流科学技术方面的标准化经验
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：交流标准化经验的国际科学技术会议，从五月二十二日到二十六日在莫斯科举行。参加会议的有阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、中国、朝鲜、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联和捷克斯洛伐克等国的代表团。
    会上，各国标准化部门的负责人报告了标准化的工作情况。会议讨论了在这方面进一步发展合作和交流经验的问题，还听取了许多专家的报告。
    会议通过了关于在标准化的组织问题和技术问题方面扩大经验交流，以及关于更加积极地参加国际标准化的组织和其他组织的决议。
