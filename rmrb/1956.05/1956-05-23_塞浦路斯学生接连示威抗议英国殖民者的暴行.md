Title: Cypriot Students Continuously Demonstrate Against the Brutalities of British Colonialists
Author:
Date: 1956-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nicosia, May 22nd: Cypriot students held successive demonstrations in Nicosia on May 20th and 21st to protest against the British colonialists' massacre of local patriotic youth. The students held high the Greek flag and placards with slogans such as "The Struggle for Cyprus organization will avenge the deaths of patriots" and "Demand unification with Greece."

The British troops used tear gas to attack the students for two consecutive days. The students angrily retaliated against the British colonial army with stones. Incidents of British troops being attacked occurred continuously in the city.

Britain has transferred additional troops to Nicosia for suppression. The British authorities have imposed a curfew in the center of Nicosia and closed off the Greek ethnic residential areas.

### 塞浦路斯学生接连示威抗议英国殖民者的暴行

1956-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    塞浦路斯学生接连示威抗议英国殖民者的暴行
    据新华社22日讯　尼科西亚消息：塞浦路斯学生五月二十日和二十一日接连在尼科西亚举行示威，抗议英国殖民者屠杀当地爱国青年的暴行。学生们高举着希腊国旗和标语牌，上面写着：“为塞浦路斯而斗争组织将为爱国者的死难报仇”，“要求同希腊合并”。
    英国军队接连两天都用催泪弹向学生们袭击。学生们愤怒地用石块还击英国殖民军。城里不断发生英军遭到袭击的事件。
    英国增调军队到尼科西亚进行镇压。英国当局在尼科西亚中心实行了宵禁，封闭了希腊血统居民区。
