Title: People's Daily and Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Joint Newspaper Promotion Announcement
Author:
Date: 1956-05-30
Page: 1

People's Daily will be changed to eight pages daily, a total of two large sheets, starting from July 1st. The retail price is eight cents per copy, and two yuan and forty cents per month. Readers are requested to complete subscription procedures at local post offices.

### 人民日报  邮电部报刊推广局联合启事

1956-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    人民日报  邮电部报刊推广局联合启事
    人民日报自七月一日起改为每日八个版，共两大张。报价零售每份八分，每月二元四角。请读者向各地邮局办理订阅手续。
