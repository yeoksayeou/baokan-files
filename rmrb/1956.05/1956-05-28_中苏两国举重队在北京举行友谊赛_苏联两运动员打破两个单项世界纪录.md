Title: China and the Soviet Union Weightlifting Teams Hold Friendly Match in Beijing; Two Soviet Athletes Break Two World Records
Author:
Date: 1956-05-28
Page: 1

    China and the Soviet Union Weightlifting Teams Hold Friendly Match in Beijing
    Two Soviet Athletes Break Two World Records
    (Our newspaper, May 27th) The creator of the world's highest record for the snatch in the flyweight class, the Soviet athlete Khabutdinov, today broke his original world record of 122 kg with a result of 122.5 kg. Soviet light heavyweight weightlifter Dunganov broke the world record for the clean and jerk in the light heavyweight class with a result of 136.5 kg, surpassing the previous record of 136 kg held by Soviet weightlifter Vorobyov. They participated today in the friendly match between the Chinese and Soviet weightlifting teams held at the Beijing Gymnasium.
    The Soviet athletes who participated in today's competition won first place in all seven weight classes (flyweight, bantamweight, lightweight, light heavyweight, middleweight, light heavyweight, and heavyweight), winning with a total score of twenty-eight to twenty-one.
    Novikov, the creator of the Soviet heavyweight push-up record of 160 kg, today created a new record of 160.5 kg in the same event.
    Chinese athletes also achieved encouraging results, breaking the national record for total scores in six weight classes and fourteen national records for individual events. Bantamweight athlete Chen Jingkai's total score was 307.5 kg, breaking the national record of 295 kg. Huang Qianghui broke his own national record for the light heavyweight total score of 342.5 kg with 350.5 kg. Zhu Hongquan broke his own national record for the middleweight total score of 350 kg with 357.5 kg. The previous national record for the light heavyweight total score was 355 kg, and Li Baiyu's score today was 362.5 kg. The previous national record for the light heavyweight total score was 315 kg, and Qu Bingyu's score today was 350 kg. The previous national record for the heavyweight total score of 335 kg was created by Chang Guanqun, and today he created a new record of 355 kg.

### 中苏两国举重队在北京举行友谊赛  苏联两运动员打破两个单项世界纪录

1956-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏两国举重队在北京举行友谊赛
    苏联两运动员打破两个单项世界纪录
    本报27日讯　最轻量级举重双手推举世界最高纪录创造者、苏联运动健将哈布特吉诺夫，今天以122.5公斤的成绩打破了他原来创造的122公斤的世界最高纪录。苏联的轻重量级举重运动员杜加诺夫，今天以136.5公斤的成绩，打破了轻重量级双手抓举136公斤的世界纪录。原来的世界纪录，是苏联举重运动员沃罗比耶夫保持的。他们今天参加的是在北京体育馆举行的中国举重队同苏联举重队的友谊比赛。
    今天参加比赛的苏联运动员，在最轻级、次轻级、轻量级、轻重量级、中量级、次重量级和重量级七个等级的比赛中，都获得第一名，以二十八比二十一的总分获胜。
    苏联重量级推举最高纪录160公斤的创造者诺维科夫，今天他在同一项目中又创造了160.5公斤的成绩。
    中国的运动员们也创造了可喜的成绩，共打破了六个等级的总成绩的全国最高纪录和十四个单项的全国纪录。次轻量级运动员陈镜开的总成绩是307.5公斤，打破了295公斤的全国最高纪录。黄强辉以350.5公斤打破了他本人创造的我国轻重量级总成绩的最高纪录342.5公斤。朱鸿全以357.5公斤打破了他本人创造的我国中量级总成绩的最高纪录350公斤。我国轻重量级总成绩的最高纪录是355公斤，李白玉今天的成绩是362.5公斤。我国次重量级总成绩的最高纪录是315公斤，曲炳瑜今天的成绩是350公斤。我国重量级总成绩的最高纪录335公斤，是常冠群创造的，今天他又创造了355公斤的成绩。
