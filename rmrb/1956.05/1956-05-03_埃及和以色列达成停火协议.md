Title: Egypt and Israel Reach Ceasefire Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-05-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 2nd. TASS reported from Jerusalem on May 2nd via Agence France-Presse: The United Nations Truce Supervision Organization announced that Egypt and Israel have reached an agreement on strict adherence to the resolution regarding a ceasefire in the Gaza area. The announcement pointed out that this agreement was reached as a result of UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld's visit to Near Eastern countries.

### 埃及和以色列达成停火协议

1956-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及和以色列达成停火协议
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社巴黎2日讯：法新社驻耶路撒冷记者报道，联合国停战监督委员会公告，埃及和以色列已经就严格遵守关于加沙地区停火的决议一事达成了协议。公告指出，这个协议的达成是联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德访问近东国家的结果。
