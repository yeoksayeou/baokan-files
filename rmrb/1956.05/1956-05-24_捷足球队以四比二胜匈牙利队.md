Title: Czechoslovak Football Team Beats Hungarian Team Four to Two
Author:
Date: 1956-05-24
Page: 4

    International Sports Activities

    Czechoslovak Football Team Beats Hungarian Team Four to Two
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 22nd. Reports state that on May 20th, the Czechoslovak national football team defeated the 1954 world champion Hungarian national football team by a score of four to two in Budapest. This is the first time in twenty-nine years that Czechoslovak footballers have defeated Hungarian footballers on Hungarian soil.

### 捷足球队以四比二胜匈牙利队

1956-05-24
第4版()
专栏：国际体育活动

    捷足球队以四比二胜匈牙利队
    据新华社布拉格22日电　报纸报道，捷克斯洛伐克国家足球队五月二十日在布达佩斯以四比二打败了1954年世界冠军匈牙利国家足球队。这是捷克斯洛伐克足球队员二十九年来第一次在匈牙利国境内打败匈牙利足球队员。
