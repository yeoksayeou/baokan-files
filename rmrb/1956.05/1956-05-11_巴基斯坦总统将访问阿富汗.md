Title: Pakistani President to Visit Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1956-05-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, May 10th: According to Kabul Radio's broadcast on the evening of May 9th, Pakistani President Mirza will visit Kabul on June 10th at the invitation of the King of Afghanistan.

### 巴基斯坦总统将访问阿富汗

1956-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总统将访问阿富汗
    新华社10日讯  据喀布尔电台五月九日晚广播，巴基斯坦总统米尔扎将于六月十日应阿富汗国王的邀请，前往喀布尔进行访问。
