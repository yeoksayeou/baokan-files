Title: Ceylon Government Plans to Convert Military Base into Commercial Port After British Troops Withdraw
Author:
Date: 1956-05-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 24th: Colombo news: Ceylon's Minister of Transport and Engineering, Senanayake, said on May 22nd that when Britain withdraws from its naval base in Trincomalee, Ceylon, Ceylon will convert it into a commercial port. The Ceylon government has recently repeatedly stated that the British military base in Ceylon must be removed.
    The minister said that the government hopes to start the port's conversion work during the next fiscal year. He said that converting this British military base into a commercial port can alleviate the traffic congestion in Colombo.

### 锡兰政府打算在英军撤离后  把军事基地改建为商港

1956-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰政府打算在英军撤离后
    把军事基地改建为商港
    据新华社新德里24日电　科伦坡消息：锡兰运输和工程部长森纳那亚克五月二十二日说，当英国退出它在锡兰的亭可马里的海军基地的时候，锡兰将把它改建为一个商港。锡兰政府最近曾经一再声明，英国在锡兰的军事基地必须撤除。
    这位部长说，政府希望在下一财政年度期间开始这个港口的改建工作。他说，把这个英国的军事基地改建为商港可以减轻科伦坡的交通拥挤现象。
