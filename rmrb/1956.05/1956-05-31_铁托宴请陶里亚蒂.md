Title: Tito Hosts Togliatti at Banquet
Author:
Date: 1956-05-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 30th: According to a report from the Yugoslav News Agency, the General Secretary of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Tito, hosted a banquet in Belgrade on May 29th in honor of the General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, Togliatti.

　　Member of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party, L. Amadei, also attended the banquet.

　　Members of the Executive Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Kardelj, Ranković, Pijade, Gošnjak, and Vukmanović, also attended the banquet.

　　That afternoon, Togliatti visited a machine tool factory in Železnik near Belgrade and had a long conversation with worker representatives.

### 铁托宴请陶里亚蒂

1956-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    铁托宴请陶里亚蒂
    新华社30日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟总书记铁托五月二十九日在贝尔格莱德设宴招待意大利共产党总书记陶里亚蒂。
    意大利共产党中央委员会委员路·阿马德西也出席了宴会。
    南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟执行委员会委员卡德尔、兰科维奇、皮雅杰、戈什尼亚克和伏克曼诺维奇等也出席了宴会。
    这一天下午，陶里亚蒂到贝尔格莱德附近泽尔勒兹尼克参观了一个机床工厂，和工人代表进行了长时间的谈话。
