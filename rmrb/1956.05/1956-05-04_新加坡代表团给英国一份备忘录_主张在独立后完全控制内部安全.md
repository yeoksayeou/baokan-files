Title: Singapore Delegation Submits Memorandum to Britain, Insisting on Full Control of Internal Security After Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. According to a United Press International London dispatch: During the negotiation meeting on May 1 regarding the future political status of Singapore, the Singapore delegation handed a memorandum to British Colonial Secretary Lennox-Boyd. Well-informed sources say that the delegation unanimously reiterated its demand for complete control of Singapore's internal security after Singapore gains independence.

Well-informed sources say that in his counter-proposal, Marshall has clarified that he is unwilling to "accept two-thirds or one-third concessions." He reportedly told Lennox-Boyd: "Either we will achieve all the concessions, or we will not deal at all."

A delegation member said that if Lennox-Boyd agrees to give the Singapore government full power to control internal security, the negotiations will continue. The person said, but if he "rejects our request, we will pack our bags and return home."

### 新加坡代表团给英国一份备忘录  主张在独立后完全控制内部安全

1956-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    新加坡代表团给英国一份备忘录
    主张在独立后完全控制内部安全
    新华社3日讯　据合众社伦敦消息：在五月一日举行的关于新加坡未来政治地位问题的谈判会议中，新加坡代表团把一份备忘录交给英国殖民大臣伦诺克斯—波伊德。消息灵通人士说，这个代表团一致重申它的在新加坡获得独立后完全控制新加坡内部安全的要求。
    消息灵通人士说，马歇尔在他的反建议中已经澄清：他不愿意“接受三分之二或者三分之一的让步”。据说他告诉伦诺克斯—波伊德说：“要就是我们将取得全部的让步，要就是全然不打交道。”
    一位代表团人士说，如果伦诺克斯—波伊德同意给新加坡政府充分的权力来控制内部安全，这次谈判将继续进行。这位人士说，但是如果他“拒绝我们的要求，我们将整理行装回国”。
