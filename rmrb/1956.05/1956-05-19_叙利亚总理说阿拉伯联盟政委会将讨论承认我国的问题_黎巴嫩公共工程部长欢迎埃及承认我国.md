Title: Syrian Prime Minister Says Arab League Political Committee Will Discuss the Question of Recognizing Our Country; Lebanese Minister of Public Works Welcomes Egypt's Recognition of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1956-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 18th dispatch: Damascus News: Syrian Prime Minister Ghazzi said on May 17th that the Arab League Political Committee, currently meeting in Damascus, will discuss the issue of recognizing the People's Republic of China at its meeting on the 18th. Representatives of the various countries participating in the Political Committee will exchange views on coordinating the attitudes of Arab countries towards China.

According to a United Press report, Arab diplomatic officials said on the evening of the 17th that Egypt's recognition of China was "a serious blow to the United States."

At the same time, the press and political circles in Damascus welcomed Egypt's recognition of China. United Press said that almost all party leaders in the Syrian National Assembly want to raise the issue of recognizing China.

Former Syrian Prime Minister and leader of the People's Party, Dawalibi, said: "Recognizing a country with a population of 600 million is a just and upright duty." Former Minister of Justice Ajlani said when talking about Egypt's recognition of China: "This is a commendable decision, and Syria and other Arab countries would do well to follow suit."

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 18th: Many newspapers in Cairo published remarks made by Lebanese Minister of Public Works, Emil Bustani, who is currently in Cairo, welcoming Egypt's recognition of the People's Republic of China. He said: "This is in line with the interests of Egypt and the interests of the Arab countries." He also said that the Lebanese government and other Arab countries that have not yet recognized China should do so as soon as possible.

### 叙利亚总理说阿拉伯联盟政委会将讨论承认我国的问题  黎巴嫩公共工程部长欢迎埃及承认我国

1956-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    叙利亚总理说阿拉伯联盟政委会将讨论承认我国的问题
    黎巴嫩公共工程部长欢迎埃及承认我国
    新华社18日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总理加齐五月十七日说，目前在大马士革举行会议的阿拉伯联盟政治委员会将在十八日的会议上讨论承认中华人民共和国的问题，参加政治委员会的各国代表将就协调阿拉伯国家对中国的态度问题交换意见。
    据合众社报道，阿拉伯外交官员们十七日晚上说，埃及承认中国一事是，“对美国的一个严重打击”。
    同时，大马士革的报界和政界人士对埃及承认中国一事表示欢迎。合众社说，几乎全部叙利亚国会的政党领袖们都要提出承认中国的问题。
    叙利亚前总理、人民党领袖达瓦利比尔说：“承认一个拥有六亿人口的国家是一种公正的和光明正大的责任。”前司法部长阿吉拉尼在谈到埃及承认中国时说：“这是一个值得赞扬的决定，叙利亚和其他阿拉伯国家最好是予以效法。”
    新华社开罗18日电　开罗许多报纸都刊登了现在在开罗的黎巴嫩公共工程部长埃米尔·布斯坦尼所说的欢迎埃及承认中华人民共和国的话。他说：“这是符合埃及的利益和阿拉伯国家的利益的”。他还说：黎巴嫩政府和其他还未承认中国的阿拉伯国家政府也应该尽早地这样做。
