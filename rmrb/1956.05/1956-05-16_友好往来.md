　　Friendly Exchanges
　　△At the invitation of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, the China Political Science and Law Society dispatched a delegation of legal workers to attend the Sixth Congress of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers held in Brussels from May 21 to 25. The head of the delegation, Zhang Zhiran, the deputy head, Zhu Qiwen, and members Min Ganghou, Chen Shouyi, Ling Qihan, Zhou Nan, Wang Tieya, Rui Mu, and others, a total of eleven people, left Beijing for Belgium on the 15th.
　　△The Hague News: Chinese spectroscopists Zhao Guangzeng and He Yizhen arrived in the Netherlands on May 11 to attend the Sixth International Colloquium Spectroscopicum. This colloquium was held in Amsterdam, the capital of the Netherlands, from May 14 to 19.
　　△Nango Saburo, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Japan-China Import and Export Association, and his deputy chairman, Sida Hiroshi, and three others, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 13th. Lei Renmin and Li Zhuchen, Vice Chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, met and hosted a banquet to welcome them on the 14th. Nango Saburo and others will make a short visit to China and exchange views with relevant parties on promoting Sino-Japanese trade.
　　△The French cultural delegation of twelve people, headed by Mrs. Marter-Sellier, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 15th. They will make a two-week visit to China.
　　△Reverend Victor James, Joint Secretary of the Australian National Peace Council, who was invited to visit China by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, arrived in Beijing on the 15th.
　　△Icelandic peace representatives Elixdottir-Thorvaldsson and Ranveig Thomasdi, who were invited to visit China by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, left Beijing by plane on the 14th to return to their country. During their stay in China, they visited Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou and other places.
　　△Deqin Limao, a Burmese peace representative who was invited to visit China by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, left Beijing by plane on the 15th to return to his country.

### 友好往来

1956-05-16
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    △中国政治法律学会应国际民主法律工作者协会邀请，派遣法律工作者代表团出席五月二十一日到二十五日在布鲁塞尔举行的国际民主法律工作者协会第六届大会。代表团团长张志让，副团长朱其文，团员闵刚侯、陈守一、凌其翰、周楠、王铁崖、芮沐等十一人已经在十五日离北京前往比利时。
    △海牙消息：应邀出席第六届国际光谱学讨论会的中国光谱学家赵广增、何怡贞在五月十一日到达荷兰。这个讨论会于五月十四日至十九日在荷兰首都阿姆斯特丹举行。
    △日本日中输出入协会理事长南乡三郎、副理事长似田博等三人，在十三日坐飞机到达北京，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、李烛尘在十四日接见并设宴欢迎他们。南乡三郎等将在中国作短期的参观访问，同有关方面就促进中日贸易问题交换意见。
    △应中国人民对外文化协会邀请的以马拉特尔—塞利埃夫人为首的法国文化代表团一行十二人，已经在十五日乘飞机到达北京。他们将在中国进行为期两周的参观访问。
    △应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的澳大利亚全国和平理事会联合秘书长维克托·詹姆斯牧师，十五日到达北京。
    △应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的冰岛和平代表艾里克斯多蒂尔—索尔瓦尔森和兰维格·托马斯迪，十四日乘飞机离北京回国。她们在中国期间访问了北京、上海、杭州、广州等地。
    △应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的缅甸和平代表德钦礼貌十五日乘飞机离北京回国。
