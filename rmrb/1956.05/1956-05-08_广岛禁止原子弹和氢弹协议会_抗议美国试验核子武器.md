Title: Hiroshima Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Protests US Nuclear Weapon Tests
Author:
Date: 1956-05-08
Page: 4

    Hiroshima Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs
    Protests US Nuclear Weapon Tests
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 6th: The Hiroshima Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs today sent a telegram to US President Eisenhower, protesting the US's commencement of nuclear weapon testing on Eniwetok Atoll on the 5th, and demanding a complete halt to future tests.

### 广岛禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会  抗议美国试验核子武器

1956-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

    广岛禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会
    抗议美国试验核子武器
    新华社6日讯  东京消息：广岛禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会今天打电报给美国总统艾森豪威尔，抗议美国五日开始在恩纳威托克岛举行核子武器试验，并且要求完全停止以后的试验。
