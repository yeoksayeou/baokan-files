Title: Nie Rongzhen and Wang Congwu Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-05-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 13th: Nie Rongzhen, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Wang Congwu, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, who attended the Third Congress of the Korean Workers' Party, returned to Beijing by train this afternoon.

### 聂荣臻王从吾回到北京

1956-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻王从吾回到北京
    新华社13日讯　出席朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会的中国共产党代表、中共中央委员聂荣臻、中共中央候补委员王从吾，今天下午乘火车回到北京。
