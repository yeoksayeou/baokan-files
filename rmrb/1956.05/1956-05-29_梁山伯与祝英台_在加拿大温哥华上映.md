Title: "Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai" Screened in Vancouver, Canada
Author:
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. According to the New York "China Daily News", the Chinese film "Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai" was recently screened in Vancouver, Canada, and was warmly welcomed by overseas Chinese.

### “梁山伯与祝英台”  在加拿大温哥华上映

1956-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    “梁山伯与祝英台”
    在加拿大温哥华上映
    新华社28日讯　据纽约“美洲华侨日报”消息，中国影片“梁山伯与祝英台”不久以前在加拿大温哥华上映，受到华侨们的热烈欢迎。
