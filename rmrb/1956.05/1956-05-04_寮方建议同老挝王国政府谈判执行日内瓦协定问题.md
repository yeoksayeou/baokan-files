Title: Laotian Side Proposes Negotiations with the Royal Lao Government on Implementing the Geneva Accords
Author:
Date: 1956-05-04
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 3rd: Lao news: Prince Souphanouvong issued a statement on May 1st on behalf of the Lao Fighting Forces, saying that the Lao Fighting Forces are always willing to resolve issues between the Royal Lao Government and the Lao Fighting Forces through peaceful negotiations, maintain Laos' neutrality on the basis of the Geneva Accords, and thus build a peaceful, independent, democratic and unified Laos.
    
The statement said that the Lao Fighting Forces proposed resuming political conferences, but regrettably, the Royal Government has not yet agreed. At a time when the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference are consulting on the implementation of the Geneva Accords, I sincerely hope and welcome the two chairmen to find ways to ensure the thorough implementation of the various provisions of the Geneva Accords regarding the cessation of hostilities in Laos, and to ensure Laos' democracy, independence and national sovereignty.
    
The statement calls on the Royal Government to negotiate with the Lao Fighting Forces in a sincere manner so that the Geneva Accords can be strictly and thoroughly implemented; first and foremost, to cease hostilities and create favorable conditions for the upcoming political conference.

### 寮方建议同老挝王国政府谈判执行日内瓦协定问题

1956-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　寮方建议同老挝王国政府谈判执行日内瓦协定问题
    新华社河内3日电　老挝消息：苏发努冯亲王代表寮国战斗部队在五月一日发表声明说，寮国战斗部队随时都希望以和平谈判的方式来解决有关老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队之间的问题，在日内瓦协定的基础上保持老挝的中立，从而建成一个和平、独立、民主和统一的老挝。
    声明说，寮国战斗部队建议恢复政治会议，但是遗憾的是王国政府至今还没有答应。正当日内瓦会议的两位主席在会商日内瓦协定履行问题的时候，我很希望并且欢迎两位主席设法使日内瓦协定关于在老挝停止敌对行动的各项条款得到彻底履行，老挝的民主、独立和国家主权得到保障。
    声明要求王国政府抱着诚恳的态度和寮国战斗部队进行谈判，以使日内瓦协定得到严格和彻底的履行；首先是停止敌对行动，为将要到来的政治会议创造有利的条件。
