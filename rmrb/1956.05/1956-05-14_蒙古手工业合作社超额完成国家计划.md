Title: Mongolian Handicraft Cooperatives Exceed State Plan

Author:

Date: 1956-05-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar: Members of the Handicraft Cooperatives of the Mongolian People's Republic have already exceeded the tasks for the first three years of the Second Five-Year Plan. By the end of this Five-Year Plan (1957), the large cooperatives in the centers of all aimags (provinces) will be fully mechanized. The Handicraft Cooperatives of the Mongolian People's Republic were founded in 1931. Currently, the number of members in the Handicraft Cooperatives has increased more than twelvefold compared to 1931; the production of the cooperatives has increased more than fortyfold compared to 1931.

### 蒙古手工业合作社超额完成国家计划

1956-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古手工业合作社超额完成国家计划
    据新华社讯　乌兰巴托消息：蒙古人民共和国手工业合作社的社员们已经超额完成了第二个五年计划中头三年的任务，到这个五年计划结束（1957年）时，各艾马克（省）中心的大合作社将全部机械化。蒙古人民共和国手工业合作社是在1931年创办的。现在，手工业合作社的社员人数已经增加到1931年的十二倍多；合作社的生产已经增加到1931年的四十多倍。
