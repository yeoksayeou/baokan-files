Title: Advanced Producers' Representative Conference Holds Plenary Speeches
Author:
Date: 1956-05-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The National Advanced Producers' Representative Conference began its plenary speeches this afternoon.

　　Song Weiren, Advanced Producer Representative and Head of the Product Design Section of Dalian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Manufacturing Factory, and Ma Yongshun, a lumberjack from the Tieli Forest Industry Bureau of the Harbin Forestry Administration Bureau, vividly introduced their model deeds and advanced experiences at the conference this afternoon. They are the first two advanced producers to speak among a group of advanced producers scheduled to speak at the conference.

　　Bo Yibo, Director of the Third Office of the State Council, spoke at the conference today. In his speech, he called on all employees across the country to rapidly improve their cultural level and actively master new technologies.

　　The heads of the trade union delegations from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia, who were invited to our country, gave speeches to the conference today to express their congratulations.

　　Moskaretz, head of the Soviet trade union delegation, on behalf of the Soviet industrial and agricultural advanced producers present today, expressed his gratitude to the Chinese advanced producers for introducing their experiences to them. He said, "We believe that the exchange of production experience between the workers of our two countries will be further developed." Moskaretz expressed his sincere wishes that the advanced producers of China would achieve greater success in the noble cause of promoting advanced experience.

　　Lee Hyung-kyu, head of the Korean trade union delegation, said that the Korean people have always regarded the achievements of their neighbor, the great China with its 600 million people, as their own victory, because they know that the friendship and solidarity between the Korean and Chinese people have great significance in their struggle for unification and independence. He said that some valuable advanced experiences from this conference will also be promoted among the broad masses in their country.

　　Nguyen Van Hao, head of the Vietnamese trade union delegation, said that the unbreakable friendship between the people of Vietnam and China has played a significant role in the victories they have achieved in the past and are achieving now. The opportunity to learn from China's advanced experience in socialist construction at this conference will be invaluable to their current undertaking of economic recovery.

　　Lubsansamboo, head of the Mongolian trade union delegation, said that they have learned a lot by participating in this conference, and the friendship and cooperation between the Mongolian and Chinese people will be further developed in the future.

　　The trade union delegations from North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia, who congratulated the conference today, also presented flags to the conference respectively; the Mongolian trade union delegation also presented ten Mongolian trade union shock worker medals to the conference and asked them to be forwarded to the ten most outstanding Chinese advanced producers.

　　This morning, the representatives held group discussions on Liu Shaoqi's congratulatory message to the conference on behalf of the CPC Central Committee and Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, report at yesterday's meeting.

### 先进生产者代表会议进行大会发言

1956-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　先进生产者代表会议进行大会发言
    新华社3日讯　全国先进生产者代表会议今天下午起进入大会发言。
    先进生产者代表、大连机车车辆制造工厂产品设计科科长宋伟仁和哈尔滨森林工业管理局铁骊森林工业局伐木工人马永顺，在今天下午的大会上生动地介绍了他们的模范事迹和先进经验。这是预定在大会上发言的一批先进生产者中最先发言的两位先进生产者。
    国务院第三办公室主任薄一波，在今天的大会上讲了话。他在讲话中号召全国职工要迅速提高文化水平，积极掌握新技术。
    应邀来我国的苏联、朝鲜、越南、蒙古等四个国家的工会代表团的团长，今天分别向大会讲话祝贺。
    苏联工会代表团团长莫斯卡列茨代表今天在场的苏联工业和农业先进生产者，向为他们介绍了经验的中国先进生产者表示谢意。他说，我们相信，我们两国劳动者之间交流生产经验的活动，将会得到进一步的发展。莫斯卡列茨表示，他们衷心地预祝中国的先进生产者们在推广先进经验的高尚事业中，获得更大的成就。
    朝鲜工会代表团团长李亨奎说，朝鲜人民把六亿人口的邻邦——伟大的中国所获得的成就，一直看成是自己的胜利，因为他们知道朝中人民之间的友谊和团结，在他们争取统一和独立的斗争中有着巨大的意义。他说，这次会议的一些宝贵的先进经验，也将在他们国家的广大群众中加以推广。
    越南工会代表团团长阮文豪说，越南和中国两国人民之间的牢不可破的友谊，对于他们过去和现在所取得的胜利曾经起了极大作用。他们有机会在这次大会上学习中国在社会主义建设中的先进经验，对于他们目前进行的恢复经济的事业，将是非常宝贵的。
    蒙古工会代表团团长鲁布山桑布说，他们参加这次大会已经学习了很多东西，蒙中两国人民的友谊合作，今后将会进一步发展。
    今天向大会祝贺的朝鲜、越南、蒙古等三个工会代表团，还分别向大会献了旗；蒙古工会代表团并且把十枚蒙古工会突击队员章送给了大会，并请转送给十个最优秀的中国先进生产者。
    今天上午，代表们分组座谈和讨论了刘少奇代表中共中央向大会所作的祝词和中华全国总工会主席赖若愚在昨天会议上所作的报告。
