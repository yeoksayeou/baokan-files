Training Trainees
    The No. 8 Open Hearth Furnace of Anshan Iron and Steel Works is actively training trainees. This is Ma Zhongman (first from left), acting furnace leader of the No. 8 Open Hearth Furnace, teaching Chen Rui (female), Meng Xianzong, and Bai Hengtao, interns from the Anshan No. 2 Steel School, how to observe the temperature of the steel furnace.
