Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Appoints a Group of National Workers
Author:
Date: 1956-05-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, appointed the following individuals on the 12th, according to the decision passed by the 40th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress: Bo Yibo as Director of the State Economic Commission of the People's Republic of China, Huang Jing as Director of the State Technological Commission of the People's Republic of China, Wang Heshou as Director of the State Construction Commission of the People's Republic of China, Wang Heshou as Minister of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry of the People's Republic of China, Peng Tao as Minister of the Ministry of Chemical Industry of the People's Republic of China, Lai Jifa as Minister of the Ministry of Building Materials Industry of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Linzhi as Minister of the Ministry of Electrical Machinery Manufacturing Industry of the People's Republic of China, Wan Li as Minister of the Ministry of Urban Construction of the People's Republic of China, Sha Qianli as Minister of the Ministry of Light Industry of the People's Republic of China, Li Zhuchen as Minister of the Ministry of Food Industry of the People's Republic of China, Xu Deheng as Minister of the Ministry of Aquatic Products of the People's Republic of China, Wang Zhen as Minister of the Ministry of Land Reclamation of the People's Republic of China, and Luo Longji as Minister of the Ministry of Forestry Industry of the People's Republic of China.

### 毛泽东主席任命一批国家工作人员

1956-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席任命一批国家工作人员
    新华社12日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东十二日根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第四十次会议通过的决定，任命薄一波为中华人民共和国国家经济委员会主任，黄敬为中华人民共和国国家技术委员会主任，王鹤寿为中华人民共和国国家建设委员会主任，王鹤寿为中华人民共和国冶金工业部部长，彭涛为中华人民共和国化学工业部部长，赖际发为中华人民共和国建筑材料工业部部长，张霖之为中华人民共和国电机制造工业部部长，万里为中华人民共和国城市建设部部长，沙千里为中华人民共和国轻工业部部长，李烛尘为中华人民共和国食品工业部部长，许德珩为中华人民共和国水产部部长，王震为中华人民共和国农垦部部长，罗隆基为中华人民共和国森林工业部部长。
