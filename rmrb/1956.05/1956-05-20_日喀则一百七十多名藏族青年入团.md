Title: One Hundred and Seventy Tibetan Youths in Xigaze Join the Communist Youth League

Author: 
Date: 1956-05-20
Page: 3

Xigaze: 171 Tibetan youths in Xigaze City have joined the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. This is the first group of Tibetan youths in the Xigaze area of Tibet to join the Youth League. On the 19th, they held an oath-taking ceremony at the Linka (garden) of Deqing Pozhang, the villa of the Panchen Erdeni.

The new League members participating in the oath-taking ceremony included outstanding students from the Panchen Kanpo Lija Committee's cadre training class, model students from Xigaze Elementary School, members of the Xigaze Patriotic Youth Cultural Association and the Patriotic Women's Association who actively participate in social activities, and construction workers.

At the oath-taking ceremony, Zhang Guohua, Acting Secretary of the CCP Tibet Working Committee, congratulated them on behalf of the CCP Tibet Working Committee. Bai Yunfeng, Secretary of the Youth League Tibet Working Committee, also spoke. Qia Bei, Director of the Xigaze Patriotic Youth Cultural Association, delivered a congratulatory message to the newly admitted League members on behalf of the youth of all circles in Xigaze.

(Xinhua News Agency)

### 日喀则一百七十多名藏族青年入团

1956-05-20
第3版()
专栏：

    日喀则一百七十多名藏族青年入团
    日喀则市171名藏族青年加入了中国新民主主义青年团，这是西藏日喀则地区第一批加入青年团的藏族青年。十九日，他们在班禅额尔德尼的别墅——德庆颇章的林卡里，举行了入团宣誓仪式。
    参加宣誓的新团员，有班禅堪布会议厅委员会干部训练班的优秀学员，日喀则小学的模范学生，积极参加社会活动的日喀则市爱国青年文化联谊会和爱国妇女联谊会的会员，以及修建工人等。
    在宣誓仪式上，中共西藏工作委员会代书记张国华代表中共西藏工委向他们祝贺，青年团西藏工作委员会书记白云峰也讲了话。日喀则市爱国青年文化联谊会主任恰贝，代表日喀则各界青年向新入团的青年们致了贺词。
            （新华社）
