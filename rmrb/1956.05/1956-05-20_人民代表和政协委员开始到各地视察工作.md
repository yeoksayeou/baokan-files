Title: People's Representatives and CPPCC Members Begin Inspecting Work in Various Places
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 19th: National People's Congress representatives and members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference have begun and are about to begin inspection work. As of tonight, 139 representatives and members in Beijing have left for various locations to conduct inspections.

　　Some representatives and members have already begun inspecting various locations in Jiangsu. After arriving in Nanjing, they listened to reports from responsible persons of the relevant departments of the Jiangsu Provincial People's Committee on "The Situation of Agricultural Cooperativization and Agricultural Production in Jiangsu Province," "The Situation of Transforming Capitalist Industry and Commerce," and "The Situation of Intellectuals' Work." After specific study, they determined the locations and times for in-depth inspections. Wang Baozhen, Mei Ru'ao, Yu Huancheng, and others have been in Nanjing, Jiangning, Changzhou, Wuxi, Changshu, and other places since the 19th to understand and inspect agricultural cooperativization, agricultural production, and other conditions.

　　Today, the Beijing Municipal People's Committee also held a meeting to discuss issues related to inspection work with the representatives and members who came to Beijing for inspection. Starting from the 20th, more than a hundred representatives and members will begin inspection work successively.

### 人民代表和政协委员开始到各地视察工作

1956-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

    人民代表和政协委员开始到各地视察工作
    据新华社19日讯　全国人民代表大会代表和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员已经开始和即将开始视察工作。到今晚止，在京的代表和委员离京去各地视察工作的已有139人。
    一部分代表和委员们，今天已开始陆续在江苏各地进行视察。他们在来到南京以后，曾听了江苏省人民委员会有关部门负责人所作的“江苏省农业合作化和农业生产情况”、“对资本主义工商业进行改造情况”、“关于知识分子工作情况”等方面的汇报，并且经具体地研究后确定了深入视察的地点和时间。王葆真、梅汝璈、俞寰澄等十人，十九日已经分别在南京、江宁、常州、无锡、常熟等地了解和视察农业合作化和农业生产等情况。
    今天，北京市人民委员会也举行了会议，同到京视察工作的代表和委员们商谈了有关视察工作的问题。从二十日起，一百多个代表和委员将陆续开始视察工作。
