Title: Albanian Labor Party Congress Continues; Delegates Speak in Unanimous Agreement with the Party Central Committee's Domestic and Foreign Policies; Heads of Soviet and Chinese Delegations Address the Congress

Author:
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 27th: According to a TASS report from Tirana: The Third Congress of the Albanian Labor Party continued on the 26th. At the morning session, Tahi Jako delivered a summary report on the work of the Central Auditing Committee.

Subsequently, the discussion of the summary report of the Central Committee and the report of the Central Auditing Committee began.

Those who spoke during the discussion included Paçrami, editor-in-chief of the Albanian Labor Party's central organ "People's Voice," Dervish, first secretary of the Tirana City Party Committee, and representatives of other local party organizations. They cited many examples to illustrate the great achievements made in various regions under the leadership of the Party. At the same time, they also exposed shortcomings hindering the further development of industry, agriculture, and culture. Nushi, Secretary of the Party Central Committee, who spoke last at the morning session, emphasized that the victorious realization of the tasks of the Second Five-Year Plan requires the entire Party organization to greatly improve its leadership over the national economy and gain a deeper understanding of economic issues.

The head of the Soviet Communist Party delegation, Soviet Communist Party Central Committee Secretary Pospelov, spoke at the afternoon session. He gave a detailed account of the glorious struggle history of the Albanian people and their achievements in socialist construction since liberation, as well as the profound friendship between the Soviet and Albanian peoples. Pospelov concluded by reading the congratulatory message from the CPSU Central Committee to the Congress. His speech was repeatedly interrupted by warm applause.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 28th: According to a TASS report from Tirana: The afternoon session of the Third Congress of the Albanian Labor Party on the 26th continued the discussion of the summary reports of the Central Committee and the Central Auditing Committee.

In their speeches, the delegates unanimously agreed with the Central Committee's domestic and foreign policies. The goal of this policy is to further develop the economy and culture, continue to improve the people's material well-being, and consolidate world peace.

While describing Albania's achievements in economic and cultural construction, Central Committee Political Bureau member and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Hysni Kapo, emphasized that these achievements would have been hard to imagine without the help of the Soviet Union and the people's democratic countries. When talking about the tasks of developing agriculture in the Second Five-Year Plan, he said that we should greatly expand the production of agricultural products in order to fully ensure that the people obtain sufficient food and that industry obtains raw materials.

The 26th session also heard a report from Piro Koçi, Chairman of the Credentials Committee. He said that there were a total of 675 delegates with voting rights and 122 delegates with speaking rights at the Congress. The Congress unanimously approved the report of the Credentials Committee.

The head of the Chinese Communist Party delegation, CCP Central Committee member Zeng Shan, delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting, which was warmly welcomed.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表大会继续开会  代表们发言一致同意党中央的内外政策  苏联和我国代表团团长分别向大会致辞

1956-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表大会继续开会
    代表们发言一致同意党中央的内外政策
    苏联和我国代表团团长分别向大会致辞
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第三次代表大会二十六日继续进行。在上午会议上，塔希科作了关于中央检查委员会工作的总结报告。
    随后开始讨论中央委员会的总结报告和中央检查委员会的报告。
    在讨论中发言的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央机关报“人民之声报”总编辑帕奇拉米，地拉那市党委员会第一书记德尔维什和其他一些地方党组织的代表。他们举出许多例子来说明各地区在党的领导下所取得的巨大成就。同时，他们也揭露了妨碍着工农业和文化进一步发展的缺点。在上午会议上最后发言的党中央委员会书记努什强调指出，要胜利实现第二个五年计划的任务，就要求整个党组织大大改进对国民经济的领导、更深刻地了解经济问题。
    苏联共产党代表团团长、苏联共产党中央委员会书记波斯伯洛夫在下午会议上讲了话。他详细叙述了阿尔巴尼亚人民的光荣斗争历史和解放以后在社会主义建设中的成就以及苏、阿两国人民的深厚友谊。波斯伯洛夫最后宣读了苏共中央给代表大会的贺词。他的讲话多次为热烈的掌声所打断。
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第三次代表大会二十六日下午会议，继续讨论中央委员会和中央检查委员会的总结报告。
    代表们在发言中一致同意中央委员会的内政和外交政策。这个政策的目标是：进一步发展经济和文化，继续提高人民的物质福利，巩固世界和平。
    中央委员会政治局委员、部长会议第一副主席希·卡博在叙述阿尔巴尼亚在经济和文化建设方面的成绩的时候，强调指出，这些成绩如果没有苏联和人民民主国家的帮助是很难想像的。他在谈到第二个五年计划中发展农业的任务的时候说，我们应当大大地扩大农产品的生产，以便充分地保证人民获得足够的粮食，保证工业获得原料。
    二十六日会议还听取了资格审查委员会主席皮洛·科金的报告。他说，大会代表中有选举权的代表共有675名，有发言权的代表共有122名。大会一致批准了资格审查委员会的报告。
    中国共产党代表团团长、中共中央委员曾山在会上致祝词，受到了热烈的欢迎。
