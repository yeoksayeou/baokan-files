Title: Central Delegation's First Sub-Delegation Leaves Xigaze, Second Sub-Delegation Leaves Qamdo
Author:
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Xigaze, 26th: After a fourteen-day visit to Xigaze, Gyantse, and Yatung, the first sub-delegation of the Central Delegation left Xigaze yesterday. More than 6,000 people from all walks of life in Xigaze went to the Nyang Chu River to see them off. On the 24th, the Panchen Kanpo Conference Hall Committee and the Xigaze Sub-committee of the CPC Tibet Working Committee jointly held a farewell banquet.
    Xinhua News Agency, Qamdo, 28th: The second sub-delegation of the Central Delegation left Qamdo today, warmly seen off by more than 5,000 people. During its stay in Qamdo, the Central Delegation visited mass organizations, lamaseries, government offices, and the Qamdo Elementary School in Qamdo City, and extended greetings to the People's Liberation Army stationed in Qamdo and the officers and soldiers of the 9th Daibon Tibetan Army, as well as the patients recuperating in the hospital.

### 中央代表团第一分团离日喀则第二分团离昌都

1956-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中央代表团第一分团离日喀则第二分团离昌都
    据新华社日喀则26日电　中央代表团第一分团在日喀则、江孜、亚东进行了十四天的访问以后，昨天离开日喀则。日喀则各界六千多人，前往年楚河边送行。二十四日班禅堪布会议厅委员会和中共西藏工委日喀则分工委联合举行了欢送宴会。
    据新华社昌都28日电　中央代表团第二分团今天离开昌都，五千多人热烈欢送。中央代表团在昌都期间，曾访问了昌都市群众团体、喇嘛寺院、机关和昌都小学等单位，慰问了驻昌都人民解放军和藏军第九代本官兵以及住在医院休养的病员。
