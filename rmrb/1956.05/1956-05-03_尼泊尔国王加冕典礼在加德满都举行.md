Title: Nepalese King's Coronation Ceremony Held in Kathmandu
Author:
Date: 1956-05-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, May 2nd: The coronation ceremony of the King of Nepal, Mahendra Bir Bikram Shah Deva, was held on May 2nd in the ancient Hanuman Dhoka Palace. Government delegations from sixteen countries, including the People's Republic of China, India, Indonesia, Burma, Ceylon, Pakistan, Afghanistan, Thailand, the United Kingdom, the United States, France, and Japan, attended the coronation ceremony. The Special Envoy of the People's Republic of China, Vice Premier of the State Council Ulanhu, led the delegation of the People's Republic of China and was introduced to the King of Nepal at the coronation ceremony.
　　On this day, the Nepalese capital of Kathmandu was beautifully decorated. The streets were hung with the national flag of Nepal and the flags of China and other countries. When the King of Nepal rode a richly decorated elephant from his residence to the Hanuman Dhoka Palace, tens of thousands of Nepalese people lined the streets to cheer the King.
　　The coronation celebrations will continue for a week. On the afternoon of May 1st, King Mahendra presided over the opening ceremony of an exhibition on the country's development. The Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Pan Zili, attended the opening ceremony.
　　On the evening of May 1st, the Chinese delegation attended a reception held at the Royal Palace.

### 尼泊尔国王加冕典礼在加德满都举行

1956-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　尼泊尔国王加冕典礼在加德满都举行
    新华社加德满都2日电　尼泊尔国王马亨德拉·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦的加冕典礼五月二日在古老的哈努曼·多卡宫中举行。出席加冕典礼的有中华人民共和国、印度、印度尼西亚、缅甸、锡兰、巴基斯坦、阿富汗、泰国、联合王国、美国、法国、日本等十六个国家的政府代表团。中华人民共和国特使、国务院副总理乌兰夫率领的中华人民共和国代表团在加冕典礼上被介绍同尼泊尔国王相见。
    在这一天，尼泊尔的首都加德满都被装饰得美丽多采。街道上悬挂着尼泊尔的国旗以及中国和其他国家的国旗。当尼泊尔国王骑着一匹装饰华丽的大象从他的寓所到哈努曼·多卡宫的时候，成千上万的尼泊尔人民沿途列队向国王欢呼。
    加冕典礼的节日将继续一星期。在五月一日下午，国王马亨德拉主持了关于国家发展情况的展览会的开幕式。中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力出席了这个开幕式。
    在五月一日的晚上，中国代表团出席了在王宫举行的招待会。
