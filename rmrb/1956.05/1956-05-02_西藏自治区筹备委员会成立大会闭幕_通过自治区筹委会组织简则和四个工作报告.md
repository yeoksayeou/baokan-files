Title: Closing of the Inaugural Meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region: Adoption of the Organizational Regulations of the Preparatory Committee and Four Work Reports
Author:
Date: 1956-05-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 1st: The inaugural meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region concluded in Lhasa today. All members unanimously approved the organizational regulations of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and the work reports presented to the meeting by the Dalai Lama, Panchen Erdeni, Zhang Guohua, and Wang Qimei.

　　At today's meeting, the Dalai Lama, Panchen Erdeni, and Living Buddha Pabala, representing the Tibetan local government, the Panchen Kampus Assembly Hall Committee, and the People's Liberation Committee of the Qamdo area, respectively, presented banners and gifts to Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and the Central Delegation. Chen Yi, head of the Central Delegation and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Zhang Jingwu and Wang Feng, deputy heads of the Central Delegation, accepted the banners and gifts presented on behalf of Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and the Central Delegation. When the banner and gift presentation ceremony took place, thunderous applause erupted in the venue and lasted for a long time. Chen Yi, Zhang Jingwu, and Wang Feng also accepted gifts presented by the Tibetan local government, the Panchen Kampus Assembly Hall Committee, and the People's Liberation Committee of the Qamdo area to the Central National Song and Dance Troupe, the Wuhan Peking Opera Troupe, and the Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe that came to Lhasa with the Central Delegation.

　　The meeting unanimously approved a congratulatory telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and the State Council, to be forwarded to Chairman Mao. When the congratulatory telegram was passed, thunderous applause resounded once again in the venue.

　　Chen Yi gave instructions at today's meeting. He said: Unity is the source of great strength. In the past few years, the central government and the Tibetan local government have been united, the two leaders of the Tibetan people—the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni—have been united, and the Kashag, the Kampus Assembly Hall Committee, and the People's Liberation Committee of the Qamdo area have also been united. Our unity has enabled the Tibetan region to make great progress and development in recent years, and on this basis, the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region has been successfully established. We firmly believe that after the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, as long as we continue to implement the central government's ethnic policies and policies protecting freedom of religious belief, respect and understand each other in all aspects, take care of the small ones like brothers, the small ones support the big ones, see more of others' strengths, and examine and criticize our own shortcomings more, then our unity and cooperation will be even better, our various tasks will be able to achieve even more brilliant achievements, and the Tibetan people will make great contributions to the construction of the great motherland.

　　After Chen Yi's speech, Panchen Erdeni delivered the closing remarks, announcing the successful conclusion of the inaugural meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

　　At today's meeting, Huang Zhengqing (Tibetan), Vice Governor of Gansu Province, Anniwar Jiaculin (Kazak), member of the National People's Congress Ethnic Affairs Committee, and Gara Cang (Mongolian), Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, continued to address the meeting with congratulatory speeches.

### 西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会闭幕  通过自治区筹委会组织简则和四个工作报告

1956-05-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会闭幕
    通过自治区筹委会组织简则和四个工作报告
    据新华社拉萨1日电　西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会今天在拉萨闭幕。全体委员在会上一致通过了西藏自治区筹备委员会组织简则和达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼、张国华、王其梅向大会所作的工作报告。
    在今天的大会上，达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉活佛，分别代表西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会和昌都地区人民解放委员会向毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理和中央代表团献旗献礼，中央代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅和中央代表团副团长张经武、汪锋，代表毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理和中央代表团接受了他们所献的锦旗和礼品。在献旗献礼仪式进行的时候，会场里响起了暴风雨般的掌声，经久不息。陈毅、张经武、汪锋还接受了西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会和昌都地区人民解放委员会赠给随中央代表团来拉萨的中央民族歌舞团、武汉京剧团和重庆杂技团的礼品。
    大会一致通过了致中国共产党中央委员会、全国人民代表大会常务委员会、国务院并转毛主席的致敬电。在通过致敬电的时候，会场里又一次响起暴风雨般的掌声。
    陈毅在今天的大会上作了指示。他说：团结是产生伟大力量的源泉。过去几年，中央和西藏地方是团结的，西藏人民的两位领袖——达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼是团结的，噶厦、堪布会议厅委员会和昌都地区人民解放委员会也是团结的。我们的团结，已经使西藏地区这几年来有了很大的进步和发展，并且在这一基础之上，胜利地成立了西藏自治区筹备委员会。我们深信，在西藏自治区筹备委员会成立之后，只要继续贯彻中央的民族政策和保护宗教信仰自由政策，各方面互相尊重、互相谅解，像兄弟一样地大的照顾小的，小的支持大的，多看到别人的长处，多检查、批评自己的缺点，那么，我们的团结合作就会搞得更好，我们的各项工作就能够取得更加辉煌的成就，西藏民族将在伟大祖国的建设中作出巨大贡献。
    陈毅讲话后，班禅额尔德尼致闭幕词，宣布西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会胜利闭幕。
    在今天的会上，继续有甘肃省副省长黄正清（藏族）、全国人民代表大会民族委员会委员安尼瓦尔·加库林（哈萨克族）、中国佛教协会副会长噶喇藏（蒙古族）向大会致祝词。
