Title: Soviet High-Energy Research Work Two or Three Years Ahead of the United States - Talk by Famous American Chemist Dr. Libby
Author:
Date: 1956-05-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, May 15th, New York: According to the "New York Post" in early May, Dr. Libby, a member of the U.S. Atomic Energy Commission and a famous chemist, recently said that the Soviet Union's research work in the extremely important field of high energy is at least two to three years ahead of the United States. Research in high energy is to study how to control and harness thermonuclear power for peaceful uses.

    Libby said that this is "an important new development in nuclear physics," and we have learned that "the Russians are ahead in this regard." Libby said that the United States is currently using two smaller accelerators, and the United States does not have an accelerator as large as the Soviet Union's.

    The "New York Post" said that Libby made these remarks at a closed-door meeting with leaders of the Congressional Atomic Energy Committee. At the meeting, he requested an increase in U.S. nuclear research funding.

### 苏联的高能研究工作比美国先进两三年  美国著名化学家利毕博士的谈话

1956-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联的高能研究工作比美国先进两三年
    美国著名化学家利毕博士的谈话
    新华社15日讯　纽约消息：据五月上旬的“纽约邮报”报道，美国原子能委员会委员、著名化学家利毕博士最近说：苏联在极其重要的高能方面的研究工作走在美国前面至少两年到三年，高能方面的研究工作是研究如何控制和驾驭热核子动力而把它用于和平用途。
    利毕说，这方面是“核子物理方面的重要新事物”，我们已经获悉“俄国人在这方面领先”。利毕说，美国现在正在使用两架较小的加速器，美国没有苏联那么大的加速器。
    “纽约邮报”说，利毕是在同国会原子能委员会领袖们举行的一次秘密会议上谈到这些情况的。他在会上要求增加美国的核子研究经费。
