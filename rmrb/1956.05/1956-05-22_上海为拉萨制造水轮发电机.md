Shanghai Manufactures Water Turbine Generators for Lhasa
The Shanghai Electric Machinery Plant recently manufactured three water turbine generators specially designed for the Lhasa region. Tests have proven that these three generators will be able to generate electricity normally under the atmospheric pressure conditions of the Tibetan Plateau.
　　Each of these three water turbine generators has a generating capacity of 360 kilowatts. The electricity they generate can meet the needs of Lhasa for lighting and industrial electricity. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 上海为拉萨制造水轮发电机

1956-05-22
第2版()
专栏：

    上海为拉萨制造水轮发电机
    为拉萨地区特制的三台水轮发电机最近在上海电机厂制造出来。经过试验，证明这三台发电机将来在西藏高原的气压条件下，也能正常发电。
    这三台水轮发电机每台的发电能力是三百六十瓩。它们发出的电力，可以满足拉萨地区的照明和工业用电的需要。　（新华社）
