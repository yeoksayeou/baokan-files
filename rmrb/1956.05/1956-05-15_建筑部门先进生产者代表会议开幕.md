Title: Opening of the Advanced Producers' Representative Conference of the Construction Department
Author:
Date: 1956-05-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 14th: The Advanced Producers' Representative Conference of the Ministry of Construction Engineering and the Ministry of Urban Construction opened in Beijing this afternoon.

Zhou Rongxin, Vice Minister of Construction Engineering, said in his opening speech today that the purpose of this conference is to summarize and exchange advanced experiences in various aspects of the construction industry, implement the spirit of the National Advanced Producers' Representative Conference, deepen the advanced producers' movement, and mobilize the broad masses of workers to complete the construction tasks stipulated by the state in a greater, faster, better, and more economical way.

There are 499 advanced producer representatives attending today's meeting, including advanced figures from all aspects of construction and installation enterprises. 172 representatives attended today's meeting as observers, including advanced producers from construction and installation enterprises affiliated to the former Ministry of Heavy Industry and the Ministry of Electric Power Industry, and the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

At today's meeting, Zhang Xiuzhu, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, representatives of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and representatives of suburban farmers of Beijing, all spoke and congratulated the advanced producers.

Also attending today's meeting was the Soviet Construction Industry Advanced Producers Delegation headed by Baranovsky. After Comrade Baranovsky delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting, he presented a gift to the conference on behalf of the Central Committee of the Soviet Urban and Rural Construction Workers' Union.

### 建筑部门先进生产者代表会议开幕

1956-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

    建筑部门先进生产者代表会议开幕
    据新华社14日讯　建筑工程部和城市建设部先进生产者代表会议今天下午在北京开幕。
    建筑工程部副部长周荣鑫今天在会议开幕词中说，这次会议是要总结和交流建筑业各个方面的先进经验，贯彻全国先进生产者代表会议的精神，深入开展先进生产者运动，动员广大职工又多、又快、又好、又省地完成国家规定的建设任务。
    出席今天会议的先进生产者代表有499人，包括了建筑安装企业各个方面的先进人物。列席今天会议的代表172人，其中有原重工业部和电力工业部、中国人民解放军总后勤部等部门所属建筑安装企业的先进生产者。
    在今天的会议上，中华全国总工会书记处书记张修竹、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会代表、北京市郊区农民代表，都讲了话，向先进生产者祝贺。
    出席今天会议的，还有以巴兰诺夫斯基为首的苏联建筑业先进生产者代表团。巴兰诺夫斯基团长在会上致祝词以后，代表苏联城乡建筑工会中央委员会向大会献礼。
