Title: Tito to Visit Soviet Union Next Month
Author:
Date: 1956-05-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 29th (TASS) -- It is reported that Josip Broz Tito, President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, has accepted the invitation of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet and the government of the Soviet Union and will visit the Soviet Union in June of this year.

### 铁托下月将访问苏联

1956-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    铁托下月将访问苏联
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科29日讯：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托接受苏联最高苏维埃主席团和政府的邀请，将在今年六月到苏联访问。
