Title: New Swedish Ambassador to China, Klas Book, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-05-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 4th—Klas Book, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Sweden to China, arrived in Beijing by car this afternoon. Wang Yutian, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present at the station to welcome him.

　　Also present at the station to welcome him were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China. The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy in China, Hjalmar Hammarskjöld, and embassy diplomats were also there to welcome him.

### 瑞典新任驻我国大使克拉斯·波克到达北京

1956-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　瑞典新任驻我国大使克拉斯·波克到达北京
    新华社4日讯　瑞典王国新任驻华特命全权大使克拉斯·波克在今天下午乘车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有外交部礼宾司副司长王雨田。
    到车站欢迎的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。瑞典驻华大使馆临时代办哈马孝尔德和使馆外交人员也前往欢迎。
