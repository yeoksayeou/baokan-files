Title: Zhejiang Province Establishes Schistosomiasis Prevention and Control Scientific Research Committee
Author:
Date: 1956-05-09
Page: 3

    Zhejiang Province Establishes Schistosomiasis Prevention and Control Scientific Research Committee

    The Zhejiang Province Schistosomiasis Prevention and Control Scientific Research Committee was established in Hangzhou City on the 7th.

    The task of this committee is mainly to research the prevention and control of schistosomiasis and other parasitic diseases throughout the province. The fifty-eight committee members are all Chinese and Western medicine doctors from all over the province and professors from Zhejiang Medical College. They have rich experience in medical science research, especially in scientific research and clinical treatment for the prevention and control of schistosomiasis.

    This committee is divided into four research groups: clinical treatment, experimental treatment, prevention, and experimental diagnosis.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 浙江省成立血吸虫病防治科学研究委员会

1956-05-09
第3版()
专栏：

    浙江省成立血吸虫病防治科学研究委员会
    浙江省血吸虫病防治科学研究委员会七日在杭州市成立。
    这个委员会的任务，主要是研究全省血吸虫病及其他各种寄生虫病的防治工作。组成这个委员会的五十八个委员都是来自全省各地的中、西医师和浙江医学院的教授，他们在医学科学的研究上，特别是在防治血吸虫病方面的科学研究和临床治疗方面都有丰富的经验。
    这个委员会分临床治疗、实验治疗、预防和实验诊断等四个研究组。
                      （新华社）
