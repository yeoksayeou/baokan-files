Title: Saudi Arabian King Supports Egypt's Recognition of China
Author:
Date: 1956-05-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 26th. News from Saudi Arabia states that the King of Saudi Arabia, Saud, has expressed his heartfelt support for Egypt's recognition of China.
    This news was published in the Egyptian "Al-Gomhuria" on May 25th. The news quoted the King of Saudi Arabia as saying that he believes Egypt's reasons for recognizing China are correct, and he wishes the Egyptian Prime Minister, Gamal Abdel Nasser, success in the policies he is implementing.

### 沙特阿拉伯国王支持埃及承认中国

1956-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯国王支持埃及承认中国
    新华社26日讯　开罗消息：来自沙特阿拉伯的消息说，沙特阿拉伯国王沙特对埃及承认中国一事表示衷心的支持。
    这则消息登载在五月二十五日的埃及“共和国报”上。这则消息引述沙特阿拉伯国王的话说，他相信埃及承认中国的理由是正确的，并且祝埃及总理阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔在他所执行的政策中获得成就。
