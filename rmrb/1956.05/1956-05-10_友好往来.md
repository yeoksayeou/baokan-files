Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-10
Page: 4

    Friendly Exchanges
    △ The Polish film workers delegation, consisting of four members, who came to China for a visit at the invitation of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, returned home by plane on the 9th.
    △ The Sudanese trade union delegation, headed by Abd al-Latif Ali al-Durr, which came to China for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Beijing by train on the 9th.

### 友好往来

1956-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    △应中华人民共和国文化部邀请来中国访问的波兰电影工作者代表团一行四人在九日乘飞机回国。
    △应中华全国总工会邀请来中国参观访问的以阿卜杜勒·拉蒂尔·阿里·埃尔·多尔为首的苏丹工会代表团，九日乘火车到达北京。
