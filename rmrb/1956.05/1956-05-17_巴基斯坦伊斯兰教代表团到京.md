Title: Pakistani Islamic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-05-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 16th - The Pakistani Islamic delegation of eight people, led by Chairman Syed Hussaini Imam, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 16th, at the invitation of the China Islamic Association.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included Nur Muhammad Dapusheng, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association, Muhammad Ali Zhang Yuzhen, Secretary-General, Muhammad Mubini Mamingji, Deputy Secretary-General, as well as members in Beijing, Ahong in Beijing, responsible persons of the China Hui Nationality Cultural Advancement Association, and famous Beijing Ahongs, responsible persons, teachers, and students of the China Islamic Theological Seminary.

Personnel from the Pakistani Embassy in China also went to the station to welcome them.

When the distinguished guests got off the train, the hosts and guests greeted each other with "As-salamu alaykum," and the students of the seminary presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

The members of the delegation are:

Grand Mullah Muhammad Abdul Hamid Qadri Badyouni
Grand Mullah Abdul Kodus Hashimi
Grand Mullah Syed Abdul Monem Edwe
Dr. Muhammad Shahidullah
Grand Mullah Azizurrahman (Imam)
Grand Mullah Mustafizur Rahman
Dr. Shirajul Haq

### 巴基斯坦伊斯兰教代表团到京

1956-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦伊斯兰教代表团到京
    新华社16日讯　应中国伊斯兰教协会的邀请前来中国访问的巴基斯坦伊斯兰教代表团一行八人，由团长赛义德·侯赛尼依玛木率领，在十六日晚乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的，有中国伊斯兰教协会副主任努尔·穆罕默德·达浦生，秘书长穆罕默德·阿里·张玉珍，副秘书长穆罕默德·穆比尼·马明基以及在京委员、在京罕智，中国回民文化协进会负责人和北京著名阿訇，中国伊斯兰教经学院负责人和教师、学生们。
    巴基斯坦驻华大使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
    当贵宾们下车后，宾主互道“色蓝”，经学院的学生们向贵宾们献了花。
    代表团团员有：
    大毛拉　穆罕默德·阿卜杜勒·阿米德·夸德里·巴迪昂尼
    大毛拉　阿卜杜勒·柯多斯·哈希米
    大毛拉　赛义德·阿卜杜勒·摩奈姆·埃德韦
    穆罕默德·沙希杜拉博士
    大毛拉　阿齐朱尔拉赫曼（依玛木）
    大毛拉　穆斯塔菲朱尔·拉赫曼
    希拉朱尔·哈克博士
