Title: Shen Yanbing Holds Reception to Bid Farewell to Burmese Film Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-05-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 14th: Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing held a reception this evening to bid farewell to U Aung Na, head of the Burmese Film Delegation, and all members of the delegation, who are about to leave Beijing to visit other parts of the country.

Present at the reception were Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maw and his wife, Vice Ministers of Culture Ding Xilin and Xia Yan, Vice President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Qian Weichang, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhao Yimin and Yang Hansheng, and Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture Wang Lanxi, among others.

In his speech at the reception, Shen Yanbing said that the visit of the Burmese Film Delegation to China had promoted cultural exchange between China and Burma. During the delegation's visit to Beijing, Chinese film workers exchanged experiences with them and learned a great deal. He hoped that the film circles of the two countries would maintain regular contact in the future and continue to exchange experiences with each other.

U Aung Na then delivered a speech, saying, "Since our arrival in Beijing, we have experienced the friendship of the Chinese people. We are particularly pleased to see with our own eyes that New China is advancing towards a new life, which has greatly encouraged the Burmese people."

At the reception, the Ministry of Culture and the Burmese Film Delegation exchanged gifts.

### 沈雁冰举行酒会  欢送缅甸电影代表团

1956-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰举行酒会
    欢送缅甸电影代表团
    据新华社14日讯　文化部部长沈雁冰今天晚间举行酒会，欢送即将离北京到各地参观的缅甸电影代表团团长吴仰拿和全体团员。
    出席酒会的，有缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和夫人，文化部副部长丁西林、夏衍，中缅友好协会副会长钱伟长，中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏、阳翰笙，文化部电影事业管理局局长王阑西等。
    沈雁冰在酒会上致词说，缅甸电影代表团来中国访问，促进了中缅两国的文化交流。在代表团访问北京期间，中国电影工作者同他们交流经验，学到了很多东西。他希望两国电影界在今后保持经常接触，继续互相交流经验。
    吴仰拿接着致词说，从我们来到北京以后，我们就体会到了中国人民的友谊。我们特别感到高兴的是，亲眼看到新中国正在向新生活进军，这使缅甸人民得到了很大的鼓舞。
    在酒会上，文化部和缅甸电影代表团互相赠送了礼品。
