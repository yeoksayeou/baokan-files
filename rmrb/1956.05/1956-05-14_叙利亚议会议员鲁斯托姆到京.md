Title: Syrian Parliamentarian Rustom Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-05-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 13th: Abdul Majid Rustom, a member of the Syrian Parliament, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 13th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs for a visit to China.

### 叙利亚议会议员鲁斯托姆到京

1956-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚议会议员鲁斯托姆到京
    新华社13日讯　叙利亚议会议员阿卜杜勒·麦吉德·鲁斯托姆，应中国人民外交学会邀请来中国访问，已于十三日下午抵达北京。
