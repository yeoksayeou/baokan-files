Title: The CPC Central Committee Sends Zeng Shan and Xu Yixin as Representatives to Attend the Albanian Party of Labour Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-05-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th—The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has sent Zeng Shan, member of the CPC Central Committee, and Xu Yixin, Chinese Ambassador to Albania, as representatives to attend the Third Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour, which will be held on May 25th. Zeng Shan already departed from Beijing by plane to Albania on the morning of the 15th.

### 参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表大会  中共中央派曾山和徐以新为代表

1956-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表大会
    中共中央派曾山和徐以新为代表
    新华社15日讯　中国共产党中央委员会派中共中央委员曾山和中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使徐以新为代表，参加即将在五月二十五日召开的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第三次代表大会。曾山已经在十五日晨由北京乘机前往阿尔巴尼亚。
