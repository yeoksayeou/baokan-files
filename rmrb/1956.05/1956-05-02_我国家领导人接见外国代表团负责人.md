Title: Our National Leaders Receive Heads of Foreign Delegations
Author:
Date: 1956-05-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 1st. Chairman Mao Zedong and Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai received the heads of various delegations and prominent figures from more than 50 countries on the evening of "May Day" and watched fireworks with them on Tiananmen Square, together with tens of thousands of people who were celebrating on Tiananmen Square.

### 我国家领导人接见外国代表团负责人

1956-05-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国家领导人接见外国代表团负责人
    新华社1日讯　毛泽东主席和朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理在“五一”节的晚上，接见了来自五十多个国家的各代表团负责人和著名人士，并且同他们一起在天安门上观看了焰火和数以万计的群众在天安门广场上的联欢。
