Title: Jordanian King Condemns Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1956-05-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Amman, May 17th: King Hussein of Jordan denounced on May 16th that "imperialism divides Arab countries and keeps them hundreds of years behind in civilization."
    Hussein said that imperialism has made the Arabs politically divided and disunited. He made these remarks at a social welfare discussion jointly sponsored by the Arab League and the United Nations in cooperation with the Jordanian government. Representatives from various Arab countries attended the discussion.

### 约旦国王谴责帝国主义

1956-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

    约旦国王谴责帝国主义
    新华社17日讯　安曼消息：约旦国王侯赛尼五月十六日指责“帝国主义分裂阿拉伯国家，并且使它们停留在文明的后面好几百年”。
    侯赛尼说，帝国主义已经使阿拉伯人在政治上陷于分裂和不统一。他是在阿拉伯联盟和联合国在约旦政府的合作下联合主办的一次社会福利讨论会上说这番话的。阿拉伯各国的代表参加了这个讨论会。
