Title: French Police Confiscate "L'Humanité"; Duclos Questions the Government
Author:
Date: 1956-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 14th -- TASS reports: According to the May 13th edition of "L'Humanité Dimanche," French Communist Party parliamentary group leader Duclos questioned the government in parliament regarding the police confiscation of the May 11th "L'Humanité." The questioning pointed out that the May 11th "L'Humanité" advocated for "a peaceful resolution to the Algerian issue that would benefit both France and Algeria."

### 法警察当局没收“人道报”  杜克洛向政府提出质问

1956-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    法警察当局没收“人道报”  杜克洛向政府提出质问
    新华社14日讯　塔斯社巴黎讯：据十三日出版的“人道报星期日刊”消息，法国共产党议会党团主席杜克洛就警察没收五月十一日“人道报”一事在议会中向政府提出了质询。质询中指出，这一天的“人道报”主张“和平解决阿尔及利亚问题以有利于法国和阿尔及利亚两国”。
