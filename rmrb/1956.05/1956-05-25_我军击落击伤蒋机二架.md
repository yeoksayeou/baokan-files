Title: Our Army Shot Down and Damaged Two Chiang Kai-shek Planes
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shantou, 24th. At 9:10 AM on the 24th, four Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter planes intruded into the airspace over the Shantou area of Guangdong Province. The People's Liberation Army's anti-aircraft artillery units intercepted them with fierce artillery fire, shooting down and damaging one Chiang Kai-shek plane each. The shot-down Chiang Kai-shek plane crashed into the sea near Nanpeng Island.

### 我军击落击伤蒋机二架

1956-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我军击落击伤蒋机二架
    新华社汕头24日电　二十四日九时十分，蒋军F—84型战斗机四架窜扰广东省汕头地区上空，中国人民解放军高射炮兵部队以猛烈炮火截击，击落、击伤蒋机各一架。被击落的蒋机坠入南澎岛附近的海里。
