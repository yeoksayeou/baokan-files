Title: Japanese Student Delegation Departs to Attend Asian-African Students Conference
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-25
Page: 4

    Attending Asian-African Students Conference
    Japanese Student Delegation Departs
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 23rd. The Japanese student delegation attending the Asian-African Students Conference left Tokyo for Indonesia at midnight on the 22nd. The head of the delegation is Watanabe Hideo of Takushoku University in Tokyo.

### 参加亚非学生会议  日本学生代表团启程

1956-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

    参加亚非学生会议
    日本学生代表团启程
    新华社23日讯　东京消息：出席亚非学生会议的日本学生代表团，已经在二十二日午夜离开东京前往印度尼西亚。代表团的团长是东京拓殖大学的渡边秀雄。
