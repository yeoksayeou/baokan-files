Title: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds its Fortieth Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th.

    The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its fortieth meeting on the afternoon of the 12th.

    After hearing the explanation from Wang Zi'yi, Vice Minister of Internal Affairs, the meeting discussed the suggestions put forward by Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai regarding the number of deputies to the people's congresses of municipalities directly under the central government, counties, cities, municipal districts, townships, ethnic townships, and towns, and adopted the decision on the number of deputies to the people's congresses of counties, cities, municipal districts, townships, ethnic townships, and towns. The meeting also adopted the decision on the election time of deputies to the people's congresses at all levels below the municipalities directly under the central government and counties in 1956.

    After hearing the explanation from Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai regarding the two proposals to adjust the organizational structure of the financial and economic departments under the State Council and to request the approval of the establishment of the Bureau of Experts under the State Council, the meeting adopted the resolution on adjusting the organizational structure of the departments under the State Council.

    The meeting also adopted a resolution on preparing to convene the Third Session of the First National People's Congress.

    The meeting also adopted several decisions on appointments and removals.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行第四十次会议

1956-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会举行第四十次会议
    新华社12日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会第四十次会议在十二日下午举行。
    会议在听了内务部副部长王子宜的说明后，讨论了国务院总理周恩来提出的关于直辖市、县、市、市辖区、乡、民族乡、镇人民代表大会代表名额等问题的建议，通过了关于县、市、市辖区、乡、民族乡、镇人民代表大会代表名额等问题的决定。会议还通过了关于1956年直辖市和县以下各级人民代表大会代表选举时间的决定。
    会议在听了国务院总理周恩来关于提请调整国务院所属财经部门组织机构和提请批准国务院设立专家局的两个议案的说明后，通过关于调整国务院所属组织机构的决议。
    会议还通过关于准备召集第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议决议。
    会议并通过决定任免事项多起。
