Title: Japan Forms Committee to Welcome Chinese Peking Opera Troupe
Author:
Date: 1956-05-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 19th: A committee to welcome the Chinese Peking Opera Delegation to Japan was formed on May 18th in Tokyo by people from all walks of life in Japan, centered on the Japanese literary and art circles.

The welcoming committee includes Kubota Mantaro, president of the Japan Theatre Association; Kawatake Shigetoshi, director of the Tsubouchi Memorial Theatre Museum at Waseda University; Ichikawa Ennosuke, a famous Kabuki actor; Aono Suekichi, president of the Japan Literary Artists Association; Abe Yoshishige, president of Gakushuin University; Furukaki Tetsuro, president of the Japan Broadcasting Corporation (NHK); Nobuo Kanichiro, editor-in-chief of the Asahi Shimbun; Suzuki Mosaburo, chairman of the Japan Socialist Party; Suma Yakichiro, a member of the Diet from the Liberal Democratic Party; and over 150 other prominent figures such as Ikeda Masanosuke, Murata Shozo, Matsumoto Jiichiro, Katayama Tetsu, Kuhara Fusanosuke, Shimazu Tadanari, and Oku Umeo.

At the meeting, Aono Suekichi said that the Japanese literary world sincerely welcomes the Chinese Peking Opera Delegation to Japan and hopes to take the opportunity of the visit of the Chinese Peking Opera representatives to Japan to realize a wide range of cultural exchanges between Japan and China.

The Chinese Peking Opera Delegation to Japan is scheduled to begin its first performance in Tokyo on May 30th, but tickets were all reserved by enthusiastic audiences before they went on sale.

### 日本组成欢迎我国京剧团的委员会

1956-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    日本组成欢迎我国京剧团的委员会
    新华社19日讯　东京消息：以日本文艺界为中心的日本各阶层人士五月十八日在东京开会，成立了欢迎中国访日京剧代表团的委员会。
    参加欢迎委员会的有日本演剧协会会长久保田万太郎、早稻田大学坪内博士纪念演剧博物馆馆长河竹繁俊、日本歌舞伎著名演员市川猿之助、日本文艺家协会会长青野季吉、学习院大学校长安倍能成、日本广播协会会长古垣铁郎、“朝日新闻”编辑局长信夫韩一郎、日本社会党主席铃木茂三郎、自由民主党国会议员须磨弥吉郎以及池田正之辅、村田省藏、松本治一郎、片山哲、久原房之助、岛津忠承、奥梅绪等著名人士一百五十多人。
    青野季吉在会上说，日本文学界衷心欢迎中国访日京剧代表团，并且希望借中国京剧界代表访问日本的机会实现日中文化的广泛交流。
    中国访日京剧代表团预定五月三十日在东京开始首次演出，但是戏票在出售前就被热心的观众预订光了。
