Title: China Power Sports Association Men's and Women's Basketball Teams to Visit North Korea
Author:
Date: 1956-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Chinese Power Sports Association Men's and Women's Basketball Teams, invited to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, left Beijing by train for North Korea on the 17th. This sports team is composed of employees from the power industry system. It is the first sports team from China to make a formal visit to North Korea, and also the first employee sports team to visit a foreign country.

### 中国动力体育协会男女篮球代表队去朝鲜访问

1956-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中国动力体育协会男女篮球代表队去朝鲜访问
    据新华社17日讯　应邀前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问的中国动力体育协会男女篮球代表队，十七日乘火车离开北京去朝鲜。这个体育队是由电业系统的职工组成的，是中国到朝鲜作正式访问的第一个运动队，也是第一个出国访问的职工运动队。
