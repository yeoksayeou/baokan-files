Title: Cotton Bumper Harvest Experience Being Studied

Author: 
Date: 1956-05-08
Page: 1

More than two hundred agricultural technicians from Northeast China, North China, and the Yellow River and Yangtze River basins are studying cotton bumper harvest experience in the agricultural production units of the Xinjiang Military Region. This is them with Ainaituola (center) of the Tuanjie Agricultural Cooperative in Toksun County, Xinjiang, measuring the depth of cotton seeds sown by the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps.
