Title: Sweden, Denmark and Norway Invite Soviet Leaders to Visit

Author: 
Date: 1956-05-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 23rd: TASS reports: On May 22nd, the Swedish Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Solman, the Danish Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Mook, and the Norwegian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Brodland, visited Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov, representing their respective governments to invite the Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Bulganin, and the member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, to visit Sweden, Denmark and Norway in the first half of next year.

Molotov thanked them for the invitations and said that he would pass these invitations on to Bulganin and Khrushchev.

### 瑞典、丹麦和挪威三国邀请苏联领导人访问

1956-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典、丹麦和挪威三国邀请苏联领导人访问
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：瑞典驻苏联大使索尔曼，丹麦驻苏联大使穆克和挪威驻苏联大使布罗德兰德，在五月二十二日拜访了苏联外交部长莫洛托夫，代表他们各自的政府邀请苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫在明年上半年访问瑞典、丹麦和挪威。
    莫洛托夫感谢他们的邀请，并且表示他将把这些邀请转达给布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫。
