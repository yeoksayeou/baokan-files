Title: Strengthen Orchard Management (Picture)
Author: Gao Liang
Date: 1956-05-05
Page: 2

　　Strengthen Orchard Management
　　The Friendly Agricultural Production Cooperative in Jin County, Liaoning Province, a nationally famous apple producing area, has completed spraying its eight hundred apple trees with insecticide powder, sulfur solution, and diesel emulsion. It is estimated that this year's apple harvest will reach 210,000 jin, an increase of approximately 40,000 jin compared to last year.
　　Our reporter Gao Liang, photographed.

### 加强果树管理（图片）
高粮
1956-05-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　加强果树管理
    全国著名苹果产地——辽宁省金县友好农业生产合作社的八百棵苹果树已经完成了喷射杀虫粉、硫黄水和柴油乳剂等工作。预计今年可收二十一万斤苹果，比去年增产四万斤左右。
    本报记者　高粮摄
