Title: Arab Countries Coordinate Arms Production
Author:
Date: 1956-05-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 13th - According to Agence France-Presse in Cairo: The Deputy Minister of Armaments of Egypt, General Ragab, said on May 12th that Egypt has established a joint committee with Saudi Arabia, Syria, and Yemen to coordinate the work of arms production.

Ragab said that Egypt is the only country in the Middle East with factories that possess the raw materials needed for the arms production industry. The steel plant to be built in the near future will enable Egypt to manufacture tanks and heavy armaments.

### 阿拉伯国家统筹军备生产

1956-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯国家统筹军备生产
    新华社13日讯　据法新社开罗消息：埃及军备部副部长拉加布将军五月十二日说，埃及已经同沙特阿拉伯、叙利亚和也门成立了联合委员会，以统筹军备生产的工作。
    拉加布说，中东只有埃及拥有生产军备工业所需要的原料的工厂。在最近将来要兴建的钢铁厂将使埃及能够制造坦克和重型军备。
