Title: People's Daily and Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Newspaper and Magazine Promotion Bureau Joint Announcement
Author:
Date: 1956-05-31
Page: 4

    People's Daily and Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Newspaper and Magazine Promotion Bureau Joint Announcement
    Beginning July 1st, People's Daily will be changed to eight pages daily, totaling two large sheets. The retail price is eight cents per copy, and two yuan and forty cents per month. Readers are requested to subscribe at local post offices.

### 人民日报  邮电部报刊推广局联合启事

1956-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    人民日报  邮电部报刊推广局联合启事
    人民日报自七月一日起改为每日八个版，共两大张。报价零售每份八分，每月二元四角。请读者向各地邮局办理订阅手续。
