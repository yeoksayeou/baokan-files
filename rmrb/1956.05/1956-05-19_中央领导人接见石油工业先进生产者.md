Title: Central Leaders Receive Advanced Producers of the Petroleum Industry
Author:
Date: 1956-05-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Kang Sheng, Dong Biwu, and Deng Xiaoping, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received all the representatives attending the National Petroleum Industry Advanced Producers Representative Conference this afternoon.

### 中央领导人接见石油工业先进生产者

1956-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中央领导人接见石油工业先进生产者
    新华社18日讯　中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、康生、董必武、邓小平，在今天下午接见了出席全国石油工业先进生产者代表会议的全体代表。
