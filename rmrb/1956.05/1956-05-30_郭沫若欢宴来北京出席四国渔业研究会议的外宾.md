Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Foreign Guests Attending the Four-Nation Fisheries Research Conference in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 29th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, held a banquet in Beijing this evening to entertain the foreign delegations who have come to China to attend the Soviet, Korean, Vietnamese, and Chinese four-nation conference on coordinating research in fisheries, oceanography, and limnology, which is about to be held in Beijing.

Invited to the banquet were: the Soviet government delegation, headed by Professor Moiseyev, Deputy Director of the All-Union Research Institute of Marine Fisheries and Oceanography of the Soviet Ministry of Fisheries and ichthyologist; the Korean government delegation, headed by Kim Jae-pil, Vice Minister of the Korean Ministry of Fisheries; and the Vietnamese government delegation, headed by Le Duy Trinh, Deputy Minister of the Vietnamese Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry.

Also invited to the banquet were Pham Binh, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, and Kim Kuk-bo, Commercial Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China.

Guo Moruo spoke at the banquet, first extending a warm welcome on behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences to the representatives of the three countries. He said that, whether in production and construction or in scientific research, international cooperation—especially international cooperation based on the advanced experience of the Soviet Union—is the best way for us to learn from each other and work together. Guo Moruo said that, thanks to the joint hard work of the four countries' representatives during the preparatory period of the conference over the past month, this conference will soon achieve good results. Guo Moruo concluded by wishing the four countries brilliant achievements in fisheries cooperation and joint academic research.

Among those present at the banquet were the Chinese government delegation, headed by Xu Deheng, Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Aquatic Products, who are attending the four-nation fisheries research conference, responsible persons from relevant departments of the Chinese government, and scientists from relevant disciplines in Beijing.

A total of more than fifty people attended the banquet this evening.

### 郭沫若欢宴来北京出席四国渔业研究会议的外宾

1956-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若欢宴来北京出席四国渔业研究会议的外宾
    新华社29日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今晚在北京举行宴会，招待来中国出席即将在北京举行的苏、朝、越、中四国协调渔业、海洋学和湖沼学研究会议的外国代表团。
    应邀出席宴会的是：以苏联渔业部全苏海洋渔业和海洋学研究所副所长、鱼类学家莫伊谢耶夫教授为首的苏联政府代表团，以朝鲜水产省副相金载弼为首的朝鲜政府代表团，以越南农林部副部长黎唯贞为首的越南政府代表团。
    应邀出席宴会的还有越南驻华大使馆临时代办范平、朝鲜驻华大使馆商务参赞金国辅。
    郭沫若在宴会上讲话，他首先代表中国科学院对三国代表表示热烈欢迎。他说，无论在生产建设或科学研究方面，进行国际性合作——特别是在苏联的先进经验的基础上进行国际性合作，是我们相互学习和共同工作的最好方式。郭沫若说，由于四国代表在一个多月来的会议筹备期间共同的辛勤劳动，这次会议很快就要收到很好的成果。郭沫若最后预祝四国在渔业合作和学术性的共同研究上获得辉煌成就。
    出席宴会作陪的有出席四国渔业研究会议的以中国水产部部长许德珩为首的中国政府代表团，中国政府有关部门的负责人，以及在北京的有关学科的科学家。
    今晚参加宴会的共有五十多人。
