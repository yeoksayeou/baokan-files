    More than two hundred workers from the Shanghai Electric Machinery Plant visited the high-speed cutting performance of Soviet advanced producer Breikin. When he finely ground the rotor of a 6,000-kilowatt steam turbine generator, the speed of one fine grinding was more than sixty times higher than that of the workers in the Shanghai Electric Machinery Plant.
　　　Photo by Zuo Jiazhong (Xinhua News Agency draft)
