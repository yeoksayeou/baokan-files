Title: Beijing People's Machine Factory Achieves 1957 Production Level

Author: 
Date: 1956-05-01
Page: 1

    In the labor competition to greet the "May 1st" holiday, the Beijing People's Machine Factory announced on April 29th that the total output value of the factory had reached the 1957 production level. Now the workers are working hard to achieve new production results.
