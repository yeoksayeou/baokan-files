Title: Kim Il-sung to Visit Mongolia at Invitation
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 4th – It was announced here that Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, will lead a government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to Mongolia for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 金日成应邀将访问蒙古

1956-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成应邀将访问蒙古
    新华社平壤4日电　此间宣布，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅应蒙古人民共和国政府的邀请，将率领朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团到蒙古进行友好访问。
