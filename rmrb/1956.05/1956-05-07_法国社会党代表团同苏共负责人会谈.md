Title: French Socialist Party Delegation Holds Talks with Soviet Communist Party Leaders
Author:
Date: 1956-05-07
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 6th: According to TASS, the French Socialist Party delegation, which was invited to visit the Soviet Union, held several hours of talks with the Soviet Communist Party delegation on May 4th and 5th. (Note: The French Socialist Party delegation arrived in Moscow on April 28th at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party).
　　The Soviet side participating in the talks included Khrushchev, Mikoyan, Suslov, and Shepilov, among others. The French Socialist Party delegation was represented by the delegation leader and General Secretary of the French Socialist Party, Guy Mollet, among others.
　　The talks were conducted in a friendly and fully frank atmosphere.

### 法国社会党代表团同苏共负责人会谈

1956-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　法国社会党代表团同苏共负责人会谈
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：五月四日和五日，应邀到苏联访问的法国社会党代表团同苏联共产党代表团进行了数小时的会谈。（按法国社会党代表团是应苏共中央的邀请于四月二十八日到达莫斯科的）。
    苏共方面参加会谈的有赫鲁晓夫、米高扬、苏斯洛夫和谢皮洛夫等人。法国社会党代表团方面是代表团团长、法国社会党总书记比·戈曼等。
    会谈是在友好和充分坦率的气氛中进行的。
