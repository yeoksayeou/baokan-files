Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting; Passes Cultural and Entertainment Tax Regulations
Author:
Date: 1956-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 35th meeting on the morning of the 3rd.

　　After listening to an explanation from Wu Bo, Vice Minister of Finance, the meeting passed the Cultural and Entertainment Tax Regulations. According to the cultural and entertainment tax rates stipulated in these regulations, the tax rates for all seven items, including opera, have been reduced. With the exception of a very small number of cities where the film tax rate was previously low and has now been slightly increased, the film tax rates have been reduced in all 729 towns and cities nationwide where the cultural and entertainment tax is levied. The meeting also approved a temporary two-year exemption from cultural and entertainment tax for seven items, including opera, drama, opera, dance, music, folk art, and acrobatics, after these regulations are promulgated.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议   通过文化娱乐税条例

1956-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议
    通过文化娱乐税条例
    新华社3日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在三日上午举行第三十五次会议。
    会议在听了财政部副部长吴波的说明后，通过了文化娱乐税条例。按照这个条例所订的文化娱乐税的税率，戏曲等七个项目的税率已全部降低；电影税率除极少数的几个城市由于过去偏低，现在略予提高外，其余在全国开征文化娱乐税的729个城镇都已降低了。会议还批准了戏曲、话剧、歌剧、舞蹈、音乐、曲艺、杂技等七项文化娱乐税在本条例公布后，暂时免征两年。
