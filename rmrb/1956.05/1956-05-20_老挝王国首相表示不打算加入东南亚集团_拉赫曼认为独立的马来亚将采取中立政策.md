Title: Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos States He Does Not Intend to Join the Southeast Asia Group; Rahman Believes Independent Malaya Will Adopt a Neutral Policy
Author:
Date: 1956-05-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 18th: London dispatch: When the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, Prince Souvanna Phouma, was asked about whether Laos intends to join the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, he said: "We believe that if the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, especially the principles of non-aggression and non-interference, are strictly adhered to, this issue will not arise, and that would be better." Prince Phouma made these remarks when he received journalist Béchard in Laos, as reported in the "Worker's Daily" on May 17th.

Phouma also said that Laos would welcome trade with China. He said that he watched with "the warmest sympathy" the strict neutral stance recently adopted by Cambodia.

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Kuala Lumpur dispatch: The Chief Minister of the Federation of Malaya, Rahman, said that an independent Malaya would maintain neutrality between the East and the West. He said, "We will work for world peace in the spirit of Bandung and the spirit of Geneva."

Rahman made these remarks when delivering a speech at the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the establishment of the United Malays National Organization on May 17th.

The Chief Minister said, "The policy of independent Malaya will be free from any influence. We will not formulate any domestic or foreign policy because of enticement or threat. We will not conclude any agreement with other countries solely for their benefit. If we conclude agreements with them, it will be for our benefit and for the benefit of world peace."

### 老挝王国首相表示不打算加入东南亚集团  拉赫曼认为独立的马来亚将采取中立政策

1956-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝王国首相表示不打算加入东南亚集团
    拉赫曼认为独立的马来亚将采取中立政策
    新华社布拉格18日电　伦敦消息：老挝王国政府首相富马亲王在回答关于老挝是否打算加入东南亚条约组织问题的时候说：“我们认为，如果五项原则政策，特别是其中的互不侵犯和互不干涉原则得到严格遵守的话，这个问题将不会发生，而这样是比较好的。”富马亲王是在老挝接见新闻记者贝却敌的时候这样说的，五月十七日的“工人日报”刊载了这次谈话。
    富马还说，老挝将欢迎同中国贸易。他说，他怀着“最热烈的同情”注视着最近柬埔寨所采取的严格的中立立场。
    新华社19日讯　吉隆坡消息：马来亚联合邦首席部长拉赫曼说，独立的马来亚将在东西方之间保守中立。他说：“我们将本着万隆精神和日内瓦精神为世界和平而努力。”
    拉赫曼是五月十七日在纪念马来民族联合机构成立十周年的会上发表演说的时候说这番话的。
    这位首席部长说，“独立的马来亚的政策将摆脱任何影响。我们制定任何对内或对外政策都不会是由于受到诱惑或威胁。我们不会仅仅为了其他国家的利益而同它们缔结任何协定。如果我们同它们缔结协定，这将是为了我们的利益和为了世界和平的利益。”
