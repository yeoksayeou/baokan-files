Title: Chen Yi Receives Tibetan Officials and Great Living Buddhas of Various Sects; Tibetan Local Government, Panchen Kampus Assembly Hall, and Others Hold Celebration Banquets
Author:
Date: 1956-05-01
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 29th -- Chen Yi, head of the Central Delegation, and deputy heads Zhang Jingwu, Wang Feng, Huang Qixiang, and Gong Tianmin, received officials of the Tibetan Local Government and the Panchen Kampus Assembly Hall Committee above the third rank, as well as the director and deputy directors of the Qamdo People's Liberation Committee, and great Living Buddhas of various sects in Lhasa today.

Zhang Guohua, acting secretary of the Tibet Work Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Fan Ming, deputy secretary, and Tan Guansan, political commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, were present at the reception.

A forum was held after the reception. Chen Yi first thanked the Dalai Lama, the Panchen Erdeni, and the monastic and secular officials of the Tibetan region for their strong support to the Central Delegation. Then, Chen Yi gave important instructions on the policy of protecting freedom of religious belief and the unity between all ethnic groups, the internal unity of ethnic groups, and issues related to future reforms. Responsible officials from the Tibetan Local Government, the Panchen Kampus Assembly Hall Committee, and the Qamdo People's Liberation Committee also spoke at the forum. Everyone engaged in sincere conversation at the meeting. The meeting proceeded in a cordial and warm atmosphere throughout.

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 29th -- The Tibetan Local Government, the Panchen Kampus Assembly Hall Committee, and the Qamdo People's Liberation Committee jointly held a banquet at the Shölkhang Linka in Lhasa this afternoon to celebrate the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

Sokhang Wangqing Gelek, Kalön of the Tibetan Local Government, Zasa Lhamin Yixi Chuchen of the Panchen Kampus Assembly Hall Committee, and Chaya Lodrö Gyaltsen, deputy director of the Qamdo People's Liberation Committee, all spoke at the banquet, unanimously expressing their determination to work hard for the construction of a prosperous and happy Tibet.

Chen Yi, head of the Central Delegation and Vice Premier of the State Council, said at the banquet that the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region's establishment conference was very successful due to the unity of the two Tibetan leaders, the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni, and the unity of the responsible officials from all aspects of Tibet. This is a major event worthy of historical record. Chen Yi said: Now that we are doing a very good job in unity, we will do even better in the future, and the development prospects of Tibet will be infinitely bright. Finally, at Chen Yi's suggestion, everyone raised their glasses together to toast the great unity.

More than 500 people attended the banquet today, including representatives attending the establishment conference of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and all members of the Central Delegation.

### 陈毅接见西藏官员和各教派大活佛  西藏地方政府班禅堪布会议厅等举行庆祝宴会

1956-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见西藏官员和各教派大活佛
    西藏地方政府班禅堪布会议厅等举行庆祝宴会
    新华社拉萨29日电　中央代表团团长陈毅，副团长张经武、汪锋、黄琪翔、巩天民，今天在拉萨接见了西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会三品以上的官员和昌都地区人民解放委员会的主任、副主任，以及各教派的大活佛。
    接见时在座的有中国共产党西藏工作委员会代书记张国华、副书记范明，中国人民解放军西藏军区政治委员谭冠三。
    接见后举行了座谈会。陈毅首先向达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼和西藏地区的僧俗官员给中央代表团的大力帮助表示感谢。接着，陈毅就保护宗教信仰自由政策和各民族之间的团结、民
  族内部团结以及有关今后改革等问题，作了重要指示。西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会和昌都地区人民解放委员会的负责官员，也在座谈会上讲了话。大家在会上进行了诚恳的交谈。会议始终在亲切热烈的气氛中进行。
    新华社拉萨29日电　西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会和昌都地区人民解放委员会，今天下午在拉萨的雪策林卡联合举行宴会，庆祝西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立。
    西藏地方政府噶伦索康·旺清格勒、班禅堪布会议厅委员会扎萨拉敏·益喜楚臣和昌都地区人民解放委员会副主任察雅·罗登协饶，都在宴会上讲了话，一致表示要为建设繁荣幸福的西藏而努力。
    中央代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅在宴会上说，由于达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼两位西藏领袖团结，由于西藏各方面负责官员的团结，这次西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会开得很顺利。这是历史上值得记载的大事。陈毅说：现在我们把团结工作做得很好，今后我们会做得更好，西藏的发展前途将为无限美好。最后，大家在陈毅的倡议下，一齐举杯为伟大的团结干杯。
    今天参加宴会的有出席西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会的代表和中央代表团的全体人员共五百多人。
