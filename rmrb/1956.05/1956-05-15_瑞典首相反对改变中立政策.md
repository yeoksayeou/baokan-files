Title: Swedish Prime Minister Opposes Changing Neutrality Policy
Author:
Date: 1956-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, May 14th: Swedish Prime Minister and Social Democrat, Erlander, at the opening ceremony of the annual Social Democratic Party congress on May 13th, accused Swedish Conservative Party leader, Hjalmarson, of attempting to bring neutral Sweden into the Western camp. Erlander said that the Conservative Party's recently published new program advocates Sweden joining a Western military alliance through a Scandinavian military alliance plan and through military cooperation with Denmark and Norway (both of which are member states of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization - editor).

Erlander said: "We attach considerable importance to the unity of our country's foreign policy, but we will not hesitate to argue with those who believe we should abandon our neutrality."

The Prime Ministers of Norway and Denmark, and the Prime Minister of Finland all attended the opening ceremony of the Swedish Social Democratic Party congress.

### 瑞典首相反对改变中立政策

1956-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典首相反对改变中立政策
    新华社14日讯　斯德哥尔摩消息：瑞典首相，社会民主党人埃兰德五月十三日在社会民主党每年举行的代表大会开幕式上，指责瑞典保守党领袖希尔马尔逊企图使中立的瑞典加入西方阵营。埃兰德说，保守党最近发表的新纲领主张瑞典通过一项斯堪的纳维亚国家的军事联盟计划和通过同丹麦和挪威（这两个国家是北大西洋公约组织成员国——编者）的军事合作，使瑞典加入西方军事联盟。
    埃兰德说：“我们相当重视我国外交政策中的团结一致，但是我们毫不犹豫地要同那些认为我们应该放弃我们的中立的人们争辩。”
    挪威、丹麦的首相和芬兰的总理都参加了瑞典社会民主党这次代表大会的开幕式。
