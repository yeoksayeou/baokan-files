Introduction to the Chinese Peking Opera Troupe Visiting Japan

    During the visit and performance of the Japanese Kabuki troupe in China in October 1955, Bai Shi Fan, representative of the Asahi Shimbun, Matsuo Kunizo, representative of the Grand Kabuki Troupe of Japan, Narasaki Tomio, representative of Matsumoto Jiichiro, president of the Japan-China Friendship Association, and Nochi Shumi, permanent director of the Japan-China Friendship Association, along with Yang Hansheng, representative of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, further discussed the visit and performance of the Chinese Peking Opera Troupe in Japan, in accordance with the spirit of the "Agreement between the Japan-China Friendship Association, the representative of the Grand Kabuki Troupe of Japan, and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries on Inviting the Peking Opera Troupe of the People's Republic of China to Visit and Perform in Japan" signed in August 1955, and reached a new agreement. According to the provisions of this agreement, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, at the invitation of the Asahi Shimbun, recently organized the Chinese Peking Opera Troupe Visiting Japan.

    The Chinese Peking Opera Troupe Visiting Japan consists of eighty-six people. Head of the delegation: Mei Lanfang (President of the China Peking Opera Theatre, President of the Chinese Academy of Traditional Opera, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, Deputy to the National People's Congress); First Deputy Head and General Director: Ouyang Yuqian (President of the Central Academy of Drama, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, Deputy to the National People's Congress); Deputy Heads: Ma Shaobo, concurrently Secretary-General (Vice President of the China Peking Opera Theatre, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Traditional Opera, Standing Director of the Chinese Dramatists Association), Liu Jia (Director of the Chinese Dramatists Association), Sun Pinghua (Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries). Deputy Secretary-General: Ouyang Shanzun (Vice President of the Beijing People's Art Theatre, Standing Director of the Chinese Dramatists Association). Stage Supervisor: Yuan Guanghe (Director of the Performance Department of the China Peking Opera Theatre). The delegation's famous actors include: Li Shaochun, Yuan Shihai, Li Hezeng, Jiang Miaoxiang, etc. (Attached pictures)

### 中国访日京剧代表团介绍

1956-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

    中国访日京剧代表团介绍
    1955年10月日本歌舞伎剧团在中国访问演出期间，日本朝日新闻社代表白石凡、日本大歌舞伎一座代表松尾国三、日本中国友好协会会长松本治一郎代表楢崎富男、日本中国友好协会常务理事能智修弥与中国人民对外文化协会代表阳翰笙，根据1955年8月签订的“日本中国友好协会、日本大歌舞伎一座代表、中国人民对外文化协会关于邀请中华人民共和国京剧团赴日本访问演出的议定书”的精神，对于中国京剧团赴日本访问演出事作了进一步的商谈，并达成了新的协议。根据这一协议的规定，中国人民对外文化协会应日本朝日新闻社的邀请，在最近组织了中国访日京剧代表团。
    中国访日京剧代表团由八十六人组成。团长：梅兰芳（中国京剧院院长、中国戏曲研究院院长、中国戏剧家协会副主席、全国人民代表大会代表）；第一副团长兼总导演：欧阳予倩（中央戏剧学院院长、中国戏剧家协会副主席、全国人民代表大会代表）；副团长：马少波兼秘书长（中国京剧院副院长、中国戏曲研究院副院长、中国戏剧家协会常务理事），刘佳（中国戏剧家协会理事），孙平化（中国人民对外文化协会联络部副主任）。副秘书长：欧阳山尊（北京人民艺术剧院副院长、中国戏剧家协会常务理事）。舞台监督：袁广和（中国京剧院演出处处长）。代表团的著名演员有：李少春、袁世海、李和曾、姜妙香等。（附图片）
    中国访日京剧代表团团长梅兰芳在赴日前接到不少日本朋友给他的欢迎信。
             本报记者　苍石、王志渊摄
