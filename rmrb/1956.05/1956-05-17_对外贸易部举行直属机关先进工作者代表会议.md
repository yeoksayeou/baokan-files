Title: Ministry of Foreign Trade Holds Meeting of Representatives of Advanced Workers from Directly Subordinate Organizations
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-17
Page: 2

    Ministry of Foreign Trade Holds Meeting of Representatives of Advanced Workers from Directly Subordinate Organizations
    The meeting of representatives of advanced workers from directly subordinate organizations of the Ministry of Foreign Trade opened on the 15th.
    Attending this meeting were 172 advanced workers and representatives of advanced units from the Ministry of Foreign Trade and its affiliated import and export specialized general corporations. The 22 representatives from the foreign trade system who attended the National Conference of Advanced Producers also attended the meeting.
    Minister of Foreign Trade, Ye Jizhuang, delivered a report at the meeting on the 15th. He said that China now has trade relations with more than 60 countries and regions, and has signed trade agreements with the governments of 21 of these countries. The total import and export volume in 1955 increased by more than one and a half times compared to 1950. Ye Jizhuang requested the advanced workers attending the meeting to work together with all employees in the foreign trade system to strive to complete and overfulfill the foreign trade plans for this year and next year more, faster, better, and more economically, and to improve the level of foreign trade work.
    The meeting is scheduled to last for six days, and the representatives will exchange various advanced experiences. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 对外贸易部举行直属机关先进工作者代表会议

1956-05-17
第2版()
专栏：

    对外贸易部举行直属机关先进工作者代表会议
    对外贸易部直属机关先进工作者代表会议十五日开幕。
    出席这次会议的有对外贸易部和所属各进出口专业总公司的172名先进工作者和先进单位的代表，对外贸易系统出席全国先进生产者代表会议的二十二名代表也出席了会议。
    对外贸易部部长叶季壮在十五日的会议上作了报告。他说，中国现在已经同六十多个国家和地区进行了贸易往来，并且同其中二十一个国家的政府签订了贸易协定，1955年的进出口总额比1950年增加了一倍半以上。叶季壮要求出席会议的先进工作者同对外贸易系统全体职工一道，争取更多、更快、更好、更省地提前完成和超额完成今明两年的对外贸易计划，并提高对外贸易工作的水平。
    会议预定开六天，代表们将交流各种先进经验。　（据新华社讯）
