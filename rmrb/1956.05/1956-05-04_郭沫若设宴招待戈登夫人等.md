Title: Guo Moruo Hosts a Banquet for Mrs. Gordon and Others
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd—Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet today in Beijing for Mrs. Eugénie Cotton of France, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, Mrs. Monica Felton of England, Standing Committee Member, and Mrs. Margarita de Ponce of Argentina, Council Member, who are in Beijing to attend the Council meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation.

Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, were among those present.

### 郭沫若设宴招待戈登夫人等

1956-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若设宴招待戈登夫人等
    据新华社3日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天设宴招待来北京出席国际民主妇女联合会理事会会议的世界和平理事会副主席法国欧仁尼·戈登夫人、常务委员英国蒙尼卡·费尔顿夫人、理事阿根廷玛格丽塔·德庞赛夫人。
    出席作陪的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平等人。
