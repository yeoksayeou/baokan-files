Title: Albanian Ambassador to China, Nasse, Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-05-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Nasse, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Albania to China, has been recalled and left Beijing with his wife on the morning of the 16th to return to his country. He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department, went to the airport to see him off. Personnel from the Albanian Embassy in China and diplomatic envoys and embassy personnel from various countries in China also went to see him off.

### 阿驻华大使纳赛回国

1956-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    阿驻华大使纳赛回国
    新华社16日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻中国首任特命全权大使纳赛已奉调在十六日晨同他的夫人离开北京回国。前往机场送行的有外交部部长助理何伟、礼宾司司长柯华。阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使馆人员和各国驻华使节及使馆人员也前往送行。
