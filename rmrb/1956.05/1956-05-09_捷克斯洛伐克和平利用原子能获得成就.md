Title: Achievements of Czechoslovakia in the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1956-05-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 8th. Czechoslovakia is actively carrying out various works for the peaceful utilization of atomic energy.

　　The construction of the country's first atomic reactor is proceeding according to plan. The reactor, which is about to be built, will produce a sufficient number of artificial radioactive isotopes for Czechoslovakia's industrial, medical, and scientific research fields. Staff at the Hradec Králové Boiler Research Institute have manufactured equipment that transfers the heat generated by the reactor to the steam boiler. This equipment uses a mixture of liquid lead and bismuth for heat transfer, which is simpler and cheaper than equipment using heavy water. This equipment is now in trial operation.

　　The scope of using atomic energy in industry, agriculture, and medicine is becoming increasingly wide in Czechoslovakia. At present, about six thousand enterprises across the country are using radioactive isotopes supplied by the Soviet Union. In 1955, the First Gottwald Machinery Factory in Brno used radioactive isotopes to inspect two thousand products. Radioactive materials have also been used in canned food factories for cold sterilization of food, to increase the sugar content and yield per unit area of ​​sugar beets, to promote the germination and ripening of grains, peas, and cucumbers, and to delay potato germination for storage. In the treatment of malignant tumors, thyroid diseases, etc., the Endocrinology Research Institute in Prague alone has treated two thousand patients with radioactive iodine in the past five years. This institute has also used radioactive phosphorus to save the lives of patients suffering from polycythemia vera.

　　Czechoslovakia is training and cultivating working cadres in nuclear physics. Charles University in Prague added the Department of Nuclear and Technical Physics last autumn, with one hundred and eighty students. The newly established Prague Nuclear Technology Specialized School is training secondary technical talents in this field.

### 捷克斯洛伐克和平利用原子能获得成就

1956-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷克斯洛伐克和平利用原子能获得成就
    据新华社布拉格8日电  捷克斯洛伐克正在积极地开展和平利用原子能的各种工作。
    全国第一座原子反应堆的建设工程正在按照计划进行中。这个即将建成的反应堆将为捷克斯洛伐克的工业、医疗和科学研究等方面生产出足够数量的人造放射性同位素。赫拉岱茨克拉罗夫锅炉研究所的工作人员已经制造好一种把反应堆发生的热能传送到蒸气锅的设备。这种设备利用铅和铋的混合液体传热，比利用重水的设备简便和便宜得多。现在这种设备已经进行试运转。
    捷克斯洛伐克在工业、农业、医疗等方面利用原子能的范围愈来愈广泛。目前全国约有六千个企业使用苏联供给的放射性同位素。1955年，布尔诺的第一哥特瓦尔德机械工厂曾经利用放射性同位素检验了两千件产品。放射性物质还被用于罐头食品工厂对食物进行冷杀菌，用来提高甜菜的含糖成分和单位面积产量，促使谷物、豌豆和黄瓜发芽和成熟，延迟土豆发芽以便于储存等。在治疗恶性肿瘤甲状腺症等疾病方面，仅仅布拉格的内分泌研究所在过去五年中就曾经利用放射性碘治疗了两千个病人。这个研究所还使用放射性磷挽救了患红血球过多症的病人的生命。
    捷克斯洛伐克正在训练和培养核子物理科学的工作干部。布拉格卡罗瓦大学去年秋天增设了核子和技术物理系，有学生一百八十人。新建立的布拉格核子技术专门学校正在培养这方面的中等技术人材。
