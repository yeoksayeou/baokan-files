Title: "People's Daily" Carries Article Pointing Out Significant Achievements of the Belgrade Declaration Over the Past Year
Author:
Date: 1956-05-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th: The "Pravda" published an article on May 27th commemorating the first anniversary of the signing of the Soviet-Yugoslav Declaration, stating that the talks between the Soviet and Yugoslav government delegations in Belgrade a year ago and the Soviet-Yugoslav Declaration signed as a result of the talks brought about fundamental changes in the relations between the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia. It laid, restored, and developed a solid foundation for the brotherly friendship and comprehensive cooperation between the two peoples, which had been temporarily damaged in 1948. Yugoslav President Tito's upcoming visit to the Soviet Union will be a new step on the road to the smooth development of friendship between the Soviet and Yugoslav peoples.
The author of this article, Danilov, wrote that the Soviet-Yugoslav Declaration is an embodiment of Lenin's principles of peaceful coexistence, equality and mutual respect between all nations, large and small, and non-interference in each other's internal affairs. Although only a year has passed since the signing of this declaration, the achievements in implementing the declaration are significant. The implementation of the declaration and the restoration of cooperation between the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia in the struggle for peace, democracy, and socialism on the basis of the declaration, promoted the easing of international tensions and the consolidation of peace-loving forces. The declaration played an important role in restoring economic ties and scientific and technological cooperation between the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia.
Danilov concluded by writing that the effective implementation of the Soviet-Yugoslav Declaration has created favorable conditions for further consolidating the fraternal and friendly cooperation between the two peoples in all aspects of political, economic, and cultural life, and for expanding contacts and cooperation in accordance with the Party's line. Life proves that the friendship between the Soviet and Yugoslav peoples is an important condition for consolidating the forces of peace, democracy, and socialism, and is an important factor in easing international tensions.

### “真理报”载文指出  苏南宣言一年来成果很大

1956-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    “真理报”载文指出
    苏南宣言一年来成果很大
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：五月二十七日，“真理报”登载了一篇纪念苏南宣言签订一周年的文章说，一年前苏联和南斯拉夫两国政府代表团在贝尔格莱德进行的会谈和就会谈结果签署的苏南宣言，在苏联和南斯拉夫之间的关系上引起根本的变化。它奠定、恢复和发展了在1948年暂时被破坏的两国人民兄弟友谊和全面合作的巩固基础。南斯拉夫总统铁托的即将访问苏联，将是苏联和南斯拉夫人民的友谊顺利发展的道路上的新的一步。
    这篇文章的作者达尼洛夫写道，苏南宣言是列宁的和平共处、大小民族一律平等和互相尊重、互不干涉内政的原则的体现。这个宣言签署后虽然只不过一年，然而实现宣言的成果是很大的。实现宣言和在宣言的基础上恢复苏联和南斯拉夫在争取和平、民主和社会主义斗争中的合作，促进了国际紧张局势的和缓和爱好和平力量的巩固。宣言在恢复苏联和南斯拉夫之间的经济联系和科学技术合作上起了重要的作用。
    达尼洛夫最后写道，有效地执行苏南宣言，为进一步巩固两国人民的政治、经济和文化生活各方面的兄弟友好合作、为根据党的路线扩大接触和合作创造了良好的条件。生活证明，苏南两国人民的友谊是巩固和平、民主和社会主义力量的重要条件，是缓和国际紧张局势的重要因素。
