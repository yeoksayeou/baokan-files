Title: Chen Yi Holds Banquet to Entertain People from All Walks of Life in Xigazê
Author:
Date: 1956-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xigazê, May 18th: Chen Yi, head of the Central Delegation and Vice Premier of the State Council, held a grand banquet on the afternoon of the 17th at the Linka in Deqing Pozhang, west of Xigazê, to entertain all the monastic and secular officials of the Panchen Kanpo Lija Office, the officials of Xigazê Dzong, responsible cadres and officers of various organs and troops in Xigazê, and heads of various mass organizations in Xigazê.

	The Panchen Erdeni also attended the banquet.

Xinhua News Agency, Xigazê, May 18th: Chen Yi, head of the Central Delegation, Huang Qixiang, deputy head, and some members of the delegation received the monastic and secular officials of the Panchen Kanpo Lija Office who were of the fourth rank or above on the morning of the 18th. Present at the reception were Pingcuo Wangjie, member of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, Liang Xuanxian, secretary of the Xigazê Sub-Working Committee, and Dorje Phuntsok Rabgye, Kalon of the Tibetan local government.

	During the reception, Head of Delegation Chen Yi gave important instructions on further strengthening the unity between the Tibetan people and all ethnic groups in the motherland, the internal unity of Tibet, and the future construction and development of Tibet.

### 陈毅举行宴会招待日喀则各界人士

1956-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅举行宴会招待日喀则各界人士
    新华社日喀则18日电　中央代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅，十七日下午在日喀则西郊德庆颇章的林卡里举行盛大宴会，招待班禅堪布会议厅委员会全体僧俗官员，日喀则宗的官员，日喀则各机关、部队的负责干部和军官以及日喀则各群众团体负责人。
    班禅额尔德尼也出席了这个宴会。
    新华社日喀则18日电　中央代表团团长陈毅、副团长黄琪翔和部分团员十八日上午接见了班禅堪布会议厅委员会四品以上的僧俗官员。接见时在座的有中共西藏工委委员平错旺阶、日喀则分工委书记梁选贤、西藏地方政府噶伦朵喀·彭错饶杰。
    陈毅团长在接见时，就进一步加强藏族同祖国各民族之间的团结和西藏内部的团结问题，以及今后建设和发展西藏的问题作了重要指示。
