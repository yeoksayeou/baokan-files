Title: Hungary to Increase Basic Wages for Workers Starting June 1st
Author:
Date: 1956-05-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, May 25th (TASS): The Hungarian Council of Ministers has decided that, starting June 1st, the basic wage for industrial workers should be at least 650 forints.
    Following a suggestion from the Central Council of Hungarian Trade Unions, the Council of Ministers also decided to shorten the work week in industrial sectors harmful to health, starting June 1st.

### 匈牙利六月一日起提高工人的基本工资

1956-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利六月一日起提高工人的基本工资
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯二十五日讯：匈牙利部长会议决定：从六月一日起，工业工人的基本工资至少应为六百五十福林。
    根据匈牙利工会中央理事会的建议，部长会议还决定从六月一日起缩短有害健康的工业部门的工作周。
