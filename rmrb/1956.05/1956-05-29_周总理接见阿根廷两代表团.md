Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Two Argentine Delegations
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received the Argentine Medical Delegation headed by Algens and the Argentine Cultural Delegation headed by Amerigo Nunciata on the afternoon of May 28th. Present at the reception were Fu Lianzhang, Director of the Chinese Medical Association, Fang Shishan, Deputy Director, and Zhao Yimin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 周总理接见阿根廷两代表团

1956-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见阿根廷两代表团
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在二十八日下午接见了以阿尔根斯为首的阿根廷医学代表团和以阿美利哥·努恩西亚达为首的阿根廷文化代表团。接见的时候，在座的有中华医学会理事长傅连暲、副理事长方石珊，中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏等人。
