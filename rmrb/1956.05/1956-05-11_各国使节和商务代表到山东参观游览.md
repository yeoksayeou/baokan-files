Title: Envoys and Business Representatives from Various Countries Visit Shandong
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th.
    Envoys, business representatives, and their wives from various countries stationed in China visited Jinan, Qufu, and Qingdao in Shandong Province from May 2nd to 8th at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Those who participated included the ambassadors of Burma, Romania, Mongolia, Hungary, Finland, Yugoslavia, India, the German Democratic Republic, Norway, Afghanistan, and Denmark; the minister of Switzerland; the chargés d'affaires of the Netherlands and the United Kingdom; the acting chargés d'affaires of Indonesia, Poland, and Bulgaria; the Egyptian business representative; the Soviet counselor; their wives; and the wives of the ambassadors of Bulgaria and Czechoslovakia. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Yuan Zhongxian and his wife, as well as Director of the Protocol Department Ke Hua and others accompanied them.
    In Jinan, they visited the museum, the handicraft exhibition, and the Dongfanghong Agricultural Production Cooperative, and toured the scenic spots; in Qufu, they visited the Confucius Temple and the Confucius Forest; in Qingdao, they visited factories and also enjoyed the sea view on a Chinese destroyer.

### 各国使节和商务代表到山东参观游览

1956-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    各国使节和商务代表到山东参观游览
    新华社10日讯  各国驻华使节、商务代表和他们的夫人应中国外交部的邀请，从五月二日到八日到山东省济南、曲阜和青岛进行了参观游览。参加的有缅甸、罗马尼亚、蒙古、匈牙利、芬兰、南斯拉夫、印度、德意志民主共和国、挪威、阿富汗、丹麦的大使，瑞士的公使，荷兰和英国的代办，印度尼西亚、波兰、保加利亚的临时代办，埃及商务代表，苏联参赞，他们的夫人，以及保加利亚和捷克斯洛伐克大使的夫人。外交部副部长袁仲贤和夫人及礼宾司司长柯华等陪同他们前往。
    他们在济南参观了博物馆、手工业展览会和东方红农业生产合作社，游览了名胜；在曲阜参观了孔庙和孔林；在青岛参观了工厂，还乘中国的驱逐舰观赏了海上的景色。
