Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Return to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-05-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th April. After visiting Britain, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Bulganin and member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Khrushchev, along with their entourage, returned to Moscow by plane at 2:50 PM on April 30th.

More than 10,000 people, including members and alternate members of the Presidium of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Soviet ministers, heads of social organizations, and representatives from all walks of life in Moscow, were present at the Central Airport to welcome them.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union also came to the airport to welcome them.

Bulganin and Khrushchev accepted flowers presented by children after disembarking from the plane.

Worker Ryabov from the "Hammer and Sickle" factory and other personnel delivered speeches on behalf of the people of Moscow at a mass meeting to welcome Bulganin and Khrushchev.

Bulganin and Khrushchev then spoke at the meeting, pointing out the important political and international significance of the visit.

The mass meeting unanimously passed a resolution expressing warm approval of the results of the visit and talks.

Many Muscovites gathered on Gorky Street, which was adorned in festive attire, to welcome the representatives of the Soviet people returning from Britain.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫回到莫斯科

1956-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫回到莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科30日电　苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫和随从人员在访问英国之后，四月三十日下午两点五十分乘飞机回到莫斯科。
    到中央机场欢迎的有苏联共产党主席团委员和候补委员、苏联各部长、各社会团体负责人以及莫斯科各界代表共一万多人。
    各国驻苏联外交使节也到机场欢迎。
    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫下飞机后，接受了少年儿童们的献花。
    “镰刀与锤子”工厂工人里亚波夫和其他人员代表莫斯科人在群众会上致词，欢迎布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫。
    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在会上相继讲了话。他们指出了这次访问的重要政治意义和国际意义。
    群众大会最后一致通过决议，表示热烈赞同这次访问和会谈的结果。
    在披着节日盛装的高尔基大街上聚集着许多莫斯科人，欢迎从英国回来的苏联人民的代表。
