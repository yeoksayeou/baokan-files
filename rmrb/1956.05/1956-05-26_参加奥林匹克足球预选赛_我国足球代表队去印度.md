Title: To Participate in the Olympic Football Qualifying Tournament, Our National Football Team Goes to India
Author:
Date: 1956-05-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, May 25th: The first group of eight members of the Chinese football delegation participating in the 16th Olympic Games football qualifying tournament, led by Huang Zhong, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, left Kunming by plane this morning, May 25th, for India via Rangoon. The second group of eighteen people will depart on the 26th.

　　The Chinese football delegation will arrive in Calcutta in time to participate in the two football qualifying matches between China and the Philippines for the 16th Olympic Games, scheduled for June 3rd and June 5th.

　　The Chinese football delegation, at the invitation of the All India Football Federation, also plans to hold friendly matches with Indian football teams in India.

### 参加奥林匹克足球预选赛  我国足球代表队去印度

1956-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    参加奥林匹克足球预选赛
    我国足球代表队去印度
    新华社昆明25日电　参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会足球预选赛的中国足球代表队第一批人员八人，二十五日早晨在领队、中华全国体育总会副秘书长黄中率领下，坐飞机离开昆明经仰光去印度。第二批十八人将在二十六日启程。
    中国足球代表队将及时赶到加尔各达，参加规定在六月三日和六月五日举行的中国同菲律宾足球队在第十六届奥林匹克运动会以前的两场足球预选赛。
    中国足球代表队已经应全印足球联合会的邀请，还准备在印度同印度的足球队举行友谊比赛。
