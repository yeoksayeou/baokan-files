Title: Kim Il-sung Receives the Delegation of the CPC Central Committee
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 4th: Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, received Nie Rongzhen, head of the delegation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party attending the Third Congress of the Korean Workers' Party, and members Wang Congwu and Qiao Xiaoguang, on the morning of May 4th.

Present at the reception were Choi Yong-kun, Kim Il, and Pak Kum-chol, members of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party.

### 金日成接见中共中央代表团

1956-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成接见中共中央代表团
    新华社平壤4日电　朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成五月四日上午接见了参加朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会的中国共产党中央委员会代表团团长聂荣臻、团员王从吾和乔晓光。
    接见时在座的有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会常务委员会委员崔庸健、金一、朴金喆。
