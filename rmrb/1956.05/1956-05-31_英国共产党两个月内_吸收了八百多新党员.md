Title: Communist Party of Great Britain Absorbs More Than Eight Hundred New Members in Two Months
Author:
Date: 1956-05-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, May 30th -- According to a report in London's "Daily Worker," the Central Organization Department of the Communist Party of Great Britain announced on May 27th that the Communist Party of Great Britain had increased its membership by more than eight hundred in March and April. Five hundred and fifty-three people were admitted in March and two hundred and seventy-seven in April.
     An article published in the "Daily Worker" pointed out that the leading bodies of the Communist Party of Great Britain believe that this is a major step towards the goal of 50,000 members stipulated by the Party's Twenty-Fourth Congress.

### 英国共产党两个月内  吸收了八百多新党员

1956-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    英国共产党两个月内
    吸收了八百多新党员
    新华社30日讯　据伦敦“工人日报”报道，英国共产党中央组织部五月二十七日宣布，在三月份和四月份，英国共产党增加了八百多个党员。三月份有五百五十三人被吸收，四月份有二百七十七人。
     “工人日报”发表的一篇文章指出，英国共产党的领导机构认为，这是走向党的第二十四次代表大会所规定的五万党员这个目标的一个重大的进展。
