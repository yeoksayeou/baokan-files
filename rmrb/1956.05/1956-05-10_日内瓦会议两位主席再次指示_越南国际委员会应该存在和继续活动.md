Title: Geneva Conference Co-Chairmen Reiterate Instructions: International Commission for Vietnam Should Exist and Continue Activities
Author:
Date: 1956-05-10
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 8th – The two co-chairmen of the 1954 Geneva Conference have reiterated instructions to the International Commission for Vietnam, stating that even after April 28th, the International Commission should continue to exist and carry out its normal activities. April 28th is the date when the French Expeditionary Corps began its complete withdrawal from South Vietnam.

　　In a letter to the Chairman of the International Commission on April 27th, the two chairmen also stated that they are continuing to study the implementation of the Geneva Accords in Vietnam.

### 日内瓦会议两位主席再次指示  越南国际委员会应该存在和继续活动

1956-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    日内瓦会议两位主席再次指示
　　越南国际委员会应该存在和继续活动
    新华社河内8日电  1954年日内瓦会议两位主席再次指示越南国际委员会，即使在四月二十八日以后，国际委员会仍然应该存在和继续其正常活动。四月二十八日是法国远征军开始完全撤离越南南方的日子。
    这两位主席在四月二十七日给国际委员会主席的信中还通知说，两位主席正在继续研究日内瓦协定在越南履行的情况。
