Title: Panchen Erdeni Leaves Lhasa to Return to Xigaze
Author:
Date: 1956-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 7th: Panchen Erdeni left Lhasa at noon today to return to Xigaze. This morning, Chen Yi, head of the Central Delegation and Vice Premier of the State Council, presented hadas to Panchen Erdeni at the Jokhang Temple to say goodbye. This morning, the Dalai Lama specially sent representatives to the Jokhang Temple to present gifts to Panchen Erdeni as a farewell. The Dalai Lama's tutor, the Living Buddha Lincang, the deputy tutor, the Living Buddha Chijiang, as well as the Kalons of the Tibetan local government and the representatives of the People's Liberation Committee of the Qamdo area, also went to the Jokhang Temple to bid farewell.

The ceremony to bid farewell to Panchen Erdeni was held in the western suburbs of Lhasa today.

When Panchen Erdeni arrived at the farewell location in a yellow car, he accepted flowers from children and then entered the farewell tent of the CPC Tibet Working Committee and the Tibet Military Region. Here, Zhang Jingwu and Wang Feng, deputy heads of the Central Delegation, and the deputy heads of various ethnic groups, Zhang Guohua, acting secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, Fan Ming, deputy secretary, and Lieutenant General Tan Guansan of the Tibet Military Region, exchanged hadas with Panchen Erdeni. Wang Feng delivered a farewell speech on behalf of Chen Yi and the delegation members, and Zhang Guohua delivered a farewell speech on behalf of the working personnel stationed in Tibet and the People's Liberation Army. Then, Panchen Erdeni delivered a reply.

In the farewell tent specially set up by the Tibetan local government, Kalon Sokang Wangqing Geleg, Dorje Phuntsok Rabgye, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Acting Kalon Gakang Losang Rinchen, Assistant Kalon Xianka Jumay Dorje, and other monks and lay officials paid religious homage to Panchen Erdeni and presented him with hadas. Panchen Erdeni touched their heads one by one.

### 班禅额尔德尼离拉萨回日喀则

1956-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　班禅额尔德尼离拉萨回日喀则
    据新华社拉萨7日电  班禅额尔德尼在今天中午离拉萨返日喀则。今天上午，中央代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅到大昭寺和班禅额尔德尼互赠哈达道别。达赖喇嘛在今天早上特派代表到大昭寺，向班禅额尔德尼赠礼品送别。达赖喇嘛的经师林仓活佛、副经师赤江活佛以及西藏地方政府的噶伦和昌都地区人民解放委员会的代表，也都先后到大昭寺去送别。
    今天欢送班禅额尔德尼的仪式在拉萨西郊举行。
    班禅额尔德尼乘坐黄色小汽车到达欢送地点的时候，接受了少年儿童献花，然后进入中共西藏工委和西藏军区的欢送帐篷。在这里，中央代表团副团长张经武、汪锋和各民族副团长，中共西藏工委代书记张国华、副书记范明，西藏军区谭冠三中将都和班禅额尔德尼互送了哈达。汪锋代表陈毅和代表团人员，张国华代表进藏的工作人员和人民解放军，分别致欢送词。接着，班禅额尔德尼致答词。
    在西藏地方政府特设的欢送的帐篷里，噶伦索康·旺清格勒、朵喀·彭措饶杰、阿沛·阿旺晋美、代理噶伦噶章·洛桑日增、助理噶伦先喀·居美多杰和其他僧俗官员都以宗教仪式朝拜班禅额尔德尼，并向他献哈达。班禅额尔德尼为他们一一摸了顶。
