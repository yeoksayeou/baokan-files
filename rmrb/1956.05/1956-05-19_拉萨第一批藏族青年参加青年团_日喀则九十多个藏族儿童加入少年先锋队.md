Title: First Group of Tibetan Youths in Lhasa Join the Communist Youth League; Over Ninety Tibetan Children in Rikaze Join the Young Pioneers

Author:
Date: 1956-05-19
Page: 3

    The Lhasa Primary School branch of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League admitted its first group of Tibetan and Mongolian youths into the League on the 17th.
    The eighteen young people who were admitted this time included some students who were good at studying, good at uniting, and loved labor, and some Tibetan young teachers who were enthusiastic about teaching. They were all eager to join the League. Some of them wrote applications to the League branch last year and studied the League Constitution and basic knowledge of the League.
    As of the 17th, more than 100 Tibetan and Hui youth students and teachers from Lhasa Primary School and social education classes alone have applied to join the League, and more than 400 people have applied to join the Young Pioneers. (Xinhua News Agency)
    Ninety-two Tibetan children in Rikaze City joined the Chinese Young Pioneers. On the afternoon of the 15th, they held an oath-taking ceremony in the Linka of Panchen Erdeni's villa, Deqing Pozhang. This is the second group of members developed by Rikaze Primary School since it established the Young Pioneers organization on April 8 this year, and it is also the second group of children in the Tibetan region to wear red scarves.
    Panchen Erdeni and the head of the Central Delegation and Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, deputy heads Zhang Jingwu, Wang Feng, Huang Qixiang, and Sangjiyuexi all came to the Linka to congratulate the children. Luo Yi, secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Zhang Guohua, acting secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China, Bai Yunfeng, secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Youth League, Galun Duoga·Pengcuo Raojie of the Tibet Local Government, Zhan Dong·Jijinmei, director of the Panchen Kanpo Conference Hall Committee, and Liang Xuanxian, secretary of the Rikaze Sub-Committee of the Communist Party of China, also came to the Linka to congratulate the children. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 拉萨第一批藏族青年参加青年团  日喀则九十多个藏族儿童加入少年先锋队

1956-05-19
第3版()
专栏：

    拉萨第一批藏族青年参加青年团
    日喀则九十多个藏族儿童加入少年先锋队
    中国新民主主义青年团拉萨小学支部，十七日通过第一批藏族、蒙古族青年入团。
    这次通过入团的十八个青年人，有些是学习好、团结好和爱劳动的学生，有些是热心教学的藏族青年教师。他们都迫切要求入团，有的去年就向团支部写了入团申请书，并且学习了团章和团的基本知识。
    到十七日为止，仅拉萨小学和社会教育班，已经有一百多藏、回族青年学生和教师申请入团，有四百多人申请加入少年先锋队。（新华社）
    日喀则市九十二名藏族儿童加入了中国少年先锋队。十五日下午，他们在班禅额尔德尼的别墅——德庆颇章的林卡里举行了入队宣誓仪式。这是日喀则小学在今年四月八日建立了少先队组织以后，发展的第二批队员，也是西藏地区第二批带上红领巾的儿童。
    班禅额尔德尼和中央代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅，副团长张经武、汪锋、黄琪翔、桑吉悦希都到林卡里来向孩子们祝贺。来向孩子们贺喜的还有青年团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅，中共西藏工作委员会代书记张国华，青年团西藏工委书记白云峰，西藏地方政府噶伦朵喀·彭措饶杰，班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任詹东·计晋美，中共日喀则分工委书记梁选贤等。　　　（据新华社讯）
