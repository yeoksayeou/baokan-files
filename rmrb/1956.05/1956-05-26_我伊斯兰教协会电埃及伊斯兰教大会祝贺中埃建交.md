Title: China Islamic Association Sends Congratulations to Egyptian Islamic Congress on Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-05-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 25th. Mohammad Ali Zhang Yuzhen, Secretary-General of the China Islamic Association, today sent a telegram to Anwar Sadat, Secretary-General of the Egyptian Islamic Congress, congratulating them on the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Egypt. The telegram said:
    "We are very happy to hear the news of the establishment of formal diplomatic relations and the exchange of diplomatic representatives between China and Egypt. On this memorable day, the Muslims of China extend their heartfelt congratulations to the Muslims of Egypt and wish our Muslim brothers in both countries to jointly contribute to the further development and consolidation of the traditional friendship between China and Egypt."

### 我伊斯兰教协会电埃及伊斯兰教大会祝贺中埃建交

1956-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我伊斯兰教协会电埃及伊斯兰教大会祝贺中埃建交
    新华社25日讯　中国伊斯兰教协会秘书长穆罕默德·阿里·张玉珍今天打电报给埃及伊斯兰教大会秘书长安瓦尔·萨达特，祝贺中埃建交。电文说：
    “我们很高兴地听到中埃两国建立正式外交关系和互派外交代表的消息。在这值得纪念的日子里，中国穆斯林谨向埃及穆斯林致以衷心的祝贺，并愿我们两国穆斯林兄弟为进一步发展和巩固中埃两国的传统友谊而共同作出贡献。”
