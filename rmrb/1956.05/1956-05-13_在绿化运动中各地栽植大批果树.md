Title: Large Numbers of Fruit Trees Planted in Greenification Campaign
Author:
Date: 1956-05-13
Page: 2

In the tree-planting campaign to green barren mountains and wasteland, large numbers of fruit trees have been planted in various regions. According to incomplete statistics from the five provinces of Shaanxi, Sichuan, Zhejiang, Fujian, and Guangdong, over three million citrus trees will be planted this spring. In Fujian Province this spring, twenty-one new fruit tree nurseries have been established in Zhangpu, Nanqing, Putian, Changle, and other places. Moreover, a number of small nurseries have been established in agricultural production cooperatives with suitable conditions in Longxi, Minhou, Fuzhou, and other places, with plans to cultivate various high-quality fruit seedlings such as citrus, longan, lychee, loquat, and peach. This year, approximately nine million apple trees will also be planted nationwide.

### 在绿化运动中各地栽植大批果树

1956-05-13
第2版()
专栏：

    在绿化运动中各地栽植大批果树
    在绿化荒山荒地的植树运动中，各地栽植了大批果树。据陕西、四川、浙江、福建、广东五省不完全的统计，今春栽植的柑桔将达三百多万株。福建省今春在漳浦、南清、莆田、长乐等地，新建了二十一个果树苗圃，并且在龙溪、闽侯、福州等地有条件的农业生产合作社建立了一批小型苗圃，有计划地培育柑桔、龙眼、荔枝、枇杷、桃等各种优良品种的果苗。今年全国还将栽植苹果树九百万株左右。
