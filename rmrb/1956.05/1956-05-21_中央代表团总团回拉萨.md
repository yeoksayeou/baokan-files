Title: The Central Delegation's Main Group Returns to Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1956-05-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 20th: The Central Delegation's main group returned to Lhasa at 6:00 PM today.

Those who went to the Jiaoguanting in the suburbs to welcome them included responsible personnel from the CCP Tibet Work Committee, the Tibet Military Region, the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, the Tibetan Local Government, and the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation.

### 中央代表团总团回拉萨

1956-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中央代表团总团回拉萨
    新华社拉萨20日电　中央代表团总团今天下午六时回到拉萨。
    到市郊接官厅迎接的，有中共西藏工委、西藏军区、西藏自治区筹备委员会、西藏地方政府、拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会的负责人员等。
