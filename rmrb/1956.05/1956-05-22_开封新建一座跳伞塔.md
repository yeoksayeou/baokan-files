Title: Kaifeng Builds a New Parachute Tower
Author:
Date: 1956-05-22
Page: 3
Sports News:

    Kaifeng Builds a New Parachute Tower
    A parachute tower, eighty-five meters high, has been newly built next to Yuwangtai in the southern suburbs of Kaifeng, Henan Province. Three steel arms extend horizontally from the tower at a height of seventy-seven meters, and there is a six-lobed jumping platform at a height of sixty-six meters.
    Below the parachute tower is a soft yellow sandy soil, forming a natural landing site.
    This parachute tower can be used for three types of parachuting: sensory, direct ascent, and tower gate.
    This parachute tower will be open in June, and mass parachuting activities will be carried out here on holidays and festivals. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 开封新建一座跳伞塔

1956-05-22
第3版()
专栏：体育消息

    开封新建一座跳伞塔
    在河南省开封市南郊禹王台旁，新建了一座高达八十五公尺的跳伞塔。在塔高七十七公尺处平伸出三支钢臂，六十六公尺高处有一六瓣形的跳台。
    跳伞塔的下面是一片松软的黄沙土，形成了天然的着陆场地。
    这座跳伞塔可以进行感性、直升、塔门等三种跳伞。
    这座跳伞塔将在六月开放，在例假日和节日将在这里开展群众性的跳伞活动。　（新华社）
