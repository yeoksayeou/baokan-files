Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Egyptian Professor Fekri
Author:
Date: 1956-05-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 24th—Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet last night in honor of Egyptian ancient historian Professor A. Fekri, who is visiting and lecturing in China according to the Sino-Egyptian Cultural Cooperation Talks memorandum.

Professor Shawarbi from Egypt, who is also lecturing in China, was also invited to the banquet.

Professor A. Fekri arrived in Beijing on April 13th. He has given several academic lectures in Beijing and is currently systematically teaching ancient Egyptian history at Peking University.

### 郭沫若举行宴会  招待埃及费克里教授

1956-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若举行宴会
    招待埃及费克里教授
    据新华社24日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若在昨晚设宴招待根据中埃文化合作会谈纪要来中国访问和讲学的埃及古代史学家阿·费克里教授。
    前来中国讲学的埃及的夏瓦比教授也应邀参加了宴会。
    阿·费克里教授是四月十三日来到北京的。他在北京曾经作了多次学术讲演，目前正在北京大学系统地讲授埃及古代史。
