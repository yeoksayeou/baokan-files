Title: Rafting Timber (Picture)
Author: Wang Yinan
Date: 1956-05-12
Page: 2

    Rafting Timber
    The fir wood produced in the southeastern mountainous areas of Guizhou Province is solid in texture and has straight trunks, the tallest reaching thirty meters. Now is the season for rafting timber. The picture shows a large number of timber being released along the Bala River. Photo by Wang Yinan (Xinhua News Agency manuscript)

### 流放木材（图片）
王翼南
1956-05-12
第2版()
专栏：

    流放木材
    贵州省东南部山区出产的杉材，质地坚实，树身通直，最高的长达三十公尺。现在正是木材流放的季节。图为沿巴拉河流放出的大批木材。　王翼南摄（新华社稿）
