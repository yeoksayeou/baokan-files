Title: Central Delegation Holds慰问 Meeting in Qamdo Beijing Holds Evening Party to Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet
Author:
Date: 1956-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Qamdo, 24th - The Second Sub-Delegation of the Central Delegation held a 慰问 meeting in the new urban area of Qamdo this afternoon, with more than 6,000 people participating. Monks and laypeople of Qamdo, cadres of government organs, officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Qamdo, officers and soldiers of the 9th generation of the Tibetan Army, and all representatives attending the Fourth People's Representative Conference of Qamdo Prefecture participated in the meeting.

栗再温, Head of the Second Sub-Delegation of the Central Delegation, first expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome and hospitality shown by the people of Qamdo Prefecture to the Central Delegation along the way. He said that this fully demonstrates the unity and friendship between all nationalities in the big family of the motherland, and also fully demonstrates the love of the Tibetan people for the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, Chairman Mao, and the central leaders. He expressed his cordial greetings on behalf of the Central Government to people of all social strata in Qamdo Prefecture.

格桑旺堆, Vice-Chairman of the People's Liberation Committee of Qamdo Prefecture, expressed his deep gratitude to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao for their meticulous care for the people of Qamdo Prefecture in his reply. He said that the Central Delegation's transmission and explanation of the central government's reform policies in ethnic minority areas has greatly enhanced the policy understanding of people of all strata in our region. It is the fundamental wish of the Tibetan people to enter socialist society together with all fraternal nationalities in the country. In order to adapt to the development of the socialist economy, it is necessary to gradually carry out some reforms. According to the instructions of the Central Government, the reforms will benefit all strata within the nation. 格桑旺堆 said that Qamdo Prefecture was liberated relatively early and has better conditions in all aspects. In the future, when reforms are carried out, it can only be at the forefront of Tibet and will never lag behind. He finally stated that in the future, under the leadership of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region headed by the Dalai Lama, they will further strengthen unity, strive for progress, and gradually catch up with the level of other nationalities in the country in terms of construction in all aspects to repay the care of the Central Government.

At the 慰问 meeting, the Central Delegation presented锦旗 and gifts to the People's Liberation Committee of Qamdo Prefecture and its 28宗, various people's organizations, and troops and organs stationed in Qamdo. At this time, applause and music continued for several tens of minutes in the venue.

After the 慰问 meeting, the 京剧 troupe that accompanied the delegation performed a 慰问 performance in the square.

Xinhua News Agency, 24th - The Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China and the Central Institute for Nationalities held an evening party on the 23rd to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the "Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet." Ethnic minority cadres and students, as well as personnel from the Dalai Lama's Beijing Office and the Panchen Lama's Beijing Office, totaling more than 1,000 people, attended the party.

### 中央代表团在昌都举行慰问大会   北京举行晚会庆祝西藏和平解放五周年

1956-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中央代表团在昌都举行慰问大会
    北京举行晚会庆祝西藏和平解放五周年
    新华社昌都24日电　中央代表团第二分团今天下午在昌都新市区广场举行了有六千多人参加的慰问大会。昌都市僧俗人民、机关干部、人民解放军驻昌都部队和藏军第九代本官兵以及出席昌都地区第四届人民代表会议的全体代表，都参加了大会。
    中央代表团第二分团团长栗再温首先对昌都地区人民在沿途对中央代表团的热烈欢迎和招待表示感谢。他说，这充分表现了祖国大家庭中各民族间的团结友爱，也充分表现了西藏人民对中国共产党、中央人民政府、毛主席和中央首长的爱戴。他代表中央对昌都地区各阶层人民致亲切的慰问。
    昌都地区人民解放委员会副主任格桑旺堆在致答词中，表示深深感谢中共中央、中央人民政府和毛主席对昌都地区人民无微不至的关怀。他说，中央代表团有关中央在少数民族地区改革政策的传达解释，大大提高了我区各阶层人士的政策认识。西藏民族和全国各兄弟民族共同进入社会主义社会，这是藏族人民的根本愿望。为了适应社会主义经济的发展，逐步进行一些改革是必需的。按照中央的指示，改革以后对民族内部各阶层都有利。格桑旺堆说，昌都地区解放比较早，各方面条件也好些，将来改革的时候，只能走在全西藏的前面而决不落在后面。他最后表示，今后在以达赖喇嘛为首的西藏自治区筹备委员会的领导下，进一步加强团结，努力进步，以各方面的建设事业的更加发展逐步赶上国内各民族的水平来报答中央的关怀。
    在慰问大会上，中央代表团向昌都地区人民解放委员会及其所属二十八个宗，各人民团体和驻昌都的部队、机关，分别赠送了锦旗和礼品。这时候，会场里掌声和乐曲声一直延续了几十分钟。
    慰问大会结束后，由随团的京剧团在广场上作了慰问演出。
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国民族事务委员会和中央民族学院在二十三日举行晚会，庆祝关于和平解放西藏办法的协议签订五周年。参加晚会的有各民族干部和学员，还有达赖喇嘛驻京办事处和班禅驻京办事处的人员，共一千多人。
