Title: Syrian Prime Minister Announces Next Cabinet Meeting to Discuss Recognition of China

Author:

Date: 1956-05-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 20th: According to TASS from Cairo: Cairo Radio broadcasted that Syrian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Ghazi has announced that the issue of formally recognizing the People's Republic of China will be discussed at the next Syrian Cabinet meeting.

The foreign ministers of Egypt, Syria, Lebanon, and Jordan recently held a meeting in Damascus where they informally discussed Egypt's decision to recognize the People's Republic of China. Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi explained to the meeting the reasons that prompted Egypt to formally recognize the People's Republic of China.

Syrian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Ghazi said that Egypt is absolutely free to recognize a country with a population of 600 million whose policies align with those of Egypt and other Arab countries.

Lebanese Prime Minister Yafi said that Egypt is an independent and sovereign state, and it is free to recognize any country, especially a great country like China.

### 叙利亚总理宣布  下次内阁会议将讨论承认中国问题

1956-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    叙利亚总理宣布
    下次内阁会议将讨论承认中国问题
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社开罗讯：据开罗电台广播，叙利亚总理兼外交部长加齐已经宣布，在叙利亚下次内阁会议上将讨论正式承认中华人民共和国问题。
    埃及、叙利亚、黎巴嫩和约旦四国外交部长最近在大马士革举行的会议曾经非正式地讨论了埃及承认人民中国政府的决定。埃及外交部长法齐向这次会议说明了促使埃及正式承认人民中国政府的理由。
    叙利亚总理兼外交部长加齐说，埃及绝对有自由承认一个拥有六亿人口而且它的政策符合埃及和其他阿拉伯国家的政策的国家。
    黎巴嫩总理雅非说，埃及是一个独立和主权国家，它有自由承认任何国家，特别是像中国这样一个伟大的国家。
