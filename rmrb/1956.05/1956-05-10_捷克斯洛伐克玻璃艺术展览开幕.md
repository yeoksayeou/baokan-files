Title: Czechoslovakian Glass Art Exhibition Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-05-10
Page: 3

　　Czechoslovakian Glass Art Exhibition Opens

　　The Czechoslovakian Modern Glass Art Exhibition opened at the Yuexin Hall in Beihai Park, Beijing, on May 9th, the National Day of the Czechoslovak Republic.

　　Among the more than 180 exquisite glass items on display, various kiln glass utensils are exhibited in China for the first time. These exhibits are beautiful in form, and some colors are similar to amber, some are similar to tea-colored crystal, and blue and yellow jade, which are generally praised by visitors. On display are also crystal clear vases and small cups engraved with the Czechoslovak national emblem, pictures, and portraits; small bowls and vases with bright grinding and polishing; lead glass utensils with gorgeous colors; simple and elegant fine yarn blown glass products; and gilded glass utensils. Forty pictures are displayed in the corridor in front of the exhibition hall, introducing some glass artworks made in Czechoslovakia since the fourteenth century.

　　The exhibition is organized by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations according to the 1956 implementation plan of the Sino-Czechoslovak Cultural Cooperation Agreement, and will be on display until May 30th.

　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 捷克斯洛伐克玻璃艺术展览开幕

1956-05-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷克斯洛伐克玻璃艺术展览开幕
    捷克斯洛伐克现代玻璃艺术展览在捷克斯洛伐克共和国国庆日——五月九日在北京北海公园悦心殿开幕。
    会上展出的一百八十多件精美玻璃品中，有首次在中国展出的各种窑玻璃器皿。这些展品形式美观，颜色有的类似琥珀，有的类似茶色水晶及蓝色和黄色玉石，受到参观者的普遍赞扬。在会上展出的，还有磨雕有捷克斯洛伐克国徽、图画和人像的晶莹的花瓶和小杯，磨琢光亮的小钵、花瓶等器皿，色泽绝美的铅玻璃器皿，朴素大方的细纱吹玻璃制品和描金的玻璃器皿。在展览室前面的走廊上陈列有四十幅图片，介绍了捷克斯洛伐克从十四世纪以来制作的一些玻璃艺术品。
    展览会是对外文化联络局根据中捷文化合作协定1956年执行计划举办的，将展出到五月三十日。
                      （新华社）
