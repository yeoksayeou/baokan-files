Title: CPSU Central Committee Meeting Discusses the Establishment of Boarding Schools

Author:

Date: 1956-05-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th - According to the Soviet "Pravda" report: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held a meeting from the 8th to the 10th to discuss the issue of organizing boarding schools in accordance with the resolutions of the 20th Congress of the CPSU.
    The meeting discussed various aspects of establishing boarding schools, as well as the content and organization of teaching work in boarding schools. The Ministers of Education of the various Union Republics reported on the measures implemented in various regions in organizing boarding schools.
    Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and Suslov, Furtseva, Aristov, and Belyayev, Secretaries of the CPSU Central Committee, participated in the meeting.

### 苏共中央开会讨论组织寄宿学校问题

1956-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央开会讨论组织寄宿学校问题
    新华社莫斯科12日电　据苏联“真理报”报道：苏联共产党中央委员会在八日到十日举行会议，讨论关于按照苏共第二十次代表大会的决议组织寄宿学校的问题。
    会议多方面讨论了创设寄宿学校的各种问题以及寄宿学校教学工作的内容和组织。各加盟共和国教育部长在会上报告了各地在组织寄宿学校方面所实行的措施。
    苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫和苏共中央书记苏斯洛夫、福尔采娃、阿里斯托夫、别里亚耶夫参加了会议。
