Title: Soviet Poet Vurgun Dies
Author:
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 28th: According to TASS, Baku: The outstanding Soviet poet, People's Poet of the Azerbaijan Republic, Samed Vurgun Vekilov, passed away on May 27th after a long and serious illness, at the age of 51. Samed Vurgun was a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Azerbaijan, a deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Vice President of the Academy of Sciences of the Azerbaijan Republic, and a member of the Committee of the Writers' Union of the USSR and the Writers' Union of Azerbaijan.

### 苏联诗人维基洛夫逝世

1956-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联诗人维基洛夫逝世
    新华社莫斯科28日电　据塔斯社巴库讯：苏联杰出诗人、阿塞拜疆共和国人民诗人萨麦德·武尔贡·维基洛夫在长期重病之后，在五月二十七日逝世，享年五十一岁。萨麦德·武尔贡生前是阿塞拜疆共产党中央委员、苏联最高苏维埃代表、阿塞拜疆共和国科学院副院长、苏联作家协会和阿塞拜疆作家协会委员会的委员。
