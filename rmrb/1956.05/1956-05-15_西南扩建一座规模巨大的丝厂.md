Title: Southwest Expansion of a Huge Silk Factory

Author: 
Date: 1956-05-15
Page: 2

    Southwest expands a huge silk factory
    The expansion project of the Second Silk Filature of the Southwest Silk Company officially started at the end of April. At present, building materials such as steel bars, cement, wood, bricks, and tiles, as well as some machinery and equipment, have been successively transported to the construction site. The attached wood processing plant, steel bar processing plant, and parts processing plant have all started production. The first group of silk reeling machines is expected to be installed at the end of May. After the expansion, this factory will add 10,000 ends of the latest silk reeling equipment. (Nanchong Newspaper Editorial Department)

### 西南扩建一座规模巨大的丝厂

1956-05-15
第2版()
专栏：

    西南扩建一座规模巨大的丝厂
    西南蚕丝公司第二制丝厂的扩建工程已经在四月底正式动工。目前钢筋、水泥、木料、砖瓦等建筑材料和部分机器设备已经陆续运往工地。附设的木料加工场、钢筋加工场、零件加工场已经全部开工生产。第一组的部分缫丝机器预计在五月底安装。扩建后这个厂将增加一万绪最新式的缫丝设备。　（南充报编辑部）
