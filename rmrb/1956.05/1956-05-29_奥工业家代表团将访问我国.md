Title: Austrian Industrial Delegation to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, May 28th: The Austrian Industrial Association announced on the 26th that a delegation composed of Austrian industrialists will visit China at the end of June. This delegation, invited by the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, will come to China to negotiate trade issues.

### 奥工业家代表团将访问我国

1956-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    奥工业家代表团将访问我国
    新华社28日讯　维也纳消息：奥地利工业家协会二十六日宣布，一个由奥地利工业家组成的代表团将在六月底前来中国进行访问。这个代表团应中国国际贸易促进委员会的邀请，将来中国谈判贸易问题。
