Title: Philippine Senate Passes Resolution Rejecting U.S. Land Ownership of Bases

Author: 
Date: 1956-05-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 4th: According to a May 4th Agence France-Presse report, the Philippine Senate unanimously passed a resolution rejecting the United States' demand to hold land ownership of the twenty-two U.S. military bases located in the Philippines.

    The resolution stated that the Philippine government will never relinquish the aforementioned land.

    Agence France-Presse reported that the purpose of this resolution is to support the Philippines' position in the upcoming negotiations with the United States regarding the revision of the military base treaty. This resolution will be sent to the House of Representatives for similar action.

### 菲律宾参议院通过决议拒绝美国保有基地土地所有权

1956-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾参议院通过决议拒绝美国保有基地土地所有权
    新华社4日讯　据法新社五月四日报道，菲律宾参议院在一项一致通过的决议中拒绝了美国对设在菲律宾的二十二个美国军事基地握有土地所有权的要求。
    这项决议说，菲律宾政府永远不会放弃上述土地。
    法新社说，这一个决议的目的在于支持菲律宾在将要同美国举行的关于修改军事基地条约的谈判中的地位。这项决议将送交众议院，以便众议院采取同样的行动。
