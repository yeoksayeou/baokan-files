Title: Mei Lanfang Visits Executives of "Asahi Shimbun" and Other Organizations
Author:
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 28th – Mei Lanfang, head of the Chinese Peking Opera Delegation visiting Japan, and other leaders, this morning visited executives of the "Asahi Shimbun" and the chairman of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, Tetsu Katayama, and the chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Jiichiro Matsumoto.

　　In the afternoon, the Peking Opera Delegation held a press conference at the Imperial Hotel. More than 40 journalists from major Tokyo newspapers such as "Asahi Shimbun," "Mainichi Shimbun," and "Yomiuri Shimbun," as well as radio station reporters, attended the conference.

　　At the meeting, Mei Lanfang, head of the Chinese Peking Opera Troupe, and Ouyang Yuqian, the first deputy head, explained the significance and purpose of the Peking Opera Delegation's visit to Japan for performances, and answered in detail questions raised by Japanese reporters regarding the reform of Peking Opera and other issues.

### 梅兰芳拜会“朝日新闻”社等团体负责人

1956-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    梅兰芳拜会“朝日新闻”社等团体负责人
    新华社东京28日电　中国访日京剧代表团团长梅兰芳等领导人今天上午拜会了“朝日新闻”社负责人和日中文化交流协会会长片山哲、日中友好协会会长松本治一郎。
    下午，京剧代表团在帝都饭店举行了记者招待会。参加招待会的有：“朝日新闻”、“每日新闻”、“读卖新闻”等东京各大报纸的记者和广播电台记者四十多人。
    会上，中国京剧团团长梅兰芳和第一副团长欧阳予倩说明了京剧代表团来日本进行访问演出的意义和目的，并且详细地回答了日本记者提出的关于京剧改革等问题。
