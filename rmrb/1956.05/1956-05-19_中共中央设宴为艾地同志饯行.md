Title: The CPC Central Committee Hosts a Farewell Banquet for Comrade Aidit
Author:
Date: 1956-05-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 18th. Comrade D.N. Aidit, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia, returned to Beijing from Korea and continued his tour. He will soon leave Beijing to return to Indonesia. The CPC Central Committee held a banquet on May 18th to bid him farewell. Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, Wang Jiaxiang and others from the CPC Central Committee attended the banquet.

### 中共中央设宴为艾地同志饯行

1956-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央设宴为艾地同志饯行
    新华社18日讯　印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会总书记艾地同志自朝鲜返抵北京，继续参观访问，日内即将离京返印度尼西亚，中共中央于五月十八日特设宴为他饯行。中共中央方面出席宴会的有刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、邓小平、王稼祥等同志。
