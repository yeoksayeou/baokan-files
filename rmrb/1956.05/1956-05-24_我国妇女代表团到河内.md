Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1956-05-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 23rd. A Chinese women's delegation led by Shuai Mengqi, member of the Executive Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, arrived in Hanoi on the afternoon of May 22nd. They were invited to attend the Second National Congress of Vietnamese Women to be held on May 25th.

Two representatives of the International Democratic Women's Federation and a Korean women's delegation also arrived in Hanoi on the morning of the 22nd. They, along with the women's delegation from the German Democratic Republic, which arrived in Hanoi a few days ago, will all attend the Second National Congress of Vietnamese Women.

### 我国妇女代表团到河内

1956-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我国妇女代表团到河内
    新华社河内23日电　由中华全国民主妇女联合会执行委员会委员帅孟奇率领的中国妇女代表团五月二十二日下午到达河内。她们是应邀来参加将在五月二十五日举行的越南全国妇女第二届代表大会的。
    国际民主妇女联合会的两个代表和朝鲜妇女代表团也在二十二日上午到达河内，她们和前几天已经来河内的民主德国的妇女代表团都将参加越南全国妇女第二届代表大会。
