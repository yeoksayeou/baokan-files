Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Receives Advanced Producers in the Construction Industry
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China received all the representatives attending the Advanced Producers Representative Conference of the Ministry of Construction Engineering and the Ministry of Urban Construction this morning.

### 朱德副主席接见建筑业先进生产者

1956-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席接见建筑业先进生产者
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国副主席朱德今天上午接见了出席建筑工程部和城市建设部先进生产者代表会议的全体代表。
