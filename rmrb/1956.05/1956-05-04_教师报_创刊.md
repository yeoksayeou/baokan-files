Title: "Teacher's Newspaper" Launched

Author:

Date: 1956-05-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency report:

    "Teacher's Newspaper" Launched
    "Teacher's Newspaper," jointly sponsored by the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China and the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China, was launched on May 1st.
    "Teacher's Newspaper" is a comprehensive newspaper reporting on the basic situation of education work nationwide and guiding general education and normal school education work nationwide. It is published twice a week, on Tuesdays and Fridays, each issue being one broadsheet.
                  (According to Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### “教师报”创刊

1956-05-04
第3版()
专栏：

    “教师报”创刊
    中华人民共和国教育部和中国教育工会全国委员会联合举办的“教师报”五月一日创刊。
    “教师报”是报道全国教育工作基本情况、指导全国普通教育、师范教育工作的综合性报纸，每星期二、五出版一次，每次对开一张。
                  （据新华社讯）
