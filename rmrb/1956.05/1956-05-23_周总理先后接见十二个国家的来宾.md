Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Guests from Twelve Countries
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received fourteen delegations and prominent figures from twelve countries from May 4th to May 22nd.

The following were received: the Indonesian Labor Delegation headed by Sanjono; the Chairman of the Nepal Women's Federation, Punya Prava, and the Nepalese women representatives, Raki Devi Seva; the Japanese Metal and Machinery Industry Workers' Union Delegation to China headed by Matsuo Kyo, the Japanese Trade Union Delegation headed by Konno Jiro, and the Japanese Newspaper Delegation headed by Sakurai Toshio; the Indonesian Journalists Delegation headed by Aziz; the New Zealand Cultural Figures Delegation to China headed by Ormond Wilson and James Bertram; the Burmese Film Delegation headed by U Aung Na, the Polish Film Workers' Delegation headed by Radkiewiczowa, and the Czechoslovak Film Workers' Delegation headed by Jiří Marek; the Indian-China Friendship Association Cultural Delegation headed by P. V. Bapat and B. S. Guha; the Japanese Peace Delegation headed by Ishikawakyoshi, and the Japanese peace representative Miyazaki Ryusuke and his wife; the Syrian Legal Workers Delegation headed by Adnan Kuatly; the Australian Cultural Delegation headed by Professor C. P. Fitzgerald; the Director General of the Japan-China Export-Import Association, Nango Saburo, and Deputy Director General Nita Hiroshi and two others; the Colombian peace activist and reporter for the Mexican "Excelsior" newspaper, Hugo Latorre Cabal; the reporter for the Australian Sydney Morning Herald, Selwyn Speight; and the Sudanese Cultural Friendship Delegation headed by Izzeldin Ali Amir.

### 周总理先后接见十二个国家的来宾

1956-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理先后接见十二个国家的来宾
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在五月四日到二十二日期间先后接见了十二个国家的十四个代表团和著名人士。
    受接见的有：以桑约诺为首的印度尼西亚劳动代表团，尼泊尔妇女联合会主席普尼亚·普拉瓦和尼泊尔妇女代表拉基·德维·塞瓦，以松尾乔为首的日本五金机械产业工会访华代表团、以今野治郎为首的日本工会代表团和以樱井俊夫为首的日本机关报代表团，以阿齐兹为首的印度尼西亚新闻工作者代表团，以奥蒙德·威尔逊和詹姆斯·贝特兰为首的新西兰文化界人士访华团，以吴仰拿为首的缅甸电影代表团、以拉德凯维超娃为首的波兰电影工作者代表团和以伊里·马里克为首的捷克斯洛伐克电影工作者代表团，以普·维·巴帕特和博·斯·古哈为首的印中友好协会文化代表团，以石河京市为首的日本和平代表团和日本和平代表宫崎龙介及夫人，以阿德南·库阿特利为首的叙利亚法律工作者代表团，以查·帕·费兹吉拉德教授为首的澳大利亚文化代表团，日本日中输出入协会理事长南乡三郎和副理事长似田博等三人，哥伦比亚和平人士、墨西哥“至上报”记者乌果·拉托雷·卡巴尔，澳大利亚悉尼“先驱晨报”记者塞尔温·斯派特和以伊兹尔丁·阿里·阿密尔为首的苏丹文化友好代表团。
