Title: Lhasa Mass Rally Celebrates the Establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region (Photo)
Author: Ren Yongzhao
Date: 1956-05-30
Page: 3

    Lhasa Mass Rally Celebrates the Establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region
    More than 30,000 Tibetan, Han, and Hui people in Lhasa warmly celebrated the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region. The picture shows a panoramic view of the celebration rally held in the People's Square in front of the Potala Palace on May 2nd.
　Xinhua News Agency reporter Ren Yongzhao Photo

### 拉萨庆祝西藏自治区筹委会成立的群众大会（图片）
任用昭
1956-05-30
第3版()
专栏：

    拉萨庆祝西藏自治区筹委会成立的群众大会
    拉萨市三万多藏、汉、回族人民热烈欢庆西藏自治区筹备委员会成立。图为五月二日在布达拉宫前人民广场上举行的庆祝大会会场全景。
　新华社记者　任用昭摄
