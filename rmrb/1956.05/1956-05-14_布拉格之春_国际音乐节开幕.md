Title: "Prague Spring" International Music Festival Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-05-14
Page: 4
    
    "Prague Spring" International Music Festival Opens

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 13th: The 11th "Prague Spring" International Music Festival officially opened on the evening of the 12th. The Czech Philharmonic Orchestra performed Mozart's famous works.

    According to the "Prague Spring" Secretariat, musicians from more than ten countries, including the Soviet Union, People's Democratic countries, the United States, France, Italy, and West Germany, will also perform. Among them are many famous pianists, violinists, and orchestra conductors. The Czech Philharmonic Orchestra, with a history of sixty years, and the Vienna Symphony Orchestra of Austria are both world-renowned. China has sent a delegation of nearly twenty people to participate. The music festival will last until June 3rd, with a total of more than sixty performances. Most of the tickets have already been sold.

### “布拉格之春”国际音乐节开幕

1956-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

    “布拉格之春”国际音乐节开幕
    据新华社布拉格13日电　第十一届“布拉格之春”国际音乐节十二日晚正式开幕。捷克管弦乐队演奏了莫扎特的名曲。
    据“布拉格之春”秘书处宣布，苏联、人民民主国家、美国、法国、意大利、西德等十多个国家的音乐家也将进行表演，他们当中有许多著名的钢琴家、提琴家和乐队指挥。有六十年历史的捷克管弦乐队和奥地利的维也纳交响乐团等都是世界闻名的。中国派有将近二十人组成的代表团参加。音乐节将举行到六月三日结束，一共要表演六十多次。其中大部分票目前已经售光。
