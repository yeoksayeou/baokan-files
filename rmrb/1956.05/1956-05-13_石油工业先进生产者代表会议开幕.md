Title: Opening of the Petroleum Industry Advanced Producers Representative Conference
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The 115 advanced producer representatives from the petroleum industry sector who had just completed the National Advanced Producers Representative Conference gathered again today with more than two hundred advanced producer representatives from various petroleum industry departments to jointly discuss implementing the spirit of the National Advanced Producers Representative Conference and further exchange their advanced experiences in finding oil, building oilfields and oil refineries, extracting oil, and refining oil. Zhou Wenlong, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, said at the opening of the National Petroleum Industry Advanced Producers Representative Conference today that this conference was convened after the successful conclusion of the National Advanced Producers Representative Conference. Its main purpose is to mobilize all employees to further extensively, deeply, and persistently carry out the advanced producer movement and promote the accelerated development of the petroleum industry. He said that the development of the petroleum industry requires not only overall planning and the adoption of necessary measures but, more importantly, relies on all advanced producers to lead all employees, give full play to their initiative and creativity, and continuously learn and master new technologies and improve the management level of enterprises. He asked advanced producers to regard this conference as a school for learning advanced experiences.
    Dong Xin, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Secretariat, delivered a congratulatory speech at the opening ceremony. Representatives from the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and Soviet experts also spoke at the meeting.
    The conference is scheduled to last for eight days. During the conference, the delegates will exchange more than one hundred kinds of advanced experiences, of which more than thirty will be promoted nationwide.

### 石油工业先进生产者代表会议开幕

1956-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    石油工业先进生产者代表会议开幕
    新华社11日讯　刚开完全国先进生产者代表会议的115名石油工业部门的先进生产者代表，今天又和来自各地的石油工业部门的二百多名先进生产者代表聚会一堂，共同讨论贯彻全国先进生产者代表会议的精神，进一步交流他们在寻找石油，建设油田、油厂，采油和炼油等方面的先进经验。石油工业部副部长周文龙在今天开幕的全国石油工业先进生产者代表会议上说，这次会议是在全国先进生产者代表会议胜利闭幕以后召开的，它的主要目的是动员全体职工进一步广泛、深入和持久地开展先进生产者运动，促进石油工业的加速发展。他说，发展石油工业除了要有全面规划和采取必要措施以外，更重要的是要靠各位先进生产者带动全体职工，发挥积极性和创造性，不断地学习和掌握新技术，改善和提高企业的经营管理水平。他要求先进生产者把这次会议看成是学习先进经验的学校。
    中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕在开幕式上致祝词。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的代表和苏联专家等，也在会上讲了话。
    这次会议预定举行八天，到会代表将在会议期间交流一百多种先进经验，其中有三十多种将在全国普遍推广。
