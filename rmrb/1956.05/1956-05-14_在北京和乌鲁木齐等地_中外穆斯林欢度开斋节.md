Title: Chinese and Foreign Muslims Celebrate Eid al-Fitr in Beijing, Urumqi and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1956-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 13th—More than 80,000 people of various ethnic groups in Beijing who believe in Islam celebrated Eid al-Fitr today. More than 80 mosques in the city held grand festive prayers.

　　In the famous Dongsi Pailou Mosque, more than 2,000 Muslims from six ethnic groups, dressed in festive costumes and carrying incense, attended the congregational prayer this morning. Among them were Da Pongsheng, vice chairman of the China Islamic Association, and Ma Songting, vice chairman of the China Hui Culture Advancement Association. Guests from Egypt and Sudan, as well as diplomatic officials from the Pakistani Embassy in China, also participated in the prayer.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, May 12th—Today is the Islamic Eid al-Fitr. Representatives of the Pakistani trade union delegation and the Sudanese cultural and friendly delegation who are visiting Urumqi, and Muslims of all ethnic groups in Urumqi, celebrated the festival together.

　　This morning, ten representatives of the two delegations, accompanied by Yi Ming Mahe Su Mu, vice chairman of the China Islamic Association, and others, gathered at the famous Nanguan Grand Mosque and prayed with more than 2,000 Muslims. After the prayer, the representatives visited the city. Many Muslims saw these foreign friends and went to shake hands with them, and the hosts and guests greeted each other with "Salaam."

　　At noon, the guests went to the homes of Saifuddin, chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, and Vice Chairmen Yi Minov, A. Said, deputy mayor of Urumqi, and others to offer their congratulations. The hosts received them with abundant refreshments and roast mutton, and took photos with them.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, May 13th—The twelve guests from the Indonesian Islamic delegation who were invited by the China Islamic Association to participate in Eid al-Fitr in China celebrated the festival with Muslims in Xi'an today. This morning, in the Huajue Lane Mosque, which was built more than a thousand years ago, the guests joined more than 1,500 people in Xi'an who believe in Islam, including cadres from government agencies, workers, private individuals from joint state-private enterprises, teachers and students, and small vendors, for prayers. They joined Chinese Muslims in praying for world peace.

　　The Indonesian Islamic delegation arrived in Xi'an on the morning of the 11th. That afternoon, the guests attended the "Juma Day" prayer at the Huajue Lane Mosque.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 13th—Hussaini Imam, head of the Pakistani Islamic delegation invited by the China Islamic Association to participate in Eid al-Fitr in China, and the first group of three members who arrived in Guangzhou with him, celebrated Eid al-Fitr with Chinese Muslims in Guangzhou today.

### 在北京和乌鲁木齐等地  中外穆斯林欢度开斋节

1956-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    在北京和乌鲁木齐等地
    中外穆斯林欢度开斋节
    据新华社13日讯　北京市信奉伊斯兰教的八万多各族人民今天欢度了开斋节，全市八十多个清真寺都分别举行隆重的节日礼拜。
    在著名的东四牌楼清真寺，今天上午有两千多个、包括六个民族的穆斯林，穿着节日盛装、拿着美香去参加聚礼。这里面还有中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生、中国回民文化协进会副主任马松亭等。埃及、苏丹的宾客和巴基斯坦驻我国大使馆的外交官员也参加了礼拜。
    据新华社乌鲁木齐12日电　今日是伊斯兰教的开斋节。前来乌鲁木齐市访问的巴基斯坦工会代表团、苏丹文化友好代表团的代表们和乌鲁木齐市的各民族穆斯林们一起欢度了节日。
    清晨，这两个代表团的十个代表在中国伊斯兰教协会副主任依明·马合苏木等的陪同下，在著名的南关清真大寺和两千多名穆斯林一起聚礼。聚礼后，代表们参观了市容。许多穆斯林看见这些来自国外的友人，都走去和他们握手，主人和客人们互道“色兰”。
    中午，客人们到新疆维吾尔自治区主席赛福鼎、副主席伊敏诺夫、乌鲁木齐市副市长阿·赛德等人的家中贺节。主人们用丰盛的茶点和烤羊肉等接待他们，并且和他们合影留念。
    据新华社西安13日电　应中国伊斯兰教协会邀请来中国参加开斋节的印度尼西亚伊斯兰教代表团十二位客人，今天和西安市的穆斯林一起欢度开斋节。上午，在一千多年前兴建的化觉巷清真大寺里，客人们同西安市信奉伊斯兰教的机关干部、工人、公私合营企业的私方人员、教员和学生、小商贩等一千五百多人一起进行礼拜。他们同中国的穆斯林一起祈祷世界和平。
    印度尼西亚伊斯兰教代表团是在十一日上午到达西安的。当天下午，客人们到化觉巷清真大寺参加了“主玛日”的礼拜。
    据新华社广州13日电　应中国伊斯兰教协会邀请前来我国参加开斋节的巴基斯坦伊斯兰教代表团团长侯赛尼依玛木和同他一起到达广州的第一批团员三人，今天在广州同中国的穆斯林一起欢度开斋节。
