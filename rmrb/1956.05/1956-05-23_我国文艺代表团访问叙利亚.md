Title: Chinese Art Troupe Visits Syria
Author:
Date: 1956-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, May 22nd: The Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation arrived in Damascus, the capital of Syria, on May 21st from Cairo at the invitation of Syrian President Kuwatly and the government. They will visit and perform in Syria.

Welcoming the delegation at the airport were representatives of the Syrian government, including Sara Burmeida, Director of the Propaganda Department of the Broadcasting and Propaganda Ministry, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Syria.

When the delegation left Cairo, Egyptian Minister of Religious Affairs Bakuri and Deputy Minister of Education Hashim (representing Minister of Education Hussein), as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Egypt, went to the airport to see them off.

### 我国文艺代表团访问叙利亚

1956-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文艺代表团访问叙利亚
    新华社大马士革22日电　中国文化艺术代表团应叙利亚总统库阿特利和政府的邀请在五月二十一日从开罗到达叙利亚首都大马士革。他们将在叙利亚访问和举行演出。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有叙利亚政府的代表、广播和宣传部宣传司司长萨拉·伯尔迈达，以及各国驻叙利亚外交使节。
    代表团在离开开罗的时候，埃及宗教事务部部长巴库里和教育部次长哈希姆（代表教育部长侯赛尼），以及各国驻埃及外交使节曾到机场欢送。
