Title: Disarmament Subcommittee Adjourns
Author:
Date: 1956-05-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, May 5th -- TASS reported from London on May 5th: The United Nations Disarmament Commission Subcommittee held a meeting in London on May 4th, chaired by the Soviet representative Gromyko. A communique was issued stating: In accordance with the United Nations Disarmament Commission's resolution of January 23, 1956, the Subcommittee decided to adjourn temporarily and submit a report to the United Nations Disarmament Commission. The Subcommittee will reconvene after the United Nations Disarmament Commission studies the Subcommittee's report, and upon agreement by the members of the Subcommittee.

### 裁军小组委员会休会

1956-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    裁军小组委员会休会
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社伦敦五日讯：联合国裁军委员会小组委员会五月四日在伦敦举行会议，由苏联代表葛罗米柯担任主席。会议发表一项公报说：根据联合国裁军委员会1956年1月23日的决议，小组委员会决定暂时休会，并且向联合国裁军委员会提出报告。小组委员会将在联合国裁军委员会研究小组委员会的报告以后，在小组委员会委员们的协议下再行复会。
