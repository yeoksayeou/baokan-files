Title: Czech Republic Uncovers US Spy Organization
Author:
Date: 1956-05-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 12th: The Czechoslovak News Agency reported on the 11th that the Czechoslovak Ministry of the Interior had uncovered a treasonous organization directed by the West Berlin spy center of the American spy organization. Ten criminals in this organization have been arrested.

The main targets of this spy organization's activities were to collect information on Czechoslovakia's aircraft industry, especially the production of jet aircraft and military airfields.

### 捷破获一美国间谍组织

1956-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    捷破获一美国间谍组织
    新华社布拉格12日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社十一日报道：捷克斯洛伐克内务部破获了一个受美国间谍组织西柏林间谍中心指挥的叛国组织。这个组织中的十个罪犯已经被捕。
    这个间谍组织的主要活动目标是收集捷克斯洛伐克的飞机工业、特别是喷气式飞机生产以及军用机场的情报。
