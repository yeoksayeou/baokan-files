Title: Vice Premier He Long Hosts a Banquet for Eight Minority Nationality Visiting Groups and Receives a Delegation of Minority Nationalities from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, He Long, held a banquet tonight to welcome eight minority nationality visiting groups from all over the country.

The Yunnan Province Dai Buddhist Visiting Group, headed by Huba Meng; the Gansu Province Pastoral Area Minority Nationalities Visiting Group, headed by Daji; the Qinghai Province Minority Nationalities Visiting Group, headed by Wulan; the Jilin Province Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture Ethnic Groups Visiting Group, headed by Cui Cai; the Yunnan Province Ethnic Groups Visiting Group, headed by Zhang Min; the Guangxi Province Ethnic Groups Visiting Group, headed by Mo Jin; the Tibet Sports Observation Group, headed by Suokang Jimei; and the Sichuan Province Ethnic Groups Youth Visiting Group, headed by Da Pengzhi, as well as representatives attending the National State-Owned Commerce Work Conference in Minority Nationality Areas, attended the banquet.

Vice Premier He Long delivered a welcoming speech at the beginning of the banquet, saying: You have come to the capital—Beijing—during the high tide of socialist construction and socialist transformation in the motherland. Every day, new things are constantly emerging and presenting a new atmosphere throughout the country. During your visit, you will specifically see these facts, and thereby see more clearly the glorious future of our great socialist motherland family.

Vice Premier He Long then said: In recent years, the people of all nationalities in our country have become increasingly closely united. Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao, we have correctly implemented the policy of equality among nationalities and freedom of religious belief, and have done a lot of work in promoting national regional autonomy, training ethnic minority cadres, and developing the economy and culture of various fraternal nationalities, so that all fraternal nationalities in our country have achieved great progress and development, and the faces of various ethnic minority areas have undergone significant changes. The common goal of the people of all nationalities in our country is to build our country into a great socialist country with a strong industry and agriculture, and a high level of culture and science. This requires further strengthening and consolidating the unity among all nationalities in the motherland, giving full play to the strength of the people of all nationalities, and making long-term and arduous efforts. He Long finally proposed a toast to our great motherland and the unity of all nationalities, to the socialist future of all nationalities in our country, and to the health of Chairman Mao, the leader of the people of all nationalities in our country.

The banquet was filled with an atmosphere of harmony and love in the great family of all nationalities of the motherland. Members of the visiting groups of various nationalities toasted each other, congratulating the great unity of the people of all nationalities in the motherland. Representatives of the Korean, Tibetan, Yi, Kazakh, Hui, Uygur, and other nationalities sang loudly in their own languages at the banquet, singing about the socialist future of the motherland, and singing about the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, the leader of the people of all nationalities.

After the banquet, a acrobatic performance was held.

Among those present at the banquet were Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, and Ministers Zhang Naiqi, Zeng Shan, Zhang Bojun, and Liang Xi, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Liu Chun and Sa Kongliao, Deputy Chairman of the National People's Congress Nationalities Committee Xie Fumin, as well as Deputy Ministers of various ministries and responsible persons of various people's organizations.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, He Long, today received the delegation of minority nationalities from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Sa Wenming, and had a cordial conversation with the members of the delegation. Delegation leader Sa Wenming presented a gift to Vice Premier He Long. Liu Chun and Sa Kongliao, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, were also present at the reception.

### 贺龙副总理欢宴八个少数民族参观团  并接见了越南民主共和国少数民族代表团

1956-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　贺龙副总理欢宴八个少数民族参观团
    并接见了越南民主共和国少数民族代表团
    新华社7日讯  中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙今天晚上举行宴会，欢迎来自祖国各地的八个少数民族参观团。
    以祜巴勐为首的云南省傣族佛教参观团、以达吉为首的甘肃省牧区少数民族参观团、以乌兰为首的青海省少数民族参观团、以崔采为首的吉林省延边朝鲜族自治州各民族参观团、以张敏为首的云南省各民族参观团、以莫矜为首的广西省各民族参观团、以索康计美为首的西藏体育观摩团和以达鹏志为首的四川省各民族青年参观团，以及参加全国少数民族地区国营商业工作会议的代表都出席了宴会。
    贺龙副总理在宴会开始时致欢迎词说：各位是在祖国社会主义建设和社会主义改造的高潮中来到首都——北京的。现在每一天全国各地都不断地涌现出新的事物，呈现出新的气象。各位在参观过程中，将会具体地看到这些事实，由此更加清楚地看到我们伟大社会主义祖国大家庭的光辉的未来。
    贺龙副总理接着说：几年来我国各族人民已经日益紧密地团结起来了。在中国共产党、中央人民政府和毛主席的领导下，我们正确地执行了民族平等和宗教信仰自由政策，在推行民族区域自治、培养民族干部和发展各兄弟民族的经济和文化等方面都作了许多工作，从而使我国各兄弟民族都得到了很大的进步和发展，各民族地区的面貌都有了显著的变化，我国各族人民的共同目标就是把我国建设成为一个有强大的工业和农业、有高度的文化科学水平的伟大社会主义国家，这就需要更进一步加强和巩固祖国各民族间的团结，充分发挥各族人民的力量，作长期的、艰苦的努力。贺龙最后提议为我们伟大的祖国和各民族的团结，为我国各民族的社会主义前途，为我国各族人民的领袖毛主席的健康干杯。
    宴会上充满祖国各民族大家庭的和睦友爱的气氛。各民族参观团的团员们互相敬酒，祝贺祖国各族人民的大团结。朝鲜族、藏族、彝族、哈萨克族、回族、维吾尔族等民族的代表在宴会上用本民族的语言高声唱歌，歌唱祖国的社会主义前途，歌唱中国共产党和各族人民的领袖毛主席。
    在宴会以后，举行了杂技表演。
    出席宴会作陪的，有国务院秘书长习仲勋，各部部长章乃器、曾山、章伯钧、梁希，民族事务委员会副主任刘春、萨空了，全国人民代表大会民族委员会副主任委员谢扶民，以及各部副部长和各人民团体负责人。
    新华社7日讯  中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙今天接见了以沙文明为首的越南民主共和国少数民族代表团，和代表团团员们进行了亲切的交谈。代表团团长沙文明向贺龙副总理赠送了礼物。接见时，中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任刘春和萨空了也在座。
