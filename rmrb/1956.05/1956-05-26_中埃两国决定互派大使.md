Title: China and Egypt Decide to Exchange Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1956-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 25th. The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Egypt have decided to exchange ambassadors and establish embassies in each other's countries.

### 中埃两国决定互派大使

1956-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中埃两国决定互派大使
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国政府和埃及共和国政府业已决定互派大使，并且互设大使馆。
