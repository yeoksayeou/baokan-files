Title: Kim Il-sung to Visit Poland
Author:
Date: 1956-05-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th—According to the Korean Central News Agency on the 30th, the Polish government has invited a North Korean government delegation headed by Premier Kim Il-sung to pay a friendly visit to Poland.
    Premier Kim Il-sung has gladly accepted the invitation.

### 金日成将访问波兰

1956-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成将访问波兰
    新华社平壤30日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社三十日讯：波兰政府邀请以金日成首相为首的朝鲜政府代表团到波兰进行友好访问。
    金日成首相已经满意地接受了邀请。
