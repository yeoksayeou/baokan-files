Title: Britain Recognizes the Independence of Morocco and Tunisia
Author:
Date: 1956-05-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, 11th. The British government issued a statement on the 10th, announcing the official recognition of the independence of Morocco and Tunisia.
    A British Foreign Office spokesman said that the British government intends to exchange ambassadors with Morocco and Tunisia in the near future.

### 英国承认摩洛哥和突尼斯独立

1956-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    英国承认摩洛哥和突尼斯独立
    新华社11日讯　伦敦消息：英国政府在十日发表声明，宣布正式承认摩洛哥和突尼斯的独立。
    英国外交部发言人说，英国政府打算在最近的将来同摩洛哥和突尼斯互派大使。
