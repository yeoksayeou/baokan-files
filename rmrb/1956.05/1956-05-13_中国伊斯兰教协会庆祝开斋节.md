Title: China Islamic Association Celebrates Eid al-Fitr
Author:
Date: 1956-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 12th. More than one hundred prominent figures of Islam in China celebrated their festival—Eid al-Fitr—for the first time together with Muslims from Arab countries and Indonesia.

This afternoon at 7:30 PM, Chinese Muslims dressed in festive attire met eighteen Muslim brothers from Egypt, Syria, Sudan, and Indonesia at the Dongsi Mosque, which has a history of over 500 years. They exchanged festive greetings in Arabic. The hall resounded with "Eid Mubarak!"

Dapu Sheng spoke at the Eid al-Fitr celebration banquet held by the China Islamic Association.

This devout and well-known Imam, with his flowing silver beard, extended festive greetings on behalf of Chinese Muslims to Muslim brothers who had come from afar.

Dapu Sheng cited many examples illustrating the development of contact and friendship between Muslims in Asia and Africa over the past year. He said that these religious, cultural, and economic contacts and exchanges are beneficial to consolidating and developing the friendship and unity between China and Muslims in Asia and Africa. He hoped that these friendly contacts would be further developed in the future.

Representatives from Syria, Sudan, Indonesia, and Egypt said in their speeches that they had seen Chinese Muslim brothers living free and happy lives in China, and expressed that they would convey this happy news to the Muslims in their own countries.

During the banquet, Chinese and foreign Muslims enjoyed delicious dishes such as hand-grabbed mutton while engaging in cordial conversations. They wished all Muslim brothers around the world a happier life year after year and expressed their willingness to unite and work together for world peace.

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, May 12th. People of Uyghur, Kazakh, Hui, Tatar, and other seven ethnic groups in Urumqi who believe in Islam celebrated "Eid al-Fitr" today. At 9:00 AM, festive "congregational prayers" were held in the city's forty-eight mosques, with more than 30,000 people participating.

After the prayers, Han cadres, workers, and citizens from various government organs, factories, and residential areas visited the homes of fraternal ethnic groups who believe in Islam to extend holiday greetings. Wang Enmao, First Secretary of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and other leaders of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region People's Committee visited the homes of Saifuddin, Chairman of the Autonomous Region People's Committee, and other leading cadres of fraternal ethnic groups to celebrate the festival.

### 中国伊斯兰教协会庆祝开斋节

1956-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中国伊斯兰教协会庆祝开斋节
    新华社12日讯　中国的一百多位伊斯兰教名流，第一次同阿拉伯国家及印度尼西亚的穆斯林们在一起欢度自己的佳节——开斋节。
    今天下午七时半，穿着节日盛装的中国穆斯林们，在有着五百多年历史的东四清真寺内，同来自埃及、叙利亚、苏丹和印度尼西亚的十八位穆斯林兄弟会面了。他们用阿拉伯话互相道着节日的祝贺。大厅响起了一片“尔德木巴丰凯（贺喜）！”的声音。
    达浦生在中国伊斯兰教协会举行的这个庆祝开斋节的宴会上讲了话。
    这位飘着银色美髯的虔诚的著名阿訇代表中国的穆斯林向不远万里而来的各国穆斯林兄弟致节日的祝贺。
    达浦生以许多事例说明了一年来亚非地区穆斯林之间的接触和友谊的发展，他说，这些宗教的、文化的、经济的接触和交流，对巩固和发展中国和亚非地区穆斯林之间的友谊和团结都是有益的，希望这些友谊的接触今后得到更大的发展。
    叙利亚、苏丹、印度尼西亚和埃及的代表在讲话中说，他们在中国看到了中国的穆斯林兄弟们，都过着自由和愉快的生活，表示要把这一愉快的消息告诉自己国家的穆斯林们。
    席间，中外穆斯林们一边吃着抓羊肉等丰美的菜肴，一边进行着亲切的交谈。他们祝全世界穆斯林兄弟的生活一年比一年幸福，并且愿所有穆斯林团结一致为世界和平而努力。
    据新华社乌鲁木齐12日电　乌鲁木齐市信仰伊斯兰教的维吾尔、哈萨克、回、塔塔尔等七个民族的人民今天欢度“开斋节”。上午九时，全市四十八个清真寺分别举行了节日“聚礼”，参加聚礼的有三万多人。
    聚礼完毕后，各个机关、工厂和居民中的汉族干部、工人、市民到信仰伊斯兰教的各兄弟民族家中拜节。中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会第一书记王恩茂等和新疆维吾尔自治区人民委员会等负责人，到自治区人民委员会主席赛福鼎和其他兄弟民族的领导干部家中祝贺佳节。
