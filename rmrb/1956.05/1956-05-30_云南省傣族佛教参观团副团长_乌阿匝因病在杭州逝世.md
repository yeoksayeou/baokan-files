Title: Deputy Head of Yunnan Dai Buddhist Delegation, U Aza, Dies of Illness in Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-05-30
Page: 3

    Deputy Head of Yunnan Dai Buddhist Delegation,
    U Aza, Dies of Illness in Hangzhou
    U Aza, Deputy Head of the Yunnan Dai Buddhist Delegation, died of illness in Hangzhou. A memorial service was held on the morning of the 28th at the Baoshan Funeral Home in Hangzhou.
    The memorial service was presided over by Peng Ruilin, Secretary-General of the Zhejiang Provincial People's Committee, with Gu Chunlin, Deputy Mayor of Hangzhou, and Liu Jiqing, Secretary-General of the Hangzhou Municipal People's Committee, as co-officiants. More than 200 people attended the memorial service, including representatives from the Nationalities Affairs Commission and the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, Master Mingzhen, a standing director of the Buddhist Association of China, all members of the delegation, and members of the Buddhist community in Hangzhou. Kan Jingtai, Deputy Governor of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture of Yunnan Province, also came from Yunnan to attend the memorial service.
    Gu Chunlin, Deputy Mayor of Hangzhou, reported on U Aza's illness and treatment process at the meeting, and read out condolence messages from Premier Zhou Enlai, Xirao Gyatso, President of the Buddhist Association of China, and the Nationalities Affairs Commission, the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, and the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee. Kan Jingtai, Deputy Governor of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture of Yunnan Province, and Lang Dege, Deputy Head of the Yunnan Dai Buddhist Delegation, also spoke at the meeting.
    U Aza suffered from severe septicemia and lobar pneumonia on the 22nd, complicated by acute adrenocortical insufficiency leading to peripheral circulatory failure, and died in Hangzhou on the morning of the 23rd. A cremation ceremony was held at the Fengshan Funeral Home in Hangzhou at 3:00 p.m. on the 27th. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 云南省傣族佛教参观团副团长  乌阿匝因病在杭州逝世

1956-05-30
第3版()
专栏：

    云南省傣族佛教参观团副团长
    乌阿匝因病在杭州逝世
    云南省傣族佛教参观团副团长乌阿匝在杭州因病逝世。二十八日上午有关各界在杭州市宝善殡仪馆举行了追悼大会。
    追悼大会由浙江省人民委员会秘书长彭瑞林主祭，杭州市副市长顾春林、杭州市人民委员会秘书长刘季青陪祭。参加追悼大会的有民族事务委员会和国务院宗教事务局的代表、中国佛教协会常务理事明真法师、参观团的全体人员和杭州市佛教界人士等二百多人。云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治州副州长衎景泰也从云南赶来参加追悼大会。
    杭州市副市长顾春林在会上报告了乌阿匝副团长患病和治疗经过，并且宣读了周恩来总理和中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错以及民族事务委员会、国务院宗教事务局、云南省人民委员会等单位的唁电。云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治州副州长衎景泰、云南省傣族佛教参观团副团长郎德哥等也在会上讲了话。
    乌阿匝副团长二十二日患严重败血症、大叶性肺炎，并发急性肾上腺皮质功能衰竭而致周围循环衰竭，二十三日早晨在杭州病逝。二十七日下午三时在杭州市凤山殡仪馆举行火葬。　（新华社）
