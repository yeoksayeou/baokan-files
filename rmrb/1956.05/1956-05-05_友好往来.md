Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1956-05-05
Page: 4

    △ The Brazilian Peace Delegation, composed of five members, and the Japanese Peace Delegation, composed of seven members, arrived in Beijing on the 3rd at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.
    △ Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Delhi on the evening of May 1st at the invitation of the Indian National Trade Union Congress. He will attend the 8th Annual Meeting of the Indian National Trade Union Congress to be held in Surat on May 6th and 7th.

### 友好往来

1956-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    △应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的巴西和平代表团一行五人和日本和平代表团团员七人，已在三日到达北京。
    △中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一应印度全国职工大会的邀请在五月一日晚上到达德里，他将参加印度全国职工大会五月六日和七日在苏拉特举行的第八次年会。
