Title: Central Delegation Distributes Alms to Temples in Xigazê
Author:
Date: 1956-05-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xigazê, May 15th: The Central Delegation's main group and the first sub-group distributed alms to over four thousand lamas in the Zhaxilhunbu Monastery and several nearby temples in Xigazê on the 14th.

	Zhang Jingwu, Deputy Head of the main delegation, and Wang Zaitian, Head of the sub-delegation, along with other delegation members, visited the Zhaxilhunbu Monastery accompanied by Öljü Living Buddha, the Panchen Erdeni's Deputy Scripture Teacher. The lamas of the monastery lined the route from the foot of the mountain at the temple gate all the way to the Great Sutra Hall on the mountainside, welcoming the delegation.

### 中央代表团向日喀则寺庙发放布施

1956-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中央代表团向日喀则寺庙发放布施
    据新华社日喀则15日电　中央代表团总团和第一分团十四日向日喀则扎什伦布寺和附近几个寺庙的四千多喇嘛，发放了布施。
    总团副团长张经武和分团团长王再天以及其他团员，在班禅额尔德尼副经师欧居活佛的陪同下，参观了扎什伦布寺。这个寺的喇嘛从山脚下的寺门口一直排到山腰的大经堂，夹道欢迎代表团。
