Title: Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Holds Reception to Celebrate Fourth Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1956-05-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The Sino-Burmese Friendship Association held a reception tonight to celebrate the fourth anniversary of the association's founding and to welcome the Burmese film delegation to China for a visit.

    Present at tonight's reception were the Burmese film delegation headed by U Ohn Maung, Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung and embassy staff, the heads and representatives of the Chinese agricultural, cultural, and Buddhist delegations who had visited Burma, and well-known figures from all walks of life in China.

    Qian Weichang, Vice President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, said in his speech at the meeting: The close friendship between the peoples of China and Burma is developing day by day. We believe that through this visit to China by outstanding representatives of the Burmese film art world, the flowers of the close friendship between China and Burma will surely bear more fruitful results.

    In his speech, Qian Weichang also expressed special thanks to the Burma-China Friendship Association for its contributions to consolidating Sino-Burmese friendship.

    U Ohn Maung, head of the Burmese film delegation, said in his speech that the delegation's visit to China had left a deep impression on them of the friendship given by the Chinese people. He warmly congratulated Burma-China friendship and wished it "ten thousand years," and they "are willing to work for this ten thousand years."

    U Tun Wai, Executive Committee member and Secretary of the Burma-China Friendship Association, also spoke at the meeting. He said: When the Burma-China Friendship Association and the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association were established in those years, we all hoped to build a building of friendship; today we are very happy to see that this building has been built. On behalf of the Burma-China Friendship Association, he expressed his willingness to work with the members of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, like building the close friendship between Burma and China, to consolidate this friendship.

    The Sino-Burmese Friendship Association and the Burma-China Friendship Association, and the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association and the Burmese film delegation exchanged gifts at the meeting.

    At the meeting, the excellent Burmese film "Her Love" was shown; and a tea party was held.

### 中缅友协举行招待会庆祝成立四周年

1956-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅友协举行招待会庆祝成立四周年
    新华社11日讯　中缅友好协会今晚举行招待会，庆祝协会成立四周年和欢迎缅甸电影代表团来华访问。
    出席今晚招待会的有以吴仰拿为首的缅甸电影代表团，缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和使馆人员，曾经访问过缅甸的中国农业、文化、佛教代表团的负责人和代表，中国各界著名人士。
    中缅友好协会副会长钱伟长在会上致词说：中缅两国人民的亲密友谊，正在一天天地向前发展。我们相信，通过这次缅甸电影艺术界优秀的代表们访问中国，一定使中缅两国亲密的友谊的花朵，结出更多的丰硕的果实。
    钱伟长在讲话中还特别感谢缅中友好协会在巩固中缅友好工作中所作的贡献。
    缅甸电影代表团团长吴仰拿在讲话中说，代表团在中国访问，已对中国人民所给予他们的友谊留下深刻的印象。他热烈祝贺缅中友好万岁，他们并“愿为这个万岁而努力”。
    缅中友好协会执行委员兼秘书吴吞卫也在会上讲了话。他说：在当年缅中友协和中缅友协成立的时候，我们都希望能建立一个友好大厦；今天我们很高兴地看到，这个大厦已经建立。他代表缅中友协，表示愿同中缅友协的会员们一起，像建立缅中两国间亲密友谊一样，为巩固这个友谊而奋斗。
    中缅友协和缅中友协、中缅友协和缅甸电影代表团都在会上互赠了礼物。
    在会上，放映了缅甸优秀电影“她的爱”；并且举行了茶会。
