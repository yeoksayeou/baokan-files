Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Islamic Representatives from Two Countries and Meets Syrian Member of Parliament Rustum
Author:
Date: 1956-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, this afternoon received the Indonesian Islamic delegation headed by West Abbas and Ali Akbar, and the Pakistani Islamic delegation headed by Grand Mullah Etshamul Haq and Saeed Hosseini Imam.

    Present at the reception were Nur Muhammad Dapusheng, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association; Muhammad Ali Zhang Yuzhen, Secretary-General; Haji Abdul Alim Masongting, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Hui People's Cultural Advancement Association; Ali Gan Chunlei, Secretary-General; Zhemallungin Li Shu, Vice President of the China Islamic Theological Institute; Yusuf Ho Liu, member of the China Islamic Association; Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Sino-Indonesian Friendship Association; and Qian Duansheng, Vice President of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association.

    He Chengxiang, Director of the Bureau of Religious Affairs of the State Council, and Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, were also present at the reception.

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, this afternoon received Abdul Majid Rustum, a member of the Syrian Parliament. Luo Longji, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General; and Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, were present at the reception.

### 周总理接见两个国家的伊斯兰教代表  并接见了叙利亚议会议员鲁斯托姆

1956-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见两个国家的伊斯兰教代表
    并接见了叙利亚议会议员鲁斯托姆
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天下午接见了以西·阿巴斯和阿里·阿克巴尔为首的印度尼西亚伊斯兰教代表团，以大毛拉·埃特沙穆尔·哈克和赛义德·侯赛尼·依玛木为首的巴基斯坦伊斯兰教代表团。
    接见的时候，在座的有中国伊斯兰教协会副主任努尔·穆罕默德·达浦生、秘书长穆罕默德·阿里·张玉珍，中国回民文化协进会副主任哈志·阿不都来里木·马松亭、秘书长阿里·甘春雷，中国伊斯兰教经学院副院长哲玛伦丁·李恕，中国伊斯兰教协会委员尤苏夫·霍流，中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之，中巴友好协会副会长钱端升。
    接见的时候，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，外交部亚洲司副司长章文晋也在座。
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天下午接见了叙利亚议会议员阿卜杜勒·麦吉德·鲁斯托姆。接见的时候，在座的有中国人民外交学会副会长罗隆基、秘书长吴茂荪，外交部亚洲司副司长章文晋。
