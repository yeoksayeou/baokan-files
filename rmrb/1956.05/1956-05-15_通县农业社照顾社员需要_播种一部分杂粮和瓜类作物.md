Title: Tong County Agricultural Cooperatives Take Care of Members' Needs by Planting Some Coarse Grains and Melons

Author: 
Date: 1956-05-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Report:

    Tong County Agricultural Cooperatives Take Care of Members' Needs
    Planting Some Coarse Grains and Melons

    Agricultural production cooperatives in Tong County, Hebei Province, while planting according to the national plan, have also taken into account the daily needs of their members, planting some coarse grains and melons, which has satisfied the members. According to statistics, agricultural cooperatives throughout the county have planted more than 2,000 mu of melons and more than 80,000 mu of sorghum, millet, and soybeans, etc., according to the various requirements of the majority of members, such as eating melons in the summer and having sticky cakes for festivals.
          (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 通县农业社照顾社员需要  播种一部分杂粮和瓜类作物

1956-05-15
第2版()
专栏：

    通县农业社照顾社员需要
    播种一部分杂粮和瓜类作物
    河北省通县各农业生产合作社，在按照国家计划播种中又照顾了社员日常生活需要，播种了一部分杂粮和瓜类作物，社员们都感到满意。据统计，全县农业合作社已按照广大社员夏天要吃瓜、过节有粘糕等各种要求，种植了二千余亩瓜类作物和八万多亩黍稷、江豆、高粮等杂粮。
          （据新华社讯）
