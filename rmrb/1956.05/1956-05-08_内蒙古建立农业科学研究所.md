Title: Inner Mongolia Establishes Agricultural Science Research Institute
Author:
Date: 1956-05-08
Page: 3

    Inner Mongolia Establishes Agricultural Science Research Institute
    The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Agricultural Science Research Institute was established on May 3. The institute has three departments: crops, soil agrochemistry, and plant protection, and is starting research work on forty projects related to agricultural production. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 内蒙古建立农业科学研究所

1956-05-08
第3版()
专栏：

    内蒙古建立农业科学研究所
    内蒙古自治区农业科学研究所已在五月三日成立。研究所设作物、土壤农化和植物保护三个系，开始进行有关农业生产的四十个项目的研究工作。  （据新华社讯）
