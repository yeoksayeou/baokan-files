Title: The US Interest Rate is Too High: Lebanon Refuses to Accept US Loan
Author:
Date: 1956-05-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, May 22nd, TASS: According to the "News" report, authoritative sources revealed that the Lebanese government has rejected a loan of 17 million Lebanese pounds offered by the United States. This amount was intended to be used for the construction of highways between Lebanon and other Arab countries. The reason for the rejection is that the interest rate demanded by the United States is too high.

### 美国要的利息太高  黎巴嫩拒绝接受美国的贷款

1956-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    美国要的利息太高
    黎巴嫩拒绝接受美国的贷款
    新华社23日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特二十二日讯：据“消息报”报道，权威人士透露，黎巴嫩政府已经拒绝了美国提供的一千七百万黎巴嫩镑的贷款。这笔款是预定用来建筑黎巴嫩同其他阿拉伯国家之间的公路的。拒绝的原因是，美国所要求的利息太高。
