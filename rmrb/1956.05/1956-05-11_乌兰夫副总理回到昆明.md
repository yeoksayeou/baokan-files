Title: Vice Premier Ulanhu Returns to Kunming
Author:
Date: 1956-05-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 9th -- The delegation of the People's Republic of China, which participated in the coronation ceremony of King Mahendra of Nepal, led by the special envoy of the People's Republic of China, Vice Premier of the State Council Ulanhu, returned to China by plane this afternoon and arrived in Kunming.

### 乌兰夫副总理回到昆明

1956-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    乌兰夫副总理回到昆明
    据新华社昆明9日电  参加尼泊尔国王马亨德拉加冕典礼的中华人民共和国代表团，在中华人民共和国特使、国务院副总理乌兰夫率领下，今天下午乘飞机回国到达昆明。
