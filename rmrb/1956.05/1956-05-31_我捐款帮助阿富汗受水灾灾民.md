Title: I Donate to Help Afghan Flood Victims
Author:
Date: 1956-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- The Chinese Red Cross Society announced yesterday that after learning of the recent floods in the southwestern and northeastern regions of Afghanistan, it telegraphed the Afghan Red Crescent Society to express its condolences and offer assistance. The Afghan Red Crescent Society replied on May 28, thanking the Chinese Red Cross Society for its condolences and welcoming any form of assistance from China. The Chinese Red Cross Society has already telegraphed RMB 10,000 (equivalent to £1,443) yesterday to help the flood-stricken people of Afghanistan.

### 我捐款帮助阿富汗受水灾灾民

1956-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我捐款帮助阿富汗受水灾灾民
    新华社30日讯　　中国红十字会昨天宣布，它在得到阿富汗西南部和东北部地区最近发生水灾的消息后，曾经打电报给阿富汗红新月会表示慰问并提出愿意给予援助。阿富汗红新月会在五月二十八日回电感谢中国红十字会的慰问并欢迎中国给予任何形式的帮助。中国红十字会已经在昨天电汇人民币一万元（折合1，443英镑），帮助阿富汗受灾人民。
