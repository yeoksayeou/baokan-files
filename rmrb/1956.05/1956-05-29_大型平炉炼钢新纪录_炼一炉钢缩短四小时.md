Title: New Record for Large Open-Hearth Steelmaking: Steel production time per furnace reduced by four hours
Author:
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, May 28th: Workers at the Youth Friendship Furnace of the Anshan Iron and Steel Company's Steelmaking Plant created a new record for rapid steelmaking in a large open-hearth furnace this morning, producing a furnace of steel in five hours and forty-five minutes. This is approximately four hours shorter than the state-planned steelmaking time. The quality of the steel meets the standards. Currently, this new steelmaking method is being widely promoted in all open-hearth furnaces in the steelmaking plant.

### 大型平炉炼钢新纪录  炼一炉钢缩短四小时

1956-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

  　大型平炉炼钢新纪录
    炼一炉钢缩短四小时
    据新华社鞍山28日电　鞍山钢铁公司炼钢厂青年友谊炉职工今天上午创造了大型平炉五点四十五分钟炼一炉钢的快速炼钢新纪录，约比国家计划炼钢时间缩短了四个钟头。钢的质量合乎标准。目前，这一新的炼钢方法正在炼钢厂各平炉上普遍推广。
