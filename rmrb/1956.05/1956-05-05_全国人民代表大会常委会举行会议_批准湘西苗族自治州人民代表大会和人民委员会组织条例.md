Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting to Approve Organizational Regulations for the People's Congress and People's Committee of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture
Author:
Date: 1956-05-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th -- The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its thirty-sixth meeting on the afternoon of the 4th. After hearing an explanation from Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, the meeting passed a resolution approving the Organizational Regulations for the People's Congress of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture and the Organizational Regulations for the People's Committee of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture, which were formulated by the Second Session of the First People's Congress of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议  批准湘西苗族自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例

1956-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议
　　批准湘西苗族自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例
    新华社4日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会四日下午举行第三十六次会议。会议在听了民族事务委员会副主任刘春的说明后，通过一项决议，批准湘西苗族自治州第一届人民代表大会第二次会议制定的湘西苗族自治州人民代表大会组织条例和湘西苗族自治州人民委员会组织条例。
