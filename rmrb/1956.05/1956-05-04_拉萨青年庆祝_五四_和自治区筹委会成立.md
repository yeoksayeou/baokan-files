Title: Lhasa Youth Celebrate "May Fourth" and the Establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region

Author:

Date: 1956-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 3rd. More than 1,300 youths from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Lhasa held a grand evening party in the newly built Lhasa Auditorium tonight to celebrate the "May Fourth" Youth Day and the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

　　After Xuekang Tudeng Nima, Deputy Director of the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association, delivered the opening speech, Luo Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and Secretary-General of the Central Delegation, met with all the youths present amidst warm applause. Luo Yi said in his speech: Tibet, like other parts of the motherland, is also advancing. In the past few years, Tibetan youths have played a prominent role in promoting national unity and various constructions. He encouraged the youths to study diligently, strive to become knowledgeable and good at labor, and better build the motherland and Tibet.

　　Pingcuo Wangqiu, a national youth socialist construction activist, and Haba Nobu, a Tibetan youth worker, representing all the youths in Tibet, stated that they must follow the advanced youths of the country and contribute all their strength to the construction of their hometown and the great motherland.

　　Bai Yunfeng, Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Youth League, and Tan Guansan, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China, also spoke.

　　On behalf of the Central Committee of the Youth League and the All-China Youth Federation, Luo Yi presented Tibetan youth with more than 300 items, including film projectors, radios, accordions, transportation vehicles, and some equipment for the Children's Palace and military sports training grounds. In addition, many books and banners were also presented.

　　After the meeting, the literary and artistic troupe that accompanied the central delegation to Tibet performed songs, dances, and acrobatics for the youths.

### 拉萨青年庆祝“五四”和自治区筹委会成立

1956-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　拉萨青年庆祝“五四”和自治区筹委会成立
    新华社拉萨3日电　拉萨市各族各界青年一千三百多人，今天晚上在新建的拉萨大礼堂举行隆重的晚会，庆祝“五四”青年节和西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立。
    拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会副主任委员雪康·土登尼玛致开会词后，在热烈的掌声中，中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记、中央代表团秘书长罗毅同全体到会青年见了面。罗毅在讲话中说：西藏和祖国各地一样也在前进，西藏青年几年来在增进民族团结和各项建设中起了突出的作用。他勉励青年们勤奋学习，努力使自己成为有知识又善于劳动的人，更好地建设祖国，建设西藏。
    全国青年社会主义建设积极分子平错旺秋和藏族青年工人哈巴诺布，代表西藏全体青年表示一定要追随全国的先进青年，为建设自己的家乡和伟大祖国贡献出全部力量。
    青年团西藏工作委员会书记白云峰和中共西藏工委副书记谭冠三也都讲了话。
    罗毅代表青年团中央和中华全国民主青年联合会向西藏青年赠送了电影放映机、收音机、手风琴、交通车，以及少年宫和军事体育教练场等用的一部分设备，共计三百多件。此外，还赠送了许多书籍和锦旗。
    会后，随中央代表团赴藏的文艺工作队为青年们表演了歌舞和杂技。
