Title: China and Burma Exchange Animals
Author:
Date: 1956-05-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, May 21st: A tiger and a pair of golden pheasants from China were transported to Rangoon from Kunming by special plane on May 19th. These animals are a gift from Song Qingling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, to the Government of the Union of Burma.

The Government of the Union of Burma returned the gift of a Himalayan bear, a pair of white ducks, and twelve long-bearded goats to Vice-Chairman Song Qingling, which were transported to Kunming by the same special plane on the same day.

### 中缅互赠动物

1956-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅互赠动物
    新华社仰光21日电　来自中国的一只老虎和一对金色雉鸟已经在五月十九日由专机从昆明运到仰光。这些动物是中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄送给缅甸联邦政府的礼物。
    缅甸联邦政府回赠给宋庆龄副委员长的一只喜马拉雅山熊、一对白鸭和十二只长须羊已经在同一天由同一架专机运到昆明。
