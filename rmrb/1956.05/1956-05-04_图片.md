Title: Shanghai Port's First Water-Land Transportation Dock - Kaiping Dock Successfully Reconstructed
Author:
Date: 1956-05-04
Page: 1

　　Shanghai Port's first water-land transportation dock, Kaiping Dock, has been successfully reconstructed. Through mechanized loading and unloading tools, it enables ten-thousand-ton ships to directly load and unload goods with land-based trains, accelerating the turnover of materials and reducing handling costs.
