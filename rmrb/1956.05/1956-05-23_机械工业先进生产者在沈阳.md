Title: Advanced Producers in the Mechanical Industry in Shenyang

Author:

Date: 1956-05-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, May 22nd – Since arriving in Shenyang from Beijing on the 16th, nine advanced producers in the mechanical industry from Shenyang, Dalian, Harbin, Qiqihar and other places, who are attending the National Congress of Advanced Producers, have conveyed the spirit of the conference to workers in three machine tool factories and held discussions with workers and technicians on advanced experiences. Beginning on the 20th, they began to impart their own and newly learned advanced experiences to workers at the Shenyang No. 3 Machine Tool Factory. More than 130 workers and technicians from the No. 3 Machine Tool Factory have learned these advanced experiences.

### 机械工业先进生产者在沈阳

1956-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    机械工业先进生产者在沈阳
    据新华社沈阳22日电　出席全国先进生产者代表会议的沈阳、大连、哈尔滨、齐齐哈尔等地的九个机械工业先进生产者，自十六日由北京来到沈阳以后，向三个机床厂工人传达了大会精神，并且和工人、技术人员座谈先进经验。从二十日开始，他们向沈阳第三机床厂工人传授了自己的和新学来的先进经验。第三机床厂已有一百三十多名工人和技术人员学会了这些先进经验。
