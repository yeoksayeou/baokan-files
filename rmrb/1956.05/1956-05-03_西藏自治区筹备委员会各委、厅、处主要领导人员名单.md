Title: List of Major Leaders of Committees, Offices, and Departments of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1956-05-03
Page: 3

The Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region issued an order on the 1st, announcing the list of major leaders of committees, offices, and departments of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region. This list was approved at the State Council's Standing Committee meeting on April 20, 1956.

The main responsible personnel of each committee, office, and department are as follows:

Secretary-General: Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Deputy Secretaries-General: Lhalu Tsewang Dorje, Phagpa Gelek Namgyal, Chen Jingbo. Director of the General Office: Tenzin Gyatso, Deputy Directors: Tsukerbpa Tenzin Tsewang, Liu Shiyuan. Chairman of the Finance and Economy Committee: Xia Zhongyuan, Deputy Chairmen: Gyaltsen Thondup Namgyal, Ngawang Jimpa, Horkha Sey Silang Jiangcun. Chairman of the Religious Affairs Committee: Kyabje Dorjechang Losang Yishey, Deputy Chairmen: Phagpala, Dinggye, Wei Huang. Director of the Civil Affairs Department: Thubten Dandar, Deputy Directors: Wang De, Yang Dongsheng. Director of the Construction Department: Tsarong Dasang Dramdul, Deputy Directors: Japale Ngawang Dorje, Hou Jie. Director of the Culture and Education Department: Sungkar Badan, Deputy Directors: Kharag Khenchog Dekhen, Dorje Tseten. Director of the Health Department: Rangba Ongi Wangdu, Deputy Directors: Tanpa Rizé, Liang Hong. Director of the Agriculture and Forestry Department: Losang Tshenley, Deputy Directors: Losang Gyantsen, Han Qianzhi. Director of the Animal Husbandry Department: Chaya Loden Sherab, Deputy Directors: Thubten Jangchup, Wang Yongkui. Director of the Transportation Department: Changchin Thubten Tsepal, Deputy Directors: So Lang, Zhou Huabiao. Director of the Industry and Commerce Department: Phunda Yangpel, Deputy Directors: Thubten Chinrab, Niu Qingshan. Director of the Finance Department: Bai Yunfeng, Deputy Directors: Shegurbpa Gyakya Khyenjuk, Tunkhang Sonam Dorje. Director of the Public Security Department: Zhi Zemin, Deputy Directors: Tara Losang Sandan, Tanpa. Director of the Justice Department: Yuthok Tashi Dhondup, Deputy Directors: Banchen Losang Gyayang, Yaocang Losang Genchuk, Song Changhua.

### 西藏自治区筹备委员会各委、厅、处主要领导人员名单

1956-05-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　西藏自治区筹备委员会各委、厅、处主要领导人员名单
    西藏自治区筹备委员会一日发布命令，公布西藏自治区筹备委员会各委、厅、处主要领导人员名单。这个名单是经1956年4月20日国务院常务会议通过的。
    各委、厅、处主要负责人员如下：
    秘书长：阿沛·阿旺晋美，副秘书长：拉敏·益喜楚臣、邦达多吉、陈竞波。办公厅主任：丹增嘉措，副主任：崔克巴·登珠泽仁、刘士元。财政经济委员会主任：夏仲远，副主任：詹东·洛桑郎杰、纳旺金巴、霍尔加色·思郎江村。宗教事务委员会主任：赤江·罗桑益西，副主任：帕巴拉、定杰、卫璜。民政处处长：土登丹达，副处长：汪德、杨东生。建设处处长：擦绒·达桑占度，副处长：呷日本·才旺多吉、侯杰。文教处处长：孙格巴登，副处长：喀拉·堪巧德钦、多杰才旦。卫生处处长：然巴·昂吉旺堆，副处长：丹巴日则、梁洪。农林处处长：洛桑称烈，副处长：洛桑坚赞、韩倩之。畜牧处处长：察雅·罗登协绕，副处长：土登降秋、王永魁。交通处处长：强钦·土登才白，副处长：索郎、周华彪。工商处处长：邦达养丕，副处长：土登钦饶、牛青山。财政处处长：白云峰，副处长：雪古巴·嘉样凯珠、团康·索南刀杰。公安处处长：智泽民，副处长：达拉·洛桑三旦、丹巴。司法处处长：宇妥·扎西顿珠，副处长：班帐·洛桑加洋、尧仓·洛桑根却、宋长华。
