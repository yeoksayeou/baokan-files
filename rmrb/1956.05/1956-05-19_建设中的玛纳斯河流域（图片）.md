Title: The Manas River Basin Under Construction (Picture)
Author: Wu Chunzhan
Date: 1956-05-19
Page: 2

    The Manas River Basin Under Construction
    Soldiers of the Production and Construction Corps of the Xinjiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have built buildings in the reclamation area of the Manas River Basin and are carrying out greening work near the new buildings. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Chunzhan photographed.
