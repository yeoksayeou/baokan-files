Title: Ethiopian Emperor Receives Chinese Art Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-05-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Addis Ababa, May 5th: Ethiopian Emperor Haile Selassie I received the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation at 9:00 AM on May 3rd. The delegation included head Bao Erhan, deputy heads Ma Yuhuai and Ma Hanbing, advisor Zhang Yue, secretary-general Song Xiao, and several prominent artists.

Bao Erhan presented gifts from Chairman Mao Zedong to Emperor Selassie. In his address, he conveyed the respects of the government and people of the People's Republic of China to the Emperor, government, and people of Ethiopia, and expressed the hope that the delegation's visit would promote friendship and cultural exchange between China and Ethiopia.

Selassie replied, saying: Ethiopia is a country with a long history, and China is a country with an even longer history. In the past, friendly relations existed between our two countries. Last year, our representatives met with the Chinese Foreign Minister and heard him express China's sympathy and goodwill towards Ethiopia.

He said that we know that when Ethiopia was in dark days, the Chinese people gave us sympathy and support. He also expressed gratitude for Chairman Mao Zedong's goodwill and congratulated the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation on its successful performances in Ethiopia.

The Ethiopian Emperor had a friendly conversation with Bao Erhan. He praised the achievements of the Chinese people. He said that both Ethiopia and China love independence, and they maintain friendship between them. The visit of the Cultural and Art Delegation is only the beginning of establishing this friendly relationship.

The reception was filled with a friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Addis Ababa, May 5th: The head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, Bao Erhan, deputy heads Ma Yuhuai and Ma Hanbing, and advisor Zhang Yue paid a visit to Ethiopian Foreign Minister Akliou Habte-Wold on May 3rd. Bao Erhan conveyed the greetings of Chinese Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai to Akliou Habte-Wold.

Akliou Habte-Wold said during the meeting: The Bandung Conference restored the connection between China and Ethiopia. The Ethiopian government hopes to maintain this connection, which should not be limited to cultural aspects, but should be expanded to all aspects. The current cultural connection is only the first step in restoring multifaceted relations between the two countries.

### 埃塞俄比亚皇帝接见我国艺术代表团

1956-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　埃塞俄比亚皇帝接见我国艺术代表团
    新华社亚的斯亚贝巴5日电　埃塞俄比亚皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世在五月三日上午九时接见了中国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉、副团长马玉槐、马寒冰、顾问张越、秘书长宋肖和代表团的几位著名的艺术家。
    鲍尔汉代表毛泽东主席向塞拉西皇帝赠送了礼物。他在致词中代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向埃塞俄比亚皇帝、政府和人民致敬意，并且表示希望代表团的访问能够促进中国和埃塞俄比亚两国之间的友谊和文化交流。
    塞拉西致答词说：埃塞俄比亚是一个有着悠久历史的国家，中国是一个有着更悠久的历史的国家。在过去，在我们两国之间存在着友好关系。去年，我们的代表们会见了中国外交部长，并且听见他表示中国对埃塞俄比亚的同情和亲善。
    他说，我们知道，当埃塞俄比亚处在黑暗的日子里的时候，中国人民曾给我们以同情和支持。他还表示感谢毛泽东主席的善意，并且祝贺中国文化艺术代表团在埃塞俄比亚演出成功。
    埃塞俄比亚皇帝同鲍尔汉进行了友好的交谈。他称赞中国人民取得的成就。他说，埃塞俄比亚和中国都热爱独立，它们之间保持着友谊。文化艺术代表团的访问只不过是建立这种友好关系的开端。
    这次接见充满了友好的空气。
    新华社亚的斯亚贝巴5日电　中国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉、副团长马玉槐、马寒冰和顾问张越五月三日拜访了埃塞俄比亚外交大臣阿克利劳·沃尔德。鲍尔汉向阿克利劳·沃尔德转达了中国总理兼外交部长周恩来的问候。
    阿克利劳·沃尔德在会见时说：万隆会议恢复了中国和埃塞俄比亚之间的联系。埃塞俄比亚政府希望保持这种联系，这种联系不应仅限于文化方面，而且要扩大到各方面。目前的文化联系只是恢复两国多方面联系的第一步。
