Title: State Council Holds 29th Plenary Meeting, Approves Trade Agreement with Cambodia and Other Decisions
Author:
Date: 1956-05-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 25th – The State Council of the People's Republic of China held its 29th plenary meeting on the 25th.

    After hearing an explanation from Minister of Justice Shi Liang, the meeting discussed the Ministry of Justice's report on several issues related to judicial administration, and in principle, adopted the "Provisional Measures of the People's Republic of China for the Collection of Civil Litigation Fees (Draft)"; the Ministry of Justice's report on establishing lawyer work and the "Provisional Measures for Lawyer Fees"; the Ministry of Justice's report on carrying out notarization work; and the Ministry of Justice's opinions on the number, term of office, and methods of selection of people's assessors.

    After hearing an explanation from Vice Minister of Labor Mao Qihua, the meeting discussed and approved the "Factory Safety and Hygiene Regulations," "Construction and Installation Engineering Safety Technical Regulations," and "Regulations on Reporting Worker and Staff Injury and Death Accidents," and adopted the State Council's resolution on earnestly implementing the above three regulations.

    The meeting also passed the "State Council's Decision on Preventing Silica Dust Hazards in Factories and Mines."

    After hearing an explanation from Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, the meeting approved the "Trade Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia" and the "Payment Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia."

    The meeting approved the "State Council's Decision on Establishing Shenyang County and Benxi County in Liaoning Province," the "State Council's Decision on Changing the Shuangyashan Mining Area in Heilongjiang Province to Shuangyashan City," and the "State Council's Decision on Abolishing the Changshan Island Special Zone and Establishing Changdao County."

    Finally, the meeting approved several personnel appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第二十九次全体会议  批准中柬贸易协定和其他决定

1956-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第二十九次全体会议
    批准中柬贸易协定和其他决定
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第二十九次会议，在二十五日举行。
    会议在听了司法部长史良的说明后，讨论了司法部关于司法行政工作方面几个问题的请示报告，并且原则通过“中华人民共和国征收民事诉讼费用暂行办法（草案）”；司法部关于建立律师工作的请示报告和“律师收费暂行办法”；司法部关于开展公证工作的请示报告；司法部关于人民陪审员的名额、任期和产生办法等意见。
    会议在听了劳动部副部长毛齐华的说明后，讨论和批准了“工厂安全卫生规程”、“建筑安装工程安全技术规程”和“工人职员伤亡事故报告规程”，并且通过了国务院关于切实执行上述三项规程的决议。
    会议还通过了“国务院关于防止厂、矿企业中矽尘危害的决定”。
    会议在听了对外贸易部长叶季壮的说明后，批准了“中华人民共和国政府和柬埔寨王国政府贸易协定”、“中华人民共和国政府和柬埔寨王国政府支付协定”。
    会议批准了“国务院关于设置辽宁省沈阳县和本溪县的决定”、“国务院关于将黑龙江省双鸭山矿区改设为双鸭山市的决定”和“国务院关于撤销长山岛特区改为长岛县的决定”。
    会议最后通过了任免事项多起。
