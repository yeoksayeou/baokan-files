Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives the Indian Consul-General in Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1956-05-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 1st. Chen Yi, head of the Central Delegation and Vice Premier of the State Council, received Menon, the Indian Consul-General in Lhasa, Mr. Nyodak Bikhe, the Nepalese representative, and seven others in Lhasa this afternoon. In their conversation, both Chen Yi and Menon mentioned the great progress made in Tibet recently.

Chen Yi finally invited Menon and others to watch the performance of the Central Delegation's Cultural and Art Troupe at the celebration party for the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

### 陈毅副总理接见印度驻拉萨总领事

1956-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见印度驻拉萨总领事
    新华社拉萨1日电　中央代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅今天下午在拉萨接见了印度驻拉萨总领事梅农、尼泊尔于大必公兴先生等九人。陈毅和梅农在谈话中，都提到西藏最近以来有很大的进步。
    陈毅最后邀请梅农等人，一起观看中央代表团文艺工作队在庆祝西藏自治区筹备委员会成立晚会上的演出。
