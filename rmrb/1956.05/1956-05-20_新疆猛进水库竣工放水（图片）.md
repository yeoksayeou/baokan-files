Title: Completion and Water Release of Xinjiang Mengjin Reservoir (Photo)
Author: Sha Sha
Date: 1956-05-20
Page: 2

    Completion and Water Release of Xinjiang Mengjin Reservoir
    The Mengjin Reservoir project, expanded by the Agricultural Production and Construction Corps of the Xinjiang Military Region in the Urumqi River basin, was completed and released water on May 4th. After the expansion, this reservoir can store 60 million cubic meters of water, twenty times more than the original storage capacity. The irrigated area can be increased from the original more than 6,000 mu to 260,000 mu.
                             Sha Sha photographed (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 新疆猛进水库竣工放水（图片）
莎沙
1956-05-20
第2版()
专栏：

    新疆猛进水库竣工放水
    新疆军区农业生产建设部队在乌鲁木齐河流域扩建的猛进水库工程，已经在五月四日竣工放水。这个水库经过扩建可以蓄水六千万立方公尺，比原来的蓄水量扩大了二十倍。灌溉面积可由原来的六千多亩增加到二十六万亩。
                             莎沙摄（新华社稿）
