Title: Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1956-05-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 27th. Today, under the chairmanship of the Dalai Lama, the Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region held its second meeting. Responsible cadres from various commissions and offices attended the meeting. The meeting unanimously approved the "Motion on the Establishment of Organizations for the Various Commissions and Offices of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and the Appointment of Section-level Cadres in the General Office and the Civil Affairs Office." At the same time, it studied and approved the "Management Plan for the Outflow of Silver Dollars from the Tibet Region." In addition, it was decided that every Friday would be the regular meeting time for the committee.

### 西藏自治区筹委会常委会开会

1956-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏自治区筹委会常委会开会
    新华社拉萨27日电　今天，在达赖喇嘛的主持下，西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员会召开了第二次会议。各委、处的负责干部出席了会议。会上一致通过了“关于西藏自治区筹备委员会各委、处的机构建立和办公厅、民政处科级干部任职的议案”，同时研究并通过了“关于西藏地区银元外流管理方案”，另外还决定每个星期五为该会的例会时间。
