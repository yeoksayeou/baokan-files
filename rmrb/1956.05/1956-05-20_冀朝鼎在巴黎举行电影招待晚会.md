Title: Ji Chaoding Holds Film Reception in Paris
Author:
Date: 1956-05-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 19th: A Paris dispatch reports that over one thousand people from all walks of life in Paris attended the film reception held by Ji Chaoding, head of the Chinese delegation to the Paris Exposition, on the evening of May 17th.
　　The Chinese films "Mei Lanfang's Stage Art", "Folk Sports Performance", and "New Hangzhou of the People" were screened at the reception.

### 冀朝鼎在巴黎举行电影招待晚会

1956-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    冀朝鼎在巴黎举行电影招待晚会
    新华社布拉格19日电　巴黎消息：一千多名巴黎各界人士应邀出席了中国参加巴黎博览会展览团团长冀朝鼎五月十七日晚上举行的电影招待晚会。
    晚会上放映了“梅兰芳的舞台艺术”、“民间体育表演”、“人民的新杭州”等三部中国影片。
