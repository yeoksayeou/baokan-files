Title: Lhasa Youth Request Establishment of Communist Youth League and Young Pioneers

Author: 
Date: 1956-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 17th: The Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association held a meeting on the afternoon of the 16th to discuss and request the development of members of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and Young Pioneers among Tibetan and Hui youths, and also requested the establishment of a unified Patriotic Youth Cultural Association for the Tibet region.

At the meeting, Dala Losang Sandan, Director of the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association, said: With the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, the desire of the vast Tibetan youth for progress is becoming increasingly urgent. In order to build a prosperous and happy new Tibet and the socialist society of the great motherland, they are all actively requesting to establish and join their own organizations. He also said that Lhasa youth's request has been submitted to the Dalai Lama and has obtained his approval.

The people attending the meeting unanimously agreed with Dala Losang Sandan's speech.

Apei Awang Jinmei, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, congratulated the young people, saying: The establishment of the Communist Youth League and Young Pioneers organizations among Tibetan youth is of great significance. Tan Guansan, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Liang Feng, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Youth League, also pointed out in their speeches that the establishment of the Communist Youth League and Young Pioneers organizations in the Tibet region shows the improvement of the awareness of youths of all ethnic groups in Tibet.

The request of the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association to establish the Communist Youth League, Young Pioneers, and establish a unified Patriotic Youth Cultural Association in the Tibet region has been approved by the Tibet Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League.

### 拉萨青年要求建立青年团和少先队

1956-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    拉萨青年要求建立青年团和少先队
    据新华社拉萨17日电　拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会，十六日下午开会讨论并要求在藏族和回族青少年中，发展中国新民主主义青年团团员和少年先锋队队员，并要求成立西藏地区统一的爱国青年文化联谊会。
    会上，拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会主任达拉·洛桑三旦说：随着西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，广大藏族青年要求进步的心情越来越迫切，他们为了建设繁荣幸福的新西藏和伟大祖国的社会主义社会，都积极要求建立和参加自己的组织。他还说，拉萨青年的这种要求，已经请示了达赖喇嘛，取得了他的赞同。
    参加这次会议的人们，一致赞成达拉·洛桑三旦的发言。
    参加会议的西藏自治区筹备委员会秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美向青、少年们祝贺，他说：在藏族青、少年中建立青年团和少先队的组织，是具有重要意义的。中共西藏工委副书记谭冠三和青年团西藏工委副书记梁枫在讲话中，也指出：西藏地区建立青年团和少先队的组织，表明了西藏各族青少年觉悟的提高。
    拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会提出的建立青年团、少先队和成立西藏地区统一的爱国青年文化联谊会的要求，已经获得中国新民主主义青年团西藏工委的同意和批准。
