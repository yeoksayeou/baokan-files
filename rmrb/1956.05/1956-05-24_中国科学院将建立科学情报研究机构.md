Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences to Establish Scientific Intelligence Research Institution
Author:
Date: 1956-05-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency:

    The Chinese Academy of Sciences is preparing to establish a Scientific Intelligence Research Institute in Beijing within this year to collect, study, and disseminate information on domestic and international scientific developments and the latest achievements. This was discussed and approved at the executive meeting of the Chinese Academy of Sciences on the 22nd.

    After its establishment this year, this research institution will initially publish two types of scientific abstracts and a journal reporting on the world's latest and most important scientific achievements.

### 中国科学院将建立科学情报研究机构

1956-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

    中国科学院将建立科学情报研究机构
    中国科学院准备今年年内在北京建立一个科学情报研究所，搜集、研究、传播国内外科学发展情况和最新成就的资料。这是二十二日中国科学院院务常务会议上讨论通过的。
    这个研究机构今年成立以后，将先出版两种科学文摘和一种报道世界上最新、最重要的科学成就的期刊。
                        （新华社）
