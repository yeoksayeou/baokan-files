Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Grand Reception for Scientists Participating in National Science Planning
Author:
Date: 1956-05-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai held a grand reception at Huairentang Hall in Zhongnanhai tonight to entertain the scientists participating in the national science planning work.

	The three hundred-plus scientists attending the reception today, who are participating in the compilation of the national long-term science plan, have preliminarily proposed more than fifty important tasks in natural science and technology after more than five months of hard work. These include the latest fields in world science, such as research on computing technology, research on semiconductors, research on electronics and radio technology, as well as automatic control and remote manipulation.

	At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai went to every table, warmly shaking hands with prominent natural scientists and social scientists from all over the country, and conversed with many scientists. Premier Zhou Enlai encouraged the scientists to strive to carry out scientific research, learn from the advanced science and technology of the Soviet Union and all other countries, and strive to bring China's important and urgently needed science and technology sectors close to and up to the world's advanced level within twelve years.

	Premier Zhou Enlai spoke with many scientists who had studied in Western countries, saying that while we oppose the political and social systems of capitalism, we should humbly learn from the advanced science and technology of capitalist countries.

	The reception proceeded in an atmosphere of joy and delight. The scientists gathered together constantly toasted the broad prospects for development that the motherland has opened up for scientific research work, and for the unity and cooperation of scientists.

	At the reception, Vice Premier Li Fuchun, President Guo Moruo, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, and heads of various departments of state organs enthusiastically exchanged views with the scientists attending the reception, congratulating the scientists on achieving even greater success in their future scientific research work.

### 周恩来总理举行盛大酒会  招待参加全国科学规划工作的科学家

1956-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理举行盛大酒会
    招待参加全国科学规划工作的科学家
    新华社26日讯　周恩来总理今晚在中南海怀仁堂举行了一个盛大的酒会，招待参加全国科学规划工作的科学家们。
    今天出席酒会的三百多位参加编制全国长期科学规划工作的科学家们，经过五个多月来的辛勤劳动，已经初步提出了五十多项自然科学技术方面的重要任务，其中包括世界科学中最新的部门，例如计算技术的研究，半导体的研究，电子学和无线电技术的研究，以及自动控制和远距离操纵等。
    周恩来总理在酒会上，走遍每一张餐桌，同来自全国各地的著名自然科学家和社会科学家们一一亲切握手，并且和许多科学家作了谈话。周恩来总理勉励科学家们，要努力开展科学研究工作，向苏联和其他一切国家的先进科学技术学习，争取在十二年内使我国重要和急需的科学技术部门接近和赶上世界先进水平。
    周恩来总理同许多曾留学西方国家的科学家们谈了话，他说，我们反对资本主义的政治和社会制度，但对资本主义国家的先进科学技术，则应该虚心的学习。
    酒会一直在欢乐和愉快的空气中进行。聚集一堂的科学家，不断的为祖国给科学研究工作开辟的广阔的发展前景，为科学家们的团结合作而干杯。
    酒会上，李富春副总理、郭沫若院长、聂荣臻元帅和国家机关各部门的负责人，都同参加酒会的科学家们热烈的交谈，祝贺科学家们在今后的科学研究工作中取得更大的成就。
