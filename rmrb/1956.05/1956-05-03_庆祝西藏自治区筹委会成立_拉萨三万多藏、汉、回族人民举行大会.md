Title: Lhasa 30,000+ Tibetan, Han, and Hui People Hold a Grand Meeting to Celebrate the Establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1956-05-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 2nd: People's Square in front of the Potala Palace was decorated with colorful banners today as more than 30,000 Tibetan, Han, and Hui people in Lhasa enthusiastically celebrated the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region. From early morning, Lhasa citizens dressed in festive attire gathered in the square from all directions. Actors from nineteen Tibetan opera troupes and song and dance troupes from various parts of Tibet, who had rushed to Lhasa, also put on their costumes. More than 500 Tibetan workers from the First Engineering Office of the First Engineering Bureau enthusiastically participated in the meeting today. When they were building the Lhasa River levee, their work efficiency nearly doubled in celebration of the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region. Many farmers and herdsmen came from places three or four days away from Lhasa with their entire families to participate in the meeting.

　　At the two entrances and on both sides of the celebration meeting, huge banners were erected, inscribed in gold letters in Tibetan and Chinese with slogans such as "Long Live Unity!" and "The Establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region is Another Glorious Achievement of the Chinese Communist Party's National Policy," "Further Strengthen National Unity and Internal Unity in Tibet, and Strive to Develop Tibet's Political, Economic, and Cultural Construction Undertakings." On both sides of the rostrum were tent-like long viewing platforms, where members of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region, all members of the Central Delegation, responsible cadres of various organs of the Tibet Working Committee of the CPC, and officers above the rank of colonel of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and local Tibetan troops watched the ceremony.

　　At noon, the celebration meeting began with the national anthem and salutes. Lobsang Rinchen, Acting Kalon of the Tibetan Local Government, Jamyang Jigme, Director of the Panchen Kanpo Lija Committee, Bangda Doje, Deputy Director of the Qamdo District People's Liberation Committee, and Bai Yunfeng, Deputy Director of the Organization Department of the Tibet Working Committee of the CPC, together raised the national flag in the venue. Among the salutes fired today were Tibetan gunpowder cannons, which are only used during solemn ceremonies. Ape Awang Jinmei, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, served as chairman of the celebration meeting. In his speech at the meeting, he said: The inaugural meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region has come to a successful conclusion. This is a meeting of unity, a meeting of progress, and the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region will bring more favorable conditions for the development of various undertakings in Tibet. The Dalai Lama's representative, Living Buddha Riqiong Lobsang Yeshe, and the Panchen Erdeni's representative, Living Buddha Ouju, also spoke at the celebration meeting.

　　Tan Guansan, representative of the Tibet Working Committee of the CPC and the Tibet Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, delivered a congratulatory speech at the celebration meeting. He congratulated the Tibetan people on actively creating conditions under the leadership of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region headed by the Dalai Lama, striving to establish a unified Tibet Autonomous Region as soon as possible, and exerting greater strength in building the great socialist motherland.

　　Zhang Jingwu, Deputy Head of the Central Delegation, also spoke.

　　Subsequently, representatives of various people's organizations in Tibet and representatives of the Zong and Xika under the Tibetan Local Government, the Panchen Kanpo Lija Committee, and the Qamdo District People's Liberation Committee presented flags to the meeting, supporting the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

　　The meeting also passed a congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong.

　　Finally, Tibetan opera troupes, song and dance troupes, and the literary and art work team that accompanied the Central Delegation to Tibet performed in the square.

### 庆祝西藏自治区筹委会成立  拉萨三万多藏、汉、回族人民举行大会

1956-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝西藏自治区筹委会成立
　　拉萨三万多藏、汉、回族人民举行大会
    新华社拉萨2日电　布达拉宫前的人民广场，今天装饰得五彩缤纷，拉萨市三万多藏、汉、回族人民在这里热烈的庆祝西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立。从清早起，穿着节日盛装的拉萨市民就从四面八方汇集到广场来。从西藏各地赶来拉萨的十九个藏戏队和歌舞队的演员，也都穿上了戏装。第一工程局第一工程处的五百多名藏族工人，今天怀着兴奋的心情来参加大会。他们在修筑拉萨河堤的时候，为了庆祝自治区筹备委员会的成立，工效比过去提高了将近一倍。许多农民和牧民全家老小从远离拉萨三、四天路程的地方赶来参加大会。
    庆祝大会的两个进口和两旁，竖立了用藏、汉文金字书写的“团结万岁！”、和“西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，是中国共产党民族政策的又一光辉成就”、“进一步加强民族团结和西藏内部团结，为发展西藏的政治、经济、文化建设事业而努力”的巨幅标语。主席台两旁是帐篷式样的长形观礼台，在这里观礼的有自治区筹备委员会的委员、中央代表团的全体团员，以及中共西藏工委各机关负责干部和中国人民解放军西藏军区、西藏地方部队校级以上的军官等。
    正午十二时，庆祝大会在国歌和礼炮声中开始。西藏地方政府代理噶伦噶章·洛桑日增、班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任委员詹东·计晋美、昌都地区人民解放委员会副主任邦达多吉和中共西藏工委组织部副部长白云峰，一起在会场中升起了国旗。今天鸣放的礼炮中，还有西藏的火药炮，这是在隆重的典礼时才用的。庆祝大会由西藏自治区筹备委员会秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美担任主席。他在大会上致词说：西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会胜利闭幕了。这是一个团结的大会，是一个进步的大会，随着西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，即将给西藏各项事业的发展带来更多的有利条件。达赖喇嘛的代表赤江·罗桑益西活佛、班禅额尔德尼的代表欧居活佛也在庆祝大会上讲了话。
    中共西藏工委和人民解放军西藏军区代表谭冠三在庆祝大会上致祝词。他祝贺西藏人民在达赖喇嘛为首的西藏自治区筹备委员会的领导下，积极创造条件，争取早日成立统一的西藏自治区，在建设伟大的社会主义祖国中发挥更大的力量。
    中央代表团副团长张经武也讲了话。
    接着，西藏各人民团体和西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会和昌都地区人民解放委员会所属的宗和溪卡的代表向大会献旗，拥护西藏自治区筹备委员会成立。
    大会还通过了给毛泽东主席的致敬电。
    最后，大会由西藏的藏戏队、歌舞队和随中央代表团赴藏的文艺工作队，在广场上作了演出。
