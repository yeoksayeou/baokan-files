Title: National Advanced Producers Representative Conference Continues; Lai Ruoyu Gives Report on the Issue of Persistently Carrying Out the Advanced Producer Movement
Author:
Date: 1956-05-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The National Advanced Producers Representative Conference representatives listened to a report today by Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

The meeting today began at 2:30 PM.

Lai Ruoyu's report discussed the issue of how to persistently carry out the advanced producer movement. The first part of the report pointed out the significant role of the labor enthusiasm and creativity of all employees in accelerating socialist construction.

In the second, third, and fourth parts of his report, Lai Ruoyu gave detailed explanations of the tasks and principles of socialist competition, the implementation of the "more, faster, better, and more economical" policy in the competition, and the rapid acquisition of culture, science, and technology by the Chinese working class.

The last part of Lai Ruoyu's report specifically addressed the issues of breaking down conservative thinking and opposing bureaucracy, and called on all regions to further strengthen leadership over socialist competition.

This morning, medical worker representatives from the advanced producer delegations of seven provinces and cities, including Guangdong, Guangxi, Heilongjiang, Zhejiang, Fujian, Jiangsu, and Shanghai, and advanced producer representatives from the municipal administration of Beijing and Shanghai, exchanged advanced experiences in their respective industries. Some advanced producer representatives also visited and exchanged advanced experiences with each other this morning.

Fifty advanced producer representatives from the forestry industry and the delegation of advanced producers from the Soviet forestry industry held a discussion meeting in the morning. At the meeting, the representative of the Central Committee of the Soviet Union Forest and Paper Industry Workers' Union presented gifts to the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Forestry Trade Union.

### 全国先进生产者代表会议继续举行  赖若愚作报告谈持久开展先进生产者运动的问题

1956-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国先进生产者代表会议继续举行
　　赖若愚作报告谈持久开展先进生产者运动的问题
    据新华社2日讯　全国先进生产者代表会议代表，今天听取了中华全国总工会主席赖若愚的报告。
    今天的会议是从下午2时半开始的。
    赖若愚的报告讲述了关于如何把先进生产者运动持久地开展下去的问题。报告的第一部分指出了全体职工的劳动积极性和创造性对于加速社会主义建设的重大作用。
    赖若愚在报告的第二、第三和第四部分里，对于社会主义竞赛的任务和原则、在竞赛中贯彻“多、快、好、省”的方针和我国工人阶级迅速掌握文化、科学技术等问题，作了详细说明。
    赖若愚报告的最后一部分特别谈到了打破保守思想、反对官僚主义的问题，要求各地进一步加强对社会主义竞赛的领导。
    今天上午，广东、广西、黑龙江、浙江、福建、江苏、上海等七个省市先进生产者代表团的医务工作者代表，北京、上海两市市政方面的先进生产者代表，都分别交流了自己行业的先进经验。还有一些先进生产者代表，也在今天上午互相进行了访问和交流了先进经验。
    五十个森林工业方面的先进生产者代表和苏联森林工业先进生产者代表团，在上午举行了座谈会。会上苏联森林工业先进生产者代表团代表苏联森林和造纸工业工会中央委员会向中国林业工会筹备委员会送了礼物。
