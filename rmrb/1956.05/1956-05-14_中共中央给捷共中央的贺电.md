Title: CPC Central Committee Congratulatory Message to the Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1956-05-14
Page: 1

CPC Central Committee Congratulatory Message to the Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee
To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia:

    On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm congratulations to you.

    We wish the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia further success in the struggle to build socialism and defend European and world peace.

                    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                             May 12, 1956

### 中共中央给捷共中央的贺电

1956-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央给捷共中央的贺电捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会：
    值此捷克斯洛伐克共产党三十五周年纪念之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向你们致以热烈的祝贺。
    我们谨祝捷克斯洛伐克共产党在建设社会主义和保卫欧洲及世界和平的斗争中，获得进一步的成功。
                    中国共产党中央委员会
                             1956年5月12日
