The state-owned Guangzhou Paper Mill, which is about to be expanded into the largest newsprint manufacturing plant in China, is now accelerating various expansion projects. This is the paper machine which has been basically installed. After this modern paper machine is put into production, the total output of the factory will increase by 70.76% compared to the current level.
