Title: Rapidly Transform Advanced Production Levels into the Production Level of the Whole Society: National Conference of Advanced Producers Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-05-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — The National Conference of Advanced Producers opened in Beijing today. The opening ceremony began at 4 PM.

Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Peng Zhen, Dong Biwu, Zhang Wentian, Peng Dehuai, Kang Sheng, and Deng Xiaoping, attended today's opening ceremony.

Vice Premier of the State Council and Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the National Conference of Advanced Producers, Li Fuchun, delivered the opening speech (see page two).

Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Shaoqi, delivered a congratulatory speech to the conference on behalf of the CPC Central Committee. When he stepped onto the podium, all the representatives burst into warm applause. In his congratulatory speech, Liu Shaoqi said: "On behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, I extend warm congratulations to the National Conference of Advanced Producers, and to the national advanced producers and advanced workers who are attending and not attending this conference!"

In his congratulatory speech, Liu Shaoqi called on every representative and every advanced producer and advanced worker they represent, as well as every advanced element among farmers, handicraftsmen, People's Liberation Army soldiers, and other patriotic people, under the leadership of the Party Central Committee and the State Council, to closely connect with the masses, lead the masses to continuously learn from advanced levels, continuously advance and advance again, and strive to build our country into a great socialist country with strong industry, agriculture, and a high level of culture.

There were a total of 6,156 official representatives and specially invited representatives attending today's meeting. They represent advanced producers of twenty-one ethnic groups across the country, taking an oath here to march towards socialism.

Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers of the State Council Deng Zihui, Li Xiannian, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Li Siguang, Zhang Bojun, heads of various offices, ministries, and commissions of the State Council, as well as heads of various people's organizations, also attended today's opening ceremony.

The Hong Kong and Macao workers' "May Day" sightseeing delegation to Beijing attended the opening ceremony of the meeting today at the invitation of the organizers.

The members of the eleven advanced Soviet producer delegations who were invited to China to impart advanced experience were warmly welcomed by all the representatives at the opening ceremony.

Also attending the meeting today were trade union delegations and representatives from thirty-seven countries who were invited to China to participate in the "May Day" celebrations.

Foreign envoys to China were also invited to attend the opening ceremony today.

The conference venue was set up in the Beijing Gymnasium. At the top of this magnificent building, the national flag of the People's Republic of China and colorful flags were flying. A huge conference commemorative badge was hung above the main entrance of the venue. In the middle of the commemorative badge was engraved the image of the working class marching forward holding a red flag and the four golden characters "Glory to Labor." On a light blue curtain above the rostrum, a portrait of Chairman Mao was hung. Opposite the rostrum, a fighting slogan issued by the conference to the people on all fronts of the country was prominently displayed: "Improve technology, raise technology, learn and master new technologies!" "Do all undertakings more, faster, better, and more economically, and complete the first five-year plan ahead of schedule!" "March towards socialism!"

Tomorrow, the representatives attending the meeting will join people from all walks of life in Beijing to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebration parade in the capital.

### 迅速地把先进的生产水平变为全社会的生产水平  全国先进生产者代表会议在京开幕

1956-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    迅速地把先进的生产水平变为全社会的生产水平
    全国先进生产者代表会议在京开幕
    据新华社30日讯　全国先进生产者代表会议，今天在北京开幕。开幕式在下午四时开始。
    毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、彭真、董必武、张闻天、彭德怀、康生、邓小平，出席了今天的开幕式。
    国务院副总理、全国先进生产者代表会议筹备委员会主任委员李富春致开幕词（见第二版）。
    中共中央书记处书记刘少奇，代表中共中央向大会致祝词。当他走上讲台的时候，全体代表响起了热烈的掌声。刘少奇在祝词里说：“我代表中国共产党中央委员会，向全国先进生产者代表会议，向出席和没有出席这次会议的全国先进生产者和先进工作者，致以热烈的祝贺！”
    刘少奇在祝词中号召每个代表和他们所代表的每个先进生产者和先进工作者，以及农民、手工业者、人民解放军战士和其他爱国人民中的每一个先进分子，在党中央和国务院的领导下，密切地联系群众，带领群众不断地向先进的水平看齐，不断地前进再前进，为把我国建设成为一个伟大的、有强大的工业、农业和有高度文化的社会主义国家而奋斗。
    出席今天会议的正式代表和特邀代表，共有6，156人。他们代表着全国二十一个民族的先进生产者，在这里誓师向社会主义进军。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、郭沫若、陈叔通，国务院副总理邓子恢、李先念，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李济深、黄炎培、李四光、章伯钧，国务院各办公室、各部、各委员会负责人，以及各人民团体的负责人，也出席了今天的开幕式。
    香港、澳门工人“五一”劳动节赴京观光团，今天应邀出席了会议的开幕式。
    应邀来我国传授先进经验的苏联十一个先进生产者代表团人员，在开幕仪式上受到了全体代表的热烈欢迎。
    今天出席会议的还有应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼的三十七个国家的工会代表团和代表。
    各国驻中国使节今天也应邀参加了开幕式。
    大会会场设在北京体育馆。在这个雄伟的建筑物上端，飘扬着中华人民共和国的国旗和各色彩旗。会场正门上端挂着巨大的会议纪念章。在纪念章中间，镌刻着手执红旗前进的工人阶级队伍的形象和“劳动光荣”四个金字。在会场主席台上面一幅浅蓝色的帷幕上挂着毛主席的画像。主席台对面高悬着会议向全国各个战线上的人们发出的战斗口号：“改进技术、提高技术，学习与掌握新技术！”“把各项事业做得又多、又快、又好、又省，提前完成第一个五年计划！”“向社会主义进军！”
    明天，出席会议的代表将和北京各界人民一起，参加首都的“五一”国际劳动节的庆祝游行。
