Title: Extending Brotherly Congratulations: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Trade Unions of Various Countries Exchange Greetings for May Day

Author:

Date: 1956-05-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:
    Extending Brotherly Congratulations
    All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Trade Unions of Various Countries Exchange Greetings for May Day

    Xinhua News Agency: On the eve of May Day, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent telegrams to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions of the Soviet Union, the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Mongolia, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Poland, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia, the Free German Trade Union Federation, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Romania, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Hungary, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Bulgaria, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Albania, and the Confederation of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia, congratulating them on International Labor Day.

    The telegrams warmly congratulated the workers and people of various countries on the brilliant achievements they have made in the cause of socialist construction, and wished the workers and people of various countries even greater achievements in building socialism and safeguarding world peace.

    Xinhua News Agency: On the eve of International Labor Day, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions successively received congratulatory telegrams from trade union organizations in the Soviet Union, Poland, Romania, Albania, Korea, Mongolia, Vietnam, and Burma, wishing the Chinese working people new achievements in the struggle to build socialism and strive for lasting peace.

### 致以兄弟般的祝贺  我总工会和各国工会互相电贺“五一”节

1956-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    致以兄弟般的祝贺
    我总工会和各国工会互相电贺“五一”节
    新华社讯　中华全国总工会在“五一”节前夕，打电报给全苏工会中央理事会、朝鲜职业总同盟、越南总工会、蒙古工会中央理事会、波兰工会中央理事会、捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会、自由德国工会联合会、罗马尼亚工会中央理事会、匈牙利工会中央理事会、保加利亚工会中央理事会、阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会、南斯拉夫工会联合会，祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。
    电文中热烈祝贺各国工人和人民在社会主义建设事业中所取得的光辉成就，并预祝各国工人和人民在建设社会主义、在维护世界和平的斗争中，取得更大的成就。
    新华社讯　中华全国总工会在“五一”国际劳动节前夕先后收到苏联、波兰、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚、朝鲜、蒙古、越南和缅甸工会组织拍来的贺电，祝中国劳动人民在建设社会主义和争取持久和平的斗争中取得新的成就。
