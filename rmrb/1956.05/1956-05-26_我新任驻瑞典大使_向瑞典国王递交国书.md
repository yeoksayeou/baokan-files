Title: Our New Ambassador to Sweden Presents Credentials to the Swedish King
Author:
Date: 1956-05-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 25th: Han Nianlong, the newly appointed Chinese Ambassador to Sweden, presented his credentials to King Gustaf VI Adolf of Sweden on May 24th. After the presentation of credentials, the King had a brief conversation with the Ambassador.

### 我新任驻瑞典大使  向瑞典国王递交国书

1956-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    我新任驻瑞典大使
    向瑞典国王递交国书
    新华社布拉格25日电　新任中国驻瑞典大使韩念龙五月二十四日向瑞典国王古斯塔夫六世·阿道尔夫递交国书。递交国书后，国王与大使进行了简短的谈话。
