Title: Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region Establishes and Starts Operation of its General Office
Author:
Date: 1956-05-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 14th. The Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region held a meeting this afternoon to formally establish the General Office of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, and began operations today. The General Office has three sections: Secretariat, Translation and Compilation, and General Affairs.

The meeting was presided over by Tenzin Gyatso, Director of the General Office. Before the meeting, Ape A-wang Jin-mei, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, Deputy Secretaries-General Lamin Yexi Chuchen, Bonda Toji, and Chen Jingbo, first studied the issue of staffing the sections and units of the General Office with Tudeng Lie-men, Director of the Civil Affairs Department of the Preparatory Committee, and Yang Dongsheng, Deputy Director.

At the meeting, Ape A-wang Jin-mei announced the list of heads and staff members of the various sections and units of the General Office. He said: The General Office is the first agency established among the fourteen institutions affiliated with our Autonomous Region Preparatory Committee, and it is an agency that connects the upper and lower levels and does a good job of internal and external liaison. He requested all Tibetan and Han cadres working in the General Office to take care of the overall situation under the unified leadership of the Autonomous Region Preparatory Committee and do a good job in unity. He said: Strengthening mutual assistance and learning is the basis for doing all work well.

### 西藏自治区筹备委员会成立办公厅开始办公

1956-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏自治区筹备委员会成立办公厅开始办公
    新华社拉萨14日电　西藏自治区筹备委员会在今天下午举行会议，正式成立西藏自治区筹委会办公厅，并从今天开始办公。这个办公厅下设秘书、编译和总务等三个科。
    这次会议由办公厅主任丹增嘉措主持。在开会以前，西藏自治区筹委会秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美，副秘书长拉敏·益喜楚臣、邦达多吉、陈竞波等，先和筹委会民政处长土登列门、副处长杨东生研究了办公厅各科股的干部配备问题。
    会上，阿沛·阿旺晋美宣布了办公厅各科股负责人和工作人员名单。他讲话说：办公厅是我们自治区筹委会所属十四个机构中成立的头一个机关，是承上启下作好内外联系的机关。他要求办公厅全体藏、汉族工作干部在统一的自治区筹委会的领导下照顾全面，作好团结工作。他说：加强互相帮助和学习，是作好一切工作的基础。
