Title: Japanese Painters Toshiko Akamatsu and Iri Maruki Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-05-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Japanese peace activists and renowned painters Toshiko Akamatsu and Iri Maruki, who were invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to visit China, arrived in Beijing today.

### 日本画家赤松俊子、丸木位里到京

1956-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

    日本画家赤松俊子、丸木位里到京
    新华社16日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的日本和平人士、著名画家赤松俊子和丸木位里在今天到达北京。
