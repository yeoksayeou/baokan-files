Title: U.S. Prepares to Seize More Bases in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1956-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd dispatch from New York: According to the "New York Post," the United States is negotiating with the Chiang Kai-shek clique to acquire more bases in Taiwan in order to strengthen the U.S. military occupation of Taiwan.

This information was disclosed by Crichlow, Deputy Director of the U.S. Navy's Coastal Facilities Department, to the House Appropriations Committee. U.S. naval authorities are requesting an appropriation of $500,000 for the U.S. military command in Taiwan. Crichlow revealed that part of this money will be used to establish a fairly large communications system on the coastal islands.

Crichlow said that the U.S. and Chiang are expected to reach an agreement on these issues before July 1.

### 美国准备在台湾占取更多基地

1956-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    美国准备在台湾占取更多基地
    新华社22日讯　纽约消息：据“纽约邮报”报道，美国正在同蒋介石集团进行谈判，以便在台湾取得更多的基地，来加强美国对台湾的军事侵占。
    这个情报是美国海军海岸设施部门的副主任克里奇顿告诉众议院拨款委员会的。美国海军当局正在要求拨款五十万美元给台湾美军司令部。克里奇顿透露，这笔钱的一部分将用在沿海岛屿上设立一个规模相当大的通讯系统。
    克里奇顿说，美蒋之间可望在七月一日以前就这些问题达成协议。
