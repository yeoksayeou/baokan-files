Title: Vietnamese Consul-General to India Takes Office
Author:
Date: 1956-05-30
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 29th: Nguyen Khac Thach, the first Consul-General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to India, along with his entire staff, departed from Hanoi on the 28th to take office in New Delhi. India established a Consulate-General in Hanoi in February of last year.

### 越南驻印总领事赴任

1956-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    越南驻印总领事赴任
    新华社河内29日电　越南民主共和国派驻印度首任总领事阮基石，偕同全体随员已经在二十八日从河内启程前往新德里就任。印度在去年二月间已经在河内设立总领事馆。
