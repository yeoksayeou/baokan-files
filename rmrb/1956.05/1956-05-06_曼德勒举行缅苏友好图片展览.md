Title: Burmese-Soviet Friendship Photo Exhibition Held in Mandalay
Author:
Date: 1956-05-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, May 5th: The Mandalay Burmese newspaper "Bahosi Daily" reported today that the Burmese-Soviet Friendship Photo Exhibition, initiated by the Burmese-Soviet Cultural Association, was held in Mandalay on May Day.

　　The exhibition displayed pictures of U Nu's visit to the Soviet Union, as well as Soviet sports, industry, and agriculture.

### 曼德勒举行缅苏友好图片展览

1956-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    曼德勒举行缅苏友好图片展览
    新华社仰光5日电　曼德勒的缅文报纸“巴霍西报”今天报道，曼德勒在“五一”节举行了缅苏文化协会发起的缅苏友好图片展览会。
    展览会上展出的有吴努访苏、苏联运动、工业和农业方面的图片。
