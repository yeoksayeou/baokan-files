Title: Soviet-Thai Legations Upgraded

Author:

Date: 1956-05-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 5th. Delhi Radio broadcast on May 4th that Thailand and the Soviet Union have agreed to upgrade their respective legations to embassies.

### 苏泰两国公使馆升格

1956-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    苏泰两国公使馆升格
    新华社新德里5日电　据德里电台五月四日广播，泰国和苏联已经同意相互把驻对方的公使馆升格为大使馆。
