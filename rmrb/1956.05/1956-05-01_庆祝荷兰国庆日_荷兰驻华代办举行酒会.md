Title: Celebrating Dutch National Day, Dutch Chargé d'Affaires in China Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1956-05-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Today is Dutch National Day. Dutch Chargé d'Affaires in China, Mr. S.H. de Vries, held a reception this afternoon in celebration.
    Among those invited to the reception were Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department.
    Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials of various countries in China were also invited to the reception.
    The reception took place in a friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝荷兰国庆日  荷兰驻华代办举行酒会

1956-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝荷兰国庆日
    荷兰驻华代办举行酒会
    新华社30日讯　今天是荷兰国庆日。荷兰驻华代办司曼在下午举行了庆祝酒会。
    应邀参加酒会的有中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华，礼宾司司长柯华等。
    应邀参加酒会的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    酒会在友好的空气中进行。
