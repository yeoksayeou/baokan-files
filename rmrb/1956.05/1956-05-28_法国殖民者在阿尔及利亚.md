French Colonialists in Algeria
Troop Increase Reaches 400,000

    According to a United Press International dispatch from Algiers: The number of French troops engaged in large-scale military suppression operations in Algeria has increased to nearly 400,000. French Governor-General of Algeria, Lacoste, also admitted on the 23rd that French troops are constantly being sent to Algeria.

Mass Arrests of Patriots

    On the 24th and 25th, French police in Algeria carried out large-scale arrests of leaders of the nationalist movement in Algiers and Oran. Seventy people were arrested in Algiers. Sources say that all leaders of the General Union of Muslim Workers were arrested. Many people were also arrested in Oran.

Closure of Nationalist Magazine

    French Governor-General of Algeria, Lacoste, ordered the prohibition of the continued publication of the weekly "Algerian Worker" on the 25th. This weekly was the last remaining nationalist magazine in Algiers.

    On the same day, Lacoste's office also announced regulations strictly controlling news reports on the situation in Algeria. The regulations stipulate that journalists may not report on the number of French military casualties without the permission of the French authorities. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 法国殖民者在阿尔及利亚

1956-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    法国殖民者在阿尔及利亚
    增兵已达四十万
    据合众社从阿尔及尔发出的一条消息说：在阿尔及利亚从事大规模军事镇压行动的法国军队，已经增加到了将近四十万人。法国驻阿尔及利亚总督拉戈斯特也在二十三日承认，法国军队正在不断向阿尔及利亚增兵。
    大批逮捕爱国者
    阿尔及利亚法国警察二十四日和二十五日在阿尔及尔和阿兰大举逮捕民族主义运动的领袖。在阿尔及尔有七十人被捕。有消息说，回教工人总工会全体领导人员都被逮捕。在阿兰也逮捕了许多人。
    封闭民族主义者杂志
    法国驻阿尔及利亚总督拉戈斯特二十五日下令禁止“阿尔及利亚工人”周刊继续出版，这家周刊是阿尔及尔剩下的最后一家民族主义者的杂志。
    拉戈斯特办公室在同一天并且公布了严格控制关于阿尔及利亚局势的新闻报道的条例。条例里规定，不得法国当局允许，记者不得报道法军伤亡人数。（新华社）
