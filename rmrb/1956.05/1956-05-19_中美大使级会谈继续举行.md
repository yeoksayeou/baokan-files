Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 17th - The Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks held their forty-seventh meeting at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 AM on May 17th. The meeting adjourned at 12:00 PM noon. The next meeting will be held on May 24th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦17日电　中美两国大使级会谈五月十七日上午十时在国联大厦举行第四十七次会议，会议在中午十二时休会，下次会议将在五月二十四日举行。
