Title: China to Supply Pakistan with 300,000 Tons of Coal
Author:
Date: 1956-05-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, May 11th: China will supply Pakistan with 300,000 tons of coal at a fair and reasonable price.
    The contract for the supply of Chinese coal was signed in Karachi on May 10th. Representing the Pakistani side was Wahid, Director of the Coal Bureau of the Pakistani Ministry of Commerce and Industry; representing the Chinese side was Qin Shenzhi, First Secretary of the Chinese Embassy.
    In April of this year, China and Pakistan also signed a contract to supply Pakistan with 125,000 tons of coal.

### 我国将供应巴基斯坦三十万吨煤

1956-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我国将供应巴基斯坦三十万吨煤
    新华社喀喇蚩11日电　中国将以公平合理的价格供应巴基斯坦三十万吨煤。
    五月十日在喀喇蚩签订了这项供应中国煤的合同。代表巴基斯坦方面签字的是巴基斯坦商务和工业部煤炭局局长华希德；代表中国方面签字的是中国大使馆一等秘书秦慎之。
    在今年四月，中国同巴基斯坦也曾经签订了一个供应十二万五千吨煤给巴基斯坦的合同。
