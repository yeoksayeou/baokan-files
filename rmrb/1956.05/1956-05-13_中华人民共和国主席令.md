Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1956-05-13
Page: 1

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    The Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China adopted the Decision on the Number of Deputies to People's Congresses of Counties, Cities, Municipal Districts, Townships, Ethnic Townships, and Towns at its 40th meeting on May 12, 1956, which is hereby promulgated.
            Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                             May 12, 1956

### 中华人民共和国主席令

1956-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于1956年5月12日第四十次会议通过了关于县、市、市辖区、乡、民族乡、镇人民代表大会代表名额等问题的决定，现予公布。
            中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                             1956年5月12日
