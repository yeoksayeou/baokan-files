Title: Kim Il-sung to Visit Albania at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-05-27
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th. It is announced here that Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, will lead a government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to Albania for a friendly visit at the invitation of the People's Republic of Albania.

### 金日成应邀将访问阿尔巴尼亚

1956-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成应邀将访问阿尔巴尼亚
    新华社平壤26日电　据此间宣布，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成应阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的邀请，将率领朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团到阿尔巴尼亚进行友好访问。
