Title: Jiang Shujun Sets New Women's 3,000 Meter Cycling Record
Author:
Date: 1956-05-07
Page: 3

Sports News

　　Jiang Shujun Sets New Women's 3,000 Meter Cycling Record
    Jiang Shujun, the young female athlete who just broke the national record for the women's 1,500-meter cycling race on the 2nd, broke Li Fengqin's national record of six minutes and fourteen seconds point four for the women's 3,000-meter cycling race, which was set at the 1955 Liaoning Province Six City Cross-country and Cycling Competition, with an excellent time of five minutes and fifty-six seconds point two at the Chongqing City First Workers' Games on the afternoon of the 5th. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 蒋淑筠创女子三千公尺自行车新纪录

1956-05-07
第3版()
专栏：体育消息

　　蒋淑筠创女子三千公尺自行车新纪录
    二日刚刚打破女子一千五百公尺自行车比赛全国最高纪录的女青年运动员蒋淑筠，五日下午又在重庆市第一届工人体育运动大会上，以五分五十六秒二的优异成绩，打破了李凤琴在1955年辽宁省六市越野、自行车比赛大会上所创造的六分十四秒四的女子三千公尺自行车比赛全国最高纪录。　（新华社）
