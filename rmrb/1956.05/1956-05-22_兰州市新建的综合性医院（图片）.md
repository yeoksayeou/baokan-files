Title: Newly built comprehensive hospital in Lanzhou (picture)
Author: Hu Yue
Date: 1956-05-22
Page: 3

    Lanzhou's Newly Built Comprehensive Hospital
    A modern comprehensive hospital, the Gansu Provincial No. 1 People's Hospital, has been completed in Lanzhou and officially began accepting patients in early May. It is currently the largest and best-equipped hospital in Gansu Province. The picture shows a bright and spacious pediatric ward. Xinhua News Agency reporter Hu Yue.

### 兰州市新建的综合性医院（图片）
胡越
1956-05-22
第3版()
专栏：

    兰州市新建的综合性医院
    一所现代化的综合性医院——甘肃省第一人民医院已在兰州市建成，并在五月初正式接纳病人。这是目前甘肃省规模最大、设备最完善的一所医院。图为明亮宽敞的小儿科病房。　新华社记者　胡越摄
