Title: U.S. Insists on Testing Hydrogen Bombs in the Pacific
Author:
Date: 1956-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch. TASS Washington, 1st dispatch: A representative of the U.S. Atomic Energy Commission said on April 30th that the hydrogen bomb test in the Pacific would be held on May 8th as originally planned.

### 美国坚持要在太平洋试验氢弹

1956-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国坚持要在太平洋试验氢弹
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社华盛顿一日讯：美国原子能委员会的一个代表在四月三十日说，太平洋中的氢弹试验将按照原订计划在今年五月八日举行。
