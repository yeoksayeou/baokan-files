Title: The Third Congress of the Workers' Party of Korea Concludes: Kim Il-sung Elected Chairman of the New Central Committee, Choi Yong-kun and Others as Vice-Chairmen
Author:
Date: 1956-05-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th -- The Third Congress of the Workers' Party of Korea concluded on the 29th after a seven-day session.

    At the meeting on the 29th, the election of the new central organs of the Workers' Party was conducted. As a result of the election, Kim Il-sung, Kim Tu-bong, Choi Yong-kun, Park Jung-ai, Kim Il, Park Kum-chol and 71 others were elected as members of the Central Committee, and 45 as alternate members; Li Ju-yeon and 17 others were elected as members of the Central Auditing Committee.

    Finally, Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, delivered the closing address at the meeting. He called on party organizations at all levels and all party members to take the historical decisions of this congress as the program of struggle for their work, to study them seriously and actively put them into practice. At the same time, party organizations at all levels and all party members must be loyal to the doctrines of Marxism-Leninism, strengthen internationalist friendship and unity with the people of the Soviet Union, China, all people's democratic countries, and all countries and peace-loving people who support the unification and independence of Korea; and must further promote the cause of striving for lasting world peace.

    In his address, Kim Il-sung thanked the representatives of the fraternal parties who attended the congress on behalf of the congress and all party members.

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, elected by the Third Congress, held a plenary meeting on April 29th and elected the members of the various organs of the Central Committee.

    The members of the Standing Committee of the newly elected Central Committee are: Kim Il-sung, Kim Tu-bong, Choi Yong-kun, Park Jung-ai, Kim Il, Park Kum-chol, Lim Hae, Choi Chang-ik, Jung Il-ryong, Kim Kwang-hyup, Nam Il. The alternate members of the Standing Committee are: Kim Chang-man, Li Jong-ok, Li Hyo-soon, Park Ui-wan.

    Kim Il-sung is the Chairman of the Central Committee, and the Vice-Chairmen are: Choi Yong-kun, Park Jung-ai, Park Kum-chol, Jung Il-ryong, Kim Chang-man.

    The members of the Organization Committee are: Kim Il-sung, Choi Yong-kun, Park Jung-ai, Park Kum-chol, Jung Il-ryong, Kim Chang-man, Han Sang-du.

    Kim Ik-sun is the Chairman of the Inspection Committee.

    On the same day, the Central Auditing Committee of the Workers' Party held a plenary meeting and elected Li Ju-yeon as Chairman.

### 朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会闭幕  新的中央委员会选出金日成为委员长崔庸健等为副委员长

1956-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会闭幕
    新的中央委员会选出金日成为委员长崔庸健等为副委员长
    新华社平壤30日电　朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会在进行了七天会议以后，在二十九日闭幕。
    在二十九日的会议上，进行了劳动党新的中央机构的选举。选举结果，选出了金日成、金枓奉、崔庸健、朴正爱、金一、朴金喆等七十一名中央委员会委员和四十五名候补委员；选出了李周渊等十七人为中央检查委员会委员。
    最后，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成在会上致闭幕词。他号召劳动党各级组织和全体党员，把这次大会的历史性的决定作为自己工作的斗争纲领，认真地加以研究并且积极付诸实践。同时，各级党组织和全体党员必须忠实于马克思列宁主义的学说，同苏联、中国、各人民民主国家人民和支援朝鲜统一独立的一切国家和爱好和平的人民加强国际主义友谊团结；必须进一步促进争取世界持久和平的事业。
    金日成在致词中，以代表大会和全体劳动党员的名义向参加这次大会的各兄弟党的代表致谢。
    新华社平壤30日电　朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会选出的中央委员会四月二十九日召开全体会议，选出了中央委员会各项机构的成员。
    新选出的中央委员会常务委员会的委员是：金日成、金枓奉、崔庸健、朴正爱、金一、朴金喆、林海、崔昌益、郑一龙、金光侠、南日。常务委员会的候补委员是：金昌满、李锺玉、李孝淳、朴义琓。
    中央委员会委员长是金日成，副委员长是：崔庸健、朴正爱、朴金喆、郑一龙、金昌满。
    组织委员会的成员是：金日成、崔庸健、朴正爱、朴金喆、郑一龙、金昌满、韩相斗。
    检阅委员会的委员长是金翊善。
    同日，劳动党中央检查委员会举行全体会议，选出李周渊为委员长。
