Title: Twelve National Organizations Prepare to Form Sino-Pakistani Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1956-05-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 27th: Twelve national people's organizations in China, in order to promote cultural exchange and friendly relations between the people of China and Pakistan, initiated the establishment of the "China-Pakistan Friendship Association" in Beijing on the 25th.

The twelve initiating organizations are: Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, China Islamic Association, Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, All-China Sports Federation, Chinese Medical Association, China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, All-China Federation of Trade Unions, All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and All-China Youth Federation.

Representatives of the initiating organizations held a preparatory meeting on the afternoon of the 25th. Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, presided over the meeting. He pointed out that the friendly relations and cultural ties between China and Pakistan have been constantly developing in recent years, especially after the Asian-African Conference. The establishment of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association will help further develop the friendly relations and cultural ties between the people of China and Pakistan.

The preparatory meeting unanimously adopted the charter of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association and elected Liu Geping as President of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association, and Iminov and Qian Duansheng as Vice Presidents. The 32 directors elected at the meeting are: Wang Chuanbin, Fang Shishan, Wen Shizhen, Liu Geping, Liu Qingyang, Zhu Shilun, Iminov, Li Wentao, Li Menghua, Wu Hongye, Sha Ting, Zheng Boqi, Rashida, Chen Ming, Chen Jingyu, Hou Debang, Gong Naiquan, Luo Jingyi, Ouyang Shan, Ma Jia, Ma Chungu, Ma Teng'ai, Yuan Bo, Cambol Khan, Zhang Jinyi, Huang Ganying, Zhang Jie, Zhang Qian, Zhang Mingyang, Yang Jingren, Qian Duansheng, Zhong Huilan.

After the preparatory meeting, the first board meeting of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association was held, presided over by Vice President Qian Duansheng. The board elected Director Luo Jingyi as the association's Secretary-General and discussed the association's future work.

The China-Pakistan Friendship Association will hold its inaugural meeting regularly.

### 十二个全国性团体筹组中巴友好协会

1956-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    十二个全国性团体筹组中巴友好协会
    新华社27日讯　中国十二个全国性的人民团体为了促进中国和巴基斯坦两国人民之间的文化交流和友好关系，二十五日在北京发起筹组“中巴友好协会”。
    这十二个发起的团体是：中国人民对外文化协会、中国伊斯兰教协会、中国人民外交学会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国体育总会、中华医学会、中国国际贸易促进委员会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会和中华全国民主青年联合会。
    各发起团体的代表在二十五日下午举行了筹备会议。中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南主持会议。他在会上指出，中巴两国间的友好关系和文化关系在最近几年来，特别是亚非会议之后，不断得到发展。中巴友好协会的成立，将有助于中巴两国人民的友好关系和文化联系的进一步发展。
    筹备会议一致通过了中巴友好协会的章程，并且选出了刘格平为中巴友好协会会长，伊敏诺夫、钱端升为副会长。会议选出的理事三十二人是：王传斌、方石珊、文士桢、刘格平、刘清扬、朱世纶、伊敏诺夫、李文涛、李梦华、吴鸿业、沙汀、郑伯奇、拉希达、陈明、陈经畬、侯德榜、宫乃泉、罗静宜、欧阳山、马加、马纯古、马腾霭、袁勃、康巴尔汗、章靳以、黄甘英、张杰、张茜、张明养、杨静仁、钱端升、钟惠澜。
    筹备会议以后，中巴友好协会随即由副会长钱端升主持举行了第一次理事会，推选出理事罗静宜为协会总干事，并且商讨了今后的会务工作。
    中巴友好协会将定期举行成立大会。
