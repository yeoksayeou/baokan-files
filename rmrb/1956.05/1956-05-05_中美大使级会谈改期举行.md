Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Rescheduled
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 4th—Due to administrative reasons, the ambassadors of the United States and China, who are holding ambassadorial talks here, have agreed to reschedule their meeting originally scheduled for May 5th to May 11th (next Friday).

### 中美大使级会谈改期举行

1956-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈改期举行
    新华社日内瓦4日电　在这里举行中美两国大使级会谈的两国大使因为行政性原因，同意把他们原定五月五日举行的会议改期到五月十一日（下星期五）举行。
