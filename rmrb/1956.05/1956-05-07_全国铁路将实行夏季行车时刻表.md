Title: National Railways to Implement Summer Timetable

Author:

Date: 1956-05-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch

　　National Railways to Implement Summer Timetable
　　The national railways will implement a new summer train timetable starting from 00:00 on May 11th.

　　The summer timetable stipulates the addition of special express passenger trains between Beijing and Shenyang, Beijing and Shanghai, and Beijing and Hankou. It also specifies all passenger express trains or direct through trains from Beijing to major provinces and cities without requiring transfers.

　　The total travel time for all special express passenger trains, passenger express trains, and ordinary passenger trains stipulated in the summer timetable has been greatly reduced. The special express train from Beijing to Shenyang will take only 14 hours and 7 minutes for the entire journey, a reduction of 3 hours and 38 minutes compared to the current schedule. The special express train from Beijing to Shanghai will take 28 hours and 34 minutes for the entire journey, a reduction of 5 hours and 22 minutes. The special express train from Beijing to Hankou will take 27 hours and 30 minutes for the entire journey, a reduction of 2 hours and 25 minutes. The total travel time for other trains has also been reduced to varying degrees. The number of suburban passenger trains stipulated in the summer timetable has also increased by 152% compared to the current schedule.

　　The summer timetable also improves services for international through passenger trains. The total travel time for the Beijing-Moscow international train from Beijing to Manchuria is 45 hours and 5 minutes, a reduction of 50 minutes compared to the current schedule. The total travel time for the Beijing-Pyongyang international train from Beijing to Andong is 22 hours and 10 minutes, a reduction of 2 hours and 5 minutes. The total travel time for the Beijing-Ulaanbaatar international through train from Beijing to Erlian is 29 hours and 15 minutes, a reduction of 3 hours and 25 minutes. The total travel time for the Beijing-Hanoi international through train from Beijing to Pingxiang is 70 hours and 55 minutes, a reduction of 16 hours and 35 minutes.

　　The summer timetable increases the average travel speed of freight trains on the entire railway by one to three kilometers compared to the current schedule. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国铁路将实行夏季行车时刻表

1956-05-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国铁路将实行夏季行车时刻表
    全国铁路从五月十一日零点起实行新的夏季火车时刻表。
    夏季时刻表中，规定增加了北京沈阳间、北京上海间、北京汉口间的旅客特别快车，并且规定了由北京到各主要省市的各次旅客快车或不换乘的直通客车。
    夏季时刻表中所规定的各次旅客特别快车、旅客快车和一般旅客列车的全程旅行时间大大缩短了。从北京到沈阳的特别快车，全程只要十四小时零七分，比现行缩短了三小时三十八分；北京到上海的特别快车，全程为二十八小时三十四分，缩短了五小时二十二分；北京到汉口的特别快车，全程为二十七小时三十分，缩短了两小时二十五分。其他各次列车的全程旅行时间也都有不同程度的缩短。夏季时刻表中所规定的近郊旅客列车数，也比现行增加了152％。
    夏季时刻表中对于国际联运旅客列车的服务也有所改善。北京莫斯科国际列车由北京到满洲里的全程旅行时间，是四十五小时零五分，比现行缩短五十分；北京平壤国际列车由北京到安东的全程旅行时间，是二十二小时十分，比现行缩短两小时零五分；北京乌兰巴托国际联运车由北京到二连的全程旅行时间，是二十九小时十五分，比现行缩短三小时二十五分；北京河内国际联运车由北京到凭祥的全程旅行时间，是七十小时五十五分，比现行缩短十六小时三十五分。
    夏季时刻表中对全路货物列车的旅行速度，平均比现行提高了一到三公里。　（据新华社讯）
