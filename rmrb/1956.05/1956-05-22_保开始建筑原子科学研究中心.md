Title: Construction Begins on Bulgarian Atomic Science Research Center
Author:
Date: 1956-05-22
Page: 4

    Bulgarian Atomic Science Research Center Begins Construction
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 21st. According to a report by the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency on the 20th, construction has begun on an atomic science research center near Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria. Bulgarian scientists will conduct research in nuclear physics there.

### 保开始建筑原子科学研究中心

1956-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    保开始建筑原子科学研究中心
    新华社布拉格21日电　据保加利亚通讯社二十日报道，保加利亚首都索非亚附近已经开始建筑一个原子科学研究中心。保加利亚科学家将在那里进行核子物理学的研究工作。
