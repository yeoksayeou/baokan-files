Title: Egypt Decides to Withdraw Recognition of the Chiang Kai-shek Clique Chiang Kai-shek Clique Embarrassedly Announces Severance of "Diplomatic Relations" with Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 18th—The Cabinet of the Republic of Egypt passed a resolution on the evening of May 16, 1956, deciding to withdraw recognition of the Chiang Kai-shek clique in Taiwan. At the same time, the Cabinet decided to recognize the People's Republic of China and expressed its desire to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic envoys. Mr. Far, the Commercial Representative of the Republic of Egypt to our country, notified the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the full text of this decision on the 18th.

Xinhua News Agency, May 18th—According to a report by the United Press, after the Egyptian government formally recognized the People's Republic of China, the Chiang Kai-shek clique in Taiwan fell into a very embarrassing situation. The Chiang gang's "Ministry of Foreign Affairs" issued a statement on May 17, announcing the severance of its "diplomatic relations" with the Republic of Egypt.

### 埃及决定撤回对蒋介石集团的承认  蒋介石集团狼狈宣布同埃及断绝“外交关系”

1956-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及决定撤回对蒋介石集团的承认
    蒋介石集团狼狈宣布同埃及断绝“外交关系”
    新华社18日讯　埃及共和国内阁会议在1956年5月16日晚上通过决议，决定撤回对台湾蒋介石集团的承认。同时，内阁会议决定承认中华人民共和国并表示了建立外交关系和交换外交使节的愿望。埃及共和国驻我国商务代表法尔先生将这一决定的全文于十八日通知了中国外交部。
    新华社18日讯　据合众社报道，在埃及政府正式承认中华人民共和国以后，台湾蒋介石集团陷于十分狼狈的境地。蒋帮“外交部”五月十七日发表声明，宣布断绝它同埃及共和国的“外交关系”。
