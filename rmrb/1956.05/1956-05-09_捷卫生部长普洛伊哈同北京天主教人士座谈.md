Title: Czechoslovakian Minister of Health Plojhar Holds Discussions with Beijing Catholics
Author:
Date: 1956-05-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Czechoslovakian Minister of Health and Chairman of the Czechoslovak People's Party, Father Plojhar, held a discussion today with Catholic priests and Catholic followers in Beijing.

At the discussion, Plojhar said that he hopes that the Catholic Churches of Czechoslovakia and China will closely unite, strengthen ties, and work together to safeguard world peace.

Plojhar introduced the situation of the Catholic Church in Czechoslovakia to the Chinese clergy present and discussed some issues concerning Catholic dogma and church affairs with Chinese Father Wang Ruji, Father Ma Wenchun, and Jia Zhenmin, head of the Beijing Catholic Patriotic Organization.

### 捷卫生部长普洛伊哈同北京天主教人士座谈

1956-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷卫生部长普洛伊哈同北京天主教人士座谈
    据新华社7日讯  捷克斯洛伐克卫生部长、捷人民党主席普洛伊哈神父今天同北京的天主教神父和天主教教徒们举行座谈会。
    普洛伊哈在座谈会上说，他希望捷中两国的天主教会紧密地团结，加强联系，共同为保卫世界和平而努力。
    普洛伊哈向到会的中国神长们介绍了捷克斯洛伐克天主教会的情况，和中国的王汝楫神父、马文纯神父以及北京市天主教爱国组织负责人贾振民等交谈了天主教教义和教务方面的一些问题。
