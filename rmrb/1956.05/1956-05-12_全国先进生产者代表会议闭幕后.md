Title: After the Closing of the National Conference of Advanced Producers

Author:

Date: 1956-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: After the closing of the National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives, various departments separately received the representatives.

Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, He Long, Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission, Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education, and Huang Songling, Deputy Minister of Higher Education, today separately received advanced producer representatives from the fields of science, sports, and education who attended the National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives, and held discussions with them.

Various bureaus under the Ministry of Culture also began today to invite advanced producer representatives from the cultural system who attended the National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives to hold discussions.

Women's Federation and Others Convene Symposiums

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The six hundred and eighty-three female representatives who attended the National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives today participated in a symposium jointly convened by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and the Women Workers Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, spoke at the meeting. She encouraged all female representatives to continue to be leaders, promote advanced experiences, achieve greater, more numerous, and more outstanding achievements, more closely connect with the masses, rely on the masses, and unite more closely around the Party.

Yang Zhihua, Minister of the Women Workers Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, in her speech, requested that the female representatives study diligently, actively participate in the ranks advancing towards culture and science, and strive to overcome the unique difficulties and problems faced by women.

Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Chen Shaomin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, also met with the representatives.

At today's meeting, after all the female representatives listened to the report of Wu Ping, the Chinese representative who attended the preparatory meeting of the World Women Workers' Representatives Conference in Bucharest, they also adopted a telegram to the Preparatory Committee of the World Women Workers' Representatives Conference, expressing warm support and heartfelt endorsement for the convening of the World Women Workers' Representatives Conference.

In the afternoon, some of the female representatives and family representatives participated in group discussions to exchange work experiences.

Children Hold a Get-Together with the Representatives

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Tonight at the Children's Palace Square, more than three thousand children from Beijing held a get-together with the representatives who attended the National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives.

When more than three hundred and forty advanced producers entered, the children jumped up and surrounded them, holding them tightly. A representative of the Young Pioneers said: When we grow up, we must be like our uncles and aunts and become glorious advanced producers.

More than a hundred small circles were formed on the square. The advanced producers enthusiastically told the children about their advanced deeds. Many children took out small notebooks to ask the advanced producers for autographs. Tan Baocheng, an advanced producer and Xiang opera actor, and Wang Sujun, a Henan opera actor, also sang for the children. Writer Lao She enthusiastically encouraged the children, saying: The future advanced producers are you, and each of you will become even better advanced producers than us in the future, building the motherland even more beautifully than it is today.

### 全国先进生产者代表会议闭幕后

1956-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    全国先进生产者代表会议闭幕后
    各部门分别接见代表
    新华社11日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若、体育运动委员会主任贺龙、教育部部长张奚若、高等教育部副部长黄松龄在今天分别接见了出席全国先进生产者代表会议的科学、体育和教育方面的先进生产者代表，并且同他们进行了座谈。
    文化部所属各局也从今天起分别邀请出席全国先进生产者代表会议的文化系统的先进生产者代表，进行座谈。
    妇联等召集座谈会
    新华社11日讯　出席全国先进生产者代表会议的六百八十三位女代表，今天参加了中华全国民主妇女联合会和中华全国总工会女工部联合召集的座谈会。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超在会上讲了话。她鼓励全体女代表继续做带头人，推广先进经验，做出更大更多更出色的成绩，更加密切地联系群众，依靠群众，更紧密地团结在党的周围。
    中华全国总工会女工部部长杨之华在讲话中要求女代表们努力学习，积极参加向文化和科学进军的队伍，努力克服女性所特有的困难问题。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅和中华全国总工会书记处书记陈少敏也和代表们见了面。
    在今天的会上，全体女代表听取了出席世界女工代表会议布加勒斯特筹备会议的中国代表吴平的传达报告以后，还通过了致世界女工代表会议筹备委员会的电文，表示热烈支持和衷心拥护世界女工代表会议的召开。
    下午，一部分女代表和家属代表参加了分组座谈会，交流工作经验。
  少年儿童同代表联欢
    新华社11日讯　今晚在少年之家广场上，北京市的三千多个少年儿童和出席全国先进生产者代表会议的代表们一起联欢。
    当三百四十多个先进生产者进来的时候，少年儿童们跳跃地拥上去，紧紧拉住他们。少先队员代表讲话说：等我们长大了，一定要像叔叔姑姑们一样，做一个光荣的先进生产者。
    广场上围成了一百多个小圆圈。先进生产者热情地给孩子们讲自己的先进事迹。许多小朋友们拿出了小笔记本请先进生产者签名。先进生产者、湘剧演员谭保成，豫剧演员王素君，还给少年儿童们演唱。作家老舍热情地鼓励孩子们说：未来的先进生产者是你们，你们将来每一个都会做比我们更好的先进生产者，把祖国建设得比今天更加美丽。
