Title: Lanzhou Ring Railway Completed
Author:
Date: 1956-05-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency:

    Lanzhou Ring Railway Completed
    After the last rail was nailed at the Sigou Bridgehead on the 17th, the newly built Lanzhou ring railway allowed long trains to start running on the entire ring line.
    This ring railway is thirteen kilometers long. One end of it is connected to the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway. At present, the construction materials needed by some large factories in Lanzhou can be transported to various construction sites via the ring railway.

### 兰州市环行铁路建成

1956-05-22
第2版()
专栏：

    兰州市环行铁路建成
    兰州市新建的环行铁路十七日在寺沟桥头钉上最后一根钢轨后，长长的列车就在全环行线上开动起来。
    这条环行铁路全长十三公里。它的一端和兰新铁路相衔接。目前，兰州市有些大工厂所需要的建设物资，都可通过环行铁路运送到各建筑工地。　（新华社）
