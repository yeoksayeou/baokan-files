Title: French Foreign Minister Pineau Visits Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1956-05-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 26th: French Foreign Minister Christian Pineau arrived in Prague on May 25th, en route from the Soviet Union, for a visit.

　　Pineau arrived by plane in the morning. At the airport, he told those welcoming him that his stopover was also an opportunity to re-emphasize the traditional friendly relations that have existed between France and Czechoslovakia for many years.

　　During his stay, Pineau visited Czechoslovak Foreign Minister David and had friendly talks with him.

　　Pineau also toured the city of Prague.

　　The Foreign Minister left Prague for Paris that afternoon.
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: Paris news: French Foreign Minister Christian Pineau returned to Paris by plane on May 25th after visiting the Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia.

　　At the Paris airport, Pineau said in a statement that his talks with the Soviet government leaders were "very beneficial." He emphasized that the talks were filled with a sincere atmosphere. He said that this atmosphere enabled "many things to be brought up for discussion."

### 法国外长比诺访捷

1956-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    法国外长比诺访捷
    新华社布拉格26日电　五月二十五日，法国外交部长比诺在从苏联回国途中抵达布拉格访问。
    比诺是在上午搭飞机到达这里的。他在飞机场向欢迎的人说，他这次停留也是重新强调法捷两国之间许多年来所存在的传统友好关系的一次机会。
    比诺在逗留期间曾经访问捷克斯洛伐克外交部长戴维，同他进行了友好的交谈。
    比诺还游览了布拉格市。
    当天下午，这位外交部长离开布拉格回巴黎。
    新华社25日讯　巴黎消息：法国外交部长比诺在访问了苏联和捷克斯洛伐克后，在五月二十五日乘飞机回到巴黎。
    比诺在巴黎机场上发表谈话说，他和苏联政府领导人举行的会谈是“非常有益的”。他着重指出，会谈中充满着诚恳气氛。他说，这种气氛使“许多事情能够提出来讨论”。
