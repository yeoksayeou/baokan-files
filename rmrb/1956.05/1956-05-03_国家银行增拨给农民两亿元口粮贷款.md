Title: State Bank Increases Grain Loan to Farmers by 200 Million Yuan
Author:
Date: 1956-05-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The State Bank has increased its living grain loan to farmers by 200 million yuan, which will be distributed to various regions. This is another effective measure taken by the state to support agricultural cooperativization and help poor peasants financially. This loan will be used to help disaster-stricken people in disaster areas last year and poor cooperative members and poor individual farmers in general areas to solve the difficulties of living grain during the spring plowing season.

### 国家银行增拨给农民两亿元口粮贷款

1956-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　国家银行增拨给农民两亿元口粮贷款
    新华社2日讯　国家银行为农民增拨生活口粮贷款两亿元，分配各地发放。这是国家从财政上支援农业合作化、扶助贫农的又一有效措施。这部分贷款，将用于帮助去年灾区受灾群众和一般地区贫困社员、贫困个体农民解决在春耕期中的生活口粮困难。
