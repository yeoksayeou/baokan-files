Title: Three Decisions of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-05-09
Page: 1

　　Decision on the Term of Office of the People's Congresses and People's Committees of Autonomous Prefectures
    (Adopted at the 39th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on May 8, 1956)
    The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, at its 39th meeting on May 8, 1956, discussed the term of office of the People's Congresses and People's Committees of autonomous prefectures and decided: The term of office of the People's Congresses of autonomous prefectures shall be determined to be two years. The term of office of the People's Committees of autonomous prefectures shall be the same as the term of office of the People's Congresses of autonomous prefectures.
    Decision on Cases That Shall Not Be Heard in Public
    (Adopted at the 39th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on May 8, 1956)
    The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, at its 39th meeting on May 8, 1956, discussed the question raised by the Supreme People's Court regarding what cases may not be heard in public. It was decided: People's Courts may not conduct hearings in public in cases involving state secrets, cases involving the privacy of the parties, and cases of crimes committed by juveniles under the age of eighteen.
    Decision on Whether Persons Deprived of Political Rights May Serve as Defenders
    (Adopted at the 39th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on May 8, 1956)
    The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, at its 39th meeting on May 8, 1956, discussed the question raised by the Supreme People's Court regarding whether persons deprived of political rights may serve as defenders, and decided: Persons deprived of political rights shall not serve as defenders during the period of deprivation of political rights. However, persons deprived of political rights may serve as defenders if they are close relatives or guardians of the defendant.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会三项决定

1956-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常务委员会三项决定
    关于自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会每届任期问题的决定
    （1956年5月8日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第39次会议通过）
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会于1956年5月8日第三十九次会议讨论了自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会每届任期的问题，决定：自治州人民代表大会每届任期，决定为二年。自治州人民委员会每届任期同自治州人民代表大会每届任期相同。
    关于不公开进行审理的案件的决定
    （1956年5月8日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第39次会议通过）
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会于1956年5月8日第三十九次会议讨论了最高人民法院提出的什么案件可以不公开进行审理的问题。决定：人民法院在审理有关国家机密的案件，有关当事人阴私的案件和未满十八周岁少年人犯罪的案件，可以不公开进行。
    关于被剥夺政治权利的人可否充当辩护人的决定
    （1956年5月8日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第39次会议通过）
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会于1956年5月8日第三十九次会议讨论了最高人民法院提出的关于被剥夺政治权利的人可否充当辩护人的问题，决定：被剥夺政治权利的人在被剥夺政治权利期间，不得充当辩护人。但是，被剥夺政治权利的人如果是被告人的近亲属或者监护人，可以充当辩护人。
