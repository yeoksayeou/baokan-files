Title: New Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Bjork, Pays Courtesy Call on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1956-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th. The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Sweden to China, Mr. Klas Bjork, accompanied by the Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy in China, Mr. Hammarskjöld, paid a courtesy call to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on May 5th to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Yutian, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 瑞典新任驻我国大使波克拜会周恩来外长

1956-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　瑞典新任驻我国大使波克拜会周恩来外长
    新华社5日讯　瑞典王国新任驻华特命全权大使克拉斯·波克由瑞典驻华大使馆临时代办哈马孝尔德陪同于五月五日到外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈递交国书事宜。在座的还有：外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华和礼宾司副司长王雨田。
