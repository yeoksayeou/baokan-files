Title: Joint Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Standing Committee of CPPCC National Committee Decides on Matters Concerning Inspection Work
Author:
Date: 1956-05-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th.
    Joint Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Standing Committee of CPPCC National Committee Decides on Matters Concerning Inspection Work
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th.  The Thirty-ninth Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Twenty-second Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee were held jointly on the afternoon of the 8th.
    The meeting heard a report by Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, on the inspection work of the National People's Congress deputies and the National Committee members of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in the second half of 1955.
    The meeting discussed relevant matters concerning inspection work in the first half of 1956, and exchanged views on relevant issues in the inspection work.
    The meeting decided to issue a joint notice to the people's committees of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government, and the committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government regarding these matters.
    The meeting decided that this inspection work would be jointly conducted by representatives of the National People's Congress and representatives of the People's Congresses of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government, and by members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and members of the local committees of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government, from May 12th to June 10th.  Those who are old and weak or unable to leave their posts may conduct on-the-spot inspections or not participate in the inspection.
    The meeting also decided that the items, locations, objects of inspection, as well as the cadres and masses to be approached, should be selected by the representatives and members themselves. It is necessary to inspect good examples, as well as bad and average examples, in order to have a comprehensive understanding of the situation.
    Regarding the inspection areas, the meeting decided that representatives of the National People's Congress and members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference may choose one or several areas to inspect; representatives of the People's Congresses of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government and local committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference may choose one or several areas to inspect within their respective provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government. Representatives and members inspecting the same area or the same unit may jointly conduct inspections, or they may go out for inspections separately.
    The meeting also decided that problems discovered during the inspection shall still be handled in accordance with the Decision on the Inspection Work of Representatives adopted by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on August 6, 1955. Inspection reports can be written or not written; they can be written collectively or individually; they can be written about comprehensive issues or individual issues.
    The Thirty-ninth Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress also passed the Decision on the Term of Office of the People's Congresses and People's Committees of Autonomous Prefectures, the Decision on Cases to be Tried in Private, and the Decision on Whether Persons Deprived of Political Rights May Serve as Defenders.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会和政协全国委员会常委会  联合开会决定有关视察工作事项

1956-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会和政协全国委员会常委会
　　联合开会决定有关视察工作事项
    新华社8日讯  全国人民代表大会常务委员会第三十九次会议和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第二十二次会议在八日下午联合举行。
    会议听取了全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏关于全国人民代表大会代表和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员1955年下半年视察工作情况的报告。
    会议讨论了关于1956年上半年视察工作的有关事项，对视察工作中的有关问题交换了意见。
    会议决定就这些事项，向各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会和中国人民政治协商会议各省、自治区、直辖市委员会发出联合通知。
    会议决定，这次视察工作由全国人民代表大会代表和省、自治区、直辖市人民代表大会代表，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员和省、自治区、直辖市地方委员会委员，在五月十二日到六月十日共同进行。年老体弱或者不能离开工作岗位的，可以就地视察或者不参加视察。
    会议还决定，视察的项目、地点、对象以及接近的干部和群众都由代表和委员自行选定。要视察好的典型，也要视察坏的和中等的典型，以便能对情况作全面的了解。
    关于视察地区，会议决定，全国人民代表大会代表和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员可以选择一个或几个地区视察；省、自治区、直辖市人民代表大会代表和中国人民政治协商会议地方委员会，可以在本省、自治区、直辖市范围内自行选择一个或者几个地区视察。到同一地区、同一单位视察的代表和委员，可以联合起来进行视察，也可以单独出去视察。
    会议还决定，视察中发现的问题，仍按照全国人民代表大会常务委员会1955年8月6日通过的关于代表视察工作的决定办理。视察报告可以写，也可以不写；可以集体写，也可以个人写；可以写全面的问题，也可以写个别的问题。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会第三十九次会议还通过了关于自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会每届任期问题的决定，关于不公开进行审理的案件的决定，关于被剥夺政治权利的人可否充当辩护人的决定。
