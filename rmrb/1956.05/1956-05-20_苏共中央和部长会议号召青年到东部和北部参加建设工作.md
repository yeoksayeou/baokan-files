Title: The CPC Central Committee and the Council of Ministers Call on Youth to Participate in Construction Work in the East and North

Author:

Date: 1956-05-20
Page: 4

    The CPC Central Committee and the Council of Ministers Call on Youth to Participate in Construction Work in the East and North
    Moscow Dispatch, May 19th. The CPC Central Committee and the Soviet Council of Ministers issued a letter to the All-Union Komsomol organization, Komsomol members, and all Soviet youth, calling for the mobilization of 400,000 to 500,000 young men and women in the next three years to participate in the construction of hydroelectric power stations, factories, mines, and railways in the east, north, and Donbass. The letter pointed out that there is enormous construction work in the east and north of the Soviet Union waiting for young patriots to participate in, and expressed confidence that Komsomol members will carry forward the glorious tradition of developing the east and reclaiming wasteland in the past and respond to the call of the Party and the government without fear of difficulties.

### 苏共中央和部长会议号召青年到东部和北部参加建设工作

1956-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央和部长会议号召青年到东部和北部参加建设工作
    本报莫斯科19日电　苏共中央和苏联部长会议发布了告全苏共青团组织、共青团员和全苏青年的信，号召在今明后三年动员四五十万男女青年参加东部、北部和顿巴斯的水电站、工厂、矿山和铁路的建设。信里指出，苏联东部和北部有巨大的建设工作等待青年爱国者去参加，相信共青团员能够发扬过去开发东部和开垦荒地的光荣的传统和不怕困难地响应党和政府的号召。
