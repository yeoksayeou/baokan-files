Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Cables Hiratsuka Tsunejiro, Expressing Hope for Further Cooperation Between Chinese and Japanese Fishery Circles

Author:

Date: 1956-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China today sent a telegram to Hiratsuka Tsunejiro, Chairman of the Japan-China Fisheries Council, congratulating him on the signing of the protocol extending the validity of the Sino-Japanese non-governmental fisheries agreement for one year. In his telegram, Premier Zhou Enlai also expressed his hope for further friendly cooperation between the fishery circles of China and Japan, and wished prosperity to the Japanese fishery industry.

Hiratsuka Tsunejiro sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai on May 8, congratulating him on the successful conclusion of the signing ceremony of the protocol on extending the validity of the Japan-China Fisheries Agreement. In his telegram, he also wished Premier Zhou Enlai good health and China prosperity.

### 周总理回电平冢常次郎希望中日渔业界进一步合作

1956-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理回电平冢常次郎希望中日渔业界进一步合作
    新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天回电报给日本日中渔业协议会会长平冢常次郎，祝贺中日民间渔业协定有效期限延长一年的议定书的签订。周恩来总理在电报中还希望中日两国渔业界进一步友好合作，并且祝贺日本渔业界的繁荣。
    平冢常次郎曾经在五月八日给周恩来总理来电，祝贺关于延长日中渔业协定有效期限的议定书签字仪式圆满结束。他在电报中并且祝周恩来总理身体健康和中国繁荣。
