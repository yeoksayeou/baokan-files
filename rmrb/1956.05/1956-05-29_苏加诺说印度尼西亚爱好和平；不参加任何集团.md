Title: Sukarno Says Indonesia Loves Peace; Will Not Participate in Any Bloc
Author:
Date: 1956-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: Indonesian President Sukarno said, "We do not approve of any blocs, we love peace." He said this in a pre-recorded television interview broadcast in New York yesterday when answering the question of why Indonesia does not participate in the Manila Treaty Organization.

In answering the question of why Indonesia recognizes China, Sukarno said that mainland China has hundreds of millions of people, and you "must face reality." He also said, regarding the West Irian issue, "We are not prepared to fight, we are a peace-loving people. We will conduct negotiations."

Sukarno is currently visiting the state of Michigan in the United States. He concluded his visit to New York on May 25th. He then visited some places in Pennsylvania and Illinois.

### 苏加诺说印度尼西亚爱好和平；不参加任何集团

1956-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏加诺说印度尼西亚爱好和平；不参加任何集团
    新华社28日讯　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺说：“我们不赞成任何集团，我们爱好和平。”他是在昨天纽约播送的预先摄制的电视谈话里回答印度尼西亚为什么不参加马尼拉条约组织的问题时说这番话的。
    在回答印度尼西亚为什么承认中国的问题时，苏加诺说，中国大陆有好几亿人民，你“必须正视现实的东西”。他还说，在西伊里安问题上“我们不准备作战，我们是爱好和平的人民。我们将进行谈判”。
    苏加诺目前正在美国密执安州访问。他是在五月二十五日结束在纽约的访问的。接着，他还访问了宾夕法尼亚州和伊利诺斯州的一些地方。
