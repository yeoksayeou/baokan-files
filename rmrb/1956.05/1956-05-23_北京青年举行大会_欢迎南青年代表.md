Title: Beijing Youth Hold Meeting to Welcome Yugoslav Youth Representatives
Author:
Date: 1956-05-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: More than 1,700 Beijing youth held a meeting at the CPPCC Auditorium today to welcome the Yugoslav Youth Delegation.

Wang Zhaohua, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, on behalf of the League members and youth of Beijing, expressed the warmest welcome to the Yugoslav Youth Delegation. In his speech, he said, "We firmly believe that through your visit, the brotherly friendship between the youth of China and Yugoslavia will be infinitely strengthened."

Milijan Neoričić, head of the Yugoslav Youth Delegation, Chairman of the Central Committee of the People's Youth Union of Yugoslavia, and Member of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, then spoke. He said, "We bring you – the young builders of socialism, the sons and daughters of the great Chinese people – the warm greetings of the socialist Yugoslav youth." He said, "In the past few days, we have been very happy to see your great achievements in your country, because your achievements are also our achievements." At the end of his speech, he presented the medal awarded by the People's Youth Union of Yugoslavia to outstanding youth in socialist construction to the Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and requested Wang Zhaohua to transfer another ten medals to outstanding youth in Beijing for building socialism, and presented the flag of the People's Youth Union of Yugoslavia to the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League.

Hu Yaobang thanked the Yugoslav Youth Delegation for the noble friendship expressed in the gifts. He said, "We understand the achievements of the Yugoslav people and youth in the struggle for national liberation and the construction of socialism, and we have always regarded their victory as our own joy. We will use all our strength to strengthen the friendship with the Yugoslav people and youth."

After the meeting, the Song and Dance Troupe of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army performed song and dance programs.

### 北京青年举行大会  欢迎南青年代表

1956-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    北京青年举行大会
    欢迎南青年代表
    据新华社22日讯　北京市一千七百多个青年今天在政协礼堂举行大会，欢迎南斯拉夫青年代表团。
    青年团北京市委员会第二书记王照华代表北京市的青年团员和青年，表示对南斯拉夫青年代表团最热诚的欢迎。他致词说，我们坚信，通过你们的访问，中国和南斯拉夫青年之间兄弟般的友谊一定会无限地加强起来。
    南斯拉夫青年代表团团长、南斯拉夫人民青年联盟中央委员会主席、南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会委员米利扬·内奥里契奇接着讲话。他说，我们给你们——社会主义的年青的建设者，伟大的中国人民的儿女，带来了社会主义的南斯拉夫青年热烈的敬礼。他说，几天来，我们在你们国家很高兴地看到了你们伟大的成就。因为你们的成就也是我们的成就。他在结束讲话以后，把南斯拉夫人民青年联盟授予在社会主义建设中优秀青年的奖章赠给了青年团中央委员会书记处书记，把另外十枚奖章请王照华转赠给北京市建设社会主义优秀的青年，并且把南斯拉夫人民青年联盟盟旗赠送给青年团北京市委员会。
    胡耀邦在最后讲话中感谢南斯拉夫青年代表团赠给的礼品所表达的高贵友谊。他说，我们了解南斯拉夫人民和青年在民族解放和建设社会主义斗争中的成就，我们曾经把他们的胜利当作自己的欢乐。我们要以一切力量，来加强同南斯拉夫人民和青年的友谊。
    会后，中国人民解放军总政治部文工团演出了歌舞节目。
