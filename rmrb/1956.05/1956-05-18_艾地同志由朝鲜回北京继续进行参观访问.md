Title: Aidit Returns to Beijing from Korea to Continue Visit

Author:

Date: 1956-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Aidit, General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, who attended the Third Congress of the Korean Workers' Party, has returned to Beijing a few days ago.
　　
Aidit continued to visit and tour Peking University, the Soviet Exhibition Center, the Forbidden City, Zhongshan Park, and other places in Beijing.

### 艾地同志由朝鲜回北京继续进行参观访问

1956-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    艾地同志由朝鲜回北京继续进行参观访问
    新华社17日讯　参加朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会的印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地，已于日前回到北京。
  艾地继续在北京参观和游览了北京大学、苏联展览馆、故宫、中山公园等地方。
