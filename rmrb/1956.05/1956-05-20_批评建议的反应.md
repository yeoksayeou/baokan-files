Date: 1956-05-20

Reactions to Criticism and Suggestions

    A letter from the Cotton Procurement Office of the Hubei Provincial Agricultural Products Procurement Department: After the publication of the letter "Correcting the Phenomenon of Undergrading and Price Suppression in Cotton Procurement Work" in the "Reader's Letters" column on February 19, the Financial and Trade Office of our province and Party committees at all levels attached great importance to this issue and have taken concrete measures to correct the phenomenon of undergrading and price suppression in procurement work.
        ×××
    A letter from the Datong City Urban Planning Committee of Shanxi Province: After seeing the letter of suggestions "Do Not Relocate Technical Personnel's Jobs Too Frequently" written by Comrade Dong Daxin in the "Reader's Letters" column on April 22, we conducted an inspection and decided to let Comrade Dong Daxin continue to do technical work.
       ×××
    After the publication of two readers' letters "Do Not Easily Allow Students with Specialties to Change Professions" in the "Reader's Letters" column on April 1, the Cadre Department of the Southwest Geological Bureau and the Cadre Department of the Central South Geological Bureau have sent letters of self-criticism.
          ×××
    The "Reader's Letters" column on February 5 published a letter "The People Do Not Need Inferior Quality Products," which criticized the decline in product quality in some factories in Liaoning Province. The Liaoning Provincial Department of Industry has inspected the work of these factories and submitted measures to improve product quality in a letter.
      ×××
    The cartoon "Easily Defeated" published in the "Reader's Letters" column on April 9 criticized the poor quality of hammerheads supplied by the Wuhan Municipal Handicraft Industry Bureau Supply and Marketing Department. The Wuhan Municipal Handicraft Industry Bureau has sent a letter of self-criticism, stating that it will improve product quality in the future and has decided to recall unsuitable hammers.
       ×××
    A letter from the Capitalist Industry and Commerce Transformation Office of the Huanggang Prefectural Committee of the Communist Party of China in Hubei Province: We accept the criticism in the letter "Do Not Be in a Hurry to Decorate the Facade" in the "Reader's Letters" column on April 16. The departments responsible for the transformation of capitalist industry and commerce in the relevant areas have conducted inspections and are determined to implement the principle of diligence and thrift in management in the future.

### 批评建议的反应

1956-05-20
第6版()
专栏：批评建议的反应

    批评建议的反应
    湖北省农产品采购厅棉花采购处来信：二月十九日“读者来信专页”发表了“纠正棉花收购工作中的压级压价现象”一信以后，我省财粮贸办公室和各级党委都很重视这个问题，并采取了具体措施来纠正收购工作中的压级压价现象。
        ×××
    山西大同市城市规划委员会来信：看到四月二十二日“读者来信专页”上董大新同志所写的“不要过于频繁地调动技术人员的工作”的意见信以后，我们就进行了检查，并且决定仍然让董大新同志做技术工作。
       ×××
    四月一日“读者来信专页”发表了“不要轻易地让有专业的学生改行”的两封读者来信后，西南地质局干部处和中南地质局干部处已经分别来信作了检讨。
          ×××
    二月五日“读者来信专页”发表了“人民不需要质量低劣的产品”一信，对辽宁省某些工厂产品质量下降的情况提出了批评。辽宁省工业厅已经检查了这些工厂的工作，并且来信提出了提高产品质量的措施。
      ×××
    四月九日“读者来信专页”发表了“不堪一击”的漫画，批评武汉市手工业管理局供销经理部供应的铁锤锤头质量不好。武汉市手工业管理局已经来信作了检讨，表示今后要提高产品质量，并且决定收回不适用的铁锤。
       ×××
    中共湖北省黄冈地委资本主义工商业改造办公室来信：我们接受四月十六日“读者来信专页”中“不要忙于装修门面”一信的批评。有关地区负责资本主义工商业改造工作的部门已经作了检查，决心今后贯彻勤俭经营的方针。
