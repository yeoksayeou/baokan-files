Title: Bulganin Receives Japanese Minister of Agriculture and Forestry
Author:
Date: 1956-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th—TASS, Moscow, 9th: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, received the Japanese Minister of Agriculture and Forestry, Mr. Ichiro Kono, on May 9th and held talks with him.

Present at the meeting were the Soviet Minister of Fisheries, Ishkov, and the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Fedlin.

### 布尔加宁接见日本农林相

1956-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁接见日本农林相
    新华社9日讯  塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁，在五月九日接见了日本农林相河野一郎先生，并且同他进行了谈话。
    接见的时候在座的有苏联渔业部长伊什科夫和苏联外交部副部长费德林。
