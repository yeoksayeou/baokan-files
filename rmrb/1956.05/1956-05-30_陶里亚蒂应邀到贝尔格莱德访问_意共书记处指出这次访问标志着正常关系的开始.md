Title: Togliatti Invited to Visit Belgrade; PCI Secretariat Points Out Visit Marks Beginning of Normal Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-05-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 29th dispatch from Rome: According to the Yugoslav News Agency, the Secretariat of the Italian Communist Party issued a communiqué in Rome on the evening of the 28th, stating that Togliatti’s visit to Yugoslavia would mark the beginning of normal relations between the Italian Communist Party and the Yugoslav Communists.

The communiqué stated that the purpose of this visit was to establish preliminary contact.

The communiqué continued, "Italian communists cannot but value the effective contribution made by Yugoslav leaders to further easing international tensions, and cannot but value the fact that, despite the serious events that have occurred in the past few years, these leaders are still leading their country on the road of socialist development. Therefore, we hope to better understand this experience so that we can make a correct assessment of it and better develop our international policies."

The communiqué concluded: "Comrade Togliatti's trip to Yugoslavia is the first step that must be taken and will certainly produce meaningful and good results."

Xinhua News Agency, May 29th dispatch from Belgrade: According to the Yugoslav News Agency, the General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, Togliatti, arrived in Belgrade on the evening of the 28th for a two-day visit.

Togliatti is visiting Yugoslavia at the invitation of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia. During the visit, he will hold political talks with the leaders of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia.

### 陶里亚蒂应邀到贝尔格莱德访问  意共书记处指出这次访问标志着正常关系的开始

1956-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    陶里亚蒂应邀到贝尔格莱德访问
    意共书记处指出这次访问标志着正常关系的开始
    新华社29日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社罗马讯：意大利共产党书记处二十八日晚在罗马发表的一项公报说，陶里亚蒂的访问南斯拉夫，将标志着意大利共产党和南斯拉夫共产主义者之间的正常关系的开始。
    公报说，这次访问的目的是要建立初步的接触。
    公报继续说，“意大利共产党人不能不重视南斯拉夫领袖对进一步缓和国际紧张局势的如此有效的贡献，并且不能不重视这个事实：尽管有过去几年来发生的严重的事件，这些领袖们仍然领导他们的国家走着社会主义发展的道路。因此我们希望更好地了解这种经验，以便能够对它作出正确的估价，更好地发展我们的国际政策。”
    公报最后说：“陶里亚蒂同志到南斯拉夫的旅行是必须采取的、并且肯定将产生有意义和良好结果的第一个步骤。”
    新华社29日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社贝尔格莱德讯：意大利共产党总书记陶里亚蒂，在二十八日晚间到达贝尔格莱德作为期两天的访问。
    陶里亚蒂是应南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会的邀请到南斯拉夫访问的。在访问期间，他将同南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟的领导人举行政治会谈。
