Title: Arab League Political Committee Decides to Submit the Algerian Question to the Security Council
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 20th - The Arab League Political Committee announced on May 19th that it would request the United Nations Security Council to discuss the Algerian situation. The committee also stated that it would make further efforts to urge France to cease military operations in Algeria and recognize the national rights of Algeria.

### 阿拉伯联盟政委会决定向安理会  提出阿尔及利亚问题

1956-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟政委会决定向安理会
    提出阿尔及利亚问题
    新华社20日讯　大马士革消息：阿拉伯联盟政治委员会五月十九日宣布，它将要求联合国安全理事会讨论阿尔及利亚局势问题。委员会并且表示，它将作进一步努力，以促使法国停止在阿尔及利亚的军事行动和承认阿尔及利亚的民族权利。
