Title: A Forestry Workers' Hospital
Author:
Date: 1956-05-12
Page: 3

    A Forestry Workers' Hospital
    A new forestry workers' hospital has been built in Huma County, Heilongjiang Province. The hospital covers an area of 4,626 square meters and is expected to be completed before National Day this year. After completion, the hospital will have a total of one hundred and fifty beds, as well as two sets of X-ray machines and other electrical equipment.
       (Our correspondent in Harbin)

### 一座林业职工医院

1956-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    一座林业职工医院
    黑龙江省呼玛县新建一座林业职工医院。这个医院占地面积4，626平方公尺，今年国庆节前即可竣工。医院建成后共有一百五十张病床，还有爱克斯光机两套和其他电气设备。
       （本报驻哈尔滨记者）
