Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Holds Banquet, Inviting Trade Union Representatives from Various Countries to Celebrate "May Day"

Author: 
Date: 1956-05-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions held a banquet on the afternoon of the 1st, inviting trade union representatives from 38 countries visiting China and Soviet advanced producers to jointly celebrate International Labor Day on May 1st. Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, said at the meeting that our gathering symbolizes the solidarity of the working class around the world.
    The banquet was held after the celebration of the "May Day" International Labor Day rally in the capital. Trade union representatives from various countries from five continents and Chinese trade union workers and advanced producers gathered together to enthusiastically discuss their impressions of the parade and express the desire of workers from various countries to seek peace and strengthen friendship, and together they toasted the solidarity of the international working class. Joyful songs and laughter filled the venue. The representatives finally linked arms and formed several circles, singing and dancing. Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Vice Chairmen Liu Changsheng, Zhu Xuefan, Secretariat Secretaries Li Jiebo, Zhang Weizhen, Dong Xin, Zhang Xiuzhu and the heads of various industrial unions went to the seats of trade union representatives from various countries and raised their glasses to congratulate them on this international workers' holiday.
    At the banquet, Lai Ruoyu, on behalf of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, extended a warm welcome and congratulations to the guests. He said: Today, we are gathered here, including trade union leaders, advanced producers and worker brothers from various countries. Our gathering symbolizes the increasing solidarity of the working class around the world. This solidarity is a great force. Relying on this solidarity, we can defend and strive for the rights of the working class, and we can make great contributions to the cause of defending world peace.
    After the banquet, trade union representatives from various countries went to Tiananmen Square to watch the festive fireworks and participate in the gala evening.

### 中华全国总工会举行宴会  邀请各国工会代表同庆“五一”节

1956-05-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会举行宴会
　　邀请各国工会代表同庆“五一”节
    据新华社1日讯　中华全国总工会一日下午举行宴会，邀请来我国访问的三十八个国家的工会代表和苏联先进生产者共同庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。中华全国总工会主席赖若愚在会上说，我们的聚会象征着全世界工人阶级的团结。
    宴会在首都各界庆祝“五一”国际劳动节大会以后举行，来自五大洲的各国工会代表同中国的工会工作者、先进生产者欢聚在一堂，热烈地谈论着游行大会上的观感，表达着各国工人要求和平、加强友谊的愿望，并且在一起为国际工人阶级的团结干杯。欢愉的歌声和笑声充溢着会场，代表们最后还挽着臂膀围成几个圈子，且歌且舞。中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，副主席刘长胜，朱学范，书记处书记李颉伯、张维桢、董昕、张修竹和各产业工会的负责人都走到各国工会代表的席前，举杯向他们祝贺这个国际工人阶级的节日。
    赖若愚在宴会上代表中华全国总工会向来宾表示热烈的欢迎和祝贺，他说：今天在这里聚会一堂的都是各国的工会领袖、先进生产者和工人兄弟，我们的聚会象征着全世界工人阶级的团结日益加强。这种团结是伟大的力量，依靠这种团结，我们就可以保卫和争取工人阶级的权利，我们就可以在保卫世界和平事业中作出巨大的贡献。
    宴会后，各国工会代表到天安门前观赏节日的焰火和参加联欢晚会。
