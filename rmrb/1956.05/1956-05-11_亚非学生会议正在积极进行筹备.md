Title: Asian-African Student Conference Being Actively Prepared
Author:
Date: 1956-05-11
Page: 4

    Asian-African Student Conference Being Actively Prepared
    Sukarno Supports the Convening of the Conference
    Xinhua News Agency, Bandung, 10th: President Sukarno said here on the evening of May 8th that the upcoming Asian-African Student Conference will definitely succeed in uniting Asian and African students, because they all have the same lofty ideals.
    Sukarno was speaking at a student gathering attended by about 500 people. Participants included members of the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian-African Student Conference and the Indonesian National Preparatory Committee.
    The President pointed out that some people had initially predicted that the Asian-African Conference would only be an afternoon tea party, but now its great significance is universally recognized. The upcoming Asian-African Student Conference may also be the same.
    He asked Indonesian students to unite when carrying out conference preparations, in order to set an example for the unity between students from Asian and African countries.
    Sukarno once again assured moral and material support for the conference and handed a check for 150,000盾 (rupiah) to the representative of the Indonesian National Preparatory Committee on the spot.
    Chinese Student Delegation Leaves Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th: The Chinese student delegation to the Asian-African Student Conference left Beijing for Indonesia today.
    The delegation consists of fifteen people. On the evening of the 9th, Sani, the Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China, and his wife held a reception at the embassy to bid farewell to the Chinese student delegation. Indonesian students studying in Beijing also attended the evening party and had a get-together with the Chinese students.
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 10th: Qian Dawei, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Students' Federation, arrived here by plane on the afternoon of May 9th. Qian Dawei is representing Chinese student organizations to participate in the work of the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian-African Student Conference.
    Thai Students Have Decided to Send a Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th: Bangkok News: According to a report in the "Siam Masses Daily" on May 3rd, Thai students have decided to send a delegation to the Asian-African Student Conference scheduled to be held in Bandung, Indonesia at the end of May. Student representatives from five Thai universities recently held a meeting and elected five representatives to attend the conference.
    The Thai government has expressed its willingness to sponsor Thai students to participate in this conference financially.
    Malayan Students Will Send Representatives to Attend the Conference
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th: According to Singapore newspaper reports: Student groups from all over Malaya held a preparatory meeting at the end of April to select representatives to attend the Asian-African Student Conference. The meeting decided that five student groups, including the All-Malaya Student Federation, would each send one person to form the Malayan delegation to the Asian-African Student Conference.

### 亚非学生会议正在积极进行筹备

1956-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非学生会议正在积极进行筹备
    苏加诺支持会议的召开
    新华社万隆10日电  苏加诺总统五月八日晚上在这里说，即将举行的亚非学生会议在团结亚非学生方面一定会获得成功，因为他们都具有相同的远大理想。
    苏加诺是在一个约有五百人参加的学生集会上讲话。参加这次集会的包括亚非学生会议国际筹备委员会和印度尼西亚全国筹备委员会的委员们。
    总统指出，当初曾有些人预料亚非会议将只是午后的茶会而已，但是现在，它的重大意义得到了普遍的承认。即将举行的亚非学生会议也可能如此。
    他要求印度尼西亚学生在进行会议筹备工作的时候团结一致，以便替亚非各国学生之间的团结树立榜样。
    苏加诺再一次保证给予会议以道义和物质的支持，并且当场又交给印度尼西亚全国筹委会代表一张十五万盾的支票。
    中国学生代表团离开北京
    新华社10日讯  出席亚非学生会议的中国学生代表团今天离北京前往印度尼西亚。
    代表团由十五人组成。九日晚上，印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办萨尼和夫人在大使馆举行了招待晚会，欢送中国学生代表团。印度尼西亚在北京的留学生也出席了这个晚会，并且同中国学生联欢。
    新华社雅加达10日电  中华全国学生联合会副秘书长钱大卫在五月九日下午乘飞机到达这里。钱大卫是代表中国学生组织前来参加亚非学生会议国际筹备委员会的工作的。
    泰国学生已决定派遣代表团
    新华社8日讯  曼谷消息：据“暹罗群众报”五月三日报道，泰国学生已经决定派遣代表团出席定于五月底在印度尼西亚的万隆召开的亚非学生会议。五个泰国大学的学生代表最近曾经举行了会议，推选出五个出席这个会议的代表。
    泰国政府表示了它将在经费上赞助泰国学生参加这个会议。
    马来亚学生将派代表出席会议
    新华社8日讯  据新加坡报纸消息：全马来亚的学生团体在四月底举行了一次选派代表出席亚非学生会议的筹备会议。会议决定由马来亚学生联合会等五个学生团体各派一人，组成马来亚出席亚非学生会议的代表团。
