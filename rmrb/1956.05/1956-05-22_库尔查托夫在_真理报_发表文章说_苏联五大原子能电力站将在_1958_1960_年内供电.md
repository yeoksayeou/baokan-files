Title: Kurchatov's Article in "Pravda" States That Five Major Soviet Atomic Power Stations Will Supply Electricity in 1958-1960
Author:
Date: 1956-05-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 21st. The five major atomic power stations that the Soviet Union plans to build in the new Five-Year Plan will successively supply electricity within the three years from 1958 to 1960. In order to slow down the process of atomic fission, these power stations will use water, heavy water, and graphite as moderators.

The famous Soviet physicist Academician Kurchatov said this in an article published in "Pravda" on May 20th. He also said that many countries are now facing the threat of fuel reserves being exhausted, which has led to the new problem of finding and using other sources of power. Atomic energy should play a major role in solving this problem.

The Soviet Union possesses a wide variety of natural power resources in vast Siberia. In the next ten years, the existing resources in the European part of the Soviet Union will be sufficient, but in the slightly longer term, atomic energy may be an inexhaustible and relatively cheap source of power for this part. Our task is to establish an atomic energy power economy. And building atomic power stations with a generating capacity of about 400,000 to 600,000 kilowatts each can achieve economically favorable indicators.

### 库尔查托夫在“真理报”发表文章说  苏联五大原子能电力站将在1958—1960年内供电

1956-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    库尔查托夫在“真理报”发表文章说
    苏联五大原子能电力站将在1958—1960年内供电
    新华社莫斯科21日电　苏联在新五年计划中规定建设的五个大的原子能电力站，将先后在1958年到1960年的三年内供电。为了减慢原子分裂的过程，这些电力站将采用水、重水和石墨等作为减速剂。
    苏联著名的物理学家库尔查托夫院士五月二十日在“真理报”发表的一篇文章中说了上面这番话。他还说，在许多国家面前，现在实际上已经产生了燃料储备耗尽的威胁，于是就产生了寻找和使用其他动力泉源的新问题。原子能应当在解决这个问题方面起重大的作用。
    苏联在广阔的西伯利亚拥有各种各样的天然动力资源。在今后十年中，苏联欧洲部分现有的资源将是足够的了，但是在稍微比较长远的将来，原子能可能是这个部分的无穷尽的、比较便宜的动力泉源。我们的任务就是要建立原子能动力经济。而建筑发电能力每座约为四十万到六十万瓩的原子能发电站，可以达到经济上有利的指标。
