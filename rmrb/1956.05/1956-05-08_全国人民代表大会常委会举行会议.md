Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th — The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 37th and 38th meetings on the mornings of the 5th and 7th respectively.

　　The 37th meeting heard a report by Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court, on the Third National Judicial Work Conference. The 38th meeting heard a report by Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, on the summary of procuratorial work in 1955 and the tasks for 1956. In the above two reports and the discussions in the meeting reviewing the above reports, the achievements of the Supreme People's Court and the Supreme People's Procuratorate were affirmed, and some shortcomings and errors in their work were criticized.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议

1956-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议
    新华社7日讯  全国人民代表大会常务委员会在五日上午和七日上午，先后举行了第三十七次和第三十八次会议。
    第三十七次会议听取了最高人民法院院长董必武关于第三届全国司法工作会议情况的报告，第三十八次会议听取了最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞关于1955年检察工作总结和1956年工作任务的报告。在上述两个报告和会议审查上述报告的讨论中，肯定了最高人民法院和最高人民检察院的工作成绩，并且对工作中存在的一些缺点和错误作了批评。
