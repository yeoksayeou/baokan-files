Title: First Group of New League Members in Lhasa Hold Admission Oath Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1956-05-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 22nd. On the evening of the 21st, the first group of 223 Tibetan and Hui youths from various organs, organizations, and schools in Lhasa joined the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and held a solemn admission oath ceremony in the Lhasa Auditorium.

Among these new members are Tibetan female tractor drivers, factory technicians, young workers, female nurses, and elementary school students. In the front row of the venue sat a Tibetan girl named Puzhen. Two years ago, she was a day laborer making a living by selling her labor. Now, she is a skilled female tractor driver at the Lhasa Agricultural Experiment Station.

After the meeting began, Liang Feng, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Youth League, who was the supervisor of the oath, spoke first. She encouraged all new League members to resolutely implement the various decisions of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and to strive for the construction of a new Tibet. Zhang Jingwu, Deputy Head of the Central Delegation, also attended the meeting and congratulated all new League members on behalf of the CPC Central Committee, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao. He said: With the Youth League as the core, the broad masses of young people in Tibet will surely make rapid progress and play a more significant role in the great cause of building a new Tibet and building the motherland. His speech was repeatedly interrupted by warm applause.

Losang Sandan, Chairman of the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association, also spoke, saying: The Dalai Lama is very satisfied and pleased that a large number of outstanding Tibetan youths have joined the Youth League and instructed me to congratulate all Youth League members on his behalf at the meeting. In his speech, he repeatedly thanked the Communist Party and the Youth League for their care and cultivation of Tibetan youths. Subsequently, representatives of new League members, veteran League members, and youth mass representatives spoke at the meeting.

Apei A Wang Jinmei, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, Tan Guansan and Fan Ming, Deputy Secretaries of the Tibet Working Committee of the CPC, and others attended the meeting to congratulate all new League members.

### 拉萨第一批新团员举行入团宣誓

1956-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    拉萨第一批新团员举行入团宣誓
    新华社拉萨22日电　二十一日晚上，拉萨市各机关团体和学校中第一批加入中国新民主主义青年团的223名藏、回族青年，在拉萨大礼堂举行了庄严的入团宣誓仪式。
    这些新团员中有藏族女拖拉机手、工厂技术员、青年工人、女护士和小学生等。会场上的第一排上坐着一个叫做扑珍的藏族姑娘，两年前她还是一个靠卖劳力度日的小工，现在已经是拉萨农业试验场熟练的女拖拉机手了。
    大会开始后，监誓人青年团西藏工委副书记梁枫首先讲话，她勉励全体新团员坚决贯彻西藏自治区筹备委员会的各项决定，为新西藏的建设而奋斗。中央代表团副团长张经武也到会代表中共中央、中央人民政府和毛主席向全体新团员祝贺。他说：有了青年团作为核心，西藏的广大青年必将突飞猛进地进步，在建设新西藏和建设祖国的伟大事业中，发挥更重大的作用。他的讲话一再为热烈的掌声所打断。
    拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会主任委员洛桑三旦也讲了话，他说：达赖喇嘛对于大批优秀的西藏青年参加青年团，感到非常满意和愉快，命我代表他在大会上向全体青年团员祝贺。他在讲话中一再感谢共产党和青年团对西藏青年的关怀和教养。接着在会上讲话的有新团员代表、老团员代表、青年群众代表等。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美，中共西藏工委副书记谭冠三、范明等，都到会向全体新团员祝贺。
