Title: South Yugoslavia and Hungary Sign Agreement on Financial, Economic, and Scientific-Technological Cooperation, Paving the Way for Further Development of Economic Cooperation Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-05-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 30th: According to a report from the Yugoslav News Agency: Yugoslavia and Hungary signed an agreement in Belgrade on May 29th to resolve the outstanding financial and economic issues between the two countries. At the same time, the two countries also signed an agreement on scientific and technological cooperation.

According to the first agreement, in order to repay Yugoslavia's demands, Hungary will supply Yugoslavia with goods worth 85 million U.S. dollars, delivering them in equal installments over five years. The second agreement stipulates the development and strengthening of economic relations between the two countries through scientific and technological cooperation.

A communique issued after the signing of the agreement stated that the negotiations were conducted in a spirit of understanding and sincerity. The signing of the financial and economic agreement paves the way for a significant expansion of economic ties and trade between the two countries.

After the signing of the agreement, representatives of both countries stated that these agreements make it possible for the two countries to develop their friendly relations and are the basis for further developing cooperation between the two countries in economic and other fields.

### 南匈签订财政经济和科学技术合作协定  为进一步发展两国间经济合作开辟了道路

1956-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    南匈签订财政经济和科学技术合作协定
    为进一步发展两国间经济合作开辟了道路
    据新华社30日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道：南斯拉夫和匈牙利五月二十九日在贝尔格莱德签订了一项解决两国之间悬而未决的财政和经济问题的协定。同时两国还签订了一项关于科学技术合作的协定。
    根据第一个协定，为了偿付南斯拉夫的要求，匈牙利将以价值八千五百万美元的货物供应南斯拉夫，并且在五年中平均分期交货。第二个协定规定通过科学和技术合作来发展和加强两国之间的经济关系。
    在签订协定以后发表的公报说，谈判是本着谅解和诚恳的精神进行的。财政和经济协定的签订为大大扩大两国之间的经济联系和贸易开辟了道路。
    在协定签字以后，两国代表表示，这些协定使两国有可能发展它们之间的友好关系，而且是进一步发展两国的经济和其他方面的合作的基础。
