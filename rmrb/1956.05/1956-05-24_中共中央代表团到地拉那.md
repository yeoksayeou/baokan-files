Title: CCP Central Delegation Arrives in Tirana
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-24
Page: 4

    Tirana, May 21 (By our own correspondent) The Chinese Communist Party delegation, which came to attend the Third Congress of the Albanian Labor Party, arrived in Tirana on the 21st. In the afternoon of the same day, Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Albanian Labor Party, and members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee received Zeng Shan, head of the delegation, and Xu Yixin, a member of the delegation.

### 中共中央代表团到地拉那

1956-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央代表团到地拉那
    本报地拉那21日电　前来参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第三届代表大会的中国共产党代表团已于二十一日到达地拉那。同日下午阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第一书记霍查和中央委员会政治局各委员接见了代表团团长曾山和团员徐以新。
