Title: The Chinese Pavilion at the Paris Exposition is the Most Attractive to Visitors; Hundreds of Traders Contacted Us to Discuss Trade Issues

Author:

Date: 1956-05-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 10th: Paris dispatch: Since the opening of the Paris Exposition on May 5th, the Chinese Pavilion has been a central attraction for visitors. French newspapers generally believe that the Chinese Pavilion is one of the most splendid exhibition halls in this year's Paris Exposition.

On May 8th alone, 45,000 people visited the Chinese Pavilion. During the lunch break, several hundred visitors were already waiting at the entrance of the Chinese Pavilion for it to open.

Visitors expressed surprise and admiration for the rapid development of China's economy, especially its industry. In front of the exhibition stands displaying Chinese silk and tea, the sounds of praise from the audience could be heard everywhere.

Hundreds of traders have already contacted the Chinese exhibition delegation to discuss trade issues.

### 巴黎博览会的中国馆最吸引观众  好几百名贸易商同我国接触，商谈进行交易问题

1956-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    巴黎博览会的中国馆最吸引观众
    好几百名贸易商同我国接触，商谈进行交易问题
    新华社柏林10日电　巴黎消息：自从巴黎博览会在五月五日开幕以来，中国馆一直是吸引参观者的一个中心。法国报纸普遍认为中国馆是今年巴黎博览会中最精彩的展览馆之一。
    在五月八日一天里，有四万五千人参观了中国馆。中午休息时间，也有好几百名观众早就来到中国馆门口等待开放。
    观众们对于新中国经济特别是工业的迅速发展表示惊奇和赞叹。在陈列中国的丝绸和茶叶的展览台前面，到处可以听到观众们的赞美声。
    已经有好几百名贸易商同中国展览团接触，商谈进行交易的问题。
