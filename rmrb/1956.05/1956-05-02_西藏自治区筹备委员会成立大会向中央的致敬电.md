Title: Congratulatory Telegram from the Inaugural Meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region to the Central Government
Author:
Date: 1956-05-02
Page: 1

　　Congratulatory Telegram from the Inaugural Meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region to the Central Government
　　To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and the State Council, and forwarding to the esteemed Chairman Mao Zedong:

　　We would like to report to you with boundless joy: The Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region has been officially established. The inaugural meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region is proceeding smoothly and has concluded victoriously under the personal guidance and assistance of the Central Delegation headed by Vice Premier Chen Yi, under the personal direction of the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni, and with the united and concerted efforts of all participants.

　　The establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region is an important step for the Tibetan people in implementing regional autonomy and a significant milestone for the Tibetan people on the road to a happy and socialist future. It is a major joyous event of historical significance in the political life of the Tibetan people. People of all strata, both monks and laypeople, in Tibet are celebrating this great event with joy and excitement.

　　The inaugural meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region is an unprecedented gathering of great unity in Tibetan history. Representatives from all regions, aspects, strata, religions, mass organizations, and notable figures in Tibet participated in this meeting. This demonstrates the new atmosphere of unity within the Tibetan nationality. At the same time, representatives of fraternal nationalities from all over the country gathered with us, fully demonstrating the consolidation and strengthening of the unity among all nationalities in our great motherland.

　　We sincerely thank the great Communist Party of China, the Central People's Government, and the esteemed leader Chairman Mao Zedong for their meticulous care for the Tibetan people; thank the Central Delegation headed by Vice Premier Chen Yi for their kind guidance and assistance; and thank all fraternal nationalities for their warm congratulations and the encouragement and help they have given us.

　　In these days of jubilation, we have a deeper understanding that only under the wise and correct leadership of the Communist Party of China, the Central People's Government, and the esteemed Chairman Mao, can the Tibetan nationality prosper.

　　On behalf of all monks and laypeople in Tibet, we hereby assure you that under your leadership, and under the direct leadership of the Tibet Working Committee of the CPC, the Dalai Lama, and the Panchen Erdeni, we will resolutely follow the correct policy of "unity, progress, and greater development," make a hundredfold effort to fulfill the glorious task of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, to consolidate the southwest national defense of the motherland, and to strive for the glorious cause of jointly building our great motherland!

　　Inaugural Meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region
　　May 1, 1956

### 西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会向中央的致敬电

1956-05-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会向中央的致敬电
　　中国共产党中央委员会、全国人民代表大会常务委员会、国务院并转敬爱的毛泽东主席：
    我们谨以无限愉快的心情向您报告：西藏自治区筹备委员会正式成立了。自治区筹备委员会成立大会，是在以陈毅副总理为首的中央代表团的亲临指导和帮助下，在达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的亲自主持下，和在与会全体人员的团结一致共同努力下，顺利进行着，已经胜利闭幕了。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，是西藏人民实行区域自治的重要步骤，是西藏人民向美满幸福社会主义大道前进中的重要里程碑。它是西藏人民政治生活中具有历史意义的一件大喜事。西藏各阶层僧俗人民都在欢欣鼓舞地庆祝这一大喜事。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会是西藏历史上空前大团结的聚会，参加这个大会的有来自西藏各地、各方面、各阶层、各教派、各群众团体和社会贤达等代表。这显示了西藏民族内部的团结的新气象。同时，也有来自全国各地的兄弟民族代表和我们欢聚一堂，这充分表明着我们伟大祖国各民族间的团结的巩固和加强。
    我们衷心感谢伟大的中国共产党、中央人民政府和敬爱的领袖毛泽东主席对西藏人民的无微不至的关怀；感谢以陈毅副总理为首的中央代表团亲切指导和帮助；感谢各兄弟民族的热情祝贺和给我们的鼓舞和帮助。
    在这万众欢腾的日子里，我们更加深刻的认识到：只有在中国共产党、中央人民政府和敬爱的毛主席的英明正确领导下，西藏民族才能繁荣昌盛。
    我们谨代表西藏全体僧俗人民向您保证：在您的领导下，在中共西藏工委、达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的直接领导下，我们坚决遵循着“团结、进步、更加发展”的正确方针，百倍努力，为完成西藏自治区筹备委员会的光荣任务，为巩固祖国西南国防，为共同建设我们伟大祖国的光荣事业而奋斗！
    西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会
                    1956年5月1日
