Title: Czechoslovak Ambassador Holds Reception to Celebrate Czechoslovak National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-05-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: More than five hundred Chinese and foreign guests gathered this afternoon in the magnificent hall of the Beijing Hotel to celebrate the National Day of the Czechoslovak Republic.

　　At this grand cocktail party, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, and Vice Chairman Chen Shutong all toasted with the host of the reception, Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Grigor, to celebrate the friendship between the people of China and Czechoslovakia.

　　Vice Chairman Zhu De had a cordial conversation with Ambassador Grigor, mentioning that he had visited many places in Czechoslovakia not long ago and was left with a very good impression. Vice Chairman Zhu De also spoke with Czechoslovak Minister of Health and Chairman of the Czechoslovak People's Party, Father Plojhar, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of Czechoslovakia, Kozor, and Deputy Minister of Heavy Machinery Industry, Šmok.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai had cordial conversations with heads of diplomatic missions and commercial representatives from various countries in the hall. Premier Zhou Enlai expressed to the Chargé d'affaires of the Pakistani Embassy that he looked forward to meeting Prime Minister Mohammed Ali.

　　It was a pleasant and relaxed reception. Chinese and foreign guests chatted happily and clinked glasses. Pleasant music played in the hall. Ambassador Grigor and his wife were everywhere, graciously receiving guests.

　　Guo Rong, Director of the China-Czechoslovakia Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative, also rushed from the suburbs today to offer congratulations. He told Ambassador Grigor that the members of the cooperative had received the ambassador's letter, and tonight the members would also hold a celebration meeting to celebrate the National Day of the Czechoslovak Republic.

### 捷大使举行酒会庆祝捷国庆节

1956-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷大使举行酒会庆祝捷国庆节
    新华社9日讯  五百多位中外人士今天下午聚集在北京饭店金碧辉煌的大厅里，欢庆捷克斯洛伐克共和国国庆节。
    在这个盛大的鸡尾酒会上，朱德副主席、周恩来总理、李先念副总理和陈叔通副委员长，都同酒会的主人——捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使格里哥尔干杯祝贺中捷两国人民的友谊。
    朱德副主席同格里哥尔大使亲切谈话，他谈到不久前访问捷克斯洛伐克时，曾参观过许多地方，留下很美好的印象。朱德副主席还同捷克斯洛伐克卫生部长、捷人民党主席普洛伊哈神父，捷克斯洛伐克对外贸易部第一副部长科佐尔和重型机械工业部副部长什莫克等进行了交谈。
    周恩来总理在大厅里分别同各国驻华使节和商务代表进行了亲切谈话。周恩来总理向巴基斯坦大使馆的临时代办表示，他盼望和穆罕默德·阿里总理会面。
    这是一次愉快的、轻松的酒会。中外人士在一起欢乐地交谈着，碰着杯。大厅里奏着悦耳的音乐。格里哥尔大使和他的夫人到处殷勤地招待着客人。
    中捷友好农业生产合作社主任郭荣今天也从郊区赶来祝贺。他告诉格里哥尔大使，合作社的社员们已收到了大使的信，今晚社员们还要举行一个庆祝大会，庆祝捷克斯洛伐克共和国国庆。
