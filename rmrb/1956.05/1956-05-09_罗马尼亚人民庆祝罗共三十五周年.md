Title: Romanian People Celebrate the 35th Anniversary of the Romanian Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1956-05-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, May 8th: May 8th marks the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Romanian Communist Party (now the Romanian Workers' Party).

The Romanian working class and the entire people are celebrating this significant event amidst a great political and labor upsurge. Grand rallies, evening parties, and meetings between working people and veteran fighters and old party members of the workers' movement have been organized in enterprises and institutions, cities and villages throughout the country.

On May 7th, a conference of capital youth activists was held in Bucharest to celebrate this anniversary. Ni. Ceausescu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, delivered a report at the meeting.

Today, Romanian newspapers published editorials celebrating the Party's birthday, as well as memoirs of participants in the First Party Congress and historical materials about the Party.

### 罗马尼亚人民庆祝罗共三十五周年

1956-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚人民庆祝罗共三十五周年
    新华社8日讯  据塔斯社布加勒斯特八日讯：五月八日是罗马尼亚共产党（即现在的罗马尼亚工人党）成立三十五周年纪念日。
    罗马尼亚工人阶级和全体人民在巨大的政治和劳动高潮中庆祝这个意义重大的事件。在全国的企业和机关、城市和乡村里，都组织了隆重的集会、晚会以及劳动人民同工人运动老战士和老党员的会面。
    五月七日，在布加勒斯特举行了一个首都青年积极分子会议，来庆祝这个纪念日。罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会政治局委员尼·齐奥塞斯库在会上作了报告。
    今天罗马尼亚报纸都发表了庆祝党的生日的社论，登载了第一次党代表大会参加者的回忆录和党史材料。
