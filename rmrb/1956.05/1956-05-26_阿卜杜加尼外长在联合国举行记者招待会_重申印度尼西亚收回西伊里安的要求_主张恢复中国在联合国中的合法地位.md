Title: Foreign Minister Abdulgani Holds Press Conference at UN, Reaffirms Indonesia's Demand for the Return of West Irian, Advocates Restoring China's Legitimate Seat in the UN
Author: 
Date: 1956-05-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 25th: According to a United Press International dispatch from the United Nations: Indonesian Foreign Minister Abdulgani, who accompanied Sukarno on his visit to the United States, said at a press conference on the 24th that it "is not impossible" that Indonesia will submit the West Irian issue to the UN General Assembly for the third consecutive year.

    Abdulgani emphasized Indonesia's demand for the return of West Irian territory, saying: As long as West Irian is occupied by a foreign country, "we will never feel safe."

    Abdulgani said that after discussing the West Irian issue with Dulles, he had learned that "the United States cannot definitively support" Indonesia. He believes that the United States' understanding of this national demand of Indonesia "has not reached a sufficient level, and we will make this understanding more complete."

    Abdulgani said that the legitimate seat of the People's Republic of China should be restored in the United Nations. Indonesia takes this position not only because of "the actual situation in Asia," but also because "a government and country rooted" among hundreds of millions of people "must not be excluded from the conference table for discussing peace issues."

    Abdulgani then reiterated that Indonesia will continue to pursue a peaceful and neutral policy. He said that Indonesia is willing to maintain normal relations with the Netherlands, and Indonesia does not exclude direct assistance from any country.

### 阿卜杜加尼外长在联合国举行记者招待会  重申印度尼西亚收回西伊里安的要求  主张恢复中国在联合国中的合法地位

1956-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    阿卜杜加尼外长在联合国举行记者招待会
    重申印度尼西亚收回西伊里安的要求
    主张恢复中国在联合国中的合法地位
    据新华社25日讯　据合众社联合国消息：随同苏加诺访问美国的印度尼西亚外交部长阿卜杜加尼二十四日在记者招待会上说，印度尼西亚连续第三年把西伊里安问题提交联合国大会并“不是不可能的”。
    阿卜杜加尼着重谈到印度尼西亚对于收回西伊里安领土的要求，他说：只要西伊里安被外国占领，“我们就永远不会感到安全”。
    阿卜杜加尼说，在他同杜勒斯讨论了西伊里安问题后，他已经了解到“美国是不能肯定地支持”印度尼西亚的。他认为，美国对印度尼西亚的这个民族要求的了解“还没有达到充分的程度，我们将使这种了解比较充分”。
    阿卜杜加尼说，应该恢复中华人民共和国在联合国的合法地位。印度尼西亚采取这种立场不只是由于“亚洲的实际情况”，而且还由于：一个在数亿人民中间“扎了根的政府和国家决不能被排除在讨论和平问题的会议桌之外”。
    阿卜杜加尼接着重申，印度尼西亚将继续执行和平中立政策。他说，印度尼西亚愿意同荷兰保持正常的关系，印度尼西亚不排除任何国家给予的直接援助。
