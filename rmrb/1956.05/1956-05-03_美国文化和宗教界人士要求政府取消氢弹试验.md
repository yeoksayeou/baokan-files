Title: American Cultural and Religious Figures Demand Government Cancel Hydrogen Bomb Tests
Author:
Date: 1956-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, May 1st (TASS) – Nineteen American cultural leaders and representatives of religious organizations have published an open letter to Eisenhower demanding the cancellation of the US hydrogen bomb tests scheduled for early May in the Pacific.

The letter pointed out that the US conducting hydrogen bomb tests would force other countries to test their weapons. The letter also said that the Soviet Union has suggested "no more tests, and if we do the same."

The letter concluded: "We ask you to delay the hydrogen bomb tests in the Pacific this spring and to seek an agreement with Russia on the issue of stopping hydrogen bomb tests. In making this request, we recognize that such an agreement can only be a first step towards a more comprehensive international solution, but it can be an important step, and holding hydrogen bomb tests again in the Pacific may... lose the confidence of most Asian countries in our peaceful intentions."

Signatories to the letter include: Mr. Boss, Executive Secretary of the "World Peace Committee" of the Methodist Church; Professor Riesman of the University of Chicago; Davison, one of the two chairmen of the Chicago Atomic Scientists Association; and Mr. Sparling, President of "Roosevelt University" in Chicago.

### 美国文化和宗教界人士要求政府取消氢弹试验

1956-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国文化和宗教界人士要求政府取消氢弹试验
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社纽约一日讯：美国十九名文化界领袖和宗教团体代表发表了一封给艾森豪威尔的公开信，要求取消美国定于五月初在太平洋举行的氢弹试验。
    信中指出，美国进行氢弹试验将迫使其他国家试验它们的武器，信中还说，苏联已经建议“不再进行试验，如果我们也这样做的话”。
    这封信最后说：“我们要求你延迟今年春天在太平洋上举行的氢弹试验，并且设法就停止氢弹试验问题同俄国取得协议。我们在要求这一点的时候认识到，这种协议只能是导致更加全面的国际解决的第一步，但是它可以是重要的一步，而再在太平洋上举行氢弹试验也许会……丧失大多数亚洲国家对我们的和平意图的信心。”
    在信上签名的有：美以美会“世界和平委员会”执行秘书博斯、芝加哥大学教授里斯门、芝加哥原子科学家协会两位主席之一戴维逊、芝加哥“罗斯福大学”校长斯巴林等。
