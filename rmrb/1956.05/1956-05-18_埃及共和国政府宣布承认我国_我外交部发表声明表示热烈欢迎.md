Title: The Government of the Arab Republic of Egypt Announces Recognition of Our Country; Our Ministry of Foreign Affairs Issues Statement Expressing Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1956-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 17th. The Cabinet of the Arab Republic of Egypt, at the conclusion of its meeting on the evening of May 16, 1956, formally announced the Egyptian government's recognition of the People's Republic of China. This decision was formally communicated to Li Yingji, the Commercial Representative of the People's Republic of China to the Arab Republic of Egypt, by Nusseir, the Minister of Commerce and Industry of the Arab Republic of Egypt, and Shaburi, the Director of the Office of the Prime Minister of Egypt.

At the same time, Mr. Faer, the Commercial Representative of the Arab Republic of Egypt to the People's Republic of China, also formally notified the government of the People's Republic of China of this decision.

Xinhua News Agency, May 17th. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China issued a statement on May 17th, the full text of which is as follows:

Since the Asian-African Conference, especially since the contacts between the Premiers of China and Egypt during that conference, the relations between the People's Republic of China and the Arab Republic of Egypt have developed daily. On May 16, 1956, the government of the Arab Republic of Egypt formally announced its recognition of the People's Republic of China. The Chinese government and the Chinese people warmly welcome this friendly gesture by the Egyptian government. The People's Republic of China, on the day of its founding, declared its willingness to establish diplomatic relations with any foreign country that adheres to the principles of equality, mutual benefit, and mutual respect for sovereignty and territorial integrity.The government of China hopes that China and Egypt will be able to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic envoys soon.

### 埃及共和国政府宣布承认我国  我外交部发表声明表示热烈欢迎

1956-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及共和国政府宣布承认我国
    我外交部发表声明表示热烈欢迎
    新华社17日讯　埃及共和国内阁会议在1956年5月16日晚上举行的会议结束时正式宣布了埃及政府承认中华人民共和国。这个决定是由埃及共和国的工商部长努赛尔和埃及总理办公厅主任沙布立正式通知中华人民共和国驻埃及共和国的商务代表李应吉的。
    同时埃及共和国驻中华人民共和国商务代表法尔先生也将此项决定正式通知了中华人民共和国政府。
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国外交部五月十七日发表声明，全文如下：
    自从亚非会议以来，特别是中国和埃及两国总理在那次会议期间进行接触以来，中华人民共和国和埃及共和国的关系日益发展。1956年5月16日埃及共和国政府正式宣布承认中华人民共和国。中国政府和中国人民热烈地欢迎埃及政府这一友好的表示。中华人民共和国在成立的一天就曾经宣告愿意同遵守平等、互利和互相尊重主权和领土完整的任何外国建立外交关系。中国政府希望中埃两国能够很快地建立外交关系并且互换外交使节。
