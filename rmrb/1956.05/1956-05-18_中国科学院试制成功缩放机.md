Title: China Academy of Sciences Successfully Trials Reduction Pantograph
Author:
Date: 1956-05-18
Page: 3

    China Academy of Sciences Successfully Trials Reduction Pantograph
    Researchers at the Instrument Hall of the Chinese Academy of Sciences have successfully trial-produced a precision instrument essential for manufacturing optical instruments: the reduction pantograph. This reduction pantograph is used to engrave precision markings or graphics on optical instrument parts. Its precision reaches an error of only three-thousandths of a millimeter.
             (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国科学院试制成功缩放机

1956-05-18
第3版()
专栏：

    中国科学院试制成功缩放机
    中国科学院仪器馆研究人员试制成功一种制造光学仪器不可缺少的精密仪器——缩放机。这种缩放机是在制造光学仪器零件上刻划精密记号或图形用的。它的精密度达到千分之三毫米的误差。
             （新华社）
