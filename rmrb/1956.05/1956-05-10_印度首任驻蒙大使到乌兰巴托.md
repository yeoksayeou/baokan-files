Title: India's First Ambassador to Mongolia Arrives in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1956-05-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar dispatch, TASS Ulaanbaatar dispatch: Ratan Kumar Nehru, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of India to the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Ulaanbaatar on May 8.

On the same day, Mongolian Foreign Minister D. Adilbish received Ambassador Nehru to discuss matters concerning his upcoming presentation of credentials to the Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia.

### 印度首任驻蒙大使到乌兰巴托

1956-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度首任驻蒙大使到乌兰巴托
    新华社9日讯  塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：印度共和国驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使拉坦·库马尔·尼赫鲁于五月八日到达乌兰巴托。
    在同一天，蒙古外交部长达奥·阿吉尔毕希接见了尼赫鲁大使，商谈关于他即将向蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席递交国书事宜。
