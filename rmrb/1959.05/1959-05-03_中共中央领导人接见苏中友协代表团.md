Title: CCP Central Leaders Receive Delegation from the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1959-05-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd. CCP Central Leaders Zhou Enlai, Lin Biao, Deng Xiaoping, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, and Peng Zhen received all members of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation headed by Fedorov on the afternoon of the 2nd and had a cordial conversation. Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, were present at the reception.

Also present at the reception were Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin and Counselor of the Embassy Sudarikov. (Attached picture)
    
    Vice Chairman of the CCP Central Committee Zhou Enlai talking with Fedorov (center), head of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation. On the right is Ambassador Yudin.

### 中共中央领导人接见苏中友协代表团

1959-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见苏中友协代表团
    新华社2日讯　中共中央领导人周恩来、林彪、邓小平、林伯渠、董必武、彭真在2日下午接见了以费多罗夫为首的苏中友好协会代表团全体团员，作了亲切的谈话。接见时在座的有中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，秘书长钱俊瑞。
    接见时，苏联驻中国大使尤金和大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫也在座。（附图片）
    中共中央副主席周恩来和苏中友好协会代表团团长费多罗夫（中）谈话。右为尤金大使。
    新华社记者　塔吉古勒摄
