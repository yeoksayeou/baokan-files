Title: PLA Logistics Work Advanced Units and Activists Representative Meeting Calls: Serve the Troops Diligently
Author:
Date: 1959-05-24
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd: The Second Representative Meeting of Advanced Units and Activists in Logistics Work of the Chinese People's Liberation Army successfully concluded today. A "learn from the advanced, catch up with the advanced" movement, centered on diligently serving the troops, continuing to exert great effort, accelerating logistics construction, and adapting to the needs of our army, will be launched more deeply and extensively on the logistics front through this meeting.

    During the meeting, Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo attended and delivered a speech, encouraging the logistics soldiers to make greater achievements in the continued Great Leap Forward.

    Today, the conference unanimously adopted a letter of proposal to all military logistics personnel. The proposal calls on all logistics personnel to take action and offer suggestions, continue to carry out technical innovation and scientific research, improve logistics technology and equipment, improve operating methods, improve work efficiency, improve scientific and technological levels, and adapt to the needs of the entire army's construction. The proposal calls on all personnel to continue to implement the policy of building the army through diligence and thrift, promote the style of diligence and thrift, practice economy, maximize the effectiveness of every penny and every item, save grain, coal, and fuel, and tap the potential for savings in all aspects to support socialist construction. The proposal also calls on everyone to study diligently, seriously study Marxism-Leninism and Chairman Mao's works, improve their military and political theoretical level and professional ability, carry forward the glorious tradition of our army as both a fighting force and a production force, do a good job in agricultural and sideline production, and carry out the patriotic health campaign in depth. All logistics personnel must diligently and wholeheartedly serve the troops, be unafraid of hardship, be unafraid of difficulty, work hard without complaint, bury themselves in hard work, carry forward the fine style of hard work, simplicity, unity and mutual assistance, share weal and woe with the people, and do their job well.

    This conference lasted twelve days. Fifty-eight representatives introduced the achievements and experiences of themselves or their units since the Great Leap Forward last year. According to the different positions of the representatives, the conference also held separate professional meetings on military supplies, ordnance, finance, health, transportation, etc., to carefully exchange experiences in various professional work.

    During the meeting, some supply personnel also held a forum to exchange experiences in saving grain and coal.

    At today's closing ceremony, General Li Jukui, on behalf of the General Logistics Department, presented flags and certificates of award to the 788 representatives of advanced units and activists who attended the meeting. Lieutenant General Li Xuesan, in his speech, encouraged all the representatives to closely connect with the masses, be modest and prudent, guard against arrogance and rashness, follow the spirit of combining revolutionary enthusiasm and scientific analysis, make persistent efforts, and continue to leap forward under the leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao.

### 解放军后勤工作先进单位和积极分子代表会议号召  勤勤恳恳为部队服务

1959-05-24
第2版()
专栏：

    解放军后勤工作先进单位和积极分子代表会议号召
    勤勤恳恳为部队服务
    新华社23日讯　中国人民解放军第二次后勤工作先进单位和积极分子代表会议今天胜利闭幕。一个以勤勤恳恳为部队服务，继续鼓足干劲，加速后勤建设，适应我军需要的“学先进，赶先进”运动，将通过这次大会在后勤战线上更加深入广泛地开展起来。
    会议期间，国务院副总理薄一波到会讲了话，勉励后勤战士们在继续大跃进中作出更大的成绩。
    今天，大会一致通过了向全军后勤人员的倡议书。倡议书号召全体后勤人员，人人动手，个个献策，继续开展技术革新和科学研究工作，来改善后勤技术装备，改进操作方法，提高工作效率，提高科学技术水平，适应全军建设的需要。倡议书号召全体人员继续贯彻勤俭建军方针，大兴勤俭朴素之风，厉行节约，发挥一钱一物的最大效能，节约粮食、煤炭和油料，从各方面挖掘节约潜力，支援社会主义建设。倡议书还号召大家勤学苦练，认真学习马克思列宁主义和毛主席著作，提高军事、政治理论水平和业务能力，发扬我军既是战斗队又是生产队的光荣传统，搞好农副业生产并深入开展除四害、讲卫生运动。全体后勤人员，必须勤勤恳恳，全心全意为部队服务，不怕苦，不怕难，任劳任怨，埋头苦干，发扬艰苦朴素、团结互助，与人民同甘共苦的优良作风，作好本职工作。
    这次大会共开了十二天。有五十八名代表在大会上介绍了自己或本单位从去年大跃进以来取得的成绩和经验。大会根据代表们各种不同的职务，还分别召开了军需、军械、财务、卫生、运输等专业会议，细致地交流了各专业工作的经验。
    会议期间，一部分给养人员还举行座谈会交流了节约粮食和煤炭的经验。
    在今天的闭幕式上，李聚奎上将代表总后勤部向到会的七百八十八位先进单位代表和积极分子发了奖旗和奖状。李雪三中将在讲话中勉励全体代表，在党和毛主席的领导下，密切联系群众，虚心谨慎，戒骄戒躁，遵循革命热情和科学分析相结合的精神，再接再厉，继续跃进。
