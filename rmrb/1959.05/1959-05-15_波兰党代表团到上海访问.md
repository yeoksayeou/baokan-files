Title: Polish Party Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-05-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 14th. The Polish United Workers' Party delegation, led by Jerzy Morawski, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, arrived in Shanghai by train this morning from Hangzhou.

　　Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Party Committee, Shi Ximin and Liu Shuzhou, alternate secretaries of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Party Committee, and heads of various departments of the Municipal Party Committee greeted them at the station. Also present at the station to welcome them was the Polish Consul General in Shanghai, Biernacki.

　　In the morning, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Shi Ximin, toured the city.

### 波兰党代表团到上海访问

1959-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰党代表团到上海访问
    新华社上海14日电　由波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、书记处书记耶日·莫拉夫斯基率领的波兰统一工人党代表团，今天上午由杭州乘火车到达上海。
    中共中央政治局委员、上海市委第一书记柯庆施，中共上海市委书记处候补书记石西民、刘述周和市委各部门负责人到车站欢迎。到车站欢迎的还有波兰驻上海总领事贝尔纳茨基。
    上午，贵宾们在石西民陪同下，游览了市区。
