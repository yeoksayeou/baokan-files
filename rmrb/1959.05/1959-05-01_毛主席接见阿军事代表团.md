Title: Chairman Mao Meets Algerian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-05-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: Chairman Mao Zedong received Omar Oussedik, head of the Algerian military delegation, and all members of the delegation this evening.

Present at the reception were General Huang Kecheng and Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan.

### 毛主席接见阿军事代表团

1959-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见阿军事代表团
    新华社30日讯　毛泽东主席今天晚间接见了阿尔及利亚军事代表团团长奥马尔·乌西迪克和代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的，有黄克诚大将、杨秀山中将。
