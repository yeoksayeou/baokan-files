Title: Hungarian Prime Minister Münnich Congratulates Premier Zhou

Author:

Date: 1959-05-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Münnich Ferenc, head of the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation currently visiting our country and Prime Minister of the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, congratulated Zhou Enlai on his appointment as Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China.
　　Prime Minister Münnich Ferenc's congratulations to Premier Zhou were announced in a press release by the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation.

### 匈总理明尼赫祝贺周总理

1959-05-04
第3版()
专栏：

    匈总理明尼赫祝贺周总理
    新华社3日讯　正在我国访问的匈牙利党政代表团团长、匈牙利工农革命政府总理明尼赫·费伦茨博士对周恩来被任命为中华人民共和国国务院总理表示祝贺。
    明尼赫·费伦茨总理对周总理的祝贺是匈牙利党政代表团在一项新闻公报中宣布的。
