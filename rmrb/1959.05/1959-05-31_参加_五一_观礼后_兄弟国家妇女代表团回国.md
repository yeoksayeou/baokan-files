Title: Women's Delegations from Fraternal Countries Return Home After Attending "May Day" Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1959-05-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- Women's delegations from the Soviet Union, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mongolia, Albania, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Poland, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, who were invited by the All-China Women's Federation to attend the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and to conduct visits in our country, have successively returned home.

### 参加“五一”观礼后  兄弟国家妇女代表团回国

1959-05-31
第3版()
专栏：

    参加“五一”观礼后
    兄弟国家妇女代表团回国
    新华社30日讯　应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼和进行参观访问的苏联、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、蒙古、阿尔巴尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、波兰和越南民主共和国的妇女代表团已陆续回国。
