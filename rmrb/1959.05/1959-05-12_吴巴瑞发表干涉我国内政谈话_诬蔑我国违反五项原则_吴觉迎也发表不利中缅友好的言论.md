Title: U Ba Swe Issues Speech Interfering in China's Internal Affairs, Slandering China for Violating the Five Principles; U Kyaw Nyein Also Issues Statements Detrimental to Sino-Burmese Friendship

Author:
Date: 1959-05-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 11th: The chairman of the Burmese Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, U Ba Swe, insisted that China's quelling of the Tibetan rebellion "seriously violated the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence." According to him, this "constitutes a serious threat to the peace and security of Asian countries." According to a report in the "Burma New Times" on the 10th, U Ba Swe also stated at the Trade Union Congress (Burma) Third Supreme Council meeting held in Rangoon on the 9th that the Tibet issue, the Taiwan issue, and the issues of Jinmen and Mazu Islands need to be resolved quickly, "because these issues risk pushing the world towards another war."

According to reports, U Ba Swe explained the views of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League on these issues: First, China's seat in the United Nations should be restored; second, it advocates that Taiwan—an inseparable part of Chinese territory—should hold a "plebiscite under the auspices of the United Nations"; third, it believes that Tibetan affairs "should be handled in accordance with the 1951 China-Tibet agreement."

He also advocated that neutral countries "should be allowed" to help resolve the Tibetan issue, which is purely China's internal affair.

On the same day, in a conversation with a reporter from "The Nation," U Ba Swe believed that Tibet only "accepted China's suzerainty." According to him, the Tibetan "autonomy" stipulated in the 1951 "China-Tibet" agreement "has been undermined." This "may also mean the expansion of the Cold War."

Another dispatch: Another leader of the Burmese Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, U Kyaw Nyein, who is visiting Israel, said in an inflammatory tone in Tel Aviv on the 7th: "We find ourselves in the same unpleasant position as India because we want to maintain friendly relations with China, but this is not always easy."

### 吴巴瑞发表干涉我国内政谈话  诬蔑我国违反五项原则  吴觉迎也发表不利中缅友好的言论

1959-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    吴巴瑞发表干涉我国内政谈话
    诬蔑我国违反五项原则
    吴觉迎也发表不利中缅友好的言论
    新华社仰光11日电　缅甸巩固的反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴巴瑞硬说中国平定西藏叛乱“严重地违背了和平共处五项原则”。据他说，这“构成对亚洲国家的和平和安全的严重威胁”。据“缅甸新时代报”10日报道，吴巴瑞在9日在仰光举行的工会大会
  （缅甸）第三届最高委员会会议上还表示西藏问题、台湾问题以及金门和马祖岛群的问题需要迅速解决，“因为这些问题有把世界推向又一次大战的危险”。
    据报道，吴巴瑞说明了巩固的反法西斯人民自由同盟对这些问题的看法：第一、应该恢复中国在联合国中的席位；第二、它主张台湾——中国领土不可分割的一部分——却应该在“联合国的主持下举行一次公民投票”；第三、它认为西藏事务“应该根据1951年中国—西藏协议来处理”。
    他还主张“应该允许”中立国家来帮助解决纯属中国内政的西藏问题。
    在同一天，吴巴瑞在对“民族报”记者发表谈话时认为，西藏只“接受了中国的宗主权”。据他说，1951年“中国—西藏”协议所规定的西藏“自治权遭到了破坏”。这“可能也意味着冷战的扩大”。
    又讯：在以色列访问的缅甸巩固的反法西斯人民自由同盟的另一领导人吴觉迎，7日在特拉维夫以煽动的口吻说：“我们发见我们处于同印度一样的不愉快的地位，因为我们希望同中国保持友好关系，但是这并不总是很容易的。”
