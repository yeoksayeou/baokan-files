Lu 591 Scissor-Type Harvester

The Lu 591 scissor-type harvester is suitable for harvesting wheat with a yield of around 500 catties per mu. The harvested wheat falls down in sequence, arranged in rows, to facilitate bundling and transport. During operation, it is pulled by a strong animal, with one person controlling the animal, one person operating the machine, and two to three people bundling and transporting the wheat. It can harvest approximately 20 mu per day. The specifications, structure, and usage method are introduced as follows:

I. Main Specifications

(1) Cutting width: 60 cm; (2) Stubble height: 8 to 20 cm; (3) Ground wheel diameter: 50 cm (excluding the thickness of the cleats); (4) Crankshaft speed: calculated at a cattle walking speed of 0.8 meters per second, it is 647.8 revolutions per minute; calculated at a mule or horse walking speed of 1.1 meters per second, it is 943.6 revolutions per minute; (5) Average cutting speed of the blade: calculated at a cattle walking speed, it is 1.08 meters per second; calculated at a mule or horse walking speed, it is 1.57 meters per second; (6) Average working pulling force: about 70 kg; (7) Wooden overturning wheel diameter: 1.2 meters; (8) Number of wooden overturning wheel blades: 4; (9) Wooden overturning wheel speed: 30 to 44 revolutions per minute.

II. Structure and Function

The structure of the scissor-type harvester (Figure 1) mainly includes cutting, wooden overturning wheel, transmission system, frame parts, etc. A brief description is as follows:

(1) Cutting part (Figure 2): Consists of a moving blade and a fixed blade. During operation, the moving blade reciprocates on top of the fixed blade, cutting the wheat down.

(2) Wooden overturning wheel part (see Figure 1): The structure of the wooden overturning wheel is very simple, consisting of four wooden strips nailed into two crosses, with four blades. The wooden overturning wheel shaft is a square shaft made of hardwood, running through the center of the frame, with a 20 cm diameter pulley mounted on the shaft and both ends mounted in the shaft holes of the wooden overturning wheel support.

(3) Ground wheel and transmission part: Consists of two ground wheels, a main shaft, a sub-shaft, a crankshaft, four pulleys driving the cutting part, two pulleys driving the wooden overturning wheel, and a pair of bevel gears.

(4) Frame part and others: The frame is entirely made of wood. It consists of four longitudinal beams, three cross beams, two uprights, three wooden overturning wheel supports, two handles, inner and outer baffles, a divider, and a front guide wheel. It bears the entire harvester's components and forces, playing the role of connecting and supporting other components.

III. Matters to Note During Manufacturing

(1) The scissor-type harvester, except for the cutting part, gears, transmission shafts, and other components, is entirely made of wood. Because it is subjected to greater force during harvesting, the frame, wooden overturning wheel, pulleys, etc., must be made of dry hardwood to prevent deformation and premature damage.

(2) The bearings of this machine are all embedded in the wooden frame. Since iron and wood are not easily firmly combined, special attention must be paid to embedding them tightly and correctly during manufacturing. Otherwise, wheel axle skew, poor gear meshing, and other phenomena are likely to occur, which will increase resistance and easily cause damage.

(3) The specifications of the cutting part directly affect the quality of harvesting. During manufacturing, attention must be paid to planing the knife bar flat and straight, and the moving blade and fixed blade must maintain a close fit. Moreover, the blade must be carburized and quenched to keep the blade sharp. (Attached pictures)

### 鲁591号剪刀式收割机

1959-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　鲁591号剪刀式收割机
    鲁591号剪刀式收割机适于收割亩产五百斤左右的小麦，割下的小麦顺序倒下，排列成行，以利捆运。工作时，由一头壮畜牵引，一人掌握牲口，一人操纵机器，二到三人捆麦、运麦，每天约可收割二十亩左右。现将其规格、构造、使用方法介绍如下：
    一、主要规格
    （1）割幅：六十公分；（2）割茬高度：八至二十公分；（3）地轮直径：五十公分（不包括抓地板厚度）；（4）曲拐轴转速：按牛行步速每秒钟零点八公尺计算，每分钟为六百四十七点八转；按骡马步速每秒钟一点一公尺计算，每分钟为九百四十三点六转；（5）刀片平均切割速度：按牛行步速计算，每秒钟为一点零八公尺；按骡马步速计算，每秒钟为一点五十七公尺；（6）平均工作拉力：七十公斤左右；（7）木翻轮直径：一点二公尺；（8）木翻轮叶片数：4；（9）木翻轮转速：每分钟三十至四十四转。
    二、构造和作用
    剪刀式收割机的构造（图1）主要包括切割、木翻轮、传动系统、机架部分等。简述如下：
    （1）切割部分（图2）：由动刀和定刀组成。工作时动刀在定刀上面往复运动，把小麦割下来。
    （2）木翻轮部分（见图1）：木翻轮的构造很简单，由四根木条钉成二个十字架，有四个叶片。木翻轮轴是由硬木做成的方轴，贯穿木架当中，轴上装有一个直径二十公分的皮带轮，两端装在木翻轮支柱的轴孔里。
    （3）地轮和传动部分：由两个地轮，一根主轴，一根副轴，一根曲拐轴，四个驱动切割部分的皮带轮、二个驱动木翻轮的皮带轮和一对锥齿轮组成。
    （4）机架部分和其他：机架全用木制。由四根顺梁、三根横梁、两根立柱、三根木翻轮支架二根把手、内外挡板、分禾器、前导轮组成。它承担着整个收割机的全部机件和力量，起着连结和支持其他机件的作用。
    三、制造中应注意的事项
    （1）剪刀式收割机除切割部分、齿轮、传动轴等部件外，全用木制。由于收割时受力较大，故机架、木翻轮、皮带轮等均须采用干燥的硬木制造，以防变形和过早损坏。
    （2）本机的轴承都镶在木架上，由于铁和木头结合起来，不易牢固，故在制造时须特别注意将其镶紧镶正，否则容易出现轮轴歪斜、齿轮咬合不良等现象，既增加了阻力，还容易损坏。
    （3）切割部分的规格直接影响收割的好坏，制造时必须注意将刀杆刨平、刨直，动刀和定刀要保持紧密配合。而且刀片要经过渗炭淬火，使刀刃保持锋利。（附图片）
