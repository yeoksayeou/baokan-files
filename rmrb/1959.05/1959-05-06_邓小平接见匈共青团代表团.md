Title: Deng Xiaoping Receives Hungarian Communist Youth League Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-05-06
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, received the Hungarian Communist Youth League delegation headed by Komocsin Zoltán, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Communist Youth League, today and had a cordial conversation with them.

　　Present at the reception were Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Hu Keshi and Wang Wei, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League.

### 邓小平接见匈共青团代表团

1959-05-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　邓小平接见匈共青团代表团
    新华社5日讯　中共中央总书记邓小平今天接见了以匈牙利共青团中央第一书记科莫钦·佐尔坦为首的匈牙利共青团代表团，并和他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中共中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆，共青团中央第一书记胡耀邦，共青团中央书记处书记胡克实、王伟等。
