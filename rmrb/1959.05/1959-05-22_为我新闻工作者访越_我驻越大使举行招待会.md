Title: For Visit to Vietnam by Chinese Journalists, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1959-05-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 21st. Chinese Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, He Wei, held a reception on the evening of the 20th for the visit of the Chinese Journalists Delegation to Vietnam, inviting members of the Vietnamese press. Huang Song, Vice Chairman of the Vietnamese Journalists Association and Editor-in-Chief of the "People's Daily," the organ of the Vietnamese Labor Party, Huang Jun, Director of the Vietnam News Agency, and Chen Mingjue, Director of the Information Office of the Premier's Office, were invited to attend the reception.

### 为我新闻工作者访越  我驻越大使举行招待会

1959-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    为我新闻工作者访越
    我驻越大使举行招待会
    新华社河内21日电　中国驻越南民主共和国大使何伟20日晚上为中国新闻工作者代表团访问越南举行招待会，招待越南新闻界。越南新闻工作者协会副主席、越南劳动党机关报“人民报”总编辑黄松，越南通讯社社长黄俊，总理府新闻处处长陈明爵等应邀出席招待会。
