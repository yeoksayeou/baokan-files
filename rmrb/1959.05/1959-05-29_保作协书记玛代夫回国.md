Title: Bulgarian Writers' Union Secretary, Madaev, Returns Home

Author: 
Date: 1959-05-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: Pavel Madaev, Secretary of the Bulgarian Writers' Union, member of the editorial board of the "Flame" literary magazine, and poet, who visited China according to the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

### 保作协书记玛代夫回国

1959-05-29
第5版()
专栏：

    保作协书记玛代夫回国
    新华社28日讯　根据中保文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国访问的保加利亚作家协会书记、“火熖”文学杂志编委、诗人巴维尔·玛代夫，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
