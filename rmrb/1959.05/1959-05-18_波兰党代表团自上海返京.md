Title: Polish Party Delegation Returns to Beijing from Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-05-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 17th - The Polish United Workers' Party delegation, headed by Jerzy Morawski, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee, returned to Beijing by plane today after a three-day visit to Shanghai.

　　When the delegation left Shanghai, those who went to the airport to see them off included: Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee, Shi Ximin and Liu Shuzhou, alternate secretaries of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, and heads of various departments of the Municipal Committee.  Bernadzki, the Polish Consul General in Shanghai, also went to the airport to see them off.

　　Last night, Ke Qingshi hosted a banquet for the distinguished guests and had cordial conversations.

　　During their stay in Shanghai, the delegation visited the No. 2 State Cotton Mill, Jiangnan Shipyard, Shanghai Broadcasting Equipment Factory, Fudan University, the Second Medical College, and the Hudong Workers' Cultural Palace.  The distinguished guests also held discussions with Ke Qingshi, Chen Pixian, Cao Diqiu, Ma Tianshui, Shi Ximin, Liu Shuzhou and other responsible persons of the Municipal Committee, as well as responsible persons of various relevant departments of the Municipal Committee.

### 波兰党代表团自上海返京

1959-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰党代表团自上海返京
    新华社17日讯　以波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、书记处书记耶日·莫拉夫斯基为首的波兰统一工人党代表团，在上海参观访问了三天以后，今天乘飞机回北京。
    代表团离开上海时，前往机场欢送的有：中共中央政治局委员、上海市委第一书记柯庆施，中共上海市委书记处候补书记石西民、刘述周以及市委各部门负责人等。波兰驻上海总领事贝尔纳茨基也到机场欢送。
    昨晚，柯庆施设宴招待贵宾，并作了亲切交谈。
    代表团在上海期间，曾参观访问了国棉二厂、江南造船厂、上海广播器材厂、复旦大学、第二医学院、沪东工人文化宫等处。贵宾们还同柯庆施、陈丕显、曹荻秋、马天水、石西民、刘述周等市委负责人以及市委各有关部门负责人举行了座谈。
