Title: All-China Women's Federation Chairman Cai Chang, Vice-Chairpersons Deng Yingchao, Kang Keqing Meet Panchen Lama's Mother and Apé Agyö Tseten Dolkar

Author:

Date: 1959-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and Vice-Chairpersons Deng Yingchao and Kang Keqing, along with the secretaries of the Secretariat, met this afternoon with the Panchen Lama's mother, Gyalyum Sonam Drolma, a member of the Shigatse Patriotic Women's Federation, and Apé Agyö Tseten Dolkar, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibetan Patriotic Women's Federation.

During the meeting, Chairman Cai Chang, on behalf of the All-China Women's Federation, extended a warm welcome to them. Apé Agyö Tseten Dolkar reported to the leaders of the All-China Women's Federation on the work of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibetan Patriotic Women's Federation. She expressed that in the future, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, and under the guidance of the All-China Women's Federation, the Preparatory Committee of the Tibetan Patriotic Women's Federation would actively carry out various tasks and strive to realize democratic reform in Tibet and build a new Tibet.

After Apé Agyö Tseten Dolkar reported on her work, Chairman Cai Chang gave principled instructions on women's work in the Tibetan region in the future, and entrusted Apé Agyö Tseten Dolkar to act as the director before Yabshi Tsering Dolma, the director of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibetan Patriotic Women's Federation, returns to the country (Yabshi Tsering Dolma accompanied the Dalai Lama to India when the Dalai Lama was abducted to India).

During the meeting, Chairman Cai Chang, on behalf of the All-China Women's Federation, presented a complete set of film projection equipment to the Preparatory Committee of the Tibetan Patriotic Women's Federation.

After the meeting, Chairman Cai Chang hosted a simple banquet to entertain Gyalyum Sonam Drolma and Apé Agyö Tseten Dolkar.

### 全国妇联蔡畅主席邓颖超康克清副主席  会见班禅母亲和阿沛·才丹卓噶

1959-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇联蔡畅主席邓颖超康克清副主席
    会见班禅母亲和阿沛·才丹卓噶
    新华社12日讯  全国妇联主席蔡畅，副主席邓颖超、康克清和书记处的书记们，今天下午会见了班禅大师的母亲、日喀则爱国妇女联谊会委员甲雍·索南卓玛和西藏爱国妇女联谊会筹委会副主任阿沛·才丹卓噶。
    会见时，蔡畅主席代表全国妇联向她们表示热烈欢迎。阿沛·才丹卓噶向全国妇联负责人汇报了西藏爱国妇女联谊会筹委会的工作情况。她表示今后在中国共产党和毛主席的领导下，在全国妇联的指导下，西藏爱国妇女联谊会筹委会将积极开展各项工作，为实现西藏的民主改革、建设新西藏而努力。
    蔡畅主席在阿沛·才丹卓噶汇报工作后，对今后西藏地区的妇女工作作了原则指示，并且委托阿沛·才丹卓噶在西藏爱国妇女联谊会筹委会主任尧西·泽仁卓玛未回国以前代理主任职务（尧西·泽仁卓玛是在达赖喇嘛被劫持去印度时，随同达赖喇嘛一起去印度的）。
    会见时，蔡畅主席代表全国妇联赠给西藏爱国妇女联谊会筹委会全套电影放映器材。
    会见后，蔡畅主席设便宴招待甲雍·索南卓玛和阿沛·才丹卓噶。
