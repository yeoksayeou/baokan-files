Title: Soviet Georgian Gymnastics Team Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 6th - The Soviet Georgian Republic men's and women's gymnastics team, a group of twenty led by Samsonidze, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
    After arriving in Beijing, the men's and women's gymnastics team will first go to Shanghai, and are scheduled to hold their first friendly competition with the Shanghai men's and women's gymnastics team on May 10.
    Gymnastics is relatively widespread in the Soviet Georgian Republic. The men's team once won fourth place in the 1958 All-Soviet Gymnastics Championship, and the women's team also won fifth place. The gymnastics team that came to China this time consists of seven male athletes and six female athletes, including two Honored Masters of Sport of the Soviet Union, and the other eleven are Master Athletes. The two Honored Masters of Sport are Sharabidze and Zhugli; they participated in the Soviet national gymnastics team that came to China in 1953.

### 苏联格鲁吉亚体操队到北京

1959-05-07
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联格鲁吉亚体操队到北京
    新华社6日讯　苏联格鲁吉亚共和国男、女体操队一行二十人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    这个由萨姆松尼泽率领的男、女体操队到北京后，将先去上海，预定5月10日同上海男、女体操队举行第一次友谊比赛。
    苏联格鲁吉亚共和国体操运动开展得比较普遍，男子队曾经获得1958年全苏体操冠军赛的第四名，女子队也获得第五名。这次来中国的体操队共有七名男运动员和六名女运动员，其中有两名苏联功勋运动员，其他十一名都是运动健将。两名功勋运动员是沙拉比泽和朱格丽，她们1953年曾参加苏联国家体操队来过中国。
