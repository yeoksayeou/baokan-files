Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Indonesian Industrial Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-05-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Indonesian Ministry of Industry delegation headed by Sanusi, Director of the Industrial Bureau of the Indonesian Ministry of Industry, this afternoon.
    Present at the reception were Liu Xiwen, Deputy Director of the Fourth Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Yang Wei and Peng Jinbo, Deputy Directors of the Complete Equipment Bureau, and Suleiman, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China.

### 陈毅副总理接见印度尼西亚工业考察团

1959-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见印度尼西亚工业考察团
    新华社16日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了以印度尼西亚工业部工业司长沙努西为首的印度尼西亚工业部考察团。
    接见时，对外贸易部第四局副局长刘希文，成套设备局副局长杨威、彭金波和印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办苏雷曼等也在座。
