Title: New Type of Thresher
Author: Min Guang
Date: 1959-05-12
Page: 2

    Leap Forward Showcase

    New Type of Thresher
    The Jiamusi Machinery Factory recently designed and trial-produced China's first 900-type double-roller, high-efficiency automatic thresher. This machine can thresh more than two and a half tons of grain per hour, but compared with the MCA large-scale thresher of the same efficiency, it saves more than 90% of materials. (Min Guang)

### 新型脱谷机
敏光
1959-05-12
第2版()
专栏：跃进橱窗

    新型脱谷机
    佳木斯机械厂最近设计并试制出我国第一台900型双滚桶、高效率的自动脱谷机。这种机器每小时可脱谷物二吨半以上，但和同等效率的MCA大型脱谷机相比，材料却要节约90％以上。（敏光）
