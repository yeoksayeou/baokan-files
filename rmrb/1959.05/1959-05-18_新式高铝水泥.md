Title: New-type High-Alumina Cement
Author: Lu Xiping
Date: 1959-05-18
Page: 2

    New-type High-Alumina Cement
    The Xi'an Baqiao Building Materials Factory has successfully tested a new type of high-alumina cement. Its characteristics are strong compressive resistance; after scientific identification, its compressive strength after twenty-four hours is 1,100 kilograms, its heat resistance is 1,410 degrees, and there is no shrinkage rate, making it most suitable for installing precision machine foundations. In extremely cold construction projects, the use of this cement can guarantee project quality. In addition, it can also be used for smelting rare metals, boilers, and other high-heat furnace walls.
                    (Lu Xiping)

### 新式高铝水泥
鲁西平
1959-05-18
第2版()
专栏：简讯

    新式高铝水泥
    西安坝桥建筑材料厂试验成功一种新式的高铝水泥。它的特点是耐压性强；经过科学鉴定，它在二十四小时的抗压强度为一千一百公斤，耐热度为一千四百一十度，没有收缩率，最适用于安装精密机器打底坐。在极寒的工程建筑中，使用这种水泥，可以保证工程质量。此外，它还可以用于熔炼稀有金属、锅炉和其他高热炉壁之用。
                    （鲁西平）
