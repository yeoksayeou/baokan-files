Title: Andreyev and Popova Congratulate Soong Ching Ling on "May Day"
Author:
Date: 1959-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, received a "May Day" congratulatory telegram from Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, and Popova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Council of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. In the congratulatory telegram, Andreyev and Popova sincerely wished the great Chinese people, under the leadership of the glorious Chinese Communist Party, new and brilliant victories in the socialist construction of the People's Republic of China. The telegram also wished that the eternal friendship and fraternal cooperation between the people of the Soviet Union and China—a powerful bulwark of world peace—would be increasingly consolidated and prosper.

### 安德烈耶夫、波波娃向宋庆龄祝贺“五一”

1959-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    安德烈耶夫、波波娃向宋庆龄祝贺“五一”
    新华社30日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄收到了苏联苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫、苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会理事会主席团主席波波娃发来的“五一”节贺电。安德烈耶夫和波波娃在贺电中衷心地祝愿伟大的中国人民，在光荣的中国共产党的领导下，在中华人民共和国的社会主义建设中，获得新的辉煌的胜利。贺电还祝苏中两国人民的永恒友谊和兄弟般的合作——世界和平的强大堡垒——日益巩固与繁荣。
