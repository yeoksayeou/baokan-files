Title: Council for Mutual Economic Assistance Meeting Opens; Observers from Vietnam, China, North Korea, and Mongolia Attend
Author:
Date: 1959-05-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 13th: The eleventh meeting of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance (COMECON) opened in Tirana this morning.

    Participating in the meeting were delegations from Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, and Czechoslovakia. Representatives from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, China, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Mongolia attended the meeting as observers.

    After the head of the Albanian delegation, Koleka, declared the meeting open, Albanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Labor Party, Shehu, congratulated the opening of the meeting on behalf of the Albanian government and party, and warmly congratulated the tenth anniversary of the establishment of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance.

    COMECON Secretary Fadeyev delivered a report on the work of the council at the meeting today.

### 经济互助委员会会议开幕  越中朝蒙派观察员出席

1959-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

    经济互助委员会会议开幕
    越中朝蒙派观察员出席
    新华社地拉那13日电  经济互助委员会第十一次会议今天上午在地拉那开幕。
    参加会议的有：阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联和捷克斯洛伐克的代表团。越南民主共和国、中国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和蒙古的代表以观察员身份出席了会议。
    阿尔巴尼亚代表团团长科列加宣布开会以后，由阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席、劳动党中央政治局委员谢胡代表阿尔巴尼亚政府和党祝贺会议的开幕，并且热烈祝贺经济互助委员会成立十周年。
    经济互助委员会书记法捷耶夫今天在会上作了关于委员会的工作的报告。
