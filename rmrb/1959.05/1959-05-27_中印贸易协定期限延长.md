Title: Extension of Sino-Indian Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-05-27
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 25th: Du Yun, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in India, and K.B. Lall, Joint Secretary of the Indian Ministry of Commerce and Industry, exchanged letters today regarding the extension of the validity of the trade agreement signed between India and China in 1954 to the end of 1959.

### 中印贸易协定期限延长

1959-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    中印贸易协定期限延长
    新华社新德里25日电  中国驻印度大使馆商务参赞杜毓澐和印度工商部联合秘书克·贝·拉尔今天交换了关于把印度和中国在1954年签订的贸易协定的有效期限延长到1959年底的信件。
