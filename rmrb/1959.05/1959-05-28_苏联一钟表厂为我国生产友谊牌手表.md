Title: A Soviet Watch Factory Produces "Friendship" Brand Watches for China
Author:
Date: 1959-05-28
Page: 4

    Soviet Watch Factory
    Produces "Friendship" Brand Watches for China
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 27th. TASS reports from Kazan: The Chistopol Watch Factory in the Kazan Autonomous Republic is producing a large number of "Friendship" brand men's watches for China. The watch dial features a two-color design of the Chinese and Soviet national flags and the Tiananmen Gate. The words "Friendship" on the dial are in Chinese.

### 苏联一钟表厂为我国生产友谊牌手表

1959-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联一钟表厂
    为我国生产友谊牌手表
    新华社莫斯科27日电  据塔斯社喀山讯：喀山自治共和国的契斯托波尔钟表厂正在为中国生产大批“友谊”牌男手表。在这种手表的表盘上绘有套色的中苏两国国旗和天安门的图案。表盘上的“友谊”二字用的是中文。
