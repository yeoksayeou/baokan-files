Title: China's First Ambassador to Morocco Presents Credentials to the King of Morocco
Author:
Date: 1959-05-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 29th - Bai Ren, the first Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Morocco, presented his credentials to King Mohammed V of Morocco today at the Royal Palace.

In his address, Ambassador Bai Ren said that the Chinese government and the Chinese people particularly cherish the traditional friendly relations between China and Morocco and firmly believe that they will be increasingly consolidated and developed.

Ambassador Bai Ren said that the Chinese government and the Chinese people are willing to continue their efforts to strengthen cooperation and friendly relations between the two countries on the basis of the Bandung Conference spirit and the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. In his reply, the King of Morocco expressed his belief that the arrival of Ambassador Bai Ren will develop and strengthen the long-standing friendly relations between the peoples of China and Morocco.

### 我国首任驻摩洛哥大使  向摩洛哥国王递交国书

1959-05-01
第5版()
专栏：

    我国首任驻摩洛哥大使
    向摩洛哥国王递交国书
    新华社拉巴特29日电　中华人民共和国驻摩洛哥王国首任大使白认今天在利亚德宫向摩洛哥国王穆罕默德五世递交了国书。
    白认大使在致颂词中说，中国政府和中国人民特别珍视中摩两国之间的传统友好关系，并且坚信它将日益得到巩固和发展。
    白认大使说，中国政府和中国人民愿意继续努力，在万隆会议精神和和平共处五项原则的基础上，加强两国之间的合作和友好关系。摩洛哥国王在致答词时表示相信，白认大使的到任，将使摩中两国人民之间的由来已久的友好关系得到发展和加强。
