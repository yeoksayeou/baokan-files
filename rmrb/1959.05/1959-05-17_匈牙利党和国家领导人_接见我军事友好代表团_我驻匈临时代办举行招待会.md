Title: Hungarian Party and State Leaders Receive Chinese Military Friendship Delegation; Our Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to Hungary Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1959-05-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, May 15th: Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, and Kádár, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, received the entire delegation of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, at the Parliament Building this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Marosán, Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, and General Révész, Minister of National Defense of Hungary.

Xie Feng, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our Embassy in Hungary, was also present.

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, May 15th: Xie Feng, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our Embassy in Hungary, held a reception tonight for the visit of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, to Hungary.

Among those invited to the reception were Hungarian Party and government leaders Dobi, Kádár, Marosán, Biszku, and Révész, as well as more than two hundred senior generals of the Hungarian People's Army.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Hungary also attended the reception. Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Xie Feng delivered a speech at the meeting first. Subsequently, Marshal Peng Dehuai spoke. He first expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the warm welcome the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation received during its visit to Hungary. Marshal Peng Dehuai said that the more than six hundred million Chinese people will forever unite with the Hungarian people and unremittingly struggle for the common cause of socialism.

General Révész, Minister of National Defense of Hungary, also spoke at the meeting. He pointed out that the Hungarian working people and army are proud and full of strength and confidence to be able to advance together with the fraternal Chinese people to build and defend the common cause.

On the same morning, the delegation members split into two groups and visited the Hungarian Optical Works and the Budapest University of Technology, respectively.

### 匈牙利党和国家领导人  接见我军事友好代表团  我驻匈临时代办举行招待会

1959-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

    匈牙利党和国家领导人
    接见我军事友好代表团
    我驻匈临时代办举行招待会
    新华社布达佩斯15日电　匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比、社会主义工人党中央委员会第一书记卡达尔，今天下午在国会大厦接见了以国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的有：匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员、中央书记马罗山和匈牙利国防部长雷维斯上将。
    我国驻匈牙利大使馆临时代办谢丰也在座。
    据新华社布达佩斯15日电　我国驻匈牙利大使馆临时代办谢丰今晚为以国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团访问匈牙利举行招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的有：匈牙利党和政府领导人道比、卡达尔、马罗山、比斯库、雷维斯，以及匈牙利人民军高级将领等二百多人。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻匈牙利的外交使节。谢丰临时代办首先在会上致词。随后，彭德怀元帅讲了话。他首先对中国军事友好代表团在匈牙利访问期间所受到的热烈欢迎表示衷心的感谢。彭德怀元帅说，六亿多中国人民将和匈牙利人民永远地团结在一起，为着共同的社会主义事业奋斗不懈。
    匈牙利国防部部长雷维斯上将也在会上讲了话。他指出，匈牙利劳动人民和军队为能够同兄弟的中国人民一起前进，建设和保卫共同的事业而感到自豪，并且充满了力量和信心。
    同日上午，代表团团员分为两组分别参观了匈牙利光学用品厂和布达佩斯工业大学。
