Title: Belgian Communist Party Central Committee Calls on Members and Sympathizers to Actively Participate in "National Day of Action" to Support Workers in Fighting for Living Rights
Author:
Date: 1959-05-29
Page: 5

Brothers Parties' Activities

    The Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party published an appeal in "The Red Flag" on the 25th, calling on members and sympathizers of the party to actively support the call of the Belgian General Federation of Labor to hold a "National Day of Action" on May 29 to oppose factory closures and mass unemployment.

    The Central Committee also called on all workers' organizations to show their strength and will on the "National Day of Action" so that the workers' demands can be victorious.

    All activists of the Belgian Communist Party have been mobilized to participate in the "National Day of Action." In the past two weeks, they have distributed thousands of propaganda materials.
  　(Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 比共中央号召党员和同情者  积极参加“全国行动日”  支持工人争取生活权利

1959-05-29
第5版()
专栏：兄弟党的生活

    比共中央号召党员和同情者
    积极参加“全国行动日”  支持工人争取生活权利
    比利时共产党中央委员会25日在“红旗报”上发表的一个呼吁书中号召党员和同情党的人积极支持比利时总工会的号召，在5月29日举行“全国行动日”来反对关闭工厂和大批失业。
    中央委员会同时要求所有的工人组织在“全国行动日”这一天显示它们的力量和意志，以便使工人的要求得到胜利。
    比利时共产党的全部积极分子都已经动员起来，准备参加“全国行动日”。他们在最近两个星期中，就散发了数以千计的宣传文件。
  　（据新华社讯）
