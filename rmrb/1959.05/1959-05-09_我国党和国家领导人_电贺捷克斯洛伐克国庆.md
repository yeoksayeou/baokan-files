Title: Chinese Party and State Leaders Send Congratulatory Telegrams on Czechoslovak National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-05-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th May - Chinese Party and state leaders today sent telegrams to the Czechoslovak Party and state leaders, congratulating them on the 14th anniversary of the founding of the Czechoslovak Republic. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Antonín Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Comrade Zdeněk Fierlinger, Chairman of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic, and Comrade Viliam Široký, Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic:

On the occasion of the 14th anniversary of the founding of the Czechoslovak Republic, we, on behalf of the Communist Party of China, the Government of the People's Republic of China, and the Chinese people, extend our warm, fraternal congratulations to you and, through you, to the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic, and the people of Czechoslovakia.

In the past year, under the correct leadership of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, the Czechoslovak people have made significant achievements in developing the socialist economy and improving the material and cultural living standards of the people. Currently, the Czechoslovak people are full of confidence and striving to comprehensively realize the grand goals of completing socialist construction put forward by the 11th Congress of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia. The Czechoslovak people, who have always been faithful to proletarian internationalism and love peace have made important contributions to the great struggle of strengthening the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, defending the purity of Marxism-Leninism, resolutely opposing modern revisionism, and safeguarding world peace.

The Chinese people are very grateful to the government and people of the Czechoslovak Republic for the fraternal assistance and support they have given us in our socialist construction and in the struggle to safeguard our sovereignty and territorial integrity and oppose aggression and foreign intervention by US imperialism. We are deeply convinced that with the vigorous development of socialist construction in our two countries, the fraternal friendship and close cooperation between the Chinese and Czechoslovak peoples will be further consolidated and developed, thereby helping to strengthen the great unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and safeguard the cause of world peace. We wish the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Czechoslovak peoples will last forever.

        Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
        Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
        Zhu De, Chairman

### 我国党和国家领导人  电贺捷克斯洛伐克国庆

1959-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我国党和国家领导人
    电贺捷克斯洛伐克国庆
    新华社8日讯　我国党和国家领导人今天致电捷克斯洛伐克党和国家领导人，祝贺捷克斯洛伐克共和国十四周年国庆。电文如下：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记、捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安·诺沃提尼同志，捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席兹·费林格同志，捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理威·西罗基同志：
    在捷克斯洛伐克共和国十四周年国庆的时候，我们谨代表中国共产党、中华人民共和国政府和人民向你们，并且通过你们向捷克斯洛伐克共产党、捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府和人民致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。
    过去一年来，捷克斯洛伐克人民在捷克斯洛伐克共产党的正确领导下，在发展社会主义经济、提高人民的物质和文化生活水平等方面取得了重大的成就。目前捷克斯洛伐克人民正满怀信心，为全面地实现捷克斯洛伐克共产党第十一次代表大会所提出的完成社会主义建设的宏伟目标而奋斗。一贯忠实于无产阶级国际主义、热爱和平的捷克斯洛伐克人民在加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结、捍卫马列主义的纯洁性、坚决反对现代修正主义以及维护世界和平的伟大斗争中作出了重要的贡献。
    中国人民十分感谢捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府和人民在我国社会主义建设和维护主权和领土完整、反对美帝国主义侵略和外国干涉的斗争中所给予我们的兄弟般的援助和支持。我们深信，随着我们两国社会主义建设事业的蓬勃发展，中捷两国人民的兄弟友谊和亲密合作必将进一步地巩固和发展，从而有助于加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的伟大团结和维护世界和平的事业。祝中捷两国人民牢不可破的兄弟友谊万古长青。
        中国共产党中央委员会主席　毛泽东
        中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
  　　  中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长  朱德
        中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                  1959年5月8日于北京
    新华社8日讯　我国外交部长陈毅今天致电捷克斯洛伐克共和国外交部长瓦·戴维，祝贺捷克斯洛伐克共和国十四周年国庆。电文如下：捷克斯洛伐克共和国外交部长瓦·戴维同志：
    值此捷克斯洛伐克共和国十四周年国庆之际，我谨向你表示热烈的祝贺。一贯奉行和平外交政策的捷克斯洛伐克政府在反对美帝国主义的战争政策以及维护欧洲和世界和平的斗争中作出了重大的贡献。祝你在继续执行和平外交政策的事业中获得更大的成就。
　　　　中华人民共和国外交部长　陈　毅
                                    1959年5月8日于北京
