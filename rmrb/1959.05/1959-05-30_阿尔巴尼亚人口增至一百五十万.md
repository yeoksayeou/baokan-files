Title: Albania's Population Increases to 1.5 Million
Author:
Date: 1959-05-30
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana - Albania's national population has increased from over 1.39 million in October 1955 to 1.5 million, with the birth rate of infants increasing year by year. In 1938, there were 16.9 infant births per year for every two thousand people in the country, while in 1958, there were 31.9 infant births per year for every one thousand people.
    In order to further care for the health of children and pregnant women, the Albanian Council of Ministers recently decided that prenatal examinations and hospital births for pregnant women will be provided free of charge. Mothers and infants who give birth before their due date can receive free treatment and care. All children under the age of four and children suffering from rickets can also enjoy free medical treatment.
    The Council of Ministers also decided that, starting next year, children's food stores will be universally established throughout the country, and the prices of children's food and dairy products will be reduced.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人口增至一百五十万

1959-05-30
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人口增至一百五十万
    新华社地拉那电　阿尔巴尼亚全国人口已由1955年10月的一百三十九万多人增加到一百五十万人，婴儿的出生率一年比一年增高。1938年全国每两千个人中一年有十六点九个婴儿出生，而1958年每一千个人中即有三十一点九个婴儿出生。
    为了进一步关怀儿童和孕妇的健康，阿尔巴尼亚部长会议最近决定，孕妇产前的检查和住院生产一律享受免费待遇。在预产期前生产的母亲和婴儿可得到免费治疗和抚养。全国所有四岁以下的儿童和患有软骨病的儿童也可享受公费治疗待遇。
    部长会议还决定，从明年起，在全国各地将普遍设立儿童食品店，并将减低儿童食品和乳制品的价格。
