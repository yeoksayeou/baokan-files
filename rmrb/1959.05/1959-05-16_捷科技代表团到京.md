Title: Czechoslovak Science and Technology Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: The Czechoslovak Science and Technology Cooperation Delegation, consisting of nine members, led by Mr. Přešek, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Group of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee for Scientific and Technical Cooperation and First Deputy Chairman of the Czechoslovak State Planning Commission, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 14th to attend the 7th session of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee for Scientific and Technical Cooperation.  Greeting them at the airport were Wang Daohan, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee for Scientific and Technical Cooperation and Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, and others. Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušiňák, was also present at the airport to welcome them.

### 捷科技代表团到京

1959-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    捷科技代表团到京
    新华社15日讯　前来我国参加中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会第七届会议的捷克斯洛伐克科学与技术合作代表团一行九人，在委员会捷方组主席、捷克斯洛伐克国家计划委员会第一副主席普泽克率领下，14日乘飞机到达北京。前往机场迎接的有中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会中国组主席、第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵等。捷克斯洛伐克大使布希尼亚克也到机场欢迎。
