Title: Millions of Germans Sign Petition Demanding a German Peace Treaty
Author:
Date: 1959-05-19
Page: 5

    Demanding the Conclusion of a German Peace Treaty
    Millions of Germans Sign
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 17th: The National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany has received numerous statements, resolutions, and letters this week bearing the signatures of three million people, supporting the positions taken by the Soviet and German Democratic Republic delegations at the Geneva Foreign Ministers' Conference, and calling on the participants of the Geneva Foreign Ministers' Conference to dedicate themselves to the conclusion of a German peace treaty and the elimination of the abnormal state of West Berlin. The signatories include residents from all strata of the two Germanies. The delegation of the German Democratic Republic to the Geneva Conference has also received letters bearing the signatures of more than 1.5 million residents, making the same demands. (Picture attached)
    Staff members of the Free German Trade Union Federation are loading signed petitions, resolutions, letters, etc. onto trucks, preparing to send them to the Geneva Foreign Ministers' Conference. (DTS [German News Agency] draft, Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 要求缔结对德和约  数百万德国人签名

1959-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    要求缔结对德和约
    数百万德国人签名
    新华社柏林17日电　民主德国全国阵线全国委员会这一星期来收到了有三百万人签名的许多声明、决议和信件，支持苏联和德意志民主共和国代表团在日内瓦外长会议上采取的立场，并呼吁日内瓦外长会议的参加者致力于缔结对德和约和消除西柏林的不正常状态。签名的人包括两个德国各阶层的居民。德意志民主共和国出席日内瓦会议的代表团也收到了有一百五十万以上居民签字的提出同样要求的信件。（附图片）
    自由德国工会联合会的工作人员正在将签名书、决议书、信件等装车，准备送到日内瓦外长会议去。　德意志通讯社稿（新华社发）
