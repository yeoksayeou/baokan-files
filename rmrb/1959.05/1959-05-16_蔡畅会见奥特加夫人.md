Title: Cai Chang Meets with Mrs. Ortega
Author:
Date: 1959-05-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, met today with Mrs. Ortega, Chairman of the Women's Committee of the Chilean People's Action Front, and Mrs. Alvarez, who came to China for a visit with her.

Present at the meeting were Liu Qingyang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and other leaders of the All-China Women's Federation.

After the meeting, a tea party was held, and the hosts and guests had a cordial and warm conversation.

### 蔡畅会见奥特加夫人

1959-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    蔡畅会见奥特加夫人
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会主席蔡畅今天会见了智利人民行动阵线妇女委员会主席奥特加夫人和与她同来中国访问的阿尔瓦莱斯夫人。
    会见时在座的有全国妇联副主席刘清扬和全国妇联的其他领导人。
    会见后举行了茶会，宾主进行了亲切热烈的谈话。
