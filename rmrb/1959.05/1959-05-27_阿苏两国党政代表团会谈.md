Title: Albanian and Soviet Party and Government Delegations Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1959-05-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 26th: The Albanian and Soviet Party and Government Delegations began talks here on the afternoon of the 25th.

    The Albanian side participating in the talks included: Hoxha, Shehu, Kobo, Baluku, Nushi, Koleka, Belishova, Mufti, and others.

    The Soviet side participating in the talks included: Khrushchev, Mukhitdinov, Malinovsky, Filyubin, and others.

    On the same afternoon, the Soviet Party and Government Delegation paid a courtesy call on the Albanian Party and state leaders. Khrushchev had a cordial conversation with Hoxha, Leshi, Shehu, and others.

### 阿苏两国党政代表团会谈

1959-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    阿苏两国党政代表团会谈
    新华社地拉那26日电  阿尔巴尼亚和苏联两国党和政府代表团，25日下午在这里开始会谈。
    阿尔巴尼亚方面参加会谈的是：霍查、谢胡、卡博、巴卢库、努什、柯列加、贝利绍娃、穆夫蒂乌等。
    苏联方面参加会谈的是：赫鲁晓夫、穆希金诺夫、马利诺夫斯基、费留宾等人。
    同天下午，苏联党政代表团拜会了阿尔巴尼亚党和国家领导人。赫鲁晓夫同霍查、列希、谢胡等人进行了亲切的交谈。
