Title: All-China Committee of the India-China Friendship Association Formulates Work Plan to Vigorously Carry Out Activities to Strengthen India-China Friendship; To Hold India-China Friendship Month to Celebrate the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1959-05-15
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 13th: The All-China Committee of the India-China Friendship Association issued a notice today stating that the association has formulated a work plan for strengthening India-China friendship in the near future.

According to this plan, the India-China Friendship Association will hold appropriate celebrations at the end of June to commemorate the fifth anniversary of the announcement of Panchsheel.

The association will also prepare to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. From September 1st to October 1st, the association will hold India-China Friendship Month, with activities including public gatherings, film and cultural festivals, and exhibitions. It will also develop its membership on a large scale to ensure the expansion of the organization and consolidate this noble cause.

The India-China Friendship Association will also exchange delegations with China and introduce various aspects of Chinese national life to the Indian people.

### 印中友协全国委员会拟定工作计划  大力开展加强印中友好活动  将举办印中友好月庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年

1959-05-15
第5版()
专栏：

    印中友协全国委员会拟定工作计划
    大力开展加强印中友好活动
    将举办印中友好月庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年
    新华社新德里13日电　印中友好协会全国委员会今天发出的通告说，协会已经拟定了它在最近将来的关于加强印中友谊的工作计划。
    根据这项计划，在6月底，印中友好协会将举行适当规模的庆祝活动，纪念潘查希拉宣布五周年。
    协会还将准备庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年。从9月1日到10月1日，协会将举办印中友好月，活动内容包括举行公众集会、电影与文化节和展览会，并且将大规模发展会员，以保证扩大组织，巩固崇高的事业。
    印中友好协会还将同中国互派代表团，并且向印度人民介绍中国国家生活各个方面的情况。
