Title: Gromyko Hosts Banquet for Couve de Murville
Author:
Date: 1959-05-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 21st: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko hosted a banquet on the 20th at the Soviet delegation's residence in honor of French Foreign Minister Couve de Murville.

    Members of the Soviet and French delegations attending the Geneva Conference also attended the banquet.

### 葛罗米柯设宴招待德姆维尔

1959-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯设宴招待德姆维尔
    新华社21日讯　塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯20日在苏联代表团住所设宴招待法国外交部长德姆维尔。
    参加宴会的还有出席日内瓦会议的苏联代表团和法国代表团团员。
