Title: Albanian Council of Ministers Awards Prizes for Culture, Science, and Art
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 12th: The Albanian Council of Ministers awarded the Republic's Cultural, Scientific, and Artistic Prizes for the years 1957-1958 on the 11th. A total of fourteen people received awards, including writers, composers, scientists, actors, and directors.

The renowned composer P. Jakova received the first prize for creating "Mrika," Albania's first grand opera using national forms and folk music melodies. Actor L. Filip received a prize for portraying the image of Lenin on the Albanian stage.

### 阿尔巴尼亚部长会议  颁发文化科学艺术奖金

1959-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚部长会议
    颁发文化科学艺术奖金
    新华社地拉那12日电  阿尔巴尼亚部长会议11日颁发了1957—1958年度共和国文化科学艺术奖金。获奖者共十四人，其中包括作家、作曲家、科学家、演员和导演等。
    著名作曲家普·雅可瓦由于运用民族形式和民间音乐旋律创作了阿尔巴尼亚第一部大型歌剧“姆里卡”而获得一等奖。演员拉·菲利普由于在阿尔巴尼亚的舞台上塑造了列宁的形象而获得奖金。
