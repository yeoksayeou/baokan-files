Title: Our Military Friendship Delegation Arrives in Bucharest; Minister Sălăjan Hosts Banquet for Our Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-05-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 18th — The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, headed by Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, arrived here this afternoon from Budapest by special train. The delegation is visiting Romania for five days at the invitation of Sălăjan, Minister of the Romanian Armed Forces.
　　
Those who went to the station to welcome the delegation included: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee; Emil Bodnăraş, Vice-Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers and member of the Political Bureau of the Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee; Alexandru Drăghici, Minister of Internal Affairs; Liviu Buneațanu, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Sălăjan, Minister of the Armed Forces, as well as senior generals of the Romanian People's Army and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Romania. More than 3,000 soldiers of the Romanian People's Army and representatives from all walks of life also went to the station to welcome them.
　　
Wang Luming, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, and embassy staff also went to the station to welcome the delegation.
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 18th — Sălăjan, Minister of the Romanian Armed Forces, held a grand banquet at the "House of Soldiers" this evening to entertain the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense.
　　
Those present at the banquet included: Romanian party and government leaders Chivu Stoica, Emil Bodnăraş, Alexandru Drăghici, government ministers, generals of the Romanian People's Army, and military attachés from embassies of various socialist countries.
　　
Wang Luming, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, also attended the banquet.
　　
Minister Sălăjan said in his speech at the banquet that, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party headed by Comrade Mao Zedong, the working people of your country are engaged in socialist construction with soaring enthusiasm. The heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army has gloriously completed the tasks assigned to them by the people and the Party.
　　
Sălăjan said that the friendly and cooperative relations between our two peoples are based on the rock-solid foundation of Marxism-Leninism and the common goals of socialism and defending peace. Sălăjan pointed out that the past mutual visits of leaders of Romania and China and the current visit of the delegation have strengthened the friendly relations between the Romanian and Chinese peoples and have contributed to strengthening the solidarity of the socialist camp.
　　
In his speech, Marshal Peng Dehuai first expressed his heartfelt gratitude to Minister Sălăjan for inviting the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to visit Romania and for everyone's warm reception. He then said that the hardworking and brave Romanian people, under the leadership of the Romanian Workers' Party headed by Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, have made brilliant achievements on all fronts of socialist construction. The glorious Romanian People's Army has become a strong force for defending the motherland and safeguarding peace in Europe and the world. We are delighted and encouraged by these achievements of the fraternal Romanian people and army.
　　
That afternoon, the delegation laid wreaths at the Monument to the Martyrs of the Romanian People's Army and the Monument to the Soviet Army Martyrs.

### 我军事友好代表团到达布加勒斯特  萨拉扬部长设宴招待我国代表团

1959-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    我军事友好代表团到达布加勒斯特
    萨拉扬部长设宴招待我国代表团
    新华社布加勒斯特18日电　以国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团今天下午由布达佩斯乘专车到达这里。代表团应罗马尼亚武装部队部部长萨拉扬的邀请，将在罗马尼亚访问五天。
  前往车站欢迎代表团的有：罗马尼亚部长会议副主席、罗马尼亚工人党中央政治局委员波德纳拉希、内政部部长德勒吉奇、外交部部长布纳丘、武装部队部部长萨拉扬以及罗马尼亚人民军的高级将领和各国驻罗马尼亚的外交使节等。三千多名罗马尼亚人民军士兵和各界代表也前往车站欢迎。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办王鲁明和使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
    新华社布加勒斯特18日电　罗马尼亚武装部队部部长萨拉扬，今晚在“军人之家”举行盛大宴会，招待以国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团。
    出席宴会的有：罗马尼亚党和政府领导人基伏·斯托伊卡、波德纳拉希、德勒吉奇、政府的部长、罗马尼亚人民军的将领以及各社会主义国家大使馆的武官等。
    中国驻罗马尼亚临时代办王鲁明也出席了宴会。
    萨拉扬部长在宴会上致词说，在以毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党的领导下，你们国家的劳动人民以冲天干劲从事着社会主义建设。英勇的中国人民解放军光荣地完成了人民和党所交给他们的任务。
    萨拉扬说，我们两国人民之间的友好和互助关系是建立在马克思列宁主义思想以及社会主义和保卫和平的共同目标的坚如磐石的基础之上的。萨拉扬指出，罗中两国领导人过去对两国的相互访问和代表团现在的访问，加强了罗中两国人民之间的友好关系，并对加强社会主义阵营的团结作出了贡献。
    彭德怀元帅在讲话中首先对萨拉扬部长邀请中国军事友好代表团访问罗马尼亚以及大家的热情接待，表示衷心的感谢。他接着说，勤劳勇敢的罗马尼亚人民，在以乔治乌—德治同志为首的罗马尼亚工人党的领导下，在社会主义建设的各个战线上都取得了辉煌的成就。光荣的罗马尼亚人民军已经成为一支保卫祖国、维护欧洲和世界和平的坚强力量。我们对兄弟的罗马尼亚人民和军队的这些成就，感到欢欣鼓舞。
    当天下午，代表团曾向罗马尼亚人民军烈士纪念碑和苏军烈士纪念碑献了花圈。
