Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Albanian Labor Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 13th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China met this evening with all the comrades of the Albanian Labor Party delegation headed by Hysni Kapo, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, and had a cordial conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Central Secretariat, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee.

Also present at the meeting was Albanian Ambassador to China, Bali Ylli. (Attached picture)
Chairman Mao Zedong met with the Albanian Labor Party delegation headed by Hysni Kapo (fourth from right in the front row). The picture shows a group photo taken during the meeting.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao

### 毛主席会见阿劳动党代表团

1959-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席会见阿劳动党代表团
    新华社13日讯  中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东今晚会见了以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党政治局委员、中央书记处书记希斯尼·卡博为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团全体同志，并且同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
  会见时在座的有中共中央总书记邓小平，中央书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员伍修权。
    会见时，阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使巴利里也在座。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席会见了以团长希斯尼·卡博（前排右第四人）为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团。图为会见时合影。
    新华社记者  孟庆彪摄
