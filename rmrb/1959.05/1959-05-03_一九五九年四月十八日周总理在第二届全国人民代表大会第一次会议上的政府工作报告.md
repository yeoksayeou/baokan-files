Title: Premier Zhou's Government Work Report at the First Session of the Second National People's Congress on April 18, 1959
Author:
Date: 1959-05-03

Whether in rural or urban areas, it is essential to utilize the forms of three-level, four-level, five-level, and six-level cadre meetings, and to utilize cadre meetings attended by grassroots cadres and by advanced and non-advanced elements from the masses, so that the policies of the Party and the state and the decisions of higher authorities can directly meet with grassroots cadres and the masses, and so that the opinions of the masses and grassroots cadres can directly meet with higher authorities. Experience has proven that this type of meeting is most conducive to concentrating the wisdom of the masses and strengthening unity within the people, and is an effective weapon for promoting people's democracy.
     Premier Zhou's Government Work Report at the First Session of the Second National People's Congress on April 18, 1959

### 一九五九年四月十八日周总理在第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议上的政府工作报告

1959-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

    无论农村城市，一定要利用三级、四级、五级、六级干部会议的形式，利用有基层干部参加的、有群众中的先进分子和非先进分子参加的干部会议的形式，使党和国家的政策和上级的决定直接同基层干部和群众见面，使群众和基层干部的意见直接同上级见面。经验证明，这种会议最便于集中群众智慧，加强人民内部的团结，是发扬人民民主的一种有效的武器。
     一九五九年四月十八日周总理在第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议上的政府工作报告
