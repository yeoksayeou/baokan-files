Title: GDR People's Chamber Delegation Arrives in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 6th. The delegation of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, led by Mattern, First Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Shenyang today by special plane from Pyongyang after completing its visit to Korea for a visit.
	Some members of the delegation who had completed their visit to Mongolia also arrived in Shenyang today from Beijing, accompanied by Wang Daier, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China.
	Today, the national flags of China and Germany fluttered over Shenyang East Tower Airport. When the special plane carrying First Deputy Chairman Mattern and other distinguished guests landed, 400 welcoming people beat gongs and drums and applauded warmly. Huang Oudong, Governor of Liaoning Province, and Liu Baotian, Mayor of Shenyang, who had been waiting at the airport, stepped forward to shake hands with the distinguished guests. A group of young women presented flowers to the distinguished guests. Afterwards, the distinguished guests met and shook hands with the heads of local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and constantly waved to the cheering masses.
	Liu Changsheng and Wu Xinyu, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, who had arrived in Shenyang earlier, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation. Kim Yong-bin, Consul General of Korea in Shenyang, also went to the airport to welcome them. This afternoon, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Zhang Like, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall and toured the urban area and Zhongshan Park.
	In the evening, Governor Huang Oudong hosted a banquet to welcome the distinguished guests.

### 德人民议院代表团到沈阳

1959-05-07
第2版()
专栏：

    德人民议院代表团到沈阳
    新华社沈阳6日电　由德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团第一副主席马特恩率领的德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团，在结束了对朝鲜的访问以后，今天由平壤乘专机到达沈阳参观访问。
    结束了对蒙古访问的代表团部分团员，也在今天由德意志民主共和国驻我国大使汪戴尔陪同从北京到达沈阳。
    沈阳东塔机场上空今天飘扬着中德两国国旗，当马特恩第一副主席和其他贵宾们乘坐的专机着陆时，四百名欢迎群众敲锣打鼓，热烈鼓掌。久候机场迎接的辽宁省省长黄欧东、沈阳市市长刘宝田等人迎上前去同贵宾们一一亲切握手。一群女青年向贵宾们献了鲜花。随后贵宾们同前来欢迎的各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体负责人一一见面握手，并不断地向欢呼群众招手致意。
    到机场欢迎代表团的还有先期到达沈阳的人大常务委员会委员刘长胜、武新宇。朝鲜驻沈阳总领事金龙彬也到机场欢迎。今天下午，贵宾们由沈阳市副市长张力克陪同，参观了东北工业陈列馆，游览了市区和中山公园。
    晚间，黄欧东省长举行宴会欢迎贵宾。
