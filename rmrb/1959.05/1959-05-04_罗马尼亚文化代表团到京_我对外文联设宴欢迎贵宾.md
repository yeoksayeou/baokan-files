Title: Romanian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing; All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Hosts Banquet to Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-05-04
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Romanian Cultural Delegation headed by Octav Livezeanu, Vice-Chairman of the Romanian Association for Foreign Cultural Relations. Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Romanian Ambassador to China, Teodor Chahalariu, attended the banquet tonight.
The delegation arrived in Beijing on April 30th for a friendly visit to China in accordance with the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 罗马尼亚文化代表团到京  我对外文联设宴欢迎贵宾

1959-05-04
第3版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚文化代表团到京
    我对外文联设宴欢迎贵宾
    新华社3日讯　对外文化联络委员会今晚举行宴会，欢迎以罗马尼亚对外文化联络协会副主席奥克塔夫·利韦泽亚努为首的罗马尼亚文化代表团。对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊，罗马尼亚驻华大使查哈勒斯库出席了今晚的宴会。
    代表团根据中罗文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国进行友好访问，在4月30日到达北京。
