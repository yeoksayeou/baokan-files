Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station, etc. Today Broadcasting Live Coverage of Capital Commemoration of "May Fourth" Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd. The capital's commemoration of the 40th anniversary of the "May Fourth" Movement will be held at the Beijing Xian Nong Tan Stadium on the afternoon of the 3rd. This commemoration conference is jointly organized by the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China, the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, the China Association for Science and Technology, the All-China Youth Federation, and the All-China Students' Federation. The Central People's Broadcasting Station, Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, and Beijing Television Station will broadcast live coverage of the conference from 1:30 p.m. on the 3rd.

### 中央人民广播电台等  今日广播首都纪念“五四”大会实况

1959-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台等
    今日广播首都纪念“五四”大会实况
    新华社2日讯　首都各界纪念“五四”四十周年大会3日下午将在北京先农坛体育场举行。这个纪念大会是由中国共产主义青年团中央委员会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国总工会、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会、中华人民共和国科学技术协会、中华全国青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会联合举办的。中央人民广播电台、北京人民广播电台、北京电视台将从3日下午一点三十分起转播这次大会的实况。
