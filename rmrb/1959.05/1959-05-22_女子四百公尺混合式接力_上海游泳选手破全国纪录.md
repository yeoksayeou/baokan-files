Title: Women's 400m Medley Relay: Shanghai Swimmers Break National Record
Author:
Date: 1959-05-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 20th: In the Shanghai Second Municipal Games swimming competition held this morning, female athletes Zhou Yongqi, Wang Peidi, Lin Jiafeng, and Zheng Wei broke the national record for the women's 400m medley relay with a time of 5 minutes 28.1 seconds.

This is the second time this national record has been broken this year. During the National First-Class and Master-Class Spring Swimming Competition held in Beijing in April, the Shanghai women's relay team, consisting of Zhou Yongqi, Liu Yusen, Dai Lihua, and Zheng Wei, had already broken the national record with a better time of 5 minutes 24.2 seconds.

### 女子四百公尺混合式接力  上海游泳选手破全国纪录

1959-05-22
第6版()
专栏：

    女子四百公尺混合式接力
    上海游泳选手破全国纪录
    新华社上海20日电　在今天上午举行的上海市第二届运动会的游泳比赛中，女运动员周咏琪、王培蒂、林嘉凤和郑玮以五分二十八秒一的成绩，打破了女子四百公尺混合式接力五分三十秒九的全国纪录。
    这是这项全国纪录今年以来第二次被打破。今年4月间在北京举行全国一级、健将级春季游泳比赛时，由周咏琪、柳玉森、戴丽华和郑玮四人组成的上海女子接力队，就曾经以五分二十四秒二的更好成绩打破过这项全国纪录。
