Title: Suburban Agriculture: Vegetables First (Picture)
Author: Zhao Qun, Cai Zhongzhi, Sun Yuedong
Date: 1959-05-25
Page: 2

    Suburban Agriculture: Vegetables First
    Left picture: Members of the Xijiao People's Commune in Jinzhou, Liaoning Province, are implementing the policy of suburban production serving the city, and have expanded the area of vegetable cultivation this year. This is a picture of commune members topdressing the vegetable fields. Photo by Zhao Qun (Xinhua News Agency draft)
    Top right picture: People's Communes in the suburbs of Guangzhou transport a large amount of vegetables to Guangzhou to supply the needs of the urban people's lives. The picture shows the staff of the Fruit and Vegetable Company sorting hairy gourds and cucumbers, preparing them for market.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Cai Zhongzhi photo
    Bottom right picture: The Jinniu People's Commune in the suburbs of Chengdu, Sichuan Province, has expanded its vegetable planting area this year to actively support the city. This is a picture of female commune members building trellises in the high-yield cucumber experimental field to ensure the smooth growth of the vines.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Yuedong photo

### 郊区农业  蔬菜第一（图片）
赵群  蔡忠植  孙跃冬
1959-05-25
第2版()
专栏：

    郊区农业  蔬菜第一
    左图：辽宁省锦州市西郊人民公社贯彻郊区生产为城市服务的方针，今年扩大了蔬菜种植面积。这是社员们在蔬菜地里追肥。　赵群摄（新华社稿）
    右上图：广州市郊人民公社把大量的蔬菜运来广州，供应城市人民生活需要。图为水果蔬菜公司的工作人员在整理毛瓜、黄瓜，准备上市。
    新华社记者　蔡忠植摄
    右下图：四川成都市郊区金牛人民公社今年扩大了蔬菜种植面积，积极支援城市。这是女社员在黄瓜高产试验地里搭架，使藤蔓顺利生长。
    新华社记者　孙跃冬摄
