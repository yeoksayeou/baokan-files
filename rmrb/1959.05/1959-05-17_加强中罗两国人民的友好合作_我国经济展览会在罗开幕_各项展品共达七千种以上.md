Title: Strengthening Friendly Cooperation Between the Chinese and Romanian People: China's Economic Exhibition Opens in Romania, Featuring Over 7,000 Exhibits

Author: 
Date: 1959-05-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, May 15th: The Chinese Economic Construction Exhibition opened here this afternoon at the Stalin Park Exhibition Hall. This is the first comprehensive exhibition held by China in Romania. The exhibition area is 4,500 square meters, with more than 7,000 exhibits.

Romanian Council of Ministers Vice-Chairmen Moghioros, Voitec, and G. Popa attended the opening ceremony. Also attending the opening ceremony were more than 500 people, including diplomatic envoys from various countries to Romania and representatives from all walks of life.

Romanian Deputy Minister of Trade Radulescu delivered a speech at the opening ceremony first. He said that the exhibition will help strengthen mutual understanding between the Romanian and Chinese people, enhance friendship and cooperation between them, and contribute to the struggle to defend peace.

Wang Luming, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, then spoke. He expressed his gratitude to the Romanian government and people for their care and support for this exhibition.

The exhibits at this exhibition include China's traditional export products and new products manufactured during the Great Leap Forward. Among them, heavy industrial products account for 60% of the exhibits, including the M-131W universal grinder, the C868A lead screw lathe, the X460 electric profiling milling machine, and the 80-horsepower Hongqi brand tractor. Other industrial products include colorful silks and satins, exquisite handicrafts, and various daily necessities and stationery.

Models of indigenous blast furnaces and converters, which played a great role on China's steel front, are also on display in the exhibition hall. Various products manufactured by students in universities, middle schools, and primary schools last year under the policy of combining education with productive labor are also on display here.

The exhibition will be on display here for twenty-one days.

### 加强中罗两国人民的友好合作  我国经济展览会在罗开幕  各项展品共达七千种以上

1959-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

    加强中罗两国人民的友好合作
    我国经济展览会在罗开幕
    各项展品共达七千种以上
    新华社布加勒斯特15日电　中国经济建设展览会15日下午在这里的斯大林公园展览馆开幕。这是中国在罗马尼亚举行的第一次综合性展览会。展览面积为四千五百平方公尺，展品共七千种以上。
    罗马尼亚部长会议副主席莫吉奥罗希、沃伊捷克和格·波帕出席了开幕式。出席开幕式的还有各国驻罗马尼亚外交使节和各界代表等五百多人。
    罗马尼亚贸易部副部长拉杜列斯库首先在开幕式上致词。他说，展览会有助于加强罗、中两国人民的互相了解，有助于增进他们之间的友谊和合作，同时也有助于保卫和平的斗争。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办王鲁明接着讲话。他对罗马尼亚政府和人民对这个展览会所给予的关怀和支持表示感谢。
    这次展览会的展品包括中国的传统性出口产品和在大跃进中制造的新产品。其中，重工业产品占展品的60％，有M一三一W型万能磨床、C八六八A型丝杠车床、X四六○型电动仿型铣床和八十马力的红旗牌拖拉机等。其它工业品有彩色艳丽的绸缎、精致玲珑的手工艺品、各种各样的日用品和文具等。
    在展览馆中还陈列着在中国钢铁战线上发挥极大威力的土高炉、转炉模型。去年大、中、小学学生在教育与劳动生产相结合的方针下所制造的各种产品也在这里展出。
    展览会将在这里展出二十一天。
