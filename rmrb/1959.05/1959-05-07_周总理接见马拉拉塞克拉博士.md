Title: Premier Zhou Receives Dr. Malalasekera
Author:
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 6th — Premier Zhou Enlai received Dr. G.P. Malalasekera, Honorary President of the World Fellowship of Buddhists and Ceylon's Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and his wife this afternoon. Dr. Malalasekera is visiting China at the invitation of the Buddhist Association of China.

Present at the meeting were Liu Xiao, China's Ambassador to the Soviet Union; Ding Xilin, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; He Chengxiang, Director of the Bureau of Religious Affairs of the State Council; He Ying, Deputy Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Zhou Shujia, Vice-President of the Buddhist Association of China.

Also present at the meeting were Ceylon's Ambassador to China, Gunapala Piyasena de Silva de Fonseka and his wife.

### 周总理接见马拉拉塞克拉博士

1959-05-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见马拉拉塞克拉博士
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了世界佛教徒联谊会名誉会长、锡兰驻苏联大使马拉拉塞克拉博士和他的夫人。马拉拉塞克拉是应中国佛教协会的邀请来中国访问的。
    接见时在座的，有我国驻苏联大使刘晓，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，外交部第一亚洲司副司长何英，中国佛教协会副会长周叔迦。
    接见时，锡兰驻中国大使高伯拉瓦和他的夫人也在座。
