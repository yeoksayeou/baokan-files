Title: Khrushchev Awarded Lenin Peace Prize for Outstanding Achievements in Maintaining World Peace; Grand Award Ceremony Held in the Grand Kremlin Palace in Moscow
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th – The Committee for the International Lenin Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" held a grand award ceremony in the Grand Kremlin Palace in Moscow this afternoon, awarding the 1958 International Lenin Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" to Khrushchev, a distinguished Soviet social and state activist, in recognition of his outstanding achievements in maintaining and strengthening the cause of peace.

Present at the award ceremony were Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the Committee for the International Lenin Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations," Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman, and heads of the committee. When Khrushchev stepped onto the stage, everyone stood up and applauded in unison.

Soviet party and government leaders Aristov, Kirichenko, Kozlov, Mikoyan, Suslov, Furtseva, Mukhitdinov, and others also attended the award ceremony.

Also participating in the award ceremony were representatives of advanced industrial and agricultural producers in Moscow, youth representatives, representatives from government agencies and military units, representatives from science, culture, and the arts, and representatives from social organizations such as the Soviet Peace Committee, totaling several thousand people.

The award ceremony was presided over by Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the Committee for the International Lenin Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations." He introduced Khrushchev's enormous contributions to the struggle for world peace. He said that Khrushchev tirelessly struggles to realize Lenin's policy of peaceful diplomacy. The whole world knows that Khrushchev's contribution to easing international tension and consolidating the cause of peace is enormous.

Then, Skobeltsyn presented Khrushchev with a gold medal and certificate and wished him even greater achievements in further safeguarding world peace. At this moment, a group of Young Pioneers walked onto the stage to present flowers to Khrushchev.

Next, Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Committee for the International Lenin Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" and Chairman of the Chinese Committee for the Defense of World Peace, spoke. He also expressed his heartfelt and warm congratulations to Khrushchev on behalf of the entire Chinese people.

Also speaking at the award ceremony were Mrs. Gordon, Vice President of the World Peace Council and President of the Women's International Democratic Federation; Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee; Sundaral, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council and an Indian writer; Anna Seghers, a member of the Committee for the International Lenin Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" and a famous German female writer; and representatives of factory youth and collective farms.

Then, Khrushchev delivered a long speech. He believed that awarding him the International Lenin Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" was an acknowledgement of the great efforts of the Soviet people and their contribution to the struggle for peace and universal security. He thanked everyone for their congratulations. He stated that he would do his best to ensure the victory of the cause of peace. He also spoke about some current international issues. His speech was repeatedly interrupted by warm applause.

When Khrushchev finished his speech, all the participants stood up in unison to express their congratulations.

### 在维护世界和平事业中功绩卓著  赫鲁晓夫接受列宁和平奖金  莫斯科大克里姆林宫中举行隆重授奖仪式

1959-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

    在维护世界和平事业中功绩卓著
    赫鲁晓夫接受列宁和平奖金
    莫斯科大克里姆林宫中举行隆重授奖仪式
    新华社莫斯科16日电　“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金委员会今天下午在莫斯科大克里姆林宫举行隆重授奖仪式，把1958年“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金授给苏联杰出的社会活动家和国家活动家赫鲁晓夫，以表彰他在维护和加强和平事业中的卓越功绩。
    出席授奖仪式的有“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金委员会主席斯科贝尔琴、副主席郭沫若和委员会的负责人。当赫鲁晓夫走上主席台时全体一致起立鼓掌。
    苏联党和政府领导人阿里斯托夫、基里钦科、科兹洛夫、米高扬、苏斯洛夫、福尔采娃、穆希金诺夫等也出席了授奖仪式。
    参加授奖仪式的还有莫斯科工农业先进生产者代表、青年代表、机关和部队的代表、科学、文化艺术部门的代表、苏联保卫和平委员会等社会团体的代表等数千人。
    授奖仪式由“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金委员会主席斯科贝尔琴主持。他向大家介绍了赫鲁晓夫为世界和平事业斗争的巨大贡献。他说，赫鲁晓夫不倦地为实现列宁的和平外交政策而斗争。全世界都知道赫鲁晓夫对缓和国际紧张局势、对巩固和平事业的贡献是十分巨大的。
    接着斯科贝尔琴把一枚金质奖章和证书授给了赫鲁晓夫，并祝他在进一步为维护世界和平的事业中取得更大的成就。这时，一群少先队员走上主席台向赫鲁晓夫献花。
    接着“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金委员会副主席、中国保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若讲了话。他并代表全体中国人民向赫鲁晓夫表示衷心的、热烈的祝贺。
    在授奖仪式上讲话的还有世界和平理事会副主席、国际民主妇女联合会主席戈登夫人，苏联保卫和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫，全印和平理事会主席、印度作家森德拉尔，“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金委员会委员、德国著名女作家西格斯以及工厂青年和农庄的代表等。
    接着赫鲁晓夫发表了长篇演说。他认为授给他“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金是承认苏联人民的巨大努力和他们对于争取和平和普遍安全的斗争的贡献。他感谢大家对他的祝贺。他表示，他将尽力使和平事业获得胜利。他还谈到一些目前的国际问题。他的讲话不时地为热烈的掌声所打断。
    赫鲁晓夫讲话结束时，全体与会者一致起立表示祝贺。
