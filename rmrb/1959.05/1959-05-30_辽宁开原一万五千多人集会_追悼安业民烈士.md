Title: More Than 15,000 People Gather in Kaiyuan, Liaoning, to Commemorate Martyr An Yemin
Author:
Date: 1959-05-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, May 29th. A memorial service was held today in Kaiyuan County, Liaoning Province, to mourn the communist fighter, Martyr An Yemin, who heroically sacrificed his life in the battle to severely punish the Chiang Kai-shek troops on the Fujian front.

　　The meeting was held in An Yemin's hometown, Sizaizi Village. More than 15,000 workers, peasants, soldiers, students, and representatives from various places in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City attended. Wang Qingxiang, the head of Kaiyuan County, called on all the people at the meeting to take Martyr An Yemin as an example and better complete the glorious task of building the motherland. Senior Colonel Liu Yi, a representative of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, extended cordial greetings to the martyr's family, saying: The heroic tenacity and selfless communist spirit of Martyr An Yemin will inspire all commanders and fighters to better defend the motherland and defend socialist construction. An Kunjiu, the martyr's father, said in his speech that he would learn from the heroic Mother Huang, work hard in production, and be a model family member of a revolutionary martyr. The leaders of the Party, government, and military in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City, as well as representatives of various people's organizations, also spoke at the meeting. After the meeting, the representatives presented honorary plaques and gifts to the martyr's family.

　　Prior to this, the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League had issued instructions to the youths of the province, calling on them to learn from An Yemin's lofty communist ideological qualities and heroic deeds, and to make greater achievements for the socialist construction of the motherland.

### 辽宁开原一万五千多人集会  追悼安业民烈士

1959-05-30
第6版()
专栏：

    辽宁开原一万五千多人集会
    追悼安业民烈士
    新华社沈阳29日电　辽宁省开原县今天举行追悼大会，悼念在福建前线严惩金门蒋军战斗中，英勇牺牲的共产主义战士安业民烈士。
    大会在安业民家乡四寨子村举行。到会的有一万五千多名工人、农民、士兵、学生和辽宁省、沈阳市等各地的代表。开原县县长王庆祥在会上号召全体人民以安业民烈士为榜样，更好地完成建设祖国的光荣任务。中国人民解放军海军代表柳夷上校向烈士家属致以亲切慰问，他说：安业民烈士的英勇顽强，舍己为公的共产主义精神，将鼓舞着全体指战员更好地保卫祖国，保卫社会主义建设。烈士的父亲安焜久在讲话中表示，他要向英雄的黄妈妈学习，努力生产，做个模范烈属。辽宁省和沈阳市党政军负责人和各人民团体的代表也在会上讲了话。会后，代表们向烈士家属献了光荣匾和礼品。
    在这以前，共青团辽宁省委曾向全省青年发出指示，号召全省青年学习安业民崇高的共产主义思想品质和英雄行为，为祖国社会主义建设事业作出更大的成绩。
