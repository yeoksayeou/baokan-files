Title: Hungarian Aeromodelling Team Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Hungarian Aeromodelling Team, a group of six led by team leader Rudolf Beck, arrived in Beijing by plane today at our invitation. They will be performing exciting aeromodelling demonstrations in Beijing and other cities in China. The Hungarian Aeromodelling Team has won many international championships. Among the five team members who came to China this time, George Benedict has held two world records; Imre Toth won the championship at the World Control Line Model Aircraft Championships held in Brussels, Belgium last year.

### 匈航空模型运动队到京

1959-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　匈航空模型运动队到京
    新华社8日讯　应邀来我国访问的匈牙利航空模型运动队一行六人，今天由领队鲁道夫·别克率领，乘飞机到达北京。他们这次将在北京和我国其他几个城市举行精彩的航空模型飞行表演。匈牙利航空模型运动队曾多次获得国际比赛的冠军。这次来中国的五名队员中，乔治·班尼迪克曾保持过两项世界纪录；伊姆雷·托特去年在比利时布鲁塞尔举行的世界线操纵模型飞机锦标赛中，曾获得冠军。
