Title: Afghan Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Denies Rumor: British Newspaper's Claim of Soviet Troops Entering Afghan Territory is Pure Fabrication
Author:
Date: 1959-05-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, May 14th: According to TASS, the Afghan Foreign Ministry spokesperson, in a conversation with an Eastern News Agency reporter, refuted the British newspaper reports about Soviet troops entering the Jelalabad (Afghanistan) city area.

The Eastern News Agency, in its report on this matter, stated that the London "Daily Telegraph's" publication of news regarding Soviet troops entering Afghan territory (Jelalabad Province) and the Soviet army constructing fortifications in this area bordering Iran is ridiculous and baseless.

### 阿富汗外交部发言人辟谣  英报所谓苏军进入阿境纯属捏造

1959-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗外交部发言人辟谣
    英报所谓苏军进入阿境纯属捏造
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社喀布尔14日讯：阿富汗外交部发言人在同东方通讯社记者谈话时，驳斥了英国报纸所报道的关于苏联军队开入界拉特（阿富汗）城市地区的消息。
    东方通讯社在关于这件事的报道中说，伦敦“每日电讯报”刊登的关于苏联军队开入阿富汗境内（界拉特省）和苏军在与伊朗相毗邻的这一地区修筑工事的消息，是可笑的和没有根据的。
