Title: Further Strengthen the Brotherly Friendship Between Bulgaria and Czechoslovakia: Bulgarian Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Czechoslovakia for Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-05-22
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 21st: At the invitation of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the government, a Bulgarian party and government delegation headed by Todor Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, arrived here by train this morning.

    The members of the delegation included: Yugov, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Damyianov, Member of the Political Bureau and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Kubadinski, Member of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat; Lukanov, Member of the Central Committee and Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Karadzhoff, Bulgarian Ambassador to Czechoslovakia.

    Welcoming the delegation at the station were: Antonín Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and President of the Republic; Prime Minister Široký, and other party and government leaders.

    Zhivkov spoke at the station. He expressed full support for the participation of Czechoslovakia and Poland in the current East-West Foreign Ministers' Conference in Geneva, and hoped that the spirit of reason and consultation would prevail in Geneva.

    Zhivkov said that the visit of the Bulgarian Party and Government Delegation and the upcoming talks would help further develop relations between the two countries in all aspects and help strengthen the friendship between the two parties.

    Novotný warmly welcomed the delegation's visit in his speech. He emphasized that the two countries are closely linked together by their common path towards socialism and communism and by being members of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

### 进一步加强保捷两国兄弟友谊  保党政代表团抵捷访问

1959-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步加强保捷两国兄弟友谊
    保党政代表团抵捷访问
    新华社布拉格21日电　应捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府的邀请，以保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫为首的保加利亚党和政府代表团今天上午乘车到达这里。
    代表团团员有：保加利亚共产党中央政治局委员、部长会议主席于哥夫、政治局委员、部长会议第一副主席达米扬诺夫、中央委员会委员、书记处书记库巴丁斯基、中央委员会委员、外交部长卢卡诺夫，保加利亚驻捷克斯洛伐克大使卡拉召夫。
    在车站上欢迎代表团的有：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记、共和国总统诺沃提尼、总理西罗基以及其他党和政府领导人。
    日夫科夫在车站上讲了话。他表示完全支持捷克斯洛伐克和波兰参加目前在日内瓦举行的东西方外长会议，并且希望，理智和协商的精神将在日内瓦取得胜利。
    日夫科夫说，保加利亚党和政府代表团的这次访问以及即将举行的会谈将有助于两国之间各方面关系的进一步发展，有助于加强两党的友谊。
    诺沃提尼在讲话中表示热烈欢迎代表团的访问。他着重指出，共同走向社会主义和共产主义的道路和都是以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的成员，使得两国的人民密切地联系在一起。
