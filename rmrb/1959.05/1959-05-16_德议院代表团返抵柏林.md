Title: GDR Parliamentary Delegation Returns to Berlin
Author:
Date: 1959-05-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 14th:
    The German Democratic Republic People's Chamber delegation, led by Dieckmann and Matern, returned to Berlin on May 14th after a friendly visit to China, Vietnam, Korea, and Mongolia.
    During its stay in Asia, the delegation also visited India and Ceylon.
    People's Chamber Vice-Chairman Bach, Deputy Prime Minister Loch, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Schwab, and others went to the airport to welcome them.
    The ambassadors of China, Vietnam, Korea, and Mongolia to the German Democratic Republic, as well as diplomatic envoys from other countries, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 德议院代表团返抵柏林

1959-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    德议院代表团返抵柏林
    新华社柏林14日电　以狄克曼和马特恩为首的德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团在中国、越南、朝鲜、蒙古进行友好访问后已于14日返抵柏林。
    代表团在亚洲期间还访问了印度和锡兰。
    前往机场欢迎的有人民议院副议长巴赫、副总理洛赫、外交部副部长施瓦布等人。
    中国、越南、朝鲜、蒙古驻德意志民主共和国大使及其他国家外交使节也都前往机场欢迎。
