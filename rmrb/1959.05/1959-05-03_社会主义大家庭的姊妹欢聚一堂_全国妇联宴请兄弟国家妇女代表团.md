Title: Sisters of the Socialist Family Gather Together Joyfully: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for Women's Delegations from Brotherly Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-05-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd May: The All-China Women's Federation held a grand banquet tonight to warmly welcome the women's delegations from eleven brotherly countries who came to our country to attend the May Day International Labor Day celebrations and to visit.

　　The banquet hall of the Peace Hotel tonight was filled with an atmosphere of unity, friendship and joy. The sisters of the socialist family gathered together and cordially expressed their wishes for friendship. Everyone frequently raised their glasses, using different languages to wish the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union greater prosperity, to wish the women of the socialist countries to unite more closely, and to wish for world peace.

　　Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, spoke at the beginning of the banquet. She extended a warm welcome on behalf of the All-China Women's Federation to the women representatives of various countries who were invited to visit our country. She said that under the international situation in which the East wind is further prevailing over the West wind, the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union is thriving, more united, and more consolidated. In the cause of building socialism and communism, and in the struggle to defend world peace, the women of our socialist countries have made significant contributions and achieved brilliant achievements.

　　Cai Chang said that under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, and under the shining light of the general line for building socialism, the people and women of our country have made tremendous achievements in industrial and agricultural production and in various socialist construction undertakings. Our achievements are inseparable from the fraternal and selfless assistance of the socialist camp countries headed by the great Soviet Union. Cai Chang finally pointed out that we should better learn from the people and women of the great Soviet Union and socialist countries in the future.

　　The women's delegations from eleven brotherly countries including the Soviet Union, Albania, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Mongolia, Romania, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Poland attended the banquet tonight.

　　Qian Ying, Minister of Internal Affairs, Zhang Qinqiu, Vice Minister of Textile Industry, and Luo Shuzhang, Vice Minister of Light Industry, were invited to attend as guests.

　　Xu Guangping, Shi Liang, Yang Zhihua, Liu Qingyang, and Kang Keqing, vice-chairmen of the All-China Women's Federation, and women representatives and members attending the National People's Congress and the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing also attended the banquet.

### 社会主义大家庭的姊妹欢聚一堂  全国妇联宴请兄弟国家妇女代表团

1959-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    社会主义大家庭的姊妹欢聚一堂
    全国妇联宴请兄弟国家妇女代表团
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会今晚举行盛大宴会，热烈欢迎来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼和访问的十一个兄弟国家的妇女代表团。
    和平宾馆的宴会厅里，今晚充满了一片团结、友好欢乐的气氛。社会主义大家庭的姊妹们欢聚一堂，亲切地互相表达友谊的祝愿。大家频频举杯，用不同的语言祝以苏联为首的社会主义阵营更加繁荣昌盛，祝社会主义各国的妇女更加紧密团结，祝世界和平。
    全国妇女联合会主席蔡畅在宴会开始时讲话。她代表全国妇女联合会向应邀前来我国参观访问的各国妇女代表们表示亲切热烈的欢迎。她说，在东风进一步压倒西风的国际形势下，以苏联为首的社会主义阵营是欣欣向荣，更加团结，更加巩固了。在建设社会主义和共产主义的事业中，在保卫世界和平的斗争中，我们社会主义各国妇女都作出了重大的贡献，取得了光辉的成就。
    蔡畅说，我国人民和妇女在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，在建设社会主义总路线的光辉照耀下，在工农业生产以及各项社会主义建设事业中都取得了巨大的成就。我们这些成就是和以伟大的苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国兄弟般的无私援助分不开的。蔡畅最后指出，我们今后要更好地向伟大的苏联以及社会主义各国的人民和妇女学习。
    出席今晚宴会的有来自苏联、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、蒙古、罗马尼亚、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南民主共和国和波兰等十一个兄弟国家的妇女代表团。
    应邀作陪的有内务部部长钱瑛，纺织工业部副部长张琴秋，轻工业部副部长罗叔章，中华全国总工会副主席陈少敏。
    出席宴会作陪的还有全国妇女联合会副主席许广平、史良、杨之华、刘清扬、康克清和在北京出席人大、政协会议的妇女代表和委员等。
