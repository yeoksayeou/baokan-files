Title: Discussing the Progress of the Geneva Conference, Four Foreign Ministers Frequently Exchange Opinions
Author:
Date: 1959-05-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 30th (TASS dispatch): Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, U.S. Secretary of State Herter, British Foreign Secretary Lloyd, and French Foreign Minister Couve de Murville returned to Geneva from Washington on the same plane on the 29th.

Upon arrival at the airport, the following statement was made to reporters:

"The four foreign ministers had the opportunity to discuss the progress of the Geneva Conference and certain details of the positions adopted by the various parties on the plane. Everyone agreed that the exchange of views was beneficial. The four foreign ministers agreed to hold another private and informal meeting at Mr. Lloyd's villa at five o'clock today."

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 30th (TASS dispatch): The foreign ministers of the Soviet Union, Britain, the United States, and France met on the 29th at the British delegation's residence in Geneva. The foreign ministers held a routine informal meeting, one of many held from time to time to discuss issues under consideration at the Foreign Ministers' Conference.

### 讨论日内瓦会议的进程  四外长频繁交换意见

1959-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    讨论日内瓦会议的进程
    四外长频繁交换意见
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯29日同美国国务卿赫脱、英国外交大臣劳埃德和法国外交部长德姆维尔乘同一飞机由华盛顿回到日内瓦。
    在机场上向记者们发表了下列声明：
    “四国外交部长有机会在飞机上讨论了日内瓦会议的进程和各方所采取的立场的某些细节。大家都认为，交换意见是有益的。四国外长同意，今天五时在劳埃德先生的别墅再举行秘密的和非正式的会晤。”
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏、英、美、法四国外长29日在日内瓦英国代表团寓所会晤。外长们进行了一次普通的非正式会谈，这次会谈是他们就外长会议所讨论的问题不时举行的多次会谈中的一次。
