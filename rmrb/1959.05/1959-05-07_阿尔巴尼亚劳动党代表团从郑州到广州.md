Title: Albanian Labor Party Delegation Travels from Zhengzhou to Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 6th May: After a three-day visit in Henan Province, the Albanian Labor Party delegation left Zhengzhou by plane this morning for Guangzhou. Shi Xiangsheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Miao Huaming, Deputy Secretary-General of the Provincial Committee, and Wang Lizhi, First Secretary of the Zhengzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, were at the airport to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 6th May: The Albanian Labor Party delegation arrived in Guangzhou by plane this afternoon from Zhengzhou.

The distinguished guests were welcomed at the airport by Wang De, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee, Zhu Guang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Municipal Committee and Mayor, Ou Chu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Provincial Committee, Wang Yanguang, Secretary-General of the Municipal Committee, Huang Zhongqiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Provincial People's Committee, and others.

This afternoon, the delegation, accompanied by Mayor Zhu Guang, toured Yuexiu Mountain and ascended the Zhenhai Tower to overlook the city of Guangzhou. In the evening, the Guangdong Provincial Committee and the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party hosted a banquet for the delegation.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团从郑州到广州

1959-05-07
第2版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团从郑州到广州
    新华社郑州6日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团在河南省参观访问了三天以后，今天上午乘飞机离开郑州前往广州。中共河南省委书记处书记史向生、省委副秘书长苗化铭和中共郑州市委第一书记王黎之等到机场欢送。
    新华社广州6日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团今天下午从郑州乘飞机到达广州。
    贵宾们在机场上受到中共广东省委书记处候补书记、中共广州市委第一书记王德，市委书记处书记、市长朱光，省委副秘书长欧初，市委秘书长王炎光，省人民委员会副秘书长黄中强等人的欢迎。
    今天下午，代表团在朱光市长陪同下游览了越秀山，并登上镇海楼俯瞰广州市容。晚上，中共广东省委和广州市委设宴招待代表团。
