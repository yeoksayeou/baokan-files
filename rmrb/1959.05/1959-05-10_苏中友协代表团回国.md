Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Returns Home

Author:

Date: 1959-05-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, headed by Fedorov, returned home by plane this morning after completing its visit to China. During its stay in China, the delegation visited Beijing, Jinan, Shanghai and other places.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Shao Lizi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, and Qian Ying, Minister of Internal Affairs. Antonov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Sudarikov also went to the airport to see them off.

### 苏中友协代表团回国

1959-05-10
第2版()
专栏：

    苏中友协代表团回国
    新华社9日讯　以费多罗夫为首的苏中友好协会代表团在我国访问结束后，已经在今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。代表团在中国期间，曾在北京、济南、上海等地进行了参观访问。
    到机场欢送的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长邵力子、秘书长钱俊瑞，内务部部长钱瑛等。苏联驻中国大使馆参赞安东诺夫和苏达利柯夫也到机场送行。
