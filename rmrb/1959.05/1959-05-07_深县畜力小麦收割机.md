Title: Shen County Animal-Drawn Wheat Harvester
Author:
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 4

The animal-drawn wheat harvester modified from a double-furrow plow by Anping Universal Machinery Factory in Shen County, Hebei Province, has a structure basically similar to the small animal-drawn harvester in Shaanxi. The National Experience Exchange Symposium on Selection and Testing of Animal and Human Powered Wheat Harvesters unanimously agreed that its advantages are: (1) Good cutting quality; (2) Stable operation; (3) Modified using a double-furrow plow frame, it can still be restored to its original double-furrow plow state.

Its disadvantages are: (1) The transmission part is manufactured coarsely, the gear module is too large, and the clearance from the ground is too small (only 3 cm), so it is easy to get tangled with grass and mud, affecting normal operation. (2) The wooden turning wheel diameter is too small, the speed is low, and the missed cutting rate reaches 15%; at the same time, chain transmission is unreliable, and the chain often falls off; the adjustment range is small, and the adaptability to wheat of different heights is poor. (3) There is no clutch mechanism. (4) Side wheels cause severe trampling of wheat, flattening a width of ten to twelve fen each time.

The working width of this harvester is 60 cm, the total weight of the machine is 110 kg, it has 8 moving blades, 8 fixed blades, and 2 pressure edge devices. The average cutting speed is 1.06 meters per second, the wooden turning wheel diameter is 80 cm, the tangential velocity is 1.17 meters per second, and the ground wheel diameter is 50 cm. With one person in charge and one animal pulling, working for 10 hours a day, it can harvest 20 mu of wheat.

Its main components are the frame, harvesting platform, transmission part, cutting part, wooden turning wheel, and ground wheel, etc. (See picture)

### 深县畜力小麦收割机

1959-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　深县畜力小麦收割机
    河北省深县安平通用机械厂利用双铧犁改装的畜力小麦收割机，构造和陕西的小型畜力收割机基本相似。全国人畜力小麦收割机评选试验经验交流座谈会上一致认为它的优点是：（1）切割质量较好；（2）操作平稳；（3）利用双铧犁架子改装，仍能恢复双铧犁原状。
    它的缺点是：（1）传动部分制造粗糙，齿轮模数太大，距离地面的间隙太小（仅三公分），因此容易缠草塞泥，影响正常作业。（2）木翻轮直径太小，转速低，漏割率达15％；同时链条传动不可靠，经常掉链；调节范围小，对不同高度的麦子，适应性较差。（3）没有合离机构。（4）侧轮轧麦比较厉害，每次轧倒十至十二分宽。
    这种收割机的工作宽度为六十公分，全机重一百一十公斤，有八个活动刀片，八个固定刀片，压刃器有二个，平均每秒钟切割速度为一点零六公尺，木翻轮直径为八十公分，每秒钟切线速度为一点十七公尺地轮直径五十分。每天一人掌握、一畜牵引工作十小时，可以割麦二十亩。
    它的主要构成部分是机架、收割台、传动部分、切割部分、木翻轮及地轮等。（附图片）
