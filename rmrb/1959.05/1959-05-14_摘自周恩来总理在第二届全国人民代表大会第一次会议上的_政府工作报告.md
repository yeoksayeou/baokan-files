Title: Excerpt from Premier Zhou Enlai's "Government Work Report" at the First Session of the Second National People's Congress
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-14
Page: 2

In production and construction, the possibilities for technical innovation and technical revolution are limitless. Improving tools, improving equipment, increasing equipment utilization, improving product design and architectural design, improving operating methods and construction methods, saving labor and raw materials, utilizing various substitutes and various "waste products," improving product quality and project quality, and reducing defective product rates – all of these can increase labor productivity and reduce costs.
    ——Excerpt from Premier Zhou Enlai's "Government Work Report" at the First Session of the Second National People's Congress

### 摘自周恩来总理在第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议上的“政府工作报告”

1959-05-14
第2版()
专栏：

    在生产和建设中，技术革新和技术革命的可能性是无限的。改良工具，改善设备，提高设备利用率，改进产品设计和建筑设计，改进操作方法和施工方法，节约劳动力和原料、材料，利用各种代用品和各种“废品”，提高产品质量和工程质量，降低废品次品率，所有这些，都能够提高劳动生产率和降低成本。
    ——摘自周恩来总理在第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议上的“政府工作报告”
