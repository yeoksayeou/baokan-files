Title: Soviet Peace Committee Calls on All Soviet People to Launch a Movement to Promote High-Level Talks and End the Cold War
Author:
Date: 1959-05-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 26th: The Soviet Peace Committee, at its plenary session on May 26th, unanimously adopted a resolution calling on all Soviet people to fully launch a movement to promote high-level talks and end the "Cold War." The resolution expressed firm support for the resolutions of the World Peace Council meeting in Stockholm.
    This meeting was convened to commemorate the tenth anniversary of the World Peace Movement, to summarize the World Peace Council meeting in Stockholm, and to discuss the tasks of the Soviet Peace Committee.
    Participants in the meeting included representatives of Moscow mass organizations, and representatives from the Soviet Union, the United States, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Japan, Iraq, and Canada who attended the Stockholm meeting. World Peace Council President Bernal, who is currently visiting Moscow, also attended the meeting.
    Soviet Peace Committee Chairman Tikhonov said in his speech that the World Peace Movement has achieved powerful development over the past ten years. The World Peace Council meeting in Stockholm demonstrated the unprecedented strength of the world's peace forces. The Soviet people will further strive to achieve lasting world peace, promote peaceful resolution of international disputes, and eliminate the "Cold War."
    Subsequently, World Peace Council Presidium member and Soviet writer Korneichuk delivered a report on summarizing the World Peace Council meeting in Stockholm and the tasks of the Soviet Peace Committee.
    The report said that currently, the ideas of resolving international disputes through peaceful negotiations, reducing armaments, stopping the "Cold War," and strengthening friendly cultural exchanges between countries have been endorsed by the broad masses of people in the world. Now, peace defenders should concentrate all their efforts to promote high-level talks between East and West, conclude a peace treaty with Germany, and ban nuclear weapons testing. The Soviet people have consistently fought for the peaceful resolution of these issues.

### 苏联和平委员会号召全苏人民开展运动  促进高级会谈和结束冷战

1959-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和平委员会号召全苏人民开展运动
    促进高级会谈和结束冷战
    新华社莫斯科26日电  苏联保卫和平委员会在26日举行的委员会全体会议上，一致通过决议，号召全苏联人民全力开展运动，以促进高级会谈和结束“冷战”。决议表示坚决支持世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议的决议。
    这次会议的召开是为了纪念世界和平运动十周年，总结世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议和讨论苏联保卫和平的任务。
    参加会议的有：莫斯科群众团体的代表，出席斯德哥尔摩会议的苏联、美国、越南民主共和国、日本、伊拉克和加拿大的代表。正在莫斯科访问的世界和平理事会主席贝尔纳也参加了会议。
    苏联保卫和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫致词说，十年来，世界和平运动获得了强大的发展。世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议显示了世界和平力量的空前强大。苏联人民将进一步为争取世界持久和平、促进和平解决国际争端问题和消除“冷战”而斗争。
    接着，世界和平理事会主席团委员、苏联作家考涅楚克在会上作了关于世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议的总结和苏联保卫和平的任务的报告。
    报告说，目前，要求以和平谈判来解决国际争端问题，要求裁减军备、停止“冷战”和加强各国文化友好交流等思想，已经得到世界广大人民的赞同。现在，和平保卫者应当集中一切力量，来促进东西方高级会谈、缔结对德和约和禁止核武器试验。苏联人民一贯为和平解决这些问题而斗争。
