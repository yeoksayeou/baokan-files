Title: Albanian Labor Party Delegation Returns to Tirana After Concluding Friendly Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-05-17
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 15th. The Albanian Labor Party delegation, headed by Hysni Kapo, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, returned to Tirana by plane today after visiting our country.
    Among those who went to the airport to greet them were: Balluku, Nushi, Belishova, Mufti and others, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party.
    Luo Shigao, Chinese Ambassador to Albania, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 结束在我国的友好访问后  阿劳动党代表团回地拉那

1959-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

    结束在我国的友好访问后
    阿劳动党代表团回地拉那
    新华社地拉那15日电　以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记希斯尼·卡博为首的劳动党代表团在访问我国后今天乘飞机回到地拉那。
    前往机场迎接的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员巴卢库、努什、别利绍娃、穆夫蒂乌等人。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高也到机场迎接。
