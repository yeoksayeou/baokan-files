Title: Dependents of Martyrs and Disabled, Demobilized, Retired, and Transferred Servicemen Carry Forward Revolutionary Traditions and Actively Participate in Construction; Marshal Luo Ronghuan Meets with Delegates to the Conference; Ministry of National Defense Holds Banquet to Warmly Entertain Relatives

Author: 
Date: 1959-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: Marshal Luo Ronghuan met this evening with the delegates attending the National Conference of Activists in Socialist Construction of Dependents of Martyrs, Military Dependents, and Disabled, Demobilized, Retired, and Transferred Servicemen.

Among the delegates meeting were Deng Fangzhi, mother of Huang Jiguang; Hu Wenxiu, mother of Liu Hulan; Rong Guanxiu, mother of the People's Liberation Army; Dong Quanzhong, father of Dong Cunrui; Luo Diekai, father of Luo Shengjiao; veteran Red Army fighters Fang Heming, Kuang Zhangda, Ma Songsheng, Li Benyun, Fan Deyou; as well as socialist construction activists among the disabled, demobilized, retired, and transferred servicemen. When they arrived at the hall of the Ministry of National Defense, Marshal Luo Ronghuan cordially shook hands with them and wished them good health.

Also present at the meeting were Generals Huang Kecheng, Tan Zheng, Chen Geng, Xiao Jingguang, and Xu Guangda, as well as senior generals from the Army, Navy, and Air Force.

After the meeting, the Ministry of National Defense held a banquet to entertain the delegates. At the banquet, Marshal Luo Ronghuan raised his glass and toasted, expressing his hope that the activists attending the conference would continue to carry forward revolutionary traditions and actively participate in socialist construction. At the banquet, marshals, generals, and delegates talked enthusiastically, as close as family.

### 勉烈军属和残废复员退伍转业军人发扬革命传统积极参加建设  罗荣桓元帅会见大会代表  国防部举行宴会热情招待亲人

1959-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    勉烈军属和残废复员退伍转业军人发扬革命传统积极参加建设
    罗荣桓元帅会见大会代表
    国防部举行宴会热情招待亲人
    新华社25日讯  罗荣桓元帅今晚会见了出席全国烈属、军属和残废、复员、退伍、转业军人社会主义建设积极分子大会的代表。
  会见的代表中有黄继光的妈妈邓芳芝、刘胡兰的妈妈胡文秀、子弟兵母亲戎冠秀、董存瑞的父亲董全忠、罗盛教的父亲罗迭开、老红军战士方和明、旷章大、马松生、李本云、范德友以及残废、复员、退伍和转业军人中的社会主义建设积极分子等。当他们来到国防部大厅时，罗荣桓元帅亲切地和他们握手，并祝他们身体健康。
    会见时在座的有黄克诚、谭政、陈赓、萧劲光和许光达大将以及陆海空军高级将领们。
    会见以后，国防部举行宴会招待代表。宴会上，罗荣桓元帅举杯祝酒说，希望参加大会的积极分子，继续发扬革命传统，积极参加社会主义建设。宴会上，元帅、将军和代表们，热情交谈，亲如家人。
