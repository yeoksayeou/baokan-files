Title: Indian President Prasad Sends Congratulations to Chairman Liu Shaoqi
Author:
Date: 1959-05-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: Indian Republic President Prasad sent a congratulatory telegram to Chairman Liu Shaoqi. The text is as follows:
To His Excellency Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:
    I am pleased to learn of Your Excellency's election to the lofty position of Chairman of the People's Republic of China. On behalf of the Government and people of India, and in my own name, I extend to you my warmest congratulations and best wishes, wishing you good health and your country and its people prosperity and happiness.
                                    Dr. Rajendra Prasad

### 印度总统普拉沙德  电贺刘少奇主席

1959-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    印度总统普拉沙德
    电贺刘少奇主席
    新华社4日讯　印度共和国总统普拉沙德致刘少奇主席的贺电，文如下：中华人民共和国主席刘少奇先生阁下：
    我高兴地获悉阁下当选为中华人民共和国主席的崇高的职位。我代表印度政府和人民并且以我个人的名义，向你致以最热烈的祝贺和最良好的愿望，祝你身体健康，并祝贵国和贵国人民繁荣和幸福。
                                    拉金德拉·普拉沙德博士
