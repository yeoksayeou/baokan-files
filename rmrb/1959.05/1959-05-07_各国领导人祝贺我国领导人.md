Title: Foreign Leaders Congratulate Chinese Leaders
Author:
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 2

President Nasser of the United Arab Republic Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Liu Shaoqi
To His Excellency Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of your election as Chairman of the People's Republic of China, I am very pleased to extend to Your Excellency my warm congratulations and best wishes, wishing you good health and happiness, and the Chinese people happiness and prosperity.
                              Gamal Abdel Nasser
                              May 5, 1959, at the Presidential Palace in Cairo

President Nasser of the United Arab Republic Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Zhu De
To Marshal Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of your election as Chairman of the Standing Committee of The National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, I extend my congratulations to you and wish you success and happiness.
                                Gamal Abdel Nasser
                              May 5, 1959, at the Presidential Palace in Cairo

President Nasser of the United Arab Republic Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai
To Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of your appointment as Premier of the State Council, I am pleased to extend to you my heartfelt congratulations, wishing you success and the Chinese people prosperity and flourishing.
                              Gamal Abdel Nasser
                                May 5, 1959, at the Presidential Palace in Cairo

Vice President Radhakrishnan of India Sends Congratulatory Letter to Chairman Liu Shaoqi
Dear Mr. Liu Shaoqi:
    I remember the pleasant days I spent with you in Beijing. I extend to you my best wishes for success in your term as Chairman of the People's Republic of China. Please convey my regards to your colleagues Mao Zedong, Zhu De, and Zhou Enlai. With kindest regards.
                                        S. Radhakrishnan
                                        New Delhi, May 4, 1959

Prime Minister Sukselainen of Finland Sends Congratulatory Letter to Chairman Liu Shaoqi
To His Excellency Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:
    I often recall the summer of 1956, when you were the outstanding host to the Finnish parliamentary delegation. Please allow me to extend to Your Excellency my heartfelt congratulations and wish you success in your important and arduous task as head of state.
                              V. J. Sukselainen, Prime Minister of Finland
                              Helsinki, April 28, 1959

### 各国领导人祝贺我国领导人

1959-05-07
第2版()
专栏：

    各国领导人祝贺我国领导人
    阿联纳赛尔总统致刘少奇主席贺电中华人民共和国主席刘少奇阁下：
    在阁下当选为中华人民共和国主席的时候，我非常愉快地向阁下表示热烈的祝贺和最良好的祝愿，祝您本人身体健康和幸福，祝中国人民幸福和繁荣。
                              加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔
                              1959年5月5日于开罗总统府
　　阿联纳赛尔总统致朱德委员长贺电中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德元帅：
    在您当选为中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长的时候，我向您表示祝贺，并祝您成功和幸福。
                                加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔
                              1959年5月5日于开罗总统府
　　阿联纳赛尔总统致周恩来总理贺电中华人民共和国总理周恩来先生：
    在您被任命为国务院总理的时候，我愉快地向您致以衷心的祝贺，并祝您获得成功，祝中国人民昌盛、繁荣。
                              加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔
                                1959年5月5日于开罗总统府
　　印度副总统拉达克里希南致刘少奇主席贺信亲爱的刘少奇先生：
    我记得在北京与你相处的愉快的日子。我向你致最良好的愿望，祝你在任中华人民共和国主席职务期间获得成功。请代为问候你的同事毛泽东、朱德和周恩来。此致最亲切的敬意。
                                        萨·拉达克里希南
                                        1959年5月4日新德里
　　芬兰总理苏克舍拉宁致刘少奇主席贺函中华人民共和国主席刘少奇阁下：
    我经常怀念着1956年夏季，接待芬兰议会代表团的杰出的主人。请允许我向阁下致以衷心的祝贺并祝您在国家元首的重要而艰巨的任务中获得成就。
                              芬兰总理维·约·苏克舍拉宁
                              1959年4月28日赫尔辛基
