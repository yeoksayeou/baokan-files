Title: Gromyko Meets with Lloyd
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 26th. Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko met with British Foreign Secretary Lloyd this morning and held talks with him. Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom Malik and members of the British delegation Ruskie attended the meeting.

### 葛罗米柯会见劳埃德

1959-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯会见劳埃德
    新华社26日讯  塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯今天上午会见了英国外交大臣劳埃德并同他进行了会谈。参加会谈的有苏联驻英国大使马立克和英国代表团成员拉斯基。
