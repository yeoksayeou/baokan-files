Title: Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China Promulgates the Emblem of the Communist Youth League of China
Author:
Date: 1959-05-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China's decision on the promulgation of the emblem of the Communist Youth League of China:

    Today is the 40th anniversary of the May Fourth Movement. The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China has decided to promulgate the emblem of the Communist Youth League of China on this glorious festival of the Chinese people and Chinese youth.

    The emblem of the Communist Youth League of China is the symbol of the Communist Youth League of China. It symbolizes that the Communist Youth League of China, under the leadership and kind guidance of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, closely unites the youth of the whole country, holds high the banner of socialism and communism, and bravely advances in the direction pointed out by the Party; it symbolizes that the Chinese youth generation inherits the revolutionary tradition of the Chinese people, carries forward the spirit of hard struggle, is forever loyal to the motherland, loyal to the people, energetic, and strives for progress.

    The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China calls on all members of the Communist Youth League to cherish their league emblem, safeguard the honor of the league, work actively, study hard, unite the masses, and use their exemplary behavior to show that they are worthy of the glorious title of Communist Youth League member.

    The Emblem of the Communist Youth League of China.

    I. The content of the emblem is the League flag, gear, wheat ears, the rising sun and its rays, and a ribbon with the five characters "中国共青团" (Chinese Communist Youth League).

    II. The diameter of the emblem is two centimeters.

    III. The position and size of each part of the pattern shall be reduced or enlarged according to the proportion of the grid ink line drawing.

    IV. The color of the emblem is gold and red. The flag surface and ribbon of the League flag are red, and the five-pointed star and the circle surrounding it, the flag edge, the flag pole, the gear, the wheat ears, the rising sun and its rays, and the five characters "中国共青团" (Chinese Communist Youth League) are all gold. Red is true red (the badge is made of enamel fired into earth-red), and gold is great scarlet gold.　Xinhua News Agency draft (with pictures)

### 中国共产主义青年团中央委员会  颁布中国共产主义青年团团徽

1959-05-04
第3版()
专栏：

    中国共产主义青年团中央委员会
    颁布中国共产主义青年团团徽
    新华社4日讯　中国共产主义青年团中央委员会关于颁布中国共产主义青年团团徽的决定：
    今天是五四运动的四十周年纪念日。中国共产主义青年团中央委员会决定在这个中国人民和中国青年的光辉节日颁布中国共产主义青年团团徽。
    中国共产主义青年团团徽，是中国共产主义青年团的标志。它象征着中国共产主义青年团，在中国共产党和毛主席的领导和亲切教导下，紧密地团结全国青年，高举社会主义和共产主义的旗帜，朝着党所指引的方向奋勇前进；它象征着中国青年一代继承中国人民的革命传统，发扬艰苦奋斗的精神，永远忠于祖国，忠于人民，朝气蓬勃，努力向上。
    中国共产主义青年团中央委员会号召每一个共青团员，都要爱护自己的团徽，维护团的荣誉，积极工作，努力学习，团结群众，以自己的模范行为表明无愧于共青团员这一光荣称号。
    中国共产主义青年团团徽。
    一、团徽的内容为团旗、齿轮、麦穗、初升的太阳及其光芒，写有“中国共青团”五字的绶带。
    二、团徽徽章的直径为两公分。
    三、图案各部分的地位、尺寸，按照方格墨线图的比例缩小或放大。
    四、团徽涂色为金红二色。团旗的旗面和绶带为红色，团旗的五角星和环绕它的圆圈、旗边、旗杆、齿轮、麦穗、初升的太阳及其光芒、“中国共青团”五个字都为金色。红为正红（徽章则用珐琅烧制成靠地红色），金为大赤金。　新华社稿（附图片）
