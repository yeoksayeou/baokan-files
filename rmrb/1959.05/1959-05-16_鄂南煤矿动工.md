Title: Hunan South Coal Mine Begins Construction
Author:
Date: 1959-05-16
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 14th. The construction of the Hunan South Coal Mine in Hubei Province has officially begun. According to the principle of "simultaneous exploration, design, construction, and production," the mining area plans to build the first batch of mine shafts this year, and construction has already started on eight pairs. After these mine shafts are completed and put into production, they will produce more than one million tons of coal for the country next year, which is of great significance for changing the coal shortage situation in Hubei Province. The central and provincial and municipal leading organs attach great importance to the construction of the Hunan South coal base, and have now transferred a large number of management cadres, technicians and well construction forces from old mining areas in various places.

### 鄂南煤矿动工

1959-05-16
第2版()
专栏：

    鄂南煤矿动工
    据新华社武汉14日电　湖北省鄂南煤矿建设工程已经正式动工。根据“边勘探、边设计、边施工、边生产”的方针，这个矿区今年计划新建第一批矿井，目前开工建设的已有八对。这些矿井建成并投入生产后，明年将为国家生产一百多万吨煤炭，对改变湖北省缺煤的情况具有重大的意义。中央和省、市有关领导机关对鄂南煤炭基地的建设极为重视，现已从各地老矿区调来了大批管理干部，技术人员和建井施工力量。
