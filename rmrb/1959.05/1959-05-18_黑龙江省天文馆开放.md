Title: Heilongjiang Provincial Planetarium Opens
Author:
Date: 1959-05-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, May 17th. The Heilongjiang Provincial Planetarium officially opened on the 15th, and welcomed its first group of visitors that evening.

This planetarium provides visitors with venues to acquire various astronomical knowledge, including a planetarium hall and a cinema hall for showing films about the universe. In the exhibition room, there are various astronomical pictures and historical materials on the development of astronomy in China. The reference room stores books and periodicals on astronomical science. The planetarium also plans to absorb astronomy enthusiasts from factories, enterprises, government offices, and schools to form astronomy knowledge research groups to carry out celestial observation and other research activities.

This small planetarium was jointly prepared and established by the Harbin Municipal Science and Technology Association and the Harbin Municipal Workers' Cultural Palace.

### 黑龙江省天文馆开放

1959-05-18
第6版()
专栏：

    黑龙江省天文馆开放
    新华社哈尔滨17日电　黑龙江省天文馆15日正式开放，当晚接待了第一批观众。
    这个天文馆为观众们安排了获得各种天文知识的场所，其中有天象厅，有放映关于宇宙世界影片的电影厅。在展览室里，陈列着多种有关天文的图片和我国天文学发展的历史资料，在资料室存放着天文科学的书刊。天文馆还准备吸收各工厂、企业、机关、学校的天文爱好者成立天文知识研究小组，进行天体观察等研究活动。
    这个小型的天文馆，是由哈尔滨市科学技术协会和哈尔滨市工人文化宫联合筹备成立的。
