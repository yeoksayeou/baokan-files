Title: Trade Union Delegations from Various Countries Return Home After Attending Our "May Day" Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1959-05-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Trade union delegations from the Soviet Union, Finland, and the Cameroons, among other countries, who were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and conduct friendly visits in our country, concluded their visit to China and left Beijing by plane today to return home.

Prior to this, trade union delegations from Madagascar, the Netherlands, Mauritius, Black Africa, Cyprus, Iran, France, Pakistan, and other countries and regions had already returned home.

### 参加我“五一”观礼后  各国工会代表团回国

1959-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    参加我“五一”观礼后
    各国工会代表团回国
    新华社19日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼和进行友好访问的苏联、芬兰等国和喀麦隆地区的工会代表团，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    在这以前，已经回国的有马达加斯加、荷兰、毛里求斯、黑非、塞浦路斯、伊朗、法国、巴基斯坦等国家和地区的工会代表团。
