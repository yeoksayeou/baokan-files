Title: Khrushchev Receives Indian Ambassador
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 29th. TASS reports: The Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Khrushchev, received the Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, K. P. S. Menon, on the 29th at his request, and talked with him.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见印度大使

1959-05-01
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见印度大使
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫29日应印度驻苏联大使克·普·西·梅农的请求接见了他，并且同他谈了话。
