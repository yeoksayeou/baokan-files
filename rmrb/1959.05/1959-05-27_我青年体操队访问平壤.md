Title: Our Youth Gymnastics Team Visits Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1959-05-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 26th. The Chinese Youth Gymnastics Team, which is visiting Korea under the 1959 Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan, arrived in Pyongyang this afternoon by international train. The gymnastics team consists of eleven male and female athletes, and the leader is Leng Ming.

Korean sports figures and personnel from the Chinese Embassy in Korea welcomed the gymnastics team at the station. (Attached picture)

The women's organization in the Mien Ha district of Hanoi, Democratic Republic of Vietnam, has organized a manure accumulation and conservation campaign, providing fertilizer to a region in Ha Dong province. The picture shows the women transporting fertilizer.

Vietnam News Agency draft (Xinhua News Agency)

### 我青年体操队访问平壤

1959-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    我青年体操队访问平壤
    新华社平壤26日电  根据1959年中朝文化合作执行计划来朝鲜访问的中国青年体操队，今天下午乘国际列车到达平壤。体操队共包括男女运动员十一人，领队是冷明。
    到车站欢迎体操队的有朝鲜体育界人士和中国驻朝鲜大使馆人员。（附图片）
    越南民主共和国河内棉行区的妇女组织了一个积肥保肥运动，给河东省的一个地区提供肥料。这是妇女们正在运送肥料的情形。
                               越南通讯社稿（新华社发）
