Folk Songs

　　Apple peaches cover the hillside

　　Silkworms on the mountain cover the hillside,

　　Fish in the pond dart like shuttles

　　Rabbits leap in their nests,

　　Bees brew abundant honey.

　　Apple peaches cover the hillside,

　　Grapes in the orchard rival lanterns,

　　Pears, apricots, melons, and dates are everywhere,

　　Hawthorns, walnuts, and chestnuts abound.

　　Orchard management is very important

　　Orchard management is very important,

　　Technical measures must be well-defined,

　　The orchard must be thoroughly cleaned,

　　Abandoned embankments and weedy areas must all be removed.

　　Preventing and controlling pests and diseases must be timely,

　　Scraping and treating rot must be done early,

　　Eradicating woolly aphids should be complete,

　　Quality cannot be less than perfect.
　　          (Folk song from Jin County, Liaoning)

### 民歌

1959-05-10
第7版()
专栏：

　　民歌
　　苹果桃子满山坡
　　山上蚕儿满山坡，
　　池中鱼儿串如梭
　　兔儿窝中跳，
　　蜂儿酿蜜多。
　　苹果桃子满山坡，
　　园里葡萄赛灯萝，
　　梨杏瓜枣到处有，
　　山楂核桃栗子多。
　　果园管理很重要
　　果园管理很重要，
　　技术措施要订好，
　　园内彻底大清扫，
　　荒堰草格全去掉。
　　防治病虫要及时，
　　刮治腐烂早做好，
　　消灭绵虫应彻底，
　　质量不能差分毫。
　　          （辽宁金县民歌）
