International Briefs

    Algerian Vice-Premier Arrives in Rabat
  Rabat Dispatch: The Vice-Premier and Minister of National Defense of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Krim Belkacem, arrived in Rabat, the capital of Morocco, on the 12th.
    He left Tunisia on the 11th. Chilean Communist Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
    Montevideo Dispatch: Chile's "Popular Daily" reported that the Central Committee of the Chilean Communist Party recently held a plenary session. General Secretary Luis Corvalán delivered a report at the meeting.
    West German Artillery Battalion Receives First American Missile
    Bonn Dispatch: According to a report by the German News Agency on the 13th, the command of the West German Federal "Defense Forces" in Kitzingen announced that the first missile unit of the "Defense Forces," an artillery battalion stationed in Kitzingen, Hesse, received its first "Honest John" missile from the American army on the 12th.
    U.S. Population Figures
    Washington Dispatch: The U.S. Bureau of the Census announced on the 12th that as of April 1 this year, the total population of the United States is estimated to be 176,466,000, an increase of 3,071,000 from a year earlier.
    U.S. Crime Rate Reaches Record High Last Year
    Washington Dispatch: FBI Director J. Edgar Hoover recently testified before the House Appropriations Committee, saying that the number of people committing serious crimes in the United States last year was 11% higher than in 1957, the year with the highest number in past history. In 1957, 2,796,000 people committed serious crimes.
    Hoover's testimony was released by the committee on the 10th.
    United States Increases Troops in Cuba
    New York Dispatch: Cuba's "Hoy" reported that the United States has recently increased the number of troops stationed at U.S. military bases in Cuba.
    The newspaper wrote, "According to information we have obtained, the number of American soldiers arriving in Caimanera (near the Guantanamo Naval Base) is not just the 5,000 we reported earlier, but several times more. There are usually 5,000 American soldiers stationed in Caimanera. This shows that there is currently a large number of American troops in Cuba."

### 国际简讯

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    阿尔及利亚副总理到达拉巴特
  拉巴特讯：阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府副总理兼国防部长克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆于12日到达摩洛哥首都拉巴特。
    他是在11日离开突尼斯的。智共中央委员会举行全体会议
    蒙得维的亚讯：智利“人民报”报道，智利共产党中央委员会最近召开全会。总书记路易斯·科尔巴兰在会上作了报告。 
    西德炮兵营得到第一个美国导弹
    波恩讯：据德意志新闻社13日报道，西德联邦“国防军”歧孙司令部宣布，“国防军”的第一批导弹部队在黑森州歧孙地方驻扎的一个炮兵营在12日从美国军队那里得到了第一个“诚实的约翰”式导弹。美国人口数字
    华盛顿讯：美国国情普查局12日宣布：到今年4月1日，美国人口总数估计达一亿七千六百四十六万六千人，比一年以前增加了三百零七万一千人。
    美国去年犯罪人数创最高纪录
    华盛顿讯：美国联邦调查局局长胡佛最近在众议院拨款委员会作证时说，去年美国犯严重罪行的人数比过去历史上最多的年份1957年还多11％。1957年犯严重罪行的人有二百七十九万六千人。
    胡佛的证词是在10日由这个委员会公布的。
    美国增加在古巴的兵力
    纽约讯：古巴“今日报”报道，美国最近增加了派驻古巴美军基地的兵力。
    报纸写道，“根据我们获得的消息，到达卡伊马内拉（位于关塔那摩海军基地附近）的美国士兵不只是我们早先报道过的五千人，而是要多若干倍。驻扎在卡伊马内拉的美国士兵通常是五千人。这说明了目前在古巴有大量的美国军队。”
