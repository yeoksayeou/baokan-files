Title: Vietnamese Peace Delegation Arrives in Iraq for Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-05-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, May 18th: The Vietnamese Peace Delegation, after attending the World Peace Council meeting in Stockholm, arrived here this afternoon. The Vietnamese Peace Delegation was warmly welcomed by Iraqi peace defenders, representatives of various people's organizations, and democratic organizations.

The delegation will visit Iraq for about a week.

### 越南和平代表团  到达伊拉克访问

1959-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    越南和平代表团
    到达伊拉克访问
    新华社巴格达18日电　越南和平代表团在参加世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议后，今天下午到达这里。越南和平代表团受到伊拉克的和平保卫者、各人民团体和民主组织的代表的热烈欢迎。
    代表团将在伊拉克访问一星期左右。
