Title: China Red Cross Donates 40,000 Yuan to Aid Uruguayan and Argentine Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1959-05-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th – The Red Cross Society of China recently sent telegrams to the Red Cross Societies of Uruguay and Argentina, expressing deep sympathy and condolences to the disaster victims in these two countries who suffered from floods, and donated RMB 20,000 yuan to each country to provide relief to the disaster victims.

The Uruguay River on the border between Uruguay and Argentina and the Parana River within Argentina experienced severe flooding in mid-April.

### 我国红十字会捐款四万元  救济乌拉圭阿根廷灾民

1959-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我国红十字会捐款四万元
    救济乌拉圭阿根廷灾民
    新华社18日讯　中国红十字会最近分别致电乌拉圭和阿根廷红十字会，对这两个国家遭到水灾的灾民表示深切同情和慰问，并分别捐给人民币二万元救济灾民。
    乌拉圭和阿根廷边境的乌拉圭河和阿根廷境内的巴阿那河，在4月中旬发生洪水泛滥。
