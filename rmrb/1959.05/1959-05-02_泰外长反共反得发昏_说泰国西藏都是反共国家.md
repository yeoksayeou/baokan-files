Title: Thai Foreign Minister's Anti-Communist Hysteria: Says Thailand and Tibet are Both Anti-Communist Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-05-02
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, 30th: Thai Foreign Minister Nai Thanat Khoman told the press again on the 27th that the Thai government is particularly "concerned" about the Tibet incident. According to him, this is because the people of Thailand and the people of Tibet both believe in Buddhism, and Thailand and Tibet are both "anti-communist countries."

　　He stated that Thailand has not yet decided whether to aid the Tibetan rebels, and can only give "the Tibetan people" verbal support and sympathy at present.

　　Prior to this, Princess Mongkol Ponpisamai, president of the Buddhist Association of Thailand, gave a statement to the press on the 26th, openly advocating that the United Nations pass a resolution to give China "formal condemnation."

### 泰外长反共反得发昏  说泰国西藏都是反共国家

1959-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    泰外长反共反得发昏
    说泰国西藏都是反共国家
    据新华社30日讯  曼谷消息：泰国外交部长乃他纳·科曼在27日再次向报界说，泰国政府特别“关怀”西藏事件。据他说，这是因为泰国人民和西藏人民都信仰佛教，泰国和西藏都是“反共的国家”。
    他表示，泰国目前还没有决定是否援助西藏叛乱分子，现在只能给予“西藏人民”声援和同情而已。
    在此之前，泰国佛教协会主席蒙昭蓬坡沙迈女亲王曾在26日向报界发表谈话，公然主张由联合国通过决议给中国以“正式的谴责”。
