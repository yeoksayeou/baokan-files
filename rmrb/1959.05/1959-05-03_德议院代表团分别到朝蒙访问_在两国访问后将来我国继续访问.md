Title: GDR Parliamentary Delegation Visits North Korea and Mongolia Separately; To Continue Visit to Our Country After Visits to Both Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-05-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 2nd: The German Democratic Republic (GDR) People's Chamber delegation, led by Hermann Matern, First Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the GDR People's Chamber, arrived in Pyongyang by plane at noon today. The delegation will visit North Korea at the invitation of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea. Among those who welcomed the delegation at the airport were Han Seol-ya, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea, Park Jung-ae and Kim Chang-man, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Nam Il, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Choi Won-taek, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea. Han Seol-ya and Matern delivered speeches at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, May 2nd: The German Democratic Republic People's Chamber delegation, led by Johannes Dieckmann, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber, arrived in Ulan Bator by special plane today. The delegation will pay a four-day visit here at the invitation of the Mongolian Great People's Hural.

The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Jalkhasaikhan, Chairman of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, Balzhinnyam, Vice Chairman of the Presidium, Shara, Secretary, and people from all walks of life. Jalkhasaikhan and Dieckmann delivered speeches at the airport. This afternoon, Dieckmann and members of the delegation paid a courtesy call on Jalkhasaikhan, Chairman of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia.

Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd: The German Democratic Republic People's Chamber delegation, divided into two groups led by Johannes Dieckmann, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber, and Hermann Matern, First Deputy Chairman, left Beijing by plane this morning to pay friendly visits to the Mongolian People's Republic and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, respectively. After visiting Mongolia and North Korea, the delegation will return to Beijing to continue its visit to our country.

### 德议院代表团分别到朝蒙访问  在两国访问后将来我国继续访问

1959-05-03
第5版()
专栏：

    德议院代表团分别到朝蒙访问
    在两国访问后将来我国继续访问
    新华社平壤2日电　由德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团第一副主席赫尔曼·马特恩率领的德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团今天中午乘飞机到达平壤。代表团应朝鲜最高人民会议的邀请，将在朝鲜进行访问。到机场欢迎代表团的有，朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长韩雪野，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、金昌满，内阁副首相兼外务相南日，朝鲜最高人民会议议长崔元泽等。韩雪野和马特恩先后在机场上讲了话。
    新华社乌兰巴托2日电　德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团在人民议院主席团主席狄克曼的率领下，今天乘专机抵达乌兰巴托。代表团应蒙古大人民呼拉尔的邀请，将在这里进行四天访问。
    代表团在机场上受到了蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席贾尔卡塞汗、主席团副主席巴尔吉尼雅姆、秘书长沙拉布以及各界人士的热烈欢迎。贾尔卡塞汗和狄克曼先后在机场上讲了话。今天下午，狄克曼和代表团团员拜会了蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席贾尔卡塞汗。
    新华社2日讯　德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团今天分作两批，分别由德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团主席狄克曼、第一副主席马特恩率领，在今天上午乘飞机离开北京，分别前往蒙古人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国进行友好访问。代表团访问蒙古、朝鲜后将返回北京，继续在我国访问。
