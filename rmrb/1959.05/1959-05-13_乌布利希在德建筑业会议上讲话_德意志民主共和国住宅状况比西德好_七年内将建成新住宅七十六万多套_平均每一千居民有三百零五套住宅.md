Title: Ulbricht Speaks at German Construction Industry Conference: GDR Housing Situation Better Than West Germany; More Than 760,000 New Homes to Be Built in Seven Years; Average of 305 Homes per 1,000 Residents
Author:
Date: 1959-05-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 11th - Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, stated at the recent Third Construction Industry Conference of the German Democratic Republic that the housing situation in the GDR is now better than in West Germany. At the end of 1958, there were 305 homes per 1,000 residents in the GDR, compared to only 271 in West Germany. He said that the GDR's construction industry must become a highly developed industrial sector in the short term.

The Third Construction Industry Conference of the German Democratic Republic was jointly convened by the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and the Ministry of Construction. The conference was held in Berlin from the 6th to the 7th of May.

The main task of the conference was to discuss the seven-year plan for the development of the construction industry, published by the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party in April, in order to make the construction industry move forward by leaps and bounds towards industrialization. According to the seven-year plan for the development of the construction industry proposed by the Politburo, the construction industry in the German Democratic Republic will be fully mechanized by 1965.

The plan stipulates that in the next seven years, the annual construction capacity will increase from 5.7 billion marks to 11 billion marks. Labor productivity will double. 764,000 homes will be built in seven years. By 1965, there will be 339 homes per 1,000 residents in the Republic.

Ulbricht pointed out at the meeting that with the realization of this housing construction plan, the German Democratic Republic will, for the first time in history, eliminate the housing poverty of the working people that has existed for hundreds of years in the shortest possible time. ### 乌布利希在德建筑业会议上讲话  德意志民主共和国住宅状况比西德好  七年内将建成新住宅七十六万多套  平均每一千居民有三百零五套住宅

1959-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    乌布利希在德建筑业会议上讲话
    德意志民主共和国住宅状况比西德好
    七年内将建成新住宅七十六万多套
    平均每一千居民有三百零五套住宅
    新华社柏林11日电  德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希在德意志民主共和国最近举行的第三届建筑业会议上说，目前，德意志民主共和国的住宅状况已经比西德要好，1958年底，在德意志民主共和国的一千个居民中，有三百零五套住宅，而在西德只有二百七十一套。他说，德意志民主共和国的建筑业必须在短期内成为高度发达的工业部门。
    德意志民主共和国第三届建筑业会议是由德国统一社会党中央委员会和建筑部联合召开的。会议于6日到7日在柏林举行。
    这次会议的主要任务是讨论统一社会党中央委员会政治局4月间公布的建筑业发展的七年计划，以便使建筑业向工业化方向大踏步地前进。根据政治局所提出的建筑业发展的七年计划，到1965年，德意志民主共和国的建筑业将完全机械化。
    计划规定今后七年内，每年的建筑能力将从五十七亿马克增加到一百一十亿马克。劳动生产率将增加一倍。七年中将建成七十六万四千套住宅。到1965年，共和国每一千个居民中将有三百三十九套住宅。
    乌布利希在会上指出，实现这个住宅建筑计划，德意志民主共和国将首次在历史上，在最短期限内消除几百年来存在的劳动人民住宅穷困的状况。（附图片）
    1959年，德意志民主共和国的劳动人民将获得九万一千五百幢新的住宅，比去年建成的住宅多二万九千四百幢。这是斯大林城的一个住宅建筑区。
                  德意志通讯社稿（新华社发）
