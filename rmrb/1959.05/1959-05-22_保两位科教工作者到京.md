Title: Two Scientific and Educational Workers from Bulgaria Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — According to the 1959 execution plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, two Bulgarian science and education workers who came to China for inspection, namely, Tsan G. Marinov, Director of the Agricultural Economics School Management Department of the Ministry of Culture and Education of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and Pan S. Panchev, Director of the Culture and Education Department of the Russe State People's Council of Bulgaria, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Relevant officials from the Ministry of Education went to the airport to welcome them.

### 保两位科教工作者到京

1959-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    保两位科教工作者到京
    新华社20日讯　根据中保文化合作协定1959年执行计划前来我国考察的保加利亚科学教育工作者、保加利亚人民共和国文化教育部农业经济学校管理司司长仓·格·马林诺夫和保加利亚卢斯州人民委员会文化教育处处长潘·辛·潘切夫，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。教育部有关负责人前往机场欢迎。
