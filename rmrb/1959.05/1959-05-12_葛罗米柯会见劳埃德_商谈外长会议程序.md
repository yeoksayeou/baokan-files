Title: Gromyko Meets with Lloyd to Discuss Procedures for Foreign Ministers' Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-05-12
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 11th -- TASS reported from Geneva: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko received British Foreign Secretary Lloyd on the afternoon of the 10th and held talks with him on issues related to the Foreign Ministers' Conference.
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 11th -- TASS reported from Geneva: British Foreign Secretary Lloyd paid a visit to Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko this afternoon. They continued to exchange views on the procedural issues of the Foreign Ministers' Conference.

### 葛罗米柯会见劳埃德  商谈外长会议程序

1959-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯会见劳埃德
    商谈外长会议程序
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯10日下午接见了英国外交大臣劳埃德，并就有关外长会议的问题同他进行了谈话。
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦讯：英国外交大臣劳埃德今天下午拜会了苏联外交部长葛罗米柯。他们继续就外长会议的程序问题交换了意见。
