Title: Czechoslovakian Alliance of the Disabled Delegation Visits China at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1959-05-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th – At the invitation of the China Blind Welfare Association and the China Deaf and Mute Welfare Association, a delegation from the Czechoslovakian Alliance of the Disabled, led by Dr. Rodolf Dirl, Chairman of the Czechoslovakian Alliance of the Disabled, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China.

Among those who welcomed them at the airport were Xie Juezai, Chairman of the China Blind Welfare Association, Wu Yunfu, Chairman of the China Deaf and Mute Welfare Association, and Chen Qiyuan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

### 捷残缺人联盟代表团应邀到我国进行访问

1959-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    捷残缺人联盟代表团应邀到我国进行访问
    新华社20日讯　应中国盲人福利会和中国聋哑人福利会邀请前来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克残缺人联盟代表团，在捷克斯洛伐克残缺人联盟主席罗道尔夫·迪尔博士率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国盲人福利会主任委员谢觉哉，中国聋哑人福利会主任委员伍云甫，内务部副部长陈其瑗等。
