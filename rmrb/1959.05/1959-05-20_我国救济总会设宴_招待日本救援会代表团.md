Title: China Relief Association Hosts Banquet for Japanese Relief Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-05-20
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th -- The Japanese National Relief Association delegation of six members, led by delegation head Namba Hideo, arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 18th at the invitation of the China People's Relief Association. Wu Yunfu, Secretary-General of the China People's Relief Association, Pu Huairen, Director of the Supervisory Committee Office, Ouyang Jingrong, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Political Science and Law Society, and others welcomed them at the railway station.
    This morning, the delegation visited the China People's Relief Association and the Red Cross Society of China, and held cordial talks with Wu Yunfu and Hu Lansheng, Vice President of the Red Cross Society of China, and others.
    Tonight, the China People's Relief Association hosted a banquet to welcome the delegation.
    Among those present at the banquet were Hu Lansheng, Vice President of the Red Cross Society, Shao Naifen, Deputy Director of the International Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Xiao Hongqi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Railway Trade Union, and others. Mr. Kinkazu Saionji, a Japanese peace figure in Beijing, also attended the banquet.

### 我国救济总会设宴  招待日本救援会代表团

1959-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    我国救济总会设宴
    招待日本救援会代表团
    新华社19日讯  应中国人民救济总会邀请来我国访问的日本国民救援会代表团一行六人，在团长难波英夫率领下，18日晚到达北京。中国人民救济总会秘书长伍云甫、监察委员会办公室主任浦化人、政法学会书记处候补书记欧阳景荣等人到车站欢迎。
    今天上午，代表团访问中国人民救济总会和中国红十字会，同伍云甫和中国红十字会副会长胡兰生等进行了亲切的交谈。
    今天晚上，中国人民救济总会设宴招待代表团。
    出席宴会作陪的有红十字会副会长胡兰生，中华全国总工会国际部副部长邵乃奋、铁路工会全国委员会副主席肖洪启等。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一先生也出席了宴会。
