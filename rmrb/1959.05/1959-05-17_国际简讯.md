International Briefs

    Indonesian Authorities Arrest American Who Sneaked into the Country
    Jakarta dispatch: The Indonesian news agency reported on the 14th that Indonesian authorities recently arrested an American named Frank Sta in Pontianak, West Kalimantan, who had illegally sneaked into the country. The news agency quoted well-informed sources as saying that Frank Sta had been actively engaged recently, hoping to act as a "mediator" for the "Revolutionary Government of the Republic of Indonesia" rebel group to hold "peace negotiations" with the Indonesian government.
    British-Iranian Talks
    London dispatch: Iranian King Pahlavi held talks with British Prime Minister Macmillan in London on the 10th. It is reported that the talks mainly discussed issues related to the Middle East situation, such as Iraq's withdrawal from the Baghdad Pact, the breakdown of Soviet-Iranian negotiations, and the future policies of the Baghdad Alliance.
    Pakistani Finance Minister to Go to Washington Again to Ask for "American Aid"
    Karachi dispatch: Pakistani Finance Minister Shoaib will soon go to Washington again to request American aid for Pakistan's 1959-1960 fiscal year budget.

### 国际简讯

1959-05-17
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    印度尼西亚当局逮捕一潜入国境的美国人
    雅加达讯：印度尼西亚新闻社14日报道，印度尼西亚当局不久前在西加里曼丹的坤甸逮捕了一个非法潜入国境的名叫弗兰克·斯塔的美国人。该通讯社援引消息灵通人士的话说，弗兰克·斯塔不久前曾经积极进行活动，希望为“印度尼西亚共和国革命政府”叛乱集团同印度尼西亚政府举行“和平谈判”充当“调解人”
    英国伊朗会谈
    伦敦讯：伊朗国王巴列维10日在伦敦同英国首相麦克米伦举行了会谈。据报道，会谈主要讨论了伊拉克退出巴格达条约、苏、伊（朗）谈判破裂、巴格达联盟将来的政策等同中东局势有关问题。
    巴财长又将去华盛顿要“美援”
    卡拉奇讯：巴基斯坦财政部长沙伊卜最近将再次去华盛顿，要求美国对巴基斯坦1959—1960财政年度的预算给予援助。
