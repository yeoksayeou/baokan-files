Title: Yumen Sino-Romanian Friendship Thermal Power Plant Goes into Production
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-16
Page: 2

    Yumen Sino-Romanian Friendship Thermal Power Plant Goes into Production

    (Our report) The Yumen Sino-Romanian Friendship Thermal Power Plant has recently been completed and officially put into production.

    This fully automated thermal power plant, from technical data design to the complete set of power generation equipment, was supplied by the Romanian People's Republic. During the construction process, six experts from Romania and the German Democratic Republic helped and guided the construction.

    At the handover and production signing ceremony, Vicianu, Counselor of the Romanian Embassy in China, Constantin, head of the expert group, Fläger and Mälger, experts from the German Democratic Republic, and Sun Xiaofeng, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Petroleum, spoke at the meeting.

### 玉门中罗友好热电厂投入生产

1959-05-16
第2版()
专栏：

    玉门中罗友好热电厂投入生产
    本报讯　玉门中罗友好热电厂，已在最近建成并正式投入生产。
    这座完全自动化的热电厂，从技术资料设计到整套发电设备，都是罗马尼亚人民共和国供给的。在建厂过程中，罗马尼亚和德意志民主共和国派了六位专家，帮助和指导建厂。
    在举行移交生产签字仪式大会上，罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆参赞维强努、专家组长康斯坦丁，德意志民主共和国专家福来格尔、麦尔格耳，石油部办公厅主任孙晓风等都在会上讲了话。
