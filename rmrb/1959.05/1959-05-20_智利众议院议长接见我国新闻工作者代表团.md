Title: Chilean Speaker of the House Receives Chinese Journalists Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, May 18th: Chilean Speaker of the House Raúl Juliet received the Chinese journalists delegation on the 18th and had a friendly conversation. Juliet also accompanied the delegation on a tour of the parliament.

On the same day, the delegation also visited the "El Informador" newspaper and other newspaper organizations.

The Chinese journalists delegation, headed by Yao Zhen, arrived in Santiago, the capital of Chile, on the 14th at the invitation of the Chilean Journalists Association and began its visit.

### 智利众议院议长接见我国新闻工作者代表团

1959-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    智利众议院议长
    接见我国新闻工作者代表团
    新华社圣地亚哥18日电　智利众议院议长劳尔·胡利埃特18日接见了中国新闻工作者代表团，进行了友好的谈话。胡利埃特还陪同代表团参观了议会。
    在同一天，代表团还参观了“报信者”报和其他一些报社组织。
    以姚溱为首的中国新闻工作者代表团是应智利记者协会的邀请，于14日到达智利首都圣地亚哥开始进行访问的。
