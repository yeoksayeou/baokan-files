Title: Indian "National Herald" editorial says the so-called "All-Parties Tibet Conference" is harmful to peace in India and Asia
Author:
Date: 1959-05-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 13th. The "National Herald" published an editorial on the 12th, criticizing the so-called "All-Parties Tibet Conference" held by the Indian Praja Socialist Party in Jullundur, Punjab, on the 10th. The editorial said, "This kind of conference will only bring great harm to peace in India and Asia."

The editorial said, "Panchsheel remains the basis of Indian policy. India wants to continue to be a friend of China, and it also hopes that Tibet will soon return to normal, so that the Dalai Lama can return to Lhasa."

The editorial said, "Some people have suggested that India raise the demand for autonomy for Tibet in the United Nations. This would not only involve India in a cold war with China, but also undermine the entire foundation of Indian foreign policy and undermine the peace of the entire South and Southeast Asia."

The editorial disagreed with the Praja Socialist Party's practice of slandering the Communist Party of India by using the Tibet incident.

### 印度“国民先驱报”社论说  所谓“各党派西藏会议”  有害于印度和亚洲和平

1959-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

    印度“国民先驱报”社论说
    所谓“各党派西藏会议”
    有害于印度和亚洲和平
    新华社新德里13日电  “国民先驱报”在12日发表社论，抨击印度人民社会党10日在旁遮普省朱龙多搞的所谓“各党派西藏会议”。社论说，“这种会议只会给印度和亚洲和平带来很大的害处。”
    社论说，“潘查希拉仍然是印度政策的基础。印度要继续做中国的朋友，它还希望西藏不久将恢复正常，从而使达赖喇嘛能够返回拉萨。”
    社论说，“有人建议，由印度在联合国提出给予西藏自治的要求，这不仅使印度卷入一场同中国进行的冷战中，而且还将破坏印度外交政策的全部基础以及破坏整个南亚和东南亚的和平。”
    社论不同意人民社会党借西藏事件来诽谤印度共产党的做法。
