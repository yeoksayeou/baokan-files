    Warrior's Poems
    A hoe and a gun
    My two treasures,
    A hoe and a gun;
    Gun hangs on my shoulder,
    Hoe in my hand.
    On the labor front, I use the hoe,
    On the battlefield, I wield the gun;
    A model in labor and production,
    Always in the lead during combat.
    My two treasures,
    A hoe and a gun;
    They are my good partners,
    Together striving for glory for the motherland.
              (Liang Zhipeng)
    Militia
    The assembly whistle, sounds once,
    The villagers move like lightning,
    Each like a mighty lion,
    Standing in a line.
    The drill whistle, sounds three times,
    Male and female villagers train diligently,
    Sweat soaking their clothes,
    Their will to train is firm.
    Villagers, train hard,
    Defend the motherland, go to the front lines,
    Skilled in production,
    Leading the way in killing the enemy.
              (Ye Xiaoshan)

### 战士的诗

1959-05-31
第5版()
专栏：

    战士的诗
    一把锄头一支枪
    我的宝贝有两样，
    一把锄头一支枪；
    枪儿挂在我肩膀，
    锄头拿在我手上。
    劳动战场用锄头，
    杀敌疆场把枪拿；
    劳动生产是模范，
    战斗当中跑在前。
    我的宝贝有两样，
    一把锄头一支枪；
    它俩是我的好伙伴，
    同为祖国争光荣。
              （梁志鹏）
    民兵
    集合哨，响一遍，
    社员动作如闪电，
    个个像雄狮，
    站成一条线。
    操练哨，响三遍，
    男女社员勤操练，
    汗水湿透衣，
    练兵意志坚。
    社员们，勤操练，
    保卫祖国上前线，
    生产是能手，
    杀敌走在前。
              （叶晓山）
