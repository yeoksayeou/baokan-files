Title: Premier Zhou Receives Austrian Journalist Bruno Frei
Author:
Date: 1959-05-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd Premier Zhou Enlai received Bruno Frei, a Beijing-based correspondent for the Austrian newspaper "Volksstimme," this afternoon. Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present at the reception.

### 周总理接见奥地利记者布罗诺·佛莱

1959-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见奥地利记者布罗诺·佛莱
    新华社2日讯　周恩来总理在今日下午接见了奥地利“人民之声”报驻北京记者布罗诺·佛莱。接见时，在座的有外交部新闻司副司长徐晃。
