Title: Completing the Task of Reviewing Achievements, Exchanging Experiences, and Selecting Outstanding Athletes: The People's Liberation Army Games Conclude Successfully
Author:
Date: 1959-05-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 16th dispatch: The Second Sports Games of the Chinese People's Liberation Army solemnly closed today at the Xian Nong Tan Stadium in Beijing. After more than forty days of intense competition (including competitions in Nanjing, Wuxi, Pingliang, Kaifeng, and other places), the Games successfully completed the tasks of reviewing achievements, exchanging experiences, and selecting outstanding athletes. As a result of the competition, eighteen events, forty-four individuals, and two teams broke and set world records and international competition records in shooting, radio transmission and reception, aviation models, military practical skills, armed swimming, and other events. Fifty events, one hundred and one individuals, and six teams broke national records and set national best scores in various sports events, and the competition results of thirty-eight athletes reached the standard of Master of Sports, and three hundred and forty-five athletes reached the level of first-class athletes. This Games will play a major role in promoting the further development of combat-oriented, mass-oriented, and regular sports activities throughout the army in the future and in further improving the level of sports in the army.

Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Chen Yi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Cheng Qian, Lin Feng, President of the Supreme People's Court Xie Juezai and others attended today's closing ceremony.

Also attending the closing ceremony were responsible persons from central state organs, various democratic parties, and people's organizations, senior generals from various services and arms of the People's Liberation Army, and representatives of workers, peasants, students, and officers and soldiers of the PLA units stationed in Beijing.

More than 40,000 athletes, performing teams, and spectators attended the closing ceremony.

The weather in Beijing was clear today. At 3:05 pm, General Yang Yong, the commander-in-chief of the Games, announced the opening of the closing ceremony of the Second Sports Games of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The military band played the "Liberation Army March," and more than 5,000 athletes wearing sports uniforms of seventeen different colors marched in magnificent strides from the east, south, and north gates at the same time. Among them were young athletes who broke world records and international competition records, creators of national records and national best scores, champions in various sports events, famous athletes on the sports field, and a large number of new forces. Nearly 30,000 spectators continuously congratulated the loyal defenders of the motherland on their glorious achievements in sports with warm applause.

Then, the solemn closing ceremony began. The military band played the national anthem.

Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and Director of the Judging Committee of the Second Sports Games of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, made a summary and evaluation at the meeting. (Full text see separate article). After the speech, General Tan Zheng, Director of the General Political Department, presented awards. The seventeen delegations of this Games won forty-five first-place group awards respectively, and a total of two thousand and thirty-five individuals won personal awards. Among them, thirty-three people including Zhang Hong, who broke world records and international competition records, received material awards, and among the forty-eight people who broke national records, five people including Huang Zhiyong received gold medals for "Breaking National Records in Sports" issued by the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission. In addition, the Beijing Municipal Group Gymnastics Team, the Beijing Military Region bayonet team, and the armored corps motorcycle team, who gave wonderful performances during the Games, also received pennants respectively.

After the awards were presented, the athletes held prizes and pennants, wore medals, and circled the field, waving to the audience frequently. At this time, two helicopters flew over the sky, scattering colorful leaflets as a salute. Suddenly, colorful flowers fluttered in the sports field, and the crowd cheered and applauded.

After the closing ceremony, the performance program began. The Beijing Municipal Children's Group Gymnastics Team performed children's exercises and girls' exercises at the meeting. The eight relay teams who won the final rights during the Games performed a 1,600-meter relay race. Immediately after the students of the Beijing Institute of Physical Education performed various graceful gymnastics poses on motorcycles, the armored corps motorcycle team rushed into the arena and performed various thrilling motorcycle stunts. These wonderful performances constantly won warm applause from the audience.

Finally, the "August 1st" and Beijing handball teams held a tense and fierce handball exhibition match.

Tonight, 35,000 people from all walks of life in the capital, officers and soldiers of the troops stationed in Beijing, and all the athletes of the People's Liberation Army Sports Games also held a grand garden party at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace to warmly congratulate the excellent achievements made by the Second Sports Games of the People's Liberation Army and the successful conclusion of the Games.

### 完成检阅成绩交流经验选拔优秀运动员的任务  解放军运动大会胜利闭幕

1959-05-17
第2版()
专栏：

    完成检阅成绩交流经验选拔优秀运动员的任务
    解放军运动大会胜利闭幕
    新华社16日讯　中国人民解放军第二届体育运动大会今天在北京先农坛体育场隆重闭幕。大会经过四十多天的紧张比赛（包括在南京、无锡、平凉、开封等地的比赛），胜利完成了检阅成绩、交流经验、选拔优秀运动员的任务。比赛结果，有十八项、四十四人次、两队次在射击、无线电收发报、航空模型、军事实用、武装泅渡等项目上，突破和创造了世界纪录和国际比赛纪录；有五十项、一百零一人次、六队次在各运动项目上突破了国家纪录和创造了国家的最高成绩，并有三十八名运动员的竞赛成绩，达到了运动健将的标准，三百四十五名选手达到了一级运动员的水平。这次大会，对于全军今后进一步开展战斗性、群众性、经常性的体育运动和进一步提高全军体育运动水平，将起重大的推动作用。
    国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠、李济深、黄炎培、程潜、林枫，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉等出席了今天的闭幕式。
    参加闭幕式的还有中央国家机关、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，人民解放军各军种、各兵种的高级将领，首都的工人、农民、学生和解放军驻京陆、海、空军各部队官兵代表等。
    参加闭幕式的运动员、表演队伍和观众共四万多人。
    今天北京天气晴朗。下午三时零五分，大会总指挥杨勇上将宣布中国人民解放军第二届体育运动大会闭幕式开始。军乐队高奏“解放军进行曲”，穿着十七种不同颜色运动衣的五千多名运动员迈着雄壮的步伐由东、南、北三个门同时入场。他们中有打破世界纪录和国际比赛纪录的青年选手，有国家纪录和国家最高成绩的创造者，有各个运动项目的冠军获得者，有运动场上的名将，也有大批的新生力量。近三万观众不断以热烈的掌声，祝贺祖国忠诚的保卫者在体育运动上所取得的光辉成就。
    接着，庄严隆重的闭幕式开始。军乐队高奏国歌。
    中国人民解放军总政治部副主任、中国人民解放军第二届体育运动大会评选委员会主任刘志坚中将在会上作总结讲评。（全文见另条），讲话后，由总政治部主任谭政大将发奖。这届运动大会十七个代表队分别获得四十五个团体分第一名奖，获得个人奖的共有二千零三十五人次。其中，突破世界纪录和国际比赛纪录的张鈜等三十三人获得物质奖，打破国家纪录的四十八人中，黄志勇等五人获得国家体育运动委员会颁发的“体育运动破全国纪录”金质奖章。此外，在大会期间作了精采表演的北京市团体操队，北京部队刺杀队和装甲兵摩托车队也分别获得锦旗。
    发奖毕，运动员手捧奖品、奖旗，胸佩奖章，绕场一周，向观众频频招手致敬。这时，两架直升飞机飞临上空，遍撒彩色传单致意。运动场上顿时彩花纷飞，人群欢跃，掌声和欢呼声响成一片。
    闭幕式后，表演项目开始。北京市少年团体操队，在会上表演了少年操和少女操。大会期间获得决赛权的八个接力队，表演了一千六百公尺接力赛跑。紧接着北京体育学院学生在摩托车上表演各种健美的体操姿势后，装甲兵摩托车队飞驰入场，表演了各种惊险的摩托车特技。这些精采的表演，不断博得全场热烈的掌声。
    最后，“八一”和北京手球队进行了一场紧张激烈的手球表演赛。
    今天晚上，首都各界人民、驻京部队官兵和全军体育运动大会的全体运动员们三万五千多人，还在北京市劳动人民文化宫举行了盛大的游园晚会，热烈祝贺人民解放军第二届体育运动大会所取得的优良成就和大会的胜利闭幕。（附图片）
