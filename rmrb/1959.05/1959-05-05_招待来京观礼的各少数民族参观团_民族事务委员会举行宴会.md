Title: Nationalities Affairs Commission Hosts Banquet for Visiting Minority Nationalities Delegations Here to Observe the Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1959-05-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, May 4th. The Nationalities Affairs Commission hosted a banquet at the Beijing Hotel tonight to entertain five minority nationalities delegations who are here to participate in the “May Day” celebrations in the capital. These delegations come from Ningxia, Yunnan, Guizhou and other places, encompassing more than twenty nationalities, totaling more than two hundred people.

Sa Kongliao, Yang Jingren, and Xie Hechou, vice chairmen of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, attended the banquet.

At the banquet, representatives of all nationalities frequently toasted the great unity of all nationalities in the motherland, the great victories achieved on all fronts of industry and agriculture, the new life of the Tibetan people, and the health of Chairman Mao, the great leader of all nationalities.

### 招待来京观礼的各少数民族参观团  民族事务委员会举行宴会

1959-05-05
第2版()
专栏：

    招待来京观礼的各少数民族参观团
    民族事务委员会举行宴会
    新华社4日讯　民族事务委员会今晚在北京饭店举行宴会，招待参加首都“五一”节观礼的五个少数民族参观团。这些参观团来自宁夏、云南、贵州等地，包括二十多个民族，共有二百多人。
    民族事务委员会副主任萨空了、杨静仁、谢鹤筹参加了宴会。
    宴会上，各族代表频频为祖国各民族的大团结、为工农业各个战线上取得的伟大胜利、为西藏人民的新生、为各民族伟大领袖毛主席的健康干杯。
