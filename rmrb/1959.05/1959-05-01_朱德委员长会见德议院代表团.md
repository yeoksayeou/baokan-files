Title: Chairman Zhu De Meets Delegation from the German Parliament
Author:
Date: 1959-05-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 30th: Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, met this evening with Otto Grotewohl, President of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, his first vice-president, Hermann Matern, and all members of the delegation of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic led by them.

    Present at the meeting were Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Saifudin, Lin Feng, Xu Bing, Shi Liang, Xu Guangping, Zhang Su, Ji Pengfei, Kang Keqing and others.

    Also present at the meeting was Wang Guozhen, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China.

    The hosts and guests had a cordial conversation during the meeting.

### 朱德委员长会见德议院代表团

1959-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德委员长会见德议院代表团
    新华社30日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，今天晚间会见了德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团主席狄克曼、第一副主席马特恩以及由他们率领的德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团全体团员。
    会见时在座的有林伯渠、李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通、赛福鼎、林枫、徐冰、史良、许广平、张苏、姬鹏飞、康克清等。
    会见时，德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔也在座。
    会见时，宾主进行了亲切交谈。
