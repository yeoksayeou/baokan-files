Title: Two Hundred and Twenty-Four Archers in Grand Competition: National Archery Competition Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd: The 1959 National Archery Championships, with the participation of two hundred and twenty-four male and female archers, began today at the Guanyuan Stadium in Beijing. In the first stage of the most difficult 90-meter target archery competition, the Shanghai men's team performed relatively well. Male archer Zhang Langshui temporarily leads with a score of 94 rings from 36 arrows.

This is the largest national archery competition held in our country since 1953. The one hundred and twelve male archers and one hundred and twelve female archers come from the Chinese People's Liberation Army and nineteen provinces, cities, and autonomous regions, including the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Qinghai, totaling twenty units. In 1953, only five athletes from four units participated in this competition, and in 1958, only sixty-four people from fifteen units participated.

This competition adopts international archery competition methods. The target distances for men are four (30, 50, 70, and 90 meters) instead of the previous three (20, 30, and 40 meters); the target distances for women are also four (30, 50, 60, and 70 meters) instead of the previous two (20 and 30 meters). Male and female archers each shoot seventy-two arrows at each distance, whereas previously they shot ten arrows.

This championship includes team and individual competitions for both men and women. The entire competition will last four days.

### 二百二十四名射手大比武  全国射箭比赛在京举行

1959-05-24
第2版()
专栏：

    二百二十四名射手大比武
    全国射箭比赛在京举行
    新华社23日讯　有二百二十四名男、女射手参加的1959年全国射箭锦标赛，今天在北京官园体育场开始举行。上海男子队今天在最困难的九十公尺射准的第一阶段比赛中，成绩比较突出，男射手张浪水以三十六箭命中九十四环的成绩暂时领先。
    这是自1953年以来，我国举行的规模最大的一次全国性射箭比赛，一百一十二名男射手和一百一十二名女射手们，分别来自中国人民解放军和内蒙古自治区、青海等十九个省、市、自治区共二十个单位。而1953年参加这项比赛的只有来自四个单位的五名运动员，1958年也只有十五个单位的六十四人参加。
    这次比赛采用了国际射箭比赛方法，男子射准的距离是四个（三十、五十、七十、九十公尺），而不是过去的三个（二十、三十、四十公尺）；女子射准的距离也是四个（三十、五十、六十、七十公尺），而不是过去的两个（二十、三十公尺）。男、女射手每种距离都射七十二箭，而过去是射十箭。
    这次锦标赛男、女都有队际比赛和个人比赛。全部比赛共举行四天。
