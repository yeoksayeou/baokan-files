Title: Polish Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-30
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The five-member Polish cultural delegation, headed by Tadeusz Zaorski, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China according to the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Qu Wu, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Xiuzhu, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 波文化代表团到京

1959-05-30
第5版()
专栏：

    波文化代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国访问的波兰文化代表团一行五人，在团长塔戴乌什·萨奥尔斯基率领下，今天乘飞机到北京。前往机场欢迎的有对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武，中华全国总工会书记处书记张修竹等。
