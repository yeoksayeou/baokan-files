Title: Ode to Marsh Gas
Author:
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 3

　　Ode to Marsh Gas
　　Look at this unique bus,
　　With a large bag on its roof.
　　This is not a decoration,
　　But a new creation of Shanghai workers to save gasoline.
　　The bag is filled with marsh gas,
　　—a natural gas that comes from the ground.
　　At the terminal station, there is a marsh gas well,
　　The marsh gas is pumped into the bag through a hose behind the bus,
　　And from another hose on the bag it goes into the engine.
　　Using it to power the bus,
　　One bag can run for thirty kilometers.
　　The driver praises marsh gas:
　　The car has great impetus and runs fast when it uses it;
　　There is no longer an unpleasant smell of oil in the carriage;
　　The greatest benefit is:
　　The 22,000 liters of gasoline needed by each vehicle every year,
　　Will be replaced by it. (Attached picture)
　　Marsh Gas Car　　　Our Reporter　Ye Shitao

### 沼气赞

1959-05-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　沼气赞
　　瞧，这辆别致的公共汽车，
　　车顶上张着个大口袋。
　　这不是什么装饰品，
　　而是上海工人节约汽油的新创造。
　　口袋里盛的是沼气，
　　——一种地里冒出来的天然气。
　　终点车站有个沼气井，
　　沼气从车后的皮管打进口袋，
　　又从口袋的另一根管子通到引擎里。
　　用它开动汽车，
　　一口袋就能跑上三十公里。
　　驾驶员称赞沼气：
　　汽车用它发动冲力大、跑的快；
　　车厢里不再有难闻的油味；
　　最大的好处是：
　　每辆每年需用的二万二千公升汽油，
　　就此被它代替。（附图片）
　　沼气汽车　　　本报记者　叶世涛摄
