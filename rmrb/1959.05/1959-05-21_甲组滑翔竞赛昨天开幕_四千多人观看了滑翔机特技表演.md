Title: Group A Gliding Competition Opens Yesterday; Over Four Thousand People Watch Glider Aerobatics Display
Author:
Date: 1959-05-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The Group A Gliding Competition, held in advance for the First National Games, opened this morning at the Liangxiang China Aviation Sports Club in Beijing.

　　More than four thousand spectators attending the opening ceremony arrived at the venue, where dozens of gliders and towing aircraft painted in various colors were parked, at a little after eight in the morning. Li Da, Director of the All-China National Defense Sports Association, and Huang Zhong, Deputy Director of the National Sports Commission, attended the opening ceremony.

　　When the seventy-eight male and female athletes (including reserve members) from nineteen gliding teams representing the Chinese People's Liberation Army and eighteen provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions marched into the venue in neat formation, enthusiastic applause immediately erupted from the audience.

　　Subsequently, Wu Jingyu, Director of the Aviation Sports Competition Committee of the First National Games, delivered the opening address. He wished the athletes participating in the competition excellent results.

　　After the opening ceremony, the athletes performed glider formation flying and aerobatics, which were well-received by the audience. (Attached picture)

　　After the opening ceremony, a glider is towed into the air by a Yak-18 aircraft, preparing for aerobatic performance. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photo

### 甲组滑翔竞赛昨天开幕  四千多人观看了滑翔机特技表演

1959-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    甲组滑翔竞赛昨天开幕
    四千多人观看了滑翔机特技表演
    据新华社20日讯　第一届全国运动会提前举行的甲组滑翔竞赛，今天上午在北京良乡中国人民航空俱乐部举行开幕式。
    早晨八点多钟，出席开幕式的四千多名观众，就来到了停着几十架漆着各种颜色的滑翔机和牵引飞机的会场。中国人民国防体育协会主任李达和国家体委副主任黄中等，都出席了开幕式。
    当来自中国人民解放军和十八个省、市、自治区共十九个滑翔代表队的七十八名男、女运动员（包括预备队员），迈着整齐的步伐走入会场时，观众中立刻响起了热烈的掌声。
    接着，第一届全国运动会航空运动竞赛委员会主任吴静宇致开幕词。他预祝参加这次竞赛的运动员们取得优秀的成绩。
    开幕式后，运动员们举行了滑翔机编队和特技表演，受到观众的欢迎。（附图片）
    开幕式后滑翔机由雅克——十八型飞机牵引到空中，准备进行特技表演。　新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
