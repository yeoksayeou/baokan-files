Title: Indian Defense Minister Menon Says India Will Not Be Drawn Into World Conflict
Author:
Date: 1959-05-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 12th. According to the Indian Press Trust, Indian Defense Minister K. Menon said in New Delhi on the 11th that "no matter what provocative actions there are, India will never be drawn into a world conflict or global war."

Menon made these remarks while addressing students from the U.S. Military Academy visiting India. He said that India's reduction of its defense spending for economic development is its "actual desire."

He believes that military alliances of any nature are in "direct violation of the United Nations Charter," and that these (military alliance) treaties cannot be justified morally or politically.

### 印度国防部长梅农说  印度不会被拖入世界冲突

1959-05-13
第5版()
专栏：

    印度国防部长梅农说
    印度不会被拖入世界冲突
    新华社新德里12日电  据印度报业托辣斯报道，印度国防部长克·梅农11日在新德里说，“不管有什么样的挑衅行动，印度永远不会被拖入一场世界性的冲突或全球战争。”
    梅农是在向访问印度的美国军事学院学员们发表讲话时这样说的。他说，印度为了经济发展而削减它的国防开支是它的“实际的愿望”。
    他认为，任何性质的军事联盟都是“直接违反联合国宪章的”，在道义上或者政治上都不能为这些（军事联盟）条约辩解。
