Title: Polish Archery Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 15th. The first archery team from a fraternal country to visit China—the Polish men's and women's archery team, a total of six people, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 14th, led by the team leader, W. Narewajko.

　　The Polish men's and women's archery team consists of two male and two female athletes. They are all top athletes in Poland. The two female athletes are: Zugowska, the all-Poland champion, and Kannicka, the runner-up; the two male athletes are: Monczanski, the all-Poland runner-up, and Nowakowski, who came in third place.

### 波兰射箭队到京

1959-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰射箭队到京
    据新华社15日讯　兄弟国家来我国访问的第一个射箭运动队——波兰男、女射箭队，全体六人14日由领队瓦·纳列瓦伊科率领，乘飞机到达北京。
    波兰男、女射箭队中共有男、女运动员各两名。他们都是波兰的运动健将。两名女运动员是：全波冠军祖戈夫斯卡、亚军坎尼茨卡；两名男运动员是：全波亚军蒙契安斯基和第三名诺瓦科夫斯基。
