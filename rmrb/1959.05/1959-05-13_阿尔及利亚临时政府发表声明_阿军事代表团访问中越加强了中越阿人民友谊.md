Title: Algerian Provisional Government Issues Statement: Algerian Military Delegation's Visit to China and Vietnam Strengthens Friendship Between Chinese, Vietnamese and Algerian People
Author:
Date: 1959-05-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 11th - The Algerian Republic Provisional Government issued a brief statement here today saying that the Algerian military delegation's visit to China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has strengthened the friendly ties between the people of China, Vietnam, and Algeria.
    The delegation arrived in Cairo on the 9th.

### 阿尔及利亚临时政府发表声明  阿军事代表团访问中越加强了中越阿人民友谊

1959-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚临时政府发表声明
    阿军事代表团访问中越加强了中越阿人民友谊
    新华社开罗11日电  阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府今天在这里发表的一项简短声明说，阿尔及利亚军事代表团对中国和越南民主共和国的访问加强了中国、越南人民和阿尔及利亚人民之间的友好联系。
    代表团已经在9日到达开罗。
