Title: Polish People's Army Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Win in Tianjin Competition
Author:
Date: 1959-05-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, May 3rd: The Polish People's Army men's and women's volleyball teams, currently visiting our country, held friendly matches this afternoon in Tianjin against the Chinese Public Security Sports Association Men's Volleyball Team and the Hebei Province Women's Volleyball Team. As a result, the Polish People's Army men's team won three to two, and the Polish People's Army women's team won three to zero. The Polish People's Army men's and women's volleyball teams are scheduled to hold two more friendly matches in Beijing on May 5th and 7th.

### 波人民军男女排球队在津比赛获胜

1959-05-04
第3版()
专栏：

    波人民军男女排球队在津比赛获胜
    据新华社天津3日电　来我国访问的波兰人民军男、女排球队，今日下午在天津同我国公安体育协会男子排球队和河北省女子排球队进行友谊比赛。结果，波兰人民军男子队以三比二获胜，波兰人民军女子队以三比零获胜。波兰人民军男、女排球队预定5月5日和7日还将在北京举行两次四场友谊比赛。
