Title: The Presidium of the Vietnamese National Assembly Announces that the "Compensation Agreement" between Japan and South Vietnam is Invalid
Author:
Date: 1959-05-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 27th: The Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hoang Van Hoan, announced this afternoon at the tenth session of the National Assembly Presidium that the so-called "Vietnam War Compensation" agreement recently signed separately by the Kishi Nobusuke government and the South Vietnamese authorities is illegal and completely invalid.

He said that the Presidium of the meeting fully agreed with the representatives' proposal and would instruct the Standing Committee of the National Assembly to express its opposition to this illegal act by the Kishi Nobusuke government to the Japanese Diet.

He said that the Presidium of the meeting expressed its agreement with the statement issued and the measures taken by the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the above issue.

### 越南国会主席团宣布  日本同南越“赔偿协定”无效

1959-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国会主席团宣布
    日本同南越“赔偿协定”无效
    新华社河内27日电  越南民主共和国国会常务委员会副委员长黄文欢今天下午代表国会第十次会议主席团在会上宣布，岸信介政府最近单独同南越当局签订的所谓“越南战争赔偿”协定，是非法的和完全无效的。
    他说，会议主席团完全同意代表们的建议，将责成国会常务委员会向日本国会表示反对岸信介政府的这一非法行为。
    他说，会议主席团表示赞同越南民主共和国政府对上述问题所发表的声明和采取的措施。
