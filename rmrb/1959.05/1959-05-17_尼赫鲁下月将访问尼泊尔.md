Title: Nehru to Visit Nepal Next Month
Author:
Date: 1959-05-17
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 15th: According to a dispatch from Kathmandu, the Nepalese Foreign Ministry announced on the 14th that Indian Prime Minister Nehru will arrive in the Nepalese capital of Kathmandu on June 11th for a four-day official visit. Nehru is visiting Nepal at the invitation of King Mahendra of Nepal.

### 尼赫鲁下月将访问尼泊尔

1959-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁下月将访问尼泊尔
    新华社新德里15日电　据加德满都消息，尼泊尔王国外交部14日宣布，印度总理尼赫鲁将在6月11日到达尼泊尔首都加德满都，进行为期四天的正式访问。尼赫鲁是应尼泊尔国王马亨德拉的邀请去尼泊尔访问的。
