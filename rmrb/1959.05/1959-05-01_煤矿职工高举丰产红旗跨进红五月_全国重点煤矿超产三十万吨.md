Title: Coal Mine Workers Raise High-Yield Red Flags and Enter Red May; National Key Coal Mines Exceed Production by 300,000 Tons
Author:
Date: 1959-05-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 30th – Coal mine workers across the country, holding high the red flag of high yield, have entered Red May. National key coal mines have exceeded the April production plan by 300,000 tons. In April, national key coal mines produced a total of 21.6 million tons of raw coal, equivalent to the total coal production of the entire country in the eight months following the founding of the People's Republic.

Among these key coal mines, more than 150 were enterprises formerly under the administration of the Ministry of Coal Industry before the decentralization of coal mining enterprises. The coal production of these large coal mines in April this year increased by more than 63% compared with the same period last year. In the first four months of this year, China's coal production has made another great leap forward.

In the April red flag competition for high quality, high yield, and large-scale bumper harvests, seventeen mining bureaus completed their production plans two days ahead of schedule. These included Fushun, Jingxi, Fengfeng, Huainan, Jiaozuo, Xuzhou, Pingdingshan, Jixi, Shuangyashan, Liaoyuan, Tonghua, Baotou, and Hebi mining bureaus. Among them, the Huainan Mining Bureau, characterized by its excellent ideological work, increased its coal production by 25% in April compared to March without increasing manpower or equipment. Workers have also made great achievements in achieving large-scale bumper harvests.

### 煤矿职工高举丰产红旗跨进红五月  全国重点煤矿超产三十万吨

1959-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    煤矿职工高举丰产红旗跨进红五月
    全国重点煤矿超产三十万吨
    新华社30日讯　全国二百多个重点煤矿超额三十万吨煤完成了4月份的生产计划。全国重点煤矿在4月份共生产了二千一百六十万吨原煤，等于我国建国那年全国煤矿八个月的煤产量的总和。
    这些重点煤矿中，有一百五十多个是煤矿企业下放以前属煤炭工业部管理的企业，这些大煤矿今年4月份的煤产量比去年同期增长了63％以上。我国在今年的前四个月里，煤炭生产方面又有了巨大的跃进。
    在4月份的优质高产大面积丰产的红旗竞赛中，有十七个矿务局提前两天完成了生产计划。其中有抚顺、京西、峰峰、淮南、焦作、徐州、平顶山、鸡西、双鸭山、辽源、通化、包头、鹤壁等矿务局。其中以思想工作做得好为特色的淮南矿务局，4月份在没有增人增设备的情况下，煤产量比3月份增加了25％。职工们在实现大面积丰产方面也获得了很大成绩。
