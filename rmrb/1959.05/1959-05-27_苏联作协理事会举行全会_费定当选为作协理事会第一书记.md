Title: USSR Writers' Union Board Holds Plenary Session; Fedin Elected First Secretary of the Board

Author:

Date: 1959-05-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 25th: The Writers' Union Board, newly elected at the Third Congress of Soviet Writers, held its first plenary session on the 25th to discuss organizational matters.

The meeting unanimously elected Konstantin Fedin as the First Secretary of the Writers' Union Board, and elected Abashidze, Abdumomunov, Barang, Brovka, Gonchar, Gribachev, Guseyn, Kerbabaev, Korneichuk, Leonov, Lukss, Lupan, Markov, Mezhelaitis, Musrepov, Novichenko, Nugmanov, Panferov, Salynsky, Sobolev, Surkov, Smul, Tikhonov, Tvardovsky, Tursun-Zade, and Topchyan as Secretaries of the Board.

The meeting decided that Smirnov would continue to serve as the editor-in-chief of "Literary Gazette."

### 苏联作协理事会举行全会  费定当选为作协理事会第一书记

1959-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联作协理事会举行全会
    费定当选为作协理事会第一书记
    新华社莫斯科25日电  苏联第三次作家代表大会新选出的作家协会理事会，在25日举行第一次全体会议，讨论组织问题。
    会上一致选出康士坦丁·费定为作家协会理事会第一书记，并选出阿巴希泽、阿卜杜莫穆诺夫、巴浪、布罗夫卡、冈查尔、格里巴切夫、古谢恩、凯尔巴巴耶夫、考涅楚克、列昂诺夫、卢克斯、卢潘、马尔科夫、麦热拉伊季斯、穆斯列波夫、诺维钦科、努格曼诺夫、潘菲罗夫、萨林斯基、索波列夫、苏尔科夫、斯穆尔、吉洪诺夫、特瓦尔多夫斯基、图尔逊—扎杰、托普昌为理事会书记。
    会议决定由斯米尔诺夫继续担任“文学报”总编辑。
