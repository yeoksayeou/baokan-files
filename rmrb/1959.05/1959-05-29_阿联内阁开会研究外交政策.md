Title: UAR Cabinet Meets to Study Foreign Policy
Author:
Date: 1959-05-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 28th: According to local newspapers today, the UAR Central Cabinet held a meeting on May 27th, presided over by President Nasser, to discuss the UAR's foreign policy.

After a two-and-a-half-hour meeting, Ali Sabri, Minister of State for Presidential Affairs, told reporters that the meeting reviewed "the international situation and our relations with all countries."

This was the second in a series of scheduled cabinet meetings. According to "Al-Ahram" on the 27th, quoting official sources, the purpose of these meetings is to "thoroughly adjust national policy and use all resources to implement development plans."

According to reports in "Al-Shaab", "Al-Gomhouria" and "Al-Ahram", the discussions on foreign policy at this meeting involved the following issues: 1. The UAR's relations with major powers; 2. The situation in the Middle East from recent developments; 3. Foreign policy towards various Arab countries, especially Iraq; 4. The Algerian question; 5. The UAR's relations with neutral countries; 6. The UAR's policy in Asia and Africa and its relations with Latin American countries; 7. The Israeli question.

### 阿联内阁开会研究外交政策

1959-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联内阁开会研究外交政策
    新华社开罗28日电　据这里的报纸今天报道，阿联中央内阁5月27日在纳赛尔总统主持下举行会议，讨论了阿联的外交政策。
    在历时两个半小时的会议之后，负责总统事务的国务部长阿里·萨布里对新闻记者说，这次会议回顾了“国际局势以及我们同所有国家的关系”。
    这是预定举行一系列内阁会议中的第二次会议。第一次会议是在26日举行的。据“金字塔报”27日引述官方人士的话说，这些会议的目的是要“彻底调整国家政策和运用所有的资源来实行发展计划”。
    据“人民报”、“共和国报”和“金字塔报”报道，这次会议对外交政策的讨论涉及到以下几个问题：一、阿联同各大国的关系；二、从最近的发展来看中东局势；三、对各个阿拉伯国家，特别是对伊拉克的外交政策；四、阿尔及利亚问题；五、阿联同中立国家的关系；六、阿联在亚非的政策以及同拉丁美洲国家的关系；七、以色列问题。
