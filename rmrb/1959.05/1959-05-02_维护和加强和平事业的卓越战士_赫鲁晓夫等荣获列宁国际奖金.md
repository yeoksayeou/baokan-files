Title: Outstanding Warriors in Maintaining and Strengthening the Cause of Peace - Khrushchev and Others Awarded Lenin International Peace Prize
Author:
Date: 1959-05-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 1st: TASS reports that the Committee for the International Lenin Peace Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" has decided to award the 1958 "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" Lenin International Peace Prize to the following individuals in recognition of their outstanding contributions to the struggle for maintaining and strengthening peace:

	Nikita Khrushchev—Soviet social and state activist, who most fully and vividly embodies the peace-loving policies of the Soviet socialist state and the Communist Party in his activities to maintain peace, policies which have always regarded maintaining and strengthening peace as their primary task.

	William Du Bois—American scholar, writer, and social activist, whose multifaceted educational and political activities are inextricably linked to the struggle for peace and the national liberation movements of oppressed peoples in various countries.

	Otto Buchwitz—Activist in the German Democratic Republic's workers' movement, who has spared no effort in consistently fighting for a unified Germany on a democratic and peaceful basis.

	Kostas Varnalis—Greek writer whose works are full of noble ideals and closely linked to the lofty cause of strengthening peace and the ideas of social progress.

	Ivor Montagu—British political commentator, member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, and a prominent and reputable activist in the peace movement.

The Committee for the International Lenin Peace Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" held meetings on April 25th and 27th under the chairmanship of Skobeltsyn, Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and chairman of the committee. The resolutions on awarding the prizes were signed by Skobeltsyn, Vice-Chairmen of the Committee Guo Moruo and Aragon, and the members of the committee.

### 维护和加强和平事业的卓越战士  赫鲁晓夫等荣获列宁国际奖金

1959-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    维护和加强和平事业的卓越战士
    赫鲁晓夫等荣获列宁国际奖金
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科1日讯：“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金委员会决定将1958年“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金授予以下人士，以表彰他们在维护和加强和平斗争中的卓越功绩：
    尼基塔·赫鲁晓夫——苏联的社会活动家和国家活动家，他在自己维护和平的活动中最充分和最鲜明地体现了苏维埃社会主义国家和共产党的爱好和平的政策，它们始终把维护和加强和平当作自己首要的任务。
    威廉·杜波依斯——美国学者、作家、社会活动家，他的多方面的教育和政治活动同争取和平的斗争，同各国受压迫人民的民族解放运动有着不可分割的联系。
    奥托·布赫威茨——德意志民主共和国工人运动活动家，他不遗余力地和始终如一地为在民主和平的基础上建立统一的德国而斗争。
    柯斯塔·瓦尔纳利斯——希腊作家，他的作品充满着高尚的理想，并且同加强和平的崇高事业、同社会进步的思想有着紧密的联系。
    艾佛·蒙塔古——英国政论家，世界和平理事会常务委员会委员，保卫和平运动的著名的和有声望的活动家。
    “加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金委员会于4月25日和27日在委员会主席、苏联科学院院士斯科贝尔琴的主持下举行了会议。在关于授奖的决议上签字的有斯科贝尔琴，委员会副主席郭沫若和阿拉贡以及委员会的委员们。
