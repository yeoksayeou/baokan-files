Title: Iraqi Supreme Power Committee Chairman Congratulates Chairman Liu Shaoqi
Author:
Date: 1959-05-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The message of congratulations from Iraqi Republic Supreme Power Committee Chairman Najib al-Rubai to Chairman Liu Shaoqi is as follows:

To His Excellency, Mr. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of Your Excellency's assumption of the esteemed position of Chairman of the People's Republic of China, I am pleased to extend to Your Excellency my sincere congratulations and best wishes, wishing Your Excellency health and happiness, and wishing the great Chinese people prosperity and happiness.

Najib al-Rubai
Chairman of the Supreme Power Committee
Baghdad, May 7, 1959

### 伊最高权力委员会主席  电贺刘少奇主席

1959-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　伊最高权力委员会主席
    电贺刘少奇主席
    新华社8日讯　伊拉克共和国最高权力委员会主席纳吉布·鲁巴伊给刘少奇主席的贺电，文如下：中华人民共和国主席刘少奇先生阁下：
    在阁下就任中华人民共和国主席的崇高职位的时候，我很愉快地向阁下表示诚挚的祝贺和最良好的愿望，祝阁下健康和幸福，并祝伟大的中国人民昌盛和幸福。
                        最高权力委员会主席　纳吉布·鲁巴伊
                                        1959年5月7日巴格达
