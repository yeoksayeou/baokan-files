Title: Rainbow
Author:
Date: 1959-05-15
Page: 6

    A rainbow rises in the east,
    This is the direction Chairman Mao points,
    Following this broad road to advance,
    The herdsmen are no longer bullied;
    The commune's cattle and sheep are thriving in herds,
    Life is happy and sweet.
      (Qinghai Tibetan folk song)

### 彩虹

1959-05-15
第6版()
专栏：

    彩虹东方升起一道彩虹，这是毛主席指引的方向，沿着这条大路前进，牧民不再受欺凌；公社肥壮的牛羊成群，生活美满又香甜。
      （青海藏族歌谣）
