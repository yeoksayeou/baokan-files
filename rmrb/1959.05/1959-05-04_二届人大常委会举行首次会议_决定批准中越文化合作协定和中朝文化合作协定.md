Title: Second NPC Standing Committee Holds First Meeting; Decides to Approve Sino-Vietnamese and Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreements
Author:
Date: 1959-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - The Second National People's Congress Standing Committee held its first meeting today in Ziguang Pavilion, Zhongnanhai.

Chairman Zhu De presided over the meeting.

The meeting decided to approve the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and decided to approve the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. With this, China now has cultural cooperation agreements with all socialist countries.

The meeting appointed Zhang Su, Yu Xinqing, Sun Qimeng, Lian Guan, Hu Yuzhi, and Chen Cisheng as Deputy Secretaries-General of the Standing Committee.

### 二届人大常委会举行首次会议  决定批准中越文化合作协定和中朝文化合作协定

1959-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    二届人大常委会举行首次会议
    决定批准中越文化合作协定和中朝文化合作协定
    新华社3日讯　第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天在中南海紫光阁举行第一次会议。
    朱德委员长主持这次会议。
    会议决定批准中华人民共和国政府和越南民主共和国政府文化合作协定，决定批准中华人民共和国政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府文化合作协定。至此，中国和所有的社会主义国家都已经有了文化合作协定。
    会议任命张苏、余心清、孙起孟、连贯、胡愈之、陈此生为常务委员会的副秘书长。
