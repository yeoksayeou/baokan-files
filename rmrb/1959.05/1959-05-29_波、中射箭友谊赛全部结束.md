Title: Sino-Polish Archery Friendship Competition Concludes
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th. The Sino-Polish archery friendship competition concluded this morning in Beijing with the final two distances—50 and 30 meters—of the target archery event. With this, the entire competition has come to an end. Over the past two days, in the men's and women's competitions at each of the two distances, Polish men's archery runner-up, Męczyński, and women's archery runner-up, Kaniucka, achieved total scores of 970 rings and 931 rings respectively (out of a possible 1440 rings), securing first place in the men's and women's groups. Today, Chinese young male and female archers broke five national archery championship records from 1959 in the competition.

    Twenty-year-old Męczyński performed exceptionally well today. In the 30-meter event, he hit 313 rings out of 36 arrows (a possible 360 rings), averaging nearly 9 rings per arrow. On one occasion, he consecutively hit three arrows into the 10-ring within two minutes. Today, his score in the 50-meter target archery event was 236 rings.

    The total scores of the other three archers in the men's group over the four distances in the past two days are: Nowakowski (Poland) 783 rings; Xu Kaicai (China) 666 rings; and Zhang Langshui (China) 526 rings. Xu Kaicai's total score today and his 30-meter target archery score of 269 rings broke the previous national archery championship records of 624 rings and 268 rings, respectively, which were set by Zhang Langshui and People’s Liberation Army archer Wu Hanmin.

    The second place in the women's group was taken by Polish archery champion Żygowska, with a score of 898 rings. Although she surpassed Polish runner-up Kaniucka in the final 30-meter event today (Żygowska scored 295 rings, while Kaniucka scored 284 rings), she had fallen behind significantly in the previous three distances, particularly in the 50-meter event (Żygowska scored 171 rings in the 50-meter event, while Kaniucka scored 205 rings), so Kaniucka ultimately emerged victorious.

    Chinese women's archery champion Yang Xingjiao broke three national archery championship records from 1959 today. These three records are: a total score of 692 rings in the four distances of target archery (original record 568 rings), 161 rings in the 50-meter event (original record 138 rings), and 265 rings in the 30-meter event (original record 235 rings). Chinese women's archery runner-up Hu Qingmei's total score of 607 rings and her 50-meter score of 142 rings also broke the national archery championship records from 1959.

    The Polish men's and women's archery teams left Beijing this afternoon for a visit to Hohhot.

### 波、中射箭友谊赛全部结束

1959-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    波、中射箭友谊赛全部结束
    新华社28日讯　波、中射箭友谊赛今天上午在北京举行最后两个射程——五十和三十公尺距离的射准比赛，至此，比赛已全部结束。两天来，在男、女各两个射程的比赛中，波兰男子射箭亚军蒙契安斯基和女子射箭亚军坎尼茨卡，分别以九百七十环和九百三十一环的总成绩（满环一千四百四十环），获得了男子和女子组的第一名。中国青年男、女射手今天在比赛中又打破了五项1959年全国射箭锦标赛中的最高纪录。
    二十岁的蒙契安斯基今天的成绩很出色，在三十公尺的比赛中，他全部三十六箭共命中了三百一十三环（满环三百六十环），平均每一箭的命中率近九环。有一次他在两分钟内连中率近九环。有一次他在两分钟内连发三箭，都命中十环。今天他的五十公尺射准的成绩是二百三十六环。
    男子组其他三名射手两天来四个射程的总成绩分别是：波兰诺瓦科夫斯基七百八十三环；中国徐开财六百六十六环；中国张浪水五百二十六环。徐开财今天的总成绩和三十公尺射准二百六十九环的成绩，分别打破了张浪水和解放军射手吴汉民在全国射箭锦标赛中创造的六百二十四环和二百六十八环的最高纪录。
    女子组的第二名是波兰射箭冠军祖戈夫斯卡，她的成绩是八百九十八环。她今天虽然在最后一项三十公尺比赛中，成绩超过了波兰亚军坎尼茨卡（祖戈夫斯卡是二百九十五环，坎尼茨卡是二百八十四环），但由于她在前三个射程，特别是在五十公尺的比赛中，成绩落后了很多（祖戈夫斯卡五十公尺一百七十一环，坎尼茨卡是二百零五环），所以最后仍然是坎尼茨卡获胜。
    中国女子射箭冠军杨型姣，今天共打破了三项1959年全国射箭锦标赛中的最高纪录。这三项是：四个射程射准的总成绩六百九十二环（原纪录五百六十八环）、五十公尺一百六十一环（原纪录一百三十八环）、三十公尺二百六十五环（原纪录二百三十五环）。中国女子射箭亚军胡青梅今天的总成绩六百零七环和五十公尺一百四十二环的成绩，也都打破了1959年全国射箭锦标赛中的最高纪录。
    波兰男、女射箭队今天下午已经离开北京，去呼和浩特访问。
