Title: Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Wang Jiaxiang Meet with Matern
Author:
Date: 1959-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, and Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, met with Matern, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, this morning and had a cordial conversation. Wang Guoquan, the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, was also present at the meeting.

At the meeting, Matern, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, presented Chairman Mao Zedong with the classic works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, and the works of President Wilhelm Pieck and Premier Grotewohl.

### 邓小平彭真王稼祥会见马特恩

1959-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    邓小平彭真王稼祥会见马特恩
    新华社11日讯　中共中央总书记邓小平，政治局委员、中央书记处书记彭真和中央书记处书记王稼祥，今天上午会见了德国统一社会党中央政治局委员马特恩，并且进行了亲切的谈话。会见时，德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔也在座。
    会见的时候，马特恩代表德国统一社会党中央委员会向毛泽东主席赠送马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的经典著作和威廉·皮克总统、格罗提渥总理的著作。
