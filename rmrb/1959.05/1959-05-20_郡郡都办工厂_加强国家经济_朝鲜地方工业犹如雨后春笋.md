Title: Counties All Run Factories to Strengthen the National Economy: Local Industries in North Korea Spring Up Like Bamboo Shoots After Rain
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th - According to the Korean Central News Agency, since the June 1958 Plenary Meeting of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea made a resolution on improving and strengthening the food processing industry and the production of daily necessities, more than 1,000 local industrial factories have been built in various parts of North Korea.

These factories include 176 food factories, 385 daily necessities factories, 121 farm tool factories, 235 building materials factories, and some other factories.

The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea last June required each county to establish more than one local factory. However, currently, an average of more than five local factories have been established in each county.

At present, North Korea's local industries have a total of more than 2,000 medium and small-sized enterprises. This year, the total output value of North Korea's local industries will reach 2.6 times that of last year and will account for 27.5% of North Korea's total industrial output value.

The development of local industries in North Korea plays a significant role in strengthening North Korea's national economy and improving the people's living standards. (Attached photo)

### 郡郡都办工厂　加强国家经济  朝鲜地方工业犹如雨后春笋

1959-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    郡郡都办工厂　加强国家经济
    朝鲜地方工业犹如雨后春笋
    新华社平壤19日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：在1958年6月朝鲜劳动党中央委员会全体会议作出关于改善和加强食品加工工业和日用品的生产的决议以后这一期间，朝鲜北方各地已经建成了一千多个地方工业工厂。
    这些工厂包括一百七十六个食品工厂，三百八十五个日用品工厂，一百二十一个农具工厂，二百三十五个建筑材料工厂和其他一些工厂。
    朝鲜劳动党去年6月中央全会曾经要求每个郡要兴办一个以上的地方工厂。但是，目前每个郡中平均已经建立了五个以上的地方工厂。
    目前，朝鲜地方工业总共已经拥有两千以上的中、小型企业，今年，朝鲜地方工业的总产值将要达到去年的二点六倍，并且将占朝鲜工业总产值的27.5％。
    朝鲜地方工业的发展在加强朝鲜国家经济和提高人民生活的水平方面起着很大的作用。（附图片）
