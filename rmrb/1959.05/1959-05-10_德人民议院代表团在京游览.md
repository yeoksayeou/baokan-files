Title: GDR People's Chamber Delegation Tours Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 9th May: The First Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Mr. Materne, and members of the People's Chamber delegation visited the Summer Palace this afternoon.

Accompanying the distinguished guests were Li Jishen and Lin Feng, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhang Su, a member of the Standing Committee, and Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General.

In the Summer Palace, where flowers were in full bloom and the shade of the trees was lush, the distinguished guests strolled along the Long Corridor, admired the pavilions, terraces, towers and lodges in the garden, and then took a painted boat to tour Kunming Lake, enjoying the scenery of the lake and mountains. Tourists enjoying their weekend boating frequently waved to the friendly envoys of the German people to show their greetings.

This morning, the distinguished guests also visited the Thirteen Tombs Reservoir, the Changling Tomb, the Great Wall, and the Palace Museum.

### 德人民议院代表团在京游览

1959-05-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　德人民议院代表团在京游览
    新华社9日讯　德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团第一副主席马特恩和人民议院代表团团员今天下午游览了颐和园。
    陪同贵宾们游览的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、林枫，常务委员张苏，副秘书长连贯。
    在百花盛开、绿荫正浓的颐和园内，贵宾们漫步长廊，观赏了园内亭、台、阁、榭，以后乘画舫游览昆明湖，观看湖光山色。泛舟欢度周末的游人，频频向德国人民的友谊使者招手致意。
    今天上午，贵宾们还分别参观游览了十三陵水库、长陵、长城、故宫等。
