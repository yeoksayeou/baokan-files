Title: Arm the Working People with Marxist-Leninist Theory: New Edition of "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" Forthcoming
Author:
Date: 1959-05-13
Page: 4

    Arm the Working People with Marxist-Leninist Theory
    New Edition of "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" Forthcoming
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 12th: The first batch of 50,000 copies of the new edition of the textbook "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" has been bound and will soon be available to readers.
    The publication of the new edition of the textbook "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" is of great political significance. The broad masses of working people will learn from this book about the path to revolutionary victory of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the laws of building socialism and communism, and further arm themselves with Marxist-Leninist theory.
    This new edition of the textbook is divided into eighteen chapters. It begins with the start of the Russian workers' movement in 1883 and the spread of Marxism in Russia, and continues to the period when the Soviet Union entered the stage of comprehensively building a communist society, the Twenty-First Party Congress.
    This textbook was compiled jointly by many historians, economists, and philosophers under the leadership of P. N. Ponomarev, Corresponding Member of the USSR Academy of Sciences.

### 用马克思列宁主义理论武装劳动人民  新版“苏共党史”即将问世

1959-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    用马克思列宁主义理论武装劳动人民
    新版“苏共党史”即将问世
    新华社莫斯科12日电  新版“苏联共产党党史”教科书第一批五万册已经装订完毕，不久即将与读者见面。
    新版“苏联共产党党史”教科书的出版具有重大的政治意义。广大的劳动人民将从这本书中学习到苏联共产党革命胜利的道路和建设社会主义与共产主义的规律的知识，并进一步用马克思列宁主义理论来武装自己。
    这本新版教科书共分十八章。从1883年俄国工人运动的开始和马克思主义在俄国的传播起一直写到苏联进入全面展开共产主义社会建设时期，第二十一次党代表大会。
    这本教科书是在苏联科学院通讯院士波诺马烈夫的领导下，由许多历史学家、经济学家和哲学家共同编写的。
