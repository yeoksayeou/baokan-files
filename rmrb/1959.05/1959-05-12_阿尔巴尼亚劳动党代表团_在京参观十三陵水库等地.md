Title: Albanian Labor Party Delegation Visits the Ming Tombs Reservoir and Other Places in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The Albanian Labor Party delegation currently visiting China today toured the Ming Tombs Reservoir and visited the Changling Tomb and the Badaling Great Wall.
    After attending the “May Day” Labor Day celebrations in the capital, the Albanian Labor Party delegation visited Henan, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other places, and returned to Beijing yesterday by special plane.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团  在京参观十三陵水库等地

1959-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团
    在京参观十三陵水库等地
    新华社11日讯　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党访华代表团今天参观了十三陵水库、游览了长陵和八达岭。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党访华代表团在参加首都“五一”劳动节观礼后，曾去河南、广州、武汉等地进行参观访问，在昨天乘专机回到北京。
