Title: Romanian National Men's and Women's Track and Field Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th -- The Romanian National Men's and Women's Track and Field Team, consisting of twenty-five members including the world record holder for women's high jump, Iolanda Balaș, arrived in Beijing by plane today, led by the member of the Presidium of the Romanian Sports Commission, Ion Țiriac. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Huang Zhong, Vice Minister of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of China, and more than twenty male and female track and field athletes.

Balaș, who is one meter and eighty-four centimeters tall, hugged and kissed Zheng Fengrong, the former world record creator of women's high jump, who came to the airport to welcome her, as soon as she got off the plane today. Zheng Fengrong presented Balaș with a bouquet of flowers, and Balaș also gave Zheng Fengrong gifts brought from her hometown.

The Romanian National Men's and Women's Track and Field Team is visiting China at the invitation, and is scheduled to hold a formal competition with the Chinese Men's and Women's Track and Field Team in Beijing on May 14th.

### 罗国家男女田径队到京

1959-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗国家男女田径队到京
    新华社8日讯　包括女子跳高世界纪录保持者巴拉斯在内的罗马尼亚国家男、女田径队一行二十五人，今天由罗马尼亚体育运动委员会主席团委员扬·图尔潘率领，乘飞机到达北京。他们在飞机场受到我国国家体委副主任黄中和二十多名男、女田径运动员的热烈欢迎。
    身高一公尺八十四公分的巴拉斯，今天一下飞机，就和前往飞机场欢迎她的前世界女子跳高纪录创造者郑凤荣拥抱、亲吻。郑凤荣向巴拉斯献了一束鲜花，巴拉斯也送给郑凤荣由她家乡带来的礼物。
  　罗马尼亚国家男、女田径队这次应邀来中国访问，预定5月14日首先在北京同中国男、女田径队举行正式比赛。
