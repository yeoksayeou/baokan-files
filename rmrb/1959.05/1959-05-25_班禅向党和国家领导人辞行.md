Title: Panchen bids farewell to Party and State leaders
Author:
Date: 1959-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th. Panchen Erdeni bid farewell to Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Chairman Zhu De, Vice Premier Deng Xiaoping, and Vice Chairman Li Weihan this afternoon. Chairman Mao and others held cordial talks with Panchen Erdeni. Also present were Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Zhang Jingwu, Central Representative in Tibet, and Tan Guansan, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region. ### 班禅向党和国家领导人辞行

1959-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅向党和国家领导人辞行
    新华社24日讯　班禅额尔德尼今天下午向毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席、朱德委员长、邓小平副总理和李维汉副委员长辞行。毛主席等同班禅额尔德尼进行了亲切的交谈。在座的有民族事务委员会副主任汪锋，中央驻西藏代表张经武，西藏军区政治委员谭冠三。（附图片）
