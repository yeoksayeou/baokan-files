Title: Britain Openly Defends Laotian Government, Supports Laotian Authorities' Opposition to Resumption of Activities by International Commission, Even Claims Our Government's Accusations are "Groundless"
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, May 26th. A British Foreign Office spokesman today openly defended the Laotian Royal Government's actions in violating the Geneva Accords, saying, "We believe that the Laotian government has consistently acted in accordance with the Geneva Accords, and the integration of Laotian troops does not violate the agreement."

Referring to a letter sent by the Government of the People's Republic of China protesting the Laotian Royal Government's serious violation of the Geneva Accords, the spokesman said, "The Foreign Office is studying this letter." He added that the accusations made by the Government of the People's Republic of China are "groundless."

The spokesman also said that the Laotian International Commission is in a "moribund" state, and although, in theory, it could be reorganized, the Laotian government has expressed its opposition to reorganizing (the Laotian International Commission).

The spokesman said, "The British government's view is that the wishes of the Laotian government on this issue should be respected."

### 英国公开为老挝政府辩护  支持老挝当局反对国际委员会恢复活动  竟然说我国政府提出的指责“没有根据”

1959-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    英国公开为老挝政府辩护
    支持老挝当局反对国际委员会恢复活动
    竟然说我国政府提出的指责“没有根据”
    新华社伦敦26日电  英国外交部发言人今天公开地为老挝王国政府破坏日内瓦协议的行为辩护，他说，“我们认为，老挝政府一贯按照日内瓦协议行事，合并寮国军队的行为，并不违反协议。”
    这位发言人在谈到中华人民共和国政府送交的一封抗议老挝王国政府严重违反日内瓦协议的信件时说，“外交部正在研究这封信”。他又说，中华人民共和国政府提出的指责是“没有根据的”。
    这个发言人还说，老挝国际委员会已处于“奄奄一息”的状态，虽然从理论上说来，它可以重新组织起来，但是，老挝政府已表示它反对重新组织（老挝国际委员会）。
    这个发言人说，“英国政府的看法是，老挝政府在这个问题上的愿望应当得到尊重。”
