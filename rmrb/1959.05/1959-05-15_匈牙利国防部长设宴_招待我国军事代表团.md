Title: Hungarian Defense Minister Hosts Banquet for Chinese Military Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, May 13th. Hungarian Defense Minister Révesz hosted a grand banquet at the Budapest Officers' Club on the evening of May 13th to welcome the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation led by Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense.

Among those present at the banquet were Marosán, member of the Political Bureau of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Secretary of the Central Secretariat; Biszku, member of the Central Political Bureau and Minister of the Interior; Péter, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Molnár, Chairman of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association; Usta, First Deputy Minister of Defense; and senior generals of the Hungarian People's Army.

Minister Révesz and Marshal Peng Dehuai delivered speeches at the banquet.

The hosts and guests had cordial conversations at the banquet and repeatedly toasted to the friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Hungary.

### 匈牙利国防部长设宴  招待我国军事代表团

1959-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利国防部长设宴
    招待我国军事代表团
    据新华社布达佩斯13日电　匈牙利国防部长雷维斯13日晚在布达佩斯军官俱乐部举行盛大宴会，招待以国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团。
    出席宴会的有匈牙利社会主义工人党政治局委员、中央书记处书记马罗山，中央政治局委员、内务部长比斯库，外交部第一副部长彼得，匈中友好协会主席莫尔纳尔，国防部第一副部长乌斯塔以及匈牙利人民军的高级将领等。
    雷维斯部长和彭德怀元帅先后在宴会上讲了话。
    在宴会上宾主双方进行了亲切的交谈，并不断地为中匈两国人民和军队之间的友谊干杯。
