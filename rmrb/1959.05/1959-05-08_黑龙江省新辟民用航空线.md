Title: Heilongjiang Province Opens New Civil Aviation Route
Author:
Date: 1959-05-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 7th — A newly opened civil aviation route in Heilongjiang Province, the Yilan to Jiamusi route, successfully completed its trial flight on May 4th. It will soon be officially opened. In addition to transporting passengers and goods, the aircraft on this route can also carry out work such as pest control, fertilization, and sowing in farmland.

### 黑龙江省新辟民用航空线

1959-05-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　黑龙江省新辟民用航空线
    新华社7日讯　黑龙江省一条新开辟的民用航空线  依兰到佳木斯的航线，在5月4日试航成功。不久以后，就将正式开航。这条航线上的飞机，除了运载旅客、货物以外，还可以进行农田灭虫、施肥和播种等工作。
