Title: Swiss Labor Party "Seventh Congress" Concludes; Edgar Woog Re-elected as General Secretary
Author:
Date: 1959-05-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 20th - TASS: The Swiss Labor Party held its Seventh Congress in Geneva from May 16th to 18th.

According to the "Voice of Labor" newspaper, the participants of the congress listened to the report "On the Political Situation and the Tasks of the Party" made by Edgar Woog, the General Secretary of the Labor Party, discussed and unanimously adopted the new party program of the Labor Party, and also discussed many other issues related to the party's political activities.

The delegates to the congress adopted an appeal to the Geneva Foreign Ministers' Conference, hoping that the conference would achieve success in resolving major issues in the spirit of peaceful coexistence.

The congress re-elected Edgar Woog as General Secretary of the Labor Party.

The congress read out congratulatory messages to the congress from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Communist Party of China, and other fraternal communist parties, which won warm applause from the delegates. Communist parties from Italy, France, Austria, Belgium, the United Kingdom, and Sweden sent representatives to attend the congress.

### 瑞士劳动党“七大”闭幕  埃德加·沃克再次当选为总书记

1959-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞士劳动党“七大”闭幕
    埃德加·沃克再次当选为总书记
    新华社20日讯　塔斯社日内瓦讯：瑞士劳动党5月16日至18日在日内瓦举行了第七次代表大会。
    据“工人之声报”报道，大会参加者听取了劳动党总书记埃德加·沃克所作的“关于政治局势和党的任务”的报告，讨论并且一致通过了劳动党新党纲，还讨论了与党的政治活动有关的其他许多问题。
    大会代表通过了致日内瓦外长会议的呼吁书，希望会议根据和平共处的精神在解决主要问题方面取得成就。
    代表大会再次选举埃德加·沃克为劳动党总书记。
    代表大会宣读了苏联共产党、中国共产党和其他兄弟共产党中央委员会给大会的贺电，这些贺电赢得了代表们的热烈掌声。意大利、法国、奥地利、比利时、英国和瑞典等国的共产党派代表参加了大会。
