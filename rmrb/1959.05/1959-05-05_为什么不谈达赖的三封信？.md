Title: Why Not Talk About the Dalai Lama's Three Letters?
Author: Guo Zhaolin
Date: 1959-05-05
Page: 2

Why Not Talk About the Dalai Lama's Three Letters?

In his speech on the Tibetan situation in the People's House on April 27, Indian Prime Minister Nehru said that the Dalai Lama went to India of his own volition. But he avoided mentioning the fact that he himself had acknowledged the three letters from the Dalai Lama to General Tan Guansan. The problem is obvious: Prime Minister Nehru knew that if he recounted those three letters, there would be nothing to make a fuss about.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Yangcun Machinery Factory, Wuqing County, Guo Zhaolin

### 为什么不谈达赖的三封信？
郭兆林
1959-05-05
第2版()
专栏：

    为什么不谈达赖的三封信？
    印度总理尼赫鲁先生4月27日在人民院发表关于西藏局势的讲话中说，达赖喇嘛去印度是出于自己的意愿。但他却避而不谈他自己已经承认了的达赖喇嘛给谭冠三将军的三封信的事实。问题很明显，尼赫鲁总理知道，如果述说那三封信，就没有什么文章可作了。
                                武清县杨村机械厂　郭兆林
