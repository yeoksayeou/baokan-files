Title: Celebrating "May Day" Night on the Tiananmen Rostrum, Chinese Leaders Meet with Foreign Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-05-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st May: Our correspondent reports: Party and state leaders Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Dong Biwu, Lin Biao, Deng Xiaoping, and others, joyfully spent the "May Day" night on the Tiananmen Rostrum tonight together with foreign guests from more than fifty countries.

At the beginning of this night of revelry, Party and state leaders, together with honored guests from the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation, the People's Chamber Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, the Polish United Workers' Party Delegation, the Albanian Labor Party Delegation, the Indonesian Communist Party Delegation, the Italian Communist Party Delegation, and the Australian Communist Party Delegation, watched the mass celebration on Tiananmen Square.

Subsequently, Party and state leaders held cordial meetings with the heads of delegations from various countries of the five continents, prominent figures from various countries, and ambassadors from various countries to China. They warmly welcomed the guests to celebrate the common festival of the working people of the world together with the Chinese people, and wished the friendship between the people of all countries to continue to develop. During the meeting, the honored guests of the Iraqi Trade Union Delegation conveyed Prime Minister Qasim's greetings to our country's leaders to Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou Enlai. Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou Enlai asked the honored guests to convey their greetings to Prime Minister Qasim, wishing Prime Minister Qasim good health and the Iraqi people happiness. The honored guests of the Algerian Military Delegation and Trade Union Delegation paid tribute to Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou Enlai in the name of the fighting Algerian people, and thanked the people of China for their support of the Algerian people. Omar Oussedik, head of the Algerian Military Delegation, said: "We will continue to fight until the final victory." Chairman Liu Shaoqi said: "Your fight is going well. You will surely achieve final victory!" During the meeting, the famous American female writer Anna Louise Strong presented Premier Zhou Enlai with a book she had recently written about the Chinese People's Commune.

After the meeting, Party and state leaders, together with guests from various countries, enjoyed wonderful song and dance and acrobatics performances on both sides of the Tiananmen Rostrum.

### 在天安门城楼上欢度“五一”之夜  我国领导人会见各国外宾

1959-05-02
第1版()
专栏：

    在天安门城楼上欢度“五一”之夜
    我国领导人会见各国外宾
    据新华社1日讯　本社记者报道：党和国家领导人刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、董必武、林彪、邓小平等，今晚同来自五十多个国家的外宾一起，在天安门城楼上欢乐地度过了“五一”之夜。
    在这狂欢之夜开始的时候，党和国家的领导人，同匈牙利党政代表团、德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团、波兰统一工人党代表团、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团、印度尼西亚共产党代表团、意大利共产党代表团、澳大利亚共产党代表团的贵宾们一起，观看了天安门广场上的群众大联欢。
    随后，党和国家的领导人同来自世界五大洲的各国代表团的负责人和各国著名人士以及各国驻华使节，亲切地会见。他们热烈欢迎各国来宾们同中国人民一起欢度全世界劳动人民共同的节日，祝各国人民之间的友谊不断发展。在会见的时候，伊拉克工会代表团的贵宾们向刘少奇主席、朱德委员长和周恩来总理转达了卡塞姆总理对我国领导人的问候，刘少奇主席、朱德委员长和周恩来总理都请贵宾们把他们的问候带给卡塞姆总理，祝卡塞姆总理身体健康、祝伊拉克人民幸福。阿尔及利亚军事代表团和工会代表团的贵宾们，以战斗的阿尔及利亚人民的名义向刘少奇主席、朱德委员长和周恩来总理致敬，感谢中国人民对阿尔及利亚人民的支持。阿尔及利亚军事代表团团长奥马尔·乌西迪克说：“我们要继续战斗下去，直到最后胜利为止。”刘少奇主席说：“你们的战斗进行得很好。你们一定能够获得最后胜利！”在会见的时候，美国著名女作家安娜·路易·斯特朗把她最近写成的一部中国人民公社的书送给了周恩来总理。
    会见以后，党和国家的领导人同各国来宾一起，在天安门城楼两侧欣赏了精彩的歌舞和杂技表演。
