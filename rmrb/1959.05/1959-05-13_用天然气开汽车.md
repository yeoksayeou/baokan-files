Title: Cars Powered by Natural Gas
Author: Zhang Jixuan
Date: 1959-05-13
Page: 2

Brief News

    Cars Powered by Natural Gas
    The Pudong Public Transportation Company of Shanghai is making extensive use of waste gas from nearby oil refineries. On the Tangqiao-Chuansha line, they have opened a long-distance bus route that uses natural gas instead of gasoline. Tests have proven that every cubic meter of natural gas can travel five kilometers. Because all the buses on the line use natural gas, the country can save more than 9,000 liters of gasoline each month.
      (Zhang Jixuan)

### 用天然气开汽车
张记轩
1959-05-13
第2版()
专栏：简讯

    用天然气开汽车
    上海市浦东公共交通公司大量利用附近炼油厂的废气，在塘（桥）川（沙）线上，开辟了一条使用天然气代替汽油行驶汽车的长途汽车路线。试验证明，每立方公尺天然气可以行驶五公里。由于全线行驶的汽车都使用天然气，每月可为国家节约汽油九千多公升。
      （张记轩）
