Title: Rukman Visits Anshan

Author:
Date: 1959-05-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 8th. After visiting Shenyang for two days, Rukman, First Deputy General Secretary of the Communist Party of Indonesia, left here by train this morning to visit Anshan and Lüda.

　　During his stay in Shenyang, Rukman visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall, Shenyang Cable Factory, Heavy Machinery Factory, and other places. He also visited the Fushun Open-pit Mine, the No. 1 Petroleum Factory, and the workers' nursing home in Fushun, where he was warmly welcomed by the workers.

### 鲁克曼去鞍山大访问

1959-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　鲁克曼去鞍山大访问
    新华社沈阳8日电　印度尼西亚共产党第一副总书记鲁克曼在沈阳访问两天后，今天早晨离开这里乘火车去鞍山、旅大参观访问。
    鲁克曼在沈阳期间，参观访问了东北工业陈列馆、沈阳电缆厂、重型机器厂等处，还曾去抚顺参观了抚顺露天矿、石油一厂和工人养老院，受到了工人们的热烈欢迎。
