Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1959-05-20
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 19th: The eighty-ninth meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw this afternoon. The meeting lasted one hour and forty-five minutes.

　　After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would continue on Friday, June 19th, at 2:00 p.m.

### 中美大使会谈继续举行

1959-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使会谈继续举行
    新华社华沙19日电　中美两国大使级会谈第八十九次会议今天下午在华沙举行。本次会议历时一小时四十五分。
    会后双方宣布，下次会议将在6月19日（星期五）下午二时继续举行。
