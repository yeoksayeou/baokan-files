Title: New Swiss Ambassador Presents Credentials to Chairman Liu
Author:
Date: 1959-05-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 20th: The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Swiss Confederation to China, Mr. René Naville, accompanied by diplomatic officials from the embassy, presented his credentials to Chairman Liu Shaoqi this afternoon.

Present at the ceremony were: Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department; and Song Zhiguang, Deputy Director of the Department of West European Affairs.

Prior to this, on the morning of May 19th, Ambassador Naville met with Foreign Minister Chen Yi to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials. (Attached picture)

The first group of Tibetan students returning to Tibet to participate in democratic reform and construction work were warmly welcomed by the responsible persons of the State Nationalities Affairs Commission and their fellow students when they left Beijing.
                 Xinhua News Agency Reporter Yu Chengjian

### 瑞士新任大使向刘主席递国书

1959-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞士新任大使向刘主席递国书
    新华社20日讯　瑞士联邦新任驻华特命全权大使雷纳·纳维义今天下午偕大使馆外交官员向刘少奇主席递交国书。
    在场的有：外交部副部长罗贵波，礼宾司司长俞沛文，西欧司副司长宋之光。
    在这以前，纳维义大使曾在19日上午会见我外交部长陈毅，商谈递交国书事宜。（附图片）
    第一批返回西藏参加民主改革和建设工作的藏族学生离开北京时，受到民委负责人和同学们的热烈欢送。
                 新华社记者　于澄建摄
