Title: Polish Party Delegation Heads to Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-05-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 10th: The Polish United Workers' Party delegation, led by Jerzy Morawski, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee, left for Hangzhou by special plane this morning after a two-day visit to Guangzhou.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Chen Yu, member of the CPC Central Committee, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Guangdong Provincial Committee, and Governor of Guangdong Province; Wang De, First Secretary of the CPC Guangzhou Municipal Committee; Zhu Guang, Secretary of the Secretariat and Mayor of the Municipal Committee; and Jiao Linyi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Municipal Committee, and others. During their stay in Guangzhou, the delegation visited Huangpu Port, toured the former site of the Huangpu Military Academy, the Guangzhou Daxin Ivory Craft Factory, and the 1959 Spring China Export Commodities Fair which is currently being held. The distinguished guests also held discussions with Chen Yu, Wang De, Zhu Guang, Jiao Linyi, and other responsible comrades of the CPC Guangdong Provincial Committee and the CPC Guangzhou Municipal Committee, and had cordial conversations.

### 波兰党代表团去杭州

1959-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰党代表团去杭州
    新华社广州10日电　以波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、书记处书记耶日·莫拉夫斯基为首的波兰统一工人党代表团，在广州访问两天后，于今天上午乘专机去杭州。
    前往机场欢送的有：中共中央委员、中共广东省委书记处书记、广东省长陈郁，中共广州市委第一书记王德，市委书记处书记、市长朱光，市委书记处书记焦林义等人。代表团在广州期间，访问了黄埔港，参观了黄埔军官学校旧址、广州大新象牙工艺厂以及正在举行的1959年春季中国出口商品交易会。贵宾们还和中共广东省委、中共广州市委负责人陈郁、王德、朱光、焦林义等举行座谈会，作了亲切的交谈。
