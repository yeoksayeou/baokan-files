　　Tibetan Proverbs
　　To a person with awareness,
　　Even a single maxim can make him improve.
　　To a newly sprouted bamboo shoot,
　　Even light rain can help it become a forest.

### 藏族谚语

1959-05-07
第8版()
专栏：

　　藏族谚语
　　有觉悟的人，
　　一句格言也能使他长进。
　　刚萌芽的笋，
　　细雨小水也能助它成林。
