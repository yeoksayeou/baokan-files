Title: Discussion on the Overall Mechanization and Automation of Industrial Construction; CPSU Central Committee to Hold Plenary Session Next Month
Author:
Date: 1959-05-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th (TASS): Soviet newspapers published a communiqué from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union regarding the decision to hold a plenary session of the Central Committee on June 24th of this year.

The communiqué stated that the plenary session of the CPSU Central Committee will discuss measures to implement the resolutions of the 21st Congress of the CPSU regarding the adoption of overall mechanization and automation in industry and construction.

The plenary session will also hear a report from the State Chemistry Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers on the implementation of the resolution of the CPSU Central Committee of May 7th of last year regarding the acceleration of the development of the chemical industry.

### 讨论工业建筑业全盘机械化自动化问题  苏共中央下月举行全会

1959-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    讨论工业建筑业全盘机械化自动化问题
    苏共中央下月举行全会
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科10日讯：苏联各报发表了苏联共产党中央委员会关于决定在今年6月24日举行中央委员会全体会议的公报。
  公报说，苏共中央这次全体会议将讨论在完成苏共第二十一次代表大会关于在工业和建筑业中采用全盘机械化和自动化的决议方面的措施问题。
    全体会议还将听取苏联部长会议国家化学委员会对苏共中央全会去年5月7日关于加速发展化学工业的决议的执行情况的报告。
