Title: Invited to Attend the Soviet Writers' Congress, Our Writers' Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1959-05-17
Page: 3

    Invited to Attend the Soviet Writers' Congress
    Our Writers' Delegation Arrives in Moscow
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th - The Chinese Writers' Delegation, led by Mao Dun, invited to attend the Third All-Union Congress of Soviet Writers, arrived in Moscow by plane today. Greeting them at the airport were Markov, Secretary of the Board of the Soviet Writers' Union, and Soviet writers Kerbabaev and Zalygin, among others. Zhang Yingwu, Cultural Counselor of our Embassy in the Soviet Union, also greeted them at the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th - The head of the Chinese Writers' Delegation, Mao Dun, and members Lao She and Yu Heiding, who were invited by the Soviet Writers' Association to go to Moscow to attend the Third All-Union Congress of Soviet Writers, took a plane to Moscow this morning. Among those who saw them off at the airport were Shao Quanlin, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers' Association, and Secretaries of the Secretariat Cao Yu and Yan Wenjing. Sudarikov, Cultural Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and others also saw them off at the airport.

### 应邀出席苏联作家代表大会  我作家代表团抵莫斯科

1959-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

    应邀出席苏联作家代表大会
    我作家代表团抵莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科16日电　应邀参加第三次全苏作家代表大会的中国作家代表团团长茅盾等一行今天飞抵莫斯科。到机场欢迎的有苏联作家协会理事会书记马尔科夫、苏联作家克尔巴巴耶夫、扎雷金等人。我国驻苏联大使馆文化参赞张映吾也到机场迎接。
    新华社16日讯　应苏联作家协会邀请前往莫斯科出席第三次全苏作家代表大会的中国作家代表团团长茅盾，团员老舍、于黑丁，今日上午乘飞机去莫斯科。到机场送行的有中国作家协会副主席邵荃麟、书记处书记曹禺、严文井等，苏联驻华大使馆文化参赞苏达利柯夫等也到机场送行。
