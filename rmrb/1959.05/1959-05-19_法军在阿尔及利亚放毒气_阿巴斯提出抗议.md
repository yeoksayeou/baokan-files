Title: French Army Uses Poison Gas in Algeria; Abbas Protests
Author:
Date: 1959-05-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, May 17th: It is reported that Abbas, Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, sent a telegram to the International Committee of the Red Cross on May 14, protesting the French invading army's use of poison gas against Algerian civilians.

The telegram stated that one hundred Algerians, including women and children, took refuge in a forest to avoid harm from the French army's attacks. The French army then used poison gas against them.

### 法军在阿尔及利亚放毒气  阿巴斯提出抗议

1959-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    法军在阿尔及利亚放毒气
    阿巴斯提出抗议
    新华社17日讯  突尼斯消息：据报道，阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯14日打电报给红十字国际委员会，抗议法国侵略军向阿尔及利亚平民施放毒气。
    电报说，有一百名阿尔及利亚人，其中包括妇女和儿童，为了避免法国军队发起战斗的伤害，而躲在一个树林里。法国军队就对他们施放毒气。
