Title: Capital Cultural and Scientific Circles Gather to Commemorate German Geographer Humboldt
Author:
Date: 1959-05-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Today marks the centenary of the death of the great German geographer Friedrich Alexander von Humboldt. The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Sino-German Democratic Republic Friendship Association, the Geographical Society of China, and the Beijing Association for Science and Technology jointly held a memorial meeting in Beijing tonight.

More than 500 people from cultural and scientific circles in the capital attended the memorial meeting, including Ding Xilin, Liu Aifeng, Huang Bingwei, and Mao Yisheng.

Professor Syhre, Academician of the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic, Dr. Otto Schwarz, President of Friedrich-Schiller University and member of the People's Chamber delegation of the German Democratic Republic currently visiting China, and personnel from the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China also attended the memorial meeting.

Huang Bingwei, Vice Chairman of the Geographical Society of China, delivered a report on "Humboldt's Life and Contributions."

Dr. Otto Schwarz spoke at the meeting. He thanked the Chinese people for their respect for the German scientist Humboldt. After the meeting, geographical science films such as "Treading Through Glaciers and Eternal Snow" were shown.

### 首都文化科学界人士集会  纪念德国地理学家洪堡

1959-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

    首都文化科学界人士集会
    纪念德国地理学家洪堡
    新华社6日讯　今天是德国伟大地理学家弗·亚·洪堡逝世一百周年。中国人民对外文化协会、中华人民共和国德意志民主共和国友好协会、中国地理学会、北京市科学技术协会等单位，今晚在北京联合举行了纪念会。
    出席纪念会的有首都文化科学界人士丁西林、刘皑风、黄秉维、茅以升等五百多人。
    德意志民主共和国科学院院士叙勒教授和正在我国访问的德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团团员、弗里德里希——席勒大学校长奥托·施瓦茨博士，以及德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆人员，也参加了这个纪念会。
    中国地理学会副理事长黄秉维在会上作了关于“洪堡的生平及其贡献”的报告。
    奥托·施瓦茨博士在会上讲了话。他感谢中国人民对德国科学家洪堡的尊敬。会后，放映了“踏破冰川万年雪”等地理科学影片。
