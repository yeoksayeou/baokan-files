Title: US Troops Stationed in West Germany Conduct Military Exercises
Author:
Date: 1959-05-22
Page: 4

　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, 20th - TASS, Bonn, 19th: According to West German newspapers, US troops stationed in West Germany are conducting exercises in Bavaria and Hesse, two states directly adjacent to the German Democratic Republic. Atomic cannons participated in the exercises in the Fulda area, only thirty-five kilometers from the East-West German border.

### 驻西德美军  举行军事演习

1959-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    驻西德美军
    举行军事演习
    新华社20日讯  塔斯社波恩19日讯：据西德报纸报道，驻扎在西德境内的美国军队正在巴伐利亚和黑森这两个同德意志民主共和国直接毗邻的州内进行演习。在距离东西德边境线只有三十五公里的孚尔达城一带，原子炮参加了演习。
