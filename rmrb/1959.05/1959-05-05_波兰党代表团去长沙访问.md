Title: Polish Party Delegation Visits Changsha

Author: 
Date: 1959-05-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, May 4th: The Polish United Workers' Party delegation, headed by Jerzy Morawski, concluded its visit to Wuhan and left Wuhan by train this evening for a visit to Changsha.

Those who went to the station to see off the distinguished guests included Zhang Pinghua, Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.

After arriving in Wuhan on the 2nd, the delegation visited the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, the Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation, the Wuhan Heavy Machinery Plant, the Guanshan Industrial Zone, and the Yangtze River Basin Planning Office. Some members of the delegation also visited the Nanhu People's Commune and the Hubei Provincial College of Traditional Chinese Medicine.

### 波兰党代表团去长沙访问

1959-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    波兰党代表团去长沙访问
    新华社武汉4日电　以耶日·莫拉夫斯基为首的波兰统一工人党代表团结束了在武汉的访问，今晚乘火车离开武汉前往长沙访问。
    到车站欢送贵宾的，有中共湖北省委书记张平化、中共武汉市委第一书记宋侃夫等。
    代表团2日到武汉后，先后参观了武汉长江大桥，参观和访问了武钢、武汉重型机床厂、关山工业区和长江流域规划办公室。部分团员还访问了南湖人民公社和湖北省中医学院。
