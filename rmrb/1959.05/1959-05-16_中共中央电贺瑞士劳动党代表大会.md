Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to Swiss Party of Labour Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-05-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 15th

    CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to Swiss Party of Labour Congress

    Xinhua News Agency, May 15th The Swiss Party of Labour will hold its Seventh Congress in Geneva from the 16th to the 18th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has sent a telegram of congratulations, the full text of which is as follows:

    To all delegates of the Seventh Congress of the Swiss Party of Labour:

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, on behalf of all members of the Communist Party of China and all the people of China, extends warm and fraternal congratulations to the Seventh Congress of the Swiss Party of Labour.

    The Swiss Party of Labour stands at the forefront of the Swiss working class and working people, waging a persistent struggle to defend world peace, safeguard the democratic rights and vital interests of the Swiss working people, strengthen the unity of the working masses and democratic forces, and strive for political reform and social progress. The Communist Party of China and the Chinese people are watching the struggle of the Swiss Party of Labour and the Swiss working people with great concern and sympathy. We wish the Seventh Congress of the Swiss Party of Labour complete success, and wish the Swiss Party of Labour new victories in consolidating the party ranks, strengthening the unity of the working class and all democratic forces, and safeguarding the democratic rights and vital interests of the working people. We wish the Swiss working people greater achievements in defending European security and world peace, developing friendly cooperation among the peoples of all countries, and promoting friendship between the Chinese and Swiss peoples.

                                    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                            May 14, 1959

### 中共中央电贺瑞士劳动党代表大会

1959-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺瑞士劳动党代表大会
    新华社15日讯　瑞士劳动党将于16日至18日在日内瓦召开第七次代表大会。中国共产党中央委员会特地去电报表示祝贺，贺电全文如下：瑞士劳动党第七次代表大会全体代表同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨代表中国共产党全体党员和中国全体人民，向瑞士劳动党第七次代表大会致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    瑞士劳动党站在瑞士工人阶级和劳动人民的前列，为保卫世界和平、捍卫瑞士劳动人民的民主权利和切身利益，加强劳动群众和民主力量的团结，争取政治革新和社会进步，正在进行着坚持不懈的斗争。中国共产党和中国人民抱着极大的关怀和同情，注视着瑞士劳动党和瑞士劳动人民的斗争。我们谨祝瑞士劳动党第七次代表大会完满成功，祝瑞士劳动党在巩固党的队伍、加强工人阶级和一切民主力量的团结、维护劳动人民民主权利和切身利益的斗争中获得新的胜利。祝瑞士劳动人民在保卫欧洲安全与世界和平、发展各国人民之间的友好合作、促进中瑞两国人民友谊的努力中取得更大的成就。
                                    中国共产党中央委员会
                                            1959年5月14日
