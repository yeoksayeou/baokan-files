Title: Hungarian Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-05-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 18th -- The Hungarian Cultural Delegation, a group of five led by head of the delegation Biro Vera, left Beijing today to return home.

Qu Wu, President of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association, held a reception on the evening of the 17th to bid farewell to the guests.

### 匈牙利文化代表团回国

1959-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利文化代表团回国
    新华社18日讯　匈牙利文化代表团一行五人，在团长比罗·维拉率领下，今天离开北京回国。
    中国匈牙利友好协会会长屈武17日晚上举行酒会欢送客人。
