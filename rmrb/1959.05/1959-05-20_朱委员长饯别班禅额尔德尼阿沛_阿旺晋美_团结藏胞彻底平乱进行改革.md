Title: Chairman Zhu Fetes Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme: Unite Tibetan Compatriots to Thoroughly Quell the Rebellion and Carry Out Reform
Author:
Date: 1959-05-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 19th: Chairman Zhu De held a banquet at Ziguang Pavilion in Zhongnanhai tonight to bid farewell to Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Vice-Chairman and Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as Bainqen Erdini's accompanying officials, who are about to return to Tibet.

Chairman Zhu De delivered a speech at the banquet, warmly congratulating Bainqen Erdini and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme on their return to Tibet to further unite the Tibetan people and achieve victory in thoroughly quelling the rebellion and carrying out democratic reforms in Tibet. Chairman Zhu De proposed a toast to the unity of the motherland and the great unity of all nationalities, and to the health of Bainqen Erdini and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme!

Bainqen Erdini then delivered a speech at the banquet. He said that carrying out democratic reforms and taking the socialist road is the unanimous wish of all nationalities in the country, including the Tibetan people. It is the general trend and the desire of the people, and no one can stop it. He said that the socialist system is the bright road truly leading to the paradise of mankind. Such an incomparably superior system must also be realized in the Tibetan region.

Bainqen Erdini said: I firmly believe that under the wise leadership of the Central Government, under the direct leadership of the Tibet Work Committee of the Communist Party of China, and with the strong support of the people of the whole country, the democratic reforms and socialist transformation that the Tibetan people have long hoped for will surely be realized, the national regional autonomy in Tibet will surely be fully realized, and the task of building a new democratic and socialist Tibet will surely be completed victoriously. The Tibetan people, like other nationalities, have fulfilled their glorious responsibility in the creation and development of the great motherland, and will certainly be able to make greater contributions in the glorious ranks of building a socialist motherland.

Finally, Bainqen Erdini proposed a toast to the great unity of the people of all nationalities and to the good health of Chairman Mao, the great leader of all nationalities!

Those present at the banquet included Vice-Chairmen Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Cheng Qian, and Lin Feng; Vice-Premiers He Long, Chen Yi, Nie Rongzhen, and Xi Zhongxun; President of the Supreme People's Court Xie Juezai; Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Bao Erhan; Vice-Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong; Chairman of the NPC Nationalities Committee Liu Geping and Vice-Chairman Xie Fumin; Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Wang Feng, Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, Yang Jingren, and Xie Hechou; Deputy Ministers of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee Xu Bing, Ping Jiesan, and Xue Zizheng; Central Representative in Tibet Zhang Jingwu; Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region Tan Guansan; President of the Chinese Buddhist Association Xirao Gyatso; and Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association Da Pongsheng.

Also present at the banquet were members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and responsible persons of relevant government departments in Beijing.

After the banquet, Bainqen Erdini, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, and others attended a farewell evening party and watched wonderful Peking Opera and Sichuan Opera performances. (Picture attached)

### 朱委员长饯别班禅额尔德尼阿沛·阿旺晋美  团结藏胞彻底平乱进行改革

1959-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

    朱委员长饯别班禅额尔德尼阿沛·阿旺晋美
    团结藏胞彻底平乱进行改革
    班禅说，无比优越的社会主义制度必须在西藏地区实现
    新华社19日讯　朱德委员长今晚在中南海紫光阁举行宴会，欢送即将回西藏的全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和政协全国委员会副主席、西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员兼秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美，以及班禅额尔德尼的随行官员。
    朱德委员长在宴会上致词，热烈祝贺班禅额尔德尼和阿沛·阿旺晋美回到西藏以后，进一步团结西藏人民，在彻底平息叛乱和进行西藏的民主改革中取得胜利。朱德委员长提议，为祖国的统一和各民族的伟大团结，为班禅额尔德尼和阿沛·阿旺晋美的健康干杯！
    班禅额尔德尼接着在宴会上致词。他说，经过民主改革，走社会主义道路，是全国各族人民包括西藏人民的一致愿望，是大势所趋，人心所向，任何人都阻挡不了的。他说，社会主义制度是真正通向人类极乐世界的光明大道。这样一个无比优越的制度，也必须在西藏地区实现。
    班禅额尔德尼说：我坚信，在中央的英明领导下，在中共西藏工委的直接领导下，在全国人民的有力支援下，西藏人民所久已期望的民主改革和社会主义改造一定能够实现，西藏地方民族区域自治也一定得到充分的实现，建设民主和社会主义的新西藏的任务一定能够胜利完成。西藏人民和其他各族人民一样，在伟大祖国的创造和发展过程中，尽了自己的光荣责任，在建设社会主义祖国的光荣行列里，一定能做出更大的贡献。
    班禅额尔德尼最后提议为各民族人民的大团结，为全国各族人民伟大领袖毛主席的身体健康干杯！
    出席宴会的，有副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、程潜、林枫，副总理贺龙、陈毅、聂荣臻、习仲勋，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，国防委员会副主席张治中，人大民族委员会主任委员刘格平、副主任委员谢扶民，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、刘春、萨空了、杨静仁、谢鹤筹，中共中央统战部副部长徐冰、平杰三、薛子正，中央驻西藏代表张经武，西藏军区政治委员谭冠三，中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生。
    出席宴会的，还有在北京的人大常委委员和政府有关部门的负责人。
    宴会结束后，班禅额尔德尼和阿沛·阿旺晋美等出席了欢送晚会，观看了精采的京剧和川剧节目。（附图片）
    在宴会上，左起：班禅额尔德尼、朱德、阿沛·阿旺晋美。
