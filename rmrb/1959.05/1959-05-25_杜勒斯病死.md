Title: Dulles Dies of Illness
Author:
Date: 1959-05-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 24th—According to an announcement today by the U.S. State Department, former U.S. Secretary of State Dulles died today at Walter Reed Army Hospital.

	Dulles died of cancer. In April of this year, he was forced to resign from his position as Secretary of State, which he had held for six years, due to serious illness.

### 杜勒斯病死

1959-05-25
第5版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯病死
    新华社24日讯　华盛顿消息：据美国国务院今天宣布，美国前任国务卿杜勒斯今天在华尔特·里德陆军医院病逝。
    杜勒斯是患癌症死去的。今年4月间，他因患重病被迫辞去了他已经担任了六年的国务卿的职务。
