Title: Beijing Sino-Czech Friendship People's Commune Celebrates the 14th Anniversary of Czechoslovak State Founding
Author:
Date: 1959-05-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 9th Dispatch: Members of the Beijing Sino-Czech Friendship People's Commune held a gathering on the evening of the 8th to celebrate the 14th anniversary of the founding of Czechoslovakia.

　　At the meeting, Guo Rong, the deputy director of the commune, spoke, warmly celebrating the 14th anniversary of the founding of Czechoslovakia.

　　Štěpán Štegina, Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, also attended the celebration and spoke at the meeting.

### 北京中捷友好公社庆祝捷建国十四周年

1959-05-10
第2版()
专栏：

    北京中捷友好公社
    庆祝捷建国十四周年
    据新华社9日讯　北京中捷友好公社社员在8日晚举行集会，庆祝捷克斯洛伐克建国十四周年。
    会上，公社副主任郭荣讲了话，他热烈庆祝捷克斯洛伐克的建国十四周年。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆参赞史杰吉纳也出席了庆祝会，并在会上讲了话。
