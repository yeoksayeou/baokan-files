Title: Zuo Hai　Lin Ximing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 8

　　Column: Poetic Moods

　　United in heart to quell the turmoil, bodies overturned,
　　Han and Tibetan soldiers and civilians, a brotherhood of flesh and blood.
　　A handful of clear spring water welcomes the warriors,
　　Affection as long as the water, water from the same source.
　　　　　　        Poem by Zuo Hai　Papercut by Lin Ximing
