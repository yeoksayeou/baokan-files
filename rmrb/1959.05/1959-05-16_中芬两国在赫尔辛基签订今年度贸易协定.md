Title: China and Finland Sign Trade Agreement for This Year in Helsinki
Author:
Date: 1959-05-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 15th: China and Finland signed a trade agreement for 1959 in Helsinki today.

The agreement is retroactive and will expire on October 31 this year. The trade methods and import commodities between China and Finland this year remain unchanged.

Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Trade, signed the agreement on behalf of China, and Finnish Trade Minister Karjalainen signed on behalf of Finland.

### 中芬两国在赫尔辛基签订今年度贸易协定

1959-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    中芬两国在赫尔辛基签订今年度贸易协定
    新华社15日讯　赫尔辛基消息：中国和芬兰1959年度贸易协定今天在赫尔辛基签字。
    协定具有追溯性，将在今年10月31日满期。中芬今年贸易方式和进口商品都没有变更。
    协定由中国对外贸易部副部长卢绪章代表中国签字，芬兰贸易部长卡尔亚莱宁代表芬兰签字。
