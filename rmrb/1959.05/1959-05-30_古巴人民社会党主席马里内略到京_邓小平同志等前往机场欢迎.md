Title: Cuban People's Socialist Party Chairman Marinello Arrives in Beijing Comrade Deng Xiaoping and Others Go to the Airport to Welcome Him
Author:
Date: 1959-05-30
Page: 1

    Cuban People's Socialist Party Chairman Marinello Arrives in Beijing
    Comrade Deng Xiaoping and Others Go to the Airport to Welcome Him
    Xinhua News Agency, May 29th. Juan Marinello, Chairman of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, were at the airport to greet him.
    During his stay in China, Juan Marinello will visit various places. (Attached picture)
    On the afternoon of May 29th, Juan Marinello, Chairman of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, arrived in Beijing. Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and others went to the airport to welcome him. The picture shows Deng Xiaoping and Juan Marinello at the airport.

### 古巴人民社会党主席马里内略到京  邓小平同志等前往机场欢迎

1959-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    古巴人民社会党主席马里内略到京
    邓小平同志等前往机场欢迎
    新华社29日讯　古巴人民社会党主席胡安·马里内略，在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有中共中央总书记邓小平，中共中央书记处书记王稼祥。
    胡安·马里内略在中国期间，将到各地进行参观访问。（附图片）
    5月29日下午，古巴人民社会党主席胡安·马里内略到京，中共中央总书记邓小平等前往机场欢迎。图为邓小平和胡安·马里内略在机场上。
      新华社记者　牛畏予摄
