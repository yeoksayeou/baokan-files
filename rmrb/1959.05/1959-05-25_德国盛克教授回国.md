Title: German Professor Schenk Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-05-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. Professor Paul Schenk, a music theorist from the German Democratic Republic, who came to China for a visit in accordance with the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, concluded his visit to China and left Beijing by plane today to return home. Jiang Dingxian, director of the Composition Department of the Central Conservatory of Music, and others were at the airport to see him off.

### 德国盛克教授回国

1959-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

    德国盛克教授回国
    新华社23日讯　根据中德文化合作协定1959年执行计划前来我国访问的德意志民主共和国音乐理论家保尔·盛克教授，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有中央音乐学院作曲系主任江定仙等。
