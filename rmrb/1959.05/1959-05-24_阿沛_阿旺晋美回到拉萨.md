Title: Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme Returns to Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1959-05-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 23rd: Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and Deputy Director and Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, returned to Lhasa by special plane this afternoon.

Ngapoi Tseden Drolkar, representative of the National People's Congress, member of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, member of the National Committee of the CPPCC, and acting chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Patriotic Women's Federation, also returned to Lhasa on the same plane.

Liang Hong, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Tibet Work Committee, Chen Jingbo, Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, Jiantong Losang Langjie, Director of the Construction Department of the Preparatory Committee, and Zhang Ming, Deputy Director of the Mass Work Department of the Tibet Military Region, and others went to the airport near Lhasa to welcome Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme and others.

Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme and his party left Beijing on the 20th. They passed through Xi'an on the same day and Xining on the 22nd. When they passed through the two cities, they were welcomed and seen off by the responsible persons and people from all walks of life of the Shaanxi and Qinghai Provincial People's Committees. On the 21st, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme also took a special car from Xi'an to Xianyang, Shaanxi Province, to inspect the Tibet Public School located there.

### 阿沛·阿旺晋美回到拉萨

1959-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    阿沛·阿旺晋美回到拉萨
    新华社拉萨23日电　政协全国委员会副主席、西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员兼秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美，今天下午乘专机回到拉萨。
    全国人民代表大会代表、西藏自治区筹备委员会委员协饶登珠和政协全国委员会委员、西藏自治区筹备委员会委员、西藏爱国妇女联谊会筹委会代理主任委员阿沛·才丹卓噶，也同机回到拉萨。
    前往拉萨附近机场欢迎阿沛·阿旺晋美等人的，有中共西藏工委统战部副部长梁洪、西藏自治区筹备委员会副秘书长陈竞波，筹委会建设处处长詹东·洛桑郎杰和西藏军区群众工作部副部长张铭等。
    阿沛·阿旺晋美一行二十日离开北京，当天路过西安，22日路过西宁。他们路过两地时，受到了陕西省和青海省人民委员会负责人和各界人士的欢迎和欢送。21日，阿沛·阿旺晋美并从西安乘专车到陕西省咸阳视察了设在那里的西藏公学。
