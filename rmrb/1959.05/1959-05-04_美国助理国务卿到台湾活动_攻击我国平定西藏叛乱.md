Title: U.S. Assistant Secretary of State Carries Out Activities in Taiwan, Attacks China's Suppression of the Tibetan Rebellion
Author:
Date: 1959-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch from Taipei: U.S. Assistant Secretary of State for Far Eastern Affairs, Parsons, carried out three days of aggressive activities in Taiwan. Parsons held three consecutive meetings with Chiang Kai-shek in Taipei. Reports say that the talks covered the situation in Taiwan, the coastal islands issue, and the Tibetan issue. In Taipei, Parsons viciously attacked China's suppression of the Tibetan rebellion as "the suppression of Tibet" and "atrocities," and was extremely hateful of China's implementation of the people's commune system.

Parsons left Taipei on the afternoon of the 2nd for Japan.

### 美国助理国务卿到台湾活动  攻击我国平定西藏叛乱

1959-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    美国助理国务卿到台湾活动
    攻击我国平定西藏叛乱
    新华社3日讯　台北消息：美国国务院负责远东事务的助理国务卿帕森斯到台湾进行了三天的侵略活动。帕森斯在台北同蒋介石连续举行了三次会谈。有消息说，会谈中讨论了台湾局势、沿海岛屿问题和西藏问题。帕森斯在台北恶毒地攻击我国平定西藏叛乱为“对西藏的镇压”和“暴行”，并且对我国实行人民公社制度十分仇恨。
    帕森斯已在2日下午离开台北前往日本。
