Title: Su Wenren Creates New 10,000-Meter Record
Author:
Date: 1959-05-31
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 29th. Chinese track and field athlete Su Wenren set a new Chinese record in the 10,000-meter race in Potsdam on the 28th with a time of 30 minutes 6.8 seconds.

Su Wenren's result was 1 minute 5.8 seconds faster than the previous national record set by People's Liberation Army athlete Huang Zhiyong on May 7th, making him the first athlete in China to complete the 10,000 meters in under 31 minutes and approach the 30-minute mark.

The 23-year-old Su Wenren achieved this result in a friendly competition with the "Forward" Sports Association of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic. The friendly competition consisted of twelve events, and the Chinese track and field team won four events: the men's 100-meter sprint, 200-meter sprint, 110-meter hurdles, and pole vault.

The Chinese track and field women's team participated in a competition held in Oranienburg on the 27th, winning first place in the 80-meter low hurdles, high jump, long jump, and 400-meter relay.

### 苏文仁创万公尺新成绩

1959-05-31
第2版()
专栏：

    苏文仁创万公尺新成绩
    据新华社柏林29日电　中国田径运动员苏文仁28日在波茨坦以三十分六秒八创造了中国一万公尺赛跑的最高成绩。
    苏文仁的成绩比中国人民解放军运动员黄志勇5月7日创造的全国最高成绩缩短了一分五点八秒之多，成为中国第一个能用不到三十一分的时间跑完一万公尺并且逼近三十分大关的运动员。
    二十三岁的苏文仁是在同德意志民主共和国国家人民军“前进”体育协会友谊赛中创造这个成绩的。这次友谊赛一共进行十二个项目的比赛，中国田径队在男子一百公尺赛跑，二百公尺赛跑，一百一十公尺高栏，撑竿跳高四个项目中取得胜利。
    中国田径队女子队27日参加了在奥兰宁堡举行的比赛，中国队获得八十公尺低栏、跳高、跳远、四百公尺接力的第一名。
