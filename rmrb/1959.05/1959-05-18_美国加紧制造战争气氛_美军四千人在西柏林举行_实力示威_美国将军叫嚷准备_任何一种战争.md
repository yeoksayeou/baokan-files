Title: The United States Intensifies the Creation of a War Atmosphere: American Troops Stage "Show of Force" in West Berlin with Four Thousand Soldiers; American General Yells to Prepare for "Any Kind of War"
Author:
Date: 1959-05-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 17th, West Berlin Dispatch: The four thousand American occupation troops stationed in West Berlin staged a show of force in West Berlin on the 16th, attempting to create international tension and obstruct negotiations during the East-West Geneva Foreign Ministers Conference. The four thousand American troops were fully armed, equipped with tanks, heavy mortars, and other weapons, marching in procession, with helicopters circling in the air for a show of force.

    U.S. Army Europe Commander General Eddleman, in a speech, made it clear that the United States would adopt a stubborn and provocative attitude on the peaceful resolution of the West Berlin issue. He said that the troops stationed in West Berlin are "small in number but very powerful." He said that the troops are stationed here as "full proof that we will honor our obligations."

    Xinhua News Agency, May 17th, Washington Dispatch: Belligerent American generals are again creating a war atmosphere over the Berlin issue. U.S. Chief of Naval Operations Admiral Burke said in Washington on the 14th that NATO forces are "well prepared" to deal with any kind of war. He said that if a "dispute" occurs in Berlin, he does not believe the "dispute" will be "limited to that area."

    On the same day, NATO Supreme Allied Commander, General Norstad, at a luncheon in Toronto, Canada, issued a nuclear war provocation, saying that NATO forces would "not limit the weapons used" to force so-called "aggressors" to cease conflict or attack in Europe.

### 美国加紧制造战争气氛  美军四千人在西柏林举行“实力示威”  美国将军叫嚷准备“任何一种战争”

1959-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    美国加紧制造战争气氛
    美军四千人在西柏林举行“实力示威”
    美国将军叫嚷准备“任何一种战争”
    新华社17日讯　西柏林消息：驻在西柏林的四千名美国占领军16日在西柏林举行实力示威，企图在东西方日内瓦外长会议期间制造国际紧张局势，阻挠谈判。四千名美军全副武装，配有坦克、重迫击炮等武器，出动游行，还有直升飞机在空中盘旋示威。
    美国驻欧洲陆军司令埃德尔曼在讲话中表明美国将在和平解决西柏林问题上采取顽固的挑衅态度。他说：驻西柏林部队“人数虽少但是很有力量”。他说，部队驻扎在这里“充分证明我们要尊重我们的义务。”
    新华社17日讯　华盛顿消息：好战的美国将军又在柏林问题上制造战争气氛。美国海军作战部长、海军上将伯克14日在华盛顿说，北大西洋公约组织部队有“很好的准备”来对付任何一种战争。他说：如果在柏林发生“争端”的话，他不相信“争端”将“仅限于这个地区”。
    同一天，北大西洋集团军总司令、美国将军诺斯塔德在加拿大多伦多市的一次午餐会上发出核战争挑衅说，北大西洋集团军队将“不限制所使用的武器”来在欧洲迫使所谓“侵略者”中止冲突或进攻。
