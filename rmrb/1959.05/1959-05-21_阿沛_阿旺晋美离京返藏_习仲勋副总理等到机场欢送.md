Title: Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme Departs Beijing to Return to Tibet; Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun and Others See Him Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-05-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 20th: Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, left Beijing by special plane today to return to Tibet. Traveling back to Tibet on the same plane were also National People's Congress representative and member of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, Xirao Dengzhu, and member of the National Committee of the CPPCC, member of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Acting Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Patriotic Women's Federation of Tibet, Ngapoi Cedan Zhoigar.
    Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, was at the airport to see them off.
    Also at the airport to see them off were Ping Jiesan, Deputy Director of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee; Liu Geping, Chairman of the National People's Congress Nationalities Committee; Xie Fumin, Vice Chairman of the National People's Congress Nationalities Committee; Wang Feng, Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, Yang Jingren, and Xie Hechou, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Shi Liang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation and Standing Committee Member of the National Committee of the CPPCC; Zhang Jingwu, Central Government Representative in Tibet; Tan Guansan, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region; the Panchen Lama's accompanying officials; the heads of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing, and the CPC Tibet Work Committee's Office in Beijing, as well as cadres from the Nationalities Affairs Commission and students from the Central Institute for Nationalities. (Attached picture)

### 阿沛·阿旺晋美离京返藏  习仲勋副总理等到机场欢送

1959-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    阿沛·阿旺晋美离京返藏
    习仲勋副总理等到机场欢送
    新华社20日讯　政协全国委员会副主席、西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员兼秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美今天乘专机离北京回西藏。同机回西藏的，还有全国人民代表大会代表、西藏自治区筹备委员会委员协饶登珠，政协全国委员会委员、西藏自治区筹备委员会委员、西藏爱国妇女联谊会筹委会代理主任阿沛·才丹卓噶。
    国务院副总理习仲勋到机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的还有中共中央统战部副部长平杰三，人大民族委员会主任委员刘格平、副主任委员谢扶民，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、刘春、萨空了、杨静仁、谢鹤筹，全国妇联副主席、政协全国委员会常务委员史良，中央驻西藏代表张经武，西藏军区政治委员谭冠三，班禅的随行官员，达赖驻京办事处、班禅驻京办事处、中共西藏工委驻京办事处的负责人，以及民族事务委员会的干部和中央民族学院的学生。（附图片）
