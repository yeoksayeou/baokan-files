    International Briefs
    Soviet Union Donates Books to Cambodia
    Phnom Penh dispatch: The Soviet Embassy in Cambodia donated a collection of Russian and Soviet authors' books, as well as Soviet literary publications in foreign languages, to the Cambodian National Education Ministry on the 9th.
    United States Provides Missile Equipment to Denmark
    Washington dispatch: Amidst the United States' intensification of supplying North Atlantic Treaty Organization countries' armies with nuclear weapons and missiles, the United States Army authorities transferred all the equipment to outfit a Nike missile battalion to the Danish government on the 7th.

### 国际简讯

1959-05-12
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    苏联赠送柬埔寨一批书籍
    金边讯：苏联驻柬埔寨大使馆9日把俄国作家和苏联作家的一批书籍以及用外文出版的苏联文学书刊赠送给柬埔寨国民教育部。
    美国向丹麦提供导弹装备
    华盛顿讯：在美国加紧以核武器和导弹供应北大西洋集团国家军队声中，美国陆军当局在7日又把装备一个奈克导弹营的全部设备移交给丹麦政府。
