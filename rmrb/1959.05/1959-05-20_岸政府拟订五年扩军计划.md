Title: Kishi Government Drafts Five-Year Expansion Plan
Author:
Date: 1959-05-20
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 18th: The Nobusuke Kishi government is drafting a new five-year military expansion plan to further revive militarism and implement nuclear armament.

The purpose of this five-year plan is to "maximize the improvement" of the "quality" of the Japanese army and strengthen the system of "opposing aggression." According to this plan, the Japanese army will be expanded to 200,000 men by 1965. The Navy will be equipped with submarines, submarine chasers, aircraft carriers, and missile-carrying destroyers. All Air Force aircraft will be equipped with American "Sidewinder" air-to-air missiles.

### 岸政府拟订五年扩军计划

1959-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    岸政府拟订五年扩军计划
    新华社18日讯　东京消息：岸信介政府正在拟订一项新的五年扩军计划，来进一步复活军国主义和实行核武装。
    这个五年计划的目的在于“最大限度地改善”日本军队的“质量”，加强“反对侵略”的体制。根据这个计划，日本陆军1965年将要扩充到二十万人。海军将配备有潜水艇、驱潜艇、航空母舰和载有导弹的驱逐舰。空军所有的飞机要装备美国“响尾蛇”式空空导弹。
