Title: Further Strengthen the Brotherly Friendship Between the Chinese and Hungarian Peoples. Hungarian Party and Government Delegation Visits North Korea. 100,000 People in Pyongyang Warmly Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-05-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th May. A Hungarian Party and Government delegation, led by Ferenc Munnich, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Prime Minister of the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, arrived in Pyongyang by special plane this noon. The delegation was warmly welcomed by Korean party and government leaders headed by Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Premier of the Cabinet, and approximately 100,000 Pyongyang citizens.

Today, the entire city of Pyongyang was festooned with the national flags of both Korea and Hungary. The special plane carrying the delegation landed at Pyongyang Airport at 12:30 pm amidst cheers. After disembarking, Premier Munnich, accompanied by Premier Kim Il-sung, reviewed the guard of honor and received flowers from children.

Welcoming the distinguished guests at the airport today were Kim Il, First Vice-Premier of the Cabinet; Vice-Premiers of the Cabinet Jung Il-ryong, Nam Il, and Jung Joon-taek; Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Park Jung-ae and Kim Chang-man; as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to North Korea.

Premier Kim Il-sung delivered a welcome speech at the airport, saying that the delegation's visit to Korea would make a significant contribution to further strengthening the friendship between the peoples of Korea and Hungary. He expressed gratitude to the Hungarian people for their assistance to the Korean people during and after the war. He said that the delegation's visit would help the Korean people in their cause of achieving peaceful reunification of their motherland.

Premier Munnich then spoke amidst cheers. He said that the peoples of Hungary and Korea had already established an unbreakable friendship between them. The Hungarian people greatly admired the heroic struggle waged by the heroic Korean people during the Korean War and in the post-war period. Premier Munnich expressed his gratitude to the Korean people for the material and moral assistance given to the Hungarian people during the counter-revolutionary riots in Hungary in 1956. Munnich said that the Hungarian people supported the Korean people's struggle for the peaceful reunification of their motherland.

Premier Munnich, accompanied by Premier Kim Il-sung, travelled in an open-top car to the Pyongyang Guest House, and was warmly welcomed along the way by crowds holding flowers and colored flags.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th May. The Hungarian Party and Government delegation, headed by Ferenc Munnich, member of the Political Bureau, Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Premier of the Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, paid a courtesy call this afternoon to the leaders of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Cabinet, and the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly.

Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Premier of the Cabinet, Kim Il, First Vice-Premier of the Cabinet, Jung Il-ryong, Nam Il, and Jung Joon-taek, Vice-Premiers of the Cabinet, Park Jung-ae and Kim Chang-man, Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, and Lee Keuk-ro and Ko Joon-taek, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and others received the delegation.

The two sides held talks in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 进一步加强朝匈两国人民的兄弟友谊  匈牙利党政代表团到朝鲜访问  平壤十万群众热烈欢迎贵宾

1959-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

    进一步加强朝匈两国人民的兄弟友谊
    匈牙利党政代表团到朝鲜访问
    平壤十万群众热烈欢迎贵宾
    新华社平壤7日电　由匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会政治局委员、匈牙利工农革命政府总理明尼赫·费伦茨率领的匈牙利党政代表团今天中午乘专机到达平壤。代表团受到以朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长、内阁首相金日成为首的朝鲜党政领导人和大约十万平壤市民的热烈欢迎。
    今天，平壤全城到处飘扬着朝、匈两国的国旗。代表团乘坐的专机在十二时三十分在万众欢呼声中在平壤机场着陆。明尼赫总理下飞机后，由金日成首相陪同检阅了仪仗队，并且接受儿童献花。
    今天在机场欢迎贵宾的，有朝鲜内阁第一副首相金一，内阁副首相郑一龙、南日、郑准泽，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、金昌满以及各国驻朝鲜的外交使节。
    金日成首相在机场致欢迎词说，代表团这次访问朝鲜，将对进一步加强朝匈两国人民之间的友谊作出重大贡献。他表示感谢匈牙利人民在战时和战后给予朝鲜人民的援助。他说，代表团的访问将有助于朝鲜人民争取和平统一祖国的事业。
    明尼赫总理接着在欢呼声中讲了话。他说，匈朝两国人民已经在他们之间建立了牢不可破的友谊。匈牙利人民十分敬佩英雄的朝鲜人民在朝鲜战争时期和战后所进行的英勇斗争。明尼赫总理表示感谢朝鲜人民在1956年匈牙利发生反革命暴乱时给予匈牙利人民以物质和精神上的援助。明尼赫说，匈牙利人民支持朝鲜人民争取和平统一祖国的斗争。
    明尼赫总理由金日成首相陪同，乘敞篷汽车前往平壤市迎宾馆，沿途受到拿着鲜花和彩旗的群众的热烈欢迎。
    新华社平壤7日电　以匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会政治局委员、工农革命政府总理明尼赫·费伦茨为首的匈牙利党政代表团，今天下午拜会了朝鲜劳动党中央委员会、内阁和最高人民会议常任委员会的领导人。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长、内阁首相金日成，内阁第一副首相金一，内阁副首相郑一龙、南日、郑准泽，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、金昌满，最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长李克鲁·高峻泽等接见了代表团。
    双方在亲切友好的气氛中进行了会谈。
