Title: German People Demand Peace Treaty: Over Five Million Signatures Delivered to Foreign Ministers' Conference
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 27th, Berlin, May 26th (TASS): A batch of letters signed by five and a half million residents of the two German states have been handed over to the secretariat of the Foreign Ministers' Conference on May 25th by Zimmering, the permanent representative and advisor of the German Democratic Republic to Geneva, on behalf of the National Committee of the National Front of Democratic Germany. These letters insist on the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany and the resolution of the Berlin issue. (Attached picture)
    
    Right: Members of the delegation of the Government of the German Democratic Republic attending the Geneva Conference receiving a large number of telegrams and letters from the two German states.

### 德国人民要求缔结和约  五百多万人的签名信送外长会议

1959-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    德国人民要求缔结和约
    五百多万人的签名信送外长会议
    新华社27日讯  据塔斯社柏林26日讯：有两个德国五百五十万居民签名的一批信件已经由德意志民主共和国驻日内瓦常任代表和顾问齐墨林代表民主德国全国阵线全国委员会在25日交给外长会议秘书处。这些信件坚持要求缔结对德和约和解决柏林问题。（附图片）
    右图为德意志民主共和国政府出席日内瓦会议代表团人员在收阅从两个德国寄来的大批电报和信件。
     德意志通讯社稿      （新华社发）
