Title: Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee, Holds Banquet to See Off Delegation from the German People's Chamber

Author:
Date: 1959-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing, hosted a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the delegation from the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber, Dieckmann, and First Vice Chairman, Matern. The delegation will leave Beijing tomorrow morning to return home.
    The banquet was filled with a joyful and friendly atmosphere. Peng Zhen, Dieckmann, Matern, and others repeatedly raised their glasses to congratulate the everlasting brotherly friendship between the Chinese and German people. Peng Zhen warmly wished the people of the German Democratic Republic victory in their struggle for national reunification.
    Those present at the banquet included Li Jishen and Lin Feng, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premier of the State Council; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Liu Ren, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Chen Kehan, Secretary of the Secretariat; Wan Li, Feng Jiping, Wu Han, Jia Tingsan, and Le Songsheng, Vice Mayors of Beijing; and people from all walks of life in Beijing.
    The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Wang Dael, was invited to attend the banquet. (Attached picture)
    From the left in the back row: Dieckmann, Peng Zhen, Matern, etc. at the banquet.

### 彭真副委员长举行宴会   欢送德人民议院代表团

1959-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真副委员长举行宴会
    欢送德人民议院代表团
    新华社11日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，今天晚间设宴欢送以德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团主席狄克曼、第一副主席马特恩为首的德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团。代表团将在明天上午离北京回国。
    宴会上洋溢着欢乐和友好的气氛。彭真、狄克曼、马特恩等一再举杯，祝贺中德人民的兄弟友谊万古长青。彭真热情地祝德意志民主共和国人民在争取祖国统一的斗争中取得胜利。
    出席宴会的，有人大常委会副委员长李济深、林枫，国务院副总理习仲勋，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中共北京市委第二书记刘仁、书记处书记陈克寒，北京市副市长万里、冯基平、吴晗、贾庭三、乐松生，以及北京市各界人士等。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔应邀出席宴会。（附图片）
    后左起：狄克曼、彭真、马特恩等在宴会上
    新华社记者　塔吉古勒摄
