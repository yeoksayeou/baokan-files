Title: Polish United Workers' Party Delegation Returns Home; Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, and Others Welcome and See Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-05-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd: The Polish United Workers' Party delegation, headed by Jerzy Morawski, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee, left Beijing by plane today to return home, concluding its month-long visit to China.

Those who welcomed and saw off the delegation at the airport included: Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee; and Xiong Fu, Li Qixin, Zhang Xiangshan and other responsible personnel from the organs of the CPC Central Committee.

Also at the airport to see the delegation off was Furato, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China. (Attached picture)

Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, and others went to the airport to welcome and see off the Polish United Workers' Party delegation. The picture shows Deng Xiaoping and Jerzy Morawski at the airport.
                  Xinhua News Agency reporter, Taji Gule.

### 波兰统一工人党代表团回国  中共中央总书记邓小平等到机场欢送

1959-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党代表团回国
    中共中央总书记邓小平等到机场欢送
    新华社23日讯　以波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、书记处书记耶日·莫拉夫斯基为首的波兰统一工人党代表团，结束了在我国历时一个月的参观访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有：中共中央总书记邓小平，中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员伍修权以及中共中央机关负责工作人员熊复、李启新、张香山等人。
    到机场欢送的还有波兰驻我国大使馆临时代办傅拉托。（附图片）
    中共中央总书记邓小平等前往机场欢送波兰统一工人党代表团。图为邓小平和耶日·莫拉夫斯基在机场上。
                  新华社记者　塔吉古勒摄
