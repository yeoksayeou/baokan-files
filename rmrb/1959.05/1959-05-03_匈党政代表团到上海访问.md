Title: Hungarian Party and Government Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-05-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd. The Hungarian Party and Government Delegation, headed by Münnich Ferenc, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Prime Minister of the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, flew to Shanghai for a visit this morning, accompanied by Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and our Ambassador to Hungary Hao Deqing.

Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Chen Yi, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the CPC and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 2nd. Today, the Longhua Airport in Shanghai was decorated with colorful flags, and the national flags of China and Hungary fluttered in the wind. A huge banner reading "Welcome the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation headed by Prime Minister Münnich Ferenc" was hung high on the airport building. At 12:45 noon, the special plane carrying the distinguished guests landed slowly amidst welcoming music and enthusiastic applause and cheers. When Prime Minister Münnich stepped off the plane, he was warmly welcomed by Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee; Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Secretariat and Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Shi Ximin, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee; Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat and Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Zhao Zukang, Vice Mayor of Shanghai; and responsible persons of the Shanghai Municipal Party, government, military, local organizations of various democratic parties, and people's organizations.

As the distinguished guests walked towards the welcoming crowds, young women presented bouquets of flowers to Prime Minister Münnich. The masses waiting at the airport waved flowers and colored ribbons, enthusiastically shouting: "Long live Sino-Hungarian friendship! Long live the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union!"

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation today were consuls of various countries stationed in Shanghai and Hungarian experts in Shanghai.

In the afternoon, the distinguished guests toured the city.

In the evening, the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Culture held a Yue Opera performance, featuring the Yue Opera "Zhui Yu" performed by famous Yue Opera actors Xu Yulan, Wang Wenjuan, and Jin Caifeng to entertain the distinguished guests.

### 匈党政代表团到上海访问

1959-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    匈党政代表团到上海访问
    新华社2日讯　以匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会政治局委员、工农革命政府总理明尼赫·费伦茨为首的匈牙利党政代表团，今天早晨由副总理习仲勋、外交部副部长曾涌泉、我国驻匈牙利大使郝德青等陪同，乘专机前往上海参观访问。
    到机场送行的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫。
    据新华社上海2日电　今天，上海龙华机场彩旗招展，中匈两国国旗迎风飘扬，机场大厦上高悬着“欢迎以明尼赫·费伦茨总理为首的匈牙利党政代表团”的巨幅标语。中午十二点四十五分，贵宾乘坐的专机在欢迎乐曲和一片热情的掌声、欢呼声中徐徐降落。明尼赫总理步下飞机时，受到中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显，书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，中共上海市委书记处候补书记石西民，书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，上海市副市长赵祖康及上海市党、政、军、各民主党派地方组织和各人民团体负责人的热烈欢迎。
    当贵宾们走向欢迎群众行列时，姑娘们纷纷把鲜花献给明尼赫总理，守候在机场上欢迎的群众挥舞鲜花和彩巾，热情欢呼：“中匈友好万岁！以苏联为首的社会主义阵营团结万岁！”
    今天到机场欢迎的还有各国驻上海领事和匈牙利在上海的专家等。
    下午，贵宾们游览了市区。
    晚上，上海市文化局举行越剧晚会，由著名越剧演员徐玉兰、王文娟、金采凤等演出越剧“追鱼”招待贵宾。
