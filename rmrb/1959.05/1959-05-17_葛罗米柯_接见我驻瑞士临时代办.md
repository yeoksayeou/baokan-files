Title: Gromyko Receives China's Chargé d'affaires ad interim to Switzerland
Author:
Date: 1959-05-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 16th, TASS dispatch: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko received Lai Yali, China's Chargé d'affaires ad interim to Switzerland, this morning and had a conversation with him.
    Zemskov, Secretary-General of the Soviet Delegation to the Geneva Conference, participated in the conversation.

### 葛罗米柯  接见我驻瑞士临时代办

1959-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯
    接见我驻瑞士临时代办
    新华社16日讯　塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯今天上午接见了中华人民共和国驻瑞士临时代办赖亚力并同他进行了谈话。
    参加谈话的有出席日内瓦会议的苏联代表团秘书长泽姆斯科夫。
