Title: Picture: Qíng Yún, Xú Yǎtáng
Author:
Date: 1959-05-11
Page: 3

    Picture

    Wheat has already headed,
    Thinking management yields no profit.
　　Only dreaming of a bumper harvest,
　　In the end, the wheat ears are like ants.

    Break through five barriers without giving up,
　　Enough water, ample fertilizer, and more fuel,
　　Detect pests and diseases and eliminate them promptly,
　　Ensure a great harvest on every mu.

    Qíng Yún (drawing), Xú Yǎtáng (poetry) (Originally published in Hebei Daily)
