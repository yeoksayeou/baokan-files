Title: Ending a Friendly Visit to Poland, Our Military Friendship Delegation Arrives in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1959-05-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 29th: At the invitation of the government and Ministry of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic, the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, headed by Marshal Peng Dehuai, arrived in Berlin on April 29th from Warsaw. Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included General Stoph, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic, and Lieutenant General Hoffmann, First Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of Staff of the National People's Army, among others.

　　General Stoph and Marshal Peng Dehuai spoke at the airport successively.

　　General Stoph first welcomed the delegation on behalf of the government and the Ministry of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic. He said that the friendly visit of the Chinese Military Delegation to the German Democratic Republic demonstrates the close and fraternal friendship between the peoples of our two countries.

　　Stoph expressed his gratitude to the Chinese comrades and friends for their help and support to the people of the German Democratic Republic in their struggle to conclude a peace treaty with Germany and to peacefully resolve the West Berlin issue. He also expressed his support for the just struggle of the Chinese people to liberate Taiwan and safeguard peace in the Far East.

　　Peng Dehuai said in his speech that the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army have always cherished deep friendship and respect for the people and army of the German Democratic Republic, who stand on the western front of the socialist camp. The 600 million Chinese people fully support the unremitting efforts made by the German Democratic Republic in building socialism, striving for the peaceful reunification of Germany, and defending European and world peace. The people and armies of China and Germany will always unite, cooperate closely, and advance shoulder to shoulder towards a common goal in the common cause of opposing imperialist war policies, defending world peace, and safeguarding the security of the socialist camp.

　　Peng Dehuai said that in 1957, Comrade Willi Stoph led a military delegation from the German Democratic Republic to visit our country, making valuable contributions to enhancing the friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Germany. ### 结束在波兰的友好访问  我军事友好代表团抵柏林

1959-05-01
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在波兰的友好访问
    我军事友好代表团抵柏林
    新华社柏林29日电　应德意志民主共和国政府和国防部的邀请，以彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团，4月29日从华沙到达柏林。前往机场欢迎的有德意志民主共和国副总理兼国防部长斯多夫上将、国防部第一副部长兼国家人民军参谋长霍夫曼中将等人。
    斯多夫上将和彭德怀元帅在机场上先后讲了话。
    斯多夫上将首先代表德意志民主共和国政府和国防部对代表团表示欢迎。他说，中国军事代表团到德意志民主共和国进行友好访问，表明了：我们两国人民之间的密切的兄弟般的友谊关系。
    斯多夫表示感谢中国同志和朋友在德意志民主共和国人民为争取缔结对德和约与和平解决西柏林问题的斗争中给予他们的帮助和支持。他同时表示支持中国人民为解放台湾和维护远东和平而进行的正义斗争。
    彭德怀在讲话中说，中国人民和中国人民解放军，对于站在社会主义阵营西方前线的德意志民主共和国人民和军队，一向怀着深厚的友情和敬意。对于德意志民主共和国为建设社会主义、争取和平统一德国、保卫欧洲和世界和平所进行的不懈努力，六亿多中国人民是全力支持的。中德两国人民和军队，在反对帝国主义的战争政策、保卫世界和平、保卫社会主义阵营安全的共同事业中，将永远团结一致，密切合作，向着共同目标并肩前进。
    彭德怀说，1957年，威利·斯多夫同志率领德意志民主共和国军事代表团访问了我国，对增进中德两国人民和军队间的友谊，作出了宝贵的贡献。这次中国军事友好代表团的访问，使我们有机会向德意志民主共和国国家人民军学习。我们相信通过这次访问，我们两国人民和军队之间的团结和友谊，必将进一步地巩固和发展。
    由彭德怀元帅率领的中国军事友好代表团是在29日上午乘飞机离开华沙，前往德意志民主共和国访问的。波兰人民军总政治部副主任伯德纳尔兹陪同代表团去柏林。
