Title: Gromyko Travels to the US to Attend Dulles' Funeral
Author:
Date: 1959-05-27
Page: 4

    Gromyko Travels to the US
    Preparing to Attend Dulles' Funeral
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 26th: TASS, Geneva, May 26th: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko flew to Washington this afternoon to attend the funeral of former U.S. Secretary of State Dulles.

### 葛罗米柯赴美  准备参加杜勒斯葬礼

1959-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯赴美
    准备参加杜勒斯葬礼
    新华社26日讯  塔斯社日内瓦26日讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯今天下午乘飞机前往华盛顿参加前美国国务卿杜勒斯的葬礼。
