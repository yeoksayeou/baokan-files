Title: Lesser Khingan Range
Author: Yan Chen
Date: 1959-05-22
Page: 8

    1
    Opening the pocket-sized map,
    I searched for the Lesser Khingan Range,
    That not-so-small blank space,
    What kind of enigma does it contain?
    I walked into the depths of the Lesser Khingan Range,
    Only then did I discover this was a new world,
    An endless sea of green trees,
    So rich and so magical!
    2
    Thanks to the forest survey team,
    The first to traverse the primeval forest,
    Giving new names to many mountain valleys:
    Meixi, Weilan, Dafeng, Changqing…
    Thanks to the geological exploration workers,
    Who traveled all the large and small mountains,
    Unveiling the secrets of the underground,
    Awakening every rock stratum.
    3
    In the morning I climb high and look out,
    Trees are above the clouds, clouds are above the trees,
    I don't see any lumberjacks there,
    Only hearing the sound of large trees falling echoing through the mountains.
    At night I climb high and look out at the sky, shining with fiery red light,
    Molten iron flows in the blast furnace,
    People and horses bustling around the blast furnace.
    4
    I love the folk songs of the Khingan Range:
    The trees here are money trees,
    The mountains here are treasure mountains,
    The rivers here are gold and silver rivers.
    Please allow me to add a sentence:
    The people here are heroes.
    It is precisely hundreds of thousands of pairs of hardworking hands,
    That have completely changed the desolate appearance.

### 小兴安岭

1959-05-22
第8版()
专栏：

    小兴安岭
　　严辰
    1
    打开袖珍本的地图，
    我把小兴安岭寻觅，
    那一片不小的空白，
    包涵了什么样的哑谜？
    我走进小兴安岭深处，
    才发现这是个新的天地，
    无穷无尽的绿色树海，
    那么丰富又那么神奇！
    2
    感谢森林调查队员，
    第一个踏遍了原始森林，
    给许多山沟取了新的名字：
    美溪、蔚蓝、大丰、常青……
    感谢地质勘探工作者，
    走遍了大大小小的山岭，
    揭穿了地下的秘密，
    把每一处岩层敲醒。
    3
    早晨我登高眺望，
    树在云上、云在树上，
    不见伐木工人在那里，
    只听得大树伐倒应山响。
    夜晚我登高眺望天空闪耀着通红的火光，
    高炉里铁水奔流，
    高炉边人马喧嚷。
    4
    我喜爱兴安岭的民歌：
    这里的树是摇钱树，
    这里的山是万宝山，
    这里的河是金银川。
    请容许我加上一句：
    这里的人是英雄汉。
    正是几十万双勤劳的手，
    才教荒凉的面貌彻底改观。
