Author:
Date: 1959-05-21
Page: 5

　　Cuba's Land Reform Law

On May 17, the Cuban Provisional Government's Council of Ministers promulgated the Land Reform Law, abolishing the latifundia system and distributing land to landless peasants. It stipulates that the maximum area of land that a natural or legal person can own is generally 30 caballerías (each caballería equals 13.43 hectares). Land exceeding this limit will be expropriated and distributed to landless peasants and agricultural workers. Land used for growing sugarcane, rice paddies, or animal husbandry can exceed this limit, but shall not exceed 100 caballerías at most. State-owned land will be distributed.

The Land Reform Law stipulates that any joint-stock company whose shares are not held by Cubans, or that does not have Cubans as its owners, shareholders, or managers, cannot operate sugarcane plantations. Non-Cuban citizens are not allowed to purchase any rural property. The Land Reform Law also points out that, up to now, nearly 75% of the fertile land is controlled by American companies, and these companies, on the land they control, do not actually recognize Cuban law.

The Land Reform Law stipulates that soldiers of the Cuban armed forces and their families, as well as the families of revolutionary martyrs, will have priority in obtaining land.

In this Land Reform Law, it is also recognized that property owners can obtain appropriate compensation after their land is expropriated. This compensation will be paid in the form of bonds with an annual interest rate of no more than 4% within twenty years.

The Land Reform Law stipulates that cases arising from land reform shall be tried by land courts.
　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 古巴的土地改革法

1959-05-21
第5版()
专栏：小资料

    古巴的土地改革法
    5月17日，古巴临时政府部长会议颁布了土地改革法，废除大庄园制度，将土地分配给无地农民。它规定，一个自然人或法人可拥有土地的最大面积一般为三十卡瓦耶里亚（每卡瓦耶里亚等于十三点四三公顷），超过这个限度的土地将被征用并且分配给无地农民和农业工人。种植甘蔗、稻田或用于畜牧业的土地可以多于这个限额，但最多亦不得超过一百卡瓦耶里亚。国有土地将进行分配。
    土地改革法规定，任何股份公司如果它的股票不是由古巴人持有，如果没有古巴人当它的业主、股东或管理者，就不能经营甘蔗园。非古巴公民一律不得购买任何农村产业。土地改革法还指出：到目前为止，近75％的肥沃土地掌握在美国公司手里，这些公司在它们掌握的土地上，实际上不承认古巴的法律。
    土地改革法规定，古巴武装部队的战士和他们的家属以及革命烈属，将有取得土地的优先权。
    在这个土地改革法中，还承认地产所有者在土地被征用后可以取得适当的赔偿，这种赔偿将以年息不超过4％的债券的形式在二十年内支付。
  土地改革法规定，由于土地改革而产生的案件，由土地法庭审理。
　（新华社）
