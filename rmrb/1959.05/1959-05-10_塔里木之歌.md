Title: Song of the Tarim
Author: Zhao Lixin
Date: 1959-05-10
Page: 5

　　Song of the Tarim
　　(I)
　　The Tarim River's water, green and lush,
　　Has flowed idly for centuries thus.
　　North and south shores, fertile land lie,
　　Wild grass grows into barren sky.
　　Vast earth, dark clouds loom,
　　Wind and sand, herdsmen in gloom.
　　Suddenly, a spring breeze blows at night,
　　Warm sun and clear sky reveal a verdant sight.
　　(II)
　　The Tarim River's water, connects with the sky,
　　Golden waves and silver blossoms, no end in sight,
　　Soldiers determined to build a farm,
　　Heroes gather, reminiscing when times were hard,
　　Scenery boundless on both shores,
　　Surpasses Nanniwan of yore,
　　Raise arms and shout, spring thunder roars,
　　Command the Gobi to transform to Jiangnan's shores.
　　(III)
　　The Tarim River's water, surging and high,
　　Heroes' enthusiasm soars to the sky,
　　Yellow and Yangtze Rivers, one step they stride,
　　Hands reach for the sun and moon, courage their guide,
　　Laugh at wind and rain as a sweat towel to dry,
　　Try to use the bright moon as a lamp in the sky,
　　Dig canals and fill earth, forty cubic meters they try,
　　Only then does the sun stretch its lazy sigh.
　　(IV)
　　The Tarim River's water, clear and bright,
　　Happiness flows endlessly with all its might,
　　North and south shores, different sights,
　　Red flags flutter, the years feel new and right,
　　Cars like flowing water, horses swift in their flight,
　　Wheat like golden mountains, cotton like silver so white,
　　Farm after farm, team after team, news of victory rings,
　　Cotton doubles, grain reaches a thousand jins.
                        Zhao Lixin

### 塔里木之歌
赵力心
1959-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

　　塔里木之歌
　　（一）
　　塔河水，绿油油，
　　千百年来空自流，
　　南北两岸好土地，
　　野草丛生成荒丘，
　　大地苍茫阴云暗，
　　风沙蔽日牧人愁，
　　忽然一夜东风起，
　　日暖天晴现绿洲。
　　（二）
　　塔河水，水连天，
　　金浪银花不见边，
　　战士决心建农场，
　　英雄会师忆当年，
　　两岸风光无限好，
　　胜似当年南泥湾，
　　振臂一呼春雷动，
　　命令戈壁变江南。
　　（三）
　　塔河水，浪滔滔，
　　英雄干劲冲云霄，
　　黄河长江一步跨，
　　手攀日月胆气豪，
　　笑将风雨作汗巾，
　　试将明月当灯照，
　　挖渠填土四十方，
　　方见太阳伸懒腰。
　　（四）
　　塔河水，水盈盈，
　　幸福源远流不尽，
　　南北两岸风景异，
　　红旗飘拂岁月新，
　　车如流水马如龙，
　　麦似金山棉似银，
　　场场队队传捷报，
　　棉花双百粮千斤。
                        赵力心
