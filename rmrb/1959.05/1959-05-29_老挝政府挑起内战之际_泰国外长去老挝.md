Title: As the Laotian Government Provokes Civil War, Thai Foreign Minister Visits Laos
Author:
Date: 1959-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th, Bangkok: Thai Foreign Minister Nai Thanat Khoman, at the invitation of the Royal Lao Government, left Bangkok by plane on the 27th for a visit to Laos.

The Thai Foreign Minister is visiting Laos at a time when the Royal Lao Government has launched an armed offensive against the Lao Patriotic Forces, thereby reigniting the civil war.

### 老挝政府挑起内战之际  泰国外长去老挝

1959-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝政府挑起内战之际
    泰国外长去老挝
    新华社28日讯　曼谷消息：泰国外交部长乃他纳·科曼应老挝王国政府的邀请，在27日乘飞机离开曼谷，前往老挝访问。
    泰国外交部长是在老挝王国政府向前寮国战斗部队发动武装进攻从而重新挑起内战的时候到老挝进行访问的。
