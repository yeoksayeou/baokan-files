Title: 100-meter Sidestroke: Xu Wangcai Creates New Record
Author:
Date: 1959-05-22
Page: 6
    100-meter Sidestroke
    Xu Wangcai Creates New Record
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 21st – Xu Wangcai, a 21-year-old male swimmer from Shanghai, broke his own national record of 1 minute 10.5 seconds for the third time in the men's 100-meter sidestroke final of the Shanghai Second Municipal Games on the 20th. On this day, he created the national best record of 1 minute 9.6 seconds.
    Xu Wangcai's time when he first broke this national record in February this year was 1 minute 10.2 seconds. On May 19, in the preliminary round of this games, his time was 1 minute 10.4 seconds, breaking his own national record for the second time.
    Zhou Yongqi, a female swimmer from Shanghai, created a new national record of 2 minutes 57.7 seconds in the women's 200-meter backstroke final, a new event held at this games.
    Since the beginning of this year, male and female swimmers from all over the country have broken eleven national swimming records. Among them, seven national records were broken by Shanghai swimmers.

### 百公尺侧泳  徐旺彩创新成绩

1959-05-22
第6版()
专栏：

    百公尺侧泳
    徐旺彩创新成绩
    新华社上海21日电　上海二十一岁的男子游泳选手徐旺彩，20日在上海市第二届运动会男子一百公尺侧泳决赛中，第三次打破了他自己保持的一分十秒五的全国纪录。这天他创造了全国最高成绩——一分九秒六。
    徐旺彩今年2月第一次打破这项全国纪录时的成绩是一分十秒二，5月19日在这次运动会的预赛中，他的成绩是一分十秒四，第二次打破了自己保持的全国纪录。
    上海女子游泳选手周咏琪在这次运动会举行的一个新项目——女子二百公尺仰泳决赛中，创造了二分五十七秒七的全国新成绩。
    今年以来，全国各地的男、女游泳运动员已经打破了十一项游泳全国纪录。其中有七项全国纪录是上海游泳选手打破的。
