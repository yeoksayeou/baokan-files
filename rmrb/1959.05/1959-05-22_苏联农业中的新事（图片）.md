Title: New Developments in Soviet Agriculture (Photos)
Author: Ao. Kuzmin Ye. Shulepov A. Batanov
Date: 1959-05-22
Page: 5

Soviet Agriculture:

    Vegetable hotbeds with plastic covers
    The Lyubertsy State Farm in the southeastern suburbs of Moscow uses hotbeds to cultivate vegetable seedlings, which are covered with protective covers made of plastic. These plastic covers not only replace glass for cold protection but also have other uses. The picture shows female farm workers watering tomatoes and cabbage grown on hotbeds using plastic covers.
    Photo by Ao. Kuzmin, TASS draft (Xinhua News Agency)

    Cultivating high-oil sunflowers
    The high-oil sunflower varieties jointly cultivated by the famous Soviet breeder Vasily Pustovoit and other Soviet scientists have been widely adopted in the Soviet Union, accounting for 75% of the total planting area. This high-oil sunflower can yield 3 tons per hectare, with an oil yield of 52.7%. The picture shows Vasily Pustovoit (left) inspecting the planting of high-oil sunflowers.
    Photo by Ye. Shulepov, TASS draft (Xinhua News Agency)

    Using radioactive isotopes to increase egg production of poultry
    Scientific staff at the Institute of Evolutionary Genetics of the Soviet Academy of Sciences are using radioactive isotopes to study the organisms of chickens and other poultry to increase their egg production. The picture shows scientists preparing to inject chickens with preparations of radioactive isotopes.
    Photo by A. Batanov, TASS draft (Xinhua News Agency)

### 苏联农业中的新事（图片）
奥·库兹明  叶·舒列波夫  阿·巴坦诺夫
1959-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联农业中的新事
    用塑料护盖蔬菜温床
    莫斯科东南郊区柳别尔茨的国营农场，用温床培育蔬菜幼苗，这种温床是用由塑料制作的防护盖覆盖着的。这种塑料防护盖不但可以代替玻璃起防寒作用，而且还有其他用途。图是农场女工正在给使用塑料防护盖的温床上种的蕃茄和白菜喷水。
    奥·库兹明摄　塔斯社稿（新华社发）
    培育多油脂向日葵
    由著名的苏联育种家瓦西里·普斯托沃伊特和其他苏联科学家共同培育的多油脂向日葵的品种，在苏联被广泛采用，已占总播种面积的75％。这种多油脂向日葵每公顷可收获三十公担，出油率达52.7％。图是瓦西里·普斯托沃伊特（左）在视察多油脂向日葵的播种情况。
    叶·舒列波夫摄　塔斯社稿（新华社发）
    利用放射性同位素  提高家禽的产卵量
    苏联科学院遗传进化学研究所的科学工作人员利用放射性同位素研究鸡和其他家禽的机体，以便提高它们的产卵量。图是科学工作者正在准备给鸡注射放射性同位素的制剂。
      阿·巴坦诺夫摄　塔斯社稿（新华社发）
