Title: Shenyang Celebrates "May Fourth" with Grand Ceremony, Resolves to Carry Forward Revolutionary Tradition and Launch Increase Production and Practice Economy Campaign
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, May 2nd: More than 20,000 people from all walks of life in Shenyang City gathered solemnly today to commemorate the 40th anniversary of the "May Fourth" Movement.

Li Huang, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke at the meeting, calling on the people of the city, especially young people from all walks of life, to carry forward the glorious revolutionary tradition of the "May Fourth" Movement. Under the leadership of the Party, they should go all out, overcome all difficulties, launch a vigorous campaign to increase production and practice economy, vigorously promote technical innovation and technical revolution, strengthen learning, continuously improve their political, cultural, and technical levels, and outstandingly complete this year's production and construction plans centered on the four major indicators.

Afterwards, representatives of people from all walks of life in Shenyang City expressed their determination to the Party at the meeting. Representatives from all circles expressed their utmost indignation at the acts of imperialism and Indian expansionists in interfering in China's internal affairs.

Finally, seven hundred young people performed a collective recitation at the conference, expressing the firm will of the people of the city to advance bravely in the direction pointed out by the Party. Various wonderful sports performances were held after the meeting.

### 沈阳各界隆重纪念“五四”  决心发扬革命传统开展增产节约运动

1959-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    沈阳各界隆重纪念“五四”
    决心发扬革命传统开展增产节约运动
    据新华社沈阳2日电　沈阳市各界人民两万多人今天隆重集会，纪念“五四”运动四十周年。
    中共辽宁省委书记李荒在大会上讲话，号召全市人民尤其是各界青年发扬“五四”运动的光荣革命传统，在党的领导下，鼓足冲天干劲，战胜一切困难，开展轰轰烈烈的增产节约运动，大闹技术革新和技术革命，加强学习，不断提高政治文化技术水平，出色地完成今年以四大指标为中心的各项生产建设计划。
    接着，沈阳市各界人民代表在会上向党表示决心。各界代表都对帝国主义和印度扩张主义分子干涉我国内政的行为表示万分愤怒。
    大会最后由七百名青年集体大朗诵，表达了全市人民朝着党指引的方向奋勇前进的坚强意志。会后举行了各种精彩的体育表演。
