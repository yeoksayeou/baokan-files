Title: Speaker of the Indian Lok Sabha Sends Congratulations to Chairman Zhu De
Author:
Date: 1959-05-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The telegram of congratulations from the Speaker of the Indian Lok Sabha, Ayengar, to Chairman Zhu De is as follows:

To His Excellency Marshal Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China:

Please accept my sincerest congratulations on your election as Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of The People’ Republic Of China .

### 印度人民院议长  电贺朱德委员长

1959-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度人民院议长
    电贺朱德委员长
    新华社8日讯　印度人民院议长阿延加尔给朱德委员长的贺电，文如下：中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德元帅阁下：
    在阁下当选为中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长的时候，请接受我的诚挚的祝贺。
                                  阿南塔亚萨纳姆·阿延加尔
