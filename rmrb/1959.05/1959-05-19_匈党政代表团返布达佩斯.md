Title: Hungarian Party and Government Delegation Returns to Budapest
Author:
Date: 1959-05-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, May 17th: The Hungarian Party and Government Delegation, headed by Munnich, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Prime Minister of the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, returned here on the 17th after visiting China, Vietnam, Korea, and Mongolia.

Welcoming them at the airport were Kadar, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, and Political Bureau members Kallai, Ronai, Fock, Biszku, Somogyi, and other party and government leaders, as well as more than 8,000 residents of the capital. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Hungary also came to welcome them.

### 匈党政代表团返布达佩斯

1959-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    匈党政代表团返布达佩斯
    据新华社布达佩斯17日电　以匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员、工农革命政府总理明尼赫为首的匈牙利党政代表团在中国、越南、朝鲜和蒙古访问后，于17日回到这里。
    到机场欢迎的有匈牙利社会主义工人党中央第一书记卡达尔、政治局委员卡拉伊、罗奈伊、福克、比斯库、绍莫吉等党和政府领导人以及八千多名首都居民。各国驻匈牙利的外交使节也前往欢迎。
