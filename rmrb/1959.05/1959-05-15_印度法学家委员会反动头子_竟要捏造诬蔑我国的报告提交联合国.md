Title: Reactionary Ringleader of the Indian Commission of Jurists Actually Fabricates and Slanders Our Country in a Report to be Submitted to the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1959-05-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 14th, New Delhi dispatch: Purshottam Trikamdas, Secretary-General of the Indian Committee of the "International Commission of Jurists," announced in New Delhi on the 12th that he had, at the request of this "Commission," fabricated a report slandering China regarding so-called "suppression of human rights and lack of rule of law in Tibet."

This "Commission" is a non-governmental organization with consultative status at the United Nations. It played an ugly role in fabricating a report slandering Hungary after the counter-revolutionary riots in Hungary in 1956.

Trikamdas said that he will fly to Geneva on May 24 to personally submit this "report" slandering China and interfering in China's internal affairs to the "Commission."

In order to cover up his fabrication tricks, this lawyer said that the "report" was written based on materials collected "from Tibet and other sources." According to a report in the "Times of India" on the 12th, the Indian Commission of Jurists has sent people to "collect evidence on the Tibet incident from various sources." The newspaper also said that this "report" will be submitted to the United Nations by the "International Commission of Jurists."

### 印度法学家委员会反动头子  竟要捏造诬蔑我国的报告提交联合国

1959-05-15
第5版()
专栏：

    印度法学家委员会反动头子
    竟要捏造诬蔑我国的报告提交联合国
    新华社14日讯　新德里消息：“国际法学家委员会”印度委员会秘书长普绍坦·特里孔达斯12日在新德里宣布，他已经应这个“委员会”的要求，制造了一份关于所谓“在西藏压制人权和没有法制”的诬蔑中国的报告。
    这个“委员会”是在联合国有咨询资格的非政府机构，它曾经在1956年匈牙利反革命暴乱发生后制造过一份诬蔑匈牙利的报告，从而扮演了一个丑恶的角色。
    特里孔达斯说，他将在5月24日飞往日内瓦，亲自向这个“委员会”提出这个诬蔑中国、干涉中国内政的“报告”。
    这个律师为了掩饰他的捏造伎俩，说这个“报告”是根据“从西藏和其他方面”所搜集的材料写成的。据“印度时报”12日消息说，印度的法学家委员会已经派人“从各个方面搜集关于西藏事件的证据”。这家报纸还说，这份“报告”将由“国际法学家委员会”提交联合国。
