Title: Panchen Erdeni Leaves Beijing for Tibet; Vice Premier Deng Xiaoping and Over a Thousand People from All Walks of Life See Him Off at the Station
Author:
Date: 1959-05-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 25th. Panchen Erdeni Chökyi Gyaltsen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region of Tibet, and his accompanying officials left Beijing by special train this morning to return to Tibet.

Vice Premiers of the State Council Deng Xiaoping and Li Xiannian, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, and Li Weihan, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Li Siguang, Central Representative to Tibet Zhang Jingwu, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region Tan Guansan, and over a thousand people from all walks of life in the capital warmly saw them off at the Beijing Railway Station.

Xie Hechou, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, accompanied Panchen Erdeni to Xining on the special train today.

Today, the Beijing Railway Station was decorated with lanterns and colored streamers. Huge banners written in both Chinese and Tibetan were hung on the platform, reading "Farewell to Panchen Erdeni Chökyi Gyaltsen," "Thoroughly Quell the Rebellion in Tibet and Safeguard the Unification of the Motherland," "Build a New Democratic and Socialist Tibet," "Long Live the People's Republic of China," "Long Live the Chinese Communist Party," and "Long Live Chairman Mao, the Great Leader of All Ethnic Groups."

At 10:50 a.m., when Deng Xiaoping and other state leaders walked onto the platform with Panchen Erdeni, enthusiastic applause erupted from the farewell crowd. Children of the capital presented flowers to Panchen Erdeni and his accompanying officials.

Before boarding the train, Panchen Erdeni and his accompanying officials presented *hadas* to Deng Xiaoping and other state leaders, who reciprocated with *hadas*. At 11:00 a.m., the special train slowly started amidst thunderous applause and music. Panchen Erdeni stood at the train door for a long time, constantly waving bouquets to bid farewell to the people who came to see him off.

Among those who saw them off at the station today were also members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Wang Weizhou, Xing Xiping, and Zhang Su; members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the CPPCC Xu Deheng, Cai Tingkai, Shi Liang, and Xu Guangping; Chairman of the National People's Congress Nationalities Committee Liu Geping and Vice Chairman Xie Fumin; Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council Qi Yanming; ministers and vice ministers of various ministries Chen Qiyuan, Xu Zirong, Wang Lei, Lü Zhengcao, Wang Shoudao, Xia Yan, Wei Que, and Li Dequan; Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Wang Feng, Sa Kongliao, and Yang Jingren; Director of the Government Offices Administration Bureau of the State Council Gao Dengbang; Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han; President of the Chinese Buddhist Association Xirao Gyatso, Vice Presidents Galazang, Zhao Puchu, Zhou Shujia, and Neng Hai; Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association Da Pucheng; Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing Dingjia · Dorje Jicun, Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing Sunge Badun, Deputy Director Lian Huizhu, Deputy Director of the Beijing Office of the CPC Tibet Working Committee Gu Ruiting, and Tibetan people in Beijing. ### 班禅额尔德尼离京返藏  邓小平副总理等和各界千余人到车站欢送

1959-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼离京返藏
    邓小平副总理等和各界千余人到车站欢送
    新华社25日讯  全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和他的随行官员今天上午乘专车离北京回西藏。
    国务院副总理邓小平、李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、黄炎培、李维汉，国防委员会副主席张治中，政协全国委员会副主席李四光，中央驻西藏代表张经武，西藏军区政治委员谭冠三，和首都各界一千多人到北京车站热烈欢送。
    民族事务委员会副主任谢鹤筹今天一同乘专车陪送班禅额尔德尼去西宁。
    今天北京车站上悬灯结彩，月台上挂着“欢送班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞”、“彻底平定西藏叛乱，维护祖国统一”、“建设民主和社会主义新西藏”、“中华人民共和国万岁”、“中国共产党万岁”、“各族人民伟大领袖毛主席万岁”等汉藏两种文字书写的巨幅标语。
    十时五十分，邓小平等国家领导人同班禅额尔德尼走进车站月台时，欢送队伍里响起热烈的掌声，首都少年儿童向班禅额尔德尼和他的随行官员献花。
    班禅额尔德尼和他的随行官员在上车前向邓小平等国家领导人献了哈达，邓小平等回敬了哈达。十一时，专车在雷动掌声和乐曲声中徐徐开动，班禅额尔德尼久久伫立在车门前，向欢送的人们不断挥动花束告别。
    今天到车站欢送的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员王维舟、邢西萍、张苏，政协全国委员会常务委员许德珩、蔡廷锴、史良、许广平，全国人民代表大会民族委员会主任委员刘格平、副主任委员谢扶民，国务院副秘书长齐燕铭，各部部长和副部长陈其瑗、徐子荣、王磊、吕正操、王首道、夏衍、韦悫、李德全，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、萨空了、杨静仁，国务院机关事务管理局局长高登榜。北京市副市长吴晗，中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错、副会长噶喇藏、赵朴初、周叔迦、能海，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生，达赖驻京办事处处长定甲·多吉吉村、班禅驻京办事处处长孙格巴顿、副处长联慧珠、中共西藏工委驻京办事处副处长顾瑞亭和在京的藏族人士，都到车站欢送。（附图片）
