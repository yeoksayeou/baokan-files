Title: Snow Behind the Mountains (and another poem)
Author: Zang Kejia
Date: 1959-05-09
Page: 8

　　Snow Behind the Mountains (and another poem)
　　Zang Kejia
　　The snow behind the mountains,
　　Will melt when it sees the sun,
　　The barbarous serf system,
　　Can we let it remain intact?
　　On the pure white mother,
　　Scars must be completely removed,
　　The earth is full of light,
　　We cannot leave a black hole!
　　I am already over fifty years old,
　　And I still clearly remember things from my childhood,
　　"Tibetans and Han are brothers" was written in elementary school textbooks,
　　The snow-capped Himalayas are towering in the distance.
　　Tibet, you are in the distant borderlands of the motherland,
　　Casting a brand new image in everyone's heart;
　　From the voices of all ethnic groups,
　　I hear a solemn and harmonious grand symphony.

### 山后的雪（外一章）
臧克家
1959-05-09
第8版()
专栏：

　　山后的雪（外一章）
　　臧克家
　　山后的雪，
　　见了太阳要消融，
　　野蛮的农奴制，
　　能让它原封不动？
　　洁白的母体上，
　　疮疤要去净，
　　大地上一片光明，
　　不能留个黑窟窿！
　　我已经五十多岁，
　　还清楚地记得儿时的事，
　　“藏族、汉族是兄弟”写在小学课本上，
　　头戴雪帽的喜马拉雅山高高在望。
　　西藏，你在祖国辽远的边疆，
　　在人人心上投下个崭新的形象；
　　从各民族的声音里，
　　我听到了庄严和谐的大乐章。
