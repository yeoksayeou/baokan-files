Title: Gromyko Arrives in Washington
Author:
Date: 1959-05-28
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, May 27th. TASS Washington, May 27th: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko arrived in Washington today to attend the funeral of former US Secretary of State Dulles.
    Gromyko told reporters at the airport: "I came to the United States to attend the funeral of former Secretary of State Dulles. I have known Mr. Dulles for a long time—about fifteen years. Of course, we had disagreements on many issues, including important international issues. However, it cannot be denied that Mr. Dulles was an outstanding diplomat."

### 葛罗米柯到华盛顿

1959-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯到华盛顿
    新华社27日讯  塔斯社华盛顿27日讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯今天到达了华盛顿，参加美国前国务卿杜勒斯的葬礼。
    葛罗米柯在机场上向记者们发表讲话说：“我到美国来是为了参加前国务卿杜勒斯的葬礼。我和杜勒斯先生相识已有很长的时间——大约有十五年。当然，在很多问题上，其中包括重要的国际问题，我们之间存在着分歧。但是，不能不认为，杜勒斯先生是一位杰出的外交家”。
