Title: Comrade Lukman Leaves Beijing for Home; Comrade Deng Xiaoping and Others See Him Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-05-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Comrade Lukman, First Deputy General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, concluded his twenty-six-day visit to China and left Beijing by plane today.

Those who went to the airport to see him off were: Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee, and others.

Supit, member of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, Bille, member of the Indonesian Communist Party's parliament, and Siti Ketabat, who came to China with Comrade Lukman for a visit, left Beijing for home on May 4.

### 鲁克曼同志离京回国  邓小平同志等到机场欢送

1959-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    鲁克曼同志离京回国
    邓小平同志等到机场欢送
    新华社16日讯　印度尼西亚共产党第一副总书记鲁克曼结束了在我国历时二十六天的参观访问，今天乘飞机离开北京。
    到机场欢送的有：中共中央总书记邓小平，中央书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员伍修权等。
    同鲁克曼一道前来我国参观访问的印度尼西亚共产党中央委员苏皮特、印度尼西亚共产党国会议员比勒，以及西蒂·克打巴第等三人，已经在5月4日离开北京回国。
