Title: Vice Premier Luo Ruiqing Encourages Activists Among Families of Martyrs, Military Dependents, and Disabled, Demobilized, Retired, and Transferred Servicemen: Forever Be Advanced Producers in National Construction

Author: 
Date: 1959-05-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — Vice Premier Luo Ruiqing today, on behalf of the State Council, extended warm congratulations to the National Congress of Socialist Construction Activists of Families of Martyrs, Military Dependents, and Disabled, Demobilized, Retired, and Transferred Servicemen, and delivered a speech.

Comrade Luo Ruiqing said that the families of martyrs, military dependents, and disabled, demobilized, retired, and transferred servicemen in our country are a very large force. Under the leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao, this force has played a tremendous role in our country's revolution and construction, and will play an even greater role in the future.

He then said to the attendees that the Party and the state attach great importance to you, and the people of the whole country respect you very much, not only because your children or husbands, and you yourselves, have made contributions to the revolution and the people in the past, but also because you are outstanding members of the people.

Comrade Luo Ruiqing hoped that families of martyrs, military dependents, and disabled, demobilized, retired, and transferred servicemen will always remember the instructions of the Party and Chairman Mao: "Carry forward the revolutionary tradition and strive for greater glory." He said, what is our revolutionary tradition? That is: always listen to the Party and Chairman Mao, maintain close ties with the masses, actively participate in labor, continuously improve political awareness and cultural level, and always play a leading role, backbone role, and bridge role among the masses, and strive to the end for the cause of socialism and communism. What is greater glory? That is: to always be an advanced producer and advanced defender of socialist construction, constantly create new model deeds, and, under the leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao, together with the people of the whole country, build our country into a great socialist country with modern industry, modern agriculture, and modern culture and science as soon as possible.

The representatives present repeatedly applauded warmly, welcoming Comrade Luo Ruiqing's speech and thanking the State Council for their concern.

The congress began its plenary session speeches today.

### 罗瑞卿副总理勉励烈属军属和残废、复员、退伍、转业军人积极分子  永远做祖国建设的先进生产者

1959-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    罗瑞卿副总理勉励烈属军属和残废、复员、退伍、转业军人积极分子
    永远做祖国建设的先进生产者
    新华社20日讯　罗瑞卿副总理今天在全国烈属、军属和残废、复员、退伍、转业军人社会主义建设积极分子大会上代表国务院向大会致以热烈的祝贺，并讲了话。
    罗瑞卿同志说，我们国家的烈属、军属和残废、复员、退伍、转业军人，是一支很大的力量。这一支力量，在党和毛主席的领导下，对于我国的革命和建设事业，起了巨大的作用，今后还要起更大的作用。
    他接着向到会人员说，党和国家十分重视你们，全国人民非常尊敬你们，不仅是因为你们的子弟或丈夫，以及你们自己过去曾经为革命为人民立了功勋，而且因为你们是人民中的优秀分子。
    罗瑞卿同志希望烈属、军属和残废、复员、退伍、转业军人要永远记住党和毛主席的指示：“发扬革命传统，争取更大光荣”。他说，什么是我们的革命传统呢？这就是：永远听党和毛主席的话，密切联系群众，积极参加劳动，不断提高政治觉悟和文化水平，要在群众中始终起带头作用、骨干作用和桥梁作用，为社会主义、共产主义事业奋斗到底。什么是更大光荣呢？这就是：永远做一个社会主义建设的先进生产者和先进保卫者，不断创造新的模范事迹，在党和毛主席的领导下，同全国人民一起，把我们的国家，尽快地建设成为一个具有现代工业、现代农业和现代文化科学的伟大的社会主义国家。
    到会的代表不断地热烈鼓掌，欢迎罗瑞卿同志的讲话，感谢国务院对他们的关怀。
    大会在今天开始进行大会发言。
