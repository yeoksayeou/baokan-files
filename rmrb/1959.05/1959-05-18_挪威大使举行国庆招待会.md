Title: Norwegian Ambassador Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1959-05-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Norwegian Ambassador to China, Mr. Erik Dons, held a reception this afternoon to celebrate Norwegian National Day.
    Our Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Luo Guibo, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were invited to attend the reception.

### 挪威大使举行国庆招待会

1959-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威大使举行国庆招待会
    新华社17日讯　挪威驻华大使依里克·唐斯今天下午举行招待会，庆祝挪威国庆。
    我国外交部副部长罗贵波等和各国驻华使节应邀出席了招待会。
