Title: With the help of Chinese experts, the Hodeidah-Sanaa Highway in Yemen Begins Construction
Author:
Date: 1959-05-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hodeidah, 12th: With the help of Chinese experts, the Hodeidah-Sanaa Highway project in Yemen has begun on a large scale.

　　The subgrade construction of the section from Hodeidah to Bajil in the Tihama Shamo area along the Red Sea, which is twenty-seven kilometers long, has been completed. At the same time, many Chinese drilling machines are working intensely in the Halas mountain area, the most difficult part of the middle section of this highway project.

### 在我国专家帮助下  也门的荷—萨公路动工

1959-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

    在我国专家帮助下
    也门的荷—萨公路动工
    新华社荷台达12日电　也门的荷台达—萨那公路工程在中国专家帮助下，已经大规模动工。
    从荷台达到红海沿岸蒂哈马沙莫区的巴吉勒一段，有二十七公里的路基工程已经完成。同时，许多中国的钻孔机正在这条公路中段工程最艰巨的哈拉斯高山地区紧张地工作着。
