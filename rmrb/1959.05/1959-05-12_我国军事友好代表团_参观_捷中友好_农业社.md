Title: Chinese Military Friendship Delegation Visits "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship" Agricultural Cooperative
Author:
Date: 1959-05-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 10th. The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Marshal Peng Dehuai visited the "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship" Unified Agricultural Cooperative in the village of Vinařice on the 10th. The delegation received a warm welcome from the cooperative members there.

　　Representatives of the cooperative, in the most solemn ceremony, wearing national costumes, presented bread and salt to Marshal Peng Dehuai. Accompanied by the chairman of the cooperative, the village head, and the village Party branch secretary, the delegation visited the crops planted by the cooperative, the newly built pig houses, cattle houses, etc., and also had close conversations with the cooperative members.

　　Marshal Peng Dehuai also handed over a letter and some crop seeds from the China-Czechoslovakia Friendship People's Commune to the chairman of the cooperative, and presented gifts on behalf of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation.

### 我国军事友好代表团  参观“捷中友好”农业社

1959-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    我国军事友好代表团
    参观“捷中友好”农业社
    新华社布拉格10日电　以彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团10日参观了维纳尔日采村的“捷中友好”统一农业合作社。在那里，代表团受到了社员们的隆重欢迎。
  合作社的代表以最隆重的礼节，穿着民族服装把面包和盐献给了彭德怀元帅。代表团在合作社主席、村长和村党支书等的陪同下参观了合作社种的庄稼、新建的养猪房、养牛房等，并且还同社员们进行了亲密的交谈。
    彭德怀元帅还向合作社主席转交了中捷友好人民公社的一封信和一些农作物的种子，并且代表中国军事友好代表团赠送了礼物。
