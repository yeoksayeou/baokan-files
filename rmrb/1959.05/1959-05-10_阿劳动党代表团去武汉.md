Title: Albanian Labor Party Delegation Visits Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1959-05-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 9th: The Albanian Labor Party delegation concluded its visit to Guangzhou and departed by special plane this morning for Wuhan. Those who saw them off at the airport included Wang De, Alternate Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CCP, Liu Tianfu, member of the Provincial Committee and Vice Governor, and Sun Leyi, member of the Municipal Committee and Vice Mayor.

During its stay in Guangzhou, the delegation visited the site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute formerly run by Comrade Mao Zedong, the 1959 Spring China Export Commodities Fair, toured Huanghuagang, the Guangzhou Zoo, and the Guangzhou Sports Club, paid respects at the Guangzhou Martyrs' Cemetery, and laid a wreath at the martyrs' tomb.

### 阿劳动党代表团去武汉

1959-05-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　阿劳动党代表团去武汉
    新华社广州9日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团结束了在广州的访问，今天早晨乘专机前往武汉。到机场欢送的有中共广东省委书记处候补书记、中共广州市委第一书记王德，省委常委、副省长刘田夫和市委常委、副市长孙乐宜等。
    代表团在广州期间，曾参观了毛泽东同志当年主办的农民运动讲习所的旧址、1959年春季中国出口商品交易会，游览了黄花岗、广州动物园、广州体育俱乐部，瞻仰了广州烈士陵园，并在烈士陵墓前献了花圈。
