Title: Party and State Leaders Receive Representatives of Four Conferences
Author:
Date: 1959-05-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 21st – Party and state leaders Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, Zhu De, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Lin Biao, Deng Xiaoping, Lin Boqu, Peng Zhen, Li Fuchun, Bo Yibo, Lu Dingyi, and others, this afternoon received all the representatives attending the Second Advanced Units and Activists Representative Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Logistics Work, the National Congress of Socialist Construction Activists of Revolutionary Martyrs’ Families, Military Families, Disabled, Demobilized, Retired and Transferred Soldiers, and the National Congress of Advanced Workers of Public Security, Procuratorate and Judicial Organs, as well as all personnel attending the All-Army Cadre Work Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. (Attached image)
    Party and state leaders receiving representatives of the four conferences. The picture shows the scene during the reception.
　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo photographed

### 党和国家领导人接见四个会议代表

1959-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    党和国家领导人接见四个会议代表
    新华社21日讯　党和国家的领导人刘少奇、宋庆龄、董必武、朱德、周恩来、陈云、林彪、邓小平、林伯渠、彭真、李富春、薄一波、陆定一等，今天下午接见了出席中国人民解放军第二次后勤工作先进单位和积极分子代表会议，全国烈属、军属和残废、复员、退伍、转业军人社会主义建设积极分子大会和全国公安检察司法先进工作者大会的全体代表，以及出席中国人民解放军全军干部工作会议的全体人员。（附图片）
