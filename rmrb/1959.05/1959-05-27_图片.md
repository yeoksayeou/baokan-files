    The upper picture shows commune members of Xinglong People's Commune in Yangzhong County, Jiangsu Province, harvesting wheat (photographed by Ma Weiqun, draft from Xinhua Daily). Commune members of Yuejin People's Commune in Yuechi County, Sichuan Province, are transplanting rice seedlings using the "directed close planting strip planting" method (photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Qicai). Youth shock brigade members of Youlan People's Commune in Nanchang County, Jiangxi Province, are weeding rice (photographed by Du Xianyao, draft from Jiangxi Youth Daily). Commune members of the Maitang Sixth Team of Lehui People's Commune in Qionghai County, Hainan Island, are transporting the abundant rice harvest to the state granary (photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Fubei).
