Title: End of Visit to Indonesia: Chinese Military Friendship Delegation Holds Farewell Reception; Indonesian Acting President Hosts Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1959-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 13th - The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, led by General Yang Chengwu, held a reception here tonight to express gratitude to the Indonesian people and armed forces for their warm hospitality and to bid them farewell.

Present at the reception were: Lieutenant General Gatot Soebroto, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Army representing the Indonesian Army Chief of Staff; Vice Admiral Soebyakto, Chief of Staff of the Navy; Air Vice-Marshal Soerjadarma, Chief of Staff of the Air Force; Hidayat, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Defense; Mansjur, Chairman of the National Assembly's Defense Group; and other senior military and government officials, members of the National Assembly, members of the National Council, and more than 200 people including diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries stationed in Indonesia. Huang Zhen, the Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, also attended the reception.

General Yang Chengwu said at the reception that the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation came to Indonesia with the friendship of the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and with the sincere desire to learn from the Indonesian people. Now, the delegation will return home with the friendship of the Indonesian people and the Indonesian armed forces, as well as their valuable experience. He expressed his heartfelt thanks to the Indonesian President, Acting President, and other military and government leaders, as well as the Indonesian armed forces, for their warm reception.

General Yang Chengwu pointed out that the armed forces of the two countries bear the glorious tasks of defending national independence, opposing imperialist and colonialist aggression, and especially liberating the lands still under imperialist occupation. All of this enables the people and armed forces of the two countries to be united in spirit, and no force can prevent the friendship between them.

Hidayat, Secretary-General of the Indonesian Ministry of Defense, spoke on behalf of the Indonesian Minister of Defense. He said that the visit of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to Indonesia had enhanced the understanding between the people and armed forces of the two countries, thereby further strengthening and consolidating the friendship between the two countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 13th - Indonesian Acting President Sardjono held a banquet today to entertain the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by General Yang Chengwu.

Huang Zhen, the Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, attended the banquet.

On the Indonesian side, those present at the banquet included Vice Admiral Soebyakto, Chief of Staff of the Navy; Vice Marshal Soerjadarma, Chief of Staff of the Air Force; Colonel Hidayat, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Defense; Mansjur, Chairman of the National Assembly's Defense Group, and his group members, as well as many senior military officers. Lieutenant General Nasution, Chief of Staff of the Army, was inspecting Sulawesi and did not attend the banquet.

Speaking at the luncheon, Acting President Sardjono, on behalf of the Indonesian people and armed forces, welcomed the visit of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to Indonesia. He said that this visit would help strengthen the friendship between the people and armed forces of the two countries.

General Yang Chengwu expressed his heartfelt thanks to the Acting President for arranging this luncheon for the delegation. He said that the honor given to the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation was also an honor given to the Chinese people and armed forces.

Acting President Sardjono and General Yang Chengwu exchanged gifts at the meeting.

### 结束在印度尼西亚的访问  我军事友好代表团举行告别招待会  印度尼西亚代理总统曾宴请代表团

1959-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在印度尼西亚的访问
    我军事友好代表团举行告别招待会
    印度尼西亚代理总统曾宴请代表团
    新华社雅加达13日电　中国军事友好代表团团长杨成武上将今天晚上在这里举行招待会，感谢印度尼西亚人民和武装部队的热情款待，并且向他们告别。
    出席招待会的有：印度尼西亚陆军参谋长的代表、陆军副参谋长加托特·苏布罗托少将，海军参谋长苏比雅克托海军中将，空军参谋长苏里亚达马空军中将，国防部秘书长希达雅特，国会国防小组主席曼休尔和其他高级军政官员、国会议员、民族委员会委员等，以及各国驻印度尼西亚外交使节和大使馆武官等二百多人。中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇也出席了招待会。
    杨成武上将在招待会上说，中国军事友好代表团是带着中国人民和中国人民解放军的友谊并且抱着向印度尼西亚人民学习的真诚愿望来到印度尼西亚的。现在代表团将带着印度尼西亚人民和印度尼西亚武装部队的友谊以及他们宝贵的经验回国。他对印度尼西亚总统、代理总统和其他军政领袖以及印度尼西亚武装部队的热烈款待，表示衷心的感谢。
    杨成武上将指出，两国的武装部队肩负着保卫民族独立、反对帝国主义和殖民主义的侵略，特别是解放仍然在帝国主义占领下的土地的光荣任务。所有这些使得两国人民和武装部队能够在精神上团结一致，任何力量都不能阻止他们之间的友谊。
    印度尼西亚国防部秘书长希达雅特代表印度尼西亚国防部长发表了谈话。他说，中国军事友好代表团对印度尼西亚的访问，增进了两国人民和两国武装部队之间的了解，从而进一步加强和巩固了两国之间的友谊。
    新华社雅加达13日电　印度尼西亚代理总统沙多诺今天举行宴会，招待以杨成武上将为首的中国军事友好代表团。
    中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇出席了宴会。
    印度尼西亚方面出席宴会的有海军参谋长苏比雅克托海军中将、空军参谋长苏里亚达马中将、国防部秘书长希达雅特上校、国会国防小组主席曼休尔和他的组员以及许多高级军官。陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安中将正在苏拉威西视察而没有出席宴会。
    代理总统沙多诺在午宴上讲话时，代表印度尼西亚人民和武装部队，对中国军事友好代表团的访问印度尼西亚表示欢迎。他说，这次访问将有助于加强两国人民和武装部队之间的友谊。
    杨成武上将表示衷心感谢代理总统为代表团安排的这次午宴。他说，给予中国军事友好代表团的这种荣誉也是给予中国人民和武装部队的荣誉。
    沙多诺代理总统和杨成武上将在会上互相交换了礼物。
