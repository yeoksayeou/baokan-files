Title: Visiting the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe: Chinese Self-Funded Travelers Depart

Author:

Date: 1959-05-28
Page: 4

Visiting the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe
Chinese Self-Funded Travelers Depart

(Our dispatch) The first group of twenty-five self-funded travelers organized by China International Travel Service in 1959 to visit the Soviet Union and Eastern European socialist countries departed from Beijing by plane on the 26th for Moscow. Among these travelers are deputies to the National People's Congress, members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and deputies to the People's Congresses and members of the CPPCC of Guangdong and Fujian provinces. They will travel and visit twenty-eight cities in seven countries, including the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, and Romania, over a period of fifty-three days. During their travels, in addition to visiting the socialist construction and historical sites of various countries, they will also visit factories, farms, schools, and hospitals, and make extensive contact with the people of the countries they visit, in order to enhance friendship and solidarity with the peoples of those countries.

### 去苏联和东欧访问  我国自费旅行者启程

1959-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    去苏联和东欧访问
    我国自费旅行者启程
    本报讯  中国国际旅行社1959年度组织的第一批赴苏联及东欧社会主义国家的自费旅行者一行二十五人，26日乘飞机离开北京前往莫斯科。这批旅行者中有全国人民代表大会代表、全国政协委员和广东、福建的省人民代表和政协委员。他们将在五十三天的时间内旅行访问苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、保加利亚、罗马尼亚等七个国家的二十八个城市。在旅行期间，他们除参观各国社会主义建设事业和历史名胜外，还将访问工厂、农庄、学校和医院等单位，同所到国家人民广泛接触，以增进同各国人民的友谊和团结。
