Title: Sino-Finnish Trade Negotiations Concluded
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 12th: Lu Xuzhang, head of the Chinese trade delegation visiting Finland and Vice Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Trade, arrived in Helsinki from Moscow by plane on the 11th. He will sign the Sino-Finnish trade agreement on behalf of the Chinese side.

The Finnish and Chinese trade negotiations ended on May 8. The Chinese trade delegation participating in the negotiations was led by Deputy Head Zhang Guangdou and arrived in Helsinki on March 10.

### 中芬贸易谈判结束

1959-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    中芬贸易谈判结束
    新华社12日讯  赫尔辛基消息：到芬兰访问的中国贸易代表团团长、中国对外贸易部副部长卢绪章11日乘飞机从莫斯科到达赫尔辛基。他将代表中国方面在芬中贸易协定上签字。
    芬兰和中国的贸易谈判已于5月8日结束。参加这次谈判的中国贸易代表团是由副团长张光斗率领在3月10日到达赫尔辛基的。
