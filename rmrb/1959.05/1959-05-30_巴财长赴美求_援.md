Title: Pakistani Finance Minister Goes to the US Seeking "Aid"
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, May 28th: Pakistani Finance Minister Shoaib left here on the evening of the 27th, heading to Washington via Cairo to seek US "aid" for Pakistan. At Karachi Airport, Shoaib told reporters that he hoped to enable the United States to "better understand what the Pakistani government expects to receive in the next fiscal year."

### 巴财长赴美求“援”

1959-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    巴财长赴美求“援”
    据新华社卡拉奇28日电　巴基斯坦财政部长沙伊卜27日晚上离开这里，取道开罗前往华盛顿，为巴基斯坦寻求美“援”。沙伊卜在卡拉奇机场上向记者发表谈话，表示希望，能使美国“更好地了解巴基斯坦政府期望在下一个财政年度将得到什么东西。”
