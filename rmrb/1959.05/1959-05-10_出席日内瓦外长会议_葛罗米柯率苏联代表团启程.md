Title: Gromyko Leads Soviet Delegation to Geneva Foreign Ministers' Meeting
Author:
Date: 1959-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th May. (TASS report from Moscow, 8th May) The Soviet Council of Ministers has appointed the Soviet delegation to attend the Geneva Foreign Ministers' Conference. The head of the delegation is the Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, and the members of the delegation are: Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Zorin, Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom Malik, Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic Pervukhin, Soviet Ambassador to the Federal Republic of Germany Smirnov, and Soviet Foreign Ministry Collegium Member and Director of the American Countries Department Soldatov.

The delegation's advisors are: Ilyichev, Bazarov, and eleven others.

Xinhua News Agency, 9th May. (TASS report from Moscow) The Soviet delegation to the Geneva East-West Foreign Ministers' Conference, led by the head of the delegation, Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, left Moscow by plane this morning for Geneva.

At the airport to see them off were Soviet Deputy Foreign Ministers Kuznetsov and Pushkin, and other officials. Chinese Deputy Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian and some diplomatic envoys from other countries in Moscow were also at the airport to see them off.

### 出席日内瓦外长会议  葛罗米柯率苏联代表团启程

1959-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    出席日内瓦外长会议
    葛罗米柯率苏联代表团启程
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科8日讯：苏联部长会议任命了出席日内瓦外长会议的苏联代表团。代表团团长是苏联外交部长葛罗米柯，代表团团员有：苏联外交部副部长佐林，苏联驻英国大使马立克，苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使别尔乌辛，苏联驻德意志联邦共和国大使斯米尔诺夫和苏联外交部部务委员、美洲司司长索尔达托夫。
    代表团的顾问是：伊利切夫、巴扎罗夫等十一人。
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：出席日内瓦东西方外长会议的苏联代表团，在团长、苏联外交部长葛罗米柯率领下，今天上午乘飞机离开莫斯科去日内瓦。
    在机场送行的有苏联外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫和普希金以及其他官员。中国外交部副部长张闻天和一些国家驻莫斯科的使节也到机场送行。
