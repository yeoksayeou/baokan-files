Title: Li Baiyu Breaks National Record for Snatch in Light Heavyweight Division
Author:
Date: 1959-05-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 6th — Chinese weightlifter Li Baiyu, at the Second Chongqing Municipal Games on the 5th, broke his own national record of 127.5 kilograms in the light heavyweight snatch with a lift of 128 kilograms.

This is the third time Li Baiyu has broken this national record this year. However, this result is lower than the 130.5 kilograms he achieved in Taiyuan on April 23rd.

### 李白玉打破次重量级抓举全国纪录

1959-05-08
第6版()
专栏：

　　李白玉打破次重量级抓举全国纪录
    新华社重庆6日电　我国举重运动健将李白玉5日在重庆市第二届体育运动大会上，以抓举一二八公斤的成绩，打破了他去年创造的次重量级抓举一二七点五的全国纪录。
    这是李白玉今年以来第三次打破这项全国纪录。但这个成绩不如他今年4月23日在太原创造的一三○点五公斤的成绩。
