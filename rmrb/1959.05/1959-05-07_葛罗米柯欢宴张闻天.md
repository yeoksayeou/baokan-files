Title: Gromyko Hosts Banquet for Zhang Wentian
Author:
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 5th: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko hosted a banquet on the 5th in honor of Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of China.

    Present at the luncheon were Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Pushkin, Director of the Far East Department, Tsimianin, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, Zhang Weilie, and others.

    The guests and hosts engaged in friendly conversation during the banquet.

### 葛罗米柯欢宴张闻天

1959-05-07
第6版()
专栏：

　　葛罗米柯欢宴张闻天
    新华社莫斯科5日电　苏联外交部长葛罗米柯在5日设宴招待中国外交部副部长张闻天。
    出席午宴的有苏联外交部副部长普希金，远东司司长齐米亚宁，中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办张伟烈等。
    席间宾主进行了友好的交谈。
