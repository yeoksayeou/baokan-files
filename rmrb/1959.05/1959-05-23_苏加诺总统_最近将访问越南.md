Title: President Sukarno Will Visit Vietnam Soon
Author:
Date: 1959-05-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 22nd – The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam today issued a communique stating that, at the invitation of Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Dr. Sukarno, President of the Republic of Indonesia, will visit Vietnam in the near future.

    In order to grandly receive President Sukarno, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has established a reception committee, with Huang Mingjian, Minister of Culture, as the director and Yong Wenqian, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, as the deputy director.

### 苏加诺总统  最近将访问越南

1959-05-23
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统
    最近将访问越南
    新华社河内22日电　越南民主共和国政府今天发表公报说，应越南民主共和国主席胡志明的邀请，印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺博士将在最近期间访问越南。
    为了隆重接待苏加诺总统，越南民主共和国政府成立了以文化部部长黄明鉴为主任、外交部副部长雍文谦为副主任的接待委员会。
