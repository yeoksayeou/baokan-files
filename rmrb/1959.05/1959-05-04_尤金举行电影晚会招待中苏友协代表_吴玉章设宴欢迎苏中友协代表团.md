Title: Yujin Hosts Film Reception for Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Representatives; Wu Yuzhang Hosts Banquet Welcoming Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-05-04
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd: Yujin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, hosted a film reception this evening to welcome the delegates to the Third National Congress of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

Invited to attend the reception were Wu Yuzhang and Shao Lizi, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Hu Weide and Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretaries-General; Cao Jinghua and Liao Mosha, Vice Presidents of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Vice Chairman of the CPPCC, Bao Erhan, and delegates from various local Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations attending the congress.

The latest Soviet large-scale documentary films "News from Artificial Satellites" and "Flash Flood" were screened at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd: Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, hosted a banquet at noon today to welcome the delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association headed by Fedorov.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted the unbreakable brotherly friendship between China and the Soviet Union, and the further strengthening of the work of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association.

Antonov and Sudarikov, counselors of the Soviet Embassy in China, were also invited to attend the banquet.

Among those present were Shao Lizi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Qian Junrui, Secretary-General; Cao Jinghua and Liao Mosha, Vice Presidents of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC; and Qian Ying, Minister of Internal Affairs.

### 尤金举行电影晚会招待中苏友协代表  吴玉章设宴欢迎苏中友协代表团

1959-05-04
第3版()
专栏：

    尤金举行电影晚会招待中苏友协代表
    吴玉章设宴欢迎苏中友协代表团
    新华社3日讯　苏联驻中国大使尤金今晚举行电影招待会，欢迎中苏友好协会第三次全国代表会代表。
    应邀出席晚会的有中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、邵力子，副秘书长胡伟德、戈宝权，北京市中苏友好协会副会长曹靖华、廖沫沙，政协副主席包尔汉和出席代表会的各地中苏友好协会的代表。
    晚会上放映了苏联最新的“从人造卫星传来的消息”和“山洪暴发”大型纪录电影片。
    新华社3日讯　中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，今天中午设宴欢迎以费多罗夫为首的苏中友好协会代表团。
    席间，宾主频频为中苏两国兄弟般的牢不可破的友谊，为中苏友好协会和苏中友好协会的工作进一步加强干杯。
    苏联驻我国大使馆参赞安东诺夫和苏达利柯夫也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的有中苏友好协会总会副会长邵力子，秘书长钱俊瑞；北京市中苏友好协会副会长曹靖华、廖沫沙；政协副主席包尔汉和内务部部长钱瑛等。
