Shui Ethnic Group Folk Song

　　Chairman Mao, you are both near and far from us,
　　Some say you are in Beijing,
　　But I feel you are by our side.
　　With you, the Shui people have good days,
　　With you, the world is forever spring.

### 水族山歌

1959-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    水族山歌
    毛主席，你离我们既近又远，
    有人说你在北京，
    我却感到你在我们身边。
    有了你，水族人民才有好日子过，
    有了你，人间永远是春天。
