Title: Polish United Workers' Party Delegation Arrives in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1959-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, May 2nd: A delegation from the Polish United Workers' Party, led by Jerzy Morawski, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, arrived in Wuhan by plane today for a visit from Beijing.

The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Zhang Pinghua, Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, among others. The Polish Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications delegation, which is currently visiting Wuhan, also came to the airport to welcome them.

In the evening, Zhang Pinghua and Song Kanfu hosted a banquet for the distinguished guests.

### 波兰统一工人党代表团到武汉

1959-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党代表团到武汉
    新华社武汉2日电　由波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、书记处书记耶日·莫格夫斯基率领的波兰统一工人党代表团，今天从北京乘飞机到达武汉访问。
    代表团在机场上受到中共湖北省委书记张平化、中共武汉市委第一书记宋侃夫等人的热情欢迎。正在武汉访问的波兰邮电部代表团也到机场欢迎。
    晚间，张平化、宋侃夫设宴招待了贵宾。
