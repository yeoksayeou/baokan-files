Title: Preparing to Plant Late Rice (Picture)
Author: Guo Weiqi
Date: 1959-05-30

    Preparing to Plant Late Rice
    Cadres and members of the Fengxi Commune in Chao'an, Guangdong Province, began preparing for late rice planting half a month earlier than in previous years. This is a picture of commune members soaking seeds. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Guo Weiqi)
