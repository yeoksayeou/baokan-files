Title: Under the Leadership of the Trade Minister, British Trade Delegation Departs for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1959-05-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 12th: TASS, London, May 12th: The British trade delegation, led by British Trade Minister Eccles, flew from London to Moscow today.
    Eccles, speaking to reporters at London Airport, expressed the hope that the Anglo-Soviet trade talks would greatly expand trade between the two countries.

### 在贸易大臣率领下  英贸易代表团赴苏

1959-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    在贸易大臣率领下
    英贸易代表团赴苏
    新华社12日讯  塔斯社伦敦12日讯：以英国贸易大臣埃克尔斯为首的英国贸易代表团今天从伦敦乘飞机前往莫斯科。
    埃克尔斯在伦敦机场对记者发表谈话时表示希望，英苏贸易谈判将会使两国贸易大大扩大。
