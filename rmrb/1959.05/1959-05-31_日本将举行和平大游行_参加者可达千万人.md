Title: Japan to Hold Peace March; Participants Could Reach Ten Million
Author:
Date: 1959-05-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 29th: The Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs announced today that people from all prefectures in Japan will hold a massive peace march, with Hiroshima as the final destination.

The Council said that the entire route of this march is estimated to be about 5,000 kilometers, and the total number of participants will reach ten million.

According to the announcement, the marchers from various prefectures will converge in the three cities of Tokyo, Niigata, and Kagoshima, and then proceed towards Hiroshima in three large groups.

### 日本将举行和平大游行  参加者可达千万人

1959-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    日本将举行和平大游行
    参加者可达千万人
    据新华社29日讯　东京消息：日本禁止氢弹和原子弹协议会今天宣布，日本全国各县人民将举行以广岛为终点的声势浩大的和平大游行。
    协议会说，估计这次游行的全部路程约有五千公里，参加游行的总人数将达一千万人。
    据宣布，各县游行队伍将在东京、新澙、鹿儿岛三个城市汇合，然后分三路大军向广岛进发。
