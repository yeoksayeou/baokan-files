Title: Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Receives Czechoslovak Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-05-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 19th: Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Scientific and Technological Commission, Nie Rongzhen, received the entire Czechoslovak Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation led by Přeček, First Deputy Chairman of the Czechoslovak State Planning Commission, this morning and had a cordial conversation. The delegation is in Beijing to attend the Seventh Session of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation.

Present at the reception were Wang Daohan, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation and Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Wang Xinyuan, member and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Light Industry, and others.

Also present at the reception was Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák.

### 聂荣臻副总理  接见捷科技合作代表团

1959-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻副总理
    接见捷科技合作代表团
    新华社19日讯　国务院副总理、国家科学技术委员会主任聂荣臻，今天上午接见了以捷克斯洛伐克国家计划委员会第一副主席普泽克为首的捷克斯洛伐克科学与技术合作代表团全体人员，并进行了亲切的谈话。这个代表团是来北京参加中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会第七届会议的。
    接见时在座的有中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会中方组主席、第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，委员、轻工业部副部长王新元等。
    接见时在座的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克。
