Title: Indian People's Assembly to Discuss the Tibet Issue
Author:
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 5th: According to an announcement today in the People's Assembly by the Indian Minister of Parliamentary Affairs, Satya Narayan Sinha, the Indian People's Assembly, following the Council of States, will hold a debate on the 8th on the so-called "developments in Tibet," a matter that is purely China's internal affair.

### 印度人民院也要讨论西藏问题

1959-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    印度人民院也要讨论西藏问题
    新华社新德里5日电　据印度议会事务部长萨蒂亚·纳拉扬·辛哈今天在人民院宣布，印度人民院继联邦院之后将在8日就纯属中国内政问题的所谓“西藏事态的发展”进行辩论。
