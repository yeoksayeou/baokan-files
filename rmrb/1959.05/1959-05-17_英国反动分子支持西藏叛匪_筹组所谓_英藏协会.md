Title: British Reactionaries Support Tibetan Rebels, Preparing to Form So-Called "Anglo-Tibetan Association"
Author:
Date: 1959-05-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. A dispatch from London by Agence France-Presse on the 15th reported that some people in Britain are busily preparing to establish a so-called "Anglo-Tibetan Association."

According to reports, the headquarters of this organization, which obviously harbors imperialist aims, will be located in London. It will pose as a "representative of the Tibetan people," distort Tibetan history according to the needs of British imperialists, and provide "assistance" to Tibetan rebels who have fled abroad. According to the Agence France-Presse, a spokesman for the "Anglo-Tibetan Association" preparatory committee told its reporters that,
"The main purpose of this association will be to provide more information about the history of Tibet, 'not to let people forget what happened in Tibet,' to provide assistance to Tibetan refugees, including financial assistance, and to assist the Dalai Lama 'in every possible way.'" When the Agence France-Presse reporter asked if the "Anglo-Tibetan Association" would assist Tibetan rebels in establishing contacts abroad, the spokesman said: "Everything possible will be done."

### 英国反动分子支持西藏叛匪  筹组所谓“英藏协会”

1959-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

    英国反动分子支持西藏叛匪
    筹组所谓“英藏协会”
    新华社16日讯  据法新社15日从伦敦发出的一条消息报道，英国有人正在为成立一个所谓“英藏协会”而奔忙。
    据报道，这个显然带有帝国主义目的的组织的总部将设在伦敦。它将冒充“西藏人民代表”进行活动，并且将按照英帝国主义者的需要去歪曲西藏的历史和向亡命国外的西藏叛乱分子提供“援助”。法新社说，“英藏协会”筹备委员会的发言人对这家通讯社的记者说，
  “这个协会的主要目的将是提供关于西藏历史的更多的情报，‘不让人们忘记在西藏发生的事情’，对西藏难民提供援助，其中包括财政援助在内，以及‘用一切可能的方式’援助达赖喇嘛。”当法新社记者问到“英藏协会”是否将协助西藏叛乱分子在国外建立联系时，这个发言人说：“将做一切可能做的工作。
