Title: Foreign Leaders Send Congratulatory Telegrams to Our National Leaders
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-01
Page: 4
Leaders of Various Countries Send Congratulatory Telegrams to Our National Leaders

    President Ton Duc Thang of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Sends Telegram to Chairman Zhu
To: Comrade Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China:

    I am immensely pleased to learn that you have been elected as Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China. On behalf of the Vietnamese people, the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and in a personal capacity, I extend my warmest congratulations to you.

    The Vietnamese people and the National Assembly of Vietnam firmly believe that the fraternal friendship and close cooperation between the peoples of Vietnam and China will be increasingly consolidated and will continue to make important contributions to strengthening the solidarity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and to safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

    I wish you eternal good health and greater achievements in your noble position.

                Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
                                      April 29, 1959, in Hanoi

    Prime Minister Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou
To: Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government of the People's Republic of China:

    I am extremely pleased to learn that you have been re-elected to the important position of Premier of the People's Republic of China. Please allow me, on behalf of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and in my personal capacity, to extend my warmest congratulations to you.

    Under the wise leadership of the Communist Party of China and the beloved Chairman Mao Zedong, the People's Republic of China has made tremendous progress in socialist construction over the past period. The strength of the Chinese people has continuously increased, and their lives have continuously improved, thereby inspiring and strengthening the confidence of working people worldwide.

    The People's Republic of China has made many important contributions to strengthening the fraternal solidarity among the socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union, as well as strengthening the friendly unity among the nations of Asia, Africa, and Latin America, jointly opposing imperialism and colonialism, supporting national liberation movements, and safeguarding world peace.

    The traditional friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and China will continue to be consolidated and developed.

    The Vietnamese people and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam firmly believe that under the wise leadership of the Communist Party of China and the beloved Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese people will achieve even greater victories.

    I wish the fraternal friendship between the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the People's Republic of China ever closer.

                          Pham Van Dong, Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
                                             April 29, 1959

    Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou
To: Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
Most Respected Premier!

    On the occasion of your re-appointment as Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, I extend to you, on behalf of the Government of the German Democratic Republic and in my personal capacity, my most heartfelt congratulations. The Government of the People's Republic of China, closely linked with the Chinese people, has achieved great successes under your outstanding leadership.

    I wish you continued success in the socialist construction of the People's Republic of China and in the struggle to maintain world peace.

    With fraternal greetings!

                      Otto Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic

    Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, Lleshi, Sends Telegram to Chairman Liu
To: Comrade Liu Shaoqi, President of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    On the occasion of your election as President of the People's Republic of China, please accept, Comrade President, the most heartfelt congratulations of the Presidium of the People'sAssembly of the People's Republic of Albania and myself, and wish you new achievements in your important work for the great Chinese people, for the cause of socialism and peace.

    We are confident that the work you are doing in the position of President of the People's Republic of China will help strengthen and further develop the friendship and noble cooperation between our two fraternal peoples, as well as consolidate the solidarity of the powerful and rock-solid socialist camp led by the Soviet Union.

          Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania
                                      April 29, 1959, in Tirana

    Speaker of the Albanian Parliament, Shtulla, Sends Telegram to Chairman Zhu
To: Comrade Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    On the occasion of your election as Chairman the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, I extend to you, on behalf of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania and myself, my warmest fraternal congratulations.

    I wish you good health and further achievements in activities dedicated to the benefit of the great Chinese people, the consolidation of the sincere friendship between our two countries, and the cause of socialism and peace.

          Medar Shtylla, Speaker of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania
                                      April 29, 1959, in Tirana

    Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, Shehu, Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou
To: Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    On the occasion of your re-appointment as Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, please accept, Comrade Premier, the most heartfelt congratulations of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania and myself.

    I sincerely wish you good health and new achievements in your so important duties for the happiness of the brotherly Chinese people, in the continuous consolidation of the unity between the countries of the powerful socialist camp led by the Soviet Union, in the cause of peace and friendship between the peoples of all countries.

              Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania
                                      April 29, 1959, in Tirana

    Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, Ganev, Sends Telegram to President Liu
To: Comrade Liu Shaoqi, President of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    Respected Comrade Liu Shaoqi, on the occasion of your election as President of the People's Republic of China, at a time when the Chinese people have bestowed upon you this lofty honor and great trust, I extend to you, on behalf of the Bulgarian people, the Presidium of the National Assembly, the Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and myself, my heartfelt comradely congratulations.

    I believe that your fruitful work in the new and important state leadership position will contribute to the further prosperity of the People's Republic of China, to the consolidation of the great family of the socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union, to the victory of socialism and the guarantee of world peace, and to the further deepening and development of the fraternal friendship and cooperation between the Bulgarian and Chinese peoples.

    Comrade President, I wish you good health and numerous achievements in your work for the benefit of the people.

          Georgi Ganev, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria
                                    April 29, 1959, in Sofia

    Kabo, Head of the Albanian Labor Party Delegation, Sends Letter to President Liu
    Xinhua News Agency, April 30th: Liu Shaoqi received a congratulatory letter from Hysni Kapo, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and Head of the Albanian Labor Party Delegation visiting our country, congratulating him on his election as President of the People's Republic of China.

    The letter said:
      "On the occasion of your election as President of the People's Republic of China, the Albanian Labor Party delegation, which recently came to your beautiful country, congratulates you and sincerely wishes you success in your noble position, for the happiness of the brotherly Chinese people.

    Long live the unbreakable friendship between the Albanian and Chinese peoples."

### 各国领导人电贺我国领导人

1959-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    各国领导人电贺我国领导人
    越南民主共和国孙德胜委员长致朱委员长电中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德同志：
    我怀着无比高兴的心情获悉您被选为中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长，我谨代表越南人民，越南民主共和国国会并以我个人名义向您致以最热烈的祝贺。
    越南人民和越南国会深信越中两国人民兄弟般的友谊和密切的合作关系将日益牢固，并将继续对巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结，对保卫亚洲及世界和平事业作出重要的贡献。
    祝您身体永远健康，并祝您在您崇高的工作岗位上取得更重大的成就。
                越南民主共和国国会常务委员会委员长　孙德胜
                                      1959年4月29日于河内
    越南民主共和国范文同总理致周总理电中华人民共和国政府总理周恩来同志：
    我极其愉快地获悉您再度荣任中华人民共和国总理这一重要的职务，请允许我代表越南民主共和国政府，并以我个人的名义，向您致以最热烈的祝贺。
    在中国共产党和敬爱的毛泽东主席英明的领导下，中华人民共和国在过去这段时间中，在社会主义建设方面有了飞跃的进步，中国人民的力量不断地增强，他们的生活不断地获得改善，从而给全世界劳动人民以鼓舞，并增强了他们的信心。
    中华人民共和国在加强以苏联为核心的社会主义阵营各国之间的兄弟团结，以及加强亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲各民族之间的友好团结，共同反对帝国主义、殖民主义、支持民族解放运动和保卫世界和平等方面作出了许多重要的贡献。
    越中两国人民之间的传统友谊将不断地得到巩固和发展。
    越南人民和越南民主共和国政府深信：在中国共产党和敬爱的毛泽东主席的英明领导下，中国人民将获得更巨大的胜利。
    谨祝越南民主共和国和中华人民共和国之间的兄弟友谊日益密切。
                          越南民主共和国政府总理　范文同
                                             1959年4月29日
    德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥致周总理电北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志最尊敬的总理同志！
