Date: 1959-05-08

Waterwheel Ballad

　　Little waterwheel, a zhang long,
　　Singing songs all day long,
　　Singing until the sun's face turns red,
　　Singing until the moon's light is shed,
　　Singing until the river climbs the hill,
　　Singing until the paddy fields are white and still,
　　Singing until the seedlings are green and tender,
　　Singing until the rice flowers spread a fresh fragrance,
　　Singing until the grains are big and the ears are long,
　　Singing until the granaries are full with nowhere to throng,
　　Singing until the hearts of the people blossom,
　　Singing until everyone is beaming with joy.

### 水车谣

1959-05-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　水车谣
　　小水车，一丈长，
　　一天到晚把歌唱，
　　唱得太阳红了脸，
　　唱得月亮减了光，
　　唱得河水上山岗，
　　唱得稻田白茫茫，
　　唱得秧苗青又嫩，
　　唱得稻花散青香，
　　唱得粒大穗子长，
　　唱得仓满无处装，
　　唱得人心开了花，
　　唱得个个喜洋洋。
