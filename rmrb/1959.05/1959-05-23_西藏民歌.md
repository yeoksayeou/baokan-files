Tibetan Folk Songs

    Golden light shines on the plateau
    The brilliant sunlight illuminates the plateau,
    Golden light shines on the plateau,
    This golden light will never disappear,
    Because it is the great Communist Party.
    Auspicious Mountain
    From the top of the auspicious mountain,
    A golden peacock flew,
    It gave the hard-working girls,
    Necklaces of golden beads.
    From the hillside of the auspicious mountain,
    A cuckoo with blue feathers flew,
    It gave the hard-working girls,
    Earrings of pine and stone.
    From the foot of the auspicious mountain,
    A conch-like bird flew,
    It gave the hard-working girls,
    Bracelets of conch shells.
    From the auspicious east,
    A fiery red sun rose,
    It gave the Tibetan people,
    Warmth and hope.

### 西藏民歌

1959-05-23
第8版()
专栏：

    西藏民歌
    高原上闪着金光
    灿烂的阳光照耀着高原，
    高原上闪耀着金光，
    这金光永不会消失，
    因为她是伟大的共产党。
    吉祥的山
    从吉祥的山顶上，
    飞来了一只金色的孔雀，
    它给勤劳的姑娘们，
    留下了金珠子的项链。
    从吉祥的山腰里，
    飞来了一只蓝羽的杜鹃，
    它给勤劳的姑娘们，
    留下了松儿石耳环。
    从吉祥的山脚下，
    飞来了一只海螺般的鸟，
    它给勤劳的姑娘们，
    留下了海螺的手镯。
    从吉祥的东方，
    升起了火红的太阳，
    它给西藏人民，
    留下了温暖和希望。
