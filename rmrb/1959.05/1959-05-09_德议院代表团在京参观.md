Title: GDR Parliamentary Delegation Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 8th: Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Dieckmann, First Deputy Chairman Matern, and the distinguished guests of the People's Chamber delegation of the German Democratic Republic, accompanied by Lin Boqu, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhang Su, Standing Committee Member, and Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee, respectively visited and toured the Temple of Heaven, Beihai Park, the Forbidden City, and the Beijing Exhibition Center this afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, May 8th: First Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Hermann Matern, and some members of the People's Chamber delegation of the German Democratic Republic, after completing their visits to Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, and other cities, left here by special plane this morning to return to Beijing.

During their stay in Shenyang, the distinguished guests visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall, toured the city and Zhongshan Park, and attended a banquet held by the Governor of Liaoning Province, Huang Oudong. On the 7th, the delegation visited the steelmaking plant, large rolling mill, seamless steel pipe plant of Anshan Iron and Steel Works, and the Fushun Open-Pit Coal Mine in separate groups to Anshan and Fushun. Anshan and Fushun held rallies with nearly a thousand people participating to warmly welcome the distinguished guests.

### 德议院代表团在京参观

1959-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　德议院代表团在京参观
    新华社8日讯　德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团主席狄克曼、第一副主席马特恩和德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团的贵宾们，今天下午分别由人大常委会副委员长林伯渠、常务委员张苏、常委会副秘书长连贯陪同，分别参观和游览了天坛、北海、故宫和北京展览馆等。
    新华社沈阳8日电  德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团第一副主席赫尔曼·马特恩和德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团部分团员，结束了在沈阳、鞍山、抚顺等市的参观访问后，今天早晨乘专机离开这里回北京。
    贵宾们在沈阳期间参观了东北工业陈列馆，游览了市区和中山公园，并出席了辽宁省省长黄欧东举行的宴会。7日，代表团分批去鞍山、抚顺参观访问了鞍钢的炼钢厂、大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂和抚顺露天煤矿。鞍山、抚顺两市都举行了有近千名群众参加的大会，热烈欢迎贵宾。
