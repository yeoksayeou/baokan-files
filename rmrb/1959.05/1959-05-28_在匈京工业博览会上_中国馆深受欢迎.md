Title: At the Hungarian Industrial Exposition, the Chinese Pavilion is Very Popular
Author:
Date: 1959-05-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, May 26th. The Budapest Industrial Exposition, which opened here on May 15th, closed on the evening of the 25th.

　　This exposition was a great success. More than 1.1 million visitors toured the exhibits.

　　The exhibits in the Hungarian and Soviet pavilions attracted a wide audience. Experts warmly praised the Soviet metal-cutting machine tools and precision machinery.

　　The exhibits in the Chinese pavilion were widely praised by the audience. People unanimously praised the creative labor of the Chinese people and the achievements made in the Great Leap Forward.

### 在匈京工业博览会上  中国馆深受欢迎

1959-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    在匈京工业博览会上
    中国馆深受欢迎
    新华社布达佩斯26日电  5月15日在这里开幕的布达佩斯工业博览会，25日晚间闭幕。
    这次博览会获得了很大成功。有一百一十多万观众参观了展出。
    匈牙利的展览馆和苏联馆的展品吸引了广大的观众。专家们都热烈称赞苏联的金属切削机床和精密机械等。
    中国馆的展览品受到了观众的普遍称赞。人们同声赞扬中国人民的创造性劳动和在大跃进中取得的成就。
