Title: Handbag-Style Handcart
Author:
Date: 1959-05-29
Page: 2

    Handbag-Style Handcart
    The Beijing Children's Toy Factory recently produced a handbag-style handcart. This cart is small and exquisite, easy to carry, and weighs only about four kilograms. When in use, simply lift the handle upwards and the two sides of the cart will separate.

### 提包式手推车

1959-05-29
第2版()
专栏：

    提包式手推车
    北京市儿童玩具工厂最近制成一种提包式手推车。这种车小巧玲珑，携带方便，重量只四公斤左右，用时只要把车把向上一提、车的两厢分开就行了。
