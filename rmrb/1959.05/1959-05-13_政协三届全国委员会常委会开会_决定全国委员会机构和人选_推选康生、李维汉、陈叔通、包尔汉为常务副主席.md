Title: Standing Committee of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC Holds Meeting: Decisions on Organizations and Personnel of the National Committee; Kang Sheng, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, and Bao Erhan Elected as Executive Vice-Chairmen
Author:
Date: 1959-05-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The Standing Committee of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC held its first plenary meeting this afternoon. The meeting elected Kang Sheng, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, and Bao Erhan as Executive Vice-Chairmen of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC and decided on the working organizations of the CPPCC National Committee and the personnel for each working organization.

Chairman Zhou Enlai presided over today's meeting.

After discussion, the meeting decided to retain the original working organizations of the CPPCC National Committee, including the Study Committee, the Local Work Committee, the Journal Editorial Committee, and the Secretariat. The original eleven working groups were reorganized into nine working groups for International Issues, Industry and Commerce, Culture and Education, Science and Technology, Medicine and Health, Ethnic Affairs, Overseas Chinese Affairs, Religion, and Women. It was also decided to establish a Liaison Committee and a Committee for the Study of Cultural and Historical Materials to further strengthen the daily work of the CPPCC National Committee and absorb more members to participate in daily work.

The meeting also decided on the candidates for Deputy Secretary-General of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC, Directors and Deputy Directors of various committees, Heads and Deputy Heads of various groups, and Directors and Deputy Directors of the Secretariat.

### 政协三届全国委员会常委会开会  决定全国委员会机构和人选  推选康生、李维汉、陈叔通、包尔汉为常务副主席

1959-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    政协三届全国委员会常委会开会
    决定全国委员会机构和人选
    推选康生、李维汉、陈叔通、包尔汉为常务副主席
    新华社12日讯  政协第三届全国委员会常务委员会今天下午举行第一次全体会议。会议推选康生、李维汉、陈叔通、包尔汉为政协第三届全国委员会的常务副主席，决定了政协全国委员会的工作机构和各工作机构负责人的人选。
    周恩来主席主持了今天的会议。
    会议经过讨论，决定保留政协全国委员会原有的学习委员会、地方工作委员会、会刊编辑委员会、秘书处等工作机构，将原有的十一个工作组改设为国际问题、工商、文化教育、科学技术、医药卫生、民族、华侨、宗教、妇女等九个工作组；并且决定增设联络委员会和文史资料研究委员会，以便进一步加强政协全国委员会的日常工作，吸收更多的委员参加日常工作。
    会议还决定了政协第三届全国委员会副秘书长、各委员会主任副主任、各组组长副组长和秘书处处长副处长的人选。
