Title: CPI Secretary Gupta Exposes That Indian People's Socialist Party Leader's Attack on China Aims to Undermine India's Foreign Policy
Author:
Date: 1959-05-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 28th. Communist Party of India National Committee Secretariat Secretary Gupta said today that the People's Socialist Party leaders' attacks and interference against China are "solely intended to undermine India's foreign policy based on Panchsheel (Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence)."

Gupta made these remarks at a mass rally in Madras while addressing the audience's questions about the Tibetan incident. He condemned People's Socialist Party leader Kripalani's preparation to convene the so-called "All-India Tibet Conference" at the end of May and many other meetings discussing China's internal affairs. He said, "Tibet is merely being used as a pretext to mask a movement against peaceful coexistence and cooperation."

Gupta pointed out that the leaders of the People's Socialist Party want to create a split between India and the socialist camp. When he called on the Indian people to unite and defeat the imperialist plot to create division between India and China, the audience applauded enthusiastically, and deafening slogans rang out in the crowded venue: "Long live the solidarity with socialist countries!" "Long live Sino-Indian friendship!" Gupta pointed out that the leaders of the People's Socialist Party support the reactionary forces in Tibet and defend them. Their motives are obvious: they hope to undermine India's neutral policy based on Panchsheel, but they dare not admit it.

### 印共书记古普塔揭露  印度人民社会党头子攻击中国  目的在于破坏印度的外交政策

1959-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    印共书记古普塔揭露
    印度人民社会党头子攻击中国  目的在于破坏印度的外交政策
    新华社新德里28日电　印度共产党全国委员会书记处书记古普塔今天说，人民社会党的领导人对中国的攻击和干涉“完全是为了想破坏以潘查希拉（和平共处五项原则）为基础的印度外交政策”。
    古普塔是在马德拉斯举行的一次群众大会上应听众的要求谈西藏事件时这样说的。他谴责人民社会党领袖克里帕拉尼准备在5月底召开的所谓“全印西藏会议”等许多讨论中国内政问题的会议。他说，“西藏只不过是被用来掩饰反对和平共处和合作政策的运动的一个幌子。”
    古普塔指出，人民社会党的领导人想在印度和社会主义阵营之间制造分裂。当他呼吁印度人民团结起来击败帝国主义在印度和中国之间制造分裂的阴谋的时候，听众热烈地鼓掌，在挤满了人的会场上响起了震耳欲聋的口号声：“同社会主义国家的团结万岁！”“印中友好万岁！”古普塔指出，人民社会党的领导人支持西藏的反动势力，并且为他们辩护，他们的动机是明显的，他们希望破坏以潘查希拉为基础的印度的中立政策，而又不敢承认这一点。
