Title: The Wonderful "Quiz Machine"
Author:
Date: 1959-05-29
Page: 2

    The "Quiz Machine" (also known as a computer), newly designed and successfully produced by the Shanghai Kangyuan Toy Factory, has four boxes on the front, containing the four arithmetic symbols “+”, “-”, “×”, and “÷”. It can calculate forty-eight different arithmetic problems. It can cultivate children's interest in arithmetic.

### 奇妙的“测验机”

1959-05-29
第2版()
专栏：

    奇妙的“测验机”
    上海康元玩具厂最新设计成功的“测验机”（又名计算机），机身前有四个方框，里面有“＋”“－”“×”“÷”四种算式，能计算四十八种不同的算术题。可以培养孩子们对算术的兴趣。
