Title: Hungarian Ambassador Holds Reception for Exhibition of Hungarian Telecommunications Equipment in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 20th. Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nogradi, held a reception this afternoon at the Embassy for the exhibition of Hungarian telecommunications equipment in Beijing.

Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and East European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and other responsible persons from relevant departments were invited to attend the reception.

### 为匈电讯设备在京展出  匈大使举行招待会

1959-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    为匈电讯设备在京展出
    匈大使举行招待会
    新华社20日讯　匈牙利驻华大使诺格拉第为匈牙利电讯设备在北京展出，今天下午在大使馆举行招待会。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸、外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清等有关部门的负责人，应邀出席了招待会。
