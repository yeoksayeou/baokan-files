Title: Cuban Government Enacts Land Reform Law; Castro and Others Personally Lead Land Reform in the Sierra Maestra; People's Socialist Party Supports Land Reform
Author:
Date: 1959-05-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency report: Havana news: The Cuban Provisional Government's Council of Ministers enacted the Land Reform Law on the afternoon of the 17th in the Sierra Maestra mountains of Oriente Province, the old base area of the Cuban eastern guerrilla war.

The Cuban Provisional Government President Urrutia, Prime Minister Castro, heads of various government departments, and senior generals of the armed forces arrived in the Sierra Maestra mountains of Oriente Province on the 17th to lead the land reform work. When the Cuban national leaders arrived in the Sierra Maestra mountains, tens of thousands of farmers warmly cheered and saluted them, and demanded that the government issue them weapons to defend the land and farm implements they were about to receive.

At the conference where the Council of Ministers signed the Land Reform Law, President Urrutia appointed Prime Minister Castro as Chairman of the National Land Reform Committee, and appointed Núñez Jiménez, one of the leaders of the Cuban Armed Forces, as Director of the Committee. The Land Reform Committee was announced by Castro before he left the capital.

In his speech at the conference, Castro pointed out that land reform will increase the income of two million Cubans, which will create a domestic market conducive to industrial development.

According to the Cuban "Revolución" newspaper, the Land Reform Law will affect 220,000 caballerías of land owned by large landowners.

The provincial farmers' association has been established in Oriente Province. The Land Reform Committee has been established in the Calzada area of ​​Santiago.

The chairman of the Cuban National Federation of Small Businesses announced that the Federation has formed a land reform brigade of Cuban small businesses in order to fully support land reform.

The Cuban newspaper "Hoy" published a statement on the 19th issued by the Executive Bureau of the National Committee of the People's Socialist Party, supporting the Land Reform Law promulgated by the Cuban Provisional Government on the 17th.

The statement pointed out that the vast number of peasants and workers must be mobilized to ensure the correct and complete implementation of the Land Reform Law. This law will form a regrouping of social forces.

The statement emphasized that land reform will provide favorable conditions for industrialization and changing the single-crop economy, and will strike a blow at the main enemies of the revolution—the large landowners and imperialism.

### 古巴政府颁布土地改革法  卡斯特罗等亲自到马埃斯特腊山区领导土改  人民社会党支持土改

1959-05-21
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴政府颁布土地改革法
    卡斯特罗等亲自到马埃斯特腊山区领导土改
    人民社会党支持土改
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：哈瓦那消息：古巴临时政府的部长会议17日下午在古巴东部游击战争的老根据地奥连特省的马埃斯特腊山区颁布了土地改革法。
    古巴临时政府总统乌鲁蒂亚、总理卡斯特罗、政府各部门首长以及武装部队的高级将领一行，17日到达奥连特省的马埃斯特腊山区领导土地改革工作。当古巴国家领袖到达马埃斯特腊山区时，成千上万的农民向他们热烈欢呼和致敬，并且要求政府把武器发给他们以便保卫将要得到的土地和农具。
    乌鲁蒂亚总统在部长会议签署土地改革法的大会上，任命卡斯特罗总理为土地改革全国委员会的主席，并任命古巴武装部队领袖之一努涅斯·希门尼斯为该委员会的主任。土地改革委员会是由卡斯特罗在他离开首都前宣布成立的。
    卡斯特罗在这次大会的讲话中指出，土地改革将使二百万古巴人增加收入，这将造成一个有利于工业发展的国内市场。
    据古巴“革命报”报道，土地改革法对大庄园主们所拥有的二十二万卡瓦耶里亚的土地将发生影响。
    在奥连特省已经成立了该省的农民协会。在圣地亚哥的卡耳萨达地区成立了土地改革委员会。
    古巴全国小商贩联合会主席宣布，该联合会已经组成了一支古巴小商业的土改大队，以便全力支持土地改革。
      古巴“今日报”19日登载了人民社会党全国委员会执行局发表的一项声明，支持古巴临时政府17日颁布的土地改革法。
    这个声明指出，必须动员广大农民和工人来保证正确和完全地实现土地改革法。这个法律将形成社会力量的重新集结。
    声明强调指出，土地改革将为工业化和改变单一经济作物制提供有利条件，并将给予革命的主要敌人——大庄园主和帝国主义以打击。
