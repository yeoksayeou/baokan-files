Title: Afghan Ambassador Holds Reception to Celebrate Afghan Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1959-05-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 27th: The Afghan Ambassador to China, Abdul Samad, held a reception at the embassy this afternoon to celebrate the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

Vice Chairman Dong Biwu was invited to attend the reception.

Ambassador Samad delivered a welcoming speech at the reception, proposing a toast to the prosperity of the Chinese people and to the health of Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Vice Chairman Dong Biwu.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian delivered a speech at the reception. He said that the Kingdom of Afghanistan, by resolutely pursuing a policy of peace and neutrality and developing friendly relations with all countries in the world, is playing an active role in safeguarding peace in Asia and the world. This policy of the Kingdom of Afghanistan has won the praise of the Chinese people and all peace-loving countries.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian said, "We are also pleased to see that the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Afghanistan have seen new developments in the past year. Our cultural delegation will soon visit Afghanistan at the invitation of the Afghan government. I am convinced that through the joint efforts of both sides, the traditional friendship between our two countries will be strengthened day by day."

Vice Premier Li Xiannian concluded by proposing a toast to the friendship between China and Afghanistan, to the friendly unity of the peoples of Asia and Africa and to world peace, and to the health of His Majesty King Mohammed Zahir Shah.

Among those invited to attend the reception were Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Cheng Qian, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Zhang Hanfu, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China.

### 阿富汗大使举行招待会庆祝阿独立日

1959-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗大使举行招待会庆祝阿独立日
    新华社27日讯  阿富汗驻华大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德今天下午在大使馆举行招待会，庆祝阿富汗王国独立纪念日。
    董必武副主席应邀出席了招待会。
    萨马德大使在招待会上首先致词欢迎他的客人，他提议为中国人民的繁荣，为刘少奇主席和董必武副主席的健康干杯。
    李先念副总理在招待会上致词。他说，阿富汗王国由于坚决执行和平中立、同世界各国发展友好关系的政策，它在维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中正在起着积极的作用。阿富汗王国的这种政策获得了中国人民和一切爱好和平的国家和人民的赞扬。
    李先念副总理说，我们也高兴地看到中、阿两国间的友好合作关系在过去一年中又有了新的发展。我国文化代表团即将应邀访问阿富汗。我深信，通过我们双方的共同努力，我们两国间的传统友谊，必将日益加强。
    李先念副总理最后提议，为中、阿两国之间的友谊，为亚、非各国人民的友好团结和世界和平，为穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿国王陛下的健康干杯。
    应邀出席招待会的，有国务院副总理薄一波，人大常委会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、程潜，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫，以及各国驻华使节。
