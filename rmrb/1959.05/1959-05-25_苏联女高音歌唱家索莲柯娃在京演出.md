Title: Soviet Soprano Solenkova Performs in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 24th: Soviet soprano A.G. Solenkova’s first performance in China, held tonight at the Tianqiao Theater, was warmly welcomed by the capital's audience.

This famous young Soviet singer, who won the first prize and a gold medal at the 5th World Youth Festival singing competition, sang more than ten famous songs from various countries and famous Russian songs for more than 1,500 listeners tonight. The soprano's mellow and bright voice and rich performing emotions deeply moved the audience. Each time she finished singing a song, prolonged applause rang out in the theater.

After the performance, the capital's music circles presented flowers to Solenkova. Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and musicians Zhou Weizhi, Zhao Feng, Yu Yixuan and others warmly shook hands with the Soviet singer to congratulate her on her successful performance.

### 苏联女高音歌唱家索莲柯娃在京演出

1959-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联女高音歌唱家索莲柯娃在京演出
    新华社24日讯　苏联女高音歌唱家阿·格·索莲柯娃今晚在天桥剧场举行的访华首次演出，获得了首都听众的热烈欢迎。
    这位荣获第五届世界青年联欢节歌唱比赛一等奖和金质奖章的苏联著名青年歌唱家，今晚为一千五百多听众演唱了十多首各国著名歌曲和俄罗斯名歌。女歌唱家圆润嘹亮的歌喉和丰富的表演感情深深打动了听众，当她每唱完一支歌时，剧场里都响起了长时间的掌声。
    演出结束后，首都音乐界向索莲柯娃献花。我国对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊和音乐界人士周巍峙、赵沨、喻宜萱等人，同苏联女歌唱家热烈握手，祝贺她演唱成功。
