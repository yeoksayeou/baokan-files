Title: To Allow Jinmen Compatriots to Happily Celebrate the Laboring People's Holiday, Fujian Front Troops Ordered to Cease Bombardment on "May 1st"
Author:
Date: 1959-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 30th—A spokesman for the Command Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front announced: May 1st is International Labor Day. In order to allow the compatriots and Chiang Kai-shek's soldiers and officers on the islands of Greater Jinmen, Lesser Jinmen, Greater Dadan, and Lesser Dadan to happily celebrate this great holiday for the world's laboring people, the artillery troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front have been ordered to cease bombardment on May 1st.

### 让金门同胞欢度劳动人民的节日  福建前线部队奉命“五一”停止炮击

1959-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    让金门同胞欢度劳动人民的节日
    福建前线部队奉命“五一”停止炮击
    新华社福建前线30日电　中国人民解放军福建前线司令部发言人宣称：5月1日，是国际劳动节。为了使大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的同胞和蒋军官兵欢度这个伟大的世界劳动人民的节日，福建前线中国人民解放军炮兵部队，奉命在5月1日停止炮击。
