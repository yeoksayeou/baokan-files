Title: Our Military Delegation Returns to Jakarta After Concluding Visits Across Indonesia

Author:

Date: 1959-05-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 12th—The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, headed by General Yang Chengwu, concluded its visit activities in various parts of Indonesia and returned to Jakarta by plane from Medan this afternoon, accompanied by Major General Gatot, First Deputy Chief of Staff of the Indonesian Army.

Starting from April 24th, the delegation visited Bandung, Bali, Yogyakarta, Solo, Bangka Island, Palembang, Medan, and other places. They visited many military organs, training centers, factories, plantations, and historical sites.

The delegation members also had extensive and friendly contacts with leaders of the Indonesian armed forces and government.

Wherever the delegation went, they were warmly welcomed by Indonesian civilian and military officials.

Last night, a gift-giving ceremony was held at the First Military District Command in Medan. Lieutenant Colonel Kintinggs, Commander of the First Military District, delivered a long speech at the ceremony, praising the achievements of the Chinese people in their construction efforts. He believed that this visit by the delegation to Indonesia was of great significance in strengthening the relationship between the armed forces of the two countries.

When the delegation visited Bangka Island, the center of tin production, local residents and overseas Chinese stood on both sides of the street to welcome the delegation. Many residents there shouted slogans such as "Long live China-Indonesia friendship" and "Long live China" to the delegation.

Before leaving for Medan, the delegation made a brief stop in Palembang, South Sumatra, and was warmly welcomed by the Acting Commander of the Second Military District, the Governor of South Sumatra, the Speaker of the local Parliament, and military and political officials.

### 结束在印度尼西亚各地的访问  我国军事代表团返雅加达

1959-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在印度尼西亚各地的访问
    我国军事代表团返雅加达
    据新华社雅加达12日电  以杨成武上将为首的中国军事友好代表团结束了在印度尼西亚各地的访问活动，今天下午在印度尼西亚陆军第一副参谋长加托特少将的陪同下，从棉兰乘飞机回到雅加达。
    从4月24日开始，代表团访问了万隆、巴厘、日惹、梭罗、邦加岛、巨港、棉兰以及其他地方。他们访问了许多军事机关、训练中心、工厂、种植场以及名胜古迹。
    代表团人员还同印度尼西亚武装部队和政府的领导人员进行了广泛和友好的接触。
    代表团无论到那里，到处都受到印度尼西亚文武负责官员的热烈欢迎。
    昨晚在棉兰第一军区司令部举行了赠送礼物的仪式。第一军区司令京廷斯中校在仪式上发表长篇演说，称赞中国人民在建设中所取得的成就。他认为，代表团这次访问印度尼西亚，对于加强两国武装部队之间的关系有巨大的意义。
    在代表团访问产锡中心邦加岛时，那里的居民和华侨站在街道两旁欢迎代表团。那里的许多居民向代表团高呼中国—印度尼西亚友好万岁和中国万岁的口号。
    代表团在动身前往棉兰之前，曾经在南苏门答腊的巨港作过短暂的逗留，并且受到第二军区代理司令、南苏门答腊省长、当地议会议长和军政负责人员的热烈欢迎。
