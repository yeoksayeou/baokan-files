Title: A Gang of Anti-China Reactionaries in Japan Gathered in Tokyo to Make Anti-China Rants
Author:
Date: 1959-05-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 14th dispatch from Tokyo: A small group of reactionaries in Japan today held a so-called "National Assembly on the Tibet Issue" in Tokyo, supporting Tibetan rebel elements and openly interfering in China's internal affairs.

This conference was organized by the notorious "National Council on the Tibet Issue," a reactionary organization jointly led by Tokuaki Matsumoto, who planned the Japan-Germany-Italy alliance, and Yasaburo Shimo-Naka.

They used the bait of showing a film to gather and lure approximately one thousand people to participate in the gathering.

The so-called declaration issued by this gathering slandered China's suppression of the Tibetan rebellion as "an invasion of national autonomy relying on force" and a "serious threat to the nationalities of Asia and world peace."

The reactionaries also passed a resolution at the conference demanding that the United Nations, the Asian Solidarity Committee, and religious organizations around the world "take immediate action to resolve the Tibet issue."

Norbu, the Dalai Lama's brother, who resides in the United States, made a special trip to Japan today to participate in this reactionary gathering. He delivered a speech at the meeting, fiercely slandering China. He said: "The Chinese Communist Party's atrocities in Tibet have shown the world the true purpose of communist expansion. This expansion means no treaties, no borders, and no humanity."

Norbu stated at the meeting that he was "determined to fight the Chinese Communist Party to the end."

### 日本一帮敌视中国的反动分子  在东京聚众作反华叫嚣

1959-05-15
第5版()
专栏：

    日本一帮敌视中国的反动分子
    在东京聚众作反华叫嚣
    新华社14日讯　东京消息：日本一小撮反动分子今天在东京举行所谓  “西藏问题国民大会”，支持西藏叛乱分子，公开干涉中国内政。
    这个大会是由臭名远扬的、曾策划过日、德、意同盟的松本德明和下中弥三郎共同领导的反动组织“西藏问题国民协议会”主办的。
    他们以放映电影为饵纠集和诱骗了约一千人参加集会。
    这个集会发表的所谓宣言污蔑中国平息西藏叛乱是“依靠武力对民族自治的侵犯”，是“对亚洲各民族和世界和平的严重威胁”。
    反动分子们还在大会上通过了一项决议，要求联合国、亚洲团结委员会和全世界的宗教团体“立即采取行动解决西藏问题”。
    居住在美国的达赖喇嘛的哥哥诺布，今天特地赶到日本参加了这个反动的集会。他在会上发表讲话，激烈地诽谤中国。他说什么：“中国共产党在西藏的暴行向全世界表明了共产党扩张的真正目的。这种扩张意味着没有条约、没有国境、没有人道”。
    诺布在会上表示他要“决心同中国共产党斗争到底”。
