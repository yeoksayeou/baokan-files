Title: Miniature X-ray Machine
Author:
Date: 1959-05-05
Page: 3

    Miniature X-ray Machine
    The Shanghai Precision Medical Equipment Factory has recently produced a miniature X-ray machine weighing less than seven kilograms. It is most suitable for dentists to use when making house calls to take X-rays of the structure of the gums.

    微型X光机
