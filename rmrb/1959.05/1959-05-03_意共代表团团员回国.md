Title: Italian Communist Party Delegation Members Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd: The members of the Italian Communist Party Central Committee delegation, Antonio Rossi, Celso Ghini, Luciano Barca, Maria Michetti, and Gilard Chiaromonte, concluded their one-month visit to China and returned home via Moscow by plane on May 2nd.

Those who went to the airport to see them off were: Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat; Liu Ningyi, Wu Xiuquan, members of the Central Committee; and Xiong Fu, Li Qixin, Zhang Xiangshan and other comrades in charge of the organs of the CPC Central Committee.

### 意共代表团团员回国

1959-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    意共代表团团员回国
    新华社2日讯　意大利共产党中央委员会代表团团员安东尼奥·罗西欧、切尔索·吉尼、卢契安诺·巴尔卡、玛丽亚·米凯蒂、吉拉德·基阿罗蒙特结束了在中国历时一月的访问后，在5月2日乘飞机经莫斯科返国。
    到机场欢送的有：中共中央政治局委员彭真，中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员刘宁一、伍修权以及中共中央机关负责工作人员熊复、李启新、张香山等同志。
