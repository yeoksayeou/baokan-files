Title: Marshal Chen Yi Receives Algerian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd — Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Marshal Chen Yi received all members of the Algerian Military Delegation, headed by Omar Oussedik, this afternoon.

Marshal Chen Yi and Head of Delegation Omar Oussedik, among others, held a long and cordial conversation, and had dinner with all members of the military delegation.

Also present at the reception were General Huang Kecheng, Senior General Li Juikui, Major General Wang Xinggang, Senior Colonel Du Ping, and Colonel Zhang Bingyu.

### 陈毅元帅接见阿尔及利亚军事代表团

1959-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅元帅接见阿尔及利亚军事代表团
    新华社2日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅今天下午接见了以奥马尔·乌西迪克团长为首的阿尔及利亚军事代表团全体人员。
    陈毅元帅和奥马尔·乌西迪克团长等作了长时间的亲切交谈，并且和军事代表团全体人员共进晚餐。
    接见时在座的，有黄克诚大将、李聚奎上将、王兴纲少将、杜平大校、张秉玉上校。
