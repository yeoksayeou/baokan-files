Title: UAE Delegation Arrives in Moscow to Study High Dam Construction Plan
Author:
Date: 1959-05-08
Page: 5

　　UAE Delegation Arrives in Moscow to Study High Dam Construction Plan
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th - According to TASS Moscow: A delegation of experts from the United Arab Republic for the construction of the Aswan High Dam arrived in Moscow by plane from Cairo on the 6th. The head of the delegation is Arafa, Minister of Public Works of the Egyptian region of the UAE.
    In Moscow, UAE experts will study the plan prepared by Soviet experts for organizing the construction of the dam and the model of this water conservancy project.

### 研究高水坝建筑计划  阿联代表团到莫斯科

1959-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

　　研究高水坝建筑计划
    阿联代表团到莫斯科
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：阿拉伯联合共和国阿斯旺高水坝建筑专家代表团，6日从开罗乘飞机抵莫斯科。代表团团长是阿联埃及地区公共工程部长阿拉法。
    阿联专家在莫斯科将研究苏联专家所准备的组织水坝建筑的计划和这个水利工程建筑的模型。
