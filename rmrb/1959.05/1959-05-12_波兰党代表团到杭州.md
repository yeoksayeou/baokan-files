Title: Polish Party Delegation Arrives in Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-05-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 11th May. The Polish United Workers' Party delegation led by Jerzy Morawski arrived in Hangzhou by special plane this afternoon. The delegation came to Hangzhou from Guangzhou via Nanchang.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included: Jiang Hua, Alternate Member of the CPC Central Committee and First Secretary of the CPC Zhejiang Provincial Committee; Huo Shilian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Zhejiang Provincial Committee and Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province; Wang Pingyi and Li Yuanzhen, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Hangzhou Municipal Committee; and Xian Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Hangzhou Municipal Committee and Vice Mayor of Hangzhou.

In the afternoon, the distinguished guests boated on West Lake, admiring the lake and mountain scenery in the light rain, and visited scenic spots and historical sites such as Three Pools Mirroring the Moon, Flower Harbor Park, Lingyin Temple, and Jade Spring.

### 波兰党代表团到杭州

1959-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰党代表团到杭州
    新华社杭州11日电　由耶日·莫拉夫斯基率领的波兰统一工人党代表团，今天下午乘专机到达杭州。代表团是从广州经南昌来到杭州的。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有：中共中央候补委员、中共浙江省委第一书记江华，中共浙江省委书记处书记、浙江省副省长霍士廉，中共杭州市委书记处书记王平夷、李元贞，中共杭州市委书记处书记、杭州市副市长冼依。
    下午，贵宾们泛舟西湖，观赏了微雨中的湖光山色，并游览了三潭印月、花港观鱼公园、灵隐、玉泉等名胜古迹。
