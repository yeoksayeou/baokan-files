Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Sign This Year's Sino-Soviet Scientific Cooperation Implementation Plan; Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Meets with Soviet Scientists
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd. The Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation, led by the President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Nesmeyanov, arrived in Beijing on May 20th and 22nd. The delegation is visiting China to sign the 1959 implementation plan in accordance with the Sino-Soviet Academy of Sciences scientific cooperation agreement.

Members of the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation include: Soviet mathematician Academician Dorodnicyn, petroleum geologist and Deputy Director of the Siberian Branch Academician Trofimuk, chemist Corresponding Academician Fredlina, Deputy Director of the State Science and Technology Committee Koromytsev, Director of the Academy of Sciences Department of Cooperation with People's Democratic Countries Prasolov, and Mrs. Nesmeyanov.

Welcoming the delegation at the airport today were Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Li Siguang, Zhang Jingfu, Tao Menghe, and Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents; Pei Lisheng, Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Yan Jici, Yin Zanxun, Tong Dizhou, and Yun Ziqiang, Directors and Deputy Directors of the various departments of the Academy of Sciences; and scientists Qian Sanqiang, Qian Xuesen, Hua Luogeng, and others.

Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin also welcomed the delegation at the airport. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, held a banquet tonight to welcome all members of the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation. Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen attended the banquet. Ambassador Yudin was also invited to attend the banquet.

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd. Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen met today with Nesmeyanov, head of the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation and President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and all members of the delegation.

Present at the meeting were Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Li Siguang, Vice President, and Han Guang, Deputy Director of the State Science and Technology Commission, among others.

Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin was also present at the meeting.

### 为签订今年中苏科学合作执行计划  苏联科学院代表团到京  聂荣臻副总理会见苏联科学家

1959-05-23
第5版()
专栏：

    为签订今年中苏科学合作执行计划
    苏联科学院代表团到京
    聂荣臻副总理会见苏联科学家
    新华社22日电　由苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫率领的苏联科学院代表团，已于5月20日和22日先后到达北京。代表团是根据中苏两国科学院科学合作协议书，为签订1959年执行计划前来我国访问的。
    苏联科学院代表团成员有：苏联数学家朵罗多尼钦院士，石油地质学家、西伯利亚总分部副主任特罗菲木克院士，化学家弗列德林娜通讯院士，国家科学技术委员会副主任柯罗米采夫，科学院人民民主国家合作局局长普拉索罗夫，以及涅斯米扬诺夫夫人。
    今天到机场欢迎的，有中国科学院院长郭沫若、副院长李四光、张劲夫、陶孟和、吴有训，中国科学院秘书长裴丽生，科学院各学部正副主任严济慈、尹赞勋、童弟周、恽子强和科学家钱三强、钱学森、华罗庚等人。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金也到机场欢迎。中国科学院院长郭沫若今晚举行宴会欢迎苏联科学院代表团的全体人员。聂荣臻副总理出席宴会。尤金大使也应邀出席了宴会。
    新华社22日讯　国务院副总理聂荣臻今天会见了苏联科学院代表团团长、苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫和代表团全体人员。
    会见时在座的，有中国科学院院长郭沫若、副院长李四光，国家科学技术委员会副主任韩光等。
    会见时，苏联驻中国大使尤金也在座。
