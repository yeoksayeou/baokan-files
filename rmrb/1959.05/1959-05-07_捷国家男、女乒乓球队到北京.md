Title: Czechoslovakian National Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 6th: The renowned Czechoslovakian National Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams, a group of six, arrived in Beijing by plane today, led by Kuhari, the Chairman of the Czechoslovakian Table Tennis Association.

Czechoslovakia has a high level of table tennis. In the twenty-five World Table Tennis Championships held so far, the Czechoslovakian men's team has won the team championship six times, and the women's team has won the team championship four times. The three male athletes who came to China this time are Stipek and Vinarovsky, the runners-up in the men's doubles at the 25th World Table Tennis Championships and world-renowned players, and Polakovics, who is sixteen years old. The two female athletes are Kloboukova, the All-Czechoslovakia champion, and Grafkova, who participated in the 25th World Table Tennis Championships.

### 捷国家男、女乒乓球队到北京

1959-05-07
第2版()
专栏：

    捷国家男、女乒乓球队到北京
    新华社6日讯　著名的捷克斯洛伐克国家男、女乒乓球队一行六人，今天在捷克斯洛伐克乒乓球协会主席库哈日率领下，乘飞机到达北京。
    捷克斯洛伐克乒乓球运动水平很高。在先后举行过的二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛中，捷克斯洛伐克男子队曾先后获得六届团体赛冠军，女子队也曾先后获得四届团体赛冠军。这次来中国的三名男运动员，是第二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛男子双打亚军、世界名将斯蒂佩克和维纳诺夫斯基，和十六岁的波拉科维奇；两名女运动员，是全捷冠军克洛乌波娃和参加过第二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛的格拉夫科娃。
