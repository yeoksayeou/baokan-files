Title: Panchen Visits Ministry of National Defense and General Staff Department, Hears Briefing on Combat Situation of Rapidly Quelling the Tibetan Rebellion
Author:
Date: 1959-05-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd -- Panchen Erdeni Chokyi Gyaltsen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Acting Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, accompanied by Zhang Jingwu, Representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet, visited the Ministry of National Defense and the General Staff Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army this afternoon at their invitation. He listened to a briefing by Lieutenant General Wang Shangrong, Director of the Operations Department, on the combat situation of the People's Liberation Army rapidly quelling the Tibetan rebellion with the active support and close cooperation of the Tibetan people.
    Marshal Luo Ronghuan, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, General Huang Kecheng, General Tan Zheng, General Chen Geng, and Senior General Li Juikui, along with others, warmly received Panchen Erdeni at the Operations Department, as well as Xiraojiatso, President of the Buddhist Association of China, Lieutenant General Apai Awang Jinmei, Deputy Commander of the Tibet Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, Jiantong Jijinmei, Director of the Panchen Kanpo Lija, and well-known Tibetan figures from Sichuan and Gansu, including Awang Jia Cuo and Huang Zhengqing, who came to visit with him.
    In the evening, Panchen Erdeni and others attended a welcoming banquet hosted by the Ministry of National Defense.

### 班禅访问国防部和总参谋部  听取关于迅速平定西藏叛乱的战斗情况介绍

1959-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅访问国防部和总参谋部
    听取关于迅速平定西藏叛乱的战斗情况介绍
    新华社3日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，在中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武的陪同下，今天下午应邀访问了国防部和中国人民解放军总参谋部，听取了作战部部长王尚荣中将关于人民解放军在西藏人民的积极支援和密切配合下，迅速平定西藏叛乱战斗情况的介绍。
    罗荣桓元帅、聂荣臻元帅和黄克诚大将、谭政大将、陈赓大将、李聚奎上将等在作战部亲切地接待了班禅额尔德尼以及和他一起前来访问的中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错，人民解放军西藏军区副司令员阿沛·阿旺晋美中将，班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任委员詹东·计晋美和四川、甘肃藏族著名人士阿旺嘉错、黄正清等。
    晚间，班禅额尔德尼等出席了国防部的欢迎宴会。
