Title: Some Pakistani Newspapers Continue to Sow Discord Between China and India
Author:
Date: 1959-05-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Karachi dispatch: Some Pakistani newspapers continue to use the Tibetan rebellion to sow discord between China and India, and are attempting to win over India to engage in so-called "common defense" with it.

"The Battle" said in today's editorial, in an alarmist tone, that now is "a critical moment, at this time, for India, acquiring the friendship of Pakistan becomes a matter of survival."

The newspaper provoked by saying, "A red storm is advancing towards India, and if this storm is not stopped, it will endanger India's survival." The newspaper believes that "in such a situation, only one safe path remains for India. It should reconcile and reach an understanding with Pakistan, and strive for Pakistan's support to stop the advance of this red storm."

"Achievement News" said in today's editorial, in an undisguised cajoling tone, "India has been using various weapons to oppose Pakistan for eleven years. But on the Tibet issue, when the powerful armed forces of People's China loomed over India as a huge danger," Pakistani President Ayub Khan "did not express happiness at India's misfortune and did not encourage China."

The newspaper said that Ayub Khan "did not take advantage of the enemy's (India's) misfortune," but said to his enemy, "I am willing to help you (India) even today, we should" resolve our differences "in a friendly manner." The newspaper said, "This will not only make the relationship between the two countries (India and Pakistan) forever pleasant, but will also provide the best defense for Pakistan and the Indian subcontinent."

### 巴基斯坦一些报纸  继续挑拨中印关系

1959-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦一些报纸
    继续挑拨中印关系
    新华社7日讯　卡拉奇消息：巴基斯坦一些报纸继续利用西藏叛乱事件挑拨中国和印度之间的关系，并且试图拉拢印度同它搞所谓“共同防御”。
    “战斗报”在今天的社论中耸人听闻地说，现在是“一个危急的时候，在这个时候，对印度说来，取得巴基斯坦的友谊成为一个有关存亡的问题”。
    这家报纸挑拨说，“红色风暴正朝着印度前进，这种风暴如果不停止的话，将危及印度的生存”。这家报纸认为，“在这样的情况下，对印度来说只留下一条安全的道路。它应该和巴基斯坦和解和达成谅解，争取巴基斯坦的支持来制止这种红色风暴的前进。”
    “成就报”在今天的社论中以露骨的笼络口吻说，“印度使用各种各样的武器来反对巴基斯坦已经有十一年了。但是在西藏问题上，当人民中国的强大的武装力量作为一种巨大的危险笼罩在印度上空的时候”，巴基斯坦总统阿尤布汗“并没有对印度的不幸表示高兴，并且没有鼓励中国”。
    这家报纸说，阿尤布汗“没有利用敌人（印度）的不幸”，而是向他的敌人说，“我即使在今天也愿意帮助你们（印度），我们应该”以友好的方式“来解决我们的分歧”。这家报纸说，“这将不仅使（巴印）两国之间的关系永远愉快，而且将对巴基斯坦和印度大陆提供最好的防御。”
