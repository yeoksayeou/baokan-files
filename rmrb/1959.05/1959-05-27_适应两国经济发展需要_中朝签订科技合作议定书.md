Title: China and North Korea Sign Scientific and Technological Cooperation Protocol to Meet the Needs of Economic Development in Both Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-05-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang.
    The second meeting of the Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee of the People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea concluded on May 21. The meeting began on April 30 in Pyongyang.

    Both sides of the committee noted with satisfaction that in the past year, scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries has made important contributions to promoting the development of economic construction and strengthening fraternal friendship.

    In order to meet the needs of the rapid development of socialist economic construction in both countries, the meeting discussed relevant issues on further strengthening scientific and technological cooperation between the two sides and signed a protocol. The protocol stipulates that the Chinese side will provide the Korean side with design materials for turbine manufacturing, cold storage, flour mills, technical data for metallurgy, chemical industry, light industry, etc., as well as seeds, seedlings, and bacterial strains.

    Korea will provide China with technical data for metallurgy, mining, agriculture, etc., and seeds, seedlings, etc.

    Both parties will send technical personnel to relevant factories and mining enterprises of the other party for inspection.

     The protocol was signed by Liu Yin, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of the Ministry of First Machine Building Industry, and Lin Bingshan, Chairman of the Korean Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Metal Industry.

### 适应两国经济发展需要  中朝签订科技合作议定书

1959-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    适应两国经济发展需要
    中朝签订科技合作议定书
    新华社平壤讯  中华人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的科学技术合作委员会第二届会议已经在5月21日结束。这个会议是从4月30日开始在平壤举行的。
    委员会双方满意地指出：在过去一年中，通过两国间的科学技术合作，对促进两国经济建设事业的发展和加强兄弟般的友谊作出了重要的贡献。
    为适应两国社会主义经济建设事业迅速发展的需要，这次会议对进一步加强双方科学技术合作的有关问题进行了讨论，并签订了议定书。议定书规定中国方面将供给朝鲜方面水轮机制造图纸、冷冻仓库、面粉厂的设计资料，冶金、化学、轻工业等方面的技术资料，以及种籽、苗木、菌种等。
    朝鲜将供给中国方面冶金、矿业、农业等方面的技术资料和种籽、苗木等。
    双方将互派技术人员到对方有关厂矿企业进行考察。
    在议定书上签字的是：委员会中国组主席、第一机械工业部副部长刘寅，委员会朝鲜组主席、金属工业省副相林炳善。
