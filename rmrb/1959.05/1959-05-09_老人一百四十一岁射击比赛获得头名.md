Title: 141-Year-Old Man Wins Shooting Competition
Author:
Date: 1959-05-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tbilisi, 8th: According to TASS, a 141-year-old man, Mito Khubulov, recently won first place in a 50-meter small-bore rifle shooting competition, hitting 48 out of 50 rings.
　　Khubulov is from the village of Tsinagari in the South Ossetian Autonomous Oblast of the Caucasus. He achieved this miracle in a shooting competition organized by the village's youth.
　　This elderly man, born in 1818, is still healthy and full of energy. Even now, he is unwilling to give up his work in the collective farm.

### 老人一百四十一岁射击比赛获得头名

1959-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　老人一百四十一岁射击比赛获得头名
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社第比利斯讯：苏联一位一百四十一岁的高龄老人米托·胡布洛夫最近获得五十公尺小口径步枪射击比赛的第一名，他的成绩是五十环中击中了四十八环。
    胡布洛夫是高加索南奥谢季自治省齐纳加里村人，他是在该村青年举办的射击比赛中创造这一奇迹的。
    这位1818年出生的高龄老人仍身体健康，精神抖擞。直至现在，他还不愿放弃自己在集体农庄中的工作。
