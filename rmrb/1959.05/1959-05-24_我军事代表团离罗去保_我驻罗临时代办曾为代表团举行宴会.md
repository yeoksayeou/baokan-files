Title: Our Military Delegation Leaves Romania for Bulgaria; Our Chargé d'Affaires in Romania Holds Banquet for the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-05-24
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, May 23rd: The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, headed by Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai, left Bucharest by special plane this morning for a friendly visit to Bulgaria.
    Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, Alexandru Drăghici, member of the Political Bureau and Minister of the Interior, General Leontin Sălăjan, Minister of the Armed Forces, and Corneliu Mănescu, Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    Wang Luming, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, embassy staff, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Romania also saw the delegation off at the airport.
    More than 3,000 representatives from all walks of life in Bucharest also saw the delegation off in the rain.
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, May 22nd: Wang Luming, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, hosted a banquet this evening for the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai, which is visiting Romania.

### 我军事代表团离罗去保  我驻罗临时代办曾为代表团举行宴会

1959-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团离罗去保
    我驻罗临时代办曾为代表团举行宴会
    新华社布加勒斯特23日电　以国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团23日上午乘专机离开布加勒斯特前往保加利亚进行友好访问。
    到机场欢送的有罗马尼亚部长会议副主席、罗马尼亚工人党中央政治局委员波德纳拉希，政治局委员、内政部长德勒吉奇，武装部队部长萨拉扬上将、外交部部长布纳丘等。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办王鲁明和使馆工作人员，以及各国驻罗马尼亚的外交使节也到机场欢送。
    布加勒斯特各界代表三千余人也冒雨欢送了代表团。
    新华社布加勒斯特22日电　中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办王鲁明今晚为以国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团访问罗马尼亚举行宴会。
