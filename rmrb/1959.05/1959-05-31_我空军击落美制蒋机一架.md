Title: Our Air Force Shoots Down a U.S.-Made Chiang Kai-shek Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1959-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: A fighter jet unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force shot down a U.S.-made B-17 Chiang Kai-shek aircraft, serial number 815, over Enping, Guangdong Province, at 23:40 on the 29th. After being hit, the enemy aircraft immediately caught fire and crashed in the Naguan area south of Enping, killing all twelve people on board.

### 我空军击落美制蒋机一架

1959-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我空军击落美制蒋机一架
    新华社30日讯　中国人民解放军空军某歼击机部队，于29日二十三时四十分在广东省恩平附近上空击落美造B—17型蒋机一架，机号八一五。这架敌机被击中后，当即起火坠落在恩平南面那关地区，机中十二人全部毙命。
