Title: The Human Body's Water Balance
Author:
Date: 1959-05-05
Page: 8

Column: Wonderful Facts

    Under normal circumstances, an adult takes in an average of five *jin* of water per day:
    Drinking water: two *jin* and six *liang*;
    Water contained in food: one *jin* and seven *liang*;
    Water produced after metabolism in the body: seven *liang*;
    Similarly, five *jin* of water are also expended each day:
    Water excreted with urine: three *jin*
    Water evaporated by the skin: one *jin*
    Water exhaled during breathing: eight *liang*
    Water excreted with feces: two *liang*

### 人体的水分收支

1959-05-05
第8版()
专栏：奇妙的事实

    人体的水分收支
    正常的情况下，成年人平均每天要收入五斤水：
    饮水　二斤六两；
    食物中所含的水　一斤七两；
    体内代谢后产生的水　七两；
    同样，每天也要支出五斤水：
    随尿排出之水　三斤
    皮肤蒸发掉的水　一斤
    呼吸时呼出之水　八两
    随粪使泄出之水　二两
