Title: Chairman Mao Receives Panchen Erdeni, Also Receives Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme and Jiantong·Jijimei, Party and State Leaders Receive Minority Nationalities Visiting Group
Author:
Date: 1959-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th May: Chairman Mao Zedong received Panchen Erdeni Choskyi Gyaltsen, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Vice-Chairman and Secretary-General, and Jiantong·Jijimei, Standing Committee Member, along with accompanying Tibetan officials this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling, Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice-Premiers Lin Biao, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Luo Ruiqing, Xi Zhongxun, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Liu Geping, Wang Feng, Zhang Jingwu, and others.

During the reception, Panchen Erdeni Choskyi Gyaltsen and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme presented Hada scarves to Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling, Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou Enlai. Chairman Mao Zedong and others reciprocated with Hada scarves.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th May: Party and state leaders Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Soong Ching Ling, Lin Biao, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, He Long, Ulanhu, Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, Panchen Erdeni Choskyi Gyaltsen, Huang Kecheng, Luo Ruiqing, Xi Zhongxun, and others, successively received the minority nationality visiting groups and youth visiting groups from Yunnan, Guizhou, and Ningxia Hui Autonomous Regions, the Chinese People's Liberation Army "May Day" observation group, and all personnel attending the Party Representative Meeting of the Third Department of the General Staff Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army this afternoon. ### 毛主席接见班禅额尔德尼  同时接见阿沛·阿旺晋美和詹东·计晋美  党和国家领导人接见少数民族参观团

1959-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见班禅额尔德尼
    同时接见阿沛·阿旺晋美和詹东·计晋美
    党和国家领导人接见少数民族参观团
    新华社7日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午接见了西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞、副主任委员兼秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美和常务委员詹东·计晋美以及随行藏族官员。
    接见时在座的，有刘少奇主席、宋庆龄副主席，朱德委员长，周恩来总理，林彪、邓小平、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、罗瑞卿、习仲勋副总理，沈钧儒、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通副委员长，刘格平、汪锋、张经武等。
    在接见的时候，班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和阿沛·阿旺晋美等向毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席、宋庆龄副主席、朱德委员长、周恩来总理敬献哈达，毛泽东主席等向他们回敬了哈达。
    新华社7日讯　党和国家的领导人毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、宋庆龄、林彪、邓小平、彭真、陈毅、贺龙、乌兰夫、黄炎培、陈叔通、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞、黄克诚、罗瑞卿、习仲勋等，今天下午先后接见了云南、贵州、宁夏回族自治区的少数民族参观团和青年参观团、中国人民解放军“五一”节观礼团、出席中国人民解放军总参谋部第三部党代表会议的全体人员。（附图片）
