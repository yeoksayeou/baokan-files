Title: Party and State Leaders Receive Delegation from the GDR Parliament
Author:
Date: 1959-05-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th - Party and state leaders Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai received this evening the Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Dieckmann, First Deputy Chairman Matern, and all members of the delegation of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic led by them.

    Present at the reception were Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Xu Bing and Zhang Su, members of the Standing Committee.

    The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Wang Dairen, was also present. (Attached photo)

### 党和国家领导人接见德议院代表团

1959-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    党和国家领导人接见德议院代表团
    新华社10日讯　党和国家领导人毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来今天晚间接见了德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团主席狄克曼、第一副主席马特恩和由他们率领的德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团的全体团员。
    接见时在座的，有人大常委会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、彭真、陈叔通，常务委员徐冰和张苏。
    德意志民主共和国驻我国大使汪戴尔也在座。（附图片）
    党和国家领导人接见德人民议院代表团，图为接见后合影。前排左起：戈丁、彭真、沈钧儒、汪戴尔、周恩来、狄克曼夫人、狄克曼、毛泽东、马特恩、刘少奇、亨内克、李济深、陈叔通。
