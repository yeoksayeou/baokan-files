	International Briefs
	Omani Nationalists Strike British Troops
		Cairo dispatch: The Omani Imam's office in Cairo, citing a telegram received on the morning of the 7th, issued an announcement stating that Omani nationalists recently launched a surprise attack on British invading forces in Firq, resulting in the death of five British soldiers. The announcement stated that the battle lasted for more than thirty minutes.
	New Jordanian Cabinet Formed
		Damascus dispatch: Amman Radio announced on the evening of the 6th that a new cabinet of the Kingdom of Jordan, headed by Hazza' Majali, had been formed. Majali is the former Minister of the Royal Court and has served as Prime Minister. He was authorized by King Hussein to form the cabinet after the resignation of former Prime Minister Rifai on the 5th.
	Tubman Reelected as President of Liberia
		London dispatch: According to a Reuters reporter from Monrovia, the capital of Liberia, the results of the recent Liberian election show that William Tubman, leader of the "True Whig Party," Liberia's only political party, has been re-elected as President of the Republic of Liberia.

### 国际简讯

1959-05-09
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
    阿曼民族主义者打击英军
    开罗讯：据阿曼教长驻开罗办事处援引7日上午收到的一封电报发表公告说，阿曼民族主义者在菲尔克最近对英国侵略军发动了一次突袭，结果打死五名英国士兵。公告说，战斗持续了三十多分钟。
    约旦新内阁组成
    大马士革讯：据安曼电台6日晚上宣布，以哈扎·马贾利为首的约旦王国新内阁已经组成。马贾利是前内阁宫廷大臣、曾经担任过首相，他是在前首相里法伊5日辞职后，由国王侯赛因授权组阁的。
    杜伯曼蝉联利比里亚总统
    伦敦讯：据路透社记者自利比里亚首都蒙罗维亚报道，利比里亚最近大选结果，利比里亚的唯一政党“真正独立党”的领袖威廉·杜伯曼再次当选为利比里亚共和国总统。
