Title: Hungarian Party and Government Delegation Leaves Shanghai for Hangzhou

Author: 
Date: 1959-05-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 4th. The Hungarian Party and Government Delegation, headed by Münnich Ferenc, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Prime Minister of the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, concluded its visit to Shanghai and left for Hangzhou by special plane this morning. Xi Zhongxun, member of the Central Committee of the CPC and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Hao Deqing, Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, accompanied them.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included: Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, and Mayor of Shanghai; Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC; Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Shi Ximin, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC; Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Zhao Zukang, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; and responsible persons from Shanghai Party, government, and military organs, local organizations of various democratic parties, and people's organizations.
    Hungarian experts in Shanghai and consuls of various countries in Shanghai were also present at the airport to see them off.

### 匈牙利党政代表团  离上海去杭州访问

1959-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    匈牙利党政代表团
    离上海去杭州访问
    新华社上海4日电　以匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会政治局委员、匈牙利工农革命政府总理明尼赫·费伦茨为首的匈牙利党政代表团，结束了在上海的访问，今天上午乘专机前往杭州。中共中央委员、国务院副总理习仲勋和我国驻匈牙利大使郝德青陪同前往。
    到机场欢送的有：中共中央政治局委员、上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施，中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，中共上海市委书记处候补书记石西民，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，上海市副市长赵祖康及上海党、政、军机关、各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体负责人。
    到机场欢送的还有匈牙利在上海的专家、各国驻上海领事等。
