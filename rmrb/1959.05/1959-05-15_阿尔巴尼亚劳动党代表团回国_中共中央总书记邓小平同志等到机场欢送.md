Title: Albanian Labor Party Delegation Returns Home; Comrade Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, and Others Welcomed and Saw Them Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-05-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Albanian Labor Party delegation, headed by Hysni Kapo, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, concluded its one-month visit to our country and left Beijing by plane today to return home.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat, Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee, and Xiong Fu, Li Qixin, Zhang Xiangshan and other responsible working personnel of the organs of the CPC Central Committee.
    Also present to see them off was the Albanian Ambassador to China, Balili. (Attached pictures)
    Right picture: Comrade Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee (first from the left in the front row), and others saw off the Albanian Labor Party delegation headed by Hysni Kapo at the airport. Left picture: After boarding the plane, the head of the delegation, Hysni Kapo, waved goodbye to those seeing him off. Xinhua News Agency reporter Niu Weiyu

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团回国  中共中央总书记邓小平同志等到机场欢送

1959-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团回国
    中共中央总书记邓小平同志等到机场欢送
    新华社14日讯　以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党政治局委员、中央书记处书记希斯尼·卡博为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团，结束了在我国历时一月的参观访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有：中共中央总书记邓小平、中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员伍修权以及中共中央机关负责工作人员熊复、李启新、张香山等人。
    前往欢送的还有阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使巴利里。（附图片）
