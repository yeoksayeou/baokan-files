Title: Soviet Satellite Provides Valuable Materials: Electron Ring Exists Around Earth's Magnetic Field; Halo Extends a Distance Equivalent to Ten Earth Radii
Author:
Date: 1959-05-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th: According to TASS reports, the Soviet Union's third artificial satellite has been in space for a full year as of May 15th. It has provided extremely important materials for human research on the space near Earth and discovered an electron ring around the Earth's magnetic field.
    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the launch of the third artificial satellite, the well-known Soviet cosmic ray expert Vernov spoke to a TASS reporter about the important discoveries of this satellite. He pointed out that the most important material it provided was the discovery of an electron ring around the Earth's magnetic field.
    This new physical phenomenon was investigated using a special counter on the satellite. This counter worked continuously for three months, and signals about the instrument's operation were received around the world. Based on these signals, an accurate map of the electron ring around the Earth can be drawn. Thanks to the help of Soviet space rockets, the final answer regarding the size of this electron ring has been obtained—the distance this halo extends is equivalent to ten Earth radii.
    Vernov speculated that it seems that any cosmic body with a magnetic field may have such a ring.
    When talking about defending against the harm of this electron ring to space travelers, Vernov proposed a specific method. He said that the intensity of the electron ring is hundreds of times greater than the intensity of cosmic rays, but the harm to space travelers can be avoided. To this end, the launch point of the spacecraft should be located in areas above 65 degrees Earth latitude, because the electron ring does not exist in the sky above these areas.

### 苏联人造卫星提供珍贵材料  围绕地球磁场有个电子环  光环延伸距离相当于十个地球半径

1959-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联人造卫星提供珍贵材料
    围绕地球磁场有个电子环
    光环延伸距离相当于十个地球半径
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科报道：截至5月15日止，苏联第三个人造卫星上天已整整一年，它为人类对地球附近空间的研究提供了极重要的材料，并发现了围绕地球磁场存在一个电子环。
    在第三个人造卫星上天一周年之际，苏联著名的宇宙线专家维尔诺夫对塔斯社记者谈到了这个卫星的重要发现，他指出，它提供的材料中，最重要的是发现了围绕地球磁场存在着一个电子组成的环。
    这种新的物理现象是依靠卫星上的一种特制的计数器考察出来的。这个计数器不间断地工作了三个月，世界各地都收到了关于这部仪器工作情况的信号。根据这些信号即可绘制一幅精确的地球周围电子环图。由于苏联宇宙火箭的帮助已经得出了关于这个电子环的大小的最后答案——这个光环所伸延出去的距离相当于十个地球半径。
    维尔诺夫设想说，看来，凡是有磁场的宇宙体都可能存在这种环。
    在谈到防御这种电子环对宇宙飞行者的危害时，维尔诺夫提出了一项具体的办法。他说，电子环的强度比宇宙线的强度还要大几百倍，但是它对宇宙飞行者的危害是可以避免的。为此，宇宙飞船的出发点应当设在地球纬度于六十五度以上的地区，因为，在这些地区的上空，电子环是不存在的。
