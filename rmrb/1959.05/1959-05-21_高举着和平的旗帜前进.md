Title: Moving Forward, Holding High the Banner of Peace
Author: Chu Tunan
Date: 1959-05-21
Page: 4

    Moving Forward, Holding High the Banner of Peace
    Chu Tunan
    The banner of peace flies high in the sky,
    The songs of peace are like surging waves.
    This represents the demands of hundreds of millions of people,
    And also demonstrates the power of hundreds of millions of people:
    To achieve lasting world peace,
    Without war and terror,
    Without exploitation and oppression,
    Let reason and friendship control and dominate the world,
    Let labor and creation satisfy people's ideals and hopes!
    Washing away the bloodshed of past history,
    And also stopping the madness of the current warmongers!
    Peace warriors of the new era,
    Hold the banner of peace even higher,
    Let our songs be even louder and more resonant!
    We are filled with new spirit and new feelings,
    We will use the common language of humanity
    To write a new history of mankind,
    Singing brand new chapters of poetry for mankind.
    New world, new humanity, new songs:
    Peace, friendship, labor, creation, and progress,
    And mankind's most beautiful and glorious hopes.
    Ten years of work—the first step towards the light!
    We have won the praise of peace-loving people all over the world,
    We have expanded our ranks,
    We have enriched our strength.
    This is unstoppable and unconquerable!
    Because on our banner—
    Forever brilliantly radiates the light of justice and wisdom,
    It forever represents the common hopes and common ideals of good people and working people!
    Holding high the banner of peace,
    Advance more bravely, advance more firmly!
    Let darkness and sighs be forgotten,
    All violence and threats will also be submerged in the surging vast ocean of peace!
    Peace is not begged for, peace is not given as charity, peace does not need to be waited for,
    Ten years of work—ten years of brilliant achievements,
    Proves that the peace movement itself is a great and irresistible force!
    Hold the banner of peace even higher,
    Move forward with even greater confidence!
    Hundreds of millions of hands are clasped together,
    Hundreds of millions of burning hearts are linked together,
    This is the great unity of peace forces,
    This is a powerful march for mankind's bright, happy, and peaceful progress,
    This is the guarantee of mankind's bright, happy, and peaceful progress!
    Therefore, peace warriors of the new era,
    Sing more vigorously,
    Let our songs of peace be as long as heaven and earth!
    Hold the banner of peace even higher,
    Our banner of peace will forever point to the light and hope of mankind,
    Forever illuminating the road ahead for mankind, like a glorious and beautiful never-setting sun!

### 高举着和平的旗帜前进
楚图南
1959-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    高举着和平的旗帜前进
    楚图南
    和平的旗帜在高空飘扬，
    和平的歌声如同汹涌的巨浪。
    这表示了亿万人的要求，
    也显示了亿万人的力量：
    要实现世界的持久和平，
    没有战争和恐怖，
    没有剥削和压迫，
    让理智和友爱掌握和支配了世界，
    让劳动和创造满足了人们的理想和希望！
    洗去过去历史上的血腥，
    也制止当前的战争贩子们的疯狂！
    新时代的和平的战士们，
    更高的高举着和平的旗帜，
    让我们的歌声也更高昂而响亮！
    我们充沛着新的精神和新的感情，
    我们将以人类共同的言语
    写下了人类的新的历史，
    歌唱着人类崭新的诗歌的篇章。
    新的世界、新的人类、新的歌声：
    和平、友爱、劳动、创造和进步，
    还有人类最美满、最光辉的希望。
    十年来的工作——走向光明的第一步！
    我们已赢得了全世界和平人民的赞扬，
    我们扩大了我们的队伍，
    我们已充实了我们的力量。
    这是不可阻止，也是不可征服的！
    因为我们的旗帜上——
    永远灿烂地放射着正义和智慧的光芒，
    它永远代表了善良人民和劳动人民的共同的希望和共同的理想！
    高举着和平的旗帜，
    更英勇的前进、更坚定的前进！
    让黑暗和叹息都被遗忘了罢，
    一切的暴力和威胁也将淹没在波澜澎湃的和平的广阔的海洋！
    和平不是乞求得来、和平也不要施舍、和平也不须等待，
    十年来的工作——十年来的光辉成就，
    证明了和平运动的自身便是伟大无比的不可抗拒的力量！
    更高的高举着和平的旗帜，
    更加信心百倍地前进罢！
    亿万人的手紧握在一起，
    亿万人的炽热的心连结在一起，
    这是和平力量的大团结，
    这是人类光明、幸福和和平进步的有力的进军，
    这是人类光明、幸福和和平进步的胜利的保障！
    所以新的时代的和平的战士们，
    更雄昂地歌唱，
    让我们的和平的歌声如同天地一样的久长！
    更高的高举和平的旗帜，
    我们的和平的旗帜永远指向人类的光明和希望、
    永远照耀着人类前进的道路、如同光辉美丽的永不没落的太阳！
