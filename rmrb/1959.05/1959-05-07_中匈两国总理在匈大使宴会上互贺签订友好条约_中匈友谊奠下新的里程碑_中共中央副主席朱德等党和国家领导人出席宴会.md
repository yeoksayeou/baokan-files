Title: Chinese and Hungarian Prime Ministers Congratulate Each Other at Hungarian Ambassador's Banquet on the Signing of the Friendship Treaty. Sino-Hungarian Friendship Lays a New Milestone. Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee Zhu De and Other Party and State Leaders Attend the Banquet

Author: 
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - At the grand banquet held tonight by Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nogradi, in honor of the Hungarian Party and Government delegation's visit to China, hundreds of Chinese and foreign guests warmly congratulated the signing of the Sino-Hungarian Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation, which marks a new milestone in Sino-Hungarian friendship, and celebrated the further development of the comprehensive friendly and cooperative relations between the two peoples.

Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Central Committee Political Bureau member and Premier of the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, Münnich Ferenc, and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, Zhou Enlai, spoke at the banquet successively. They pointed out that the signing of the Sino-Hungarian Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation further consolidated and developed the fraternal friendship between the two peoples. Premier Münnich said in his speech that the Hungarian Party and Government delegation's visit to China and the signing of the Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation have made the friendly and comradely relations between the two countries even closer and deeper. In the future, the two countries will be able to cooperate more closely and support each other better in political, economic, and cultural aspects. Premier Zhou also emphasized in his speech that the Sino-Hungarian Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation is a new milestone in the development of Sino-Hungarian friendship and a major event worthy of celebration by the people of both countries. This treaty will surely further develop the comprehensive friendly and cooperative relations between the two peoples, which is very beneficial to the people of China and Hungary, the entire socialist camp, and world peace.

Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhu De, was invited to attend tonight's banquet. Also present at the banquet were: members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Peng Zhen; member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi; Vice Premiers of the State Council, Nie Rongzhen, Luo Ruiqing, Xi Zhongxun; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, Cheng Qian, Bainqen Erdini Qoigyijabu; President of the Supreme People's Court, Xie Juezai; Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Zhang Dingcheng; and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, Zhang Zhizhong; and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People’s Political Consultative Conference, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme.

Also present at the banquet were Secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, Alternate Secretary, Yang Shangkun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Hanfu, China's Ambassador to Hungary, Hao Deqing, heads of various departments of state organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to China.

Premier Münnich spoke first after the banquet began, warmly congratulating the Chinese people on their greater achievements in socialist construction, and praising the Chinese people for their efforts to safeguard world peace. He also said in his speech that our meeting with the Chinese working people, their brotherly love for us, and the enthusiastic and hard work being carried out in your country have left us with an indelible impression. We will bring the thousands of greetings entrusted to us by the Chinese people to the Hungarian people.

Premier Zhou said in his speech at the banquet that the party and government leaders of China and Hungary had sincere and friendly talks on international issues of mutual concern, the socialist construction of the two countries, and the further expansion of friendly cooperation between the two countries and parties, and reached a complete consensus on these issues. Premier Zhou emphasized that the Hungarian Party and Government delegation's visit will have a profound impact on the development of the fraternal friendship between the people of China and Hungary, and will be recorded as a glorious page in the history of Sino-Hungarian friendship.

The banquet hall of the Hungarian Embassy was decorated with the national flags of Hungary and China. After the speeches of Premier Münnich and Premier Zhou, the national anthems of China and Hungary were played respectively.

During the nearly two-hour banquet, the hall was filled with joyful laughter and cordial wishes. The guests and hosts enthusiastically raised their glasses frequently to celebrate the eternal friendship between the people of China and Hungary and the great solidarity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

### 中匈两国总理在匈大使宴会上互贺签订友好条约  中匈友谊奠下新的里程碑  中共中央副主席朱德等党和国家领导人出席宴会

1959-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　中匈两国总理在匈大使宴会上互贺签订友好条约
    中匈友谊奠下新的里程碑
    中共中央副主席朱德等党和国家领导人出席宴会
    新华社6日讯　在匈牙利驻华大使诺格拉第今晚为匈牙利党政代表团访问中国而举行的盛大宴会上，几百位中外来宾热烈祝贺作为中匈友谊新的里程碑的中匈友好合作条约的签订，庆贺两国人民全面的友好合作关系的进一步发展。
    匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会政治局委员、工农革命政府总理明尼赫·费伦茨和中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来在宴会上先后讲话，他们指出，中匈友好合作条约的签订进一步巩固和发展了两国人民的兄弟友谊。明尼赫总理在讲话中说，匈牙利党政代表团的这次访问中国和两国友好合作条约的签订，已经使两国之间的友好和同志关系变得更加亲密和深入，今后两国将能够合作得更加紧密，在政治、经济和文化等各方面将能够更好地互相支持。周总理在讲话中也强调说，中匈友好合作条约是中匈友谊发展的新的里程碑，是值得我们两国人民共同庆贺的大事。这个条约必将使两国人民之间的全面的友好合作关系得到进一步发展，这对于中匈两国人民、对于整个社会主义阵营和世界和平都是十分有利的。
    中共中央副主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德应邀出席了今晚的宴会。出席宴会的还有：中共中央政治局委员彭真，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅，国务院副总理聂荣臻、罗瑞卿、习仲勋，副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、陈叔通、程潜、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席张治中，政协全国委员会副主席阿沛·阿旺晋美。
    出席宴会的，还有中共中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆，外交部副部长章汉夫，我国驻匈牙利大使郝德青，国家机关各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人和各国驻华使节。
    明尼赫总理在宴会开始以后首先讲话，热烈祝贺中国人民在社会主义建设中获得更大成就，赞扬中国人民正在为维护世界和平而努力。他在讲话中还说，我们同中国劳动人民的会见，他们对我们表现的兄弟般的热爱，和在你们国家里进行着的热情而辛勤的劳动，给我们留下了不可磨灭的印象，我们将把中国人民委托转达的千万个问候带给匈牙利人民。
    周总理在宴会上的讲话中说，中匈两国的党政领导人这次就双方关心的国际问题、两国社会主义建设的情况以及进一步扩大两国和两党的友好合作关系问题进行了诚恳亲切的会谈，并且在这些问题上取得了完全一致的认识。周总理着重指出，这次匈牙利党政代表团的访问，将对中匈两国人民的兄弟友谊的发展产生深远的影响，将作为光辉的一页载入中匈友好的史册。
    匈牙利大使馆的宴会厅里悬挂着匈中两国国旗。在明尼赫总理和周总理讲话结束以后，分别奏起了中匈两国国歌。
    在将近两个小时的宴会中，充满了欢乐的笑声和亲切的祝愿，宾主热情地频频举杯，欢庆中匈两国人民永恒的友谊，欢庆以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的伟大团结。
