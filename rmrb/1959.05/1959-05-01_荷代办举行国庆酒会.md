Title: Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1959-05-01
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- Dutch Kingdom Chargé d'Affaires to China, C.G. Sickinghe, held a reception this afternoon in celebration of the birthday of Queen Juliana of the Netherlands and Dutch National Day. Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, was invited to attend the reception. Also invited were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

### 荷代办举行国庆酒会

1959-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    荷代办举行国庆酒会
    新华社30日讯　荷兰王国驻中国代办司灵博为荷兰女王朱丽安娜的诞辰和荷兰国庆日，今天下午举行酒会。我外交部副部长罗贵波应邀出席了酒会。应邀出席的还有各国的驻华使节和外交官员。
