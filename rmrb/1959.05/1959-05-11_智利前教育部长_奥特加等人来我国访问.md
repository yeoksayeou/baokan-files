Title: Former Chilean Minister of Education Ortega and Others Visit China
Author:
Date: 1959-05-11
Page: 4

    Former Chilean Minister of Education
    Ortega and Others Visit China
    Xinhua News Agency, May 10th. Former Chilean Minister of Education Ortega and his wife, Chilean Socialist Party Deputy Secretary-General Alvarez and his wife, and former Mayor of Concepción Ramirez, arrived in Beijing this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs for a friendly visit to our country.

### 智利前教育部长  奥特加等人来我国访问

1959-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    智利前教育部长
    奥特加等人来我国访问
    新华社10日讯　智利前教育部长奥特加夫妇、智利社会党副总书记阿尔瓦莱斯夫妇和公塞普森市前市长拉米莱斯，应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国进行友好访问，已经在今天下午到达北京。
