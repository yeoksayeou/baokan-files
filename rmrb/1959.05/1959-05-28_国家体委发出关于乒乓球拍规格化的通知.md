Title: State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Issues Notice on Standardization of Ping-Pong Rackets

Author:

Date: 1959-05-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 27th: The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission today issued a notice on the standardization of ping-pong rackets, deciding that from July 1st of this year, all ping-pong rackets used in any form of competition in China must comply with the following new regulations.

The main content of these new regulations includes: ping-pong rackets must be made of wood, with no restrictions on size, shape, and weight. Ordinary pimpled rubber (pips out) can be attached to the wooden board, or sponge rubber (i.e., ordinary pimpled rubber attached to sponge, with the pips either facing inward or outward) can be attached. However, the thickness of each pimpled rubber side shall not exceed two millimeters; the thickness of each sponge rubber side shall not exceed four millimeters.

This type of racket is consistent with the ping-pong racket specifications decided by the International Table Tennis Federation at its annual meeting in Dortmund this year. It will promote the improvement of the technical level of table tennis in our country.

### 国家体委发出关于乒乓球拍规格化的通知

1959-05-28
第6版()
专栏：

    国家体委发出关于乒乓球拍规格化的通知
    新华社27日讯  国家体委今天发出关于乒乓球拍规格化的通知，决定从今年7月1日开始，在我国任何形式比赛中使用的乒乓球拍，一律要符合以下的一些新规定。
    这些新规定的主要内容包括：乒乓球拍必须是木制的，大小、形状和重量不限，木板上可以附着普通的颗粒胶皮（胶粒向外），也可以附着海绵胶皮（即海绵上面再贴上普通颗粒胶皮，胶粒可以向里或向外）。但每面颗粒胶皮的厚度不得超过二公厘；每面海绵胶皮的厚度不得超过四公厘。
    这种球拍和国际乒乓球联合会今年在多特蒙德年会上决定的乒乓球拍规格是一致的。它将促进我国乒乓球运动技术水平的提高。
