Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairpersons Soong Ching Ling and Dong Biwu Receive Ambassadors and Chargé d'affaires of Various Countries to China
Author:
Date: 1959-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Vice-Chairpersons Soong Ching Ling and Dong Biwu, received the ambassadors, chargés d'affaires, and acting chargés d'affaires of various countries to China at Ziguang Pavilion in Zhongnanhai this afternoon. They were accompanied by Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu, and Vice Minister Luo Guibo.

During the reception, Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Vice-Chairpersons Soong Ching Ling and Dong Biwu shook hands with the ambassadors, chargés d'affaires, and acting chargés d'affaires of various countries. The ambassadors, chargés d'affaires, and acting chargés d'affaires extended warm congratulations to Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Vice-Chairpersons Soong Ching Ling and Dong Biwu, wishing them good health. Chairman Liu Shaoqi raised his glass to wish the guests good health, world peace, happiness to the people of all countries, and the continuous development of friendship between the people of all countries. Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairpersons Soong Ching Ling and Dong Biwu took a group photo with the ambassadors, chargés d'affaires, and acting chargés d'affaires of various countries to China.
	Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo

### 刘少奇主席宋庆龄董必武副主席接见各国驻我国的大使和代办

1959-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席宋庆龄董必武副主席
    接见各国驻我国的大使和代办
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，副主席宋庆龄、董必武，今天下午在中南海紫光阁接见了各国驻中国的大使、代办和临时代办。陪同的，有陈毅副总理兼外交部长、章汉夫副部长和罗贵波副部长。
    在接见的时候，刘少奇主席，宋庆龄、董必武副主席同各国大使、代办和临时代办一一握手。各国大使、代办和临时代办向刘少奇主席，宋庆龄、董必武副主席表示热烈的祝贺，祝他们身体健康。刘少奇主席举杯祝客人们身体健康，祝世界和平，祝各国人民幸福，祝各国人民之间的友谊不断发展。（附图片）
