Title: Tarazi Appointed UAR Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1959-05-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 1st: The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the United Arab Republic announced today that, according to a Republic order, Tarazi has been appointed UAR Ambassador to the People's Republic of China.
　　Tarazi formerly served as the UAR Ambassador to Czechoslovakia.

### 塔拉齐任阿联驻我大使

1959-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    塔拉齐任阿联驻我大使
    新华社开罗1日电　阿拉伯联合共和国外交部今天宣布，根据共和国命令，已经任命塔拉齐为阿联驻中华人民共和国大使。
    塔拉齐曾经担任阿联驻捷克斯洛伐克大使。
