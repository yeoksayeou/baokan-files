Title: Chairman Liu Approves Sino-Vietnamese and Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreements
Author:
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th dispatch: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, in accordance with the decision of the First Session of the Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress, approved on the 6th the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, signed in Hanoi on January 16th of this year, as well as the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, signed in Pyongyang on February 21st of this year.

### 刘主席批准中越和中朝文化合作协定

1959-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘主席批准中越和中朝文化合作协定
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇根据第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第一次会议的决定，在6日批准了今年1月16日在河内签订的中华人民共和国政府和越南民主共和国政府文化合作协定以及今年2月21日在平壤签订的中华人民共和国政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府文化合作协定。
