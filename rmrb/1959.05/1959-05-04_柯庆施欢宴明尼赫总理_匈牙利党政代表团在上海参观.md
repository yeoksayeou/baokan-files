Title: Ke Qingshi Hosts Banquet for Prime Minister Münnich; Hungarian Party and Government Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 3rd: Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, and Mayor of Shanghai, held a grand banquet tonight to warmly welcome all the distinguished guests of the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation headed by Prime Minister Münnich.

In his speech at the banquet, Mayor Ke Qingshi warmly praised the achievements of the Hungarian people in socialist construction. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude in particular for the strong support and enthusiastic help given by the Hungarian people to the socialist construction of the people of Shanghai and the Chinese people. He said that the visit of the distinguished guests to Shanghai brought profound friendship and great encouragement to the people of Shanghai.

In his speech, Prime Minister Münnich mentioned that during their visit to various parts of China, they saw everywhere that China's socialist construction was developing rapidly, and many new things were replacing the old. He said, "We are happy for the socialist construction achievements we have made, and we are also happy for the victories achieved by our Chinese friends." Münnich enthusiastically raised his glass and suggested that everyone drink to the growing strength of the socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union.

Xi Zhongxun, member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, who accompanied the distinguished guests from Beijing to Shanghai, and Hao Deqing, China's ambassador to Hungary, were invited to attend the banquet.

Also present were Chen Pixian, Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee Secretariat, Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Municipal Committee and Deputy Mayor, Shi Ximin, Alternate Secretary of the Municipal Committee Secretariat, Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat and Deputy Mayor, Zhao Zukang, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, heads of relevant departments in Shanghai, Hungarian experts in Shanghai, and consuls of various countries in Shanghai.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 3rd: Prime Minister Münnich Ferenc and all the distinguished guests of the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation, accompanied by Xi Zhongxun, member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee Secretariat and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Hao Deqing, China's Ambassador to Hungary, and others, visited the Shanghai Construction Machinery Factory and the No. 2 State Cotton Mill this morning. In the afternoon, the distinguished guests visited the Children's Palace.

The distinguished guests were very interested in the Construction Machinery Factory's method of manufacturing large-scale equipment by "ants gnawing at bones."

### 柯庆施欢宴明尼赫总理  匈牙利党政代表团在上海参观

1959-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　柯庆施欢宴明尼赫总理
    匈牙利党政代表团在上海参观
    新华社上海3日电　中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施今晚举行盛大宴会，热烈欢迎以明尼赫总理为首的匈牙利党政代表团的全体贵宾。
    柯庆施市长在宴会的讲话中热烈赞扬匈牙利人民在社会主义建设中取得的成就，他特别对匈牙利人民给予上海人民和中国人民的社会主义建设的有力支持及热情帮助，表示衷心的感谢。他说，贵宾们这次到上海访问给上海人民带来深厚的友谊和巨大的鼓舞。
    明尼赫总理在讲话中谈到，这次他们到中国各地访问，到处都看到中国的社会主义建设正在飞速发展，许多新的东西正在代替旧的东西。他说，我们为自己取得的社会主义建设成就而高兴，也为中国朋友所取得的胜利而高兴。明尼赫热情地举杯，建议大家为以苏联为首的社会主义各国力量的日益强大而干杯。
  陪同贵宾们由北京到上海的中共中央委员、国务院副总理习仲勋，我国驻匈牙利大使郝德青等人应邀出席宴会作陪。
    出席作陪的，还有中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显，市委书记、副市长曹荻秋，市委书记处候补书记石西民，书记处候补书记、副市长刘述周，上海市副市长赵祖康，上海市各有关部门的负责人，在上海的匈牙利专家和各国驻上海领事。
    新华社上海3日电　明尼赫·费伦茨总理和匈牙利党政代表团的全体贵宾，今天上午由中共中央委员、国务院副总理习仲勋，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，外交部副部长曾涌泉，我国驻匈牙利大使郝德青等陪同，参观了上海建设机器厂和国棉二厂。下午，贵宾们参观了少年宫。
    贵宾们对建设机器厂以“蚂蚁啃骨头”的方法制造大型设备，感到很大的兴趣。
