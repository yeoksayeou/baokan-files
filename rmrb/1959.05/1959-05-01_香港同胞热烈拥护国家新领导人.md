Title: Hong Kong Compatriots Warmly Support the New National Leaders
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, 29th - Compatriots from all walks of life in Hong Kong warmly support the newly elected and appointed national leaders of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress.

    When the news of the election of the new national leaders reached here on the afternoon of the 27th, Hong Kong's "Wen Wei Po," "Ta Kung Pao," and other newspapers reported this good news on the front page.

    Workers from various industries in Hong Kong, such as Yau Wing, Dairy Farm, motorcycle, Hai Woo, and live pig trades, waited by the radio on the afternoon of the 27th for the good news. In the Hong Kong Yau Wing Workers' Union, when the Central People's Broadcasting Station broadcast the news of the newly elected and appointed national leaders of the Second National People's Congress, the workers present applauded enthusiastically and unanimously expressed their firm support.

    Chen Yaocai, President of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Trade Union Federation, said in a statement last night: "The newly elected national leaders are the most ideal candidates and meet the aspirations of the workers in Hong Kong. We express our warm support." He emphasized: "Although Chairman Mao is no longer the Chairman of the State, he is still the leader sincerely loved by all ethnic groups in the country. Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, under the leadership of our great socialist motherland's great leader Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu Shaoqi and other national leaders, our great socialist motherland will be built even better."

    Bai Xueshu, Vice Chairman of the Hong Kong Chinese Muslim Association, expressed his heartfelt support for the newly elected national leaders. He said: Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, Chairman Mao, and the newly elected national leaders, our motherland will continue to develop and achieve even greater accomplishments.

    Su Chongming, Chairman of the Hong Kong Chinese Clerical Staff Association, Tang Bingda, Chairman of the Hong Kong North and South Firms Association, and Feng Yong, Chairman of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Planting General Association, also made statements, expressing their joy and encouragement at the newly elected national leaders and the achievements of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress.

### 香港同胞热烈拥护国家新领导人

1959-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    香港同胞热烈拥护国家新领导人
    新华社香港29日电　香港各界同胞热烈拥护第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议新选出和任命的国家领导人。
    当国家新的领导人员选出的消息在27日下午传到这里后，香港
  “文汇报”、“大公报”和其他一些报纸都以一版头条地位报道了这个喜讯。
    香港洋务、牛奶、摩托、海坞、生猪行等各业工人，27日下午就守在收音机旁，等候佳音。在香港洋务工会里，当中央广播电台播送了第二届全国人民代表大会新选出和任命的国家领导人的消息后，在场的工友热烈鼓掌，并一致表示坚决拥护。
    港九工会联合会会长陈耀材昨晚发表谈话说：“新当选的国家领导人员都是最理想的人选，符合香港工人同胞的心意，我们表示热烈拥护。”他强调指出：“毛主席不当国家主席，仍然是全国各族人民衷心敬爱的领袖。我们伟大的社会主义祖国在中国共产党领导下，在中国人民伟大领袖毛主席和刘少奇主席等国家领导人的领导下，将会建设得更加美好。”
    香港中国回教协会副主席白学修表示衷心拥戴这次新选出的国家领导人。他说：我们祖国在中国共产党、毛主席和新选出的国家领导人的领导下，将会继续向前发展，获得更加伟大的成就。
    香港华人文员协会主席苏重明、香港南北行公所理事长汤秉达、港九种植总公会理事长冯镛等都纷纷发表谈话，对新选出的国家领导人和第二届人代会第一次会议的成就感到欢欣鼓舞。
