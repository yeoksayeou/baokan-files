Title: Youth League Representatives from Four Latin American Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-05-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, May 12th. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, representatives from the Communist Youth Leagues of Argentina, Chile, Uruguay, and Venezuela, one from each country, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to our country.

### 拉丁美洲四个国家共青联盟代表到京

1959-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲四个国家共青联盟代表到京
    新华社12日讯  应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷、智利、乌拉圭、委内瑞拉四国共产主义青年联盟的代表各一人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
