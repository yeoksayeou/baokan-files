    Nanjing workers are actively engaged in the production increase and economy practice movement centered on technological innovation. This is Deng Heming (left), a technician, and Lin Shouren (middle), and Qian Zhongyu (right), technicians from the optical workshop of the Nanjing Teaching Instrument Factory, inspecting the quality of eyepiece tubes made of bakelite instead of steel.
                Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Qinghua
