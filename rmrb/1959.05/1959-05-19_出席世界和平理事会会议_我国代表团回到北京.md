Title: Chinese Delegation Returns to Beijing After Attending World Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1959-05-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Chinese delegation to the special session of the World Peace Council in Stockholm, led by its head Guo Moruo, returned to Beijing by plane today. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Lian Guan, Xin Zhi-chao, Da Pǔshēng, Jù Zàn, Zhang Jie, Luo Jun, Pei Lisheng, Wang Tuo and other people from all walks of life.
    Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji also returned to Beijing on the same plane.

### 出席世界和平理事会会议  我国代表团回到北京

1959-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会会议
    我国代表团回到北京
    新华社18日讯　中国出席世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩特别会议代表团，在团长郭沫若率领下，今天乘飞机回到北京。前往机场欢迎的有连贯、辛志超、达浦生、巨赞、张杰、罗俊、裴丽生、王拓等各界人士。
    日本和平人士西园寺公一也同机回到北京。
