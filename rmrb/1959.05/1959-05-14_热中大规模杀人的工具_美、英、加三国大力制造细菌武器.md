Title: Tools for Frenzied Mass Murder: US, UK, and Canada Vigorously Manufacture Bacteriological Weapons
Author:
Date: 1959-05-14
Page: 5

    Tools for Frenzied Mass Murder
    US, UK, and Canada Vigorously Manufacture Bacteriological Weapons
    Xinhua News Agency, May 12th, Chicago dispatch: Former Director-General of the World Health Organization of the United Nations, Brock Chisholm, revealed on the 11th that the United States, Britain, and Canada are vigorously developing bacteriological weapons.
    Chisholm wrote in the "Bulletin of the Atomic Scientists" that in Fort Detrick near Washington, the US capital, in Porton Down in the UK, and in Suffield, Canada, there are many huge research institutes studying and developing various biological weapons for mass killing.
    Chisholm particularly touted the "extraordinary potency" of a botulinum toxin.

### 热中大规模杀人的工具  美、英、加三国大力制造细菌武器

1959-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    热中大规模杀人的工具
    美、英、加三国大力制造细菌武器
    新华社12日讯  芝加哥消息：联合国世界卫生组织前总干事布罗克·契泽姆11日透露，美国、英国和加拿大正在大力发展细菌武器。
    契泽姆在“原子科学家公报”上写道，在美国首都华盛顿附近的德特利克堡、在英国的波尔顿和在加拿大的苏菲德，有许多巨大的研究所正在研究发展各种大批杀人的生物武器。
    契泽姆特别吹嘘一种腊肠毒杆菌毒素“异乎寻常的效力”。
