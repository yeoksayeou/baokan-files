Title: Peng Zhen Meets with Mayor of Phnom Penh, Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1959-05-10
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: Peng Zhen, the Mayor of Beijing, met this afternoon with the Mayor of Phnom Penh, Cambodia, Mac Guang, and the Director of the Phnom Penh Tax Bureau, Di Tong, and the City Appearance and Architectural Engineer, Bu Hua.

    Present at the meeting were Jia Xingwu, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, and others. Yang An, Counselor of the Cambodian Embassy in China, was also present.

    The Phnom Penh delegation, headed by Mac Guang, is visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow Soviet, and is passing through Beijing. They will visit Beijing for two days.

### 彭真会见柬埔寨金边市市长

1959-05-10
第2版()
专栏：

    彭真会见柬埔寨金边市市长
    新华社9日讯　北京市市长彭真今天下午会见了柬埔寨金边市市长麦广和金边市税务局局长狄通、市容建筑工程师布华。
    会见时，在座的有北京市人民委员会秘书长贾星五等。柬埔寨驻中国大使馆参赞杨安也在座。
    以麦广为首的金边市代表团，是应莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会主席邀请访问苏联路过北京的。他们将在北京参观访问两天。
