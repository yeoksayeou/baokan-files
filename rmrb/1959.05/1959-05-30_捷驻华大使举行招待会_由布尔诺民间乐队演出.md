Title: Czechoslovak Ambassador Holds Reception; Brno Folk Band Performs

Author:
Date: 1959-05-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušniak, held a reception tonight featuring a performance by the Czechoslovak Brno Folk Band. This band was passing through Beijing on its way home after a performance tour in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 捷驻华大使举行招待会  由布尔诺民间乐队演出

1959-05-30
第5版()
专栏：

    捷驻华大使举行招待会
    由布尔诺民间乐队演出
    新华社29日讯　捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克今晚举行招待会，由捷克斯洛伐克布尔诺民间乐队举行演出。这个乐队是到越南民主共和国进行访问演出后，在归国途中路过北京的。
