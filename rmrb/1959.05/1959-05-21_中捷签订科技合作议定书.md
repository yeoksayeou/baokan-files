Title: China and Czechoslovakia Sign Scientific and Technological Cooperation Protocol
Author: 
Date: 1959-05-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation held its seventh session in Beijing from the 15th to the 20th of this month, and on the afternoon of the 20th, signed the protocol of the seventh session of the Sino-Czechoslovak Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee.

    Signing the protocol on behalf of the Chinese side was Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building and Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee; signing on behalf of the Czechoslovak side was Pú Zěkè, First Deputy Chairman of the Czechoslovak State Planning Commission and Chairman of the Czechoslovak Group.

    The meeting was conducted in an extremely friendly atmosphere, in the spirit of mutual selfless assistance to accelerate the common development of science and technology in the two countries.

    At this session, both sides discussed issues related to further expanding scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries and strengthening mutual ties between relevant ministries and research institutes.

    According to the provisions of the protocol, both sides will provide each other with technical data in metallurgy, machinery, chemical industry, light industry, and other fields, and will receive and dispatch experts for inspection and technical assistance.

### 中捷签订科技合作议定书

1959-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    中捷签订科技合作议定书
    新华社20日讯　中华人民共和国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国科学与技术合作联合委员会于本月15日至20日在北京召开了第七届会议，并在20日下午签订了中捷科学技术合作委员会第七届会议议定书。
    代表中方在议定书上签字的是第一机械工业部副部长、委员会中方组主席汪道涵；代表捷方在议定书上签字的是捷克斯洛伐克国家计划委员会第一副主席、捷方组主席普泽克。
    会议是本着相互无私地援助以加速两国科学技术共同发展的精神在极其友好气氛中进行的。
    在这届会议上，双方曾讨论了有关进一步扩大两国间的科学技术合作和加强有关部和研究所间的相互联系等问题。
    根据议定书的规定，双方将在冶金、机械、化工、轻工等方面相互提供技术资料，并接待和派遣考察专家和技术援助专家。
