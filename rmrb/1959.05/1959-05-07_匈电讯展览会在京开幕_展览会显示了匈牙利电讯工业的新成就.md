Title: Hungarian Telecommunications Exhibition Opens in Beijing; Exhibition Showcases New Achievements of Hungarian Telecommunications Industry
Author:
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Hungarian Telecommunications Exhibition opened in Beijing today.

　　The exhibition introduces the development of the Hungarian telecommunications industry. The exhibits include telephone equipment, radio, television, broadcasting, microwave, nuclear instruments, and instruments for industrial and electrical measurements, demonstrating the latest achievements of the Hungarian telecommunications industry. During the exhibition, Hungarian experts will exchange technical experiences with technical personnel from relevant departments in China.

　　Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Hao Deqing, Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, and others attended the opening ceremony of the exhibition.

　　Members of the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation currently visiting China, including Csergő János, Minister of Metallurgy and Machine Industry, Incze Jenő, Minister of Foreign Trade, Kang Baoertai, Head of the Hungarian Telecommunications Exhibition Group, and Szigeti, Counselor of the Hungarian Embassy in China, also participated in the opening ceremony.

　　Chairman Nan Hanchen and Minister Csergő János spoke at the meeting successively.

　　On the evening of the 5th, Chairman Nan Hanchen held a banquet to celebrate the opening of the exhibition.

### 匈电讯展览会在京开幕  展览会显示了匈牙利电讯工业的新成就

1959-05-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　匈电讯展览会在京开幕
    展览会显示了匈牙利电讯工业的新成就
    新华社6日讯　匈牙利电讯展览会今天在北京开幕。
    展览会介绍了匈牙利电讯工业发展的情况，展出的电话设备、无线电、电视、广播、微波、核子仪器、工业及电工测量用的仪器等展品，显示了匈牙利电讯工业的最新成就。展览期间，匈牙利专家们将同我国有关部门的技术人员交流技术经验。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸，第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青等，出席了展览会的开幕式。
    正在我国访问的匈牙利党政代表团团员、冶金和机械工业部长策尔戈·亚诺什，对外贸易部长英切·耶诺，匈牙利电讯展览团团长康包尔台，匈牙利驻华大使馆参赞西格蒂等都参加了开幕式。
    南汉宸主席和策尔戈·亚诺什部长先后在会上讲了话。
    5日晚上，南汉宸主席曾举行宴会，庆祝展览会的举办。
