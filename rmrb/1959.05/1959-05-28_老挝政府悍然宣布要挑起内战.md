Title: Lao Government Brazenly Announces Intention to Provoke Civil War
Author:
Date: 1959-05-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Vientiane, May 26th: According to an Agence France-Presse dispatch from Vientiane on May 26th, the Royal Lao Government, at a special cabinet meeting held on the evening of the 25th, further formally announced its intention to reignite the civil war in Laos. It is reported that the Sananikone government formally decided at this meeting to use force against the former Pathet Lao Fighting Units, "and to treat them as rebels."
Prior to this, the Sananikone government had been launching armed persecution against the former Pathet Lao Fighting Units since May 11th.
On May 23rd, the Royal Lao Government forces launched an attack on the former Pathet Lao Fighting Units in the Plain of Jars in Xieng Khouang Province, thereby effectively dragging Laos back into a state of civil war.

### 老挝政府悍然宣布要挑起内战

1959-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝政府悍然宣布要挑起内战
    新华社27日讯  据法新社万象26日消息，老挝王国政府在25日晚间举行的内阁特别会议上，进一步正式宣布要重新挑起老挝的内战。据报道，萨纳尼空政府在这次会议上正式决定以武力对付前寮国战斗部队，“并且把他们当作叛乱分子来对付”。
    在此以前，萨纳尼空政府从5月11日起就对前寮国战斗部队发动武装迫害。
    5月23日，老挝王国政府军对川圹省查尔平原的前寮国战斗部队发动进攻，从而在实际上已经把老挝重新拖入内战的局面。
