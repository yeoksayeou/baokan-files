Title: Chairman Liu, Premier Zhou, and Foreign Minister Chen Send Telegrams to Congratulate Afghanistan's Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1959-05-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 26th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi sent a telegram on the 25th to King Mohammed Zahir Shah of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, congratulating him on the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. The telegram reads as follows:

Your Majesty Mohammed Zahir Shah, King of the Kingdom of Afghanistan:

On the occasion of the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of Afghanistan on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. May the traditional friendship between China and Afghanistan develop day by day. I wish the Kingdom of Afghanistan prosperity, its people happiness, and Your Majesty good health.

Liu Shaoqi
Chairman of the People's Republic of China
May 25, 1959, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, May 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram on the 25th to Sardar Mohammed Daoud Khan, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, congratulating him on the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. The telegram reads as follows:

His Royal Highness Sardar Mohammed Daoud Khan, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan:

On the occasion of the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Highness and the Government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.

The Chinese people are pleased to see that the deep friendship between China and Afghanistan, built on the basis of the Five Principles, is constantly developing, and the friendly cooperative relations between the two countries are becoming closer. I wish the Kingdom of Afghanistan and its people new achievements in building their own country and safeguarding peace in Asia and the world. I wish Your Highness good health.

Zhou Enlai
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
May 25, 1959

Xinhua News Agency, May 26th: Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, sent a telegram on the 25th to Sardar Mohammed Naim Khan, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, congratulating him on the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. The telegram reads as follows:

Your Royal Highness Sardar Mohammed Niam Khan, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Afghanistan:

I am delighted to congratulate you sincerely on the occasion of the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. I wish the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Afghanistan further development. I wish China and Afghanistan jointly make new contributions to the cause of safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

Chen Yi
Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
May 25, 1959

### 刘主席  周总理  陈外长  电贺阿富汗独立纪念日

1959-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席  周总理  陈外长
    电贺阿富汗独立纪念日
    新华社26日讯  刘少奇主席25日致电阿富汗王国国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿，祝贺阿富汗王国独立纪念日。电文如下：阿富汗王国国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿陛下：
    值此阿富汗王国独立纪念日之际，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义向陛下和阿富汗人民表示热诚的祝贺。祝中、阿两国间的传统友谊日益发展。祝阿富汗王国昌盛，人民幸福，并祝陛下身体健康。
                        中华人民共和国主席
     刘少奇  1959年5月25日于北京
    新华社26日讯  周恩来总理25日致电阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德汗，祝贺阿富汗王国独立纪念日，电文如下：阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德汗殿下：
    值此阿富汗王国独立纪念日之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府并且以我个人的名义向殿下和阿富汗王国政府表示热烈的祝贺。
    中国人民高兴地看到，中阿两国建立在五项原则基础上的深厚友谊正在不断发展，两国间的友好合作关系日趋密切。祝阿富汗王国和人民在建设自己国家和维护亚洲及世界和平的事业中取得新的成就。祝殿下身体健康。
             中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来  1959年5月25日
    新华社26日讯  陈毅副总理兼外交部长25日致电阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗，祝贺阿富汗王国独立纪念日。电文如下：阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗殿下：
    欣逢阿富汗王国独立纪念日，请接受我诚挚的祝贺。祝中、阿两国间的友好合作关系进一步发展。祝中、阿两国在维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中共同作出新的贡献。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长  陈毅  1959年5月25日
