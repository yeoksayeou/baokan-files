Title: Chairman Mao Receives Guests from Asia, Africa, and the Americas. On the Same Day, He Received Chilean Political Figures. Chairman Liu Receives Kaba Mamadi
Author:
Date: 1959-05-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: Chairman Mao Zedong received delegations and representatives from sixteen countries and regions in Asia, Africa, and Latin America who are currently visiting China at Zhongnanhai's Ziguang Pavilion at noon today. He also held cordial conversations with the guests.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and others were present during the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: Chairman Mao Zedong received Chilean political figures visiting China this afternoon: Professor Ortega, Chairman of the Chilean Radical Party, member of the National Committee of the People's Action Front, and former Minister of Education; Mrs. Ortega, Chairwoman of the Women's Committee of the People's Action Front; Professor Alvarez, Central Committee member and Chairman of the National Discipline Committee of the Chilean Socialist Party, and former Deputy Secretary-General of the Socialist Party, and his wife; Professor Ramirez, head of the Concepcion branch of the Chilean Socialist Party and former Mayor of Concepcion.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, and Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Shen Jian were present at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi received Kaba Mamadi, member of the Political Bureau of the Guinea Democratic Party and Secretary-General of the Guinean Confederation of Workers' Unions, this afternoon.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Li Jiebo, and others were present at the reception. (Attached pictures)

### 毛主席接见亚非美三洲来宾  同日接见了智利政界人士  刘主席接见卡巴·玛玛迪

1959-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见亚非美三洲来宾
    同日接见了智利政界人士
    刘主席接见卡巴·玛玛迪
    新华社15日讯　毛泽东主席今天中午在中南海紫光阁接见了亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲的十六个国家和地区正在我国访问的代表团和代表，并且同客人们作了亲切的谈话。
    陪同接见的，有周恩来总理、陈毅副总理等。
    新华社15日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午接见了前来我国访问的智利政界人士：智利激进主义党主席、人民行动阵线全国委员会委员、前教育部长奥特加教授，人民行动阵线妇女委员会主席奥特加夫人，智利社会党中央委员和全国纪律委员会主席、社会党前副总书记阿尔瓦莱斯教授和他的夫人，智利社会党公塞普森市负责人、前公塞普森市市长拉米莱斯教授。
    接见时在座的，有周恩来总理，陈毅副总理，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，外交部美澳司司长申健。
    新华社15日讯　刘少奇主席今天下午接见了几内亚民主党政治局委员、几内亚工人工会联合会总书记卡巴·玛玛迪。
    接见时在座的，有外交部副部长章汉夫，中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯等。（附图片）
