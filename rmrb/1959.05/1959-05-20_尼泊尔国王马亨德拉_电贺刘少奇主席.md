Title: King Mahendra of Nepal Sends Telegram Congratulating Chairman Liu Shaoqi
Author:
Date: 1959-05-20
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 19th -- King Mahendra of Nepal's telegram to Chairman Liu Shaoqi reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Mr. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:
    I extend my warmest congratulations to you on the auspicious occasion of your election to the esteemed position of Chairman of the People's Republic of China. I am deeply confident that, during your term of office, the friendly ties existing between our two countries will be further strengthened.
　　Mahendra

### 尼泊尔国王马亨德拉  电贺刘少奇主席

1959-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔国王马亨德拉
    电贺刘少奇主席
    新华社19日讯　尼泊尔国王马亨德拉给刘少奇主席的贺电，文如下：中华人民共和国主席刘少奇先生阁下：
    欣逢阁下当选担任中华人民共和国主席的崇高职位的时候，我向你致最热烈的祝贺。我深信，在你任职期间，存在于我们两国之间的友善联系将得到进一步加强。　马亨德拉
