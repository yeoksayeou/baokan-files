Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Chilean Guests

Author:

Date: 1959-05-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet on the evening of the 11th to welcome the former Chilean Minister of Education, Mr. and Mrs. Ortega, Mr. and Mrs. Alvarez, members of the Central Committee of the Chilean Socialist Party, and Mr. Ramirez, the former Mayor of Concepcion, Chile.

Those attending the banquet included Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhou Peiyuan, Vice President of Peking University, and Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation.

### 张奚若宴请智利外宾

1959-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    张奚若宴请智利外宾
    新华社12日讯  中国人民外交学会会长张奚若11日晚举行宴会，欢迎智利前教育部长奥特加夫妇、智利社会党中央委员阿尔瓦莱斯夫妇和智利公塞普森市前市长拉米莱斯。
    参加宴会的有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通、中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林、北京大学副校长周培源、全国妇联书记处书记曹孟君等。
