Title: Blessing Tibet
Author: Liu Lanshan
Date: 1959-05-07
Page: 8

　　Blessing Tibet
　　Liu Lanshan
　　Along the Qinghai-Tibet Highway, crossing high mountains and deep valleys,
　　Spring breeze, please carry our sincere hearts
　　To greet every mountain gully, every small house,
　　To greet every farmer, herdsman, monk, nun, and Liberation Army soldier.
　　Winter has passed, and the ice and snow have melted,
　　The shackles on the heads of slaves have been shattered.
　　Spring breeze, please blow swiftly,
　　So that the wheat planted in spring may ear early.
　　Spring breeze, please blow fiercely,
　　Following the footsteps of the glorious People's Liberation Army,
　　Fiercely pursuing the remnant bandits who betrayed the motherland,
　　Demanding their surrender, letting them embark on a new life.
　　Bless you, Tibetan compatriots who are just beginning to live a happy life,
　　Bless you, the reborn plateau of the motherland!
　　On the roof of the world, which is making a great leap forward towards happiness,
　　Spring breeze, please bring our best wishes!

### 祝福西藏
刘岚山
1959-05-07
第8版()
专栏：

　　祝福西藏
　　刘岚山
　　沿着青藏公路，越过高山深谷，
　　春风，请你带着我们热诚的心
　　去问候每条山沟、每座矮屋，
　　去问候每个农牧僧尼和解放军。
　　冬天已经过去，冰雪已经融解，
　　压在奴隶头上的枷锁已经粉碎。
　　春风，请你迅速地吹吧，
　　让春天里种下的麦子早点抽穗。
　　春风，请你猛烈地刮吧，
　　随着光荣的人民解放军脚步，
　　猛追着背叛祖国的残余匪帮，
　　要他们投降，让他们走上新生的路。
　　祝福你，才过幸福生活的藏族同胞，
　　祝福你，新生了的祖国的高原！
　　在向着幸福大跃进的世界屋脊上，
　　春风，请你带去我们最好的祝愿！
