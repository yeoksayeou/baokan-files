Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Hosts German Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-05-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 29th — Chairman Ho Chi Minh hosted a banquet at the Presidential Palace this evening in honor of the German Democratic Republic People's Chamber delegation led by Chairman Dieckmann and First Deputy Chairman Matern.

    Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly, Truong Chinh, Deputy Prime Minister of the Government, Phan Ke Toai and others attended the banquet. Before the banquet, more than two hundred Young Pioneers held a garden party in the Presidential Palace to welcome the delegation.

### 胡志明主席欢宴德国贵宾

1959-05-01
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席欢宴德国贵宾
    新华社河内29日电　胡志明主席今晚在主席府设宴招待由狄克曼主席和马特恩第一副主席率领的德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团。
    越南国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜、政府副总理长征、潘继遂等出席了宴会。宴会前，二百多个少年先锋队员在主席府花园里举行游艺晚会欢迎代表团。
