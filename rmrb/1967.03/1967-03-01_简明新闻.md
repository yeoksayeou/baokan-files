Japanese Workers and Students Demand Removal of U.S. Military Bases

Three thousand Japanese workers and students held a large rally and demonstration in front of the U.S. Air Force base in Tachikawa, on the outskirts of Tokyo, on the 26th. They demanded the withdrawal of all U.S. military bases in Japan and condemned U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam.

The demonstrators shouted in front of the Tachikawa base gate: "Withdraw all U.S. military bases in Japan!"

Naga Armed Forces Attack Indian Government Troops

Approximately 250 armed forces of the Naga tribe, residing on the border between India and Burma, attacked an Indian reactionary military camp in Manipur. They captured the sentinels on duty and seized more than a dozen rifles.

Ecuadorian Navy Seizes U.S. Pirate Fishing Vessels

Three U.S. pirate fishing vessels recently sneaked into Ecuadorian territorial waters to fish and were seized by the Ecuadorian Navy.

It was announced that these U.S. pirates will be tried in Ecuadorian courts. Since the beginning of the year, seven U.S. vessels have been seized for fishing within the 200-nautical-mile territorial waters declared by Ecuador.

### 简明新闻

1967-03-01

专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    日本工人学生要求撤除美军基地
    日本工人和青年学生三千人，二十六日在东京郊外美国空军立川基地前面举行了声势浩大的集会和示威游行，要求撤除一切在日美军基地，并谴责美帝国主义侵略越南。
    示威者在立川基地的大门前高呼：“撤除一切美军在日基地！”
    印度那加族武装力量袭击政府军
    约二百五十名居住在印度和缅甸边境的那加族的武装力量，袭击了曼尼普尔的印度反动军队的军营，俘虏了正在值勤的哨兵和缴获了十几支步枪。
    厄瓜多尔海军扣押美国海盗渔船
    三艘美国海盗渔船最近偷偷侵入厄瓜多尔领海捕鱼，被厄瓜多尔海军扣押。
    据宣布，这些美国海盗将受到厄瓜多尔法庭审讯。年初以来已有七艘美国船只因侵入厄瓜多尔宣布的二百海浬领海内捕鱼而被扣押。
                            （据新华社讯）
