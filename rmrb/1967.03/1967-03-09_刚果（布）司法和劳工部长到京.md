Title: Minister of Justice and Labor of Congo (Brazzaville) Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-03-09
Edition: 5

    Minister of Justice and Labor of Congo (Brazzaville) François Makosso and his wife arrived in Beijing in the evening of March 7. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Zhang Zhiran, vice-chairman of the Association of Political and Legal Sciences, representatives of the revolutionary faction of the proletariat of the Association of Political and Legal Sciences, and other relevant personnel.

    Ambassador of Congo (Brazzaville) to China, Apollinaire Bazenga, and his wife also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 刚果（布）司法和劳工部长到京

1967-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）司法和劳工部长到京
    据新华社八日讯　刚果（布）司法和劳工部长弗朗索瓦·马科索和他的夫人，七日晚到达北京。到机场欢迎的，有政法学会副会长张志让，政法学会无产阶级革命派代表和其他有关方面人士。
    刚果（布）驻中国大使阿波里奈尔·巴赞加和他的夫人也前往机场迎接。
