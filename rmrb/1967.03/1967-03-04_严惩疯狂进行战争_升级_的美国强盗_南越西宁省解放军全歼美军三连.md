Title: Severely Punish the American Bandits for Their Mad War "Escalation"
Author:
Date: 1967-03-04
Edition: 6

Liberation Army in South Vietnam's Xinhai Province Annihilates an American Army Platoon

According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 2nd: According to the Liberation Communications Society of South Vietnam, the liberation armed forces in the provinces of Xinhai, Ba Dia, Thua Thien, and Quang Tri in South Vietnam have recently launched frequent attacks, achieving great victories.

On the afternoon of February 26th, the liberation armed forces in Xinhai Province launched a fierce ambush on a group of American invading troops in the Da Xi area, annihilating an entire enemy platoon. That same night, the liberation armed forces attacked the American artillery positions in the Chuang Xing Gong area, annihilating two American artillery platoons and destroying twelve 105mm cannons. Before this, the liberation armed forces in Xinhai Province also, from February 20th to 25th, consecutively ambushed the enemy in San Mei, An Ke, Bei Bao, and Ru Dun, killing and wounding 179 enemy soldiers, including 132 American invading troops and South Korean mercenaries, shooting down nine enemy planes, and damaging three.

From February 4th to 15th, the liberation armed forces in Ba Dia Province killed and wounded 200 American invading troops, destroying and damaging 45 tanks and armored vehicles.

In Thua Thien Province, the liberation armed forces, on February 18th and 23rd, respectively, annihilated 155 enemy soldiers in Guang Tian County and Fu Wang County, including the complete annihilation of four enemy platoons.

According to Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd: Saigon News: The liberation armed forces in South Vietnam have recently severely punished the American invading troops for their mad war "escalation."

According to Western news agencies, from the evening of February 28th to the early morning of March 2nd, the liberation armed forces in South Vietnam, with fierce artillery fire, continuously bombarded the American long-range cannon positions set up south of the demilitarized zone to shell North Vietnam. At the same time, the liberation armed forces also, on the evenings of February 28th and March 1st, launched fierce attacks on a thousand-strong American Marine Corps unit conducting "sweeping" operations south of the demilitarized zone, causing heavy casualties to the enemy, including the death of a senior American military officer.

In the people's struggle against American aggression for national salvation in South Vietnam, numerous heroes have emerged. The picture shows the "brave warriors who annihilated Americans" of a certain regiment's third platoon, who were awarded medals for their achievements. Xinhua News Agency photo.

### 严惩疯狂进行战争“升级”的美国强盗  南越西宁省解放军全歼美军三连

1967-03-04
第6版()
专栏：

    严惩疯狂进行战争“升级”的美国强盗
    南越西宁省解放军全歼美军三连
    据新华社河内二日电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方西宁、巴地、承天、广治等省解放武装力量最近频繁出击，取得巨大战果。
    西宁省解放武装力量二月二十六日下午在达西地区猛烈截击一股美国侵略军，全歼敌人一个连。当天夜里，解放武装力量又袭击庄行贡地区的美军炮兵阵地，全歼美军两个炮兵连，击毁一○五毫米口径大炮十二门。在这以前，西宁省解放武装力量还在二十日到二十五日期间，接连在桑梅、安克、北保、若敦等地截击敌人，打死打伤敌人一百七十九名，其中包括美国侵略军和南朝鲜帮凶军一百三十二名，击落敌机九架，击伤三架。
    从二月四日到十五日期间，解放武装力量在巴地省打死打伤美国侵略军二百名，击毁击伤坦克、装甲车四十五辆。
    在承天省，解放武装力量二月十八日和二十三日两天，分别在广田县和富汪县消灭伪军一百五十五名，其中包括全歼敌人四个排。
    据新华社三日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量连日来严惩疯狂地进行战争“升级”的美国侵略军。
    据西方通讯社报道，从二月二十八日晚到三月二日清晨，越南南方解放武装力量以猛烈的炮火，连续三次猛轰设在非军事区以南用以轰击越南北方的美军远程大炮阵地。与此同时，解放武装力量还在二月二十八日和三月一日晚上，猛烈袭击在非军事区以南进行“扫荡”的一支上千名的美国海军陆战队，使敌军遭到严重伤亡，一名美军上校指挥官也被击毙。
