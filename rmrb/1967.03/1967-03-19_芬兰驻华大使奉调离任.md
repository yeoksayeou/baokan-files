Title: Finnish Ambassador to China Leaves Post
Author:
Date: 1967-03-19
Edition: 5

Finnish Ambassador to China Toivo Vahtera has been recalled and will leave his post. Today, he departed Beijing by airplane.

### 芬兰驻华大使奉调离任

1967-03-19
第5版()
专栏：

    芬兰驻华大使奉调离任
    新华社十八日讯　芬兰驻中国大使托依伏拉奉调离任，今天乘飞机离开北京。
