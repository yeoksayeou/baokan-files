Title: Celebrating the Success of the Albanian Song and Dance Ensemble's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1967-03-04
Edition: 5

	Chinese Charge d'Affaires in Albania Holds Reception

	The Chinese Embassy in Albania held a reception on February 27 to celebrate the successful visit of the Albanian State Song and Dance Ensemble to China. The reception was hosted by Li Tinguan, the Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania. The entire ensemble, which had just returned from China, was in attendance.

	Attendees also included Treksa, Chairman of the Albanian Foreign Cultural Commission, and Spase, Vice Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, among other relevant officials.

	Li Tinguan and the director of the Albanian State Song and Dance Ensemble, Pejo, both spoke at the reception. They repeatedly expressed their wishes for the good health and longevity of the great leaders of the Chinese and Albanian peoples, Chairman Mao Zedong and Comrade Enver Hoxha.

	During the reception, the Albanian artists held up the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and enthusiastically sang in Chinese, "Long Live Chairman Mao," "The East is Red," and a song based on Chairman Mao's quotations, which was composed by the artistic director of the ensemble during their visit to China. The Chinese comrades also sang Albanian songs in Albanian, including "The Partisan Song" and "One Hand with a Pickaxe, One Hand with a Gun."

	As the reception came to a close, everyone sang "The East is Red" in Chinese. An Albanian artist led the crowd in shouting, "Long Live Chairman Mao!" "Long Live Comrade Enver Hoxha!" "Long Live the Communist Party of China!" "Long Live the Party of Labor of Albania!" The entire reception was filled with a fraternal and revolutionary atmosphere.

### 祝贺阿歌舞团访华演出成功  我驻阿临时代办举行招待会

1967-03-04
第5版()
专栏：

    祝贺阿歌舞团访华演出成功
    我驻阿临时代办举行招待会
    新华社地拉那电　中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃二月二十七日举行招待会，招待刚从中国访问归来的阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团全体成员，热烈祝贺他们在中国演出成功。出席招待会的还有阿尔巴尼亚对外文委主席特雷斯卡和阿中友协副主席斯帕塞等有关方面的负责人。
    李庭荃临时代办和阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团团长佩约先后在招待会上讲了话，双方频频祝愿中阿两国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席和霍查同志健康长寿。
    阿尔巴尼亚艺术家们在招待会上拿着《毛主席语录》，热情地用中文高唱《万岁毛主席》、《大海航行靠舵手》和由歌舞团艺术指导在访华期间谱曲的毛主席语录歌。中国同志也齐声用阿文歌唱《游击队之歌》、《一手拿镐，一手拿枪》等阿尔巴尼亚歌曲。
    招待会结束时，全体同志用中文齐声高唱《东方红》。一位阿尔巴尼亚艺术家带头用中文高呼
  “毛主席万岁！”“恩维尔·霍查同志万岁！”“中国共产党万岁！”“阿尔巴尼亚劳动党万岁！”招待会自始至终洋溢着兄弟般的战斗情谊和革命气氛。
