Title: Welcoming Comrade Wilcox to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-03-09
Edition: 1

At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade V. J. Wilcox, General Secretary of the New Zealand Communist Party, arrived in Beijing this evening by airplane from Guangzhou for a friendly visit to our country.

Comrade Zhou Enlai, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, Comrade Liu Ningyi, a member of the Central Committee, and Comrade Zhao Yimin, an alternate member of the Central Committee, as well as hundreds of representatives of the revolutionary masses of the proletariat in the capital, went to the airport to welcome him.

The revolutionary masses who went to the airport to welcome the guests held up huge banners with the words: "Long live the fighting friendship between the Communist Party of China and the Communist Party of New Zealand!" "Long live the invincible Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought!"

When Comrade Wilcox met with the welcoming masses, people waved red copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and repeatedly shouted: "Warmly welcome Comrade V. J. Wilcox, General Secretary of the New Zealand Communist Party!" "Workers of the world, unite!" "Long live our great leader Chairman Mao!" "Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!"

Representatives of the revolutionary masses in the capital presented Comrade Wilcox with Mao badges and copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao."

Also present at the airport to welcome Comrade Wilcox were New Zealand comrades who are in Beijing.

Comrade Wilcox arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon.

### 应中共中央邀请来我国进行友好访问   威尔科克斯同志到北京   周恩来康生等同志到机场欢迎

1967-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    应中共中央邀请来我国进行友好访问
    威尔科克斯同志到北京
    周恩来康生等同志到机场欢迎
    新华社八日讯　新西兰共产党总书记维·乔·威尔科克斯同志，应中共中央的邀请来我国进行友好访问，今晚乘飞机由广州到达北京。
    中共中央政治局常委周恩来、康生，中共中央书记处书记刘宁一，中共中央候补委员赵毅敏等同志，以及首都无产阶级革命派代表数百人，前往机场欢迎。
    前往机场欢迎的革命群众高举着巨幅横标，上面写着：“中国共产党和新西兰共产党的战斗友谊万岁！”“战无不胜的马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想万岁！”
    威尔科克斯同志同欢迎群众见面时，人们挥动着红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，不断高呼：“热烈欢迎新西兰共产党总书记威尔科克斯同志！”“全世界无产者联合起来！”“我们伟大的领袖毛主席万岁！”“无产阶级文化大革命万岁！”
    首都革命派代表向威尔科克斯同志赠送了毛主席像章和《毛主席语录》。
    到机场欢迎威尔科克斯同志的还有在北京的新西兰同志。
    威尔科克斯同志是于七日下午到达广州的。
