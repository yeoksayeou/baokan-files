Title: Cadres of Xigou Brigade Resolve to Strive for Revolution and Production
Author:
Date: 1967-03-28
Edition: 3

The cadres of Xigou Brigade have resolved to follow Chairman Mao's teachings, to grasp revolution and vigorously promote production. This is Li Shunda (pushing the cart) and the comrades from the county's military department working together to push soil for land leveling. Photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
