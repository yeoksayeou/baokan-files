Title: Go to the Countryside!
Author:
Date: 1967-03-29
Edition: 3

Go to the countryside! Go to the countryside to accept re-education from the poor and lower-middle peasants! This is a great political movement. This is a great cultural revolution. This is a great revolution that touches the soul.

The countryside is a vast school. The poor and lower-middle peasants are the best teachers. Revolutionary teachers and students should go to the countryside to receive education. They should go to the countryside to accept re-education from the poor and lower-middle peasants. They should go to the countryside to change their world outlook, to change their ideology, to change their feelings, to change their soul, and to become workers and peasants' own intellectuals and revolutionaries.

The countryside is a vast battlefield. The poor and lower-middle peasants are the best commanders. Revolutionary teachers and students should go to the countryside to fight. They should go to the countryside to participate in the class struggle, to participate in the struggle for production, and to participate in the scientific experiment movement. They should go to the countryside to become workers and peasants' own fighters and revolutionaries.

The countryside is a vast garden. The poor and lower-middle peasants are the best gardeners. Revolutionary teachers and students should go to the countryside to cultivate. They should go to the countryside to plant trees, to plant flowers, to plant grass, and to plant revolutionary thoughts. They should go to the countryside to become workers and peasants' own gardeners and revolutionaries.

The countryside is a vast stage. The poor and lower-middle peasants are the best actors. Revolutionary teachers and students should go to the countryside to perform. They should go to the countryside to sing revolutionary songs, to dance revolutionary dances, to act revolutionary plays, and to spread revolutionary thoughts. They should go to the countryside to become workers and peasants' own actors and revolutionaries.

Go to the countryside! Go to the countryside to accept re-education from the poor and lower-middle peasants! This is a great political movement. This is a great cultural revolution. This is a great revolution that touches the soul.
