Title: Spreading the Great Thoughts of Mao Zedong and Promoting Sino-Japanese Friendship
Author:
Date: 1967-03-25
Edition: 5

The Chinese News Agency held a grand celebration of its establishment in Tokyo.

According to the New China News Agency in Tokyo on the 24th, the Chinese News Agency, which aims to spread the great thoughts of Mao Zedong, introduce the socialist revolution and socialist construction in China, and promote the unity and friendship between the peoples of China and Japan, held a grand celebration of its establishment on the 23rd in Tokyo.

The event was attended by approximately 200 people, including Nakajima Kenzo, Chairman of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association; Ito Takeo, Vice Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Genuine); Kobayashi Yuichi, Chairman of the Japan Federation of Newspaper Workers' Unions; Kenya Kaneda, Vice Chairman of the Japanese Trade Union Confederation; prominent Japanese figures Nishizawa Ryuji; Socialist Party member Ishino Hisao; renowned female critic Matsuoka Yoichi; representatives from various Japanese newspapers, news agencies, radio and television stations; and Kan Wenfang, Chairman of the Tokyo Chinese Overseas Federation.

Sun Pinghua, the Chief Representative of the Liaison Office of the Liaison Office of the Liaison Office in Tokyo, attended the meeting and delivered a speech.

The Chinese News Agency was established by patriotic overseas Chinese in Japan. The news and pictures from Xinhua News Agency that it relays are highly welcomed by the broad masses of the Japanese people and people from all walks of life.

### 传播伟大的毛泽东思想　增进中日人民团结友好  中国通讯社在东京举行成立庆祝大会

1967-03-25
第5版()
专栏：

    传播伟大的毛泽东思想　增进中日人民团结友好
    中国通讯社在东京举行成立庆祝大会
    据新华社东京二十四日电　以传播伟大的毛泽东思想、介绍中国社会主义革命和社会主义建设、增进中日两国人民团结友好为宗旨的中国通讯社，二十三日在东京隆重举行成立庆祝大会。
    日中文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏、日中友好协会（正统）总部副会长伊藤武雄、日本记者同盟主席小林雄一、日本工会总评议会副议长兼田富太郎、日本著名人士西泽隆二、社会党议员石野久男、著名女评论家松冈洋子和日本各报社、通讯社、广播电视台的代表以及东京华侨总会会长甘文芳等约二百人出席了大会。
    廖承志办事处驻东京联络事务所首席代表孙平化出席了大会并且讲了话。
    中国通讯社是旅日爱国华侨创办的。这个通讯社转发的新华社消息和图片，深受广大日本人民和各界人士的欢迎。
