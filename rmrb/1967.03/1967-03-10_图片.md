Title: East Is Red Brigade of Tongji University in Shanghai Wields Red Brushes
Author:
Date: 1967-03-10
Edition: 6

In the great proletarian cultural revolution, the revolutionary teachers and students of Tongji University in Shanghai have formed the East Is Red Brigade, which is wielding the red brushes to create revolutionary artworks. The brigade has produced a series of revolutionary paintings and calligraphy, which have been exhibited in the university and have received high praise from the revolutionary teachers, students, and workers.

The East Is Red Brigade has been actively participating in the great proletarian cultural revolution. They have held discussions and criticisms of the reactionary bourgeois art and literature, and have created revolutionary artworks that reflect the spirit of the times. The brigade has also organized exhibitions and performances to propagate the revolutionary ideas and to inspire the revolutionary enthusiasm of the masses.

The East Is Red Brigade has been deeply influenced by Chairman Mao's teachings. They have studied Chairman Mao's works diligently and have applied his teachings to their artistic creation. The brigade has also been actively participating in the revolutionary struggles and has been contributing to the great proletarian cultural revolution.

The East Is Red Brigade will continue to wield the red brushes and to create more revolutionary artworks. They will continue to propagate the revolutionary ideas and to inspire the revolutionary enthusiasm of the masses. The brigade will also continue to study Chairman Mao's teachings and to apply them to their artistic creation. The East Is Red Brigade will always be a reliable force in the great proletarian cultural revolution.
