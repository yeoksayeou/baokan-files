Title: Soldiers of the People's Liberation Army Stationed on Hainan Island Assist Local Farmers in Spring Ploughing
Author:
Date: 1967-03-16
Edition: 2

Soldiers and officers of the People's Liberation Army stationed on Hainan Island are actively assisting mountain farmers in their spring ploughing. This is a scene of soldiers and poor peasants from the lower classes working together, strengthening the management of early-season rice in the fields.
