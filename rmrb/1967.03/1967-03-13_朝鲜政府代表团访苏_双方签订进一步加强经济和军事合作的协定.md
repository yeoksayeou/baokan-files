Title: North Korean Government Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1967-03-13
Edition: 6

A North Korean government delegation led by Kim Il, a member of the Political Committee of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, a secretary of the Central Committee, and the first deputy premier of the cabinet, recently visited the Soviet Union for 19 days. During the visit, the delegation signed agreements with the Soviet government to further strengthen economic and military cooperation between the two countries.

According to TASS, the North Korean delegation, headed by Kim Il, arrived in the Soviet Union on February 13. During their visit, the delegation handed over a letter from Kim Il Sung, the general secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and the premier of the cabinet, to Brezhnev and Kosygin. They also held talks with them and with a Soviet government delegation led by Mazurov. The discussions covered issues of "further developing economic and scientific and technological cooperation" and "further strengthening the defensive capabilities of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea." As a result of the talks, the two sides signed the "Agreement on Cooperation in Further Strengthening the Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea," the "Agreement on Economic and Scientific and Technological Cooperation," and the "1967 Soviet-North Korean Trade Agreement."

On March 2, Kim Il held a reception in Moscow to celebrate the "successful conclusion" of the North Korean-Soviet talks.

### 朝鲜政府代表团访苏  双方签订进一步加强经济和军事合作的协定

1967-03-13
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团访苏
    双方签订进一步加强经济和军事合作的协定
    新华社莫斯科十一日电　由朝鲜劳动党中央政治委员会常务委员会委员、中央书记、内阁第一副首相金一率领的朝鲜政府代表团，最近在苏联进行了为期十九天的访问，并同苏联政府签订了关于进一步加强朝苏两国经济和军事合作的协定。
    据塔斯社报道，以金一为首的朝鲜政府代表团是在二月十三日到达苏联的。访苏期间，朝鲜代表团向勃列日涅夫和柯西金转交了朝鲜劳动党中央总书记、内阁首相金日成写给他们的一封信，并同他们以及以马祖罗夫为首的苏联政府代表团分别举行会谈，讨论了双方“进一步发展经济、科学技术方面的合作问题”，以及“进一步加强朝鲜民主主义人民共和国防御能力的问题”。会谈结果，双方签订了
  “关于在进一步加强朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国防方面进行合作的协定”，以及“关于经济和科学技术合作协定”和“苏朝两国一九六七年换货协定”。
    三月二日，金一为朝苏会谈的“圆满结束”，在莫斯科举行了招待会。
