Title: Revolutionary Masses and Poor Peasants in Shanghai's Jiading County Respond to Chairman Mao's Call to "Seize the Revolution and Promote Production"
Author:
Date: 1967-03-02
Edition: 2

The proletarian revolutionary masses and poor and lower-middle peasants of the Chenbei Production Brigade, Chengxi People's Commune, Jiading County, Shanghai, have responded with the utmost resolve to Chairman Mao's great call to "seize the revolution and promote production." This is a scene of them strengthening the field management of summer crops.
