Title: Vietnamese News Agency Authorized Statement: China Safely Transports Aid to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-03-02
Edition: 4

	Vietnamese News Agency Authorized Statement: China Safely Transports Aid to Vietnam

	New China News Agency Hanoi, February 28th — The Vietnamese News Agency authorized the release of a statement on the 28th. The statement said: Recently, American and other Western news agencies have spread fabricated news regarding the transportation of aid from the Soviet Union and other countries to Vietnam via China. The Vietnamese News Agency is authorized to declare that the news spread by American and other Western news agencies is entirely fabricated and its intentions are extremely malicious.

	The statement continued: The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has repeatedly stated that China is wholeheartedly providing assistance and has safely and on schedule transported the aid from the Soviet Union and other countries to Vietnam.

### 越南通讯社受权声明说  中国把援越过境物资妥善运到越南

1967-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    越南通讯社受权声明说
    中国把援越过境物资妥善运到越南
    新华社河内二十八日电　越南通讯社二十八日受权发表一项声明。声明说：最近，美国和其它西方通讯社就苏联和其他一些国家的援助物资经过中国运往越南的问题散播了捏造的消息。越南通讯社受权声明，美国和其它西方通讯社散播的这些消息，纯系捏造，其目的是非常恶毒的。
    声明接着说：越南民主共和国政府已经多次申明，中国全心全意地提供帮助，把苏联和其他一些国家的援助物资妥善地并且按照预定时间运到越南。
