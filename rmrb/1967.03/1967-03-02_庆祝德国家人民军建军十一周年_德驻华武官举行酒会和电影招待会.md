Title: Celebrating the 11th Anniversary of the Founding of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1967-03-02
Edition: 4

The military attaché of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, Colonel Rudi Pankow, held a reception and film screening tonight to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic.

Relevant officials from our country, including Liu Shaowen, Xu Wenlie, and Pan Zhenwu, were invited to attend. Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries stationed in China also attended the reception and film screening.

### 庆祝德国家人民军建军十一周年  德驻华武官举行酒会和电影招待会

1967-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝德国家人民军建军十一周年
    德驻华武官举行酒会和电影招待会
    新华社一日讯　德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆武官鲁迪·潘克夫上校今晚举行酒会和电影招待会，庆祝德意志民主共和国国家人民军建军十一周年。
    我国有关方面负责人刘少文、徐文烈、潘振武等应邀出席。各国驻中国使节和大使馆武官，也出席了酒会和电影招待会。
