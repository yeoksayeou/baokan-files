Title: Congolese (Brazzaville) Guests Depart Beijing for Visits Elsewhere
Author:
Date: 1967-03-16
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the Chinese Association of Political and Legal Sciences, the Minister of Justice and Labor of Congo (Brazzaville), Francois Makosso, and his wife, arrived in China for a friendly visit. Today, they left Beijing to tour other parts of the country before returning home. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Zhang Zhiran, Vice-President of the Political and Legal Sciences Association, representatives of the revolutionary faction of the proletariat, and other relevant personnel. The Congolese (Brazzaville) Ambassador to China, Apollinaire Bazanga, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 刚果（布）客人离京去外地访问

1967-03-16
第5版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）客人离京去外地访问
    新华社十五日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国进行友好访问的刚果（布）司法和劳工部长弗朗索瓦·马科索和他的夫人，今天离开北京去外地参观访问，然后回国。到机场欢送的有政法学会副会长张志让，政法学会无产阶级革命派代表和其他有关方面人士。刚果（布）驻中国大使阿波利奈尔·巴赞加也前往机场欢送。
