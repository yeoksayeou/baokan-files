Title: The People of Mali Have Limitless Love for Chairman Mao, Warmly Praise China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-03-24
Edition: 5

The people of Mali have limitless love for Chairman Mao and warmly praise China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The image shows friends from Mali joyfully viewing the picture of Chairman Mao receiving the Red Guards, which was published in the People's Daily.
