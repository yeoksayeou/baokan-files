Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Launches Ferocious Attack on Da Nang U.S. Air Base
Author: 
Date: 1967-03-13
Edition: 6

	South Vietnamese Liberation Army Launches Ferocious Attack on Da Nang U.S. Air Base
	Destroying 26 Enemy Aircraft, Annihilating Over 500 U.S. Troops

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 12th — According to the Voice of South Vietnam Liberation Radio: On the night of February 27th, the liberation armed forces of Quang Nam Province in South Vietnam launched a fierce attack on the Da Nang U.S. Air Base, achieving a resounding victory. Preliminary reports indicate that they destroyed 26 U.S. jet aircraft, damaged many more, destroyed 22 military vehicles, and annihilated over 500 U.S. invading troops, the majority of whom were U.S. Air Force officers and technical personnel.

	During this fierce attack, the liberation armed forces' artillery accurately hit their targets. The base's airfield and many important military facilities were hit and set ablaze. The thunderous sound of artillery shook the heavens and the earth, even causing the doors and windows of buildings in Da Nang City to rattle. The fires burning from the airfield and various military facilities lasted for several hours.

	In addition, the liberation armed forces also destroyed a 50-meter section of the runway, demolished the base's radio station, and the enemy's largest radar center. The U.S. Air Force personnel's dormitories and other buildings were also severely damaged.

	The radio broadcast said that the U.S. invaders, in order to cover up their heavy losses, dared not transport the wounded to Da Nang for treatment, but instead secretly transported the wounded and the bodies of the killed U.S. troops to warships using helicopters.

	The liberation armed forces' victorious attack on the Da Nang U.S. base has brought immense joy and excitement to the people of Da Nang. People rushed to share this thrilling news. Slogans such as "Celebrate the Liberation Army's Attack on Da Nang Airfield!" and "Down with U.S. Aggressors!" appeared all over the streets of Da Nang.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 10th — The Voice of South Vietnam Liberation reported: In February, the military and civilians of the Quang Tri-Thua Thien region in South Vietnam fought bravely and achieved brilliant results, annihilating 1,845 enemy troops, including completely wiping out three enemy companies and twelve platoons.

	During the battles, the military and civilians also destroyed or damaged 76 enemy vehicles of various types, shot down six enemy aircraft, damaged three, blew up two cannons, and five bridges. They also frequently cut off the first highway, paralyzing the enemy's transportation and logistics.

### 南越解放军猛袭岘港美空军基地  炸毁敌机二十六架，歼灭美军五百多名

1967-03-13
第6版()
专栏：

    南越解放军猛袭岘港美空军基地 
    炸毁敌机二十六架，歼灭美军五百多名
    新华社河内十二日电  据越南南方解放电台广播：越南南方广南省解放武装力量，二月二十七日凌晨猛烈袭击岘港美国空军基地，取得了辉煌战果。据初步消息，共炸毁美国喷气式飞机二十六架，炸伤多架，击毁军车二十二辆，歼灭美国侵略军五百多名，其中绝大部分是美国空军军官和技术人员。
    在这次猛烈的袭击战中，解放武装力量的炮弹准确地命中目标，基地里的飞机场和许多重要军事设施都中弹起火，隆隆的炮声震天动地，连岘港市内的建筑物的门窗也嘎嘎作响。从机场及各军事设施升起的烈火，一直燃烧了几个小时。
    此外，解放武装力量还破坏了五十米长的飞机跑道，摧毁了基地的无线电发报站和敌人最大的一个雷达中心。美国空军人员宿舍等建筑物也遭到严重破坏。
    广播说，美国侵略者为了掩盖他们的惨重损失，不敢把伤员运到岘港去治疗，而是把这些伤员连同被打死的美军尸体用直升飞机偷偷地运到军舰上去。
    解放武装力量这次对岘港美军基地的胜利袭击，使岘港市人民无比欢欣鼓舞，人们奔走相告这一激动人心的消息。“欢呼解放军袭击岘港机场！”“打倒美国侵略者！”等标语纷纷在岘港市街头出现。
    据新华社河内十日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广治—承天地区军民，二月份英勇战斗，取得了辉煌的战果，共消灭美、伪军一千八百四十五名，其中全歼敌人三个连和十二个排。
    在战斗中，军民们还击毁、击伤敌人各种军车七十六辆，击落敌机六架，击伤三架，炸毁大炮两门和桥梁五座，同时经常切断第一号公路，使敌人的交通运输陷于瘫痪。
