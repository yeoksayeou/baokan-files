Title: Nepalese Friends Praise the Great Chinese Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-03-07
Edition: 4

Some Nepalese friends recently received a reporter from Xinhua News Agency and enthusiastically praised the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China. They said, "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is excellent." This revolution is "a great contribution to the people of the world by the Chinese people armed with Mao Zedong Thought."

A friend named Bahadur said, "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution led by the great leader Chairman Mao is advancing in big strides. It greatly inspires the people of the world in their struggle against U.S. imperialism and modern revisionism."

He said, "The brilliant and correct Mao Zedong Thought illuminates our hearts. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution gives great inspiration to the revolutionary people of the world. Let us cheer for the greatest and most correct leader of the people of the world, Chairman Mao! Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China!"

Another friend named Narayan said, "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has uprooted the roots of revisionism in China, which is a great thing for the people of the world. This is a great contribution to the people of the world by the Chinese people armed with Mao Zedong Thought."

He said, "Currently, imperialism, modern revisionism, and all reactionaries are using their propaganda machines to slander the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China. They spread all sorts of rumors about the Cultural Revolution. They spread germs like flies. But the people of the world will not believe their lies. The people of the world believe in truth, which is the great Mao Zedong Thought."

A Nepalese friend named Govinda told the Xinhua News Agency reporter, "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is of great significance not only to China but also to the people of the world."

He said, "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China makes the revolutionary people of the world advance along the bright path and inspires the people of the world to achieve victory in their struggle against U.S. imperialism and modern revisionism. For this, the revolutionary people of the world are very grateful to the greatest and most brilliant leader, Chairman Mao."

Another friend named Padayi said, "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution initiated and led by Chairman Mao is a great innovation in world history. This revolution not only ensures that China will never change color but also points out the bright path for the people of the world."

He said, "Modern revisionists slander the Chinese Cultural Revolution because they are very afraid of the Chinese Cultural Revolution and very afraid of the great Mao Zedong Thought. Modern revisionists join hands with U.S. imperialists and all reactionaries to slander the Chinese Cultural Revolution. But the people of the world firmly support the Chinese Cultural Revolution. The more modern revisionists oppose the Chinese Cultural Revolution, the more the people of the world support the Chinese Cultural Revolution." He shouted, "Long live the great leader of the people of the world, Chairman Mao!"

### 尼泊尔朋友说中国文化大革命是伟大创举  文化大革命给世界人民指出光明大道  现代修正主义者越是反对，世界人民越是支持

1967-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔朋友说中国文化大革命是伟大创举
    文化大革命给世界人民指出光明大道
    现代修正主义者越是反对，世界人民越是支持
    据新华社加德满都电  一些尼泊尔朋友在最近接见新华社记者时热烈赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命。他们说：“中国无产阶级文化大革命好得很”，这场革命“是用毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民对世界人民的伟大贡献。”
    一位名叫巴哈杜尔的朋友说，由伟大的领袖毛主席领导的中国无产阶级文化大革命正在大踏步前进。它大大鼓舞了全世界人民反对美帝国主义和现代修正主义的斗争。
    他说：“伟大英明的毛泽东思想照得我们的心里亮堂堂，无产阶级文化革命给了世界革命人民巨大的鼓舞。让我们欢呼世界人民最伟大的最正确的领袖毛主席万岁！中国无产阶级文化大革命万岁！”
    另一位名叫纳拉扬的朋友说：“无产阶级文化大革命拔掉了中国的修正主义根子，这对世界人民来说，是一件大好事。这是用毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民对世界人民的伟大贡献。”
    他说：“目前，帝国主义、现代修正主义和一切反动派正在开动宣传机器来诬蔑中国的无产阶级文化大革命。他们散布关于文化大革命的各式各样的谣言。他们象苍蝇一样地散布细菌。但是世界人民决不会相信他们的谎言。世界人民相信真理，那就是伟大的毛泽东思想。”
    一位名叫戈文达的尼泊尔朋友对新华社记者说：“中国的无产阶级文化大革命，不仅对中国有非常重要的意义，而且对世界人民也有非常重要的意义”。
    他说：“中国的无产阶级文化大革命促使世界革命人民沿着光明大道前进，鼓舞世界人民在反对美帝国主义和现代修正主义的斗争中取得胜利。为此，世界革命人民非常感谢最伟大和英明的领袖毛主席”。
    另一位名叫帕戴伊的朋友说：“由毛主席发动和领导的中国无产阶级文化大革命，是世界历史上一个伟大的创举。这场革命不仅保证中国永不变色，而且也给世界人民指出了光明大道。”
    他说，现代修正主义者诽谤中国文化大革命是因为他们非常害怕中国文化革命，非常害怕伟大的毛泽东思想。“现代修正主义者同美帝国主义者和一切反动派联合起来对中国文化大革命进行诬蔑。但是全世界人民坚决支持中国文化大革命。现代修正主义者越是反对中国的文化大革命，世界人民越是支持中国的文化大革命”。他高呼：“世界人民的伟大领袖毛主席万万岁！”
