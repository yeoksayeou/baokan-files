Title: Greek Progressive Student Union Praises the Great Significance of China's Cultural Revolution to the World
Author:
Date: 1967-03-30
Edition: 5

	Greek Progressive Student Union Praises the Great Significance of China's Cultural Revolution to the World
	Shakes the Foundation of Imperialism and Reactionary Rule

	NEW DELHI, March 30 (Xinhua) -- According to a report by the Greek weekly "People's Road," the Greek Progressive Student Union's representative conference sent a message of greeting to the Chinese people, enthusiastically praising the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China.

	The Greek Progressive Student Union held its first representative conference at the end of January. The message of greeting to the Chinese people adopted at the conference said that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution underway in China is a major event of world significance. It has shaken the foundation of world imperialism and the reactionary rule in various countries.

	The message of greeting expressed warm congratulations to the Chinese people for their persistent struggle against U.S. imperialism and modern revisionism. It pointed out that China is a beacon lighting the way for the national liberation movements of the world. The message of greeting stated that the conference condemns the anti-China "holy alliance" formed by the U.S. imperialist and Soviet revisionist leadership cliques. It called on all progressive students and the Greek working people to actively support the great People's Republic of China.

### 希腊进步学联赞扬中国文化大革命的重大世界意义  从根本上动摇帝国主义和反动派的统治

1967-03-30
第5版()
专栏：

    希腊进步学联赞扬中国文化大革命的重大世界意义
    从根本上动摇帝国主义和反动派的统治
    新华社讯　雅典消息：据希腊《人民之路》周刊报道，希腊进步学生联合会代表大会在给中国人民的致敬电中热情赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命。
    希腊进步学生联合会在一月底举行了第一次代表大会。会上通过的给中国人民的致敬电说，在中国进行的伟大的无产阶级文化大革命，是具有世界意义的重大事件，它从根本上动摇了世界帝国主义和各国反动派的统治。
    致敬电对坚持反对美帝国主义和现代修正主义斗争的中国人民表示热烈祝贺，并且指出，中国是照耀世界民族解放运动的灯塔。致敬电表示，代表大会谴责美帝国主义和苏联修正主义领导集团结成的反华“神圣同盟”，并且呼吁所有进步学生和希腊劳动人民积极支持伟大的中华人民共和国。
