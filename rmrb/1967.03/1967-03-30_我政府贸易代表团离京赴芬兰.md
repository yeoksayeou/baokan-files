Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Departs for Finland
Author:
Date: 1967-03-30
Edition: 5

A Chinese government trade delegation left Beijing today for Finland to discuss the 1967 Sino-Finnish government trade agreement. Chargé d'affaires of the Finnish Embassy in China, Mr. Lindroos, was at the airport to see the delegation off.

### 我政府贸易代表团离京赴芬兰

1967-03-30
第5版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团离京赴芬兰
    新华社二十九日讯　中国政府贸易代表团今天离开北京前往芬兰，商谈一九六七年中芬两国政府贸易协定。芬兰驻中国大使馆临时代办林杜拉赫提到机场送行。
