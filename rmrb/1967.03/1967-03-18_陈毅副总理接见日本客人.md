Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-03-18
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Japanese guests this morning. The New China News Agency reported on March 17th that Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the delegation of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association's friendly trade mission to China, led by Kawase Ichikan, the secretary-general Hagiwara Sadashi, and other members of the delegation. They had a friendly conversation.

Present at the meeting were Liao Chengzhi and others. Japanese friends in Beijing, such as Nishisonoji Koichi and Suzuki Kazuo, were also present.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本客人

1967-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本客人
    新华社十七日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了日本国际贸易促进协会访华友好贸易代表团团长川濑一贯、秘书长萩原定司和代表团团员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有廖承志等。
    在北京的日本朋友西园寺公一、铃木一雄等也在座。
