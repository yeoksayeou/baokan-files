Title: U.S. Warships and Military Aircraft Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1967-03-05
Edition: 5

	U.S. warships and military aircraft intruded into our territorial waters and airspace. We issue the 427th serious warning.

	New China News Agency, March 4th report: On March 4th, from 3:23 AM to 6:51 AM, two U.S. warships intruded into our territorial waters southeast of Pinghai, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 9:21 AM to 7:57 PM, one U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters southeast of Huangqi Peninsula, Fujian Province. On the same day, from 10:40 AM to 11:08 AM, two U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. Regarding the military provocations by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, issues the 427th serious warning.

### 美国军舰和军用飞机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百二十七次严重警告

1967-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    美国军舰和军用飞机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百二十七次严重警告
    新华社四日讯　三月四日三时二十三分至六时五十一分，美国军舰两艘侵入我广东省平海东南领海。同日九时二十一分至十九时五十七分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省黄歧半岛东南领海。同日十时四十分至十一时零八分，美国军用飞机两架侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区上空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百二十七次严重警告。
