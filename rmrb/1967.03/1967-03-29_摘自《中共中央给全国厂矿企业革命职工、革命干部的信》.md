Title: Message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to Revolutionary Workers and Cadres in All Factories and Mines
Author:
Date: 1967-03-29
Edition: 2

	Chairman Mao calls on you, and the Party Central Committee calls on you, to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end. We hope that in this great proletarian cultural revolution, and under these new conditions, you will redouble your efforts, strive to be in the forefront, and become the most outstanding models for the working people of the nation in seizing the revolution and promoting production in this stage.
