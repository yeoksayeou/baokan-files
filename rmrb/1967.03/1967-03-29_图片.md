Title: Propaganda Team of Mao Zedong Thought Enters the Grassroots
Author:
Date: 1967-03-29
Edition: 2

The propaganda team of Mao Zedong Thought, organized by the Qingdao Revolutionary Committee and composed of officers from the troops stationed in Qingdao, revolutionary students, and revolutionary cadres, has gone deep into the grassroots to propagate Mao Zedong Thought. The picture shows the comrades of the Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team who entered Qingdao Textile Mill No. 9 studying Chairman Mao's works together with young workers.
