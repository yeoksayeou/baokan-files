Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Invades Our Guangdong Province Airspace
Author:
Date: 1967-03-15
Edition: 6

	U.S. military aircraft invaded our Guangdong Province airspace. We issue the 428th serious warning.

	Xinhua News Agency, March 14th: On March 14th, from 1:32 AM to 2:25 AM, 1:56 AM to 2:10 AM, 8:55 AM to 9:05 AM, 9:04 AM to 9:22 AM, 9:31 AM to 9:37 AM, and 10:08 AM to 10:41 AM, a total of six U.S. military aircraft successively invaded the airspace over the western region of Hainan Island in Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 12:11 PM to 12:30 PM, one U.S. military aircraft invaded the airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding the military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on instructions, issues the 428th serious warning.

### 美军用飞机侵入我广东省领空  我提出第四百二十八次严重警告

1967-03-15
第6版()
专栏：

    美军用飞机侵入我广东省领空
    我提出第四百二十八次严重警告
    新华社十四日讯　三月十四日一时三十二分至二时二十五分，一时五十六分至二时十分，八时五十五分至九时零五分，九时零四分至九时二十二分，九时三十一分至九时三十七分，十时零八分至十时四十一分，共有美国军用飞机六架，先后侵入我广东省海南岛西部地区领海上空。同日十二时十一分至十二时三十分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百二十八次严重警告。
