Title: Central Committee of the Communist Party of China United Front Work Department and Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Hold Memorial Ceremony to Commemorate the 42nd Anniversary of the Passing of Dr. Sun Yat-sen
Author:
Date: 1967-03-13
Edition: 2

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China United Front Work Department and the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held a memorial ceremony to commemorate the 42nd anniversary of the passing of Dr. Sun Yat-sen. Various leaders from Shanghai and Nanjing also held memorial activities.

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, March 12 — A memorial ceremony for the 42nd anniversary of the passing of Dr. Sun Yat-sen was held this morning at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall in Beiyun Temple, Xishan.

	Attending the ceremony were Jin Cheng, Vice Minister of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China United Front Work Department, and Zhang Zhongming and Cheng Qian, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, as well as some members of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang who are in Beijing.

	Also present were Mr. Li Zongren and others.

	After the ceremony began, the attendees stood in silence in front of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's portrait to pay their respects. Cheng Qian, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, presented a wreath to Dr. Sun Yat-sen's portrait.

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, March 12 — Today marks the 42nd anniversary of the passing of Dr. Sun Yat-sen. Leaders of the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee, including Zhang Chunqiao and Yao Wenyuan, representatives of various revolutionary mass organizations in Shanghai, and representatives from various relevant sectors in Shanghai, visited Dr. Sun Yat-sen's former residence in Shanghai.

	Leaders from the Jiangsu Provincial and Nanjing Municipal governments, representatives of revolutionary rebels, and leaders from various democratic parties and people's organizations in the province and city held a memorial ceremony at the Mausoleum of Dr. Sun Yat-sen this morning.

### 中共中央统战部和民革中央负责人举行纪念仪式  纪念孙中山先生逝世四十二周年  上海南京各方面负责人士分别举行纪念活动

1967-03-13
第2版()
专栏：

    中共中央统战部和民革中央负责人举行纪念仪式
    纪念孙中山先生逝世四十二周年
    上海南京各方面负责人士分别举行纪念活动
    新华社十二日讯  孙中山先生逝世四十二周年纪念仪式，今天上午在西山碧云寺孙中山纪念堂举行。
    中共中央统一战线工作部副部长金城，民革中央副主席程潜、张治中，民革中央部分在京常委，参加了纪念仪式。
    参加纪念仪式的还有李宗仁先生等。
    纪念仪式开始后，参加仪式的人员在孙中山先生遗像前肃立致敬，程潜代表民革中央向孙中山先生遗像献了花篮。
    新华社十二日讯今天是孙中山先生逝世四十二周年纪念日。上海市革命委员会领导成员张春桥、姚文元等同志，上海各革命群众组织代表，以及上海各有关方面人士，前往瞻仰了孙中山先生在上海的故居。
    江苏省及南京市级机关负责人和革命造反派的代表，省、市各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，今天上午在中山陵举行了纪念仪式。
