Title: Vietnamese Women at the Forefront of Anti-American Struggle Love Chairman Mao and His Works
Author:
Date: 1967-03-22
Edition: 5

Vietnamese women standing at the forefront of the anti-American struggle love Chairman Mao and his works. The photo shows four young women from Hanoi, after receiving a portrait of Chairman Mao and his works, looking at Chairman Mao's portrait with boundless joy. Photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
