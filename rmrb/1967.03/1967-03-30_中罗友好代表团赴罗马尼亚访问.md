Title: Chinese-Romanian Friendship Delegation Visits Romania
Author:
Date: 1967-03-30
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the Romanian Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, a six-member Chinese-Romanian Friendship Delegation departed Beijing by plane today for Romania. The delegation is led by Liu Luoming, a member of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Romania and the political commissar of a certain army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Relevant officials from our side and the Chargé d'Affaires of the Romanian Embassy in China were at the airport to see them off.

### 中罗友好代表团赴罗马尼亚访问

1967-03-30
第5版()
专栏：

    中罗友好代表团赴罗马尼亚访问
    新华社二十九日讯　应罗马尼亚对外文协邀请前往罗马尼亚访问的中罗友好代表团一行六人，今天乘飞机离京赴罗。代表团是由中罗友协理事、中国人民解放军某军政治委员刘路明率领的。我有关方面负责人和罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办曾到机场送行。
