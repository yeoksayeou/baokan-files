Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Four U.S. Aircraft and Damage Three U.S. Ships
Author:
Date: 1967-03-04
Edition: 6

Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down four U.S. aircraft and damage three U.S. ships

According to Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on the 2nd, it was announced here on the 2nd: On the 1st, the military and civilians of Thanh Hoa and Quang Ninh provinces in North Vietnam shot down three invading U.S. aggressor aircraft. Prior to this, on the 28th of February, the military and civilians of the western region of Ha Tinh province also shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft.

Thus, by the 1st of March, the total number of U.S. aggressor aircraft shot down by the military and civilians of North Vietnam had reached 1,702.

According to another report, the military and civilians of Nghe An and Quang Binh provinces, in their heroic battles on the 27th and 28th of February, damaged three U.S. warships that had savagely attacked the coastal residential areas of these two provinces.

### 越南军民击落美机四架击伤美舰三艘

1967-03-04
第6版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美机四架击伤美舰三艘
    据新华社河内二日电　据这里二日公布：越南北方清化、广宁两省军民一日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机三架。在这之前，二月二十八日，广平省西部地区军民也击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    这样，到三月一日为止，越南北方军民击落的美国强盗飞机总数已达一千七百零二架。
    另据报道，义安、河静两省军民，在二月二十七日和二十八日的英勇战斗中，击伤野蛮袭击这两省沿海居民区的美国军舰三艘。
