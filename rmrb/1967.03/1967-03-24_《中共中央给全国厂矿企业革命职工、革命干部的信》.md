Title: Message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to Revolutionary Workers and Revolutionary Cadres in All Factories and Mines Across the Country
Author:
Date: 1967-03-24
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao calls on you, the Party Central Committee calls on you, to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end. We hope that in this great proletarian cultural revolution, under these new conditions, you will redouble your efforts, strive for the forefront, and become the most outstanding models of grasping revolution and promoting production among the laboring masses of the nation in this stage.
