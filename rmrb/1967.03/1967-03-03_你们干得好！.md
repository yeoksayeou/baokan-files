Title: You've Done Well!
Author:
Date: 1967-03-03
Edition: 5

You've done well!

Japanese youth Kama-saki Jiluo recently sent a poem to the *East Wind* newspaper published in Tokyo. He is full of passion in praising the Chinese Red Guards and expresses that Japanese youth will forever be loyal students of Chairman Mao, holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and marching forward bravely. The poem reads:

	You've done well!
	You are the loyal executors of Mao Zedong Thought,
	You are Chairman Mao's best students,
	Fearlessly advancing in the storm of revolution.
	Imperialism, revisionism, and reactionaries of all countries
	Are afraid of your rebellion,
	Are afraid of your revolution,
	Are afraid of you—Chinese Red Guards.
	Imperialism, revisionism, and reactionaries of all countries
	Are even more afraid of your great leader,
	Who is also our great leader
	—Mao Zedong....
	We have heard,
	Your voices coming from across the ocean,
	We have seen,
	Your fighting spirit,
	We have felt,
	Your revolutionary fervor.
	We, the Japanese youth, the revolutionary people of Japan,
	Stand with you,
	Join hands with you.
	We will advance,
	We will also rebel,
	We will also make a revolution.
	We will forever defend the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought,
	Forever be Chairman Mao's loyal students.

### 你们干得好！

1967-03-03
第5版()
专栏：

    你们干得好！
    日本青年釜崎次郎最近寄诗给在东京出版的《东风报》，满怀激情地赞扬中国红卫兵，表示日本青年要永远做毛主席的忠实学生，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗奋勇前进。诗中写道：
    你们干得好！
    你们是毛泽东思想忠实的执行
      者，
    你们是毛主席最好的学生，
    大无畏地前进在革命的暴风雨
        中。
    帝国主义、修正主义、各国反动
        派，
    害怕你们造反，
    害怕你们革命，
    害怕你们——中国红卫兵。
    帝国主义、修正主义、各国反动
        派，
    更害怕你们的伟大领袖，
    也是我们的伟大领袖
    ——毛泽东。……
    我们听见了，
    从海洋彼岸传来了你们的声音，
    我们看见了，
    你们战斗的英姿，
    我们体会到了，
    你们革命的热情。
    我们日本青年，日本革命的人民，
    同你们在一起，
    同你们携起手来。
    我们要前进，
    我们也要造反，
    我们也要革命。
    我们也要永远保卫毛泽东思想伟大
        红旗，
    永远做毛主席忠实的学生。
