Title: China Donates Rice Milling Machines to Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1967-03-15
Edition: 5

China Donates Rice Milling Machines to Pakistan

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Rawalpindi, a ceremony to present ten rice milling machines donated by the Chinese government to the Pakistani government was held in the afternoon of March 9 in Karachi.

During the ceremony, a demonstration of the operation of the rice milling machines was performed, which was highly praised by Pakistani friends.

### 中国赠送巴基斯坦碾米机

1967-03-15
第5版()
专栏：

    中国赠送巴基斯坦碾米机
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第电　中国政府向巴基斯坦政府赠送十部碾米机的仪式九日下午在卡拉奇举行。
    在仪式上作了碾米机操作的表演，受到巴基斯坦朋友的赞扬。
