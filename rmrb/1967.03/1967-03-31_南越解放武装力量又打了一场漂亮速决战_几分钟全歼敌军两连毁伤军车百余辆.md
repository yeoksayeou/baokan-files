Title: The Liberation Forces in South Vietnam Have Won Another Swift and Decisive Battle, Annihilating Two Enemy Companies and Destroying Over a Hundred Vehicles in a Few Minutes
Author:
Date: 1967-03-31
Edition: 5

The liberation forces in South Vietnam have won another swift and decisive battle. In just a few minutes, they annihilated two enemy companies and destroyed or damaged over a hundred vehicles.

	Hanoi, March 30 (Xinhua) According to a report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, on March 24, the liberation forces in Quang Nam Province ambushed a convoy of American and puppet military vehicles loaded with weapons and military supplies near Da Nang. Preliminary reports indicate that two enemy companies were annihilated, 127 various military vehicles (including seven armored cars) were destroyed or damaged, and a large amount of military supplies were seized.

	The report said that at 6 PM on that day, the enemy convoy of 200 vehicles, traveling from Tuy Hoa to Da Nang, fell into the ambush of the liberation forces and immediately came under heavy fire. The liberation soldiers bravely rushed onto the road and engaged in close combat with the enemy. In just a few minutes of fighting, they achieved the above-mentioned brilliant results.

	Hanoi, March 30 (Xinhua) According to a report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam: From February 17 to March 19, the guerrilla forces and local troops in Phu Yen and Quang Nam Provinces continuously attacked the enemy, killing 857 enemy soldiers (including 604 American invaders), shooting down five helicopters, destroying five armored vehicles and other military vehicles, and blowing up one bridge.

	The report said that during this period, the guerrilla forces and local troops in Le Hong Phong and Thanh Nghia counties of Phu Yen Province alone killed 496 American invaders and 53 puppet soldiers, shot down five helicopters, and destroyed two armored vehicles.

	Hanoi, March 30 (Xinhua) According to a report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam: On March 24 and 25, the liberation forces and guerrilla troops in Ninh Thuan Province launched four consecutive attacks on the enemy on Highway 20, killing or wounding 180 American invaders and South Korean mercenaries, and 363 puppet soldiers. They also shot down 11 enemy aircraft, destroyed one military vehicle, and seized a batch of various weapons and military supplies.

	According to another report, the liberation forces in Binh Dinh Province, on March 22 and 23, in the battle to intercept the enemy on the Cam Ranh River, sank or damaged eight landing craft, annihilating all the enemy soldiers on board.

### 南越解放武装力量又打了一场漂亮速决战  几分钟全歼敌军两连毁伤军车百余辆

1967-03-31
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放武装力量又打了一场漂亮速决战
    几分钟全歼敌军两连毁伤军车百余辆
    新华社河内三十日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方广南省解放武装力量，二十四日在岘港南面截击一支满载武器和军用物资的美伪军车队，据初步消息，共全歼美伪军两个连，击毁击伤各种军车一百二十七辆（其中有装甲车七辆），并缴获大批军用物资。
    报道说，当天下午六时，这支由二百辆军车组成的敌军车队，在从朱莱开往岘港的途中，陷入解放武装力量的伏击阵地，立即遭到密集火力的猛烈射击。解放军战士英勇地冲到公路上，和敌人打近战。仅经过几分钟的战斗，就取得上述辉煌战果。
    新华社河内三十日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方平顺、广南两省游击队和地方部队，在二月十七日到三月十九日期间，连续袭击敌人，共消灭敌人八百五十七名（其中包括美国侵略军六百零四名），击落直升飞机五架，击毁装甲车和其他军车五辆，炸毁桥梁一座。
    报道说，在这期间，仅平顺省黎鸿峰县和性灵县游击队和地方部队就消灭美国侵略军四百九十六名、伪军五十三名，击落直升飞机五架，击毁装甲车两辆。
    新华社河内三十日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方林同省解放武装力量和游击队，二十四日和二十五日两天接连在第二十号公路上四次袭击敌人，共打死打伤美国侵略军和南朝鲜帮凶军一百八十名，伪军三百六十三名，击落敌机十一架，击毁军车一辆，缴获各种武器和军用物资一批。
    另据报道，槟椥省解放武装力量，二十二日和二十三日两天，在截击咸龙河上敌人的战斗中，击沉击伤登陆艇八艘，消灭艇上的全部敌军。
