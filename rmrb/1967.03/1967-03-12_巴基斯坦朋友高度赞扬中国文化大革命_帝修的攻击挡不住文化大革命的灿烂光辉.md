Title: Pakistani Friends Highly Praise China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-03-12
Edition: 5

Pakistani friends highly praise China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, and they strongly condemn the malicious slanders of imperialism and modern revisionism against the Chinese Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

A reporter from Karachi said: "The vigorous and extensive development of the Chinese Cultural Revolution reflects Chairman Mao Zedong's boundless trust in the vast Chinese people, and it reflects the Chinese people's boundless trust in their great leader, Chairman Mao Zedong. Only with such firm mutual trust between the great leader and the people can such a large-scale, unprecedented Cultural Revolution be carried out, can such fearless great democracy under the dictatorship of the proletariat be practiced, and can all the harm caused by the bourgeoisie and revisionism be thoroughly exposed and eliminated."

When talking about the slanders, fabrications, and distorted reports by Western and Soviet revisionist newspapers about the Chinese Cultural Revolution, he quoted Chairman Mao's words, saying that being opposed by the enemy is a good thing. He said: "From the vicious and frenzied degree of imperialism and revisionism's attacks on the Cultural Revolution, one can also see the correctness and greatness of the Chinese Cultural Revolution. The more they attack the Chinese Cultural Revolution, the more it shows the brilliant vitality of the Cultural Revolution."

A social worker said that the revolutionary workers, peasants, and students in China have set a good example for all revolutionary people in the world. They dare to rebel against imperialism and modern revisionism without fear. He said: "Chairman Mao Zedong's initiative to launch the Cultural Revolution has developed Marxism-Leninism and added new experiences to the practice of proletarian revolution and proletarian dictatorship."

A trade union leader from Karachi said: "Pakistani workers fully believe that with the Cultural Revolution unfolding across China, the situation in China is excellent and will become even better. The broad masses of the Chinese proletarian revolutionaries who have emerged during the Cultural Revolution have written a brilliant page in the history of the Chinese revolution and the world revolution. The slanders and defamations of U.S. imperialism and modern revisionism cannot in the slightest affect the great achievements of the Chinese Cultural Revolution and its enormous influence on the world proletarian revolutionary movement."

A friend from East Pakistan recently said in a speech: "Only under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought can all revolutionary people in the world successfully fight against imperialism and colonialism and ultimately eliminate them."

He also said: "After summing up the lessons of the Soviet leadership's degeneration into modern revisionism, Chairman Mao Zedong launched the Cultural Revolution, ensuring that China forever maintains its brilliant Marxist-Leninist character."

### 巴基斯坦朋友高度赞扬中国文化大革命  帝修的攻击挡不住文化大革命的灿烂光辉

1967-03-12
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦朋友高度赞扬中国文化大革命
    帝修的攻击挡不住文化大革命的灿烂光辉
    据新华社卡拉奇电  巴基斯坦朋友高度赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命，并且痛斥帝国主义和现代修正主义对中国无产阶级文化大革命的恶毒诬蔑。
    卡拉奇的一位记者说：“中国文化大革命蓬勃广泛的开展反映出，毛泽东主席无限信任中国广大的人民群众，反映出中国人民无限信任他们伟大的领袖毛泽东。伟大领袖和人民群众之间有了这样坚定不移的相互信任，才能这样大规模地开展史无前例的文化大革命，才能这样无所畏惧地实行无产阶级专政下的大民主，才能这样彻底地揭露和清除一切资产阶级和修正主义的祸害。”
    他在谈到西方和苏修报刊对中国文化大革命的诬蔑、捏造和歪曲报道时，引用毛主席的话说，被敌人反对是好事。他说：“从帝国主义和修正主义诬蔑文化大革命的恶毒和疯狂程度，也可以看出中国文化大革命的正确和伟大了。他们愈是攻击中国文化大革命，愈是显示出文化大革命光辉灿烂的生命力。”
    一位社会工作者说，革命的中国工人、农民和学生为世界一切革命人民树立了一个好榜样，他们敢于造帝国主义和现代修正主义的反，而无所畏惧。他说：“毛泽东主席发动文化大革命这一伟大的创举发展了马克思列宁主义，给无产阶级革命和无产阶级专政的实践增添了新的经验。”
    卡拉奇的一位工会领导人说：“巴基斯坦工人完全相信，由于文化大革命在中国全国范围内展开，中国的形势大好，而且会越来越好。在文化大革命中涌现出来的广大中国无产阶级革命派群众，在中国革命和世界革命史上写下了光辉的一页。美帝国主义和现代修正主义的一切诽谤诬蔑，丝毫也影响不了中国文化大革命的伟大成就及其对世界工人阶级革命运动的巨大影响。”
    东巴基斯坦的一位朋友最近发表谈话说：“只有在毛泽东思想的指导下，全世界革命人民才能胜利地向帝国主义和殖民主义作斗争，最后把它们消灭掉。”
    他还说：“毛泽东主席在总结了苏共领导集团堕落到现代修正主义的教训之后，发动了文化大革命，使中国永远保持光辉的马克思列宁主义的本色。”
