Title: Military and Civilians in North Vietnam's Quang Tri Province Damage a U.S. Warship; U.S. Imperialists Brazenly Drop Mines in North Vietnamese Rivers
Author:
Date: 1967-03-01
Edition: 6

	Military and civilians in North Vietnam's Quang Tri Province damage a U.S. warship
	U.S. imperialists brazenly drop mines in North Vietnamese rivers

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 28th – According to reports from here: On the 26th, the people's armed forces in North Vietnam's Quang Tri Province damaged an invading U.S. warship.

	That afternoon at 5:30 PM, the U.S. aggressors dispatched warships to shell residential areas along the coast of Quang Tri Province. The people's armed forces of Quang Tri Province immediately retaliated, damaging one of the ships. Seeing the situation was unfavorable, the U.S. pirates hastily fled south.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 28th – The U.S. imperialists have brazenly dispatched aircraft to drop mines in some rivers in North Vietnam. This is a new escalation of the war by the U.S. aggressors after they used long-range artillery from South Vietnam to shell the demilitarized zone and areas north of the demilitarized zone in North Vietnam.

	The Liaison Team of the General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army sent an urgent telegram to the International Control Commission in Vietnam on the 27th, strongly protesting this new, brutal war crime by U.S. imperialism.

### 越北方清化省军民击伤美军舰一艘  美帝公然向越南北方一些河流投放水雷

1967-03-01
第6版()
专栏：

    越北方清化省军民击伤美军舰一艘
    美帝公然向越南北方一些河流投放水雷
    新华社河内二十八日电　据这里公布：越南北方清化省人民武装力量二十六日击伤来犯的美国军舰一艘。
    当天下午五时三十分，美国强盗派遣军舰，炮轰清化省沿海的一些居民区。清化省人民武装力量立即予以反击，击伤其中一艘。美国海盗见势不妙，慌忙向南逃窜。
    新华社河内二十八日电　美帝国主义公然派遣飞机向越南北方的一些河流投放水雷。这是美国强盗继从越南南方用远程大炮轰击越南非军事区和非军事区以北的地区之后，对越南民主共和国所采取的新的战争“升级”行动。
    越南人民军总司令部联络代表团二十七日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会，强烈抗议美帝国主义这一新的残暴的战争罪行。
