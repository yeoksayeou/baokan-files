Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1967-03-27
Edition: 4

A delegation from the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union, led by Vice Chairman Wang Shengyao, arrived in Brazzaville by plane on the evening of the 25th.

The Chinese Trade Union delegation was invited by the Congolese (Brazzaville) Trade Union Confederation to attend the second congress of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Trade Union Confederation.

The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Alphonse Moussoundi, Administrative Secretary of the Executive Bureau of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Trade Union Confederation, General Treasurer Diodonne Miyakasisa, and Legal Secretary Floran Maranda.

There were also more than 150 Congolese workers, youth, and members of the National Guard present at the airport to welcome the Chinese delegation.

Li Chen Guang, the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Congo (Brazzaville), was also at the airport to greet the delegation.

### 我工会代表团到达刚果（布）

1967-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团到达刚果（布）
    新华社布拉柴维尔二十五日电　由中国邮电工会全国委员会副主任王声耀率领的中国工会代表团，二十五日晚上乘飞机到达布拉柴维尔。
    中国工会代表团是应刚果（布）工会联合会的邀请前来参加刚果（布）工会联合会第二次代表大会的。
    中国工会代表团在机场受到刚果（布）工会联合会执行局行政书记阿方斯·穆顺迪、总司库迪厄多内·米亚卡西萨和司法书记弗洛朗·马朗达的热烈欢迎。
    机场上还有一百五十多名刚果（布）工人、青年和全国民卫团的成员欢迎中国代表团。
    中国驻刚果（布）大使馆临时代办李琛光也到机场迎接。
