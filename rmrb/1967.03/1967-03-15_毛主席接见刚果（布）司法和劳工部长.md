Title: Chairman Mao Receives the Minister of Justice and Labor of Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1967-03-15
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao Zedong, our most beloved and great leader, received Congolese (Brazzaville) Minister of Justice and Labor Francois Makosso and his wife this evening.

Chairman Mao had a warm and friendly conversation with the Congolese (Brazzaville) guests.

Vice Premier of the State Council, Xie Fuzhi, and others participated in the reception.

Congo (Brazzaville) Ambassador to China, Apollinaire Bazenga, was also present at the reception. (Photo attached)

Our most beloved and great leader, Chairman Mao Zedong, received Congolese (Brazzaville) Minister of Justice and Labor Francois Makosso and his wife on March 14. On the left is Congolese (Brazzaville) Ambassador to China, Apollinaire Bazenga.

Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.

### 毛主席接见刚果（布）司法和劳工部长

1967-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见刚果（布）司法和劳工部长
    新华社十四日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛泽东主席，今晚接见了刚果（布）司法和劳工部长弗朗索瓦·马科索和他的夫人。
    毛主席同刚果（布）客人进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    参加接见的有国务院副总理谢富治等。
    接见时，刚果（布）驻中国大使阿波利奈尔·巴赞加也在座。（附图片）
    我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛泽东主席，三月十四日接见刚果（布）司法和劳工部长弗朗索瓦·马科索和他的夫人。左一是刚果（布）驻中国大使阿波利奈尔·巴赞加。
                            新华社记者摄
