Title: Speed Up Production to Support Spring Ploughing
Author: Pan Hang
Date: 1967-03-10
Edition: 6

	Shanghai Dazhong Machinery Factory is actively organizing production and ensuring the timely delivery of agricultural machinery to support the spring ploughing. The factory has made significant progress in recent days, with workers and technicians working tirelessly to meet the demands of the agricultural sector. The factory's leadership has emphasized the importance of quality and efficiency, ensuring that every piece of machinery produced is reliable and durable. The workers have responded with enthusiasm, vowing to complete the tasks ahead of schedule. The factory's efforts are part of a broader campaign to support the rural areas and ensure a bountiful harvest. The workers are determined to contribute to the country's agricultural development and support the ongoing revolution.
