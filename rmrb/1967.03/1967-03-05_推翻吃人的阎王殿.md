Title: Overthrow the Cannibalistic Hell of the Imperialists
Author:
Date: 1967-03-05
Edition: 5

Imperialism and the reactionaries of various countries are a gang of living hell lords who devour people.

This gang of living hell lords controls the military, police, courts, and the entire apparatus of oppression and aggression, enslaving and plundering the working people. They employ various tricks to deceive the working people. They use both soft and hard tactics, combining military and political means, with the sole purpose of making the oppressed working people of the world forever toil like beasts of burden, becoming their "speaking tools" for extracting profits.

However, where there is oppression, there is resistance. The oppressed nations and peoples of the world fear neither the suppression and intimidation of imperialism and its running dogs nor their sweet talk. They stand up to oppose the exploitation and oppression of imperialism and its running dogs. Even in Latin America, which is called the "backyard" of the United States, the people's struggle is developing day by day. The flames of revolutionary struggle by the oppressed nations and peoples have already burned into the hell lords' lair of American imperialism and its running dogs.

Chairman Mao said, "American imperialism and all other harmful creatures have already prepared their own gravediggers, and the day of their burial will not be far off." The oppressed nations and peoples of the world must overthrow the hell lords' lair of imperialism and all exploiters, overturn their cannibalistic banquet, and create a brand-new world without exploitation and oppression.

### 推翻吃人的阎王殿

1967-03-05
第5版()
专栏：短评

    推翻吃人的阎王殿
    帝国主义和各国反动派是一帮吃人的活阎王。
    这帮活阎王手里掌握着军队、警察、法院等专政工具和全套的侵略机器，奴役和掠夺劳动人民。他们耍弄种种花招，欺骗劳动人民。他们软硬兼施、文武并用，目的只有一个，就是要使全世界被压迫的劳动人民永远做牛做马，充当他们榨取利润的“会说话的工具”。
    但是，有压迫，就有反抗。全世界被压迫民族和被压迫人民，一不怕帝国主义及其走狗的镇压和恐吓，二不听它们的“好话”。他们挺身起来反对帝国主义及其走狗的剥削和压迫。即使在被称为美国的“后院”的拉丁美洲，人民的斗争也正在一天一天地发展。被压迫民族和被压迫人民革命斗争的熊熊烈火，已经烧进了美帝国主义及其走狗的阎王殿。
    毛主席说，“美帝国主义和其他一切害人虫已经准备好了自己的掘墓人，他们被埋葬的日子不会太长了。”全世界被压迫民族和被压迫人民，一定要推翻帝国主义和一切剥削者的阎王殿，掀翻他们的吃人的筵席，创造出一个没有剥削没有压迫的崭新的世界来。
