Title: The Most Cowardly Are the Most Brutal
Author:
Date: 1967-03-18
Edition: 5

The inhuman American imperialists, in defiance of the strong condemnation of world public opinion, are increasingly frenziedly developing chemical and biological weapons to suppress the national liberation movements in Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

A message from the Associated Press on the 14th revealed that the funds allocated by the U.S. forces invading Vietnam for chemical agents used to destroy crops have been increasing year by year: according to official figures, the fiscal year 1965-1966 saw $10 million, this year it surged to $39.5 million, and next year it will further increase to $49.5 million.

Previously, the American magazine *Science* reported that the United States is currently making "broad and massive efforts" to develop chemical and biological weapons. The funds allocated by the U.S. Department of Defense for this purpose have increased from $57 million in 1961 to approximately $150 million per year at present.

In reporting the scale of the U.S. military's development of bacterial weapons, the magazine cited the example of the highly secretive biological weapons research center at Fort Detrick, where 550 scientists, 14 doctors, and 34 veterinarians are at work. There is also "one of the world's largest livestock farms" and "the most advanced" equipment for researching pathogenic organisms.

### 怯懦者最残暴  美国疯狂发展生物和化学武器

1967-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    怯懦者最残暴
    美国疯狂发展生物和化学武器
    据新华社十七日讯　美国报刊、通讯社的消息表明，灭绝人性的美帝国主义者，不顾世界舆论的强烈谴责，正越来越疯狂地发展化学和生物武器，用它来镇压亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲的民族解放运动。
    美联社十四日从华盛顿发出的一条消息透露，侵越美军用于破坏庄稼的化学药剂的经费逐年增加：根据官方数字，一九六五——一九六六财政年度是一千万美元，本年度激增为三千九百五十万美元，下一年度将进一步增加到四千九百五十万美元。
    在这以前，美国《科学》杂志曾经报道说，美国目前正在作出“广泛和巨大的努力”来发展化学和生物武器，美国国防部用于这方面的经费，已经由一九六一年的五千七百万美元，增加到目前的每年一亿五千万美元左右。
    这家杂志在报道美国军方发展细菌武器的规模时，举出美国陆军的一个极秘密的细菌武器实验中心狄特里克堡为例说，在那里工作的包括五百五十名科学家，十四名医生，三十四名兽医。在那里还有“一个世界上最大的畜牧场”和对病原有机体进行研究的“最先进的”设备。
