Title: Chairman Azhari Presides Over the Opening Ceremony of the Sudan-China Friendship Association Club
Author:
Date: 1967-03-18
Edition: 4

Chairman Azhari Presides Over the Opening Ceremony of the Sudan-China Friendship Association Club
Hopes for the Complete Victory of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution

According to Xinhua News Agency in Khartoum on the 16th, Chairman Ismail al-Azhari of the Supreme Council of the State of Sudan presided over the opening ceremony of the Sudan-China Friendship Association Club in Khartoum on the evening of the 14th.

In his speech at the ceremony, Chairman Azhari praised China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and expressed his hope that the great Chinese people, under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, would achieve complete victory in this cultural revolution.

Chairman Azhari expressed his wish for the Sudan-China Friendship Association to achieve complete success in promoting the ties between the two countries' peoples.

Chairman Azhari also wrote a warm message in the guest book: "I hope this friendship will become the best bond between beloved Sudan and great China."

Dr. Abdel Wahab Zein Abdelin, Chairman of the Sudan-China Friendship Association, and Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Sudan, Xue Na, also spoke at the ceremony.

Dr. Abdel Wahab said that the friendship between the peoples of Sudan and China would become closer and deeper with the joint efforts of the two peoples. Xue Na, in his speech, praised the heroic struggle of the Sudanese people against the imperialist invaders during their rule. He expressed his belief that the Sudan-China Friendship Association would make further contributions to strengthening the mutual understanding and friendship between the two peoples.

Four hundred people attended the opening ceremony, including Chief Justice of the Sudan Supreme Court, Babiker Awadallah, Member of the Sudan Constitutional Assembly and former Minister, Qubara Awad, and many other social figures.

When the club opened, crowds of visitors flocked into the club's library, eagerly reading Chairman Mao's works and pamphlets about the Chinese Cultural Revolution.

### 阿扎里主席主持苏丹—中国友协俱乐部开幕式  希望中国文化大革命取得完全胜利

1967-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    阿扎里主席主持苏丹—中国友协俱乐部开幕式
    希望中国文化大革命取得完全胜利
    据新华社喀土穆十六日电　苏丹国家最高委员会主席伊斯梅尔·阿扎里十四日晚上在喀土穆主持了苏丹—中国友好协会俱乐部的开幕式。
    阿扎里主席在这个仪式上的讲话中赞扬了中国无产阶级文化大革命，并表示希望伟大的中国人民在毛泽东主席领导下开展的这场文化大革命取得完全胜利。
    阿扎里主席表示祝苏丹—中国友好协会在促进两国人民的联系方面取得圆满成功。
    阿扎里主席还在留言簿上写下了热情的留言：“我希望这种友谊成为可爱的苏丹和伟大的中国之间最好的纽带”。
    苏丹—中国友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·瓦哈卜·宰因·阿卜丁博士和中国驻苏丹大使馆临时代办雪呐也先后在仪式上讲了话。
    阿卜杜勒博士说，苏丹和中国两国人民之间的友谊将在两国人民共同努力下越来越密切和深厚。雪呐在讲话中赞扬了苏丹人民在帝国主义侵略者统治期间同他们进行的英勇斗争。他表示深信，苏丹—中国友好协会将对加强两国人民的相互了解和友谊作出进一步的贡献。
    出席开幕式的有四百人。其中有：苏丹最高法院首席法官巴比克尔·阿瓦达拉，苏丹制宪议会议员、前部长古巴拉·阿瓦德以及许多社会知名人士。
    当俱乐部开放的时候，成群结队的参观者挤进俱乐部里的图书馆，如饥似渴阅读毛主席的著作和关于中国文化大革命的小册子。
