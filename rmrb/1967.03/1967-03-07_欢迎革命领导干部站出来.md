Title: Welcome Revolutionary Cadres to Step Forward
Author: Luo Shisong
Date: 1967-03-07
Edition: 6

	Welcome Revolutionary Cadres to Step Forward (Couplets)
		Luo Shisong
		甲: Revolutionary cadres, 乙: Are the Party's precious assets;
		甲: Revolutionary cadres, 乙: Are the people's loyal servants.
		甲: They followed Chairman Mao, 乙: And fought in countless battles;
		甲: They followed Chairman Mao, 乙: And ignited the flames of revolution!
		甲: For the liberation of the nation, 乙: They fearlessly sacrificed their blood;
		甲: To establish a revolutionary government, 乙: They made significant contributions.
		甲: They are veteran revolutionaries, 乙: With rich experience in struggle.
		甲: Welcome revolutionary cadres 乙: To stand firmly on Chairman Mao's revolutionary line;
		甲: To uphold revolutionary integrity, 乙: And draw a clear line with the capitalist-roaders within the Party;
		甲: To diligently study and apply the "Three Articles," 乙: Correct mistakes and overcome shortcomings!
		甲: To join the revolutionary masses, 乙: And overthrow the old world!
		甲: To thoroughly expose and denounce 乙: The bourgeois reactionary line!
		甲: We revolutionary masses, 乙: Must set an example in executing the highest directives:
		甲: "Who are our enemies? Who are our friends? 乙: This is the primary question of revolution."
		We must not be the least bit vague!
		甲: The slogans of "rejecting all" and "overthrowing all" 乙: Are incorrect and must be severely criticized!
		甲: According to Chairman Mao's policy on cadres, 乙: Correctly handle cadres and prevent recklessness;
		甲: Under the great banner of Mao Zedong Thought, 乙: Unite the majority and fight together!
		甲: Implement the great alliance of revolutionary masses, 乙: Promote the "three-in-one combination" experience.
		甲: Only with the "three-in-one combination," 乙: Can we seize the leadership;
		甲: Only with the "three-in-one combination," 乙: Can we firmly grasp revolutionary power!
		甲: We must heighten our revolutionary vigilance, 乙: And expose the counter-revolutionary tricks of the class enemy;
		甲: Use Mao Zedong Thought to observe and analyze everything, 乙: To grasp the revolutionary direction and march forward to victory!
		甲: Step forward, revolutionary cadres, 乙: In this decisive moment of class struggle,
		甲: Be bold and decisive! 乙: Make new contributions in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!
		甲: Go to the broad revolutionary masses, 乙: Transform and temper yourself!
		甲: Work together with the revolutionary masses, 乙: Seize power from the capitalist-roaders within the Party!
		甲: Work together with the revolutionary masses, 乙: Grasp revolution and promote production.
		甲: Hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, 乙: Revolution to the end, with unchanging red hearts!
		甲: Look! The revolutionary storm sweeps the nation, 乙: Listen! The victory songs echo through the skies.
		甲: The "three-in-one combination" seizes power excellently! 乙: Chairman Mao's revolutionary army marches forward in a surging tide!
