Title: President of Congo (Brazzaville) Receives Chinese Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-03-27
Edition: 4

According to Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville, March 25th: On the afternoon of the 24th, President of Congo (Brazzaville) Massamba-Debat and his wife received the Chinese Women's Delegation and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

Massamba-Debat expressed his admiration for China's Cultural Revolution. He said, "The imperialists are especially afraid of China's cultural revolution."

When talking about the U.S. aggression in Vietnam, he emphasized that the failure of the U.S. aggressors in Vietnam proves that the U.S. imperialists are paper tigers.

Massamba-Debat concluded by expressing that in the struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism, the women of Congo (Brazzaville) stand together with the women of China.

### 刚果（布）总统接见我妇女代表团

1967-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）总统接见我妇女代表团
    据新华社布拉柴维尔二十五日电  刚果（布）总统马桑巴—代巴和夫人二十四日下午在这里接见了中国妇女代表团，并同她们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    马桑巴—代巴总统对中国的文化大革命表示赞赏。他说，帝国主义者特别害怕中国的文化革命。
    他在谈到美国在越南的侵略时强调指出：美国侵略者在越南的失败，证明美帝国主义者是纸老虎。
    马桑巴—代巴总统最后表示，在反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的斗争中，刚果（布）妇女同中国妇女站在一起。
