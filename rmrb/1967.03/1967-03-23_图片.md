Title: Qingdao Steel Plant's Revolutionary Faction Takes Control, Production Soars
Author:
Date: 1967-03-23
Edition: 4

After the revolutionary faction took control of the Qingdao Steel Plant, production has been thriving. The image shows a large batch of steel bars produced by this plant, which are being prepared for shipment to various locations to support the socialist construction of the motherland. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
