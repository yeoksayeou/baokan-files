Title: Commemorating the 17th Anniversary of the National Anti-American Struggle Day in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-03-26
Edition: 6

	Vietnamese South National Liberation Front's Deputy Head of the Permanent Delegation to China, Nguyen Minh Hoang, held a film screening event today to commemorate the 17th anniversary of the "March 19" National Anti-American Struggle Day in Vietnam.

	Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Guo Moruo, leaders from the People's Liberation Army, various government departments and relevant sectors, as well as representatives from the Red Guards of Beijing and representatives from the revolutionary proletariat of various sectors in Beijing, attended the film screening event.

	Guests invited to the film screening event also included Charge d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, Le Chung Chi, and friends from various countries in Beijing. The film screening event featured a documentary reflecting the struggle of the people of South Vietnam against American aggression and for national salvation, as well as the Vietnamese film "Nguyen Van Truong."

### 纪念越南全国反美斗争日十七周年  阮明芳代团长举行电影招待会

1967-03-26
第6版()
专栏：

    纪念越南全国反美斗争日十七周年
    阮明芳代团长举行电影招待会
    新华社二十五日讯  越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳今天举行电影招待会，纪念“三·一九”越南全国反美斗争日十七周年。
    人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，中国人民解放军、政府各部门和有关方面负责人以及首都红代会的代表和首都各界无产阶级革命派的代表，出席了电影招待会。
    应邀出席电影招待会的，还有越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始和在北京的各国朋友。电影招待会上放映了反映越南南方人民抗美救国斗争的纪录影片和越南故事片《阮文追》。
