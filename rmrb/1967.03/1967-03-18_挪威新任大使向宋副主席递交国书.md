Title: New Norwegian Ambassador Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Song
Author:
Date: 1967-03-18
Edition: 4

New Norwegian Ambassador Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Song

Xinhua News Agency, March 17th — The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Norway to the People's Republic of China, Ole Ohlsen, presented his credentials to Vice Chairman Song Qingling of the People's Republic of China this afternoon, accompanied by diplomatic staff from the embassy.

After presenting his credentials, Vice Chairman Song Qingling had a friendly conversation with Ambassador Ohlsen. Present were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and others.

### 挪威新任大使向宋副主席递交国书

1967-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威新任大使向宋副主席递交国书
    新华社十七日讯　挪威王国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使乌勒·奥尔高，今天下午偕同大使馆外交人员，向中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄递交国书。
    递交国书后，宋庆龄副主席同奥尔高大使进行了友好的谈话。在场的有外交部副部长罗贵波等。
