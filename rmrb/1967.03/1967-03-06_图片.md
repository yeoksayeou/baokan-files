Title: Dazhai Villagers Enthusiastically Respond to Chairman Mao's Call to "Seize the Revolution and Promote Production"
Author:
Date: 1967-03-06
Edition: 3

The villagers of Dazhai are enthusiastically responding to Chairman Mao's great call to "seize the revolution and promote production." They are actively repairing terraced fields, striving for an even greater harvest this year. Photographed by a reporter from this newspaper.
