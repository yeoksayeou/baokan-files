Title: China Completes Warehouse Project in Nepal, King Mahendra Attends Completion Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1967-03-28
Edition: 5

	China's aid to Nepal for the construction of a warehouse in Birganj has been completed. King Mahendra presided over the completion ceremony on March 21.

	Birganj is Nepal's second-largest city. Construction of the warehouse began in November 1965 and covers an area of 7,000 square meters.

	At the grand completion ceremony, Nepal's Minister of Commerce and Industry, Nakindra Prasad Rijal, praised China for providing aid without any conditions, stating that it was entirely for the development of Nepal's economy. He said, "The friendship between Nepal and China is based on the principle of equality." He expressed his gratitude on behalf of the Nepalese government for China's recent decision to provide 20,000 tons of rice as aid.

	Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Yang Gongsu, spoke about the great significance of the Cultural Revolution currently underway in China. He pointed out that this revolution would make China more united, stronger, and more consolidated, and would more resolutely implement Chairman Mao's correct policies and revolutionary line. China would provide more effective support to the just struggles of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

### 我援助尼泊尔一仓库工程完工  马亨德拉国王主持落成典礼

1967-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    我援助尼泊尔一仓库工程完工
    马亨德拉国王主持落成典礼
    据新华社加德满都电　中国援助尼泊尔的伯甘吉仓库工程已经最后完工，马亨德拉国王于三月二十一日亲自主持了这座仓库的落成典礼。
    伯甘吉是尼泊尔第二大城市，这座仓库从一九六五年十一月开始修建，占地面积为七千平方米。
    在隆重的落成典礼上，尼泊尔工商大臣纳金德拉·普拉萨德·里贾尔在讲话中赞扬中国不附带任何条件提供各方面的援助，完全是为了尼泊尔经济的发展。他说：“尼中两国的友谊是建立在平等原则的基础上的。”他代表尼泊尔政府，对中国最近作出的无偿援助两万吨大米的友好决定再次表示感谢。
    中国驻尼泊尔大使杨公素在讲话中概述了目前正在中国蓬勃开展的无产阶级文化大革命的伟大意义。他指出，这场文化大革命，将使中国更加团结、强大和巩固，更坚决地执行毛主席的正确政策和革命路线，中国将给予亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲各国人民的正义斗争以更有效的支持。
