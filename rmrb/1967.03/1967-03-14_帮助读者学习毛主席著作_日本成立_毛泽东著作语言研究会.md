Title: Helping Readers Study Chairman Mao's Works: Japan Establishes "Mao Zedong Works Language Research Society"
Author:
Date: 1967-03-14
Edition: 4

	Japanese linguists have recently initiated the establishment of the "Mao Zedong Works Language Research Society" and have published the magazine "Mao Zedong Works Language Research." The purpose of establishing the "Mao Zedong Works Language Research Society" is to help readers easily read the Chinese version of Mao Zedong's works and to promote the correct understanding of New China and modern Chinese language among the Japanese people. In addition to publishing the "Mao Zedong Works Language Research" magazine, the society also compiles and publishes annotated textbooks of Mao Zedong's works, vocabulary indexes, and dictionaries of Mao Zedong's works.

	One of the editors of "Mao Zedong Works Language Research," Professor Junichi Kaban of Osaka City University, praised the Chinese people's enthusiasm for studying and applying Chairman Mao's works in the editorial note of the inaugural issue. He said that the Japanese people must seriously study Mao Zedong's works if they truly want to understand China.

	The inaugural issue also published a congratulatory letter from the Institute of Linguistics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, celebrating the establishment of the research society.

### 帮助读者学习毛主席著作  日本成立“毛泽东著作语言研究会”

1967-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    帮助读者学习毛主席著作
    日本成立“毛泽东著作语言研究会”
    新华社东京电　日本一些著名语言学家，最近发起成立“毛泽东著作语言研究会”，并且出版了《毛泽东著作语言研究》杂志。成立“毛泽东著作语言研究会”的目的，是为了帮助读者容易阅读中文版的毛泽东著作，并且促进日本人民正确地理解新中国和中国现代语言。这个研究会除了出版《毛泽东著作语言研究》杂志以外，还编辑出版毛泽东著作注解的课本、毛泽东著作语汇索引和毛泽东著作辞典。
    《毛泽东著作语言研究》的编辑人之一，大阪市立大学教授香板顺一在这个杂志创刊号的编辑后记中，称赞中国人民满腔热情地活学活用毛主席著作。他说，日本人民要想真正了解中国，就必须认真地学习毛泽东著作。
    创刊号刊登了中国科学院语言研究所祝贺这个研究会成立的贺信。
