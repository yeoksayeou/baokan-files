Title: Thunderous Spring Thunder Resounds Across the Seas, Revolutionary Workers Joyful
Author:
Date: 1967-03-21
Edition: 6

	Spring thunder roars across the seas,
	Revolutionary workers' hearts fill with joy.
	Seize the revolution, promote production,
	Two heavy burdens are shouldered.
