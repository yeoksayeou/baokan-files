Title: The Liberation Army of South Vietnam Scores Another Brilliant Annihilation Battle in Bau Bang
Author:
Date: 1967-03-25
Edition: 6

The Liberation Army of South Vietnam has once again achieved a brilliant annihilation battle in Bau Bang. In just one hour of combat, they completely wiped out an entire battalion of the U.S. military.

The people and military of Xining Province have, in 23 days, annihilated over 5,000 U.S. troops.

HANOI, March 23 (Xinhua) -- According to the Liberation Communications Agency of South Vietnam, the liberation armed forces of the Vietnamese province of Tuyen Duc on March 19 completely annihilated a battalion of the U.S. 9th Infantry Division in the Bau Bang area.

The report said that at 13:45 on March 19, the liberation armed forces launched a fierce attack on the U.S. 9th Infantry Division's battalion stationed in the northern part of the Bau Bang area on Highway 13. The liberation armed forces, divided into multiple units, swiftly infiltrated the enemy lines, directly striking the enemy's artillery command center and heavy vehicle depot, and simultaneously firing at the fleeing U.S. troops. After one hour of fighting, the liberation armed forces completely annihilated this battalion, which included one infantry company and two armored companies. They destroyed over 100 vehicles, including 54 M-41 tanks and M-113 armored personnel carriers, shot down one helicopter, and seized a batch of weapons and military supplies.

This is the fourth major victory achieved by the liberation armed forces of Tuyen Duc Province in the Bau Bang area since July 1965. Prior to this, the liberation armed forces had annihilated five U.S. invasion army companies and two puppet army companies in this area in July and November 1965 and March 1966.

HANOI, March 23 (Xinhua) -- According to the Liberation Communications Agency of South Vietnam, the liberation armed forces of Xining Province, on March 21, achieved a brilliant victory in the battle against the "sweeping" operation, completely annihilating one U.S. invasion army company and severely damaging another.

In this battle, the liberation armed forces also destroyed 72 enemy vehicles of various types and 14 105mm and 155mm artillery pieces, and shot down nine aircraft of various types.

The report said that on the morning of March 21, the liberation armed forces of Xining Province launched a fierce attack on a unit of the U.S. 4th Infantry Division's 3rd Brigade stationed about 30 kilometers northeast of Xining City in the Dong Nai area. As soon as the fierce artillery fire directed at the enemy stopped, the brave spearhead units of the liberation armed forces immediately charged towards the enemy, killing many enemy soldiers. The liberation armed forces ended this brilliant ambush battle in just ten minutes.

HANOI, March 24 (Xinhua) -- According to the Liberation Communications Agency of South Vietnam, the heroic military and people of Xining Province, from February 22 to March 16, in the battle against the large-scale "sweeping" operation launched by the U.S. invaders, annihilated 5,216 U.S. invaders (previously reported as 4,516) and 62 South Korean mercenaries, destroyed 420 tanks, armored vehicles, and other vehicles (previously reported as 406), shot down 91 helicopters (previously reported as 85), including six heavy-lift helicopters known as "flying cranes," and destroyed 55 artillery pieces and mortars. The report said that among the annihilated enemy forces, there were two armored battalions and one armored company, eight infantry companies, and three artillery companies of the U.S. invasion army.

The guerrilla forces also made significant contributions to this brilliant victory, annihilating 2,090 U.S. invaders, destroying 106 tanks and armored vehicles, and shooting down 60 helicopters.

### 南越解放军在保邦又打了一场漂亮的歼灭战  一小时战斗全歼美军一个战团  西宁省军民二十三天共歼灭美军五千多

1967-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

    南越解放军在保邦又打了一场漂亮的歼灭战
    一小时战斗全歼美军一个战团
    西宁省军民二十三天共歼灭美军五千多
    新华社河内二十三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方土龙木省解放武装力量，十九日在保邦地区干净彻底地消灭了美国第九步兵师的一个战团。
    报道说，十九日十三时四十五分，解放武装力量向驻扎在第十三号公路上保邦地区北部的美国第九步兵师的一个战团发起猛烈进攻。解放武装力量分成多路，迅速插入敌阵，直捣敌人炮兵指挥部和重型军车场，同时猛烈射击四处逃窜的美军。经过一个小时的战斗，解放武装力量就全部消灭了这个包括一个步兵营和两个装甲营的战团，击毁军车一百多辆，其中有M—41型坦克和M—113型装甲车五十四辆，击落直升飞机一架，缴获武器和军用物资一批。
    这是土龙木省解放武装力量自一九六五年七月以来在保邦地区取得的第四次大捷。在这之前，解放武装力量曾于一九六五年七月、十一月和一九六六年三月先后在这个地区全歼美国侵略军五个营和伪军两个营。
    新华社河内二十三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方西宁省解放武装力量二十一日在反“扫荡”战斗中，取得全歼美国侵略军一个营、重创另一个营的辉煌胜利。
    在这次战斗中，解放武装力量还击毁敌人各种军车七十二辆和一○五、一五五毫米口径大炮十四门，击落各种类型飞机九架。
    报道说，西宁省解放武装力量二十一日上午猛烈袭击离西宁市东北面约三十公里的同若地区的美国第四步兵师第三旅的一支部队。当射向敌人的猛烈炮火刚刚停止，英勇的解放武装力量的各尖刀组立即冲向敌军，杀得敌人死伤累累。解放武装力量仅用十分钟的时间就结束了这场漂亮的奇袭战。
    据新华社河内二十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社补充报道，英雄的越南南方西宁省军民，二月二十二日到三月十六日，在反击美国侵略者发动的大规模“扫荡”的战斗中，共消灭美国侵略军五千二百一十六名（前报为四千五百一十六名）和南朝鲜帮凶军六十二名，击毁坦克、装甲车和其它军车四百二十辆（前报为四百零六辆），击落直升飞机九十一架（前报为八十五架），其中包括号称“飞行起重机”的重型直升飞机六架，击毁大炮和迫击炮五十五门。报道说，在被消灭的敌人中，包括全歼美国侵略军装甲部队两个战团和一个装甲营、八个步兵连和三个炮兵连。
    游击队对这次战斗的辉煌战绩也作出了重大的贡献，共消灭美国侵略军二千零九十名，击毁坦克和装甲车一百零六辆，击落直升飞机六十架。（附图片）
