Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Invades Airspace Over Guangdong Province
Author:
Date: 1967-03-01
Edition: 6

U.S. military aircraft invaded the airspace over Guangdong Province.

On February 27, at 11:44 AM to 11:53 AM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above the sea near the northeastern region of Wenchang County, Hainan Island, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 10:12 AM to 10:32 AM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Dong Island and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. In response to this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, issued the 426th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我广东省领空  我提出第四百二十六次严重警告

1967-03-01
第6版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我广东省领空
    我提出第四百二十六次严重警告
    新华社二十七日讯　二月二十七日十一时四十四分至十一时五十三分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省海南岛文昌县东北地区领海上空。同日十时十二分至十时三十二分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的东岛、永兴岛地区领空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百二十六次严重警告。
