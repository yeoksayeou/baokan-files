Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Deals Heavy Blow to South Korean Mercenary Forces
Author:
Date: 1967-03-08
Edition: 5

South Vietnamese Liberation Army Deals Heavy Blow to South Korean Mercenary Forces

Attacks Mercenary Base, Annihilates Over 600 Enemies

According to a supplementary report by the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, the liberation armed forces of Quang Ngai Province in South Vietnam, in their attack on the South Korean mercenary base in Son Ha Commune, Son Tinh District on February 15, and in their anti-"sweeping" battles along the banks of the Tra Khuc River from February 17 to 19, completely annihilated a South Korean mercenary battalion and two puppet battalions. They also inflicted heavy casualties on three puppet battalions, eliminating nearly 1,900 enemies, including over 600 South Korean mercenaries. They captured and forced the enemy to withdraw from three bases, destroyed or damaged 32 enemy cannons of various types, burned down two ammunition depots, destroyed two M-113 amphibious armored vehicles and two helicopters, and seized a large quantity of weapons and military supplies.

### 南越解放军狠揍南朝鲜帮凶军  袭击帮凶军据点歼敌六百多名

1967-03-08
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放军狠揍南朝鲜帮凶军
    袭击帮凶军据点歼敌六百多名
    据新华社河内七日电　据越南南方解放通讯社补充报道：越南南方广义省解放武装力量，在二月十五日袭击山晶县山金乡南朝鲜帮凶军据点和二月十七日到十九日在茶曲江两岸进行的反“扫荡”战斗中，全歼南朝鲜帮凶军一个营和伪军两个营，另外还严重杀伤伪军三个营，消灭敌人将近一千九百名，其中有南朝鲜帮凶军六百多名。攻克和迫使敌人撤出三个据点，击毁和击伤敌人的各种大炮三十二门，烧毁弹药库两座，击毁M—113型两栖装甲车两辆和直升飞机两架，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
