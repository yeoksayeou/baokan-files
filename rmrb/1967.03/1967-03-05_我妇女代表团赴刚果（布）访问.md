Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Visits Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1967-03-05
Edition: 4

At the invitation of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Revolutionary Women's Union to participate in the celebration of its second anniversary, the Chinese women's delegation, led by Yang Luying, deputy director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Women's Federation, departed Beijing by plane this morning for Congo (Brazzaville).

### 我妇女代表团赴刚果（布）访问

1967-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    我妇女代表团赴刚果（布）访问
　　新华社三日讯　应邀参加刚果（布）革命妇女联盟成立两周年庆祝活动的中国妇女代表团，由全国妇联国际联络部副部长杨榴英率领，今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往刚果（布）。
