Title: Chairman Mao Meets Comrade Wilcox
Author:
Date: 1967-03-13
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao Meets Comrade Wilcox

A very warm conversation was held during the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, March 12th: Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the great leader of the Chinese people, met with Comrade V.J. Wilcox, General Secretary of the New Zealand Communist Party, this afternoon.

Comrade Mao Zedong had a very warm conversation with Comrade Wilcox.

Those who attended the meeting included Comrades Zhou Enlai and Kang Sheng, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee.

Comrade Rita Smith, member of the National Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party who is in Beijing, also attended the meeting. (Photo attached)

### 毛主席会见威尔科克斯同志  会见时进行了极为亲切的谈话

1967-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席会见威尔科克斯同志
    会见时进行了极为亲切的谈话
    新华社十二日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席、中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东同志，今天下午会见了新西兰共产党总书记维·乔·威尔科克斯同志。
    毛泽东同志同威尔科克斯同志进行了极为亲切的谈话。
    参加会见的，有中共中央政治局常委周恩来同志和康生同志，中央书记处书记刘宁一同志。
    在北京的新西兰共产党全国委员会委员丽塔·史密斯同志也参加了会见。（附图片）
    中国共产党中央委员会主席、中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东同志，三月十二日会见新西兰共产党总书记维·乔·威尔科克斯同志（左三）。右二是在京的新西兰共产党全国委员会委员丽塔·史密斯同志。
                                          新华社记者摄
