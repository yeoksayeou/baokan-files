Title: The State Council Approves the China-Mauritania Economic, Cultural, and Trade Agreements
Author:
Date: 1967-03-25
Edition: 2

The State Council of the People's Republic of China approved on March 14 the Economic and Technical Cooperation Agreement, the Cultural Cooperation Agreement, and the Trade Agreement signed on February 16, 1967, in Beijing between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania.

### 国务院核准中毛经济、文化和贸易协定

1967-03-25
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院核准中毛经济、文化和贸易协定
    新华社二十四日讯　中华人民共和国国务院在三月十四日核准了一九六七年二月十六日在北京签订的中华人民共和国政府和毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国政府经济技术合作协定、文化合作协定和贸易协定。
