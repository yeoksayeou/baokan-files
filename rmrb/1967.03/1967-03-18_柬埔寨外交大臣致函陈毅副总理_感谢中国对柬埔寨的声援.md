Title: Cambodian Foreign Minister Writes to Vice Premier Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1967-03-18
Edition: 4

Cambodian Foreign Minister Writes to Vice Premier Chen Yi
Expressing Gratitude for China's Support to Cambodia

Xinhua News Agency, March 17th: Chinese Foreign Minister Chen Yi received a letter from Cambodian Foreign Minister Norodom Phurissara on March 11th. In the letter, he expressed gratitude for the statement issued by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China on February 25th, which condemned the repeated provocations and armed invasions by U.S. imperialists and their lackeys against Cambodia.

The letter stated: "The government of the People's Republic of China strongly condemned this act of aggression and firmly supported the just stance of the Royal Government of Cambodia. This once again demonstrates that the government of the People's Republic of China actively and fraternally supports our struggle to maintain independence and territorial integrity."

In his letter, Norodom Phurissara, on behalf of the Royal Government of Cambodia and the Cambodian people, expressed sincere and heartfelt thanks to Chinese Foreign Minister Chen Yi and the Chinese people.

### 柬埔寨外交大臣致函陈毅副总理  感谢中国对柬埔寨的声援

1967-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨外交大臣致函陈毅副总理
    感谢中国对柬埔寨的声援
    新华社十七日讯　中国外交部长陈毅收到柬埔寨外交大臣诺罗敦·富里萨拉三月十一日的一封来信，信中表示感谢中华人民共和国外交部今年二月二十五日，就美帝国主义者及其走狗不断对柬埔寨蓄意进行挑衅和进行武装入侵一事所发表的声明。
    来信说：“中华人民共和国政府强烈谴责这种侵略罪行，并且坚决支持柬埔寨王国政府的正义立场，这就再一次向我们表明，中华人民共和国政府对我们维护独立和领土完整的斗争给予积极的兄弟般的声援。”
    诺罗敦·富里萨拉在来信中代表柬埔寨王国政府和柬埔寨人民向中国外交部长陈毅以及中国人民表示热诚和真挚的感谢。
