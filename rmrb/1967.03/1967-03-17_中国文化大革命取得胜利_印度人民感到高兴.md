Title: Victory of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution Delights Indian People
Author:
Date: 1967-03-17
Edition: 5

Recent reports indicate that several friends in India have been praising the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, and the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution of China.

One Indian friend wrote in a letter to a Chinese friend, "The oppressed and exploited peoples of the world are grateful to the great and beloved Chairman Mao, the great Communist Party of China, and the Chinese people. They have inspired the peoples of the world to become more conscious and more resolute in their struggle against imperialism, modern revisionism, and all reactionaries."

The letter states, "The achievements of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China are the most reliable guarantee for the ultimate victory of communism in the world. The oppressed and exploited peoples of the world, including those in India, rejoice at every victory achieved in this great revolution."

Another Indian friend wrote in a letter: "Chairman Mao is forever with the suffering people."

### 中国文化大革命取得胜利  印度人民感到高兴

1967-03-17
第5版()
专栏：

    中国文化大革命取得胜利
    印度人民感到高兴
    据新华社讯　最近，印度的一些朋友，纷纷赞扬中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席和中国无产阶级文化大革命。
    一位印度朋友在写给中国朋友的信中说，世界上被压迫和被剥削的人民，感谢伟大的和敬爱的毛主席、伟大的中国共产党和中国人民，因为他们鼓舞世界人民更加自觉和更加坚决地向帝国主义、现代修正主义和一切反动派作斗争。
    信中说，伟大的中国无产阶级文化大革命的成就是“共产主义在世界上取得最后胜利的最可靠的保证。包括印度在内的全世界被压迫和被剥削的人民，对这场伟大革命所取得的每一个胜利都感到高兴。”
    另一位印度朋友在信中说：毛主席永远同受苦受难人民在一起。
