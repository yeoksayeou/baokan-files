Title: Workers at Shanghai Electric Machine Factory Seize Power and Promote Production
Author:
Date: 1967-03-30
Edition: 1

After seizing power, the revolutionary masses of the proletariat and the broad revolutionary workers at the Shanghai Electric Machine Factory have been steadfast in promoting revolution and boosting production. This is a scene of revolutionary workers in a large electric motor workshop assembling a 1350-kilowatt, eight-pole electric motor. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
