Title: Pakistani Laborers Warmly Hail the Great World Significance of the Cultural Revolution Led by Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-03-27
Edition: 5

Pakistani laborers warmly hail the great world significance of the Cultural Revolution personally led by Chairman Mao. The photo shows Pakistani masses at Lahore Airport, who were seeing off the Chinese folk song and dance troupe, holding high portraits of Chairman Mao and shouting, "Long live Chairman Mao!" Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
