Title: Celebrating the 22nd Anniversary of the Establishment of the Myanmar Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1967-03-28
Edition: 5

The military attaché of the Embassy of the Union of Burma in China, Rear Admiral Kyaw Khin, held a reception tonight to celebrate the 22nd anniversary of the establishment of the Union of Burma Armed Forces.

The Deputy Minister of National Defense, Xiao Jinguang, the Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Peng Shaohui, and the heads of various departments, services, and branches, as well as relevant officials, were invited to attend the reception.

The Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, Thakin Than Tun, and the ambassadors and military attachés of various countries in China also attended the reception.

### 庆祝缅甸武装部队建立二十二周年  缅甸驻华武官举行招待会

1967-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝缅甸武装部队建立二十二周年
    缅甸驻华武官举行招待会
    新华社二十七日讯　缅甸联邦驻中国大使馆武官齐柯柯海军上校今晚举行招待会，庆祝缅甸联邦武装部队建立二十二周年。
    国防部副部长萧劲光，中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉，以及各总部、各军种、各兵种的负责人和有关方面的负责人，应邀出席了招待会。
    缅甸联邦驻中国大使沙马杜瓦·信瓦瑙，各国驻中国的使节和武官，也出席了招待会。
