Title: On the Revolutionary "Three-in-One Combination"
Author: 
Date: 1967-03-16
Edition: 3

The editorial "On the Revolutionary 'Three-in-One Combination'" from the fifth issue of 1967 of the *Red Flag* magazine, along with the editorials "The Revolutionary 'Three-in-One Combination' is the Guarantee of Victory in the Struggle for Power" from the *People's Daily*, "Firmly Defend the Correct Policy of the 'Three-in-One Combination'", "Thoroughly Smash the Counter-Revolutionary Tide" from the *Liberation Army Daily*, and "The Revolution Knows No Sequence" from the *Wenhui Daily*, totaling five articles, have been compiled into a book by the People's Publishing House and published.

This book will be distributed in Beijing starting March 16 and will be successively distributed across the country.

### 《论革命的“三结合”》一书出版

1967-03-16
第3版()
专栏：

    《论革命的“三结合”》一书出版
    新华社十五日讯　《红旗》杂志一九六七年第五期社论《论革命的“三结合”》，以及《人民日报》社论《革命的“三结合”是夺权斗争胜利的保证》、《坚决捍卫“三结合”的正确方针》、《解放军报》社论《彻底粉碎反革命逆流》和《文汇报》社论《革命不分先后》，共五篇文章，已由人民出版社汇集成书出版。
    这本书于三月十六日起在北京发行，全国各地也将陆续发行。
