Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-03-19
Edition: 1

The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is a powerful driving force for the development of our country's productive forces. It is incorrect to oppose the Cultural Revolution to the development of production.

### 《中国共产党中央委员会关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定》

1967-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    无产阶级文化大革命是使我国社会生产力发展的一个强大的推动力。把文化大革命同发展生产对立起来，这种看法是不对的。
