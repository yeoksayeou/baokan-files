Title: China and Bulgaria Sign Science and Technology Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1967-03-01
Edition: 6

The tenth session of the Sino-Bulgarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee was held in Beijing from February 18 to 25, and an agreement was signed.

Zhang Ben, chairman of the Chinese delegation and representative of the State Science and Technology Commission, and Stoyan Shulemezov, chairman of the Bulgarian delegation and first deputy chairman of the State Planning Commission, signed the agreement.

Attending the signing ceremony were Wu Weizhen, representative of the State Science and Technology Commission, and Li Lianqing, deputy director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Kyril Stoichev, the Bulgarian ambassador to China, also attended the signing ceremony.

The Bulgarian scientific and technological delegation, after visiting and touring China, left Beijing today to return home.

### 中保签订科技合作议定书

1967-03-01
第6版()
专栏：

    中保签订科技合作议定书
    新华社二十七日讯　中华人民共和国和保加利亚人民共和国科学技术合作委员会第十届会议已于二月十八日至二十五日在北京举行，并签订了议定书。
    委员会中国组主席、国家科委代表张本和委员会保加利亚组主席、国家计委第一副主席斯·修列麦佐夫在议定书上签了字。
    参加签字仪式的，有国家科委代表吴为真，外交部苏欧司副司长李连庆。保加利亚驻中国大使克·斯托伊切夫也出席了签字仪式。
    保加利亚科技代表团在我国参观访问以后，已在今天离开北京回国。
