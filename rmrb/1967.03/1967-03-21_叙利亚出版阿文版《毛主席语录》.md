Title: Syria Publishes Arabic Version of Chairman Mao's Quotations
Author:
Date: 1967-03-21
Edition: 4

The long-awaited red book, "Chairman Mao's Quotations," in Arabic, was published here on March 20th, and has been warmly welcomed by the broad readership. Before this, many readers inquired at bookstores about when the Arabic version of "Chairman Mao's Quotations" would be published, and some even pre-ordered it, hoping to obtain this precious book for revolutionaries as soon as possible.

Translator Fa'ad told Xinhua News Agency reporter: "Mao Zedong Thought is a revolutionary weapon for the struggle of the Arab people. Mao Zedong Thought has developed Marxism-Leninism theory and is the most powerful weapon against revisionism." Reader Rikaab said: "The publication of 'Chairman Mao's Quotations' is helpful to the struggle of the Arab and world peoples." Reader Shamir said: "Chairman Mao is the leader of the oppressed peoples of the world. His thoughts belong not only to the Chinese people but also to the peoples of the world."

Reader Da'as said: "Chairman Mao's works illuminate our path like the sun shining on the earth."

### 叙利亚出版阿文版《毛主席语录》

1967-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚出版阿文版《毛主席语录》
    新华社大马士革二十日电　阿拉伯革命人民渴望已久的红书《毛主席语录》阿拉伯文版二十日在这里出版，受到广大读者的热烈欢迎。在此以前，许多读者到书店去问，阿文版《毛主席语录》什么时候出版，有的提前预订，希望早日得到这本革命人民的宝书。
    翻译法阿德对新华社记者说：“毛泽东思想是阿拉伯人民斗争的革命武器。毛泽东思想发展了马克思列宁主义理论，是反对修正主义的最强大的武器。”读者雷卡勃说：《毛主席语录》的出版“有助于阿拉伯和世界人民的斗争。”读者沙米尔说：“毛主席是世界被压迫人民的领袖。他的思想不仅是属于中国人民的，而且也是属于世界人民的。”
    读者达阿斯说：“毛主席著作象阳光普照大地一样，照亮我们的道路。”
