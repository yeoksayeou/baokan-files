Title: Chairman Mao Issues a Call, Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants Are Joyful
Author:
Date: 1967-03-05
Edition: 6

Chairman Mao issues the call,
The poor and lower-middle peasants are joyful.
Thousands of soldiers and horses mobilize,
Launching a great spring plowing campaign.
