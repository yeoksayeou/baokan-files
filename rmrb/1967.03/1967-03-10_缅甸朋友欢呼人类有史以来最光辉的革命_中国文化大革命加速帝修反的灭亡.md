Title: Burmese Friends Hail the Most Glorious Revolution in Human History
Author:
Date: 1967-03-10
Edition: 4

Burma's friends enthusiastically hail China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, pointing out that this great revolution will accelerate the downfall of imperialism, revisionism, and all reactionaries.

One Burmese friend exclaimed, "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China will sweep away all monsters and demons, further consolidating the dictatorship of the proletariat in China." He said, "The revolutionary people of the world regard the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China as part of their own cause and give it their full and heartfelt support."

A Burmese intellectual said, "Throughout human history, various revolutionary struggles have taken place. Among these, China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is the largest and most glorious revolutionary struggle. The Cultural Revolution has ushered in a new era in human history. Its great victory will shake the world and greatly promote the revolutionary movement of the people of the world, accelerating the downfall of imperialism, revisionism, and all reactionaries."

Another Burmese friend said, "The Cultural Revolution will ensure that China—the base of world revolution and the pillar of the anti-imperialist struggle—will forever advance along the revolutionary path. Therefore, the Cultural Revolution has great significance for China and for the revolutionary cause of the people of the world."

### 缅甸朋友欢呼人类有史以来最光辉的革命  中国文化大革命加速帝修反的灭亡

1967-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸朋友欢呼人类有史以来最光辉的革命
    中国文化大革命加速帝修反的灭亡
    据新华社仰光电　缅甸朋友热烈欢呼中国无产阶级文化大革命，指出这场伟大的革命加速了帝国主义、修正主义和一切反动派的灭亡。
    一位缅甸朋友欢呼，中国无产阶级文化大革命将铲除一切牛鬼蛇神，进一步巩固中国的无产阶级专政。他说，世界革命人民把中国无产阶级文化大革命看作是他们自己的事业的一部分，并给予完全的和衷心的支持。
    一位缅甸知识界的人士说，人类有史以来，进行了各种各样的革命斗争，在这些革命中，中国的无产阶级文化大革命是规模最大，最光辉的伟大的革命斗争。文化大革命使人类历史进入一个崭新的阶段，它的伟大胜利将震撼全世界，大大促进世界人民的革命运动，加速帝国主义、修正主义和一切反动派的灭亡。
    另一位缅甸朋友说，文化大革命将使中国——世界革命的基地和反帝斗争的支柱——永远沿着革命道路前进，因此，文化大革命对中国，对世界人民的革命事业都具有重大的意义。
