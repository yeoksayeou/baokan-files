Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-03-02
Edition: 1

	Only when the enthusiasm of the leading cadres is combined with the enthusiasm of the broad masses can we avoid the futile efforts of a few. However, if there is only the enthusiasm of the broad masses without a powerful leading cadre to properly organize the enthusiasm of the masses, then the enthusiasm of the masses will neither be sustained nor will it move in the correct direction or reach a higher level.

		From "Some Questions on the Method of Leadership"

### 毛主席语录

1967-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    只有领导骨干的积极性，而无广大群众的积极性相结合，便将成为少数人的空忙。但如果只有广大群众的积极性，而无有力的领导骨干去恰当地组织群众的积极性，则群众积极性既不可能持久，也不可能走向正确的方向和提到高级的程度。
             《关于领导方法的若干问题》
