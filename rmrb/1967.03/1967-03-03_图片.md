Title: Chairman of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninists) E.F. Hill and His Wife Visit the New Peking University Cultural Revolution Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1967-03-03
Edition: 5

    Chairman of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninists) E.F. Hill and his wife visited the New Peking University Cultural Revolution Exhibition. Comrade Hill expressed his support and praise for the revolutionary spirit of rebellion among the revolutionary teachers and students of New Peking University.
