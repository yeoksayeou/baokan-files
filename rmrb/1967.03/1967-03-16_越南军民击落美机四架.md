Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Four American Planes
Author:
Date: 1967-03-16
Edition: 6

Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down four American planes

	New Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 15th – According to reports from here: On the 8th, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province in North Vietnam shot down one American aggressor plane. On the 11th, they also damaged one American warship. On the 13th, the military and civilians of Quang Ninh Province also shot down one American aggressor plane.

	According to a report from New Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 12th – According to reports from here: On the 12th, the American imperialists dispatched planes to invade the airspace of Peace and Son La Provinces in North Vietnam. The local military and civilians shot down one enemy plane each before the aggressor planes could cause any harm.

### 越南军民击落美机四架

1967-03-16
第6版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美机四架
    新华社河内十五日电　据这里公布：越南北方义安省军民八日击落美国强盗飞机一架，十一日击伤美国军舰一艘。广宁省军民十三日也击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    据新华社河内十二日电　据这里公布：美帝国主义十二日派遣飞机侵犯越南北方和平、山萝两省上空，当地军民在强盗飞机还未来得及逞凶时就各击落敌机一架。
