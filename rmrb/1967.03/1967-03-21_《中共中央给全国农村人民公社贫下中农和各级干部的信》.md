Title: Message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants and Cadres at All Levels in Rural People's Communes Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1967-03-21
Edition: 1

The poor and lower-middle peasants are the main force for carrying out the revolution and promoting production in rural areas. As spring plowing begins, Chairman Mao calls on you, and the Party Central Committee calls on you, to seriously carry out the revolution, promote production, mobilize all forces, and immediately work hard to ensure a good spring plowing and production.

### 《中共中央给全国农村人民公社贫下中农和各级干部的信》

1967-03-21
