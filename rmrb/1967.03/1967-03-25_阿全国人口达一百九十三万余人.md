Title: Albania's Population Reaches Over 1.93 Million
Author:
Date: 1967-03-25
Edition: 5

Albania's population has reached 1,938,500 by the end of 1966, according to statistics from the State Planning Commission's Statistical Bureau, as reported by the Albanian News Agency.

The report states that last year, Albania's population increased by 48,700, with a growth rate of 25.4 per thousand.

The report attributes the rapid population growth in the People's Republic of Albania to the continuous improvement in the people's living standards, the Party and government's great attention to public health, and the continuous increase in birth rates and significant decrease in mortality rates.

### 阿全国人口达一百九十三万余人

1967-03-25
第5版()
专栏：欧洲的社会主义明灯

    阿全国人口达一百九十三万余人
    新华社地拉那二十四日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社引国家计划委员会统计局的材料报道：截至一九六六年底，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的人口已达一百九十三万八千五百人。
    报道说，去年，阿尔巴尼亚全国人口共增加四万八千七百人，人口增长率为千分之二十五点四。
    报道说，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人口之所以增长这样快，是由于人民物质福利条件的日益提高，党和政府对于人民保健事业的巨大关怀，以及出生率的不断提高和死亡率的显著降低。
