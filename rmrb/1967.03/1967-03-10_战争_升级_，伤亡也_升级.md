Title: Escalation of War, Escalation of Casualties
Author:
Date: 1967-03-10
Edition: 5

The escalation of war leads to an escalation of casualties. In an attempt to salvage its failing aggression in Vietnam, American imperialism, like a desperate gambler, is frantically sending more troops to South Vietnam and intensifying the war, hoping to find a way out. However, the cruel reality is: the more American aggressors send troops to South Vietnam, the greater the casualties among American invasion forces.

Every time the U.S. escalates the war in Vietnam, its failures and casualties increase proportionally. This has become a direct correlation. The following statistics are the best evidence. (See attached image)
