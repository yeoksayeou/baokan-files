Title: The Book "Resolutely Implementing the Great Policy of 'Grasp the Revolution, Promote Production' in Rural Areas" Published
Author:
Date: 1967-03-22
Edition: 2

The book "Resolutely Implementing the Great Policy of 'Grasp the Revolution, Promote Production' in Rural Areas" has been published. The book includes "The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's Letter to All Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants and Cadres at All Levels in Rural People's Communes Across the Country," as well as the editorial article "Defend the Great Achievements of the Four Cleanups Movement" from the magazine "Red Flag," "Responding to the Call of Chairman Mao and the Central Committee of the Party, Launch a High Tide of Spring Ploughing and Production" from the "People's Daily," the editorial "Establish the Frontline Command for Grasping the Revolution and Promoting Production," "Production Brigades and Teams Should Not Seize Power During the Spring Ploughing Period," and the editorial "Fully Utilize the Role of Militia in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution" from the "Liberation Army Daily."

This book will be distributed in Beijing starting March 22 and will be distributed nationwide in the coming days.

### 《在农村中坚决贯彻“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针》一书出版

1967-03-22
第2版()
专栏：

    《在农村中坚决贯彻“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针》一书出版
    新华社二十一日讯　《中共中央给全国农村人民公社贫下中农和各级干部的信》，以及《红旗》杂志评论员文章《保卫四清运动的伟大成果》、《响应毛主席、党中央的号召，掀起春耕生产的高潮》，《人民日报》社论《建立抓革命促生产第一线指挥部》、《春耕期间生产大队和生产队不夺权》和《解放军报》社论《充分发挥民兵在无产阶级文化大革命中的作用》，已由人民出版社编成《在农村中坚决贯彻“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针》一书出版。
    这本书于三月二十二日起在北京发行，并将在全国各地陆续发行。
