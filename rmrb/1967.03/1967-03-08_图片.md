Title: Chairman Mao and the Central Committee's Letter to the Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants and Revolutionary Cadres of Xizhang Village, Zhuxiang Production Brigade, Taishan Commune, Shandong Province
Author:
Date: 1967-03-08
Edition: 6

Chairman Mao and the Central Committee's letter to the poor and lower-middle peasants and revolutionary cadres of Xizhang Village, Zhuxiang Production Brigade, Taishan Commune, Shandong Province has pointed out the direction for their advance and added boundless strength. Now, the revolutionary peasants of this production brigade are actively engaged in the spring plowing battle. This shows people deep plowing the land, creating conditions for a bumper harvest.
