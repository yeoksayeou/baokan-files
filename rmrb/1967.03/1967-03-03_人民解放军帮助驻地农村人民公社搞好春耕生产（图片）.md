Author:
Date: 1967-03-03
Edition: 6

The People's Liberation Army Assists Local People's Communes with Spring Plowing

The Qingdao-based land forces held a rally titled "Firmly Respond to Chairman Mao's Great Call, Greatly Support and Assist Spring Plowing Production." This is the rally venue.

		_Provided by Qingdao Daily_

The soldiers and officers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Laoshan County, Shandong, use their rest time in the fields to study with the commune members the editorial from the fourth issue of 1967 of the *Red Flag* magazine, "We Must Correctly Handle Cadres," and other documents.

		_Provided by Qingdao Daily_

In supporting agricultural production, cadres, soldiers, and revolutionary masses work side by side. This is a soldier from a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army pushing a cart with commune members to transport soil. 	_Photographed by Bao Daozhu_

Cadres and soldiers from a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army go deep into the local villages, using various forms to widely propagate the "Letter from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to All Poor and Lower-Middle Peasant Members of People's Communes and All Levels of Cadres in Rural Areas" to the broad masses of poor and lower-middle peasants. 	_Photographed by Xiang Dongbing, War Cry_

A unit of the Jinan-based forces goes deep into the countryside to assist with spring plowing, sweating alongside the poor and lower-middle peasants, and firing the first shot for spring plowing. 	_Photographed by Lu Hongbing_

A unit of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Hebei Province responds to Chairman Mao's great call of "Grasp Revolution, Promote Production," goes deep into the countryside, and works with the broad masses of commune members to vigorously carry out spring plowing production. This is them working with commune members to improve the land. 	_Photographed by Jiang Bo, Wang Xiao_

The People's Liberation Army is a fighting force, a work force, and a production force. This is a cadre and soldier from a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army propagating Mao Zedong Thought while working with commune members, helping the production team carry sand to improve the soil. 	_Photographed by Xu Xiang_

### 人民解放军帮助驻地农村人民公社搞好春耕生产（图片）

1967-03-03
第6版()
专栏：

    人民解放军帮助驻地农村人民公社搞好春耕生产
    驻青岛陆军部队召开了“坚决响应毛主席的伟大号召，大力支持帮助春耕生产誓师大会”。这是大会会场。
                                《青岛日报》供稿
    驻山东崂山县人民解放军指战员们，利用地头休息时间和社员们一起学习《红旗》杂志一九六七年第四期社论《必须正确地对待干部》等文件。
                                《青岛日报》供稿
    在支援农业生产中，干部战士和革命群众并肩劳动。这是人民解放军某部战士和社员一起推车运土。　鲍道铸摄人民解放军某部干部战士深入驻地农村，通过各种形式向广大贫下中农广泛宣传《中共中央给全国农村人民公社贫下中农和各级干部的信》。　向东兵　战号摄
    济南部队某部深入农村帮助春耕，和贫下中农一起挥汗奋战，打响春耕第一炮。             鲁红兵摄
    驻河北省解放军某部，响应毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大号召，深入农村，和广大社员一起大搞春耕生产。这是他们和社员在一块修整土地。      江波　王孝摄
    人民解放军是一支战斗队、工作队和生产队。这是解放军某部干部战士一面宣传毛泽东思想，一面和社员一起劳动，帮助生产队担沙子改良土壤。                徐相摄
