Title: Zimbabwe African National Union Calls for People to Join Armed Struggle
Author:
Date: 1967-03-15
Edition: 6

Zimbabwe African National Union Calls for People to Join Armed Struggle
	Propagandize and Mobilize the Masses to Strike at the Colonialists

	According to a report from Cairo by Xinhua News Agency, the recent issue of the Zimbabwe African National Union's official newspaper "Today's Zimbabwe" published an article calling on all African people in Zimbabwe to join the armed struggle and to support the freedom fighters in every possible way to bring about a new situation in Zimbabwe's armed struggle in 1967.

	The article calls for the propagation of armed struggle among the masses to mobilize the broad masses to fight against the colonial rule of Smith. The article says that in 1966, under the leadership of the Zimbabwe African National Union, the armed struggle of the Zimbabwean people against British colonial rule achieved great success. Over the past year, Zimbabwean freedom fighters have annihilated more than 200 Smith colonial soldiers and police.

### 津巴布韦非洲民族联盟号召人民参加武装斗争  展开宣传　动员群众　打击殖民者

1967-03-15
第6版()
专栏：

    津巴布韦非洲民族联盟号召人民参加武装斗争
    展开宣传　动员群众　打击殖民者
    据新华社开罗电　津巴布韦非洲民族联盟机关报《今日的津巴布韦》最近一期发表文章，号召津巴布韦全体非洲人民参加武装斗争，尽一切办法支援自由战士，使津巴布韦的武装斗争在一九六七年出现新的局面。
    文章要求在群众中展开关于武装斗争的宣传工作，以便动员广大群众为反对史密斯殖民政权而奋斗。文章说，在一九六六年，津巴布韦人民进行的反对英国殖民主义统治的武装斗争，在津巴布韦非洲民族联盟的领导下，取得重大成就。过去一年中，津巴布韦自由战士已歼灭了二百多名史密斯殖民军警。
