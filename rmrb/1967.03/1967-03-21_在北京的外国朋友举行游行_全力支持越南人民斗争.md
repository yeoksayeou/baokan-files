Title: Foreign Friends in Beijing Hold a Parade to Fully Support the Struggle of the Vietnamese People
Author:
Date: 1967-03-21
Edition: 5

Foreign friends working in Beijing from 18 countries today held a parade in front of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic's embassy in China and the residence of the Vietnam South National Liberation Front's permanent representative in China. They also read out a "Letter to the Vietnamese People."

The letter strongly condemns American imperialism's policy of escalating the war in Vietnam. It also strongly condemns the revisionist leadership of the Soviet Union for collaborating with the common enemy of the world's people, American imperialism, to promote the "peace talks" deception and betray the revolutionary struggle of the Vietnamese people.

The letter points out that the Vietnamese issue can only be resolved on the basis of the four-point proposal of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic and the five-point declaration of the Vietnam South National Liberation Front, the sole genuine representative of the people of South Vietnam. The letter expresses that foreign workers from five continents in Beijing fully support the just struggle of the Vietnamese people to drive out the American aggressors from Vietnam.

More than 80 foreign friends participated in the parade. They chanted slogans during the parade: "American imperialism must get out of Vietnam!" "Down with American imperialism!" "Smash the 'peace talks' conspiracy of American imperialism and Soviet revisionism!" "Vietnam will win, the U.S. will lose!"

### 在北京的外国朋友举行游行  全力支持越南人民斗争

1967-03-21
第5版()
专栏：

    在北京的外国朋友举行游行
    全力支持越南人民斗争
    据新华社十九日讯　在北京工作的包括有十八个国家的国际友人，今天到越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆和越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团住地门前举行游行，并宣读《致越南人民信》。
    信中强烈谴责美帝国主义在越南推行战争“升级”政策；强烈谴责苏修领导集团配合世界人民的共同敌人美帝国主义大搞“和谈”骗局，背叛越南人民的革命斗争事业。
    信中指出，越南问题只能在越南民主共和国四项主张的基础上，在越南南方真正唯一代表越南南方民族解放阵线五点声明的基础上解决。信中表示，在北京的来自五大洲的外籍工作人员，全力支持越南人民的正义斗争，把美国侵略者赶出越南。
    有八十多位外国朋友参加了游行。他们在游行中不断高呼口号：“美帝国主义必须从越南滚出去！”“打倒美帝国主义！”“粉碎美帝国主义和苏修的‘和谈’阴谋！”“越南必胜，美帝必败！”
