Title: Chairman Mao's Quotations
Author:
Date: 1967-03-03
Edition: 1

	The world is yours, it is also ours, but in the final analysis, it is yours. You young people are in the prime of life, like the sun at eight or nine in the morning. Our hopes are placed on you.

		From a talk with Chinese students and trainees in Moscow.

### 毛主席语录

1967-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    世界是你们的，也是我们的，但是归根结底是你们的。你们青年人朝气蓬勃，正在兴旺时期，好象早晨八、九点钟的太阳。希望寄托在你们身上。
                     在莫斯科会见我国留学生和实习生时的谈话
