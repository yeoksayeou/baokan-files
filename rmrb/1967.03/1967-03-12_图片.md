Title: Soldiers Support Local Production Teams in Terraced Farming
Author: Zhang Youlin
Date: 1967-03-12
Edition: 3

Soldiers from the logistics department of a certain unit in the Luda Garrison District have resolutely responded to Chairman Mao's great call. They are actively supporting the local production brigades in transforming hillside land, building terraced fields, and expanding the area under cultivation. They strive to achieve a bumper harvest this year.
