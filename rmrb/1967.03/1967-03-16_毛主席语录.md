Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-03-16
Edition: 1

We should go among the masses, learn from them, and synthesize their experiences into better, more systematic principles and methods. Then we should tell the masses (propagate) and mobilize them to put these into practice, solve the problems of the masses, and bring about their liberation and happiness.

		*Organize*

### 毛主席语录

1967-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    我们应该走到群众中间去，向群众学习，把他们的经验综合起来，成为更好的有条理的道理和办法，然后再告诉群众（宣传），并号召群众实行起来，解决群众的问题，使群众得到解放和幸福。
                                             《组织起来》
