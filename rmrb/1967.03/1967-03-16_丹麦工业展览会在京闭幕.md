Title: The Danish Industrial Exhibition Closes in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-03-16
Edition: 5

The Danish Industrial Exhibition, organized by the Danish Government's Overseas Exhibition Committee in Beijing, concluded today.

During the exhibition period, technical and trade personnel from both China and Denmark engaged in friendly exchanges to share technological experiences and discuss trade opportunities. These interactions have made a beneficial contribution to furthering mutual understanding and friendship between the peoples of China and Denmark.

### 丹麦工业展览会在京闭幕

1967-03-16
第5版()
专栏：

    丹麦工业展览会在京闭幕
    新华社十五日讯　由丹麦政府国外展览委员会在北京举办的丹麦工业展览会，已在今天闭幕。
    展览会在展出期间，中丹双方的科技、贸易人员，为交流技术经验和洽谈贸易，进行了友好的接触，对进一步增进中丹两国人民之间的相互了解和友谊作出了有益的贡献。
