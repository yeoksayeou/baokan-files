Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-03-28
Edition: 1

	Workers, peasants, and young people, as well as young people in the army, are brave, active, and disciplined. Without them, the cause of revolution and construction would not be victorious.

	_From the Directive on the Direction of the Work of the Communist Youth League_
