Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1967-03-22
Edition: 6

According to a report from here: On the 17th, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft and severely damaged one U.S. warship.

It is also reported that on the 15th, the military and civilians of the Vinh Yen area shot down one invading U.S. jet aircraft.

### 越南北方军民击落美机两架

1967-03-22
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机两架
    新华社河内二十日电　据这里公布：越南北方广平省军民，十七日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架和严重击伤美国军舰一艘。
    另据公布，永灵区军民十五日击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架。
