Title: British Labour Government Faces Deep Financial Crisis
Author:
Date: 1967-03-01
Edition: 6

The British Labour Party government, which follows the lead of American imperialism, has fallen into a deep financial crisis. Workers' strikes and protests are becoming increasingly frequent. The image shows workers organizing and demonstrating outside the conference hall during the British Labour Party's annual meeting in Brighton.
