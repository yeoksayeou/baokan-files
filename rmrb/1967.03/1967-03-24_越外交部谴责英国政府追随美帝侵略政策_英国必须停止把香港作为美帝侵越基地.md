Title: Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Condemns British Government for Following US Aggression Policies
Author:
Date: 1967-03-24
Edition: 4

The Vietnamese Foreign Ministry spokesman strongly condemned the British government for allowing the US to use Hong Kong as a base for its aggression against Vietnam. The spokesman pointed out that, at a time when the US is frantically escalating its war of aggression against South Vietnam and intensifying its new war of aggression against North Vietnam, the aforementioned actions by the British government pose a more serious threat to the peace and security of the peoples of Indochina and Asia.

The spokesman demanded that the British government must stop providing Hong Kong as a base for the US to wage its war of aggression against Vietnam. The spokesman said, if the British government continues to follow the US policy of aggression, then the British government must bear full responsibility for all the serious consequences arising from its actions.

### 越外交部谴责英国政府追随美帝侵略政策  英国必须停止把香港作为美帝侵越基地

1967-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    越外交部谴责英国政府追随美帝侵略政策
    英国必须停止把香港作为美帝侵越基地
    据新华社河内二十三日电　越南民主共和国外交部发言人二十三日强烈谴责英国政府纵容美帝利用香港作为美国侵越战争的活动基地。发言人指出，当美帝疯狂地扩大侵略越南南方的战争和加紧对越南北方进行新的战争升级的时候，英国政府的上述行动，更加严重地威胁着印度支那和亚洲各民族的和平与安全。发言人要求英国政府，必须停止继续把香港提供给美国作为侵越战争的活动基地。发言人说，如果英国政府继续追随美帝的侵略政策，那么，英国政府必须对由它的这种行动所引起的一切严重后果承担全部责任。
