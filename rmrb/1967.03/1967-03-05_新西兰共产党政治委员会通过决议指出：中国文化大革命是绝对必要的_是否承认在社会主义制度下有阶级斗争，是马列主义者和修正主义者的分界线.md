Title: The Political Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party Adopts a Resolution Stating: The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China Is Absolutely Necessary
Author:
Date: 1967-03-05
Edition: 4

The Political Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party has adopted a resolution enthusiastically hailing the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China as excellent. The resolution emphasizes that this great revolution "has world significance" and is "an absolutely necessary movement to prevent the restoration of capitalism." The resolution was published in the February 28 issue of the party's organ, the weekly newspaper "People's Voice."

The resolution states: "The Cultural Revolution is being carried out under the banner of Mao Zedong Thought and is personally led by Chairman Mao. This revolution is accomplishing a great historical task, that is, ensuring the victory of socialist ideology, culture, customs, and habits over the ideology, culture, customs, and habits of the exploiting classes."

The resolution states: "The task of thoroughly changing the world outlook of 700 million Chinese people requires a deep and prolonged struggle of the masses. Without such a movement to change the people's world outlook, it is impossible to guarantee the victory of the socialist road. This is a lesson that every true socialist has learned from the negative example of the Soviet revisionists."

The resolution points out that one of Mao Zedong's greatest achievements is that he has restored and deepened the Marxist-Leninist theory of class and class struggle, "and has shown the necessity of applying this theory in the current era of the collapse of imperialism and the transition of the whole world to socialism."

The resolution states: "Whether or not one acknowledges the existence of class struggle under the socialist system is one of the decisive dividing lines between Marxist-Leninists and revisionists. Failure to see the class struggle under the socialist system will open the door to revisionism and the restoration of capitalism due to internal degeneration, as has happened in the Soviet Union and in other countries under revisionist leadership."

The resolution further emphasizes: "The Cultural Revolution has a great influence on the present and the future. Because the Cultural Revolution ensures that capitalism will never be restored in China, it points the way forward for the people of other socialist countries. Therefore, imperialists and revisionists do not conceal their hatred for the success of the Cultural Revolution. They spare no effort to slander China and Chairman Mao, and to defame the Cultural Revolution.

"However, all true socialists and revolutionary people around the world firmly support China, support Chairman Mao, and support the Cultural Revolution. Because the struggle in China to achieve the victory of socialist ideology over capitalist ideology is an important part of the struggle of the people of the world against imperialism and its running dog, revisionism.

"Therefore, the victory of the Cultural Revolution is the victory of all those who cherish socialism, the victory of all the people who struggle for socialism, for national liberation, and against imperialism."

The resolution concludes: "The New Zealand Communist Party is convinced that the development of the Cultural Revolution will lead to further great advances in China's production, technology, and science; because through the current struggle, the minds of hundreds of millions of people are changing.

"This in turn will further strengthen the struggle against imperialism internationally and the revolutionary and socialist tide in the world.

"However hard the imperialists and modern revisionists try, they cannot stop this tide. History is moving towards socialism, and the Cultural Revolution in China is giving it a great new impetus."

### 新西兰共产党政治委员会通过决议指出：中国文化大革命是绝对必要的  是否承认在社会主义制度下有阶级斗争，是马列主义者和修正主义者的分界线

1967-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    新西兰共产党政治委员会通过决议指出：
    中国文化大革命是绝对必要的
    是否承认在社会主义制度下有阶级斗争，是马列主义者和修正主义者的分界线
    新华社惠灵顿三日电　新西兰共产党政治委员会通过一项决议，热烈欢呼中国的无产阶级文化大革命好得很。决议强调指出，这场大革命“具有世界意义”，它“是防止资本主义复辟的一次绝对必要的运动”。这个决议刊登在二月二十八日出版的一期新共机关报《人民之声》周报上。
    决议说：“文化革命是在毛泽东思想的旗帜下进行的，是由毛主席亲自领导的。这场革命正在完成伟大的历史任务，即保证使社会主义的思想、文化、风俗、习惯战胜剥削阶级的思想、文化、风俗、习惯。”
    决议说：“彻底改变七亿中国人民的整个世界观的任务需要进行深入而长期的群众斗争。不进行这样一次改变人民世界观的运动，就不能保证社会主义道路的胜利。这是苏联修正主义者用反面例子给予每一个真正的社会主义者的一个教训。”
    决议指出，毛泽东同志的最伟大成就之一，在于他恢复和深化了马克思列宁主义关于阶级和阶级斗争的学说，“并表明了在当前帝国主义崩溃、全世界向社会主义过渡的时代实际运用这个学说的必要性”。
    决议说：“是否承认在社会主义制度下有阶级斗争，这是马克思列宁主义者和修正主义者之间决定性的分界线之一。看不到在社会主义制度下有阶级斗争，就会由于内部蜕化而向修正主义和资本主义复辟敞开大门，象在苏联和在修正主义领导下的其它一些国家所发生的那样。”
    决议接着强调：“文化革命对现时代和将来都有巨大的影响。由于文化革命使资本主义永远不可能在中国复辟，从而向其他社会主义国家的人民指出了前进的道路，所以帝国主义者和修正主义者毫不掩饰他们对文化革命成功的仇恨。他们不遗余力地诬蔑中国和毛主席，诋毁文化革命。
    “但是，全世界一切真正的社会主义者和革命人民坚决支持中国，支持毛主席，支持文化革命。因为，在中国开展的争取社会主义意识形态战胜资本主义意识形态的斗争，是全世界人民反对帝国主义及其仆从修正主义的具有历史意义的斗争的一部分。
      “因此，文化革命的胜利，也就是所有珍视社会主义的人们的胜利，就是所有为社会主义、为民族解放和反对帝国主义而斗争的人民的胜利。”
    决议最后说：“新西兰共产党深信，文化革命的发展正导致中国在生产、技术和科学领域内取得进一步巨大的新进展；因为通过当前的斗争，亿万人民的思想正在发生变化。
      “这反过来又将进一步加强国际上反对帝国主义的斗争和全世界革命和社会主义的潮流。
    “帝国主义者和现代修正主义者无论怎样卖力，都阻挡不了这种潮流，历史正朝着社会主义的方向前进，中国的文化革命正在给予它巨大的新的推动。”
