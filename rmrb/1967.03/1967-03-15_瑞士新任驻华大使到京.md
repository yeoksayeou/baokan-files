Title: New Swiss Ambassador Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-03-15
Edition: 5

The new Swiss Federal Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China, Oscar Rossetti, arrived in Beijing by airplane this evening.

### 瑞士新任驻华大使到京

1967-03-15
第5版()
专栏：

    瑞士新任驻华大使到京
    新华社十四日讯　瑞士联邦新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使奥斯卡·罗赛蒂，今晚乘飞机到北京。
