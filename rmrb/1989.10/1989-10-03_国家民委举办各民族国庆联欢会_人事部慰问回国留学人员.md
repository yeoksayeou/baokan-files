Title: State Ethnic Affairs Commission Hosts National Day Gala for All Ethnic Groups
Author:
Date: 1989-10-03
Edition: 2

The State Ethnic Affairs Commission hosted a gala celebrating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, with over 1,000 representatives from ethnic minorities gathering to celebrate the occasion.

This afternoon, the banquet hall of the Beijing Hotel was brightly lit and filled with lively songs. Representatives from 56 ethnic groups joyfully chatted, sharing their ethnic bonds and praising the great achievements of the motherland over the past 40 years. They also celebrated the advanced deeds of various ethnic groups in contributing to the Four Modernizations. Hab Comin, Peng Chong, Ngapo Ngawang Jigme, Liao Hansheng, Ni Zhifu, Chen Junsheng, and Sima Yi Aimaidi attended the gala.

Sima Yi Aimaidi, the head of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, spoke at the event. He said, "At this wonderful moment, ethnic children from all over the country have gathered in Beijing to celebrate the festival together, sharing in the victory. We are immensely proud and excited about the splendid achievements of our motherland. We firmly believe that through the joint efforts of all ethnic peoples and in the face of new historical opportunities, our great motherland will become even more prosperous and strong, and all ethnic groups in China will thrive."

Attendees also included minority comrades attending the National Labor Model and Advanced Worker Award Conference, members of the National Ethnic Minority National Day Visiting Group, and representatives of minority artists from 56 ethnic groups.

Beijing, October 2 (Reported by Yan Dong and Jiang Jianke) The Ministry of Personnel held a cultural and entertainment evening for returned overseas students at the Beijing International Hotel today. Over 210 returned overseas students from various regions and industries across the country were invited to attend.

It is reported that over the past 40 years, China has sent more than 70,000 students to 76 countries and regions, with over 40,000 already returned. They have made significant contributions to international scientific and cultural exchanges and to tracking the latest global technological developments. The representatives attending the evening event were selected from various regions based on recommendations. Among them are individuals who overcame numerous difficulties and returned to China after the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riot in Beijing, internationally recognized young academic leaders, recipients of the national May 1st Labor Medal, and the majority are those who were sent abroad and returned in recent years.

### 国家民委举办各民族国庆联欢会  人事部慰问回国留学人员

1989-10-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国家民委举办各民族国庆联欢会
    人事部慰问回国留学人员
    据新华社北京10月2日电　（记者格来）国家民委举办庆祝建国40周年各民族联欢会，1000多少数民族代表欢聚一堂共庆佳节。
    今天下午北京饭店宴会厅，灯火辉煌，歌声嘹亮，56个民族的代表欢声笑语共叙民族情谊，赞颂伟大祖国建国40年来的巨大成就、各族人民团结进步为四化建设做贡献的先进事迹。习仲勋、彭冲、阿沛·阿旺晋美、廖汉生、倪志福、陈俊生、司马义·艾买提等出席了联欢会。
    国家民委主任司马义·艾买提在会上讲了话。他说，在这个美好的时刻，来自全国各地的各民族儿女，欢聚在首都北京，共度节日，同庆胜利，为祖国所取得的辉煌成就感到无比自豪和欢欣鼓舞。我们坚信，在各族人民的共同奋斗之中，在新的历史机会面前，我们伟大的祖国必将更加繁荣富强，我国各民族必将更加兴旺发达。
    参加联欢会的还有出席全国劳动模范和先进工作者表彰大会的少数民族同志、全国少数民族国庆参观团成员、56个民族的少数民族艺术家代表。
    本报北京10月2日讯　记者雁冬、蒋建科报道：人事部今天在北京国际饭店举行回国留学人员文艺联欢晚会，来自全国各地各行业的210多名回国留学人员应邀参加。
    据介绍，40年来，我国先后向76个国家和地区派遣留学人员7万多人，目前已有4万多人学成回国。他们立足我国现有条件，艰苦奋斗，勤俭创业，在开展国际科技文化交流、追踪世界最新科技发展方面发挥了重要作用。参加文艺晚会的代表是在各地推荐的基础上选出的，他们中有排除重重困难、在平息北京反革命暴乱后回国的人员，有国际公认的年轻的学科带头人，有全国“五一”劳动奖章获得者，大多数是近几年派出和回国的。
