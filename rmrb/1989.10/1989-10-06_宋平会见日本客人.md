Title: Song Ping Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 5th.

Song Ping, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, met with Mr. Kazuo Kimura, Chairman of the Japan-China Economic and Trade Center, in the Great Hall of the People this afternoon.

The two sides had a friendly conversation.

Kimura is in China at the invitation of the China International Trust and Investment Corporation to attend a seminar on Sino-foreign economic cooperation.

### 宋平会见日本客人

1989-10-06
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    宋平会见日本客人
    新华社北京10月5日电　中共中央政治局常委宋平今天下午在人民大会堂会见了日本日中经济贸易中心会长木村一三。
    宾主进行了友好的谈话。
    木村一三是应中国国际投资信托公司的邀请前来参加中外经济合作研讨会的。
