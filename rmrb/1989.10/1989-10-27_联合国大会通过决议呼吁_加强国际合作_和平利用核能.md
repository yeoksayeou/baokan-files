Title: United Nations General Assembly Passes Resolution Calling for Strengthened International Cooperation and Peaceful Use of Nuclear Energy
Author: Liú Kāichén
Date: 1989-10-27
Edition: 3

The United Nations General Assembly today unanimously adopted a resolution calling on countries around the world to strengthen effective international cooperation to promote the peaceful use of nuclear energy.

The Assembly urged relevant countries to take necessary measures to further enhance the safety of nuclear facilities and minimize risks to life, health, and the environment.

The Assembly also urged the International Atomic Energy Agency to strengthen technical assistance to developing countries, enabling these countries to promote economic development through the peaceful use of nuclear technology and nuclear energy.

The Assembly further urged relevant countries to ensure the strict implementation of the safeguards system of the Atomic Energy Agency, ensuring that nuclear materials under safeguards are not used for military purposes.

According to a report submitted by the Atomic Energy Agency to the Assembly, in 1987, there were 417 nuclear power plants worldwide, generating more than 16% of the world's total electricity. In 1988, 14 new nuclear power plants were added, increasing nuclear power generation capacity by 4.3%.

### 联合国大会通过决议呼吁  加强国际合作　和平利用核能
刘开宸
1989-10-27
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　联合国大会通过决议呼吁
　　加强国际合作　和平利用核能
    本报联合国10月25日电  记者刘开宸报道：第44届联合国大会今天一致通过一项决议，呼吁世界各国加强有效的国际合作，促进核能的和平利用。
    大会要求有关国家采取必要措施，进一步加强核设施安全，尽量减少对生命、健康和环境的危险。
    大会还要求国际原子能机构加强向发展中国家提供技术援助，使这些国家通过和平利用核技术和核能促进经济发展。
    大会还要求有关国家确保原子能机构的保障制度的认真执行，凡接受保障的核材料，不得用于军事上。
    据原子能机构向大会提出的报告，1987年，世界上共有417座核电厂，生产电力占世界总电力的16％以上。1988年，新增加14个核电厂，核能发电能力增加4.3％。
