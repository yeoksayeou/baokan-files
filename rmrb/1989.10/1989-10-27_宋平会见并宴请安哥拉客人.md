Title: Song Ping Meets and Dines with Angolan Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 26th.

Song Ping, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Minister of the Organization Department, met and dined with a delegation from the Central Committee of the People's Movement for the Liberation of Angola - Labor Party, led by Central Committee member and Organization Department head Bornieto de Sousa Baltazar Diogo, at the Great Hall of the People this evening.

During the meeting, Song Ping praised the achievements of the Angolan people under the leadership of President dos Santos and the MPLA in domestic construction and the search for a political solution to the problems in southern Africa.

Both sides reviewed the traditional friendship between the two parties, the two countries, and their peoples, and expressed their commitment to promoting the development of friendly cooperative relations.

Song Ping introduced China's experience in party building and the multi-party cooperation system led by the Communist Party of China. He said that the leadership of the Communist Party of China and its current strong position are the result of long-term historical development, and that China's socialist modernization construction cannot be separated from the strong leadership of the Communist Party.

The delegation arrived in China on October 23 at the invitation of the Communist Party of China.

### 宋平会见并宴请安哥拉客人

1989-10-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　宋平会见并宴请安哥拉客人
    新华社北京十月二十六日电　中共中央政治局常委、中组部部长宋平今晚在人民大会堂会见并宴请了以中央委员、中央组织部长博尔尼托·德索萨·巴尔塔扎尔·迪奥戈为团长的安哥拉人民解放运动——劳动党中央组织部代表团。
    会见时，宋平赞扬安哥拉人民在多斯桑托斯总统和人运的领导下，在国内建设和寻求政治解决南部非洲问题方面所取得的成就。
    双方回顾了两党、两国和两国人民的传统友谊，并表示将推进这种友好合作关系的发展。
    宋平向客人们介绍了中国共产党党的建设的经验和中国共产党领导的多党合作制。他说，中国共产党的领导及其现在的坚强地位是长期历史形成的，中国进行社会主义现代化建设，离不开共产党的坚强领导。
    代表团应中国共产党的邀请于本月二十三日来访。
