Title: Israel Principally Accepts Baker Plan
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-10-25
Edition: 3

Israel Principally Accepts Baker Plan

CAIRO, Oct. 24 (Xinhua) — The Israeli cabinet today expressed its principal acceptance of U.S. Secretary of State Baker's five-point proposal for direct Israeli-Palestinian dialogue.

Israeli Foreign Minister Arens sent a telegram to Secretary Baker today, expressing this stance, though the detailed contents have not yet been disclosed. Israeli radio reported that Arens, in the telegram, requested that the U.S. guarantee that the Palestinian delegation would not include members of the PLO, and that the dialogue would focus on the issue of elections in the occupied territories.

### 以色列原则接受贝克计划
林皎明
1989-10-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　以色列原则接受贝克计划
    本报开罗10月24日电　记者林皎明报道：据此间电台今天下午5时消息，以色列内阁今天表示原则接受美国国务卿贝克有关进行以巴直接对话的5点建议。
    以色列外长阿伦斯今天打电报给贝克国务卿表示上述立场，但详细内容尚未透露。以色列电台报道说，阿伦斯在电报中要求美国保证巴勒斯坦代表团成员中不包括巴解组织成员，对话集中讨论被占领土选举问题。
