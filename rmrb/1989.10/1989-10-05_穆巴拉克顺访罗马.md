Title: Mubarak Visits Rome
Author: Luo Jinbiao
Date: 1989-10-05
Edition: 3

Egyptian President Mubarak arrived in Rome yesterday for a brief visit. He held separate meetings with Italian President Cossiga and Prime Minister Andreotti. The discussions primarily focused on the Middle East peace issue.

### 穆巴拉克顺访罗马
罗晋标
1989-10-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    穆巴拉克顺访罗马
    本报罗马10月4日电　记者罗晋标报道：埃及总统穆巴拉克昨日抵达罗马作短暂访问，分别会见了意大利总统科西加和总理安德烈奥蒂，双方主要就中东和平问题进行了会谈。
