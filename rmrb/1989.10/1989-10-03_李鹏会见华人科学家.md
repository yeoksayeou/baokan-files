Title: Li Peng Meets with Overseas Chinese Scientists
Author:
Date: 1989-10-03
Edition: 1

Li Peng Meets with Overseas Chinese Scientists

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 2: Premier Li Peng met with 16 Chinese scientists and some of their spouses from the United States and Canada this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People. He had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

These scientists were invited by the State Science and Technology Commission to participate in the celebrations for the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. These professors and experts have long been dedicated to promoting scientific and technological exchanges and cooperation between China and the United States and Canada, and have contributed to enhancing friendship between the peoples.

The guests expressed that the broad masses of overseas Chinese and their descendants have a strong love for their homeland and are proud of the achievements made by New China over the past 40 years. They are also concerned about China's future development and are willing to contribute to China's modernization drive.

At the guests' request, Li Peng introduced China's domestic situation and answered some of their questions. The guests expressed their willingness to continue working to promote friendship between China and other countries. Those present at the meeting included Vice Premier and Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission Song Jian, and relevant officials Zhou Guangzhao, Gao Zhenning, and Zhu Lilan.

### 李鹏会见华人科学家

1989-10-03
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏会见华人科学家
    据新华社北京10月2日电　国务院总理李鹏今天下午在人民大会堂会见了来自美国和加拿大的16位华人科技专家和部分专家的夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    这些科学家应国家科委的邀请前来参加中华人民共和国成立40周年庆祝活动。这些教授和专家长期以来，为推进中美、中加科技交流和合作，为增进人民间的友谊，作出了贡献。
    客人们表示，广大华侨和华人热爱故土的心情是强烈的，对新中国40年来所取得的成就同样感到骄傲，对中国今后的发展前景也很关心，愿意为中国的四化建设尽一份力量。
    应客人们的要求，李鹏介绍了中国的国内情况，并回答了他们提出的一些问题。客人们表示将继续为促进中外友好而努力。会见时在座的有国务委员兼国家科委主任宋健和有关方面负责人周光召、高镇宁、朱丽兰等。
