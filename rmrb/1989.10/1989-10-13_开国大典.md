Title: The Founding Ceremony
Author: Lǐ Zìguó
Date: 1989-10-13
Edition: 8

    The Founding Ceremony
    Li Ziguo

    No matter how far history has traveled,
    That great moment
    Will forever be a memorial.
                   —Prologue

    When we return to our childhood,
    Gathered beneath the city gate, holding our breath, waiting,
    The sun reappears in the sky,
    Hovering above our heads, rising,
    The sea. Flames. Wind and cloud sails,
    Three hundred thousand heads
    Gathered in a place deeper than the ocean,
    Those arms waving with tears,
    The splendor of the founding fathers,
    That string of red stars, lanterns, cannons, and liberation shoes,
    Forty years have passed
    As if it were a night in childhood,
    Mother under the red flag,
    The legend of the sickle and hammer,
    Makes her gaze climb the twenty-two-meter-high pole,
    Our hearts
    Swell with your fluttering,
    Generation after generation gazes up,
    With the voices of fifty-six ethnic groups
    Calling out from both shores,
    The giant lantern
    Witnessed the endless night's return,
    Our seasonal sea
    Has risen,
    And the associations follow behind,
    A name in English, trampled,
    A land under the Stars and Stripes,
    The silent wheel shows the other side of the ocean,
    The setting sun hangs in the mango grove's waterfall,
    And I am the sailor of your tomorrow,
    When comforting your wounds, forgive me,
    For not being in the era to fight for you,
    The season we conquered
    Leaves behind this ship's song,
    How can sailing the sea be without a helmsman?!
    Tears wash out the great memorial,
    A billion pairs of eyes shoot towards the sky,
    The sky slowly raises its head,
    Overlooking the entire East,
    The entire East's coastline,
    To discover a fiery world,
    Moving between solid and liquid,
    Between melody and color,
    Between joy and pain,
    Between the Milky Way and the ocean,
    Between the seedlings and skin color,
    Between inspiration and wheels,
    Between bullets and lead clouds,
    Between high noses and blue eyes,
    The great forest rushes past the wilderness,
    Taking away the entire night,
    In the map of memory,
    The rooster crows,
    Listening to the call of the wilderness,
    The deck, widened day by day by gazes,
    Surges with waves and the captain's heart,
    The radiant shore! The lightning of thought,
    Illuminates another vast ocean within our bodies,
    Truth. Dawn. The discovery of man,
    Information. Challenge. The ever-changing future,
    New China, New China,
    On the mirror of rebirth,
    You let me place my purest face,
    That great relief sculpture of sorrow,
    Do you still recognize me? Your square,
    Is the place where my heart beats day and night,
    I have poured all my passion and blood
    Into your veins, your red walls, and your streets,
    Into every dream on your wheels,
    The rotating country, like a stage,
    Just like that,
    Remember the great man's sweeping hand,
    And I am the childhood
    That once shone brightly under the red building,
    That childhood with the five-pointed star.

### 开国大典
李自国
1989-10-13
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    开国大典
    李自国
    不管历史走了多远
    那个伟大的时刻
    都会成为永恒的纪念
                   ——题记
    当我们重新回到童年
    聚集在城楼下，屏息，等待
    太阳重现的天空
    停在我们头顶，升起
    大海。火苗。风和云帆
    三十万颗头颅
    攒动在比海洋还深的地带
    那双挥动热泪的手臂
    那些开国元勋的神采
    那串红星提灯，礼炮，解放鞋
    归来时，整整四十年
    仿佛童年的某个夜晚
    红旗下的母亲呵
    镰刀与铁锤的传说
    使她的目光爬上二十二米高竿
    我们的胸怀
    因你的飘扬而日益澎湃
    一代代人仰望
    有五十六个民族的声音
    在两岸呼喊
    巨大的灯笼
    目睹了不尽长夜的归宿之后
    我们季候的大海
    涨潮了
    而联想一直跟在后面
    一个被蹂躏的英文的名字
    一个被星条旗垂目的河山
    巨轮沉默给大洋彼岸看
    给落日挂入芒果林的瀑布看
    而我就是你明天的水手呀
    抚慰你的伤痕时，原宥我
    没赶上为你而战的年代
    我们征服的季节
    就留下这支船歌
    大海航行怎能没有舵手？！
    泪水把伟大的纪念冲洗出来
    十亿双瞳仁齐射向天空
    天空漫漫抬起头
    鸟瞰整个东方
    整个东方的海岸
    去发现一个火红的世界
    往返于块状与液态之间
    往返于旋律与色彩之间
    往返于欢乐与痛苦之间
    往返于银河与海洋之间
    往返于禾苗与肤色之间
    往返于灵感与车轮之间
    往返于流弹与铅云之间
    往返于高鼻子与蓝眼睛之间
    大森林从荒园急急驰过
    带走整个夜晚
    在记忆的版图里
    雄鸡引吭
    聆听旷野的呼唤
    被目光一天天拓宽的甲板呵
    涌入浪浪与船长的胸怀
    光辉的彼岸呵！思想的闪电
    照亮我们身躯里的另一座重洋
    真理。曙光。人的发现
    信息。挑战。千变万化的未来
    新中国，新中国呵
    在新生的镜面上
    你让我安放最纯真的容颜
    那块悲天泣地的大浮雕
    还认识我吗？你的广场
    是我心脏日夜跳荡的地方
    我已把全部热情和血液
    流进你的脉管你的红墙和大街
    你的每个轮上的梦
    旋转中的国度呵如一方舞台
    就这么记住了
    那位伟人的巨手一挥之间
    而我就是当年红楼下
    生动过的童年
    那个与五角星一起
    闪亮过的童年呵
