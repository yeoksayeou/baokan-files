Title: Vietnamese Comrade Nguyen Co Thach Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-10-04
Edition: 3

According to a report by the Vietnamese *People's Daily* on October 2, Nguyen Co Thach, Vice Chairman of the Vietnamese Council of State, passed away on September 30 at the age of 76. Nguyen Co Thach joined the Vietnamese Communist Party in 1945 and was one of the leaders in the anti-American war in southern Vietnam.

### 越南黄晋发去世

1989-10-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    越南黄晋发去世
    据越南《人民报》10月2日报道，越南国务委员会副主席黄晋发于9月30日去世，终年76岁。黄晋发于1945年加入越南共产党，是在越南南部反美战争中的领导人之一。
