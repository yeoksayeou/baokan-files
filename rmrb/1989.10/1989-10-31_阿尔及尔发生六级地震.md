Title: A Six-Magnitude Earthquake Strikes Algiers
Author:
Date: 1989-10-31
Edition: 3

    A six-magnitude earthquake struck Algiers, the capital of Algeria, on the evening of the 29th. The first quake, measuring six on the Richter scale, occurred at 10:10 PM, with its epicenter located 60 kilometers southwest of the city. A second quake followed at 10:25 PM. The earthquakes resulted in 19 deaths and 157 injuries. Algerian Prime Minister Hamroush rushed to the disaster area for an inspection shortly after the earthquakes occurred. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 阿尔及尔发生六级地震

1989-10-31
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　阿尔及尔发生六级地震
    阿尔及利亚首都阿尔及尔二十九日晚上十点十分和十点二十五分连续发生两次强烈地震。第一次地震为六级，地震中心位于首都西南部六十公里处。地震中，十九人死亡，一百五十七人受伤。阿尔及利亚总理哈姆鲁什于发生地震后当晚赶往灾区视察。　（据新华社）
