Title: India Decides to Purchase MiG-29 from the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1989-10-08
Edition: 3

According to reports from newspapers in New Delhi, India has made the final decision to purchase another squadron of MiG-29 fighter jets from the Soviet Union, bringing the total to three squadrons.

The report states that it is now certain that India has been granted permission to manufacture MiG-29 fighter jets at Hindustan Aeronautics Limited.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 印决定从苏购买米格—29

1989-10-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    印决定从苏购买米格—29
    据印度首都报纸报道，印度已最后决定再向苏联购买一个中队的米格—29战斗机，至此将增强到三个中队。
    报道说，现在可以肯定，印度已获准将在兴都斯坦航空公司制造米格—29战斗机。
                                    （据新华社）
