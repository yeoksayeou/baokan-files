Title: Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Says Chatchai's Visit to China Reflects Sino-Thai Friendship; CCP Will Develop Relations with Friendly Parties Based on Four Principles
Author: He Chongyuan
Date: 1989-10-27
Edition: 2

Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Says Chatchai's Visit to China Reflects Sino-Thai Friendship; CCP Will Develop Relations with Friendly Parties Based on Four Principles

	Beijing, October 26 (Reported by He Chongyuan) – Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Li Zhaoxing said today that Thai Prime Minister Chatichai Choonhavan's working visit to China reflects the close and friendly relations between China and Thailand, and is beneficial for further strengthening coordination and cooperation between the two countries in politically resolving the Cambodian issue.

	Li Zhaoxing said at the press conference this afternoon in response to a reporter's question that during Chatichai's visit to China, he exchanged views with Chinese leaders in a friendly and cordial atmosphere on bilateral relations between China and Thailand and the Cambodian issue.

	In response to the question of which party in Hungary the Communist Party of China will establish relations with, he said that the Communist Party of China will continue to develop relations with friendly parties in various countries based on the four principles of party-to-party relations, namely independence, complete equality, mutual respect, and non-interference in each other's internal affairs. He said that the recent developments in Hungary are Hungary's own affairs. China will develop friendly and cooperative relations with various countries on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

### 外交部发言人说  差猜访华体现了中泰友好  中共将按四项原则发展同友党关系
何崇元
1989-10-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外交部发言人说
　　差猜访华体现了中泰友好
　　中共将按四项原则发展同友党关系
    本报北京10月26日讯　记者何崇元报道：外交部发言人李肇星今天说，泰国总理差猜·春哈旺对中国的工作访问体现了中泰之间亲密友好的关系，有利于进一步加强双方在政治解决柬埔寨问题上的协调和合作。
    李肇星在下午的新闻发布会上回答记者提问时说，差猜总理此次访华期间，在亲切友好的气氛中同中国领导人就中泰双边关系和柬埔寨问题交换了意见。
    在回答中国共产党同匈牙利哪个党发生关系问题时，他说，中国共产党将继续按照党与党的之间关系四项原则，即独立自主、完全平等、互相尊重、互不干涉内部事务的原则，来发展同各国友好政党的关系。他说，匈牙利最近的事态发展是匈牙利自己的事情。中国将在和平共处五项原则基础之上发展同各国的友好合作关系。
