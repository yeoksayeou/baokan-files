Title: Tian Jiyun Meets with the Grants
Author: Yang Guoqing
Date: 1989-10-18
Edition: 2

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun of the State Council met this afternoon in Zhongnanhai with James P. Grant, Executive Director of the United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF), and his wife, who are on a working visit to China.

Tian Jiyun, on behalf of the Chinese government, extended a warm welcome to Grant. He expressed gratitude for Grant's contributions to the cooperation between China and UNICEF since he took office as the Executive Director of the Fund, and he was pleased with the achievements in cooperation in the fields of maternal and child health, child nutrition, immunization, and basic education. He hoped that both sides would continue to strengthen their cooperation.

Tian Jiyun described Grant as an "old friend of the Chinese people." He said that China and UNICEF had carried out fruitful cooperation over the past ten years. "China will, as always, do its utmost to promote this cooperation and advance the cause of children's education and health."

Grant expressed his pleasure at visiting China on the tenth anniversary of cooperation between China and UNICEF and the 40th anniversary of the founding of New China. He praised China's outstanding achievements in children's affairs over the past 40 years.

He said that over the next five years, the Fund would focus its assistance on improving children's education and health in China's poor areas, especially in the old, minority, border, and impoverished regions. He hoped that China would play a greater role in international children's development.

From 1979 to 1989, the Fund provided nearly $100 million in assistance to China. The Fund will also provide assistance to China from 1990 to 1994.

Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin and officials from the Fund's office in China attended the meeting.

Grant was born in China and had worked in the Jin-Cha-Ji border area, holding deep affection for the Chinese people.

### 田纪云会见格兰特夫妇
杨国强
1989-10-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    田纪云会见格兰特夫妇
    新华社北京10月17日电（记者杨国强）国务院副总理田纪云今天下午在中南海会见正在中国进行工作访问的联合国儿童基金会执行主任格兰特及其夫人。
    田纪云代表中国政府对格兰特来访表示热烈欢迎。他对格兰特就任儿童基金会执行主任以来对中国与儿童基金会合作的贡献表示感谢，并对双方在妇幼保健、儿童营养、计划免疫、基础教育等方面的合作成果表示满意。他表示希望双方继续加强合作。
    田纪云称格兰特是“中国人民的老朋友”。他说，中国与儿童基金会十年来进行了卓有成效的合作。“中国将一如既往从各方面尽力推动这种合作并促进儿童教育、保健事业的发展。”
    格兰特对在中国与儿童基金会合作十周年和新中国成立40周年之际来访表示高兴，并对中国40年来在儿童事业方面取得的卓越成就表示钦佩。
    他说，今后五年，基金会的援助重点将放在对中国的贫困区，即老少边穷地区的儿童教育和保健的改善。他希望中国在国际儿童发展事业方面起更大的作用。
    1979年至1989年间，基金会向中国提供近一亿美元的援助。1990年至1994年，基金会还将向中国提供援助。
    对外经济贸易部部长郑拓彬和基金会驻华办事处官员参加了会见。
    格兰特出生在中国，曾在晋察冀边区工作，对中国人民有着深厚的感情。
