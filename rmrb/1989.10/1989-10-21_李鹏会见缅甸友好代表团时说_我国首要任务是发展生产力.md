Title: Li Peng Meets with Myanmar Friendship Delegation, Says China's Primary Task is to Develop Productive Forces
Author:
Date: 1989-10-21
Edition: 1

Li Peng Meets with Myanmar Friendship Delegation, Says China's Primary Task is to Develop Productive Forces

	New China News Agency, Beijing, October 20th - Premier Li Peng met with a Myanmar friendship delegation led by General Danrui, Chief of the Army, this afternoon at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

	Li Peng said, "China and Myanmar are friendly neighbors, and our relations have always been good. The friendship between China and Myanmar was initiated by the older generation of politicians in both countries, and we should cherish this relationship. We believe that with joint efforts from both sides, the friendly cooperative relationship between China and Myanmar can continue to be consolidated and developed on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence."

	General Danrui agreed with this view. He said, "The friendship between Myanmar and China, based on mutual trust and sympathy, is deepening, consolidating, and developing. Both sides have the responsibility to maintain and develop this friendship. He said the purpose of the delegation's visit is to further develop the friendship between the two countries and their armies."

	Li Peng expressed satisfaction with the friendly meetings and talks between the Myanmar friendship delegation and relevant Chinese departments. He said, "The prospects for exchanges and cooperation between the two sides in the fields of economy, trade, science and technology, and the military are broad."

	Li Peng also briefly introduced China's development strategy and the current situation regarding economic rectification. He said, "China's primary task at present is to develop productive forces and gradually improve the people's living standards and cultural quality. To this end, we must make great efforts."

	After the meeting, General Der Huangtian, Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army, met and hosted a banquet for the Myanmar guests.

### 李鹏会见缅甸友好代表团时说  我国首要任务是发展生产力

1989-10-21
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏会见缅甸友好代表团时说
　　我国首要任务是发展生产力
    新华社北京10月20日电　国务院总理李鹏今天下午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见由陆军总司令丹瑞中将率领的缅甸友好代表团。
    李鹏说，中国和缅甸是友好邻邦，两国关系一直很好。“中、缅友谊是两国老一辈政治家开创的，我们应该珍惜这种关系。我们相信，在双方共同努力下，中缅友好合作关系能在和平共处五项原则的基础上继续得到巩固和发展。”
    丹瑞中将对此表示同意。他说，建立在相互信任和同情基础上的缅中友谊正日益深化、巩固和发展，双方有责任把这种友谊保持和发展下去。他说，代表团来访的目的就是要进一步发展两国、两国军队的友谊。
    李鹏对缅甸友好代表团同中国有关部门的友好会晤和会谈表示满意。他说，双方在经济、贸易、科技和军事方面的交流和合作的前景是广阔的。
    李鹏还向客人简要介绍了中国的发展战略和当前对经济治理整顿的有关情况。他说，中国当前第一位的任务是发展生产力，逐步提高人民的生活水平和文化素质。“为此，我们要做出很大的努力。”
    会见后，中国人民解放军总参谋长迟浩田上将会见并宴请缅甸贵宾。
