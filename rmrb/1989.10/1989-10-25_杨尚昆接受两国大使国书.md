Title: Yang Shangkun Accepts Letters of Credence from Two Countries' Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1989-10-25
Edition: 2

Yang Shangkun Accepts Letters of Credence from Two Countries' Ambassadors

New China News Agency, Beijing, October 24 — State Chairman Yang Shangkun this morning at the Great Hall of the People respectively accepted the letters of credence from the new Algerian Ambassador to China, Murad Bensheikh, and the new Fijian Ambassador to China, Charles Walker.

The two ambassadors arrived in Beijing on October 21 and 23, respectively.

### 杨尚昆接受两国大使国书

1989-10-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　杨尚昆接受两国大使国书
    新华社北京10月24日电　国家主席杨尚昆今天上午在人民大会堂分别接受了阿尔及利亚新任驻华大使穆拉德·邦谢克和斐济新任驻华大使查尔斯·沃克递交的国书。
    这两位大使分别于10月21日和23日抵京。
