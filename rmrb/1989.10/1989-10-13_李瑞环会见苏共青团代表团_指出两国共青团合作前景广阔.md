Title: Li Ruihuan Meets with Soviet Komsomol Delegation
Author: Yang Zidi
Date: 1989-10-13
Edition: 1

Li Ruihuan Meets with Soviet Komsomol Delegation
Points Out Broad Prospects for Cooperation Between Two Countries' Youth Organizations

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 12 — (Reporter Yang Zidi) Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Li Ruihuan this afternoon met in Zhongnanhai with the Soviet Komsomol delegation, the first to visit China since the two countries' youth organizations suspended relations more than 20 years ago.

	Li Ruihuan told the delegation's head, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Komsomol Milonenko, "With the resumption of relations between the two countries' communist parties, the youth organizations of the two countries have also returned to normal exchanges. We are very pleased about this."

	Li Ruihuan said that mutual exchange and learning between the youth organizations of the two countries, learning from each other's strengths and making up for each other's weaknesses, is in the interest of both sides and also in line with the wishes of the youth of the two countries.

	Milonenko expressed the desire to strengthen cooperation between the youth organizations of the two countries during the meeting. Li Ruihuan agreed with this.

	Li Ruihuan said that the prospects for cooperation between the youth organizations of the two countries are broad.

	The Soviet Komsomol delegation arrived in Beijing on October 6 at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China for a seven-day visit to China.

### 李瑞环会见苏共青团代表团  指出两国共青团合作前景广阔
杨子迪
1989-10-13
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李瑞环会见苏共青团代表团
    指出两国共青团合作前景广阔
    新华社北京10月12日电　（记者杨子迪）中共中央政治局常委李瑞环今天下午在中南海会见了自中国和苏联两国共青团中断关系20多年后第一个访华的苏联列宁共青团代表团。
    李瑞环对代表团团长、苏联列宁共青团中央第一书记米洛年科说：“随着两国共产党恢复了关系，两国共青团也已走上了正常交往的轨道。对此我们非常高兴。”
    李瑞环说，两国共青团相互交流学习，取长补短，是符合双方的利益的，也符合两国青年的愿望。
    米洛年科在会见时表达了加强两国共青团合作的愿望。李瑞环对此表示赞同。
    李瑞环说，两国共青团合作前景是广阔的。
    苏联列宁共青团代表团是应中国共青团中央的邀请于6日到达北京对中国进行为期7天的访问的。
