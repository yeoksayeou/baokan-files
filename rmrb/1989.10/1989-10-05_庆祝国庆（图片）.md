Title: Celebrating National Day
Author: Lin Rongwen Lin Rongbin
Date: 1989-10-05
Page: 8

The 40th anniversary of the founding of New China is approaching. The entire nation is filled with a sense of pride and joy. The people are celebrating this significant day with great enthusiasm. The streets are adorned with colorful decorations, and the atmosphere is vibrant and festive.

In the past 40 years, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, the Chinese people have achieved remarkable successes in various fields. The country has undergone tremendous changes, and the living standards of the people have significantly improved. The achievements are a testament to the wisdom and hard work of the Chinese people.

As we celebrate this national holiday, we should also reflect on the past and look forward to the future. We must cherish the hard-won peace and stability, and continue to work together to build a prosperous and strong nation. The road ahead may be challenging, but with unity and determination, we can overcome any obstacle and achieve even greater heights.

Let us join hands and celebrate this special day with pride and joy. Long live the People's Republic of China! Long live the Communist Party of China!
