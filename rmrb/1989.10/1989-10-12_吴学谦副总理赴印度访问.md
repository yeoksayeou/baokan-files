Title: Vice Premier Wu Xueqian Visits India
Author:
Date: 1989-10-12
Edition: 2

At the invitation of the Indian government, Vice Premier Wu Xueqian departed from Beijing this morning by airplane to visit India on a friendly tour.

Secretary of the State Council Luo Gan, Indian Ambassador to China J. N. Dixit, and others went to the airport to see him off.

### 吴学谦副总理赴印度访问

1989-10-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    吴学谦副总理赴印度访问
    本报北京10月11日讯　应印度政府邀请，吴学谦副总理今天上午乘飞机离开北京，前往印度进行友好访问。
    国务院秘书长罗干、印度驻华大使任嘉德等前往机场送行。
