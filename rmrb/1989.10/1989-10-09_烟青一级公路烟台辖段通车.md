Title: The Yantai Section of the Yantai-Qingdao Expressway Opens to Traffic
Author: Liu Shubin, Zhong Zhaopeng
Date: 1989-10-09
Page: 2

Transmitted by: Four Corners

The Yantai section of the Yantai-Qingdao Expressway opened to traffic today. This high-grade road, designed by the Yantai Municipal Road Administration, is one of the key road construction projects in the national "Seventh Five-Year Plan." It traverses five counties and districts in Yantai City: Zhifu, Fushan, Qixia, Haiyang, and Laiyang, with a total length of 107.8 kilometers. The completion of this road will provide favorable conditions for the economic development of the coastal open cities of Yantai and Qingdao, further promoting the economic development of the Shandong Peninsula.

### 烟青一级公路烟台辖段通车
刘树斌　仲昭鹏
1989-10-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    烟青一级公路烟台辖段通车
    本报烟台10月8日讯　横贯胶东腹地的烟台——青岛一级公路烟台辖段今天剪彩通车。
    由烟台市公路部门自行设计的这段高等级公路是国家“七五”重点公路建设项目之一。它穿越烟台市的芝罘、福山、栖霞、海阳、莱阳5个县市区，全长107.8公里。这段路的建成，为繁荣烟台、青岛两个沿海开放城市的经济提供了有利条件，将进一步促进山东半岛的经济发展。
                    （刘树斌　仲昭鹏）
