Title: Autumn Herding (and One More)
Author: Qiáoyán
Date: 1989-10-30
Edition: 8

	Autumn Herding (and One More)

	Wielding
	The magic whip from fairy tales
	Driving away
	A cluster of scorching clouds
	A roaring river
	A flowing canvas

	Autumn's herding
	Harvesting
	The winter's snow and frost
	The summer's heat
	The spring's chill

	Wielding
	The magic whip from fairy tales
	Driving away
	A mountain in motion
	An inexhaustible wealth
	A boundless silk brocade

	Autumn's herding
	Harvesting
	Solid hopes
	Complete wishes
	Unconditional fulfillment
