Title: Young German Artist Philip Schaffner Holds First Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1989-10-30
Edition: 7

Philip Schaffner, a 3-year-old painter from the Federal Republic of Germany, began learning to paint at the age of 1. Recently, he held his first exhibition in his hometown of Bremen, showcasing over 500 of his watercolor paintings. The image shows Philip in the midst of creating a painting.
