Title: The Korean People's Armed Forces Ministry Holds a Banquet to Commemorate the 39th Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' Entry into Korea
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-10-25
Edition: 3

The Korean People's Armed Forces Ministry held a banquet tonight at the Okryu Pavilion in Pyongyang to commemorate the 39th anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' entry into Korea.

The Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, Choe Kwang, Vice Premier Kang Hye-sok of the Cabinet, and Chairman Ri Chae-bang of the Central Committee of the Korea-China Friendship Association attended the banquet.

Chinese Ambassador to Korea Wen Yezhan, Chinese delegate to the Military Armistice Commission in Korea Tian Sheng, and Director Zhao Sian of the Chinese People's Volunteers' Liaison Office in Kaesong were invited to attend the banquet.

Korean People's Army Vice Chief of the General Staff Ji Ki-sun delivered the first speech. He emphasized that the Korean people will forever remember the great achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteers in the Korean War and post-war reconstruction. He said that currently, the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army, under the leadership of the new leadership of the Communist Party of China, are adhering to the four cardinal principles, deepening reform and opening up, and achieving great successes in the struggle to implement the spirit of the 13th Party Congress and the Fourth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee.

Ambassador Wen Yezhan, in his speech, reviewed the history of the two countries' people jointly resisting aggression 39 years ago. He pointed out that this not only defended the peace of the Far East and the world but also greatly inspired and promoted the flourishing of the national liberation movement after World War II, leaving an indelible brilliant page in the history of the world's progressive people. He also praised the achievements of the Korean people's socialist construction and once again emphasized that the Chinese people will, as always, support the Korean people's socialist construction and the struggle for the independent and peaceful reunification of the motherland.

### 朝人民武装力量部举行宴会  纪念我志愿军赴朝参战39周年
徐宝康
1989-10-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　朝人民武装力量部举行宴会
　　纪念我志愿军赴朝参战39周年
    本报平壤10月24日电　记者徐宝康报道：朝鲜人民武装力量部今晚在平壤玉流馆举行宴会，纪念中国人民志愿军赴朝参战39周年。
    朝鲜人民军总参谋长崔光、政务院副总理姜希源、朝中友协中央委员会委员长李子方等出席宴会。
    中国驻朝鲜大使温业湛、朝鲜军事停战委员会中国人民志愿军田胜委员以及中国人民志愿军驻开城联络处处长赵嗣安等应邀出席宴会。
    朝鲜人民军副总参谋长池基善首先讲话。他强调指出，朝鲜人民将永远铭记中国人民志愿军在朝鲜战争和战后恢复建设中所建立的丰功伟绩。他说，目前，中国人民和中国人民解放军正以胜利粉碎反革命暴乱的气势，在中国共产党新领导班子的领导下，坚持四项基本原则，深化改革开放，在贯彻党的十三大和十三届四中全会精神的斗争中，取得巨大成就。
    温业湛大使在讲话中回顾了39年前中朝两国人民共同抗击侵略的历史。他指出，这不仅捍卫了远东和世界的和平，而且极大地鼓舞和推动了第二次世界大战后的民族解放运动的蓬勃兴起，在世界人民进步史上，留下了不可磨灭的光辉。他还赞扬了朝鲜人民社会主义建设的成就，并再次强调，中国人民将一如既往支持朝鲜人民的社会主义建设和争取祖国自主和平统一的斗争。
