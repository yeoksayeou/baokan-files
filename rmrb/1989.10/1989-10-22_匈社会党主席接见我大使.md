Title: Chairman of the Hungarian Socialist Party Meets Chinese Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1989-10-22
Edition: 3

Chairman of the Hungarian Socialist Party meets Chinese Ambassador
	Hungarian Socialist Party Chairman Nérsh Rezső met with the outgoing Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, Zhu Ankang, on October 18. Nérsh expressed the hope to continue developing friendly relations between the two parties and the two countries.

### 匈社会党主席接见我大使

1989-10-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　匈社会党主席接见我大使
    匈牙利社会党主席涅尔什·雷热18日接见了即将离任的中国驻匈牙利大使朱安康。涅尔什在接见时表示希望继续发展中匈两党和两国之间的友好关系。
