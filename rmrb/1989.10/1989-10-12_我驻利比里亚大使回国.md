Title: Chinese Ambassador to Liberia Returns to China
Author:
Date: 1989-10-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Monrovia, October 10th.

After China's announcement to suspend diplomatic relations with Liberia, the Chinese Ambassador to Liberia, Cao Yuanxin, left Monrovia for China this evening.

Due to the Liberian government's formal signing of a joint communiqué with Taiwan on October 9th to "re-establish diplomatic relations," the Chinese Foreign Ministry announced on October 10th the suspension of diplomatic relations with Liberia.

### 我驻利比里亚大使回国

1989-10-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    我驻利比里亚大使回国
    新华社蒙罗维亚10月10日电　在中国宣布中止同利比里亚外交关系后，中华人民共和国驻利比里亚特命全权大使曹元欣今天晚上离开这里回国。
    由于利比里亚政府10月9日正式同中国的台湾签署重建“外交关系”的联合公报，中国外交部10日宣布中止同利比里亚的外交关系。
