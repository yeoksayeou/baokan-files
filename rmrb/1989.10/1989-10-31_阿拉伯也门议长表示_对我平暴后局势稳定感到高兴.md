Title: Yemeni Parliament Speaker Expresses Joy Over Stability in China After Suppression of Counter-Revolutionary Riot
Author:
Date: 1989-10-31
Edition: 3

Yemeni Parliament Speaker Expresses Joy Over Stability in China After Suppression of Counter-Revolutionary Riot

	Xinhua News Agency, Sana'a, October 29th - The Speaker of the Consultative Council of the Yemen Arab Republic, Abdul Karim Al-Iryani, expressed on the 29th that he is pleased with the stability in China following the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riot.

	Al-Iryani said during a meeting with Chinese Ambassador Zheng Dayong that Yemen and China have a traditional friendly relationship. The Yemeni people are very concerned about the developments in China's domestic situation and are pleased with the stable situation that has emerged after the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riot.

	During the meeting, Ambassador Zheng Dayong briefed Al-Iryani on the truth behind China's suppression of the counter-revolutionary riot. He reaffirmed that China will continue to adhere to an independent and peaceful foreign policy and will remain committed to reform and opening up. Al-Iryani expressed his sincere wishes for the success of China's reform and opening up and for greater achievements in all areas.

### 阿拉伯也门议长表示  对我平暴后局势稳定感到高兴

1989-10-31
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　阿拉伯也门议长表示
　　对我平暴后局势稳定感到高兴
    新华社萨那10月29日电　阿拉伯也门共和国协商议会议长阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·阿尔希29日表示，对中国平暴后局势稳定感到高兴。
    阿尔希在接见中国大使郑达庸时说，阿拉伯也门同中国有着传统的友好关系，也门人民对中国国内形势发展十分关注，对中国平息反革命暴乱后出现的稳定局势感到高兴。
    接见时，郑达庸大使向阿尔希议长介绍了我平息反革命暴乱的真相，重申我将继续奉行独立自主的和平外交政策，坚持改革开放不变。对此，阿尔希议长表示衷心祝愿中国人民改革开放取得成功，在各方面取得更大胜利。
