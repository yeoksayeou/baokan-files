Title: Sunflowers
Author: Tao Shuny
Date: 1989-10-17
Edition: 8

	Sunflowers
		By Tao Shuny

	Only when the sun comes out
	Can you open your eyes
	You open your eyes
	To see the sky
	To see the clouds
	To see the endless scenery
	To see the heart
	That has been suppressed
	For an entire season
	Blooming in all colors
	Sunlight seems to be your lifeblood
	Flowing through your entire body
	Like my veins bulging on my arms
	Reaching towards the blue sky
	Calling for the eternal sunshine
	I am not this flower
	But I am also
	A bud that cannot live without sunlight
	With a sincere heart
	Waiting for a gift
	From Schumann

1989-10-17

    太阳花
    陶顺义
    只有在出了太阳的时候
    你才可睁开眼睛

    看天
    看云
    看无边的风景
    看被压抑了
    一个季节的心情
    五颜六色地开放
    阳光似乎是你的血脉
    流灌于你的全身
    如我青筋凸起的双臂
    伸向蓝天
    呼唤艳阳永驻
    我并非此花
    却也是
    离不开日照的
    一苞花蕾
    拳拳之心
    等待着一种
    舒曼的赐与
