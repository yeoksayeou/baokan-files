Title: The Situation in the National Football First Division A Group Becomes Clear
Author: Fan Xing, Wu Xiaolin
Date: 1989-10-24
Edition: 4

The situation in the National Football First Division A Group has become clear. The People's Daily reported from Nanjing on October 21 that today, four matches were held in the Nanjing and Hefei competition zones. The matches were Shanghai vs. Guangzhou, the National Second Team vs. Tianjin, Liaoning vs. Shandong, and Bayi vs. Guangdong. All four matches ended in 0-0 draws. Starting from October 24, the 8 teams in Group A will compete in the final two rounds. The champion will be decided between the National Second Team and Liaoning.

### 全国足球甲级队联赛A组形势明朗
范星　吴晓林
1989-10-24
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　全国足球甲级队联赛A组形势明朗
    本报南京10月21日电　全国足球甲级队联赛今天在南京和合肥两个赛区进行了四场比赛。上海队对广州队，国家二队对天津队，辽宁队对山东队，八一队对广东队，四战均以0∶0告终。本月24日起，A组的8个队将进行最后两轮交锋，冠军将在国家二队与辽宁队之间产生。（范星　吴晓林）
