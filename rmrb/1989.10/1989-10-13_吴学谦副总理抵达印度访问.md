Title: Vice Premier Wu Xueqian Arrives in India for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1989-10-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 11th

Vice Premier Wu Xueqian arrived here tonight to begin a seven-day official visit to India.

Vice Premier Wu Xueqian and his entourage were warmly welcomed by Indian Foreign Minister Rao and other officials at the New Delhi airport.

At the New Delhi airport, Vice Premier Wu Xueqian delivered a written speech. After reviewing the deep traditional friendship between the Chinese and Indian peoples, he said, "In recent years, with the continuous increase in friendly exchanges between the two countries, relations between the two countries have improved and developed, and mutual understanding and friendship between the two peoples have been strengthened. I believe that as long as we adhere to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence jointly advocated by the leaders of the two countries, and through the joint efforts of both sides, Sino-Indian relations will definitely be further improved and developed."

During the visit, Wu Xueqian will meet with Indian President Venkataraman and Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi, and hold talks with Foreign Minister Rao on bilateral relations and international issues of common concern.

In addition to New Delhi, Wu Xueqian and his entourage will also visit Bangalore, Goa, and Mumbai.

### 吴学谦副总理抵达印度访问

1989-10-13
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    吴学谦副总理抵达印度访问
    新华社新德里10月11日电  国务院副总理吴学谦今晚到达这里，对印度进行为期七天的正式访问。
    吴学谦副总理一行在新德里机场受到印度外交部长拉奥和其他官员的热烈欢迎。
    吴学谦副总理在新德里机场发表书面讲话，在回顾了中国人民和印度人民间存在的深厚的传统友谊后说，近年来，随着两国友好交往的不断增加，两国关系得到了改善和发展，两国人民之间的相互了解和友谊得到了加强。我相信，只要恪守两国领导人共同倡导的和平共处五项原则，经过双方共同努力，中印关系一定能够得到进一步的改善和发展。
    访问期间，吴学谦将会见印度总统文卡塔拉曼和总理拉·甘地，并同拉奥外长就双边关系和共同关心的国际问题举行会谈。
    除了新德里以外，吴学谦一行还将访问班加罗尔、果阿和孟买。
