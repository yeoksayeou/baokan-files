Title: Chairman Obeng Ends Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1989-10-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 14th.

Chairman Obeng of the Republic of Ghana's Council of Ministers ended his visit to Shanghai and returned to Beijing this evening before departing for his home country.

Before leaving Beijing, Premier Li Peng sent a telephone message to express his greetings, congratulating Chairman Obeng on the success of his visit, which he said had deepened mutual understanding, solidarity, and friendship between China and Ghana, promoted bilateral relations, and achieved complete success.

The leader of the accompanying delegation, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Yang Taifang, saw Chairman Obeng off at the airport.

During his visit to Shanghai, Chairman Obeng toured local factories, rural areas, and Sino-foreign joint ventures. On the evening of the 13th, Mayor Zhu Rongji of Shanghai met and hosted a dinner for Chairman Obeng at the Xijiao Guesthouse.

### 奥斌主席结束访华

1989-10-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    奥斌主席结束访华
    据新华社北京10月14日电　加纳共和国部长委员会主席保罗·维克托·奥斌结束了在上海的访问，于今晚从上海回到北京，离京回国。
    奥斌主席离京前，李鹏总理向他致以电话问候，祝贺他这次访问增进了中加两国之间的相互了解、团结和友谊，促进了双边关系，获得了圆满成功。
    陪同团团长、邮电部长杨泰芳到机场送行。
    访沪期间，奥斌主席参观了当地的工厂、农村及中外合资企业。13日晚，上海市市长朱熔基在西郊宾馆会见并宴请了奥斌主席。
