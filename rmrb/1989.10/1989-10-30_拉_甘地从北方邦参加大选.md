Title: Rajiv Gandhi to Contest Elections from Uttar Pradesh
Author:
Date: 1989-10-30
Edition: 3

Indian Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi announced on the 24th that he would contest the upcoming elections from the Amethi constituency in Uttar Pradesh, which are scheduled to be held at the end of November. According to reports, Rajiv Gandhi filled out his nomination form in the Amethi constituency on the 24th. The report stated that Rajiv Gandhi told reporters that he did not wish to contest from any other constituency, clarifying the widespread speculation in the media about his candidacy.

### 拉·甘地从北方邦参加大选

1989-10-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　拉·甘地从北方邦参加大选
    印度总理拉吉夫·甘地二十四日宣布他将从北方邦的阿梅提选区参加将于十一月底举行的大选。据报道，拉·甘地二十四日到阿梅提选区填写了提名登记表。报道说，拉·甘地向记者透露，他不想再从其它选区竞选，澄清了报界对他竞选的盛传。　（据新华社）
