Title: Japan Develops Aromatic Air Conditioning Technology
Author:
Date: 1989-10-28
Edition: 7

Japan's Hitachi Comprehensive Equipment Construction Company recently completed foundational research on "aromatic air conditioning technology" in collaboration with the Ministry of Construction's Building Research Institute. They plan to commercialize this technology in the near future.

"Aromatic air conditioning technology" involves extracting aromatic components from plants and releasing them through air conditioning equipment to make the living environment more natural. Initial test results show that in an environment with natural plant aromas, 30% of people experience reduced eye fatigue, and 30% to 50% of people see an improvement in work efficiency. Based on this technology, Hitachi Comprehensive Equipment Construction Company and the Building Research Institute have developed an aromatic air conditioning device that can adjust the concentration of aromatic components in the air. The basic structure of this device involves placing extracted essential oils in a release container and then using an air pump to deliver the aroma through a tube into the room.

### 日本研究出调香空调技术

1989-10-28
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

　　日本研究出调香空调技术
    日本日立成套设备建设公司最近与建设省建筑研究所共同完成了关于“调香空调技术”的基础研究，并决定在不远的将来将该技术转化成商品。
      “调香空调技术”是把从植物中提取的芳香物质成分再经过空调设备释放出来，使人的生活环境更接近于大自然。据初步测试结果表明，在具有天然植物芳香的环境中，有30％的人可减轻用眼疲劳，30％至50％的人能提高工作效率。日立成套设备建设公司和建筑研究所根据“调香空调技术”研制出的调香空调装置具有可调节空气中芳香物质成分含量的功能，它的基本结构是将提取的芳香精油装入发生容器内，然后用气泵将香气通过导管输送到室内。　　　　　　（据新华社）
