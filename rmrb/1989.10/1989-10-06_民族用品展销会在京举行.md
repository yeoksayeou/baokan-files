Title: Ethnic Products Expo Held in Beijing
Author: Er Mu
Date: 1989-10-06
Edition: 2

The National Ethnic Products and Famous, Special, and Excellent Products Expo began on September 29 at the Beijing Ethnic Culture Palace.

Organized by the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, this expo features products from 26 provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions, with over 700 companies showcasing more than 3,000 outstanding items across 18 categories.

### 民族用品展销会在京举行
二木
1989-10-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    民族用品展销会在京举行
    本报讯　全国民族用品暨名特优产品展销会9月29日起在北京民族文化宫举行。
    由国家民委组织的这次展销会，汇集了26个省、市、自治区700多个厂家的18大类、3000多种优秀产品。　　（二木）
