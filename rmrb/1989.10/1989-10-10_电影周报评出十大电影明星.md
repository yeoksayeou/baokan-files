Title: China Film News Selects Top Ten Film Stars
Author:
Date: 1989-10-10
Edition: 4

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 9th: Zhao Dan, Bai Yang, Cui Wei, Sun Daolin, Yu Lan, Wang Xingang, Xie Fang, Liu Xiaqi, Pan Hong, and Jiang Wen have been selected as the top ten film stars of the past 40 years since the founding of the nation. This event, organized by the China Film News, aims to commend the creative efforts of China's film actors in promoting the development of socialist film creation.

### 电影周报评出十大电影明星

1989-10-10
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    电影周报评出十大电影明星
    据新华社北京十月九日电　赵丹、白杨、崔嵬、孙道临、于蓝、王心刚、谢芳、刘晓庆、潘虹、姜文被评为建国四十年来的十大电影明星。由《中国电影周报》主办的这项活动，旨在褒扬我国电影表演艺术家为繁荣社会主义电影创作而进行的创造性劳动。
