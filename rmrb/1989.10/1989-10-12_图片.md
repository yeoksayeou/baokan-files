Title: Honoring Consumers and Expanding Sales
Author: Zeng Zhaoqin Wang Yunfeng
Date: 1989-10-12
Page: 5

    The city center hardware store in Yicheng County, Hubei Province, adheres to the business philosophy of prioritizing credibility and customer satisfaction. The store implements a reward system for customers who find defective bicycles, thereby protecting consumer interests and expanding sales. Look, the customers in the photo are so happy to have selected satisfactory bicycles!

    Zeng Zhaoqin and Wang Yunfeng (photographers)
