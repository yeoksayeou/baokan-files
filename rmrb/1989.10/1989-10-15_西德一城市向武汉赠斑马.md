Title: A City in West Germany Donates Zebras to Wuhan
Author: Tian Shaozhang
Date: 1989-10-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, October 14th.

The handover ceremony for the pair of zebras donated by the city of Duisburg in the Federal Republic of Germany to the Wuhan Zoo was held this afternoon in Wuhan.

Wuhan and Duisburg established a sister-city relationship on October 8, 1982. Last year, Wuhan built the Chinese garden "Yingqu Garden" in the Duisburg Zoo and donated a pair of panda cubs. In return, a hippopotamus was subsequently sent.

### 西德一城市向武汉赠斑马
田绍章
1989-10-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    西德一城市向武汉赠斑马
    据新华社武汉10月14日电（通讯员田绍章）联邦德国杜伊斯堡市赠送一对斑马给武汉动物园的交接仪式今天下午在武汉举行。
    武汉与杜伊斯堡于1982年10月8日结为友好城市关系。武汉于去年为杜伊斯堡市在动物园内兴建了中国园林“郢趣园”，并赠送一对小熊猫。回赠的还有一只河马随后运来。
