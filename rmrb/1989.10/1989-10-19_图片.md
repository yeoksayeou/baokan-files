Title: Pattaya, Thailand's Tourist Paradise
Author: Shēn Míng Hé
Date: 1989-10-19
Page: 7

Pattaya is a renowned tourist destination in Thailand, often referred to as the "Eastern Hawaii." Here, the sparkling blue waters of the bay, the pristine white sand beaches, the swaying palm trees, and the lush, refreshing parks together create a beautiful scene that attracts numerous domestic and international tourists. The image shows a uniquely designed sculpture park.

Photographed by our reporter, Shēn Míng Hé.
