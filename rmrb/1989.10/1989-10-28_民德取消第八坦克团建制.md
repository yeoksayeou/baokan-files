Title: East Germany Dissolves the Eighth Tank Regiment
Author:
Date: 1989-10-28
Edition: 3

The Eighth Tank Regiment of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic was reduced by 126 soldiers and 31 tanks on October 24 in Goltberg, and the regiment's structure was dissolved.

This was announced by Horst Stechbarth, the Deputy Minister of Defense and Commander of the Ground Forces of the National People's Army, at a press conference held on October 24.

### 民德取消第八坦克团建制

1989-10-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　民德取消第八坦克团建制
    民主德国国家人民军第八坦克团二十四日在戈尔特贝格裁减一百二十六名士兵、三十一辆坦克，并取消了该团建制。
    这是民主德国国防部副部长、国家人民军陆军司令霍·施特希巴尔特上将二十四日举行的记者招待会上宣布的。
