Title: South Korea Defeats Saudi Arabia to Secure Qualification
Author:
Date: 1989-10-26
Edition: 3

South Korea defeated Saudi Arabia 2-0 to secure qualification.
The other spot awaits the final battle among the top four.

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, October 25 - Breaking News: The fourth round of the final stage of the Asian qualifiers for the World Cup football tournament concluded today with the last match. South Korea emerged victorious with a 2-0 win over Saudi Arabia. Currently, South Korea has 7 points and has secured qualification. Saudi Arabia's hopes of qualifying are dashed.

### 南朝鲜胜沙特队获出线权  另一名额待四强最后拚争

1989-10-26
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　南朝鲜胜沙特队获出线权
　　另一名额待四强最后拚争
    新华社新加坡10月25日电　快讯：世界杯足球赛亚洲区预选赛决赛第4轮今天进行了最后一场比赛，结果南朝鲜队以2∶0战胜沙特阿拉伯队。目前，南朝鲜队积7分，已稳获出线权。沙特队出线无望。
