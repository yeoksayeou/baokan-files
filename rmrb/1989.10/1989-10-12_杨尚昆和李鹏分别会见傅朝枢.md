Title: Yang Shangkun and Li Peng Meet with Fu Chaojun
Author:
Date: 1989-10-12
Edition: 1

State Chairman Yang Shangkun met with Fu Chaojun, the founder of the American Chinese Daily News, in a friendly talk at the Wangfu Hotel this morning. After the meeting, Yang Shangkun hosted a banquet in honor of Fu Chaojun. Those who attended the meeting and banquet included Wang Feng and Yang Sidede. Fu Chaojun came to China at the invitation to participate in the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Premier Li Peng met with Fu Chaojun, the founder of the American Chinese Daily News, this afternoon in Zhongnanhai for a cordial and friendly talk.

Li Peng commended Fu Chaojun for his long-standing patriotic stance and his concern for the country's construction and the cause of national reunification. Attending the meeting were officials from relevant departments, Yang Sidede and Zeng Jianhui.

### 杨尚昆和李鹏分别会见傅朝枢

1989-10-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨尚昆和李鹏分别会见傅朝枢
    新华社北京10月11日电　国家主席杨尚昆今天上午在王府饭店会见美洲《中报》创办人傅朝枢，同他进行了友好的谈话。会见后，杨尚昆设宴招待傅朝枢。参加会见和宴会的有汪锋、杨思德。傅朝枢是应邀前来参加中华人民共和国成立40周年庆祝活动的。
    新华社北京10月11日电　国务院总理李鹏今天下午在中南海会见美洲《中报》创办人傅朝枢，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    李鹏对傅朝枢这些年来坚持爱国立场，关心国家的建设和祖国统一事业表示赞赏。有关方面负责人杨思德、曾建徽参加了会见。
