Title: Aftershocks Strike San Francisco Area Again
Author: Lù Wényuè
Date: 1989-10-23
Edition: 3

    San Francisco area experienced another strong aftershock today. According to the U.S. Geological Survey, the earthquake measured 4.6 on the Richter scale, with its epicenter about 80 kilometers from San Francisco Bay, near the source of the 6.9 magnitude quake that struck on October 17. The extent of the damage is currently unknown.

    Yesterday and the day before, the San Francisco area experienced five aftershocks measuring above 4.0, with the strongest reaching 5.1. In San Jose and Santa Cruz, some already damaged buildings collapsed.

    Statistics show that since the major earthquake on October 17, the San Francisco area has experienced more than 2,000 aftershocks.

### 旧金山地区又发生余震
陆文岳
1989-10-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　旧金山地区又发生余震
    新华社华盛顿10月21日电　（记者陆文岳）据美国地质测量中心报告，旧金山地区今日下午又发生里氏4.6级的较强余震，震中距旧金山湾约80公里，靠近17日6.9级大震的震源。目前损失情况不详。
    昨天和前天，旧金山地区共发生5次4级以上的余震，最大的一次是5.1级，圣何塞和圣克鲁斯都有一些已被震裂的房屋倒塌。
    据统计，自17日大震以来，旧金山地区共发生2000多次余震。
