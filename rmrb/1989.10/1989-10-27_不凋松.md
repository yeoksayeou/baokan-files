Title: Evergreen Pine
Author: Wang Jingzhi
Date: 1989-10-27
Page: 8

The pine stands tall, unyielding to snow and frost,
Its verdant branches sway like a dragon.
Through three gales, it bends but does not break,
Its loyal and steadfast spirit remains evergreen.

### 不凋松
汪静之
1989-10-27
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　不凋松
　　汪静之
　　傲雪凌霜不改翠，
　　苍松夭矫势如龙。
　　妖风三压复三起，
　　忠贞劲节不凋松。
