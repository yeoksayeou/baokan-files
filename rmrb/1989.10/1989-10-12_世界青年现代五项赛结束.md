Title: The 24th World Youth Modern Pentathlon Championships Concludes
Author: Xiao Ziquan
Date: 1989-10-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Torres Vedras, Portugal, October 10th

The 24th World Youth Modern Pentathlon Championships concluded on October 10th in the central Portuguese city of Torres Vedras.

Modern pentathlon includes equestrian, fencing, shooting, swimming, and cross-country running, with the total scores of the five events determining the team and individual rankings. The Soviet Union team secured the team championship with a total score of 16,473 points.

The Chinese team achieved a total score of 15,092 points, placing 9th. Zinovka of the Soviet Union won the individual championship.

### 世界青年现代五项赛结束
肖自权
1989-10-12
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    世界青年现代五项赛结束
    据新华社葡萄牙托雷斯·韦德拉10月10日电　（记者肖自权）第24届世界青年现代五项锦标赛10日在葡萄牙中部城市托雷斯·韦德拉结束。
    现代五项包括马术、击剑、射击、游泳和越野跑，按5个项目的总成绩排定团体和个人的名次。苏联队以16473分的总成绩获得团体冠军。
    中国队的总成绩是15092分，排在第9位。苏联的泽诺夫卡获个人冠军。
