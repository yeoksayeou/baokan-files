Title: Lebanese Parliament Meeting Stalls
Author:
Date: 1989-10-18
Edition: 3

The Lebanese parliament meeting held in Taif, Saudi Arabia, hit an impasse on October 15 over the issue of Syrian troops stationed in Lebanon.

Reports from Taif said that the military presence of Syria in Lebanon remained a point of contention among the 63 Christian and Muslim parliamentarians who were trying to reach a peace agreement.

On the 14th, the meeting showed signs of optimism. However, a statement issued by Oren on the 14th urging Lebanese parliamentarians not to compromise on the issue of Lebanese sovereignty dampened this optimistic outlook.

### 黎议会会议陷入僵局

1989-10-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    黎议会会议陷入僵局
    新华社开罗10月15日电　在沙特阿拉伯塔伊夫举行的黎巴嫩议会会议15日在叙利亚驻军问题上陷入僵局。
    来自塔伊夫的报道说，叙利亚在黎巴嫩的军事存在仍是与会63名基督教和穆斯林议员达成一项和平计划的分歧点。
    在14日的会议上曾经出现了较为乐观的迹象。但是，奥恩14日发表声明呼吁黎巴嫩议员不要在黎巴嫩主权问题上妥协，使这一乐观前景暗淡下来。
