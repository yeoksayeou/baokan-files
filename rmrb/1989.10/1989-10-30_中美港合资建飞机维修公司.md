Title: Sino-U.S.-Hong Kong Joint Venture to Establish Aircraft Maintenance Company
Author:
Date: 1989-10-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 28th.

A joint venture aircraft maintenance company, Guangzhou Aircraft Maintenance Engineering Co., Ltd., was established today in Guangzhou. This company is a joint venture between China, the United States, and Hong Kong. The initial investment for the first phase is $30 million. Among this, the investment from the Civil Aviation Administration of China's Guangzhou Management Bureau accounts for 50% of the shares, while the investment from the U.S. Lockheed International Aircraft Services Company and the Hong Kong and Wharf (China) Limited each account for 25% of the shares.

### 中美港合资建飞机维修公司

1989-10-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　中美港合资建飞机维修公司
    据新华社广州10月28日电　由中国、美国和香港合资兴建的广州飞机维修工程有限公司，今天在这里成立。
    这家合资公司的首期投资额为3000万美元，其中中国民航广州管理局的投资占股份的50％，美国洛克希德国际飞机服务公司和香港和记黄埔（中国）有限公司的投资各占股份的25％。
