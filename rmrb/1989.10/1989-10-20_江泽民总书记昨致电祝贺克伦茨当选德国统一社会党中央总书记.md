Title: General Secretary Jiang Zemin Congratulates Egon Krenz on His Election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany

Author:

Date: 1989-10-20

Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 19th.

General Secretary Jiang Zemin sent a telegram on October 19th to Comrade Egon Krenz, congratulating him on his election as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Comrade Egon Krenz,

Respected Comrade Egon Krenz,

On the occasion of your election as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, I, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and in my personal capacity, extend my warm congratulations to you. I sincerely wish you energy and great achievements in your important leadership position. I believe that under the leadership of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, headed by you, the people of the German Democratic Republic will certainly overcome all difficulties on the road ahead and achieve new victories in the great cause of building socialism. They will also make new contributions to the struggle for maintaining peace in Europe and the world.

### 江泽民总书记昨致电祝贺克伦茨当选德国统一社会党中央总书记

1989-10-20
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民总书记昨致电祝贺克伦茨当选德国统一社会党中央总书记
    新华社北京10月19日电　中共中央总书记江泽民19日打电报给埃贡·克伦茨同志，热烈祝贺他当选为德国统一社会党中央总书记。贺电全文如下：柏林德国统一社会党中央委员会总书记埃贡·克伦茨同志尊敬的埃贡·克伦茨同志：
    在您当选为德国统一社会党中央委员会总书记之际，我谨代表中国共产党中央委员会，并以我个人的名义，向您致以热烈的祝贺。衷心祝愿您在责任重大的领导岗位上精力充沛，取得巨大成就。我相信，在以您为首的德国统一社会党的领导下，民主德国人民一定能够战胜前进道路上的各种困难，在建设社会主义的伟大事业中取得新的胜利，在维护欧洲和世界和平的斗争中作出新的贡献。
    愿我们两党、两国和两国人民之间的传统友谊和友好合作关系在我们为共同的事业所进行的斗争中不断得到巩固和发展。
              中共中央总书记　江泽民
                  1989年10月19日于北京
