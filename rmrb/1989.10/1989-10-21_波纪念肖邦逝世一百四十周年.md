Title: Poland Commemorates the 140th Anniversary of Chopin's Death
Author:
Date: 1989-10-21
Edition: 3

Poland's various sectors commemorated the 140th anniversary of the death of the great Polish composer and pianist Chopin in different ways on October 17.

The Chopin Academy of Music in Warsaw designated the day of Chopin's death as the opening ceremony of the new academic year. The academy's director revealed in his speech to the students that the academy has established the Chopin Academy of Music Foundation.

That evening, a solemn memorial ceremony and a Chopin music concert were held at the Church of the Holy Cross in Warsaw, where Chopin's heart is preserved.

### 波纪念肖邦逝世一百四十周年

1989-10-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　波纪念肖邦逝世一百四十周年
    波兰各界十七日以不同形式隆重纪念伟大的波兰作曲家、钢琴家肖邦逝世一百四十周年。
    以肖邦的名字命名的华沙音乐学院，今年把肖邦逝世的日子定为新学年开学典礼日。学院院长在向学员讲话时透露，学院设立了肖邦音乐学院基金会。
    当晚，在保存着肖邦心脏罐的华沙圣十字教堂举行了隆重纪念仪式和肖邦乐曲音乐会。
                                    （据新华社）
