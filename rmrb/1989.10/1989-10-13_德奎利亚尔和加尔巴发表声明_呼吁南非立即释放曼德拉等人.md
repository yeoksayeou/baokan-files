Title: De Cuellar and Garba Issue Statements Calling for the Immediate Release of Mandela and Others
Author: Liu Kai Chen
Date: 1989-10-13
Edition: 3

United Nations, October 11 (Xinhua) -- Correspondent Liu Kai Chen reports: United Nations Secretary-General Javier Perez de Cuellar and the President of the 44th General Assembly, Joseph Garba, issued statements today, calling on the South African authorities to immediately release black leader Nelson Mandela and other political prisoners.

South African President De Klerk announced yesterday that he would release eight leaders of the African National Congress, including Sisulu. In addition to expressing relief and welcome, both De Cuellar and Garba pointed out that this is not enough and demanded that the South African authorities release all political prisoners fighting against racial discrimination, lift the state of emergency, and lift the ban on political parties. De Cuellar also hopes that the South African authorities will engage in dialogue with the true representatives of the South African majority to peacefully abolish the apartheid system.

### 德奎利亚尔和加尔巴发表声明  呼吁南非立即释放曼德拉等人
刘开宸
1989-10-13
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    德奎利亚尔和加尔巴发表声明
    呼吁南非立即释放曼德拉等人
    本报联合国10月11日电  记者刘开宸报道：联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔和第44届联大主席约瑟夫·加尔巴今天分别发表讲话和声明，呼吁南非当局立即释放黑人领袖纳尔逊·曼德拉和其它政治犯。
    南非总统德克勒克昨天宣布将释放南非非洲人国民大会党领导人西苏鲁等8人。德奎利亚尔和加尔巴除分别表示宽慰和欢迎外，都指出这还很不够，要求南非当局释放为反对种族歧视而斗争的全部政治犯，解除紧急状态，解除对政党的禁令。德奎利亚尔还希望南非当局与南非大多数人的真正代表进行对话，以便和平地废除种族主义制度。
