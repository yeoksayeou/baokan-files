Title: Chinese Civil Aviation and Others Donate to the Asian Games
Author:
Date: 1989-10-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency

    The Civil Aviation Administration of China and the China Nonferrous Metal Industry Corporation each donated 1.5 million yuan and 1 million yuan respectively to the organizing committee of the 11th Asian Games. Vice Minister of the Ministry of Radio, Film, and Television, Chen Haosu, donated 1,500 yuan.

### 中国民航等向亚运会捐款

1989-10-18
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    中国民航等向亚运会捐款
    本报讯  中国民用航空局、中国有色金属工业总公司分别向第十一届亚运会组委会捐款人民币一百五十万元和一百万元，广播电影电视部副部长陈昊苏捐赠一千五百元。
