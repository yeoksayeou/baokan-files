Title: Standardized Chinese Proficiency Test for Foreigners and Non-Han Ethnic Minorities to be Held Three Times a Year
Author:
Date: 1989-10-10
Edition: 2

The National Office for Teaching Chinese as a Foreign Language, approved by the State Education Commission, will hold the Chinese Proficiency Test (HSK) on October 15 at the Beijing Language Institute. The HSK is a standardized test designed to measure the Chinese language proficiency of foreigners and non-Han ethnic minorities. Those who achieve the required standard will be awarded a "Chinese Proficiency Certificate" (initial level certificates A, B, C, and intermediate level certificates A, B, C). This certificate can serve as proof of a foreign student's actual Chinese language proficiency and can also be used as a reference for employers hiring Chinese language personnel.

It is understood that starting from October 15 this year, the Chinese Proficiency Certificate Examination will be held three times a year at the Beijing Language Institute, on January 15, June 15, and October 15.

### 专为外国人和非汉族人开设  汉语水平考试每年举行三次

1989-10-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    专为外国人和非汉族人开设
    汉语水平考试每年举行三次
    本报北京10月9日讯　经国家教委批准，国家对外汉语教学领导小组办公室汉语水平考试部将于10月15日在北京语言学院举办汉语水平考试。汉语水平考试（简称HSK）是专为测量外国人和非汉族人的汉语水平而设计的一种标准化考试。凡考试成绩达到规定标准者，颁发“汉语水平证书（初等水平证书A、B、C三级，中等水平证书A、B、C三级）。该证书可作为外国留学生实际汉语水平的一种证明，也可作为聘用机构录用汉语人员的参考证明。
    据悉，从今年10月15日开始，汉语水平证书考试每年将在北京语言学院举办三次，时间为1月15日，6月15日，10月15日。
