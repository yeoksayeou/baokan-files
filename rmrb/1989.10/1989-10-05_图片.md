Title: North Korean Art Troupe Performs at the Second China Arts Festival
Author:
Date: 1989-10-05
Edition: 7

The art troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea was invited to participate in the recently held Second China Arts Festival in Beijing, where they presented a splendid array of songs and dances. The image shows the troupe performing the traditional folk dance "Three-Person Dance."
