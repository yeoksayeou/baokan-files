Title: Li Ruihuan Meets with Arthur Hummel
Author: Yang Zidi
Date: 1989-10-25
Edition: 1

    Li Ruihuan Meets with Arthur Hummel
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 24 (Reporter: Yang Zidi) — Member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Li Ruihuan met with former U.S. Ambassador to China Arthur Hummel this morning in Zhongnanhai.

    Both sides exchanged frank views on Sino-U.S. relations. Li Ruihuan answered questions raised by Hummel.

    Hummel came to China at the invitation of the Institute of Contemporary International Relations.

### 李瑞环会见恒安石
杨子迪
1989-10-25
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李瑞环会见恒安石
    新华社北京10月24日电　（记者杨子迪）中共中央政治局常委李瑞环今天上午在中南海会见了美国前任驻华大使恒安石。
    双方就中美关系问题坦率地交换了意见。李瑞环回答了恒安石提出的问题。
    恒安石是应中国现代国际关系研究所邀请前来访华的。
