Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1989-10-31
Edition: 3

The Chinese trade union delegation, led by Nie Rongzhi, chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, was recently received by Yakovlev, a member of the Politburo and secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, in Moscow.

Nie Rongzhi and his delegation of six people arrived in the Soviet Union on October 6 at the invitation of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions. This is the first time in nearly 30 years that the All-China Federation of Trade Unions has sent a delegation led by its chairman to visit the Soviet Union. The Chinese delegation held talks with Shalaev, chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, and others.

### 我工会代表团访苏

1989-10-31
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我工会代表团访苏
    新华社莫斯科电　苏共中央政治局委员、中央书记雅科夫列夫最近在这里会见了中华全国总工会主席倪志福。
    倪志福率领的中国工会代表团一行6人是应全苏工会中央理事会的邀请，于10月6日抵达苏联访问的。这是中华全国总工会近30多年来首次派出以主席为首的代表团访苏。中国代表团与全苏工会中央理事会主席沙拉耶夫等举行会谈。
