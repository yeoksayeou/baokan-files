Title: Who is This Year's Chinese Go "Meijin"?
Author: Wu Huo, Miu Lu
Date: 1989-10-20
Edition: 4

Who is This Year's Chinese Go "Meijin"?
Ma Xiaochun to Challenge Liu Xiaoguang

Beijing, October 19 (Xinhua) - Reporters Wu Huo and Miu Lu report: The 1988 Chinese Go Meijin, Liu Xiaoguang, will defend his title. The result will be announced in early November. On October 28, Liu Xiaoguang will face a strong challenge from Ma Xiaochun, a 9-dan player, in his hometown of Zhengzhou, Henan.

Since February this year, the second Chinese Go Meijin tournament has undergone nearly a hundred intense matches in Beijing and Hainan. By the end of July, Ma Xiaochun emerged as the challenger. According to the competition rules, Ma Xiaochun and Liu Xiaoguang will compete in a best-of-three series to determine the 1989 Meijin. This high-level domestic Go tournament, co-sponsored by the People's Daily Overseas Edition and the Chinese Go Association, has consistently attracted the attention of the Go community and enthusiasts. To further promote the development of Go, the organizers decided to move the final to Zhengzhou, Henan. This decision was strongly supported by the Henan Provincial Radio and Television News Center, the Xiangjiang Hotel of the Zhongyuan International Tourism Group, and the Henan Zhongzhou Hotel.

The national Go team players will soon depart from Beijing to Zhengzhou.

### 谁是今年中国围棋“名人”  马晓春将向刘小光挑战
吴骅  缪鲁
1989-10-20
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    谁是今年中国围棋“名人”
    马晓春将向刘小光挑战
    本报北京10月19日讯　记者吴骅、缪鲁报道：1988年中国围棋名人获得者刘小光是否能保住名人称号，结果将于11月初揭晓。本月28日开始，刘小光将在自己的家乡——河南郑州接受马晓春九段的强有力挑战。
    今年2月以来，第二届中国围棋名人战经过北京、海南两个阶段近百场激烈争夺，于7月底产生了挑战者马晓春。按照比赛规程规定，马晓春和刘小光须经三番棋决战，产生1989年度新名人。这项由人民日报海外版和中国围棋协会共同举办的国内高水平围棋大赛进行期间，始终得到围棋界和广大围棋爱好者的关注。主办单位为进一步推动围棋事业的发展，决定将决赛移至河南郑州举行，此举得到了河南省广播电视新闻中心、中原国际集团旅游总公司香江宾馆和河南中州宾馆等单位的鼎力支持。
    国家围棋队参赛棋手将于近日离京赴郑州。
