Title: Shanxi Datong Holds Reportage Literature Symposium
Author:
Date: 1989-10-24
Page: 6

The Shanxi Provincial Writers and Entrepreneurs Association and the *Writers and Entrepreneurs Chronicles* magazine recently co-hosted a reportage literature symposium in Datong. The symposium discussed the reportage works *Land Without a Surname* and *Black Turmoil*, as well as some issues in contemporary reportage literature creation. *Land* reflects the problems of "shrinking" and "wasteland" in the rural economic contract system; *Black* exposes some abnormal phenomena in the production, circulation, and sales of coal. Both works analyze and dissect issues related to land and energy, which are essential for human survival. Over 50 literary and business figures from Beijing and Shanxi attended the meeting.

### 山西大同举办报告文学研讨会

1989-10-24
第6版(文艺评论)
专栏：

　　山西大同举办报告文学研讨会
    山西省作家企业家联谊会、《作家企业家纪实》杂志社最近联合在大同举行报告文学研讨会，就《土地无姓氏》、《黑色的骚乱》两部报告文学以及当前报告文学的创作中的一些问题进行了讨论。《土地》是反映农村经济承包制中如何解决土地在“萎缩”、“荒芜”等问题；《黑色》反映了煤炭生产、流通、销售等方面存在的一些不正常的现象。两部作品对人类赖以依存的土地和能源问题进行了分析和剖析。京晋两地的文学界和企业界人士50余人参加了会议。
