Title: Autumn Canton Fair Opens
Author: Liang Zhaoming
Date: 1989-10-16
Edition: 1

The 1989 Autumn China Export Commodities Fair, which will last for half a month, opened today in Guangzhou. This is the 66th session since the first one was held in 1957.

It is learned that this session of the fair has invited customers from 136 countries and regions, totaling 39,638, which is more than the last session. Judging from the opening ceremony, the number of invited foreign businessmen will not be much different from previous sessions. All the guest rooms in major hotels and tourist hotels in Guangzhou are almost fully booked.

In addition to the original 18 trading groups, this session has added a trading group composed of economic zones such as Xiamen, Shenzhen, Zhuhai, Shantou, and Hainan. They are participating in the fair for the first time with a strong presence, and the commodities on display are varied. Shenzhen alone has over 1,000 varieties of exhibits.

### 秋季广交会开幕  海外客商踊跃赴会
梁兆明
1989-10-16
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    秋季广交会开幕
    海外客商踊跃赴会
    本报广州10月15日电　记者梁兆明报道：为期半个月的1989年秋季中国出口商品交易会，于今天上午在广州隆重开幕。这是自1957年首届举办以来的第66届广交会。
    据悉，本届交易会向136个国家和地区邀请客户39638个，比上一届多。从开幕的盛况看，应邀赴会的客商人数与往届相比，不会有多大变化。广州各大旅游宾馆酒店的客房，均告基本订满。
    这届交易会除了原有的18个交易团外，新增了由厦门、深圳、珠海、汕头、海南等经济特区组成的经济特区交易团。他们首次组团，声势颇大，展出的商品琳琅满目，其中深圳的展品就有1000多个品种。
