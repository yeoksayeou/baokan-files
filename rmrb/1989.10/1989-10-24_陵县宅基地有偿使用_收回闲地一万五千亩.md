Title: Ling County Implements Paid Use of Residential Land
Author:
Date: 1989-10-24
Edition: 2

Ling County in Shandong Province has taken concrete measures to implement a system of paid use for residential land. In just one year, the county has reclaimed over 15,000 acres of idle residential land. It is estimated that the county will not need to use any more arable land for housing construction in the next 10 years.

### 陵县宅基地有偿使用  收回闲地一万五千亩

1989-10-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　陵县宅基地有偿使用
　　收回闲地一万五千亩
    新华社济南10月23日电　山东省陵县采取切实措施，实行宅基地有偿使用制度，一年时间全县退出空闲宅基地1.5万余亩。据估算，这个县今后10年内无需再占用耕地建房。
