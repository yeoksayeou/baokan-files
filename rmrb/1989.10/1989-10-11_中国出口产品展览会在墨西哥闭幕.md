Title: China's Export Products Exhibition Closes in Mexico
Author: Guo Weicheng
Date: 1989-10-11
Edition: 3

China's Export Products Exhibition, which began on October 2, concluded this evening in Mexico City. A total of approximately $3 million in various supply contracts were signed.

This exhibition was the largest economic and trade exhibition and negotiation event held by China in Mexico in recent years. Thirty-five foreign trade companies, factories, and some township enterprises from Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Liaoning, Anhui, Fujian, and Zhejiang participated in the exhibition and conducted trade negotiations. During the exhibition period, customers from near and far flocked to the event, also attracting a large number of Mexican consumers. Each exhibiting unit conducted business negotiations with over a thousand customers. The products that have been traded so far mainly include machinery from Beijing, carpets and bicycles from Tianjin, televisions, tools, and textiles from Shanghai and Jiangsu.

### 中国出口产品展览会在墨西哥闭幕
郭伟成
1989-10-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    中国出口产品展览会在墨西哥闭幕
    本报墨西哥城10月9日电　记者郭伟成报道：10月2日开始的中国出口产品展览会今天晚间在墨西哥城闭幕，共签订各种供货合同总额约300万美元。
    这次展览会是我国近年来在墨西哥举行的最大规模经济贸易展览与洽谈活动。北京、天津、上海、江苏、辽宁、安徽、福建、浙江等省市的35家外贸专业公司、工厂及部分乡镇企业参加了展出并进行贸易洽谈。展览期间，远近客户纷至沓来，也吸引了墨西哥广大消费者。各参展单位与上千家客户进行了业务洽谈。目前已成交的产品主要是北京的机械设备，天津的地毯、自行车，上海、江苏的电视机、工具、纺织品等。
