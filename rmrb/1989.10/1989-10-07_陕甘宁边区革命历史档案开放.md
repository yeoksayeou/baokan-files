Title: Archives of the Revolutionary History of the Shaan-Gan-Ning Border Region Opened to the Public
Author: Zhang Ruoyu
Date: 1989-10-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an

The archives of the revolutionary history of the Shaan-Gan-Ning Border Region have been open to the public at the Shaanxi Provincial Archives since September 28.

This collection consists of 56 complete sets of documents, totaling over 20,000 volumes. It systematically reflects the actual conditions of democratic governance, rent and interest reduction, land revolution, streamlining the bureaucracy, the Great Production Movement, and the Rectification Movement in the Shaan-Gan-Ning Border Region from 1937 to 1950. It is one of the most complete sets of archives preserved from a revolutionary base area.

### 陕甘宁边区革命历史档案开放
张若愚
1989-10-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    陕甘宁边区革命历史档案开放
    新华社西安电（记者张若愚）陕甘宁边区革命历史档案9月28日起在陕西省档案馆对外开放。
    这套档案共56个全宗，2万余卷，比较系统地反映了1937年至1950年陕甘宁边区民主建政、减租减息、土地革命、精兵简政、大生产运动、整风运动等实况。是保存下来比较完整的革命根据地档案。
