Title: FAO Commemorates "World Food Day"
Author:
Date: 1989-10-24
Page: 3

The Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations held a conference at its headquarters in Rome on the 16th to commemorate "World Food Day." This year's theme for the commemorative activities is environmental protection and increased food production. FAO Director-General Edouard Saouma pointed out at the meeting that the global effort to stop environmental degradation is an important part of the fight against hunger and poverty. Dutch Prime Minister Ruud Lubbers and Italian Prime Minister Giulio Andreotti also spoke at the meeting.

### 粮农组织纪念“世界粮食日”

1989-10-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　粮农组织纪念“世界粮食日”
    联合国粮农组织十六日在罗马总部举行了纪念“世界粮食日”大会。今年纪念活动的主题是保护环境和增加粮食生产。粮农组织总干事萨乌马在会上指出，全世界共同努力制止环境恶化，是反饥饿和贫困斗争的重要组成部分。荷兰首相吕贝尔斯、意大利总理安德烈奥蒂也在会上讲了话。
