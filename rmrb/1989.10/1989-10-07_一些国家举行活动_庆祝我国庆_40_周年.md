Title: Some Countries Hold Activities to Celebrate the 40th Anniversary of Our National Day
Author: Guo Weicheng
Date: 1989-10-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 7th

Some countries have held activities to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	•	The Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association held a lecture on September 29 in Budapest to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The chairman of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association and Sinologist, Talos Borna, introduced the different characteristics of the two countries' relationships in various stages over the past 40 years. He expressed that the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association will continue to contribute to maintaining and promoting the friendship between the two countries.

	Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, Zhu Ankang, attended the lecture.

	•	Nepalese Prime Minister Shrestha attended a celebration meeting held by the Nepal-China Friendship Association on September 30, and delivered a speech saying that the friendly relations between Nepal and China are based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and this friendly relationship will surely be further developed.

	•	The Philippine-China Friendship Association held a celebratory reception on October 1. More than 100 representatives from various sectors of the Philippines' Chinese community attended the reception. The President of the Philippine Senate, Hovito Salonga, attended the reception and delivered a speech. In his speech, he reviewed China's great achievements over the past 40 years. He said he was very pleased with China's current reform and opening-up policy. He hoped that the friendship between the Philippines and China would continue to develop.

	•	The Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association passed a resolution at a celebration meeting held on September 30, stating that China has achieved great achievements recognized by the world in recent years. "The living standards of the Chinese people have been unprecedentedly improved, and China has successfully solved the problem of feeding more than ten billion people."

	The resolution expressed confidence that China has the ability to handle the situation in China after the suppression of the riots.

	•	The National People's Congress of Sierra Leone and the Sierra Leone-China Friendship Association held a grand mass rally on September 29 at the Friendship Building in the capital, Freetown, to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	Sierra Leone's Second Vice President, Sheriff, Foreign Minister and Chairman of the Sierra Leone-China Friendship Association, Koroma, National Administrative Secretary of the National People's Congress, Kamara, and more than 10 ministers and deputy ministers, as well as more than 300 people from all walks of life, and Chinese Ambassador to Sierra Leone, Gao Jianzhong, attended.

	After the meeting, Sheriff cut the ribbon for the opening ceremony of the "Exhibition of Achievements and Books of the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of New China" held by the Chinese Embassy in Sierra Leone.

	•	The Ministry of Culture and Information of Iraq and the Chinese Embassy in Iraq jointly held an exhibition of achievements in the construction of New China on September 27 in Baghdad to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	•	The "Welcome to the 40th Spring of China" photo exhibition held by the Chinese Embassy in Chad opened on September 25 at the Youth Cultural Center in N'Djamena, the capital of Chad.

	•	The Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition opened on September 28 in the afternoon in Lagos, the capital of Nigeria. Nigerian Minister of Information and Culture, Momoh, said in his opening speech that China and Nigeria are "very friendly countries" that cooperate in the fields of culture, education, medicine, science and technology, and all other fields.

	He hoped that this exhibition would promote the sharing of new skills, new technologies, and new ideas between the two peoples.

	•	The Chilean-Chinese Cultural Association held a lecture on October 3 in the Honor Hall of the University of Chile in the capital, Santiago, to warmly celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	Professor Sanchez of the University of Chile gave a report titled "China's March Towards the Year 2000," followed by Chinese Ambassador to Chile, Huang Shikang, introducing the achievements made by New China over the past 40 years to the attendees.

	Previously, the Santiago Chinese Association and the Chilean-Chinese community held a joint gala with embassy staff on October 1.

	Our reporter Guo Weicheng reported from Mexico City: On October 1, various sectors of the Mexican people gathered in front of the Chinese Embassy in Mexico to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to support China in suppressing the counter-revolutionary riots.

	Engineer Jimenez read an open letter "To the People, Communist Party, and Government of China" on behalf of the gathering, highly praising the great historical significance of the Chinese revolution. The open letter pointed out, "The road of socialist construction in China has made a special contribution to the development of Marxism-Leninism in our era and to the transition of humanity from the realm of necessity to the realm of freedom." "The suppression of the counter-revolutionary riots in China is a decisive measure taken to defend the victory of the revolution and construction, and adhering to the four cardinal principles is the path to victory for the Chinese people." Some friendly people who have studied and visited in China expressed that they voluntarily participated in the gathering on behalf of people from all walks of life to express their sympathy and support for the Chinese revolution; and condemn the interference and subversive activities of imperialist forces against the Chinese revolutionary cause.

### 一些国家举行活动  庆祝我国庆40周年
郭伟成
1989-10-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    一些国家举行活动
    庆祝我国庆40周年
    本报综合新华社消息　一些国家举行活动，庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    ▲匈牙利匈中友协9月29日在布达佩斯举行报告会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年。匈中友协主席、汉学家塔洛什·鲍尔瑙在会上介绍了匈中两国40年来各阶段的不同特点和两国关系的发展情况。他表示匈中友协将为维护和增进两国友谊继续作出贡献。
    中国驻匈牙利大使朱安康出席了报告会。
    ▲尼泊尔首相什雷斯塔出席尼中友协9月30日举行的庆祝集会，并发表讲话说，尼中两国之间的友好关系是建立在和平共处五项原则基础上的，这种友好关系必将得到进一步的发展。
    ▲菲律宾菲中了解协会10月1日举行庆祝招待会。来自菲律宾各界的华人代表100多人参加了招待会。菲律宾参议院主席霍维托·萨隆加出席了招待会并发表了讲话。他在讲话中回顾了中国40年来取得的伟大成就。他说，他为中国目前正在实行的改革开放政策感到非常高兴。他希望菲中两国之间的友谊继续得到发展。
    ▲斯里兰卡斯中友协在9月30日举行的庆祝集会上通过一项决议说，中国近年来取得了举世公认的伟大成就。“中国人民的生活得到了空前的改善，中国成功地解决了十多亿人口的吃饭问题”。
    决议表示相信中国有能力处理好平暴后中国的事情。
    ▲塞拉利昂全国人民大会党和塞中友协9月29日在首都弗里敦友谊大楼举行盛大群众集会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    塞拉利昂第二副总统谢里夫、外交部长兼塞中友协主席科罗马、大会党全国行政书记卡马拉和10多位正、副部长、各界人士以及中国驻塞拉利昂大使高建中等300多人出席。
    大会结束后，谢里夫为中国驻塞拉利昂大使馆举行的“新中国成立40周年成就图片和图书展览”开幕式剪彩。
    ▲伊拉克文化和新闻部和中国驻伊拉克使馆，为庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年，联合举办的新中国建设成就图片展览于9月27日在巴格达开幕。
    ▲中国驻乍得使馆举办的
  “迎来中国40年春”图片展览于9月25日在乍得首都恩贾梅纳青年文化之家开幕。
    ▲中国工艺美术展览9月28日下午在尼日利亚首都拉各斯开幕。尼日利亚新闻和文化部长莫莫在开幕式的讲话中说，中国和尼日利亚是在文化、教育、医药、科学技术和各个领域进行合作的“非常友好的国家”。
    他希望这次展览能促使两国人民互相共享新工艺、新技术和新思想。
    ▲智利智中文化协会10月3日晚在首都圣地亚哥的智利大学荣誉厅举行报告会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    智利大学教授桑切斯在会上作了题为《中华人民共和国奔向2000年》的报告，接着中国驻智利大使黄士康向与会者介绍了新中国成立40年来所取得的成就。
    在此之前，圣地亚哥中华会馆和旅智华裔组织在10月1日同使馆馆员一起举行了联欢晚会。
    本报墨西哥城电　记者郭伟成报道：墨西哥各界群众10月1日在中国驻墨西哥大使馆门前集会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年，声援中国平息反革命暴乱。
    工程师希梅内斯代表集会群众宣读了“致中国人民、中国共产党、中国政府”的公开信，高度赞扬中国革命的伟大历史意义。公开信指出，“中国进行社会主义建设的道路为发展我们时代的马列主义，为使人类从必然王国向自由王国的过渡作出了特殊的贡献。”“中国平息反革命暴乱是为保卫革命与建设的胜利而采取的断然措施，坚持四项基本原则，是中国人民的胜利之路。”一些在中国学习、访问过的友好人士表示，他们代表各阶层人士自愿参加集会，是为了表明他们对中国革命的同情与支持；谴责帝国主义势力对中国革命事业的干涉与颠覆行径。
