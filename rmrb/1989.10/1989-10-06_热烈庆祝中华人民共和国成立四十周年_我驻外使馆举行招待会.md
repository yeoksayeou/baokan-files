Title: Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1989-10-06
Edition: 3

Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Our Embassies Abroad Hold Receptions

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 5 - A compilation of reports from our correspondents in Asia and Africa: Chinese ambassadors in 11 countries in Asia and Africa recently held receptions to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Chinese Ambassador to Zambia Zhou Mingji held a reception on October 3. Several ministers and friends from various circles, totaling over 400 people, attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Sierra Leone Gao Jianzhong held a reception on October 2. Five ministers and over 300 people from various circles attended the reception.

Chinese Chargé d'Affaires to Djibouti Ma Zhixue held a reception on October 1. Government high-level officials and people from various circles, totaling over 140 people, attended the reception.

Chinese Chargé d'Affaires to Seychelles Zhang Tongsheng held a reception on the evening of September 30. Five ministers, representatives of the Chinese community, and others, totaling 130 people, attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Gabon An Fengshi held a reception on September 30. Gabonese Democratic Party Political Bureau Secretary Oge and 280 others attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Rwanda Tian Yimin held a reception on September 29. Three ministers and over 220 friends from various circles attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Guinea-Bissau Shi Wushan held a reception on September 29. Over 110 government high-level officials and others attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Burundi Wang Jianbang held a reception on September 29. Three ministers and over 260 military and political high-level officials attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Congo Wu Shunyu held a reception on September 29. Congolese Labor Party Central Political Bureau member Gabriel Oba Apunu and 600 others attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Oman Zang Shixiong held a reception on October 1. Over 240 government officials and people from various circles attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Emirates Huang Zhen held a reception on October 1. Over 270 government high-level officials and friends from various circles attended the reception.

### 热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立四十周年  我驻外使馆举行招待会

1989-10-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立四十周年
    我驻外使馆举行招待会
    新华社北京10月5日电　综合本社驻亚洲和非洲地区记者报道，中国驻亚洲和非洲11个国家的使节近日举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    中国驻赞比亚大使周明基10月3日举行招待会，一些部长以及各界朋友共400多人出席了招待会。
    中国驻塞拉里昂大使高建中10月2日举行招待会，5名部长和各界人士300多人参加了招待会。
    中国驻吉布提大使馆临时代办马志学10月1日举行招待会，出席招待会的有政府高级官员和各界人士共140多人。
    中国驻塞舌尔大使馆临时代办张同生9月30日晚举行招待会，5名部长、华侨代表等共130人出席了招待会。
    中国驻加蓬大使安峰石9月30日举行招待会，加蓬民主党政治执行书记奥热等280人出席了招待会。
    中国驻卢旺达大使田逸民9月29日举行招待会，3名部长及各界朋友220人出席了招待会。
    中国驻几内亚比绍大使石午山9月29日举行招待会，政府高级官员等110多人出席了招待会。
    中国驻布隆迪大使王建邦9月29日举行招待会，3名部长和军政高级官员等260多人出席了招待会。
    中国驻刚果大使吴顺豫9月29日举行招待会，刚果劳动党中央政治局委员加布里埃尔·奥巴·阿普努等600人出席了招待会。
    中国驻阿曼国大使臧士雄10月1日举行招待会，政府官员和各界人士240多人出席了招待会。
    中国驻阿联酋大使黄振10月1日举行招待会，出席招待会的有政府高级官员和各界朋友270多人。
