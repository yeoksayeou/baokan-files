Title: The Opening of the Early Childhood Education Symposium
Author: Gōng Yǒngquán
Date: 1989-10-25
Edition: 2

The six-day International Symposium on Early Childhood Education opened today at Nanjing Normal University. This symposium is held to commemorate the 10th anniversary of the International Year of the Child and the 10th anniversary of the United Nations Children's Fund's assistance to China. Scholars from mainland China, Taiwan, Hong Kong, as well as the United States, Britain, Australia, Japan, and the Soviet Union, totaling 145 participants, attended the symposium. The main topic of discussion is the reform of kindergarten curricula. Vice Minister of the State Education Commission Liu Bin and officials from the United Nations Children's Fund's Beijing office attended the opening ceremony.

### 幼教研讨会开幕
龚永泉
1989-10-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　幼教研讨会开幕
    本报南京10月24日电　记者龚永泉报道：为期6天的幼儿教育国际研讨会今天在南京师范大学开幕，这是为纪念国际儿童年10周年和联合国儿童基金会援华10周年而召开的。来自中国大陆、中国台湾、香港地区及美国、英国、澳大利亚、日本、苏联等国的共145名学者出席研讨会，议题是幼儿园课程改革。国家教委副主任柳斌和联合国儿童基金会驻北京办事处官员出席了开幕式。
