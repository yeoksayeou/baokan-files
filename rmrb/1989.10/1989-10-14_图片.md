Title: A Japanese Kindergarten Opens a Program Called "Nurturing by Mother Earth"
Author:
Date: 1989-10-14
Edition: 7

A Japanese kindergarten has launched an educational program titled "Nurturing by Mother Earth." Every day, children are arranged to play in a specially designed mud pool for one hour, allowing them to experience nature firsthand from a young age.
