Title: Jiang Zemin Meets with North Korean Comrades
Author:
Date: 1989-10-01
Edition: 1

    Jiang Zemin Meets with North Korean Comrades

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30 — This afternoon, CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin met with a North Korean party and government delegation led by Kim Jong-yong, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Vice Chairman of the State Affairs Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, at the Great Hall of the People. He extended a warm welcome to them for coming to participate in the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 江泽民会见朝鲜同志

1989-10-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民会见朝鲜同志
    新华社北京9月30日电　中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午在人民大会堂会见了由朝鲜劳动党中央政治局委员、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国家副主席李钟玉率领的朝鲜党政代表团，对他们前来参加中华人民共和国成立40周年庆祝活动表示热烈欢迎。
