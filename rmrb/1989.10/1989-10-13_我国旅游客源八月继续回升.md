Title: China's Tourism Market Continues to Rebound in August
Author:
Date: 1989-10-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 12th.

According to tourism statistics released by the State Tourism Administration, the tourism market in China continued to rebound in August. The total number of inbound tourists reached more than 70% of the same period last year, with the number of foreign tourists reaching more than 60%. The number of organized tourists received nationwide and the number of tourists received by tourism departments increased by more than 30% compared to July.

In August, the total number of inbound tourists was 2,095,300.

### 我国旅游客源八月继续回升

1989-10-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    我国旅游客源八月继续回升
    新华社北京10月12日电　据国家旅游局提供的旅游统计快讯，8月份，我国旅游客源市场继续回升，来华旅游入境总人数已达去年同期的7成多，外国游客人数达6成多。全国有组织接待人数、旅游部门接待人数比7月份增长30％以上。
    8月份，来华旅游入境总人数为209.53万人。
