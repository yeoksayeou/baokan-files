Title: Capital Holds Gala to Celebrate 40th Anniversary of National Founding
Author: Wang Chuanguo
Date: 1989-10-01
Edition: 2

On the evening of September 30, Party and state leaders Jiang Zemin, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, Wan Li, and other leaders gathered with people from all walks of life in the capital at the Great Hall of the People to watch a celebratory performance marking the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Attending the gala were also a group of visiting foreign dignitaries and foreign envoys stationed in China.
