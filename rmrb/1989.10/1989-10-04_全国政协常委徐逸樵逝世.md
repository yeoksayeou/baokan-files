Title: Former Nationalist Party Official and NPC Standing Committee Member Xu Yiqing Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing,

The Standing Committee Member of the 7th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Xu Yiqing, passed away due to illness in Beijing on September 30, 1989, at the age of 89.

Xu Yiqing previously served as the Secretary-General of the 20th Army Group of the Nationalist Party, the Director of the Training Department of the Central Organization Department of the Nationalist Party, and the Advisor to the National Government's Representative Team in Japan. After the founding of New China, he resigned from his Nationalist Party positions and conducted historical research in Japan. In 1978, he returned to China to settle permanently and served as a Standing Committee Member of the 5th and 6th National Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 全国政协常委徐逸樵逝世

1989-10-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    全国政协常委徐逸樵逝世
    新华社北京电　政协第七届全国委员会常务委员徐逸樵同志，因病于1989年9月30日在北京逝世，终年89岁。
    徐逸樵曾任国民党部队第二十集团军秘书长，国民党中央组织部训练处长，国民政府驻日本代表团顾问。新中国成立后，他辞去国民党的职务，在日本从事历史研究，1978年回国定居，历任第五、六届全国政协常委。
