Title: North and South Korea Hold Third Sports Talks
Author: Zhū Kèchuān
Date: 1989-10-22
Edition: 4

North and South Korea Hold Third Sports Talks

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Panmunjom, October 20th – (Reporter Zhū Kèchuān) The North and South Korean sports delegations held their third round of talks today in Panmunjom regarding the formation of a unified team to participate in the 11th Asian Games. Both sides reached a consensus on the issue of the unified team's flag. The future unified team's flag will be a white background with a light blue map of the Korean Peninsula, without any textual markings.

	Regarding the method of forming the team, both sides have agreed on the basic principle of selecting the best athletes to join the unified team and avoiding mutual distrust and misunderstandings during the selection process.

### 朝鲜北南方举行第三次体育会谈
朱克川
1989-10-22
第4版(综合)
专栏：

　　朝鲜北南方举行第三次体育会谈
    据新华社板门店10月20日电　（记者朱克川）朝鲜北方和南方的体育代表团今天在板门店举行的有关组成统一队参加第十一届亚运会的第三次会谈中，双方比较顺利地就统一队的队旗问题取得了一致意见。未来的统一队队旗将是一面在白色的底上印有天蓝色朝鲜半岛地图的旗帜，上面没有任何文字标记。
    关于组队方法问题，双方只有在选拔最优秀运动员参加统一队和在选拔赛中避免造成互不信任和误会这一基本原则取得了一致意见。
