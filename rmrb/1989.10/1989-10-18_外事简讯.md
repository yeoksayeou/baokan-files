Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-10-18
Edition: 2

Following China's announcement to suspend diplomatic relations with Liberia, Ambassador Cao Yuanxin returned to Beijing by plane on the 16th. Assistant Foreign Minister Yang Fuchang and others welcomed him at the airport.

On the 16th, Wang Shoudao, a standing committee member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and president of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, met with Douglas MacArthur, chairman of the American Bear Panda Automobile Company, and his delegation at the Great Hall of the People.

On the 16th, Polish Ambassador Woszniak held a luncheon at the embassy to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Poland and China. Foreign Minister Qian Qichen and others were invited to attend.

On the 16th, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lei Jieqiong met with the Mongolian Peace Protection Committee delegation led by Vice Chairman Udval at the Great Hall of the People. This is the first time in nearly 30 years that the Mongolian Peace Committee has sent a delegation to China, mainly to rebuild friendly relations between the two countries' peace organizations.

At the invitation of the Fatherland Front of Bulgaria, a delegation of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, led by Vice Chairman Ma Wenrui, departed on the 16th for a one-week friendly visit to Bulgaria.

On the 16th, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Xi Zhongxun met and hosted a banquet for the delegation of the Malian People's Democratic Union, led by Central Executive Committee member and economic secretary Dianka Diaguitai. Department head of the International Liaison Department Zhu Liang attended the meeting.

On the 16th, State Councilor and Mayor of Beijing Chen Xitong met with the delegation of the Yugoslavian Economic Review, led by editor-in-chief Georgijevich, in Beijing. Chen Xitong emphasized to the guests the achievements made by Beijing's enterprises, especially state-owned factories, in implementing the contract responsibility system.

### 外事简讯

1989-10-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲中国宣布中止同利比里亚外交关系后，曹元欣大使16日乘飞机回到北京。外交部部长助理杨福昌等到机场迎接。
    ▲中顾委常委、中国国际友好联络会会长王首道16日在人民大会堂会见了美国熊猫汽车公司董事长道格拉斯·麦克阿瑟一行。
    ▲波兰驻华大使沃希尼亚克16日在大使馆举行午宴，庆祝波中建交40周年。钱其琛外长等应邀出席。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长雷洁琼16日在人民大会堂会见了以副主席乌德瓦尔为团长的蒙古保卫和平委员会访华团。这次是蒙古和委会时隔近30年后首次派团访华，主要目的是重建两国和平组织的友好关系。
    ▲应保加利亚祖国阵线邀请，以马文瑞副主席为团长的全国政协代表团于16日离京前往保加利亚进行为期1周的友好访问。
    ▲人大常委会副委员长习仲勋16日在人民大会堂会见并宴请由中央执行局成员、经济书记迪安卡·迪亚吉泰率领的马里人民民主联盟休假团。中联部部长朱良参加了会见。
    ▲国务委员兼北京市市长陈希同16日在北京会见了南斯拉夫《经济评论》报总编辑乔尔杰维奇一行。陈希同着重向客人们介绍了北京市企业尤其是国营工厂实行承包制所取得的成绩。
