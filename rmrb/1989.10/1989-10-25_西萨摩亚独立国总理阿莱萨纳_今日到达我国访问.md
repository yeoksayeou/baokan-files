Title: Prime Minister of the Independent State of Western Samoa, Aleisana, Arrives in China Today
Author:
Date: 1989-10-25
Edition: 1

At the invitation of Premier Li Peng, Tofilau Eti Alesana, Prime Minister of the South Pacific island nation of Western Samoa, will arrive in China today, October 25, for an official friendly visit.

Alesana was born in Eastern Samoa in 1924. In his early years, he studied in both Eastern and Western Samoa and later served in the military. From 1949 to 1957, he engaged in commerce and agriculture. In 1957, he entered the parliament. In 1959, he served as the Minister of Health. From 1982 to 1985, he served as the Prime Minister. In 1988, his Human Rights Protection Party won the election, and he once again took office as the Prime Minister.

Since taking office as Prime Minister, Alesana has advocated for strengthening friendly exchanges with China and has emphasized the importance of developing bilateral economic and technological cooperation with China.

Alesana is married, and his wife is of Chinese descent. He has 14 children.

### 西萨摩亚独立国总理阿莱萨纳  今日到达我国访问

1989-10-25
第1版(要闻)
专栏：人物介绍

　　西萨摩亚独立国总理阿莱萨纳今日到达我国访问
    应李鹏总理的邀请，南太平洋岛国——西萨摩亚独立国总理托菲劳·埃蒂·阿莱萨纳将于10月25日对我国进行正式友好访问。
    阿莱萨纳1924年生于东萨摩亚。早年曾在东萨摩亚和西萨摩亚读书，后在军队服役。1949年至1957年经商并从事种植业。1957年后进入议会。1959年任卫生部长。1982年至1985年任总理。1988年，以他为领袖的人权保护党在大选中获胜后，再次出任总理。
    阿莱萨纳出任总理后，主张与我国加强友好往来，重视发展同中国的双边经济技术合作。
    阿莱萨纳总理已婚，妻子为华裔，他有14个孩子。（新华社发）（附图片）
