Title: Foreign Ministry Spokesperson: Solving the Hong Kong Issue is a Matter for China and Britain; Discussing It in Any International Forum is Inappropriate
Author:
Date: 1989-10-19
Edition: 1

	Foreign Ministry Spokesperson: Solving the Hong Kong Issue is a Matter for China and Britain; Discussing It in Any International Forum is Inappropriate

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 18th - The Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesperson stated today that solving the Hong Kong issue is entirely a matter between China and Britain. We firmly oppose the British side's attempt to internationalize the Hong Kong issue.

	The spokesperson made these remarks in response to a reporter's question. A reporter asked: According to the Hong Kong "Kuai Bao" (Fast News), the British Foreign Ministry spokesperson stated that the Commonwealth Heads of Government Meeting, which opened today in Kuala Lumpur, will discuss the Hong Kong issue informally. What is your comment on this? The spokesperson said that implementing the Sino-British Joint Declaration to solve the Hong Kong issue is entirely a matter between China and Britain. China will unswervingly fulfill all the obligations promised in the Sino-British Joint Declaration on the Hong Kong issue. Discussing the Hong Kong issue, whether formally or informally, in any international forum is inappropriate.

### 外交部发言人指出  解决香港问题是中英两国的事  在任何国际场合讨论都不适宜

1989-10-19
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    外交部发言人指出
    解决香港问题是中英两国的事  在任何国际场合讨论都不适宜
    新华社北京10月18日电　中国外交部发言人今天在这里指出，解决香港问题完全是中英两国之间的事。我们坚决反对英方将香港问题国际化的企图。
    发言人是在回答记者提问时说这番话的。有记者问：据香港《快报》报道，英国外交部发言人表示，今天在吉隆坡开幕的英联邦政府首脑会议将非正式讨论香港问题。对此，你有何评论？这位发言人说贯彻执行中英联合声明以解决香港问题，这完全是中英两国之间的事。中国将坚定不移地履行中英关于香港问题联合声明中所承诺的各项义务。在任何国际场合，不管是正式还是非正式地讨论香港问题，都是不适宜的。
