Title: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress is drafting relevant laws to institutionalize the supervision of the "One Government and Two Courts"
Author:
Date: 1989-10-27
Edition: 2

The Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress has included the formulation of relevant laws on supervision in its legislative plan. The Office of the Standing Committee is currently expediting the drafting work to legalize and institutionalize the supervision of the National People's Congress and its Standing Committee over the "One Government and Two Courts."

It is reported that, in response to proposals from National People's Congress deputies and the needs of legal system construction, the National People's Congress and its Standing Committee, along with the State Council and other relevant departments, are accelerating the formulation of a batch of laws and regulations. These laws and regulations include: "Temporary Regulations on the Declaration of Property and Income by State Administrative Personnel," "Supervision Regulations," "Decision of the State Council on the Detention and Education of Prostitutes and Their Clients," "Law on the Protection of Women," "Regulations on the Management of Fixed Asset Investment," "Banking Law," "Price Law," "Law on Foreign Trade," "Regulations on Rural Collective Enterprises," "Regulations on Urban Collective Enterprises," and "Regulations on Labor Protection," among others.

### 人大常委会正起草有关法律对“一府两院”监督将制度化

1989-10-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　人大常委会正起草有关法律对“一府两院”监督将制度化
    新华社北京10月26日电  七届全国人大常委会现已把制定有关监督方面的法律列入立法规划，全国人大常委会办公厅正在抓紧进行起草工作，以使人大及其常委会对“一府两院”的监督工作法律化、制度化。 
    据悉，根据全国人大代表的提案和法制建设的需要，人大及其常委会和国务院等有关方面正在加紧制定一批法律、法规。这些法律、法规包括：《国家行政机关工作人员申报财产和收入的暂行规定》、《监察条例》、《国务院关于收容教育卖淫嫖娼人员的决定》、《妇女保护法》、《固定资产投资管理条例》、《银行法》、《价格法》、《对外贸易法》、《乡村集体企业条例》、《城镇集体企业条例》、《劳动保护条例》等。
