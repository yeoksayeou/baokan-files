	Palestinian State and U.S. Ambassadors in Tunisia Discuss Methods to Develop Bilateral Dialogue

	BEIJING, Oct. 22 (Xinhua) -- The Palestinian State and the U.S. ambassadors in Tunisia held talks last night to discuss the necessity of advancing Palestinian-U.S. dialogue and the escalating repression by Israel in the occupied territories.

	According to the Palestinian News Agency, the talks between Palestinian Ambassador Barawi and U.S. Ambassador Pelletreau were conducted within the framework of dialogue between the U.S. and the PLO. The two ambassadors explored methods and possibilities for developing Palestinian-U.S. dialogue to advance the Middle East peace process.

	The two ambassadors also discussed Israel's brutal repression of residents in the occupied territories, particularly in the village of Beit Sahur on the West Bank. Israeli forces declared the village a "military zone," arbitrarily arresting local residents and confiscating or looting their property. The Palestinian ambassador pointed out that the U.S. should bear responsibility for the dangerous situation created by Israel in the occupied territories. He emphasized the urgency of taking action to end this situation.

### 巴勒斯坦国美国驻突大使会谈  讨论发展双方对话的方法

1989-10-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　巴勒斯坦国美国驻突大使会谈
　　讨论发展双方对话的方法
    新华社突尼斯十月二十二日电　巴勒斯坦国和美国驻突尼斯大使昨天晚上在这里举行会谈，讨论推进巴、美对话的必要性和以色列在被占领土上镇压居民的行径不断升级问题。
    据巴勒斯坦通讯社报道，巴大使巴拉维和美大使佩莱特鲁之间的会谈是在美国和巴解组织之间对话范畴内进行的。两位大使研讨了为中东和平进程而发展巴、美对话的方法和可能性。
    两国大使还讨论了以色列在被占领土，尤其在约旦河西岸的贝伊特·萨乌尔村镇压抗税居民的野蛮行径。以军把该村宣布为“军事禁区”，任意逮捕当地居民，没收、抢劫他们的财产。巴大使指出，美国应对以色列在被占领土制造的危险局势负责。他强调了采取行动结束这种状态的紧迫性。
