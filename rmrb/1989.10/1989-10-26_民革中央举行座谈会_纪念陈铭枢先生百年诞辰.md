Title: The Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association Holds a Symposium to Commemorate the 100th Anniversary of Mr. Chen Mingxun's Birth
Author: Yuan Jianda
Date: 1989-10-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 25th.

The Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association held a symposium this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People to commemorate the 100th anniversary of the birth of the renowned patriot and democrat Chen Mingxun.

Today's commemorative event was presided over by Qu Wu, Honorary Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association. Ding Guangen, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and a member of the Secretariat, attended the meeting. Hab Comrade Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, expressed deep remembrance to Mr. Chen Mingxun on behalf of the CPC Central Committee.

### 民革中央举行座谈会  纪念陈铭枢先生百年诞辰
袁建达
1989-10-26
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　民革中央举行座谈会
　　纪念陈铭枢先生百年诞辰
    本报北京10月25日讯　记者袁建达报道：民革中央今天下午在人民大会堂举行座谈会，纪念著名爱国民主人士陈铭枢先生诞辰100周年。
    今天的纪念活动由民革中央名誉主席屈武主持。中共中央政治局候补委员、书记处书记丁关根出席了会议。全国人大常委会副委员长习仲勋代表中共中央向陈铭枢先生表示深切的怀念。
