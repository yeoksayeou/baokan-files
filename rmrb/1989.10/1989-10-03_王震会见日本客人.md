Title: Wang Zhen Meets with Japanese Guests
Author: Xǔ Yì Chún
Date: 1989-10-03
Edition: 2

Wang Zhen Meets with Japanese Guests

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 2 (Reporter Xu Yichun) - Vice President of the State and Honorary President of the China-Japan Friendship Association Wang Zhen met with a delegation led by Japanese Socialist Party Senator and President of the Japan Agricultural, Forestry, and Fisheries Exchange Association Hachihata Masashi at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse this afternoon.

Wang Zhen, on behalf of the Chinese people, extended a warm welcome to Hachihata Masashi and his delegation. He praised Hachihata Masashi for his contributions to the development of Sino-Japanese friendship and agricultural technology exchange between the two countries.

Hachihata Masashi expressed that Japan should strengthen exchanges with China in agricultural technology, especially in grain yield technology.

The 84-year-old Hachihata Masashi is an old friend of the Chinese people. To commend Hachihata Masashi's contributions to cooperation between the two countries, the Ministry of Agriculture awarded him the "International Agricultural Cooperation Award."

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 2 - Vice President of the State and Honorary President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Wang Zhen met with Kumagai Taiichiro, President of Kumagai Group Corporation, here today. After the meeting, Wang Zhen hosted a banquet for Kumagai Taiichiro and his delegation.

### 王震会见日本客人
许忆纯
1989-10-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    王震会见日本客人
    新华社北京10月2日电　（记者许忆纯）国家副主席、中日友好协会名誉会长王震今天下午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了日本社会党参议员、日本农林水产交流协会会长八百板正率领的日中农交代表团一行。
    王震代表中国人民对八百板正一行表示欢迎。他赞扬了八百板正为发展中日友好和两国农业技术交流事业所作出的贡献。
    八百板正表示，日本应加强同中国在农业技术，特别是粮食增产技术方面的交流。
    84岁的八百板正是中国人民的老朋友。为表彰八百板正在两国合作中作出的贡献，农业部授予他“国际农业合作奖”。
    新华社北京10月2日电  国家副主席、中国国际友好联络会名誉会长王震今天在这里会见了日本熊谷组株式会社社长熊谷太一郎。会见后，王震设宴招待了熊谷太一郎一行。
