Title: Wang Zhen Meets with Chairman of Fulu Company
Author:
Date: 1989-10-05
Edition: 2

Vice Chairman of the State, Honorary Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Wang Zhen met with and hosted a dinner for David Tappan, Chairman of the American Fulu Company, who was invited to visit China, this evening at the Great Hall of the People.

State Councilor Zhu Qihua attended the meeting and dinner.

### 王震会见福陆公司董事长

1989-10-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    王震会见福陆公司董事长
    新华社北京10月4日电  国家副主席、中国国际友好联络会名誉会长王震今晚在人民大会堂会见并宴请了应邀来访的美国福陆公司董事长大卫·塔潘一行。
    国务委员邹家华等参加了会见和晚宴。
