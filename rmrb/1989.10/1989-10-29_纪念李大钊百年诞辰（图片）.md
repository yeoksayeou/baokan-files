Commemorating the 100th Anniversary of Li Dazhao's Birth

	Left top image: Li Dazhao in 1905 while studying at Yongping Prefecture School (now Tangshan No. 1 Middle School).
	Right top image: In February 1914, Li Dazhao in Tokyo, Japan. He went to Japan to study in 1913 with the help of friends.
	Right bottom image: On April 28, 1927, Comrade Li Dazhao on the enemy's execution ground. After being captured, Li Dazhao remained steadfast and unyielding, displaying the noble character of a Communist. He delivered a speech on the execution ground, proclaiming the truth that communism will inevitably triumph.
	Top image: Li Dazhao (front row, fifth from the left) with the editorial staff of the "Morning Bell" newspaper. In 1916, Li Dazhao served as the chief editor of the "Morning Bell" newspaper in Beijing. In the inaugural issue, he published the article "The Mission of the Morning Bell," calling on the youth to break free from the shackles of old forces and struggle for an "ideal China."

Xinhua News Agency

### 纪念李大钊百年诞辰（图片）

1989-10-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　纪念李大钊百年诞辰
    左上图：李大钊1905年在永平府中学堂（今唐山一中）读书时留影。
    右上图：1914年2月，李大钊在日本东京。他是在朋友的资助下，于1913年东渡日本留学的。
    右下图：1927年4月28日，李大钊同志在敌人的刑场上。李大钊被捕后，始终坚贞不屈，大义凛然，表现了共产党人的崇高气节。他在刑场上发表了演讲，宣传共产主义必定胜利的真理。
    上图：李大钊（前排左五）与《晨钟报》编辑部人员合影。1916年，李大钊任北京《晨钟报》总编辑。他在创刊号上发表《晨钟之使命》一文，号召青年冲破旧势力的束缚，为“索我理想之中华”而斗争。　新华社稿
