Title: Xingguo Hosts "Biographical Exhibition of Xingguo Generals"
Author: Jing Hong
Date: 1989-10-07
Page: 4

Xingguo County in Jiangxi Province recently held a "Biographical Exhibition of Xingguo Generals." This exhibition showcased the lives of 54 generals from Xingguo who were awarded ranks between 1955 and 1965, including two upper generals, five lieutenant generals, and forty-seven major generals. The exhibition featured over 380 historical artifacts and photographs.

### 兴国举办“兴国籍将军生平展”
京红
1989-10-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    兴国举办“兴国籍将军生平展”
    本报讯　江西省兴国县最近举办“兴国籍将军生平展”。这次展览展出了1955年至1965年被授衔的二名上将、五名中将、四十七名少将等54名兴国籍将军的生平，共展出历史文物照片380余件。　　　（京红）
