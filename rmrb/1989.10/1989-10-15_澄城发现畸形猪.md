Title: Abnormal Pig Found in Chengcheng
Author:
Date: 1989-10-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, October 14th.

A farmer named Liu Yangyu from Chengcheng County's Tiaohu Township in Shaanxi Province had a sow that gave birth to six piglets on May 16 this year. Among them, one piglet had six legs, two tails, two buttocks, and both male and female reproductive organs. According to Liu Yangyu, this abnormal pig is developing normally.

### 澄城发现畸形猪

1989-10-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    澄城发现畸形猪
    新华社西安10月14日电　陕西省澄城县醍醐乡醍醐村农民刘羊育，家里饲养的一只母猪，今年5月16日一胎生下6只猪崽。其中一只猪崽长有6条腿，2条尾巴，2个屁股和一公一母两副生殖器官。据刘羊育说，这只怪猪发育正常。
