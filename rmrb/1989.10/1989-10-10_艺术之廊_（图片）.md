Title: Art Gallery
Author: Zhu Genhua
Date: 1989-10-10
Edition: 7

The "Art Gallery" is a special column in the international section of the People's Daily, showcasing various forms of art from around the world. This column aims to provide readers with a window into the diverse and rich artistic expressions that exist globally. Through this platform, we hope to foster cultural exchange and understanding, celebrating the beauty and creativity that art brings to our lives.
