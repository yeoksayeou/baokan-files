Title: The Polish People's Army Art Troupe's First Performance in Beijing
Author: Chen Yuan
Date: 1989-10-19
Edition: 2

The Polish People's Army Art Troupe, led by Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Polish People's Army, Major General Zdzisław Rdzanek, with over 100 members, performed at the China Theatre in Beijing this evening at the invitation of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Members of the Central Military Commission, including Hong Xuezhi, Qin Jiwei, and Chi Haotian, along with over 3,000 military personnel and civilians from the capital, enjoyed the performance.

The Polish People's Army Central Art Troupe, originally known as the Polish People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, has a history of 46 years. In 1955, they visited China and received high praise.

### 波兰人民军艺术团在京首演
陈原
1989-10-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    波兰人民军艺术团在京首演
    本报北京10月18日讯　记者陈原报道：应中国人民解放军总政治部邀请，由波兰人民军总政治部副主任兹基斯瓦夫·罗兹毕茨基少将率领的人民军中央艺术团100余人，今晚在北京的中国剧院举行了首场演出。中央军委领导洪学智、秦基伟、迟浩田等和首都3000余军民欣赏了表演。
    波兰人民军中央艺术团原名波兰人民军歌舞团，已有46年的历史，1955年曾来华访问，颇受好评。
