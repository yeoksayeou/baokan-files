Title: Ethiopian Rebel Forces Launch Offensive
Author:
Date: 1989-10-20
Edition: 3

Ethiopian rebel forces launch offensive
Mengistu urges no division of the motherland

NEW DELHI, Oct. 18 (Xinhua) - Ethiopian President Mengistu Hailemariam delivered a nationwide radio and television address tonight, warning the people that the country faces the danger of disintegration.

Mengistu said the people have two choices: either allow the Tigray People's Liberation Front to conspire to split off the northern region of Eritrea and dismember Ethiopia, or achieve a thorough strategic victory over the anti-government forces.

Mengistu made the remarks after the Tigray People's Liberation Front claimed that it had launched an offensive against government troops in the northern region bordering Tigray Province and achieved significant victories.

He firmly believed that the People's Democratic Republic of Ethiopia includes all parts of society, of course, not excluding Tigray.

He said that the unity, peace, and social welfare of the Ethiopian people are more important than anything else and should be maintained at all costs.

### 埃塞俄比亚反政府武装发动攻势  门格斯图呼吁不要分裂祖国

1989-10-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    埃塞俄比亚反政府武装发动攻势
    门格斯图呼吁不要分裂祖国
    新华社亚的斯亚贝巴10月18日电　埃塞俄比亚总统门格斯图今天晚上发表全国广播电视讲话，向人民发出警告：祖国有分裂的危险。
    门格斯图说，摆在人民面前有两种选择，要么允许提格雷人民解放阵线通过阴谋手段把厄立特里亚北部地区分裂出去，肢解埃塞俄比亚；要么取得对反政府武装的彻底的战略性胜利。
    门格斯图是在反政府武装提格雷人民解放阵线声称，该组织在与提格雷省接壤的北部地区向政府军发动进攻，并取得重大胜利之后发表上述讲话的。
    他坚决认为，埃塞俄比亚人民民主共和国包括全社会的各个部分，当然不把提格雷排斥在外。
    他说，埃塞俄比亚人民的团结、和平和社会福利比其他任何东西更重要，应不惜一切代价进行维护。
