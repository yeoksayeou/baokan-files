Title: Commonwealth Heads of Government Meeting Elects New Secretary-General
Author:
Date: 1989-10-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 19th.

The Commonwealth Heads of Government Meeting, currently being held in Kuala Lumpur, the capital of Malaysia, held a secret ballot yesterday. The result was that Nigeria's Anyaoku defeated former Australian Prime Minister Malcolm Fraser to become the next Secretary-General of the Commonwealth.

Shridath Ramphal, who has held the position of Secretary-General of the Commonwealth for fourteen years, is about to retire. Anyaoku, who is currently fifty-six years old, has served as Nigeria's Foreign Minister. He will officially take office next July, with a term of five years.

### 英联邦首脑会议选出新秘书长

1989-10-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英联邦首脑会议选出新秘书长
    新华社北京十月十九日电　吉隆坡消息：正在马来西亚首都吉隆坡召开的英联邦国家首脑会议昨天举行秘密投票，结果是尼日利亚的安约库击败澳大利亚前总理马尔科姆·弗雷泽，当选为下一任英联邦秘书长。
    已经担任英联邦秘书长之职达十四年之久的什里达思·法兰尔即将退休。安约库，现年五十六岁，曾任尼日利亚外长。他将于明年七月份正式就任，任期五年。
