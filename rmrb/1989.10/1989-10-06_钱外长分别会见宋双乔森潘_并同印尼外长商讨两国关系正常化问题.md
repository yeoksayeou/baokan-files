Title: Chinese Foreign Minister Meets with Son Sann and Khieu Samphan and Discusses Normalization of Relations with Indonesian Foreign Minister
Author: Yao Haiquan
Date: 1989-10-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, United Nations, October 4th.

Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met separately with Son Sann, the Prime Minister of the Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea (CGDK), and Khieu Samphan, the Vice-Chairman of the CGDK, on today and yesterday. Qian exchanged views with them on the political resolution of the Cambodian issue.

Both Son Sann and Khieu Samphan expressed their hope that the international community would continue to pay attention to the situation in Cambodia and that they were willing to make efforts to achieve a comprehensive, fair, and reasonable political resolution to the Cambodian issue as soon as possible.

Qian stated that China has consistently advocated for a fair and reasonable political resolution to the Cambodian issue, supports Prince Norodom Sihanouk's series of political proposals, and hopes that this session of the United Nations General Assembly will pass a resolution on the Cambodian issue to facilitate the process of politically resolving the Cambodian issue.

Xinhua News Agency, United Nations, October 4th.

China will send a delegation led by a director from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to Jakarta at the end of November and early December to continue discussions with the Indonesian side on technical issues related to the normalization of relations between the two countries.

This was agreed upon during a meeting between Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen and Indonesian Foreign Minister Ali Alatas at the United Nations headquarters today. Both foreign ministers expressed satisfaction with the consultations on the normalization of relations between the two countries that had taken place in the previous phase.

The two sides also exchanged views on the Cambodian issue during today's meeting.

### 钱外长分别会见宋双乔森潘  并同印尼外长商讨两国关系正常化问题
姚海泉
1989-10-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    钱外长分别会见宋双乔森潘
    并同印尼外长商讨两国关系正常化问题
    本报联合国10月4日电  今天和昨天，钱其琛外长分别会见了民柬联合政府总理宋双和民柬副主席乔森潘。钱外长同他们就柬埔寨问题的政治解决交换了意见。
    宋双总理和乔森潘副主席都表示希望国际社会继续关注柬埔寨局势，并表示愿意做出努力争取使柬埔寨问题尽快获得全面、公正、合理的政治解决。
    钱外长表示，中国一贯主张公正、合理地政治解决柬埔寨问题，支持西哈努克亲王的一系列政治主张，并希望本届联大就柬埔寨问题通过决议，以利于推动政治解决柬埔寨问题的进程。
    本报联合国10月4日电  中国将于11月底、12月初派遣一个由外交部一位司长率领的代表团前往雅加达，同印尼方面继续商讨有关两国关系正常化的技术性问题。
    这是中国和印尼两国外长今天在联合国总部的会晤中商定的。钱其琛外长和阿拉塔斯外长均对前一阶段两国关系正常化问题所进行的协商表示满意。
    双方在今天的会晤中还就柬埔寨问题交换了意见。
              （姚海泉）
