Title: Over 20,000 Joint Ventures Approved
Author: Zhang Yi
Date: 1989-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 30th.

Over 20,000 Joint Ventures Approved

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 30th. (Reporter Zhang Yi) Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade, Zheng Tuobin, recently revealed at a meeting that in the first nine months of this year, the country approved 4,280 new projects utilizing foreign investment, an increase of 16.1 percent compared to the same period last year. As a result, the number of "three types of enterprises" (joint ventures, cooperative enterprises, and wholly foreign-owned enterprises) approved in China has reached 20,175.

Zheng Tuobin said that in the first nine months of this year, the agreed investment amount for new projects utilizing foreign investment was $4.06 billion, and the actual amount of foreign investment introduced was $2.16 billion.

### 批准建立的三资企业逾两万家
张毅
1989-10-31
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　批准建立的三资企业逾两万家
    据新华社北京十月三十日电　（记者张毅）对外经济贸易部部长郑拓彬日前在一个会议上透露，今年头九个月，全国新批准利用外商投资项目四千二百八十个，比去年同期增长百分之十六点一。至此，我国批准建立的“三资”企业已达二万零一百七十五家。
    郑拓彬说，今年头九个月新批准的利用外商投资项目的协议金额为四十点零六亿美元，实际投入外资金额二十点一六亿美元。
