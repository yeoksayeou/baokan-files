Title: Summer
Author: Chen Min
Date: 1989-10-31
Edition: 8

Summer
Chen Min

In vibrant hues and graceful forms,
Summer flows through every street and alley.
A gentle breeze lifts the hem of a skirt,
A ray of sunlight is tucked away in the heart.
In sparkling eyes, passion surges,
In slender figures, beauty is enriched.
In the north, in the south,
There are always fiery tales waiting to be told.
On the shallow sands, boys run,
Leaving behind two bright trails of footprints,
And summer shimmers.
When the fishing rod, in a graceful arc,
Shows the perfect curve,
With fish leaping vaguely,
Endlessly,
Eyes and souls are rejuvenated.
Thus,
Kapok flowers and gardenias bloom together,
White doves carry the distant blue sky,
Flying vividly.
Countless melodies and colors are bright and radiant,
Countless people always
Carry fervent emotions.
Summer, along the rivers, mountains, villages, and cities,
No longer wanders.

### 夏季
陈敏
1989-10-31
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　夏季
　　陈敏
　　五颜六色，婀娜多姿
　　便是大街小巷流行的夏季
　　挽一把熏风拂展裙裾
　　摘一缕阳光藏在心底
　　在盈盈秀目里火辣辣涌动
　　在苗条的身段里丰富着美丽
　　在北方，在南国
　　总有如火的故事亟待构思
　　那浅浅沙滩上奔跑的男孩
　　留下两行晶亮的沙窝
　　便有夏季在闪烁
　　当钓竿以一个优美造型昭示
　　圆满的曲线上，系着
　　朦朦胧胧鱼们的腾跃
　　经久不息
　　目光和心灵因之而年轻
　　就这样
　　木棉花和达子香一齐开放
　　白鸽衔着蔚蓝色远方
　　生动地飞翔
　　无数的乐音色彩明媚而灿烂
　　无数的人儿总是
　　流动热烈的情绪
　　夏季，沿着河流山峦村庄市区
　　不再游移
