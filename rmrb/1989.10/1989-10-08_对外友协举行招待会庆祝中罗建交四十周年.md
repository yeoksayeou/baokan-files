Title: Celebrating 40 Years of China-Romania Diplomatic Relations
Author: Lǐ Huáilín
Date: 1989-10-08
Edition: 2

The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Romania Friendship Association held a reception today to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Romania.

Full text:

National People's Congress Standing Committee Vice Chairman Peng Chong, Deputy Foreign Minister Tian Zengpei, and President of the China-Romania Friendship Association Li Qiang attended the reception. Romanian Ambassador to China Angelo Mikulescu was also invited to attend.

Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Liu Gengyin, in his speech, said that on October 1, 1949, the People's Republic of China was established. On October 5, the Romanian People's Republic established diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China, which was a powerful support for the just cause of the Chinese people.

He said that in the long-term socialist construction, the two parties, two countries, and two peoples of China and Romania have formed a close cooperative partnership.

Ambassador Mikulescu said that the relationship between Romania and China has continued to develop and has never been interrupted, standing the test of time. The peoples of the two countries have helped each other in times of difficulty and have become friends in adversity.

He said, "We trust each other and have the ability to overcome all difficulties and steadfastly follow the socialist path."

Those invited to attend the reception also included officials from the Romanian Embassy in China, experts, journalists, and representatives of students studying in China.

### 对外友协举行招待会庆祝中罗建交四十周年
李怀林
1989-10-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    对外友协举行招待会庆祝中罗建交四十周年
    新华社北京10月7日电　（记者李怀林）中国人民对外友好协会和中国罗马尼亚友好协会今天在这里举行庆祝中罗建交40周年招待会。
    全国人大常委会副委员长彭冲，外交部副部长田曾佩、中国罗马尼亚友好协会会长李强出席了招待会。罗马尼亚驻华大使安杰洛·米库列斯库应邀出席了招待会。
    中国人民对外友好协会副会长刘庚寅在致词中说，1949年10月1日，中华人民共和国成立。10月5日，罗马尼亚人民共和国即与中华人民共和国建立了外交关系，这是对中国人民正义事业的有力支持。
    他说，在长期的社会主义建设事业中，中罗两党、两国和两国人民结成了亲密的合作伙伴。
    米库列斯库大使说，罗中关系日益发展，从未间断，经历了时间的考验。两国人民在困难的时候互相帮助，彼此成为患难中的朋友。
    他说，“我们相互信任，都有能力战胜一切困难，坚定不移地走社会主义道路。”
    应邀出席招待会的还有罗马尼亚驻华使馆官员，在华专家、记者和留学生代表。
