	Foreign Affairs Brief

	▲ Vice Premier Zhou Jiahuai met with Sir Lodrick, Chairman of the British Lloyd's Register of Shipping, in the Great Hall of the People in the afternoon of October 14.

	▲ Vice Premier and Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission Song Jian met with the delegation from the Hong Kong Jian-Gang Academic Exchange Center in the Great Hall of the People in the morning of October 14.

	▲ The China Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Mongolia Friendship Association held a reception in Beijing on October 14 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Mongolia. Wang Hanbin, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, attended the reception. Meng Ying, President of the China-Mongolia Friendship Association, and O. Ochir, Ambassador of Mongolia to China, made toasts at the reception, expressing that the friendly and cooperative relationship between China and Mongolia will surely be further developed in the years to come.

	▲ Nie Rongzhu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, met with Katsuo Maruyama, Consultant to the General Council of the Japanese Trade Union Confederation and Member of the Governing Body of the International Labour Organization, in Beijing on October 14.

### 外事简讯

1989-10-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务委员邹家华14日下午在人民大会堂会见英国劳氏船级社主席洛德里克爵士。
    ▲国务委员兼国家科委主任宋健14日上午在人民大会堂会见了香港京港学术交流中心代表团。
    ▲对外友协和中蒙友协14日在北京举行招待会，庆祝中蒙建交40周年。全国人大常委会副委员长王汉斌出席了招待会。中蒙友协会长孟英和蒙古驻华大使云·奥其尔在招待会上祝酒时表示，中蒙两国睦邻友好合作关系一定会在今后的岁月里得到进一步的发展。
    ▲中华全国总工会主席倪志福14日在北京会见了国际劳工组织理事、日本工会总评议会顾问丸山康雄。
              （新华社）
