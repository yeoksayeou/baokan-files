Title: Ceausescu Emphasizes the Superiority of Socialism
Author:
Date: 1989-10-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, October 17th.

Ceausescu Emphasizes the Superiority of Socialism

The People's Daily reported on October 17th that Romanian President Nicolae Ceausescu said on October 16th during a meeting with Soviet representatives attending an international expo that, "Currently, there is a lot of talk around the world about how socialism is in crisis. I say, Romania will never return to capitalism. Everything we have done in Romania, and everything done in the Soviet Union and other socialist countries, fully demonstrates the superiority of socialism. It shows that only socialism is the path to independent development. Socialism represents the future, not only for socialist countries but for the entire world."

He further stated, "We must look forward, have confidence in socialism, and not look back."

### 齐奥塞斯库强调社会主义优越性  对社会主义应充满信心

1989-10-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    齐奥塞斯库强调社会主义优越性
    对社会主义应充满信心
    新华社布加勒斯特10月17日电　据《火花报》17日报道，罗马尼亚总统齐奥塞斯库10月16日接见参加国际博览会的苏联代表时发表谈话说：“现在世界大加议论，说什么社会主义处于危机之中。我说，罗决不会回到资本主义。我们在罗所做的一切，在苏联和其他社会主义国家所作的一切都充分表明了社会主义的优越性，表明只有社会主义才是独立发展的道路。社会主义代表未来，这不仅对社会主义国家如此，对全世界也是如此。”
    他还说，我们必须向前看，对社会主义应充满信心，而不是向后看。
