Title: Exhibition of Books and Pictures from the German Democratic Republic Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-10-06
Edition: 2

To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic and to promote friendship and mutual understanding between the peoples of China and the Democratic Republic of Germany, the cultural departments and news agencies of the two countries jointly organized the "Exhibition of Books and Pictures from the German Democratic Republic." The exhibition opened on October 5 at the exhibition hall of the Beijing Library.

Vice Minister of Culture Liu Deyou and the First Deputy Director of the German News Agency, Rolf Saberinski, delivered opening speeches. Xinhua News Agency President Mu Qing and the Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Germany to China, Betthold, cut the ribbon.

### 民德图书图片展在京开幕

1989-10-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    民德图书图片展在京开幕
    据新华社北京10月5日电  为庆祝德意志民主共和国成立40周年，增进中国和民主德国人民之间的友谊与相互了解，由两国文化部和通讯社联合主办的《德意志民主共和国图书图片展览》，10月5日在北京图书馆展览厅开幕。
    文化部副部长刘德有和德通社第一副社长罗尔夫·萨伯林斯基致开幕词，新华社社长穆青和民主德国驻华大使贝特霍尔德剪彩。
