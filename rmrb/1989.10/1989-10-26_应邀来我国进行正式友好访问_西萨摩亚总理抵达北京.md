Title: Samoan Prime Minister Arrives in Beijing for Official Friendly Visit
Author:
Date: 1989-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 25th.

At the invitation of the Chinese government for an official friendly visit, the Prime Minister of the South Pacific island nation of Western Samoa, Tofilau Eti Alesana, arrived in Beijing by airplane this evening. Prime Minister Tofilau is visiting at the invitation of Premier Li Peng. Accompanying the Prime Minister are his wife and the Minister of Finance of Western Samoa, Tuilaepa Sailele Malielegaoi.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included the leader of the Chinese government's escort team and the Director of the State Sports Commission, Wu Shaozu, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Liu Huaqiu.

### 应邀来我国进行正式友好访问  西萨摩亚总理抵达北京

1989-10-26
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　应邀来我国进行正式友好访问
    西萨摩亚总理抵达北京
    新华社北京10月25日电　南太平洋岛国——西萨摩亚独立国总理托菲劳·埃蒂·阿莱萨纳今天晚上乘飞机抵达北京，开始对中国进行正式友好访问。托菲劳总理是应李鹏总理邀请来访的。随同来访的有托菲劳总理夫人、西萨摩亚财政部长图依拉埃帕·萨依莱莱。
    到机场迎接的有中国政府陪同团团长、国家体委主任伍绍祖，外交部副部长刘华秋等。
