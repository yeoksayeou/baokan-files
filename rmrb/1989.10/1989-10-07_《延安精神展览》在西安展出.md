Title: The "Yan'an Spirit Exhibition" Opens in Xi'an
Author: Meng Xian
Date: 1989-10-07
Page: 4

The "Yan'an Spirit Exhibition," jointly organized by the Yan'an Revolutionary Memorial Hall and the Yan'an Spirit Research Association, officially opened in Xi'an today. The exhibition features nearly 300 precious documents and photographs, highlighting the themes of "the birthplace of the Yan'an Spirit, a precious spiritual wealth, and the enduring brilliance of the Yan'an Spirit."

### 《延安精神展览》在西安展出
孟西安
1989-10-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    《延安精神展览》在西安展出
    本报西安10月6日电　记者孟西安报道：延安革命纪念馆、延安精神研究会联合举办的《延安精神展览》，今天开始正式在西安展出。展览用近300件珍贵的资料和照片，突出了“延安精神的发祥地、宝贵的精神财富、延安精神永放光芒”等内容。
