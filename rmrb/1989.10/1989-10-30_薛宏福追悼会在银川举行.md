Title: Funeral of Xue Hongfu Held in Yinchuan
Author:
Date: 1989-10-30
Edition: 2

The funeral of Xue Hongfu, an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China, a loyal communist warrior, and the chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference of Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, was held in Yinchuan today.

Xue Hongfu suffered a cerebral hemorrhage during a meeting and, despite efforts to save him, passed away in Yinchuan on October 14 at the age of 70.

Yao Yilin, Wang Zhen, Song Renqiong, Liu Lan Tao, Yu Quli, Kang Shien, Xi Zhongxun, Apao Awang Jinmei, Liao Hansheng, Fei Xiaotong, Lei Jieqiong, and Qian Zhengying, as well as the Central Office of the Communist Party of China, the Office of the Central Advisory Commission, and the Central Organization Department, sent wreaths.

Xue Hongfu was born in Yan'an, Shaanxi. He joined the revolution in 1936 and became a member of the Communist Party of China in 1937.

### 薛宏福追悼会在银川举行

1989-10-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　薛宏福追悼会在银川举行
    新华社银川10月27日电　中国共产党的优秀党员、忠诚的共产主义战士、中共宁夏回族自治区顾问委员会主任薛宏福同志的追悼会今天在银川举行。
    薛宏福同志在开会时突发脑出血，终因抢救无效，于10月14日在银川逝世，终年70岁。
    姚依林、王震、宋任穷、刘澜涛、余秋里、康世恩、习仲勋、阿沛·阿旺晋美、廖汉生、费孝通、雷洁琼、钱正英和中共中央办公厅、中顾委办公厅、中央组织部等送了花圈。
    薛宏福是陕西延安市人，1936年参加革命，1937年加入中国共产党。
