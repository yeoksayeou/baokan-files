Date: 1989-10-30
Edition: 2

Foreign Affairs Briefing

	▲ State Councilor Li Tieying held a meeting with former U.S. President Nixon in the Great Hall of the People on the morning of October 29. The two sides had a broad and candid discussion on education and other issues.

	Li Tieying introduced to the guest the achievements of China's educational undertakings, the situation of China's exchanges and cooperation with foreign countries in the field of education, and the government's approach of being both enthusiastic and strict with young students.

	▲ Ding Guangen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, met in the Great Hall of the People on the morning of October 29 with a delegation from the *Rodong Sinmun* of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Deputy Editor-in-Chief Park Chung-sun.

	The meeting was attended by Gao Di, President of the People's Daily, and Ju Changjun, Ambassador of the DPRK to China, among others.

	▲ Thai Deputy Prime Minister Pichai Radakorn and his entourage concluded their 9-day friendly visit to Beijing, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Shantou, and Shenzhen, and departed by car from Shenzhen via Hong Kong on the afternoon of October 27.

	▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Ye Fei met with a delegation of Philippine congressional officials in the Great Hall of the People on October 28.

	▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Ni Zhifu met and hosted a banquet for a delegation from the Japanese city of Nihonmatsu on October 28 in the Great Hall of the People.

	▲ State Councilor and Governor of the People's Bank of China Li Guixian met with Jean-Yves Habere, Chairman of the Board of Directors of Crédit Lyonnais, and his entourage in the Great Hall of the People on the morning of October 28. Habere was invited by the Bank of China to visit China this time, mainly to understand the current political and economic situation and reform and opening-up policies of China, in order to seek specific ways to further strengthen cooperation.

	▲ State Councilor and Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission Song Jian met with the Executive Secretary of the United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific, Samir Kibria, and his entourage in the Great Hall of the People on the afternoon of October 28.

	▲ State Councilor Li Tieying met with a delegation from the Chadian government led by Minister of Culture, Youth, and Sports Gibrilla Hussein Greenkey in the Purple Light Pavilion of Zhongnanhai on the afternoon of October 28.

	▲ Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin met with Yuli Yun, heiress of Eli Lilly and Company and Ambassador-at-Large for International Goodwill of Guam, in Beijing on the afternoon of October 28.

### 外事简讯

1989-10-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外事简讯
    ▲国务委员李铁映29日上午在人民大会堂会见了美国前总统尼克松，宾主就教育等问题进行了广泛、坦诚的交谈。
    李铁映向客人介绍了新中国教育事业的成就、中国同外国在教育领域进行交流与合作的情况，以及中国政府对青年学生既满腔热情又严格要求的作法。
    ▲中共中央政治局候补委员、书记处书记丁关根29日上午在人民大会堂会见了由副总编辑朴忠顺率领的朝鲜《劳动新闻》代表团。
    人民日报社社长高狄、朝鲜驻华大使朱昌骏等参加了会见。
    ▲泰王国副总理披猜·拉达军一行结束了对中国的北京、杭州、广州、汕头、深圳为期9天的友好访问，27日下午乘车离开深圳经香港回国。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长叶飞28日在人民大会堂会见了菲律宾国会官员代表团。
　　▲全国人大常委会副委员长倪志福28日在人民大会堂会见并宴请了日本二本松市友好访华团一行。
    ▲国务委员兼中国人民银行行长李贵鲜28日上午在人民大会堂会见了法国里昂信贷银行董事长让·伊夫·阿贝莱一行。阿贝莱此次应中国银行的邀请来访，主要目的是了解当前中国的政治经济形势和改革开放政策，以寻求进一步加强合作的具体办法。
    ▲国务委员兼国家科委主任宋健28日下午在人民大会堂会见联合国亚洲和太平洋经济社会委员会执行秘书夏·基勃里亚一行。
    ▲国务委员李铁映28日下午在中南海紫光阁会见了由文化、青年、体育部长吉布里纳·侯赛因·格林基率领的乍得政府文化代表团。
    ▲经贸部长郑拓彬28日下午在北京会见了美国礼来公司财产继承人、美国关岛国际亲善大使余丽云女士。
              （新华社）
