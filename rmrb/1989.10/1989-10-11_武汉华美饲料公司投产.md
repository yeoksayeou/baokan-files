Title: Wuhan Huamei Feed Company Commences Production
Author: Gong Dafa
Date: 1989-10-11
Page: 2

Our reporter, Gong Dafa, reports from Wuhan: The Wuhan Huamei Feed Company, a Sino-American joint venture, officially commenced production today.

This company, jointly operated by the China National Cereals, Oils and Foodstuffs Import and Export Corporation, Wuhan Feed Company, and the American Continental Grain Company, has an annual production capacity of 50,000 tons of feed for pigs, chickens, and ducks in a single shift. The fully imported American processing machinery is one of the most advanced complete sets of feed processing equipment in the world.

### 武汉华美饲料公司投产
龚达发
1989-10-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    武汉华美饲料公司投产
    本报武汉10月10日电　记者龚达发报道：中美合资的武汉华美饲料有限公司，今天在此间正式投产。
    这家由中国粮油进出口总公司、武汉饲料公司和美国大陆谷物公司合资经营的企业，单班年产5万吨猪、鸡、鸭饲料。全套引进的美国加工机械，是目前世界上最先进的成套饲料加工设备之一。
