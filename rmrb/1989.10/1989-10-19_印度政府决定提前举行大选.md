Title: The Indian Government Decides to Hold Early Elections
Author:
Date: 1989-10-19
Edition: 3

The Indian government decided to hold early elections after two constitutional amendments were rejected in the Federal Council. On the 17th, the government decided to hold early elections for the Lok Sabha (lower house of parliament) by the end of November.

According to official sources, after the cabinet meeting on the morning of the 17th, a letter was sent to the Election Commission requesting early elections for the Lok Sabha.

The cabinet proposed election dates of November 22nd, 25th, and 27th. The Election Commission will make the final decision.

### 印度政府决定提前举行大选

1989-10-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    印度政府决定提前举行大选
    在两项宪法修正案在联邦院被否决以后，印度政府十七日决定提前在十一月底举行大选。
    据官方人士透露，内阁在十七日上午举行会议以后致函选举委员会，要求提前举行人民院（议会下院）选举。
    内阁建议的选举日期为十一月二十二日、二十五日和二十七日，选举委员会将作出最后决定。
