Title: Innovative Method of Electrotherapy for Malignant Tumors by Xin Yuling
Author: Zou Peiyan
Date: 1989-10-18
Edition: 2

    The use of direct current to treat malignant tumors has been successfully applied for the first time in China by Professor Xin Yuling and his team at the China-Japan Friendship Hospital. This method has shown significant therapeutic effects. Experts believe it is a new and promising approach for treating malignant tumors, particularly suitable for patients who are not eligible for surgery, radiation therapy, or chemotherapy.

    So far, the China-Japan Friendship Hospital has treated 41 malignant tumor patients using direct current. After 6 months to 1.5 years of observation, 75.63% of the patients showed complete tumor disappearance or significant reduction.

### 电疗恶性肿瘤  辛育龄创新法
邹沛颜
1989-10-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    电疗恶性肿瘤  辛育龄创新法
    新华社北京10月17日电（记者邹沛颜）中日友好医院辛育龄教授等在我国首次应用直流电治疗恶性肿瘤病人，取得明显疗效。有关人士称这是一种新的、有前途的治疗恶性肿瘤的方法。它适合于治疗不适应手术治疗、放疗和化疗也难以接受的恶性肿瘤病人。
    到目前为止，中日友好医院已应用直流电在临床上治疗了41例恶性肿瘤病人，经6个月至1年半时间的观察，肿瘤消失或显著缩小的病例占75.63％以上。
