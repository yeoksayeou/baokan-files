Title: Central Leaders Meet with Minority Nationalities, Artists, and PLA Representatives
Author:
Date: 1989-10-03
Edition: 1

Central leaders meet with representatives of minority nationalities, artists, and the PLA

New China News Agency, Beijing, October 2 — Jiang Zemin, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, Wan Li, Qiao Shi, and Song Ping, among others, met this morning in the Great Hall of the People with representatives from the national minority nationalities observation team, the national minority artists joint performance team, the PLA grassroots advanced representatives, and representatives of famous military dependents and other preferential treatment objects from across the country, as well as performers from the second China Arts Festival. They also posed for a group photo with everyone.

Other leaders present at the meeting included: Qin Jiwei, Song Rong, Hab Comber, Abo Ajianjinmei, Chen Xitong, Chen Junsheng, Sima Yi Aimaidi, Hong Xuezhi, and Liu Huaqing.

### 中央领导会见少数民族、艺术家、解放军代表

1989-10-03
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    中央领导会见少数民族、艺术家、解放军代表
    新华社北京10月2日电  江泽民、杨尚昆、李鹏、万里、乔石、宋平等同志，今天上午在人民大会堂会见了参加国庆40周年活动的全国少数民族参观团、少数民族艺术家联合演出团、人民解放军基层先进代表、全国著名烈军属等优抚对象代表、第二届中国艺术节的演员代表，并同大家一起合影留念。参加会见的领导同志还有：秦基伟、宋任穷、习仲勋、阿沛·阿旺晋美、陈希同、陈俊生、司马义·艾买提、洪学智、刘华清等。（附图片）
    江泽民、杨尚昆等会见少数民族参观团等代表。
                          新华社记者　胥志成摄
