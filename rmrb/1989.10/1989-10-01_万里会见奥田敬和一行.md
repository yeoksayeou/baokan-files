Title: Wan Li Meets with Okuda Keiwa and Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-10-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30th.

Wan Li, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, met this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People with a delegation led by Japanese Diet member Okuda Keiwa, part of the Long City Plan Japanese Youth Delegation to China.

Wan Li welcomed and thanked the delegation for coming to China to visit and participate in the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Wan Li said that China and Japan are neighbors, and since the establishment of diplomatic relations, the friendly cooperative relationship between the two countries in politics, economy, culture, and other fields has developed well.

He said that strengthening exchanges between the parliaments of China and Japan is conducive to promoting the friendly cooperative relationship between the two countries.

Wan Li said that the friendship between China and Japan from generation to generation is the wish of the peoples of both countries, which is beneficial to Asian and world peace and will also promote the economic development of both countries.

Okuda Keiwa expressed his willingness to work for the development of Sino-Japanese friendly relations. He said that this time he led the Long City Plan Japanese Youth Delegation to visit China to strengthen exchanges between Japanese and Chinese youth. Strengthening exchanges between Japanese and Chinese youth is of great significance in promoting Sino-Japanese friendly cooperation and in ensuring that the two countries and their peoples remain friendly for generations to come. Liu Yandong, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, was present at the meeting.

### 万里会见奥田敬和一行

1989-10-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    万里会见奥田敬和一行
    新华社北京9月30日电  全国人大常委会委员长万里今天下午在人民大会堂会见由日本众议院议员奥田敬和率领的长城计划日本中国青少年交流使节团部分成员。
    万里对代表团前来中国访问并参加中国建国40周年庆祝活动表示欢迎和感谢。
    万里说，中日两国是近邻，自建交以来，两国在政治、经济、文化等方面的友好合作关系发展得很好。
    他说，加强中日两国议会之间的交往对两国友好合作关系是一个促进。
    万里说，中日两国世世代代友好下去，是两国人民的愿望，对亚洲和世界和平有利，同时也会促进中日两国各自的经济发展。
    奥田敬和表示愿为促进日中友好关系的发展作出努力。他说，他这次率领长城计划日本中国青少年使节团访华，就是为了加强日中两国青少年之间的交往。日中两国青少年之间交往的加强对促进日中友好合作关系，对使日中两国、两国人民世世代代友好下去有十分重要的意义。全国青联主席刘延东会见时在座。
