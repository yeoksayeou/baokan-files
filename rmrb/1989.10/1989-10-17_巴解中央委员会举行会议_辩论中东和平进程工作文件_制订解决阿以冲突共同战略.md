Title: The PLO Central Committee Holds a Meeting to Debate the Middle East Peace Process Working Document and Formulate a Joint Strategy to Resolve the Arab-Israeli Conflict
Author:
Date: 1989-10-17
Edition: 3

The Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO) Central Committee began a two-day meeting in Baghdad today to discuss a working document on the Middle East peace process.

PLO Chairman Yasser Arafat attended the meeting. It is expected that the meeting will seek a common strategy in response to the proposals by the United States and Egypt for direct Arab-Israeli dialogue to end the Middle East conflict.

According to Palestinian sources, the working document emphasizes the need to adhere to the resolutions of the Palestine National Council on the peace process and to seek a comprehensive and lasting peace in the Middle East.

The PLO Central Committee will also discuss and strengthen methods for the Palestinian uprising in the occupied territories and call for united action by all Palestinian organizations.

The working document calls on the international community to protect Palestinians in the West Bank and Gaza Strip from Israeli repression and to impose sanctions on Israel.

PLO spokesman Rahman Ahmed Abdel said the PLO Central Committee meeting will discuss all peace proposals that have emerged in recent months and issue a policy statement.

The meeting is being held amidst Israel's rejection of all recent peace proposals. Egypt proposed a 10-point peace plan and offered to host Arab-Israeli talks in Cairo, demanding that Israel accept the principle of "land for peace." U.S. Secretary of State Baker proposed a five-point plan, calling for consultations between the Israeli and Egyptian foreign ministers to arrange Arab-Israeli talks.

However, Israeli Prime Minister Shamir refused to withdraw from the West Bank and Gaza Strip, rejected the proposal for a Palestinian state, and refused to allow Palestinians outside the occupied territories to participate in any dialogue with Israel.

### 巴解中央委员会举行会议  辩论中东和平进程工作文件  制订解决阿以冲突共同战略

1989-10-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴解中央委员会举行会议
    辩论中东和平进程工作文件
    制订解决阿以冲突共同战略
    新华社巴格达10月15日电　巴勒斯坦解放组织中央委员会今天在这里开始举行两天的会议，就有关中东和平进程的工作文件进行辩论。
    巴勒斯坦国总统阿拉法特出席了这次会议。预计，这次会议将就美国和埃及提出的进行阿以直接对话结束中东冲突的建议寻求共同战略。
    据巴勒斯坦人士说，工作文件强调，需要坚持巴勒斯坦全国委员会关于和平进程的决议，寻求实现中东地区全面持久的和平。
    巴解组织中央委员会还将讨论和加强被占领土巴勒斯坦起义的方法，并呼吁巴勒斯坦各组织采取统一行动。
    工作文件呼吁国际社会保护约旦河西岸和加沙地带的巴勒斯坦人不受以色列的镇压，对以色列进行制裁。
    巴解组织发言人拉赫曼·艾哈迈德·阿卜杜勒说，巴解组织中央委员会会议将讨论最近几个月出现的所有和平设想，并发表一项政策声明。
    这次会议是在以色列反对最近提出的所有和平建议的情况下召开的。埃及提出了10点和平计划并提出主持在开罗举行阿以对话，要求以色列接受“以土地换和平的原则”。美国国务卿贝克提出5点计划，呼吁以色列和埃及外长就安排阿以对话进行磋商。
    但是，以色列总理沙米尔拒绝撤离约旦河西岸和加沙地带，拒绝接受巴勒斯坦国的建议，拒绝被占领土以外的巴勒斯坦人参加与以色列的任何对话。
