Title: Some Party and Government Leaders from Various Countries Send Congratulatory Messages to Chinese Leaders on the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1989-10-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 10th.

Some party and government leaders from various countries and international organizations have recently sent congratulatory telegrams and letters to Chairman Yang Shangkun and General Secretary Jiang Zemin to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The following leaders sent congratulatory telegrams to Chairman Yang Shangkun:

* Chairman of the Mozambican Liberation Front and President of the People's Republic of Mozambique, Joaquim Alberto Chissano;
* Emir of Bahrain, Isa bin Salman Al Khalifa;
* President of the Republic of Cameroon, Paul Biya;
* Chairman of the Movement for the Liberation of São Tomé and Príncipe and President of the Democratic Republic of São Tomé and Príncipe, Manuel Pinto da Costa;
* President of the Republic of Rwanda and Chairman of the National Revolutionary Movement for Development—Founder, Major General Juvénal Habyarimana;
* President of the Republic of Chile, General Augusto Pinochet Ugarte;
* President of the Republic of Gambia, Dawda Jawara;
* President of the Republic of Turkey, Kenan Evren;
* King of Saudi Arabia, Fahd bin Abdulaziz Al Saud;
* Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Belgium, Wilfried Martens;
* Prime Minister of Barbados, Erskine Sandiford.
* United Nations Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar.

Arab League Secretary-General Chedli Klibi sent a congratulatory letter to General Secretary Jiang Zemin.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 10th.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and party and state leaders have recently received congratulatory telegrams from leaders of foreign political parties celebrating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The following leaders sent congratulatory telegrams to the Central Committee of the CPC:

* Chairman of the National Leadership Committee of the Communist Party of Brazil, Salomão Malina;
* First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Bolivia, Humberto Ramirez Cardenas;

The following leader sent a congratulatory telegram to General Secretary Jiang Zemin:

* Chairman of the United Left Revolutionary Front of Bolivia, Oscar Zamora Medina;

The following leaders sent congratulatory telegrams to Chairman Yang Shangkun:

* Chairman of the Socialist Party of Cyprus, Vassos Lyssaridis;
* General Secretary, Takis Hadjiemmanuil.

### 一些国家党政领导人电函我国领导人  祝贺我国庆四十周年

1989-10-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    一些国家党政领导人电函我国领导人
    祝贺我国庆四十周年
    新华社北京10月10日电　杨尚昆主席和江泽民总书记近日来又收到一些国家和政党及国际组织的领导人热烈祝贺中华人民共和国成立40周年的贺电和贺函。
    向杨尚昆主席发来贺电的有：
    莫桑比克解放阵线党主席、莫桑比克人民共和国总统若阿金·阿尔贝托·希萨诺；
    巴林国埃米尔伊萨·本·萨勒曼·阿勒哈利法；
    喀麦隆共和国总统保罗·比亚；
    圣多美和普林西比解放运动主席、圣多美和普林西比民主共和国总统曼努埃尔·平托·达科斯塔；
    卢旺达共和国总统、全国发展革命运动主席——创始人朱韦纳尔·哈比亚利马纳少将；
    智利共和国总统奥古斯托·皮诺切特·乌加特上将；
    冈比亚共和国总统达乌达·凯拉巴·贾瓦拉；
    土耳其共和国总统凯南·埃夫伦；
    沙特阿拉伯国王法赫德·本·阿卜杜勒·阿齐兹；
    比利时王国首相维尔弗莱德·马尔滕斯；
    巴巴多斯总理厄斯金·桑迪福德。
    联合国秘书长哈维尔·佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔。
    阿拉伯国家联盟秘书长谢德利·卡利比向江泽民总书记发来了贺函。
    新华社北京10月10日电　中国共产党中央委员会以及党和国家领导人，近日来又收到一些外国政党领导人发来的热烈祝贺中华人民共和国成立40周年的贺电。
    向中共中央发来贺电的有巴西的共产党全国领导委员会主席萨洛芒·马利纳，玻利维亚共产党中央第一书记温贝托·拉米雷斯·卡德纳斯；
    向江泽民总书记发来贺电的有玻利维亚左派革命阵线主席奥斯卡·萨莫拉·梅迪纳塞利；
    向杨尚昆主席发来贺电的有塞浦路斯社会党主席瓦索斯·里萨利迪斯、总书记塔基斯·哈季迪米德里欧。
