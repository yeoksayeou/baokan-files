Title: Chinese Magic Masters Win Awards in Bulgaria
Author: Wang Jinbo
Date: 1989-10-26
Edition: 4

Chinese magic master Yang Baolin, with his unique traditional Chinese magic tricks "Fire Basin Toss" and "Moon from the Sleeve," won the grand prize at the Sofia International Magic Festival.

Another Chinese magic master, Tuerli, won the second prize in the individual event with his performance of "Fishing Live Fish."

The Sofia International Magic Festival is also the 3rd International Magic Competition. Magic masters from 17 countries, including Bulgaria, the Soviet Union, Poland, Hungary, Romania, Yugoslavia, and Austria, gave splendid performances and competed at the Sofia Circus.

### 我魔术大师在保获奖
王金柏
1989-10-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我魔术大师在保获奖
    新华社索非亚10月23日电　（记者王金柏）中国魔术大师杨宝林，以中华特有的传统古彩戏法在索非亚国际魔术节表演的“火盆回托”和“怀里捞月”，荣获本届比赛大奖。
    另一位中国魔术大师提日力表演的“钓活鱼”荣获单项第2名。
    索非亚国际魔术节也是第3届国际魔术会演。保加利亚、苏联、波兰、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、南斯拉夫、奥地利等17个国家的魔术大师在索非亚杂技院进行了精彩的表演和比赛。
