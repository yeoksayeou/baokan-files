Title: Jiang Zemin Meets with Czechoslovak Trade Union Delegation and Separately with American Professors and Businesspeople
Author:
Date: 1989-10-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 2nd.

General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Jiang Zemin, met this morning with a delegation from the Czechoslovak Trade Union led by Miroslav Zavagil, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and Chairman of the Central Council of Trade Unions. The host and guests had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Jiang Zemin first welcomed Comrade Zavagil and his delegation for their visit and expressed his pleasure that they participated in the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Zavagil conveyed greetings from General Secretary of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Milos Jakes, and invited Jiang Zemin to visit Czechoslovakia. He also introduced the work of the Czechoslovak Trade Unions and economic reforms, expressing the desire for various forms of economic and technological cooperation with China.

Jiang Zemin thanked Comrade Jakes for his greetings and invitation, and asked Zavagil to convey his greetings to Comrade Jakes. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Ni Zhifu, was present at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 2nd.

General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Jiang Zemin, met this morning in Zhongnanhai with Professor Gu Yishu of the University of Pennsylvania and his wife, Wang Wanjing, as well as Professor Cheng Junfu. They had a cordial conversation.

Member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Ding Guanggen, was present at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 2nd.

General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Jiang Zemin, met this afternoon in Zhongnanhai with a delegation from the Coca-Cola Company led by President John Sculley. General Secretary Jiang welcomed the delegation, noting that their visit coincided with the 40th anniversary of the founding of New China, allowing the guests to witness China's stable situation and prosperous scene. Jiang Zemin praised the cooperation between the Coca-Cola Company and China, stating that this cooperation was forward-looking and hoped that such cooperation would continue to develop.

Sculley expressed his willingness to further cooperate with China.

### 江泽民会见捷工会代表团  同日分别会见美国教授和企业家

1989-10-03
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民会见捷工会代表团
    同日分别会见美国教授和企业家
    新华社北京十月二日电　中共中央总书记江泽民今天上午会见了由捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央主席团委员、工会中央理事会主席米罗斯拉夫·扎瓦吉尔率领的捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团，宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    江泽民首先对扎瓦吉尔同志率团来访表示欢迎，并对他们参加我国国庆四十周年庆祝活动感到高兴。
    扎瓦吉尔转达了捷共总书记雅克什对江泽民的问候和对江泽民访捷的邀请，介绍了捷克斯洛伐克工会工作和经济改革的情况，表达了与我国进行各项经济技术合作的愿望。
    江泽民对雅克什同志的问候和邀请表示感谢，并请扎瓦吉尔转达对雅克什同志的问候。全国人大常委会副委员长、中华全国总工会主席倪志福会见时在座。
    新华社北京十月二日电　中共中央总书记江泽民今天上午在中南海会见了美国宾夕法尼亚大学荣休教授顾毓琇及其夫人王婉靖女士，以及程君复教授，同他们进行了亲切交谈。
    中共中央政治局候补委员丁关根会见时在座。
    新华社北京十月二日电  中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午在中南海会见了美国可口可乐（国际）公司总裁约翰·乔格斯一行。江总书记对乔格斯一行来访表示欢迎，认为乔格斯一行来访正值新中国成立四十周年，这使客人们有机会看到中国稳定的局势和歌舞升平的景象。江泽民赞赏美国可口可乐公司同中国的合作，认为这一合作是有长远眼光的，希望双方的这种合作能进一步发展。
    乔格斯表示愿意进一步与中国开展合作。
