Title: The United States to Sell Advanced Fighter Jets to South Korea
Author:
Date: 1989-10-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, October 25th.

According to a recent agreement between the United States and South Korea, the United States will sell 120 F-16 and F-18 fighter jets to South Korea. Of these, 108 will be assembled or produced in South Korea using parts exported from the United States. This $2 billion arms deal is said to further enhance the modernization of the South Korean military.

### 美国将向南朝鲜出售先进战斗机

1989-10-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美国将向南朝鲜出售先进战斗机
    本报华盛顿10月25日电　根据美国和南朝鲜近日谈妥的协议，美国将向南朝鲜出售120架F—16和F—18战斗机，其中108架将由美国出口部件在南朝鲜装配或在南朝鲜生产。据称这笔20亿美元的军火交易将进一步提高南朝鲜军队的现代化程度。
