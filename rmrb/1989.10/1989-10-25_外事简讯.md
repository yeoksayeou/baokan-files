Foreign Affairs News Brief

	▲ On the evening of October 24, Wang Zhen, a member of the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, met with a delegation from the People's Progressive Party of the Gambia led by Central Secretariat Secretary Yaya Sesay at the Great Hall of the People. The People's Progressive Party is the ruling party of the Gambia. The delegation was invited by the Communist Party of China for a visit to China.

	▲ At the invitation of the Ministries of Defense of the German Democratic Republic and the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic, General Xu Xin, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, led a Chinese military friendship delegation to pay an official friendly visit on October 23.

	▲ On the evening of October 23, the China-Britain Friendship Group of the National People's Congress held a banquet at the Great Hall of the People to welcome Robert Aickman, chairman of the British Parliament's China Group, and his entourage, who were invited to visit. In the afternoon of October 23, Yao Guang, a member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and vice chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee, held a working meeting with Aickman.

### 外事简讯

1989-10-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：外事简讯

　　外事简讯
    ▲中顾委常委耿飚24日晚上在人民大会堂会见了由中央书记处书记雅雅·塞塞率领的冈比亚人民进步党代表团。冈比亚人进党是冈比亚执政党。代表团是应中国共产党的邀请前来访华的。
    ▲应德意志民主共和国和捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国两国国防部邀请，中国人民解放军副总参谋长徐信上将23日率中国军事友好代表团前往进行正式友好访问。
    ▲全国人大中英友好小组23日晚在人民大会堂举行宴会，欢迎应邀来访的英国议会中国小组主席罗伯特·艾德礼一行。23日下午，人大常委会委员、人大外委会副主任委员姚广同艾德礼举行工作会谈。
