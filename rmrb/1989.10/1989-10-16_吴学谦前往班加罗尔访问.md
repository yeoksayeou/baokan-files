Title: Wu Xueqian Visits Bangalore
Author:
Date: 1989-10-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 14th.

Chinese Vice Premier Wu Xueqian and his delegation left here today for Bangalore, the capital of the Indian state of Karnataka.

Indian Foreign Minister Narasimha Rao and other officials saw Wu Xueqian off at the airport.

Bangalore is the second stop for the Chinese delegation's visit to India. Wu Xueqian and his delegation will also visit the Union Territory of Goa and Mumbai, the capital of Maharashtra. They will tour some factories and scientific research institutes. Wu Xueqian and his delegation arrived in New Delhi on October 11th.

### 吴学谦前往班加罗尔访问

1989-10-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    吴学谦前往班加罗尔访问
    新华社新德里10月14日电  中国副总理吴学谦及其一行今天离开这里前往印度卡纳塔尔邦首府班加罗尔访问。
    印度外交部长纳拉辛哈·拉奥和其他一些官员到机场为吴学谦送行。
    班加罗尔是中国代表团访印的第二站。此后吴学谦一行还将访问果阿中央直辖区和马哈拉施特拉邦首府孟买。他们将参观一些工厂和科技研究所。吴学谦一行是11日抵达新德里的。
