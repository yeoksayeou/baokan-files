Title: Beijing Capital International Airport Expands Fuel Storage Facility
Author: Feng Guangda
Date: 1989-10-24
Page: 2

The first phase of the expansion project for the large aviation fuel storage facility at Beijing Capital International Airport, which includes 4 fuel tanks each with a capacity of 10,000 cubic meters, a fuel unloading station with 26 positions, fire-fighting facilities, and an automated control system, has been completed after more than three years of construction and installation. Currently, this large fuel storage facility, which was expanded with a state investment of 12.8 million yuan, has increased its fuel storage capacity to 46,000 tons.

### 首都国际机场扩建燃料储库
冯广大
1989-10-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　首都国际机场扩建燃料储库
    本报讯　首都国际机场大型航空燃料储库的一期扩建工程，包括4个1万立方米航空燃料储罐及26个货位的卸燃站桥、消防设施和自动化控制系统等项目，经过3年多的施工和安装，全部完工，目前，这座由国家投资1280万元扩建的大型燃料库，储燃能力增加到46000吨。（冯广大）
