Title: Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Meets with Bangladeshi Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-10-19
Edition: 2

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun met with a delegation from the Dhaka Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Bangladesh, led by Chairman Salman Rahman, at the Zhongnanhai Zhongguang Hall today. The delegation is in China at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade to discuss with relevant Chinese departments and companies, exploring the possibilities for expanding trade and economic cooperation.

### 田纪云会见孟加拉客人

1989-10-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    田纪云会见孟加拉客人
    新华社北京10月18日电　国务院副总理田纪云今天在中南海紫光阁会见了由会长萨尔曼·拉赫曼先生率领的孟加拉达卡市工商协会代表团。代表团是应中国国际贸易促进会的邀请前来访华的，目的是与中国有关部门和公司进行洽谈，探寻扩大贸易和经济合作的可能性。
