Title: Singapore National Stadium Rekindles the Battle
Author:
Date: 1989-10-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, October 16th.

The World Cup football qualifying final in the Asian region reignited tonight. Qatar drew 1-1 with Saudi Arabia, while South Korea won 1-0 against North Korea. Qatar conceded a goal just two minutes into the match but equalized three minutes before the final whistle. South Korea, in their first-ever World Cup match against North Korea, scored the winning goal in the 20th minute.

### 新加坡国家体育场重燃战火  卡塔尔再平对手　南朝鲜初尝胜果

1989-10-17
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    新加坡国家体育场重燃战火
    卡塔尔再平对手　南朝鲜初尝胜果
    据新华社新加坡10月16日电　世界杯足球赛亚洲区预选决赛今晚重燃战火。卡塔尔队以1∶1逼平沙特队，南朝鲜队1∶0胜朝鲜队。卡塔尔队开场两分钟先失一球，终场前3分钟将比分扳平。南朝鲜队在世界杯足球赛史上与朝鲜队首次交锋，在第20分时攻破对方大门。
