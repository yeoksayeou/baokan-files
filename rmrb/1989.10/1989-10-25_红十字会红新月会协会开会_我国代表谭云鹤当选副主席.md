Title: The International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies Holds a Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-10-25
Edition: 3

The seventh conference of the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies was held in Geneva on October 21. On October 23, a new executive board and leadership were elected.

Tan Yunhe, the vice president of the Chinese Red Cross Society, was elected as the vice president of the federation, with a term of four years. This is the first time that the Chinese Red Cross Society has held the position of vice president in this organization.

Randal from Venezuela was re-elected as the chairman. Additionally, representatives from Canada and seven other countries were also elected as vice presidents.

The International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies was established in 1919 and has 148 national Red Cross and Red Crescent societies as members. It holds a general assembly every two years.

### 红十字会红新月会协会开会  我国代表谭云鹤当选副主席

1989-10-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　红十字会红新月会协会开会
　　我国代表谭云鹤当选副主席
    本报讯　红十字会与红新月会协会第七次大会10月21日在日内瓦召开，10月23日选出了新的执行理事会和协会领导机构。
    中国红十字会常务副会长谭云鹤当选为协会副主席，任期4年。这是中国红十字会第一次担任该组织的副主席。
    委内瑞拉的兰德尔再次蝉联主席。另外，加拿大等7个国家的代表也被选为副主席。
    红十字会与红新月会协会1919年成立，共有148个国家红十字会、红新月会参加，每两年召开一次代表大会。
