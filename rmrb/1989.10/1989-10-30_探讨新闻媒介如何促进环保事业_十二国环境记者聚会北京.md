Title: Exploring How News Media Can Promote Environmental Protection
Author: Xú Yàozhōng  Xiāo Tǐhuàn
Date: 1989-10-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 30th. Reporters Xú Yàozhōng and Xiāo Tǐhuàn reported: The Asian Healthy Environment and Sustainable Development News Seminar recently concluded in Beijing. Seventeen environmental journalists from Nepal, India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Thailand, and eight other countries joined Chinese journalists in discussing how news media can promote the development of environmental protection.

The seminar, which opened on October 23, was jointly organized by the Asian Environmental Journalists Forum and the Chinese Environmental Journalists Forum. The Asian Environmental Journalists Forum is currently the only pan-Asian network organization for environmental journalists, and this was the first time it held an environmental news seminar. During the seminar, environmental journalists from 12 countries paid special attention to prominent global environmental issues of the 1980s, such as acid rain, ozone layer depletion, the greenhouse effect, and hazardous waste. They expressed their commitment to using their pens to improve the global ecological environment and promote the exchange and cooperation of environmental protection work among Asian countries.

### 探讨新闻媒介如何促进环保事业  十二国环境记者聚会北京
徐耀中  肖体焕
1989-10-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　探讨新闻媒介如何促进环保事业
　　十二国环境记者聚会北京
    本报讯　新华社记者徐耀中、本报记者肖体焕报道：亚洲健全环境与持续发展新闻研讨会日前在北京闭幕，来自尼泊尔、印度、巴基斯坦、孟加拉、泰国等11个国家的17位环境新闻记者，与我国的记者一起，就新闻媒介如何促进环境保护事业的发展等问题进行了广泛的探讨。
    10月23日开幕的这次研讨会，是由亚洲环境新闻工作者论坛和中国环境新闻工作者论坛联合举办的。亚洲环境新闻工作者论坛是目前世界上唯一的全洲性环境新闻工作者网络组织，举办环境新闻研讨会也是第一次。在研讨会上，12个国家的环境新闻工作者对80年代突出的全球性环境问题，如酸雨、臭氧层破坏、温室效应、危险废物等给予了特别的关注。他们表示要用自己的笔为改善地球生态环境、促进亚洲各国环保工作的交流和合作尽到自己的责任。
