Title: Over Thirty Commercial Overseas Cemeteries in Operation
Author: Jī Yúnchéng
Date: 1989-10-10
Edition: 2

	The reporter learned from the recently concluded Second National Funeral Services Conference that currently, cities such as Beijing and Shanghai have over thirty commercial overseas cemeteries. These cemeteries annually receive thousands of overseas Chinese, Hong Kong and Macao compatriots, Taiwanese compatriots, and foreign nationals of Chinese descent who return to their homeland to bury the ashes of their relatives.

	Recently, the Ministry of Civil Affairs approved Guangzhou, Hebei's Yixian County, and Xiamen to introduce foreign capital to trial-run Sino-foreign joint ventures or cooperative cemeteries.

### 经营性涉外公墓达三十多座
姬云程
1989-10-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    经营性涉外公墓达三十多座
    新华社哈尔滨十月九日电　（记者姬云程）记者从刚刚结束的第二次全国殡葬工作会议上了解到，目前，北京、上海等地共有经营性涉外公墓三十多座，每年要接待几千名海外侨胞、港澳同胞、台湾同胞和外籍华人回乡安葬亲人骨灰。最近，民政部又批准广州、河北易县和厦门等地引进外资，试办中外合资或合作经营的公墓。
