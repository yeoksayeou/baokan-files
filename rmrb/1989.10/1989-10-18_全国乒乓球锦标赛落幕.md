Title: National Table Tennis Championship Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-10-18
Edition: 4

The National Table Tennis Championship concluded tonight at the Shijingshan Sports Gymnasium.

In the men's singles final, Beijing veteran Chen Zhibin defeated Sichuan's Xiao Zhan with a score of 3:0 to win the championship. He also teamed up with Yu Shengpeng from the national team to win the men's doubles title. Henan's Deng Yaping defeated Hubei's Chen Jing with a score of 3:1 to win the women's singles championship. She also partnered with Ma Wenjie from the national team to win the mixed doubles title. The women's doubles champions were Hubei's national team players Chen Jing and Hu Xiaoxin.

### 全国乒乓球锦标赛落幕

1989-10-18
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    全国乒乓球锦标赛落幕
    本报北京10月17日讯　全国乒乓球锦标赛今晚在石景山体育馆结束。
    北京老将陈志斌在决赛中以3∶0战胜四川的肖战，获得男子单打冠军。他还与国家队的于沈潼合作，夺得男双冠军。河南的邓亚萍以3∶1击败湖北的陈静，获女子单打冠军。她与国家队马文革合作，还取得了混双冠军。女双冠军是来自湖北的国手陈静和胡小新。
