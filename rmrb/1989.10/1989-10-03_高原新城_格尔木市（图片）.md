Title: The Plateau New City – Golmud
Author: Han Wuping
Date: 1989-10-03
Edition: 5

With the construction of the Qinghai-Tibet Highway, Golmud City in Qinghai Province has gradually risen. Recently, the city's building area has reached over 1.4 million square meters. Trade is thriving, and both domestic and foreign tourists are increasing. It is like a brilliant pearl, nestled at the foot of the Kunlun Mountains.

    高原新城——格尔木市
