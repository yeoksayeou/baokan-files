Title: Egypt Proposes Three Principles to Promote Middle East Peace; PLO Leader Rejects Baker Plan
Author:
Date: 1989-10-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 16th.

According to today's report by Egypt's *Al-Ahram* newspaper, Egyptian Foreign Minister Esmat Abdel-Meguid has written a letter to UN Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar, further elaborating on Egypt's stance on the Middle East peace process.

The letter, handed over by Egypt's Permanent Representative to the UN, Abdel-Halim Badawi, addresses substantive issues such as the critical stage of diplomatic contacts between Egypt, the United States, and the PLO.

In addition to insisting on Egypt's previously proposed 10-point peace plan, the letter summarizes Egypt's stance on the Middle East peace process into three principles:

	• Egypt will not interfere in the selection of Palestinian representatives to negotiate with Israel. All reports that Egypt has proposed a list of Palestinian representatives are false and groundless.
	• The Egypt-sponsored Palestinian-Israeli dialogue has no topic restrictions. The two sides can raise any issue at the negotiating table.
	• Egypt will continue its contacts with the United States and the PLO, aiming to persuade Israel to agree to dialogue and advance the peace process.

Observers here note that Egypt's three principles are clearly aimed at breaking the stalemate in the Middle East peace process caused by Israel's refusal to engage in dialogue with the Palestinians.

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, October 16th.

The leadership of the Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO) on October 16th rejected the proposal by U.S. Secretary of State James Baker for talks between Palestinians and Israelis, viewing it as an attempt to advance Israel's plan for elections in the occupied territories.

A PLO spokesman stated that PLO Executive Committee Chairman Yasser Arafat sought the Central Committee's endorsement of this stance.

The PLO Central Committee on October 16th discussed the proposals for Palestinian-Israeli talks put forward by Baker and Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak. Arafat delivered a speech at the meeting. Earlier, Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Shamir had apparently poured cold water on the American and Egyptian proposals.

A Likud Party member stated on October 15th that the PLO's opposition would help ease American pressure on Israel to engage in talks with the Palestinians.

The spokesman said that the PLO Executive Committee document, read by Arafat, called on the Central Committee to reject Baker's plan and any other proposals that contradict the PLO's initiatives.

He said the document viewed the Baker plan as a new conspiracy to suppress the Palestinian initiative and support the Shamir plan.

He said the document called for accelerating the Palestinian uprising in the West Bank and Gaza Strip, and strictly adhering to the resolutions of the Palestine National Council and the Casablanca Arab Summit.

The PLO Central Committee meeting on October 16th discussed issues such as U.S.-PLO dialogue, the Shamir and Baker plans, Syrian-Palestinian relations, and Arab financial support for the PLO.

The spokesman said that Palestinian-Israeli talks must include representatives from the five permanent members of the UN Security Council, the UN Secretary-General, and all relevant parties.

### 埃及提出推动中东和平三原则  巴解领导人拒绝贝克计划

1989-10-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    埃及提出推动中东和平三原则
    巴解领导人拒绝贝克计划
    新华社开罗10月16日电  据埃及《金字塔报》今天报道，埃及外长马吉德已写信给联合国秘书长德奎利亚尔，进一步阐述埃及对待中东和平进程问题的立场。
    由埃及常驻联合国代表阿卜杜勒·哈利姆·巴达维转交了这一信件，信中涉及到埃及、美国和巴解组织三方进行的外交接触已进入关键阶段等实质问题。
    除坚持埃及早些时候提出的10点和平计划外，信件把埃及关于中东和平进程问题的立场概括为三项原则：
    ——埃及将不介入选择同以色列会谈的巴勒斯坦代表团成员问题。关于埃及已提出巴勒斯坦代表团成员名单的所有报道都是不真实的和毫无根据的。
    ——埃及所倡导的巴—以对话没有议题限制。对话双方在谈判桌上可以提出任何问题。
    ——埃及将从促使以色列同意对话的建议和推进和平进程的目的出发，同美国和巴解组织继续进行接触。
    这里的观察家指出，埃及提出的三项原则显然是为了打破在推进中东和平进程中由于以色列拒绝与巴勒斯坦对话而造成的僵局。
    新华社巴格达10月16日电  巴勒斯坦解放组织领导人16日拒绝了由美国国务卿贝克提出的巴勒斯坦和以色列举行会谈的设想，认为这种会谈是企图推动以色列提出的在被占领土举行选举的计划。
    巴解组织一位发言人说，巴解组织执委会主席阿拉法特要求该组织的中央委员会赞同上述态度。
    巴解组织中央委员会16日讨论了由贝克和埃及总统穆巴拉克提出的巴勒斯坦与以色列会谈的建议。阿拉法特在会上发表了讲话。在此之前，以色列总理沙米尔显然是对美国和埃及的设想泼了冷水。
    以色列利库德集团的人士15日说，巴勒斯坦的这种反对态度有助于缓和美国压迫以色列同巴勒斯坦人进行会谈。
    发言人说，由阿拉法特宣读的巴解组织执委会文件呼吁中央委员会拒绝贝克的计划和任何与巴解组织提出的倡议相悖的其他建议。
    他说，文件认为贝克计划是扼杀巴勒斯坦倡议和支持沙米尔计划的新阴谋。
    他说，文件呼吁加速约旦河西岸和加沙地带的巴勒斯坦起义，坚决恪守巴勒斯坦全国委员会决议和卡萨布兰卡阿拉伯首脑会议决议。
    巴勒斯坦中央委员会会议16日讨论了美巴对话、沙米尔和贝克计划、叙巴关系和阿拉伯财政资助巴解组织等问题。
    这位发言人说，巴以会谈必须要有联合国安理会5个常任理事国的代表、联合国秘书长和所有有关各方参加。
