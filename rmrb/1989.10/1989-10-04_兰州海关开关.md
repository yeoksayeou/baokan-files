Title: Lanzhou Customs Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, October 4th.

Lanzhou Customs, another inland customs office in China, officially opened on September 28th and began handling external customs business on October 1st.

Lanzhou Customs is directly under the leadership of the General Administration of Customs and is the 188th customs office established in China. Lanzhou Customs is responsible for customs operations in the provinces of Gansu, Qinghai, and the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region.

### 兰州海关开关

1989-10-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    兰州海关开关
    新华社兰州电  我国又一内地海关——兰州海关9月28日开关，10月1日办理海关对外业务。
    兰州海关直属海关总署领导，是我国建立的第188个海关。兰州海关负责甘肃、青海、宁夏三省、区的海关业务。
