Title: National CPPCC Members Inspect Beijing Suburbs
Author: Wáng Yù
Date: 1989-10-06
Edition: 2

A delegation of over 30 National CPPCC members, forming the National CPPCC Beijing Region Inspection Team, visited Changping County in the suburbs of Beijing this morning.

It is understood that the delegates will spend 10 days inspecting Changping, Shunyi, the Asian Games Village, and other locations. They will also present opinions and suggestions on relevant issues to the Beijing Municipal Government.

### 全国政协委员视察北京市郊
王蔚
1989-10-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    全国政协委员视察北京市郊
    据新华社北京10月5日电　（记者王蔚）由30多名全国政协委员组成的全国政协北京地区视察团，今天上午来到北京市郊昌平县进行视察。
    据了解，委员们将用10天的时间先后到昌平、顺义、亚运村等地视察，并将就有关问题向北京市政府提出意见和建议。
