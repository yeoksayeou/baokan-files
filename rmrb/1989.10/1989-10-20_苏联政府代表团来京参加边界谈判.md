Title: Soviet Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing for Border Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1989-10-20
Edition: 2

A delegation from the Soviet government, led by Deputy Foreign Minister Rozhkov, arrived in Beijing this afternoon to participate in the fourth round of Sino-Soviet border negotiations. The delegation was welcomed at the airport by Deputy Foreign Minister Tian Zengpei, head of the Chinese government delegation, and Soviet Ambassador to China Troyanovsky.

### 苏联政府代表团来京参加边界谈判

1989-10-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    苏联政府代表团来京参加边界谈判
    新华社北京10月19日电　前来参加第四轮中苏边界谈判的苏联政府代表团团长、外交部副部长罗高寿一行于今天中午乘专机到达北京。中国政府代表团团长、外交部副部长田曾佩和苏联驻中国大使特罗扬诺夫斯基等前往机场迎接。
