Title: The Magnificent Mosques of Gansu's Linxia Hui Autonomous Prefecture
Author: Han Wuping
Date: 1989-10-13

Gansu's Linxia Hui Autonomous Prefecture is a region where numerous ethnic minorities reside. The prefecture is home to 758 mosques, each magnificent and vibrant, showcasing diverse styles and designs.
