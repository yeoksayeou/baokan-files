Title: English Version of Jiang Zemin's National Day Speech Published
Author:
Date: 1989-10-20
Edition: 4

Jiang Zemin's speech delivered at the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China has been published in English by the New Star Press. The French, Spanish, Japanese, German, Russian, and Traditional Chinese versions of the speech will also be published by this press in the near future.

### 江泽民国庆讲话英文版出版

1989-10-20
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    江泽民国庆讲话英文版出版
    新华社北京10月19日电  江泽民同志在庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年大会上的讲话，已由新星出版社出版英文版。讲话的法、西班牙、日、德、俄和中文繁体字版也将由这个出版社陆续出版。
