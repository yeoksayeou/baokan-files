Title: Gabon Exposes Anti-Government Plot
Author:
Date: 1989-10-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Dakar, October 3rd

Libreville - Gabon recently exposed a plot against President Omar Bongo. Several people have been arrested in the capital, Libreville.

### 加蓬揭露反政府阴谋

1989-10-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    加蓬揭露反政府阴谋
    据新华社达喀尔10月3日电  利伯维尔消息：加蓬最近揭露一起反对邦戈总统的阴谋，首都利伯维尔已有多人被捕。
