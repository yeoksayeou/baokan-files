Title: Collection of Twentieth-Century Foreign Educational Classics
Author:
Date: 1989-10-20
Edition: 6

The Anhui Education Press has published a series titled "Collection of Twentieth-Century Foreign Educational Classics." This series includes works such as "The Changing Concept of Educational Evaluation" by Ralph W. Tyler, "Education and the New Man" by William G. Bagley, "Defending Classical Education" by R. W. Livingston, "All Children Can Learn" by Benjamin S. Bloom, and "A History of Educational Problems" by Paul Woodruff. The series compiles educational treatises by renowned scholars and educators from abroad in the 20th century. It includes retrospectives on educational history and analyses of contemporary educational issues, reflecting these educators' views on educational problems. Readers can learn about the research results and trends in contemporary world educational science from these translations, gain valuable knowledge, and broaden their horizons.

### 《二十世纪外国教育名著译丛》

1989-10-20
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：

    《二十世纪外国教育名著译丛》
    安徽教育出版社出版
    这套译丛包括《变化中的教育评价概念》（泰勒著）、《教育与新人》（威廉·G·巴格莱著）、《保卫古典教育》（R·W·利文斯通著）、《所有的儿童都要学习》（布卢姆著）、《教育问题史》（布鲁巴克著）等，收集了国外本世纪著名的学者、教育家的教育论著，其中有对教育历史的回顾，也有对现实教育问题的分析，反映出这些教育家对教育问题的见解，读者可以从这些译著中了解当代世界教育科学的研究成果与动向，汲取有益的知识，开阔自己的视野。
