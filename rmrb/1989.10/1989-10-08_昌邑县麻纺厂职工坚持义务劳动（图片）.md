Title: Workers of Changyi County Hemp Spinning Mill Persist in Voluntary Labor
Author: Liu Yongqin
Date: 1989-10-08
Edition: 2

The voluntary labor activities of the workers of Changyi County Hemp Spinning Mill in Shandong Province have persisted for half a year. The picture shows workers helping to soak unqualified hemp cakes in the raw material warehouse after work.

### 昌邑县麻纺厂职工坚持义务劳动（图片）
刘永勤
1989-10-08
第2版(国内新闻)

    昌邑县麻纺厂职工坚持义务劳动
