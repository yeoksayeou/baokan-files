Title: The Soviet Union Revokes Brezhnev's Medal
Author:
Date: 1989-10-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency,

The Soviet Union has revoked the Victory Medal awarded to former Soviet leader Leonid Brezhnev. On September 21, Soviet President Mikhail Gorbachev signed an order to cancel the medal, which was awarded to Brezhnev in February 1978. The order stated that the medal was revoked because its conferral did not comply with the regulations for awarding the Victory Medal.

### 苏联取消给勃氏的勋章

1989-10-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联取消给勃氏的勋章
    苏联最高苏维埃主席戈尔巴乔夫9月21日签署命令，取消1978年2月授予苏联前领导人勃列日涅夫的胜利勋章。命令说，取消向勃列日涅夫颁发的胜利勋章是因为这不符合该勋章颁发条例。
            （据新华社）
