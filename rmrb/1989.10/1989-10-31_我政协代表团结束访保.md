Title: Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Delegation Concludes Visit to Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1989-10-31
Edition: 3

The Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference delegation, led by Vice Chairman Ma Wenrui of the National Committee, concluded its eight-day visit to Bulgaria on October 24 and departed from Sofia by plane to return to China.

During their visit, Ma Wenrui and his delegation held friendly meetings and discussions with leaders of the Bulgarian Fatherland Front National Committee, including Kubadinski, and also met with leaders of the Bulgarian Agrarian National Union. They exchanged experiences and insights on how social and political organizations and democratic parties actively participate in national governance and assist the Communist Party in leading socialist countries. Both sides agreed that maintaining the leadership of the Communist Party and the socialist path is a necessary condition for the comprehensive development of socialist countries.

### 我政协代表团结束访保

1989-10-31
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我政协代表团结束访保
    新华社索非亚电　政协全国委员会副主席马文瑞率领的中国政协代表团10月24日结束了对保加利亚的8天访问后乘飞机离开索非亚回国。
    马文瑞一行在访问期间，同保加利亚祖国阵线全国委员会领导人库巴丁斯基等举行了友好会见和座谈，并会见了保加利亚农民党领导人，相互交流社会政治组织和民主党派积极参加国家管理、协助共产党领导社会主义国家的经验和体会。双方一致认为，坚持共产党的领导和社会主义道路，是社会主义国家全面发展的必要条件。
