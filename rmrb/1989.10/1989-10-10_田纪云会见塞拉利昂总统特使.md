Title: Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Meets with Special Envoy of the President of Sierra Leone
Author:
Date: 1989-10-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 9th.

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun met with and hosted a dinner for the Special Envoy of the President of the Republic of Sierra Leone, Second Vice President Sallia Jusu-Sheriff and his delegation, this evening at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

Sheriff arrived in Beijing this morning. Sierra Leone's Minister of Foreign Affairs, Abdul Karim Koroma, accompanied the visit.

Tian Jiyun welcomed the guests at the airport.

### 田纪云会见塞拉利昂总统特使

1989-10-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    田纪云会见塞拉利昂总统特使
    新华社北京10月9日电　国务院副总理田纪云今天晚上在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请塞拉利昂共和国总统特使、第二副总统萨利亚·朱苏·谢里夫一行。
    谢里夫是今天上午抵京的。塞拉利昂外交部长阿卜杜尔·卡里姆·科罗马随同来访。
    田纪云到机场迎接客人。
