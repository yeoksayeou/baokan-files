Title: Albanian Leader Meets Chinese Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1989-10-17
Edition: 3

Albanian Leader Meets Chinese Ambassador

Albanian Prime Minister Adil Çarçani on October 12th met with the outgoing Chinese Ambassador to Albania, Fan Chengzuo, expressing his hope for further development of relations between Albania and China. Çarçani asked Ambassador Fan to convey his greetings to Premier Li Peng and wished the Chinese people new achievements.

### 阿领导人接见我大使

1989-10-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿领导人接见我大使
    阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席查尔查尼12日在接见即将离任的中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使范承祚时，表示希望进一步发展阿中两国关系。查尔查尼请范承祚大使转达他对李鹏总理的问候，并祝中国人民取得新的成就。
            （据新华社）
