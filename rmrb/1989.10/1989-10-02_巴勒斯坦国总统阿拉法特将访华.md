Title: Palestinian President Arafat to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-10-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30th.

At the invitation of Chairman Yang Shangkun, President Yasser Arafat of the State of Palestine will pay an official friendly visit to China from October 4th to 5th.

### 巴勒斯坦国总统阿拉法特将访华

1989-10-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    巴勒斯坦国总统阿拉法特将访华
    新华社北京9月30日电　应国家主席杨尚昆的邀请，巴勒斯坦国总统亚西尔·阿拉法特将于10月4日至5日对中国进行正式友好访问。
