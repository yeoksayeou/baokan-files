Title: Large-scale Medical Glucose Factory in Jinan Military Region Completed and Put into Operation
Author: Zheng Ruiting
Date: 1989-10-15
Edition: 2

The largest medical glucose factory in the entire military—Jinan Military Region Medical Glucose Factory—has recently been completed and put into operation in the western suburbs of Jinan. The construction of this factory, with an annual production capacity of 3,000 tons, is of great significance in enhancing the self-reliance capabilities of the troops, alleviating market supply and demand contradictions, and promoting exports.

### 大规模医药葡萄糖厂在济南军区建成投产
郑瑞亭
1989-10-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    大规模医药葡萄糖厂在济南军区建成投产
    本报讯　全军规模最大的医药葡萄糖厂——济南军区医药葡萄糖厂，最近在济南西郊建成投产。建设这座年产3000吨的医药葡萄糖厂，对于增强部队自我保障能力、缓和市场供需矛盾、发展对外出口具有重要意义。　（郑瑞亭）
