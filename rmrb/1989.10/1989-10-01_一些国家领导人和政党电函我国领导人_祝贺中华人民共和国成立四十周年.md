Title: Some Foreign Leaders and Parties Send Congratulatory Messages to Chinese Leaders on the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1989-10-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30th.

In recent days, Chinese party and state leaders have received congratulatory telegrams from leaders of foreign parties and governments, warmly celebrating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The following sent joint telegrams to General Secretary Jiang Zemin, Premier Li Peng, and Chairman Wan Li:

- The General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, J. Batmonkh, and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers, D. Sodnom;
- The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, and the Council of Ministers;
- The Chairman of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, N. Gergely, the General Secretary, K. Grosz, the Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, S. F. Brunó, and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers, M. Nemeth.

French President François Mitterrand sent a congratulatory telegram to Chairman of the Central Military Commission Deng Xiaoping.

The following sent congratulatory telegrams to Chairman Yang Shangkun:

- The Chairman of the Presidency of the Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia, Janez Drnovšek;
- The President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Muhammad Zia-ul-Haq;
- The King of Nepal, Birendra Bir Bikram Shah Dev;
- The President of the People's Republic of Bangladesh, H. M. Ershad;
- The President of the Republic of India, Ramaswamy Venkataraman;
- The Governor-General of the Independent State of Papua New Guinea, Ignatius Kilage;
- The Leader of the September 1 Revolution of the Great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya, Muammar al-Gaddafi;
- The King of Morocco, Hassan II;
- The President of the Arab Republic of Egypt, Hosni Mubarak;
- The President of the Republic of Malta, Vincent Tabone;
- The First Secretary and Chairman of the Zimbabwe African National Union (Patriotic Front), and President of the Republic of Zimbabwe, Robert Gabriel Mugabe;
- The President of the Democratic People's Republic of Algeria, Chadli Bendjedid;
- The Governor-General of Canada, Jeanne Sauvé;
- The Queen of the Netherlands, Beatrix;
- The King of Norway, Olav V;
- The Queen of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, Elizabeth II;
- The Emperor of Japan, Akihito;
- The President of the United States of America, George H. W. Bush;
- The President of the French Republic, François Mitterrand;
- The President of the Swiss Confederation, Jean-Pascal Delamuraz;
- The Chairman of the Council of State of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, Võ Chí Công;
- The President of the Republic of Singapore, Wee Kim Wee.

The following sent congratulatory telegrams to Premier Li Peng:

- The President of the People's Republic of Bangladesh, H. M. Ershad;
- The Chairman of the Federal Executive Council of the Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia, Ante Marković;
- The Prime Minister of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Benazir Bhutto;
- The Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Rajiv Gandhi;
- The Prime Minister of the Democratic People's Republic of Algeria, Mouloud Hamrouche;
- The Prime Minister of Mauritius, Anerood Jugnauth;
- The Prime Minister of the Independent State of Papua New Guinea, Rabbie Namaliu.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30th.

In recent days, some foreign parties and party leaders have sent congratulatory telegrams and letters to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and General Secretary Jiang Zemin, warmly celebrating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The following sent congratulatory messages to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:

- The National Committee of the Communist Party USA;
- The Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninist), Conway;
- The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Palestine;
- The Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Portuguese Communist Party;
- The Central Committee of the Progressive Party of Working People of Cyprus.

The following sent congratulatory messages to General Secretary Jiang Zemin:

- The Chairman of the Presidency of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Milan Pančevski;
- The General Secretary of the All India Forward Bloc, Chitta Basu;
- The Chairman of the Progressive Socialist Party of Lebanon, Walid Jumblatt.
