Title: Wang Zhen Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-10-13
Edition: 2

Wang Zhen Meets with Japanese Guests

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 12th — Vice Chairman of the State Wang Zhen met with Professor Kamaya Fujii from Keio University in the Great Hall of the People this afternoon.

Wang Zhen introduced the domestic situation in China and the country's internal and external policies to the guest.

Kamaya Fujii was invited by the Institute of Modern International Relations in China.

### 王震会见日本客人

1989-10-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    王震会见日本客人
    新华社北京10月12日电　国家副主席王震今天下午在人民大会堂会见了日本庆应义塾大学教授神谷不二。
    王震向客人介绍了中国的国内形势以及对内对外政策。
    神谷不二是应中国现代国际关系研究所邀请来访的。
