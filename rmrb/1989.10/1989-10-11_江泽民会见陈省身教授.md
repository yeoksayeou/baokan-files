Title: Jiang Zemin Meets with Professor Chen Xingshen
Author:
Date: 1989-10-11
Edition: 1

Jiang Zemin Meets with Professor Chen Xingshen

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 10 — Tonight, CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin met and hosted a dinner for U.S. National Academy of Sciences academician, renowned mathematician, University of California, Berkeley professor, and director of the Nankai Mathematical Research Institute Chen Xingshen and his wife, Madame Zheng Shineng.

During the cordial and friendly conversation, Jiang Zemin highly praised Chen Xingshen for his long-standing dedication to China's educational cause and his contributions.

Jiang Zemin emphasized the importance of promoting traditional Chinese culture, especially the education of Chinese youth in national spirit, patriotism, and socialism.

Vice Minister of the State Education Commission He Dongchang attended the meeting and dinner.

Professor Chen Xingshen came to China at the invitation of the State Education Commission to conduct short-term work.

### 江泽民会见陈省身教授

1989-10-11
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民会见陈省身教授
    新华社北京10月10日电　中共中央总书记江泽民今天晚上在中南海会见并宴请了美国科学院院士、著名数学家、伯克莱加州大学教授、南开数学研究所所长陈省身和夫人郑士宁女士。
    在亲切友好的交谈中，江泽民高度评价陈省身多年来热心于中国的教育事业并作出了贡献。
    江泽民强调了发扬中华民族的传统文化、特别是对青少年要进行中华民族气节教育、爱国主义教育和社会主义教育的重要性。
    国家教委副主任何东昌参加了会见和宴会。
    陈省身教授是应国家教委的邀请来华进行短期工作的。
