Title: Li Peng and Wan Li Congratulate Xu Deheng on His 100th Birthday
Author: Zou Aiguo
Date: 1989-10-18
Edition: 1

Honorary Chairman of the China Democratic League Xu Deheng turned 99 today and entered his 100th year. Premier Li Peng of the State Council visited Beijing Hospital in the morning to represent the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council, and General Secretary Jiang Zemin in wishing him a happy birthday and good health.

Xu Deheng, dressed in a purple robe, sat on a sofa. In front of him were flower baskets sent by Jiang Zemin and Deng Yingchao, as well as birthday cakes sent by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the General Office of the State Council, the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, and the Central United Front Work Department.

The renowned political activist Xu Deheng was born in 1890 in Jiujiang, Jiangxi. He is one of the founders of the China Democratic League, and has served as the Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. In his early years, he participated in the Xinhai Revolution and the anti-Yuan Shikai movement, and was one of the student leaders of the May Fourth Movement. After the September 18th Incident, he actively participated in the resistance movement, calling for a fight to the end. During the War of Liberation, he served as a professor at Peking University, actively supporting student movements and participating in the struggle against civil war, hunger, and oppression, becoming a famous democratic professor. In 1949, he attended the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, participating in the preparatory work for the founding of the People's Republic of China. In March 1979, at the age of 89, he was introduced by Deng Yingchao and Ulanfu to join the Communist Party of China. His oath at the time was: "For the cause of communism, I will dedicate my life and struggle to the end!"

Li Peng, in his congratulatory remarks to Xu Deheng, said that Mr. Xu is an outstanding representative of Chinese intellectuals. From a democratic revolutionary warrior to a steadfast communist, he is a pride of the China Democratic League and also a pride of the Communist Party of China. Li Peng hoped that everyone would learn from his revolutionary firmness, his boundless concern and enthusiasm for the youth, his great communist ideals, unite as one, overcome difficulties, and advance the cause of the Chinese revolution.

The Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Wan Li, and Vice Chairmen Habib Cui, Peng Chong, and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme also visited the hospital to congratulate Xu Deheng. Wan Li said to Xu Deheng, "You have made great contributions to the history of the Chinese revolution. Congratulations on your 100th birthday!" Xu Deheng held their hands for a long time, reluctant to let go.

Wang Renzhong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, also visited the hospital in the morning to congratulate Xu Deheng on his 100th birthday on behalf of Chairman Li Xiannian.

This morning, those who also visited the hospital to congratulate included: Ding Guanggen, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Song Renqiong, vice chairman of the Central Advisory Commission, Luo Gan, secretary-general of the State Council, and Wu Lianyuan and Song Kun, vice ministers of the Central United Front Work Department.

The honorary chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, Yan Jici, and Jin Shengbao, chairman Zhou Peiyuan, as well as other responsible persons from the Central Committee and the Advisory Committee of the China Democratic League in Beijing also went to the hospital to congratulate.

### 李鹏万里等祝贺许德珩百岁生日  江泽民邓颖超送花篮
邹爱国
1989-10-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏万里等祝贺许德珩百岁生日
    江泽民邓颖超送花篮
    新华社北京10月17日电（记者邹爱国）九三学社名誉主席许德珩今天告别99岁，步入百年。国务院总理李鹏上午来到北京医院，代表党中央、国务院和江泽民总书记祝他生日愉快、健康长寿。
    身着紫红色长袍的许德珩端坐在沙发上。他的面前摆放着江泽民、邓颖超同志送来的花篮，全国人大常委会、政协全国委员会、国务院办公厅、九三学社中央、中央统战部送来的生日蛋糕。
    著名的政治活动家许德珩1890年生于江西九江。他是九三学社的创始人之一，曾任九三学社中央主席、全国政协副主席、全国人大常委会副委员长。他早年参加辛亥革命及讨袁运动，是五四运动的学生领袖之一。“九一八”事变后，他积极参加救亡运动，呼吁抗战到底。解放战争时期，他任北京大学教授，积极支持学生运动并参加反内战、反饥饿、反迫害的斗争，是著名的民主教授。1949年，他出席中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议，参与中华人民共和国成立的筹备工作。1979年3月，他89岁高龄时，由邓颖超、乌兰夫同志介绍加入中国共产党。他当时的誓言是：“为共产主义事业，生死以之，奋斗到底！”
    李鹏在向许德珩祝贺时说，许老是中国知识分子的优秀代表。他从一个民主主义革命战士到一名坚定的共产主义者，是九三学社的光荣，也是中国共产党的光荣。李鹏希望大家学习他革命的坚定性，学习他对青年无限的关怀和热忱，学习他远大的共产主义理想，团结一致，克服困难，把中国的革命事业推向前进。
    全国人大常委会委员长万里、副委员长习仲勋、彭冲、阿沛·阿旺晋美上午也到医院向许德珩表示祝贺。万里对许德珩说：“你在中国革命的历史上有很大贡献，祝贺你百岁生日”！许德珩握着他们的手久久不肯放下。
    全国政协副主席王任重代表李先念主席，上午也到医院祝贺许德珩百岁诞辰。
    今天上午，到医院祝贺的还有：中共中央政治局候补委员丁关根，中顾委副主任宋任穷，国务院秘书长罗干，中共中央统战部副部长武连元、宋堃等。
    九三学社中央名誉主席严济慈、金善宝，主席周培源以及九三学社中央委员会、参议委员会的其他在京负责人也前往医院祝贺。
