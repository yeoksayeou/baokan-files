Title: Han Xu Appointed President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-10-29
Edition: 2

	Han Xu has recently taken over from Zhang Wenjin as the president of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries.

	In an interview with Xinhua News Agency today, Han Xu said he was happy to take on the role of president of the association but also felt the weight of responsibility. He said he would work hard to promote friendship and mutual understanding between the Chinese people and people of other countries.

	Han Xu reiterated that the development of people-to-people diplomacy is aimed at promoting understanding, fostering friendship, maintaining world peace, and promoting exchanges and cooperation in various fields among countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit.

	Han Xu is 65 years old and has long been engaged in diplomatic work. He has served as the director of the Protocol Department, the director of the American and Oceanian Affairs Department, and the vice minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 韩叙任对外友协会长

1989-10-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　韩叙任对外友协会长
    新华社北京10月28日电　前驻美大使韩叙最近接替章文晋担任中国人民对外友好协会会长。
    韩叙今天在接受本社记者采访时说，他对担任对外友协会长的职务感到高兴，但又感到责任重大。他说，他愿意为增进中国人民同各国人民的友谊和相互了解而不断努力工作。
    韩叙重申，发展民间外交就是为了促进了解，发展友谊，维护世界和平，促进各国人民在平等互利的基础上开展各个领域里的交流和合作。
    韩叙今年65岁，长期在外交部门工作，曾任外交部礼宾司司长、美大司司长、副部长等职。
