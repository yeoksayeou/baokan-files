Title: Nigerian Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-10-09
Edition: 2

Nigerian Guests Arrive in Beijing

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 8th — At the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, Nigerian Army Chief of Staff General Sani Abacha and his delegation arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to make a friendly visit to China for six days.

Chinese People's Liberation Army Deputy Chief of Staff General Xu Huizhi welcomed the Nigerian guests at the airport and hosted a banquet in their honor in the evening.

### 尼日利亚客人抵京

1989-10-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    尼日利亚客人抵京
    据新华社北京10月8日电　应中国国防部的邀请，尼日利亚陆军参谋长萨尼·阿巴查中将一行今天下午乘飞机到达北京，对中国进行为期6天的友好访问。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长徐惠滋中将到机场迎接尼日利亚贵宾，并在晚上设宴欢迎。
