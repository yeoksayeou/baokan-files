Title: Chairman Yang Appoints and Dismisses Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1989-10-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 21st.

Chairman Yang Shangkun, in accordance with the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, has appointed Huang Zhi Liang as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Nicaragua. He has also relieved Yu Cheng Ren of his duties as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Nicaragua.

### 杨主席任免驻外大使

1989-10-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　杨主席任免驻外大使
    新华社北京10月21日电  中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会的决定，任命黄志良为中华人民共和国驻尼加拉瓜共和国特命全权大使；免去俞成仁的中华人民共和国驻尼加拉瓜共和国特命全权大使职务。
