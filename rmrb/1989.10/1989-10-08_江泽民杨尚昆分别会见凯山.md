Title: Jiang Zemin and Yang Shangkun Meet with Kaysone
Author: 
Date: 1989-10-08
Edition: 1

	Jiang Zemin and Yang Shangkun Meet with Kaysone
	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 7th – Correspondent Sun Yi reports: CCP Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin warmly received Lao People's Democratic Republic Prime Minister and Lao People's Revolutionary Party General Secretary Kaysone Phomvihane this evening at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse. The two sides had a friendly conversation.

	Kaysone Phomvihane said that now, the two parties of China and Laos not only have restored relations but have also further developed friendship and cooperation. Jiang Zemin expressed thanks for Kaysone Phomvihane's remarks. He said that we should cherish the friendship between the two parties.

	General Secretary Jiang Zemin also conveyed the greetings of Chairman Li Xiannian to this old friend, wishing him good health.

	Later, General Secretary Jiang Zemin hosted a dinner to entertain Kaysone Phomvihane and his delegation.

	Wang Zhaoguo, Director of the International Liaison Department, and the head of the Chinese Government's accompanying delegation, Minister of Labor Yuan Chongwu, attended the meeting and dinner.

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 7th – Correspondent Sun Yi reports: State Chairman Yang Shangkun, while introducing China's economic reforms to Lao People's Democratic Republic Prime Minister and Lao People's Revolutionary Party General Secretary Kaysone Phomvihane, said: "Both internal reforms and opening up to the outside world have a problem of being suitable for the country's level of development, and one must act according to one's abilities."

	The meeting was held in the Xinjiang Hall of the Great Hall of the People.

	At the beginning of the meeting, the two old friends exchanged greetings. Kaysone Phomvihane said, "In the past, I often came to China. At that time, we were in the period of armed struggle. Now, when we exchange views, it is generally about economic issues, but this does not mean we will abandon armed struggle." Yang Shangkun followed up, "Yes, we still need to hold onto our guns."

	Kaysone Phomvihane introduced the achievements of Laos' reforms to Chairman Yang. He expressed his satisfaction with the Chinese Communist Party's adherence to the four cardinal principles, the achievements of China's reform and opening up, and the improvement of party and state relations with this neighboring country. He said he would do his utmost to develop the relations between the two parties and countries, making them bloom and bear fruit, and pushing the relations between the two countries to a new level.

	Chairman Yang expressed his thanks for Kaysone Phomvihane's remarks. He said, "The past is the past, let's not dwell on it. Our friendly relations have deep roots. This normalization of relations between the two countries and parties is a good thing. I believe that through this visit, the relations between the two sides can develop to a new stage." Chairman Yang also said to Kaysone Phomvihane, "I hope that in the future, we will have more exchanges and introduce each other's situations."

### 江泽民杨尚昆分别会见凯山
孙毅
1989-10-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民杨尚昆分别会见凯山
    本报北京10月7日讯　记者孙毅报道：中共中央总书记江泽民今天晚上在钓鱼台国宾馆亲切会见了老挝部长会议主席、人民革命党总书记凯山·丰威汉。宾主在友好的气氛中进行了交谈。
    凯山·丰威汉说，现在，中老两党不仅恢复了关系，而且进一步发展了双方的友谊与合作。江泽民对凯山·丰威汉的讲话表示感谢。他说，我们要共同珍惜中老两党的友谊。
    江泽民总书记还转达了李先念主席对这位老朋友的问候，祝他身体健康。
    随后，江泽民总书记设晚宴招待了凯山·丰威汉一行。
    中联部部长朱良、中国政府陪同团团长、劳动部部长阮崇武等参加了会见和宴会。
    本报北京10月7日讯　记者孙毅报道：国家主席杨尚昆今天上午在向老挝部长会议主席、人民革命党总书记凯山·丰威汉介绍我国经济改革的情况时说：“对内改革、对外开放都有一个适合本国发展程度的问题，要量力而行”。
    会见是在人民大会堂新疆厅进行的。
    会见开始时，两位老朋友共叙友情。凯山·丰威汉说，过去我常来中国。那时候我们正是在武装斗争时期，现在我们之间交换意见，一般都是针对经济问题来的，但这并不是说我们要放弃武装斗争。杨尚昆接着说，对，枪还是要拿着的。
    凯山·丰威汉向杨主席介绍了老挝改革的成就。他表示对中国共产党坚持四项基本原则、对中国改革开放取得的成就、对同中国这个近邻国家改善了党和国家的关系感到高兴。他说，他将竭尽全力发展两党两国关系，使之进一步开花结果，把两国关系推向新的水平。
    杨尚昆表示感谢凯山·丰威汉的这一席话。他说，过去的事情就过去了，不管它了。我们之间的友好关系有着很深的根源。这次中老两国两党关系恢复正常是很好的事情。相信通过这次访问能使双方的关系发展到一个新的阶段。杨主席还对凯山·丰威汉说，希望我们今后还要多多往来，互相介绍情况。
