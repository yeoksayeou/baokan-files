    Panzhihua Steel's No. 4 Blast Furnace Commences Operation

    People's Daily, Chengdu, October 4, 1989

    The No. 4 blast furnace at Panzhihua Steel, one of the largest in the country and a key project of the national "Seventh Five-Year Plan," officially started producing iron on September 25.

    The No. 4 blast furnace at Panzhihua Steel has a volume of 1,350 cubic meters and a designed annual capacity of 8.3 million tons of iron. The total investment for the project is over 1.3 billion yuan, with 210 million US dollars directly borrowed from abroad. The project was funded and constructed by the enterprise itself, with a construction period of 29 months.

### 攀钢四号高炉投产

1989-10-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    攀钢四号高炉投产
    本报成都电　国家“七五”重点建设项目——全国最大高炉之一的攀钢四号高炉，9月25日正式出铁投产。
    攀钢四号高炉容积为1350立方米，设计能力为年产铁83万吨，工程总投资13亿多元。其中，直接借外资2.1亿美元。这项工程由建设企业自筹资金自组施工队伍进行，共用了29个月时间。
