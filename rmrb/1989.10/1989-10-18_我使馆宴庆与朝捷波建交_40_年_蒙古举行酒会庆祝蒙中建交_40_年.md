Title: Celebrations for 40 Years of Diplomatic Relations with North Korea, Czechoslovakia, Poland, and Mongolia
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-10-18
Edition: 3

Our Embassy Hosts Banquet to Celebrate 40 Years of Diplomatic Relations with North Korea, Czechoslovakia, and Poland
Mongolia Holds Reception to Mark 40 Years of Diplomatic Relations with China

	P'yŏngyang, October 13 (Xinhua) — Chinese Ambassador to North Korea Wen Yezhan hosted a banquet at the embassy tonight to celebrate the 40th anniversary of diplomatic relations between China and North Korea.

	Members of the Chinese Foreign Ministry delegation, who are visiting North Korea to participate in the 40th anniversary celebrations of diplomatic relations, and North Korean Vice Foreign Minister Kim Jae-suk, as well as officials from relevant departments, attended the banquet.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 9 — Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Wang Xingda hosted a dinner on October 9 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of diplomatic relations between China and Czechoslovakia. Czech Foreign Minister Johannes and other officials from relevant departments attended. Wang Xingda and Johannes both spoke at the event.

	Warsaw, October 10 (Xinhua) — Chinese Ambassador to Poland Pei Yuanying hosted a banquet at the embassy on October 10 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of diplomatic relations between China and Poland.

	Polish Foreign Minister Stefan Skubiszewski was invited to attend the banquet. Also present were Polish Deputy Foreign Minister Majewski and officials from the Poland-China Friendship Association.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, October 16 — The Executive Committee of the Mongolian Peace and Friendship Organizations and the Mongolia-China Friendship Association held a film and reception event today to celebrate the 40th anniversary of diplomatic relations between China and Mongolia.

	Vice Chairman of the Mongolian Peace and Friendship Organizations Executive Committee Bilegtei and Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Zhang Delin both spoke at the event.

	Mongolian Vice Chairman of the Great People's Khural Gurlagcha, First Deputy Foreign Minister Yundun, and other officials from the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia attended the film and reception event.

### 我使馆宴庆与朝捷波建交40年  蒙古举行酒会庆祝蒙中建交40年
徐宝康
1989-10-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我使馆宴庆与朝捷波建交40年
    蒙古举行酒会庆祝蒙中建交40年
    本报平壤10月13日电　记者徐宝康报道：中国驻朝鲜大使温业湛今晚在大使馆举行宴会，庆祝中朝建交40周年。
    正在朝鲜访问并参加中朝建交40周年庆祝活动的中国外交部代表团成员和朝鲜外交部副部长金在淑以及朝鲜有关部门负责人出席宴会。
    据新华社布拉格10月9日电　中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使王行达9日举行晚宴，庆祝中捷建交40周年。捷外长约翰内斯以及捷其他有关部门的负责人出席。王行达和约翰内斯分别讲话。
    新华社华沙10月10日电　中国驻波兰大使裴远颖10日在使馆举行宴会，庆祝中波建交40周年。
    波兰外交部长斯库比谢夫斯基应邀出席了宴会。出席宴会的还有波兰外交部副部长马耶夫斯基和波中友协负责人。
    据新华社乌兰巴托10月16日电　蒙古和平与友好组织联合会执委会和蒙中友协今天在这里举行电影酒会，庆祝中蒙建交40周年。
    蒙古和平与友好组织联合会执委会副主席比列格特和中国驻蒙大使张德麟分别讲话。
    蒙古大人民呼拉尔副主席古尔拉格查、外交部第一副部长云登以及中国驻蒙古大使馆外交官员等出席了电影酒会。
