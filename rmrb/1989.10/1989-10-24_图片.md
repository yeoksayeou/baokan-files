Title: Celebrating the Elderly
Author: Lán Hóngguāng
Date: 1989-10-24
Edition: 7

September 15 is Japan's traditional "Respect for the Aged Day." On this day, various celebrations are held nationwide for the elderly. The elderly in a residential area of Tokyo are dancing and singing at a special dance party held for the elderly, celebrating their own festival.
