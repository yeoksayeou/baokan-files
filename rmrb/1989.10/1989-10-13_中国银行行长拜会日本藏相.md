Title: President of the Bank of China Meets with Japanese Finance Minister
Author: Yu Qing
Date: 1989-10-13
Edition: 3

    President of the Bank of China Wang Dexian today met with Japanese Finance Minister Hashimoto Ryutaro in Tokyo.

    Wang Dexian briefed Hashimoto on the recent developments in China's financial sector and cooperation between the financial sectors of China and Japan. Hashimoto stated that China has many friends in the world, and as a neighboring country, Japan has even more friends in China.

    Chinese Ambassador to Japan Yang Zhenya was present at the meeting.

    Wang Dexian's visit to Tokyo was en route back to China from attending the annual meetings of the International Monetary Fund and the World Bank in the United States. On October 6, Wang Dexian met with former Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita Noboru.

### 中国银行行长拜会日本藏相
于青
1989-10-13
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    中国银行行长拜会日本藏相
    本报东京10月9日电　记者于青报道：中国银行行长王德衍今天在东京拜会了桥本龙太郎藏相。
    王德衍向桥本介绍了最近中国国内金融界和中日金融界合作的情况。桥本说，中国在世界上有很多朋友，日本与中国是近邻，中国在日本有更多朋友。
    拜会时中国驻日大使杨振亚在座。
    王德衍行长是在美国参加国际货币基金和世界银行年会后，回国途中顺访东京的。6日，王德衍拜会了日本前首相竹下登。
