Title: Former U.S. President Nixon to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-10-27
Edition: 2

The spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Li Zhaoxing, announced at today's press conference that at the invitation of the Chinese government, former U.S. President Richard Nixon will visit China starting from October 28.

### 美前总统尼克松将访华

1989-10-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　美前总统尼克松将访华
    新华社北京十月二十六日电　外交部发言人李肇星在今天举行的新闻发布会上宣布：应中国政府邀请，美国前总统理查德·尼克松先生将于十月二十八日起对中国进行访问。
