Title: More and More Compatriots from Taiwan Visit Xiamen for Sightseeing
Author: Huang Xiaoxiong
Date: 1989-10-19
Edition: 2

Recently, a large number of compatriots from Taiwan have come to Xiamen for tourism and sightseeing. The Xiamen Jinqiao Tourism Company, Xiamen International Travel Service, and Xiamen China Travel Service have all received large groups of Taiwanese tourists, each with over a hundred people.

According to statistics from the Xiamen Tourism Bureau, from January to September this year, the number of Taiwanese tourists visiting Xiamen reached 53,691, an increase of 32.50% compared to the same period last year. Among them, 6,401 Taiwanese tourists visited Xiamen in September alone, an increase of 14.82% compared to the same period last year.

The main reason for the increasing number of Taiwanese tourists visiting Xiamen is the growing economic and cultural exchanges between Xiamen and Taiwan.

### 厦门观光台胞最近不断增多
黄小雄
1989-10-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    厦门观光台胞最近不断增多
    新华社厦门十月十七日电（记者黄小雄）最近，又有大批台胞到厦门旅游观光。厦门金桥旅游公司、厦门国际旅行社、厦门中国旅行社等单位均接待了上百人的大型台胞旅行团。
    据厦门旅游局提供的数字表明，今年一至九月份，到厦门旅游的台胞人数为五万三千六百九十一人，比去年同期增长百分之三十二点五零。其中九月份到厦门的台胞为六千四百零一人，比去年同期增长百分之十四点八二。
    到厦台胞不断增多的主要因素是厦门与台湾日益发展的经济文化往来。
