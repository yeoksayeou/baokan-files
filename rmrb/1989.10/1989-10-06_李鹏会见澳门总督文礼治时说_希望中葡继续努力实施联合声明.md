Title: Li Peng Meets with Macao Governor Veiga when he says, "Hope that China and Portugal Continue to Work Together to Implement the Joint Declaration"
Author: Sun Yi
Date: 1989-10-06
Edition: 1

Li Peng Meets with Macao Governor Veiga when he says, "Hope that China and Portugal Continue to Work Together to Implement the Joint Declaration"

BEIJING, Oct. 5 (Xinhua) -- Premier Li Peng today met with Macao Governor Carlos Melo Veiga, who was visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese government. Li said he hopes that the joint declaration on the Macao issue between China and Portugal will serve as a model for implementing the "one country, two systems" policy and for resolving historical issues between countries based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

Li said that since the signing of the joint declaration on the Macao issue, both sides have been serious in their attitudes. He expressed satisfaction with the smooth development of relations between China and Portugal.

Li said that the Macao issue is a link between China and Portugal, and he hopes that both sides will work together to further implement the joint declaration and promote the comprehensive development of cooperation between the two countries in political, economic, and cultural fields.

Li also praised the Portuguese government's cautious attitude towards China's "crackdown on the counter-revolutionary riot" and said, "History will prove that your approach is wise."

Governor Veiga said that the Portuguese government will handle relations with China in accordance with the principle of mutual respect in international relations. He said that the Portuguese government will continue to strictly abide by the joint declaration on the Macao issue between China and Portugal.

In another development, Ma Jian, Director of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office, tonight warmly received Governor Veiga and his entourage at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse. After the meeting, Ma hosted a dinner for Veiga and his wife.

### 李鹏会见澳门总督文礼治时说  希望中葡继续努力实施联合声明
孙毅
1989-10-06
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏会见澳门总督文礼治时说
    希望中葡继续努力实施联合声明
    本报北京10月5日讯　记者孙毅报道：国务院总理李鹏今天下午在中南海紫光阁会见应邀来访的澳门总督文礼治时表示，希望中葡两国关于澳门问题的联合声明成为实行“一国两制”的典范，成为国与国之间在和平共处五项原则基础上解决历史遗留问题的典范。
    李鹏总理说，自中葡关于澳门问题联合声明签署以来，双方的态度都是认真的。
    他对中葡两国关系的顺利发展表示满意。
    李鹏说，澳门问题是连接中葡两国关系的一个纽带，希望双方为声明的进一步贯彻实施共同努力，并推动中葡两国在政治、经济、文化等各个领域合作的全面发展。
    李鹏总理还对葡萄牙政府对中国“平暴”行动采取的审慎态度表示赞赏。他说，“历史将证明，你们这样做是明智的”。
    文礼治总督表示，葡萄牙政府将本着负责的态度，依照国际关系中互相尊重的原则来处理对华关系问题。他说，葡萄牙政府将一如既往，严格遵守中葡两国关于澳门问题的联合声明。
    又讯　国务院港澳办公室主任姬鹏飞今天晚上在钓鱼台国宾馆亲切会见了澳门总督文礼治一行。会见后，姬鹏飞设晚宴招待了文礼治及其夫人。
