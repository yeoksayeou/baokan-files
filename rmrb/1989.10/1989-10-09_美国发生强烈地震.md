Title: A Strong Earthquake Occurs in the United States
Author:
Date: 1989-10-09
Edition: 3

A strong earthquake occurred in the United States. According to the U.S. Pacific Tsunami Warning Center, at 7:49 AM local time on Saturday, a 6.7 magnitude earthquake struck the seabed near Amchitka Island in Alaska.

The epicenter was located 210 kilometers southeast of the island. Four aftershocks occurred within an hour after the earthquake.

The center reported that no tsunami affecting the Pacific Ocean occurred, and there were no reports of casualties.

### 美国发生强烈地震

1989-10-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美国发生强烈地震
    据美国太平洋海啸警报中心报告，星期六当地时间上午七时四十九分，在美国阿拉斯加州阿姆奇特卡岛附近海底发生里氏六点七级的强烈地震。
    地震中心位于该岛东南二百一十公里处。在地震发生一小时后还发生了四次余震。
    该中心报告说，没有发生波及太平洋的海啸，也没有收到有关伤亡的报道。
                                    （据新华社）
