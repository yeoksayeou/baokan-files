Title: Workers in Kent County's Greenhouse
Author: Lù Yùnxiáng
Date: 1989-10-23
Edition: 7

Workers in the greenhouse of Turnham in Kent County, Britain, are managing tomatoes on an automated electric car. The pipelines laid between the tomato racks serve both as tracks for the automated electric car and as water pipes, making management more convenient. Photograph by New China News Agency reporter Lù Yùnxiáng.
