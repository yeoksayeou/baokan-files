Title: North Korean Newspaper Praises the Internationalist Spirit of Chinese Volunteers
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-10-26
Edition: 4

North Korean Newspaper Praises the Internationalist Spirit of Chinese Volunteers

North Korean Party and Government Agencies Lay Wreaths at the Sino-North Korean Friendship Tower

PLAINER, October 25 (Xinhua) — Reporter Xu Baokang reports: The Workers' Party of Korea's official newspaper, Rodong Sinmun, published an editorial today to mark the 39th anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' entry into the Korean War. The editorial praises the proletarian internationalist spirit of the Chinese People's Volunteers and their great achievements in Korea, emphasizing that the Korean people's firm will is to continue strengthening and developing Sino-Korean friendship.

Rodong Sinmun said in its editorial that the entry of the Chinese People's Volunteers into the Korean War demonstrated the Chinese people's fighting friendship with the Korean people, serving as a living example of proletarian internationalism. It made a great contribution to peace in Asia and the world, and its international status and influence are growing. The two countries are effectively building socialism, and Sino-Korean friendship will be further consolidated and developed.

The editorial of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's official newspaper, Minju Choson, pointed out that the Korean people highly value Sino-Korean friendship, and regard the achievements made by the Chinese people in their revolution and construction as their own achievements, wishing the Chinese people greater victories under the leadership of the Party.

PLAINER, October 25 (Xinhua) — Reporter Xu Baokang reports: To commemorate the 39th anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' entry into the Korean War, the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Central People's Committee of the Republic, and the Cabinet, as well as the Ministry of People's Armed Forces, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and the Ministry of Social Security, among other units, laid wreaths at the Sino-North Korean Friendship Tower located at the foot of Mount Taedong in the center of Pyongyang to commemorate the glorious achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteers.

The Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, General Choi Kwang, and the Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Vice Premier Kang Hye-won, attended the wreath-laying ceremony.

Chinese Ambassador to North Korea Wen Yizhan and Major General Tian Sheng of the Chinese People's Volunteers Committee of the Military Armistice Commission attended today's wreath-laying ceremony and laid wreaths on behalf of the Chinese Embassy and the Chinese People's Volunteers Delegation. In recent days, local party and government agencies and military units in Pyongyang, Kaesong, and other cities and counties where the Chinese People's Volunteers' martyrs are buried have also laid wreaths at the martyrs' tombs.

### 朝报盛赞我志愿军国际主义精神  朝党政机关向朝中友谊塔献花圈
徐宝康
1989-10-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　朝报盛赞我志愿军国际主义精神
　　朝党政机关向朝中友谊塔献花圈
    本报平壤10月25日电　记者徐宝康报道：朝鲜劳动党机关报《劳动新闻》今天就中国人民志愿军赴朝参战39周年发表社论，赞扬中国人民志愿军的无产阶级国际主义精神和在朝鲜建树的丰功伟绩，并强调继续加强和发展中朝友谊是朝鲜人民坚定的意志。
    《劳动新闻》在社论中说，中国人民志愿军赴朝参战体现了中国人民对朝鲜人民的战斗友谊，是无产阶级国际主义的活典范，对亚洲和世界和平做出了巨大贡献，国际地位和影响力日益提高。朝中两国正在卓有成效的建设社会主义，朝中友谊将进一步得到巩固和发展。
    朝鲜政府机关报《民主朝鲜》的社论指出，朝鲜人民十分珍视朝中友谊，并把中国人民在革命和建设中取得的成就，看作是自己的成就一样感到高兴，祝愿中国人民在党的领导下，取得更大的胜利。
    本报平壤10月25日电　记者徐宝康报道：为纪念中国人民志愿军赴朝参战39周年，今天朝鲜劳动党中央委员会、共和国中央人民委员会和政务院以及人民武装力量部、外交部、社会安全部等单位向位于平壤市中心牡丹峰山麓的朝中友谊塔敬献花圈，缅怀中国人民志愿军烈士的光辉业绩。
    朝鲜劳动党中央政治局候补委员、人民军总参谋长崔光大将和朝鲜劳动党中央政治局候补委员、政务院副总理姜希源等参加了献花圈仪式。
    中国驻朝鲜大使温业湛和朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面中国人民志愿军委员田胜少将参加了今天的献花圈仪式，并以大使馆和中国人民志愿军代表团的名义向友谊塔献了花圈。在平壤、开城等中国人民志愿军烈士陵墓，当地道、市、郡的党政机关和军事单位近日也向志愿军烈士墓献了花圈。
