Title: Wang Zhen Meets with Officials from the International Civil Defense Organization
Author:
Date: 1989-10-19
Edition: 2

Wang Zhen, Vice Chairman of the State, met with a delegation led by Khalid Tarawneh, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the International Civil Defense Organization and Director of the Jordanian Civil Defense, in the Great Hall of the People this afternoon.

The primary purpose of Khalid's visit to China is to exchange views with relevant Chinese authorities on issues related to disaster relief, prevention, mitigation, emergency response, and China's potential membership in the International Civil Defense Organization.

Wang Zhen stated that China is a country frequently affected by natural disasters. The Chinese government has done a lot of work in disaster relief but needs to exchange experiences and learn from international organizations and friendly countries. Regarding China's membership in the International Civil Defense Organization, the Chinese government will give it active consideration. Khalid welcomed this. During the meeting, both sides also expressed a desire to strengthen cooperation between China, the International Civil Defense Organization, and the Jordanian Civil Defense. Minister of Civil Affairs, Cui Naiwu, participated in the meeting.

### 王震会见国际民防组织官员

1989-10-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    王震会见国际民防组织官员
    新华社北京10月18日电  国家副主席王震今天下午在人民大会堂会见国际民防组织执委会主席、约旦民防局局长哈立德·塔拉沃奈一行。
    哈立德访华的主要目的是就自然灾害的救济、防灾、抗灾、急救工作，以及中国加入国际民防组织等问题同中国有关方面交换意见。
    王震说，中国是个自然灾害发生频繁的国家，中国政府在救灾方面做了许多工作，但需要同国际组织和友好国家交流经验、互相学习。对于中国加入国际民防组织一事，中国政府将予以积极考虑。哈立德对此表示欢迎。在会见中，双方还表示要加强中国与国际民防组织和约旦民防局之间的合作。民政部部长崔乃夫参加了会见。
