Title: Research on Zhou Enlai: Diplomatic Thought and Practice
Author: Shì Zhī
Date: 1989-10-05
Edition: 4

The book "Research on Zhou Enlai: Diplomatic Thought and Practice" will be published this month by the World Knowledge Press.

This book is the first specialized collection of essays on the study of Comrade Zhou Enlai's diplomatic thoughts and practices both domestically and internationally. It is edited by the Editorial Office of the History of Diplomacy of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and includes forty essays. Comrade Yang Shangkun wrote the title, and Comrades Li Peng and Li Xiannian wrote the prefaces.

### 《研究周恩来——外交思想和实践》将出版
世之

第4版(综合)
专栏：

    《研究周恩来——外交思想和实践》将出版
    本报讯《研究周恩来——外交思想和实践》一书本月将由世界知识出版社出版。
    本书是国内外研究周恩来同志外交思想与实践的第一部专题文集，由外交部外交史编辑室编辑，共收文稿四十篇。杨尚昆同志题写书名，李鹏、李先念同志写了序言。（世之）
