Title: Foreign Agricultural Innovations
Author: Wen Youren
Date: 1989-10-21
Edition: 7

In Poland, the use of scientific methods in apple cultivation allows the fruit to remain partially on the branches even after the leaves fall in autumn. This extends the harvesting period and enables natural preservation of the apples.

New China News Agency reporter Wen Youren photographed this.
