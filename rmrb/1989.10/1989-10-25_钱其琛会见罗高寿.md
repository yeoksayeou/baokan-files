Title: Qian Qichen Meets with Ro Gaus
Author:
Date: 1989-10-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 24th.

Qian Qichen Meets with Ro Gaus

Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met with Ro Gaus, the head of the Soviet government delegation and Deputy Foreign Minister, who came to participate in the fourth round of Sino-Soviet border talks, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse this afternoon.

During the meeting, Foreign Minister Qian pointed out that some progress had been made in the previous rounds of border talks. Both sides should conduct the negotiations seriously, adhere to and implement the relevant agreements reached during the high-level Sino-Soviet meetings, and strive to resolve the historical legacy of the Sino-Soviet border issue fairly and reasonably as soon as possible.

Present at the meeting were the head of the Chinese government delegation and Vice Foreign Minister Tian Zengpei, and the Soviet Ambassador to China, Troyanovsky.

### 钱其琛会见罗高寿

1989-10-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　钱其琛会见罗高寿
    新华社北京10月24日电　外交部长钱其琛今天下午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了前来参加第四轮中苏边界谈判的苏联政府代表团团长、外交部副部长罗高寿。会见时钱外长指出，在过去的几轮边界谈判中已经取得了一些进展，双方应该认真地进行谈判，恪守和落实中苏高级会晤的有关协议，争取早日公正合理地解决历史遗留的中苏边界问题。
    会见时在座的有中国政府代表团团长、外交部副部长田曾佩和苏联驻中国大使特罗扬诺夫斯基。
