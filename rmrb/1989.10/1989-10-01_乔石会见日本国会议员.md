Title: Qiao Shi Meets with Japanese Diet Members
Author:
Date: 1989-10-01
Edition: 2

Qiao Shi, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, met with the chairman of the Japan Socialist Party's Special Committee on China, House of Representatives member Minoru Kawakami, and the director of the Socialist Party's National Movement Bureau, Senate member Haji Fukuda, in the Great Hall of the People this afternoon. The Japanese guests were invited to visit China by the International Liaison Department.

### 乔石会见日本国会议员

1989-10-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    乔石会见日本国会议员
    新华社北京9月30日电　中共中央政治局常委乔石今天下午在人民大会堂会见了日本社会党日中特别委员会委员长、众议院议员河上民雄和社会党国民运动局长、参议院议员深田肇，日本客人是应中联部邀请来华访问的。
