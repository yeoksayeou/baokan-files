Title: Heilongjiang Reclamation Area Exceeds Wheat Quota
Author: Wáng Láixǐ
Date: 1989-10-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, October 12th – (Reporter Wang Laixi) Currently, the state farms in Heilongjiang Reclamation Area have delivered 855 million kilograms of wheat to the state, completing this year's wheat delivery quota 10% ahead of schedule.

Heilongjiang Reclamation Area is one of China's major commercial grain bases. This year, 11.4 million mu of spring wheat were sown. Due to timely sowing and the widespread adoption of scientific farming practices, a bumper harvest was achieved, with a total production of 1.8 billion kilograms, an increase of over 600 million kilograms compared to last year. The workers in various state farms, mindful of the nation's needs in times of plenty, carefully calculated and enthusiastically delivered new grain to the state. Statistics show that 90% of the wheat delivered from the entire reclamation area is of the first grade.

### 黑龙江垦区多交小麦
王来喜
1989-10-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    黑龙江垦区多交小麦
    据新华社哈尔滨10月12日电　（记者王来喜）目前，黑龙江垦区各国营农场已向国家上交小麦8.55亿多公斤，超额10％完成今年的小麦上交任务。
    黑龙江垦区是我国重要的商品粮基地之一，今年播种的1140万亩春小麦，由于播期适时，又普遍采用了配套科学种田措施，获得了大丰收，总产量达18亿公斤，比上年增产6亿多公斤。各农场职工丰收不忘国家，精打细算，踊跃向国家交售新粮。据统计，全垦区上交的小麦90％是一等粮。
