Title: Beijing Aviation Museum Opens
Author: Liú Xiāo
Date: 1989-10-24
Edition: 2

The newly opened Beijing Aviation Museum has not only attracted great interest from domestic tourists but has also caught the attention of the international aviation museum community.

Situated at the foot of Dàtāngshān Mountain in Changping County, on the outskirts of Beijing, the Aviation Museum fills a void in China's museum landscape. It covers an area of 480,000 square meters and houses over 70 types and more than 140 aircraft, as well as more than 500 samples of ground-to-air missiles, anti-aircraft guns, air defense radars, and aviation bombs.

### 北京航空博物馆落成
刘霄
1989-10-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　北京航空博物馆落成
    本报讯  记者刘霄报道：新近落成的北京航空博物馆不仅引起国内游人的极大兴趣，而且引起了国际航空博物馆界的注意。
    坐落于京郊昌平县大汤山脚下的航空博物馆填补了我国博物馆的一项空白。它占地四点八万平方米，收存有七十余种型号、一百四十多架飞机和地空导弹、高炮、防空雷达、航空炸弹等五百多种武器装备样品。
