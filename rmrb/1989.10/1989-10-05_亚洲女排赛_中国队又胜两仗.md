Title: China Women's Volleyball Team Wins Two More Matches at Asian Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-10-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, October 4th.

The Chinese women's volleyball team tonight won their third match in the preliminary round of the fifth Asian Women's Volleyball Championship by defeating Indonesia 3-0 (15-0, 15-3, 15-4).

On the evening of October 3rd, the Chinese team also defeated Australia 3-0.

### 亚洲女排赛  中国队又胜两仗

1989-10-05
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    亚洲女排赛
    中国队又胜两仗
    据新华社香港10月4日电  中国女子排球队今天晚上在这里以3∶0（15∶0、15∶3、15∶4）轻取印度尼西亚队，获得第五届亚洲女子排球锦标赛分组赛第三场比赛的胜利。
    10月3日晚上，中国队以3∶0轻取澳大利亚队。
