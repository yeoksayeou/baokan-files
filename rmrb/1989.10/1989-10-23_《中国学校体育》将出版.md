Title: China's School Sports to Be Published
Author: Lǐ Huá
Date: 1989-10-23
Edition: 4

A large-scale pictorial titled "China's School Sports" will be published and distributed soon, vividly showcasing the thriving development of school sports in China over the past 40 years since the founding of the nation. This bilingual (Chinese and English) pictorial vividly recounts the achievements in primary, secondary, and higher education, as well as in preschool education, reflecting the accomplishments in school sports and health work.

This pictorial is commissioned by the State Education Commission and jointly compiled and printed by the All-China Students' Federation and the All-China Middle School Students' Federation.

### 《中国学校体育》将出版
李华
1989-10-23
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　《中国学校体育》将出版
    本报讯　形象地展现建国四十年来我国学校体育蓬勃发展的大型画册《中国学校体育》将于近期出版发行，这部中英文对照的大型画册生动地记述了我国大中小学和幼儿教育等方面取得的成果，反映了学校体育卫生工作的成绩。
    这部画册是国家教委委托全国大学生体协和全国中学生体协联合编印的。　（李华）
