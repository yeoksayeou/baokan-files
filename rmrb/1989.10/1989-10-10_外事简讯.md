Foreign Affairs Brief

	Foreign Minister Qian Qichen concluded his friendly visits to Jordan, Egypt, Syria, Tunisia, and Iran and attended the 44th session of the United Nations General Assembly. He returned to Beijing by plane on the 9th.

	The delegation of the National People's Congress, led by Vice Chairman Wang Hanbin, concluded its friendly visits to Guinea-Bissau, Mali, Ivory Coast, and Cameroon. They returned to Beijing by plane on the 6th.

	North Korean Ambassador Chu Changjun held a film reception at the embassy in the afternoon of the 9th to celebrate the 44th anniversary of the founding of the Workers' Party of Korea. Department Head Zhu Liang of the International Liaison Department attended the reception.

	On the 9th, Wang Renzhi, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Minister of the Propaganda Department, met and hosted a banquet for the delegation of the Romanian Communist Party's theoretical journals "Socialist Era" and "Party Work." The delegation came to China at the invitation of the "Seeking Truth" magazine.

### 外事简讯

1989-10-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：外事简讯

    外事简讯
    ▲外交部长钱其琛结束对约旦、埃及、叙利亚、突尼斯和伊朗的友好访问并出席联合国第四十四届大会之后，于九日乘飞机回到北京。
    ▲由全国人大常委会副委员长王汉斌率领的全国人大代表团结束了对几内亚比绍、马里、科特迪瓦和喀麦隆等四国的友好访问之后，六日乘飞机回到北京。
    ▲朝鲜驻华大使朱昌骏九日下午在使馆举行电影招待会，庆祝朝鲜劳动党建党四十四周年。
    中联部部长朱良出席了招待会。
    ▲中共中央委员、中宣部部长王忍之九日在北京会见并宴请了罗马尼亚共产党中央政治理论刊物《社会主义时代》和《党的工作》杂志联合组成的罗共党刊代表团。
    代表团是应《求是》杂志社邀请来华访问的。
                                    （新华社）
