Title: Jiang Zemin Meets with Professor Xue Jundu
Author:
Date: 1989-10-10
Edition: 1

    Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the Communist Party of China, met with Professor Xue Jundu of the Political Science Department at the University of Maryland this afternoon in Zhongnanhai. They had a friendly conversation.

    Xue Jundu came to visit at the invitation of the International Strategic Studies Foundation.

### 江泽民会见薛君度教授

1989-10-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民会见薛君度教授
    新华社北京十月九日电　中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午在中南海会见了美国马里兰大学政治系教授薛君度，并同他进行了友好的谈话。
    薛君度是应国际战略研究基金会的邀请前来访问的。
