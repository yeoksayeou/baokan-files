Title: Kenyan President Moi Meets with Garang
Author:
Date: 1989-10-18
Edition: 3

Kenyan President Moi met with Sudanese rebel leader John Garang in Nairobi on the 16th to discuss the peace issue in Sudan.

Garang had just concluded a visit to six countries in southern Africa. He said that the leaders of the six countries he visited all supported his peace proposals.

According to reports, Garang's peace proposals mainly include establishing a broad-based government, forming a new Sudanese army that includes the Sudan People's Liberation Army, establishing a national constitutional assembly, and holding general elections.

### 肯尼亚总统莫伊同加朗会谈

1989-10-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    肯尼亚总统莫伊同加朗会谈
    据肯尼亚通讯社十六日报道，肯尼亚总统莫伊十六日在内罗毕同苏丹反政府武装人民解放军领导人加朗举行会谈，讨论苏丹和平问题。
    加朗刚刚结束对南部非洲六国的访问。他说，他所访问的六国的领导人都支持他的和平建议。
    据报道，加朗提出的和平建议主要内容包括，建立广泛基础的政府，组成包括苏丹人民解放军在内的新的苏丹军队，成立全国制宪会议和举行大选。
