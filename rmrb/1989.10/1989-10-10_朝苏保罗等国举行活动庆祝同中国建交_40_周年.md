Title: North Korea, Soviet Union, and Brazil Celebrate 40th Anniversary of Diplomatic Relations with China
Author: Zhū Kèchuān  Zhāng Hànwén  Zhōu Róngzǐ
Date: 1989-10-10
Edition: 3

North Korea, the Soviet Union, and Brazil hold events to celebrate the 40th anniversary of establishing diplomatic relations with China.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 6 (Reporter Zhu Kechuan) The Foreign Ministry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held a banquet at the Okryu Guesthouse in Pyongyang this evening to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the DPRK and China.

Kim Yong-nam, Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the DPRK, presided over the banquet and delivered a speech. He said that over the past 40 years since the establishment of diplomatic relations between the DPRK and China, the peoples of the two countries have fought side by side in the socialist revolution and construction, sharing life and death and a common destiny, leaving a proud record in the history of DPRK-China friendship. The deep-rooted DPRK-China friendship will become even more unbreakable as time goes by.

Kim Yong-nam, in discussing China's suppression of the counter-revolutionary rebellion, said that this event once again powerfully proves that no force can stop the Chinese people, led by the Communist Party, from advancing victoriously under the banner of socialism.

Qi Huaiyuan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, who is visiting Pyongyang, also delivered a speech at the banquet.

Before the banquet, Kim Yong-nam met with the Chinese Foreign Ministry delegation led by Qi Huaiyuan.

China's Ambassador to the DPRK, Wen Yizhan, attended the banquet this evening.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 4, the Soviet Union Friendship Society, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the House of Friendship with Peoples of Various Countries jointly held an evening event to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and the Soviet Union and the establishment of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Chaprin and others attended the evening event. The Chairman of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the famous Sinologist Zikhrensky, spoke. Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Yu Hongliang also spoke.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, October 4, Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria Li Fenglin held a banquet on October 4 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Bulgaria.

Bulgarian guests, including Politburo member and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Chairman of the Central Committee's Foreign Affairs Commission Balev, and First Deputy Foreign Minister Ivanov, attended the banquet.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, October 5, a "Chinese Culture Day" began in Chustendil, a city in southwestern Bulgaria, on October 5 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Bulgaria.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, October 5 (Reporters Zhang Hanwen, Zhou Rongzi) Romanian Finance Minister Jan Parang said today that the Romanian Party and government highly evaluate the achievements made in the comprehensive development of Romanian-Chinese relations over the past 40 years and are determined to make unremitting efforts to strengthen the friendship and unity between the Romanian and Chinese peoples.

Jan Parang made these remarks at a reception held by Chinese Ambassador to Romania Wang Jinqing to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Romania.

The Romanian Foreign Affairs Association and the Romanian-Chinese Friendship Association held a reception on October 4 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Romania. Romanian Deputy Prime Minister S. Andrei and Ambassador Wang Jinqing attended the reception and had a friendly and cordial conversation.

Meanwhile, the Romanian Socialist Cultural and Educational Commission and the Romanian-Chinese Friendship Association held an exhibition of photographs of the 40-year construction achievements of the People's Republic of China in Bucharest.

### 朝苏保罗等国举行活动庆祝同中国建交40周年
朱克川  张汉文  周荣子
1989-10-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    朝苏保罗等国举行活动庆祝同中国建交40周年
    据新华社平壤10月6日电（记者朱克川）朝鲜外交部今晚在平壤玉流馆举行宴会，庆祝朝中两国建交40周年。
    朝鲜政务院副总理兼外交部长金永南主持宴会并讲了话。他说，朝鲜和中国建立外交关系40年来，两国人民在社会主义革命和建设中并肩战斗，同生死，共命运，在朝中友谊史上留下了值得骄傲的纪录。根深蒂固的朝中友谊，随着岁月流逝将更加牢不可破。
    金永南在谈到中国平息反革命暴乱一事时说，这一事件又一次有力地证明，任何力量都阻挡不了共产党领导的中国人民高举社会主义旗帜胜利前进的步伐。
    正在平壤访问的中国外交部副部长齐怀远也在宴会上讲了话。
    宴会开始以前，金永南会见了由齐怀远率领的中国外交部代表团。
    中国驻朝鲜大使温业湛出席了今晚的宴会。
    据新华社莫斯科10月4日电　苏联对外友协、苏中友协和各国人民友谊之家4日联合举行晚会，庆祝中苏建交和中苏友协成立40周年。
    苏联外交部副部长恰普林等出席晚会。苏中友协主席、著名汉学家齐赫文斯基讲话。中国驻苏联大使于洪亮也讲了话。
    新华社索非亚10月4日电中国驻保加利亚大使李凤林4日举行晚宴，庆祝中保两国建交40周年。
    保共中央政治局委员、书记处书记兼保共中央外事委员会主席巴列夫和外交部第一副部长伊万诺夫等保加利亚客人出席了晚宴。
    据新华社索非亚10月5日电　保加利亚西南部城市丘斯滕迪尔5日开始举办“中国文化日”，庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年和中保建交40周年。
    据新华社布加勒斯特10月5日电（记者张汉文、周荣子）罗马尼亚财政部长扬·珀仓今天说，罗党和政府高度评价40年来在全面发展罗中关系方面所取得的成果，并决心为加强罗中两国人民之间的友谊和团结作出坚持不懈的努力。
    扬·珀仓是今晚在中国驻罗马尼亚大使王荩卿为庆祝中罗建交40周年而举行的招待会上说这番话的。
    罗马尼亚对外文协、罗中友协10月4日也为庆祝中罗建交40周年举行了招待会。罗马尼亚副总理斯·安德烈和王荩卿大使等出席了招待会，并进行了亲切、友好的交谈。
    与此同时，罗马尼亚社会主义文化和教育委员会、罗中友协在布加勒斯特举办了中华人民共和国40年建设成就图片展览。
