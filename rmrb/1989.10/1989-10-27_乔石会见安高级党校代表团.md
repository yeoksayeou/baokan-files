Title: Qiao Shi Meets with Delegation from Angola's High-Level Party School
Author:
Date: 1989-10-27
Edition: 1

Qiao Shi, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China (CPC) Central Committee and president of the CPC Central Party School, met with a delegation from the Antonio Agostinho Neto Doctoral High-Level Party School of the People's Movement for the Liberation of Angola (MPLA) – Labor Party in the Great Hall of the People this evening. The delegation was led by Zeferino Estevão Juliana, the school's president.

Both sides held friendly talks.

The delegation from the Angola High-Level Party School came to China at the invitation of the CPC Central Party School.

After the meeting, Qiao Shi hosted a banquet in honor of the Angolan guests.

### 乔石会见安高级党校代表团

1989-10-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　乔石会见安高级党校代表团
    新华社北京十月二十六日电　中共中央政治局常委、中央党校校长乔石今晚在人民大会堂会见了由校长泽费里诺·埃斯特旺·儒利阿纳率领的安哥拉人运—劳动党安东尼奥·阿戈斯蒂纽·内图博士高级党校代表团。
    双方进行了友好的谈话。
    安高级党校代表团是应中央党校的邀请前来访问的。
    会见后，乔石设宴款待了安哥拉客人。
