Title: Zheng Hongye Appointed as President of China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and China International Chamber of Commerce
Author:
Date: 1989-10-22
Edition: 2

	Zheng Hongye Appointed as President of China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and China International Chamber of Commerce
	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 21 — The China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the China International Chamber of Commerce recently elected Zheng Hongye as the President of both organizations.

	The 60-year-old Zheng Hongye previously served as the Minister Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Japan. He has also held the positions of Vice President and Acting President of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the China International Chamber of Commerce.

### 郑鸿业任中国贸促会国际商会会长

1989-10-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　郑鸿业任中国贸促会国际商会会长
    新华社北京十月二十一日电　中国国际贸易促进委员会、中国国际商会最近选举郑鸿业为中国贸促会、中国国际商会会长。
    现年六十岁的郑鸿业，曾任我国驻日本国大使馆公使衔参赞，中国贸促会、中国国际商会副会长、代会长。
