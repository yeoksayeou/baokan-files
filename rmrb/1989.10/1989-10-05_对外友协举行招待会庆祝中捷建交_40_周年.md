Title: China Council for the Promotion of International Friendship Holds Reception to Celebrate the 40th Anniversary of China-Czechoslovakia Diplomatic Relations

Author:

Date: 1989-10-05

Edition: 2

The China Council for the Promotion of International Friendship and the China-Czechoslovakia Friendship Association held a reception today to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Czechoslovakia.

Attending the reception were: Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Chen Mu Hua, Czechoslovakian Ambassador to China Eduard Saur, Head of the Czechoslovakian International Relations Association Friendship Delegation to China Stefan Mulin, and Head of the Power Expert Delegation Kubicek.

Vice President of the China Council for the Promotion of International Friendship Liu Gengyin, in his speech at the reception, said that the peoples of China and Czechoslovakia have a long-standing traditional friendship. Whether in the revolutionary war years or in the peaceful period of socialist construction, China and Czechoslovakia have always been sympathetic, supportive, and learning from each other. He expressed gratitude for the understanding and support given by the Czechoslovakian government and people to the measures taken by the Chinese government to quell the counter-revolutionary rebellion.

Liu Gengyin said that in recent years, with the normalization of relations between the two parties and the two countries, friendly cooperation between China and Czechoslovakia in various fields has been continuously developing, and mutual understanding and friendship between the peoples of the two countries have been deepening.

Ambassador Saur, in his speech, congratulated the People's Republic of China on the 40th anniversary of its founding. He expressed his belief that, with the joint efforts of the two countries and their peoples, the friendly cooperative relations between the two countries will definitely bear more abundant fruits, and the traditional friendship between the peoples of the two countries will be further consolidated.

### 对外友协举行招待会庆祝中捷建交40周年

1989-10-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    对外友协举行招待会庆祝中捷建交40周年
    据新华社北京10月4日电  中国人民对外友好协会、中国捷克斯洛伐克友好协会今天在这里举行招待会，庆祝中捷建交40周年。
    出席招待会的有：全国人大常委会副委员长陈慕华、捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使爱德华·萨乌尔、捷克斯洛伐克国际关系协会友好访华团团长什特凡·穆林、电力专家代表团团长库比切克。
    对外友协副会长刘庚寅在招待会讲话中说，中捷两国人民有着悠久的传统友谊，不论在革命战争年代，还是在建设社会主义的和平时期，中捷两国都是相互同情、支持、学习的。他对捷克斯洛伐克政府和人民对中国政府平息反革命暴乱所采取的措施给予的理解和支持表示感谢。
    刘庚寅说，近年来，随着中捷两党、两国关系的全面正常化，两国在各个领域的友好合作关系不断发展，两国人民之间的相互了解和友谊日益加深。
    萨乌尔大使在讲话中祝贺中华人民共和国成立40周年。他表示相信，在捷中两国、两国人民的共同努力下，两国的友好合作关系一定会结出更加丰硕的果实，两国人民的传统友谊定将更加巩固。
