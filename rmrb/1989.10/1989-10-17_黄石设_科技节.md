Title: Huangshi Establishes "Science and Technology Festival"
Author: Gōng Dáfā
Date: 1989-10-17
Page: 2

	Xinhua News Agency, Huangshi, October 16th - Reporter Gōng Dáfā

	Huangshi Establishes "Science and Technology Festival"

	Twenty-five years ago today, our country successfully detonated its first atomic bomb. Huangshi, Hubei, a city that made significant contributions to this achievement, has designated this day as the Science and Technology Festival, demonstrating the city's resolve to promote technological advancement. The establishment of this festival aims to continually enhance the scientific and technological awareness of the entire city, foster a societal culture that respects knowledge and science, motivate scientific and technological personnel, and sustain a continuous wave of learning and applying science to promote economic development.

### 黄石设“科技节”
龚达发
1989-10-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    黄石设“科技节”
    本报黄石十月十六日电  记者龚达发报道：二十五年前的今天，我国成功地爆炸了第一颗原子弹。曾经为之作出过贡献的原材料基地——湖北黄石市，把这一天定为科技节，表示了这个城市科技兴市的决心。建立这样一个节日，目的是要不断增进全市人民的科技意识，树立尊重知识、尊重科学的社会风尚，调动科技人员的积极性，持续不断地掀起学科学用科学的热潮，促进经济发展。
