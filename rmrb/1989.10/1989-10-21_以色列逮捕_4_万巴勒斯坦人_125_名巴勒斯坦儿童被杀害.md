Title: Israel Arrests 40,000 Palestinians, 125 Palestinian Children Killed
Author:
Date: 1989-10-21
Edition: 3

	Israel Arrests 40,000 Palestinians

	125 Palestinian Children Killed

	Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, October 19

	Since the outbreak of the uprising against Israeli occupation in the occupied territories, the Israeli military has arrested 40,000 Palestinians, of whom 13,600 are currently being held in Israeli prisons.

	According to reports from Israeli newspapers, due to the overcrowding in Israeli prisons, the authorities are currently building new concentration camps.

	Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, October 16

	Since the outbreak of the uprising against Israeli occupation in the occupied territories in December 1987, 125 Palestinian boys and children under the age of 16 have been killed by the Israeli occupying forces.

	According to a report from the Palestine News Agency today, the youngest Palestinian child recently killed by the Israeli military was 3-year-old Attiya Fayyani. He was shot in the head by Israeli soldiers on October 12 and died in an Israeli hospital on October 15.

### 以色列逮捕4万巴勒斯坦人  125名巴勒斯坦儿童被杀害

1989-10-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　以色列逮捕4万巴勒斯坦人
　　125名巴勒斯坦儿童被杀害
    新华社突尼斯10月19日电  自被占领土爆发反以占领起义以来，以色列军队共逮捕了40000巴勒斯坦人，其中13600人目前被关押在以色列监狱中。
    另据以色列报纸报道，由于以色列监狱已爆满，以当局正修建新的集中营。
    新华社突尼斯10月16日电  自1987年12月在被占领土爆发反以起义斗争以来，已有125名年龄不满16岁的巴勒斯坦少年儿童被以色列占领军杀害。
    据巴勒斯坦通讯社今天报道，以色列军队最近打死的年龄最小的巴勒斯坦儿童是年仅3岁的阿蒂亚·费亚尼。他是本月12日被以军开枪击中头部的，15日死在以色列一家医院中。
