Title: Jiang Zemin Meets with Romanian Communist Party Delegation, Stating that Developing Sino-Romanian Friendship is a Consistent Policy of Our Party
Author: Gù Yùqīng
Date: 1989-10-06
Edition: 1

Jiang Zemin Meets with Romanian Communist Party Delegation, Stating that Developing Sino-Romanian Friendship is a Consistent Policy of Our Party

	BEIJING, Oct. 5 (Xinhua) -- Xinhua reporter Gu Yuqing reports: CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin this afternoon at 3 p.m. cordially met with the delegation of the Romanian Communist Party visiting China, led by Jan Stoyan, a candidate member of the Central Committee and Central Committee Secretary of the RCP.

	Stoyan first conveyed the cordial greetings and good wishes of Comrade Nicolae Ceausescu and Comrade Elena Ceausescu to Jiang Zemin, Deng Xiaoping, Yang Shangkun, and Li Peng.

	Jiang Zemin expressed his thanks for this and requested Stoyan to convey his and the same greetings and wishes from Deng Xiaoping, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, and himself to Comrade Ceausescu and Comrade Elena Ceausescu.

	General Secretary Jiang stated that the CPC attaches great importance to Sino-Romanian friendship. Sino-Romanian relations have always been close. It is a consistent policy of our Party to fully, steadily, and long-term develop friendly relations between China and Romania.

	General Secretary Jiang reiterated that the People's Republic of China will, as always, adhere to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and the CPC will adhere to the four principles of party-to-party relations, and continue to develop friendly relations with countries and political parties.

	Stoyan said that the relations between our two parties are very good. Sino-Romanian relations are based on the principles of mutual respect, non-interference in internal affairs, and independence. We believe that this friendly cooperative relationship will be further developed and strengthened.

	Stoyan also conveyed the invitation from General Secretary Ceausescu for General Secretary Jiang to visit Romania and invited the CPC to send a delegation to attend the 14th Congress of the RCP.

	General Secretary Jiang once again expressed his thanks. He happily recalled his past visits to Romania and his work there. He said that at an appropriate time, he would revisit the old places.

	Present at the meeting were Zhu Liang, Director of the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee, and Ambassador of Romania to China, Angelo Mikulescu.

### 江泽民会见罗共访华代表团时说  发展中罗友谊是我党一贯方针
顾玉清
1989-10-06
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民会见罗共访华代表团时说
    发展中罗友谊是我党一贯方针
    本报北京10月5日讯　记者顾玉清报道：中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午3时在中南海亲切会见了由罗共中央候补执委、中央书记扬·斯托扬率领的罗马尼亚共产党访华代表团。
    斯托扬首先转达了尼古拉·齐奥塞斯库同志和埃列娜·齐奥塞斯库同志对江泽民、邓小平、杨尚昆和李鹏同志的亲切问候和良好祝愿。
    江泽民总书记对此表示感谢，并请斯托扬转达邓小平、杨尚昆、李鹏和他本人对齐奥塞斯库同志和埃列娜·齐奥塞斯库同志的同样问候与祝愿。
    江总书记表示，中国共产党非常重视中罗友谊，中罗关系一直很密切，全面、稳定、长期地发展中罗友好关系是我们党的一贯方针。
    江总书记重申，中华人民共和国将一如既往按照和平共处五项原则，中国共产党将按照党的关系的四项原则，继续发展同各国和各政党的友好关系。
    斯托扬说，我们两党的关系很好。中罗关系是建立在互相尊重、互不干涉内部事务、独立自主等原则基础上的，相信这种友好合作关系必将得到进一步发展和加强。
    斯托扬还转达了齐奥塞斯库总书记对江泽民总书记访罗的邀请，并邀请中共派代表团参加罗共十四大。
    江泽民总书记再次表示感谢。他愉快地回忆了过去多次访罗和在罗工作时的情景。他表示，适当的时候，他将会旧地重游。
    会见时，中联部部长朱良、罗马尼亚驻华大使安杰洛·米库列斯库等在座。
