Title: Xi'an Hosts Exhibition on Soong Ching-ling's Life and Shanghai Lays Foundation for Soong Ching-ling Kindergarten
Author: Meng Xi'an
Date: 1989-10-21
Edition: 4

The exhibition "The Life of Comrade Soong Ching-ling, Honorary Chairman of the State" opened today at the Youth Palace in the ancient city of Xi'an.

Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Chairman of the Soong Ching-ling Foundation Kang Keqing, and Secretary of the CPC Shaanxi Provincial Committee Zhang Boxing cut the ribbon for the exhibition.

The Shanghai Hongqiao area will soon have a large, modern kindergarten—Soong Ching-ling Kindergarten.

Executive Chairman of the China Welfare Institute Huang Hua and Chairman of the Shanghai Soong Ching-ling Foundation Wang Daohan today cut the ribbon and unveiled the plaque for the kindergarten's foundation.

After completion, the kindergarten will enroll 500 boarding children.

### 西安举办宋庆龄生平展  宋庆龄幼儿园在沪奠基
孟西安
1989-10-21
第4版(综合)
专栏：

　　西安举办宋庆龄生平展
　　宋庆龄幼儿园在沪奠基
    本报西安10月20日电　记者孟西安报道：《国家名誉主席宋庆龄同志生平展览》今日在古城西安青少年宫开幕。
    全国政协副主席、宋庆龄基金会主席康克清和陕西省委书记张勃兴为展览剪了彩。
    本报上海10月20日电　记者萧关根报道：上海虹桥地区将出现一所大型的现代化幼儿园——宋庆龄幼儿园。
  中国福利会执行主席黄华和上海宋庆龄基金会主席汪道涵今天为幼儿园奠基剪彩、揭碑。
    该园建成后，将招收寄宿制幼儿500名。
