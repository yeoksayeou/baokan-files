Title: Guangzhou Telephones Achieve Programmable Control
Author: Liang Zhaoming
Date: 1989-10-05
Edition: 2

Guangzhou's telephones achieved programmable control starting from midnight on September 28.

From this point on, all 170,000 telephone lines in the city have the advanced functions of direct domestic and international long-distance dialing and more.

### 广州电话实现程控化
梁兆明
1989-10-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    广州电话实现程控化
    本报广州电　记者梁兆明报道：广州市的电话9月28日零时起全部实现程控化。
    至此，全市17万门电话，门门具有直拨国内外长途电话等多种先进功能。
