Title: Yao Yilin Arrives in Berlin to Participate in Democratic Germany's National Day Activities
Author: Yǔ Hóngzhì, Wáng Défēng
Date: 1989-10-03
Edition: 3

Yao Yilin Arrives in Berlin to Participate in Democratic Germany's National Day Activities

BERLIN, Oct. 2 (Xinhua) -- At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and the Government of the German Democratic Republic, a Chinese party and government delegation led by Yao Yilin, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, arrived in Berlin this morning on a Chinese civilian aircraft. The delegation will pay a one-week official friendly visit to the Democratic Republic of Germany and participate in the celebrations marking the 40th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

Guenther Kleiber, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Democratic Republic of Germany, and others welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 姚依林抵达柏林  参加民主德国国庆活动
许宏治  王德峰
1989-10-03
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    姚依林抵达柏林
    参加民主德国国庆活动
    本报柏林10月2日电　记者许宏治、新华社记者王德峰报道：应德国统一社会党中央委员会和德意志民主共和国政府的邀请，以中共中央政治局常委、国务院副总理姚依林为首的中国党政代表团今天上午乘中国民航班机抵达柏林，对民主德国进行为期一周的正式友好访问，并参加德意志民主共和国成立40周年庆祝活动。
    德国统一社会党中央政治局委员、民主德国部长会议第一副主席京特·克莱伯等到机场迎接姚依林同志等中国党政代表团一行。
