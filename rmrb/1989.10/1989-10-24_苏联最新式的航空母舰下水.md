Title: The Soviet Union's Latest Aircraft Carrier Launched
Author:
Date: 1989-10-24
Edition: 3

The Soviet Union's latest aircraft carrier, the "Tbilisi," has recently been launched, according to a report in the Soviet Union's "Pravda" newspaper on the 19th.

Admiral Chernavin, Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Navy, told a reporter from the newspaper that the "Tbilisi" is the Soviet Union's first fully functional aircraft carrier. This new aircraft carrier is approximately 300 meters long, over 70 meters wide, has a power of 200,000 horsepower, a maximum speed of 30 nautical miles per hour, and can carry 60 aircraft. It will strengthen the Soviet Union's naval power.

### 苏联最新式的航空母舰下水

1989-10-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏联最新式的航空母舰下水
    据苏联《真理报》十九日报道，苏联最新式的第五艘航空母舰“第比利斯”号最近下水。
    苏联海军总司令切尔纳温海军上将对该报记者介绍说，“第比利斯”号是苏联第一艘功能齐全的航空母舰。这艘新航空母舰长约三百米，宽七十多米，有二十万匹马力功能，最高时速达每小时三十海里，可载六十架飞机，它将加强苏联的海军力量。
