Title: Capital Gathering to Commemorate World Standards Day
Author: Wu Xiaoming
Date: 1989-10-15
Edition: 2

Today marks the 20th World Standards Day. The State Bureau of Technical Supervision and the China Association for Standardization held a "Commemorative Conference for the 20th World Standards Day" in Beijing today. Vice Premier and Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission Song Jian, along with nearly 200 standardization workers from the capital, attended the conference.

### 首都集会纪念世界标准日
吴小明
1989-10-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    首都集会纪念世界标准日
    本报北京10月14日讯　今天是第20届世界标准日。国家技术监督局和中国标准化协会今天在北京举行“纪念第20届世界标准日大会”。国务委员、国家科委主任宋健和首都近200名标准化工作者参加了大会。　（吴小明）
