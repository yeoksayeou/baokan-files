Title: Retired Official of the Textile Industry Department, Su Jing, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-10-04
Edition: 2

A veteran communist fighter, an outstanding member of the Chinese Communist Party, and a retired senior official of the Textile Industry Department, Comrade Su Jing, passed away in Beijing on September 16, 1989, due to illness. He was 77 years old.

Comrade Su Jing joined the Communist Youth League in 1926 and the Chinese Communist Party in 1936. Before his passing, he served as the Director of the Office of the Textile Industry Department. According to Comrade Su Jing's will, the funeral will be simple, with no memorial service or farewell ceremony for the body.

### 纺织部离休干部苏镜逝世

1989-10-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    纺织部离休干部苏镜逝世
    新华社北京电　久经考验的共产主义战士，中国共产党优秀党员，纺织工业部离休老干部苏镜同志，因病于1989年9月16日在北京逝世，终年77岁。
    苏镜同志1926年加入共青团，1936年加入中国共产党，生前曾任纺织部办公厅主任。遵苏镜同志遗嘱丧事从简，不开追悼会，不举行遗体告别仪式。
