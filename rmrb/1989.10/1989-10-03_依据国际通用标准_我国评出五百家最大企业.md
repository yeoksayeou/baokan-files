Title: China's Top 500 Industrial Enterprises Selected Based on International Standards
Author: Zhang Chaowen
Date: 1989-10-03
Edition: 2

According to international standards, China has selected its top 500 industrial enterprises. Recently, the National Bureau of Statistics evaluated large and medium-sized enterprises from 1988 using internationally accepted criteria, ranking the top 500 industrial enterprises in China.

According to statistics, in 1988, these 500 enterprises achieved a total industrial output value of 301.2 billion yuan, accounting for 16.5% of the national industrial output value. They contributed over 60 billion yuan in taxes, approximately one-quarter of the country's fiscal revenue for that year. These 500 enterprises are mainly concentrated in key industries that significantly impact national economic development, including energy, raw materials, machinery, and electronics, representing the most powerful forces in these sectors. However, compared to international large-scale industrial enterprises, China's top 500 enterprises still have relatively small production scales and levels, and are in the developmental growth stage.

The top 20 of China's 500 largest enterprises are: Anshan Iron and Steel Company, Daqing Petroleum Administration Bureau, Wuhan Iron and Steel Company, Beijing Yanshan Petrochemical Company, Capital Iron and Steel Company, Shanghai Petrochemical Complex, Second Automobile Works, Shengli Oilfield Command, Fushun Petrochemical Company, Qilu Petrochemical Company, Daqing Petrochemical Company, Shanghai Baoshan Iron and Steel Company, Jilin Chemical Industry Company, First Automobile Works, Jinling Petrochemical Company, Benxi Iron and Steel Company, Maoming Petroleum Industry Company, Shanghai Gaohua Petrochemical Company, Taiyuan Iron and Steel Company, and Tianjin Petrochemical Company.

### 依据国际通用标准  我国评出五百家最大企业
张超文
1989-10-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    依据国际通用标准
    我国评出五百家最大企业
    据新华社北京10月2日电（记者张超文）谁可以进入中国工业的国家队？最近国家统计局运用国际通行的标准，对1988年大中型企业进行评估，排列出我国最大的500家工业企业。
    据统计，1988年这500家企业完成工业总产值3012亿元，占全国工业总产值的16.5％；上交利税600多亿元。约占当年国内财政收入的1／4，这500家企业主要分布在对国民经济发展影响较大的支柱行业，集中了我国能源、原材料、机械电子等主要行业最具实力的主力军。但是同国际上大工业企业相比，我国500家大企业的生产规模和水平尚低，还处于发展成长阶段。
    我国500家最大企业的前20名是：鞍山钢铁公司、大庆石油管理局、武汉钢铁公司、北京燕山石化公司、首都钢铁公司、上海石油化工总厂、第二汽车制造厂、胜利油田会战指挥部、抚顺石油化工公司、齐鲁石油化工公司、大庆石油化工总厂、上海宝山钢铁总厂、吉林化学工业公司、第一汽车制造厂、金陵石油化工公司、本溪钢铁公司、茂名石油工业公司、上海高桥石油化工公司、山西太原钢铁公司、天津石油化工公司。
