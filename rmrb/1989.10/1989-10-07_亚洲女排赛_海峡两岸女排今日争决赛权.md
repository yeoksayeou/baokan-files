Title: The Asian Women's Volleyball Championship: Teams from Both Sides of the Taiwan Strait Compete for a Spot in the Finals
Author: Yao Datian
Date: 1989-10-07
Edition: 4

The fifth Asian Women's Volleyball Championship group stage concluded today after five days of competition. The Chinese team and the Japanese team, as well as the South Korean team and the Chinese Taipei team, secured the top two spots in their respective groups and advanced to the semifinals.

During tonight's match against the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Chinese team frequently substituted new players. The result was a 15-7, 15-6, and 15-13 victory for China. With this win, China secured the top spot in Group B with a perfect 4-0 record. Japan finished second in Group B.

The semifinal matchups on October 7 will be: China vs. Chinese Taipei and Japan vs. South Korea.

### 亚洲女排赛  海峡两岸女排今日争决赛权
姚达添
1989-10-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    亚洲女排赛
    海峡两岸女排今日争决赛权
    据新华社香港10月5日电　（记者姚达添）第五届亚洲女排锦标赛分组赛经过5天角逐后今天在这里结束，中国队和日本队、南朝鲜队和中国台北队分获两个组的前两名，进入四强。
    今晚中国队在同朝鲜民主主义人民共和国队比赛过程中不时换上新队员。结果，中国队以15∶7、15∶6和15∶13获胜。至此中国队以4战全胜的成绩获得B组第一名。获B组第二名的为日本。
    7日四强的对阵形势是：中国队对中国台北队、日本队对南朝鲜队。
