Title: Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and the Soviet Union
Author: Wáng Shìlóng
Date: 1989-10-06
Edition: 2

The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Soviet Friendship Association held a reception today to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.

Attendees included Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Chong, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and President of the China-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Xiuquan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Zhang Wenjin, Soviet Ambassador to China Troianovskii, and Soviet experts and students in Beijing.

Wu Xiuquan, in his speech, said that China and the Soviet Union are neighboring countries with shared mountains and rivers. In the long-term revolutionary struggle and socialist construction, the two peoples have inspired, learned from, and supported each other, forging a deep friendship.

He pointed out that over the 40 years since the establishment of diplomatic relations, the two countries have experienced both good times and difficulties. However, even during the tough times in bilateral relations, the friendly feelings between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union have never been interrupted.

He said that the friendship associations of the two countries have played a very important role in promoting mutual understanding, exchanges, and friendship between the two peoples over the years.

He said, "In this new historical period of Sino-Soviet relations, I believe that the friendship associations of the two countries will definitely play a new and great role in promoting exchanges and cooperation in various fields between the two countries, and in activating exchanges between the two countries and their peoples."

Troianovskii, in his speech, first congratulated the People's Republic of China on the 40th anniversary of its founding. He said that the normalization of relations between China and the Soviet Union has opened up the possibility of further developing bilateral cooperation, and the prospects for cooperation between the two countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit are broad.

He said that the development of friendly and cooperative relations between the Soviet Union and China is in the interests of the peoples of the two countries and is also conducive to peace and development in Asia and the world.

### 对外友协庆祝中苏建交40周年
王士龙
1989-10-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    对外友协庆祝中苏建交40周年
    新华社北京10月5日电（记者王士龙）中国人民对外友好协会、中国苏联友好协会今天在这里举行招待会，庆祝中国同苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟建交40周年。
    出席招待会的有：全国人大常委会副委员长彭冲，中顾委常委、中苏友协会长伍修权，对外友协会长章文晋，苏联驻华大使特罗扬诺夫斯基以及在京的苏联专家和留学生等。
    伍修权在讲话中说，中苏是山水相连的邻国。两国人民在长期革命斗争和社会主义建设中，曾经相互鼓舞、学习和支持，结下了深厚的友谊。
    他指出，在中苏建交以来的40年中，两国关系有过美好时期，同时也经历过一段曲折，但是，即使在两国关系困难的日子中，中苏两国人民的友好感情始终没有中断。
    他说，中苏两国的友协组织多年来在促进两国人民的互相了解、交往和友谊方面，发挥了十分重要的作用。
    他说，“在中苏关系新的历史时期中，我相信两国友协组织也一定能在促进中苏各个领域联系与合作、活跃两国和两国人民交往方面发挥出新的巨大作用。”
    特罗扬诺夫斯基在讲话中首先祝贺中华人民共和国成立40周年。他说，中苏两国关系正常化为进一步发展双边合作开辟了可能性，两国在平等互利基础上发展合作的前景是广阔的。
    他说，苏中两国友好合作关系的发展有利于两国人民的利益，也有利于亚洲与世界的和平与发展。
