Title: The United Kingdom Launches the "Ten Commandments of Cancer Prevention"
Author: Wáng Guóruì
Date: 1989-10-18
Edition: 3

As part of the European Cancer Week, the United Kingdom recently launched the "Ten Commandments of Cancer Prevention" campaign.

This nationwide campaign aims to inform the public that changing unhealthy lifestyles is the first step in reducing the number of cancer deaths.

This campaign is also part of the European Cancer Year. It focuses on ten key points, aiming to reduce the number of cancer deaths by 15% by the year 2000.

These ten points were unanimously agreed upon by the health ministers of the European Community. They are:

1. Do not smoke. If you smoke, quit as soon as possible. Do not smoke in the presence of others.
2. Drink alcohol in moderation. Excessive alcohol consumption combined with smoking increases the risk of esophageal cancer.
3. Avoid excessive sun exposure.
4. When handling carcinogenic substances, follow safety procedures.
5. Eat more fresh fruits, vegetables, and high-fiber grains, and less fatty foods.
6. Avoid becoming overweight.
7. If you notice changes in moles, lumps, or unusual bleeding, consult a doctor.
8. If you experience persistent coughing, hoarseness, urinary problems, or unexplained weight loss, consult a doctor.
9. Women should have regular cervical smear tests.
10. Women should regularly check their breasts.

### 英国开展防癌十要运动
王国瑞
1989-10-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英国开展防癌十要运动
    新华社伦敦10月16日电（记者王国瑞）作为欧洲防癌周的一部分，英国最近开展了防癌十要运动。
    这项在全国范围内开展的运动告诉人们，改变不健康的生活方式是减少癌症死亡人数的第一步。
    这项运动也是欧洲防癌年的一部分。它集中提出10点要求，目的是到2000年把癌症死亡人数减少15％。
    这10点要求是欧洲共同体卫生部长一致同意的。它们是：
    1、不要抽烟。如果你抽烟，尽快戒掉。不要在别人面前抽烟。
    2、少喝酒。喝酒多，抽烟多，会增加食道癌的死亡率。
    3、避免过多晒太阳。
    4、处理致癌物质时，坚持安全操作。
    5、多吃新鲜水果、蔬菜和多纤维谷物，少吃脂肪食物。
    6、避免发胖。
    7、如果发现肿块、痣有变化和不正常出血，请医生诊治。
    8、如果经常咳嗽或哑嗓，便溺有问题，原因不明的消瘦，请医生诊治。
    9、妇女应该定期做子宫颈涂片检查。
    10、妇女应该定期检查乳房。
