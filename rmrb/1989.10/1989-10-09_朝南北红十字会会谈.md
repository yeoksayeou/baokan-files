Title: North and South Korean Red Cross Societies Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1989-10-09
Page: 3

The second round of working-level talks between the North and South Korean Red Cross Societies was held on the morning of October 6th in Panmunjom. The representatives from both sides discussed the implementation of the second round of visits by separated families and the mutual exchange of art troupes, as well as the holding of the 11th round of North-South Red Cross talks. However, no consensus was reached.

During the talks, the South Korean representatives proposed that the 11th round of North-South Red Cross talks be held in Pyongyang on November 1st of this year to discuss the mutual dispatch of the second round of separated family visits and art troupes.

### 朝南北红十字会会谈

1989-10-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    朝南北红十字会会谈
    朝鲜南北红十字会代表第二次工作会谈六日上午在板门店举行。双方代表就实现第二次离散家属故乡访问团和艺术团互访、举行第十一次南北红十字会会谈问题进行了讨论，但没有取得一致意见。
    南方代表在会谈中建议，第十一次南北红十字会会谈于今年十一月一日在平壤举行，以讨论互派第二次离散家属故乡访问团和艺术团问题。
