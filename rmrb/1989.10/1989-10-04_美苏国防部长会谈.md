Title: U.S. and Soviet Defense Ministers Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1989-10-04
Edition: 3

Soviet Defense Minister Yazov met with U.S. Defense Secretary Cheney on October 2 at the Pentagon and held the first-ever talks between the military leaders of the two countries.

This is the first time a Soviet defense minister has visited the United States.

### 美苏国防部长会谈

1989-10-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美苏国防部长会谈
    苏联国防部长亚佐夫10月2日在五角大楼会见美国国防部长切尼，并举行了两国军事首脑的首次会谈。
    这是苏联国防部长首次对美国进行访问。
            （本报专电）
