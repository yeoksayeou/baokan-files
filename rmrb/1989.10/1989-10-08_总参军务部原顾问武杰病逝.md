Title: Former Advisor to the General Staff Department's Military Affairs Department, Wu Jie, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-10-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 5th.

Former Advisor to the General Staff Department's Military Affairs Department, Wu Jie, passed away due to illness on March 27, 1989, in Beijing. He was 72 years old.

Wu Jie was from Bazhong County, Sichuan. He joined the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in 1932 and became a member of the Communist Party of China in 1935. He held various positions, including Clerk, Reconnaissance Squad Leader, Communications Chief, Battalion Commander, Division Chief, Deputy Director of the Military Affairs Department of the General Staff, Director, and Deputy Director.

### 总参军务部原顾问武杰病逝

1989-10-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    总参军务部原顾问武杰病逝
    新华社北京10月5日电　解放军总参谋部军务部原顾问武杰同志因病于1989年3月27日在北京逝世，终年72岁。
    武杰同志是四川巴中县人，1932年参加中国工农红军，1935年加入中国共产党，历任干事、侦察股长、通信主任、队列科长、军分区参谋长、总参军务部副处长、处长、副部长等职。
