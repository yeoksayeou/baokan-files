Title: Italy Men's Volleyball Team Dominates Europe
Author: Xǔ Fùruì
Date: 1989-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, October 1st

The European Men's Volleyball Championship concluded today here, with the Italian team securing the European men's volleyball crown for the first time. The host team, Sweden, took the silver medal.

In the semifinals, Sweden narrowly defeated the Soviet Union with a 3-2 victory. This marked the first time in over 20 years that the Soviet Union failed to reach the finals of the European Men's Volleyball Championship.

In the match for third and fourth places, the Netherlands defeated the Soviet Union 3-0, securing third place, while the Soviet Union finished fourth.

### 意大利男排称雄欧洲
许福瑞
1989-10-04
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    意大利男排称雄欧洲
    据新华社斯德哥尔摩10月1日电　（记者许福瑞）欧洲男排锦标赛今天在这里结束，意大利队首次登上欧洲男排冠军宝座。东道主瑞典队获亚军。
    瑞典队在半决赛中以3∶2险胜苏联队。这是苏联队在过去20多年中首次失去欧洲男排锦标赛决赛权。
    在决定第三、四名的比赛中，荷兰队以3∶0战胜苏联队，获得第三名，苏联队获第四名。
