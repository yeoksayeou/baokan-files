Title: Vice Chairman Ri Chong-yong Awards Medals to Chinese Military Delegation
Author: Zhang Jinfang
Date: 1989-10-11
Edition: 3

Vice Chairman Ri Chong-yong Awards Medals to Chinese Military Delegation

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 10 (Reporter Zhang Jinfang) - Today, Vice Chairman Ri Chong-yong of the State Affairs Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea awarded medals to the Chinese military delegation at the Mansudae Assembly Hall in Pyongyang.

Ri Chong-yong read the decree issued by the Central People's Committee of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, awarding medals and commendations to the Chinese military delegation. This decree was signed by President Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Ri Chong-yong, on behalf of the Central People's Committee of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, awarded the First Class National Flag Medal to the delegation leader, Vice Secretary of the Central Military Commission Liu Huaqing. He also awarded the First Class Friendship Medal to Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army Han Huizhi, Commander of the Lanzhou Military Region Zhao Xianshun, and Deputy Commander and Chief of Staff of the Jinan Military Region Ma Weizhi. Other members of the delegation and accompanying personnel were awarded the Second Class Friendship Medal and Friendship Commendation.

Vice Minister of the People's Armed Forces Ministry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea General Chon Won-sik and Chinese Ambassador to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Wen Yizhan attended the award ceremony.

On October 3, Standing Committee Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Minister of the People's Armed Forces Ministry Wu Jin-u met with the Chinese military delegation led by Vice Secretary of the Central Military Commission Liu Huaqing in Pyongyang.

### 李钟玉副主席向我军事代表团授勋
张锦芳
1989-10-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    李钟玉副主席向我军事代表团授勋
    新华社平壤10月10日电　（记者张锦芳）朝鲜国家副主席李钟玉今天在平壤万寿台议事堂向中国军事代表团授勋。
    李钟玉宣读了朝鲜中央人民委员会颁发的关于授予中国军事代表团勋章和奖章的政令。这个政令是由朝鲜国家主席金日成签署的。
    李钟玉代表朝鲜中央人民委员会授予代表团团长、中央军委副秘书长刘华清上将一级国旗勋章。他还将一级友谊勋章授予中国人民解放军副总参谋长韩怀智中将、兰州军区司令员赵先顺中将和济南军区副司令员兼参谋长马伟志中将。代表团其他团员和随行人员分别被授予二级友谊勋章和友谊奖章。
    朝鲜人民武装力量部副部长全文燮大将和中国驻朝鲜大使温业湛等出席了授勋仪式。
    朝鲜劳动党中央政治局常委、人民武装力量部部长吴振宇10月3日在平壤会见了以中央军委副秘书长刘华清为团长的中国军事代表团。
