Title: Qinghai Potassium Fertilizer Plant
Author: Han Wuping
Date: 1989-10-31
Edition: 5

Qinghai Potassium Fertilizer Plant is China's first large-scale potassium salt production enterprise, located in the Qaidam Basin's Chaerhan Salt Lake. The entire plant has fixed assets of 448 million yuan. After the first phase of the project goes into production, the annual output value can exceed 70 million yuan. Currently, it can provide 50,000 tons of potassium chloride and 100,000 tons of carnallite annually. The image shows the plant's main production workshop.
