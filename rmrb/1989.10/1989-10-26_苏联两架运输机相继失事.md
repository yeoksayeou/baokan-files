Title: Two Soviet Transport Planes Crash in Succession
Author:
Date: 1989-10-26
Page: 4

According to a report by the Soviet newspaper *Izvestia* on the 20th, on the night of the 19th, an Il-76 transport plane carrying construction machinery to the earthquake-stricken area in Armenia crashed near Leninakan in Armenia, resulting in the deaths of all 17 people on board.

Another report stated that on the evening of the 18th, a Soviet Il-76 military transport plane crashed in Azerbaijan due to an engine fire, killing all seven crew members and 50 paratroopers on board.

### 苏联两架运输机相继失事

1989-10-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏联两架运输机相继失事
    据苏联《消息报》二十日报道，十九日夜间，一架向亚美尼亚地震灾区运送建筑机械的伊尔—七六运输机在亚美尼亚境内的列宁纳坎市附近失事，机上十七名人员全部遇难。
    另据报道，十八日晚，苏联一架伊尔—七六军用运输机因发动机起火在阿塞拜疆境内失事，七名机组人员和五十名空降兵全部遇难。
