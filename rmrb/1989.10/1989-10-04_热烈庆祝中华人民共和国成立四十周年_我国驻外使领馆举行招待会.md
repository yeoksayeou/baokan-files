Title: Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China: Chinese Embassies and Consulates Hold Receptions
Author:
Date: 1989-10-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 4th

China's embassies and consulates in various European, North American, and Latin American countries, as well as the representative office in Geneva, have recently held receptions to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

**Europe**

- In Malta, over 300 dignitaries, including President Vincent Tabone, Acting Prime Minister Guido de Marco, and Speaker Lawrence Gonzi, attended the reception hosted by Ambassador Mei Ping.
- Over 300 guests, including United Nations Deputy Secretary-General and Director of the Geneva Office Martinsson, Secretary-General of the World Meteorological Organization Obasi, and Secretary-General of the International Telecommunication Union Buttler, attended the reception hosted by Ambassador Fan Guoxiang of the Chinese Permanent Mission to the United Nations in Geneva.
- Nearly 500 guests, including former NATO Secretary-General Luns, attended the reception hosted by Ambassador Liu Shan in Belgium.
- Over 700 guests from various sectors in the Federal Republic of Germany attended the reception hosted by Ambassador Mei Zhaorong.
- Over 300 guests from various sectors in Switzerland attended the reception hosted by Ambassador Cai Fangbo in Switzerland.
- In Madrid, over 500 guests, including Vice President of the Spanish Senate Juan Aresparacocha, attended the reception hosted by Ambassador Yuan Tao.

**North America**

- In Canada, 350 guests from various sectors, Chinese communities, and foreign diplomats attended the reception hosted by Ambassador Zhang Wenpo.
- Consul General Tang Fuxuan in Toronto and Consul General Duan Jin in Vancouver also held receptions.

**Latin America**

- In Port of Spain, over 450 guests, including President Noor Hassan Ali of Trinidad and Tobago, attended the reception hosted by Ambassador Zhang Ruizhi.
- In Georgetown, high-ranking officials and friends from various sectors, including President Desmond Hoyte of Guyana, attended the reception hosted by Ambassador Yang Zengye.
- In Montevideo, over 350 guests, including Deputy Speaker of the Uruguayan House of Representatives Cesar Bloom, attended the reception hosted by Ambassador Yang Xuqiang.
- In Guayaquil, Ecuador, about 150 guests attended the national day reception hosted by Consul General Jiang Zhanpan.

### 热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立四十周年  我国驻外使领馆举行招待会

1989-10-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立四十周年
    我国驻外使领馆举行招待会
    本报综合新华社消息　中国驻欧洲、北美洲和拉丁美洲一些国家使领馆及驻联合国日内瓦办事处代表近日分别举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    欧洲
    ▲马耳他总统文森特·塔博恩、代总理圭多·德马科、议长劳伦斯·冈齐和各界人士300多人出席了梅平大使举行的招待会。
    ▲出席中国驻联合国日内瓦办事处代表范国祥大使举行的招待会的有联合国副秘书长、日内瓦办事处主任马丁森、世界气象组织秘书长奥巴齐、国际电讯联盟秘书长布特莱等各界人士300多人。
    ▲出席中国驻比利时大使刘山举行的招待会的有前北约秘书长伦斯等各界人士近500人。
    ▲联邦德国各界来宾700多人出席了梅兆荣大使举行的招待会。
    ▲瑞士各界人士300余人出席了中国驻瑞士大使蔡方柏举行的招待会。
    ▲在马德里，西班牙参议院副议长胡安·阿雷斯帕科查加等500多名客人出席了原涛大使举行的招待会。
    北美洲
    ▲加拿大各界友好人士、华人团体和驻加外国使节共350人出席了中国驻加拿大大使张文朴举行的招待会。
    中国驻多伦多总领事唐福泉和驻温哥华总领事段津也分别举行了招待会。
    拉丁美洲
    ▲在西班牙港，特立尼达和多巴哥总统努尔·哈桑阿里等450名来宾出席了张瑞琮大使举行的招待会。
    ▲在乔治敦，圭亚那总统德斯蒙德·霍伊特等高级官员和各界朋友出席了杨增业大使的招待会。
    ▲在蒙德维的亚，乌拉圭众议院副议长塞萨尔·布鲁姆等350名客人出席了杨许强大使举行的招待会。
    ▲在厄瓜多尔瓜亚基尔，约150名客人出席了中国驻瓜亚基尔总领事蒋振潘举行的国庆招待会。
