Title: International Verification Committee Arrives in Honduras
Author:
Date: 1989-10-17
Edition: 3

The International Support and Verification Committee, established in accordance with the spirit of the Central American summit, arrived at the southern border camp in Honduras on October 12. The committee announced the dissolution of the Nicaraguan anti-government armed forces stationed there and called on them to return to their homes peacefully.

Approximately 10,000 members of the Nicaraguan anti-government armed forces are stationed in Honduras. On August 7, the presidents of the five Central American countries reached a peace agreement, calling for the establishment of a joint committee by the United Nations and the Organization of American States to assist in the demobilization and resettlement of the Nicaraguan anti-government armed forces in Honduras.

A United Nations official representing the United Nations Secretary-General's office conveyed the resettlement plan proposed by the Nicaraguan government to the Nicaraguan anti-government armed forces at the camp. According to this plan, the Nicaraguan government will establish several reception centers to handle the repatriation procedures for the returnees and provide two months of food supplies, as well as building materials and household items.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, October 13 - Tegucigalpa: Nicaraguan anti-government armed forces leader Enrique Bermúdez stated on the 13th that they are not prepared to withdraw from Honduras before December 8.

	Bermúdez made these remarks during a meeting with the International Support and Verification Committee, which was visiting the Nicaraguan anti-government armed forces camp in Honduras.

### 国际核查委员会抵洪都拉斯  宣布解散尼反政府武装  尼反政府武装不准备从洪撤走

1989-10-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    国际核查委员会抵洪都拉斯
    宣布解散尼反政府武装
    尼反政府武装不准备从洪撤走
    本报华盛顿10月14日电  据《华盛顿邮报》今天报道，根据中美洲首脑会议精神而建立的国际支持与核查委员会，10月12日到达洪都拉斯南部边境营地，向集结在那里的尼加拉瓜反政府武装力量官兵宣布解散这支武装力量，并要求他们和平地返回家园。
    尼加拉瓜反政府武装力量在洪都拉斯境内约有1万人。今年8月7日中美洲五国总统达成一项和平协定，要求联合国和美洲国家组织成立一个联合委员会，协助遣散并安置在洪都拉斯的尼反政府武装力量。
    代表联合国秘书长办公室的联合国官员在营地向尼反政府武装力量转达了尼加拉瓜政府提出的安置计划。根据这一计划，尼政府将设立若干接待中心，为遣返人员办理回国手续，并提供2个月的食品以及建房材料和家庭生活用具。
    据新华社墨西哥城10月13日电　特古西加尔巴消息：尼加拉瓜反政府武装领导人贝穆德斯13日表示，他们不准备在12月8日前从洪都拉斯撤走。
    贝穆德斯是同正在洪都拉斯访问尼反政府武装营地的国际支持与核查委员会会谈时说这番话的。
