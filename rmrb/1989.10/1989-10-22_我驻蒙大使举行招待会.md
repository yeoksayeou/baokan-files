Title: Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Hosts Reception
Author:
Date: 1989-10-22
Edition: 3

Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Zhang Delin hosted a reception on the evening of October 17 at the embassy to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Mongolia. Ambassador Zhang Delin and Mongolian Foreign Minister Gombosuren both spoke at the reception. Mongolian People's Great Hural Deputy Chairman Gurragcha and officials from various ministries and commissions of the Mongolian government attended the reception.

### 我驻蒙大使举行招待会

1989-10-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我驻蒙大使举行招待会
    中国驻蒙古大使张德麟17日晚在大使馆举行招待会，庆祝中蒙建交40周年。张德麟大使和蒙古外长贡布苏伦在招待会上分别讲了话。蒙古大人民呼拉尔副主席古尔拉格查和蒙古政府一些部委的负责人等出席了招待会。
          （据新华社）
