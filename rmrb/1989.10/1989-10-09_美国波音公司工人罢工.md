Title: Boeing Company Workers Strike
Author:
Date: 1989-10-09
Edition: 3

More than 57,000 mechanics of Boeing Aircraft Manufacturing Company began a strike early this morning, demanding an increase in basic wages and improvement of other benefits. This strike involves seven factories of Boeing Company and is the largest labor dispute in the company in 12 years.

### 美国波音公司工人罢工

1989-10-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美国波音公司工人罢工
    新华社华盛顿10月4日电　5.7万多名波音飞机制造公司的机械工人今天凌晨起开始罢工，要求增加基本工资和改善其它待遇。这次罢工涉及波音公司的7个工厂，是该公司12年来最大的一次工潮。
