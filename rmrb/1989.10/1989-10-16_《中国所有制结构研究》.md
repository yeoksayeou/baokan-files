Title: Study on China's Ownership Structure
Author: Lù Nánrú
Date: 1989-10-16
Page: 6

The book "Study on China's Ownership Structure" is authored by Xiǎo Liàng and published by Shanxi People's Publishing House and the Chinese Social Sciences Publishing House. This book is part of the series "Studies on China's Socialist Economic Structure," edited by Mǎ Hóng and Sūn Shàngqīng. The entire book discusses and analyzes the category of ownership, changes in China's ownership structure in the current stage, socialist public ownership, rural collective ownership, urban collective ownership (cooperative economy), individual economy in the current stage and the development of employing workers based on individual economy, state capitalism, socialist group ownership, township enterprises, economic associations, and the ownership relations of shareholding enterprises. (Lù Nánrú)
