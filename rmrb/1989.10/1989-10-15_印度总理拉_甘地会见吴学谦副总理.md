Title: Indian Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi Meets Vice Premier Wu Xueqian
Author:
Date: 1989-10-15
Edition: 3

Indian Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi met with Chinese Vice Premier Wu Xueqian this evening. He said that maintaining good relations between India and China is very important.

The two leaders exchanged views on bilateral relations and international issues of common interest during the meeting.

Rajiv Gandhi said that both countries face some new challenges and have their own experiences. They should strengthen cooperation in all fields and learn from each other's strengths to make up for their weaknesses.

Wu Xueqian expressed satisfaction with the development of relations between the two countries since Rajiv Gandhi's visit to China last December. He said that both China and India are populous developing countries facing common problems. They should increase exchanges, enhance understanding, and strengthen cooperation.

### 印度总理拉·甘地会见吴学谦副总理

1989-10-15
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    印度总理拉·甘地会见吴学谦副总理
    新华社新德里十月十三日电　印度总理拉·甘地今天傍晚在会见中国副总理吴学谦时说，印中两国保持良好关系十分重要。
    两国领导人在会见时就双边关系和共同感兴趣的国际问题广泛地交换了看法。
    拉·甘地说，两国都面临一些新的挑战，都有各自的经验，应该加强在各个领域的合作，取长补短。
    吴学谦对拉·甘地总理去年十二月访华以来两国关系的发展表示满意。他说，中国和印度都是人口众多的发展中国家，面临共同的问题，应该增加交往，增进了解，加强合作。
