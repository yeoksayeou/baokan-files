Title: Chinese Ambassadors to Latin American Countries Hold Receptions to Celebrate the 40th Anniversary of National Day
Author:
Date: 1989-10-10
Edition: 3

Chinese ambassadors to Bolivia and Suriname respectively held national day receptions to enthusiastically celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Chinese Ambassador to Bolivia Chen Dongsheng held a reception at the embassy on September 29. Chairman of the ruling party in Bolivia, the Revolutionary Left Movement, Ed and Senate President Balda, among 300 guests, attended at the invitation.

Chinese Ambassador to Suriname Shen Zhihuan held a reception at the embassy on October 2. Suriname President Shankar and Commander-in-Chief of the Suriname Armed Forces, Bouterse, attended at the invitation.

### 我驻拉美国家大使举行招待会  热烈庆祝国庆四十周年

1989-10-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我驻拉美国家大使举行招待会
    热烈庆祝国庆四十周年
    新华社北京10月9日电  中国驻玻利维亚和苏里南大使分别举行国庆招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    中国驻玻利维亚大使陈东声9月29日在使馆举行招待会，玻利维亚执政党左派革命运动主席埃德、参议院议长巴尔达等300人应邀出席。
    中国驻苏里南大使沈智焕10月2日在使馆举行招待会，苏里南总统尚卡尔和苏里南武装部队司令鲍特瑟应邀出席。
