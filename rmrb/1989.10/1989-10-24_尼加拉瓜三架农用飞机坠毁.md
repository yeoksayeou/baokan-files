Title: Three Agricultural Aircraft Crash in Nicaragua
Author:
Date: 1989-10-24
Page: 3

Three agricultural aircraft crashed in Nicaragua. Two of the planes crashed on October 21st while spraying pesticides over cotton fields in the eastern part of the country, resulting in the death of one pilot.

According to a report by the Nicaraguan newspaper *Nuevo Diario* on the 20th, an agricultural aircraft from the Nicaraguan Ministry of Agriculture crashed upon landing at the San José de Tilarán airport after completing a pesticide-spraying mission. The pilot survived, but the material damage exceeded $60,000.

### 尼加拉瓜三架农用飞机坠毁

1989-10-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　尼加拉瓜三架农用飞机坠毁
    尼加拉瓜二架农用飞机二十一日在该国东部为棉花种植园喷撒农药时坠毁，造成一名飞行员死亡。
    据尼加拉瓜《新日报》二十日报道，尼加拉瓜农业部一架农用飞机执行撒药任务后在圣何塞·德特立卡机场降落时坠毁，飞行员幸免于难，但物质损失超过六万美元。
