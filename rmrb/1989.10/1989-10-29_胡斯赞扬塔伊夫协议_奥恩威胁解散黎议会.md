Title: Hus Praises the Taif Agreement; Aoun Threatens to Dissolve the Lebanese Parliament
Author:
Date: 1989-10-29
Edition: 3

Lebanese Muslim Acting Prime Minister Salim Hus today said: "The Taif Agreement is the only means to save Lebanon."

In an interview with the Egyptian Middle East News Agency, Hus said: "Except for Aoun, all Lebanese people have a consistent view on the Taif Agreement."

He added: "There is nothing better than this agreement."

He pointed out: "Everyone basically supports this agreement, but on specific issues, 'everyone has reservations.'"

Hus emphasized that after some modifications, the agreement must be implemented. He said: "The train of peace has started, and no one can stop it."

Meanwhile, the leader of the Lebanese Christian militia government, Aoun, said today at a press conference: "It is impossible and groundless to elect a new president within 7 days. The necessary conditions for holding a smooth election must first be guaranteed."

The Arab League's three-party committee for mediating the Lebanese crisis proposed a few days ago that a new Lebanese president be elected within a week to implement the Taif Agreement.

Lebanese Christian militia government leader Aoun today threatened that unless Lebanese parliamentarians return to the country and reach a consensus on the controversial agreement reached at the Taif meeting, he will take severe measures, including dissolving the parliament.

General Aoun, in a speech to his supporters at the presidential palace, condemned Christian parliamentarians for accepting the terms of partial Syrian withdrawal at the Taif meeting in Saudi Arabia. He pointed out that the government will exercise various powers to deal with this "reckless behavior."

Since last week, when Lebanese Christian and Muslim parliamentarians reached an agreement in Taif on Syrian partial withdrawal and Lebanese political reforms, the Lebanese Christian faction, led by General Aoun, has consistently refused to accept the agreement, insisting that Syrian troops must completely withdraw from Lebanon and determine a withdrawal schedule.

### 胡斯赞扬塔伊夫协议  奥恩威胁解散黎议会

1989-10-29
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　胡斯赞扬塔伊夫协议
　　奥恩威胁解散黎议会
    新华社贝鲁特10月27日电  黎巴嫩穆斯林政府代总理萨利姆·胡斯今天说：“塔伊夫协议是拯救黎巴嫩的唯一手段。”
    在接受埃及中通社的采访时，胡斯说：“除奥恩以外所有黎巴嫩人都对塔伊夫协议持有一致观点。”
    他还说：“没有比这协议更好的了。”
    他指出：“大家大体上都支持这项协议”，但在具体问题上
  “每个人都有所保留”。
    胡斯强调，在作出一些修改后协议必须实施。他说：“和平的列车已经启动，谁也无法阻挡它。”
    与此同时，黎基督教军政府领导人奥恩今天在一次记者招待会上说：“要在7天之内选举出一位新总统是不可能的，没有根据的。举行一次顺利选举的必要条件首先必须得到保障。”
    阿盟调解黎危机3方委员会在几天前建议，在一周内选出黎新总统，实施塔伊夫协议。
    新华社贝鲁特10月27日电  黎巴嫩基督教军政府领导人奥恩今天威胁说，除非黎巴嫩议员回国并就塔伊夫会议有争议的协议取得一致意见，否则他将采取严厉措施，包括解散议会。
    奥恩将军在总统府对他的支持者发表演讲时，谴责基督教派议员在沙特塔伊夫城举行的会议上接受叙利亚部分撤军的条款。他指出，政府将行使各种权力对付这种“鲁莽的行为”。
    自上周黎巴嫩基督教派议员和穆斯林派议员在塔伊夫就叙利亚部分撤军和黎巴嫩政治改革问题达成协议以来，以奥恩将军为首的黎巴嫩基督教派一直拒绝接受这个协议，强调叙利亚军队必须完全撤出黎巴嫩，并确定撤军时间表。
