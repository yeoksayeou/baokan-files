Title: Celebrating the Double Ninth Festival
Author: Meng Renquan
Date: 1989-10-09
Edition: 2

October 8th marked the Double Ninth Festival. At the celebration held at the China Children and Youth Activity Center, 82-year-old Qinghai resident Mo Ruzhi encouraged the Young Pioneers from Beijing Beigongshilu Primary School to become the successors of the Four Modernizations.

### 欢度重阳节（图片）
孟仁泉
1989-10-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    欢度重阳节
    10月8日适逢重阳节，在中国儿童少年活动中心举行的老老幼幼欢度重阳节联欢会上，82岁的青海老人莫如志鼓励北京北礼士路一小的少先队员当好四化建设接班人。
                                  本报记者　孟仁泉摄
