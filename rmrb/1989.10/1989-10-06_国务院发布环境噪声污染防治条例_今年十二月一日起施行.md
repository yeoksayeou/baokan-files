Title: The State Council Issues Regulations on the Prevention and Control of Environmental Noise Pollution
Author:
Date: 1989-10-06
Edition: 1

The State Council has issued the "Regulations on the Prevention and Control of Environmental Noise Pollution of the People's Republic of China." This regulation, consisting of eight chapters and forty-seven articles, provides specific provisions on environmental noise standards and monitoring, industrial noise pollution prevention, construction noise pollution prevention, traffic noise pollution prevention, social life noise pollution prevention, legal responsibilities, and other aspects. The issuance and implementation of this regulation will help in preventing and controlling environmental noise pollution, ensuring a good living environment for people, and protecting human health.

This regulation will come into effect on December 1 of this year.

### 国务院发布环境噪声污染防治条例  今年十二月一日起施行

1989-10-06
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    国务院发布环境噪声污染防治条例
    今年十二月一日起施行
    新华社北京十月五日电　国务院总理李鹏今天签署发布了《中华人民共和国环境噪声污染防治条例》。这个条例共八章四十七条。它对环境噪声标准和环境噪声监测、工业噪声污染防治、建筑施工噪声污染防治、交通噪声污染防治、社会生活噪声污染防治、法律责任等都作出了具体规定。这个条例的发布实施，有利于防治环境噪声污染，保障人们有良好的生活环境，保护人体健康。
    这个条例自今年十二月一日起施行。
