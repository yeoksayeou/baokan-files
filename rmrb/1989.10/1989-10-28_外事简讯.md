Author:
Date: 1989-10-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 28th.

　　Foreign Affairs Brief
    ▲ Ambassador Betthold of the German Democratic Republic held a banquet at the embassy on the 25th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and the German Democratic Republic. Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Qichen and others were invited to attend.

    ▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Ni Zhifu met with a delegation from the trade unions of the German Democratic Republic led by State Secretary and Deputy Chairman of the Free German Trade Union Federation Johanna Tepfer on the evening of the 26th.

    ▲ Vice Premier and Minister of Finance Wang Bingqian met with a delegation from the Soviet Ministry of Finance led by First Deputy Minister V.G. Panskov at the Great Hall of the People on the evening of the 26th. The Soviet delegation exchanged views with Chinese financial officials on budget, taxation, and enterprise financial management.

    ▲ General Yang Baibing, Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, met with and hosted a banquet for a delegation from the Main Political Directorate of the Polish People's Army led by Deputy Director Zdzisław Rodziewicz at the Great Hall of the People on the 26th.

    ▲ Vice Premier and Minister of the Ministry of Machinery and Electronics Industry Zhu Rongji met with a delegation from the Thai government led by Minister of the Prime Minister's Office Anuwat Wanapongsiri at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on the afternoon of the 26th. Wanapongsiri came to discuss trade and economic cooperation with Chinese economic departments.

### 外事简讯

1989-10-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外事简讯
    ▲民主德国驻华大使贝特霍尔德25日在大使馆举行宴会，庆祝中、民德建交40周年。外交部长钱其琛等应邀出席。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、中华全国总工会主席倪志福26日晚会见了以国务委员、自由德国工会联合会副主席约翰娜·特普费尔为团长的民主德国工会代表团。
    ▲国务委员兼财政部长王丙乾26日晚在人民大会堂会见了财政部第一副部长弗·格·潘斯科夫率领的苏联财政部代表团。苏联代表团将与中国财政部门官员就预算、税收、企业财务管理等方面进行了广泛的交流。
    ▲中国人民解放军总政治部主任杨白冰上将26日在人民大会堂会见并宴请由总政副主任兹基斯瓦夫·罗兹毕茨基率领的波兰人民军总政治部代表团。
    ▲国务委员兼机械电子工业部部长邹家华26日下午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了由总理府国务部长阿努瓦·瓦那蓬西里率领的泰国政府代表团。瓦那蓬西里是前来同中国经济部门商谈贸易和经济合作事宜的。
                          （据新华社）
