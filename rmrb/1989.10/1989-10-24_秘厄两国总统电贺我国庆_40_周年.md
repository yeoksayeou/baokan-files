Title: Presidents of Peru and Ecuador Send Congratulatory Messages on the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the PRC
Author:
Date: 1989-10-24
Edition: 3

President Yang Shangkun recently received telegrams from the President of the Republic of Peru, Alan Garcia, and the Constitutional President of Ecuador, Rodrigo Borja, warmly congratulating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 秘厄两国总统电贺我国庆40周年

1989-10-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　秘厄两国总统电贺我国庆40周年
    新华社北京10月23日电　杨尚昆主席近日收到秘鲁共和国总统阿兰·加西亚和厄瓜多尔宪法总统罗德里戈·博尔哈发来的电报，热烈祝贺中华人民共和国成立40周年。
