Title: The Founding of the National Business Daily "Zhonghua Business Daily"
Author: Yang Zhaobo
Date: 1989-10-10
Edition: 4

A nationwide newspaper primarily targeting businesspeople, the "Zhonghua Business Daily," was launched in Beijing on October 5. This newspaper is published by the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce.

### 中华《工商时报》创刊
杨兆波
1989-10-10
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    中华《工商时报》创刊
