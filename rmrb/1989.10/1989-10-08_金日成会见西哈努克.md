Title: Kim Il-sung Meets Sihanouk
Author:
Date: 1989-10-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 7th.

Kim Il-sung and his wife met with Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, and his wife, who were visiting Pyongyang. The two sides had a warm and friendly conversation.

After the meeting, they watched a Cambodian film directed by Prince Sihanouk.

Vice-Chairman of the State Affairs Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Ri Chong-yong, and the Ambassador of Democratic Kampuchea to the DPRK, Song Ch'un, were present at the meeting.

On the same day, Kim Il-sung hosted a banquet for Prince Sihanouk.

Prince Sihanouk and his entourage arrived in Pyongyang on October 4th.

### 金日成会见西哈努克

1989-10-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    金日成会见西哈努克
    新华社平壤10月7日电  据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，朝鲜国家主席金日成和夫人6日会见了正在平壤访问的民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人，宾主进行了热情友好的谈话。
    会见结束后，他们一起观看了西哈努克亲王编导的一部柬埔寨故事片。
    朝鲜国家副主席李钟玉和民主柬埔寨驻朝鲜大使宋春会见时在座。
    这一天，金日成主席为西哈努克亲王举行了宴会。
    西哈努克亲王一行是4日抵达平壤的。
