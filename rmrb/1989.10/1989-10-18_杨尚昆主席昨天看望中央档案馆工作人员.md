Title: Chairman Yang Shangkun Visits Central Archives Staff
Author:
Date: 1989-10-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 17th.

Chairman Yang Shangkun visited the staff of the Central Archives today. October 8th marked the 30th anniversary of the Central Archives. This morning, Chairman Yang Shangkun visited all the staff of the Central Archives and some directors of provincial, autonomous region, and municipal archives bureaus and libraries.

Premier Wen Jiabao, Vice Chairman Wang Shoudao, Song Shiwu, and veteran leaders who have long been engaged in archival work, as well as relevant leaders who care about and support the archival cause, also went to visit. Subsequently, Chairman Yang Shangkun and other comrades toured the 30-year exhibition of the Central Archives.

### 杨尚昆主席昨天看望中央档案馆工作人员

1989-10-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨尚昆主席昨天看望中央档案馆工作人员
    新华社北京10月17日电　10月8日是中央档案馆建馆30周年。国家主席杨尚昆今天上午看望了中央档案馆全体工作人员及部分省、自治区、直辖市档案局、馆长。
    温家宝、王首道、宋时轮和长期从事档案工作的老领导老同志以及关心和支持档案事业的有关领导同志也一起前往看望。接着，杨尚昆等同志参观了中央档案馆30年展览。
