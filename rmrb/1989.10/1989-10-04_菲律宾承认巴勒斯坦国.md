Title: The Philippines Recognizes the State of Palestine
Author:
Date: 1989-10-04
Edition: 3

The Philippines announced on September 28 that it formally recognizes the State of Palestine and acknowledges the PLO as the sole legitimate representative of the Palestinian people.

### 菲律宾承认巴勒斯坦国

1989-10-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    菲律宾承认巴勒斯坦国
    菲律宾9月28日宣布正式承认巴勒斯坦国，并承认巴解组织是巴勒斯坦人民唯一合法的代表。
