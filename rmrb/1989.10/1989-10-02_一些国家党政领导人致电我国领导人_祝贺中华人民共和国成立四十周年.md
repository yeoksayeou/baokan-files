Title: Some Countries' Party and Government Leaders Send Congratulatory Messages to China's Leaders on the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1989-10-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 1st.

Some countries' party and government leaders have recently sent messages to Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Yang Shangkun, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Li Peng, Premier of the State Council, and Wan Li, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, Milos Jakes, the President of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic, Gustav Husak, the Chairman of the Federal Assembly, Alois Indra, and the Prime Minister, Ladislav Adamec, jointly sent a congratulatory message to Jiang Zemin, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, and Wan Li.

The Chairman of the Council of State and the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Cuba, Fidel Castro Ruz, sent a congratulatory message to Yang Shangkun and Li Peng.

The following sent congratulatory messages to Yang Shangkun:

- The Yang di-Pertuan Agong of Malaysia, Sultan Azlan Shah;
- The President of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, Ranasinghe Premadasa;
- The King of Spain, Juan Carlos I;
- The President of the Italian Republic, Francesco Cossiga;
- The President of the Republic of Suriname, Ramsewak Shankar;
- The President of the Republic of Tunisia, Zine El Abidine Ben Ali;
- The President of the Republic of Sierra Leone, Major General Joseph Saidu Momoh;
- The Chairman of the Council of State of the Republic of Guinea-Bissau, General Joao Bernardo Vieira;
- The Chairman of the Central Committee of the People's Revolutionary Party of Benin, President of the People's Republic of Benin, Head of State, and Chairman of the National Executive Committee, Mathieu Kerekou;
- The Chairman of the National Revolutionary Military Council of the Republic of Guinea, Head of State, and President, General Lansana Conte;
- The President of the Portuguese Republic, Mario Soares;
- The President of the Republic of Colombia, Virgilio Barco Vargas;
- The Chairman and Founder of the People's Revolutionary Movement of Zaire, Marshal Mobutu Sese Seko Kuku Ngbendu Wa Za Banga, President of the Republic of Zaire;
- The General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Ethiopia, President of the People's Democratic Republic of Ethiopia, and Commander-in-Chief of the Revolutionary Armed Forces, Mengistu Haile Mariam;
- The President of the Republic of Seychelles, France-Albert Rene;
- The President and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, General Ibrahim Babangida;
- The President of the Eastern Republic of Uruguay, Julio Maria Sanguinetti;
- The President of the Republic of Nicaragua, Daniel Ortega Saavedra;
- The President of the Co-operative Republic of Guyana, Hugh Desmond Hoyte;
- The President of the Hellenic Republic, Christos Sartzetakis;
- The President of the Republic of Indonesia, Suharto;
- The Acting President of the Republic of Venezuela, Alejandro Izaguirre.

The following sent congratulatory messages to Li Peng:

- The Prime Minister of Malaysia, Mahathir Mohamad;
- The Prime Minister of the Republic of Tunisia, Hamed Karoui;
- The Prime Minister of the Republic of Turkey, Turgut Ozal.

The Chairman of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Hungary, Szilveszter Mészáros, sent a congratulatory message to Wan Li.

### 一些国家党政领导人致电我国领导人  祝贺中华人民共和国成立四十周年

1989-10-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    一些国家党政领导人致电我国领导人
    祝贺中华人民共和国成立四十周年
    新华社北京10月1日电  一些国家的党政领导人近日致电江泽民总书记、杨尚昆主席、李鹏总理和万里委员长，热烈祝贺中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会总书记米洛什·雅克什、捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国总统古斯塔夫·胡萨克、联邦议会主席阿洛伊斯·英德拉和政府总理拉吉斯拉夫·阿达麦茨联合向江泽民总书记、杨尚昆主席、李鹏总理、万里委员长发来了贺电。
    古巴共和国国务委员会主席兼部长会议主席菲德尔·卡斯特罗·鲁斯向杨尚昆主席、李鹏总理发来了贺电。
