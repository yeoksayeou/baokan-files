Title: Why These New Graduates Are Smiling
Author: Cheng Yuyang
Date: 1989-10-28
Page: 5

This group of university students, who have only recently graduated, are smiling so happily. They come from Tsinghua University and other institutions, and have been assigned to the Beijing Municipal Construction Company No. 5. After touring the company's construction achievements, they genuinely smiled.
