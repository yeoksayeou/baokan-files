Title: Holographic Biology Comes to the Screen
Author: Lú Jìchuán
Date: 1989-10-05
Page: 2

Reported by our reporter Lu Jichuan: The Shanghai Science and Education Film Studio has produced the film "Holographic Embryo - New Discoveries in Biological Science," which premiered at Shandong University on September 25.

The film vividly presents the theory of holographic biology and its applications in medicine, agriculture, and other fields.

### 全息生物学搬上银幕
卢继传
1989-10-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    全息生物学搬上银幕
    本报讯　记者卢继传报道：上海科学教育电影制片厂摄制的《全息胚——生物科学的新发现》影片，于9月25日在山东大学举行首映式。
    该片以形象的画面，介绍了全息生物学理论及其在医学、农业等方面的应用。
