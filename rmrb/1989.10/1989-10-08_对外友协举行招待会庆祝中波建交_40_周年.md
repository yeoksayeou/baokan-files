Title: China-Poland Friendship Association Holds Reception to Celebrate 40th Anniversary of Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-10-08
Edition: 2

The China International Friendship Association and the China-Poland Friendship Association held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the 40th anniversary of diplomatic relations between China and Poland.

Vice Chairman Liu Gengyin of the China International Friendship Association said in his toast, "We are pleased to see that in recent years, the traditional friendship between the peoples of China and Poland has been further developed."

He said that the friendly cooperation between the two countries has a broad prospect, and the friendly organizations of the two countries will make greater contributions to the development and consolidation of this friendly cooperation, and to strengthen mutual understanding and friendship.

Polish Ambassador to China, M. Woszniak, in his speech, congratulated China on the great achievements it has made in the past 40 years. He said that in recent years, cooperation between China and Poland in the fields of economy, trade, science and technology, culture, and education has developed smoothly. He hoped that the two countries could continue to consolidate and develop the traditional friendship between their peoples.

Those attending the reception today included Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Hab Comin, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and President of the China-Poland Friendship Association Zhou Peiyuan, as well as diplomats from the Polish Embassy in China, journalists stationed in Beijing, and representatives of Polish experts and students in China.

### 对外友协举行招待会庆祝中波建交40周年

1989-10-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    对外友协举行招待会庆祝中波建交40周年
    新华社北京10月7日电　对外友协和中波友协今天下午在这里举行招待会，庆祝中国和波兰建交40周年。
    对外友协副会长刘庚寅在祝酒辞中说：“我们高兴地看到，近几年来，中波两国人民的传统友谊得到了进一步发展”。
    他说，两国之间的友好合作有着广阔的前景，两国友好组织将为发展和巩固这种友好合作、加强相互了解和友谊做出更大的贡献。
    波兰驻华大使马·沃希尼亚克在讲话中对中国在40年中所取得的伟大成就表示祝贺。他说，近几年来，中波在经济、贸易、科技、文化、教育等领域内的合作发展顺利。他希望两国能继续巩固和发展两国人民之间的传统友谊。
    出席今天招待会的有全国人大常委会副委员长习仲勋，全国政协副主席、中波友协会长周培源，以及波兰驻华使馆外交官、驻京记者、在华的波兰专家和留学生代表等。
