Title: Prime Ministers of Thailand and Western Samoa to Visit China Later This Month
Author:
Date: 1989-10-20
Edition: 2

Prime Ministers of Thailand and Western Samoa to Visit China Later This Month

	BEIJING, Oct. 19 (Xinhua) — The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Thailand, Chatichai Choonhavan, will pay a working visit to China from Oct. 24 to 26.

	This was announced today by the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The spokesperson also announced:

	At the invitation of Premier Li Peng of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, the Prime Minister of the Independent State of Western Samoa, Tofilau Eti Alesana, will pay an official friendly visit to China starting from Oct. 25.

	The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Bhutan, Dawa Tshering, will lead a delegation to Beijing on Oct. 29 to attend the sixth round of China-Bhutan border talks.

	At the invitation of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Executive Secretary of the United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific, Kim Hak-su, will visit China on Oct. 26.

### 泰国总理和西萨摩亚总理本月下旬来我国进行访问

1989-10-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    泰国总理和西萨摩亚总理本月下旬来我国进行访问
    本报北京10月19日讯　泰王国总理差猜·春哈旺将于10月24日至26日对我国进行工作访问。
    这是外交部新闻发言人今天宣布的。他还宣布：
    应中华人民共和国国务院总理李鹏的邀请，西萨摩亚独立国总理托菲劳·埃蒂·阿莱萨纳将于10月25日起对我国进行正式友好访问。
    不丹外交大臣达瓦·策林将于10月29日率团来北京参加中不第6轮边界会谈。
    应外交部邀请，联合国亚太经社会执行秘书基勃里亚将于10月26日访问我国。
