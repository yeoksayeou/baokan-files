Title: Poplar Forest
Author:
Date: 1989-10-30
Edition: 8

	Poplar Forest
	Like a row of mighty soldiers
	Guarding a tranquil expanse
	But they are not for reflecting
	Themselves
	Like a green Great Wall
	Blocking the desert storms
	Offering clarity to the ecosystem
	No longer polluting the eyes
	But they are not for reflecting
	Themselves
	Like a group of hot-blooded men
	Hand in hand, shoulder to shoulder, standing on the earth
	At night, guarding the starry sky
	By day, guarding the vast clouds
	But they are not for reflecting
	Themselves
	Like a series of beautiful harps
	Playing a series of melodious songs
	In summer, singing the scorching heat
	In spring, singing the inviting green
	But they are not for reflecting
	Themselves

### 胡杨林

1989-10-30
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　胡杨林
　　像一排威武的士兵
　　护卫着一片宁静
　　但它们不是为了倒映
　　自己
　　像一座绿色的长城
　　阻挡来自沙漠的暴风
　　让明净献给生态
　　不再污染一双双瞳孔
　　但它们不是为了倒映
　　自己
　　像一个个热血男儿
　　手拉手肩并肩屹立大地
　　夜里守满天星光
　　日里守云涛万里
　　但他们不是为了倒映
　　自己
　　像一架架美丽的竖琴
　　奏出一支支悠扬的歌曲
　　夏唱灼人的火热
　　春唱诱人的碧绿
　　但他们不是为了倒映
　　自己
