Title: Wang Zhen Meets with North Korean Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-10-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 1st.

Wang Zhen, Vice Chairman of the State, met with Li Chong-yul, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Vice Chairman of the State of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and his delegation this noon in Beijing.

Wang Zhen welcomed Li Chong-yul's delegation for participating in the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. He said that over the past 40 years, the Chinese people have achieved great successes. The support provided by the Korean people, led by Kim Il-sung, to the Chinese revolution and socialist construction has been a great inspiration to the Chinese people. Over the decades, the two countries have supported each other, and their friendship is precious.

Li Chong-yul said that the peoples of China and Korea are comrades-in-arms who have fought side by side. He came to celebrate the National Day of China with the same feelings as the Chinese people. He expressed his gratitude for the warm reception by the Chinese side.

After the meeting, Wang Zhen hosted a banquet for the North Korean guests. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Hab Comin attended the meeting and the banquet.

### 王震会见朝鲜客人

1989-10-02
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    王震会见朝鲜客人
    新华社北京十月一日电　国家副主席王震今天中午在北京会见了朝鲜劳动党中央政治局委员、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国家副主席李钟玉及其一行。
    王震对李钟玉率团前来参加中华人民共和国成立四十周年的庆祝活动表示欢迎。他说，四十年来，中国人民取得了伟大的成就。由金日成领导的朝鲜人民对中国革命和社会主义建设的支援，是对中国人民的巨大鼓舞。几十年来，中朝两国互相支援，友谊是宝贵的。
    李钟玉说，中朝两国人民是并肩战斗的战友。他怀着同中国人民一样的心情来庆祝中国国庆。他对中方的热情接待表示感谢。
    会见后，王震设宴招待朝鲜客人。全国人大常委会副委员长习仲勋参加了会见和宴请。
