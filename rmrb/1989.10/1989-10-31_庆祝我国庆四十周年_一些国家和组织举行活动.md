Title: Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author: Yan Zheng, Wei Wei
Date: 1989-10-31
Edition: 3

To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Federal Archives of Yugoslavia and the Chinese Embassy in Yugoslavia jointly organized a photo exhibition showcasing China's construction achievements, which opened today in Belgrade.

M. Ostojic, a member of the Presidency of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, attended the opening ceremony and delivered a speech.

From Brussels, October 28th, our correspondent Wei Wei reports: On the afternoon of October 27th, the photo exhibition "China 1949-1989" opened at the Anspach International Exhibition Center in the heart of Brussels.

Chinese Ambassador to Belgium Liu Shan delivered a speech at the opening ceremony.

Those who attended the opening ceremony included Vice Chairman Li Dehan of the Sino-Belgian Economic Committee, Vice Chairman De Vitte of the Sino-Belgian Cultural Center, Secretary-General Jean-Paul of the Sino-Belgian Association, Secretary-General Charles Weijia of the Antwerp Sino-Belgian Association, Secretary-General Van der Putte of the Brussels International Exhibition, and several diplomatic envoys stationed in Belgium.

From Prague, Xinhua News Agency reports: Some units in Czechoslovakia continue to hold activities to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Czechoslovakia.

On the evening of October 11th, the Czechoslovak Association for International Relations held a banquet to celebrate the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries.

The Chinese Bookplate Art Exhibition, organized by the Czechoslovak Association for International Relations, opened on October 11th at the National Library of the Czech Republic in Prague.

The three-week-long Chinese Cultural Festival, held in the county of Trutnov in East Bohemia, also opened on October 10th.

From Rome, Xinhua News Agency reports: To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Italy-China Friendship Association screened the Chinese film "The Chess King" on October 11th, which was well-received by the audience.

From Montevideo, Xinhua News Agency reports: The Uruguay-China Cultural Association held a lecture on October 21st to introduce modern China.

Ector Rodriguez, a Uruguayan friend who had visited China, delivered an important speech at the lecture. He cited a large number of figures to enthusiastically praise the achievements made by New China in the 40 years since its founding.

From Quito, Xinhua News Agency reports: A photo exhibition jointly organized by the Chinese Embassy in Ecuador, the Quito City Government, and the Ecuador-China Friendship Association opened on October 23rd at the Quito City Hall.

The mayor of Quito attended the opening ceremony and delivered a speech.

### 庆祝我国庆四十周年  一些国家和组织举行活动
严正  魏崴
1989-10-31
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　庆祝我国庆四十周年
　　一些国家和组织举行活动
    本报贝尔格莱德10月30日电　记者严正报道：为庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年，由南斯拉夫联邦档案局和中国驻南斯拉夫大使馆共同举办的中国建设成就图片展览今天在这里隆重开幕。
    南斯拉夫劳动人民社会主义联盟主席团成员米·奥斯托伊奇出席开幕式并发表讲话。
    本报布鲁塞尔10月28日电  记者魏崴报道：10月27日下午，《中国1949—1989年》图片展览于布鲁塞尔市中心昂斯巴克国际博览会中心开幕。
    中国驻比利时大使刘山在开幕式上发表讲话。
    今天出席开幕式的有比中经济委员会副主席李德汉，比中文化中心副主席德维特、比中协会负责人让保罗、安特卫普比中协会负责人魏查理，布鲁塞尔国际博览会秘书长范得普特，以及一些驻比利时外交使节。
    新华社布拉格电　捷克斯洛伐克一些单位继续举办活动，庆祝我国国庆40周年和中捷建交40周年。
    捷克斯洛伐克国际关系协会10月11日晚为庆祝两国建交举行晚宴。
    由捷克斯洛伐克国际关系协会举办的中国藏书票艺术展览10月11日在布拉格的捷克国家图书馆开幕。
    在东捷克州的特鲁特诺夫县举行的为期3周的中国文化节也于10月10日开幕。
    新华社罗马电　为庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年，意中友好协会10月11日在这里放映中国影片《棋王》，受到观众的欢迎。
    新华社蒙得维的亚电　乌拉圭—中国文化协会10月21日晚在这里举行报告会，介绍现代中国。
    曾经访问过中国的乌拉圭友人埃克托尔·罗德里格斯在报告会上作了重要发言。他列举了大量数字，热情赞扬新中国成立40年来所取得的成就。
    新华社基多电　由中国驻厄瓜多尔使馆、基多市政府和厄中友协联合举办的中国图片展览10月23日在厄首都基多市政厅开幕。
    基多市市长出席了开幕式并发表讲话。
