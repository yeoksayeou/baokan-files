Title: UN General Assembly Passes Resolution Condemning Israeli Suppression of Palestinian Civilians
Author: Liú Kāi Chén
Date: 1989-10-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, United Nations, October 6th.

The 44th session of the United Nations General Assembly today passed by an overwhelming majority a resolution condemning the continued atrocities committed by the Israeli occupying authorities against Palestinian civilians.

This session's resolution calls on the Security Council to provide international protection for Palestinian civilians in the occupied territories since 1967.

At the request of the Arab Group, represented by Libya, the session inserted the agenda item to discuss the current Palestinian issue during the general debate.

During the vote on the draft resolution, "The Uprising of the Palestinian People," proposed by Libya and 23 other countries, China and 139 other countries voted in favor, while Israel and the United States voted against.

Over the past two years, the Palestinian people have been engaged in a struggle against Israeli occupation in the West Bank and Gaza Strip.

The Israeli authorities have escalated their suppression measures, including killings, expulsions, demolitions of homes, and collective punishments. The Assembly expressed deep shock and regret over these actions.

The Assembly demanded that the occupying power, Israel, strictly adhere to the 1949 Geneva Convention Relative to the Protection of Civilian Persons in Time of War and immediately cease policies and practices that violate the provisions of the Convention.

The Assembly finally reaffirmed that Israel's occupation of Palestinian territories (including Jerusalem) and other Arab lands since 1967 cannot change the "legal status" of these territories.

### 联大通过决议  抗议以色列镇压巴勒斯坦平民
刘开宸
1989-10-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    联大通过决议
    抗议以色列镇压巴勒斯坦平民
    本报联合国10月6日电　记者刘开宸报道：第44届联合国大会今天下午以压倒多数通过一项决议，谴责以色列占领当局继续滥杀巴勒斯坦平民的暴行。
    本届联大的这项决议要求安理会对1967年以来的以占区的巴勒斯坦平民提供国际保护。
    本届联大应代表阿拉伯国家集团的利比亚的要求，在一般性辩论的过程中，插入了审议目前巴勒斯坦问题的议程。
    会上就利比亚等24国提出的决议草案，“巴勒斯坦人民的起义”进行表决时，中国等140票赞成，以色列和美国2票反对。
    近两年来，巴勒斯坦人民在约旦河西岸和加沙地带进行了反对以色列占领的斗争。
    以色列当局采取镇压措施不断升级，如枪杀、驱逐出境、夷平住房和集体惩罚等，大会对此深表震惊和遗憾。
    大会要求占领国以色列严格遵守1949年《关于战时保护平民的日内瓦公约》，并立即停止执行违反该公约规定的政策和做法。
    大会最后重申，以色列自1967年以来对巴勒斯坦（包括耶路撒冷）及其他阿拉伯领土的占领决不能改变这些领土的“法律地位”。
