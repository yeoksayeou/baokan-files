Title: Soldiers Provide Legal Consultations in Kunming
Author: Geng Shaoyuan
Date: 1989-10-20
Edition: 5

Recently, soldiers from the Yunnan Border Defense Corps of the People's Armed Police came to the streets of Kunming, the Spring City, to provide legal consultations for the public. Their enthusiastic, thoughtful, and patient answers were welcomed and praised by the people.

Look, the old man's "knot" in his heart has been untied.
