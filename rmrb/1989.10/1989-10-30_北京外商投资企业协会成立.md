Title: Beijing Foreign Investment Enterprises Association Established
Author: Li Yafei
Date: 1989-10-30
Edition: 2

Representatives from 235 foreign-invested enterprises gathered at the Great Hall of the People today to celebrate the establishment of the Beijing Foreign Investment Enterprises Association. Vice Premier Tian Jiyun attended the event to offer congratulations.

### 北京外商投资企业协会成立
李亚非
1989-10-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　北京外商投资企业协会成立
    据新华社北京10月28日电　（记者李亚非）今天，235家外商投资企业代表欢聚在人民大会堂，庆祝北京外商投资企业协会成立。国务院副总理田纪云到会祝贺。
