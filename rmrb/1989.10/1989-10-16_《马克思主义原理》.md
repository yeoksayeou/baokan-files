Title: Principles of Marxism
Author:
Date: 1989-10-16
Edition: 6

	Principles of Marxism
	Edited by Wang Yongxiang
	Published by Renmin University of China Press

	This book is a newly structured textbook for liberal arts programs in higher education institutions. The book covers the origins of labor and human society, the unified picture of the world and its general laws of development, the essence and development laws of cognition, the structure and general laws of development of human society, the origins and development of private ownership, class, and the state, the essence of the capitalist system, the monopoly stage of capitalist development, the modern forms of capitalism, the internal contradictions and historical fate of the capitalist system, the establishment of the socialist system, the essence and characteristics of the socialist system, and the self-improvement and future development of socialist society. The preface analyzes the origins of Marxism, the scientific theoretical system and basic characteristics of Marxism, the distinctive features of the current era and the new challenges facing Marxism, and the importance of approaching Marxism with a scientific attitude.

### 《马克思主义原理》

1989-10-16
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：

    《马克思主义原理》
    汪永祥主编
    中国人民大学出版社出版
    本书是按新的体系编写的高等学校文科教材。全书讲了劳动和人类社会的产生，世界的统一图景及其发展的一般规律，认识的本质及其发展规律，人类社会的结构和发展的一般规律，私有制、阶级、国家的起源和发展，资本主义制度及其本质，资本主义发展的垄断阶段，资本主义的现代形式，资本主义制度的内在矛盾和历史命运，社会主义制度的建立，社会主义制度的本质和特征，社会主义社会的自我完善和未来发展等问题。本书的绪论对马克思主义的产生、马克思主义的科学理论体系及其基本特征、当今时代特点和马克思主义面临的新课题、以科学态度对待马克思主义等问题进行了分析。
