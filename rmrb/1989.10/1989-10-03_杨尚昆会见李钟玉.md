Title: Yang Shangkun Meets with Li Jong-om
Author:
Date: 1989-10-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 2nd.

CPC Central Committee Politburo Member and State President Yang Shangkun met with the delegation of the DPRK party and government led by Kim Jong-om, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Vice President of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse this evening.

Yang Shangkun had a very cordial conversation with Kim Jong-om.

At the same time, Yang Shangkun also met with the delegation of the China-DPRK Friendship Group led by Yun Ki-chin, Minister of Finance of the DPRK.

After the meeting, Yang Shangkun hosted a banquet for the Korean guests. CPC Central Committee Politburo Member and Vice Premier of the State Council Wu Xueqian attended the meeting and the banquet.

### 杨尚昆会见李钟玉

1989-10-03
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨尚昆会见李钟玉
    新华社北京10月2日电　中共中央政治局委员、国家主席杨尚昆今天晚上在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了朝鲜劳动党中央政治局委员、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国家副主席李钟玉率领的朝鲜党政代表团。
    杨尚昆同李钟玉进行了十分亲切的谈话。
    同时，杨尚昆还会见了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国财政部长尹基贞率领的朝中友好代表团。
    会见后，杨尚昆设宴款待朝鲜客人。中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理吴学谦参加了会见和宴会。
