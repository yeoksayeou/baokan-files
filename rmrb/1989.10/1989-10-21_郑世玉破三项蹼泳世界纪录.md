Title: Zheng Shiyu Breaks Three Underwater Swimming World Records
Author:
Date: 1989-10-21
Edition: 4

Liaoning female athlete Zheng Shiyu broke the world record in the women's 100-meter underwater swimming preliminary round held in Zhanjiang, Guangdong on the morning of the 20th with a time of 4 minutes and 42.50 seconds. On the 17th and 19th, she successively created new world records in the women's 100-meter apparatus swimming with a time of 37.92 seconds and the women's 50-meter breath-holding swimming with a time of 17.19 seconds. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 郑世玉破三项蹼泳世界纪录

1989-10-21
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　郑世玉破三项蹼泳世界纪录
    辽宁女选手郑世玉20日上午在广东湛江举行的全国蹼泳锦标赛女子100米蹼泳预赛中，以4分42秒50的成绩打破世界纪录。17日和19日，她先后创造了女子100米器泳37秒92和女子50米屏气潜泳17秒19的世界新纪录。　（据新华社）
