Title: Guangzhou Civil Aviation Plane Makes First Flight to Kuala Lumpur
Author:
Date: 1989-10-08
Edition: 2

Guangzhou Civil Aviation Company's plane made its first flight to the Malaysian capital, Kuala Lumpur, this morning. The air route between Guangzhou and Kuala Lumpur, which was officially opened on June 6 this year, has been operated by Malaysian Airlines until today. Starting today, Guangzhou Civil Aviation will use Boeing 757 aircraft to join this route, flying once a week on Fridays with a round trip on the same day.

### 穗民航飞机首航吉隆坡

1989-10-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    穗民航飞机首航吉隆坡
    新华社广州10月6日电  广州民航公司的飞机今天上午首航马来西亚首都吉隆坡。广州和吉隆坡之间的航线今年6月6日正式开通后，一直由马来西亚航空公司的飞机飞行。广州民航从今天起，使用波音757飞机加入这条航线飞行，每周逢星期五飞行一班，当天往返。
