Title: Bipedal Walking Robot Developed
Author: Jī Yúnchéng, Cháng Yùlǐ
Date: 1989-10-15
Edition: 2

A bipedal walking robot has recently been developed at Harbin Institute of Technology.

This bipedal walking robot weighs 70 kilograms, is 110 centimeters tall, and has 10 moving joints. It can walk on flat ground, move sideways, and climb stairs, with a stride of 45 centimeters and a step time of 10 seconds. The robot uses offline calculation of state and ground pressure hybrid control, and its structure features a parallelogram frame, which gives it high rigidity and stability. Experts have praised its design.

Bipedal walking technology for robots is a high-tech area currently being developed internationally.

### 双足步行机器人制成
姬云程  常玉礼
1989-10-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    双足步行机器人制成
    新华社哈尔滨10月14日电　（记者姬云程、通讯员常玉礼）一台双足步行机器人，最近在哈尔滨工业大学问世。
    这台双足步行机器人重70公斤，高110厘米，有10个运动关节，可以进行平地前进、左右侧行和上下阶梯步行，步幅为45厘米，每步用时10秒。这个机器人由于采用离线计算的状态和地面压力混合控制，在结构上采用平行四边形框架，使得它结构刚度大，稳定性强，得到了专家们的肯定。
    机器人二足步行技术是国际上正在开发的高技术。
