Title: The State Council Sends Condolences to the People of Shanxi Earthquake-Affected Areas
Author:
Date: 1989-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 22nd.

The State Council sent a message of condolence to the Shanxi Provincial Government and the earthquake-affected areas on October 21st. The full text of the message is as follows:

To the Shanxi Provincial Government and the Governments of the earthquake-affected areas in Datong, Yanggao, and other places, as well as to the broad masses of cadres, the people, and the soldiers and officers of the People's Liberation Army and the People's Armed Police who are aiding the disaster areas:

On the night of October 18th to the early morning of the 19th, 1989, an earthquake measuring 6.1 on the Richter scale struck Datong County and Yanggao County in Shanxi Province, causing loss of life and property. The State Council is deeply concerned about this and extends its heartfelt condolences to the people who have suffered from the disaster. The State Council also expresses its sincere thanks and condolences to the broad masses of cadres, the people, medical personnel, and the soldiers and officers of the People's Liberation Army and the People's Armed Police who are fighting on the front lines of disaster relief.

The State Council hopes that the people of the disaster areas will carry forward the revolutionary spirit of hard struggle, unite as one, strive for progress, rely on their own efforts, and quickly restore and develop production. They should focus on production self-reliance and rebuild their homes.

The State Council believes that under the leadership of the Shanxi Provincial Government and with the active cooperation of relevant departments and the People's Liberation Army and the People's Armed Police, the people of the disaster areas will definitely be able to overcome the earthquake disaster and achieve victory in disaster relief.

The State Council
October 21, 1989

### 国务院电慰山西地震灾区人民  希望灾区人民奋发图强搞好生产自救重建家园

1989-10-23
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　国务院电慰山西地震灾区人民
　　希望灾区人民奋发图强搞好生产自救重建家园
    新华社北京10月22日电　国务院21日向山西省人民政府和地震灾区发出了慰问电。电报全文如下：山西省人民政府并大同、阳高等地震灾区人民政府，广大干部、群众和支援灾区的人民解放军、武警部队指战员：
    1989年10月18日深夜至19日凌晨，山西省大同县、阳高县一带发生6.1级地震，人民生命安全和财产受到损失，国务院对此极为关怀，特向遭受灾害的人民群众致以亲切的慰问，向战斗在抗震救灾第一线的广大干部、群众、医护人员和人民解放军、武警部队指战员，表示衷心的感谢和慰问。
    国务院希望灾区人民发扬艰苦奋斗的革命精神，团结一致，奋发图强，自力更生，尽快恢复和发展生产，搞好生产自救，重建家园。
    国务院相信，在山西省人民政府的领导下，在各有关部门和人民解放军、武警部队的积极配合下，灾区人民一定能够战胜地震灾害，取得抗震救灾的胜利。
                                国务院
                          1989年10月21日
