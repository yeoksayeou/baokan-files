Title: Arafat Leaves Beijing for East Germany
Author:
Date: 1989-10-07
Edition: 2

PLO Chairman Yasser Arafat concluded his official friendly visit to China today. This morning, he departed Beijing by special plane for the German Democratic Republic. Minister of Aerospace Industry Lin Zongtang and others saw him off at the airport.

### 阿拉法特离京赴民主德国

1989-10-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    阿拉法特离京赴民主德国
    新华社北京10月6日电　巴勒斯坦国总统亚西尔·阿拉法特结束了对中国的正式友好访问，今天早晨乘专机离京赴德意志民主共和国。航空航天工业部部长林宗棠等到机场送行。
