Title: A Large Salt Mine in Huaiyin to Enter Industrial Development
Author: Yang Weiyi Zhang Fen Tu
Date: 1989-10-15
Edition: 2

A large salt mine in Huaiyin County, Zhaoji Salt Mine, is about to enter large-scale industrial development. This salt mine is located on the shores of Hongze Lake in Jiangsu Province, covering a total area of 150 square kilometers, with 82 square kilometers of land. The total proven rock salt reserves amount to 27 billion tons. With its thick salt layers (38 meters), large reserves, high grade (average 76%), stable layer position, and moderate depth, it has been listed as a national large-scale salt mine. The prospects for the exploitation of Zhaoji Salt Mine are bright. According to trial mining calculations, a single well can produce 150,000 cubic meters of brine per year.

### 淮阴一大型盐矿将投入工业开发
杨维义　张奋图
1989-10-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    淮阴一大型盐矿将投入工业开发
    本报讯　我国又一座大型盐矿——淮阴县赵集盐矿，即将投入大规模工业开发。这个盐矿位于江苏洪泽湖畔，总面积150平方公里，陆地面积82平方公里，已探明岩盐总储量270亿吨，以其盐层厚（38米）、储量大、品位高（平均76％）、层位稳定、深度适中等特点，被列入国家大型盐矿行列。赵集盐矿的开采前景光明，据试采测算，单口井年产卤水15万原方。　（杨维义　张奋图）
