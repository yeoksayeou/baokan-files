Title: Yao Yilin Meets with Soviet Friends
Author: Zhou Ciping
Date: 1989-10-13
Edition: 1

	Yao Yilin Meets with Soviet Friends
	Hosts and Guests Hope to Further Develop Friendly Cooperation Between the Two Countries

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 12 — (Reporter Zhou Ciping) Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council Yao Yilin met this afternoon with a delegation of Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association veterans led by Deputy Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and former First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, I.V. Arkhipov, at Zhongnanhai.

	Yao Yilin warmly welcomed the Soviet guests who had helped China's revolution and construction in the 1950s or earlier. He said that the Chinese people greatly miss their Soviet friends who had helped China in the past. The Chinese people not only hope that they will come to China to see for themselves, but also hope that they will come often.

	Yao Yilin said that during the period of China's revolutionary struggle and socialist construction in the 1950s, the Soviet Union provided China with great assistance. The Chinese people will never forget this.

	Arkhipov said that during this visit, the delegation has seen with their own eyes that the Chinese people, like the Soviet people, remember the past friendship and are willing to further develop friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries.

	During the conversation, the Soviet friends shared their impressions from their visits to Beijing, Dalian, Shanghai, and Hangzhou. Yao Yilin introduced the current economic situation in China to the guests.

	The hosts and guests also exchanged views on expanding economic and trade cooperation between China and the Soviet Union in a friendly and frank atmosphere.

	Chairman of the China-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Xiuquan participated in the meeting.

	The Soviet guests will end their visit to China tomorrow and depart from Beijing to return home.

### 姚依林会见苏联朋友  宾主希望进一步发展两国友好合作
周慈朴
1989-10-13
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    姚依林会见苏联朋友
    宾主希望进一步发展两国友好合作
    新华社北京10月12日电　（记者周慈朴）中共中央政治局常委、国务院副总理姚依林今天下午在中南海会见了由苏中友协副主席、苏联前部长会议第一副主席依·瓦·阿尔希波夫率领的苏中友协老战士代表团。
    姚依林对曾在50年代或更早时期帮助中国革命和建设的苏联客人来中国访问表示热烈欢迎。他说，中国人民非常思念过去曾帮助过中国的苏联朋友。中国人民不仅希望他们来中国看一看，而且希望他们常来。
    姚依林说，在中国革命年代以及50年代的社会主义建设时期，苏联给了中国很大帮助。对此，中国人民是不会忘记的。
    阿尔希波夫说，代表团在这次访问中亲眼看到，中国人民同苏联人民一样，都记得以往的友谊，都愿意进一步发展两国友好合作关系。
    在交谈中，苏联朋友畅叙了这次在北京、大连、上海和杭州参观访问时的感受。姚依林向客人们介绍了中国当前的经济情况。
    宾主还在亲切而又坦率的气氛中就中苏两国扩大经贸合作问题交换了看法。
    中苏友协会长伍修权参加了会见。
    苏联客人将于明天结束对中国的访问，离开北京回国。
