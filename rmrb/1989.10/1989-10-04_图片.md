Title: Exhibition of Paintings by Young Artist Jian Lianbin from Heilongjiang to Open at Central Academy of Fine Arts
Author:
Date: 1989-10-04
Page: 8

An exhibition of paintings by young artist Jian Lianbin from Heilongjiang will open tomorrow at the Central Academy of Fine Arts Gallery. Among the works on display is the ink painting "Silent Spring Water."
