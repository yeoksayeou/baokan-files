Title: Some Foreign Party and State Leaders Send Congratulatory Messages to Chinese Leaders on the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1989-10-03
Edition: 3

Over the past few days, the party and state leaders of our country have received congratulatory messages from some foreign party and state leaders, warmly celebrating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

New China News Agency, Beijing, October 2nd - Recently, our country's party and state leaders have received congratulatory telegrams from some foreign party and state leaders, warmly celebrating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The following have sent congratulatory telegrams to Jiang Zemin, General Secretary, Yang Shangkun, Chairman, and Li Peng, Premier:

The General Secretary of the Socialist Party of the People's Democratic Republic of Yemen, Ali Salem Beid, the Chairman of the Supreme People's Council of the People's Democratic Republic of Yemen, Haidar Abu Bakr Al-Attas, and the Prime Minister of the Government, Yasin Said Numan, jointly sent a congratulatory telegram to Jiang Zemin, Yang Shangkun, and Li Peng.

The following have sent congratulatory telegrams to Jiang Zemin, General Secretary, and Yang Shangkun, Chairman:

The General Secretary of the Lebanese Communist Party, George Hawi, and the Executive Secretary of the People's Progressive Party of Guyana, Janet Jagan, respectively sent congratulatory letters to Jiang Zemin, General Secretary, and Yang Shangkun, Chairman.

The following have sent congratulatory telegrams to Yang Shangkun, Chairman:

King Bhumibol Adulyadej of Thailand;

### 一些国家和政党领导人致电我国领导人  祝贺中华人民共和国成立四十周年

1989-10-03
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    一些国家和政党领导人致电我国领导人
    祝贺中华人民共和国成立四十周年
    新华社北京10月2日电　连日来，我国党和国家领导人又收到一些外国党政领导人的贺电，热烈祝贺中华人民共和国成立40周年。
