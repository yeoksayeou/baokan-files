Title: Handan Establishes Specialized College Courses for Model Workers
Author: Zhang Junyou
Date: 1989-10-08
Page: 2

    Handan Establishes Specialized College Courses for Model Workers

    Xinhua News Agency, Handan, October 8th - Recently, the Handan Municipal Federation of Trade Unions and the Handan Municipal Model Workers Association jointly launched the first batch of specialized college courses for model workers. The aim is to enhance their scientific and cultural knowledge as well as their professional theoretical expertise.

    The college courses span one and a half years, with students studying on a semi-attendance basis. Currently, over 80 model workers have enrolled, including veterans from the 1950s and new stars from the 1980s.

### 邯郸为劳模办大专班
张俊有
1989-10-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    邯郸为劳模办大专班
    本报讯　最近，河北省邯郸市工会和市劳模联谊会联合举办首届劳动模范大专《专业证书》班，以提高他们的科学文化知识和专业理论水平。
    大专班学制一年半，学员半脱产学习。目前，已有80余名劳动模范步入校门，他们中有50年代的老劳模，也有80年代涌现的新秀。　（张俊有）
