Title: The Final Three Matches of the Lion City Showdown to Be Held Simultaneously
Author: Zhang Qianli, Li Yongming
Date: 1989-10-27
Edition: 4

The final three matches of the Asian qualifying round for the World Cup football tournament will be held simultaneously on October 28 at three stadiums in Singapore.

The organizing committee announced today that the three matches scheduled for October 28—China vs. Qatar, South Korea vs. the United Arab Emirates, and North Korea vs. Saudi Arabia—will kick off at 5:00 PM at the National Stadium, Jurong Stadium, and Toa Payoh Stadium, respectively.

This arrangement aims to ensure fair competition among the teams and prevent any potential cheating. So far, South Korea has secured a spot with 7 points, while Saudi Arabia, with 2 points, has no chance of advancing. The United Arab Emirates (5 points), China (4 points), North Korea (3 points), and Qatar (3 points) still have a chance to qualify. Therefore, the final three matches will be decisive.

South Korean coach Lee Hoe-taek said that although South Korea has already qualified, the team will still give their best in the final match against the United Arab Emirates.

### 狮城决战最后3场将同时进行  南朝鲜队教练表示要尽力而为
张千里  李永明
1989-10-27
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　狮城决战最后3场将同时进行
    南朝鲜队教练表示要尽力而为
    新华社新加坡10月26日电  （记者张千里、李永明）世界杯足球赛亚洲区预选赛决赛的最后3场比赛，将于28日在新加坡3个足球场同时进行。
    组委会今天宣布，于28日举行的中国队对卡塔尔队、南朝鲜队对阿拉伯联合酋长国队、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国队对沙特阿拉伯队的3场比赛，将于下午5时正分别在国家体育场、裕廊体育场和惹兰勿刹体育场同时展开。
    这种安排的目的是为了使各队公开竞争，避免可能发生的舞弊行为。到目前为止，除南朝鲜队已积7分，稳获出线权、沙特队积2分，已无望出线外，阿联酋队（5分），中国队（4分），朝鲜队（3分），卡塔尔队（3分）均有出线的可能性。因此，最后3场比赛将是决定性的。
    南朝鲜队教练李会泽说，南朝鲜队虽已出线，但在最后一战与阿拉伯联合酋长国队交锋时，还要尽力而为。
