Title: Pravda Warns the West Not to Rock the Socialist Ship
Author:
Date: 1989-10-23
Edition: 3

    The Soviet newspaper Pravda published an article on October 21, warning the West not to attempt to rock the socialist ship.

    The article, written by the paper's political observer Korionov, states that some Western politicians have recently tried to use events in Poland and Hungary to "change the face of Europe." Some Western publications have also commented on this, offering advice and strategies.

    The article argues that in today's interdependent world, significant political events in one country can have repercussions in other parts of the globe, and this is especially true for the war-torn European continent. However, the processes currently unfolding in Poland, Hungary, and other countries are internal affairs of these nations.

    The author points out that there are many politicians in the West who want to use the "historic opportunity" of reforms in the Soviet Union and other socialist countries to steer them in the opposite direction.

    In the United States, some forces have not yet laid down their arms, believing that they are now closer than ever to realizing their anti-socialist action plans.

    The article emphasizes that reality does not depend on the wishes of these people. Those who disrupt European security will end up hurting themselves. Those who try to rock the socialist ship also risk rocking or even capsizing the European and world ship.

### 苏联《真理报》发表文章告诫西方  不要试图摇晃社会主义航船

1989-10-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏联《真理报》发表文章告诫西方
　　不要试图摇晃社会主义航船
    新华社莫斯科10月21日电　苏联《真理报》21日发表文章，告诫西方不要试图摇晃社会主义的航船。
    这篇由该报政治观察家科里奥诺夫撰写的文章说，最近西方有一些政治家试图利用在波兰和匈牙利发生的事件来“改变欧洲面貌”。某些西方报刊也为此发表评论，出谋划策。
    文章认为，在当今相互依赖的世界上，发生在这个或那个国家内的重要政治事件会在地球的另外一些地区引起反响，这是无可争议的，对于饱经苦难的欧洲大陆来说更是如此。但现在波兰、匈牙利和其他一些国家出现的进程则是这些国家人民的内部事务。
    作者指出，西方国家中现有不少这样的政客，他们想利用苏联和其他一些社会主义国家改革这一“历史性机遇”使它们朝相反的方向发展。
    在美国，有些势力根本还没有放下武器，这些势力认为它们现在比以往任何时候更接近于实现自己的反社会主义行动计划。
    文章强调，然而现实不取决于这些人的愿望。谁要是破坏欧洲安全，将搬起石头砸自己的脚。谁试图摇晃社会主义的航船，同时也在冒摇晃甚至掀翻欧洲和世界航船的风险。
