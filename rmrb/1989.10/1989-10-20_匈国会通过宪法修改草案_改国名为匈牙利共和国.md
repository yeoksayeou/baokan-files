Title: Hungarian Parliament Passes Constitutional Amendment Bill
Author:
Date: 1989-10-20
Edition: 3

Hungarian Parliament Passes Constitutional Amendment Bill
Changes Country Name to Hungarian Republic

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, October 18th - The Hungarian Parliament passed a constitutional amendment bill on the 18th, changing the country's name from the Hungarian People's Republic to the Hungarian Republic. The amended constitution removes the provision regarding the leading role of Marxist-Leninist parties, establishes that the state practices "parliamentary democracy," and declares that "the Hungarian Republic is an independent, democratic, and legal state, embodying the values of both bourgeois democracy and democratic socialism."

The amended constitution also stipulates that Hungary will adopt a market economy, ensuring equal status for public and private economies during the transition of the economic model.

The constitutional amendment also stipulates the abolition of the collective head of state, the Presidential Council, and the adoption of a presidential system.

The constitutional amendment declares the abolition of the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Militia.

### 匈国会通过宪法修改草案  改国名为匈牙利共和国

1989-10-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    匈国会通过宪法修改草案
    改国名为匈牙利共和国
    新华社布达佩斯10月18日电　匈牙利国会18日通过宪法修改草案，将原国名匈牙利人民共和国改为匈牙利共和国。修改后的宪法取消了关于马克思、列宁主义政党的领导作用的规定，确定国家实行“议会民主”，并宣布“匈牙利共和国是个独立、民主的法制国家，同时体现资产阶级民主与民主社会主义的价值”。
    修改后的宪法还规定，匈牙利采用市场经济，在改变经济模式的过程中保障公有经济与私有经济地位平等。
    宪法修改后还规定，匈牙利取消作为集体国家元首的共和国主席团而改行国家总统制。
    宪法修正案宣布取消匈牙利工人民兵组织。
