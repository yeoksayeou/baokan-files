Title: Philippines to Decide on US Base Stay Through Citizen Vote
Author:
Date: 1989-10-18
Page: 3

The Philippines House of Representatives passed a bill on the 16th, proposing a national referendum on whether to retain U.S. military bases after the 1991 expiration of the U.S.-Philippines Military Bases Agreement.

According to the bill, the referendum will be held 60 days before the final agreement on the U.S. military base issue is signed by the Philippine and U.S. negotiating teams.

The current U.S.-Philippines Military Bases Agreement is set to expire in 1991.

### 菲将美基地去留交公民决定

1989-10-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    菲将美基地去留交公民决定
    菲律宾众议院十六日通过一项议案，要求就一九九一年菲美军事基地条约到期后美国军事基地的去留问题进行一次全国公民投票。
    根据这一议案，公民投票将在菲美谈判代表团签署有关美国在菲军事基地去留问题协议的最后文件前六十天举行。
    目前的菲美军事基地条约将于一九九一年期满。
