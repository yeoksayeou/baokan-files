Title: Meeting Between Kaifu and Arafat
Author:
Date: 1989-10-04
Edition: 3

Japanese Prime Minister Kaifu Toshiki held a meeting with PLO Chairman Yasser Arafat on the morning of October 3 at the Prime Minister's Official Residence. Arafat expressed his agreement with the proposal made by Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak for direct dialogue between Palestine and Israel.

Kaifu stated that Japan would expand its aid to Palestine through relevant United Nations agencies.

### 海部和阿拉法特会谈

1989-10-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    海部和阿拉法特会谈
    日本首相海部俊树10月3日上午在首相官邸与巴勒斯坦解放组织主席阿拉法特举行会谈。阿拉法特表示同意埃及总统穆巴拉克关于巴勒斯坦与以色列直接对话的提案。
    海部表示，日本将通过联合国有关机构扩大对巴勒斯坦的援助。
