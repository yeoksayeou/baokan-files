Title: At the Invitation of Premier Li Peng, Chairman Kaysone Phomvihane Arrives in Beijing for a Formal Friendly Visit
Author:
Date: 1989-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 5th.

At the invitation of Premier Li Peng, Chairman of the Council of Ministers and General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party, Kaysone Phomvihane, arrived in Beijing this afternoon to begin his formal friendly visit to China.

This is Chairman Kaysone's first visit to China since 1977.

Accompanying Chairman Kaysone on his visit to China are: Political Bureau member, Secretary of the Secretariat, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Minister of Economy, Planning, and Finance, Saly Vongkhamxao; Central Committee member, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs, Head of the International Liaison Department, Thongsavanh Phommalath; and Central Committee member, Minister of Trade and Foreign Economic Relations, Chairman of the Foreign Economic Cooperation and Liaison Committee, Pone Khampheng.

The Chinese government's welcoming delegation, led by Minister of Labor, Yuan Chongwu, welcomed Chairman Kaysone and his entourage at the airport.

Tomorrow morning, Premier Li Peng will hold a ceremony to welcome Chairman Kaysone and his delegation.

### 应邀来华进行正式友好访问  凯山·丰威汉到达北京

1989-10-06
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    应邀来华进行正式友好访问
    凯山·丰威汉到达北京
    新华社北京10月5日电　应李鹏总理邀请，老挝人民民主共和国部长会议主席、老挝人民革命党中央委员会总书记凯山·丰威汉今天下午抵达北京，开始对中国进行正式友好访问。
    这是自1977年以来凯山主席首次率团访华。
    随同凯山访华的主要成员包括：政治局委员、书记处书记、部长会议副主席、经济、计划、财政部长沙立·冯坎肖，中央委员、外交部代部长、中联部部长通沙瓦·凯坎皮吞和中央委员、贸易和对外经济联络部部长、对外经济合作和联络委员会主任炮·本纳喷等。
    中国政府陪同团团长、劳动部部长阮崇武等到机场迎接凯山一行。
    明天上午，李鹏总理将举行仪式，欢迎凯山一行。
