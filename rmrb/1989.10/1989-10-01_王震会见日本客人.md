Title: Wang Zhen Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-10-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30th.

Vice President Wang Zhen met with four groups of Japanese guests this morning at the Great Hall of the People.

The guests included a group of renowned Japanese calligraphers led by Liu Tianyun, who came to China to hold an exhibition; a delegation led by Vice President of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Huang Zhefu, celebrating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China; a delegation from Daizi Town, Ibaraki Prefecture, Japan, led by the president of the Daizi Town Japan-China Friendship Association, Zhai Tiejun; and the president of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Xiang Fanglong.

### 王震会见日本客人

1989-10-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    王震会见日本客人
    新华社北京9月30日电　国家副主席王震今天上午在人民大会堂会见了四批日本客人。
    他们是前来我国举办展览的日本著名汉字书法家柳田泰云一行；由日中友协副会长荒哲夫率领的日中友好中国建国40周年庆祝团；由大子町日中友协会长斋藤毅一郎率领的日本茨城县大子町访华团及日中协会会长向坊隆。
