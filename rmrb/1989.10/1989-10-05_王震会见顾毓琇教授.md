Title: Wang Zhen Meets with Professor Gu Yishu
Author:
Date: 1989-10-05
Edition: 2

Vice Chairman of the State Wang Zhen met with Professor Gu Yishu, a retired professor from the University of Pennsylvania in the United States, and his wife Wang Wancheng this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People. Professor Cheng Junfu, who accompanied Gu Yishu to participate in China's National Day activities, also attended the meeting.

### 王震会见顾毓琇教授

1989-10-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    王震会见顾毓琇教授
    新华社北京10月4日电　国家副主席王震今天下午在人民大会堂会见了美国宾夕法尼亚大学荣休教授顾毓琇及夫人王婉清。陪同顾毓琇前来参加中国国庆活动的程君复教授也参加了会见。
