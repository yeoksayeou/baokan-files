Title: Hunan Hosts Folk Drama Festival
Author: Liáng Chángcūn  Zhāng Xīngmín
Date: 1989-10-05
Edition: 4

A grand theatrical event initiated and organized by the folk—Hunan's first "Maple Red" Folk Drama Festival—opened in Changsha in a grand ceremony on the eve of National Day. Sixteen folk professional troupes will perform eighty plays from late September to the end of the year.

专栏：

    湖南举办民间戏剧节
    本报讯  一个由民间发起并组织主办的盛大戏剧活动——湖南首届“映山红”民间戏剧节于国庆前夕在长沙隆重开幕。十六个民间职业剧团将从九月下旬至年底演出八十台戏。（梁长村  张兴民）
