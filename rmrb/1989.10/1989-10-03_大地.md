Title: The Earth
Author: Huang Yongmei
Date: 1989-10-03
Edition: 8

	The Earth
	Huang Yongmei

	Waves of wind bring whispers
	You are rugged and vast
	You flow orderly and wildly
	I love you all the same

	You gave me not just a place to stand
	But also blood and soul
	Because you are yellow earth
	I am honored to be as yellow as a handful of soil

	Because you are boiling
	I boil like a dragon
	Because you are the source of civilization
	I am so loyal to you

	Some elegant phrases float towards me
	Inciting me to despise you
	But I remain steadfast in my love for you
	If I do not love you
	Is that not despising my own blood and soul?

    煽动我唾弃你
    而我始终如一地爱你
    如果不爱你
    那岂不是不爱自已的血脉与灵
      魂？
