Title: Inputting the Complete Works of Lu Xun into a Computer
Author: Yuan Xi
Date: 1989-10-30
Edition: 4

    The Complete Works of Lu Xun have been inputted into a computer system. According to a report by our reporter Yuan Xi, Lu Xun's lifetime works total 2,985,168 characters (including punctuation and foreign words), utilizing 7,397 different Chinese characters. This is the result of inputting the Complete Works of Lu Xun into the computer system.

    The Lu Xun Museum in Beijing and the Beijing Computer Factory No. 3 jointly developed this system, which has two main functions: statistical analysis and retrieval.

### 《鲁迅全集》输入微机
袁晞
1989-10-30
第4版(综合)
专栏：

　　《鲁迅全集》输入微机
    本报讯　记者袁晞报道：鲁迅先生一生的著作共有二百九十八万五千一百六十八字（含标点和外文），用过不同的汉字共七千三百九十七个。这是《鲁迅全集》被输入微机系统的统计结果。
    北京鲁迅博物馆和北京计算机三厂联合研制的这套系统，具有统计和检索两大功能。
