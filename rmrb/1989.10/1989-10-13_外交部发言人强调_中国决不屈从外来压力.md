Title: Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Emphasizes China Will Not Yield to External Pressure
Author: Wēn Xiàn
Date: 1989-10-13
Edition: 2

	Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Emphasizes
	China Will Not Yield to External Pressure

	BEIJING, Oct. 12 (Xinhua) -- "The Chinese people have never and will never yield to any external pressure, whether it is political or economic." Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Li Zhaoxing made the above remarks today in response to a question about "some Western countries still insisting on imposing economic sanctions on China."

	He further stated, "Economic exchanges between countries should be based on equality and mutual benefit, promoting friendship and cooperation among nations, rather than being used as a means to exert pressure and interfere in internal affairs. Using economic sanctions to achieve certain political goals is a manifestation of hegemony, which violates the norms of international relations and the historical trend of development. In fact, it is fundamentally unworkable."

### 外交部发言人强调  中国决不屈从外来压力
温宪
1989-10-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外交部发言人强调
    中国决不屈从外来压力
    本报北京10月12日讯　记者温宪报道：“中国人民从来没有、今后也决不会屈从任何外来压力，不管是政治上的，还是经济上的。”外交部发言人李肇星今天下午在回答“有的西方国家至今仍坚持对中国实行经济制裁”的问题时做了上述表示。
    他还说，“国与国之间的经济交往应该建立在平等互利的基础上，应该促进各国的友好合作，而不应该成为施加压力和干涉内政的手段。为了达到某种政治目的而搞什么经济制裁是强权主义的一种表现，违背了国际关系准则和历史发展潮流，实际上也是根本行不通的。”
