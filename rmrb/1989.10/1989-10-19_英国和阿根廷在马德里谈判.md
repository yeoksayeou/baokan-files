Title: Britain and Argentina Negotiate in Madrid
Author:
Date: 1989-10-19
Edition: 3

Britain and Argentina began negotiations in the Spanish capital of Madrid on the 17th, aimed at restoring bilateral relations. This is the first comprehensive round of talks between the two countries on their relationship in over five years. Senior diplomats from both sides participated in the two-day meeting.

### 英国和阿根廷在马德里谈判

1989-10-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英国和阿根廷在马德里谈判
    英国和阿根廷两国十七日在西班牙首都马德里开始举行旨在恢复双边关系的谈判。
    这是五年多来，双方就两国关系进行的第一次全面的谈判。双方的高级外交官参加了这次为期两天的会谈。
                                      （本报专电）
