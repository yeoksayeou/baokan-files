Title: Soviet Union Completes This Year's Plan to Withdraw Troops from Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1989-10-11
Page: 3

Tass, Moscow, October 9th - The Soviet Union has completed its 1989 plan to withdraw part of its troops from Mongolia. This withdrawal began on May 15th of this year. According to reports, the number of Soviet troops in Mongolia has been reduced by 50,320 personnel. A total of 3,118 tanks, 768 artillery pieces, and 351 military aircraft have been withdrawn.

### 苏完成今年从蒙撤军计划

1989-10-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏完成今年从蒙撤军计划
    新华社莫斯科10月9日电　据塔斯社9日报道，苏联已完成1989年从蒙古撤走部分军队的计划。这次撤军是从今年5月15日开始的。据报道，目前苏联驻蒙古军队已减少5.0320万人。先后撤出3118辆坦克、768门火炮和351架军用飞机。
