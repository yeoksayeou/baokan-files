Title: The Great Transformation of Anshan Iron and Steel
Author: 
Date: 1989-10-07
Edition: 4

    To comprehensively summarize and publicize the experiences of Anshan Iron and Steel's 10 years of reform and opening up, Liaoning University Press recently published the book "The Great Transformation of Anshan Iron and Steel." Premier Li Peng wrote the book's title, and Comrade Qiao Shi provided the preface.
