Title: Peru, Bolivia, and Colombia Decide to Jointly Combat Drug Trafficking; Bush to Attend Latin American Anti-Drug Summit
Author: Zhū Mǎn Tíng
Date: 1989-10-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Lima, Peru, October 10th.

The presidents of Bolivia, Colombia, and Peru held a meeting today and decided to take joint action to combat drug trafficking. They also proposed holding a summit with U.S. President Bush on the issue within 90 days.

The meeting of the three presidents was held in the city of Ica, located 300 kilometers south of the Peruvian capital, Lima. After the meeting, the three presidents issued a brief joint statement.

The statement welcomed Bush's initiative on the drug issue and agreed to invite him to join the summit. They also expressed their intention to invite European countries to participate in the meeting. To this end, the three countries will hold a preparatory meeting in Santa Cruz, Bolivia, on November 20th of this year.

Bolivia, Colombia, and Peru are the main producers of coca and cocaine.

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, October 10th.

White House spokesman Fitzwater announced today that President Bush supports the proposal by the three South American presidents to hold a summit on the drug issue and will attend the meeting.

### 秘玻哥决定联合缉毒  布什将参加拉美反毒品首脑会议
朱满庭
1989-10-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    秘玻哥决定联合缉毒
    布什将参加拉美反毒品首脑会议
    本报伊卡（秘鲁）10月10日电　记者朱满庭报道：玻利维亚、哥伦比亚和秘鲁3国总统今天举行会晤，决定采取联合行动打击贩毒活动，并建议在90天内与美国总统布什就反毒品问题举行一次首脑会议。
    3国总统的会晤是在秘鲁首都利马以南300公里的伊卡市举行的。会后3位总统发表了一份简短的公报。
    公报对布什总统关于反毒品问题上的倡议表示欢迎，一致同意邀请布什一道举行会议，并尽可能请欧洲国家也参加这次会议。为此，3国准备在今年11月20日在玻利维亚的圣克鲁斯召开一次预备会议。
    玻利维亚、哥伦比亚和秘鲁是古柯和可卡因的主要生产国。
    本报华盛顿10月10日电 白宫发言人菲茨沃特今天宣布，布什总统赞同南美三国总统提出的召开一次毒品问题的首脑会议，并将出席这一会议。
