Title: Artificial Lens Implantation Technology Training Center Established
Author: Huang Ruoyin
Date: 1989-10-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, October 18th.

The World Artificial Lens Implantation Technology China Training Center was recently established in Tianjin. This training center was initiated by Professor Lim Sau Ming, an ophthalmology expert from the National University of Singapore, and was funded by donations from around the world. The center will promote advanced methods of artificial lens implantation for the treatment of cataracts and will train 100 ophthalmologists nationwide each year.

### 人工晶体植入技术培训中心成立
黄若音
1989-10-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    人工晶体植入技术培训中心成立
    据新华社天津10月18日电　（通讯员黄若音）世界人工晶体植入技术中国培训中心日前在天津成立。这个培训中心是由新加坡国立大学眼科专家林少明教授倡议、世界各地募集资金而建立的。培训中心将推广先进的人工晶体植入治疗白内障的方法，每年将为全国培训100名眼科医生。
