Title: Soviet Union Withdraws Part of Its Troops Stationed in Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1989-10-08
Edition: 3

According to a report from the Mongolian News Agency, the commander of the Soviet troops in Mongolia, Major General Mayorov, announced on the 5th in Ulaanbaatar that the Soviet Union has completed the first phase of its plan to withdraw 75% of its troops from Mongolia this August.

Mayorov said that the facilities left by the withdrawn Soviet troops have been handed over to the Mongolian side, including 52 barracks and 123 residential buildings.

### 苏联撤出部分驻蒙古军队

1989-10-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联撤出部分驻蒙古军队
    据蒙通社报道，驻蒙苏军司令马约罗夫少将五日在乌兰巴托宣布，苏联已于今年八月完成撤出百分之七十五驻蒙苏军的第一阶段撤军计划。
    马约罗夫说，撤走的苏军留下的设施已移交给蒙方，其中包括五十二座兵营、一百二十三栋住宅楼。
