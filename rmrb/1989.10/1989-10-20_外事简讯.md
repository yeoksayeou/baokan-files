Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-10-20
Edition: 2

	▲ Standing Committee Vice Chairman of the National People's Congress, Ju Xuefan, met and hosted a banquet on October 19th in the Great Hall of the People for a delegation of South African friends led by Peter Sol, a member of the South African Democratic Party. At the invitation of the China International Exchange Association, the South African guests came to visit to strengthen people-to-people exchanges and to understand China's political and economic situation.

	▲ On October 19th, China and Czechoslovakia signed the minutes of the 31st meeting of the Joint Commission for Scientific and Technological Cooperation in Beijing. Guo Shuyan, Vice Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission, and Frantisek Brom, Vice Chairman of the Czechoslovak State Commission for Science, Technology, and Investment Development, signed the document. Song Jian, Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission, attended the signing ceremony. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 外事简讯

1989-10-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长朱学范19日在人民大会堂会见并宴请了由南非民主党国会议员彼得·索尔率领的南非友好人士访华团。应中国国际交流协会的邀请，南非客人这次来访是为了加强同中国的民间交往并了解中国的政治经济形势。
    ▲中国和捷克斯洛伐克19日在北京签署科学技术合作联合委员会第31届会议议定书。国家科委副主任郭树言和捷国家科学技术与投资发展委员会副主席弗·布罗姆在文件上签字。国家科委主任宋健出席了签字仪式。　（据新华社）
