Title: Yu Ping County Launches Agricultural Water Conservancy Infrastructure Construction
Author: Zhao Er Xiao
Date: 1989-10-31
Edition: 1

Yu Ping County is increasing agricultural investment to ensure a bumper harvest. Recently, the county and township governments decided to invest 3 million yuan in agricultural water conservancy infrastructure construction, determining 83 projects for land reclamation.
