Title: Key Points for This Issue
Author:
Date: 1989-10-10
Page: 5

From five aspects, improve the structure of highway transportation.
	△ “Low standards, vegetables as substitutes,” bring invisible waste to highway transportation. It is suggested to pay attention to improving the grade of highways, and gradually implement lane driving on major highways.
	△ In the context of tight funds, “building fewer factories and more transportation infrastructure” can achieve twice the results with half the effort.

    本期要点
    △从5个方面改善公路运输结构
