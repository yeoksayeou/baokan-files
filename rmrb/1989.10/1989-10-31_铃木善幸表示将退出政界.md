Title: Suzuki Zenko Announces Retirement from Politics
Author:
Date: 1989-10-31
Page: 3

Former Japanese Prime Minister Suzuki Zenko announced on the morning of the 28th at his Tokyo residence that he will not participate in the next House of Representatives election and will officially retire from politics starting tomorrow. The 78-year-old Suzuki Zenko took over as Prime Minister in July 1980 following the death of Ohira Masayoshi due to illness, serving for two years and three months.

### 铃木善幸表示将退出政界

1989-10-31
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　铃木善幸表示将退出政界
    日本前首相铃木善幸二十八日晨在东京私邸向记者表示，他将不参加下届众院大选，从明天起正式宣布退出政界。现年七十八岁的铃木善幸是一九八○年七月接替因病逝世的大平正芳就任首相的，在位二年零三个月。
