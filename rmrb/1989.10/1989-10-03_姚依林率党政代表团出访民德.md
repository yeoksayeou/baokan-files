Title: Yao Yilin Leads Party and Government Delegation to Visit the GDR
Author:
Date: 1989-10-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 2nd.

At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and the Government of the German Democratic Republic, Comrade Yao Yilin, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, will lead a Chinese party and government delegation to Berlin to participate in the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic and to conduct a friendly visit.

Comrades Wu Xueqian, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, Luo Gan, Secretary-General of the State Council, Zhu Liang, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Director of the International Liaison Department, and Rolf Betthold, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, saw the delegation off at the airport.

### 姚依林率党政代表团出访民德

1989-10-03
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    姚依林率党政代表团出访民德
    新华社北京10月2日电  应德国统一社会党中央和德意志民主共和国政府邀请，中共中央政治局常委、中华人民共和国国务院副总理姚依林率领中国党政代表团今天上午离开北京前往柏林参加德意志民主共和国建国40周年庆祝活动并进行友好访问。前往机场送行的有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理吴学谦，国务院秘书长罗干，中共中央委员、中联部部长朱良，民主德国驻华大使罗尔夫·贝特霍尔德。
