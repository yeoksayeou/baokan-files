Title: Jiao Ziyang Meets and Hosts Dinner for Huang Wenhuan
Author:
Date: 1989-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 5th.

CPC Central Committee Politburo Standing Committee member Jiao Ziyang today met and hosted a dinner for China's old friend Huang Wenhuan at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

The host and guest had a cordial and friendly conversation.

CPC Central Committee International Liaison Department Director Zhu Liang attended the meeting and dinner.

### 乔石会见宴请黄文欢

1989-10-06
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    乔石会见宴请黄文欢
    新华社北京10月5日电　中共中央政治局常委乔石今天在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请中国人民的老朋友黄文欢。
    宾主进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    中共中央对外联络部部长朱良参加了会见和宴会。
