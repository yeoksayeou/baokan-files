Title: Yang Shangkun and Wang Zhen Meet Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-10-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30th.

State Chairman Yang Shangkun and Vice Chairman Wang Zhen met with a delegation of Japanese guests led by Japanese Diet member Okuda Keiichi in the Great Hall of the People this afternoon. The delegation included some members of the Long City Plan Japanese-Chinese Youth Exchange Mission. They also took a group photo together.

After the photo session, Wang Zhen had a friendly and cordial conversation with Okuda Keiichi and his delegation.

Wang Zhen expressed his satisfaction with the development of friendly cooperation between China and Japan. He said that China's policy of ensuring that the two countries and their peoples remain friendly for generations to come will not change.

He said that youth are the future of the world. For China and Japan to remain friendly for generations, it is necessary to strengthen exchanges between young people in the two countries, so that they understand the importance of Sino-Japanese friendship for the development of both countries and for maintaining peace in Asia and the world. Okuda Keiichi agreed with this view. He said that the purpose of his visit to China was also for this reason.

### 杨尚昆王震会见日客人

1989-10-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨尚昆王震会见日客人
    新华社北京9月30日电  国家主席杨尚昆、副主席王震今天下午在人民大会堂会见了由日本众议院议员奥田敬和率领的长城计划日本中国青少年交流使节团部分成员，并同他们一起合影留念。
    合影后，王震同奥田敬和一行进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    王震对中日两国友好合作关系的发展表示高兴，他说，中国关于中日两国、两国人民要世世代代友好下去的政策不会变。
    他说，青年是世界的未来。中日两国、两国人民要世世代代友好下去，必须加强两国青少年交往，使他们认识到中日友好对两国发展有利，对维护亚洲和世界和平有着重要的意义。奥田敬和对此表示同意。他说，他此次访华的目的也在于此。
