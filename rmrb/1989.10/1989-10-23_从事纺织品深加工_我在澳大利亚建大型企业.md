Title: Engaging in Deep Processing of Textiles
Author: Xǔ Fēng
Date: 1989-10-23
Edition: 2

According to Xinhua News Agency in Shenzhen, October 21st, (reporter Xu Feng) the reporter learned today from relevant sources that our country has purchased 50,000 square meters of land in the Darwin Trade Development Zone in Australia's Northern Territory. With a total investment exceeding 200 million Australian dollars (approximately 170 million US dollars), a large-scale textile deep processing and export enterprise is being established.

This is the first industrial enterprise in our country to purchase land and make the largest investment in Australia's Northern Territory. It is also the first industrial enterprise set up in the Darwin Trade Development Zone, which offers favorable investment conditions. The enterprise will use Chinese textile raw materials for deep processing and then export them to various parts of the world.

This project was formally signed on October 14th in Darwin, Australia, by S·Z·Australia Group Limited with the Darwin Trade Development Zone. The group company is composed of five cooperating companies: Hong Kong Shenzhen (Group) Limited, Shenzhen Ruijing Industrial Limited, Sichuan Branch of China National Textile Import and Export Corporation, Shenzhen International Trust and Investment Corporation, and Australian Han Yang Darwin Limited.

### 从事纺织品深加工  我在澳大利亚建大型企业
许烽
1989-10-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　从事纺织品深加工
　　我在澳大利亚建大型企业
    据新华社深圳10月21日电　（记者许烽）记者今天从有关方面获悉，我国在澳大利亚北部省的达尔文贸易发展区购地5万平方米、以超过2000万澳元（约合1700万美元）的总投资建立一家大型纺织品深加工出口企业。
    这是我国在澳大利亚北部省第一个购有土地的和投资的最大工业企业，也是我国在投资条件优惠的达尔文贸易发展区内设立的第一家工业企业。它将采用中国的纺织品原材料进行深加工后出口世界各地。
    这个项目是S·Z·澳洲集团有限公司于10月14日在澳大利亚达尔文市，与达尔文贸易发展区正式签约的。这个集团公司是由香港深业（集团）有限公司、深圳瑞景实业有限公司、中国纺织品进出口总公司四川分公司、深圳国际信托投资总公司和澳洲汉洋达尔文有限公司等5家合作组成。
