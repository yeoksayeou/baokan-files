Title: China and UNICEF Cooperation Achieves Significant Results Over Ten Years
Author: Xǔ Yì Chún
Date: 1989-10-17
Edition: 2

China and the United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF) have achieved significant results in their 10 years of cooperation, providing nearly $100 million in aid to China. These funds have been used for maternal and child health care, child nutrition, basic education, and other areas, achieving remarkable social benefits.

This was stated by Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin at a reception held by UNICEF Representative to China Man Ahmed to welcome the visit of UNICEF Executive Director James P. Grant and his wife. He hoped that during the implementation of the new phase (1990-1994) cooperation plan, the cooperative relationship between the two sides would be further developed.

Invited Chinese guests included Song Qingling Foundation Chairperson Kang Keqing, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lei Jieqiong, and Minister of Civil Affairs Cui Naiwu. Ambassadors from various countries and representatives of UN agencies in Beijing were also invited to the reception.

### 我同联合国儿童基金会合作十年取得显著成果
许忆纯
1989-10-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    我同联合国儿童基金会合作十年取得显著成果
    据新华社北京10月16日电（记者许忆纯）联合国儿童基金会和中国合作10年来，已向中国提供了近1亿美元的援助。这些援助在用于中国妇幼保健、儿童营养、基础教育等方面取得了显著的社会效益。
    这是经贸部部长郑拓彬在出席联合国儿童基金会驻华代表曼·阿赫默德为欢迎基金会执行主任格兰特和夫人访华举行的招待会上讲话时说的。他希望在新周期（1990—1994）合作方案的执行中，双方的合作关系得到进一步的发展。
    应邀参加今天招待会的中国客人有宋庆龄基金会主席康克清，全国人大常委会副委员长雷洁琼，民政部部长崔乃夫等。各国驻华大使和联合国机构驻京代表也应邀参加了今天的招待会。
