Title: The Strait Two-Sides Martial Arts Exhibition Match Held in Xiamen
Author: Lu Rugang
Date: 1989-10-31
Edition: 4

The Stone Chemical Cup Strait Two-Sides Martial Arts Exhibition Invitational Tournament was held on October 21 at the Jimei Gymnasium in the famous overseas Chinese hometown of Xiamen, Fujian Province.

The participating teams in this invitational tournament included the Taipei National Martial Arts Association, the Singapore Wushu Coaching Association, the Okinawa Ancient Martial Arts Preservation Association from Japan, and teams from Fuzhou, Xiamen, Zhangzhou, and the Fujian Sports Institute in Fujian Province, totaling 7 teams with 55 male and female athletes. Among them, the Taipei team had 13 members.

### 海峡两岸武术观摩赛在厦门举行
罗如岗
1989-10-31
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　海峡两岸武术观摩赛在厦门举行
    本报讯　石化杯海峡两岸武术观摩邀请赛10月21日在著名侨乡福建厦门市集美体育馆举行。
    参加这次邀请赛的有台北国术会、新加坡武术教练协会、日本冲绳古武道保存会武术代表队和福建省的福州、厦门、漳州、福建体院等共7支代表队，男女运动员55名。其中，台北队有13名。
  　（罗如岗）
