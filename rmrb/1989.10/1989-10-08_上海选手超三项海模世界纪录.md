Title: Shanghai Athletes Break Three National Records in Model Boat Racing
Author:
Date: 1989-10-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency

The 1989 National Model Boat (Appearance and Powerboat) Championships concluded in Shanghai on October 6.

Shanghai's Yao Wenkai broke the national record of 13.5 seconds he set on October 4 with a time of 13.3 seconds in the F1-EIKG category, surpassing the world record. Pu Haicheng from Shanghai Jiao Tong University broke his own national record of 11.5 seconds set last year with a time of 11.3 seconds in the F1-V6.5 category, also surpassing the world record. Zhou Jianming from Shanghai Jiao Tong University broke the national record of 11.9 seconds in the F1-V3.5 category with a time of 11.8 seconds, exceeding the world record.

### 上海选手超三项海模世界纪录

1989-10-08
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    上海选手超三项海模世界纪录
    1989年全国航海模型（外观及动力艇）锦标赛10月6日在上海结束。
    上海的姚文凯以13秒3的成绩打破他于4日创造的F1—EIKG13秒5的全国纪录（超世界纪录）。上海交通大学的浦海清以11秒3的成绩打破他去年创造的F1—V6.5全国纪录（超世界纪录）。上海交大的周建明以11秒8的成绩打破F1—V3.5全国纪录（11秒9），并超过世界纪录。　（据新华社）
