Title: UN General Assembly Elects New Non-Permanent Members of the Security Council
Author:
Date: 1989-10-21
Page: 3

The United Nations General Assembly on the 18th elected the People's Democratic Republic of Yemen, Romania, Cuba, Ivory Coast, and Zaire as new non-permanent members of the Security Council.

The Security Council, composed of fifteen members, includes five permanent members: China, the United States, the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, and France. The remaining five non-permanent members are currently Colombia, Ethiopia, Canada, Malaysia, and Finland.

### 联大选举安理会非常任理事国

1989-10-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　联大选举安理会非常任理事国
    联合国大会十八日选举民主也门人民共和国、罗马尼亚、古巴、科特迪瓦和扎伊尔为安理会新的非常任理事国。
    有十五个成员国组成的安理会除中国、美国、苏联、英国和法国五个常任理事国外，其余五个非常任理事国为哥伦比亚、埃塞俄比亚、加拿大、马来西亚和芬兰。
