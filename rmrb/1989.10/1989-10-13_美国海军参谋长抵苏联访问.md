Title: U.S. Navy Chief of Staff Arrives in the Soviet Union for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1989-10-13
Edition: 3

At the invitation of the Soviet Ministry of Defense, Admiral Carl E. Trost, Chief of Naval Operations of the United States, arrived in the Soviet Union on October 9 for an official visit. Trost met with Admiral Vladimir Chernavin, Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Navy, on October 8. He will also visit the Soviet Black Sea Fleet and meet with Soviet Defense Minister Dmitry Yazov and Soviet Chief of the General Staff Mikhail Moiseyev.

### 美国海军参谋长抵苏联访问

1989-10-13
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美国海军参谋长抵苏联访问
    应苏联国防部邀请，美国海军参谋长卡莱尔·特罗斯特上将九日抵达苏联进行正式访问。特罗斯特八日会见了苏联海军总司令切尔纳温上将，还将访问苏联黑海舰队及会晤苏联国防部长亚佐夫和苏军总参谋长莫伊谢耶夫。　（据新华社）
