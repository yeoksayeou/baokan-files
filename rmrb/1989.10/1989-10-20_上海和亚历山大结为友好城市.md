Title: Shanghai and Alexandria Establish Sister City Relationship
Author:
Date: 1989-10-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

Shanghai, China, and Alexandria, Egypt, have decided to establish a sister city relationship.

Chinese Ambassador to Egypt, Zhan Shiliang, and Governor of Alexandria Province, Jusiki, announced this news at the Chinese Consulate-General in Alexandria during a reception held to celebrate China's National Day.

### 上海和亚历山大结为友好城市

1989-10-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    上海和亚历山大结为友好城市
    中国上海市和埃及亚历山大市决定建立友好城市关系。
    中国驻埃及大使詹世亮和亚历山大省省长乔塞基在中国驻亚历山大总领馆最近举行的国庆招待会上宣布了这一消息。　（据新华社）
