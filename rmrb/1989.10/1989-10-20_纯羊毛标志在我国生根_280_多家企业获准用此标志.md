The Pure Wool Mark Becomes Established in China
Over 280 Enterprises Approved to Use the Mark

BEIJING, Oct. 19 (Xinhua) - The pure wool mark of the International Wool Secretariat is a globally recognized symbol of quality. Currently, over 280 Chinese export enterprises have been approved by the Secretariat to use this mark, achieving good reputation and benefits in the international market.

To enhance the competitiveness of wool textile exports, as early as 1981, the China National Quality Inspection and Quarantine Service signed a cooperation agreement with the International Wool Secretariat to conduct inspections and management of the mark on exported garments.

Recently, the China National Quality Inspection and Quarantine Service and the International Wool Secretariat reached an agreement. In the future, all Chinese exports with the pure wool mark, including garments, sweaters, yarn, fabric, blankets, and carpets, will be inspected and managed by the China National Quality Inspection and Quarantine Service. New testing and inspection centers will be established in Tianjin, Beijing, Shanghai, Guangdong, Jiangsu, Liaoning, and Heilongjiang. Starting from October 1, all export products intended to bear the pure wool mark will undergo strict testing and inspection according to global unified standards.

### 纯羊毛标志在我国生根  280多家企业获准用此标志
刘霄
1989-10-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    纯羊毛标志在我国生根
    280多家企业获准用此标志
    本报讯　记者刘霄报道：国际羊毛局的纯羊毛标志是世界公认的优质标志。目前我国已有280多家出口企业经该局批准使用这一标志，在国际市场上取得良好的信誉和效益。
    为了提高毛纺织品的出口竞争能力，早在1981年，中国商检总公司就与国际羊毛局签署了合作协议，对出口服装加贴标志进行检验和管理。
    最近，商检总公司和国际羊毛局又达成协议，今后中国出口的所有贴纯羊毛标志的纺织品，其检验和管理工作统交该公司办理，并确定天津、北京、上海、广东、江苏、辽宁、黑龙江等地成立新的测试检验中心。从10月1日起，不仅出口服装，而且羊毛衫、毛线、服装面料、毛毯、地毯等凡是准备加贴纯羊毛标志的出口商品，都要按世界统一标准进行严格的测试和检验。
