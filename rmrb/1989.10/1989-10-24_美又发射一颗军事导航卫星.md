Title: The United States Launches Another Military Navigation Satellite
Author:
Date: 1989-10-24
Edition: 3

The U.S. Air Force launched a military navigation satellite on the 21st, which is expected to enter a synchronous orbit after circling the Earth 43 times.

This is the fourth of the 21 global positioning satellites planned for launch by the United States. This satellite system will assist U.S. naval vessels, aircraft, and other military units in determining their positions globally, with an error margin of no more than 16 meters.

### 美又发射一颗军事导航卫星

1989-10-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美又发射一颗军事导航卫星
    美国空军二十一日将一颗军事导航卫星送入空间，预计这颗卫星在绕地球运转四十三圈之后进入同步轨道。
    这是美国计划发射的二十一颗全球导航定点卫星中的第四颗。这一卫星系统将帮助美国在全球的舰艇、飞机和其他军事单位测定自己所在的位置，误差不超过十六米。
