Title: The Second National Book Fair Opens
Author: Xībīng
Date: 1989-10-10
Edition: 2

The second national book fair opened today at the Beijing Workers' Cultural Palace. It is jointly organized by the New China Bookstore and the Beijing New China Bookstore.

According to the organizers, the book fair features nearly 40,000 titles, almost covering all the high-quality academic and artistic works published in the past one or two years.

### 第二届全国书市开幕
锡兵
1989-10-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    第二届全国书市开幕
    本报北京10月9日讯　第二届全国书市今天在北京劳动人民文化宫开幕。这是由新华书店总店和北京市新华书店联合主办的。
    据主办单位称，书市上推出的近4万种书籍，几乎可以囊括近一两年出版的学术、艺术水平较高的著作。　（锡兵）
