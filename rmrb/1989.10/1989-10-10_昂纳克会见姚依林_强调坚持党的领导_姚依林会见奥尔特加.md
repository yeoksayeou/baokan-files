Title: Honecker Meets Yao Yilin, Emphasizes Party Leadership; Yao Yilin Meets Ortega
Author: Wang Defeng, Xu Hongzhi, Yan Jianwei
Date: 1989-10-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 9th.

Democratic Germany's party and state leader Honecker received a Chinese party and government delegation led by Yao Yilin, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, at the Central Committee building of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany on the morning of the 9th. Both sides had a friendly and cordial exchange.

Honecker first welcomed the Chinese party and government delegation for visiting Democratic Germany to participate in the 40th anniversary celebrations and for the formal friendly visit. Honecker said that in the past 40 years, Democratic Germany has achieved tremendous successes. Honecker emphasized that in the future, Democratic Germany will continue to implement a policy of combining continuity with innovation. Honecker also introduced the preparations for the 12th Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. He said that the Socialist Unity Party of Germany is studying the development of socialism in the 1990s and pointed out that even in the 1990s, there cannot be a lack of party leadership.

Vice Premier Yao Yilin introduced the situation in China and pointed out that although China and Democratic Germany have different national conditions, their views on a series of major issues are consistent. He said that the great efforts made by Democratic Germany to adhere to the socialist path and maintain world peace have left a deep impression on him.

During the meeting, Yao Yilin conveyed greetings from the Chinese leaders to Honecker. Honecker also asked Yao Yilin to convey his greetings to the Chinese leaders.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 7th.

People's Daily reporter Xu Hongzhi, Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Defeng.

On the afternoon of the 7th, Yao Yilin, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, met with Nicaraguan President Ortega, who was participating in the 40th anniversary celebrations of Democratic Germany, at the Chinese Embassy in Democratic Germany.

### 昂纳克会见姚依林  强调坚持党的领导  姚依林会见奥尔特加
王德峰  许宏治  严建卫
1989-10-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    昂纳克会见姚依林  强调坚持党的领导
    姚依林会见奥尔特加
    新华社柏林10月9日电（新华社记者王德峰、人民日报记者许宏治、光明日报记者严建卫）民主德国党和国家领导人昂纳克9日上午在德国统一社会党中央委员会大厦会见了正在这里访问的由中共中央政治局常委、国务院副总理姚依林率领的中国党政代表团。双方进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    昂纳克首先对中国党政代表团来民主德国参加40周年国庆庆典并进行正式友好访问表示欢迎。昂纳克说，40年来，民主德国取得了巨大的成就。昂纳克强调，今后民主德国将继续执行连续性和革新相结合的政策。昂纳克还介绍了筹备德国统一社会党十二大的情况。他说，德国统一社会党正在研究90年代社会主义的发展问题，并且指出，到了90年代也不能没有党的领导。
    姚依林副总理介绍了中国的情况，同时指出，中国和民主德国虽然国情不同，但在一系列重大问题上观点一致。他说，民主德国为坚持社会主义道路和维护世界和平所作的巨大努力给他留下了深刻印象。
    会见时，姚依林转达了中国领导人对昂纳克的问候。昂纳克也请姚依林转达他对中国领导人的问候。
    新华社柏林10月7日电（人民日报记者许宏治　新华社记者王德峰）中共中央政治局常委、国务院副总理姚依林，7日下午在中国驻民主德国大使馆会见了参加民主德国40周年国庆活动的尼加拉瓜总统奥尔特加。
