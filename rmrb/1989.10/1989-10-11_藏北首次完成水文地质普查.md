Title: Northern Tibet Completes First Hydrogeological Survey
Author: Chen Yan, Xu Zhengyu
Date: 1989-10-11
Edition: 2

The People's Liberation Army's water supply engineering unit stationed in Xinjiang has worked tirelessly and successfully completed a survey of the water supply conditions in the northern Tibetan Plateau. The first hydrogeological report for the northern Tibetan Plateau has been released, marking the end of an era without hydrogeological data in this region.

The hydrogeological workers set out for the work area in the early part of the year, braving the bitter cold. They completed the hydrogeological survey of the northern Tibetan Plateau a year and a half ahead of schedule, obtaining valuable hydrogeological data.

### 藏北首次完成水文地质普查
陈焱  徐正玉
1989-10-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    藏北首次完成水文地质普查
    据新华社乌鲁木齐10月10日电（记者陈焱、通讯员徐正玉）人民解放军驻疆某给水工程团艰苦奋战，出色完成对西藏北部高原给水条件的调查工作。藏北高原第一份水质化验报告于日前出台，从而结束了这一地区无水文地质资料的历史。
    水文地质工作者今年初冒着严寒向作业区进发。他们提前一年半完成藏北高原水文地质调查，获得了宝贵的水文地质资料。
