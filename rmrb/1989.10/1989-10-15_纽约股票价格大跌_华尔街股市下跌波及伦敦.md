Title: New York Stock Prices Plunge; Wall Street Crash Affects London
Author: Zhang Liang, Chen Te'an
Date: 1989-10-15
Page: 3

People's Daily, Washington, October 14th - Reporter Zhang Liang: The New York Stock Exchange yesterday faced another severe crisis. The Dow Jones Industrial Average unexpectedly plummeted by 190.58 points, falling to 2569.26 points. This is the largest drop since the "Black Monday" of October 19, 1987, when the Dow Jones index plummeted by 508 points.

The news immediately sparked panic in the United States and became the headline news on major television networks, radio stations, and several leading newspapers. A widespread concern is whether the New York stock market will repeat the collapse of two years ago?

Experts analyzed that the trigger for yesterday's market crash was the likely failure of a massive transaction to acquire United Airlines. After the news spread, shareholders rushed to sell the company's stocks, quickly affecting other airlines, companies that might be acquired, and so-called "junk bonds" that are risky and have recently been volatile. Some believe that October 16th will be a bad day, and the Dow Jones index may continue to plummet.

People's Daily, London, October 14th - Reporter Chen Te'an: Following yesterday's plunge in the New York Wall Street stock market, investors here are facing a fearful weekend, worried about the arrival of another "Black Monday."

Local analysts predict that after the Wall Street stock market crash on Friday, the London stock market prices will likely fall next Monday, but the decline will not reach the disastrous levels of 1987, possibly dropping by around 100 points.

### 纽约股票价格大跌  华尔街股市下跌波及伦敦
张亮  陈特安
1989-10-15
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    纽约股票价格大跌
    华尔街股市下跌波及伦敦
    本报华盛顿10月14日电  记者张亮报道：纽约股票交易所昨天再度出现严重危机，道·琼斯30种工业股票平均价格指数出人意料地猛跌190.58点，跌至2569.26点。这是1987年10月19日“黑色星期一”（道·琼斯指数狂泻508点）以来跌幅最大的一次。
    消息传出之后，立即在美国引起一场惊慌，并成了各大电视网、电台和几家主要大报的头条新闻。人们普遍忧虑的一个问题是：纽约股票市场是否会重蹈两年前的覆辙？
    专家们分析，昨天股市暴跌的导火线是兼并联合航空公司的巨额交易很可能流产，消息传开后，股东们便纷纷抛售该公司的股票，迅速波及其它一些航空公司、有可能被兼并的公司的股票及风险大、最近又剧烈动荡的所谓“垃圾债券”。有人认为，16日凶多吉少，道·琼斯指数很可能继续大幅度下跌。
    本报伦敦10月14日电　记者陈特安报道：在昨天纽约华尔街股市暴跌后，这里的投资者面临一个恐惧的周末，担心“黑色星期一”的来临。 
    此间分析家预计，在星期五华尔街股市暴跌后，下星期一伦敦股市价格势将下跌，但跌幅不会达到1987年那种灾难性的程度，可能会下跌100点左右。
