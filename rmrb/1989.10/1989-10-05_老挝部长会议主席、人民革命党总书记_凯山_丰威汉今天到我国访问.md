Title: Chairman of the Council of Ministers and General Secretary of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party Kaysone Phomvihane Visits China
Author:
Date: 1989-10-05
Edition: 1

At the invitation of Premier Li Peng, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Lao People's Democratic Republic and General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party Kaysone Phomvihane arrived in Beijing on October 5 to make an official friendly visit to China. This is his fourth visit to China since 1971.

Kaysone Phomvihane was born on December 13, 1920, in Nasa Village, Kantaburi District, Savannakhet Province. He studied law at Hanoi University in Vietnam. In 1942, he participated in the anti-French and anti-Japanese student movement. In 1946, he joined the Indochinese Communist Party and led the anti-French movement among Lao people in Vietnam. In 1947, he became the leader of the resistance movement in northeastern Laos. In 1949, he established the Lao People's Armed Forces, known as "Latsavong," in Xieng Khouang Province. In 1950, he served as the Minister of Defense of the Resistance Government. In 1955, when the Lao People's Party (now the Lao People's Revolutionary Party) was founded, he was elected as the General Secretary and re-elected in the "Second," "Third," and "Fourth" Congresses. In 1958, he participated in the coalition government in Vientiane and was elected as a member of the National Assembly. In 1959, he returned to the Xieng Khouang base area to lead the national liberation war and served as the Deputy Chairman of the Lao Patriotic Front. In December 1975, after the establishment of the Republic, he served as the Prime Minister of the Government; in September 1982, the government was renamed the Council of Ministers, and he served as the Chairman.

### 老挝部长会议主席、人民革命党总书记  凯山·丰威汉今天到我国访问

1989-10-05
第1版(要闻)
专栏：人物介绍

    老挝部长会议主席、人民革命党总书记
    凯山·丰威汉今天到我国访问
    应李鹏总理的邀请，老挝人民民主共和国部长会议主席、老挝人民革命党中央委员会总书记凯山·丰威汉于本月五日到达北京对中国进行正式友好访问。这是他从一九七一年以来对中国的第四次访问。
    凯山·丰威汉一九二○年十二月十三日生于沙湾拿吉省康塔布里县纳赛村。早年就学越南河内大学法律系。一九四二年参加反法、反日学生运动。一九四六年加入印度支那共产党，负责领导旅越老侨的反法运动。一九四七年为老挝东北地区抵抗运动负责人。一九四九年在桑怒省组建名为“拉萨冯”的老挝人民武装力量。一九五○年任抗战政府国防部长。一九五五年老挝人民党（今老挝人民革命党）成立，当选为总书记，在“二大”、“三大”、“四大”均当选连任。一九五八年参加万象联合政府，当选为国民议会议员。一九五九年重返桑怒根据地，领导民族解放战争，并担任老挝爱国战线副主席。一九七五年十二月共和国成立，任政府总理；一九八二年九月政府改称部长会议，任主席。（新华社）（附图片）
