Title: Liu Huimin Completes 30 Years of Work in 10 Years
Author:
Date: 1989-10-10
Edition: 1

Liu Huimin, a milling machine operator at the Mechanical and Electrical Repair General Factory of Fuxin Mining Bureau in Liaoning Province, has completed the work of 30 years and 5 months in just 10 years and 6 months.

In 1980, when the workshop was short of milling machine operators, Liu Huimin voluntarily took on the work of three people, operating eight milling machines simultaneously.

### 刘会民10年干30年活

1989-10-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    刘会民10年干30年活
    据新华社沈阳十月九日电　辽宁阜新矿务局机电修配总厂铣工刘会民，在十年零六个月时间里完成了三十年零五个月的工作量。
    一九八零年，在车间一时派不出铣工的情况下，刘会民主动要求，一人挑起了三个人的担子，操纵八台铣床。
