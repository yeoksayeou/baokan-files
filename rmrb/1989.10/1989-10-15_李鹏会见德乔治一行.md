Title: Li Peng Meets with the Delegation Led by De George
Author:
Date: 1989-10-15
Edition: 1

Premier Li Peng met with and hosted a dinner for the delegation led by Pierre De George, Chairman and CEO of the Franco-British General Electric Alsthom Transnational Corporation, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse this evening. Both sides had a friendly and cordial conversation.

State Councilor Zhou Jiahua, Minister of Energy Huang Yicheng, General Manager of the China National Nuclear Corporation Jiang Xionghong, Chairman of the China Huadian Corporation Wang Defang, and General Manager Wang Chuanshu attended the meeting and dinner.

### 李鹏会见德乔治一行

1989-10-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏会见德乔治一行
    新华社北京10月14日电　国务院总理李鹏今晚在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请了法英通用电气阿尔斯通跨国公司董事长兼总经理德乔治一行，双方进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    国务委员邹家华、能源部长黄毅诚、中国核工业总公司总经理蒋心雄、华能集团公司董事长汪德方及总经理王传剑等参加了会见和晚宴。
