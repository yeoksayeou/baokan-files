Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 22nd.

Foreign Affairs Briefs

	▲ State Councilor Zhou Jiaxuan met with foreign experts attending the Second World Non-Metallic Minerals Conference on the evening of the 20th at the Great Hall of the People in Beijing.

	The Second World Non-Metallic Minerals Conference opened on October 17th in Beijing, with more than 300 representatives from over 40 countries participating in the conference.

	▲ Prosecutor-General Liu Fuzhi of the Supreme People's Procuratorate met with a delegation from the Soviet Prosecutor's Office led by Deputy Prosecutor-General K. S. Pobedimov in the afternoon of the 20th at the Great Hall of the People. The delegation arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 20th at the invitation of Liu Fuzhi.

	▲ Chairman Wu Xiuquan of the China-Soviet Friendship Association met with a delegation of veteran Soviet journalists led by Deputy Editor-in-Chief of Pravda Z. V. Valovoy on the 21st at the Great Hall of the People, expressing welcome to the Soviet guests and exchanging friendly sentiments with them. On the evening of the 20th, Chairman Wu Lengxi of the Chinese Journalists Association hosted a banquet for the Soviet guests at the Beijing Hotel.

	▲ State Councilor and Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission Song Jian met with Professor Ieoh Ming Pei of the University of Waterloo, Canada, on the 20th at the Great Hall of the People. The main purpose of Pei's visit is to promote the development of human resources between Canada and China, to implement the China-Canada university education management project, and to discuss issues related to the development of education, science and technology, and the economy.

	▲ An exhibition of works by the famous Sri Lankan artist Senaka Senanayake opened on the 19th at the National Art Museum of China.

### 外事简讯

1989-10-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：外事简讯

　　外事简讯
    ▲国务委员邹家华20日晚在北京人民大会堂会见了参加第二届世界非金属矿物会议的外国专家。
    第二届世界非金属矿物会议10月17日在北京开幕，来自世界40多个国家的代表300余人参加了会议。
    ▲最高人民检察院检察长刘复之20日下午在人民大会堂会见由副总检察长何·谢·波别日莫夫率领的苏联检察院代表团。代表团是应刘复之邀请于20日上午抵达北京的。
    ▲中苏友协会长伍修权21日在人民大会堂会见由《真理报》副总编辑季·瓦·瓦洛沃伊为团长的苏联老新闻工作者代表团，对苏联客人来访表示欢迎，宾主共叙友情。
    20日晚，中国记协主席吴冷西在北京饭店宴请了苏联客人。　（本报讯）
    ▲国务委员兼国家科委主任宋健20日在人民大会堂会见了加拿大滑铁卢大学贝聿渠教授。贝聿渠此行的主要目的是促进加、中人才资源的开发，执行中、加大学教育管理项目，并与有关方面讨论教育和科技、科技和经济的发展等问题。
    ▲斯里兰卡著名画家塞纳卡·森纳那亚克作品展19日在中国美术馆开幕。　（新华社）
