Title: Chinese Embassies Hold Receptions to Celebrate the 40th Anniversary of National Day
Author:
Date: 1989-10-07
Edition: 3

Chinese embassies in Asia and Africa recently held receptions to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Chinese Ambassador to Japan Yang Zhenya held a reception on October 6 for Chinese residents in the Tokyo area. Chen Huangwang, president of the Tokyo Chinese Association, and nearly 200 Chinese representatives attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to the Central African Republic Zhou Xianjue held a reception on September 30. Foreign Minister Briya represented President Kolinga and attended the reception, which was attended by more than 250 people from all walks of life.

Chinese Ambassador to Libya Wang Houli held a reception on September 30, attended by more than 200 friends from all walks of life.

Chinese Chargé d'Affaires to Cape Verde Mo Shiheng held a reception on September 30, attended by some friendly people.

### 我驻外使馆举行招待会热烈庆祝国庆四十周年

1989-10-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我驻外使馆举行招待会热烈庆祝国庆四十周年
    新华社北京10月6日电  综合本社驻亚洲和非洲地区记者报道，中国驻亚洲和非洲4个国家的使节近日举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    中国驻日本大使杨振亚6日为在东京地区的华侨举行招待会，东京华侨总会会长陈焜旺及近200名华侨代表出席了招待会。
    中国驻中非大使周贤觉9月30日举行招待会，外交部长布里亚代表总统科林巴出席，共有各界人士250多人参加了招待会。
    中国驻利比亚大使王厚立9月30日举行招待会，各界朋友200多人出席了招待会。
    中国驻佛得角大使馆临时代办宓世衡9月30日举行招待会，一些友好人士参加了招待会。
