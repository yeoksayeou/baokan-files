Title: Our Banner Will Be Published
Author: Xuē Xī
Date: 1989-10-06
Edition: 4

	Our Banner Will Be Published

	The editorial department of the Study magazine has invited comrades who participated in the drafting work and relevant departments of the Central Committee to write the book "Our Banner: Building Socialism with Chinese Characteristics—Study Materials for Jiang Zemin's Speech at the 40th Anniversary Celebration of the Founding of the People's Republic of China." The book will be published soon by the China Excellent Publishing Company.

	This is to accompany the propaganda and study of Comrade Jiang Zemin's important speech on National Day.

### 《我们的旗帜》将出版
雪希
1989-10-06
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    《我们的旗帜》将出版
    本报讯　为配合宣传、学习江泽民总书记的国庆重要讲话，《学习》杂志编辑部邀请参加起草工作和中央有关部门的同志编写的《我们的旗帜：建设有中国特色的社会主义——学习江泽民同志在中华人民共和国成立四十周年大会上的讲话辅导材料》一书，近日由中国卓越出版公司出版。
        （雪希）
