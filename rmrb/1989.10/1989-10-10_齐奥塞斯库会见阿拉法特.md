Title: Ceausescu Meets Arafat
Author:
Date: 1989-10-10
Edition: 3

Romanian President Ceausescu met with visiting Palestinian President Arafat on October 8. During the meeting, both sides agreed that the recent proposals by Egyptian President Mubarak and the Israeli proposal for elections in the occupied territories could become part of the measures to establish Middle East peace. The two presidents also emphasized the need to use the opportunity of the current session of the United Nations General Assembly to strengthen political and diplomatic contacts and actions, in order to resolve the Middle East issue, including direct contact and talks between Israel and the Palestinian state.

### 齐奥塞斯库会见阿拉法特

1989-10-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    齐奥塞斯库会见阿拉法特
    罗马尼亚总统齐奥塞斯库八日会见了来访的巴勒斯坦国总统阿拉法特。在会见中，双方认为，埃及总统穆巴拉克最近提出的建议以及以色列提出的在以占区进行选举的建议可能成为建立中东和平措施的组成部分。两位总统还强调指出应该利用本届联大的机会加强政治—外交接触和行动，以便解决中东问题，包括让以色列和巴勒斯坦国进行直接接触和会谈。
