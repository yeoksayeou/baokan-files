Title: Fudan University Students Visit Jiangnan Shipyard
Author:
Date: 1989-10-15
Page: 2

On October 14th, Fudan University's honorary president, Xie Xide (third from the right), along with students from the university's School of Journalism and outstanding youth league cadres, visited the Jiangnan Shipyard to learn about the factory's construction. Fudan University has appointed some cadres, veteran workers, and outstanding intellectuals from this shipyard as consultants for the school's political and ideological education work.

New China News Agency, Photo by Wang Ziyin (telephoto)

### 复旦学生到江南造船厂考察（图片）
王子瑾
1989-10-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    复旦学生到江南造船厂考察
    10月14日，上海复旦大学名誉校长谢希德（右三）和该校新闻学院学生及优秀团干部一起，到上海江南造船厂了解工厂建设情况。复旦大学聘请这个厂的一些干部、老工人和优秀知识分子，做学校的政治思想教育工作的顾问。
                  新华社记者　王子瑾摄（传真照片）
