Title: Lebanese National Reconciliation Document Sparks Diverse Reactions
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-10-25
Edition: 3

The Lebanese parliament approved the national reconciliation document in Taif on the 22nd, sparking varied reactions from different factions within the country.

The Oren military government ordered its armed forces to be on the highest state of alert. The three-party committee envoy Ibrahim Ibrahim has handed over the national reconciliation document to Oren and invited him to attend today's parliamentary closing ceremony in Jeddah. After the meeting, Oren said that accepting this document is a "crime." He denied that refusing to accept the document means resuming hostilities with Syrian troops. Oren launched a fierce attack on Christian faction parliamentarians who voted in favor of the reconciliation document and accused the United States of being the mastermind behind the scenes. It is reported that there is a split within the Christian faction over the reconciliation document.

Druze leader Jumblatt stated that he "accepted the reconciliation document at the request of Syria," but the document "has no effect on Lebanon."

The pro-Iranian Lebanese Shia organization openly threatened to assassinate parliamentarians who voted in favor of the document.

The pro-Syrian Hussein interim government welcomed the parliament's adoption of the reconciliation document and called on all factions to unconditionally implement the document's provisions. Hussein said that rejecting the document is tantamount to "suicide." This is Lebanon's "last chance" to avoid disaster.

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, October 23rd. U.S. State Department spokesman Richard Boucher today said the U.S. government welcomes the Lebanese parliament's approval of a national reconciliation compromise document aimed at ending a 15-year civil war.

The document, drafted by the Arab League's three-party committee, calls for a fair redistribution of power among Lebanese factions and outlines steps to eliminate the causes of the civil war.

Boucher said that all parties concerned must seize this special opportunity, consolidate the achievements made, and ensure the full implementation of the document.

He also called on the international community to "take appropriate measures" to support this plan.

Meanwhile, the Arab League urged the United States to re-deploy its diplomatic personnel to Beirut and provide economic aid to Lebanon to show its support for the reconciliation plan.

However, Boucher said the U.S. government currently has no plans to reopen its embassy in Beirut.

The United States closed its embassy in Beirut on September 6th this year on security grounds and withdrew its diplomatic personnel.

### 黎民族谅解文件引起不同反应  黎基督教派军队处于戒备状态
林皎明
1989-10-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　黎民族谅解文件引起不同反应
　　黎基督教派军队处于戒备状态
    本报开罗十月二十四日电　记者林皎明报道：黎巴嫩议会二十二日在塔伊夫通过全国和解文件，引起国内各派不同反应。
    奥恩军政府下令所属武装进入最高戒备状态。三方委员会特使易卜拉希米已将全国和解文件面交奥恩，邀请他出席今天在吉达举行的议会闭幕式。奥恩会见后说，接受这份文件是“犯罪”。他否认拒绝接受文件意味着与叙利亚军队重新开火。奥恩对投票赞成和解文件的基督教派议员进行猛烈抨击，同时指控美国是背后阴谋策划者。据报道，基督教派内部就和解文件出现分裂。
    德鲁兹派领导人琼卜拉特声明，他“应叙利亚的要求接受和解文件”，但文件“对黎巴嫩毫无作用”。
    亲伊朗的黎巴嫩什叶派组织则公开威胁要刺杀对文件投赞同票的议员。
    亲叙利亚的胡斯临时政府欢迎议会通过和解文件，呼吁各派无保留地执行文件条款。胡斯表示，拒绝文件“无异于自杀”。这是黎巴嫩避免灾难的“最后一次机会”。
    新华社华盛顿十月二十三日电　美国国务院发言人塔特怀勒今天表示，美国政府欢迎黎巴嫩议会为结束长达十五年的内战而批准通过的一项全国民族和解妥协文件。
    这项由阿盟三方委员会制定的文件要求黎巴嫩各派重新公正地分配权力，并规定了消除内战因素的各项步骤。
    塔特怀勒表示，目前有关各方必须抓住这一特殊的机会，巩固已取得的成果，使文件得以全面实施。
    她还要求国际社会“采取适当的措施”支持这一方案。
    与此同时，阿拉伯联盟组织呼吁美国向贝鲁特重新派驻外交人员，并向黎巴嫩提供经济援助，以表明美国对和解计划的支持。
    但是，塔特怀勒表示，美国政府目前尚未有计划重新开放在贝鲁特的使馆。
    美国于今年九月六日以安全为理由关闭了它在贝鲁特的使馆，并撤走了外交人员。
