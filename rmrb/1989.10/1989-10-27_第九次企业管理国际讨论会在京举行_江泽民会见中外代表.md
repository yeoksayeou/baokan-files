Title: The Ninth International Conference on Enterprise Management Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-10-27
Edition: 1

The Ninth International Conference on Enterprise Management was held in Beijing. Jiang Zemin met with Chinese and foreign representatives.

New China News Agency, Beijing, October 26 - General Secretary of the Communist Party of China, Jiang Zemin, met this afternoon with representatives from China and abroad who are attending the Ninth International Conference on Enterprise Management in Beijing.

Jiang Zemin had a friendly conversation with the representatives and answered their questions.

Jiang Zemin reiterated that China will continue to adhere to the policy of reform and opening up, and welcomes foreign enterprises to continue investing in China.

The conference, which opened yesterday, was jointly organized by the China Enterprise Management Association and the World Economic Forum. More than 70 foreign businesspeople from over 20 countries and regions participated in this annual conference along with Chinese representatives.

### 第九次企业管理国际讨论会在京举行  江泽民会见中外代表

1989-10-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　第九次企业管理国际讨论会在京举行
    江泽民会见中外代表
    新华社北京10月26日电　中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午在人民大会堂会见了出席在北京举行的第九次企业管理国际讨论会的中外代表。
    江泽民同代表们进行了友好的谈话并回答了他们的问题。
    江泽民重申，中国将继续坚持改革开放政策，欢迎外国企业继续在中国投资。
    昨天开幕的这个讨论会是由中国企业管理协会和世界经济论坛联合召开的。来自20多个国家和地区的70多位外国企业家与中国代表一起参加了这个一年一度的讨论会。
