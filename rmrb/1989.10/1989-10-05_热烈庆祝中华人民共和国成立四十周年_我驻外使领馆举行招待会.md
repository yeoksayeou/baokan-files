Title: Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China: Our Embassies and Consulates Hold Receptions

Author:

Date: 1989-10-05

Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th

China's embassies and consulates in some Asian and African countries recently held receptions to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Asia

	•	Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, Zhang Delin, held a reception in Ulaanbaatar on September 29. Mongolian Party Central Committee Politburo Member and Central Secretary Morolmzamts and Vice Premier Suren attended the reception.

	•	Chinese Consul General to Dubai, Yu Xingzhi, held a reception on September 30. Over 250 guests attended the reception.

	•	Chinese Ambassador to the People's Democratic Republic of Yemen, Lin Zhen, held a reception on October 1. Political Bureau Member of the Yemeni Socialist Party and Prime Minister Numan, along with several ministers and officials, attended the reception.

	•	Chinese Ambassador to Qatar, Hu Changlin, held a reception on October 1. Sheikh Mohammed Al Thani, brother of the Emir, and several ministers attended, with over 100 guests in total.

	•	Chinese Ambassador to Bangladesh, Chen Songlu, held a reception on October 1. Bangladeshi Prime Minister Khaleque Zia Ahmed and other high-ranking officials and friends from various sectors attended, with over 500 guests in total.

Africa

	•	Chinese Ambassador to Somalia, Shi Chengxun, held a reception on September 30. Somali Revolutionary Socialist Party Deputy Secretary General Mohammed, Deputy Prime Minister Abdullah Mohammed Fadhil, and Deputy Chief of the General Staff Mohammed Salah attended, with over 300 guests in total.

	•	Chinese Ambassador to Angola, Hu Liping, held a reception on September 29. Angolan party and government officials, representatives from mass organizations, and friends from various sectors attended the reception.

	•	Chinese Ambassador to Côte d'Ivoire, Cai Zaidu, held a reception on September 29. Over 300 government officials and people from various sectors attended the reception.

	•	Chinese Ambassador to Morocco, Wan Yongxiang, held a reception on September 29. Four Moroccan ministers and over 400 people from various sectors attended the reception.

	•	Chinese Ambassador to Cameroon, Shen Lianrui, held a reception on October 2. Vice President of the National Assembly Mukuru Enosh and several ministers and high-ranking officials attended, with over 300 guests in total.

	•	Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, Yu Huimin, held a reception on September 30. Several Guinean ministers and military and political officials, along with friends from various sectors, attended, with over 300 guests in total.

	•	Chinese Ambassador to Chad, Zhou Zhendong, held a reception on October 1. Two Chadian ministers and over 170 guests attended the reception.

### 热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立四十周年  我驻外使领馆举行招待会

1989-10-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立四十周年
    我驻外使领馆举行招待会
    本报综合新华社消息　中国驻亚洲和非洲一些国家的使领馆近日分别举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    亚洲
    ▲中国驻蒙古大使张德麟9月29日在乌兰巴托举行招待会，蒙党中央政治局委员、中央书记莫洛姆扎木茨和部长会议副主席苏伦等出席了招待会。
    ▲中国驻迪拜总领事郁兴志9月30日举行招待会，250多名客人出席了招待会。
    ▲中国驻民主也门大使林真1日举行招待会，民主也门社会党政治局委员、政府总理努曼和一些部长、官员等出席了招待会。
    ▲中国驻卡塔尔大使胡昌林1日举行招待会，埃米尔殿下之兄谢赫·穆罕默德·阿拉萨尼亲王和一些大臣等100余人出席了招待会。
    ▲中国驻孟加拉国大使陈嵩禄10月1日晚举行招待会，孟加拉国总理卡齐·扎法尔·艾哈迈德和其他高级官员、各界朋友共500多人出席了招待会。
    非洲
    ▲中国驻索马里大使施承训9月30日晚举行招待会，索马里革命社会党副总书记穆罕默德、副总理阿卜杜拉·穆罕默德·法迪勒和国防军副总参谋长穆罕默德·萨拉赫等300人出席了招待会。
    ▲中国驻安哥拉大使胡立鹏9月29日晚举行招待会，安哥拉党政官员、群众组织的代表以及各界友好人士出席了招待会。
    ▲中国驻科特迪瓦大使蔡再杜9月29日举行招待会，科特迪瓦政府官员及各界人士约300人出席了招待会。
    ▲中国驻摩洛哥大使完永祥9月29日举行招待会，摩洛哥4名大臣及各界人士400多人出席了招待会。
    ▲中国驻喀麦隆大使申连瑞2日晚举行招待会，喀麦隆国民议会副议长默库鲁·埃诺什和一些部长、高级官员等共300多人出席了招待会。
    ▲中国驻几内亚大使禹惠民9月30日晚举行招待会，几内亚政府一些部长和军、政官员及各界朋友共300多人出席了招待会。
    ▲中国驻乍得大使周振东1日举行招待会，乍得2名部长等170多人出席了招待会。
