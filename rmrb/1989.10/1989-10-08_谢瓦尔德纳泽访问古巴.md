Title: Shevardnadze Visits Cuba
Author:
Date: 1989-10-08
Edition: 3

    Shevardnadze Visits Cuba
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Soviet Foreign Minister Shevardnadze concluded his 24-hour visit to Cuba on October 5.

    It is reported that during his stay in Havana, Shevardnadze held talks with Cuban leader Castro, and the two sides exchanged views on bilateral relations and Central American issues. Shevardnadze stated, "The Soviet Union will continue to support Cuba's socialist construction."

### 谢瓦尔德纳泽访问古巴

1989-10-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    谢瓦尔德纳泽访问古巴
    据新华社哈瓦那电　苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽5日结束了对古巴24小时的访问。
    据悉，谢瓦尔德纳泽在哈瓦那逗留期间与古巴领导人卡斯特罗举行了会谈，双方就两国关系以及中美洲问题交换了意见。谢瓦尔德纳泽表示，“苏联将继续支持古巴的社会主义建设。”
