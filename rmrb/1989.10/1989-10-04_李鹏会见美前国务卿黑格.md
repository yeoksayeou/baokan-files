Title: Li Peng Meets with Former U.S. Secretary of State Haig
Author:
Date: 1989-10-04
Edition: 1

Li Peng Meets with Former U.S. Secretary of State Haig

New China News Agency, Beijing, October 3 — Premier Li Peng met with former U.S. Secretary of State and Chairman of the American United Global Corporation, Alexander Haig, this morning at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

During the meeting, Li Peng said to Haig, “You are an old friend of the Chinese people. We will not forget the contributions made by old friends like Nixon, Kissinger, and you for the development of Sino-American relations.” He said that politicians like Nixon had a strategic vision for developing Sino-American relations, and their contributions will be recorded in the annals of Sino-American history.

Li Peng briefed Haig on the domestic situation in China. Both sides also exchanged views on some international issues and economic cooperation between the United Global Corporation and China in a frank and friendly atmosphere. Li Peng expressed the hope that businesspeople from both China and the United States would strengthen ties and cooperation on the basis of mutual benefit.

Following the meeting, Li Peng hosted a luncheon in honor of Haig and his delegation.

Wang Daohan, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the China International Trust and Investment Corporation, and others attended the meeting and the luncheon.

Haig came to China at the invitation of the China International Trust and Investment Corporation to participate in a seminar on Sino-foreign economic cooperation.

### 李鹏会见美前国务卿黑格

1989-10-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏会见美前国务卿黑格
    新华社北京10月3日电  国务院总理李鹏今天上午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了美国前国务卿、美国环球联合公司董事长亚历山大·黑格。
    会见时，李鹏对黑格说：“你是中国人民的老朋友。我们不会忘记尼克松、基辛格和你等老朋友为中美关系的发展所作出的贡献。”他说，尼克松等政治家对发展中美关系具有战略眼光，他们为此作出的贡献将载入中美关系的史册。
    李鹏向黑格介绍了中国国内的情况。双方还在坦诚、友好的气氛中，就一些国际问题以及环球联合公司同中国的经济合作交换了意见。李鹏表示，希望中美两国企业界人士在互惠互利的基础上加强联系和合作。
    会见后，李鹏设午宴招待黑格一行。
    全国人大常委会副委员长、中国国际信托投资公司董事长荣毅仁等参加了会见和宴会。
    黑格是应中信公司的邀请前来参加中外经济合作研讨会的。
