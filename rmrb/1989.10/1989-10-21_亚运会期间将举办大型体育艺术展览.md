Title: A Major Sports Art Exhibition to be Held During the Asian Games
Author: Jiang Zhengmao
Date: 1989-10-21
Page: 4

A major sports art exhibition will be held in Beijing during the 11th Asian Games, according to information obtained by our reporter, Jiang Zhengmao, today from relevant departments.

The exhibition will include the following sections: Chinese Sports Fine Arts Exhibition, Chinese Sports Photography Art Exhibition, Asian Youth and Children's Sports Painting Exhibition, and Asian Sports Picture Exhibition.

The exhibition aims to create a warm and friendly atmosphere for the Asian Games and to showcase the achievements of sports in China and Asia.

### 亚运会期间将举办大型体育艺术展览
江正茂
1989-10-21
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　亚运会期间将举办大型体育艺术展览
    本报北京10月19日讯　记者江正茂今天从有关部门获悉：第十一届亚运会期间，将在北京举办大型体育艺术展览。
    这次展览的内容有，中国体育美术展、中国体育摄影艺术展、亚洲少年儿童体育绘画展和亚洲体育图片展。
    展览旨在为亚运会创造热烈友好的气氛，展示中国和亚洲体育运动所取得的成就。
