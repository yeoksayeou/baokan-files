Title: Chinese Foreign Minister Holds Reception at the UN for National Day
Author: Yao Haiquan
Date: 1989-10-04
Edition: 3

Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, who is attending the 44th session of the UN General Assembly, hosted a reception at the Chinese Permanent Mission to the United Nations this evening to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The entrance to the Chinese Mission near the Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts in Manhattan was adorned with four large red lanterns, creating a festive atmosphere. UN Secretary-General Javier Perez de Cuellar, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, President of the Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea, and Khieu Samphan, Vice President of the Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea, as well as foreign ministers from more than 20 countries and numerous representatives from other countries, totaling about 500 people, attended the reception.

Old friends of the Chinese people, such as Henry Kissinger and Lord Salisbury, also attended to offer their congratulations.

### 钱外长在联合国举行国庆招待会
姚海泉
1989-10-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    钱外长在联合国举行国庆招待会
    本报联合国10月2日电　出席第44届联大的中国外长钱其琛今晚在中国驻联合国代表团举行招待会，欢庆中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    位于纽约曼哈顿林肯艺术中心附近的中国代表团驻地，门口高悬
  4盏大红宫灯，显得格外热闹。联合国秘书长德奎利亚尔，民柬联合政府总理宋双、民柬副主席乔森潘，以及二十几个国家的外长和许多国家驻联合国代表共约500多人出席了今天的招待会。
    基辛格、索尔兹伯里等中国人民的老朋友也到场祝贺。（姚海泉）
