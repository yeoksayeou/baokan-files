Title: U.S. House of Representatives Passes Bill to Ban Flag Burning
Author:
Date: 1989-10-16
Page: 3

The U.S. House of Representatives on the 12th passed a bill to ban the burning and destruction of the American flag. The bill stipulates that anyone who intentionally burns, tears, defiles, or tramples on the American flag, or throws the American flag on the ground, will be sentenced to one year in prison and fined $1,000. The bill has now been submitted to U.S. President Bush for approval. Bush said today at the White House that he will allow the bill to take effect without signing it.

### 美众院通过法案禁止焚烧国旗

1989-10-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美众院通过法案禁止焚烧国旗
    美国众议院十二日通过一项法案，禁止焚烧和毁坏美国国旗。这项法案规定，任何人不得故意焚烧、撕毁、污损和踩踏美国国旗或将美国国旗扔在地上，否则将被判处一年监禁并罚款一千美元。目前，这项法案已提交美国总统布什批准。布什总统今天在白宫说，他将以不签名的形式允许这项法案生效。
