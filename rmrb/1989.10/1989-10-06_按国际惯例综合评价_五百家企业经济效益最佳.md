Title: Comprehensive Evaluation According to International Norms: The Top 500 Enterprises in Economic Performance

Author: Lǐ Niánguì, Liú Xiàoyáng

Date: 1989-10-06

Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 5th. Economic performance is the foundation of industrial enterprises and national economic development. Which enterprises have the best economic performance?

The State Council Development Research Center's "Management World," the China Enterprise Evaluation Center, and the National Bureau of Statistics' Industrial Department today announced the 500 best-performing enterprises in China for 1988.

After comprehensive evaluation, the top-performing enterprises in 13 industries, including metallurgy, mining, chemical, textile, power, machinery, and electronics, are: Capital Iron and Steel Company, Daqing Petroleum Administration Bureau, Dalian Petrochemical Company, North China Pharmaceutical Factory, Shanghai Electric Power Industry Bureau, Pingdingshan Nylon Curtain Factory, Shanghai Volkswagen Automobile Company, Changsha Refrigerator Factory, Shanghai Bicycle Factory, Fenjiu Winery, Yuxi Cigarette Factory, Jidong Cement Factory, and Jiamusi Paper Factory.

These 500 enterprises are the main sources of national revenue and the foundation of the national economy. Last year, these enterprises contributed 72.27 billion yuan in taxes and profits, accounting for nearly one-third of the total taxes and profits of all industrial enterprises in the country.

Among them, Capital Iron and Steel Company contributed 2.1 billion yuan in taxes and profits, while Daqing Oil Field and Yuxi Cigarette Factory each contributed over 1.1 billion yuan in taxes and profits.

This evaluation was conducted according to international standards, using five key indicators that best reflect enterprise economic performance: labor productivity, per capita taxes and profits, net value rate, sales profit rate, and capital profit rate. The results were then comprehensively evaluated to determine the rankings.

### 按国际惯例综合评价  五百家企业经济效益最佳
李年贵  刘燮阳
1989-10-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    按国际惯例综合评价
    五百家企业经济效益最佳
    本报北京10月5日讯　新华社记者李年贵、本报记者刘燮阳报道：经济效益是工业企业和国民经济发展之本。谁家的经济效益最好？
    国务院发展中心《管理世界》、中国企业评价中心和国家统计局工交司，今天公布了1988年我国500家最佳经济效益企业。
    经过综合评价，处于冶金、采矿、化工、纺织、电力、机械、电子等13个行业最佳经济效益企业之首的是：首都钢铁公司、大庆石油管理局、大连石化公司、华北制药厂、上海电力工业局、平顶山锦纶帘子布厂、上海大众汽车有限公司、长沙电冰箱厂、上海自行车三厂、杏花村汾酒厂、玉溪卷烟厂、冀东水泥厂、佳木斯造纸厂。
    这500家企业是我国财政收入的主要来源、国民经济的重要基础。去年这些企业上交利税达722.7亿元，占全国工业企业利税总数的近1／3。
    其中，首钢上交利税达21亿元，大庆油田、玉溪卷烟厂上交的利税也都超过11亿元。
    这次评价是按照国际惯例，采用最能反映企业经济效益的劳动生产率、人均利税、净产值率、销售利润率和资金利润率等五项指标，进行综合评价得出的结果排出名次。
