Title: UN Secretary-General Submits Report to UNGA, Urges More Aid for Cambodian Refugees
Author:
Date: 1989-10-29
Edition: 3

The United Nations Secretary-General today submitted a report to the UN General Assembly, calling on the international community to provide more assistance to the tens of thousands of Cambodian refugees gathered along the Cambodian-Thai border.

The report notes that as of the end of September, there were approximately 309,000 Cambodian refugees along the Cambodian-Thai border, who are entirely dependent on UN relief aid. This has resulted in the UN relief project facing a deficit of $5 million for the first time.

Perez de Cuellar urged donor countries to make greater contributions to meet the urgent needs of the Cambodian refugees.

### 联合国秘书长向联大提交报告  呼吁给柬埔寨难民更多援助

1989-10-29
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    联合国秘书长向联大提交报告
　　呼吁给柬埔寨难民更多援助
    新华社联合国10月27日电  联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔今天在提交给联大讨论的一份报告中呼吁国际社会向聚集在柬泰边境的成千上万柬埔寨难民提供更多的援助。
    报告指出，截至9月底，柬泰边境约有30.9万柬埔寨难民，他们完全依靠联合国的救济援助，因而使联合国救济项目第一次出现了500万美元的亏空。
    佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔呼吁各援助国家为解决柬埔寨难民的迫切需求作出更大的贡献。
