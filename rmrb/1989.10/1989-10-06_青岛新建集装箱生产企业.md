Title: New Container Production Enterprise Established in Qingdao
Author: Jì Yuánzhāo
Date: 1989-10-06
Edition: 2

Qingdao General Container Engineering Co., Ltd. was recently established. This is a joint venture between Qingdao Shipyard, the Shandong branch of the China Foreign Transport Corporation, and the German company Habtek-Schuech GmbH Container Engineering Co., Ltd. The total investment is $17.5 million, with a registered capital of $7 million. The joint venture will produce 15,000 international maritime containers annually, all of which will be exported.

### 青岛新建集装箱生产企业
纪援朝
1989-10-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    青岛新建集装箱生产企业
    本报讯　青岛通用集装箱工程有限公司最近成立。这是由青岛造船厂、中国对外贸易运输公司山东分公司和联邦德国哈勃泰克——施尔汉集装箱工程有限公司三家合资兴办的，总投资1750万美元，注册资本700万美元。合资企业年产1.5万个国际海运集装箱，产品全部出口。　　　　（纪援朝）
