Title: The Rodong Sinmun Calls on Youth to Fully Understand the Superiority of Socialism
Author:
Date: 1989-10-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 13th.

The Rodong Sinmun, the organ of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, today published an article calling on the youth of Korea to fully understand the superiority of socialism and to defend the banner of socialism.

The article states that fully understanding the superiority of socialism and carrying the socialist cause through to the end is a fundamental requirement for today's youth. At a time when imperialist countries are beautifying capitalism and slandering socialism, it has become an urgent issue for the youth to understand the socialist system. Especially for the younger generation who have not experienced the life of an exploitative society, it is all the more important to raise their understanding of the socialist system.

The article calls on the youth of Korea to smash various reactionary ideological challenges and firmly defend the banner of socialism.

The article also calls on the youth to strengthen organizational life, fully utilize the role of school youth organizations, eliminate various reactionary currents, and maintain the health and purity of the youth movement. To this end, they must arm themselves with ideological theory and strengthen exchanges with progressive youth organizations around the world.

### 朝鲜《劳动新闻》号召青年  充分认识社会主义优越性

1989-10-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    朝鲜《劳动新闻》号召青年
    充分认识社会主义优越性
    新华社平壤10月13日电  朝鲜劳动党中央机关报《劳动新闻》今天发表署名文章，号召朝鲜青年充分认识社会主义的优越性，捍卫社会主义的旗帜。
    文章说，充分认识社会主义的优越性，把社会主义事业进行到底，这是对当代青年的基本要求。今天，在帝国主义国家美化资本主义，对社会主义进行诽谤中伤的情况下，青年对社会主义制度的认识就成了迫切的问题。特别是青年一代没有经历过剥削社会的生活，提高对社会主义制度的认识就尤为重要。
    文章要求朝鲜青年粉碎各种反动思想的挑战，牢牢地捍卫社会主义的旗帜。
    文章还要求青年加强组织生活，高度发挥学校青年组织的作用，清除各种反动思潮，以保持青年运动的健康性和纯洁性。为此，要以思想理论为武器，加强与世界进步青年组织的交流。
