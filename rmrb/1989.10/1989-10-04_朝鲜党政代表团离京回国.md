Title: North Korean Party and Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1989-10-04
Edition: 2

North Korean Party and Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 3rd: The delegation of the North Korean Workers' Party Central Committee and the North Korean government, led by Kim Jong-yul, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the North Korean Workers' Party and Vice Chairman of the State Affairs Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, left Beijing by special plane this afternoon after participating in the celebrations for the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Wang Xiancheng, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, went to the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse to bid farewell to the North Korean guests. Wang Xiancheng said to Kim Jong-yul, "The Chinese Party and government thank Chairman Kim Il-sung for sending the delegation to participate in China's celebrations." Kim Jong-yul said, "During the celebration activities, they clearly saw the joy of victory expressed by the Chinese people after suppressing the turmoil."

### 朝鲜党政代表团离京回国

1989-10-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    朝鲜党政代表团离京回国
    新华社北京10月3日电  朝鲜劳动党中央政治局委员、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国家副主席李钟玉率领的朝鲜党政代表团，在参加了中华人民共和国成立40周年的庆祝活动之后，今天下午乘专机离开北京回国。
    中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理吴学谦前往钓鱼台国宾馆同朝鲜客人话别。吴学谦对李钟玉说，中国党和政府感谢金日成主席派遣代表团参加中国的庆典。李钟玉说，他们在参加庆祝活动中清楚地看到中国人民在平息动乱之后所表现出来的胜利喜悦。
