Title: Some Countries and Party Leaders Send Telegrams to Chinese Leaders Congratulating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1989-10-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 4th.

In recent days, leaders of some countries and political parties have sent telegrams to Chairman Yang Shangkun, Premier Li Peng, and Chairman Wan Li, warmly congratulating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Those who sent congratulatory telegrams to Chairman Yang Shangkun include:

President Ali Abdullah Saleh of the Republic of Yemen, Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly;
President Kenneth David Kaunda of the Republic of Zambia;
President Hissène Habré of the Republic of Chad, Head of State;
Chairman of the Central Committee of the Congolese Labor Party, President of the People's Republic of the Congo, and Head of Government Denis Sassou Nguesso;
Founder and Chairman of the Togolese People's Union, President of the Togolese Republic Gnassingbé Eyadéma;
Founder and Chairman of the Democratic Party of Equatorial Guinea, President of the Republic of Equatorial Guinea, Head of State, and Head of Government Teodoro Obiang Nguema Mbasogo;
President Carlos Menem of the Argentine Republic.

Those who sent congratulatory telegrams to Premier Li Peng include:

President Carlos Menem of the Argentine Republic;
Prime Minister Mahmoud Zu'bi of the Syrian Arab Republic;
Prime Minister and Head of Government Celestino da Costa of the Democratic Republic of São Tomé and Príncipe.

Those who sent congratulatory telegrams to Chairman Wan Li include:

Chairman of the Senate of the Polish People's Republic Andrzej Stelmachowski and Speaker of the Parliament Mieczysław Kozakiewicz;
Speaker of the National People's Assembly of the Republic of Guinea-Bissau Tiago Allérua Lopes.

King Jigme Singye Wangchuck of Bhutan also sent congratulatory telegrams to Premier Li Peng and Chairman Wan Li.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 4th.

In recent days, some foreign political parties and their leaders have sent congratulatory telegrams and letters to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and General Secretary Jiang Zemin, warmly congratulating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Those who sent congratulatory letters and telegrams to the CPC Central Committee include:

General Secretary of the Mexican Socialist People's Party Indalecio Saáyago Herrera;
Central Committee of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine;
Central Committee of the People's Vanguard Party of Costa Rica.

Those who sent congratulatory letters and telegrams to General Secretary Jiang Zemin include:

First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party Mieczysław F. Rakowski;
General Secretary of the Communist Party of Peru (Red Fatherland) Alberto Moreno;
Chairman of the Revolutionary Left Alliance of Peru Rolando Breña Pantoha;
General Secretary of the Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine Naif Hawatmeh.

### 一些国家和政党领导人电函我国领导人  祝贺中华人民共和国成立四十周年

1989-10-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    一些国家和政党领导人电函我国领导人
    祝贺中华人民共和国成立四十周年
    新华社北京10月4日电　近日来，一些国家和政党领导人分别致电杨尚昆主席、李鹏总理和万里委员长，热烈祝贺中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    向杨尚昆主席发来贺电的有：
    阿拉伯也门共和国总统、武装部队总司令、全国人民大会常委会主席阿里·阿卜杜拉·萨利赫；
  赞比亚共和国总统肯尼思·戴维·卡翁达；
    乍得共和国总统、国家元首侯赛因·哈布雷；
    刚果劳动党中央委员会主席、刚果人民共和国总统、政府首脑德尼·萨苏·恩格索；
    多哥人民联盟创始人、主席、多哥共和国总统纳辛贝·埃亚德马；
    赤道几内亚共和国总统、国家元首、政府首脑、赤道几内亚民主党创始人、主席奥比昂·恩圭马·姆巴索戈；
    阿根廷共和国总统卡洛斯·梅内姆。
    向李鹏总理发来贺电的有：
    阿根廷共和国总统卡洛斯·梅内姆；
    阿拉伯叙利亚共和国总理马哈茂德·祖埃比；
    圣多美和普林西比民主共和国总理、政府首脑塞莱斯蒂诺·罗沙·达科斯塔。
    向万里委员长发来贺电的有：
    波兰人民共和国参议院主席安·斯泰尔马霍夫斯基和议会议长米·科扎凯维奇；
    几内亚比绍共和国全国人民议会议长蒂亚戈·阿莱路亚·洛佩斯。
    不丹国王吉格梅·辛格·旺楚克也分别向李鹏总理和万里委员长发来了贺电。
    新华社北京10月4日电　近日来，又有一些外国政党及其领导人，分别给中国共产党中央委员会和中共中央总书记江泽民同志发来贺电贺信，热烈祝贺中华人民共和国成立40周年。
    给中共中央发来贺信贺电的有：
    墨西哥社会主义人民党总书记因达莱西奥·萨亚戈·埃雷拉；
    解放巴勒斯坦人民阵线中央委员会；
    哥斯达黎加人民先锋党中央委员会。
    给江泽民总书记发来贺信贺电的有：
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记米奇斯瓦夫·弗·拉科夫斯基；
    秘鲁共产党（红色祖国）总书记阿尔韦托·莫雷诺；
    秘鲁革命左派联盟主席罗兰多·布雷尼亚·潘托哈；
    解放巴勒斯坦民主人民阵线总书记纳耶夫·哈瓦特迈赫。
