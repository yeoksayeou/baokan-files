Title: Standing Committee Chairman Wan Li and Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Meet with Soviet Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-10-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 30th.

Standing Committee Chairman Wan Li and Vice Premier Tian Jiyun met with a delegation from the Soviet Union this evening at the Great Hall of the People. The delegation was led by Deputy Chairman of the Soviet-China Friendship Association and former First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, I.V. Arkhipov.

The hosts and guests reminisced about their past friendly exchanges.

Arkhipov served as the chief advisor for Soviet experts in China during the 1950s.

### 万里田纪云会见苏联客人

1989-10-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    万里田纪云会见苏联客人
    新华社北京9月30日电  全国人大常委会委员长万里和国务院副总理田纪云今天晚上在人民大会堂会见了由苏中友协副主席、苏联前部长会议第一副主席依·瓦·阿尔希波夫率领的苏联对外友协老战士访华团。
    宾主共叙了昔日的友好交往。
    阿尔希波夫于50年代担任过苏联援华专家总顾问。
