Title: Red Cross Workers Kidnapped in Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1989-10-08
Page: 3

Two employees of the International Committee of the Red Cross from Switzerland were kidnapped early on the 6th in the southern Lebanese port city of Sidon.

A spokesperson for the International Committee of the Red Cross, headquartered in Geneva, said the two individuals are named Schristan, 32, and Erlich, 23, both of whom are technical staff at a plastic surgery center.

### 红十字会人员在黎遭绑架

1989-10-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    红十字会人员在黎遭绑架
    两名瑞士红十字国际委员会的工作人员六日晨在黎巴嫩南部的港口城市赛达遭到绑架。
    总部设在日内瓦的红十字国际委员会的发言人说，这两人一个叫什里斯唐、三十二岁，另一个叫厄尔里克兹、二十三岁，均为整形中心的技术人员。
