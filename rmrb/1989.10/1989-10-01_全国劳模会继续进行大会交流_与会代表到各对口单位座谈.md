Title: Nationwide Labor Model Conference Continues with Symposium and Visits to Relevant Departments
Author: Míng Wàng
Date: 1989-10-01
Edition: 2

The nationwide conference to honor labor models and advanced workers continued this morning with a symposium at the Great Hall of the People.

Members of the Political Bureau Standing Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier Yao Yilin, State Councilors Song Jian and Wang Fang, and State Council Secretary Luo Gan attended the meeting. Zhao Dongwen, Minister of the State Personnel Department, presided over the conference.

The first speaker was Chen Dengming, the leader of the "Tiger" tunneling team at the No. 30 site of the Huabei Coal Mining Construction Company in Anhui. Known for his hard work and dedication, he is affectionately called "Little Tiger" by his colleagues. The renowned farmer entrepreneur, Lu Guanqiu, the general manager of Hangzhou Wanxiang Universal Joint Factory in Zhejiang, spoke on the topic "Breaking Free from Narrow-Minded Rural Thinking, Relying on Quality Products to Go Global." Another speaker from the industrial front was Shen Guan, the leader of the 1205 drilling team of the Daqing Oil Field Drilling Company. He led the team, famous nationwide for the spirit of the "Iron Man" Wang Jinxi, to continue and carry forward the spirit of the "Iron Man," making outstanding achievements in oil field construction.

Sun Li Hua, a worker from the River West District Environmental Sanitation Bureau in Tianjin, Xu Xiu Ting, deputy director of the Central Tax Bureau in Weifang, Shandong, Sun Qiao Li, a senior engineer specializing in ancient geology at the First District Geological Survey Team of the Xinjiang Geological Bureau, and Yu Guoyin, a rural postal worker from the Yangxi Post Office in Yun County, Hubei, each shared their stories of diligent and dedicated work in their respective positions for the country and the people.

Today in the afternoon, some representatives attending the nationwide conference to honor labor models and advanced workers visited relevant departments for discussions. These departments included the State Education Commission, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, the Ministry of Metallurgy, the Ministry of Aeronautics and Astronautics, the Ministry of Health, the Ministry of Agriculture, the Ministry of Forestry, the Ministry of Textile Industry, the Ministry of Justice, the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade, the State Bureau of Building Materials, and the General Administration of Customs.

"We are a drop of water in the vast ocean, and we must return to the ocean." The labor models expressed: The work we have done is still far from the honor bestowed by the Party and the government. We will continue to strive and make greater contributions in our future work. Many labor models offered constructive opinions and suggestions to the departments they visited. Li Tieying, Rong Yiren, Ni Zhifu, and Wang Guangying attended the discussions with the labor models.

Today, the All-China Women's Federation held a meeting in Beijing to award the national "March 8th Red Banner" honor to 435 women attending the nationwide conference to honor labor models and advanced workers, and presented them with certificates of honor.

Chen Muxia, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairperson of the All-China Women's Federation, spoke at the meeting, hoping that the honored women would inspire all women to advance bravely in the four modernizations and the women's liberation movement.

### 全国劳模会继续进行大会交流  与会代表到各对口单位座谈
明旺
1989-10-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    全国劳模会继续进行大会交流
    与会代表到各对口单位座谈
    据新华社北京9月30日电　全国劳动模范和先进工作者表彰大会今天上午在人民大会堂继续进行大会经验交流。
    中共中央政治局常委、国务院副总理姚依林，国务委员宋健、王芳，国务院秘书长罗干等参加了大会。国家人事部部长赵东宛主持会议。
    今天第一个登上讲台的是安徽淮北煤矿建设公司三十处“猛虎”掘进队队长陈登明。由于他在煤矿建设中不怕苦、不怕累，被同志们称为“小老虎”。著名农民企业家、浙江杭州万向节总厂厂长鲁冠球的发言题目为《破小农意识狭隘眼光，靠优质产品走向世界》。另一个来自工业战线的是大庆油田钻井公司1205钻井队队长申冠，他率领这支因铁人王进喜而闻名全国的钻井队，继承发扬铁人精神，在油田建设中做出突出业绩。
    天津市河西区环卫局工人孙丽华、山东潍坊市央子税务所副所长徐秀亭、新疆地矿局第一区调大队地层古生物高级工程师孙巧缡和湖北省郧县杨溪邮电支局乡邮员余国印分别介绍了他们在各自平凡岗位上为祖国、为人民勤勤恳恳、踏踏实实努力工作的事迹。
    据新华社北京9月30日电　今天下午，在京参加全国劳动模范和先进工作者表彰大会的部分代表分别到各对口单位进行了座谈。这些对口单位是：国家教委、全国总工会、全国妇联、工商联、冶金部、航空航天部、卫生部、农业部、林业部、纺织部、司法部、经贸部、国家建材局、海关总署等。
    “我们是大海里的一滴水，还要回到大海去。”劳模们纷纷表示：我们所做的工作与党和政府给予的荣誉还有很大距离。在今后的工作中要再接再厉，做出更大的贡献。许多劳模对所在部委的工作提出了一些建设性的意见和建议。李铁映、荣毅仁、倪志福、王光英等同志参加了同劳模们的座谈。
    本报北京9月30日讯　今天，全国妇联在京集会，向435名参加全国劳动模范和先进工作者表彰大会的妇女授予全国“三八”红旗手荣誉称号，并颁发荣誉证书。
    全国人大常委会副委员长、全国妇联主席陈慕华在会上讲了话，希望受表彰的同志带动广大妇女在四化建设和妇女解放运动中奋勇前进。
    （明旺）
