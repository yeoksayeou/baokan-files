Title: The PLO Executive Committee Issues a Statement
Author:
Date: 1989-10-19
Edition: 3

The PLO Executive Committee issued a statement after a two-day meeting on October 17, reaffirming its commitment to the resolutions of the Palestine National Council and the Palestinian peace plan.

The statement said that the PLO Executive Committee opposes any other plans because the Palestinian peace plan includes principles for achieving lasting and just peace in Palestine.

On the issue of Palestinian-Israeli dialogue, the PLO Executive Committee stated that only the PLO has the right to choose representatives to engage in dialogue with Israel. The PLO Executive Committee proposed several principles for the Palestinian-Israeli dialogue:

Only the PLO has the right to select representatives from Palestinians both inside and outside the occupied territories to form a Palestinian delegation; the Palestinian position will be based on the Palestinian peace plan; the agenda for the Palestinian-Israeli dialogue must be open and without any preconditions; such a dialogue should be seen as a preparatory step for an international Middle East conference; preliminary Palestinian-Israeli talks should be conducted with the participation of the five permanent members of the UN Security Council and the UN Secretary-General.

### 巴解中央委员会发表声明  坚持巴勒斯坦和平计划

1989-10-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴解中央委员会发表声明
    坚持巴勒斯坦和平计划
    新华社巴格达10月17日电  巴勒斯坦中央委员会在举行两天会议后，17日在此发表声明，表示坚持巴勒斯坦全国委员会的决议和巴勒斯坦和平计划。
    声明说，巴中央委员会反对任何其他计划，因为，巴勒斯坦的和平计划包含有争取实现巴勒斯坦持久和公正的和平的原则。
    在巴勒斯坦和以色列对话问题上，巴中央委员会认为，巴解组织唯一有权选择同以色列对话的代表。巴中央委员会提出了巴以对话的几点原则：
    巴解组织唯一有权从被占领土内外的巴勒斯坦人中挑选代表组成巴勒斯坦代表团；巴勒斯坦的立场将以巴勒斯坦和平计划为基础；巴以对话的议程必须是公开的和不附带任何前提条件的；这样的巴以对话应当被视为举行国际中东和会的准备步骤；巴以的预备对话应在联合国安理会5个常任理事国和联合国秘书长的参加下进行。
