Title: North Korean Ambassador Hosts Banquet to Celebrate 40th Anniversary of China-North Korea Diplomatic Relations
Author: Cheng Yuansheng
Date: 1989-10-09
Edition: 2

North Korean Ambassador Hosts Banquet to Celebrate 40th Anniversary of China-North Korea Diplomatic Relations

	People's Daily, Beijing, October 8th — Reporter Cheng Yuansheng reports: North Korean Ambassador Zhu Changjun hosted a banquet today to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China.

	Ambassador Zhu Changjun and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of China Liu Shucai both delivered speeches at the banquet. They pointed out that over the past 40 years, the two countries have effectively cooperated in various fields including politics, diplomacy, economy, and culture, achieving tremendous results. They jointly wished for the continued development of the friendship between the peoples of China and North Korea, which has been forged in blood.

	The Deputy Foreign Minister of North Korea, Cha Fungju, who came to Beijing specifically to participate in the celebration activities, attended the banquet. Also present were some officials from relevant Chinese departments.

### 朝鲜驻华大使举行宴会  庆祝朝中建交40周年
成元生
1989-10-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    朝鲜驻华大使举行宴会
    庆祝朝中建交40周年
    本报北京10月8日讯　记者成元生报道：朝鲜驻华大使朱昌骏今天中午举行宴会庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和中华人民共和国建交40周年。
    朱昌骏大使和我国外交部副部长刘述卿在宴会上发表讲话。他们指出，两国建交40年来在政治、外交、经济、文化等各个领域里进行着有效的合作，取得了巨大的成果。他们共同祝愿中朝两国人民用鲜血凝成的友谊不断发展。
    专程来京参加庆祝活动的朝鲜外交部副部长车风柱出席了宴会。参加今天宴会的还有有关中国部门的一些负责人。
