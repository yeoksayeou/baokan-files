Title: Jiang Zemin's National Day Speech Published in Five Ethnic Scripts
Author:
Date: 1989-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 18th.

Jiang Zemin's speech, "Speech at the Celebration of the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China," has been published in Mongolian, Tibetan, Uyghur, Kazakh, and Korean—five minority ethnic scripts. The publication began circulation today in Inner Mongolia, Tibet, Xinjiang, and the three northeastern provinces.

### 江泽民国庆讲话用五种民族文字出版

1989-10-19
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    江泽民国庆讲话用五种民族文字出版
    新华社北京10月18日电　江泽民总书记《在庆祝中华人民共和国成立四十周年大会上的讲话》已用蒙古、藏、维吾尔、哈萨克、朝鲜5种少数民族文字出版，并于今日开始向内蒙古、西藏、新疆和东北三省发行。
