Title: Former Punjab Governor Returns to People's Party
Author:
Date: 1989-10-18
Page: 3

Pakistan People's Party leader Wali Muhammad Mustafa Khan announced at a press conference on October 16 that he would return to the ruling Pakistan People's Party. He had previously served as the governor of Punjab.

He said that he made this decision considering the current unstable political situation in Pakistan.

### 巴前旁遮普省长重返人民党

1989-10-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴前旁遮普省长重返人民党
    巴基斯坦国家人民党领导人吴拉姆·穆斯塔法·哈尔十六日在一次记者招待会上宣布，他将重返正在执政的巴基斯坦人民党。他曾担任旁遮普省省长。
    他说，他是因为考虑到巴基斯坦目前不稳定的政治局势而作出上述决定的。
