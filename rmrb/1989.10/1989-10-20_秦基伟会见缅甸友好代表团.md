Title: General Qin Jwei Meets with Myanmar Friendly Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-10-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 19th.

General Qin Jwei, State Councilor and Minister of National Defense, met with a Myanmar friendly delegation led by General Danrui, Commander-in-Chief of the Army, this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People.

Qin Jwei first extended a warm welcome to the Myanmar guests on behalf of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense. He said that China and Myanmar are very friendly, and the peoples of the two countries have a long-standing, traditional friendship.

Qin Jwei said that over the past 40 years, especially in the 10 years since the reform and opening up, China has achieved tremendous progress. The national economy has grown, the living standards of urban and rural residents have improved, and the defense industry has also made significant achievements along with the development of the national economy.

He said that China adheres to an independent and peaceful foreign policy and strengthens solidarity and cooperation with Third World countries. This is a steadfast policy. He pointed out: "Strengthening friendship and exchanges between the armies of China and Myanmar is in the interests of both countries."

General Danrui said that Myanmar attaches great importance to and cherishes the friendship between Myanmar and China. The delegation's visit is to further strengthen friendly exchanges between the two countries and their armies.

Both sides also exchanged views on other issues of common concern.

After the meeting, Vice Chief of the General Staff Xu Xin hosted a banquet to welcome the Myanmar guests.

### 秦基伟会见缅甸友好代表团

1989-10-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    秦基伟会见缅甸友好代表团
    据新华社北京10月19日电　国务委员兼国防部长秦基伟上将今天下午在人民大会堂会见由陆军总司令丹瑞中将率领的缅甸友好代表团。
    秦基伟首先代表中国国防部对缅甸贵宾来访表示热烈欢迎。他说，中缅两国非常友好，两国人民有着悠久的、传统的友谊。
    秦基伟说，40年来，特别是改革开放10年来，中国取得了巨大成就，国民经济增长了，城乡人民生活水平提高了，国防工业也随着国民经济的发展取得了很大的成就。
    他说，奉行独立自主的和平外交政策，加强与第三世界国家的团结与合作，是中国坚定不移的方针。他指出：“加强中、缅两国军队的友谊与交往符合两国的利益”。
    丹瑞中将说，缅甸重视和珍惜缅中友谊，代表团来访是为了进一步加强两国、两军的友好交往关系。
    双方还就共同关心的其它问题交换了意见。
    会见后，徐信副总参谋长举行宴会欢迎缅甸贵宾。
