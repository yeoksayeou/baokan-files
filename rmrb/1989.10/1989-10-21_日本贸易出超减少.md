Title: Japan's Trade Surplus Decreases
Author:
Date: 1989-10-21
Page: 7

Japan's Ministry of Finance recently released trade statistics for the first half of this fiscal year (April to September), indicating that during this period, Japan's trade surplus decreased by 12.8 percent compared to the same period last year.

During this period, Japan's export value increased by 3.7 percent compared to the same period last year, reaching $13.7212 billion; the import value increased by 10.3 percent, reaching $10.4218 billion. The main products that saw an increase in exports were office equipment, transportation machinery, and electronic components. The main products that saw an increase in imports were crude oil, as well as textiles and office equipment.

### 日本贸易出超减少

1989-10-21
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

　　日本贸易出超减少
    日本大藏省最近发表的今年度上半年（四月至九月）贸易统计表明，这一期间日本贸易出超比去年同期减少百分之十二点八。
    这一期间，日本出口额比去年同期增加百分之三点七，为一千三百七十二点一二亿美元；进口额增加百分之十点三，为一千零四十二点一八亿美元。日本出口增加的商品是办公机器、运输机械和电子零件。进口增加的商品主要是原油以及纺织品和办公机器等。　（据新华社）
