Title: The Release of "Good Youth of China" and "Good Children of China"
Author: Wáng Nàméi
Date: 1989-10-11
Page: 4

China Youth Daily, Beijing, October 10th.

Reporter Wang Nàméi reports: On the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Young Pioneers, China Youth Daily and Hebei Fine Arts Publishing House jointly held the launch ceremony for the books "Good Youth of China" and "Good Children of China," as well as the award ceremony for "Good Youth of China," "Good Children of China," "Good Youth," and "Good Children" in the Beijing region, at the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference this morning. Leaders such as Wáng Shǒudào, Hú Qiúmù, Chéng Sīyuǎn, and Lǚ Zhèngcāo, along with relevant department heads, attended the event. They presented certificates and books to the representatives of "Good Youth of China" and "Good Children of China."

### 《中国好少年》《中国好儿童》发行
王娜梅
1989-10-11
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    《中国好少年》《中国好儿童》发行
    本报北京十月十日讯　记者王娜梅报道：在少先队建队四十周年的日子里，中国少年报社和河北美术出版社今天上午在全国政协礼堂联合举办《中国好少年》《中国好儿童》两书首发式暨“中国好少年”、“中国好儿童”、“好少年”、“好儿童”北京地区发奖会。王首道、胡乔木、程思远、吕正操等领导同志及有关部门负责人出席了这个会，并向“中国好少年”、“中国好儿童”的代表发了证书和书籍。
