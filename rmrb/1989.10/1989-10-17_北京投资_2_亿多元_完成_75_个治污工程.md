Title: Beijing Invests Over 200 Million Yuan to Complete 75 Pollution Control Projects
Author: Feng Yuanyan
Date: 1989-10-17
Edition: 2

Beijing has completed 75 key pollution control projects, further improving the capital's environment.

Among these projects, the most notable include the dredging and restoration of Honglingjin Park, the treatment of alcohol waste liquid at the Beijing Alcohol Factory, and the sewage treatment and comprehensive utilization at the Beijing Fermented Bean Curd Factory. These projects cover water, air, waste, and noise pollution, with a total investment of over 200 million yuan.

### 北京投资2亿多元  完成75个治污工程
冯元雁
1989-10-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    北京投资2亿多元  完成75个治污工程
    本报讯　北京市75个重点污染治理项目完工，首都环境得到进一步改善。
    这批污染治理项目最引人注目的有：红领巾公园清淤恢复原貌治理工程，北京酒精厂的酒精糟液治理工程；北京腐乳厂的污水处理综合利用等。这批污染治理项目包括水、气、渣、噪声等，总投资达2亿元。　　　　（冯元雁）
