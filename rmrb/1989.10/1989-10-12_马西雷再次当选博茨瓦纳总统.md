Title: Masire Re-elected as President of Botswana
Author:
Date: 1989-10-12
Edition: 3

After securing a landslide victory in the sixth general election held last week, Dr. Masire, who has been re-elected as the President of Botswana, took the oath of office on the 10th.

In his inaugural address delivered in front of the Parliament building, Masire pledged to continue maintaining social prosperity and economic growth. He called on everyone to continue making selfless contributions for this purpose. In last week's election, the ruling Botswana Democratic Party won 31 out of the 34 parliamentary seats, an increase of three seats from the 28 seats won in 1984.

### 马西雷再次当选博茨瓦纳总统

1989-10-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    马西雷再次当选博茨瓦纳总统
    在上周举行的第六届大选中获得压倒胜利后，蝉联博茨瓦纳总统的马西雷博士十日宣誓就职。
    在议会大楼前面发表的就职演说中，马西雷表示要继续保持社会的繁荣和经济增长，他吁请大家继续为此作出无私的奉献。在上周的选举中，执政的博茨瓦纳民主党获得了议会三十四个席位的三十一席，比一九八四年的二十八席增加三个席位。
