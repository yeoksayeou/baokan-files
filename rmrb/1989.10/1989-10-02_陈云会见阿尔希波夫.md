Title: Chen Yun Meets Arkhipov
Author: Zhou Cixu
Date: 1989-10-02
Edition: 1

Chen Yun Meets Arkhipov

New China News Agency, Beijing, October 1 (Reporter Zhou Cixu) The Chairman of the Central Advisory Commission of the Communist Party of China, Chen Yun, met with his old friend from the 1950s, the Vice Chairman of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and former First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Ivan V. Arkhipov, this evening on Tiananmen Gate.

Chen Yun and Arkhipov had a warm and friendly conversation.

Arkhipov told Chen Yun that over the past few years, Sino-Soviet relations have undergone significant changes. Chen Yun expressed his satisfaction with the recent developments in Sino-Soviet relations.

### 陈云会见阿尔希波夫
周慈朴
1989-10-02
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    陈云会见阿尔希波夫
    新华社北京10月1日电（记者周慈朴）中共中央顾问委员会主任陈云今天晚上在天安门城楼上会见了他50年代的老朋友、苏中友协副主席、苏联前部长会议第一副主席伊·瓦·阿尔希波夫。陈云和阿尔希波夫进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    阿尔希波夫对陈云说，几年来，苏中关系发生了很大的变化。陈云对中苏关系近来的发展表示高兴。
