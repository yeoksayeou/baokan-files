Title: New Chinese Ambassador to the U.S., Zhu Qizhen, Arrives in Washington
Author: Zhang Qixun
Date: 1989-10-22
Edition: 3

New Chinese Ambassador to the U.S., Zhu Qizhen, Arrives in Washington

	Washington, October 20 (Xinhua) — New Chinese Ambassador to the U.S., Zhu Qizhen, arrived in Washington today. He told reporters upon his arrival that as China's ambassador, he hopes to do some positive work to promote the development of Sino-U.S. relations and friendship between the two peoples during his term.

	He said that indeed, Sino-U.S. relations are currently facing difficulties. He believes that as long as both sides adhere to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the principles established in the three Sino-U.S. joint communiqués, Sino-U.S. relations can overcome the current difficulties and return to the path of normal development.

### 我新任驻美大使朱启祯抵华盛顿
张启昕
1989-10-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我新任驻美大使朱启祯抵华盛顿
    本报华盛顿10月20日电  记者张启昕报道：新任中华人民共和国驻美国大使朱启祯今天上午乘飞机抵达华盛顿。他抵达时对记者发表简短谈话说，他作为中国大使，希望在他任期内能够为促进中美关系的发展和中美两国人民的友谊作些积极的工作。
    他说，诚然中美关系目前处于困难时期，他相信只要双方恪守和平共处五项原则和中美三个联合公报所确立的原则，中美关系是能够渡过目前困难，回到正常发展的道路上来的。
