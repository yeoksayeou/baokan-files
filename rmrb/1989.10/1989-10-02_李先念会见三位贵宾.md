Title: Li Xiannian Meets Three Distinguished Guests
Author: Zhou Cixu
Date: 1989-10-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 1st. (Reporter Zhou Cixu) Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Li Xiannian met with three distinguished guests this evening at the Tiananmen Gate Tower. They were the Deputy Chairman of the Soviet-China Friendship Association and former First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, I.V. Arkhipov, old friend of the Chinese people Huang Wenhuan, and Speaker of the National Assembly of Pakistan, Mairaj Khalid. The hosts and guests had a warm and friendly conversation.

	Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Chen Yun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Zhen, and guests from the German Democratic Republic had a friendly talk at the Tiananmen Gate Tower.

	Xinhua News Agency, Photographer Wang Jingde
