Title: Summit of Three Central African Countries Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-10-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency,

The summit of the heads of state of Cameroon, Congo, and Gabon concluded on the 9th in Yaoundé. The meeting discussed the worrying economic, monetary, and fiscal situations in Africa, particularly the foreign debt issues of the three countries.

### 中部非洲三国首脑会议闭幕

1989-10-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    中部非洲三国首脑会议闭幕
    喀麦隆、刚果和加蓬三国首脑会议九日在雅温得闭幕。会议讨论了非洲令人忧虑的经济、货币和财政形势，特别是三国的外债问题。
      （据新华社）
