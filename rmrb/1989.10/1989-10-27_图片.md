Title: A Path Up the Cold Mountain
Author: Li Yan
Date: 1989-10-27
Edition: 8

	The path winds up the cold mountain, where white clouds gather and houses stand;
	The carriage stops, enchanted by the maple forest in the evening;
	The frost-kissed leaves are redder than the flowers of February.
