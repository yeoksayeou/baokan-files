Title: New Syllabus for Adult College Entrance Exam Preparation
Author:
Date: 1989-10-12
Edition: 2

To ensure the quality of new students entering various adult higher education institutions nationwide, the State Education Commission has formulated a new "National Adult Higher Education Entrance Examination Syllabus" and a vocabulary list to accompany the syllabus for English, Russian, and Japanese. These materials will be published by the People's Education Press in mid-October.

The syllabus outlines the essential high school-level knowledge and skills required for new students entering adult higher education institutions. It serves as a guide for systematic review for adult examinees and as a basis for unified examination question setting.

The syllabus covers eight subjects: politics, Chinese language, mathematics, physics, chemistry, geography, history, and foreign languages (including general English, specialized English, general Russian, specialized Russian, general Japanese, and specialized Japanese).

### 成人高考复习有新大纲

1989-10-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    成人高考复习有新大纲
    据新华社北京10月11日电  为保证全国各类成人高等学校入学新生的质量，国家教育委员会制定了新的《全国各类成人高等学校招生复习考试大纲》及与大纲配套使用的《全国各类成人高等学校招生复习考试大纲英、俄、日语词汇表》。它们将于10月中旬由人民教育出版社出版。
    《大纲》规定了成人高等学校入学新生所必需具备的高中文化基础知识和能力要求。它既对成人考生起系统复习的指导作用，又作为统一考试命题的依据。
    《大纲》包括政治、语文、数学、物理、化学、地理、历史、外语（含公共英语、专业英语、公共俄语、专业俄语、公共日语、专业日语）8个科目。
