Title: Soviet Foreign Minister Visits Poland; Polish Prime Minister to Visit the Soviet Union
Author: Ma Yunliang
Date: 1989-10-26
Edition: 4

Soviet Foreign Minister Visits Poland

Polish Prime Minister to Visit the Soviet Union

	Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 24th - (Reporter: Ma Yunliang) Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze arrived in Warsaw by airplane today, beginning a two-day working visit to Poland. Polish Foreign Minister Stefan Skubiszewski welcomed his Soviet counterpart at the airport.

	According to reports, during his visit, Shevardnadze will exchange views with Polish President Wojciech Jaruzelski, Prime Minister Tadeusz Mazowiecki, and the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Mieczysław Rakowski, on bilateral relations. The issue of Mazowiecki's visit to the Soviet Union will also be discussed.

	Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 24th - Polish Prime Minister Tadeusz Mazowiecki will visit the Soviet Union on November 23rd at the invitation of the Soviet government.

	This was announced after Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze, who is currently visiting here, held two rounds of working-level talks with the Polish Foreign Minister and had a private meeting with Polish Prime Minister Tadeusz Mazowiecki.

	Shevardnadze said today to Polish reporters that the visit of the Polish Prime Minister to the Soviet Union is of great significance for strengthening relations and cooperation between the two countries.

	Sources here revealed that the talks between the two foreign ministers, especially the private meeting between the two foreign ministers before the talks, involved the "blank spots" in the relations between the two countries, that is, the historical issues left over from the past, including the Katyn massacre, the rehabilitation of victims of the Stalin era, and the issue of dual citizenship for Poles residing in the Soviet Union. Both sides expressed a desire to resolve these issues as soon as possible to improve relations between the two countries and promote friendship between the two peoples.

	Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze arrived in Warsaw on October 24th for a working visit to Poland.

### 苏联外长访问波兰  波兰总理将访问苏联
马云亮
1989-10-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏联外长访问波兰
　　波兰总理将访问苏联
    新华社华沙10月24日电  （记者马云亮）苏联外交部长谢瓦尔德纳泽今天乘飞机抵达华沙，开始对波兰进行为期两天的工作访问。波兰外长斯库比舍夫斯基到机场迎接这位苏联同行。
    据报道，在访问期间，谢瓦尔德纳泽将同波兰总统雅鲁泽尔斯基、总理马佐维耶茨基、波兰统一工人党中央第一书记拉科夫斯基等就双边关系问题交换意见。马佐维耶茨基总理访苏问题也是讨论的内容。
    新华社华沙10月24日电  波兰政府总理马佐维耶茨基将于11月23日应邀访问苏联。
    这是正在这里访问的苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽与波兰外长举行两轮工作性会谈和与波兰总理马佐维耶茨基单独会晤后宣布的。
    谢瓦尔德纳泽今天对波兰记者说，波兰政府总理访苏对加强苏波两国关系和合作“具有重大意义”。
    此间人士透露，两国外长会谈，特别是会谈前两国外长的单独会晤，涉及到波苏两国关系中的“空白点”，即两国历史上遗留的问题，其中包括卡廷事件、为斯大林时期受害者平反、迁居苏境波兰人的双重国籍问题等。双方表示尽快解决上述问题，以改善两国关系，增进两国人民的友谊。（附图片）
