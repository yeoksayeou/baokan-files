Title: Peruvian Congress Passes Resolution on China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1989-10-02
Edition: 3

Peruvian Congress Passes Resolution on China's National Day, Noting Establishment of New China as Major World Event

LIMA, Sept. 29 (Xinhua) -- The Peruvian Congress passed a resolution on Sept. 28 to mark the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The resolution stated that the establishment of the People's Republic of China is one of the most significant socio-political events in the world.

After extending greetings to China, the Peruvian Congress expressed its hope to strengthen friendly ties with China in various fields, including cultural exchanges and economic cooperation.

The resolution noted that the Peruvian people are familiar with the history of the Chinese people's struggle and the transformation and development of the world's most populous country over the past 40 years. The resolution further stated that China has achieved great goals in industry, food production, education, and cultural arts over the past 40 years.

According to a report from our correspondent in Mexico City, the Mexico-China Friendship Association published an article in the Mexican newspaper *El Dia* on Sept. 29 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The official Mexican news agency Notimex also broadcast a special report on Sept. 29.

### 秘鲁议会就我国庆通过一项动议指出  新中国的成立是世界重要事件

1989-10-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    秘鲁议会就我国庆通过一项动议指出
    新中国的成立是世界重要事件
    新华社利马9月29日电  秘鲁议会28日就中华人民共和国成立40周年通过一项动议。动议说，中华人民共和国的成立是世界上最重要的社会政治事件之一。
    秘鲁议会在动议中向中国致意后表示希望加强秘、中两国在文化交流和经济合作等各方面的友好联系。
    动议指出，秘鲁人民了解中国人民的斗争历史和近40年来这个世界人口最多的国家的变革和发展进程。动议接着说，中国40年来在工业、食品生产、教育和文化艺术等方面实现了自己的伟大目标。
    另据本社驻墨西哥城记者报道，墨西哥人民中国之友协会29日在墨西哥《日报》刊文庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年。墨西哥官方通讯社墨通社也在29日播出一篇专稿。
