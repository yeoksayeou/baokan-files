Title: A Massive Solar Flare Appears on the Sun's Surface
Author:
Date: 1989-10-22
Page: 3

Scientists from the U.S. National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration observed a massive solar flare on the sun's surface on the 19th. The strong magnetic storm it released could disrupt Earth's communication and power transmission systems over the next two days; the Northern Lights may also appear in the North Pole.

An official from the U.S. National Aeronautics and Space Administration stated that the radiation from the massive solar flare could affect satellites and probes in space. However, the U.S. "Atlantis" space shuttle, which is currently in orbit, and the "Galileo" Jupiter probe it launched are estimated to be safe, as they are equipped with devices that can withstand strong radiation.

### 太阳表面出现巨大的耀斑

1989-10-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　太阳表面出现巨大的耀斑
    美国国家海洋与大气管理局的科学家十九日观测到太阳表面出现巨大的耀斑。它喷发出的强磁暴，可能在近二天内使地球上的通讯系统和电力传输系统出现混乱；北极还可能出现极光。
    美国宇航局的一位官员说，太阳巨大耀斑发出的辐射可能会影响在太空运转的卫星和探测器，但是正在轨道上运行的美国“阿特兰蒂斯”号航天飞机，以及由它送入轨道的“伽利略”号木星探测器估计不会出现什么危险，因为它上面装有抗强辐射的装置。
