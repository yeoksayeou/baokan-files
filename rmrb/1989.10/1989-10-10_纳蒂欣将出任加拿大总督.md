Title: Natyhin to Assume the Position of Governor General of Canada
Author:
Date: 1989-10-10
Page: 3

The Prime Minister's Office of Canada announced on the 6th that Ramon John Natyhin will assume the position of Governor General of Canada in January next year, succeeding Jeanne Sauvé. Natyhin's appointment was recommended by Canadian Prime Minister Mulroney and approved by Queen Elizabeth II. His duties will primarily involve representing the Queen in Canada for a five-year term, performing ceremonial functions, and not interfering in the political and administrative affairs of the Canadian government. Natyhin is 55 years old and was born in Saskatchewan, Canada.

### 纳蒂欣将出任加拿大总督

1989-10-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    纳蒂欣将出任加拿大总督
    加拿大总理办公室六日宣布，拉蒙·约翰·纳蒂欣将于明年一月接替让娜·索维出任加拿大总督。纳蒂欣的任命是由加拿大总理马尔罗尼推荐、英国女王伊丽莎白二世批准的。他的职责主要是在五年的任期内，代表女王在加拿大行使礼仪职能，并不介入加拿大政府的政治和行政事务。纳蒂欣现年五十五岁。出生于加拿大萨斯喀彻温省。
