Title: China Successfully Produces Three-Coordinate Measuring Machines
Author:
Date: 1989-10-06
Edition: 2

A high-efficiency digital precision measuring device—the three-coordinate measuring machine—has achieved significant progress in domestic production, with the overall domestication rate reaching 66%.

The 303 Research Institute under the Aerospace Department introduced the mechanical manufacturing technology for the three-coordinate measuring machine from Italy in 1985. Since 1986, the institute has, through digestion and absorption, provided users with 36 such machines, saving the country over $4 million in foreign exchange.

### 我国已能产三座标测量机

1989-10-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    我国已能产三座标测量机
    本报讯　一种高效数字化精密测量设备——三座标测量机在实现国产化方面取得重大成果，整机国产化率已达到66％。
  航空航天部303研究所1985年引进意大利三座标测量机机械部分的专有制造技术。1986年以来，该所通过消化吸收，已向用户提供这种机器36台，为国家节约400余万美元的外汇支出。
