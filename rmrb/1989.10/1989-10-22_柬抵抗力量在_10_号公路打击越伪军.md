Title: Cambodian Resistance Forces Strike Vietnamese and Phnom Penh Regime Forces on National Route 10
Author:
Date: 1989-10-22
Edition: 3

Cambodian resistance forces recently destroyed three town strongholds guarded by Vietnamese and Phnom Penh regime forces along National Route 10 in Battambang Province, rendering part of the road unusable.

The broadcast said that on the night of October 14 to the morning of October 15, the resistance forces launched attacks from four directions on the enemy strongholds along National Route 10, including Sre Ambel Town, Pnong Tboung Town, Dey Krahom Town, and the road section between Dey Krahom and Pnong Tboung. After fierce fighting, the resistance forces killed 21 Vietnamese soldiers, wounded 37, and destroyed the Vietnamese and Phnom Penh regime forces' strongholds in Sre Ambel Town, the outer strongholds in Pnong Tboung Town, and the strongholds in Dey Krahom Town. They also laid mines along a 36-kilometer stretch of National Route 10 between Dey Krahom and Pnong Tboung, and damaged part of the road.

On October 14, the resistance forces attacked a battalion stronghold and three company strongholds guarded by Vietnamese and Phnom Penh regime forces in Samrong County, Kampong Speu Province. They annihilated 55 Vietnamese soldiers and damaged about 7 kilometers of National Route 6.

### 柬抵抗力量在10号公路打击越伪军

1989-10-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　柬抵抗力量在10号公路打击越伪军
    新华社北京10月21日电  据民主柬埔寨电台今天广播，柬埔寨抵抗力量最近在马德望省10号公路沿线摧毁了越南—金边军队驻守的3个镇的据点，使10号公路部分路段无法使用。
    广播说，10月14日夜间到15日上午，抵抗力量分四路向越南—金边军队驻守的斯楞镇、普农三伯镇、德楞镇和德楞镇到普农三伯镇之间的10号公路路段沿线的敌军据点发动了进攻。经过激战，抵抗力量打死越军21人，打伤37人，摧毁了越伪军驻守的斯楞镇据点、普农三伯镇的外围据点群和德楞镇据点群，在德楞到普农三伯之间10号公路36公里长的路段上埋上了地雷，并破坏了部分路段。
    10月14日，抵抗力量在磅同省山图克县袭击了越伪军驻守的一个营据点和三个连据点。歼灭越军55人，破坏6号公路约7公里长的路段。
