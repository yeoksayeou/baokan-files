Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-10-04
Edition: 2

	Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Ni Zhifu met with the delegation from the Hong Kong Federation of Trade Unions led by Chairman Li Zetian and the delegation from the General Union of Neighborhood Associations of Macao led by Director Tang Xingqian on the morning of October 1. The delegations from Hong Kong and Macao were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	The delegation from the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, led by Egon Krenz, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, Secretary of the Central Committee, and Vice Chairman of the State Council of the German Democratic Republic, departed from Beijing on the morning of October 2 after visiting China. Li Tieying, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and a State Councilor, and Zhu Liang, a member of the CPC Central Committee and Director of the International Liaison Department, saw them off at the airport. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 外事简讯

1989-10-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、中华全国总工会主席倪志福10月1日上午会见以香港工会联合会李泽添会长为团长的香港工会代表团和以澳门工会联合总会唐星樵理事长为团长的澳门工会代表团。港澳工会代表团是应全国总工会邀请前来参加中华人民共和国成立40周年庆祝活动的。
    ▲由德国统一社会党中央政治局委员、中央书记、民主德国国务委员会副主席埃贡·克伦茨率领的民主德国党和国家代表团在访问中国后，于2日上午离京回国。中共中央政治局委员、国务委员李铁映，中共中央委员、中联部部长朱良到机场送行。　（新华社）
