Title: Hong Kong Government and Xinhua Hong Kong Branch Decide to Resume Deportation of Illegal Entrants Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1989-10-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, October 23rd.

A spokesperson for the Xinhua Hong Kong Branch stated today: The Hong Kong Government and the Xinhua Hong Kong Branch have reached a consensus after discussions. It has been decided that the work of deporting illegal entrants will resume starting October 24th. Both sides reaffirmed their understanding reached in 1974, 1980, and 1982 regarding the handling of illegal entrants and holders of two-way permits, and will fulfill their respective obligations in the future.

### 港府和新华社香港分社决定  今起恢复遣返非法入境者

1989-10-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　港府和新华社香港分社决定
　　今起恢复遣返非法入境者
    新华社香港10月23日电　新华社香港分社发言人今日称：香港政府和新华社香港分社商量后达成一致意见，决定：于10月24日恢复遣返非法入境者的工作。双方重申了他们于1974年、1980年和1982年就处理非法入境者和双程证持有者问题上达成的谅解，并将在今后切实履行双方所承担的义务。
