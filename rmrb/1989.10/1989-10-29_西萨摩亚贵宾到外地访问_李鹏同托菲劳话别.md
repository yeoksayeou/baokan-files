Title: Western Samoa Dignitaries Depart for Visits Elsewhere
Author: Zhou Cixu, Yang Guangqiang
Date: 1989-10-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 28th.

Western Samoa dignitaries are departing for visits elsewhere. Premier Li Peng bids farewell to Tofilau.

Premier Li Peng and his wife, Zhu Lin, this morning went to the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse to bid a warm farewell to Western Samoa Prime Minister Tofilau Eti Alesana and his wife.

Tofilau said to Li Peng, "I am very happy to have realized this long-awaited visit. During my stay in Beijing, I have renewed friendships with Chinese leaders. The meetings and talks we have had will surely bear fruit in the years to come."

Li Peng said, "Prime Minister Tofilau's visit to China has been very successful, deepening the precious friendship between the peoples of China and Western Samoa. He and his wife will be visiting Guangzhou and Shenzhen, where they will see more clearly that China's policy of reform and opening up will not change and will be implemented even better."

After the farewell, the Western Samoa dignitaries, accompanied by the head of the Chinese government's escort team and the director of the State Sports Commission, Wu Shaozu, visited the Chairman Mao Memorial Hall.

### 西萨摩亚贵宾到外地访问  李鹏同托菲劳话别
周慈朴  杨国强
1989-10-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　西萨摩亚贵宾到外地访问
　　李鹏同托菲劳话别
    新华社北京10月28日电　（记者周慈朴、杨国强）国务院总理李鹏和夫人朱琳今天上午前往钓鱼台国宾馆，同西萨摩亚总理托菲劳·埃蒂·阿莱萨纳和夫人亲切话别。
    托菲劳总理对李鹏总理说，他对这次能实现早已期望的访问感到非常高兴。他这次在北京同中国领导人重叙了友谊，他们之间所进行的会见和会谈必将会在今后岁月里结出成果。
    李鹏说，托菲劳总理这次访华非常成功，加深了中国人民和西萨摩亚人民之间的珍贵友情。他说，托菲劳总理和夫人一行将去广州、深圳访问，在那里他们将更清楚地看到，中国的改革开放政策不会变，而且会贯彻执行得更好。
    话别后，西萨摩亚贵宾在中国政府陪同团团长、国家体委主任伍绍祖的陪同下，参观了毛主席纪念堂。
