Title: Untitled
Author: Yú Shā
Date: 1989-10-05
Page: 8

When seeds dream in the soil, the bud is her smile.
The sunlight colors her smile; the fine rain cleans her smile;
The gentle breeze plays music for her smile.
The bee is a visitor from the sky, using its sting as a pen, pouring a heart full of love into her smile. Only the earthworm silently follows the trajectory of her dream, writing a biography for her in pictographs.

A mother brings a small life into the world in an instant;
The bamboo shoot pierces the frozen soil in an instant; the fruit falls from the branch in an instant; dripping water eventually wears through the stone, also in an instant.
An instant is the silent, joyful explosion of a long period of gestation, accompanying the miraculous birth.
Without the instant, one loses the long period, the gestation, and the creation of the miracle.
When you see the swift-moving meteor, the fleeting smile of the ephemeral flower, and the sunset's last glance, what do you think of?

### 无题
于沙
1989-10-05
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    无题
    于沙
    种籽在泥里做梦的时候，花蕾是她的笑容。
    阳光为她的笑容着色；细雨为她的笑容洗尘；
    和风为她的笑容奏乐。
    蜜蜂是天外来客，用刺当笔，为她的笑容，注入一腔恋情。只有蚯蚓默默追踪她梦的轨迹，用象形文字，为她撰写着一部传记文学。
    母亲把小生命送到世上，在一瞬间；
    笋尖刺破冻土，在一瞬间；果子坠落枝头，在一瞬间；滴水终究穿石，也在一瞬间。
    一瞬间，是长时间孕育的沉默的欣然爆响，伴奏着奇迹的呱呱诞生。
    失去了瞬间，便失去了长时间，便失去了孕育，便失去了奇迹的创造。
    眼见流星迅跑、昙花浅笑、夕阳回眸时，你想到了什么？
