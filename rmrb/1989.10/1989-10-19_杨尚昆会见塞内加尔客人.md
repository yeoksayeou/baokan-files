Title: Yang Shangkun Meets with Senegalese Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 18th.

President Yang Shangkun met with a delegation from Senegal led by Minister of Culture and Central Political Bureau member of the Senegalese Socialist Party, Mustafa Ka, this morning at the Great Hall of the People.

Mustafa Ka conveyed greetings from the President of Senegal and expressed gratitude from the Senegalese government and people for China's political and economic support. Mustafa Ka said that although Senegal and China are far apart, cultural ties bind the two countries together. He hoped that cultural exchanges between Senegal and China would be further strengthened in the future.

Yang Shangkun said that since the establishment of diplomatic relations, the friendly relations between the two countries have continued to develop, which is the result of the joint efforts of the leaders and people of both countries. He said that the prospects for cultural cooperation between the two countries are broad, and cooperation is beneficial to both sides. He appreciated Senegal's stance on developing its own culture.

Yang Shangkun also expressed China's support for Senegal's policy of friendly neighborliness. He asked Mustafa Ka to convey his greetings to the President of Senegal. Deputy Minister of Culture He Jingzhi participated in the meeting.

### 杨尚昆会见塞内加尔客人

1989-10-19
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨尚昆会见塞内加尔客人
    新华社北京10月18日电　国家主席杨尚昆今天上午在人民大会堂会见了由文化部长、塞内加尔社会党中央政治局委员姆斯塔法·卡率领的塞内加尔政府文化代表团。
    姆斯塔法·卡向杨尚昆转达了塞内加尔总统的问候，并转告，塞总统感谢中国政府和人民对塞内加尔政府和人民在政治上和经济上的支持。姆斯塔法·卡说，塞中两国虽然距离遥远，但是文化的纽带把塞中两国联系在一起。他希望今后能进一步加强塞中文化交流。
    杨尚昆说，中塞建交以来两国友好关系不断发展，这是两国领导人和人民共同努力的结果。他说，两国文化合作的前景是广阔的，合作对双方都有好处。他赞赏塞内加尔的发展本国文化的主张。
    杨尚昆还表示中国支持塞内加尔的友好睦邻政策。他请姆斯塔法·卡向塞内加尔总统转达问候。文化部代部长贺敬之参加了会见。
