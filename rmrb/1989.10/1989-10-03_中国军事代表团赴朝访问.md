Title: Chinese Military Delegation Visits North Korea
Author:
Date: 1989-10-03
Edition: 2

At the invitation of the Ministry of People's Armed Forces of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, General Liu Huaqing, Vice Secretary of the Central Military Commission of the Communist Party of China, led a Chinese military delegation to visit North Korea for a friendly visit this afternoon.

The main members of the delegation include: Lieutenant General Han Huizhi, Deputy Chief of the General Staff, General Zhao Xianshun, Commander of the Lanzhou Military Region, Lieutenant General Ma Weizhi, Deputy Commander of the Jinan Military Region, and Major General Fu Jiaping, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense.

General Der Hao-tien, Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army, and Ambassador Chu Chang-jun of North Korea to China, among others, saw off the delegation at the station.

### 中国军事代表团赴朝访问

1989-10-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    中国军事代表团赴朝访问
    新华社北京10月2日电  应朝鲜人民武装力量部邀请，中共中央军委副秘书长刘华清上将率中国军事代表团今天下午离京赴朝鲜进行友好访问。
    代表团主要成员有：副总参谋长韩怀智中将、兰州军区司令员赵先顺中将、济南军区副司令员马伟志中将、国防部外事局副局长傅加平少将。
    中国人民解放军总参谋长迟浩田上将、朝鲜驻中国大使朱昌骏等到车站送行。
