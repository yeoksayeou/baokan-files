Title: National People's Congress Overseas Chinese Affairs Committee Sends Condolences to San Francisco Chinese Community
Author:
Date: 1989-10-26
Edition: 2

The eighth meeting of the National People's Congress Overseas Chinese Affairs Committee, currently being held in Beijing, has expressed deep concern over the strong earthquake that occurred in the San Francisco Bay Area on October 17. The committee extends sincere condolences to the Chinese community and all overseas Chinese in the affected region.

The letter of condolence states: "We were shocked to learn of the strong earthquake in the San Francisco Bay Area, which has caused varying degrees of damage to the lives and property of our overseas Chinese. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to the Chinese community and all overseas Chinese in the disaster-stricken area. We hope that our overseas Chinese will unite and cooperate, take good care of themselves, overcome difficulties, and return to normal life as soon as possible."

### 全国人大侨委函慰旧金山侨胞

1989-10-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　全国人大侨委函慰旧金山侨胞
    新华社北京10月25日电　正在北京召开的全国人民代表大会华侨委员会第八次会议上，全体委员对美国旧金山湾区10月17日发生强烈地震表示深切关注，对这一地区的华侨社团和全体侨胞表示诚挚的慰问。
    慰问信中说，惊悉旧金山湾区发生强烈地震，侨胞的生命财产遭受不同程度的损失，我们谨向地震灾区各华侨社团和全体侨胞表示亲切的慰问。希望侨胞们团结协作，珍摄起居，克服困难，早日恢复正常生活。
