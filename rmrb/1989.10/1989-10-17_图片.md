Title: Kang Le Dining Utensils Factory Strives for Quality to Survive
Author: Shǐ Bǎitián
Date: 1989-10-17
Edition: 5

The Kang Le Dining Utensils Factory of Dongjingcheng Forestry Bureau in Heilongjiang Province has been pursuing quality to ensure survival. Its products have been praised by the Japanese Sanryo Company as being of such high quality that they are exempt from inspection. The factory has consecutively completed its tasks with surplus for five years, and this year alone has earned over 600,000 US dollars in foreign exchange. The image shows workers at the factory inspecting and packing the products.
