Title: Beijing Military Region Football Team Wins the August First Cup
Author: Wang Dazhao
Date: 1989-10-03
Edition: 3

The annual August First Cup military football tournament concluded with the championship match between two top-tier national football teams just before National Day. Reporter Wang Dazhao reports: Beijing Military Region Team's number 9, Wang Xin, scored the only goal of the match in the second half by heading in a corner kick, defeating the Shenyang Military Region Team. With four consecutive victories, the Beijing Military Region Team became the three-time champion of this event. Some coaches hope to make this traditional competition even better, reviving the former glory of the military football teams.

### 北京军区足球队获八一杯
汪大昭
1989-10-03
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    北京军区足球队获八一杯
    本报讯　记者汪大昭报道：一年一度的全军八一杯足球赛，国庆前夕由两支全国甲级足球队进行了争夺冠军的决赛。北京军区队9号王鑫下半时利用发角球之机，顶入全场唯一的进球，击败了沈阳军区队。四战全胜的北京军区队于是成为这项赛事的三连冠。一些教练员希望能将这个传统性比赛办得更好，重振部队足球队昔日雄风。
