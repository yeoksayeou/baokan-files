Title: Informal Contacts Between Soviet and American Intelligence Agencies
Author:
Date: 1989-10-06
Edition: 3

According to the latest issue of the Moscow weekly "Arguments and Facts," the world's two largest intelligence agencies, the Soviet Union's State Security Committee (KGB) and the U.S. Central Intelligence Agency (CIA), have recently begun informal contacts.

The report states that the informal contacts between KGB and CIA personnel began on September 26 in Los Angeles and lasted for one week.

The report says that during this contact, the two sides mainly discussed anti-terrorism activities. Experts from both countries exchanged views on mutual intelligence sharing and cooperation in anti-terrorism activities. Both sides plan to hold another meeting in Moscow in January next year to continue discussing related issues.

### 苏美情报机构非正式接触

1989-10-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏美情报机构非正式接触
    据莫斯科最新一期《论据与事实》周报报道，世界上两个最大的情报机构苏联国家安全委员会（克格勃）和美国中央情报局最近开始了非正式接触。
    报道说，克格勃和美国中央情报局人员的非正式接触是九月二十六日在美国洛杉矶开始的，为期一周。
    报道说，在这次接触中，双方主要讨论了反恐怖活动问题。两国专家就互通侦察情报以及反恐怖活动中的合作途径等问题交换了意见。双方计划明年一月在莫斯科再次举行会晤，继续讨论有关问题。
