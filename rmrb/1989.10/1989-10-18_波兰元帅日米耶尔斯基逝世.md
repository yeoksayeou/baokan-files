Date: 1989-10-18
Edition: 3

Marshal of Poland, Mierzwinski, Passes Away

According to a report from the Polish News Agency, the last Marshal of Poland, M. Mierzwinski, passed away on the 15th in Warsaw at the age of 99.

Mierzwinski contributed significantly to Poland's independence and social liberation in his early military career. He was one of the founders of the Polish People's Army. During World War II, as the commander-in-chief of the Polish People's Army, he led the forces to cooperate with the Soviet Red Army in capturing Berlin. After the establishment of the Polish People's Republic, he served as the Minister of Defense and later became the honorary chairman of the Polish Alliance for Freedom and Democracy.

### 波兰元帅日米耶尔斯基逝世

1989-10-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    波兰元帅日米耶尔斯基逝世
    据波通社报道，波兰最后一位元帅米·日米耶尔斯基十五日在华沙逝世，享年九十九岁。
    日米耶尔斯基早年参军为波兰独立和社会解放作出过重大贡献。他是波兰人民军创始人之一。在第二次世界大战期间，他作为人民军总司令曾率领波兰人民军配合苏联红军攻克柏林。波兰人民共和国成立后，他出任国防部长，后改任波兰争取自由和民主战士联盟名誉主席。（据新华社）
