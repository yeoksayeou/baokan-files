Title: Tan Shaowen Appointed as Tianjin Municipal Party Secretary
Author:
Date: 1989-10-05
Page: 1

People's Daily, Tianjin, October 4th - Today, CPC Central Political Bureau Standing Committee member Li Ruihan conveyed in Tianjin the decision of the CPC Central Committee to appoint Tan Shaowen as the Secretary of the CPC Tianjin Municipal Committee and to add Zhang Lichang as the Deputy Secretary of the Municipal Committee.

### 谭绍文任天津市委书记

1989-10-05
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    谭绍文任天津市委书记
    本报天津10月4日电　中共中央政治局常委李瑞环今天在天津传达了中共中央决定，任命谭绍文为中共天津市委书记，增补张立昌为市委副书记。
