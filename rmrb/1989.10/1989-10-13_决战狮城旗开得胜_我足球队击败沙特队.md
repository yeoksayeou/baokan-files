Title: Victory in Singapore: China Defeats Saudi Arabia
Author: Zhang Qianli, Li Yongming
Date: 1989-10-13
Edition: 4

The 1990 World Cup football Asia zone qualifying final kicked off tonight at the Singapore National Stadium. China won the opening match, defeating the Asian champion Saudi Arabia 2-1. The Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the United Arab Emirates played to a 0-0 draw.

The China-Saudi match was intense. China, after conceding a goal, fought back with short passes, flank attacks, and midfield breakthroughs. Number 17, Mai Chao, scored two goals in 11 minutes, securing the victory for China. In the 62nd minute, China was awarded a penalty, which Mai Chao converted. Ten minutes later, Mai Chao broke through the left side and scored the winning goal in the opponent's penalty area.

Saudi Arabia's goal came in the first half when number 8, Y. Al-Dorlon, scored from a penalty.

In tonight's first match, North Korea and the UAE faced off for the first time in history. North Korea had the upper hand in terms of offense but failed to capitalize on their opportunities.

### 决战狮城旗开得胜  我足球队击败沙特队
张千里  李永明
1989-10-13
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    决战狮城旗开得胜
    我足球队击败沙特队
    新华社新加坡10月12日电（记者张千里、李永明）1990年世界杯足球赛亚洲区预选决赛今晚在新加坡国家体育场揭开战幕，中国队旗开得胜以2∶1击败亚洲冠军沙特阿拉伯队，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国队与阿拉伯联合酋长国队以0∶0踢平。
    中沙之战十分紧张，中国队在先失一球的情况下，以三两个人的短传配合边路进攻与中路突破，打得很坚决。17号麦超在11分钟内两次攻破对方球门，为中国队获胜立下丰功。比赛进行到第62分钟时，中国队获点球机会，由麦超射入，10分钟后，麦超又从左路突破在对方罚球区内攻入致胜一球。
    沙特阿拉伯队踢进的一球是上半时利用点球机会，由8号约·阿尔多隆利射入的。
    在今晚进行的首场比赛中，朝鲜队与阿联酋队进行了历史上的第一次交锋。朝鲜队在攻势上略占优势，但得势不得分。
