Foreign Affairs Brief

	▲ State Councilor and Minister of the State Education Commission Li Tieming held a reception and banquet for Professor Chen Xingshen of the University of California, Berkeley, and Director of the Nankai Mathematical Research Institute, and his wife Zheng Shining, at the Great Hall of the People on the evening of the 6th. Li had a cordial conversation with them. Professor Chen Xingshen is visiting China for a week at the invitation of the State Education Commission.

	▲ Romanian Ambassador to China, Miculescu, held a banquet at the embassy on the evening of the 6th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Romania. Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin and other relevant department heads attended the banquet.

	▲ The Democratic German Film Week opened in Beijing on the 6th. During the film week, five excellent films from the Democratic German Republic will be screened. This film week is organized according to the 1988-1989 cultural exchange plan between China and the Democratic German Republic. The film week will also be held in Shanghai and Shenyang.

	▲ Vice Premier Wu Xueqian met with a delegation led by Pan Wan Namiti, Director of the Thai International Research Center, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse in the afternoon of the 7th.

	▲ Vice Premier Wu Xueqian met with a delegation led by Mrs. Menon, President of the Indian Foreign Service Association, in Beijing in the afternoon of the 7th.

	▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Lei Jieqiong, met with a delegation led by Acting Vice President Masakazu Hayakawa of the Japan-China Youth Exchange Association at the Great Hall of the People in the afternoon of the 7th.

### 外事简讯

1989-10-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：外事简讯

    外事简讯
    ▲国务委员兼国家教委主任李铁映6日晚上在人民大会堂会见并宴请美国伯克利加州大学教授、南开数学研究所所长陈省身和夫人郑士宁，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。陈省身教授应国家教委邀请来华访问一周。
    ▲罗马尼亚驻华大使米库列斯库6日晚在大使馆举行宴会，庆祝中罗建交40周年。应邀出席宴会的有对外经济贸易部部长郑拓彬等有关部门负责人。
    ▲民主德国电影周6日在京开幕。电影周期间，将有5部民主德国优秀影片上映。这次电影周是根据中国与民主德国1988—1989年文化交流计划而举办的。电影周还将在上海和沈阳举办。
    ▲国务院副总理吴学谦7日下午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了泰国国际研究中心主任潘·万纳米提一行。
    ▲国务院副总理吴学谦7日下午在北京会见了由印度外交官协会会长梅农夫人率领的访华团。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长雷洁琼7日下午在人民大会堂会见由代理副会长早川正一率领的日中青年研修协会访华团。
                                          （新华社）
