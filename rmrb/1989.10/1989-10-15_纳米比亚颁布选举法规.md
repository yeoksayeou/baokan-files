Title: Namibia Enacts Election Regulations
Author:
Date: 1989-10-15
Edition: 3

After three months of arduous discussion and revision of the flawed election law draft proposed by South Africa, the election regulations guiding Namibia's independence elections in November were officially promulgated here today.

The legislation sets November 7 as the voting day for the parliamentary elections under United Nations supervision. The regulations stipulate that each registered political party must nominate at least 24 candidates for the parliament by October 24.

To ensure the elections are conducted freely and fairly, the regulations stipulate the protection of voting secrecy, the timely counting of ballots, and the participation of all political parties without intimidation.

### 纳米比亚颁布选举法规

1989-10-15
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    纳米比亚颁布选举法规
    据新华社温得和克10月13日电　在对南非提出的漏洞百出的选举法草案进行3个月艰苦讨论和修订后，一部指导纳米比亚11月独立大选的选举法规今天在这里正式颁布。
    这项立法规定11月7日为联合国监督下的议会选举投票日。法规要求每个登记注册的政党在10月24日前至少提出24名议会候选人。
    为保证选举能自由、公正地进行，法规规定，保障投票保密、及时清点选票以及所有的政党参加选举不受到恐吓。
