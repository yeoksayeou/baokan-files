Title: Beijing Railway Bureau Achieves 1000 Days of Safe Operation
Author: Zhang Yu Fang, Deng Zhen Dong
Date: 1989-10-04
Page: 2

    Beijing Railway Bureau Achieves 1000 Days of Safe Operation
    According to a report, by 18:00 on September 29, the Beijing Railway Bureau had achieved its first 1000 days of safe operation in its 40-year history. Since the implementation of the railway responsibility system, the bureau has adopted target management and strengthened fundamental work to ensure safe transportation. Since 1987, the entire bureau has prevented 9840 various types of operational accidents, safely transporting 165 million passengers and 122 million tons of cargo.

### 北京铁路分局安全行车千天
张玉芳　邓振东
1989-10-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    北京铁路分局安全行车千天
    本报讯　截至9月29日18时，北京铁路分局实现了建局40年来的首次安全行车1000天。铁路大包干后，这个分局实行目标管理，强化基础工作，确保安全运输。1987年以来，全分局共防止各类行车事故9840件，安全发送旅客1.65亿人、货物1.22亿吨。　（张玉芳　邓振东）
