Title: Largest Telescope in the Far East Completed
Author: Yang Zhaobo
Date: 1989-10-24
Edition: 2

China's research in astrophysics has taken a significant step forward. The largest telescope in the Far East, the 2.16-meter telescope at the Beijing Astronomical Observatory, has been successfully developed. Experts say that once this telescope is put into use, it will lead to many important astronomical discoveries.

The telescope stands on the southern slope of the main peak of the Yanshan Mountains, north of the Great Wall, at an altitude of 960 meters. Experts explain that the telescope includes optical, mechanical, drive, automatic control, starlight detection equipment, and an observation room. Its primary mirror is a 2.2-meter diameter, 30-centimeter thick, 3-ton optical glass, polished to a high degree of precision. The large aperture of the mirror provides strong light-gathering power, enabling it to observe very faint celestial bodies—down to the 25th magnitude, equivalent to seeing the light of a match burning 20,000 kilometers away.

This telescope is a symbol of China's self-reliance in developing large precision equipment. It was jointly developed by the Beijing Astronomical Observatory, the Nanjing Astronomical Instruments Factory, the Institute of Automation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and other units over a period of 15 years.

### 远东最大天文望远镜制成  我国自行研制直径两米多
杨兆波
1989-10-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　远东最大天文望远镜制成
　　我国自行研制直径两米多
    据新华社北京10月23日电  （记者杨兆波）我国天体物理研究又迈出重要一步。远东地区最大的北京天文台2.16米望远镜研制成功。专家们说，这台望远镜投入使用后，将会导致许多比较重要的天文发现。
    这台望远镜耸立在燕山主峰南麓、长城北侧的连营寨山头，海拔960米。专家介绍，这台望远镜包括光学、机械、驱动、自控、星光探测装置、观测室等部分。其主镜为一个直径2.2米、厚30厘米、重3吨的光学玻璃研磨而成，巨大的镜面口径，聚光力极强，因而可以观测到极暗的星体——其最暗程度为25等，相当于可以看到两万公里外一根火柴燃烧的亮光。
    这台望远镜是我国自力更生研制大型精密设备的标志。它是由北京天文台、南京天文仪器厂、中国科学院自动化研究所等单位历时15年联合攻关协作研制成功的。
