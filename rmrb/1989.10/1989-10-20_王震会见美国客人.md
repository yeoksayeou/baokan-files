Title: Wang Zhen Meets with American Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-10-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 19th.

Wang Zhen, Vice Chairman of the State and Honorary Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, met with Dr. Joseph Chubb, President of the American International Security Council, this morning in the Great Hall of the People.

Wang Zhen, on behalf of Chairman Yang Shangkun and himself, warmly welcomed Dr. Chubb's visit to China. He praised Chubb for his contributions to world peace and security.

Chubb stated that China's prosperity and development play an important role in the peace and development of the continents and the world.

Wang Zhen said that China's socialist modernization construction requires a lasting international peaceful environment and a stable and united domestic environment. He emphasized that China will continue to implement the policy of opening up to the outside world.

The International Security Council is a civilian institution in the United States that engages in research on international issues. Dr. Chubb arrived in Beijing on the 17th of this month at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries. He exchanged views with relevant Chinese departments and academic institutions on international issues of common concern.

### 王震会见美国客人

1989-10-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    王震会见美国客人
    新华社北京10月19日电  国家副主席、中国国际友好联络会名誉会长王震今天上午在人民大会堂会见了美国国际安全理事会会长约瑟夫·丘巴博士。
    王震代表杨尚昆主席和他本人热烈欢迎丘巴博士来华访问。他赞扬丘巴为世界和平与安全事业作出了贡献。
    丘巴说，中国的繁荣和发展对五洲乃至世界的和平与发展起着重要作用。
    王震说，中国进行社会主义现代化建设，需要一个持久的国际和平环境和安定团结的国内环境。他强调，中国将继续实行对外开放政策。
    国际安全理事会是美国从事国际问题研究的民间机构。丘巴博士是应中国国际友好联络会的邀请于本月17日来京访问的。他同中国有关部门和学术单位就共同关心的国际问题交换了意见。
