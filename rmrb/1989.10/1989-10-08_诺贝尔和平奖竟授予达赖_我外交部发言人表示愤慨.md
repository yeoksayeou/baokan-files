Title: Nobel Peace Prize Awarded to the Dalai Lama, Chinese Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Expresses Indignation
Author:
Date: 1989-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 7th.

The spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs stated today that China deeply regrets and is indignant about the decision by the Nobel Peace Prize Committee to award the 1989 Nobel Peace Prize to the Dalai Lama.

He said: Tibet is an inseparable part of China's territory. The Tibet issue is purely China's internal affair, and no foreign government, organization, or individual has the right to interfere. The Norwegian Nobel Peace Prize Committee's decision to award the Nobel Peace Prize to the Dalai Lama is a blatant support for the Dalai Lama and Tibetan separatists' activities to undermine national unity and split the country, a gross interference in China's internal affairs, and a serious affront to the feelings of the Chinese people. "We express deep regret and indignation over this."

The spokesperson added that it must be pointed out that the Nobel Peace Prize Committee's erroneous decision also directly contradicts the committee's purpose of awarding the prize for efforts and contributions to "friendship and harmony between nations." The spokesperson made these remarks in response to a journalist's question about China's comments on the Nobel Peace Prize Committee awarding the 1989 Nobel Peace Prize to the Dalai Lama.

### 诺贝尔和平奖竟授予达赖  我外交部发言人表示愤慨

1989-10-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    诺贝尔和平奖竟授予达赖  我外交部发言人表示愤慨
    新华社北京10月7日电　外交部发言人今天在这里说，中国对诺贝尔和平奖委员会授予达赖1989年度诺贝尔和平奖表示极大遗憾和愤慨。
    他说：西藏是中国领土不可分割的一部分。西藏问题纯属中国的内政，任何外国政府、组织或个人都无权干涉。挪威诺贝尔和平奖委员会决定将诺贝尔和平奖授予达赖，这是对达赖和西藏分裂主义分子破坏民族团结、分裂祖国活动的公然支持，是对中国内政的粗暴干涉，严重伤害了中国人民的感情。“对此，我们表示极大遗憾和愤慨。”
    发言人说，还必须指出，诺贝尔和平奖委员会这一错误决定也是直接背离了该委员会关于为“民族间的和睦亲善”作出努力和贡献的颁奖宗旨的。发言人是在回答记者问及中国对诺贝尔和平奖委员会授予达赖喇嘛1989年度诺贝尔和平奖一事有何评论时说这番话的。
