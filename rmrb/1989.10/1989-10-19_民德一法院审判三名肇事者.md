Title: A Court in the GDR Judges Three Rioters
Author:
Date: 1989-10-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

A court in Dresden, the German Democratic Republic, tried three main rioters involved in the disturbances on October 4th and 5th. The court sentenced Schmidt to three years and six months, Glick to four years and six months, and Hesk to three years and eight months in prison. Each was also fined 1,000 Marks.

The three rioters led the destruction at Dresden's main railway station during the disturbances on October 4th and 5th. They smashed all the doors and windows on the ground floor, ten ticket vending machines, and all the traffic signal facilities. A dining car worth over 30,000 Marks was completely destroyed, causing direct economic losses of approximately 170,000 Marks.

### 民德一法院审判三名肇事者

1989-10-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    民德一法院审判三名肇事者
    民主德国德累斯顿地方法院十三日对十月四日和五日在德累斯顿闹事的三名主要肇事者进行审理，并分别判处施密特、格里克和赫斯克三年零六个月、四年零六个月和三年零八个月的徒刑，并课以每人一千马克的罚金。
    这三名肇事者在本月四日和五日德累斯顿总火车站的闹事活动中，带头砸坏了该车站底层楼的所有门窗、十个自动售票机和所有的交通信号设施。一辆价值三万多马克的餐车也完全被毁，造成直接经济损失约十七万多马克。（据新华社）
