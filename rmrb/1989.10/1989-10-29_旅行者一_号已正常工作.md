Title: Voyager 1 Resumes Normal Operation
Author:
Date: 1989-10-29
Edition: 3

Voyager 1, the U.S. automatic interstellar probe that had stopped sending information to Earth, has resumed normal operation.

According to the Associated Press, American ground control personnel reported that Voyager 1 suddenly stopped sending information to Earth at 9 AM on the 23rd. Engineers sent commands to adjust the telemetry modulator and restart the probe at 3 PM on the 24th. Communication with Earth was restored by the morning of the 25th. (Xinhua News Agency)

### “旅行者一”号已正常工作

1989-10-29
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　“旅行者一”号已正常工作
    据美联社报道，中断向地面传送信息的美国“旅行者1”号自动星际探测器，现已恢复正常工作。
    美国地面控制人员说，“旅行者1”号于二十三日上午九点突然中断向地面传送信息，工程技术人员于二十四日下午三点向探测器发出调整遥测调节器和令其重新工作的指令，直到二十五日早晨才恢复同地面的联系。（据新华社）
