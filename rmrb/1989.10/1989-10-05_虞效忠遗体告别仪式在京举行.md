Title: Funeral of Yu Xiaozhong Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-10-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 5th.

The funeral of Yu Xiaozhong, a member of the 7th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and a Standing Committee Member of the 5th Central Consultative Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, was held on September 27th in the Hall of the Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery in Beijing. Yu passed away on September 15th in Beijing due to illness, at the age of 72.

Sun Qimen, Wang Renzhong, Sun Xiaocun, and other dignitaries attended the funeral and sent wreaths.

Attendees also included some members of the National Committee of the CPPCC, members and alternate members of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association in Beijing, members of the Central Consultative Committee, staff of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, executive members of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce in Beijing, and leaders from other democratic parties' central committees, as well as friends of Yu Xiaozhong.

Rong Yiren, Chen Muxia, Wang Hanbin, Yan Mingfu, Yang Jingren, Hu Zihang, Wang Guangying, and Liu Jingji sent wreaths. The National Committee of the CPPCC, the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, the central committees of various democratic parties, and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce also sent wreaths.

Yu Xiaozhong was born in Wujin County, Jiangsu Province, in 1917. He joined the China Democratic National Construction Association in 1949. After the founding of the People's Republic of China, Yu Xiaozhong actively devoted himself to the socialist cause of the motherland, serving as the Director of the Financial Department of the Ministry of Light Industry, among other positions. In 1958, he was transferred to the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, where he served as the Editor-in-Chief of the "New Commerce" magazine, Director of the Propaganda Department, and Deputy Secretary-General, among other roles. He was a member of the 2nd, 3rd, and 4th Central Committees of the China Democratic National Construction Association, a Standing Committee Member of the 3rd and 4th Central Committees, and a Standing Committee Member of the 5th Central Consultative Committee. He was also a member of the 4th, 5th, 6th, and 7th National Committees of the CPPCC.

### 虞效忠遗体告别仪式在京举行

1989-10-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    虞效忠遗体告别仪式在京举行
    新华社北京电　第七届全国政协委员、中国民主建国会第五届中央咨议委员会常务委员虞效忠同志，因病于9月15日在北京逝世，终年72岁。向虞效忠同志遗体告别仪式9月27日下午在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。孙起孟、王任重、孙晓村等参加遗体告别仪式并送了花圈。
    参加遗体告别仪式的还有：部分全国政协委员，民建在京中央委员、候补中央委员、中央咨议委员，民建中央机关工作人员，全国工商联在京执行委员，各民主党派中央有关领导人以及虞效忠同志的生前友好。
    荣毅仁、陈慕华、王汉斌、阎明复、杨静仁、胡子昂、王光英、刘靖基等送了花圈。全国政协、中共中央统战部、各民主党派中央、全国工商联等有关单位也送了花圈。
    虞效忠同志1917年生于江苏省武进县，1949年参加中国民主建国会。新中国成立后，虞效忠同志积极投身于祖国的社会主义事业，曾任轻工业部财务司司长等职。1958年调中国民主建国会中央工作，曾任《新工商》杂志总编辑、宣传部部长、副秘书长等职。他是民建第二、三、四届中央委员，第三、四届中央常务委员，第五届中央咨议委员会常务委员，第四、五、六、七届全国政协委员。
