Title: Norodom Sihanouk Leaves Beijing for North Korea
Author:
Date: 1989-10-05
Edition: 2

Democratic Kampuchea Chairman Norodom Sihanouk and his wife left Beijing by special plane this morning for the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Deputy Foreign Minister Liu Shucai and others went to the airport to see them off.

### 西哈努克离京赴朝鲜

1989-10-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    西哈努克离京赴朝鲜
    新华社北京10月4日电　民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人今天上午乘专机离开北京前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国。外交部副部长刘述卿等前往机场送行。
