Title: New Tournament Added to the Chess Scene: The National Youth Chess Championship Begins
Author:
Date: 1989-10-24
Edition: 4

The first national youth chess championship began today in Beijing. Young male and female chess players under the age of 20 from 13 provinces and cities will compete in 13 rounds to determine China's first youth national champion.

Currently, China's chess scene is rapidly approaching the world's highest level. Establishing a youth championship will help cultivate more high-level players and make China's chess tournaments more complete. Prior to this, national competitions for both adults and juniors have already been formally established.

### 棋坛又添新赛事  全国青年国际象棋赛开始

1989-10-24
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　棋坛又添新赛事全国青年国际象棋赛开始
    本报北京10月23日讯　首届全国青年国际象棋冠军赛今天在北京开幕。来自13个省市的20岁以下的男、女棋手，将进行13轮的角逐，以产生中国第一个青年全国冠军。
    我国的国际象棋目前正在迅速接近世界最高水平，设立青年冠军赛将有助于培养更多高水平棋手，并且使我国的国际象棋赛事正式配套。在此之前，已经正式设立了成年和少年的全国比赛。
