Chairman Yang Appoints and Dismisses Ambassadors Abroad

New China News Agency, Beijing, October 25th - The Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Yang Shangkun, in accordance with the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, has appointed and dismissed the following ambassadors abroad:

1. Appoint Guo Maoxuan (female) as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Socialist Republic of Albania. Relieve Fan Chengzuo of his duties as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Socialist Republic of Albania.

2. Appoint Guan Zihuai as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China concurrently to the State of Bahrain.

### 杨主席任免驻外大使

1989-10-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　杨主席任免驻外大使
    新华社北京10月25日电　中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会的决定任免下列驻外大使：
    一、任命顾懋萱（女）为中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚社会主义人民共和国特命全权大使。免去范承祚的中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚社会主义人民共和国特命全权大使职务。
    二、任命管子怀为中华人民共和国兼驻巴林国特命全权大使。
