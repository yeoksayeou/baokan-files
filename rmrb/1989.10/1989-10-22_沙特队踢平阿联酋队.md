Title: Saudi Arabia Draws with the United Arab Emirates
Author:
Date: 1989-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, October 21st.

In the final round of the third round of the Asian qualifying matches for the World Cup, the Saudi Arabia team and the United Arab Emirates team played to a 0-0 draw. Currently, the standings are as follows: South Korea 5 points, United Arab Emirates 4 points, North Korea 3 points, China, Qatar, and Saudi Arabia each with 2 points.

### 沙特队踢平阿联酋队

1989-10-22
第4版(综合)
专栏：

　　沙特队踢平阿联酋队
    新华社新加坡10月21日电  世界杯足球赛亚洲区预选赛决赛第三轮最后一场比赛，沙特队与阿联酋队0∶0战平。目前各队积分是：南朝鲜队5分，阿联酋队4分，朝鲜队3分，中国队、卡塔尔队和沙特队同积两分。
