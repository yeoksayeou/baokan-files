Title: Chairman Nan Hanchen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Hosts a Banquet for Delegation from the Japan National Federation of Commerce and Industry Organizations
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet this evening in honor of the delegation from the Japan National Federation of Commerce and Industry Organizations, headed by Teizaburo Kawano. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also invited to attend the banquet.

Prior to the banquet, Nan Hanchen received all members of the delegation and engaged in cordial conversations with the guests.

### 中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸　宴请日本全国商工团体联合会代表团

1960-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸
　　宴请日本全国商工团体联合会代表团
    新华社28日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今晚设宴招待以河野贞三郎为首的日本全国商工团体联合会代表团。在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会前，南汉宸接见了代表团全体人员，并且和客人们进行了亲切交谈。
