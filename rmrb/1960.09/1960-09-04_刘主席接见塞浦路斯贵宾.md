Title: Chairman Liu Receives Cypriot Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-09-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd September. Chairman Liu Shaoqi received this afternoon all the members of the Cyprus Farmers' Association delegation to China, headed by Kyriakos Syrtas, and had a friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Liu Changsheng, Chen Yu and others. (Attached picture)
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi meets with the Cyprus Farmers' Association delegation to China, headed by Kyriakos Syrtas (third from right), and had a cordial conversation with them.

### 刘主席接见塞浦路斯贵宾

1960-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见塞浦路斯贵宾
    新华社3日讯　刘少奇主席在今天下午接见了以凯里阿柯斯·西塔斯为首的塞浦路斯农民协会访华代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有刘长胜、陈宇等人。（附图片）
    刘少奇主席接见以团长凯里阿柯斯·西塔斯（右三）为首的塞浦路斯农民协会访华代表团，和他们进行了亲切的谈话　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
