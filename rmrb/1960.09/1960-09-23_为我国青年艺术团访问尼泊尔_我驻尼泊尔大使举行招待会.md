Title: Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Holds Reception for Visit of Chinese Youth Art Troupe to Nepal
Author:
Date: 1960-09-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, September 22nd: Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Zhang Shijie, held a reception at the Sital Niwas Palace in Kathmandu at 7:00 PM on the 21st to welcome the visiting Chinese Youth Art Troupe to Nepal.

Among those attending the reception were Nepalese Deputy Prime Minister Subarna Shamsher, Education Minister Choudhary, Health Minister Gautam, Speaker of the Lower House Upadhyaya, and other high-ranking officials. Foreign diplomatic envoys to Nepal also attended the reception. Also in attendance were the Chairman and Vice-Chairman of the Kathmandu Municipal Council, the Chairman and Vice-Chairman of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, the Secretary of the Nepal-China Cultural Association, leaders of Nepalese youth organizations, famous poets, writers, artists, and other prominent figures from all walks of life in Nepal, totaling over two hundred people.

The reception was held in a friendly and warm atmosphere. After the reception, the artists of the Chinese Youth Art Troupe gave a performance.

### 为我国青年艺术团访问尼泊尔　我驻尼泊尔大使举行招待会

1960-09-23
第6版()
专栏：

　  为我国青年艺术团访问尼泊尔
　　我驻尼泊尔大使举行招待会
    新华社22日讯　加德满都消息：中国驻尼泊尔大使张世杰21日下午七时在加德满都的西塔尔尼瓦斯宫举行招待会，招待在尼泊尔访问的中国青年艺术团。
    出席招待会的有尼泊尔副首相苏巴纳·沙姆谢尔，教育大臣乔杜里，卫生大臣高塔姆，下议院议长乌帕德亚亚和其它高级官员。外国驻尼泊尔的外交使节也出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有加德满都市政委员会主席和副主席，尼泊尔—中国友好协会会长和副会长，尼泊尔—中国文化协会秘书，尼泊尔青年团体的负责人，著名诗人、作家、艺术家和尼泊尔其它各界著名人士共两百多人。
    招待会是在友好和热情的气氛中进行的。招待会结束后，中国青年艺术团的艺术家们举行了演出。
