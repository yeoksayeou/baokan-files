Title: Marshal Lin Biao Meets and Hosts Banquet for General Ne Win
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, Marshal Lin Biao, and his wife met and hosted a banquet tonight in honor of General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, and his wife.

　　The meeting and the banquet were conducted in a very friendly atmosphere. Attending the meeting and banquet were Brigadier Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma and the Burmese Chief Representative of the Joint Sino-Burmese Boundary Committee; the Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyao Winn, and his wife; as well as Lieutenant Colonel Lun and Captain Du Su Lai, who accompanied General Ne Win.

　　Present as hosts were General Luo Ruiqing, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and his wife; General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Geng Biao; Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense; Major General Wang Xinggang, Deputy Director; and Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Burma.

　　During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted each other.

### 林彪元帅会见并设便宴招待奈温将军

1960-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　林彪元帅会见并设便宴招待奈温将军
　　新华社29日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅和夫人今天晚上会见并设便宴招待缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人。
　　会见和便宴均在十分友好的气氛中进行。参加会见和便宴的有：缅甸联邦国防军副总参谋长、中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表昂季准将；缅甸驻我国大使叫温和夫人，以及奈温将军的随行人员伦中校和杜素莱上尉。
　　出席作陪的，有中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将和夫人，总政治部副主任萧华上将，外交部副部长耿飚，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将、副主任王兴纲少将，我国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定大校。
　　席间，宾主频频互相祝酒。
