Title: Chairman of the Australia-China Association Melbourne Branch Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 16th: Mr. Sandy, Chairman of the Australia-China Association Melbourne Branch, and his wife, who are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 15th.

Wen Pengjiu, Jiang Hongji, and other representatives from the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries were at the airport to welcome them.

### 澳中协会墨尔本分会主席到京

1960-09-17
第6版()
专栏：

    澳中协会墨尔本分会主席到京
    新华社16日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的澳中协会墨尔本分会主席桑迪和他的夫人，在15日乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会代表温朋久、姜鸿基等。
