Title: Chairman Liu Presents a Batch of Gifts to Nkrumah
Author:
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Accra, 6th. Huang Hua, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Ghana, presented a batch of gifts from Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, to Kwame Nkrumah, President of the Republic of Ghana, at the Presidential Palace this morning. President Nkrumah personally accepted the gifts and expressed his heartfelt gratitude to Chairman Liu Shaoqi. Those present at the presentation included Ghanaian Minister of Agriculture and Minister of State in charge of National Assembly Affairs, Kojo Botsio, Minister of Foreign Affairs Ako Adjei, and General Secretary of the Convention People's Party, Paa Willie Adamfio. Wang Yimu, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Ghana, was also present.

### 刘主席赠给恩克鲁玛一批礼物

1960-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    刘主席赠给恩克鲁玛一批礼物
    新华社阿克拉6日电　中华人民共和国驻加纳共和国大使黄华今天上午在总统府把中华人民共和国主席刘少奇的赠礼递交加纳共和国总统恩克鲁玛。恩克鲁玛总统亲自接受了礼物，并且对刘少奇主席表示衷心的谢意。赠礼时，在场者有加纳农业部长和负责国民议会事务国务部长科佐·博齐约，外交部长阿科·阿杰伊，人民大会党总书记培瓦·阿达马费奥。在场者还有中国驻加纳大使馆参赞王一木。
