Title: Our Aeromodelling Team to Hungary
Author:
Date: 1960-09-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The Chinese aeromodelling team, consisting of eight members, left Beijing by plane today for a visit to Hungary.

　　They will first attend the World Radio Control Aeromodelling Competition held there from September 8th to 12th, and then hold friendly matches with Hungarian athletes.

### 我航空模型运动队赴匈

1960-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    我航空模型运动队赴匈
    据新华社6日讯　中国航空模型运动队一行八人，今天乘飞机离北京去匈牙利访问。
    他们将先参观9月8日到12日在那里举行的世界线操纵航空模型竞赛，然后同匈牙利选手举行友谊比赛。
