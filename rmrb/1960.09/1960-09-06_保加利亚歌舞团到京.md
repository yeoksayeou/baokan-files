Title: Bulgarian Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-06
Page: 4

    Bulgarian Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, September 5th—According to the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the Bulgarian National Folk Song and Dance Troupe, a group of one hundred people, led by the head of the troupe, Secretary-General of the Bulgarian Composers Association, and People's Actor Filip Kutev, arrived in Beijing at noon today.
    More than one hundred people came to the station to welcome them, including Zhang Zhixiang, Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Qu Wu, Deputy Director, Xia Yan, Deputy Minister of Culture, and Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association. Stoyev, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Bulgarian Embassy in China, and other diplomatic officials also came to the station to welcome them.

### 保加利亚歌舞团到京

1960-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚歌舞团到京
    新华社5日讯　根据中保文化合作协定1960年执行计划，保加利亚国家民间歌舞团一行一百人，在团长、保加利亚作曲家协会总书记、人民演员菲利浦·库捷夫率领下，于今日中午抵达北京。
    到车站欢迎的，有对外文委代理主任张致祥、副主任屈武，文化部副部长夏衍，中国音乐家协会主席吕骥等一百多人。保加利亚驻华使馆临时代办斯托耶夫和其他外交官员也到车站欢迎。
