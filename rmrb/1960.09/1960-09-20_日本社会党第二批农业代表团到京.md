Title: Japanese Socialist Party's Second Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-20
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, September 19th. The second agricultural delegation of the Japanese Socialist Party, consisting of fourteen members and led by Shohara Tatsu, Chief of the General Affairs Bureau of the Socialist Party, arrived in Beijing by train today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the Chinese Society of Agronomy.
    Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Xie Nanguang, Director, Xiao Xiangqian, Deputy Secretary-General, Guan Wenqi, Director of the Chinese Society of Agronomy, and others were at the station to welcome them.
    Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is currently in Beijing, also came to the station to welcome them.

### 日本社会党第二批农业代表团到京

1960-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    日本社会党第二批农业代表团到京
    新华社19日讯　应中国人民外交学会和中国农学会邀请来我国访问的日本社会党第二批农业代表团一行十四人，在团长、社会党庶务部长庄原达率领下，今天乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪、理事谢南光、副秘书长肖向前，中国农学会理事关文启等人。
    在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也到车站欢迎。
