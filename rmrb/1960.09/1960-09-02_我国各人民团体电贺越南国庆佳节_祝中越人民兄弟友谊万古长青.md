Title: Chinese People's Organizations Send Cables to Congratulate Vietnam on National Day, Wishing the Fraternal Friendship Between the Chinese and Vietnamese Peoples Everlasting

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 1st—Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, All-China Federation of Trade Unions, All-China Women's Federation, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, All-China Youth Federation, and All-China Students' Federation, sent telegrams to relevant Vietnamese people's organizations on September 1st and August 31st, respectively, warmly congratulating the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace said in its telegram to the Vietnamese Committee for World Peace that over the past fifteen years, under the wise leadership of the Vietnamese Labor Party and President Ho Chi Minh, the Vietnamese people have defeated the French imperialist aggressors and achieved brilliant victories in socialist revolution and socialist construction. The great achievements of the Vietnamese people in socialist construction have strengthened the power of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and made significant contributions to the cause of defending peace in Asia and the world.

The telegram said that the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. imperialism and for national reunification has powerfully safeguarded peace in Indochina and the world, and has been universally praised and admired by people all over the world. The telegram said that the Chinese people firmly believe that the cause of the Vietnamese people's reunification of their motherland will surely be realized, and the great cause of the Vietnamese people's socialist construction will surely achieve even more brilliant achievements. Victory belongs to the heroic Vietnamese people.

The Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity sent a telegram to the Vietnamese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, saying that the people of China and Vietnam have a deep traditional friendship, and we will always help and support each other in the common cause of fighting against imperialism and building socialism.

Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, said in his telegram to Hoang Quoc Viet, Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, that the Chinese people resolutely and fully support the Vietnamese people's just struggle for the peaceful reunification of their motherland and against U.S. imperialist aggression, and firmly believe that the heroic Vietnamese people with a long tradition of anti-imperialist struggle, with the support of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and all peace-loving people in the world, will certainly be able to drive U.S. imperialism out of Vietnamese territory and will be able to finally complete the great cause of unifying the motherland.

The All-China Women's Federation sent a telegram to the Vietnam Women's Union saying that the people and women of China and Vietnam are fraternal sisters who share weal and woe. We have always helped and supported each other in the struggle to build socialism and oppose imperialism, and have formed a fighting comradeship.

The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China, the All-China Youth Federation, and the All-China Students' Federation jointly sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Youth League, the Vietnam Youth Federation, and the Vietnam Students' Union, warmly congratulating them on the festival. The telegram said that the brilliant achievements you have made in socialist revolution and socialist construction make the Chinese youth feel happy and encouraged. The telegram stated that the Chinese youth, together with the Chinese people, will unremittingly make the greatest efforts to develop the friendship between the people and youth of China and Vietnam, and to strengthen the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, September 1st—The China-Vietnam Friendship Association sent a telegram to the Vietnam-China Friendship Association on the 31st, warmly congratulating the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The telegram said that the Chinese people are extremely excited and encouraged by the victories and achievements of the Vietnamese people. The telegram said that the people of China and Vietnam have a deep traditional friendship. On the occasion of the great festival of the Vietnamese people, we would like to wish the Vietnamese people even more brilliant achievements in socialist construction, and wish the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the people of China and Vietnam everlasting.

### 我国各人民团体电贺越南国庆佳节  祝中越人民兄弟友谊万古长青

1960-09-02
第6版()
专栏：

    我国各人民团体电贺越南国庆佳节
    祝中越人民兄弟友谊万古长青
    据新华社1日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国亚非团结委员会、中华全国总工会、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会以及中国共产主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会，分别在9月1日和8月31日致电越南有关人民团体，热烈祝贺越南民主共和国成立十五周年。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会在给越南保卫世界和平委员会的电报中说，十五年来，越南人民在越南劳动党和胡志明主席的英明领导下，击败了法帝国主义侵略者，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中取得了辉煌的胜利。越南人民社会主义建设的巨大成就，增强了以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的力量，对保卫亚洲及世界和平事业作出了重大的贡献。
    电报说，越南人民反对美帝国主义、争取祖国统一的斗争有力地维护了印度支那和全世界的和平，受到世界各国人民的普遍赞扬和钦佩。电报说，中国人民深信，越南人民统一祖国的事业一定会实现，越南人民建设社会主义的伟大事业一定会获得更辉煌的成就，胜利属于英雄的越南人民。
    中国亚非团结委员会打电报给越南亚非团结委员会说，中越两国人民有着传统的深厚友谊，我们在反对帝国主义的斗争和建设社会主义的共同事业中，将永远互相帮助，互相支持。
    中华全国总工会主席刘宁一在致越南总工会主席黄国越的电报中说，中国人民坚决全力支持越南人民争取祖国和平统一、反对美帝国主义侵略的正义斗争，并且深信，有长期反帝斗争传统的英雄的越南人民，在以苏联为首的社会主义阵营和世界一切爱好和平人民的支持下，一定能够把美帝国主义赶出越南国土，一定能够最后完成统一祖国的伟业。
    中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会打电报给越南妇女联合会说，中越两国人民和妇女是休戚与共的兄弟姊妹，我们在建设社会主义和反对帝国主义的斗争中，一向相互帮助，相互支援，结成了战斗的友谊。
    中国共产主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国青年联合会和中华全国学生联合会联名致电越南劳动青年团中央委员会、越南青年联合会、越南大学生联合会，热烈祝贺节日。电报说，你们在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中所取得的辉煌成就，使中国青年感到欢欣鼓舞。电报中表示，中国青年和中国人民一起，将坚持不懈地为发展中越两国人民和青年的友谊，为加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结而作出最大努力。
    据新华社1日讯　中越友好协会31日致电越中友好协会，热烈祝贺越南民主共和国成立十五周年。
    电报说，中国人民为越南人民的胜利和成就感到无比兴奋和鼓舞。电报说，中越两国人民有着深厚的传统友谊，值此越南人民的伟大节日，谨祝越南人民在社会主义建设中取得更加辉煌的成就，祝中越两国人民牢不可破的兄弟友谊万古长青。
