Title: Nepalese Chief Representative of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee Holds Reception for Chinese Representatives
Author:
Date: 1960-09-18
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, September 17th: Padam Bahadur Khatri, Nepalese Chief Representative of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee, held a reception at the "Summer Palace" on the evening of the 16th to entertain Zhang Shijie, Chinese Chief Representative of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee and Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, and other representatives. Those present at the reception included Deputy Prime Minister Subarna Shamsher, cabinet ministers, and other high-ranking civil and military officials. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Nepal also attended the reception.
    The reception proceeded in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere.

### 中尼边界联合委员会尼泊尔首席代表  举行招待会招待我国代表

1960-09-18
第6版()
专栏：

    中尼边界联合委员会尼泊尔首席代表
    举行招待会招待我国代表
    新华社17日讯　加德满都消息：中尼边界联合委员会尼泊尔首席代表帕达姆·巴哈杜尔·卡特里少将16日晚在“涼宫”举行招待会，招待中尼边界联合委员会中国首席代表、中国驻尼泊尔大使张世杰和其他代表。出席招待会的有副首相苏巴纳·沙姆谢尔，内阁大臣们和其它高级文武官员。各国驻尼泊尔外交使节也出席了招待会。
    招待会在友好融洽的气氛中进行。
