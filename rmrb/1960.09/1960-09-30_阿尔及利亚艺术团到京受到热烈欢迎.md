Title: Algerian Art Troupe warmly welcomed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 6
　
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. The Algerian Art Troupe, a group of twenty-four people, led by Afa Abdul Madi, the representative of the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic to the Art Troupe, and Benlas Boualang, the head of the troupe, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. The welcoming crowd surged forward, applauding, shaking hands, and presenting flowers to the artists from the heroic country at the forefront of the anti-imperialist and anti-colonialist struggle.

The Algerian Art Troupe is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association to participate in the celebration of the eleventh anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and will perform in China. This is the first African art troupe received in Beijing.

Those who welcomed the troupe at the airport today included: Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association; Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee and Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association; Xu Pingyu, Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association and Deputy Minister of Culture; Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Lu Jin, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League; and well-known figures from the capital's literary and art circles and literary and art workers from the armed forces, including Xie Bingxin, Wang Yuanfang, Chen Qitong, Ding Li, Mu Hong, Yu Lan, Zhao Qing, and others.

### 阿尔及利亚艺术团到京受到热烈欢迎

1960-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿尔及利亚艺术团到京受到热烈欢迎
    新华社29日讯　阿尔及利亚艺术团一行二十四人，在阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府派驻艺术团代表阿法·阿卜杜勒·玛蒂，团长本拉斯·布阿朗率领下，今天上午乘飞机抵达北京。欢迎的人们涌上前去，向来自反帝国主义反殖民主义斗争前线的英雄国家的艺术家们鼓掌、握手、献花。
    阿尔及利亚艺术团应中国非洲人民友好协会的邀请，前来参加中华人民共和国成立十一周年的庆祝活动，并将在中国进行访问演出。这是在北京接待的第一个非洲艺术团。
    今天在机场欢迎的有：中非友协会长刘长胜，对外文化联络委员会副主任、中非友协副会长屈武，中非友协副会长、文化部副部长徐平羽，中国人民对外文化协会秘书长周而复，共青团中央宣传部副部长陆进，以及首都文艺界著名人士和部队文艺工作者谢冰心、王元方、陈其通、丁里、牧虹、于蓝、赵青等。
