Title: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Appoints Ministers of Three Ministries of Machine Building Based on the Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-09-14
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th—According to the decision of the 29th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Chairman Liu Shaoqi appointed Duan Junyi as Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Liu Jie as Minister of the Second Ministry of Machine Building, and Zhang Liankui as Minister of the Third Ministry of Machine Building.

### 刘少奇主席根据人大常委会决定  任命三个机械工业部的部长

1960-09-14
第2版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席根据人大常委会决定
    任命三个机械工业部的部长
    新华社13日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十九次会议的决定，任命段君毅为第一机械工业部部长，刘杰为第二机械工业部部长，张连奎为第三机械工业部部长。
