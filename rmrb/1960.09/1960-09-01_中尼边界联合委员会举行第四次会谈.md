Title: China-Nepal Joint Boundary Committee Holds Fourth Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-09-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 30th -- News: The fourth meeting of the first session of the China-Nepal Joint Boundary Committee was held at the Shital Niwas in Kathmandu, the capital of the Kingdom of Nepal, from 10:00 to 12:30 this morning.

    The meeting was held in a sincere and friendly atmosphere. The next meeting will be held on September 2nd.

### 中尼边界联合委员会举行第四次会谈

1960-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    中尼边界联合委员会举行第四次会谈
    新华社30日讯　加德满都消息：中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议第四次会谈30日上午十时到十二时三十分在尼泊尔王国首都加德满都的狮宫举行。
    会谈是在诚挚和友好的气氛中举行的。下次会谈将在9月2日举行。
