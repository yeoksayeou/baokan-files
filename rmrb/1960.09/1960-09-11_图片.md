Author:

Date: 1960-09-11

President Liu Shaoqi met with President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea and all members of the Government Delegation of the Republic of Guinea he led on the afternoon of the 10th. The picture shows a group photo during the meeting. Front row, from left: Cheng Qian, Chen Shutong, Huang Yanpei, Zhou Enlai, Keita N. Famara, Dong Biwu, Sékou Touré, Liu Shaoqi, Diallo Saifoulaye, Zhu De, Louis Lansana Beavogui, Chen Yi, Shen Junru, He Long, Lin Feng, Zhang Zhizhong. Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian and Meng Qingbiao photographed.
President Liu Shaoqi delivers a welcome speech at the airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dongao photographed.
President Sékou Touré, accompanied by President Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai, waves to the welcoming crowds along the streets of Beijing. Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian photographed.
Speaker of the National Assembly of Guinea, Diallo Saifoulaye, accompanied by Chairman Zhu De and Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, is warmly welcomed by the people of the capital on the way to the hotel in an open car. Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian photographed.
Minister of Planning of the Republic of Guinea, Keita N. Famara, and Minister of National Economy, Louis Lansana Beavogui, accompanied by Vice Premier Bo Yibo, are warmly welcomed by the people of the capital as they pass through the streets of Beijing. Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian photographed.
From left: Speaker Diallo Saifoulaye, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, and Chairman Zhu De at the banquet. Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian photographed.
Youths of all ethnic groups warmly welcome the distinguished guests from Guinea. Xinhua News Agency reporter Huang Jingda photographed.
The people of the capital warmly welcome the distinguished guests from Guinea. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhu Lirong photographed.
