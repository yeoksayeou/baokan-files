Title: Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premier, Receives Vietnamese Financial Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, received the Financial Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam this morning. Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun held cordial and friendly talks with the head of the Vietnamese Financial Delegation, Nguyen Thanh Son, Deputy Minister of Finance of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and all members of the delegation.
　　Present at the reception were Jin Ming, Vice Minister of Finance; Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Huang Zichun, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Finance.
　　Tran Trong Quat, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, was also present at the reception.
　　The delegation arrived in our country on August 28th and visited and inspected Beijing, Liaoning, Zhejiang and other places, and exchanged work experience with Chinese financial personnel. The delegation has completed its inspection work and will leave Beijing by train tonight to return to its country.

### 习仲勋副总理接见越南财政考察团

1960-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　  习仲勋副总理接见越南财政考察团
    新华社24日讯　国务院副总理习仲勋今天上午接见了来我国访问的越南民主共和国财政考察团。习仲勋副总理同越南财政考察团团长、越南民主共和国财政部副部长阮清山及考察团的全体团员进行了亲切、友好的谈话。
    陪同接见的，有财政部副部长金明，外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮，财政部办公厅副主任黄子纯。
    接见时，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办陈仲括也在座。
    考察团于8月28日来我国访问，在北京、辽宁、浙江等地进行了访问和考察，同我国财政工作人员交流了工作经验。考察团已经结束了考察工作，在今天晚上乘火车离京回国。
