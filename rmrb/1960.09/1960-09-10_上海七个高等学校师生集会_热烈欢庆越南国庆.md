Title: Shanghai Seven Universities and Colleges Hold Gathering: Enthusiastically Celebrate Vietnamese National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 9th: Over one thousand faculty members and students from seven universities and colleges in Shanghai, including Shanghai Fisheries College, Shanghai Railway College, Shanghai Shipbuilding School, Tongji University, Jiaotong University, and East China Textile Institute of Technology, jointly held a grand gathering at the auditorium of Jiaotong University on the afternoon of the 8th, warmly celebrating the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The entire delegation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, headed by Tran Huy Lieu, and Vietnamese students studying in Shanghai attended the celebration.

Today afternoon, Jiaotong University was adorned with red flags and filled with a joyous atmosphere, brimming with the festive spirit. As the Vietnamese guests, accompanied by Yang Yongzhi, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and President of the Shanghai Academy of Social Sciences, arrived at Jiaotong University, faculty and students waiting on both sides of the school gate beat drums and gongs, warmly applauding their welcome.

Yao Li, Director of the Shanghai Higher Education Bureau, spoke at the meeting. He said that the Vietnamese people are currently engaged in a struggle against the war and aggression policies of U.S. imperialism, striving for the peaceful reunification of their country. We fully support this just struggle of the Vietnamese people.

Head of Delegation Tran Huy Lieu spoke amidst warm applause. After detailing the long-standing fraternal friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and China, and the brilliant achievements of the Vietnamese people over the past fifteen years, he said that the Vietnamese people are elated and encouraged by the victories achieved by the Chinese people in socialist revolution and socialist construction, as well as in the Great Leap Forward.

After Tran Huy Lieu's speech, he presented a banner to the assembly, at which point enthusiastic applause resounded throughout the hall.

Student representative Li Wanxia from Shanghai universities and colleges, and student representative Nguyen Kim Quang from Vietnam also spoke at the meeting. They both spoke of the deep friendship between the peoples of China and Vietnam, and the close friendship between the young students of China and Vietnam.

### 上海七个高等学校师生集会  热烈欢庆越南国庆

1960-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    上海七个高等学校师生集会
    热烈欢庆越南国庆
    据新华社上海9日电　上海水产学院、上海铁道学院、上海船舶制造学校、同济大学、交通大学、华东纺织工学院等七个高等学校的一千多名师生，8日下午在交通大学大礼堂联合举行隆重集会，热烈庆祝越南民主共和国成立十五周年。
    以陈辉燎为首的越中友好协会代表团全体贵宾，以及在上海学习的越南留学生参加了庆祝大会。
    今天下午，交通大学红旗招展，喜气洋洋，充满着欢乐的节日气氛。当越南贵宾在中共上海市委宣传部副部长、上海社会科学院院长杨永直等陪同下来到交通大学时，等候在学校门口两旁的师生们敲锣打鼓，热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    上海市高等教育局局长姚力在会上讲了话。他说，目前越南人民正在进行着反对美帝国主义的战争政策、侵略政策，争取和平统一祖国的斗争。我们全力支持越南人民的这一正义斗争。
    陈辉燎团长在热烈的掌声中讲话。他详细叙述了越中两国人民之间历史悠久的兄弟友谊，越南人民十五年来所取得的辉煌成就以后说，越南人民为中国人民在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中，以及在大跃进中所取得的胜利而感到欢欣鼓舞。
    陈辉燎团长讲话结束后，把一面锦旗送给大会，这时全场响起了热烈的掌声。
    上海高等学校学生代表李皖霞、越南留学生代表阮金光也在会上讲了话。他们都谈到了中越两国人民的深厚友谊，以及中越两国青年学生的亲密友谊。
