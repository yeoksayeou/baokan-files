Title: Czechoslovak New Ambassador to China, Cedive, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic to China, Josef Cedive, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.  Ge Buhai, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Xu Ming, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs, were at the airport to welcome them.  Štěpka, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and embassy officials were also at the airport to greet them.

### 捷克斯洛伐克新任驻华大使赛迪维到京

1960-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克新任驻华大使赛迪维到京
    新华社23日讯　捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国新任驻华特命全权大使约瑟夫·赛迪维及夫人，今日上午乘飞机抵达北京。前往机场欢迎的有外交部礼宾司副司长葛步海和苏联东欧司副司长徐明。捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆临时代办石杰基那和大使馆官员也到机场迎接。
