Title: After Visiting Haiphong and Ha Long Bay, Our Party and Government Delegation Returns to Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1960-09-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 10th: The Chinese Party and Government Delegation, invited to participate in the 15th anniversary celebration of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Third National Congress of the Vietnamese Labor Party, returned to Hanoi this afternoon after visiting Haiphong, an important seaport of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and touring the famous scenic spot of Ha Long Bay from the 8th to the 10th.

When the delegation arrived in Haiphong on the afternoon of the 8th, it was warmly welcomed by Nguyen Duc Thinh, Standing Committee Member of the Haiphong City Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Member of the City Administrative Committee, Nguyen Xuan Dai, Vice President of the Haiphong Branch of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, as well as representatives from the Fatherland Front, the Women's Union, and the Overseas Chinese Federation.

The delegation visited the Haiphong Shipyard, which is under construction. Head of the delegation Li Fuchun spoke at a welcome meeting held by the workers of the factory. He also wrote an inscription wishing for the vigorous development of Vietnam's shipbuilding and maritime industry.

### 在海防市和下龙湾参观游览后  我党政代表团回到河内

1960-09-11
第5版()
专栏：

    在海防市和下龙湾参观游览后
    我党政代表团回到河内
    新华社河内10日电　应邀参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年庆典和越南劳动党第三次全国代表大会的中国党政代表团从8日到10日参观了越南民主共和国重要海港海防市和游览了著名风景区下龙湾后，今天下午回到河内。
    代表团8日下午到达海防市时，受到越南劳动党海防市常务委员、市行政委员会委员阮德盛，越中友好协会海防分会副会长阮春台以及祖国战线、妇女联合会、华侨联合会代表等热烈欢迎。
    代表团参观了正在兴建中的海防造船厂。李富春团长在这家工厂的工人欢迎大会上讲了话。他并题词祝越南造船和航海事业蓬勃发展。
