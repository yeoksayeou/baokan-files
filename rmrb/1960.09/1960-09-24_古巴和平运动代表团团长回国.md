Title: Head of Cuban Peace Movement Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: Mrs. Yolanda Pérez, head of the Cuban Peace Movement Delegation and secretary of the Cuban Movement for the Defense of Peace and Sovereignty of Peoples, who was invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to visit our country, left Beijing by plane today to return to her country after visiting Beijing, Guangzhou and other places. The other two members of the delegation, Masola Hernández and Armando Vila, had already left Beijing and returned to their country. Among those who saw them off at the airport were Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others.

### 古巴和平运动代表团团长回国

1960-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

　  古巴和平运动代表团团长回国
    新华社22日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国访问的古巴和平运动代表团团长、古巴保卫和平和各国人民主权运动书记约兰达·佩雷兹夫人，在北京、广州等地参观访问后，今天乘飞机离开北京返国。代表团其他两位成员马索拉·埃尔南德和阿曼多·维拉已先行离京返国。到机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长王明远等人。
