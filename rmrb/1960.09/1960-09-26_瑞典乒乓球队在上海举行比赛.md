Title: Swedish Table Tennis Team Holds Competition in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 24th: The Swedish men's table tennis team held a friendly match with the Shanghai men's table tennis team one on the evening of the 23rd in Shanghai. As a result, Shanghai Team One won with a score of five to zero.

Prior to the match, Jin Zhonghua, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, met with the head of the visiting team, Karl Albert Raben, and Ekt Leiyan, sports reporter for the Swedish "New Daily."

### 瑞典乒乓球队在上海举行比赛

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　瑞典乒乓球队在上海举行比赛
    据新华社上海24日电　瑞典男子乒乓球队23日晚在上海与上海男子乒乓球一队举行友谊比赛。结果上海一队以五比零获胜。
    比赛前，上海市副市长金仲华接见了客队领队卡尔·艾伯特·拉本和瑞典“新日报”体育记者埃克特·莱扬。
