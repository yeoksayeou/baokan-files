Title: Chinese Geological Department Delegation Leaves Hungary for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, September 18th: The Chinese Geological Department delegation led by He Changgong, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Geology, concluded its visit to Hungary and left Budapest by plane on the afternoon of the 17th to return home.

Those who saw them off at the airport included the负责人 (fuzeren - people in charge) of the Hungarian Geological Bureau and other relevant departments.

Former Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nogradi, was also there to see them off.

Hao Deqing, the Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, also went to the airport to see them off.

The delegation visited Hungary at the invitation of the people in charge of the Hungarian Geological Bureau on August 24th.

### 我国地质部访问团离匈回国

1960-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    我国地质部访问团离匈回国
    新华社布达佩斯18日电　由中国地质部副部长何长工率领的中国地质部访问团，结束了在匈牙利的访问，于17日下午乘飞机离开布达佩斯回国。
    到机场欢送的，有匈牙利地质局和其他有关部门的负责人。
    前往送行的还有前匈牙利驻华大使诺格拉第。
    中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青也前往机场欢送。
    访问团是应匈牙利地质局负责人的邀请，于8月24日到匈牙利进行访问的。
