Title: Two Cuban Journalists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - At the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association, Peregrín Durán, Director of the International Department of the Cuban newspaper "Hoy," and Luis Baez, Editor of the magazine "Verde Olivo," arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to our country.

### 古巴两位新闻工作者到京

1960-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴两位新闻工作者到京
    新华社11日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的古巴“今日报”国际部主任佩雷格林·杜拉斯和“绿橄榄树”杂志编辑部主任路易士·巴风，今天乘飞机到达北京。
