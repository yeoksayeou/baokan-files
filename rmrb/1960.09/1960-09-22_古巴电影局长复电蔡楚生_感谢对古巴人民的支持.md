Title: Cuban Film Bureau Director Replies to Cai Chusheng, Thanks for Support of Cuban People
Author:
Date: 1960-09-22
Page: 5

　　Cuban Film Bureau Director Replies to Cai Chusheng
　　Thanks for Support of Cuban People

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Cai Chusheng, Chairman of the Chinese Film Workers Association, received a telegram from Alfredo Guevara, Director of the Cuban Institute of Art and Film Industry. In the telegram, Guevara expressed his gratitude to the Chinese film workers for their support of the Cuban people and the Cuban film workers' struggle against American imperialist aggression. Alfredo Guevara's telegram said: "The directors, playwrights, cinematographers, technicians, and actors of the new Cuban cinema thank the Film Workers Association of the People's Republic of China for its warm telegram, supporting our people's struggle to defend national independence, sovereignty, and culture against the aggression and ugly infiltration of American imperialism.

　　"My film workers, already liberated from imported and commercial culture, uphold Fidel's words: 'Rather die without a homeland than live without one!'"

### 古巴电影局长复电蔡楚生  感谢对古巴人民的支持

1960-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

　  古巴电影局长复电蔡楚生
　  感谢对古巴人民的支持
    据新华社讯　中国电影工作者协会主席蔡楚生收到了古巴电影艺术和电影工业局局长阿尔弗雷多·格瓦拉的一份电报。格瓦拉在电报中对中国电影工作者支持古巴人民和古巴电影工作者反对美帝国主义侵略的斗争表示感谢。阿尔弗雷多·格瓦拉的电报说：“古巴新电影的导演、剧作家、摄影师、技师和演员们感谢中华人民共和国电影工作者协会热情的来电，支援我国人民保卫民族独立、主权和文化，反对美帝国主义的侵略和丑恶渗透的斗争。
    “已经从进口的和商业性文化中解放出来的我电影工作者拥护菲德尔的话：无祖国毋宁死！”
