Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Appoints Zhang Weilie as Ambassador to Iraq
Author:
Date: 1960-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th -- Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Zhang Weilie as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Iraq.

Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has removed Chen Zhifang from his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Iraq.

### 刘少奇主席任命张伟烈为驻伊拉克大使

1960-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席任命张伟烈为驻伊拉克大使
    新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命张伟烈为中华人民共和国驻伊拉克共和国特命全权大使。
  中华人民共和国主席刘少奇免去陈志方的中华人民共和国驻伊拉克共和国特命全权大使的职务。
