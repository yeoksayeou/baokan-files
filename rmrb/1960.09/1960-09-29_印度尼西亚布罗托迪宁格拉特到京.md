Title: Indonesian Broto Diningrat Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 3
 　 Xinhua News Agency, September 28th - Broto Diningrat, First Secretary of the Office of the President of Indonesia, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by train today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.
 　 Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Chen Bozhong, Deputy Director of the Domestic Work Department of the All-China Women's Federation, were at the station to greet them.

### 印度尼西亚布罗托迪宁格拉特到京

1960-09-29
第3版()
专栏：
 　 印度尼西亚布罗托迪宁格拉特到京
    新华社28日讯　印度尼西亚总统府办公厅第一秘书布罗托迪宁格拉特和夫人应中国人民外交学会邀请，在今天乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站迎接的，有中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达和全国妇联国内工作部副部长陈伯仲。
