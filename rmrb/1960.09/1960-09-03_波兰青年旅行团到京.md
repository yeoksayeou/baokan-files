Title: Polish Youth Travel Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Polish youth travel group, headed by Alitman Josef, Minister of the National Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the Polish Socialist Youth League, arrived in Beijing this morning.

    Lu Jindong, Alternate Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, hosted a banquet for the guests in the evening.

### 波兰青年旅行团到京

1960-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰青年旅行团到京
    新华社2日讯　以波兰社会主义青年联盟中央委员会国家联络部部长阿力特曼·约瑟夫为首的波兰青年旅行团在今天上午到达北京。
    中国共产主义青年团中央候补书记路金栋晚上设宴招待客人。
