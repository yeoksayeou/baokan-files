Title: Locomotive Sports Association Men's Basketball Team, Coal Mine Sports Association Women's Basketball Team Visit Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The Chinese Locomotive Sports Association Men's Basketball Team and the Chinese Coal Mine Sports Association Women's Basketball Team, a group of thirty people invited to visit the Soviet Union, set off by plane today, led by Zhang Zhi, Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union.
	The basketball teams will first go to Moscow, and then to Lithuania, Leningrad, and other places for a twenty-day visit and competition.

### 火车头体协男子煤矿体协女子篮球队赴苏访问

1960-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    火车头体协男子煤矿体协女子篮球队赴苏访问
    新华社11日讯　应邀到苏联访问的中国火车头体育协会男子篮球队和中国煤矿体育协会女子篮球队一行三十人，今天由中国铁路工会全国委员会秘书长章智率领乘飞机启程。
    篮球队将先到莫斯科，然后到立陶宛、列宁格勒等地作为时二十天的访问比赛。
