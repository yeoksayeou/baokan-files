Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Answers Questions from Reporters on the Eve of the 15th Anniversary of National Day: The Vietnamese People are Determined to Reunify Their Motherland
Author:
Date: 1960-09-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 30th: Premier Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam told a "Voice of Vietnam" radio reporter that the Vietnamese people are determined to wage an unremitting struggle against the aggressive US imperialism and its running dogs, in order to reunify the motherland on the basis of independence and democracy and through peaceful means stipulated in the Geneva Accords.

Premier Pham Van Dong made these remarks when answering questions from a "Voice of Vietnam" radio reporter on the eve of the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

When talking about the achievements of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the past fifteen years, Premier Pham Van Dong pointed out that the fifteen years since the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam have been years of heroic struggle and brilliant victories. He then reviewed Vietnam's victory over the invading imperialists in the nine-year war of resistance.

He pointed out that since the restoration of peace, in the completely liberated North Vietnam, after healing the wounds of war and basically completing the national democratic revolution, the people's democratic state has carried out the historical task of the dictatorship of the proletariat and launched socialist transformation and socialist construction in the economic, cultural and ideological fields.

He said that by the end of this year, the socialist transformation in North Vietnam will achieve a decisive victory. The rapidly developing economy and culture are gradually changing the face of North Vietnam, its society and its people along the socialist path.

Premier Pham Van Dong then pointed out that the main reasons for the great achievements of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam are the unity of the Vietnamese people under the correct leadership of the Vietnamese Labor Party headed by President Ho Chi Minh, their heroic fighting spirit and creative labor. The victory of the Vietnamese people is the victory of Marxism-Leninism.

He said that the Vietnamese people also deeply realize that all the achievements they have achieved or will achieve are inseparable from the sympathy and enthusiastic support of peace-loving and just people all over the world, and from the generous and most valuable assistance given to them by the socialist brother countries, especially the Soviet Union and China.

He also said that the Vietnamese people realize that a major guarantee for the success of the revolution is the growing strength and unbreakable unity of the socialist camp headed by the powerful Soviet Union. The Vietnamese people will make unremitting efforts to contribute to further consolidating this unity based on Marxism-Leninism and in line with the lofty principles of proletarian internationalism.

He pointed out that the glorious achievements of the Soviet Union in building communism and the amazing progress of the People's Republic of China and other socialist countries are a great encouragement and favorable condition for the revolutionary cause of the Vietnamese people.

Talking about the future tasks and prospects of the Vietnamese people's revolutionary struggle, Premier Pham Van Dong said that Vietnam has not yet been unified due to the aggressive policy of US imperialism. South Vietnam is becoming a new type of colony, a military base for US imperialism and a danger of war in Indochina. The Vietnamese people are determined to wage an unremitting struggle against the US imperialist aggressors and their running dogs in order to reunify the motherland on the basis of independence and democracy and through peaceful means stipulated in the Geneva Accords.

He said that the Democratic Republic of Vietnam—a people's democratic state currently building socialism in the North—plays a significant role in the struggle for national reunification. Considering Vietnam's characteristics and combining these characteristics with the universal principles of Marxism-Leninism, the Vietnamese Labor Party has formulated a general line for the socialist revolution in North Vietnam, which is to carry out socialist transformation of agriculture, handicrafts, small commerce and private capitalist industry and commerce. The Party develops the state-owned economic sector, carries out socialist industrialization by appropriately prioritizing the development of heavy industry, while developing agriculture and light industry. The Party carries out socialist revolution in ideology, culture and technology, constantly improves the people's living standards, and makes North Vietnam a socialist country with modern industry, modern agriculture, and advanced science and culture.

He said that according to this general line, Vietnam's first Five-Year Plan (1961-1965) will mark a new period in the development of the socialist revolution in the North. It will complete a step in socialist industrialization, complete socialist transformation, and promote cultural, ideological, and technological revolutions, thereby further improving the living standards of the people of North Vietnam and increasing its strength in all aspects.

Premier Pham Van Dong said that relying on their own unity and struggle, with the valuable assistance of the socialist camp, especially the Soviet Union and China, and with the warm support of the people of the world, the Vietnamese people firmly believe that the cause of socialist construction in the North and the peaceful reunification of the country will surely be victorious, thereby realizing a peaceful, unified, independent, democratic, and prosperous Vietnam.

Premier Pham Van Dong finally said that the upcoming Vietnamese Labor Party Congress is a very powerful encouragement for the Vietnamese people to enthusiastically advance to complete their new tasks and strive for new victories.

### 范文同总理在建国十五周年前夕答记者问  越南人民决心要重新统一祖国

1960-09-01
第6版()
专栏：

    范文同总理在建国十五周年前夕答记者问
    越南人民决心要重新统一祖国
    指出北方地区社会主义改造和社会主义建设的事业已获辉煌成就
    表示决心同侵略成性的美帝国主义及其走狗进行坚持不懈的斗争
    新华社河内30日电　越南民主共和国总理范文同对
  “越南之声”电台记者说，越南人民决心同侵略成性的美帝国主义及其走狗进行坚持不懈的斗争，以便在独立和民主的基础上并通过日内瓦协议规定的和平途径重新统一祖国。
    范文同总理是在越南民主共和国成立十五周年前夕答复“越南之声”电台记者提出的问题时说这番话的。
    范文同总理在谈到越南民主共和国过去十五年所取得的成就时指出，越南民主共和国建国以来的十五年，是进行英勇斗争和取得辉煌胜利的年代。他接着回顾了越南在历时九年的抗战中，对进行侵略的帝国主义所取得的胜利。
    他指出，自从和平恢复以来，在获得完全解放的越南北方，在治愈战争创伤、基本上完成民族民主革命以后，人民民主国家就执行无产阶级专政的历史任务和在经济、文化和思想方面展开社会主义改造和社会主义建设。
    他说，到今年年底，越南北方的社会主义改造将获得决定性的胜利。迅速发展的经济和文化方面正在按照社会主义路线逐步地改变着越南北方、它的社会及其人民的面貌。
    范文同总理接着指出，使越南民主共和国取得伟大成就的主要原因是，越南人民在以胡志明主席为首的越南劳动党的正确领导下的团结力量，英勇的斗争精神和创造性的劳动。越南人民的胜利就是马克思列宁主义的胜利。
    他说，越南人民还深深认识到，他们已经取得或将取得的一切成就同全世界爱好和平和正义的人民的同情和热情支持，同社会主义兄弟国家，首先是苏联和中国，给予他们的慷慨和最宝贵的援助是分不开的。
    他还说，越南人民认识到，革命成功的一个主要保证是以强大的苏联为首的社会主义阵营的日益壮大的力量和不可动摇的团结一致。越南人民将进行坚持不懈的努力，为进一步巩固这种以马克思列宁主义为基础的、符合无产阶级国际主义的崇高原则的团结一致而作出贡献。
    他指出，苏联建设共产主义的光辉成就，中华人民共和国和其他社会主义国家的惊人进步，对于越南人民的革命事业是巨大鼓舞和有利条件。
    谈到越南人民革命斗争今后的任务和前景，范文同总理说，由于美帝国主义的侵略政策，越南还没有统一。越南南方正在成为一种新型的殖民地，成为美帝国主义的军事基地和印度支那的战争危险。越南人民决心同美帝国主义侵略者及其走狗进行坚持不懈的斗争以便在独立和民主的基础上，并通过日内瓦协议规定的和平途径重新统一祖国。
    他说，越南民主共和国——目前正在北方建设社会主义的人民民主国家——对于争取祖国统一的斗争具有重大的作用。越南劳动党考虑越南的特点，并把这些特点同马克思列宁主义的普遍原则结合起来，制订了对农业、手工业、小商业和私营资本主义工商业进行社会主义改造的越南北方社会主义革命的总路线。党发展国营经济部分，通过适当地优先发展重工业，进行社会主义工业化，同时发展农业和轻工业。党在思想、文化和技术方面进行社会主义革命，不断提高人民的生活水平，使越南北方成为一个具有现代化工业、现代化农业和先进的科学和文化的社会主义国家。
    他说，根据这个总路线，越南第一个五年计划（1961—1965年）将标志着北方社会主义革命发展的新的时期。它将在社会主义工业化方面完成一个步骤，完成社会主义改造以及推动文化、思想和技术革命，从而进一步提高越南北方人民的生活水平和增加它在各方面的力量。
    范文同总理说，依靠自己的团结和斗争，在社会主义阵营、首先是苏联和中国的宝贵援助下，以及在世界人民的热烈支持下，越南人民坚决相信，北方的社会主义建设和全国和平统一的事业一定能够取得胜利，从而实现一个和平、统一、独立、民主和繁荣的越南。
    范文同总理最后说，即将召开的越南劳动党代表大会对于越南人民热情地为完成他们的新任务和争取新的胜利而前进，是一个非常有力的鼓舞。
