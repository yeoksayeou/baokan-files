Title: Our Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Hanoi to Attend Vietnam National Day Celebrations and the Labor Party Congress; Vietnamese Party and Government Leaders Give Warm Welcome at Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 31st: The Chinese Communist Party and Chinese Government delegation, invited by the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to attend the 15th anniversary celebration of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Third National Congress of the Vietnam Workers' Party, arrived in Hanoi at 10:50 am today, led by Li Fuchun, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council.

When Li Fuchun, Marshal Ye Jianying, member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, and Liao Chengzhi, member of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council, and other comrades stepped off the plane, they were warmly welcomed at the airport by Vietnamese Party and government leaders, including Pham Van Dong, member of the Political Bureau of the Vietnam Workers' Party Central Committee and Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; General Vo Nguyen Giap, member of the Political Bureau and Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense; Nguyen Duy Trinh, member of the Political Bureau and Deputy Prime Minister; Phan Ke Toai, Deputy Prime Minister; Hoang Van Hoan, member of the Political Bureau and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly; Ho Hoang Anh, member of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat; and Hoang Quoc Viet, Nguyen Xuan Thuy, and Ung Van Khiem, members of the Central Committee.

After the band played the national anthems of China and Vietnam, Vice Premier Li Fuchun and Marshal Ye Jianying, accompanied by Prime Minister Pham Van Dong and General Vo Nguyen Giap, reviewed the guard of honor. As the distinguished Chinese guests passed by the welcoming crowds, warm applause resounded throughout the airport.

He Wei, the Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, as well as the Guangdong Provincial Party and Government Delegation headed by Lin Qiangyun, the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association Delegation headed by Sun Xiaocun, and some Chinese experts working in Vietnam were also at the airport to welcome them.

Diplomatic envoys of various countries to Vietnam were also present at the airport to welcome them. (Attached picture)

Our Party and government delegation, led by Li Fuchun, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, arrived in Hanoi on the morning of August 31. The picture shows Prime Minister Pham Van Dong shaking hands with head of delegation Li Fuchun at the airport. (Faxed photo by Niu Weiyu, Xinhua News Agency)

### 参加越南国庆盛典和劳动党代表大会  我国党政代表团到达河内  越南党政领导人到机场热烈欢迎

1960-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    参加越南国庆盛典和劳动党代表大会
    我国党政代表团到达河内
    越南党政领导人到机场热烈欢迎
    新华社河内31日电　应越南劳动党中央委员会和越南民主共和国政府邀请，前来参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年庆典和越南劳动党第三次全国代表大会的中国共产党和中国政府代表团，在中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理李富春的率领下，今天上午十时五十分到达河内。
    当李富春和中共中央委员、国防委员会副主席叶剑英元帅，中共中央委员、国务院外事办公室副主任廖承志等同志走下飞机后，受到越南劳动党中央政治局委员、越南民主共和国总理范文同，政治局委员、副总理兼国防部长武元甲大将，政治局委员、副总理阮维祯，副总理潘继遂，政治局委员、国会常务委员会副主席黄文欢，中央委员、书记处书记黄英和中央委员黄国越、阮春水、雍文谦等党政领导人的热烈欢迎。
    在乐队高奏中越两国国歌后，李富春副总理、叶剑英元帅在范文同总理、武元甲大将的陪同下，检阅了仪仗队。当中国贵宾们走过迎候的群众队伍时，机场上响起了热烈的掌声。
    中国驻越南大使何伟和以林锵云为首的广东省党政代表团，以孙晓村为首的中越友好协会代表团以及在越南工作的一部分中国专家都到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有各国驻越南的外交使节。（附图片）
