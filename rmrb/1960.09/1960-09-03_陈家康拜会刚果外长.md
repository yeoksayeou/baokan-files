Title: Chen Jiakang Calls on Congolese Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Léopoldville, September 1st. Chen Jiakang, the Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, called on Congolese Foreign Minister Bomboko here today.

Chen Jiakang conveyed the regards of Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, to the Congolese Foreign Minister. Bomboko expressed his gratitude for this.

They had a friendly conversation during the meeting.

Ambassador Chen Jiakang recently arrived here. He was invited to attend the Foreign Ministers' Conference of Independent African States as an observer of the People's Republic of China.

### 陈家康拜会刚果外长

1960-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    陈家康拜会刚果外长
    新华社利奥波德维尔1日电　中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康今天在这里拜会了刚果外交部长邦博科。
    陈家康转达了中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅对刚果外交部长的致意。邦博科对此表示感谢。
    他们在会见时进行了友好的谈话。
    陈家康大使最近来到这里。他是以中华人民共和国观察员的身份应邀参加非洲独立国家外长会议的。
