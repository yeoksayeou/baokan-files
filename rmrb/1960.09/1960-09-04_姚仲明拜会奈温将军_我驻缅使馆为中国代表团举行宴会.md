Title: Yao Zhongming Calls on General Ne Win; Chinese Embassy in Burma Holds Banquet for Chinese Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 2nd – Yao Zhongming, Chief Representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, called on General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, here this morning.

    Also present were Lei Yang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché.

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 2nd – Lei Yang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, held a banquet this evening for the Chinese delegation to the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, headed by Yao Zhongming.

    Among those attending the banquet were U Myint Thein, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of Burma; General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces; Sao Khun Kyi, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities; Sao Khun Hkio, Minister of Foreign Affairs and Minister for Shan State Affairs; Bohmu Aung, Minister of Transport; Sama Duwa Sinwa Nawng, Minister for Kachin State Affairs; former Deputy Prime Minister U Thein Maung; former Foreign Minister U Chan Tun Aung; former Minister of Culture U Chit Thoung; James Barrington, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, as well as secretaries of the Burmese Parliament and leaders of various political parties. Also attending the banquet were Kyao Winn, Burmese Ambassador to China, Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Chief Representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, and other Burmese representatives and advisors.

    The counselors of the Chinese Embassy in Burma and Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché, attended the banquet.

    The banquet was filled with a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 姚仲明拜会奈温将军  我驻缅使馆为中国代表团举行宴会

1960-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    姚仲明拜会奈温将军
    我驻缅使馆为中国代表团举行宴会
    新华社仰光2日电　中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明今天上午在这里拜会了缅甸国防军总参谋长奈温将军。
    在场的有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬和武官蒋克定上校。
    新华社仰光2日电　中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬今晚为以姚仲明为首的中缅边界联合委员会中国代表团举行宴会。
    出席宴会的有缅甸最高法院首席法官吴敏登，国防军总参谋长奈温将军，民族院议长藻昆基，外交部长兼掸邦事务部长藻昆卓，运输部长波木昂，克钦邦事务部长沙马杜瓦信瓦瑙，前副总理吴登貌，前外交部长吴千吞昂，前文化部长吴漆东，外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿以及缅甸议会秘书和各政党领袖。出席宴会的还有缅甸驻中国大使叫温、中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表昂季准将以及其他缅甸代表和顾问。
    中国驻缅甸大使馆的参赞们和武官蒋克定上校出席了宴会。
    宴会上充满了亲切友好气氛。
