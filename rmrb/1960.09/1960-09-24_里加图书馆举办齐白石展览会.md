Title: Riga Library Hosts Qi Baishi Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. According to TASS: The National Library of Riga, the capital of the Latvian Soviet Socialist Republic, opened a Qi Baishi exhibition on the 22nd to commemorate the famous Chinese painter.
    Replicas of Qi Baishi's works, as well as commentaries on Qi Baishi's works by famous Chinese and Soviet artists and critics, are displayed on the shelves of the exhibition hall. Riga's "Soviet Latvia" newspaper published an article that day introducing Qi Baishi, entitled "Outstanding Chinese Artist."

### 里加图书馆举办齐白石展览会

1960-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

    里加图书馆举办齐白石展览会
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联拉脱维亚共和国首都里加的国家图书馆，22日起举办纪念中国著名画家齐白石的展览会。
    在展览厅的陈列架上展出了齐白石作品的复制品以及中苏两国著名艺术家和评论家关于齐白石作品的评论。里加的“苏维埃拉脱维亚报”这一天刊登了一篇介绍齐白石的文章，题目是“杰出的中国艺术家”。
