Title: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister Receives Members of Our Team
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, August 31st: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister Ali Muhammad received the members of the Beijing Student Men's Basketball Team on August 30th.

Present at the meeting was Hao Ting, the Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan.

Ali Muhammad expressed his gratitude to the Chinese team members for participating in the Afghan Independence Day celebrations. He asked the team members to convey the Afghan youth's best wishes and high regards to the Chinese youth. He said that the mutual visits of athletes would further enhance the friendship between China and Afghanistan. He presented the Chinese athletes with commemorative medals and gifts.

Ren Chao, the leader of the Beijing Student Men's Basketball Team, said that the Chinese team members were pleased to be able to participate in the Afghan Independence Day celebrations and thanked the Afghan government for their warm welcome.

### 阿富汗副首相接见我球队队员

1960-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗副首相接见我球队队员
    新华社31日讯　喀布尔消息：阿富汗副首相阿里·穆罕默德8月30日接见了北京市学生男子篮球队的队员。
    接见时在座的有中国驻阿富汗大使郝汀。
    阿里·穆罕默德对中国球队队员参加阿富汗独立节庆祝活动表示感谢，他请队员们向中国青年转致阿富汗青年的最好的祝愿和崇高的问候。他说，运动员的互相访问将进一步增进中国和阿富汗之间的友谊。他向中国运动员赠送了纪念章和礼物。
    北京市学生男子篮球队领队任超表示，中国球队队员为能够参加阿富汗独立节的庆祝典礼而感到高兴，并感谢阿富汗政府给予他们的热烈欢迎。
