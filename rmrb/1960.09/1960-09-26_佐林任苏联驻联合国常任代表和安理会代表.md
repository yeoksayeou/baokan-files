Title: Zorin Appointed Soviet Permanent Representative to the UN and Representative to the Security Council
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 24th: According to TASS, Moscow, September 24th: Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs V. Zorin has been appointed as the Soviet Union's Permanent Representative to the United Nations and Representative to the United Nations Security Council.

　　Former Soviet Permanent Representative to the United Nations and Representative to the United Nations Security Council, A. Sobolev, has been appointed as Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.

### 佐林任苏联驻联合国常任代表和安理会代表

1960-09-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　佐林任苏联驻联合国常任代表和安理会代表
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科24日讯：苏联外交部副部长瓦·佐林已被任命为苏联驻联合国常任代表和驻联合国安全理事会代表。
    原任苏联驻联合国常任代表和驻联合国安理会代表阿·索波列夫，已被任命为苏联外交部副部长。
