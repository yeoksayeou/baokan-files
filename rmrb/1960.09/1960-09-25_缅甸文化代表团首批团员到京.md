Title: First Group of Members of Burmese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 24th. The first group of 120 members of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, who are coming to China to attend the National Day celebrations and the signing ceremony of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 24th and were warmly welcomed by people from the capital's literary and art circles.

In order to solemnly celebrate the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty during our National Day, the Burmese Union Government delegation, headed by Prime Minister U Nu, will lead a large-scale delegation of various professional groups to visit our country. The Cultural Delegation is one of these professional delegations.

Today, the first group of delegation members was led by Deputy Head U Su Wu, along with Captain Kyaw Win, leader of the first team, and Captain Lun Tin, leader of the second team. Colonel So Min, the head of the delegation, had already arrived in Beijing with the Burmese delegation of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee and went to the airport to welcome them today.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Chu Tunan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Jian Bozan, Vice President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, Senior Colonel Li Wei of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and more than 300 well-known figures from the capital's literary and art and film circles, including Xu Guangping, Lü Ji, Zhang Jinhui, Wang Dong, Chen Jinqing, Li Delun, Guo Lanying, Wang Yang, Zhao Ziyue, Tian Hua, and Qin Wen.

Among those who came to the airport to welcome them were Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Burmese dignitaries in Beijing, U Aung Chin, First Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China, and embassy diplomats.

The Burmese Cultural Delegation consists of 166 members, composed of film actors group, light music band, film projection group, Kachin State and Shan State folk dance group, Yangon West District amateur dance group, dance and drama group, Burmese band, Yangon and Mandalay Normal College dance group and other literary and art units. The film actors group includes famous film actors such as U Tun Wai, U Tin Maung, and Min Min Khin, who have won the Best Actor Award in Burma. Most of the members of the delegation arrived in Beijing today, and the remaining members will arrive in Beijing successively.

When the Burmese Cultural Delegation arrived in Kunming on the 23rd, they were warmly welcomed by literary and art circles in Yunnan Province and Kunming City. Ding Ke, Director of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, and famous film actor Li Baiwan made a special trip to Kunming to welcome the guests.

### 缅甸文化代表团首批团员到京

1960-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化代表团首批团员到京
    新华社24日讯　来我国参加国庆观礼并参加签定中缅边界条约庆祝活动的缅甸文化代表团首批团员一百二十人，24日下午到达北京，受到首都文艺界人士的热烈欢迎。
    为了在我国国庆节期间隆重地庆祝中缅边界条约的签订，以吴努总理为首的缅甸联邦政府代表团将率领规模庞大的各种专业代表团来访我国。文化代表团是这些专业代表团中的一个。
    今天，代表团的首批团员是由副团长吴苏吴以及第一队队长叫温上尉和第二队队长伦丁上尉率领到达的。代表团团长苏敏上校已随中缅边界联合委员会缅甸代表团先行到京，今天也去机场迎接。
    前往机场欢迎的，有我国对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南，文化部副部长夏衍，中国人民对外文化协会秘书长周而复，中缅友好协会副会长翦伯赞，中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部副部长李伟大校，以及首都文艺界、电影界著名人士许广平、吕骥、张金辉、王栋、陈锦清、李德伦、郭兰英、汪洋、赵子岳、田华、秦文等三百多人。
    到机场迎接的，有以昂季准将为首的缅甸在京贵宾，缅甸驻华大使馆一等秘书吴翁钦和使馆外交官员。
    缅甸文化代表团成员一百六十六人，由电影演员组、轻音乐乐队、电影放映组、克钦邦和掸邦民间舞蹈组、仰光西城区业余舞蹈组、舞蹈与戏剧组、缅甸乐队、仰光和曼德勒师范学院舞蹈组等文艺单位组成。电影演员组中包括缅甸最佳演员奖获得者吴吞威、吴丁貌、敏敏钦等著名电影演员。今天到京的是代表团的大部分团员，其余的团员将陆续到京。
    缅甸文化代表团23日到达昆明时，曾受到了云南省和昆明市文艺界人士的热烈欢迎。中缅友好协会理事丁克和著名电影演员李百万曾专程到昆明迎接客人。
