Title: Tunisian Neo Destour Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: At the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation and the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, the six-member delegation of the Tunisian Neo Destour Party, led by Ben Amar Hassib, Chairman of the Social and Economic Revitalization Department of the Tunisian Neo Destour Party, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Zhang Chao, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Wang Zhaohua, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Student Federation.

### 突尼斯新宪政党代表团到京

1960-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    突尼斯新宪政党代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　应中华全国青年联合会和中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的突尼斯新宪政党代表团一行六人，在团长、突尼斯新宪政党社会经济复兴部主席本·阿玛尔·哈西卜率领下，今天乘飞机抵京。
    到机场欢迎的有全国青联副主席、共青团中央书记处书记张超，共青团中央书记处书记王照华，全国学联主席胡启立等人。
