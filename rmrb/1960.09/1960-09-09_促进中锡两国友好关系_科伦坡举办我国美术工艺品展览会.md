Title: Promote Friendly Relations Between China and Ceylon: Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition Held in Colombo
Author:
Date: 1960-09-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 7th. A Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition opened in Colombo on the 7th, presided over by the Ceylonese Minister of Internal Affairs, Industry, and Culture, Maitripala Senanayake.

    The exhibition is jointly organized by the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Ceylon and the Ceylon-China Friendship Association. The exhibition features pottery, porcelain, lacquerware, textiles, embroidery, and other works of art.

    Minister Senanayake said at the opening ceremony of the exhibition that as Ceylon hopes to develop arts and crafts, this exhibition will be a source of inspiration and encouragement. He said that we Asians can be proud of the measures China has taken to develop arts and crafts in the past eleven years. He hoped that this exhibition will strengthen the friendly relations between China and Ceylon.

    Wilmot Perera, Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, also spoke at the opening ceremony.

    Yao Dengshan, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy, said that the friendship between China and Ceylon has a long history. Since the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries, this friendly relationship has been further developed on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. He hoped that through cultural, economic, and other exchanges, the friendship between the two countries will deepen day by day.

    Senior Ceylonese officials, diplomatic envoys, monks, and a large number of people from all walks of life attended the opening ceremony of the exhibition.

    The exhibition will run until September 18th.

### 促进中锡两国友好关系  科伦坡举办我国美术工艺品展览会

1960-09-09
第5版()
专栏：

    促进中锡两国友好关系
    科伦坡举办我国美术工艺品展览会
    新华社科伦坡7日电　中国美术工艺品展览会7日在锡兰内政、工业和文化部长迈特里帕拉·森纳那亚克主持下在科伦坡开幕。
    展览会是由中华人民共和国驻锡兰大使馆和锡中友好协会联合举办的。展览会展出的有陶器、瓷器、漆器、纺织品、刺绣和其它艺术品。
    森纳那亚克部长在展览会开幕仪式上说，当锡兰正希望发展美术工艺品的时候，这次展览会将成为启发和鼓舞的源泉。他说，我们亚洲人可以为十一年来中国为发展美术工艺品所采取的措施而感到骄傲。他希望，这次展览会将加强中国和锡兰的友好关系。
    锡中友好协会主席威尔莫特·佩雷拉也在开幕仪式上讲了话。
    中国大使馆临时代办姚登山说，中国和锡兰的友谊有着悠久的历史。自从两国建立外交关系以来，这种友好关系在和平共处五项原则的基础上得到了进一步的发展。他希望，通过文化、经济和其它方面的交流，两国的友谊将日益加深。
    出席展览会开幕式的有锡兰的高级官员，外交使节，僧侣和大批各界人士。
    展览会将一直展出至9月18日。
