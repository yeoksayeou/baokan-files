Title: Bulgaria and Cambodia Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1960-09-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 19th - According to a report by the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency: In order to consolidate the friendly ties between the two countries, the governments of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the Kingdom of Cambodia have decided to establish diplomatic relations at the level of ministers.

### 保加利亚和柬埔寨决定建交

1960-09-21
第6版()
专栏：

    保加利亚和柬埔寨决定建交
    新华社索非亚19日电　据保加利亚通讯社报道：保加利亚人民共和国和柬埔寨王国政府为了巩固两国间的友好联系，决定建立公使级外交关系。
