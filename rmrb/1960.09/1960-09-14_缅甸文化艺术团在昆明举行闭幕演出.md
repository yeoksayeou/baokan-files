Title: Burmese Cultural Arts Troupe Holds Closing Performance in Kunming
Author:
Date: 1960-09-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, September 13th: The Burmese Cultural Friendship Arts Troupe held its closing performance of its visit to China in Kunming on the evening of the 12th.

    The venue was decorated with the national flags of China and Burma, and a huge banner written in both Chinese and Burmese that read "Long Live Sino-Burmese Friendship!" creating a warm and friendly atmosphere.

    The closing ceremony was presided over by Chu Tunan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Those present at the performance included: Yan Hongyan, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China; Yu Yichuan, Governor of Yunnan Province; and Deputy Governors Liu Minghui and Guo Chao. The Chinese representatives and advisors of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee in Kunming also watched the performance.

    U Maung Maung So, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and Vu Van Huong, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, also attended the closing ceremony.

    Chu Tunan spoke first at the closing ceremony, expressing his gratitude on behalf of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of the People's Republic of China to the Burmese artists for bringing the profound friendship of the Burmese people and the Burmese art with strong national characteristics to the Chinese people. He warmly congratulated the Burmese friends on the success of their performances in China.

    U Ba Saing, head of the Burmese Cultural Friendship Arts Troupe, spoke, expressing his deep gratitude for the warm reception and welcome from the Chinese government and people.

    During the closing performance, the Burmese artists' rich and varied music and dance once again left an unforgettable impression on the audience. The audience applauded again and again, congratulating the Burmese artists on the success of their performance.

### 缅甸文化艺术团在昆明举行闭幕演出

1960-09-14
第2版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化艺术团在昆明举行闭幕演出
    据新华社昆明13日电　缅甸文化友好艺术团12日晚在昆明举行在我国访问演出的闭幕式。
    会场上悬挂着中、缅两国的国旗和用中缅两国文字写成的“中缅友好万岁！”的巨幅标语，洋溢着一片亲切友好的气氛。
    闭幕式由对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南主持。出席观看演出的有：中共云南省委第一书记阎红彦，云南省省长于一川，副省长刘明辉、郭超等。中缅边界联合委员会在昆明的中国代表和顾问也观看了演出。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭、越南民主共和国驻昆明领事武文享也都出席了闭幕式。
    楚图南在闭幕式上首先讲话，他代表中华人民共和国对外文化联络委员会感谢缅甸艺术家给中国人民带来了缅甸人民的深厚友谊和具有浓厚民族特色的缅甸艺术。他热烈祝贺缅甸朋友在中国的演出成功。
    缅甸文化友好艺术团团长吴巴盛讲了话，他对中国政府和人民的热情接待和欢迎，表示非常感谢。
    在闭幕演出中，缅甸艺术家们丰富多采的音乐和歌舞，再一次给观众留下难忘的印象。观众们一次又一次地鼓掌，祝贺缅甸艺术家们的演出成功。
