Title: Cyprus Farmers Association Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 5th - The Cyprus Farmers Association delegation, invited by the National Committee of the Agricultural and Forestry Trade Union of China to visit our country, concluded its visit to our country and left Beijing by plane today to return home.
    During its stay in our country, the delegation was received by Chairman Liu Shaoqi and visited Beijing, Luoyang, Sanmenxia, Zhengzhou, Wuhan, Shanghai and other cities.

### 塞浦路斯农民协会代表团回国

1960-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    塞浦路斯农民协会代表团回国
    新华社5日讯　应中国农林工会全国委员会邀请前来我国访问的塞浦路斯农民协会代表团，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离京回国。
    代表团在我国期间，受到了刘少奇主席的接见，并且访问了北京、洛阳、三门峡、郑州、武汉、上海等城市。
