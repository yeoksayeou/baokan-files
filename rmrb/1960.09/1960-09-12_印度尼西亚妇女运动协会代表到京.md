Title: Indonesian Women's Movement Association Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The second group of representatives from the Indonesian Women's Movement Association delegation, including Mrs. Siti Asijah Setiagraha, totaling five people, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon, invited by the All-China Women's Federation for a visit to our country. Wu Quanheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 印度尼西亚妇女运动协会代表到京

1960-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚妇女运动协会代表到京
    新华社11日讯　应全国妇联邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚妇女运动协会代表团第二批代表西蒂·阿希亚·色迪亚格拉哈夫人等五人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的，有全国妇联书记处书记吴全衡等。
