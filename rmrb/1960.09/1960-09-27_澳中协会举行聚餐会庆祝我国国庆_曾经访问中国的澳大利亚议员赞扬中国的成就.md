Title: Australia-China Association Holds Dinner Party to Celebrate China's National Day; Australian Parliamentarians Who Visited China Praise China's Achievements
Author:
Date: 1960-09-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, 26th. The Australia-China Association held a grand dinner party in Sydney last night with over 150 people attending, to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    New South Wales Member of Parliament, Mr. Rigby, and Federal Opposition Member of Parliament, Mr. Uren, among others, successively proposed toasts to China during the dinner. Rigby said that he was amazed by China's development. After visiting China two years ago, he was deeply convinced that the Chinese people wanted peace. Uren said that after his recent visit to China, he was convinced that China would become a strong nation in the world within this century. The Chinese people love the people and strive to make them happy. He said: "We must understand that in China, all ethnic groups are brothers; with a leader like Mao Zedong, they cannot fail to succeed." He proposed a toast to peace and the friendship between the Australian and Chinese people.

### 澳中协会举行聚餐会庆祝我国国庆  曾经访问中国的澳大利亚议员赞扬中国的成就

1960-09-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　澳中协会举行聚餐会庆祝我国国庆
　  曾经访问中国的澳大利亚议员赞扬中国的成就
    新华社26日讯　悉尼消息：澳中协会昨晚在悉尼举行了有一百五十多人参加的盛大聚餐会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年。
    新南威尔士议会议员里格比和联邦议会反对党议员尤伦等人在席间先后提议为中国干杯。里格比说，他对于中国的发展感到惊异，在两年前访问中国以后，他就深信中国人民是要和平的。尤伦说，他在最近访问了中国以后深信：中国将在本世纪内成为世界上强盛的国家。中国人热爱人民，并且努力使人民幸福。他说：“我们必须懂得，在中国，各族人民是兄弟；有了像毛泽东这样的领袖，他们不会不获得成功。”他提议为和平和澳中两国人民的友谊干杯。
