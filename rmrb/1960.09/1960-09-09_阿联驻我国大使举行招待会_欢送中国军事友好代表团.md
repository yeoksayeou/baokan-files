Title: UAE Ambassador to China Holds Reception to See Off Chinese Military Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Ambassador of the United Arab Republic to China, Tarazi, held a reception this evening to see off the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, led by General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, which is about to pay a friendly visit to the UAR.

General Luo Ruiqing, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, was invited to attend the reception.

In his speech at the reception, Ambassador Tarazi welcomed the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation's friendly visit to the UAR. He said that this friendly visit is a symbol of the dedication of our two countries, the two peoples, and the armed forces to achieving peace and strengthening friendship between the two countries.

General Zhang Zongxun said in his speech at the reception that the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation is about to pay a friendly visit to the UAR, carrying the friendship of the Chinese people and army. We believe that this visit will help further develop and consolidate the friendly relations between the peoples and armies of China and the UAR.

All members of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation were invited to attend the reception this evening.

Also invited to the reception were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Major General Wang Xinggang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, Bao Erhan, President of the Sino-Arab Friendship Association, Da Pongsheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and Yue SongSheng. Diplomatic envoys and military attachés of various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 阿联驻我国大使举行招待会  欢送中国军事友好代表团

1960-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联驻我国大使举行招待会
    欢送中国军事友好代表团
    新华社8日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国驻我国大使塔拉齐今晚举行招待会，欢送即将去阿联进行友好访问的、由中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将率领的中国军事友好代表团。
    中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将应邀出席了招待会。
    塔拉齐大使在招待会上致词，对中国军事友好代表团去阿联进行友好访问表示欢迎。他说，这种友好访问是我们两国、两国人民和武装部队所致力的、为了达到和平和增强两国之间友谊的象征。
    张宗逊上将在招待会上讲话说，中国军事友好代表团即将带着中国人民和军队的友谊，去阿联进行友好访问。我们相信，这次访问将有助于中阿两国人民和军队之间的友好关系进一步发展和巩固。
    中国军事友好代表团全体成员应邀出席了今晚的招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的还有外交部副部长章汉夫、中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华上将、国防部办公厅副主任王兴纲少将、中阿友好协会会长包尔汉、中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生、北京市副市长吴晗、乐松生。各国驻华使节和武官也出席了招待会。
