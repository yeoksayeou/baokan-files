Title: Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Hosts Reception for the Signing of Agreement Between China and Mongolia on China Dispatching Workers to Assist Mongolia in Construction
Author:
Date: 1960-09-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 22nd: Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, Xie Fusheng, hosted a reception tonight for the signing of the "Agreement Between the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic on the Dispatch of Workers from the People's Republic of China to Assist in Production and Construction in the Mongolian People's Republic" by the government delegations of China and Mongolia.

Among those attending the reception were Molomjamts, First Vice Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers; Maidar, Vice Chairman; Baljid, head of the Mongolian government delegation and Chairman of the Foreign Economic Liaison Committee; Ragchaa, Chairman of the State Planning Committee; Shagdar Suran, Minister of Foreign Affairs; as well as all members of the Chinese and Mongolian government delegations and Chinese experts in Mongolia.

Cao Diqiu, head of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association delegation, Jiya Tai, deputy head, and members of the delegation were also invited to attend the reception.

Xie Fusheng, Molomjamts, Cao Diqiu, Baljid, and Maidar, among others, successively raised their glasses at the banquet, wishing for the further consolidation and development of the friendly cooperation between the Chinese and Mongolian peoples, for greater achievements in the socialist construction of China and Mongolia, and for the greater unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

The meeting was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 为中蒙签订关于中国派遣工人援助蒙古建设的协定　我驻蒙古大使举行招待会

1960-09-23
第6版()
专栏：

　　为中蒙签订关于中国派遣工人援助蒙古建设的协定
　　我驻蒙古大使举行招待会
    新华社乌兰巴托22日电　中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生今晚为中蒙两国政府代表团签订“关于中华人民共和国派遣工人援助蒙古人民共和国生产建设的协定”举行招待会。
    出席招待会的，有蒙古部长会议第一副主席莫洛姆扎木茨，副主席迈达尔，蒙古政府代表团团长、对外经济联络委员会主席巴尔吉德，国家计划委员会主席拉格查、外交部长沙格达尔苏伦，以及中蒙两国政府代表团全体团员和中国在蒙古的专家。
    中蒙友好协会代表团团长曹荻秋、副团长吉雅泰和代表团团员也应邀出席了招待会。
    谢甫生、莫洛姆扎木茨、曹荻秋、巴尔吉德和迈达尔等人在宴会上先后举杯，祝中蒙两国人民的友好合作进一步巩固发展，祝中蒙两国的社会主义建设取得更大成就，祝以苏联为首的社会主义阵营更加团结。
    会上洋溢着热烈友好的气氛。
