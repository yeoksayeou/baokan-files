Title: Sichuan Provincial Song and Dance Troupe Performs for UAR Soldiers; Brigadier General Gamal Holds Tea Party to Entertain the Troupe
Author:
Date: 1960-09-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Alexandria, September 19th: The Chinese Sichuan Provincial Song and Dance Troupe, currently visiting the United Arab Republic, performed for over two thousand UAR soldiers in Alexandria on the 18th and 19th, and was warmly welcomed.

The audience greatly appreciated the Chinese folk music and dance performed by the troupe. They continuously praised the performances.

During their stay in Alexandria, Zhang Tiesheng, head of the song and dance troupe, visited Vice Admiral Mahmoud Muhammad Nashed, Chief of Staff of the UAR Naval Command, and Brigadier General Abdel Aziz Gamal, Commander of the UAR Northern Command, and had sincere conversations with them.

Brigadier General Gamal also held a tea party this afternoon to entertain all members of the song and dance troupe.

### 我四川省歌舞团为阿联军人演出  加米勒准将举行茶会招待歌舞团

1960-09-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　我四川省歌舞团为阿联军人演出
　  加米勒准将举行茶会招待歌舞团
    新华社亚历山大港19日电　正在阿拉伯联合共和国访问的中国四川省歌舞团，18日和19日在亚历山大港为两千多名阿联军人演出，受到了热烈欢迎。
    观众非常欣赏歌舞团演出的中国民间音乐和舞蹈。他们对演出的节目不断赞扬。
    在亚历山大港逗留期间，歌舞团团长张铁生拜访了阿联海军司令部参谋长马哈茂德·穆罕默德·纳希德海军中将和阿联北部司令部司令官阿卜杜勒·阿齐兹·加米勒准将，并同他们进行了诚挚的交谈。
    加米勒准将也在今天下午举行了茶会，招待歌舞团的全体团员。
