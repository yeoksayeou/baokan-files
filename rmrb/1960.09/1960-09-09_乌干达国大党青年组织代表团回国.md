Title: Uganda National Congress Youth Organization Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 8th — The Uganda National Congress Youth Organization delegation of four people, headed by M. F. Aruema, Deputy Secretary-General of the Uganda National Congress Youth Organization, concluded their friendly visit to our country and have recently left Beijing by plane to return home.

### 乌干达国大党青年组织代表团回国

1960-09-09
第5版()
专栏：

    乌干达国大党青年组织代表团回国
    新华社8日讯　以乌干达国大党青年组织副总书记姆·弗阿鲁玛为首的乌干达国大党青年组织代表团一行四人，结束了在我国的友好访问，已在最近先后乘飞机离开北京回国。
