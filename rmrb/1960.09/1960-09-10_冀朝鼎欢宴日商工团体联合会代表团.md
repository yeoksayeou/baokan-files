Title: Ji Chao Ding Hosts Banquet for Japanese Business and Industry Associations Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. Ji Chao Ding, Vice-Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Japanese National Federation of Business and Industry Associations Delegation headed by Teizaburo Kono. Hu Ziang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Chen Hansheng, member of the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee, attended the banquet as guests.

Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also invited to attend the banquet.

The Japanese National Federation of Business and Industry Associations Delegation, a group of ten, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 8th at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade for a visit to our country.

### 冀朝鼎欢宴日商工团体联合会代表团

1960-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    冀朝鼎欢宴日商工团体联合会代表团
    新华社9日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎今晚设宴欢迎以河野贞三郎为首的日本全国商工团体联合会代表团。出席宴会作陪的有中华全国工商业联合会副主任委员胡子昂和中国亚非团结委员会委员陈翰笙等人。
    在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也应邀出席了宴会。
    日本全国商工团体联合会代表团一行十人，是应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请来我国访问的。他们在8日下午乘火车到达北京。
