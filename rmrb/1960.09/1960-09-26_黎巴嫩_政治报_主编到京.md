Title: Lebanese "Political News" Editor-in-Chief Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: Assad Moukaddem, Editor-in-Chief of the Lebanese "Political News," arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association to visit our country.

### 黎巴嫩“政治报”主编到京

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　  黎巴嫩“政治报”主编到京
    新华社25日讯　黎巴嫩“政治报”主编阿萨德·穆卡丹应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问，24日下午乘飞机到达北京。
