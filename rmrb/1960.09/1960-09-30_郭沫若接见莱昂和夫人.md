Title: Guo Moruo Receives León and Wife
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th — Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, received Augusto León, Venezuelan Senator and member of the World Peace Council, and his wife this morning, and had a cordial conversation with them. Present at the reception were Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. Mr. Venturieri, a Chilean peace activist who is in Beijing, was also present at the reception. (Attached picture)
    Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, received Augusto León, Venezuelan Senator and member of the World Peace Council, and his wife, who are visiting our country, on September 29th.

### 郭沫若接见莱昂和夫人

1960-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若接见莱昂和夫人
    新华社29日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天上午接见了委内瑞拉参议员、世界和平理事会理事奥古斯托·莱昂和他的夫人，同他们进行了亲切的交谈。接见时在座的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长王明远，中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达。接见时，在京的智利和平人士万徒勒里也在座。（附图片）
