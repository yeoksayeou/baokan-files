Title: Delegation of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association Leaves Beijing for Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-09-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The delegation of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, led by Cao Diqiu, head of the delegation and vice president of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, and Ji Yatai, deputy head of the delegation and vice president of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, left Beijing by train this afternoon for the Mongolian People's Republic.

The delegation of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association is visiting the Mongolian People's Republic at the invitation of the Mongolia-China Friendship Association to participate in the "Mongolia-China Friendship Days" activities and to conduct a friendly visit.

Those who saw them off at the station included Zhang Zhixiang, Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and President of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association; Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Tian Xiujuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation; and Ma Yanxiang and Weng Dujian, directors of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association.

Toyev, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy in China, also saw them off at the station.

### 中蒙友协代表团离京去蒙古

1960-09-18
第5版()
专栏：

    中蒙友协代表团离京去蒙古
    新华社17日讯　中蒙友好协会代表团在团长、中蒙友好协会副会长曹荻秋和副团长、中蒙友好协会副会长吉雅泰率领下，于今天下午乘车离北京前往蒙古人民共和国。
    中蒙友协代表团是应蒙中友好协会邀请，前往蒙古人民共和国参加“蒙中友好旬”活动并进行友好访问的。
    到车站送行的，有对外文委代主任、中蒙友好协会会长张致祥，外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮，对外文协秘书长周而复，全国妇联书记处书记田秀涓，中蒙友协理事马彦祥、翁独健等。
    蒙古驻华大使馆临时代办托依夫等也到车站送行。
