Title: Secretary at the Furnace
Author: Feng Zheng
Date: 1960-09-15
Page: 3

    Secretary at the furnace late at night,
    People's enthusiasm grows, the furnace produces more steel,
    Each furnace yields high-quality products,
    The rolling steel flows like an ocean!

### 书记到炉旁
冯政
1960-09-15
第3版()
专栏：

    书记到炉旁
            冯政
    书记深夜到炉旁，
    人增干劲炉增钢，
    炉炉炼成优质品，
    滚滚钢水似海洋！
