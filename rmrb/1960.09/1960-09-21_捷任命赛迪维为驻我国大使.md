Title: Czechoslovakia Appoints Šejdí as Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1960-09-21
Page: 6
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 20th: According to local newspapers, the President of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic, Novotný, has newly appointed Jaroslav Šejdí as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China.

### 捷任命赛迪维为驻我国大使

1960-09-21
第6版()
专栏：

　  捷任命赛迪维为驻我国大使
    新华社布拉格20日电　据此间报纸消息，捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国总统诺沃提尼新任命约·赛迪维为驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。
