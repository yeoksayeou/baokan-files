Title: Secretary-General of the Arab Federation of Chambers of Commerce, Industry and Agriculture Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 25th – Burhan Dajani, Secretary-General of the Arab Federation of Commerce, Industry and Agriculture, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade for a visit to our country.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Ji Chao-ding, Vice-Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and others.

### 阿拉伯商工农业联合会秘书长到京

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　  阿拉伯商工农业联合会秘书长到京
    新华社25日讯　应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请来我国访问的阿拉伯商业、工业和农业联合会秘书长包尔汉·达贾尼今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎等人。
