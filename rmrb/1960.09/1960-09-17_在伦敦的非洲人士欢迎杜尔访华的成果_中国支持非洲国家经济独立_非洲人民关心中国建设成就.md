Title: African Figures in London Welcome the Results of Touré's Visit to China; China Supports the Economic Independence of African Countries; African People Are Concerned About China's Construction Achievements
Author:
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, London, September 15th: African figures in London welcome Guinean President Sékou Touré's visit to China, and welcome the Treaty of Friendship and the Agreement on Economic and Technical Cooperation signed between China and Guinea.

    Dennis Pomboyeh, Secretary-General of the African Organization Committee, told Xinhua News Agency today: "The Chinese government's intention to maintain friendly relations with African countries and help them achieve economic independence is becoming increasingly clear. This shows that the Chinese government is unremittingly pursuing the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence with all countries."

    Pomboyeh emphasized: "We hope to see more African leaders visit China and establish diplomatic, economic, and cultural relations with the People's Republic of China."

    Nana Mohomo, a leader of the South African United Front, welcomed the first visit of an African head of state to China, saying that Touré's visit would definitely consolidate the friendly relations between China and Guinea and the friendly relations between China and African countries. He also said that the African people are paying close attention to the news about the remarkable achievements of the construction of People's China.

### 在伦敦的非洲人士欢迎杜尔访华的成果  中国支持非洲国家经济独立  非洲人民关心中国建设成就

1960-09-17
第6版()
专栏：

    在伦敦的非洲人士欢迎杜尔访华的成果
    中国支持非洲国家经济独立  非洲人民关心中国建设成就
    新华社伦敦15日电　在伦敦的非洲人士欢迎几内亚总统塞古·杜尔访问中国，欢迎中国和几内亚签订的友好条约和经济技术合作协定。
    非洲组织委员会总书记丹尼斯·波姆贝赫今天对新华社记者说：“中国政府想同非洲国家保持友好关系和帮助它们取得经济独立的意图是愈来愈明显了。这说明，中国政府坚持不懈地奉行同各国和平共处的五项原则。”
    波姆贝赫强调说：“我们希望看到更多非洲国家的领袖访问中国，并同中华人民共和国建立外交、经济和文化关系。”
    南非统一阵线的一位领袖纳纳·莫霍莫欢迎非洲国家首脑第一次访问中国，他说，杜尔的访问肯定会巩固中国和几内亚的友好关系以及中国和非洲国家的友好关系。他还说，非洲人民密切注意关于人民中国的建设的惊人成就的消息。
