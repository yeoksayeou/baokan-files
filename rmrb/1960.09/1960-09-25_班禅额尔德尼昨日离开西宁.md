Title: Panchen Erdene Leaves Xining Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xining, September 24th. Panchen Erdene, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Acting Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, left Xining by plane this morning to attend the 11th Anniversary National Day celebrations in Beijing.

Guo Xilan, Deputy Secretary of the CCP Tibet Working Committee, also left Xining to attend the National Day celebrations in Beijing. Panchen Erdene's father, mother, and scripture teacher, Enjiu Living Buddha, also went to Beijing to attend the celebrations.

Vice Chairman Panchen Erdene arrived in Xining on the 22nd. During his stay in Xining, he was warmly welcomed by the responsible persons of the Qinghai Provincial Party, government, and military, as well as representatives from ethnic and religious circles, and had cordial conversations with the responsible persons of the Qinghai Provincial Party, government, and military. The Qinghai Provincial People's Committee hosted a banquet in honor of Vice Chairman Panchen Erdene and his entourage.

### 班禅额尔德尼昨日离开西宁

1960-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　班禅额尔德尼昨日离开西宁
    新华社西宁24日电　前往北京参加建国十一周年庆祝典礼的全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼，于今日上午乘飞机离开西宁。
    前往北京参加国庆观礼的中共西藏工委副书记郭锡兰一同离开西宁。同去北京观礼的，还有班禅额尔德尼的父亲母亲和经师恩久活佛。
    班禅额尔德尼副委员长是在22日到达西宁的。他在西宁逗留期间，受到青海省党政军负责人和民族、宗教界代表人士的热烈欢迎，并和青海省党政军负责人进行了亲切交谈。青海省人民委员会曾设宴招待了班禅额尔德尼副委员长及其随行官员。
