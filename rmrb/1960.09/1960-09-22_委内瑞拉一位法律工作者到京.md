Title: A Venezuelan Legal Worker Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 21st—Dr. Octavio Andrade Delgado, President of the Venezuelan Democratic Lawyers Association and Senator of the Upper House, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Association to visit our country.

Zhang Zhiran, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Association, and others welcomed the guest at the airport.

### 委内瑞拉一位法律工作者到京

1960-09-22
第6版()
专栏：

    委内瑞拉一位法律工作者到京
    新华社21日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的委内瑞拉民主法协主席、上议院议员奥克塔维奥·安德拉德·德耳加多博士，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    中国政治法律学会副会长张志让等到机场欢迎客人。
