Title: Cultural Delegations from Six Latin American Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. A Cuban cultural delegation of eleven people led by Dr. Vincentina Antuña Davio, Director of the Cultural Department of the Cuban Ministry of Education; a Brazilian cultural delegation of nine people led by Pelegrino Junívor, President of the Brazilian Writers' Association and President of the Brazilian Academy of Letters; a Colombian cultural delegation of seven people led by Nicolás Buenaventura; a Bolivian cultural delegation of three people led by Abelardo Villalpando, President of Tomás Frías University; a Uruguayan cultural delegation of four people led by composer Casto Canel; and the Dominican cultural delegation, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They were all invited by the China-Latin America Friendship Association to visit our country.

Chu Tunan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, Vice President Zhou Erfu, Standing Director Shen Jian, Secretary-General Chen Jin, Tian Xiujuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, Tian Han, Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, and Hou Jinjing, Director of the Chinese Writers Association, and others greeted the guests at the airport.

### 拉丁美洲六个国家的文化代表团到京

1960-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　拉丁美洲六个国家的文化代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　由古巴教育部文化司司长维森蒂娜·安图尼亚·达维奥博士率领的古巴文化代表团一行十一人，由巴西作家协会主席、巴西文学院院长贝雷格里诺·尤尼沃尔率领的巴西文化代表团一行九人，由尼古拉斯·布埃纳万徒拉率领的哥伦比亚文化代表团一行七人，由托马斯·弗利亚斯大学校长阿贝拉尔多·维约尔邦多率领的玻利维亚文化代表团一行三人，由作曲家卡斯多·卡奈尔率领的乌拉圭文化代表团一行四人，以及多米尼加文化代表团，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们都是应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的。
    中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南、副会长周而复、常务理事申健、总干事陈金，全国妇联书记处书记田秀涓，中国戏剧家协会主席田汉，中国作家协会理事侯金镜等人分别到机场欢迎客人。
