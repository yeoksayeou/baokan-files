Title: Teachers' Union Representatives from Cuba, Brazil, Mexico, Uruguay, and Ecuador Leave Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 4th. Ten teachers' union representatives from Cuba, Brazil, Mexico, Uruguay, and Ecuador, who came to China for a visit at the invitation of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China, left Beijing by plane on September 4th to return to their countries.

Li Zaizhen of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee for the Educational Workers’ Union of China, and others were at the airport to see them off.

During their stay in China, the teachers' representatives from various countries were received by Chairman Liu Shaoqi and visited cities such as Beijing, Luoyang, Zhengzhou, Wuhan, and Shanghai. The delegation of the National Association of Teachers and Equivalent Teachers of Cuba signed a joint statement with the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China.

### 古巴、巴西、墨西哥、乌拉圭、厄瓜多尔  五国教师工会代表离京回国

1960-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴、巴西、墨西哥、乌拉圭、厄瓜多尔
    五国教师工会代表离京回国
    新华社4日讯　应中国教育工会全国委员会邀请，前来我国访问的古巴、巴西、墨西哥、乌拉圭、厄瓜多尔等五个国家的教师工会代表十人，于9月4日乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场送行的，有中华全国总工会书记处书记栗再温、中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明等。
    各国教师代表在我国期间，受到了刘少奇主席的接见，并访问了北京、洛阳、郑州、武汉、上海等城市。古巴全国师范及同级教师学会代表团代表古巴全国师范及同级教师学会同中国教育工会全国委员会签订了联合声明。
