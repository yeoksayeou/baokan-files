Title: Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu Receive Prime Minister U Nu and General Ne Win
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his wife and daughter, and the Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of Burma, General Ne Win, and his wife this evening, and had a cordial conversation with them.

Also received were the following distinguished Burmese guests: Minister for Kachin State Affairs of the Union Government, Duwa Sinwa Nau; Minister of Education and Justice of the Union Government, Dr. U E Maung; Chief Representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, Brigadier General Aung Gyi; Head of the Burmese Military Friendship Delegation, Brigadier General Maung Maung; Attorney General of the Union of Burma, U Yan Aung; Advisor to Prime Minister U Nu, U Wan; Minister of Education and Health of the Shan State Government, Sao Htun Aye; Minister of Education and Public Works of the Kachin State Government, Duwa Zori; Head of the Burmese Trade Delegation, U Myo; Head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, Colonel Soe Min; Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ba Lington; Members of Parliament U Da Shin and U Bo Shin; Representative of the Burma Union Party, Bo Din Maung Gyi; Representative of the National United Front, Bo Bo Kun; Head of the Burmese Sports Delegation, Lieutenant Colonel Maung Win; Head of the Burmese News Delegation, U Khin, and others.

The Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyao Win, and his wife also attended the reception.

Also present at the reception were Vice-Chairman Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice-Chairmen Huang Yanpei and Chen Shutong, Vice-Premiers He Long, Chen Yi, and Luo Ruiqing, as well as Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Xiruo, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee Yao Zhongming, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang, and others. ### 毛主席刘主席接见吴努总理奈温将军

1960-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席刘主席接见吴努总理奈温将军
    新华社29日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席今天晚间接见了缅甸联邦总理吴努和夫人及女儿、缅甸国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    同时被接见的缅甸贵宾还有：联邦政府克钦邦事务部长杜瓦信瓦瑙，联邦政府教育与司法部长吴埃貌博士，中缅边界联合委员会缅方首席代表昂季准将，缅甸军事友好代表团团长貌貌准将，缅甸联邦检察总长吴延昂，吴努总理的顾问吴旺，掸邦政府教育和卫生部长苏吞埃，克钦邦政府教育和公共工程部长杜瓦佐里，缅甸贸易代表团团长吴妙，缅甸文化代表团团长苏敏上校，外交部常任秘书巴林顿，国会议员吴达信、吴波信，缅甸联邦党代表波丁貌季，民族团结阵线代表波波昆，缅甸体育代表团团长貌温中校，缅甸新闻代表团团长吴钦等。
    缅甸驻中国大使叫温和夫人也参加了接见。
    接见时在座的，有董必武副主席，朱德委员长，周恩来总理，黄炎培、陈叔通副委员长，贺龙、陈毅、罗瑞卿副总理，以及对外贸易部部长叶季壮，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，外交部副部长章汉夫，中缅边界联合委员会中国方面代表姚仲明，中国驻缅甸大使李一氓等。（附图片）
