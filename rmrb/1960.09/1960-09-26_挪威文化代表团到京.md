Title: Norwegian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. The Norwegian Cultural Delegation, consisting of four members and led by the head of the delegation, Norwegian playwright Norum, arrived in Beijing by airplane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
　　Among those welcoming them at the airport was Zhang Lao, a director of the Chinese Dramatists Association.

### 挪威文化代表团到京

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　  挪威文化代表团到京
    新华社25日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的挪威文化代表团一行四人，在团长、挪威戏剧家诺鲁姆率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国戏剧家协会理事章烙等。
