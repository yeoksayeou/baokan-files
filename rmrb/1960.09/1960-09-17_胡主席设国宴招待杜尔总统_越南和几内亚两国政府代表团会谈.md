Title: Chairman Hu Hosts State Banquet for President Touré; Vietnamese and Guinean Government Delegations Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 15th – According to the Vietnam News Agency, President Ho Chi Minh hosted a state banquet at the Presidential Palace tonight to welcome President Sékou Touré and his entourage.

Attending the banquet from the Vietnamese side were Tôn Đức Thắng, Vice-President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front; Trường Chinh, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly; Prime Minister Phạm Văn Đồng; Hoàng Văn Hoan, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly; Deputy Prime Ministers Phạm Hùng, Võ Nguyên Giáp, Nguyễn Duy Trinh, and Phan Kế Toại; Hoàng Quốc Việt, Chief Procurator and Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor; Nguyễn Xuân Thủy, Vice-Chairman of the Vietnam Asian-African Solidarity Committee, and others.

Foreign guests attending the banquet included Dimitar Halov, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to Vietnam and doyen of the diplomatic corps in Vietnam, and François Billoux, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, who is currently visiting Vietnam.

In his speech at the banquet, President Ho Chi Minh praised the achievements of the Republic of Guinea in various fields over the past few years. He said, "The victory of the Guinean people has further inspired the people of African countries who are struggling against colonialism and for independence and freedom. The victory of the Guinean people is promoting the faster elimination of imperialism. Therefore, the Vietnamese people regard the victory of the fraternal Guinean people as their own victory."

President Ho Chi Minh said, "The Vietnamese people resolutely stand with the Guinean people in their struggle and resolutely support the liberation movements of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America."

President Sékou Touré then proposed a toast to the health of President Ho Chi Minh, to the prosperity of Vietnam, and to peace.

After the banquet, the guests watched the Vietnamese film "My Homeland - Vietnam."

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 16th – It was announced here today that the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by President Ho Chi Minh, and the government delegation of the Republic of Guinea, headed by President Sékou Touré, held talks this morning.

The Vietnamese side participating in the talks included President Ho Chi Minh, Trường Chinh, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, and Prime Minister Phạm Văn Đồng.

The Guinean side participating in the talks included President Sékou Touré and Diallo Saifoulaye, President of the National Assembly.

The two sides exchanged views on a number of issues concerning the interests of both countries. The talks were held in a cordial atmosphere.

### 胡主席设国宴招待杜尔总统  越南和几内亚两国政府代表团会谈

1960-09-17
第6版()
专栏：

    胡主席设国宴招待杜尔总统
    越南和几内亚两国政府代表团会谈
    新华社河内15日电　据越南通讯社报道，胡志明主席今晚在主席府举行国宴招待塞古·杜尔总统和随行人员。
    参加宴会的有越南民主共和国副主席、越南祖国战线中央委员会主席孙德胜，国会常务委员会主席长征，范文同总理，国会常务委员会副主席黄文欢，副总理范雄、武元甲、阮维祯、潘继遂，检察院院长、越南总工会主席黄国越，越南亚非团结委员会副主席阮春水等。
    参加宴会的外宾中包括驻越南的外交团团长、保加利亚人民共和国驻越南大使迪米特尔·哈洛夫和正在越南访问的法国共产党中央委员会政治局委员弗朗索瓦·皮佑。
    胡志明主席在宴会上讲话时，赞扬了几内亚共和国几年来各方面获得的成就。他说，“几内亚人民的胜利进一步鼓舞了正在为反对殖民主义和争取独立自由而进行斗争的非洲各国人民。几内亚人民的胜利促使更迅速地消灭帝国主义。因此，越南人民把兄弟的几内亚人民的胜利看作自己的胜利。”
    胡志明主席说，“越南人民坚决地同几内亚人民站在一起斗争，坚决地支持亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲各国人民的解放运动。”
    塞古·杜尔总统接着建议大家为胡志明主席的健康、为越南的繁荣、为和平而干杯。
    宴会后，宾主观看了越南影片“我的祖国——越南”。
    新华社河内16日电　此间今天公布，以胡志明主席为首的越南民主共和国政府代表团和以塞古·杜尔总统为首的几内亚共和国政府代表团今天上午进行了会谈。
    越南方面参加会谈的有胡志明主席、国会常务委员会主席长征、总理范文同等。
    几内亚方面参加会谈的有塞古·杜尔总统、国民议会议长迪阿洛·塞福拉耶等。
    双方就有关两国利益的许多问题交换了意见。会谈是在亲切的气氛中进行的。
