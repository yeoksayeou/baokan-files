Title: Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe of China Welcomed in Cairo Performance
Author:
Date: 1960-09-05
Page: 6
　
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 3rd. The Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe of China gave its first performance in Cairo tonight and was warmly welcomed.
　　The Cairo July 26th Theatre was packed tonight. The audience greatly appreciated the Chinese folk dances and music reflecting the happy life of the Chinese people and the face of new China. They gave warm applause and cheers to every performance.
　　Before the performance, Lei Yi, a responsible official from the Ministry of Culture and National Guidance, delivered a speech welcoming the Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe of China to visit the United Arab Republic. Zhang Tiesheng, head of the Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe of China, delivered a reply, expressing his hope that the visit of the song and dance troupe would further strengthen mutual understanding and friendship between the people of the two countries.
　　Among the people from all walks of life in the Egyptian region who attended the performance today were: Hashim, Minister of Education of the Egyptian Region, Undersecretary of the Ministry of Culture and National Guidance of the Egyptian Region, Omar Mari, Secretary-General of the Arab-China Friendship Association, and foreign diplomatic envoys stationed in Cairo.

### 我四川歌舞团在开罗演出受到欢迎

1960-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    我四川歌舞团在开罗演出受到欢迎
    新华社开罗3日电　中国四川省歌舞团今晚在开罗举行首次演出，受到热烈欢迎。
    开罗7月26日戏院今天晚上座无虚席。观众们非常欣赏反映中国人民目前幸福生活和新中国面貌的中国民间舞蹈和音乐。他们对演出的每一个节目，都给以热烈的鼓掌和欢呼。
    在演出前，文化和国家指导部的一位负责官员雷伊致辞欢迎中国四川省歌舞团访问阿联。中国四川省歌舞团团长张铁生致答辞，表示希望通过歌舞团的访问，进一步加强两国人民之间的相互了解和友谊。
    在今天出席观看演出的埃及地区各阶层人士中有：埃及地区教育部长哈希姆，埃及地区文化和国家指导部次长，阿拉伯—中国友好协会秘书长欧默尔·马里，以及驻开罗的各国外交使节。
