Title: "Warsaw Autumn" Music Festival Concludes
Author:
Date: 1960-09-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, September 26th. The fourth "Warsaw Autumn" Music Festival, lasting nine days, closed in Warsaw on the 25th.

　　The music festival opened on the 17th. Countries participating included the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Japan, and France. During the music festival, a total of seventeen concerts and performances were held.

　　The Chinese cultural delegation headed by Shen Yanbing attended the opening ceremony of the music festival during their visit to Poland.

### “华沙之秋”音乐节闭幕

1960-09-27
第6版()
专栏：

    “华沙之秋”音乐节闭幕
    新华社华沙26日电　历时九天的第四届“华沙之秋”音乐节已于25日在华沙闭幕。
    音乐节是在17日开幕的。参加的国家有苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、日本、法国等。音乐节期间共举行了十七次音乐会和表演会。
    以沈雁冰为首的中国文化代表团在访问波兰期间，曾应邀参加音乐节的开幕式。
