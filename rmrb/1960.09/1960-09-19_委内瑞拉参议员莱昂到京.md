Title: Venezuelan Senator León Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 18th. Carlos Augusto León, Venezuelan Senator, member of the World Peace Council, and member of the Executive Committee of the Venezuela-China Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs for a visit to our country, together with his wife.

Welcoming them at the airport were Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and Vice President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General, and Shen Zijiu, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and others.

### 委内瑞拉参议员莱昂到京

1960-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    委内瑞拉参议员莱昂到京
    新华社18日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的委内瑞拉参议员、世界和平理事会理事、委内瑞拉中国之友协会执委会委员卡洛斯·奥古斯托·莱昂和他的夫人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会副会长、中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长胡愈之、副秘书长吴晓达，全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九等人。
