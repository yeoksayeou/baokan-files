Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into China's Airspace; 121st Serious Warning Issued

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 1

    U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into China's Airspace
    I Issue 121st Serious Warning

    Xinhua News Agency, 18th. At 10:20 a.m. to 10:26 a.m. on September 18th, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 121st serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我国领空  我提出121次严重警告

1960-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我国领空
    我提出121次严重警告
    新华社18日讯　9月18日十时二十分至十时二十六分，美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一二一次严重警告。
