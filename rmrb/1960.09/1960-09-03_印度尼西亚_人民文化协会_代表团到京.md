Title: Indonesian "People's Cultural Association" Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd. The delegation of the Indonesian "People's Cultural Association," invited to visit China by the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane today and on August 31st, led by the head of the delegation, Shaman Chaya.

### 印度尼西亚“人民文化协会”代表团到京

1960-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚“人民文化协会”代表团到京
    新华社2日讯　应中国印度尼西亚友好协会邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚“人民文化协会”代表团一行三人，由团长沙曼查雅率领，分别在今天和8月31日乘飞机到北京。
