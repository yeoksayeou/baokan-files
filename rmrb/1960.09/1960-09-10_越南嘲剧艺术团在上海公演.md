Title: Vietnamese Classical Drama Troupe Performs in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 9th: The Classical Drama Troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam gave its premiere performance at the Shanghai People's Grand Theatre on the evening of the 8th, performing the multi-act play "The Magic Water Bottle." This play, adapted from a Vietnamese folk tale, sings the praises of goodness and resistance to oppression, and was warmly welcomed by more than two thousand spectators.
    Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Mayor of Shanghai, and other party and government leaders of Shanghai, watched the troupe's wonderful performance.
    Also watching the performance was Tran Huy Lieu, head of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association delegation currently visiting Shanghai.
    During the intermission, Ke Qingshi met with Wu Qiu, head of the art troupe, Gao Jindian, deputy head, and leading actors, and had a cordial conversation. Tran Huy Lieu, head of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association delegation, was also present at the meeting.
    After the performance, Ke Qingshi and well-known figures from Shanghai's literary and art circles went on stage together to shake hands with the Vietnamese artists one by one, warmly congratulating them on the success of their performance.

### 越南嘲剧艺术团在上海公演

1960-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    越南嘲剧艺术团在上海公演
    新华社上海9日电　越南民主共和国嘲剧艺术团8日晚在上海人民大舞台举行首次公演，演出了多幕剧“神水瓶”。这个根据越南民间故事改编的、歌颂善良、反抗压迫的戏，受到全场两千多名观众的热烈欢迎。
    中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施和上海市其他党政领导人，观看了艺术团的精彩演出。
    观看演出的，还有正在上海访问的越中友协代表团团长陈辉燎。
    演出休息时，柯庆施接见了艺术团团长武秋、副团长高金典和主要演员，并进行了亲切的谈话。接见时，越中友协代表团团长陈辉燎也在座。
    演出结束后，柯庆施和上海文艺界著名人士等一起上台同越南艺术家们一一握手，热烈祝贺他们演出成功。
