Title: Castro to Lead Delegation to UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1960-09-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, September 13 - A press release from the Cuban Presidential Palace stated that the Cuban Revolutionary Government today appointed Prime Minister Fidel Castro as the head of the Cuban delegation to the United Nations General Assembly.

In the Prime Minister's absence, the delegation will be led by Foreign Minister Raúl Roa.

### 卡斯特罗将率领代表团出席联大

1960-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    卡斯特罗将率领代表团出席联大
    新华社哈瓦那13日电　古巴总统府的新闻公告说，古巴革命政府今天已经任命菲德尔·卡斯特罗总理为古巴出席联合国大会的代表团团长。
    在总理缺席时，代表团将由外交部长劳尔·罗亚领导。
