Title: Chinese News Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Chinese news delegation, led by Zhu Muzhi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association and Deputy Director of the Xinhua News Agency, returned to Beijing by plane this morning after visiting Chile, Bolivia, Uruguay, and Brazil in Latin America.

### 我国新闻代表团回到北京

1960-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    我国新闻代表团回到北京
    新华社11日讯　由中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席、新华通讯社副社长朱穆之同志率领的我国新闻工作者代表团，在先后访问了拉丁美洲的智利、玻利维亚、乌拉圭、巴西各国之后，已在今天上午乘飞机回到北京。
