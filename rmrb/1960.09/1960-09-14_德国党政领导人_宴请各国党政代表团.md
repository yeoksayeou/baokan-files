Title: German Party and State Leaders Host Banquet for Party and Government Delegations from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-09-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 12th: Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany; Otto Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic; Johannes Dieckmann, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber; and Erich Correns, Chairman of the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany, held a grand reception on the evening of the 12th to host the party and government delegations, government delegations, and fraternal party delegations from various countries who came to attend President Wilhelm Pieck's funeral.

### 德国党政领导人  宴请各国党政代表团

1960-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    德国党政领导人
    宴请各国党政代表团
    新华社柏林12日电　德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希、德意志民主共和国政府总理格罗提渥、人民议院主席团主席狄克曼和民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席柯伦斯，12日晚上举行盛大招待会，宴请前来参加威廉·皮克总统葬礼的各国党政代表团、政府代表团和兄弟党代表团。
