Title: U.S. Warships and Warplanes Again Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace I Lodge the 120th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 7th. From 21:11 on September 6th to 4:40 on the 7th, four U.S. warships intruded into the sea area south of Shanwei and Pinghai in Guangdong Province. From 8:27 to 8:39 on the 7th, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the aforementioned area. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to lodge the 120th serious warning.

### 美舰和美机又侵入我领海和领空  我提出第120次严重警告

1960-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    美舰和美机又侵入我领海和领空
    我提出第120次严重警告
    新华社7日讯　9月6日二十一时十一分至7日四时四十分，美国军舰四艘，侵入我广东省汕尾、平海以南地区海域。7日八时二十七分至八时三十九分美国军用飞机一架，侵入我上述地区上空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百二十次严重警告。
