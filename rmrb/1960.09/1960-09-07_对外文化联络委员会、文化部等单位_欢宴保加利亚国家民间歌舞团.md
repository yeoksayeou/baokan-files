Title: Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, Ministry of Culture and other units host banquet for Bulgarian National Folk Song and Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1960-09-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, the Ministry of Culture, and the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association held a banquet at the Beijing Hotel tonight to warmly welcome the Bulgarian National Folk Song and Dance Ensemble, headed by Filip Kutev, Secretary-General of the Bulgarian Composers Association and People's Artist.

Stoyev, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Bulgarian Embassy in China, and others were invited to attend tonight's banquet.

Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, spoke at the banquet. He first extended a warm welcome on behalf of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, the Ministry of Culture, and the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association to the comrades of the Bulgarian song and dance ensemble from the close fraternal country.

Qu Wu said that the Bulgarian National Folk Song and Dance Ensemble is one of the most prestigious song and dance ensembles in Bulgaria. The colorful song and dance art with excellent national traditions performed by you will surely be warmly welcomed by our audiences. He said that the friendship between the Chinese and Bulgarian people is profound, and this friendship cannot be shaken by any force. Qu Wu wished the song and dance ensemble success in its performances throughout China.

Filip Kutev, head of the song and dance ensemble, said in his speech that although China is a country far away from us, it is a close fraternal country to us in emotion. It is difficult for us to express our gratitude for your warm reception. We can only say that when we sing and dance in front of the Chinese people, we regard you as our dearest people, and we will convey the greetings of the Bulgarian people to the Chinese people through our songs and dances, and convey the wishes of the Bulgarian people that the hardworking, heroic, and creative Chinese people will always be happy.

The atmosphere at the banquet was filled with joy. The guests repeatedly toasted to the prosperity of Bulgaria's socialist art, to the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Bulgarian people, and to the great unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union. The Bulgarian comrades enthusiastically shouted in Bulgarian: "Eternal friendship, eternal friendship," and sang heartily: "Song of the Partisans" and other songs.

Those present at the banquet included: Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, Liu Ruilong, President of the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association, Nie Zhen, Vice President, Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, and famous figures from the capital's literary and art circles such as Zhao Feng, Zhou Weizhi, and Dai Ailian.

### 对外文化联络委员会、文化部等单位  欢宴保加利亚国家民间歌舞团

1960-09-07
第4版()
专栏：

    对外文化联络委员会、文化部等单位
    欢宴保加利亚国家民间歌舞团
    新华社6日讯　对外文化联络委员会、文化部、中国保加利亚友好协会今晚在北京饭店举行宴会，热烈欢迎以保加利亚作曲家协会总书记、人民演员菲利浦·库捷夫为首的保加利亚国家民间歌舞团。
    保加利亚驻中国大使馆临时代办斯托耶夫等应邀出席了今晚的宴会。
    对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武在宴会上讲话。他首先代表对外文化联络委员会、文化部、中国保加利亚友好协会向来自亲密的兄弟之邦的保加利亚歌舞团的同志们表示热烈的欢迎。
    屈武说，保加利亚国家民间歌舞团是保加利亚最负有声誉的歌舞团之一，你们所表演的具有民族优秀传统的、丰富多采的歌舞艺术定将得到我国观众热烈欢迎，他说，中保两国人民的友谊是深厚的，这种友谊是任何力量也不能动摇的。屈武预祝歌舞团在我国各地演出获得成功。
    歌舞团团长菲利浦·库捷夫在讲话中说，中国虽是距离我们遥远的国家，但在情感上却是我们亲近的兄弟之邦。我们很难表达出对你们这样热情招待的谢意，我们只能说，当我们在中国人民面前歌舞的时候，把你们看成我们最亲爱的人们，要通过我们的歌唱和舞蹈转达保加利亚全国人民向中国人民的问候，转达保加利亚人民祝勤劳英勇的具有创造性的中国人民永远幸福。
    席间，洋溢着一片欢乐气氛。宾主不断为保加利亚社会主义艺术事业的繁荣，为中保两国人民之间牢不可破的兄弟友谊，为以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的伟大团结干杯。保加利亚的同志们用保加利亚话热情地高呼：“永恒的友谊、永恒的友谊”，纵情地齐声高唱：“游击队之歌”等歌曲。
    出席宴会的有：文化部副部长夏衍，中国保加利亚友好协会会长刘瑞龙、副会长聂真，外交部副部长罗贵波，中国音乐家协会主席吕骥，以及首都文艺界著名人士赵沨、周巍峙、戴爱莲等。
