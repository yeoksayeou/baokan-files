Title: Hungarian Applied Arts Artist Kónya Kálmánné Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 16th - Hungarian applied arts artist, Kónya Kálmánné, who visited China according to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Hungarian cultural cooperation agreement, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to her country after completing her visit in China.

### 匈工艺美术家科尼亚·卡尔曼妮回国

1960-09-17
第6版()
专栏：

    匈工艺美术家科尼亚·卡尔曼妮回国
    新华社16日讯　根据中匈文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的匈牙利工艺美术家科尼亚·卡尔曼妮，在结束了在我国的访问之后，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
