Title: Polish Youth Travelers Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The Polish Youth Travelers Delegation, headed by Arletman Józef, Director of the International Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the Polish Socialist Youth Union, concluded its visit to our country and left Beijing by plane today to return home.
    The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League held a banquet on the 17th to bid farewell to the guests. Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, Wang Wei, Secretary of the Secretariat, and others attended the banquet.

### 波兰青年旅行者代表团回国

1960-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰青年旅行者代表团回国
    新华社18日讯　以波兰社会主义青年联盟中央委员会国际联络部部长阿力特曼·约瑟夫为首的波兰青年旅行者代表团结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    中国共产主义青年团中央委员会17日设宴欢送客人。中国共产主义青年团中央委员会第一书记胡耀邦、书记处书记王伟等人出席了宴会。
