Title: The Hundredth Meeting of Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, September 6th. The hundredth meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw at 2:00 PM today. This meeting lasted for two hours and thirty-five minutes.

After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would continue on October 18th (Tuesday) at 2:00 PM.

### 中美大使级一百次会谈

1960-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级一百次会谈
    新华社华沙6日电　中美大使级会谈第一百次会议今天下午二时在华沙举行。本次会谈历时二小时三十五分钟。
    会后双方宣布，下次会谈在10月18日（星期二）下午二时继续举行。
