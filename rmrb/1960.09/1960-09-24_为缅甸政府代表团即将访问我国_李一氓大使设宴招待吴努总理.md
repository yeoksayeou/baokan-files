Title: Ambassador Li Yimang Hosts a Banquet for Prime Minister U Nu in Honor of the Upcoming Visit of the Burmese Government Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 22nd. Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Li Yimang, held a banquet here tonight in honor of Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and the Burmese government delegation he is leading, who are about to visit China. They will travel to China to participate in the Chinese National Day celebrations and the signing ceremony of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty in Beijing.
    Prime Minister U Nu and his wife attended the banquet.
    Also present at the banquet were Foreign Minister and Minister for Shan State Affairs Sao Khun Kyi, Minister for Justice and Education U Aye Maung, Attorney General U Yan Aung, Union Government Minister for Kachin State Affairs Duwa Sinwa Nawng, Prime Minister’s Advisor U Wang, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs James Barrington, and other senior government officials.
    Brigadier General Maung Maung, head of the Burmese Military Goodwill Delegation to China, U Mya, head of the Burmese Trade Delegation to China, and U San Mya Wu, deputy head of the Burmese Cultural Goodwill Delegation to China, also attended the banquet.
    Other members of the Burmese government delegation to China were also present at the banquet. The banquet was held in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 为缅甸政府代表团即将访问我国　李一氓大使设宴招待吴努总理

1960-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    为缅甸政府代表团即将访问我国
　　李一氓大使设宴招待吴努总理
    新华社仰光22日电　中国驻缅甸大使李一氓今天晚上在这里举行宴会，招待缅甸总理吴努和他率领的即将访问中国的缅甸政府代表团。他们将前往中国参加中国国庆节和在北京签订的中缅边界条约的仪式。
    吴努总理和他的夫人出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有外交部长兼掸邦事务部长藻昆卓，司法和教育部长吴埃貌，检察总长吴延昂，联邦政府克钦邦事务部长杜瓦信瓦瑙，总理顾问吴旺，外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿，以及政府其他高级官员。
    缅甸访华军事友好代表团团长貌貌准将、缅甸访华贸易代表团团长吴妙、缅甸访华文化友好代表团副团长吴山伍也出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有缅甸政府访华代表团的其他团员。宴会在十分热烈和友好的气氛中进行。
