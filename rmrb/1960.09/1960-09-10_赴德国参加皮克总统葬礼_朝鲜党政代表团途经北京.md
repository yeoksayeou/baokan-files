Title: Korean Party and Government Delegation En Route to Germany for President Pieck's Funeral Transits Through Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: The delegation of the Korean Workers' Party and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Nam Il, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and Vice Premier of the Korean Cabinet, and Kim Tae-gun, member of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and Minister of Construction and Transportation of the Central Committee, left Beijing by plane at noon today for Berlin to attend the state funeral of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic. The Korean Party and Government delegation arrived in Beijing on the 8th from Pyongyang.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council; Liu Lantao, alternate member of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Saifudin , Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Burhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC; Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Li Dequan, Minister of Health and President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association; General Yang Yong of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; and Li Jiebo, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

Wandel, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and Li Yong-ho, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, also went to the airport to see them off. When the Korean Party and Government delegation arrived in Beijing on the 8th, Bo Yibo, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, welcomed them at the airport.

### 赴德国参加皮克总统葬礼  朝鲜党政代表团途经北京

1960-09-10
第6版()
专栏：

    赴德国参加皮克总统葬礼
    朝鲜党政代表团途经北京
    新华社9日讯　参加德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克同志国葬典礼的朝鲜劳动党和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团团长、朝鲜劳动党中央常务委员、朝鲜内阁副首相南日，代表团团员、朝鲜劳动党中央委员、党中央建设运输部部长金泰根，今天中午乘飞机离开北京前往柏林。朝鲜党政代表团是8日由平壤到达北京的。到飞机场送行的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅，中共中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长赛福鼎，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，外交部副部长罗贵波，卫生部部长、中朝友好协会会长李德全，中国人民解放军杨勇上将，对外文委副主任屈武，对外贸易部副部长林海云，中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯等。德意志民主共和国驻中国大使汪戴尔，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐，也到机场送行。朝鲜党政代表团8日到达北京时，中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理薄一波到飞机场迎接。
