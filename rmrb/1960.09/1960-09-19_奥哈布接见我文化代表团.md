Title: Ochab Receives Chinese Cultural Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, September 17th: Ochab, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee, received the Chinese cultural delegation headed by Cultural Minister Shen Yanbing this morning.
    Present at the reception were Polish Minister of Culture and Art, Galinski, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Wang Bingnan, and Cultural Counselor, Zhu Ze. They had a cordial conversation.

### 奥哈布接见我文化代表团

1960-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    奥哈布接见我文化代表团
    新华社华沙17日电　波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、书记处书记奥哈布，在今天上午接见了以文化部长沈雁冰为首的中国文化代表团。
    接见时在座的有波兰文化艺术部部长加林斯基，中国驻波兰大使王炳南和文化参赞朱仄等人。双方进行了亲切的交谈。
