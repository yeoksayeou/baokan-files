Title: China-Latin America Friendship Association Sends Congratulatory Telegram on the Establishment of the Cuba-China Friendship Association in Oriente Province, Cuba
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. The China-Latin America Friendship Association today sent a telegram to the Cuba-China Friendship Association in Oriente Province, Cuba, warmly congratulating Oriente Province on the upcoming establishment of the Cuba-China Friendship Association.

The telegram said: "We are delighted to learn that the Cuba-China Friendship Association of Oriente Province will soon be established in the heroic Sierra Maestra mountains. We extend our warm congratulations to you. The establishment of your association will further enhance the friendship between the people of our two countries.

"We wish the Cuban people continuous and great victories in their just struggle against U.S. imperialism!"

"Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Cuban people!"

### 中拉友协致电祝贺古巴奥连特省成立古中友协

1960-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    中拉友协致电祝贺古巴奥连特省成立古中友协
    新华社29日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会今天打电报给古巴奥连特省古巴中国友好协会，热烈祝贺奥连特省即将成立古中友协。
    电报说：“欣闻在英雄的马埃斯特腊山区即将成立奥连特省古巴中国友好协会，谨向你们热烈地祝贺。贵会的成立将进一步增进我们两国人民的友谊。
    “祝古巴人民在反对美帝国主义的正义斗争中不断地取得巨大的胜利！”
    “中古两国人民的友谊万岁！”
