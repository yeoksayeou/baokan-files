Title: Four Legal Professionals from Argentina, Peru, and Chile Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: Dr. Benito Marianetti, a famous Argentine lawyer, and Mr. Angel Bustelo, a famous lawyer, as well as Dr. Manuel Ravat, Secretary General of the National Federation of Lawyers' Associations of Peru, and his wife, and Dr. Jorge Siles, a famous Chilean lawyer, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society for a visit to our country.
　　Zhang Zhiran, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Society, and others went to the airport to welcome the guests.

### 阿根廷秘鲁智利四位法律工作者到京

1960-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿根廷秘鲁智利四位法律工作者到京
    新华社22日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷名律师贝尼托·马里亚内蒂博士和名律师安赫耳·布斯特洛，以及秘鲁律师公会全国联合会总书记曼努埃尔·拉瓦特博士和夫人，智利名律师豪尔赫·希莱斯博士，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    中国政治法律学会副会长张志让等前往机场欢迎客人。
