Title: Vietnamese Classical Drama Troupe to Visit and Perform in Various Locations
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 5th—After giving five performances in Beijing, the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Classical Drama Troupe left this morning to visit and perform in Shanghai, Changsha, Guangzhou and other places. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and more than one hundred people from the capital's literary and art circles, including Ma Yanxiang.

### 越嘲剧艺术团去各地访问演出

1960-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    越嘲剧艺术团去各地访问演出
    据新华社5日讯　越南民主共和国嘲剧艺术团在北京进行了五场演出之后，于今日上午去上海、长沙、广州等地访问演出。前往车站送行的，有对外文委副主任屈武，首都文艺界人士马彦祥等一百多人。
