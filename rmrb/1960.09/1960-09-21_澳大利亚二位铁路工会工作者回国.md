Title: Two Australian Railway Union Workers Return Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 20th: Richard James Bohlen, Chairman of the Victorian Branch of the Australian Railway Union, and William Raymond Busson, Secretary of the Southwestern Region of the Queensland Branch of the Australian Railway Union, concluded their visit to China and left Beijing by plane today to return home. Wang Zhijie, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Union, and Xu Ping, Vice Chairman, were at the airport to see them off.

### 澳大利亚二位铁路工会工作者回国

1960-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

　  澳大利亚二位铁路工会工作者回国
    新华社20日讯　澳大利亚铁路工会维多利亚州分会主席理查德·詹姆斯·波林和澳大利亚铁路工会昆士兰州西南地区书记威廉·雷蒙·布森，结束了在我国的参观访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有中国铁路工会全国委员会主席王志杰、副主席徐平等。
