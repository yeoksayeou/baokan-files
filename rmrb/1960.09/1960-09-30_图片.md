　　The Algerian Art Troupe, which came to China for a performance visit, arrived in Beijing under the leadership of Afa Abd El-Mati, the representative of the Provisional Government of Algeria stationed with the Art Troupe, and Benlahreche Boualem, the head of the troupe. Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, and others welcomed them at the airport. The third person from the left in the front row is Afa Abd El-Mati, and the fourth person is Benlahreche Boualem.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Gu Songnian photo
