Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Meets President Touré
Author:
Date: 1960-09-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, met with the President of the Republic of Guinea, Sékou Touré, and all members of the Government Delegation of the Republic of Guinea this afternoon, and had a friendly conversation.

Also present at the meeting were Diallo Saifoulaye, President of the National Assembly of the Republic of Guinea; Louis Lansana Béavogui, Minister of National Economy; Keita N'Famara, Minister of Planning; Diallo Mouctar, Political Secretary of the Guinea Democratic Party, Conakry Second District Committee; André Sassine, Chief of Protocol of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Also present at the meeting were: Dong Biwu, Vice-Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, Cheng Qian, and Lin Feng, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; He Long and Chen Yi, Vice-Premiers of the State Council; Zhang Zhizhong, Vice-Chairman of the National Defense Council; Zhang Hanfu, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ke Hua, Chinese Ambassador to Guinea; He Ying, Director of the Department of West Asian and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 刘少奇主席会见杜尔总统

1960-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席会见杜尔总统
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇在今日下午会见了几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔和几内亚共和国政府代表团全体成员，并进行了友好的谈话。
    几内亚共和国国民议会议长迪阿洛·塞福拉耶，国民经济部长路易·兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉，计划部长凯塔·恩法马拉，几内亚民主党科纳克里二区区委政治书记迪阿洛·穆克塔，外交部礼宾司长安德烈·萨松等都参加了会见。
    会见时在座的有：中华人民共和国副主席董必武，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、陈叔通、程潜、林枫，国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，国防委员会副主席张治中，外交部副部长章汉夫，以及我国驻几内亚大使柯华，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英、礼宾司司长俞沛文。
