Title: Promoting Cultural Exchange and Traditional Friendship Between China and Nepal: Our Art Troupe's First Performance in Kathmandu Attended by the King and Prime Minister of Nepal, Leaders of Various Political Parties, and Others
Author:
Date: 1960-09-16
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, September 15th: The China Youth Art Troupe gave its first performance at the Lion Palace in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, on the 14th.

    Those present included the King and Queen of Nepal, Prince Himalaya and Princess, and other members of the royal family; Prime Minister Koirala and his wife, the Deputy Prime Minister and other cabinet members; speakers of both the upper and lower houses of parliament, the Commander-in-Chief of the Army, the Chief Justice and other high-ranking civil and military officials, as well as leaders of various political parties, prominent figures from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Nepal, totaling more than 400 people.

    Education Minister Chaudhuri welcomed the China Youth Art Troupe on behalf of the government of the Kingdom of Nepal. He said that Nepal and China have a long history of friendship and friendly cultural relations. He firmly believes that cultural exchange between Nepal and China will help promote friendly relations between the two countries.

    Wen Fei, head of the China Youth Art Troupe, extended the most solemn respect to the government and people of the Kingdom of Nepal on behalf of all members of the art troupe. He said that the China Youth Art Troupe is visiting and performing in Nepal with friendly wishes and a desire to learn from our Nepalese friends. This visit will not only help cultural exchange between China and Nepal, but will also promote the traditional friendship between the people of our two countries.

    Following this, the art troupe performed the Peking Opera "San Cha Kou" and "Havoc in the Dragon Palace," as well as Chinese folk songs and dances, ancient Chinese magic, and famous Nepalese songs and dances. Every program was warmly welcomed by the audience.

    After the performance, the King of Nepal, accompanied by Prime Minister Koirala, Ambassador Zhang Shijie, and head of the troupe Wen Fei, went on stage to shake hands with all the performers of the art troupe and expressed his gratitude for their wonderful performance.

    The art troupe's performances in Kathmandu will continue until September 25.

    The proceeds from the performances will be used for the Prime Minister's Fund of Nepal.

### 促进中尼两国文化交流和传统友谊  我艺术团在加德满都首次演出  尼泊尔国王和首相以及各政党领袖等出席观看

1960-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

    促进中尼两国文化交流和传统友谊
    我艺术团在加德满都首次演出
    尼泊尔国王和首相以及各政党领袖等出席观看
    新华社15日讯　加德满都消息：中国青年艺术团14日在尼泊尔首都加德满都狮宫举行首次演出。
    出席观看的有尼泊尔国王和王后，喜马拉雅亲王和王妃以及王室其它成员；首相柯伊拉腊和夫人，副首相和其它内阁成员；上下议院的议长，陆军总司令、首席法官和其它高级文武官员，以及各政党领袖、各界著名人士和各国驻尼泊尔外交使节共四百多人。
    教育大臣乔杜里代表尼泊尔王国政府对中国青年艺术团表示欢迎。他说，尼泊尔和中国具有悠久的友谊和友好的文化关系。他深信，尼泊尔和中国之间的文化交流将有助于促进两国之间的友好关系。
    中国青年艺术团团长文菲代表艺术团的全体团员对尼泊尔王国政府和人民致以最崇高的敬意。他说，中国青年艺术团是抱着友好的愿望和向我们的尼泊尔朋友学习的要求到尼泊尔来进行访问和演出的。这次访问不仅有助于中国和尼泊尔之间的文化交流，而且将促进我们两国人民之间的传统友谊。
    接着，艺术团演出了京剧“三岔口”和“闹龙宫”以及中国民间歌舞、中国古代魔术和尼泊尔著名歌舞。每一个节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    演出后，尼泊尔国王在柯伊拉腊首相和张世杰大使以及文菲团长陪同下，上台同艺术团的全体演员握手，并对他们的精彩演出表示感谢。
    艺术团在加德满都的演出将继续到9月25日。
    演出的收入将充作尼泊尔首相的基金。
