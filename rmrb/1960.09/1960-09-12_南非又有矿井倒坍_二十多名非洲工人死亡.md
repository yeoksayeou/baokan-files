Title: Another Mine Collapse in South Africa: Over Twenty African Workers Die
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 10th Dispatch from Johannesburg: Another mine collapse occurred recently in the Witwatersrand mines near Johannesburg, South Africa. Twelve African miners were buried in a mine 7,000 feet deep. It is believed that they are dead. The report said that another thirteen African miners were killed by falling rocks during the mine collapse. In the Union of South Africa, due to the authorities' disregard for the lives of Africans, mine collapses causing deaths occur frequently.

### 南非又有矿井倒坍  二十多名非洲工人死亡

1960-09-12
第6版()
专栏：

    南非又有矿井倒坍
    二十多名非洲工人死亡
    据新华社10日讯　约翰内斯堡消息：南非约翰内斯堡附近韦特瓦特斯兰德矿最近发生矿井倒坍事件，有十二名非洲矿工被埋在七千英尺深的矿井下面。人们认为，他们已经死亡。消息说，在矿井倒坍时，另有十三名非洲矿工被倒下的岩石砸死。在南非联邦，由于当局漠视非洲人的生命，经常发生矿井倒坍造成死亡的事件。
