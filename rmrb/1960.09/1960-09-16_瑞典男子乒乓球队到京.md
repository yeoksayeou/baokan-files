Title: Swedish Men's Table Tennis Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: The Swedish men's table tennis team, a delegation of four people led by team leader Karl Albert Rabe, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of our country for a friendly visit and competition. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by representatives of the All-China Sports Federation and Chinese table tennis players. The visiting team will first go to Guangzhou on the 16th and is scheduled to hold its first friendly match in China there on the 18th with the Guangdong Table Tennis Team. Afterwards, they will also visit Shanghai and Beijing.

### 瑞典男子乒乓球队到京

1960-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

    瑞典男子乒乓球队到京
    据新华社15日讯　应邀来我国进行访问比赛的瑞典男子乒乓球队一行四人，今天由领队卡尔·艾伯特·拉本率领乘飞机到达北京。他们在飞机场受到中华全国体育总会的代表和中国乒乓球运动员们的热烈欢迎。客队16日将先去广州，预定18日在那里同广东乒乓球队举行访华第一次友谊比赛。以后，他们还将访问上海和北京。
