Title: Burmese News Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The Burmese news delegation of twelve people, led by U Khin, Deputy Director of the Burmese News Bureau, who accompanied Premier U Nu on his visit to our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

At the Capital Airport, the distinguished Burmese guests were warmly welcomed by Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Li Wu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association, Wang Jiyuan, Deputy Director of the China News Service, and people from the capital's press circles. A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to the distinguished Burmese guests.

### 缅甸新闻代表团到京

1960-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    缅甸新闻代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　随同吴努总理来我国访问的以缅甸新闻局副局长吴钦为首的缅甸新闻代表团一行十二人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    缅甸贵宾们在首都机场上，受到外交部新闻司副司长康矛召，中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处书记李伍，中国新闻社副社长王纪元，以及首都新闻界人士的热烈欢迎。一群少先队员向缅甸贵宾们献了鲜花。
