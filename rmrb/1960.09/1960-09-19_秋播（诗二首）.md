Title: Autumn Planting (Two Poems)
Author: Sù Hègāo
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 8

    Autumn Planting (Two Poems)	Shuangjiang Commune, Tongdao Dong Autonomous County, Hunan Province, Sù Hègāo
        Mountain Song
    October in Hunan is spring everywhere,
    Sweet potato seedlings are tender green, late rice is verdant,
    The autumn planting red flag laughs atop the slope,
    Waves of mountain songs circle white clouds.
    Young lads shout to the oxen leading the way,
    Girls follow closely, smiles on their faces.
    Sowing, ah, sowing,
    Cultivating, ah, cultivating...
    Mountain songs rise from the ground,
    Commune members are all singing folk,
    Worker Brother comes to accompany—
    The planter is a grand piano.
        Rushing to Plant
    Eighth month, osmanthus fragrance,
    Ninth month, chrysanthemums yellow,
    The sky is high, the air is crisp, wild geese fly south,
    Autumn planting is a good time.
    In the daytime, smoke obscures the sun,
    At night, fires fill the ridges,
    Burning the fields and applying tens of thousands of dan of fertilizer,
    Commune members are busy planting.
    Sow a handful of seeds early,
    Reap a thousand baskets of grain more,
    The eighth and ninth months are worth more than gold,
    Don't wait for the frost to fall!

### 秋播（诗二首）
粟鹤皋
1960-09-19
第8版()
专栏：

    秋播（诗二首）	湖南通道侗族自治县双江公社   粟鹤皋
        山歌
    十月湖南遍地春，
    薯苗嫩绿晚禾青，
    秋播红旗坡顶笑，
    阵阵山歌绕白云。
    小伙子吆牛前边走，
    姑娘们含笑紧相跟。
    播种啊播种，
    耕耘啊耕耘……
    山歌声声平地起，
    社员都是唱歌人，
    工人大哥来伴奏——
    播种机是架大钢琴。
        赶播
    八月丹桂香，
    九月菊花黄，
    天高气爽雁南飞，
    秋播好时光。
    白天烟遮日，
    夜晚火满岗，
    烧垦再施肥万担，
    社员播种忙。
    早撒一把籽，
    多收千箩粮，
    八九两月金不换，
    莫待寒霜降！
