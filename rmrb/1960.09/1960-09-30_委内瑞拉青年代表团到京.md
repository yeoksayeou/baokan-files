Title: Venezuelan Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 29th - The Venezuelan Youth Delegation, led by the head of the delegation José Anael Branco Burns, arrived in Beijing today at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation for a visit to our country. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Zhang Chao, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, Wang Zhaohua, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation.

### 委内瑞拉青年代表团到京

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

    委内瑞拉青年代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的委内瑞拉青年代表团在团长何塞·阿纳尔·伯朗哥·勃恩斯的率领下，今天到达北京。到机场欢迎的有全国青联副主席张超，共青团中央书记处书记王照华，全国学联主席胡启立等人。
