Title: Togolese Labor Youth League Chairman Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: Kouakouvi Anjena, Chairman of the Togolese Labor Youth League, who was invited to visit China by the All-China Youth Federation, concluded his visit to China and left Beijing by plane on the 14th to return to his country.

### 多哥劳动青年联盟主席回国

1960-09-17
第6版()
专栏：

    多哥劳动青年联盟主席回国
    新华社15日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的多哥劳动青年联盟主席库阿库维·昂热纳，结束了在中国的访问，14日乘飞机离开北京回国。
