Title: Vice Chairman Song Receives Chairman of the Indonesian Party

Author:
Date: 1960-09-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 27th: Song Qingling, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, received Asmara Hadi, Chairman of the Indonesian Party, and his wife, Asnawi Said, Secretary-General of the Indonesian Party, who are currently visiting Shanghai, this afternoon, and had cordial and friendly talks with them. Present at the reception were Liu Shuzhou, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, Zhang Yaohui, Director of the Shanghai Council of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and Deputy Secretary-General of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, and others.
    Asmara Hadi and his party arrived in Shanghai on the 25th from Nanjing. (Attached photo)
    Vice Chairman Song Qingling meets with Asmara Hadi (second from right), Chairman of the Indonesian Party, in Shanghai. The photo shows a group photo after the meeting. (Fax photo) by Zhang Ping, Xinhua News Agency reporter.

### 宋副主席接见印度尼西亚党总主席

1960-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

　  宋副主席接见印度尼西亚党总主席
    新华社上海27日电　中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄，今天下午接见了正在上海访问的印度尼西亚党总主席阿斯马腊·哈迪和他的夫人、印度尼西亚党秘书长阿斯纳威·赛义德，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的交谈。接见时在座的，有上海市副市长刘述周，中国人民外交学会在沪理事、上海市人民委员会副秘书长张耀辉等。
    阿斯马腊·哈迪等是在25日从南京到达上海的。（附图片）
