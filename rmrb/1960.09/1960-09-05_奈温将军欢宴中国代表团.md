Title: General Ne Win Hosts Banquet for Chinese Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 3rd: General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Chinese delegation of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, headed by Yao Zhongming.

    Among those present at the banquet were Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Kyi, Minister for Trade Development U Tun, Minister for Kachin State Affairs Duwa Sinwa Nawng, Attorney General U Yan Aung, Deputy Chief of Staff (Air Force) of the Defense Forces Brigadier General Clift, former Foreign Minister U Chan Tun Aung, Brigadier General Tin Pe, Director of Military Training Bureau Colonel Maung Maung, Director of Military Intelligence Department of the Defense Forces Lieutenant Colonel Lun, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs James Barrington, Executive Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Soe Tin, Director of the Asian and African Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Ba Chyi, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Ba Maung, and other senior military officers.

    Also present at the banquet were Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Chief Burmese Representative of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, as well as other Burmese representatives and advisors.

    Also present at the banquet were Lei Yang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, embassy counselors, and Colonel Jiang Keding, military attaché.

    The banquet was filled with a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

    After the banquet, the Chinese guests watched a song and dance performance.

### 奈温将军欢宴中国代表团

1960-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    奈温将军欢宴中国代表团
    新华社仰光3日电　缅甸国防军总参谋长奈温将军今晚举行宴会，欢迎以姚仲明为首的中缅边界联合委员会中国代表团。
    出席宴会的有缅甸外交部长藻昆卓、贸易发展部长吴敦、克钦邦事务部长杜瓦信瓦瑙、检察总长吴延昂、国防军副总参谋长（空军）克利夫特准将、前外交部长吴千吞昂、丁佩准将、军事训练局局长貌貌上校、国防军军事情报处处长伦中校、外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿、外交部执行秘书吴苏丁、外交部亚非司司长吴巴漆、外交部礼宾司司长吴巴茂和其他高级军官。
    出席宴会的还有中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表昂季准将以及其他缅甸代表和顾问。
    出席宴会的还有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬、大使馆的参赞们和武官蒋克定上校。
    宴会上充满亲切友好的气氛。
    宴会以后，中国贵宾观看了歌舞演出。
