Title: Czechoslovak New Ambassador Meets with Foreign Minister Chen Yi to Discuss Presentation of Credentials
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 24th - Josef Sedivy, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic to China, met with Foreign Minister Chen Yi this morning to discuss matters relating to the presentation of his credentials. Present at the meeting were Ge Buhai, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet Union and Eastern European Affairs.

### 捷新任大使会见陈毅外长  商谈递交国书事宜

1960-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    捷新任大使会见陈毅外长
　  商谈递交国书事宜
    新华社24日讯　捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国新任驻华特命全权大使约瑟夫·赛迪维，今天上午会见我外交部长陈毅，商谈递交国书事宜。会见时在座的有外交部礼宾司副司长葛步海和苏联东欧司副司长余湛。
