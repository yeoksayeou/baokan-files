Title: On the Cuban People's National Assembly's Decision to Establish Diplomatic Relations with China, the Cuba-China Friendship Association Cables Premier Zhou to Express Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1960-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 7th: The Cuba-China Friendship Association yesterday cabled Premier Zhou Enlai to express congratulations on the Cuban People's National Assembly's decision to establish diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China.

The cable stated, "The Cuba-China Friendship Association, reflecting the feelings of all the Cuban people, expresses fraternal friendship to the Chinese government and people, and expresses heartfelt joy for the resolution made by the Cuban People's National Assembly presided over by Fidel Castro in the Havana Declaration, especially the decision to establish diplomatic relations with the heroic People's Republic of China.

"We also reiterate our joy for the unconditional support and assistance offered by a government representing 650 million Chinese people, as recently expressed by you and reiterated by Vice Premier Bo Yibo on the 1st and 2nd. We have long known that when Cuba secures political sovereignty, establishing relations with your country on the basis of mutual respect is essential for the development and progress of both countries. We respectfully offer you our congratulations."

The congratulatory telegram was signed by the National Executive Committee of the Cuba-China Friendship Association.

The Cuba-China Friendship Association also sent a congratulatory letter to Cuban Premier Castro for the Cuban People's National Assembly's decision to establish diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China.

### 就古巴人民全国大会决定同中国建交  古中友协电周总理表示祝贺

1960-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    就古巴人民全国大会决定同中国建交
    古中友协电周总理表示祝贺
    新华社哈瓦那7日电　古巴—中国友好协会昨天就古巴人民全国大会通过的同中华人民共和国建立外交关系的决定致电周恩来总理表示祝贺。
    电文说，“古巴—中国友好协会反映全体古巴人民的感情，向中国政府和人民表示兄弟般的友情，并为菲德尔·卡斯特罗所主持的古巴人民全国大会在哈瓦那宣言中作出的决议、特别是同英雄的中华人民共和国建立外交关系的决定，表示衷心的喜悦。
      “我们并再一次表示为您最近所表示的和您在2日以及薄一波副总理在1日所重申的一个代表六亿五千万中国人民的政府提供的无条件的支持和援助而感到的喜悦心情。我们早就知道，当古巴掌握了政治主权的时候，和您的国家在互相尊重的基础上建立关系，对两国的发展和进步是必不可少的。我们尊敬地向您表示祝贺。”
    签署这份贺电的是古巴—中国友好协会全国执行委员会。
    古巴—中国友好协会也为古巴人民全国大会通过同中华人民共和国建立外交关系的决议，向古巴总理卡斯特罗发出了祝贺信”。
