International Briefs

    Colombian Bank Employees Strike
    Bogota Dispatch: 14,000 Colombian bank employees began a strike on the 13th to protest the employer's disregard for their union rights. As a result of the strike, many banks in Bogota, Barranquilla, Cartagena, Bucaramanga, and other cities have ceased operations.

    France Announces Recognition of Senegal
    Paris Dispatch: After engineering the split of the Mali Federation, France has decided to recognize the Republic of Senegal, which recently announced its withdrawal from the Mali Federation, and has decided to recommend its admission to the United Nations.

### 国际简讯

1960-09-17
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    哥伦比亚银行职工罢工
    波哥大讯　一万四千名哥伦比亚银行职工为抗议资方漠视他们的工会权利，从13日开始罢工。由于这次罢工，波哥大、巴兰基雅、喀他亨那、布卡拉曼加以及其他城市的许多银行都停止了营业。
    法国宣布承认塞内加尔
    巴黎讯　法国在制造马里联邦的分裂之后，已经决定承认最近宣布退出马里联邦的塞内加尔共和国，并且决定介绍它进入联合国。
