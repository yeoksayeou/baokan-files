Title: Ambassador He Wei Hosts Banquet for Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association Delegation's Visit to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-09-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 13th. Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, He Wei, held a banquet tonight for the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association delegation, which is visiting to participate in the 15th anniversary celebration of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and to conduct a friendly visit.

Invited to the banquet were Nguyen Xuan Thuy, Acting President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association; Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace; Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Hanoi Administrative Committee; Yin Jishen, Chairman of the Hanoi Fatherland Front; and Nguyen Van Huyen and Nguyen Thi Luu, members of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association.

Huang Mei, Deputy Director of the Asian-African Department of the Vietnamese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Vu Quoc Vy, Director of the Vietnamese Foreign Cultural Liaison Department, were also invited to the banquet.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 为中越友协代表团访越  何伟大使举行宴会

1960-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    为中越友协代表团访越
    何伟大使举行宴会
    据新华社河内13日电　中国驻越南大使何伟，今天晚上为前来参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年盛典，并进行友好访问的中越友好协会代表团举行宴会。
    应邀参加宴会的有越中友好协会代理会长阮春水，越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探，河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴、河内市祖国战线主席尹继善，越中友好协会委员阮文煊、阮氏榴等人。
    越南外交部亚非司副司长黄梅和越南对外文化联络司司长武国威也应邀参加了宴会。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
