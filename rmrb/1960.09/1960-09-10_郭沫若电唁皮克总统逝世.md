Title: Guo Moruo Sends Condolences on the Death of President Pieck
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, today sent a telegram to W. Hack, President of the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic, expressing his deep condolences on the death of President Pieck. The telegram is as follows:

Comrade W. Hack, President of the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic:

I was shocked to learn of the unfortunate death of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic. On behalf of all Chinese scientific workers, I extend my deepest condolences. Comrade Pieck will live forever!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Guo Moruo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 9, 1960

### 郭沫若电唁皮克总统逝世

1960-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若电唁皮克总统逝世
    新华社9日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今天致电德意志民主共和国科学院院长维·哈克，对皮克总统的逝世表示沉痛哀悼。电文如下：德意志民主共和国科学院院长维·哈克同志：
    惊悉德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克同志不幸逝世，谨代表中国全体科学工作者致以沉痛的哀悼，皮克同志永垂不朽！
                                中国科学院院长　郭沫若
                                                1960年9月9日
