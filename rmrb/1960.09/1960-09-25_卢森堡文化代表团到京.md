Title: Luxembourg Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 24th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, a six-member Luxembourg cultural delegation, led by Mr. Jones, a well-known Luxembourg doctor, arrived in Beijing today for a visit to our country. Huang Dingchen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Medical Association, and others were at the airport to greet them.

### 卢森堡文化代表团到京

1960-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　  卢森堡文化代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，前来我国参观访问的卢森堡文化代表团一行六人，在团长、卢森堡著名医生约内斯的率领下，今日到达北京。到机场迎接的，有中华医学会副秘书长黄鼎臣等人。
