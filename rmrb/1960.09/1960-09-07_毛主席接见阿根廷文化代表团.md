Title: Chairman Mao Receives Argentine Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Chairman Mao Zedong today received Professor Carlos Astrada, head of the Argentine Cultural Delegation currently visiting our country, and delegation members Carlos Alberto Mogli, Nezah Nathaniel Godelman, and Oscar Alfredo Pizarro. Chairman Mao had a cordial conversation with the guests.

Present at the reception were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and Zhou Erfu, Vice President. (Attached photo)

Chairman Mao Zedong received the Argentine Cultural Delegation on September 6. The picture shows a group photo after the meeting. The fourth person from the left is the head of the delegation, Professor Carlos Astrada.
Xinhua News Agency reporters Hou Bo and Meng Qingbiao

### 毛主席接见阿根廷文化代表团

1960-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见阿根廷文化代表团
    新华社6日讯　毛泽东主席今天接见了正在我国访问的阿根廷文化代表团团长卡洛斯·阿斯特拉达教授和代表团团员：卡洛斯·阿尔贝托·莫格利亚、奈萨奈尔·哥德尔曼、奥斯卡·阿尔弗雷多·彼萨罗。毛主席同客人们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的有：中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南、副会长周而复。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席9月6日接见了阿根廷文化代表团。图为接见后合影。左起第四人是团长卡洛斯·阿斯特拉达教授。
      新华社记者　侯波、孟庆彪摄
