Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Hussein, Chairman of the Greater Somalia League
Author:
Date: 1960-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Premier Zhou Enlai received Haji Muhammad Hussein, Chairman of the Greater Somalia League, and Abdulehi Hashi, a member of the Central Committee of the Greater Somalia League, this afternoon, and had friendly talks with them.

Present at the reception were Liu Changsheng, He Ying, and Zhu Ziqi. (Attached photo)

Premier Zhou Enlai received Haji Muhammad Hussein, Chairman of the Greater Somalia League, and Abdulehi Hashi, a member of the Central Committee of the Greater Somalia League, who are visiting our country, on September 6. The picture shows the scene during the reception.

Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

### 周总理接见大索马里联盟主席侯赛因

1960-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见大索马里联盟主席侯赛因
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理在今天下午接见了大索马里联盟主席哈吉·穆罕默德·侯赛因和大索马里联盟中央委员阿布杜勒希·哈希，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有刘长胜、何英、朱子奇。（附图片）
    周恩来总理九月六日接见了正在我国访问的大索马里联盟主席哈吉·穆罕默德·侯赛因和大索马里联盟中央委员阿布杜勒希·哈希。图为接见时情形。
                                     新华社记者　杜修贤摄
