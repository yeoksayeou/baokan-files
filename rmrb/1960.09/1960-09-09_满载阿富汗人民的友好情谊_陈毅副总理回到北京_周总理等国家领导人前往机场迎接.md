Title: Fully Loaded with the Friendly Affection of the Afghan People, Vice Premier Chen Yi Returns to Beijing; Premier Zhou and Other National Leaders Go to the Airport to Greet Him
Author:
Date: 1960-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Having visited the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Marshal Chen Yi, returned to Beijing by special plane at noon today, full of the friendly affection of the Afghan people.

Premier Zhou Enlai went to the airport to greet him. Also present at the airport to welcome him were Vice Premiers of the State Council Li Xiannian and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Fu Zuoyi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council Zhang Yan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Luo Guibo, and Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of National Defense General Tan Zheng, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping, and people from various fields.

As Vice Premier Chen Yi stepped out of the airport, the welcomers burst into warm applause, congratulating Vice Premier Chen Yi on the great success of his visit abroad. Young Pioneers presented Vice Premier Chen Yi, as well as Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council Kong Yuan and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Geng Biao, who accompanied him on the visit, with flowers.

Also present at the airport today were Aqim, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Afghan Embassy in China, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 8th. Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Marshal Chen Yi, who returned victoriously after visiting the Kingdom of Afghanistan, left Lanzhou for Beijing by special plane on the morning of September 8th, after staying in Lanzhou for two days on his way back to Beijing. The main responsible persons of the Party, government, and military of Gansu Province and Lanzhou City all went to the airport to see him off.

### 满载阿富汗人民的友好情谊  陈毅副总理回到北京  周总理等国家领导人前往机场迎接

1960-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    满载阿富汗人民的友好情谊
    陈毅副总理回到北京
    周总理等国家领导人前往机场迎接
    新华社8日讯　访问阿富汗王国以后，回国的国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅，满载阿富汗人民的友好情谊，于今天中午乘专机回到北京。
    周恩来总理到飞机场迎接。到机场迎接的，还有国务院副总理李先念、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，国防委员会副主席傅作义，国务院外事办公室副主任张彦，外交部副部长章汉夫、罗贵波、姬鹏飞，国防部副部长谭政大将，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，对外文委代主任张致祥，北京市副市长冯基平等各方面人士。
    陈毅副总理步出机场时，欢迎的人们发出热烈的掌声，祝贺陈毅副总理这次出国访问获得巨大的成功。少年先锋队员向陈毅副总理和随同访问回来的国务院外事办公室副主任孔原、外交部副部长耿飚，献了鲜花。
    今天到机场欢迎的，还有阿富汗驻中国大使馆临时代办阿基姆以及各国驻中国的使节和外交官员。
    据新华社兰州8日电　访问阿富汗王国胜利归国的国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅，返回北京途中在兰州逗留了两天以后，已于9月8日上午乘专机离开兰州前往北京。甘肃省和兰州市的党政军主要负责人都到机场欢送。
