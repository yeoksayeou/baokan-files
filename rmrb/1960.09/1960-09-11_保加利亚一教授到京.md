Title: Bulgarian Professor Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Professor Dimitar Petrov Volkov of the Sofia Georgi Dimitrov Agricultural Academy, who is visiting China for investigation according to the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane yesterday. Liu Ziyu, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education, greeted him at the airport.

### 保加利亚一教授到京

1960-09-11
第3版()
专栏：

    保加利亚一教授到京
    新华社10日讯　根据中保文化合作协定1960年执行计划前来我国考察的索非亚格奥尔基·季米特洛夫农学院迪米特尔·彼得洛夫·沃尔科夫教授，昨天乘飞机到达北京。教育部办公厅副主任刘子余到机场迎接。
