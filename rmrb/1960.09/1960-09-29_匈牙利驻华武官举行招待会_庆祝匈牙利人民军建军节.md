Title: Hungarian Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the Hungarian People's Army
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 3
　
Xinhua News Agency, 28th: Lieutenant Colonel Mashi Ferenc, Military Attaché of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, and his wife, held a reception tonight to celebrate the anniversary of the founding of the Hungarian People's Army.

General Tan Zheng, Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, was invited to attend the reception. He extended the warmest festive greetings on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to all officers and soldiers of the fraternal Hungarian People's Army. In his toast, General Tan Zheng said, "May the Hungarian People's Army achieve greater success in safeguarding the security of its own country, combating the imperialist war of aggression conspiracy, and defending world peace; for the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the people and armies of China and Hungary; for the great unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union; and for world peace!"

In his toast at the reception, Lieutenant Colonel Mashi Ferenc said that all officers and soldiers of the Hungarian People's Army are pleased to be able to fight shoulder to shoulder with the glorious Soviet Army, the powerful Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the armies of other fraternal socialist countries to defend world peace. He proposed a toast to the unbreakable fighting friendship between the Hungarian People's Army and the Chinese People's Liberation Army; to the eternal fraternal friendship between the peoples of Hungary and China, and to the unity and consolidation of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

Ferenc Martin, Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, attended the reception.

Also invited to attend the reception tonight were General Gan Siqi, General Chen Bojun, Lieutenant General Yuan Ziqin, Lieutenant General Wang Huiqiu of the Air Force, Major General Shen Qixian of the Air Force, Major General Duan Dechang of the Navy, Major General Zhang Hancheng of the Navy, Major General Li Xin, Major General Zhang Zizhen, Major General Liao Jingxiang, Major General Cheng Shiqing, Major General Li Dun, Major General Zhang Zhengguang, Major General Xu Shaohua, Major General Su Huanqing, Major General Sun Yizhi, Major General Gao Tigan, Major General Tao Hanzhang, Major General Sun Chaoqun, Major General Tang Tingbo, Major General Zhu Guang, and other senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as more than 70 representatives of officers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force stationed in Beijing.

Also invited to attend the reception were Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Wan Li, Deputy Mayor of Beijing.

Ambassadors of socialist countries to China and military attachés of various countries' embassies in China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 匈牙利驻华武官举行招待会　庆祝匈牙利人民军建军节

1960-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    匈牙利驻华武官举行招待会
　　庆祝匈牙利人民军建军节
    新华社28日讯　匈牙利人民共和国驻华武官马希·费伦茨中校及夫人今晚举行招待会，庆祝匈牙利人民军建军节。
    中国人民解放军总政治部主任谭政大将应邀出席了招待会，他代表中国人民解放军全体指战员向兄弟的匈牙利人民军全体官兵，致以最热烈的节日祝贺。谭政大将在祝酒词中说，为匈牙利人民军在保卫自己祖国的安全，打击帝国主义的侵略战争阴谋和捍卫世界和平的斗争中，取得更大的成就；为中匈两国人民和军队之间牢不可破的兄弟友谊；为以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的伟大团结，为世界和平而干杯。
    马希·费伦茨中校在招待会上的祝酒词中说，匈牙利人民军全体官兵引以为高兴的是，他们能够同光荣的苏联军队、强大的中国人民解放军以及其他兄弟的社会主义国家的军队一起肩并肩地为保卫世界和平进行斗争。他建议为匈牙利人民军与中国人民解放军之间牢不可破的战斗友谊；为匈中两国人民永恒的兄弟般的友谊，为以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结和巩固干杯。
    匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使马尔丁·费伦茨出席了招待会。
    应邀出席今晚招待会的，还有甘泗淇上将、陈伯钧上将、袁子钦中将、王辉球空军中将、沈启贤空军少将、段德彰海军少将、张汉丞海军少将、李信少将、张梓桢少将、廖井祥少将、程世清少将、李敦少将、张正光少将、徐绍华少将、苏焕清少将、孙仪之少将、高体乾少将、陶汉章少将、孙超群少将、唐廷伯少将、朱光少将等中国人民解放军高级将领以及陆、海、空军驻京部队军官代表七十多人。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有外交部副部长罗贵波，北京市副市长万里。
    社会主义国家驻中国大使和各国驻中国大使馆武官也应邀出席了招待会。
