Title: Our Party and Government Delegation Leaves Berlin for Home After Attending President Pieck's State Funeral
Author:
Date: 1960-09-14
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 13th: The Chinese Party and Government Delegation, led by Xi Zhongxun, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, which came to attend the state funeral of the late President Wilhelm Pieck, left here at 10:00 am on the 13th to return home.
Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic Paul Scholz, Vice Chairman of the People's Chamber and Deputy Chairman of the State Council Heymann, as well as representatives from the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
Staff members of the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic also saw the delegation off at the airport.

### 参加皮克总统国葬典礼后  我党政代表团离柏林回国

1960-09-14
第2版()
专栏：

    参加皮克总统国葬典礼后
    我党政代表团离柏林回国
    新华社柏林13日电　由中共中央委员、国务院副总理习仲勋率领的前来参加已故总统威廉·皮克国葬典礼的中国党政代表团，13日上午十时离开这里回国。
    到机场送行的有德意志民主共和国副总理保罗·绍尔茨，人民议院副主席、国务委员会副主席海·霍曼，以及民主德国全国阵线全国委员会和外交部的代表。
    中国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆的工作人员也到机场送行。
