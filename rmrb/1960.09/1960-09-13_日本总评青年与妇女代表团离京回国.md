Title: Japanese General Council of Trade Unions Youth and Women's Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The Japanese General Council of Trade Unions Youth and Women's Delegation, consisting of fourteen members led by head of delegation Tanehashi Shigeru, who had been invited to visit our country, left Beijing by train on the 10th to return home.

　　Among those seeing them off at the station were Zhang Chao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and others.

### 日本总评青年与妇女代表团离京回国

1960-09-13
第6版()
专栏：
日本总评青年与妇女代表团离京回国
    新华社11日讯　应邀来我国进行访问的日本总评青年与妇女代表团一行十四人，在团长种桥茂率领下，于10日乘车离北京回国。
    到车站欢送的，有中华全国青年联合会副主席张超等人。
