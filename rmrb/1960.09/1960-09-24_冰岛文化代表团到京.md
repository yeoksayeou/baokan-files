Title: Icelandic Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. The Icelandic Cultural Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries for a visit to China, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation was led by Sigurdur Gudmundsson, editor of the Icelandic newspaper "People's Will." Cheng Guangrui, editor of the International Department of the "People's Daily", and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 冰岛文化代表团到京

1960-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    冰岛文化代表团到京
    新华社23日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国参观访问的冰岛文化代表团一行三人，在团长、冰岛“人民意志报”编辑西古杜·格德门森率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有“人民日报”国际部编辑程光锐等人。
