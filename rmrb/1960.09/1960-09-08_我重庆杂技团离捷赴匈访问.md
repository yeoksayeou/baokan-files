Title: Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe Leaves Czechoslovakia for Hungary

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 6th -- The Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe, having concluded its performance tour in Czechoslovakia, departed today for Hungary for a visit.

During its nineteen days in Czechoslovakia, the acrobatic troupe visited fifteen major industrial cities and resorts, including Prague, Bratislava, and Pilsen, performing fifteen shows for more than 65,000 spectators.

The acrobatic troupe's performances were warmly welcomed by the broad masses of the Czechoslovak people.

### 我重庆杂技团离捷赴匈访问

1960-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    我重庆杂技团离捷赴匈访问
    据新华社布拉格6日电　在捷克斯洛伐克结束了访问演出的中国重庆杂技团今天前往匈牙利进行访问。
    杂技团在捷克斯洛伐克的十九天中，共访问了布拉格、布拉迪斯拉发和比尔森等十五个重要工业城市和疗养胜地，为六万五千余观众演出了十五场。
    杂技团的演出得到了捷克斯洛伐克广大人民的热烈欢迎。
