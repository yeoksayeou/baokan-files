Title: At the Invitation of the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal Our Youth Art Troupe Arrives in Kathmandu
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 8th. Upon the invitation of the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal, the Chinese Youth Art Troupe, led by head of the delegation, Wen Fei, arrived in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, by plane this morning.
    Those who welcomed the art troupe at the airport included Prakash Man Singh, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Purna Bahadur, President of the Nepal-China Friendship Association; Gan Lu Xi, President of the Nepal Buddhist Revival Association; the Secretary of the Nepal-China Cultural Association, the Secretary of the Nepal Youth League, and other prominent figures. Zhang Shijie, the Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, also went to the airport to welcome them.
    This afternoon, Wen Fei went to the Royal Palace to sign the guest book.

### 应尼泊尔王国政府的邀请  我青年艺术团到加德满都

1960-09-10
第6版()
专栏：

    应尼泊尔王国政府的邀请
    我青年艺术团到加德满都
    新华社8日讯　加德满都消息：应尼泊尔王国政府的邀请，中国青年艺术团在文菲团长率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达尼泊尔首都加德满都。
    到机场欢迎艺术团的有外交部礼宾司司长普腊卡斯·曼·辛格，尼泊尔—中国友好协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜尔，尼泊尔佛教复兴会会长甘露喜，尼泊尔—中国文化协会秘书，尼泊尔青年联盟秘书和其他著名人士。中国驻尼泊尔大使张世杰也到机场迎接。
    文菲今天下午到王宫在留名簿上签名致意。
