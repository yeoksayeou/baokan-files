Title: Sino-Korean Friendship Association Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 31st. The Chinese-Korean Friendship Association delegation, headed by Li Dequan as the leader and Li Jiebo as the deputy leader, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon, carrying the deep friendship of the heroic Korean people, after participating in the celebration of the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea and visiting Korea.
    Those who came to the airport to welcome them included Lao She, Chu Tunan, Xu Zhizhen, and He Biao.
    Li Yongho, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and diplomatic officials from the Embassy also came to the airport to welcome them.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 31st. The Chinese-Korean Friendship Association delegation, headed by Li Dequan, which was invited to participate in the "August 15th" celebration of the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea, left Pyongyang by plane at noon today to return to China after a twenty-day visit to Korea, laden with the deep friendship of the Korean people for the Chinese people.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Ha Ang-cheon, Chairman of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association, Song Yong, Chairman of the Korean Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, Kim Jong-heng, Minister of Higher Education, Hyun Pil-hoon, Vice Chairman of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association, and Kim Tae-hee, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    Liu Xianglun, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, and other officials of the Embassy also went to the airport to see them off.

### 中朝友协代表团回到北京

1960-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    中朝友协代表团回到北京
    新华社31日讯　以团长李德全和副团长李颉伯为首的中国朝鲜友好协会代表团，在参加了朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝活动和在朝鲜访问之后，带着英雄的朝鲜人民的深厚友谊，于今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有老舍、楚图南、许之桢、贺彪等。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐和大使馆外交官员也到机场欢迎。
    新华社平壤31日电　应邀前来参加“八·一五”朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝典礼的以李德全为首的中朝友好协会代表团在朝鲜进行了二十天的访问以后，满载着朝鲜人民对中国人民的深厚友情，在今天中午乘飞机离开平壤回国。
    到机场送行的有：朝中友好协会委员长河仰天，朝鲜对外文化联络委员会委员长宋影，高等教育相金钟恒，朝中友好协会副委员长玄弼勋，外务省副相金泰熙等。
    我国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办刘祥纶和使馆其它官员也到机场送行。
