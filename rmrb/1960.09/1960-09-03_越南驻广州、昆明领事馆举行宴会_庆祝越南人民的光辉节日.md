Title: Vietnamese Consulates in Guangzhou and Kunming Hold Banquets to Celebrate the Glorious Festival of the Vietnamese People
Author:
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 2nd: Vietnamese Consul in Guangzhou, Le Chung Thuy, and his wife held a reception at the Guangdong Guest House tonight to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chen Yu, Secretary of the Provincial Committee and Governor, as well as responsible persons from provincial and municipal Party and government organs, people's organizations, and local military units, attended the reception.

In his speech at the reception, Le Chung Thuy described the brilliant achievements made by the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in various fields over the past fifteen years. He severely condemned the crimes of U.S. imperialism in blatantly undermining the Geneva Accords for six years. He said that U.S. imperialism has turned southern Vietnam into a military base for its aggression. The people of the South have heroically and unyieldingly launched a tenacious struggle against U.S. imperialism, and our motherland will surely be unified.

Governor Chen Yu, on behalf of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee and the people of the province, wished the Vietnamese people greater victories in their socialist cause and in their just struggle against U.S. imperialist aggression and for the realization of national reunification.

The reception proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, September 2nd: The Consulate of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming held a banquet tonight to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Invited to attend the banquet were: Yan Hongyan, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China; Yu Yichuan, Secretary of the Provincial Committee and Governor; Ma Jikong, Secretary of the Provincial Committee; Liu Zhuofu, Secretary of the Provincial Committee, Vice Governor, and President of the Yunnan Branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association; Zhao Zengyi, First Secretary of the Kunming Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Pan Shoduan, Mayor; Qin Jiwei, Lieutenant General, and Chen Kang, Lieutenant General, leaders of the Kunming Garrison, and other responsible persons from provincial and municipal Party, government, and military units.

U Maung Maung So, Consul General of Burma in Kunming, was also invited to attend the banquet.

Vu Van Huong, Consul of the Vietnamese Consulate in Kunming, spoke at the banquet. He described the great achievements made by the Vietnamese people under the leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party and President Ho Chi Minh over the past fifteen years. He then severely condemned U.S. imperialist aggression against South Vietnam and its obstruction of the peaceful reunification of the Vietnamese people. He expressed his firm belief that the Vietnamese people's struggle to realize the reunification of their motherland will surely be victorious. Yu Yichuan, on behalf of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Provincial People's Committee, and the people of the province, warmly congratulated the fraternal Vietnamese people on their festival.

An evening party was held after the banquet.

### 越南驻广州、昆明领事馆举行宴会  庆祝越南人民的光辉节日

1960-09-03
第2版()
专栏：

    越南驻广州、昆明领事馆举行宴会
    庆祝越南人民的光辉节日
    新华社广州2日电　越南民主共和国驻广州领事黎终始和夫人今晚在广东迎宾馆举行酒会，庆祝越南民主共和国成立十五周年。
    中共广东省委第一书记陶铸，省委书记、省长陈郁，以及省市党和政府机关、各人民团体的负责人和当地驻军负责人都出席了酒会。
    黎终始在酒会上的讲话中讲述了越南民主共和国十五年来在各个方面取得的辉煌成就。他严厉谴责了美帝国主义六年来明目张胆地破坏日内瓦协议的罪行，他说，美帝国主义把我国南方变成了它进行侵略的军事基地。南方人民英勇不屈地和美帝国主义展开了顽强的斗争，我们的祖国一定能够统一。
    陈郁省长在酒会上代表广东省人民委员会和全省人民预祝越南人民今后在社会主义事业中，在反对美帝国主义侵略和实现祖国统一的正义斗争中获得更大的胜利。
    酒会在友好热烈的气氛中进行。
    新华社昆明2日电　越南民主共和国驻昆明领事馆今晚举行宴会，庆祝越南民主共和国成立十五周年。
    应邀出席宴会的有：中共云南省委第一书记阎红彦，省委书记、省长于一川，省委书记马继孔，省委书记、副省长、中越友协云南分会会长刘卓甫，中共昆明市委第一书记赵增益，市长潘朔端，驻昆明部队首长秦基伟中将、陈康中将等省市党、政府和军队的负责人。
    缅甸驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭也应邀出席了宴会。
    越南驻昆明领事馆领事武文享在宴会上讲了话。他叙述了十五年来越南人民在越南劳动党和胡志明主席领导下所取得的巨大成就。他接着严厉谴责了美帝国主义对越南南部的侵略和阻挠越南人民和平统一祖国的罪行。他表示深信，越南人民为实现统一祖国的斗争一定会胜利。于一川在会上代表中共云南省委、省人民委员会和全省人民向兄弟的越南人民热烈祝贺节日。
    宴会后举行了晚会。
