Title: Nan Hanchen Hosts Banquet for Honorary Secretary-General of the Nepal Chamber of Commerce
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Honorary Secretary-General of the Nepal Chamber of Commerce, Mr. Eng. B. Shrestha and his wife, as well as the Member of the Nepal Chamber of Commerce and Propaganda Secretary, Mr. Si. P. Rauniar and his wife. Invited to accompany them were Liu Xiven, Director of the Fourth Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Zhang Yihua, Director-General of the China-Nepal Friendship Association.
    The Nepalese guests arrived in Beijing by plane on the 22nd at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

### 南汉宸欢宴尼泊尔商会名誉秘书长

1960-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

　  南汉宸欢宴尼泊尔商会名誉秘书长
    新华社23日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今晚设宴欢迎尼泊尔商会名誉秘书长英·巴·什雷斯塔与夫人以及尼泊尔商会委员兼宣传秘书希·普·拉乌尼亚尔与夫人。应邀作陪的有对外贸易部第四局局长刘希文和中国尼泊尔友好协会总干事张亦桦等。
    尼泊尔客人是应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请来我国访问，在22日乘飞机到达北京的。
