Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Meets British Journalist Greene
Author:
Date: 1960-09-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Premier Zhou Enlai, in response to a request for a televised interview from British journalist Greene, met with Greene this noon and answered the questions he raised.
    Present at the meeting were Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director, among others.

### 周总理接见英国记者格林

1960-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见英国记者格林
    新华社5日讯　周恩来总理应英国记者格林提出的电视访问的要求，今天中午接见了格林，并回答了他所提出的问题。
    接见时，在座的有外交部新闻司司长龚澎、副司长康矛召等。
