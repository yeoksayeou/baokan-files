Title: For the Burmese Goodwill Delegation's Visit to Our Country to Participate in the National Day Celebrations, Our Ambassador to Burma Holds a Grand Reception: Hosts and Guests Jointly Celebrate the Comprehensive and Complete Resolution of the Sino-Burmese Border Issue
Author:
Date: 1960-09-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 20th: Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Li Yimang, held a grand banquet here tonight to entertain all members of the Burmese military, trade, cultural, and sports goodwill delegations who are about to go to China to participate in the celebration of China's National Day and the signing of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty in Beijing.

Among those present at the reception were Brigadier General Maung Maung, head of the Burmese military delegation, U Mya, head of the Burmese trade delegation, Chief of Staff Kyaw Nyein, and U Khin, Deputy Director of the Burmese News Bureau, and more than 400 others.

Reporters and prominent figures from all walks of life who will accompany Prime Minister U Nu on his visit to China also attended the banquet.

The banquet took place in a very cordial and friendly atmosphere. Hosts and guests frequently proposed toasts to the comprehensive and complete resolution of the Sino-Burmese border issue and to the further strengthening of the friendship between the peoples of China and Burma.

In his speech at the banquet, Ambassador Li Yimang warmly welcomed the Burmese military, trade, cultural, and sports goodwill delegations to visit China and expressed his hope that they would have a pleasant visit in China.

Famous singers and dancers from the Burmese cultural goodwill delegation performed beautiful Burmese songs and dances. Burmese artists also sang "Sino-Burmese Friendship Song" and "The Sun Rises on the Grassland" in Chinese at the banquet, and the hosts and guests repeatedly applauded their performances enthusiastically.

### 为缅甸友好代表团来我国参加国庆盛典　我驻缅甸大使举行盛大招待会  宾主同祝中缅边界问题全面圆满解决

1960-09-22
第6版()
专栏：

    为缅甸友好代表团来我国参加国庆盛典
　　我驻缅甸大使举行盛大招待会
    宾主同祝中缅边界问题全面圆满解决
    新华社仰光20日电　中国驻缅甸大使李一氓今晚在这里举行盛大宴会，招待即将前往中国参加庆祝中国国庆节和庆祝在北京签订中缅边界条约的活动的缅甸军事、贸易、文化和体育友好代表团的全体人员。
    出席招待会的有缅甸军事代表团团长貌貌准将，缅甸贸易代表团团长吴妙，参谋长庆牛和缅甸新闻局副局长吴钦等四百多人。
    将随同吴努总理前往中国访问的记者和各界著名人士也出席了宴会。
    宴会在非常亲切友好的气氛中进行。宾主频频提议为中缅边界问题的全面和圆满解决以及进一步加强中缅两国人民的友谊干杯。
    李一氓大使在宴会上讲话时，热烈欢迎缅甸军事、贸易、文化和体育友好代表团前往中国访问，并希望他们在中国进行愉快的访问。
    缅甸文化友好代表团的著名歌唱家和舞蹈家表演了优美的缅甸歌舞。缅甸艺术家们还在宴会上用汉语演唱了“中缅友好歌”和“草原上升起不落的太阳”，宾主一再对他们的演唱报以热烈的掌声。
