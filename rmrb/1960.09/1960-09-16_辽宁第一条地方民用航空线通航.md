Title: Liaoning's First Local Civil Aviation Route Opens
Author:
Date: 1960-09-16
Page: 3

    Liaoning's First Local Civil Aviation Route Opens
    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, September 14th. Liaoning Province's first local civil aviation route—the Shenyang to Chaoyang air route—has officially opened recently.
    This air route is 290 kilometers long and undertakes passenger, cargo, and newspaper and mail transportation. After its opening, it more closely connects Chaoyang, an emerging city in the mountainous area of western Liaoning Province, with Shenyang, the political, economic, and cultural center of Liaoning Province. The aircraft flying on this route is the An-2 type aircraft manufactured in our own country.

### 辽宁第一条地方民用航空线通航

1960-09-16
第3版()
专栏：

    辽宁第一条地方  民用航空线通航
    新华社沈阳十四日电辽宁省第一条地方民用航空线——沈阳至朝阳航空线已在最近正式通航。
    这条航线全长二百九十公里，担负客、货和报纸、邮件运输。它通航以后，使辽宁省西部山区的新兴城市朝阳和辽宁省的政治经济文化中心沈阳市，更为紧密地联结起来。在这条航线上飞行的，是我国自己制造的安二型飞机。
