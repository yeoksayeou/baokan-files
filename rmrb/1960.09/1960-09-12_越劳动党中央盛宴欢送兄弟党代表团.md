Title: Vietnamese Labor Party Central Committee Hosts Grand Banquet to Bid Farewell to Fraternal Party Delegations
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 11th: The Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party held a grand banquet at the Presidential Palace at 6 p.m. tonight to bid farewell to the delegations of fraternal parties who were invited to attend the Third National Congress of the Vietnamese Labor Party.

    Attending the banquet were Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, First Secretary Le Duan, all members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, and some members of the Central Committee. Chairman Ho Chi Minh spoke at the banquet on behalf of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, expressing gratitude to the fraternal parties for traveling from afar to attend the Third Congress of the Vietnamese Labor Party.

    Invited to the banquet were delegations from the fraternal parties of eleven socialist countries, including the Soviet Union, China, Albania, Bulgaria, Poland, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Mongolia, Romania, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Czechoslovakia, as well as delegations from the Communist Parties of India and Indonesia, and envoys from relevant countries to Vietnam.

### 越劳动党中央盛宴欢送兄弟党代表团

1960-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    越劳动党中央盛宴欢送兄弟党代表团
    新华社河内11日电　越南劳动党中央委员会今晚六时在主席府举行盛大宴会，欢送应邀参加越南劳动党第三次全国代表大会的各兄弟党代表团。
    出席这次宴会的有越南劳动党中央委员会主席胡志明，第一书记黎笋和越南劳动党中央政治局全体委员和一部分中央委员。胡志明主席代表越南劳动党中央委员会在宴会上讲话，对各兄弟党远道前来参加越南劳动党第三次代表大会表示感谢。
    应邀出席宴会的有苏联、中国、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、波兰、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、蒙古、罗马尼亚、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克等十一个社会主义国家兄弟党代表团和印度、印度尼西亚的共产党代表团和有关各国驻越南的使节。
