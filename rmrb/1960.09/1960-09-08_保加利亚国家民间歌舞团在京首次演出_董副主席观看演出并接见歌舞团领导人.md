Title: Bulgarian National Folk Song and Dance Ensemble Gives First Performance in Beijing; Vice Chairman Dong Watches Performance and Meets with Ensemble Leaders
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 7th: The Bulgarian National Folk Song and Dance Ensemble gave its first performance at the Tianqiao Theater tonight.

Vice Chairman Dong Biwu watched the ensemble's performance and, during the intermission, met with the ensemble's leader, Filipp Kutev, Secretary-General of the Bulgarian Composers Association and People's Actor, and other leaders, and had a cordial conversation with them.

Tonight, the ensemble's program began with the "Loch Chacha" dance and ended with the "Shopo Region Suite," featuring more than twenty programs including dances, choruses, and folk music, which were warmly received by the audience.

Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Panchevski, and others also watched tonight's performance.

Also in attendance were Zhang Zhixiang, Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Deputy Directors Chu Tunan, Ding Xilin, and Qu Wu, Vice Minister of Culture Xu Pingyu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, President of the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association Liu Ruilong, Vice President Nie Zhen, and figures from the capital's literary and art circles.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to China also watched tonight's performance.

After the performance, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu went on stage, shook hands with the artists of the song and dance ensemble, and congratulated them on the success of their performance. (Attached picture)

### 保加利亚国家民间歌舞团在京首次演出  董副主席观看演出并接见歌舞团领导人

1960-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚国家民间歌舞团在京首次演出
    董副主席观看演出并接见歌舞团领导人
    据新华社7日讯　保加利亚国家民间歌舞团今晚在天桥剧场举行首次演出。
    董必武副主席观看了歌舞团的演出，并在演出休息时间接见歌舞团团长、保加利亚作曲家协会总书记、人民演员菲利浦·库捷夫等领导人，和他们进行亲切的谈话。
    今晚，艺术团演出的节目以“洛其?查”舞蹈开始，到“绍波地区组舞”结束，共演出舞蹈、合唱、民间乐曲等二十多个节目，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
    保加利亚驻中国大使潘切夫斯基等也观看了今晚的演出。
    观看演出的还有，对外文化联络委员会代主任张致祥、副主任楚图南、丁西林、屈武，文化部副部长徐平羽，外交部副部长罗贵波，中保友好协会会长刘瑞龙、副会长聂真，和首都文艺界人士等。
    各国驻中国使节和外交官员也观看了今晚的演出。
    演出结束后，董必武副主席走上舞台，同歌舞团的艺术家们握手，祝贺他们演出的成功。（附图片）
