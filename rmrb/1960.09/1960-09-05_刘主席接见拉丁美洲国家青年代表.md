Title: Chairman Liu Receives Youth Representatives from Latin American Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 4th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi received this evening youth and student representatives from four Latin American countries, Cuba, Venezuela, Guatemala and Panama, who are currently visiting China, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

    The foreign guests were: Niurka Escalante, Carlos Quintera from Cuba; Jesús Antonio Valedón, Ramón Canrales from Venezuela; Julio César Segura Trujillo from Guatemala; Luis Carlos Jiménez from Panama.

    Also present at the reception were Hu Yaobang, Zhang Chao, Hu Qili, Wang Disan, Qian Dawei, and others. (Attached Photo)

### 刘主席接见拉丁美洲国家青年代表

1960-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见拉丁美洲国家青年代表
    新华社4日讯　刘少奇主席今天晚间接见了正在我国访问的古巴、委内瑞拉、危地马拉、巴拿马四个拉丁美洲国家的青年和学生代表，并同他们进行了友好的亲切的谈话。
    这些外宾是：古巴的尼乌尔加·埃斯卡兰特，卡洛斯·金特拉；委内瑞拉的赫苏斯·安东尼奥·瓦来顿，拉蒙·坎拉来斯；危地马拉的胡利奥·塞萨·塞古拉·特鲁希略；巴拿马的路易斯·卡洛斯·希梅内斯。
    接见时在座的有胡耀邦、张超、胡启立、王第三、钱大卫等。（附图片）
