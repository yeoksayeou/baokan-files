Title: Romanian Table Tennis Team Concludes Visit and Matches in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1960-09-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, September 18th: The Romanian men's and women's table tennis teams held friendly matches with the Chinese youth men's and women's table tennis second teams in Tianjin today. The result was a victory for the visiting women's team and a defeat for the men's team.
    The women's team competition went all the way to the fifth match before the visiting team won three to two. The men's team competition was a four-person round-robin, and after fifteen fierce matches, the home team won nine to six.
    The Romanian men's and women's table tennis teams will go to Beijing on the 19th to continue their friendly matches.

### 罗乒乓球队结束在天津的访问比赛

1960-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    罗乒乓球队结束在天津的访问比赛
    据新华社天津18日电　罗马尼亚男、女乒乓球队今天在天津与中国青年男女乒乓球二队举行友谊比赛，结果客队女胜男败。
    女子团体赛直打到第五场，客队才以三比二获胜。男子团体赛是四人循环赛，经过十五场激战，主队才以九比六获胜。
    罗马尼亚男、女乒乓球队将于19日去北京，继续进行友谊比赛。
