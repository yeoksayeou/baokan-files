Title: Khrushchev and Nehru Meet

Author:
Date: 1960-09-28
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, 27th -- TASS News Agency reported from New York: Soviet Minister Chairman Khrushchev met with Indian Prime Minister Nehru at his New York residence on the 26th.
　　Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko and Indian Foreign Secretary Dutt also participated in the meeting. The meeting was conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

### 赫鲁晓夫和尼赫鲁会谈

1960-09-28
第5版()
专栏：

　  赫鲁晓夫和尼赫鲁会谈
    新华社27日讯　塔斯社纽约讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫26日在纽约住所同印度总理尼赫鲁举行了会谈。
    苏联外交部长葛罗米柯和印度外交秘书杜特也参加了会谈。会谈是在友好的气氛中进行的。
