Title: Belgian Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: A delegation from the Belgian Union of Employees, Technicians, and Executives, headed by Oscar Leclercq, General Secretary of the union, arrived in Beijing by plane today. Liu Shanchang, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Financial and Trade Union, and others were present at the airport to welcome them.

    The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Financial and Trade Union for a friendly visit.

### 比利时一工会代表团到京

1960-09-17
第6版()
专栏：

    比利时一工会代表团到京
    新华社15日讯　以比利时职员、技术员和干部工会总书记奥斯卡·勒克莱克为首的访华代表团一行三人，今天乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国财贸工会全国委员会副主席刘善长等人。
    代表团是应中国财贸工会全国委员会邀请前来我国进行友好访问的。
