Title: Chairman Liu and President Touré Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th September: Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People’s Republic of China and President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea continued their talks this afternoon.
    The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
    The Guinean side participating in the talks included: Diallo Saifoulaye, President of the National Assembly; Louis Lansana Béavogui, Minister of National Economy; and Keita N’famara, Minister of Planning.
    The Chinese side participating in the talks included: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Chen Yi and Bo Yibo, Vice Premiers; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ke Hua, Chinese Ambassador to Guinea; and He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department.

### 刘主席和杜尔总统继续会谈

1960-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席和杜尔总统继续会谈
    新华社12日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔，今天下午继续进行会谈。
    会谈在友好的气氛中进行。
    几内亚方面参加会谈的有：国民议会议长迪阿洛·塞福拉耶，国民经济部长路易·兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉，计划部长凯塔·恩法马拉。
    中国方面参加会谈的有：国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈毅、薄一波，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫，我国驻几内亚大使柯华，西亚非洲司司长何英等。
