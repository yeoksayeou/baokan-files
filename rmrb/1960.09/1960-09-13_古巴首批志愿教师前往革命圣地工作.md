Title: Cuba's First Volunteer Teachers Go to Work in the Holy Land of the Revolution
Author:
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 11th - Cuba's first group of volunteer teachers left here by train last night for the Sierra Maestra mountains. This group of young teachers, who have undergone three months of intensive training, will begin their teaching work in the rural areas there.

According to "Revolución" newspaper, another 1,500 volunteer teachers will soon begin teaching training in the Sierra Maestra.

The Cuban revolutionary government will establish 3,000 schools for rural children this year. Premier Castro recently announced that Cuba will launch a campaign to eliminate remaining illiteracy next year.

### 古巴首批志愿教师前往革命圣地工作

1960-09-13
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴首批志愿教师前往革命圣地工作
    新华社哈瓦那11日电　古巴第一批志愿教师昨天晚上乘火车离开这里前往马埃斯特腊山区。这一批经过了三个月集中训练的青年教师，即将在那里的农村中开始教学工作。
    据“革命报”报道，另一批一千五百名志愿教师不久即将在马埃斯特腊山开始受教学训练。
    古巴革命政府今年将为乡村儿童创办三千所学校。卡斯特罗总理最近宣布说，古巴明年将开展消灭残余文盲的斗争。
