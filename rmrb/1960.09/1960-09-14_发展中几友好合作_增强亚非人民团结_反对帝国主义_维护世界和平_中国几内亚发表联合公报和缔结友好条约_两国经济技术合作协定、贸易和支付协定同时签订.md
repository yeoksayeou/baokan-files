Title: Developing Sino-Guinean Friendly Cooperation, Enhancing Solidarity Among Asian and African Peoples, Opposing Imperialism, and Safeguarding World Peace: China and Guinea Issue Joint Communiqué and Conclude Treaty of Friendship; Agreement on Economic and Technical Cooperation, Trade and Payment Agreement Signed Simultaneously
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th: The "Joint Communiqué of the President of the People's Republic of China and the President of the Republic of Guinea" was signed in Beijing today. Simultaneously, the "Treaty of Friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Guinea," the "Agreement on Economic and Technical Cooperation between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic Guinea," and the "Trade and Payment Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Guinea" were concluded.

The signing and publication of the aforementioned joint communiqué, treaty of friendship, and agreements signify that the friendly relations between China and Guinea have developed to a new stage. Chairman Liu Shaoqi and President Sékou Touré stated in the joint communiqué that during the talks, the two sides conducted friendly and sincere discussions on the current international situation, the situation in the Asian-African region, and the further development of friendly and cooperative relations between China and Guinea, and reached satisfactory consensus. In the Treaty of Friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Guinea, both sides firmly believe that strengthening friendly cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Guinea is in the fundamental interests of the two peoples, helps to enhance the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Guinean peoples and the peoples of Asia and Africa, and is conducive to world peace..

According to the provisions of the Agreement on Economic and Technical Cooperation between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Guinea, in order to help the Government of the Republic of Guinea develop its economy, the Government of the People's Republic of China will provide the Government of the Republic of Guinea with interest-free loans without any conditions or privileges during the period from September 13, 1960, to June 30, 1963. The loan amount is 100 million Rubles. The "Trade and Payment Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Guinea" is valid for five years, and trade between the two countries should be based on the principle of balancing the total value of imported and exported goods, with each side exporting approximately 12 billion Guinean francs annually.

The signing ceremony for the aforementioned joint communiqué, treaty of friendship, and agreements was held at the Great Hall of the People at 11:00 a.m. today. Chairman Liu Shaoqi and President Sékou Touré first signed the "Joint Communiqué of the President of the People's Republic of China and the President of the Republic of Guinea," followed by Premier Zhou Enlai and President Sékou Touré signing the "Treaty of Friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Guinea." Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jiuzhuang, on behalf of China, and Minister of National Economy Louis Lansana Béavogui and Minister of Planning Keita N Famara, on behalf of Guinea, signed the trade and payment agreement and the economic and technical cooperation agreement, respectively.

After the signing, the two sides warmly shook hands and offered champagne to express their congratulations.

The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included: Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Zhu De, Vice Chairmen Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Saifudin, Lin Feng, Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Bo Yibo, Tan Zhenlin, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Li Siguang, Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, President of the Supreme People's Court Xie Juezai, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lu Xuzhang, Chinese Ambassador to Guinea Ke Hua, Director of the West Asian and African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs He Ying, and Director of the Protocol Department Yu Peiwen.

The Guinean side participating in the signing ceremony included: President of the Guinean National Assembly Diallo Saifoulaye, Political Secretary of the Conakry II District Committee of the Guinean Democratic Party Diallo Mouctar, and Chief of Protocol of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs André Sassine, etc.

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi and President of the Republic of Guinea Sékou Touré continued their talks today.

The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

The Guinean side participating in the talks included: President of the Guinean National Assembly Diallo Saifoulaye, Minister of National Economy Louis Lansana Béavogui, and Minister of Planning Keita N Famara.

The Chinese side participating in the talks included: Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Zhu De, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Guinea Ke Hua, and Director of the West Asian and African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs He Ying. ### 发展中几友好合作　增强亚非人民团结　反对帝国主义　维护世界和平  中国几内亚发表联合公报和缔结友好条约  两国经济技术合作协定、贸易和支付协定同时签订

1960-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    发展中几友好合作　增强亚非人民团结　反对帝国主义　维护世界和平
    中国几内亚发表联合公报和缔结友好条约
    两国经济技术合作协定、贸易和支付协定同时签订
    新华社13日讯　“中华人民共和国主席和几内亚共和国总统联合公报”今天在北京签字。同时还缔结了：“中华人民共和国和几内亚共和国友好条约”、“中华人民共和国政府和几内亚共和国政府经济技术合作协定”和“中华人民共和国政府和几内亚共和国政府贸易和支付协定”。
    上述联合公报、友好条约和协定的签订和发表，标志着中国和几内亚两国友好关系已经发展到一个新的阶段。刘少奇主席和塞古·杜尔总统在联合公报中说，在会谈中，双方就当前国际局势、亚非地区形势和进一步发展中几两国友好合作关系等问题进行了友好诚挚的会谈，并且满意地取得了一致的意见。在中华人民共和国和几内亚共和国友好条约中，双方深信，加强中华人民共和国和几内亚共和国之间的友好合作符合于两国人民的根本利益，有助于增强中国、几内亚两国人民和亚洲、非洲人民的友谊和团结，并且有利于世界和平。
    根据中华人民共和国政府和几内亚共和国政府经济技术合作协定的规定，为了帮助几内亚共和国政府发展经济，中华人民共和国政府将在1960年9月13日—1963年6月30日的时期内，给予几内亚共和国政府以无息的、不附带任何条件和特权的贷款。贷款金额为一亿卢布。“中华人民共和国政府和几内亚共和国政府贸易和支付协定”有效期为五年，两国间的贸易应以进口商品总值和出口商品总值平衡为原则，双方每年各出口十二亿左右几内亚法郎。
    上述联合公报、友好条约和协定的签字仪式今天上午十一时在人民大会堂举行。刘少奇主席和塞古·杜尔总统首先分别在“中华人民共和国主席和几内亚共和国总统联合公报”上签字，接着，周恩来总理和塞古·杜尔总统分别在“中华人民共和国和几内亚共和国友好条约”上签字。分别在贸易和支付协定、经济技术合作协定上签字的，中国方面是对外贸易部长叶季壮，几内亚方面是国民经济部长路易·兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉和计划部长凯塔·恩法马拉。
    签字以后，双方热烈握手，并且进香槟酒表示祝贺。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，副委员长沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通、赛福鼎、林枫，国务院副总理陈毅、李先念、薄一波、谭震林，政协全国委员会副主席李四光、包尔汉，国防委员会副主席张治中，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长卢绪章，中国驻几内亚大使柯华，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英、礼宾司司长俞沛文。
    几内亚方面参加签字仪式的有：几内亚国民议会议长迪阿洛·塞福拉耶，几内亚民主党科纳克里二区区委政治书记迪阿洛·穆克塔，外交部礼宾司长安德烈·萨松等。
    新华社13日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔在今天继续举行了会谈。
    会谈在友好的气氛中进行。
    几内亚方面参加会谈的有：几内亚国民议会议长迪阿洛·塞福拉耶，国民经济部长路易·兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉，计划部长凯塔·恩法马拉。
    中国方面参加会谈的有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻几内亚大使柯华，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英。（附图片）
