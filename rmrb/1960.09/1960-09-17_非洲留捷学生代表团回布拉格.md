Title: African Student Delegation from Czechoslovakia Returns to Prague
Author:
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: The delegation of African students studying in Czechoslovakia, led by团长 Kenenes Komah, who visited our country at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation, left Beijing by plane on the 14th for Prague.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and others.

### 非洲留捷学生代表团回布拉格

1960-09-17
第6版()
专栏：

    非洲留捷学生代表团回布拉格
    新华社15日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的非洲留捷克斯洛伐克学生代表团，在团长肯涅斯·柯马率领下，14日乘飞机离开北京去布拉格。
    到机场欢送的有中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立等人。
