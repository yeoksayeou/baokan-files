Title: Economic Mutual Assistance Council Machine Manufacturing Economic and Technological Cooperation Standing Committee Meeting Decides to Further Strengthen Scientific and Technological Cooperation Among Member States
Author:
Date: 1960-09-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 12th. The Standing Committee on Economic, Scientific, and Technological Cooperation in Machine Manufacturing of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance (COMECON), composed of the Soviet Union and European socialist countries, held a meeting here from September 5th to 12th. In addition to the delegations from the member countries of COMECON, a delegation from the People's Republic of China participated in the meeting as an observer.

The meeting focused on discussing a work plan of the Standing Committee – on how to cooperate with the long-term development plans of the machine manufacturing industries of the member countries up to 1980. The Standing Committee discussed and adopted suggestions for specialization and collaboration among the member countries in the production of the most important types of machines.

The Standing Committee believes that broad cooperation in semiconductors and among the research institutes of the member countries in metallurgy and welding technology is beneficial.

### 经济互助委员会机器制造经济、科技合作常委会开会  决定进一步加强成员国间的科技合作

1960-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

    经济互助委员会机器制造经济、科技合作常委会开会
    决定进一步加强成员国间的科技合作
    新华社布拉格12日电　由苏联和欧洲社会主义国家组成的经济互助委员会机器制造经济、科学和技术合作常设委员会，从9月5日到12日在这里举行了会议。除经济互助委员会的各成员国的代表团外，中华人民共和国的代表团以观察员的身份参加了会议。
    会议集中讨论了常设委员会的一项工作计划——关于如何配合各成员国的机器制造工业到1980年为止的长期发展计划的问题。常设委员会讨论和通过了各成员国在最重要的机器类型的生产方面实行专业化和协作的建议。
    常设委员会认为，在半导体方面以及各成员国的研究院之间在冶金和焊接技术方面进行广泛合作是有利的。
