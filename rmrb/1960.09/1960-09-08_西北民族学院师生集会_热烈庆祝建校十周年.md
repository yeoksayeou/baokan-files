Title: Northwest Nationalities Institute Holds a Grand Gathering: Warmly Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the School's Founding
Author:
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, September 5th: The faculty and students of the Northwest Nationalities Institute held a grand gathering on September 1st to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the school's founding.

Wang Feng, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Xie Hechou, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Liu Guanying, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Zhongliang, First Secretary of the CPC Gansu Provincial Committee, and other leaders of the Party and government attended the meeting. Also attending the meeting were Xiong Shouqi, Vice President of the Central Nationalities Institute, and representatives from Shaanxi, Qinghai, Xinjiang, and Ningxia provinces and autonomous regions.

At the meeting, Chen Zenggu, Xie Hechou, and Liu Guanying congratulated all the faculty and students and delivered speeches.

Comrade Meng Dingjun, President of the Northwest Nationalities Institute, introduced the achievements and experiences of the Northwest Nationalities Institute over the past ten years. He said that over the past ten years, the institute has trained more than 6,500 cadres for Party and government work and specialized technical personnel for ethnic minority areas in Gansu, Qinghai, Xinjiang, Ningxia, Shaanxi, Inner Mongolia, Tibet, and other regions.

### 西北民族学院师生集会  热烈庆祝建校十周年

1960-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    西北民族学院师生集会
    热烈庆祝建校十周年
    据新华社兰州5日电　西北民族学院全体师生9月1日隆重集会，庆祝建校十周年。
    中共中央统一战线工作部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋，民族事务委员会副主任谢鹤筹，全国人民代表大会民族委员会办公室副主任刘冠英，中共甘肃省委第一书记张仲良等党和政府的领导人出席了大会。出席大会的还有中央民族学院副院长熊寿祺，陕西、青海、新疆、宁夏等省、区的代表。
    会上，陈曾固、谢鹤筹、刘冠英分别向全体师生祝贺，并在会上讲了话。
    西北民族学院院长蒙定军同志介绍了西北民族学院十年来的成绩和经验。他说，十年来，这个学院给甘肃、青海、新疆、宁夏、陕西、内蒙古、西藏等民族地区培养了六千五百多名党和政府等方面的工作干部和专业技术人材。
