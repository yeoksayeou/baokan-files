Title: Burmese Political Parties Hold Representative Meeting, Decide to Grandly Celebrate Sino-Burmese Border Treaty
Author:
Date: 1960-09-28
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 27th: The Burmese Union League, the Burmese National United Front, and the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League held a representative meeting here yesterday to discuss activities for celebrating the signing of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty. The meeting made arrangements for the mass rally celebrating the signing of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty to be held in Rangoon on October 1st.
    The meeting passed a statement on supporting the celebration activities of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty, which will be widely distributed.
    In order to celebrate the signing of the treaty, the Burma-China Friendship Association will hold an evening party on the evening of September 30th, with Burmese and Chinese cultural performances to entertain guests.

### 缅甸各政党举行代表会议　决定盛大庆祝中缅边界条约

1960-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

　  缅甸各政党举行代表会议
　　决定盛大庆祝中缅边界条约
    新华社仰光27日电　缅甸联邦同盟、缅甸民族团结阵线和反法西斯人民自由同盟，昨天在这里举行代表会议，讨论庆祝中缅边界条约签订的活动。会议对于将在10月1日在仰光举行的庆祝中缅边界条约签订的群众大会，作出了安排。
    会议通过了一项关于支持中缅边界条约的庆祝活动的声明，这项声明将大量印发。
    为了庆祝条约的签订，缅中友好协会将在9月30日晚上举行晚会，演出缅甸和中国的文艺节目招待来宾。
