Title: Japanese Hozumi Shichiro Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 14th — Hozumi Shichiro, a member of the Japanese Socialist Party Diet and Chairman of the Socialist Party's Special Committee for Upholding the Constitution, who came to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, left Beijing today to return home. Yang Jin, Yang Chunsong, Xie Nanguang and others went to the airport to see him off.

While in Beijing, Mr. Hozumi Shichiro visited Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, and Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and held talks with them.

### 日本穗积七郎回国

1960-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    日本穗积七郎回国
    新华社14日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的日本社会党国会议员、社会党拥护宪法特别委员会委员长穗积七郎，今天离开北京回国。前往机场欢送的有杨进、杨春松、谢南光等人。
    穗积七郎先生在北京期间，曾分别访问了中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志、中国国际贸易促进会副主席雷任民，并且同他们进行了谈话。
