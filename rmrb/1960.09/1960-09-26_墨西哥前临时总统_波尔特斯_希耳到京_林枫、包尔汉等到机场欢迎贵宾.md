Title: Former Interim President of Mexico, Portes Gil, Arrives in Beijing; Lin Feng, Bao Erhan, and Others Greet the Distinguished Guest at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The Mexican cultural delegation, invited by the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association and led by its head, Emilio Portes Gil, the former interim president, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association; Zhou Erfu, Vice President; Zhang Youyu, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Association; Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and his wife; Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation; and Qiao Peixin, Vice President of the People's Bank of China, among others.

Basols, a member of the delegation who arrived in Beijing on the 22nd, also went to the airport to greet them. (Attached picture)

Mexican cultural delegation leader, former interim president Emilio Portes Gil (fourth from the left in the front row) and others, accompanied by Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, walk out of the airport.

### 墨西哥前临时总统  波尔特斯·希耳到京  林枫、包尔汉等到机场欢迎贵宾

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　墨西哥前临时总统
　  波尔特斯·希耳到京
    林枫、包尔汉等到机场欢迎贵宾
    新华社25日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的墨西哥文化代表团，在团长、前临时总统埃米略·波尔特斯·希耳率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南、副会长周而复，中国政治法律学会副会长张友渔，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎及夫人，全国妇联书记处书记曹孟君，中国人民银行副行长乔培新等人。
    22日抵京的代表团团员巴索尔斯也前往机场迎接。（附图片）
    墨西哥文化代表团团长、前临时总统埃米略·波尔特斯·希耳（前排左起第四人）等在全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫陪同下步出机场
