Title: Chinese Mechanical Engineering Union and Water Conservancy and Electric Power Union Welcome Delegation from Canadian Electrical and Mechanical Union
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th—The Chinese Mechanical Engineering Union and the Water Conservancy and Electric Power Union held a joint social gathering on the evening of the 10th to welcome the delegation from the United Electrical, Radio and Machine Workers of Canada, led by Ross Russell, which is currently visiting our country.

    Yu Huan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Mechanical Engineering Union, spoke at the meeting, extending a warm welcome to the guests.

    Delegation members Bowsen and Armstrong spoke successively at the meeting, expressing their warm gratitude for the cordial reception from China. Zhang Xiuzhu, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Huang Minwei, Chairman of the Chinese Water Conservancy and Electric Power Union, attended the evening party.

    Beijing mechanical workers and the Canadian guests all performed cultural programs at the meeting. The delegation from the United Electrical, Radio and Machine Workers of Canada also presented gifts to the Chinese workers.

### 中国机械工会和水利电力工会  欢迎加拿大电气和机械工会代表团

1960-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    中国机械工会和水利电力工会
    欢迎加拿大电气和机械工会代表团
    据新华社11日讯　中国机械工会、水利电力工会在10日晚举行联欢晚会，欢迎正在我国访问的由罗斯·鲁塞尔率领的加拿大联合电气、无线电、机械工人工会代表团。
    中国机械工会全国委员会副主席于桓在会上讲话，对客人们表示热诚的欢迎。
    代表团团员鲍森和阿姆斯特朗先后在会上讲了话，对中国盛情的接待表示热烈的谢意。中华全国总工会书记张修竹和中国水利电力工会主席黄民伟参加了晚会。
    北京机械工人和加拿大的客人们都在会上演出了文娱节目。加拿大联合电气、无线电、机械工人工会代表团还赠送礼品给中国工人。
