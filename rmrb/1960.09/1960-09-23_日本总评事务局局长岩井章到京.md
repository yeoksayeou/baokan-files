Title: Japanese General Council of Trade Unions (Sohyo) Secretary-General Akira Iwai Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd. Akira Iwai, Secretary-General of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), and Secretary Kami Tsuma Yoshiaki, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 日本总评事务局局长岩井章到京

1960-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    日本总评事务局局长岩井章到京
    据新华社22日讯　日本工会总评议会事务局局长岩井章和秘书上妻美章，应中华全国总工会邀请，今天下午由广州乘飞机到达北京。
