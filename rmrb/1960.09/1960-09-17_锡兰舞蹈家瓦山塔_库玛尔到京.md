Title: Ceylonese Dancer Wasantha Kumar Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 16th: Ceylonese dancer Wasantha Kumar arrived in Beijing by plane on the 15th. He is in China for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries.

Lin Lin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and others welcomed him at the airport.

### 锡兰舞蹈家瓦山塔·库玛尔到京

1960-09-17
第6版()
专栏：

    锡兰舞蹈家瓦山塔·库玛尔到京
    新华社16日讯　锡兰舞蹈家瓦山塔·库玛尔已经在15日乘飞机到达北京，他是应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长林林等人。
