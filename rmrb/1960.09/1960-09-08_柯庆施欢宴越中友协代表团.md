Title: Ke Qingshi Hosts Banquet for Vietnam-China Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 7th: Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, and Mayor of Shanghai, held a banquet tonight to warmly welcome the Vietnam-China Friendship Association delegation headed by Tran Huy Lieu on their visit to Shanghai. Vu Thu, head of the Vietnamese Mock Theatre Troupe, and Cao Kim Dien, deputy head, were also invited to attend the banquet.

Present at the banquet were Chen Pixian, Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee; Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Song Jiwen, Song Richang, and Jin Zhonghua, Deputy Mayors of Shanghai; Hu Juewen, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC; Wang Bicheng, Lieutenant General and Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others. Representatives of Vietnamese interns and students in Shanghai also attended the banquet.

In his speech at the banquet, Ke Qingshi said: The glorious victory of the Vietnamese people's war against aggression and in their reconstruction has strengthened the power of the entire socialist camp, defended the southern position of the socialist camp, and inspired the oppressed people's determination to fight for independence and freedom.

Ke Qingshi warmly praised the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for its unremitting struggle against U.S. imperialist aggression and for upholding the Geneva Accords and the peaceful reunification of the country, and severely condemned the criminal activities of U.S. imperialism and its lackey, the Ngo Dinh Diem clique, in undermining the Geneva Accords, obstructing the peaceful reunification of Vietnam, and exacerbating the situation in Indochina.

Head of the delegation Tran Huy Lieu also spoke at the banquet. He spoke of the achievements made by the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the past fifteen years, as well as the traditional friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and China, and raised his glass to the fraternal friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and China and to the increasing consolidation and development of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

Vu Thu, head of the Vietnamese Mock Theatre Troupe, also spoke at the banquet.

Before the banquet, Mayor Ke Qingshi met with all members of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association delegation headed by Tran Huy Lieu, as well as Vu Thu, head of the Vietnamese Mock Theatre Troupe, and Cao Kim Dien, deputy head. After the banquet, the distinguished guests also watched a performance.

The Vietnam-China Friendship Association delegation arrived in Shanghai by train this afternoon from Nanjing. The Vietnamese Mock Theatre Troupe arrived in Shanghai on the 6th from Beijing.

### 柯庆施欢宴越中友协代表团

1960-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    柯庆施欢宴越中友协代表团
    据新华社上海7日电　中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎以陈辉燎为首的越中友好协会代表团到上海访问。越南嘲剧艺术团团长武秋、副团长高金典也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的有中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，上海市副市长宋季文、宋日昌、金仲华，政协上海市委员会副主席胡厥文，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将等。越南在上海实习生和留学生的代表也出席了宴会。
    柯庆施在宴会上讲话，他说：越南人民反侵略战争和恢复建设的辉煌胜利，加强了整个社会主义阵营的力量，捍卫了社会主义阵营的南方阵地，鼓舞了被压迫人民争取独立自由的斗争意志。
    柯庆施热烈赞扬了越南民主共和国为反对美帝国主义侵略、维护日内瓦协议与和平统一祖国所进行的坚持不懈的斗争，并且严厉谴责了美帝国主义及其走狗吴庭艳集团破坏日内瓦协议、阻挠越南和平统一、恶化印度支那局势的罪恶活动。
    陈辉燎团长也在宴会上讲了话。他谈到了越南民主共和国十五年来所取得的成就，以及越中两国人民的传统友谊，并且举杯为越中两国人民的兄弟友谊、为以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的日益巩固和发展而干杯。
    越南嘲剧艺术团团长武秋也在宴会上讲了话。
    宴会前，柯庆施市长接见了以陈辉燎为首的越中友好协会代表团全体成员和越南嘲剧艺术团团长武秋、副团长高金典。宴会后，贵宾们还观看了文艺演出。
    越中友好协会代表团今天下午由南京乘火车到达上海，越南嘲剧艺术团6日由北京到达上海。
