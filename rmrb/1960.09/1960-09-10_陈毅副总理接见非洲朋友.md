Title: Chen Yi Receives African Friends
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the delegation from the People's Liberation Movement of Angola of Portuguese Guinea, headed by Mona Mendum and the delegation from the African Independence Party of Portuguese Guinea, headed by Kaba Adeer, this evening and had a cordial conversation with them.

    Those present at the reception included Zhu Ziqi, He Gongkai, and Wu Xiaoda.

### 陈毅副总理接见非洲朋友

1960-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见非洲朋友
    新华社9日讯　陈毅副总理今晚接见了以莫纳·孟杜为首的葡属安哥拉人民解放运动访华代表团和以卡巴·阿代耳为首的葡属几内亚非洲独立党访华代表团全体成员，并且进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时，在座的有朱子奇、何功楷和吴晓达等。
