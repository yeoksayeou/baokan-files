Title: Two Japanese Peace Groups' Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 3
　　
Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The delegation of the Japanese Peace Committee, headed by Yanagita Kenjuro, and the delegation of the Japanese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, headed by Sakamoto Tokumatsu, arrived in Beijing by the same train this afternoon, invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations.

　　They were warmly welcomed at the Beijing Railway Station by Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Chairman of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Vice Chairman of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, and Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, as well as people from all walks of life in Beijing, including Qu Tangliang, Zhao Puchu, Chen Yu, Huang Ganying, Yang Jin, Wang Mingyuan, Zheng Senyu, Qi Muguang, Yang Chunsong, and Wang Xiaoyun.

　　Koichi Saionji, a Japanese peace activist in Beijing, and Hwang Bong-koo, a Korean peace activist, also went to the station to welcome them.

　　The delegation of the Japanese Peace Committee and the delegation of the Japanese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee arrived in Guangzhou by train from Shenzhen on the 25th. That evening, Du Guoxiang, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet to welcome the guests.

### 日本两个和平团体代表团到京

1960-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　日本两个和平团体代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　以团长柳田谦十郎为首的日本和平委员会代表团和以团长坂本德松为首的日本亚非团结委员会代表团，分别应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国亚非团结委员会的邀请来我国访问并参加国庆观礼，今天下午乘同一次火车到达北京。
    他们在北京车站受到了中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席、中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席、中国亚非团结委员会副主席包尔汉和中国亚非团结委员会副主席李德全以及北京各界人士区棠亮、赵朴初、陈宇、黄甘英、杨进、王明远、郑森禹、戚慕光、杨春松、王晓云等人的热情欢迎。
    在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一和朝鲜和平人士黄凤九也到车站欢迎。
    日本和平委员会代表团和日本亚非团结委员会代表团是在25日乘火车从深圳到达广州的。当晚，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广东省分会副主席杜国庠设宴欢迎客人。
