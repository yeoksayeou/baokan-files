Title: People of Various Ethnic Groups in Santai County, Laos, Determined to Crush All Conspiracies of the Nosavan Group
Author:
Date: 1960-09-01
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 31st. According to a broadcast on August 30th by the Lao Combat Forces Radio Station, hundreds of people from various ethnic groups and social strata in Santai County, Sam Neua Province, Laos, expressed at a recent gathering their determination to prevent and crush all conspiracies of the U.S. imperialist stooge, the Phoumi Nosavan group, which attempts to undermine peace, neutrality, and national harmony.

The meeting enthusiastically supported the statement on the Laotian situation issued by Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, and supported the genuine policy of peace, neutrality, and national harmony put forward by the coup committee and the Phouma government.

The meeting passed a resolution demanding that the coup committee and the Phouma government correctly implement the policies and guidelines they have put forward, and oppose the government's concessions to the Phoumi Nosavan traitorous clique. (Attached picture)

### 决心粉碎诺萨万集团的一切阴谋  老挝山德县各族人民集会

1960-09-01
第5版()
专栏：

    决心粉碎诺萨万集团的一切阴谋
    老挝山德县各族人民集会
    新华社河内31日电　据寮国战斗部队广播电台30日广播，老挝桑怒省山德县有几百名来自各民族、各阶层的人民在最近举行的集会上表示，决心阻止和粉碎企图破坏和平、中立和民族和睦的美帝国主义走狗——富米·诺萨万集团的一切阴谋。
    会议热烈支持老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席苏发努冯亲王就老挝局势发表的声明，并且支持政变委员会和富马政府所提出的真正的和平、中立和民族和睦政策。
    会议通过决议，要求政变委员会和富马政府正确地执行自己所提出的政策、路线，反对政府对富米·诺萨万卖国集团让步。（附图片）
