Title: SED Central Committee Decides to Rename Pieck's Hometown Pieck City
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 5

    SED Central Committee Decides
    Pieck's Hometown Renamed Pieck City
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 11th - The Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany (SED) has decided to take measures to commemorate the late President Wilhelm Pieck of the German Democratic Republic.
    These measures are: to rename President Pieck's hometown, the town of Guben, Wilhelm Pieck City, and to establish the Wilhelm Pieck Memorial Hall; to erect Wilhelm Pieck monuments in Berlin and Guben; to rename the University of Rostock Wilhelm Pieck University; to open permanent exhibition sections in twelve historical museums to commemorate Comrade Pieck; to establish the Wilhelm Pieck Prize; to establish a memorial hall in the Wilhelm Pieck "Young Pioneer Republic" on Lake Werbellin; to immediately issue a set of commemorative stamps; and to hold commemorative activities on January 3, 1961, the 85th anniversary of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck's birth. (Attached picture)
    The photo shows the Chinese Party and Government Delegation keeping vigil before President Pieck's remains. Transmitted by German News Agency (Xinhua News Agency)

### 德统一社会党中央决定  皮克故乡改名皮克城

1960-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    德统一社会党中央决定
    皮克故乡改名皮克城
    新华社柏林11日电　德国统一社会党中央委员会决定采取措施，以纪念德意志民主共和国已故总统威廉·皮克。
    这些措施是：把皮克总统的故乡古本城改名为威廉·皮克城，并建立威廉·皮克纪念馆；在柏林和古本建立威廉·皮克纪念碑；把罗斯托克大学改名为威廉·皮克大学；在十二个历史博物馆中为纪念皮克同志开设常设的展览部；创设威廉·皮克奖金；在维尔贝林湖的威廉·皮克“少先队共和国”设立纪念馆；立即发行一套纪念邮票；在1961年1月3日威廉·皮克同志诞生八十五周年时，举行纪念活动。（附图片）
