Title: Koirala Says Sino-Nepalese Relations are Very Friendly; Border Issue Being Resolved Very Amicably
Author:
Date: 1960-09-20
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala told reporters in Hong Kong on September 17th, en route to New York to attend the United Nations General Assembly, that relations between Nepal and China are "very friendly." He said that the border issue between the two countries "is currently being resolved very amicably." According to the "Nepali Language Newspaper," Prime Minister Koirala, before departing from Kathmandu Airport on the 16th, stated that Nepal supports any proposal to restore China's legitimate rights in the United Nations. He said that restoring China's legitimate seat in the United Nations "can help ease international tensions."

### 柯伊拉腊说尼中关系非常友好  两国边界问题正在非常友好地进行解决

1960-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    柯伊拉腊说尼中关系非常友好
    两国边界问题正在非常友好地进行解决
    新华社18日讯　尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊9月17日在去纽约参加联合国大会途经香港时对记者说，尼泊尔和中国的关系“非常友好”。他说，两国之间的边界问题，“目前正在非常友好地进行解决”。据“尼泊尔语言报”消息，柯伊拉腊首相动身之前16日在加德满都机场发表谈话时表示，尼泊尔支持关于恢复中国在联合国的合法权利的任何建议。他说，恢复中国在联合国的合法席位，“能有助于缓和国际紧张局势”。
