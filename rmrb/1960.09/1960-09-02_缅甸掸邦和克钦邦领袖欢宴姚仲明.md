Title: Shan and Kachin State Leaders of Burma Host Yao Zhongming

Author:
Date: 1960-09-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 31st. The Shan State leader of Burma, Zao Kun Zhuo, and the Kachin State leader, Sama Duwa Sinwa Nau, jointly held a banquet here on the evening of August 31 to welcome the Chinese delegation of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee headed by Yao Zhongming.

Among those attending the banquet were the Chief Justice of the Burmese Supreme Court, U Myint Thein, the Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities, Zao Kun Ji, the Minister of Justice, U Aye Maung, the Minister of Trade Development, U Tun, the Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, James Barrington, members of parliament, and the secretaries of the Shan State Affairs Department and the Kachin State Affairs Department.

Also present were the Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, the chief Burmese representative of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces, and other Burmese representatives and advisors.

Also present were Lei Yang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché, and counselors of the embassy.

The banquet took place in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 缅甸掸邦和克钦邦领袖欢宴姚仲明

1960-09-02
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸掸邦和克钦邦领袖欢宴姚仲明
    新华社仰光8月31日电　缅甸掸邦领袖藻昆卓和克钦邦领袖沙马杜瓦信瓦瑙8月31日晚间在这里联合举行宴会，欢迎以姚仲明为首的中缅边界联合委员会的中国代表团。
    出席宴会的有缅甸最高法院首席法官吴敏登，民族院议长藻昆基，司法部长吴埃貌，贸易发展部长吴敦，外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿，国会议员们，以及掸邦事务部秘书和克钦邦事务部秘书等。
    出席宴会的还有缅甸驻中国大使叫温，中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表、国防军副总参谋长昂季准将，以及其他缅甸代表和顾问等。
    出席的还有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬、武官蒋克定上校和大使馆参赞等。
    宴会是在亲切和友好的气氛中进行的。
