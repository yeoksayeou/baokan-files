Title: Vietnamese Academy of Letters Delegation Leaves Nanning for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, September 25th: The three-member delegation from the Vietnamese Academy of Letters, led by Đoàn Hữu Công (Hoài Thanh), which came to China for a visit in accordance with the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement, concluded its visit to China and left Nanning by train on the afternoon of the 24th to return home.

Those who saw them off at the station included Liu Chun'an, Vice President of the Guangxi Branch of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Guo Ming, Chairman of the Autonomous Region Federation of Literary and Art Circles. The delegation arrived in Beijing on August 11th. They visited Beijing, Shanghai, Guilin, Nanning, and other places, and were warmly welcomed by literary and art circles in these places. The Autonomous Region Federation of Literary and Art Circles held a banquet on the 22nd to bid farewell to the distinguished guests. Qin Yingji, Secretary of the CPC Autonomous Region Committee and Vice Chairman of the Autonomous Region, attended the banquet.

### 越南文学院代表团离南宁回国

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

    越南文学院代表团离南宁回国
    新华社南宁25日电　根据中越文化合作协定来我国访问的越南文学院代表团一行三人，在团长怀青率领下结束了在我国的访问，于24日下午乘车离开南宁回国。
    到车站欢送的，有中国科学院广西分院副院长刘春安、自治区文联主席郭铭等。代表团是在8月11日到达北京的。他们访问了北京、上海、桂林、南宁等地，受到了这些地方文艺界人士的热烈欢迎。自治区文联22日曾设宴欢送贵宾，中共自治区委员会书记、自治区副主席覃应机出席了宴会。
