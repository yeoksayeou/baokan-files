Title: At the invitation of Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Guinean President will visit our country
Author:
Date: 1960-09-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 5th. At the invitation of Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, the President of the Republic of Guinea, Sékou Touré, and the government delegation of the Republic of Guinea he leads, are scheduled to leave Conakry on the 5th and arrive in our country on the 10th for a friendly visit.

### 应刘少奇主席的邀请  几内亚总统将来我国访问

1960-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    应刘少奇主席的邀请
    几内亚总统将来我国访问
  新华社5日讯　应中华人民共和国主席刘少奇的邀请，几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔和他率领的几内亚共和国政府代表团定于5日动身离开科纳克里，将于10日到达我国进行友好访问。
