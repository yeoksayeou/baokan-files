Title: Fraternal Countries and Brotherly Parties' Delegations Lay Wreaths at the Monument to the Vietnamese People's Army
Author:
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 2nd. According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency today, on the afternoon of September 1st, delegations from the party and government of eleven socialist countries, as well as delegations and representatives from communist parties of other countries, laid wreaths at the Monument to the Vietnamese People's Army.

The delegations that laid wreaths included: the party and government delegations of the Soviet Union, China, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the German Democratic Republic, Albania, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Mongolia, Hungary, and Romania, as well as the delegations and representatives of the communist parties of France, Japan, India, Indonesia, and Canada.

The Guangdong Provincial Party and Government Delegation of China also laid a wreath at the monument.

### 兄弟国家和兄弟党代表团  向越人民军纪念碑献花圈

1960-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    兄弟国家和兄弟党代表团
    向越人民军纪念碑献花圈
    新华社河内2日电　据越南通讯社今天报道，十一个社会主义国家的党政代表团和其他国家共产党的代表团和代表们1日下午在越南人民军纪念碑前献了花圈。
    前往献花圈的有：苏联、中国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古、匈牙利、罗马尼亚的党政代表团，法国、日本、印度、印度尼西亚和加拿大共产党的代表团和代表。
    中国广东省党政代表团也在纪念碑前献了花圈。
