Title: Nepalese Prime Minister Receives Chinese Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1960-09-15
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 14th—Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala received Wen Fei, head of the Chinese Youth Art Troupe, and the main actors of the art troupe at the Lion Palace this morning during their visit and performance here.
    Present at the reception were Zhang Shijie, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, and Joshi, Director of the Cultural Department of the Nepalese Ministry of Education.

### 尼泊尔首相接见我艺术团

1960-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首相接见我艺术团
    新华社14日讯　加德满都消息：尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊今天上午在狮宫接见了正在这里访问演出的中国青年艺术团团长文菲和艺术团的主要演员。
    接见时在座的有中国驻尼泊尔大使张世杰和尼泊尔教育部文化司司长乔希。
