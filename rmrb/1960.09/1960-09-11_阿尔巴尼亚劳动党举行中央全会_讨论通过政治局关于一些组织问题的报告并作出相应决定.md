Title: Albanian Labor Party Holds Central Plenary Session; Discusses and Approves the Political Bureau's Report on Certain Organizational Matters and Makes Corresponding Decisions
Author:
Date: 1960-09-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, September 9th: The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party announced on the 9th the communique of a recent plenary session. The full text is as follows:

The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, chaired by Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, held a plenary session. The session discussed and approved the report of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, made by Comrade Ri. Marko, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Party Central Committee, "On Some Organizational Issues of the Party," and made corresponding decisions on these issues.

After discussing the serious errors in the Party line of Comrade Li. Belishova, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Labor Party Central Committee, the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party unanimously decided to expel her from the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party.

The plenary session of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party elected Comrade Ra. Alia, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, as Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party.

At a joint meeting with the Central Control Commission, the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party discussed the anti-Party and hostile activities of Comrade Ko. Tashko, Chairman of the Central Control Commission, and unanimously decided to expel him from the Central Control Commission and expel him from the Party.

As the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party held its plenary session, it was saddened to learn of the passing of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, a famous fighter of the international communist movement, President of the German Democratic Republic, and a respected friend of the Albanian people. The Central Committee observed a minute of silence in mourning.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党举行中央全会  讨论通过政治局关于一些组织问题的报告并作出相应决定

1960-09-11
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党举行中央全会
    讨论通过政治局关于一些组织问题的报告并作出相应决定
    新华社地拉那9日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会9日公布了最近举行的一次全体会议的公报，全文如下：
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查同志的主持下举行了全体会议，会议讨论和通过了由政治局委员、党中央委员会书记里·马尔科所作的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会政治局“关于党的一些组织问题”的报告，并就这些问题作出了相应的决定。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会在讨论了政治局委员、劳动党中央委员会书记丽·贝利绍娃同志在党的路线上的严重错误以后，一致决定把她开除出阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会全会选举阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会政治局候补委员拉·阿利雅同志为阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会书记。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会在同中央监察委员会的联席会议上讨论了中央监察委员会主席科·塔什科反党的敌对活动，并一致决定把他开除出中央监察委员会和开除出党。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会在举行全会之际悲痛地得悉国际共产主义运动的著名战士、德意志民主共和国总统和阿尔巴尼亚人民的尊贵朋友威廉·皮克同志逝世的消息，中央委员会敬默一分钟表示哀悼。
