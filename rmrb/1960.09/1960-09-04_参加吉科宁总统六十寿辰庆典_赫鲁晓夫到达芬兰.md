Title: Khrushchev Arrives in Finland to Attend President Kekkonen's Sixtieth Birthday Celebration
Author:
Date: 1960-09-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, September 3rd: According to TASS, Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Khrushchev arrived in Helsinki on September 2nd to attend the celebration of Finnish President Kekkonen's sixtieth birthday. Khrushchev departed from Moscow on the afternoon of September 2nd.

Khrushchev was welcomed at the Helsinki station by Finnish President Kekkonen, Prime Minister Sukselainen, and other senior officials.

Finnish Prime Minister Sukselainen hosted a dinner in honor of Khrushchev. The Finnish side attending the banquet included Finnish President Kekkonen, Minister of Foreign Affairs Törngren, other government officials, and prominent figures from Finnish industry. The Soviet side attending the banquet included Khrushchev, Soviet Ambassador to Finland Zakharov, Director of the Scandinavian Countries Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Lunkov, and responsible personnel from the Soviet Embassy in Finland.

### 参加吉科宁总统六十寿辰庆典  赫鲁晓夫到达芬兰

1960-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    参加吉科宁总统六十寿辰庆典
    赫鲁晓夫到达芬兰
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社赫尔辛基讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫2日到达赫尔辛基参加芬兰总统吉科宁六十寿辰的庆典。赫鲁晓夫是在2日下午离开莫斯科的。
    在赫尔辛基车站欢迎赫鲁晓夫的有芬兰总统吉科宁、总理苏克舍拉宁和其他高级官员。
    芬兰总理苏克舍拉宁设晚宴招待赫鲁晓夫。芬兰方面出席宴会的有芬兰总统吉科宁、外交部部长特恩格林，其他政府官员、芬兰实业界著名人士。苏联方面出席宴会的有赫鲁晓夫、苏联驻芬兰大使扎哈罗夫、外交部斯堪的纳维亚国家司司长隆科夫和苏联驻芬兰大使馆的负责人员。
