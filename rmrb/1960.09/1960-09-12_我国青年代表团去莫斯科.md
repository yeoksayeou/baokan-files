Title: China Youth Delegation to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. At the invitation of the Soviet Youth Organizations Committee, the Chinese youth delegation, consisting of five members headed by Wang Zhaohua, representing the All-China Youth Federation to participate in the preparatory meeting of the World Youth Forum to be held in Moscow, left Beijing by plane for Moscow on the 11th. Qian Liren, Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation, and others saw them off at the airport.

### 我国青年代表团去莫斯科

1960-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    我国青年代表团去莫斯科
    新华社11日讯　应苏联青年组织委员会邀请，代表中华全国青年联合会参加即将在莫斯科举行的世界青年论坛筹备工作会议的中国青年代表团一行五人，在团长王照华率领下，于11日乘飞机离开北京前往莫斯科。到机场送行的，有中华全国青年联合会秘书长钱李仁等人。
