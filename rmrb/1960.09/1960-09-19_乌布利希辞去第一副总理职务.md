Title: Ulbricht Resigns from Position of First Deputy Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 17th: A notice published here states that the Prime Minister of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, granted the request of Walter Ulbricht, Chairman of the State Council of the German Democratic Republic, to relieve him of his duties as First Deputy Prime Minister of the Council of Ministers.

### 乌布利希辞去第一副总理职务

1960-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    乌布利希辞去第一副总理职务
    新华社柏林17日电　这里发表的一项通告说，德意志民主共和国部长会议总理奥托·格罗提渥满足了德意志民主共和国国务委员会主席瓦尔特·乌布利希的请求，解除他的部长会议第一副总理的职务。
