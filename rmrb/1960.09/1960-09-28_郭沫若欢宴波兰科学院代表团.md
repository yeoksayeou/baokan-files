Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Polish Academy of Sciences Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, held a banquet on the evening of the 26th to welcome the entire delegation of the Polish Academy of Sciences, led by Academician Tadeusz Kotarbiński, President of the Polish Academy of Sciences.

　　Guo Moruo spoke at the banquet, extending a warm welcome on behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences to the delegation of the Polish Academy of Sciences, and expressing heartfelt gratitude for the assistance Polish scientists have given to the development of China's scientific undertakings. Guo Moruo said that we hope that scientific cooperation between China and Poland will be further developed, making further contributions to our common socialist cause and world peace.

　　Academician Kotarbiński also spoke at the banquet, saying that from the moment the delegation set foot on Chinese soil, they deeply felt the warm friendship of the Chinese people towards them. He hoped that the friendship and cooperation between Chinese and Polish scientists would be further strengthened.

　　Polish Ambassador to China Jerzy Knothe and Counselor Dumbnicki attended the banquet.

　　Among those present were Zhang Jingfu and Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and many well-known scientists from the capital.

　　Before the banquet, President Guo Moruo met with all members of the delegation of the Polish Academy of Sciences.

### 郭沫若欢宴波兰科学院代表团

1960-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

　  郭沫若欢宴波兰科学院代表团
    新华社27日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若26日晚举行宴会，欢迎以波兰科学院院长塔德乌什·科塔宾斯基院士为首的波兰科学院代表团全体成员。
    郭沫若在宴会上讲话，他代表中国科学院向波兰科学院代表团表示热烈的欢迎，并对波兰科学家对我国科学事业的发展给予的帮助，表示衷心的感谢。郭沫若说，我们希望中波两国的科学合作进一步发展，为着我们共同的社会主义事业和世界和平作出进一步的贡献。
    科塔宾斯基院士也在宴会上讲话，他说，当代表团踏上中国土地的第一步，即深深感到了中国人民对他们的热诚的友谊。他希望中波两国科学家间的友谊和合作进一步加强。
    波兰驻华大使耶日·克诺泰、参赞邓布尼茨基等出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的，有中国科学院副院长张劲夫、吴有训和首都著名科学家多人。
    宴会开始前，郭沫若院长会见了波兰科学院代表团全体成员。
