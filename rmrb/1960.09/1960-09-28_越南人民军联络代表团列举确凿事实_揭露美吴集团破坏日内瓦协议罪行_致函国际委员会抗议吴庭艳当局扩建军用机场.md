Title: The Liaison Delegation of the Vietnamese People's Army Lists Conclusive Facts to Expose the Crimes of the U.S.-Ngo Dinh Diem Clique in Undermining the Geneva Accords; Sends Letter to the International Commission Protesting the Ngo Dinh Diem Authorities' Expansion of Military Airports
Author:
Date: 1960-09-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 27th: According to the Vietnamese "People's Daily," between September 16th and 22nd, the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army sent thirty-three letters to the International Commission in Vietnam, accusing the U.S. imperialists and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique of undermining the Geneva Accords in South Vietnam.

　　These accusations pointed out that in just five days in early June this year, five foreign warships transported more than 700 tons of weapons and other military supplies to ports such as Saigon, Hoi An, and Vung Tau. These included tank parts, artillery, anti-aircraft guns, heavy machine guns, radios, and engineering equipment.

　　After the International Commission reached an affirmative conclusion on the "U.S. Military Assistance Advisory Group" by a majority vote, the United States and the South Vietnamese authorities took the opportunity to openly increase the number of U.S. military personnel in South Vietnam. In May, aircraft transported 143 U.S. military personnel to South Vietnam on twenty-eight occasions. On August 27th, U.S. General McGarr arrived in South Vietnam to replace General Williams as the head of the "U.S. Military Assistance Advisory Group."

　　During five days at the end of July and the first half of August, the U.S.-Ngo clique illegally transported a total of 540 people, including some foreign officers and soldiers, into the demilitarized zone of South Vietnam. On September 15th, an Ngo Dinh Diem "C-47" aircraft illegally violated the airspace over the Vinh Linh demilitarized zone in North Vietnam.

　　The Liaison Delegation specifically drew the International Commission's attention to the Ngo Dinh Diem clique's retaliatory "sweeps" in Quang Ngai Province, the large-scale massacre of former resistance personnel in Cao Lanh Prison, the brutal mistreatment of more than 4,000 patriotic people in Con Dao concentration camp, and the large-scale arrests of patriotic intellectuals in the South.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 22nd: According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 21st, the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army recently wrote to the International Commission to protest the Ngo Dinh Diem authorities' violation of the Geneva Accords by expanding four military airports in South Vietnam as part of the U.S. war preparations.

　　The locations of these airports are: Rach Gia and Ca Mau (southwest of Saigon), Bien Hoa (north of Saigon), and Lien Khuong, 32 kilometers south of Da Lat Province (south-central Vietnam). After being expanded from the end of last year to the beginning of this year, the runways have been extended from 500-800 meters to over 1,000 meters. Modern U.S. radio equipment and warehouses have been established at the airports.

　　Among them, the runway at Lien Khuong Airport has been extended to nearly 2,000 meters, making it an important military airport in the U.S. air force base system in the central highlands of South Vietnam.

　　Many U.S. military aircraft have unloaded large quantities of equipment at this airport and Rach Gia Airport. U.S. military advisors have also arrived.

　　The Liaison Delegation of the Vietnamese People's Army requested the International Commission to send mobile task forces to investigate the illegal expansion of the aforementioned airports and prevent the recurrence of such actions that violate the Geneva Accords.

　　Earlier, the delegation had protested the expansion of Tan Son Nhut Airport near Saigon and Can Tho Airport in Can Tho Province, southwest of Saigon.

### 越南人民军联络代表团列举确凿事实  揭露美吴集团破坏日内瓦协议罪行  致函国际委员会抗议吴庭艳当局扩建军用机场

1960-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　越南人民军联络代表团列举确凿事实
　  揭露美吴集团破坏日内瓦协议罪行
    致函国际委员会抗议吴庭艳当局扩建军用机场
    新华社河内27日电　据越南“人民报”报道，在9月16日到22日期间，越南人民军总司令部联络代表团寄给越南国际委员会三十三封信，控告美帝国主义和吴庭艳集团在越南南方破坏日内瓦协议的行动。
    这些控告书指出，仅在今年6月初的五天中，就有五艘外国军舰向西贡、会安和头顿等港口运进七百多吨武器和其他军用物资，其中包括坦克零件、大炮、高射炮、重机关枪、无线电机和工兵用具等。
    在国际委员会以多数票对“美国军事援助顾问团”作出肯定结论后，美国和越南南方当局更趁机公开向越南南方增加美国军事人员。5月间，有二十八次飞机运载了一百四十三名美国军事人员进入越南南方；8月27日，美国麦克加尔将军到越南南方任“美国军事援助顾问团”团长，替换威廉斯将军。
    7月底的五天和8月上半月中，美吴集团共把包括一些外国军官和士兵在内的五百四十人非法运入越南南方非军事区。9月15日，吴庭艳的一架“C—47型”飞机非法侵犯越南北方永灵非军事区上空。
    联络代表团特别提请国际委员会注意吴庭艳集团在广义省进行的报复性的“扫荡”、在高岭监狱大规模屠杀前抗战人员和在昆仑岛集中营残酷虐待四千多名爱国人民的罪行以及大肆逮捕南方爱国知识分子的行动。
    新华社河内22日电　据越南通讯社21日讯：越南人民军总司令部联络代表团最近写信给国际委员会，抗议吴庭艳当局违反日内瓦协议，在越南南方扩建四个军事机场，作为美国备战活动的一部分。
    这些机场的位置在：迪石和金瓯（在西贡西南），比宁（西贡以北）和大叻省（越南中南部）以南三十二公里的廉庆。从去年年底到今年年初，这些机场经过扩建后，跑道已由五百至八百米延长到一千米以上，机场上已建立了现代化的美国无线电设备和仓库。
    其中，廉庆机场的跑道已延长到将近两千米，成了越南中南部高原上的美国空军基地体系中的一个重要军用机场。
    许多美国军用飞机在这个机场和迪石机场上卸下了大量装备。美国军事顾问也已抵达。
    越南人民军联络代表团要求国际委员会派遣机动工作队对非法扩建上述机场事进行调查，并防止再次发生这种违反日内瓦协议的行动。
    早些时候，代表团曾对扩建西贡附近的新山一机场和西贡西南的芹苴省机场提出过抗议。
