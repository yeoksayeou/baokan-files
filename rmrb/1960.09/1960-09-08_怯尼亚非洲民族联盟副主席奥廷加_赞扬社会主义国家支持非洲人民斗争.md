Title: Kenyan African National Union Vice President Odinga Praises Socialist Countries for Supporting the Struggle of the African People
Author:
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo – The Kenyan office of the African Association announced here on the evening of the 1st a statement by Odinga Odinga, Vice President of the Kenyan African National Union. He said that the leaders of the socialist countries have promised to continue to give us moral support in our struggle against colonialism and imperialism. He said, "I am encouraged by their unconditional support for our struggle for freedom and independence."

Odinga attended the Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs in Tokyo and visited socialist countries such as the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union.

Odinga said: "I am very amazed at the tremendous progress and achievements that have already occurred and are taking place in those socialist countries."

He said, "The progress within these countries is amazing and enormous, and it is developing at such a constantly remarkable speed that poverty is shrinking and disappearing at the same rate." He finally emphasized the demand for the immediate and unconditional release of Kenyan national movement leader Jomo Kenyatta, and for complete independence for Kenya now.

### 怯尼亚非洲民族联盟副主席奥廷加  赞扬社会主义国家支持非洲人民斗争

1960-09-08
第6版()
专栏：

    怯尼亚非洲民族联盟副主席奥廷加
    赞扬社会主义国家支持非洲人民斗争
    新华社开罗讯　非洲人协会怯尼亚办事处1日晚在这里公布了怯尼亚非洲民族联盟副主席奥金加·奥廷加的谈话。他说，社会主义国家的领袖们答应继续对我们反对殖民主义和帝国主义的斗争给予道义上的支持。他说，“对于他们对我们争取自由和独立的无条件支持，我受到鼓舞。”
    奥廷加出席了在东京举行的第六届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会，并访问了中华人民共和国和苏联等社会主义国家。
    奥廷加说：“我对于那些社会主义国家已经发生和正在发生的巨大进步和成就，感到非常惊异。”
    他说，“这些国家内的进展是惊人的和巨大的，它并以这样一种经常引人注目的速度发展着，因此，贫困在缩小，并以同样的速度在消失。”他最后强调要求立即无条件地释放怯尼亚民族运动领袖乔莫·肯雅塔，并在现在让怯尼亚完全独立。
