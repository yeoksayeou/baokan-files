Title: Direct Telegraph Communication Established Between Moscow and Havana
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 7

　　Moscow-Havana
　  Direct Telegraph Communication Established
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 27th: According to TASS, direct radio-telegraph communication between Moscow and the Cuban capital, Havana, was established on the morning of the 27th.
    Currently, the Soviet Union has regular telephone, telegraph, and radio circuits with eighty-six countries.

### 莫斯科哈瓦那之间  建立直达电报通信

1960-09-29
第7版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科哈瓦那之间
　  建立直达电报通信
    新华社莫斯科27日电　据塔斯社讯，莫斯科和古巴首都哈瓦那之间于27日晨建立了直达无线电报通信。
    现在，苏联同八十六个国家有着经常的电话电报无线电路。
