Title: Chinese-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation Leaves Beijing for Moscow to Attend Celebrations of the 11th Anniversary of the Founding of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. At the invitation of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, the delegation of the Chinese-Soviet Friendship Association, led by Xu Guangda, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and head of the delegation; Mao Yisheng, vice-chairman of the China-Soviet Friendship Association, vice-chairman of the Science and Technology Association of the People's Republic of China, and member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and deputy head of the delegation; and Yang Shu, secretary-general, vice president of the Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association, and member of the Standing Committee of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, left Beijing by plane this morning for Moscow to participate in the celebrations of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to pay a friendly visit.
Members of the delegation include Guo Mingqiu, Zhang Tianmin (currently abroad), Lu Zhao, Wang Wenshi (currently abroad), and Zhang Benlin.
Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Liao Chengzhi, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and vice president of the Chinese-Soviet Friendship Association, Li Dequan, vice president of the Chinese-Soviet Friendship Association, and Zhang Zhixiang, Gan Siqi, Fu Qiutao, Lin Bin, Lu Yang, Du Yanqing, Wang Zhaohua, Tian Xiujuan, Yang Shuo, Li Zhanwu, and others.
Soviet Embassy in China Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Sudarikov also saw them off at the airport.

### 应邀去苏参加庆祝我国成立十一周年活动　中苏友协代表团离京去莫斯科

1960-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    应邀去苏参加庆祝我国成立十一周年活动
　　中苏友协代表团离京去莫斯科
    新华社28日讯　应苏联对外友好和文化联系协会联合会和苏中友好协会的邀请，去苏联参加中华人民共和国成立十一周年的庆祝活动并进行友好访问的中苏友好协会代表团，在团长、中共中央委员许光达，副团长、中苏友好协会总会理事、中华人民共和国科学技术协会副主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员茅以升和秘书长、北京市中苏友好协会副会长、中共北京市委员会常务委员杨述率领下，今天上午由北京乘飞机前往莫斯科。
    代表团团员有，郭明秋、张天民（现在国外）、鲁钊、王汶石（现在国外）、张本林。
    到机场欢送的有，中共中央委员、中苏友好协会总会副会长廖承志，中苏友好协会总会副会长李德全以及张致祥、甘泗淇、傅秋涛、林彬、路扬、杜延庆、王照华、田秀涓、杨朔、李霑吾等。
    苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办苏达利柯夫也到机场欢送。
