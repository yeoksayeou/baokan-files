Title: Major Events in the Socialist Industrialization Process: Vietnam's First Iron and Steel Complex Begins Construction
Author:
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 5

    Major Events in the Socialist Industrialization Process
    Vietnam's First Iron and Steel Complex Begins Construction
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 1st: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the construction of the Thai Nguyen Iron and Steel Complex, the first iron and steel complex in all of Vietnam, officially began on August 31st after more than a year of active preparation.
    This project is being built with Chinese loans and technical assistance from China.
    After more than a year of active preparation, equipment such as an oxygen workshop, a welding workshop, a high-temperature water pool, and a power station have been established.
    Nguyen Van Tran, Minister of Heavy Industry of Vietnam, said at a meeting attended by 10,000 workers on August 31st that the construction of this iron and steel complex is a major event in the process of Vietnam's socialist industrialization.
    Amidst a storm of applause from the participants, Nguyen Van Tran poured concrete for the foundation of the first blast furnace.

### 社会主义工业化进程中的大事  越南第一个钢铁联合企业动工

1960-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    社会主义工业化进程中的大事
    越南第一个钢铁联合企业动工
    新华社河内1日电　据越南通讯社报道，全越南第一个钢铁联合企业——太原钢铁联合企业，经过一年多的积极准备后，8月31日正式动工兴建。
    这项工程是用中国的贷款以及在中国的技术援助下兴建的。
    经过一年多的积极准备，已经建立了一个氧气车间、一个焊接车间、一个高温水池和一座发电站等设备。
    越南重工业部部长阮文珍8月31日在有一万名工人参加的大会上讲话说，这座钢铁联合企业的兴建是越南社会主义工业化过程中的一件大事。
    在与会者的暴风雨般掌声中，阮文珍为建设第一座高炉的地基浇灌了混凝土。
