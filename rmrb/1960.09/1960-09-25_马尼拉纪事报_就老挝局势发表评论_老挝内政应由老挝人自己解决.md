Title: "Manila Chronicle" Publishes Commentary on Laotian Situation: Laotian Internal Affairs Should Be Solved by the Laotian People Themselves
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Manila news - The "Manila Chronicle" published an editorial on the 23rd entitled "Don't Interfere in Laos," stating, "The struggle in Laos is a civil war, an internal affair that should be resolved by the Laotian people themselves." The editorial condemned the Savannakhet rebel group, led by Phoumi Nosavan, for attempting to use the pretext of "anti-communism" to incite Western countries and non-communist Asian nations to oppose the legitimate Laotian government. The editorial pointed out that Thailand is already clamoring for direct intervention. The editorial emphasized that the Laotian people should be allowed to resolve what is clearly an internal affair.

### “马尼拉纪事报”就老挝局势发表评论　老挝内政应由老挝人自己解决

1960-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    “马尼拉纪事报”就老挝局势发表评论
　　老挝内政应由老挝人自己解决
    新华社24日讯　马尼拉消息：“马尼拉纪事报”23日发表题为“不要干涉老挝”的社论说，“老挝的斗争是一场内战，是应该由老挝人自己来解决的内政问题。”社论谴责以富米·诺萨万为首的沙湾拿吉叛乱集团企图借口“反共”来使西方国家和亚洲的非共产党国家来反对合法的老挝政府。社论指出，泰国已在叫嚣直接干涉。社论强调说，应该让老挝人自己来解决显然属于内政的问题。
