Title: U.S. Imperialism Persists in Antagonizing the Chinese People; Manipulates Voting Machine to Decide Not to Include the Question of Chinese Representation on the UN General Assembly Agenda
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, 28th dispatch from New York: The General Committee of the United Nations General Assembly held a meeting on the 27th to discuss the Soviet Union's request to include the issue of China's representation in the United Nations on the agenda of the current session of the United Nations General Assembly.

    U.S. Representative Wadsworth spoke first, vehemently opposing the inclusion of the China representation issue on the General Assembly agenda, again slandering China for "engaging in aggression" and "advocating violence," and that China "has been advancing southward from Tibet through armed invasion in order to extend its rule to other countries." He also described the Chinese people's resolute opposition and exposure of the U.S. imperialist conspiracy to use the United Nations to swallow the Congo as an "example" of behavior that "worships fanatical violence."

    British Representative Dean echoed the U.S. position and opposed including the China representation issue on the agenda.

    Soviet Representative Zorin emphasized in his speech that the Soviet delegation believes it is essential to include the issue of China's representation in the United Nations on the agenda. This is an urgent and long-overdue issue. The fact that a major power, one of the founding members of the United Nations, is deprived of the right to participate in the work of the United Nations is becoming more intolerable year by year. Zorin said that excluding the Chiang Kai-shek elements, who represent no one, from the United Nations and restoring the legitimate rights of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations would be a major contribution to improving the international situation and would help to effectively solve major international problems.

    Zorin condemned the U.S. for its attitude of opposing the inclusion of the China representation issue on the agenda and the UK for following the U.S. position.

    Representatives from countries such as Bulgaria, Romania, Ceylon, Iraq, and Sudan supported the inclusion of the China representation issue on the agenda.

    However, under the manipulation of the United States, the General Committee passed the U.S. proposal against including the China representation issue on the agenda. The voting result of the committee on the U.S. proposal was: twelve votes in favor, seven votes against, and one abstention. Representatives of the Soviet Union, Romania, Bulgaria, Ceylon, Iraq, Sudan, and Yugoslavia voted against. ### 美帝国主义坚持与中国人民为敌  操纵表决机器决定不把中国代表权问题列入联大议程

1960-09-29
第7版()
专栏：

　　美帝国主义坚持与中国人民为敌
    操纵表决机器决定不把中国代表权问题列入联大议程
    新华社28日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会总务委员会27日举行会议，讨论苏联提出的关于把中国在联合国的代表权问题列入本届联合国大会议程的要求。
    美国代表华兹沃斯首先发言，竭力反对把中国代表权问题列入大会议程，再次诬蔑中国“实行侵略”和“鼓吹暴力行为”，“一直在通过武力入侵，从西藏向南推进，以便把统治权扩展到其他国家”。他还把中国人民坚决反对和揭露美帝国主义利用联合国来鲸吞刚果的阴谋，说成是“崇尚狂热的暴力”行为的“例证”。
    英国代表迪安附和美国的立场，反对把中国代表权问题列入议程。
    苏联代表佐林在发言时强调说，苏联代表团认为，把中国在联合国的代表权问题列入议程是十分必要的。这是一个迫切的、早已成熟了的问题。作为联合国创始国之一的大国被剥夺了参加联合国工作的权利，这种情况一年比一年令人不能容忍。佐林说，把任何人也不代表的蒋介石分子从联合国中排除出去，恢复中华人民共和国在联合国中的合法权利将是对改善国际局势的重大贡献，并将有助于有效地解决重大国际问题。
    佐林谴责美国采取反对把中国代表权问题列入议程的态度和英国追随美国的立场。
    保加利亚、罗马尼亚、锡兰、伊拉克和苏丹等国家的代表支持把中国代表权问题列入议程。
    但是在美国的操纵下，总务委员会通过了美国反对把中国代表权问题列入议程的提案。委员会对美国提案的表决结果是：十二票赞成、七票反对、一票弃权。投反对票的是苏联、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、锡兰、伊拉克、苏丹和南斯拉夫的代表。投赞成票的是美国、英国、法国、意大利、加拿大、日本、巴基斯坦、哥斯达黎加、海地、巴拿马、委内瑞拉和蒋介石分子。弃权的是利比亚代表。
    接着总务委员会转入对苏联提出的另一个项目的讨论——“关于对刚果共和国的政治独立和领土完整的威胁”。美国代表团也同样反对把这个问题列入联大议程。美国代表借口联大紧急会议已就刚果局势通过了一项决议，因此他认为进一步讨论刚果局势是“不适宜的”。但是，从代表们的发言中可以看出，美国代表团没有得到必要的支持。英国代表团为了使他的伙伴不致丢脸，建议无限期地延期讨论。这样实际上把刚果问题从大会议程中剔除出去。委员会终于通过了英国的建议。
