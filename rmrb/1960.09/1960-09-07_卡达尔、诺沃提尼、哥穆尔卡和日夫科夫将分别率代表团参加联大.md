Title: Kádár, Novotný, Gomułka, and Zhivkov to Lead Delegations to the UN General Assembly Respectively
Author:
Date: 1960-09-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 4th: According to reports in local newspapers, Hungarian Minister of State Kádár will lead the Hungarian delegation to the Fifteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly.

	This was designated by the Hungarian Council of Ministers at its meeting on September 1st and approved by the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th: According to reports in local newspapers, Czechoslovak President Novotný will lead the Czechoslovak delegation to the Fifteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly.

	This was decided by the Czechoslovak government at a meeting held on the 2nd.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 6th: The Polish Council of State and the Council of Ministers held a joint meeting on the 3rd, deciding that Gomułka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and member of the Council of State, will lead the Polish delegation to the Fifteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 6th: According to reports in local newspapers, the Bulgarian government has decided to send a delegation headed by Zhivkov, member of the Presidium of the Parliament, to attend the upcoming Fifteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 卡达尔、诺沃提尼、哥穆尔卡和日夫科夫将分别率代表团参加联大

1960-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    卡达尔、诺沃提尼、哥穆尔卡和日夫科夫将分别率代表团参加联大
    新华社布达佩斯4日电　据此间报纸报道：匈牙利国务部长卡达尔将率领匈牙利代表团参加联合国大会第十五届会议。
    这是匈牙利部长会议在9月1日的会议上指定，并由匈牙利人民共和国主席团批准的。
    新华社布拉格4日电　据此间报纸报道：捷克斯洛伐克总统诺沃提尼将率领捷克斯洛伐克代表团参加联合国大会第十五届会议。
    这是捷克斯洛伐克政府在2日举行的会议上决定的。
    新华社华沙6日电　波兰国务委员会和部长会议3日举行联席会议，决定由波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记、国务委员会委员哥穆尔卡率领波兰代表团参加联合国大会第十五届会议。
    新华社索非亚6日电　据此间报纸报道，保加利亚政府决定派遣以议会主席团委员日夫科夫为首的代表团前往参加即将召开的联合国大会第十五届会议。
