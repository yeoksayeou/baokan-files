Title: Mongolian Cycling Team Competes in Changchun
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, September 24th: The men's and women's cycling teams from the Mongolian People's Republic held a friendly competition today in Changchun with the Chinese cycling team and the Jilin Provincial cycling team.

　　As a result, the Chinese team won the women's 25-kilometer and men's 100-kilometer team championships; the Jilin Provincial team won the women's 25-kilometer runner-up. The visiting team won the men's 100-kilometer team runner-up.

　　After the competition, Zhang Wenhai, Vice Governor of Jilin Province, and Li Beihai, Vice Mayor of Changchun, met with the members of both teams.

### 蒙古自行车队在长春举行比赛

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　蒙古自行车队在长春举行比赛
    据新华社长春24日电　蒙古人民共和国男女自行车队，今天在长春市与中国自行车队和吉林省自行车队举行友谊比赛。
    结果中国队获得女子二十五公里、男子一百公里团体赛冠军；吉林省队获得女子二十五公里亚军。客队获得男子一百公里团体赛亚军。
    比赛结束后，吉林省副省长张文海、长春市副市长李北淮接见了双方队员。
