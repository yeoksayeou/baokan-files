Title: Chinese Music Workers Delegation Leaves for Geneva to Participate in the 16th International Music Competition
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. A five-member delegation of Chinese music workers, led by Zhuang Ying, left by plane for Geneva today to participate in the 16th International Music Competition held in Geneva. The delegation includes pianists Zhao Pingguo and Lin Ling, horn player Han Xianguang, and accompanist Zhu Yaqing.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Qu Wu, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Xu Guangxiao, Vice Minister of Culture, and others.

### 参加第十六届国际音乐比赛会  我国音乐工作者代表团赴日内瓦

1960-09-10
第6版()
专栏：

    参加第十六届国际音乐比赛会
    我国音乐工作者代表团赴日内瓦
    新华社9日讯　应邀参加在日内瓦举行的第十六届国际音乐比赛会的中国音乐工作者代表团一行五人，今天乘飞机前往日内瓦。代表团由庄映率领，团员中有钢琴家赵屏国、林玲，圆号韩铣光，伴奏朱雅青。
    前往机场欢送的有：对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武、文化部副部长徐光霄等。
