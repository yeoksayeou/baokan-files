Title: Iwai Akira Leaves Beijing to Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-09-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 26th. At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Iwai Akira, Secretary-General of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, and his secretary, Uetsuma Yoshiaki, left Beijing for the Soviet Union on the morning of the 26th. They were seen off at Beijing by Li Jiebo, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the Secretariat, and Chen Yu, Director of the International Liaison Department.
    Also seeing them off was Japanese peace figure Kinkazu Saionji, who was in Beijing.

### 岩井章离北京去苏联访问

1960-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　岩井章离北京去苏联访问
　  新华社26日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的日本工会总评议会事务局局长岩井章和秘书上妻美章，在26日早晨离开北京去苏联访问。他们离开北京时，欢送的有中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯、书记处书记康永和、国际联络部部长陈宇等。
    欢送的还有在京日本和平人士西园寺公一。
