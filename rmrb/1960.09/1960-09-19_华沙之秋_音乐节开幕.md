Title: "Warsaw Autumn" Music Festival Opens
Author:
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 5

    "Warsaw Autumn" Music Festival Opens
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 18th—The annual "Warsaw Autumn" Music Festival opened on the evening of the 17th at the Warsaw National Opera House.
    The Chinese Cultural Delegation, headed by Shen Yanbing, who is currently visiting Warsaw, was invited to attend the opening ceremony and watched the performance.

### “华沙之秋”音乐节开幕

1960-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    “华沙之秋”音乐节开幕
    新华社华沙18日电　一年一度的“华沙之秋”音乐节17日晚在华沙国家歌剧院举行了开幕式。
    正在华沙访问的以沈雁冰为首的中国文化代表团应邀参加了开幕式，并观看了演出。
