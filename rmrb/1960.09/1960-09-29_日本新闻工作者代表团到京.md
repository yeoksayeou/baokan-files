Title: Japanese Journalists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. A Japanese Journalists Delegation of twelve people, led by Kobayashi Yuichi, Chairman of the Japan Congress of Journalists, arrived in Beijing this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association for a visit.

Welcoming them at the airport were Wang Yunsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, and other members of the press from the capital.

### 日本新闻工作者代表团到京

1960-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

　  日本新闻工作者代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来华访问的日本新闻工作者代表团一行十二人，在日本记者会议议长小林雄一率领下今天下午到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席王芸生以及首都新闻界其他人士。
