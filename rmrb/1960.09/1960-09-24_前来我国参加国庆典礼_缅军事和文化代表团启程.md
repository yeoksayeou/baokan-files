Title: Burmese Military and Cultural Delegations Depart to Attend National Day Celebrations in Our Country
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 23rd. The Burmese Military Friendship Delegation to China, a group of twenty-eight, led by Brigadier General Maung Maung, Director of the Burmese Military Training Bureau, left here by plane this morning for Beijing.

    The delegation also includes Deputy Leader Colonel Kyaw Soe, Military Secretary of the Ministry of Defence, Colonel Kyi Maung, Commander of the Rangoon Military Region, Colonel Tin Maung, Commander of the 12th Infantry Brigade, and Lieutenant Colonel Khin Maung Kyaw, Commander of the 2nd Infantry Brigade, among others.

    During their stay in Beijing, the delegation will participate in the celebrations of the eleventh anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

    Brigadier General Tin Pe, Colonel Kyi Myaing, Colonel Yin Lyun, Colonel Min Din and other officers were at the airport today to see the delegation off.

    Li Yimang, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, Jiang Keding, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and Lu Zhan, Deputy Military Attaché, also went to the airport to see them off.

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 23rd. The first group of members of the Burmese Cultural Delegation to China, led by Deputy Leader U Soe Wu, Deputy Director of the Army Psychological Warfare and Education Bureau, left here by plane this morning for Beijing.

    The delegation will participate in China's National Day celebrations and the celebration of the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

    The leader of the delegation, Colonel So Min, Director of the Border Areas Administration, has already arrived in Beijing.

    The delegation consists of one hundred and sixty-six people, including Shan and Kachin artists, film actors, and students from the National Music and Dance School. The second and final groups of members of the delegation will depart on September 24th and 25th, respectively.

    Lei Yang, Xiao Ming, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and other members of the Embassy were at the airport today to see off the Burmese artists.

### 前来我国参加国庆典礼　缅军事和文化代表团启程

1960-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

    前来我国参加国庆典礼
　　缅军事和文化代表团启程
    新华社仰光23日电　缅甸访华军事友好代表团一行二十八人在团长、缅甸军事训练局局长貌貌准将率领下今天上午乘飞机离开这里前往北京。
    代表团还包括副团长、国防部军事秘书觉梭上校，仰光军区司令基貌上校，第十二步兵旅旅长丁貌上校和第二步兵旅旅长钦貌觉中校等。
    代表团在北京逗留期间，将参加中华人民共和国建国十一周年庆典。
    今天在机场为代表团送行的有丁坡准将、漆棉上校、饮略上校、敏登上校和其他军官。
    中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使李一氓、中国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定和副武官路展也到机场送行。
    新华社仰光23日电　缅甸访华文化代表团第一批团员在副团长、陆军心理战和教育局副局长吴苏吴率领下，今天上午乘飞机离开这里前往北京。
    代表团将参加中国国庆典礼和庆祝中缅边界条约签字。
    代表团团长、边境地区管理局局长苏敏上校现在已经到达北京。
    代表团由一百六十六人组成，其中包括掸族和克钦族的艺术家、电影演员和国立音乐舞蹈学校的学生。代表团的第二批和最后一批团员将分别在9月24日和25日动身。
    今天到机场为缅甸艺术家送行的有中国驻缅甸大使馆参赞雷扬、萧明和大使馆其他工作人.
