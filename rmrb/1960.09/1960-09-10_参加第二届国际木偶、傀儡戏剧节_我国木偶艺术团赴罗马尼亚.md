Title: China Puppet Art Troupe to Romania to Participate in the Second International Puppet and Marionette Theater Festival
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. According to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the Chinese Puppet Art Troupe, consisting of forty people led by Chen Hong, departed Beijing by plane today for Bucharest to participate in the Second International Puppet and Marionette Theater Festival held in Romania. Hua Jia, the Chinese judge attending the festival, also traveled on the same flight.

Those who saw them off at the airport today included Qu Wu, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Xu Guangxiao, Vice Minister of Culture, among others.

Bashkiliro, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, also saw them off at the airport.

### 参加第二届国际木偶、傀儡戏剧节  我国木偶艺术团赴罗马尼亚

1960-09-10
第6版()
专栏：

    参加第二届国际木偶、傀儡戏剧节
    我国木偶艺术团赴罗马尼亚
    新华社9日讯　根据中罗文化合作协定1960年执行计划，我国参加在罗马尼亚举行的第二届国际木偶、傀儡戏剧节的中国木偶艺术团一行四十人，在团长陈虹率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京去布加勒斯特。我国出席这个戏剧节的评判委员华嘉也同机前往。
    今天到机场欢送的有：对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武，文化部副部长徐光霄等。
    罗马尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办巴什基洛也到机场欢送。
