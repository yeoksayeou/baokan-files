Title: International Conference of Food, Tobacco, Hotel and Restaurant Workers' Unions Decides to Strengthen Struggle to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, September 1st: The Third International Conference of Food, Tobacco Industry, Hotel and Restaurant Workers' Unions was held in Budapest from August 27th to 31st. Representatives of trade union organizations from thirty-six countries attended the conference.

After discussion, the conference adopted two resolutions: one on "the struggle of workers in the food, tobacco industry, hotels and restaurants to improve living and working conditions, to defend trade union rights and democratic freedoms, to strengthen solidarity and defend world peace," and the other on "the struggle of workers in the food, tobacco industry, hotels and restaurants in colonial and dependent countries to improve living and working conditions and to realize their national aspirations, and the current state of the struggle."

The conference also expressed firm support for the anti-imperialist struggles of the people of Cuba, the Congo, Algeria and Japan.

The telegram to the Cuban people adopted by the conference said that the representatives of the conference paid tribute to the heroic struggle of the Cuban people and government to defend national sovereignty and complete economic independence.

In a telegram to the Congolese people, the conference expressed its support for the Congolese people's current heroic struggle for national unity and complete independence from the colonialists, and condemned the divisive activities carried out by the imperialists, headed by the United States, in order to plunder the natural resources of Katanga Province.

In a letter to the Algerian people, the conference said that the representatives of the conference paid tribute to the Algerian people who had waged a heroic struggle for national independence for six years, and expressed fraternal solidarity and sympathy.

### 食品、烟草，旅馆与餐馆工会国际代表会议  决定加强斗争保卫和平

1960-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

    食品、烟草，旅馆与餐馆工会国际代表会议
    决定加强斗争保卫和平
    据新华社布达佩斯1日电　食品、烟草工业，旅馆与餐馆工人工会国际第三次代表会议从8月27日到31日在布达佩斯举行。出席这次会议的有三十六个国家的工会组织的代表。
    会议经过讨论，通过了关于“食品、烟草工业，旅馆与餐馆的劳动者为改善生活与劳动条件，为保卫工会权利和民主自由权利，为加强团结和保卫世界和平的斗争”，和“殖民地和附属国食品、烟草工业，旅馆和餐馆的劳动者为改善生活和劳动条件，为实现他们民族愿望的斗争和斗争现状”的两个决议。
    会议并对古巴、刚果、阿尔及利亚和日本人民反对帝国主义的斗争表示坚决的支持。
    会议通过的给古巴人民的电报中说，这次会议的代表对古巴人民和政府为捍卫国家的主权和经济的完全独立所进行的英勇斗争表示敬意。
    会议在给刚果人民的电报中表示支持刚果人民目前为国家的统一和完全独立而同殖民主义者所进行的英勇斗争，并谴责以美国为首的帝国主义者为了掠夺加丹加省的天然资源而进行的分裂活动。
    会议在告阿尔及利亚人民书中说，会议的代表向为了民族独立进行了六年英勇斗争的阿尔及利亚人民致敬，并表示兄弟般的团结和同情。
