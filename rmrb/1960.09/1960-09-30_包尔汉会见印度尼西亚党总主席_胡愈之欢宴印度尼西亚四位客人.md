Title: Burhan Meets with Indonesian Party Chairman / Hu Yuzhi Hosts Banquet for Four Indonesian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Burhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, met today with Asmara Hadi, Chairman of the Indonesian Party, and his wife, as well as Asnawi Said, Secretary-General of the Indonesian Party. The hosts and guests had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet today to warmly welcome Rusli, Secretary-General of the Indonesian Parliament, and his wife, as well as Brotodingingrat, First Secretary of the Indonesian Presidential Office, and his wife, who are visiting China.

Burhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, Zhang Su, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chen Cisheng, Deputy Secretary-General, Mei Gongbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Foreign Affairs Institute, and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General, attended the banquet as companions.

Hu Yuzhi, on behalf of the Foreign Affairs Institute, expressed a warm welcome to the friends from Indonesia. He said that the friendly relations between our two countries have a long history and many commonalities. We want to liberate Taiwan, and you want to recover West Irian. We are all participants in the Bandung Asian-African Conference and are working to realize the Bandung spirit. Your visit is of great significance to promoting the development of friendly relations between our two countries. Hu Yuzhi proposed a toast to the victory against imperialism and colonialism, and to the consolidation and development of the friendship between our two countries.

The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere, and the hosts and guests chatted cordially around the table. Burhan said that your visit to China is a manifestation of the deep friendship between the peoples of China and Indonesia, and this friendship cannot be destroyed by any force. Rusli said that after coming to China, he saw many common points, which are the foundation of our two countries' friendship. We believe that small setbacks in the friendly relationship will be resolved smoothly. Brotodingingrat also said that our history and goals are consistent, and our friendship will last forever.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly stood up and toasted to the fight against imperialism and colonialism, and to the development of friendship between the two countries.

### 包尔汉会见印度尼西亚党总主席  胡愈之欢宴印度尼西亚四位客人

1960-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　包尔汉会见印度尼西亚党总主席
　  胡愈之欢宴印度尼西亚四位客人
    新华社29日讯　中国印度尼西亚友协会长包尔汉今天会见了印度尼西亚党总主席阿斯马腊·哈迪和他的夫人以及印度尼西亚党秘书长阿斯纳威·赛义德。宾主之间进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    新华社29日讯　中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之今天举行宴会。热烈欢迎来我国访问的印度尼西亚国会秘书长鲁斯里和夫人，印度尼西亚总统府办公厅第一秘书布罗托迪宁格拉特和夫人。
    中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员张苏、副秘书长陈此生，政协全国委员会副秘书长梅龚彬，外交学会秘书长吴茂荪、副秘书长吴晓达等出席宴会作陪。
    胡愈之代表外交学会对来自印度尼西亚的朋友表示热烈欢迎。他说，我们两个国家的友好关系历史悠久，并且有很多共同的地方，我们要解放台湾，你们要收回西伊里安。我们都是万隆亚非会议的参加者，都为实现万隆精神而努力。你们的来访，对促进我们两个国家间友好关系的发展有着重大意义。胡愈之提议，为了反对帝国主义和殖民主义的胜利，为了巩固和发展我们两国的友谊干杯。
    宴会上充满了热情、友好气氛，宾主围桌亲切畅叙。包尔汉说，你们来中国访问，是中国、印度尼西亚两国人民深厚友谊的表现，这个友谊是任何力量破坏不了的。鲁斯里说，来中国后看到了很多共同点，这是我们两国友谊的基础，我们相信在友谊关系中出现的小波折，是会顺利解决的。布罗托迪宁格拉特也说，我们的历史和奋斗目标是一致的，我们的友谊万古长青。
    席间，宾主不断起立，为反对帝国主义和殖民主义，为两国友谊的发展干杯。
