Title: Pieck's State Funeral Scheduled for the 23rd
Author:
Date: 1960-09-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 20th: According to today's "Neues Deutschland" report, the German Democratic Republic will hold a state funeral at 4:00 PM on September 23rd at the Socialist Memorial Cemetery in Berlin-Friedrichsfelde to bury the ashes of the late President of the German Democratic Republic, Wilhelm Pieck.

At that time, people from all walks of life will attend the funeral.

### 皮克国葬定23日举行

1960-09-21
第6版()
专栏：

　  皮克国葬定23日举行
    新华社柏林20日电　据“新德意志报”今天报道，德意志民主共和国23日下午四时将在柏林—腓特烈费耳德的社会主义者纪念公墓举行国葬仪式，安葬德意志民主共和国已故总统威廉·皮克的骨灰。
    届时各界公众将参加葬仪。
