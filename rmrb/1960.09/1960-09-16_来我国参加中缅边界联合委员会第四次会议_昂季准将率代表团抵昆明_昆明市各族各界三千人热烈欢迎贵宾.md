Title: Brigadier General Aung Gyi Leads Delegation to Kunming for the Fourth Meeting of the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee; Three Thousand People from All Ethnic Groups and Circles in Kunming Give a Warm Welcome to the Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 15th - The Burmese delegation, led by the Chief Representative of Myanmar and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, Brigadier General Aung Gyi, along with advisors and accompanying personnel, a total of twenty-five people, arrived in Kunming today from Yangon on a special plane to attend the Fourth Meeting of the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee. They received a warm welcome from three thousand people from all ethnic groups and circles in Kunming.

At 12:00 noon, the plane carrying the distinguished guests landed slowly. Liu Minghui, Deputy Governor of Yunnan Province, Lu Ruilin, Major General of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, and others walked to the front of the plane to greet the guests with warm handshakes. The distinguished guests accepted flowers presented by the youth and shook hands with the heads of various departments of the province and city, as well as officers of the troops stationed in Kunming. When the distinguished guests, accompanied by Deputy Governor Liu Minghui, Major General Lu Ruilin, and others, toured the airport to meet the welcoming crowds, the scene was filled with the sounds of gongs and drums, and continuous applause. People shouted in both Chinese and Burmese: "Long live China-Myanmar friendship!"

Among the welcoming crowd, young men and women from more than twenty ethnic groups in Yunnan Province, dressed in festive costumes, beat elephant-foot drums, played the large three-stringed instrument, and waved bouquets and colorful scarves to express their respect to the distinguished guests. When Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Brigadier General San Yu, and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Deputy Governor Liu Minghui and Major General Lu Ruilin, left the airport in special cars to their residences, they were again warmly welcomed by more than a thousand people from all ethnic groups and circles in Kunming lining the streets.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee; Li Ping, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Provincial People's Committee; Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming; Li Xifu, Major General of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming; Cun Shusheng and Wang Shaoyan, Chairmen of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Dao Jingban (Dai nationality), Governor of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture; Si Lasha (Jingpo nationality), Deputy Governor; Hu Zhonghua (Kawa nationality), Deputy Commissioner of the Lincang Special District; Chen Yanbin and Huang Fenglin, Chinese representatives of the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee; and Ding Rongchang, Major General and advisor.

U Maung Maung So, Consul-General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 15th - Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Chief Representative of the Burmese delegation to the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee, and representatives U San Lwin, Lieutenant Colonel So Min, U Khun Aung Kya, as well as delegation advisors Brigadier General San Yu, Lieutenant Colonel La Aung, and Lieutenant Colonel Ba Than, paid a visit to Yu Yichuan, Governor of Yunnan Province, this afternoon. Governor Yu Yichuan also had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Liu Minghui and Liu Zhuofu, Deputy Governors of Yunnan Province; Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Provincial People's Committee; and Li Ping, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Provincial People's Committee.

Also present were Huang Fenglin, the Chinese representative of the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee in Kunming, and Ding Rongchang, Major General and advisor.

U Maung Maung So, Consul-General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, was also present.

Brigadier General Aung Gyi and the representatives and advisors of the Burmese delegation to the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee also paid a visit this afternoon to Lieutenant General Chen Kang, the head of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming.

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, 15th - Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Chief Representative of the Burmese delegation to the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee, left here by plane this morning to go to Beijing to attend the fourth meeting of the Joint Committee.

Accompanying Brigadier General Aung Gyi were the Burmese representatives, advisors, and staff members of the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee.

Colonel Kyin Nyo of the General Staff Department and others saw them off at Yangon Airport.

Li Yimang, Chinese Ambassador to Myanmar, Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché, and personnel from the Embassy also saw them off at the airport. (Attached picture)

The Burmese delegation led by Brigadier General Aung Gyi received a warm welcome from the people of all ethnic groups and circles in Kunming.
                                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Lü Huachang

### 来我国参加中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议  昂季准将率代表团抵昆明  昆明市各族各界三千人热烈欢迎贵宾

1960-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    来我国参加中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议
    昂季准将率代表团抵昆明
    昆明市各族各界三千人热烈欢迎贵宾
    新华社昆明15日电　来我国参加中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议的以缅甸首席代表、缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季准将为首的缅甸代表团和顾问及随行人员一行二十五人，今天乘专机由仰光到达昆明，受到昆明市各族各界三千人的热烈欢迎。
    中午十二时，贵宾们乘坐的飞机徐徐着陆，云南省副省长刘明辉，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队负责人鲁瑞林少将等走到飞机前面迎接，和贵宾们热烈握手。贵宾们接受了青年们献的鲜花，并和省、市各部门的负责人，驻昆明部队军官等一一握手。当贵宾们由刘明辉副省长、鲁瑞林少将等陪同，绕机场一周和欢迎的群众见面时，全场锣鼓喧天，掌声不息。人们用中缅两国语言高呼：“中缅友好万岁！”
    在欢迎的人群中，云南省二十多个民族的男女青年，穿着节日盛装，敲着象脚鼓，弹起大三弦，挥舞着花束和彩巾，向贵宾们表示敬意。当昂季准将和山友准将等贵宾，在刘明辉副省长和鲁瑞林少将陪同下，乘专车离机场到住所时，又受到昆明市各族各界一千多人的夹道欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的，还有云南省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，省人民委员会外事处处长李萍，昆明市市长潘朔端，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队负责人黎锡福少将，政协云南省委员会主席寸树声、王少岩，德宏傣族景颇族自治州州长刀京版（傣族）、副州长司拉山（景颇族），临沧专区副专员胡忠华（佧佤族），中缅边界联合委员会中国代表陈雁彬、黄凤麟和顾问丁荣昌少将等。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭等，也到机场欢迎。
    新华社昆明15日电　中缅边界联合委员会缅甸代表团首席代表昂季准将和代表吴山伦、苏敏上校、吴昆翁佳以及代表团顾问山友准将、拉昂上校、巴丹上校，今天下午拜会了云南省省长于一川。于一川省长并且同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    拜会时在座的有：云南省副省长刘明辉、刘卓甫，省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，省人民委员会外事处处长李萍等。
    在座的，还有中缅边界联合委员会在昆明的中国代表黄凤麟、顾问丁荣昌少将。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭也在座。
    昂季准将和中缅边界联合委员会缅甸代表团的代表、顾问们，今天下午还拜会了中国人民解放军驻昆明部队负责人陈康中将。
    新华社仰光15日电　中缅边界联合委员会的缅甸首席代表昂季准将今天上午乘飞机离开这里前往北京参加联合委员会第四次会议。
    随同昂季准将前往的还有中缅边界联合委员会的缅甸代表、顾问和工作人员。
    到仰光机场送行的有总参谋部的庆牛上校等。
    中国驻缅甸大使李一氓、武官蒋克定上校和大使馆人员等也到机场送行。（附图片）
    以昂季准将为首的缅甸代表团受到昆明市各族各界人民的热烈欢迎
                                    新华社记者　吕华昌摄
