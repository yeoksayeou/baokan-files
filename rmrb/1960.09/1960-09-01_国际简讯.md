International Briefs

    Mongolian Ambassador to Guinea Presents Credentials
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Ochirbat, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Republic of Guinea, presented his credentials to the President of Guinea, Sékou Touré, on August 15.

    East Germany and Burma Decide to Establish Consulates General
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the governments of the German Democratic Republic and the Union of Burma have decided to establish consulates general in their respective capitals.

    Soviet Union Agrees to Provide India with Another Loan
    Moscow Dispatch: The Soviet government has agreed to provide India with another loan of 500 million rubles. This was the result of talks between Indian Finance Minister Desai and Soviet government leaders during his visit to Moscow in July this year.

    Soviet Union and Ghana Sign Economic Agreement
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Soviet Union and the Republic of Ghana signed an agreement on economic and technical cooperation and a trade agreement in Moscow on August 28.

    The economic and technical agreement stipulates that the Soviet government will provide the government of the Republic of Ghana with a long-term loan of 160 million rubles to cover Ghana's expenses for Soviet technical assistance.

    The trade agreement stipulates that both parties will provide each other with most-favored-nation treatment on various issues related to trade and navigation.

### 国际简讯

1960-09-01
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    蒙驻几内亚大使呈递国书
    据新华社讯　蒙古人民共和国驻几内亚共和国特命全权大使奥其尔巴特8月15日向几内亚总统塞古·杜尔递交国书。
    德缅决定互建领事馆
    据新华社讯　德意志民主共和国政府和缅甸联邦政府已经决定在两国首都建立总领事馆。
    苏联同意再给印度一笔贷款
    莫斯科讯　苏联政府同意再给印度五亿卢布贷款。这是印度财政部长德赛在今年7月访问莫斯科期间同苏联政府领导人会谈所取得的结果。
    苏联加纳签订经济协定
    据新华社讯　8月28日苏联和加纳共和国在莫斯科签订了经济和技术合作协定及贸易协定。
    经济和技术协定规定，苏联政府向加纳共和国政府提供一亿六千万卢布的长期贷款，作为加纳支付苏联所提供的技术援助的费用。
    贸易协定规定，双方在贸易和航行的各种问题上互相提供最惠国条件。
