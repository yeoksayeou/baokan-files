Title: My Newly Appointed Ambassador to Iraq, Zhang Weilie, Arrives in Baghdad
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, September 17th. China's newly appointed ambassador to Iraq, Zhang Weilie, arrived here by plane this morning.

At the airport, Ambassador Zhang Weilie was welcomed by Rafik, Director of the Protocol Department of the Iraqi Ministry of Foreign Affairs, diplomatic envoys from socialist countries stationed in Iraq, representatives of the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association, and diplomatic personnel from the Chinese Embassy.

### 我新任驻伊拉克大使张伟烈抵巴格达

1960-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    我新任驻伊拉克大使张伟烈抵巴格达
    新华社巴格达17日电　中国新任驻伊拉克大使张伟烈今天上午乘飞机到达这里。
    张伟烈大使在机场上受到伊拉克外交部礼宾司司长拉菲克、社会主义国家驻伊拉克外交使节、伊拉克—中国友好协会的代表以及中国大使馆外交人员的欢迎。
