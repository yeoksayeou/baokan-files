Title: President Touré to Visit Vietnam at the Invitation of President Ho Chi Minh
Author:
Date: 1960-09-14
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 13th: The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam announced today that, at the invitation of President Ho Chi Minh, President Sékou Touré and the Guinean government delegation will arrive in Hanoi on September 15, 1960.

### 应胡志明主席的邀请  杜尔总统将访越南

1960-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    应胡志明主席的邀请
    杜尔总统将访越南
    新华社河内13日电　越南民主共和国外交部今天发表通告说：应胡志明主席的邀请，塞古·杜尔总统和几内亚共和国政府访越代表团，将于1960年9月15日到达河内。
