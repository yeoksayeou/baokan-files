Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Brazilian Architects Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 14th – Vice Premier Chen Yi received the entire Brazilian architects delegation headed by João Baptista Vilanova Artigas, Vice President of the Brazilian Institute of Architects, this evening and held cordial and friendly talks with them. Present at the reception were Yang Chunmao and Liang Sicheng, Vice Chairmen of the Architectural Society of China, and Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association. (Attached picture)
Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Brazilian architects delegation visiting our country on September 14th. The picture shows a group photo before the reception. The third person from the right in the front row is the head of the delegation, João Baptista Vilanova Artigas, Vice President of the Brazilian Institute of Architects. ### 陈毅副总理接见巴西建筑家代表团

1960-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见巴西建筑家代表团
    新华社14日讯　陈毅副总理今晚接见了以巴西建筑学会副主席约奥·巴蒂斯托·威拉诺瓦·阿蒂格斯为首的巴西建筑家代表团全体成员，并且进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时，在座的有中国建筑学会副理事长杨春茂、梁思成，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复等。（附图片）
    陈毅副总理9月14日接见了正在我国访问的巴西建筑家代表团，图为接见前合影。前排右起第三人是团长、巴西建筑学会副主席约奥·巴蒂斯托·威拉诺瓦·阿蒂格斯。新华社记者　张彬摄
