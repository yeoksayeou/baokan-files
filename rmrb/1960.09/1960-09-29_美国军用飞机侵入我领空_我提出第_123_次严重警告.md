Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into Our Airspace; 123rd Serious Warning Issued

Author:

Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. At 12:04 PM to 12:09 PM on September 28th, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, a spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 123rd serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我领空　我提出第123次严重警告

1960-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我领空
　　我提出第123次严重警告
    新华社28日讯　9月28日十二时四分至十二时九分，美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百二十三次严重警告。
