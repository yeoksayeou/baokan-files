Title: China-Latin America Friendship Association and Chinese Medical Association Host Banquet for Brazilian Medical Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: The Vice President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, Zhou Erfu, and the President of the Chinese Medical Association, Fu Lianzhang, hosted a banquet on the 13th to welcome the Brazilian medical delegation headed by Cameses. Among those present at the banquet were Cui Yitian, Vice President of the Chinese Medical Association, and Bai Xiqing and Wu Jieping from the medical and health circles.

The Brazilian medical delegation arrived in Beijing on the 11th at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association.

### 中国拉丁美洲友协和中华医学会  设宴欢迎巴西医学代表团

1960-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

    中国拉丁美洲友协和中华医学会
    设宴欢迎巴西医学代表团
    新华社15日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复和中华医学会会长傅连暲，13日设宴欢迎以卡姆色斯为首的巴西医学代表团。出席宴会作陪的有中华医学会副会长崔义田，医药卫生界人士白希清、吴阶平等。
    巴西医学代表团是应中华医学会邀请前来中国访问，在11日到达北京的。
