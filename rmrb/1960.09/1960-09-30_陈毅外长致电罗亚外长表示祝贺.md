Title: Foreign Minister Chen Yi Sends Congratulations to Foreign Minister Roa
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. Foreign Minister Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Foreign Minister Raúl Roa of the Republic of Cuba to congratulate him on the establishment of formal diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Cuba. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Dr. Raúl Roa, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Revolutionary Government of the Republic of Cuba, Havana:

On the occasion of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Cuba, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency. May the profound friendship between the fraternal peoples of China and Cuba be further consolidated and developed with the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries in the common struggle against the aggression of U.S. imperialism and in defense of world peace.

Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi
September 29, 1960, Beijing

### 陈毅外长致电罗亚外长表示祝贺

1960-09-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　陈毅外长致电罗亚外长表示祝贺
    新华社29日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅就中华人民共和国和古巴共和国建立正式外交关系致电古巴共和国革命政府外交部长劳尔·罗亚表示祝贺。电文全文如下：哈瓦那古巴共和国外交部长劳尔·罗亚博士阁下：
    值此中古两国建立外交关系之际，我谨向阁下致以诚挚的祝贺。愿中古两国兄弟人民的深厚友谊在反对美帝国主义侵略和保卫世界和平的共同斗争中，随着两国外交关系的建立将得到进一步巩固和发展。
                中华人民共和国外交部长　陈　毅
                            1960年9月29日于北京
