Title: Vietnam-China Friendship Association Delegation Leaves Nanning for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, September 22nd – The delegation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, led by head of delegation Tran Huy Lieu, celebrated the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam together with the Chinese people and, after visiting various places, left Nanning by plane this afternoon to return home.

覃应机, Secretary of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Vice Chairman of the Autonomous Region, and President of the Guangxi Branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, along with more than 250 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Nanning, went to the airport to warmly bid farewell to the distinguished guests. Young women presented flowers to all the distinguished guests.

Also at the airport to see off the distinguished guests were Mo Naiqun, Vice Chairman of the Autonomous Region and Vice President of the Guangxi Branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, Ge Zhen, Vice President of the Guangxi Branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, and heads of various people's organizations of the Autonomous Region and Nanning City.

Yesterday afternoon, Wei Guoqing, Chairman of the Autonomous Region, hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the distinguished guests. Wei Guoqing and Tran Huy Lieu spoke at the banquet.

In the evening, the Guangxi Branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association held a farewell party for the guests.

### 越中友协代表团离南宁回国

1960-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

　  越中友协代表团离南宁回国
    据新华社南宁22日电　由陈辉燎团长率领的越中友好协会代表团同我国人民一起欢庆越南民主共和国成立十五周年和在各地访问后，今天下午乘飞机离开南宁回国。
    中共广西僮族自治区委员会书记、自治区副主席、中越友好协会广西分会会长覃应机，南宁市各族各界群众二百五十多人，到机场热烈欢送贵宾。女青年们向全体贵宾献了鲜花。
    到机场欢送贵宾的，还有自治区副主席、中越友好协会广西分会副会长莫乃群，中越友好协会广西分会副会长葛震，和自治区、南宁市各人民团体负责人。
    昨天下午，自治区主席韦国清设宴欢送贵宾。韦国清和陈辉燎团长在宴会上讲了话。
    当晚，中越友好协会广西分会为贵宾们举行了欢送晚会。
