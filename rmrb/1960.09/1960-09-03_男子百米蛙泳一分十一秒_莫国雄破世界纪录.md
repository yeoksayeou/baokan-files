Title: Men's 100m Breaststroke in 1 Minute 11 Seconds: Mo Guoxiong Breaks World Record
Author:
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, September 1st: Guangdong's 25-year-old swimming athlete Mo Guoxiong broke the world record in the men's 100-meter breaststroke this afternoon while participating in the National Swimming and Diving Championships in Chengdu. He achieved an excellent time of 1 minute 11 seconds in the preliminary round, surpassing the world record of 1 minute 11.1 seconds set by Hebei athlete Mu Xiangxiong at the First National Games last year.

This is the fifth time that Chinese swimmers have broken the world record in this event since 1957. In May 1957, Guangdong's famous athlete Qi Lieyun set the world record for the first time with a time of 1 minute 11.6 seconds. In September of the same year, Soviet athlete Minashkin surpassed Qi Lieyun's record with a time of 1 minute 11.5 seconds. In December 1958, Mu Xiangxiong shortened the world record to 1 minute 11.4 seconds. In August and September 1959, Mu Xiangxiong refreshed the world record twice with times of 1 minute 11.3 seconds and 1 minute 11.1 seconds respectively.

Mo Guoxiong's 100-meter breaststroke time was only 1 minute 20.8 seconds when he participated in the National Swimming Competition in 1955. Over the past few years, he has continued to train diligently and has made considerable progress, finally achieving this good result.

### 男子百米蛙泳一分十一秒  莫国雄破世界纪录

1960-09-03
第2版()
专栏：

    男子百米蛙泳一分十一秒
    莫国雄破世界纪录
    据新华社成都1日电　广东二十五岁的游泳运动健将莫国雄，今天下午在成都参加全国游泳、跳水锦标赛时，打破了男子一百米蛙泳的世界纪录。他在这项预赛中创造了一分十一秒的优异成绩，超过了河北选手穆祥雄在去年第一届全国运动会上创造的一分十一秒一的世界纪录。
    这是我国游泳选手自1957年以来第五次打破这个项目的世界纪录。1957年5月，广东著名选手戚烈云以一分十一秒六的成绩，第一次创造了这个项目的世界纪录。这年9月，苏联选手米纳什金以一分十一秒五的成绩超过了戚烈云的纪录。1958年12月，穆祥雄把这项世界纪录缩短到一分十一秒四。1959年8月和9月，穆祥雄又先后以一分十一秒三和一分十一秒一的成绩，两次刷新这项世界纪录。
    莫国雄1955年参加全国游泳比赛时，一百米蛙泳成绩只达到一分二十秒八。几年来他继续刻苦锻炼，又有长足的进步，终于创造了这个好成绩。
