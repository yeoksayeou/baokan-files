Title: Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam: Enthusiastic Celebrations Held Across China

Author:

Date: 1960-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: Celebrations for the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam have concluded across China.

Many major cities in China held extensive celebrations for the 15th anniversary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Ten cities, including Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Xi'an, Nanjing, Zhengzhou, Nanning, and Kunming, successively held "Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Weeks" in the first half of September.

Border counties and cities in China, adjacent to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, also held various activities. In Xichou County, Wenshan Zhuang and Miao Autonomous Prefecture, Yunnan Province, more than 1,000 people from all ethnic groups held a celebration rally. Luo Yuntong, the head of the autonomous prefecture, and Nguyen Tien Loc, head of the Party and government delegation from Ha Giang Province, Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and a member of the Ha Giang Provincial Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, who were invited to attend the conference, both spoke at the meeting. More than 1,000 people in Hekou County, Honghe Hani and Yi Autonomous Prefecture, also held a celebration rally. A Party and government delegation headed by Truong Minh, Secretary of the Lao Cai Provincial Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Chairman of the Administrative Committee of Lao Cai Province, Democratic Republic of Vietnam, was invited to attend the conference. More than 1,000 people from all ethnic groups in Dongxing Various Nationalities Autonomous County, Guangdong, held a celebration rally. Du Min, a representative from Hai Ninh Province, Democratic Republic of Vietnam, who was invited to attend the conference, spoke at the meeting. Ningming, Longjin, Daxin, Jingxi, and Mubian counties, and Pingxiang city in the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, also organized a border county delegation to participate in local celebrations in Lang Son and Cao Bang at the invitation of the two border provinces of Lang Son and Cao Bang in Vietnam.

The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Film Week held in many cities was enthusiastically welcomed by audiences.

### 欢庆越南民主共和国成立十五周年　我国各地热烈举行庆祝活动

1960-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　欢庆越南民主共和国成立十五周年
　　我国各地热烈举行庆祝活动
    据新华社22日讯　我国各地庆祝越南民主共和国成立十五周年的活动已经结束。
    我国的许多大城市为越南民主共和国成立十五周年举行了广泛的庆祝活动。北京、上海、天津、武汉、广州、西安、南京、郑州、南宁、昆明十个城市，9月上半月先后举行了“中越友好周”。
    和越南民主共和国毗邻的我国边境县、市，也纷纷举行各种活动。云南省文山僮族苗族自治州西畴县各族各界人民有一千多人举行了庆祝集会。自治州州长罗运通和应邀参加大会的越南民主共和国河江省党政代表团团长、越南劳动党河江省委员会委员阮进禄都在会上讲了话。红河哈尼族彝族自治州河口县的一千多人也举行了庆祝集会，越南劳动党老街省委书记、越南民主共和国老街省行政委员会主席长明为首的党政代表团，应邀参加了大会。广东东兴各族自治县有一千多人举行了庆祝集会。应邀前来参加大会的越南民主共和国海宁省代表杜敏在会上讲了话。广西僮族自治区宁明、龙津、大新、靖西、睦边等县和凭祥市，还应越南谅山、高平两个边境省分的邀请，组织了一个边境县代表团，到谅山和高平参加了当地的庆祝活动。
    许多城市举行的越南民主共和国电影周受到观众的热烈欢迎。
