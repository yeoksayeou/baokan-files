Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Brigadier General Aung Gyi
Author:
Date: 1960-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th — Vice Premier Chen Yi received Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of the Burmese side of the China-Burma Joint Boundary Committee and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Armed Forces, this afternoon, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

Also present at the reception were the Burmese representatives of the China-Burma Joint Boundary Committee: Burmese Ambassador to China, U Kyaw Winn; Secretary of the Kachin State Affairs Department, U San Lwin; Director of the Border Areas Administration Bureau, Colonel So Min; Commissioner of the Northern Shan State, U Kun Aung Kya; and advisor, Commander of the Northern Burma Military Region, Brigadier General San Yu.

Also present at the reception were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chief Representative of the Chinese side of the China-Burma Joint Boundary Committee Yao Zhongming, and Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Shen Ping.

### 陈毅副总理接见昂季准将

1960-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见昂季准将
    新华社19日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表、缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季准将，并且进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    同时被接见的，还有中缅边界联合委员会缅甸代表：缅甸驻中国大使叫温，克钦邦事务部秘书吴山伦，边境地区管理局局长苏敏上校，北掸邦专员吴昆翁佳和顾问、缅北军区司令山友准将等。
    接见时在座的，有外交部副部长章汉夫、中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明，外交部第一亚洲司副司长沈平等。（附图片）
