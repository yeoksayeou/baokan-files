Title: Marshal Lin Biao Receives Iraqi Military Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, received the entire Iraqi Military Friendship Delegation headed by Brigadier General Mahmoud Abdul Razzaq this afternoon and had a friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were General Luo Ruiqing, General Xiao Hua, General Zhang Aiping, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice Admiral Zhao Qimin of the Navy, Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun of the Air Force, He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Colonel Xu Yimin, Military Attaché of our country's Embassy in the Republic of Iraq.

Also present at the reception was Fadil, Ambassador of the Republic of Iraq to our country.

### 林彪元帅接见伊拉克军事友好代表团

1960-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　林彪元帅接见伊拉克军事友好代表团
    新华社26日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅，今天下午接见了以马哈茂德·阿卜杜勒·拉扎克准将为首的伊拉克军事友好代表团全体成员，并进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有罗瑞卿大将、萧华上将、张爱萍上将、萧向荣中将、赵启民海军中将、常乾坤空军中将，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英和我国驻伊拉克共和国大使馆武官徐毅民上校。
    接见时，伊拉克共和国驻我国大使法迪尔也在座。（附图片）
