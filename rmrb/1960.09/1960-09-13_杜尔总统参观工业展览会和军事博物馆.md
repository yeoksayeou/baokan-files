Title: President Touré Visits Industrial Exhibition and Military Museum
Author:
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - President Sékou Touré and the entire delegation of the Government of the Republic of Guinea today visited the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition and the Chinese People's Revolution Military Museum.

	Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi and Vice Premier and Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Luo Ruiqing, accompanied President Sékou Touré and Speaker Diallo Saifoulaye and other distinguished guests to view the exhibits at the Industrial Exhibition and the Military Museum, respectively. The distinguished guests enthusiastically praised that there were many things here that were very useful to them.

	Also accompanying the distinguished guests were Song Yangchu, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, Ji Chaoding, Head of the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition, General Xiao Hua, Lieutenant General Wu Faxian of the Air Force, Vice Admiral Zhou Xihan of the Navy, Lieutenant General Kuang Yumin, and Major General Jia Ruoyu, Curator of the Chinese People's Revolution Military Museum.

### 杜尔总统参观工业展览会和军事博物馆

1960-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    杜尔总统参观工业展览会和军事博物馆
    新华社12日讯　塞古·杜尔总统和几内亚共和国政府代表团全体贵宾，今天参观了全国工业交通展览会和中国人民革命军事博物馆。
    国务院副总理陈毅、副总理兼中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将分别陪同塞古·杜尔总统和迪阿洛·塞福拉耶议长等贵宾观看了工业展览会和军事博物馆展出的展品。贵宾们热情地赞扬说，这里有不少东西对他们很有用。
    陪同贵宾参观的还有：国家计划委员会副主任宋养初、全国工业交通展览会负责人冀朝鼎，萧华上将、吴法宪空军中将、周希汉海军中将、匡裕民中将和中国人民革命军事博物馆馆长贾若瑜少将等。
