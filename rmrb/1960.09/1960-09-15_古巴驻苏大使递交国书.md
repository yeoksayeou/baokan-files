Title: Cuban Ambassador to the Soviet Union Presents Credentials
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 14th: TASS, Moscow, September 14th—The Cuban Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Faure Chomón Mediavilla, presented his credentials today to Arushanyan, Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. Ambassador Faure Chomón Mediavilla and Arushanyan exchanged speeches. After the presentation of credentials, they had a conversation.

### 古巴驻苏大使递交国书

1960-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴驻苏大使递交国书
    新华社14日讯　塔斯社莫斯科14日讯：古巴驻苏联大使福雷·乔蒙·梅迪亚维利亚今天向苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席阿鲁沙尼扬递交国书。福雷·乔蒙·梅迪亚维利亚大使和阿鲁沙尼扬互相致了词。在递交国书以后，他们进行了谈话。
