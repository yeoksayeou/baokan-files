Title: US Uses Honduran Islands Against Cuba; Honduran Government Protests to US
Author:
Date: 1960-09-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. According to Latin American Press Agency reports, the Honduran government recently sent a note to the US government protesting the US turning the Swan Islands (Islas Santanilla), belonging to Honduras, into a radio propaganda center against the Cuban revolution.

On the Swan Islands, occupied by the United States, a huge American airport has been built. The "Swan Radio," controlled by the United Fruit Company of America, broadcasts from the Swan Islands.

Reportedly, ten Honduran mass organizations published a statement in the Argentine "Action" weekly, strongly protesting the US annexation of the Swan Islands. The statement condemned the United States for turning the islands into a "center for US military operations controlling the Caribbean region."

### 美国利用洪都拉斯的岛屿反对古巴　洪都拉斯政府向美国提出抗议

1960-09-21
第6版()
专栏：

    美国利用洪都拉斯的岛屿反对古巴
　　洪都拉斯政府向美国提出抗议
    新华社20日讯　据拉丁美洲通讯社报道，洪都拉斯政府最近向美国政府提出一份照会，抗议美国把属于洪都拉斯的西斯内群岛（天鹅群岛）变成反对古巴革命的广播宣传中心。
    在美国占领下的西斯内群岛上，建筑了巨大的美国机场。美国联合果品公司所控制的“天鹅电台”从西斯内群岛进行广播。
    据报道，洪都拉斯十个群众团体曾在阿根廷“行动”周刊上发表声明，对美国并吞西斯内群岛提出了强烈的抗议。声明谴责说，美国把这一群岛变成了“美国控制加勒比地区的军事行动中心”。
