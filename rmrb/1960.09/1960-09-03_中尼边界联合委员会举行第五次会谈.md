Title: Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee Holds Fifth Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 2nd - The fifth meeting of the first session of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee was held in the Lion Palace in Kathmandu from 10:00 to 12:00 this morning.

The meeting was conducted in a sincere and friendly atmosphere. The next meeting will be held on September 8th.

### 中尼边界联合委员会举行第五次会谈

1960-09-03
第2版()
专栏：

    中尼边界联合委员会举行第五次会谈
    新华社2日讯　加德满都消息：中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议第五次会谈今天上午十时到十二时在加德满都的狮宫举行。
    会谈是在诚挚和友好的气氛中进行的。下次会谈将在9月8日举行。
