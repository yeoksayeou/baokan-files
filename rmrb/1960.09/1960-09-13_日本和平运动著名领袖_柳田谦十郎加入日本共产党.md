Title: Prominent Leader of Japanese Peace Movement, Kenjuro Yanagida, Joins Japanese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 12th: According to the "Akahata" newspaper, Kenjuro Yanagida, a prominent leader of the Japanese peace movement and philosopher, has joined the Japanese Communist Party.

    Kenjuro Yanagida wrote in "Akahata" that for many years, he had stood from an idealist position, but under the influence of the enormous changes in Japanese social life and on the international stage, he gradually established a Marxist worldview.

    In his statement of joining the Party, Kenjuro Yanagida emphasized that the Communist Party is the vanguard at the forefront of the struggle for social progress.

    The Japanese Communist Party has significantly increased its prestige in the struggle against the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty" and for independence, peace, democracy, and neutrality, and more and more people are joining the Communist Party. In some prefectures, the number of JCP members has more than doubled.

### 日本和平运动著名领袖  柳田谦十郎加入日本共产党

1960-09-13
第5版()
专栏：

    日本和平运动著名领袖
    柳田谦十郎加入日本共产党
    新华社12日讯　东京消息：据“赤旗报”报道，日本著名的和平运动领袖、哲学家柳田谦十郎加入了日本共产党。
    柳田谦十郎在“赤旗报”上写道，多年来，他一直站在唯心主义的立场上，但是，在日本社会生活和国际舞台上的巨大变化的影响下，他逐步建立了马克思主义世界观。
    柳田谦十郎在他的入党声明书中着重指出，共产党是走在争取社会进步斗争的最前列的先锋队。
    日本共产党在反对日美“安全条约”，争取独立、和平、民主、中立的斗争中威信显著提高，参加共产党的越来越多。在有一些县中，日共的党员人数增加了一倍以上。
