Title: Vice Chairman Putrament of the Polish Writers Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: Jerzy Putrament, Vice Chairman of the Polish Writers Association, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese Writers Association.

Welcoming them at the airport were Liu Baiyu, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, Yang Shuo, Director of the Foreign Literature Committee of the Chinese Writers Association, Ruan Zhangjing, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the "Poetry Magazine" editorial department, He Chengxiang, Vice President of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, and others. Mr. Dumbnitzky, Counselor of the Polish Embassy in China, also greeted them at the airport.

### 波作家协会副主席普特拉曼特到京

1960-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

　  波作家协会副主席普特拉曼特到京
    新华社23日讯　应中国作家协会邀请前来我国访问的波兰作家协会副主席耶日·普特拉曼特及夫人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国作家协会副主席刘白羽、中国作家协会外国文学委员会主任杨朔、“诗刊”编辑部副主编阮章竞、中波友好协会副会长何成湘等。波兰驻华大使馆参赞邓布尼茨基也到机场迎接。
