    On September 1, people from all walks of life in the capital held a rally to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The picture shows the presidium of the rally. From the left: Liu Changsheng, Huang Yanpei, Guo Moruo, Gao Jindian, Peng Zhen, Vu Thu, Dong Biwu, Tran Tu Binh, Truong Tuong, Chen Huy Lieu, Zhou Enlai, Mrs. Tran Tu Binh, Bo Yibo, Huai Qing, Shen Junru, Xi Zhongxun, Yang Xiufeng, Ji Pengfei.
