Title: Joint Press Communiqué Issued by the Governments of China and Burma: Prime Minister U Nu to Visit China Soon to Sign the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty and Participate in China's National Day Celebrations; Premier Zhou to Visit Burma Early Next Year to Exchange Instruments of Ratification of the Boundary Treaty and Participate in Burma's Independence Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th: Joint Press Communiqué of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Union of Burma
September 24, 1960

	At the invitation of the Government of the People’s Republic of China and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council, the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, the Honorable U Nu, will arrive in Beijing at the end of September 1960 for a friendly visit. During his visit, Prime Minister U Nu will sign the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty with Premier Zhou Enlai and will participate in the 11th anniversary celebrations of the founding of the People’s Republic of China. Also invited to visit China with Prime Minister U Nu is General Ne Win.
	In order to celebrate the peaceful and friendly resolution of the Sino-Burmese boundary issue, and to express the kinship-like feelings of the Burmese people to the Chinese people, the Burmese government will, on the same day the boundary treaty is signed, donate 2,000 tons of rice and 1,000 tons of salt to the approximately one million Chinese residents living near the Sino-Burmese border through officials designated by the Chinese government. The Chinese government expresses its deepest gratitude for this.
	At the invitation of the Government of the Union of Burma and Prime Minister U Nu, Premier Zhou Enlai will arrive in Rangoon at the beginning of January 1961 for a friendly visit. During his visit, Premier Zhou Enlai will exchange instruments of ratification of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty with Prime Minister U Nu and will participate in the 13th anniversary celebrations of the independence of the Union of Burma.
	In order to celebrate the entry into force of this historic boundary treaty, and to express the kinship-like feelings of the Chinese people to the Burmese people, the Chinese government will, on the same day that the instruments of ratification of the boundary treaty are exchanged, donate 2.4 million meters of printed cloth and 600,000 porcelain plates to the approximately 1.2 million Burmese residents living near the Sino-Burmese border through officials designated by the Burmese government. The Burmese government expresses its deepest gratitude for this.
	The signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty, the mutual friendly visits of the two countries' premiers during each other's National Day celebrations, and the mutual gifting of goods to border residents by the two governments mark a further consolidation and development of the friendly relations between the two countries. The governments of both China and Burma are very satisfied with this.

### 中缅两国政府发表联合新闻公报　吴努总理即将来我国访问，签订中缅边界条约，参加我国庆庆典　周总理明年初访问缅甸，交换边界条约批准书，参加缅国庆庆典

1960-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　中缅两国政府发表联合新闻公报
　　吴努总理即将来我国访问，签订中缅边界条约，参加我国庆庆典
　　周总理明年初访问缅甸，交换边界条约批准书，参加缅国庆庆典
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国政府和缅甸联邦政府联合新闻公报
                     一九六○年九月二十四日
    应中华人民共和国政府和国务院周恩来总理的邀请，缅甸联邦总理吴努阁下将于1960年9月末抵达北京进行友好访问。吴努总理在访问期间将同周恩来总理签订中缅边界条约，并将参加中华人民共和国成立十一周年庆典。同时应邀与吴努总理到中国进行友好访问的，还有奈温将军阁下。
    为了庆祝中缅边界问题得到和平友好的解决，为了向中国人民表示缅甸人民的亲戚般的感情，缅甸政府将在签订边界条约的同一天，通过中国政府所指定的官员向居住在中缅边界附近大约一百万中国居民赠送两千吨大米和一千吨食盐。中国政府对此表示深切的谢意。
    应缅甸联邦政府和吴努总理的邀请，周恩来总理将于1961年1月初抵达仰光进行友好访问。周恩来总理在访问期间将同吴努总理交换中缅边界条约的批准书，并参加缅甸联邦独立十三周年庆典。
    为了庆祝这个历史性的边界条约的生效，为了向缅甸人民表达中国人民的亲戚般的感情，中国政府将在互换边界条约的批准书的同一天，通过缅甸政府所指定的官员，向居住在中缅边界附近大约一百二十万缅甸居民赠送二百四十万米的花布和六十万个瓷盘。缅甸政府对此表示深切的谢意。
    中缅边界条约的签订、两国总理相互在对方国庆期间进行友好访问和两国政府相互对对方边民赠礼，标志着两国友好关系的进一步巩固和发展。对此，中缅两国政府都感到十分满意。
