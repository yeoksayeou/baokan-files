Title: Albanian "People's Voice" Condemns Yugoslav Bandits' Provocations; Modern Revisionists are Agents of Imperialism; The Struggle for Peace is Inseparable from the Struggle Against Revisionism
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 8th September: The Albanian "People's Voice" published a commentary on the 8th concerning the recent intrusion of Yugoslav bandits into Albanian territory, exposing the destructive and provocative policies that the Belgrade revisionist group has consistently adopted towards Albania.
    The commentary stated that many events and facts prove that the Belgrade revisionist group's continuous policies of conspiracy, sabotage, and provocation against the People's Republic of Albania are policies against Albania's freedom, independence, and sovereignty, and against the Albanian people's iron-like unity around the heroic Labor Party. The main aim of this sinister policy has always been to realize the old dreams of the Serbian emperors – to annex Albania. This policy clearly shows the world the true face of the Yugoslav revisionists – enemies of socialism serving American imperialism. The ruling clique in Washington has ordered their special envoys in the Balkans, first and foremost the most aggressive forces – the Belgrade revisionists – to use blackmail, provocation, conspiracy, and terror to undermine the peaceful construction work of the Albanian people, weaken and divide the powerful socialist camp and the unity of the international communist and workers' movement, and maintain international tension. The Belgrade leadership group's consistent hostile attitude towards our country once again convincingly demonstrates that the Yugoslav revisionists are completely and faithfully acting as cunning agents of American imperialism. The commentary said that the Yugoslav revisionists are enraged by the unremitting struggle waged by our Labor Party to defend the purity of Marxism-Leninism, because this struggle exposes them as traitors to socialism.
    The commentary pointed out that the so-called "active coexistence" so loudly touted by Tito's followers means, for the Titoites, actively interfering in the internal affairs of other countries, rudely violating the national sovereignty of other countries, and engaging in conspiracies, sabotage, and assassination activities. All these show that modern revisionism, especially Yugoslav revisionism, remains the main danger to the international communist and workers' movement. Therefore, we must unswervingly fight to the end to expose revisionism. The struggle against imperialism and for peace is inseparable from the struggle against revisionism.
    The commentary concluded by saying that we will maintain constant vigilance against the conspiracies and tricks of the Yugoslav revisionists and other tools of American imperialism.

### 阿“人民之声报”斥责南斯拉夫匪徒的挑衅  南修正主义者是帝国主义代理人  争取和平的斗争同反修正主义斗争不可分割

1960-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    阿“人民之声报”斥责南斯拉夫匪徒的挑衅
    南修正主义者是帝国主义代理人
    争取和平的斗争同反修正主义斗争不可分割
    新华社地拉那8日电　阿尔巴尼亚“人民之声报”8日就南斯拉夫匪徒最近侵入阿尔巴尼亚国境一事发表评论，揭露贝尔格莱德修正主义集团对阿尔巴尼亚所一贯采取的破坏和挑衅政策。
    评论说，很多事件和事实证明，贝尔格莱德修正主义集团对阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国不断进行阴谋、破坏和挑衅的政策，是反对阿尔巴尼亚的自由、独立和主权的政策，是反对阿尔巴尼亚人民在英雄的劳动党的周围的钢铁般的团结的政策。这种罪恶政策的主要目的始终是要实现塞尔维亚皇帝的旧的梦想——吞并阿尔巴尼亚。这种政策十分清楚地向全世界说明了南斯拉夫修正主义者——为美帝国主义服务的社会主义的敌人——的真正嘴脸。华盛顿统治集团授命他们在巴尔干的特派员、首先是最富侵略性的部队——贝尔格莱德修正主义者以讹诈、挑衅、阴谋和恐怖等手段来破坏阿尔巴尼亚人民的和平建设劳动，削弱和分裂强大的社会主义阵营和国际共产主义和工人运动的团结，保持国际紧张局势。贝尔格莱德领导集团对我们祖国的始终一贯的敌对态度再一次十分令人信服地说明了，南斯拉夫修正主义者是完完全全的忠实于美帝国主义的老奸巨猾的代理人。评论说南斯拉夫修正主义者由于我国劳动党为保卫马克思列宁主义的纯洁性进行了不懈斗争而忿怒若狂，因为这一斗争揭露了他们是社会主义的叛徒。
    评论指出，贝尔格莱德所大吹大擂的所谓“积极共处”对铁托分子来说，就是积极干涉其他国家内政，就是粗暴地破坏其他国家的国家主权，进行阴谋、破坏和暗害活动。所有这些都表明，现代修正主义，特别是南斯拉夫修正主义仍然是国际共产主义和工人运动的主要危险。因此我们必须始终不渝地斗争到底，揭露修正主义。反对帝国主义、争取和平的斗争同反对修正主义斗争是不可分割的。
    评论最后说，我们将对南斯拉夫修正主义者和美帝国主义的其他工具的阴谋诡计保持经常的警惕。
