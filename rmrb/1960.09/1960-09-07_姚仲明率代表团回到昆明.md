Title: Yao Zhongming Leads Delegation Back to Kunming
Author:
Date: 1960-09-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 6th September. The Chinese delegation that attended the third meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee in Rangoon, led by Chief Representative Yao Zhongming, returned to Kunming by plane this afternoon from Rangoon after the meeting concluded.

Among those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation back were Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee, Li Ping, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Provincial People's Committee, and Major General Ding Rongchang, head of the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Kunming.

U Maung Maung So, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, also went to the airport to welcome them.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 6th September. The Chinese delegation to the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, headed by Yao Zhongming, left here by plane this morning to return home after attending the third meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee in Rangoon from August 25th to September 4th.

Those who went to the airport to see off the Chinese delegation included Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the Burmese Chief Representative of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Defence Forces; Colonel So Min, Director of the Border Areas Administration Bureau; Colonel La Aung, Director of the Survey Bureau; Colonel Ba Than, Director of the Psychological Warfare and Education Bureau of the Defence Forces; Lieutenant Colonel Lun, Director of the Military Intelligence Office of the Defence Forces; U San Lun, Secretary of the Kachin State Affairs Department; U Aung Khin Htee, Commissioner of the Northern Shan State; U Khin Nyo, Joint Secretary of the Border Areas Administration Bureau; and former Attorney General U Ba Sein. The Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyao Winn, also went to the airport to see them off.

Li Yimang, Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Lei Yang, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy, Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché, and other counselors also went to the airport to see them off.

### 姚仲明率代表团回到昆明

1960-09-07
第4版()
专栏：

    姚仲明率代表团回到昆明
    新华社昆明6日电　出席在仰光举行的中缅边界联合委员会第三次会议的中国代表团，在会议结束之后，今天下午由首席代表姚仲明率领，从仰光乘飞机回到昆明。
    前往机场欢迎代表团归来的，有云南省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，省人民委员会外事处处长李萍，人民解放军驻昆明部队首长丁荣昌少将等。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭也到机场欢迎。
    新华社仰光6日电　以姚仲明为首的出席中缅边界联合委员会的中国代表团，从8月25日到9月4日在仰光出席了中缅边界联合委员会第三次会议后，今天上午乘飞机离开这里回国。
    到机场欢送中国代表的有中缅边界联合委员会的缅甸首席代表、国防军副总参谋长昂季准将，边境地区管理局局长苏敏上校，勘察局局长拉昂上校，陆军心理战和教育局局长巴丹上校，国防军军事情报处处长伦中校，克钦邦事务部秘书吴山伦，北掸邦专员吴翁昆佳，边境地区管理局联合秘书吴钦纽和前检察总长吴巴盛。缅甸驻中国大使叫温也到机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的还有中国驻缅甸大使李一氓，中国大使馆参赞雷扬，武官蒋克定上校和其他参赞。
