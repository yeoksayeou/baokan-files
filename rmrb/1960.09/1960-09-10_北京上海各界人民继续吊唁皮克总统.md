Title: People from All Walks of Life in Beijing and Shanghai Continue to Mourn President Pieck
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. Today, more than 20,000 people from all walks of life in the capital went to the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China to mourn the death of President Wilhelm Pieck. Among those mourning were workers, farmers, officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army, students, and citizens.
　　In the past two days, a total of more than 30,000 people have gone to the embassy to pay their respects. The mourners presented many wreaths to the portrait of President Wilhelm Pieck.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 9th. People from all walks of life in Shanghai continued to visit the Consulate General of the German Democratic Republic in Shanghai today to mourn the death of President Wilhelm Pieck. Including yesterday, the number of people who went to the Consulate General to mourn is nearly 20,000.
　　People came to the hall of the Consulate General and silently mourned in front of the portrait of President Wilhelm Pieck. The portrait of President Pieck was surrounded by wreaths, including those presented by the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the three armed services of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, the Shanghai local organizations of various democratic parties, various people's organizations, and organs, factories, and schools.
　　The entire delegation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, which is currently visiting Shanghai, also went to the Consulate General this morning, led by delegation leader Chen Hui Liao, to mourn the death of President Pieck.
　　Consuls General and Consuls of various countries stationed in Shanghai also went to mourn one after another.

### 北京上海各界人民继续吊唁皮克总统

1960-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    北京上海各界人民继续吊唁皮克总统
    新华社9日讯　今天又有两万多首都各界人民到德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆，吊唁威廉·皮克总统的逝世。吊唁的人们中有工人、农民、解放军官兵、学生和市民等。
    两天以来，到大使馆吊唁的人共有三万多人。吊唁的人们向威廉·皮克总统遗像献了许多花圈。
    新华社上海9日电　上海市各界人民今天继续前往德意志民主共和国驻上海总领事馆吊唁威廉·皮克总统的逝世。连同昨天一起，到总领事馆吊唁的人数将近两万人。
    人们来到总领事馆的大厅里，在威廉·皮克总统的遗像前默默致哀。皮克总统的遗像两边堆满着花圈，其中有中共上海市委员会、上海市人民委员会、政协上海市委员会、中国人民解放军驻上海陆海空三军部队、各民主党派上海地方组织、各人民团体以及机关、工厂、学校等敬献的花圈。
    正在上海访问的越中友好协会代表团全体成员，在陈辉燎团长率领下，今天上午也到总领事馆吊唁皮克总统的逝世。
    各国驻上海总领事、领事也先后前往吊唁。
