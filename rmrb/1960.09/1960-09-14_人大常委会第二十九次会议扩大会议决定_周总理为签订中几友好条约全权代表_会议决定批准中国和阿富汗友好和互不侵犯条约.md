Title: The 29th Session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress (Enlarged Meeting) Decides: Premier Zhou Enlai to be Plenipotentiary Representative for Signing Sino-Guinean Friendship Treaty; Meeting Decides to Ratify Sino-Afghan Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression
Author:
Date: 1960-09-14
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 13th — The Enlarged Meeting of the 29th Session of the Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress was held this morning. The meeting decided to appoint Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai as the plenipotentiary representative for signing the Friendship Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Guinea.
    The meeting decided to ratify the "Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Afghanistan."
    Based on the proposal of the plenary session of the State Council, the meeting decided to establish the Third Ministry of Machine Building Industry of the People's Republic of China.
    The meeting approved a list of appointments and removals.

### 人大常委会第二十九次会议扩大会议决定  周总理为签订中几友好条约全权代表  会议决定批准中国和阿富汗友好和互不侵犯条约

1960-09-14
第2版()
专栏：

    人大常委会第二十九次会议扩大会议决定
    周总理为签订中几友好条约全权代表
    会议决定批准中国和阿富汗友好和互不侵犯条约
    新华社13日讯　第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十九次会议扩大会议今天上午举行。会议决定派国务院总理周恩来为签订中华人民共和国和几内亚共和国友好条约的全权代表。
    会议决定批准“中华人民共和国和阿富汗王国友好和互不侵犯条约”。
    根据国务院全体会议的建议，会议决定设立中华人民共和国第三机械工业部。
    会议通过了任免名单。
