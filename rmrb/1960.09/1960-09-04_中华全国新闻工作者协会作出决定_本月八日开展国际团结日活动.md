Title: All-China Journalists Association Decides to Launch International Solidarity Day Activities on the 8th of This Month
Author:
Date: 1960-09-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association recently discussed the issue of holding International Solidarity Day of Journalists (September 8th).

The All-China Journalists Association believes that holding International Solidarity Day is of great significance for strengthening the solidarity of honest journalists from all countries and supporting the just struggle of journalists against imperialism and for democracy and national independence. The Association has decided to launch extensive International Solidarity Day activities on September 8th this year. The Association calls for support for progressive journalists from all countries who are persecuted by imperialists and reactionaries.

International Solidarity Day of Journalists was established on September 8th based on the proposal of the International Organization of Journalists in 1958. This day is the anniversary of the death of Julius Fučík, an outstanding journalist from Czechoslovakia.

### 中华全国新闻工作者协会作出决定  本月八日开展国际团结日活动

1960-09-04
第3版()
专栏：

    中华全国新闻工作者协会作出决定
    本月八日开展国际团结日活动
    新华社3日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处最近讨论了关于举行新闻工作者国际团结日（9月8日）的问题。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会认为，举行国际团结日对加强各国正直的新闻工作者的团结，支持反对帝国主义、争取民主和民族独立的新闻工作者的正义斗争，有很重大的意义。协会决定，今年9月8日广泛开展国际团结日的活动。协会号召支援遭受帝国主义和反动派迫害的各国进步记者。
    新闻工作者国际团结日是根据国际新闻工作者协会1958年的倡议订于9月8日举行的，这一天是捷克斯洛伐克的优秀新闻工作者尤利乌斯·伏契克遇难的日子。
