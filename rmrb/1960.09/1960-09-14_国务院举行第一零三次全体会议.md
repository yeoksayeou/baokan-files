Title: State Council Holds 103rd Plenary Session
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 13th - The State Council held its 103rd plenary session today. The meeting approved the nomination of Premier Zhou Enlai as plenipotentiary to sign the Treaty of Friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Guinea, to be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for decision.

    The meeting approved the "Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Afghanistan," which will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and approval.

    The meeting also approved the "Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Cuba on Cultural Cooperation."

    The meeting approved a proposal to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress to establish the Third Ministry of Machine Building Industry of the People's Republic of China, in order to further develop China's machine building industry.

    The meeting also approved matters concerning changes in the administrative divisions of counties and cities, and appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第一零三次全体会议

1960-09-14
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第一零三次全体会议
    新华社13日讯　国务院今天举行第一零三次全体会议。会议通过提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会决定派国务院总理周恩来为签订中华人民共和国和几内亚共和国友好条约的全权代表。
    会议通过了“中华人民共和国和阿富汗王国友好和互不侵犯条约”，这个条约将提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议还核准了“中华人民共和国政府和古巴共和国政府关于文化合作的协定”。
    会议通过为了进一步发展我国机械工业，提请人大常委会决定设立中华人民共和国第三机械工业部。
    会议还通过了关于县、市行政区划变更事项和任免事项。
