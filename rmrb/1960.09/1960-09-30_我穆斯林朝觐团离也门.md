Title: Our Muslim Hajj Delegation Leaves Yemen
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Taiz, 26th — The Chinese Muslim Hajj delegation concluded its three-week visit to Yemen and left Yemen on the 24th.
    Yemeni Crown Prince Al-Badr received the Hajj delegation on the 15th. He expressed a warm welcome to the delegation and said that the visit of the Hajj delegation had enhanced the friendship between China and Yemen. Crown Prince Al-Badr also expressed his gratitude to the Chinese experts working in Yemen and the Chinese government for their assistance to Yemen.
    On their way from Taiz to Sana'a, the Hajj delegation visited several cities and received a warm welcome from local governments and prominent figures in religious circles. They visited Islamic seminaries and famous historical sites in Sana'a and also performed prayers at the Sana'a Mosque.
    The Hajj delegation traveled from Sana'a to Hodeidah via the highway being built by Chinese experts and Yemeni workers. They visited Hodeidah for four days.

### 我穆斯林朝觐团离也门

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

    我穆斯林朝觐团离也门
    新华社塔兹26日电　中国穆斯林朝觐团结束了在也门的三星期访问，24日离开也门。
    也门王太子巴德尔曾经在15日接见了朝觐团。他表示热烈欢迎朝觐团，并且说，朝觐团的访问增进了中也两国的友谊。巴德尔王太子还表示感谢在也门工作的中国专家和中国政府对也门的帮助。
    朝觐团在从塔兹前往萨纳的途中，访问了一些城市，受到当地政府和宗教界著名人士的热烈欢迎。他们在萨纳参观了伊斯兰经学堂和各名胜古迹，并且曾经在萨纳清真寺做礼拜。
    朝觐团从萨纳经过中国专家和也门工人正在建造中的公路前往荷台达。他们在荷台达访问了四天。
