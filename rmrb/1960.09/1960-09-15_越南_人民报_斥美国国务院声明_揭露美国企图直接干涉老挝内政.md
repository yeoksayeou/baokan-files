Title: Vietnamese "People's Daily" Denounces U.S. State Department Statement, Exposing U.S. Attempt to Directly Interfere in Laotian Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1960-09-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 13th: The Vietnamese "People's Daily" published a commentary today, strongly condemning the U.S. State Department's statement on September 10th, which reveals the U.S. attempt to directly interfere in Laotian internal affairs.

The commentary stated that the U.S. instigated Phoumi Nosavan and Boun Oum to establish the so-called "Revolutionary Committee" in Savannakhet, in order to overthrow the legitimate Souvanna Phouma government, abolish the current constitution of the Kingdom of Laos, antagonize the powers of the Laotian King, and split the Laotian territory. Following this, the U.S. State Department issued a statement on the evening of September 10th, exposing the blatant interference and malicious conspiracy of U.S. imperialism in Laos. The commentary, when discussing the U.S. State Department's statement regarding "any other foreign country or its agents using the existing turmoil to directly or indirectly interfere [in Laos]," pointed out that the U.S. State Department is once again playing the trick of "stop thief, cry thief" to deceive world public opinion and cover up the criminal acts of U.S. imperialism in Laos. The commentary said, "Clearly, U.S. imperialism is conspiring to directly interfere in Laos."

### 越南“人民报”斥美国国务院声明  揭露美国企图直接干涉老挝内政

1960-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    越南“人民报”斥美国国务院声明
    揭露美国企图直接干涉老挝内政
    据新华社河内13日电　越南“人民报”今天发表评论，严厉谴责美国国务院9月10日发表的美国企图直接干涉老挝内政的声明。
    评论说，美国唆使富米·诺萨万和文翁在沙湾拿吉成立所谓“革命委员会”，以推翻合法的富马政府，取消老挝王国的现行宪法，敌视老挝国王的权力和分裂老挝国土。在这以后，美国国务院又在9月10日晚上发表声明，这暴露了美帝国主义对老挝的明目张胆的干涉和恶毒的阴谋。评论在谈到美国国务院声明中所谓“任何其他外国或其代理人利用（老挝）存在的骚乱情况直接或间接进行干涉”的论调时指出，美国国务院又在玩弄贼喊捉贼的手法，以欺骗世界舆论和掩盖美帝国主义在老挝的罪恶行为。评论说，“显然，美帝国主义正在阴谋直接干涉老挝。”
