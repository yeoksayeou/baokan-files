Title: Our Youth Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 5th. China's youth men's and women's basketball teams concluded their visit to Romania and returned home by plane today. Since arriving in Romania on August 20th, the Chinese youth men's and women's basketball teams have played fourteen matches with Romanian basketball teams in seven cities including Bucharest, winning ten and losing four (each of the men's and women's teams lost two).

### 我青年男女篮球队返国

1960-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    我青年男女篮球队返国
    新华社布加勒斯特5日电　中国青年男女篮球队结束了在罗马尼亚的访问，今天乘飞机回国。中国青年男女篮球队自8月20日到达罗马尼亚后，先后在布加勒斯特等七个城市同罗马尼亚的篮球队进行了十四场比赛，共胜十场、败四场（男女队各败二场）。
