Title: Japanese Dockworkers Delegation Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. A seven-member Japanese dockworkers delegation, led by Igarashi Takeo, Secretary-General of the All-Japan Dockworkers' Union, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 8th. Tang Zhang, Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union, was at the airport to welcome them.

The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union for a friendly visit.

### 日本港湾工人访华代表团到京

1960-09-10
第6版()
专栏：

    日本港湾工人访华代表团到京
    新华社9日讯　日本港湾工人访华代表团一行七人，在全日本港湾工会书记长五十岚武雄的率领下，8日乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国海员工会全国委员会秘书长唐章。
    代表团是应中国海员工会全国委员会的邀请来我国作友好访问的。
