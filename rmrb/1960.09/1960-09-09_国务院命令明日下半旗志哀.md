Title: State Council Order: National Flags to be Flown at Half-Mast for Mourning Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1960-09-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th September: The State Council of the People's Republic of China issued an order on September 8th that on September 10th – the day of the funeral of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic – the institutions, troops, factories, mines, enterprises, schools, and people's organizations in the locations of the People's Committees of all provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government, and the People's Committees of all cities under the jurisdiction of the provinces, as well as Chinese ships docked in ports, shall all fly the national flag at half-mast for one day of mourning.

### 国务院命令明日下半旗志哀

1960-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院命令明日下半旗志哀
    新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国国务院9月8日发出命令，在9月10日——德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克同志殡葬的日子，全国各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会和各省辖市人民委员会所在地的机关、部队、厂矿、企业、学校、人民团体和停在港口内的中国轮船，一律下半旗一日志哀。
