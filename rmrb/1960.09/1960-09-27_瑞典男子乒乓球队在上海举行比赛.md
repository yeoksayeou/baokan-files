Title: Swedish Men's Table Tennis Team Competes in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-09-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 26th: The Swedish men's table tennis team held a friendly match with the Shanghai men's table tennis team two on the evening of the 24th in Shanghai. As a result, Shanghai Team Two won with a score of five to one. Of the five games won by Shanghai Team Two, four had scores of two to zero, and one had a score of two to one; the visiting team won one game with a score of two to zero. The Swedish men's table tennis team will leave Shanghai on the 26th and head to Beijing.

### 瑞典男子乒乓球队在上海举行比赛

1960-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

　  瑞典男子乒乓球队在上海举行比赛
    据新华社上海26日电　瑞典男子乒乓球队24日晚在上海与上海男子乒乓球二队举行友谊比赛。结果，上海二队以五比一获胜。上海二队所胜的五场，四场的比数是二比○，一场是二比一；客队胜的一场比数是二比○。瑞典男子乒乓球队将于26日离上海，前往北京。
