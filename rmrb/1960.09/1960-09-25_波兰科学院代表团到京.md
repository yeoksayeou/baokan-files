Title: Polish Academy of Sciences Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: A delegation of the Polish Academy of Sciences, consisting of six members led by Academician Tadeusz Kotarbinski, President of the Polish Academy of Sciences, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 24th to discuss the revision of the scientific cooperation agreement between the Chinese and Polish Academies of Sciences and to discuss the signing of the 1961 executive plan for scientific cooperation between the two academies.

Members of the delegation included: Academician Witold Stefański, Academician Ignacy Malecki, and Mr. Kłobukowski. Academician Tadeusz Kotarbinski's wife and Academician Witold Stefański's wife also arrived in Beijing with the delegation.

Greeting them at the airport were Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Wu Youxun, Vice President; Qin Lisheng, Deputy Secretary-General; Pan Zinian, Deputy Director of the Department of Philosophy and Social Sciences; Yan Jici, Director of the Department of Technical Sciences; Lin Rong, Deputy Director of the Department of Biological Sciences; Pan Chun, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Foreign Liaison, and others.

Dembnicki, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 波兰科学院代表团到京

1960-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　  波兰科学院代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　来华商谈修订中波两国科学院科学合作协议和商谈签订中波两国科学院科学合作1961年执行计划的波兰科学院代表团一行六人，在波兰科学院院长塔德乌什·柯塔宾斯基院士率领下，于24日乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团团员有：维托尔得·斯捷凡斯基院士、伊格纳茨·马来茨基院士和克道布罗沃尔斯基硕士。塔德乌什·柯塔宾斯基院士的夫人和维托尔得·斯捷凡斯基院士的夫人也随代表团到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国科学院院长郭沫若，副院长吴有训，副秘书长秦力生，哲学社会科学部副主任潘梓年，技术科学部主任严济慈，生物学部副主任林熔、对外联络局副局长潘纯等。
    波兰驻华大使馆临时代办邓布尼茨基也到机场欢迎。
