Title: President Touré Visits Moscow: Supreme Soviet Presidium and Government Host Banquet in Welcome
Author:
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th September. TASS Moscow, 6th September: The President of the Republic of Guinea, Touré, arrived in Moscow today for an official visit.

At the airport to welcome President Touré were Khrushchev, Mikoyan, Ignatov, Polyansky, representatives of the Moscow public, and students from African countries studying in Moscow schools.

Khrushchev and Touré exchanged greetings at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th September. According to TASS, Moscow: The President of the Republic of Guinea, Sékou Touré, today paid a courtesy call on the Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Khrushchev, and held friendly talks.

The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and the Soviet Government today held a banquet for Sékou Touré. The Speaker of the Guinean National Assembly, Diallo Saifoulaye, the Guinean Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Seydou Conté, and others attended the banquet. On the Soviet side, Khrushchev, Mikoyan, Ignatov, Polyansky, Arushanyan, and others attended the banquet.

Khrushchev and Sékou Touré spoke at the banquet.

The banquet took place in a friendly atmosphere.

### 杜尔总统到莫斯科访问  苏最高苏维埃主席团和政府设宴欢迎

1960-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    杜尔总统到莫斯科访问
    苏最高苏维埃主席团和政府设宴欢迎
    新华社7日讯　塔斯社莫斯科6日讯：几内亚共和国总统杜尔今天到达莫斯科作正式访问。
    到机场欢迎杜尔总统的有，赫鲁晓夫、米高扬、伊格纳托夫、波利扬斯基，莫斯科公众代表，在莫斯科学校里学习的非洲国家的学生。
    赫鲁晓夫和杜尔在机场上相互致词。
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔今天拜会了苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫，并且进行了友好的谈话。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联政府今天为塞古·杜尔举行了宴会。几内亚国民议会议长迪阿洛·赛福拉耶、几内亚驻苏大使塞杜·贡特等出席了宴会。苏联方面出席宴会的，有赫鲁晓夫、米高扬、伊格纳托夫、波利扬斯基、阿鲁沙尼扬等人。
    赫鲁晓夫和塞古·杜尔在宴会上先后讲了话。
    宴会是在友好的气氛中进行的。
