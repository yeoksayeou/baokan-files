Title: Three Legal Professionals from Senegal and Argentina Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Fadilou Diop, a Senegalese legal professional and lawyer, Dr. Rodolfo Araoz Alfaro, an Argentine legal professional and lawyer, and his wife, and Dr. Juan Martin Guidi, an Argentine legal professional and lawyer, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 24th at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Association.
    Zhang Youyu, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Association, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 塞内加尔、阿根廷三位法律工作者到京

1960-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　塞内加尔、阿根廷三位法律工作者到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的塞内加尔法律工作者、律师法迪卢·迪奥普，阿根廷法律工作者、律师罗多耳福·阿劳斯·阿耳法罗博士和夫人，以及阿根廷法律工作者、律师胡安·马丁·吉迪博士于24日乘飞机到达北京。
    中国政治法律学会副会长张友渔等前往机场欢迎。
