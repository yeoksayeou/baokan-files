Title: Song Qingling and Ke Qingshi Mourn President Pieck; Over 8,000 People in the Capital Paid Their Respects at the German Embassy Yesterday

Author:
Date: 1960-09-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 8th - After learning the unfortunate news of the passing of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, Song Qingling, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, and Mayor of Shanghai, visited the Consulate General of the German Democratic Republic in Shanghai this morning to offer condolences and express their deep sorrow over the death of President Wilhelm Pieck.
    Also present at the Consulate General of the German Democratic Republic in Shanghai this morning to pay respects were Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee; Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Wei Wenbo and Ma Tianshui, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee; Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Song Jiwen, Song Richang, and Jin Zhonghua, Deputy Mayors of Shanghai; Hu Juewen, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC; Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison District of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; and responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations.
    The broad masses of the people of Shanghai also visited the Consulate General of the German Democratic Republic in Shanghai today with grief to offer condolences. Those who came to pay respects included workers, staff of government offices, soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, professors, students, scientists, writers, and street women.
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th - More than 8,000 responsible persons from various departments of state organs and people from all walks of life in the capital went to the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China today to mourn the unfortunate death of President Wilhelm Pieck. The mourners, wearing black gauze and carrying wreaths, solemnly entered the hall of the embassy to stand in silent tribute before the portrait of President Wilhelm Pieck.
    In the hall of the embassy were placed many wreaths, including those sent by Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairmen Song Qingling and Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou Enlai, as well as wreaths sent by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the State Council, and the National Committee of the CPPCC.
    Among those who went to the embassy to pay respects today were: Vice Premier Chen Yi, members of the CPC Central Committee and responsible persons of various departments of the CPC Central Committee, responsible persons of various departments of state organs, various democratic parties, people's organizations, and Beijing Municipality, generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and people from all walks of life in the capital.
    Diplomats and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China also went to the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China today to express their condolences and sent letters to the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China to express their condolences. ### 宋庆龄和柯庆施吊唁皮克总统  首都八千多人昨日到德大使馆吊唁

1960-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄和柯庆施吊唁皮克总统
    首都八千多人昨日到德大使馆吊唁
    新华社上海8日电　中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄，中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施等，在获悉德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克同志不幸逝世的消息后，今天上午前往德意志民主共和国驻上海总领事馆吊唁，对威廉·皮克总统的逝世，表示沉痛悼念。
    今天上午前往德意志民主共和国驻上海总领事馆吊唁的还有中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，中共上海市委书记处书记魏文伯、马天水，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，上海市副市长宋季文、宋日昌、金仲华，政协上海市委员会副主席胡厥文，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将以及各民主党派、各人民团体负责人等。
    上海市广大人民今天也怀着悲痛的心情络绎不绝地前往德意志民主共和国驻上海总领事馆吊唁。前往吊唁的有工人、机关工作者、中国人民解放军战士、教授、学生、科学家、作家和街道妇女等。
    新华社8日讯　国家机关各部门的负责人和首都各界人士八千多人，今天到德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆吊唁威廉·皮克总统的不幸逝世。吊唁的人们戴着黑纱，抬着花圈，肃穆地走进大使馆的大厅，在威廉·皮克总统的遗像前静默悼念。
    在大使馆的大厅里摆着许多花圈，其中有毛泽东主席，刘少奇主席，宋庆龄、董必武副主席，朱德委员长和周恩来总理送的花圈，也有中国共产党中央委员会、全国人民代表大会常务委员会、国务院、政协全国委员会送的花圈。
    今天，前往大使馆吊唁的有：陈毅副总理，中共中央委员和中共中央各部门的负责人，国家机关各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体和北京市的负责人，中国人民解放军的将军，以及首都各界人士。
    各国驻中国的使节和外交官员，今天也前往德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆表示哀悼和致函德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆表示哀悼。（附图片）
