Title: People in Lebanon's Capital Rally in Support of the Congolese People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, September 1st: The Lebanese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee held a mass rally in Beirut on the evening of August 31st to support the Congolese people's struggle to defend their national sovereignty and independence.

    The four speakers at the rally unanimously condemned the conspiracies of Belgian and American imperialists to split the Congo, and expressed their solidarity with the Congolese people.

    More than 500 people from all walks of life in Beirut attended the rally. The rally passed two telegrams, addressed separately to the United Nations and the Prime Minister of the Congo, expressing full support for the Congolese people's struggle against the imperialist conspiracy to split the Congo.

### 黎巴嫩首都人民集会  支持刚果人民斗争

1960-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩首都人民集会
    支持刚果人民斗争
    新华社贝鲁特1日电　黎巴嫩亚非团结委员会8月31日晚在贝鲁特举行群众大会，支持刚果人民为保卫国家的主权和独立而进行的斗争。
    四个在大会上发言的人，一致谴责比利时和美帝国主义者分裂刚果的阴谋，并且表示声援刚果人民。
    贝鲁特各界人士五百多人参加了这次大会。大会通过了分别打给联合国和刚果总理的两封电报，表示完全支持刚果人民为反对帝国主义者分裂刚果的阴谋而进行的斗争。
