Title: Zhou Yang Receives Putrament; Mao Dun Hosts Banquet for Polish Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Zhou Yang, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, received Putrament, Vice Chairman of the Polish Writers Association, and his wife, who are visiting China, this afternoon.

Accompanying the reception were Liu Baiyu, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, Yang Shuo, Director of the Foreign Literature Committee of the Chinese Writers Association, and Ruan Zhangjing, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Poetry Magazine," among others.

Mao Dun, Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association and President of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, held a banquet on the evening of the 24th to welcome Putrament, Vice Chairman of the Polish Writers Association, and his wife.

Among those attending the banquet were Zhou Yang and Liu Baiyu, Vice Chairmen of the Chinese Writers Association, He Chengxiang, Vice President of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, Yang Shuo, Director of the Foreign Literature Committee of the Chinese Writers Association, Xing Ye, Deputy Director, and Ruan Zhangjing, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Poetry Magazine," among others.

Dembnicki, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 周扬接见普特拉曼特  茅盾欢宴波兰客人

1960-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　  周扬接见普特拉曼特
    茅盾欢宴波兰客人
    新华社24日讯　中共中央宣传部副部长、中国作家协会副主席周扬，24日下午接见了来我国访问的波兰作家协会副主席普特拉曼特和夫人。
    陪同接见的，有中国作家协会副主席刘白羽、中国作家协会外国文学委员会主任杨朔、“诗刊”副主编阮章竞等。
    中国作家协会主席、中波友好协会会长茅盾于24日晚举行宴会，欢迎波兰作家协会副主席普特拉曼特和夫人。
    参加宴会的，有中国作家协会副主席周扬、刘白羽，中波友协副会长何成湘，中国作家协会外国文学委员会主任杨朔、副主任邢野、“诗刊”副主编阮章竞等。
    波兰驻华大使馆临时代办邓布尼茨基也应邀出席了宴会。
