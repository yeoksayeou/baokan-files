Title: Nakajima Kenzo Expresses Gratitude to Chu Tunan and Yang Hansheng for the Japanese Theater Troupe's Performances in China
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Vice President Yang Hansheng, today received a telegram from Tokyo sent by Nakajima Kenzo, Director-General of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, on the occasion of the opening ceremony for the Japanese Theater Troupe's performances in China.

The telegram said: "As the Japanese Theater Troupe is visiting and performing in your esteemed country, and the opening ceremony is being held, I express my heartfelt joy and gratitude. This visit and performance demonstrates that in the struggle against American imperialism, the friendly unity and cultural exchange between the peoples of Japan and China has been further strengthened. No force can undermine this friendship and unity. It is my greatest honor to have the opportunity to convey to you two gentlemen and to the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the people of your country, the heartfelt respect, friendship, and gratitude of the entire Japanese people who are fighting to abolish the Japan-U.S. Military Alliance Treaty, withdraw American military bases, and strive for independence, peace, democracy, and neutrality. Through this visit and performance, the friendly unity and cultural exchange between the two peoples will be increasingly strengthened. This is a brilliant victory for the peoples of Japan and China!"

### 中岛健藏为日本话剧团在我国演出　致电楚图南阳翰笙表示谢意

1960-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　中岛健藏为日本话剧团在我国演出
  　致电楚图南阳翰笙表示谢意
    新华社24日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长阳翰笙今天收到日本中国文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏为日本话剧团在中国演出举行开幕式从东京发来的电报。
    电报说：“正当日本话剧团在贵国访问演出，举行开幕式的时候，我衷心地表示喜悦和谢意。这次访问演出，表明在反对美帝国主义的斗争中，日中两国人民的友好团结和文化交流得到了进一步的加强。任何势力都不能损害这种友好和团结。能够有机会传达为要求废除日美军事同盟条约、撤退美国军事基地和争取独立、和平、民主、中立而斗争的全体日本人民，对两位先生，中国人民对外文化协会、中国文学艺术界联合会以及贵国人民，致以衷心的尊敬、友谊和感谢之意，是我最大的光荣。通过这次访问演出，两国人民的友好团结和文化交流将日益加强，这是日中两国人民的光辉胜利！”
