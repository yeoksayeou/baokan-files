Title: Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee Holds Sixth Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, September 12th: The sixth meeting of the first session of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee was held at the Singha Durbar in Kathmandu from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM today.

The meeting was conducted in a sincere and friendly atmosphere.

The next meeting will be held on September 19th.

### 中尼边界联合委员会举行第六次会谈

1960-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    中尼边界联合委员会举行第六次会谈
    新华社12日讯　加德满都消息：中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议第六次会谈，今天上午十时到十二时在加德满都的狮宫举行。
    会谈是在真诚和友好的气氛中进行的。
    下次会谈将在9月19日举行。
