Title: Supporting the Just Struggle of Congolese Brothers: Africans in London Hold Demonstration, Strongly Condemning UN Interference, Angrily Declaring the United States an Aggressor Nation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 18th: The African Organizations Committee here held a mass meeting in Trafalgar Square today, attended by 1,500 people. The meeting loudly proclaimed: "Stop the UN's conspiracy in the Congo!" "Support the Lumumba government!"

Prior to the meeting, about seven hundred Africans held a demonstration. They carried signs from Hyde Park, through the city center to Trafalgar Square. In their signs, they expressed their anger at the UN's interference in the Congo and their determination to support the legitimate government of Lumumba in the Congo.

Kwesi Armah, Chairman of the African Organizations Committee, spoke at the meeting. He condemned the NATO countries for plotting to overthrow the Lumumba government and create chaos in the Congo. He demanded that the tools in the hands of the imperialists – the secessionists – be disarmed. He said angrily: "We don't trust the UN!"

Kwesi Armah's words aroused a warm response among the audience. They shouted: "We want Lumumba!" "American imperialists and NATO get out of the Congo!" "Hammarskjöld must resign!"

John La Rose, Assistant General Secretary of the African Organizations Committee, asked the audience: Do you think the UN headquarters should be moved from the United States to another place, because the United States is an aggressor nation? At this point, the audience applauded enthusiastically and shouted in response: "Yes!"

At the end of the meeting, many Africans voluntarily donated to establish a fund that would be used to tell the European public the truth about the Congo. This is because Western newspapers spread false information and greatly distorted the true situation in the Congo.

### 支持刚果兄弟的正义斗争  伦敦非洲人举行示威  强烈谴责联合国的干涉行为  愤怒宣布美国是一个侵略国

1960-09-20
第6版()
专栏：

    支持刚果兄弟的正义斗争
    伦敦非洲人举行示威
    强烈谴责联合国的干涉行为  愤怒宣布美国是一个侵略国
    新华社伦敦18日电　这里的非洲组织委员会今天在特拉法加广场召开了有一千五百人参加的群众大会。大会大声疾呼：“制止联合国在刚果的阴谋！”“支持卢蒙巴政府！”
    大会召开之前，大约有七百名非洲人举行了示威游行。他们带着标语牌从海德公园出发，经过市中心到特拉法加广场。他们在标语中表达了谴责联合国干涉刚果的愤怒心情和支持刚果卢蒙巴合法政府的决心。
    非洲组织委员会主席奎西·阿马赫在大会上讲了话。他谴责北大西洋公约组织国家阴谋推翻卢蒙巴政府和在刚果制造混乱。他要求把帝国主义者手中的工具——分裂分子解除武装。他愤愤地说：“我们不信任联合国！”
    奎西·阿马赫的话在听众中引起了热烈的反应。他们高呼：“我们要卢蒙巴！”“美帝国主义者和北大西洋公约组织滚出刚果！”“哈马舍尔德必须辞职！”
    非洲组织委员会助理总书记约翰·郎莫尔讲话时向听众问道：你们是否认为联合国总部应当从美国迁到别的地方去，因为美国是一个侵略国。这时听众们热烈鼓掌，高声回答：“是的！”
    大会结束时，许多非洲人自动为建立一笔基金捐款，这笔基金将用于把刚果的真实情况告诉欧洲的公众的工作。因为西方报纸散布假消息，大肆歪曲了刚果的真实情况。
