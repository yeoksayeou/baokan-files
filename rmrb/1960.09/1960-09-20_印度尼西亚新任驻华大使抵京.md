Title: New Indonesian Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 19th. The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Indonesia to China, Sukarni Kartodiwirjo, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon.

Those who went to the station to welcome him included Ge Buhai, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Department. Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia in China, Suleiman, and embassy officials also went to the station to greet him.

### 印度尼西亚新任驻华大使抵京

1960-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚新任驻华大使抵京
    新华社19日讯　印度尼西亚共和国新任驻华特命全权大使苏卡尼·卡托迪维约今天下午乘火车抵达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有外交部礼宾司副司长葛步海和第一亚洲司副司长沈平。印度尼西亚共和国驻华大使馆临时代办苏雷曼及大使馆官员也前往车站迎接。
