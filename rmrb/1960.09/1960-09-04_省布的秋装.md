    Autumn Clothes That Save Fabric
    Designed by the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Light Industry Clothing, Shoes, and Hats Research Institute
    This set of autumn clothing is designed using navy blue wool fabric, with a simple and elegant style. The main feature of its tailoring is: 1. Changing the two-piece sleeve to a one-piece sleeve; 2. Changing inseam pockets to patch pockets; 3. Making the collar into a stand-up or fold-down convertible style; back belt stitching. Because the designer has adopted the above clever tailoring methods, it can save about three feet of fabric compared to ordinary women's clothing. If you use brightly colored plaid wool or woolen fabric, it will also be very beautiful and elegant to wear during festivals.

    省布的秋装
    北京市轻工业局服装鞋帽研究所设计
    这套秋装是用毛蓝布设计的，式样简单大方。它的裁剪主要特点是：一、改两片袖为一片袖；二、开袋改为明贴袋；三、领子做成立、翻两用式；后背带缝。由于设计者采用了以上的几种巧裁法，因此比一般女装能节约三尺布左右。如果用鲜艳色的格呢或线呢做，在节日穿用也是非常美观大方的。排料说明
