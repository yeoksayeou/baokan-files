Title: Chairman Liu Approves Sino-Czechoslovak Consular Treaty
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, in accordance with the decision of the Enlarged 27th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, approved on September 9, 1960, the "Consular Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic" signed in Prague on May 7, 1960.

### 刘主席批准中捷领事条约

1960-09-11
第2版()
专栏：

    刘主席批准中捷领事条约
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十七次会议扩大会议的决定，于1960年9月9日批准了1960年5月7日在布拉格签订的“中华人民共和国和捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国领事条约”。
