Title: Burmese Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Second Group of Burmese Cultural Delegation Members Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The Burmese Trade Delegation, led by U Mya, Member of Parliament of the Union of Burma, arrived in Beijing today by plane from Kunming.

　　This delegation is a specialized delegation belonging to the Burmese Union Government Delegation headed by Prime Minister U Nu, which will soon visit our country.

　　Among those who went to the airport to welcome them were Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Liu Xiwu, Director of the Fourth Bureau, and the responsible persons of other relevant bureaus and import and export corporations. Peng Jinbo, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, also went to the airport to welcome them.

　　Brigadier General Aung Gyi and other Burmese guests in Beijing, as well as U Onh Kin, First Secretary, and U Yong Lin, Second Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The second group of 39 members of the Burmese Cultural Delegation arrived in Beijing this afternoon by plane from Kunming. Among those who went to the airport to welcome them were Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Hu Yuzhi, Vice Minister of Culture, Jian Bozan, Vice President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, and more than 100 people from the capital's literary and art circles.

　　Brigadier General Aung Gyi and other Burmese guests in Beijing, as well as U Onh Kin, First Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China, and representatives of the Cultural Delegation who arrived earlier, also went to the airport to welcome them.

　　With the exception of Deputy Head of the Delegation Ba Than and Secretary-General U Pe Kin, who have not yet arrived in Beijing, all 164 members of the Burmese Cultural Delegation have now arrived in Beijing.

### 缅甸贸易代表团到京　缅甸文化代表团第二批团员到京

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅甸贸易代表团到京
　  缅甸文化代表团第二批团员到京
    据新华社25日讯　缅甸贸易代表团在缅甸联邦国会议员吴妙率领下，今天由昆明乘飞机到达北京。
    这个代表团是即将来我国访问的以吴努总理为首的缅甸联邦政府代表团所属的一个专业代表团。
    前往机场欢迎的有对外贸易部副部长卢绪章，第四局局长刘希文，其他有关局和进出口专业公司的负责人。前往机场欢迎的还有我国驻缅甸大使馆商务参赞彭金波。
    昂季准将等缅甸在京贵宾和缅甸驻华大使馆一等秘书吴翁钦、二等秘书吴容林也到机场欢迎。
    新华社25日讯　缅甸文化代表团第二批团员三十九人，今天下午由昆明乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林、文化部副部长胡愈之、中缅友协副会长翦伯赞及首都文艺界人士共一百多人。
    以昂季准将为首的缅甸在京贵宾、缅甸驻华大使馆一等秘书吴翁钦和先行抵京的文化代表团代表也到机场欢迎。
    缅甸文化代表团除副团长巴丹、秘书长吴佩建尚未到京外，全团一百六十四人已全部到达北京。
