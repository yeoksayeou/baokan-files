Title: Ding Xilin and Yang Shuo Depart for Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: At the invitation of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and Yang Shuo, council member, left Beijing today for Ceylon to participate in the celebrations of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China organized by the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, and to pay a friendly visit.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Luo Jun, Standing Council Member of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Lin Lin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries; and Xing Ye, Deputy Director of the Foreign Literature Committee of the Writers Association.

Also at the airport to see them off was Yugosuntnan, First Secretary of the Ceylon Embassy in China.

### 丁西林、杨朔前往锡兰

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

    丁西林、杨朔前往锡兰
    新华社29日讯　应锡兰中国友好协会邀请，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林、理事杨朔，今日离开北京前往锡兰，参加锡中友好协会举办的中华人民共和国成立十一周年的庆祝活动，并进行友好访问。
    到机场送行的，有对外文协常务理事、对外文委副主任罗俊，对外文协副秘书长林林，作家协会外国文学委员会副主任邢野。
    到机场送行的，还有锡兰驻华大使馆一等秘书尤戈孙特南。
