Title: China's Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe Performs in Hungary
Author:
Date: 1960-09-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 8th—The Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe gave its opening performance in Budapest on the evening of the 7th.
　　The rich and colorful Chinese acrobatic art with national characteristics won repeated warm applause from more than five thousand spectators.
　　The Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe arrived in Budapest on the 6th. According to the 1960 implementation plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the acrobatic troupe will be on a month-long visit and performance in Hungary.

### 我国重庆杂技团在匈牙利演出

1960-09-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我国重庆杂技团在匈牙利演出
    新华社布达佩斯8日电　中国重庆杂技团7日晚在布达佩斯举行开幕演出。
    丰富多彩、具有民族特色的中国杂技艺术博得了五千多观众的多次热烈掌声。
    中国重庆杂技团是在6日抵达布达佩斯的。根据中匈文化合作协定1960年执行计划，杂技团将在匈牙利作为期一个月的访问演出。
