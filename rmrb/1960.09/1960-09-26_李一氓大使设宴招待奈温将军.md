Title: Ambassador Li Yimang Hosts Banquet for General Ne Win
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 24th. Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Li Yimang, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 24th for Burmese Chief of Staff of the Defence Forces, General Ne Win, and his wife. They will soon be travelling to China at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai to participate in China's National Day celebrations and to attend the signing ceremony of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty in Beijing on October 1st.

Lieutenant Colonel Lun, Director of Burmese Military Intelligence, also attended the banquet.

Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and Senior Colonel Lu Zhan, Deputy Military Attaché, also attended the banquet.

The banquet took place in an extremely warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 李一氓大使设宴招待奈温将军

1960-09-26
第5版()
专栏：

　  李一氓大使设宴招待奈温将军
    新华社仰光24日电　中国驻缅甸大使李一氓24日晚上设宴招待缅甸国防军总参谋长奈温将军及其夫人。他们应周恩来总理的邀请，不久将前往中国参加中国国庆节庆典，并参加10月1日在北京举行的中缅边界条约的签字仪式。
    缅甸军事情报处处长伦中校也出席了宴会。
    中国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定大校和副武官路展上校也出席了宴会。
    宴会是在极为热烈和友好的气氛中进行的。
