Title: Joint Statement Signed by Chinese Machinery Trade Union and British Boiler Workers Union: Develop Friendship Between Chinese and British Workers, Expressing Firm Support for the Struggle of Asian, African and Latin American Peoples to Safeguard National Independence
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 21st: The National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union and the United Kingdom Boilermakers, Shipbuilders and Structural Workers Society today issued a joint statement in Beijing. The statement pointed out that, based on their mutual support in the common struggle to safeguard peace, Chinese machinery workers and British boiler workers will further develop the friendship between the workers and peoples of China and Britain, and contribute to lasting world peace. At the same time, both sides firmly support the struggle of the peoples of Asian, African, and Latin American countries to win and safeguard national independence, and believe that the growing national independence movement in Asia, Africa, and Latin America is an important part of safeguarding world peace.

    The signing ceremony of the joint statement was held this afternoon. He Ruilin and Yu Huan, Vice-Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union, signed on behalf of the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union, while John Hepplewhite and Albert Chalkley, Executive Council Members of the United Kingdom Boilermakers, Shipbuilders and Structural Workers Society, signed on behalf of the Society.

    On the Chinese side, Li Jiebo, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Chen Yu, Director of the International Liaison Department, and Liu Jingmao, Director of the General Office of the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union, attended the signing ceremony.

    On the British side, Joseph Gormley and Edmund Elimor, members of the British Boilermakers Union delegation, attended the signing ceremony.

    Xinhua News Agency, September 21st: The Chinese Machinery Trade Union held a celebratory gathering tonight to celebrate the signing of the joint statement by the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union and the United Kingdom Boilermakers, Shipbuilders and Structural Workers Society.

    The entire British Boilermakers Union delegation, headed by John Hepplewhite, attended the gathering.

    Yu Huan, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union, spoke at the gathering. He first congratulated the signing of the joint statement by the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union and the United Kingdom Boilermakers, Shipbuilders and Structural Workers Society. He said that this statement fully demonstrated the solidarity and mutual support of the Chinese and British workers. This statement marks a new stage in the friendly relations between our two trade union organizations. It will further strengthen the unity between Chinese machinery workers and British boiler workers and their unions. Our two trade unions will continue to work together for the friendship between the Chinese and British workers and peoples, and for world peace.

    John Hepplewhite, head of the British Boilermakers Union delegation, said in his speech that today is a historic day, and I am proud to have signed a joint statement with the Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union for our common purpose. He said that this joint statement is only a beginning, and he hopes that this example will be followed by more British trade unions in the future, and that more British trade union delegations will visit China, and more Chinese trade union delegations will visit Britain. In this way, we can have more mutual exchanges, contacts, and understanding, and we can strengthen friendship between each other, and promote world peace.

    The gathering was filled with a cordial and warm atmosphere. Representatives of Chinese machinery workers, model workers and advanced producers of industrial production in the capital, and British boiler worker brothers gathered together to enjoy themselves, sing, and dance. Hepplewhite and Gormley sang British folk songs with great interest at the gathering, and danced British folk dances with the Chinese workers.

    Before the gathering, the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union held a reception to celebrate the signing of the joint statement with the United Kingdom Boilermakers, Shipbuilders and Structural Workers Society.

    He Ruilin, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union, Chen Yu, Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, representatives of workers in the capital, and advanced producers attended the gathering and reception.

### 中国机械工会和英国锅炉工会签订联合声明　发展中英两国工人的友谊　表示坚决支持亚非拉丁美洲人民维护民族独立的斗争

1960-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中国机械工会和英国锅炉工会签订联合声明
　　发展中英两国工人的友谊
  　表示坚决支持亚非拉丁美洲人民维护民族独立的斗争
    新华社21日讯　中国机械工会全国委员会和联合王国锅炉制造、造船和结构工人联合协会今天在北京发表联合声明。声明指出，中国机械工人和英国锅炉工人基于他们在维护和平的共同斗争中的相互支持，将进一步发展中英两国工人和人民的友谊，并为持久的世界和平作出贡献。同时双方坚决支持亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲各国人民争取与维护民族独立的斗争，并认为亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲日益发展的民族独立运动是维护世界和平的一个重要部分。
    联合声明的签字仪式在今天下午举行。代表中国机械工会全国委员会签字的，是中国机械工会全国委员会副主席贺瑞林、于桓，代表联合王国锅炉制造、造船和结构工人联合协会签字的，是英国锅炉制造工人协会执行委员约翰·赫波怀德、艾伯特·乔克利。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有：中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯、国际联络部部长陈宇，中国机械工会全国委员会办公室主任刘京茂等。
    参加签字仪式的，英国方面有：英国锅炉工会代表团团员约瑟夫·戈姆利、埃德蒙德·艾利摩。
    新华社21日讯　中国机械工会今晚举行联欢晚会，庆祝中国机械工会全国委员会和联合王国锅炉制造、造船和结构工人联合协会签订的联合声明。
    以约翰·赫波怀德为首的英国锅炉制造工人协会代表团全体成员出席了晚会。
    中国机械工会全国委员会副主席于桓在晚会上讲话。他首先祝贺中国机械工会全国委员会和联合王国锅炉制造、造船和结构工人联合协会签订的联合声明。他说，这个声明充分表明了中国工人和英国工人之间的团结、相互支持的愿望。这个声明标志着我们两个工会组织友好关系的新阶段，它将进一步加强中国机械工人和英国锅炉工人及其工会之间的团结，我们两个工会将继续为中英两国工人和人民的友谊，为世界和平而共同努力。
    英国锅炉制造工人协会代表团团长约翰·赫波怀德在讲话中说，今天是一个有历史意义的日子，我感到自豪的是，为了我们共同的目的同中国机械工会副主席签订了联合声明。他说，这个联合声明只是一个开始，希望这一例子为今后更多的英国工会所仿效，希望能有更多的英国工会代表团访问中国，有更多的中国工会代表团访问英国。这样就能更多的互相交往、互相接触、互相了解，就更能加强彼此之间的友谊，就能促进世界和平。
    晚会充满了亲切热烈的欢乐气氛。中国机械工人代表、首都工业生产劳动模范和先进生产者同英国锅炉工人兄弟们欢聚在一起，尽情地欢乐、唱歌、跳舞。赫波怀德和戈姆利兴致勃勃地在晚会上演唱了英国的民歌，并且同中国工人们一起跳英国的民间舞蹈。
    晚会前，中国机械工会全国委员会为祝贺同联合王国锅炉制造、造船和结构工人联合协会签订联合声明举行了酒会。
    出席晚会和酒会的有中国机械工会全国委员会副主席贺瑞林，中华全国总工会国际联络部部长陈宇和首都工人代表、先进生产者等。
