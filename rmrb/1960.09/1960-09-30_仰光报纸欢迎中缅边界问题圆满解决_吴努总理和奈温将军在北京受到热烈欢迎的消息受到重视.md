Title: Rangoon Newspapers Welcome Successful Resolution of Sino-Burmese Border Issue; News of Warm Welcome for Prime Minister U Nu and General Ne Win in Beijing Receives Attention
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7
　　
　　Rangoon Newspapers Welcome Successful Resolution of Sino-Burmese Border Issue
　  News of Warm Welcome for Prime Minister U Nu and General Ne Win in Beijing Receives Attention
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 29th - Newspapers in the Burmese capital, Rangoon, today welcomed the successful resolution of the Sino-Burmese border issue. The newspapers also prominently featured news of Prime Minister U Nu and General Ne Win's arrival in Beijing on their front pages.

    The "Justice Daily" stated in its editorial today that the successful resolution of the Sino-Burmese border issue is a very pleasing event. The newspaper emphasized, "Our two countries believe that the successful conclusion of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty is a victory."

    The newspaper also said, "Many Burmese representatives will participate in the signing ceremony of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty held in Beijing. In the past, we have never sent such a friendly delegation, including so many people, abroad."

    "Federal Daily," "Mirror Daily," and "Vanguard Daily," etc., used striking headlines when reporting the news of Prime Minister U Nu and General Ne Win's arrival in Beijing. The headlines stated, "Thousands of People Welcome U Nu and Ne Win," "Relations Between the Two Countries Achieve Victory Based on the Five Principles."

### 仰光报纸欢迎中缅边界问题圆满解决  吴努总理和奈温将军在北京受到热烈欢迎的消息受到重视

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

　　仰光报纸欢迎中缅边界问题圆满解决
　  吴努总理和奈温将军在北京受到热烈欢迎的消息受到重视
    新华社仰光29日电　缅甸首都仰光报纸今天欢迎中缅边界问题的成功解决。各报还在头版显著地位刊载了吴努总理和奈温将军到达北京的消息。
    “公正报”在今天的社论中说，中缅边界问题的成功解决，是令人非常高兴的事。这家报纸强调说，“我们两国认为，中缅边界条约的成功缔结是一个胜利。”
    这家报纸还说，“在北京举行的中缅边界条约的签字仪式，将有许多缅甸代表参加。过去从没有派遣包括这么多人的这样的友好代表团到国外去过。
    “联邦报”、“镜报”和“先锋报”等在报道吴努总理和奈温将军到达北京的消息时用了醒目的大字标题。标题说，“成千上万的人欢迎吴努和奈温”，“两国关系根据五项原则获得胜利”。
