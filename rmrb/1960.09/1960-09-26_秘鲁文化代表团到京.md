Title: Peruvian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. At the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, a three-member Peruvian cultural delegation, led by Mrs. Elisa de Maus, Secretary for Cultural Liaison of the Peru-China Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to our country.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, Vice President Zhou Erfu, and Secretary-General Chen Jin, among others.

### 秘鲁文化代表团到京

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

    秘鲁文化代表团到京
    新华社25日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国参观访问的秘鲁文化代表团一行三人，在团长、秘中友协文化联络秘书埃莉萨·德马修斯夫人率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南、副会长周而复和总干事陈金等人。
