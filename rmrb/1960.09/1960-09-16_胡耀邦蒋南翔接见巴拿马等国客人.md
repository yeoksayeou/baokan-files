Title: Hu Yaobang and Jiang Nanxiang Receive Guests from Panama and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-09-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th: Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and Jiang Nanxiang, Vice Minister of Education, received representatives from the national student unions of Panama, Puerto Rico, Guatemala, Honduras, and El Salvador, who are currently visiting China, on the 12th and held cordial talks with them.

Present at the reception were Zhang Yinan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and others.

### 胡耀邦蒋南翔接见巴拿马等国客人

1960-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

    胡耀邦蒋南翔接见巴拿马等国客人
    新华社13日讯　中国共产主义青年团中央委员会第一书记胡耀邦、教育部副部长蒋南翔，12日先后接见了正在我国访问的巴拿马、波多黎各、危地马拉、洪都拉斯和萨尔瓦多全国学联的代表们，并和他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中华全国学生联合会副主席张寅安等人。
