Title: German Democratic Republic Announces West German Fighter Jets Violate German Airspace
Author:
Date: 1960-09-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 25th: According to a Deutsche Presse-Agentur report on the 24th, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic announced that on the morning of the 22nd, two West German "F-86" fighter jets violated the airspace of the German Democratic Republic. The two aircraft flew from the direction of Bad Harzburg, penetrating about eight kilometers into the territory of the German Democratic Republic. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic also pointed out that the West German government bears full responsibility for this renewed and serious violation of the airspace of the German Democratic Republic.

### 德意志民主共和国宣布　西德战斗机侵犯德国领空

1960-09-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　德意志民主共和国宣布
　　西德战斗机侵犯德国领空
    新华社柏林25日电　据德意志通讯社24日报道：德意志民主共和国外交部宣布，在22日上午，有两架西德“F—86”型战斗机侵犯了德意志民主共和国的领空。这两架飞机从巴德哈次堡方向飞来，侵入德意志民主共和国境内约八公里。德意志民主共和国外交部并指出，西德政府应对这种再度严重侵犯德意志民主共和国领空的行动负完全的责任。
