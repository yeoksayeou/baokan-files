Title: The Soviet Union, Ukrainian and Belarusian Republics Will Send Delegations to the UN General Assembly Separately
Author:
Date: 1960-09-09
Page: 5

    The Soviet Union, Ukrainian and Belarusian Republics
    Will Send Delegations to the UN General Assembly Separately
    Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: According to TASS, the Councils of Ministers of the Ukrainian and Belarusian Soviet Socialist Republics have determined the list of delegations to attend the 15th session of the United Nations General Assembly. The Ukrainian delegation will be led by Podgorny. The Belarusian delegation will be led by Mazurov.

### 苏联乌克兰和白俄罗斯共和国  将分别派代表团出席联大

1960-09-09
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联乌克兰和白俄罗斯共和国
    将分别派代表团出席联大
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联乌克兰和白俄罗斯加盟共和国部长会议确定了参加联合国大会第十五届会议的代表团名单。乌克兰代表团由波德戈尔内率领。白俄罗斯代表团由马祖罗夫率领。
