Title: Chairman Liu Sends Congratulations to Finnish President on His Sixtieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd September. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram to the President of the Republic of Finland, Urho Kekkonen, congratulating him on his sixtieth birthday. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Urho Kaleva Kekkonen, President of the Republic of Finland:

On the occasion of Your Excellency's sixtieth birthday, I extend my sincere congratulations. I wish Your Excellency good health and happiness, and new achievements in the work of enhancing the friendship between the peoples of China and Finland.

                Liu Shaoqi
                Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                September 1, 1960

### 刘主席电贺芬总统六十寿辰

1960-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席电贺芬总统六十寿辰
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇致电芬兰共和国总统吉科宁祝贺他的六十寿辰。电文如下：芬兰共和国总统乌尔霍·卡勒瓦·吉科宁先生阁下：
    欣逢阁下六十寿辰，我谨表示诚挚的祝贺。祝阁下健康幸福，并在增进中芬两国人民友谊的工作中取得新的成就。
                中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                                1960年9月1日
