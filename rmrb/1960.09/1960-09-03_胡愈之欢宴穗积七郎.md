Title: Hu Yuzhi Hosts a Banquet for Suemitsu Shichiro
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd -- Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 1st to welcome Suemitsu Shichiro, a member of the Japanese Socialist Party's Diet and Chairman of the Socialist Party's Special Committee for the Protection of the Constitution.
    Those attending the banquet included Zhao Anbo, Xie Nanguang, Sun Pinghua, Wu Maosun, Wu Xiaoda, Xiao Xiangqian, etc. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, also attended the banquet.

### 胡愈之欢宴穗积七郎

1960-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    胡愈之欢宴穗积七郎
    新华社2日讯　中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之1日晚设宴欢迎日本社会党国会议员、社会党拥护宪法特别委员会委员长穗积七郎。
    参加宴会的，有赵安博、谢南光、孙平化、吴茂荪、吴晓达、肖向前等。在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也参加了宴会。
