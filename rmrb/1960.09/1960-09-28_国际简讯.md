　　International Briefs
　  Chilean President Issues Electoral Reform Law
    Santiago dispatch: The Chilean President issued an electoral reform law on the 23rd, abolishing the previous provision that members of the House of Representatives could form campaign agreements in elections. According to this reform law, the People's Action Front, composed of the Communist Party, Socialist Party, and other parties, will not be able to nominate joint candidates in elections.
　  Japanese Government Plans to Establish Minister of Intelligence
    Tokyo dispatch: Japanese Prime Minister Hayato Ikeda revealed on the 26th that his government is preparing to establish a Minister of Intelligence to strengthen control over news and conduct intelligence activities.

### 国际简讯

1960-09-28
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
　  智利总统颁布选举改革法
    圣地亚哥讯　智利总统在23日颁布了一项选举改革法，废除了以前众议员可以在选举中成立竞选协定的规定。按照这一改革法，由共产党、社会党及其他政党组成的人民行动阵线将不能在选举中提出联合候选人。
　  日本政府计划设立情报相
    东京讯　日本首相池田勇人26日透露，他的政府正准备设立情报相，加强控制新闻和进行情报活动。
