Title: Three Revolutionary Poems from Guinea
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 8

Long Live Independence!
    Sori Popo
  Long live independence!
  It is the light of independence
      that gives us new life!
  It is the light of independence
      that restores our freedom!
  Independence—our mother,
      Long live! Long live!
  Thanks be to Allah, thanks be to the African Democratic Rally,
      Our wish has finally been fulfilled!
  Lies have been exposed, long live our mother! The wishes of our predecessors have finally been realized, long live independence!
    The French Have Scrammed!
    Franqi Xijiasegu·Du'er ah! The French have scrammed,
  Without even saying goodbye! How embarrassing!
  The French have finally left us! How embarrassing! I swear in the name of Allah,
  Without even saying goodbye! The French have really scrammed,
  Without even having time to say goodbye!
    Farewell, Europeans!
    Weng·Gaisaige
  Farewell, Europeans! Don't blame the heavens, don't blame others! Look at me, I'm not upset about this. Farewell, it's time for each of you to return home;
  The old history should have ended cleanly long ago! Farewell, may you never again disturb
  us! May you from now on understand
  how to abide by
      inescapable
      obligations and responsibilities!
                Translated by Gao Fei

### 几内亚革命诗歌三首

1960-09-10
第8版()
专栏：

    几内亚革命诗歌三首
    万岁！独立万岁！
    索里·波波
  万岁！独立万岁！
  重给我们以新生命的，
      是独立的光辉！
  使我们重新得到自由的，
      是独立的光辉！
  独立啊——我们的母亲，
      万岁！万岁！
  感谢真主，感谢非洲民主联盟，
      我们的心愿终于得遂！
  谎言已经破产了，我们的母亲啊，万岁！前人的愿望到底实现了，万岁！独立万岁！
    法国佬滚蛋了！
    弗朗其·西嘉塞古·杜尔啊！法国佬滚蛋了，
  连再见都没说一声！多么丢脸啊！
  法国佬到底离开了我们！多丢脸啊！凭真主的名起誓，
  就连再见也没说一声！法国佬真的滚蛋了，
  连再见都没来得及说一声！
    再见吧，欧洲人！
    翁·盖赛格再见吧，欧洲人！不要怨天，不要尤人！看我，就没为这个不高兴。再见吧，是各自回老家的时候
  了；老历史早该结束干净！再见吧，愿你们永不再来打扰
  我们！但愿你们从今以后懂得
  应该如何遵守
      逃避不了的
      义务和责任！
                高　飞译
