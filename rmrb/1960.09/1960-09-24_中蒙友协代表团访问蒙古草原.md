Title: Delegation from the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association Visits Mongolian Grasslands
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 23rd: The delegation from the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, headed by Cao Diqiu and deputy head Ji Yatai, visited the Mongolian grasslands today to understand Mongolia's agricultural and pastoral production.
    Prior to this, the delegation from the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association visited the wool textile factory, flour mill, brick and tile factory, as well as museums, nurseries, and other places in Ulaanbaatar, and participated in Mongolian workers' Mongolian-Chinese friendship activities.
    The delegation will visit the Mongolian grasslands for four days.

### 中蒙友协代表团访问蒙古草原

1960-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　中蒙友协代表团访问蒙古草原
    新华社乌兰巴托23日电　以团长曹荻秋、副团长吉雅泰为首的中蒙友好协会代表团今天到蒙古草原参观访问，了解蒙古的农牧业生产情况。
    在这以前，中蒙友好协会代表团曾在乌兰巴托参观了毛纺厂、面粉厂、砖瓦厂以及博物馆、托儿所等处，并且参加了蒙古工人举行的蒙中友好活动。
    代表团将在蒙古草原访问四天。
