Title: Liu Changsheng Hosts Farewell Banquet for Kozonguizi
Author:
Date: 1960-09-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 5th. Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People’s Friendship Association, on behalf of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee and the Chinese-African People’s Friendship Association, hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to Kozonguizi, Chairman of the South West African National Union.

Among those present at tonight’s banquet were Zhu Ziqi, Wu Xueqian, Yang Shuo, He Gongkai, Wu Xiaoda and others. Sudanese peace activist Ahmed Mohammed Kheir and his wife, who are in Beijing, also attended the banquet.

### 刘长胜设宴欢送科章吉济

1960-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    刘长胜设宴欢送科章吉济
    新华社5日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜代表中国亚非团结委员会和中国非洲人民友好协会，今晚设宴欢送西南非洲民族联盟主席科章吉济。
    出席今晚宴会的有朱子奇、吴学谦、杨朔、何功楷、吴晓达等人。在京的苏丹和平人士艾哈迈德·穆罕默德·凯尔和他的夫人也出席了宴会。
