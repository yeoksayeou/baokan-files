Title: Cuban Hotel Workers Union Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 19th. The Cuban Hotel Workers Union Delegation, consisting of three members, who came to China at the invitation of the Chinese Light Industry Trade Union, left Beijing by plane today to return home after visiting Beijing, Wuhan, Shanghai and other cities.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Li Zaiwen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Du Yanqing, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Light Industry Trade Union, and Vice Chairman Li Shuying.

### 古巴旅馆业工会代表团回国

1960-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴旅馆业工会代表团回国
    新华社19日讯　应中国轻工业工会邀请来我国访问的古巴旅馆业工会代表团一行三人，在访问了北京、武汉、上海等城市后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中华全国总工会书记处书记栗再温，中国轻工业工会全国委员会主席杜延庆、副主席李淑英等人。
