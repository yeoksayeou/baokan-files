Title: China-Indonesia Friendship Association Holds Reception to Welcome Chairman of the Indonesian Party

Author:

Date: 1960-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd — The China-Indonesia Friendship Association held a reception this evening to welcome Asmara Hadi, Chairman of the Indonesian Party, his wife, and Asnawi Said, Secretary-General of the Indonesian Party.

	Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, spoke at the reception. He said that China and Indonesia are two friendly countries, and there is a long-standing and profound friendship between the two peoples. Now both of our countries are building our own nations, we both oppose imperialism and colonialism, and we are fighting to enhance the unity of Asian and African countries and to defend Asian and world peace. Consolidating and developing the friendship between our two countries is in line with the wishes and fundamental interests of our two peoples. He believes that Mr. Hadi and others' visit to China will surely enhance mutual understanding and promote friendship between the two countries.

	Asmara Hadi, Chairman of the Indonesian Party, also spoke at the reception. He said that the friendship between China and Indonesia began very early. After the two countries achieved independence, this friendship has been further developed. The Indonesia-China Friendship Association recently held a meeting and decided to strengthen the friendly relations between Indonesia and China. He said that both of our countries have suffered from imperialist oppression. Indonesia is facing the task of liberating West Irian, and China's territory of Taiwan must also be liberated. Therefore, a common struggle unites us together. Let us unite and develop the friendship between our two countries. He proposed a toast to strengthen the friendship between the two countries.

	Those present at the reception included: Da Punsheng and Jian Bozan, directors of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Sun Xiaocun, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and cultural figures Zhao Feng, Lin Lin, and Wang Yafan.

### 中国印度尼西亚友协举行酒会  欢迎印度尼西亚党总主席

1960-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

　  中国印度尼西亚友协举行酒会
    欢迎印度尼西亚党总主席
    新华社22日讯　中国印度尼西亚友好协会今晚举行酒会，欢迎印度尼西亚党总主席阿斯马腊·哈迪和他的夫人以及印度尼西亚党秘书长阿斯纳威·赛义德。
    中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之在酒会上讲话。他说，中国和印度尼西亚是两个友好的国家，两国人民之间长期存在着深厚的友谊。现在我们两国都在建设自己的国家，我们都反对帝国主义和殖民主义，为增进亚非各国的团结和保卫亚洲和世界和平而斗争。巩固和发展我们两国之间的友谊，是符合两国人民的愿望和根本利益的。他相信，哈迪先生等这次来中国访问，一定会增进两国的相互了解和促进两国的友谊。
    印度尼西亚党总主席阿斯马腊·哈迪也在酒会上讲话。他说，中国和印度尼西亚之间的友谊很早就开始。在两国取得独立以后，这种友谊得到了进一步的发展。印度尼西亚—中国友好协会最近开会，决定加强印度尼西亚同中国的友好关系。他说，我们两国都遭受着帝国主义的压迫，印度尼西亚面临着解放西伊里安的任务，中国的领土台湾也必须解放。因此，共同的斗争把我们团结在一起，让我们团结起来发展我们两国的友谊。他建议为加强两国的友谊干杯。
    出席酒会的有：中国印度尼西亚友好协会理事达浦生、翦伯赞，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林，政协全国委员会副秘书长孙晓村，全国妇联主席团委员曹孟君，文化界人士赵沨、林林、王亚凡等。
