Title: Australian Peace Representatives Leave Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 11th: The Australian Peace Delegation, including Australian Member of Parliament Thomas Uren and six others, who visited China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, concluded their visit to China and left Guangzhou for home today.

The Australian guests visited Beijing, Luoyang, Zhengzhou, Sanmenxia, Wuhan, Shanghai, and other places. They arrived in Guangzhou from Shanghai on the 10th.

### 澳大利亚和平代表离广州回国

1960-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚和平代表离广州回国
    新华社广州11日电　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中华全国总工会邀请，来我国访问的澳大利亚和平代表团部分团员、澳大利亚国会议员汤姆斯·尤伦等一行七人，结束了在我国的访问，今天离广州回国。
    澳大利亚客人们访问了北京、洛阳、郑州、三门峡、武汉、上海等地后，于10日从上海到达广州。
