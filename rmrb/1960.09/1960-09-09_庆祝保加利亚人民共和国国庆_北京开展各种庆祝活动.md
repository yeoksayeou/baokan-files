Title: Celebrating the National Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Beijing Carries Out Various Celebration Activities
Author:
Date: 1960-09-09
Page: 4

    Celebrating the National Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria
    Beijing Carries Out Various Celebration Activities
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th September. September 9th is the National Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria—the 16th anniversary of the socialist revolution. Tonight, the Chinese Foreign Cultural Committee and the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association jointly held a film reception, and the faculty and staff of the Institute of International Relations held a gathering to celebrate the glorious festival of the Bulgarian people.
    The Bulgarian feature film "The First Lesson" was shown at the film reception. Among those present at the reception were Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Committee, Liu Ruilong, President of the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association, Xu Ming, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, directors of the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association, and people from various fields. Panchevski, the Bulgarian Ambassador to China, and diplomatic officials from the embassy attended the reception.
    At the gathering held at the Institute of International Relations, Chen Xinren, Secretary of the Party Committee and President of the Institute of International Relations, and Stoyev, First Secretary of the Bulgarian Embassy in China, both spoke. In his speech, Chen Xinren extended warm congratulations to the Bulgarian people on behalf of the faculty and students of the entire institute. Stoyev described the tremendous achievements made by the People's Republic of Bulgaria in socialist construction over the past sixteen years. In their speeches, Chen Xinren and Stoyev both expressed their determination to work hard to strengthen and consolidate the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and Bulgaria. After the celebration, the Bulgarian film "On the Horizon" was shown.

### 庆祝保加利亚人民共和国国庆  北京开展各种庆祝活动

1960-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝保加利亚人民共和国国庆
    北京开展各种庆祝活动
    新华社8日讯　9月9日是保加利亚人民共和国国庆—社会主义革命十六周年。今晚，我国对外文委和中保友好协会联合举行电影招待会，国际关系学院师生员工举行集会，庆祝保加利亚人民的光荣节日。
    在电影招待会上放映了保加利亚故事片“第一课”。出席招待会的有对外文委副主任屈武，中保友好协会会长刘瑞龙，外交部苏联东欧司副司长徐明，中保友好协会的理事和各方面的人士。保加利亚驻中国大使潘切夫斯基和大使馆外交官员出席了招待会。
    在国际关系学院举行的集会上，中共国际关系学院委员会书记兼院长陈辛仁和保加利亚驻华大使馆一等秘书斯托耶夫都讲了话。陈辛仁在讲话中代表全院师生向保加利亚人民热烈祝贺节日。斯托耶夫讲述了保加利亚人民共和国十六年来在社会主义建设中所取得的巨大成就。陈辛仁和斯托耶夫在讲话中都表示要努力加强和巩固中保两国人民牢不可破的友谊。庆祝会结束后，放映了保加利亚电影“在水平线上”。
