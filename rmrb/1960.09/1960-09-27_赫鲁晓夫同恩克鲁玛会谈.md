Title: Khrushchev Holds Talks with Nkrumah
Author:
Date: 1960-09-27
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 26th: According to TASS, Ghanaian President Nkrumah and other members of the Ghanaian delegation attending the United Nations General Assembly met with Khrushchev at Glen Cove on the 25th.

　　Khrushchev and Nkrumah held talks. The Soviet side participating in the talks included Foreign Minister Gromyko, Soviet delegation secretary to the United Nations General Assembly, Moljakov, and delegation advisor Adzhubei. The Ghanaian side participating in the talks included Foreign Minister Adjei, Minister of Transport Edusei, Minister of Information and Broadcasting Boateng, Ghanaian Ambassador to Moscow Elliot, and Ghanaian Permanent Representative to the United Nations, Quaison-Sackey. The talks lasted nearly two hours.

### 赫鲁晓夫同恩克鲁玛会谈

1960-09-27
第5版()
专栏：

　　赫鲁晓夫同恩克鲁玛会谈
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社纽约25日讯：加纳总统恩克鲁玛和出席联合国大会的加纳代表团其他团员25日到格兰考夫同赫鲁晓夫会晤。
    赫鲁晓夫和恩克鲁玛举行了会谈。苏联方面参加会谈的有：外交部长葛罗米柯，苏联出席联合国大会代表团秘书莫利亚科夫和代表团顾问阿朱别伊。加纳方面参加会谈的有：外交部长阿杰伊，交通部长埃杜塞，新闻和广播部长博阿坦，加纳驻莫斯科大使埃利奥特和加纳驻联合国常任代表奎森—萨基。会谈继续了将近二小时。
