Title: Romanian Grand National Assembly Holds Special Session, Appoints Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej as Head of Delegation to UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, September 1st. The Romanian Grand National Assembly held a special session from August 30th to 31st. The meeting heard the report by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, on the international situation and Romania's foreign policy.

The meeting discussed Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej's report, with eighteen deputies speaking. The meeting unanimously passed a resolution approving the report.

The meeting also unanimously passed a resolution, based on the proposals of the Presidium of the Romanian Grand National Assembly and the government, appointing Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej as the head of the delegation of the Romanian People's Republic to the 15th session of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 罗大国民议会开特别会议  任命乔治乌—德治为出席联大代表团团长

1960-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    罗大国民议会开特别会议
    任命乔治乌—德治为出席联大代表团团长
    新华社布加勒斯特1日电　罗马尼亚大国民议会在8月30日到31日举行了特别会议。会议听取了罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记乔治乌—德治关于国际形势和罗马尼亚外交政策的报告。
    会议对乔治乌—德治的报告进行了讨论，有十八个议员发了言。会议一致通过了批准这个报告的决议。
    会议还根据罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团和政府的建议，一致通过决议，任命乔治乌—德治为罗马尼亚人民共和国出席联合国大会第十五届会议代表团团长。
