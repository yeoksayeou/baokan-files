Title: Death of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th – Here today at 13:00 (Berlin time), the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Government of the German Democratic Republic, the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, and the Presidium of the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany jointly announced the death of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic and member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party, at 8:55 today in Berlin.

    A funeral committee for Comrade Wilhelm Pieck has been formed. The committee includes the following party and government members: Chairman: Ulbricht; Members: Grotewohl, Dr. Dieckmann, Professor Correns, August Bach, Max Sefrin, Ernst Goldenbaum, Dr. Lothar Bolz, Herbert Warnke, Ilse Thiele, Horst Schumann, Friedrich Wehmer; Secretary: Otto Schön.

    It has been ordered that a period of national mourning will be observed from today until the day of the funeral. During this period, public entertainment will be suspended.

    The people of the German Democratic Republic express their deep condolences on the death of the President. Working people throughout the capital are mourning the great cause he accomplished. Flags are flying at half-mast everywhere to express sorrow after the announcement was released.

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th – According to the German News Agency, President Wilhelm Pieck passed away at 8:55 this morning (Berlin time). He suffered a sudden heart weakness on September 4th, followed by pneumonia. Although his temperature had dropped, it was impossible to reverse the weakness of his heart. Due to further weakening of his heartbeat and blood circulation, he finally passed away.

    The bulletin about his death was signed by the four doctors who had been treating him.
    First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Ulbricht, was the first to pay his respects to the deceased.

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 6th – According to the German News Agency, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Ulbricht, and Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl, visited President Pieck, who was seriously ill.

    Ulbricht and Grotewohl conveyed the concern of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party, the Government of the Republic, and all the people for President Pieck's health. (Attached picture)

### 德意志民主共和国总统  威廉·皮克同志逝世

1960-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国总统
    威廉·皮克同志逝世
    新华社柏林7日电　这里在今天十三时（柏林时间）发表了德国统一社会党中央委员会、德意志民主共和国政府、德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团和民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席团签署的关于德意志民主共和国总统、统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员威廉·皮克同志于今日八时五十五分在柏林逝世的公告。
    威廉·皮克同志治丧委员会已经组成。委员会包括下列党和政府的成员：主席：乌布利希，委员：格罗提渥、狄克曼博士、柯伦斯教授、奥古斯特·巴赫、马克斯·苏尔比尔、恩斯特·果登包姆、洛塔·博尔茨博士、赫伯特·瓦恩克、伊尔泽·蒂勒、豪斯特·舒曼、弗里德里希·韦默，秘书：奥托·舒恩。
    已经通令从今天起到安葬之日止为国丧期。在这个期间内，公众娱乐暂停。
    德意志民主共和国人民对总统的逝世表示深切的哀悼。首都各处的劳动人民都在缅怀他所完成的伟大事业。公告发表后，各地都悬半旗致哀。
    新华社柏林7日电　据德意志通讯社报道，威廉·皮克总统在今天上午八点五十五分（柏林时间）逝世。他在9月4日患突发性心脏衰弱症，继而肺炎并发。虽然他的体温已下降，但是已不可能再挽回他心脏的衰弱。由于心脏跳动和血液循环进一步变弱，他终于与世长辞。
    关于他逝世的公报是由原先为他治疗的四位医生签署的。
  德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记、德意志民主共和国副总理乌布利希首先前往瞻仰遗容。
    新华社柏林6日电　据德意志通讯社报道，德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希、德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥先后探视了身患重病的皮克总统。
    乌布利希和格罗提渥转达了统一社会党中央、共和国政府和全体人民对皮克总统健康情况的关怀。（附图片）
