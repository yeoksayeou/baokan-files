Title: Prime Minister Abbas Departs for China at the Invitation of Premier Zhou to Attend China's National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 27th: Algerian Republic Provisional Government Prime Minister Abbas, at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai, left here today with a delegation, via Moscow, to go to Beijing to attend the 11th anniversary celebrations of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    The delegation members include Algerian Minister of Interior, Ahder Ben Tobal, and four other senior officials.
    Chen Jiakang, the Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, and other embassy staff went to the airport to see them off.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 27th: Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic, Abbas Ferhat, and his entourage, who were invited to visit China and participate in the 11th anniversary celebrations of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Moscow by plane on the afternoon of the 27th.
    Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, greeted them at the airport.
    Also greeting them at the airport were Soviet First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Kosygin; Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Chairman of the State Planning Committee, Novikov; Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Semenov, and others.
    Many diplomatic envoys of Asian, African, and socialist countries to the Soviet Union also went to the airport to greet them.
    Prime Minister Abbas is scheduled to leave Moscow for Beijing on the 28th.

### 应周总理邀请前来参加我国国庆庆典　阿巴斯总理启程来华

1960-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　  应周总理邀请前来参加我国国庆庆典
　　阿巴斯总理启程来华
    新华社开罗27日电　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯应周恩来总理的邀请，今天率领代表团离开这里，取道莫斯科，前往北京参加中华人民共和国建国十一周年庆典。
    代表团团员有阿尔及利亚内政部长阿赫达尔·本·陶巴勒和其他四位高级官员。
    前往机场欢送的有中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康和使馆其他人员。
    新华社莫斯科27日电　应邀前往中国访问，并参加中华人民共和国成立十一周年庆典的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯·费尔哈特和他的随行人员27日下午乘飞机抵达莫斯科。
    我国驻苏联大使刘晓到机场迎接。
    到机场迎接的还有苏联部长会议第一副主席柯西金，部长会议副主席、国家计划委员会主席诺维科夫，外交部副部长谢苗诺夫等人。
    许多亚非国家和社会主义国家驻苏联外交使节也到机场迎接。
    阿巴斯总理定28日离开莫斯科赴北京。
