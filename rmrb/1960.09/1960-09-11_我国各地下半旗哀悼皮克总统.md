Title: Half-Mast Flags Flown Across China to Mourn President Pieck

Author:

Date: 1960-09-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Today is the day of the funeral of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic. Organs, troops, factories, mines, enterprises, schools, people's organizations, and Chinese ships berthed in ports in all provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government, and in cities under the jurisdiction of provinces, flew flags at half-mast for one day to mourn the death of President Pieck.

Due to the arrival of President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea in Beijing today for a friendly visit to our country, the organs, troops, factories, mines, enterprises, schools, and people's organizations in the capital raised the national flag before 4 p.m. to express a warm welcome. After 4 p.m., the aforementioned units in Beijing flew flags at half-mast to express deep condolences for the unfortunate passing of President Pieck, the most outstanding son of the German working class and working people, and the closest friend of the Chinese people.

### 我国各地下半旗哀悼皮克总统

1960-09-11
第5版()
专栏：

    我国各地下半旗哀悼皮克总统
    新华社10日讯　今天是德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克同志殡葬的日子，全国各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会和各省辖市人民委员会所在地的机关、部队、厂矿、企业、学校、人民团体和停泊在港口内的我国轮船，都下半旗一日，哀悼皮克总统的逝世。
    由于几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔今天到达北京开始对我国进行友好访问，首都的机关、部队、厂矿、企业、学校、人民团体在下午四时前悬挂国旗表示热烈欢迎，下午四时后，北京的上述单位都悬挂半旗，对德国工人阶级和劳动人民的最优秀的儿子、中国人民最亲密的朋友皮克总统的不幸逝世表示沉痛哀悼。
