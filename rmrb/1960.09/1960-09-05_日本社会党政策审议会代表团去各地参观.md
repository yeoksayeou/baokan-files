Title: Japanese Socialist Party Policy Deliberation Council Delegation Visits Various Places
Author:
Date: 1960-09-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Japanese Socialist Party Policy Deliberation Council Delegation of ten people concluded their visit to Beijing and took a train south today to continue their visits before returning home.
    Xie Nanguang, Xiao Xiangqian, Wang Xiaoyun and others were at the station to see them off.

### 日本社会党政策审议会代表团去各地参观

1960-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    日本社会党政策审议会代表团去各地参观
    新华社3日讯　日本社会党政策审议会代表团一行十人结束了在北京的访问，今天乘火车南下继续参观然后回国。
    到车站欢送的有谢南光、萧向前、王晓云等人。
