Title: German Print Exhibition Expert Werner Schade Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Werner Schade, an expert who came to China according to the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-German cultural cooperation agreement to hold a German Democratic Republic print exhibition, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 18th.

### 德版画展览专家维尔纳·夏德到京

1960-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    德版画展览专家维尔纳·夏德到京
    新华社19日讯　根据中德文化合作协定1960年执行计划前来我国举办德意志民主共和国版画展览的专家维尔纳·夏德，已经在18日乘飞机到达北京。
