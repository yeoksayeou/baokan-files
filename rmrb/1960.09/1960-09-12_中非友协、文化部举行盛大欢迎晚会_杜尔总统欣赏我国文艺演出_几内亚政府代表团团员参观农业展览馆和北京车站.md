Title: Sino-African Friendship Association and Ministry of Culture Hold Grand Welcome Reception; President Touré Enjoys Chinese Artistic Performance; Members of Guinean Government Delegation Visit Agricultural Exhibition Hall and Beijing Railway Station
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th September: The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the Ministry of Culture of the People’s Republic of China held a grand soiree this evening at the Great Hall of the People to welcome the dear friend of the Chinese people, President Sékou Touré, and the Guinean government delegation.
	President Sékou Touré, Speaker Diallo Saifoulaye, and other distinguished Guinean guests, accompanied by Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premiers Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Bo Yibo, Tan Zhenlin, and Luo Ruiqing, attended the soiree.
	At the soiree, artists from the capital performed many wonderful programs. These included "Please Stay, Guests from Afar," the Guinean songs "Ode to the President," and "Long Live People's Friendship," as well as Guinean folk dances, Chinese acrobatics "Vehicle Skills," and the Peking Opera "Jingwei Filling the Sea with Stones," each of which was warmly welcomed.
	After the performance, President Sékou Touré and Speaker Diallo Saifoulaye and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and others, walked onto the stage and warmly shook hands with the performers, thanking them for their wonderful performance.
	Also attending the soiree were: Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Burhan; President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Africa, Liu Changsheng; Vice Ministers of Culture, Qian Junrui and Xia Yan; Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Liao Chengzhi; Vice Chairman, Liu Ningyi; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Hanfu and Luo Guibo; Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Zhixiang; Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, Ke Hua; as well as people from all walks of life in the capital.
	African friends visiting our country, and ambassadors and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend tonight's soiree.
	This morning, members of the Guinean government delegation: Guinean Minister of National Economy, Louis Lansana Béavogui, and Minister of Planning, Keita N Famara, visited the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall and Beijing Railway Station.

### 中非友协、文化部举行盛大欢迎晚会  杜尔总统欣赏我国文艺演出  几内亚政府代表团团员参观农业展览馆和北京车站

1960-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中非友协、文化部举行盛大欢迎晚会
    杜尔总统欣赏我国文艺演出
    几内亚政府代表团团员参观农业展览馆和北京车站
    新华社11日讯　为欢迎中国人民亲密的朋友塞古·杜尔总统和几内亚共和国政府代表团，中国非洲人民友好协会、中华人民共和国文化部今晚在人民大会堂举行盛大的文艺晚会。
    塞古·杜尔总统、迪阿洛·塞福拉耶议长和几内亚的其他贵宾们，由董必武副主席，朱德委员长，周恩来总理，陈毅、李先念、薄一波、谭震林、罗瑞卿副总理陪同，出席了文艺晚会。
    在晚会上，首都的文艺工作者演出了许多精采的节目。其中有“远方的客人请你留下来”，几内亚歌曲“总统颂”、“人民友谊万岁”和几内亚民间舞，中国杂技“车技”和京剧“衔石填海”等，每一个节目都受到了热烈的欢迎。
    演出结束后，塞古·杜尔总统和迪阿洛·塞福拉耶议长等贵宾由董必武副主席，朱德委员长，周恩来总理等陪同走上舞台，同演员们亲切地握手，感谢他们精采的演出。
    出席晚会的还有：政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜，文化部副部长钱俊瑞、夏衍，中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志、副主席刘宁一，外交部副部长章汉夫、罗贵波，对外文化联络委员会代主任张致祥，中国驻几内亚大使柯华，以及首都各方面人士。
    在我国访问的非洲朋友，各国驻中国的使节和外交官员也应邀出席了今晚的晚会。
    今天上午，几内亚共和国政府代表团团员：几内亚国民经济部长路易·兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉和计划部长凯塔·恩法马拉等参观了全国农业展览馆和北京车站。
