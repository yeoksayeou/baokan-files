Title: Joint Sino-Burmese Boundary Committee Issues Communiqué of Third Meeting; Satisfactorily Resolves Issues Raised by Joint Survey Team
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 5th: The Joint Sino-Burmese Boundary Committee today issued here a communiqué regarding its third meeting. The full text is as follows:

The Joint Sino-Burmese Boundary Committee held its third meeting in Rangoon from August 25 to September 4, 1960.

Attending the meeting were Mr. Yao Zhongming, Chief Representative of China, Brigadier Aung Gyi, Chief Representative of Burma, as well as representatives and advisors from both sides.

During its stay in Rangoon, the Chinese delegation paid calls on President U Win Maung, Prime Minister U Nu, General Ne Win, Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio, and others. Thanks to the gracious arrangements of the Burmese government, the Chinese delegation also visited Bassein, where they were warmly welcomed by local military and administrative authorities and people from all walks of life.

The Joint Committee reviewed the work of the Joint Survey Team and expressed satisfaction with the rapid progress of the surveying work, the close cooperation between the surveying personnel of both sides, and the timely completion of the tasks entrusted to them. The Joint Committee therefore commended the surveyors for their spirit of hard work and perseverance in overcoming difficulties. The Joint Committee also, in the spirit of mutual understanding and mutual accommodation, studied and satisfactorily resolved the issues raised by the Joint Survey Team through consultation.

In order to ensure the timely completion of all preparations for the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty in Beijing in October this year, the two sides further exchanged views on the draft treaty.

Based on the principles of mutual respect for territorial sovereignty and the desire to avoid possible disputes between the border residents of both sides, the Joint Committee reached an agreement on the principles for handling the issue of cross-border cultivation by border residents of both sides.

The Joint Committee also discussed other relevant issues.

Both sides expressed extreme satisfaction with the fact that all the issues scheduled for discussion at this meeting, as stipulated in the second meeting, had been carefully studied and resolved in an atmosphere of friendly cooperation.

The two sides decided that the fourth meeting of the Joint Committee would be held in Beijing on September 19.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 4th: The final session of the third meeting of the Joint Sino-Burmese Boundary Committee was held here today. The chief representatives, representatives and advisors of both sides attended the meeting. At the meeting, the Joint Committee reviewed the work of the treaty drafting group in drafting the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty, agreed on the agreement on the principles for resolving the issue of cross-border cultivation of land by border residents of both China and Burma, and agreed on the recommendations and solutions to the issues raised by the Joint Survey Team, and decided on the date and agenda for the fourth meeting of the Joint Sino-Burmese Boundary Committee.

### 中缅边界联合委员会发表第三次会议公报  圆满解决联合勘察队提出的问题

1960-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅边界联合委员会发表第三次会议公报
    圆满解决联合勘察队提出的问题
    双方就中缅边界条约草案进一步交换了意见并就边民过境耕地问题的处理原则达成协议
    新华社仰光5日电　中缅边界联合委员会今天在这里发表了关于第三次会议的公报，全文如下：
    中缅边界联合委员会从1960年8月25日到9月4日在仰光举行了第三次会议。
    出席会议的有中国首席代表姚仲明先生、缅甸首席代表昂季准将以及双方代表和顾问。
    中国代表团在仰光逗留期间，拜会了吴温貌总统、吴努总理、奈温将军、外交部长藻昆卓以及其他人。由于缅甸政府的盛情安排，中国代表团还访问了勃生市，他们在那里受到当地军政当局和各界人士的热烈欢迎。
    联合委员会检查了联合勘察队的工作，对勘察工作的迅速进展、双方勘察人员之间的密切合作和如期完成所交托的任务感到满意。因此联合委员会对勘察人员吃苦耐劳克服困难的精神表示赞扬。联合委员会还根据互谅、互让的精神，通过协商，研究和圆满地解决了联合勘察队提出的问题。
    为了保证如期完成今年10月在北京签订中缅边界条约的全部准备工作，双方就条约草案进一步交换了意见。
    根据互相尊重领土主权的原则和避免双方边民之间可能发生的纠纷的愿望，联合委员会就双方边民过境耕地问题的处理原则达成了协议。
    联合委员会还就其他有关问题进行了讨论。
    双方对第二次会议规定这次会议讨论的一切问题得到认真的研究并且在友好合作的气氛中获得解决这一事实极为满意。
    双方确定，联合委员会第四次会议于9月19日在北京举行。
    新华社仰光4日电　中缅边界联合委员会第三次会议的最后一次会谈今天在这里举行。出席这次会谈的有双方的首席代表、代表和顾问们。在会上，联合委员会检查了条约起草小组草拟中缅边界条约的工作，同意关于解决中国和缅甸双方边界居民过境耕种土地问题的原则的协议，以及同意对联合勘察队提出的问题的建议和解决方法，并且决定举行中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议的日期和议程。
