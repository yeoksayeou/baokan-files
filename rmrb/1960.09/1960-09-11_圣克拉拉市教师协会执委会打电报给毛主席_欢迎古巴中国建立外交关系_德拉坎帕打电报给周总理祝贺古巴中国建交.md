Title: Santa Clara City Teachers Association Executive Committee Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao, Welcoming the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between Cuba and China; De la Campa Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou Congratulating the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between Cuba and China
Author:
Date: 1960-09-11
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, September 10th: The Executive Committee of the Santa Clara City Teachers Association of Cuba sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong on September 3rd, welcoming Cuba's decision to establish diplomatic relations with China. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
To Chairman Mao Zedong,

    The Santa Clara City Teachers Association, representing the Cuban teaching community, welcomes the decision made by the Cuban people and government to establish diplomatic relations with People's China.
                      The Executive Committee
    Santa Clara, September 3, 1960
Xinhua News Agency, September 10th: Odon Alvarez de la Campa, Secretary of Foreign Relations of the Cuban Workers' Confederation, sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai on September 7th, congratulating Cuba on its decision to establish diplomatic relations with China. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
To Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China:

    The Cuban Workers' Confederation, representing Cuban workers and the Cuban people, is overjoyed by the decision in the Havana Declaration announced by the Cuban National Congress regarding the establishment of diplomatic relations with the heroic People's Republic of China. We, the Cuban workers and people, thank the Chinese government and people for their strong support. Please accept our fraternal congratulations.
       Odon Alvarez de la Campa, Secretary of Foreign Relations, Cuban Workers' Confederation
        Havana, September 7, 1960

### 圣克拉拉市教师协会执委会打电报给毛主席  欢迎古巴中国建立外交关系  德拉坎帕打电报给周总理祝贺古巴中国建交

1960-09-11
第2版()
专栏：

    圣克拉拉市教师协会执委会打电报给毛主席
    欢迎古巴中国建立外交关系
    德拉坎帕打电报给周总理祝贺古巴中国建交
    新华社10日讯　古巴圣克拉拉市教师协会执行委员会在9月3日打电报给毛泽东主席，表示欢迎古巴关于同中国建立外交关系的决定。电报全文如下：毛泽东主席
    古巴圣克拉拉市教师协会代表古巴教师界，欢迎古巴人民和政府作出的关于同人民中国建立外交关系的决定。
                      执行委员会
    1960年9月3日于圣克拉拉
    新华社10日讯　古巴工人联合会对外关系书记奥东·阿耳瓦雷斯·德拉坎帕在9月7日打电报给周恩来总理，就古巴关于同中国建立外交关系的决定表示祝贺。电报全文如下：中华人民共和国周恩来总理：
    古巴工人联合会代表古巴劳动者和人民，对古巴人民全国大会宣布的哈瓦那宣言中关于同英雄的中华人民共和国建立外交关系的决定表示欢欣鼓舞。我们古巴劳动者和人民感谢中国政府和人民给予的坚强支持。请接受我们兄弟般的祝贺。
       古巴工人联合会对外关系书记　奥东·阿耳瓦雷斯·德拉坎帕
        1960年9月7日于哈瓦那
