Title: Korean Film Delegation Leaves Shenyang for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-09-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, September 2nd: The five-member film delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Kim Han-gyu, which came to our country to participate in the Korean Film Week, left Shenyang by train today to return home after visiting Shenyang, Anshan, and other places. Among those at the station to see them off were leading members of the cultural departments of Liaoning Province and Shenyang City, as well as people from film circles in Shenyang.

Before the delegation left Shenyang, Li Huang, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Party Committee and President of the Liaoning Provincial China-Korea Friendship Association, and other responsible comrades from the provincial and municipal Party and government organizations, received them and held a farewell banquet for them.

### 朝鲜电影代表团离沈阳回国

1960-09-04
第3版()
专栏：

    朝鲜电影代表团离沈阳回国
  新华社沈阳2日电　应邀前来我国参加朝鲜电影周的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国电影代表团一行五人，在团长金汉奎率领下访问了沈阳、鞍山等地以后，今天乘火车离开沈阳回国。到车站欢送的有辽宁省和沈阳市文化部门的负责人以及沈阳市电影界人士。
    代表团离开沈阳前，中共辽宁省委书记处书记、辽宁省中朝友好协会会长李荒等省、市党和政府负责人接见了他们，并且为他们举行了送别宴会。
