Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Iwai Akira
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai received Iwai Akira, Secretary-General of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), and his secretary, Uezuma Yoshiaki, this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Li Jiebo, Chen Yu, Yang Zheng, and others.

Also present was Japanese peace advocate Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing. (Attached photo)

　　Premier Zhou Enlai in a cordial conversation with Iwai Akira (center). On the right is Uezuma Yoshiaki.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

### 周恩来总理接见岩井章

1960-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理接见岩井章
    新华社24日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了日本工会总评议会事务局局长岩井章和随他同来的秘书上妻美章，并且进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有廖承志、李颉伯、陈宇、杨正等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。（附图片）
　　周恩来总理和岩井章（中）亲切谈话。右为上妻美章。
