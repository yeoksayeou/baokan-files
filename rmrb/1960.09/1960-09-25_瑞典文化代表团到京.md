Title: Swedish Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency. A four-member Swedish cultural delegation, led by Head of Delegation Mr. Hellmanson, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 21st at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Among those welcoming them at the airport were Pan Fei, Deputy Director of the International Department of the "People's Daily," and Zhang Suwen, representative of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 瑞典文化代表团到京

1960-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　  瑞典文化代表团到京
    新华社讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的瑞典文化代表团一行四人，在团长海尔曼逊率领下，21日乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有“人民日报”国际部副主任潘非、中国人民对外文化协会代表张苏文等。
