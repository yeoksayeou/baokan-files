Title: The Climate of Africa
Author: Niè niè kè hā lǐ
Date: 1960-09-15
Page: 8

    The Climate of Africa	〔Guinea〕Nié né Khali
    Oh, Sun!——
    In the African sky, how abundant is the sunlight,
    Even the earth, in receiving it, feels weary.
    I want to escape your fiery power
    So I stroll on the fresh farmland,
    But my nostrils can never catch their breath
    Your heat marks the boiling point of a new life.
    Oh, Rain!——
    On the African land, how much rain there is!
    The land, storing the rain in its belly, also feels weary.
    I drink and drink, endlessly drinking the water you shed,
    I lift my head, facing the sky,
    I open my mouth to drink, my heart also drinks,
    And my thirst is already quenched, yet you keep falling without end.
    Oh, Wind!——
    There is too much wind in Africa! When it
    Blows along the earth's chest,
    Even the earth grows weary of watching it.
    I want to spread a thousand light wings,
    In order to fly all over the world.
    Sirocco and Bassat (note),
    For God's sake, stop!
    From now on, I will open a new era of combat.
                              (Translated by Zhang Xian)
    [Note] Sirocco and Bassat are the names of two tropical winds.

### 非洲的气候
聂湼·克哈里
1960-09-15
第8版()
专栏：

    非洲的气候	〔几内亚〕聂湼·克哈里
    太阳啊！——
    在非洲的天空中，阳光多么多啊，
    连大地接受它，都感到疲乏了。
    我想把你的炎威躲开
    于是我在清新的田垅上散步，
    而鼻孔总是透不过气来
    你的炎热标志着——新生活的沸腾。
    雨啊！——
    在非洲的土地上，雨这么多啊！
    土地把雨水装在肚中，也感到疲乏了。
    我喝呀，喝呀，不停地喝你流下的水，
    我仰起头来，面向天空，
    我张开嘴喝，我的心也在喝，
    而我的口渴已经止了，你还是落个不停。
    风啊！——
    风在非洲太多了！当它
    沿着大地的胸膛刮起的时候，
    连大地望它也望疲乏了。
    我要张开千百只轻轻的翅膀，
    为了飞遍全世界。
    西罗科和巴萨特（注）啊，
    看上帝的情面，你们停下吧！
    从今以后，我要开辟战斗的新纪元。
                              （张弦译）
    〔注〕西罗科和巴萨特是两种热带风的名字。
