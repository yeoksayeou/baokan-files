Title: China-Mongolia Friendship Association Elects Cao Diqiu as Vice President
Author:
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - The Council of the China-Mongolia Friendship Association today elected Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Mayor of Shanghai, as Vice President of the China-Mongolia Friendship Association.

### 中蒙友协增选曹荻秋为副会长

1960-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    中蒙友协增选曹荻秋为副会长
    新华社12日讯　中国蒙古友好协会理事会今天增选中国共产党上海市委员会书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋为中蒙友好协会副会长。
