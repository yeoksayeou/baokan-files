Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts Banquet for Overseas Chinese Visiting Group and Hong Kong and Macao Delegation; Vice Premier He Long Extends Warm Welcome on Behalf of the Party and the State to Compatriots from Overseas
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: This noon, Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, held a banquet in the banquet hall of the Great Hall of the People to warmly welcome over 300 members of the 1960 National Day Overseas Chinese Visiting Group and the Hong Kong and Macao Delegations.
　　Vice Premier of the State Council He Long attended the banquet. He extended a warm welcome on behalf of the Party and the State to Overseas Chinese from all over the world and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao.
　　The members of the Overseas Chinese Visiting Group came from Asia, Africa, Europe, America, and Australia, residing in dozens of countries and regions such as Burma, Cambodia, and Indonesia. When the invited Overseas Chinese and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao arrived in front of the solemn and beautiful banquet hall of the Great Hall of the People, Director Liao Chengzhi, and Vice Directors Fang Fang, Zhuang Xiquan, Huang Changshui, and Zhuang Mingli warmly welcomed them at the door, shaking hands and exchanging greetings with each one.
　　During the banquet, Liao Chengzhi, on behalf of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, extended a warm welcome to the Overseas Chinese and compatriots. Liao Chengzhi raised his glass to wish all Overseas Chinese and compatriots in Hong Kong and Macao good health, family happiness, and success in their endeavors, and to toast the great patriotic unity of Overseas Chinese.
　　Invited to attend today's banquet as guests were Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture; Dong Chuncai, Vice Minister of Education; Sun Daguang, Vice Minister of Communications; Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce; Chen Qiyuan and You Yangzu, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese; Chen Qiyou, Chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party; as well as members of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission in Beijing and well-known returned Overseas Chinese.

### 廖承志欢宴华侨观光团和港澳观礼团　贺龙副总理代表党和国家热烈欢迎来自海外同胞

1960-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　廖承志欢宴华侨观光团和港澳观礼团
　　贺龙副总理代表党和国家热烈欢迎来自海外同胞
    新华社29日讯　今天中午，华侨事务委员会主任廖承志在人民大会堂宴会厅举行宴会，热烈欢迎1960年国庆节华侨回国观光团、港澳各界观礼团全体成员三百多人。
    国务院副总理贺龙出席了宴会。他代表党和国家向来自海外各地的华侨和港澳的同胞表示热烈欢迎。
    华侨回国观光团团员来自亚、非、欧、美、澳各洲，他们侨居在缅甸、柬埔寨、印度尼西亚等数十个国家和地区。当应邀赴宴的侨胞们和港澳同胞们来到庄严美丽的人民大会堂宴会厅前时，廖承志主任，方方、庄希泉、黄长水、庄明理副主任一齐到门前热情欢迎，和他们一一握手问好。
    席间，廖承志代表华侨事务委员会对侨胞们、同胞们表示热烈欢迎。廖承志举杯祝贺海外全体侨胞们、港澳同胞们的健康，为他们的家庭幸福、事业发展，为华侨爱国大团结干杯。
    应邀出席今天宴会作陪的，有对外贸易部副部长雷任民，文化部副部长夏衍，教育部副部长董纯才，交通部副部长孙大光，中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通，中华全国归国华侨联合会副主席陈其瑗、尤扬祖、中国致公党主席陈其尤，以及在京的华侨事务委员会委员和归侨知名人士。
