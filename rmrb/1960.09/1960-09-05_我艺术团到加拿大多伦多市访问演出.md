Title: Our Art Troupe Visits and Performs in Toronto, Canada
Author:
Date: 1960-09-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Toronto, 3rd: The Chinese Art Troupe arrived in Toronto, Canada’s industrial, commercial, and cultural center, on the morning of the 2nd, and will stay here for sixteen days for a visit and performance.

Prior to this, the Chinese Art Troupe held two performances in Winnipeg, the capital of Manitoba Province, which were warmly welcomed by local audiences and overseas Chinese, causing a sensation in Canada's fourth-largest city.

### 我艺术团到加拿大多伦多市访问演出

1960-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    我艺术团到加拿大多伦多市访问演出
    据新华社3日讯　多伦多消息：中国艺术团2日上午到达加拿大的工商业和文化中心多伦多市，将在这里逗留十六天进行访问演出。
    在这以前，中国艺术团在马尼托巴省的首府温尼伯举行了两次演出，受到当地观众和华侨极其热烈的欢迎，轰动了这个加拿大的第四大城市。
