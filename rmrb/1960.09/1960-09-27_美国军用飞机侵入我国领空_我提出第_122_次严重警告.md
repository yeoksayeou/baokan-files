Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into China's Airspace; 122nd Serious Warning Issued

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: On September 26th, from 12:00 to 12:10, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 14:29 to 14:42, another U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace south of Shanwei and Pinghai in Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 122nd serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我国领空　我提出第122次严重警告

1960-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我国领空
　　我提出第122次严重警告
    新华社26日讯　9月26日十二时至十二时十分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空。同日十四时二十九分至十四时四十二分，又有美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省汕尾、平海以南地区领空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百二十二次严重警告。
