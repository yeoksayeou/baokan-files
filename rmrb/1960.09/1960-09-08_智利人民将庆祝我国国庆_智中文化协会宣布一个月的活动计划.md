Title: Chilean People to Celebrate China's National Day; Chile-China Cultural Association Announces Month-Long Program of Activities
Author:
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, 6th. The President of the Chile-China Cultural Association, Radical Party Congressman Juan Martínez Campos, and other leaders of the Chile-China Cultural Association, announced a program of activities to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China at a press conference. These celebrations will begin on the 8th of this month with a report by Chilean agricultural experts who recently returned from a visit to China, on Chinese agricultural issues, and will conclude with a grand people's celebration at the sports club on October 8th. Other celebrations will include exhibitions of handicrafts, art photos, newspapers and magazines, film screenings, and various gatherings. The President of the University of Chile, Juan Gómez Millas, and other professors who have visited China will give reports on the situation in China at the University of Chile.

It was announced that the Chile-China Cultural Association has established branches in nine cities across the country.

### 智利人民将庆祝我国国庆  智中文化协会宣布一个月的活动计划

1960-09-08
第6版()
专栏：

    智利人民将庆祝我国国庆
    智中文化协会宣布一个月的活动计划
    新华社圣地亚哥6日电　智利中国文化协会主席、激进党众议员胡安·马丁内斯·坎普斯和智中文化协会的其他领导人在记者招待会上，公布了智中文化协会庆祝中华人民共和国建国十一周年的活动计划。这些庆祝活动将于本月8日以最近访问中国归来的智利农业专家在座谈会上作关于中国农业问题的报告开始，而以10月8日在体育俱乐部举行盛大的人民庆祝活动结束。其它庆祝活动将包括举办手工艺品、艺术图片和报刊杂志展览会，放映电影以及举行各种集会。智利大学校长胡安·戈麦斯·米利亚斯和其他访问过中国的教授将在智利大学作有关中国情况的报告。
    据宣布，智中文化协会已经在全国九个城市中设立了分会。
