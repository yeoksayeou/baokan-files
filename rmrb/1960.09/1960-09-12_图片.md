On the evening of September 11, the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association and the Ministry of Culture held a soiree to welcome President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea and the Government Delegation of the Republic of Guinea he led. The picture shows President Sékou Touré and other distinguished Guinean guests, as well as Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and others posing with the performers after the performance.
