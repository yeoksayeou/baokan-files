　　International Briefs
　　Sukarno Appoints Speaker of the Cooperation Parliament
    Jakarta Dispatch, according to the Indonesian News Agency, President Sukarno has appointed Zainul Arifin as Speaker of the Cooperation Parliament and Arudji Kartawinata as Deputy Speaker. Other Deputy Speakers will be appointed soon.
    This was announced in a communique issued by Indonesian State Secretary Tansil.
　　Federation of Malaya Elects New Head of State
    Kuala Lumpur Dispatch, Tuanku Syed Putra of Perlis, 39, was elected as the third Yang di-Pertuan Agong (Supreme Head of State) of the Federation of Malaya for a term of five years at the Conference of Rulers of the Federation of Malaya on the 20th.
    Sultan Ismail Nasiruddin Shah (54) of Trengganu was elected as Deputy Supreme Head of State, also for a term of five years.

### 国际简讯

1960-09-26
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
　  苏加诺任命合作议会议长
    雅加达讯　据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，苏加诺总统任命蔡努耳·阿里芬为合作议会议长，阿鲁季·卡塔威纳塔为副议长。其他副议长将在最近任命。
    这是由印度尼西亚国务秘书坦西尔所签发的一项公报中宣布的。
　  马来亚联合邦选出新元首
    吉隆坡讯　马来亚联合邦玻璃市三十九岁的王公赛义德·普特拉20日在马来亚联合邦各州苏丹议会上当选为马来亚联合邦第三任最高元首，任期为五年。
    丁加奴邦苏丹伊斯梅尔·纳希鲁丁·沙阿（五十四岁）当选为副最高元首，任期也是五年。
