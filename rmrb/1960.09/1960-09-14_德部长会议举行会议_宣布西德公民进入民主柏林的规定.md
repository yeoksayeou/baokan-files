Title: The German Council of Ministers Holds Meeting Announcing Regulations for West German Citizens Entering Democratic Berlin
Author:
Date: 1960-09-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 10th - The German Democratic Republic's decree regarding West German citizens entering Democratic Berlin has come into effect on the 9th. This decree supplements the November 21, 1953 decree regarding interactions between the two Germanys.

    The Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic passed this decree at a meeting held on the 8th. On the same day, the Ministry of Interior of the German Democratic Republic announced the decree.

    According to this decree, citizens of West Germany must present a permit at designated checkpoints when entering the capital of the German Democratic Republic (Democratic Berlin). The decree stipulates: "Citizens and institutions of the German Democratic Republic residing in Berlin, or citizens of the German Federal Republic themselves, may apply for a permit to enter the capital of the German Democratic Republic (Democratic Berlin)."

### 德部长会议举行会议  宣布西德公民进入民主柏林的规定

1960-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    德部长会议举行会议
    宣布西德公民进入民主柏林的规定
    新华社柏林10日电　德意志民主共和国关于西德公民进入民主柏林的法令已从9日起生效。这个法令补充了1953年11月21日的关于两个德国之间往来的法令。
    德意志民主共和国部长会议在8日举行的会议上已通过了这项法令。同日，德意志民主共和国内政部公布了这个法令。
    根据这个法令，西德的公民在进入德意志民主共和国首都（民主柏林）时，必须在指定的监督站出示许可证。法令规定：“居住在柏林的德意志民主共和国公民和机构或者德意志联邦共和国的公民本人可以申请进入德意志民主共和国首都（民主柏林）的许可证。”
