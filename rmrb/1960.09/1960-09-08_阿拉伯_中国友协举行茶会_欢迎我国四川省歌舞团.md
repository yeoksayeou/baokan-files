Title: Arab-China Friendship Association Holds Tea Party to Welcome China's Sichuan Provincial Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 6th -- The Arab-China Friendship Association held a tea party tonight to welcome the Chinese Sichuan Provincial Song and Dance Troupe, headed by Zhang Tiesheng. The tea party took place in a warm atmosphere.

Earlier, Zhang Tiesheng and the Secretary-General of the Arab-China Friendship Association, Omar Mari, visited each other and held friendly talks. Zhang Tiesheng also presented gifts to the Arab-China Friendship Association in the name of the China-Arab Republic Friendship Association.

### 阿拉伯——中国友协举行茶会  欢迎我国四川省歌舞团

1960-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯——中国友协举行茶会
    欢迎我国四川省歌舞团
    新华社开罗6日电　阿拉伯—中国友好协会今晚举行茶会，欢迎以张铁生为首的中国四川省歌舞团。茶会是在热烈的气氛中进行的。
    早些时候，张铁生和阿拉伯—中国友好协会秘书长欧默尔·马里曾互相拜访并且作了友好的谈话。张铁生还以中华人民共和国—阿拉伯联合共和国友好协会的名义向阿拉伯—中国友好协会赠送了礼品。
