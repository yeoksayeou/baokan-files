Title: Khrushchev Proposes to the UN General Assembly Adding Representatives from India and Five Other Countries to the Disarmament Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 7
　Xinhua News Agency, 28th: According to TASS, New York: Soviet Premier Khrushchev wrote to the President of the United Nations General Assembly on the 26th, submitting a proposal on the membership of the Disarmament Committee for consideration by the Fifteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly.

　The proposal requests the United Nations General Assembly to decide to increase the number of members of the Ten-Nation Disarmament Committee. The Soviet Union proposes that, in addition to the representatives of the original ten countries, the Disarmament Committee should also include representatives from India, Indonesia, the United Arab Republic, Ghana, and Mexico. The original ten countries are: the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Romania, Bulgaria, the United States, the United Kingdom, France, Italy, and Canada.

### 赫鲁晓夫向联大提出提案  建议裁军委员会增加印度等五国代表

1960-09-29
第7版()
专栏：

　  赫鲁晓夫向联大提出提案
    建议裁军委员会增加印度等五国代表
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在26日写信给联合国大会主席，就裁军委员会成员问题提出一项提案，供联合国大会第十五届会议审议。
    提案要求联合国大会决定增加十国裁军委员会成员的数目。苏联建议除了原来十个国家的代表外，裁军委员会还包括印度、印度尼西亚、阿拉伯联合共和国、加纳和墨西哥等国的代表。原来的十国代表是：苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、美国、英国、法国、意大利和加拿大。
