Title: Pakistani Halim Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 3

　  Pakistani Halim Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: Professor Halim, Chairman of the Council of the Pakistan Institute of International Affairs, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association.
    Wen Shizhen, director of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association, and Luo Jingyi, director-general, welcomed the guests at the airport.

### 巴基斯坦哈利姆到京

1960-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

　  巴基斯坦哈利姆到京
    新华社28日讯　应中国巴基斯坦友好协会邀请来我国访问的巴基斯坦国际事务学会理事会主席哈利姆教授，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    中国巴基斯坦友好协会理事文士桢，总干事罗静宜等人到机场欢迎客人。
