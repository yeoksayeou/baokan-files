Title: Secretary of the British Cypriot Committee, Pivkos, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: Pivkos, the secretary of the British Cypriot Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries.

### 英国塞浦路斯人委员会书记皮夫考斯到京

1960-09-10
第6版()
专栏：

    英国塞浦路斯人委员会书记皮夫考斯到京
    新华社9日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的英国塞浦路斯人委员会书记皮夫考斯，今天乘飞机到达北京。
