Title: Capital's Press Circles Gather to Commemorate International Day of Journalists' Solidarity
Author:
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 7th. Press circles in the capital gathered tonight to commemorate the "International Day of Journalists' Solidarity" on September 8th. Foreign journalists stationed in Beijing and visiting Beijing were invited to attend today's gathering.

Mei Yi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, said at the meeting that Chinese journalists are determined to work with comrades in socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union, to oppose imperialist wars of aggression, to strive for lasting world peace and the cause of human progress, to further strengthen the international solidarity of journalists from all countries in the world, and to support all persecuted journalist colleagues with our greatest strength.

Mei Yi said that journalists must strengthen their unity, stand at the forefront of the worldwide people's united front against imperialism, and use all news tools to support the resolute struggles of people all over the world against imperialism and for lasting peace and the cause of human progress. Mei Yi, on behalf of Chinese journalists, expressed support and condolences to all the fighters on the news front who have suffered barbarous persecution by imperialists and reactionaries.

Today's gathering was held by the All-China Journalists Association in accordance with the proposal of the International Organization of Journalists. September 8th is the anniversary of the death of Julius Fučík, an outstanding Czechoslovak journalist. The International Organization of Journalists decided in 1958 that this day would be "International Day of Journalists' Solidarity."

### 首都新闻界集会纪念国际新闻工作者团结日

1960-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    首都新闻界集会纪念国际新闻工作者团结日
    新华社7日讯　首都新闻界人士今晚举行集会，纪念9月8日“国际新闻工作者团结日”。驻北京的和正在北京访问的外国新闻工作者应邀参加了今天的集会。
    中国新闻工作者协会副主席梅益在会上讲话说，中国新闻工作者决心同以苏联为首的社会主义国家的同志们一道，为反对帝国主义的侵略战争，为争取世界持久和平和人类进步事业，为进一步加强世界各国新闻工作者的国际团结，为支持一切被迫害的新闻同行，贡献出我们最大的力量。
    梅益说，新闻工作者必须加强团结，站在全世界人民反帝国主义统一战线的最前列，运用一切新闻工具，支持全世界人民为反对帝国主义、争取持久和平和人类进步事业而进行的坚决斗争。梅益代表中国新闻工作者向所有遭受帝国主义和反动派野蛮迫害的新闻战线上的战士们，表示支持和慰问。
    今天的集会是由中国新闻工作者协会根据国际新闻工作者协会的倡议而举办的。9月8日是捷克斯洛伐克的优秀新闻工作者尤利乌斯·伏契克遇难的日子。国际新闻工作者协会1958年决定这一天为“国际新闻工作者团结日”。
