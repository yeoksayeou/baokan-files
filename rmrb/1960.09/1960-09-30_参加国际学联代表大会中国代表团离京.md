Title: Chinese Delegation to International Union of Students Congress Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. The Chinese student delegation to the Sixth Congress of the International Union of Students, which will be held in Baghdad on October 8th, left Beijing by plane today. The delegation was led by Wang Wei and consisted of four members. The other members of the delegation departed Beijing a few days ago.

### 参加国际学联代表大会中国代表团离京

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

　  参加国际学联代表大会中国代表团离京
    新华社29日讯　参加将于10月8日在巴格达召开的国际学联第六次代表大会的中国学生代表团一行四人，在团长王伟率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京。代表团的另一部分团员已于前数日离京。
