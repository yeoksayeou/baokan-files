Title: Party and State Leaders Receive Vietnamese Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-09-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Party and state leaders Zhou Enlai, Dong Biwu, and Peng Zhen received this morning all members of the Viet-Nam China Friendship Association Delegation headed by Tran Huy Lieu, the leader of the Vietnam Democratic Republic Cheo (satirical opera) Art Troupe, Vu Thu, the deputy leader Cao Kim Dien, and the leader of the Vietnam Institute of Literature Delegation, Hoai Thanh, and other distinguished guests, and held cordial talks with them.

Present at the reception were Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Bo Yibo, Xi Zhongxun, Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Zhixiang, Ji Pengfei, Qian Junrui, Lei Renmin, Liu Changsheng, Feng Jiping and others.

Also present at the reception was Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China.

### 党和国家领导人接见越南贵宾

1960-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    党和国家领导人接见越南贵宾
    新华社1日讯　党和国家领导人周恩来、董必武、彭真，今天上午接见了以陈辉燎为首的越中友好协会代表团全体成员，越南民主共和国嘲剧艺术团团长武秋、副团长高金典，越南文学院代表团团长怀青等贵宾，并且和他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在场的，有沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、薄一波、习仲勋、杨秀峰、张致祥、姬鹏飞、钱俊瑞、雷任民、刘长胜、冯基平等。
    接见时，越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平也在场。
