    International Briefs
    Sihanouk Leaves for France for Medical Treatment
    Phnom Penh dispatch: Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk left Phnom Penh on the afternoon of the 5th to go to France for medical treatment.
    Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires to the Netherlands Returns Home
    Jakarta dispatch: Due to the severance of diplomatic relations between Indonesia and the Netherlands, the Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires to the Netherlands, Susanto Djojosugito, returned to Jakarta on the afternoon of the 4th.

### 国际简讯

1960-09-08
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    西哈努克前往法国就医
    金边讯　柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王在5日下午离开金边前往法国就医。
    印度尼西亚驻荷兰代办回国
    雅加达讯　由于印度尼西亚和荷兰断绝了外交关系，印度尼西亚驻荷兰代办苏桑托·若约苏吉托已在4日下午回到雅加达。
