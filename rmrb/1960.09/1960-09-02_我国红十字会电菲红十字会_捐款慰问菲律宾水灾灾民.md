Title: China Red Cross Society Sends Telegram to Philippine Red Cross Society, Donating Funds to Console Philippine Flood Victims
Author:
Date: 1960-09-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 1st. Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, today sent a telegram to the Philippine Red Cross Society, expressing condolences to the residents of the Pampanga River basin in Central Luzon who suffered from floods. The Red Cross Society of China also remitted RMB 15,000 along with the telegram to help alleviate the suffering of the victims and express the Chinese people's friendly wishes to the Philippine people.

### 我国红十字会电菲红十字会  捐款慰问菲律宾水灾灾民

1960-09-02
第6版()
专栏：

    我国红十字会电菲红十字会
    捐款慰问菲律宾水灾灾民
    新华社1日讯　中国红十字会总会会长李德全今天打电报给菲律宾红十字会，对吕宋岛中部彭潘加河流域遭受水灾的居民表示慰问。中国红十字会总会还随电汇去人民币一万五千元，帮助减轻灾民的痛苦，并表达中国人民对菲律宾人民的友好愿望。
