Title: Ceylon Communist Youth and Progressive Youth League Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 8th. A ten-member delegation from the Ceylon Communist Youth and Progressive Youth League Federation, led by N.L. Perera, General Secretary of the Ceylon Communist Youth and Progressive Youth League Federation, and Deputy Leader W.A. Hermadasa, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation for a visit to China.

### 锡兰共产主义青年和进步青联代表团到京

1960-09-09
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰共产主义青年和进步青联代表团到京
    新华社8日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请前来中国访问的锡兰共产主义青年和进步青年联盟联合会代表团一行十人，在团长、锡兰共产主义青年和进步青年联盟联合会总书记纳·勒·佩雷拉和副团长瓦·艾·赫尔马达萨率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
