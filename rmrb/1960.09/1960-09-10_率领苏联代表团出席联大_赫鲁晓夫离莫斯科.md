Title: Khrushchev Leaves Moscow to Lead Soviet Delegation to UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 9th, TASS Moscow, September 9th: Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Khrushchev left Moscow at 16:00 (Moscow time) today to attend the 15th session of the United Nations General Assembly.

Accompanying Khrushchev were representatives, deputy representatives, advisors, and experts of the Soviet delegation.

Khrushchev and the members of the Soviet delegation flew to Kaliningrad today, where they will transfer to the "Baltika" turbo-electric ship to New York.

### 率领苏联代表团出席联大  赫鲁晓夫离莫斯科

1960-09-10
第6版()
专栏：

    率领苏联代表团出席联大
    赫鲁晓夫离莫斯科
    新华社9日讯　塔斯社莫斯科9日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在今天十六时（莫斯科时间）离开莫斯科，出席联合国大会第十五届会议。
    与赫鲁晓夫同行的，还有苏联代表团的代表、副代表、顾问和专家等人。
    赫鲁晓夫和苏联代表团团员们今天乘飞机前往加里宁格勒，将再从那里改乘“波罗的海号”透平电动机船前往纽约。
