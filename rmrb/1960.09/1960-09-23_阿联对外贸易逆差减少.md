Title: UAE Reduces Foreign Trade Deficit
Author:
Date: 1960-09-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 20th: The United Arab Republic's Central Economic Minister, Kaissouny, told reporters here today that in the first seven months of this year, the UAE's foreign trade deficit was 4.44 million Egyptian pounds, a decrease of more than 35.8 million Egyptian pounds compared to the same period last year. During this period, the total value of the UAE's exports was 126.39 million Egyptian pounds, an increase of 40.51 million Egyptian pounds compared to the same period last year. The minister also said that in the first seven months of this year, the largest buyer of exports from the Egyptian region was the Soviet Union, followed by China, India, the United States, and others.

### 阿联对外贸易逆差减少

1960-09-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　阿联对外贸易逆差减少
    新华社开罗20日电　阿联中央经济部长凯舒尼今天在这里对新闻记者说，今年头七个月，阿联对外贸易的逆差是四百四十四万埃镑，比去年同期减少了三千五百八十多万埃镑。这一时期阿联的出口总值是一亿二千六百三十九万埃镑，比去年同期增加了四千零五十一万埃镑。这位部长又说，在今年头七个月里，埃及地区出口货的最大买主是苏联，其次是中国、印度、美国等。
