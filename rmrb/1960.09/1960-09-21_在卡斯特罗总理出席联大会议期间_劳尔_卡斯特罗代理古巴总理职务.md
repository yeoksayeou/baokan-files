Title: Raúl Castro Serves as Acting Prime Minister of Cuba During Prime Minister Castro's Attendance at the UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1960-09-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 19th -- Raúl Castro, Minister of the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Cuba, has been appointed to serve as Acting Prime Minister of the Cuban Revolutionary Government during Prime Minister Castro's absence from the country.

Prime Minister Castro has already led the Cuban delegation to New York on the 18th to attend the Fifteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 在卡斯特罗总理出席联大会议期间  劳尔·卡斯特罗代理古巴总理职务

1960-09-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　在卡斯特罗总理出席联大会议期间
　  劳尔·卡斯特罗代理古巴总理职务
    新华社哈瓦那19日电　古巴革命武装部部长劳尔·卡斯特罗受命在卡斯特罗总理出国期间，代理古巴革命政府总理的职务。
    卡斯特罗总理已经率领古巴代表团在18日到达纽约出席第十五届联合国大会。
