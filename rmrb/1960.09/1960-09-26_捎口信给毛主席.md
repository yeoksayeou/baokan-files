Title: A Message to Chairman Mao
Author: Yan Zhongfu
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 7

　　A Message to Chairman Mao
    Yan Zhongfu
    Where I used to roam, the "Aor'erte,"
    Today, land is cultivated.
    I am the girl who once wielded the lasso,
    Now I know how to open up wasteland, sow seeds, and also hold the plow;
    Yesterday I newly learned how to fertilize and weed,
    Today I've also started driving a tractor.
    We herdsmen have learned to farm the land,
    And are eating fried millet rice we grew ourselves,
    What a great joy!
    Please take this happy news,
    And send a message to our beloved Chairman Mao.

### 捎口信给毛主席
阎中伏
1960-09-26
第7版()
专栏：

　　捎口信给毛主席
    阎中伏
    在我从前走“敖尔特”的地方，
    今天种了地。
    我是拿套马杆的姑娘，
    现在会开荒、播种、也会扶犁；
    昨天新学了施肥除草，
    今天又开起了拖拉机。
    我们牧人学会了种地，
    吃上了自种糜谷炒的米，
    天大的喜事啊！
    请把这喜事，
    捎口信给敬爱的毛主席。
