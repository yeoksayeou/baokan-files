Title: Delegation from the Japan-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. The delegation from the Japan-China Friendship Association, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China for friendship and to participate in the National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon. The delegation of twenty members was led by Shinichiro Omori, the head of the delegation, permanent director of the Japan-China Friendship Association, and member of the Organization Committee.

The delegation, composed of heads of various departments of the Japan-China Friendship Association headquarters and local branches throughout Japan, was warmly welcomed at the Beijing Railway Station by Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Deputy Secretary-General Lin Lin, and more than 40 people from all walks of life in the capital, including Chen Yu, Shen Zijiu, Xie Nanguang, Yang Chunsong, Peng Yan, Qi Mugguang, Yang Zheng, Ding Tuo, Wang Xiaoyun, Xia Rong, Wang Geng, and Li Mengjing. Yang Hansheng and others warmly shook hands with all members of the delegation, and the guests received flowers from the youth.

Also at the station to greet them today were Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji and Korean peace activist Hwang Bong-koo, both residing in Beijing.

Secretary-General of the Japanese Theatre Troupe, Yue Toshio, who is currently in Beijing for a performance tour, also came to the station to greet them.

The delegation from the Japan-China Friendship Association arrived in Guangzhou by train from Shenzhen on the afternoon of the 24th. That evening, Du Guoxiang, President of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet to welcome the guests. On the 25th, the guests also paid visits in Guangzhou.

### 日中友好协会访华代表团到达北京

1960-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　日中友好协会访华代表团到达北京
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国作友好访问并参加国庆观礼的日中友好协会访华代表团一行二十人，在团长、日中友协常任理事兼组织委员会委员长大森真一郎率领下，今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    这个由日中友好协会总会各部门及日本国内各地支会负责人组成的代表团，在北京车站受到中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙、副秘书长林林以及陈宇、沈兹九、谢南光、杨春松、彭炎、戚慕光、杨正、丁拓、王晓云、夏荣、王耕、李孟竟等首都各方面人士四十多人的热烈欢迎。阳翰笙等与代表团全体成员一一亲切握手，客人们接受了青年们的献花。
    今天到车站迎接的还有日本在京和平人士西园寺公一，朝鲜在京和平人士黄凤九。
    正在北京进行访问演出的日本话剧团秘书长越寿雄也到车站迎接。
    日中友好协会代表团是24日下午从深圳乘火车到达广州的。当晚，中国人民对外文化协会广州分会会长杜国庠曾设宴欢迎客人。25日，客人们在广州还进行了参观访问。
