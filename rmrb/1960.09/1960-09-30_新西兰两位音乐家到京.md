Title: Two New Zealand Musicians Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. Ms. Vanya Manste, a New Zealand female pianist, and Mr. Frederick Page, Head of the Music Department of Victoria University, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Sun Shen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Musicians Association, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 新西兰两位音乐家到京

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

    新西兰两位音乐家到京
    新华社29日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的新西兰女钢琴家凡奈塔·曼斯蒂女士、维多利亚大学音乐系主任弗莱德利克·派奇，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国音乐家协会书记处书记孙慎等。
