Title: Burmese Art Troupe Holds Farewell Banquet in Kunming, Hosts and Guests Mutually Wish for Continuous Development and Consolidation of China-Myanmar Friendship
Author:
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, September 12th. The head of the Burmese Cultural Friendship Art Troupe, U Ba Swe, held a farewell banquet at the Kunming Hotel this noon. In his speech at the banquet, he expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Chinese people for their deep friendship with the Burmese people and for the warm welcome the art troupe received during its visit to China.

Yu Yichuan, Governor of Yunnan Province; Chu Tunan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Liu Minghui and Guo Chao, Vice Governors of Yunnan Province; Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee; Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming; Jing Zhuru, Deputy Mayor; Major General Lu Ruilin and Major General Ding Rongchang, leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming; Cun Shusheng, Wang Shaoyan, Wu Zhiyuan, and Dao Jingban, Vice Chairmen of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, were all invited to attend the banquet.

Also invited to the banquet were the Chinese representatives and advisors of the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee in Kunming, as well as figures from the literary and art circles of Yunnan Province and Kunming City.

U Maung Maung Soe, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and Vu Van Huong, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, also attended the banquet.

Head of the troupe U Ba Swe delivered a farewell speech at the banquet. He said that the Burmese Cultural Friendship Art Troupe received a warm reception and grand welcome from the Chinese people wherever they went during their visit to China, which fully demonstrates that the friendship between the peoples of Myanmar and China is being further consolidated and developed. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude on behalf of all members of the art troupe.

He said that their visit and performance in China this time was very successful, not only bringing Burmese culture and art but also bringing the Burmese people's close friendship with the Chinese people. At the same time, we saw that the Chinese people hold great enthusiasm and friendship for the Burmese people, which we will never forget. We must bring this enthusiasm and friendship of the Chinese people back home so that the friendship between our two countries can be further consolidated and developed on the original basis. Finally, he raised his glass to wish for greater development of the friendship between Myanmar and China and for further prosperity of the culture and art of Myanmar and China.

Governor Yu Yichuan spoke at the banquet. He said that Burmese culture and art are rich in national characteristics, and many excellent programs fully demonstrate the diligence, wisdom, and enthusiasm of the Burmese people. Every program made us feel very kind. He said that the enthusiastic response of the broad audience in Kunming to the art troupe's performances fully demonstrates the heartfelt love of the people of all ethnic groups in Yunnan Province for Burmese culture and art and their friendly feelings towards the Burmese people.

Chu Tunan and U Maung Maung Soe also spoke at the banquet, wishing for further development and consolidation of the traditional friendship between the people of China and Myanmar.

The banquet was held in a very cordial and warm atmosphere. The hosts and guests also sang the Burmese song "Song of China-Myanmar Friendship" together at the banquet.

### 缅甸艺术团在昆明举行告别宴会  宾主互祝中缅友谊不断发展巩固

1960-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸艺术团在昆明举行告别宴会
    宾主互祝中缅友谊不断发展巩固
    新华社昆明12日电　缅甸文化友好艺术团团长吴巴盛今天中午在昆明饭店举行告别宴会。他在宴会上的讲话中表示衷心感谢我国人民对缅甸人民的深厚情谊和艺术团在访问中国期间所受到的热烈欢迎。
    云南省省长于一川，对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南，云南省副省长刘明辉、郭超，省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，昆明市市长潘朔端、副市长经竹如，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长鲁瑞林少将、丁荣昌少将，政协云南省委员会副主席寸树声、王少岩、吴志渊、刀京版等，都应邀出席了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的还有中缅边界联合委员会在昆明的中国代表、顾问和云南省、昆明市的文艺界人士等。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭和越南民主共和国驻昆明领事武文享也出席了宴会。
    吴巴盛团长在宴会上致告别词。他说，缅甸文化友好艺术团来到中国访问，不论到什么地方，都受到中国人民的亲切接待和隆重欢迎，这充分说明缅中两国人民的友谊正在进一步巩固和发展。他代表艺术团全体团员表示衷心感谢。
    他说，我们这次到中国的访问演出是很成功的，不仅带来了缅甸的文化艺术，也带来了缅甸人民对中国人民的亲密友谊。同时，我们看到了中国人民对缅甸人民抱着巨大的热情和友谊，这是我们永远不能忘记的。我们一定要把中国人民的这种热情和友谊带回去，使我们两国的友谊在原来的基础上更加巩固和发展。他最后举杯祝缅中两国的友谊更加发展，祝缅中两国的文化艺术进一步繁荣。
    于一川省长在宴会上讲了话。他说，缅甸文化艺术富有民族特色，许多优秀节目充分表现了缅甸人民的勤劳、智慧和热情，每一个节目都使我们感到非常亲切。他说，昆明广大观众对艺术团演出的热烈反应，充分表现了云南省各族人民对缅甸文化艺术的衷心喜爱和对缅甸人民的友好感情。
    楚图南和吴茂茂梭也分别在宴会上讲了话，祝中缅两国人民的传统友谊进一步发展和巩固。
    宴会在十分亲切、热烈的气氛中进行。宾主在宴会上还一起唱了缅甸歌曲“中缅友谊之歌”。
