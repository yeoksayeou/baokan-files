Title: Full of Friendship Between the Chinese and Vietnamese People: Burmese Art Troupe Returns to Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-09-15
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, September 14th: The Burmese Federal Cultural Friendship Art Troupe, led by head U Ba Swe, returned home today by special plane from Kunming after concluding a friendly visit and performance in our country lasting more than a month.
    Before the plane took off, Vice Chairman Chu Tunan warmly shook hands with head U Ba Swe and the Burmese friends to say goodbye. The people who came to see them off enthusiastically shouted: "Long live Sino-Burmese friendship!" and sang "Sino-Burmese Friendship Song." The Burmese artists also shouted: "Long live Burmese-Chinese friendship!" and sang "Burmese-Chinese Friendship Song." Head U Ba Swe and the Burmese friends accepted flowers presented by representatives of the province and city's literary and art circles, and stood at the cabin door waving flowers enthusiastically, bidding farewell to their Chinese friends with reluctance. As the plane took off, the crowd waving flowers and colorful scarves at the airport waved goodbye to the Burmese artists, wishing them a safe journey!
    On the afternoon of the 13th, Chu Tunan held a farewell banquet for the art troupe. Yunnan Provincial Governor Yu Yichuan, Deputy Governor Liu Minghui, and others attended the banquet. Chu Tunan spoke at the banquet, on behalf of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee he congratulated and thanked the Burmese Cultural Friendship Art Troupe for their successful visit and performance in China. U Ba Swe, head of the Burmese Cultural Friendship Art Troupe, also spoke, expressing his gratitude to the Chinese people and the people of Yunnan Province for their warm reception and welcome to the art troupe, and that they will bring this deep friendship of the Chinese people back to the Burmese people.

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 14th: The Burmese Cultural Friendship Art Troupe, led by U Ba Swe, Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Culture, returned here by plane from Kunming this afternoon after visiting the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    U Ba Swe, head of the Burmese Cultural Friendship Art Troupe, said after getting off the plane that the art troupe's performance in China was a great success. He also said that the Burmese art troupe was warmly welcomed by the Chinese people wherever they went. U Ba Swe said, "Our visit is not only for cultural exchange, but especially for the friendship between the two countries."
    Those who welcomed the Burmese art troupe at the airport included U Tin Aung, Deputy Secretary of the Ministry of Culture, Burmese cultural and art figures, and officials from the Ministry of Culture. Chinese Ambassador Li Yimang and Xiao Ming, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 满载中、越人民的友谊  缅甸艺术团回到仰光

1960-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    满载中、越人民的友谊
    缅甸艺术团回到仰光
    新华社昆明14日电　以吴巴盛团长为首的缅甸联邦文化友好艺术团，结束了在我国历时一个多月的友好访问演出后，今日乘专机离开昆明回国。
    飞机起飞前，楚图南副主任和吴巴盛团长及缅甸朋友们亲切握手道别。欢送的人们热烈高呼：“中缅友好万岁！”并欢唱“中缅友好歌”。缅甸艺术家们也高呼：“缅中友好万岁”，唱“缅中友谊之歌”。吴巴盛团长和缅甸朋友们接受了省、市文艺界代表的献花，并站在机舱口热烈挥动鲜花，依依不舍地向中国朋友告别。飞机起飞了，机场上欢送的人群挥舞手里的鲜花和彩巾，向缅甸艺术家们送别，祝他们一路平安！
    13日下午，楚图南为艺术团举行了欢送宴会。云南省省长于一川、副省长刘明辉等出席了宴会。楚图南在宴会上讲话，他代表对外文化联络委员会对缅甸文化友好艺术团在中国的访问演出成功表示祝贺和感谢。缅甸文化友好艺术团团长吴巴盛也讲了话，他表示感谢中国人民和云南省人民对艺术团的热情接待和欢迎，并且将把中国人民的这种深厚友谊带回给缅甸人民。
    新华社仰光14日电　由缅甸文化部秘书吴巴盛率领的缅甸文化友好艺术团在访问中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国以后，今天下午乘飞机从昆明回到这里。
    缅甸文化友好艺术团团长吴巴盛在下飞机后说，艺术团在中国的演出获得很大成功。他又说，缅甸艺术团所到之处受到中国人民的热烈欢迎。吴巴盛说，“我们的访问不仅是为了文化交流，而且特别是为了两国之间的友谊”。
    在机场上欢迎缅甸艺术团的有文化部副秘书吴丁奥、缅甸文化艺术界人士和文化部的官员等。中国大使李一氓和中国驻缅甸大使馆文化参赞萧明也到机场欢迎。
