Title: Vietnam-China Friendship Association Delegation to Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The distinguished guests of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association delegation, headed by Tran Huy Lieu, left Beijing by train at noon today for Nanjing. The distinguished guests will participate in local mass celebrations of the National Day of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam or visit local areas in Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Nanning and other cities. Today, more than 200 people from all walks of life in the capital went to the Beijing Railway Station to see off the distinguished guests. Amidst warm applause, a group of young women presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Yang Xiufeng, President of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association, Ding Xilin and Qu Wu, Vice-Chairmen of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Feng Jiping, Vice Mayor of Beijing, Xu Zhizhen, Zhou Peiyuan, Jin Zhao, Zhang E, Yang Shaoren, Li Menghua, and other members of the Council of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association and people from various fields. Tran Trong Quat, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and other diplomatic officials also went to the station to see them off.

### 越中友协代表团去南京

1960-09-04
第3版()
专栏：

    越中友协代表团去南京
    新华社3日讯　以陈辉燎为首的越中友好协会代表团的贵宾，于今天中午乘火车离开北京前往南京。贵宾们将在南京、上海、杭州、广州、南宁等城市，参加当地群众庆祝越南民主共和国国庆活动或在当地参观访问。今天，首都各界群众二百多人到北京车站欢送贵宾。在热烈的掌声中，一群女青年向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    到车站欢送的，有中越友好协会会长杨秀峰，对外文委副主任丁西林、屈武，文化部副部长夏衍，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，北京市副市长冯基平，中越友协理事和各方面人士许之桢、周培源、金照、张谔、杨少任、李梦华等。越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆参赞陈仲括等外交官员，也到车站送行。
