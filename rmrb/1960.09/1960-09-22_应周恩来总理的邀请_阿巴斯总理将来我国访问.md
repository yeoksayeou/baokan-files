Title: Premier Abbas to Visit China at the Invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-22
Page: 1

    Upon the Invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai
　  Premier Abbas to Visit China
    Xinhua News Agency, September 21st. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People’s Republic of China has invited Ferhat Abbas, Premier of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, to visit China and attend the celebrations for the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People’s Republic of China. Premier Abbas has accepted this invitation and will soon depart for China.

### 应周恩来总理的邀请  阿巴斯总理将来我国访问

1960-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    应周恩来总理的邀请
　  阿巴斯总理将来我国访问
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来邀请阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理费尔哈特·阿巴斯前来我国访问，并参加中华人民共和国建国十一周年庆典。阿巴斯总理已接受这一邀请，即将启程来华。
