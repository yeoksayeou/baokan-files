Title: Our National Song and Dance Troupe Leaves for Performance in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd. The Chinese National Song and Dance Troupe, consisting of sixty-five members and led by its head Xia Furen, left Beijing by plane at noon today for Moscow to perform in the Soviet Union as part of the 1960 Sino-Soviet Cultural Agreement execution plan.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Chu Tunan, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Liu Tieshan, Deputy Head of the Central National Song and Dance Troupe.

Ablova, Second Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, and others also saw them off at the airport.

### 我民族歌舞团赴苏演出

1960-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    我民族歌舞团赴苏演出
    新华社2日讯　根据中苏文化协定1960年执行计划赴苏联作访问演出的中国民族歌舞团一行六十五人，由团长夏辅仁率领，于今天中午乘飞机离开北京前往莫斯科。
    到机场欢送的，有对外文委副主任楚图南，中央民族歌舞团副团长刘铁山等。
    苏联驻中国大使馆二等秘书阿布洛娃等也到机场欢送。
