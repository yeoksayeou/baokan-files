Title: Norwegian Pathology Professor Leif Kreyberg Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 11th: Leif Kreyberg, a Norwegian pathology professor who was invited by the Chinese Medical Association to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by train on the 9th.
　　Huang Dingchen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Medical Association, and others were at the station to greet him.

### 挪威病理学教授莱夫·克赖堡到京

1960-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    挪威病理学教授莱夫·克赖堡到京
    新华社11日讯　应中华医学会邀请前来我国进行友好访问的挪威病理学教授莱夫·克赖堡在9日乘火车到达北京。
    到车站迎接的，有中华医学会副秘书长黄鼎臣等人。
