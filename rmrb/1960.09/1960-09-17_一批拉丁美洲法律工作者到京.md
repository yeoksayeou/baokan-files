Title: A Group of Latin American Legal Professionals Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: At the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society, Brazilian legal professional and Secretary of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, Liete Lerte Elba Rodrigues de Brito; Secretary of the Venezuelan Democratic Law Association, Dr. José Sánchez Mijares; and Peruvian legal professionals: Vice Chairman of the National Bar Association of Peru, Dr. Raúl Torres Fernández and his wife; President of the Peruvian Democratic Law Association, Moisés Arroyo Posadas; President of the Cusco Bar Association of Peru, Dr. Carlos Ferdinand Guaderos; and lawyer Yang Ya Estrella arrived in Beijing by plane today.
Zhang Zhiran and Zhang Youyu, Vice Presidents of the China Political Science and Law Society, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 一批拉丁美洲法律工作者到京

1960-09-17
第6版()
专栏：

    一批拉丁美洲法律工作者到京
    新华社15日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的巴西法律工作者、国际民主法律工作者协会书记莱特耳巴·罗德里格斯·德布里托，委内瑞拉民主法协书记荷斯·桑切斯·米哈列斯博士，以及秘鲁法律工作者：秘鲁全国律师代表大会副主席劳尔·托列士·费尔南地斯博士和他的夫人，秘鲁民主法协主席摩舍斯·阿罗约·波沙达斯，秘鲁古斯科市法协主席卡罗斯·费尔地南德·古阿德罗斯博士和律师杨雅·埃斯特列拉，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    中国政治法律学会副会长张志让、张友渔等到机场欢迎。
