Title: Korean Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: The scientific and technological cooperation delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK), led by Park Yong-sung, the head of the delegation, chairman of the Korean group of the China-Korea Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, and vice chairman of the Light Industry Commission of the DPRK, arrived in Beijing by train today to attend the third meeting of the China-Korea Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee. The delegation consisted of four members.

### 朝鲜科学技术合作代表团到京

1960-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜科学技术合作代表团到京
    据新华社22日讯　前来我国出席中朝科学技术合作委员会第三届会议的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国科学技术合作代表团一行四人，在团长、中朝科学技术合作委员会朝鲜组主席、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国轻工业委员会副委员长朴龙成率领下，今天乘火车到达北京。
