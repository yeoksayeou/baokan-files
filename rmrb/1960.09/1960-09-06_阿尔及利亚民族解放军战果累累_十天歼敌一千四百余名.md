Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Achieves Numerous Victories: Annihilating More Than 1,400 Enemy Troops in Ten Days
Author:
Date: 1960-09-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 4th: According to a military communique released tonight by the headquarters of the Algerian National Liberation Army, from August 22nd to 31st, the Algerian National Liberation Army killed 795 French soldiers and wounded 660.

The communique stated that during this period, the Algerian National Liberation Army attacked 30 French military strongholds, engaged in 30 battles with enemy forces, and ambushed enemy military convoys 31 times. Commando units launched 44 attacks on police outposts and other places frequently visited by the enemy.

The communique stated that the French forces also lost 60 military vehicles and 11 aircraft, including several helicopters. Four trains were destroyed. Algerian nationalists also seized 389 weapons.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放军战果累累  十天歼敌一千四百余名

1960-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚民族解放军战果累累
    十天歼敌一千四百余名
    新华社开罗4日电　据阿尔及利亚民族解放军总部今晚发表的军事公报说，自8月22日至31日，阿尔及利亚民族解放军打死七百九十五名法国兵，打伤六百六十人。
    公报说，在这期间，阿尔及利亚民族解放军攻击了三十个法军军事据点，与敌军进行了三十次战斗，并对敌军军用车队进行了三十一次伏击。突击队对警察前哨和敌人常去的其它一些地方进行了四十四次进攻。
    公报说，法军方面还损失了六十辆军用车辆，十一架飞机，其中包括几架直升飞机。四列火车被炸毁。阿尔及利亚民族主义者还缴获了三百八十九件武器。
