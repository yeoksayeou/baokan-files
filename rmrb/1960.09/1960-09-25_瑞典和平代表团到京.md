Title: Swedish Peace Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 24th. The Swedish Peace Delegation, headed by Gunnar Inge, member of the Presidium of the Swedish National Peace Committee and member of the World Peace Council, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 24th. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

　　Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Li Dequan, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, and Zhu Ziqi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others.

### 瑞典和平代表团到京

1960-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　  瑞典和平代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　以瑞典全国和平委员会主席团委员、世界和平理事会理事冈纳尔·英哲为首的瑞典和平代表团一行十人，于24日乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的。
    前往机场欢迎的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会副主席李德全，以及中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长朱子奇等人。
