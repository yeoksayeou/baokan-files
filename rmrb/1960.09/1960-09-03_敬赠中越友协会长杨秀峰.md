Title: Respectfully Dedicated to Yang Xiufeng, President of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association
Author: Chen Huiliao
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 2

    Respectfully Dedicated to Yang Xiufeng, President of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association
    Chen Huiliao, Vice President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association
      For fifteen years, the sun has grown redder, and the east wind rises all over the South.
      The old friendship between Vietnam and China enters a new era, the Yangtze and Red Rivers linked as one.
               Inspired by the celebration of Vietnamese National Day in Beijing, September 2nd
