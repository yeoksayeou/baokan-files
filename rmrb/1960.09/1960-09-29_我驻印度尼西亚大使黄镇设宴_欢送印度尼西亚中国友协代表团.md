Title: My Ambassador to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, Hosts a Banquet to Bid Farewell to the Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 27th. Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to the Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation.

　　The delegation is about to go to Beijing to participate in the celebration of the eleventh anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

　　Professor Suripto, head of the delegation, Sain Nasution, deputy head of the delegation, and the four members of the delegation attended the banquet. Also present at the banquet was Suroto, chairman of the Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association, and others.

　　The banquet proceeded in a very friendly and pleasant atmosphere.

　　The delegation is scheduled to leave for China on the 29th.

### 我驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇设宴　欢送印度尼西亚中国友协代表团

1960-09-29
第7版()
专栏：

　　我驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇设宴
　　欢送印度尼西亚中国友协代表团
    新华社雅加达27日电　中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇今晚设宴欢送印度尼西亚—中国友好协会代表团。
    代表团即将前往北京参加中华人民共和国成立十一周年庆典。
    代表团团长苏里普托教授、副团长赛因·纳苏蒂安和代表团的四位团员出席了宴会。出席宴会的还有印度尼西亚—中国友好协会主席苏洛托等。
    宴会在非常友好和愉快的气氛中进行。
      代表团预定29日前往中国。
