Title: Strengthen the Unity of the Working Class and Intensify the Struggle Against Imperialism: The Uruguayan People Celebrate the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of Uruguay

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, 21st: According to TASS, the working people of various cities and towns in Uruguay held gatherings on the 21st to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Uruguay.

    The celebrations were carried out under the slogan of strengthening the unity of the working class, intensifying the struggle, and opposing the conspiracies of imperialism in Uruguay and throughout Latin America. Leaders of the Central Committee of the Communist Party and local party organizations delivered reports at the above-mentioned gatherings on the historical path of the Communist Party of Uruguay and the struggle to meet the demands of the working people.

    The Uruguayan Communist Youth League held sports events to commemorate this significant day.

    The celebrations for the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party will continue until October 8, culminating in a large-scale mass rally.

### 加强工人阶级的团结，加强反对帝国主义的斗争　乌拉圭人民庆祝乌共建党四十年

1960-09-23
第5版()
专栏：

    加强工人阶级的团结，加强反对帝国主义的斗争
　　乌拉圭人民庆祝乌共建党四十年
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社蒙得维的亚21日讯：乌拉圭各城市和村镇的劳动人民在21日纷纷举行集会庆祝乌拉圭共产党成立四十周年。
    庆祝活动是在加强工人阶级团结、加强斗争、反对帝国主义在乌拉圭和整个拉丁美洲的阴谋这一口号下进行的。共产党中央委员会和地方党组织的领导人分别在上述集会上作了关于乌拉圭共产党的历史道路和争取满足劳动人民要求的斗争的报告。
    乌拉圭共产主义青年联盟为纪念这个意义重大的日子举行了运动会。
    庆祝共产党成立四十周年的庆祝活动将进行到10月8日，最后将举行一次大规模的群众大会。
