Title: Moroccan National Radio Reporter Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th - Rabbi Sakali, a reporter from Moroccan National Radio, arrived in Beijing this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association for a visit to our country.

### 摩洛哥国家广播电台记者到京

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥国家广播电台记者到京
    新华社29日讯　摩洛哥国家广播电台记者拉比·萨卡利应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问，今天下午到达北京。
