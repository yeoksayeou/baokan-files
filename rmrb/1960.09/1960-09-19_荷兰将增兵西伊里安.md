Title: Netherlands to Increase Troops in West Irian
Author:
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, 17th Dispatch: The Dutch Defense Ministry announced today that eight hundred Dutch infantry soldiers will be sent to West Irian in Indonesia on September 24th.

It is reported that a Dutch aircraft carrier recently transported twelve "Hunter" jet fighters to West Irian. Another twelve are about to be shipped.

### 荷兰将增兵西伊里安

1960-09-19
第6版()
专栏：

    荷兰将增兵西伊里安
    据新华社17日讯　海牙消息：荷兰国防部今天宣布，八百名荷兰步兵将在9月24日被派往印度尼西亚的西伊里安去。
    据报道，最近一艘荷兰的航空母舰已经把十二架“猎人”式喷气战斗机运到西伊里安。另外十二架即将运去。
