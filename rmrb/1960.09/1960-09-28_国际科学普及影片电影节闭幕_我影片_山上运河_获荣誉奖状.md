Title: International Science Popularization Film Festival Closes; Chinese Film "Canal on the Mountain" Receives Honorary Diploma
Author:
Date: 1960-09-28
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 26th. The 14th International Scientific Film Association Conference and the 3rd International Science Popularization Film Festival closed in Prague on the 24th. The Chinese film "Canal on the Mountain," directed by Wang Minsheng, received an honorary diploma.
    The International Scientific Film Association Council, based on the recommendation of the Science Popularization Film Department, awarded honorary diplomas to a total of nine films participating in the Third International Science Film Festival. In addition to "Canal on the Mountain," films from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Hungary, Japan, West Germany, the United Kingdom, and France also received diplomas. Films from Bulgaria and Japan each received special mention. During the discussions, conference participants viewed seventy scientific films from twenty-seven countries.

### 国际科学普及影片电影节闭幕　我影片“山上运河”获荣誉奖状

1960-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　国际科学普及影片电影节闭幕
　　我影片“山上运河”获荣誉奖状
    新华社布拉格26日电　第十四届国际科学电影协会会议和第三届国际科学普及影片电影节24日在布拉格闭幕。中国影片、王敏生导演的“山上运河”获得了荣誉奖状。
    国际科学电影协会理事会根据科学普及影片部的推荐，一共向参加第三届国际科学影片电影节的九部影片颁发了荣誉奖状。除“山上运河”外，获得奖状的还有苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、匈牙利、日本、西德、英国、法国的影片。保加利亚和日本还各有一部影片受到表扬。在进行讨论过程中，协会会议参加者观看了二十七个国家的七十部科学影片。
