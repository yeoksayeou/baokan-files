Title: Hu Yuzhi Hosts Banquet for Chairman of Indonesian Party

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th - Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Foreign Cultural Relations, and his wife, Shen Zijiu, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of Asmara Hadi, member of the Supreme Advisory Council of Indonesia and Chairman of the Indonesian Party, and his wife, as well as Asnawi Said, Secretary-General of the Indonesian Party.

Those present at the banquet included Bao Erhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association and Director of the China Islamic Association; Zhang Zhiyi, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the CPPCC; Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; and representatives from the Chinese Writers Association and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Suleiman, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China, and his wife were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 胡愈之宴请印度尼西亚党总主席

1960-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    胡愈之宴请印度尼西亚党总主席
    新华社19日讯　中国人民外文学会副会长胡愈之和夫人沈兹九，今晚设宴欢迎印度尼西亚最高评议院委员、印度尼西亚党总主席阿斯马腊·哈迪和夫人以及印度尼西亚党秘书长阿斯纳威·赛义德。
    出席宴会的有中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长、中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉，政协全国委员会副秘书长张执一，全国妇联主席团委员曹孟君，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪和中国作家协会、中国人民对外文化协会的代表。
    印度尼西亚驻我国大使馆临时代办苏雷曼和夫人也应邀出席宴会。
