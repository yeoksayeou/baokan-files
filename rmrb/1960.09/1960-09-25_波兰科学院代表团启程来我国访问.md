Title: Polish Academy of Sciences Delegation Leaves for Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, September 23rd: A delegation from the Polish Academy of Sciences, headed by the President of the Polish Academy of Sciences, Kotarbinski, and consisting of four members, left by plane for China today at noon for a visit. Officials from the Polish Academy of Sciences and Wang Bingnan, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, were at the airport to see the delegation off.

### 波兰科学院代表团启程来我国访问

1960-09-25
第7版()
专栏：

　  波兰科学院代表团启程来我国访问
    新华社华沙23日电　以波兰科学院长科塔宾斯基为首的由四人组成的波兰科学院代表团今天中午乘飞机前往中国访问。波兰科学院负责人员和中国驻波兰大使王炳南曾到机场为代表团送行。
