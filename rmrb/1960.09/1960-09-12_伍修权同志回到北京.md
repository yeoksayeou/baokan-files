Title: Comrade Wu Xiuquan Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Comrade Wu Xiuquan, the representative of the Chinese Communist Party, who was invited to Cuba to attend the Eighth National Congress of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, returned to Beijing by plane this morning.

### 伍修权同志回到北京

1960-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    伍修权同志回到北京
　　新华社11日讯　应邀前往古巴参加古巴人民社会党第八次全国代表大会的中国共产党代表伍修权同志，在今日上午乘飞机回到北京。
