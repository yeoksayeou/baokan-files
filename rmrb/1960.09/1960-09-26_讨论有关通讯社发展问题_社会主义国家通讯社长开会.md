Title: Discussion on Issues Concerning the Development of News Agencies: Heads of News Agencies of Socialist Countries Hold Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, September 24th: The Fourth Conference of Heads of News Agencies of Socialist Countries was held here from September 20th to 23rd.

　　The participants in the meeting discussed issues related to the further development of socialist news agencies and exchanged experiences.

　　Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Yugov, and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Grigorov, received the participants of the meeting on the 22nd. During the reception, the hosts and guests had a cordial conversation.

### 讨论有关通讯社发展问题　社会主义国家通讯社长开会

1960-09-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　讨论有关通讯社发展问题
　　社会主义国家通讯社长开会
    新华社索非亚24日电　第四次社会主义国家通讯社社长会议从9月20日到23日在这里举行。
    会议的参加者讨论了有关社会主义通讯社的进一步发展问题，并交流了经验。
    保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫和保加利亚共产党中央书记处书记格里戈罗夫22日曾经接见了这次会议的参加者。接见时，宾主进行了亲切的交谈。
