Title: Czechoslovak Chemist F. Herout Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 11th – Academician František Herout, a corresponding member of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences and a chemist specializing in natural substances, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 11th for an academic visit to China according to the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Czechoslovak Academies of Sciences Scientific Cooperation Agreement. Among those who greeted him at the airport were Yun Ziqiang, Deputy Director of the Department of Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and others.

### 捷化学家弗·赫罗特到京

1960-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    捷化学家弗·赫罗特到京
    新华社11日讯　按中捷两国科学院科学合作1960年执行计划来我国作学术访问的捷克斯洛伐克科学院天然物质化学家弗·赫罗特通讯院士在11日乘飞机到达北京。到机场迎接的，有中国科学院数学物理学化学部副主任恽子强等人。
