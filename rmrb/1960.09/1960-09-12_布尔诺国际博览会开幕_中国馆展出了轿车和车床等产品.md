Title: Brno International Trade Fair Opens, Chinese Pavilion Exhibits Cars, Lathes and Other Products
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 10th. The second Brno International Trade Fair opened in Brno on the 10th. This fair features various types of mechanical industrial products from twenty-seven countries. It is estimated that the fair will be visited by 100,000 people each day.

The opening ceremony was held at 10:00 a.m. on the 10th. The head of the Czechoslovak government delegation to the fair, Deputy Prime Minister Barak, delivered a speech at the opening ceremony.

Government delegations from the Soviet Union, China, and other socialist countries attended the opening ceremony. The Chinese government delegation was led by Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia.

The Czechoslovak pavilion displayed more than 3,000 machines and mechanical components, of which more than 280 were new products from the Czechoslovak machine-building industry.

The Soviet pavilion was the largest of all the foreign pavilions, covering more than 6,000 square meters. In the Chinese pavilion, the "Red Flag" sedan and the electronic tube copying vertical lathe produced by Qiqihar No. 1 Machine Tool Factory attracted the attention of visitors. The exhibits in the pavilions of the socialist countries demonstrated the latest technological achievements of the socialist countries' machinery industries.

The fair will close on September 25th.

### 布尔诺国际博览会开幕  中国馆展出了轿车和车床等产品

1960-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔诺国际博览会开幕
    中国馆展出了轿车和车床等产品
    新华社布拉格10日电　第二届布尔诺国际博览会，10日在布尔诺开幕。这次博览会展出了二十七个国家的各种类型的机械工业产品。据估计，每天参观博览会的观众将达十万人。
    10日上午十时，博览会举行了开幕仪式。参加博览会的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团团长、副总理巴拉克在开幕式上致词。
    参加开幕式的有苏联、中国和其他社会主义国家的政府代表团。中国政府代表团是由中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛率领的。
    在博览会的捷克斯洛伐克展览馆中，共展出了三千多台（件）机器和机械，其中有二百八十多台（件）展览品是捷克斯洛伐克机器制造业的新产品。
    在所有外国展览馆中，以苏联展览馆为最大，它占地六千多平方米。在中国展览馆中，红旗牌小轿车和齐齐哈尔第一机床厂生产的电子管仿型立式车床受到了观众的注意。各社会主义国家展览馆中展出的展览品，显示了社会主义国家机械工业的最新技术成就。
    博览会将于9月25日闭幕。
