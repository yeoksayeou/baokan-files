Author:

Page: 6

Advanced Science and Technology of Fraternal Countries

    New Tractor Can Plow, Plant, and Harvest; Carries Heavy Loads, Travels Over Snow, Traverses Off-Road, and Turns Nimbly
    The Kharkov Tractor Factory in the Soviet Union recently produced a “T—90” type universal tractor. This tractor can plow, till, plant, and harvest at high speeds. When equipped with five plows, it can travel five kilometers per hour.
    This tractor is also an excellent transportation machine. When equipped with a special device for eight-and-a-half-ton trailers, it resembles an ordinary truck. Moreover, it can travel in fields without roads, unafraid of mud, snow, or deep ditches. Although this tractor is large, it turns more nimbly than a small car.

### 新型拖拉机能耕能种能收割  载重运输踏雪越野转弯灵便

1960-09-16
第6版()
专栏：兄弟国家的先进科学技术

    新型拖拉机能耕能种能收割  载重运输踏雪越野转弯灵便
    苏联哈尔科夫拖拉机工厂不久以前制成了一种“T—90”型万能拖拉机。这种拖拉机能耕地、整地、播种、收割，速度很高。带五个铧犁时每小时能走五公里。
    这种拖拉机又是极好的运输机器，它装上供八吨半拖车用的专用装置时，很像普通的载重汽车。而且它还可以在没有道路的地里行驶，它不怕泥泞、也不怕风雪，更不怕深沟。这种拖拉机虽然很大，但是转弯时比小汽车还要灵活。
