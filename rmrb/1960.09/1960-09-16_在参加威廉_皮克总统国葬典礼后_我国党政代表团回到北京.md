Title: Our Party and Government Delegation Returns to Beijing After Attending President Wilhelm Pieck's State Funeral
Author:
Date: 1960-09-16
Page: 1

    After attending the state funeral of President Wilhelm Pieck
    Our Party and Government Delegation Returns to Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 15th - Comrade Xi Zhongxun, Head of the Chinese Communist Party and Chinese Government Delegation, Member of the CPC Central Committee, and Vice Premier of the State Council, along with delegation members Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Guoquan, our country's ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after attending the state funeral of Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic. Those who greeted them at the airport included Tan Zhenlin, Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council; Liu Ningyi, Member of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council; Qi Yanming, Zeng Yifan, Zhao Shougong, Gao Dengbang, Deputy Secretaries-General of the State Council; and Han Nianlong, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others.
    Wang Dandier, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, also greeted them at the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th - The Chinese Party and Government Delegation led by Xi Zhongxun, Member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, left Moscow by plane on the evening of September 14th to return home.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included Ignatov, Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, Yelyutin, Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Soviet Union, Pushkin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Popov, Minister of Culture of the Russian Federation, as well as responsible personnel from the CPSU Central Committee and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and staff members of the embassy also saw them off at the airport.
    Prior to this, Comrade Ignatov hosted a banquet in honor of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation.
    The Chinese Party and Government Delegation arrived in Moscow on September 13th from Berlin on its way back home after attending the funeral of President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic.

### 在参加威廉·皮克总统国葬典礼后  我国党政代表团回到北京

1960-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    在参加威廉·皮克总统国葬典礼后
    我国党政代表团回到北京
    新华社15日讯　中国共产党和中国政府代表团团长、中共中央委员、国务院副总理习仲勋，团员外交部副部长姬鹏飞和我国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权，在参加了德意志民主共和国威廉·皮克总统的国葬典礼后，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。到机场迎接的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理谭震林，中共中央委员、国务院外事办公室副主任刘宁一，国务院副秘书长齐燕铭、曾一凡、赵守攻、高登榜，外交部部长助理韩念龙等。
    德意志民主共和国驻中国大使汪戴尔也到机场迎接。
    新华社莫斯科15日电　由中共中央委员、国务院副总理习仲勋率领的中国党政代表团，于9月14日晚离开莫斯科乘飞机回国。
    到机场欢送的有：苏共中央主席团委员伊格纳托夫、苏联高等和中等专业教育部部长叶留金、苏联外交部副部长普希金、俄罗斯联邦文化部部长波波夫以及苏共中央、外交部负责人员。
    到机场欢送的还有中国驻苏联大使刘晓和大使馆的工作人员。
    在这以前，伊格纳托夫同志设宴招待了中国党政代表团。
    中国党政代表团是在参加德意志民主共和国总统皮克葬礼后，于9月13日从柏林回国路经苏联时到达莫斯科的。
