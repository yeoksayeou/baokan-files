Title: Chinese Youth Delegation to Visit Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League, the Chinese Youth Delegation, led by Zhang Yu, head of the delegation, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and secretary of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, departed by train on the afternoon of the 24th for a friendly visit to the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 中国青年代表团去蒙古访问

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

  　中国青年代表团去蒙古访问
    新华社24日讯　应蒙古革命青年团中央的邀请，中国青年代表团一行八人在团长、共青团中央委员、共青团河北省委书记张玉率领下，于24日下午乘车前往蒙古人民共和国进行友好访问。
