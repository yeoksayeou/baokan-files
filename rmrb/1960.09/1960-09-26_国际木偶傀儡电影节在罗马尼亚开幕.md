Title: International Puppet Film Festival Opens in Romania
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, September 24th: The Second International Puppet Film Festival opened here on the 23rd.

Thirty-six puppet films from fourteen countries, including the Soviet Union, China, and Romania, are participating in this film festival. China is participating in this film festival with three films: "The Story of Carving the Dragon," "The Hunting Story," and "Monkey and Turtle Divide the Tree."

The film festival will end on September 30th.

### 国际木偶傀儡电影节在罗马尼亚开幕

1960-09-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　国际木偶傀儡电影节在罗马尼亚开幕
    新华社布加勒斯特24日电　第二届国际木偶、傀儡电影节23日在这里开幕。
    参加这次电影节的有苏联、中国、罗马尼亚等十四个国家的三十六部木偶和傀儡影片。中国参加这次电影节的有“雕龙记”、“打猎记”、“猴龟分树”三部影片。
    电影节将在9月30日结束。
