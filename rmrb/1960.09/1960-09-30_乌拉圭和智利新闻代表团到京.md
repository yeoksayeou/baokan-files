Title: Uruguayan and Chilean Press Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. An Uruguayan journalists' delegation of four, led by Hector Menoni, head of the delegation and Secretary-General of the Uruguayan Journalists' Association, and a Chilean journalists' delegation of five, led by Mario Planete, head of the delegation, leading member of the Chilean Journalists' Association, and director of the Santiago Journalists' Society, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Zhu Muzhi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association and Deputy Director of the Xinhua News Agency, as well as people from the capital's press circles, went to the airport to welcome the guests.

### 乌拉圭和智利新闻代表团到京

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

　  乌拉圭和智利新闻代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　由团长、乌拉圭新闻工作者协会总书记埃克托·梅诺尼率领的乌拉圭新闻工作者代表团一行四人，由团长、智利新闻工作者协会领导成员、圣地亚哥新闻工作者学会理事马里奥·普拉内特率领的智利新闻工作者代表团一行五人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席、新华社副社长朱穆之以及首都新闻界人士前往机场迎接客人。
