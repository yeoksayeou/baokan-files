Title: Plovdiv Fair Opens
Author:
Date: 1960-09-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, September 19th: The 19th Plovdiv International Fair opened on the 18th in Plovdiv, Bulgaria's second largest city. Bulgarian party and government leaders Ga? Fu, Yu Gov, and others attended the opening ceremony.

Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Member and Minister of Foreign Trade, Kumbiliev, spoke at the opening ceremony. He warmly congratulated the opening of the fair on behalf of the Bulgarian government.

A total of twenty-four countries are participating in the exhibition, including eight socialist countries such as Bulgaria, the Soviet Union, China, Hungary, Poland, the German Democratic Republic, Romania, and Czechoslovakia, as well as Austria, Britain, Italy, the United States, France, and West Germany.

### 普罗夫迪夫博览会开幕

1960-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    普罗夫迪夫博览会开幕
    新华社索非亚19日电  第十九次普罗夫迪夫国际博览会，18日在保加利亚第二大城市普罗夫迪夫开幕。保加利亚党政领导人加?夫、于哥夫等出席了开幕式。
    保共中央委员、对外贸易部长库姆比利耶夫在开幕式上讲了话，他代表保加利亚政府热烈祝贺博览会开幕。
    参加这次博览会展出的共有二十四个国家，其中包括保加利亚、苏联、中国、匈牙利、波兰、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克等八个社会主义国家，以及奥地利、英国、意大利、美国、法国和西德等。
