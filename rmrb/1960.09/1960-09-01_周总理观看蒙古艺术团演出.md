Title: Premier Zhou Watches Performance by Mongolian Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1960-09-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 31st. Premier Zhou Enlai watched the performance of the Kobdo Provincial Music and Drama Theatre Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic at the Great Hall of the People tonight. During the intermission, he met with the head of the delegation, Guulagcha, and other leaders and principal actors of the art troupe, and had a cordial conversation with them.

　　The evening's performance began with the art troupe's chorus of the Mongolian folk song "Ode to Altai," featuring a total of fifteen programs including folk songs, choruses, and dances, which were warmly welcomed by the audience.

　　Tudev, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy in China, also watched the performance tonight.

　　Zhang Zhixiang, Xia Yan, Chu Tunan, Qu Wu and others also watched the performance.

　　After the performance, Premier Zhou stepped onto the stage, warmly shook hands with the Mongolian artists, and congratulated them on the success of their performance. (Attached picture)
　　On the evening of August 31st, Premier Zhou Enlai watched the performance of the Kobdo Provincial Music and Drama Theatre Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic at the Great Hall of the People. The photo shows Premier Zhou Enlai with the performers after the performance.　Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

### 周总理观看蒙古艺术团演出

1960-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理观看蒙古艺术团演出
    新华社31日讯　周恩来总理今晚观看了蒙古人民共和国科布多省音乐话剧院艺术团在人民大会堂的演出，并且在演出休息期间接见了古尔拉格查团长等领导人和艺术团的主要演员，和他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    今晚演出的节目以艺术团的合唱蒙古民歌“阿尔泰颂”开始，共演出民歌、合唱、舞蹈等十五个节目，受到观众热烈的欢迎。
    蒙古驻中国大使馆临时代办托依夫也观看了今晚的演出。
    观看演出的还有张致祥、夏衍、楚图南、屈武等。
    演出结束后，周总理步上舞台，同蒙古艺术家们亲切握手，祝贺他们演出的成功。（附图片）
