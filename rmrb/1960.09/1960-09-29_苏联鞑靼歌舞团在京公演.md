Title: Soviet Tatar Song and Dance Ensemble Performs in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th: The Soviet Tatar Autonomous Republic State Song and Dance Ensemble, which left Beijing a month ago for a performance tour of various regions, returned to Beijing on the 27th. On the 28th, they continued their performances at the Tianqiao Theater and hosted cultural attachés from various foreign embassies in China. Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Xu Pingyu, Deputy Minister of Culture, and Sudarikov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, watched the performance. After the performance, they went on stage to shake hands with the performers and congratulate them on their success.

The State Song and Dance Ensemble of the Soviet Tatar Autonomous Republic came to China in August for a performance tour at the invitation of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. They were warmly welcomed by audiences in Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanjing, Tianjin and other cities.

The actors of the State Song and Dance Ensemble of the Soviet Tatar Autonomous Republic will celebrate China's National Day with the Chinese people in Beijing, and then go to Shenyang, Harbin and other places to continue their performance tour.

### 苏联鞑靼歌舞团在京公演

1960-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联鞑靼歌舞团在京公演
    新华社北京28日讯　一月前离京赴各地访问演出的苏联鞑靼自治共和国国家歌舞团，已于27日回到北京，28日在天桥剧场继续公演，并招待各国驻华使馆文化专员。对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊、文化部副部长徐平羽和苏联驻华大使馆临时代办苏达利柯夫观看了演出，并在演出结束后，上台与演员们握手，祝贺演出成功。
    苏联鞑靼自治共和国国家歌舞团应我国对外文化联络委员会邀请，在8月来我国进行访问演出。他们在北京、上海、杭州、南京、天津等城市受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    苏联鞑靼自治共和国国家歌舞团的演员们，将在北京与我国人民一起欢度我国国庆节，然后去沈阳、哈尔滨等地继续进行访问演出。
