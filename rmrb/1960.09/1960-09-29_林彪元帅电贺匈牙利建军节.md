Title: Marshal Lin Biao Sends Congratulatory Telegram on Hungarian Army Day
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, sent a telegram to Lieutenant General Czinege Lajos, Minister of National Defense of the Hungarian People's Republic, congratulating him on the Hungarian People's Army Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Lieutenant General Czinege Lajos, Minister of National Defense of the Hungarian People's Republic,

Respected Minister Comrade:

On the occasion of the Hungarian People's Army Day, on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and in my own name, I extend warm and fraternal congratulations to you and all officers and soldiers of the Hungarian People's Army.

The Hungarian People's Army has made remarkable achievements in political education and military training, and in safeguarding the socialist construction of the Hungarian working people. I wish the Hungarian People's Army will continuously enhance its combat effectiveness and make new contributions in the glorious struggle to defend the peaceful labor of the people, combat imperialist aggression and war conspiracies, and defend peace in Europe and the world.

May the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the people and armies of China and Hungary last forever.

　　Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
　　Marshal of the People's Republic of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 27, 1960, in Beijing

### 林彪元帅电贺匈牙利建军节

1960-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　林彪元帅电贺匈牙利建军节
    新华社28日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅致电匈牙利人民共和国国防部长齐奈盖·拉约什中将，祝贺匈牙利人民军建军节。电文如下：匈牙利人民共和国国防部长齐奈盖·拉约什中将同志敬爱的部长同志：
    欣逢匈牙利人民军建军节，我代表中国人民解放军全体指战员，并以我个人名义，向您和匈牙利人民军全体官兵致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    匈牙利人民军在政治教育和军事训练工作中，在保卫匈牙利劳动人民进行社会主义建设的安全方面，已经取得了显著成就。祝匈牙利人民军不断地提高自己的战斗威力，在保卫人民的和平劳动、打击帝国主义的侵略和战争阴谋、捍卫欧洲与世界和平的光荣斗争中作出新的贡献。
    祝中、匈两国人民和军队之间牢不可破的兄弟友谊万古常青。
　　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长
　　中华人民共和国元帅　林彪
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　1960年9月27日于北京
