Title: President Pieck's State Funeral Ceremony to be Held Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1960-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 8th -- The Funeral Committee for Comrade Wilhelm Pieck announced that President Pieck's state funeral ceremony will be held on the afternoon of September 10th.

President Pieck's remains will lie in state at the Central Committee office building of the Socialist Unity Party on September 10th, so that the people of the whole country can pay their respects.

A public memorial service will be held at the Central Committee office building of the Socialist Unity Party from 2:00 PM to 3:00 PM (Berlin time) on the 10th. After the public memorial service, President Pieck's hearse will pass through the main streets of Berlin's democratic district and be sent to the Baumschulenweg Crematorium.

A memorial ceremony will be held at the crematorium at 5:00 PM (Berlin time) on the 10th. Memorial ceremonies will also be held throughout the country at the same time.

### 皮克总统国葬典礼明日举行

1960-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    皮克总统国葬典礼明日举行
    新华社柏林8日电　威廉·皮克同志治丧委员会宣布，皮克总统的国葬典礼将在9月10日下午举行。
    皮克总统的遗体9月10日将停放在统一社会党中央委员会办公大厦，以便全国人民瞻仰。
    10日下午二时至三时（柏林时间）在统一社会党中央办公大厦举行公祭。公祭后，皮克总统的灵车将经过柏林民主区主要街道，送达鲍姆舒伦韦格火葬场。
    10日下午五时（柏林时间）将在火葬场举行追悼仪式。全国各地也将在同时举行追悼仪式。
