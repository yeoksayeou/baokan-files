Title: Japan-China Friendship Association Permanent Directors Yoshiada Hohei and Others Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th - Yoshiada Hohei and Hasegawa Toshizo, permanent directors of the Japan-China Friendship Association, left Beijing by plane at noon today to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Lin Lin, Deputy Secretary-General, and Yang Chunsong, Director.

### 日中友协常任理事吉田法晴等回国

1960-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

　  日中友协常任理事吉田法晴等回国
    新华社20日讯　日中友好协会常任理事吉田法晴、长谷川敏三，今天中午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，副秘书长林林，理事杨春松等。
