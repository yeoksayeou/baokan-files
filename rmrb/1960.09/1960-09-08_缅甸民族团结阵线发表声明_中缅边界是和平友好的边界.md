Title: The Burmese National United Front Issues a Statement: The Sino-Burmese Border is a Border of Peace and Friendship
Author:
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 6th. The Burmese National United Front issued a statement today, warmly welcoming Prime Minister U Nu's speech in the Chamber of Deputies on August 22 concerning the progress of the Sino-Burmese border talks. The statement pointed out that the Sino-Burmese border issue was a problem left over by imperialism with malicious intentions.

The statement said that, however, the Sino-Burmese border issue has been peacefully resolved, and no unpleasant incidents have occurred. Now the Sino-Burmese border has become a border of peace and friendship. The statement said, "This is a great victory for the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence."

The statement highly praised the sincerity and spirit of mutual cooperation shown by the leaders of China and Burma in resolving the Sino-Burmese border issue.

### 缅甸民族团结阵线发表声明  中缅边界是和平友好的边界

1960-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸民族团结阵线发表声明
    中缅边界是和平友好的边界
    新华社仰光6日电　缅甸民族团结阵线今天发表声明，热烈欢迎8月22日吴努总理在代表院就中缅边界会谈的进展发表的讲话。声明指出，中缅边界问题是帝国主义怀着恶毒的阴谋遗留下来的问题。
    声明说，可是，中缅边界问题已经得到和平解决，并没有发生任何不愉快的事件。现在中缅边界已经变成了和平和友好的边界。声明说，“这是和平共处五项原则的巨大胜利。”
    声明盛赞中缅两国的领袖们在解决中缅边界问题上所表现的真诚和相互合作的精神。
