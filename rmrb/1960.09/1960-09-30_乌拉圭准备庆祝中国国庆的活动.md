Title: Uruguay Prepares Activities to Celebrate China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, September 29th: The Uruguay-China Cultural Association has drafted a plan to hold activities celebrating the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

　　According to this plan, a photo exhibition introducing China will open on October 1st at the Uruguay-China Cultural Association office. On the 2nd, a cultural conference will be held, where the president of the Uruguay-China Cultural Association, the writer Sarandy Cabrera, will give a report on the 11th anniversary of China's founding. During this conference, the Chinese film "Song Jingshi" will also be screened. On the 11th, Dr. Magdalena Sueiro of the Cultural Association will give a report introducing various aspects of the new China.

　　The president of the Uruguayan Journalists Association, Carlos Borche, and the Secretary of Foreign Relations, Rodriguez Bella, will also hold report meetings to talk about their impressions of their recent visits to China. Borche has given a lengthy report on the new rural areas of China, praising the cause of the People's Communes.

　　Rodriguez Bella, a parliamentary reporter for the Uruguayan government newspaper "Debate," has also written articles introducing the great country of China.

　　The color film exhibition on China's celebration of the 10th anniversary of its founding has ended. This film exhibition aroused great interest among the Uruguayan public.

### 乌拉圭准备庆祝中国国庆的活动

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

　　乌拉圭准备庆祝中国国庆的活动
    新华社29日讯　蒙得维的亚消息：乌拉圭—中国文化协会已经拟定举行庆祝中华人民共和国建国十一周年的活动的计划。
    根据这项计划，一个介绍中国的图片展览会将在10月1日在乌拉圭—中国文化协会办事处开幕。2日，将举行一次文化大会，由乌拉圭—中国文化协会主席、作家萨兰达·卡布雷拉做关于中国建国十一周年的报告。在这次大会上，还将放映中国电影“宋景诗”。11日，将由文化协会的马格达莱娜·苏埃罗博士做介绍新中国各方面情况的报告。
    乌拉圭新闻工作者协会主席卡洛斯·博尔切和对外关系秘书罗德里格斯·贝拉也将举行报告会，谈他们最近访问中国的观感。博尔切曾经做过关于中国新农村情况的长篇报告，盛赞人民公社的事业。
    乌拉圭政府机关报“辩论报”的议会记者罗德里格斯·贝拉也写了介绍伟大的国家——中国的文章。
    中国庆祝建国十周年情况的彩色电影展览已经结束。这次电影展览在乌拉圭公众中引起巨大兴趣。
