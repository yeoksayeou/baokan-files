Title: Cyrankiewicz Visits Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, September 27th: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, Józef Cyrankiewicz, arrived here by plane this afternoon for a state visit. He was accompanied by his wife and a party of fourteen people. Greeting the Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers at the airport were the Prime Minister of Ceylon, Mrs. Sirimavo Bandaranaike, cabinet ministers, the Chief Justice, members of parliament, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Ceylon. Cyrankiewicz made a brief speech at the airport. He was then accompanied by the Prime Minister of Ceylon in a car to the Governor-General's residence. He was welcomed by large crowds along the way. The welcoming crowds cheered the distinguished guests and shouted slogans in support of friendship between Ceylon and Poland.

### 西伦凯维兹到锡兰访问

1960-09-29
第7版()
专栏：

　　西伦凯维兹到锡兰访问
　　新华社科伦坡27日电　波兰人民共和国部长会议主席西伦凯维兹今天下午乘飞机到达这里进行国事访问。同他一起来访问的有他的夫人等一行十四人。前往机场欢迎波兰部长会议主席的有锡兰总理西丽马沃·班达拉奈克夫人，内阁部长们，首席法官，议会议员们以及各国驻锡兰外交使节等。西伦凯维兹在机场发表了简短的讲话。然后他由锡兰总理陪同乘车前往总督官邸。沿途受到大批群众的欢迎。欢迎的人向贵宾们欢呼并高呼拥护锡兰和波兰友好的口号。
