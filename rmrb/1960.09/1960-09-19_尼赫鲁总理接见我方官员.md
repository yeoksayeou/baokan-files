Title: Prime Minister Nehru Receives Our Officials
Author:
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 18th. Indian Prime Minister Nehru received Chinese officials who were participating in the Sino-Indian officials' meeting this afternoon: Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yang Gongsu, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and others. Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to India, was also present. Also present at the meeting were Meda, Director of the Northern Division of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Gopal, Director of the Historical Division, and others.

### 尼赫鲁总理接见我方官员

1960-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理接见我方官员
    新华社18日讯　新德里消息：印度总理尼赫鲁今天下午接见了参加中印官员会晤的中国官员：外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋、西藏自治区筹备委员会外事处处长杨公素等人。中国驻印度大使潘自力也在座。接见时在座的，有印度外交部北方司司长梅达，历史司司长戈帕尔等人。
