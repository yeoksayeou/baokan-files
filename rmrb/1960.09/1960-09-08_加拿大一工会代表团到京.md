Title: Canadian Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. A five-member delegation from the United Electrical, Radio and Machine Workers of Canada, led by the head of the delegation, Ross Russell, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a friendly visit to our country.

    Huang Minwei, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Water Conservancy and Electric Power Trade Union, and Yu Huan, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union, were among those who welcomed them at the airport.

### 加拿大一工会代表团到京

1960-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    加拿大一工会代表团到京
    新华社7日讯　加拿大联合电气、无线电、机械工人工会代表团一行五人，在团长罗斯·鲁塞尔率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团将在我国进行友好访问。
    到机场欢迎的有中国水利电力工会全国委员会主席黄民伟和中国机械工会全国委员会副主席于桓等人。
