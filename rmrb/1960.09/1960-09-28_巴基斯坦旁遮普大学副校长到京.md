Title: Vice-Chancellor of Pakistan's Punjab University Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 27th — At the invitation of Peking University, the Vice-Chancellor of Pakistan's Punjab University, Karamat, and his wife arrived in Beijing by train today. Greeting them at the station were Yan Rengeng, Assistant to the President of Peking University, and Cui Xiongkun, Dean of Studies.
　　
That evening, Lu Ping, President of Peking University, hosted a banquet to welcome the guests.

### 巴基斯坦旁遮普大学副校长到京

1960-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

　  巴基斯坦旁遮普大学副校长到京
    新华社27日讯　巴基斯坦旁遮普大学副校长克拉马特及夫人应北京大学邀请，今天乘火车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有北京大学校长助理严仁赓和教务长崔雄昆等。
    当晚，北京大学校长陆平设宴欢迎客人。
