Title: German Ambassador to China, Wang Dair, Sends Letter to Xinhua News Agency, Thanking Our People for Mourning President Pieck
Author:
Date: 1960-09-11
Page: 5

    German Ambassador to China, Wang Dair, Sends Letter to Xinhua News Agency
    Thanking Our People for Mourning President Pieck
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Xinhua News Agency received a letter today from the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China. Paul Wandel, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, expressed his sincere gratitude in the letter for the deep condolences expressed by the people of our country on the death of President Pieck.
    The full text of the letter is as follows:
    "At the time of the death of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, the working people of the People's Republic of China, the organizations of the Chinese Communist Party, the democratic parties and mass organizations expressed their deep condolences to the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic for the heavy losses suffered by the working people of the German Democratic Republic, the entire German people, and the international workers' movement. As it is impossible to thank all individuals and units one by one, Paul Wandel, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to China, hereby expresses his sincere gratitude to all units and individuals who came to pay their respects and expressed their deep condolences through letters and telegrams."

### 德驻华大使汪戴尔致函新华社  感谢我国人民哀悼皮克总统

1960-09-11
第5版()
专栏：

    德驻华大使汪戴尔致函新华社
    感谢我国人民哀悼皮克总统
    新华社10日讯　新华通讯社今天收到了德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆的一封信。德意志民主共和国驻华大使保尔·汪戴尔在信中对我国人民对皮克总统的逝世所表示的沉痛哀悼，表示真诚的感谢。
    这一封信的全文如下：
    “在德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克同志逝世的时候，中华人民共和国的劳动人民、中国共产党的组织、各民主党派和群众团体对德意志民主共和国劳动人民、全德人民以及国际工人运动所遭到的沉重损失向德意志民主共和国大使馆表示了深切的哀悼。由于不可能向所有个人、单位一一致谢，德意志民主共和国特命全权驻华大使保尔·汪戴尔特在此向所有前来吊唁以及通过书信电报表示沉痛哀悼的单位和个人表示真诚的感谢。”
