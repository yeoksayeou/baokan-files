Title: Marshal Lin Biao Sends Greetings on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Bulgarian People's Army
Author:
Date: 1960-09-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd. Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, today sent a telegram to General Mikhailov, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, congratulating him on the anniversary of the founding of the Bulgarian People's Army. The telegram reads as follows:

Respected Comrade General Mikhailov, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Bulgaria:

　　On the eve of the anniversary of the founding of the Bulgarian People's Army, please accept the most sincere congratulations from all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and from myself personally.

　　I wish the Bulgarian People's Army new and significant achievements in strengthening political education and military training, constantly improving its combat effectiveness, so as to ensure the safety of the Bulgarian working people in carrying out socialist construction, and making greater contributions in the struggle against imperialism and in defense of world peace.

　　I wish the fraternal friendship between the people and armies of China and Bulgaria will be strengthened day by day.

　　Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Marshal of the People's Republic of China

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 22, 1960, in Beijing

### 林彪元帅电贺保加利亚人民军建军节

1960-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　林彪元帅电贺保加利亚人民军建军节
    新华社22日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅今日致电保加利亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国防部长米哈依洛夫大将，祝贺保加利亚人民军建军节。电文如下：保加利亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国防部长米哈依洛夫大将敬爱的大将同志：
    在保加利亚人民军建军节的前夕，请接受中国人民解放军全体指战员和我个人的最真诚的祝贺。
    祝保加利亚人民军在加强政治教育和军事训练方面取得新的重大的成就，不断提高自己的战斗威力，以保障保加利亚劳动人民进行社会主义建设的安全，并在反对帝国主义、保卫世界和平的斗争中做出更多的贡献。
  祝中保两国人民和军队之间的兄弟友谊日益增强。
　　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长
　　中华人民共和国元帅　林彪
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　1960年9月22日于北京
