Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visit to Hungary
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Chinese Women's Delegation, led by Li Baoguang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, a group of four people, left Beijing by plane today for a visit to the Hungarian People's Republic at the invitation of the Hungarian National Council of Women and the Hungarian "Women" magazine.

### 我国妇女代表团离京去匈访问

1960-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    我国妇女代表团离京去匈访问
    新华社11日讯　由全国妇联书记处书记李宝光率领的中国妇女代表团一行四人，应匈牙利妇女全国理事会和匈牙利“妇女”杂志社的邀请，今天乘飞机离开北京去匈牙利人民共和国访问。
