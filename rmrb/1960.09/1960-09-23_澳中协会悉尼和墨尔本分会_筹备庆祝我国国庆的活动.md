Title: Australian-Chinese Association Sydney and Melbourne Branches Prepare for Activities to Celebrate China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-09-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd, Sydney: The Sydney and Melbourne branches of the Australian-Chinese Association are preparing to celebrate China's National Day.

Sydney will hold a three-day celebration from September 28th to 30th, featuring film screenings and concerts, exhibitions of Chinese toys and paintings, and reports introducing China's construction and development.

Melbourne's celebration activities include a dinner party, screenings of films about New China, and a Chinese arts and crafts exhibition on September 24th.

### 澳中协会悉尼和墨尔本分会　筹备庆祝我国国庆的活动

1960-09-23
第6版()
专栏：

　  澳中协会悉尼和墨尔本分会
　　筹备庆祝我国国庆的活动
    新华社22日讯　悉尼消息：澳中协会悉尼和墨尔本分会筹备庆祝中国国庆。
    悉尼将从9月28日到30日举行为时三天的庆祝活动，放映电影和举行音乐会，展览中国玩具和绘画以及举行介绍中国建设发展情况的报告会。
    墨尔本的庆祝活动包括聚餐会、放映关于新中国的电影，并在9月24日举办中国工艺美术品展览会。
