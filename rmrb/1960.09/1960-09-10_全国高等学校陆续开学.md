Title: National Universities and Colleges Commence Classes Successively
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: National universities and colleges have successively commenced classes in early September. After sufficient rest and a certain amount of physical labor during the summer vacation, the vast number of teachers and students are welcoming the tasks of the new academic year with full vigor.

According to the arrangements of universities and colleges in various regions, in the new academic year, all schools will further strengthen political and ideological education, strengthen physical labor, strengthen the teaching of basic theories, and further improve the quality of teaching. In arranging all these tasks, all schools have paid attention to combining work and rest, and according to the requirements of these tasks, they will conduct in-depth teaching reforms in the new academic year, vigorously carry out scientific research, and further implement the policy of combining teaching, productive labor, and scientific research.

Before the start of the new semester, universities and colleges in various regions also studied how to promote the spirit of hard work, running schools diligently and thriftily, and running all undertakings diligently and thriftily in schools, according to the requirements of carrying out the production increase and conservation campaign centered on grain and steel, and decided to further carry out activities to support agriculture and industry in the new academic year. Many universities and colleges in Beijing, Shanghai, Shenyang, Guangdong, Jiangsu, and other provinces and cities will further adopt the method of long-term and comprehensive linkage with communes in the new academic year to increase the proportion of agricultural research projects. Universities and colleges in various regions have also appropriately arranged labor time and combined productive labor to organize teachers and students to vigorously support agriculture.

### 全国高等学校陆续开学

1960-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    全国高等学校陆续开学
    据新华社9日讯　全国高等学校已于9月上旬陆续开学。广大师生经过暑期足够的休息和一定的劳动锻炼，正以饱满的精力迎接新学年的任务。
    根据各地高等学校的安排，新学年中，各校将进一步加强政治思想教育，加强劳动锻炼，加强基础理论教学，进一步提高教学质量。各校在安排这一切工作时，都注意了劳逸结合，并根据这些工作的要求，在新学年中将深入进行教学改革，大力开展科学研究，进一步贯彻教学、生产劳动和科学研究三结合的方针。
    开学前，各地高等学校还根据开展以粮、钢为中心的增产节约运动的要求，研究了如何在学校中发扬艰苦奋斗、勤俭办校、勤俭办一切事业的精神，并决定新学年中进一步开展支援农业、工业的活动。北京、上海、沈阳、广东、江苏等省、市的许多高等学校，在新学年中将进一步采取与公社长期、全面挂钩的办法，增加农业研究项目的比重。各地高等学校还适当安排了劳动时间，结合生产劳动，组织师生大力支援农业。
