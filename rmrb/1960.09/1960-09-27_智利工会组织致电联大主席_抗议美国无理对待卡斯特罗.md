Title: Chilean Trade Union Organization Calls UN General Assembly President to Protest US's Unreasonable Treatment of Castro
Author:
Date: 1960-09-27
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: According to Latin American News Agency reports, the Chilean Workers' United Trade Union recently sent a telegram to the President of the Fifteenth United Nations General Assembly, strongly protesting the unreasonable treatment of Cuban Prime Minister Castro by the US authorities when he attended the United Nations General Assembly. The telegram pointed out that Chilean workers support the aims of the Cuban Revolution and the position of the Cuban government in the United Nations General Assembly. The Chilean National Movement for Support and Defense of the Cuban Revolution also sent a similar telegram to the United Nations General Assembly.

### 智利工会组织致电联大主席  抗议美国无理对待卡斯特罗

1960-09-27
第5版()
专栏：

　  智利工会组织致电联大主席
　  抗议美国无理对待卡斯特罗
    新华社24日讯　据拉丁美洲通讯社报道，智利工人统一工会近日致电第十五届联合国大会主席，强烈抗议美国当局对出席联合国大会的古巴总理卡斯特罗的无理对待。电报指出，智利工人支持古巴革命的宗旨以及古巴政府在联合国大会上的立场。智利全国支援和保卫古巴革命运动也给联合国大会打了一份类似的电报。
