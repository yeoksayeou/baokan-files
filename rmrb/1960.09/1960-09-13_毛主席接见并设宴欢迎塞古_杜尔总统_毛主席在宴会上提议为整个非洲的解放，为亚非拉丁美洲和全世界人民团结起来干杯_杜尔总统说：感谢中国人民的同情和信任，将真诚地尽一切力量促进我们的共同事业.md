Title: Chairman Mao Meets and Hosts Banquet in Honor of President Sékou Touré; Chairman Mao Proposes a Toast at the Banquet to the Liberation of All of Africa, and to the Unity of the People of Asia, Africa, Latin America and the World
Author:
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received Sékou Touré, President of the Republic of Guinea and Secretary-General of the Democratic Party of Guinea, Diallo Saifoulaye, Speaker of the National Assembly of Guinea and Political Secretary of the Democratic Party of Guinea, Louis Lansana Béavogui, Minister of National Economy and Member of the Political Bureau of the Democratic Party of Guinea, Keita N'Famara, Minister of Planning and Member of the Political Bureau of the Democratic Party of Guinea, and accompanying members of the delegation of the Government of the Republic of Guinea at the Qinzheng Hall in Zhongnanhai this evening. Chairman Mao Zedong held a cordial conversation with the distinguished guests.

Present at the reception were: Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De, Vice-Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, Peng Zhen and Chen Yi, members of the Political Bureau, Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Zhixiang, Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Ke Hua, Chinese Ambassador to Guinea.

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, held a banquet at the Qinzheng Hall in Zhongnanhai this evening to welcome Sékou Touré, President of the Republic of Guinea and Secretary-General of the Democratic Party of Guinea, and all the distinguished guests of the Government Delegation of the Republic of Guinea.

Diallo Saifoulaye, Speaker of the National Assembly of Guinea and Political Secretary of the Democratic Party of Guinea, Louis Lansana Béavogui, Minister of National Economy and Member of the Political Bureau of the Democratic Party of Guinea, Keita N'Famara, Minister of Planning and Member of the Political Bureau of the Democratic Party of Guinea, and the accompanying members of the delegation were all invited to the banquet.

Present at the banquet were: Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De, Vice-Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, and Tan Zhenlin, members of the Political Bureau, Lu Dingyi and Bo Yibo, alternate members of the Political Bureau, Liu Lantao and Yang Shangkun, alternate members of the Secretariat, An Ziwen, Minister of the Organization Department of the CPC Central Committee, and Ma Mingfang, Minister of the Finance and Trade Work Department.

Also present at the banquet were: Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Zhixiang, Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Ke Hua, our country's Ambassador to Guinea.

Chairman Mao Zedong raised his glass at the banquet and proposed a toast to the health of President Sékou Touré, to the liberation of all of Africa, and to the unity of the people of Asia, Africa, Latin America, and all countries of the world.

President Touré delivered a speech at the banquet. He said that we express our respect to the Chinese people through the wise leader of the Chinese people. Thank you to the Chinese people for their spontaneous sympathy and trust in the Guinean people. We assure the leaders of the Chinese people that our government and party will sincerely do everything in our power to promote our common cause. President Touré raised his glass to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and all national leaders of China, to the prosperity of the Chinese people and to the progress of mankind in the world.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly and cordial atmosphere. (Photo attached)

### 毛主席接见并设宴欢迎塞古·杜尔总统  毛主席在宴会上提议为整个非洲的解放，为亚非拉丁美洲和全世界人民团结起来干杯  杜尔总统说：感谢中国人民的同情和信任，将真诚地尽一切力量促进我们的共同事业

1960-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见并设宴欢迎塞古·杜尔总统
    毛主席在宴会上提议为整个非洲的解放，为亚非拉丁美洲和全世界人民团结起来干杯
    杜尔总统说：感谢中国人民的同情和信任，将真诚地尽一切力量促进我们的共同事业
    新华社12日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东今晚在中南海勤政殿接见了几内亚共和国总统、几内亚民主党总书记塞古·杜尔和几内亚国民议会议长、几内亚民主党政治书记迪阿洛·塞福拉耶，国民经济部长、几内亚民主党政治局委员路易·兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉，计划部长、几内亚民主党政治局委员凯塔·恩法马拉和几内亚共和国政府代表团的随行人员。毛泽东主席同贵宾们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的有：中共中央副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德，总书记邓小平，政治局委员彭真、陈毅，中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外文化联络委员会代主任张致祥，中国驻几内亚大使柯华等。
    新华社12日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东今晚在中南海勤政殿举行宴会，欢迎几内亚共和国总统、几内亚民主党总书记塞古·杜尔和几内亚共和国政府代表团全体贵宾。
    几内亚国民议会议长、几内亚民主党政治书记迪阿洛·塞福拉耶，国民经济部长、几内亚民主党政治局委员路易·兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉，计划部长、几内亚民主党政治局委员凯塔·恩法马拉和代表团的随行人员都应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的有：中共中央副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德，总书记邓小平，政治局委员彭真、陈毅、李先念、谭震林，政治局候补委员陆定一、薄一波，书记处候补书记刘澜涛、杨尚昆，中共中央组织部部长安子文、财政贸易工作部部长马明方。
    出席宴会作陪的还有：中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外文化联络委员会代主任张致祥，我国驻几内亚大使柯华等。
    毛泽东主席在宴会上举杯致词，提议为塞古·杜尔总统阁下的健康，为整个非洲的解放，为亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲和全世界各国人民团结起来干杯。
    杜尔总统在宴会上致词。他说，我们通过中国人民英明的领袖向中国人民表示敬意。感谢中国人民对几内亚人民自发的同情和信任。我们向中国人民的领导者保证：我国政府和党将真诚地尽一切力量来促进我们的共同事业。杜尔总统举杯祝毛泽东主席和中国所有国家领导人的健康，祝中国人民的繁荣和世界人类的进步。
    宴会在友好和亲切的气氛中进行。（附图片）
