Title: Mountaineering Heroes Wang Fuzhou and Qu Yinhua Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 21st: Mountaineering heroes Wang Fuzhou and Qu Yinhua, who conquered the world's highest peak, Mount Everest, arrived in Beijing by plane from Lhasa on the 20th. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Huang Zhong, Vice Minister of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, Li Shubin, Chairman of the Chinese Mountaineering Association, Shi Zhanchun, Captain of the Chinese Mountaineering Team, and representatives from the Beijing Institute of Geology and the China National Forestry Union.
　　Wang Fuzhou, a graduate of the Beijing Institute of Geology, and Qu Yinhua, a forestry worker from Sichuan, along with their close comrade, Tibetan mountaineer Gonbu, successfully reached the summit of Mount Everest, at an altitude of 8,882 meters above sea level, at 4:20 a.m. Beijing time on May 25th of this year, making a great contribution to the motherland. However, both of them suffered frostbite to their hands and feet during the ascent, and therefore remained in Lhasa for recuperation after the mountaineering expedition. Now, their health has greatly improved.

### 登山英雄王富洲屈银华到京

1960-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　登山英雄王富洲屈银华到京
    新华社21日讯　征服世界最高峰——珠穆朗玛峰的登山英雄王富洲和屈银华，20日由拉萨乘飞机到达北京。他们在飞机场上受到国家体委副主任黄中、中国登山协会主席栗树彬、中国登山队队长史占春，以及北京地质学院和中国林业工会的代表们的热烈欢迎。
    北京地质学院毕业生王富洲、四川林业工人屈银华，今年5月25日晨北京时间四点二十分曾经同亲密的战友——藏族登山队员贡布一起，登上了拔海八千八百八十二米的珠穆朗玛峰顶峰，为祖国创立了功勋。但他们两人在登山过程中，手、脚遭到冻伤，因而在登山活动结束后一直留在拉萨疗养。现在，他们的健康情况已经大大好转。
