Title: Our Delegation Travels to Berlin to Attend President Pieck's State Funeral

Author:

Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th September: A Chinese Communist Party and Chinese Government delegation left Beijing by plane this noon for Berlin to attend the state funeral of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic.

The head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation is Xi Zhongxun, member of the Central Committee of the CPC and Vice Premier of the State Council. The members are Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Guoquan, our country's ambassador to the German Democratic Republic (already in Berlin).

Those who saw the delegation off at the airport were Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the CPC and Vice Premier of the State Council, Liu Lantao, alternate secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the CPC, Saifuddin, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Li Dequan, Minister of Health, General Yang Yong of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Li Jiebo, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

Wang Deier, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and Li Yonghao, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, also saw the delegation off at the airport.

### 参加皮克总统国葬典礼  我代表团赴柏林

1960-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    参加皮克总统国葬典礼
    我代表团赴柏林
    新华社9日讯　参加德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克同志国葬典礼的中国共产党和中国政府代表团，今天中午乘飞机离开北京前往柏林。
    中国党政代表团的团长是中共中央委员、国务院副总理习仲勋，团员有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，我国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权（已在柏林）。
    到飞机场送行的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅，中共中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长赛福鼎，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，外交部副部长罗贵波，卫生部部长李德全，中国人民解放军杨勇上将，对外文委副主任屈武，对外贸易部副部长林海云，中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯等。
    德意志民主共和国驻中国大使汪戴尔，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐，也到飞机场送行。
