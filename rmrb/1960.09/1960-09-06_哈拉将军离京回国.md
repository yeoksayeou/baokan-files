Title: General Jara Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 5th. General Heriberto Jara, a renowned Mexican social activist and Chairman of the Mexican National Peace Council, who was invited to visit our country by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, concluded his tour and visit in China and left Beijing by plane today to return home.

　　Among those who went to the airport to see him off were Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretary-General, and others.

### 哈拉将军离京回国

1960-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    哈拉将军离京回国
    新华社5日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国访问的墨西哥著名社会活动家、墨西哥全国和平理事会主席埃里维尔托·哈拉将军，结束了在我国的参观访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    前往机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席包尔汉、副秘书长王明远等人。
