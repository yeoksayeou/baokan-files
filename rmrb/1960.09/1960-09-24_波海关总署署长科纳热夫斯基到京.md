Title: Polish Customs Director Konarzewski Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: Konarzewski, Director of the Polish Customs Administration, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China at the invitation of Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade and Director of the General Administration of Customs.

Hu Renkui and Yin Zhiyue, Deputy Directors of the General Administration of Customs of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, were at the airport to welcome him. Mirowski, Acting Commercial Counselor of the Commercial Office of the Polish Embassy in China, was also at the airport to greet him.

### 波海关总署署长科纳热夫斯基到京

1960-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

　  波海关总署署长科纳热夫斯基到京
    新华社23日讯　应对外贸易部副部长兼海关总署署长林海云邀请来我国访问的波兰海关总署署长科纳热夫斯基，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    对外贸易部海关总署副署长胡仁奎、殷之钺等到机场欢迎。波兰驻华大使馆商务处代理商务参赞米罗夫斯基也到机场迎接。
