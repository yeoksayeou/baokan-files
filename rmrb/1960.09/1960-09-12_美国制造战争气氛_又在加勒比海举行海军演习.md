Title: The United States Creates an Atmosphere of War; Conducts Naval Exercises in the Caribbean Sea Again
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 9th September: According to the United States Information Service in Caracas on the 7th, the "Joint Naval Exercises," planned by the United States since the beginning of June with the aim of creating an atmosphere of war and strengthening military control over Latin America, began on August 29 in the Caribbean Sea.

According to the planned schedule, this "Joint Naval Exercise," presided over by US Navy Rear Admiral Lee, will last until December 14. The United States has roped in a total of eight Latin American countries to participate. Currently, the first phase of the exercise is underway, with five US warships and warships from Colombia and Venezuela participating.

### 美国制造战争气氛  又在加勒比海举行海军演习

1960-09-12
第6版()
专栏：

    美国制造战争气氛
    又在加勒比海举行海军演习
    新华社9日讯　据美国新闻处加拉加斯7日消息：美国从6月初就开始策划的旨在制造战争气氛和加强对拉丁美洲军事控制的“联合海军演习”，已经于8月29日在加勒比海地区开始。
    按照预定计划，由美国海军少将李德主持的这次“联合海军演习”将延续到12月14日，美国共拉拢了八个拉丁美洲国家参加。目前开始的是第一阶段演习，参加的有五艘美国军舰以及哥伦比亚和委内瑞拉的军舰。
