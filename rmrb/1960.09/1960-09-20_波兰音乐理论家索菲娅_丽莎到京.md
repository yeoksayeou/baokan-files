Title: Polish Music Theorist Zofia Lissa Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-20
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Polish music theorist Zofia Lissa arrived in Beijing by plane on the 17th for a visit to China in accordance with the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 波兰音乐理论家索菲娅·丽莎到京

1960-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰音乐理论家索菲娅·丽莎到京
    新华社17日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1960年执行计划，前来我国进行访问的波兰音乐理论家索菲娅·丽莎，17日乘飞机到达北京。
