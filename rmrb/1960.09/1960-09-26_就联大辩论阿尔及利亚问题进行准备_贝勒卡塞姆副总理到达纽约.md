Title: Deputy Prime Minister Belkacem Arrives in New York to Prepare for UN General Assembly Debate on Algerian Question
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 25th: Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Algerian Provisional Government, Belkacem, arrived in New York on the 24th to prepare for the debate on the Algerian question at the Fifteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly.
　　As previously reported, the General Committee of the United Nations General Assembly, at its meeting on the 22nd, had once again included the Algerian question on the agenda of the General Assembly, based on a proposal by twenty-five Asian and African countries.
　　It is reported that Belkacem will stay in New York for two weeks, then return to North Africa, and come to New York again in November when the Political Committee debates the Algerian question.

### 就联大辩论阿尔及利亚问题进行准备  贝勒卡塞姆副总理到达纽约

1960-09-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　就联大辩论阿尔及利亚问题进行准备
　  贝勒卡塞姆副总理到达纽约
    新华社25日讯　纽约消息：阿尔及利亚临时政府副总理兼外交部长贝勒卡塞姆24日到达纽约，为联合国大会第十五届会议关于阿尔及利亚问题的辩论进行准备。
    前已报道，联合国大会总务委员会已经在22日的会议上，根据二十五个亚非国家的提议，再次把阿尔及利亚问题列入了大会议程。
    据报道，贝勒卡塞姆将在纽约逗留两周，然后返回北非，待11月政委会辩论阿尔及利亚问题时再来纽约。
