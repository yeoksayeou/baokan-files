Title: Yao Zhongming Hosts Banquet for Burmese Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 1st: Yao Zhongming, Chief Representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of the Burmese delegation to the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee, headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi.

Also present at the banquet were General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces; U Myint Thein, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court; Sao Khun Kyi, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities; Sao Khun Cho, Minister of Foreign Affairs and Minister of Shan State Affairs; Thakin Tin, Minister of Finance; Bo Min Gaung, Minister of Transportation; U Tun, Minister of Trade Development; U Ba Swe, Minister of Industry; Sama Duwa Sinwa Naw, Minister of Kachin State Affairs; Lieutenant Colonel Lun, Director of Military Intelligence; Dr. Htin Paw, Chairman of the Burma-China Friendship Association; leaders of the Union Alliance and the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, and other senior government officials.

Also present at the banquet were Lei Yang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché, and counselors.

The banquet was held in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

Since August 30th, Yao Zhongming, Chief Representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee, has paid courtesy calls on Sao Khun Kyi, Speaker of the Burmese Chamber of Nationalities; U Ba Swe, Chairman of the Burmese Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League; Thakin Tin, Minister of Finance; and U Ba Swe, Minister of Industry.

### 姚仲明欢宴缅甸代表团

1960-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    姚仲明欢宴缅甸代表团
    新华社仰光1日电　中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明，今天晚上设宴招待以昂季准将为首的中缅边界联合委员会缅甸代表团。
    出席宴会的还有缅甸国防军总参谋长奈温将军，最高法院首席法官吴敏登，民族院议长藻昆基，外交部长兼掸邦事务部长藻昆卓，财政部长德钦丁，运输部长波木昂，贸易发展部长吴敦，工业部长吴拉实，克钦邦事务部长沙马杜瓦信瓦瑙，军事情报处处长伦中校，缅中友好协会主席信波博士，联邦同盟和巩固的反法西斯人民自由同盟领袖以及政府其他高级官员。
    出席宴会的还有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬，武官蒋克定上校和参赞等。
    宴会在亲切和友好的气氛中进行。
    8月30日以来，中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明曾拜会了缅甸民族院议长藻昆基，缅甸巩固的反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴巴瑞，财政部长德钦丁和工业部长吴拉实。
