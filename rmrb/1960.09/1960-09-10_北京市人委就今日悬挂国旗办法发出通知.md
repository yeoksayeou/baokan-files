Title: Beijing Municipal People's Committee Issues Notice on Methods for Raising the National Flag Today
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 1

The Beijing Municipal People's Committee, according to instructions from the State Council, hereby issues the following notice:

    I.  President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea will be paying a friendly visit to our country on September 10th. From the morning of the 10th until 4:00 PM, all government organs, military units, factories, mines, enterprises, schools, and people's organizations in the capital should raise the national flag to express a warm welcome.

    II. The funeral of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, will be held on September 10th. After 4:00 PM on the 10th, all government organs, military units, factories, mines, enterprises, schools, and people's organizations in the capital should fly the national flag at half-mast as a sign of mourning.

　　September 9, 1960

### 北京市人委就今日悬挂国旗办法发出通知

1960-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市人委就今日悬挂国旗办法发出通知
    北京市人民委员会根据国务院指示，特作如下通知：
    一、几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔于9月10日来我国进行友好访问，从10日上午到下午四时前，首都各机关、部队、厂矿、企业、学校、人民团体一律悬挂国旗，表示热烈欢迎。
    二、德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克同志于9月10日殡葬，在10日下午四时后，首都各机关、部队、厂矿、企业、学校、人民团体，一律下半旗志哀。　　1960年9月9日
