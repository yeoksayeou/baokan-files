Title: Conscientiously Study Marxism-Leninism and Chairman Mao's Writings, Gradually Transform Worldview to Serve Socialism: The Second (Enlarged) Session of the Fourth National Congress of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang (RCCK) Adopts Resolution, Defining the Overall Policy and Tasks for the Future

Author:

Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 24th. The Second Plenary (Enlarged) Session of the Fourth Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, lasting over a month, concluded on the 19th.

At the closing session, a resolution was unanimously adopted. The resolution called upon all members of the RCCK to: conscientiously study Marxism-Leninism and the writings of Chairman Mao, gradually transform their worldview; hold high the three red banners of the General Line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Communes, and together with the people of the whole country, actively participate in the vigorous mass movement of increasing production and practicing economy with grain and steel as the center, and contribute their strength to the cause of socialist construction of the motherland; and continue to participate in the struggle against imperialism headed by the United States and in defense of world peace.

The resolution emphatically pointed out that we feel immense anger at the US imperialism's continued occupation of our territory Taiwan and its creation of the "Two Chinas" plot. We must continue to work hard in all aspects to fight for the liberation of Taiwan and the completion of the great cause of the unification of the motherland. The resolution called on the Kuomintang military and political personnel in Taiwan to stand on the banner of patriotism, work together with the people of the motherland, and drive US imperialism out of Taiwan.

He Xiangning, Chairman of the RCCK, delivered a report on behalf of the Standing Committee of the RCCK Central Committee at the closing session. She gave a detailed explanation and analysis of the progress made by RCCK members in the past two years and the central tasks and work policies of the RCCK in the future.

He Xiangning said that under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, we have made great progress in serving socialism and in conducting self-education, and the achievements are the main thing. However, we still have problems in this regard, and we must continue to strengthen our transformation. She emphasized that the general policy and fundamental task of the RCCK's work in the future is: to continue to carry out the socialist revolution in the political and ideological fronts in depth, conscientiously study Marxism-Leninism and Chairman Mao's writings, break the capitalist stand, establish the socialist stand, gradually transform our worldview, actively participate in the mass movement of technological revolution and cultural revolution, and strive to serve socialism.

At the closing session, Cheng Qian, Vice Chairman of the RCCK, said in his speech that this meeting was very successful and fruitful. It summarized the work of the RCCK since the Fourth National Congress, defined the future policies and tasks, and exchanged experiences. He said that the most important thing is that we have deeply studied and discussed the current domestic and international situation, greatly improved our understanding, clarified our tasks, and boosted our morale.

During the meeting, the leaders of the CPC Central Committee, Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Peng Zhen, etc., received the representatives attending the meeting. Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, delivered important reports respectively. RCCK members generally believed that these reports gave them a profound education and great encouragement.

During the meeting, all the representatives present adopted a telegram of salute to Chairman Mao from the RCCK and other democratic parties such as the China Democratic League, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, the China Zhi Gong Party, and the Jiusan Society.

### 认真学习马克思列宁主义和毛主席著作　逐步改造世界观为社会主义服务　民革四届二次会议（扩大）通过决议，确定今后总的方针和任务

1960-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　认真学习马克思列宁主义和毛主席著作
　　逐步改造世界观为社会主义服务
　　民革四届二次会议（扩大）通过决议，确定今后总的方针和任务
    新华社24日讯　中国国民党革命委员会第四届中央委员会第二次全体会议（扩大），历时一个多月，已在19日闭幕。
    在闭幕会上，全体一致通过了决议。决议号召：民革全体成员要
  认真学习马克思列宁主义和毛主席的著作，逐步改造世界观；高举总路线、大跃进、人民公社三面红旗，和全国人民一道，积极投入轰轰烈烈的以粮、钢为中心的全民增产节约运动，为祖国社会主义建设事业贡献出自己的力量；继续参加反对以美国为首的帝国主义和保卫世界和平的斗争。
    决议着重指出，我们对于美帝国主义继续霸占我国领土台湾，制造“两个中国”的阴谋，感到无比的愤怒。我们要继续在各方面努力工作，为解放台湾、完成祖国的统一大业而斗争。决议号召台湾国民党军政人员，站到爱国主义的旗帜下来，同祖国人民一致努力，把美帝国主义从台湾赶出去。
    民革主席何香凝代表民革中央常务委员会在闭幕会上作了报告。她对民革成员在近两年来所获得的进步，民革今后的中心任务、工作方针等问题作了详细的说明和分析。
    何香凝说，我们在中国共产党领导下，在为社会主义服务方面，在进行自我教育方面，都大有进步，成绩是主要的。但是在这方面我们还存在问题，还要继续加强改造。她强调说，民革工作今后总的方针和根本任务是：继续深入进行政治战线和思想战线上的社会主义革命，认真学习马克思列宁主义和毛主席著作，破资本主义立场，立社会主义立场，逐步改造世界观，积极投入技术革命和文化革命的群众运动中去，努力为社会主义服务。
    闭幕会上，民革副主席程潜致词说，这次会议开得很成功，收获大。它总结了民革第四届代表大会以来的工作，确定了今后的方针和任务，交流了经验。他说，最重要的是，我们深入地学习讨论了当前国内外形势，大大提高了认识，明确了任务，鼓舞了斗志。
    会议期间，中共中央领导人毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、彭真等接见了出席会议的代表。中共中央副主席周恩来、中共中央政治局委员陈毅、中共中央统一战线工作部部长李维汉分别作了重要报告。民革成员普遍认为，这些报告使他们受到了深刻的教育和巨大的鼓舞。
    会议期间，到会全体代表通过了民革和民盟、民进、农工、致公、九三等民主党派向毛主席的致敬电。
