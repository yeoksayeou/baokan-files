Title: Abolish the U.S.-Japan Treaty, Restore Sino-Japanese Diplomatic Relations, Oppose Military Bases, Oppose Government Suppression: Japan's "Sohyo" Decides on Current Action Plan; Sixteen Groups Decide to Strive for Restoration of Sino-Japanese Diplomatic Relations and Sino-Japanese Trade
Author:
Date: 1960-09-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 21st: The General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo) decided on its current action plan today, stipulating that it will launch a powerful unified action to demand the abolition of the new U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty," the restoration of Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations, opposition to military bases, and opposition to government suppression.

The plan also stipulates that Sohyo's demands in opposing unemployment and poverty and defending livelihoods are: a substantial increase in wages, the establishment of a uniform national minimum wage of 8,000 yen, expansion of social insurance, and opposition to rising prices.

According to the action plan, Sohyo will hold a unified action from October 19th to 22nd, with workers as the core, to strive for the realization of the above-mentioned demands of "defending peace and democracy, opposing unemployment and poverty." Especially on the 22nd, large-scale mass rallies and demonstrations will be held across the country; in Tokyo, a large-scale demonstration of 100,000 people will be held around the Diet.

In order to successfully carry out the unified action, Sohyo will also send organizational work teams to various places to strengthen organizational work.

The plan stipulates that in order to realize the demand for a uniform wage increase of 3,000 yen for civil servants, Sohyo will hold a second unified action on September 30th and a third unified action on October 15th.

Regarding support for the struggle of the Japan Coal Miners' Union, centered on the Miike miners' struggle, against "enterprise rationalization," the plan stipulates that in the event that the coal miners' union launches a force struggle, each industrial union should hold workplace conferences and irregular strikes as much as possible.

The plan stipulates the establishment of joint struggle organizations and the holding of conferences to support the struggle of small and medium-sized enterprise workers.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 22nd: Representatives of sixteen groups participating in the National Conference for Preventing the Revision of the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty" and the National Conference for Restoring Sino-Japanese Diplomatic Relations held a gathering today to listen to the impressions of Socialist Party member of the Diet, Shichiro Hozumi, on his recent visit to China. The participating representatives unanimously decided to strengthen propaganda activities to strive for the restoration of diplomatic relations with China and the conclusion of intergovernmental trade agreements with China.

The meeting also decided to hold a national conference in mid-October demanding the restoration of Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations and Sino-Japanese trade. Hozumi Shichiro reported on Premier Zhou Enlai's remarks on Sino-Japanese trade at the meeting.

The sixteen groups include: the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, the Japan Trade Promotion Association, the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade, the Japan Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, the Tokyo Regional Council of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the Diet Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade, the National Federation of Farmers' Associations, and the Japan Federation of Small and Medium Enterprise Organizations, etc.

### 废除日美条约　恢复日中邦交　反对军事基地　反对政府镇压  日本“总评”决定当前行动方针  十六团体决定力争恢复日中邦交和日中贸易

1960-09-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　废除日美条约　恢复日中邦交　反对军事基地　反对政府镇压
　  日本“总评”决定当前行动方针
    十六团体决定力争恢复日中邦交和日中贸易
    新华社21日讯　东京消息：日本工会总评议会今天决定了当前行动方针，规定要展开强大的统一行动，要求废除新日美“安全条约”、恢复日中邦交、反对军事基地、反对政府镇压。
    方针还规定，“总评”在反对失业和贫困、保卫生活方面的要求是，大量增加工资，建立全国一律的八千日元的最低工资制，扩大社会保险，反对物价上涨。
    根据行动方针，“总评”将从10月19日到22日的四天间，以工人为核心举行争取实现上述要求的“保卫和平和民主，反对失业和贫困”统一行动。特别是在22日，要在全国各地举行大规模群众集会和示威；在东京，要在国会四周举行有十万人参加的大规模示威游行。
    为了胜利展开统一行动，“总评”还要派遣组织工作团到各地加强组织工作。
    方针规定，为了实现公务人员一律增加工资三千日元的要求，“总评”将在9月30日举行第二次统一行动，在10月15日举行第三次统一行动。
    关于支援以三池矿工斗争为中心的日本煤矿工会反对“企业合理化”的斗争，方针规定，在煤矿工会展开实力斗争的情况下，各个产业工会要尽可能地举行工作场所大会、不定期罢工。
    方针规定要成立共同斗争组织和举行大会，来支援中小企业工人的斗争。
    新华社22日讯　东京消息：参加日本阻止修改日美“安全条约”国民会议和日本恢复日中邦交国民会议的十六个团体的代表们今天举行集会，听取社会党议员穗积七郎报告他最近访问中国之行的观感。与会代表们一致决定要加强宣传活动，争取同中国恢复外交关系并同中国缔结政府间的贸易协定。
    会议并且决定在10月中旬举行要求恢复日中邦交和日中贸易的国民大会。穗积七郎在会上就中国周恩来总理关于中日贸易的谈话作了报告。
    十六个团体包括：日中友好协会、日中文化交流协会、日本贸易促进会、日本国际贸易促进协会、日本亚非团结委员会、日本工会总评议会东京地方评议会、国会议员促进日中贸易联盟、全日本农民协会联合会和日本中小企业团体联盟等。
