Title: Albanian Delegation Arrives in New York to Attend the 15th Session of the United Nations General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1960-09-21
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 20th - According to a TASS report from New York on September 20th: Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, arrived in New York today leading the Albanian delegation to attend the 15th session of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 出席联合国大会第十五届会议  阿尔巴尼亚代表团到达纽约

1960-09-21
第6版()
专栏：

    出席联合国大会第十五届会议
　  阿尔巴尼亚代表团到达纽约
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社纽约20日讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席谢胡今天率领阿尔巴尼亚代表团到达纽约，出席联合国大会第十五届会议。
