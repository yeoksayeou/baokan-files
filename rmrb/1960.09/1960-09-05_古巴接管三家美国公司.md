Title: Cuba Takes Over Three American Companies
Author:
Date: 1960-09-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 3rd – According to a report in today's "Combate," the Cuban Minister of Labor has issued a decision regarding the takeover of Firestone, Goodyear, and U.S. Rubber companies.

    The decision points out that these three U.S. rubber companies have been engaged in conspiracies for several weeks to obstruct production and undermine the Cuban national economy. It has been announced that the takeover of these three companies will be for a period of one year.

### 古巴接管三家美国公司

1960-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴接管三家美国公司
    新华社哈瓦那3日电　据“战斗报”今天报道，古巴劳工部长颁布了一项关于接管费尔斯通、固特异和美国橡胶公司的决定。
    这项决定指出，这三家美国橡胶公司几个星期来一直进行着妨碍生产和破坏古巴国民经济的阴谋活动。据宣布，接管这三家公司的期限为一年。
