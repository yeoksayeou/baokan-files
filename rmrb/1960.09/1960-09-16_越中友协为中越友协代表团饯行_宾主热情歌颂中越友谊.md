Title: Vietnam-China Friendship Association Hosts Farewell Banquet for China-Vietnam Friendship Association Delegation; Guests Enthusiastically Praise China-Vietnam Friendship
Author:
Date: 1960-09-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 15th: The Vietnam-China Friendship Association held a banquet today to bid farewell to the China-Vietnam Friendship Association delegation, which was invited to participate in the 15th anniversary celebration of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and to pay a friendly visit.

Those present at the banquet included: Nguyen Xuan Thuy, Acting President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association; Nguyen Van Huyen, Nguyen Thi Luu, members of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association; Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee; and Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace.

He Wei, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, was also invited to attend the banquet.

At the banquet, Nguyen Xuan Thuy delivered a welcoming speech. Sun Xiaocun, head of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association delegation, spoke enthusiastically about the China-Vietnam friendship he personally felt during the past eighteen days while participating in the 15th anniversary celebration of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and visiting Vietnamese factories, agricultural cooperatives, handicraft cooperatives, schools, and kindergartens. He also sang Chinese and Vietnamese folk songs such as "Singing the Erlang Mountain" and "Aiya, don't go back!" in duet with a Vietnamese female singer. Mr. Doan Ke Thien, a member of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association in his sixties, recited his passionate poem, warmly farewelling the China-Vietnam Friendship Association delegation.

### 越中友协为中越友协代表团饯行  宾主热情歌颂中越友谊

1960-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

    越中友协为中越友协代表团饯行
    宾主热情歌颂中越友谊
    新华社河内15日电　越中友好协会今天举行宴会，欢送应邀参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年庆典和作友好访问的中越友好协会代表团。
    出席宴会的有：越中友好协会代理会长阮春水，越中友好协会委员阮文煊、阮氏榴，河内市行政委员会主席陈维新，越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探等。
    中国驻越南大使何伟也应邀出席了宴会。
    在宴会上，阮春水致欢迎词。中越友好协会代表团团长孙晓村畅谈了他在过去十八天参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年庆典和访问越南工厂、农业合作社、手工艺合作社、学校、幼儿园等活动中亲身感受到的中越友谊，并且和越南女歌唱家对唱了“歌唱二郎山”，“哎呀呀，不要回去吧！”等中国和越南民歌。越中友好协会六十多岁的会员尹继善老人朗诵了他的热情洋溢的诗篇，热情欢送中越友协代表团。
