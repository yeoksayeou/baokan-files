Title: Many Japanese Civil Organizations Welcome Premier Zhou's Speech; Decide to Go All Out to Strive for an Early Government-to-Government Trade Agreement with China; Businessmen Hold a Meeting in Tokyo to Establish the "Political and Economic Roundtable Conference for Improving Japan-China Relations"
Author:
Date: 1960-09-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 13th -- News: Japanese organizations related to trade with China, after learning the full text of Premier Zhou Enlai's speech on Sino-Japanese relations and Sino-Japanese trade, have decided to make comprehensive efforts to enable the Japanese government to fulfill the three principles regarding Sino-Japanese political relations and to conclude a trade agreement with the Chinese government.

The heads of the Japan-China Friendship Association, the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, and the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association unanimously decided at a joint meeting held this morning that the urgent task for pro-China friendship organizations and organizations promoting Japan-China trade is to go all out to strive for an early conclusion of a government-to-government trade agreement with China. The Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, led by Kazuo Suzuki, also held an emergency board meeting today, deciding to fully support Premier Zhou Enlai's speech on Sino-Japanese relations and Sino-Japanese trade, and to vigorously launch a campaign to urge the Japanese government to sign a trade agreement with the Chinese government.

The meeting decided to launch a publicity campaign to ensure that manufacturers across the country related to Sino-Japanese trade generally understand Premier Zhou's speech and to make every effort to unite all relevant trading companies into the movement.

The meeting also decided to immediately contact the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade regarding private contracts to prepare for the resumption of trade.

Yesterday, seven trade organizations, including the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, held a joint meeting and decided to launch a propaganda campaign to demand that the government conclude a trade agreement with China in accordance with the principles pointed out in Premier Zhou Enlai's speech. All seven groups expressed warm welcomes to the Chinese Premier's speech.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th -- News from Tokyo: Japanese business figures held a meeting in Tokyo on the 8th and established the "Political and Economic Roundtable Conference for Improving Japan-China Relations."

The purpose of this new organization is to seek cooperation between the business community and insightful figures in the Liberal Democratic Party to strive for the cause of improving Japan-China relations and restoring Japan-China trade.

After the inaugural meeting, Eiichi Yadoya, Vice Chairman of the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade, announced at a press conference that the roundtable conference had called on 65 Liberal Democratic Party members to provide assistance.

Thirty-five people attended the roundtable conference, including Kumaichi Yamamoto, Chairman of the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade, Den Kawakatsu, Chairman of Japan Spinning Wheel Axle Company, and Minoru Shiramizu, Chairman of Toho Trading Company.

In addition to members, six Liberal Democratic Party members including Kenzo Matsumura, Yutaro Takeyama, Tokuma Utsunomiya, and Saburo Hirano attended the inaugural meeting.

### 日本很多民间团体欢迎周总理谈话  决定全力以赴争取早日同中国缔结政府间的贸易协定  贸易界人士在东京举行大会成立“改善日中关系政经恳谈会”

1960-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    日本很多民间团体欢迎周总理谈话
    决定全力以赴争取早日同中国缔结政府间的贸易协定
    贸易界人士在东京举行大会成立“改善日中关系政经恳谈会”
    新华社13日讯　东京消息：日本的同中国贸易有关的团体获悉周恩来总理关于中日关系和中日贸易的谈话全文后纷纷决定，要作出全面的努力，来使日本政府履行关于日中政治关系的三原则，并同中国政府缔结贸易协定。
    日中友好协会，恢复日中邦交国民议会和日中贸易促进会的负责人在今天上午举行的联席会议上一致决定：目前，争取同中国友好的团体和促进日中贸易的团体的紧急任务是，全力以赴，争取同中国早日缔结政府间的贸易协定。由铃木一雄领导的日中贸易促进会在今天还举行理事会紧急会议，决定全面地支持周恩来总理关于中日关系和中日贸易的谈话，并且要大力展开促使日本政府同中国政府签订贸易协定的运动。
    会议决定展开宣传活动，使全国同日中贸易有关的厂商普遍了解周总理的谈话，尽全力把所有有关的贸易公司团结到运动中来。
    会议还决定，关于民间合同，要立即同中国国际贸易促进委员会进行联系，做好恢复贸易的准备。
    昨天，日中贸易促进会等七个贸易团体曾经举行联席会议，决定展开宣传运动，要求政府根据周恩来总理谈话中所指出的原则同中国缔结贸易协定。这七个团体都对中国总理的谈话表示热烈的欢迎。
    新华社10日讯　东京消息：日本贸易界人士8日在东京举行大会，成立了“改善日中关系政经恳谈会”。
    这个新组织的宗旨是谋求贸易界和自由民主党有见识人士之间的合作，争取改善日中关系和恢复日中贸易的事业。
    成立大会结束后日本国际贸易促进协会副会长宿谷荣一在记者招待会上宣布，恳谈会已经呼吁六十五名自由民主党议员给予协助。
    参加恳谈会的有日本国际贸易促进协会会长山本熊一、日本纺车车轴公司董事长川胜传、东邦商会董事长白水实等三十五人。
    参加成立大会的除了会员以外，还有松村谦三、竹山祐太郎、宇都宫德马、平野三郎等六名自由民主党议员。
