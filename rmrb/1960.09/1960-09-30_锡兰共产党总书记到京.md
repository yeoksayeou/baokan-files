Title: Ceylon Communist Party General Secretary Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 29th - Peter Keuneman, General Secretary of the Ceylon Communist Party, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning at the invitation of the CPC Central Committee to attend the celebration of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, Wu Xiuquan, a member of the Central Committee, and Li Qixin, a responsible staff member of the CPC Central Committee organs, were at the airport to greet them.

### 锡兰共产党总书记到京

1960-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　锡兰共产党总书记到京
　　新华社29日讯　应中共中央邀请前来参加中华人民共和国成立十一周年庆典的锡兰共产党总书记彼得·克尼曼和夫人，今天上午乘飞机抵达北京。到机场迎接的有中共中央副主席朱德、中央委员伍修权、中共中央机关负责工作人员李启新。
