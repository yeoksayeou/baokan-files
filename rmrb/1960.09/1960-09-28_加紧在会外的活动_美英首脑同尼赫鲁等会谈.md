Title: Stepping Up Activities Outside the Meeting: US and British Leaders Hold Talks with Nehru and Others
Author:
Date: 1960-09-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 27th: U.S. President Eisenhower held talks with Indian Prime Minister Nehru, who is attending the United Nations General Assembly, on the afternoon of the 26th. According to U.S. Assistant Secretary of State for Public Affairs, Berding, Eisenhower and Nehru held an hour-long conversation in a "cordial atmosphere," discussing the "current situation of the General Assembly" and issues related to India.

Berding also said that Eisenhower was pleased with the India-Pakistan Indus Waters Treaty. U.S. Secretary of State Herter and Indian Foreign Secretary Dutt also attended the meeting.

It is also reported that on the same day, British Prime Minister Macmillan invited Canadian Prime Minister Diefenbaker and Prime Minister Nehru of India to a luncheon meeting. Later, Macmillan also held a fifty-minute secret meeting with Hammarskjöld.

### 加紧在会外的活动  美英首脑同尼赫鲁等会谈

1960-09-28
第5版()
专栏：

    加紧在会外的活动
    美英首脑同尼赫鲁等会谈
    新华社27日讯　纽约消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔26日下午同出席联合国大会的印度总理尼赫鲁举行了会谈。据美国公共事务助理国务卿伯丁说，艾森豪威尔同尼赫鲁在“亲切的气氛中”会谈了一小时，讨论了“联大的目前情况”和有关印度的问题。
    伯丁还说，艾森豪威尔对印度—巴基斯坦的印度河水条约表示欣慰。美国国务卿赫脱和印度外交秘书杜特也参加了这次会谈。
    据报道，同一天，英国首相麦克米伦邀请加拿大总理迪芬贝克和印度总理尼赫鲁举行了午餐会谈。晚些时候，麦克米伦还同哈马舍尔德举行了五十分钟的秘密会谈。
