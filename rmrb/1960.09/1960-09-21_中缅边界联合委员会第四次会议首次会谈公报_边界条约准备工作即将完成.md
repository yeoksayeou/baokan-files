Title: Communiqué of the First Meeting of the Fourth Session of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee: Preparations for the Boundary Treaty Nearing Completion
Author:
Date: 1960-09-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th -- Press Communiqué of the First Meeting of the Fourth Session of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee

The first meeting of the Fourth Session of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee was held in Beijing on the morning of September 20. The meeting was attended by the Chinese Chief Representative Yao Zhongming, the Burmese Chief Representative Brigadier General Aung Gyi, and all representatives and advisors from both sides. Yao Zhongming served as chairman.

The meeting decided on the agenda for this session and designated personnel to form a special group to study the draft of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty and the work of producing the attached maps.

Both sides expressed their confidence that all necessary preparations for the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty by the two governments will be successfully completed before October 1. The meeting was conducted in a very friendly atmosphere.

### 中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议首次会谈公报　边界条约准备工作即将完成

1960-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议首次会谈公报
　　边界条约准备工作即将完成
    新华社20日讯　中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议第一次会谈新闻公报
    中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议于9月20日上午在北京举行第一次会谈。出席会议的有中方首席代表姚仲明、缅方首席代表昂季准将及双方全体代表和顾问。姚仲明担任主席。
    会议决定了这次会议的议程，并指定人员组成专门小组就中缅边界条约的草案和附图制作等工作进行研究。
    双方满怀信心地认为，两国政府签订中缅边界条约的一切必要准备工作将能在10月1日以前顺利完成。会议在十分友好的气氛中进行。
