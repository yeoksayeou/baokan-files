Title: Romanian Table Tennis Team Concludes Friendly Matches in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: The Romanian men's and women's table tennis teams played their last friendly match in China tonight in Beijing against the second team of the Chinese youth table tennis team. The results of the match: the guest women's players won three of the four singles matches, and the guest men's team also won nine to zero. The only game won by the home team was when female player Wang Jian defeated Pitica two to one.
    The Romanian men's and women's table tennis teams are about to leave Beijing and return home. During their visit to our country, they played eleven friendly matches in Beijing, Guangzhou, Shanghai, Tianjin and other places. The guest women's team achieved a record of eight wins and three losses, and the men's team had three wins and eight losses.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: The Romanian men's and women's table tennis teams played their tenth friendly match in China tonight in Beijing against the second team of the Chinese youth table tennis team. The results of the match: the guest women's team won three to zero; the guest men's team lost seven to thirteen to the home team.
    The guest team will hold its last friendly match in China in Beijing on the 24th.

### 罗乒乓球队在京友谊赛结束

1960-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　  罗乒乓球队在京友谊赛结束
    据新华社24日讯　罗马尼亚男女乒乓球队今晚在北京同中国青年乒乓球队二队进行访华最后一次友谊比赛。比赛结果：客队女选手在四场单打比赛中赢了三场，客队男子队也以九比零获胜。主队唯一获胜的一场比赛，是女选手王健以二比一战胜皮蒂卡。
    罗马尼亚男女乒乓球队即将离北京回国，他们在我国访问期间，曾先后在北京、广州、上海、天津等地进行了十一次友谊比赛，客队女子队取得了八胜三负的战绩，男子队是三胜八负。
    据新华社22日讯　罗马尼亚男女乒乓球队今晚在北京同中国青年乒乓球队二队进行访华第十次友谊比赛。比赛结果：客队女子队以三比○获胜；客队男子队以七比十三败于主队。
    客队24日将在北京举行访华最后一场友谊比赛。
