Title: Further Strengthen International Intermodal Transport and Scientific and Technological Cooperation: Socialist Countries Hold Railway Ministers Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 22nd: The fifth session of the Railway Ministers Conference of the "Railway Cooperation Organization" of socialist countries was held in Pyongyang from September 17th to 22nd.

　　The following countries' railway, automobile, and highway transportation delegations participated in the conference:

　　Bulgaria, Hungary, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam， the German Democratic Republic, China, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mongolia, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, and Czechoslovakia.

　　The conference was presided over by Comrade Kim Hoi-il, Minister of Transportation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

　　In order to further complete and improve the international intermodal transport and scientific and technological cooperation of railways, automobiles, and highways in socialist countries, the conference made a series of important resolutions.

　　The conference was conducted in an atmosphere of friendship, mutual understanding, and close cooperation among the participating countries of the Railway Cooperation Organization. All resolutions were unanimously adopted.

### 进一步加强国际联运和科技合作  社会主义国家举行铁路部长会议

1960-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

　  进一步加强国际联运和科技合作
    社会主义国家举行铁路部长会议
    新华社平壤22日电　社会主义国家“铁路合作组织”第五届铁路部长会议于9月17—22日在平壤举行。
    参加会议的，有下列国家的铁路、汽车和公路运输代表团：
    保加利亚、匈牙利、越南民主共和国、德意志民主共和国、中国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联和捷克斯洛伐克。
    会议由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国交通相金会一同志主持。
    会议为了进一步完成和改善社会主义国家铁路、汽车和公路运输的国际联运和科学技术合作，作出了一系列的有重要意义的决议。
    会议是在铁路合作组织各参加国友好、互相谅解和紧密合作的气氛中进行的。各项决议都是一致通过的。
