Title: Our Military Delegation Visits the United Arab Republic

Author:

Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. The People's Republic of China Military Friendship Delegation, led by General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, at the invitation of Marshal Abdel Hakim Amer, Vice President and Minister of Defense of the United Arab Republic, left Beijing by plane today for a friendly visit to the UAR.

The members of the delegation are: Lieutenant General Liu Shao Wen, Rear Admiral Zhang Yuanpei, Air Force Major General Wu Zongxian, Armored Corps Major General Deng Jiatai, Major General Xiao Yingtang, Senior Colonel Qian Diqian, Senior Colonel Zhang Bingyu, Senior Colonel Tian Tao, and Colonel Hu Yucun.

Those who saw them off at the airport included General Luo Ruiqing, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, as well as Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice Admiral Zhou Xihan, Air Force Lieutenant General Tan Jiashu, Major General Lin Bin, Major General Sun Yizhi, and He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

UAR Ambassador to China, Tarazi, Deputy Military Attache Lieutenant Colonel Wu Bo, and military attachés of various countries' embassies in China also saw them off at the airport.

### 我军事代表团赴阿联访问

1960-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军事代表团赴阿联访问
    新华社23日讯　应阿拉伯联合共和国副总统兼国防部长阿卜杜勒·哈基姆·阿密尔元帅邀请作友好访问的中华人民共和国军事友好代表团，在团长、中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将率领下，今天由北京乘飞机前往阿联。
    代表团团员有：刘少文中将、张元培海军少将、吴宗先空军少将、邓家泰装甲兵少将、萧应棠少将、钱抵千大校、张秉玉大校、田涛大校、胡雨村上校。
    到机场欢送的有，中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将、总政治部副主任萧华上将以及萧向荣中将、周希汉海军中将、谭家述空军中将、林彬少将、孙仪之少将，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英等。
    阿联驻我国大使塔拉齐、副武官吴勃中校和各国驻华大使馆武官也到机场欢送。
