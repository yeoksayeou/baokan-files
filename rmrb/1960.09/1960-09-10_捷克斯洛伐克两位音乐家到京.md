Title: Two Czechoslovak Musicians Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: Czechoslovak musicians Josef Stanislav and Alexander Moyzes, who are visiting China under the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane today. Wang Yuanfang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Musicians Association, and others went to the airport to welcome them. Tobiáš, Second Secretary of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, also went to greet them.

### 捷克斯洛伐克两位音乐家到京

1960-09-10
第6版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克两位音乐家到京
    新华社9日讯　根据中捷文化合作协定1960年执行计划前来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克音乐家约瑟夫·斯坦尼斯拉夫和亚历山大·莫伊赛司，在今天乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国音乐家协会书记处书记王元方等。捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆二等秘书多比亚士也前往迎接。
