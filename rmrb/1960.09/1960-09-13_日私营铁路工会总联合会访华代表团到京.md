Title: Delegation of the Japan Private Railway Workers' Union General Federation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 12th: The delegation of the Japan Private Railway Workers' Union General Federation, led by Chairman Hiroshi Sato, arrived in Beijing by train today at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union. This is their third visit to China.

Greeting them at the station was Xiao Hongqi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union, and others.

### 日私营铁路工会总联合会访华代表团到京

1960-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    日私营铁路工会总联合会访华代表团到京
    新华社12日讯　应中国铁路工会全国委员会的邀请、由团长佐藤弘率领的日本私营铁路工会总联合会第三次访华代表团一行十人，今天乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席肖洪启等人。
