Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Pifkos

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 25th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received Pifkos, the secretary of the British Cyprus Committee, this morning, and had a cordial conversation with him. Present at the reception were Zhang Zhixiang, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and Secretary General Zhou Erfu.

### 陈毅副总理接见皮夫考斯

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　  陈毅副总理接见皮夫考斯
    新华社25日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了英国塞浦路斯人委员会书记皮夫考斯，并同他进行了亲切的谈话。接见时在座的，有中国人民对外文化协会副会长张致祥，秘书长周而复。
