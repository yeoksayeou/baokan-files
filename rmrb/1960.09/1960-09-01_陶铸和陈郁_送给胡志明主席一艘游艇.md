Title: Tao Zhu and Chen Yu Present a Yacht to Chairman Ho Chi Minh
Author:
Date: 1960-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 31st: A yacht presented to Chairman Ho Chi Minh by Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, was handed over at a ceremony in Haiphong City on the afternoon of the 30th.

Lin Qiangyun, head of the Guangdong Provincial Party and Government Delegation currently visiting Vietnam, member of the Standing Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC, and Deputy Governor, transferred the yacht to the Vietnamese side on behalf of comrades Tao Zhu and Chen Yu. Hoang Huu Nhan, Secretary of the Haiphong City Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Chairman of the Administrative Committee, accepted the yacht on behalf of Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the Central Committee of the Labor Party.

### 陶铸和陈郁  送给胡志明主席一艘游艇

1960-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    陶铸和陈郁
    送给胡志明主席一艘游艇
    新华社河内31日电　中国共产党广东省委员会第一书记陶铸、省长陈郁赠送给胡志明主席的一艘游艇30日下午在海防市举行交接仪式。
    正在越南访问的广东省党政代表团团长、中共广东省委常委、副省长林锵云代表陶铸、陈郁同志把这艘游艇移交越方。越南劳动党海防市委书记兼行政委员会主席黄友仁受胡志明主席和劳动党中央的委托，接受了这艘游艇。
