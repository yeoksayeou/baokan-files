Page: 1

　　Prime Minister Abbas Ferhat
　　Prime Minister Abbas Ferhat was born in the village of Taher, Constantine, eastern Algeria, and is now sixty-one years old. Prime Minister Abbas is one of the famous leaders in the Algerian national independence movement. As early as his student days, he engaged in the struggle for national independence and freedom, and was arrested twice by the French authorities in 1943 and 1945.
　　In 1924, he initiated and organized the "North African Muslim Student Association." In 1943, he participated in drafting the "Algerian People's Declaration." In 1944, he created the "Friends of the Declaration and Freedom" organization. In 1946, he created the "Algerian Democratic Union of the Manifesto," and served as the chairman of the union until November 1, 1954. After the armed uprising, he joined the Algerian National Liberation Front, and in February 1956, he was sent to strengthen the work of the external delegation of the Algerian National Liberation Front.
　　On August 20, 1956, he was elected as a member of the Algerian National Revolutionary Committee. In August 1957, the Algerian National Revolutionary Committee elected him as a member of the "Coordination and Implementation Committee."
　　On September 19, 1958, when the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic was established, Abbas Ferhat became Prime Minister. (Xinhua News Agency) (with picture)

### 阿巴斯·费尔哈特总理

1960-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　阿巴斯·费尔哈特总理
    阿巴斯·费尔哈特总理生于阿尔及利亚东君士坦丁塔赫尔村，现年六十一岁。阿巴斯总理是阿尔及利亚民族独立运动中的著名领袖之一。早在学生时代他就从事争取民族独立和自由的斗争，曾于1943年和1945年两次被法国当局逮捕。
    1924年，他发起组织“北非穆斯林学生协会”。1943年参加起草“阿尔及利亚人民宣言”。1944年创建“宣言与自由之友”组织。1946年创建“阿尔及利亚宣言民主联盟”，并一直担任该联盟主席到1954年11月1日。武装起义后，他参加了阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线，并在1956年2月被派去加强阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线对外代表团的工作。
    1956年8月20日他被选为阿尔及利亚革命全国委员会委员。1957年8月，阿尔及利亚革命全国委员会选他为“协调与执行委员会”委员。
    1958年9月19日阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府成立时，阿巴斯·费尔哈特出任总理。　（新华社）（附图片）
