Title: Delegations from the Soviet Union, Hungary, Bulgaria, and Romania Depart to Attend the 15th UN General Assembly

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kaliningrad, 10th September – According to TASS, the Soviet delegation led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Khrushchev, left Kaliningrad on the 9th aboard the "Baltika" turbo-electric ship for New York to attend the 15th session of the United Nations General Assembly.

The Hungarian delegation led by Kádár, the Bulgarian delegation led by Zhivkov, and the Romanian delegation led by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej also traveled to New York on the same ship to attend the current session of the UN General Assembly.

### 参加第十五届联大会议  苏匈保罗四国代表团启程

1960-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    参加第十五届联大会议
    苏匈保罗四国代表团启程
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社加里宁格勒9日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫率领的苏联代表团，在9日乘“波罗的海号”透平电动机船离开加里宁格勒前往纽约，出席联合国大会第十五届会议。
    同船前往纽约出席联大本届会议的还有：由卡达尔率领的匈牙利代表团，由日夫科夫率领的保加利亚代表团和由乔治乌—德治率领的罗马尼亚代表团。
