Title: Shehu to Lead Delegation to UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1960-09-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, September 7th. The Albanian Council of Ministers held a meeting on September 3rd and decided that Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Shehu, will lead the Albanian delegation to attend the 15th session of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 谢胡将率代表团出席联大

1960-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    谢胡将率代表团出席联大
    新华社地拉那7日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议3日举行会议，决定由部长会议主席谢胡率领阿尔巴尼亚代表团出席联合国大会第十五届会议。
