Title: Demanding the recovery of sovereignty over the Panama Canal Zone, the Panamanian people hold anti-American demonstrations

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Panama City, September 22nd: The Panamanian people held anti-American demonstrations in the Canal Zone on the 21st, demanding the recovery of Panama's full sovereignty over the Canal Zone.

Panamanian patriots held the demonstration during a "flag-raising ceremony" of the Panamanian flag held by the American occupation authorities in the Shaler Triangle Square in the Canal Zone. In order to ease the anti-American sentiment of the Panamanian people and their struggle to recover the Canal Zone, U.S. President Eisenhower was forced on the 17th to order the "permission" to fly the Panamanian flag only within the confines of this square. During this "flag-raising ceremony," the U.S. authorities stubbornly refused to allow Panamanian President de la Guardia to raise the Panamanian flag, and instead had U.S. police raise the American and Panamanian flags in the square. President de la Guardia angrily refused to participate in this deceptive "ceremony."

People and students from all walks of life in Panama held demonstrations after the "flag-raising ceremony." Demonstrators sang the Panamanian national anthem and shouted: "The 'flag-raising ceremony' is a deception of the Panamanian people!" "Yankees go home!" "We want Panama, not Yankees!" The signs they held read: "A country's sovereignty cannot be limited to a triangular area!" "The Canal Zone is not Shaler Triangle Square!" "We want the motherland, not deception!" Demonstrators hung many Panamanian flags in the Canal Zone. (Attached photo)

September 21, the Panamanian flag was raised in the Canal Zone. Panamanian students participating in the flag-raising ceremony held up signs reading "Yankees go home" and held an anti-American demonstration.

### 要求收回对巴拿马运河区的主权　巴拿马人民举行反美示威

1960-09-24
第5版()
专栏：

　  要求收回对巴拿马运河区的主权
　　巴拿马人民举行反美示威
    新华社22日讯　巴拿马城消息：巴拿马人民21日在运河区举行反美示威游行，要求收回巴拿马对运河区的全部主权。
    巴拿马爱国者是在美国占领当局在运河区的夏勒三角形广场举行巴拿马国旗的“升旗典礼”时举行示威的。为了缓和巴拿马人民的反美情绪和他们争取收回运河区的斗争，美国总统艾森豪威尔在17日被迫下令“准许”在仅限于这个广场的范围内悬挂巴拿马国旗。在举行这次“升旗典礼”时，美国当局蛮横地不准巴拿马总统德拉古阿迪亚到场升起巴拿马国旗，而由美国警察在广场上升起美国和巴拿马国旗。德拉古阿迪亚总统愤然拒绝参加这次骗人的“典礼”。
    巴拿马各界人民和学生在“升旗典礼”结束后，举行了示威游行。示威者高唱巴拿马国歌，并且高呼：“‘升旗典礼’是对巴拿马人民的愚弄！”“美国佬滚回去！”“要巴拿马，不要美国佬！”他们举着的标语牌上写道：“一国的主权不能限制在一个三角形地区内！”“运河区不是夏勒三角形广场！”“我们要祖国，不要欺骗！”示威群众把许多巴拿马国旗悬挂在运河区内。（附图片）
    9月21日，巴拿马国旗在运河区升起。参加升旗典礼的巴拿马学生高举着“美国佬滚回去”的标语牌，举行了反美示威。
