Title: Guinea President Touré Departs for China for Visit at the Invitation of Chairman Liu Shaoqi
Author:
Date: 1960-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, September 5th: Guinean Republic President Sékou Touré and the Guinean Republic government delegation he leads left Conakry on the morning of September 5th for a friendly visit to China at the invitation of the People's Republic of China Chairman Liu Shaoqi.

Members of the delegation include Diallo Saifoulaye, Speaker of the Guinean National Assembly and Political Bureau Secretary of the Guinean Democratic Party; Louis Lansana Béavogui, Minister of Economic Affairs; Keita N’ Famara, Minister of Planning; and Dr. Saïdou Conté, Guinean Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

The delegation will arrive in Beijing on September 10th. The delegation will also visit the Soviet Union, the Mongolian People's Republic, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Republic of Indonesia, and the United Arab Republic.

Among those seeing off the delegation were all members of the Political Bureau of the Guinean Democratic Party, ministers of various ministries of the Guinean government, Zhao Yuan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Guinea, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Guinea.

Xinhua News Agency, September 6th: TASS, Moscow, September 5th: It is officially announced here that Guinean Republic President Sékou Touré will arrive in Moscow on September 6th for an official visit.

Accompanying President Touré on the visit are Diallo Saifoulaye, Speaker of the Guinean National Assembly; Louis Lansana Béavogui, Minister of Economic Affairs; Keita N’ Famara, Minister of Planning; and other officials.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 6th: According to a report in today’s Mongolian “Truth Daily,” the Guinean government delegation led by Guinean Republic President Touré will arrive in Ulaanbaatar on September 9th for a visit at the invitation of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural and the government of the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 应刘少奇主席的邀请访问我国  几内亚总统杜尔启程来华

1960-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    应刘少奇主席的邀请访问我国
    几内亚总统杜尔启程来华
    新华社科纳克里5日电　应中华人民共和国主席刘少奇的邀请，几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔和他率领的几内亚共和国政府代表团在5日上午离开科纳克里前往中国进行友好访问。
    代表团的团员有几内亚国民议会议长、几内亚民主党政治局书记迪阿洛·塞福拉耶、经济部长路易·兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉、计划部长凯塔·恩法马拉和几内亚驻苏联大使赛杜·贡特博士。
    代表团将在9月10日到达北京。代表团还将访问苏联、蒙古人民共和国、越南民主共和国、印度尼西亚共和国和阿拉伯联合共和国。
    为代表团送行的有几内亚民主党政治局全体委员、几内亚政府各部部长、中国驻几内亚大使馆临时代办赵源以及驻几内亚的各国外交使节。
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社莫斯科5日讯：这里正式宣布，几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔将在9月6日到达莫斯科进行正式访问。
    同杜尔总统一道访问的有：几内亚国民议会议长迪阿洛·塞福拉耶、经济部长路易·兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉、计划部长凯塔·恩法马拉和其他官员。
    新华社乌兰巴托6日电　据蒙古“真理报”今天报道，应蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团和政府的邀请，由几内亚共和国总统杜尔率领的几内亚政府代表团，将在9月9日到达乌兰巴托访问。
