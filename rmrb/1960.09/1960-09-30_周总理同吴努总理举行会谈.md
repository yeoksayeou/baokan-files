Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Talks with Prime Minister U Nu
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, held talks with the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, this morning. Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Marshal Chen Yi, and Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, General Ne Win, participated in the talks. The talks proceeded in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 周总理同吴努总理举行会谈

1960-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理同吴努总理举行会谈
　　新华社29日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同缅甸联邦总理吴努今天上午举行了会谈。国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅和缅甸国防军总参谋长奈温将军参加了会谈。会谈在亲切友好气氛中进行。
