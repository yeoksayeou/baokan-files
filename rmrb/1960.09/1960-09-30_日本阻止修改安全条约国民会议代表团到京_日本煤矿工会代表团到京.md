Title: Japanese Delegation from the National Council Against Revision of the Security Treaty Arrives in Beijing; Japanese Coal Miners' Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: The Japanese Delegation from the National Council Against Revision of the Security Treaty, led by Chairman Takeo Luye and Vice-Chairmen Bunichiro Sano and Koryu Togo, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

Welcoming them at the airport were Liao Chengzhi and Bao Erhan, Vice-Chairmen of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, as well as Chu Tunan, Xu Guangping, Hu Yuzhi, Zhao Puchu, Qu Tangliang, Wu Maosun, Wang Mingyuan, Yang Chunsong, Zheng Senyu, and Li Chunlin, prominent figures from various circles in Beijing.

Japanese peace advocate Kinkazu Saionji and Korean peace advocate Hwang Bong-koo, who are currently in Beijing, also came to the airport to welcome the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: The Japanese Coal Miners' Union Delegation, led by Chairman Reijiro Takagaki, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a friendly visit to China and to participate in the National Day celebrations at the invitation of the National Committee of the Coal Miners' Union of China.

Li Chunming, Acting Chairman of the National Committee of the Coal Miners' Union of China, and others were at the airport to welcome them. (Attached picture)

### 日本阻止修改安全条约国民会议代表团到京  日本煤矿工会代表团到京

1960-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　日本阻止修改安全条约国民会议代表团到京
    日本煤矿工会代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　由团长卢叶武和副团长佐野文一郎、东宫考率领的日本阻止修改安全条约国民会议代表团一行十二人，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、包尔汉，北京各方面人士楚图南、许广平、胡愈之、赵朴初、区棠亮、吴茂荪、王明远、杨春松、郑森禹、李春林等。
    在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一和朝鲜和平人士黄凤九也到机场欢迎。
    新华社29日讯　应中国煤矿工会全国委员会邀请来我国进行友好访问并参加国庆观礼的日本煤矿工会代表团一行十人，在团长高垣礼二郎率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国煤矿工会全国委员会代理主席李春明等。（附图片）
