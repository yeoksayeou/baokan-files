Title: Delegation of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association Returns to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th - The delegation of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association, led by Sun Xiaocun, Vice President of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association, returned to Beijing by plane today after visiting Vietnam at the invitation of the Viet-China Friendship Association to participate in the celebrations of the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Yang Xiufeng, President of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association, and Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and Director of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association, welcomed the delegation at the airport.
    Tran Trong Quat, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 中越友协代表团回到北京

1960-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    中越友协代表团回到北京
    新华社20日讯　由中越友好协会副会长孙晓村率领的中越友好协会代表团，应越中友好协会邀请去越南参加了越南民主共和国成立十五周年的庆祝活动后，今天乘飞机回到北京。
    中越友好协会会长杨秀峰和对外文化联络委员会副主任、中越友协理事丁西林到机场欢迎。
    越南民主共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办陈仲括也到机场欢迎。
