Title: Chinese Acrobatic Troupe Returns to Beijing After Performance Tour in Yemen
Author:
Date: 1960-09-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 13th -- The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, consisting of twenty-seven members led by Wu Kunshan, returned to Beijing on September 11 after a two-month performance tour in Yemen at the invitation of the government of the Kingdom of Yemen. The troupe's performances were warmly welcomed by the vast number of Yemeni audiences.

### 赴也门访问演出的中国杂技团回京

1960-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    赴也门访问演出的中国杂技团回京
    新华社13日讯　应也门王国政府邀请在也门作了两个月访问演出的中国杂技团一行二十七人，在团长伍坤山率领下，于9月11日回到北京。杂技团的表演，受到广大也门观众的热烈欢迎。
