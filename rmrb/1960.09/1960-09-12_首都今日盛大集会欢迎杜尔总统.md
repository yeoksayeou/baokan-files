Title: Grand Rally in the Capital Today to Welcome President Touré
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th. People from all walks of life in the capital will hold a grand rally on the afternoon of the 12th to warmly welcome President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea and the government delegation of the Republic of Guinea he leads. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of the conference from 5:20 pm on the 12th, using the frequency of the first program. Beijing Television will also broadcast simultaneously.

### 首都今日盛大集会欢迎杜尔总统

1960-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    首都今日盛大集会欢迎杜尔总统
    新华社11日讯　首都各界人民将在12日下午举行盛大集会，热烈欢迎几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔和由他率领的几内亚共和国政府代表团。中央人民广播电台将从12日下午五点二十分开始，用第一套节目的频率转播这次大会的实况。北京电视台也同时转播。
