Title: Argentine Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: The Argentine Trade Union Delegation, consisting of fifteen members, led by Juan Iapiichino, Secretary of the Argentine National United Movement of Food Industry Workers, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions for a friendly visit to China and to participate in the National Day celebrations. Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, He Ruilin, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union, and Fan Jixian, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Agricultural and Forestry Trade Union.
The delegation members, Mr. and Mrs. Iscaro, are on their way to China.

### 阿根廷工会代表团到京

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

    阿根廷工会代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　应中华全国总工会的邀请来我国作友好访问并参加国庆观礼的阿根廷工会代表团一行十五人，在团长、阿根廷全国食品业工人统一运动书记胡安·伊阿皮奇诺率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会书记处书记康永和，中国机械工会全国委员会副主席贺瑞林和中国农林工会全国委员会副主席范继先等。
    代表团成员伊斯卡罗夫妇正在来华途中。
