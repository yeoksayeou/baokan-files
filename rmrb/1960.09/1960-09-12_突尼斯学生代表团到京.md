Title: Tunisian Student Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Tunisian student delegation, consisting of five members, led by Ben Nour Ahmed, Deputy Secretary-General of the Tunisian National Student Union, arrived in Beijing by plane yesterday at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation.

　　Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 突尼斯学生代表团到京

1960-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    突尼斯学生代表团到京
    新华社10日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的突尼斯学生代表团一行五人，在突尼斯全国学生联合会副总书记本努尔·艾哈迈德率领下，在昨天乘飞机到达北京。
  　前往机场欢迎的有中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立等人。
