Title: Panchen Erdeni Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: Panchen Erdeni, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Acting Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning from Xi'an to attend the celebration of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
　　Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long and Xi Zhongxun, and Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Huang Yanpei and Li Weihan, welcomed him at the airport.
　　Wang Feng, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, accompanied Panchen Erdeni to Beijing from Xi'an.
　　Guo Xilan, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Work Committee of the Communist Party of China, Panchen Erdeni's parents, and Laming Yexi Chuchen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and the Khenpo Enqiu Living Buddha, also arrived in Beijing on the same plane today.
　　Panchen Erdeni stepped off the plane amidst warm applause and presented Hadas to Vice Premiers He Long and Xi Zhongxun, and Vice Chairmen Huang Yanpei and Li Weihan. Vice Premier He Long and others returned the Hadas. Children of the capital presented flowers to Panchen Erdeni. Then, Zhang Su, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, accompanied Panchen Erdeni and others by car to the guesthouse.
　　Also at the airport to welcome him were Wang Weizhou, Zhang Zhizhong, Ping Jiesan, Zhang Zhiyi, Wang Shoudao, Chen Qiyuan, Wang Jinxiang, and Xia Yan, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and ministers and vice ministers of various ministries of the government, Xie Fumin, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, Sa Kongliao and Xie Hechou, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and He Chengxiang, Director of the Bureau of Religious Affairs.
　　Also at the airport to welcome him were Zhang Guohua, Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region who is in Beijing, and Tan Guansan, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Work Committee of the Communist Party of China, and others.
　　Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Xirao Jiacuo, President of the Buddhist Association of China, Dapu Sheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, and more than two hundred people from all walks of life in the capital, were also at the airport today to welcome him.

### 班禅额尔德尼到达北京

1960-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　班禅额尔德尼到达北京
    新华社27日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼今天上午乘飞机从西安到达北京，前来参加建国十一周年庆祝典礼。
    国务院副总理贺龙、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培、李维汉到机场欢迎。
    中共中央统战部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋从西安陪同班禅额尔德尼到京。
    中共西藏工委副书记郭锡兰，班禅额尔德尼的父亲、母亲和西藏自治区筹备委员会副秘书长拉敏·益喜楚臣、经师恩久活佛等，也在今天同机到达北京。
    班禅额尔德尼在热烈的掌声中走下飞机，向贺龙、习仲勋副总理，黄炎培、李维汉副委员长献了哈达，贺龙副总理等回敬了哈达。首都少年儿童向班禅额尔德尼献了花。然后，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员张苏、民族事务委员会副主任刘春，陪同班禅额尔德尼等乘车前往宾馆。
    到机场欢迎的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、政协全国委员会委员和政府各部部长、副部长王维舟、张治中、平杰三、张执一、王首道、陈其瑗、汪金祥、夏衍，人大民族委员会副主任委员谢扶民，民族事务委员会副主任萨空了、谢鹤筹，宗教事务局局长何成湘。
    到机场欢迎的，还有在京的西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员张国华，中共西藏工委副书记谭冠三等。
    北京市副市长吴晗，中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生和首都各界人民二百多人，今天都到机场欢迎。
