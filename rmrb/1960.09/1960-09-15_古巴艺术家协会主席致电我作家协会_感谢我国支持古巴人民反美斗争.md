Title: Chairman of the Cuban Artists Association Sends a Telegram to Our Writers Association, Thanking China for Supporting the Cuban People's Anti-American Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-09-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 14th. The Chinese Writers Association today received a telegram from Violeta Casal, Chairman of the Cuban Artists Association. In the telegram, Violeta Casal thanked the Chinese writers for supporting the Cuban people and Cuban writers in their struggle against American imperialism.
Violeta Casal's telegram stated: "Thank you to the Chinese Writers Association for your support of our struggle against American imperialism. We know that with the help of the heroic Chinese people, as well as the assistance of other socialist countries and peace-loving people of all countries, we will surely achieve victory."

### 古巴艺术家协会主席致电我作家协会  感谢我国支持古巴人民反美斗争

1960-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴艺术家协会主席致电我作家协会
    感谢我国支持古巴人民反美斗争
    新华社14日讯　中国作家协会今天收到了古巴艺术家协会主席维奥莱塔·卡萨尔的一份电报。维奥莱塔·卡萨尔在电报中感谢中国作家们支持古巴人民和古巴作家反对美帝国主义的斗争。
    维奥莱塔·卡萨尔的电报说：“感谢中国作家协会对我们反对美帝国主义斗争的支援。我们知道，有了英雄的中国人民的援助及其他社会主义国家和各国爱好和平人民的援助，我们一定能够取得胜利。”
