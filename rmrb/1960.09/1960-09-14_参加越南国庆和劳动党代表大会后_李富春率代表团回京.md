Title: Li Fuchun Leads Delegation Back to Beijing After Attending Vietnam's National Day and the Labor Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-09-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th – After attending the celebrations for the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Third National Congress of the Vietnamese Labor Party, Li Fuchun, head of the Chinese Communist Party and Chinese government delegation, Marshal Ye Jianying, a member of the delegation, and all accompanying staff returned to Beijing by special plane this afternoon.

Those who greeted them at the airport included He Long, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council; Luo Ruiqing, Vice Premier of the State Council; Cheng Qian and Lin Feng, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Fu Zuoyi, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council; Tan Zheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Minister of National Defense; Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Yang Xiufeng, President of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association; Liu Ningyi and Zhang Yan, Deputy Directors of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council; Wang Guangwei, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission; Qi Yanming, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council; and Han Nianlong, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Liao Chengzhi, member of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council, and a member of the Chinese Party and government delegation, who had returned earlier, also greeted them at the airport.

Trần Trọng Quát, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and embassy officials also greeted them at the airport.

### 参加越南国庆和劳动党代表大会后  李富春率代表团回京

1960-09-14
第2版()
专栏：

    参加越南国庆和劳动党代表大会后
    李富春率代表团回京
    新华社13日讯　中国共产党和中国政府代表团团长李富春、团员叶剑英元帅和全体随行人员，在应邀参加了越南民主共和国成立十五周年庆祝典礼和越南劳动党第三次全国代表大会后，今天下午乘专机回到北京。
    到机场迎接的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理贺龙，国务院副总理罗瑞卿，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长程潜、林枫，国防委员会副主席傅作义，中共中央书记处书记、国防部副部长谭政，中共中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆，中越友好协会会长杨秀峰，国务院外事办公室副主任刘宁一、张彦，国家计划委员会副主任王光伟，国务院副秘书长齐燕铭和外交部部长助理韩念龙等。
    到机场迎接的，还有先期回国的中国党政代表团团员、中共中央委员、国务院外事办公室副主任廖承志。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办陈仲括和大使馆官员也到机场迎接。
