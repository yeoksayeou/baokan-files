Title: US-Thailand Conspiracy to Overthrow the New Government of Laos; Nosavan Group's "Revolutionary Committee" Threatens Non-Recognition of Phouma Cabinet; Phouma Declares State of Emergency and Charges Royal Army with Restoring National Tranquility

Author:

Date: 1960-09-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 12th: Vientiane Dispatch: U.S. imperialism and Thailand are executing a plot to overthrow the new government of Laos.

According to Vientiane Radio, the Savannakhet Radio, controlled by Phoumi Nosavan, broadcast on the 10th a "statement" in the name of the so-called "Revolutionary Committee" Chairman, Lao National Inspection Minister Boun Oum Na Champassak, aimed at overthrowing the new Lao government headed by Prince Souvanna Phouma, threatening that this "Revolutionary Committee" would "restore national peace and order," "not recognize the Lao new government headed by Prince Souvanna Phouma," and would "stop the implementation of national laws." According to Reuters from Vientiane, Savannakhet Radio also broadcast a statement by Boun Oum Na Champassak on the 11th, prohibiting anyone from listening to programs from the Lao Fighting Forces Radio, Vientiane Radio, Hanoi Radio, and Beijing Radio. The statement also threatened that no contact with Vientiane, Laos, and the outside world would be allowed without the approval of the "Revolutionary Committee."

Vientiane Radio broadcast on the evening of the 10th that the Lao Royal Government issued an announcement on the 10th regarding the "statement" of this so-called "Revolutionary Committee," saying that the statement of the "Revolutionary Committee" "only benefits the enemies of the country." Vientiane Radio also broadcast on the evening of the 11th that the President of the Lao Royal Council, Kou Abhay, the Speaker of the National Assembly, Somseanhit, and Prime Minister Prince Phouma met with the King on September 11th to report on the situation of this "Revolutionary Committee" creating chaos in the country.

The radio station also broadcast a public statement issued by Prime Minister Prince Souvanna Phouma of the Lao Royal Government on the 10th, announcing a state of emergency throughout the country. The statement said, "According to the provisions of the law, the Commander-in-Chief of the Royal Army, Ratikoun, has been ordered to use military force to restore national tranquility."

Hanoi's "People's Daily" pointed out in a commentary today that this "Revolutionary Committee" is manipulated by U.S. imperialism and Phoumi Nosavan. U.S. imperialism has increased the allocation of 15 million U.S. dollars to the Nosavan Group, while instructing Thai reactionaries to cooperate with the Phoumi Group.

### 美国泰国阴谋推翻老挝新政府  诺萨万集团的“革命委员会”扬言不承认富马内阁  富马宣布紧急状态并责成王国军队恢复全国安宁

1960-09-14
第6版()
专栏：

    美国泰国阴谋推翻老挝新政府
    诺萨万集团的“革命委员会”扬言不承认富马内阁
    富马宣布紧急状态并责成王国军队恢复全国安宁
    据新华社河内12日电　万象消息：美帝国主义和泰国正在执行一个推翻老挝新政府的阴谋。
    据万象电台报道，受富米·诺萨万控制的沙湾拿吉电台10日广播了以所谓“革命委员会”主席、老挝国家监察部部长文翁·纳占巴塞的名义发表的、旨在推翻以富马亲王为首的老挝新政府的一项“声明”，扬言这个“革命委员会”要“恢复国家的安宁秩序”，“不承认以梭发那·富马亲王为首的老挝新政府”，要“停止执行国家的法律”。据路透社自万象报道，沙湾拿吉电台11日还广播了文翁·纳占巴塞的一个声明，禁止任何人收听寮国战斗部队电台、万象电台、河内电台和北京电台的节目，声明还扬言，非经“革命委员会”批准，禁止同万象、寮国和外界接触。
    万象电台10日晚上广播说，老挝王国政府10日就这个所谓“革命委员会”的“声明”发表公告说，
  “革命委员会”的这个声明“只有利于国家的敌人”。万象电台11日晚上还广播说，老挝皇廷会议议长古·阿派，国民议会议长松萨尼特和首相富马亲王在9月11日晋见了国王，向国王报告了这个“革命委员会”在国内制造混乱的情况。
    这家电台还广播了老挝王国政府首相梭发那·富马亲王10日发表的宣布全国进入紧急状态的一项公报，公报说，“根据法律的规定，已责成王国军队总司令拉迪功使用军队力量来恢复全国安宁。”
    河内“人民报”今天在评论里指出，这个“革命委员会”是由美帝国主义和富米·诺萨万操纵的。美帝国主义已经增拨一千五百万美元给诺萨万集团，同时指使泰国反动派与富米集团配合行动。
