Title: Japanese Democratic Youth League Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 16th. The Japanese Democratic Youth League delegation, led by Maeda Minoru, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Japanese Democratic Youth League, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 15th for a visit to China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation.

That evening, the All-China Youth Federation hosted a banquet to welcome the guests. Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and Secretaries of the Secretariat Hu Keshi and Liang Buting, as well as Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation Wang Chuanbin, attended the banquet.

### 日本民主青年同盟代表团到京

1960-09-17
第6版()
专栏：

    日本民主青年同盟代表团到京
    新华社16日讯　由日本民主青年同盟中央委员会总书记前田稔率领的日本民主青年同盟代表团，应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问，已经在15日下午乘火车到达北京。
    当晚，全国青联设宴欢迎客人。中国共产主义青年团中央委员会第一书记胡耀邦和书记处书记胡克实、梁步庭，全国青联副秘书长王传斌等出席了宴会。
