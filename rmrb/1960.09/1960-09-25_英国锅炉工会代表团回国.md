Title: British Boiler Workers' Union Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: The British Boiler Workers' Union delegation of four, led by the head of the delegation, John Heppell, left Beijing by plane yesterday to return home. He Ruilin and Yu Huan, vice chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Workers' Union, were among those who went to the airport to see them off.

### 英国锅炉工会代表团回国

1960-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　英国锅炉工会代表团回国
    新华社24日讯　英国锅炉工会代表团一行四人，在团长约翰·赫波怀德率领下，昨天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有中国机械工会全国委员会副主席贺瑞林、于桓等。
