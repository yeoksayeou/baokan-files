Title: Soviet Three Musicians Return Home After Several Performances in Various Parts of China
Author:
Date: 1960-09-20
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, September 19th. T. Nikolayeva, Soviet pianist, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, and winner of multiple international piano competitions, who visited China according to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 18th to return home. Among those who saw her off at the airport were Wang Yi, Deputy Director of the First Bureau of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and Li Delun, conductor of the Central Philharmonic Orchestra.

    Z. Sikhmurzayeva, Soviet violinist and winner of the Tchaikovsky International Violin Competition, and A. Sobolev, piano accompanist, who accompanied Nikolayeva on her visit to China, had returned home earlier.

    The three musicians visited Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Harbin and other cities, holding nine solo concerts, and jointly held two symphony concerts with the Central Philharmonic Orchestra and the Shanghai Symphony Orchestra respectively, which were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 在我国各地举行多场演出后  苏联三位音乐家回国

1960-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    在我国各地举行多场演出后
    苏联三位音乐家回国
    新华社19日讯　根据中苏文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的苏联钢琴家、俄罗斯联邦社会主义共和国功勋演员、多次国际钢琴比赛获奖者塔·尼古拉耶娃，18日早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场送行的有对外文化联络委员会第一司副司长王仪、中央乐团指挥李德伦等。
    随尼古拉耶娃一同来中国访问的苏联小提琴家、柴可夫斯基国际小提琴比赛获奖者佐·西赫木尔扎耶娃和钢琴伴奏亚·索波列夫，已先期回国。
    三位音乐家曾先后访问了北京、上海、天津、沈阳、哈尔滨等城市，举行了九场独奏音乐会，并分别与中央乐团和上海交响乐团联合举行了两场交响音乐会，受到了听众的热烈欢迎。
