Title: Danish Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 5th September. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Danish cultural delegation, consisting of Mr. Knud Lomholt, Mr. P.V. Glob, and Mr. Poul Hansen, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 丹麦文化代表团到京

1960-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    丹麦文化代表团到京
    新华社5日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的丹麦文化代表团克努德·隆贝克、彼·维·格洛布、保罗·汉森等一行三人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
