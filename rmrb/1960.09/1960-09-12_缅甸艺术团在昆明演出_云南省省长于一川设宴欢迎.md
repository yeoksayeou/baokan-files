Title: Burmese Art Troupe Performs in Kunming; Yunnan Governor Yu Yichuan Hosts Banquet

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, September 11th: The Burmese Federal Cultural and Friendship Art Troupe performed in Kunming on the evening of the 10th, receiving a warm welcome.

	Yunnan Governor Yu Yichuan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, and others watched the Burmese artists' excellent performances.

	Burmese Federal Consul General in Kunming U Maung Maung So, and Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming Vu Van Huong, also watched the performance.

	During the evening, the Burmese artists performed the songs "Sino-Burmese Friendship Song" and "Blessings," praising the long and profound friendship between China and Burma, which were unanimously praised by the audience. The Burmese artists also sang the song "The East is Red," which is loved by the Chinese people, and received a warm welcome from the audience.

	After the performance, Yunnan Governor Yu Yichuan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, and others went on stage to congratulate the Burmese artists on their successful performance. Yunnan literary and art workers presented flowers to the Burmese artists. The Burmese artists waved bouquets and cheered: "Long live Sino-Burmese friendship! Long live! Long live!!" At this time, the audience applauded thunderously, filled with an atmosphere of cordiality and friendship.

	Yunnan Governor Yu Yichuan held a banquet on the afternoon of September 9th to warmly welcome the Burmese Federal Cultural and Friendship Art Troupe.

	Those attending the banquet included Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan and others.

	Burmese Federal Consul General in Kunming U Maung Maung So and his wife, and Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming Vu Van Huong also attended the banquet.

	At the meeting, Yu Yichuan and the head of the Burmese Federal Cultural and Friendship Art Troupe, U Ba Swe, both spoke.

	Major General Ding Rongchang, head of the Kunming garrison, held a banquet at noon on the 11th to warmly welcome the Burmese Federal Cultural and Friendship Art Troupe for their wonderful performance for the Kunming garrison.

	Recently, the Burmese artists also conducted sightseeing visits in Kunming.

### 缅甸艺术团在昆明演出  云南省省长于一川设宴欢迎

1960-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸艺术团在昆明演出
    云南省省长于一川设宴欢迎
    据新华社昆明11日电　缅甸联邦文化友好艺术团10日晚在昆明演出，受到热烈欢迎。
    云南省省长于一川，对外文委副主任楚图南等观看了缅甸艺术家的精彩演出。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭，越南民主共和国驻昆明领事武文享，也观看了演出。
    晚会上，缅甸艺术家们演出了歌颂中缅两国悠久、深厚友谊的歌曲“中缅友谊之歌”和“祝福”，受到听众的同声赞扬。缅甸艺术家们还演唱了我国人民喜爱的歌曲“东方红”，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    演出结束，云南省省长于一川、对外文委副主任楚图南等登上舞台，祝贺缅甸艺术家演出成功。云南省文艺工作者向缅甸艺术家献了花。缅甸艺术家们挥舞花束欢呼：“缅中友好万岁！万岁！万岁！！”这时，全场掌声雷动，充满了亲切、友好的气氛。
    云南省省长于一川9日下午举行宴会，热烈欢迎缅甸联邦文化友好艺术团。
    出席宴会的，有对外文委副主任楚图南等。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭夫妇，越南民主共和国驻昆明领事武文享也出席了宴会。
    会上，于一川和缅甸联邦文化友好艺术团团长吴巴盛都讲了话。
    驻昆明部队负责人丁荣昌少将11日中午举行宴会，热烈欢迎缅甸联邦文化友好艺术团为驻昆明部队进行的精彩演出。
    近日，缅甸艺术家们还在昆明进行了参观访问。
