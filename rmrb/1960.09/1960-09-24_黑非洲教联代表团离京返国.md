Title: Black African Teachers' Federation Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: The Black African Teachers' Federation delegation, consisting of eleven members led by N'Dongo Amadou, Deputy Secretary-General of the Unified Non-Religious Education Union of Senegal, left Beijing by plane today to return home. Among those who welcomed them at the airport were Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Educational Workers' Union, and others.

### 黑非洲教联代表团离京返国

1960-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

　  黑非洲教联代表团离京返国
    新华社23日讯　黑非洲教师联合会代表团一行十一人，在团长、塞内加尔非宗教教育统一工会副总书记恩多·阿玛杜率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明等。
