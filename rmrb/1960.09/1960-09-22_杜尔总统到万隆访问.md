Title: President Touré Visits Bandung
Author:
Date: 1960-09-22
Page: 6
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 21st: Guinean President Sékou Touré and his entourage, accompanied by President Sukarno, arrived in Bandung this afternoon by car from Bogor.

    Before leaving Bogor this morning, they visited the Bogor Botanical Gardens.

    According to the prearranged plan, the Guinean President will visit the historical building in Bandung where the Asian-African Conference was held in 1955.

    Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio told reporters today that after President Touré returns to Jakarta by plane tomorrow, he will hold formal talks with President Sukarno at the Independence Palace in the afternoon.

    President Touré left Jakarta for Bogor on the 20th. Before leaving Jakarta, President Sukarno hosted a state banquet for President Touré at the Presidential Palace.

### 杜尔总统到万隆访问

1960-09-22
第6版()
专栏：

　  杜尔总统到万隆访问
    新华社雅加达21日电　几内亚总统塞古·杜尔和他的随行人员在苏加诺总统陪同下今天下午乘汽车从茂物到达万隆。
    今天上午离茂物以前他们曾参观了茂物植物园。
    按照预定计划，几内亚总统将在万隆参观1955年举行过亚非会议的历史性建筑。
    据印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约今天对记者说：杜尔总统明天乘飞机回到雅加达之后，下午将在独立宫同苏加诺总统举行正式会谈。
    杜尔总统是在20日离开雅加达到茂物访问的。在离雅加达前苏加诺总统在总统府设国宴招待杜尔总统。
