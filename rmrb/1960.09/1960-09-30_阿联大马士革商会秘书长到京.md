Title: Secretary-General of the Damascus Chamber of Commerce of the United Arab Republic Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Dr. Hanmour, Secretary-General of the Damascus Chamber of Commerce of the United Arab Republic, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this morning at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.
    Ji Chao-ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, greeted them at the airport.

### 阿联大马士革商会秘书长到京

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

    阿联大马士革商会秘书长到京
    新华社29日讯　应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请前来我国访问的阿拉伯联合共和国大马士革商会秘书长汉莫尔博士和夫人今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎前往机场迎接。
