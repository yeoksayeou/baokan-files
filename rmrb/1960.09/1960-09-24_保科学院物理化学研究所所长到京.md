Title: Director of the Institute of Physical Chemistry of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. Rostislav Atanasov Kaishev, Corresponding Academician and Director of the Institute of Physical Chemistry of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit and lectures in accordance with the 1960 executive plan for scientific cooperation between the Chinese and Bulgarian Academies of Sciences.

　　Those who went to the airport to greet him were: Yun Zi-qiang, Deputy Director of the Department of Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Shi Ru-wei, Director of the Institute of Applied Physics; Zhao Jiu-zhang, Director of the Institute of Geophysics, and others.

### 保科学院物理化学研究所所长到京

1960-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

　  保科学院物理化学研究所所长到京
    新华社23日讯　根据中保两国科学院科学合作1960年执行计划规定来我国访问和讲学的保加利亚科学院物理化学研究所所长罗斯季斯拉夫·阿塔纳索夫·卡伊谢夫通讯院士，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有：中国科学院数学物理学化学部副主任恽子强、应用物理研究所所长施汝为、地球物理研究所所长赵九章等。
