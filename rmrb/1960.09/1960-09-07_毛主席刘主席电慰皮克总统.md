Title: Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu Send Telegram of Condolences to President Pieck
Author:
Date: 1960-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th September. Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Liu Shaoqi today sent a telegram of condolences to the President of the German Democratic Republic, Wilhelm Pieck. The telegram reads as follows:

Berlin

To Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic,

Upon learning of your serious illness, we send you our sincere condolences with the utmost concern and sincerely hope for your speedy recovery.

					Mao Zedong Liu Shaoqi
					September 6, 1960

### 毛主席刘主席电慰皮克总统

1960-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席刘主席电慰皮克总统
    新华社6日讯　毛泽东主席和刘少奇主席今天打电报慰问德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克，电文如下：柏林德意志民主共和国总统亲爱的威廉·皮克同志：
  获悉你患重病的消息，我们怀着最关切的心情，向你致诚挚的慰问，并衷心地盼望你早日恢复健康。
                    毛泽东　刘少奇	                    1960年9月6日
