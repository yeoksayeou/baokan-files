Title: Ceylon Medical Delegation Visits China
Author:
Date: 1960-09-20
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, September 18th: A ten-member Ceylon medical delegation led by Arumanayagam left here today for China for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association.
　　Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon Zhang Canming held a reception at the embassy last night to bid farewell to the delegation. Ambassador Zhang said at the reception that, through mutual visits by delegations from the two countries in the past, friendship and mutual understanding between the two countries have been greatly promoted. He believes that the delegation's visit will not only provide opportunities for the medical circles of the two countries to learn from each other and promote common scientific development, but also further strengthen the friendship between the people of the two countries.
　　Wilmot Perera, Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, also attended the reception.

### 锡兰医学界代表团来我国访问

1960-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰医学界代表团来我国访问
    新华社科伦坡18日电　由阿鲁马那亚干率领的锡兰医学界代表团一行十人应中华医学会的邀请今天离开这里前往中国进行友好访问。
    中国驻锡兰大使张灿明昨天晚上在大使馆举行了招待会欢送代表团。张大使在招待会上说，过去通过两国代表团的互相访问，已经大大地促进了两国之间的友谊和相互了解。他相信这个代表团的访问不仅将提供两国医学界相互学习的机会和促进共同的科学发展，而且将进一步加强两国人民之间的友谊。
    锡中友好协会主席威尔莫特·佩雷拉也出席了招待会。
