Title: Singing the Praises of the Glorious Achievements of the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation: Hanoi Youth Hold Grand Garden Party Evening
Author:
Date: 1960-09-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 3rd. More than 20,000 young people from all walks of life in Hanoi held a grand garden party this evening in Bach Thao Park, singing heartily about the great achievements made by the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the past fifteen years.

    President Ho Chi Minh, the beloved leader of the Vietnamese people, and Party and state leaders such as Le Duan, Truong Chinh, Pham Van Dong, Ton Duc Thang, and Vo Nguyen Giap, spent this joyful evening with the youth.

    The party and government delegations and the delegations of fraternal parties from fraternal countries who were invited to participate in the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Third National Congress of the Vietnam Workers' Party, as well as other distinguished guests, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to Vietnam, all attended tonight's garden party.

    Tonight, Bach Thao Park was transformed into a sea of ​​joy, with thousands of colorful electric lights shining brilliantly among the green trees, the lake water sparkling, and blooming lotuses and various exotic flowers and plants fragrant. Artists from Hanoi's art troupes performed wonderful programs at the garden party.

    Young people sang and danced, enjoying themselves to the fullest. The garden party continued until late at night.

### 尽情欢唱建国十五年的辉煌成就  河内青年举行盛大游园晚会  越南领导人和兄弟国家兄弟党代表团出席联欢

1960-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    尽情欢唱建国十五年的辉煌成就
    河内青年举行盛大游园晚会
    越南领导人和兄弟国家兄弟党代表团出席联欢
    新华社河内3日电　河内各界青年两万多人，今天晚上在百草公园举行盛大游园联欢晚会，纵情欢唱越南民主共和国十五年来所取得的伟大成就。
    越南人民敬爱的领袖胡志明主席和黎笋、长征、范文同、孙德胜、武元甲等党和国家领导人，同青年们一起度过了这个欢乐的夜晚。
    应邀前来参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年和越南劳动党第三次全国代表大会的各兄弟国家党政代表团和各兄弟党的代表团以及其他贵宾，各国驻越南外交使节都参加了今晚的游园晚会。
    今晚，百草公园变成一片欢乐的海洋，绿树丛中万盏彩色电灯灿烂辉煌，湖水波光闪闪，盛开的荷花和各种奇花异草芳香四溢。河内各文艺团体的艺术家们在游园会上表演了精彩的节目。
    青年们载歌载舞，尽情欢乐，游园一直进行到深夜。
