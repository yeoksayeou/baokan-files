Title: Swedish Men's Table Tennis Team Competes in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-09-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 20th: The Swedish men's table tennis team played its second friendly match in China against the Guangzhou Youth Team on the evening of the 19th. After more than three hours of fierce fighting, the visiting team won with a score of 5-4.
    The visiting team is scheduled to fly to Shanghai on the 21st.

### 瑞典男子乒乓球队在广州举行比赛

1960-09-21
第6版()
专栏：

　  瑞典男子乒乓球队在广州举行比赛
    据新华社广州20日电　瑞典男子乒乓球队19日晚和广州青年队作访华第二场友谊比赛，经激战三个多小时，结果客队以五比四获胜。
    客队定于21日飞往上海。
