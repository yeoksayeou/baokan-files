Title: West German Revanchists Deliberately Provoke, Shoot at Border Posts of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1960-09-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 19th: According to reports in the newspapers here today, the West German side engaged in provocations on the border from 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM on September 17th.

The report stated that several West Germans in military uniform and one person in civilian clothes fired nine shots at the border post of the People's Police of the German Democratic Republic. These border police were performing their duties in the Gardebusch district of the Schwerin region.

### 西德复仇主义分子蓄意寻衅  射击德意志民主共和国边防岗哨

1960-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

　  西德复仇主义分子蓄意寻衅
    射击德意志民主共和国边防岗哨
    新华社柏林19日电　据这里的报纸今天报道：西德方面于9月17日十五时到十六时在边界寻衅。
    报道说，一些身穿军服的西德人和一个身穿便服的人向德意志民主共和国的人民警察边防岗哨开了九枪。这些边防警察正在什未林专区的加德布施县执行任务。
