Title: Friendship Between Chinese and Mongolian People Further Enhanced; Mongolian "Youth Truth" Newspaper Congratulates Sino-Mongolian Friendship Week
Author:
Date: 1960-09-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 26th. The Mongolian "Youth Truth" newspaper published a commentary on the 25th entitled "The Eternal Friendship Between Our Two Peoples," congratulating the "Sino-Mongolian Friendship Week" activities currently being held here.

The commentary said that "Sino-Mongolian Friendship Week" activities are being held in various parts of Mongolia. Members of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League and young people are actively participating in this activity.

The commentary pointed out that the unbreakable and rock-solid friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian people has a glorious history. The friendly relations established between our two countries express the lofty aspiration of the two peoples to build socialism and communism with common struggle and strength.

The commentary pointed out that, based on the Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement signed between Mongolia and China in 1952, cooperation between the two countries has developed even further. The People's Republic of China's repeated brotherly and selfless assistance to Mongolia has made a significant contribution to accelerating Mongolia's socialist construction.

The commentary pointed out that the treaties and agreements signed during Premier Zhou Enlai's friendly visit to Mongolia once again pushed the friendly cooperation between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples to a new stage.

The commentary pointed out that the "Sino-Mongolian Friendship Week" activities have contributed to further enhancing the friendship between the two peoples and further inspired the Mongolian people's confidence in building socialism, which is of great significance to enhancing the solidarity of the entire socialist camp.

### 蒙中两国人民友谊进一步增强　蒙古“青年真理报”祝贺蒙中友好旬

1960-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　蒙中两国人民友谊进一步增强
　　蒙古“青年真理报”祝贺蒙中友好旬
    新华社乌兰巴托26日电　蒙古“青年真理报”25日发表了题为“我们两国人民的永恒友谊”的评论，祝贺正在这里举行的“蒙中友好旬”的活动。
    评论说，蒙古各地正在举行“蒙中友好旬”的活动。我们蒙古革命青年团团员和青年群众积极参加了这项活动。
    评论指出，蒙中两国人民牢不可破的坚如磐石的友谊有着光辉的历史。我们两国之间建立的友好关系，表达了两国人民用共同的斗争和力量来建设社会主义和共产主义的崇高愿望。
    评论指出，在蒙中两国1952年签订的经济及文化合作协定的基础上，两国之间的合作有了更大的发展。中华人民共和国给予蒙古的一次又一次兄弟般的大公无私的援助，对加快蒙古社会主义建设作出了巨大贡献。
    评论指出，周恩来总理在蒙古友好访问期间签订的条约和协定，再一次把蒙中两国人民的友好合作推向了一个新的阶段。
    评论指出，“蒙中友好旬”活动为进一步增进两国人民的友谊作出了贡献，并进一步鼓舞了蒙古人民建设社会主义的信心，它对增进整个社会主义阵营的团结有着很大意义。
