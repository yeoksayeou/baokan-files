Title: Sichuan Provincial Song and Dance Troupe's Performance in Ismailia Receives Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ismailia, 16th - The Chinese Sichuan Provincial Song and Dance Troupe gave a special performance to 4,500 soldiers and civilians in Ismailia in the Suez Canal Zone of the United Arab Republic on the 15th, and was warmly welcomed. The song and dance troupe performed eighteen programs full of national characteristics. The audience responded to each program with prolonged applause.

This morning, Zhang Tiesheng, the head of the song and dance troupe, and Lieutenant General Hassan Abdul Latif of the Eastern Military Region of the United Arab Republic visited each other and held friendly talks.

### 我四川省歌舞团在伊士美利亚演出受热烈欢迎

1960-09-17
第6版()
专栏：

    我四川省歌舞团在伊士美利亚演出受热烈欢迎
    新华社伊士美利亚16日电　中国四川省歌舞团15日在阿联苏伊士运河区的伊士美利亚为四千五百名军人和市民作了一次特别演出，受到热烈欢迎。歌舞团表演了十八个富有民族色彩的节目。观众对每个节目都报以长时间的掌声。
    今天上午，歌舞团团长张铁生和阿联东部军区的哈桑·阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫中将相互访问，并进行了友好的交谈。
