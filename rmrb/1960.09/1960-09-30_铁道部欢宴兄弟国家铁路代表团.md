Title: Ministry of Railways Hosts Banquet for Railway Delegations from Brotherly Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 6
　　
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: The Ministry of Railways hosted a banquet tonight to warmly welcome the railway delegations from eight Socialist countries—Bulgaria, Hungary, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the German Democratic Republic, the Mongolian Peoples Republic, Poland, Romania, and Czechoslovakia—who are invited to participate in our country's National Day celebrations, as well as staff members from the Railway Cooperation Organization and the Railway Transport Committee. These railway delegations from brotherly countries arrived in Beijing today after attending the Fifth Conference of Ministers of Railways of Socialist Countries held in Pyongyang, North Korea.

Present at today's banquet were Lu Zhengcao, Acting Minister of Railways, Vice Ministers Liu Jianzhang, Shi Zhiren, Guo Lu, and Vice Minister of Communications Pan Qi, among others.

### 铁道部欢宴兄弟国家铁路代表团

1960-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　铁道部欢宴兄弟国家铁路代表团
    新华社29日讯　铁道部今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎应邀来我国参加国庆节庆祝活动的保加利亚、匈牙利、越南民主共和国、德意志民主共和国、蒙古人民共和国、波兰、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克等八个社会主义国家的铁路代表团以及铁路合作组织、铁路运输委员会的工作人员。这些兄弟国家的铁路代表团是出席在朝鲜平壤举行的第五届社会主义国家铁道部长会议后在今天来到北京的。
    出席今天宴会的有铁道部代理部长吕正操，副部长刘建章、石志仁、郭鲁和交通部副部长潘琪等。
