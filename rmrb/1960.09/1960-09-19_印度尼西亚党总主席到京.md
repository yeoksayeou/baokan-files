Title: Indonesian Party Chairman Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, September 18th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Asmara Hadi, Chairman of the Indonesian Party, his wife, and Asnawi Said, Secretary-General of the Indonesian Party, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to our country. Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General, and others welcomed the guests at the airport.

### 印度尼西亚党总主席到京

1960-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚党总主席到京
    新华社18日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚党总主席阿斯马腊·哈迪和他的夫人以及印度尼西亚党秘书长阿斯纳威·赛义德，今天乘飞机到达北京。中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、副秘书长吴晓达等到机场欢迎客人。
