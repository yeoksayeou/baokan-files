Title: Socialist Countries and Fraternal Party Delegations Arrive in Hanoi One After Another to Attend Vietnam's National Day Celebrations and the Third National Congress of the Labor Party

Author:

Date: 1960-09-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 31st. Party and government delegations from socialist countries and delegations from fraternal parties, invited to attend the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Third National Congress of the Vietnam Workers' Party, are arriving in Hanoi one after another.

To date, the party and government delegations from socialist countries that have arrived are: the Soviet Party and Government Delegation, the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, the Korean Party and Government Delegation, the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, the Mongolian Party and Government Delegation, the Czechoslovak Party and Government Delegation, the Polish Party and Government Delegation, the Romanian Party and Government Delegation, the Bulgarian Party and Government Delegation, the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Delegation, and the Government Delegation.

The fraternal party delegations that have arrived are: the Communist Party of India Delegation, and the Communist Party of Indonesia Delegation.

In addition, the World Peace Council representative, the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council representative, the China-Vietnam Friendship Association delegation, and the Chinese Guangdong Provincial Party and Government Delegation, invited to attend the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, have also arrived in Hanoi.

### 参加越南国庆大典和劳动党第三次代表大会  社会主义国家和兄弟党代表团陆续到河内

1960-09-01
第6版()
专栏：

    参加越南国庆大典和劳动党第三次代表大会
    社会主义国家和兄弟党代表团陆续到河内
    新华社河内31日电　应邀前来参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年庆祝典礼和越南劳动党第三次全国代表大会的各社会主义国家党政代表团和各兄弟党的代表团正在陆续到达河内。
    到今天为止，社会主义国家党政代表团已经到达的有：苏联党政代表团、中国党政代表团、朝鲜党政代表团、阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团、蒙古党政代表团、捷克斯洛伐克党政代表团、波兰党政代表团、罗马尼亚党政代表团、保加利亚党政代表团、匈牙利社会主义工人党代表团和政府代表团。
    各兄弟党代表团已经到达的有：印度共产党代表团、印度尼西亚共产党代表团。
    此外，应邀参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年庆典的世界和平理事会代表、亚非人民团结理事会代表、中越友好协会代表团、中国广东省党政代表团也已经到达河内。
