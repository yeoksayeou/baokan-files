Title: Indonesian Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd. At the invitation of the National Committee of the Agricultural and Forestry Workers' Union of China, the delegation of the Indonesian Farmers' Front and the United Association of Village Public Servants, led by Ismail, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Fan Jixian, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Agricultural and Forestry Workers' Union of China, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 印度尼西亚一代表团到京

1960-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚一代表团到京
    新华社22日讯　应中国农林工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚农民阵线及乡村公署人员联合会代表团，由伊斯迈尔率领，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国农林工会全国委员会副主席范继先等。
