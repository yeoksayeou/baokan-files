Title: China-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Moscow to Attend Celebrations of China's National Day in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 28th. The delegation of the China-Soviet Friendship Association, led by团长 Xu Guangda, arrived in Moscow by plane on the afternoon of the 28th, having been invited to the Soviet Union to participate in the celebrations of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

　　Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Yelyutin, Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the USSR; Ivanov, First Vice Chairman of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Lieutenant General Danilov, representative of the Soviet Ministry of Defense; Dubrovinina, Vice Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-China Friendship Association; and Danilov, Director of the Department of Oriental Socialist Countries of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others.

　　Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Major General Pan Zhenwu, Military Attaché, Counselor Huang Yiran, and others also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 应邀去苏参加庆祝中国国庆的活动　中苏友协代表团抵莫斯科

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

　　应邀去苏参加庆祝中国国庆的活动
　　中苏友协代表团抵莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科28日电　应邀前来苏联参加庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年庆祝活动的中苏友好协会代表团，在团长许光达率领下，于28日下午乘飞机抵达莫斯科。
    前往机场迎接的有苏联高等和中等专业教育部部长叶留金、苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会第一副主席伊凡诺夫、苏联国防部代表达尼洛夫中将、苏中友好协会理事会副主席杜伯洛维娜、苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会东方社会主义国家部主任达尼洛夫等人。
    中国驻苏大使刘晓、武官潘振武少将、参赞黄一然等也到机场迎接。
