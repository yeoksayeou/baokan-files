Title: Mongolian People's Republic Cycling Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th - The cycling team of the Mongolian People's Republic, consisting of both men and women, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit and competition in our country. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by representatives of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of our country and cyclists. The team consists of six male athletes and five female athletes, all of whom are road cyclists, and they are scheduled to hold friendly competitions with Chinese cyclists in Beijing and Lüda.

Friendship between Chinese and Mongolian cyclists was established as early as 1954. In that year, the cycling team of the Mongolian People's Republic spent half a month traveling more than 1,400 kilometers by bicycle from Ulaanbaatar to Beijing for a visit. In 1957, the Chinese cycling team also visited Ulaanbaatar and held the first friendly competition there with the Mongolian People's Republic cycling team. In the four events, the Chinese team won the men's 50km team race, the women's 25km team race, and the women's 50km individual race. The Mongolian cycling team won the men's 100km individual race.

### 蒙古人民共和国自行车队到京

1960-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国自行车队到京
    新华社16日讯　应邀来我国访问比赛的蒙古人民共和国男女自行车队一行十三人，今天由领队尼玛苏伦率领乘飞机到达北京。他们在飞机场受到了我国国家体育运动委员会代表和自行车运动员们的热烈欢迎。
    客队中包括六名男运动员和五名女运动员，他们都是公路自行车选手，预定将在北京和旅大同我国自行车选手举行友谊比赛。
    中、蒙两国自行车运动员早在1954年就建立了友谊。那一年，蒙古人民共和国自行车队曾经用半个月的时间，长途跋涉一千四百多公里，由乌兰巴托骑自行车来北京访问。1957年，中国自行车队也访问了乌兰巴托，并在那里同蒙古人民共和国自行车队举行了第一次友谊比赛，在四个比赛项目中，中国队获得了男子五十公里团体赛、女子二十五公里团体赛和女子五十公里个人赛三项冠军，蒙古自行车队获得了男子一百公里个人赛冠军。
