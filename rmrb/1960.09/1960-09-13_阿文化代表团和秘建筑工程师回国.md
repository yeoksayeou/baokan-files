Title: Argentine Cultural Delegation and Peruvian Architectural Engineer Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-09-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 12th -- The Argentine Cultural Delegation and Peruvian architectural engineer Manuel Chambi Lopez, who were invited by the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association to visit our country, concluded their visit to our country and left Beijing by plane today to return home.
    Chen Jin, Secretary-General of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and others saw them off at the airport.

### 阿文化代表团和秘建筑工程师回国

1960-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    阿文化代表团和秘建筑工程师回国
    新华社12日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷文化代表团和秘鲁建筑工程师马努埃尔·昌比·洛佩斯，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    中国拉丁美洲友好协会总干事陈金等到机场送行。
