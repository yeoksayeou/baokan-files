Title: New Zealand Reverend Charles Chandler Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-23
Page: 4
　
　  Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Reverend Charles Chandler, President of the New Zealand Peace Council and member of the World Peace Council, as well as Ms. Katherine Hall, member of the New Zealand Peace Council, arrived in Beijing by train today.

### 新西兰查尔斯·昌德勒教长到京

1960-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

　  新西兰查尔斯·昌德勒教长到京
    据新华社22日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国访问的新西兰和平理事会会长、世界和平理事会理事查尔斯·昌德勒教长以及新西兰和平理事会理事凯瑟琳·霍尔女士，在今天乘火车到达北京。
