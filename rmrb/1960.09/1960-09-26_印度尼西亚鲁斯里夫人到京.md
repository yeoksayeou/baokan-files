Title: Indonesian Mrs. Rusli Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4

　  Indonesian Mrs. Rusli, Secretary-General of the Parliament, Arrives in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Mrs. Rusli, Secretary-General of the Indonesian Parliament, arrived in Beijing by plane today. Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and representatives from the All-China Women's Federation were at the airport to greet her.

### 印度尼西亚鲁斯里夫人到京

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　  印度尼西亚鲁斯里夫人到京
    新华社25日讯　印度尼西亚国会秘书长鲁斯里夫人应中国人民外交学会邀请，今天乘飞机到达北京。前往机场迎接的有中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达和全国妇联的代表。
