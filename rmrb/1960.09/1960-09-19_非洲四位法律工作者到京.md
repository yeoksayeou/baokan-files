Title: Four African Legal Workers Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Tunisan legal worker Ben Mustapha Abdel Hamid, Algerian legal worker Bouzid Al-Ziki, Moroccan legal workers Abed Awa El Alami and Ben Slimane arrived in Beijing by plane today, invited by the China Political Science and Law Association to visit our country.
    Wu Defeng and Zhang Zhizhi, vice presidents of the China Political Science and Law Association, went to the airport to welcome them.

### 非洲四位法律工作者到京

1960-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    非洲四位法律工作者到京
    新华社18日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的突尼斯法律工作者本·穆斯塔法·阿卜杜勒·哈米德，阿尔及利亚法律工作者布齐德·阿勒齐基，摩洛哥法律工作者阿布德·奥瓦·埃尔·阿拉米和本·斯利曼在今天乘飞机到达北京。
    中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰、张志让等前往机场欢迎。
