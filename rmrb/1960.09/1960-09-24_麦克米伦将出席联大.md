Title: Macmillan to Attend the UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, London, September 22nd: The British government announced tonight that British Prime Minister Macmillan will leave London for New York on the 25th to attend the United Nations General Assembly.

### 麦克米伦将出席联大

1960-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

　  麦克米伦将出席联大
    新华社伦敦22日电　英国政府今天晚上宣布，英国首相麦克米伦将在25日离伦敦去纽约出席联合国大会。
