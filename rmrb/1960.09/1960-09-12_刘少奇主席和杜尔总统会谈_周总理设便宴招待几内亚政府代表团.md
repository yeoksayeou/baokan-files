Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Holds Talks with President Touré; Premier Zhou Hosts a Banquet for the Guinean Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th September: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, and President of the Republic of Guinea, Sékou Touré, held talks this afternoon in a friendly atmosphere.

    On the Guinean side, those participating in the talks included: Diallo Saifoulaye, President of the National Assembly; Louis Lansana Béavogui, Minister of National Economy; and Keita N’famara, Minister of Planning.

    On the Chinese side, those participating in the talks included: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice-Premiers Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, and Bo Yibo, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Guinea Ke Hua, and Director of the West Asia and Africa Department He Ying, among others.

Xinhua News Agency, 11th September: Premier Zhou Enlai held a buffet dinner this evening in the Great Hall of the People to entertain President of the Republic of Guinea, Sékou Touré, and all the distinguished guests of the Guinean Government Delegation.

    Chairman Zhu De attended the banquet.

    Premier Zhou Enlai gave a speech at the banquet, saying that we are very pleased to welcome the President of the heroic Guinean people from Africa for the first time in Beijing. This is a great honor and a great encouragement to the Chinese people. He proposed a toast to the health of His Excellency President Sékou Touré and all the distinguished Guinean guests.

    In his speech, President Sékou Touré proposed a toast to the health of Premier Zhou Enlai, to the prosperity of the Chinese people, and to the friendly unity between the governments and peoples of Guinea and China.

    Among those present at the banquet were Vice-Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Bo Yibo, Tan Zhenlin, and Luo Ruiqing, Vice-Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Luo Guibo, and Geng Biao, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity Liao Chengzhi, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association Liu Changsheng, Chinese Ambassador to Guinea Ke Hua, and heads of relevant departments of the government and Beijing Municipality. ### 刘少奇主席和杜尔总统会谈  周总理设便宴招待几内亚政府代表团

1960-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席和杜尔总统会谈
    周总理设便宴招待几内亚政府代表团
    新华社11日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔，今天下午在友好的气氛中进行了会谈。
    几内亚方面参加会谈的有：国民议会议长迪阿洛·塞福拉耶，国民经济部长路易·兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉，计划部长凯塔·恩法马拉。
    中国方面参加会谈的有：国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈毅、李先念、薄一波，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫，我国驻几内亚大使柯华，西亚非洲司司长何英等。
    新华社11日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间在人民大会堂举行便宴，招待几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔，和几内亚共和国政府代表团全体贵宾。
    朱德委员长出席了宴会。
    周恩来总理在宴会上致词，他说，我们很高兴在北京第一次欢迎从非洲来的英雄的几内亚人民的总统。这对中国人民说来是极大的光荣，是极大的鼓舞。他提议，为塞古·杜尔总统阁下和几内亚全体贵宾们的健康，干杯。
    塞古·杜尔总统在致词时提议，为周恩来总理的健康，为中国人民的繁荣，为几内亚和中国两国政府和两国人民之间的友好团结，干杯。
    出席宴会作陪的有，国务院副总理陈毅、李先念、薄一波、谭震林、罗瑞卿，外交部副部长章汉夫、罗贵波、耿飚，中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜，我国驻几内亚大使柯华，以及政府各有关部门和北京市的负责人。（附图片）
