Title: Ceylon Prime Minister Visits Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1960-09-19
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, September 17th: Ceylon Prime Minister Mrs. Bandaranaike visited the Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition this morning.
    She was welcomed by Zhang Canming, Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon, and his wife, as well as Mr. Perera, Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association.
    Mrs. Bandaranaike left a message in the visitors' book, congratulating the Chinese Embassy in Ceylon and the Ceylon-China Friendship Association for jointly holding this exhibition, and expressing her belief that Ceylon's craftsmen can learn a lot from this exhibition.
    The exhibition opened on the 7th. So far, 20,000 people have visited it.

### 锡兰总理参观我工艺品展览会

1960-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理参观我工艺品展览会
    据新华社科伦坡17日电　锡兰总理班达拉奈克夫人今天上午参观了中国美术工艺品展览会。
    她受到中国驻锡兰大使张灿明和夫人以及锡中友好协会主席佩雷拉的欢迎。
    班达拉奈克夫人在来宾留言簿上留言，祝贺中国驻锡兰大使馆和锡中友好协会联合举办这次展览会，并表示相信，锡兰的手工业者可以从这个展览会中学到许多东西。
    展览会是7日开幕的。到目前为止，已有两万人参观过。
