Title: President Touré Arrives in Ulaanbaatar After Visiting Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 9th: President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea and the government delegation he led arrived in Ulaanbaatar by plane this morning at the invitation of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural and the government of Mongolia.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia, Tsend, Acting Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and other Mongolian party and government leaders. Xie Fusheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, and diplomatic envoys from various countries also welcomed them at the airport.

Touré, accompanied by Sambuu and Tsend, reviewed the guard of honor. A group of Mongolian children presented flowers to the distinguished Guinean guests.

President Touré said at the airport that the establishment of diplomatic relations between Guinea and Mongolia shows that we will cooperate for the common good of both countries. He added that we now have fraternal friendly relations. We are faced with a great task of striving for the victory of the struggle against imperialism.

Sambuu said in his welcoming speech that the Mongolian people welcome with joy the envoys of the Guinean people who have won freedom and independence through their heroic struggle. Sambuu said that we believe that President Touré's visit will help further develop the friendly relations between the two countries based on the Bandung Principles and will make a valuable contribution to safeguarding and consolidating the cause of world peace.

When the distinguished Guinean guests entered the city, they were warmly welcomed by the citizens of Ulaanbaatar lining the streets.

This noon, President Touré and others paid a courtesy call on Sambuu and Tsend. Sambuu hosted a luncheon for Touré and other distinguished Guinean guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 8th: President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea left Moscow by plane this afternoon to visit the Mongolian People's Republic and then to visit the People's Republic of China.

Soviet party and government leaders, diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union, and representatives from all walks of life went to the airport to see him off today.

Khrushchev and Touré exchanged speeches at the airport. Khrushchev said that thanks to President Touré's visit, the friendly relations between the Soviet Union and Guinea have been further strengthened. Touré said in his speech that the Guinean people are determined to do their best to strengthen cooperation with the Soviet Union.

### 在访问莫斯科以后  杜尔总统到达乌兰巴托

1960-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

    在访问莫斯科以后
    杜尔总统到达乌兰巴托
    新华社乌兰巴托9日电　几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔和他所率领的政府代表团，应蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团和政府的邀请，今天上午坐飞机到达乌兰巴托。
    到机场欢迎的有：蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布，代理部长会议主席曾德和蒙古其他党政领导人。中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生和各国外交使节也到机场欢迎。
    杜尔由桑布、曾德陪同，检阅了仪仗队。一群蒙古儿童向几内亚贵宾献了花。
    杜尔总统在机场上讲话说，几内亚和蒙古建立了外交关系，这表明我们将为了两国共同的美好事业而进行合作。他又说，现在我们已经有了兄弟般的友好关系。我们面临着一个争取反对帝国主义斗争胜利的伟大任务。
    桑布致欢迎词说，蒙古人民以欢欣的心情迎接用自己的英勇斗争取得了自由、独立的几内亚人民的使者。桑布说，我们相信，杜尔总统的这次访问将有助于进一步发展两国基于万隆原则的友好关系，对维护和巩固世界和平事业将作出宝贵贡献。
    几内亚贵宾进入市区时，沿途受到乌兰巴托市民的夹道欢迎。
    今天中午，杜尔总统等拜会了桑布和曾德。桑布设午宴招待杜尔和几内亚其他贵宾。
    据新华社莫斯科8日电　几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔今天下午乘飞机离开莫斯科前往乌兰巴托访问蒙古人民共和国，然后前往中华人民共和国访问。
    今天前往机场送行的有苏联党政领导人、各国驻苏联外交使节和各界代表。
    赫鲁晓夫和杜尔在机场上互相致词。赫鲁晓夫说，由于杜尔总统的访问，苏联和几内亚两国的友好关系进一步加强了。杜尔致词说，几内亚人决心尽力加强同苏联的合作。
