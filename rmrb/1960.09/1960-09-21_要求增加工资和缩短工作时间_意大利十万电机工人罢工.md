Title: Demanding Wage Increases and Reduced Working Hours: 100,000 Italian Electrical Workers Strike
Author:
Date: 1960-09-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 19th: Ten thousand workers in the Italian electrical and mechanical industry held a nationwide 24-hour strike today, demanding wage increases and reduced working hours.

　　The strike was initiated by three labor organizations—the Italian General Confederation of Labor (the main one), the Catholic Democratic Party, and the Social Democratic Party's trade union. From 1955 to 1959, the output value of this industrial sector increased from 283 billion lire to 500 billion lire. The officially announced profit growth of the various associations was 46%, and labor productivity increased by 35%, while actual wages only increased by 3.8%. These labor organizations, in calling for the strike, demanded that future wages should be linked to labor productivity.

### 要求增加工资和缩短工作时间　意大利十万电机工人罢工

1960-09-21
第5版()
专栏：

　  要求增加工资和缩短工作时间
　　意大利十万电机工人罢工
    新华社19日讯　罗马消息：意大利电气机械工业的十万名工人今天举行全国性的二十四小时罢工，要求增加工资和缩短工作时间。
    罢工是由三个劳工组织——意大利总工会（是其中主要的）、天主教民主党和社会民主党的工会——发起的。从1955年到1959年，这个工业部门的产值从二千八百三十亿里拉增长到五千亿里拉。各协会正式宣布的利润增长了46％，劳动生产率增长35％，而实际工资只增长3.8％。这些劳工组织在号召罢工时，要求今后工资应同劳动生产率联系起来。
