Title: China-Nepal Friendship Association Holds Reception to Celebrate 4th Anniversary and Welcome Nepalese Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The China-Nepal Friendship Association held a reception at the International Club today at noon to celebrate the 4th anniversary of its founding on September 28th, and to welcome Mr. Ing. B. Shresta, Honorary Joint Secretary of the Nepal Chamber of Commerce, and his wife, Mr. Shi. Pu. Rauniar, member of the Working Committee of the Nepal Chamber of Commerce, and his wife, as well as Nepalese students studying in our country.

	Zhou Shujia, Vice President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association, spoke at the reception. He said that since its establishment in Beijing four years ago on September 28th, the China-Nepal Friendship Association has engaged in close and friendly cooperation with the Nepal-China Friendship Association, further enhancing the traditional friendly relations between our two countries.

	Zhou Shujia pointed out that it is particularly gratifying that after the friendly visits of the Prime Ministers of our two countries, the China-Nepal Agreement on the Boundary Question between the Two Countries, the China-Nepal Economic Assistance Agreement, and the China-Nepal Treaty of Peace and Friendship, which are of great historical significance, were signed. The signing of these agreements and treaties has added a new page to the history of friendly relations between our two countries. This is a powerful proof of the strong vitality of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and a brilliant example of our good-neighborly and friendly relations.

	Zhou Shujia said, we believe that through our joint efforts, the friendship between the people of China and Nepal will be as eternal and enduring as the towering Himalayas that connect our two countries.

	Shi. Pu. Rauniar, member of the Working Committee of the Nepal Chamber of Commerce, spoke at the reception. He said that the achievements China has made in more than ten years are tremendous and amazing. He said that they were invited by the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade to visit China this time, which will definitely strengthen the friendly cooperation and trade relations between Nepal and China. He proposed a toast to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong.

	Bashyama Dapeng, Chairman of the Nepalese Students' Union, praised the China-Nepal Friendship Association for its contribution to promoting friendly cooperation between China and Nepal. He said that Nepalese students in China have seen the friendship and goodwill of the Chinese people towards the Nepalese people everywhere. Similarly, the Nepalese people feel the same way towards the Chinese people, which shows that the future of the friendship between the people of China and Nepal is boundless.

	Those present at the reception included Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ji Chao Ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, as well as Hou Wailu, Chen Banding, Shen Qiyi, Lin Lin, Han Ming, Wang Chuanbin, Zhang Yihua and many others.

### 中国尼泊尔友好协会举行酒会　庆祝友协成立四周年并欢迎尼泊尔客人

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国尼泊尔友好协会举行酒会
　　庆祝友协成立四周年并欢迎尼泊尔客人
    新华社25日讯　9月28日是中国尼泊尔友好协会成立四周年。中国尼泊尔友好协会今天中午在国际俱乐部举行了庆祝酒会，并且欢迎尼泊尔商会名誉联合秘书英·巴·什雷斯塔和夫人，尼泊尔商会工作委员会委员希·普·拉乌尼亚尔和夫人，以及在我国学习的尼泊尔留学生。
  中国尼泊尔友好协会副会长周叔迦在酒会上讲话。他说，中尼友好协会自从四年前的9月28日在北京成立以后，与尼中友好协会进行了密切友好的合作，进一步增强了我们两国传统友好的关系。
    周叔迦指出，特别值得高兴的是，我们两国的总理和首相相互进行了友好访问后，签订了具有伟大历史意义的中尼关于两国边界问题的协定、中尼经济援助协定和中尼和平友好条约，这些协定和条约的签订在我们两国友好关系史上增添了新的一页。这是和平共处五项原则强大生命力的有力证明，是我们睦邻友好关系的光辉范例。
    周叔迦说，我们相信，通过我们的共同努力，中尼两国人民的友谊，将会像连接我们两国的巍峨的喜马拉雅山一样天长地久、与世共存。
    尼泊尔商会工作委员会委员希·普·拉乌尼亚尔在酒会上讲话。他说，中国在十年多取得的成就是巨大的，令人惊讶的。他说，他们这次应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请来中国访问，一定会加强尼泊尔和中国的友好合作和贸易关系。他提议为毛泽东主席的健康干杯。
    尼泊尔留学生学生会主席巴夏玛大澎在讲话时，赞扬了中国尼泊尔友好协会对促进中尼两国友好合作所作出的贡献。他说，尼泊尔留学生在中国处处都看到了中国人民对尼泊尔人民的友情和善意。同样，尼泊尔人民对中国人民也是如此，这就说明中尼两国人民之间的友谊的前途是无尽的。
    出席酒会的有，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎，以及侯外庐、陈半丁、沈其益、林林、韩明、王传斌、张亦桦等多人。
