Title: At the Invitation of the China-Albania Friendship Association, Delegation of the Albania-China Friendship Association Sets Off for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 27th. At the invitation of the China-Albania Friendship Association to participate in the celebrations of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the delegation of the Albania-China Friendship Association departed from Tirana at noon today for Beijing.

The delegation of seven people is led by Abdul Kellezi, Vice-Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, member of the Central Committee of the Labor Party, and Chairman of the Albania-China Friendship Association. The members include Prokop Murra, member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and Minister of the Central Committee's Department of Industry and Mining; Yordan Pano, First Secretary of the Berat Regional Committee of the Labor Party; Hilmi Zajmi, Director of the Lushnjë State Farm; Abdulla Baftjari, Chairman of the Pëshkopi Agricultural Cooperative; Eftimi Lito, Hero of Socialist Labor; and Vangjel Majle, Principal of the Tirana Normal School.

On the same day, Misto Treska, Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, and Shefkat Musaraj, Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association, also set off for Beijing at the invitation of the Chinese Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Writers Association, respectively.

### 应中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会邀请  阿中友好协会代表团启程来京

1960-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    应中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会邀请
　  阿中友好协会代表团启程来京
    新华社地拉那27日电　应中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会的邀请到我国参加中华人民共和国成立十一周年庆典的阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团已于今日中午由地拉那启程前往北京。
    代表团一行七人由阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席、劳动党中央委员、阿中友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·凯列齐率领，团员有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、党中央工矿部部长普罗科·穆拉，劳动党培拉特区委第一书记约尔丹·帕诺，卢什涅国营农场场长希勒米·扎伊米，佩什科比农业合作社主席阿卜杜拉·巴弗蒂亚里，社会主义劳动英雄埃弗提米·利托和地拉那师范学校校长万杰利·迈莱。
    同一天启程去北京的还有应我国对外文化联络委员会和作家协会分别邀请前往中国访问的阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席米斯托·特雷斯卡和阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长谢夫卡特·穆萨莱。
