Title: Japanese Architectural Institute Vice President Nishiyama Uzo Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: Nishiyama Uzo, Vice President of the Japanese Architectural Institute, and one accompanying member, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 24th, at the invitation of the Architectural Society of China for a visit. Liang Sicheng, Vice Chairman of the Architectural Society of China, and others welcomed the guests at the station.

### 日本建筑学会副会长西山卯三到京

1960-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　  日本建筑学会副会长西山卯三到京
    新华社25日讯　应中国建筑学会邀请来我国参观访问的日本建筑学会副会长西山卯三和随员一人，24日下午乘火车到达北京。中国建筑学会副理事长梁思成等到车站欢迎客人。
