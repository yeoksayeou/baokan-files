Title: Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe Gives First Performance in Albania

Author:

Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, September 29th. The Chinese Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe gave its first performance in Tirana tonight. The performance was warmly welcomed by the audience.

The song and dance troupe is visiting and performing at the invitation of the cultural cooperation agreement between China and Albania.

Albanian Deputy Minister of Education and Culture, Mitro Jorgji, Director of the Arts Department of the Ministry of Education and Culture, Ismail Hoxha, Director of the Albanian National Song and Dance Troupe and Director of the Acrobatics Theater, Kasapi, and Chinese Ambassador to Albania, Luo Shigao, and embassy staff attended the performance.

Before the performance, Kasapi spoke, warmly welcoming the Chinese Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe's visit to Albania and wishing the performance a success.

The troupe's beautiful dances and melodious singing were warmly praised by the audience. In particular, the Lion Dance and Albanian folk songs attracted every audience member. They repeatedly applauded to welcome them.

Kasapi, Director of the Albanian National Song and Dance Troupe, said that the troupe's program is full of optimism and fully demonstrates the Chinese people's yearning for a new life and their love for the Chinese Communist Party and leader Mao Zedong. I. Hoxha, Director of the Arts Department of the Ministry of Education and Culture, said that the troupe's performance is full of vigor and makes people feel like they are living in spring.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 27th. The Chinese Sichuan Provincial Song and Dance Troupe, led by Zhang Tiesheng, concluded its visit and performance in the United Arab Republic and left Cairo this morning for Tirana, the capital of the People's Republic of Albania.

### 我四川歌舞团在阿尔巴尼亚首次演出

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

　　我四川歌舞团在阿尔巴尼亚首次演出
    新华社地拉那29日电　中国四川歌舞团今晚在地拉那举行首次演出。演出受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
    歌舞团是根据中国和阿尔巴尼亚两国文化合作协定应邀前来访问演出的。
    阿尔巴尼亚教育和文化部副部长米特罗约尔基，教育和文化部艺术司长伊斯梅尔·霍查，阿尔巴尼亚民族歌舞团团长、杂技剧院院长卡萨皮和中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高以及使馆人员都出席观看了表演。
    在演出前，卡萨皮讲了话，他热烈欢迎中国四川歌舞团访问阿尔巴尼亚，并预祝演出成功。
    歌舞团的优美舞蹈和动听的歌唱受到观众的热烈赞赏。特别是狮子舞，阿尔巴尼亚民歌等节目更吸引了每个观众。他们一再地鼓掌欢迎。
    阿尔巴尼亚民族歌舞团团长卡萨皮说，歌舞团的节目充满了乐观主义的精神，充分表现了中国人民对新生活的向往和对中国共产党、对领袖毛泽东的热爱。教育和文化部艺术司长伊·霍查说，歌舞团的表演充满了朝气，使人感觉像生活在春天一样。
    据新华社开罗27日电　由张铁生率领的中国四川省歌舞团结束了在阿拉伯联合共和国的访问演出，今天上午离开开罗前往阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国首都地拉那。
