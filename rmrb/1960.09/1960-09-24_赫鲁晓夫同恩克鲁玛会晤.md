Title: Khrushchev Meets with Nkrumah
Author:
Date: 1960-09-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 23rd. TASS reported on the 22nd that the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, the head of the Soviet delegation to the 15th session of the United Nations General Assembly, Khrushchev, on the afternoon of September 22nd, exchanged views with the President of the Republic of Ghana, Nkrumah, on the international situation of mutual interest and on issues related to the current session of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 赫鲁晓夫同恩克鲁玛会晤

1960-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　赫鲁晓夫同恩克鲁玛会晤
　　新华社23日讯　据塔斯社纽约22日讯：苏联部长会议主席、苏联出席联合国大会第十五届会议代表团团长赫鲁晓夫，在9月22日下午同加纳共和国总统恩克鲁玛就彼此感到兴趣的国际局势问题以及有关本届联合国大会会议的问题交换了意见。
