Title: Moroccan Kingdom Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-30
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. At the invitation of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the four-member cultural delegation from the Kingdom of Morocco, led by its head, Mohammed Aziz Lahbabi, arrived in Beijing this afternoon.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Zhou Erfu, Tian Han, and Lin Lin.

### 摩洛哥王国文化代表团到京

1960-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥王国文化代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　应对外文化联络委员会邀请来中国访问的摩洛哥王国文化代表团一行四人，在团长穆罕默德·阿齐兹·拉赫巴比率领下，今天下午到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有周而复、田汉、林林等。
