Title: General Ne Win Visits Military Museum
Author:
Date: 1960-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th – General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, visited the Chinese People's Revolution Military Museum this morning, accompanied by General Luo Ruiqing, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

General Ne Win was warmly welcomed by Major General Jia Ruoyu, the museum's curator, upon his arrival at the museum.

Accompanying the visit were Kyaw Winn, the Burmese Ambassador to China, and Senior Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Burma.

### 奈温将军参观军事博物馆

1960-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    奈温将军参观军事博物馆
    新华社28日讯　缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军今天上午在中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将陪同下，参观了中国人民革命军事博物馆。
    奈温将军到达博物馆时，受到了馆长贾若瑜少将的热烈欢迎。
    陪同参观的，还有缅甸驻我国大使叫温和我国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定大校。
