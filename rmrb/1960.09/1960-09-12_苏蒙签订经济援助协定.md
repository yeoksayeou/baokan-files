Title: Soviet-Mongolian Agreement Signed on Economic Aid
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 9th: TASS reports: On September 9th, in the Kremlin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, signed an agreement on new economic and technical assistance from the Soviet Union to help the Mongolian People's Republic realize its Third Five-Year Plan (1961-1965).

### 苏蒙签订经济援助协定

1960-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    苏蒙签订经济援助协定
    据新华社9日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔，9日在克里姆林宫签署了关于苏联为帮助蒙古人民共和国实现第三个五年计划（1961—1965年）而给予新的经济援助和技术援助的协定。
