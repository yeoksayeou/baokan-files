Title: Ministry of Culture Hosts Banquet for Burmese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: The Ministry of Culture and the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a grand banquet tonight to warmly welcome the Burmese Cultural Delegation on their visit and performance in China. Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing and Colonel So Min, head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, spoke at the banquet. They both wished that the friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people would flow forever like the Yangtze River and the Irrawaddy River. During the banquet, Minister Shen Yanbing, Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang, and Colonel So Min toasted Chinese and Burmese guests at each table, wishing the friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people a long life.

In his speech, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing expressed his heartfelt and warm welcome to Colonel So Min and all the Burmese artists. He said: The Burmese Cultural Delegation's visit to China, accompanying Prime Minister U Nu, comes at a time when the friendly relations between China and Burma are constantly being consolidated and are growing daily, as cultural and friendly envoys of the Burmese government and people. Through the visit and performance of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, the flower of Sino-Burmese friendship will bloom even more brightly and beautifully, further promoting mutual understanding and the development of friendly relations between the Chinese and Burmese people. Shen Yanbing pointed out that China and Burma are not only close neighbors but also "pauk-phaw" (relatives), and the Chinese people have always cherished the friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people and also love Burmese national art very much. He wished the Burmese artists success in their performances in China.

Colonel So Min, head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, spoke. He said that the Burmese artists were very happy to have the opportunity to visit China, perform, and exchange experiences with Chinese artists. This kind of cultural exchange will greatly promote understanding and friendship between China and Burma. He expressed his firm belief that the flower of Sino-Burmese friendship will bloom more vividly under the irrigation of the cultural and artistic waters of the two countries. He said that the friendship between the Chinese and Burmese relatives and "pauk-phaw", with the joint efforts of the leaders of the two countries and on the basis of the Five Principles, will be further consolidated and developed.

Among those attending the banquet were Vice Ministers of Culture Xia Yan and Xu Pingyu, Vice Chairmen of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan and Ding Xilin, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Jian Bozan and Zhao Puchu, Secretary-General of the Foreign Cultural Association Zhou Erfu, Deputy Minister of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Senior Captain Li Wei, as well as famous musicians, filmmakers, dramatists, and dancers from the capital, including Lü Ji, Zhou Weizhi, Cai Chusheng, Situ Huimin, Zha Fuxi, Zhao Feng, Dai Ailian, Cui Wei, Li Baiwan, Jin Shan, and Qin Wen.

Daw Khin, wife of the Burmese Ambassador to China, and U Aung Kyin, First Secretary of the Embassy, were also invited to attend the banquet.

During the banquet, Chinese artists and Burmese artists performed wonderful songs and dances. The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere filled with friendship.

### 文化部欢宴缅甸文化代表团

1960-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　文化部欢宴缅甸文化代表团
    新华社26日讯　文化部和对外文化联络委员会今晚举行盛大宴会，热烈欢迎缅甸文化代表团来华访问演出。文化部部长沈雁冰和缅甸文化代表团团长苏敏上校在宴会上讲了话。他们都祝愿中缅两国人民的友谊如同长江和伊洛瓦底江的流水一样，永远长流不息。席间，沈雁冰部长、对外文委代理主任张致祥和苏敏上校并逐席向中缅人士敬酒，祝中缅两国人民友谊万岁。
    文化部部长沈雁冰在讲话中，向苏敏上校和全体缅甸艺术家们表示衷心的热烈的欢迎。他说：缅甸文化代表团随同吴努总理访问中国，是在中缅两国友好关系不断巩固和日益发展的时候，作为缅甸政府和人民的文化和友好的使者前来的。通过缅甸文化代表团的访问演出，将使中缅友谊之花开得更加鲜艳美丽，更加促进中、缅两国人民互相了解和友好关系的发展。沈雁冰指出，中缅两国不仅是近邻，而且是胞波，中国人民一向非常珍视中缅两国人民的友谊，也十分喜爱缅甸的民族艺术。他祝缅甸艺术家们在中国的演出成功。
    缅甸文化代表团团长苏敏上校讲话。他说，缅甸艺术家们能有机会来中国访问演出，并与中国艺术家们交流经验，他感到非常高兴。这种文化交流，对中缅两国之间的了解和友谊将起很大的促进作用。他表示深信中缅两国友谊之花在两国文化艺术之水的灌溉下，将更加鲜艳盛开。他说，中缅两国亲戚和胞波的友谊，在中缅两国领导人的共同努力下，在五项原则的基础上，将获得更大的巩固和发展。
    出席宴会的有文化部副部长夏衍、徐平羽，对外文委副主任楚图南、丁西林，中缅友好协会副会长翦伯赞、赵朴初，对外文化协会秘书长周而复，中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部副部长李伟大校，以及吕骥、周巍峙、蔡楚生、司徒慧敏、查阜西、赵沨、戴爱莲、崔嵬、李百万、金山、秦文等首都音乐、电影、戏剧、舞蹈界著名人士。
    缅甸驻中国大使叫温的夫人、大使馆一等秘书吴翁钦，也应邀出席了宴会。
    席间，中国文艺工作者和缅甸的艺术家表演了精采的歌舞节目。宴会在充满友谊的气氛中进行。
