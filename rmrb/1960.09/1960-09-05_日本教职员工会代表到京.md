Title: Japanese Teachers' Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The delegation of the Japan Teachers' Union, headed by Yoshimaru Ito, arrived in Beijing today at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Educational Workers' Union for a visit to China on their way back to Japan after attending the Third World Teachers' Conference in Conakry.

### 日本教职员工会代表到京

1960-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    日本教职员工会代表到京
    新华社3日讯　以伊藤吉春为首的日本教职员工会代表五人在参加科纳克里第三次世界教师大会后，回日本途中应中国教育工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问，已经在今天到达北京。
