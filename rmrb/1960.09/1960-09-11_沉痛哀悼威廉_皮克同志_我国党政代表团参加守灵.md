Title: Deepest Condolences on the Passing of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck
Author:
Date: 1960-09-11
Page: 5

    Deepest Condolences on the Passing of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck
    Our Party and Government Delegation Participates in the Vigil
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 10th. The Chinese Communist Party and Chinese Government Delegation, headed by Xi Zhongxun, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Premier of the State Council, laid a wreath before the remains of President Wilhelm Pieck at the Central Committee Building of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany this morning and participated in the vigil.
    The delegation also expressed its deep condolences and sympathy to Prime Minister Grotewohl and the family of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck.
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 9th. Xi Zhongxun, head of the Chinese Communist Party and Chinese Government Delegation, member of the CPC Central Committee, and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Ji Pengfei, member of the delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, who are participating in the state funeral of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Berlin at 23:30 today.
    They were welcomed at the airport by: Materne，Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and first deputy chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber, Kurella, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany，Vice Premier Scholz, members of the People's Chamber and representatives of the National Front of Democratic Germany.
    Wang Guoquan, member of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation and Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, was also at the airport to welcome them.

### 沉痛哀悼威廉·皮克同志  我国党政代表团参加守灵

1960-09-11
第5版()
专栏：

    沉痛哀悼威廉·皮克同志
    我国党政代表团参加守灵
    新华社柏林10日电　以中国共产党中央委员、国务院副总理习仲勋为首的中国共产党和中国政府代表团今天上午到德国统一社会党中央委员会大厦在威廉·皮克总统遗体前献了花圈，并且参加了守灵。
    代表团还向格罗提渥总理和威廉·皮克同志的家属表示了深切的哀悼和慰问。
    新华社柏林9日电　参加德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克同志国葬典礼的中国共产党和中国政府代表团团长、中共中央委员、国务院副总理习仲勋和团员、外交部副部长姬鹏飞，在今天二十三时三十分到达柏林。
    到机场欢迎的有：德国统一社会党中央政治局委员、人民议院主席团第一副主席马特恩，德国统一社会党中央政治局候补委员库雷拉，副总理绍尔茨，人民议院议员和民主德国全国阵线的代表。
    到机场上欢迎的还有中国党政代表团团员、中国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权。
