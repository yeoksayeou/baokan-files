Title: Communiqué on President Wilhelm Pieck's Health Condition
Author:
Date: 1960-09-07
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 5th - German News Agency today published a communiqué regarding the health condition of Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic.
    The communiqué stated that President Pieck's health had deteriorated due to a sudden onset of heart weakness. His temperature rose to 38.4 degrees Celsius, and his condition is very serious.
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 6th - According to a bulletin on President Pieck's condition released here today: President Wilhelm Pieck of the German Democratic Republic has been in worsening condition since yesterday. Although his temperature has dropped to 37.2 degrees Celsius, his heartbeat and blood circulation are further weakening.
    This communiqué was signed by four doctors.

### 关于威廉·皮克总统健康情况的公报

1960-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    关于威廉·皮克总统健康情况的公报
    新华社柏林5日电　德意志通讯社今天发表了关于德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克健康情况的公报。
    公报中说，皮克总统健康情况由于突发的心脏衰弱症而恶化。体温上升至三十八点四度，病情很严重。
    新华社柏林6日电　据今天这里发表的关于皮克总统的病情公报说：德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克自昨天以来病情恶化。虽然他的体温已降至三十七点二度，但心脏跳动和血液循环在进一步变弱。
    这份公报是由四位医生签署的。
