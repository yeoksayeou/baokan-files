Title: Indian West Bengal Police Shoot Miners
Author:
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: The "Hindustan Standard" reported on September 10th that a shooting incident by police against workers occurred on September 7th at the Dabor coal mine in Salanpur, West Bengal, India. In this incident, two coal miners were shot dead, many were injured, and about one hundred were arrested.

According to the newspaper, citing official sources, on the day of the incident, two hundred workers went to the coal mine company's office to request work. The management refused their request and mobilized the police to suppress them.

Prior to this, the coal mine company management had closed the mine due to a labor dispute.

### 印度西孟加拉邦警察枪杀矿工

1960-09-17
第5版()
专栏：

    印度西孟加拉邦警察枪杀矿工
    新华社15日讯　9月10日的“印度斯坦旗报”透露，7日，在印度西孟加拉萨兰普尔的达博尔煤矿发生警察枪杀工人的事件。在这次事件中，有两个煤矿工人被打死，许多人被打伤，约一百人被逮捕。
    据这家报纸引用官方的消息说，这个事件发生的那天，有两百名工人前往煤矿公司的办事处要求工作，资方拒绝了他们的要求，并且调动警察进行镇压。
    在这以前，煤矿公司资方因劳资纠纷关闭了煤矿。
