Title: Beijing Railway Institute, No. 26 Middle School, and Beijing Children Hold Gatherings to Celebrate the Glorious Festival of the Vietnamese People
Author:
Date: 1960-09-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 4th: Beijing Railway Institute, Beijing No. 26 Middle School, and Beijing children held gatherings on the 4th and 3rd respectively, to warmly celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    The wife of the Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Chen Ziping, the Chargé d'affaires of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, Chen Zhongkuo, and his wife, as well as other diplomatic officials from the embassy, attended the celebration held by the Beijing Railway Institute tonight, and were warmly welcomed by three thousand teachers, students, and Vietnamese students of the Railway Institute. Wang Yi, Deputy Director of the First Department of the Foreign Cultural Commission, also attended the celebration.

    Qian Yinglin, Secretary of the CPC Beijing Railway Institute Committee and President, spoke at the meeting. He praised the heroic Vietnamese people for the great achievements they have made in socialist transformation and socialist construction under the wise leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party headed by President Ho Chi Minh.

    The Chargé d'affaires of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, Chen Zhongkuo, spoke at the meeting. He said that the people of Vietnam and China originally had friendly relations, and these relations are now even closer. Let us sing the praises of the sun of our two countries—the Chinese Communist Party and the Vietnam Workers' Party; sing the praises of the great leaders of the two peoples, Chairman Mao and President Ho.

    Chen Min, the representative of the Vietnamese students at the Railway Institute, also spoke at the meeting.

    Gifts were exchanged at the meeting. After the meeting, Chinese and Vietnamese students performed cultural programs and held a dance.

    One thousand teachers and students from Beijing No. 26 Middle School celebrated the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam this morning with Chen Zhongkuo, Chargé d'affaires of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, and other diplomatic officials from the embassy.

    Lin Hueru, Secretary of the CPC No. 26 Middle School Branch and Principal, said at the meeting, let the people of China and Vietnam unite more closely, support each other, and encourage each other. No matter what kind of conspiracies American imperialism plays, the just demand of the Vietnamese people to unify their motherland will surely be realized.

    Chen Zhongkuo, Chargé d'affaires of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, introduced the happy study life of students in North Vietnam to the teachers and students of No. 26 Middle School at the meeting. Vietnamese friends presented gifts to No. 26 Middle School at the meeting. After the meeting, the students performed cultural programs.

    Six hundred children in Beijing held a get-together with Vietnamese children in Beijing at the Children's Palace on the evening of the 3rd. Nguyen Tien, First Secretary of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, and other diplomatic officials also attended the get-together. Chinese child Xu Hongyi spoke at the meeting. She said, dear Vietnamese children, let us join hands, study hard, make progress every day, and be good children of Chairman Mao and President Ho. At the meeting, Nguyen Tien presented gifts to the Chinese children. Chinese children performed cultural programs such as "Ode to Ho Chi Minh."

### 北京铁道学院、二十六中和北京少年儿童分别集会  庆祝越南人民的光辉节日

1960-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    北京铁道学院、二十六中和北京少年儿童分别集会
    庆祝越南人民的光辉节日
    据新华社4日讯　北京铁道学院、北京市第二十六中学、北京市少年儿童在4日和3日分别集会，热烈庆祝越南民主共和国成立十五周年。
    越南驻华大使陈子平的夫人、越南驻华大使馆临时代办陈仲括和夫人，以及大使馆的其他外交官员出席了北京铁道学院在今天晚上举行的庆祝会，受到铁道学院三千名师生和越南留学生的热烈欢迎。对外文委第一司副司长王仪，也参加了庆祝会。
    中共北京铁道学院委员会书记兼院长钱应麟在会上讲话。他赞扬英雄的越南人民在以胡志明主席为首的越南劳动党的英明领导下，在社会主义改造和社会主义建设中取得的巨大成就。
    越南驻华大使馆临时代办陈仲括在会上讲话。他说，越中两国人民原来就有友好的关系，这种关系现在更加紧密了。让我们歌颂我们两国的太阳——中国共产党和越南劳动党；歌颂两国人民的伟大领袖毛主席和胡主席。
    铁道学院越南留学生代表陈敏也在会上讲了话。
    会上，互相赠送了礼品，会后，中越两国学生演出了文艺节目，并举行了舞会。
    北京市第二十六中学的一千名师生今天上午和越南驻华大使馆临时代办陈仲括、及大使馆的其他外交官员一起欢庆越南民主共和国成立十五周年。
    中共二十六中支部书记兼校长林惠如在会上讲话说，让我们中越两国人民更加紧密地团结起来，互相支持，互相鼓舞。不管美帝国主义玩弄怎样的阴谋，越南人民统一祖国的正义要求一定会实现。
    越南驻华大使馆临时代办陈仲括在会上向二十六中学师生介绍了越南北方学生们幸福的学习生活情况。会上越南朋友向二十六中学赠送了礼物。会后，学生们演出了文艺节目。
    北京市六百名少年儿童3日晚和在北京的越南少年儿童一起在少年宫举行了联欢会。越南驻华大使馆一等秘书阮进和其他外交官员也出席了联欢会。中国儿童徐宏懿在会上讲话，她说，亲爱的越南小朋友，让我们携起手来，努力学习，天天向上，做毛主席和胡主席的好孩子。会上阮进向中国少年儿童赠送了礼物。中国少年儿童们演出了“胡志明颂歌”等文艺节目。
