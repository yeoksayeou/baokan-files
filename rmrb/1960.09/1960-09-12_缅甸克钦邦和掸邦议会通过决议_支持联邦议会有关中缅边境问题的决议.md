Title: Kachin and Shan State Assemblies in Burma Pass Resolutions Supporting the Federal Assembly's Resolution on the Sino-Burmese Border Issue
Author:
Date: 1960-09-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 9th: The Burmese Kachin State Assembly unanimously passed a resolution at its third special session today, supporting the Federal Assembly's enactment of the necessary legislation to return Hpimaw, Gawlan, and Gangfang to China.

The Shan State Assembly also passed a resolution today, supporting the Federal Assembly's enactment of the necessary legislation to cede Panglong and Panghkam to China in exchange for the Namwan Assigned Tract belonging to China.

### 缅甸克钦邦和掸邦议会通过决议  支持联邦议会有关中缅边境问题的决议

1960-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸克钦邦和掸邦议会通过决议
    支持联邦议会有关中缅边境问题的决议
    新华社仰光9日电　缅甸克钦邦议会第三次特别会议今天一致通过决议，支持联邦议会制订关于把片马、古浪和岗房归还中国的必要法案。
    掸邦议会今天也通过一项决议，支持联邦议会制订关于把班老和班洪划归中国以交换属于中国的南碗指定区的必要法案。
