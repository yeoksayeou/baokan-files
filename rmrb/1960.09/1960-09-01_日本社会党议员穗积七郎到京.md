Title: Japanese Socialist Party Diet Member Hozumi Shichiro Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-01
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, August 31st: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Hozumi Shichiro, Japanese Socialist Party Diet member and Chairman of the Socialist Party's Special Committee for the Protection of the Constitution, arrived in Beijing today by plane from Moscow.
    Zhao Anbo, Xiao Xiangqian, and Wang Xiaoyun were at the airport to welcome him.
    Japanese peace advocates in Beijing, including Kinkazu Saionji, Kazuo Suzuki, Standing Director of the Japan Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, and Hiroshi Teshima, head of the Japan Socialist Party Policy Deliberation Council Delegation to China, were also at the airport to welcome him.

### 日本社会党议员穗积七郎到京

1960-09-01
第6版()
专栏：

    日本社会党议员穗积七郎到京
    新华社31日讯　日本社会党国会议员、社会党拥护宪法特别委员会委员长穗积七郎，应中国人民外交学会的邀请，今天由莫斯科乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有赵安博、萧向前、王晓云。
    在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一、日本亚非团结委员会常务理事铃木一雄、日本社会党政策审议会访华代表团团长手岛博等也到机场欢迎。
