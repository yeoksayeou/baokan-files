Title: Two Bulgarian Scientists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-09-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: According to the 1960 executive plan for scientific cooperation between the Chinese and Bulgarian Academies of Sciences, Academician Dimitar Ivanov, an aromatic oil chemist from the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, and Professor Va. Min. Staykov, a rose cultivation expert, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

Yun Ziqiang, Deputy Director of the Department of Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 保加利亚两位科学家到京

1960-09-10
第6版()
专栏：

    保加利亚两位科学家到京
    新华社9日讯　根据中保两国科学院科学合作1960年执行计划的规定，保加利亚科学院芳香油化学家季米特尔·伊凡诺夫通讯院士和玫瑰栽培学家瓦·明·斯达依柯夫教授今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国科学院数学物理学化学部副主任恽子强等。
