Title: The United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee Holds a Report Meeting; Comrade Chen Yi Delivers a Report on the Political Situation
Author:
Date: 1960-09-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee held a report meeting this morning. Chen Yi, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, delivered a report on the political situation at the meeting.

Those attending the report meeting included representatives of the Second Plenary (Enlarged) Session of the Fourth Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, as well as representatives of various democratic parties and non-party democratic personages.

### 中共中央统战部举行报告会  陈毅同志作政治形势报告

1960-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央统战部举行报告会
    陈毅同志作政治形势报告
    新华社16日讯　中共中央统一战线工作部今天上午举行报告会。中共中央政治局委员陈毅在会上作了关于政治形势的报告。
    出席报告会的有中国国民党革命委员会第四届中央委员会第二次全体会议（扩大）的代表，还有各民主党派和无党派民主人士等。
