Title: Our Ambassador to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, Hosts a Banquet for the Indonesian Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1960-09-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 2nd. Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, hosted a banquet on the evening of September 1st in honor of Sukarni, the Indonesian Ambassador to China, who will take office on September 8th.

    The banquet took place in a very friendly atmosphere. In his speech, Ambassador Huang Zhen extended a warm welcome to Sukarni on his appointment as Ambassador to China. He proposed a toast to the growing consolidation and development of friendly relations between China and Indonesia, to the lasting friendship between the two peoples, to the further development of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the Bandung spirit, and to peace in Asia and the world.

    In his brief speech, Sukarni said that the friendship between Indonesia and China cannot be destroyed and should be made even closer. He expressed his belief that, based on the Bandung spirit, he would be facilitated in carrying out this task; there would be a bright future for the friendly relations between Indonesia and China. Abdurgani, Vice-Chairman of the Supreme Advisory Council of Indonesia, Arudji, Deputy Speaker of the Cooperation Parliament, former Speaker of the Parliament Sardjono, Minister of Justice Sahardjo, Minister of Finance Notohamiprodjo, Minister of Manpower Mobilization Sudjono, Chairman of the Indonesian Communist Party Aidit, Vice-Chairmen Lukman and Njoto, First Chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party Suwirjo, Chairman of the Consultative Conference on Citizenship Hsiao Yu-tsan, Chairman of the Islamic Party of Indonesia Abbas, Chairman of the Common People's Party Suwarto, Chief Representative of the Indonesian side of the Joint Committee on Dual Nationality of China and Indonesia Susanto, members of parliament, senior officials of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and more than 100 social celebrities attended the banquet.

    In addition, the General Association of Overseas Chinese Groups held a banquet on August 30 to bid farewell to Sukarni.

### 我驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇  欢宴印度尼西亚驻华大使

1960-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇
    欢宴印度尼西亚驻华大使
    新华社雅加达2日电　中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇1日晚间举行宴会，招待即将在9月8日赴任的印度尼西亚驻中国大使苏卡尼。
    宴会在非常友好的气氛中进行。黄镇大使在讲话时对苏卡尼被任命为驻中国大使表示热烈欢迎，他建议为中国和印度尼西亚友好关系的日益巩固和发展、为两国人民持久的友谊、为和平共处五项原则和万隆精神的进一步发展以及为亚洲和世界和平干杯。
    苏卡尼在简短的讲话中说，印度尼西亚和中国的友谊是破坏不了的，而且应当使之更加紧密。他表示相信，根据万隆精神，他将得到执行这项任务的方便；印度尼西亚和中国之间的友好关系将有光明的前景。印度尼西亚最高评议院副主席阿卜杜加尼、合作国会副议长阿鲁季、前国会议长沙多诺、司法部长沙哈尔佐、财政部长诺多哈米普罗佐、人力动员部长苏佐诺、印度尼西亚共产党主席艾地、副主席鲁克曼和约多、印度尼西亚国民党第一主席苏维约、国籍协商会主席萧玉灿、白尔蒂伊斯兰党主席阿巴斯、平民党主席苏瓦托、中国和印度尼西亚双重国籍联合委员会印度尼西亚方面首席代表苏山多、国会议员、外交部高级官员和社会名流等一百多人出席了宴会。
    另外，中华侨团总会曾在8月30日举行宴会欢送苏卡尼。
