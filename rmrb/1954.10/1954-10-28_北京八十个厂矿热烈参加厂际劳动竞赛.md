Title: Eighty Factories and Mines in Beijing Enthusiastically Participate in Inter-Factory Labor Emulation

Author: 
Date: 1954-10-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

Eighty Factories and Mines in Beijing Enthusiastically Participate in Inter-Factory Labor Emulation
    The Inter-Factory Labor Emulation Judging Committee of state-owned and locally state-owned factories and mines in Beijing has received contest conditions proposed by eighty factories and mines. These factories and mines have enthusiastically responded to the call of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions and registered to participate in this city-wide inter-factory labor emulation.
    The directors of factories such as the Beijing Liulihe Cement Plant, the Beijing No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, the Beijing Paper Mill, the Beijing Textile Printing and Dyeing Factory, and the Beijing Metal Structure Factory are directly participating in the leadership of the competition. They summarized past production work at general meetings of all employees and proposed measures to strive to fulfill and overfulfill the annual state production plan in the fourth quarter. The Jingxi Mining Bureau, Changxindian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Repair Factory, Fengtai Bridge Factory, Xinhua Printing Factory, Beijing Hardware Materials Factory, Beijing Glass Factory and other factories and mines have launched or are preparing to further launch various forms of labor emulation within the factory. Within the Jingxi Mining Bureau, there are already fourteen coal mining teams, twenty-seven tunneling teams, and drilling workers from eighteen drilling rigs participating in the same type of work competition. Forty departments, including electromechanical, management, operations, and material supply, are participating in the same business competition.

### 北京八十个厂矿热烈参加厂际劳动竞赛

1954-10-28
第2版()
专栏：

    北京八十个厂矿热烈参加厂际劳动竞赛
    北京市国营和地方国营厂矿企业的厂际劳动竞赛评判委员会已收到八十个厂矿提出的竞赛条件。这些厂矿都热烈响应北京市总工会的号召，报名参加了这次全市性的厂际劳动竞赛。
    北京市琉璃河水泥厂、北京第一机床厂、北京造纸厂、北京市机织印染厂、北京市金属结构工厂等工厂的厂长都直接参加竞赛的领导工作，他们在全体职工大会上总结了过去的生产工作，提出在第四季度争取完成和超额完成全年国家生产计划的措施。京西矿务局、长辛店机车车辆修理工厂、丰台桥梁工厂、新华印刷厂、北京市五金材料厂、北京市玻璃厂等厂矿并已展开或准备进一步展开厂内的各种形式的劳动竞赛。京西矿务局内参加同工种竞赛的已有十四个回采组、二十七个掘进组、十八部钻机的钻探工人，参加同业务竞赛的已有机电、经理、营运、材料供应等四十个科室。（新华社）
