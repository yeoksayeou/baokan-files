Title: Indian Trade Union Congress Friendly Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-13
Page: 4

    Indian Trade Union Congress Friendly Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, October 8] The friendly delegation of the Indian Trade Union Congress (INTUC), invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to visit China, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 8th, led by K.P. Tripathi, General Secretary of the INTUC. The delegation members include the editor-in-chief of the Indian trade union newspaper and trade union workers from various provinces of India.

### 印工会大会友好代表团到京

1954-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    印工会大会友好代表团到京
    【新华社八日讯】应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的印度全国工会大会友好代表团一行五人，在团长、印度全国工会大会总书记卡·普·特里帕蒂率领下在八日下午到达北京。代表团团员有印度工会报纸的主编和印度各省工会工作者。
