Title: Performance by the Soviet State Folk Dance Ensemble; Chairman Mao Attends and Receives Leaders of the Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1954-10-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Soviet State Folk Dance Ensemble gave a performance at Huairentang Hall on the evening of the 4th.

　　The performance was sponsored by the Exhibition of Achievements in Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction.

　　At 8:10 PM, Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Shaoqi, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, and the head of the Soviet Government Delegation invited to China to attend the 5th Anniversary of the National Day, N.S. Khrushchev, delegation members N.A. Bulganin, A.I. Mikoyan, N.M. Shvernik, head of the Romanian People's Republic Government Delegation G. Apostol, delegation member Dr. P. Groza, head of the Mongolian People's Republic Government Delegation Z. Sambu, head of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation V. Kopecky, head of the Hungarian People's Republic Government Delegation A. Hegedüs, head of the Bulgarian People's Republic Government Delegation R. Damianov, head of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Government Delegation Hoang Minh Giam, member of the Government Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Ri Young, etc., entered the hall. At this moment, the entire audience stood up, and a storm of applause erupted.

　　Also watching the performance were Vice Chairmen and members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice Premiers, Secretary-General, Ministers and Vice Ministers of the State Council, Directors and Deputy Directors of various ministries and commissions, the President of the Supreme People's Court, the Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Vice Chairmen and members of the National Defense Council, representatives of the National People's Congress, senior cadres of the Chinese Communist Party and various democratic parties, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various people's organizations, the Vice Mayor of Beijing, and figures from cultural, artistic, scientific, educational, and journalistic circles of the capital.

　　Members of the government delegations from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Romania, Mongolia, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Bulgaria, Vietnam, and Albania, who were invited to China to participate in the celebrations for the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, were also present to watch the performance.

　　Also watching the performance was the Soviet cultural delegation headed by A.I. Denisov, which was invited to China to participate in the celebrations for the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

　　Also watching the performance were the envoys of the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries stationed in China.

　　At the beginning of the performance, the national anthems of China and the Soviet Union were played.

　　Each program performed tonight by the Soviet State Folk Dance Ensemble, led by Igor Moiseyev, was exceptionally brilliant and received warm welcome and appreciation. The actors took curtain calls repeatedly amidst applause.

　　During the intermission, Chairman Mao Zedong received Igor Moiseyev, the artistic director of the Soviet State Folk Dance Ensemble, and A. Konstantinov, the head of the troupe.

　　Present at the reception were N.S. Khrushchev, N.A. Bulganin, A.I. Mikoyan, and N.M. Shvernik.

　　Also present at the reception were Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai.

### 苏联国立民间舞蹈团举行表演会  毛主席出席观看并接见舞蹈团负责人员

1954-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联国立民间舞蹈团举行表演会
    毛主席出席观看并接见舞蹈团负责人员
    【新华社四日讯】苏联国立民间舞蹈团四日晚在怀仁堂举行表演会。
      表演会由苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主办。
    八时十分，中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、副主席朱德、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来和应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的苏联政府代表团团长尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫、团员尼·亚·布尔加宁、阿·伊·米高扬、尼·米·什维尔尼克，罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长格·阿波斯托尔、团员彼·格罗查博士，蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长扎·桑布，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团长瓦·柯别茨基，匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团团长安·赫格居斯，保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团团长拉·达米扬诺夫，越南民主共和国政府代表团团长黄明鉴，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长贝·什图拉，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团团员李英等走进大厅。这时，全场起立，响起了暴风雨般的掌声。
    观看表演的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长和委员，国务院副总理、秘书长、各部部长、副部长、各委员会主任、副主任，最高人民法院院长，最高人民检察院检察长，国防委员会副主席和委员，全国人民代表大会代表，中国共产党和各民主党派的高级干部，中国人民解放军的高级将领，各人民团体的负责人和北京市副市长以及首都文化、艺术、科学、教育、新闻界人士等。
    应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的苏联、朝鲜、罗马尼亚、蒙古、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、保加利亚、越南和阿尔巴尼亚等国政府代表团团员，也到会观看表演。
    观看表演的，还有应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的以亚·伊·杰尼索夫为首的苏联文化代表团。
    观看表演的，还有苏联和各人民民主国家驻我国的使节。
    表演会开始时，奏中苏两国国歌。
    由伊戈尔·莫伊塞耶夫领导的苏联国立民间舞蹈团今晚所表演的每个节目都异常精彩，受到了热烈欢迎和赞赏。演员们在掌声中一再谢幕。
    在表演会休息时间，毛泽东主席接见了苏联国立民间舞蹈团的艺术指导伊戈尔·莫伊塞耶夫、团主任安·康斯坦丁诺夫。
    接见时在座的，有尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫、尼·亚· 布尔加宁、阿·伊·米高扬、尼·米·什维尔尼克。
    接见时在座的，还有朱德、刘少奇、周恩来。
