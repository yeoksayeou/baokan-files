Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Deputies Volkov and Others Send Telegram to Dr. Herriot Congratulating Him on the 30th Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between the Soviet Union and France
Author:
Date: 1954-10-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 28th: According to TASS, some deputies of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union sent a telegram to Dr. Édouard Herriot, Honorary President of the French National Assembly, Academician of the French Academy of Sciences, and Mayor of Lyon, congratulating him on the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and France.

The telegram first expressed congratulations to Herriot, and then said: "Thirty years ago, you advocated the establishment and development of relations between the Soviet Union and France, and with full justification, you believed that cooperation between countries, regardless of their social and political systems, is in the fundamental interests of all peoples and is necessary to ensure universal security. The establishment of diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and France played an important role in the history of international relations."

The telegram continued: "You correctly believed that the resurgence of German militarism would pose a fatal danger to the people of Europe, including the French people. You regarded the close cooperation of European countries, first and foremost the strengthening of cooperation between the Soviet Union and France in maintaining peace in Europe, as the cornerstone of European peace and security. These words of yours, which are beneficial to the Soviet Union and France and for the interests of cooperation for world peace, have always aroused warm responses from the broad masses of the Soviet people."

The telegram concluded: "We wish you good health and continued efforts for the well-being of the French people and all peace-loving people in the world."

The names of the Soviet Supreme Soviet deputies who signed the congratulatory telegram are: Volkov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union; Lacis, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities; Suslov, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Soviet of the Union; Shepilov, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Soviet of Nationalities; Yasnov, Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet; Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences; as well as Skobeltsyn, Ehrenburg, and Tikhonov.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表沃尔科夫等致电赫里欧博士祝贺苏法建交三十周年

1954-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表沃尔科夫等
    致电赫里欧博士祝贺苏法建交三十周年
    【新华社二十九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十八日讯：苏联最高苏维埃一些代表致电法国国民议会名誉主席、法国科学院院士、里昂市长爱德华·赫里欧博士，祝贺苏联和法国建交三十周年。
    电文首先向赫里欧表示祝贺，然后说：“三十年前您主张确立和发展苏联和法国之间的关系，并且以充分的理由认为，各国之间不问其社会制度和政治制度如何而进行合作是符合各国人民的根本利益的，也是保障普遍安全所必需的。苏联和法国外交关系的建立，在国际关系史中起着重要的作用。”
    电文接着说：“你正确地认为：德国军国主义的复活会给包括法国人民在内的欧洲各国人民造成致命的危险。你把欧洲各国的密切合作，首先是加强苏联和法国在维持欧洲和平事业上的合作看成是欧洲和平和安全的基石。你这些有益于苏联和法国以及为了世界和平的利益而合作的言论始终引起苏联广大人民群众的热烈反应。”
    电文最后说：“我们敬祝你健康，继续为法国人民以及世界上一切爱好和平的人民的福利而努力。”
    在贺电上签署的苏联最高苏维埃代表的名字是：联盟院主席沃尔科夫、民族院主席拉齐斯、联盟院外交委员会主席苏斯洛夫、民族院外交委员会主席谢皮洛夫、莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫、苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫以及斯科贝尔琴、爱伦堡、吉洪诺夫。
