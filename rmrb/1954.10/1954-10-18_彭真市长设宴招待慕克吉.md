Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet for Mookerjee
Author:
Date: 1954-10-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 16th: Mayor Peng Zhen of Beijing Municipality hosted a banquet on the evening of the 16th for Mr. Mookerjee, the Mayor of Calcutta, India, who was invited to Beijing.

    Mr. Gao Beden, Counselor of the Indian Embassy in China, was also invited to attend the banquet.

    Vice Mayors Zhang Youyu and Wu Han of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, as well as the Secretary-General and Deputy Secretary-General of the Municipal Government and responsible staff members of various departments, were present.

    Also invited to attend were Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Lao She, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Workers, and heads of other people's organizations in Beijing.

    The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 彭真市长设宴招待慕克吉

1954-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真市长设宴招待慕克吉
    【新华社十六日讯】北京市人民政府市长彭真十六日晚设宴招待应邀来北京的印度加尔各答市市长慕克吉先生。
    印度驻我国大使馆参赞高伯登先生也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的有北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、吴晗，市府正副秘书长及各部门负责工作人员。
    应邀出席作陪的还有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会主席张奚若、北京市文学艺术工作者联合会主席老舍及北京市其他各人民团体负责人。
    宴会在友好的空气中进行。
