Title: Indira Gandhi Continues Visit in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-23
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd) Indira Gandhi visited the Beijing Municipal Maternity and Child Health Experimental Institute, a health station, and the Beijing Municipal People's Court on the 22nd.

Indira Gandhi visited the maternity ward, infant room, delivery room, operating room, outpatient department, and the experimental nursery attached to the Beijing Municipal Maternity and Child Health Experimental Institute. She learned in detail about the various organizational forms and working methods of maternity and child health care in Beijing, as well as the development and achievements of this work since liberation.

At a health station in the Qianmen District, the female station director introduced Indira Gandhi to the health station's work in promoting maternity and child health care among the citizens, with the assistance and support of the People's Government.

In the afternoon, during her visit to the Beijing Municipal People's Court, Indira Gandhi inquired about the organization and trial system of the People's Court and observed the trial of a marriage case.

### 英迪拉·甘地夫人继续在北京参观访问

1954-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

     英迪拉·甘地夫人继续在北京参观访问
    【新华社二十二日讯】英迪拉·甘地夫人在二十二日访问了北京市妇幼保健实验院、一个保健站和北京市人民法院。
    英迪拉·甘地夫人参观了北京市妇幼保健实验院的产妇室、婴儿室、产房、手术室、门诊部和这个保健院附设的实验托儿所。她详尽地了解了北京市妇幼保健事业方面的各种组织形式和工作方法，以及解放以来这一事业的发展情况和成就。
    在前门区的一个保健站里，女站长向英迪拉·甘地夫人介绍了在人民政府的协助和支持下，保健站在市民群众中推广妇幼保健工作的情况。
    下午，在访问北京市人民法院时，英迪拉·甘地夫人询问了关于人民法院的组织及审判制度等方面的情况，并参观了一件婚姻案件的审判。
