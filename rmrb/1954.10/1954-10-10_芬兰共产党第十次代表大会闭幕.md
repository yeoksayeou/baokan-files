Title: Closing of the 10th Congress of the Communist Party of Finland
Author: 
Date: 1954-10-10
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, dispatch from Helsinki, October 8th) According to TASS, the 10th Congress of the Communist Party of Finland concluded on the 5th.

    At the meeting, in addition to the summary report on the work of the Party Central Committee made by the Party's General Secretary Ville Pessi, Aimo Aaltonen, Chairman of the Party Central Committee, also made a report on organizational work, and Matti Janhunen, Member of the Party Central Committee and Chairman of the Party Constitution Committee, made a report on the revision of the Party Constitution.
    After several days of discussion, the Congress unanimously approved the Party's line and passed resolutions on the tasks of struggling for peace and the vital interests and democratic rights of the working people, as well as resolutions on organizational work and the Party's mass work.
    The Congress also unanimously endorsed the report on the revision of the Party Constitution. The report emphasized that the proposed amendments to the Party Constitution were related to the Party's new tasks. The report emphasized the significance of Marxist-Leninist theory as the strength of the Party, and the significance of consolidating the unity of the working class and collective leadership.
    On the 5th, the Congress elected a new Central Committee by secret ballot. The newly elected Central Committee immediately held a plenary meeting and elected Ville Pessi as the General Secretary of the Communist Party of Finland, Aimo Aaltonen as the Chairman of the Communist Party, and Yrjö Murto as the Vice-Chairman of the Communist Party.
    The new Central Committee plenary meeting also elected members of the Politburo, who are: Aimo Aaltonen, Arni Huldkonen, Paavo Koskinen, Herta Kuusinen, Inga Lähteenmäki, Matti Malmberg, Yrjö Murto, Aili Mäkinen, Ville Pessi, and Mauri Ryömä.

### 芬兰共产党第十次代表大会闭幕

1954-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰共产党第十次代表大会闭幕
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社讯：芬兰共产党第十次代表大会已经在五日闭幕。
    这次会议除了由党的总书记维利·培西作了关于党中央委员会工作的总结报告外，还由党中央委员会主席艾摩·阿尔托宁作了有关组织工作问题的报告，党中央委员、党章问题委员会主席马蒂·扬胡宁作了关于修改党章的报告。
    经过几天的讨论，代表大会一致赞成党的路线，并通过了关于为和平和劳动人民的切身利益和民主权利而斗争的任务的决议，以及关于组织工作问题和党的群众工作问题的决议。
    代表大会也一致拥护关于修改党章的报告。这个报告着重指出，这次所建议的对党章的修改是和党的新任务有关的。报告强调了马克思—列宁主义理论作为党的力量的意义，强调巩固工人阶级团结一致和集体领导的意义。
    代表大会在五日以秘密投票方式选出了新的中央委员会。新选出的中央委员会随即举行全体会议，选举了维利·培西为芬兰共产党总书记，艾摩·阿尔托宁为共产党主席，伊尔约·穆尔托为共产党副主席。
    新的中央委员会全体会议还选举了政治局委员，他们是：艾摩·阿尔托宁、阿尔尼·胡尔科宁、帕沃·柯斯基宁、赫塔·库西宁、英·莱蒂宁、马蒂·马尔姆伯格、伊尔约·穆尔托、阿伊利·麦基宁、维利·培西和毛里·卢玛。
