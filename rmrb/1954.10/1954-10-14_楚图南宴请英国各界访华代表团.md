Title: Chu Tunan Hosts a Banquet for the British Delegation from Various Circles Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1954-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 13th: Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 13th for all members of the British delegation of parliamentarians, trade union workers, cooperative workers, scientists, teachers, and artists visiting China.

### 楚图南宴请英国各界访华代表团

1954-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    楚图南宴请英国各界访华代表团
    【新华社十三日讯】中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南在十三日晚宴请了英国议员、工会工作者、合作社工作者、科学家、教师和艺术家访华代表团的全体团员。
