Title: Polish and German Government Delegations Leave Beijing to Visit Various Locations

Author: 
Date: 1954-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 4th:

    The Government Delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, headed by First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Bolesław Bierut, left Beijing on the morning of the 4th to visit various locations. Huang Jing, Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the People's Republic of China, accompanied the delegation.

    During its stay in Beijing, the delegation visited and toured scenic spots and historical sites such as the Summer Palace, the Forbidden City, and Beihai Park.

Xinhua News Agency, October 4th:

    The Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Dr. Lothar Bolz, left Beijing on the morning of the 4th to visit various locations. Lai Jifa, Deputy Minister of Heavy Industry of the People's Republic of China, accompanied the delegation.

    During its stay in Beijing, the delegation visited the Guanting Reservoir, the Beijing No. 1 Cotton Mill, and the city of Beijing, and toured the Summer Palace, the Forbidden City, the Yonghe Temple, the Temple of Heaven, and the Temple of Azure Clouds.

### 波、德两国政府代表团离北京到各地参观

1954-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    波、德两国政府代表团离北京到各地参观
    【新华社四日讯】以波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波·贝鲁特为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团，在四日上午离开北京到各地参观。陪同前往的有中华人民共和国第一机械工业部部长黄敬。
    代表团在北京期间，曾参观游览了颐和园、故宫、北海等名胜古迹。
    【新华社四日讯】以德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长洛·博尔茨博士为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，在四日上午离开北京到各地参观。陪同前往的，有中华人民共和国重工业部副部长赖际发。
    代表团在北京期间，曾参观了官厅水库、北京国棉一厂和北京市市容，并游览了颐和园、故宫、雍和宫、天坛和碧云寺。
