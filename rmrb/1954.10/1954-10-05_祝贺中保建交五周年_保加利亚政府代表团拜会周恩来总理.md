Title: Congratulating the Fifth Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations between China and Bulgaria: Bulgarian Government Delegation Visits Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-10-05
Page: 1

    Congratulating the Fifth Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations between China and Bulgaria
    Bulgarian Government Delegation Visits Premier Zhou Enlai
    [Xinhua News Agency, 4th] October 4th marks the fifth anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Bulgaria. To commemorate this occasion, the head of the Bulgarian Government Delegation, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of People's Republic of Bulgaria, Raiko Damyanov, delegation members: member of the Central Committee of Bulgarian Communist Party and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Mincho Neychev, and member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Ambassador to China, Dimov, visited Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai on October 4th to express congratulations. Raiko Damyanov conveyed congratulations on behalf of Comrade Chervenkov and wished China further achievements in building socialism. Premier Zhou Enlai expressed his gratitude. Both Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Chairman Damyanov expressed their desire to further develop the friendly relations between China and Bulgaria. Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan was present during Premier Zhou Enlai's meeting.

### 祝贺中保建交五周年  保加利亚政府代表团拜会周恩来总理

1954-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺中保建交五周年
    保加利亚政府代表团拜会周恩来总理
    【新华社四日讯】十月四日为中华人民共和国和保加利亚人民共和国建交五周年。为了纪念这个日子，保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团团长、保加利亚共产党中央政治局委员、保加利亚人民共和国部长会议副主席拉·达米扬诺夫，团员：保加利亚共产党中央委员、保加利亚人民共和国外交部部长明·涅伊切夫以及保加利亚共产党中央委员、驻我国大使迪莫夫在十月四日拜会我国务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，表示祝贺。拉·达米扬诺夫并代表契尔文科夫同志表示祝贺之意，祝我国在建设社会主义中获得进一步的成就。周恩来总理表示谢意。周恩来总理和达米扬诺夫副主席均表示了进一步发展中保两国之间友好关系的愿望。周恩来总理接见时有我外交部副部长伍修权在座。
