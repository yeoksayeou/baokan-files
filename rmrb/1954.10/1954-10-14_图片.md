Title: Picture
Author: Wang Zhiyuan
Date: 1954-10-14
Page: 2

    Agricultural scientists from the North China Agricultural Science Institute held a discussion after reading the Sino-Soviet Communiqué. They all believed that the signing of the Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement would further promote the development of agricultural science and technology in our country. (Photo by Wang Zhiyuan of this newspaper)
