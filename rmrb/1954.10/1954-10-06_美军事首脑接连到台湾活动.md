Title: US Military Leaders Conduct Successive Activities in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1954-10-06
Page: 4

US Military Leaders Conduct Successive Activities in Taiwan

Xinhua News Agency, 4th Dispatch: US Pacific Air Force Commander Sory Smith arrived in Taiwan on the 1st and held secret talks with the head of the Chiang bandit army. According to Agence France-Presse, Smith's activities in Taiwan are related to the United States accelerating the transfer of Saber-type jets to the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique. Before leaving Taiwan on the 2nd, Smith also inspected the Chiang bandit gang's air force bases.

At the same time, according to Reuters, Frederick Kivette, commander of the US Seventh Fleet's Task Force 72, made a "hurried visit" to Taipei on the 1st and held talks with Chase, the head of the US Military Assistance Advisory Group in Taiwan.

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd Dispatch: After plotting with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique to strengthen the military power of the Chiang bandit army, Taylor, commander of the US Eighth Army in Korea, left Taiwan on September 30 and returned to South Korea. During his three-day stay in Taiwan, Taylor held talks with the traitor Chiang Kai-shek, the heads of various branches of the Chiang bandit army, and senior officers of the US Military Assistance Advisory Group, and extensively inspected the Chiang bandit army's military institutions, military bases, military schools, and the equipment and training of various branches of the armed forces.

### 美军事首脑接连到台湾活动

1954-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    美军事首脑接连到台湾活动
   【新华社四日讯】美国驻太平洋空军司令索里·史密斯在一日抵达台湾，和蒋贼军头目进行秘密会谈。据法新社透露，史密斯这次到台湾活动和美国加速把佩刀式喷气机移交给蒋介石卖国集团一事有关。二日，史密斯在离开台湾以前，还视察了蒋匪帮的空军基地。
    同时，据路透社报道，美国第七舰队第七十二特遣舰队司令弗雷德里克·基维特曾在一日到台北作了一次“急促的访问”，和美国驻台湾军事援助顾问团团长蔡斯进行会谈。
   【新华社二日讯】侵朝美军第八军军长泰勒在和蒋介石卖国集团策划加强蒋贼军的军事力量以后，已在九月三十日离台湾回到南朝鲜。泰勒在逗留台湾的三天期间，和卖国贼蒋介石、蒋贼军各兵种的头目和美国军事援助顾问团的高级军官进行了会谈，并广泛视察了蒋贼军的军事机构、军事基地、军事学校及各兵种部队的装备和训练情况。
