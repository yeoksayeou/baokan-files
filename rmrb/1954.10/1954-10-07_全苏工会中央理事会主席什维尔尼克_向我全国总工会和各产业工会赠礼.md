Title: Chairman Shvernik of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions Presents Gifts to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Various Industrial Unions

Author: 
Date: 1954-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: Ni.M. Shvernik, a member of the Soviet government delegation and Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, who was invited by the Chinese government to participate in the celebration of the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, presented gifts to the responsible comrades of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and various industrial unions on the evening of the 5th.

Those attending the gift presentation included Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Vice-Chairmen Liu Ningyi, Liu Changsheng, and Zhu Xuefan; Secretaries of the Secretariat Xu Zhizhen, Li Jiebo, Liu Ziju, Zhang Weizhen, and Li Zaiwen; Wang Weigang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Second China Machinery Workers' Union; Kang Yonghe, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the First China Machinery Workers' Union; Zhao Guoqiang, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Heavy Industry Union; Jin Zhifu, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Coal Miners' Union; Cai Shubin, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Electrical Workers' Union; Zhang Qingchun, Secretary-General of the National Committee of the China Shop Employees' Union; An Lifu, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Highway Transportation Union; Wang Zhijie, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the China Railway Union; Shao Jingwa, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the China Textile Workers' Union; Xiang Minghua, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union; Zhang Jin, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Construction Workers' Union; and Song Chuan, Deputy Director of the Agricultural, Forestry, and Water Conservancy Trade Union Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

The gifts included the latest and best Soviet-made film projectors, cameras, and many other valuable gifts carefully manufactured by the Soviet working class.

### 全苏工会中央理事会主席什维尔尼克  向我全国总工会和各产业工会赠礼

1954-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    全苏工会中央理事会主席什维尔尼克
    向我全国总工会和各产业工会赠礼
    【新华社六日讯】五日晚，应中国政府邀请来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的苏联政府代表团团员、全苏工会中央理事会主席尼·米·什维尔尼克向中华全国总工会及各产业工会的负责同志赠礼。
    参加受礼的有中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，副主席刘宁一、刘长胜、朱学范，书记处书记许之桢、李颉伯、刘子久、张维桢、栗再温，中国第二机械工会全国委员会主席王维纲，中国第一机械工会筹备委员会主任康永和，中国重工业工会全国委员会主席赵国强，中国煤矿工会全国委员会主席金直夫，中国电业工会全国委员会主席蔡书彬，中国店员工会全国委员会秘书长张庆春，中国公路运输工会全国委员会主席安力夫，中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席王志杰，中国纺织工会全国委员会副主席邵井蛙，中国邮电工会全国委员会副主席向明华，中国建筑工会筹备委员会副主任张进，中华全国总工会农林水利工会工作委员会副主任宋川。
    所赠礼品中有苏联最新出品的最好的电影放映机、照像机及其他许多苏联工人阶级精心制造的珍贵礼品。
