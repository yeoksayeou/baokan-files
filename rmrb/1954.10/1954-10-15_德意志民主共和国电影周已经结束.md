Title: German Democratic Republic Film Week Has Ended
Author:
Date: 1954-10-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th: To celebrate the 5th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, the German Democratic Republic Film Week, which began on the 7th in 20 major cities in our country, has ended. According to statistics from ten cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Wuhan, Xi'an, Guangzhou, Nanjing, Jinan, Taiyuan, and Nanning, a total of 987 screenings were held, with over 660,000 audience members.
    To meet the needs of the broad audience, the number of screenings was increased in cities such as Wuhan and Taiyuan. Shanxi People's Broadcasting Station also introduced the main content of each film to the public. Local newspapers also published articles introducing the film content and the audience's impressions.
    The delegation of film workers from the German Democratic Republic, who were visiting Nanjing, met with the audience at the Dahua Cinema in Nanjing. The representative of the delegation, Irene Korb, the female lead of "Men and Women Advancing Shoulder to Shoulder," addressed the audience, saying, "Through the holding of this film week, the friendship between the people of China and Germany will be further strengthened." The film workers delegation and Irene Korb's speech were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 德意志民主共和国电影周已经结束

1954-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国电影周已经结束
    【新华社十四日讯】为庆祝德意志民主共和国建国五周年，自七日起在我国二十个大城市举行的德意志民主共和国电影周已经结束。据北京、上海、天津、武汉、西安、广州、南京、济南、太原、南宁等十个城市的统计，共放映了九百八十七场，观众达六十六万多人次。
    为满足广大观众的要求，在武汉、太原等城市都增加了映出场数。山西人民广播电台还向群众介绍了各部影片的主要内容。各地报纸还发表了有关介绍影片内容和群众观后感的文章。
    在南京访问的德意志民主共和国电影工作者代表团在南京市大华电影院和观众见面。代表团代表、“男女并肩前进”的女主角伊伦内·阔尔布向观众讲了话，她说：“通过这次电影周的举行，中德两国人民的友谊将更加加强。”电影工作者代表团和伊伦内·阔尔布的讲话受到观众热烈欢迎。
