Title: Government Delegations from the Soviet Union, China and Other Countries Arrive in Berlin to Attend the Fifth Anniversary Celebration of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1954-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 6th. TASS, Berlin, 5th: The Soviet government delegation, led by the First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov, arrived in Berlin on the 5th to participate in the celebrations for the fifth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

In addition to the head of the delegation, Molotov, the Soviet government delegation consists of the following personnel: Soviet Communist Party Central Committee Secretary Pospelov, Soviet Academy of Sciences President Nesmeyanov, Ukrainian Communist Party Central Committee Secretary Ivashchenko, and Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic Pushkin, who is already in Berlin.

Welcoming them at the airport were senior leaders of the party, government, and mass organizations, headed by German Democratic Republic Prime Minister Grotewohl and German Socialist Unity Party Central Committee First Secretary and German Democratic Republic Deputy Prime Minister Ulbricht.

Also welcoming them at the airport were Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic Pushkin and embassy staff, Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet forces in Germany, General Grechko, and representatives of the Soviet army.

Also welcoming them at the airport were the government delegations of the People's Republic of China and other People's Democracies, as well as diplomatic envoys and personnel from various countries stationed in Germany.

Molotov made a statement at the airport. He said: The German Democratic Republic is five years old. These five years have been five years of vigorous economic and cultural development for the German Democratic Republic, five years of continuous growth in the welfare of its residents, and five years of persistent struggle by the German Democratic Republic for the unification of Germany and for peace and international security.

Molotov said: The Soviet people are pleased with the achievements of the people of the German Democratic Republic in economic and cultural construction, and are pleased with the achievements they have made in bringing the working people of East and West Germany closer together, which has created the most important prerequisite for resolving the fundamental issue of restoring German unity on a peaceful and democratic basis. Solving this problem well is in line with the desire of all peace-loving people to ensure and consolidate peace.

Molotov said: The German people can be deeply confident that the Soviet Union will, as before, provide all-round friendly assistance to their just cause.

Finally, Molotov wished the unbreakable friendship between the Soviet and German people, a guarantee of lasting peace and security in Europe, to grow and strengthen.

On the same day, German Democratic Republic President Pieck received the Soviet government delegation. Grotewohl and Ulbricht were present at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 6th: Chen Yi, head of the government delegation of the People's Republic of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, and delegation member Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, arrived in Berlin by plane on the 5th to participate in the celebrations for the fifth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic. Government delegations from Korea, Mongolia, Vietnam and Albania arrived in Berlin on the same plane as the government delegation of the People's Republic of China.

Welcoming them at the airport were German Democratic Republic Deputy Prime Ministers Heinrich Rau, Otto Nuschke, and Hans Schulz, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Hanke, German Socialist Unity Party Central Committee Politburo members Matern, Oesner, and Sydow, as well as other senior responsible persons from the German Democratic Republic government, the German Socialist Unity Party, and people's organizations.

Also welcoming them at the airport were diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in the German Democratic Republic.

Ji Pengfei, the other member of our government delegation currently in Berlin and the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Germany, and all embassy staff were also at the airport to greet them.

### 应邀参加德意志民主共和国成立五周年国庆典礼  苏中等国政府代表团到达柏林

1954-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参加德意志民主共和国成立五周年国庆典礼
    苏中等国政府代表团到达柏林
   【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社柏林五日讯：参加德意志民主共和国成立五周年国庆典礼的苏联政府代表团在团长苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部长莫洛托夫的率领下，已在五日抵达柏林。
    苏联政府代表团除团长莫洛托夫外，由下列人员组成：苏联共产党中央委员会书记波斯伯洛夫，苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫，乌克兰共产党中央委员会书记伊瓦希钦科以及已在柏林的苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使普希金。
    前往机场欢迎的有：以德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥以及德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记、德意志民主共和国副总理乌布利希为首的党、政、群众团体的高级领导人员。
    前往机场欢迎的还有：苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使普希金和大使馆工作人员，苏联驻德国军队总司令格列奇科大将和苏军代表们。
    前往机场欢迎的还有：中华人民共和国、其他人民民主国家的政府代表团和各国驻德外交使节和人员。
    莫洛托夫在机场发表了谈话。他说：德意志民主共和国成立五周年了。这五年是德意志民主共和国的经济和文化蓬勃发展，它的居民福利不断增长的五年，也是德意志民主共和国为德国的统一、为和平和国际安全而进行顽强的斗争的五年。
    莫洛托夫说：苏联人民为德意志民主共和国人民在经济和文化建设中所获得的成就感到高兴，为他们在使东德和西德的劳动人民接近的工作中所做出的成绩感到高兴，这种接近使在和平民主的基础上解决恢复德国统一这个基本问题创造了最重要的前提。很好地解决这个问题是符合一切爱好和平的人民争取保证和巩固和平的愿望的。
    莫洛托夫说：德国人民可以深信，苏联将和以前一样地会对他们这个正义的事业作各方面的友好的援助。
    最后，莫洛托夫预祝欧洲持久和平和安全的保障——苏德两国人民牢不可破的友谊日益发展和巩固。
    当天，德意志民主共和国总统皮克接见了苏联政府代表团。接见时在座的有格罗提渥和乌布利希。
   【新华社柏林六日电】应邀参加德意志民主共和国成立五周年国庆典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅，团员、中国科学院副院长竺可桢已在五日乘飞机抵达柏林。与中华人民共和国政府代表团同机到达柏林的还有朝鲜、蒙古、越南和阿尔巴尼亚等国的政府代表团。
    到机场欢迎的有：德意志民主共和国政府副总理亨利希·劳、奥托·努舍克、汉斯·舒尔茨，外交部代理部长韩克、德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员马特恩、厄斯纳和希德万，以及德意志民主共和国政府、德国统一社会党和人民团体的其他高级负责人员。
    到机场欢迎的还有驻在德意志民主共和国的各国外交使节。
    现在柏林的我国政府代表团另一团员、中华人民共和国驻德大使姬鹏飞及大使馆全体人员也在机场迎接。
