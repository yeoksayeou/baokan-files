Title: Polish Government Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Various Places
Author:
Date: 1954-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The Polish People's Republic Government Delegation, headed by the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Bolesław Bierut, and accompanied by Huang Jing, Minister of our First Ministry of Machine Building, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 9th after visiting Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Anshan, Shenyang and other places from the 4th to the 8th.

In Nanjing, the delegation visited Dr. Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum and the Yuhuatai Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery and laid wreaths, and toured the Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum, Linggu Temple and Xuanwu Lake. In Shanghai, they visited the State-owned Shanghai No. 1 Cotton Mill, Shanghai Machine Tool Plant and Tongji University. In Hangzhou, they toured the scenic spots and historical sites of West Lake and visited the Qiantang River Bridge and the Longjing tea area. In Anshan, they visited the Anshan Iron and Steel Works, and in Shenyang, they visited the Shenyang Pneumatic Tool Factory.

The delegation also visited workers' residential areas, clubs and sanatoriums in various places, and had cordial conversations with the workers.

The delegation received a warm welcome wherever they went.

### 波兰政府代表团在各地参观后回到北京

1954-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰政府代表团在各地参观后回到北京
   【新华社九日讯】以波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波·贝鲁特为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团由我第一机械工业部部长黄敬陪同，在四日至八日赴南京、上海、杭州、鞍山、沈阳等地参观后，已于九日下午乘飞机返抵北京。
    代表团在南京时曾谒中山陵和雨花台革命烈士陵并献花圈，游览了明孝陵、灵谷寺和玄武湖。在上海时参观了国营上海第一棉纺织厂、上海机床厂和同济大学。在杭州时游览了西湖的名胜古迹，参观了钱塘江大桥和龙井茶区。在鞍山时参观了鞍山钢铁厂，在沈阳时参观了沈阳风动工具厂。
    代表团还访问了各地的工人住宅区、俱乐部和疗养院，并和工人们进行了亲切地交谈。
    代表团所到之处，都受到了热烈欢迎。
