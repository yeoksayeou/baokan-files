Title: Indian Newspapers Publish News of Prime Minister Nehru's Arrival in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 21st: Indian newspapers on the 20th almost all published news on the front page with banner headlines about Indian Prime Minister Nehru's warm welcome in Beijing and Chairman Mao Zedong's meeting with Prime Minister Nehru.

    The "Free Press" headline stated: "Beijing Warmly Welcomes Nehru"; "Warm Manifestation of Sincere Feelings."

    "The Hindustan Times" said: "China Gives Mr. Nehru the Grandest Welcome."

    "The Times of India" headline said: "Beijing Warmly Welcomes Mr. Nehru"; "Crowds Cheered All the Way from the Airport."

    "The Indian Express" banner headline said: "Nehru Meets Mao Zedong"; "The Chinese Capital is Like a Holiday."

    Many of these newspapers also published photographs of Chairman Mao Zedong and Prime Minister Nehru.

### 印度报纸刊载尼赫鲁总理到北京消息

1954-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    印度报纸刊载尼赫鲁总理到北京消息
   【新华社新德里二十一日电】印度报纸二十日几乎全都在第一版用通栏大标题刊登了印度总理尼赫鲁在北京受到热烈欢迎以及毛泽东主席接见尼赫鲁总理的消息。
    “自由新闻”的标题说：“北京热烈欢迎尼赫鲁”；“真挚感情的热烈表现。”
    “印度斯坦时报”说：“中国给予尼赫鲁先生以最盛大的欢迎。”
    “印度时报”的标题说：“北京热烈欢迎尼赫鲁先生”；“从机场起沿路群众夹道欢呼。”
    “印度快报”的通栏标题说：“尼赫鲁会见毛泽东”；“中国首都像过节日一般。”
    这些报纸中有许多还刊登了毛泽东主席和尼赫鲁总理的照片。
