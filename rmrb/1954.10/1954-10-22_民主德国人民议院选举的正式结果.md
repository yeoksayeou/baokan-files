Title: Official Results of the People's Chamber Election in the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1954-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 21st: TASS reported from Berlin on October 21st: The Central Election Committee responsible for the People's Chamber election in the German Democratic Republic announced the final official results of the People's Chamber election in the German Democratic Republic held on October 17th as follows:

    The total number of eligible voters nationwide was 12,085,380; the total number of participants in the election was 11,892,849, accounting for 98.41 percent of the electorate.

    The total number of votes in favor of the candidate list of the National Front of Democratic Germany was 11,828,877, accounting for 99.46 percent of the total votes cast.

    The number of invalid ballots and ballots against the unified list of the National Front was 63,972, accounting for 0.54 percent.

    The preliminary results of the election of the People's Representatives Assembly of Greater Berlin, which had been previously published, are the final official results.

### 民主德国人民议院选举的正式结果

1954-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国人民议院选举的正式结果
   【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社柏林二十一日讯：负责德意志民主共和国人民议院选举的中央选举委员会发表了关于十月十七日举行的德意志民主共和国人民议院选举的最后正式结果如下：
    全国有选举权的人共一千二百零八万五千三百八十人；参加选举的共有一千一百八十九万二千八百四十九人，即占选民人数的百分之九十八点四一。
    投票赞成民主德国全国阵线候选人名单的共有一千一百八十二万八千八百七十七人，即占投票人数的百分之九十九点四六。
    作废的选票和不赞成全国阵线统一名单的选票是六万三千九百七十二票，即占百分之零点五四。
    以前已经发表的关于大柏林市人民代表会议选举的初步结果是最后的正式结果。
