Title: Japanese Workers' Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: The Japanese workers' delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend China's National Day celebrations, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 23rd to return home.

After arriving in Beijing on September 30th, the delegation, in addition to participating in China's National Day celebrations, held forums with the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and many industrial unions, and visited factories, schools, and the Guanting Reservoir in Beijing. The delegation then visited Shanghai and Shenyang, Fushun, and Anshan in Northeast China. They visited steel plants, machinery factories, textile mills, paper mills, coal mines, wharves, railways, public transportation and postal and telecommunications departments, employee hospitals and workers' cultural palaces, as well as women and children's welfare organizations.

On the eve of the delegation's departure from Beijing, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions held a banquet to bid them farewell.

### 日本工人代表团回国

1954-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    日本工人代表团回国
    【新华社二十三日讯】应中华全国总工会邀请来参加我国国庆观礼的日本工人代表团，已在二十三日晨乘飞机离北京转道返国。
    代表团在九月三十日到达北京后，除参加我国国庆观礼外，还和中华全国总工会以及许多产业工会举行了座谈会，参观了北京的工厂、学校以及官厅水库。代表团随后访问了上海和我国东北的沈阳、抚顺、鞍山。他们在那些地方参观了钢铁厂，机器厂，纺织厂，造纸厂，煤矿，码头，铁路，公共交通和邮电部门，职工医院和工人文化宫，以及妇女儿童福利机关等。
    代表团离京前夕，中华全国总工会曾举行宴会欢送。
