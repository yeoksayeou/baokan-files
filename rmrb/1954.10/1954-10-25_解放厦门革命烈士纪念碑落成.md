Title: Completion of the Monument to Revolutionary Martyrs for the Liberation of Xiamen
Author:
Date: 1954-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, October 24th: The monument to revolutionary martyrs, built by the people of Xiamen City to commemorate the revolutionary martyrs who gloriously sacrificed themselves five years ago in the liberation of Xiamen, has been completed and a grand unveiling ceremony was held on October 17th, the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Xiamen.

The monument stands at the foot of the scenic Wanshi Rock on Xiamen Island, with the golden characters "The heroic spirit of the martyrs will forever guard the sea frontier," inscribed on the monument by Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council.

More than 1,500 people from all walks of life in Xiamen and representatives of the garrison troops attended the unveiling ceremony. Zhang Daoshi, Deputy Secretary of the Xiamen Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and the head of a certain unit of the Xiamen garrison, both spoke at the unveiling ceremony.

### 解放厦门革命烈士纪念碑落成

1954-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    解放厦门革命烈士纪念碑落成
    【新华社福州二十四日电】厦门市人民为纪念五年前解放厦门光荣牺牲的革命烈士而修建的革命烈士纪念碑已经落成，并于厦门解放五周年纪念日——十月十七日隆重举行了揭幕典礼。
    纪念碑矗立在厦门岛上风景美丽的万石岩下，碑上刻着国防委员会副主席陈毅所题的“先烈雄风永镇海疆”的金色大字。
    参加揭幕典礼的有厦门市各界人民和驻军代表一千五百余人。中共厦门市委员会副书记张道时、厦门市驻军某部首长，在揭幕典礼上都讲了话。
