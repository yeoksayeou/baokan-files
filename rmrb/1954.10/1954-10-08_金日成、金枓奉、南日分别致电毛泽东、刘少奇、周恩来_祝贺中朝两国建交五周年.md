Title: Kim Il-sung, Kim Tu-bong, and Nam Il Send Cables to Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai Congratulating the Fifth Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and North Korea
Author:
Date: 1954-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. October 6th marked the 5th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China. Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Tu-bong, Chairman, and Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs, sent cables on the 5th to Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman, and Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs, respectively, to express their congratulations.

### 金日成、金枓奉、南日分别致电毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来  祝贺中朝两国建交五周年

1954-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成、金枓奉、南日分别致电毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来
    祝贺中朝两国建交五周年
    【新华社七日讯】十月六日为朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和中华人民共和国建交五周年，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国金日成首相、金枓奉委员长、南日外务相在五日曾分别致电我中华人民共和国毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来外长表示祝贺。
