Title: All-China Women's Federation Holds Tea Party to Welcome Mrs. Indira Gandhi
Author:
Date: 1954-10-21
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Mrs. Indira Gandhi, daughter of Prime Minister Nehru, who is accompanying Prime Minister Nehru on his visit to our country, attended a welcome tea party held by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation on the afternoon of the 20th.

    The hosts attending the tea party included Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Standing Committee members and executive members Shen Zijiou, Kang Keqing, Lei Jieqiong, Luo Qiong, Bai Lang, Liao Mengxing, Liu Qingyang, and others, more than twenty people in total.

    The guests and hosts had a cordial conversation on issues related to women and children in China and India. The tea party proceeded in a pleasant and friendly atmosphere from beginning to end.

### 全国妇联举行茶会欢迎英迪拉·甘地夫人

1954-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇联举行茶会欢迎英迪拉·甘地夫人
    【新华社二十日讯】随同尼赫鲁总理前来我国访问的尼赫鲁总理的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人，在二十日下午应邀参加了中华全国民主妇女联合会的欢迎茶会。
    主人方面参加茶会的，有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴，常务委员及执行委员沈兹九、康克清、雷洁琼、罗琼、白朗、廖梦醒、刘清扬等二十多人。
    宾主双方就中印两国有关妇女、儿童方面的问题进行了亲切的交谈，茶会自始至终在愉快友好的空气中进行。
