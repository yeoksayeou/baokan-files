Title: Communist Party of the Soviet Union Central Committee's Slogans for the 37th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution
Author:
Date: 1954-10-25
Page: 1

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
Slogans for the 37th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution

	I. Long live the thirty-seventh anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution!
	II. Long live the international solidarity of the working people of the world!
	III. A fraternal salute to all peoples fighting for peace, democracy, and socialism!
	IV. Working people of all countries! Peace can be maintained and consolidated if the people of all countries take up the cause of defending peace and persist in it to the end! Consolidate the unity of the people of all countries in the struggle for peace! Long live lasting peace among the people of all countries!
	V. Long live the powerful world peace movement! Working people of all countries, actively fight against the threat of a new world war and for the peaceful cooperation of all countries!
	VI. Working people of all countries! Fight for the prohibition of atomic weapons, hydrogen weapons, and other weapons of mass destruction, and for universal disarmament!
	VII. May the unity of the people of all countries in the struggle against the revival of German militarism and against the establishment of aggressive military blocs be strengthened day by day! Strive for lasting peace and the collective security of the people of all European countries!
	VIII. A fraternal salute to the working people of all People's Democratic countries who are victoriously fighting for the further development of the national economy, for the continuous improvement of the people's welfare, and for the building of a socialist society! Long live the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the People's Democratic countries and the Soviet Union! And may this friendship and cooperation be strengthened day by day!
	IX. A fraternal salute to the great Chinese people who are victoriously fighting for the development of the national economy and culture, for the industrialization of the country, and for the construction of the foundations of socialism! Long live the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the people of the Soviet Union and China, a powerful factor for world peace! May this friendship and cooperation flourish and develop!
	X. A fraternal salute to the heroic people of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea who are fighting for the restoration of the national economy, for peace, and for the national reunification of Korea on a democratic basis!
	XI. A fraternal salute to the heroic people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam who are fighting for peace and democracy and for the restoration of their motherland's national economy!
	XII. A salute to the peace forces of Germany who are fighting against the criminal plan to turn West Germany into a breeding ground for the Third World War! Long live the German Democratic Republic, a reliable bulwark in the struggle for a unified, independent, democratic, and peaceful Germany! In the interests of European peace, may the unbreakable friendship between the German and Soviet people develop and strengthen!
	XIII. In the interests of world peace, may the friendship and cooperation between the people of the Soviet Union and India develop and strengthen!
	XIV. A fraternal salute to the people of colonies and dependent countries who are fighting against imperialist oppression and for their freedom and national independence!
	XV. Long live the friendship of the people of Britain, the United States, and the Soviet Union in the struggle for further easing international tensions, stopping war, and ensuring lasting world peace!
	XVI. May the friendship and cooperation between the Soviet people and the people of France and Italy in the struggle for peace, against the revival of German militarism, and for the establishment of European collective security be strengthened day by day!
	XVII. A salute to the Japanese people who are bravely struggling for national independence, the democratic development of their motherland, against the revival of Japanese militarism, and against turning Japan into an imperialist military springboard in the Far East!
	XVIII. Long live the Soviet Union's peaceful foreign policy—a policy of steadfastly safeguarding and consolidating peace, a policy of fighting against the preparation and launching of a new war, and a policy of international cooperation and developing affairs with all countries!
	XIX. Soldiers and sailors of the Soviet Army and Navy! Persistently improve your military and political knowledge, enhance your combat skills, and master new technologies and weapons! Long live the victorious Soviet armed forces, defending peace and the security of our motherland! May they grow stronger!
	XX. Working people of the Soviet Union! Let us unite more closely around the Communist Party and the Soviet government, mobilize our strength and creativity to engage in the great cause of building a communist society in our country! Long live the unbreakable unity of the Party, the government, and the people!
	XXI. Long live the unbreakable alliance of the working class and the collective farmers, the unshakable foundation of the Soviet system!
	XXII. Long live the fraternal friendship of all nationalities of our country, the source of the strength and power of the multinational socialist country!
	XXIII. Working people of the Soviet Union! Strive to realize the Party and government's policy aimed at further developing the Soviet economy, increasing the material and cultural welfare of the people, and strengthening the power and security of our motherland!
	XXIV. Working people of the Soviet Union! Strive for new achievements in the socialist competition to complete the Fifth Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule! Launch a nationwide movement to strive for high labor productivity—the foundation for further developing the national economy and increasing the material welfare of the Soviet people!
	XXV. Workers, engineers, technicians, and foremen, both men and women! Strive to fulfill the production plans for all kinds of products! Discover and utilize new production potential! Apply the achievements of science and technology and advanced experience to production more extensively! Improve product quality and reduce production costs!
	XXVI. Workers, engineers, and technicians, both men and women, in the coal mining industry! Strive for each mine to complete its production plan! Adopt the cyclical chart method of work in production organization! Build new mines and open-pit mines faster and strive to reach the established production capacity! Produce more coal for the national economy!
	XXVII. Workers, engineers, and technicians, both men and women, in the petroleum industry! Strive to increase the extraction and refining of oil, and adopt rapid drilling methods more extensively! Accelerate the development of new oil fields and the construction of new refineries! Produce more high-quality oil and petroleum products!
	XXVIII. Soviet metallurgical workers! Better utilize the production capacity of metallurgical and mining enterprises and improve production technology! Strive for each enterprise to complete its production plans! We must supply the country with more milled iron, steel, steel材 (steel products), and non-ferrous metals!
	XXIX. Workers, engineers, and technicians, both men and women, in the electrical and radio industries! Do your best to increase the production of high-quality equipment, instruments, and materials! Produce more cultural and daily-use products for our residents!
	XXX. Workers at power stations! Put new power equipment into operation more quickly and adopt advanced technology more extensively!
	XXXI. Workers, engineers, and technicians, both men and women, in the machine-building industry! Expand the production of new types of machines, instruments, and equipment, and improve their quality! Strive to increase the output of tractors, automobiles, combine harvesters, agricultural machinery, and spare parts!
	XXXII. Workers in the chemical industry! Do your best to increase the production of mineral fertilizers, high-quality dyes, and other chemical products for the national economy!
	XXXIII. Workers, engineers, and technicians, both men and women, in the construction industry! Build new enterprises, state-owned farms, machine tractor stations, houses, schools, hospitals, children's institutions, and cultural institutions more quickly! Reduce construction costs and improve construction quality!
	XXXIV. Workers, engineers, and technicians, both men and women, in the building materials industry! Increase the production of cement, reinforced concrete structures, components, various products, and materials! Produce more high-quality building materials for our motherland's construction sites!
	XXXV. Workers, engineers, and technicians, both men and women, in the timber, paper, and woodworking industries! Increase logging and paper production! Improve labor productivity and make full use of machinery! We must produce more high-quality timber, paper, and furniture for the country!
	XXXVI. Workers in the daily necessities industry! Produce more beautiful and durable textiles, beautiful clothes, sturdy and elegant boots and shoes, and other high-quality commodities! Expand the variety of products and save raw materials! We must ensure the rapid development of daily necessities production!
	XXXVII. Workers in the food and meat and dairy industries! Do your best to expand food production and improve product quality! Produce more sugar, oils, meat products, dairy products, and other foods for our residents!
	XXXVIII. Fishery workers! Increase the catch of fish, develop fish farming, and improve product quality! Better utilize fishing boats and fishing gear!
	XXXIX. Agricultural workers! Strive to greatly improve all sectors of socialist agriculture! Produce more grain, milk, meat, wool, cotton, flax, beets, potatoes, vegetables, and other agricultural products for the country!
	XL. Collective farm members, both men and women! Improve labor productivity and strengthen labor discipline! Study and promote scientific achievements and advanced experience! Ensure the further strengthening of the collective economy of collective farms, increase the income of collective farms, and improve the welfare of collective farm members!
	XLI. Agricultural workers! Strive to obtain bumper harvests of all crops! Wage an unremitting struggle to develop and strengthen the grain economy! Launch a socialist competition to continue to reclaim virgin and fallow land and strive for a bumper harvest in 1955! Produce hundreds of millions of poods of grain for the country!
	XLII. Collective farm members, workers of state-owned farms and machine tractor stations! Do your best to develop publicly owned animal husbandry, increase the number of livestock, and increase the productivity of livestock products! Ensure that livestock overwinter well! Produce more livestock products for the country!
	XLIII. Collective farm members, both men and women, workers of machine tractor stations and state-owned farms! Expand the production of industrial raw material crops, vegetables, and potatoes! Strive to increase the yield per unit area of these crops, implement mechanization in production, and promote advanced agricultural technology!
	XLIV. Workers of machine tractor stations! Strive to increase the yield per unit area of various crops, increase the number of publicly owned livestock, and increase the productivity of livestock products! Ensure the timely repair of tractors, combine harvesters, and other machinery!
	XLV. Workers of state-owned farms! Increase the yield per unit area of land and livestock product productivity! Improve production organization and the use of machinery, and strive to reduce product costs! Make all state-owned farms model farms with high commodity output!
	XLVI. Workers in local industries and handicraft cooperatives! Increase the production of daily necessities, household appliances, and building materials! Improve product quality and reduce product costs, and make better use of local raw materials! Expand enterprises that serve the daily needs of residents and improve the work of these enterprises!
	XLVII. Workers in state-owned trade and cooperative trade! Do your best to develop Soviet urban and rural trade and do your best to meet the needs of the working people for necessities! Improve the level of Soviet trade work! Improve the organization of procurement and acquisition of agricultural products and raw materials!
	XLVIII. Railway transport workers! Strive to increase freight volume and better serve passengers! Make full use of the transport capacity of vehicles, increase train speed, and increase the operating rate of vehicles and locomotives! Ensure that railway work operates on time and without interruption under winter conditions!
	XLIX. Maritime and inland waterway transport workers! Strive to fulfill the freight transport plan! More boldly promote and utilize advanced methods of ships and ports! Ensure that the fleet is prepared in an exemplary manner to welcome the 1955 shipping season!
	L. Motor transport and highway workers! Increase the volume of motor transport and improve passenger transport work! Make better use of motor transport and maintain highways in an exemplary manner! Expand the construction of new highways!
	LI. Postal and telecommunications workers! Constantly develop and improve communication tools! Strive to do a good job in postal, telegraph, telephone, and radio work! Serve residents better!
	LII. Workers of Soviet agencies! Improve the work of state organs and eradicate bureaucracy and procrastination! Strive to strengthen state discipline and strictly abide by socialist laws! Care about the demands and needs of the working people! Reduce the expenses of state organs and strengthen their ties with the broad masses!
	LIII. Workers of scientific research institutions and institutions of higher education! Advance Soviet science and technology! More boldly unfold criticism of shortcomings in scientific work! Enhance the role of Soviet science in developing the national economy and ensuring our country's technological progress! Improve the work of training specialists!
	LIV. Literary and art workers! Further develop Soviet literature and art! Improve the ideological and artistic level of your creations! Create works worthy of our great people!
	LV. National education workers! Improve the quality of school teaching work! Educate children to love and be loyal to the Soviet motherland, and cultivate in them a spirit of friendship with the people of all countries! Cultivate socialist society's cultured, educated, and labor-loving citizens, active communist builders!
	LVI. Medical workers! Improve and develop people's health care and improve the quality of work in medical institutions and sanatoriums! Apply medical achievements to practice!
	LVII. Soviet trade unions! More widely unfold socialist competition, strive to improve labor productivity, and strive to fulfill and overfulfill national economic plans! Promote the experience of production innovators! Tirelessly care about further improving the material welfare and cultural level of workers and staff! Long live the school of communism—the Soviet trade unions!
	LVIII. Soviet women! Strive for new achievements in all sectors of the national economy, science, and culture, and in the noble cause of educating children for the welfare and happiness of the Soviet people! Long live the active builders of communism—Soviet women!
	LIX. Long live the Young Communist League, the vanguard of young communist builders, the organizer of Soviet youth, and the active assistant and reserve force of the Communist Party!
	LX. Young men and women, our glorious Soviet youth! Participate more actively in economic and cultural construction and participate in various social and political activities of the country! Persistently study the achievements of advanced science and technology and master production knowledge of industry and agriculture! Firmly and bravely fight for the victory of the great cause of communism in our country!
	LXI. Young Pioneers and students! Master knowledge tenaciously and assiduously! Love labor, observe discipline, and strive to achieve success in learning!
	LXII. Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members! Stand at the forefront of the battle, strive for the further development of our socialist industry, the rapid upsurge of agriculture, the continuous improvement of the welfare of the Soviet people, and the building of communism in the Soviet Union!
	LXIII. Long live the great Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, the glorious fortress of friendship of all nationalities of our country and the indestructible fortress of world peace!
	LXIV. Long live the great Soviet people, the builders of communism!
	LXV: Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the great inspiring and guiding force of the Soviet people in their struggle to build communism!
	LXVI. Under the banner of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, and under the leadership of the Communist Party, advance towards the victory of communism!

### 苏联共产党中央委员会  庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命卅七周年的口号

1954-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党中央委员会
    庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命卅七周年的口号
    一、伟大的十月社会主义革命三十七周年万岁！
    二、全世界劳动人民的国际团结万岁！
    三、向一切为和平、民主和社会主义而斗争的人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    四、各国的劳动人民！如果各国人民将维护和平的事业担当起来，并且把这一事业坚持到底，和平就能够保持和巩固！巩固各国人民在争取和平的斗争中的团结！各国人民之间的持久和平万岁！
    五、强大的世界和平运动万岁！各国的劳动人民，为反对新世界大战的威胁，争取各国人民的和平合作而积极奋斗！
    六、各国的劳动人民！为禁止原子武器、氢武器和其他大规模毁灭性武器，为普遍裁减军备而奋斗！
    七、祝各国人民在反对复活德国军国主义，反对建立侵略性的军事集团的斗争中的团结日益巩固！争取持久和平和欧洲各国人民的集体安全！
    八、向正在为进一步发展国民经济、为不断提高人民的福利、为建成社会主义社会而胜利地进行斗争的各人民民主国家的劳动人民致兄弟的敬礼！各人民民主国家和苏联的牢不可破的友谊和合作万岁！并祝这种友谊和合作日益巩固！
    九、向正在为发展国民经济和文化，为国家的工业化，为建设社会主义基础而胜利地进行斗争的伟大的中国人民致兄弟的敬礼！世界和平的强大因素——苏中两国人民的牢不可破的友谊和合作万岁！祝这种友谊和合作繁荣发展！
    十、向正在为恢复国民经济，为和平，为在民主的基础上求得朝鲜的民族统一而奋斗的英雄的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    十一、向正在为和平与民主，为恢复祖国的国民经济而奋斗的英雄的越南民主共和国人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    十二、向正在为反对变西德为第三次世界大战的温床的罪恶计划而奋斗的德国和平力量致敬！争取建立统一、独立、民主和和平的德国的斗争的可靠堡垒——德意志民主共和国万岁！为了欧洲和平的利益，祝德国和苏联两国人民的牢不可破的友谊日益发展和巩固！
    十三、为了世界和平的利益，祝苏联和印度两国人民的友谊和合作日益发展和巩固！
    十四、向正在为反对帝国主义压迫，为争取自己的自由和民族独立而奋斗的殖民地和附属国的人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    十五、英、美、苏三国人民在为进一步缓和国际紧张局势、制止战争和保障世界持久和平的斗争中的友谊万岁！
    十六、祝苏联人民同法、意两国人民在争取和平、反对复活德国军国主义、争取建立欧洲集体安全的斗争中的友谊和合作日益巩固！
    十七、向正在为民族独立，为自己祖国的民主发展，反对复活日本军国主义，反对变日本为帝国主义者在远东的军事跳板而英勇斗争的日本人民致敬！
    十八、苏联的和平外交政策——坚定不移地维护和巩固和平的政策，为反对准备和发动新战争而斗争的政策，国际合作和同一切国家发展事务联系的政策万岁！
    十九、苏联陆海军军人们！坚持不渝地提高自己的军事和政治知识、增强自己的作战技能、掌握新的技术和武器！享有胜利光荣的、捍卫着和平和我们祖国的安全的苏联武装部队万岁！祝它们更加壮大！
    二十、苏联的劳动人民！我们要更加紧密地团结在共产党和苏维埃政府的周围、动员我们的力量和创造力来从事在我国建设共产主义社会的伟大事业！党、政府和人民的牢不可破的团结万岁！
    二十一、苏维埃制度的不可动摇的基础——工人阶级和集体农民的牢不可破的联盟万岁！
    二十二、多民族的社会主义国家的力量和威力的泉源——我国各族人民的兄弟友谊万岁！
    二十三、苏联的劳动人民！为实现党和政府旨在进一步发展苏联经济、增进人民的物质和文化福利及加强我们祖国的威力和安全的政策而奋斗！
    二十四、苏联的劳动人民！在争取提前完成第五个五年计划的社会主义竞赛中努力取得新的成就！展开全民运动以争取高度劳动生产率——进一步发展国民经济和增进苏联人民物质福利的基础！
    二十五、男女工人、工程师和技术员、领班们！为完成各种产品的生产计划而努力！发掘和利用新的生产潜力！更广泛地把科学、技术的成就和先进经验应用到生产中去！提高产品质量，降低生产成本！
    二十六、采煤工业的男女工人、工程师和技术员们！争取每个矿井都完成生产计划！采用生产组织中的循环图表的工作法！更快地建设新矿井和露天矿场、争取达到预定的生产能力！为国民经济生产更多的煤！
    二十七、石油工业的男女工人、工程师和技术员们！为增加石油的开采量和提炼量而努力，更广泛地采用快速钻井法！加速开发新油田和建造新的炼油厂！生产更多的高质量的石油和石油产品！
    二十八、苏联的冶金工作者们！更好地利用冶金和采矿企业的生产能力，改进生产技术！争取每个企业都完成各项生产计划！我们要供给国家更多的铣铁、钢、钢材和有色金属！
    二十九、电气工业和无线电工业的男女工人、工程师和技术员们！尽力增加高质量的设备、仪器和器材的生产！为我国居民出产更多的文化生活用品！
    三十、电力站的工作者们！更迅速地使新的电力设备发电，更广泛地采用先进技术！
    三十一、机器制造业的男女工人、工程师和技术员们！扩大新式机器、仪器和设备的生产，提高它们的质量！努力增加拖拉机、汽车、收割联合机、农业机器和备件的产量！
    三十二、化学工业的工作者们！为国民经济尽力增加矿质肥料、高质量的染料以及其他化学产品的生产！
    三十三、建筑业的男女工人、工程师和技术员们！更迅速地建筑新企业、国营农场、机器拖拉机站、住宅、学校、医院、儿童机构和文化机构！降低建筑成本，提高建筑质量！
    三十四、建筑材料工业的男女工人、工程师和技术员们！增加水泥、钢筋混凝土结构、构件、各种制品和材料的生产！为我们祖国的建筑工地生产更多的高质量的建筑材料！
    三十五、木材、造纸和木材加工工业的男女工人、工程师和技术员们！增加伐木和造纸量！提高劳动生产率，充分利用机械！我们要为国家生产更多的高质量的木材、纸张和家具！
    三十六、日用品工业的工作者们！生产更多美观耐用的纺织品，漂亮的衣服，结实雅致的靴鞋及其他高质量的商品！扩大产品的种类，节省原料！我们要保证日用品生产的急速发展！
    三十七、食品、肉乳工业的工作者们！尽力扩大食品生产，改进产品质量！为我国居民生产更多的糖、油脂、肉制品、乳制品和其他食品！
    三十八、渔业工作者们！增加捕鱼量，发展养鱼业，提高产品质量！更好地利用渔船和捕鱼工具！
    三十九、农业工作者们！为大力提高社会主义农业的一切部门而奋斗！为国家生产更多的谷物、乳类、肉类、羊毛、棉花、亚麻、甜菜、马铃薯、蔬菜和其他农产品！
    四十、集体农庄的男女庄员们！提高劳动生产率并加强劳动纪律！研究和推广科学成就和先进经验！保证进一步加强集体农庄的公有经济，增加集体农庄的收入和提高集体农庄庄员的福利！
    四十一、农业工作者们！争取一切作物获得丰收！为发展和加强谷物经济而不倦地进行斗争！为继续开垦生荒地和熟荒地、为争取一九五五年获得丰收而展开社会主义竞赛！为国家多生产成亿普特的谷物！
    四十二、集体农庄庄员们，国营农场和机器拖拉机站的工作者们！尽力发展公有畜牧业，增加牲畜的头数，提高牲畜的产品生产率！保证牲畜很好地过冬！为国家生产更多的畜牧业产品！
    四十三、集体农庄的男女庄员们，机器拖拉机站和国营农场的工作者们！扩大工业原料作物、蔬菜和马铃薯的生产！争取提高这些作物的单位面积产量，在生产中实行机械化和推广先进的农业技术！
