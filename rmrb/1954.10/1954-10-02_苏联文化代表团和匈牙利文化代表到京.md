Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation and Hungarian Cultural Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-02
Page: 3

    Soviet Cultural Delegation and Hungarian Cultural Representatives Arrive in Beijing
    The Soviet Cultural Delegation of eleven members and three staff, invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to attend the National Day celebrations in our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 30th.
    The head of the Soviet Cultural Delegation is Professor A. I. Denisov, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Union of Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Doctor of Law. The members are: K.V. Ostrovityanov, Vice-President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Academician, Doctor of Economics; A.M. Pankratova, Editor-in-Chief of the Soviet journal "Historical Issues," Academician, Doctor of History, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR; I.G. Petrovsky, President of the Moscow State University, Doctor of Physics and Mathematics; A.M. Gerasimov, President of the Soviet Academy of Arts, People's Artist of the USSR, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR; D.A. Kunaev, President of the Academy of Sciences of the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic, Mining Engineer, Academician, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR; M.A. Melnikov, Vice-President of the Academy of Pedagogical Sciences of the Russian Federation, Academician of the Academy of Pedagogical Sciences; M.V. Nesterov, Chairman of the USSR Chamber of Commerce; A.T. Ventzlova, Soviet writer, Stalin Prize laureate, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR; L.E. Kiziya, Chairman of the Council of the Ukrainian Society for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Master of History; and N.G. Erofeev, Director of the Department of Oriental Republics of the Soviet Union of Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Wu Yuzhang, Vice-President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Qian Junrui, Secretary-General; Xin Zichao, Deputy Secretary-General; and many well-known figures from China's scientific, cultural, and artistic circles, such as Zhang Jiafu, Qian Sanqiang, Hua Luogeng, Ji Chaoding, Di Chaobai, Yang Hansheng, Ke Zhongping, and Jiang Feng.
    Vasikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, Romanin, Representative of the Soviet Union of Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, and Borisinko, Director of the Exhibition of Achievements of Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction, also went to the airport to welcome them.
    After disembarking from the plane, the Soviet Cultural Delegation warmly shook hands and embraced the welcomers. (Xinhua News Agency)
    According to the 1954 Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan, the Hungarian writers Tamás Aczél and László Benjamin, museum expert Tibor Horváth, and Imre Patkó, reporter of the Hungarian "Free People's Daily," arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 30th for a visit to our country. Both Tamás Aczél and László Benjamin are recipients of the Hungarian Kossuth Prize; Tamás Aczél won the Stalin Prize for his book "Under the Canopy of Freedom."
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 苏联文化代表团和匈牙利文化代表到京

1954-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联文化代表团和匈牙利文化代表到京
    应中苏友好协会总会邀请前来我国参加国庆节观礼的苏联文化代表团十一人和工作人员三人在三十日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    苏联文化代表团的团长是：苏联对外文化协会理事会主席、法学博士亚·伊·杰尼索夫教授，团员是：苏联科学院副院长、院士、经济学博士、康·华·奥斯特罗维季扬诺夫，苏联“历史问题”杂志总编辑、院士、历史学博士、苏联最高苏维埃代表安·米·潘克拉托娃，苏联国立莫斯科大学校长、数理学博士伊·格·彼得罗夫斯基，苏联艺术研究院院长、苏联人民美术家、苏联最高苏维埃代表阿·米·格拉西莫夫，哈萨克苏维埃社会主义共和国科学院院长、探矿工程师、院士、苏联最高苏维埃代表丁·阿·库纳耶夫，俄罗斯联邦共和国教育科学研究院副院长、教育科学研究院院士米·阿·麦尔尼科夫，苏联商会主席米·华·涅斯捷罗夫，苏联作家、斯大林奖金获得者、苏联最高苏维埃代表安·托·文茨洛瓦，乌克兰对外文化协会理事会主席、历史学硕士卢·叶·基齐亚，苏联对外文化协会东方各人民共和国部主任尼·格·叶罗菲耶夫。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，总干事钱俊瑞，副总干事辛志超等以及我国科学、文化及艺术界等著名人士张稼夫、钱三强、华罗庚、冀朝鼎、狄超白、阳翰笙、柯仲平、江丰等多人。
    苏联大使馆参赞华司考及苏联对外文化协会驻我国代表罗满宁，苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科等也到机场欢迎。
    苏联文化代表团在下机后和欢迎者热烈握手和拥抱。　（新华社）
    根据一九五四年中匈文化合作协定执行计划来我国访问的匈牙利作家塔马希·阿塞尔、拉斯洛·本雅明，博物馆专家蒂波尔·霍瓦特及匈牙利“自由人民报”记者伊姆雷·帕特科四人，在三十日下午乘飞机到达北京。塔马希·阿塞尔及拉斯洛·本雅明都是匈牙利科苏特奖金得奖人；塔马希·阿塞尔曾以“在自由的天幕下”一书获得斯大林奖金。
                                  （新华社）
