Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Chairman Petru Groza

Author:

Date: 1954-10-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, on October 11th and held friendly talks. Present at the reception were Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Chen Yun.

### 毛泽东主席接见彼·格罗查主席

1954-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见彼·格罗查主席
   【新华社十一日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东在十月十一日接见罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查博士，进行了友好的会谈。接见时在座的有刘少奇、周恩来和陈云。
