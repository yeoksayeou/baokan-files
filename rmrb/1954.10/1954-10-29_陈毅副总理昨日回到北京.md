Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Returns to Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1954-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: Vice Premier Chen Yi, head of the government delegation of the People's Republic of China, who was invited to Berlin to participate in the celebrations of the fifth anniversary of the German Democratic Republic, returned to Beijing on the 28th after participating in the fifth anniversary celebrations of the German Democratic Republic and visiting the Polish People's Republic.

    Those who welcomed him at the airport included: Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Director of the General Office of the CPC Central Committee Yang Shangkun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Wang Tuo, and others.

    Also present at the airport to welcome him were: Koenig, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Kiryluk, Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, and staff members of the two embassies.

### 陈毅副总理昨日回到北京

1954-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理昨日回到北京
    【新华社二十八日讯】应邀到柏林参加德意志民主共和国国庆五周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长陈毅副总理，在参加了德意志民主共和国五周年庆祝典礼并访问了波兰人民共和国以后，已在二十八日回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：国务院副总理李先念、秘书长习仲勋，中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，外交部副部长伍修权、苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处副处长王拓等。
    到机场欢迎的还有：德意志民主共和国驻华大使柯尼希、波兰人民共和国驻华大使基里洛克以及两使馆人员。
