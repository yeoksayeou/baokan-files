Title: U.S. Military Officer McGarr Visits Taiwan for Activities
Author:
Date: 1954-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: U.S. Marine Corps Deputy Commander of the Pacific Command, McGarr, arrived in Taiwan on the 19th to conduct conspiratorial activities under the guise of "inspection." According to Reuters, after arriving in Taiwan, McGarr listened to reports from officials of the U.S. Embassy in Taiwan and Chase, the head of the U.S. Military Assistance Advisory Group in Taiwan. McGarr will also hold talks with the head of the Chiang bandit Marine Corps in southern Taiwan and inspect the training of the Chiang bandit Marine Corps.

### 美国军人麦克格里到台湾活动

1954-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    美国军人麦克格里到台湾活动
   【新华社二十日讯】美国太平洋司令部海军陆战队副司令麦克格里十九日到达台湾，以“视察”为名进行阴谋活动。据路透社报道，麦克格里在到达台湾后，就听取了美国驻台湾“大使”馆官员和美国驻台湾军事援助顾问团团长蔡斯的报告。麦克格里还将在台湾南部和蒋贼海军陆战队头目进行会谈，并视察蒋贼海军陆战队的训练情况。
