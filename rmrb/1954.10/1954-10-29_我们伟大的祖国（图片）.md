    Our Great Motherland
    The "large building blocks" trial-produced at the construction site of the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant have been officially used in the construction of factory buildings. This is another major reform in bricklaying, paving the way for mechanized and industrialized construction.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    第一汽车制造厂工地试制的“大型砌块”，已经正式使用到厂房的建筑上了。这是砌墙工程上又一巨大的改革，给机械化、工厂化施工开辟了道路。
