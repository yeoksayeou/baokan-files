Title: Soviet, Romanian, and Vietnamese Government Delegations Attend Peking Opera Reception

Author:

Date: 1954-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Soviet Government Delegation, led by N.S. Khrushchev, and including members N.A. Bulganin, A.I. Mikoyan, N.M. Shvernik and the entire delegation, who were invited to attend our National Day celebrations for the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China; Dr. Petru Groza and some members of the Romanian People's Republic Government Delegation; and Hoang Minh Giam, leader of the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the entire delegation, attended a Peking Opera reception on the evening of the 6th. The program for the evening included "The Drunken Concubine" starring the famous Chinese actor Mei Lanfang; "Havoc in Heaven" starring Li Shaochun; and "Autumn River" starring Ye Shengzhang.

Accompanying them were Chen Yun and Ulanhu, Vice Premiers of the State Council, and Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.

### 苏、罗、越政府代表团出席京剧招待晚

1954-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    苏、罗、越政府代表团出席京剧招待晚会
   【新华社六日讯】应邀前来参加我国建国五周年国庆典礼的苏联政府代表团团长尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫，团员：尼·亚·布尔加宁，阿·伊·米高扬，尼·米·什维尔尼克和代表团全体团员；罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团员彼·格罗查博士和部分团员；越南民主共和国政府代表团团长黄明鉴和代表团全体团员在六日晚出席了京剧招待晚会。晚会节目有我国著名演员梅兰芳主演的“贵妃醉酒”；李少春主演的“闹天宫”；叶盛章主演的“秋江”。
    陪同观剧的，有我国务院副总理陈云、乌兰夫，外交部副部长伍修权。
