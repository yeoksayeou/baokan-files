Title: Four Youth Representatives from India and Other Countries Return Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 10th: Four individuals, including Indian student representative Bukar, Indonesian youth representative Baharuddin, and Japanese youth representatives Sasaki Kazushi and Suzuki Juro, departed Guangzhou on the 10th to return to their respective countries.

### 印度等国青年代表四人回国

1954-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    印度等国青年代表四人回国
    【新华社广州十日电】印度学生代表布卡尔、印度尼西亚青年代表巴哈鲁丁、日本青年代表佐佐木一司、铃木重郎等四人十日离广州回国。
