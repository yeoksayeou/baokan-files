Title: China's Chargé d'Affaires to the UK, Huan Xiang, Arrives in London
Author: 
Date: 1954-10-29
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 28th: London dispatch: Huan Xiang, Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China to the United Kingdom, and his entourage of eight people arrived in London by plane at 12:00 noon on the 27th.

    K.T. Crowe, Head of the Far Eastern Department of the British Foreign Office, greeted them at the airport.

    Also present at the airport to welcome them were Nikolai Belokhvostikov, Chargé d'Affaires of the Soviet Embassy in the United Kingdom, Josef Uher, the Czechoslovak Ambassador to the United Kingdom, and representatives from the Polish, Hungarian, and Bulgarian Embassies in the United Kingdom.

    Representatives of the British-Chinese Friendship Association also welcomed them at the airport.

    Also welcoming them at the airport were staff from the London branch of the Bank of China and many overseas Chinese in the United Kingdom.

### 我国驻英国代办宦乡到达伦敦

1954-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻英国代办宦乡到达伦敦
    【新华社柏林二十八日电】伦敦消息：中华人民共和国驻英国代办宦乡及随行人员等一行八人在二十七日中午十二时乘飞机抵达伦敦。
    英国外交部远东司司长克·特·克劳到机场迎接。
    到机场欢迎的还有苏联驻英国大使馆代办尼古拉·别洛赫沃斯齐罗夫、捷克斯洛伐克驻英国大使约瑟夫·乌里赫及波兰、匈牙利、保加利亚等国驻英国使馆代表。
    英中友好协会代表也在机场欢迎。
    在机场欢迎的还有中国银行伦敦分行人员及在英国的华侨多人。
