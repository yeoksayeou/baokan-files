Title: Malenkov and Molotov Receive British Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-10-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 16th: According to TASS, Moscow, October 16th, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, G.M. Malenkov, and the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, V.M. Molotov, received the British parliamentary delegation visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR on October 16th.

Those received included the head of the delegation, Sir Coleridge, the deputy head of the delegation, Ness Edwards, and all members of the delegation.

The conversation lasted for more than an hour and took place in an unconstrained and friendly atmosphere. They discussed issues related to the international situation and relations between the Soviet Union and the United Kingdom.

Present at the meeting with the British parliamentary delegation was the British Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, William Hayter.

Xinhua News Agency, October 16th: According to TASS, Moscow, the Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, A.P. Volkov, and the Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, V.T. Lacis, held a reception on the 15th to entertain the British parliamentary delegation visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

Invited to the reception were Sir Coleridge, the head of the British parliamentary delegation, Ness Edwards, the deputy head, and members of the delegation, as well as Lord Hayter, the British Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

Also attending the reception were M.Z. Saburov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the USSR, M.P. Tarasov, Deputy Chairman of the Presidum of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federated Socialist Republic, representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, ministers of various ministries of the USSR, responsible personnel of government agencies and people's organizations, production innovators, and representatives from the fields of science, literature, art, and journalism.

### 马林科夫和莫洛托夫接见英国议员代表团

1954-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    马林科夫和莫洛托夫接见英国议员代表团
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十六日讯：苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫和苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫十月十六日接见应苏联最高苏维埃邀请来苏联访问的英国议员代表团。
    被接见的有，代表团团长科勒雷英爵士，代表团副团长纳斯·爱德华兹和代表团全体团员。
    谈话进行了一个多小时，并且是在无拘束和友好的气氛中进行的。他们谈到了有关国际局势与苏联和英国之间的关系问题。
    陪同英国议员代表团在座的有英国驻苏联特命全权大使威廉·海特尔。
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席亚·彼·沃尔科夫和苏联最高苏维埃民族院主席维·特·拉齐斯在十五日举行招待会，招待应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请访问苏联的英国议员代表团。
    应邀出席招待会的，有英国议员代表团团长科勒雷英爵士、副团长纳斯·爱德华兹和代表团团员，还有英国驻苏大使海特尔勋爵。
    出席招待会的，还有苏联部长会议副主席、苏联国家计划委员会主席马·扎·萨布罗夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席、俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席米·彼·塔拉索夫和苏联最高苏维埃代表，苏联各部部长及政府机关和人民团体的负责人员，生产革新者及科学、文学、艺术和新闻界的代表。
