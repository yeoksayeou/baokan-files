Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Accepts "Gandhi's Biography" Presented by Indian Ambassador Raghavan

Author:

Date: 1954-10-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai received Indian Ambassador to China, Raghavan, at 12:00 noon on October 2nd, and accepted two sets of the book "Gandhi's Biography" presented by the Indian Ambassador on behalf of the Indian government to the Chinese government and Premier Zhou Enlai, in commemoration of the 85th anniversary of Gandhi's birth.

Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present at the meeting.

### 周总理接受印度大使赖嘉文赠送的“甘地传”

1954-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接受印度大使赖嘉文赠送的“甘地传”
    【新华社二日讯】我国务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在十月二日中午十二时，接见了印度驻我国大使赖嘉文，并接受作为纪念甘地诞生八十五周年，印度大使代表印度政府赠送中国政府和周总理的书籍“甘地传”各一套。
    接见时在座的，有我外交部亚洲司司长陈家康。
