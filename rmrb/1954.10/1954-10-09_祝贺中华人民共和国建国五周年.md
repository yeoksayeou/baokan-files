Title: Congratulating the 5th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1954-10-09
Page: 4

    Congratulating the 5th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
    Telegram of Greetings from Palmiro Togliatti, General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, to Chairman Mao
    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:
    On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the founding of your country, I extend my warmest congratulations to you personally, to the Communist Party of China, and to all the people of the great People's Republic.
    All true democrats in Italy are watching you and the new China with sincere admiration and deep faith, because in the great Asian continent and throughout the world, you are a new and indestructible bastion of civilization, progress, and peace.
    We will strive to restore genuine cooperative relations in all aspects between the new China and our country, to ensure that China's national rights are respected by all, to defeat the pitiful conspiracies of imperialism against China and all Asian peoples, and to achieve victory for the cause of peace and independence for all nations.
                                         Palmiro Togliatti
                                    Rome, October 1, 1954
    Telegram of Greetings from the Central Committee of the Norwegian Communist Party to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    The Central Committee of the Norwegian Communist Party sends a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, congratulating the People's Republic of China on its National Day. The telegram states: On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the People's Republic of China, the Norwegian Communist Party extends its congratulations to you. The historic victory of the Chinese people over foreign oppressors and the fascist terrorist regime of Chiang Kai-shek is a giant leap forward for mankind on the road to liberation. The Chinese people will achieve new and great progress in their future struggles and in building socialism.

### 祝贺中华人民共和国建国五周年

1954-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺中华人民共和国建国五周年
    意大利共产党总书记陶里亚蒂给毛主席的贺电中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志：
    值此你们的国家成立五周年纪念日之际，我谨向您个人，中国共产党和伟大的人民共和国的全体人民致最热烈的祝贺。
    意大利全体真正的民主主义者怀着真诚的钦佩和深挚的信心注视着您和新中国，因为在伟大的亚洲大陆以及全世界，你们是文明、进步和和平的新的不可摧毁的堡垒。
    我们将争取使新中国和我们的国家间恢复各方面的真诚的合作关系，使中国的民族权利受到一切人的尊重，使帝国主义反对中国和全亚洲各族人民的可怜的阴谋遭到失败，使各国人民和平和独立的事业获得胜利。
                                         帕尔米罗·陶里亚蒂
                                    一九五四年十月一日于罗马
    挪威共产党中央给中国共产党中央的贺电
    挪威共产党中央委员会致电中国共产党中央委员会，祝贺中华人民共和国国庆。电文说：值此中华人民共和国五周年纪念日之际，挪威共产党向你们祝贺。中国人民战胜外国压迫者及蒋介石法西斯恐怖政权的历史性的胜利，是人类在走向解放的道路上的一个巨大跨步。中国人民在他们将来的斗争中，在建设社会主义方面将获得新的巨大的进展。
