Title: The National Defense Council Holds Its First Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 1

     The National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China held its first meeting on the 18th. At the meeting, Chairman Mao Zedong gave important instructions, and Vice Chairman Peng Dehuai delivered a report.

### 国防委员会举行第一次会议

1954-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

     国防委员会举行第一次会议
    【新华社十八日讯】中华人民共和国国防委员会在十八日举行了第一次会议。会上，毛泽东主席作了重要指示，彭德怀副主席作了报告。
