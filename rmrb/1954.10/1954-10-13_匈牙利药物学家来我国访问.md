Title: Hungarian Pharmacologist Visits China
Author:
Date: 1954-10-13
Page: 4

    Hungarian Pharmacologist Visits China
      [Xinhua News Agency] According to the 1954 implementation plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Hungarian pharmacologist Paul Brown, who is visiting China, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 8th.

### 匈牙利药物学家来我国访问

1954-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利药物学家来我国访问
      【新华社讯】根据中匈文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国访问的匈牙利药物学家保罗·布朗已于八日下午到达北京。
