Title: Mongolian Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 17th: The Mongolian People's Republic Government Trade Delegation, led by its head, the Minister of Trade of the Mongolian People's Republic, Batar, arrived in Beijing on the 17th (one member of the delegation was already in Beijing).
    Xu Xuehan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, went to the airport to welcome them.
    The Mongolian Ambassador to China, Ochirbat, and Counselor Lobsang Chultem, also went to the airport to welcome them.
    List of the Mongolian Government Trade Delegation:
    Head: Batar
    Deputy Head: Gobozhab
    Members: Ichinkhorloo
          Ayush

### 蒙古政府贸易代表团到达北京

1954-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古政府贸易代表团到达北京
    【新华社十七日讯】蒙古人民共和国政府贸易代表团由团长、蒙古人民共和国贸易部部长巴塔尔率领，在十七日到达北京（代表团有一人已在北京）。
    中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒前往机场欢迎。
    蒙古驻华大使奥其尔巴特，参赞罗布桑楚勒图姆等也前往机场欢迎。
    蒙古政府贸易代表团名单：
    团长：巴塔尔
    副团长：戈博扎布
    团员：伊钦贺洛
          阿尤什
