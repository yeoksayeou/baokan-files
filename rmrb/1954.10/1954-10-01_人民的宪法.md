Title: The People's Constitution
Author: Huang Yanpei
Date: 1954-10-01
Page: 5

    The People's Constitution
    Huang Yanpei
    (I)
    The faint autumn sun,
    Five-star flags fluttering all over the city,
    How magnificent!
    How solemn! Zhongnanhai,
    Huai Ren Tang,
    Radiating bright light, the National People's Congress opened,
    A joyful gathering, most beloved are the brothers and sisters of the ethnic minorities,
    Most respected are the labor models, most revered are the combat heroes,
    Especially the elders of the families of the martyrs. Different units,
    Different languages, different costumes,
    One thing is the same, everyone united, demanding—
    Unity, peace, democracy, unification, and prosperity.
    Everyone was about to see the longevity appearance of the 94-year-old Qi Baishi,
    Suddenly,
    The whole hall stood up together, in a storm of applause,
    The great people's teacher, Chairman Mao Zedong, entered.
    (II)
    We will no longer call it a charter,
    The Constitution of the People's Republic of China is put to the vote.
    The representatives of the National People's Congress sit solemnly in the hall,
    The chairman solemnly announced,
    Let's adopt a secret ballot method!
    Secret ballot,
    Good, that's the highest form.
    As a result,
    The chairman announced: Of the one thousand one hundred and ninety-seven people who voted,
    One thousand one hundred and ninety-seven votes agreed,
    Not one more,
    Not one less,
    Instantly, long live! Long live! Cheers like a raging tide,
    Especially loud applause from the foreign guest gallery.
    Gorky once said:
    Even the stones are singing and laughing.
   (III)
    The worker-peasant alliance,
    Is the foundation of the People's Republic of China.
    Economic construction,
    Is the main task of the people of the whole country at present.
    Walk slowly,
    Who will wait for you;
    Run fast!
    If you fall, what then?
    There is a ladder here,
    One ladder every five steps,
    Step by step,
    Ladder by ladder,
    There will naturally be many problems,
    It is easy to humbly and carefully seek solutions.
    There will be difficulties,
    Overcoming them is also possible.
    Advance! Advance!
    To that time,
    Will it be mature?
    Look up,
    How beautiful!
    Socialism.
  (IV)
    This is a design drawing for moving towards socialism.
    This is a guarantee for world peace.
    The full text has one hundred and six articles,
    Eight thousand nine hundred and fifty-four words,
    Also collected more than one million one hundred and eighteen thousand opinions from the whole country,
    The heart is so meticulous! And so humble!
    Every article, every word,
    Has been repeatedly deliberated,
    And carefully considered.
    Who knows:
    This most beautiful Constitution of the People's Republic of China,
    Was originally drafted,
    In the most beautiful West Lake of the people of Hangzhou!
    (V)
    The quiet square, allowing the majestic Tiananmen to stand tall,
    Morning and evening,
    Thousands upon thousands of pedestrians,
    Come and go endlessly,
    Looking south,
    What does that symbolize?
    The tall monument, with Chairman Mao's own handwriting.
    Let's read it once!
    Unconsciously,
    Attention!
    Silence!
    And how many people are moved to tears!
    Everyone knows:
    The pen and ink that wrote this Constitution of the People's Republic of China,
    Are the white bones and red blood of countless people's heroes.

### 人民的宪法
黄炎培
1954-10-01
第5版()
专栏：

    人民的宪法
    黄炎培
    （一）
    淡淡的秋阳，
    满城五星旗在飘扬，
    多么雄丽！
    多么庄严！中南海，
    怀仁堂，
    放射着电光雪亮，全国人民代表大会开幕了，
    一堂欢聚，最亲爱是兄弟民族，
    最敬重是劳动模范，最尊崇是战斗英雄，
    尤其是烈士家庭的亲长。不同的单位，
    不同的语言，不同的服装，
    一点相同的，万众一心，要求——
    团结、和平、民主、统一和富强。
    大家正要见一下九十四高龄齐白石老人的寿者相，
    忽然，
    全堂一齐起立着，暴风雨般掌声中，
    伟大的人民导师毛泽东主席上场。
    （二）
    我们将不再称它做宪章，
    中华人民共和国宪法付表决了。
    满堂整肃地排坐着全国人民代表大会的代表，
    主席严肃地宣布，
    我们采用无记名投票方式吧！
    无记名，
    好，那是最高级形式了。
    结果，
    主席宣布：投票的一千一百九十七个人，
    同意的一千一百九十七张票，
    一张也不多，
    一张也不少，
    一时万岁！万岁！欢呼声像怒潮，
    特别外宾旁听席上掌声大闹。
    高尔基曾经说过：
    虽是石头也在唱歌，也在欢笑。
   （三）
    工农联盟，
    是中华人民共和国立国的始基。
    经济建设，
    是全国人民当前主要的课题。
    慢慢地走，
    谁来等你；
    飞奔吧！
    摔一交，怎么呢？
    有梯子在这里，
    五步一梯，
    一步又一步，
    一梯又一梯，
    问题自然多，
    虚心地、小心地去求解决也是容易。
    困难会有的，
    克服也可以。
    进！进！
    到那个时期，
    成熟了么？
    抬头看，
    多么美丽！
    社会主义。
  （四）
    这是一幅走向社会主义的设计图。
    这是一张导致世界和平的保证书。
    全文一百零六条，
    八千九百五十四个字，
    还收集了全国一百一十八万多条的意见，
    心那么细！又那么虚！
    每一条，每一字，
    都经过反复地推敲，
    周详地考虑。
    谁知道：
    这最美丽的中华人民的宪法，
    当初起草，
    是在最美丽的杭州人民的西湖！
    （五）
    静荡荡的广场，让多么雄壮的天安门耸立，
    朝朝暮暮，
    千千万万行人，
    往来不绝，
    向南一望，
    那象征什么呢？
    高高的纪念碑，有毛主席亲题的笔迹。
    大家读一遍吧！
    不知不觉地，
    立正！
    静默！
    还使多少人们感泣！
    谁都知道：
    写成这一部中华人民共和国宪法的笔和墨，
    是无数人民英雄们的白骨和赤血。
