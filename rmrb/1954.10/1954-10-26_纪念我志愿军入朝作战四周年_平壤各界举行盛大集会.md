Title: Pyongyang Circles Hold Grand Meeting to Commemorate the Fourth Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's Entry into Korea to Fight
Author:
Date: 1954-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 25th: Pyongyang circles held a grand meeting at the Moranbong Theater on the evening of the 24th to solemnly commemorate the fourth anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's entry into Korea to fight.

Those attending the meeting included: Park Jung-ae, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Choi Yong-kun, Vice Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Minister of National Defense; Vice Premiers Park Chang-ok, Kim Il, Hong Myung-hee, and other party and government leaders; senior generals and combat heroes of the Korean People's Army; heads of democratic parties and social organizations; and approximately one thousand representatives of the working people of Pyongyang.

Invited to attend were: a 150-member delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army headed by General Wang Ping.

Also invited to attend the meeting were: Li Shi, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries to North Korea, and foreigners in Pyongyang.

At the start of the meeting, the orchestras played the national anthems of Korea and China.

General Choi Jong-hak, Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, delivered a report at the meeting. He first expressed gratitude and congratulations on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army to all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, the entire Chinese people, the Chinese Communist Party, the Government of the People's Republic of China, and Chairman Mao Zedong. He pointed out: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army, holding high the righteous banner of resisting America and aiding Korea, fought side by side with the Korean People's Army on the Korean battlefield and achieved a historic victory. After the armistice, the heroic Chinese People's Volunteer Army actively helped the Korean people in their reconstruction efforts, giving the Korean people great encouragement. He said: The entire Korean people will further consolidate the friendship and unity with the people of the great Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and the various people's democratic countries. The Korean People's Army will further strengthen its combat friendship with the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, overcome all difficulties, and advance victoriously to assist in the completion of the post-war reconstruction plan and strive for the peaceful reunification of Korea.

Then, Park Kwang-hee, Vice Chairman of the Pyongyang Municipal People's Committee, presented a silk banner to all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on behalf of all the working people of Pyongyang.

General Wang Ping, Chief Representative of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation, spoke after receiving the banner. He extended high respect on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to the heroic Korean military and civilians and expressed heartfelt thanks to the Korean Workers' Party, the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Marshal Kim Il-sung. He pointed out: Our common struggle has shown a truth: The people who rise up to defend their motherland and defend peace are invincible, this was the case in the past, and it will be even more so in the future. He also said: All commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army will continue to fight together with the Korean people for the victory of our common cause. We will never let down the entrustment of the people of our motherland and the ardent expectations of the Korean people.

The meeting proceeded in an atmosphere full of internationalist close unity. After the meeting, the Korean National Art Theater performed wonderful programs for the conference.

### 纪念我志愿军入朝作战四周年  平壤各界举行盛大集会

1954-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念我志愿军入朝作战四周年
    平壤各界举行盛大集会
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】平壤各界二十四日晚在牡丹峰剧场举行盛大集会，隆重纪念中国人民志愿军入朝作战四周年。
    参加大会的有：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相兼民族保卫相崔庸健，副首相朴昌玉、金一、洪命熹等党和政府的领导人，朝鲜人民军高级将领、战斗英雄，各民主政党、社会团体负责人以及平壤市劳动人民代表约一千人。
    应邀出席的有：以王平将军为首的中国人民志愿军代表团一百五十人。
    应邀出席大会的还有：中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办李石和各国驻朝外交使节和使馆人员以及在平壤的外国人士等。
    大会开始时，乐队高奏朝中两国国歌。
    朝鲜人民军总政治局局长崔宗学中将在会上做了报告。他首先代表朝鲜人民军全体将士向中国人民志愿军全体指挥员和战斗员、全中国人民、中国共产党、中华人民共和国政府和毛泽东主席表示感谢和祝贺。他指出：中国人民志愿军高举抗美援朝的正义旗帜，在朝鲜战场上和朝鲜人民军并肩作战，取得了历史性的胜利。停战以后，英雄的中国人民志愿军又积极帮助朝鲜人民进行着恢复建设事业，给了朝鲜人民极大的鼓舞。他说：全朝鲜人民将进一步巩固和伟大的苏联、中华人民共和国及各人民民主国家人民的友谊团结。朝鲜人民军将进一步加强和中国人民志愿军的战斗友谊，克服一切困难，为协助战后恢复建设计划的完成和争取朝鲜的和平统一而胜利前进。
    接着，平壤市人民委员会副委员长朴光熙代表平壤市全体劳动人民向中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员赠送锦旗一面。
    中国人民志愿军代表团首席代表王平将军在受旗后讲话。他代表中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员向英雄的朝鲜军民致崇高的敬意，向朝鲜劳动党、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和金日成元帅表示衷心的感谢。他指出：我们的共同斗争表明了一个真理：为保卫祖国、保卫和平而奋起的人民是不可战胜的，过去是这样，今后更将是这样。他又说：中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员将继续和朝鲜人民一起为我们共同事业的胜利而斗争，我们绝不会辜负祖国人民对我们的委托和朝鲜人民对我们的热望。
    大会在充满国际主义亲密团结的空气中进行。会后，朝鲜国立艺术剧场为大会演出了精彩节目。
