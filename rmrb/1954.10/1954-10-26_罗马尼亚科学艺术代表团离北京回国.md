Title: Romanian Scientific and Artistic Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: The Romanian Scientific and Artistic Delegation, which came to China for a visit according to the 1954 executive plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, ended its visit to China on the 25th and left Beijing by plane for home. During its two-month stay in China, the Romanian Scientific and Artistic Delegation visited Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Tianjin, Shenyang, and other places. Academician Stefan Nicolau, the head of the delegation, gave more than ten reports on "Epidemic Hepatitis," "Ultramicrobes and Ultramicrobiology," and other topics. Yang Voicu, a member of the delegation and a violinist, held several concerts. Siegfried Wolfester, a member of the delegation, director and artist, also gave a report on the development of Romanian drama. Their reports and performances were warmly welcomed by the audiences. In addition, they also conducted extensive exchanges of experience with relevant experts from all aspects of our country.

Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, received the Romanian Scientific and Artistic Delegation on the afternoon of the 24th.

### 罗马尼亚科学艺术代表团离北京回国

1954-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚科学艺术代表团离北京回国
    【新华社二十五日讯】根据中罗文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国访问的罗马尼亚科学艺术代表团，二十五日结束在我国的访问乘飞机离开北京回国。罗马尼亚科学艺术代表团在中国的两个月期间，曾访问和参观了北京、上海、杭州、广州、天津、沈阳等地。团长斯捷凡·尼古劳院士作了“流行性肝炎”、“超微生物与超微生物学”等报告十多次。团员、提琴家扬·沃伊库曾举行了多次演奏会。团员、导演兼美术家色克夫利德·沃尔费捷尔也曾作了有关罗马尼亚戏剧发展情况的报告。他们的报告和演奏都受到了到会群众的热烈欢迎。此外，他们还和我国各方面的有关专家进行了广泛的经验交流。
    中华人民共和国国务院秘书长习仲勋曾在二十四日下午接见了罗马尼亚科学艺术代表团。
