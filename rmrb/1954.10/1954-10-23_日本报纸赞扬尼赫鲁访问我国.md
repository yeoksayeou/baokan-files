Title: Japanese Newspapers Praise Nehru's Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-10-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 22nd -- Japanese "Asahi Shimbun" and "Hokkaido Shimbun" published editorials commenting on Indian Prime Minister Nehru's visit to China.
    The "Asahi Shimbun" editorial on the 18th stated that Nehru's visit to China would be a "major historical event." The "Asahi Shimbun" also recalled the achievements of Premier Zhou Enlai's talks with Prime Minister Nehru during his visit to India in June this year – the famous Five Principles – and praised Nehru's interactions with China.
    The "Hokkaido Shimbun" editorial on the 19th also considered Nehru's visit to China an important event in world politics. The newspaper said that India, Burma, and Indonesia are vehemently opposed to the Southeast Asia military alliance led by the United States. The Indian Prime Minister believes that countries with different social systems need to coexist peacefully. Moreover, these countries are trying to establish a zone of peace.

### 日本报纸赞扬尼赫鲁访问我国

1954-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    日本报纸赞扬尼赫鲁访问我国
    【新华社二十二日讯】东京消息：日本“朝日新闻”和“北海道新闻”发表社论，评论印度总理尼赫鲁访问中国。
    “朝日新闻”十八日的社论说，尼赫鲁这次访问中国将成为“重大的历史性事件”。“朝日新闻”还追述了今年六月间周恩来总理访问印度时和尼赫鲁总理会谈的成果——著名的五项原则，并赞扬尼赫鲁和中国的来往。
    “北海道新闻”十九日的社论也认为尼赫鲁访问中国，在世界政治上是重要事件。报纸说，印度、缅甸、印度尼西亚各国正在猛烈反对以美国为首的东南亚军事联盟。印度总理认为，不同社会制度的国家有和平共处的必要。而且，这些国家正在想建立起和平地区。
