Title: Soviet Newspaper Workers Delegation Arrives in Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th. The Soviet newspaper workers delegation, invited by our People's Daily to visit China, headed by Podkourkov, member of the editorial board of "Pravda," flew from Moscow to Beijing by plane on the 13th. The delegation includes key personnel from the central newspapers "Pravda," "Trud," "Komsomolskaya Pravda," "Selskaya Zhizn," "Literaturnaya Gazeta," "Sovetskaya Kultura," and the magazine "Kommunist." The delegation also includes the chief editors of "Soviet Ukraine," "Red Uzbekistan," and "Volga Commune" of Kuibyshev Oblast.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Sachkov, deputy editor-in-chief of "Pravda," Li Huichuan, secretary of our embassy in the Soviet Union, Li He, Moscow correspondent of the People's Daily, and Li Nan, Moscow correspondent of the Xinhua News Agency.

### 苏报刊工作者代表团前来我国

1954-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    苏报刊工作者代表团前来我国
    【新华社莫斯科十三日电】应我人民日报邀请赴中国访问的苏联报刊工作者代表团一行在团长“真理报”编委波德库尔科夫率领下，已在十三日由莫斯科乘机飞赴北京。代表团中包括中央各报“真理报”、“劳动报”、“共青团真理报”、“农业报”、“文学报”、“苏维埃文化报”及“共产党人”杂志主要负责工作人员。代表团还包括加盟共和国的“苏维埃乌克兰报”、“红色乌兹别克斯坦报”以及古比雪夫州的“伏尔加公社报”的主编。
    到机场送行的有“真理报”副总编辑萨丘科夫，我驻苏大使馆秘书李汇川，人民日报驻莫斯科记者李何，新华社驻莫斯科记者李楠等人。
