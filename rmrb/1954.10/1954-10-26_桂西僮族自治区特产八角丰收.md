Title: Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region's Star Anise Harvest Bumper Crop
Author:
Date: 1954-10-26
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

    Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region's Famous Star Anise Harvest Bumper Crop
    The famous star anise of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region has achieved an unprecedented bumper harvest this year. The star anise producing areas include more than ten counties such as Ningming, Longjin, Baise, and Debao. It is estimated that the total value of this year's star anise production in the entire region is about 30 billion yuan, an average increase of more than double compared to last year. The star anise produced in the autonomous region is sold to various places at home and abroad. It is a raw material for industry and medicine, and also a very good flavoring spice.

### 桂西僮族自治区特产八角丰收

1954-10-26
第2版()
专栏：

    桂西僮族自治区特产八角丰收
    广西省桂西僮族自治区的著名特产八角，今年获得空前丰收。八角产区包括宁明、龙津、百色和德保等十多个县。据估计，今年全区八角产量价值约三百亿元，平均比去年增产一倍多。自治区所产的八角，行销到国内外各地，是工业上、医药上的原料，也是很好的调味香料。　（新华社）
