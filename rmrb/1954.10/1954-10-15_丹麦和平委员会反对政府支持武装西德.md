Title: Danish Peace Committee Opposes Government Support for Arming West Germany
Author:
Date: 1954-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, 12th: According to a TASS report from Copenhagen: The Danish National Peace Committee issued a statement on October 10 stating that the National Peace Committee held a meeting in Odense on the 9th and called on Danish residents to express their protests to the government and the lower house against Denmark's support for German remilitarization and Denmark's joining the North Atlantic bloc.

### 丹麦和平委员会反对政府支持武装西德

1954-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    丹麦和平委员会反对政府支持武装西德
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社哥本哈根讯：丹麦全国和平委员会十月十日发表声明说：全国和平委员会九日在鄂丹斯举行会议，号召丹麦居民对政府和下议院表示抗议，反对丹麦支持德国重新军国主义化和使丹麦加入北大西洋集团。
