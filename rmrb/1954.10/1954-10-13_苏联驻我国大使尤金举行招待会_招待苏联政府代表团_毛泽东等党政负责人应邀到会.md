Title: Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin Holds Reception to Welcome the Soviet Government Delegation; Mao Zedong and Other Party and Government Leaders Attend
Author:
Date: 1954-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Soviet Ambassador to China, Academician Yudin, held a grand reception on the evening of the 12th for the Soviet Government Delegation, which was invited to China to participate in the celebration of the 5th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The Head of the Soviet Government delegation, First Secretary of The Central Committe of the Communist Party of The Soviet Union, and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, N.S. Khrushchev, members of the Soviet Government Delegation: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR N.A. Bulganin, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR A.I. Mikoyan, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions N.M. Shvernik, Minister of Culture of the USSR G.F. Alexandrov, Editor-in-Chief of "Pravda" D.T. Shepilov, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union E.A. Furtseva, Minister of the Building Materials Industry of the Uzbek Republic E.S. Nasriddinova, and responsible staff member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union V.P. Stepanov all attended the reception.

Invited to attend the reception were: Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Zhu De, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Shaoqi, and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai.

At the reception, Soviet Ambassador to China, Academician Yudin, delivered a speech first, followed by speeches from Head of the Delegation Khrushchev and Premier Zhou Enlai.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

After the reception, a Soviet film was shown, and the Soviet National Folk Dance Ensemble headed by I. Moiseyev performed wonderful programs.

### 苏联驻我国大使尤金举行招待会  招待苏联政府代表团  毛泽东等党政负责人应邀到会

1954-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联驻我国大使尤金举行招待会
    招待苏联政府代表团
    毛泽东等党政负责人应邀到会
    【新华社十二日讯】苏联驻我国大使尤金院士为应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的苏联政府代表团，特在十二日晚举行盛大招待会。
    苏联政府代表团团长、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫，苏联政府代表团团员：苏联部长会议第一副主席尼·亚·布尔加宁，苏联部长会议副主席阿·伊·米高扬，全苏工会中央理事会主席尼·米·什维尔尼克，苏联文化部部长格·费·亚历山德罗夫，“真理报”总编辑德·特·谢皮洛夫，苏联共产党莫斯科市委员会书记叶·阿·福尔采娃，乌兹别克共和国建筑材料工业部部长叶·斯·纳斯里金诺娃和苏联共产党中央委员会负责工作人员维·普·斯捷潘诺夫都出席了招待会。
