Title: Nepalese Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Address Reporters, Expressing Welcome for the Section Related to Nepal in Premier Zhou's Report
Author:
Date: 1954-10-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi: According to a report by the Indian Press Trust on September 27th, Premier Zhou Enlai's mention of China's readiness to establish normal relations with Nepal in the "Government Work Report" delivered at the First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on the 23rd has been welcomed in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal.

    Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala and Foreign Minister Regmi told Indian Press Trust reporters, "We welcome the Chinese Premier's desire to establish normal relations between China and Nepal. China is our neighbor, and our relationship with China has a long history. We will seriously consider this issue."

### 尼泊尔首相和外交大臣对记者发表谈话  表示欢迎周总理报告中与尼泊尔有关部分

1954-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首相和外交大臣对记者发表谈话
    表示欢迎周总理报告中与尼泊尔有关部分
   【新华社新德里讯】据印度报业托辣斯九月二十七日报道，周恩来总理二十三日在中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议上作的“政府工作报告”中提到中国也准备同尼泊尔建立正常关系一点，在尼泊尔首都加德满都受到欢迎。
    尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊和外交大臣雷格米对印度报业托辣斯记者说，我们欢迎中国总理对于中国和尼泊尔建立正常关系的愿望。中国是我们的邻国，而且我国和中国的关系是已有长久历史的。我们将认真考虑这个问题。
