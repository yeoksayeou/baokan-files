Title: Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe Performs in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1954-10-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

    Following its arrival in Warsaw on the 11th, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe participated in the celebration of the Polish People's Army Day (October 12th) held in the Warsaw Sports Hall on the same evening at the invitation of the Polish Ministry of National Defense, and performed for the assembly. When the members of the Song and Dance Troupe entered the venue, they were warmly applauded by those present. After the celebration, the Song and Dance Troupe began their performance amidst thunderous applause from the audience.

    Among those who watched the performance were: Polish Council of State Chairman Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense Marshal Konstanty Rokossowski, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party Ochab and Dworakowski, as well as other party and government leaders, senior generals of the Polish People's Army, combat heroes, people from Warsaw's cultural and artistic circles, and model workers from industry and agriculture, totaling more than 7,000 people.

    Also present were Lin Fang, Military Attaché of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Poland, and military attachés from the embassies of the Soviet Union and other countries in Poland.

    During the performance, the audience expressed great interest in the Chinese songs and dances performed by the Song and Dance Troupe, which were full of combativeness and national characteristics. The Army Waist Drum performance by the Song and Dance Troupe was particularly well-received. Their performance of the "Song of the Polish People's Army" and the Polish folk song "Little Cuckoo" in Polish were also praised by the audience, and they were even requested to sing them again at the end.

    After the performance, dozens of representatives of the Polish People's Army and model workers from industry and agriculture went on stage to present flowers to the members of the Song and Dance Troupe. The entire audience stood up and expressed their deep gratitude for the performance with prolonged applause.

    On the evening of the 14th, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe left Warsaw for Gdańsk and other places for a tour performance.

### 我国人民解放军歌舞团在华沙演出

1954-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我国人民解放军歌舞团在华沙演出
    据新华社讯：中国人民解放军歌舞团在十一日到达华沙后，当晚即应波兰国防部的邀请，参加在华沙体育馆大厅举行的波兰人民军建军节（十月十二日）庆祝大会，并为大会演出。当歌舞团人员进入会场时，到会者热烈鼓掌。庆祝大会举行后，歌舞团即在全场雷动的掌声中开始表演节目。
    在场观看演出的有：波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基，部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，部长会议副主席、国防部部长罗科索夫斯基元帅，统一工人党中央委员会书记处书记奥哈布和德沃拉科夫斯基，以及党和政府的其他领导人、波兰人民军高级将领、战斗英雄，华沙文化、艺术界人士和工农业劳动模范等七千多人。
    在场观看演出的还有中华人民共和国驻波兰大使馆武官林放以及苏联和其他国家驻波兰使馆的武官。
    演出中观众对歌舞团所表演的富于战斗性和民族色彩的中国歌舞表示极大的兴趣。歌舞团表演的陆军腰鼓尤其受到欢迎。他们用波兰语演唱的“波兰人民军军歌”和波兰民歌“小杜鹃”也受到观众的赞扬，最后还应观众的要求重新演唱了一遍。
    表演完毕，波兰人民军代表和工农业劳动模范等数十人登台向歌舞团人员献花。全场一致起立，用长时间的鼓掌，来对歌舞团的演出表示深切的感谢。
    十四日晚，中国人民解放军歌舞团离开华沙前往革但斯克等地作巡回演出。
