Title: Soviet, Romanian, Czechoslovak, and Albanian Government Delegations Return to Beijing After Visiting Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1954-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The Soviet government delegation, headed by N.S. Khrushchev, returned to Beijing on the afternoon of October 10th, after visiting Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, and Hankou from October 6th to 10th.

During its stay in Nanjing, the delegation paid respects to the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and the Yuhuatai Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery and laid wreaths. In Shanghai, they visited the Shanghai No. 1 State-owned Cotton Mill and toured the Huangpu River. In Hangzhou and Guangzhou, they visited local scenic spots and historical sites. In Hankou, they toured the city.

While in Beijing, the delegation visited the Summer Palace and other scenic spots and historical sites. Some members also visited the Tianjin No. 2 Cotton Mill in Tianjin.

The delegation was warmly welcomed and received everywhere they went.

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The head of the Romanian People's Republic government delegation, Gheorghe Apostol, and delegation member Gheorghe Platonass, accompanied by Song Shaowen, Deputy Minister of Light Industry, flew back to Beijing on the afternoon of the 12th after visiting Shenyang, Anshan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places from the 5th to the 12th.

While in Shenyang, Comrade Apostol and others visited the Pneumatic Drill Factory, the Workers' New Village, and the Northeast Institute of Technology. In Anshan, they visited the Anshan Iron and Steel Works. In Nanjing, they paid respects to the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and the Yuhuatai Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery and laid wreaths. In Shanghai, they visited the No. 1 Printing and Dyeing Factory and the No. 1 State-owned Cotton Mill and toured the Huangpu River. In Hangzhou, they toured the scenic spots of West Lake and visited the Qiantang River Bridge. Comrade Apostol and others were warmly welcomed everywhere they went.

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The Czechoslovak government delegation, headed by Václav Kopecký, accompanied by Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, flew back to Beijing on the 12th after visiting Anshan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places from the 8th to the 12th.

While in Anshan, the delegation visited the Anshan Iron and Steel Works. In Nanjing, they paid respects to the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and the Yuhuatai Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery and laid wreaths. In Shanghai, they visited the Steam Turbine Factory, the Electrical Machinery Factory, the Museum, and the Workers' Cultural Palace. In Hangzhou, they toured the scenic spots and historical sites of West Lake and the Qiantang River Bridge.

The delegation was warmly welcomed everywhere they went.

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The Albanian People's Republic government delegation, headed by Behar Shtylla, accompanied by Xie Xuegong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, returned to Beijing by plane on the 12th after visiting Shenyang, Anshan, Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places from the 5th to the 11th.

While in Shenyang, the delegation visited the Northeast Institute of Technology and the Gaokan Agricultural Production Cooperative. In Anshan, they visited the Anshan Iron and Steel Works. In Tianjin, they visited the Tanggu New Port and the Workers' Cultural Palace. In Nanjing, they paid respects to the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and the Yuhuatai Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery and laid wreaths, and toured Xuanwu Lake. In Shanghai, they visited the Shanghai Machine Tool Factory, the Printing and Dyeing Factory, and the State-owned Department Store, and toured the Huangpu River. In Hangzhou, they toured the scenic spots of West Lake and visited the Longjing Tea area.

The delegation was warmly welcomed wherever they went.

### 苏、罗、捷、阿政府代表团在各地参观后回京

1954-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏、罗、捷、阿政府代表团在各地参观后回京
    【新华社十二日讯】以尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫团长为首的苏联政府代表团，在十月六日至十日赴南京、上海、杭州、广州、汉口等地参观后，在十日下午回到北京。
    代表团在南京时曾谒中山陵和雨花台革命烈士陵并献花圈；在上海参观了国营上海第一棉纺织厂，游览了黄浦江；在杭州和广州时游览了当地的名胜古迹；在汉口时参观了市区。
    代表团在北京期间曾参观游览了颐和园等名胜古迹，部分团员还前往天津参观了天津第二棉纺织厂。
    代表团到处都受到了热烈欢迎和接待。
    【新华社十二日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长格·阿波斯托尔和团员格·普利奥蒂亚萨由我轻工业部副部长宋劭文陪同，在五日至十二日参观了沈阳、鞍山、南京、上海、杭州等地后已在十二日下午飞返北京。
    阿波斯托尔团长等在沈阳时曾参观了风镐厂、工人新村和东北工学院；在鞍山时参观了鞍山钢铁厂；在南京时曾谒中山陵和雨花台革命烈士陵并献花圈；在上海时参观了印染一厂、国棉一厂并游览了黄浦江；在杭州时游览了西湖名胜并参观了钱塘江大桥。阿波斯托尔团长等在各地都受到了热烈欢迎。
    【新华社十二日讯】以瓦·柯别茨基为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团由我外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航陪同，在八日至十二日参观了鞍山、南京、上海、杭州等地后已在十二日飞返北京。
    代表团在鞍山时参观了鞍山钢铁厂；在南京时曾谒中山陵和雨花台革命烈士陵并献花圈；在上海时参观了汽轮机厂、电机厂、博物馆和工人文化宫；在杭州时游览了西湖名胜古迹和钱塘江大桥。
    代表团到处都受到了热烈欢迎。
    【新华社十二日讯】以贝·什图拉为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团由我对外贸易部副部长解学恭陪同，在五日至十一日到沈阳、鞍山、天津、南京、上海、杭州等地参观后，已在十二日乘飞机回到北京。
    代表团在沈阳时参观了东北工学院和高坎农业生产合作社；在鞍山时参观了鞍山钢铁厂；在天津时参观了塘沽新港和工人文化宫；在南京时曾谒中山陵和雨花台革命烈士陵并献花圈，游览了玄武湖；在上海时参观了上海机床厂、印染厂和国营百货公司，游览了黄浦江；在杭州时游览了西湖名胜古迹并参观了龙井茶区。
    代表团所到之处，都受到了热烈欢迎。
