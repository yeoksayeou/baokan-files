Title: Speech by Comrade Bolesław Bierut, Head of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of Poland
Author: Bèi lǔ tè
Date: 1954-10-01
Page: 2

Dear Chairman, dear comrades:

	On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I extend my warmest and most sincere respects and congratulations to you on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of Poland, the Polish United Workers' Party, and the entire Polish people.

	The Polish people congratulate the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on the adoption of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China with particular joy. This constitution consolidates the significant victory of the Chinese people in national and social terms, and points out the path for the Chinese people to a happy future.

	Since the founding of the People's Republic of China, the lives of the Chinese people have undergone decisive changes in the past five years. Under the leadership of the heroic Communist Party of China, the great Chinese people have resolutely embarked on the road of socialist construction.

	Your path to victory and freedom has not been easy. The Communist Party of China, which creatively applies the immortal doctrine of Marxism-Leninism to the specific environment of China, has led the Chinese people in a protracted struggle against domestic reactionaries, against Japanese aggression, and against the traitorous Chiang Kai-shek clique and American imperialists. In these struggles, the unity of the Chinese people has been consolidated and strengthened.

	In the years between the two world wars, when the workers and peasants of Poland were fighting against reactionaries and fascism, they cared for the liberation struggle waged in distant China with deep sympathy and support. We rejoiced at your victories and grieved at your setbacks. In the ranks of our workers' demonstrations, the slogan "Get out of China!" often appeared. Every blow you inflicted on imperialism also supported the Polish people's struggle to liberate themselves from the shackles of landlords and the bourgeoisie.

	Today, the people of the whole world can see how great a force the 600 million Chinese people, unprecedentedly united around the People's Government and Chairman Mao Zedong, under the leadership of the Chinese working class and the glorious Communist Party of China, have shown in the cause of safeguarding peace and freedom in Asia and the world.

	The new life of the Chinese people in the five years since the founding of the People's Republic of China shows the infinite strength latent in the liberated masses and how brilliant a cause the people can accomplish. These years show that your historic victory, gained in the spirit of the great October Socialist Revolution, is opening up broad prospects for the development of world civilization.

	The forces of peace in the world are growing and consolidating day by day, effectively shattering the conspiracies of warmongers and paving the way for a bright future for mankind. The great Chinese people have made a great contribution to the sacred cause of all peoples in striving for peace and progress and saving mankind from the disaster of a new world war. The unbreakable unity of our camp, our fraternal unity around the invincible fortress of peace and socialism—the Soviet Union—is the guarantee of the victory of our just cause.

	We congratulate you, dear comrades and friends, on further great achievements in the cause of economic and cultural construction, in the large-scale development of China's economic productivity, and in the construction of the socialist foundation that guarantees the welfare, happiness, and prosperity of the Chinese people.

	Long live the great Chinese people!
	
	Long live the eternal friendship between the Chinese and Polish people!

	Long live world peace!

### 波兰人民共和国政府代表团团长  贝鲁特的讲话

1954-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    波兰人民共和国政府代表团团长
    贝鲁特的讲话亲爱的主席同志，亲爱的同志们：
    在中华人民共和国成立五周年之际，我代表波兰人民共和国政府、波兰统一工人党和全体波兰人民向您致最热烈、最诚恳的敬意和祝贺。
    波兰人民怀着特别高兴的心情，祝贺中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会通过了中华人民共和国宪法。这部宪法巩固了中国人民在民族和社会方面具有重大意义的胜利，并且指出了中国人民走向幸福未来的道路。
    自从中华人民共和国成立以后，五年来，中国人民的生活发生了决定性的改变。伟大的中国人民在英勇的中国共产党的领导下，坚决地走上了社会主义建设的道路。
    你们走上胜利和自由的道路不是容易的。把不朽的马克思列宁主义学说创造性地运用到中国的具体环境中来的中国共产党，领导中国人民进行了反对国内反动派、反对日本侵略、反对蒋介石卖国集团和美国帝国主义者的长期斗争。在这些斗争里，中国人民的团结获得了巩固和加强。
    在两次世界大战之间的年代里，正当波兰的工人和农民进行反对反动派和法西斯主义的斗争的时候，他们怀着深切的同情和支持的心情关怀着在遥远的中国所进行的解放斗争。我们为你们的胜利而高兴，为你们的挫折而忧伤。在我们工人示威游行的行列里常常出现“从中国滚出去！”的口号。你们给予帝国主义的每一个打击，也就支持了波兰人民从地主与资产阶级的奴役桎梏中解放出来的斗争。
    今天，全世界人民都看得到，空前紧密地团结在人民政府和毛泽东主席周围的六亿中国人民，在中国工人阶级和光荣的中国共产党的领导下的六亿中国人民，在维护亚洲和世界和平与自由的事业中显出了多么巨大的力量。
    中华人民共和国成立后，五年来中国人民的新生活显示出解放了的人民群众潜藏着无穷无尽的力量，显示出人民群众能够完成多么辉煌的事业。这些年代显示出，你们根据伟大的十月社会主义革命的精神所获得的历史性的胜利，正在为全世界文明的发展开辟着广阔的远景。
    全世界的和平力量正日益壮大和巩固，它有效地粉碎着战争贩子们的阴谋诡计，并为人类的美好未来铺平道路。在各国人民争取和平与进步、拯救人类免于新世界大战灾难的神圣事业中，伟大的中国人民所作的贡献是巨大的。我们阵营的牢不可破的团结，我们在不可战胜的和平与社会主义堡垒——苏联周围兄弟般的团结，是我们正义事业胜利的保证。
    我们祝贺你们，亲爱的同志们与朋友们，在经济和文化的建设事业中，在大规模发展中国经济生产力和保证中国人民的福利、幸福和繁荣的社会主义基础的建设事业中获得进一步的伟大成就。
    伟大的中国人民万岁！
    中波两国人民的永恒的友谊万岁！
    世界和平万岁！
    以苏联为首的和平与社会主义阵营万岁！
