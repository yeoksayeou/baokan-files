Title: Soviet Press Workers Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visits to Various Places; Soviet Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visits to Various Places
Author:
Date: 1954-10-27
Page: 1
　
　　Soviet Press Workers Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visits to Various Places
　　Soviet Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visits to Various Places
　　[This newspaper's dispatch] The Soviet press workers delegation of twenty people, invited by this newspaper to visit our country, led by head Podkourkov, left Beijing by train on the evening of the 26th to visit various parts of our country.
　　During its stay in Beijing, the delegation held various thematic report meetings and forums, and had extensive contacts with journalists, literary and art workers, and scientific workers in the capital, and visited factories, rural areas, universities, and scenic spots and historical sites.
　　When the delegation arrived in Beijing, Lu Dingyi, Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, hosted a banquet on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; the All-China Journalists Association also held a welcome reception for the delegation.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 26th] The Soviet Cultural Delegation, after fifteen days of visits in Shenyang, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other places, returned to Beijing by plane from Wuhan at noon on the 26th, and continued to visit and hold forums in Beijing in the afternoon.
　　Pankratova, editor-in-chief of the Soviet "Historical Issues" magazine, gave a report to more than 1,000 historians, university professors and students in Beijing on "The International Significance of the Russian Revolution of 1905 to 1907." Melnikov, Vice President of the Academy of Educational Sciences of the Russian Federation, gave a report on teaching methodology to more than 400 professors and graduate students at Beijing Normal University. Both reports were warmly welcomed by the audience. The teachers and students of Beijing Normal University presented self-made factory models and other gifts to the members of the delegation.
　　Petrovsky, President of Moscow State University and mathematician, held a forum with Chinese mathematicians.
　　Delegation leader Denisov and some members of the delegation visited the Beijing Library and the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace, where they carefully inspected and inquired about various facilities for the cultural life of the Chinese working people. Kunayev, President of the Academy of Sciences of the Kazakh Republic, visited the Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel Technology. In addition, Jiang Feng, head of the Chinese Artists Association, and others visited the famous Soviet painter Gerasimov.
　　During its stay in Wuhan, the delegation participated in various academic report meetings and forums, and visited flood control projects, Wuhan University, and scenic spots such as Yellow Crane Tower, Shouyi Park, and East Lake Scenic Area.

### 苏联报刊工作者代表团离京去各地访问  苏联文化代表团在各地访问后回到北京

1954-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联报刊工作者代表团离京去各地访问
    苏联文化代表团在各地访问后回到北京
    【本报讯】应本报邀请来我国访问的苏联报刊工作者代表团一行二十人，在团长波德库尔科夫率领下，已在二十六日晚乘火车离北京赴我国各地参观访问。
    代表团在北京期间，曾举行各种专题报告会和座谈会，和首都新闻工作者、文艺工作者、科学工作者进行了广泛的接触，并访问了工厂、农村、大学和名胜古迹。
    在代表团到达北京时，中国共产党中央宣传部部长陆定一曾代表中国共产党中央委员会设宴招待；中国新闻工作者联谊会也曾为代表团举行欢迎酒会。
    【新华社二十六日讯】苏联文化代表团在沈阳、南京、上海、杭州、广州、武汉等地进行了十五天的参观和访问后，在二十六日中午从武汉乘飞机回到北京，下午继续在北京进行参观和座谈。
    苏联“历史问题”杂志总编辑潘克拉托娃向北京的历史学家、各大学教授和学生共一千多人作了“一九零五年到一九零七年俄国革命的国际意义”的报告。俄罗斯联邦共和国教育科学院副院长麦尔尼科夫向北京师范大学的教授和研究生四百多人作了有关教学法问题的报告。这两个报告都受到听众的热烈欢迎。北京师范大学的师生们把自制的工厂模型等礼品赠送给代表团团员。
    国立莫斯科大学校长、数学家彼得罗夫斯基同我国的数学家进行了座谈。
    代表团团长杰尼索夫和部分团员参观了北京图书馆和北京市劳动人民文化宫，他们详细地参观并询问了中国劳动人民文化生活的各方面的设施。哈萨克共和国科学院院长库纳耶夫参观了北京钢铁工业学院。此外，我美术家协会负责人江丰等访问了苏联名画家格拉西莫夫。
    代表团在武汉期间，分别参加了各种学术性的报告会和座谈会，参观了防汛工程、武汉大学以及黄鹤楼、首义公园、东湖风景区等名胜。
