Title: Yi Xiuxiang, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Dies in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-11
Page: 3

    Yi Xiuxiang, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Dies in Beijing
    Yi Xiuxiang, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, passed away in Beijing at 4:18 AM on October 8th, at the age of 49. Vice Chairman Yi Xiuxiang was a native of Gan County, Jiangxi Province. He joined the revolution in 1929 and successively served as a political instructor of a company, political commissar of a hospital, Minister of the Supply Department of the Central Military Commission, Deputy Minister of the Commerce Department of the Northeast People's Government, Standing Committee Member of the CPC Central China South Bureau, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Deputy Director of the Finance and Economics Committee of the Central South Administrative Committee, and member of the CPC Central South Bureau. Vice Chairman Yi Xiuxiang dedicated himself to the Chinese revolution for a long time and suffered from heart disease. He died of coronary artery sclerosis this time. A funeral committee has been formed to handle the funeral arrangements for Vice Chairman Yi Xiuxiang, consisting of Ye Jianying, Li Fuchun, Ma Mingfang, Gu Dacun, and Feng Baiju.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 广东省人民政府副主席  易秀湘在北京病逝

1954-10-11
第3版()
专栏：

    广东省人民政府副主席
    易秀湘在北京病逝
    广东省人民政府副主席易秀湘在十月八日上午四时十八分病逝于北京，享年四十九岁。易秀湘副主席江西省赣县人，一九二九年参加革命，历任连政治指导员、医院政治委员、中共中央军委供给部部长、东北人民政府商业部副部长、中共中央华南分局常委、广东省人民政府副主席、中南行政委员会财政经济委员会副主任、中共中央中南局委员等职。易秀湘副主席长期献身中国革命事业，患有心脏病，这次因心脏冠状动脉硬化病逝。为办理易秀湘副主席的丧葬事宜，已由叶剑英、李富春、马明方、古大存、冯白驹等组成治丧委员会。
                                  （新华社）
