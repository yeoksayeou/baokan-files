Title: German Film Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: A delegation of film workers from the German Democratic Republic, consisting of six members and led by the head of the delegation, Inge Lookhoff, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 6th, according to the 1954 implementation plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement for a visit to our country. Among the delegation members is Irene Korb, the main actress in the film "Men and Women March Forward Together," which will be screened during the film week, and winner of the National Prize of the German Democratic Republic.

　　After participating in the opening ceremony of the "German Democratic Republic Film Week" in Beijing, the delegation will visit various places.

### 德电影工作者代表团到京

1954-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    德电影工作者代表团到京
   【新华社六日讯】根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国访问的德意志民主共和国电影工作者代表团一行六人，在团长英格·洛克霍弗率领下，已于六日下午乘飞机到达北京，代表团中间有将在电影周期间映出的“男女并肩前进”一片中主要演员、德意志民主共和国国家奖金得奖人伊伦内·阔尔布。
    代表团在北京参加“德意志民主共和国电影周”开幕式以后，将去各地参观、访问。
