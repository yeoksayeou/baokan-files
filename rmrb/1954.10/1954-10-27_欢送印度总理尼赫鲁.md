Title: Farewell Reception for Indian Prime Minister Nehru
Author:
Date: 1954-10-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 26th:

    Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen held a grand reception
    To bid farewell to Indian Prime Minister Nehru

    Mrs. Indira Gandhi, N.R. Pillai, Bahadur Singh, M.L. Gaind, K.F. Rustomji and the other eight members accompanying Prime Minister Nehru on his visit to China were invited to attend the reception.

    Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and embassy staff were also invited to attend. Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China attended the reception.

    Also attending the reception were Song Qingling, Chen Shutong, and the Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Panchen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaltsen and other members, as well as the Vice-President of the Supreme People's Court and the Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate.

    Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Ministers of the various ministries Shi Liang, Qian Ying, Zhang Naiqi, Jiang Guangnai, Sha Qianli, Teng Daiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Liao Luyan, Liang Xi, Fu Zuoyi, Shen Yanbing, Zhang Xiruo, Vice-Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Vice-Ministers of the various ministries, Deputy Directors of the various commissions, Vice-Mayors of Beijing Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, also attended the reception.

    Zhang Zhizhong, Vice-Chairman of the National Defense Council, Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Xiao Hua and Gan Siqi, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department, and many senior generals also attended the reception.

    Yuan Zhongxian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, also attended the reception.

    Also attending the reception were Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Chen Hansheng, Vice-President, Standing Committee members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing, responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, members of the Beijing Municipal People's Government and responsible persons of various departments, members of the Beijing Municipal Consultative Committee, responsible persons of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC and the Beijing organizations of various democratic parties, responsible persons of various people's organizations in Beijing, people from the capital's scientific, educational, literary, artistic, religious, and journalistic circles, representatives of ethnic minorities and model workers, as well as members of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association delegation who had visited India and responsible persons of various units visited by Prime Minister Nehru and others in Beijing.

    The foreign envoys and diplomatic personnel stationed in China who were invited to attend the reception included: Burmese Ambassador U Hla Maung, Pakistani Ambassador Raza, Czechoslovak Ambassador Kousal, Polish Ambassador Kiryluk, Vietnamese Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan, Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat, Indonesian Ambassador Mononutu, German Ambassador König, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov, Hungarian Ambassador Sikladan, Finnish Minister Sundström, Danish Minister গ্রেggersen, Swiss Minister Beno, British Chargé d'Affaires TW Williams, Norwegian Chargé d'Affaires Gjemre, Swedish Chargé d'Affaires Hammarskjöld, Romanian Chargé d'Affaires Montanu, Korean Chargé d'Affaires Lee Chung-ik, Albanian Chargé d'Affaires Jaşa, Dutch Negotiator Smand and others.

    A total of more than 700 people attended the reception.

    The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 欢送印度总理尼赫鲁

1954-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市彭真市长举行盛大酒会
    欢送印度总理尼赫鲁
    【新华社二十六日讯】北京市人民政府市长彭真，二十六日下午举行盛大酒会，欢送行将离开北京的印度共和国总理尼赫鲁。
    随同尼赫鲁总理访问我国的英迪拉·甘地夫人、纳·鲁·皮莱、巴哈杜尔·辛格、墨·勒·盖恩德、克·弗·鲁斯滕吉等八人都应邀出席了酒会。
    印度驻我国大使赖嘉文和使馆人员也应邀出席了酒会。中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来出席了酒会。
    出席酒会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、陈叔通、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措，委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和其他委员，以及最高人民法院副院长，最高人民检察院副检察长。
    出席酒会的，还有国务院秘书长习仲勋，各部部长史良、钱瑛、章乃器、蒋光鼐、沙千里、滕代远、朱学范、廖鲁言、梁希、傅作义、沈雁冰、张奚若，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，各部副部长，各委员会副主任，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、吴晗。
    出席酒会的，还有国防委员会副主席张治中，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任萧华、甘泗淇和高级将领多人。
    中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤也出席了酒会。
    出席酒会的，还有中印友好协会会长丁西林、副会长陈翰笙，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在京常务委员，各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，北京市人民政府委员和各部门负责人，北京市协商委员会委员，中共北京市委员会和各民主党派北京市组织的负责人，北京市各人民团体的负责人，首都科学、教育、文学、艺术、宗教、新闻界人士，少数民族代表和劳动模范，以及曾访问过印度的中印友好协会代表团人员和尼赫鲁总理等在京参观访问过的各单位负责人。
    应邀出席酒会的各国驻我国使节和外交人员有：缅甸大使吴拉茂，巴基斯坦大使罗查，捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉，波兰大使基里洛克，越南民主共和国大使黄文欢，蒙古大使奥其尔巴特，印度尼西亚大使莫诺努图，德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希，苏联大使尤金，保加利亚大使迪莫夫，匈牙利大使希克拉丹，芬兰公使孙士敦，丹麦公使格瑞杰生，瑞士公使贝努义，英国代办杜维廉，挪威临时代办季茂登，瑞典临时代办哈马孝尔德，罗马尼亚临时代办孟天努，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国临时代办李忠翼，阿尔巴尼亚临时代办雅沙，荷兰谈判代表司曼等。
    出席酒会的，共有七百多人。
    酒会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
