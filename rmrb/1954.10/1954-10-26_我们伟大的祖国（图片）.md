Our Great Motherland
The state-owned Changsha Machine Tool Factory, in accordance with Soviet blueprints, has manufactured the Model 872 bow saw, which is being supplied in succession to basic construction projects such as the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant. Here, inspectors are inspecting a batch of newly manufactured Model 872 bow saws ready for delivery.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营长沙机床厂按照苏联图纸制成了八七二型弓锯床，陆续供给第一汽车制造厂等基本建设工程的需要。检验员正在这里检验一批准备出厂的新制八七二型弓锯床。
