    Soldiers of an anti-aircraft machine gun company of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have created their own dances, which they perform during rest periods to encourage training enthusiasm. The picture shows a scene of an anti-aircraft machine gunner performing a dance about shooting down airplanes. Photo by Xue Sifang.
