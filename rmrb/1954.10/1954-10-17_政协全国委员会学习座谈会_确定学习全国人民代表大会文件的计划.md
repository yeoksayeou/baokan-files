Title: CPPCC National Committee Study Symposium Decides on Plan to Study National People's Congress Documents
Author:
Date: 1954-10-17
Page: 1

    CPPCC National Committee Study Symposium
    Decides on Plan to Study National People's Congress Documents
    [Xinhua News Agency, 15th] The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) study symposium has decided on a plan to study the main documents of the First Session of the First National People's Congress. According to this plan, the study symposium will begin on October 22nd to study the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, Chairman Mao Zedong's opening address, Liu Shaoqi's report on the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China, and Premier Zhou Enlai's government work report. This study is scheduled to end at the end of December.
    In order to strengthen the leadership of the study, the Secretariat of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference study symposium has decided to reorganize into the Study Secretariat of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, responsible for presiding over the work of the study symposium and leading the work of the study committees of various democratic parties' organs.
    The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference will issue a notice to the CPPCC committees of all provinces and cities, calling on them to conscientiously study the documents of the First Session of the First National People's Congress under the unified arrangement of the local leading study organs.

### 政协全国委员会学习座谈会  确定学习全国人民代表大会文件的计划

1954-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会学习座谈会
    确定学习全国人民代表大会文件的计划
    【新华社十五日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会学习座谈会决定了学习第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议主要文件的计划。根据这个计划，学习座谈会将从十月二十二日开始学习中华人民共和国宪法、毛泽东主席的开幕词、刘少奇关于中华人民共和国宪法草案的报告和周恩来总理的政府工作报告。这一学习预定在十二月底结束。
    为了加强学习的领导，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会学习座谈会干事会，决定改组为中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会学习干事会，负责主持学习座谈会的工作和领导各民主党派机关干部学习委员会的工作。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会将向各省、市协商委员会发出通知，号召他们在当地领导学习的机关的统一布置下，认真学习第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的文件。
