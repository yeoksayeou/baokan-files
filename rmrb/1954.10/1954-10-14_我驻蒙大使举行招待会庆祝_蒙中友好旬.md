Title: Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Holds Reception to Celebrate "Mongolia-China Friendship Week"
Author: 
Date: 1954-10-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, October 10th: The Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Mongolian People's Republic, He Ying, held a reception on the evening of October 9th to celebrate "Mongolia-China Friendship Week" and the visit of the Chinese Art Troupe and Acrobatics Troupe to Mongolia.
    Those present at the reception included: First Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, S. Luvsan, Deputy Prime Minister Maidar, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Dugersuren, as well as ministers and deputy ministers of various ministries and well-known figures in the cultural and artistic circles.
    Also invited were: Soviet Ambassador to Mongolia, Pisarev, North Korean Ambassador to Mongolia, Hong Dong-cheol, and staff members of the two embassies. Cao Yu, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, Lu Yaowu, head of the Acrobatics Troupe, and all members of the Art Troupe and Acrobatics Troupe were also invited to attend.
    Approximately two hundred people attended the reception.
    The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 我驻蒙大使举行招待会庆祝“蒙中友好旬”

1954-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻蒙大使举行招待会庆祝“蒙中友好旬”
    【新华社十日讯】乌兰巴托消息：中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国大使何英为庆祝“蒙中友好旬”并中国文艺代表团和杂技团到蒙古访问，在十月九日晚间举行了招待会。
    出席招待会的有蒙古人民共和国第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理迈达尔，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记杜格苏伦以及各部部长、副部长和文化艺术界著名人士。
    应邀出席的还有：苏联驻蒙古大使皮萨列夫、朝鲜驻蒙古大使洪东喆和两国使馆人员。中国文艺代表团团长曹禺、杂技团团长卢耀武和文艺代表团、杂技团全体团员也都应邀出席。
    出席招待会的共约二百余人。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
