Title: Chinese Urban Construction Delegation Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 16th—The Chinese urban construction delegation of fourteen members, led by Xu Yingnian, the deputy director of the Urban Construction Bureau of the Chinese Ministry of Construction Engineering, arrived in Warsaw by plane on the 15th, according to the Sino-Polish technical and technical-scientific cooperation agreement to conduct an inspection in Poland.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Czachowski, Deputy Minister of the Polish Ministry of Urban and Rural Construction, and representatives from the State Economic Planning Commission and the Polish Architects Association. Liu Ruoming, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Poland, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 我国城市建设考察团到华沙

1954-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我国城市建设考察团到华沙
    【新华社华沙十六日电】根据中波技术和技术科学合作协定到波兰考察的中国城市建设考察团一行十四人，在团长、中国建筑工程部城市建设局副局长许英年率领下，已于十五日乘飞机到达华沙。
    前往机场欢迎的有波兰城乡建设部副部长查科夫斯基，国家经济计划委员会和波兰建筑师协会的代表等多人。中国驻波兰大使馆商务参赞刘若明也往机场欢迎。
