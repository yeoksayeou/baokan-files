Title: Science and Technology Association Delegation Departs for Soviet Union

Author:
Date: 1954-10-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: The delegation organized by the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization at the invitation of the Soviet Association for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge, and led by head of delegation Mao Yisheng, left Beijing by train for the Soviet Union on the 24th. The delegation consists of twenty-five members, mainly natural scientists, as well as social scientists, youth workers, and trade union workers. The delegation's main task is to learn from the Soviet Association for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge about their experience in popularizing political and scientific knowledge to the masses in the Soviet Union's communist construction, especially their experience in organizational leadership and propaganda work.

### 科学技术协会赴苏访问团启程

1954-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    科学技术协会赴苏访问团启程
   【新华社二十四日讯】中华全国科学技术普及协会应苏联政治与科学知识普及协会邀请所组织的赴苏访问代表团，二十四日在团长茅以升率领下乘车离京赴苏。代表团的成员共二十五人，以自然科学家为主，此外还有社会科学家、青年工作者和工会工作者。代表团的主要任务是学习苏联政治与科学知识普及协会在苏联共产主义建设事业中向人民群众普及政治和科学知识的经验，特别是他们在组织领导工作方面和宣传工作方面的经验。
