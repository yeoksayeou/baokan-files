Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-10-07
Page: 1

After the Yongli Chemical Industrial Company's Ningchang Plant implemented joint public-private ownership in 1952, the labor enthusiasm of all employees soared to unprecedented levels. Last year, the plant overfulfilled the state's production plan; this year, the fertilizer powder produced is more than last year, enough to supply 800,000 mu of farmland. This is a workshop for manufacturing fertilizer powder.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    永利化学工业公司宁厂自一九五二年实行公私合营后，全厂职工的劳动热情空前高涨。去年该厂超额完成了国家的生产计划；今年比去年多生产的肥田粉，可供八十万亩农田使用。这是制造肥田粉的一个车间。
