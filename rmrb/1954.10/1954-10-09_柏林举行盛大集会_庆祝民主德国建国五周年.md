Title: Grand Rally Held in Berlin to Celebrate the 5th Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1954-10-09
Page: 4
   Xinhua News Agency, October 8th: According to a report by the German News Agency: On the 6th, a grand celebration was held in Berlin to celebrate the 5th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

    Attending the celebration were: Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, Deputy Prime Minister Walter Ulbricht, National Council Chairman of the National Front of Democratic Germany Erich Correns, Chairman of the Communist Party of Germany Max Reimann, and representatives from various democratic parties, mass organizations, economic and cultural circles of the German Democratic Republic.

    Also attending the celebration were the Soviet government delegation headed by Vyacheslav Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Soviet Foreign Minister, the government delegation of the People's Republic of China headed by Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and personnel from the government delegations of various People's Democratic countries.

    Also attending the celebration were diplomatic envoys from various countries to the German Democratic Republic.

    Ulbricht paid tribute to the foreign guests present amid thunderous applause.

    Grotewohl, Molotov, and Chen Yi delivered speeches at the meeting successively. Their speeches were frequently interrupted by warm applause.

### 柏林举行盛大集会  庆祝民主德国建国五周年

1954-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    柏林举行盛大集会
    庆祝民主德国建国五周年
   【新华社八日讯】据德意志通讯社报道：六日，柏林举行了盛大的庆祝会，庆祝德意志民主共和国建国五周年。
    出席这次庆祝大会的有：德意志民主共和国总统皮克，总理格罗提渥，副总理乌布利希，民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席柯伦斯，德国共产党主席雷曼以及德意志民主共和国各民主党派、各群众团体，经济和文化界的代表人士。
    出席庆祝大会的还有以苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部长莫洛托夫为首的苏联政府代表团，以中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团及各人民民主国家的政府代表团人员。
    出席庆祝大会的还有各国驻德意志民主共和国的外交使节。
    乌布利希在掌声雷动中向到会的外宾致敬。
    格罗提渥、莫洛托夫和陈毅等相继在会上发表了演说，他们的演说经常为热烈的掌声所打断。
