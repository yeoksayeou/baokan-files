Title: Korean Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: The government trade delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Hwang Tae-sung, Vice Minister of Trade of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, arrived in Beijing on the 18th.

Xu Xuehan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, and others welcomed them at the station.

Choi Il, the Korean Ambassador to China, Kim Kuk-bo, Commercial Counselor, and others also welcomed them at the station.

The list of the Korean Government Trade Delegation:
	Head: Hwang Tae-sung
	Members: Choi Kuk-bo
		Park Yu-jae
		Han Cheol-hwan

### 朝鲜政府贸易代表团到达北京

1954-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府贸易代表团到达北京
    【新华社十八日讯】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府贸易代表团由团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国贸易省副相黄泰成率领，在十八日到达北京。
    中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒等前往车站欢迎。
    朝鲜驻华大使崔一、商务参赞金国辅等也前往车站欢迎。
    朝鲜政府贸易代表团名单：
    团长：黄泰成
    团员：崔国辅
          朴有在
          韩喆焕
