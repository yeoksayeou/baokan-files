Title: Fifth Anniversary of National Day in the Capital (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1954-10-02
Page: 4

National Day's Fifth Anniversary in the Capital
    Producing more cotton to meet the needs of industrial construction – Members of agricultural production cooperatives in the suburbs of Beijing carry cotton models in a parade. Photo by Ge Liqun, Xinhua News Agency
    Workers from the industrial sector carry models of high-voltage power lines as they march towards Tiananmen Square.
                  Photo by Zheng Jingkang, Xinhua News Agency
    During the grand celebration of the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, Chairman Mao, along with other leaders of the Party and the government, reviewed the mighty People's Liberation Army and the demonstrating masses in Tiananmen Square. Comrade Khrushchev and other distinguished guests also participated in the review. Special reporter Wu Benli.
    People marching hold up banners with the words "We must liberate Taiwan." Photo by Yu Huiru, Xinhua News Agency
    A contingent carrying a huge model of the "Constitution of the People's Republic of China" is marching towards Tiananmen.
　Photo by Zhang Li
    This is the honor guard at the head of the parade. Photo by Gao Liang
    Colorful balloons rise from the Young Pioneers' ranks, and cheers resound through Tiananmen Square. Photo by Zhang Jiahua, Xinhua News Agency
    Representatives of ethnic minorities on the Tiananmen rostrum. Photo by Qian Sijie, Xinhua News Agency
    Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai reviews the troops lined up in the square. Photo by Yu Huiru, Xinhua News Agency
    The cultural and artistic contingent, symbolizing "Let a hundred flowers bloom, weed through the old to bring forth the new," passes before the reviewing stand.
　Photo by Jiang Qisheng, Xinhua News Agency
    Foreign guests on the Tiananmen rostrum. Photo by Ge Liqun, Xinhua News Agency
    The heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force and rocket artillery units pass through Tiananmen Square. Photo by Qi Guanshan, Xinhua News Agency
    The inspected naval units pass through Tiananmen Square. They represent the increasingly powerful naval fleet of the motherland, which is constantly maintaining combat readiness for the liberation of Taiwan. Photo by Gao Liang
    A mighty procession of 500,000 workers, farmers, government employees, students, and citizens of the capital passes through Tiananmen Square. Special reporter Wu Benli.

### 国庆五周年在首都（图片）

1954-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    国庆五周年在首都
    生产更多的棉花供应工业建设的需要——京郊农业生产合作社的社员们抬着棉花模型列队前进。　新华社记者　葛力群摄
    工业部门的工作人员们抬着高压电线的模型向天安门广场前进。
                  新华社记者　郑景康摄
    在中华人民共和国建国五周年的伟大节日，毛主席偕同党和政府的其他领导者在天安门检阅了强大的人民解放军和示威游行的群众队伍。参加检阅的还有贵宾赫鲁晓夫同志等。本报特约记者　吴本立摄
    游行的人们高举着“我们一定要解放台湾”的字标。　新华社记者　喻惠如摄
    抬着巨大的“中华人民共和国宪法”模型的队伍正向天安门前进。
　本报张力摄
    这是游行队伍最前列的仪仗队。　本报记者　高　粮摄
    五彩缤纷的气球从少年先锋队队伍中升起，天安门广场上的欢呼声响彻云霄。新华社记者  张家骅摄
    在天安门观礼台上的少数民族代表。    新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
    国防部部长彭德怀检阅排列在广场上的部队。　新华社记者　喻惠如摄
    象征着“百花齐放、推陈出新”的文艺大军，行经检阅台前。
　新华社记者　蒋齐生摄
    在天安门观礼台上的各国来宾。     新华社记者　葛力群摄
    英勇的中国人民解放军空军机群和火箭炮部队，通过天安门广场。新华社记者　齐观山摄
    受检阅的海军部队通过天安门广场。他们代表着祖国日益强大、为解放台湾而经常保持战斗准备的海军舰队。　本报记者　高　粮摄
    首都五十万工人、农民、机关工作人员、学生和市民浩浩荡荡通过天安门广场。　本报特约记者　吴本立摄
