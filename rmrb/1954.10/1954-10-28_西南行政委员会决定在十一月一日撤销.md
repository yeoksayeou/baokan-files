Title: Southwest Administrative Committee Decides to Dissolve on November 1st
Author:
Date: 1954-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, October 27th. The Southwest Administrative Committee held its final administrative meeting on October 26th. Attending the meeting were the Vice-Chairmen of the Southwest Administrative Committee, committee members in Chongqing, and heads of various departments, totaling sixty-nine people. The meeting heard a report from Kang Nai'er, Secretary-General of the Southwest Administrative Committee, on the conclusion of its work. It was immediately decided that the Southwest Administrative Committee would cease issuing documents and operating from November 1st and announced its dissolution.

### 西南行政委员会决定在十一月一日撤销

1954-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    西南行政委员会决定在十一月一日撤销
    【新华社重庆二十七日电】西南行政委员会在十月二十六日举行最后一次行政会议。出席这次会议的有西南行政委员会各位副主席，在重庆的委员及各部门负责人共六十九人。会议听了西南行政委员会秘书长康乃尔关于结束工作的报告，当即决定西南行政委员会在十一月一日起停止行文和办公，并宣布撤销。
