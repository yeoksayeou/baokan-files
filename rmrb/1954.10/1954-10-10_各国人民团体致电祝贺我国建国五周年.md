Title: People's Organizations from Various Countries Send Congratulatory Cables on the Fifth Anniversary of the Founding of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-10-10
Page: 4
    People's Organizations from Various Countries Send Congratulatory Cables on the Fifth Anniversary of the Founding of Our Country

    This newspaper reports: Namba Hideo, Vice Chairman of the Japan National Relief Association, the Ceylon Equal Society Party, Williams, Chairman of the Australian Miners' Federation, Yevon Pae, Chairman of the Finland-China Association, Laurie Kennan, Secretary-General, Yusuf Katchalia and Mistry, Joint Secretaries of the South African Indian Congress, and the Steering Committee of the France-China Friendship Association, sent cables or letters to the Chinese government and Chairman Mao Zedong around China's National Day, congratulating them on the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

    The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace received congratulatory telegrams from the Soviet Peace Committee, the Vietnamese Committee for the Defense of World Peace, the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace, the German Peace Committee, and the Mongolian Committee for the Defense of Peace, congratulating China on its National Day.

    The All-China Democratic Women's Federation received congratulatory telegrams or letters from Raicho Hiratsuka, President of the Federation of Japanese Women's Organizations, the Indonesian Women's Association, Chaleiporn Ganlayanamit, representative of the Democratic Women's Federation of Thailand, Pushpamoyee Bose, President of the All India Women's Conference, and Japanese famous musician Seki Kanshi and critic Ishigaki Ayako, congratulating China on its National Day.

### 各国人民团体致电祝贺我国建国五周年

1954-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    各国人民团体致电祝贺我国建国五周年
    本报讯：日本国民救援会副会长难波英夫，锡兰平等社会党，澳大利亚矿工联合会主席威廉斯，芬中协会主席叶文佩、秘书长劳里凯南，南非印度人大会联合书记优素福·卡查利亚、米斯特里，法中友好协会指导委员会，在我国国庆节前后分别致电或写信给我国政府和毛泽东主席，祝贺中华人民共和国成立五周年。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会收到了苏联拥护和平委员会，越南保卫世界和平委员会，捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会，德国和平委员会，蒙古保卫和平委员会等国和平组织的贺电，祝贺我国国庆。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会收到了日本妇女团体联合会会长平塚雷鸟，印度尼西亚妇女协会，泰国民主妇女联合会代表乍蕾蓬·干拉耶那密，印度全国妇女联合会主席普什帕莫耶·鲍斯，日本著名音乐家关鉴子和评论家石垣绫子的贺电或贺信，祝贺我国国庆。
