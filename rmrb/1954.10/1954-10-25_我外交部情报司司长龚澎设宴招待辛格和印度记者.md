Title: Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hosts Singh and Indian Journalists at a Banquet
Author:
Date: 1954-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet on the 23rd to entertain Bahadur Singh, Deputy Secretary and Director of the Press Office of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who accompanied Indian Prime Minister Nehru on his visit to China, and the Indian journalists who were invited by the All-China Journalists Association to cover the visit. These journalists are Mankikar of the "Times of India", Abbaswamy of the "Hindu", and Swaminathan of the "Free Press" (representing the All India Newspaper Editors' Conference), Saney of the "Lion", Das Gupta of the "Hindustan Standard", Ramchandran of the Press Trust of India (representing the Indian Federation of Working Journalists), and Bhatia of "The Statesman", Viswanath of the "Indian Express", and Binoy Gupta of the United Press of India (representing the Delhi News Association); Also invited to the banquet was Reith, the Beijing correspondent of the "Times of India". Korean and Vietnamese journalists in Beijing, as well as heads of the All-China Journalists Association and Beijing journalists, also attended the banquet.

On the same evening, Bahadur Singh hosted a banquet to entertain Gong Peng and the heads of the All-China Journalists Association and Beijing journalists. Many foreign journalists in Beijing also attended the banquet.

### 我外交部情报司司长龚澎设宴招待辛格和印度记者

1954-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我外交部情报司司长龚澎设宴招待辛格和印度记者
    【新华社二十三日讯】中华人民共和国外交部情报司司长龚澎二十三日设宴招待随印度总理尼赫鲁来我国访问的印度外交部副秘书、新闻处处长巴哈杜尔·辛格，及应中国新闻工作者联谊会的邀请来华采访的印度记者，他们是“印度时报”的曼基卡尔，“印度教徒报”的阿巴斯瓦米，“自由新闻”的斯瓦米纳坦（以上代表全印报纸编辑会议），“狮报”的萨奈，“印度斯坦旗报”的达斯·古普塔，印度报业托辣斯的拉姆钱德兰（以上代表印度新闻从业员联合会），以及“政治家报”的巴蒂亚，“印度快报”的维斯瓦纳特，印度统一通讯社的比诺伊·古普塔（以上代表德里新闻协会）；应邀参加宴会的还有“印度时报”驻北京记者雷第。参加宴会的还有在北京的朝鲜及越南记者，以及中国新闻工作者联谊会负责人及北京新闻界人士多人。
    同日晚，巴哈杜尔·辛格设宴招待龚澎和中国新闻工作者联谊会负责人和北京新闻界人士，参加宴会的还有在北京的外国记者多人。
