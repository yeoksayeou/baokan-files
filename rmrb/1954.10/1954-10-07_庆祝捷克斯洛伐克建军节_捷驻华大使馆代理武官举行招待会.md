Title: Celebrating Czechoslovak Army Day: Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1954-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: To celebrate Czechoslovak Army Day on October 6th, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Captain Muha of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China held a reception in Beijing on the evening of the 6th.

Those invited to attend the reception included: Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China Nie Rongzhen, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Xiao Hua and Gan Siqi, Director of the General Office Xiao Xiangrong, many senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yan Baohang, as well as representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's delegation returning home for National Day celebrations, combat heroes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and many literary and art workers from the troops.

Also invited were military attachés from the embassies of the Soviet Union, India, Poland, Indonesia, Romania, and Burma in China.

Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Muha delivered a speech first. He said that the Czechoslovak army, modeled after the Soviet army, has become an organized and powerful force, always ready to defend its motherland. Our people and their army extend warm greetings to the Chinese people and their army with deep friendship, and express their full support for the just struggle of the Chinese people to liberate Taiwan, an integral part of Chinese territory. He also congratulated the unbreakable friendship between the people and armies of the People's Republic of China and Czechoslovakia, and congratulated the mighty peace camp led by the Soviet Union.

Vice Chairman Nie Rongzhen then delivered a speech, extending his heartfelt congratulations to the Czechoslovak army. He said that the friendship between the people and armies of China and Czechoslovakia is constantly being consolidated and developed, and the further consolidation and development of this fraternal friendship is of great significance for strengthening the world's peace forces led by the Soviet Union.

The Czechoslovak films "1954 Parade" and other documentaries were shown at the reception.

In addition: Prior to Czechoslovak Army Day, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China Peng Dehuai sent a telegram to General Čepička, Minister of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Republic, expressing his congratulations.

### 庆祝捷克斯洛伐克建军节  捷驻华大使馆代理武官举行招待会

1954-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝捷克斯洛伐克建军节
    捷驻华大使馆代理武官举行招待会
    【新华社六日讯】十月六日是捷克斯洛伐克建军节，捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆代理武官慕哈大尉六日晚在北京举行招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的有：中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻，总政治部副主任萧华、甘泗淇，办公厅主任萧向荣，中国人民解放军高级将领多人，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，以及中国人民志愿军国庆节归国观礼代表团代表，中国人民解放军战斗英雄，部队文艺工作者多人。
    应邀出席的还有：苏联、印度、波兰、印度尼西亚、罗马尼亚和缅甸等国驻华大使馆武官。
    慕哈代理武官首先致词。他说，以苏联军队为榜样的捷克斯洛伐克军队已成为时刻准备着挺身保卫自己祖国的有组织的强大力量。我国人民和其军队怀着深厚友谊的心情向中国人民及其军队致以热烈的敬礼，并且表示完全拥护中国人民为解放中国领土不可分割的一部分——台湾而进行的正义斗争。他并祝贺中华人民共和国和捷克斯洛伐克两国人民及军队之间的牢不可破的友谊万岁，祝贺以苏联为首的强大的和平阵营万岁。
    聂荣臻副主席接着致词，对捷克斯洛伐克军队表示衷心的祝贺。他说，中捷两国人民及其军队间的友谊正在不断地巩固和发展，这种兄弟般友谊的进一步巩固和发展，对于加强以苏联为首的世界和平力量，具有重大的意义。
    招待会上放映了捷克斯洛伐克的“一九五四年阅兵式”和其它纪录影片。
    又讯：中华人民共和国国防部部长彭德怀在捷克斯洛伐克建军节前夕，曾致电捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部部长契皮茨卡大将，表示祝贺。
