Title: Congratulatory Telegrams Exchanged Between Premier Zhou and Premier Tsedenbal on the Second Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-10-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd.

    Congratulatory Telegrams Exchanged Between Premier Zhou and Premier Tsedenbal on the Second Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement
    Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Premier Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic, congratulating him on the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The telegram reads as follows: Comrade Tsedenbal, Premier of the Mongolian People's Republic:

    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural cooperation agreement, I would like to express my warmest congratulations to you, Premier, and through you to the government and people of the Mongolian People's Republic.

    Over the past two years, the profound fraternal friendship and friendly cooperation between the peoples of China and Mongolia have been further developed due to the signing and implementation of this agreement. This has made a beneficial contribution to the peace and democratic forces headed by the Soviet Union, and to the peace of Asia and the world.

    I wish the unbreakable friendship between the peoples of China and Mongolia will be further consolidated and developed.

    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
    October 3, 1954, in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Premier Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, congratulating him on the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The telegram reads as follows: Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

    On the happy occasion of the second anniversary of the signing of the Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the Mongolian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China, I would like to extend my most sincere respects to you, Premier, the Government of the People's Republic of China, and the entire great, fraternal Chinese people. This historically significant Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement is producing good results and is further strengthening the fraternal friendship between our two peoples.

    Long live the fraternal and unbreakable friendship between the peoples of Mongolia and China!

    Long live the powerful camp of peace, democracy, and socialism headed by the great Soviet Union!

    Tsedenbal, Premier of the Mongolian People's Republic
    October 2, 1954, in Ulaanbaatar

### 祝贺中蒙经济及文化合作协定签订两周年  周总理和泽登巴尔总理互致贺电

1954-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺中蒙经济及文化合作协定签订两周年
    周总理和泽登巴尔总理互致贺电
    【新华社三日讯】　中华人民共和国国务院周恩来总理致电蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，祝贺中蒙经济及文化合作协定签订两周年。电文如下：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔同志：
    值此中蒙经济及文化合作协定签订两周年之际，谨向您，总理同志，并通过您向蒙古人民共和国政府和人民表示最热烈的祝贺。
    两年来，中蒙两国人民之间兄弟般的深厚友谊和友好合作由于这一协定的签订和实施得到了进一步的发展。这对以苏联为首的和平民主力量、对亚洲及世界的和平作了有益的贡献。
    祝中、蒙两国人民之间牢不可破的友谊日臻巩固和发展。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
    一九五四年十月三日于北京
    【新华社三日讯】蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔致电中华人民共和国国务院周恩来总理，祝贺中蒙经济及文化合作协定签订两周年。电文如下：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    欣逢蒙古人民共和国与中华人民共和国经济及文化合作协定签订第二周年，谨向您，总理同志，中华人民共和国政府，和全体伟大的、兄弟般的中国人民致最诚挚的敬意。这一有历史意义的经济及文化合作协定正在产生良好的结果，正在进一步加强我们两国人民之间兄弟般的友谊。
    蒙、中两国人民兄弟般的、牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    以伟大苏联为首的强大的和平、民主、社会主义阵营万岁！
    蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔
    一九五四年十月二日于乌兰巴托
