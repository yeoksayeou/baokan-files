Title: President Pieck Receives Chen Yi, Head of Our Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 12th: President Wilhelm Pieck of the German Democratic Republic received Chen Yi, head of the Chinese government delegation and Vice Premier of the State Council, as well as delegation members Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Ji Pengfei, our country's ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, at the Presidential Office in the afternoon of the 11th. The delegation attended the National Day celebrations of the German Democratic Republic. During the reception, our government delegation presented President Wilhelm Pieck with a gift: a full-view ivory carving of Longevity Hill in Beijing.

### 皮克总统接见我政府代表团团长陈毅

1954-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    皮克总统接见我政府代表团团长陈毅
    【新华社柏林十二日电】德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克十一日下午在总统办公厅接见参加德意志民主共和国国庆典礼的中国政府代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅，团员、中国科学院副院长竺可桢和团员、我国驻德意志民主共和国大使姬鹏飞。在接见时我政府代表团将礼品北京万寿山全景象牙雕刻赠送给威廉·皮克总统。
