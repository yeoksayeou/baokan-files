Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Receives Professor Bernal
Author:
Date: 1954-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress received Professor John Bernal, the famous British physicist, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, and recipient of the Stalin International Prize for "Strengthening International Peace," who was invited by the Chinese Academy of Sciences to visit China, on the afternoon of the 24th.

Those present at the meeting included: Zhang Jiafu, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China; and Lu Dong, Deputy Minister of Heavy Industry.

### 刘少奇委员长接见贝尔纳教授

1954-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇委员长接见贝尔纳教授
   【新华社二十四日讯】我全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇在二十四日下午接见了应中国科学院邀请前来我国访问的著名英国物理学家、世界和平理事会副主席、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金获得者约翰·贝尔纳教授。
    陪见的有：中国科学院副院长张稼夫、中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长雷任民、重工业部副部长吕东。
