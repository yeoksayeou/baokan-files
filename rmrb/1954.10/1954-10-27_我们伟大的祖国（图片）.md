Our great motherland
Comprehensive development of agriculture, forestry, and animal husbandry is the most effective way to prosper the economy of mountainous areas and improve the lives of people in mountainous areas. This is a flock of sheep grazing at the Wuhou Pear Agriculture, Forestry and Animal Husbandry Production Cooperative in Yangjingdi Township, Pingshun County, Shanxi Province.
