Title: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Holds Tea Party to Welcome Soviet Cultural Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1954-10-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 4th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, held a tea party on the afternoon of the 4th to welcome the Soviet cultural delegation invited to China to attend the National Day celebrations.
    The head of the Soviet cultural delegation, Denisov, and all members of the delegation attended the invitation.
    Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, and Academician Alexandrov, the Minister of Culture of the Soviet Union and a member of the Soviet government delegation, were also invited to attend.
    Those present at the tea party included: Chen Boda, Li Siguang, Zhang Jiafu, Tao Menghe, and Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education, Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Minister of Culture, and more than sixty prominent figures from the Chinese scientific and cultural circles.
    At the meeting, President Guo Moruo first extended a warm welcome on behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chinese scientists to the Soviet cultural delegation's visit to China, saying: "Our country has made great achievements in all aspects of construction in the past five years, but any achievement is inseparable from the selfless assistance of our elder brother—the great Soviet Union. Here, I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude on behalf of the Chinese scientific workers and the Chinese people."
    Next, Denisov, the head of the Soviet cultural delegation, spoke. He said: "We are very pleased and happy to see that the experience of the Soviet people in economic and cultural construction is increasingly becoming a model for the construction of New China. The Soviet people, like our Chinese brothers, will continue to strengthen our friendship and continue to develop economic and cultural cooperation between our two peoples in the future."
    Academician Ostrovityanov, Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and a member of the Soviet cultural delegation, delivered a congratulatory speech to the Chinese Academy of Sciences on behalf of the Soviet Academy of Sciences: "On this day of the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we, the Soviet scholars, sincerely congratulate our dear friends, the Chinese scholars, on their further achievements on the scientific and cultural fronts, in the noble cause of serving the people, and in the cause of striving for peace, building socialism, and making their motherland strong for the benefit of all the people."
    He then presented many valuable Soviet scientific, historical, and economic books to the Chinese Academy of Sciences on behalf of the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences.
    Pankratova, a member of the Soviet cultural delegation and a representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, warmly congratulated the Chinese scientists on behalf of the Soviet scientists.
    Li Siguang, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, expressed his heartfelt gratitude on behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences for the gifts and warm congratulations from the Soviet Academy of Sciences. He said: "We will repay your kindness by diligently studying advanced Soviet science and by serving the people and socialist construction with science."
    Chen Boda, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, expressed his deep gratitude to the Soviet scientific community on behalf of the Chinese scientific community. He pointed out that learning from the advanced scientific experience of the Soviet Union is the task of Chinese scientists, and he hoped that the Soviet cultural delegation would offer valuable opinions and criticisms on China's scientific work.
    Alexandrov also spoke at the meeting.
    Then, Soviet and Chinese scientists held separate discussions.

### 中国科学院院长郭沫若举行茶会  欢迎苏联文化代表团

1954-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院院长郭沫若举行茶会
    欢迎苏联文化代表团
    【新华社四日讯】中国科学院院长郭沫若在四日下午举行茶会，欢迎应邀来我国参加国庆节观礼的苏联文化代表团。
    苏联文化代表团团长杰尼索夫和全体团员都应邀出席。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金院士、苏联政府代表团团员苏联文化部部长亚历山德罗夫院士，也应邀出席。
    出席茶会的有：中国科学院副院长陈伯达、李四光、张稼夫、陶孟和、吴有训，高等教育部部长杨秀峰、文化部副部长郑振铎和中国著名科学文化界人士等六十多人。
    会上，郭沫若院长首先代表中国科学院和中国科学工作者向苏联文化代表团来我国访问表示热烈的欢迎后说：“五年来我们的国家在各方面的建设都有很大的成就，但任何成就都和我们的老大哥——伟大的苏联的无私的帮助是分不开的，我在这里谨代表中国科学工作者和中国人民表示衷心的感谢。”
    接着，苏联文化代表团团长杰尼索夫讲话。他说：“当我们看到苏联人民在经济和文化建设方面的经验日益成为新中国建设的榜样时，感到十分愉快和高兴。苏联人民同我们的中国兄弟一样，今后还要继续加强我们的友谊，继续发展我们两国人民之间的经济和文化的合作。”
    苏联文化代表团团员、苏联科学院副院长奥斯特罗维季扬诺夫院士代表苏联科学院向中国科学院致祝词说：“当中华人民共和国成立五周年的今天，我们——苏联学者们，衷心祝贺我们亲爱的朋友——中国的学者们在科学和文化的阵线上，在为人民服务的崇高事业中，在为争取和平、建设社会主义、谋取全民的福利而使自己祖国强大的事业中，获得进一步的成就。”
    接着他代表苏联科学院主席团把许多珍贵的苏联科学、历史和经济书籍赠送给中国科学院。
    苏联文化代表团团员、苏联最高苏维埃代表潘克拉托娃代表苏联科学家向中国科学家们热烈祝贺。
    中国科学院副院长李四光代表中国科学院对苏联科学院赠送的礼品和热烈的祝贺表示衷心感谢。他说：“我们将以努力学习苏联先进科学，以科学服务于人民，服务于社会主义建设来报答你们的盛意。”
    中国科学院副院长陈伯达代表中国科学界向苏联科学界表示深切感谢，他指出学习苏联先进科学经验是我国科学家的任务，并希望苏联文化代表团对我国的科学工作提出宝贵的意见和批评。
    亚历山德罗夫也在会上讲了话。
    然后，苏联和中国的科学家们分别进行座谈。
