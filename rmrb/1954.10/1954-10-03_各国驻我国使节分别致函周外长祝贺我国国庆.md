Title: Envoys from Various Countries to China Send Letters to Foreign Minister Zhou to Congratulate China's National Day

Author:
Date: 1954-10-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. To celebrate the fifth anniversary of the founding of our country, National Day, Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Dimov, Romanian Ambassador to China, Guo Zuowen, Hungarian Ambassador to China, Hickladen, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Cui Yi, Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Konsala, Polish Ambassador to China, Kirillok, Mongolian Ambassador to China, Ochirbat, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, König, Albanian Ambassador to China, Nasse, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Hoang Van Hoan, Swiss Minister to China, Benuyi, Danish Minister to China, Graedisen, British Chargé d'affaires, Du William, Norwegian Negotiating Representative, Timothy, and Dutch Negotiating Representative, Sima, sent letters to our Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Enlai, to express their congratulations.

Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Konsala, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Choi Il, and Pakistani Ambassador to China, Rocha, also sent letters to Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, and Chairman Liu Shaoqi to express their congratulations.

### 各国驻我国使节分别致函周外长祝贺我国国庆

1954-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    各国驻我国使节分别致函周外长祝贺我国国庆
      【新华社三十日讯】为庆贺我国成立五周年国庆，保加利亚驻华大使迪莫夫、罗马尼亚驻华大使郭佐文、匈牙利驻华大使希克拉丹、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使崔
  一、捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使康萨拉、波兰驻华大使基里洛克、蒙古驻华大使奥其尔巴特、德意志民主共和国驻华大使柯尼希、阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使纳赛、越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢、瑞士驻华公使贝努义、丹麦驻华公使格瑞杰生、英国代办杜维廉、挪威谈判代表季茂登、荷兰谈判代表司曼、分别致函我外交部部长周恩来表示祝贺。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使康萨拉、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使崔一、巴基斯坦驻华大使罗查并分别致函毛泽东主席、朱德副主席和刘少奇委员长表示祝贺。
