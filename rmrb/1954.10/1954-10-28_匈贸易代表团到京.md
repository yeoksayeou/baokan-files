Title: Hungarian Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: A trade delegation from the Hungarian People's Republic, led by the head of the delegation, Comrade Dróba Gusztáv, Deputy Minister of Domestic and Foreign Trade of the Hungarian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing on the 27th.

Those who went to the airport to greet them included Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of China, Zhang Huadong, Director of the Import Bureau, Jia Shi, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau, Luo Baoyi, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau, and Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department.

Wang Hanmin, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Hungarian People's Republic, also went to the airport to greet them.

Nagy, Commercial Counselor, and Csatórdai, Secretary, of the Hungarian Embassy in China also went to the airport to greet them.

### 匈贸易代表团到京

1954-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    匈贸易代表团到京
    【新华社二十七日讯】匈牙利人民共和国贸易代表团，由团长、匈牙利人民共和国政府国内和对外贸易部副部长德洛巴·古斯达夫率领，于二十七日到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有我对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒、进口局局长张化东、第二局副局长贾石、出口局副局长罗抱一、交际处处长杨勉等人。
    到机场迎接的还有我驻匈牙利人民共和国大使馆商务参赞王汉民。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆商务参赞那吉、秘书察托尔道伊等亦前往机场迎接。
