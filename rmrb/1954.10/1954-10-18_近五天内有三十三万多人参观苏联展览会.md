Title: Over 330,000 People Visit Soviet Exhibition in the Past Five Days
Author:
Date: 1954-10-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 17th: From October 13th to 17th, another 330,000+ people visited the Exhibition of Achievements in Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction, with an average of over 60,000 visitors each day. Many people came from Tianjin, Hebei, Shanxi, and other places specifically to visit the exhibition. Art workers from Tianjin Municipality made a special trip to visit the art works hall of the exhibition, with many people watching for two consecutive days. More than 1,000 students from over 40 ethnic groups at the Central Nationalities Institute also visited the exhibition on the 16th.

    On the morning of October 17th, Soviet experts and representatives of the installation team held a technical learning symposium. In the afternoon of the same day, some workers from the Beijing No. 1 Machine Tool Plant held a forum in the exhibition hall, and the workers expressed their impressions of the visit.

    The Soviet National Folk Dance Troupe has been performing at the open-air theater in the Soviet Exhibition Hall since the 14th. More than 10,000 people have enjoyed Soviet folk dance art here. The film hall screened the Soviet science education film "Advanced Square Cultivation Method" and the news documentary "The Outskirts of Moscow," etc.

### 近五天内有三十三万多人参观苏联展览会

1954-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    近五天内有三十三万多人参观苏联展览会
    【新华社十七日讯】从十三日到十七日，又有三十三万多人参观了苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会，每天参观人数平均在六万人以上。有不少人是从天津、河北、山西等地赶来参观的。天津市的美术工作者特意到展览会的美术作品馆来参观，许多人接连看了两天。中央民族学院四十多个民族的一千多名学生也在十六日参观了展览会。
    十七日上午，苏联专家和安装大队工人代表举行了技术学习座谈会；当日下午又有北京第一机床厂的部分工人在展览馆里召开了座谈会，工人们纷纷发表了他们参观的感想。
    苏联国立民间舞蹈团十四日起在苏联展览馆内露天剧场演出。有一万余人在这里欣赏了苏联民间舞蹈艺术。电影馆放映了苏联科学教育影片“先进的方形耕作法”和新闻纪录片“莫斯科的郊区”等。
