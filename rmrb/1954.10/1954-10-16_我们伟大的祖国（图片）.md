Author:
Date: 1954-10-16

    Our Great Motherland
    Workers at the Fengtai Locomotive Depot of the Beijing Railway Bureau warmly support the communique of the Sino-Soviet talks and express their determination to learn better from the Soviet Union. This is the crew of the "Sino-Soviet Friendship" locomotive actively learning the advanced experience of the Soviet "Lunin locomotive maintenance method."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京铁路管理局丰台机务段工人热烈拥护中苏两国举行会谈的公报，并表示要更好地向苏联学习，这是“中苏友好号”机车的乘务员在积极学习苏联“鲁宁机车保养法”的先进经验。
