Title: Malenkov Receives Head of Indian Cultural Delegation Visiting the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-10-08
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th: TASS Moscow reported: Georgy Maximilianovich Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, received Mrs. M. Chandrasekhar, Deputy Minister of Health of India and Member of Parliament, head of the Indian cultural and artistic workers delegation visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Soviet Ministry of Culture, on the 6th.
    Present at the reception was Andrei Andreyevich Gromyko, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.
    Also present at the reception was P.N. Haksar, First Secretary of the Indian Embassy in the Soviet Union.

### 马林科夫接见访苏的印度文化代表团团长

1954-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    马林科夫接见访苏的印度文化代表团团长
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫六日接见了应苏联文化部的邀请来苏联访问的印度文化和艺术工作者代表团团长、印度卫生部副部长、国会议员马·钱德拉塞卡尔夫人。
    接见时在座的有苏联外交部第一副部长安·安·葛罗米柯。
    接见时，印度驻苏联大使馆一等秘书普·纳·高尔也在座。
