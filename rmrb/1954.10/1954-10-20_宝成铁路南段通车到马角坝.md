Title: Southern Section of Baoji-Chengdu Railway Opens to Majiaoba
Author:
Date: 1954-10-20
Page: 2

    Southern Section of Baoji-Chengdu Railway Opens to Majiaoba
    Xinhua News Agency: The temporary operating line of the southern section of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway was extended 57 kilometers forward from Zhongba to Majiaoba Station in Jiangyou County on October 15. Thus, the southern section of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway has now opened to traffic for 215 kilometers.
    Majiaoba is located on the edge of the Jianmen Mountains. Currently, one pair of mixed trains runs between Chengdu and Majiaoba every day. The daily necessities, salt, cloth, and means of production needed by the people in the mountainous areas of Jiangyou County will be supplied in a timely manner. Iron produced in Majiaoba and wood produced in Yuanba will be directly transported to Chengdu, Chongqing, and other places to support national construction.

### 宝成铁路南段通车到马角坝

1954-10-20
第2版()
专栏：

    宝成铁路南段通车到马角坝
    宝成铁路南段临时营业线路，十月十五日由中坝向前伸延五十七公里到江油县的马角坝车站。至此，宝成铁路南段已通车二百一十五公里。
    马角坝位于剑门山区的边缘。现在，每天成都—马角坝开行混合列车一对。江油县山区人民需要的日用百货、食盐、布匹及生产资料，将会得到及时的供应。马角坝出产的铁和原坝出产的木材等，将直接运到成都、重庆等地，支援国家建设。（新华社）
