Title: Central Government Directly Subordinate Organs and Beijing Municipal Organs Cadres Begin Studying People's Congress Documents
Author:
Date: 1954-10-15
Page: 1

    Central Government Directly Subordinate Organs and Beijing Municipal Organs Cadres
    Begin Studying People's Congress Documents
    [This Newspaper News] The cadres of the organs directly under the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the organs directly under the Central People's Government, and the organs of Beijing Municipality, have begun studying Chairman Mao Zedong's opening address at the First Session of the First National People's Congress, Comrade Liu Shaoqi's "Report on the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China," and Premier Zhou Enlai's "Government Work Report" since October 4.
    In this study, the cadres of various departments will further understand the great historical significance of the First Session of the First National People's Congress, and understand the great role of the Constitution in promoting China's socialist construction; strengthen the concept of abiding by the Constitution, laws, and discipline, and improve their political enthusiasm and work enthusiasm. The Propaganda Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China issued a notice requiring on-the-job cadres to study the basic content of the Constitution in depth, and the problems that were not resolved during the past study and discussion of the draft Constitution should be focused on and resolved; it requires a comprehensive understanding of the content of the "Government Work Report" during the study, and should focus on studying the parts related to the work of the department, so as to deepen the understanding in connection with the work.
    In order to help cadres study these documents in depth and make this study more effective, all units have formulated specific plans and methods. The Working Committee of the Organs Directly under the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China stipulates that at the beginning of the study, the party organizations of major units should invite the responsible comrades of the unit or the representatives of the First National People's Congress to make a briefing report. Many units of the Central People's Government are prepared to invite the ministers and vice-ministers of their departments who attended or attended the meeting as observers to make a briefing report. The Propaganda Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China will also hold a report meeting. Some of the cadres of the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China listened to the briefing report made by Comrade Zeng San, Deputy Secretary of the Working Committee of the Organs Directly under the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Representative of the First National People's Congress, on the 13th. Zhang Linzhi, Vice Minister of the Second Ministry of Machine Building Industry of the Central People's Government, and Gao Kelin, Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, have also made briefing reports and mobilization reports to the cadres of their units. Some units plan to organize reports again one or two weeks after the study, combined with the problems raised by the cadres of the organs. For staff members who have not participated in theoretical study, special personnel are designated to make briefing reports to them. The district committees of various districts and the party committees of various organs and factories in Beijing have respectively formulated specific plans for study.
    Some units have combined the business work of their departments and the ideological situation of cadres in the study, and put forward the key points of the cadres' study in their organs. The cadres of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs conducted in-depth research on the parts of the documents related to foreign policy and foreign trade during the study. The Party Committee of the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau, the Beijing Agricultural Machinery Factory and other units have studied how to connect with reality when studying the "Government Work Report."

### 中央直属机关和北京市各机关干部  开始学习人民代表大会文件

1954-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中央直属机关和北京市各机关干部
    开始学习人民代表大会文件
    【本报讯】中共中央直属机关、中央人民政府直属机关和北京市的机关干部，从十月四日起开始学习毛泽东主席在第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议上的开幕词、刘少奇同志“关于中华人民共和国宪法草案的报告”和周恩来总理的“政府工作报告”。
    各部门的干部在这次学习中，将进一步了解第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的伟大历史意义，了解宪法对促进我国社会主义建设事业的巨大作用；加强遵守宪法、法律和纪律的观念，提高政治积极性和工作积极性。中共北京市委宣传部发出通知，要求在职干部深入学习宪法的基本内容，对于过去学习和讨论宪法草案时没有解决的问题，应该着重学习并加以解决；要求在学习“政府工作报告”时，应对报告内容作全面的了解，并应着重学习和本部门工作有关的部分，以便联系工作，加深体会。
    为帮助干部深入地学习这些文件，使这次学习收到更好的效果，各单位都制订了具体的计划和办法。中共中央直属机关委员会规定：在学习开始时，由各大单位党的组织请本单位负责同志或第一届全国人民代表大会的代表作传达报告。中央人民政府的许多单位都准备聘请本部门出席或列席这次会议的部长、副部长作传达报告。中共北京市委宣传部也将举办报告会。中共中央办公厅的一部分机关干部，已在十三日听了第一届全国人民代表大会代表、中共中央直属机关委员会副书记曾三同志的传达报告。中央人民政府第二机械工业部副部长张霖之、最高人民检察院副检察长高克林也已向所属单位的干部作了传达报告和动员报告。有的单位拟在学习进行一二周以后，结合机关干部所提出的问题，再组织报告。对未参加理论学习的工作人员，指定专人对他们作传达报告。北京市各区区委和各机关、工厂党委已分别拟订了学习的具体计划。
    有些单位在学习中结合本部门的业务工作和干部的思想情况，提出了本机关干部学习的重点。外交部干部在学习中对文件中有关外交政策和对外贸易部分进行深入的研究。北京电信局党委、北京农业机械厂等单位都研究了在学习“政府工作报告”时如何联系实际的问题。
