Title: Korean Government Delegation Returns to Pyongyang from Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-10-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th: The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, who was invited to Beijing to attend the celebration of the 5th anniversary of the founding of our country, returned to Pyongyang by special plane on the afternoon of the 5th.

Those who welcomed them at Pyongyang Airport included Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Park Jung-ai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Choe Chang-ik and Hong Myung-hee, Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il, and representatives from all walks of life.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Soviet Ambassador to Korea, V.N. Razuvayev, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Korea, Gan Yetao, and diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from other countries stationed in Korea.

### 朝鲜政府代表团从我国回到平壤

1954-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团从我国回到平壤
    【新华社平壤六日电】应邀到北京参加我国建国五周年国庆典礼的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团一行在团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅率领下，在五日下午乘专机返抵平壤。
    到平壤机场欢迎的有朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，内阁副首相崔昌益、洪命熹，外务相南日和各界代表等。
    到机场欢迎的还有苏联驻朝鲜大使苏兹达列夫，中华人民共和国驻朝鲜大使馆代办甘野陶和其他各国驻朝鲜的外交使节和外交官员。
