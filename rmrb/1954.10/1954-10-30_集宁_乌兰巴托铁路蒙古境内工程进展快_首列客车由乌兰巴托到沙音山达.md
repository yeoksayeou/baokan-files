Title: Progress of the Jining-Ulaanbaatar Railway Project in Mongolia is Rapid; First Passenger Train Travels from Ulaanbaatar to Sainshand
Author:
Date: 1954-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar dispatch, October 29th: The first passenger train departing from Ulaanbaatar arrived in Sainshand, the center of the East Gobi Aimak (province), on October 28th, according to TASS. This city is not far from the Chinese border and is located on the Jining-Ulaanbaatar railway line connecting the three fraternal countries of the Soviet Union, Mongolia, and China.

The first passenger train to arrive in Sainshand carried: Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic; Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural; Lhamsuren, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; Zhanchibu, Minister of Internal Affairs; and Idankhabu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic.

Also arriving in Sainshand on the train were Soviet Ambassador Pisarev and Chinese Ambassador He Ying.

Many construction workers and representatives of the working people of the East Gobi Aimak held a rally in the station square. Speakers at the rally included Sumiya, Secretary of the East Gobi Aimak Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and Davaagsanbu, Chairman of the "New Life" Herdsmen's Production Cooperative. They congratulated the railway builders and thanked the Soviet Union for helping the Mongolian people build the railway. The construction workers pledged to extend the railway to the Chinese border ahead of schedule, enabling direct through traffic between the three countries in 1955.

### 集宁——乌兰巴托铁路蒙古境内工程进展快  首列客车由乌兰巴托到沙音山达

1954-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    集宁——乌兰巴托铁路蒙古境内工程进展快
    首列客车由乌兰巴托到沙音山达
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：十月二十八日，从乌兰巴托开出的第一列铁路客车到达了东戈壁艾马克（省）的中心沙音山达城。这个城离中国边境不远，位置在联结苏、蒙、中三个兄弟国家的集宁到乌兰巴托的铁路线上。
    到达沙音山达的第一列客车里载着：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记拉姆苏伦，内务部部长占奇布，蒙古人民共和国外交部副部长伊丹甲布。
    乘车到达沙音山达的还有苏联大使皮萨列夫和中华人民共和国大使何英。
    许多筑路工人和东戈壁艾马克的劳动人民代表在车站广场上举行了集会，在会上演说的有蒙古人民革命党东戈壁艾马克书记苏米亚和“新生活”牧民生产合作社主席达瓦格桑布，他们祝贺铁路建筑者并感谢苏联援助蒙古人民建筑铁路。筑路工人保证提前把铁路通到中国边境，使一九五五年三国间可以直接通车。
