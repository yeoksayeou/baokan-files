Title: Signing of the Protocol of the Sino-Romanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-10-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The second session of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technical Cooperation was held in Beijing from September 27th to October 15th. After sincere and friendly negotiations between China and Romania, the protocol of the second session of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technical Cooperation was signed on October 15th.

Representing the Chinese side in signing the protocol was Li Renjun, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the People's Republic of China. Representing the Romanian People's Republic in signing the protocol was Emil Stanciu, Chairman of the Romanian Group of the Committee and First Vice Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the Romanian People's Republic.

Also present at the signing ceremony from the Chinese side were: Zhang Qinqiu, Vice Minister of Textile Industry; Song Shaowen, Vice Minister of Light Industry; Zhang Zike, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Romanian People's Republic; Guan Hantao, Deputy Director of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the State Planning Commission; Chang Yanqing, Deputy Director of the Complete Equipment Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Dong Xibai, Chief Secretary of the Treaty Committee.

Also present at the signing ceremony were: members of the Romanian delegation attending the second session of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technical Cooperation, Meng Tiannu, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Romanian People's Republic Embassy in China, and An Ding, Economic Counselor.

### 中罗科学技术合作委员会会议议定书签字

1954-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

     中罗科学技术合作委员会会议议定书签字
    【新华社十六日讯】　中罗科学和技术合作联合委员会第二届会议在九月二十七日到十月十五日在北京举行。经过中罗双方真诚友好地谈判，已在十月十五日签订了中罗科学和技术合作联合委员会第二届会议议定书。
    代表中国方面在议定书上签字的，是委员会中国组主席中华人民共和国燃料工业部副部长李人俊。代表罗马尼亚人民共和国方面在议定书上签字的，是委员会罗马尼亚组主席罗马尼亚人民共和国国家计划委员会第一副主席埃米尔·斯坦裘。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面还有：纺织工业部副部长张琴秋，轻工业部副部长宋劭文，我国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使馆商务参赞张子克，国家计划委员会技术合作局副局长管寒涛，对外贸易部成套设备局副局长常彦卿，外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇，条约委员会主任秘书董希白等人。
    参加签字仪式的还有：出席中罗科学和技术合作联合委员会第二届会议的罗马尼亚代表团人员，罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办孟天努和经济参赞安定。
