Title: Calcutta Mayor Mukherjee of India Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 15th—Mr. Mukherjee, the Mayor of Calcutta, India, arrived in Beijing on the 15th at the invitation of Peng Zhen, the Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, for a visit to Beijing.

Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Chai Zemin, Deputy Secretary-General, and Ma Zhenwu, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office, welcomed him at the airport.

### 印度加尔各答市市长慕克吉到达北京

1954-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    印度加尔各答市市长慕克吉到达北京
    【新华社十五日讯】应北京市人民政府市长彭真的邀请来北京访问的印度加尔各答市市长慕克吉先生，十五日到达北京。
    北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，副秘书长柴泽民，外事处处长马振武等到机场欢迎。
