Title: Soviet Government Delegation Leaves Berlin for Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 14th: The Soviet Government Delegation, led by V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Soviet Foreign Minister, who was invited to attend the celebrations of the 5th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, left Berlin for Moscow on the 13th.

Those who went to Berlin-Schönefeld Airport to see them off included: Dieckmann, Chairman of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic; Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic; Ulbricht, Deputy Premier of the German Democratic and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, as well as senior officials of the Government of the German Democratic Republic, the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, various democratic parties, and mass organizations.

Also present at the airport to see them off were: the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, the Government Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, who had come to attend the 5th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, and diplomatic envoys of various countries stationed in Germany.

Also present at the airport to see them off were: Pushkin, Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, and staff of the embassy, and General Grechko, Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Forces in Germany, and representatives of the Soviet Army.

Molotov delivered a speech at the airport. He first expressed his gratitude to the government of the German Democratic Republic, the working people of the German Democratic Republic and its capital for the warm welcome and gracious hospitality extended to the Soviet Government Delegation.

He said: During these days, we had the opportunity to visit factories, mines, agricultural production cooperatives, scientific institutions, and other cultural institutions, and to talk with workers, farmers, and social and cultural workers. We are particularly grateful to our German friends for these cordial meetings and conversations.

Molotov said: We have seen that people everywhere are working hard to strive for economic and cultural upsurges and have achieved significant victories in this regard. We have seen that the well-being of workers, farmers, and working intellectuals is constantly improving.

Molotov said: The German people's desire to restore German unity on the basis of peace and democracy is very strong. We are convinced that this desire will receive strong support from all peace-loving people. We are convinced that the friendship between the German people and the Soviet people is growing and consolidating day by day.

Finally, Molotov wished the peaceful and democratic forces of the German people unity in the struggle to restore German unity, and wished the friendship between the peoples of the Soviet Union and Germany, the bulwark of international peace and security, to grow and consolidate day by day.

### 苏联政府代表团离柏林回莫斯科

1954-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联政府代表团离柏林回莫斯科
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：应邀参加德意志民主共和国成立五周年国庆典礼的苏联政府代表团，十三日在代表团团长、苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫率领下，离柏林返回莫斯科。
    前往柏林什湼菲尔德机场欢送的有：德意志民主共和国人民议院主席狄克曼，德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥，德意志民主共和国副总理、德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希以及德意志民主共和国政府、德国统一社会党、各民主党派和群众组织的高级负责人员。
    前往机场欢送的还有：前来参加德意志民主共和国成立五周年国庆典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团，以及各国驻德外交使节。
    前往机场欢送的还有：苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使普希金和大使馆工作人员，苏联驻德国军队总司令格列奇科大将和苏军代表们。
    莫洛托夫在机场发表了谈话。他首先向德意志民主共和国政府、德意志民主共和国及其首都劳动人民对苏联政府代表团的热烈欢迎和殷勤招待表示谢意。
    他说：在这些日子里，我们有机会参观工厂、矿井、农业生产合作社、科学机关和其他文化机关，并和工人、农民以及社会和文化工作者交谈。我们特别为这些亲切的会晤和交谈向我们的德国朋友表示感激。
  莫洛托夫说：我们看到人民到处在刻苦努力，以争取经济和文化的高涨，并且在这方面获得了重大的胜利。我们看到工人、农民和劳动知识分子的福利正在不断提高。
    莫洛托夫说：德国人民要求在和平和民主的基础上恢复德国统一的愿望是非常强烈的。我们确信，这种愿望将得到一切爱好和平的人民的强有力的支持。我们确信，德国人民对苏联人民的友情正在日益增长和巩固。
    最后，莫洛托夫祝德国人民的和平和民主力量在争取恢复德国统一的斗争中团结一致，祝国际和平和安全的堡垒——苏德两国人民的友谊日益增长和巩固。
