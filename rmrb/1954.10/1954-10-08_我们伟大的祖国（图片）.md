    Our Great Motherland
    The Xiejiaji No. 2 vertical shaft, built by the Huainan Mining Bureau this year, is one of the larger vertical shafts built in coal mines this year. After this mine is put into production, the annual output of the Huainan Coal Mine will increase by one-third compared to this year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    淮南矿务局今年建设的谢家集二号竖井，是今年煤矿建设中规模较大的竖井之一。这座矿井投入生产后，淮南煤矿的年产量将比今年增加三分之一。
