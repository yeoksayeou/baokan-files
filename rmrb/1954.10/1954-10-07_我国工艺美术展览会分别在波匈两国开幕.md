Title: Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition Opens in Poland and Hungary
Author:
Date: 1954-10-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 5th: The "Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition" opened on the 4th at the Zachęta National Gallery of Art in Warsaw. The arts and crafts on display include: porcelain from Jingdezhen, various embroideries, silk, cotton, and wool textiles, various woven products and carvings, clay sculptures, and metal utensils.

    Among those attending the opening ceremony were Polish Deputy Minister of Culture and Art, Wierczyński, Secretary-General of the Polish Committee for Cultural Cooperation with Foreign Countries, Wind, and representatives of the cultural and artistic circles of Warsaw, totaling more than 200 people.

    Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Zeng Yongquan, also attended the opening ceremony.

    Wind cut the ribbon for the opening of the exhibition. There was a large and enthusiastic turnout after the opening.

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 4th: Budapest dispatch: The People's Republic of China Arts and Crafts Exhibition opened in Budapest on October 1st.

    Among those attending the opening ceremony were: Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, Dániel Nagy, Speaker of the National Assembly, Rónai, Minister of Culture, Dárvas, First Deputy Minister, János, Deputy Minister, Mihályfi, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Sík, as well as representatives from all walks of life and art workers, totaling more than 200 people.

    Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Hungary, Zhang Shijie, and diplomatic envoys from various countries also attended the opening ceremony.

    At the opening ceremony, Hungarian Minister of Culture, Dárvas, delivered a speech first. He pointed out: The exhibits in this exhibition show the high level of Chinese folk art and arts and crafts. It will not only arouse the interest of the broad masses of the Hungarian people, but also provide Hungarian artists with much to learn. At the same time, this exhibition will also help to deepen the friendly relations between the Chinese and Hungarian peoples.

    Zhang Shijie then delivered a speech. He introduced the significance and content of the exhibition, and mentioned the "Hungarian Folk Art Exhibition" held in Beijing, China, not long ago. He said that the two similar exhibitions held by each of us will help consolidate and develop the unity and friendship between the Chinese and Hungarian peoples and the cultural and artistic exchanges between the two peoples.

    Those present praised the various exquisite exhibits on display.

### 我国工艺美术展览会分别在波匈两国开幕

1954-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    我国工艺美术展览会分别在波匈两国开幕
   【新华社华沙五日电】“中国工艺美术展览会”四日在华沙的萨希达大厦开幕。展览会展出的工艺美术品有：景德镇的磁器，各种刺绣，丝、棉、毛等纺织品，各种编织品和雕刻，泥塑和金属器皿等。
    出席开幕式的有波兰文化艺术部副部长维尔契克，波兰对外文化合作委员会秘书长温德，和华沙文化艺术界的代表共二百多人。
    中国驻波兰大使曾涌泉也参加了开幕式。
    展览会由温德剪彩。开幕后，参观的人很踊跃。
    【新华社布拉格四日电】布达佩斯消息：中华人民共和国工艺美术展览会十月一日在布达佩斯开幕。
    出席开幕式的有：匈牙利人民共和国主席团副主席但·纳吉，国民议会议长罗奈伊，文化部部长达尔瓦斯、第一副部长雅诺希、副部长米哈伊费，外交部副部长西克，以及各界代表和美术工作者等二百多人。
    中华人民共和国驻匈牙利大使馆临时代办张世杰和各国外交使节也出席了开幕式。
    在开幕式上，匈牙利文化部部长达尔瓦斯首先致词。他指出：这个展览会的展品显示出了中国民间艺术和工艺美术的高度水平。它不仅会引起匈牙利广大群众的兴趣，而且会给匈牙利的艺术工作者很多可以学习的东西。同时，这个展览会也将有助于加深匈中两国人民的友好关系。
    张世杰接着致词。他介绍了展览会的意义和内容，并提到了不久前在中国北京举办的“匈牙利民间艺术展览会”。他说，我们互相举办的两个同类的展览会，将有助于巩固和发展中匈两国人民的团结友好及两国人民之间的文化艺术交流。
    到会者对展览会所展出的各种精美展品都称赞不已。
