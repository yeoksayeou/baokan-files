Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-10-23
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    China's first domestically produced new-type passenger ship, the "Minzhong" (People) is already sailing on the Yangtze River. Passengers enjoy a pleasant and comfortable life during the voyage. This is the scene of passengers leaning on the railing, overlooking the emerging industrial city of Huangshi along the Yangtze River.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国自制的第一艘新型大客轮——民众号，已经开始在长江中航行。旅客们在航行中过着愉快、舒适的生活。这是旅客们凭栏眺望长江沿岸新兴的工业城市——黄石市的情形。
