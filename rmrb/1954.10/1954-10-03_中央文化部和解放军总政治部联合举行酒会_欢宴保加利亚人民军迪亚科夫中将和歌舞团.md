Title: Ministry of Culture and PLA General Political Department Jointly Host Reception to Welcome Lieutenant General Dyakov of the Bulgarian People's Army and Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1954-10-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd. The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army jointly held a reception at noon on the 2nd to bid farewell to Lieutenant General Dyakov of the Bulgarian People's Army and the Song and Dance Troupe of the Bulgarian People's Army, who are about to leave Beijing for sightseeing and performances in various places.

The reception was hosted by Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, and Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Lieutenant General Dyakov and all members of the People's Army Song and Dance Troupe attended the reception.

At the beginning of the reception, after a brief speech by Minister Shen Yanbing, he presented gifts to Lieutenant General Dyakov and the People's Army Song and Dance Troupe on behalf of the Ministry of Culture and the General Political Department. Deputy Director Xiao Hua presented commemorative medals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to Lieutenant General Dyakov and seven other leading cadres on behalf of the People's Liberation Army. Lieutenant General Dyakov and the leading comrades of the People's Army Song and Dance Troupe repeatedly raised their glasses to thank the Chinese hosts for their warm hospitality during their stay in Beijing.

Lieutenant General Dyakov and the People's Army Song and Dance Troupe are scheduled to leave Beijing on the 3rd for sightseeing and performances in various places.

### 中央文化部和解放军总政治部联合举行酒会  欢宴保加利亚人民军迪亚科夫中将和歌舞团

1954-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中央文化部和解放军总政治部联合举行酒会
    欢宴保加利亚人民军迪亚科夫中将和歌舞团
    【新华社二日讯】中华人民共和国文化部和中国人民解放军总政治部在二日中午联合举行酒会，欢送即将离京赴各地参观、演出的保加利亚人民军迪亚科夫中将和保加利亚人民军歌舞团。
    酒会由文化部沈雁冰部长和中国人民解放军总政治部萧华副主任主持。迪亚科夫中将和人民军歌舞团全体人员都出席了酒会。
    酒会开始，沈雁冰部长在简短地致词以后，代表文化部和总政治部向迪亚科夫中将和人民军歌舞团赠送了礼品；萧华副主任代表人民解放军送给迪亚科夫中将等七位领导干部以中国人民解放军纪念章。迪亚科夫中将和保人民军歌舞团各领导同志一再举杯答谢中国主人对他们在北京期间的殷勤款待。
    迪亚科夫中将和人民军歌舞团定于三日离京赴各地参观、演出。
