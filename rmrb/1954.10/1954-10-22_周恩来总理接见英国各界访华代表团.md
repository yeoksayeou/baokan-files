Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives British Delegation from All Circles

Author:
Date: 1954-10-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 21st: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, received on the afternoon of the 21st the British delegation to China consisting of members of parliament, trade union workers, cooperative workers, scientists, teachers, and artists, headed by Ellis Smith and George Lin Green, as well as Stanley Spencer and John Dre Chinare, representatives of the British literary and scientific circles.

Present at the meeting were: Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Ding Xilin, Yang Hansheng, and Hong Shen, Vice-Presidents of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Chen Zhongjing, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; and Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China.

### 周恩来总理接见英国各界访华代表团

1954-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见英国各界访华代表团
     【新华社二十一日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在二十一日下午接见了以埃利斯·史密斯、乔治·林格林为首的英国议员、工会工作者、合作社工作者、科学家、教师和艺术家访华代表团，以及英国文艺、科学界代表团斯坦莱·斯潘塞和约翰·德莱·奇纳莱。
    陪见的有：中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林、阳翰笙、洪深、秘书长陈忠经，中华人民共和国外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华。
