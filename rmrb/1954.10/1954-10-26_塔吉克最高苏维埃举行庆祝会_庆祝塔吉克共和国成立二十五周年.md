Title: Tajik Supreme Soviet Holds Celebration Meeting to Celebrate the 25th Anniversary of the Founding of the Tajik Republic
Author:
Date: 1954-10-26
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, October 25th) According to TASS: On the 23rd, the Supreme Soviet of the Tajik Republic held a celebration meeting in Stalinabad, the capital of Tajikistan, to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Tajik Republic. The meeting was attended by representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the Tajik Republic, advanced workers in industry and agriculture, people from the scientific, cultural, and artistic circles, and delegations from various Soviet republics.

D. Rasulov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Tajik Republic, delivered a commemorative report at the meeting. He spoke in detail about the achievements of the Tajik people under the leadership of the Communist Party in developing Tajikistan's national economy and culture. He also spoke of the great assistance given to Tajikistan by the great Russian nation and other fraternal nationalities of the Soviet Union.

Also speaking at the meeting were A.N. Kosygin, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and heads of delegations from various fraternal republics of the Soviet Union. When Kosygin read out the congratulatory telegram from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the attendees burst into stormy cheers.

The attendees passed a message of respect to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union with great excitement.

### 塔吉克最高苏维埃举行庆祝会  庆祝塔吉克共和国成立二十五周年

1954-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    塔吉克最高苏维埃举行庆祝会庆祝塔吉克共和国成立二十五周年
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社消息：二十三日，塔吉克共和国最高苏维埃在塔吉克首都斯大林纳巴德举行庆祝会，庆祝塔吉克共和国成立二十五周年。到会的有塔吉克共和国最高苏维埃的代表，工业和农业的先进工作者，科学、文化和艺术界人士，以及苏联各加盟共和国的代表团。
    塔吉克共和国部长会议主席德·拉苏洛夫在会上作了纪念报告。他详细地讲到了塔吉克人民在共产党的领导下，在发展塔吉克的国民经济和文化方面所获得的成就。他还谈到了伟大的俄罗斯民族以及苏联其他兄弟民族给予塔吉克的巨大帮助。
    在会上讲话的还有苏联部长会议副主席柯西金和苏联各兄弟共和国代表团团长等。当柯西金在宣读苏联最高苏维埃主席团、苏联部长会议和苏联共产党中央委员会的贺电时，到会者发出了暴风雨般的欢呼。
    到会者极其兴奋地通过了给苏联最高苏维埃、苏联部长会议和苏联共产党中央委员会的致敬电。
