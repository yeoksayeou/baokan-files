Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Chairman Groza
Author:
Date: 1954-10-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, on the afternoon of the 29th.
    Present at the reception were Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, and Song Qingling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China.
    After the reception, they had dinner together.

### 毛泽东主席接见格罗查主席

1954-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见格罗查主席
    【新华社二十九日讯】中华人民共和国毛泽东主席在二十九日下午接见了罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查博士。
    接见时在座的有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈云、邓小平和中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄。
    接见后并共进晚餐。
