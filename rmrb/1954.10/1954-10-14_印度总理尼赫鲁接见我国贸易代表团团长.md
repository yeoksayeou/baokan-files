Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru Receives Head of Chinese Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-10-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 13th — Indian Prime Minister Nehru received Kong Yuan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China and Head of the Chinese Trade Delegation, and Yang Lin, Deputy Head of the Delegation, on the morning of the 12th. Shen Jian, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in India, was present at the reception.

    During the conversation, Prime Minister Nehru stated that economic and trade relations between China and India should be further developed. Prime Minister Nehru also expressed great interest in China's industrial construction. Head of Delegation Kong Yuan, on behalf of the Chinese Trade Delegation, expressed gratitude to the Indian government and people for their welcome, hospitality, and concern.

    On the same morning, Head of the Chinese Trade Delegation Kong Yuan and Deputy Head Yang Lin paid a visit to N.R. Pillai, Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, and R.K. Nehru, Foreign Secretary.

### 印度总理尼赫鲁接见我国贸易代表团团长

1954-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁接见我国贸易代表团团长
    【新华社新德里十三日电】印度总理尼赫鲁十二日上午接见了中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长、中国贸易代表团团长孔原和副团长杨琳。陪同受接见的有中国驻印度大使馆临时代办申健。
    尼赫鲁总理在谈话中表示，中印两国之间的经济和贸易关系应该进一步发展。尼赫鲁总理并对中国的工业建设表示了很大的兴趣。孔原团长代表中国贸易代表团，对印度政府和人民所给予的欢迎招待与关切表示谢意。
    同日上午，中国贸易代表团团长孔原和副团长杨琳拜会了印度外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱和外事秘书拉·库·尼赫鲁。
