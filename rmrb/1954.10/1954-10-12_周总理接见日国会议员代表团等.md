Title: Premier Zhou Receives Delegation of Japanese Diet Members, etc.
Author:
Date: 1954-10-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 11th: On the morning of October 11th at 10:00 AM, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, received the entire delegation of 31 members of the Japanese Diet Members' Visiting China Delegation, including Suzuki Mosaburo, Tokuji Tokonami, Genjiro Sugiyama, Kikuichiro Yamaguchi, Jiichiro Matsumoto, Taichi Hirabayashi, Kanichi Kawakami, and Kenji Nakahara, as well as the 13 members of the Japanese Academic and Cultural Visiting China Delegation, including Abe Yoshishige.

### 周总理接见日国会议员代表团等

1954-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日国会议员代表团等
   【新华社十一日讯】十月十一日上午十时，中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来接见了日本国会议员访华代表团铃木茂三郎、床次德二、杉山元治郎、山口喜久一郎、松本治一郎、平林太一、川上贯一、中原健次等全体团员三十一人和日本学术文化访华代表团安培能成等十三人。
