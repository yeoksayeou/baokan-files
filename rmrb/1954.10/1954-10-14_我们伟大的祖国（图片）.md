Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-10-14
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    People of all ethnic groups and all walks of life across the country are welcoming the publication of the Sino-Soviet talks communiqué with joy and excitement. This is a group of students of all ethnic groups at the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League School reading the communiqué of the Sino-Soviet talks.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国各族各界人民都以欢欣鼓舞的心情来迎接中苏会谈公报的公布。这是中国新民主主义青年团中央团校的各族学生在阅读中苏会谈的公报。
