Title: Comrade Groza Visits Various Places
Author:
Date: 1954-10-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 12th:

    Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, accompanied by Li Enqiu, Counselor of our Embassy in Romania, who is currently in Beijing, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 12th to visit various places. Romanian writers Yu. Camila and I. Vitner also traveled to various places on the same plane.

### 格罗查同志去各地参观

1954-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    格罗查同志去各地参观
    【新华社十二日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查博士由现在北京的我国驻罗马尼亚大使馆参赞李恩求陪同，在十二日晨乘飞机离京赴各地参观。同机前往各地参观的还有罗马尼亚作家尤·卡米拉和依·维特奈。
