Title: Czechoslovak, Hungarian, and German Government Delegations Return to Beijing After Visiting Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1954-10-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation led by V. Kopecký and the Hungarian People's Republic Government Delegation led by A. Hegedüs, accompanied by Dong Chun-cai, Deputy Minister of Education, and Liu Rui-long, Deputy Minister of Agriculture, respectively, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 7th after visiting Tianjin on the 5th and 6th.

During their stay in Tianjin, the Czechoslovak and Hungarian government delegations visited the state-owned Tianjin No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory, the state-owned Tianjin Paper Mill, the Workers' Cultural Palace, and the state-owned department store, and also visited the Tanggu New Port.

They were warmly welcomed in Tianjin.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The German Democratic Republic Government Delegation led by Dr. Lothar Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, accompanied by Lai Jifa, Deputy Minister of Heavy Industry, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 7th after visiting Nanjing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou on the 4th, 5th, and 6th.

While in Nanjing, the delegation laid wreaths at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and the Yuhuatai Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery. In Shanghai, they visited a shipbuilding factory, a state-owned department store, and toured the Huangpu River. In Hangzhou, they toured the scenic spots of West Lake and visited the Qiantang River Bridge and the Longjing tea area.

The delegation was warmly welcomed wherever they went.

### 捷、匈、德政府代表团到各地参观后回京

1954-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    捷、匈、德政府代表团到各地参观后回京
    【新华社七日讯】以瓦·柯别茨基团长为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团和以安·赫格居斯团长为首的匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团分别由我教育部副部长董纯才和我农业部副部长刘瑞龙陪同，在五日和六日参观天津后，已在七日下午乘飞机返回北京。
    捷、匈两国政府代表团在天津期间，参观了国营天津第二棉纺织厂、国营天津造纸厂、工人文化宫和国营百货公司等处，并曾前往塘沽新港参观。
    他们在天津时受到了热烈欢迎。
    【新华社七日讯】以德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长洛·博尔茨博士为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团由我重工业部副部长赖际发陪同，在四、五、六日参观了南京、上海、杭州等地后，已于七日下午乘飞机返回北京。
    代表团在南京时曾经谒中山陵和雨花台革命烈士陵并献花圈。在上海时参观了造船工厂、国营百货公司和游览了黄浦江。在杭州时游览了西湖的名胜古迹，参观了钱塘江大桥和龙井茶区等处。
    代表团所到各地，都受到了热烈欢迎。
