Title: Korean Government Delegation Left Beijing Yesterday to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th—The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and including Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Marshal Choe Yong-gon, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly Li Young, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Li Dong-geon, who came to China at the invitation of our government to attend the National Day celebrations for the fifth anniversary of the founding of our country, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 5th to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off were Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Vice Premiers Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Ulanhu, Li Xiannian; Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen; Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Yang Chengwu; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Li Kenong, Zhang Hanfu; Vice Minister of Public Security Xu Zirong; Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Xu Xuehan; Vice Minister of Railways Lü Zhengcao; Vice Minister of Culture Zheng Zhenduo; Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan; Deputy Director Yan Baohang; Deputy Director of the Foreign Policy Committee Qiao Guanhua; and Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru.

Also present at the airport were members of the National Defense Council Chen Geng, Deng Hua, Yang Dezhi, Zhou Chunquan, Hong Xuezhi, Su Zhenhua, and Wang Bingzhang; and Deputy Director of the General Political Department Gan Siqi.

Also present at the airport were Lu Dingyi, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the CPC Central Committee; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Xu Guangping, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; and Qu Tangliang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation.

Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and staff members of the embassy were also at the airport to see them off.

Diplomatic envoys and personnel from various countries stationed in China who went to the airport to see them off included U Ohn Maung, Ambassador of Burma; Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Johannes König, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic; Pavel Yudin, Ambassador of the Soviet Union; Šmíd, Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy; Lubsanchultem, Counselor of the Mongolian Embassy; Konstantinov, Commercial Counselor of the Bulgarian Embassy; Mentianu, First Secretary of the Romanian Embassy; Gula, First Secretary of the Polish Embassy; Csator Dai, First Secretary of the Hungarian Embassy; Yasha, Second Secretary of the Albanian Embassy; and Sharir, Third Secretary of the Indonesian Embassy.

The national flags of Korea and China, as well as the flags of the Soviet Union and other people's democratic countries, were hung at the airport.

After the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Kim Il-sung, arrived at the airport the band played the national anthems of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China. Marshal Kim Il-sung and others inspected the guard of honor lined up at the airport.

After warmly shaking hands with those who came to see them off, Kim Il-sung and his party boarded the plane and left Beijing.

During their stay in Beijing, the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea visited and toured scenic spots and historical sites such as the Summer Palace, the Forbidden City, and Beihai Park.

### 朝鲜政府代表团昨离京回国

1954-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团昨离京回国
    【新华社五日讯】应我国政府邀请前来参加我国建国五周年国庆典礼的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅，团员：副首相兼民族保卫相崔庸健次帅，最高人民会议议长李英，外务省副相李东建，已在五日上午乘飞机离京返国。
    到机场送行的，有中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，副总理彭德怀、邓小平、贺龙、乌兰夫、李先念，北京市市长彭真，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武，外交部副部长李克农、章汉夫，公安部副部长徐子荣，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，铁道部副部长吕正操，文化部副部长郑振铎，外交部办公厅主任王炳南、副主任阎宝航、外交政策委员会副主任委员乔冠华、交际处处长王倬如。
    到机场送行的，还有国防委员会委员陈赓、邓华、杨得志、周纯全、洪学智、苏振华、王秉璋，总政治部副主任甘泗淇。
    到机场送行的，还有中共中央宣传部部长陆定一、办公厅主任杨尚昆，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席区棠亮。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一和使馆人员也到机场送行。
    到机场送行的各国驻华使节和外交人员，有缅甸大使吴拉茂，越南民主共和国大使黄文欢，德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希，苏联大使尤金，捷克斯洛伐克大使馆参赞石密茨，蒙古大使馆参赞罗布桑楚勒图姆，保加利亚大使馆商务参赞康士坦丁诺夫，罗马尼亚大使馆一等秘书孟天努，波兰大使馆一等秘书古拉，匈牙利大使馆一等秘书察托尔道伊，阿尔巴尼亚大使馆二等秘书雅沙，印度尼西亚大使馆三等秘书沙里尔。
    机场上悬挂着朝中两国国旗，以及苏联和各人民民主国家的国旗。
    以金日成团长为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团到达机场后，乐队奏起朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国歌。金日成团长等检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。
    金日成团长等一行在和送行者亲切握手后，即登机离开北京。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团在北京期间曾参观游览了颐和园、故宫、北海等名胜古迹。
