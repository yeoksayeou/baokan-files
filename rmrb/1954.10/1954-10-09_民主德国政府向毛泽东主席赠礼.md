Title: East German Government Presents Gift to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1954-10-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: The presentation ceremony for the electronic microscope, a gift from the government of the German Democratic Republic to Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, was held in Beijing on the afternoon of August 8th. This large microscope has a precise and complex structure and is of great value in scientific research work. Mr. Koenig, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, presented the gift on behalf of the government of the German Democratic Republic, and Mr. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, accepted the gift on behalf of Chairman Mao Zedong. On the German side, those present at the presentation ceremony included Dr. Lothar Bolz, head of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic and Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, who was invited to China to attend the celebrations for the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and staff members of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China. On the Chinese side, those present included: Zhang Jiafu, Tao Menghe, and Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, and prominent Chinese scientists Zhou Peiyuan, Qian Sanqiang, Yan Jici, Lu Xueshan, Wang Ganchang, Shi Ruwei, and many others.

### 民主德国政府向毛泽东主席赠礼

1954-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府向毛泽东主席赠礼
    【新华社八日讯】德意志民主共和国政府赠送给中华人民共和国毛泽东主席一架电子显微镜的赠礼仪式，八日下午在北京举行。这架大型显微镜构造精密、复杂，在科学研究工作方面有极大的价值。德意志民主共和国驻我国大使柯尼希代表德意志民主共和国政府赠礼，中国科学院院长郭沫若代表毛泽东主席受礼。德国方面出席赠礼仪式的有应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的德意志民主共和国政府代表团团长、德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长洛·博尔茨博士及德意志民主共和国驻我国使馆人员。中国方面出席的有：中国科学院副院长张稼夫、陶孟和、吴有训、中华人民共和国外交部副部长伍修权、交际处副处长王拓及中国著名科学家周培源、钱三强、严济慈、陆学善、王淦昌、施汝为等多人。
