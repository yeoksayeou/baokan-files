Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army Leadership Organ Holds Meeting to Commemorate the Fourth Anniversary of the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea
Author:
Date: 1954-10-27
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th: The leadership organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army held a meeting on October 25th to commemorate the fourth anniversary of the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea. More than 3,000 cadres and soldiers attended the meeting. Li Xiaochun, head of the delegation of the Korean people congratulating the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the fourth anniversary of the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea and Chairman of the Central Inspection Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and all members of the delegation, the Korean People's Army Ensemble, and representatives of the Korean government and people in the area where the leadership organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army was stationed also attended the meeting.

    General Zhang Nansheng, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, spoke at the meeting. Zhang Nansheng said: In the past few years, although our Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the heroic Korean People's Army have achieved a great victory in the cause of opposing U.S. aggression and defending peace, the U.S. aggressors are still hostile to peace, and the threat of aggression and war still exists seriously. In order to ensure the Korean armistice, strive for a peaceful settlement of the Korean issue, ensure the safe socialist construction of the motherland, support the people of the motherland and the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan, and defend peace in the Far East and the world, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army must better stick to the fighting post of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, continue to fight alongside the heroic Korean People's Army, always maintain a high degree of vigilance and preparedness against the enemy, further strengthen the combat effectiveness and regularization and modernization construction of the troops, continue to consolidate and develop the great internationalist unity and friendship between the Korean and Chinese people and the Korean and Chinese people's armies, and unswervingly strive to complete the great struggle task of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea assigned to us by the people of the motherland!

    Li Xiaochun, head of the delegation of the Korean people congratulating the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the fourth anniversary of the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting. Head of Delegation Li Xiaochun said: From the first day of participating in the Korean people's war against aggression, the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, together with our officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army, displayed unparalleled collective heroism and lofty proletarian internationalism on the battlefield, and established combat merits and achievements that will forever radiate light in the history of our country. He said: After the Korean armistice, the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, together with the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army, have been monitoring the enemy's conspiratorial actions with high vigilance, defending the peace that has been achieved. At the same time, the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army have also vigorously assisted us in carrying out post-war reconstruction work. We, the Korean people, are determined to continue to strengthen the fraternal friendship and unity with the great Chinese people and the heroic officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and will continue to contribute all our strength to support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. With the strong assistance of peace-loving people all over the world, headed by the great Soviet Union, and under the strong leadership of the Korean Workers' Party and the Republic government, the Korean people are working hard to overcome all difficulties and complete the Three-Year Plan for Post-War National Economic Recovery and Development and the great cause of peaceful reunification of the motherland.

    Amidst warm applause and music, Head of Delegation Li Xiaochun and Shi Bo, Director of the Organization Department of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, presented flags to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on behalf of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Korean People's Army, respectively. General Zhang Nansheng, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, accepted the flags on behalf of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

    After the meeting, the Korean People's Army Ensemble performed wonderful cultural programs.

### 中国人民志愿军领导机关举行大会纪念抗美援朝四周年

1954-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军领导机关举行大会纪念抗美援朝四周年
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】中国人民志愿军领导机关在十月二十五日举行了抗美援朝四周年纪念大会。参加大会的有干部和战士三千多人。朝鲜人民祝贺中国人民志愿军抗美援朝四周年代表团团长、朝鲜劳动党中央检阅委员会委员长李孝淳和全体团员，朝鲜人民军协奏团和中国人民志愿军领导机关驻地的朝鲜政府和人民代表，也参加了大会。
    中国人民志愿军政治部副主任张南生将军在大会上讲话。张南生说：在过去几年中，我们中国人民志愿军和英雄的朝鲜人民军虽然已经取得了反对美国侵略、保卫和平事业的巨大胜利，但是美国侵略者仍然在敌视和平，侵略和战争的威胁仍然严重地存在着。为了保障朝鲜停战、争取和平解决朝鲜问题，为了保障祖国安全地进行社会主义建设事业，支援祖国人民和人民解放军解放台湾，为了保卫远东和世界的和平，中国人民志愿军必须更好地坚守抗美援朝的斗争岗位，继续和英雄的朝鲜人民军一道进行斗争，时刻保持对敌人的高度警惕和戒备，进一步加强部队的战斗力和正规化现代化建设，继续巩固和发展朝中人民和朝中人民军队之间的伟大的国际主义团结和友谊，坚定不移地为完成祖国人民交给我们的抗美援朝的伟大斗争任务而奋斗！
    朝鲜人民祝贺中国人民志愿军抗美援朝四周年代表团团长李孝淳在会上致祝词。李孝淳团长说：中国人民志愿军将士们从参加朝鲜人民反侵略战争的第一天起，就和我们朝鲜人民军将士一起在战场上发挥了无比的集体英雄主义和崇高的无产阶级国际主义精神，建立了在我们祖国史册上永远放射光辉的战斗功勋和业迹。他说：朝鲜停战以后，中国人民志愿军将士们又和朝鲜人民军将士一道，以高度的警惕监视着敌人的阴谋行动，保卫着已经取得的和平。同时，中国人民志愿军将士们还大力协助我们进行战后恢复工作。我们朝鲜人民决心继续加强和伟大的中国人民和英雄的中国人民志愿军将士之间的兄弟般的友谊团结，并将继续贡献出一切力量来支援中国人民志愿军。朝鲜人民在以伟大的苏联为首的全世界爱好和平的人民的强有力的援助下，在朝鲜劳动党和共和国政府的坚强领导下，努力克服一切困难，完成战后人民经济恢复发展三年计划和和平统一祖国的伟大事业。
    在热烈的掌声和音乐声中，李孝淳团长和朝鲜人民军总政治局组织部部长石波分别代表朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁和朝鲜人民军，向中国人民志愿军献旗。中国人民志愿军政治部副主任张南生将军代表中国人民志愿军受旗。
    会后，朝鲜人民军协奏团演出了精彩的文娱节目。
