Title: Czechoslovak National Front Issues Proclamation to Citizens in Preparation for Elections to the Czechoslovak National Assembly and the Slovak National Council
Author:
Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 17th: TASS reports that the Czechoslovak National Front has issued a proclamation to all citizens of the Czechoslovak Republic regarding the elections to the National Assembly and the Slovak National Council, scheduled for November 28th.
    The proclamation summarizes the tremendous achievements made by Czechoslovakia in all areas since its liberation in 1945, particularly since the last elections to the National Assembly, and discusses the historically significant path for further development of the national economy pointed out to the people of the entire country by the Tenth Congress of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia.
    The proclamation emphasizes that the elections to the National Assembly are a major event in our country's life. The working people of town and country, united indissolubly within the National Front and under the leadership of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, will participate in the elections to the National Assembly and the Slovak National Council. We have a wonderful goal before us: to build a happy life for all the people of our free and independent country. We have the strength to achieve this goal. Our people's democratic system provides unprecedented conditions for bringing into play the creativity and initiative of the people. It is our sacred responsibility to maintain and strengthen our friendship and cooperation with the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, the People's Democracies, the German Democratic Republic, and all progressive forces in the world.

### 为准备捷国民议会和斯洛伐克民族议会的选举  捷克斯洛伐克民族阵线发表告公民书

1954-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    为准备捷国民议会和斯洛伐克民族议会的选举
    捷克斯洛伐克民族阵线发表告公民书
     【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社布拉格十七日讯：捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克民族阵线就定于十一月二十八日举行的国民议会和斯洛伐克民族议会的选举，发表了告捷克斯洛伐克共和国全体公民书。
    告公民书综述了捷克斯洛伐克从一九四五年解放以来，特别是从上届国民议会选举以来在各方面所取得的巨大成就，并谈到了具有历史意义的捷克斯洛伐克共产党第十次代表大会向全国人民所指出的进一步发展国民经济的道路。
    告公民书着重指出，国民议会的选举是我国生活中的一件大事。城乡劳动人民牢不可破地团结在民族阵线内，在捷克斯洛伐克共产党的领导下，将参加国民议会和斯洛伐克民族议会的选举。我们前面有着一个美好的目的：为我们自由、独立的国家的全体人民建设幸福的生活。我们有力量达到这一目的。我国人民民主制度为发挥人民的创造性和积极性提供了空前未有的条件。我们的神圣责任是：维护和巩固我们和苏联、中华人民共和国、各人民民主国家、德意志民主共和国及世界上所有进步力量之间的友谊和合作。
