Title: Vietnamese Women's Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 28th. The women's delegation from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Nguyen Thuc Vien, Vice-Chairman of the Vietnam Women's Union, returned home on the 27th after being invited by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to attend the National Day celebrations in our country. After arriving in Guangzhou on the 24th, the delegation visited historical sites and schools in Guangzhou for several days.

The Japanese youth representative, Kohei Fukushida, left Guangzhou on the 26th to return home.

### 越南妇女代表团回国

1954-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    越南妇女代表团回国
    【新华社广州二十八日电】应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请前来参加我国国庆观礼的越南民主共和国妇女代表团，在团长、越南妇女联合会副主席阮淑媛率领下，二十七日离开广州回国。代表团二十四日到达广州后，连日参观了广州名胜古迹和学校。
    日本青年代表鲋子田耕作已在二十六日离开广州回国。
