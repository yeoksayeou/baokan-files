Title: The U.S.-Chiang Kai-shek Clique Will Form a "Joint Headquarters" in Taiwan to Plot to Obstruct Our People's Liberation of Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1954-10-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th
    The U.S. forces in the Pacific region will establish a "joint headquarters" with the Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops in Taiwan to jointly plot to obstruct the Chinese people's liberation of Taiwan. According to a report by International News Service, this "joint headquarters" will be formed in three months and will include the U.S. Pacific Command, the U.S. Seventh Fleet, the U.S. Military Assistance Advisory Group stationed in Taiwan, the U.S. Thirteenth Air Force stationed in the Philippines, and representatives of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops. It is reported that after the establishment of this "joint headquarters," the U.S. Pacific Command will send a large delegation to be stationed in Taiwan.

### 美蒋将在台湾合组“联合总部”  策划阻挠我国人民解放台湾

1954-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    美蒋将在台湾合组“联合总部”
    策划阻挠我国人民解放台湾
      【新华社六日讯】美国驻太平洋地区部队将和蒋贼军在台湾成立一个“联合总部”，以共同策划阻挠中国人民解放台湾。据国际新闻社报道，这个
  “联合总部”将在三个月后组成，其中包括美国太平洋司令部、美国第七舰队、美国驻台湾的军事援助顾问团、美国驻菲律宾的第十三航空队和蒋贼军的代表。有消息说，在这个“联合总部”成立以后，美国太平洋司令部将派遣一个很大的代表团驻在台湾。
