Title: Indonesian Youth Representatives Return Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 15th. The nine Indonesian youth representatives who participated in the Beijing meeting of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council and continued to tour various places, left Guangzhou on the 14th via Hong Kong to return to their country.

### 印度尼西亚青年代表回国

1954-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚青年代表回国
     【新华社广州十五日电】参加世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议后，继续到各地参观游览的印度尼西亚青年代表九人，已在十四日离开广州经香港返国。
