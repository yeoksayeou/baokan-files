Title: Ceylon Cabinet Approves China-Ceylon Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-10-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 17th - A dispatch from Colombo: The Ceylon Cabinet approved the trade agreement signed in Beijing on the 8th by the Ceylon government trade delegation and the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, at a meeting held on the 14th.

### 锡兰内阁批准中锡贸易合同

1954-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰内阁批准中锡贸易合同
    【新华社新德里十七日电】科伦坡消息：锡兰内阁已经在十四日举行的会议中批准了锡兰政府贸易代表团八日在北京同中华人民共和国政府对外贸易部签订的贸易合同。
