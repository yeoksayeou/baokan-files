Title: Indian Newspapers Comment on the Great Significance of Nehru's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 18th: Indian newspapers widely believe that Prime Minister Nehru's visit to China at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China is a significant event in Asian affairs today.

    The Urdu "Vigor Daily" editorial on the 17th said that Prime Minister Nehru's visit to China will strengthen the friendship between China and India. The editorial said that the result of Prime Minister Nehru's visit will be: "China and India will be closer, and Asian unity will be further strengthened."
    The "Tribune" commented on the 16th that Prime Minister Nehru's visit to China is an event of profound significance. This event reflects the urgent desire of the Indian people to establish closer relations with their Chinese neighbors. The "Tribune" pointed out that the relations between China and India have a long history; at present, the two countries have adopted different political systems, but this fact cannot be allowed to hinder the connection between the two countries. "Tribune" said: "For the peace and prosperity of Asia and the world, it is absolutely necessary for India and China to work closely together."
    The "Free Press Journal" of Bombay said in its editorial on the 16th that, in light of current Asian affairs, Prime Minister Nehru's visit to China is an event of great political significance. The editorial said: The upcoming Beijing talks mark the highest-level contact between two countries with the oldest world civilizations. The editorial pointed out that in the two thousand years of interaction between China and India, there has never been any event that has provoked conflict between the two countries. Today, despite differences in ideological viewpoints, China and India are working towards the same goal.
    The "Bombay Chronicle" editorial on the same day said that Prime Minister Nehru's visit to China today will particularly attract strong attention from the whole world. The editorial then pointed out that the international importance of China and India has increased rapidly and significantly. In addition to marking the resurgence of Asia, they have proposed new trends and new possibilities for world peace and progress.
    The "Amrita Bazar Patrika" editorial on the 15th said that every Indian will wish Prime Minister Nehru's trip to China a success. The editorial said that Prime Minister Nehru's visit to China will strengthen the friendship between the 960 million people of China and India. This will be a powerful force to defend the peace of Asia, which is currently under constant threat in many ways.

### 印度报纸评尼赫鲁访问我国的重大意义

1954-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    印度报纸评尼赫鲁访问我国的重大意义
    【新华社新德里十八日电】印度报纸广泛认为，尼赫鲁总理应中华人民共和国政府的邀请到中国访问是今天亚洲事务中具有重大意义的事件。
    乌尔都文“活力报”十七日的社论说，尼赫鲁总理访问中国将会加强中国和印度的友谊。社论说，尼赫鲁总理访问的结果将是：“中国和印度将更加接近，亚洲的团结将得到进一步的加强”。
    “论坛报”十六日评论说：尼赫鲁总理访问中国是具有深刻意义的事件。这件事反映了印度人民希望同他们的中国邻邦建立更密切的关系的迫切愿望。“论坛报”指出，中印两国的关系具有悠久的历史；目前这两个国家采取了不同的政治制度，但是，不能容许这个事实妨碍两国的联系。“论坛报”说：“为了亚洲和世界的和平和繁荣，印中两国绝对有必要密切一致地共同努力。”
    孟买“自由新闻”十六日的社论说，从目前亚洲的事务来看，尼赫鲁总理访问中国是具有重大政治意义的事件。社论说：即将开始的北京会谈标志着具有最古老世界文明的两个国家的最高级的联系。社论指出：在中印两国互相交往的两千年的历史中，从来没有发生任何挑起中印两国冲突的事件。今天，尽管有观点形态上的区别，但中印两国正在为同一目标而努力。
    “孟买纪事报”同日的社论说，尼赫鲁总理在今天访问中国将特别引起全世界的强烈的注意。社论接着指出，中国和印度在国际上的重要性有了迅速而显著的提高。除了这是标志着亚洲的重新抬头之外，它们已为世界的和平和进步提出新的趋向和新的可能。
    “甘露市场报”十五日的社论说，每一个印度人都将预祝尼赫鲁总理的中国之行取得成功。社论说，尼赫鲁总理的访问中国将会加强中印两国九亿六千万人口之间的友谊。这将是一支强大的力量，以保卫目前正在许多方面不断受到威胁的亚洲的和平。
