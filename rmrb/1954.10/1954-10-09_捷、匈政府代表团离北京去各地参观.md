Title: Czechoslovak and Hungarian Government Delegations Leave Beijing to Visit Various Places
Author: 
Date: 1954-10-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 8th.
The Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation, headed by Comrade V. Kopecky, and the Hungarian People's Republic Government Delegation, headed by Comrade A. Hegedüs, accompanied by Dong Chuncai, Vice Minister of Education, and Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Agriculture, respectively, left Beijing by the same plane on the morning of the 8th to continue their visits to various places.

### 捷、匈政府代表团离北京去各地参观

1954-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    捷、匈政府代表团离北京去各地参观
   【新华社八日讯】以瓦·柯别茨基团长为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团和以安·赫格居斯团长为首的匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团分别由我教育部副部长董纯才和我农业部副部长刘瑞龙陪同，于八日上午同机飞离北京，继续赴各地参观。
