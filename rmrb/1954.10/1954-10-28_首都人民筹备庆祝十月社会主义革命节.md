Title: Capital Residents Prepare to Celebrate the October Socialist Revolution Anniversary
Author: 
Date: 1954-10-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: People from all walks of life in the capital are preparing to celebrate the 37th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has now made specific plans for commemorative activities.

    People from all walks of life in the city will hold a commemorative meeting on November 5th. On November 8th, young workers, students and government employees in Beijing will hold a friendship soiree with Soviet youth in Beijing. Some members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association delegation to the Soviet Union will give reports to people from all walks of life on their visit to the Soviet Union before and after the festival. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Cultural Center, the Working People's Cultural Palace, Zhongshan Park, Tianqiao and other places will hold photo exhibitions with the themes of "The Peaceful and Creative Labor of the Soviet People" and "The Great Soviet Union." Small-scale Soviet photo exhibitions will be held at the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, the Shijingshan Power Plant, the People's Printing Factory and some larger construction sites. Preparations for the festival commemoration have also begun in units such as the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Corporation.

    The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has prepared Soviet films such as "Light Shines on Keaoldi Village," "Happy Life," and "The Visit of the Chinese Farmers' Delegation to the Soviet Union" for farmers in the suburbs, which will be shown in rural areas on a tour starting November 5th. In addition, more than 400 sets of "Stalin Collective Farm" posters will be supplied to farmers in the suburbs.

### 首都人民筹备庆祝十月社会主义革命节

1954-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    首都人民筹备庆祝十月社会主义革命节
    【新华社二十七日讯】首都各界人民正在筹备庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十七周年。北京市中苏友好协会现已作出纪念活动的具体计划。
    全市各界人民将在十一月五日举行纪念大会。十一月八日，北京市的青年工人、学生和机关干部将和在京的苏联青年举行友谊联欢会。中苏友好协会访苏代表团在京部分团员，将在节日前后向各界人民作访苏报告。北京市中苏友好协会文化馆、劳动人民文化宫、中山公园、天桥等处将举办以“苏联人民和平创造性的劳动”、“伟大的苏联”为题的图片展览。石景山钢铁厂、石景山发电厂、人民印刷厂和一些较大的建筑工地将举行小型的苏联图片展览会。在中苏民用航空公司等单位，也已开始节日纪念的筹备工作。
    北京市中苏友好协会为郊区农民准备了“光明照耀到克奥尔地村”、“幸福的生活”、“中国农民代表团访苏经过”等苏联影片，将在十一月五日下乡巡回放映。另外，有四百多套“斯大林集体农庄”挂图将供应郊区农民。
