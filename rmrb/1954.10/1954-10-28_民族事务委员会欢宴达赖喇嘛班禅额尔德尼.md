Title: Nationalities Affairs Commission Hosts Banquet for Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni
Author:
Date: 1954-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th—The Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China held a banquet on October 27th at the Moscow Restaurant in the Soviet Exhibition Center in the western suburbs of Beijing to entertain the Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, the Panchen Erdeni Qoiji Gyaltsen, and the secular and religious officials from the Tibet local government, all members of the Tibetan visiting delegation, and all members of the Tibetan song and dance troupe.

The Tibetan secular and religious officials attending the banquet included: Living Buddha Lincang, Living Buddha Chijiang, Gagin Losang Nyima, Living Buddha Karmapa, Sokang Wangqing Gelei, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Ji Jinmei, Losang Sandan, Yaoxi Zeren Zhuoma, Liuxia Tubden Daba, and the head of the Tibetan visiting delegation, Ramba Nangji Wangdui, deputy heads Hoba Dun, Lajiali Langji Gyatso, Ouxie Tubden Sangque, and Sende Bangguan, among others.

Also attending the banquet were: Ulanhu, Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Vice Chairmen Wang Feng and Liu Chun, and committee members in Beijing. Zhang Jingwu, Representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet, and Fan Ming and Wang Qimei, Deputy Political Commissars of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, also attended the banquet.

The banquet was held in an atmosphere of unity and friendship.

### 民族事务委员会欢宴达赖喇嘛班禅额尔德尼

1954-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    民族事务委员会欢宴达赖喇嘛班禅额尔德尼
    【新华社二十七日讯】中华人民共和国民族事务委员会二十七日在北京西郊苏联展览馆莫斯科餐厅举行宴会，欢宴达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，和西藏地方到京僧俗官员，西藏参观团全体团员，西藏歌舞团全体团员。
    出席宴会的西藏僧俗官员有：林仓活佛、赤江活佛、尕金洛桑尼玛、噶玛巴活佛、索康·旺清格来、阿沛·阿旺晋美、计晋美、洛桑三旦、尧西·泽仁卓玛、柳霞·土登塔巴，和西藏参观团团长然巴·囊吉旺堆，副团长何巴敦、拉加利·朗吉嘉措、欧协·土登桑却、森德班官等人。
    出席宴会的有：民族事务委员会主任乌兰夫，副主任汪锋、刘春，和在京委员。中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武，中国人民解放军西藏军区副政治委员范明、王其梅也出席了宴会。
    宴会在团结友爱的空气中进行。
