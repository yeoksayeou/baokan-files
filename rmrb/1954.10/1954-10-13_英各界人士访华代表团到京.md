Title: British Delegation of People from All Circles Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 12th: The British delegation of parliamentarians, trade unionists, cooperative workers, scientists, teachers, and artists, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 12th, led by the head of the delegation, Ellis Smith, and the deputy head, George Lindgren.
    The delegation of twenty people includes nine parliamentarians, seven trade union leaders, and four scholars. One person will postpone their arrival in Beijing due to illness.

### 英各界人士访华代表团到京

1954-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    英各界人士访华代表团到京
      【新华社十二日讯】应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的英国议员、工会工作者、合作社工作者、科学家、教师和艺术家访华代表团，在十二日下午由团长埃利斯·史密斯和副团长乔治·林格林率领乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团二十人中有九位议员，七位产业工会负责人和四位学者。其中有一人因病将缓期赶来北京。
