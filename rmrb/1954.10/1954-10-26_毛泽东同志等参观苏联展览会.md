Title: Comrade Mao Zedong and Others Visit the Soviet Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1954-10-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 25th: Chinese Communist Party leaders Comrades Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Peng Dehuai, Peng Zhen, Deng Xiaoping, and others visited the "Exhibition of Achievements in Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction" at the Soviet Exhibition Center in the western suburbs of Beijing at 7:25 p.m. on the 25th.
    Soviet Ambassador to China, Academician Yudin, Soviet Commercial Representative Migunov, and Director of the Exhibition of Achievements in Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction, Borisinko, accompanied Chairman Mao Zedong and others on the visit.
    Chairman Mao Zedong and others visited the exhibition for a total of four hours. They carefully toured the various sections of the exhibition, including the Industrial Hall, Agricultural Hall, Food Hall, Cultural Hall, Higher Education Hall, and Artworks Hall, and dined at the Moscow Restaurant.

### 毛泽东同志等参观苏联展览会

1954-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东同志等参观苏联展览会
    【新华社二十五日讯】中国共产党的领导者毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、林伯渠、董必武、彭德怀、彭真、邓小平等同志在二十五日下午七时二十五分，前往北京西郊苏联展览馆参观“苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会”。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金院士、苏联商务代表米古诺夫和苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科陪同毛泽东主席等参观。
    毛泽东主席等在展览会参观历时共四小时。他们仔细地参观了展览会的工业馆、农业馆、食品馆、文化馆、高等教育馆和美术作品馆等各个部分，并在莫斯科餐厅进餐。
