Title: Hoxha Receives Chinese Ambassador to Albania Xu Yixin; Congratulates China on the Adoption of the Constitution and Comrade Mao Zedong's Election as Chairman
Author:
Date: 1954-10-04
Page: 2
Hoxha Receives Chinese Ambassador to Albania Xu Yixin
Congratulates China on the Adoption of the Constitution and Comrade Mao Zedong's Election as Chairman

    Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Albanian Labor Party, received Xu Yixin, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Albania, on September 29th, congratulating the successful adoption of the Constitution by the First Session of the First National People's Congress of China and the election of Mao Zedong as Chairman of the People's Republic of China. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 霍查接见我驻阿尔巴尼亚大使徐以新  祝贺我国通过宪法和毛泽东同志当选为主席

1954-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    霍查接见我驻阿尔巴尼亚大使徐以新
    祝贺我国通过宪法和毛泽东同志当选为主席
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第一书记霍查在九月二十九日接见中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使徐以新，祝贺中国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议胜利通过宪法并选举毛泽东为中华人民共和国主席。　（新华社）
