Title: Chiang Bandit Warships Intercept British Merchant Ship
Author:
Date: 1954-10-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 16th. According to a report received by the Shanghai Coastal Radio Station from the British merchant ship "Gedda," the "Gedda" was illegally intercepted by Chiang bandit warships at 00:15 on the 15th, at 28 degrees 30 minutes north latitude and 122 degrees 15 minutes east longitude, while en route to Shanghai, and was ordered not to sail to Shanghai.

### 蒋贼军舰又拦截英国商船

1954-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋贼军舰又拦截英国商船
    【新华社上海十六日电】据上海海岸电台收到英国商船“吉达号”的报告，“吉达号”十五日零时十五分在驶往上海途中，在北纬二十八度三十分、东经一百二十二度十五分被蒋贼军舰非法拦截，并被命令不准驶往上海。
