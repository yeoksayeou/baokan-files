Title: The Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Issues a Notice to its Subordinate Organizations Calling on Members to Seriously Study the Documents of the National People's Congress Session
Author:
Date: 1954-10-20
Page: 3

The Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang issued a notice to local organizations at all levels, calling on all members to seriously study the main documents of the First Session of the First National People's Congress – the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, Chairman Mao's opening speech, Chairman Liu Shaoqi's report on the draft constitution, and Premier Zhou Enlai's government work report. The notice requires all members to clarify the great historical significance of the First Session of the First National People's Congress through study, and to understand the great role of the Constitution in promoting China's socialist construction. In the study, attention should be paid to linking theory with practice, strengthening ideological remolding, strengthening the concept of abiding by the Constitution, laws, and discipline, improving socialist awareness and a high degree of consciousness and initiative in work, ensuring the completion of job responsibilities, and especially to the current central political task of liberating Taiwan, it is necessary to give full play to strength and make contributions. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国国民党革命委员会中央向所属各级组织发出通知  号召成员认真学习人民代表大会会议文件

1954-10-20
第3版()
专栏：

    中国国民党革命委员会中央向所属各级组织发出通知
    号召成员认真学习人民代表大会会议文件
    中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会向地方各级组织发出通知，号召全体成员认真学习第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的主要文件——中华人民共和国宪法、毛主席的开幕词、刘少奇委员长的关于宪法草案的报告、周恩来总理的政府工作报告。通知要求全体成员通过学习，明确第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的伟大历史意义；领会宪法对促进我国社会主义建设事业的巨大作用。学习中应注意联系实际，加强思想改造，加强遵守宪法、法律和纪律的观念，提高社会主义觉悟和在工作中的高度自觉性和主动性，保证做好岗位工作，对当前中心政治任务之一——解放台湾，尤须充分发挥力量，做出贡献。（新华社）
