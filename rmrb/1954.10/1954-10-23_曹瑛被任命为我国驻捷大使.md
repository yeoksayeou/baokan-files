Title: Cao Ying Appointed as China's Ambassador to Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1954-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd:

    Cao Ying has been appointed as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Czechoslovak Republic, as approved by the 34th meeting of the Central People's Government Committee on September 9th.
    The 34th meeting of the Central People's Government Committee approved the removal of Tan Xilin from his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Czechoslovak Republic.

### 曹瑛被任命为我国驻捷大使

1954-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    曹瑛被任命为我国驻捷大使
    【新华社二十二日讯】九月九日中央人民政府委员会第三十四次会议通过任命曹瑛为中华人民共和国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国特命全权大使。
    中央人民政府委员会第三十四次会议通过免去谭希林的中华人民共和国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国特命全权大使的职务。
