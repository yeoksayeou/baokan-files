Title: Five Czech Writers and Artists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: Two Czechoslovak writers and three artists, who are visiting China in accordance with the 1954 executive plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane in batches on the evening of September 30.

### 捷作家和艺术家五人到京

1954-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    捷作家和艺术家五人到京
   【新华社二日讯】根据中捷文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克作家二人和艺术家三人，已在九月三十日晚上乘飞机分批到达北京。
