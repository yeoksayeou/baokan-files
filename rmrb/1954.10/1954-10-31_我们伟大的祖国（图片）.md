Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-10-31
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to ensure the completion of the state cotton purchase task and support the production of spinning mills, the Liangmian Cotton Ginning Factory in Jin County, Hebei Province, is processing a large amount of seed cotton for the Flower and Cloth Company. This seed cotton is being continuously transported to the factory.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了保证完成国家收购棉花的任务和支援纱厂生产，河北省晋县良棉轧花厂给花纱布公司加工大批籽棉。这些籽棉正源源运到厂里来。
