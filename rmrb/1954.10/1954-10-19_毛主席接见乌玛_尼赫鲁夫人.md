Title: Chairman Mao Receives Mrs. Uma Nehru
Author:
Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th—Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Mrs. Uma Nehru, head of the Indian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation and member of the Indian Parliament, on the evening of September 30th.

Present at the reception were: Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, and Peng Zhen.

### 毛主席接见乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人

1954-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人
    【新华社十八日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东曾在九月三十日晚接见了印中友好协会访华代表团团长、印度国会议员乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人。
    接见时在座的有：刘少奇，周恩来，陈云，邓小平和彭真。
