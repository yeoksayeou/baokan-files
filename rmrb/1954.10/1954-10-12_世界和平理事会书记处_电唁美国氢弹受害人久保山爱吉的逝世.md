Title: World Peace Council Secretariat Sends Condolences on the Death of Aikichi Kuboyama, Victim of U.S. Hydrogen Bomb
Author:
Date: 1954-10-12
Page: 4

    World Peace Council Secretariat
    Sends Condolences on the Death of Aikichi Kuboyama, Victim of U.S. Hydrogen Bomb
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 10th] Tokyo dispatch: The World Peace Council Secretariat sent a telegram to the Japan Peace Protection Committee on September 27 to express condolences on the death of Aikichi Kuboyama, who was killed by the American hydrogen bomb.
    The telegram said: "We are deeply saddened to learn of the death of Aikichi Kuboyama, victim of the hydrogen bomb. All mankind must not relax its vigilance against the threat and danger of the criminal use of atomic weapons."
    The telegram also said: "The world's forces for peace, in their struggle to ban weapons of mass destruction, fully support the Japanese people."

### 世界和平理事会书记处  电唁美国氢弹受害人久保山爱吉的逝世

1954-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会书记处
    电唁美国氢弹受害人久保山爱吉的逝世
     【新华社十日讯】东京消息：世界和平理事会书记处九月二十七日致电日本拥护和平委员会，吊唁被美国氢弹杀害的久保山爱吉的逝世。
    电文说：“惊闻氢弹受害人久保山爱吉逝世，我们感到非常悲痛。全人类对于原子武器的犯罪性的使用的威胁和危险，决不能放松警惕。”
    电文又说：“世界和平力量在为禁止大规模破坏性武器而进行的斗争中，完全支持日本人民。”
