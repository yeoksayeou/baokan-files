Title: Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China, Our Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian, Holds Reception; Soviet Communist Party and Government Leaders Attend

Author: 
Date: 1954-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

To celebrate the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Wentian, our Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, held a reception in Moscow on October 1st.

The following foreign envoys to the Soviet Union attended the reception: Burmese Ambassador U Wang, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Rim Hai, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Nguyen Luong Bang, Polish Ambassador Lewikowski, Czechoslovak Ambassador Vosahlik, Dutch Ambassador Mattos, Mongolian Ambassador Ajilbish, Yugoslav Ambassador Vidiz, East German Ambassador Appelt, British Ambassador Hayter, Bulgarian Ambassador Lukanov, Indonesian Ambassador Subandrio, Israeli Ambassador Eliashiv; Luxembourg Minister Blum, Finnish Minister Gartz, Syrian Minister Hani; Norwegian Chargé d'affaires Ekland, Pakistani Chargé d'affaires Ahmed, Hungarian Chargé d'affaires Kovacs, Albanian Chargé d'affaires Hero, Afghan Chargé d'affaires Kayum, Romanian Chargé d'affaires Duku, Swedish Chargé d'affaires Uller-Repin, Indian Chargé d'affaires Gore, Swiss Chargé d'affaires Stutz, Danish Chargé d'affaires Larsen, as well as personnel from various embassies and legations.

Also attending the reception were Indonesian trade delegation leader Sanusi, Mongolian cultural delegation leader Zelenjoch and the delegation leader of the Vietnam-Soviet Friendship Association, Tran Huy Lieu.

On the Chinese side, the following attended the reception: Embassy Counselors Wen Ning, Xu Tiemin, and Li Tao, Commercial Counselors Liu Fang and Xu Dashen, Deputy Military Attaché Lieutenant Colonel Shi Xia, and responsible staff members of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Soviet Union.

Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Soviet Union, delivered a speech at the reception, outlining the achievements made since the founding of the People's Republic of China five years ago. K.E. Voroshilov, G.M. Malenkov, and V.M. Molotov also delivered speeches at the meeting.

The reception proceeded in an extremely warm and friendly atmosphere throughout.

### 庆祝中华人民共和国建国五周年  我驻苏联大使张闻天举行招待会  苏联共产党和政府领导人出席

1954-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国建国五周年
    我驻苏联大使张闻天举行招待会
    苏联共产党和政府领导人出席
