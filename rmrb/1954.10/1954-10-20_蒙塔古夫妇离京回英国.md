Title: The Montagus Leave Beijing for England
Author:
Date: 1954-10-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: Mr. Montague, Secretary of the Secretariat of the World Peace Council, and his wife, who were invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to attend our National Day celebrations, left Beijing for England on the morning of the 18th.
    During their stay in China, Mr. and Mrs. Montague attended the First Session of the National People's Congress, participated in the National Day celebrations, and also visited Shanghai and Hangzhou.
    Before Mr. and Mrs. Montague left Beijing to return to their country, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Liu Guanyi, Li Yimang, and others from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace hosted a farewell banquet.

### 蒙塔古夫妇离京回英国

1954-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙塔古夫妇离京回英国
      【新华社十八日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来参加我国国庆观礼的世界和平理事会书记处书记蒙塔古和他的夫人已在十八日上午离北京回英国。
    蒙塔古夫妇在中国期间，曾旁听了我全国人民代表大会第一次会议，参加了国庆观礼，还到上海、杭州游览参观。
    在蒙塔古夫妇离开北京返国前夕，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会廖承志、刘宁一、刘贯一、李一氓等曾设宴欢送。
