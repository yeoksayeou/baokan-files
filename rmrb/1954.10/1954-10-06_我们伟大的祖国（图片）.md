Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-10-06

Our great motherland.

In recent years, the culture and art of all ethnic groups in our country have greatly developed. This is Mao Yihan, a folk artist from a herdsman family in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. He sings self-composed songs and is welcomed by the herdsmen.

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国各民族的文化艺术几年来有很大的发展。这是内蒙古自治区牧民出身的民间艺人毛依汗。他唱着自编的歌曲，受到牧民们的欢迎。
