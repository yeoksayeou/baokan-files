Title: Romanian, Mongolian, Czechoslovakian, Hungarian, Bulgarian, and Albanian Government Delegations Visit Various Locations

Author:
Date: 1954-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The head of the Government Delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, Comrade Gheorghe Apostol, member of the Political Bureau and First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and member Comrade Grigore Preoteasa, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Romanian People's Republic, who were invited to China to attend the celebrations for the 5th anniversary of the founding of the country, left Beijing on the 5th to visit various locations. They were accompanied by Song Shaowen, Deputy Minister of Light Industry of the People's Republic of China, and Guo Zuowen, the Romanian Ambassador to China.

During their stay in Beijing, the Government Delegation of the Romanian People's Republic visited the Guanting Reservoir, the State-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory, Peking University, the Liangjiadian Agricultural Production Cooperative in the Haidian District of Beijing suburbs, and the Wulidian State-owned Farm, and also toured scenic spots and historical sites such as the Summer Palace, the Temple of Azure Clouds, the Forbidden City, the Yonghe Temple, the Temple of Heaven, the Western Suburban Park, Beihai Park, and the Ming Tombs.

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Jambyn Sampildondov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, and the Government Delegation of the Bulgarian People's Republic, headed by Comrade Raiko Damyanov, Member of the Political Committee of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Bulgarian People's Republic, left Beijing on the same plane on the 5th to visit various locations. They were accompanied by Yao Yilin, Deputy Minister of Commerce of the People's Republic of China.

During their stay in Beijing, the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic visited the Guanting Reservoir, the State-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory, and Peking University, and also toured the Forbidden City, the Yonghe Temple, Beihai Park, and the Temple of Azure Clouds; the Government Delegation of the Bulgarian People's Republic visited the Guanting Reservoir and the Dongran Agricultural Production Cooperative in the Haidian District of Beijing suburbs, and also toured scenic spots and historical sites such as Beihai Park, the Yonghe Temple, the Temple of Heaven, the Forbidden City, the Summer Palace, the Temple of Azure Clouds, and the Ming Tombs.

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Government Delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, headed by Comrade Václav Kopeckij, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Culture of the Czechoslovak Republic, left Beijing on the 5th to visit various locations. They were accompanied by Dong Chuncai, Deputy Minister of Education of the People's Republic of China.

During their stay in Beijing, the delegation visited the Zhang Guozhuang Agricultural Production Cooperative in the suburbs, the Guanting Reservoir, and the Central Institute for Nationalities, and toured scenic spots and historical sites such as the Temple of Azure Clouds, the Forbidden City, the Yonghe Temple, the Temple of Heaven, and the Summer Palace.

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Government Delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, headed by Comrade András Hegedüs , First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Agriculture of the Hungarian People's Republic, Member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, left Beijing on the 5th to visit various locations. They were accompanied by Liu Ruilong, Deputy Minister of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China.

During their stay in Beijing, the delegation visited the Guanting Reservoir, the Beijing Agricultural University, Peking University, the Dongran Agricultural Production Cooperative in the Haidian District of Beijing suburbs, and the North China Institute of Agricultural Science, and also toured scenic spots and historical sites such as the Summer Palace, Beihai Park, the Forbidden City, the Working People's Palace of Culture, and the Yonghe Temple.

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Government Delegation of the Albanian People's Republic, headed by Comrade Behar Shtylla, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Albanian People's Republic, left Beijing on the 5th to visit various locations. They were accompanied by Xie Xuegong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China.

During their stay in Beijing, the delegation visited the State-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory, and toured scenic spots and historical sites such as the Summer Palace, the Forbidden City, and the Ming Tombs.

### 罗、蒙、捷、匈、保、阿政府代表团去各地参观

1954-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    罗、蒙、捷、匈、保、阿政府代表团去各地参观
   【新华社五日讯】应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长、罗马尼亚工人党中央政治局委员、书记处第一书记格·阿波斯托尔，团员、罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部第一副部长格·普利奥蒂亚萨，在五日离开北京去各地参观。陪同前往的，有中华人民共和国轻工业部副部长宋劭文和罗马尼亚驻我国大使郭佐文。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团在北京期间，曾参观了官厅水库、国营北京第一棉纺织厂、北京大学、北京郊区海淀区亮家店村农业生产合作社和五里店国营农场，并游览了颐和园、碧云寺、故宫、雍和宫、天坛、西郊公园、北海和十三陵等名胜古迹。
   【新华社五日讯】以蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席扎·桑布为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团和以保加利亚共产党中央政治局委员、保加利亚人民共和国部长会议副主席拉·达米扬诺夫为首的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团，在五日同机离开北京去各地参观。陪同前往的，有中华人民共和国商业部副部长姚依林。
    蒙古人民共和国政府代表团在北京期间，曾参观了官厅水库、国营北京第一棉纺织厂和北京大学，并游览了故宫、雍和宫、北海和碧云寺；保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团曾参观了官厅水库、北京郊区海淀区东冉村农业生产合作社，并游览了北海、雍和宫、天坛、故宫、颐和园、碧云寺和十三陵等名胜古迹。
    【新华社五日讯】以捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央政治局委员、捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理兼文化部部长瓦·柯别茨基为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团，在五日离开北京到各地参观。陪同前往的，有中华人民共和国教育部副部长董纯才。
    代表团在北京期间，曾参观了市郊张郭庄农业生产合作社、官厅水库、中央民族学院，游览了碧云寺、故宫、雍和宫、天坛、颐和园等名胜。
    【新华社五日讯】以匈牙利人民共和国部长会议第一副主席兼农业部部长、匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会委员安·赫格居斯为首的匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团，在五日离京去各地参观。陪同前往的有中华人民共和国农业部副部长刘瑞龙。
    代表团在京期间，曾参观了官厅水库、北京农业大学、北京大学、北京郊区海淀区东冉村农业生产合作社和华北农业科学研究所，并游览了颐和园、北海、故宫、劳动人民文化宫和雍和宫等名胜古迹。
   【新华社五日讯】以阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部部长贝·什图拉为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团，在五日离开北京去各地参观。陪同前往的，有中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长解学恭。
    代表团在北京期间，曾参观了国营北京第一棉纺织厂，并游览了颐和园、故宫和十三陵等名胜古迹。
