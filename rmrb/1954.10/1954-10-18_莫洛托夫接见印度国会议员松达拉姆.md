Title: Molotov Receives Indian Member of Parliament Sundaram
Author:
Date: 1954-10-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 17th: TASS, Moscow, October 16th: V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, received R. Sundaram, an Indian public figure and member of parliament, on the 16th, who is visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 莫洛托夫接见印度国会议员松达拉姆

1954-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫接见印度国会议员松达拉姆
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十六日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫，十六日接见了应苏联对外文化协会的邀请前来苏联访问的印度公众领袖和国会议员朗·松达拉姆。
