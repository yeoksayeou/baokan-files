Title: Two Musicians from East Germany Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-13
Page: 4

    Two Musicians from East Germany Arrive in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th) According to the 1954 implementation plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Professor Franz Langer and Weli Kaufmann, two musicians from the German Democratic Republic who are visiting our country, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 12th.

### 民主德国音乐家两人到京

1954-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国音乐家两人到京
    【新华社十二日讯】根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国访问的德意志民主共和国音乐家弗朗兹·朗格尔教授和韦利·考夫曼两人，十二日下午到达北京。
