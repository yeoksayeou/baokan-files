Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru Departs for Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1954-10-17
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 15) Indian Prime Minister Nehru of the Republic of India, at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, left New Delhi by plane at 7:15 am on October 15th to begin his visit to Beijing.

Accompanying Prime Minister Nehru on his visit to China are his daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, and Mr. N.R. Pillai, Secretary-General of the Indian Government's Ministry of External Affairs, along with eight others.

President of the Republic of India, Rajendra Prasad, saw him off at the airport.

Also at the airport to see him off were ministers and senior officials from various departments of the Indian government, as well as senior generals and officers from the Indian Army, Navy, and Air Force.

The chief ministers of various provinces (states) of India who are currently in Delhi were also at the airport to see him off.

Shen Jian, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in India, embassy officials, Kong Yuan, head of the Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China currently in India, deputy head Yang Lin, and members of the delegation also went to the airport to see him off.

Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in India were also at the airport to see him off.

Approximately three hundred people went to the airport to bid farewell. Many people presented Prime Minister Nehru with flower garlands.

Prime Minister Nehru will make brief stopovers in Yangon and Hanoi on his way to Beijing.

### 印度总理尼赫鲁启程来我国访问

1954-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁启程来我国访问
    【新华社新德里十五日电】应中华人民共和国政府的邀请访问中国的印度共和国总理尼赫鲁在十月十五日上午七时十五分乘飞机离开新德里，启程前往北京。
    随同尼赫鲁总理访问中国的有他的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人和印度政府外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱先生等八人。
    印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德到机场送行。
    到机场送行的还有印度政府各部部长和高级官员。印度陆、海、空军高级将领和军官。
    到机场送行的还有目前在德里的印度各省（邦）政府的首席部长们。
    中华人民共和国驻印度大使馆临时代办申健和大使馆官员，现在印度的中华人民共和国贸易代表团团长孔原、副团长杨琳及代表团人员等也到机场送行。
    到机场送行的还有各国驻印度的外交使节和外交官员。
    前往机场欢送的共约三百人。许多人向尼赫鲁总理献花环。
    尼赫鲁总理在前往北京途中将在仰光、河内等地稍作逗留。
