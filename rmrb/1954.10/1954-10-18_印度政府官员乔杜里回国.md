Title: Indian Government Official Chowdhury Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-18
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 17th. Indian Assam Province Minister of Food and Agriculture Chowdhury, who came to China to visit and investigate agriculture, arrived in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 15th and left Guangzhou on the 16th via Hong Kong to return to his country. Li Wenhua, Deputy Director of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government Agricultural Department, and Ouyang Qing, Deputy Director of the Grain Department, went to the station to see him off.

### 印度政府官员乔杜里回国

1954-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    印度政府官员乔杜里回国
    【新华社广州十七日电】前来我国访问并考察农业的印度阿萨姆省粮食与农业部部长乔杜里在十五日下午到达广州，十六日离开广州经香港返国。广东省人民政府农业厅副厅长李文华和粮食厅副厅长欧阳清都到车站欢送。
