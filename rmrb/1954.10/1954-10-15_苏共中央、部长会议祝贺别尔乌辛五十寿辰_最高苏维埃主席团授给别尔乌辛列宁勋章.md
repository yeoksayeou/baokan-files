Title: The CPC Central Committee and the Council of Ministers Congratulate Beria on His Fiftieth Birthday; The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet Awards Beria the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1954-10-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 14th: TASS Moscow, 13th: The congratulatory message of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union to Mikhail Georgievich Beria, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, on his fiftieth birthday:

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, on your fiftieth birthday, extend warm congratulations to you, an outstanding activist of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet state.

    We wish you, our friend and comrade, dear Mikhail Georgievich, good health and long life for the happiness of the Soviet people and our great motherland, and further achievements in your work.

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
                                      The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union
      Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 14th: TASS Moscow, 13th: Decree of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union on awarding the Order of Lenin to M.G. Beria:

    In view of Comrade M.G. Beria's merits to the Communist Party and the Soviet state, on the occasion of the fiftieth birthday of Comrade M.G. Beria, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Comrade M.G. Beria is hereby awarded the Order of Lenin.

                                          Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, K. Voroshilov
                                              Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, N. Pegov
                                                October 13, 1954, in the Moscow Kremlin

### 苏共中央、部长会议祝贺别尔乌辛五十寿辰  最高苏维埃主席团授给别尔乌辛列宁勋章

1954-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央、部长会议祝贺别尔乌辛五十寿辰
    最高苏维埃主席团授给别尔乌辛列宁勋章
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十三日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议向苏联部长会议副主席米·格·别尔乌辛祝贺五十寿辰的贺词：
    苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议，在你五十寿辰的时候，向你，苏联共产党和苏维埃国家的杰出活动家，表示热烈的祝贺。
    谨祝你，我们的朋友和同志，亲爱的米哈伊尔·格奥尔基耶维奇为了苏联人民和我们伟大祖国的幸福而健康长寿，并在工作中获得进一步的成就。
    苏联共产党中央委员会
                                      苏联部长会议
      【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十三日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团关于以列宁勋章授予米·格·别尔乌辛的命令：
    在苏联部长会议副主席米·格·别尔乌辛同志五十寿辰的时候，鉴于他对共产党和苏维埃国家的功绩，特授给米·格·别尔乌辛同志以列宁勋章。
                                          苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·伏罗希洛夫
                                              苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书尼·别哥夫
                                                一九五四年十月十三日于莫斯科克里姆林宫
