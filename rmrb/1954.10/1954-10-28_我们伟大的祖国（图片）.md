Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-10-28
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Zhoujiazhuang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Jinxian County, Hebei Province, a cotton-producing area, carried out single-plant selection of cotton this year. The cooperative's technicians and students from Hebei Agricultural College, who are interning in the cooperative, are jointly observing the cotton fiber in the field, preparing to select 100,000 jin of high-quality seed cotton.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    河北省产棉区晋县周家庄农业生产合作社今年进行了棉花单株选种。社里的技术员正和在社里实习的河北农学院学生在田间共同观察棉花的纤维情况，准备选十万斤品质优良的籽棉。
