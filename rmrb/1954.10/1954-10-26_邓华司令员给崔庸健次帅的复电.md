Title: Commander Deng Hua's Reply Telegram to Vice Marshal Choi Yong-kun
Author: Deng Hua
Date: 1954-10-26
Page: 1

    Commander Deng Hua's Reply Telegram to Vice Marshal Choi Yong-kun
    To Comrade Choi Yong-kun, Vice Marshal and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea:
    Your telegram of October 24, 1954, is gratefully acknowledged. I extend my heartfelt thanks to you and all the officers and men of the Korean People's Army for your congratulations.
    For the sake of peace in Asia and the world, may the fighting friendship between the armed forces of the Chinese and Korean peoples be increasingly consolidated and developed.
          Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, Deng Hua
                    October 25, 1954

### 邓华司令员给崔庸健次帅的复电
邓华
1954-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    邓华司令员给崔庸健次帅的复电
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫相崔庸健次帅：
    一九五四年十月二十四日来电敬悉。承致祝贺，我谨向您和朝鲜人民军全体将士致以衷心的谢意。
    为了亚洲和世界的和平，愿中朝两国人民武装力量的战斗友谊日臻巩固和发展。
          中国人民志愿军司令员　邓华
                    一九五四年十月二十五日
