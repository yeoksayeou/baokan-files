Title: Delegation of Trade Union from Britain-China Friendship Association Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: The delegation of trade union from the Britain-China Friendship Association, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country, left Beijing by plane this morning, October 23rd, to return home.

The delegation arrived in our country on September 16th and visited Beijing, Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Tianjin, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Wuhan, Chongqing, and Chengdu for more than a month. They attended the National Day celebrations in Beijing, audited the first session of the First National People's Congress, and held discussions with the leaders of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. They visited many factories, mines, and cooperatives in various places. They saw the Guanting Reservoir and the Dujiangyan irrigation project with a history of more than 2,000 years, as well as the Wuhan dikes and the Hanshui Railway Bridge which is about to be completed. They also toured the West Lake and famous historical sites in various places.

### 英中友协工会代表团回国

1954-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    英中友协工会代表团回国
    【新华社二十三日讯】应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的英中友好协会工会代表团，在二十三日上午乘飞机离北京转道返国。
    代表团在九月十六日到达我国，在北京、沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、天津、上海、杭州、武汉、重庆和成都访问了一个多月。他们曾在北京参加了我国国庆观礼，旁听了第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议，并和中华全国总工会负责人座谈。他们在各地参观了许多工厂、矿山和合作社。他们看到了官厅水库和有二千多年历史的都江堰水利工程，也看到了武汉堤防和即将建成的汉水铁桥。他们还游览了西湖和各地的名胜古迹。
