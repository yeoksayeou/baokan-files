Title: Commander of the US Seventh Fleet Again Visits Taiwan and Holds Secret Talks with Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Military Heads
Author: 
Date: 1954-10-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 26th: Commander of the US Seventh Fleet, Pride, arrived in Taiwan again on the 25th to conduct activities. According to a United Press report, a US military spokesman in Taipei stated that Pride's visit to Taiwan was to plot with Chiang Kai-shek bandit military heads to obstruct the Chinese people from liberating Taiwan. After arriving in Taiwan, Pride immediately held secret talks with Chiang Kai-shek bandit military heads. Prior to the talks, Pride also held secret discussions with Chase, the head of the US Military Assistance Advisory Group in Taiwan, and Kiewitt, the commander of the US Seventh Fleet Task Force 72, who had arrived in Taiwan as early as the 22nd.

### 美国第七舰队司令又到台湾和蒋贼军头目举行秘密会谈

1954-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    美国第七舰队司令又到台湾和蒋贼军头目举行秘密会谈
      【新华社二十六日讯】美国第七舰队司令普赖德又在二十五日到达台湾进行活动。据合众社报道，在台北的美国军方发言人表示，普赖德这次到台湾，是为了和蒋贼军头目策划阻挠中国人民解放台湾的问题。普赖德在到达台湾后，立即和蒋贼军头目举行了秘密会谈。在会谈之前，普赖德还同美国驻台湾的军事援助顾问团团长蔡斯和早在二十二日到达台湾的美国第七舰队第七十二特遣舰队司令基维特进行了秘密讨论。
