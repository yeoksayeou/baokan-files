Title: Premier Zhou Receives Indonesian Art Troupe Leader Nasution
Author:
Date: 1954-10-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai received Mengoadas Nasution, leader of the Indonesian Art Troupe, at 1:00 PM on October 2nd, and troupe member Henk Ngantung (painter) accepted an oil painting presented by the troupe.

Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu was also present at the reception.

Also present at the reception were Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations.

### 周总理接见印度尼西亚艺术团团长纳苏申

1954-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印度尼西亚艺术团团长纳苏申
    【新华社二日讯】我国务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在十月二日下午一时接见了印度尼西亚艺术团团长孟阿达斯·纳苏申，团员亨克·昂统（画家）并接受该团赠送的油画一幅。
    接见时，印度尼西亚驻我国大使莫诺努图也在座。
    接见时在座的，有我外交部亚洲司司长陈家康，对外文化联络事务局局长洪深。
