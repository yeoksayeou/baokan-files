Title: List of Deputy Secretaries-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-10-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The list of Deputy Secretaries-General of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China is as follows:

    Zhang Suyuan, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government
    Wu Kejian, former Secretary-General of the East China Administrative Committee
    Qu Wu, former Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
    Yu Xinqing, former Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government Committee
    Sun Qimeng, former Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
    Xin Zhichao, former Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
