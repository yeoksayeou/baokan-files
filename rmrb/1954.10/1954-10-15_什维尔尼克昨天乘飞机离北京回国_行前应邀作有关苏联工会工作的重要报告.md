Title: Shvernik Returns to His Country by Plane from Beijing Yesterday; Delivered an Important Report on the Work of Soviet Trade Unions Before His Departure
Author:
Date: 1954-10-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: Nikolai Mikhailovich Shvernik, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, delivered an important report on the work of Soviet trade unions at the conference of Chinese trade union cadres in the capital yesterday afternoon. The report meeting was presided over by Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
    Among those attending the report meeting were Liu Ningyi, Liu Changsheng, and Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Xu Zhizhen, Li Jiebo, Liu Ziku, Zhang Weizhen, and Li Zaiwen, Secretaries of the Secretariat; Wu Yuzhang, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Education Trade Union; heads of national committees of various industrial trade unions; and more than 1,000 responsible cadres from the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions and grassroots trade unions.
    In his report, Shvernik said, "The Soviet trade unions, established and nurtured by the Communist Party, have consolidated their ranks in every stage of the struggle for socialism, uniting the working class around the Party and the government, and confidently guiding the working class along the path pointed out by V.I. Lenin and J.V. Stalin, the successor to Lenin's cause." Shvernik then described the role of Soviet trade unions in building socialism, developing industry and agriculture, and improving the material living conditions and cultural level of the working class. Speaking of socialist competition, Shvernik said: Socialist competition is a powerful force for realizing the national industrialization at Bolshevik speed. He said, "The socialist competition carried out by trade unions promotes the growing creative initiative of workers." He also pointed out the various forms of competition put forward by trade unions in the struggle of socialist construction. Shvernik also spoke about the great significance of production meetings, rational wage systems, social insurance, and the mass communist education conducted by trade unions.
    Shvernik's vivid and specific introduction to the experiences of Soviet trade unions provided great inspiration and help to Chinese trade union workers and was warmly welcomed by them.
    Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: Nikolai Mikhailovich Shvernik, a member of the Soviet government delegation and Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, who was invited to our country to participate in the celebration of the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing by plane at 6:30 a.m. on the 14th to return to his country.
    Those who went to the airport to see him off included Peng Zhen, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Wu Xiuquan, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.
    Also present at the airport to see him off were Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Liu Ningyi, Liu Changsheng, and Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairmen; Xu Zhizhen, Li Jiebo, Liu Ziku, and Li Zaiwen, Secretaries of the Secretariat; Jin Zhifu, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Coal Miners' Union; and Kang Yonghe, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China First Machine Industry Union, and others.
    Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin and embassy officials also went to the airport to see him off.

### 什维尔尼克昨天乘飞机离北京回国  行前应邀作有关苏联工会工作的重要报告

1954-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    什维尔尼克昨天乘飞机离北京回国
    行前应邀作有关苏联工会工作的重要报告
    【新华社十四日讯】全苏工会中央理事会主席尼·米·什维尔尼克十三日下午应中华全国总工会的邀请，在首都中国工会干部大会上作了有关苏联工会工作的重要报告。报告会由中华全国总工会主席赖若愚主持。
    出席报告会的有中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、刘长胜、朱学范，书记处书记许之桢、李颉伯、刘子久、张维桢、栗再温，中国教育工会全国委员会主席吴玉章，以及各产业工会全国委员会负责人，北京市总工会、各基层工会负责干部一千多人。
    什维尔尼克在报告中说：“由共产党所缔造和教养起来的苏联工会在为社会主义而斗争的各个阶段中巩固了自己的队伍，把工人阶级团结在党和政府的周围，满怀信心地引导工人阶级沿着乌·伊·列宁以及列宁事业的继承者约·维·斯大林所指出的道路前进。”什维尔尼克接着叙述了苏联工会在建设社会主义，为发展工业、农业和改善工人阶级物质生活状况及提高他们的文化水平的斗争中的作用。在谈到社会主义竞赛时，什维尔尼克说：社会主义竞赛是以布尔什维克的速度实现国家工业化的强大力量。他说：“工会所开展的社会主义竞赛，发扬了工人的日益增长的创造积极性。”他并指出了在社会主义建设的斗争中，工会所提出的各种各样的竞赛形式。什维尔尼克还在报告中讲述了生产会议、合理的工资制度、社会保险以及关于工会进行群众性的共产主义教育的重要意义。
    什维尔尼克的生动而具体地介绍苏联工会经验的报告给予中国工会工作者以极大的启发和帮助，并受到他们的热烈欢迎。
    【新华社十四日讯】应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的苏联政府代表团团员、全苏工会中央理事会主席尼·米·什维尔尼克，已在十四日晨六时半乘飞机离开北京返国。
    到机场欢送的，有中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，国务院秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长伍修权、办公厅副主任阎宝航、交际处处长王倬如。
    到机场欢送的，还有中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，副主席刘宁一、刘长胜、朱学范，书记处书记许之桢、李颉伯、刘子久、栗再温，中国煤矿工会全国委员会主席金直夫，中国第一机械工会筹备委员会主任康永和等。
    苏联驻华大使尤金和使馆官员也到机场送行。
