Title: After Attending the National Day Ceremony of the German Democratic Republic and Visiting Various Towns, the Chinese Government Delegation Leaves Berlin for Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1954-10-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 20th - Chen Yi, head of the People's Republic of China Government Delegation and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Zhu Kezhen, delegation member and Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, left Berlin for Warsaw by plane at 8:00 AM on the 20th. During its stay in the German Democratic Republic, the delegation participated in the celebration of the 5th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, visited towns, factories, and cultural monuments in various places, and observed the election of the 2nd People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic.

Those who came to the airport to see them off included Nuschke, Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister, Ölsner, member of the Central Political Bureau of the Socialist Unity Party, Mückenberger, alternate member, Ziller, secretary, and many other government officials.

Also at the airport to see them off were Soviet Ambassador to Germany Pushkin, DPRK Ambassador to Germany Park Kil-ryong, and others.

Ji Pengfei, our ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, and embassy staff also came to the airport to see them off.

Before boarding the plane, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his party reviewed the guard of honor, and the band played the national anthems of the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic.
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 21st - Chen Yi, head of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Zhu Kezhen, delegation member and Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, arrived in Warsaw by plane from Berlin on the 20th at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of Poland.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Jędrychowski, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, Naszkowski, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs, Sokolowski, Minister of Culture and Arts, Wierżbicki, Vice President of the Polish Academy of Sciences, Major General Okęski, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, Bartol, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Hermann, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to Poland, Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, and embassy staff also welcomed them at the airport.

After disembarking, Vice Premier Chen Yi reviewed the guard of honor.

### 参加民主德国国庆典礼和访问各地城镇后我国政府代表团离柏林到华沙

1954-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    参加民主德国国庆典礼和访问各地城镇后
    我国政府代表团离柏林到华沙
    【新华社柏林二十日电】中华人民共和国政府代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅，团员、中国科学院副院长竺可桢，在二十日上午八时乘飞机离柏林去华沙。代表团在德意志民主共和国逗留期间，参加了德意志民主共和国成立五周年国庆典礼，访问了各地城镇工厂和文化古迹，并参观了第二届德意志民主共和国人民议院的选举。
    到机场欢送的，有德意志民主共和国副总理努舍克、副总理兼外长博尔茨，统一社会党中央政治局委员厄斯纳、候补委员穆肯柏格尔、书记齐勒和政府其他负责人多人。
    在机场欢送的还有苏联驻德大使普希金，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻德大使朴吉龙等。
    我驻德意志民主共和国大使姬鹏飞和大使馆人员也到机场欢送。
    陈毅副总理等一行在上飞机前检阅了仪仗队，乐队高奏中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国的国歌。
   【新华社华沙二十一日电】中华人民共和国政府代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅，团员、中国科学院副院长竺可桢，应波兰人民共和国政府的邀请，二十日乘飞机由柏林到达华沙。
    到机场欢迎的有：波兰部长会议副主席英德里霍夫斯基，外交部代理部长纳斯科夫斯基，文化艺术部部长索科尔斯基，波兰科学院副院长维日比茨基，国防部代表奥凯恩斯基少将，外交部交际司司长巴托尔等。
    德意志民主共和国驻波兰大使赫曼，中国驻波兰大使曾涌泉和大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    陈毅副总理下机后检阅了仪仗队。
