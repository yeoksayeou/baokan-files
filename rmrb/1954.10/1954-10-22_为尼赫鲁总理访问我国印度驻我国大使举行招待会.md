Title: Indian Ambassador to China Holds Reception for Prime Minister Nehru's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1954-10-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 21st: The Indian Ambassador to China, Raghunath Rai Kaji, held a reception on the evening of the 21st to mark the visit of the Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru, to China.

Those attending the reception from the Indian side included the Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru, as well as Nehru's daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, N.R. Pillai, Deputy Secretaries of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, Bahadur Singh, and M.L. Gainder, K.V. Rustamji, N.K. Seshan, and five others.

Those attending the reception from the Chinese side included Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liu Shaoqi, and Premier of the State Council, Zhou Enlai.

Also attending the reception were Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Soong Ching Ling, Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, the Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, Committee Member Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain, and other committee members.

Also attending the reception were Vice Premiers of the State Council, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, He Long, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Ministers of the various ministries and Chairmen of Committees Xie Juezai, Luo Ruiqing, Shi Liang, Qian Ying, Bo Yibo, Zhang Naiqi, Zeng Shan, Ye Jizhuang, Huang Jing, Zhao Erlu, Chen Yu, Li Siguang, Jiang Guangnai, Jia Tuofu, Sha Qianli, Teng Daiyuan, Zhang Bojun, Zhu Xuefan, Liao Luyan, Liang Xi, Fu Zuoyi, Shen Yanbing, Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Xiruo, Li Dequan, He Xiangning, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Li Kenong, Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, as well as vice ministers of various ministries and vice chairmen of various committees.

Also attending the reception were President of the Supreme People's Court, Dong Biwu, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council, Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, Long Yun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Su Yu, Commander of the Navy, Xiao Jingguang, and Commander of the Air Force, Liu Yalou.

Also attending the reception were Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, and Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison, Nie Rongzhen.

China's Ambassador to India, Yuan Zhongxian, also attended the reception.

Also attending the reception were President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, Vice President Chen Hansheng and directors, as well as responsible persons from organizations such as the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the All China Democratic Women's Federation, and the China Islamic Association, and figures from the capital's scientific, educational, literary, artistic, and journalistic circles.

The heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in China who attended the reception included: Ambassador of Burma, U Hla Maung, Ambassador of Pakistan, Raza, Ambassador of Czechoslovakia, Kousal, Ambassador of Poland, Kirylok, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of Mongolia, Ochirbat, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic, Koenig, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Choi Il, Ambassador of the Soviet Union, Yudin, Ambassador of Bulgaria, Dimov, Ambassador of Albania, Nase, Ambassador of Hungary, Siklós, Minister of Finland, Sundström, Minister of Denmark, Graae, Minister of Switzerland, Béguin, British Chargé d'affaires, DuVal, Swedish Chargé d'affaires ad interim, Hammarskjöld, Romanian Chargé d'affaires ad interim, Munteanu, Head of the Netherlands Delegation for Negotiations, Sman, and First Secretary of the Indonesian Embassy, Wirjowerdojo, and others.

Mr. Sahib Singh Sokhey, Vice-Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, Academician of the Indian Academy of Sciences, and Member of the Indian Parliament, who is currently visiting China, Mr. Mukherjee, the Mayor of Calcutta, India, and personnel from the Indian Trade Union Congress goodwill delegation also attended the reception.

Mr. Thekin Zin, the head of the Burmese government trade delegation, also attended the reception.

More than 300 people attended the reception in total.

At the reception, Ambassador Raghunath Rai Kaji first raised his glass and said: "Please allow me to toast His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong, the beloved leader of the great Chinese people, a great friend of India, and an active advocate of world peace!" At this moment, the band played the national anthem of the People's Republic of China.

Chairman Mao Zedong then raised his glass and said:
	"The people of China and India are both resolutely for peace. Our two peoples, like the people of the whole world, are resolutely striving for peace.
	For the cooperation between the people of China and India and the prosperity of the two peoples,
	For world peace,
	For the health of the President of the Republic of India, Prasad,
	For Prime Minister Nehru's visit to China and his health,
	For the health of the host of today's banquet, Ambassador Raghunath Rai Kaji,
	Cheers!"
At this moment, the band played the national anthem of the Republic of India.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. (Picture attached)

### 为尼赫鲁总理访问我国印度驻我国大使举行招待会

1954-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    为尼赫鲁总理访问我国印度驻我国大使举行招待会
   【新华社二十一日讯】印度驻我国大使赖嘉文，为印度共和国总理尼赫鲁访问中国，特在二十
  一日晚举行招待会。  
  　印度方面出席招待会的，有印度共和国总理尼赫鲁，以及尼赫鲁总理的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人、印度外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱、印度外交部副秘书巴哈杜尔·辛格和墨·勒·盖恩德、克·弗·鲁斯滕吉、纳·克·塞善等九人。
    中国方面出席招待会的，有中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来。
    出席招待会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、林伯渠、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措，委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和其他委员。
    出席招待会的，还有国务院副总理陈云、邓小平、邓子恢、贺龙、乌兰夫、李富春，秘书长习仲勋，各部部长和委员会主任谢觉哉、罗瑞卿、史良、钱瑛、薄一波、章乃器、曾山、叶季壮、黄敬、赵尔陆、陈郁、李四光、蒋光鼐、贾拓夫、沙千里、滕代远、章伯钧、朱学范、廖鲁言、梁希、傅作义、沈雁冰、杨秀峰、张奚若、李德全、何香凝，外交部副部长李克农、章汉夫、伍修权，以及各部副部长，各委员会副主任。
    出席招待会的，还有最高人民法院院长董必武，国防委员会副主席程潜、张治中、龙云，中国人民解放军副总参谋长粟裕，海军司令员萧劲光，空军司令员刘亚楼。
    出席招待会的，还有北京市市长彭真，京津卫戍司令员聂荣臻。
    我国驻印度大使袁仲贤也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的，还有中印友好协会会长丁西林、副会长陈翰笙和理事，以及中国人民对外文化协会、中华全国民主妇女联合会和中国伊斯兰教协会等团体的负责人，首都科学、教育、文学、艺术、新闻界人士等。
    出席招待会的各国驻我国使节和外交人员，有：缅甸大使吴拉茂，巴基斯坦大使罗查，捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉，波兰大使基里洛克，越南民主共和国大使黄文欢，蒙古大使奥其尔巴特，德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一，苏联大使尤金，保加利亚大使迪莫夫，阿尔巴尼亚大使纳赛，匈牙利大使希克拉丹，芬兰公使孙士敦，丹麦公使格瑞杰生，瑞士公使贝努义，英国代办杜维廉，瑞典临时代办哈马孝尔德，罗马尼亚临时代办孟天努，荷兰谈判代表司曼和印度尼西亚大使馆一等秘书维约维尔多约等。
    正在我国访问的全印和平理事会副主席、印度科学院院士、印度国会议员萨希布·辛格·索克，印度加尔各答市市长慕克吉先生，印度全国工会大会友好代表团人员也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的，还有缅甸政府贸易代表团团长德钦阵。
    出席招待会的共有三百多人。
    招待会上，赖嘉文大使首先举杯致词说：“请允许我为伟大的中国人民的亲爱的领袖、印度的一位伟大的朋友、世界和平的积极提倡者毛泽东主席阁下而干杯！”这时，乐队奏起中华人民共和国国歌。
    毛泽东主席接着举杯致词说：
    “中印两国人民都是坚决主张和平的。我们两国人民像全世界人民一样坚决为和平而努力。
    为中印两国人民的合作和两国人民的繁荣，
    为世界和平，
    为印度共和国总统普拉沙德的健康，
    为尼赫鲁总理到中国访问和他的健康，
    为今天的宴会主人——赖嘉文大使的健康，
  干杯！”
    这时，乐队奏起印度共和国国歌。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。（附图片）
