Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Grand Banquet to Host Indian Prime Minister Nehru
Author:
Date: 1954-10-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 20th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China held a grand banquet on the evening of the 20th to welcome Prime Minister Nehru of the Republic of India, who is visiting China at the invitation of our government.

Mrs. Indira Gandhi, daughter of Prime Minister Nehru, Mr. N.R. Pillai, Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, Mr. Bahadur Singh, Deputy Secretary of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, and Messrs. Gaind, K.F. Rustamji, and N.K. Seshan, who accompanied Prime Minister Nehru on his visit to China, were invited to attend the banquet.

Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

On the Chinese side, those attending the banquet included Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

Also attending the banquet were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian; Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun; Ministers of various ministries and Directors of various commissions Xie Juezai, Luo Ruiqing, Shi Liang, Qian Ying, Bo Yibo, Zhang Naiqi, Zeng Shan, Ye Jizhuang, Wang Heshou, Zhao Erlu, Li Siguang, Jia Tuofu, Sha Qianli, Zhang Bojun, Zhu Xuefan, Liao Luyan, Liang Xi, Fu Zuoyi, Shen Yanbing, Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Xiruo, and Li Dequan; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Li Kenong, Zhang Hanfu, and Wu Xiuquan; Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen; and Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Nie Rongzhen.

Also attending the banquet were Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lin Boqu, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, and the Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, and Standing Committee Member Panchen Erdeni Chokyi Gyaltsen.

Also attending the banquet were President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, and Long Yun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Zhang Zongxun, and Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Xiao Hua and Gan Siqi.

Also attending the banquet were President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Ding Xilin and Vice President Chen Hansheng.

Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, Yuan Zhongxian also attended the banquet.

Also attending the banquet were members of the Standing Committee of the National People' Congress, Vice Ministers and Vice Directors of various ministries and commissions of the People's Republic of China, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court, Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme Procuratorate, members of the Standing Committee and members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Consultative Conference, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible personnel of various departments of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, Vice Mayors and responsible personnel of various departments of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, people from the capital's scientific, educational, literary, artistic, religious, and journalistic circles, and representatives of ethnic minorities, as well as members of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association delegation who had visited India.

The foreign envoys and diplomatic personnel stationed in China who were invited to attend the banquet included: Ambassador of Burma U Hla Maung, Ambassador of Pakistan Raza, Ambassador of Czechoslovakia Kousal, Ambassador of Poland Kirylok, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of Mongolia Ochirbat, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Koenigs, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Choi Il, Ambassador of the Soviet Union Yudin, Ambassador of Bulgaria Dimov, Ambassador of Albania Nase, Ambassador of Hungary Sikladan, Minister of Finland Sundsten, Minister of Denmark গ্রেggeresen, Minister of Switzerland Beno, British Chargé d'affaires Twyman, Swedish Chargé d'affaires Hammarskjöld, Romanian Chargé d'affaires Montanu, and Head of the Dutch Delegation Sman, and others.

Mr. Sokhey, Fellow of the Indian Academy of Sciences, Vice Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, and Member of the Indian Parliament, Mr. Mukherjee, Mayor of Calcutta, India, and members of the Indian All-Union Trade Union Congress friendship delegation who are visiting China, were also invited to attend the banquet.

Members of the Burmese government trade delegation and foreign journalists currently in Beijing were also invited to attend the banquet.

In total, more than 700 people attended the banquet.

At the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech (full text to be published separately). At the end of his speech, he raised his glass to the health of President Prasad of the Republic of India. At this time, the band played the national anthem of the Republic of India.

Prime Minister Nehru then delivered a speech (full text to be published separately). At the end of his speech, he raised his glass to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China. At this time, the band played the national anthem of the People's Republic of China.

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of warmth and friendship. (Attached Pictures)

### 周恩来总理举行盛大宴会招待印度总理尼赫鲁

1954-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理举行盛大宴会招待印度总理尼赫鲁
    【新华社二十日讯】中华人民共和国总理周恩来在二十日晚举行盛大宴会，欢迎应我国政府邀请前来我国访问的印度共和国总理尼赫鲁。
    随同尼赫鲁总理访问我国的尼赫鲁总理的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人、印度外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱、印度外交部副秘书巴哈杜尔·辛格以及盖恩德、克·弗·鲁斯滕吉、纳·克·塞善等九人都应邀出席了宴会。
    印度驻我国大使赖嘉文和使馆人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    中国方面出席宴会的，有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇。
    出席宴会的，还有国务院副总理陈云、邓小平、邓子恢、乌兰夫、李富春、李先念，秘书长习仲勋，各部部长和各委员会主任谢觉哉、罗瑞卿、史良、钱瑛、薄一波、章乃器、曾山、叶季壮、王鹤寿、赵尔陆、李四光、贾拓夫、沙千里、章伯钧、朱学范、廖鲁言、梁希、傅作义、沈雁冰、杨秀峰、张奚若、李德全，外交部副部长李克农、章汉夫、伍修权，北京市市长彭真，京津卫戍司令员聂荣臻。
    出席宴会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措，常务委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞。
    出席宴会的，还有最高人民法院院长董必武，国防委员会副主席程潜、张治中、龙云，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任萧华、甘泗淇。
    出席宴会的，还有中印友好协会会长丁西林、副会长陈翰笙。
    中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤也出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，中华人民共和国各部副部长、各委员会副主任，最高人民法院副院长，最高人民检察院副检察长，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员和委员，中国人民解放军高级将领，中共中央各部门负责工作人员，各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，北京市人民政府副市长和各部门负责人，首都科学、教育、文学、艺术、宗教、新闻界人士和少数民族代表，以及曾访问过印度的中印友好协会代表团人员。
    应邀出席宴会的各国驻我国使节和外交人员有：缅甸大使吴拉茂，巴基斯坦大使罗查，捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉，波兰大使基里洛克，越南民主共和国大使黄文欢，蒙古大使奥其尔巴特，德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一，苏联大使尤金，保加利亚大使迪莫夫，阿尔巴尼亚大使纳赛，匈牙利大使希克拉丹，芬兰公使孙士敦，丹麦公使格瑞杰生，瑞士公使贝努义，英国代办杜维廉，瑞典临时代办哈马孝尔德，罗马尼亚临时代办孟天努，荷兰谈判代表司曼等。
    正在我国访问的印度科学院院士、全印和平理事会副主席、印度国会议员索克先生，印度加尔各答市市长慕克吉先生，印度全国工会大会友好代表团人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的，还有缅甸政府贸易代表团人员和现在北京的各国记者。
    出席宴会的，共有七百多人。
    宴会上，周恩来总理首先致词（全文另发），他在致词结束时，举杯祝印度共和国普拉沙德总统健康。这时，乐队奏起印度共和国国歌。
    尼赫鲁总理接着致词（全文另发），他在致词结束时，举杯祝中华人民共和国毛泽东主席健康。这时，乐队奏起中华人民共和国国歌。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。（附图片）
