Title: China and India Sign Agreement on Import and Export of Tobacco Leaves and Raw Silk
Author:
Date: 1954-10-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 21st: Representatives of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of India, based on the trade agreement concluded between the two countries on October 14th of this year, signed an agreement in New Delhi on October 19th, after friendly and harmonious consultations, regarding the import of 9 million pounds of tobacco leaves by the People's Republic of China from the Republic of India and the import of 90 tons of raw silk by the Republic of India from the People's Republic of China.

Signing the agreement on behalf of the Chinese side was Yang Lin, Deputy Head of the Trade Delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of China. Signing the agreement on behalf of the Indian side was K.B. Lall, Joint Secretary of the Ministry of Commerce and Industry of the Republic of India.

According to this agreement, the China National Import and Export Corporation will directly sign contracts for the purchase of tobacco leaves with Indian entities approved by the Government of the Republic of India. Indian entities approved by the Government of the Republic of India will directly sign contracts for the purchase of raw silk with the China Silk Corporation.

### 中印签订关于进出口烟叶和生丝的协议

1954-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中印签订关于进出口烟叶和生丝的协议
   【新华社新德里二十一日电】中华人民共和国政府和印度共和国政府双方的代表，根据今年十月十四日两国政府缔结的贸易协定，经过友好和谐的商谈后，于十月十九日在新德里签订了中华人民共和国自印度共和国输入九百万磅烟叶和印度共和国自中华人民共和国输入九十吨生丝的协议。
    代表中国方面签订这个协议的是中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团副团长杨琳，代表印度方面签订这个协议的是印度共和国工商部联合秘书克·布·拉尔。
    根据这个协议，中国进出口公司将直接和经过印度共和国政府批准的印度单位签订购买烟叶的合同，经过印度共和国政府批准的印度单位将直接和中国丝绸公司签订购买生丝的合同。
