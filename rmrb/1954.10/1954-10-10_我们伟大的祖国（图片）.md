Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-10-10
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The first phase of the "August 1st" Victory Canal, built by the agricultural production troops of the Xinjiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, has been completed and can irrigate more than 218,000 mu of farmland. This is a scene of people enthusiastically cheering when the sluice gates are opened to release water.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民解放军新疆军区农业生产部队修建的“八一”胜利渠第一期工程已经完成，可灌溉二十一万八千多亩农田。这是开闸放水的时候，参观的人们热烈欢呼的情形。
