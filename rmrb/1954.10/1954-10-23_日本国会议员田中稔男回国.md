Title: Japanese Diet Member Toshio Tanaka Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 22nd. Toshio Tanaka, a member of the Japanese Diet Delegation, arrived in Guangzhou on the 20th and left Guangzhou on the 21st to return to his country.

Toshio Tanaka came to our country for a visit on July 30th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 日本国会议员田中稔男回国

1954-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    日本国会议员田中稔男回国
    【新华社广州二十二日电】日本国会议员代表团团员田中稔男，在二十日到达广州，已在二十一日离广州回国。
      田中稔男是应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民对外文化协会邀请在七月三十日来到我国访问的。
