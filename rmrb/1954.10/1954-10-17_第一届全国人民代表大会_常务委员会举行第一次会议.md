Title: First Session of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress Held
Author:
Date: 1954-10-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th - The first session of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China was held on October 16th.
    The meeting heard and discussed the report by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, on the Sino-Soviet talks. The meeting expressed complete satisfaction with the significant achievements of the Sino-Soviet talks and unanimously approved the report by Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai.
    The meeting passed a decision regarding the approval procedures for treaties concluded with foreign countries.
    After hearing the report of Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, the meeting passed a resolution approving the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Albania.
    The meeting also decided on several appointment matters.

### 第一届全国人民代表大会  常务委员会举行第一次会议

1954-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    第一届全国人民代表大会
    常务委员会举行第一次会议
      【新华社十六日讯】中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会在十月十六日举行第一次会议。
    会议听取并讨论了国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来关于中苏会谈的报告。会议对中苏会谈的重大成果表示完全满意，一致赞成周恩来总理兼外交部长的报告。
    会议通过了关于同外国缔结条约的批准手续的决定。
    会议在听取文化部部长沈雁冰的报告以后，通过了批准中华人民共和国、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国文化合作协定的决议。
    会议并决定了任命事项多起。
