Title: Chairman Liu and Vice Chairman Zhu Host Tea Party for Prime Minister Nehru
Author: 
Date: 1954-10-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: Prime Minister Nehru of the Republic of India, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, and Mr. N.R. Pillai were invited to a tea party hosted by Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, on the afternoon of the 22nd.

    Also present were Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, and Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Panchen Erdeni Qoigyijabu, a member of the Standing Committee.

    Also present were Chen Yun and Ulanhu, Vice Premiers of the State Council.

    Also present was Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court.

    Yuan Zhongxian, Chinese Ambassador to India, and Raghunathrai M. Kidwai, Indian Ambassador to China, were also present.

    In the evening, Prime Minister Nehru and others, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, watched the Yue Opera "The West Chamber," performed by famous actors such as Yuan Xuefen. (Attached picture)
    [Caption] Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, held a tea party on October 22nd to welcome Nehru, Prime Minister of the Republic of India, who was invited by our government to visit our country. From left to right: Raghunathrai M. Kidwai, Yuan Zhongxian, Liu Shaoqi, Nehru, Zhu De.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan

### 刘委员长朱副主席举行茶会招待尼赫鲁总理

1954-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    刘委员长朱副主席举行茶会招待尼赫鲁总理
    【新华社二十二日讯】二十二日下午，印度共和国总理尼赫鲁和英迪拉·甘地夫人、纳·鲁·皮莱先生，应邀出席了中华人民共和国朱德副主席和全国人民代表大会常务委员会刘少奇委员长的茶会。
    在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠、李济深、张澜、沈钧儒、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措，常务委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞。
    在座的还有国务院副总理陈云、乌兰夫。
    在座的还有最高人民法院院长董必武。
    中国驻印度大使袁仲贤和印度驻中国大使赖嘉文也在座。
    晚间，尼赫鲁总理等由周恩来总理陪同，观看了由著名演员袁雪芬等演出的越剧“西厢记”。（附图片）
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇在十月二十二日举行茶会欢迎应我国政府邀请前来我国访问的印度共和国总理尼赫鲁。图中自左至右：赖嘉文、袁仲贤、刘少奇、尼赫鲁、朱德。
    新华社记者  齐观山摄
