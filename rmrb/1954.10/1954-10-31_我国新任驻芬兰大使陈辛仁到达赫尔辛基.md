Title: China's Newly Appointed Ambassador to Finland, Chen Xinren, Arrives in Helsinki
Author:
Date: 1954-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 30th: Helsinki News: Chen Xinren, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Finland, arrived in Helsinki by plane on the 29th.

At the airport to welcome him were Mr. Valikangas, Director of Protocol of the Finnish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Yao Dengshan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Finland, and embassy staff.

Diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, and the German Democratic Republic to Finland also came to the airport to welcome him.

Representatives of the Finland-China Association also came to the airport to welcome him.

### 我国新任驻芬兰大使陈辛仁到达赫尔辛基

1954-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我国新任驻芬兰大使陈辛仁到达赫尔辛基
    【新华社布拉格三十日电】　赫尔辛基消息：中华人民共和国新任驻芬兰共和国特命全权大使陈辛仁二十九日乘飞机到达赫尔辛基。
    到机场欢迎的有芬兰外交部交际司司长瓦利康加斯和中国驻芬兰大使馆临时代办姚登山和使馆人员。
    苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚及德意志民主共和国驻芬兰外交使节也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有芬中协会的代表。
