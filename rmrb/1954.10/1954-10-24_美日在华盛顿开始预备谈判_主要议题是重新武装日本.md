Title: US-Japan Preparatory Talks Begin in Washington; Main Topic is Re-Arming Japan
Author:
Date: 1954-10-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 21st dispatch: US and Japanese government officials have begun preparatory US-Japan talks in Washington.

According to United Press reports, Japanese Minister of International Trade and Industry, Kiichi Aichi, who arrived in Washington on the 18th, and other Japanese officials visited Stassen, Director of the US Foreign Operations Administration, and other related personnel on the same day. They discussed issues such as "strengthening the (Japanese) armed forces," Japan's purchase of surplus American agricultural products, and how to use the proceeds from surplus agricultural products to strengthen Japan's economic militarization. Afterwards, Aichi and others will also hold a series of meetings with US Secretary of State Dulles and others in order to "pave the way" for Yoshida's official visit to the United States.

The content of the formal US-Japan talks, for which the US and Japan are now stepping up preparations, can be clearly seen from the conversations between officials of the US and Japanese governments. Japanese Ambassador to the United States, Sadao Iguchi, said on the 17th:
	"This negotiation will discuss Japan's defense plan." United Press quoted US government officials on the 15th as revealing that the US and Japan would discuss "defense relations between the two countries," namely "increasing Japan's defense forces and what the United States should do next year"; they would discuss "modernizing Japan's defense factories to a certain extent" and the issue of US spending on "overseas procurement" in Japan; furthermore, the United States is also preparing to sign an agreement to sell a large number of surplus commodities to Japan, further enabling American monopoly capital to control Japan's domestic and foreign markets.

### 美日在华盛顿开始预备谈判  主要议题是重新武装日本

1954-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    美日在华盛顿开始预备谈判
    主要议题是重新武装日本
    【新华社二十一日讯】华盛顿消息：日本和美国政府官员已经开始在华盛顿举行美、日会谈的预备谈判。
    据合众社报道，十八日到达华盛顿的日本通商产业相爱知揆一和其他的日本官员，在当天就访问了美国援外事务管理署署长史塔生和其他有关方面的人员，就“加强（日本）武装部队”和日本购买美国剩余农产品以及如何利用剩余农产品的价款来加强日本经济军事化等问题，进行了讨论。在这以后，爱知揆一等还将和美国国务卿杜勒斯等人举行一连串的会谈，以便为吉田到达美国进行的正式会谈“铺平道路”。
    美日双方现在加紧进行准备的美、日正式会谈的内容，从美日两国政府官员的谈话中可以清楚地看出。日本驻美国大使井口贞夫曾在十七日说：
  “这次谈判将讨论日本的防御计划”。合众社在十五日引美国政府官员的话透露，美、日双方将讨论“两国间的防御关系”，即“增加日本防御部队和美国在明年应作些什么”的问题；将讨论“使日本防御工厂进行一定程度的现代化”和美国在日本进行“海外采购”的开支问题；并且，美国还准备签订把大量剩余商品卖给日本的协定，进一步使美国垄断资本控制日本的国内外市场。
