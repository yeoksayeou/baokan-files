Title: Taiwan Air Force Personnel Hu Hongyi Pilots Aircraft in Uprising, Flies Back to Motherland

Author:

Date: 1954-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, October 29th: Taiwan Air Force personnel Hu Hongyi piloted an aircraft on October 26th in an uprising, flying back to the motherland.

　　Hu Hongyi is a student of the 34th class of the Taiwan Air Force Academy. Unable to endure the dark rule of the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, he piloted an AT-6 type No. 200 advanced trainer aircraft from the Air Force Academy airport in Gangshan Township, Taiwan, resolutely revolting and flying back to the motherland, landing safely in a certain area of Fujian Province.

　　Hu Hongyi's patriotic and righteous act of returning to the embrace of the motherland was warmly welcomed by the local people.

　　Hu Hongyi's ancestral home is in Anhui, and his family moved to Hangzhou, Zhejiang in 1949.

### 台湾空军人员胡弘一驾机起义飞回祖国大陆

1954-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    台湾空军人员胡弘一驾机起义飞回祖国大陆
    【新华社福建前线二十九日电】台湾空军人员胡弘一，在十月二十六日驾驶一架飞机起义飞回祖国大陆。
    胡弘一是台湾空军军官学校第三十四期的学生。他因不能忍受蒋介石卖国集团的黑暗统治，从台湾冈山镇空军军官学校机场驾驶AT—六型二零零号高级教练机一架，毅然起义飞回祖国，在福建省某地安全着陆。
    胡弘一返归祖国怀抱的爱国义举，受到当地人民的热烈欢迎。
    胡弘一原籍安徽，他的家庭在一九四九年迁居浙江杭州。
