Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Hosts a Banquet for Prime Minister Nehru
Author:
Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 17th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam hosted a banquet at 7:00 PM (Vietnam time) on the 17th to welcome Indian Prime Minister Nehru.

    Also present at the banquet were Prime Minister Nehru's daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, and the Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Pillai.

    Attending as hosts were: Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong; Minister of National Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the People's Army, Vo Nguyen Giap; Minister of Propaganda, Hoang Minh Giam; Minister of Industry and Commerce, Phan Anh; Minister of Education, Nguyen Van Huyen; Chairman of the Hanoi Military and Administrative Committee, Vuong Thua Vu; Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Vu Dinh Huynh; and Chairman of the Hanoi Women's Union, Nguyen Khoa Dieu Hong.

    Also invited to the banquet was Luo Guibo, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    Also invited to attend the banquet were Desai, Head of the Indian Delegation to the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam; Ogrodzinski, Head of the Polish Delegation; and Gaguier, Acting Head of the Canadian Delegation.

    Chairman Ho Chi Minh delivered a welcoming speech at the banquet. Prime Minister Nehru gave a reply.

    After the banquet, Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Prime Minister Nehru held talks.

### 胡志明主席设宴招待尼赫鲁总理

1954-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席设宴招待尼赫鲁总理
    【新华社河内十七日电】越南民主共和国胡志明主席十七日晚七时（越南时间）举行宴会招待印度总理尼赫鲁。
    出席宴会的还有：尼赫鲁总理的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人和印度外交部秘书长皮莱。
    出席作陪的有：越南民主共和国政府副总理兼外交部部长范文同，国防部部长兼人民军总司令武元甲，宣传部部长黄明鉴，工商部部长潘英，教育部部长阮文煊，河内军政委员会主席王承武，外交部交际处处长武廷莹和河内妇女联合会主席阮科耀红。
    中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使罗贵波也应邀参加了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的还有，越南国际监督和监察委员会印度代表团团长德赛、波兰代表团团长奥格罗京斯基、加拿大代表团代团长加基埃。
    胡志明主席在宴会上致欢迎词。尼赫鲁总理致答词。
    宴会结束后，胡志明主席和尼赫鲁总理进行了交谈。
