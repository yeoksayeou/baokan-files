Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi and Others Leave Warsaw to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 26th: Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, and Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Zhu Kezhen, left Warsaw by plane on the 25th to return home after their visit to the People's Republic of Poland.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, Jendryhowski; Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs, Naszkowski; Minister of Culture and Art, Sokolowski; Deputy Vice President of the Polish Academy of Sciences, Wierzbicki; and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Bartol, and others.

Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to Poland, Hermann, and Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Zeng Yongquan, and embassy staff also went to the airport to see them off.

During their visit to Poland, Chen Yi and others not only visited Warsaw but also visited Gdańsk, a famous port on the Baltic Sea coast, and Kraków, a cultural ancient city of Poland.

### 陈毅副总理等离华沙回国

1954-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理等离华沙回国
    【新华社华沙二十六日电】中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅、中国科学院副院长竺可桢在波兰人民共和国访问后，于二十五日乘飞机离华沙返国。
    到机场欢送的有：波兰部长会议副主席英德里霍夫斯基，外交部代理部长纳斯科夫斯基，文化艺术部部长索科尔斯基，波兰科学院副院长维日比茨基和外交部交际司司长巴托尔等。
    德意志民主共和国驻波兰大使赫曼，中国驻波兰大使曾涌泉和大使馆人员也到机场送行。
    陈毅等在波兰访问期间，除在华沙市进行参观外，还访问了波罗的海海滨的著名港口革但斯克和波兰的文化古城克拉科夫。
