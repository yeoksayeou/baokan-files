Title: Leading Organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Holds Celebration for the Fifth Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation
Author:
Date: 1954-10-03
Page: 2

    Leading Organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army
    Holds Celebration for the Fifth Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation
    On the afternoon of the 1st, the leading organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army held a celebration for the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    Attending the meeting were Yang Yong, Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, Wang Yunrui, Deputy Chief of Staff, heads of various arms of the services and units, senior cadres from various departments of the leading organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and representatives from directly subordinate organs and units, totaling 5,000 people.
    Also attending the meeting were all members of the delegation headed by Kim Tu-bong, Head of the People's Representative Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, as well as the literary and artistic personnel accompanying the delegation.
    Also attending the meeting were 500 representatives of the Korean people and overseas Chinese from the area where the leading organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army was stationed.
    After the meeting began, General Yang Yong delivered a speech. He said that we are celebrating the National Day of our motherland in 1954 with great joy, and at the same time, we warmly celebrate the victorious conclusion of the first session of the First National People's Congress of our motherland, the promulgation of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, and the election of new national leading staff. General Yang Yong said that while celebrating the great achievements of our country in the past five years, all personnel of the Volunteer Army must continue to heighten their vigilance, strengthen military training, accelerate the construction of a modern army, continue to strengthen solidarity with the Korean people, and work together with the Korean people to ensure the victorious fruits of the Korean armistice and further strive for a peaceful settlement of the Korean issue.
    Kim Tu-bong, head of the People's Representative Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting. On behalf of the delegation, he extended warm congratulations to the great Chinese people, the glorious Chinese Communist Party and its wise leader Chairman Mao Zedong, and congratulations to all commanders and fighters of the heroic Chinese People's Volunteer Army. He said that the People's Republic of China has grown into a powerful bulwark for defending peace and security in Asia and the world, its international prestige has been greatly enhanced, and its establishment and growing strength show that the era in which American imperialism imposed its will on the Asian people is over.
    In his congratulatory speech, Kim Tu-bong described the deeds of the great internationalist assistance given by the Chinese people to the Korean people in the anti-aggression war in Korea and in the post-war reconstruction. He said that the Korean people are proud to have forged a fighting friendship with the great neighbor, the Chinese people, and the Korean people are determined to further strengthen this precious internationalist friendship and are determined to strive to complete and overfulfill the three-year plan for the restoration and development of the post-war national economy. Finally, Kim Tu-bong heartily congratulated the Chinese people on their even greater achievements in the socialist construction struggle for the infinite prosperity and development of the People's Republic of China, and the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in their peace-keeping and political and military training.
    Then, amidst thunderous applause and majestic military music, Kim Tu-bong, on behalf of the Korean Fatherland Unification Democratic Front, presented a banner to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. Han Il-mu, representative of the Supreme Command of the Korean People's Army, also presented a banner to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army at the meeting, and General Yang Yong accepted the banner on behalf of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.
    The meeting ended in warm shouts of slogans. After the meeting, an evening party was held, and the art troupe of the Political Department of the Volunteer Army and the Korean literary and artistic personnel who came with the People's Representative Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea performed wonderful song and dance programs at the evening party. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国人民志愿军领导机关  举行庆祝建国五周年大会

1954-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军领导机关
    举行庆祝建国五周年大会
    一日下午，中国人民志愿军领导机关举行庆祝中华人民共和国成立五周年大会。
    出席大会的有中国人民志愿军副司令员杨勇、副参谋长王蕴瑞和各兵种、各部队首长、中国人民志愿军领导机关各部门高级干部和直属机关部队的代表共五千人。
    出席大会的还有以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民代表团团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉为首的代表团全体团员和随同代表团前来的文艺工作人员。
    出席大会的还有中国人民志愿军领导机关驻地的朝鲜人民和华侨代表五百人。
    大会开始后，杨勇将军致词。他说，我们以十分欢欣的心情在庆祝一九五四年祖国国庆节同时，热烈庆祝祖国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的胜利闭幕，庆祝中华人民共和国宪法的颁布，庆祝新的国家领导工作人员的选出。杨勇将军说，在庆祝我国五年来伟大成就的同时，志愿军的全体人员必须继续提高警惕，加强军事训练，加速现代化军队的建设，继续加强和朝鲜人民的团结，同朝鲜人民共同努力，确保朝鲜停战的胜利果实，并进一步争取朝鲜问题的和平解决。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民代表团团长金枓奉在会上致祝词。他以代表团的名义向伟大的中国人民、光荣的中国共产党及其英明的领袖毛泽东主席致以热烈的祝贺，向英雄的中国人民志愿军全体指战员祝贺。他说，中华人民共和国已经成长为保卫亚洲和世界和平与安全的强有力的堡垒，它的国际威望大大提高，它的建立和日益强大显示了美帝国主义把自己的意志强加在亚洲人民身上为所欲为的时代已成过去。
    金枓奉在致祝词中叙述了中国人民在朝鲜人民反侵略战争中和战后的恢复建设工作中，给予朝鲜人民的伟大的国际主义援助的事迹。他说，朝鲜人民以和伟大的邻邦中国人民结成战斗友谊感到骄傲，朝鲜人民决心进一步加强这一珍贵的国际主义友谊，并决心为完成和超额完成恢复发展战后国民经济三年计划而奋斗。最后，金枓奉衷心地祝贺中国人民在为中华人民共和国的无限繁荣发展的社会主义建设斗争中，和中国人民志愿军指战员们在保卫和平和政治、军事训练中，获得更加巨大的成就。
    接着，在雷动的掌声和雄壮的军乐声中，金枓奉代表朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线向中国人民志愿军献旗。朝鲜人民军最高司令部代表韩一武也在会上向中国人民志愿军献旗，杨勇将军代表中国人民志愿军受旗。
    大会在热烈的口号声中结束。会后举行晚会，志愿军政治部文艺工作团和随同朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民代表团前来的朝鲜文艺工作人员，在晚会上演出了精彩的歌舞节目。　（新华社）
