Title: Dr. Groza Returns to Beijing After Visiting Various Parts of China
Author:
Date: 1954-10-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 28th:

    Peter Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, accompanied by Li Enqiu, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 28th after visiting Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Kunming, Chongqing and other places. During his visits to various places, Chairman Groza held discussions with local business and industrial figures. Romanian writers Yu. Camila and Yi. Vitnei also traveled to various places on the same plane and returned to Beijing on the same day.

### 格罗查博士在我国各地访问后回到北京

1954-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    格罗查博士在我国各地访问后回到北京
    【新华社二十八日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查博士由我国驻罗马尼亚大使馆参赞李恩求陪同，在南京、上海、杭州、广州、昆明、重庆等地参观游览后，已于二十八日下午乘飞机返北京。格罗查主席在各地访问时，曾和当地工商界人士进行了座谈。罗马尼亚作家尤·卡米拉和依·维特奈曾同机前往各地访问，并于同日返京。
