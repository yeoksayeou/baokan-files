Title: Administrative Changes Above County Level in August and September Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1954-10-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

Administrative changes above county level across the country in August and September are as follows:

    In Shandong Province, Linqing Town, originally under the jurisdiction of the Liaocheng Special District, was re-established as Linqing City (a city under the jurisdiction of the province), still under the leadership and supervision of the Liaocheng Special Administrative Office.

    In Henan Province, the establishment of Qi County was abolished, and it was merged into Tangyin County; the establishment of Gushu County was abolished, and the areas under its jurisdiction were respectively劃歸 (divided and归属, assigned to) Shangqiu and Yucheng counties.

    In Hunan Province, Tongdao County was renamed Tongdao Dong Autonomous County, and Gaobu and Hengling townships of Sanjiang County in Guangxi Province were placed under the leadership of Tongdao Dong Autonomous County.

    In Yunnan Province, Jinghong, the seat of the People's Government of the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Region, was renamed Yun Jinghong. The establishments of the four counties under its jurisdiction, Cheli, FoHai, Nanqiao, and ZhenYue, were abolished, and the original jurisdictions of each county were divided into twelve "Banna" (equivalent to district level) and three autonomous regions equivalent to "Banna".

### 八、九月份全国县以上行政区划变动情况

1954-10-14
第3版()
专栏：

    八、九月份全国县以上行政区划变动情况
    八、九两月份全国县以上单位的行政区划变动情况如下：
    山东省原属聊城专区的临清镇改设为临清市（省辖市），仍由聊城专署领导监督。
    河南省淇县建制撤销，并入汤阴县；谷熟县建制撤销，所辖地区分别划归商邱、虞城两县。
    湖南省通道县改设为通道侗族自治县，并将广西省三江县的高步、横岭两乡划归通道侗族自治县领导。
    云南省西双版纳傣族自治区人民政府驻地车里县景洪，改名为允景洪，所辖的车里、佛海、南峤、镇越四县建制撤销，各县的原辖区划设为十二个版纳（相当于区级）及三个相当于版纳的自治区。
                                  （新华社）
