Title: Vietnam Council of Ministers Holds Meeting in Hanoi, Hears Report on Taking Over the Capital, Decides to Pardon Criminals
Author:
Date: 1954-10-16
Page: 4

　　[Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 15th] Vietnam News Agency North Vietnam, 15th dispatch: The Council of Ministers of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a communique on the 14th stating that the Council of Ministers held a meeting on October 12, 1954, in Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, under the chairmanship of President Ho Chi Minh.

　　The communique said that after listening to the report made by the Chairman of the Hanoi Military and Administrative Committee on the takeover of the capital, the Council of Ministers considered that: the work of taking over Hanoi was carried out smoothly and in the enthusiastic atmosphere of all residents, who warmly welcomed the Vietnamese People's Army taking over the capital. The order and security of the capital have been guaranteed, and the lives and activities of the capital's residents have returned to normal in all aspects. The Council of Ministers noted that the good results of the takeover of Hanoi proved the patriotic spirit of the capital's residents, the discipline of the Vietnamese People's Army, and the sense of responsibility of government cadres.

　　The Council of Ministers decided to extend congratulations to the People's Army and cadres who contributed to the victorious takeover of the capital.

　　On the occasion of the liberation of Hanoi, the Council of Ministers decided to pardon criminals. In addition, the Council of Ministers also decided to posthumously award Father Pham Ba Truc, the late Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, the First Class Independence Medal.

### 越南部长会议在河内举行会议  听取关于接管首都的报告，决定大赦罪犯

1954-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    越南部长会议在河内举行会议
    听取关于接管首都的报告，决定大赦罪犯
      【新华社十五日讯】越南通讯社北越十五日讯：越南民主共和国部长会议十四日发表公报说，部长会议于一九五四年十月十二日在胡志明主席主持下在越南民主共和国首都河内举行了会议。
    公报说，部长会议在听取了河内军政委员会主席所作的关于接管首都的报告以后认为：接管河内的工作是在顺利的情况下和全体居民的热烈气氛中进行的，居民们热情地欢迎接管首都的越南人民军。首都的秩序和安全已经得到保证，首都居民的生活和各方面的活动都已恢复正常。部长会议注意到，接管河内工作的良好结果证明了首都居民的爱国精神，越南人民军的纪律性和政府干部的责任感。
    部长会议决定向胜利接管首都有贡献的人民军和干部致以祝贺。
    值此河内解放之际，部长会议决定大赦罪犯。另外，部长会议还决定追赠已故的国民大会常务委员会副主席范伯直神甫以一级独立勋章。
