Title: China and Ceylon Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-10-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th October:

    The Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China and the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation, after friendly and harmonious negotiations, signed in Beijing on October 8th a contract for the Government of the People's Republic of China to purchase 50,000 metric tons of rubber from the Government of Ceylon, and a contract for the Government of Ceylon to purchase 270,000 metric tons of rice from the Government of the People's Republic of China.
    Representing the Chinese side in signing these two contracts were Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, and representing the Ceylon side were S.F. Amerasinghe and K. Alvappillai, members of the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation.
    Those attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side included Ye Jiuzhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, and Li Yingji, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation.
    Those attending the signing ceremony on the Ceylon side included S.C. Shirley Corea, head of the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation, and all members of the delegation. On the same evening, Ye Jiuzhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, held a banquet to bid farewell to all members of the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation.

### 中锡两国政府签订贸易合同

1954-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中锡两国政府签订贸易合同
    【新华社八日讯】中华人民共和国对外贸易部和锡兰政府贸易代表团，经过友好和谐的谈判，于十月八日在北京分别签订了中华人民共和国政府向锡兰政府购买橡胶五万公吨的合同和锡兰政府向中华人民共和国政府购买大米二十七万公吨的合同。
    代表中国方面签订这两个合同的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部第三局局长卢绪章，代表锡兰方面签订这两个合同的是锡兰政府贸易代表团团员斯·弗·阿马拉辛和克·阿尔瓦比拉伊。
    参加签字典礼的：中国方面有对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，中国进出口公司副经理李应吉等人。
    锡兰方面参加签字典礼的有锡兰政府贸易代表团团长斯·克·秀莱·科里亚和全体团员。同日晚，我对外贸易部部长叶季壮举行宴会欢送锡兰政府贸易代表团全体团员。
