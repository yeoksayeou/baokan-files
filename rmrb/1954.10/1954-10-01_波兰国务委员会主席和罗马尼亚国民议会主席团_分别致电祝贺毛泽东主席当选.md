Title: Chairman of the State Council of Poland and Presidium of the National Assembly of Romania Send Congratulatory Telegrams on Chairman Mao Zedong's Election
Author:
Date: 1954-10-01
Page: 4

The Chairman of the State Council of Poland and the Presidium of the National Assembly of Romania
Send separate telegrams to congratulate Chairman Mao Zedong on his election as Chairman of the People's Republic of China

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

    On learning of your election and assumption of the position of Chairman of the People's Republic of China, I, on behalf of the Polish people and the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, and in my personal name, extend to you, Comrade Chairman, my most sincere congratulations.
    Comrade Chairman, I wish you a long life and, at the same time, wish you even more brilliant achievements in your activities for the happiness of the Chinese people and for peace in Asia and the world.
    Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic
                       September 29, 1954, in Warsaw

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

    On learning of your election as Chairman of the People's Republic of China, the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic extends to you its warmest congratulations and wishes you and the great Chinese people new achievements in the further development and consolidation of the cause of the People's Republic of China.
    The Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic
    September 29, 1954, in Bucharest

### 波兰国务委员会主席和罗马尼亚国民议会主席团  分别致电祝贺毛泽东主席当选

1954-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰国务委员会主席和罗马尼亚国民议会主席团
    分别致电祝贺毛泽东主席当选中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志：
    欣悉您当选就任中华人民共和国主席的职位，我谨代表波兰人民和波兰人民共和国务委员会，并以我个人的名义向您，主席同志，致最衷心的祝贺。
    主席同志，谨祝您万寿无疆，同时并祝您为中国人民的幸福、为亚洲和世界和平所进行的活动取得更辉煌的成就。波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席亚历山大·萨瓦茨基
                      一九五四年九月二十九日于华沙中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志：
    欣悉您当选为中华人民共和国主席，罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团向您致最热烈的祝贺，并祝您及伟大的中国人民在进一步发展和巩固中华人民共和国的事业中获得新的成就。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团
  一九五四年九月二十九日于布加勒斯特
