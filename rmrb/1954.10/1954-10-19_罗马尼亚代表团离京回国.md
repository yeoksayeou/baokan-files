Title: Romanian Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 17th Dispatch: The seven-member Romanian delegation attending the Second Meeting of the Sino-Romanian Joint Commission on Scientific and Technical Cooperation, led by Head of Delegation Emil Stanciu, left Beijing by plane for home on the morning of the 17th.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Li Renjun, chairman of the Chinese group of the Sino-Romanian Joint Commission on Scientific and Technical Cooperation and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry; Zhang Qinqiu, member of the Chinese group of the commission and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Textile Industry; Zhang Zike, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Romanian People's Republic; Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Also at the airport to see them off were Mentianu, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Romanian People's Republic Embassy in China, and An Ding, Economic Counselor.

### 罗马尼亚代表团离京回国

1954-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚代表团离京回国
    【新华社十七日讯】出席中罗科学和技术合作联合委员会第二届会议的罗马尼亚代表团一行七人，在团长埃米尔·斯坦裘的率领下，已在十七日晨乘飞机离京回国。
    前往机场欢送的有：中罗科学和技术合作联合委员会中国组主席、燃料工业部副部长李人俊，委员会中国组委员、纺织工业部副部长张琴秋，我驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使馆商务参赞张子克、外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇等多人。
    前往机场欢送的还有罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办孟天努，经济参赞安定。
