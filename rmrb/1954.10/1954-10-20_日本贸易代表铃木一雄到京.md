Title: Japanese Trade Representative Kazuo Suzuki Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th:

    Japanese trade representative Kazuo Suzuki, Standing Director of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, arrived in Beijing on the 19th at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade to discuss matters related to Sino-Japanese trade.

### 日本贸易代表铃木一雄到京

1954-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    日本贸易代表铃木一雄到京
    【新华社十九日讯】日本日中贸易促进会常务理事铃木一雄，应中国国际贸易促进委员会的邀请，十九日到达北京，商谈有关中日贸易事宜。
