Title: Our Army in Fujian Shoots Down One Chiang Bandit Plane
Author:
Date: 1954-10-25
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 23rd—At 15:40 on the 21st, two Chiang bandit air force F-47 type planes intruded into the airspace over Yongning, southeast of Jinjiang, from the direction of Kinmen to harass. Our army's anti-aircraft artillery units immediately opened fire, hitting the wing of one Chiang bandit plane.

### 福建我军击伤蒋贼飞机一架

1954-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    福建我军击伤蒋贼飞机一架
    【新华社福建前线二十三日电】二十一日十五时四十分，蒋贼空军F—四七型飞机两架，从金门方向窜入晋江东南之永宁上空骚扰。我军高射炮部队立即开火，击中一架蒋贼飞机的机翼。
