Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation Concludes Visit to China and Departs for Home

Author:

Date: 1954-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: The Soviet Cultural Delegation, headed by Denisov, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, concluded its month-long visit to China and departed from Beijing by plane this morning, October 29th, to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Qian Junrui, Secretary-General; various Deputy Secretaries-General and Beijing-based council members; Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education of the People's Republic of China; Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; and prominent figures from Chinese science, education, culture, and the arts.

Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, and the Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition, Borisinko, also went to the airport to see them off.

The delegation visited major cities such as Beijing, Shenyang, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Wuhan. They delivered various specialized reports to cultural, educational, and artistic circles, cadres, professors, and students in these areas. During their short one-month visit, the delegation delivered a total of forty-nine academic reports, with an audience of over 50,000 people. The delegation was warmly welcomed by the masses wherever they went. In addition, the delegation participated in symposiums invited by organizations and groups in science, culture, education, the arts, and trade. At these symposiums, the delegation introduced the achievements and advanced experiences of the Soviet Union in various fields.

During their visit to China, the delegation collected various materials and plans to write articles introducing the construction and lives of the Chinese people upon their return home. The famous Soviet painter Gerasimov has completed thirty works, including "Viewing the Ceremony at Tiananmen Square." After returning home, they will introduce everything they saw in China to the Soviet people.

### 苏联文化代表团在我国访问结束启程回国

1954-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联文化代表团在我国访问结束启程回国
    【新华社二十九日讯】以苏联对外文化协会理事会主席杰尼索夫为首的苏联文化代表团结束了在中国整整一个月的访问活动，在二十九日早晨乘飞机离京回国。
    前往机场欢送的有中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、总干事钱俊瑞、各副总干事和在京理事，中华人民共和国高等教育部部长杨秀峰，教育部部长张奚若，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，以及中国科学、教育、文化及文艺界著名人士。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金和苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科等也到机场欢送。
    代表团曾先后访问了北京、沈阳、南京、杭州、上海、广州和武汉等大城市，并向各地的文化界、教育界、文艺界人士、机关干部、教授和学生作了各种专门问题的报告。在短短的一个月访问期间，代表团共作了四十九次学术性报告，听众达五万多人。代表团在所到的地方都受到群众热烈的欢迎。此外，代表团还参加了科学、文化、教育、文艺、贸易等方面机关团体邀请的座谈会。在这些座谈会上，代表团介绍了苏联各方面的成就和先进的经验。
    代表团在我国访问期间收集了各种材料，准备回国撰写介绍有关我国人民建设及生活的文章。著名的苏联画家格拉西莫夫已完成了“天安门观礼”等三十幅作品。他们在回国后，将要把在我国所看到的一切介绍给苏联人民。
