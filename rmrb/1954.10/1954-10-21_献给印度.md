Title: Dedicated to India
Author: Yuan Shuipai
Date: 1954-10-21
Page: 3

    Dedicated to India
    Yuan Shuipai
    It is such a place of beautiful scenery,
    Everywhere is lush green, roses as big as bowls,
    In the moonlight, the peacock's emerald feathers glisten in the forest,
    Spring resides here year-round.
    It is a land full of heroic legends,
    Countless national martyrs have irrigated it with their blood.
    How brave and determined are the people here,
    Just like my own brothers and sisters!
    It is two thousand years of peaceful coexistence and friendship,
    High mountains cannot block it, time cannot erase it.
    Nine hundred and sixty million people will forever stand shoulder to shoulder,
    For world peace, for a peaceful Asia!

### 献给印度
袁水拍
1954-10-21
第3版()
专栏：

    献给印度
    袁水拍
    是这样一个风光明媚的地方，
    到处绿荫如盖，玫瑰花像碗大，
    月光下孔雀的翠羽在林间闪光，
    春天她常年在这儿住家。
    是一片充满英雄传说的土地，
    无数民族烈士用鲜血来灌溉。
    这儿的人民是多么勇敢坚毅，
    就像我自己的兄弟姐妹！
    是两千年和平共处的友谊，
    高山不能阻隔，时光不能磨灭。
    九亿六千万人永远并肩携手，
    为了世界的和平，为了和平的亚洲！
