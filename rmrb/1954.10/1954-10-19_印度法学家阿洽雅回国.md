Title: Indian Jurist Acharya Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 14th. Indian jurist Acharya arrived in Guangzhou from Shanghai on the 11th and left Guangzhou on the 13th via Hong Kong to return home.

### 印度法学家阿洽雅回国

1954-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    印度法学家阿洽雅回国
  【新华社广州十四日电】印度法学家阿洽雅在十一日由上海到达广州，十三日离广州取道香港回国。
