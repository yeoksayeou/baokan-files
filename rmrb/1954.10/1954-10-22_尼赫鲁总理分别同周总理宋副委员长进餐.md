Title: Prime Minister Nehru Dines with Premier Zhou and Vice Chairman Song Separately
Author:
Date: 1954-10-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 21st—Prime Minister Nehru of the Republic of India and his daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, were invited to dinner with Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China on the evening of the 19th. On the 21st at noon, they were invited to lunch with Vice Chairman Song Qingling of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. On the evening of the 21st, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, they attended a song, dance, and Peking opera performance presented by the Central Song and Dance Troupe, the Central Experimental Opera House, the China Peking Opera Troupe, and the famous actor Mei Lanfang, among others.

### 尼赫鲁总理分别同周总理宋副委员长进餐

1954-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理分别同周总理宋副委员长进餐
     【新华社二十一日讯】印度共和国总理尼赫鲁和他的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人，在十九日晚应邀同中华人民共和国总理周恩来共进晚餐，在二十一日中午应邀同全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄共进午餐，二十一日晚由周恩来总理陪同出席了由中央歌舞团、中央实验歌剧院、中国京剧团和著名演员梅兰芳等演出的歌舞京剧晚会。
