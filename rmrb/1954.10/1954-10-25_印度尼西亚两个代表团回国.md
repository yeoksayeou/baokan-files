Title: Two Indonesian Delegations Return Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 24th: The Indonesian Delegation to China and the Indonesian Women's Delegation arrived in Guangzhou on the 22nd and departed from Guangzhou via Hong Kong on the 23rd to return home.

The delegates toured the Pearl River in Guangzhou, and the delegates of the Women's Delegation also visited an embroidery group.

### 印度尼西亚两个代表团回国

1954-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚两个代表团回国
    【新华社广州二十四日电】印度尼西亚访华代表团和印度尼西亚妇女代表团在二十二日到达广州，已在二十三日离广州取道香港回国。
    代表们在广州曾游览了珠江，妇女代表团的代表还参观了一个刺绣小组。
