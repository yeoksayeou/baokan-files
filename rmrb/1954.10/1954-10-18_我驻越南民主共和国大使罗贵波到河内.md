Title: My Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Luo Guibo, Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1954-10-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 16th - Luo Guibo, the Plenipotentiary Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Hanoi from Thai Nguyen on the afternoon of the 16th with some members of the embassy staff.

On the evening of the 16th, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Hanoi Municipal Military and Administrative Committee held a reception to welcome Ambassador Luo Guibo. The reception was presided over by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Pham Van Dong. More than 40 people attended the reception, including ministers of various government departments and other senior officials, as well as the chairman and vice-chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Military and Administrative Committee.

### 我驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波到河内

1954-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波到河内
    【新华社河内十六日电】中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使罗贵波，十六日下午率领部分大使馆人员从太原到达河内。
    十六日晚，越南民主共和国政府外交部和河内市军政委员会举行招待会，欢迎罗贵波大使。招待会由副总理兼外交部部长范文同主持，出席招待会的有政府各部部长和其他高级官员、河内市军政委员会正副主席等四十多人。
