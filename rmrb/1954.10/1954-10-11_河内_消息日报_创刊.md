Title: Hanoi "News Daily" Begins Publication
Author:
Date: 1954-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, October 10th: The "News Daily," the organ of the Hanoi Military and Administrative Committee of the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, was launched in Hanoi on the 10th. The newspaper is a four-folio, two-page publication. The inaugural issue prominently features a portrait of Ho Chi Minh, President of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, "Letter to the Compatriots of Hanoi" by President Ho Chi Minh, and the order issued by Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army, to the troops entering Hanoi. The inaugural issue also published the eight policies of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for the newly liberated cities and an article introducing the struggle of the people of Hanoi to defend the capital.

### 河内“消息日报”创刊

1954-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    河内“消息日报”创刊
    【新华社北越十日电】越南民主共和国首都河内市军政委员会机关报“消息日报”十日在河内创刊，报纸为四开两版。创刊号以首要地位刊登越南民主共和国政府胡志明主席肖像、胡志明主席“告河内同胞书”、越南人民军武元甲总司令向进入河内的部队发布的命令。创刊号还登载了越南民主共和国政府对新解放城市的八项政策以及介绍河内人民保卫首都的斗争的文章。
