Title: Soviet Economic and Cultural Achievements Exhibition Sales Outlet Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1954-10-10
Page: 2

    Soviet Economic and Cultural Achievements Exhibition
    Sales Outlet Opens Today
    The sales outlet for the Soviet Economic and Cultural Achievements Exhibition will open today (the 10th).
    The sales outlet is divided into two locations: one in the city at Liubukou on West Chang'an Avenue; the other in the suburbs within the West Suburb Market, south of the southwest road of the exhibition hall. The supply hours are: the city sales outlet from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM; the suburban sales outlet from 9:30 AM to 7:00 PM. Currently, the goods for sale include the following: condensed milk, fruit candies, granulated sugar, biscuits, canned goods, cigarettes, wines, as well as drafting instruments, fountain pens, mechanical pencils, slide rules, and canvas cots for travel, etc.

### 苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会  售品处今日开始营业

1954-10-10
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会
    售品处今日开始营业
    苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会售品处，自今日（十日）起开始营业。
    售品处分设两处：城内在西长安街六部口；郊区在展览馆西南路南西郊商场内。供应时间是：城内售品处自上午十时起至下午八时止；郊区售品处自上午九时半起至下午七时止。目前出售的货品有下列几种：炼乳、水果糖、砂糖、饼干、罐头、香烟、酒类，以及绘图仪器、钢笔、自动铅笔、计算尺、旅行用的帆布床等。
