Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Grand Banquet to Host Government Delegations from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-10-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China held a grand banquet at the Beijing Hotel on the evening of the 2nd to host the government delegations from various countries who were invited to attend the celebration of the 5th anniversary of the founding of our country.

    The following were invited to attend the banquet: Head of the Soviet Government Delegation, N.S. Khrushchev; members N.A. Bulganin, A.I. Mikoyan, N.M. Shvernik; Head of the Government Delegation of the Polish People's Republic, B. Bierut; Head of the Government Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Marshal Kim Il-sung; Head of the Government Delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, G. Apostol; member Dr. P. Groza; Head of the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, Zh. Sambu; Head of the Government Delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, V. Kopecky; Head of the Government Delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, A. Hegedüs; Head of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, Dr. L. Bolz; Head of the Government Delegation of the Bulgarian People's Republic, R. Damianov; Head of the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hoang Minh Giam; Head of the Government Delegation of the Albanian People's Republic, B. Shtulla; and members of the government delegations from various countries.

    Foreign guests invited to attend the banquet also included experts from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries who are helping with our country's construction.

    Foreign guests invited to attend the banquet included ambassadors and diplomatic personnel from various countries to China: Burmese Ambassador U Hla Maung, Pakistani Ambassador Raza, Czechoslovak Ambassador Kousal, Polish Ambassador Kirylok, Indian Ambassador Raghawan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Hoang Van Hoan, Swedish Ambassador Wistrand, Romanian Ambassador C.     The Chinese side attending the banquet included Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Vice Chairmen Soong Ching Ling, Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, the Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, and Saifudin.     Also attending the banquet were Vice Mayors of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Zhang Youyu and Wu Han.

    Also attending the banquet were Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Liao Chengzhi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Yuzhang, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Xie Xuehong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Lai Ruoyu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Zhang Yun, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation Liu Daosheng, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation Tian Demin, Vice Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Zhou Yang, as well as Standing Committee members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and heads of other people's organizations, figures from the capital's scientific, educational, religious, literary, artistic, and journalistic circles, combat heroes, and labor models.

    More than 800 people attended the banquet.

    At the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai first delivered a speech, expressing, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, a heartfelt welcome and sincere thanks to the distinguished guests who had come to our country (full text to be published separately).

    During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly rose to toast the health of the distinguished guests of the government delegations of various countries and the leaders of the parties of various countries, the health of the diplomatic envoys of various countries, and the unity and cooperation of the peaceful and democratic forces led by the Soviet Union, and for peace in the Far East and the world.

    The banquet was filled with an atmosphere of close friendship.

### 周恩来总理举行盛大宴会  招待各国政府代表团

1954-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理举行盛大宴会
    招待各国政府代表团
    【新华社二日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，二日晚在北京饭店举行盛大宴会，招待应邀前来参加我国建国五周年国庆典礼的各国政府代表团。
    应邀出席宴会的，有：苏联政府代表团团长尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫，团员尼·亚·布尔加宁、阿·伊·米高扬、尼·米·什维尔尼克，波兰人民共和国政府代表团团长波·贝鲁特，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团团长金日成元帅，罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长格·阿波斯托尔、团员彼·格罗查博士，蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长扎·桑布，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团长瓦·柯别茨基，匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团团长安·赫格居斯，德意志民主共和国政府代表团团长洛·博尔茨博士，保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团团长拉·达米扬诺夫，越南民主共和国政府代表团团长黄明鉴，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长贝·什图拉，和各国政府代表团团员。
