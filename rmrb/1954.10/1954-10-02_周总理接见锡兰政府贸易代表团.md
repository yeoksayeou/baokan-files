Title: Premier Zhou Receives the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-10-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 1st—Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai received the head of the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation, Koliya, and delegation members Amarasinghe, Alwar Pillai, Jinadasa, James, Nanayakkara, and Kasim at 6:30 pm on October 1st.

Present at the reception were Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister Lei Renmin, and Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang.

### 周总理接见锡兰政府贸易代表团

1954-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见锡兰政府贸易代表团
    【新华社一日讯】我国务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在十月一日下午六时半接见了锡兰政府贸易代表团团长科里亚，团员阿马拉辛、阿尔瓦比拉伊、吉纳达萨、詹兹、纳那亚卡拉、卡西姆。
    接见时在座的，有我国对外贸易部部长叶季壮，副部长雷任民，外交部亚洲司司长陈家康。
