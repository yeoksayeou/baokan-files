Title: Soviet Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 13th:
The Soviet government delegation, headed by Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, which was invited by the Chinese government to attend the celebrations of the 5th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and included: Nikolai Bulganin, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; Anastas Mikoyan, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; Georgy Aleksandrov, Minister of Culture of the Soviet Union; Dmitry Shepilov, Editor-in-Chief of "Pravda"; Yekaterina Furtseva, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union; Yevdokiya Nasriddinova, Minister of the Building Materials Industry of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic; and Viktor Stepanov, a responsible staff member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, left Beijing by special plane at 11:30 a.m. on the 13th to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included ZhuDe, Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPC and Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Liu Shaoqi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPC and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPC, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Chen Yun, Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPC and Vice Premier of the State Council; Peng Dehuai, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the CPC, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense; Soong Ching-ling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Dong Biwu, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the CPC and President of the Supreme People's Court; Lin Boqu, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the CPC and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Deng Xiaoping, Member of the Central Committee of the CPC, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the CPC and Vice Premier of the State Council; Peng Zhen, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the CPC and Mayor of Beijing; and Nie Rongzhen, Member of the Central Committee of the CPC and Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison.

Nikolai Shvernik, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions and member of the Soviet government delegation, also went to the airport to see them off.

Also present at the airport to see them off were the heads and members of the government delegations of the Romanian People's Republic, the Czechoslovak Republic, and the Albanian People's Republic, who were invited to attend the celebrations of the 5th anniversary of the founding of our country.

Also present at the airport to see them off were Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, the Dalai Lama Tenzin Gyatso, Saifudin, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and members Wang Kunlun, Wang Weizhou, Gu Dacun, Wu Yaozong, Li Shucheng, Xing Xiping, Lin Feng, Zhou Jianren, Shao Lizi, the Panchen Erdeni Chokyi Gyaltsen, Xu Deheng, Chen Shaoxian, Lu Dingyi, Cheng Zihua, Huang ShaoHong, Liu Changsheng, Liu Geping, Cai Tingkai and Lai Ruoyu of the National People's Congress.

Also present at the airport to see them off were Vice Premiers of the State Council Deng Zihui, He Long, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Ministers and Directors of various ministries and commissions Xie Juezai, Luo Ruiqing, Shi Liang, Qian Ying, Bo Yibo, Zhang Naiqi, Zeng Shan, Ye Jizhuang, Wang Heshou, Huang Jing, Zhao Erlu, Chen Yu, Liu Xiufeng, Jiang Guangding, Jia Tuofu, Sha Qianli, Zhang Bojun, Zhu Xuefan, Liao Luyan, Liang Xi, Shen Yanbing, Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Xiruo, Li Dequan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Li Kenong, Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, as well as Vice Ministers of various ministries, and Deputy Directors of various commissions, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Zhang Youyu.

Also present at the airport to see them off were Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Liu Bocheng, Xu Xiangqian, Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, and Fu Zuoyi, members Wang Hongkun, Wang Zhen, Xu Guangda, Chen Geng, Chen Xilian, Su Yu, Liu Yalou, Xiao Jingguang, and Su Zhenhua, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Xiao Hua and Gan Siqi, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Xu Liqing and Lai Chuanzhu, etc.

The heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials of various countries stationed in China who went to the airport to see them off included U Law Maw, Ambassador of Burma; Jaroslav Kohout, Ambassador of Czechoslovakia; Kirilo Sokolevski, Ambassador of Poland; Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Teodor Rudenco, Ambassador of Romania; Ochirbat, Ambassador of Mongolia; Mononutu, Ambassador of Indonesia; Johannes König, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic; Choe Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Dimov, Ambassador of Bulgaria; Nase, Ambassador of Albania; Siklós, Ambassador of Hungary; Sundström, Minister of Finland; Graeger, Minister of Denmark; Béguin, Minister of Switzerland; D.W. Gajendragadkar, Counselor of the Indian Embassy; and Younas, Third Secretary of the Pakistan Embassy, and Corder, Attaché of the Netherlands Negotiation Office.

The national flags of the Soviet Union and China, as well as the flags of various people's democratic countries, were hung at the airport.

When the Soviet government delegation headed by Nikita Khrushchev arrived at the airport, the band played the national anthems of the Soviet Union and China.

Nikita Khrushchev and others inspected the honor guard lined up at the airport.

Before boarding the plane, Nikita Khrushchev delivered a speech at the airport (full text to be published separately).
