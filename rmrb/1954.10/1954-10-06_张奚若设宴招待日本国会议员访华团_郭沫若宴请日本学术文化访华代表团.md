Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Japanese Diet Members' Delegation to China; Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Japanese Academic and Cultural Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1954-10-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet on the 3rd to entertain the Japanese Diet Members' Delegation to China, who were invited to visit our country.

    Matsumoto Jiichiro, advisor to the Japanese Socialist Party (Left Wing), who arrived in Beijing on the 3rd, also attended the banquet. Tanaka Toshio, a member of the House of Representatives of the Japanese Socialist Party (Left Wing), who was already in Beijing, also attended the banquet.
     Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Guo Moruo hosted a banquet on the evening of the 5th to entertain the Japanese Academic and Cultural Delegation to China, headed by Abe Yoshishige, President of Gakushuin University of Japan. Well-known figures from Chinese cultural circles attended as guests.

      That morning, the delegation paid a visit to Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, as well as the heads of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles. During the meeting, the delegation presented gifts brought from Japan to the above three organizations.

### 张奚若设宴招待日本国会议员访华团  郭沫若宴请日本学术文化访华代表团

1954-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    张奚若设宴招待日本国会议员访华团
    郭沫若宴请日本学术文化访华代表团
   【新华社三日讯】中国人民外交学会会长张奚若在三日举行宴会，招待应邀来我国访问的日本国会议员访华团。
    出席宴会的还有在三日到达北京的日本社会党（左派）顾问松本治一郎。已在北京的日本社会党（左派）众议员田中稔男也出席了宴会。
     【新华社五日讯】郭沫若在五日晚宴请了以日本学习院大学校长安倍能成为首的日本学术文化访华代表团。出席作陪的有中国文化界的知名人士。
      这天上午，代表团拜会了中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南以及中国科学院、中国文学艺术界联合会的负责人。在会见时，代表团将从日本带来的礼品送交给上述三团体。
