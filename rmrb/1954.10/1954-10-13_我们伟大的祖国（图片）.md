    Our Great Motherland
    After the communique on the Sino-Soviet talks was published, employees of the Beijing No. 1 Machine Tool Plant posted bulletins one after another, thanking the Soviet government for its friendship and assistance. The workers are reading the breaking news about the communique of the Sino-Soviet talks here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中苏举行会谈的公报公布后，北京第一机床厂职工纷纷张贴快报，感谢苏联政府的友谊的援助。工人们正在这里阅读中苏举行会谈公报的快报消息。
