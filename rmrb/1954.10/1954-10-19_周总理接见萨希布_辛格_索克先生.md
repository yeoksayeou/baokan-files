Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Mr. Sahib Singh Sokhey

Author:

Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th—Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Mr. Sahib Singh Sokhey, Vice-Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, Fellow of the Indian Academy of Sciences, and Member of the Indian Parliament, who is visiting our country, on the afternoon of the 17th.

Present at the meeting were: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee; and Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

### 周总理接见萨希布·辛格·索克先生

1954-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见萨希布·辛格·索克先生
    【新华社十八日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在十七日午后接见了来我国访问的全印和平理事会副主席、印度科学院院士、印度国会议员萨希布·辛格·索克先生。
    在座的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、中国科学院院长、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书长刘宁一，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一。
