Title: Japanese Workers' Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-02
Page: 3

    Japanese Workers' Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    The Japanese Workers' Delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend our National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of September 30.
    The delegation consists of the following members:
    Head of the delegation: Shigeaki Aizawa (Vice Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan); Deputy Head: Kiyoshi Kamiyama (Vice Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, Chairman of the Japan Electrical Industry Workers' Union); Members: Nobuo Shioya (Director of Dispute Countermeasures Department, General Council of Trade Unions of Japan); Toshio Yokoyama (General Secretary of the National Railway Workers' Union); Norio Tsukahara (Deputy General Secretary of the Japan Teachers' Union); Mutsuo Miyagawa (Chairman of the Miike Branch of the Japan Coal Miners' Union); Tomizo Asaba (Vice Chairman of the National Federation of Local Government Workers' Unions); Shun Oide (Director of the Planning Department, All-Japan Postal Workers' Union); Shogo Oki (General Secretary of the National Telecommunications Workers' Union); Kenjiro Kakuo (Vice Chairman of the All-Agriculture and Forestry Ministry Workers' Union); Masakichi Kubo (Vice Chairman of the Japan Federation of Urban Transportation Workers' Unions); Kazuo Saito (Vice Chairman of the All-Monopoly Corporation Workers' Union); Yoshinobu Momma (Vice Chairman of the Tsurumi Steel Works Union of Nippon Kokan K.K.); Jinjuro Madori (Vice Chairman of the Japan Private Railway Workers' Union Confederation); Rokuro Nishino (General Secretary of the Synthetic Chemical Industry Workers' Union Federation); Seiji Sawada (Vice Chairman of the All-Japan Metal Mine Workers' Union Federation); Torao Suzuki (Vice Chairman of the National Metal Workers' Union); Juro Ida (Vice Chairman of the Chemical Industry Workers' Union League); Goro Inoue (Chairman of the All-Japan National Medical Workers' Union); Tokushige Amano (Chairman of the National Federation of Justice Department Workers' Unions); Michiyuki Masaki (Chairman of the All-Commerce and Industry Workers' Union); Iwao Yoshikawa (Chairman of the Japan Newspaper Workers' Union Federation); Shigeru Yasuda (Vice Chairman of the All-Japan Harbor Workers' Union); Suehiro Honda (Director of the Research Department of the National Electrical Machinery Workers' Union Federation); Shozo Kusakawa (Director of the Organization Department of the All-Japan Shipbuilding Workers' Union); Yutaka Iwasa (Chairman of the Pulp Prince Paper Workers' Union); Fujiki Kasuga (Chairman of the All-Japan Hotel and Restaurant Workers' Union Federation); Kiyoto Hayashi (Chairman of the Katakura Silk Workers' Union of the National Federation of Silk Workers' Unions); Tamishige Haga (Director of the Tokyo Regional Council Secretariat); Kisaburo Nakahashi (Chairman of the Osaka Regional Council); Isamu Koyanagi (Chairman of the Fukuoka Prefectural Council of Trade Unions); Yasu Inoue (Director of the Women's Department of the All-Japan Postal Workers' Union); Shozo Tokunaga (Chairman of the International Telecommunications and Telephone Workers' Union); Itaru Izawa (Director of the Culture Department of the All-Japan Seamen's Union).
    Kenji Kaneko, Yoshimasa Wada, representatives of the All-Japan Industrial Trade Union Conference, and Kojiro Fukuda, General Secretary of the All-Japan Automobile Industry Workers' Union, are already in Beijing. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 日本工人代表团到达北京

1954-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    日本工人代表团到达北京
    应中华全国总工会的邀请来参加我国国庆观礼的日本工人代表团，已在九月三十日晚乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团的组成如下：
    团长相泽重明（日本工会总评议会副主席），副团长神山清喜（日本工会总评议会副主席、日本电器产业工会主席），团员盐谷信雄（日本工会总评议会争议对策部长），横山利秋（国营铁道工会总书记），冢原典夫（日本教职员工会副总书记），宫川睦男（日本煤矿工会三池分会主席），浅羽富造（全国自治团体工会协议会副主席），大出俊（全递信从业员工会企划部长），大木正吾（全国电器通信从业员工会总书记），角尾坚次郎（全农林省工会副主席），久保政吉（日本都市交通工会联合会副主席）斋藤一雄（全专卖工会副主席），门间吉信（日本钢管公司鹤见制铁所工会副主席），真鸟甚十郎（日本私营铁道工会总联合会副主席），西野六郎（合成化学产业工会联合会总书记），泽田政治（全日本金属矿山工会联合会副主席），铃木寅雄（全国金属工会副主席），井田十郎（化学产业工会同盟副主席），井上五郎（全日本国立医疗工会主席），天野德重（全国司法部联员工会主席），正木道行（全商工工会主席），吉川严（日本新闻工会联合会主席），安田茂（全日本港湾工会副主席），本田末广（全国电机机器工会联合会调查部长），草川昭三（全日本造船工会组织部长），岩佐丰（纸浆王子制纸工会主席），春日藤喜（全日本旅馆餐厅从业员工会联合会主席），林清登（全国蚕丝工会联合会片仓制丝工会主席），芳贺民重（东京地评事务局长），仲桥喜三郎（大阪地评主席），小柳勇（福冈县工会总评议会主席），井上安（全递信从业员工会妇女部长），德永正三
