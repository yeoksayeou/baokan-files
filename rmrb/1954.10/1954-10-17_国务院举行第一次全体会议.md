Title: The State Council Holds its First Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1954-10-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The first plenary session of the State Council was held on the afternoon of the 15th. The meeting discussed and decided on the organization and work of the State Council. The meeting also approved the "Decision on the Approval Procedures for Treaties Concluded with Foreign Countries" (draft), which will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. After listening to Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai's report on the Sino-Soviet talks, the meeting approved the Sino-Soviet "Agreement on Selling the Soviet Shares in the Four Sino-Soviet Joint Stock Companies, namely 'Sino-Soviet Petroleum Company', 'Sino-Soviet Nonferrous and Rare Metals Company', 'Sino-Soviet Shipbuilding Company' and 'Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Company', to the People's Republic of China" and the "Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement." After listening to the explanation made by Vice Premier Li Fuchun, the meeting approved the "Sino-Albanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement" and the "Sino-Albanian Technical and Technical Science Cooperation Agreement."

### 国务院举行第一次全体会议

1954-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第一次全体会议
    【新华社十六日讯】国务院全体会议第一次会议在十五日下午举行。会议就国务院的组织和工作问题作了讨论和决定。会议还通过了“关于同外国缔结条约的批准手续的决定”（草案），这一决定（草案），将向全国人民代表大会常务委员会提出。会议在听了周恩来总理兼外交部部长所作的关于中苏会谈的报告后，通过了中苏“关于将‘中苏石油公司’、‘中苏有色及稀有金属公司’、‘中苏造船公司’和‘中苏民用航空公司’四个中苏股份公司中的苏联股份售予中华人民共和国的协定”和“中苏科学技术合作协定”。会议在听了李富春副总理所作的说明后，通过了“中阿文化合作协定”和“中阿技术和技术科学合作协定”。
