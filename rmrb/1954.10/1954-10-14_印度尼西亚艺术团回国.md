Title: Indonesian Art Troupe Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: The Indonesian Art Troupe, led by its head, Mr. Mengadas Nasushen, concluded its visit and performances in our country and left Guangzhou for home via Hong Kong on the 11th. Mr. Zeng Zhusen, Dean of the Chinese Studies Department of the University of Indonesia, also left Guangzhou to return home.

Since arriving in our country on August 3rd, the Indonesian Art Troupe has given seventeen wonderful performances in seven major cities, including Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Guangzhou. They were warmly welcomed by audiences everywhere.

### 印度尼西亚艺术团回国

1954-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚艺术团回国
    据新华社讯：印度尼西亚艺术团结束在我国的访问演出后，由团长孟阿达斯·纳苏申率领在十一日离广州经香港回国。印度尼西亚大学汉学院院长曾珠森也一同离广州返国。
    印度尼西亚艺术团自八月三日到达我国以来，曾在北京、天津、沈阳、南京、上海、杭州、广州等七大城市作了十七次精采演出。受到各地观众热烈欢迎。
