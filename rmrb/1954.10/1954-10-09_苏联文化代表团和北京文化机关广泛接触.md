Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation and Beijing Cultural Institutions Engage in Extensive Contact
Author:
Date: 1954-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Soviet Cultural Delegation continued to engage in extensive contact with various cultural institutions in Beijing on the 7th.

    Soviet Academy of Sciences Vice President Ostrovityanov, at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Marx-Lenin Academy, delivered a report on "Production and Consumption under the Socialist System." The audience included professors and students from the Marx-Lenin Academy, Renmin University of China, Peking University, and cadres from the Academy of Sciences and related institutions, totaling more than 1,500 people.

    On the same day, the Editor-in-Chief of the Soviet "Historical Issues" magazine, Pankratova, continued to participate in the "Historical Issues" symposium at the Chinese Academy of Sciences. The President of the Soviet National Moscow University, Petrovsky, was invited to participate in the symposium at the Ministry of Higher Education. The Deputy Director of the Russian Academy of Educational Sciences, Melnikov, was invited to participate in the symposium at the Ministry of Education. The Chairman of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce, Nesterov, was invited to participate in the symposium at the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

    In addition, the head of the Soviet Cultural Delegation, Denisov, and some members of the delegation visited the Marx-Lenin Academy. The President of the Kazakh Academy of Sciences, Kunaev, visited the Beijing Mining Institute. Petrovsky, Melnikov, and others visited the attached Girls' Middle School of Beijing Normal University. The Lithuanian writer and poet, Venclova, and the Chairman of the Ukrainian Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Kiziya, visited the former residence of Lu Xun. Kiziya presented Lu Xun's widow, Xu Guangping, with Ukrainian editions of "Selected Works of Lu Xun" and "Medals Commemorating the 300th Anniversary of the Reunification of Ukraine with Russia," among other gifts.

### 苏联文化代表团和北京文化机关广泛接触

1954-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联文化代表团和北京文化机关广泛接触
   【新华社七日讯】苏联文化代表团七日与北京市各文化机关继续进行广泛接触。
    苏联科学院副院长奥斯特罗维季扬诺夫应中国科学院和马列学院的邀请，作了“社会主义制度下的生产和消费”的报告。报告会的听众有马列学院、中国人民大学、北京大学的教授和学生以及科学院和有关机关的干部共一千五百多人。
    同日，苏联“历史问题”杂志总编辑潘克拉托娃继续参加了中国科学院的“历史问题”座谈会。苏联国立莫斯科大学校长彼得罗夫斯基应邀参加了高等教育部的座谈会。俄罗斯教育科学院副院长麦尔尼科夫应邀参加了教育部的座谈会。苏联商会主席涅斯捷罗夫应邀参加了中国国际贸易促进委员会的座谈会。
    此外，苏联文化代表团团长杰尼索夫和部分团员参观了马列学院。哈萨克科学院院长库纳耶夫参观了北京矿业学院。彼得罗夫斯基和麦尔尼科夫等参观了北京师范大学附属女子中学。立陶宛作家与诗人文茨洛瓦和乌克兰对外文化协会理事会主席基齐亚等参观了鲁迅故居，基齐亚赠给鲁迅夫人许广平乌克兰文的“鲁迅选集”和“乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年的纪念章”等礼品。
