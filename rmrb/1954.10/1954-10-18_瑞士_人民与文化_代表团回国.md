Title: Swiss "People and Culture" Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Swiss "People and Culture" delegation has concluded its visit to China and returned home by plane from Beijing on the 16th.

　　Prior to their departure, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a farewell banquet. At the banquet, the delegation presented the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries with a delicate wall clock made by Swiss workers. Mr. Mure, the head of the delegation, said that although Switzerland is more than 10,000 miles away from China, we have found true friendship in China. This clock symbolizes the Swiss people's friendship towards China.

### 瑞士“人民与文化”代表团回国

1954-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞士“人民与文化”代表团回国
    【新华社十六日讯】瑞士“人民与文化”代表团已经结束在中国的参观、访问，十六日乘飞机离北京返国。
    行前，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南曾设宴欢送。在宴会上，代表团将瑞士工人所制的一架精致挂钟赠给中国人民对外文化协会。团长缪雷先生说瑞士虽距中国一万多里远，但我们在中国找到真正的友谊。这架挂钟就象征瑞士人民对中国的友好。
