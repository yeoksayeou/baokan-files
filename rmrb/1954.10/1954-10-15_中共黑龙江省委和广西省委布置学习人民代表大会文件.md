Title: The Heilongjiang and Guangxi Provincial Committees of the Chinese Communist Party Deploy Study of National People's Congress Documents
Author:
Date: 1954-10-15
Page: 1

    [Reported by this newspaper] In order to enable cadres and the broad masses of people to deeply understand the great historical significance of the First Session of the First National People's Congress, to further understand the great role of the Constitution in promoting China's socialist cause, and to inspire the confidence and enthusiasm of cadres and the people in working harder for socialist construction, the Heilongjiang and Guangxi Provincial Committees of the Chinese Communist Party issued notices and instructions in early October respectively, requiring all localities to launch cadres to study and convey the various documents of the First Session of the First National People's Congress among the people for about a month starting in early October. In addition to carefully reading the documents, cadres at all levels should be organized by Party committees at all levels for reports and discussions. When conveying information to the people, attention must be paid to combining it with production and current central work in order to promote production and work. The notices and instructions all pointed out that studying and publicizing the Constitution is a regular and important task, and in the future, education on the content of the Constitution and compliance with the Constitution and laws must be conducted regularly among cadres and the people.

### 中共黑龙江省委和广西省委布置学习人民代表大会文件

1954-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中共黑龙江省委和广西省委布置学习人民代表大会文件
    【本报讯】为了使干部和广大人民群众深入理解全国人民代表大会第一次会议的伟大历史意义，进一步理解宪法对促进我国社会主义事业的巨大作用，鼓舞干部和人民群众更加努力从事社会主义建设的信心和积极性，中共黑龙江和广西省委员会在十月初分别发出通知和指示，要求各地从十月上旬开始，用一个月左右的时间，发动干部学习和在人民群众中进行传达第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的各项文件。各级干部除应认真阅读文件外，并由各级党委组织报告和讨论。在向人民群众进行传达时，必须注意结合生产和当前的中心工作，借以推动生产和工作。通知和指示中都指出：学习和宣传宪法是一项经常性的重要工作，今后必须经常向干部和人民群众进行关于宪法内容和遵守宪法与法律的教育。
