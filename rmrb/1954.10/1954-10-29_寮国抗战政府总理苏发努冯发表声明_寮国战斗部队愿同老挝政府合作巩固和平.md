Title: Lao Resistance Government Premier Souphanouvong Issues Statement: Lao Fighting Forces Willing to Cooperate with Lao Government to Consolidate Peace
Author:
Date: 1954-10-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Vientiane, October 28th: Vietnam News Agency, Laos, October 28th: Premier of the Lao Resistance Government, Prince Souphanouvong, issued the following statement on October 28, 1954, in a gathering area of the Lao Fighting Forces: I solemnly declare that the Lao Fighting Forces are willing to cooperate with the Lao Royal Government in order to fulfill the Geneva Accords, consolidate peace, and realize the unification of the Kingdom of Laos.

### 寮国抗战政府总理苏发努冯发表声明  寮国战斗部队愿同老挝政府合作巩固和平

1954-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    寮国抗战政府总理苏发努冯发表声明
    寮国战斗部队愿同老挝政府合作巩固和平
    【新华社二十八日讯】越南通讯社老挝二十八日讯：寮国抗战政府总理苏发努冯亲王一九五四年十月二十八日在寮国战斗部队集结区某地发表声明如下：我郑重声明，寮国战斗部队愿意同老挝王国政府合作，以便履行日内瓦协定，巩固和平和实现老挝王国的统一。
