Title: Guo Moruo Cables Congratulations to Columbia University on its Bicentennial

Author:

Date: 1954-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, sent a cable to the President of Columbia University in the United States, congratulating Columbia University on its bicentennial anniversary. The cable reads as follows: Respected President of Columbia University in New York, USA:

Thank you for inviting the Chinese Academy of Sciences to participate in your university's bicentennial celebration. Due to work commitments, we are unable to send a representative to attend. We hereby extend our warm congratulations. We hope that your scientific and educational endeavors will strive to safeguard world peace and benefit humanity.

Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, October 28, 1954

### 郭沫若电贺哥伦比亚大学建校二百周年

1954-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若电贺哥伦比亚大学建校二百周年
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国科学院院长郭沫若致电美国哥伦比亚大学校长，祝贺哥伦比亚大学建校二百周年纪念。电文如下：美国纽约哥伦比亚大学敬爱的校长先生：
    谢谢你们邀请中国科学院参加贵校二百周年纪念大会，但因工作关系未能派代表出席，谨此致以热烈的祝贺。希望你们的科学教育事业为保卫世界和平和造福人类而努力。
      中国科学院院长郭沫若一九五四年十月二十八日于北京
