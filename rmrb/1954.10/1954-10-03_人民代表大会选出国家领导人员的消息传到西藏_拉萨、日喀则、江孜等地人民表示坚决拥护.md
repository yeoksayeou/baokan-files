Title: The News of the National People's Congress Electing National Leaders Reaches Tibet: People in Lhasa, Xigaze, Gyangze and Other Places Express Their Firm Support

Author:
Date: 1954-10-03
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 2nd) The happy news that the First Session of the First National People's Congress elected new national leading personnel quickly spread to major cities and towns on the Tibetan Plateau through radio broadcasts. The people expressed their warm support and love for the new national leading personnel with extraordinary excitement.

On September 27, the Lhasa City wired broadcasting station rebroadcast the Central People's Broadcasting Station's Tibetan broadcast reporting the election results to the entire city. After dusk, the song "The East is Red" resounded everywhere on the streets. People from cultural and educational circles in Lhasa and officials of all levels, both monks and laymen, of the Tibetan local government held celebration symposiums on the 29th and 30th respectively.

Those who spoke at the symposiums included officials of all levels, both monks and laymen, of the Tibetan local government, representatives of the three major monasteries in Tibet, members of the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association, members of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Association, and other people from cultural and educational circles. In their speeches, people unanimously supported the election of Chairman Mao and other new national leading personnel. Xia Su·Jiumo Ciren Dorje, the acting Kalon of the Tibetan local government, gave a detailed account of the construction achievements of the new China in the past five years, and the progress made in developing politics, economy, and culture since the peaceful liberation of Tibet. He said that these achievements were entirely the result of the correct leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party. Under the leadership of Chairman Mao and other new national leading personnel, the Tibetan people will move towards a more prosperous and happy path. The symposium also sent a congratulatory telegram to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, congratulating the successful conclusion of the First Session of the First National People's Congress, and expressing firm support for all the resolutions passed by the congress.

People from all walks of life in Xigaze City and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Xigaze City expressed their heartfelt support in their talks after hearing the good news. Lhamin Yixi Chuchen, Director of the Panchen Kampus Conference Hall Committee, said: The election of Chairman Mao, the most respected leader of the people of all ethnic groups in the country, as the Chairman of the People's Republic of China, is fully in line with the wishes of the 600 million people across the country; it is a great honor for our Tibetan people that the Dalai Lama was elected as the Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Panchen Erdeni was elected as a member of the Standing Committee. Wang Lejie, a senior lama of Tashilhunpo Monastery, Nilong, Deputy Director of the Xigaze Patriotic Youth Cultural Association, and other people from all walks of life also made speeches, unanimously supporting the newly elected national leading personnel.

People from all walks of life in Gyangze City also congratulated the election of Chairman Mao and other new national leading personnel with boundless joy. Cadres from the People's Liberation Army organs stationed in Gyangze and Tibetan youths listened together to the Central People's Broadcasting Station's broadcast about the election results. When they heard the good news, cheers for "Long live Chairman Mao" and the singing of the Tibetan and Chinese versions of "The East is Red" merged with the cheers from the radio. After listening to the broadcast, they also held a celebration symposium.

### 人民代表大会选出国家领导人员的消息传到西藏  拉萨、日喀则、江孜等地人民表示坚决拥护

1954-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    人民代表大会选出国家领导人员的消息传到西藏
    拉萨、日喀则、江孜等地人民表示坚决拥护
    【新华社拉萨二日电】第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议选出新的国家领导工作人员的喜讯通过广播电台，很快传遍了康藏高原上的各大城镇。人民都以异常兴奋的心情，热烈表示他们对新的国家领导工作人员的拥护和爱戴。
    九月二十七日，拉萨市有线广播电台向全市转播了中央人民广播电台报告选举结果的藏语广播。黄昏以后，大街上到处响起“东方红”的歌声。拉萨市文化教育界人士和西藏地方政府各级僧俗官员分别在二十九、三十两日举行了庆祝座谈会。
    在座谈会上讲话的有西藏地方政府各级僧俗官员、西藏三大寺代表、拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会、拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会的会员和其他文化教育界人士。人们在发言中一致拥护毛主席和其他新的国家领导工作人员当选。西藏地方政府代理噶伦夏苏·举墨策仁多吉在发言中详细地叙述了新中国五年来的建设成就，和西藏和平解放以来在发展政治、经济、文化各方面获得的成绩。他说，这些成绩完全是毛主席、中国共产党正确领导的结果，今后在毛主席和其他新的国家领导工作人员的领导下，西藏人民将会走向更加繁荣幸福的道路。座谈会还向全国人民代表大会常务委员会发出贺电，祝贺第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议胜利闭幕，并对大会通过的一切决议表示坚决拥护。
    日喀则市各界人民和驻日喀则市人民解放军的战士们听到这个喜讯后，纷纷发表谈话表示衷心拥护。班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任委员拉敏·益喜楚臣说：全国各族人民最敬爱的领袖毛主席当选为中华人民共和国主席，这是完全符合全国六亿人民的意愿的；达赖喇嘛当选为全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、班禅额尔德尼当选为常务委员会委员，更是我们西藏人民莫大的光荣。札什伦布寺大喇嘛王乐阶、日喀则爱国青年文化联谊会副主任委员尼隆和其他各界人士也都发表谈话，一致拥护新选出的国家领导工作人员。
    江孜市各界人民也以无限欢欣的心情，祝贺毛主席和其他新的国家领导工作人员的当选。驻江孜人民解放军机关干部和藏族青年曾在一起收听了中央人民广播电台广播关于选举结果的消息。当他们听到喜讯时，欢呼毛主席万岁、合唱藏汉文“东方红”的歌声和收音机里传出的欢呼声响成一片。收听广播后，他们还召开了庆祝座谈会。
