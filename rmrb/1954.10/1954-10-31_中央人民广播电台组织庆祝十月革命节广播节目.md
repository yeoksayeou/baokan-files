Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Organizes Radio Programs Celebrating the October Revolution Day
Author: 
Date: 1954-10-31
Page: 3

    The Central People's Broadcasting Station is organizing special radio programs from November 1st to 7th to celebrate the 37th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. The programs include a speech by Han En, a representative of the National People's Congress and a national model agricultural worker: "Advancing along the road traveled by Soviet farmers"; a speech by Chen Fengtong, director of the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute: "Learning from the advanced experience of Soviet agricultural science"; a speech by Shen Tu, general manager of the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Corporation: "Cherishing forever the crystallization of sincere cooperation between China and the Soviet Union" and an article by Ma Sicong, vice chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association: "The great achievements of Russian and Soviet musical art and its influence on world music", etc. The music programs include excerpts from the famous Russian musician Glinka's opera "Ivan Susanin" and Borodin's opera "Prince Igor", Tchaikovsky's String Quartet, Shostakovich's new work, Symphony No. 10 by the Soviet People's Artist, and an edited version of the Soviet People's Artist Shaporin's opera "The Decembrists", etc. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will also organize a radio concert of Russian classical and Soviet modern musical works performed by Chinese musicians, and a concert by students of Professor Medvedev, vocal consultant of the Central Conservatory of Music. The recitation programs include excerpts from Russian and Soviet literary masterpieces such as "Anna Karenina", "Virgin Soil Upturned", "How the Steel Was Tempered", "Mother", "Far from Moscow", "The Young Guard", and "An Ordinary Soldier", as well as Gorky's "The Song of the Stormy Petrel" and Mayakovsky's "Good!". In addition, Chekhov's one-act play "The Proposal" performed by the China Youth Art Theater will be broadcast. The program will also introduce the films screened during the Soviet Film Week held this year, and broadcast excerpts from the Soviet films "Harvest" and "Shevchenko".
    The programs organized this time are very rich, with more than fifty in total.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中央人民广播电台组织庆祝十月革命节广播节目

1954-10-31
第3版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台组织庆祝十月革命节广播节目
    中央人民广播电台为庆祝十月社会主义革命三十七周年，从十一月一日到七日组织专门的广播节目。节目中有全国人民代表大会代表、全国农业劳动模范韩恩的讲话：“沿着苏联农民走过的道路前进”；华北农业科学研究所所长陈凤桐的讲话：“学习苏联农业科学先进经验”；中苏民用航空公司总经理沈图的讲话：“永远珍贵中苏两国真诚合作的结晶”和中国音乐家协会副主席马思聪的文章：“俄罗斯及苏联音乐艺术的伟大成就及其对世界音乐的影响”等。音乐节目中有著名的俄罗斯音乐家格林卡的歌剧“伊凡·苏萨宁”和鲍罗亭的歌剧“伊戈尔王”的片断，柴可夫斯基的弦乐四重奏，苏联人民艺术家萧斯塔柯维奇的新作品第十交响乐、苏联人民艺术家夏波林的歌剧“十二月党人”剪辑等。中央人民广播电台并将组织由中国音乐家演奏的俄罗斯古典和苏联现代音乐作品的广播音乐会，中央音乐学院声乐顾问梅德维耶夫教授学生的音乐会。朗诵节目中有“安娜·卡列尼娜”、“被开垦的处女地”、“钢铁是怎样炼成的”、“母”、“远离莫斯科的地方”、“青年近卫军”、“普通一兵”等俄罗斯和苏联文学名著中的片断，以及高尔基的“海燕”、马雅可夫斯基的“好啊！”。另外还将广播由中国青年艺术剧院演出的契诃夫的独幕话剧“求婚”。节目中还将介绍今年举行的苏联电影周放映的影片，并广播苏联影片“收获”、“舍甫琴柯”的剪辑。
    这次组织的节目很丰富，共有五十多个。
                                  （新华社）
