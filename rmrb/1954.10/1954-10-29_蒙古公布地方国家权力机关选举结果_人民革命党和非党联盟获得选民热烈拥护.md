Title: Mongolia Announces Results of Local State Power Organ Elections; People's Revolutionary Party and Non-Party League Receive Enthusiastic Support from Voters
Author:
Date: 1954-10-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 28th (TASS dispatch): The Mongolian "Truth Daily" published on the 27th the results of the local state power organ elections held on October 24th.
    The number of voters participating in the elections for all levels of Khurals (local state power organs) in Aimaks (provinces), cities, Sums (counties), Khorons (districts), Baghs (townships), and Khorins (sub-districts) accounted for 99.98 percent of the total number of voters. The number of voters who voted for candidates of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Non-Party People's election alliance reached more than 99 percent of the number of voters who participated in the voting.
    The Mongolian People's Republic elected a total of 32,140 representatives to local organs. Among them, members of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party accounted for 31.59 percent, non-party members accounted for 68.41 percent, and women representatives accounted for 21.89 percent of the elected representatives.

### 蒙古公布地方国家权力机关选举结果  人民革命党和非党联盟获得选民热烈拥护

1954-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古公布地方国家权力机关选举结果
    人民革命党和非党联盟获得选民热烈拥护
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：蒙古“真理报”二十七日公布了十月二十四日举行的地方国家权力机关选举的结果。
    参加艾马克（省）、市、苏木（县）、霍伦（区）、巴戈（乡）和霍林（分区）等各级呼拉尔
  （地方国家权力机关）选举的选民人数为全体选民人数的百分之九十九点九八。投票选举蒙古人民革命党和非党人士选举联盟的候选人的选民人数达参加投票人数的百分之九十九以上。
    蒙古人民共和国总共选举三万二千一百四十名地方机关的代表。其中蒙古人民革命党党员占百分之三十一点五九，非党人士占百分之六十八点四一，妇女代表占被选上的代表的百分之二十一点八九。
