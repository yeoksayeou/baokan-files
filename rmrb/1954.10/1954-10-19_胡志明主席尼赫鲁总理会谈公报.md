Title: Joint Communiqué of Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Prime Minister Nehru's Talks

Author: 
Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 17th: The joint communiqué of the meeting between Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Jawaharlal Nehru, Prime Minister of the Republic of India, in Hanoi on the 17th is as follows:
    Chairman Ho Chi Minh met with Indian Prime Minister Nehru this evening, and they discussed various issues of common concern. They both expressed concern over the full implementation of the Geneva Accords on Indochina. Chairman Ho Chi Minh reaffirmed to the Indian Prime Minister that he is giving and will continue to give full cooperation to the International Commission to enable the implementation of this agreement. He is extremely willing to resolve all outstanding issues in a peaceful and cooperative manner so that the countries of Indochina can live independently and move towards prosperity without foreign interference. He fully believes in the Five Principles agreed upon by the Chinese and Indian Prime Ministers, and hopes to apply these five principles in the relations between Vietnam and Laos and Cambodia, as well as other countries. The Indian Prime Minister also believes that the implementation of these principles will eliminate difficulties and doubts and help promote friendly relations and peaceful cooperation between countries.
    Chairman Ho Chi Minh spoke of the historically long-standing ties between the peoples of India and Vietnam, and hopes to re-establish and strengthen this relationship in the future. The Indian Prime Minister also fully believes that the relations between India and Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia, and their peoples, should be encouraged and strengthened.
   Hanoi, October 17, 1954

### 胡志明主席尼赫鲁总理会谈公报

1954-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席尼赫鲁总理会谈公报
    【新华社河内十七日电】越南民主共和国胡志明主席和印度共和国尼赫鲁总理十七日在河内会谈的联合公报如下：
    胡志明主席今天晚上会晤印度总理尼赫鲁，他们讨论了共同有关的各种问题。他们都关心关于印度支那的日内瓦协定的充分履行，胡志明主席向印度总理确申：他正在给予并且将继续给予国际委员会充分合作，以使这个协定得以履行。他极愿以和平的和合作的方法解决一切悬而未决的问题，以便使印度支那各国能在没有外来干涉的情况下独立地生活并走向繁荣。他充分相信中国总理和印度总理所同意的五项原则，并希望在越南同老挝和柬埔寨以及其他国家的关系中应用这五项原则。印度总理也认为：实施这些原则将能消除困难和疑惧，并有助于促成各国之间的友好关系和和平合作。
    胡志明主席谈到了印度和越南人民的历史悠久的联系，希望在将来能重新建立并加强这种联系。印度总理也完全认为应当鼓励并加强印度和越南、老挝及柬埔寨各国及其人民的关系。
   河内一九五四年十月十七日
