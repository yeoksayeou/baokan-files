Title: Ambassador Yudin Hosts Reception to Bid Farewell to Soviet Cultural Delegation; Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Soviet Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: Ambassador Yudin of the Soviet Union to our country held a reception on the evening of the 28th to bid farewell to the Soviet Cultural Delegation, which was invited to our country to participate in the celebrations for the 5th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	The head of the Soviet Cultural Delegation, Denisov, and all members of the delegation attended the reception.

	Among those invited to the reception were Song Qingling, Wu Yuzhang, Shen Junru, and Guo Moruo, vice-presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, director-general, various deputy directors-general, and council members in Beijing, as well as Shu Sheyu and Cao Jinghua, vice-presidents of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

	Also invited to the reception were Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education, Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education, and vice-ministers of various ministries.

	Also invited to the reception were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Xu Guangping, Vice-President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and prominent figures from the capital's scientific, political, legal, educational, and literary circles.

	Soviet Deputy Chief Advisor Maritsev and Qi Gangning, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in our country, also attended the reception.

	Ambassador Yudin spoke first at the meeting. He said: "The Soviet Cultural Delegation's visit to China has once again demonstrated the brotherly and sincere friendship between the two countries. This visit has brought the people of the two countries even closer. After returning home, the delegation will introduce everything they have seen in China to the Soviet people."

	Vice-President Song Qingling then spoke at the meeting. She said: "During its visit to our country, the Soviet Cultural Delegation conducted many reports and seminars. These beneficial activities will undoubtedly have a significant impact on the establishment of a new socialist culture in our country, thereby promoting the further development of our socialist construction. It plays a great role in enhancing and consolidating the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union." She expressed her heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet Cultural Delegation on behalf of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and cultural, scientific, and educational circles in our country.

	Head of the Delegation Denisov spoke at the meeting. He said: "During this visit, the Soviet Cultural Delegation saw the Chinese people confidently building their socialist motherland. Let us cheer, not only with our mouths but also with our hearts, Long Live the Great People's Republic of China, Long Live The unbreakable friendship between China and the Soviet Union!"

	Members of the Soviet Cultural Delegation Pankratova and Gerasimov, as well as Wu Yuzhang and Guo Moruo, vice-presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, director-general, also spoke at the meeting.

	The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

	Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a banquet at noon on the 28th to bid farewell to all members of the Soviet Cultural Delegation headed by Denisov, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

	Those attending the banquet included Wu Yuzhang, Vice-President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, Director-General, various Deputy Directors-General, and council members in Beijing. Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and prominent figures from scientific, cultural, and educational circles in Beijing were present as guests.

	On the eve of its departure from Beijing, the Soviet Cultural Delegation continued to visit Beijing and deliver reports on various specialized topics.

	On the 27th and 28th, the Soviet Cultural Delegation delivered specialized reports on politics, law, education, history, and other aspects to relevant units. More than 6,000 professors, students, and cadres from relevant agencies attended the report meetings. The delegation's reports were warmly welcomed by the audience.

	The delegation was also invited to participate in symposiums held by the Ministry of Higher Education, the Ministry of Education, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Writers Association, and the Renmin University of China.

	The delegation also presented many books and other gifts to various relevant agencies, groups, and schools. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association also presented various cultural gifts to the delegation and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 尤金大使举行招待会欢送苏联文化代表团  中苏友协总会设宴欢送苏联文化代表团

1954-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    尤金大使举行招待会欢送苏联文化代表团
    中苏友协总会设宴欢送苏联文化代表团
    【新华社二十八日讯】苏联驻我国大使尤金为欢送应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的苏联文化代表团，特在二十八日晚举行招待会。
    苏联文化代表团团长杰尼索夫和代表团全体团员都出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长宋庆龄、吴玉章、沈钧儒、郭沫若，总干事钱俊瑞、各副总干事和理事会在京理事，北京市中苏友好协会副会长舒舍予、曹靖华。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有中华人民共和国文化部部长沈雁冰、高等教育部部长杨秀峰、教育部部长张奚若、以及各部副部长。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，以及首都科学、政法、教育和文艺界著名人士。
    苏联副总顾问马里采夫和苏联对外文化协会驻我国代表齐刚宁也出席了招待会。
    尤金大使首先在会上讲话，他说：“苏联文化代表团这次来中国访问，再次证明了两国兄弟般的真诚友谊，这次访问使两国人民更加亲近了。代表团在回国后要将在中国看到的一切介绍给苏联人民。”
    宋庆龄副会长接着在会上讲话，她说：“苏联文化代表团在我国访问期间，曾进行了多次的报告和座谈，这些有益的活动，必将在我国建立社会主义新文化上，发生重大的影响，从而推进我国的社会主义建设事业进一步的向前发展。在增进和巩固中苏两国人民友谊的事业上，起着极大的作用。”她代表中苏友好协会总会和我国文化、科学以及教育各界，向苏联文化代表团表示衷心的感谢。
    杰尼索夫团长在会上讲话，他说：“苏联文化代表团在这次访问中看到了中国人民正在满怀信心地建设着社会主义的祖国，让我们不仅用我们的舌头，而且用我们的心来欢呼伟大的中华人民共和国万岁，中苏两国牢不可破的友谊万岁！”
    苏联文化代表团团员潘克拉托娃、格拉西莫夫和中苏友好协会副会长吴玉章、郭沫若及总干事钱俊瑞等都在会上讲了话。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
    【新华社二十八日讯】中苏友好协会总会在二十八日中午设宴欢送以苏联对外文化协会理事会主席杰尼索夫为首的苏联文化代表团全体团员。
    出席宴会的有中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、总干事钱俊瑞、各副总干事和在京理事。出席作陪的有中华人民共和国高等教育部部长杨秀峰，教育部部长张奚若，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，以及在京的科学、文化和教育界著名人士。
    苏联文化代表团在离开北京的前夕，继续在北京进行参观访问并作各类专门问题的报告。
    二十七日、二十八日苏联文化代表团分别向各有关单位作了政治、法律、教育、历史等方面的专门问题的报告。参加报告会的有各高等学校的教授、学生和有关机关的干部共六千多人。代表团的报告受到听众的热烈欢迎。
    代表团还分别应邀参加了高等教育部、教育部、中国科学院、中苏友好协会总会、作家协会和中国人民大学等单位的座谈会。
    代表团还把许多图书等礼品赠送给各个有关机关、团体和学校。中苏友好协会总会也将各种文化礼品赠送给代表团和苏联对外文化协会。
