Title: British Cabinet Reshuffle and Transfer of Some Officials
Author:
Date: 1954-10-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, 19th: The British Cabinet announced a reshuffle on the 18th.

　　This reshuffle involved the replacement and transfer of twenty-four ministerial-level officials, including: Defence Minister Alexander was removed from his post and succeeded by Harold Macmillan, the former Minister of Housing and Local Government; Home Secretary David Maxwell Fyfe was transferred to Lord Chancellor, and succeeded as Home Secretary and Minister for Welsh Affairs by Gwilym Lloyd George, the former Minister of Food; Deputy Foreign Secretary Selwyn Lloyd was transferred to Minister of Supply, and Anthony Nutting, the former Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, was transferred to Deputy Foreign Secretary. The positions of Prime Minister Churchill and Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Secretary Eden remained unchanged.

### 英内阁更换和调动部分官员

1954-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    英内阁更换和调动部分官员
    【新华社十九日讯】伦敦消息：英国内阁在十八日宣布改组。
    这次改组更换和调动了二十四个大臣级官员，其中包括：国防大臣亚历山大去职，由原房屋与地方政府大臣哈罗德·麦克米伦继任；内政大臣戴维·马克斯威尔·费夫调任大法官，由原粮食大臣格威林·劳埃德·乔治继任内政大臣兼威尔士事务大臣；外交副大臣塞尔温·劳埃德调任供应大臣，原外交部驻议会次官安东尼·纳丁调任外交副大臣。首相邱吉尔和副首相兼外交大臣艾登的职位没有变动。
