Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Arrives in Bombay from New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1954-10-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 22nd. The Chinese Government Trade Delegation, led by Kong Yuan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China and head of the trade delegation, arrived in Bombay by train from New Delhi on the 21st after signing the Sino-Indian trade agreement.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the station included: the Joint Director of the Directorate General of Import and Export Affairs of the Government of the Republic of India, the Secretary of the Bombay Government, and other officials of the Bombay Government. Joachim Alva, a member of the Indian Parliament currently in Bombay, presented a sandalwood garland to head of delegation Kong Yuan.

Also welcoming the delegation at the station were: Lu. Ke. Kalangjia, Vice Chairman of the India-China Friendship Association, members of the India-China Friendship Association, representatives of the Bombay Peace Committee, representatives of the Indian Chamber of Commerce, representatives of the All India Manufacturers Association, representatives of the All India Merchants and All India Importers and Exporters Association, and many major entrepreneurs.

Also welcoming the delegation at the station were: the Consul General of Czechoslovakia in Bombay, the Consul of Indonesia in Bombay, and the commercial representatives of the Soviet Union, Poland, Bulgaria, and Romania in Bombay.

Yao Nian, Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Bombay, and personnel from the consulate also came to the station to greet the delegation.

About one hundred and fifty overseas Chinese in Bombay also came to the station to welcome the delegation.

### 我国贸易代表团由新德里到达孟买

1954-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    我国贸易代表团由新德里到达孟买
    【新华社新德里二十二日电】中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团在签订了中印贸易协定以后，在贸易代表团团长、中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长孔原率领下，于二十一日乘火车由新德里到达孟买。
    到车站欢迎的有：印度共和国政府进出口事务总管理署联合署长、孟买政府秘书和孟买政府的其他官员。现在孟买的印度国会议员乔奇姆·亚尔瓦向孔原团长献檀香制的花环。
    到车站欢迎的还有：印中友好协会副主席鲁·克·卡朗吉亚和印中友好协会会员、孟买和平委员会的代表、印度商会的代表、全印制造商协会的代表、全印商人与全印进出口商协会的代表，以及许多大企业家。
    到车站欢迎的还有：捷克斯洛伐克驻孟买总领事、印度尼西亚驻孟买领事，以及苏联、波兰、保加利亚和罗马尼亚驻孟买的商务代表。
    中华人民共和国驻孟买总领事姚念和领事馆人员也到车站迎接。
    到车站欢迎的还有孟买华侨约一百五十人。
