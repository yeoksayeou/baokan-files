Title: Xiamen Various Circles Organize慰问 Teams to the Front to Comfort Combat Troops
Author:
Date: 1954-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, October 6th: Xiamen city people from all walks of life organized sixteen慰问 teams on October 3rd, carrying a large number of慰问 letters and慰问 goods, to the coastal defense front to comfort the heroic fighting People's Liberation Army.

The慰问 teams held discussion meetings and get-togethers with combat heroes and model soldiers on various positions and in various units. They shook hands with sentries, gunners, and anti-aircraft gunners on duty, and sang wonderful programs such as "We must liberate Taiwan" and the Taiwanese folk song "I Love My Taiwan" for the soldiers. At the various discussion meetings, labor models, advanced producers, and people's heroes and models from various units introduced their successful deeds in increasing production and attacking the Chiang bandit troops. They mutually assured each other that they would work together in their respective posts to liberate Taiwan and defend the socialist construction of the motherland. During the discussions, Lin Xiangfu, representative of the Xiamen Seamen's Union, and Ruan Chaoshi, representative of the fishermen, also denounced to the commanders and fighters of the coastal defense troops the heinous crimes of the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique in robbing merchant ships and fishing boats and killing people, and expressed that they would produce more actively to support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan.

The Fujian Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Fujian Provincial People's Government also sent the Fujian Peking Opera Troupe and the Minnan Opera Experimental Troupe Dance Team to various coastal areas and islands of Fujian on October 3rd to comfort the heroic fighting People's Liberation Army units.

### 厦门各界组织慰问队到前线慰问作战部队

1954-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    厦门各界组织慰问队到前线慰问作战部队
   【新华社福州六日电】厦门市各界人民在十月三日组织了十六个慰问队，带着大批慰问信和慰问品，分别到海防前线慰问英勇战斗的人民解放军。
    慰问队在各个阵地上和各部队功臣模范们举行了座谈会、联欢会，并与正在值勤的哨兵、炮手和高射炮手们握手问好，还向战士们演唱了“一定要解放台湾”和台湾民歌“我爱我的台湾”等精彩节目。在各个座谈会上，慰问队中的劳动模范、先进生产者和各部队的人民功臣、模范们各自介绍了增加生产和打击蒋贼军的胜利事迹，并相互保证：一定要在各自的岗位上共同为解放台湾、保卫祖国社会主义建设而奋斗。在座谈中，厦门市海员工会代表林祥富、渔民代表阮朝狮还向海防部队的指战员们控诉了蒋介石卖国集团抢劫商船、渔船和杀害人民的残暴罪行，并表示要更加积极生产，支援人民解放军解放台湾。
    中国共产党福建省委员会和福建省人民政府，也在十月三日派出福建京剧团、闽南戏实验剧团歌舞队，到福建沿海各地和各岛屿上，慰问英勇战斗的人民解放军各部队。
