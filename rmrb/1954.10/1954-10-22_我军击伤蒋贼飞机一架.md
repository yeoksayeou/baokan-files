Title: Our Army Shoots Down One Chiang Bandit Plane
Author:
Date: 1954-10-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency

   Our Army Shoots Down One Chiang Bandit Plane
   [Xinhua News Agency dispatch] Chiang bandit planes continue to intrude and harass the coastal areas of Fujian. At 7:20 AM on the 13th, two Chiang bandit planes intruded over the Shuitou airspace northeast of Xiamen, circling twice, and at 7:40 AM, they intruded into the Maren airspace southeast of Tong'an to harass. At that time, our army's anti-aircraft artillery units immediately opened fire, hitting the tail of one Chiang bandit plane.

### 我军击伤蒋贼飞机一架

1954-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

   我军击伤蒋贼飞机一架
   【新华社讯】蒋贼飞机继续窜扰福建沿海地区。十三日七时二十分，蒋贼飞机两架窜入厦门东北的水头上空盘旋两次，在七时四十分又窜至同安东南之马巷上空骚扰。当时我军高射炮部队立即开火，击中一架蒋贼飞机的尾部。
