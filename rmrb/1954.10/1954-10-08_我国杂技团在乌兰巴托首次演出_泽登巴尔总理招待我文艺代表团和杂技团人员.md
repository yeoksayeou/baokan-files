Title: Our Acrobatic Troupe Performs for the First Time in Ulaanbaatar; Premier Tsedenbal Hosts Our Art and Cultural Delegation and Acrobatic Troupe Members
Author:
Date: 1954-10-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 4th. News: The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, participating in the "Mongolia-China Friendship Week," gave its first performance at the Mongolian National Circus in Ulaanbaatar on the 2nd.

Attending the first performance ceremony were: Premier Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic, First Deputy Premier Shirindib, Deputy Premiers Luvsan, Maidar, Tsende, First Secretary Damba of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Secretaries Dugsuren, Ramsuren, Deputy Chairman Zedib of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, heads of various departments of the Mongolian government, and heads of various people's organizations. The ambassadors and embassy staff of our country, the Soviet Union, and North Korea to Mongolia were also invited to attend. The total audience was about 600 people.

Every performance by the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe was warmly welcomed and praised by the audience.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 5th. News: Premier Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic received Cao Yu, head of our Art and Cultural Delegation, Ma Yanxiang, a member of the delegation, and Lu Yaowu, a member of the delegation and head of our Acrobatic Troupe, on the 4th. He Ying, our ambassador to Mongolia, accompanied Cao Yu and others.

Also present at the meeting was Sharap, Secretary of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic and Director of the "Mongolia-China Friendship Week" Preparatory Committee.

Premier Tsedenbal had a cordial conversation with Head Cao Yu and others for an hour.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 7th. News: Premier Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic hosted a banquet on the evening of the 6th to entertain the Chinese Art and Cultural Delegation and Acrobatic Troupe members who were invited to participate in the "Mongolia-China Friendship Week."

He Ying, our ambassador to Mongolia, and embassy staff were also invited to attend. Also invited to attend were: Soviet Ambassador to Mongolia Pisarev, North Korean Ambassador to Mongolia Hong Dong-cheol, and embassy staff.

Attending as hosts were: First Deputy Premier Shirindib of the Mongolian People's Republic, Deputy Premiers Luvsan, Maidar, Secretary Sharap of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Idanjab, ministers of various ministries, and famous figures from the scientific, cultural, and artistic circles. About 200 people attended.

A dance party was held after the banquet. The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 我国杂技团在乌兰巴托首次演出  泽登巴尔总理招待我文艺代表团和杂技团人员

1954-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我国杂技团在乌兰巴托首次演出
    泽登巴尔总理招待我文艺代表团和杂技团人员
    【新华社四日讯】乌兰巴托消息：参加“蒙中友好旬”的中国杂技团于二日在乌兰巴托蒙古国家马戏院首次演出。
    参加首次演出仪式的有：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理鲁布桑、迈达尔、曾德，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴，书记杜格苏伦、拉姆苏伦，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席泽狄布，蒙古政府各部门首长及各人民团体负责人。我国和苏联、朝鲜驻蒙古大使及使馆人员也应邀出席。全体观众共约六百余人。
    中国杂技团演出的每个节目都受到了观众的热烈欢迎和称赞。
      【新华社五日讯】乌兰巴托消息：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔四日接见我国文艺代表团团长曹禺，团员马彦祥和团员兼我国杂技团团长卢耀武。我国驻蒙古大使何英陪同曹禺等前往。
    接见时在座的有蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团秘书、“蒙中友好旬”筹备委员会主任沙拉普。
    泽登巴尔总理与曹禺团长等作了一小时的亲切谈话。
    【新华社七日讯】乌兰巴托讯：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔在六日晚设宴招待应邀参加“蒙中友好旬”的中国文艺代表团和杂技团人员。
    我国驻蒙古大使何英和使馆人员也应邀出席。应邀出席的还有：苏联驻蒙古大使皮萨列夫、朝鲜驻蒙古大使洪东喆及使馆人员。
    出席作陪的有：蒙古人民共和国第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理鲁布桑、迈达尔，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团秘书沙拉普，外交部副部长伊丹甲布，各部部长，以及科学、文化、艺术界著名人士等。出席的共约两百余人。
    宴后并举行了舞会。招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
