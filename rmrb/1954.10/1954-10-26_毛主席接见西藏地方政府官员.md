Title: Chairman Mao Receives Tibetan Local Government Officials
Author:
Date: 1954-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Kalon Sokang Wangqing Gelei of the Tibetan Local Government at Qin Zheng Dian in Zhongnanhai, Beijing, on the afternoon of the 25th. Also received were Lobsang Sandan, Acting Chief Secretary of the Tibetan Local Government; Rampa Namgyal Wangdu, Head of the Tibetan Visiting Group; and Deputy Heads Heba Dun, Lajiali Langji Jiacuo, Ouxie Tudeng Sangque, Sende Banqin, the Red Sect Living Buddha Mozhulin, the Flower Sect Living Buddha Sakya Dongse, and Lobsang Tudan, Acting Qoi-bon Khenpo of the Panchen Kampus Assembly Hall.

    During the reception, Sokang Wangqing Gelei and the other nine presented Hada scarves to Chairman Mao Zedong as a token of respect.

    Present at the reception were Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Deng Xiaoping and Ulanhu, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Weihan, and Zhang Jingwu, Representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet.

    During the reception, Chairman Mao had cordial talks with Sokang Wangqing Gelei and others.

### 毛主席接见西藏地方政府官员

1954-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见西藏地方政府官员
    【新华社二十五日讯】中华人民共和国毛泽东主席二十五日下午在北京中南海勤政殿接见西藏地方政府噶伦索康·旺清格来。同时被接见的，还有西藏地方政府代理机巧堪布洛桑三旦，西藏参观团团长然巴·囊吉旺堆、副团长何巴敦、拉加利·朗吉嘉措、欧协·土登桑却、森德班官，红教活佛墨竹林，花教活佛萨迦东色，班禅堪布会议厅代理却本堪布洛桑图丹等人。
    接见时，索康·旺清格来等十人分别向毛泽东主席献哈达致敬。
    接见时在座的，有国务院总理周恩来，副总理邓小平、乌兰夫，秘书长习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉，中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武。
    接见时，毛主席同索康·旺清格来等人作了亲切的谈话。
