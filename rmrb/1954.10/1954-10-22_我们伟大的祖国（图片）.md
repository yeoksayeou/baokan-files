Our Great Motherland
In the vast pastoral areas of Xinjiang, our country has correctly implemented various policies to protect and develop animal husbandry production, which has gradually developed the economy of the pastoral areas. This is a herdsman living in the Kizilsu Kirghiz Autonomous Prefecture of the Pamir Plateau in Xinjiang.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国新疆广大的牧区，正确执行了保护和发展牧业生产的各项政策，使得牧区的经济逐步发展起来。这是生活在新疆帕米尔高原克兹勒苏柯尔克兹族自治区的牧民。
