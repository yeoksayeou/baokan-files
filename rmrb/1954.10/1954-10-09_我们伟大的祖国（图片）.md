Our Great Motherland
　　In Jianyang County, Sichuan Province, a bumper harvest of cotton has been achieved on more than 528,000 mu of land, with an estimated increase of 15 to 20 percent compared to last year. Members of the Cotton Harvest Agricultural Production Cooperative in the Fourth District of Jianyang County are seen here picking cotton.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    四川省简阳县五十二万八千多亩棉花普遍丰收，估计可比去年增产百分之十五到二十。简阳县四区棉丰农业生产合作社的社员们正在这里采摘棉花。
