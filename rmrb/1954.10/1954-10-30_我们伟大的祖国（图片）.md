    Our Great Motherland
    In the Hujiazhai Agricultural Production Cooperative in Linxia County, Gansu Province, the members include four ethnic groups: Hui, Han, Dongxiang, and Baoan. They harvested a bumper crop of potatoes this year. The members are using new-style walking plows to dig up potatoes here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    甘肃省临夏县姬家寨农业生产合作社，社员包括回、汉、东乡、保安四个民族成分。他们今年种植的马铃薯获得了丰收。社员们在这里正使用新式步犁挖掘马铃薯。
