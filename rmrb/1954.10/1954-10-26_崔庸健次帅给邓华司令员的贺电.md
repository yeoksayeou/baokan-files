Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Vice Marshal Choe Yong-gon to Commander Deng Hua
Author: Cui Yongjian
Date: 1954-10-26
Page: 1

    Telegram of Congratulations from Vice Marshal Choe Yong-gon to Commander Deng Hua
    To General Deng Hua, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army:
    On the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's participation in the Korean War, I, on behalf of all the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army and in my own name, extend warm congratulations to you and all the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and wish you even more brilliant achievements in your future endeavors.
      Choe Yong-gon, Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
              October 24, 1954, in Pyongyang

### 崔庸健次帅给邓华司令员的贺电
崔庸健
1954-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    崔庸健次帅给邓华司令员的贺电
    中国人民志愿军司令员邓华将军：
    值此中国人民志愿军入朝作战四周年之际，我谨以朝鲜人民军全体将士和我个人的名义，向您和中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员致以热烈的祝贺，并预祝您在今后的事业中取得更加光辉的成果。
      朝鲜民主主义人民共和国
      内阁副首相兼民族保卫相次帅　崔庸健
              一九五四年十月二十四日于平壤
