Our Great Motherland
Tens of thousands of women in New China are diligently learning technology. The 51 female apprentices trained by the Shanghai Machine Tool Factory have mastered the operation of automatic lathes in a very short period. Chen Tuzhen, a female apprentice in the standard parts workshop, is already able to operate the lathe independently.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    成千上万的新中国妇女正在努力学习技术。上海机床厂培养的五十一名女徒工，在很短的时期内就能掌握自动化车床的操作。这位标准件车间女徒工陈兔珍已经能够在车床上独立操作了。
