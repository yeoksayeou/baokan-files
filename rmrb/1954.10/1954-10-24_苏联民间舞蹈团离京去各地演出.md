Title: Soviet Folk Dance Troupe Leaves Beijing for Performances in Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1954-10-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Soviet National Folk Dance Troupe left Beijing on the 23rd to visit and perform in major cities such as Tianjin, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Shenyang, Anshan, and Lüda. Wu Xiaobang, Chairman of the Chinese Dance Art Research Association, and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, are accompanying the dance troupe. Many dancers, musicians, and actors from the capital went to the station to see them off.

### 苏联民间舞蹈团离京去各地演出

1954-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联民间舞蹈团离京去各地演出
    据新华社讯：苏联国立民间舞蹈团二十三日离京前往天津、上海、杭州、广州、武汉、沈阳、鞍山、旅大等各大城市作访问演出。陪同舞蹈团赴各地的，有中国舞蹈艺术研究会主席吴晓邦和中苏友好协会总会副总干事李沾吾。前往车站送行的，有首都的舞蹈家、音乐家和演员多人。
