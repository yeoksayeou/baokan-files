Title: Japanese Petroleum Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 18th. The Japanese Chemical and Petroleum Workers' Union delegation of five people, led by Asano Shozo, chairman of the Japan National Petroleum Industry Workers' Union Federation, Rameshwar, general secretary of the All-India Petroleum Workers' Union, Situmiong, first secretary of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Petroleum Workers' Union Federation, and Yusuf, the person in charge of the South Sumatra Petroleum Union, all arrived in Beijing on the 18th, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to visit and tour our country.

### 日本石油工会代表团等到京

1954-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    日本石油工会代表团等到京
      【新华社十八日讯】应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问参观的以日本全国石油产业工会联合会主席浅野正三为首的日本化学石油工会代表团五人、印度全印石油工人联合会总书记拉麦什瓦尔、印度尼西亚石油工人工会联合会中央委员会第一书记西图米昂、苏门答腊南部石油工会负责人优素福，都在十八日到达北京。
