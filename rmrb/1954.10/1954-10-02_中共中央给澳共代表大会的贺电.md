Title: CCP Central Committee's Congratulatory Message to the Australian Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-10-02
Page: 2

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Australia, Dear Comrade Sharkey and comrades of the 17th Congress of the Communist Party of Australia:

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm, fraternal congratulations to the 17th Congress of the Communist Party of Australia. We congratulate you on achieving further successes in defending world peace, striving for democratic freedom, and struggling to improve the lives of the working people. We wish the congress success.

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 中共中央给澳共代表大会的贺电

1954-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    中共中央给澳共代表大会的贺电澳大利亚共产党中央委员会亲爱的夏基同志并澳大利亚共产党第十七次代表大会代表同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向澳大利亚共产党第十七次代表大会致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。祝贺你们在保卫世界和平、争取民主自由和在为改善劳动人民生活的斗争中获得更进一步的成就。敬祝大会的成功。
    中国共产党中央委员会
