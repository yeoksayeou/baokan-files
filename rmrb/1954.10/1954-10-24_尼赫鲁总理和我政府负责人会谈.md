Title: Premier Nehru Holds Talks with Leaders of My Government
Author:
Date: 1954-10-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: At the invitation of the Chinese government, Indian Prime Minister Nehru, after arriving in Beijing, held talks with leaders of our government from October 19th to 23rd. Prime Minister Nehru held talks with our Premier Zhou Enlai on the evenings of the 19th, the afternoon of the 20th, and the afternoon of the 21st. On the morning of the 22nd, he held talks with our Vice Premier Chen Yun and Vice Premier Li Fuchun. At noon on the 22nd, he held talks with our Minister of Water Resources, Fu Zuoyi. On the afternoon of the 22nd, he held talks with our Vice Premier Deng Zihui. On the afternoon of the 23rd, he held talks with Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture.

### 尼赫鲁总理和我政府负责人会谈

1954-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理和我政府负责人会谈
    【新华社二十三日讯】应中国政府邀请访问我国的印度共和国总理尼赫鲁，在到达北京以后，自十月十九日至二十三日，曾先后同我国政府负责人进行了会谈。尼赫鲁总理在十九日晚上、二十日下午和二十一日下午，同我国周恩来总理进行了会谈；在二十二日上午，同我国陈云副总理、李富春副总理进行了会谈；在二十二日中午，同我国水利部傅作义部长进行了会谈；在二十二日下午，同我国邓子恢副总理进行了会谈；在二十三日下午，同中国科学院郭沫若院长和文化部沈雁冰部长进行了会谈。
