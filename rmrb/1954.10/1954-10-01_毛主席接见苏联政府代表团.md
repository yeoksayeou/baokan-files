Title: Chairman Mao Receives the Soviet Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-10-01
Page: 1
    
Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, at 3 p.m. on the 30th, received the Head of the Soviet Government Delegation, invited to our country to participate in the 5th Anniversary National Day celebrations, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, N.S. Khrushchev; delegation members: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union N.A. Bulganin, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union A.I. Mikoyan, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions N.M. Shvernik, Minister of Culture of the Soviet Union G.F. Alexandrov, Editor-in-Chief of "Pravda" D.T. Shepilov, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union Ye.A. Furtseva, Minister of the Building Materials Industry of the Uzbek Republic Ye.S. Nasriddinova, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China Yudin, and Responsible Staff Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union V.P. Stepanov.

    Present at the reception were Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Dong Biwu, Lin Boqu, Peng Dehuai, Peng Zhen, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, and Li Fuchun.

### 毛主席接见苏联政府代表团

1954-10-01
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见苏联政府代表团
    【新华社三十日讯】中华人民共和国毛泽东主席，在三十日下午三时接见了应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的苏联政府代表团团长、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫，团员：苏联部长会议第一副主席尼·亚·布尔加宁，苏联部长会议副主席阿·伊·米高扬，全苏工会中央理事会主席尼·米·什维尔尼克，苏联文化部部长格·费·亚历山德罗夫，“真理报”总编辑德·特·谢皮洛夫，苏联共产党莫斯科市委员会书记叶·阿·福尔采娃，乌兹别克共和国建筑材料工业部部长叶·斯·纳斯里金诺]娃，苏联驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使尤金，苏联共产党中央委员会负责工作人员维·普·斯捷潘诺夫。
    接见时在座的，有朱德、刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、董必武、林伯渠、彭德怀、彭真、邓小平、邓子恢、李富春。
