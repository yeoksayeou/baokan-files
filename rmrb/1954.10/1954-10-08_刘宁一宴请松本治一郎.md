Title: Liu Ningyi Hosts a Banquet for Matsumoto Jiichiro
Author:
Date: 1954-10-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, hosted a banquet on the 6th for Mr. Matsumoto Jiichiro, Vice-Chairman of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee and Chairman of the Japan Peace Liaison Council.
    Those attending the banquet included: Kameda Togo (Japan), and Manuel Ri (Chile), Deputy Secretaries-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and others.
    Also attending the banquet were: Chen Shutong and Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairmen of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Committee Member Li Dequan, Secretary-General Liu Guanyi and others.

### 刘宁一宴请松本治一郎

1954-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    刘宁一宴请松本治一郎
    【新华社七日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书长刘宁一于六日宴请亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副主席、日本和平联络会主席松本治一郎先生。
    参加宴会的有：亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍（日本）、万徒勒里（智利）等。
    参加宴会的还有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通、廖承志，委员李德全，秘书长刘贯一等。
