Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru Bids Farewell to Chairman Mao and Other Chinese National Leaders Before Leaving Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 26th: Indian Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru, who was invited by the Chinese government to visit our country, bid farewell to Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Shaoqi, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Soong Ching Ling, and Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yun on the afternoon of October 26th, before concluding his visit to Beijing and leaving our country. He was accompanied by his daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, and the Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, N.R. Pillai. They held talks for about one and a half hours.

Those present at the farewell were the Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and the Chinese Ambassador to India, Yuan Zhongxian.

### 印度总理尼赫鲁在离开我国的前夕向毛主席为首的我国国家领导人员辞行

1954-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁在离开我国的前夕
    向毛主席为首的我国国家领导人员辞行
    【新华社二十六日讯】应中国政府邀请前来我国访问的印度共和国总理尼赫鲁，在即将结束北京的访问、离开我国的前夕——十月二十六日下午，偕同他的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人和印度外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱，向中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄和国务院副总理陈云辞行，并会谈约一小时半。
    辞行时，在座的，有印度驻我国大使赖嘉文和我国驻印度大使袁仲贤。
