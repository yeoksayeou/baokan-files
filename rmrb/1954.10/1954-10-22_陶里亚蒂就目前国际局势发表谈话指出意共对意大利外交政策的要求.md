Title: Togliatti Speaks on the Current International Situation, Points Out the Italian Communist Party's Demands on Italian Foreign Policy
Author:
Date: 1954-10-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rome, October 20th (TASS dispatch): The Italian newspaper "Unità" published a statement by Palmiro Togliatti, General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, on the current international situation. Togliatti described the Italian Communist Party's demands on Italian foreign policy as follows:

　　1. We demand that the government take a firm stand and reject the policy of imperialism. Because the aim of the imperialist policy is: to divide the world into two parts while discriminating against certain countries, governments and parties, to push the world into increasingly serious and complex conflicts, and to push the world towards war.

　　2. We demand that a policy be openly adopted and declared that guarantees peaceful coexistence for all countries, opposes the establishment of any aggressive blocs, and advocates the settlement of all disputes through negotiation.

　　3. To this end, we demand de facto and de jure recognition of the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic, abandonment of all discriminatory and restrictive measures, and development of economic and cultural exchanges with all countries, especially with Eastern European and Asian countries.

　　4. On the European issue, we demand that: before any unilateral decisions are made on the German issue, especially before agreeing to arm West Germany in any form, major power negotiations should be resumed first to stop the arming of Germany and to reach a consensus on the issue of German unity and sovereignty.

　　We demand that the Italian government no longer submit to American policy, but take the initiative to take independent, open and clear actions to support a policy of easing tension and maintaining peace.

### 陶里亚蒂就目前国际局势发表谈话指出意共对意大利外交政策的要求

1954-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    陶里亚蒂就目前国际局势发表谈话
    指出意共对意大利外交政策的要求
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社罗马二十日讯：意大利“团结报”登载意大利共产党总书记陶里亚蒂对目前国际局势所发表的谈话。陶里亚蒂叙述了意大利共产党对意大利外交政策的要求如下：
    一、我们要求政府采取坚决的态度拒绝帝国主义的政策。因为帝国主义政策的目的是：在歧视某些国家、政府和党派的情况下把世界分裂成两部分，把世界推向越来越严重、越来越复杂的冲突中，把世界推向战争。
    二、我们要求公开采取和宣布执行这样的政策：保证所有国家和平共处，反对建立任何侵略性集团，主张以协商方式解决所有争执问题。
    三、为此，我们要求在事实上和法律上承认中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国，放弃一切歧视性质和限制性质的措施，以同所有国家、首先是同东欧和亚洲国家发展经济和文化交流。
    四、在欧洲问题方面，我们要求：在对德国问题作出任何片面决定之前，主要是在同意以任何形式武装西德之前，应当首先恢复大国谈判，以制止武装德国和就德国人民的统一和主权问题达成一致的协议。
    我们要求意大利政府不再屈从美国的政策，而要主动采取独立自主的、公开的和明确的行动来支持缓和紧张局势和维护和平的政策。
