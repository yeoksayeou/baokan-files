Title: Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Holds Film Reception
Author:
Date: 1954-10-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

    The Chinese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Mongolian People's Republic, He Ying, held a film reception on the evening of the 29th at the Mongolian Government House to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the founding of our country. The film "The Butterfly Lovers" was shown and was warmly welcomed by the guests.
    Among those invited to attend the reception were: Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic; First Deputy Prime Minister Sürünjavyn Shirendev; Deputy Prime Ministers Luvsan, Maydar, and Tsend; Dambadarjaa, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; Secretaries Dugersuren and Ramsuren; Zedeeb, Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic; Sharap, Secretary of the Presidium, and all members of the Presidium; and Ministers of various ministries such as the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministry of Internal Affairs, Ministry of Animal Husbandry, Ministry of Industry, Ministry of Trade, Ministry of Finance, Ministry of Transportation, Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, Ministry of Health, and Ministry of Food Industry, as well as heads of cultural, scientific, artistic circles, and mass organizations, totaling approximately 200 people.
    Soviet Ambassador to Mongolia, Pissarev, Korean Ambassador to Mongolia, Hong Dong-chul, and personnel from the embassies of these two countries, as well as Soviet experts, were also invited to attend the reception. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 我国驻蒙古大使举行电影招待会

1954-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    我国驻蒙古大使举行电影招待会
    我国驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使何英二十九日晚在蒙古政府大厦举行电影招待会，庆祝我国建国五周年。放映的影片是“梁山伯与祝英台”，受到来宾的热烈欢迎。
    应邀出席招待会的有：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理鲁布桑、迈达尔、曾德，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴，书记杜格苏伦、拉姆苏伦，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席泽狄布，主席团秘书沙拉普和主席团全体委员，外交部、内务部、畜牧部、工业部、贸易部、财政部、运输部、邮电部、卫生部、食品工业部等各部部长，文化、科学、艺术各界和群众团体负责人等共约二百余人。
    苏联驻蒙古大使皮萨列夫、朝鲜驻蒙古大使洪东喆和这两国的使馆人员以及苏联专家等也都应邀出席了招待会。　（新华社）
