Title: Accepting Machines and Equipment Donated by the Soviet Union: Our Country Will Establish a Large Farm East of Jiamusi
Author:
Date: 1954-10-23
Page: 1

    Accepting Machines and Equipment Donated by the Soviet Union
    Our Country Will Establish a Large Farm East of Jiamusi
    [Reported by this newspaper] A large grain farm with a planting area of 20,000 hectares, organized with machines and equipment donated by the Soviet people, has been confirmed to be built in the area east of Jiamusi, Heilongjiang Province.
    A site survey working group dispatched by the Ministry of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China has arrived in the wilderness area to begin surveying. The survey group departed from Beijing on the 13th. The group includes Wei Zhenwu, Director of the Northeast State Farm Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Shengsan, Deputy Director, Wang Caoli, Director of the Heilongjiang Provincial People's Government Agricultural Department, as well as experts and staff. Accompanying this survey group are four Soviet experts from the Ministry of Agriculture, the Ministry of Water Resources, and the Ministry of Construction Engineering: Stepanenko, a state farm management expert, Trusov, a mechanical engineer, Shabayev, a water conservancy expert, and Sviridov, a construction engineer. Management cadres, surveyors, and basic construction designers for the farm are also being actively assigned, and construction work will begin soon.
    The preliminary plan for this large grain farm includes five branch farms and twenty production teams. There is also a lot of undeveloped wasteland around it, which can be used for the further development of the farm.

### 接受苏联赠送的机器和设备  我国将在佳木斯东建立大型农场

1954-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    接受苏联赠送的机器和设备
    我国将在佳木斯东建立大型农场
    【本报讯】我国接受苏联人民赠送的机器和设备来组织的一个拥有两万公顷播种面积的大型谷物农场，已确定在黑龙江省佳木斯以东地区建场。
    中华人民共和国农业部派出勘查场址的工作小组，已经到达荒区开始勘查工作。勘查小组是在十三日从北京出发的。参加勘查小组的，有中华人民共和国农业部东北国营农场管理局局长魏震五、副局长张省三，黑龙江省人民政府农业厅厅长王操犁和专家及工作人员多人。和这个勘查小组一同前往的，还有农业部、水利部和建筑工程部的四位苏联专家，他们是：国营农场管理专家斯捷潘年柯、机务专家特鲁索夫、水利专家沙巴耶夫、建筑工程专家斯维里多夫。农场的管理干部、勘测人员和基本建设的设计人员也在积极调配中，建场工作即将开始。
    这个大型谷物农场初步计划将有五个分场，二十个生产队。周围并有很多未开垦的生荒地，可供农场进一步发展。
