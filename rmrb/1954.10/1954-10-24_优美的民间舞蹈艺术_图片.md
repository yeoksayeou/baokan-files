    Beautiful Folk Dance Art
    —The Soviet National Folk Dance Ensemble Performs in Beijing

    Ukrainian Suite.
    Chinese Classical Mime—"San Cha Kou" (The Crossroads Inn).
    The Soviet National Folk Dance Ensemble has performed many beautiful folk dances with various national styles in Beijing. This is a scene from the Russian Suite. This dance demonstrates the diverse forms of Russian folk dance.
    Photographers for this issue: Zhu Bo, Yuan Yiping, Wang Zhiyuan of this newspaper.
    "Guerrillas"—Dance Drama. In this dance, the actors created great images of Soviet guerrillas operating in the Caucasus region.
    Moldavian "Zhuk Dance."
    Each performance by the Soviet National Folk Dance Ensemble has been warmly welcomed by the people of the capital. After each performance, the people of the capital presented flowers to the dance ensemble and its artistic director, Igor Moiseyev, to express their respect.
    The Belorussian "Potato Dance" is created based on the theme and melody of the Belorussian folk song "Potato Dance." This dance fully expresses the labor life of the Belorussian working people in planting and harvesting potatoes.
    Chinese Dance "Large Waist Drum."
    Kazan Tatar Suite.
    Korean "Woodcutter and Village Girl" Dance.
    Russian "Clearing in the Woods" Dance.
    In the open-air theater of the Soviet Exhibition Center, thousands of spectators watched the dance art of the Soviet National Folk Dance Ensemble with great interest.

### 优美的民间舞蹈艺术(图片)

1954-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    优美的民间舞蹈艺术
    ——苏联国立民间舞蹈团在北京演出
    乌克兰组舞。
    中国古典哑剧——“三岔口”。
    苏联国立民间舞蹈团，在北京演出了许多具有各种不同民族风格的优美的民间舞蹈。这是俄罗斯组舞的一个场面。这个舞蹈表现了俄罗斯民族舞蹈的多种多样的形式。
    这一期照片摄影者：本报朱　波　袁毅平　王志渊“游击队员”——舞蹈剧。演员们在这个舞蹈中创造出了在高加索地区活动的苏维埃游击队员的伟大形象。
    莫尔达维亚“茹克舞”。
    苏联国立民间舞蹈团的每次演出都受到首都人民的热烈欢迎。每次演出完毕，首都人民向舞蹈团和该团艺术领导人伊·莫伊塞耶夫献花致敬。
    别洛露西亚“马铃薯”舞是根据别洛露西亚民歌“马铃薯舞”的题材和旋律而创造的。这个舞蹈充分表达了别洛露西亚劳动人民栽种和收割马铃薯的劳动生活。
    中国舞蹈“大腰鼓”。
    喀山鞑靼人组舞。朝鲜“樵夫与村女”舞。
    俄罗斯“林中空地”舞。
    在苏联展览馆的露天剧场内，千百个观众怀着极大的兴趣来欣赏苏联国立民间舞蹈团的舞蹈艺术。
