Title: Grand Rally Held in Ulaanbaatar to Celebrate China's National Day and the Opening of "Mongolia-China Friendship Days"
Author:
Date: 1954-10-02
Page: 3

Ulaanbaatar dispatch: To celebrate the 5th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the beginning of "Mongolia-China Friendship Days," the Mongolian people held a celebration meeting in the capital city of Ulaanbaatar at 8 p.m. on September 30th.

Present at the meeting were: Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic; Dashiin Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; J. Zhadamba, Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural; D. Dugersuren and B. Lhamsuren, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; Sonomyn Sodnomdarjaa, First Deputy Prime Minister; Batyn Dorj, Vice Prime Ministers Sonomyn Luvsan, Damdinjavyn Maidar, and Demchigiin Molom; leading staff members of various ministries; heads of people's organizations; and people from all walks of life, totaling more than 1,000 people.

Also present were: He Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia; P.V. Pisarov, Soviet Ambassador to Mongolia; Hong Dong-cheol, Korean Ambassador to Mongolia; and personnel from various embassies.

Cao Yu, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, and all members of the delegation and acrobatics troupe were also invited to attend.

The meeting was presided over by Damdin, Secretary of the Ulaanbaatar Municipal Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party. Sodnomdarjaa delivered a report at the celebration meeting, in which he detailed the great achievements made by the Chinese people in various fields over the past five years. He particularly mentioned the increase in China's international status in recent years, saying: Now, without the participation of the People's Republic of China, no major international issue can be resolved. Finally, he cited many historical facts to illustrate the deep friendship that has long existed between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples, and pointed out that this friendship is developing and consolidating.

He Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, and Cao Yu, the head of the Chinese Art Troupe, also spoke at the meeting. They both emphasized the traditional friendly relations between the Chinese and Mongolian peoples, and pointed out that through the various activities of this "Mongolia-China Friendship Days," this friendly relationship will be further promoted.

The Mongolian National Theatre also performed wonderful song and dance programs at the meeting. The meeting concluded with warm applause. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 乌兰巴托举行盛大集会  庆祝我国国庆和“蒙中友好旬”开幕

1954-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    乌兰巴托举行盛大集会
    庆祝我国国庆和“蒙中友好旬”开幕
    乌兰巴托消息：蒙古人民为庆祝中华人民共和国成立五周年国庆和“蒙中友好旬”开始，于九月三十日下午八时在首都乌兰巴托市举行庆祝大会。
    到会的有：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴，大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席泽狄布，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记杜格苏伦、拉姆苏伦，第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理鲁布桑、迈达尔、曾德，各部领导工作人员，人民团体负责人和各界人士共一千多人。
    到会的还有：中国驻蒙古大使何英，苏联驻蒙古大使皮萨列夫，朝鲜驻蒙古大使洪东喆及各国使馆人员。
    中国文艺代表团团长曹禺和代表团、杂技团全体团员也应邀参加。
    大会由蒙古人民革命党乌兰巴托市党委会书记达姆金主持。锡林迪布在庆祝大会上做了报告，他详细地介绍了中国人民五年来在各方面所取得的伟大成就。他特别提到近几年来中国国际地位的提高，他说：现在如果没有中华人民共和国的参加，任何重大国际问题都无法获得解决。最后，他列举了许多历史事实，说明蒙中两国人民间早就存在着深厚的友谊，并指出这一友谊正在发展和巩固。
    中国驻蒙古大使何英和中国文艺代表团团长曹禺也在会上讲了话。他们都强调指出中蒙两国人民间传统的友好关系，并且指出经过这次“蒙中友好旬”的各项活动，必将进一步促进这种友好关系。
    会上并由蒙古国家剧院演出了精彩歌舞节目。大会最后在热烈掌声中结束。　（新华社）
