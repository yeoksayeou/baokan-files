Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Eight Visiting Delegations to China Separately
Author: 
Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, after the National Day, separately received eight delegations from Indonesia, Burma, Chile, Pakistan, and India who came to China to attend the National Day celebrations and for visits and tours.

    On the 4th of this month, he received the Indonesian delegation to China headed by Sumannan and the Indonesian women's delegation headed by Mrs. Puzobontoro. The Indonesian Ambassador to China, Mononutu, was also present at the reception.

    On the 5th, he received the Trade Union Congress (Burma) delegation headed by Wu La Shi and Mrs. Wu La Shi. The Burmese Ambassador to China, Wu La Mao, was also present at the reception.

    On the same day, he received the Chilean delegation to China headed by Salvador Allende.

    On the 7th, he received the Pakistani women's delegation headed by Mrs. Shah Nawaz. The Pakistani Ambassador to China, Roza, and his wife were also present at the reception.

    On the 8th, he received the delegation of the India-China Friendship Association headed by Mrs. Uma Nehru, a member of the Indian Parliament, the Indian women's delegation headed by Ammu Swaminathan, and the friendly delegation of the All-India Trade Union Congress headed by K.P. Tripathi, General Secretary of the All-India Trade Union Congress. The Indian Ambassador to China, Raghavan, was also present at the reception.

### 周总理分别接见访问我国的八个代表团

1954-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理分别接见访问我国的八个代表团
    【新华社十八日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在国庆节后，曾分别接见印度尼西亚、缅甸、智利、巴基斯坦、印度五个国家来我国参加国庆观礼和访问参观的八个代表团。
    本月四日接见了以苏玛南为首的印度尼西亚访华代表团，以普佐邦托罗夫人为首的印度尼西亚妇女代表团。接见时，印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图也在座。
    五日接见了以吴拉实为首的工会大会（缅甸）代表团和吴拉实夫人。接见时，缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂也在座。
    同日接见了以萨尔瓦多·阿伦德为首的智利访华代表团。
    七日接见了以沙阿·纳瓦兹夫人为首的巴基斯坦妇女代表团。接见时，巴基斯坦驻华大使罗查和大使夫人也在座。
    八日接见了以印度国会议员乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人为首的印中友好协会访华代表团，以安穆·斯瓦米纳坦为首的印度妇女代表团，以印度全国工会大会总书记克·普·特里帕蒂为首的印度全国工会大会友好代表团。接见时，印度驻华大使赖嘉文也在座。
