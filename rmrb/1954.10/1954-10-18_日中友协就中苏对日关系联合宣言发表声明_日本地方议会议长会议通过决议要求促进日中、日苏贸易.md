Title: Sino-Japanese Friendship Association Issues Statement on the Joint Declaration of China and the Soviet Union on Relations with Japan; Conference of Japanese Prefectural Assembly Speakers Passes Resolution Calling for Promoting Sino-Japanese and Soviet-Japanese Trade
Author:
Date: 1954-10-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 17th dispatch: Tokyo News: The Japan-China Friendship Association issued a statement on the 16th regarding the joint declaration by the Chinese and Soviet governments on relations with Japan, stating:

	In order to restore diplomatic relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union as soon as possible, the two governments should consult on each issue and actively promote exchanges in politics, economics, culture, and other fields. The Japanese government should take concrete measures to actively open up the path for normalizing diplomatic relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union.
	Xinhua News Agency, 16th dispatch: Tokyo News: The speakers of the assemblies of all prefectures, Hokkaido, and Osaka, Kyoto, and other prefectures in Japan held a meeting in Kobe City on the 13th and 14th. The meeting passed a resolution calling for promoting Sino-Japanese and Soviet-Japanese trade and banning the use of atomic and hydrogen bombs.

	The meeting also decided to request the government to take immediate measures to implement these resolutions.

### 日中友协就中苏对日关系联合宣言发表声明  日本地方议会议长会议通过决议要求促进日中、日苏贸易

1954-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友协就中苏对日关系联合宣言发表声明
    日本地方议会议长会议通过决议要求促进日中、日苏贸易
    【新华社十七日讯】东京消息：日中友好协会十六日就中苏两国政府关于对日本关系的联合宣言发表声明说：
    为了及早恢复日中、日苏之间的邦交，双方政府需就每个问题进行协商，积极促进政治、经济、文化等方面的交流。日本政府应该采取具体措施，积极开辟使日中、日苏邦交正常化的道路。
    【新华社十六日讯】东京消息：日本全国都、道、府、县议会的议长，于十三、十四两日在神户市举行会议。会议通过决议，要求促进日中、日苏贸易和禁止使用原子弹、氢弹。
    会议并决定要求政府立即采取措施，实现这些决议。
