Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author: 
Date: 1954-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 30th: Indian Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru, who visited our country at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, concluded his visit and departed from Guangzhou by special plane at 7:15 AM on the 30th to return to his country. Also departing from Guangzhou were Prime Minister Nehru's daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, the Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, N.R. Pillai, the Deputy Secretary of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, Bahadur Singh, and nine others including M.L. Gainder, K.F. Rustamji, and N.K. Seshan.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Tao Zhu, Acting Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office, Zhu Guang and Qiu Zhe, Deputy Mayors of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, and heads of the Guangdong Province and Guangzhou Municipal Government organs.

Also present at the airport were Wen Niansheng, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Central-South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Liang Biye, Deputy Director of the Political Department, and senior generals of the Army, Navy, and Air Force stationed in Guangdong.

Also present at the airport were Zhao Ziyang, Deputy Secretary of the South China Sub-Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Li Xinqing, Secretary-General, representatives of local organizations of various democratic parties in South China, heads of people's organizations in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, as well as staff members of government organs, and representatives from scientific, educational, literary, artistic, journalistic, and religious circles.

Also present at the airport to see them off was Soviet Consul in Guangzhou, Koluggin.

Also present at the airport were Mrs. Chand, a member of the Indian women's delegation currently in Guangzhou, and Indian nationals residing in Guangzhou.

Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yuan Zhongxian, Chinese Ambassador to India, and R.K. Nehru, Indian Ambassador to China, who accompanied Prime Minister Nehru from Beijing to Guangzhou, were also present at the airport to see them off.

More than 20,000 people went to the airport to bid farewell.

At the airport, the welcoming crowd held high banners with slogans such as "Farewell Prime Minister Nehru," "Long Live Sino-Indian Friendship," and thousands of bright red flags.

As Prime Minister Nehru and his entourage drove through the long lines of the crowd, cheers and applause resounded continuously. Prime Minister Nehru waved to the crowd on both sides of the car.

The Indian and Chinese national flags were hung at the airport. After Prime Minister Nehru entered the airport, the band played the Indian and Chinese national anthems.

Prime Minister Nehru reviewed the guard of honor and received flowers from children in Guangzhou. Then, Prime Minister Nehru delivered a speech at the airport. He said: After ten days, I am now going back to India. To visit a great country, ten days is a very short time; but how do we calculate time? We do not only calculate time with clocks, but also with the experiences during this period. If so, then the time I spent here is not just ten days, but a much longer time. Because, I am taking back many experiences and feelings. I have seen this ancient country with a long history, I have seen this ancient country with a completely new look, and what I cannot forget especially is the faces of the young people here, the energetic, lively, and happy faces of the young men and women, boys and girls, are memories that I will never forget after I return. Your voices will echo in my ears. Thank you, the people of China, and I wish you all the best.

After Prime Minister Nehru boarded the plane, he stood at the hatch for a long time, waving goodbye to the crowd.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 30th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru, on his way back to India after visiting China, arrived in Hanoi at 9:45 AM (local time) on the 30th for a stopover of just over an hour before continuing to Saigon.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Vu Dinh Huynh, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Luo Guibo, Chinese Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Lavrishchev, Soviet Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ogrodzinski, Head of the Polish Delegation of the Vietnam International Supervisory and Control Commission, Lieutenant General Darga Ka, Indian Representative, and dozens of representatives of Indian nationals in Hanoi.

After Prime Minister Nehru disembarked, he reviewed the guard of honor and received flowers from children in Hanoi. Then, accompanied by Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong and Protocol Director Vu Dinh Huynh, he went to the Hanoi city area to meet with Indian officers and soldiers working in the Vietnam International Supervisory and Control Commission and visited the Hanoi city area.

At 11:00 AM, Prime Minister Nehru left Hanoi by plane for Saigon.

### 印度总理尼赫鲁离广州回国

1954-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁离广州回国
    【新华社广州三十日电】　应中华人民共和国政府邀请来我国访问的印度共和国总理尼赫鲁，已结束了在我国的访问，在三十日七时十五分乘专机离广州返国。同时飞离广州的还有尼赫鲁总理的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人和印度外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱、印度外交部副秘书巴哈杜尔·辛格以及墨·勒·盖恩德、克·弗·鲁斯滕吉、纳·克·塞善等九人。
    前往机场欢送的有广东省人民政府代主席陶铸，外事处处长曹若茗，广州市人民政府副市长朱光、丘哲，以及广东省、广州市政府机关的负责人。
    前往机场欢送的还有中国人民解放军中南军区、第四野战军副参谋长文年生、政治部副主任梁必业，以及驻广东的陆、海、空军部队的高级将领。
    前往机场欢送的还有：中共中央华南分局副书记赵紫阳，秘书长李心清，各民主党派华南地方组织代表，广东省和广州市人民团体的负责人，以及政府机关工作人员，科学、教育、文学、艺术、新闻、宗教界代表。
    到机场欢送的还有苏联驻广州领事柯六奇。
    到机场欢送的还有现在广州的印度妇女代表团团员昌德夫人和旅居广州的印度侨民。
    由北京陪送尼赫鲁总理到来广州的我国外交部办公厅主任王炳南、我国驻印度大使袁仲贤和印度驻我国大使赖嘉文也到机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的共有两万多人。
    在飞机场上，欢送群众高举着“欢送尼赫鲁总理”、“中印友好万岁”等横额标语和千百面灿烂的红旗。
    当尼赫鲁总理和陪同者乘坐汽车穿过漫长的群众行列时，欢呼和掌声不绝。尼赫鲁总理在车上不断向两边的群众挥手致意。
    机场上悬挂着印度和中国国旗。尼赫鲁总理进入机场后，乐队高奏印度和中国国歌。
    尼赫鲁总理检阅了仪仗队，接受了广州儿童的献花。接着尼赫鲁总理在机场上发表了谈话。他说：经过了十天之后，现在我要回印度去了。要访问一个伟大的国家，十天是一段非常短促的时间；但是，我们是怎样来计算时间的呢？我们不只是用钟表来计算时间，还要以在这一段期间内的经历来计算。如果这样的话，那么，我在这里度过的时光不止十天，而是长得多的时间。因为，我带回去的是许许多多的经验和感受。我已经看到了这个历史悠久的古国，我已经看到了这个面目焕然一新的古国，而使我特别不能忘怀的，是这里的青年人的面容，这里的青年男女、男孩子和女孩子们的这种生气勃勃的、活泼的、愉快的面容，是我回去以后特别不能忘怀的记忆，你们的声音将回绕在我的耳旁。感谢你们，中国的人民，谨祝你们好。
    尼赫鲁总理走上飞机后，长时间地站在舱口，向欢送的人群挥手告别。
    【新华社河内三十日电】　印度共和国总理尼赫鲁在访问中国后的返国途中，在三十日上午九时四十五分（当地时间）到达河内，停留一小时余，然后继续飞往西贡。
    到机场欢迎的有越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长范文同、外交部交际处处长武廷莹，中国驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波、苏联驻越南民主共和国大使拉夫里谢夫，越南国际监督和监察委员会波兰代表团团长奥格罗京斯基、印度代表达尔加卡中将，以及在河内的印度侨民代表等数十人。
    尼赫鲁总理下机后，检阅了仪仗队，并接受了河内儿童的献花。接着，在范文同副总理和交际处处长武廷莹陪同下，到河内市区同在越南国际监督和监察委员会工作的印度军官和士兵见面，并参观了河内市区。
    十一时正，尼赫鲁总理乘飞机离开河内前往西贡。
