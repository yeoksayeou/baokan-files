Title: Swedish Art History Professor Osvald Sirén Returns Home

Author:

Date: 1954-10-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 22nd: Swedish art history professor Osvald Sirén, who was invited by the Chinese Artists Association to visit China, arrived in Guangzhou on the 20th and left Guangzhou on the 21st to return to his country.

### 瑞典艺术史教授喜龙仁回国

1954-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典艺术史教授喜龙仁回国
    【新华社广州二十二日电】应中国美术家协会邀请来华访问的瑞典艺术史教授喜龙仁在二十日到达广州，已在二十一日离广州回国。
