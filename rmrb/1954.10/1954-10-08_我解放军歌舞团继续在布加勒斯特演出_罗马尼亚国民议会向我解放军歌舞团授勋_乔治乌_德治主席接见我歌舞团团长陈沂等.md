Title: Our PLA Song and Dance Troupe Continues Performances in Bucharest. Romanian National Assembly Awards Medals to Our PLA Song and Dance Troupe. Chairman Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej Receives Chen Yi, Head of Our Song and Dance Troupe, and Others.
Author:
Date: 1954-10-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest: After touring Romania, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe returned to Bucharest at the end of September to continue its performances in the Romanian capital. On September 30, the Song and Dance Troupe visited the "Mao Zedong" Steel Factory. Since the factory was named "Mao Zedong" at the end of last year, workers have enthusiastically embraced this honor with production achievements, increasing the factory's output by 20 percent. When the Song and Dance Troupe visited the workshops, they received a warm welcome from the workers. The workers repeatedly cheered for Mao Zedong's election as Chairman of the People's Republic of China, their cheers drowning out the roar of the machinery. After the visit, the Song and Dance Troupe held a small performance for the workers. The factory presented the Song and Dance Troupe with a model of a steam boiler made by the workers.

These days, the Song and Dance Troupe also participated in the Chinese Culture Festival held in Romania to celebrate China's National Day. On October 1, the Song and Dance Troupe performed for the working people of the Romanian capital at the "August 23rd" Culture and Sports Park in Bucharest.

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd: According to the Romanian News Agency, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, received Chen Yi, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, Deputy Head Dong Xiaowu, and Deputy Political Commissar Li Wei, and others on the 1st.

Present at the meeting were: First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, Iosif Chișinevschi, First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Metallurgy and Machine Industry, Gheorghe Stoica, and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Armed Forces, Emil Bodnăraș.

Wang Youping, Chinese Ambassador to Romania, accompanied Chen Yi and others on the visit.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 7th: Bucharest dispatch: The National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic held a medal-awarding ceremony in the National Assembly Hall on the 5th, awarding medals to the personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, in recognition of the great contribution the Song and Dance Troupe has made during its month-long visit and performance in Romania to promoting cultural exchange between Romania and China and enhancing the friendship and unity between the two peoples and their armies.

Attending the ceremony were Vice Chairmen of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, Sadoveanu and Mujik, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, Păvulescu, Minister of Culture, Crăciun, Secretary-General of the Presidium of the National Assembly, Bunuciu, First Deputy Minister of the Armed Forces, Lieutenant General Sălăjan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nicolae Cioroiu, First Deputy Minister of Culture, Constanța Crăciun, Vice Chairman of the Foreign Cultural Association, Szabo, and Director of the General Political Department of the Armed Forces, Major General Manescu, and others.

Wang Youping, Chinese Ambassador to Romania, and Tan Zhu, Deputy Military Attaché, were also invited to attend the ceremony.

At the medal-awarding ceremony, the National Assembly's order of award was read out. Then, Vice Chairman Sadoveanu awarded two First Class "Star of the Romanian People's Republic" Medals to the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe and the head of the troupe, Chen Yi, respectively. Then, Second, Third, Fourth, and Fifth Class "Star of the Romanian People's Republic" Medals were awarded to other leaders of the Song and Dance Troupe, famous actors of the Song and Dance Troupe, and all members of the Song and Dance Troupe, respectively. After the awarding of the medals, Sadoveanu gave a speech, congratulating all members of the Song and Dance Troupe.

Then, Chen Yi delivered a reply speech on behalf of the Song and Dance Troupe and all its members. He said: We are here today to accept this honor with the feeling that it is not only for the comrades of our Song and Dance Troupe, but also for our country, our people, and our army. He said: The Song and Dance Troupe will definitely bring this lofty honor back to the motherland and bring the love and friendship of the Romanian people and army for the Song and Dance Troupe to the Chinese people and army.

### 我解放军歌舞团继续在布加勒斯特演出  罗马尼亚国民议会向我解放军歌舞团授勋  乔治乌—德治主席接见我歌舞团团长陈沂等

1954-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我解放军歌舞团继续在布加勒斯特演出
    罗马尼亚国民议会向我解放军歌舞团授勋
    乔治乌—德治主席接见我歌舞团团长陈沂等
    据新华社布加勒斯特讯：中国人民解放军歌舞团在罗马尼亚各地巡回演出后，于九月底返回布加勒斯特，继续在罗马尼亚首都访问演出。歌舞团在九月三十日访问了“毛泽东”钢铁工厂。这个工厂从去年年底以“毛泽东”命名后，工人纷纷以生产成就接受这个光荣，工厂的生产因而提高了百分之二十。当歌舞团到各个车间参观时，受到了工人们的热情欢迎。工人们不断为毛泽东当选为中华人民共和国主席欢呼，欢呼声压倒了机器的轰鸣。歌舞团参观后，为工人们举行了小型演出会。工厂并把工人们制造的一个蒸汽锅模型赠送给歌舞团。
    这几天，歌舞团还参加了罗马尼亚为庆祝中国国庆而举行的中国文化节。十月一日，歌舞团在布加勒斯特的“八月二十三日”文化体育公园运动场为罗马尼亚首都的劳动人民举行演出。
    【新华社三日讯】据罗马尼亚通讯社消息：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席乔治乌—德治，一日接见了中国人民解放军歌舞团团长陈沂，副团长董小吾和副政治委员李伟等。
    接见时在座的有：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议第一副主席约·基辛涅夫斯基、部长会议第一副主席兼冶金和机器工业部部长基·斯托伊卡、部长会议副主席兼武装部队部部长埃·波德纳拉希。
    陪同陈沂等前往谒见的有中国驻罗马尼亚大使王幼平。
    【新华社布拉格七日电】布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会五日在国民议会大厅举行授勋典礼，以勋章授给中国人民解放军歌舞团人员，以表彰歌舞团在罗马尼亚访问演出一个月来对于促进罗中两国文化交流和增进两国人民及其军队之间的友谊和团结所作的巨大贡献。
    参加典礼的有罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团副主席萨多维亚努和穆日克，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会政治局委员帕伏列斯库，文化部部长克拉裘恩，国民议会主席团秘书长布纳邱，武装部队部第一副部长萨拉扬中将，外交部副部长尼古拉·丘罗，文化部第一副部长康·普里斯内亚，对外文化协会副主席萨博，以及武装部队部总政治部主任曼尼斯库少将等。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使王幼平、副武官谭竹也应邀出席了典礼。
    授勋典礼上宣读了国民议会的授勋命令。接着，萨多维亚努副主席把二枚一级“罗马尼亚人民共和国星”勋章分别授予中国人民解放军歌舞团和团长陈沂，然后又把二级、三级、四级、五级“罗马尼亚人民共和国星”勋章分别授予歌舞团其他领导者、歌舞团著名演员和歌舞团全体团员。授勋后，萨多维亚努讲话，向歌舞团全体团员祝贺。
    接着，陈沂代表歌舞团和全体团员致答词，他说：我们今天来接受这个荣誉，是带着这不只是给我们歌舞团同志，而且是给我们的国家、我们的人民和我们的军队的心情来接受的。他表示：歌舞团一定把这种崇高的荣誉带回祖国，把罗马尼亚人民和军队对歌舞团的热爱和友情带给中国人民和军队。
