Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Delegation of Artists from the German Democratic Republic, etc.; "One Year of New Direction in the German Democratic Republic" Photo Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th:

    Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, received the delegation of artists from the German Democratic Republic headed by Rudolf Engel, the delegation of visual artists from the German Democratic Republic, and the delegation of film workers from the German Democratic Republic headed by Inge Lökhoff on the morning of October 29th. These delegations were visiting China in accordance with the 1954 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Accompanying the reception were: Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations Hong Shen, Deputy Director Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture Chen Huangmei, Acting President of the Central Academy of Fine Arts Jiang Feng, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association Lü Ji, Manager of the China Film Distribution Company Luo Guangda, and film actress Shi Lianxing.
    Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, König, also attended the meeting.

    Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: The "One Year of New Direction in the German Democratic Republic" Photo Exhibition, sponsored by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, opened on October 29th at the Chunming Hall in Zhongshan Park, Beijing.
    In June 1953, the government of the German Democratic Republic, based on the new direction suggested by the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, adopted a series of political and economic measures. The main content of the new direction is to fundamentally improve the political and economic situation of the German Democratic Republic in order to greatly improve the living standards of the working class and all working people on this basis. At the same time, the purpose of the new direction is to strengthen the forces of peace and promote the restoration of German unity. The pictures in this exhibition provide a good explanation of the achievements of implementing this new direction over the past year.

### 陈毅副总理接见民主德国艺术家代表团等  德意志民主共和国新方针的一年图片展览会在北京开幕

1954-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见民主德国艺术家代表团等
    德意志民主共和国新方针的一年图片展览会在北京开幕
    【新华社二十九日讯】中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅二十九日上午接见了根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来华访问的以鲁道夫·恩格尔为首的德意志民主共和国艺术家代表团、德意志民主共和国造型艺术家代表团和以英格·洛克霍弗为首的德意志民主共和国电影工作者代表团。
    陪同接见的有：文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长伍修权，对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、副局长陈忠经，文化部电影局副局长陈荒煤，中央美术学院代理院长江丰，中国音乐家协会主席吕骥，中国电影发行公司经理罗光达，电影演员石联星。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使柯尼希也参加了这次会见。
    【新华社二十九日讯】对外文化联络事务局主办的德意志民主共和国新方针的一年图片展览会，十月二十九日在北京中山公园春明馆开幕。
    一九五三年六月，德意志民主共和国政府根据德国统一社会党所建议的新方针，采取了一系列的政治和经济的措施。新方针的主要内容是根本改善德意志民主共和国的政治、经济情况，以便在这个基础上大大地提高工人阶级和全体劳动人民的生活水平。同时，新方针的目的也是为着加强和平力量和促进德国恢复统一。这次展览的图片对一年来实行这一新方针的成就作了很好的说明。
