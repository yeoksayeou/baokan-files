Author:

Our people are extremely interested in the opening of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition. Yesterday, visitors came in an endless stream. A total of more than 80,000 people visited this exhibition, which shows the brilliant achievements of Soviet socialist and communist construction.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国人民对苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会的开幕感到极大的兴趣。昨日，观众络绎不绝，一共有八万多人参观了这个显示着苏联社会主义和共产主义建设辉煌成就的展览会。
