Title: Beijing Library and Others Hold Lectures Commemorating Lu Xun's Death
Author:
Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency:

    Beijing Library and the Chinese Writers Association jointly held a lecture on October 17th to commemorate the 18th anniversary of Lu Xun's death. Chen Yong, a researcher at the Institute of Literature of Peking University, spoke on "Some Issues Concerning Lu Xun's Literary Heritage." More than 1,500 people attended, including workers, literary and art workers, government cadres, soldiers, and students. After the meeting, students from Beijing Normal University recited Lu Xun's works "The Wise Man, the Fool, and the Slave" and "Kong Yiji".

### 北京图书馆等举办纪念鲁迅逝世的讲演会

1954-10-19
第3版()
专栏：

    北京图书馆等举办纪念鲁迅逝世的讲演会
    北京图书馆和中国作家协会在十月十七日联合举办纪念鲁迅逝世十八周年讲演会，由北京大学文学研究所研究员陈涌讲：“关于鲁迅文学遗产的一些问题”。到会有工人、文艺工作者、机关干部、军人和学生等一千五百多人。会后并有北京师范大学学生朗诵了鲁迅的作品“聪明人和傻子和奴才”、“孔乙己”。（新华社）
