Title: Zhou Zhuan Appointed as China's Ambassador to Bulgaria

Author: 
Date: 1954-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd—Zhou Zhuan was appointed as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Bulgaria, as approved by the 34th session of the Central People's Government Committee on September 9th.

The 34th session of the Central People's Government Committee approved the removal of Cao Xiangren from his position as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

### 周竹安被任命为我国驻保大使

1954-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周竹安被任命为我国驻保大使
    【新华社二十二日讯】九月九日中央人民政府委员会第三十四次会议通过任命周竹安为中华人民共和国驻保加利亚人民共和国特命全权大使。
    中央人民政府委员会第三十四次会议通过免去曹祥仁的中华人民共和国驻保加利亚人民共和国特命全权大使的职务。
