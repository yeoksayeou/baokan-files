Title: Chinese Embassy in India Holds Reception to Celebrate the Signing of the First Trade Agreement Between China and India
Author:
Date: 1954-10-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 18th. Shen Jian, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in India, held a reception at the Chinese Embassy on the evening of the 16th to celebrate the signing of the first Sino-Indian trade agreement and to welcome the Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China.
    Kong Yuan, head of the Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China, and Yang Lin, deputy head, as well as all members of the delegation attended the reception.
    Among those present at the reception were: Dr. Katju, Minister of the Interior of India, Kamarkar, Minister of Commerce, Manilal Chaturbhai Shah, Deputy Minister of Finance, Sardar Sujit Singh Majithia, Deputy Minister of Defense, Ra. Ku. Nehru, Foreign Secretary of the Ministry of External Affairs, Ayyangar, Secretary of the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, and officials from other ministries of the Indian government.
    Also present at the reception were Pannikar, a member of the States Reorganisation Commission and former Indian Ambassador to China, members of the Indian Parliament, prominent figures, industrialists, journalists, and overseas Chinese in Delhi.
    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to India were also invited to attend. Approximately four hundred guests attended.
    The reception proceeded in a sincere and friendly atmosphere.

### 我国驻印度大使馆举行招待会  庆祝中印两国签订第一个贸易协定

1954-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻印度大使馆举行招待会
    庆祝中印两国签订第一个贸易协定
    【新华社新德里十八日电】中华人民共和国驻印度大使馆临时代办申健十六日晚在中国大使馆举行招待会，庆祝第一个中印贸易协定的签订和欢迎中华人民共和国贸易代表团。
    中华人民共和国贸易代表团团长孔原和副团长杨琳以及全体团员出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的人士中有：印度内政部部长卡特朱博士、商业部部长卡马卡尔、财政部副部长马尼拉尔·查图巴伊·沙阿、国防部副部长萨达尔·苏吉特·辛格·马吉蒂亚、外交部外事秘书拉·库·尼赫鲁、工商部秘书艾扬格和印度政府其他各部的官员们。
    出席招待会的还有省区划分委员会委员、前印度驻中国大使潘尼迦，印度国会议员，著名人士，工商业家，新闻记者和德里的华侨。
    各国驻印度的外交使节也应邀出席。到会来宾共约四百人。
    招待会在诚挚友好的气氛中进行。
