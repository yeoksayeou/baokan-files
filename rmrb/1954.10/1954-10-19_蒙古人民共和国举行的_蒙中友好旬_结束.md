Title: "Mongolian People's Republic's 'Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Ten Days' Concludes"
Author:
Date: 1954-10-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 16th: The "Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Ten Days" held by the Mongolian People's Republic to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the founding of our country ended on October 10th. The Chinese Art Troupe and Acrobatic Troupe, which went to Mongolia to participate in the "Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Ten Days" and to conduct visits, left the Mongolian People's Republic on the 12th and 14th respectively and returned to Beijing.

During the "Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Ten Days," Party and government organs, people's organizations, and herdsmen from all over Mongolia enthusiastically participated in a series of activities to promote Mongolian-Chinese friendship. Propagandists and reporters from the Party organizations of Sukhbaatar and Middle Gobi aimags (provinces) held extensive seminars among herdsmen on the theme of "The Great Friendship Between the People of Mongolia and China." The National Theatre in Ulaanbaatar successfully performed the Chinese drama "Growing Up in Battle." Cinemas across the country continuously screened excellent Chinese films such as "Anshan Iron and Steel Works Under Construction," "Resist America, Aid Korea," "Taking Huashan by Strategy," "People on the Prairie," and "The Butterfly Lovers," and Chinese photo exhibitions held throughout the country were also warmly welcomed by the Mongolian people. Mongolian radio stations continuously broadcast Chinese programs.

The Mongolian "Truth Daily" successively published editorials such as "The Glorious Festival of the Chinese People" and "The Fraternal Friendship Between the People of Mongolia and China," and also published many articles introducing the tremendous achievements made by New China in various fields over the past five years.

Our acrobatic troupe stayed in Mongolia for fourteen days, performing nineteen shows, which were warmly welcomed and praised by the Mongolian people. In addition, they conscientiously exchanged learning with famous Mongolian acrobatic artists. On the evening of the 13th, they gave a joint performance at the Mongolian National Circus. Peng Xiaoyun, a fourteen-year-old Chinese actress, who had newly learned a Mongolian soft dance, received enthusiastic praise from the audience.

The various activities of the "Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Ten Days" held by the Mongolian People's Republic and the visits of our Art Troupe and Acrobatic Troupe to Mongolia have greatly promoted the deep friendship between the people of China and Mongolia.

### 蒙古人民共和国举行的“蒙中友好旬”结束

1954-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国举行的“蒙中友好旬”结束
    【新华社十六日讯】乌兰巴托消息：蒙古人民共和国为庆祝我国建国五周年举行的“蒙中友好旬”已在十月十日结束。赴蒙参加“蒙中友好旬”并进行访问的我国文艺代表团和杂技团已分别在十二日、十四日离开蒙古人民共和国回到北京。
    在“蒙中友好旬”期间，蒙古各地的党、政机关，人民团体以及牧民热烈地参加了一系列的促进蒙中友好的活动。苏赫—巴托尔和中戈壁等艾马克（省）党组织的宣传员和报告员，以“蒙中两国人民的伟大友谊”为题，在牧民中广泛地举行了座谈会。乌兰巴托市的国家剧院成功地演出了中国话剧
  “战斗里成长”。全国电影院连续演出了“鞍钢在建设中”、“抗美援朝”、“智取华山”、“草原上的人们”和“梁山伯与祝英台”等我国优秀影片，在全国各地举行的中国图片展览也受到了蒙古人民的热烈欢迎。蒙古广播电台不断播送了中国的节目。
    蒙古“真理报”连续发表了“中国人民的光荣的节日”和“蒙中两国人民的兄弟友谊”等社论，此外还登载了许多介绍新中国五年来在各方面所获得的巨大成就的文章。
    我国杂技团在蒙古停留了十四天，演出了十九场，受到了蒙古人民的热烈欢迎和称赞。此外还认真地与蒙古著名杂技艺术演员进行了相互学习。十三日晚又在蒙古国家马戏院作了联合演出，我国十四岁小演员彭小云新学习的蒙古柔软舞，受到了观众热烈的称赞。
    蒙古人民共和国举办的“蒙中友好旬”的各项活动以及我国文艺代表团和杂技团的访问蒙古，大大促进了中蒙两国人民之间的深厚友谊。
