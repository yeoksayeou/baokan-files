Title: Representatives of the CCP Central Committee and the CYLC Central Committee and Others Sweep Tomb of Comrade Ren Bishi

Author:
Date: 1954-10-28
Page: 3

On October 27th, the fourth anniversary of the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committe of the Communist Party of China and Honorary Chairman of the Central Committee of the China's New Democratic Youth League, representatives from the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central Committee of the China's New Democratic Youth League, and Comrade Ren Bishi's family members went to Comrade Ren Bishi's tomb in the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery in the western suburbs of Beijing to sweep the tomb on the morning of the 27th.

Representatives from units such as the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the People's Republic of China, the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, and the First Branch of the Marxist-Leninist Academy also went to sweep the tomb. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中共中央和团中央的代表等祭扫任弼时同志墓

1954-10-28
第3版()
专栏：

    中共中央和团中央的代表等祭扫任弼时同志墓
    十月二十七日是中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会名誉主席任弼时逝世四周年纪念日。中国共产党中央委员会办公厅、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的代表及任弼时同志的家属在二十七日早晨前往北京西郊八宝山革命烈士公墓任弼时同志墓地扫墓。
    前往扫墓的，还有中华人民共和国内务部、体育运动委员会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、北京市人民政府、青年团北京市委员会和马克思列宁主义学院第一分院等单位的代表。　（新华社）
