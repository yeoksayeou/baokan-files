Title: China and Albania sign cultural cooperation, technical and technical-scientific cooperation agreements
Author:
Date: 1954-10-16
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, October 15th) The following is a communique on the signing of agreements on cultural cooperation and technical and technical-scientific cooperation between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the People's Republic of Albania:

The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the People's Republic of Albania, in order to develop economic and cultural cooperation between the two countries and strengthen the friendship between the two peoples, have signed the China-Albania Cultural Cooperation Agreement and the China-Albania Technical and Technical-Scientific Cooperation Agreement in Beijing on October 14, 1954, after friendly and sincere negotiations.

Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, and Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, signed the China-Albania Cultural Cooperation Agreement and the China-Albania Technical and Technical-Scientific Cooperation Agreement on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, respectively. The head of the government delegation of the People's Republic of Albania, Foreign Minister Behar Shtylla, signed the above two agreements on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of Albania.

The Chinese side present at the signing ceremony included: Li Fuchun, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; representatives participating in the negotiations of the China-Albania Cultural Cooperation Agreement, including Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, and Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; representatives participating in the negotiations of the China-Albania Technical and Technical-Scientific Cooperation Agreement, including Sun Liyu, Director of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the State Planning Commission, and Chang Yanqing, Deputy Director of the Complete Equipment Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education; Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Dong Xibai, Chief Secretary of the Treaty Committee; and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department. The Albanian side included: Nesti Nase, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China, a representative participating in the above two agreements negotiations; Lieutenant Colonel Halim Ramohito, a member of the government delegation of the People's Republic of Albania; and Jasha, Second Secretary of the Embassy of the People's Republic of Albania in China.

Li Fuchun, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Behar Shtylla, head of the government delegation of the People's Republic of Albania and Minister of Foreign Affairs, delivered speeches at the signing ceremony to congratulate each other.

### 我国政府和阿尔巴尼亚政府签订文化合作、技术和技术科学合作两协定

1954-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府和阿尔巴尼亚政府签订文化合作、技术和技术科学合作两协定
    【新华社十五日讯】中华人民共和国政府和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府关于签订文化合作、技术和技术科学合作两项协定的公报：
    中华人民共和国政府和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府，为了发展两国间的经济和文化合作，加强两国人民之间的友谊，经过友好诚挚的谈判，已于一九五四年十月十四日在北京签订了中阿文化合作协定、中阿技术和技术科学合作协定。
    中华人民共和国文化部部长沈雁冰和农业部部长廖鲁言分别代表中华人民共和国政府在中阿文化合作协定、中阿技术和技术科学合作协定上签字，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长、外交部部长贝·什图拉代表阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府在上述两项协定上签字。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理李富春，参加中阿文化合作协定谈判的代表，对外文化联络事务局局长洪深和外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇；参加中阿技术和技术科学合作协定谈判的代表，国家计划委员会技术合作局局长孙力余和对外贸易部成套设备局副局长常彦卿；高等教育部部长杨秀峰，外交部副部长伍修权、条约委员会主任秘书董希白和交际处副处长王拓；阿尔巴尼亚方面有：参加上述两项协定的谈判代表阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻华大使纳赛；阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团员哈·拉莫希托中校和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻华大使馆二等秘书雅沙。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理李富春和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长、外交部部长贝·什图拉曾在签字仪式上先后讲话祝贺。
