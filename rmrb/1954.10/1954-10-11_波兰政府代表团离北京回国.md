Title: Polish Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1954-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: The Polish People's Republic government delegation, led by Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, and including Comrades:
	Jakub Berman, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Worker's Party and Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic;
	Stanisław Jędrychowski, Member of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers’ Party and Vice-chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic;
	Stefan Ignar, Vice-Chairman of the Supreme Executive Committee of the United Peasant Party of Poland and Vice-Chairman of the Council of State of the Polish People’s Republic, who came to our country at the invitation of our government to participate in the celebrations of the 5th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing by plane at 8:30 a.m. on the 10th to return to their country.

	Liu Shaoqi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, were at the airport to see them off.

	Also at the airport to see them off were Chen Yun and Li Fuchun, Vice-Premiers of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General, Liu Yalou and Su Zhenhua, members of the National Defense Council, Luo Ruiqing, Minister of Public Security, Huang Jing, Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Zhang Bojun, Minister of Communications, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Wu Xiuquan, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs, Xu Xuehan, Vice-Minister of Foreign Trade, Lü Dong, Vice-Minister of Heavy Industry, Li Yu, Vice-Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Wang Shoudao, Vice-Minister of Communications, Wei Que, Vice-Minister of Education, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Zeng Mei, Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Also at the airport to see them off were Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Qu Tangliang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and Yang Hansheng, Secretary-General of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles.

	Kiriloek, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Polish People's Republic to China, and officials of the embassy were also at the airport to see them off.

	The heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries to China who were at the airport to see them off included: U Ohn Maung, Ambassador of Burma,
Rochas, Ambassador of Pakistan,
Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam,
Gheorghe Ghiță, Ambassador of Romania,
Koenig, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic,
Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea,
Yudin, Ambassador of the Soviet Union,
Gopeden, Counselor of the Indian Embassy,
Šmíd, Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy,
Luvsanchultem, Counselor of the Mongolian Embassy,
Eates, Counselor of the British Representative Office,
Mattila, Commercial Counselor of the Finnish Legation, as well as officials from the embassies of Switzerland, Hungary, Indonesia, Sweden, Albania, Denmark, and Bulgaria.

	The national flags of the Polish People's Republic and the People's Republic of China, as well as the flags of the Soviet Union and other People's Democracies, were hung high at the airport.

	After the members of the government delegation of the Polish People's Republic, headed by Comrade Bolesław Bierut, entered the airport, they shook hands with the well-wishers one by one. The band played the national anthems of the Polish People's Republic and the People's Republic of China. Then, Comrade Bolesław Bierut, head of the delegation, and all members of the delegation reviewed the guard of honor lined up at the airport.

	Before boarding the plane, Delegation Head Bolesław Bierut delivered a speech at the airport (full text to be published separately), which was warmly welcomed.

### 波兰政府代表团离北京回国

1954-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰政府代表团离北京回国
   【新华社十日讯】应我国政府邀请来到我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的波兰人民共和国政府代表团团长、波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波·贝鲁特，团员：波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员、波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席雅·贝尔曼，波兰统一工人党中央委员会委员、波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席斯·英德里霍夫斯基，波兰统一农民党最高执行委员会副主席、波兰人民共和国国务委员会副主席斯·伊格纳尔，在十日上午八时半乘飞机离开北京返国。
    中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来都到机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的，还有国务院副总理陈云、李富春，秘书长习仲勋，国防委员会委员刘亚楼、苏振华，公安部部长罗瑞卿，第一机械工业部部长黄敬，交通部部长章伯钧，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，重工业部副部长吕东，第一机械工业部副部长黎玉，交通部副部长王首道，教育部副部长韦悫，外交部办公厅主任王炳南、苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处处长王倬如，以及北京市人民政府市长彭真，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部参谋长曾美。
    到机场欢送的还有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席区棠亮，中国文学艺术界联合会秘书长阳翰笙。
    波兰人民共和国驻我国特命全权大使基里洛克和使馆官员也到机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的各国驻华使节和外交官员，有缅甸大使吴拉茂，巴基斯坦大使罗查，越南民主共和国大使黄文欢，罗马尼亚大使郭佐文，德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一，苏联大使尤金，印度大使馆参赞高伯登，捷克斯洛伐克大使馆参赞石密茨，蒙古大使馆参赞罗布桑楚勒图姆，英国代办处参赞艾惕思，芬兰公使馆商务参赞马狄拉，以及瑞士、匈牙利、印度尼西亚、瑞典、阿尔巴尼亚、丹麦、保加利亚等使馆的官员们。
    机场上高悬着波兰人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国旗，以及苏联和各人民民主国家的国旗。
    以波·贝鲁特团长为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团人员走进机场后，与欢送者一一握手。乐队高奏波兰人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国歌。接着，波·贝鲁特团长和代表团全体团员检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。
    登机前，团长波·贝鲁特在机场上发表了讲话（全文另发）受到热烈欢迎。
