Title: People of Pyonggang Area in Korea Erect Monument to Martyr Qiu Shaoyun
Author:
Date: 1954-10-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 22nd: The people of the Pyonggang area in central Korea held a memorial service on October 12th to commemorate the second anniversary of the sacrifice of Qiu Shaoyun, a first-class hero of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and erected a monument to Martyr Qiu Shaoyun behind the position where he sacrificed his life.
  A representative from a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteers attended the memorial service and the monument unveiling ceremony.
  Korean people continuously came to Martyr Qiu Shaoyun's monument to offer wine and flowers.

### 朝鲜平康地区人民建立邱少云烈士纪念碑

1954-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜平康地区人民建立邱少云烈士纪念碑
    【新华社平壤二十二日电】朝鲜中部平康地区人民在十月十二日举行了纪念中国人民志愿军一级英雄邱少云烈士牺牲两周年祭奠大会，并在邱少云烈士牺牲的阵地后方立了邱少云烈士纪念碑。
  中国人民志愿军某部派代表参加了祭奠大会和立碑仪式。
    朝鲜人民络绎不绝地来到邱少云烈士纪念碑前奠酒献花。
