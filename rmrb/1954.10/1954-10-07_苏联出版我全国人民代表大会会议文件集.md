Title: Soviet Union Publishes Collection of Documents from the National People's Congress

Author: Li He
Date: 1954-10-07
Page: 4

    Soviet Union Publishes Collection of Documents from the National People's Congress
    [Moscow dispatch, October 6th] The "Pravda" Publishing House has published the "Collection of Documents from the First Session of the First National People's Congress of China," which includes: Mao Zedong's opening speech, Comrade Liu Shaoqi's report on the draft constitution, the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, and Zhou Enlai's report on government work. The "Pravda" Publishing House has also published a separate edition of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China. The State Political Books Publishing House has published separate editions of Liu Shaoqi's report on the draft constitution and Zhou Enlai's report on government work. The aforementioned documents have been widely distributed. (Li He)

### 苏联出版我全国人民代表大会会议文件集
李何
1954-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联出版我全国人民代表大会会议文件集
    【本报莫斯科六日电】“真理报”出版局出版了“中国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议文件集”，内容包括：毛泽东的开幕词，刘少奇同志关于宪法草案的报告，中华人民共和国宪法，周恩来关于政府工作的报告。“真理报”出版局并出版了中华人民共和国宪法单行本。国家政治书籍出版局出版了刘少奇关于宪法草案报告的单行本和周恩来关于政府工作报告的单行本。上述文件都已大量发行。（李　何）
