Title: Soviet Press Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 15th: The Soviet Press Workers Delegation, invited by our "People's Daily" to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 15th. The delegation of twenty people was headed by Podkourkov, a member of the editorial board of the Soviet "Pravda" and the chief editor of the "Pravda" newspaper department. The delegation includes key personnel from newspapers and periodicals such as "Pravda," "Trud," "Komsomolskaya Pravda," "Selskaya Zhizn," "Literaturnaya Gazeta," "Soviet Culture," and "Kommunist," as well as chief editors from republic newspapers such as "Soviet Ukraine" and "Red Uzbekistan," and the chief editor of the "Volga Commune" newspaper from Kuibyshev Oblast.

The delegation also includes Strepkhov, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

Welcoming them at the airport were Zhang Jichun, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Deng Tuo, Editor-in-Chief of "People's Daily," as well as responsible personnel from "People's Daily," Xinhua News Agency, the Broadcasting Bureau, "Workers' Daily," "China Youth Daily," "Ta Kung Pao," "Guangming Daily," "Beijing Daily," "China Young Pioneers," "Sino-Soviet Friendship," "Literary Gazette," "Health News," "China Agricultural News," "People's Railway" News, "Study" magazine, and "August First" magazine, and other news and publishing organizations.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Culture, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Chinese Writers Association.

Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, also welcomed them at the airport.

Also greeting them at the airport were correspondents stationed in Beijing from the Korean Central News Agency, the Vietnam News Agency, the German News Agency, and the Polish "People's Tribune," as well as correspondents stationed in Beijing from various Soviet newspapers and news agencies.

### 苏联报刊工作者代表团到达北京

1954-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联报刊工作者代表团到达北京
    据新华社十五日讯：应我“人民日报”邀请来我国访问的苏联报刊工作者代表团一行二十人，在团长、苏联“真理报”编委会委员、“真理报”报刊部主编波德库尔科夫率领下，十五日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团中包括“真理报”、“劳动报”、“共青团真理报”、“农业报”、“文学报”、“苏维埃文化报”和“共产党人”等报刊的主要负责工作人员，“苏维埃乌克兰报”、“红色乌兹别克斯坦报”等加盟共和国的报纸和古比雪夫州的“伏尔加公社报”的主编。
    代表团还包括苏联共产党中央宣传鼓动部副部长斯特列普霍夫。
    到机场欢迎的有中国共产党中央宣传部副部长张际春、“人民日报”总编辑邓拓以及“人民日报”、新华通讯社、广播事业局、“工人日报”、“中国青年报”、“大公报”、“光明日报”、
  “北京日报”、“中国少年报”、“中苏友好报”、
  “文艺报”、“健康报”、“中国农报”、“人民铁道”报、“学习”杂志、“八一”杂志等新闻出版机关的负责工作人员。
    到机场欢迎的还有外交部、文化部、中苏友好协会、中国作家协会等单位的代表。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金院士也到机场迎接。
    到机场迎接的还有朝鲜中央通讯社、越南通讯社、德意志通讯社和波兰“人民论坛报”驻北京记者以及苏联各报刊、通讯社驻北京记者。
