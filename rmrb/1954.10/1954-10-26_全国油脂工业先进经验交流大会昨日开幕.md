Title: National Oil and Fat Industry Advanced Experience Exchange Conference Opens Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1954-10-26
Page: 2

    The National Oil and Fat Industry Advanced Experience Exchange Conference, jointly convened by the Ministry of Light Industry of the People's Republic of China, the Ministry of Local Industry, the Light Industry Trade Union Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and other relevant units, opened in Beijing on the 25th.
    There were 234 representatives from industrial departments, oil and fat factories, oil and fat companies, cooperatives, and trade union systems from various regions attending the meeting.
    The main tasks of this conference are to clarify the principles and tasks of oil and fat industrial production for 1955, promote Li Chuanjiang's soybean oil extraction method, and exchange advanced experiences from various regions, so as to further reduce the residual oil rate in oil cake, increase the oil yield, and increase oil and fat production.
    Jia Tuofu, Minister of Light Industry, and Song Shaowen, Vice Minister, both delivered important reports at the meeting.

### 全国油脂工业先进经验交流大会昨日开幕

1954-10-26
第2版()
专栏：

    全国油脂工业先进经验交流大会昨日开幕
    中华人民共和国轻工业部、地方工业部，中华全国总工会轻工业工会工作委员会和其他有关单位联合召开的全国油脂工业先进经验交流大会，已于二十五日在北京开幕。
    出席会议的有各地工业部门、油脂厂、油脂公司、合作社以及工会系统的代表共二百三十四人。
    这次会议的主要任务是明确一九五五年油脂工业生产的方针和任务，推广李川江大豆榨油操作法和交流各地的先进经验，以便进一步降低饼内残油率，提高出油率，增加油脂产量。
    轻工业部部长贾拓夫和副部长宋劭文都在会上作了重要的报告。
