Title: Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on the Approval Procedures for Treaties Concluded with Foreign Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-10-18
Page: 1

    Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
    Decision on the Approval Procedures for Treaties Concluded with Foreign Countries
        (Adopted at the First Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on October 16, 1954)
    1. The People's Republic of China shall follow the approval procedures stipulated in Article 31, Paragraph 12 and Article 41 of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China for the conclusion of the following treaties with foreign countries: treaties of peace, treaties of non-aggression, treaties of friendship, alliance and mutual assistance and all treaties, including agreements, that expressly stipulate in the treaty that they must be ratified.
    2. Agreements, protocols, etc., that do not fall within the scope of the preceding article shall be approved by the State Council.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会  关于同外国缔结条约的批准手续的决定

1954-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会
    关于同外国缔结条约的批准手续的决定
        （一九五四年十月十六日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第一次会议通过）一、中华人民共和国同外国缔结下列各种条约，应当依照中华人民共和国宪法第三十一条第十
    二项和第四十一条的规定办理批准手续：和平条约，互不侵犯条约，友好同盟互助条约和
    其他在条约中明文规定必须经过批准的一切条约，包括协定在内。二、凡不属于前条规定范围内的协定、议定书等，由国务院核准。
