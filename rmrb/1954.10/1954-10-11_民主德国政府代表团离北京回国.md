Title: Delegation of the Government of the German Democratic Republic Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1954-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: The delegation of the Government of the German Democratic Republic, led by Dr. Lothar Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, and including members Mrs. Dr. G. Wittkowski, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the German Democratic Republic, who were invited to participate in the celebration of the fifth anniversary of the founding of our country, left Beijing by plane at 7:30 AM on the 10th to return home.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Deng Xiaoping and Li Fuchun, Vice Premiers of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Lai Jifa, Vice Minister of Heavy Industry; Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and East European Affairs; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Wu Han, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and Zeng Mei, Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, also saw them off at the airport.

König, the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and embassy staff members also saw them off at the airport.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to China who saw them off at the airport included U Mya Maung, the Ambassador of Burma; Kirylok, the Ambassador of Poland; Hoang Van Hoan, the Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Constantin Grigore, the Ambassador of Romania; Choi Il, the Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Yudin, the Ambassador of the Soviet Union; Šmíd, Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy; Lubsanchultem, Counselor of the Mongolian Embassy, as well as officials from the embassies of Hungary, Indonesia, Albania, and Bulgaria.

The national flags of the German Democratic Republic and the People's Republic of China, as well as the flags of the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries, were displayed at the airport.

After Dr. Lothar Bolz and other members of the delegation of the Government of the German Democratic Republic entered the airport, the band played the national anthems of the German Democratic Republic and the People's Republic of China.

Head of Delegation Lothar Bolz and others reviewed the guard of honor lined up at the airport.

Before boarding the plane, Head of Delegation Lothar Bolz delivered a speech at the airport (full text to be published separately).

### 民主德国政府代表团离北京回国

1954-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府代表团离北京回国
   【新华社十日讯】应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的德意志民主共和国政府代表团团长、德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长洛·博尔茨博士，团员、德意志民主共和国国家计划委员会副主席格·维特科夫斯基夫人、博士，已在十日上午七时半乘飞机离开北京返国。
    到机场送行的，有中华人民共和国国务院副总理邓小平、李富春，外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，重工业部副部长赖际发，文化部副部长郑振铎，外交部办公厅主任王炳南、苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部参谋长曾美等。
    到机场送行的，还有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全。
    德意志民主共和国驻我国大使柯尼希和使馆人员也到机场送行。
    到机场送行的各国驻华使节和外交官员，有缅甸大使吴拉茂，波兰大使基里洛克，越南民主共和国大使黄文欢，罗马尼亚大使郭佐文，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一，苏联大使尤金，捷克斯洛伐克大使馆参赞石密茨，蒙古大使馆参赞罗布桑楚勒图姆，以及匈牙利、印度尼西亚、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚等大使馆的官员们。
    机场上悬挂着德意志民主共和国和中华人民共和国两国国旗，以及苏联和各人民民主国家的国旗。
    以洛·博尔茨博士为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团人员走进机场后，乐队奏起德意志民主共和国和中华人民共和国两国国歌。
    洛·博尔茨团长等检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。
    登机前，洛·博尔茨团长在机场上发表了讲话（全文另发）。
