Title: French Women's Delegation Arrives in China for Visit
Author:
Date: 1954-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd: A French women's delegation of five, invited by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to visit China, arrived in Beijing on the 3rd, led by the head of the delegation, Mrs. Mariel Brunhes-Delamarre, a member of the French National Movement for the Protection of Children. The members include judges, writers, and journalists.

### 法国妇女代表团来我国访问

1954-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    法国妇女代表团来我国访问
   【新华社三日讯】应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请来我国访问的法国妇女代表团五人在团长、法国保卫儿童全国运动委员玛丽尔·布伦赫斯—德拉马尔夫人率领下在三日到达北京。团员包括法官、作家和新闻工作者。
