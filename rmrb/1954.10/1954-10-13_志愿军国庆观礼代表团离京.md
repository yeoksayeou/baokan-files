Title: Volunteer Army National Day Observation Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army National Day homecoming observation delegation left Beijing on the morning of October 10th to return to Korea.

    Those who went to the station to see them off included Zhou Xinmin, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Xu Baoju, Minister of the Organization Department of the Chinese People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association; Mo Yichang, Deputy Minister; Zeng Zhaosheng, Director of the General Office; Chen Mingde and Li Jiansheng, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Beijing People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee; Chai Zemin and Li Zongen, Standing Committee Members of the Beijing Anti-American and Aid-Korea Branch; Xue Zizheng, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 志愿军国庆观礼代表团离京

1954-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军国庆观礼代表团离京
      【新华社十日讯】中国人民志愿军国庆节归国观礼代表团十日上午离开北京返回朝鲜。
    前往车站欢送的，有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副秘书长周新民，中国人民抗美援朝总会组织部部长许宝驹、副部长莫艺昌、办公厅主任曾昭生，北京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会副秘书长陈铭德、李健生，北京市抗美援朝分会常务委员柴泽民、李宗恩，北京市人民政府秘书长薛子正及各民主党派、各人民团体和中国人民解放军的代表。
