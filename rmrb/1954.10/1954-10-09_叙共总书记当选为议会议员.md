Title: Syrian Communist Party General Secretary Elected as Member of Parliament
Author:
Date: 1954-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: The Syrian new parliamentary elections, after voting on September 24, 25 and October 4, have concluded. The election results show that the People's Party, which originally held a majority of seats in parliament, has lost ground, winning only 27 of the 142 seats.

    The General Secretary of the Syrian Communist Party, Khalid Bakdash, was elected as a member of parliament in this election. Syrian newspapers attach great importance to Bakdash's election, considering it an important event in the Middle East.

### 叙共总书记当选为议会议员

1954-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    叙共总书记当选为议会议员
    据新华社讯：叙利亚新议会选举在九月二十四日、二十五日和十月四日举行投票后，已经结束。选举结果显示，原来在议会中占多数席位的人民党失势，在一百四十二席中只得到二十七席。
    叙利亚共产党总书记哈勒德·巴克达斯在这次选举中当选为议员。叙利亚报纸十分重视巴克达斯的当选，认为是中东的重要事件。
