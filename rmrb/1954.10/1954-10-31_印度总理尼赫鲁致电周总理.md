Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru Cables Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1954-10-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: Indian Republic Prime Minister Nehru cabled Premier Zhou Enlai before leaving Guangzhou to return to his country. The text of the cable is as follows:

To Premier Zhou Enlai:

Our short visit to this great country has come to an end, and now we are about to leave here to return home. I am very happy to have come here to see a small part of the great work that is being carried out, and to have the opportunity to meet with the leaders of the Chinese people. I have seen a great country, great not only in its vast territory, but also in its substance and spirit. I express my thanks to you for the friendship and hospitality you have shown us throughout our stay.

Jawaharlal Nehru
October 30, 1954, in Guangzhou

### 印度总理尼赫鲁致电周总理

1954-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁致电周总理
    【新华社三十日讯】　印度共和国总理尼赫鲁在离开广州返国前致电周恩来总理。电文如下：周恩来总理阁下：
    我们在这伟大国家里的短期访问已告结束，现在我们即将离此返国了。我很高兴我来到此地看到了正在进行着的伟大工作中的一小部份，并有机会与中国人民的领袖们会晤。我看到了一个伟大的国家，它的伟大不仅在于幅员广阔，并且也在于它的实质和精神。我对您在我们整个停留期间所给予的友情和款待表示谢意。贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁
      一九五四年十月三十日于广州
