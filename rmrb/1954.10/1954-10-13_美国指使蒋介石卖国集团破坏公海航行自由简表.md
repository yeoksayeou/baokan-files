Title: U.S.-Directed Chiang Kai-shek Clique's Treachery to Disrupt Freedom of Navigation on the High Seas (Brief Table)
Author:

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: Under the direction and assistance of the U.S. aggressive clique, the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique has been continuously intercepting merchant ships of various countries in China's coastal waters and on the high seas since 1949. This piratical behavior of the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique seriously violates the principle of freedom of navigation on the high seas, hinders the development of normal trade relations between countries, and thus endangers peace in Asia and the world.

According to incomplete statistics, from September 1949 to October 1954, the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique carried out 111 interceptions, robberies, bombardments, or strafings against 67 merchant ships of various countries in China's coastal waters and on the high seas. Among the victims, British merchant ships suffered 43, totaling 86 incidents; Panamanian merchant ships suffered 14, totaling 15 incidents; Polish and Greek merchant ships each suffered 2, totaling 4 incidents; Soviet, Norwegian, West German, Dutch, Danish, and Italian merchant ships each suffered 1, totaling 6 incidents.

The U.S. aggressive clique also directly participated in the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique's piratical crimes. The Polish merchant ships "Praca" and "President Gottwald" were both intercepted and seized by the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique after being tracked and reconnaissance by the U.S. Air Force and Navy. The Soviet oil tanker "Tuapse" was intercepted by the U.S. Navy and handed over to the Chiang bandits to be劫往(seized and taken) to Taiwan. The U.S. Air Force also directly attacked merchant ships on the high seas. On July 26, 1954, in the area east of Hainan Island, China, two Polish ships, "Friendship" and "Peace," were attacked by U.S. aircraft.

### 美国指使蒋介石卖国集团破坏公海航行自由简表

1954-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    美国指使蒋介石卖国集团破坏公海航行自由简表
    【新华社十二日讯】蒋介石卖国集团在美国侵略集团的指使和协助之下，自一九四九年以来不断在我国沿海和公海上拦劫各国的商船。蒋介石卖国集团这种海盗行为严重地侵犯了公海航行自由的原则，并妨碍了各国之间正常贸易关系的开展，因而危害了亚洲和世界的和平。
    据不完全统计，从一九四九年九月到一九五四年十月，蒋介石卖国集团在中国沿海和公海上对六十七艘各国商船进行了一百十一次的拦截、抢劫、炮击或扫射，其中英国商船受害者四十三艘，共八十六次；巴拿马商船受害者十四艘，共十五次；波兰、希腊商船各二艘，共四次；苏联、挪威、西德、荷兰、丹麦、意大利各一艘，共六次。
    美国侵略集团还直接参与了蒋介石卖国集团的海盗罪行。波兰商船“工作号”和“哥特瓦尔德总统号”，都是由美国海空军跟踪侦察之后通知蒋介石卖国集团加以劫走的。苏联油船“图阿普斯号”是美国海军截扣以后交给蒋贼军劫往台湾的。美国空军还在公海上直接攻击商船。一九五四年七月二十六日，在我国海南岛以东地区，有两艘波兰船“友爱号”和“和平号”就遭到了美国飞机的攻击。
    美国指使蒋介石卖国集团从一九四九年九月到一九五四年十月在中国沿海和公海上拦劫各国商船破坏公海航行自由的情况如下表：
