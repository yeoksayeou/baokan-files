Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Holds Tea Party to Welcome British Professor Bernal
Author:
Date: 1954-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th October: The Chinese Academy of Sciences held a tea party on the afternoon of the 6th to welcome Professor John Bernal, Fellow of the Royal Society, a renowned British physicist, Vice-President of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, and Vice-President of the World Peace Council, who is visiting China.

The tea party was presided over by Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Attendees included Li Siguang, Tao Menghe, and Wu Youxun, Vice-Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, as well as many prominent Chinese physicists and scientists from other fields.

Guo Moruo delivered a speech at the tea party. He said: Professor Bernal is an outstanding physicist, and also a distinguished social activist. Because of his contributions to the cause of peace in recent years, Professor Bernal was awarded the Stalin International Prize for "Strengthening International Peace." Guo Moruo said: Professor Bernal's visit to China and his academic lectures will not only help promote our country's scientific research work, but also help the development of friendship between the people of China and Britain. Guo Moruo finally expressed his respect to Professor Bernal on behalf of Chinese scientists.

Professor Bernal then delivered a reply speech. He said that for thirty years, he has been concerned about the struggle of the Chinese people and is excited about the liberation of the Chinese people. He has always believed that science should serve the welfare of mankind, and in China, he sees the possibility and reality of doing so. He concluded by saying that as long as we unite, we can prevent science from being used as a tool to destroy humanity.

Li Siguang, Vice-President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chairman of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, expressed his respect for Professor Bernal's contributions to the World Federation of Scientific Workers and expressed his hope that Professor Bernal would offer valuable suggestions to Chinese scientific workers regarding their work within the scope of the World Federation of Scientific Workers.

Wu Youxun, Vice-President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, introduced Professor Bernal's contributions to science at the tea party. Zhou Peiyuan, Chairman of the Chinese Physical Society, expressed a warm welcome to Professor Bernal on behalf of Chinese physics workers.

Afterwards, Professor Bernal and the Chinese scientists present had cordial conversations in groups.

Since arriving in Beijing, Professor Bernal has toured historical sites in Beijing and visited the Institute of Applied Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. He will give academic lectures in China and exchange experiences with scientific workers in various fields in our country.

### 中国科学院举行茶会欢迎英国贝尔纳教授

1954-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院举行茶会欢迎英国贝尔纳教授
    【新华社六日讯】中国科学院在六日下午举行茶会，欢迎来我国访问的英国皇家学会会员、著名英国物理学家、世界科学工作者协会副主席、世界和平理事会副主席约翰·贝尔纳教授。
    茶会由中国科学院院长郭沫若主持，出席茶会的有中国科学院副院长李四光、陶孟和、吴有训以及中国许多著名的物理学家和其他方面的科学家。
    郭沫若在茶会上致词。他说：贝尔纳教授是一位杰出的物理学家，同时，也是一位杰出的社会活动家。由于他这些年来对和平事业的贡献，贝尔纳教授获得了“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金。郭沫若说：贝尔纳教授来我国访问并作学术讲演，不仅有助于促进我国的科学研究工作，同时，也将有助于中、英两国人民友谊的发展。郭沫若最后代表中国科学家向贝尔纳教授表示敬意。
    贝尔纳教授接着致答词。他说，三十年来，我一直关怀着中国人民的斗争，并为中国人民的获得解放而感到兴奋。我一直认为科学应为人类福利服务，在中国，我看到了这样做的可能和现实。他最后说，只要我们联合起来，就能够阻止把科学用来作为毁灭人类的工具。
    中国科学院副院长、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会全国委员会主席李四光对于贝尔纳教授对世界科学工作者协会的贡献，表示敬意，并表示希望贝尔纳教授对于中国科学工作者在世界科学工作者协会范围内的工作，提出宝贵的建议。
    中国科学院副院长吴有训在茶会上介绍了贝尔纳教授在科学上的贡献。中国物理学会理事长周培源代表中国物理学工作者，对贝尔纳教授表示热烈的欢迎。
    接着，贝尔纳教授和到会的中国科学家们分组进行了亲切的交谈。
    贝尔纳教授在到达北京后，曾游览了北京的名胜古迹，并访问了中国科学院应用物理研究所。他将在我国进行学术讲演，并和我国各方面的科学工作者交流经验。
