Title: Compatriots in Hong Kong and Macau Celebrate the National Day of the Motherland
Author: 
Date: 1954-10-03
Page: 2
Hong Kong and Macau compatriots celebrated the National Day of the Motherland with unparalleled joy.

    On the morning of the 1st, many labor unions, societies, shops, department stores, banks, newspapers, bookstores, and schools in Hong Kong hung the five-star national flag and were decorated with lanterns and colored streamers. In the more than ten large display windows of the China Aviation Company in Central, Hong Kong, were displayed pictures of the First Session of the National People's Congress and the motherland's production and construction, attracting many viewers every day. Many societies elaborately decorated huge colored arches, some with models of Tiananmen Gate and some with patterns of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China. In the afternoon, more than 1,000 representatives of various trade unions of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions held a grand celebration meeting. Chen Yaocai, chairman of the "Federation of Trade Unions," called on Hong Kong workers to further strengthen unity and love our great motherland even more. A celebration meeting attended by more than 3,000 representatives from forty-three industrial and commercial organizations, including the Hong Kong Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, was held on the morning of the 1st. The meeting praised the rapid progress of the motherland's construction and the bright future of the motherland's industrialization, calling on everyone to further unite and contribute to the motherland. The celebration meeting of the press and publishing circles was attended by more than 1,500 people from thirty-five units, followed by a grand banquet and an award ceremony for folk dance programs. On National Day, eight middle schools and some primary schools in Hong Kong and Kowloon held solemn flag-raising ceremonies and various celebration activities with patriotic educational significance. The National Day sports carnival held by the South China Film Workers Union opened at noon on the 1st, and many studio directors, directors, actors, and staff participated in the conference. They also held a carnival party to joyfully celebrate this great festival.
    On the 1st, many organizations, shops, and schools in Macau were also decorated with lanterns and colored streamers, and the national flag was hung high. Large arches were built on the prosperous streets. On the afternoon of the 1st, more than 200 representatives of industrial and commercial organizations, including the Macau Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, held a National Day cocktail party. Various neighborhood organizations, women's circles, cultural and educational circles, and Kiang Wu Hospital in Macau held separate gatherings to celebrate the National Day. Ten theaters in Macau screened excellent films from the motherland on the 1st to entertain compatriots from all walks of life. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 香港、澳门两地同胞欢庆祖国国庆节

1954-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    香港、澳门两地同胞欢庆祖国国庆节
    香港、澳门两地同胞以无比喜悦的心情庆祝祖国国庆节。
    一日清晨，香港许多工会、社团、商店、百货公司、银行、报馆、书店和学校都悬挂五星国旗，张灯结彩。中环中国航空公司的十多个大橱窗里，陈列着全国人民代表大会第一次会议和祖国生产建设的图片，每天都吸引着许多观众。许多社团精心装饰的巨大的彩牌楼上，有屹立着天安门楼模型的，有镶置中华人民共和国宪法图案的。下午，港九工会联合会各业工会代表一千多人举行盛大庆祝会，“工联”理事长陈耀材在会上号召香港工人更加加强团结，更加热爱我们伟大的祖国。香港中华总商会等四十三个工商团体三千多代表参加的庆祝大会在一日上午举行。会议盛赞祖国建设事业的突飞猛进和祖国工业化的光明前途，号召大家进一步团结起来，为祖国贡献出力量。新闻出版界的庆祝大会有三十五个单位一千五百多人参加，会后并举行盛大的宴会和民间舞蹈节目评奖晚会。香港、九龙有八所中学和部分小学校的教员学生在国庆日当天分别举行隆重的升旗礼和各种富有爱国教育意义的庆祝活动。华南电影工作者联合会举办的国庆节体育联欢大会在一日正午开幕，许多制片厂厂长、导演、演员和职工都参加了大会。他们还举行游艺晚会，愉快地欢度这个伟大的节日。
    一日，澳门许多团体、商店、学校也都张灯结彩，高悬国旗。在繁华街道上，都搭着大牌楼。一日下午，澳门中华总商会等工商界团体代表二百多人，举行庆祝国庆鸡尾酒会联欢。澳门各街坊组织、妇女界、文化教育界和镜湖医院等都分别集会欢度国庆节。澳门十家戏院都在一日放映祖国优秀的影片，招待各界同胞。　（新华社）
