Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-10-02
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This is the capital city on the night of the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. East and West Chang'an Avenue are brightly lit, and fireworks are dazzling in Tiananmen Square, where tens of thousands of people are singing and dancing to their hearts' content.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是中华人民共和国建国五周年伟大节日的首都之夜。东西长安街灯火辉煌，天安门广场上焰火灿烂，成千上万的人们在这里纵情歌舞。
