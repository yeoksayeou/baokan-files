Title: Negotiations Between Chinese and Norwegian Governments on Establishing Diplomatic Relations Successfully Concluded; Both Sides Agree to Establish Normal Diplomatic Relations and Exchange Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1954-10-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 6th:

The Governments of the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Norway, based on a common desire to promote relations between the two sides, successfully concluded negotiations on the establishment of normal diplomatic relations between the two countries on October 5, 1954. Both sides agreed to establish normal diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Norway and to exchange ambassadors.

### 我国政府和挪威政府建交谈判圆满结束  双方同意建立正常外交关系并互派大使

1954-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府和挪威政府建交谈判圆满结束
    双方同意建立正常外交关系并互派大使
    【新华社六日讯】中华人民共和国政府和挪威王国政府基于增进双方关系的共同愿望，已经在一九五四年十月五日，圆满地结束了关于两国建立正常外交关系的谈判。双方同意在中华人民共和国和挪威王国之间建立正常的外交关系，并互派大使。
