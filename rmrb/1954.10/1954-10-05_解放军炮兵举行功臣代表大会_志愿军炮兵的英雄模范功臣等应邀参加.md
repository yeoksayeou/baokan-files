Title: PLA Artillery Holds Congress of Meritorious Representatives; Heroes, Model Workers, and Meritorious Individuals from the Volunteer Army Artillery Invited to Participate
Author:
Date: 1954-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: The first Congress of Meritorious Representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Artillery was held in Beijing on September 20th and concluded victoriously on the 27th. Participating in the congress were 236 representatives of heroes, model workers, and meritorious individuals from various artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Invited to participate in the congress were 76 representatives of heroes, model workers, and meritorious individuals from the Chinese People's Volunteer Army artillery units. Representatives from the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, Air Force, Armored Corps, Engineer Corps, and the Beijing Municipal People's Government also attended the congress.

Over the past five years, the People's Liberation Army Artillery has achieved tremendous results in performing tasks such as defending the national defense and regular construction, and a large number of heroic model figures and people's meritorious individuals have emerged. According to incomplete statistics, in reserve teams, artillery units, agencies, schools, and other units, there are already 60,029 people's meritorious individuals, 2,437 heroic model figures, 28 heroic model units, and 1,401 collective meritorious units. In completing various tasks, Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members have played exemplary and leading roles. Among the total number of the aforementioned people's meritorious individuals, 32,558 are Communist Party members and 17,914 are Communist Youth League members.

Among the representatives of the meritorious individuals of the People's Liberation Army Artillery are Chang Enju, an artillery hero who made several great and small meritorious contributions in the War of Liberation; Guo Chengyang, a representative from the artillery unit on the Fujian front who participated in fiercely attacking the Chiang bandit troops on Jinmen Island; Zhang Zhong, a people's meritorious individual who achieved remarkable results in cultural studies and promoted the upsurge of creating models in the troops during military training; and Sun Huachang, a people's meritorious individual who was praised by the masses as a "road building model" in the construction of the Qinghai-Tibet Highway.

Among the representatives of meritorious individuals of the Volunteer Army Artillery are Tan Chaozhi, who was awarded the title of "Second-Class Hero" for operating one cannon, firing seventy-seven shells, and inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy; Liu Si, an anti-aircraft shooting hero who shot down twelve enemy planes and damaged sixteen enemy planes; Liu Jianhua, a hero of reconnaissance behind enemy lines; and Peng Fengen, who loves vehicles, saves a lot of state resources, and has driven safely for 47,700 kilometers.

During the meeting, all the meritorious representatives listened to speeches by Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department. Qiu Chuangcheng, Deputy Political Commissar of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Artillery, delivered a report to the congress entitled "Strive to Build a Strong People's Artillery."

The meeting also heard speeches by Su Jin, Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Artillery, and a report on the "Basic Summary of Artillery's Meritorious Work in the Past Five Years," as well as reports on the typical deeds of fourteen units and individuals. The representatives held enthusiastic discussions on these reports. The congress also awarded flags and prizes to meritorious individuals, model units, and heroic and meritorious representatives.

### 解放军炮兵举行功臣代表大会  志愿军炮兵的英雄模范功臣等应邀参加

1954-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军炮兵举行功臣代表大会
    志愿军炮兵的英雄模范功臣等应邀参加
    据新华社讯：中国人民解放军炮兵首届功臣代表大会九月二十日在北京召开，于二十七日胜利闭幕。参加大会的有中国人民解放军炮兵各部队英雄、模范和功臣代表二百三十六人。应邀参加大会的有中国人民志愿军炮兵部队的英雄、模范和功臣代表七十六人。中国人民解放军海军、空军、装甲兵、工兵和北京市人民政府都派代表参加了大会。
    人民解放军炮兵五年来在执行保卫国防，正规建设等任务中获得了巨大成绩，涌现了大批英雄模范人物和人民功臣。据不完全统计，在预备队、炮兵部队、机关、学校等单位中，已有人民功臣六万零二十九人，英雄模范人物二千四百三十七人，英雄模范单位二十八个，集体立功单位一千四百零一个。炮兵在完成各项任务中，共产党员和青年团员起了模范和带头作用。在上述人民功臣的总数中，有三万二千五百五十八人是共产党员，一万七千九百一十四人是青年团员。
    在人民解放军炮兵功臣代表中，有在解放战争中荣立数次大功和小功的炮兵英雄常恩举，来自福建前线参加痛击金门岛蒋贼军的炮兵部队代表郭承扬，在文化学习中成绩显著、在练兵中推动部队创制模型热潮的人民功臣张忠，在修筑青藏公路中被群众誉为“筑路模范”的人民功臣孙华昌等。
    在志愿军炮兵功臣代表中，有一人操作一门炮发射七十七发炮弹，大量杀伤敌人，而荣获“二级英雄”称号的谭朝志，击落敌机十二架、击伤敌机十六架的对空射击英雄刘四，敌后侦察英雄刘建华，爱护车辆、为国家节省大量资财、安全行车四万七千七百公里的彭逢恩等。
    会议期间，全体功臣代表听取了中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊、总政治部副主任甘泗淇的讲话。中国人民解放军炮兵副政治委员邱创成向大会作了“为建设强大的人民炮兵而奋斗”的报告。
    会议还听取了中国人民解放军炮兵副司令员苏进的讲话和关于“炮兵五年来立功工作的基本总结”的报告，以及十四个单位和个人的典型事迹的报告。代表们就这些报告进行了热烈的讨论。大会并将奖旗和奖品授给功臣、模范单位和英雄、功臣代表。
