Xinhua News Agency

    Annex to the Sino-Indian Trade Agreement

    (A) List of Chinese Export Commodities
    I. Cereals: Rice, grains other than rice, mung beans, green beans, and black beans.
    II. Machinery: Planers, drilling machines, other machine tools, punching machines, steam engines, harvesters, road rollers, electric water pumps, air compressors, concrete mixers, stone crushers, printing presses, molding machines, transformers, water pumps, electric motors, seeders, gear cutters, cotton spinning machines, flax spinning machines, telephone switchboards, drop wires, blowers, steam power generation equipment, DC and AC welding machines, medical equipment.
    III. Minerals: Crude antimony and pure antimony, gypsum, graphite, fluorite (fluorspar), sulfur, sulfur ore, realgar, borax, refined naphthalene, clay, arsenic (arsenic oxide).
    IV. Silk: White filature silk, spun silk, tussah silk (wild silkworm silk), douppioni silk (duplex silk), filature silk fabrics, spun silk fabrics, tussah silk fabrics.
    V. Animal Products: Wool, hides, duck feathers, goose feathers, wool yarn, beeswax, honey.
    VI. Paper and Stationery: Newsprint, woodfree paper, wrapping paper, waxed paper, blotting paper, fountain pens, pencils, ink, printing ink, numbering machines.
    VII. Chemical Raw Materials: Dinitrochlorobenzene, trisodium phosphate, carbolic acid (benzene, phenol), potassium carbonate, chlorobenzene, BHC insecticide, bleaching powder.
    VIII. Oils and Fats: Tung oil, cassia oil, peppermint oil.
    IX. Other: Camphor, cassia bark, musk, gallnuts, anise (star anise), menthol, almonds, galangal, rosin, medicinal materials, hairnets, fluorescent tubes, paint, bicycles, sports equipment, porcelain, glass and glassware, printed matter and books, canned food, flashlights, thermos bottles, buttons, lacquerware, firecrackers, stocking needles, sewing needles, fish and seafood, dried fruits, vegetables, garlic, vermicelli, Chinese films.

    (B) List of Indian Export Commodities
    Part 1 – List of Indian Export Commodities Applicable to the Entire Territory of China (Including the Tibet Region of China):
    Food and Tobacco: Grains, rice and pulses, spices (including chili, pepper), unmanufactured tobacco.
    Raw Materials and Unfinished Products:
        Ores and Concentrates: Chrome ore, kyanite, manganese ore, tin and zinc concentrates.
        Vegetable Oils: Peanut oil.
        Essential Oils: Lemongrass oil, sandalwood oil.
        Textile Fibers: Raw cotton, wool.
        Wood: Sandalwood.
        Hides: Raw goatskins, sheepskins, and tanned leather.
        Other: Myrobalan and myrobalan extract, paraffin wax, artificial shellac.
    Manufactured Goods:
        Chemicals and Pharmaceuticals: Bichromates, calcium chloride, chromic acid, glycerine, magnesium chloride, magnesium sulfate, naphthalene, potassium bromide, potassium nitrate, sodium bromide, sodium sulfide, sodium sulfite, medicines and herbs, paints, prepared dyes, cod liver oil.
        Instruments and Appliances: Thermometers, electric lights, electrical insulating materials, electrical appliances, mathematical instruments, surgical instruments, X-ray equipment, telephones, electric fans.
        Machinery: Ball and roller bearings, generators, electric motors, textile machinery including spindles, spinning frames, carding machines, looms, and calenders, boilers.
        Machine Tools: Center lathes, drilling machines, shapers, slotting machines, planers, sawing machines, power presses, lathe chucks, drill chucks, lathe centers, machine vices, drill sleeves, wood planers, round nose pliers, acetylene generators, circular seamers, electric belt lappers, head cutting shears, center shafts, hand or foot presses, surface milling machines.
        Hardware Products: Aluminum, brass, copper products, steel products excluding containers, non-ferrous metal products.
        Textiles: Cotton cloth and cotton textiles, cotton yarn and cotton thread, linen products, hemp thread and hemp rope, jute products.
        Vehicles: Bicycles, automobiles.
        Other: Indian films.
        Light Machinery: Centrifugal pumps, buckets, lanterns, sewing machines, chemical glassware, shellac, mica, asbestos, cement sheets, cement, cement pipes, building ironware, tires, machine transmission belts, paper, asphalt mixtures, agricultural implements, disinfectants.

    Part 2 – List of Other Export Commodities Applicable to the Tibet Region of China:
    Food and Tobacco: Sweets, edible oil, fruits, canned vegetables, cigarettes.
    Raw Materials and Unfinished Products:
        Vegetable Oils: Castor oil, karite oil, linseed oil, mustard oil, niger seed oil, rapeseed oil.
        Textiles: Clothing.
        Other: Plant gums other than gum arabic.
    Manufactured Goods:
        Instruments and Appliances: Storage batteries, electric wires and cables, scientific instruments, power transmission equipment, radio equipment.
        Machinery: Controlled transmission gears.
        Hardware Products: Screws and nuts, enamelware, wood screws.
        Paper and Stationery: Paper and stationery.
        Vehicles: Trucks, carts, axles.
        Other: Candles, clocks, coral products, matches, soap and soap powder, cosmetics, lard, pork, sugar, raincoats, rubber shoes, steel bars, iron wire, wire mesh, steel plates, road rollers, gasoline, kerosene, diesel oil, machine oil, galvanized iron sheets, leather and leather products, safety razor blades, biscuits, rubber products other than tires, glass sheets and glassware, sports equipment, hardwood.
