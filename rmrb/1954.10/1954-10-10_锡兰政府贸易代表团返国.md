Title: Ceylon Government Trade Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-10
Page: 1

    Ceylon Government Trade Delegation Returns Home
   [Xinhua News Agency, 9th] The Ceylon Government Trade Delegation, composed of seven members led by the Minister of Trade, Commerce and Fisheries of Ceylon, S.K. Suriyakumara Corea, which came to our country to discuss the 1955 Sino-Ceylon trade agreement on rice and rubber, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 9th to return home.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Li Yingji, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and others.

### 锡兰政府贸易代表团返国

1954-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰政府贸易代表团返国
   【新华社九日讯】前来我国商谈一九五五年中锡贸易大米、橡胶合同的锡兰政府贸易代表团一行七人，在团长、锡兰政府贸易、商务与渔业部部长斯·克·秀莱·科里亚率领下，在九日晨乘飞机离京返国。
    前往机场欢送的有：中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长雷任民，第三局局长卢绪章，外交部亚洲司司长陈家康，中国进出口公司副经理李应吉，对外贸易部交际处处长杨勉等人。
