Title: Calcutta Mayor Leaves Beijing for Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-10-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: Mr. Mukherjee, the Mayor of Calcutta, India, who was invited to Beijing by the Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, left Beijing for Moscow on the morning of the 22nd.

During his stay in Beijing, Mr. Mukherjee visited some of Beijing's municipal construction projects and places of interest, and participated in many activities welcoming Indian Prime Minister Nehru. Mr. Mukherjee also visited Nanjing.

### 加尔各答市长离北京去苏联

1954-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    加尔各答市长离北京去苏联
    【新华社二十二日讯】应北京市人民政府市长彭真邀请来北京的印度加尔各答市市长慕克吉先生，二十二日晨离开北京去莫斯科。
    慕克吉先生在北京期间曾参观了北京市的某些市政建设及名胜古迹，并参加了欢迎印度总理尼赫鲁先生的许多活动。慕克吉先生也曾访问了南京。
