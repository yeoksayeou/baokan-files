Title: Hungarian Patriotic People's Front National Committee Elects Presidium
Author:
Date: 1954-10-27
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 26th: According to TASS, the Hungarian Patriotic People's Front National Committee held its first meeting on the 24th. At the meeting, the Presidium of the National Committee was elected. The writer Pal Szabo was elected Chairman of the Patriotic People's Front; Minister of Culture Jozsef Darvas, Chairman of the Presidential Council of the Republic Istvan Dobi, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Imre Nagy, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party Matyas Rakosi, and former Deputy Mayor of Budapest Ferenc Harel were elected Vice-Chairmen of the Patriotic People's Front. First Deputy Minister of Culture Ferenc Janosi was elected Secretary-General of the Patriotic People's Front. Speaker of the National Assembly Sandor Ronai and fourteen others were elected members of the Presidium.
    According to the resolution of the National Committee of the Hungarian Patriotic People's Front, the newspaper of the Patriotic People's Front is "Magyar Nemzet".

### 匈爱国人民阵线全国委员会选出主席团

1954-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    匈爱国人民阵线全国委员会选出主席团
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利爱国人民阵线全国委员会二十四日举行了第一次会议。会上选出了全国委员会的主席团，作家保·萨波当选为爱国人民阵线主席；文化部部长约·达尔瓦斯、共和国主席团主席伊·道比、部长会议主席伊·纳吉、劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记马·拉科西、前布达佩斯副市长费·哈雷尔当选为爱国人民阵线副主席。文化部第一副部长费·雅诺希当选为爱国人民阵线秘书长。国民议会议长桑·罗奈伊等十四人当选为主席团委员。
    根据匈牙利爱国人民阵线全国委员会的决议，爱国人民阵线的机关报是“匈牙利民族报”。
