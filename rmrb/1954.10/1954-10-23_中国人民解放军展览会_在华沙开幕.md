Title: "Exhibition of the Chinese People's Liberation Army" Opens in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1954-10-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw: The "Exhibition of the Chinese People's Liberation Army" opened in Warsaw on the 18th, according to a report from the Polish Press Agency.

The exhibition's pictures and other exhibits illustrate the historical development of the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army over the past twenty-seven years. One section of the exhibits specifically introduces the achievements of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in military training and political education over the past five years; another section illustrates the contributions of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in the work of restoring the national economy. There is also a section of exhibits specifically introducing the heroic combat of the Chinese People's Volunteers in Korea.

### “中国人民解放军展览会”在华沙开幕

1954-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    “中国人民解放军展览会”在华沙开幕
    【新华社二十日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：“中国人民解放军展览会”十八日在华沙开幕。
    展览会的图片和其他展览品说明了英雄的中国人民解放军二十七年来的历史发展道路。有一部分展览品专门介绍中国人民解放军过去五年来在军事训练和政治教育方面的成就；另有一部分展览品说明中国人民解放军在恢复国民经济工作中的贡献。还有一部分展览品专门介绍中国人民志愿军在朝鲜英勇战斗的情况。
