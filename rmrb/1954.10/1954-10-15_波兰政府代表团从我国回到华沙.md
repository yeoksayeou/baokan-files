Title: Polish Government Delegation Returns to Warsaw from China
Author:
Date: 1954-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 14th: The government delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, led by the delegation head, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Bolesław Bierut, who were invited to attend the 5th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, returned to Warsaw by plane on the 13th.

On their way back to Poland, the Polish government delegation visited Ulaanbaatar at the invitation of the government of the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 波兰政府代表团从我国回到华沙

1954-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰政府代表团从我国回到华沙
    【新华社华沙十四日电】应邀参加中华人民共和国建国五周年国庆典礼的波兰人民共和国政府代表团，在代表团团长、波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波·贝鲁特率领下，已于十三日乘飞机回到华沙。
    波兰政府代表团在回国途中，曾应蒙古人民共和国政府的邀请到乌兰巴托访问。
