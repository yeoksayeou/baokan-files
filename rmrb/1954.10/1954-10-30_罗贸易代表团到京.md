Title: Romanian Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: A Romanian People's Republic trade delegation of eight people, led by Nicolae Anghel, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Romanian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing on October 29th.

Xu Xuehan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, Li Yusheng, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau, Luo Baoyi, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau, Zhou Li, Commissioner of the Import Bureau, and Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department, went to the airport to greet them.

Zhang Zike, Commercial Counselor of our Embassy in the Romanian People's Republic, also went to the airport to greet them.

Munteanu, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, Vicheanu, First Economic Counselor, and Andone, Economic Counselor, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 罗贸易代表团到京

1954-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    罗贸易代表团到京
   【新华社二十九日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国贸易代表团一行八人，由团长、罗马尼亚人民共和国对外贸易部副部长尼古拉·安格尔率领，在十月二十九日到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有我对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒、第二局副局长李郁生、出口局副局长罗抱一、进口局专员周力、交际处处长杨勉等人。
    到机场迎接的还有我驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使馆商务参赞张子克。
    罗马尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办孟天努、第一经济参赞维强努、经济参赞安定也前往机场迎接。
