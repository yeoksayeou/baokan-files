Title: Prime Minister Nehru Warmly Received During Visits to Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1954-10-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency
    Prime Minister Nehru of the Republic of India is warmly welcomed during visits to Northeast China.
    [Our Newspaper] After visiting Shenyang, Anshan, Lüda and other places in Northeast China, Prime Minister Nehru of the Republic of India and his party returned to Beijing by plane on the morning of the 26th. Greeting them at the airport were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and his wife, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department, Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, and officials from the Indian Embassy.
    After arriving in Shenyang at 10:00 a.m. on the 24th, Prime Minister Nehru and his party, accompanied by Shao Kai, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, and Hou Zhi, Director of the Shenyang Municipal Democratic Women's Federation, visited the Gaokan Agricultural Production Cooperative in the suburbs. There, they visited the cooperative's technology extension station, grain field, and the supply and marketing cooperative, which displayed 529 kinds of commodities. They also visited a primary school and a nursery there and learned about the cooperative's production organization, distribution principles, and the farmers' organization of amateur theatrical troupes to carry out cultural activities. Prime Minister Nehru and his party were warmly welcomed by the farmers during the visit. At the end of the visit, Zhang Zhenfa, director of the cooperative, greeted Prime Minister Nehru and asked him to convey the respect and friendship of all members of the Gaokan Agricultural Production Cooperative to all farmers in India.
    At 3:40 p.m. on the same day, Prime Minister Nehru and his party arrived in Anshan by special train from Shenyang. Prime Minister Nehru and his party visited the steel rolling workshop, finishing workshop, and processing workshop of the seamless steel pipe plant of Anshan Iron and Steel, and watched the entire automated process of manufacturing seamless steel pipes. They also visited the large rolling mill and watched the work in the heating, rolling, hot cutting, and processing sections. Young men and women workers operating complex machines warmly applauded to greet Prime Minister Nehru. Prime Minister Nehru and his party also visited the automated No. 7 and No. 8 blast furnaces. In Anshan City, Prime Minister Nehru and his party also visited the city and "February 19th" Park.
    At 6:00 a.m. on the 25th, Prime Minister Nehru and his party arrived in Lüda City from Anshan by car for a visit. Accompanied by Song Li, Mayor of the Lüda Municipal People's Government, they visited Dalian Seaport, the Sino-Soviet Shipbuilding Company, the Second Glass Factory, the Northeast Resources Museum, the Dalian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Manufacturing Plant, the Lüda Industrial Museum, the Lüda Industrial Technology Innovation Exhibition, and the Lüda Department Store.
    At 9:00 p.m. on the 25th, Prime Minister Nehru and his party took a train from Dalian to Shenyang and then returned to Beijing.
    Xinhua News Agency, 26th
    [Xinhua News Agency, 26th] Prime Minister Nehru of the Republic of India, accompanied by Mrs. Indira Gandhi and Mr. Na. Ru. Pillai, visited the "Exhibition of Achievements in Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction" at the Soviet Exhibition Center in the western suburbs of Beijing at noon on the 26th.
    Accompanying Prime Minister Nehru and his party were Academician Yudin, Soviet Ambassador to China, Borisinko, Director of the Exhibition of Achievements in Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction, Yuan Zhongxian, Chinese Ambassador to India, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Prime Minister Nehru and his party visited the exhibition's Industrial Pavilion, Agricultural Pavilion, Food Pavilion, Cultural Pavilion, Higher Education Pavilion, and Artworks Pavilion. During the visit, Borisinko, Director of the Exhibition of Achievements in Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction, gave them a detailed explanation.
    After the visit, Prime Minister Nehru wrote a message in the book of impressions, praising it as a beautiful and moving exhibition.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 26th] On the afternoon of the 26th, Mrs. Indira Gandhi visited the Affiliated Girls' Middle School and Primary School of Beijing Normal University. She visited the school's classrooms, physics laboratory, chemistry room, specimen room, and student dormitory, and inquired about the teaching system and the students' learning and living conditions of the two schools. During the visit, Mrs. Indira Gandhi was warmly welcomed by the students.

### 尼赫鲁总理在东北等地参观备受欢迎

1954-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理在东北等地参观备受欢迎
    【本报讯】印度共和国总理尼赫鲁等一行在参观我国东北沈阳、鞍山、旅大等地后，在二十六日上午乘飞机返抵北京。到机场迎接的，有中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫和夫人，办公厅主任王炳南，亚洲司司长陈家康，交际处副处长王拓和印度大使馆官员等多人。
    尼赫鲁总理等在二十四日上午十时到达沈阳后，即由沈阳市副市长邵凯、沈阳市民主妇女联合会主任侯志陪同，访问了市郊高坎农业生产合作社。在那里，他们参观了合作社的技术推广站、谷物场，以及陈列有五百二十九种商品的供销合作社。他们还参观了那里的一所小学校和托儿所，并且了解了这个合作社的生产组织、分配原则和农民们组织业余剧团开展文化活动的情况。尼赫鲁总理等在参观时，受到农民们的热烈欢迎。参观完毕时，合作社主任张振发向尼赫鲁总理致意，请他转达高坎农业生产合作社全体社员们对印度全体农民们的敬意和友谊。
    同日下午三时四十分，尼赫鲁总理等一行由沈阳乘专车到达鞍山。尼赫鲁总理等参观了鞍钢无缝钢管厂的轧钢间、精整间和加工间，观看了制造无缝钢管的全部自动化过程。他们还参观了大型轧钢厂，观看了加热、轧钢、热切和加工等工段的工作情况。正在操纵着复杂机器的男女青年工人们热烈地鼓掌向尼赫鲁总理致意。尼赫鲁总理等还参观了自动化的七、八号炼铁炉。尼赫鲁总理等在鞍山市还参观了市容和“二一九”公园。
    二十五日上午六时，尼赫鲁总理等由鞍山乘车抵达旅大市参观。他们在旅大市人民政府市长宋黎陪同下，参观了大连海港、中苏造船公司、第二玻璃厂、东北资源馆、大连机车车辆制造工厂、旅大工业博物馆、旅大工业技术革新展览会和旅大市百货公司。
    二十五日晚九时，尼赫鲁总理等由大连乘车赴沈阳转返北京。
      【新华社二十六日讯】印度共和国总理尼赫鲁偕同英迪拉·甘地夫人和纳·鲁·皮莱先生在二十六日中午前往北京西郊苏联展览馆参观“苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会”。
    陪同尼赫鲁总理等参观的，有苏联驻我国大使尤金院士、苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科，我国驻印度大使袁仲贤、我国外交部交际处处长王倬如等。
    尼赫鲁总理等参观了展览会的工业馆、农业馆、食品馆、文化馆、高等教育馆和美术作品馆，参观时，苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科为他们作了详细的说明。
    尼赫鲁总理参观完毕后，在感想簿上留言，他称赞这是一个美好而感人的展览会。
    【新华社二十六日讯】英迪拉·甘地夫人在二十六日下午参观了北京师范大学附属女子中学和小学。她参观了学校的教室、物理实验室、化学室、标本室和学生宿舍，并询问了这两个学校的教学制度和学生们的学习和生活情况。在参观时，英迪拉·甘地夫人受到学生们的热烈欢迎。
