Title: Chairman Voroshilov Receives Delegation of British Parliamentarians
Author:
Date: 1954-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 15th: TASS Moscow: Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, received the members of the British Parliamentary delegation headed by Lord Coleraine on October 14th.

Voroshilov welcomed the British Parliamentary delegation, which was visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

Lord Coleraine gave a reply, thanking them for the warm welcome to the delegation. He said that he was convinced that the visit of the British Parliamentary delegation to the Soviet Union would promote mutual understanding between the British and Soviet people, and would strengthen the friendly relations between them.

The conversation took place in a warm atmosphere for about an hour. Afterwards, Voroshilov wished the representatives the greatest success in their work to strengthen peace.

Present at the reception were First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Gromyko, and Deputy Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Gorkin.

Also present at the reception was the British Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, Sir William Hayter.

### 伏罗希洛夫主席接见英国议员代表团

1954-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫主席接见英国议员代表团
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·叶·伏罗希洛夫在十月十四日接见了以科勒雷英爵士为首的英国议员代表团团员。
    伏罗希洛夫对英国议员代表团表示欢迎。代表团是应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请到苏联访问的。
    科勒雷英爵士致答词，他感谢对代表团的热诚欢迎。他说，他深信英国议员代表团来苏联访问会促进英国和苏联人民之间的相互谅解，并且会加强他们之间的友好关系。
    谈话在热烈的气氛中进行了一小时左右。随后，伏罗希洛夫祝代表们在他们为加强和平的工作中取得最大的成就。
    接见时在座的有苏联外交部第一副部长葛罗米柯和苏联最高苏维埃主席团副秘书戈尔金。
    接见时在座的还有英国驻苏联特命全权大使威廉·海特尔勋爵。
