Title: Qinghai Province Achieves Bumper Harvest of Grain Crops
Author:
Date: 1954-10-27
Page: 2

    Qinghai Province has achieved a universal bumper harvest of grain crops in its agricultural areas. The total grain output of the province this year has increased by 22.6% compared to last year, and by 9.4% compared to 1952, the year with the best harvest. According to statistics from 35 out of 53 agricultural production cooperatives in the province, the average yield is 29.5% higher than that of individual farmers in the area, and 18.8% higher than the yield of mutual aid teams. Under the impetus of agricultural production cooperatives, most mutual aid teams have also achieved bumper harvests.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 青海省粮食作物丰收

1954-10-27
第2版()
专栏：

    青海省粮食作物丰收
    青海省农业地区粮食作物普遍丰收。全省今年的粮食总产量比去年提高百分之二十二点六，比收成最好的一九五二年提高百分之九点四。根据全省五十三个农业生产合作社中的三十五个社的统计，平均产量高出当地一般单干农民百分之二十九点五，高出当地互助组产量百分之十八点八。在农业生产合作社带动下，大多数互助组也获得了丰收。
                                  （新华社）
