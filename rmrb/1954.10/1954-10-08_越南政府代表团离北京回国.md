Title: Vietnamese Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1954-10-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Propaganda of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and members Tran Dang Khoa, Minister of Transport of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Nguyen Van Huyen, Minister of Education, who were invited to our country to participate in the National Day celebrations for the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing for home on the morning of the 7th.
    Those who went to the station to see them off included Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Zhang Bojun, Minister of Transport; Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture; Lai Yali, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; and Zeng Mei, Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others.
    Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and embassy officials also went to the station to see them off.
    Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in China who went to the station to see them off included König, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic; Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; and diplomatic officials from the Soviet Union, India, Czechoslovakia, Mongolia, Romania, Poland, Hungary, Burma, Indonesia, Albania, and Bulgaria.
    The national flags of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the People's Republic of China were hung at the station.
    When Head of Delegation Hoang Minh Giam and the delegation members arrived at the station, the band played the national anthems of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the People's Republic of China. Head of Delegation Hoang Minh Giam and others reviewed the guard of honor lined up at the station. Then, Head of Delegation Hoang Minh Giam delivered a speech at the station (full text to be issued separately). His speech was warmly welcomed.
    At 9:40, the train slowly started, and Head of Delegation Hoang Minh Giam and others waved goodbye to those who were seeing them off.
    During their stay in Beijing, the delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam visited the Guanting Reservoir, the Renmin University of China, the state-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Mill, and the Asian Students' Sanatorium under construction, and toured the Summer Palace, the Forbidden City, the Temple of Heaven, Beihai Park, the Temple of Azure Clouds, the Western Suburbs Park, and the Yonghe Temple and other scenic spots and historical sites.

### 越南政府代表团离北京回国

1954-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    越南政府代表团离北京回国
    【新华社七日讯】应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的越南民主共和国政府代表团团长、越南民主共和国政府宣传部部长黄明鉴，团员：越南民主共和国政府交通部部长陈登科和教育部部长阮文煊，在七日上午离京返国。
    前往车站送行的，有中华人民共和国国务院秘书长习仲勋，交通部部长章伯钧，教育部部长张奚若，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，文化部副部长丁西林，外交部办公厅副主任赖亚力、亚洲司司长陈家康、交际处处长王倬如，北京市副市长张友渔，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部参谋长曾美等。
    越南民主共和国驻我国大使黄文欢和使馆官员也到车站送行。
      到车站送行的各国驻华使节和外交人员，有德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一和苏联、印度、捷克斯洛伐克、蒙古、罗马尼亚、波兰、匈牙利、缅甸、印度尼西亚、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚等国的外交官员。
    车站上悬挂着越南民主共和国和中华人民共和国两国国旗。
    黄明鉴团长和代表团人员到达车站后，乐队高奏越南民主共和国和中华人民共和国两国国歌。黄明鉴团长等检阅了排列在车站上的仪仗队。接着，黄明鉴团长在车站上发表了谈话（全文另发）。他的讲话，受到热烈欢迎。
    九时四十分，火车徐徐开动，黄明鉴团长等和送行者挥手告别。
    越南民主共和国政府代表团在北京期间，曾参观了官厅水库、中国人民大学、国营北京第一棉纺织厂和在建筑中的亚洲学生疗养院，并游览了颐和园、故宫、天坛、北海、碧云寺、西郊公园和雍和宫等名胜古迹。
