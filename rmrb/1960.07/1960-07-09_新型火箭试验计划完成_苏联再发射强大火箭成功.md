Title: New Rocket Test Program Completed; Soviet Union Successfully Launches Another Powerful Rocket
Author:
Date: 1960-07-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 8th, TASS Moscow, July 7th, TASS communiqué: The Soviet Union successfully launched a new, powerful, multi-stage ballistic rocket for space flight for the second time on July 7, 1960.

    The rocket took off at the designated time and operated strictly according to the predetermined trajectory.

    Measurement data obtained by Soviet specialized ships equipped with various measuring instruments showed that the final stage model of the rocket landed on the water surface close to the predetermined landing point.

    The launch distance was approximately 13,000 kilometers.

    The successful launch of a new, powerful, multi-stage carrier rocket on July 5th and 7th fully completed the planned program for testing this rocket. The launch results provided all the necessary materials for manufacturing carrier rockets for further conquest of outer space.

    Due to the good results of the two rocket launches, it is no longer necessary to continue testing this rocket.

    TASS is authorized to announce that the coordinate restricted area mentioned in the TASS communiqué published on June 29, 1960, will be opened to navigation and air traffic ahead of schedule on July 8th.

### 新型火箭试验计划完成  苏联再发射强大火箭成功

1960-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    新型火箭试验计划完成
    苏联再发射强大火箭成功
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社莫斯科7日讯：塔斯社公报：苏联在1960年7月7日第二次成功地发射了用于宇宙飞行的新型威力强大的多级弹道火箭。
    火箭在规定的时间起飞，严格地按照预定的轨道运行。
    装有多种测量仪器的苏联专用船只所获得的测量材料表明，火箭的最后一级模型达到了紧紧挨着预定降落点的水面上。
    发射行程约一万三千公里。
    7月5日和7日成功地发射了新型威力强大的多级运载火箭，试验这种火箭的预定计划已全部完成。发射结果获得了制造用于进一步征服宇宙空间的运载火箭所必需的一切材料。
    由于两次发射火箭结果良好，已经没有必要继续试验这种火箭。
    塔斯社受权宣布，塔斯社于1960年6月29日发表的公报中所指出的座标限区提前于7月8日对航海和飞机航行开放。
