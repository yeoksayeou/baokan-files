Title: Premier Zhou Meets with African Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Vice Chairmen Peng Zhen and Guo Moruo met with a group of African guests in Beijing this morning and held cordial and friendly talks with them.

These guests were: Théodore Benguila, Member of the National Steering Committee and Second Deputy Secretary-General of the Congolese African Solidarity Party; Diallo Moussa Sane, Secretary-General of the Sudanese Peace Movement of the Mali Federation; Keir, a Sudanese peace activist; Ahmed Abdel Magid, head of the Sudanese student delegation; and members Abdel Rahim Mamud Hadi, Abdel Hafiz Nour Dam; and A.B. Illah, head of the Sierra Leonean student delegation, and member R.R. Shanu Wilson.

Present at the meeting were Liao Chengzhi, Liu Changsheng, Zhang Hanfu, Chu Tunan, and Zhang Zhixiang.

### 周总理接见非洲外宾

1960-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见非洲外宾
    新华社23日讯　周恩来总理，陈毅副总理，彭真、郭沫若副委员长，今天上午接见了一批在北京的非洲外宾，并同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    这些外宾是：刚果非洲团结党全国指导委员会委员、第二副总书记西奥多·本基拉，马里联邦苏丹和平运动秘书长迪亚洛·摩沙·萨内，苏丹和平人士凯尔，苏丹学生代表团团长艾哈迈德·阿卜杜勒·马吉德，团员阿卜杜勒·拉欣·马穆德·哈迪，阿卜杜勒·哈菲斯·努尔·达姆，塞拉勒窝内学生代表团团长阿·布·伊拉赫和团员尔·尔·沙努·威尔逊。
    接见时在座的，有廖承志、刘长胜、章汉夫、楚图南、张致祥。
