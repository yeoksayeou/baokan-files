Title: Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway Tracks Laid to Honghukhan
Author:
Date: 1960-07-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, July 29th: The track-laying project of the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway is advancing at high speed. As of the 25th, tracks had been laid near Honghukhan Station, only 320 kilometers away from Urumqi.

On the 10th of this month, the track-laying workers set a record of laying more than ten kilometers of track per day, surpassing the record of six to seven kilometers per day achieved in June's high-speed track-laying and the target set by the Party Committee of the Urumqi Railway Bureau. On the 17th, despite wind and rain, they created a new record of laying 11.13 kilometers of track per day using mechanical track-laying methods.

### 兰新路铺轨到红胡坎

1960-07-30
第3版()
专栏：

    兰新路铺轨到红胡坎
    据新华社乌鲁木齐29日电　兰新铁路铺轨工程高速度前进。到25日止，已经铺轨到红胡坎车站附近，距离乌鲁木齐只有三百二十公里。
    铺轨职工在本月10日，创造了日铺轨十公里多的纪录，超过了6月份高速铺轨日进六至七公里的纪录和乌鲁木齐铁路局党委提出的指标；17日，他们在风雨交集的情况之下，又创造了机械铺轨日进十一点一三公里的新纪录。
