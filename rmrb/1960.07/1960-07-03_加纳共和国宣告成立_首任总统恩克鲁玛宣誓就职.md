Title: Republic of Ghana Proclaimed, First President Nkrumah Sworn In
Author:
Date: 1960-07-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Accra, July 1st: The Republic of Ghana was proclaimed today. Former Prime Minister Kwame Nkrumah assumed office as the first President of the Republic and was sworn in.

Official delegations from many countries attended the ceremony. Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, Ke Hua, also arrived here last night from Conakry to attend today's ceremony.

According to the Republican Constitution, which received a large majority of votes in the April referendum, Ghana became a republic on July 1st. Thousands of Ghanaians in colorful national costumes participated in the inauguration ceremony. They sang and danced when the establishment of the Republic was announced. Celebrations will last for four days, including parades, fireworks, garden parties, etc.

Today, the capital Accra is dressed in festive attire. Banners and colorful flags are hung on all public buildings and squares. People everywhere are jubilantly celebrating the founding of the Republic. Nkrumah delivered a radio address to the nation tonight, calling on the people of Ghana to make every effort to build their country.

He recounted the national movement of the Ghanaian people against colonialism and imperialism. He pointed out that the Ghanaian people had achieved victory through many arduous struggles. He also said that today we have entered a new life and are starting economic and social construction in order to build Ghana into a beautiful and prosperous country.

### 加纳共和国宣告成立  首任总统恩克鲁玛宣誓就职

1960-07-03
第6版()
专栏：

    加纳共和国宣告成立
    首任总统恩克鲁玛宣誓就职
    新华社阿克拉1日电　加纳共和国今天宣告成立。前总理克瓦米·恩克鲁玛担任共和国首任总统，并且举行了宣誓就职典礼。
    参加典礼的有来自许多国家的官方代表团。中国驻几内亚大使柯华也在昨晚从科纳克里来到这里，出席了今天的典礼。
    根据在今年4月公民投票中获得绝大多数票的共和国宪法规定，加纳自7月1日起成为共和国。成千的加纳人民穿着多彩的民族服装，参加了就职典礼。当宣布共和国成立的时候，他们载歌载舞。庆祝活动将持续四天，包括游行、放焰火、游园会等。
    今天首都阿克拉披着节日盛装。所有的公共建筑物和广场上都悬挂了横幅和彩旗。各地人民欢腾地庆祝共和国的成立。恩克鲁玛今晚对全国人民发表广播演说，号召加纳人民尽一切努力来建设他们的国家。
    他追述了加纳人民反对殖民主义和帝国主义的民族运动。他指出，加纳人民经过了许多艰苦斗争才取得胜利。他还说，今天我们已经进入一种新的生活，开始进行经济和社会建设，以便把加纳建设成为一个美丽和繁荣的国家。
