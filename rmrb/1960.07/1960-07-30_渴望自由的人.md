Title: The Person Who Yearns for Freedom
Author: Ahemaide·Muhammode·He'er
Date: 1960-07-30
Page: 8

The person who yearns for freedom cannot be as docile and weak as a lamb.
The person who yearns for freedom should be a lion, untamable and powerful.
The person who yearns for freedom—should be the intense firelight erupting from a volcano.
The person who yearns for freedom—should be the howling storm that sweeps away all the filth.
I believe in you, the cause of revolution,
believe in you, the world of revival,
believe in you, the weapon of battle,
believe in you, the person who yearns for freedom!

(Translated by Tie Xian, originally published in "World Literature," June issue)

### 渴望自由的人
阿赫麦德·穆罕默德·赫尔
1960-07-30
第8版()
专栏：

    渴望自由的人
    〔苏丹〕阿赫麦德·穆罕默德·赫尔渴望自由的人，不能像羊羔那样顺从而柔弱。渴望自由的人，应当做一头狮子，不能驯服，要有力量。渴望自由的人——该是从火山口喷射出的强烈的火光。渴望自由的人——该是呼啸着的暴风，它把所有的脏物扫光。我相信你，革命的事业，相信你，复兴的世界，相信你，战斗的武器，相信你，渴望自由的人！铁弦译原载《世界文学》六月号
