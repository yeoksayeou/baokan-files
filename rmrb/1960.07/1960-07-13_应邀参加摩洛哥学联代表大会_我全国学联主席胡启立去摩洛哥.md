Title: Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Goes to Morocco to Attend the Moroccan Student Union Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-07-13
Page: 6

　　Invited to Attend the Moroccan Student Union Congress
　　Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Goes to Morocco
　　Xinhua News Agency, July 12th. Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, left Beijing by plane today for Morocco. He was invited to attend the 5th Congress of the Moroccan National Student Union. This congress will be held in Casablanca from July 16th to 20th.

### 应邀参加摩洛哥学联代表大会  我全国学联主席胡启立去摩洛哥

1960-07-13
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀参加摩洛哥学联代表大会
　  我全国学联主席胡启立去摩洛哥
    新华社12日讯　中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立今天乘飞机离开北京去摩洛哥。他是应邀前往参加摩洛哥全国学联第五届代表大会的。这次代表大会将于7月16日到20日在卡萨布兰卡举行。
