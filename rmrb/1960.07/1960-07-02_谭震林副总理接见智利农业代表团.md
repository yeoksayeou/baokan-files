Title: Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin Receives Chilean Agricultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 1st – Vice Premier of the State Council Tan Zhenlin received the entire Chilean agricultural delegation tonight, headed by the delegation leader Clodomiro Almeyda Medina, deputy leader Gastón Cavallo Derien, and Eduardo Rojas. The hosts and guests held friendly talks.

Present at the reception were Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Agriculture; Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; and Xia Ting, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Society of Agronomy.

Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was also present at the reception. (Attached picture)
Vice Premier of the State Council Tan Zhenlin received all members of the Chilean agricultural delegation on the evening of July 1. The second person from the left in the picture is Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin, and the first person on the right is the leader of the delegation, Clodomiro Almeyda Medina. ### 谭震林副总理接见智利农业代表团

1960-07-02
第6版()
专栏：

    谭震林副总理接见智利农业代表团
    新华社1日讯　国务院副总理谭震林今晚接见以团长克洛多米罗·阿耳梅达·梅迪纳，副团长卡斯东·卡瓦洛·德里恩和爱德华多·罗哈斯为首的智利农业代表团全体成员，宾主进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有农业部副部长刘瑞龙，中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达，中国农学会副秘书长夏霆。
    接见时，外交部美澳司司长申健也在座。（附图片）
