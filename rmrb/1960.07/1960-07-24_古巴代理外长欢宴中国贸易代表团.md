Title: Cuban Acting Foreign Minister Hosts Banquet for Chinese Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Havana, July 22nd. Cuban Acting Foreign Minister Carlos Olivares hosted a banquet on the evening of the 21st to entertain the Chinese trade delegation currently visiting Cuba. The hosts and guests had a conversation in a friendly atmosphere.

### 古巴代理外长欢宴中国贸易代表团

1960-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴代理外长欢宴中国贸易代表团
    新华社哈瓦那22日电　古巴代理外交部长卡洛斯·奥利瓦雷斯21日晚上设宴招待正在古巴访问的中国贸易代表团。宾主在友好的气氛中进行了交谈。
