Title: Telegram to the Belgian Government
Author:
Date: 1960-07-15
Page: 6

    Telegram to the Belgian Government
    The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, currently meeting in Stockholm, condemns the further dispatch of Belgian troops to the independent Republic of the Congo under the pretext of protecting Belgian nationals in the Congo. This action is more likely to endanger them. This is an act of aggression that undermines the sovereignty of an independent state and threatens world peace.
    All peace-loving people will regard this action as an attempt to restore the infamous Belgian colonial policy. The people of the world hail the hard-won independence of the Republic of the Congo, to which the Belgian people's actions have contributed. The people of the world must not tolerate the abandonment of the independence of the Republic of the Congo.
    We demand: no more dispatch of Belgian or other countries' troops to the Congo, the withdrawal of all troops, the dismantling of all foreign military bases, and full respect for the independence and sovereignty of the Republic of the Congo.
                    Stockholm, July 10, 1960

### 给比利时政府的电报

1960-07-15
第6版()
专栏：

    给比利时政府的电报
    正在斯德哥尔摩开会的世界和平理事会常务委员会谴责借口保护在刚果的比利时侨民而进一步派遣比利时军队到独立的刚果共和国的做法。这种做法更为可能的是使他们遭到危险。这是破坏独立国家的主权和威胁世界和平的侵略行动。
    所有爱好和平的人民将认为，这个行动是企图恢复臭名远扬的比利时殖民政策。世界人民欢呼刚果共和国得来不易的、比利时人民的行动对之作出了贡献的独立。世界人民决不能容忍废弃刚果共和国的独立。
    我们要求：不再派遣比利时或其它国家的军队到刚果去，撤出所有的军队，取消一切外国军事基地，充分尊重刚果共和国的独立和主权。
                    1960年7月10日于斯德哥尔摩
