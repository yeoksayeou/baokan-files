Title: Liu Changsheng Hosts Banquet for Bolivian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-07-14
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 13th: Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, held a banquet on the 13th to welcome Mario Torres Calleja, Secretary-General of the Bolivian Miners' Federation, who is visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union.
　　Those attending the banquet included: Li Zaiven, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Li Chunming, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union; and Shao Naifen, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
　　Before the banquet, Liu Changsheng met with Mario Torres Calleja and had a friendly and cordial conversation.

### 刘长胜欢宴玻利维亚客人

1960-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　刘长胜欢宴玻利维亚客人
    新华社13日讯　中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜在13日举行晚宴，欢迎应中国煤矿工会全国委员会邀请前来我国访问的玻利维亚矿工联合会总书记马里奥·托莱斯·卡耶哈。
    参加晚宴的有：中华全国总工会书记处书记栗再温，中国煤矿工会全国委员会副主席李春明，中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长邵乃奋等。
    在晚宴前，刘长胜接见了马里奥·托莱斯·卡耶哈，进行了友好的亲切的交谈。
