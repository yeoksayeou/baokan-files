Title: Albania Accelerates Agricultural Mechanization: 85% of National Tillage, 50% of Spring Planting, and 80% of Harvesting are Completed with Agricultural Machinery
Author:
Date: 1960-07-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 24th. According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, the degree of mechanization in Albanian agricultural labor has reached a considerable level. Currently, 85% of the national tillage workload, 50% of spring planting, and 80% of harvesting are completed using agricultural machinery. Albania has surpassed its neighboring countries of Yugoslavia, Greece, and Turkey in terms of the average number of tractors per thousand hectares of arable land.

The Albanian Party of Labor has taken many measures to accelerate the country's agricultural mechanization. Since 1956, Albania has imported 2,670 fifteen-horsepower tractors from the Soviet Union and other socialist countries. There are now 4,000 tractors and twenty-five machine tractor stations nationwide. During the Second Five-Year Plan (1956-1960), the number of tractors more than doubled.

The increase in agricultural machinery and the active labor of agricultural workers at machine tractor stations have made it possible to complete machine tillage tasks ahead of schedule and exceeding targets. According to the regulations of the Third Congress of the Albanian Party of Labor, the amount of machine tillage in 1960 should reach 715,000 hectares, but 620,000 hectares have already been tilled in the first half of this year. It is estimated that the annual total will reach 1.2 million hectares, far exceeding the original target. Despite unfavorable weather conditions this year, Albanian laboring farmers, with the help of machine tractor stations, will certainly be able to complete the planting plans for crops such as tobacco, sugar beets, and cotton.

### 阿尔巴尼亚加速农业机械化  全国耕地工作量中的百分之八十五、春播中的百分之五十、收获工作中的百分之八十，都用农业机器来完成

1960-07-25
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚加速农业机械化
    全国耕地工作量中的百分之八十五、春播中的百分之五十、收获工作中的百分之八十，都用农业机器来完成
    新华社地拉那24日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚农业劳动中的机械化程度已达到相当水平。现在，全国耕地工作量中的85％、春播中的50％、收获工作中的80％，都用农业机器来完成。在按每一千公顷可耕地平均计算的拖拉机数量方面，阿尔巴尼亚已经超过它的邻国南斯拉夫、希腊和土耳其。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党在加速国家的农业机械化方面采取了许多措施。从1956年以来，阿尔巴尼亚就从苏联和其它社会主义国家进口十五匹马力的拖拉机二千六百七十台，现在全国已有四千台拖拉机，二十五个机器拖拉机站。在第二个五年计划期间（1956—1960），拖拉机数量增加了二倍多。
    农业机器的增加及机器拖拉机站农业劳动者的积极劳动，使得有可能提前和超额完成机器耕作任务。按阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第三次代表大会规定，在1960年，机器耕地的工作量应该达到七十一万五千公顷，而今年上半年就已耕了六十二万公顷，估计全年可达到一百二十万公顷，远远超过原订指标。尽管今年气候条件不好，阿尔巴尼亚的劳动农民在机器拖拉机站的帮助下一定能够完成烟草、甜菜、棉花等农作物的种植计划。
