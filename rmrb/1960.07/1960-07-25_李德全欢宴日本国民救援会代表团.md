Title: Li Dequan Hosts Banquet for Japanese National Relief Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd: Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Relief Association, hosted a banquet tonight to warmly welcome the Japanese National Relief Association Delegation headed by Okamoto Mitsuo, Secretary-General of the Japanese National Relief Association.

Li Dequan and the head of the delegation, Okamoto Mitsuo, spoke at the banquet.

Those present at the banquet included Wu Defeng, Vice President of the Political Science and Law Society, Li Shu, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Relief Branch, and Wang Xiaoyun, Deputy Director of the Secretariat of the Foreign Affairs Society.

Huang Fengjiu, a Korean peace activist in Beijing, was also invited to the banquet.

### 李德全欢宴日本国民救援会代表团

1960-07-25
第6版()
专栏：

    李德全欢宴日本国民救援会代表团
    新华社23日讯　中国人民救济总会副主席李德全今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎以日本国民救援会事务局长冈本光雄为首的日本国民救援会代表团。
    李德全和代表团团长冈本光雄在宴会上讲了话。
    出席宴会的，有政法学会副会长吴德峰，北京市救济分会副主席李恕，外交学会秘书处副主任王晓云等。
    在北京的朝鲜和平人士黄凤九也应邀出席了宴会。
