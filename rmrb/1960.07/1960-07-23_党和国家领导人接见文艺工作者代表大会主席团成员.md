Title: Party and State Leaders Receive Presidium Members of the Literary and Art Workers Congress

Author:
Date: 1960-07-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: Party and state leaders Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Soong Ching Ling, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, Lu Dingyi, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Yang Shangkun, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, and others, this afternoon received all the members of the Presidium of the Third Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers, shook hands with them one by one, and had cordial conversations.

    (Attached picture)
    Before the opening of the Congress, Party and state leaders received the members of the Congress Presidium.

### 党和国家领导人接见文艺工作者代表大会主席团成员

1960-07-23
第2版()
专栏：

    党和国家领导人接见文艺工作者代表大会主席团成员
    新华社22日讯　党和国家领导人刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、宋庆龄、邓小平、彭真、陈毅、陆定一、陈伯达、康生、杨尚昆、沈钧儒、郭沫若等，今天下午接见了中国文学艺术工作者第三次代表大会主席团的全体成员，和他们一一握手，进行了亲切交谈。

    大会开幕前，党和国家领导人接见了大会主席团成员。
