Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet to Welcome Gabiton
Author:
Date: 1960-07-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 6th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome renowned French social activist, Professor of Law and Economic Sciences at the University of Paris, René Gabiton, and his wife, who are visiting China.

Attending the banquet were Liu Ningyi, Nan Hanchen, Ding Xilin, Hu Yuzhi, and other figures from various circles.

Xinhua News Agency, July 5th: René Gabiton, a renowned French social activist and Professor of Law and Economic Sciences at the University of Paris, along with his wife and four children, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the China Peace Committee for a visit to our country.

Greeting them at the airport were Burhan, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Secretary-General Qu Tangliang, and others.

### 郭沫若设宴欢迎加比唐

1960-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若设宴欢迎加比唐
    新华社6日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今晚设宴欢迎前来我国访问的法国著名社会活动家、巴黎大学法律及经济科学系教授雷纳·加比唐和他的夫人。
    出席宴会作陪的有刘宁一、南汉宸、丁西林、胡愈之等各界人士。
    新华社5日讯　法国著名社会活动家、巴黎大学法律及经济科学系教授雷纳·加比唐和他的夫人、子女共四人，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来我国访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席包尔汉，秘书长区棠亮等人。
