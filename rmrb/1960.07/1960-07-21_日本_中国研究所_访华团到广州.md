Title: Japanese "China Research Institute" Delegation Visits Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-07-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 20th. The Japanese "China Research Institute" delegation, led by Hirano Yoshitaro, Vice Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association and Director of the Japan Peace Committee, arrived in Guangzhou by train from Shenzhen yesterday afternoon. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries.

Wang Yanshi, Vice President of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, and Wang Lan, Deputy Secretary-General, warmly welcomed the Japanese guests at the station. In the evening, Du Guoxiang, President of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet to welcome them. At the banquet, he warmly praised the great victories achieved by the heroic Japanese people in their patriotic anti-American struggle. Hirano Yoshitaro spoke at the banquet, thanking the Chinese people for their support of the Japanese people's struggle.

### 日本“中国研究所”访华团到广州

1960-07-21
第6版()
专栏：

    日本“中国研究所”访华团到广州
    新华社广州20日电　以日中友好协会副会长、日本和平委员会理事长平野义太郎为首的日本“中国研究所”访华代表团一行五人，昨天下午从深圳乘火车到达广州。代表团是应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国进行友好访问的。
    中国人民对外文化协会广州分会副会长王燕士，副秘书长王岚等到车站热烈欢迎日本客人。晚上，中国人民对外文化协会广州分会会长杜国庠设宴欢迎，他在宴会上热烈赞扬英勇的日本人民在反美爱国斗争中所取得的巨大胜利。平野义太郎在宴会上讲话，感谢中国人民对日本人民斗争的支持。
