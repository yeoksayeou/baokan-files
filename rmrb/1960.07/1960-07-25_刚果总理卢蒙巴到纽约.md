Title: Congolese Prime Minister Lumumba Arrives in New York
Author:
Date: 1960-07-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 24th—Congolese Prime Minister Lumumba arrived in New York by plane this morning.

Lumumba left the Congolese capital of Leopoldville on the 22nd to come to New York. During his journey, he stopped in Accra, the capital of Ghana, and London, and held talks with Ghanaian President Nkrumah and British Joint Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs Profumo.

### 刚果总理卢蒙巴到纽约

1960-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    刚果总理卢蒙巴到纽约
    新华社24日讯　纽约消息：刚果总理卢蒙巴今天早上乘飞机到达纽约。
    卢蒙巴是在22日离开刚果首都利奥波德维尔前来纽约的。他在旅途中曾在加纳首都阿克拉和伦敦停留，并同加纳总统恩克鲁玛和英国协办外交国务大臣普罗富莫举行了会谈。
