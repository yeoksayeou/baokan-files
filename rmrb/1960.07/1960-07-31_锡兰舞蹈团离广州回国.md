Title: Ceylonese Dance Troupe Departs Guangzhou for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-07-31
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 30th. The eight-member Ceylonese Dance Troupe, after giving three performances in Guangzhou, left Guangzhou by train today to return home. Yang Kuizhang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and people from Guangzhou's literary and art circles saw them off at the station.

The Ceylonese Dance Troupe performed dance programs rich in national characteristics in Guangzhou. Among them, the dances from the Kandy region, such as Kulu, Naduma, and Saimanala Vannama, were welcomed by the Guangzhou audience.

During their stay in Guangzhou, the Ceylonese Dance Troupe visited the site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute, which was founded by Comrade Mao Zedong in his early years, paid respects at the Guangzhou Uprising Martyrs' Cemetery, and visited the Pingzhou People's Commune in Nanhai County.

### 锡兰舞蹈团离广州回国

1960-07-31
第6版()
专栏：

    锡兰舞蹈团离广州回国
    新华社广州30日电　锡兰舞蹈团一行八人，在广州举行了三场访问演出后，今天乘火车离开广州回国。中国人民对外文化协会广州分会副秘书长杨奎章和广州文艺界人士到车站欢送。
    锡兰舞蹈团在广州演出了富有民族特色的舞蹈节目。其中康堤地区的舞蹈库鲁、纳都玛、赛玛纳拉·凡纳玛等节目，受到广州市观众的欢迎。
    锡兰舞蹈团在广州期间，曾参观了毛泽东同志早年主办的农民运动讲习所旧址，瞻仰了广州起义烈士陵园，访问了南海县平洲人民公社。
