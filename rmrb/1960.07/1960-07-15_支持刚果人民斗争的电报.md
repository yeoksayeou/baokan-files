Telegrams Supporting the Congolese People's Struggle

　　On the occasion of the Republic of the Congo's independence, the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, meeting in Stockholm, extends its warmest congratulations to the Congolese people on behalf of all peace-loving people. The Belgian government's dispatch of troops to your country under the pretext of protecting Belgian nationals is an act of aggression that violates the sovereignty of an independent nation and threatens world peace. This act of aggression has aroused the indignation of all peace-loving people, who firmly support your just struggle. We demand that the dispatch of troops to the Congo be stopped, that all foreign troops already in the Republic of the Congo be withdrawn, and that all foreign military bases there be abolished.
　　The World Peace Council will use its united strength to fight for the preservation of the Congo's independence. Stockholm, July 10, 1960

### 支持刚果人民斗争的电报

1960-07-15
第6版()
专栏：

　  支持刚果人民斗争的电报
    在刚果共和国获得独立之际，正在斯德哥尔摩开会的世界和平理事会常务委员会谨代表一切爱好和平的人民，对刚果人民致以最热烈的祝贺。比利时政府借口保护比利时侨民而派遣军队到你们的国家，这是破坏独立国家主权和威胁世界和平的侵略行动。这个侵略行动已经引起一切爱好和平的人民的愤慨，他们坚决支持你们的正义斗争。我们要求停止再派遣军队到刚果，撤退已经在刚果共和国的一切外国军队，取消在那里的一切外国军事基地。
    世界和平理事会将用它的联合一致的力量为维护刚果的独立而斗争。　1960年7月10日于斯德哥尔摩
