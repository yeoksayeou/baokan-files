Title: At the Height of the Struggle Against the Japan-U.S. Treaty, the Japanese Communist Party Will Solemnly Commemorate the Founding Day

Author:
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 4th—News: The Japanese Communist Party has decided to hold activities around July 15th throughout the country to commemorate the 38th anniversary of the founding of the JCP.

    In order to solemnly and warmly celebrate the Party's birthday amidst the unprecedented high tide of the Japanese people's struggle against the Japan-U.S. military alliance, JCP organizations in various regions are actively preparing.

    In Tokyo, the Japanese Communist Party has decided to solemnly hold the "Central Rally to Strive for the Abolition of the Security Treaty and to Strengthen the Party's Strength for the 38th Anniversary of the Founding of the JCP" on July 16.

### 在反对日美条约的斗争高潮中  日共将隆重纪念建党节日

1960-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    在反对日美条约的斗争高潮中
    日共将隆重纪念建党节日
    新华社4日讯　东京消息：日本共产党决定7月15日左右在全国各地举行纪念日共建党三十八周年的活动。
    为了在日本人民反对日美军事同盟斗争空前未有的高潮中隆重热烈地纪念党的生日，日共各地组织正在积极进行准备工作。
    在东京，日本共产党决定于7月16日隆重举行“争取废除安全条约和加强党的力量的日共建党三十八周年纪念中央大会”。
