Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet for Japanese "China Research Institute" Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet tonight to warmly welcome the Japanese "China Research Institute" delegation headed by Hirano Yoshitaro, Vice Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association and Director-General of the Japan Peace Committee. Chu Tunan and Hirano Yoshitaro spoke at the banquet.

People from various circles in the capital, including Wu Defeng, Zhang Youyu, Zhou Erfu, Zheng Senyu, Wang Xuewen, Xie Nanguang, Yong Longgui, Lin Lin, and Wang Xiaoyun, attended the banquet.

Japanese peace figure Kinkazu Saionji and Korean peace figure Hwang Bong-koo, who are in Beijing, were invited to attend the banquet.

### 楚图南欢宴日本“中国研究所”代表团

1960-07-23
第6版()
专栏：

    楚图南欢宴日本“中国研究所”代表团
    据新华社22日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎以日本中国友好协会副会长、日本和平委员会理事长平野义太郎为首的日本“中国研究所”代表团。楚图南和平野义太郎在宴会上讲了话。
    首都各方面的人士吴德峰、张友渔、周而复、郑森禹、王学文、谢南光、勇龙桂、林林、王晓云等，出席了宴会。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一、朝鲜和平人士黄凤九，应邀出席了宴会。
