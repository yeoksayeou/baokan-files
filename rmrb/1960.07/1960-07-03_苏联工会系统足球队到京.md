Title: Soviet Trade Union System Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd—The Soviet Trade Union System football team, a group of twenty people invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today. At the airport, the guests were warmly welcomed by Li Shubin, Director of the Sports Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Beijing football players.

    The first friendly match of the Soviet Trade Union System football team in Beijing is scheduled to be held on July 5th.

### 苏联工会系统足球队到京

1960-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联工会系统足球队到京
    据新华社2日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的苏联工会系统的足球队一行二十人，今天乘飞机到达北京。在飞机场上，客人们受到中华全国总工会体育部部长栗树彬和北京市足球运动员们的热烈欢迎。
    苏联工会系统的足球队在北京的第一场友谊比赛，预定7月5日举行。
