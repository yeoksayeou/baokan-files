Title: The Origin of Two Thousand Weeding Hoes
Author: Jiao Chengwen
Date: 1960-07-18
Page: 2

　　The Origin of Two Thousand Weeding Hoes
　　After the commune party committee issued a call to intensify summer weeding, Hu Yaoting, the director of the commune iron factory, received phone calls from twelve production teams requesting support for summer weeding tools. After discussion, the iron factory party branch and all employees decided to work hard overnight to produce two thousand weeding hoes.
　　All the factory workers had just studied the issue of agriculture being the foundation and industry supporting agriculture. Everyone was very enthusiastic about increasing the production of weeding hoes to support agricultural production, but the difficulty was the lack of skilled workers.
　　What to do? The factory director immediately called the Jiahe County Second Machinery Factory, which was the closest, for assistance. The Second Machinery Factory immediately sent Communist Party member Chu Huaixin and Communist Youth League member Xiang Quankui to provide support. Overnight, all the factory workers produced two thousand weeding hoes.
　　In the early morning, the workers of the commune iron factory divided into four groups and used small rubber-wheeled carts to deliver the weeding hoes to each production team. The workers delivering the weeding hoes arrived at the Beimengzhuang Production Team early in the morning. Old man Meng Xianqi, who rings the bell in the morning, was so moved by this that he couldn't speak for a moment. He grabbed the young worker Yue Guilai with both hands and repeatedly called him
"Old Brother."
　　Jiao Chengwen, Shuangmiao Commune, Jiahe County, Hebei

### 二千张耘锄的来历
缴成文
1960-07-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　二千张耘锄的来历
    公社党委发出了加紧夏锄的号召以后，公社铁工厂厂长胡耀廷先后接到十二个生产队要求支援夏锄工具的电话。铁工厂党支部和全体职工经过讨论，决定要大干一夜，生产出两千张耘锄来。
    全厂职工刚刚学习了农业是基础和工业支援农业的问题，大家对增产耘锄支援农业生产，情绪很高，但困难的是技术工人不足。
    怎么办呢？厂长马上给距离最近的交河县第二机械厂打电话求援。第二机械厂立即派共产党员褚怀信、共青团员项铨奎前往支援。一夜工夫，全厂职工制成了两千张耘锄。
    清晨，公社铁工厂的工人分四组用小胶皮车载着耘锄，分送到各生产队。送耘锄的工人大清早来到北孟庄生产队。早晨起来敲钟的孟宪棋老汉见到这事，感动得一时说不出话来，他双手拉着青年工人岳桂来，连连叫起
  “老大哥”来了。
    河北交河县双庙公社　缴成文
