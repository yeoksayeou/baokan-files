Title: US "Aid" to Turkey: Half to Be Used for Military Spending
Author:
Date: 1960-07-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ankara, 6th. The Turkish military government's Foreign Minister and the US Ambassador to Turkey signed an agreement on July 4th, stipulating that the United States will allocate one billion lira (approximately $110 million USD) to Turkey from the proceeds of selling "surplus materials" in Turkey.

This is the first agreement of this nature signed between the United States and the Turkish military government since the military coup in Turkey in May. 500 million lira of this amount will be used for military expenditure.

On the same day, Turkish Prime Minister Gursel, speaking at a gathering of newspaper publishers and editors, also asked the United States to increase its "aid" to Turkey.

### 美国给土耳其一笔“援助”款项中半数要用作军费

1960-07-08
第6版()
专栏：

    美国给土耳其一笔“援助”款项中半数要用作军费
    新华社6日讯　安卡拉消息：土耳其军政府的外交部长和美国驻土耳其大使7月4日签订一项协定，规定美国从在土耳其出售“剩余物资”所得款项中拨出十亿里拉（约一亿一千多万美元）给土耳其使用。
    这是土耳其在今年5月间发生军事政变以来，美国同土耳其军政府签订的第一个这种性质的协定。这笔款项中有五亿里拉要用作军费。
    同一天，土耳其总理古尔塞勒在报社社长和主编的集会上讲话时，还要求美国增加对土耳其的“援助”。
