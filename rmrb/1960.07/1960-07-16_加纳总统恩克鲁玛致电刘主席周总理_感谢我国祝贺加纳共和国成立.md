Title: President Nkrumah of Ghana Cables Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou to Thank China for Congratulating the Founding of the Republic of Ghana
Author:
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 15th -- President Nkrumah of Ghana telegraphed Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China on the 13th to thank him for his congratulatory telegrams on the founding of the Republic of Ghana and his assumption of the office of the first President. The text of the telegram is as follows:
To His Excellency Mr. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

	On behalf of the government and people of the Republic of Ghana, and in my own name, I extend my deepest gratitude to Your Excellency for your telegrams of congratulations, on behalf of the Chinese people and in your own name, on the founding of the Republic of Ghana and my assumption of office as the first President of the Republic.
	In response to your good wishes, I wish you personal happiness and the people of the People's Republic of China prosperity.
						Kwame Nkrumah
						President of the Republic of Ghana
						Accra, July 13, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, July 15th -- President Nkrumah of Ghana telegraphed Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China on the 13th to thank him for his congratulatory telegrams on the founding of the Republic of Ghana and his assumption of office as the first President. The text of the telegram is as follows:
To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai, Beijing, China:

	On behalf of the government and people of the Republic of Ghana, and in my own name, I express my gratitude to Your Excellency for your kind telegrams on the founding of the Republic of Ghana and my assumption of office as the first President of the Republic.
	We, like you, hope that the friendly relations between China and Ghana will be further developed.
	I wish you personal happiness and the people of the People's Republic of China prosperity and progress.
						Kwame Nkrumah
						President of the Republic of Ghana
						Accra, July 13, 1960

### 加纳总统恩克鲁玛致电刘主席周总理　感谢我国祝贺加纳共和国成立

1960-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　加纳总统恩克鲁玛致电刘主席周总理
　　感谢我国祝贺加纳共和国成立
    新华社15日讯　加纳共和国总统恩克鲁玛在13日打电报给中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，感谢在加纳共和国成立和他担任第一任总统的时候给他的贺电，电文如下：北京中华人民共和国主席刘少奇先生阁
  下：
    我谨代表加纳共和国政府和人民，并以我个人的名义，向阁下深致谢意，感谢阁下在加纳共和国成立和我就任共和国第一任总统的时候，代表中国人民并以你个人的名义来电向我们祝贺。
    为了回答你的良好祝愿，祝你个人幸福，祝中华人民共和国人民昌盛。
            加纳共和国总统
            克瓦米·恩克鲁玛
        1960年7月13日于阿克拉
    新华社15日讯　加纳共和国总统恩克鲁玛在13日打电报给中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，感谢在加纳共和国成立和他就任第一任总统的时候给他的贺电。电文如下：中国北京周恩来总理先生阁下：
    我谨代表加纳共和国政府和人民，并以我个人的名义，向阁下表示感谢，感谢阁下在加纳共和国成立和我就任共和国第一任总统的时候的亲切的来电。
    我们和你一样希望中国和加纳之间的友好关系将进一步发展。
    祝阁下个人幸福，祝中华人民共和国人民昌盛进步。
            加纳共和国总统
            克瓦米·恩克鲁玛
        1960年7月13日于阿克拉
