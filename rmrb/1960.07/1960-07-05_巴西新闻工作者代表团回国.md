Title: Brazilian Journalists Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Brazilian journalists delegation, headed by Carlos Alberto Costa Pinto, head of the Brazilian National Federation of Professional Journalists, left Beijing by plane today to return home. Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Zhu Muzhi, vice chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, Chang Zhiqing, executive director, and other heads of the Beijing press circles.

### 巴西新闻工作者代表团回国

1960-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    巴西新闻工作者代表团回国
    新华社4日讯　以巴西全国职业新闻工作者联合会负责人卡洛斯·阿耳贝托·科斯塔·平托为首的巴西新闻工作者代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之、常务理事常芝青等北京新闻界负责人。
