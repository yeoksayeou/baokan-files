Title: Chinese Athletes Wu Yonglan, Tang Hexiu, Rong Zhixuan Break Women's Parachuting World Record
Author:
Date: 1960-07-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: Chinese parachutists recently broke another world record.

During a parachuting performance in Bengbu on July 4th, Anhui Province female parachutists Wu Yonglan, Tang Hexiu, and Rong Zhixuan broke the world record of 12.39 meters for the women's 600-meter daytime team combined parachuting event (which was also China's national record) created by Li Shuhua, Li Shuhui, and Cui Xiuying on November 5, 1958, with an excellent average distance of 8.73 meters from the target center. Their individual distances from the target center were: Wu Yonglan, 3 meters; Tang Hexiu, 3.50 meters; Rong Zhixuan, 19.70 meters.

### 我国运动员武永兰、唐荷秀、荣志玄  破一项女子跳伞世界纪录

1960-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    我国运动员武永兰、唐荷秀、荣志玄
    破一项女子跳伞世界纪录
    据新华社25日讯　我国跳伞运动员最近又打破了一项世界纪录。
    7月4日在蚌埠举行的一次跳伞表演中，安徽省女子跳伞运动员武永兰、唐荷秀、荣志玄以平均距靶心八米七十三的优异成绩，打破了李淑花、李淑慧、崔秀英在1958年11月5日创造的女子六百米日间集体综合跳伞十二米三十九的世界纪录（也是我国的全国纪录）。她们个人距靶心的距离：武永兰是三米；唐荷秀是三米五十；荣志玄是十九米七十。
