Title: China's Women's Discus National Record Broken: Wang Shiyu Creates New Score of 51.10 Meters in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-07-07
Page: 6

    China's Women's Discus National Record Broken
    Wang Shiyu Creates New Score of 51.10 Meters in Moscow
    China's young female discus athlete, Wang Shiyu, broke the Chinese national women's discus record of 50.93 meters, held by Shi Baozhu since 1956, with a score of 51.10 meters in Moscow on June 29th.

    Wang Shiyu set this record at the Moscow City Athletics Championships. She officially began participating in discus in November 1958, when her score was only 41 meters. In just over a month, it quickly increased to over 45 meters, and in October 1959, it further improved to 48.82 meters. It is rare for an athlete to improve their score by more than ten meters in just over a year, entering the ranks of world-class athletes. Last year, only eight female discus athletes worldwide exceeded 51 meters.

    In this competition, Shi Baozhu's score was 47.33 meters. Chinese athletes only participate in the competition and do not compete for rankings.

### 我国女子铁饼全国纪录刷新  王士玉在莫斯科创51.10米新成绩

1960-07-07
第6版()
专栏：

    我国女子铁饼全国纪录刷新
    王士玉在莫斯科创51.10米新成绩
    中国年轻的女子铁饼运动员王士玉6月29日在莫斯科以五十一点一零米的成绩打破了自1956年以来一直被石宝珠保持的女子铁饼五十点九三米的中国全国纪录。
    王士玉是在莫斯科市田径冠军赛中创造这一纪录的。她正式参加铁饼运动是在1958年11月，当时她的成绩仅有四十一米，一个多月后就迅速增长至四十五米多，在1959年10月再次提高到四十八点八二米。在一年多的时间里，把成绩提高了十米多，进入了世界优秀运动员的行列，这是不多见的。去年一年内，全世界女子铁饼运动员成绩超过五十一米以上的仅有八人。
    在这次比赛中石宝珠的成绩是四十七点三三米。中国运动员只参加比赛，而不计名次。
