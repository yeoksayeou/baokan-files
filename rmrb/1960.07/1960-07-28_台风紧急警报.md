Title: Typhoon Emergency Alert
Author:
Date: 1960-07-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon emergency warning at 12:00 noon on the 27th:

The fifth typhoon of 1960 was centered over the East China Sea about 300 kilometers due east of Wenzhou at 2:00 a.m. today, at approximately 28 degrees north latitude and 124 degrees east longitude. The maximum wind force near the center was level 11-12. The typhoon is currently moving northwest at an accelerated speed of 8-10 knots per hour. It is expected that the center of the typhoon will make landfall in the northern Zhejiang and southern Jiangsu area this afternoon or evening. Afterward, it will enter the sea from southern Jiangsu and move northeast. It is predicted that from now until the next 48 hours, there will be level 9-12 gales and torrential rain in northeastern Zhejiang, southeastern Jiangsu, the northern East China Sea, and the southern Yellow Sea. There will be level 7-9 gales in the central Yellow Sea; other areas of Zhejiang and Jiangsu, as well as the coastal areas of Anhui and southern Shandong, will experience level 6-8 gales and heavy rain or torrential rain. The impact of the typhoon will gradually diminish after 48 hours.

### 台风紧急警报

1960-07-28
第3版()
专栏：

    台风紧急警报
　　新华社27日讯　中央气象科学研究所27日中午十二时发布台风紧急警报：
    1960年第五号台风，今天早晨二时中心位置在温州正东方三百公里左右的东海海面上，即北纬二十八度，东经一百二十四度附近，中心附近最大风力有十一—十二级，目前台风移速加快，每小时以八—十浬的速度向西北方向移动，预计今天下午到晚上，台风中心将在浙江北部、江苏南部一带登陆，以后将从苏北南部入海，向东北方向移去，预计从现在起到未来四十八小时内，浙江东北部、江苏东南部、东海北部、黄海南部将有九—十二级大风，并有暴雨，黄海中部将有七—九级大风；浙江和江苏其它地区以及安徽、山东南部沿海将有六—八级大风，并有大雨或暴雨。四十八小时之后台风影响将逐渐减小。
