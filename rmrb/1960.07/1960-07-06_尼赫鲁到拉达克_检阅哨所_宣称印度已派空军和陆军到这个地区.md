Title: Nehru Inspects "Outposts" in Ladakh, Declares India Has Sent Air Force and Army to the Region
Author:
Date: 1960-07-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 5th. According to a report by the Indian Press Trust, Indian Prime Minister Nehru left New Delhi by plane on the morning of July 2nd and arrived in Srinagar, Kashmir, and then arrived in Leh, Ladakh, on the 4th.

It is reported that Nehru arrived in Ladakh to inspect the Indian army's outposts there.

The news said that Indian Army Chief of Staff Thimayya and Air Force Chief of Staff Mukerjee accompanied Nehru.

Nehru claimed at a civic rally in Leh today that India had made arrangements to repel what he called a "new danger" from India's northern borders. Nehru also said that the Indian Air Force and Army had been stationed in Ladakh.

On July 2nd, Nehru held a one-hour and forty-minute meeting with Kashmir Premier Bakshi in Srinagar on the issue of "defending Ladakh".

### 尼赫鲁到拉达克“检阅哨所”  宣称印度已派空军和陆军到这个地区

1960-07-06
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁到拉达克“检阅哨所”
    宣称印度已派空军和陆军到这个地区
    新华社新德里5日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道，印度总理尼赫鲁在2日上午乘飞机离开新德里到达克什米尔的斯利那加，并且在4日到达拉达克的列城。
    据报道，尼赫鲁到达拉达克是为了检阅印度军队在那里的哨所。
    消息说，陪同尼赫鲁前往的有印度陆军参谋长蒂迈雅和空军参谋长慕克吉。
    尼赫鲁今天在列城的一次市民集会上声称，印度已经作出安排以击退他所说的什么来自印度北方疆界的“新的危险”。尼赫鲁还说，印度的空军和陆军已经被派驻在拉达克。
    尼赫鲁2日在斯利那加同克什米尔总理巴克希就“保卫拉达克”的问题举行了一小时四十分钟的会谈。
