Title: Sudanese Student Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st: A three-member Sudanese student delegation, led by delegation leader Ahmed Mekki, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation for a friendly visit to our country.

### 苏丹学生代表团到京

1960-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹学生代表团到京
    据新华社21日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国进行友好访问的苏丹学生代表团三人，在团长阿哈默德麦基特率领下，今天乘飞机到北京。
