Title: Chinese Muslim Hajj Delegation Arrives in Guinea for Visit
Author:
Date: 1960-07-11
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, July 8th: The People's Republic of China Muslim Hajj delegation, headed by Iminov, arrived in Conakry this morning for a friendly visit to the Republic of Guinea.

　　Kone Djigiba, Director of the Guinean Economic Affairs Bureau, and other Guinean representatives went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

　　Ke Hua, the Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 我穆斯林朝觐团到达几内亚访问

1960-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

　  我穆斯林朝觐团到达几内亚访问
    新华社科纳克里8日电　以伊敏诺夫为首的中华人民共和国穆斯林朝觐团今天上午到达科纳克里，对几内亚共和国进行友好访问。
    前往机场欢迎代表团的有几内亚经济事务局局长科内·季吉巴和几内亚其它代表。
    中国驻几内亚大使柯华也到机场欢迎。
