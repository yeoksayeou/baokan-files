Title: Khrushchev Returns to Moscow After Concluding Visit to Austria
Author:
Date: 1960-07-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 8th July: According to TASS, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, concluded his eight-day visit to Austria today and returned to Moscow by plane from Vienna today.

During his visit to Austria, Khrushchev held talks with Austrian government leaders and visited various parts of Austria. He met with a wide range of people from all walks of life in Austria.

A joint Soviet-Austrian communique was issued in Vienna today.

The communique expressed satisfaction with the development of relations between the two countries and pointed out that the two sides discussed the development of economic and cultural relations between the two countries during the talks. The communique pointed out that the two sides agreed to expand trade and cultural exchanges between the two countries, believing that this is in the interests of both sides. The communique also made some specific arrangements for expanding trade between the two countries.

The communique said that during the talks, Khrushchev informed the Austrian government of the Soviet Union's position on the issue of concluding a peace treaty with Germany and resolving the West Berlin issue on this basis.

Both sides believe that Austria's neutrality is a contribution to the cause of peace, and the Soviet Union respects Austria's neutrality and independence.

The communique emphasized the importance of the disarmament issue and pointed out that reaching an agreement on the prohibition of nuclear testing would be an important step in resolving the disarmament issue.

After concluding his visit, Khrushchev gave a television speech and held a press conference in Vienna.

### 结束在奥地利的访问  赫鲁晓夫返抵莫斯科

1960-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在奥地利的访问
    赫鲁晓夫返抵莫斯科
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫今天结束了在奥地利历时八天的访问，并且在今天从维也纳乘飞机返抵莫斯科。
    赫鲁晓夫在访问奥地利期间同奥地利政府领导人举行了会谈，并且到奥地利各地进行了参观。会见了奥地利各界的广大人士。
    今天在维也纳发表了苏奥双方会谈的公报。
    公报对两国关系的发展表示满意，并且指出，双方在会谈中讨论了发展两国之间的经济和文化关系的问题。公报指出，双方同意扩大两国间的贸易和文化交流，认为这符合于双方的利益。公报还在扩大两国的贸易方面作出了一些具体安排。
    公报说，赫鲁晓夫在会谈期间把苏联在缔结对德和约问题上和在这个基础上解决西柏林问题方面的立场告诉了奥地利政府。
    双方认为，奥地利的中立是对和平事业的贡献，苏联尊重奥地利的中立和独立。
    公报强调裁军问题的重要性，并且指出，就禁止核试验达成协议，将是解决裁军问题的一个重要步骤。
    赫鲁晓夫在结束访问后在维也纳发表了电视演说，并举行了记者招待会。
