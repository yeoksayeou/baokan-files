Title: Beijing Da Shi Lan Clothing Store Creates "Pleated Cutting Method"
Author:
Date: 1960-07-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency draft:

    The Beijing Da Shi Lan Clothing Store has created the "pleated cutting method," which, calculated on the basis of producing 5,200 pairs of trousers per month, can save the state 1,456 feet of cloth.
