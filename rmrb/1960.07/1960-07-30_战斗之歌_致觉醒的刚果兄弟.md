Title: Song of Battle – To the Awakened Congolese Brothers
Author: Wen Jie
Date: 1960-07-30
Page: 8

    Song of Battle
    ——To the Awakened Congolese Brothers
    Wen Jie
    Awake! Sleeping Congo, surge forth!
    Congo River, choked with silt, raise your spears!
    Black brothers, standing tall on the vast African plains.
    From the ancient forests, the fighting people emerge!
    Crossing high mountains and vast deserts, bare feet stomp the earth with a trembling force,
    Drumming wood drums, blowing ox horns!
    Sun, shine upon our heads,
    Black skin glimmers with eternal gold,
    Broad chests throb with strong hearts,
    Bursting forth with the sparks of a prairie fire.
    Congo, rich land!
    Giving birth to a diligent and courageous people,
    How can Congolese be whipped like cattle and horses?
    Congolese must live like human beings!
    Break the iron shackles on your ankles,
    Shake off the shackles of ten thousand pounds on your wrists,
    Brothers! Raise stones and wooden clubs,
    Severely teach the colonialists a lesson.
    Asia has an ancient saying:
    Using tigers to repel wolves can bring deeper disasters!
    Brothers! Drive out the Belgian bandits,
    Beware of American pirates taking advantage of the chaos.
    Congo, sacred territory!
    No imperialism is allowed to reach in,
    The fate of the Congolese for generations to come!
    Entirely depends on the Congolese themselves to control.
    Arise! Independent Congo, boil!
    Congo River, rushing down for thousands of miles, raise your spears!
    Black brothers, behind you stands powerful China!

### 战斗之歌——致觉醒的刚果兄弟
闻捷
1960-07-30
第8版()
专栏：

    战斗之歌
    ——致觉醒的刚果兄弟
    闻捷醒来了！沉睡的刚果，奔流吧！泥沙壅塞的刚果河，举起投枪来！黑种的兄弟，挺立在莽莽的非洲原野。从古老的森林走来了，战斗的人民！ 迈过高山大漠，赤裸的双脚踏得大地动荡，擂动木鼓，吹响了牛角！太阳呵！照耀在头顶，黑色皮肤闪烁着永恒的金色，宽阔的胸膛跳荡坚强的心，迸发出燎原的星星之火。刚果，富庶的国土啊！诞生了勤劳而又勇敢的民族，刚果人怎能像牛马被鞭挞？刚果人要像人一样生活！扭断套在脚踝上的铁镣，挣脱手腕上万斤沉重的铁锁，兄弟呵！举起石块和木棒，狠狠地教训殖民主义者。亚洲有一个古老传说：引虎拒狼会引来更深的灾祸！兄弟呵！驱逐比利时土匪，当心美国海盗趁火打劫。刚果，神圣的版图啊！不准任何帝国主义伸手插脚，刚果人子孙万代的命运啊！全靠刚果人自己来掌握。站起了！独立的刚果，沸腾吧！一泻千里的刚果河，举起投枪来！黑种的兄弟，背后屹立着强大的中国！
