Title: Ding Xilin Hosts Banquet for Japanese Fisheries Workers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd - Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Japanese Fisheries Workers Delegation led by Kazuhara Chizuru.
    People from various circles in the capital, including Zhou Erfu, Qi Mugguang, Xiao Xiangqian, Wang Yunxiang, Luo Rong, Wang Geng, and others, attended the banquet. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji also attended the banquet.
    The 16-member Japanese-China Friendship Association Fisheries Workers Delegation, led by head Kazuhara Chizuru, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 22nd.

### 丁西林欢宴日本渔业工人代表团

1960-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    丁西林欢宴日本渔业工人代表团
    据新华社23日讯　中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林今晚举行宴会，欢迎以笠原千鹤为首的日本渔业工人代表团。
    首都各方面的人士周而复、戚慕光、萧向前、王云祥、罗荣、王耕等出席了宴会。日本和平人士西园寺公一也出席了宴会。
    日中友好协会渔业工人代表团十六人，在团长笠原千鹤率领下是22日下午乘飞机到达北京的。
