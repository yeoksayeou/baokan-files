Title: Two Hungarian Writers Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 4th. Hungarian writers Láncz Jószef and Molnár Géza arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China in accordance with the 1960 Executive Plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Among those welcoming them at the airport was translator Sun Yong.

### 两位匈牙利作家到京

1960-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    两位匈牙利作家到京
    新华社4日讯　根据中匈文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的匈牙利作家兰捷尔·约瑟夫、莫尔纳·盖召，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有翻译家孙用等。
