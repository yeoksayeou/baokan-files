Title: Commune Assembles Indigenous Automobile

Author:

Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 3

    The employees of the machinery factory of the Longtoupu Commune in the suburbs of Zhuzhou City, Hunan Province, recently assembled a simple automobile using indigenous methods and waste materials. This simple automobile can travel forty to fifty *li* per hour, and each vehicle can tow one and a half to two tons of weight, and can climb slopes and hills. The power equipment of this simple automobile is equipped with a ten-horsepower old diesel engine from the commune, and the wheels are assembled from the wheels of a broken ox-drawn rubber-tired cart. (New)

### 公社装成土汽车

1960-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

    公社装成土汽车
    湖南省株洲市郊区龙头铺公社机械厂职工，最近用土办法、废材料装成了一部简易汽车。这部简易汽车每小时可走四十至五十里，每车能拖载一吨半到两吨重，能爬坡、上岭。这部简易汽车的动力设备是用公社十匹马力的旧柴油机装配的，车轮是用一部坏了的牛拉胶轮车轮子装起来的。（新）
