Title: U.S. Warships Invade Fujian Waters; I Issue 117th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-07-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 18th: On July 17th, from 17:38 to 19:35, a U.S. warship intruded into the waters south of Haitan Island, Fujian Province. On July 18th, from 16:12 to 17:14, another U.S. warship again intruded into the aforementioned waters. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. warships, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 117th serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我福建海域　我提出第117次严重警告

1960-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　美国军舰侵入我福建海域
　　我提出第117次严重警告
    新华社18日讯　7月17日十七时三十八分至十九时三十五分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省海坛岛以南地区海域。7月18日十六时十二分至十七时十四分又有美国军舰一艘，再次侵入我上述海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百一十七次严重警告。
