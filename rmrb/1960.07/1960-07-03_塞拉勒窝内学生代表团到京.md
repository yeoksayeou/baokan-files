Title: Sierra Leone Student Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd. The Sierra Leone student delegation, led by A.B. Iraha, and member Er. Er. Shanu. Wilson, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation. This delegation was sent by the Student Representative Council of Fourah Bay College.

### 塞拉勒窝内学生代表团到京

1960-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

    塞拉勒窝内学生代表团到京
    新华社2日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的塞拉勒窝内学生代表团团长阿·勃·伊拉赫和团员尔·尔·夏努·威尔逊，今天下午乘飞机到京。这个代表团是由福勒湾大学学院学生代表理事会派遣的。
