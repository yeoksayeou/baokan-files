Title: Somaliland Premier Cables Thanks to Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity
Author:
Date: 1960-07-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 1st. The Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity received a telegram from Somaliland Premier Muhammad Ibrahim Egal thanking the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity for its congratulations on Somaliland's independence.

The Somaliland Premier's telegram stated: "On behalf of the people of Somaliland, I would like to thank you for your friendly congratulations."

### 索马里兰总理电谢我亚非团结委员会

1960-07-03
第6版()
专栏：

    索马里兰总理电谢我亚非团结委员会
    新华社1日讯　中国亚非团结委员会收到了索马里兰总理穆罕默德·易卜拉欣·埃加勒发来的感谢中国亚非团结委员会祝贺索马里兰独立的电报。
    索马里兰总理的电报说：“我谨代表索马里兰人民感谢你们友好的祝贺。”
