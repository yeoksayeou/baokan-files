Title: Chinese Journalists Delegation Arrives in Chile
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, July 21st. The Chinese journalists delegation led by Zhu Muzhi, vice chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, arrived in Santiago this morning. The delegation is visiting at the invitation of the Chilean Journalists Association. Members of the delegation include Shao Zonghan, standing director of the All-China Journalists Association, and Cui Qi, commentator of "People's Daily".

### 我国新闻工作者代表团到智利

1960-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    我国新闻工作者代表团到智利
    新华社圣地亚哥21日电　由中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之率领的中国新闻工作者代表团今天清晨抵达圣地亚哥。代表团是应智利新闻工作者协会的邀请前来访问的。代表团成员包括中华全国新闻工作者协会常务理事邵宗汉和“人民日报”评论员崔奇。
