Title: Greeting the Glorious Festival of the Mongolian People: Mongolian Working People Launch a Socialist Production Emulation Drive; Beijing Railway Workers and Teachers and Students of the Affiliated High School of Beijing Normal University Hold Grand Gatherings

Author: 
Date: 1960-07-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 10th. To greet the 39th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution (July 11), working people in various parts of Mongolia are carrying out socialist production emulation drives, using outstanding labor achievements.

The working people of Gobi-Altai province, in their competition to welcome the festival, overfulfilled the task of handing over livestock products. The province overfulfilled the task of handing over wool and hair by 3% to 9.8%. In June, agricultural and pastoral cooperatives built more than 300 new livestock sheds to prepare for the wintering of livestock.

The working people of Bulgan, East Gobi, and Arkhangai provinces have also made significant achievements in handicrafts production and construction. Employees of the handicrafts production departments in East Gobi and Bulgan provinces have all completed their production plans for the first half of the year ahead of schedule and with overfulfillment on the eve of the festival.

Construction workers in Bulgan Province put forward a guarantee to complete this year's construction tasks one month ahead of schedule; now, monthly construction plans can be completed smoothly.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. On the eve of the 39th anniversary of the victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution (July 11), railway workers in Beijing and teachers and students of the First Affiliated High School of Beijing Normal University held grand gatherings to warmly celebrate this glorious festival of the Mongolian people.

Sharab, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, was invited to attend both celebrations. In his speech, Ambassador Sharab introduced the great achievements made by the Mongolian people in socialist construction under the leadership of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party over the past 39 years.

After the meeting, cultural programs were performed and films were screened.

### 迎接蒙古人民的光辉节日　蒙古劳动人民开展社会主义生产竞赛　北京铁路工人和师大附中师生隆重集会

1960-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　迎接蒙古人民的光辉节日
　　蒙古劳动人民开展社会主义生产竞赛
　　北京铁路工人和师大附中师生隆重集会
    新华社乌兰巴托10日电　蒙古各地劳动人民开展社会主义生产竞赛，以优异的劳动成就来迎接蒙古人民革命三十九周年纪念日（7月11日）。
    戈壁阿尔泰省劳动人民在迎接节日的竞赛中，超额完成了畜产品的上缴任务。全省超额3％到9.8％完成绒、毛上缴任务。6月间，农牧业合作社新建了三百多个牲畜棚圈，为牲畜过冬做好准备。
    布尔干、东戈壁、后杭爱等省的劳动人民在手工业生产和建筑工作中也取得了显著的成绩。东戈壁和布尔干省手工业生产部门的职工在节日前夕都提前和超额完成了上半年的生产计划。
    布尔干省的建筑工人提出了提前一个月完成今年的建筑任务的保证；现在，每个月的施工计划都能顺利完成。
    新华社10日讯　在蒙古人民革命胜利三十九周年（7月11日）的前夕，北京市的铁路工人和北京师范大学第一附属中学的师生分别举行隆重集会，热烈庆祝蒙古人民这一光辉的节日。
    蒙古人民共和国驻华大使沙拉布应邀先后出席了两个庆祝会。沙拉布大使在讲话中介绍了三十九年来蒙古人民在蒙古人民革命党的领导下，在社会主义建设中所取得的巨大成就。
    会后，分别演出了文艺节目和放映了电影。
