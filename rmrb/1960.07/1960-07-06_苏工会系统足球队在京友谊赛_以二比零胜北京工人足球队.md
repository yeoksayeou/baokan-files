Title: Soviet Trade Union System Football Team Plays Friendly Match in Beijing, Wins 2-0 Against Beijing Workers Football Team
Author:
Date: 1960-07-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 5th: The Soviet Union's trade union system football team played its first friendly match in China tonight against the Beijing Workers Football Team. The visiting team won with a score of 2-0. The score at halftime was 1-0.

After the ninety-minute match, Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, met with the athletes from both sides on the field.

The visiting team will leave Beijing tomorrow to visit and play matches in Lüda.

### 苏工会系统足球队在京友谊赛  以二比零胜北京工人足球队

1960-07-06
第5版()
专栏：

    苏工会系统足球队在京友谊赛
    以二比零胜北京工人足球队
    据新华社5日讯　苏联工会系统的足球队今晚在北京同北京工人足球队举行访华第一场友谊比赛，结果客队以二比零获胜。上半场比数为一比零。
    九十分钟比赛结束后，中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜到球场上接见了双方的运动员。
    客队将在明天离北京去旅大进行访问比赛。
