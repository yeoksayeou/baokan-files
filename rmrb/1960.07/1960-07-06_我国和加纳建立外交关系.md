Title: China and Ghana Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Joint Communiqué on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Ghana.
    The Government of the Republic of Ghana and the Government of the People's Republic of China have agreed to establish diplomatic relations and to exchange diplomatic representatives at the ambassadorial level.

### 我国和加纳建立外交关系

1960-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和加纳建立外交关系
    新华社5日讯　中华人民共和国和加纳共和国关于建立外交关系的联合公报中华人民共和国政府和加纳共和国政府已经同意建立外交关系，并且互派大使级的外交代表。
