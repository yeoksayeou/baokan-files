Title: Japanese National Relief Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd—The Japanese National Relief Association delegation, consisting of nine members led by Mitsuo Okamoto, Secretary-General of the Japanese National Relief Association, arrived in Beijing by plane today from Guangzhou at the invitation of the China People's Relief Association.

### 日本国民救援会代表团抵京

1960-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    日本国民救援会代表团抵京
    新华社22日讯　应中国人民救济总会邀请前来中国访问的日本国民救援会访华代表团一行九人，在日本国民救援会事务局长冈本光雄率领下，今天从广州乘机抵北京。
