Title: Chen Yi Receives Gabiton

Author:

Date: 1960-07-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 14th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Mr. René Gabiton, a well-known French social activist and professor at the Faculty of Law and Economics of the University of Paris, and his wife this morning. They had a friendly conversation. Present at the meeting were Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Song Zhiguang, Deputy Director of the Department of Western European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理接见加比唐

1960-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见加比唐
    新华社14日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了正在我国访问的法国著名社会活动家、巴黎大学法律及经济科学系教授雷纳·加比唐和他的夫人，进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长王明远，外交部西欧司副司长宋之光。
