Title: Beijing Television Station and Nippon Television Network Corporation to Exchange and Purchase Television Newsreels

Author:

Date: 1960-07-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th. During their visit to China, Mr. Yasuo Yanagisawa, Chairman of the Board of Directors of Nippon Television Network Corporation, and Mr. Kouzo Sakurai, Managing Director, held discussions with representatives of Beijing Television Station on the issue of mutual exchange and purchase of Chinese and Japanese television newsreels, and signed relevant agreements and contracts today.

### 北京电视台和日本电波新闻社  将互相交换、购买电视新闻片

1960-07-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　北京电视台和日本电波新闻社
　  将互相交换、购买电视新闻片
    新华社17日讯　日本电波新闻社董事长柳泽恭雄和常务董事樱井功三，在中国访问期间，和北京电视台的代表就双方互相交换、购买中国和日本电视新闻片的问题进行了商谈，并于今日签订了有关的协定和合同。
