Title: Soviet "Textile Worker" Football Team Leaves Beijing for Home
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd. The Soviet Ivanovo City Colored Cotton United Factory "Textile Worker" football team, a group of twenty people led by head Ni. Nikulishen, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

### 苏联“纺织工人”足球队离京返国

1960-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联“纺织工人”足球队离京返国
    新华社22日讯　苏联伊万诺沃市有色棉联合工厂“纺织工人”足球队一行二十人，在团长尼·尼库利申率领下，于今晨乘机离京返国。
