Title: German Football Team Draws with Beijing Team; Vice Premier Chen Yi Watches the Match and Takes Photos with Players
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-27
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, July 26th. The Berlin "Dynamo" football team from the German Democratic Republic played its first friendly match in China against the Beijing football team tonight at the Beijing Workers' Stadium. After ninety minutes of fierce fighting, the two sides drew with a score of zero to zero.
    Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi, watched tonight's game. After the game, he went to the field and shook hands with all the athletes from both sides and took photos with them.
    The Berlin "Dynamo" football team is about to visit Tianjin and is scheduled to hold a friendly match with the Tianjin football team on the 28th.

### 德足球队同北京队踢成平局  陈毅副总理观看比赛并同队员合影

1960-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    德足球队同北京队踢成平局
    陈毅副总理观看比赛并同队员合影
    据新华社26日讯　德意志民主共和国柏林“狄纳莫”足球队，今晚在北京工人体育场同北京足球队进行访华第一场友谊比赛，经过九十分钟激战，双方以零比零踢成平局。
    国务院副总理陈毅观看了今晚的球赛，比赛结束后还到球场和双方运动员一一握手，并同他们一起合影。
    柏林“狄纳莫”足球队即将去天津访问，预定28日同天津足球队举行友谊赛。
