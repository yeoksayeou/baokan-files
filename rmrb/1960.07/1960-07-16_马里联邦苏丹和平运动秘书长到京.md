Title: Secretary-General of the Sudanese Peace Movement of the Mali Federation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 15th: Diallo Mohosa Sane, Secretary-General of the Sudanese Peace Movement of the Mali Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

### 马里联邦苏丹和平运动秘书长到京

1960-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

　  马里联邦苏丹和平运动秘书长到京
　  据新华社15日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的马里联邦苏丹和平运动秘书长迪亚洛·摩沙·萨内，今日乘飞机到达北京。
