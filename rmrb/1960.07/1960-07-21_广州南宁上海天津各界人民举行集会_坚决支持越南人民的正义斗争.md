Title: Guangzhou, Nanning, Shanghai, and Tianjin People from All Walks of Life Hold Rallies to Firmly Support the Just Struggle of the Vietnamese People
Author:
Date: 1960-07-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th: People from all walks of life in Guangzhou, Nanning, Shanghai, and Tianjin held rallies to firmly support the just struggle of the Vietnamese people against U.S. imperialist aggression and for peaceful reunification of their country.

　　Over 1,200 people from all walks of life in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City held a rally this morning. Lin Qiangyun, Standing Committee Member of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, and Chairman of the Guangdong Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, spoke at the rally. He said: At present, the heroic Vietnamese people are launching a massive struggle against U.S. imperialism and its running dogs in order to achieve the peaceful reunification of Vietnam. We, the people of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, together with the people of the whole country, firmly support the just struggle of the Vietnamese people and express our highest respect to the heroic and unyielding Vietnamese people who are waging this struggle.

　　Subsequently, the masses warmly welcomed the speech by Le Chung Thuy, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Guangzhou. After listing the various crimes committed by U.S. imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique in South Vietnam, he introduced the heroic and tenacious struggle of the Vietnamese people. Le Chung Thuy said that our just struggle has received the support of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, as well as the support of the people of Asian and African countries and peace-loving people of the world. The Chinese people have always warmly sympathized with and supported our people's just struggle. We firmly believe that our struggle will surely achieve final victory.

　　Fan Jingfu, Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, spoke on behalf of the people's organizations in Guangzhou, and Xiao Junying, Deputy Director of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, spoke on behalf of the various democratic parties.

　　Leading members of the Party, government, various democratic parties, and people's organizations of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City attended the meeting.

　　Tran Van Thong, Deputy Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Guangzhou, as well as Vietnamese students and interns in Guangzhou, were also invited to attend the meeting.

　　Over 1,300 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Nanning City held a rally this morning. Liu Guoping, Chairman of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Federation of Trade Unions, emphasized in his speech that the 20 million people of Guangxi are very angry at the brutal rule of U.S. imperialism in South Vietnam and its various provocative activities in Indochina. They unanimously condemn the criminal activities of U.S. imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique and resolutely support the patriotic and just struggle of the Vietnamese people against U.S. imperialist aggression and the peaceful reunification of their country. Cao Trong Gian, the person in charge of the Consulate of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Nanning, was invited to speak at the meeting. He introduced the struggle of the Vietnamese people against the U.S.-Ngo clique. He expressed his firm belief that under the leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Vietnamese people, united as one and fighting resolutely, and with the support of the socialist camp and peace-loving people all over the world, will surely achieve victory in the cause of peaceful reunification of their country.

　　In Shanghai, students from eight universities and secondary specialized schools, including Jiaotong University and the Fisheries College, held a rally on the evening of the 19th. Vietnamese students studying in Shanghai were also invited to participate. Chen Tinglai, Chairman of the Student Union of Jiaotong University, first spoke at the meeting, saying that the Chinese and Vietnamese people are close brothers who share weal and woe and are as closely related as lips and teeth. We firmly support the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. imperialist aggression and for peaceful reunification of their country. Nguyen Lam Hoai, a representative of Vietnamese students, then spoke at the meeting. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Chinese people for their support of the Vietnamese people's just struggle.

　　Over 300 people from all walks of life in Tianjin held a rally this morning. All Vietnamese students and interns in Tianjin were invited to participate. Han Jinrong, Chairman of the Trade Union of Tianjin No. 1 Steel Plant, spoke at the meeting. Wu Lihai, a representative of Vietnamese students and interns, was also invited to speak at the meeting.

### 广州南宁上海天津各界人民举行集会  坚决支持越南人民的正义斗争

1960-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

    广州南宁上海天津各界人民举行集会
    坚决支持越南人民的正义斗争
    据新华社20日讯　广州、南宁、上海、天津各界人民举行集会，坚决支持越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略、和平统一祖国的正义斗争。
    广东省、广州市各界人民一千二百多人，今天上午举行集会，中共广东省委常委、广东省副省长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广东省分会主席林锵云讲话。他说：目前，英勇的越南人民为了实现越南的和平统一，正在掀起一个规模巨大的，反对美帝国主义及其走狗的斗争风暴。我们广东省、广州市各界人民和全国人民一起，坚决支持越南人民的正义斗争，并向英勇地不屈不挠地进行斗争的越南人民致以崇高的敬意。
    接着，到会群众热烈欢迎越南民主共和国驻广州领事黎终始讲话。他列举了美帝国主义和吴庭艳集团在越南南方犯下的种种罪行后，介绍了越南人民不断进行英勇顽强斗争的情况。黎终始说，我们的正义斗争得到了以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的支持，得到了亚非国家人民和世界爱好和平人民的支持。中国人民一向热烈同情和支持我国人民的正义斗争。我们坚信，我们的斗争一定会获得最后的胜利。
    广州市总工会主席范劲夫代表广州市各界人民团体，民革广东省委员会副主任委员肖隽英代表各民主党派，都在会上讲了话。
    广东省和广州市的党、政府以及各民主党派，各人民团体的负责人都参加了大会。
    越南民主共和国驻广州副领事陈文通以及在广州的越南留学生、实习生，也应邀参加了大会。
    南宁市各族各界一千三百多人今天上午举行集会。广西僮族自治区总工会主席刘国平在会上强调指出，广西两千万人民，对美帝国主义在越南南方的残暴统治和在印度支那的种种挑衅活动，感到非常愤怒，一致声讨美帝国主义和吴庭艳集团的罪恶活动，坚决支持越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略、和平统一祖国的爱国正义斗争。越南民主共和国驻南宁领事馆负责人高仲涧应邀在大会上讲了话。他介绍了越南人民反对美吴集团的斗争情况。他表示坚信，越南人民在越南劳动党的领导下，团结一致、坚决斗争，在社会主义阵营各国及全世界爱好和平人民的支持下，和平统一祖国的事业必定取得胜利。
    在上海，交通大学、水产学院等八所高等学校和中等专业学校的学生在19日晚上举行集会，在上海学习的越南留学生也应邀参加。交通大学学生会主席陈廷莱首先在会上讲话说，中国人民同越南人民是休戚与共、唇齿相依的亲兄弟，我们坚决支持越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略、和平统一祖国的斗争。越南留学生代表阮林槐接着在会上讲话。他对中国人民给予越南人民正义斗争的支持表示衷心感谢。
    天津市各界三百多人在今天上午集会，在天津的越南留学生、实习生全体应邀参加了集会。天津第一钢厂工会主席韩锦荣在会上讲了话，越南留学生和实习生代表武立海也应邀在会上讲了话。
