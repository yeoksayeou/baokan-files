Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Telegram to Central Committee of the Martinique Communist Party to Congratulate on the Success of the Martinique Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-07-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Martinique Communist Party, congratulating the opening of the Second Congress of the Martinique Communist Party scheduled for July 30. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Martinique Communist Party,
To the Second Congress of the Martinique Communist Party,
Dear Comrades:

On behalf of all members of the Communist Party of China, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm fraternal congratulations to the Second Congress of the Martinique Communist Party, and through the Congress, expresses cordial greetings to all members of the Martinique Communist Party and the working people of Martinique.

The Martinique Communist Party has unremittingly struggled to unite the working people and progressive democratic forces of Martinique, oppose colonialism, strive for the independence and autonomy of Martinique, strive for democracy, social progress, and the rights and interests of the working people, and has achieved many successes in the struggle, thus playing an increasingly important role in the political life of Martinique. The Communist Party of China and the Chinese people are very excited about your achievements.

We wish your Congress complete success, and we wish you new achievements in leading and uniting all the working people of Martinique in the struggle for national independence and democratic freedom, and in fighting shoulder to shoulder with the people of Latin America against the war forces headed by U.S. imperialism and defending world peace!

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
July 23, 1960

### 中共中央电马提尼克共产党中央委员会  祝马提尼克共产党代表大会成功

1960-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电马提尼克共产党中央委员会
    祝马提尼克共产党代表大会成功
    新华社29日讯　中国共产党中央委员会致电马提尼克共产党中央委员会，祝贺马提尼克共产党定于7月30日举行的第二次代表大会的开幕。电报全文如下：马提尼克共产党中央委员会转马提尼克共产党第二次代表大会亲爱的同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会代表中国共产党全体党员，向马提尼克共产党第二次代表大会表示热烈的兄弟般的祝贺，并通过大会向马提尼克共产党全体党员和马提尼克劳动人民表示亲切的问候。
    马提尼克共产党不懈地为团结马提尼克劳动人民和进步民主力量、反对殖民主义、争取马提尼克的独立自主、争取民主、社会进步和劳动人民权益而斗争，在斗争中取得许多成就，因而在马提尼克政治生活中起着日益重要作用。中国共产党和中国人民对你们的成就感到非常兴奋。
    祝你们的代表大会圆满成功，祝你们在领导与团结马提尼克全体劳动人民为争取民族独立和民主自由的斗争中，在同拉丁美洲人民肩并肩为反对以美帝国主义为首的战争势力、保卫世界和平的斗争中，获得新的成就！
      中国共产党中央委员会
              1960年7月23日
