Title: National Federation of Literary and Art Circles Group Discussions Conclude; Congress Will Transition to Representatives' Meetings or Expanded Council Meetings of Various Associations, Research Societies, Liaison Societies, and Academic Societies
Author:
Date: 1960-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: The Third National Congress of Literary and Art Workers concluded its group discussions today. Over the past three days, the delegates engaged in enthusiastic discussions regarding the congratulatory speech delivered by Comrade Lu Dingyi on behalf of the CCP Central Committee and the State Council, and the report by Comrade Zhou Yang entitled "The Road of Socialist Literature and Art," and unanimously expressed their support.

The congress will transition to representatives' meetings or expanded council meetings of various associations, research societies, liaison societies, and academic societies.

### 全国文代会分组讨论结束  大会将转入各协会、研究会、联谊会、学会的代表会或理事扩大会

1960-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    全国文代会分组讨论结束
    大会将转入各协会、研究会、联谊会、学会的代表会或理事扩大会
    新华社27日讯　全国文学艺术工作者第三次代表大会分组讨论今日结束。在过去三天中，代表们对陆定一同志代表中共中央和国务院所作的祝词，对周扬同志所作的题为“社会主义文学艺术的道路”的报告，进行了热烈的讨论，并一致表示拥护。
    大会将转入各协会、研究会、联谊会、学会的代表大会或理事扩大会。
