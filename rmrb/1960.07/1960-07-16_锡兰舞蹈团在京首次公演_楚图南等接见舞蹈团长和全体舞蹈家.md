Title: Ceylon Dance Troupe's First Performance in Beijing; Chu Tunan and Others Meet with Troupe Leader and All Dancers
Author:
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 14th: The capital's audience joyfully appreciated the wonderful performance of the dancers from Ceylon tonight.

The Ceylon Dance Troupe held its first performance in China tonight, and people from all walks of life in the capital, including Chu Tunan, Ding Xilin, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Hanfu, Geng Biao, and Zhou Erfu, watched the performance. Also watching the performance with them was Ceylon's Ambassador to China, Gooneratne. The Ceylonese artists performed a total of twenty programs tonight, including South Ceylon Dance, Kandy Dance, Prayer Dance, Harvest Dance, Double Drum Dance, Elephant Dance, and Eagle Dance.

During the intermission, Chu Tunan and others met with the leader and all the dancers of the dance troupe.

### 锡兰舞蹈团在京首次公演　楚图南等接见舞蹈团长和全体舞蹈家

1960-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

　  锡兰舞蹈团在京首次公演
　　楚图南等接见舞蹈团长和全体舞蹈家
    据新华社14日讯　首都观众今晚欢快地欣赏了来自锡兰的舞蹈家们的精彩演出。
    锡兰舞蹈团今晚举行来华首次公演，首都各界人士楚图南、丁西林、张奚若、章汉夫、耿飚、周而复等观看了演出。和他们一起观看演出的有锡兰驻中国大使高伯拉瓦。锡兰艺术家们今晚共演出了南锡兰舞蹈、康堤舞蹈、祈祷舞、收割舞、双鼓舞、象舞、鹰舞等二十个节目。
    在演出休息期间，楚图南等接见了舞蹈团长和全体舞蹈家。
