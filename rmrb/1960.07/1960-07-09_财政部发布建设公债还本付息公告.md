Title: Ministry of Finance Announces Redemption and Interest Payment of National Construction Bonds
Author:
Date: 1960-07-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Ministry of Finance of the People's Republic of China announces:

In accordance with the provisions of the National Economic Construction Bond Redemption Lottery Conference on February 25, 1959, concerning the one-time lottery redemption of bonds each year, the winning numbers for the 1954-1958 National Economic Construction Bonds to be redeemed and paid interest on in 1960 are hereby announced as follows:

1954 National Economic Construction Bonds
    63 75 95 27 83  96　67 53 52 49 19 32　57　47　44
1955 National Economic Construction Bonds
    65 52 92 45 49  66  02 68 26 96
1956 National Economic Construction Bonds
    32 36 27 15 91
1957 National Economic Construction Bonds
    00 99 28 22 98
1958 National Economic Construction Bonds
    35 47 94 19 33

Any National Economic Construction Bonds of various denominations issued in each year, whose numbers end in the same two digits as the above listed numbers, are the bonds that have won the lottery for redemption and interest payment this year. The principal and interest to be paid will be redeemed by the People's Bank of China from September 30th.

The interest on the unredeemed National Economic Construction Bonds issued from 1954 to 1957 will be redeemed by the People's Bank of China from September 30th; the interest on the unredeemed National Economic Construction Bonds issued in 1958 will be paid in full at the time of principal redemption, as stipulated.

The National Economic Construction Bonds issued in 1954 and 1955 are all in old currency denominations. When redeeming principal and interest, they should be converted to new currency according to the statutory parity.

This is hereby announced.　　Minister of Finance　Li Xiannian　July 5, 1960

### 财政部发布建设公债还本付息公告

1960-07-09
第3版()
专栏：

    财政部发布建设公债还本付息公告
    新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国财政部公告
    根据1959年2月25日国家经济建设公债还本抽签大会关于各年公债一次抽签分期还本的规定，现将1954年—1958年国家经济建设公债应在1960年还本付息的中签号码公布如下：
    1954年国家经济建设公债
    63 75 95 27 83  96　67 53 52 49 19 32　57　47　44
    1955年国家经济建设公债
    65 52 92 45 49  66  02 68 26 96
    1956年国家经济建设公债
    32 36 27 15 91
    1957年国家经济建设公债
    00 99 28 22 98
    1958年国家经济建设公债
    35 47 94 19 33
    各年发行的各种面额的国家经济建设公债券，它的号码末尾两字，凡和上列号码相同的，就是今年中签还本付息的公债券。应当还付的本金和利息，自9月30日起由中国人民银行兑付。
    1954年—1957年发行的未中签国家经济建设公债券的利息，自9月30日起由中国人民银行兑付；1958年发行的未中签国家经济建设公债券的利息，按规定在偿还本金时一次付给。
    1954年和1955年发行的国家经济建设公债券，都是旧币面额，兑付本息时，应当按法定比价折合新币计算。
              特此公告　财政部部长　李先念　1960年7月5日
