Title: Both Sides Adopt a Spirit of Friendly Cooperation to Jointly Negotiate Issues: The First Meeting of the First Team of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Survey Team Successfully Concludes; Burmese Delegation Headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi Leaves Wan-t'ing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 15th: The first meeting of the first team of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Survey Team, held in Wan-t'ing, China, successfully concluded on the 14th.

　　Based on the arrangements made by the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee at its first meeting in Rangoon for joint survey work, the personnel of the survey teams from both sides conducted more detailed research in light of the specific circumstances, and made more specific regulations on the steps of the joint survey work, the time to set the boundary, and the date of completion. Due to the spirit of friendly cooperation and consultation on issues adopted by both sides, the meeting proceeded very smoothly, and the attendees from both sides expressed extreme satisfaction with the results of the meeting.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 15th: The Burmese delegation headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee, who came to Wan-t'ing, Yunnan Province, China, to participate in the first meeting of the first team of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Survey Team, left Wan-t'ing on the afternoon of the 14th to return home after the successful conclusion of the meeting.

　　Those who went to the Wan-t'ing bridgehead to bid farewell included: Liu Minghui, Deputy Governor of Yunnan Province; Yao Zhongming, the chief representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee; Major General Li Xifu, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Yunnan; Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee; Li Linwu, Lei Chunguo (Jingpo nationality), and Si Weizhang (Dai nationality), Deputy Governors of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture; personnel of the Chinese delegation to the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee; and four hundred people of all ethnic groups from Wan-t'ing Township.

　　Before the Burmese guests left, Li Linwu, Deputy Governor of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture, hosted a banquet to see them off. At the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to congratulate the success of the meeting. Brigadier General Aung Gyi and other Burmese guests visited Mangshi, the capital of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture, on the 13th.

### 双方采取友好合作精神共同协商问题　中缅边界勘察队第一队首次会议圆满结束　以昂季准将为首的缅甸代表团离畹町返国

1960-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　双方采取友好合作精神共同协商问题
　　中缅边界勘察队第一队首次会议圆满结束
　　以昂季准将为首的缅甸代表团离畹町返国
　  新华社昆明15日电　中缅边界联合勘察队第一队在中国畹町举行的首次会议，已经在14日圆满结束。
    双方勘察队的人员，根据中缅边界联合委员会在仰光举行的第一次会议对联合勘察工作的安排，结合具体情况，进行了更细致的研究，对联合勘察工作的步骤、上界时间以及完成的日期，作了更具体的规定。由于双方采取友好合作协商问题的精神，会议进行得很顺利，双方到会人员，对会议的成果都表示极为满意。
    新华社昆明15日电　前来我国云南省畹町参加中缅边界联合勘察队第一队首次会议的以中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表昂季准将为首的缅甸代表团，在会议圆满结束以后，已经在十四日下午离开畹町返国。
    前往畹町桥头欢送的有：云南省副省长刘明辉，中缅边界联合委员会我方首席代表姚仲明，中国人民解放军驻云南部队首长黎锡福少将，云南省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，德宏傣族景颇族自治州副州长李临五、雷春国（景颇族）、思伟章（傣族），以及中缅联合委员会我方代表团人员和畹町镇的四百名各族群众。
    在缅甸客人临行前，德宏傣族景颇族自治州副州长李临五曾设宴为他们饯行。宴会上，宾主频频举杯祝贺会议的成功。昂季准将等缅甸客人们十三日参观了德宏傣族景颇族自治州首府芒市。
