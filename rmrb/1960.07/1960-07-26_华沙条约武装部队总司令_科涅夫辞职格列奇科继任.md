Title: Commander-in-Chief of the Warsaw Treaty Armed Forces Konev Resigns, Grechko Succeeds Him
Author:
Date: 1960-07-26
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 25th. TASS Moscow, July 25th: The Political Consultative Committee of the Warsaw Treaty Member States has accepted the request of Soviet Marshal Konev to be relieved of his duties as Commander-in-Chief of the Joint Armed Forces of the Warsaw Treaty Member States due to health reasons.
　　Soviet Marshal Grechko has been appointed Commander-in-Chief of the Joint Armed Forces.

### 华沙条约武装部队总司令  科涅夫辞职格列奇科继任

1960-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    华沙条约武装部队总司令
    科涅夫辞职格列奇科继任
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社莫斯科25日讯：华沙条约缔约国政治协商委员会接受了苏联元帅科涅夫的请求，由于健康关系免除他担任的华沙条约缔约国联合武装部队总司令的职务。
    苏联元帅格列奇科被任命为联合武装部队总司令。
