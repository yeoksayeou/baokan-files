Title: Chinese Delegation of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee Returns to Kunming
Author:
Date: 1960-07-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, July 6th. Yao Zhongming, chief representative of the Chinese delegation to the first meeting of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee held in Rangoon, representatives Cheng Zhiping, Cheng Xueyu, Chen Yanbin, Huang Fenglin, as well as advisors and accompanying staff, a total of thirteen people, returned to Kunming by plane this afternoon from Rangoon after the successful conclusion of the meeting.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee; Dao Jingban (Dai nationality), Vice Chairman of the Provincial Political Consultative Conference and Governor of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture; Hu Zhonghua (Kawa nationality), member of the Provincial People's Committee and Deputy Commissioner of the Lincang Special District; Hu Yutang (Kawa nationality), Standing Committee member of the Provincial Political Consultative Conference; Li Ping, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Provincial People's Committee; and Senior Colonel Zhu Jiabi, head of the PLA troops stationed in Kunming.

U Maung Maung So, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and Vice-Consul U Hla Tun, also went to the airport to welcome them.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, July 6th. The Chinese delegation to the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee, headed by Yao Zhongming, left Rangoon by plane this morning to return home after holding a ten-day meeting with the Burmese delegation headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Brigadier General Aung Gyi, chief representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces; U Kyaw Winn, representative of the Burmese side and Burmese Ambassador to China; Colonel So Min, Director of the Border Areas Administration; U San Lunn, Secretary of the Kachin State Affairs Department; U Khun Ohn Kya, Commissioner of the Northern Shan State; as well as U Khin Nyo, advisor to the Burmese side and Deputy Director of the Burmese Border Areas Administration.

Li Yimang, Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and personnel from the embassy also went to the airport to see them off.

### 中缅联合委员会中国代表团回到昆明

1960-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅联合委员会中国代表团回到昆明
    新华社昆明6日电　出席在仰光举行的中缅边界联合委员会首次会议的中国代表团首席代表姚仲明，代表程之平，成学俞、陈雁彬、黄凤麟以及顾问和随行人员一行十三人，在会议胜利结束之后，今天下午由仰光乘飞机回到昆明。
    前往机场欢迎的，有云南省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，省政协副主席、德宏傣族景颇族自治州州长刀京版（傣族），省人民委员会委员、临沧专区副专员胡忠华（佧佤族），省政协常务委员胡玉堂（佧佤族），省人民委员会外事处处长李萍，解放军驻昆明部队首长朱家璧大校等。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭、副领事吴拉吞，也到机场欢迎。
    新华社仰光6日电　以姚仲明为首的中缅联合委员会中国方面代表在同以昂季准将为首的缅甸方面代表举行十天会议后，今天上午乘飞机离仰光回国。
    到机场欢送的有中缅联合委员会缅甸方面首席代表、缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季准将，缅甸方面的代表、缅甸驻中国大使叫温，边境地区管理局局长苏敏上校，克钦邦事务部秘书吴山伦，北掸邦专员吴昆翁佳，以及缅甸方面的顾问、缅甸边境地区管理局副局长吴钦纽等。
    中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和大使馆人员也到机场送行。
