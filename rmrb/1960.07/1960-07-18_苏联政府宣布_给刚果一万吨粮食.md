Title: Soviet Government Announces Granting 10,000 Tons of Grain to Congo
Author:
Date: 1960-07-18
Page: 5

　　Soviet Government Announces
　　Granting 10,000 Tons of Grain to Congo
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 17th: TASS reported that Soviet Premier Khrushchev announced in a letter to Congolese Prime Minister Lumumba on July 16th that the Soviet government has decided to immediately grant the Republic of Congo 10,000 tons of grain as aid and has notified the United Nations Secretary-General of this matter.
    In the letter, Khrushchev pointed out that the Soviet government believes this assistance reflects its sympathy for the Congo and its legitimate government, and also reflects its determination to assist the Republic of Congo in achieving victory in its just cause of struggling to safeguard its independence.
    The aid grain will be rapidly transported by aircraft and a dedicated Soviet ship.

### 苏联政府宣布　给刚果一万吨粮食

1960-07-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联政府宣布
　　给刚果一万吨粮食
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫16日在给刚果共和国总理卢蒙巴的信中宣布，苏联政府决定立即给予刚果共和国以一万吨粮食的援助，并且已将这件事通知给联合国秘书长。
    赫鲁晓夫在信中指出，苏联政府认为这种援助反映了它对刚果及其合法政府的同情，同时也反映了它决心协助刚果共和国为维护自己独立而为之奋斗的正义事业取得胜利。
    援助的粮食将由飞机和一艘专用的苏联船只迅速运到。
