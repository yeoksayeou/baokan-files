Title: Lebanese "Racing Star" Football Team Plays Against Beijing Team; Beijing Team Wins Four to One
Author:
Date: 1960-07-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, July 20th: The Lebanese "Racing Star" football team played its second friendly match of their visit to China against the Beijing football team tonight in Beijing, resulting in a four-to-one victory for the Beijing team. The visiting team led one-to-zero in the first half. The Beijing evening, after the rain, was very refreshing, and approximately 50,000 spectators watched the match. The visiting team will also play a friendly match against the "Lokomotiv" Sports Association football team in Beijing on the 23rd.

### 黎巴嫩“雷星”足球队同北京队对垒  北京队以四比一获胜

1960-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩“雷星”足球队同北京队对垒
    北京队以四比一获胜
    新华社20日讯　黎巴嫩“雷星”足球队今晚在北京同北京足球队举行了访华的第二场友谊比赛，结果北京队以四比一获胜。上半场客队以一比零领先。雨后初晴的北京夜晚，天气十分清爽，约有五万名观众观看了这场比赛。客队还将于23日在北京同“火车头”体育协会足球队举行友谊比赛。
