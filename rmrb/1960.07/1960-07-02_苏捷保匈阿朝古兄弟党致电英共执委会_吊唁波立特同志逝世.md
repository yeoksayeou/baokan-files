Title: Soviet, Czech, Bulgarian, Hungarian, Albanian, Korean, and Cuban fraternal parties send telegrams to the Executive Committee of the British Communist Party, mourning the death of Comrade Pollitt
Author:
Date: 1960-07-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The Central Committees of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, the First Secretary of the Albanian Labor Party, Hoxha, the Korean Workers' Party, and the Central Committee of the Cuban People's Socialist Party sent telegrams to the Executive Committee of the British Communist Party, mourning the death of British Communist Party Chairman Pollitt.

    The telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union stated that Harry Pollitt's life was devoted to tirelessly fighting for a bright future for the British people, for the national interests of his homeland, and for friendship between all peoples.

    The telegram stated that the Soviet people will forever remember the brilliant image of Harry Pollitt, a loyal friend of the Soviet Union.

    The telegram also pointed out that Harry Pollitt waged an unwavering struggle for the unity of the British working class, the international communist and workers' movement, and for peace and socialism.

    The telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia stated that Pollitt's life and work were inextricably linked to the international communist movement and the British Communist Party's struggle to defend the interests of the British working people, to defend peace, socialism, and to strive for the victory of Marxism-Leninism.

    The telegram from the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party stated that Pollitt's life and work were an example of selfless service to the workers and all working people of Britain, and to the cause of peace, democracy, and socialism.

    The telegram from the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party stated that Pollitt dedicated his entire life to the cause of the British and international working class. His death is not only a great loss to the British Communist Party but also to the international workers' movement and those who fight for socialism.

    The telegram from the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Hoxha, stated that Pollitt was worthy of being a son of the British working class, and he dedicated his entire life and great energy entirely to the cause of the liberation of the working class, and to democracy and socialism.

    The telegram from the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party expressed deep sorrow over the death of Pollitt.

    The telegram stated that as an outstanding fighter of the international labor movement, Pollitt dedicated his life to the cause of the working class and the struggle to defend the vital interests of the British working people.

    The telegram from the Central Committee of the Cuban People's Socialist Party stated that Pollitt was the leader of the British Communist Party, a loyal son of the British working class, and a great fighter for the independence, peace, and socialism of all peoples. The telegram expressed deep sorrow over Pollitt's death.

### 苏捷保匈阿朝古兄弟党致电英共执委会  吊唁波立特同志逝世

1960-07-02
第6版()
专栏：

    苏捷保匈阿朝古兄弟党致电英共执委会
    吊唁波立特同志逝世
    新华社30日讯　苏联共产党、捷克斯洛伐克共产党、保加利亚共产党、匈牙利社会主义工人党的中央委员会、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第一书记霍查、朝鲜劳动党和古巴人民社会党中央委员会致电英国共产党执行委员会，吊唁英共主席波立特逝世。
    苏联共产党中央委员会的唁电指出，哈里·波立特的一生都在为争取英国人民光明的未来、为争取自己祖国的民族利益和各国人民之间的友谊而进行不倦的斗争。
    唁电中说，苏联人民将永远记住这位苏联的忠诚好友哈里·波立特的光辉形象。
    唁电还指出，哈里·波立特为争取英国工人阶级、国际共产主义和工人运动的团结一致，为和平和社会主义进行了始终不渝的斗争。
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会的唁电说，波立特的一生他的工作都是同国际共产主义运动和英国共产党为保卫英国劳动人民的利益、保卫和平、社会主义和争取马克思列宁主义思想的胜利的斗争不可分地联系在一起的。
    保加利亚共产党中央委员会的唁电说，波立特的一生和他的工作是为英国的工人和全体劳动人民、为和平事业、民主和社会主义无私地服务的榜样。
    匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会的唁电说，波立特把整个一生献给了英国和国际工人阶级的事业。他的逝世不仅是英国共产党的巨大损失，而且也是国际工人运动和为社会主义而斗争的人的损失。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查的唁电说，波立特不愧是英国劳动阶级的儿子，他把他的全部生命和巨大的精力完全献给了劳动阶级的解放事业，献给了民主和社会主义。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的唁电对波立特的逝世表示深切的哀痛。
    电文说，作为国际劳工运动杰出的战士，波立特把他的一生献给了工人阶级的事业和保卫英国劳动人民切身利益的斗争。
    古巴人民社会党中央委员会的唁电说，波立特是英国共产党的领袖，英国工人阶级忠实的儿子、为各国人民的独立、和平和社会主义而斗争的伟大战士。电文对波立特的逝世表示深感悲痛。
