Title: Discussing the Overseas Chinese Issue and Ambassador Huang Zhen's Protest; Sukarno Holds Talks with Nasution and Others
Author:
Date: 1960-07-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 5th. According to a report by Antara News Agency, Indonesian President Sukarno held talks on the 5th with Foreign Minister Subandrio and Army Chief of Staff Nasution.

Subandrio told the press that they had presented a report to Sukarno on the overseas Chinese issue and the Tjimahi incident during the talks.

According to the Indonesian News Agency, during the one-hour talks, they discussed "the latest developments in the overseas Chinese issue" and the strong protest lodged by Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen to the Indonesian government on the 4th regarding the massacre of overseas Chinese by Indonesian military police.

It is reported that Sukarno chaired a core cabinet meeting after his talks with Subandrio and Nasution. After the meeting, Information Minister Maladi instructed the press to only publish official news about overseas Chinese, and not to publish any editorials or commentaries on the overseas Chinese issue.

According to the Army Intelligence Department, all Indonesian newspapers and news agencies are forbidden from providing further interpretations of the statement made yesterday by Sunarjo, the head of the Central Intelligence Department of the Indonesian Army, regarding the Tjimahi incident. After the Tjimahi massacre of overseas Chinese, Sunarjo issued a statement denying responsibility and distorting the truth.

### 讨论华侨问题和我黄镇大使的抗议  苏加诺同纳苏蒂安等会谈

1960-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

    讨论华侨问题和我黄镇大使的抗议
    苏加诺同纳苏蒂安等会谈
    新华社雅加达5日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在5日同外交部长苏班德里约和陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安举行了会谈。
    苏班德里约对报界说，他们在会谈中就华侨问题和芝马圩事件向苏加诺提出了报告。
    据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，他们在历时一小时的会谈中，讨论了“华侨问题的最新发展情况”以及中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇4日就印度尼西亚军警枪杀华侨的惨案向印度尼西亚政府提出的严重抗议。
    据报道，苏加诺在同苏班德里约和纳苏蒂安会谈后，主持了核心内阁会议。会后，情报部长马拉迪要报界只发表来自官方的有关华侨的消息，并且要报界不发表任何有关华侨问题的社论和评论。
    据陆军情报部说，全印度尼西亚的报界和通讯社都不准对印度尼西亚陆军中央情报部负责人苏纳约昨天就芝马圩事件发表的声明作进一步的解释。芝马圩枪杀华侨的事件发生后，苏纳约曾发表了抵赖罪责、歪曲事实真象的声明。
