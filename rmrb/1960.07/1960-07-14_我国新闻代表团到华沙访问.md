Title: Chinese News Delegation Visits Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1960-07-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 12th: A Chinese journalists' delegation consisting of four people, including Pan Fei, deputy director of the International Department of "People's Daily", arrived in Warsaw by plane last night. The delegation is visiting Poland according to the Sino-Polish Cultural Agreement. The delegation is expected to stay in Poland for four weeks.

### 我国新闻代表团到华沙访问

1960-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　我国新闻代表团到华沙访问
　　新华社华沙12日电　由“人民日报”国际部副主任潘非等四人组成的中国新闻工作者代表团，昨晚乘飞机到达华沙。代表团是按照中波文化协定来波兰访问的。预计代表团将在波兰逗留四周。
