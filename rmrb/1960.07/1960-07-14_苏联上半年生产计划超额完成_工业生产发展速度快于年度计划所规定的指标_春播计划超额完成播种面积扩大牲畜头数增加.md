Title: The Soviet Union Overfulfilled its Production Plan for the First Half of the Year; Industrial Production Developed Faster Than the Targets Set by the Annual Plan; Spring Planting Plan Overfulfilled, Sown Area Expanded, Livestock Numbers Increased
Author:
Date: 1960-07-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 12th: The Central Statistical Bureau of the Soviet Council of Ministers issued a communique today stating that in the first half of this year, the Soviet Union's total industrial output value plan was fulfilled by 104%, and at the same time, the development speed of industrial production was faster than the targets set by the annual plan for this year.

The communique stated that in the first half of this year, the Soviet Union's production plans for steel, rolled steel, iron ore, non-ferrous metals, many products of the machine manufacturing industry, and electricity, petroleum, coal, and other products have been completed ahead of schedule. During this period, the growth rate of steel production exceeded the originally planned annual average growth rate of the Seven-Year Plan. Compared with the first half of last year, in the first half of this year, the Soviet Union's pig iron increased by 1.9 million tons; steel increased by 2.7 million tons; rolled steel increased by 2.2 million tons; and electricity generation increased by 15 billion kilowatt-hours.

The production of consumer goods has also increased. Among them, textile production increased by 243 million square meters, and leather shoes increased by 19 million pairs.

The communique stated that in the first half of this year, Soviet industrial enterprises, together with scientific research institutes, created and mastered the production of many new types of machines, machinery, equipment, and instruments, and replaced old equipment with more advanced equipment. In the machine manufacturing industry, 30,000 machine tools and other equipment have been modernized in the first half of the year.

In terms of agricultural production, Soviet agricultural workers overfulfilled this year's spring planting plan. The total sown area of ​​crops that can be harvested this year (autumn-sown crops and spring-sown crops) is 202.9 million hectares, while last year it was 196.3 million hectares.

In the first half of this year, the number of cattle in Soviet collective farms and state farms increased by 6.3 million head, and the number of pigs increased by 4.5 million.

The material and technical foundations of collective farms and state farms have also been further strengthened. In the first half of this year, a total of 136,000 tractors (calculated at 15 horsepower per unit), 32,000 trucks, 24,000 grain combine harvesters, and many other agricultural machines and equipment were provided for agriculture.

The communique stated that in the first half of this year, the Soviet Union's basic construction plan for the national economy was completed by 100.4%. Compared with the first half of last year, the amount of construction work increased by 15%.

In the first half of this year, the number of employees in various sectors of the Soviet national economy increased by 3.9 million compared with the end of the first half of last year. With the shortening of working hours in many sectors, labor productivity in industry increased by 6.4%, and labor productivity in the construction industry increased by 9%. By the end of June this year, a total of approximately 20 million employees had transitioned to shortened working hours.

The communique stated that the Soviet Union's foreign trade is developing smoothly. The volume of foreign trade in the first half of this year increased by 6% compared with the same period last year.

This year, Soviet universities will have 350,000 young experts graduate, and technical schools and other secondary specialized schools will have more than 480,000 graduates.

The communique stated that the number of houses built with state investment in the first half of this year increased by 21% compared with the same period last year.

The communique stated that due to the continuous improvement of the material welfare of the working people and the improvement of health care, the incidence and mortality rates of the Soviet people continue to decline.

According to preliminary statistics, as of July 1, 1960, the Soviet population was 214.4 million.

### 苏联上半年生产计划超额完成　工业生产发展速度快于年度计划所规定的指标　春播计划超额完成播种面积扩大牲畜头数增加

1960-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联上半年生产计划超额完成
　　工业生产发展速度快于年度计划所规定的指标
　　春播计划超额完成播种面积扩大牲畜头数增加
    新华社莫斯科12日电　苏联部长会议中央统计局今天发表公报说，今年上半年，苏联工业生产总产值计划完成了104％，同时，工业生产的发展速度快于今年年度计划所规定的指标。
    公报说，今年上半年，苏联的钢、钢材、铁矿砂、有色金属、机器制造业的许多产品和电力、石油、煤及其他产品的生产计划都已提前完成。在这期间，钢铁生产的增长速度超过了七年计划原定的年平均增长速度。同去年上半年相比，今年上半年，苏联的生铁增加了一百九十万吨；钢增加了二百七十万吨；钢材增加了二百二十万吨；发电量增加了一百五十亿度。
    消费品的生产也增加了。其中，纺织品生产增加了二亿四千三百万平方米，皮鞋增加了一千九百万双。
    公报说，今年上半年，苏联工业企业同科学研究所一起创造并掌握了许多新型机器、机械、装置和仪器的生产，并用更完善的装备来更替陈旧的装备。在机器制造业中，在上半年已有三万台机床和其他装备现代化了。
    在农业生产方面，苏联农业劳动者超额完成了今年的春播计划。总计今年可以收割的农作物（秋播作物和春播作物）的总播种面积为两亿零二百九十万公顷，而去年为一亿九千六百三十万公顷。
    在今年上半年，苏联集体农庄和国营农场的牛的头数增加了六百三十万头，猪增加了四百五十万口。
    集体农庄和国营农场的物质技术基础也进一步加强了。今年上半年，总共为农业提供了十三万六千台拖拉机（每台以十五马力计算）、三万二千辆载重汽车两万四千台谷物自动联合收割机以及很多其他农业机器和装备。
    公报说，今年上半年苏联国民经济基本建设计划完成了100.4％。同去年上半年相比，建筑工作量增加了15％。
    今年上半年，苏联国民经济中各部门的职工人数比去年上半年末期增加了三百九十万人。在许多部门缩短了工作日的情况下，工业中的劳动生产率增长了6.4％，建筑业中的劳动生产率增长了9％。到今年6月底，总共约有两千万职工过渡到缩短了的工作日。
    公报说，苏联的对外贸易在顺利地发展。今年上半年的对外贸易额比去年同一时期增长了6％。
    苏联的高等学校今年将有三十五万名青年专家毕业，技术学校和其他中等专业学校的毕业生将有四十八万人以上。
    公报说，今年上半年由国家投资建成的住宅，比去年同期增加了21％。
    公报说，由于劳动人民物质福利的不断提高和保健医疗事业的改善，苏联人民的患病率和死亡率在继续降低。
    据初步统计，到1960年7月1日，苏联人口为两亿一千四百四十万。
