Title: Liao Chengzhi, Bao Erhan, and Others Go to Sweden to Attend the Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1960-07-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Liao Chengzhi, Bao Erhan, and other Chinese representatives who will be attending the enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council in Stockholm, left Beijing by plane today. Liu Ningyi, Xiao San, Shen Bocun, Qu Tangliang, Xiao Fangzhou, Zhang Jie, and others were at the airport to see them off.

### 出席世界和平理事会常务委员会扩大会议  廖承志包尔汉等去瑞典

1960-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会常务委员会扩大会议
    廖承志包尔汉等去瑞典
    新华社6日讯　出席世界和平理事会常务委员会斯德哥尔摩扩大会议的我国代表廖承志、包尔汉等于今日乘飞机离开北京。前往机场送行的，有刘宁一、萧三、申伯纯、区棠亮、萧方洲、张杰等人。
