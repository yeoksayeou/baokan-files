Title: Seven Cambodian Youths Come to China to Learn Basketball Techniques
Author:
Date: 1960-07-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, July 10th: Seven members of the Cambodian Royal Socialist Youth League left here today to go to China to study basketball techniques. This is one of the measures taken by the Cambodian government to improve Cambodia's sports level.

Seeing them off at the airport were Cambodian Minister of Information and Secretary-General of the Cambodian Royal Socialist Youth League, Chau Sau, and Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, Wang Youping. Prior to this, Prince Sihanouk had met with these seven league members, and the Chinese Embassy in Cambodia also held a tea party to welcome them.

After completing their studies and returning to their country, they will become members of the Cambodian national basketball team.

### 柬七名青年来我国学篮球技术

1960-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

    柬七名青年来我国学篮球技术
    新华社金边10日电　柬埔寨皇家社会主义青年联盟七名盟员今天离开这里前往中国学习篮球技术。这是柬埔寨政府为了提高柬埔寨体育水平所采取的措施之一。
    前往机场送行的有柬埔寨新闻大臣、柬埔寨皇家社会主义青年联盟秘书长肖桑和中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平。在这以前，西哈努克亲王曾经接见过这七名盟员，中国驻柬埔寨大使馆也曾举行茶会招待他们。
    他们学成回国以后，将成为柬埔寨国家篮球队队员。
