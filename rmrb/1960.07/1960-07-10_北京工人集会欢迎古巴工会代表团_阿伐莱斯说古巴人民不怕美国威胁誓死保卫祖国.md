Title: Beijing Workers Rally to Welcome Cuban Trade Union Delegation; Avales Says Cuban People Are Not Afraid of U.S. Threats and Vow to Defend Their Homeland to the Death
Author:
Date: 1960-07-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 9th. The Beijing Federation of Trade Unions held an evening party at the State-owned No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory tonight to warmly welcome the Cuban Workers' Confederation delegation. Liu Changsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Secretariat Secretaries Li Zaiwen and Zhang Xiuzhu, Li Yunchuan, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Machinery Trade Union, Zhang Jing, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Textile Trade Union, and Peng Siming, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, attended today's evening party.

When the delegation arrived at the factory, it was warmly welcomed by more than 600 workers from the No. 1 Cotton Factory and the Beijing Automobile Manufacturing Factory. The workers shouted slogans such as "Long live the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Cuban people!" and "Down with U.S. imperialism, strive for lasting world peace!" Zhang Xianwei, Chairman of the Trade Union of the No. 1 Cotton Factory, delivered a welcoming speech at the meeting. On behalf of all the workers of the factory, he expressed a warm welcome to the heroic Cuban workers' representatives who had come from afar, and expressed his respect to the heroic Cuban people who were fighting unyieldingly against U.S. imperialism.

Odon Avales de la Campa, head of the Cuban Workers' Confederation delegation and Secretary for Foreign Relations of the Cuban Workers' Confederation, then spoke at the meeting. He first extended the warmest greetings from the Cuban people to the Chinese people. He said that the common struggle of our two peoples against the common enemy of U.S. imperialism has united us and strengthened the close relationship and mutual assistance between the two countries. He said that the Cuban revolution and the Chinese revolution have many similarities. The Chinese revolution has proved to the world that when people take up arms and resolutely fight for independence and national sovereignty, anything can be done. Your revolutionary experience has helped us to carry out the struggle victoriously. After exposing the various conspiracies and sabotage activities carried out by U.S. imperialism against the Cuban revolution, he said: "In the face of U.S. imperialism's economic interference and military aggression, the Cuban people have not been intimidated. We are prepared to take up arms and oppose any interference in our country under the slogan of defending the motherland to the death." Avales said: With the support of workers and people all over the world, and relying on our own struggle and fighting determination, we will surely be able to achieve victory. During his speech, bursts of warm applause rang out in the venue.

### 北京工人集会欢迎古巴工会代表团  阿伐莱斯说古巴人民不怕美国威胁誓死保卫祖国

1960-07-10
第5版()
专栏：

    北京工人集会欢迎古巴工会代表团
    阿伐莱斯说古巴人民不怕美国威胁誓死保卫祖国
    新华社9日讯　北京市总工会今晚在国营第一棉纺织厂举行晚会，热烈欢迎古巴工人联合会代表团。中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜，书记处书记栗再温、张修竹，中国机械工会全国委员会主席李云川，中国纺织工会全国委员会副主席张静，北京市总工会副主席彭思明等参加了今天的晚会。
    当代表团到这个工厂时，受到国棉一厂和北京汽车制造厂职工六百多人的热烈欢迎。职工们高呼：“中国和古巴人民的友谊和团结万岁！”“打倒美帝国主义，争取世界持久和平”等口号。国棉一厂工会主席张宪伟在会上致欢迎词，他代表全厂职工向远道而来的英雄的古巴工人的代表们表示热烈欢迎，向英勇不屈地同美帝国主义进行斗争的古巴人民表示敬意。
    古巴工人联合会代表团团长、古巴工联对外关系书记奥东·阿伐莱斯·德·拉·坎巴接着在会上讲话。他首先代表古巴人民向中国人民致以最热烈的问候。他说，我们两国人民反对共同敌人美帝国主义的共同斗争使我们团结起来，并加强了两国之间的亲密关系和相互帮助。他说，古巴革命和中国革命有很多相似之处。中国革命向全世界证明了，当人们手里拿起武器坚决为独立和民族主权而斗争的时候，什么事情都可以做到的。你们的革命经验帮助了我们胜利地进行了斗争。他在揭露了美帝国主义对古巴革命进行的种种阴谋破坏活动后说：“在美帝国主义的经济干涉和军事进攻面前，古巴人民并没有被吓倒，我们准备在誓死保卫祖国的口号下，拿起武器去反对任何对我国的干涉。”阿伐莱斯说：在全世界工人和人民的支持下，并且依靠我们自己的斗争和战斗决心，我们一定能够取得胜利。在他讲话时，会场上不断地响起一阵阵热烈的掌声。
