Title: Delegation of the Zanzibar Nationalist Party Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, July 24th. At the invitation of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, the delegation of the Zanzibar Nationalist Party, led by Miraj Sherra Abdul, Vice-Chairman of the Zanzibar Nationalist Party, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them were: Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity; Ding Xilin, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; members of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity; and heads of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association and the All-China Youth Federation.

### 桑给巴尔民族主义党代表团人员到京

1960-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    桑给巴尔民族主义党代表团人员到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会邀请来我国访问的桑给巴尔民族主义党代表团人员，在团长、桑给巴尔民族主义党副主席米拉茨·谢拉布·阿卜杜勒率领下，于今日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中国亚非团结委员会副主席李德全、中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林、中国亚非团结委员会委员、以及中国非洲人民友好协会和中华全国青年联合会的负责人。
