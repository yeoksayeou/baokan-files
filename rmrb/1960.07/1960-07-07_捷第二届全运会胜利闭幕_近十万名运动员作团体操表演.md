Title: Czechoslovak Second National Games Victoriously Concludes; Nearly 100,000 Athletes Perform Mass Gymnastics

Author:

Date: 1960-07-07
Page: 6

The grand Second Czechoslovak National Games, lasting eleven days, victoriously concluded on the 3rd. The closing day featured a spectacular parade of 180,000 gymnasts and a mass gymnastics performance.

On the morning of the 3rd, 350,000 spectators lined the streets to watch the colorful parade. In the afternoon, 99,744 male and female gymnasts performed a grand mass gymnastics display at Strahov Stadium, Europe's largest stadium, attracting 225,000 spectators.

A total of 1,485,000 spectators watched the performances. During the Games, "Friendship Night" galas were also held. Athletes from the Soviet Union, China, and eight other countries gave wonderful performances.

### 捷第二届全运会胜利闭幕  近十万名运动员作团体操表演

1960-07-07
第6版()
专栏：

    捷第二届全运会胜利闭幕
    近十万名运动员作团体操表演
    捷克斯洛伐克盛况空前的第二届全国运动大会前后举行了十一天，已经在3日胜利闭幕。闭幕这一天举行了蔚为奇观的十八万体操运动员的游行和团体操表演。
    3日上午，有三十五万观众在沿途观看了彩色缤纷的游行队伍。下午，九万九千七百四十四名男、女体操运动员在欧洲最大的体育场——斯特拉霍夫体育场举行盛大团体操表演，吸引了二十二万五千名观众。
    一共有一百四十八万五千名观众观看了表演。运动会期间还陆续举行了“友谊之夜”晚会。苏联、中国等八个国家的运动员在会上作了精彩表演。
