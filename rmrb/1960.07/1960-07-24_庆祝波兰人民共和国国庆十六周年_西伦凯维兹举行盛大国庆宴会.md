Title: Sierankiewicz Holds Grand National Day Banquet to Celebrate the 16th Anniversary of the Founding of the Polish People's Republic
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 22nd. Polish Council of Ministers Chairman Józef Cyrankiewicz held a grand banquet here on the evening of July 22nd to celebrate the 16th anniversary of the founding of the Polish People's Republic.

Polish party and government leaders Władysław Gomułka, Aleksander Zawadzki, Eugeniusz Szyr, Zenon Kliszko, Jerzy Łog-Sowiński, and ministers of various ministries, and prominent figures from all walks of life attended the banquet.

Also attending the banquet were the head of the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation currently visiting Poland, Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Organov, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Poland.

Cyrankiewicz spoke at the banquet.

After the banquet, Polish artists performed a brilliant ballet.

### 庆祝波兰人民共和国国庆十六周年  西伦凯维兹举行盛大国庆宴会

1960-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝波兰人民共和国国庆十六周年
    西伦凯维兹举行盛大国庆宴会
    新华社华沙22日电　波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹22日晚在这里举行盛大宴会，庆祝波兰人民共和国国庆十六周年。
    波兰党政领导人哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、英德里霍夫斯基、克利什科、洛加—索文斯基等以及各部部长，各界著名人士等出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的，还有正在波兰进行访问的苏联最高苏维埃代表团团长、苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席奥尔加诺夫，以及各国驻波兰使节。
    西伦凯维兹在宴会上讲了话。
    宴会后，波兰艺术家们表演了精采的芭蕾舞剧。
