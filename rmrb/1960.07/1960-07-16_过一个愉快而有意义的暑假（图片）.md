Title: Have a Happy and Meaningful Summer Vacation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 4

　　Have a Happy and Meaningful Summer Vacation
　　1　Summer life has begun. All swimming venues in the capital have been opened. At the children's swimming pool in Shichahai Swimming Pool in Beijing, children are playing in the water. Photo by Huang Jingda, Xinhua News Agency
　　2　After returning home for summer vacation, students from Beijing No. 83 Middle School actively participate in production labor in the People's Commune. The picture shows students weeding in the Sanjie Production Team of Chengguan People's Commune in Changping County.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Photo by Na Yi, our reporter
　　3　In Huangpu Park, Shanghai, counselors from Xinguang Road Primary School are talking about the past and present of Huangpu Park, educating children in anti-imperialist patriotism.
　　4　Aim, fire. Children from Xinguang Road Primary School in Shanghai are engaged in national defense sports training in summer camp. Photo by Lu Ming, Xinhua News Agency

### 过一个愉快而有意义的暑假（图片）

1960-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　过一个愉快而有意义的暑假
　　1　夏季生活开始了。首都各游泳场所都已开放。在北京什刹海游泳场儿童游泳池，儿童们正在水中嬉戏　新华社记者　黄景达摄
　　2　北京市第八十三中学的学生放暑假回家后，在人民公社积极参加生产劳动。图为学生们在昌平县城关人民公社三街生产队锄草
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报记者　纳一摄
　　3　上海市黄浦公园里，新广路小学的辅导员在讲黄浦公园的今昔，向孩子们进行反帝爱国主义教育
　　4　瞄准，射击。上海市新广路小学的孩子们在夏令营中进行国防体育锻炼　新华社记者　卢鸣摄
