Title: Ministry of Education, Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Other Units Hold a Joint Celebration to See Off Foreign Graduates from Beijing

Author:
Date: 1960-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The Ministry of Education, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the All-China Students' Federation, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the Beijing Municipal Students' Federation held a joint celebration tonight to warmly see off the 1960 graduating foreign students from Beijing.

This group of students comes from countries such as the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, Poland, Romania, the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, and Mongolia. They have successfully completed their studies at various universities in Beijing and are about to return to their home countries.

At the celebration, filled with an atmosphere of friendship, Liu Zizai, Vice Minister of Education, and Zhang Jinlin, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, spoke successively, congratulating the graduating foreign students on completing their studies and wishing them to make more and better contributions to the construction of their own countries.

Representatives of graduating students from the Soviet Union and Vietnam also spoke at the meeting, thanking China for its cultivation and care for them.

Wang Wei, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Zhang Yin'an and Ma Xianhua, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Students' Federation, and Zhang Bingguang, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Students' Federation, attended the celebration.

### 教育部、团中央等单位举行联欢会  欢送北京市外国毕业留学生

1960-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    教育部、团中央等单位举行联欢会
    欢送北京市外国毕业留学生
    新华社26日讯　教育部、共青团中央、全国学联、共青团北京市委和北京市学联今天晚上举行联欢会，热烈欢送北京市1960年毕业留学生。
    这批学生来自苏联、朝鲜、越南、波兰、罗马尼亚、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、蒙古等国家。他们在北京各高等学校胜利完成学习任务，即将返回他们的祖国。
    在洋溢着友谊气氛的联欢会上，教育部副部长刘子载，共青团北京市委第二书记张进霖先后讲话，祝贺这批毕业留学生完成学业，祝他们将在自己祖国的建设事业中做出更多更好的贡献。
    苏联和越南毕业留学生代表也在会上讲话，感谢中国各方面对他们的培养和关怀。
    共青团中央书记王伟，全国学联副主席张寅安、马娴华，北京市学联主席张炳光等，出席了联欢会。
