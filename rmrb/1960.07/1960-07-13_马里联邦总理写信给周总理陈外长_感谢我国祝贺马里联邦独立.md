Title: Malian Federation Prime Minister Writes to Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Chen, Thanking China for Congratulating the Malian Federation on its Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-07-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Prime Minister of the Malian Federation, Modibo Keita, wrote to the Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, on June 28th, thanking Premier Zhou for his congratulations on the declaration of independence of the Malian Federation. The full text of the letter is as follows:
To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing,
    Your Excellency,
    I would like to express my sincere gratitude for your kind telegram of congratulations on the occasion of our declaration of independence. I am also pleased to learn of your decision to recognize the Malian Federation as an independent sovereign state.
    The Malian Federation is willing to make effective efforts to build a better world. To this end, it is willing to cooperate with all sincere countries that are fighting for peace and justice.
    I wish you good health and success in your endeavors.
    Please accept my highest regards.
          Modibo Keita
          Prime Minister of the Government of the Malian Federation
        Dakar, June 28, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Prime Minister of the Malian Federation, Modibo Keita, wrote to the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, on June 24th, thanking him for his congratulations on the declaration of independence of the Malian Federation. The full text of the letter is as follows:
To His Excellency Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Beijing,
    Your Excellency,
    I was pleased to receive your telegram recognizing the Malian Federation as an independent sovereign state.
    I express my heartfelt thanks to you for this, and thank you for your wishes to the people of Mali.
    After restoring its sovereignty, our country has decided to effectively dedicate itself to and cooperate with other countries in the world to safeguard and consolidate peace, so that the people of all countries can enter an era of happiness and prosperity. To this end, Mali will cooperate with all sincere countries.
    Your Excellency, please accept my highest regards.
          Modibo Keita
          Prime Minister of the Government of the Malian Federation
        Dakar, June 24, 1960

### 马里联邦总理写信给周总理陈外长　感谢我国祝贺马里联邦独立

1960-07-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　马里联邦总理写信给周总理陈外长
　　感谢我国祝贺马里联邦独立
    新华社12日讯　马里联邦总理莫迪博·凯塔在6月28日写信给中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，感谢周总理对马里联邦宣告独立的祝贺。信的全文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
  先生阁下，阁下，
    在我国宣布独立的时候，承蒙来电致友好的祝贺，我谨向阁下表示十分感谢。同时我愉快地得悉你决定承认马里联邦为独立的主权国家。
    马里联邦愿为建立一个更美好的世界而作出有效的努力。为此，它愿同一切为和平和正义而斗争的具有诚意的国家进行合作。
    祝阁下身体健康、事业成功。
    顺致最崇高的敬意。
          马里联邦政府总理
              莫迪博·凯塔
        1960年6月28日于达喀尔
    新华社12日讯　马里联邦总理莫迪博·凯塔在6月24日写信给中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅，感谢他对马里联邦宣告独立的祝贺。信的全文如下：北京中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅先生
  阁下，阁下，
    我愉快地收到了你的关于承认马里联邦为独立的主权国家的来电。
    我对此向你表示衷心的谢意，并且感谢你对马里人民的祝愿。
    我国在恢复主权后，决定有效地致力于并且协同世界其他各国一起维护和巩固和平，俾使各国人民能够进入一个幸福繁荣的纪元。为此目的，马里将同一切具有诚意的国家进行合作。
    阁下，请接受我的崇高敬意。
          马里联邦政府总理
              莫迪博·凯塔
        1960年6月24日于达喀尔
