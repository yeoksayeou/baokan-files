Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Bolivian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-07-18
Page: 5
　　
Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Bolivian Senate Delegation led by Oscar Donoso López, First Vice President of the Bolivian Senate, and Jaime Arellano Casaneda, a member of the Bolivian House of Representatives.

　　Zhang Xiruo delivered a welcoming speech at the banquet, expressing his belief that the visit of the distinguished Bolivian guests to China would contribute to promoting understanding and friendship between the peoples of China and Bolivia. In his reply, Oscar Donoso López pointed out that he and the other members of his delegation support the establishment of friendly relations between Bolivia and China. He said that before coming to China, he had seen many articles praising China, and after coming to China, he confirmed the truth of these introductions with his own eyes.

　　Zhang Su and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Mei Gongbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, Wu Defeng, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Society, Ke Bonian, Director of the China Political Science and Law Society, and Liang Sicheng, Vice Chairman of the Council of the Architectural Society of China, attended the banquet. Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also attended the banquet.

### 张奚若宴请玻利维亚外宾

1960-07-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　张奚若宴请玻利维亚外宾
    据新华社17日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚设宴欢迎由玻利维亚参议院第一副议长奥斯卡·多诺索·洛佩斯率领的玻利维亚参议院代表团和玻利维亚众议院议员海梅·阿雷利亚诺·卡萨内达。
    张奚若在宴会上致欢迎词，他表示相信玻利维亚贵宾的访问中国，将为促进中国和玻利维亚两国人民之间的了解和友谊做出贡献。奥斯卡·多诺索·洛佩斯在致答词时指出，他和他的代表团的其他成员，都支持玻利维亚与中国建立友好关系。他说，他在来中国以前，曾看到许多称赞中国的文章，这次来到中国，以自己的亲眼所见证实了这些介绍是真实的。
    全国人民代表大会常委会副秘书长张苏、余心清，政协全国委员会副秘书长梅龚彬，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复，中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰、理事柯柏年，中国建筑学会理事会副理事长梁思成等出席了宴会。外交部美澳司司长申健也出席了宴会。
