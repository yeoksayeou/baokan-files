Title: Soviet Union and Congo Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1960-07-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, 8th: TASS reported that the Soviet delegation headed by Rakhmatov, Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Tajik Republic, held talks with Congolese Prime Minister Lumumba on the evening of the 7th.
	During the talks, an agreement was reached on the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and the Republic of the Congo and the exchange of ambassadors.

### 苏联和刚果决定建交

1960-07-10
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联和刚果决定建交
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社布鲁塞尔8日讯：以苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席、塔吉克共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席拉赫马托夫为首的苏联代表团7日晚上同刚果共和国总理卢蒙巴举行了会谈。
    在会谈中达成了关于苏联和刚果共和国建立外交关系并互派大使的协议。
