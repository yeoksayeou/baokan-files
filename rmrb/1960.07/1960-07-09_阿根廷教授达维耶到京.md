Title: Argentine Professor Davier Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th. Alberto H. Davier, Head of the Department of Chemical Engineering at the National University of Litoral, Argentina, and his wife, Iris Berla Uka, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese Association for Friendship with Latin American Countries.

### 阿根廷教授达维耶到京

1960-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    阿根廷教授达维耶到京
    新华社7日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会的邀请前来我国访问的阿根廷里多热尔国立大学化学工程系主任阿尔贝托·赫·达维耶和他的夫人伊丽斯·贝尔拉·乌卡，今日乘飞机到达北京。
