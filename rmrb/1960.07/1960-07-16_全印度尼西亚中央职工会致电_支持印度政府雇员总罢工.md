Title: All-Indonesia Central Organization of Trade Unions Sends Telegram Supporting General Strike of Indian Government Employees
Author:
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 15th. The Jakarta "People's Daily" reported today that the All-Indonesia Central Organization of Trade Unions, in the name of its 2.7 million members, sent a telegram to the All India Trade Union Congress, expressing support for the general strike of Indian government employees for demands for wage increases, and protesting against the Indian government's suppression of the strike.
    The telegram said that the demands of the Indian workers are reasonable, but the Indian government's response to their demands is armed suppression and arrests. The All-Indonesia Central Organization of Trade Unions lodges a serious protest with the Indian government for this.
    The telegram demanded that the Indian government immediately stop using force to suppress the workers' movement and release the arrested strike organizers.

### 全印度尼西亚中央职工会致电　支持印度政府雇员总罢工

1960-07-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　全印度尼西亚中央职工会致电
　　支持印度政府雇员总罢工
    新华社雅加达15日电　雅加达“人民日报”今天报道，全印度尼西亚中央职工会以它的二百七十万会员的名义打电报给印度全印工会大会，表示支持印度政府雇员为要求提高工资而举行的总罢工，并抗议印度政府镇压罢工。
    电报说，印度工人的要求是合理的，但是，印度政府对他们的要求的答复是武装镇压和逮捕。全印度尼西亚中央职工会为此向印度政府提出严重抗议。
    电报要求印度政府立即停止用武力镇压工人运动和释放被捕的罢工组织者。
