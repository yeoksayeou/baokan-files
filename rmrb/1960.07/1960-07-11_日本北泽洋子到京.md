Title: Japan's Kitazawa Yoko Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 10th—Japan's Kitazawa Yoko, who was invited by the All-China Women's Federation to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Huang Gan Ying, Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Women's Federation, and others welcomed her at the airport.

### 日本北泽洋子到京

1960-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

    日本北泽洋子到京
    新华社10日讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参观访问的日本北泽洋子，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有全国妇联国际联络部部长黄甘英等。
