Title: Japanese "China Research Institute" Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: The Japanese "China Research Institute" delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country, arrived in Beijing today. The delegation was led by Hirano Yoshitaro, Vice Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association and Director-General of the Japan Peace Committee, with a total of five members.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General, Zhang Youyu, Vice President of the Political Science and Law Association, Xie Nanguang, Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and more than 20 others including Lin Lin, Yang Chunsong, and Qi Muguang.

Japanese peace figure Kinkazu Saionji, Vice Chairman of the All-Japan Metal Workers' Union Minoru Takano, and Korean peace figure Hwang Bong-koo, who are currently in Beijing, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 日本“中国研究所”代表团抵京

1960-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    日本“中国研究所”代表团抵京
    新华社21日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的日本“中国研究所”代表团，在日本中国友好协会副会长、日本和平委员会理事长平野义太郎团长率领下一行五人，于今天到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林、秘书长周而复，政治法律学会副会长张友渔，中国人民外交学会理事谢南光，以及林林、杨春松、戚慕光等二十余人。
    在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一，日本全国金属工会副主席高野实，朝鲜和平人士黄凤九也前往机场欢迎。
