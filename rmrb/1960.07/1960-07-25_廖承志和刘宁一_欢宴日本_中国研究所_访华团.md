Title: Liao Chengzhi and Liu Ningyi Host Banquet for Japanese "China Research Institute" Delegation

Author:
Date: 1960-07-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: Liao Chengzhi and Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairmen of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 24th for the Japanese "China Research Institute" delegation led by Yotaro Hirano, who are visiting our country.

Among those present at the banquet were Xu Guangping, Zhou Erfu, Zhao Puchu, Qu Tangliang, Zhao Anbo, Zhu Ziqi, Huang Ganying, Yang Jin, and people from all walks of life. Japanese peace advocate Kinkazu Saionji and his wife, and Korean peace advocate Hwang Bong-koo, who are in Beijing, were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 廖承志和刘宁一  欢宴日本“中国研究所”访华团

1960-07-25
第6版()
专栏：

    廖承志和刘宁一
    欢宴日本“中国研究所”访华团
    新华社24日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志和刘宁一，于24日夜宴请来我国访问的以平野义太郎为首的日本“中国研究所”访华代表团。
    出席宴会的有许广平，周而复，赵朴初，区棠亮，赵安博，朱子奇，黄甘英，杨进等各界人士，在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一和他的夫人，朝鲜和平人士黄凤九，也应邀出席了宴会。
