Title: Head of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association Meets with Secretary-General of the Sudanese Peace Movement of the Mali Federation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th. Zhang Tiesheng, Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association (CAPFA), met with Diallo Moussa Sane, Secretary-General of the Sudanese Peace Movement of the Mali Federation, on the evening of the 19th and had a cordial conversation with him. Vice President Zhang Tiesheng, on behalf of the CAPFA, presented the guest with a documentary film each titled "Hold High the Banner of the Bandung Conference and Support the Struggle of the African People" and "A Hundred Birds Paying Homage to the Phoenix." Also present at the meeting was Lin Lin, a standing council member of the CAPFA.

### 中非友协负责人　会见马里联邦苏丹和平运动秘书长

1960-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　中非友协负责人
　　会见马里联邦苏丹和平运动秘书长
    新华社19日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会副会长张铁生19日晚上会见了马里联邦苏丹和平运动秘书长迪亚洛·摩沙·萨内，同他进行了亲切的谈话。张铁生副会长代表中非友协赠给客人“高举万隆会议旗帜，支持非洲人民斗争”和“百凤朝阳”纪录片各一部。会见时在座的有中非友协常务理事林林。
