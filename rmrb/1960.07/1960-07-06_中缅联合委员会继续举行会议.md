Title: China-Burma Joint Committee Continues to Hold Meetings
Author:
Date: 1960-07-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, July 5th: The China-Burma Joint Committee, at its first meeting on the 4th, discussed the time and location for the first meetings of the Joint Survey Group and the Joint Investigation Group, as well as the tasks they should complete, and reached an agreement.
The meeting continued in a friendly atmosphere.
Both sides also exchanged views on the time, location, and issues to be discussed at the second meeting of the Joint Committee, and reached an agreement.
Both sides also agreed that the China-Burma Joint Committee would issue a communiqué on July 6th.

### 中缅联合委员会继续举行会议

1960-07-06
第5版()
专栏：

    中缅联合委员会继续举行会议
    新华社仰光5日电　中缅联合委员会第一次会议在4日的会议上就联合勘察组和联合调查组第一次会议举行的时间、地点和所应完成的任务进行了讨论，并且达成了协议。
    会议继续在友好的气氛中进行。
    双方还就联合委员会第二次会议举行的时间、地点和讨论的问题交换了意见，并且达成了协议。
    双方还同意中缅联合委员会在7月6日发表一项公报。
