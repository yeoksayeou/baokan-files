Title: General He Bingyan Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1960-07-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, July 2nd. He Bingyan, delegate to the National People's Congress, Standing Committee Member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Member of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, Standing Committee Member of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Director of the Sichuan Provincial Sports Commission, Secretary of the Chengdu Military Region Party Committee, General of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Commander of the Chengdu Military Region, passed away in Chengdu on the 1st due to a dissecting aortic aneurysm, despite medical treatment, at the age of forty-seven.

The Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Chengdu Military Region Party Committee of the Chinese People's Liberation Army today issued an obituary for Comrade He Bingyan. The obituary stated: "Comrade He Bingyan possessed the fine qualities of a Communist Party member, maintaining close ties with the masses, being unafraid of difficulties, and being infinitely loyal to the cause of the Party and the people; he was a good member of the Chinese Communist Party, a loyal soldier of Comrade Mao Zedong, and an outstanding and long-tested commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. He joined the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in 1929, joined the Chinese Communist Party in the same year, participated in the Second Revolutionary Civil War, went through the world-renowned 25,000-Li Long March, participated in the War of Resistance Against Japan and the War of Liberation, and served as a regimental commander, division commander of the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army, and army commander, deputy commander of the military region, and commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Under the cultivation and education of the Party, he struggled for the cause of the Party and the people for thirty-one years as one day, with a spirit of self-sacrifice. In the years of war, he worked hard, was heroic and tenacious, overcame dangers in every battle, and made outstanding contributions to the cause of liberation of the Chinese people; during the period of socialist revolution and socialist construction, he resolutely supported and implemented the Party's line, principles, and policies. In his life, he dedicated all his energy to the great cause of communism."

The Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Chengdu Military Region Party Committee of the Chinese People's Liberation Army called on all Party members and troops in the province in the obituary to learn from Comrade He Bingyan's fine qualities and exemplary struggle activities.

The funeral committee for Comrade He Bingyan has been formed, with He Long as the director and Wang Shangrong, Wang Shaonan, Wang Zhen, Deng Xihou, Wei Jie, Gan Siqi, Liu Zhong, Yan Xiufeng, Li Dazhang, Li Jingquan, Li Wenqing, Li Da, Li Zhen, Li Juikui, Xiao Hua, Du Xinyuan, He Zhengwen, Yu Shusheng, Yu Qiuli, Chen Gang, Luo Ruiqing, Jiang Qixian, Zhao Cangbi, Zhang Zongxun, Xu Bing, Guo Linxiang, Feng Picheng, Xu Guangda, Xu Mengxia, Huang Xinting, Liao Hansheng, Liao Zhigao, and Tan Zheng as members.

Marshal Lin Biao, Minister of National Defense, and the General Staff Department and General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sent telegrams of condolence to Comrade He Bingyan's family on the 1st.

### 贺炳炎上将逝世

1960-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

    贺炳炎上将逝世
    新华社成都2日电　全国人民代表大会代表、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员、中华人民共和国国防委员会委员、中共四川省委员会常务委员、四川省体育运动委员会主任、中共成都部队委员会书记、中国人民解放军上将、成都部队司令员贺炳炎同志，因患夹层动脉瘤，医治无效，于1日在成都逝世，享年四十七岁。
    中共四川省委和中共中国人民解放军成都部队委员会今天发表了贺炳炎同志逝世的讣告。讣告说：“贺炳炎同志具有共产党人密切联系群众、不怕困难、对党和人民事业无限忠诚的优良品质；是中国共产党的好党员，是毛泽东同志的忠实战士，是中国人民解放军久经考验的优秀指挥员。他1929年参加了中国工农红军，同年加入中国共产党，参加过第二次国内革命战争，经过了举世闻名的二万五千里长征，参加过抗日战争和解放战争，担任过中国工农红军的团长、师长和中国人民解放军军长、军区副司令员、司令员等职务。他在党的培养和教导下，三十一年如一日，以自我牺牲的精神为党和人民的事业而斗争。在战争年代里，艰苦奋斗，英勇顽强，历次战胜艰险，为中国人民解放事业建立了卓越功勋；在社会主义革命和社会主义建设时期，坚决拥护和贯彻执行党的路线、方针、政策。在他的一生中把全部精力献给了伟大的共产主义事业。”
    中共四川省委和中共中国人民解放军成都部队委员会在讣告中号召全省党员和军队，学习贺炳炎同志的优良品质和模范的斗争活动。
    贺炳炎同志的治丧委员会已经组成，主任是贺龙，委员是王尚荣、王绍南、王震、邓锡侯、韦杰、甘泗淇、刘中、阎秀峰、李大章、李井泉、李文清、李达、李贞、李聚奎、萧华、杜心源、何正文、余述生、余秋里、陈刚、罗瑞卿、姜齐贤、赵苍璧、张宗逊、徐冰、郭林祥、冯丕成、许光达、许梦侠、黄新廷、廖汉生、廖志高、谭政。
    国防部部长林彪元帅和中国人民解放军总参谋部、总政治部1日致电慰问贺炳炎同志的家属。
