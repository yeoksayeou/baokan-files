Title: President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, Liu Changsheng, Sends a Telegram to the President of Ghana Congratulating the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Ghana
Author:
Date: 1960-07-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 8th. Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, sent a telegram today to Kwame Nkrumah, President of the Republic of Ghana, warmly congratulating the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Ghana.

The telegram said, "On the occasion of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Ghana, I would like to extend my warmest congratulations to Your Excellency, and through Your Excellency to the people of your country, on behalf of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association. I wish the friendship between the people of China and Ghana will grow stronger day by day."

### 中非友协会长刘长胜致电加纳总统　祝贺中加两国建交

1960-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　中非友协会长刘长胜致电加纳总统
　　祝贺中加两国建交
    新华社8日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜今天致电加纳共和国总统克瓦米·恩克鲁玛，热烈祝贺中加两国建立外交关系。
    电报说，“值此中加两国建交之际，我谨代表中国非洲人民友好协会向阁下，并通过阁下向贵国人民表示最热烈的祝贺。祝中加两国人民的友谊日益发展。”
