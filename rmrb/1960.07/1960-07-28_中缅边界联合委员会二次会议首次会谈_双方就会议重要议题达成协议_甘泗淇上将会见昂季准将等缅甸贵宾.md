Title: Second Meeting of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee Holds First Talks; Both Sides Reach Agreement on Important Agenda Items; General Gan Siqi Meets Brigadier General Aung Gyi and Other Burmese Guests

Author:

Date: 1960-07-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: Press Communiqué of the First Meeting of the Second Session of the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee

The first meeting of the second session of the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee was held in Beijing at 9:00 a.m. on July 26th. The Chinese Chief Representative Yao Zhongming and the Burmese Chief Representative Brigadier General Aung Gyi, as well as other representatives and advisors from both sides, attended the meeting. The meeting was filled with a friendly and sincere atmosphere throughout. Both sides have smoothly reached an agreement on important agenda items of this meeting. Both sides agreed to continue discussions on other relevant issues.

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: General Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, met this afternoon with Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the Burmese Chief Representative of the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, and other distinguished Burmese guests.

During the meeting, the hosts and guests held cordial and friendly talks, exchanging experiences in the literary and artistic work of the troops.

Present at the meeting were Senior Colonel Li Wei, Vice Minister of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Senior Colonel Zhang Bingyu, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, among others.

U Kyaw Winn, the Burmese Ambassador to China, and Military Attaché U Khin Saw Min were also present at the meeting.

After the meeting, Brigadier General Aung Gyi and other distinguished guests, accompanied by General Gan Siqi, visited the rehearsal hall of the General Political Department's Song and Dance Ensemble, where they watched the rehearsals of dramas and dances by the military's literary and artistic workers. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the actors and actresses.

### 中缅边界联合委员会二次会议首次会谈  双方就会议重要议题达成协议  甘泗淇上将会见昂季准将等缅甸贵宾

1960-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅边界联合委员会二次会议首次会谈
    双方就会议重要议题达成协议
    甘泗淇上将会见昂季准将等缅甸贵宾
    新华社27日讯　中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议第一次会谈新闻公报
    中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议于7月26日上午九时在北京举行第一次会谈。中方首席代表姚仲明和缅方首席代表昂季准将以及双方的其他代表和顾问都参加了这次会谈，会谈自始至终充满了友好诚挚的气氛。双方已就本次会议的重要议题顺利地达成了协议。双方同意就其他有关问题继续进行会谈。
    新华社27日讯　中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将今天下午会见了中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表、缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季准将等缅甸贵宾。
    会见时，宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话，交流了部队文艺工作的经验。
    会见时在座的，有中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部副部长李伟大校，国防部外事处副处长张秉玉上校等人。
    会见时，缅甸驻中国大使叫温，大使馆武官景沙门等人也在座。
    会见以后，昂季准将等贵宾由甘泗淇上将陪同到总政文工团排演场，观看了部队文艺工作者排练的话剧和舞蹈。贵宾们在这里受到男女演员们的热烈欢迎。
