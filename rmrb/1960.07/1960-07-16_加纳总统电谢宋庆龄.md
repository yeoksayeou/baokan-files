Title: Ghanaian President Cables Thanks to Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 15th: Ghanaian President Kwame Nkrumah cabled Soong Ching Ling on the 14th, thanking her for her congratulatory message on the founding of the Republic of Ghana and his assumption of the office of the first president. The cable reads as follows:
Her Excellency Soong Ching Ling, Beijing:

We regret to learn that you are unable to attend the founding ceremony of the Republic of Ghana due to ill health.

On behalf of the government and people of the Republic of Ghana, and on my own behalf, I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude for your kind congratulatory message and your wishes for our country's prosperity and progress. We share your hope that the friendly relations existing between the Chinese and Ghanaian peoples will be further developed to the benefit of us both and for world peace.

We wish you a speedy recovery.

Kwame Nkrumah
President of the Republic of Ghana
Accra, July 14, 1960

### 加纳总统电谢宋庆龄

1960-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　加纳总统电谢宋庆龄
    新华社15日讯　加纳共和国总统恩克鲁玛在14日打电报给宋庆龄，感谢她在加纳共和国成立和他担任第一任总统的时候给他的贺电，电文如下：北京宋庆龄阁下：
    我们遗憾地得悉，你由于身体不好而不能参加加纳共和国的成立典礼。
    我谨代表加纳共和国政府和人民，并以我个人的名义，衷心地感谢你的亲切的贺电以及对我国繁荣和进步的祝愿。我们和你同样希望，中国人民和加纳人民之间存在的友好关系将进一步发展，以利于我们彼此的利益和世界和平。
    我们祝你迅速恢复健康。
            加纳共和国总统
            克瓦米·恩克鲁玛
        1960年7月14日于阿克拉
