Title: Zhang Hanfu Holds Film Reception to Entertain Envoys from Various Countries to China
Author:
Date: 1960-07-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and his wife held a film reception in the Great Hall of the People tonight to entertain envoys, diplomats, and their wives from various countries to China. The film "Premier Zhou's Visit to Six Countries" was screened at the reception.

Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, Zeng Yongquan, and Geng Biao, Vice Ministers of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin and Lin Haiyun, and Vice Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang also attended the reception.

### 章汉夫举行电影招待会　招待各国驻华使节

1960-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　章汉夫举行电影招待会
　　招待各国驻华使节
    新华社12日讯　外交部副部长章汉夫和夫人今天晚上在人民大会堂举行电影招待会，招待各国驻华使节、外交官和他们的夫人。招待会上放映了电影“周总理访问六国”。
    外交部副部长罗贵波、曾涌泉、耿飚，对外贸易部副部长雷任民、林海云，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥等也出席了招待会。
