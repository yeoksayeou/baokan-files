Title: British Labour Party Deputy Leader Bevan Dies
Author:
Date: 1960-07-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 7th: British Labour Party Deputy Leader Bevan passed away yesterday afternoon.

Bevan underwent abdominal surgery last December. Last week, his old illness relapsed and ultimately proved fatal.

### 英工党副领袖比万逝世

1960-07-09
第6版()
专栏：

    英工党副领袖比万逝世
    新华社伦敦7日电　英国工党副领袖比万昨天下午逝世。
    比万去年12月曾动过腹部手术，上星期，他旧病复发，终于不治。
