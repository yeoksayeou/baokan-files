Title: Good News: A Flying Start to July
Author:
Date: 1960-07-15
Page: 4
　　
　　Magang: Overcoming High Temperatures and Rainstorms, Output and Quality Leap Forward

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, 14th July: The workers of Ma'anshan Iron and Steel Company, who had completed their production tasks for the first half of the year ahead of schedule and exceeded their targets, achieved a double leap forward in steel output and quality in the first ten days of July, despite high temperatures and continuous rainstorms. Compared with the average daily output in the same period last month, pig iron increased by 1.16%, steel by 2.2%, steel products by 10%, and iron ore by 3.5%. In terms of product quality, the qualified rate of pig iron from blast furnaces of 200 cubic meters and above increased by an average of 15.1% compared with the previous month, and the average qualified rate of pig iron from more than 90% of the small blast furnaces reached 100%; the qualified rate of converter steel increased by more than 10% on average in the first ten days of July compared with the previous month. The variety and specifications of steel and steel products have also increased a lot.

　　Tianjin No. 4 Steel Plant: Output Rises Sharply, Quality Improves

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 14th July: Leading cadres of Tianjin No. 4 Steel Plant went deep into the front line and led production in a down-to-earth manner, achieving a double leap forward in output and quality in the first ten days of July. From the 1st to the 10th, the average daily steel output of the whole plant increased by 34.6% compared with the same period in June, the qualified rate of steel increased by 6%, and the average furnace life of the whole plant reached forty-four heats, an increase of 50% compared with the same period in June.

　　Fengman: Taking Advantage of the Flood Season to Generate and Supply More Electricity Safely and with High Output

　　Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, 14th July: The Fengman Hydropower Station completed its power generation plan for the first ten days of July three days and eight hours ahead of schedule, exceeding the target by 2.1%; all indicators such as labor productivity, cost, quality, and safety fully met the planned requirements.

　　Currently, the Songhua River has entered the flood season, which is a good season for the Fengman Power Station to generate a large amount of electricity. In order to make use of the natural conditions of this season, generate more electricity, and supply more electricity, the entire power station launched a competition aimed at increasing equipment availability and ensuring safe and high output. The leading cadres of the power station went deep into the front line of production, led all the staff, focused on daily plans, tapped potential, and increased equipment availability, creating the highest daily power generation record for six consecutive days from the 1st to the 6th. At the same time, they implemented a system of daily inspection and daily summary to ensure safe production.

　　Hebi: Output Increases, Work Efficiency Improves, and Pit Prop Consumption Decreases

　　Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 14th July: On the basis of a complete victory in June, the Hebi Coal Mine in Henan Province won another flying start in the first ten days of July. The average daily output of raw coal increased by 11% compared with the national plan, and the labor efficiency of all employees increased by 12.3% compared with the national plan. The consumption of pit props was also greatly reduced compared with the plan.

### 喜报七月开门红

1960-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    喜报七月开门红
　　马钢：战胜高温暴雨产量质量双跃进
    据新华社合肥14日电　提前超额完成上半年生产任务的马鞍山钢铁公司职工又在高温和连日暴雨的条件下，取得了7月上旬钢铁产量、质量双跃进。跟上月同期平均日产量相比，生铁增长1.16％，钢增长2.2％，钢材增长10％，铁矿石增长3.5％。在产品质量方面，二百立方米以上高炉的生铁合格率比上月平均提高15.1％，有90％以上的小高炉生铁平均合格率达到100％；转炉钢的合格率7月上旬比上月平均提高10％以上。钢和钢材的品种规格也增加了很多。
　　津钢四厂：产量猛升质量提高
    据新华社天津14日电　天津第四钢厂领导干部深入第一线，扎扎实实地领导生产，7月上旬取得了产量、质量双跃进。1至10日，全厂钢的平均日产量跟6月同期比较提高34.6％，钢的合格率提高6％，全厂平均炉龄达到四十四炉，比6月同期提高50％。
　　丰满：趁汛期多发电多供电安全高产
    新华社长春14日电　丰满水电站在7日提前三天又八小时，超额2.1％完成了7月上旬发电计划；劳动生产率、成本、质量、安全等各项指标都全面地达到了计划要求。
    目前，松花江已经进入汛期，正是丰满电站大量发电的好季节。为了利用这个季节的自然条件，多发电，多供电，全电站开展了以提高设备出率、保证安全高产为内容的竞赛运动。电站的领导干部深入生产第一线，领导全体职工，狠抓日计划，大挖潜力，提高了设备出率，在1至6日的六天中，连续创造了日发电量的最高纪录。同时，执行了天天检查、天天总结的制度，保证了安全生产。
　　鹤壁：产量增加工效提高坑木消耗减少
    据新华社郑州14日电　河南省鹤壁煤矿在6月全胜的基础上，7月上旬又夺得开门红，原煤平均日产量比国家计划提高11％，全员工效比国家计划提高12.3％。坑木消耗也比计划大大降低。
