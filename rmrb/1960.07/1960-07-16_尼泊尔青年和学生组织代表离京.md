Title: Nepalese Youth and Student Organization Representatives Leave Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 6

　  Xinhua News Agency, July 14th—The Nepalese youth and student organization representatives K.P. Shrestha, M.L. Shrestha, D.L. Dakwa, P.L. Shratend, and G.N.S. Davadi, who were invited by the All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation to visit China, departed Beijing by plane today via Kunming to return to their country after a friendly visit to China for nearly a month.

### 尼泊尔青年和学生组织代表离京

1960-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

　  尼泊尔青年和学生组织代表离京
　  据新华社14日讯　应中华全国青年联合会和中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的尼泊尔青年和学生组织代表克·普·什雷斯塔、姆·耳·什雷斯塔、德·尔·达克瓦、普·尔·什拉琴德、格·恩·斯·达瓦迪五人，在中国进行了将近一个月的友好访问后，今天乘机离京取道昆明回国。
