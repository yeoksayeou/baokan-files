Title: Sino-North Korean Academies of Science Sign Scientific Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 4th. A scientific cooperation agreement between the Academies of Science of China and North Korea was signed in Pyongyang today.

Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Park Sung-wook, Vice President of the Korean Academy of Sciences, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective academies. The two sides also signed the 1960 implementation plan for scientific cooperation between the Chinese and North Korean Academies of Science.

This agreement was signed by the Chinese and North Korean Academies of Science in accordance with the Sino-North Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement signed on February 21, 1959, in order to expand and develop scientific cooperation, exchange information on scientific activities and scientific research experience, thereby strengthening and developing the friendship between the two peoples.

The Korean side participating in the signing ceremony included: Bai Nanyun, President of the Korean Academy of Sciences, Park Wang-seop, Vice Chairman of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Liaison, and Xu Xixin, Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included: Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, all members of the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation, and Li Lin, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea.

Tonight, Park Sung-wook, Vice President of the Korean Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet to welcome the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation headed by Zhu Kezhen.

The Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation arrived in North Korea on June 24 at the invitation of the Korean Academy of Science.

### 中朝科学院签订科学合作协定

1960-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    中朝科学院签订科学合作协定
    新华社平壤4日电　中朝两国科学院科学合作协定今天在平壤签订。
    中国科学院副院长竺可桢和朝鲜科学院副院长朴成郁分别代表两国科学院在协定上签字。双方同时还签订了中朝两国科学院科学合作1960年执行计划。
    这一协定是中朝两国科学院根据1959年2月21日签订的中朝文化合作协定，为了扩大和发展科学合作，互相交换有关科学活动的资料和科学研究经验，从而加强和发展两国人民的友谊而签订的。
    朝鲜方面参加协定签字仪式的有：朝鲜科学院院长白南云、对外文化联络委员会副委员长朴旺燮、外务省第三局局长许锡信等。
  中国方面参加协定签字仪式的有：中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光、中国科学院代表团全体成员和中国驻朝鲜大使馆文化参赞李林。
    今天晚上，朝鲜科学院副院长朴成郁设宴招待以竺可桢为首的中国科学院代表团。
    中国科学院代表团是在6月24日应邀到达朝鲜访问的。
