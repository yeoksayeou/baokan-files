Title: Contemporary Hero (Picture)
Author: Luo Miaolun
Date: 1960-07-18
Page: 3

　　Contemporary Hero
    Li Peiliang (left), a young technician and Communist Party member from the Guangzhou Zhunsheng Weighing Instrument Factory, is not only actively improving the production of general products but also trial-producing high-precision products such as optical microbalances with a sensitivity of 1/100,000th of a gram and high-accuracy automatic electro-optical microbalances. He is already working on projects for 1962. Here he is seen working with an older technician to develop a new high-precision ultramicrobalance.
                                   Xinhua News Agency reporter Luo Miaolun photo

### 当代英雄（图片）
罗渺伦

第3版()
专栏：

　　当代英雄
    广州市准绳衡器厂青年技工、共产党员李培良（左），不但积极改进一般产品的生产，还试制了十万分之一克的光学微量天平和高精确度自动电光微量天平等高级精密产品。他现在已经在做1962年的工作了。这是他在和老技工一起研究试制新的高精度超微量天平
                                   新华社记者　罗渺伦摄
