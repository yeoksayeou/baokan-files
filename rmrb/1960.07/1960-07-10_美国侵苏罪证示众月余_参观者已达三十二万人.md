Title: American Crimes of Aggression Against the Soviet Union on Display for Over a Month: 320,000 Visitors

Author:

Date: 1960-07-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th July: According to TASS, more than 320,000 viewers have visited the exhibition of physical evidence and documents of the American aircraft's reconnaissance and espionage activities violating the Soviet border on May 1, 1960. The exhibition has been held in Moscow for a month and a half. One thousand four hundred and seventy-nine foreign delegations visited the exhibition. The total number of foreigners visiting the exhibition reached more than 20,000.

The audience at the exhibition unanimously agreed with the peaceful foreign policy pursued by the Soviet Union and its government, and expressed praise for the achievements of the Soviet Union in science and technology and the agile actions of the Soviet military.

At the same time, the audience strongly condemned the gangster behavior of the American aggressors. Two thousand three hundred visitors wrote their impressions in the guest book.

### 美国侵苏罪证示众月余  参观者已达三十二万人

1960-07-10
第5版()
专栏：

    美国侵苏罪证示众月余
    参观者已达三十二万人
    新华社莫斯科8日电　据塔斯社讯：有三十二万多观众参观了美国飞机在1960年5月1日侵犯苏联国境进行侦察间谍活动的实物和证件的展览会。展览会在莫斯科举办了一个半月的时间。有一千四百七十九个外国代表团参观了这个展览会。参观展览会的外国人共达二万多人。
    展览会的观众都一致赞同苏联和它的政府所奉行的和平外交政策，对苏联在科学技术上所取得的成就和苏联军人的灵敏行动表示赞扬。
    同时，观众还强烈地谴责了美国侵略者的强盗行为。有两千三百个参观者在留言簿上写下了观后感。
