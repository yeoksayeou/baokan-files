Title: Moroccan Student Union Holds Congress, Demands Immediate Withdrawal of Foreign Troops from Morocco
Author:
Date: 1960-07-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Casablanca, July 20th: The fifth congress of the Moroccan National Student Union, which closed in Casablanca tonight, passed a resolution stating that true peace can only be achieved by taking active measures to eliminate imperialism and all forms of exploitation.

    The resolution praised the heroic struggles of students in Japan, Cuba, Turkey, and South Korea against imperialism, dictatorial regimes, and American aggression and intervention, and expressed solidarity with Latin American students in their struggle against imperialism and dictatorial regimes.

    The congress demanded the immediate withdrawal of foreign troops.

    The congress also reaffirmed the principle of unity of the African student movement on the basis of the struggle against imperialism and colonialism, and the representatives reaffirmed their support for the Algerian struggle.

    Regarding domestic policy, the resolution passed by the congress called for the realization of economic liberation, land reform, and the recovery of land seized by colonialists. The resolution emphasized that Morocco's industrialization must be consistent with nationalization.

### 摩洛哥学联举行代表大会  要求外国军队立即撤出摩洛哥

1960-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥学联举行代表大会
    要求外国军队立即撤出摩洛哥
    据新华社卡萨布兰卡20日电  今天晚上在卡萨布兰卡闭幕的摩洛哥全国学生联合会第五届代表大会通过决议，认为只有采取积极措施消灭帝国主义和各种形式的剥削，才能获得真正的和平。
    这项决议赞扬日本、古巴、土耳其和南朝鲜的学生为反对帝国主义、独裁政权以及美国的侵略和干涉行动而展开的英勇斗争，并表示声援拉丁美洲学生反对帝国主义和独裁政权的斗争。
    代表大会要求立即撤出外国军队。
    这次代表大会还重申了非洲学生运动在反对帝国主义和殖民主义斗争的基础上团结一致的原则，代表还重申了他们对阿尔及利亚斗争的支持。
    关于国内政策，大会通过的决议要求在国内实现经济解放、进行土地改革和收复殖民主义者霸占的土地。决议着重指出，摩洛哥的工业化必须同国有化相一致。
