Title: The United Arab Republic Refuses Britain's Request to Upgrade Diplomatic Representation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 26th: "The Republic" newspaper reported today that the United Arab Republic has refused Britain's request to upgrade the diplomatic representation between the UAR and Britain to the ambassadorial level.

Currently, the diplomatic representatives exchanged between the two countries are Chargé d'Affaires.

The newspaper quoted UAR officials as saying that as long as British policy remains unchanged, the current status of diplomatic representation between Cairo and London will not be altered.

### 阿联拒绝英国外交代表升格的要求

1960-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联拒绝英国外交代表升格的要求
    新华社开罗26日电  “共和国报”今天报道，阿拉伯联合共和国已经拒绝英国提出的把阿联和英国的外交代表升格为大使级的要求。
    目前，这两国互派的外交代表是代办。
    这家报纸援引阿联负责人士的话说，只要英国政策不变，开罗和伦敦之间的外交代表的目前地位就不会变更。
