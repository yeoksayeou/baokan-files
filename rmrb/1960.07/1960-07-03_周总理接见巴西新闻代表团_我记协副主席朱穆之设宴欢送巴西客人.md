Title: Premier Zhou Receives Brazilian News Delegation; Vice President Zhu Muzhi of the All-China Journalists Association Hosts Banquet to See Off Brazilian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-07-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received the Brazilian journalists delegation headed by Carlos Alberto Costa Pinto, head of the Brazilian National Federation of Professional Journalists, today. Premier Zhou Enlai answered questions raised by the delegation during the meeting.

Present at the reception were Zhu Muzhi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, Shao Zonghan, Standing Director, and Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd: Zhu Muzhi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, hosted a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the Brazilian journalists delegation headed by Carlos Alberto Costa Pinto, head of the Brazilian National Federation of Professional Journalists.

Among those present at the banquet were Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association, Shao Zonghan, Standing Director, and people from the capital's journalistic circles. ### 周总理接见巴西新闻代表团  我记协副主席朱穆之设宴欢送巴西客人

1960-07-03
第6版()
专栏：

    周总理接见巴西新闻代表团
    我记协副主席朱穆之设宴欢送巴西客人
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在今天接见了以巴西全国职业新闻工作者联合会负责人卡洛斯·阿耳贝托·科斯塔·平托为首的巴西新闻工作者代表团。周恩来总理在接见的时候回答了代表团提出的问题。
    接见时在座的有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之、常务理事邵宗汉，外交部新闻司司长龚澎。
    新华社2日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之今天晚间设宴欢送以巴西全国职业新闻工作者联合会负责人卡洛斯·阿耳贝托·科斯塔·平托为首的巴西新闻工作者代表团。
    出席宴会的，有外交部新闻司司长龚澎，中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处书记王揖、常务理事邵宗汉等，以及首都新闻界人士。（附图片）
    周恩来总理七月二日接见了以卡洛斯·阿尔贝托·科斯塔·平托为首的巴西新闻工作者代表团。图为接见后合影。右起第四人是团长卡洛斯·阿尔贝托·科斯塔·平托。
                          新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
