Title: UN Security Council to Discuss Cuba's Accusations Against the United States
Author:
Date: 1960-07-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th, New York Dispatch: The United Nations headquarters announced on the 12th that the United Nations Security Council will hold a meeting on July 18th to discuss the situation arising from the United States' acts of aggression against Cuba.

The Security Council decided to convene this meeting at the request of the Cuban Minister of Foreign Affairs. In a letter submitted to the President of the Security Council, Cuban Foreign Minister Raúl Roa pointed out that the situation arising from the United States' acts of aggression against Cuba constitutes a threat to universal peace and security.

The United Nations Secretariat distributed the letter from the Cuban Foreign Minister on the evening of the 11th.

### 联合国安全理事会　将讨论古巴对美国的控诉

1960-07-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　联合国安全理事会
　　将讨论古巴对美国的控诉
    新华社13日讯　纽约消息：联合国总部在12日宣布，联合国安全理事会将在7月18日举行会议，讨论由于美国对古巴的侵略行动所引起的局势。
    安全理事会是应古巴外交部长的请求决定召开这次会议。古巴外交部长劳尔·罗亚在他提交安全理事会主席的一封信件中指出，由于美国对古巴的侵略行动而引起的局势已经构成对普遍和平和安全的威胁。
    联合国秘书处在11日晚间散发了古巴外交部长的这封信件。
