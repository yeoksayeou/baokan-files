Title: Denouncing the Crimes of the U.S.-Ngo Dinh Diem Clique: Grand Joint Performance Held in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1960-07-18
Page: 5

　　Denouncing the Crimes of the U.S.-Ngo Dinh Diem Clique
　　Hanoi Holds Grand Joint Performance
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 16th: The Central People's Cultural Troupe of Vietnam, the People's Army Cultural Troupe, the Southern Cai Luong Troupe, the Fifth Interzone Folk Song Troupe, the Radio Station Cultural Troupe, the National Cultural Troupe, and other artistic groups held a large-scale joint performance at the Hanoi People's Theater tonight to oppose the crimes of the U.S.-Ngo Dinh Diem clique, which was warmly welcomed by more than 5,000 spectators.
    Before the performance began, the famous writer and poet Lưu Trọng Lư spoke at the meeting. He said that our feelings and our roars are united with the roars of the Japanese people, the South Korean people, and the Cuban people.
    The masses present raised their arms and shouted: "U.S. imperialism get out of South Vietnam!"
    The 300 actors participating in tonight's performance presented seventeen programs, including dramas, small-scale singing, living newspaper performances, songs and dances, etc.
    Vietnamese famous poets Tố Hữu, Nguyễn Đình Thi, and Lưu Trọng Lư recited their works at the meeting tonight.
    Lê Duẩn, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, and Deputy Prime Minister Phạm Hùng watched tonight's performance.

### 声讨美吴集团罪行　河内举行盛大联合演出

1960-07-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　声讨美吴集团罪行
　　河内举行盛大联合演出
    据新华社河内16日电　越南中央人民文工团、人民军文工团、南部改良剧团、第五联区民歌团、广播电台文工团、民族文工团等文艺团体，今天晚上在河内人民剧场举行了大规模的反对美国—吴庭艳集团罪行联合演出，受到五千多名观众的热烈欢迎。
    演出开始前，著名作家、诗人刘仲庐在会上讲了话。他说，我们的心情，我们的吼声是同日本人民、南朝鲜人民和古巴人民的吼声联成一片的。
    到会群众振臂高呼：“美帝国主义滚出南越”。
    参加今晚演出的三百名演员演出了十七个节目，其中有话剧、小演唱、活报剧、歌舞等。
    越南著名诗人素友、阮亭诗、刘仲庐今晚在会上朗诵了他们的作品。
    越南劳动党中央政治局委员黎笋、副总理范雄观看了今晚的演出。
