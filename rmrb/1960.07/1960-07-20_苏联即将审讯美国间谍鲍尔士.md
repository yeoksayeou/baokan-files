Title: Soviet Union to Try American Spy Powers

Author:
Date: 1960-07-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th - TASS reports: The Military Collegium of the Supreme Court of the Soviet Union is scheduled to try American spy plane pilot Powers on August 17th. The trial will be open to the public.

Powers is accused of espionage under Soviet criminal law.

### 苏联即将审讯美国间谍鲍尔士

1960-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联即将审讯美国间谍鲍尔士
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科18日讯：苏联最高法院军事委员会定于8月17日审讯美国间谍飞机驾驶员鲍尔士。审讯将公开进行。
    根据苏联刑法，鲍尔士被控告进行间谍活动。
