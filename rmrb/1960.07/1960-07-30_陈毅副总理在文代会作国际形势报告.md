Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Delivers Report on International Situation at Literary and Art Congress

Author:

Date: 1960-07-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th—All the delegates to the Third National Congress of Literary and Art Workers this morning listened to a report on the international situation by Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council. This afternoon, the delegates held a warm discussion on Vice Premier Chen Yi's report.

### 陈毅副总理在文代会作国际形势报告

1960-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理在文代会作国际形势报告
    新华社29日讯　中国文学艺术工作者第三次代表大会的全体代表，今天上午听取了国务院副总理陈毅关于国际形势问题的报告。下午，代表们对陈毅副总理的报告进行了热烈的讨论。
