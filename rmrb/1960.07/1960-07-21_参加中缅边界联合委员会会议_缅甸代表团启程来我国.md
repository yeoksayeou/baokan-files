Title: Burmese Delegation Departs for Our Country to Attend Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-07-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, July 20th: The Burmese delegation, headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, left here by plane this morning for Beijing to attend the second meeting of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee.

The representatives of the Burmese delegation are: Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyao Winn; Secretary of the Kachin State Affairs Department, U San Lun; Director of the Border Areas Administration Bureau, Colonel Soe Min; and Commissioner of the Northern Shan State, U Khun Aung Gyi. The delegation has several advisors.

Those who saw them off at the airport included James Barrington, Permanent Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Foreign Affairs; U Ba Chit, Director of the Asian-African Department; U Ba Maung, Director of Protocol; Colonel Khin Nyo; Lieutenant Colonel Kyi Han; and Lieutenant Colonel Chit Khin.

Lei Yang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and other counselors also saw them off at the airport.

The Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee held its first meeting in Rangoon from June 27 to July 5.

### 参加中缅边界联合委员会会议  缅甸代表团启程来我国

1960-07-21
第6版()
专栏：

    参加中缅边界联合委员会会议
    缅甸代表团启程来我国
    新华社仰光20日电　以缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季准将为首席代表的缅甸代表团今天上午乘飞机离开这里前往北京参加中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议。
    缅甸代表团代表有：缅甸驻中国大使叫温，克钦邦事务部秘书吴山伦，边境地区管理局局长苏敏上校，北掸邦专员吴昆翁佳。代表团有顾问多人。
    到机场送行的有缅甸外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿，亚非司司长吴巴漆，礼宾司司长吴巴茂，钦纽上校，季汉中校和漆钦中校。
    到机场送行的还有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬和其他参赞。
    中缅边界联合委员会从6月27日到7月5日已经在仰光举行了第一次会议。
