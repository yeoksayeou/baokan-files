Title: Typhoon Alert
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 4

    Typhoon Alert
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued the following typhoon warning at 12:00 noon on the 23rd:
    The fifth typhoon of 1960 in the Western Pacific was centered this morning at 2:00 AM approximately 500 kilometers east of Taiwan; that is, around 24.0 degrees North latitude and 126.9 degrees East longitude. The maximum wind force near the center is above level 12. Currently, the typhoon center is moving northwest at a speed of three to five nautical miles per hour. It is expected that on the 25th, the wind force along the central and northern coast of Zhejiang and the southern coast of Jiangsu will gradually increase to level 7-9, with heavy rain; the wind force on the nearby sea will increase to level 8-10; and the northern coast of Fujian will also have strong winds of level 6-8. It is estimated that on the 26th, the typhoon may make landfall along the coast of Zhejiang and southern Jiangsu.

### 台风警报

1960-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    台风警报
    据新华社讯　中央气象科学研究所23日中午十二时发布台风警报如下：
    西部太平洋上1960年第五号台风，今天早晨二时中心位置在台湾以东五百公里左右的洋面上；即北纬二十四点零度东经一百二十六点九度附近。中心附近最大风力在十二级以上。目前台风中心正以每小时三到五海里的速度向西北方向移动。预计25日浙江中部北部沿海、江苏南部沿海的风力将逐渐加大到七至九级，并有暴雨；附近海面的风力将加大到八至十级；福建北部沿海也将有六至八级的大风。估计26日台风可能在浙江和江苏南部一带沿海登陆。
