Title: Bolivian Senate Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Congressman Arellano Arrives on the Same Flight

Author:
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 15th: A five-member Bolivian Senate delegation led by the First Vice-President of the Bolivian Senate, Oscar Donoso Lopez, and Jaime Arellano Casaneda, a member of the Bolivian House of Representatives, arrived in Beijing by plane at noon today. They are visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 玻利维亚参议院代表团到京  众议院议员阿雷利亚诺同机抵达

1960-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

　  玻利维亚参议院代表团到京
　  众议院议员阿雷利亚诺同机抵达
    据新华社15日讯　由玻利维亚参议院第一副议长奥斯卡·多诺索·洛佩斯率领的玻利维亚参议院代表团一行五人和玻利维亚众议院议员海梅·阿雷利亚诺·卡萨内达，今天中午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中国人民外交学会的邀请前来我国访问的。
