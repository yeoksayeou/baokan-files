Title: Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang Holds Reception for Chinese Representatives to the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, July 3rd. Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang held a reception here on the evening of the 3rd to entertain all the representatives and advisors on the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee, headed by Yao Zhongming.

Among those present at the reception were Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities Sao Khun Kyi, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Mann Ba Sae, Attorney General U Yan Aung, Chief Justice of the High Court U Chan Tun Aung, Minister of Foreign Affairs Sao Khun Cho, Minister of Justice and Education U E Maung, former Minister of Culture U Chit Thoung, Burmese Prime Minister Advisor U Wang, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs James Barrington and other senior officials.

Also present at the reception were all the representatives and advisors on the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee, headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Defence Forces.

The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests frequently toasted to further strengthening Sino-Burmese friendship and to an early and comprehensive resolution of the Sino-Burmese border issue.

### 我驻缅大使李一氓举行招待会  招待中缅联合委员会中国方面代表

1960-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻缅大使李一氓举行招待会
    招待中缅联合委员会中国方面代表
    新华社仰光3日电　中国驻缅甸大使李一氓3日晚在这里举行招待会，招待以姚仲明为首的中缅联合委员会中国方面的全体代表和顾问。
    出席招待会的有缅甸联邦总理吴努，民族院议长藻昆基，代表院议长曼巴赛，检察总长吴延昂，高等法院首席法官吴千吞昂，外交部长藻昆卓，司法和教育部长吴埃貌，前文化部长吴漆东，缅甸总理顾问吴旺，外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿和其他高级官员。
    出席招待会的还有以国防军副总参谋长昂季准将为首的中缅联合委员会缅甸方面的全体代表和顾问。
    招待会是在热烈和友好的气氛中举行的。宾主双方频频为进一步加强中缅友谊和早日全面解决中缅边界问题而干杯。
