Title: UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld Requests Countries Outside of Africa to Participate in UN Forces; US Air Force Rushes UN Troops to the Congo
Author:
Date: 1960-07-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 18th, New York: The United Nations Headquarters announced on the 17th that UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld has requested three European countries, one Asian country, and one Latin American country to send troops to the Congo to participate in the UN forces, and has consulted with these countries on this matter. The names of these countries were not disclosed.

The communiqué issued by the UN Headquarters also stated that some UN forces that have arrived in the Congo have been deployed by the acting commander of the UN forces, the American Bunch.

News from Leopoldville, the capital of the Congo, said that UN military officers and Belgian military officers held a military staff meeting on the 16th. After the meeting, the UN forces began to occupy the strongholds assigned to them by the meeting.

Currently, the United States is mobilizing more military aircraft to rush UN troops to the Congo day and night.

It is reported that by the 17th, parts of the troops from Tunisia, Ghana, Morocco, and Ethiopia participating in this force had arrived in the Congolese capital. Other news said that a UN force composed of parts of the armies of Ghana and Ethiopia had been airlifted by US transport planes to Stanleyville, where Congolese soldiers and civilians had once risen up to oppose Belgian colonialists. The commander of the Ghanaian troops (English) participating in the UN force, Alexander, has also arrived there, along with a representative of the US Embassy in the Congo and the former Belgian commander of the colonial army in Stanleyville.

### 联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德　要求非洲以外的国家参加联合国军　美国空军赶运联合国军队去刚果

1960-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德
　　要求非洲以外的国家参加联合国军
　　美国空军赶运联合国军队去刚果
    据新华社18日讯　纽约消息：联合国总部17日宣布，联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德已经要求三个欧洲国家、一个亚洲国家和一个拉丁美洲国家派军队去刚果参加联合国军，并且已经就这件事同这些国家进行了磋商。这些国家的名字没有透露。
    联合国总部发表的公报还说，已经到达刚果的某些联合国军部队，已经由联合国军代理司令、美国人本奇进行了部署。
    来自刚果首都利奥波德维尔的消息说，联合国军的军官和比利时军官在16日举行了军事参谋会议。会后，联合国军开始占领会议指派给他们的据点。
    目前，美国正在调动更多的军用飞机日夜赶运联合国的军队去刚果。
    据报道，到17日，参加这支部队的突尼斯、加纳、摩洛哥和埃塞俄比亚等国的军队的一部分已经到达刚果首都。另有消息说，一支由加纳和埃塞俄比亚一部分军队组成的联合国部队已由美国运输机空运到刚果军民曾群起反对比利时殖民者的斯坦利维尔。参加联合国部队的加纳军队的指挥官亚历山大（英籍）也已到达那里，同他一起去那里的，还有美国驻刚果大使馆的一位代表和前比利时在斯坦利维尔殖民军司令。
