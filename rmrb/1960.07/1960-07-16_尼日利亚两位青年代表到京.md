Title: Two Nigerian Youth Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 6

    Two Nigerian Youth Representatives Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, July 15th: At the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation, the Nigerian youth representatives, President of the Nigerian Union Abe Sugun and Secretary of the Women's Department of the Nigerian Union, Esan Mangali, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 尼日利亚两位青年代表到京

1960-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

    尼日利亚两位青年代表到京
    新华社15日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请前来我国访问的尼日利亚青年代表：尼日利亚联盟主席阿别苏根和尼日利亚联盟妇女部秘书埃桑·曼加利，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
