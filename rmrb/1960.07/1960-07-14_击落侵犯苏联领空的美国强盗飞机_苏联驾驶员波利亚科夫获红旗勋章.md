Title: Soviet Pilot Polyakov Awarded Order of the Red Banner for Shooting Down American Bandit Plane that Violated Soviet Airspace
Author:
Date: 1960-07-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - Soviet fighter pilot, Captain Polyakov, has been awarded the Order of the Red Banner for shooting down an American "RB-47" reconnaissance bomber that violated Soviet airspace on July 1st.

### 击落侵犯苏联领空的美国强盗飞机　苏联驾驶员波利亚科夫获红旗勋章

1960-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　击落侵犯苏联领空的美国强盗飞机
　　苏联驾驶员波利亚科夫获红旗勋章
    新华社12日讯　苏联歼击机驾驶员波利亚科夫大尉，由于在7月1日击落了侵犯苏联领空的美国“RB—47型”侦察轰炸机，被授予红旗勋章。
