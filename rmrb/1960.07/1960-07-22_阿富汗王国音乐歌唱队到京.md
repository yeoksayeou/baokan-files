Title: Afghan Royal Music and Singing Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st. The Afghan Royal Music and Singing Troupe, a group of seventeen people led by Abdul Breshna, a famous Afghan painter and composer, arrived in Beijing by plane at noon today at the invitation of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries for a performance tour in our country.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them were: Ding Xilin, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Huang Hui, Deputy Manager of the China Performing Arts Agency, and people from the music community in the capital.

Abdul Samad, the Ambassador of the Kingdom of Afghanistan to China, and diplomatic officials from the embassy also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 阿富汗王国音乐歌唱队到京

1960-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗王国音乐歌唱队到京
    新华社21日讯　应对外文化联络委员会邀请来我国访问演出的阿富汗王国音乐歌唱队一行十七人，在阿富汗著名画家、作曲家阿卜杜勒·布里希纳的率领下，今天中午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：对外文委副主任丁西林，中国演出经理公司副经理黄回以及首都音乐界人士。
    阿富汗王国驻华大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德和大使馆外交官员也到机场欢迎。
