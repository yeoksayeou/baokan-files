Title: US Aerial Espionage Case Investigation Completed; Soviet Union to Indict Powers; Case to be Tried by the Military Collegium of the Supreme Court
Author:
Date: 1960-07-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 10th: TASS Moscow, July 9th dispatch: The State Security Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union has completed the investigation of the case of American spy Powers.

Powers intruded into Soviet territory on May 1, 1960, aboard a "Lockheed-U2" aircraft to gather espionage intelligence. This spy plane was shot down by a Soviet rocket at an altitude of over 20,000 meters.

Powers will be held criminally liable under Article 2 of the Criminal Law regarding crimes against the state (espionage). The indictment against Powers has been approved by the Procurator General of the Soviet Union, and Powers will be prosecuted. According to Articles 9 and 1 of the Regulations on Military Tribunals, the case will be tried by the Military Collegium of the Supreme Court of the Soviet Union.

### 美国空中间谍案已结束侦讯　苏联将对鲍尔士提起公诉　案件由最高法院军事委员会审理

1960-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　美国空中间谍案已结束侦讯
　　苏联将对鲍尔士提起公诉
　　案件由最高法院军事委员会审理
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科9日讯：苏联部长会议国家安全委员会结束了对美国间谍鲍尔士一案的侦讯。
    鲍尔士曾于1960年5月1日乘“洛克希德—U2”型飞机侵入苏联国境收集间谍情报，这架间谍飞机在两万米以上的高空被苏联火箭击落。
    根据刑法关于国事罪的第二条
  （间谍活动）向鲍尔士追究刑事责任。对鲍尔士的起诉书已由苏联总检察长批准，将对鲍尔士提起公诉。根据关于军事法庭条例的第九条和第一条，这个案件由苏联最高法院军事委员会审理。
