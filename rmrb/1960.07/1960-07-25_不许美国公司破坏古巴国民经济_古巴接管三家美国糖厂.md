Title: U.S. Companies Not Allowed to Sabotage the Cuban National Economy: Cuba Takes Over Three American Sugar Factories
Author:
Date: 1960-07-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, July 22nd: The Cuban National Institute of Agrarian Reform has taken over the Chaparra, Delicias, and Mercedita sugar factories belonging to the American Cuban American Sugar Company.

The resolution on the takeover stated that the Cuban American Sugar Company attempted to sabotage the Cuban national economy by closing these sugar factories and refusing to pay wages to Cuban tenant farmers for this year's sugar season. The resolution emphasized that this behavior endangers national interests and violates current sugar laws.

The Chaparra and Delicias sugar factories are the two largest of the thirty-seven American sugar factories in Cuba. Last year, they produced 793,600 bags (250 pounds per bag) and 1,202,000 bags of sugar, respectively. These two sugar factories each own 4,000 and 6,000 caballerias of land (one caballeria equals 201.45 Chinese mu). The Mercedita sugar factory produced 240,712 bags of sugar last year and owns 439 caballerias of land.

The National Institute of Agrarian Reform announced yesterday that more than 300 sugarcane cooperatives with 30,000 caballerias of land have been established nationwide. These cooperatives have 60,000 members, and they will produce 1 billion arrobas (one arroba equals 11.33 kilograms) of sugarcane annually according to basic quotas. Under the mobilization of the National Federation of Sugar Industry Workers, 1,000 sugarcane production cooperatives will be established nationwide in the near future.

### 不许美国公司破坏古巴国民经济  古巴接管三家美国糖厂

1960-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    不许美国公司破坏古巴国民经济
    古巴接管三家美国糖厂
    新华社哈瓦那22日电　古巴土地改革全国委员会接管了属于美国古—美糖公司的查帕拉、德利夏斯和梅塞迪塔三家糖厂。
    关于接管的决议说，古—美糖公司企图用关闭这些糖厂和拒绝向古巴佃农发付今年蔗糖季节的工资的办法来破坏古巴的国民经济。决议强调指出，这种行为危害了民族的利益，破坏了现行的食糖法。
    查帕拉和德利夏斯糖厂是美国在古巴三十七家糖厂中最大的两家糖厂，去年分别生产了食糖七十九万三千六百袋（每袋二百五十磅）和一百二十万二千袋，这两家糖厂各拥有四千和六千卡瓦耶里亚的土地（一卡瓦耶里亚合二百零一点四五中国亩）。梅塞迪塔糖厂去年糖的产量为二十四万零七百十二袋，拥有土地四百三十九卡瓦耶里亚。
    土地改革全国委员会昨天宣布，全国已经建立起拥有三万卡瓦耶里亚土地的三百多个甘蔗合作社。这些合作社有六万社员，它们每年将按基本定额生产十亿阿罗瓦（一阿罗瓦合十一点三三公斤）的甘蔗。在全国糖业工人联合会的动员下，短期内将在全国建立起一千个甘蔗生产合作社。
