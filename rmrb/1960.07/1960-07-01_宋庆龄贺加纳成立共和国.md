Title: Soong Ching-ling Congratulates Ghana on the Establishment of the Republic
Author:
Date: 1960-07-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Soong Ching-ling sent a telegram on June 28th to Kwame Nkrumah, President of the Republic of Ghana, congratulating Ghana on the establishment of the Republic and congratulating him on his inauguration as President of the Republic of Ghana. The telegram is as follows:
His Excellency Kwame Nkrumah, President of the Republic of Ghana:
    Mr. Stephen, Chargé d'Affaires of the British Embassy in China, informed me on June 28, 1960, that the Ghanaian government invited me to attend the ceremony celebrating the establishment of the Republic in your country. I express my heartfelt thanks for your kind invitation. However, due to the urgency of time and my health, I am unable to travel to your country to attend the celebration. I apologize for this.
    I extend my warm congratulations on the establishment of the Republic of Ghana and Your Excellency's inauguration as President of the Republic. I wish Your Excellency and the people of Ghana new achievements in the cause of national prosperity and world peace, and I wish the friendship between the Chinese and Ghanaian people further development.
                                 Soong Ching-ling, June 28, 1960, Beijing

### 宋庆龄贺加纳成立共和国

1960-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄贺加纳成立共和国
    新华社1日讯　宋庆龄6月28日致电加纳共和国总统克瓦米·恩克鲁玛，祝贺加纳成立共和国，并祝贺他就任加纳共和国总统。电文如下：加纳共和国克瓦米·恩克鲁玛总统阁下：
    英国驻中华人民共和国代办施棣华先生于1960年6月28日转告，加纳政府邀请我前去参加贵国庆祝共和国成立的典礼，我对贵国的盛情邀请表示衷心感谢。但是，由于时间过于紧迫和我的健康情况，我不能前往贵国参加庆典，对此，我表示歉意。
    我对加纳共和国的成立和阁下就任共和国总统表示热烈祝贺，祝阁下和加纳人民在为国家繁荣和世界和平的事业中取得新的成就，并祝中、加两国人民的友谊进一步发展。
                                 宋庆龄　1960年6月28日于北京
