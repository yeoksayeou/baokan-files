Title: Ceylon Freedom Party's New Cabinet Sworn In; Prime Minister Mrs. Bandaranaike Concurrently Holds Posts of Minister of Defense and Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1960-07-25
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, July 23rd: The eleven-member cabinet of the Ceylon Freedom Party was sworn in tonight. Prime Minister Sirimavo Bandaranaike concurrently holds the posts of Minister of Defense and Minister of Foreign Affairs. The other members of the cabinet include: K.P. de Silva, Minister of Agriculture, Land, Irrigation and Power; Felix Dias Bandaranaike, Minister of Finance; Sam P.C. Fernando, Minister of Justice; Badiudin Mahmud, Minister of Education and Broadcasting; Mahanama Samaraweera, Minister of Local Government and Housing; Maitripala Senanayake, Minister of Home Affairs, Industry and Culture; and K. Wijesinghe, Minister of Labour and Nationalized Services.

### 锡兰自由党新阁宣誓就职  总理班达拉奈克夫人兼长国防和外交

1960-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰自由党新阁宣誓就职
    总理班达拉奈克夫人兼长国防和外交
    新华社科伦坡23日电　锡兰自由党的十一人内阁已于今晚宣誓就职。总理西丽马沃·班达拉奈克夫人兼国防部长和外交部长。内阁的其他成员包括：农业、土地、灌溉和电力部长克·帕·德席尔瓦，财政部长费利克利·迪亚斯·班达拉奈克，司法部长萨姆·帕·克·费尔南多，教育和广播部长巴迪乌丁·马哈茂德，地方管理和住房部长马哈纳马·萨马拉韦拉，内政、工业和文化部长迈特里帕拉·森纳那亚克，劳工和国有化服务事业部长克·维杰辛格。
