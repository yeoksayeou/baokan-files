Title: Cuban Cabinet Approves National Development Plan with a Budget of Four Hundred Million Pesos
Author:
Date: 1960-07-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 30th - The Cuban government held a cabinet meeting last night and approved the national budget and national development plan for the six months from July to December this year. The total budget amounts to 400 million pesos, including 52 million pesos for education. 65 million pesos in the budget will be used for public works, and 10 million pesos will be used to subsidize municipalities in implementing school construction plans.
    The cabinet meeting accepted the resignation of the Minister of Transportation, Enrique Oltuski, and appointed Raul Curbelo Morales as the Minister of Transportation.

### 古巴内阁通过国家发展计划  预算总额达四亿比索

1960-07-02
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴内阁通过国家发展计划
    预算总额达四亿比索
    新华社哈瓦那30日电　古巴政府昨晚举行内阁会议，通过了今年7月到12月六个月的国家预算和国家发展计划。这项预算的总额达四亿比索。其中教育费用为五千二百万比索。预算中有六千五百万比索用于公共工程，一千万比索用于补助各市实现兴建学校的计划。
    内阁会议接受交通部长恩里克·奥耳图斯基提出的辞呈，并任命劳尔·库尔维洛·莫拉莱斯为交通部长。
