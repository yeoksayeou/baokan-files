Title: Prince Sihanouk's Three Sons Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 20th — Prince Norodom Sihanouk of the Kingdom of Cambodia's three sons, Yuwana, Naradipo, and Kemarunurak, who came to China to study, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon, accompanied by the Cambodian Ambassador to China, Lan ?.
Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department, Wu Qing, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Commission, and others, were at the station to greet them. Zhou Suoli, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Cambodian Embassy in China, and diplomatic officials were also at the station to greet them.

1960-07-21
第6版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王的三位王子到京
    新华社20日讯　来我国学习的柬埔寨王国诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王的三位王子：尤瓦那、纳拉迪波、克马努拉克，由柬埔寨驻中国大使兰·?特陪同，于今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮，礼宾司司长俞沛文，对外文委副司长吴青等人，都到车站迎接。柬埔寨驻中国大使馆临时代办周索里和外交官员也到车站迎接。
