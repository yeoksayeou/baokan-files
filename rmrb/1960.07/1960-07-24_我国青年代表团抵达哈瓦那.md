Title: China Youth Delegation Arrives in Havana
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, July 22nd. The Chinese youth delegation, composed of three members and headed by Zhang Chao, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, arrived in Havana by plane this afternoon. They were invited to attend the First Latin American Youth Congress scheduled to open on July 26 in the Sierra Maestra. Chilean youth representatives and the youth delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea also arrived on the same flight.

### 我国青年代表团抵达哈瓦那

1960-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    我国青年代表团抵达哈瓦那
    新华社哈瓦那22日电　以中华全国青年联合会副主席、中国共产主义青年团中央书记处书记张超为首的，由三人组成的中国青年代表团今天下午乘飞机到达哈瓦那，他们应邀参加定于7月26日在马埃斯特腊山开幕的第一届拉丁美洲青年代表大会。同机到达的还有智利青年代表和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国青年代表团。
