Title: Indonesian Cabinet Decides Nasution to be in Charge of Foreign Nationals Affairs
Author:
Date: 1960-07-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 20th - According to Antara News Agency, the Indonesian Inner Cabinet approved a presidential decree at a meeting held on the night of the 19th, appointing National Security Minister General Nasution as the minister in charge of the entry, exit, and residence affairs of foreign nationals in Indonesia.

### 印度尼西亚内阁决定  纳苏蒂安负责外侨事务

1960-07-22
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚内阁决定
    纳苏蒂安负责外侨事务
    新华社雅加达20日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚核心内阁在19日夜间举行的会议上批准了一项总统决定，任命国家安全部长纳苏蒂安将军为负责印度尼西亚外侨的进出和居留事务的部长。
