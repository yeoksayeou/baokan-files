Title: Liu Chairman Sends Congratulations on Ethiopian Emperor's Birthday and National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-07-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram on July 21st to congratulate Ethiopian Emperor Haile Selassie I on his birthday and Ethiopia's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:

His Majesty Haile Selassie I, Emperor of the Kingdom of Ethiopia:

On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday and Your country's National Day, I, on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, extend my sincere congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of your country. I wish for the further development of the friendship between the peoples of China and Ethiopia. I wish Your Majesty good health.

		Liu Shaoqi
		Chairman of the People's Republic of China
		July 21, 1960

### 刘主席电贺埃塞俄比亚皇帝寿辰和国庆

1960-07-23
第2版()
专栏：

    刘主席电贺埃塞俄比亚皇帝寿辰和国庆
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇在7月21日打电报祝贺埃塞俄比亚皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世的寿辰和埃塞俄比亚的国庆，电文如下：埃塞俄比亚王国皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世陛下：值此陛下寿辰和贵国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向陛下和贵国人民表示热诚的祝贺。祝中国和埃塞俄比亚两国人民之间的友谊获得进一步的发展。祝陛下身体健康。
              　中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
              　1960年7月21日
