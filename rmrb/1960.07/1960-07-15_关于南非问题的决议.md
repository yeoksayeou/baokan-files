Author:
Date: 1960-07-15
Page: 6

    Resolution on the South African Issue
    The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council believes that the policies of apartheid and racial discrimination constitute a most cruel and inhuman exploitation of colored people in South Africa under the pretext of race and color. This policy, which is arousing the growing anger and opposition of people all over the world, is not only a serious threat to peace in the region, but also a serious threat to international peace and security.
    Therefore, the Standing Committee calls on all peace-loving people to do their utmost to support the growing worldwide movement to isolate the South African government economically and politically, and to ensure that the United Nations implements effective economic sanctions to end this tragic situation.
    The Standing Committee pledges its full support to the struggle of the South African people to eliminate apartheid and white supremacy, and to establish a fully democratic country based on universal adult suffrage regardless of race, color or gender.
                              Standing Committee of the World Peace Council
                                    Stockholm, July 11, 1960

### 关于南非问题的决议

1960-07-15
第6版()
专栏：

    关于南非问题的决议
    世界和平理事会常务委员会认为，种族隔离和种族歧视政策是以种族和肤色为借口对南非有色人种进行的一种最残酷和最不人道的剥削。激起全世界人民的日益增长的愤怒和反对的这项政策不仅是对这个地区的和平的严重威胁，而且也是对国际和平和安全的严重威胁。
    因此，常务委员会号召一切爱好和平的人民尽最大努力来支持日益高涨的世界性运动，在经济上和政治上孤立南非政府，保证由联合国实行有效的经济制裁，以结束这种悲惨局面。
    常务委员会保证完全支持南非人民为争取消灭种族隔离和白人优越地位，争取在不分种族、肤色和性别的成人普选的基础上建立一个完全民主的国家而进行的斗争。
                              世界和平理事会常务委员会
                                    斯德哥尔摩1960年7月11日
