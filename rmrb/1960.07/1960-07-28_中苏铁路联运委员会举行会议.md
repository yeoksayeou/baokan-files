Title: Sino-Soviet Railway Joint Transportation Committee Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-07-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Mudanjiang, 27th July. The Sino-Soviet Border Railway Joint Transportation Committee meeting was held in Mudanjiang City, Heilongjiang Province from July 15th to 25th.

This meeting was a routine annual business meeting held in accordance with the provisions of the Sino-Soviet Border Railway Joint Transportation Agreement. At the meeting, the railway delegations of China and the Soviet Union conducted discussions on strengthening the joint transportation work of the border railways of the two countries; and reached an agreement on issues such as accelerating the turnover of locomotives and vehicles. At the closing ceremony, the heads of the railway delegations of China and the Soviet Union delivered speeches respectively, unanimously pointing out that the signing of the minutes of this Sino-Soviet Border Railway Joint Transportation Committee meeting will further strengthen the railway joint transportation work between China and the Soviet Union and consolidate the friendship and unity between the people and railway workers of the two countries.

### 中苏铁路联运委员会举行会议

1960-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中苏铁路联运委员会举行会议
    新华社牡丹江27日电　中苏国境铁路联运委员会会议于7月15日到25日在黑龙江省牡丹江市举行。
    这次会议是根据中苏国境铁路联运协定的规定每年召开一次的业务性例会。在会议上，中苏两国铁路代表团就有关加强两国国境铁路联运工作进行了讨论；对加速机车车辆周转等问题达成了协议。在闭幕式上，中苏两国铁路代表团团长分别讲了话，一致指出：这次中苏国境铁路联运委员会会议议定书的签订，将进一步加强中苏两国铁路联运工作与巩固两国人民和铁路职工之间的友谊和团结。
