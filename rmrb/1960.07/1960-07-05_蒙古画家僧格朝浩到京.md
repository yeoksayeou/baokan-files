Title: Mongolian Painter Seng Chao-hao Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Mongolian painter, Seng Chao-hao, who is visiting China according to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Mongolian cultural cooperation agreement, arrived in Beijing by train today. Cartoonist Zhang E and others were at the station to welcome him.

### 蒙古画家僧格朝浩到京

1960-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古画家僧格朝浩到京
    新华社4日讯　根据中蒙文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的蒙古画家僧格朝浩，今天乘火车到达北京。到车站欢迎的有漫画家张谔等。
