Title: At the Invitation of Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Ghanaian President Nkrumah Will Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1960-07-07
Page: 1

    At the Invitation of Chairman Liu Shaoqi
    Ghanaian President Nkrumah Will Visit Our Country
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th. At the invitation of Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Kwame Nkrumah, President of the Republic of Ghana, will pay a friendly visit to the People's Republic of China. President Nkrumah has accepted Chairman Liu Shaoqi's invitation.

### 应刘少奇主席的邀请  加纳总统恩克鲁玛将访问我国

1960-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    应刘少奇主席的邀请
    加纳总统恩克鲁玛将访问我国
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇邀请加纳共和国总统克瓦米·恩克鲁玛来中华人民共和国进行友好访问。恩克鲁玛总统已接受了刘少奇主席的这项邀请。
