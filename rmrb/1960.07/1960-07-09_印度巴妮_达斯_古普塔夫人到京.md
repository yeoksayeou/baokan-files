Title: Indian Mrs. Bani Das Gupta Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-09
Page: 5

    Indian Mrs. Bani Das Gupta Arrives in Beijing

  Xinhua News Agency, July 8th. Mrs. Bani Das Gupta of India arrived in Beijing this morning for a visit to our country at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation.

    Huang Ganying, Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Women's Federation, and others welcomed her at the station.

### 印度巴妮·达斯·古普塔夫人到京

1960-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    印度巴妮·达斯·古普塔夫人到京
  新华社8日讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参观访问的印度巴妮·达斯·古普塔夫人，今天上午到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有全国妇联国际联络部部长黄甘英等。
