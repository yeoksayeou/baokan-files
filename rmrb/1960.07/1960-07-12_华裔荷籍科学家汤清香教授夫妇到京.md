Title: Chinese-Dutch Scientist Professor Tang Qingxiang and Wife Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-12
Page: 6
　　
Xinhua News Agency, 11th. At the invitation of Pei Lisheng, Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Professor Tang Qingxiang, a Chinese-Dutch plant virologist, and his wife, who are visiting our country for lectures and visits, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 9th.

　　Xie Xinhe, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet to welcome them on the evening of the 10th. Lin Shan, Secretary-General of the Academy of Agricultural Sciences, and Fang Xinfang, Deputy Director of the Institute of Microbiology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, were present.

### 华裔荷籍科学家汤清香教授夫妇到京

1960-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　华裔荷籍科学家汤清香教授夫妇到京
    新华社11日讯　应中国科学院秘书长裴丽生邀请，前来我国讲学访问的华裔荷兰籍植物病毒学家汤清香教授夫妇，在9日下午到达北京。
    中国科学院副秘书长谢鑫鹤在10日晚设宴欢迎。出席作陪的，有农业科学院秘书长林山和中国科学院微生物研究所副所长方心芳等。
