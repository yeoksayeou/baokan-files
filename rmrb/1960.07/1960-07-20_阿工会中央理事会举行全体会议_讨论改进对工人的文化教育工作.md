Title: Albanian Trade Union Central Council Holds Plenary Meeting to Discuss Improving Cultural and Educational Work for Workers
Author:
Date: 1960-07-20
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 16th. The Albanian Trade Union Central Council held a plenary meeting on the 15th. The meeting discussed issues concerning the improvement of the work of the trade union in educating and raising the cultural and educational level of workers, and passed corresponding resolutions.

    Albanian Trade Union Central Council Secretary Kamberi said in his report that the development speed to be determined by the Fourth Congress of the Labor Party to be held in November this year requires the broadest mobilization of the working class, and requires the working class to have a higher cultural level, so as to be able to master modern technology and complete the tasks determined by the Party and the government.

    He pointed out that educating workers on the correct attitude towards labor is the fundamental issue in trade union education. He said that trade unions should educate workers with a socialist attitude towards labor.

    Albanian Labor Party Central Political Bureau member Belishova spoke at the meeting about the warm and friendly reception she received while accompanying Albanian People's Republic People's Assembly Presidium Chairman Leshi on visits to China and other Asian brotherly countries. She also spoke about the great achievements made by these countries in socialist construction.

    Trade Union Central Council Chairman Nushi finally spoke at the meeting, pointing out that the current task of trade union organizations and the working people is to smoothly realize the production plan and the guarantees put forward for welcoming the Fourth Congress of the Labor Party.

### 阿工会中央理事会举行全体会议　讨论改进对工人的文化教育工作

1960-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿工会中央理事会举行全体会议
　　讨论改进对工人的文化教育工作
    据新华社地拉那16日电  阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会在15日举行了全体会议，这次会议讨论了关于改进工会在教育和提高工人文化教育水平方面的工作问题，并通过了相应的决议。
    阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会书记坎伯里在会上作报告时说，即将在今年11月间召开的劳动党第四次代表大会所将确定的发展速度，要求最广泛地动员工人阶级，要求工人阶级具有更高的文化水平，以便能掌握现代的技术，完成党和政府所确定的任务。
    他指出，对工人进行正确劳动态度的教育，是工会教育中的根本问题。他说，工会应当以社会主义劳动态度来教育工人。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员贝利绍娃在会上谈到了她随同阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席列希访问中国和其他亚洲兄弟国家时所受到的十分热烈和友好的接待。她还谈到了这些国家在社会主义建设方面所取得的巨大成就等。
    工会中央理事会主席努什最后在会上讲话，他指出，工会组织和劳动人民当前的任务是，顺利地实现生产计划和为迎接劳动党第四次代表大会所提出的保证。
