Title: Algerian Liberation Army Shoots Down Eleven French Planes in Four Days, Kills or Wounds Over 400 French Soldiers, Captures Over 100 French Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1960-07-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 10th. A military communique issued here on July 9th by the headquarters of the Algerian National Liberation Army said that in the battles fought from July 1st to 4th, the Algerian National Liberation Army killed 248 French soldiers, wounded 187, and captured 116 French soldiers. In addition, eleven French soldiers surrendered to the Algerian National Liberation Army with all their weapons.

The communique stated that the Algerian National Liberation Army also shot down eleven French military aircraft.

The communique stated that during this period, the Algerian National Liberation Army launched 33 attacks on French military strongholds, engaged the French army in battle in twelve locations, and ambushed French troops and military convoys 21 times. Algerian commandos also launched 32 attacks on French barracks and police stations throughout Algeria.

### 阿尔及利亚解放军四天击落法机十一架　毙伤法军四百余人　俘掳法军百余人

1960-07-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿尔及利亚解放军四天击落法机十一架
　　毙伤法军四百余人　俘掳法军百余人
    新华社开罗10日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放军总部9日在这里发表的一项军事公报说，在7月1日至4日所进行的战斗中，阿尔及利亚民族解放军共击毙法军二百四十八名，击伤一百八十七名，俘掳了一百一十六名法国兵。另外并有十一名法军带着全副武装向阿尔及利亚民族解放军投诚。
    公报说，阿尔及利亚民族解放军还打落了十一架法国军用飞机。
    公报说，在此期间，阿尔及利亚民族解放军对法军据点发动了三十三次进攻，在十二个地方同法军进行了战斗，对法国军队和军用车队进行了二十一次伏击。阿尔及利亚突击队还对阿尔及利亚各地法国兵营和警察派出所发动了三十二次袭击。
