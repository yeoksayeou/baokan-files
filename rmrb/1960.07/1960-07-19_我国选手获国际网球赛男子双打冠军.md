Title: Chinese Athletes Win Men's Doubles Title at International Tennis Tournament
Author:
Date: 1960-07-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, July 18th: The annual Budapest International Tennis Tournament closed here on the 17th. Chinese athletes Mei Fuji and Zhu Zhenhua won the men's doubles championship.

　　The competition began on the 11th. Famous athletes from Hungary, China, Poland, the German Democratic Republic, the United Kingdom, West Germany, Yugoslavia, South Africa, and other countries participated in the competition. The champions of the other events were: Men's Singles - Wilmark of South Africa, Women's Singles - Körmöczy of Hungary, Women's Doubles - Körmöczy of Hungary and Haydon of the United Kingdom, Mixed Doubles - Siksay and Brosman of Hungary.

### 我国选手获国际网球赛男子双打冠军

1960-07-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　我国选手获国际网球赛男子双打冠军
    据新华社布达佩斯18日电　一年一度的布达佩斯国际网球赛17日在这里闭幕。中国选手梅福基和朱振华获得了男子双打冠军。
    比赛是在11日开始的。参加比赛的有匈牙利、中国、波兰、德意志民主共和国、英国、西德、南斯拉夫、南非等国家的著名选手。获得其他几项冠军的是：男子单打——南非的维尔马克、女子单打——匈牙利的克尔默齐、女子双打——匈牙利的克尔默齐和英国的海顿、男女混合双打——匈牙利的西克赛和布罗斯曼。
