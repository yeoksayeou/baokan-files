Title: Hungarian Geophysical Exploration Instrument Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-01
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The Hungarian Geophysical Exploration Instrument Exhibition opened in Beijing today.

    He Changong, Vice Minister of Geology, Ji Chao-ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and other figures from the capital's scientific community attended the opening ceremony of the exhibition.

    Ji Chao-ding, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, Sigeti, and Xiuqi Yuguo, head of the Hungarian Geophysical Exploration Instrument Exhibition Delegation, spoke at the opening ceremony. They pointed out that this exhibition will achieve successful results in further strengthening the friendly unity and economic and technical cooperation between China and Hungary. In his speech, Ji Chao-ding also particularly praised the high level achieved by the Hungarian people in science and technology under the leadership of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party. He said that the Chinese people have always been encouraged by these brilliant achievements.

### 匈地球物理勘探仪器展览会在京开幕

1960-07-01
第5版()
专栏：

    匈地球物理勘探仪器展览会在京开幕
    新华社30日讯　匈牙利地球物理勘探仪器展览会今天在北京开幕。
    地质部副部长何长工，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎等人，以及首都科学界人士，参加了展览会的开幕式。
    冀朝鼎和匈牙利驻中国大使馆临时代办西格蒂，匈牙利地球物理勘探仪器展览代表团团长修契·尤果，在开幕式上讲了话。他们指出，这次展览会在进一步加强中匈两国间的友好团结和经济、技术的合作方面将取得成功的效果。冀朝鼎在讲话中还特别赞扬匈牙利人民在匈牙利社会主义工人党的领导下，在科学技术方面所达到的高度水平。他说中国人民一向为这些辉煌的成就感到欢欣鼓舞。
