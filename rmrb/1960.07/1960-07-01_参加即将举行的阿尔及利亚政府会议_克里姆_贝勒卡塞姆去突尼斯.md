Title: Krim Belkacem Goes to Tunisia to Attend Upcoming Algerian Government Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-07-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 29th — Krim Belkacem, Vice-Premier of the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic, has left Cairo for Tunisia via Libya to attend a meeting of the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic to be held there.

Belkacem told a Middle East News Agency reporter today that if Boumendjel, the Algerian Republic's Provisional Government envoy participating in the preliminary meeting for Algerian-French negotiations, succeeds in his mission in France, the Algerian Republic's Provisional Government will announce the list of delegates going to France and the date of their departure. This delegation will be composed of several military and political leaders.

Belkacem added that if Boumendjel's mission is not completed, the Algerian Republic's Provisional Government will issue a detailed communiqué on the mission of the Algerian envoy and the obstacles placed before him by the French authorities.

He concluded by saying that at the end of the negotiations, the results will be submitted to the Algerian National Revolutionary Council for a final decision.

### 参加即将举行的阿尔及利亚政府会议  克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆去突尼斯

1960-07-01
第6版()
专栏：

    参加即将举行的阿尔及利亚政府会议
    克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆去突尼斯
    新华社开罗29日电　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府副总理克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆已经离开开罗，取道利比亚前往突尼斯，去参加将在那里举行的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府会议。
    贝勒卡塞姆今天对中东通讯社记者说，如果参加阿尔及利亚和法国谈判预备会议的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府使节布曼杰尔在法国的使命成功的话，那么阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府将宣布前往法国的代表团名单和代表团动身的日期。这个代表团将由若干军政领导人组成。
    贝勒卡塞姆又说，如果布曼杰尔使命没有完成的话，阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府将就阿尔及利亚使节的使命和法国负责人在它面前摆下的障碍发表详尽的公报。
    他最后说，在谈判结束时，谈判结果将提交阿尔及利亚全国革命委员会作出最后决定。
