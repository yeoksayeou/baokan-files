Title: Delegation from Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region of China Visits Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 22nd: The delegation from the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region of the People’s Republic of China, led by Sima Yi Yasheng Nuofu, Director of the Agricultural Reclamation Work Department of the Autonomous Region’s Party Committee, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by plane this morning. The delegation is visiting at the invitation to attend the celebration of the 20th anniversary of the establishment of Bayan-Ölgii Province in Mongolia.

### 我国新疆维吾尔自治区代表团访蒙

1960-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    我国新疆维吾尔自治区代表团访蒙
    新华社乌兰巴托22日电　中华人民共和国新疆维吾尔自治区代表团在自治区党委农垦工作部部长司马益·牙生诺夫率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达乌兰巴托。代表团是应邀前来参加庆祝蒙古巴彦乌列盖省建省二十周年典礼的。
