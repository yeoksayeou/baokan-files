Title: Cuban President Receives Chinese Trade Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 19th. Cuban President Dorticos today received the Chinese trade delegation headed by Lu Xuzhang. The host and guests engaged in warm and friendly talks.

### 古巴总统接见中国贸易代表团

1960-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴总统接见中国贸易代表团
    新华社哈瓦那19日电　古巴总统多尔蒂科斯今天接见了以卢绪章为首的中国贸易代表团，宾主间进行了热烈而友好的谈话。
