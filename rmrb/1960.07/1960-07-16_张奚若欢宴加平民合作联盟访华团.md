Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Canadian Cooperative Commonwealth Federation Delegation

Author:
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 15th -- Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet this evening to warmly welcome the Canadian Cooperative Commonwealth Federation delegation, led by William Irvine, a member of the National Council of the Canadian Cooperative Commonwealth Federation and a member of the World Peace Council.

　　At this banquet filled with a friendly atmosphere, the hosts and guests had a cordial conversation. People repeatedly toasted the growing friendship between the people of China and Canada and for world peace.

　　The delegation arrived in Beijing on the 13th.

### 张奚若欢宴加平民合作联盟访华团

1960-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

　  张奚若欢宴加平民合作联盟访华团
　　据新华社15日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今天晚间设宴热烈欢迎以加拿大平民合作联盟全国理事会理事、世界和平理事会理事威廉·欧文为首的加拿大平民合作联盟人士访华团全体成员。
    在这个充满友好气氛的宴会上，宾主亲切交谈。人们不断为中国和加拿大两国人民之间日益增长的友谊、为世界和平干杯。
    代表团是于13日抵达北京的。
