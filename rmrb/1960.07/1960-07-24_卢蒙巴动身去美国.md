Title: Lumumba Departs for the United States
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Leopoldville, July 23rd: Congolese Republic Prime Minister Lumumba departed from the Congolese capital, Leopoldville, on July 22nd, bound for the United States. He was accompanied by a delegation of fifteen people.

It is reported that Detwiler, the leader of an American investment company, and his two assistants left Leopoldville on the same plane as Lumumba and his delegation.

### 卢蒙巴动身去美国

1960-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    卢蒙巴动身去美国
    新华社23日讯　利奥波德维尔消息：刚果共和国总理卢蒙巴22日从刚果首都利奥波德维尔动身前往美国。陪同他的是由十五人组成的一个代表团。
    据报道，一家美国投资公司的领导人德特威勒和他的两名助手和卢蒙巴等人同机离开了利奥波德维尔。
