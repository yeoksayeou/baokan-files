Title: Yao Zhongming Hosts Banquet for Brigadier General Aung Gyi
Author:
Date: 1960-07-26
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: Yao Zhongming, the chief representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, hosted a banquet this evening to entertain Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, as well as the representatives, advisors, and accompanying personnel.
    The Burmese Ambassador to China, Ambassador Kyao Winn, and diplomatic officials from the embassy were invited to attend the banquet. Among those present at the banquet were: Li Yimang, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, as well as the Chinese representatives and advisors of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee.
    After the banquet, the guests watched a Peking Opera performance together.

### 姚仲明欢宴昂季准将

1960-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    姚仲明欢宴昂季准将
    新华社25日讯　中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明，今天晚上举行宴会，招待中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表昂季准将和代表、顾问以及随行人员。
    缅甸驻中国大使、缅甸代表叫温和大使馆的外交官员应邀参加了宴会。参加宴会作陪的有：我国驻缅甸大使李一氓，以及中缅边界联合委员会中国代表、顾问等。
    宴会以后，宾主一同观看了京剧。
