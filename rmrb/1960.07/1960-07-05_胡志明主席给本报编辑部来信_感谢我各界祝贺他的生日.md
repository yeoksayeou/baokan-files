Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Writes to the Editorial Department of This Newspaper to Thank All Circles for Congratulating Him on His Birthday
Author:
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 1

    Chairman Ho Chi Minh's Letter to the Editorial Department of This Newspaper
    Thanking All Circles for Congratulating Him on His Birthday

    To the Editorial Department of the People's Daily:
    On the occasion of my seventieth birthday, I was very pleased to receive congratulatory telegrams, letters, and gifts from workers of many enterprises in China, members of people's communes, young students and children of schools, Vietnamese students and interns in China, and other friends.

    Please convey my heartfelt thanks through your newspaper!

                            Ho Chi Minh, July 4th

### 胡志明主席给本报编辑部来信  感谢我各界祝贺他的生日

1960-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席给本报编辑部来信
    感谢我各界祝贺他的生日人民日报编辑部：
    在我七十岁生日的时候，我很高兴地接到中国许多企业工人、人民公社社员、学校的青年学生和少年儿童、越南在中国的留学生和实习生以及其他朋友们给我的贺电、贺信和礼品。
    敬请贵报代为转达我的衷心的谢意！
                            胡志明　七月四日
