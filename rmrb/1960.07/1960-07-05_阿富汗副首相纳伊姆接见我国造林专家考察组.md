Title: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister Naim Receives Chinese Afforestation Expert Survey Group
Author:
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 3rd - Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Naim today received the Chinese afforestation expert survey group and exchanged views on the development of afforestation in both countries. Li Yuchi, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Afghanistan, was also present at the meeting.

The survey group, headed by Huang Shu, was invited by the Afghan government to study afforestation issues in Afghanistan. To this end, the survey group has visited several places in Afghanistan over the past month.

### 阿富汗副首相纳伊姆接见我国造林专家考察组

1960-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗副首相纳伊姆接见我国造林专家考察组
    新华社3日讯　喀布尔消息：阿富汗副首相兼外交大臣纳伊姆今天接见了中国造林专家考察组，就两国发展造林事业问题交换了意见。接见时，我国驻阿富汗大使馆临时代办李玉池也在座。
    以黄枢为首的这个考察组，是应阿富汗政府的邀请去研究阿富汗的造林问题的。为此，这个考察组曾在过去一个多月内到过阿富汗几个地方。
