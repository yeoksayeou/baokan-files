Title: Japanese National Relief Association Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-07-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 20th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Relief Association, the nine-member delegation of the Japanese National Relief Association, headed by Okamoto Mitsuo, arrived in Guangzhou yesterday afternoon by train from Shenzhen.

### 日本国民救援会代表团到达广州

1960-07-21
第6版()
专栏：

    日本国民救援会代表团到达广州
    据新华社广州20日电　应中国人民救济总会邀请前来我国访问的日本国民救援会代表团一行九人，在团长冈本光雄的率领下，于昨日下午从深圳乘火车到达广州。
