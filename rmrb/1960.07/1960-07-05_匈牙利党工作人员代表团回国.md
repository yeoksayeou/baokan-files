Title: Hungarian Party Workers Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th July. The Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party workers delegation, led by Sándor József, member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, left Beijing by plane today to return home after concluding its visit to our country.
    Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included An Ziwen and Liu Ningyi, members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Zhang Pan Shi and other responsible personnel from the central organs of the Communist Party of China.
    Sigeti Karoly, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 匈牙利党工作人员代表团回国

1960-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利党工作人员代表团回国
    新华社4日讯　由匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员山多尔·约瑟夫率领的匈牙利社会主义工人党工作人员代表团，结束在我国的访问后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中共中央委员安子文、刘宁一和中共中央机关负责工作人员张磐石等人。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂·卡洛伊也到机场送行。
