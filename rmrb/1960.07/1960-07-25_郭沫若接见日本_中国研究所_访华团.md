Title: Guo Moruo Receives Japanese "China Research Institute" Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1960-07-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, received the Japanese "China Research Institute" delegation headed by Heihachiro Hirano, Vice Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association and Chairman of the Japan Peace Committee, this afternoon. The guests and hosts held cordial and friendly talks during the meeting.

Present at the meeting were Zhao Puchu, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Vice Chairman of the Buddhist Association of China, and Lin Lin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others.

### 郭沫若接见日本“中国研究所”访华团

1960-07-25
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见日本“中国研究所”访华团
    新华社24日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天下午接见了以日本中国友好协会副会长、日本和平委员会理事长平野义太郎为首的日本“中国研究所”访华代表团。接见时宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员、中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初，中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长林林等人。
