Title: National Literary and Art Workers Congress Continues; Xu Zhizhen, Yang Haibo, Li Dequan, and Fu Zhong Extend Congratulations to the Congress; Mao Dun Delivers Report on "Reflecting the Socialist Leap Forward Era, Promoting the Leap Forward of the Socialist Era"
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: The Third National Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers continued today. At today's meeting, Xu Zhizhen, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Yang Haibo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the National Women's Federation, and Fu Zhong, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, delivered congratulatory speeches on behalf of workers, youth, women, and the military, respectively, and extended warm congratulations to the national literary and art workers through the Congress. Mao Dun, Vice Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, delivered a lengthy report entitled "Reflecting the Socialist Leap Forward Era, Promoting the Leap Forward of the Socialist Era."

In their congratulatory speeches, Xu Zhizhen, Yang Haibo, Li Dequan, and Fu Zhong unanimously praised the brilliant achievements made by Chinese literary and art workers under the leadership of the Party and under the encouragement and promotion of the great leap forward in industrial and agricultural production, in adhering to the direction of serving workers, peasants, and soldiers. They encouraged national literary and art workers to further implement the Party's literary and artistic policies, further integrate with the masses of workers, peasants, and soldiers, and create more literary and artistic works worthy of the era and shining with the light of communist thought. They unanimously emphasized the important role of literary and artistic work in opposing the aggressive forces of imperialism headed by the United States, opposing various bourgeois ideologies, and opposing revisionist struggles, and demanded that literary and art workers fully utilize various literary and artistic weapons to make significant contributions to the above struggles.

The Congress will hold group discussions tomorrow.

### 全国文艺工作者代表大会继续举行  许之桢、杨海波、李德全、傅钟向大会致祝词  茅盾作“反映社会主义跃进的时代，推动社会主义时代的跃进”的报告

1960-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    全国文艺工作者代表大会继续举行
    许之桢、杨海波、李德全、傅钟向大会致祝词
    茅盾作“反映社会主义跃进的时代，推动社会主义时代的跃进”的报告
    据新华社24日讯　中国文学艺术工作者第三次代表大会今天继续举行。在今天的大会上，中华全国总工会副主席许之桢、共产主义青年团中央书记处书记杨海波、全国妇女联合会副主席李德全、中国人民解放军总政治部副主任傅钟分别代表工、青、妇、部队各方面致祝词，并通过大会向全国文学艺术工作者致以热烈的祝贺。中国文学艺术界联合会副主席、中国作家协会主席茅盾，作了题为“反映社会主义跃进的时代，推动社会主义时代的跃进”的长篇的报告。
    许之桢、杨海波、李德全、傅钟在祝词中，一致赞颂我国文学艺术工作者在党的领导下，在工农业生产大跃进的鼓舞推动下，坚持为工农兵服务的方向所取得的辉煌成就。他们鼓励全国文艺工作者进一步贯彻党的文艺方针，进一步与工农兵群众密切结合，创作出更多的无愧于时代、闪烁着共产主义思想光芒的文学艺术作品。他们一致强调指出文艺工作在反对以美国为首的帝国主义侵略势力、反对各种资产阶级思想和反对修正主义斗争中的重要作用，要求文艺工作者充分运用各种文艺武器，在上述各项斗争中作出重大的贡献。
    大会明天将进行分组讨论。
