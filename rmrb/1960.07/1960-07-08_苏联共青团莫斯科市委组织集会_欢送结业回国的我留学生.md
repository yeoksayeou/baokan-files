Title: Moscow City Committee of the Soviet Komsomol Organizes a Gathering to Welcome Chinese Students Graduating and Returning Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-07-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 6th: The Moscow City Committee of the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League organized a warm send-off meeting at the Yaroslavsky Railway Station on the 6th to welcome over 400 Chinese students who have completed their studies and are returning home.

    A banner reading "Long Live the Eternal and Unbreakable Friendship between the Chinese and Soviet People!" was hung above the main entrance of the station. The national flags of China and the Soviet Union fluttered on both sides. The platform was crowded with hundreds of people who came to see them off, including teachers and staff from Soviet universities, Soviet classmates, and friends. Fartunin, First Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Lenin Communist Youth League, spoke at the farewell meeting. On behalf of the Komsomol members and youth of Moscow, he warmly congratulated the Chinese students on successfully completing their studies and wished them great achievements in participating in the construction of their motherland.

    Yudachev, Director of the Political Education Department of the Soviet Ministry of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education, encouraged the Chinese students to work and study hard after returning to their country in his speech. Kolesnikov, Minister of the Central Student Department of the Lenin Communist Youth League, praised the Chinese students for excellently completing their studies in his speech.

    Sun Xiangjun, the representative of the graduating Chinese students in the Soviet Union, once again expressed his gratitude to the Soviet Party, government, schools, and teachers for their care and help in his speech. She said that we will actively participate in the construction of our motherland and constantly consolidate and strengthen Sino-Soviet friendship. Zhang Xiongfei, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, also said in his speech that the students will certainly take the Soviet communist builders as an example, apply all the valuable knowledge and experience learned in the Soviet Union to China's socialist construction, and serve the cause of socialist construction of the motherland loyally.

    After the meeting, Chinese and Soviet friends talked cordially and exchanged farewell messages on the platform.

### 苏联共青团莫斯科市委组织集会  欢送结业回国的我留学生

1960-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联共青团莫斯科市委组织集会
    欢送结业回国的我留学生
    新华社莫斯科6日电　为欢送我国四百多名留苏学生学成回国，苏联列宁共产主义青年团莫斯科市委会6日在雅罗斯拉夫车站组织了热烈的欢送会。
    车站正门悬挂着“中苏两国人民永恒和牢不可破的友谊万岁！”等横幅标语。两旁飘扬着中苏两国国旗。站台上挤满了数百名欢送的人们，其中有苏联高等学校的教师、工作人员，苏联同学和朋友。列宁共青团莫斯科市委第一书记法尔图宁在欢送会上讲了话。他代表莫斯科共青团员和青年热烈祝贺我国留学生胜利地完成学业，祝他们在参加祖国的建设事业中获得巨大成就。
    苏联高等和中等专业教育部政治教育司司长尤达契夫在讲话中勉励我国学生回国后努力工作和学习。列宁共青团中央学生部部长科列斯尼科夫在讲话中称赞中国学生优秀地完成了学习任务。
    我国留苏毕业生代表孙湘君讲话时再次对苏联党、政府、学校以及教师的关心和帮助表示感谢。她说，我们将积极参加祖国的建设，不断地巩固和加强中苏友谊。我国驻苏使馆参赞张雄飞也讲话说，同学们一定会以苏联共产主义建设者为榜样，把在苏联学到的一切宝贵知识和经验运用到我国社会主义建设事业中去，忠诚地为祖国的社会主义建设事业服务。
    大会结束后，中苏朋友在站台上亲切交谈、相互赠言。
