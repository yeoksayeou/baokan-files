Author:
Date: 1960-07-27
Page: 2

    Typhoon Warning
    (Xinhua News Agency dispatch) The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon warning at 12:00 noon on the 26th:
    The fifth typhoon of 1960 in the Pacific Ocean was centered at 25.6 degrees north latitude and 125.5 degrees east longitude at 2:00 AM this morning. It is expected that the wind force along the Zhejiang coast and the southern Jiangsu coast will gradually increase to Force 7-9 during the daytime on the 27th, and the wind force along the northern Fujian coast will gradually increase to Force 6-7. After dawn on the 28th, the typhoon will move northward, approaching the central Zhejiang coast to the southern Jiangsu coast, or land in the aforementioned areas. The wind force along the Zhejiang coast and the southern Jiangsu coast will continue to increase to Force 8-10, with heavy rain. The wind force near the typhoon center may reach Force 11-12, and the wind force along the central Jiangsu coast will increase to Force 6-8.

### 台风警报

1960-07-27
第2版()
专栏：

    台风警报
    据新华社讯　中央气象科学研究所26日中午十二时发布台风警报：
    太平洋上的1960年第五号台风，今天早晨二时中心位置在北纬二十五点六度，东经一百二十五点五度附近。预计27日白天浙江沿海，江苏南部沿海的风力将逐渐加大到七——九级，福建北部沿海的风力将逐渐加大到六——七级。28日清晨以后台风将靠近浙江中部到江苏南部沿海北去或在上述地区登陆，浙江沿海和江苏南部沿海的风力将继续加大到八——十级，并有暴雨，其中台风中心附近风力可达十一——十二级，江苏中部沿海的风力将加大到六——八级。
