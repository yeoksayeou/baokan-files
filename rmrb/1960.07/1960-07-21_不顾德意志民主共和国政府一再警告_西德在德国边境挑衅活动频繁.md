Title: Ignoring repeated warnings from the government of the German Democratic Republic, West Germany frequently engages in provocative activities on the German border

Author:
Date: 1960-07-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 18th: According to a report by the German News Agency, West Germany, in the past few days, has once again carried out provocative activities on the border of the German Democratic Republic, ignoring repeated warnings from the government of the German Democratic Republic.

　　In the Hohenberg border area of ​​Wolfenbuttel County, five provocateurs set a fire on July 16, which spread to the territory of the German Democratic Republic and destroyed the security facilities of the border police of the German Democratic Republic.

　　On July 17, a military vehicle of the West German border guard appeared in the Tarn area of ​​West Germany. Three West German border guard soldiers opened fire on the border police sentry of the German Democratic Republic, while five other provocateurs provided them with covering fire.

### 不顾德意志民主共和国政府一再警告  西德在德国边境挑衅活动频繁

1960-07-21
第6版()
专栏：

    不顾德意志民主共和国政府一再警告
    西德在德国边境挑衅活动频繁
    新华社柏林18日电　据德意志通讯社报道，西德在过去几天中，不顾德意志民主共和国政府的一再警告，又对德意志民主共和国的边境进行了挑衅活动。
    在沃尔芬布特县的霍恩堡边境地区，有五名挑衅分子于7月16日放火，火势延烧到德意志民主共和国的领土上，并烧毁了德意志民主共和国边境警察的保安设施。
    7月17日，西德边防军的一辆军车在西德的塔恩地区出现，有三名西德边防军士兵向德意志民主共和国的边境警察前哨开枪袭击，另外五名挑衅分子给他们提供火力掩护。
