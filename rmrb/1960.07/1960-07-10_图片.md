Title: Zhang Ping
Author: Zhang Ping
Date: 1960-07-10
Page: 2

    Workers at the Shanghai Chengfu Iron Factory, in order to support drought resistance in rice-growing areas across the country in a timely manner, completed the production task of more than 1,500 sixteen-horsepower diesel engines half a year ahead of schedule. This type of diesel engine has a large displacement, is simple to operate, and easy to use, making it very suitable for rice-growing areas.　　　
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Ping
