Title: More than 2,000 People from All Walks of Life in Chengdu, Sichuan, Hold Public Memorial Service for General He Bingyan

Author:

Date: 1960-07-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, 6th - More than 2,000 people from all walks of life in Sichuan Province and Chengdu City solemnly held a public memorial service on the morning of the 5th for General He Bingyan, a representative of the National People's Congress, Standing Committee Member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, member of the National Defense Council, Standing Committee Member of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Secretary of the Chengdu Military Region Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Commander of the Chengdu Military Region, who passed away on July 1st.

The public memorial service was presided over by Liao Zhigao, Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China. In the center of the mourning hall was placed the urn containing the ashes of Comrade He Bingyan. On both sides of the mourning hall were wreaths presented by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Lin Biao, Marshal He Long, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Marshal Liu Bocheng, Marshal Luo Ronghuan, Marshal Chen Yi, Marshal Xu Xiangqian, Marshal Ye Jianying, the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Sichuan Provincial People's Committee, and the Chengdu Military Region Committee of the Communist Party of China.

After the public memorial service began, Liao Zhigao presented a wreath to the portrait of Comrade He Bingyan. Lieutenant General Huang Xinting, Deputy Commander of the Chengdu Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, introduced Comrade He Bingyan's life and deeds. Subsequently, General Wang Zhen of the People's Liberation Army delivered a eulogy on behalf of Marshal He Long. The eulogy stated: The thirty-one years of revolutionary struggle of Comrade He Bingyan fully demonstrate his excellent qualities as a communist, closely linked to the masses, overcoming difficulties, and infinitely loyal to the cause of the Party and the people. In countless battles, especially in the most critical situations, he always stepped forward and undertook the most arduous tasks. Comrade He Bingyan's excellent qualities and exemplary struggles will be studied by the vast number of Party members and the masses.

Guo Linxiang, Second Political Commissar of the Chengdu Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, delivered a eulogy on behalf of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Chengdu Military Region Committee of the Communist Party of China. In the eulogy, he said that the death of Comrade He Bingyan is a major loss to our Party and our army, and we feel extremely grieved. Comrade He Bingyan dedicated his life wholeheartedly to the revolutionary cause of the Party and the people. Under the leadership of the Party, he waged a resolute struggle to safeguard the Party's line and the Party's interests, demonstrating the firmness and Party spirit of a communist. The eulogy said that during the arduous years of war, Comrade He Bingyan fought north and south, braving death, and was wounded eleven times for the cause of the people's liberation, losing his right arm. Every scar on Comrade He Bingyan's body profoundly records his great contributions to the revolutionary cause. Comrade He Bingyan had infinite confidence and determination in the cause of communism. Although he was wounded and disabled and suffered from overwork during the long period of revolutionary struggle, he still persisted in working with illness with revolutionary optimism. Comrade He Bingyan's spirit of selflessness and self-sacrifice has given every comrade an extremely profound education.

The meeting also announced the condolences from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Marshal Lin Biao, the General Staff Department, the General Political Department, and the General Logistics Department.

Also participating in the public memorial service were Yu Qiuli, Minister of Petroleum Industry, Du Xinyuan and Zhao Cangbi, Secretaries of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liao Jingdan, Standing Committee Member of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and First Secretary of the Chengdu Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, as well as Comrade He Bingyan's family members and relatives.

After the public memorial service, Comrade He Bingyan's urn was buried in a cemetery in the suburbs of Chengdu.

### 四川成都各界两千多人  公祭贺炳炎上将

1960-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    四川成都各界两千多人
    公祭贺炳炎上将
    新华社成都6日电　四川省和成都市各界人民两千多人，5日上午隆重公祭于7月1日逝世的全国人民代表大会代表、政协全国委员会常务委员、国防委员会委员、中共四川省委员会常务委员、中共成都部队委员会书记、成都部队司令员贺炳炎上将。
    公祭大会由中共四川省委书记廖志高主祭。灵台正中安置着贺炳炎同志的骨灰罐，灵台两侧放着全国人民代表大会常务委员会、周恩来总理和林彪元帅、贺龙元帅、聂荣臻元帅、刘伯承元帅、罗荣桓元帅、陈毅元帅、徐向前元帅、叶剑英元帅以及中共四川省委员会、四川省人民委员会、中共成都部队委员会献的花圈。
    公祭大会开始后，廖志高向贺炳炎同志遗像献花圈。人民解放军成都部队副司令员黄新廷中将介绍了贺炳炎同志的生平事迹。接着，人民解放军上将王震代表贺龙元帅致悼词。悼词中说：贺炳炎同志三十一年革命斗争历史，充分证明了他具有共产党人密切联系群众、战胜困难、对党和人民事业无限忠诚的优良品质。他在无数次的战斗中，特别是在最危急的情况下，总是挺身而出，承担最艰巨的任务。贺炳炎同志的优良品质和模范的斗争活动，将为广大党员和群众所学习。
    人民解放军成都部队第二政治委员郭林祥代表中共四川省委员会、中共成都部队委员会致悼词。他在悼词中说，贺炳炎同志的逝世，是我党我军的重大损失，我们感到无比的悲痛。贺炳炎同志的一生忠心耿耿献身于党和人民的革命事业，在党的领导下，为维护党的路线和党的利益，进行了坚决的斗争，表现了共产党员的坚定性和党性。悼词中说，贺炳炎同志在艰苦的战争年代里，南征北战，出生入死，为了人民的解放事业，负伤十一次，并且失去了右臂。在贺炳炎同志身上的每一处伤痕，都深刻地记录了他对革命事业的巨大功绩。贺炳炎同志对共产主义事业具有无限的信心和决心。虽然在长期的革命斗争中负伤残废，积劳成疾，但仍以革命乐观主义精神，始终带病坚持工作。贺炳炎同志这种为公忘私、自我牺牲的精神，给了每个同志以极其深刻的教育。
    会上还宣读了全国人民代表大会常务委员会、林彪元帅、总参谋部、总政治部、总后勤部的唁电。
    参加公祭的，还有石油工业部部长余秋里，中共四川省委书记杜心源、赵苍璧，中共四川省委常务委员、中共成都市委第一书记廖井丹，以及贺炳炎同志的家属、亲友等。
    公祭仪式结束后，贺炳炎同志的骨灰罐被安葬在成都市郊墓地。
