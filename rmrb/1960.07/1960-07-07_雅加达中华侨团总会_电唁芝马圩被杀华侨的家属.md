Title: Jakarta Chinese Associations Send Condolences to Families of Killed Overseas Chinese in Tjimahi

Author: 
Date: 1960-07-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 5th. The Jakarta Chinese Associations sent a telegram yesterday to the families of Yang Mu Mei and Ye Jin Niang, overseas Chinese in Tjimahi, who were tragically killed by the West Java military authorities on the 3rd, expressing deep condolences. The telegram was sent to Yang Mu Mei's husband, Lin Dunhua, and Ye Jin Niang's husband, Lin Yu.

The telegram said that all overseas Chinese in Jakarta felt sorrow and indignation upon hearing the shocking news that Madam Yang Mu Mei and Madam Ye Jin Niang were tragically killed during the forced relocation, and that Madam Lin Pin Ying and Mr. Wu Zhiwen were shot and wounded. "We firmly demand severe punishment for the perpetrators and an end to the forced relocation. We, on behalf of all overseas Chinese in Jakarta, extend our deepest condolences to you and your families."

### 雅加达中华侨团总会  电唁芝马圩被杀华侨的家属

1960-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    雅加达中华侨团总会
    电唁芝马圩被杀华侨的家属
    新华社雅加达5日电　雅加达中华侨团总会昨天打电报给3日惨遭西爪哇军事当局杀害的芝马圩华侨杨木妹和叶金娘的家属，表示深切的哀悼。唁电是打给杨木妹的丈夫林敦华和叶金娘的丈夫林玉的。
    电文说，惊悉杨木妹女士和叶金娘女士在迫迁中惨遭杀害，林品英女士和吴志文先生被枪伤，雅加达全体华侨都感到悲痛和愤慨。“我们坚决要求严惩凶犯和制止迫迁。我们谨代表雅加达全体侨胞，向你们和你们的家属致最深切的慰问。”
