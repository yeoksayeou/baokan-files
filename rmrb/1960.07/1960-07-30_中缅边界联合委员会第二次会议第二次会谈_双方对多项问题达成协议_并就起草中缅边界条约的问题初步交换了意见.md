Title: The Second Meeting and Second Session of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee: Both Sides Reached Agreements on Several Issues and Exchanged Preliminary Views on Drafting the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 29th - Press Communique on the Second Meeting and Second Session of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee.

The Second Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee was held at 9:00 a.m. on July 29. Attending the meeting were the Chief Representative of the Chinese side, Yao Zhongming, and the Chief Representative of the Burmese side, Brigadier General Aung Gyi, as well as other representatives and advisors from both sides. The meeting proceeded smoothly, building on previous achievements and following the agenda items set for this session. Agreements were reached on several issues, and preliminary views were exchanged on drafting the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty. The entire meeting was filled with an extremely friendly atmosphere.

### 中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议第二次会谈  双方对多项问题达成协议  并就起草中缅边界条约的问题初步交换了意见

1960-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议第二次会谈
    双方对多项问题达成协议
    并就起草中缅边界条约的问题初步交换了意见
    新华社29日讯　中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议第二次会谈新闻公报
    中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议于7月29日上午九时举行第二次会谈。参加这次会谈的，有中方首席代表姚仲明和缅方首席代表昂季准将以及双方的其他代表和顾问。会谈在已取得成就的基础上循着本次会议所列入的议题顺利进行，对多项问题达成了协议，并就起草中缅边界条约的问题初步交换了意见。整个会谈充满了极为友好的气氛。
