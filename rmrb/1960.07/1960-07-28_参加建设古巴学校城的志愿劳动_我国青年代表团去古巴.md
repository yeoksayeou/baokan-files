Title: Our Youth Delegation Goes to Cuba to Participate in Volunteer Labor to Build a School City in Cuba
Author:
Date: 1960-07-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th – The Chinese Youth Delegation, invited to Cuba to participate in volunteer labor on the construction site of a school city, left Beijing by plane today, led by Zhang Jiming, head of the delegation and member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League. This delegation was sent by the All-China Youth Federation and consists of nine people, including Zhang Baifa, national model worker of industry, Beijing construction worker, and leader of a youth shock brigade; Huang Haicheng, national model worker of agriculture and deputy director of the Lianhua People's Commune in Haicheng County, Fujian; Yu Changbo, national activist in socialist construction for youth and foreman of the power station of Taiyuan Iron and Steel Company; and Feng Zhongping, student in the Architecture Department of Tsinghua University. The construction of the school city was jointly initiated by the Cuban government and the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and is located in the Sierra Maestra mountains of Cuba. Construction will begin in August.

Wang Chuanbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation, and Yu Chiqian, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, were among those who saw them off at the airport.

### 参加建设古巴学校城的志愿劳动  我国青年代表团去古巴

1960-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    参加建设古巴学校城的志愿劳动
    我国青年代表团去古巴
    新华社27日讯　应邀去古巴参加志愿劳动工地建设学校城的中国青年代表团，在团长、中国共产主义青年团中央委员会委员张继明率领下，已在今天乘飞机离开北京。这个代表团是由中华全国青年联合会派遣的，共有九人，其中有全国工业劳动模范、北京建筑工人、青年突击队队长张百发，全国农业劳动模范、福建海澄县莲花人民公社副社长黄海澄，全国青年社会主义建设积极分子、太原钢铁公司动力站工长于长波及清华大学建筑系学生冯钟平等。建设学校城是由古巴政府和世界民主青年联盟共同发起的，地点在古巴马埃斯特腊山区，建设工程从8月开始。
    到机场送行的有中华全国青年联合会副秘书长王传斌，中国共产主义青年团中央办公厅副主任于驰前等。
