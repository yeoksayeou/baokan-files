Title: Exchanging Learning Experiences from Various Regions, Helping People from All Walks of Life to Reform: A Conference on Political Theory Learning of the CPPCC Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Political Theory Learning Work Conference opened in Beijing on the 1st. The tasks and requirements of this conference are: to exchange experiences in political theory learning from people of all walks of life, and to focus on discussing how to widely and deeply carry out political theory learning with the study of Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding principle, so as to further promote people from all walks of life to foster proletarian ideology, eliminate bourgeois ideology, transform their standpoints, and transform their worldviews.

This conference was convened in accordance with the requirements of the work report of the Standing Committee of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC on March 29 this year regarding "organizing learning and helping democratic people from all walks of life to carry out political and ideological remolding."

Chen Shutong and Bao Erhan, Vice-Chairmen of the CPPCC National Committee, Zhang Zhiyi, Deputy Secretary-General, and others attended the meeting.

Sun Qimeng, Deputy Director of the Learning Committee of the CPPCC National Committee, spoke at the meeting on behalf of the Learning Committee.

Also attending the meeting were Shao Lizi, Standing Committee Member of the CPPCC National Committee and Vice-President of the Socialist Academy, Sun Qimeng, Chen Cisheng, Nie Zhen, and Sa Kongliao, Deputy Directors of the Learning Committee of the CPPCC National Committee, He Guiyan, Deputy Director of the Local Work Committee, Mei Gongbin, Secretary-General of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang (RCCK), Liu Wenhui, Standing Committee Member of the RCCK Central Committee, Deng Chumin, Vice-Chairman of the China Democratic League (CDL), Li Zhuchen and Hu Ziang, Vice-Chairmen of the China National Democratic Construction Association (CNDCA) and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce (ACFIC), Wang Shao'ao, Vice-Chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy (CAPD), Xia Kangnong, Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party (CPWDP), Chen Qiyou, Chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party, Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Jiusan Society, Xu Mengshan, Secretary-General of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League (TDSL), as well as Vice-Chairmen of the CPPCC from various provinces and municipalities and heads of democratic parties from various provinces and municipalities, totaling more than 260 people. The meetings of these two days focused on exchanging learning experiences from various regions.

### 交流各地学习经验　帮助各界人士进行改造  政协政治理论学习工作会议在京开幕

1960-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

    交流各地学习经验　帮助各界人士进行改造
    政协政治理论学习工作会议在京开幕
    新华社2日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会政治理论学习工作会议1日在北京开幕。这次会议的任务和要求是：交流各界人士政治理论学习的经验，并着重讨论如何以学习毛泽东思想为纲广泛深入开展政治理论学习问题，以便进一步推动各界人士兴无产阶级思想，灭资产阶级思想，改造立场，改造世界观。
    这次会议是根据今年3月29日政协第三届全国委员会常务委员会工作报告中关于“组织学习，帮助各界民主人士进行政治思想改造”的要求而召开的。
    政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通、包尔汉，副秘书长张执一等出席了会议。
    政协全国委员会学习委员会副主任委员孙起孟代表学委会在会上讲了话。
    出席会议的还有政协全国委员会常务委员、社会主义学院副院长邵力子，政协全国委员会学习委员会副主任委员孙起孟、陈此生、聂真、萨空了，地方工作委员会副主任委员贺贵严，民革秘书长梅龚彬，民革中央常务委员刘文辉，民盟副主席邓初民，民建、工商联副主任委员李烛尘、胡子昂，民进副主席王绍鏊，农工民主党中央委员会主席团委员夏康农，致公党主席陈其尤，九三学社主席许德珩，台盟秘书长徐萌山，以及各省市政协副主席和各省市民主党派负责人等共二百六十多人。这两天的会议，着重交流了各地的学习经验。
