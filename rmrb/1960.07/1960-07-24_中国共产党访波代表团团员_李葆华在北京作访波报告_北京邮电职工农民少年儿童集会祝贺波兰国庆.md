Title: Member of the Chinese Communist Party Delegation to Poland, Li Baohua, Gives Report on Visit to Poland in Beijing; Postal Workers, Farmers, and Children in Beijing Gather to Celebrate Polish National Day
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd. Li Baohua, member of the Chinese Communist Party delegation to Poland, member of the Central Committee of the CCP, and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Water Resources and Electric Power, gave a report on his visit to Poland today at the CPPCC Auditorium to over 1,400 cadres from the directly subordinate organs of the Central Committee of the CCP, central state organs, party and government organs in Beijing, people from various democratic parties, and cadres from factories and schools in the capital.

In his report, Li Baohua first expressed warm congratulations on the 16th anniversary of Polish National Day. He then gave a detailed introduction of the brilliant achievements made by the Polish working class and all working people in the cause of socialist construction under the leadership of the Polish United Workers' Party, as seen by the delegation. When talking about the warm welcome and hospitality the delegation received from the leading organs of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Polish people, Li Baohua pointed out that this was a manifestation of the Polish people's deep friendship for the Chinese people and a manifestation of the unity between the Chinese and Polish people.

The report aroused great interest among the audience. The report meeting ended in warm applause.

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st. Postal workers in Beijing, members of the China-Poland Friendship People's Commune, and children have been gathering in recent days to celebrate the 16th anniversary of Polish National Day.

Cultural Counselor Dumbnitzky of the Embassy of the Polish People's Republic in China and other diplomatic officials attended the celebration gatherings of various units.

Tonight, more than 600 postal workers in Beijing gathered at the Beijing Telegraph Building. Song Zhongyuan, Chairman of the Beijing Postal Workers' Union, extended festive congratulations to Polish friends at the meeting and introduced their labor achievements. He said that the postal workers in Beijing will unite with the people of socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union, and strive to oppose the US imperialist aggression and war schemes, and strive for world peace. Dumbnitzky also spoke at the meeting. When talking about the friendship between China and Poland, he said that the people of our two countries are fighting together to build socialism and eliminate imperialism. He also introduced the great achievements of the Polish United Workers' Party in leading the people of the whole country to build socialism. His speech was warmly applauded by the workers.

The China-Poland Friendship People's Commune, which won the title of advanced unit in agricultural socialist construction in Beijing, held a meeting on the 19th to celebrate Polish National Day and the first anniversary of the naming of the China-Poland Friendship People's Commune. Shao Detang, director of the commune, introduced to the Polish guests, including Dumbnitzky, who attended the meeting, that under the leadership of the Party, the commune has brought into play the superiority of the People's Commune and achieved great success in production. This year's total wheat output has increased by 15.4% compared with last year. Shao Detang expressed his gratitude for the deep friendship of the Polish people. Amidst the warm applause of the commune members, Dumbnitzky introduced to everyone the achievements of the Polish people in building socialism under the leadership of the Polish United Workers' Party in the past 16 years. He said that today the Polish people are united with the people of the fraternal Soviet Union, China, and other socialist countries, and are continuing to move forward to achieve further victories in the cause of socialism, further victories in the struggle for world peace, and for the liberation of all mankind. After the meeting, the Polish guests visited the commune's home for the elderly, vegetable fields, and grain processing factory. The commune members working in the fields entertained the guests with watermelons, cantaloupes and other produce.

On the evening of the 20th, more than 600 Young Pioneers from Beijing, together with Polish children, celebrated Polish National Day in the Young Pioneers' Palace, which was decorated with colorful lights. Liu Pei, a Young Pioneer, represented the children of Beijing in congratulating the Polish children on the festival and expressed his efforts to develop the friendship between Chinese and Polish children. Lei Ximanski, a Polish child, also took the stage to introduce the study and life of Polish children. Then, the Red Scarves of the Young Pioneers' Palace performed more than a dozen song and dance programs for the young guests.

Responsible persons from the China-Poland Friendship Association also participated in the celebration activities of various units.

### 中国共产党访波代表团团员  李葆华在北京作访波报告  北京邮电职工农民少年儿童集会祝贺波兰国庆

1960-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    中国共产党访波代表团团员
    李葆华在北京作访波报告
    北京邮电职工农民少年儿童集会祝贺波兰国庆
    新华社23日讯　中国共产党访问波兰代表团团员、中共中央委员、水利电力部副部长李葆华，今天在政协礼堂向中共中央直属机关、中央国家机关和北京市党政机关干部，各民主党派人士以及首都工厂、学校的干部等一千四百多人，作了访问波兰的报告。
    李葆华在报告中首先对波兰十六周年国庆表示了热烈的祝贺。接着详细地介绍了代表团看到的波兰工人阶级和全体劳动人民在波兰统一工人党领导下，在社会主义建设事业中所取得的辉煌成就。在谈到代表团受到波兰统一工人党领导机关和波兰人民的热情欢迎和殷勤款待时，李葆华指出，这是波兰人民对中国人民怀着深厚友情的表现，是中波两国人民团结一致的表现。
    报告引起了听众的很大兴趣。报告会在热烈的掌声中结束。
    新华社21日讯　北京市邮电职工、中波友好人民公社社员和少年儿童连日来集会，庆祝波兰国庆十六周年。
    波兰人民共和国驻华大使馆文化参赞邓布尼茨基和其他外交官员都参加了各单位庆祝集会。
    今天晚上，北京邮电职工六百多人在北京电报大楼集会，北京市邮电工会主席宋仲元在会上向波兰友人致节日的祝贺，并且介绍了他们劳动的成就。他说，北京市的邮电职工将和全国工人一起，同以苏联为首的社会主义各国人民团结一致，为反对美帝国主义侵略和战争阴谋，争取世界和平而努力。邓布尼茨基也在会上讲话，他在谈到中波两国友谊时说，我们两国人民都共同为建设社会主义、消灭帝国主义而进行斗争。他还介绍了波兰统一工人党领导全国人民建设社会主义事业的巨大成绩。他的讲话受到职工们热烈鼓掌。
    获得北京市农业社会主义建设先进单位称号的中波友好人民公社，19日集会庆祝波兰国庆和中波友好人民公社命名一周年。公社主任邵德棠向出席大会的邓布尼茨基等波兰客人介绍了这个公社在党的领导下，发挥了人民公社的优越性，生产上取得很大成绩，今年的小麦总产量比去年增长了15.4％。邵德棠对波兰人民的深厚友谊表示感谢。在社员们热烈掌声中，邓布尼茨基向大家介绍了波兰人民十六年来在波兰统一工人党领导下建设社会主义的成就。他说，今天波兰人民正同兄弟的苏联、中国和其他社会主义国家的人民团结一致，为取得社会主义事业中进一步胜利，为世界和平的斗争的进一步胜利，为全人类的解放而继续前进。会后，波兰客人们参观了公社敬老院、菜田和粮食加工厂。在田间劳动的社员们用自己生产的西瓜、香瓜等招待客人。
    20日晚上，在彩灯满院的少先宫里，六百多个北京市的少先队员，和波兰小朋友欢庆波兰国庆节。少先队员刘沛代表北京市少年儿童向波兰小朋友祝贺节日。并表示努力发展中波儿童的友谊。波兰小朋友雷·西曼斯基也上台介绍了波兰少年儿童的学习和生活情况。接着，少先宫的红领巾为小客人演出了十多个歌舞节目。
    中波友好协会的负责人也参加了各单位的庆祝活动。
