Title: Romanian Handball Team Plays Friendly Match in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-07-19
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 18th. The Romanian handball team, the world's runner-up, played its second friendly match in Shanghai against the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force handball team at the Hongkou Stadium in Shanghai on the afternoon of the 17th. The visiting team won with a score of 18-14.
　　The Romanian handball team will go to Beijing on the 19th. The schedule of matches in Beijing has been set as follows: against the Heilongjiang team on the 22nd, against the "August 1st" team on the 24th, and against the Anhui team on the 25th.

### 罗手球队在上海友谊赛

1960-07-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　罗手球队在上海友谊赛
    据新华社上海18日电　世界亚军罗马尼亚手球队17日下午在上海虹口体育场对中国人民解放军空军部队手球队进行访沪第二场友谊比赛，结果客队以十八比十四获胜。
    罗马尼亚手球队将在19日去北京。在北京的比赛日程已确定为：22日对黑龙江队，24日对“八一”队，25日对安徽队。
