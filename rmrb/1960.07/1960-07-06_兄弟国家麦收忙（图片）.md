Title: Fraternal Countries Busy with Wheat Harvest (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1960-07-06
Page: 6

Fraternal Countries Busy with Wheat Harvest
　　In the Soviet Moldavian Republic, the harvesting of grain crops has begun throughout the country. The above picture shows combines harvesting barley on the Kotovsky farm in Moldavia. Xinhua News Agency photo

　　Agricultural workers throughout Albania are engaged in the summer harvest. The above picture shows Iqbal Estreleva, a young member of an agricultural cooperative in Albania, working happily. Albanian Telegraph Agency photo
(Xinhua News Agency)

　　The summer harvest in Hungary has begun. The picture on the right shows members of the "Peace" agricultural cooperative in the Szajcz-Berek region of Hungary busy harvesting barley. Hungarian News Agency photo (Xinhua News Agency)

　　The wheat harvest in northern Korea has begun throughout the country. This year, many agricultural cooperatives estimate that each chongbo (approximately 15 mu) of barley and wheat can yield three to four tons. The picture on the left shows members of the Dongchang Agricultural Cooperative in the suburbs of Pyongyang harvesting barley.
Korean Central News Agency photo (Xinhua News Agency)
