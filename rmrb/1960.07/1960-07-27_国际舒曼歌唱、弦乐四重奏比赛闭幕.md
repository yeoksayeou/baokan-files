Title: International Schumann Singing and String Quartet Competition Concludes

Author:

Date: 1960-07-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 26th: The second International Schumann Singing and String Quartet Competition concluded in Berlin on the evening of the 25th.

The young Chinese singer Li Xinchang and the musicians of the string quartet received encouragement awards.

In the string quartet competition, Swedish musicians won the championship, Hungarian musicians won the second place, and the third place went to musicians from the German Democratic Republic.

In the singing competition, the first place was won by Gromadsky from the Soviet Union, the second place was won by Czechoslovak singer Holak and singer Helmig from the German Democratic Republic, and the third place was won by Soviet singer Shirotinina and Hungarian singer Gesti. String quartet musicians from Bulgaria and singers from Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, and Romania also received encouragement awards in the competition.

### 国际舒曼歌唱、弦乐四重奏比赛闭幕

1960-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    国际舒曼歌唱、弦乐四重奏比赛闭幕
    新华社柏林26日电　第二届国际舒曼歌唱、弦乐四重奏比赛25日晚在柏林闭幕。
    中国青年歌唱家黎信昌和弦乐四重奏的音乐家们获得了鼓励奖。
    在弦乐四重奏的比赛中，瑞典音乐家获得冠军，匈牙利音乐家获亚军，第三名是德意志民主共和国的音乐家。
    在歌唱比赛中，第一名是苏联的格罗马德斯基，第二名是捷克斯洛伐克歌唱家何拉克和德意志民主共和国歌唱家海尔米赫，苏联歌唱家希罗提尼娜和匈牙利歌唱家格斯蒂获得第三名。保加利亚的弦乐四重奏音乐家和捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚的歌唱家也在比赛中获得了鼓励奖。
