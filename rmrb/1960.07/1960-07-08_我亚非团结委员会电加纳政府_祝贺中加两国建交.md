Title: China-Africa Solidarity Committee Cables Ghanaian Government to Congratulate on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Ghana
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th. The China-Africa Solidarity Committee today sent a telegram to the government of the Republic of Ghana, warmly congratulating the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Ghana.

The telegram said, "The establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Ghana marks a new stage in the friendly relations between the people of China and Ghana, and also reflects the growing solidarity of the people of Asia and Africa. In the struggle against imperialism and colonialism, and to safeguard and strive for national independence, the Chinese people will always stand together with the people of Ghana and the people of all African countries, and wish the people of Ghana greater victories in this struggle."

### 我亚非团结委员会电加纳政府  祝贺中加两国建交

1960-07-08
第6版()
专栏：

    我亚非团结委员会电加纳政府
    祝贺中加两国建交
    新华社7日讯　中国亚非团结委员会今天致电加纳共和国政府，热烈祝贺中加两国宣布建立外交关系。
    电报说，“中、加两国建立外交关系，标志着中加两国人民的友好关系进入了一个新的阶段，同时也反映了亚洲和非洲人民的团结日益加强。在反对帝国主义和殖民主义、维护和争取民族独立的斗争中，中国人民将永远和加纳人民和非洲各国人民在一起，并祝加纳人民在这一斗争中取得更大的胜利。”
