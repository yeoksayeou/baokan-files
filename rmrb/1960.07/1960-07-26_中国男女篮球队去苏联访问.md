Title: Chinese Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-07-26
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: The Chinese men's and women's basketball teams, a group of thirty people invited to visit the Soviet Union, departed from Beijing by plane this morning.

    The basketball team is led by Han Fudong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Basketball Association. The coaches are Chen Wenbin, Zhang Zipei, and Yang Fulu. The twelve members of the men's team are: Fu Ruide, Ju Fenkang, Wang Jiazhen, Yu Yuanxu, Wang Lifa, Zhang Jingfu, Cai Jijie, Zhang Xishan, Lu Lianhan, Zhou Chunlin, Qian Chenghai, Yang Boyong. The twelve members of the women's team are: Fu Bin, Yong Rong, Chi Junhua, Yang Jie, Wu Xinci, Wang Xiafei, Zhou Yixian, Li Shaofen, Wei Meisheng, Hu Yingxin, Li Shihua, Ma Shenmei. The team also includes basketball national referee Guo Yupei and a translator.

### 中国男女篮球队去苏联访问

1960-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    中国男女篮球队去苏联访问
    据新华社25日讯　应邀到苏联访问的中国男女篮球队一行三十人，今晨乘飞机由北京启程。
    篮球队由中国篮球协会副主席韩复东率领，教练是陈文彬、张子沛和杨福鹿。男子队的十二名队员是：符瑞德、鞠汾康、王家桢、俞元煦、王利发、张景富、蔡集杰、张锡山、路廉翰、周春霖、钱澄海、杨伯镛。女子队的十二名队员是：傅彬、雍容、迟君华、杨洁、武心慈、王霞飞、周懿娴、李少芬、韦美胜、胡英信、李世华、马申妹。球队中还有篮球国家裁判郭玉佩和一名翻译。
