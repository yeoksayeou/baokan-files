Author:

Xinhua News Agency

State Council Appointment and Dismissal List

    Xinhua News Agency, The State Council, at its 100th plenary meeting held on April 29th and its 101st plenary meeting held on May 26th, respectively, approved the appointment and dismissal lists of a number of staff members of state organs and embassies abroad.
    The appointment list approved at the 100th plenary meeting of the State Council held on April 29th is as follows:
    Appoint Wang Baoxuan as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the People's Republic of Bulgaria;
    Yi Qintang as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Czechoslovak Republic;
    Zhu Jianbai as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of India;
    Sun Shengwei as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Union of Burma;
    Li Zhuozhi as Commercial Counselor of the Office of the Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China in the United Kingdom.
    The appointment and dismissal list approved at the 101st plenary meeting of the State Council held on May 26th is as follows:
    Appoint Li Chaobo as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Chemical Industry;
    Li Zhongyi as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Textile Industry;
    Xia Zhixu (female), Wang Yizhi, and Cao Lu as Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Light Industry;
    Han Quanhua (female) as Counselor of the State Council;
    Chen Weifan as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics;
    Zhang Guangtian as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Mongolian People's Republic;
    Zhang Yiming as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Finland.
    Remove Kong Xiangzhen from the post of Vice Minister of the Ministry of Communications.

### 国务院任免名单

1960-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社讯　国务院在4月29日举行的第一百次全体会议和5月26日举行的第一○一次全体会议上，分别通过了任免一批国家机关和驻外使馆工作人员的名单。
    国务院4月29日举行的第一百次全体会议通过的任命名单如下：
    任命王保宣为中华人民共和国驻保加利亚人民共和国大使馆商务参赞；
    衣钦堂为中华人民共和国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使馆商务参赞；
    朱剑白为中华人民共和国驻印度共和国大使馆商务参赞；
    孙盛渭为中华人民共和国驻缅甸联邦大使馆参赞；
    李琢之为中华人民共和国驻英国代办处商务参赞。
  国务院5月26日举行的第一○一次全体会议通过的任免名单如下：
    任命李超伯为化学工业部副部长；
    李中一为纺织工业部副部长；
    夏之栩（女）、王毅之、曹鲁为轻工业部副部长；
    韩权华（女）为国务院参事；
    陈维帆为中华人民共和国驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟大使馆参赞；
    张光天为中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国大使馆参赞；
    张一鸣为中华人民共和国驻芬兰共和国大使馆参赞。
    免去孔祥祯的交通部副部长职务。
