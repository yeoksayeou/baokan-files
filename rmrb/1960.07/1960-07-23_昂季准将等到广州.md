Title: Brigadier General Aung Gyi Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-07-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 22nd: Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, along with representatives, advisors, and fifteen accompanying staff members, arrived in Guangzhou by train via Shenzhen on the afternoon of the 22nd.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Sun Leyi, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou; Lai Chunfeng, Major General of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou; Zhu Yunqian, Air Force Major General; Luo Peiyuan, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Lu Ying, Deputy Secretary-General of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee; Lin Xi, Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Committee, and others. In the evening, Vice Mayor Sun Leyi hosted a banquet to welcome the distinguished guests. The guests are scheduled to take a special plane to Beijing tomorrow.

### 昂季准将等到广州

1960-07-23
第6版()
专栏：

    昂季准将等到广州
    新华社广州22日电　中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表昂季准将和代表、顾问以及随行工作人员一行十五人，于22日下午乘火车经深圳到达广州。
    前往车站欢迎的有：广州市副市长孙乐宜，中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长赖春风少将、朱云谦空军少将，政协广州市委员会副主席罗培元，广东省人民委员会副秘书长陆荧，广州市人民委员会秘书长林西等。当晚，孙乐宜副市长设宴欢迎贵宾。贵宾们定于明天乘专机赴北京。
