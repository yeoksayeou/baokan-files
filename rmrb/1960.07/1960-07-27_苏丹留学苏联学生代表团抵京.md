Title: Sudanese Students Studying in the Soviet Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th. The Sudanese Students Studying in the Soviet Union Delegation, invited by the All-China Students' Federation to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by train this morning.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Pan Lihua, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and others.

### 苏丹留学苏联学生代表团抵京

1960-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹留学苏联学生代表团抵京
    新华社24日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请前来我国进行友好访问的苏丹留学苏联学生代表团十人，于今晨乘火车抵达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有中华全国学生联合会副主席潘丽华等人。
