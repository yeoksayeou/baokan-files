Title: Marshal Lin Biao Sends a Telegram to General Baluku to Congratulate the 17th Anniversary of the Founding of the Albanian Army
Author:
Date: 1960-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, sent a telegram on the 8th to General Beqir Balluku, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Albania, to congratulate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian People's Army. The telegram is as follows:

Comrade General Beqir Balluku, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Albania:

On the occasion of the glorious 17th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian People's Army, I, on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, extend the warmest brotherly congratulations to you and all officers and men of the Albanian People's Army.

Under the leadership of the glorious Albanian Labor Party and government, the heroic Albanian People's Army faithfully defends the socialist achievements of the motherland and staunchly guards the southwestern outpost of the socialist camp. The Chinese people and the People's Liberation Army express their heartfelt admiration and are greatly encouraged by the heroic spirit and high revolutionary spirit of proletarian internationalism of the Albanian People's Army.

I wish the Albanian People's Army even more brilliant achievements in the cause of strengthening its national defense and in the struggle to defend world peace. May the fraternal friendship between the armies of China and Albania be increasingly consolidated and strengthened in the socialist family headed by the Soviet Union.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Vice Premier of the State Council of the People’s Republic of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Marshal of the People’s Republic of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Lin Biao
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 8, 1960, in Beijing

### 林彪元帅致电巴卢库上将  祝贺阿尔巴尼亚建军十七周年

1960-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    林彪元帅致电巴卢库上将
    祝贺阿尔巴尼亚建军十七周年
    新华社9日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅8日致电阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库上将，祝贺阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军十七周年。电文如下：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库上将同志：
    值此光荣的阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军十七周年之际，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体指战员向您和阿尔巴尼亚人民军全体官兵，致以最热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。
    英勇的阿尔巴尼亚人民军在光荣的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和政府的领导下，忠实地保卫着祖国社会主义的果实，坚强地守卫在社会主义阵营的西南前哨。中国人民和人民解放军为阿尔巴尼亚人民军的英雄气概和高度的无产阶级国际主义的革命精神表示衷心的敬佩并感到极大的鼓舞。
    祝阿尔巴尼亚人民军在加强自己的国防力量的事业和保卫世界和平的斗争中取得更加光辉的成就。愿中、阿两国军队兄弟般的友谊在以苏联为首的社会主义大家庭里日益巩固和加强。
      中华人民共和国国务院
      副总理兼国防部长
      中华人民共和国元帅
                        林彪
      一九六零年七月八日于北京
