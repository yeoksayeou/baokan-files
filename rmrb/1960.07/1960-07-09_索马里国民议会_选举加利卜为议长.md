Title: Somali National Assembly Elects Galib as Speaker
Author:
Date: 1960-07-09
Page: 6

　　Somali National Assembly
　　Elects Galib as Speaker
　　Xinhua News Agency, Mogadishu, July 7 – Jama Abdullahi Galib, a member of the Somali National League and former Speaker of the Legislative Assembly of British Somaliland, was elected Speaker of the National Assembly of the Somali Democratic Republic at today's meeting of the National Assembly, succeeding Aden Abdullah Osman, the former Speaker who has been elected Interim President of the Republic.

### 索马里国民议会  选举加利卜为议长

1960-07-09
第6版()
专栏：

    索马里国民议会
    选举加利卜为议长
    新华社摩加迪沙7日电　索马里民族联盟议员、前英属索马里兰立法议会议长贾马·阿卜杜拉·加利卜在今天的索马里民主共和国国民议会会议上当选为国民议会议长，以接替已当选为共和国临时总统的前议长亚丁·阿卜杜拉·欧斯曼。
