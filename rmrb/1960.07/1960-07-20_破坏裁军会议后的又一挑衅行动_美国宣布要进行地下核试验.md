Title: Another Provocative Act After Disrupting the Disarmament Conference: The United States Announces It Will Conduct Underground Nuclear Tests
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: Washington dispatch: The United States Department of Defense announced today that the United States will conduct eleven underground nuclear explosion tests in the next two years.

The Department of Defense said that these tests will be part of a new series of nuclear weapons tests that the United States intends to conduct. The Department of Defense did not reveal how many tests there would be in total.

The Department of Defense announced that twenty-one underground conventional explosion tests will also be carried out in the next two years.

It is estimated that these tests alone will cost $66 million in the 1961 fiscal year.

### 破坏裁军会议后的又一挑衅行动　美国宣布要进行地下核试验

1960-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　破坏裁军会议后的又一挑衅行动
　　美国宣布要进行地下核试验
    据新华社17日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国防部今天宣布，美国将在今后两年内进行十一次地下核爆炸试验。
    国防部说，这些试验将是美国要进行的一系列新的核武器试验的一部分。国防部没有透露全部试验究竟有多少次。
    国防部宣布，在今后两年内还要作二十一次地下常规爆炸试验。
    估计这些试验仅在1961财政年度就要花费六千六百万美元。
