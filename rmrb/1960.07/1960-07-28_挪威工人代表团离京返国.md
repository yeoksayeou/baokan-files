Title: Norwegian Workers' Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-07-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: The Norwegian workers' delegation of three people, led by Chairman Laurenten, who came to China for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, visited Beijing, Harbin, Anshan, Tianjin, Shanghai and other places, and took a plane today to leave Beijing for home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Zhang Tianmin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Construction Trade Union, Du Yanqing, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Light Industry Trade Union, and Li Yunchuan, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union.

### 挪威工人代表团离京返国

1960-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    挪威工人代表团离京返国
    新华社27日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的挪威工人代表团一行三人，在团长劳润特金率领下，访问了北京、哈尔滨、鞍山、天津、上海等地后，已在今天乘机离京返国。
    前往机场送行的有中国建筑工会全国委员会主席张天民，中国轻工业工会全国委员会主席杜延庆，中国机械工会全国委员会主席李云川等人。
