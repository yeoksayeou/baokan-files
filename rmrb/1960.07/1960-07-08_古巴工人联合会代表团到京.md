Title: Cuban Workers' Confederation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th. At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the four-member delegation of the Cuban Workers' Confederation, led by Odon Alvarez de la Campa, Secretary of Foreign Relations of the Cuban Workers' Confederation, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

The delegation is visiting China after visiting the Soviet Union.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Li Zaiven, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Li Yunchuan, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union.

### 古巴工人联合会代表团到京

1960-07-08
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴工人联合会代表团到京
    新华社7日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的古巴工人联合会代表团一行四人，在古巴工联对外关系书记奥东·阿伐莱斯·德·拉·坎巴的率领下，今日上午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团是在苏联参观访问后来我国访问的。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会书记处书记栗再温，中国机械工会全国委员会主席李云川等。
