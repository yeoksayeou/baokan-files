Title: Chairman Mao Receives Representatives of Literary and Art Workers
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd: Chairman Mao Zedong, along with other Party and state leaders Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Lu Dingyi, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Yang Shangkun, Xi Zhongxun, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo and others, received all the representatives attending the Third Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers this afternoon.

(Attached picture)
Chairman Mao Zedong and other Party and state leaders receive all the representatives of the Third Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers (top picture) and take a group photo (bottom picture). Our reporter Lu Xiangyou took the photos.

### 毛主席接见文艺工作者代表

1960-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见文艺工作者代表
