Title: Famous French Social Activists Mr. and Mrs. Gabitan Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd. Mr. and Mrs. René Gabitan, famous French social activists and professors of law and economics at the University of Paris, and their children, who were invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to visit our country, have left Beijing by plane on the 22nd to return to their country after visiting Beijing, Shanghai, Luoyang, Sanmenxia and other places in our country.

### 法国著名社会活动家加比唐夫妇回国

1960-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    法国著名社会活动家加比唐夫妇回国
    新华社23日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的法国著名社会活动家、巴黎大学法律及经济学系教授雷纳·加比唐夫妇和他们的子女，在我国北京、上海、洛阳、三门峡等地参观访问后，已经在22日乘飞机离开北京返国。
