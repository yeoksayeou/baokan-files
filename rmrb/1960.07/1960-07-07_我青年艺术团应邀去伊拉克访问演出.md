Title: Our Youth Art Troupe Invited to Visit and Perform in Iraq
Author:
Date: 1960-07-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Chinese Youth Art Troupe, a group of thirty people, left Beijing by plane today for Iraq. The art troupe was invited by the Iraqi government to visit and perform. The art troupe is led by Wen Fei, and the members include outstanding young actors in Peking opera, song and dance, and acrobatics.

### 我青年艺术团应邀去伊拉克访问演出

1960-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

    我青年艺术团应邀去伊拉克访问演出
    新华社6日讯　中国青年艺术团一行三十人，今天乘飞机离开北京前往伊拉克。艺术团是应伊拉克政府邀请前往进行访问演出的。艺术团由文菲率领，团员中有京剧、歌舞、杂技等方面的优秀青年演员。
