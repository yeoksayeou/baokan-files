Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Argentinian Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th — Vice Premier Chen Yi received the entire Argentinian Youth Delegation headed by Miguel Gomensor Bèsier this afternoon.
    Present at the reception were Shen Jian, Wang Zhaohua, Qian Liren and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见阿根廷青年代表团

1960-07-06
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见阿根廷青年代表团
    新华社5日讯　陈毅副总理在今天下午接见了以米盖尔·哥曼斯·贝斯西埃为首的阿根廷青年代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有申健、王照华、钱李仁等人。
