Title: Chinese Delegation Returning from Poznań International Fair

Author:
Date: 1960-07-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Chinese government delegation, led by Wei Heng, Governor of Shanxi Province, which had been invited by the government of the Polish People's Republic to attend the opening ceremony of the 29th Poznań International Fair, returned to Beijing by train at 9 a.m. today.

Welcoming them at the station were Wu Guangtang, Deputy Governor of Shanxi Province, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The Polish Embassy in China also sent a representative to welcome them at the station.

### 参加波兹南博览会的我国代表团回国

1960-07-06
第5版()
专栏：

    参加波兹南博览会的我国代表团回国
    新华社5日讯　应波兰人民共和国政府邀请前往波兰参加第二十九届波兹南国际博览会开幕典礼的我国政府代表团，在山西省省长卫恒率领下，今天上午九时乘火车回到北京。
    到车站欢迎的有山西省副省长武光汤、外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清。
    波兰驻华大使馆也派代表前往车站欢迎。
