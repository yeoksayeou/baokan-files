Title: The First Team of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Survey Held Its First Meeting in Wanding Town, China. Liu Minghui Hosted a Banquet to Welcome the Burmese Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-14
Page: 6
　　
　　The First Team of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Survey
　　Held Its First Meeting in Wanding Town, China
　　Liu Minghui Hosted a Banquet to Welcome the Burmese Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, July 13th: According to the agreement of the First Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, the first meeting of the First Team of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Survey was held in Wanding Town, China, on July 12th.
    The meeting was conducted in a warm and friendly atmosphere under the chairmanship of Yao Zhongming, the chief representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, and Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of the Burmese side. At the meeting, the two sides exchanged the lists of the captains, deputy captains, and team leaders of the First Team of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Survey. The chief representatives of both China and Burma spoke at the meeting. Members of the Joint Committee were also assigned to convey the agreement of the First Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee on survey work to the captains and deputy captains of the Joint Survey Team.
    At the meeting, the captains of both sides unanimously expressed their determination and confidence to complete the tasks assigned by the Joint Committee on schedule through close and friendly cooperation.
    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, July 13th: The Burmese delegation, headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, arrived in Wanding Town, China, at 10:00 a.m. on the 12th. They came to China to attend the first meeting of the First Team of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Survey, according to the agreement of the First Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee.
    Yao Zhongming, the chief representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee; Liu Minghui, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province; Major General Li Xifu, head of the troops stationed in Yunnan; Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee; Li Linwu, Lei Chunguo (Jingpo nationality), and Si Weizhang (Dai nationality), Vice Governors of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture; Lieutenant Colonel Chen Yanbin and Huang Fenglin, representatives of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee; Zhang Guoqi, advisor; as well as the Chinese captain and advisor of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Survey Team and 400 people, went to the bridgehead to welcome them.
    The Burmese delegation attending the meeting included: Brigadier General Aung Gyi, head of the delegation; U San Lun, Colonel Soe Myint, U Khun Ohn Kya, advisor U Ba Sein, Colonel La Aung, Colonel Ba Than, U Soe Hsaing, as well as the Burmese captain, deputy captain, team leaders, deputy team leaders, and accompanying personnel of the First Team of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Survey.
    Liu Minghui, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 12th to entertain Brigadier General Aung Gyi and all the distinguished guests of the Burmese delegation. Yao Zhongming, the chief representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, and other representatives and advisors attended the banquet. During the banquet, the guests frequently toasted to the friendship between China and Burma. The banquet ended in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 中缅边界联合勘察队第一队　在我国畹町镇举行首次会议　刘明辉设宴欢迎缅甸代表团

1960-07-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　中缅边界联合勘察队第一队
　　在我国畹町镇举行首次会议
　　刘明辉设宴欢迎缅甸代表团
    新华社昆明13日电　根据中缅边界联合委员会第一次会议的协议，中缅边界联合勘察队第一队首次会议，于7月12日在中国畹町镇举行。
    会议是在中缅边界联合委员会我方首席代表姚仲明和缅甸首席代表昂季准将的主持下，在热烈友好的气氛中进行的。在会上双方交换了中缅边界联合勘察队第一队的队长、副队长和组长的名单，中缅双方首席代表在会上讲了话。双方并指定联合委员会的成员分头向联合勘察队的队长、副队长，传达了中缅边界联合委员会第一次会议关于勘察工作的协议。
    会上，双方队长一致表示，有决心和信心，以亲密友好的合作来如期完成联合委员会所交给的任务。
    新华社昆明13日电　以中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表昂季准将为首的缅甸代表团一行三十人，于12日上午十时抵达我国边境畹町镇。他们是根据中缅边界联合委员会第一次会议的协议，到我国参加中缅边界联合勘案队第一队首次会议的。
    中缅边界联合委员会我方首席代表姚仲明，云南省副省长刘明辉，驻云南部队首长黎锡福少将，云南省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，德宏傣族景颇族自治州副州长李临五、雷春国（景颇族）、思伟章（傣族），中缅边界联合委员会我方代表陈雁彬中校、黄凤麟，顾问张国器，以及中缅边界联合勘察队的我方队长、顾问和四百名群众，前往桥头迎接。
    前来出席会议的缅甸代表团有：团长昂季准将，团员吴山伦、苏敏上校、吴昆翁佳，顾问吴巴森，拉昂上校、巴丹上校、吴苏幸，以及中缅联合委员会联合勘察队第一队的缅方队长、副队长、组长、副组长和随行人员等。
    云南省副省长刘明辉在12日晚举行宴会，招待昂季准将和缅甸代表团的全体贵宾，中缅边界联合委员会我方首席代表姚仲明及其他代表和顾问出席宴会作陪。席间，宾主双方频频为中缅两国的友好干杯。宴会在热情友好的气氛中结束。
