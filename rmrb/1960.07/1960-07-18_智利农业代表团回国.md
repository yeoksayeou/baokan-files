Title: Chilean Agricultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-07-18
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: The Chilean agricultural delegation, which was invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit our country, left Beijing this afternoon to return home.
　　The head of the delegation, Almeida, had already returned home on the 8th.

### 智利农业代表团回国

1960-07-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　智利农业代表团回国
    新华社17日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的智利农业代表团已经在今天下午离开北京返国。
    代表团团长阿耳梅达已经在8日先行返国。
