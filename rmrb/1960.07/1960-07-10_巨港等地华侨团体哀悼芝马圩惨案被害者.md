Title: Overseas Chinese Groups in Palembang and Other Places Mourn Victims of the Tjimahi Massacre
Author:
Date: 1960-07-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 8th. Overseas Chinese groups in Palembang, Padang, Jakarta and other places in Indonesia have recently sent telegrams or letters to the families of Yang Mumei and Ye Jinniang, two overseas Chinese women who were brutally murdered in the Tjimahi Massacre, expressing their deep condolences and indignation at the atrocities committed by the West Java Army authorities under the instigation of powerful groups in Indonesia. They warmly support the serious protest lodged by Huang Zhen, the Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, with the Indonesian government.

    The Palembang Overseas Chinese General Association expressed sympathy and condolences to the families of the deceased in their telegram of condolence, and expressed their support for Ambassador Huang Zhen's reasonable demands to the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs for the severe punishment of the murderers and the cessation of forced evictions. The Palembang Overseas Chinese General Association also remitted 20,000 rupiah to the victims' families as funeral expenses.

    The Padang Chinese General Association condemned the atrocities of the West Java Army, instigated by powerful groups in Indonesia, who opened fire and killed overseas Chinese in their telegram of condolence. The telegram stated that the Padang Chinese General Association has telegraphed the Indonesian military and civilian authorities, demanding that the murderers be severely punished, forced evictions be stopped, guarantees be given that the same incidents will not occur again, and the lives and property of overseas Chinese be protected.

    On the 7th and 8th, more than a dozen overseas Chinese groups in Jakarta sent telegrams or letters to the families of the victims, expressing their condolences.

### 巨港等地华侨团体哀悼芝马圩惨案被害者

1960-07-10
第5版()
专栏：

    巨港等地华侨团体哀悼芝马圩惨案被害者
    新华社雅加达8日电　印度尼西亚的巨港、巴塘、雅加达等地华侨团体，近日来纷纷打电报或写信给芝马圩惨案中惨遭杀害的两位华侨妇女杨木妹和叶金娘的家属，向他们表示深切的哀悼，并且对印度尼西亚有势力集团策动下西爪哇陆军当局残杀华侨的暴行表示愤慨。他们热烈支持中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇向印度尼西亚政府提出的严重抗议。
    巨港的华侨总会在唁电中对死者的家属表示同情和慰问，并且表示支持黄镇大使向印度尼西亚外交部提出的严惩凶手、停止迫迁等合理要求。巨港华侨总会还给受害者家属汇了二万盾作为丧葬费用。
    巴塘的中华总会在唁电中谴责印度尼西亚有势力集团策动下西爪哇陆军竟然开枪打死华侨的暴行。唁电说，巴塘中华总会已致电印度尼西亚军事和民政当局，要求严惩凶手、停止迫迁，保证不再发生同样的事件，保障华侨的生命和财产。
    7日和8日雅加达又有十几个华侨团体打电报或写信给受难者家属表示哀悼。
