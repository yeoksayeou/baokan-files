Title: The Voice of Africa
Author: Mòhǎmmòdé·Ālǐ—Fēidùlì
Date: 1960-07-30
Page: 8

    The Voice of Africa
    [Sudan] Muhammad Ali al-Fayturi
Your voice, Africa, sometimes low, sometimes loud, reaches my heart. I see this voice, I feel this voice, and in this voice, I smell the scent of the damp earth. In this voice, I hear the breath of the Congo, slowly drifting from the distant heat. Your voice shakes me. The rumbling of the storm—is the low echo of this voice. It is in the unceasing pulse of life, in the surprised eyes of youth, in the waves of youthful laughter, in the solemn steps of the masses—I love this voice, because in its roar the invaders will foresee their doom is near. I love this voice, because in this voice the truth of our hearts rings bravely and sacredly!

### 非洲的声音
穆罕默德·阿里—菲杜利
1960-07-30
第8版()
专栏：

    非洲的声音
    〔苏丹〕穆罕默德·阿里—菲杜利你的声音，非洲啊，时而低沉，时而响亮地传到我的心坎里。我看见这声音，我感到这声音，在这声音中，我嗅到那湿漉漉的大地的气味。在这声音中我听见刚果的呼吸，在炎热的远方徐徐地飘动。你的声音震荡着我。暴风雨的隆隆声——是这声音的低沉的回响。它在生命的不停的跳动里，在青年惊讶的眼睛里，在青春欢笑的声浪里，在群众庄严的步伐里——我爱这声音，因为在它的轰鸣中侵略者会预感到他的末日快要临头。我爱这声音，因为在这声音中勇敢而神圣地震响着我们心灵的真理！
