Title: United Nations African Countries Issue Statement Demanding Immediate Withdrawal of Belgian Troops from Congo
Author:
Date: 1960-07-20
Page: 6

　　United Nations African Countries Issue Statement
　　Demanding Immediate Withdrawal of Belgian Troops from Congo
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 19th - The nine African countries at the United Nations yesterday demanded the immediate withdrawal of Belgian troops from the Congo and condemned any actions aimed at separating Katanga Province from the Congo.

    These African countries issued a statement after holding a meeting saying that they firmly believe that the complete withdrawal of Belgian troops from the Congo is essential to restoring peace in the Congo. The statement demanded that Belgium immediately withdraw its troops from the Congo.

    The statement pointed out that Katanga Province is "an indivisible part of the Republic of the Congo as stipulated by the constitution and recognized at the time of Congo's independence." The African countries at the United Nations regret any attempts by the outside world to undermine the territorial integrity of the Congo.

    The members of the United Nations African Group are Ethiopia, Ghana, Guinea, Liberia, Libya, Morocco, Sudan, Tunisia, and the United Arab Republic.

### 联合国非洲国家发表声明　要求比利时军队立即撤出刚果

1960-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　联合国非洲国家发表声明
　　要求比利时军队立即撤出刚果
    新华社19日讯　纽约消息：联合国的九个非洲国家昨天要求比利时立即从刚果撤出军队，并且谴责任何旨在使刚果的加丹加省脱离刚果的行动。
    这些非洲国家在举行一次会议后发表声明说，它们坚信，比利时军队的全部撤出刚果对于恢复在刚果的和平是必不可少的。声明要求比利时立即从刚果撤出军队。
    声明指出，加丹加省是“由宪法规定和在刚果独立时就被承认是刚果共和国的不可分割的一部分”，联合国的非洲国家对外界想破坏刚果领土完整的任何企图感到非常遗憾。
    联合国非洲集团的成员是埃塞俄比亚、加纳、几内亚、利比里亚、利比亚、摩洛哥、苏丹、突尼斯和阿拉伯联合共和国。
