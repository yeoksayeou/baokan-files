Title: Contemporary Heroes (Picture)
Author: Yang Putao
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 5

A high-voltage bridge successfully tested by Wang Linhe and once modified by youth worker Xie Wendong has recently undergone another reform by workers Zhang Huifa (third from the left), Gao Weixun (second from the left), Yao Linxing (fourth from the left), and Chen Wenxiang (first from the left) at the Shanghai Electricity Meter Factory. The reformed high-voltage bridge has been reduced in size by another one-third, uses even less material, performs better, and is more convenient to use. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Putao photographed.

专栏：
当代英雄
    由王林鹤试验成功，又由青工谢文动过一次手术的高压电桥，最近又被上海电表厂工人张惠发（左起第三人）、高为勋（左起第二人）、姚林兴（左起第四人）、陈文祥（左起第一人）等进行了一次改革。改革后的高压电桥，体积又缩小三分之一，用料更省，性能更好，使用更方便  新华社记者  杨溥涛摄
