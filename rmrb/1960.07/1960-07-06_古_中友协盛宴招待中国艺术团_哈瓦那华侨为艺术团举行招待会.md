Title: Cuba-China Friendship Association Hosts Grand Banquet for Chinese Art Troupe; Overseas Chinese in Havana Hold Reception for Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1960-07-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Havana Dispatch The Cuba-China Friendship Association held a grand banquet at the Rio Cristal Park on the evening of July 1st to host the Chinese Art Troupe. The attendees at the banquet enthusiastically shouted slogans such as "Long live China!" and "Mao Zedong - Fidel!"

    Marta Frayde, Vice-President of the Cuba-China Friendship Association, said at the banquet that the visit of Chinese artists to Cuba is a "manifestation of the friendship between the Chinese and Cuban people."

    Chen Zhongjing, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, said, "We have seen the great achievements made by the Cuban Revolution. We have also witnessed with our own eyes that the Cuban Revolution is advancing, is achieving, and will achieve even greater success. The Cuban people have the firm determination and full confidence to defend their motherland and revolution." He said, "The people of the world should and must support the Cuban people. I believe that in providing fraternal support to Cuba, the 650 million Chinese people will also be no exception."

    On the afternoon of the 4th, the overseas Chinese in Havana held a grand reception for the Chinese Art Troupe.

    Guan Bangcun, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of Overseas Chinese in Cuba Welcoming the Chinese Art Troupe, delivered a welcome speech at the reception, congratulating the art troupe on its successful performances in Cuba, and presented a banner to the art troupe.

### 古—中友协盛宴招待中国艺术团  哈瓦那华侨为艺术团举行招待会

1960-07-06
第6版()
专栏：

    古—中友协盛宴招待中国艺术团
    哈瓦那华侨为艺术团举行招待会
    据新华社哈瓦那电　古巴—中国友好协会1日晚在里约里斯塔耳公园举行盛大宴会，招待中国艺术团。出席宴会的人在席间热情地高呼“中国万岁！”和“毛泽东—菲德尔”等口号。
    古巴—中国友好协会副主席玛塔·弗赖德夫人在宴会上说，中国艺术家们到古巴访问是“中国和古巴语人民之间的友谊的明证”。
    中国艺术团团长陈忠经说，我们看到了古巴革命取得的伟大的成就。我们也亲眼看到古巴革命正在前进，正在取得并将取得更大的成就。古巴人民有坚定的决心和充分的信心来保卫他们的祖国和革命。他说，“全世界人民应该而且必须支持古巴人民。我认为，在给予古巴以兄弟般的支持方面，六亿五千万中国人民也将毫不例外。”
    4日下午，哈瓦那的华侨为中国艺术团举行了盛大的招待会。
    古巴各界华侨欢迎中国艺术团筹备委员会主席关邦存在招待会上致欢迎词，祝贺艺术团在古巴的成功的演出，并且向艺术团赠送了锦旗。
