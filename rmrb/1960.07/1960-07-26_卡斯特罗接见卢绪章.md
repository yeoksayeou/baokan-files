Title: Castro Receives Lu Xuzhang
Author:
Date: 1960-07-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, July 24th: Cuban Prime Minister Castro received Lu Xuzhang, head of the Chinese trade delegation, and Chen Zhongjing, representative for the signing of the China-Cuba cultural cooperation agreement, last night. All members of the trade delegation also attended the reception. Zeng Tao, director of the Xinhua News Agency's Havana branch, and the deputy head of the Chinese Art Troupe were also present at the reception. The host and guests had a sincere and friendly long conversation.

### 卡斯特罗接见卢绪章

1960-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    卡斯特罗接见卢绪章
    新华社哈瓦那24日电　古巴总理卡斯特罗昨晚接见了中国贸易代表团团长卢绪章和签订中国—古巴文化合作协定的代表陈忠经。贸易代表团全体团员也参加了这次接见。接见时在座的有新华社哈瓦那分社社长曾涛和中国艺术团的副团长。宾主之间进行了真诚而友好的长谈。
