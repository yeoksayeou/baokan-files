Title: One Water Pump, One Heart
Author: Liu Wenzheng
Date: 1960-07-19
Page: 8

　　One Water Pump, One Heart
　　Liu Wenzheng
　　One water pump, one heart,
　　Mounted on a truck, sent to the countryside.
　　Resisting drought and draining for irrigation like rainfall,
　　The commune members are like family.
　　One water pump, one heart,
　　Flowing water sings in praise of the Great Leap Forward.
　　Greening the plains, dyeing the hillsides green,
　　Rice pierces the clouds on terraced fields above the water.
　　One water pump, one heart,
　　Wholeheartedly supporting the new countryside.
　　Workers and peasants join hands to seize a bumper harvest,
　　Granaries full of rice, granaries full of silver.

### 一台水泵一颗心
刘文正
1960-07-19
第8版()
专栏：

　　一台水泵一颗心
　　刘文正一台水泵一颗心，装上汽车送农村。抗旱排灌人降雨，公社社员是亲人。一台水泵一颗心，流水欢歌颂跃进。染绿平原染山岗，水上梯田稻穿云。一台水泵一颗心，心心支援新农村。工农携手夺丰收，满囤大米满囤银。
