Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Japanese Socialist Party Agricultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th — Vice Premier Chen Yi received the entire membership of the Japanese Socialist Party Agricultural Delegation headed by Takeshi Takeuchi this morning, and held friendly talks.

Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Hu Yuzhi, Xi Fengzhou, Xiao Xiangqian and others.

Koichi Saiyuanji, a Japanese peace activist in Beijing, was also present at the reception.

### 陈毅副总理接见日社会党农业代表团

1960-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日社会党农业代表团
    新华社4日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了以竹内猛为首的日本社会党农业访华代表团全体成员，并且进行了友好谈话。
    接见时在座的有廖承志、胡愈之、席凤洲、萧向前等。
    接见时，在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
