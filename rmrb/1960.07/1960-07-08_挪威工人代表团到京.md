Title: Norwegian Workers' Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: A Norwegian workers' delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon, led by delegation leader Laurenten.

Shao Naifen, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 挪威工人代表团到京

1960-07-08
第6版()
专栏：

    挪威工人代表团到京
    新华社7日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的挪威工人代表团，在团长劳润特金率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长邵乃奋等。
