Title: Congolese Traitor Tshombe to Send People to the United Nations to Raise the So-Called "Independence" Issue of Katanga Province

Author:

Date: 1960-07-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 29th, Elisabethville Dispatch: Tshombe, the governor of Katanga province in the Congo and an agent of foreign monopoly capital, announced on the 28th that he would send a three-person delegation to Brussels and New York on the 29th to submit the so-called "independence" issue of Katanga to the Belgian government and the United Nations.

This agent of foreign monopoly capital said at a press conference that an American corporation had suggested establishing a radio and television station in Katanga. He also said that he would welcome UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld or Deputy Secretary-General Bunche (an American) to "visit" Katanga.

### 刚果叛徒冲伯将派人去联合国  提出所谓加丹加省“独立”问题

1960-07-30
第5版()
专栏：

    刚果叛徒冲伯将派人去联合国
    提出所谓加丹加省“独立”问题
    新华社29日讯　伊利沙伯维尔消息：刚果加丹加省省长、外国垄断资本的代理人冲伯28日宣布，将在29日派一个三人代表团去布鲁塞尔和纽约，把加丹加的所谓“独立”问题提交比利时政府和联合国。
    这个外国垄断资本的代理人在记者招待会上说，一家美国大公司已经建议在加丹加建立一个无线电和电视站。他还说，他将欢迎联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德或副秘书长本奇（美国人）“访问”加丹加。
