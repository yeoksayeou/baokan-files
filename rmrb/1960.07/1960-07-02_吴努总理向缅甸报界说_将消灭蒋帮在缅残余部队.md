Title: Premier U Nu Tells Burmese Press: Chiang Gang Remnants in Burma Will Be Eliminated
Author:
Date: 1960-07-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 28th - Burmese Prime Minister U Nu said at a press conference today that the Burmese armed forces will try to eliminate the Chiang Gang remnant troops in Burma.

He said he could not say when these remnants would be completely eliminated.

U Nu revealed that the Chiang Gang remnants in Burma are still engaged in robbery and opium smuggling.

### 吴努总理向缅甸报界说  将消灭蒋帮在缅残余部队

1960-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    吴努总理向缅甸报界说
    将消灭蒋帮在缅残余部队
    新华社仰光28日电　缅甸总理吴努今天在记者招待会上说，缅甸武装部队将设法消灭在缅甸的蒋帮残余部队。
    他说，他说不上什么时候能完全消灭这些残余部队。
    吴努揭露在缅甸的蒋帮残余部队现在还在进行抢劫和走私鸦片。
