Title: Two Cuban Revolutionary Youth Organization Leaders Send Telegram Thanking China for its Support: If the Aggressors Dare to Invade Cuba, Their Corpses Will Become Fertilizer for Cuban Soil
Author:
Date: 1960-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 20th -- The All-China Youth Federation has received a telegram from Major Joel Iglesias, Head of the Youth Department of the Cuban Revolutionary Directorate, and Captain Fernando Ravelo, Head of the Cuban Rebel Army Youth Federation, thanking the Chinese people and youth for their support of the Cuban Revolution.

The telegram said, "Cuban youth thank the Chinese people and Chinese youth for their support of our revolution. Our people will firmly fight against the aggression of U.S. imperialism. We believe that Chinese and Cuban youth will further strengthen friendly ties and enhance solidarity. The beast has been mortally wounded. If they invade our territory, the corpses of the aggressors will be left to fertilize our land. We vow to defend the motherland to the death. We will be victorious."

### 古巴两位革命青年组织负责人  电谢我国人民对古巴的支持  如果侵略者胆敢侵犯古巴，他们的尸体将成为古巴土地的肥料

1960-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴两位革命青年组织负责人 
    电谢我国人民对古巴的支持
    如果侵略者胆敢侵犯古巴，他们的尸体将成为古巴土地的肥料
    新华社20日讯　中华全国青年联合会收到了古巴革命指导委员会青年部负责人霍埃尔·伊格雷西阿斯少校和古巴起义军青年联合会负责人费尔南多·勒维洛大尉发来的电报，电报中感谢中国人民和青年对古巴革命的支持。
    电报说，“古巴青年感谢中国人民和中国青年对我们革命的支持。我们的人民将坚定地为反对美帝国主义的侵略而斗争。我们相信中国青年和古巴青年将更加密切友谊联系，加强团结。野兽已经受了致命伤。如果他们侵犯我们的领土，侵略者的尸体将留下来作我们土地的肥料。誓死保卫祖国。我们将会胜利。”
