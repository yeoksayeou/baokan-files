Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi Meets with Hungarian Party Workers Delegation; Comrade Liu Lantao Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Hungarian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-07-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, met this afternoon with all members of the Socialist Workers' Party workers delegation headed by Sándor József, member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present at the meeting were Liu Lantao, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee. Károly Szigeti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, was also present at the meeting.
    Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd: Comrade Liu Lantao, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, hosted a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party workers delegation. Attending the banquet were An Ziwen and Liu Ningyi, members of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhang Panshi and other responsible staff members of the CPC Central Committee organs.
    Károly Szigeti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, also attended the banquet. (Attached picture)
    Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Head of Delegation Sándor József shake hands warmly.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao

### 刘少奇同志会见匈党工作人员代表团  刘澜涛同志设宴欢送匈牙利贵宾

1960-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇同志会见匈党工作人员代表团
    刘澜涛同志设宴欢送匈牙利贵宾
    新华社2日讯　中国共产党中央委员会副主席刘少奇今天下午会见了以匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员山多尔·约瑟夫为首的社会主义工人党工作人员代表团全体同志，并同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。会见时在座的有中共中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛、中央委员刘宁一。会见时，匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂·卡洛伊也在座。
    新华社2日讯　中共中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛同志今晚设宴欢送匈牙利社会主义工人党工作人员代表团。参加宴会的有中共中央委员安子文、刘宁一，中共中央机关负责工作人员张磐石等。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂·卡洛伊也出席了宴会。（附图片）
