Title: Burhan Receives Nigerian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-07-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 21st. Burhan, Vice President of the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association, received Nigerian youth representatives Abesu Gan, Chairman of the Nigerian League, and Essang Mangali, Secretary of the Women's Department of the Nigerian League, today and had a cordial conversation with them. On behalf of the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association, Vice President Burhan presented the guests with a documentary film entitled "Hold High the Banner of the Bandung Conference and Support the Struggle of the African People."

### 包尔汉接见尼日利亚客人

1960-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    包尔汉接见尼日利亚客人
    新华社21日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会副会长包尔汉今天接见了尼日利亚青年代表：尼日利亚联盟主席阿别苏根和尼日利亚联盟妇女部秘书埃桑·曼加利，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。包尔汉副会长代表中非友协赠给客人“高举万隆会议旗帜，支持非洲人民斗争”电影纪录片一部。
