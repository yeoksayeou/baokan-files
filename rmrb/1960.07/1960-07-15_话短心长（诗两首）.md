Author: Li Xin
Date: 1960-07-15
Page: 8

　　Few Words, Great Meaning
    (Two Poems)
    Li Xin
　　I. Sending off "Aote Gen" (Note)
　　Attending the National Congress of Heroes
　　Seeing off the child's retreating figure,
　　Also carrying the mother's heart;
　　It's that Mom also wants to see Chairman Mao,
　　Not just cherishing Aote Gen.
    (Note) Aote Gen, in Mongolian: the youngest son.
　　II. A Letter from a Female Commune Member of an Urban People's Commune
　　It's not that love has changed in any way,
　　It's that a woman has stood up.
　　I am still a female commune member;
　　Not just your she,
　　The child's mother.
　　The old calendar can't be looked at,
　　Women are no worse than men.

### 话短心长（诗两首）

1960-07-15
第8版()
专栏：

　  话短心长
    （诗两首）
    李欣
　　一、送“敖特根”（注）
　  参加全国群英会
　  送走了孩子的背影，
  　也带去了妈妈的心；
  　是妈也想看看毛主席呀，
  　不只是疼念敖特根。
    （注）敖特根，蒙语：最小的儿子。
　　二、一个城市人民公社女社员的信
　  不是爱情有什么变化，
　  是一个女人站起来啦。
　  我还是一个女社员；
　  不只是你的她，
　  孩子的妈。
　  老皇历看不得，
　  女人并不比男人差。
