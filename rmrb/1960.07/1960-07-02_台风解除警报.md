Title: Typhoon Alert Lifted
Author:
Date: 1960-07-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon alert cancellation notice at 12:00 noon on the 1st:

    The third typhoon of 1960, which made landfall in Guangdong, has brushed past the western coast of Guangdong and moved to northern Democratic Republic of Vietnam, with a significant weakening in typhoon intensity. Affected by the typhoon, it is expected that there will still be force six or seven winds in the Beibu Gulf today, and heavy to torrential rain in western Guangdong, southern Guangxi, and southern Yunnan. The impact will continue to weaken thereafter, and the typhoon alert is lifted.

### 台风解除警报

1960-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

    台风解除警报
    新华社1日讯　中央气象科学研究所1日中午12时发布台风解除警报：
    在广东登陆的1960年第三号台风，已经擦过广东西部沿海，移到越南民主共和国北部，台风强度显著减弱。受台风影响，预计今天北部湾仍有六、七级的大风，广东西部、广西南部、云南南部有大雨到暴雨。以后影响将继续减弱，台风警报解除。
