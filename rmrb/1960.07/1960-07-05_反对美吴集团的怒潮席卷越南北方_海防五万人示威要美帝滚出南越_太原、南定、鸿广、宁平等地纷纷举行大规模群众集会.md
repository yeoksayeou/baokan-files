Title: A Tide of Anger Against the US-Ngo Dinh Diem Clique Sweeps Across Northern Vietnam; 50,000 People Demonstrate in Haiphong Demanding that US Imperialists Get Out of South Vietnam; Mass Rallies Held in Thai Nguyen, Nam Dinh, Hong Gai, Ninh Binh and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 4th: The movement of people in Northern Vietnam against US imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique reached a climax on July 3rd. On this day, in addition to the rally and demonstration of 200,000 people held in the capital Hanoi, large-scale rallies and parades against US imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique were held in various places.

According to local newspapers, on the morning of July 3rd, in Thai Nguyen City, the capital of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region, more than 10,000 cadres, workers, and people of various ethnic minorities held a rally against US imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique. The conference firmly demanded that the International Commission for Vietnam revise its decision allowing the US Military Assistance Advisory Group to increase military personnel and saying that Ngo Dinh Diem's Ordinance No. 10 of 1959 was not related to the Geneva Accords. People angrily shouted "US imperialism get out of South Vietnam," and the roars shook the valleys.

After the meeting, people took hoes, shovels and other agricultural tools to the fields to participate in the "Movement to Bury the US-Ngo Clique and Accumulate Fertilizer," using practical actions to increase production to oppose US imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique.

In Nam Dinh City, the textile industry center of Northern Vietnam, 24,000 people from all walks of life held a massive rally and parade against US imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique on July 3rd. The conference strongly condemned the towering crimes of the US-Ngo clique and demanded that US imperialism get out of South Vietnam, South Korea, Japan and Taiwan. The conference passed a resolution demanding that the International Commission fairly carry out its mission and ensure the strict implementation of the Geneva Accords.

In addition, large-scale rallies and parades against US imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique were held in Hong Gai District, a famous coal mining area, and Ninh Binh City on July 3rd.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 3rd: Haiphong News: More than 50,000 people in Haiphong held a massive anti-American demonstration this afternoon. People with incomparable hatred for the US-Ngo Dinh Diem clique constantly raised their arms and shouted: "US imperialism get out of South Vietnam!" "Down with the US-Ngo clique!" The slogans resounded throughout the city.

Before the demonstration, a grand rally was held in the square in front of the Municipal Grand Theater. Tran Binh, vice chairman of the Haiphong City Committee of the Fatherland Front, spoke at the meeting. After talking about the unprecedented isolation and ugly performance of US imperialism in the face of the anti-American storm in Asia and Latin America, he severely condemned the US aggression against South Vietnam and the bloody crimes of the US-Ngo clique in South Vietnam.

Tran Binh said that the people of Haiphong fully support the struggle of the South Vietnamese people who have stood up against the persecution of the US-Ngo clique and demanded the reunification of the motherland.

### 反对美吴集团的怒潮席卷越南北方  海防五万人示威要美帝滚出南越  太原、南定、鸿广、宁平等地纷纷举行大规模群众集会

1960-07-05
第6版()
专栏：

    反对美吴集团的怒潮席卷越南北方
    海防五万人示威要美帝滚出南越
    太原、南定、鸿广、宁平等地纷纷举行大规模群众集会
    新华社河内4日电　越南北方各地人民反对美帝国主义和吴庭艳集团的运动7月3日达到了高潮。这一天，除首都河内举行了有二十万人参加的集会和示威游行外，各地都举行了大规模的反对美帝国主义和吴庭艳集团的集会和游行。
    据此间报纸报道，7月3日早晨，在越北自治区首府太原市，一万多干部、工人和各少数民族举行了反对美帝国主义和吴庭艳集团的集会。大会坚决要求越南国际委员会修正它作出的允许美国军事顾问团增加军事人员以及把吴庭艳集团1959年第十号法令说成是与日内瓦协议无关的决议。人们愤怒地高呼“美帝国主义滚出南越”，吼声震撼山谷。
    大会结束后，人们带着锄头、铁锹等农具到田里参加“埋葬美吴集团积肥运动”，用增加生产的实际行动来反对美帝国主义和吴庭艳集团。
    在越南北方纺织工业中心南定市，各界人民二万四千人7月3日举行了声势浩大的反对美帝国主义和吴庭艳集团的集会和游行。大会强烈谴责美吴集团的滔天罪行，要求美帝国主义从南越、南朝鲜、日本和台湾滚出去。大会通过决议，要求国际委员会公正地执行自己的任务，保证日内瓦协议的严正履行。
    此外，著名煤矿区鸿广区、宁平市等地7月3日都举行了大规模的反对美帝国主义和吴庭艳集团的集会和游行。
    新华社河内3日电　海防消息：海防市五万多人今天下午举行了声势浩大的反美示威。对美国—吴庭艳集团怀着无比愤恨的人们不断振臂高呼：“美帝国主义滚出南越！”“打倒美吴集团！”口号声响彻市空。
    示威之前，在市立大戏院前的广场上举行了盛大集会。祖国战线海防市委员会副主席陈平在会上讲了话。他在讲到美帝国主义在亚洲和拉丁美洲反美风暴面前空前孤立、丑态百出的情景之后，严厉谴责了美国对南越的侵略和美吴集团在南越的血腥罪行。
    陈平表示，海防人民全力支持已经站起来反对美吴集团的迫害、要求祖国统一的南越人民的斗争。
