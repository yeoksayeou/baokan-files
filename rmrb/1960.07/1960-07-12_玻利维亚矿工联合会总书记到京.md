Title: General Secretary of the Bolivian Miners' Union Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Mario Torres Calleja, General Secretary of the Bolivian Miners' Union, arrived in Beijing by plane today. He is here for a friendly visit to our country at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union.

    Li Chunming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union, and others were at the airport to welcome him.

### 玻利维亚矿工联合会总书记到京

1960-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

　  玻利维亚矿工联合会总书记到京
    新华社11日讯　玻利维亚矿工联合会总书记马里奥·托莱斯·卡耶哈，在今天乘飞机到达北京。他是应中国煤矿工会全国委员会邀请前来我国进行友好访问的。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国煤矿工会全国委员会副主席李春明等。
