Title: First Meeting of the Second Team of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Survey Team Held
Author:
Date: 1960-07-20
Page: 6
　
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, July 19th: The first meeting of the Second Team of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Survey Team was held in Hupang Town, Wa State, Burma, on July 17th and 18th.

　　The meeting was co-chaired by Cheng Zhiping, representative of the Chinese delegation of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee, Major General Ding Rongchang, advisor, and Brigadier General Aung Gyi, chief representative of the Burmese delegation; it proceeded in a harmonious and friendly atmosphere. At the meeting, the two sides exchanged lists of the captains, deputy captains, and group leaders of the Second Joint Survey Team, and respectively conveyed to their survey captains and deputy captains the agreement on survey work reached at the first meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee.

　　Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Representative Cheng Zhiping, and the heads of the survey teams from both sides spoke at the meeting successively.

　　In accordance with the arrangements for joint survey work made at the first meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee, and in light of the specific situation, personnel from both survey teams conducted detailed research and full discussions, and made more specific provisions for the steps of the joint survey work, the time of the upper boundary, and the date of completion. All attendees from both sides expressed extreme satisfaction with the results of the meeting.

　　The Chinese personnel attending the meeting left Hupang on July 19th to return to China.

### 中缅边界联合勘察队二队举行首次会议

1960-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　中缅边界联合勘察队二队举行首次会议
    新华社昆明19日电　中缅边界联合勘察队第二队首次会议于7月17日和18日在缅甸瓦邦的户板镇举行。
    会议由中缅边界联合委员会中国代表团的代表程之平和顾问丁荣昌少将及缅甸代表团首席代表昂季准将共同主持；在融洽友好的气氛中进行。双方在会上交换了联合勘察队第二队的队长、副队长和组长的名单，并分别向各自的勘察队长、副队长传达了中缅边界联合委员会第一次会议关于勘察工作的协议。
    昂季准将、程之平代表和双方勘察队负责人先后在会上讲了话。
    双方勘察队的人员，根据中缅边界联合委员会第一次会议对联合勘察工作的安排，结合具体情况，进行了详尽的研究和充分的讨论，对联合勘察工作的步骤、上界时间以及完成的日期，作了更具体的规定。双方到会人员，对会议的成果都表示极为满意。
    中国方面参加会议的人员已于7月19日离开户板返回中国境内。
