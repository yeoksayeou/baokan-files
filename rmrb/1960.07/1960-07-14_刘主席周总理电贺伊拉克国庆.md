Title: Liu Chairman and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Messages on Iraqi National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-07-14
Page: 1

　　Liu Chairman and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Messages on Iraqi National Day
Xinhua News Agency, July 13th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram today to Najib al-Ruba'i, Chairman of the Sovereignty Council of the Republic of Iraq, congratulating him on the National Day of the Republic of Iraq. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Najib al-Ruba'i, Chairman of the Sovereignty Council of the Republic of Iraq:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of Iraq, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Iraqi people on behalf of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, and in my own name. I wish the friendship between China and Iraq will grow stronger day by day. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.
                  Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                          July 13th, 1960

    Xinhua News Agency, July 13th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram today to Prime Minister of the Republic of Iraq, Qasim, congratulating him on the National Day of the Republic of Iraq. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Abdul Karim Qasim, Prime Minister of the Republic of Iraq:
    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the National Day of the Republic of Iraq, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency, the government and the people of the Republic of Iraq on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.
    In the past two years, the relations between China and Iraq have been further developed under the guidance of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the Bandung Spirit. This not only fully conforms to the interests of the people of China and Iraq, but also contributes to the lofty cause of defending world peace. I firmly believe that through joint efforts, the friendly relations between our two countries will be continuously strengthened and developed.
    I wish the Republic of Iraq prosperity and strength.
            Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                            July 13th, 1960

### 刘主席周总理电贺伊拉克国庆

1960-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘主席周总理电贺伊拉克国庆
    新华社13日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给伊拉克共和国最高权力委员会主席鲁巴伊，祝贺伊拉克共和国国庆，电文如下：伊拉克共和国最高权力委员会主席纳吉布·鲁巴伊阁下：
    值此伊拉克共和国国庆之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国和中国人民并且以我个人的名义向阁下和伊拉克人民致热诚的祝贺。祝中、伊两国间的友谊日益发展。祝贵国繁荣昌盛，人民幸福。
                  中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                          一九六○年七月十三日
    新华社13日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆，祝贺伊拉克共和国国庆。电文如下：伊拉克共和国总理阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·卡塞姆阁下：
    欣逢伊拉克共和国国庆二周年，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府并且以我个人的名义向阁下、伊拉克共和国政府和人民表示热烈的祝贺。
    两年来，中伊两国的关系，在和平共处五项原则和万隆精神的指导下，有了进一步的发展。这不仅完全符合中、伊两国人民的利益，而且有助于保卫世界和平的崇高事业。我深信，通过共同的努力，我们两国间的友好关系必将不断加强和发展。
    祝伊拉克共和国繁荣富强。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                            一九六○年七月十三日
