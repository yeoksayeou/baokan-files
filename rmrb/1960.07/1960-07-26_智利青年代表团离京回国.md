Title: Chilean Youth Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-07-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 25th July. The Chilean Youth Delegation, which came to our country for a friendly visit at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

Seeing them off at the airport were Wang Zhaohua, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China, Qian Liren, Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation, Pan Lihua, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Student Federation, and others.

### 智利青年代表团离京回国

1960-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    智利青年代表团离京回国
    新华社25日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国进行友好访问的智利青年代表团，已在今天乘飞机离京回国。
    到机场送行的有中国共产主义青年团中央书记处书记王照华，中华全国青年联合会秘书长钱李仁，中华全国学生联合会副主席潘丽华等人。
