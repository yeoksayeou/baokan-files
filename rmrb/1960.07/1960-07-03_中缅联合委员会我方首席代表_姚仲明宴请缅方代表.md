Title: Yao Zhongming, Chief Representative of the Chinese Side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee, Hosts a Banquet for Burmese Representatives
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, July 2nd: Yao Zhongming, the chief representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee, hosted a banquet here last night to entertain all the Burmese representatives of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee, headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces.

Among those attending the banquet were Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and his wife, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Myint Thein and his wife, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities Sao Khun Kyi, Minister of Foreign Affairs Sao Khun Hkio, Minister of Kachin State Affairs Duwa Sinwa Nawng, former Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities Sao Hkun Hkio and his wife, former Attorney General U Ba Swe, former Minister of Foreign Affairs U Chan Tun Aung, and other senior government officials.

U Kyaw Yein and his wife, Vice Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, also attended the banquet.

Also attending the banquet were Li Yimang, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and personnel from the Embassy.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests frequently toasted to further strengthening Sino-Burmese friendship and to the satisfactory and comprehensive resolution of the Sino-Burmese border issue.

### 中缅联合委员会我方首席代表  姚仲明宴请缅方代表

1960-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

    中缅联合委员会我方首席代表
    姚仲明宴请缅方代表
    新华社仰光2日电　中缅联合委员会中国方面首席代表姚仲明昨晚在这里设宴招待由国防军副总参谋长昂季准将为首的中缅联合委员会缅甸方面的全体代表。
    参加宴会的有缅甸总理吴努和夫人，最高法院首席法官吴敏登和夫人，民族院议长藻昆基，外交部长藻昆卓，克钦邦事务部长杜瓦信瓦瑙，前民族院议长萧恢塔和夫人，前检察总长吴巴盛，前外交部长吴千吞昂和其他政府高级官员。
    巩固的反法西斯人民自由同盟副主席吴觉迎和夫人也出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和大使馆人员。
    宴会在热烈和友好的气氛中进行。宾主双方频频为进一步加强中缅友谊，圆满和全面地解决中缅边界问题而干杯。
