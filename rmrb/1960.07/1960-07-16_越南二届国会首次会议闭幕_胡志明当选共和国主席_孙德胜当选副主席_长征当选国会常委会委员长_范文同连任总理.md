Title: Closing of the First Session of the Second National Assembly of Vietnam: Ho Chi Minh Elected President of the Republic, Ton Duc Thang Elected Vice President, Truong Chinh Elected Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, Pham Van Dong Reelected as Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 15th. The first session of the Second National Assembly of Vietnam, lasting nine days, successfully concluded this afternoon.

President Ho Chi Minh delivered a speech at the closing session. He pointed out that the territory and influence of imperialism have been greatly reduced, and the struggle movement against imperialism, especially against US imperialism, is surging in various places. The forces of peace are pushing back the forces of imperialist war. Imperialism is heading towards an inevitable path of collapse.

President Ho Chi Minh pointed out that due to the obstruction of US imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem traitorous clique, Vietnam is temporarily divided. The people of South Vietnam are under the brutal rule of the US-Ngo clique and are waging a heroic struggle for the unification and freedom of their homeland. He said that the task of the Vietnamese people, the National Assembly, and the government is to contribute to building a socialist North, achieving the peaceful reunification of the country, and safeguarding peace in Southeast Asia and the world.

President Ho Chi Minh called on the Vietnamese people to more actively participate in the patriotic emulation movement, complete and over fulfill the state's plans. He also encouraged the National Assembly deputies and government cadres to practice thrift, be selfless, and play an exemplary role in unity, work, study, and labor, and to adhere to a humble, simple style, integrating with the masses.

Finally, he exclaimed: "Long live a peaceful, unified, independent, democratic, and prosperous Vietnam!" At this moment, all the representatives stood up, applauding and chanting slogans for a long time. Subsequently, Ton Duc Thang delivered the closing speech.

This afternoon, 50,000 people from all walks of life in Hanoi gathered at the Hanoi Grand Theater Square, singing and dancing enthusiastically, warmly cheering and congratulating the newly elected national leaders.

When President Ho Chi Minh and other party and state leaders ascended the rostrum, 50,000 people burst into thunderous cheers, expressing their infinite trust and love for the national leaders. President Ho Chi Minh and Chairman Truong Chinh spoke to the cheering masses.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 15th. The first session of the Second National Assembly of Vietnam unanimously elected the most beloved leader of the Vietnamese people, Ho Chi Minh, to continue serving as the President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam this morning.

The entire venue was filled with joy this morning, and the representatives smiled happily. When the Executive Chairman of the meeting announced the good news of President Ho Chi Minh's continued election, all the representatives stood up, and stormy applause and cheers continued for a long time.

Ton Duc Thang was elected as the Vice President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The meeting also elected the Standing Committee of the National Assembly this morning. Truong Chinh was elected as the Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly. Hoang Van Hoan, Nguyen Xuan Thuy, Nguyen Tran, Chu Van Tan, Tran Dang Khoa, and Nguyen Van Huong were elected as Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly. Hoang Van Hoan also served as Secretary-General.

The representatives repeatedly stood up, warmly applauding the elected national leaders, and raised their arms and shouted: "Vietnam will definitely be unified!"

The voting was conducted by secret ballot.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 15th. The first session of the Second National Assembly of Vietnam unanimously voted this afternoon, based on the nomination of President Ho Chi Minh, to re-elect Pham Van Dong as Prime Minister of the Government.

Based on the nomination of Hoang Van Hoan on behalf of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, the meeting elected Pham Van Bach to continue serving as President of the Supreme People's Court and Hoang Quoc Viet as Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate.

Based on the nomination of President Ho Chi Minh, the meeting decided that Pham Van Dong and Vo Nguyen Giap would serve as Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council. According to the Vietnamese Constitution, the Chairman of the National Defense Council is held by the President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Based on the nomination of Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, the meeting decided that Pham Hung, Vo Nguyen Giap, Phan Ke Toai, Nguyen Duy Trinh, and Le Thanh Nghi would be Deputy Prime Ministers.

The meeting also decided on the ministers of various ministries and the directors of committees.

### 越南二届国会首次会议闭幕　胡志明当选共和国主席　孙德胜当选副主席　长征当选国会常委会委员长　范文同连任总理

1960-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　越南二届国会首次会议闭幕
　　胡志明当选共和国主席　孙德胜当选副主席
　　长征当选国会常委会委员长　范文同连任总理
    新华社河内15日电　历时九天的越南第二届国会第一次会议今天下午宣告胜利闭幕。
    胡志明主席在闭幕会上发表了讲话。他指出，帝国主义的地盘和势力大大缩小了，反对帝国主义，特别是反对美帝国主义的斗争运动正在各地汹涌澎湃。和平力量正在把帝国主义的战争力量推向后退。帝国主义正在走向不可避免的崩溃的道路。
    胡志明主席指出，由于美帝国主义和吴庭艳卖国集团的阻挠，越南暂时还被分裂。南越人民正处在美吴集团的野蛮统治之下，并正在为祖国的统一和自由进行英勇的斗争。他说，越南人民、国会和政府的任务是为建设社会主义的北方和实现和平统一祖国，为保卫东南亚和世界和平作出贡献。
    胡志明主席号召越南人民更加积极地参加爱国竞赛运动，完成和超额完成国家的计划。他还勉励国会代表和政府干部厉行节约，大公无私，在团结、工作、学习、劳动各方面起模范作用，坚持谦虚、朴素、同群众打成一片的作风。
    最后他高呼：“越南和平、统一、独立、民主和富强万岁！”，这时，全体代表起立，长时间地鼓掌和高呼口号。随后，孙德胜致闭幕词。
    今天下午，河内各界人民五万人聚集在河内大戏院广场载歌载舞，热情欢呼和祝贺新当选的国家领导人。
    当胡志明主席同其他党和国家的领导人登上主席台时，五万群众发出了震天的欢呼声，表示对国家领导人的无限信任和爱戴。胡志明主席和长征委员长向欢呼的群众讲了话。
    新华社河内15日电　越南第二届国会第一次会议今天上午一致选出越南人民最敬爱的领袖胡志明继续担任越南民主共和国主席。
    今天上午整个会场喜气洋洋，代表们笑逐颜开。当会议执行主席宣布胡志明主席继续当选的喜讯时，全体代表起立，暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼声经久不息。
    孙德胜当选为越南民主共和国副主席。
    今天上午会议还选出了国会常务委员会，长征当选国会常务委员会委员长，黄文欢、阮春水、阮阐、朱文晋、陈登科、阮文享当选国会常务委员会副委员长。黄文欢兼任秘书长。
    代表们一次又一次起立，热烈鼓掌欢迎当选的国家领导人，并且振臂高呼：“越南一定统一！”
    投票是以不记名投票方式进行的。
    新华社河内15日电　越南第二届国会第一次会议今天下午根据胡志明主席的提名，一致投票决定范文同连任政府总理。
    会议根据黄文欢代表国会常务委员会的提名，选举范文白连任最高人民法院院长、黄国越任最高人民检察院院长。
    会议根据胡志明主席的提名，决定范文同和武元甲担任国防会议副主席。根据越南宪法，国防会议主席由越南民主共和国主席担任。根据范文同总理的提名，会议决定范雄、武元甲、潘继遂、阮维祯、黎清毅为副总理。
    会议还决定了各部部长和委员会的主任。
