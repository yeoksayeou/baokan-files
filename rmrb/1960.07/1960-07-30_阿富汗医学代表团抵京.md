Title: Afghan Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 29th. The Afghan medical delegation of three, led by Mr. Mohammad Omar, arrived in Beijing on July 29th for a friendly visit to China at the invitation of Minister of Public Health Li Dequan of the People's Republic of China. Ouyang Jing, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Public Health, and others greeted them at the airport.

### 阿富汗医学代表团抵京

1960-07-30
第6版()
专栏：

    阿富汗医学代表团抵京
    新华社29日讯　应中华人民共和国卫生部李德全部长邀请来我国作友好访问的阿富汗医学代表团一行三人，在团长穆罕默德·奥玛尔先生率领下，于7月29日抵京。卫生部办公厅主任欧阳竞等前往机场迎接。
