Title: Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Chen Send Congratulations on the Independence of the Somali Republic; China Decides to Recognize Somalia
Author:
Date: 1960-07-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, today sent a telegram to Abdullahi Issa, Prime Minister of the Somali Republic, congratulating the Somali Republic on its declaration of independence. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Abdullahi Issa, Prime Minister of the Somali Republic, Mogadishu, Somalia:

On the occasion of the declaration of independence of the Somali Republic, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to the government and people of the Somali Republic on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China. I wish the Somali people further achievements in their struggle to safeguard national independence and adhere to the path of independent national development. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

            Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                        June 30, 1960, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, today sent a telegram to Abdullahi Issa, Prime Minister of the Somali Republic, congratulating the Somali Republic on its declaration of independence, and informing him that the Chinese government has decided to recognize the Somali Republic. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Abdullahi Issa, Prime Minister of the Somali Republic, Mogadishu, Somalia:

I am delighted to learn of the declaration of independence of the Somali Republic. I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to the government of the Somali Republic on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China. I am honored to inform Your Excellency that the government of the People's Republic of China has decided to recognize the Somali Republic. I hope that my government's recognition of your country will contribute to the further development of friendly relations between China and Somalia.

              Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
                        June 30, 1960, Beijing

### 周总理陈外长电贺索马里亚共和国独立  我国决定承认索马里亚

1960-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈外长电贺索马里亚共和国独立
    我国决定承认索马里亚
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给索马里亚共和国总理阿卜杜拉希·伊萨，祝贺索马里亚共和国宣告独立。电文如下：索马里亚　摩加迪沙索马里亚共和国总理阿卜杜拉希·伊萨先生阁下：
    值此索马里亚共和国宣告独立的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向索马里亚共和国政府和人民致以衷心的祝贺。祝索马里亚人民在维护民族独立、坚持民族独立发展道路的斗争中取得进一步的成就，祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                        1960年6月30日于北京
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天打电报给索马里亚共和国总理阿卜杜拉希·伊萨，祝贺索马里亚共和国宣告独立，并且通知他，中国政府已经决定承认索马里亚共和国。电文如下：索马里亚　摩加迪沙索马里亚共和国总理阿卜杜拉希·伊萨先生阁下：
    欣闻索马里亚共和国宣告独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府向索马里亚共和国政府致以诚挚的祝贺。我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认索马里亚共和国。我希望，我国政府对贵国的承认将有助于中、索两国友好关系的进一步发展。
              中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
                        1960年6月30日于北京
