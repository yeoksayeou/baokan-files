Title: Cameroon Youth Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 4th: The Cameroon Youth Delegation, which visited our country at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation, concluded its visit and left Beijing by plane today to return home. Wang Zhaohua, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China and Standing Committee Member of the All-China Youth Federation, and others saw them off at the airport.

### 喀麦隆青年代表团回国

1960-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    喀麦隆青年代表团回国
    新华社4日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的喀麦隆青年代表团，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。中国共产主义青年团中央书记处书记，中华全国青年联合会常务委员王照华等人到机场欢送。
