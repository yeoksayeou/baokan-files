Title: Pant Utters Anti-China Rants Again, Continues to Smear China for "Invading" Indian Territory
Author:
Date: 1960-07-01
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 29th. According to reports from the Indian News Service, Indian Home Minister Pant, speaking at a gathering in Nainital, Uttar Pradesh, India on the 26th, once again smeared China for "invading" Indian territory.

Pant said, without any basis, that because China "invaded" some remote places in India, the previously peaceful and trouble-free border has become a source of anxiety.

Pant hysterically clamored, "We (India) will make all sacrifices until the last drop of blood is shed, we have the strength to protect our borders."

### 潘特又发反华叫嚣  继续诬蔑我“进攻”印度领土

1960-07-01
第5版()
专栏：

    潘特又发反华叫嚣
    继续诬蔑我“进攻”印度领土
    新华社29日讯　据印度新闻处报道，印度内政部长潘特26日在印度北方邦奈尼塔尔地方的一次集会上讲话时，再次诬蔑中国“进攻”印度的领土。
    潘特毫无根据地说，由于中国“进攻”了他所说的印度一些边远的地方，过去一向是和平的没有麻烦的边界已经成为焦虑的根源。
    潘特歇斯底里地叫嚷，“我们（印度）将作出一切牺牲直到流出最后一滴血，我们有力量保护我们的边界。”
