Title: Further Implement the Correct Line for the Development of Socialist Literature and Art in Our Country: The Third National Congress of Literary and Art Circles Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1960-07-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 21st: The Third National Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers is scheduled to open in the capital on the 22nd.

More than 2,300 representatives from various provinces, municipalities, autonomous regions, central government agencies, and all literary and art work posts of the People's Liberation Army will attend this conference.

This conference will assess the achievements of our country's literary and art work, summarize and exchange experiences, and propose tasks for future literary and art work, thereby further implementing the correct line for the development of socialist literature and art in our country. It will mobilize literary and art workers nationwide to unite more closely under the direction of serving the workers, peasants, and soldiers, and serving socialism, and under the policy of letting a hundred flowers bloom and a hundred schools of thought contend. It will also raise ideological levels and artistic skills, redouble efforts, launch socialist competitions among literary and art workers, strive for greater prosperity in socialist literature and art, and unite all progressive and revolutionary literary and art workers around the world to actively participate in the struggle against imperialism and revisionism.

This conference will also revise the Constitution of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and re-elect the leading bodies.

During the Third National Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers, the Chinese Writers Association, the Chinese Dramatists Association, The Chinese Musicians Association, The China Artists Association, The China Film Workers Association, The China Ballad Singers Association, The China Folk Literature Research Association, The China Dance Art Research Association, and The China Photographic Society will hold their respective member representative conferences or expanded board meetings.

### 进一步贯彻我国社会主义文学艺术发展的正确路线  全国第三次文代大会今日开幕

1960-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    进一步贯彻我国社会主义文学艺术发展的正确路线
    全国第三次文代大会今日开幕
    新华社21日讯　中国文学艺术工作者第三次代表大会定于22日在首都召开。
    出席这次大会的，有来自各省、市、自治区和中央直属机关、人民解放军各个文学艺术工作岗位的二千三百多名代表。
    这次大会，将估计我国文学艺术工作的成绩，总结和交流经验，并提出今后文学艺术工作的任务，从而进一步贯彻我国社会主义文学艺术发展的正确路线，动员全国文艺工作者，在为工农兵服务、为社会主义服务的方向和百花齐放、百家争鸣的方针下，更加紧密地团结起来，提高思想水平和艺术技巧，鼓足干劲，开展文艺工作者之间的社会主义竞赛，争取社会主义文学艺术的更大繁荣，并团结全世界一切进步和革命的文艺工作者，积极参加反对帝国主义、反对修正主义的斗争。
    这次大会还将修改中国文学艺术界联合会章程，改选领导机构。
    在中国文学艺术工作者第三次代表大会召开的期间，中国作家协会、中国戏剧家协会、中国音乐家协会、中国美术家协会、中国电影工作者联谊会、中国曲艺工作者协会、中国民间文艺研究会、中国舞蹈艺术研究会、中国摄影学会将分别召开会员代表大会或理事扩大会。
