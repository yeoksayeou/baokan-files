　　Little Knowledge
　　The cutworm is also known as the millet armyworm, also called the five-colored worm, cotton worm, armyworm, *zifang*, and branch-shaving worm. It is a major pest of Gramineae plants such as sorghum, corn, and wheat, and is distributed throughout the north and south of China.
　　The cutworm is one of the eleven diseases and insect pests that the National Agricultural Development Program stipulates should be basically eliminated within seven or twelve years.

### 夜盗虫

1960-07-18
第2版()
专栏：小常识

　　夜盗虫
    夜盗虫就是粟粘虫，也叫五色虫、绵虫、行军虫、虸蚄、剃枝虫，是高粱、玉米、小麦等禾本科植物的一大害虫，分布我国南北各地。
    夜盗虫是全国农业发展纲要中规定的，要分别在七年或者十二年内基本上消灭的十一种病虫害中的一种。
