Title: The Japanese Literary Delegation, Full of the Brotherly Friendship of the Chinese People, Departs Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-07-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd. The Japanese Literary Delegation, headed by Hiroshi Noma, and filled with the brotherly friendship of the Chinese people, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

Today, Chu Tunan, Mao Dun, Liao Chengzhi, Yang Hansheng, and others went to the airport to see them off. The head of the Japanese Literary Delegation, Hiroshi Noma, and members Katsuiro Kamei, Yoko Matsuoka, Minoru Takeuchi, Ken Kaiko, Kenzaburo Oe, Gotaro Shirato, etc., shook hands cordially with their Chinese hosts to say goodbye. Cultural figures from the capital presented flowers to the Japanese friends.

Those who also came to the airport to see them off included Lao She, Shao Quanlin, Mei Lanfang, Ma Shaobo, Zhang Geng, Zha Fuxi, Zhao Shuli, Ye Qianyu, Zhou Peiyuan, Xiao San, Zhou Erfu, Xie Bingxin, Yang Shuo and more than two hundred other well-known figures from the cultural circles of the capital.

Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji and Korean peace activist Hwang Bong-koo, who were in Beijing, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 满载中国人民兄弟般的友谊  日本文学家代表团离京回国

1960-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    满载中国人民兄弟般的友谊
    日本文学家代表团离京回国
    新华社3日讯　以野间宏为首的日本文学家代表团满载中国人民的兄弟般的友谊，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    今天，楚图南、茅盾、廖承志、阳翰笙等人到机场送行。日本文学家代表团团长野间宏，团员龟井胜一郎、松冈洋子、竹内实、开高健、大江健三郎、白土吾夫等同中国主人亲切地握手告别。首都文化界人士向日本朋友献了鲜花。
    到机场送行的还有老舍、邵荃麟、梅兰芳、马少波、张庚、查阜西、赵树理、叶浅予、周培源、萧三、周而复、谢冰心、杨朔等首都文化界著名人士二百多人。
    在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一、朝鲜和平人士黄凤九也到机场送行。
