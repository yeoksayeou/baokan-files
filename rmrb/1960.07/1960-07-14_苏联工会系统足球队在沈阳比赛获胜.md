Title: Soviet Trade Union System Football Team Wins in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1960-07-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, July 13th: The Soviet Trade Union system football team won their third match of their visit to China in Shenyang on the 13th, defeating the Liaoning Workers' football team with a score of 2-1.

    The first half ended in a 1-1 draw.

    The Soviet Trade Union system football team had a friendly match with the Dalian Chemical Company football team in Lushun-Dalian on July 9th, winning 1-0. They will leave Shenyang for Changchun on the 14th, and are scheduled to have a friendly match with the First Automobile Works football team on the 15th.

### 苏联工会系统足球队在沈阳比赛获胜

1960-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联工会系统足球队在沈阳比赛获胜
    据新华社沈阳13日电　苏联工会系统的足球队13日在沈阳举行的访华第三场比赛中，以二比一赢了辽宁工人足球队。
    上半场双方以一比一踢成平局。
    苏联工会系统的足球队7月9日曾经在旅大同旅大化工公司足球队举行友谊比赛，以一比零获胜。他们14日将离开沈阳去长春访问，预定15日同第一汽车制造厂足球队举行友谊比赛。
