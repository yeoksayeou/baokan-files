Title: International Schumann Singing and String Quartet Competition Opens
Author:
Date: 1960-07-19
Page: 6

　  International Schumann Singing and String Quartet Competition Opens
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 18th. The Second International Schumann Singing and String Quartet Competition grandly opened on the evening of July 17th at the German State Opera in Berlin.

    This competition will be held from the 18th to the 26th. More than seventy young musicians from thirteen countries including the Soviet Union, China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Romania, France, Sweden, and Finland are participating in the competition. China's participants in the string quartet competition are Yu Lina, Ding Zhinuo, Wu Feifei, and Lin Yingrong. Participants in the singing competition are mezzo-soprano Hu Yiwen, baritone Li Xinchang, and bass Li Guanglun. Yu Yixuan, Secretariat of the Chinese Musicians Association and Professor and Head of the Vocal Music Department of the Central Conservatory of Music, serves as a member of the singing selection committee.

### 国际舒曼歌唱弦乐四重奏比赛开幕

1960-07-19
第6版()
专栏：

　  国际舒曼歌唱弦乐四重奏比赛开幕
    据新华社柏林18日电　第二届国际舒曼歌唱、弦乐四重奏比赛17日晚在柏林德国国家歌剧院隆重开幕。
    这次比赛将从18日举行到26日。参加比赛的有来自苏联、中国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、法国、瑞典、芬兰等十三个国家七十多名青年音乐家。中国参加弦乐四重奏比赛的是俞丽拿、丁芷诺、吴菲菲、林应荣。参加歌唱比赛的有女中音胡逸文、男中音黎信昌和男低音李光纶。中国音乐家协会书记处书记、中央音乐学院声乐系教授兼系主任喻宜萱担任歌唱评选委员会委员。
