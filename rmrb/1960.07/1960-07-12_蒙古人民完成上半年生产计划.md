Title: Mongolian People Fulfill First Half-Year Production Plan
Author:
Date: 1960-07-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 10th: According to today's "Unen" newspaper report in Mongolia: The Mongolian people have fulfilled the first half-year plan for agriculture, animal husbandry, industry, and other sectors of the national economy.

According to a communique issued by the Mongolian State Central Statistical Bureau on the implementation of the national economic and cultural plan for the first half of 1960, in terms of agricultural and animal husbandry production, a total of 6,833,000 young animals of various kinds were delivered in the first half of this year, with a survival rate of 90.2%. State-owned farms and agricultural and animal husbandry production cooperatives successfully completed the spring sowing plan, with the total cultivated area reaching 252,400 hectares nationwide, an increase of 89,000 hectares compared to last year. Among them, state-owned farms increased by 53%, and agricultural and animal husbandry cooperatives increased by 61.7%. Agricultural and animal husbandry operations obtained 24.4% more tractors than last year.

The communique pointed out that in the first half of this year, the state's short-term and long-term loans to cooperatives increased by 36.9% compared to the same period last year.

The communique said that the total industrial output value in the first half of this year increased by 21.2% compared to the same period last year. Among them, state-owned industry increased by 22%, and cooperative industry increased by 18.4%. Electricity, petroleum, leather shoes, soap, and other products all overfulfilled their production plans.

The communique pointed out that the total investment in various sectors of the national economy in the first half of this year increased by 37.4% compared to the same period last year. The volume of freight and passenger transport in the first half of the year both overfulfilled the plan.

In terms of cultural and educational undertakings, the number of graduates from higher education institutions this year increased by 65.2% compared to last year, and the number of graduates from primary and secondary schools increased by 8.9%. The total retail sales of goods in the first half of this year reached 106% of the plan. Foreign trade is expanding, and the import and export plans for the first half of the year have been overfulfilled. (Attached picture)

### 蒙古人民完成上半年生产计划

1960-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民完成上半年生产计划
    新华社乌兰巴托10日电　据蒙古“真理报”今天报道：蒙古人民已经完成了农牧业、工业和国民经济其他部门的今年上半年计划。
    据蒙古国家中央统计局发表的1960年上半年国民经济和文化计划执行情况的公报说，在农牧业生产方面，今年上半年各种牲畜共接产幼畜六百八十三万三千多头，成活率达90.2％。国营农牧场和农牧业生产合作社胜利完成了春耕播种计划，全国耕地总面积达二十五万二千四百公顷，比去年增加八万九千公顷。其中国营农牧场增加53％，农牧业合作社增加61.7％。农牧业获得的拖拉机比去年增多了24.4％。
    公报指出，今年上半年国家给合作社的长、短期贷款比去年同期增加了36.9％。
    公报说，今年上半年工业总产值比去年同期增长21.2％。其中国营工业增长22％，合作社工业增长18.4％。电力、石油、皮鞋、肥皂等都超额完成了生产计划。
    公报指出，今年上半年国民经济各部门的总投资额比去年同期增加了37.4％。上半年的货运量和客运量都超额完成了计划。
    在文教事业方面，今年高等学校的毕业生比去年增加65.2％，中、小学毕业生增加8.9％。今年上半年的商品零售额完成计划的106％。对外贸易正在扩大，上半年的进出口计划已超额完成。（附图片）
