Title: Chairman Mao Receives Representatives to the Literary and Art Workers Congress (Photos)
Author: Lü Houmin, Liu Dongao
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 2

    Chairman Mao Receives Representatives to the Literary and Art Workers Congress
    On July 23, Chairman Mao Zedong, along with other Party and state leaders Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping, and others, received all the representatives attending the Third Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers.
    Left 1: Chairman Mao Zedong shaking hands with representatives.
    Left 2: Chairman Mao Zedong shaking hands with Wang Weilin, a 119-year-old representative from the drama circle. Xinhua News Agency reporter Lü Houmin photo
    Premier Zhou and others receive African guests, namely Theodore Benkira, member of the National Steering Committee and Second Deputy Secretary-General of the African Solidarity Party of Congo; Diallo Mossa Sane, Secretary-General of the Sudanese Peace Movement of the Mali Federation; the Sudanese student delegation headed by Ahmed Abdul Majid; and the Sierra Leonean student delegation headed by A.B. Ilahe; Sudanese peace activist Ahmed Mohammed Keir and his wife. Xinhua News Agency reporter Lü Houmin photo
    On July 23, Premier Zhou Enlai received Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of Burma on the China-Burma Joint Boundary Committee.
        Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dongao photo
