Title: Declaration on the German Question by Representatives of Germany's Neighboring Countries Participating in the World Peace Council Standing Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-07-15
Page: 6

Representatives of the peace movements of Germany's surrounding countries – Austria, Belgium, Denmark, France, the Netherlands, Poland, and Czechoslovakia – met in Stockholm on July 10, 1960, as the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council convened.

The representatives unanimously noted the extremely serious danger posed by the rearmament of the Federal Republic of Germany to European and world peace. The Federal Republic of Germany is demanding territorial changes, reinstating former Hitler generals as heads of the Bundeswehr, supporting various revanchist manifestations, and hindering the normal development of peace-loving movements and persecuting German peace fighters.

To cope with this rapidly growing danger, the vestiges of war should be eliminated as soon as possible, which means concluding peace treaties with the two existing Germanys. This treaty should prevent the re-militarization of the Federal Republic of Germany, affirm Germany's current borders, enable the resolution of the abnormal and dangerous situation in West Berlin, and create conditions for the peaceful development of Europe.

The atomic armament of the Federal Republic of Germany must be prevented at all costs: the establishment of rockets and launch pads is a threat to European peace at all times. It should be made impossible to establish German bases in Western European countries. The actions and vigilance of the people of Europe must not be relaxed for a moment. We are in complete solidarity with all democratic forces in the Federal Republic of Germany who are fighting against militarism and for peace. We believe that German disarmament is a decisive step towards achieving universal disarmament and world peace.

				Stockholm
					July 10, 1960

### 参加世界和平理事会常委会的德国邻国代表  关于德国问题的宣言

1960-07-15
第6版()
专栏：

　　参加世界和平理事会常委会的德国邻国代表
　  关于德国问题的宣言
    德国周围各国——奥地利、比利时、丹麦、法国、荷兰、波兰和捷克斯洛伐克——的和平运动的代表们，在世界和平理事会常务委员会开会之际，于1960年7月10日在斯德哥尔摩举行会议。
    代表们一致注意到德意志联邦共和国的重新武装使欧洲和平和世界和平遭到的极端严重的危险。德意志联邦共和国正在要求改变领土，重新让过去的希特勒将军们担任联邦国防军的首脑，并支持种种复仇主义表现，同时它还妨碍拥护和平的运动的正常发展和迫害德国的和平战士。
    为了应付这种迅速增长的危险，应该从速消除战争遗迹，这就是要同目前两个德国缔结和约。这个和约应阻止德意志联邦共和国的重新军国主义化，肯定德国现行的边界，使得能够解决西柏林的不正常的危险局势，并创造欧洲和平发展的条件。
    应该不惜一切代价来阻止德意志联邦共和国的原子武装：设立火箭和发射台在任何时候都是欧洲和平的威胁。应该使得在西欧各国中设立德国基地成为不可能。欧洲各国人民的行动和警惕不能有一刻的放松。我们同德意志联邦共和国中那些为反对军国主义和争取和平而斗争的一切民主力量是完全团结一致的。我们认为，德国裁军是实现普遍裁军和世界和平的决定性的一步。
                                        斯德哥尔摩
                                            1960年7月10日
