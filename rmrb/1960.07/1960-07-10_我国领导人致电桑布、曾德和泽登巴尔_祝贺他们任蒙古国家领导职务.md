Title: Our Country's Leaders Send Telegrams to Sambuu, Tseden and Tsedenbal to Congratulate Them on Assuming Leadership Positions in Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-07-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 9th
Our country's leaders Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, and Zhou Enlai sent telegrams on July 9th to Sambuu, Tseden, and Tsedenbal to congratulate them on assuming national leadership positions in the Mongolian People's Republic. The full text of the congratulatory telegrams is as follows:

To Comrade Zh. Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Comrade L. Tsende, Chairman of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Comrade Yu. Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic:

We are delighted to learn of your honorable election as Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Chairman of the Great People's Hural, and Chairman of the Council of Ministers. On behalf of the Chinese people and the government of the People's Republic of China, we extend to you our warm congratulations.

We wish you even greater achievements in the lofty cause of building socialism, strengthening the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and defending peace in Asia and the world.

We wish further consolidation and development of the profound fraternal friendship between the people of China and Mongolia.

　　　　Chairman of the People's Republic of China　Liu Shaoqi
　　　　Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China　Zhu De
　　　　Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China　Zhou Enlai
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 9, 1960

### 我国领导人致电桑布、曾德和泽登巴尔  祝贺他们任蒙古国家领导职务

1960-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我国领导人致电桑布、曾德和泽登巴尔
    祝贺他们任蒙古国家领导职务
    新华社9日讯　我国国家领导人刘少奇、朱德和周恩来在7月9日致电桑布、曾德和泽登巴尔，祝贺他们担任蒙古人民共和国国家领导职务。贺电全文如下：蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席扎·桑布同志，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席鲁·曾德同志，蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席尤·泽登巴尔同志：
    欣悉你们光荣地当选蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席、大人民呼拉尔主席和部长会议主席，我们谨代表中国人民和中华人民共和国政府向你们表示热烈的祝贺。
    祝你们在建设社会主义、加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结和保卫亚洲和世界和平的崇高事业中取得更大的成就。
    祝中蒙两国人民间兄弟般的深厚友谊进一步巩固和发展。
      中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
      中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱　德
      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                    一九六零年七月九日
