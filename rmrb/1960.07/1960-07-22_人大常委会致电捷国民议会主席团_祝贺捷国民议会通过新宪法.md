Title: NPC Standing Committee Sends Telegram to the Presidium of the Czechoslovak National Assembly to Congratulate on the Adoption of the New Constitution by the Czechoslovak National Assembly
Author:
Date: 1960-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic, congratulating them on the adoption of the new constitution. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic:

We are delighted to learn that the Czechoslovak National Assembly passed the new Constitution of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic on the 11th of this month. We express our heartfelt congratulations to the Czechoslovak people on this historically significant victory.

The new constitution not only affirms the great achievements made by the Czechoslovak people under the leadership of the Czechoslovak Communist Party in the fifteen years since liberation, but also points out the direction of future development for socialist Czechoslovakia.

We wish the Czechoslovak people even greater achievements in the cause of building socialism and defending world peace!

                 The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
           　July 18, 1960

### 人大常委会致电捷国民议会主席团  祝贺捷国民议会通过新宪法

1960-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会致电捷国民议会主席团
    祝贺捷国民议会通过新宪法
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会，打电报给捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国国民议会主席团，祝贺通过新宪法。电报全文如下：捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国国民议会主席团：
    欣悉捷克斯洛伐克国民议会于本月11日通过了捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国新宪法。我们对于捷克斯洛伐克人民所取得的这一具有历史意义的胜利表示衷心的祝贺。
    新宪法不仅肯定了解放十五年来捷克斯洛伐克人民在捷克斯洛伐克共产党领导下所取得的伟大成就，而且指出了社会主义的捷克斯洛伐克今后发展的方向。
    我们预祝捷克斯洛伐克人民在建设社会主义和保卫世界和平的事业中取得更大的成就！
                 中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会
           　1960年7月18日
