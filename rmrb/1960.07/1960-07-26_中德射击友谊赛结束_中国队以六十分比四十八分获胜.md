Title: China-Germany Shooting Friendship Competition Concludes: Chinese Team Wins 60 to 48
Author:
Date: 1960-07-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 24th: The friendship competition between the Chinese National Defense Sports Association Shooting Team and the Core Shooting Team of the German Democratic Republic Sports and Technology Association, held in Leipzig, concluded on the 23rd. The Chinese team won with a score of 60 to 48.

### 中德射击友谊赛结束  中国队以六十分比四十八分获胜

1960-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    中德射击友谊赛结束
    中国队以六十分比四十八分获胜
    据新华社柏林24日电　中国国防体育协会射击队同德意志民主共和国体育和技术协会核心射击队在来比锡进行的友谊比赛，已经在23日全部结束。中国队以六十分比四十八分获胜。
