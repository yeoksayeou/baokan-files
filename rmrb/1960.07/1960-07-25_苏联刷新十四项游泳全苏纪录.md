Title: Soviet Union Breaks Fourteen National Swimming Records
Author:
Date: 1960-07-25
Page: 6
Brothers countries sports activities

    Soviet Union Breaks Fourteen National Swimming Records
    The 24th Soviet Swimming Championships, with 700 athletes participating, concluded in Moscow on the 10th. During the five-day championships, Soviet athletes broke a total of fourteen national swimming records, including five national records for 15-16 year old junior swimmers.

    Eighteen-year-old Estonian backstroke swimmer, Siimar, completed the 100 meters in 1 minute and 3.3 seconds, shortening the 1958 national record by 0.6 seconds. This is the best result in Europe so far this year.

    Nineteen-year-old Moscow athlete Kuzmin broke the national record for the men's 200-meter butterfly twice in the preliminary and final rounds, with times of 2 minutes 20.9 seconds and 2 minutes 18.1 seconds, respectively. This is the fourth best result in the world this year.

    The creator of the women's 100-meter backstroke national record, Viktorova, set a new national record in this competition: 1 minute 11.7 seconds, which is 0.5 seconds faster than her own record and the fifth best time in the world.

### 苏联刷新十四项游泳全苏纪录

1960-07-25
第6版()
专栏：兄弟国家的体育运动

    苏联刷新十四项游泳全苏纪录
    有七百名运动员参加的第二十四届苏联游泳锦标赛10日在莫斯科结束。在这次历时五天的锦标赛中，苏联运动员共打破了十四个全苏游泳纪录，其中包括五个十五到十六岁少年游泳的全苏纪录。
    十八岁的爱沙尼亚仰泳运动员西马尔以一分三点三秒的时间游完了一百米，比1958年的全苏纪录缩短了零点六秒。这是今年到现在为止欧洲最优异的成绩。
    十九岁的莫斯科运动员库兹明在预赛和决赛中，先后以二分二十点九秒和二分一十八点一秒两次打破了男子二百米蝶泳全苏纪录。这是今年世界第四个优异的成绩。
    女子百米仰泳全苏纪录创造者维克托罗娃在这次比赛中又创造了新的全苏纪录：一分一十一点七秒，这比她自己保持的纪录缩短了零点五秒，这是世界上第五个最好的成绩。
