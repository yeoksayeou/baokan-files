Title: Polish Painter Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-18
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: Polish painter Winiecki Raczewicz arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. He is in China to assist in the preparation of the Polish People's Republic Print Exhibition and to visit, according to the 1960-1961 executive plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 波兰一画家到京

1960-07-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　波兰一画家到京
    新华社17日讯　波兰画家维尼茨基·拉捷维奇，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。他是根据中波文化合作协定1960—1961年执行计划来华协助筹办波兰人民共和国版画展览会并进行访问的。
