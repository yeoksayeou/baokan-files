Title: Delegation of the Japan National Metal Mine Workers' Union Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd -- The delegation of the Japan National Metal Mine Workers' Union, invited by the National Committee of the Chinese Heavy Industry Trade Union to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation of ten people was led by Sawada Seiji, Vice Chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the All Japan Metal Mine Workers' Union. Zhao Guoqiang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Heavy Industry Trade Union, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 日本全国金属矿山工会代表团抵京

1960-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    日本全国金属矿山工会代表团抵京
    新华社22日讯　应中国重工业工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的日本全国金属矿山工会联合会访华代表团一行十人，在全日本金属矿山工会联合会中央执行委员会副委员长泽田政治率领下，于今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国重工业工会全国委员会主席赵国强等。
