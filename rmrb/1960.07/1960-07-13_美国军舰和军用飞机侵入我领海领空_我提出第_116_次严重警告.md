Title: U.S. Warships and Military Aircraft Invade Our Territorial Waters and Airspace; We Issue the 116th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-07-13
Page: 1

　　U.S. Warships and Military Aircraft Invade Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
　　We Issue the 116th Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, July 12th: From 3:00 to 5:35 on July 12th, two U.S. warships intruded into the sea area south of Pinghai, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 5:00 to 9:21, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area from Baiquan Islands to Dongyin area, Fujian Province.
    On the same day, from 11:07 to 11:23, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace south of Shanwei and Pinghai, Guangdong Province. Also on the same day, from 12:10 to 12:16, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Shidao and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province.
    Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft and warships, the spokesman of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 116th serious warning.

### 美国军舰和军用飞机侵入我领海领空　我提出第116次严重警告

1960-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　美国军舰和军用飞机侵入我领海领空
　　我提出第116次严重警告
    新华社12日讯　7月12日三时至五时三十五分，美国军舰两艘，侵入我广东省平海以南地区海域。同日五时至九时二十一分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省白犬列岛至东引地区海域。
    同日十一时七分至十一时二十三分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省汕尾、平海以南地区领空。又同日十二时十分至十二时十六分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的石岛、永兴岛上空。
    对于美国军用飞机和军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百一十六次严重警告。
