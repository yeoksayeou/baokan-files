Title: Factories in Quanzhou, Fujian Province, Vigorously Produce Farm Tools and Chemical Fertilizers to Support Agriculture
Author:
Date: 1960-07-28
Page: 2
　
    Fuzhou, Fujian (By Our Newspaper) - In the past six months, one hundred and twenty factories in Quanzhou City, Fujian Province, have successively manufactured more than 430,000 pieces of large and small farm tools and machinery for rural people's communes, including rice transplanters, harvesters, threshers, irrigation and drainage machinery, and simple lathes. They have also produced more than 1.92 million jin of chemical fertilizers and trained more than 800 technical personnel for the communes.

### 福建泉州各工厂  大力生产农具化肥支援农业

1960-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

    福建泉州各工厂
    大力生产农具化肥支援农业
    本报讯　福建省泉州市一百二十个工厂，半年来先后为农村人民公社制造了插秧机、收割机、打谷机和排灌机械、简易车床等大小农具和机械四十三万多部（件），化学肥料一百九十二万多斤，并为公社培养训练了八百多名技术人材。
