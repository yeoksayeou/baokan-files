Title: Japan's Ruling Party Elects Ikeda as New President
Author:
Date: 1960-07-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 14th: Japan's ruling Liberal Democratic Party held an extraordinary convention today and elected Hayato Ikeda, the Minister of International Trade and Industry in the Nobusuke Kishi cabinet, to succeed Nobusuke Kishi as president of the Liberal Democratic Party. According to Liberal Democratic Party practice, when it is in power, its president serves as prime minister and organizes the cabinet.

After being elected president, Ikeda told reporters that restoring American "trust" in Japan is "the most necessary" thing for Japan at present. He also said, "I hope to prevent being eroded by communist countries."

The ruling party's extraordinary convention was held this morning. Nobusuke Kishi, who was forced to decide to resign as prime minister, announced at the meeting that he was resigning from the presidency of the Liberal Democratic Party.

### 日本执政党选出池田为新总裁

1960-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　日本执政党选出池田为新总裁
    据新华社14日讯　东京消息：日本执政的自由民主党今天举行临时大会，选出岸信介内阁的通商产业相池田勇人接替岸信介，担任自由民主党的总裁。按照自由民主党惯例，在它执政时由它的总裁担任首相，组织内阁。
    池田在当选总裁后对新闻记者发表谈话说，目前对日本来说，恢复美国对日本的“信任”是“最必要的”。他还说，“希望做到不要被共产主义国家侵蚀”。
    执政党的这次临时大会是今天上午召开的。被迫决定辞去首相职务的岸信介在会上宣布，他辞去自由民主党总裁的职务。
