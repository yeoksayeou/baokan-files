Title: Foreign Minister Chen Yi Replies to Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong: The U.S. Imperialists Must Be Stopped from Undermining the Geneva Accords. The Chinese Government Fully Agrees with the Views and Demands Expressed in Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong's Letter to the Two Chairmen of the Geneva Conference.
Author:
Date: 1960-07-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th - Foreign Minister Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China wrote a letter on July 27th in reply to Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's letter of July 16th, expressing complete agreement with the views and demands expressed in Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong's letter to the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference (which this agency published on July 19th). In this letter, Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong requested the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference to take appropriate measures to stop and end all interference by U.S. imperialism in South Vietnam, and also demanded that the South Vietnamese authorities cease their suppression and persecution of former resistance members, and strictly implement the Geneva Accords.

The full text of Foreign Minister Chen Yi's reply to Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong is as follows:

Respected Comrade Minister Pham Van Dong, Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

    I have received your letter of July 16, 1960, and the attached copy of your letter of the same date to the two chairmen of the 1954 Geneva Conference.

    The Government of the People's Republic of China fully agrees with the views and demands expressed in your letter to the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference. It believes that the parties concerned need to consider taking appropriate measures to stop U.S. imperialism's constant undermining of the Geneva Accords on Indochina and its crude interference in South Vietnam, in order to safeguard the Geneva Accords and peace in Indochina.

    Please accept my highest respects.

    Chen Yi
    Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
    July 27, 1960, Beijing

Attached: Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong's letter to Foreign Minister Chen Yi:

Respected Comrade Minister Chen Yi, Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China:

    Thank you for your letter of July 11, 1960.

    I now have the honor to send you a copy of my letter of July 16, 1960, to the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference, for the sixth anniversary of the implementation of the Geneva Accords on Vietnam.

    The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam hopes that, for the sake of peace in Vietnam, Indochina, and Southeast Asia, the above-mentioned letter will receive the support of the Government of the People's Republic of China, which attended the 1954 Geneva Conference on Indochina and made significant contributions to the success of that conference.

    I take this opportunity to extend my highest respects to you, Comrade Minister.

    Pham Van Dong
    Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
    July 16, 1960, Hanoi

### 陈毅外长函复范文同外长  必须制止美帝破坏日内瓦协议  中国政府完全同意范文同外长给日内瓦会议两位主席的信中所表明的观点和要求

1960-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅外长函复范文同外长
    必须制止美帝破坏日内瓦协议
    中国政府完全同意范文同外长给日内瓦会议两位主席的信中所表明的观点和要求
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅在27日写信答复越南民主共和国外交部长范文同7月16日的来信，表示完全同意范文同外交部长给日内瓦会议两位主席的信（本社已在7月19日发表）中所表明的观点和要求。范文同外交部长在这封信中，要求日内瓦会议两位主席采取适当的措施来制止和结束美帝国主义对越南南方的一切干涉，同时要求越南南方当局停止对前抗战人员的镇压和迫害，并且严正地履行日内瓦协议。
  陈毅外交部长给范文同外交部长的复信全文如下：越南民主共和国外交部长范文同同志敬爱的部长同志：
    我收到了你1960年7月16日的来信和所附你在同一天给1954年日内瓦会议两位主席的信的抄件。
    中华人民共和国政府完全同意你给日内瓦会议两位主席信中所表明的观点和要求。并且认为，有关方面有必要考虑采取适当的措施来制止美帝国主义对关于印度支那问题日内瓦协议的不断破坏和对越南南方的粗暴干涉，以维护日内瓦协议和印度支那的和平。
    顺致最崇高的敬意。
              中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
                          1960年7月27日于北京
      附范文同外交部长给陈毅外交部长的信：中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅同志敬爱的部长同志：
    感谢你1960年7月11日的来信。
    现在，我谨将我在1960年7月16日为越南日内瓦协议实施六周年给日内瓦会议两位主席的信的抄件送给你。
    越南民主共和国政府希望，为了越南、印度支那和东南亚的和平利益，上述信件将会得到曾经出席1954年关于印度支那问题的日内瓦会议，并为这个会议的成功作出了重大贡献的中华人民共和国政府的支持。
    趁此机会，谨向部长同志致崇高的敬意。
              越南民主共和国外交部长　范文同
                          1960年7月16日于河内
