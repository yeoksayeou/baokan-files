Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet for Bolivian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-07-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, July 21st—Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, held a banquet today to welcome the Bolivian Senate delegation led by Oscar Donoso López, First Vice President of the Bolivian Senate, and Congressman Jaime Arellano Casaneda.

Prior to the banquet, Chu Tunan met with Oscar Donoso López and all members of the delegation, and had a cordial conversation. After the banquet, the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association held a film reception to entertain the Bolivian guests.

### 楚图南欢宴玻利维亚客人

1960-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    楚图南欢宴玻利维亚客人
    新华社21日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南今天举行宴会，欢迎玻利维亚参议院第一副议长奥斯卡·多诺索·洛佩斯率领的玻利维亚参议院代表团和众议员海梅·阿雷利亚诺·卡萨内达。
    在宴会前，楚图南会见了奥斯卡·多诺索·洛佩斯和代表团全体人员，进行了亲切的谈话。宴会后，中国拉丁美洲友好协会举行电影招待会招待玻利维亚客人。
