Title: Cuban Mrs. Hilda Valdés Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-25
Page: 6
　
Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: Cuban Mrs. Hilda Valdés arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China for a visit to our country.

　　Shen Yuanhui, Executive Committee member of the All-China Women's Federation, and others welcomed her at the airport.

### 古巴希尔达·瓦尔台斯夫人到京

1960-07-25
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴希尔达·瓦尔台斯夫人到京
    新华社24日讯　应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参观访问的古巴希尔达·瓦尔台斯夫人今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有全国妇联执行委员沈元辉等。
