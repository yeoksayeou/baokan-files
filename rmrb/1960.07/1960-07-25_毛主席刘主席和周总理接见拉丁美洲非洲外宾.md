Title: Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou Meet with Latin American and African Guests

Author:

Date: 1960-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with foreign guests from Latin America and Africa in Beijing and had cordial conversations with them.

These foreign guests are: the Bolivian Senate Delegation headed by Oscar Donoso Lopez, Bolivian trade union representative Torres, Chilean peace activist Venturieri and his wife, the Chilean youth delegation headed by Li Yaomin, Theodore Benkila, member of the National Steering Committee and Second Deputy Secretary-General of the Congolese African Solidarity Party, Diallo Mossa Sane, Secretary-General of the Sudanese Peace Movement of the Mali Federation, members of the Zanzibar Nationalist Party delegation headed by Miratz Sherab Abdul, and the Sudanese student delegation headed by Ahmed Abdul Majid.

Also present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Chu Tunan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, Liu Changsheng, Chairman of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association and Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Hu Yuzhi, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 毛主席刘主席和周总理接见拉丁美洲非洲外宾

1960-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席刘主席和周总理接见拉丁美洲非洲外宾
    新华社24日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席和周恩来总理，今天下午接见了在北京的拉丁美洲和非洲的各国外宾，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    这些外宾是：以奥斯卡·多诺索·洛佩斯为首的玻利维亚参议院代表团，玻利维亚工会代表托莱斯，智利和平人士万徒勒里和他的夫人，以李耀民为首的智利青年代表团，刚果非洲团结党全国指导委员会委员、第二副总书记西奥多·本基拉，马里联邦苏丹和平运动秘书长迪亚洛·摩沙·萨内，以米拉茨·谢拉布·阿卜杜勒为首的桑给巴尔民族主义党代表团团员，以艾哈迈德·阿卜杜勒·马吉德为首的苏丹学生代表团等。
    接见时在场的，有中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南，中国非洲人民友好协会会长、中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜，共青团中央第一书记胡耀邦，中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥等。
