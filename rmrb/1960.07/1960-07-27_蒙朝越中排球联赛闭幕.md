Title: Mongolia-North Korea-Vietnam-China Volleyball League Concludes
Author:
Date: 1960-07-27
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 26th: The four-day Third Mongolia, North Korea, Vietnam, and China Volleyball League concluded here today.

    The Chinese men's and women's teams won all three matches and won the men's and women's championships respectively.

    The North Korean men's and women's teams won two of three matches, winning the men's and women's runners-up. The third place went to the Mongolian men's and women's teams, with one win in three matches. The Vietnamese men's and women's teams were fourth.

### 蒙朝越中排球联赛闭幕

1960-07-27
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙朝越中排球联赛闭幕
    据新华社乌兰巴托26日电　为期四天的第三届蒙古、朝鲜、越南、中国四国排球联赛，今天在这里闭幕。
    中国男、女队三战三捷，分别获得男子冠军和女子冠军。
    朝鲜男、女队三战两胜，获得男子亚军和女子亚军。第三名是蒙古男、女队，三战一胜。越南男、女队是第四名。
