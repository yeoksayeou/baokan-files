Title: Our Women Parachutists Break Another World Record
Author:
Date: 1960-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 26th. Our women parachutists broke another world record on July 21st in the women's 600-meter daytime team accuracy landing event.

On this day, during a parachute test in Hefei, Anhui, Anhui Province female parachutists Du Jufang, Li Wenxiu, and Wu Yonglan created a new record of an average distance of 5.546 meters from the target center in this event, not only breaking the world record of 12.39 meters created by our female athletes in 1958 again, but also shortening the new record of 8.73 meters average distance from the target center created by Wu Yonglan, Tang Hexiu, and Rong Zhixuan on July 4th this year by more than three meters. The individual distances of the three of them from the target center were: Du, 1.89 meters; Li, 6.75 meters; and Wu, 8 meters.

### 我国女跳伞运动员又破一项世界纪录

1960-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    我国女跳伞运动员又破一项世界纪录
    据新华社26日讯　我国女跳伞运动员7月21日又一次打破女子六百米日间集体综合跳伞世界纪录。
    这一天在安徽合肥举行的一次跳伞测验中，安徽省女子跳伞运动员杜菊芳、李文秀、武永兰在这个项目中创造了平均距靶心五点五四六米的最新成绩，不仅再次打破了由我国女运动员在1958年创造的十二点三九米的世界纪录，而且比武永兰、唐荷秀、荣志玄在今年7月4日创造的平均距靶心八点七三米的新成绩，又缩短三米多。她们三人个人与靶心的距离：杜是一点八九米、李是六点七五米、武是八米。
