Title: UAR Announces Severance of Relations with Iran
Author:
Date: 1960-07-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 26th: The Foreign Ministry of the United Arab Republic summoned the Iranian Ambassador to the UAR, Jamshid Qarib, on the evening of July 26th, informing him that diplomatic relations between the two countries had been severed and requesting him and the embassy staff to leave the UAR.

President Nasser announced in a speech in Alexandria on the afternoon of July 26th that the UAR would close its embassy in Tehran because Iran had recognized Israel. Iran announced its recognition of Israel on July 23rd.

### 阿联宣布同伊朗绝交

1960-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联宣布同伊朗绝交　　
    据新华社开罗26日电　阿拉伯联合共和国外交部7月26日晚召见了伊朗驻阿联大使贾姆希德·加里卜，通知他两国之间的外交关系已经断绝，并要求他和大使馆人员离开阿联。
　　纳赛尔总统26日下午在亚历山大发表演说时宣布，由于伊朗承认了以色列，阿联将关闭它驻德黑兰的大使馆。伊朗是在7月23日宣布它承认以色列的。
