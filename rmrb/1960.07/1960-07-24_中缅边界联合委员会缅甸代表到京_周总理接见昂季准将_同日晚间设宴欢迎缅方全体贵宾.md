Title: The Burmese Delegation of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee Arrives in Beijing; Premier Zhou Receives Brigadier General Aung Gyi; Evening Reception Held for All Burmese Guests

Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd. Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief Burmese representative to the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee's second meeting, along with fifteen representatives, advisors, and accompanying personnel, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou this afternoon. Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Vice Foreign Minister Geng Biao, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Zhang Aiping, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang, Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Shen Ping, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Zhang Youyu, and Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee Xin Yi, among others. Yao Zhongming, the chief Chinese representative to the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, along with some representatives and advisors, were also present at the airport to welcome them. The Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyao Winn, a Burmese representative to the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, and diplomatic officials from the Burmese Embassy in China, also went to the airport to greet them. Brigadier General Aung Gyi and the other distinguished Burmese guests received flowers from Beijing Young Pioneers upon disembarking the plane.

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd. Premier Zhou Enlai received Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief Burmese representative to the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, this afternoon and held a cordial and friendly conversation. Also present at the meeting were Burmese representatives: Burmese Ambassador to China Kyao Winn, Secretary of the Kachin State Affairs Department U San Lun, Director of the Border Areas Administration Department Colonel So Min, Commissioner of the North Shan State U Khun Ohn Kya, as well as advisors and accompanying staff. Also present at the meeting were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Foreign Ministers Zhang Hanfu and Geng Biao, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Zhang Aiping, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang, Chief Chinese Representative to the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee Yao Zhongming, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Peiwen, and Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Department Shen Ping, among others.

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd. Premier Zhou Enlai held a banquet in the Great Hall of the People this evening to warmly welcome Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief Burmese representative to the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, and the representatives, advisors, and accompanying staff.

Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the banquet.

Premier Zhou spoke first at the banquet, extending a warm welcome to Brigadier General Aung Gyi and all the distinguished Burmese guests. He said, we believe that the second meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee will be a success with the joint efforts of both sides. He said that Beijing is now in the height of summer, and we are welcoming the distinguished guests with warm feelings like the hot weather, because we are relatives. Brigadier General Aung Gyi spoke at the banquet, saying: "We guarantee that through the cooperation of both sides of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, we will sign the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty as quickly as possible, setting an example for the world." He said that the governments of Burma and China firmly believe in the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, which have become a good foundation for us to solve the boundary issue. He said that this trip to China felt like visiting relatives.

Vice Premier Chen Yi said in a toast that the governments of China and Burma and the delegations of both countries have sincerity in resolving the Sino-Burmese boundary issue, demonstrating the eternal friendship and mutual support between our two countries, which is worthy of our celebration. The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere from beginning to end. Those attending the banquet included Zhang Hanfu, Geng Biao, Zhang Aiping, Xiao Xiangrong, Li Yimang, Yao Zhongming, Yu Peiwen, Shen Ping, and others. After the banquet, Premier Zhou, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and others watched the football match between the Chinese "Locomotive" Sports Association football team and the Lebanese "Lei Xing" Club football team at the Workers' Stadium with Brigadier General Aung Gyi and other distinguished Burmese guests.

### 中缅边界联合委员会缅甸代表到京  周总理接见昂季准将  同日晚间设宴欢迎缅方全体贵宾

1960-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅边界联合委员会缅甸代表到京
    周总理接见昂季准将
    同日晚间设宴欢迎缅方全体贵宾
    新华社23日讯　前来北京参加中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议的缅甸首席代表昂季准将和代表、顾问及随行人员一行十五人，于今天下午乘飞机由广州到达北京。到机场欢迎的有：外交部副部长耿飚，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将，中国驻缅甸大使李一氓，外交部第一亚洲司副司长沈平，中缅友好协会会长张友渔，北京市人民委员会外事处处长辛毅等。到机场欢迎的还有，中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明，部分代表和顾问等。缅甸驻中国大使、中缅边界联合委员会缅甸代表叫温，和缅甸驻中国大使馆外交官员，也到飞机场迎接。昂季准将和其他缅甸贵宾走下飞机后，接受了北京少先队员的献花。
    新华社23日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表昂季准将，进行了亲切友好的谈话。被接见的还有缅甸代表：缅甸驻中国大使叫温、克钦邦事务部秘书吴山伦、边境地区管理局局长苏敏上校、北掸邦专员吴昆翁佳和顾问以及随行工作人员。接见时在座的，有国务院副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫、耿飚，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将，中国驻缅甸大使李一氓，中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，第一亚洲司副司长沈平等。
    新华社23日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间在人民大会堂举行宴会，热烈欢迎中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表昂季准将和代表、顾问以及随行的工作人员。
    陈毅副总理出席了宴会。
    周总理在宴会上首先讲话，对昂季准将和缅甸的全体贵宾表示热烈的欢迎。他说，我们相信，中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议，在双方的共同努力下一定会获得成功。他说，现在北京正是盛夏，我们用像炎热天气一样的热的感情欢迎贵宾，因为我们是亲戚。昂季准将在宴会上讲话，他说：“我们保证通过中缅边界联合委员会双方的合作，以最快的速度来签订中缅边界条约，为世界树立模范。”他说，缅中两国政府坚信和平共处五项原则，这五项原则已成为我们解决边界问题的良好基础。他说，他们这次到中国来就好像到亲戚家里一样。
    陈毅副总理在祝酒中说，中缅两国政府和两国代表团对解决中缅边界问题都抱有诚意，表现了我们两国的永恒的友谊和互相支持，这值得我们庆贺。宴会自始至终在热烈、友好的气氛中进行。出席宴会的，有章汉夫、耿飚、张爱萍、萧向荣、李一氓、姚仲明、俞沛文、沈平等。宴会以后，周总理、陈毅副总理等同昂季准将和其他缅甸贵宾，一起在工人体育场观看了中国“火车头”体育协会足球队同黎巴嫩“雷星”俱乐部足球队的足球赛。
