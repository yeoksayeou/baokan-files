Title: Indonesian Communist Party Politburo Member Sakirman Abducted by Armed Personnel
Author:
Date: 1960-07-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 17th: The Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party issued a press release today announcing that Sakirman, Third Deputy Chairman of the Indonesian National Planning Council and member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, was "taken away" from his home in Bandung on the morning of July 16th by "people in military uniform" and has not returned home as of yet.

The press release stated that the Secretariat sent two members of the Mutual Cooperation Parliament, Mrs. Mudidjojo and Mrs. Sundari, to meet with the Indonesian Attorney General to inquire about the facts. The two of them have already met with the Attorney General.

The press release also stated that the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party is taking measures to further resolve this issue.

### 印度尼西亚共产党政治局委员　萨基尔曼被武装人员劫走

1960-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚共产党政治局委员
　　萨基尔曼被武装人员劫走
    新华社雅加达17日电　印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会书记处今天发表新闻公报宣布，印度尼西亚国家计划委员会第三副主席、印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会政治局委员萨基尔曼于7月16日清晨被“身穿军装的人”从他在万隆的家中“带走”，直到现在为止，尚未回家。
    公报说，书记处曾派合作国会的两名议员穆迪迪约夫人和顺达里夫人去见印度尼西亚最高检察长询问事实真相。她们两人已经会见了最高检察长。
    公报又说，印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会书记处正在采取措施进一步解决这个问题。
