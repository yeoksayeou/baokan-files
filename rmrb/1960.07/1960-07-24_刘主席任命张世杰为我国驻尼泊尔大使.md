Title: Chairman Liu Appoints Zhang Shijie as China's Ambassador to Nepal
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, has appointed Zhang Shijie as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Nepal.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, has removed Pan Zili from his position as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Nepal.

### 刘主席任命张世杰为我国驻尼泊尔大使

1960-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席任命张世杰为我国驻尼泊尔大使
    新华社23日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命张世杰为中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔王国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇免去潘自力中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔王国特命全权大使的职务。
