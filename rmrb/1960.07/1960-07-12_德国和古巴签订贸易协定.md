Title: Germany and Cuba Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-07-12
Page: 5

　　Germany and Cuba Sign Trade Agreement
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 8th – According to a report by the German News Agency, based on the agreement signed by the national banks of the German Democratic Republic and the Republic of Cuba, relevant departments of the two countries held negotiations in Havana and signed a treaty on the German Democratic Republic providing Cuba with the necessary machinery and equipment for industrialization.
    Cuba and the German Democratic Republic also held negotiations on providing Cuba with industrial equipment for the manufacture of radio equipment, photometers, and electrical appliances. The two countries also signed an agreement on the German Democratic Republic providing industrial equipment to two factories in Cuba.

### 德国和古巴签订贸易协定

1960-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　德国和古巴签订贸易协定
    新华社柏林8日电　据德意志通讯社报道，根据德意志民主共和国和古巴共和国两国国家银行签订的协定，两国的有关部门在哈瓦那举行了谈判，并签订了关于德意志民主共和国提供古巴工业化所必要的机器和设备的条约。
    古巴同德意志民主共和国还举行了关于向古巴提供用于制造无线电器材、光度计和电器器材等的工业设备的谈判。两国还签订了关于德意志民主共和国给古巴两个工厂提供工业设备的协定。
