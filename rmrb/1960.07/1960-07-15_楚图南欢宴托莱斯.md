Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet for Torres
Author:
Date: 1960-07-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 14th: Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese Association for Friendship with Latin America, held a luncheon on the 14th to welcome Mario Torres Calleja, Secretary-General of the Bolivian Miners' Union, who is visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union. Li Chunming, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union, and others were present.

### 楚图南欢宴托莱斯

1960-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　楚图南欢宴托莱斯
    新华社14日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南在14日举行午宴，欢迎应中国煤矿工会全国委员会邀请前来我国访问的玻利维亚矿工联合会总书记马里奥·托莱斯·卡耶哈。作陪的有中国煤矿工会全国委员会副主席李春明等。
