Title: The Soviet Union Donates a Batch of Food to the People of the Congo
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-26
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Odessa: According to TASS, a batch of food donated by the Soviet Union to the people of the Congo was shipped from Odessa on the 24th. This batch of food includes: nine thousand tons of wheat, more than one thousand tons of sugar, and three hundred thousand cans of condensed milk.

### 苏联赠送刚果人民一批食品

1960-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联赠送刚果人民一批食品
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社敖德萨讯：苏联赠送给刚果人民的一批食品已在24日从敖德萨运出。这批食品包括：九千吨小麦、一千多吨糖和三十万筒炼乳。
