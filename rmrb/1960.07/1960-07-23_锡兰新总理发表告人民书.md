Title: New Prime Minister of Ceylon Issues Message to the People

Author: 
Date: 1960-07-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, July 22nd. Ceylon’s new Prime Minister, Mrs. Sirima Bandaranaike, in her first message to the nation on Ceylon Radio last night, said that the people of Ceylon have clearly reaffirmed with their own judgment their trust in the policies initiated by the late Prime Minister Bandaranaike.

She also said that she is grateful to the political parties (the Lanka Sama Samaja Party and the Ceylon Communist Party) that cooperated in reaching a non-competition agreement. She expressed the hope that they will continue to give support to the Freedom Party to help her implement the policies of the late Prime Minister Bandaranaike.

### 锡兰新总理发表告人民书

1960-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰新总理发表告人民书
    新华社科伦坡22日电　锡兰新总理西丽马沃·班达拉奈克夫人昨晚在锡兰电台第一次发表告全国人民书时说，锡兰人民已经以自己的判断清楚地重申他们信任故总理班达拉奈克所创导的政策。
    她还说，她感激那些在达成不竞争协议方面进行合作的政党（平等社会党和锡兰共产党）。她表示希望，它们将继续给自由党以支持，帮助她执行已故的班达拉奈克总理的政策。
