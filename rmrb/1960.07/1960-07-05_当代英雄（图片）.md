Title: Contemporary Hero (Picture)
Author: Liu Zhiwei
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 3

    Contemporary Hero
    Sun Maosong, a lathe operator from Harbin No. 1 Machinery Factory and a representative to the National Congress of Heroes, has reformed cutting tools based on the characteristics of small-batch single-part production, creating a set of twenty-two excellent cutting tools, making one person's work equivalent to that of twelve people. This is him and his apprentice further studying how to improve the efficiency of the cutting tools.　Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Zhiwei
