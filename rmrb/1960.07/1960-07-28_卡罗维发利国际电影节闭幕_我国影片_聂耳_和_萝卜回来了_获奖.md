Title: Karlovy Vary International Film Festival Closes; Chinese Films "Nie Er" and "Radish is Back" Win Awards
Author:
Date: 1960-07-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 24th. The 12th Karlovy Vary International Film Festival, hosted by the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic and lasting two weeks, closed on the evening of the 23rd.

A total of forty-seven countries participated in the festival, with one hundred and four films.

In the film competition of the festival, the excellent Chinese film "Nie Er" won the biography film award. This film successfully portrays the distinct image of the great proletarian musician Nie Er, who grew up under the leadership and cultivation of the Chinese Communist Party during the era of the Chinese national democratic revolution; it vividly and specifically reflects the characteristics of the era when the proletarian cultural movement and the people's revolutionary struggle were combined in the 1920s.

The socialist countries' films that won awards in the film competition were: the Soviet film "Seryozha" won the Grand Prize, the Soviet film "Living Heroes" won the Main Prize, and the Romanian film "Burning Rivers" won the Main Prize. Blinech, the director of the Czechoslovak film "Car Accident," won the award for outstanding individual artistic technique; Erwin Geschonneck, the actor of the German Democratic Republic film "People with Wings," won the Best Actor Award; Toroczik, the actress of the Hungarian film "Naughty Girl," won the Best Actress Award; the Mongolian film "If I Had a Horse" won a special honorary certificate for artistic films; and the Czechoslovak scientific educational film "Before Flying into Space" won the documentary film award.

The Cuban film "The Birth of the Cuban Revolutionary Army," which participated in the international film festival film competition for the first time, won an honorary certificate for documentary films.

Twenty-six countries participated in the puppet film and animation film competition at the festival. The Chinese animated film "Radish is Back," which reflects the communist spirit of self-sacrifice, won the festival's honorary certificate.

Concurrently with the Karlovy Vary Film Festival, the 11th Czechoslovak Working People's Film Festival was also held in twenty-three cities in Czechoslovakia from the 8th to the 25th. This year's film festival attracted an unprecedented number of viewers. By the 19th, the nationwide film festival had already attracted 800,000 viewers. The Working People's Film Festival screened twenty-three films from seventeen countries, including Czechoslovakia, the Soviet Union, and China. The Sino-Soviet co-production film "The Wind from the East" was shown in seven cities. Audiences believe that the film shows the Sino-Soviet friendship forged in the revolutionary struggle and construction.

### 卡罗维发利国际电影节闭幕  我国影片“聂耳”和“萝卜回来了”获奖

1960-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    卡罗维发利国际电影节闭幕
    我国影片“聂耳”和“萝卜回来了”获奖
    新华社布拉格24日电　捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国主办的历时两周的第十二届卡罗维发利国际电影节已经在23日晚上闭幕。
    参加这次电影节的共有四十七个国家，一百零四部影片。
    在电影节的影片比赛中，中国优秀影片“聂耳”获得了传记片奖。这部影片成功地塑造了在中国民族民主革命时代，在中国共产党领导和培养下成长起来的、伟大的无产阶级音乐家聂耳的鲜明形象；生动具体地反映出本世纪二十年代无产阶级的文化运动和人民的革命斗争相结合的时代特征。
    参加影片比赛的社会主义国家影片获奖的有：苏联影片“谢廖沙”获得大奖，苏联影片“活着的英雄们”获主要奖，罗马尼亚影片“燃烧着的河流”获主要奖。捷克斯洛伐克影片“车祸”的导演布利涅奇获个人优秀艺术技巧奖；德意志民主共和国电影“带翅膀的人”的演员埃尔温·格塞匈内克获男演员奖；匈牙利“淘气姑娘”演员托罗齐克获女演员奖；蒙古影片“假如我有一匹马”获艺术片特别荣誉奖状；捷克斯洛伐克科学教育片“飞到宇宙之前”获纪录片奖。
    第一次参加国际电影节影片比赛的古巴影片“古巴革命军的诞生”获纪录片荣誉奖状。
    有二十六个国家参加了电影节上木偶片和动画片的比赛。反映舍己为人的共产主义风格的中国动画片“萝卜回来了”获得了电影节的荣誉奖状。
    与卡罗维发利电影节举行的同时，在捷克斯洛伐克二十三个城市内从8日到25日还举行了捷克斯洛伐克第十一届劳动人民电影节。本届电影节的观众空前众多，截至19日全国电影节的观众已达八十万人。劳动人民电影节上放映了捷克斯洛伐克、苏联、中国等十七个国家的二十三部电影。中苏合拍的影片“风从东方来”在七个城市中映出。观众认为，从这部电影中可以看到在革命斗争和建设中凝成的中苏友谊。
