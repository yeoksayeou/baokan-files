Title: Indonesian Women's Movement Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-11
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, 10th — The delegation of the Indonesian Women's Movement Association, consisting of four members led by Mrs. Suwarti, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation.

　　Those who welcomed them at the airport included Wu Qing, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, Huang Ganying, Director of the International Liaison Department, Zhang Jiexun, Secretary-General of the Beijing Women's Federation, and others.

　　Another member of the delegation will arrive in Beijing in the coming days.

### 印度尼西亚妇女运动协会代表团到京

1960-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚妇女运动协会代表团到京
    新华社10日讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚妇女运动协会代表团一行四人，在团长苏娃尔蒂夫人率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有全国妇联书记处书记吴綪、国际联络部部长黄甘英、北京市妇联秘书长张洁珣等。
  代表团的另一团员也将在日内到京。
