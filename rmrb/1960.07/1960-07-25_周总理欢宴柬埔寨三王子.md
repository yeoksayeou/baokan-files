Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for Three Cambodian Princes
Author:
Date: 1960-07-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th. Premier Zhou Enlai met and hosted a banquet this evening in honor of the three princes of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Norodom Sihanouk: Yuwana, Naradipo, and Kemanurak, who have come to China to study.

Present at the meeting and attending the banquet were: Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Rong Gaotang, as well as heads of relevant units and people from various fields.

Also present at the meeting and invited to the banquet were: the Ambassador of the Kingdom of Cambodia to China, Lan Nete, and diplomatic officials from the embassy.

The seven members of the Cambodian Royal Socialist Youth League who came to China to study basketball techniques were also invited to attend the banquet. At the banquet, which was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere, Premier Zhou Enlai and Ambassador Lan Nete delivered speeches successively.

### 周总理欢宴柬埔寨三王子

1960-07-25
第6版()
专栏：

    周总理欢宴柬埔寨三王子
    新华社24日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上会见并设宴欢迎前来我国学习的柬埔寨王国诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王的三位王子：尤瓦那、纳拉迪波、克马努拉克。
    会见时在座的和参加宴会的有：陈毅副总理，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林，体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠，以及各有关单位的负责人和各方面人士等。
    会见时在座的和应邀参加宴会的还有：柬埔寨王国驻我国大使兰·涅特和大使馆的外交官员。
    前来我国学习篮球技术的柬埔寨皇家社会主义青年联盟的七位盟员，也应邀参加了宴会。在充满热烈、友好气氛的宴会上，周恩来总理和兰·涅特大使先后讲了话。
