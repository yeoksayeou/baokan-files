Title: Comrade Le Duan of the Vietnam Workers' Party Passed Through Beijing on His Way Home; Comrades Deng Xiaoping and Liu Ningyi Saw Him Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-07-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 30th — Comrade Le Duan, head of the delegation of the Vietnam Workers' Party, who was invited to attend the Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party, and his entourage, passed through Beijing and flew back to Hanoi on the morning of June 30. Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, saw him off at the airport. Chen Zhongkuo, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, and others were also present at the airport to see him off.

### 越南劳动党黎笋同志过京回国  邓小平刘宁一同志到机场送行

1960-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党黎笋同志过京回国
    邓小平刘宁一同志到机场送行
    新华社30日讯　应邀参加罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会的越南劳动党代表团团长黎笋同志及其随行人员路经北京，在6月30日上午乘机飞回河内。中国共产党中央委员会总书记邓小平、中央委员刘宁一到机场送行。到机场送行的还有越南驻中国大使馆临时代办陈仲括等。
