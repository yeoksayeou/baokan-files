Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Chilean Agricultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-05
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, July 4th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the entire Chilean agricultural delegation this afternoon, headed by Clodomiro Almeyda Medina, with Gastón Caballo Delien and Eduardo Rojas as deputy heads. The hosts and guests engaged in friendly talks.
    Present at the reception were Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Agriculture; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association; Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General. Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was also present.

### 陈毅副总理接见智利农业代表团

1960-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见智利农业代表团
    新华社4日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见以团长克洛多米罗·阿耳梅达·梅迪纳，副团长卡斯东·卡瓦洛·德里恩和爱德华多·罗哈斯为首的智利农业代表团全体成员，宾主进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有农业部副部长刘瑞龙，中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南，中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，副秘书长吴晓达。外交部美澳司司长申健也在座。
