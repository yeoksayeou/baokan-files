Title: Resolution on the Persecution of Peace Fighters
Author:
Date: 1960-07-15
Page: 6

　　Resolution on the Persecution of Peace Fighters

　　Peace fighters in many countries have been subjected to all kinds of suppression and persecution, including the death penalty, life imprisonment, concentration camps, hard labor, and deprivation of everything that is precious to human life and dignity.

　　In some countries, this suppression and persecution has reached the point where many peace fighters have been tortured to death, their only crime being their struggle for peace and democracy.

　　The practice of depriving peace fighters of freedom of action and speech in the struggle to safeguard peace and national independence enables imperialists and enemies of peace to intensify their control over the people and continue to wantonly exploit and plunder their resources.

　　We declare that we strongly condemn these criminal acts against peace fighters, and express our deep sympathy and guarantee solidarity with these heroic fighters. We call on all peace-loving people to work to restore their basic rights and call on them to continue the struggle for peace, national liberation, and democracy.

　　　　　　　　　　　Standing Committee of the World Peace Council
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Stockholm, July 11, 1960

### 关于和平战士遭受迫害的决议

1960-07-15
第6版()
专栏：

　　关于和平战士遭受迫害的决议
    许多国家的和平战士遭到了各种各样的镇压和迫害，其中包括死刑、无期徒刑、集中营、苦役和被剥夺一切对人的生活和尊严具有珍贵意义的东西。
    在有些国家里，这种镇压和迫害达到了许多和平战士被折磨至死的程度，而他们的唯一罪名是他们为和平和民主进行了斗争。
    剥夺和平战士在维护和平和民族独立的斗争中的行动和言论自由的做法，使帝国主义者和和平的敌人能够加紧对人民的控制，继续肆意地剥削和掠夺他们的资源。
    我们宣布，我们强烈谴责这种对和平战士的罪恶行动，并且对这些英勇的战士表示深切的同情并保证声援他们，我们号召一切爱好和平的人民为恢复他们的基本权利而努力并且号召他们继续争取和平、民族解放和民主的斗争。
                            世界和平理事会常务委员会
                                  1960年7月11日于斯德哥尔摩
