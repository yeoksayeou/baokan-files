Title: Hirano Yoshitaro Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-07-29
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Hirano Yoshitaro, head of the Japanese "China Research Institute" delegation to China, took a plane today via Guangzhou to return to his country.
