Title: National Congress of Literary and Art Workers Begins Group Discussions; Enthusiastic Discussions of the CPC Central Committee and State Council's Congratulatory Message and Comrade Zhou Yang's Report
Author:
Date: 1960-07-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: The Third National Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers began group discussions today. More than 2,300 delegates were divided into many groups based on provinces, autonomous regions, central government-affiliated units, the People's Liberation Army, and the trade union system, and enthusiastically discussed the congratulatory message from the CPC Central Committee and the State Council delivered by Comrade Lu Dingyi, and the several important reports made at the congress by Comrade Zhou Yang and others, such as "The Road of Socialist Literature and Art." The group discussions of the congress will continue tomorrow.

### 全国文艺工作者代表大会开始分组讨论  热烈讨论中共中央和国务院的祝词和周扬同志的报告

1960-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    全国文艺工作者代表大会开始分组讨论
    热烈讨论中共中央和国务院的祝词和周扬同志的报告
    新华社25日讯　中国文学艺术工作者第三次代表大会，今天开始分组讨论。二千三百多代表以省、自治区和中央直属单位、解放军、工会系统，划分许多小组，热烈讨论了陆定一同志代表中共中央和国务院向大会的祝词，和周扬等同志在大会所作的“社会主义文学艺术的道路”等几个重要报告。大会分组讨论明天将继续进行。
