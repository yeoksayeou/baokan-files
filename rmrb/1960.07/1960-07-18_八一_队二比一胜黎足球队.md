Title: "August 1st" Team Wins 2-1 Against Lebanese Football Team
Author:
Date: 1960-07-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: The Lebanese Beirut "Lightning Star" club football team played its first friendly match in China against the "August 1st" football team at the Beijing Workers' Stadium tonight. As a result, the "August 1st" team won 2-1. The visiting team led 1-0 in the first half. The "August 1st" team secured the victory in the second half when left winger Ren Bin scored two consecutive goals.

After the match, General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Ma Yuehan and Huang Zhong, Chairman and Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, met with the athletes of both sides at the stadium and took photos with them.

The visiting team will also play friendly matches against the Beijing football team and the "Locomotive" Sports Association football team in Beijing on the 20th and 23rd respectively.

### “八一”队二比一胜黎足球队

1960-07-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　“八一”队二比一胜黎足球队
    据新华社17日讯　黎巴嫩贝鲁特“雷星”俱乐部足球队今晚在北京工人体育场同“八一”足球队举行访华第一场友谊比赛，结果“八一”队以二比一获胜。上半场客队曾以一比零领先。“八一”队是在下半场由左前卫任彬连射入两个球，才奠定胜利的。
    比赛结束后，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华上将，中华全国体育总会主席马约翰、副主席黄中，到球场接见了双方运动员，并同他们合影。
    客队还将于20日和23日在北京先后同北京足球队和“火车头”体育协会足球队举行友谊比赛。
