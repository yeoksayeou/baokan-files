Title: China-Burma Joint Committee Continues Meeting: Agreement Reached on Procedural Matters
Author:
Date: 1960-07-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 30th June. The China-Burma Joint Committee continued its meeting on June 30th. The talks continued in a friendly atmosphere.

The China-Burma Joint Committee has reached agreement on the following matters: (1) Matters concerning the rules of procedure of the Joint Committee; (2) Matters concerning the tasks and responsibilities of the Joint Committee; (3) Matters concerning the tasks and working procedures of the Joint Investigation Group and the Joint Survey Group.

The Joint Committee also conducted further discussions on the specific arrangements for the work of investigating and surveying the boundary between the two countries, and reached a consensus.

### 中缅联合委员会继续开会  已就议事程序等问题达成协议

1960-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    中缅联合委员会继续开会
    已就议事程序等问题达成协议
    新华社仰光30日电　中缅联合委员会6月30日继续举行会议。会谈继续在友好的气氛中进行。
    中缅联合委员会已经就下列问题达成协议：（一）联合委员会的议事程序问题；（二）联合委员会的任务和职责问题；（三）联合调查组和联合勘察组的任务和工作程序问题。
    联合委员会还就调查和勘察两国边界工作的具体安排进一步进行了讨论，并且取得了一致的看法。
