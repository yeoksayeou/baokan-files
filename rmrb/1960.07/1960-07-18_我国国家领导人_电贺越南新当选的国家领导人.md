Title: China's National Leaders Send Congratulatory Telegrams to Newly Elected Vietnamese State Leaders
Author:
Date: 1960-07-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: Chinese state leaders Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairpersons Soong Ching Ling and Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou Enlai jointly sent a telegram on July 17th to the newly elected state leaders of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, President Ho Chi Minh, Vice President Ton Duc Thang, Chairman Truong Chinh, and Premier Pham Van Dong, expressing their congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Comrade Ton Duc Thang, Vice President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Comrade Truong Chinh, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Comrade Pham Van Dong, Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

We are delighted to learn of your respective appointments as President, Vice President, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, and Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. On behalf of the Chinese people and government, we extend our heartfelt and warm congratulations to you.

We wish you even greater achievements in the noble cause of building socialism, striving for national reunification, strengthening the solidarity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and defending peace in Asia and the world.

We wish the profound fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese peoples continuous consolidation and development.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Soong Ching Ling
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Dong Biwu
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China Zhu De
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 17, 1960

### 我国国家领导人　电贺越南新当选的国家领导人

1960-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国国家领导人
　　电贺越南新当选的国家领导人
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国国家领导人刘少奇主席、宋庆龄和董必武副主席、朱德委员长、周恩来总理17日联合打电报给新当选的越南民主共和国国家领导人胡志明主席、孙德胜副主席、长征主席、范文同总理表示祝贺。电文如下：越南民主共和国主席　胡志明同志，越南民主共和国副主席　孙德胜同志，越南民主共和国国会常务委员会主席　长征同志，越南民主共和国政府总理　范文同同志：
    欣悉你们分别荣任越南民主共和国主席、副主席、国会常务委员会主席和政府总理，我们谨代表中国人民和政府向你们致衷心的、热烈的祝贺。
    祝你们在建设社会主义、争取国家统一、加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结以及保卫亚洲和世界和平的崇高事业中取得更大的成就。
    祝中越两国人民兄弟般的深厚友谊不断巩固和发展。
                  中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                  中华人民共和国副主席　宋庆龄
                                                    董必武
　　　　　　　　　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会
　　　　　　　　　常务委员会委员长　　　　　　　　　朱德
　　　　　　　　　中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
