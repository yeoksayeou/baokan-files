Title: Three Soviet Musicians Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, July 15th—According to the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the famous Soviet pianist Tatiana Nikolayeva, violinist Zorya Shikhmurzayeva, and pianist accompanist Alexander Sobolev arrived in Beijing by plane today for a performance tour in China.

### 三位苏联音乐家到京

1960-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

    三位苏联音乐家到京
    据新华社15日讯　根据中苏文化合作协定1960年执行计划来华访问演出的苏联著名钢琴家塔佳娜·尼古拉耶娃，小提琴家佐丽娅·西赫木尔扎耶娃，以及钢琴伴奏亚历山大·索波列夫三人，今日乘机到达北京。
