Be a Good "Logistics Department" for Agriculture

    Transferring Improved Seeds
    The grain and commercial departments of Sichuan Province are transferring a large quantity of late rice, miscellaneous grains, melons, beans, vegetables and other seeds to support rural people's communes in expanding their summer planting area, and strive for a bumper harvest of autumn crops. Since the beginning of the summer harvest, the grain department has adjusted and supplied more than 5 million jin of corn seeds to various communes, and transferred 20 million jin of late rice seeds from state granaries in Wenjiang, Daxian and other places to Luzhou, Chongqing and other places to support the communes in planting in a timely manner.

### 调运良种

1960-07-21
第2版()
专栏：当好农业“后勤部”

    调运良种
    四川省粮食、商业部门调运大批晚稻、杂粮、瓜豆、蔬菜等种子，支援农村人民公社扩大夏种面积，力争秋季作物丰收。从夏收开始到现在，粮食部门已调剂供应给各公社玉米种子五百多万斤，又从温江、达县等地国家粮库中拨出两千万斤晚稻种子，运往泸州、重庆等地，支援公社适时下种。
