Title: Cuban Prime Minister Castro Writes Letter Thanking Our Art Troupe for Greetings
Author:
Date: 1960-07-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, July 12th: Cuban Prime Minister Castro, who is currently ill, wrote a handwritten letter today to Chen Zhongjing, head of the Chinese art troupe, and all its members, thanking them for their kind greetings.

　　The Chinese art troupe, which is visiting here, sent representatives with a letter of sympathy and flowers to the Prime Minister after learning of Prime Minister Castro's illness, and wished him a speedy recovery.

　　In a letter to the Chinese art troupe, Castro said: "We are very pleased to know that you participated with the people of our country in the grand rally on Sunday to protest imperialist aggression. We will never forget your concern and friendly activities for us. We will always remember the Chinese art troupe as a group of outstanding people, and hope to see them again."

### 古巴总理卡斯特罗写信感谢我艺术团对他的问候

1960-07-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　古巴总理卡斯特罗写信感谢我艺术团对他的问候
    新华社哈瓦那12日电　正在病中的古巴总理卡斯特罗今天写了一封亲笔信给中国艺术团团长陈忠经和全体团员，感谢中国艺术团对他的亲切的问候。
    在这里访问的中国艺术团在得知卡斯特罗总理生病的消息后，曾派代表带着慰问信和鲜花向总理表示慰问，并祝他早日恢复健康。
    卡斯特罗在写给中国艺术团的信中说：“我们知道您们与我国人民一起参加了星期日抗议帝国主义侵略的盛大集会，我们大家都感到非常高兴。我们永远不会忘记您们对我们的关心和友好活动，我们将把中国艺术团当作一个优秀人物的团体而永远记住他们，并希望能同他们再次相见。”
