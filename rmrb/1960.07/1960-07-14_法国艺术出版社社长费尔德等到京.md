Title: French Art Publisher Feild and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-14
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Mr. Feild, the president of the French Art Publishing House, and photographer Pic, who are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 法国艺术出版社社长费尔德等到京

1960-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　法国艺术出版社社长费尔德等到京
    新华社13日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的法国艺术出版社社长费尔德和摄影师皮克，今天乘飞机到达北京。
