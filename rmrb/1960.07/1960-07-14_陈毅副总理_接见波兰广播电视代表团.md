Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Polish Radio and Television Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Polish Radio and Television Delegation headed by Sokorski, Chairman of the Polish Radio and Television Committee, this morning and had a cordial conversation.

Present at the reception were Mei Yi, Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, Jin Zhao, Deputy Director, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and East European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Jerzy Knothe, the Polish Ambassador to China, and Counselor Flato of the Embassy were also present.

### 陈毅副总理　接见波兰广播电视代表团

1960-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理
　　接见波兰广播电视代表团
    新华社13日讯　陈毅副总理在今天上午接见了以波兰广播电视委员会主席索科尔斯基为首的波兰广播电视代表团，进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有广播事业局局长梅益、副局长金照，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清。
    波兰驻中国大使耶日·克诺泰、大使馆参赞傅拉托也在座。
