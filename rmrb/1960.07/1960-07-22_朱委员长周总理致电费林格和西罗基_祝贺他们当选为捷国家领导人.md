Title: Chairman Zhu and Premier Zhou Send Telegrams to Fierlinger and Široký Congratulating Them on Their Election as National Leaders of Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1960-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st -- Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai sent telegrams to Z. Fierlinger and V. Široký, warmly congratulating them on their election as national leaders of Czechoslovakia. The full text of the telegram from Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, to Z. Fierlinger is as follows: Comrade Z. Fierlinger, Chairman of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic:

    I am delighted to learn of your continued election as Chairman of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic. On behalf of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China and in my own name, I extend to you my warm congratulations.

    I wish you new achievements in the noble cause of developing the socialist construction of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic and further strengthening the solidarity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

    Zhu De
    Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
    July 12, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st -- Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to V. Široký, warmly congratulating him on his assumption of the post of Premier of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic. The full text of the telegram is as follows: Comrade V. Široký, Premier of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic:

    I am delighted to learn of your assumption of the post of Premier of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic. On behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name, I extend to you my warm congratulations.

    I wish you even more brilliant achievements in the socialist construction of Czechoslovakia, the consolidation of the great unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and the cause of safeguarding peace in Europe and the world.

    I wish that the unbreakable brotherly friendship between the people of China and Czechoslovakia will be continuously consolidated and developed.

          Zhou Enlai
                              Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                              July 15, 1960

### 朱委员长周总理致电费林格和西罗基  祝贺他们当选为捷国家领导人

1960-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    朱委员长周总理致电费林格和西罗基
    祝贺他们当选为捷国家领导人
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德打电报给兹·费林格，热烈祝贺他继续当选为捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国国民议会主席。电报全文如下：捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国国民议会主席兹·费林格同志：
    欣悉您继续当选捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国国民议会主席，我谨代表中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会并以我个人的名义向您致热烈的祝贺。
    祝您在发展捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国的社会主义建设和进一步加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结的崇高事业中获得新的成就。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长  朱德
    1960年7月12日
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来打电报给威·西罗基，热烈祝贺他就任捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国总理。电报全文如下：捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国总理威·西罗基同志：
    欣悉您就任捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国总理，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并以我个人的名义向您表示热烈的祝贺。
    祝您在捷克斯洛伐克社会主义建设、巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的伟大团结和维护欧洲和世界和平的事业中取得更加辉煌的成就。
    祝中捷两国人民间的牢不可破的兄弟友谊日益巩固和发展。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                              1960年7月15日
