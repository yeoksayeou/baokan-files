Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Held 99th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-07-16
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 15th: The 99th meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks continued in Warsaw at 2:00 PM today. This meeting lasted one hour and thirty minutes.
    After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting will be held on September 6th.

### 中美大使级会谈  举行第99次会议

1960-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

　  中美大使级会谈
　  举行第99次会议
    新华社华沙15日电　中美大使级会谈第九十九次会议今天下午二时继续在华沙举行。本次会谈历时一小时三十分钟。
    会后双方宣布，下次会谈将在9月6日举行。
