Title: Cuban Youth Prepare to Defend the Motherland; Over 60,000 Youths Join the Rebel Army Youth League; Over 2,000 Youths Already Undergoing Military Training in the Mountains
Author:
Date: 1960-07-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 23rd (Latin American News Agency): The Vice-President of the Cuban Rebel Army Youth League, Fernando Ravelo, announced that over 60,000 Cuban youths have joined the Rebel Army Youth League, of which 2,500 members are already undergoing military training and labor in the Sierra Maestra mountains.

Ravelo said that the main purpose of this organization is to organize Cuban youth for military training in order to defend the motherland from foreign threats. The organization's aims also include raising the cultural level of the youth, conducting cultural and sports activities, assisting the army, the National Land Reform Committee, and other state organs in labor, and organizing voluntary labor teams. Ravelo said that a similar women's federation is also being organized.

### 古巴青年准备保卫祖国  六万多青年参加起义军青年联合会  二千多青年已在山区进行军事训练

1960-07-01
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴青年准备保卫祖国
    六万多青年参加起义军青年联合会
    二千多青年已在山区进行军事训练
    新华社讯　据拉丁美洲通讯社哈瓦那23日讯：古巴起义军青年联合会副主席费尔南多·拉维洛宣布，有六万多古巴青年参加起义军青年联合会，其中已经有两千五百名会员在马埃斯特腊山区进行军事训练和劳动。
    拉维洛说，这个组织的主要目的是组织古巴青年进行军事训练，以便保卫受到外国威胁的祖国。这个组织的目的还包括提高青年的文化，进行文化体育活动，协助军队、土地改革全国委员会以及其他国家机关进行劳动，和组织义务劳动队。拉维洛说，目前还在组织一个类似性质的妇女联合会。
