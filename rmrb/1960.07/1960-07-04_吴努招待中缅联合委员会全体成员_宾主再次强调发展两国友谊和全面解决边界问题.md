Title: U Nu Hosts All Members of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee, Hosts and Guests Reiterate the Development of Friendship Between the Two Countries and a Comprehensive Solution to the Border Issue
Author:
Date: 1960-07-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, July 2nd. Burmese Prime Minister U Nu held a reception here tonight to host all members of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee.

Attending the reception were all the Chinese representatives and advisors of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee headed by Yao Zhongming, and all the Burmese representatives and advisors headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi.

Also present at the reception were Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Myint Thein, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities Sao Khun Kyi, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Mann Ba Swe, Minister of Foreign Affairs Sao Khun Hkio, Minister of Transport Bo Min Gaung, Minister of Culture and Health U Ba Swe, Minister of Kachin State Affairs Duwa Sinwa Nawng, former Minister of Foreign Affairs U Chan Tun Aung, and other senior government officials.

Li Yimang, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and other diplomatic officials from the Embassy also attended the reception. The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the further development of Sino-Burmese friendship and the final and comprehensive resolution of the Sino-Burmese border issue.

After the reception, Burmese artists performed Burmese songs and dances. In addition, a documentary of "Premier Zhou Enlai's Visit to Burma" was shown.

### 吴努招待中缅联合委员会全体成员  宾主再次强调发展两国友谊和全面解决边界问题

1960-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    吴努招待中缅联合委员会全体成员
    宾主再次强调发展两国友谊和全面解决边界问题
    新华社仰光2日电　缅甸总理吴努今天晚上在这里举行招待会招待中缅联合委员会全体成员。
    出席招待会的有以姚仲明为首的中缅联合委员会中国方面全体代表和顾问以及以昂季准将为首的缅甸方面全体代表和顾问。
    出席招待会的还有最高法院首席法官吴敏登，民族院议长藻昆基，代表院议长曼巴赛，外交部长藻昆卓，运输部长波木昂，文化、卫生部长吴巴素，克钦邦事务部长杜瓦信瓦瑙，前外交部长吴千吞昂以及其他高级政府官员。
    中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和大使馆的其他外交官员也出席了招待会。招待会在热烈和友好的气氛中进行。宾主双方一再为中缅友谊的进一步发展和中缅边界问题的最后和全面解决而干杯。
    招待会结束后，缅甸艺术家表演了缅甸歌舞。另外还放映了“周恩来总理访问缅甸”的纪录片。
