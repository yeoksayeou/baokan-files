Title: Leshi Concludes Visit to Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-07-03
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 1st. Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, and all the Albanian distinguished guests, concluded their friendly visit to Mongolia and returned home by plane from here at noon today.

    Mongolian Party and state leaders Tsedenbal, Sambuu, Tsende, Molomjamts, and others, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia, saw them off at the airport.

    Chairman Lleshi and others arrived in Mongolia on June 28 for a visit. Chairman Sambuu hosted a grand state banquet for them on June 29. On June 30, people from all walks of life in Ulaanbaatar held a Mongolian-Albanian friendship rally to welcome the distinguished Albanian guests. Sambuu and Lleshi both spoke at the state banquet and the friendship rally.

### 列希结束在蒙古的访问

1960-07-03
第6版()
专栏：

    列希结束在蒙古的访问
    新华社乌兰巴托1日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希和全体阿尔巴尼亚贵宾，结束了在蒙古的友好访问，今天中午从这里乘飞机回国。
    蒙古党和国家领导人泽登巴尔、桑布、曾德、莫洛姆扎木茨等，以及各国驻蒙古的外交使节到机场欢送。
    列希主席等是在6月28日到蒙古访问的。桑布主席曾在6月29日为他们举行了盛大国宴。6月30日，乌兰巴托市各界人民举行了蒙阿友好大会，欢迎阿尔巴尼亚贵宾。桑布和列希在国宴和友好大会上都先后讲了话。
