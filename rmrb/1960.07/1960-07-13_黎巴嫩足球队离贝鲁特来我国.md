Title: Lebanese Football Team Leaves Beirut for China
Author:
Date: 1960-07-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, July 12th: The Lebanese Racing Club football team left Beirut on the evening of the 11th, via Moscow, for China.

　　This football team consists of twenty-three players, most of whom are members of the Lebanese national football team. The captain is Edmond Assaf, the chairman of the Racing Club.

### 黎巴嫩足球队离贝鲁特来我国

1960-07-13
第6版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩足球队离贝鲁特来我国
　　新华社贝鲁特12日电　黎巴嫩竞赛俱乐部足球队，11日晚离开贝鲁特取道莫斯科去中国。
    这个足球队由二十三名队员组成，大部分是黎巴嫩国家足球队的队员。队长是竞赛俱乐部的主席艾德蒙·阿萨夫。
