Title: Typhoon Warning
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 30th. The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon warning at 8:00 PM today:

    Typhoon No. 7 of 1960 in the Pacific Ocean was centered at 2:00 PM today approximately 450 kilometers east of Hengchun, Taiwan, at about 22 degrees north latitude and 125 degrees east longitude. The maximum wind force near the center is estimated to be force 12. The typhoon center is currently moving northwest at a speed of 8-10 knots per hour. Affected by the typhoon, it is expected that within the next 24 hours, the wind force in the Taiwan Strait and the southern part of the East China Sea will gradually increase to force 8-10; the wind force along the coast of Fujian and the southern coast of Zhejiang will increase to force 6-8. The wind force along the eastern coast of Guangdong and the northern coast of Zhejiang will increase to force 6-7.

    It is expected that the typhoon center will make landfall along the coast from Fujian to southern Zhejiang during the day on August 1st. There will be force 8-10 gales along the coast of Fujian and southern Zhejiang, with force 10-12 gales near the landing site. There will be force 6-8 gales along the eastern coast of Guangdong and the northern coast of Zhejiang. There will also be rainstorms in Zhejiang, Fujian, and eastern Jiangxi.

### 台风警报

1960-07-31
第5版()
专栏：

    台风警报
    新华社30日讯　中央气象科学研究所今天下午八时发布台风警报：
    太平洋上的1960年第七号台风，今天下午二时中心位置在台湾恒春以东四百五十公里左右的洋面上，就是北纬二十二度、东经一百二十五度附近。中心附近最大风力估计有十二级。目前台风中心正以每小时八—十浬的速度向西北方向移动。受台风影响，预计未来二十四小时内，台湾海峡、东海南部的风力将逐渐加强到八—十级；福建沿海和浙江南部沿海的风力将加强到六—八级。广东东部沿海、浙江北部沿海风力，将加强到六—七级。
    预计台风中心将于8月1日白天在福建到浙江南部一带的沿海登陆，福建沿海和浙江南部沿海有八—十级大风，其中登陆地点附近将有十—十二级大风，广东东部沿海和浙江北部沿海将有六—八级大风。浙江、福建、江西东部并有暴雨。
