Title:  Lleshi Returns to Tirana, Laden with Friendship from Asian Brother Countries
Author: 
Date: 1960-07-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 5th:  After paying invited visits to the four Asian brother countries of China, Vietnam, North Korea, and Mongolia, the Chairman of the Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly, Lleshi, and his entourage, returned to Tirana by plane this morning.

Kabo and Baluku, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, and Peza, Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly, were at the airport to welcome them.

Wang Dong, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, and Fang Heshou, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy in Albania, were also at the airport to welcome them.

### 满载亚洲兄弟国家的友谊  列希返抵地拉那

1960-07-06
第6版()
专栏：

    满载亚洲兄弟国家的友谊
    列希返抵地拉那
    新华社地拉那5日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席列希及其随行人员，应邀访问中、越、朝、蒙四个亚洲兄弟国家之后，今天上午乘飞机回到地拉那。
    到机场欢迎的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员卡博、巴卢库，阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团副主席佩扎等。
    我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王栋和朝鲜驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办方鹤寿也到机场欢迎。
