Title: Introducing the Achievements of Socialist Construction in Poland: Polish Print Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-07-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd. The Polish People's Republic Print Exhibition opened this morning at the Waterside Pavilion in Zhongshan Park. This exhibition is organized by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Sino-Polish Friendship Association to celebrate the National Day of the Polish People's Republic.

The exhibition features more than one hundred excellent prints, introducing the selfless labor enthusiasm and great achievements of the fraternal Polish people in socialist construction, reflecting the Polish people's struggle against imperialism, and powerfully exposing the war conspiracies of the imperialist war bloc headed by the United States.

Attending the opening ceremony were Ding Xilin, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, Feng Jiping, Vice Mayor of Beijing, He Chengxiang, Vice President of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, and Jerzy Knothe, Polish Ambassador to China.

Ding Xilin spoke at the opening ceremony, praising the achievements of Polish print art and wishing for the increasing consolidation and development of Sino-Polish friendship and cultural cooperation. Polish painter Winiecki-Raczewicz then spoke, introducing the development and achievements of Polish print art since the founding of the Polish People's Republic.

Also attending the opening ceremony were famous Chinese painters and art workers. Diplomats and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend.

### 介绍波兰社会主义建设成就  波兰版画展览会在京开幕

1960-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    介绍波兰社会主义建设成就
    波兰版画展览会在京开幕
    新华社22日讯　波兰人民共和国版画展览会今天上午在中山公园水榭开幕。这个展览会是对外文化联络委员会和中波友好协会为庆祝波兰人民共和国国庆日而举办的。
    展览会上展出的一百多幅优秀的版画作品，介绍了兄弟的波兰人民在社会主义建设中的忘我的劳动热情和取得的巨大成就，反映了波兰人民反对帝国主义的斗争，并且有力地揭露了以美国为首的帝国主义战争集团的战争阴谋。
    出席开幕式的，有对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林、外交部副部长罗贵波、文化部副部长夏衍、北京市副市长冯基平、中波友好协会副会长何成湘等，以及波兰驻中国大使耶日·克诺泰。
    丁西林在开幕式上讲话，赞扬波兰版画艺术的成就，祝中波友谊和文化合作日益巩固和发展。波兰画家维尼茨基—拉捷维奇接着讲话，他介绍了波兰版画艺术在波兰人民共和国成立以来的发展情况和所获得的成就。
    参加开幕式的还有中国著名的画家和美术工作者。各国驻中国的使节和外交官员也应邀出席。
