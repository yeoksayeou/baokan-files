Title: Khrushchev Receives Raúl Castro
Author:
Date: 1960-07-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 18th: According to TASS, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the Minister of Revolutionary Armed Forces of the Republic of Cuba, Raúl Castro, who is visiting the Soviet Union, on the 18th and had a friendly conversation with him. Mikoyan was present at the meeting.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见劳尔·卡斯特罗

1960-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见劳尔·卡斯特罗
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科18日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫18日接见了到苏联访问的、古巴共和国革命武装部部长劳尔·卡斯特罗，并且同他进行了友好的交谈。接见时在座的有米高扬。
