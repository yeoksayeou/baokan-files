International Briefs

    American "Atlas" Missile Launch Fails
    Cape Canaveral (USA) dispatch: On July 1, the United States' so-called intercontinental missile "Atlas" launch failed. The missile exploded into a cloud of smoke and fire only a hundred meters after leaving the launch pad.

    West Germany Forcibly Conscripts Another 30,000 New Soldiers
    Bonn dispatch: The West German Ministry of Defense announced that it will conscript another 33,000 new soldiers in the first few days of July. According to statistics published by the West German Ministry of Defense, after conscripting 33,000 people, the West German "Bundeswehr" has a total of 269,000 people, including 168,000 in the army, 62,000 in the air force, and 24,000 in the navy.

### 国际简讯

1960-07-03
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    美国发射“大力神”导弹失败
    卡纳维拉尔角（美国）讯：美国1日发射所谓洲际导弹“大力神”失败，导弹离开发射台只有一百多米就爆炸成一团烟火。
    西德又强召三万多新兵
    波恩讯：西德国防部通知说，在7月头几天又要征召三万三千名新兵入伍。据西德国防部发表的统计资料，在征召了三万三千人入伍以后，西德“国防军”共有二十六万九千人，其中陆军为十六万八千人，空军为六万二千人，海军为二万四千人。
