Title: Nkrumah Receives Ke Hua
Author:
Date: 1960-07-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Accra, July 2nd: Ghanaian President Nkrumah today received Ke Hua, the Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, who was here to attend the founding ceremony of the Republic of Ghana.

Ambassador Ke Hua presented President Nkrumah with congratulatory letters from Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Soong Ching Ling. He congratulated President Nkrumah on behalf of the Chinese government on the founding of the Republic of Ghana and congratulated him on becoming the first president of the Republic of Ghana. President Nkrumah expressed his gratitude for the congratulatory letters from the Chinese leaders and the congratulations from the Chinese Ambassador.

They held friendly talks during the meeting.

### 恩克鲁玛接见柯华

1960-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    恩克鲁玛接见柯华
    新华社阿克拉2日电　加纳共和国总统恩克鲁玛今天接见了到这里参加加纳共和国成立典礼的中国驻几内亚大使柯华。
    柯华大使向恩克鲁玛总统递交了中华人民共和国主席刘少奇、总理周恩来以及宋庆龄的贺信。他代表中国政府向恩克鲁玛总统祝贺加纳共和国的成立，祝贺他担任加纳共和国第一任总统。恩克鲁玛总统表示感谢中国领导人的贺信和中国大使的祝贺。
    在接见时他们进行了友好的交谈。
