Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet for Afghan Royal Music and Singing Troupe
Author:
Date: 1960-07-25
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: Chu Tunan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Afghan Royal Music and Singing Troupe. Abdul Brushena, head of the singing troupe, and all members of the troupe attended the banquet. The banquet was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere, and the guests repeatedly toasted the friendship between China and Afghanistan.
    Lin Lin, Director of the Department of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Wang Hui, Deputy Manager of the China Performing Arts Agency, and Zhao Feng and other figures from the capital's literary and art circles were present at the banquet.
    Hao Ting, China's Ambassador to the Kingdom of Afghanistan, and Abdul Samad, the Kingdom of Afghanistan's Ambassador to China, were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 楚图南欢宴阿富汗王国音乐歌唱团

1960-07-25
第6版()
专栏：

    楚图南欢宴阿富汗王国音乐歌唱团
    新华社24日讯　对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南今晚设宴欢迎阿富汗王国音乐歌唱团。歌唱团团长阿卜杜勒·布里希纳和全体团员出席了宴会。宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行，宾主频频为中阿两国的友谊而干杯。
    出席宴会作陪的有对外文委司长林林，中国演出公司副经理王回和首都文艺界人士赵沨等。
    我国驻阿富汗王国大使郝汀和阿富汗王国驻华大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德也应邀出席了宴会。
