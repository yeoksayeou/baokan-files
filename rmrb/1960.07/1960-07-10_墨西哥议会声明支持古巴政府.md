Title: Mexican Parliament Declares Support for Cuban Government
Author:
Date: 1960-07-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, July 9th -- Latin American News Agency reports that the Mexican House of Representatives and Senate, through their Standing Committees, issued a statement on July 7th reaffirming their full support for the Cuban revolutionary government led by Prime Minister Castro. The statement, issued by Emilio Sánchez Piedras, Chairman of the Standing Committee, said that at a time when the United States is adopting an unfriendly attitude towards the Cuban people, who yearn for freedom and economic independence, we reaffirm our support for the Cuban people.

The statement said that the Cuban people's right to demand social justice and economic freedom will surely take root in Latin America, and you are the hope for peace and mutual friendship.

### 墨西哥议会声明支持古巴政府

1960-07-10
第6版()
专栏：

    墨西哥议会声明支持古巴政府
    新华社9日讯　据拉丁美洲通讯社自墨西哥城报道，墨西哥众议院和参议院7日通过它们的常务委员会发表声明，重申它们完全支持卡斯特罗总理领导的古巴革命政府。这个由常务委员会主席埃米略·桑切斯·彼德拉斯发表的声明说，正当美国对渴望过自由和经济独立生活的古巴人民采取不友好的态度的时候，我们向古巴人民重申我们的支持。
    声明说，古巴人民要求社会正义和经济自由的权利一定会在拉丁美洲扎下根，你们是希望和平和互相友好的。
