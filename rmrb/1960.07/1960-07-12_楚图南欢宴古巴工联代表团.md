Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet for Cuban Labor Union Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-07-12
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 11th—Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, held a luncheon on the 9th to welcome the Cuban Workers' Confederation delegation, led by Odon Alvarez de la Campa, Secretary of Foreign Relations of the Cuban Workers' Confederation, who were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to visit our country. Odon Alvarez de la Campa is also the Vice President of the Cuban-Chinese Friendship Association, which was recently established in Havana, the capital of Cuba. Before the luncheon, Chu Tunan met with all the members of the delegation and presented gifts on behalf of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association.

### 楚图南欢宴古巴工联代表团

1960-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　楚图南欢宴古巴工联代表团
　　新华社11日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南在9日举行午宴，欢迎应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的以古巴工联对外关系书记奥东·阿伐莱斯·德·拉·坎巴为首的古巴工人联合会代表团。奥东·阿伐莱斯·德·拉·坎巴也是最近在古巴首都哈瓦那成立的古巴中国友好协会的副主席。在午宴前，楚图南会见了代表团全体人员，并代表中拉友协赠送了礼物。
