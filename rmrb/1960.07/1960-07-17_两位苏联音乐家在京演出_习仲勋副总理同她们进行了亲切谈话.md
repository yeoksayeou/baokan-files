Title: Two Soviet Musicians Perform in Beijing, Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Holds Cordial Talk with Them
Author:
Date: 1960-07-17
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, July 16th: Soviet pianist T. Nikolayeva and violinist Z. Shikhmurzaeva held their first concert in China tonight at the Capital Theater. Their brilliant performances brought great artistic enjoyment to the audience in the capital.

　　Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun attended the concert and met with the Soviet musicians during the intermission for a cordial conversation.

　　T. Nikolayeva, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic and multiple international competition winner, played Beethoven's Sonata, Liszt's Hungarian Rhapsody, other famous composers' works, and her own works tonight with consummate skill. Her performance was warmly applauded by the audience, and she took several curtain calls and performed three encores. Z. Shikhmurzaeva, who won a gold medal in the violin competition at the 6th World Youth Festival, also received praise from the audience for her performance of works by Tchaikovsky and other famous composers tonight; she performed two encores to the applause of the audience. After the performances of both musicians were completed, Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun and others walked onto the stage to congratulate them on the success of their performances.

　　Also attending tonight's concert were Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Deputy Directors Zhang Zhixiang and Ding Xilin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ge Baoquan, and members of the music community in the capital. Counselor Sudarikov of the Soviet Embassy in China attended the concert.

### 两位苏联音乐家在京演出　习仲勋副总理同她们进行了亲切谈话

1960-07-17
第6版()
专栏：

　　两位苏联音乐家在京演出
　　习仲勋副总理同她们进行了亲切谈话
    新华社16日讯　苏联钢琴家塔·尼古拉耶娃和小提琴家佐·施赫木尔扎耶娃，今晚在首都剧场举行访华首次音乐会。她们的精采的演奏为首都听众带来了极大的艺术享受。
    国务院副总理习仲勋出席了音乐会，并在演出休息期间接见了苏联音乐家，进行了亲切的谈话。
    塔·尼古拉耶娃是俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国功勋演员、国际比赛多次获奖者，今晚她以纯熟的技巧演奏了贝多芬的奏鸣曲，李斯特的匈牙利狂想曲和其他著名作曲家的作品以及她本人的作品。她的演奏受到了听众的热烈的鼓掌欢迎，曾多次谢幕，还加演了三次。佐·施赫木尔扎耶娃在第六届世界青年联欢节的小提琴比赛中曾荣获金质奖章，今晚她演奏的柴可夫斯基等著名作曲家的作品，也都受到听众的赞美；她在听众的鼓掌欢迎下加演了两次。两位音乐家的演奏全部结束后，习仲勋副总理等走上主席台，祝贺她们演出的成功。
    出席今晚音乐会的，还有对外文委主任张奚若，副主任张致祥、丁西林，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权以及首都音乐界人士等。苏联驻中国大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫出席了音乐会。
