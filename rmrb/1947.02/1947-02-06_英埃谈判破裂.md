Title: Anglo-Egyptian Negotiations Break Down
Author:
Date: 1947-02-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 30th. Reports from various sources: The Anglo-Egyptian negotiations, which have lasted for ten months, have finally broken down. Egyptian Prime Minister Nokrashy declared on the 26th that the Egyptian government had formally ceased negotiations with the British on amending the 1922 and 1936 treaties and decided to submit all outstanding issues between Britain and Egypt to the United Nations Security Council.

### 英埃谈判破裂

1947-02-06
第4版()
专栏：

    英埃谈判破裂
    【新华社延安三十日电】综合各方讯：持续十个月的英埃修的谈判，已最后破裂。埃总理诺克雷希于二十六日宣称：埃政府已正式停止英埃间关于修改一九二二年，及一九三六年条约的谈判。并决定将英埃间一切未决问题，提交联合国安全理事会。
