Title: Stalin's Sixty-Seventh Birthday: Leaders of Various Countries Send Special Telegrams of Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1946-12-26
Page: 1

　　Stalin's Sixty-Seventh Birthday
    Leaders of Various Countries Send Special Telegrams of Congratulations
    [Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 23rd] Reports from various sources: The 21st was the sixty-seventh birthday of Soviet leader Marshal Stalin. Leaders of various countries sent telegrams of congratulations, and newspapers in Berlin and Belgrade published articles offering birthday greetings.

### 斯大林六十七岁寿诞  各国政府领袖特驰电祝贺

1946-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　斯大林六十七岁寿诞
    各国政府领袖特驰电祝贺
    【新华社延安二十三日电】各方报导：二十一日为苏联元首斯大林元帅六十七岁寿诞，各国政府领袖特驰电致贺，柏林与贝尔格莱德等地的报纸并著论致祝词。
