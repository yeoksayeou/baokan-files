Title: Eat His Pawns Then Eat the Chariot
Author: Hu Cai
Date: 1946-12-10
Page: 3

　　Eat His Pawns Then Eat the Chariot
    Hu Cai
    Fighting the Chiang army is like playing chess,
    Eat his pawns then eat the chariot,
    Wipe out all his troops,
    Let's see where old Chiang can run!?

### 吃完他卒子再吃车
胡采
1946-12-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　吃完他卒子再吃车
    胡采
    打蒋军好比下象棋，
    吃完他卒子再吃车，
    全盘人马都吃尽，
    看他老蒋走那里！？
