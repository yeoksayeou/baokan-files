Title: Bian District Chairman Yang Sends Letter of Condolences to Hebei-Shandong-Henan Militia
Author:
Date: 1946-12-03
Page: 1

　　Bian District Chairman Yang
  　Sends Letter of Condolences to Hebei-Shandong-Henan Militia
    [Our newspaper, Hebei-Shandong-Henan, 1st dispatch] Chairman Yang of the Border Region Government deeply praised the development of guerrilla warfare and the heroic struggle of the militia in the Hebei-Shandong-Henan area, and sent a letter of condolences. The original letter stated: "I am very excited to come to the Hebei-Shandong-Henan Plain. I have heard that our vast militia is very actively cooperating with the army in combat, ... and disrupting the enemy in the enemy's rear, launching extensive guerrilla warfare, and creating many achievements. In my excitement, I would like to represent the Border Government in sending 200,000 yuan to comfort everyone, to show a small token of my appreciation. In the future, I hope you will work harder and more actively carry out guerrilla warfare, ... and strive for the final victory of the patriotic self-defense war. ... I extend my deep condolences to the wounded militia..." Comrade Qiao Mingfu of the area also sent a reply on behalf of all the militia to express their gratitude.

### 边府杨主席函慰冀鲁豫民兵

1946-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　边府杨主席
  　函慰冀鲁豫民兵
    【本报冀鲁豫一日电】边区政府杨主席对冀鲁豫区游击战争的开展和民兵的英勇奋斗深为赞誉，特来信慰问。原信称：“我来到冀鲁豫平原，感到非常兴奋，听说我们广大民兵非常踊跃的配合军队作战，……并在敌人后方扰乱敌人，开展了广泛的游击战争，创造了不少的成绩，我兴奋之余，谨代表边府送上二十万元慰劳大家，以示微意。今后我希望你们更加努力，更积极的开展游击战争，……争取爱国自卫战争的最后胜利。……对负伤的民兵致以深切的慰问……。”该区乔明甫同志并代表全体民兵复函致谢。
