Title: Opening of the Thirteenth Congress of the French Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1954-06-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, June 3rd: TASS reported from Paris on June 3rd that the Thirteenth Congress of the French Communist Party opened on the 3rd in Ivry, a suburb of Paris and an industrial area on the Seine River. Nine hundred and sixty delegates from the provincial committees of the Communist Party attended the congress. Guests included representatives from the Communist Parties of Britain, Belgium, Denmark, West Germany, Norway, Austria, Argentina, Sweden, Israel, and other countries.

The agenda of the congress included four items:

	I. Report of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party;
	II. Issues of party organizational work;
	III. The Communist Party and youth;
	IV. Election of the Central Committee and the Financial Supervisory Committee.

The delegates elected the presidium of the congress. The presidium included leaders of the French Communist Party and the workers' movement such as Maurice Thorez, Jacques Duclos, Marcel Cachin, Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Benoît Frachon, and Laurent Casanova.

After electing the political, organizational, youth, and credentials committees, Jacques Duclos delivered a report on the first item on the agenda.

### 法国共产党第十三次代表大会开幕

1954-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    法国共产党第十三次代表大会开幕
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社巴黎三日讯：法国共产党第十三次代表大会于三日在塞纳河上的依夫列——巴黎近郊的一个工业区——开幕。参加大会的有共产党各省省委员会的九百六十名代表，到会的来宾有英国、比利时、丹麦、西德、挪威、奥地利、阿根廷、瑞典、以色列等国共产党的代表。
    大会的议程有四项：
    一、法国共产党中央委员会的报告；
    二、党的组织工作问题；
    三、共产党和青年；
    四、选举中央委员会和财政监察委员会。
    代表们选出了大会主席团。主席团包括莫里斯·多列士、雅克·杜克洛、马赛尔·加香、弗雷德里克·约里奥—居里、贝诺瓦·弗拉商、洛朗·加桑诺瓦等法国共产党和工人运动的领袖。
    在选举了政治、组织、青年、资格审查各委员会以后，雅克·杜克洛就第一项议程作了报告。
