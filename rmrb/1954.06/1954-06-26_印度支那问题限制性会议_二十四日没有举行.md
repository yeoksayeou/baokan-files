Title: Restrictive Conference on Indochina Question Did Not Take Place on the 24th
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-26
Page: 4

    Restrictive Conference on Indochina Question
    Did Not Take Place on the 24th
    [Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 24th] It is reported that, apart from contacts between various delegations, the restrictive conference on the Indochina question, originally scheduled for the 24th, did not take place.

### 印度支那问题限制性会议  二十四日没有举行

1954-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

    印度支那问题限制性会议
    二十四日没有举行
    【新华社日内瓦二十四日电】据悉：除了各国代表团之间的接触外，原定于二十四日举行的讨论印度支那问题的限制性会议未举行。
