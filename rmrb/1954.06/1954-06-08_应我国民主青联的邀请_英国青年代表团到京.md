Title: British Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing at the Invitation of the All-China Youth Federation

Author:
Date: 1954-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. At the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation, the British Youth Delegation arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 7th for a visit to our country.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Qu Tangliang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation; Yu Beichen, Secretary-General; Wu Xueqian, Director of the International Liaison Department; Wang Zizhong, Pastor of the North China Congregational Church; Zhao Fusan, General Secretary of the North China Diocese of the Anglican Church; Chen Gengfu, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the China Coal Mine Workers' Union; Nie Qihua, representative of the Preparatory Committee of the China First Machinery Trade Union; Zhang Wenhao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation; and Guan Shixiong, Vice-Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Youth Federation, and others.

As the plane carrying the delegation slowly landed, the welcoming crowd burst into warm applause and cheers. Young Pioneers from the capital, dressed in colorful costumes, enthusiastically presented flowers and shook hands with the guests.

Qu Tangliang delivered a speech at the airport, warmly welcoming the delegation. John Woods, the head of the delegation, gave a reply expressing his thanks. He said that we all very much yearn for the new China, and are very happy to be able to visit the new China this time. He also said: This visit will strengthen the friendship between the youth of Britain and China.

### 应我国民主青联的邀请  英国青年代表团到京

1954-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    应我国民主青联的邀请
    英国青年代表团到京
    【新华社七日讯】　应中华全国民主青年联合会的邀请前来我国访问的英国青年代表团，在七日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中华全国民主青年联合会副主席区棠亮、秘书长于北辰、国际联络部部长吴学谦，华北中华基督教公理会牧师王梓仲，中华圣公会华北教区总干事赵复三，中国煤矿工会全国委员会副主席陈耕夫，中国第一机械工会筹备委员会代表聂其华，中华全国学生联合会副主席张文浩和北京市民主青年联合会副主席关世雄等。
    当载着代表团的客机徐徐着陆时，欢迎的人们立刻响起了热烈的掌声和欢呼声。穿着彩色服装的首都少年先锋队队员们热情地向宾客们献花、握手。
    区棠亮在机场致词，热烈欢迎代表团。代表团团长约翰·伍兹致答词表示感谢。他说，我们都非常向往新中国，这次能来到新中国访问感到十分高兴。他又说：这次访问将加强英中两国青年间的友谊。
