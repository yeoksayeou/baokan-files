Title: British Foreign Secretary Eden Hosts Banquet for Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-06-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 1st: British Foreign Secretary Eden hosted a banquet at 8:00 PM on June 1st to entertain Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. Those attending from the Chinese side included Zhang Wentian and Huan Xiang. Those accompanying from the British side included Cathia, Allen, and Derville.

### 英国外交大臣艾登设宴招待周恩来外长

1954-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

    英国外交大臣艾登设宴招待周恩来外长
    【新华社日内瓦一日电】英国外交大臣艾登一日下午八时设宴招待中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来。中国方面应邀出席的还有张闻天、宦乡。英国方面出席作陪的有卡西亚、艾伦、杜维廉等。
