Title: Molotov Receives Nam Il
Author:
Date: 1954-06-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 26th: TASS, Moscow: On June 25th, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, V.M. Molotov, received the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Nam Il. Nam Il was passing through Moscow on his way home from Geneva.

### 莫洛托夫接见南日

1954-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫接见南日
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：六月二十五日，苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫接见了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日。南日是从日内瓦回国途中经过莫斯科的。
