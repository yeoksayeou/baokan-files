Title: Negotiations Held Between Vietnamese and French Delegation Representatives; Agreement Reached on the Issue of Letters Regarding the Exchange of Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1954-06-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 1st. The delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued the following communiqué on the afternoon of June 1st: "Representatives of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Geneva Conference, He Wenlou, and representatives of the French delegation, Colonel Debrebisson, after negotiations, have reached an agreement on the issue of exchanging letters sent to prisoners of war by both sides and letters sent by prisoners of war from both sides.
    Regarding the issue of transporting medicines and exchanging seriously ill and wounded prisoners of war, representatives of both delegations have reached an agreement in principle on such exchanges. However, in view of the current military situation, the methods for implementing these measures can only be determined after representatives of the General Headquarters of both sides in Indochina exchange opinions."

### 越南代表团和法国代表团代表举行谈判  双方已就关于交换战俘的信件问题达成协议

1954-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

    越南代表团和法国代表团代表举行谈判
    双方已就关于交换战俘的信件问题达成协议
    【新华社日内瓦一日电】越南民主共和国代表团一日下午发表公报如下：“越南民主共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团代表何文楼和法国代表团代表德布勒比松上校进行谈判后，已经就交换寄交双方战俘和双方战俘发出的信件问题达成了协议。
    至于运送药品和交换重病、重伤战俘问题，双方代表团代表已在原则上就这种交换达成协议。但鉴于目前的军事情况，执行这种措施的办法只能经过在印度支那的双方总司令部代表交换意见后才能确定。”
