Title: Burmese President Hosts Banquet for Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1954-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 29th: The President of the Union of Burma, Ba U, hosted a banquet at 7:45 PM on the 28th to entertain Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China.

Among those accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai to Burma who attended the banquet were Qiao Guanhua, Wang Zhuoru, and five others. Yao Zhongming, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, also attended the banquet.

Burmese government officials attending the banquet included: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of the Burmese Parliament Sao Hkun Hkio, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Bo Mu Aung, Chief Justice U Thein Maung, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs U Khin Maung, Minister for Chin Affairs U Hkun Htee, Minister for Kachin State Affairs Duwa Zau Lawn, Minister for Karen State Affairs U Aung Pa, Minister for Kayah State Affairs Sao Wunna, Minister for Transport and Communications Mann Win Maung, Minister for Religious Affairs and concurrently Minister of Culture U Win, Minister for Finance and Revenue U Tin, Minister for Home Affairs Bo Khin Maung Gale, Minister for Agriculture and Forests Thakin Kyaw Dun, Minister for Health U Khin Maung Latt, Minister for Land Nationalization Thakin Tin, Minister for Commerce Thakin Tha Khin, Minister for Rehabilitation and Reconstruction Bo Min Gaung, Commander-in-Chief of the Burmese Armed Forces Lieutenant General Bo Ne Win, Supreme Court Justice U Myint Thein, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Thant Sin, Secretary of the President's Office U Ba Sein, and others.

Foreign envoys to Burma attending the banquet included: Indian Ambassador to Burma K.K. Chettur, British Ambassador Paul Henry Gore-Booth, and Soviet Chargé d'affaires Semyon P. Djukarev.

N.R. Pillai, Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, who accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai back from India, also attended the banquet.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly and cordial atmosphere.

Following the banquet, Burmese music and dance performances were enjoyed, concluding at 11:00 PM.

### 缅甸总统设宴招待周总理

1954-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸总统设宴招待周总理
    【新华社二十九日讯】仰光消息：缅甸联邦总统巴宇在二十八日下午七时四十五分举行宴会，招待中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来。
    随同周恩来总理到缅甸的人员中参加宴会的有乔冠华、王倬如等七人。中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明也参加了宴会。
    缅甸政府官员参加宴会的有：缅甸总理吴努，缅甸联邦国会民族院议长萧恢塔，代表院议长波木昂，首席法官吴登貌，外交部代理部长吴觉迎，钦族事务部部长吴幸丹，克钦邦事务部部长杜瓦藻朗，克伦邦事务部部长吴昂帕，加雅邦事务部部长萧翁纳，运输与交通部部长曼温貌，宗教事务部部长兼文化部部长吴温，财政与税务部部长吴阵，内政部部长波庆貌格礼，农林部部长德钦觉顿，卫生部部长吴钦貌腊，土地国有化部部长德钦阵，商业部部长德钦达庆，恢复重建部部长波敏贡，缅甸军总司令波尼温中将，最高法院法官吴敏登，外交部常任秘书吴顿辛，总统府秘书吴巴盛等。
    参加宴会的外国驻缅甸使节包括：印度驻缅甸大使克·克·契图尔，英国大使保罗·亨利·戈尔—布斯，苏联代办谢米昂·普·久卡列夫。
    自印度伴送周恩来总理回国的印度外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱也参加了宴会。
    宴会在友好和亲切的空气中进行。
    宴会后并观赏了缅甸音乐舞蹈表演，到晚间十一时结束。
