Title: Conditions for US Intervention in Indochina; British Newspapers Report US-French Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-06-06
Page: 4

    On the Conditions for US Intervention in Indochina
    British Newspapers Report US-French Agreement
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 5th] TASS, London, June 4th: The British "Daily Mail" prominently published an article by its Paris correspondent saying: "France and the United States have now reached a practical agreement on the conditions for US intervention in Indochina. Several weeks of diplomatic negotiations between Paris and Washington have made great progress. Both France and the United States are keeping the agreement, which is said to be of a long-term nature, strictly secret."

### 关于美国干涉印度支那的条件  英国报纸报道美法达成协议

1954-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    关于美国干涉印度支那的条件
    英国报纸报道美法达成协议
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社伦敦四日讯：英国“每日邮报”以显著的地位登载了该报驻巴黎记者的一篇文章说：“现在，法国和美国已就美国干涉印度支那的条件达成了实际的协议。在巴黎和华盛顿之间进行了几星期的外交谈判，已经有了很大的进展。法国和美国双方对据说是有着长远性质的协议都严守秘密。”
