Title: Central Institute for Nationalities Warmly Celebrates its Third Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1954-06-13
Page: 3
(Xinhua News Agency)

    The Central Institute for Nationalities warmly celebrated its third anniversary on June 11th.
    More than 1,000 people attended the celebration, including Ulanhu, President of the Central Institute for Nationalities, Vice Presidents Liu Geping, Liu Chun, Fei Xiaotong, and all faculty, students and staff.
    Invited guests included Li Weihan, Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Vice Chairmen Wang Feng and Zhang Zhiyi, Vice Minister of Higher Education Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of Education Wei Que, and all the representatives attending the Fourth Enlarged Meeting of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central Committee, which was being held in Beijing.
    Representatives from relevant units such as the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Government Office in Beijing, the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing, and alumni of the institute were also invited to celebrate the festival together.
    At 3:00 p.m., the commemorative meeting began solemnly. At the meeting, President Ulanhu reported on the development of the institute in the past three years and the tasks that the Central Institute for Nationalities should undertake during the national transition period. He called on the students to earnestly study Marxism-Leninism and the Party's nationality policy, and improve their theoretical level, in order to adapt to the needs of the entire national construction. He asked all faculty, students and staff to unite as one, work actively, and study hard, to run the Central Institute for Nationalities well and to cultivate more and better ethnic minority cadres.
    Then, student Chen Wanling spoke on behalf of the students of more than 40 ethnic groups in the institute. She thanked the Communist Party and Chairman Mao for their care and the state for training ethnic minority cadres. On behalf of all the students, she pledged to resolutely respond to Chairman Mao's call for "three goods" (good in health, good in studies, good in work), to train themselves to become talented people who love the motherland and are loyal to the people, and to become strong fighters for national construction.
    The guests and students visited the ethnic minority cultural relics exhibition and the photo exhibition held in the institute. The cultural relics exhibition room displayed handicrafts, production and living items of more than 20 ethnic groups, fully demonstrating different ethnic styles and illustrating the labor and wisdom of the ethnic minority people. In the photo exhibition, one could see the progress of the institute in the past three years and its activities in various fields. The pictures of the whole institute enthusiastically participating in the general election fully showed the preciousness and love of the ethnic minorities for their political rights.
    In the evening, there were also wonderful Peking Opera, movies and dances.

### 中央民族学院热烈庆祝成立三周年

1954-06-13
第3版()
专栏：

    中央民族学院热烈庆祝成立三周年
    中央民族学院在六月十一日热烈地庆祝了学院成立三周年校庆。
    参加校庆的有中央民族学院院长乌兰夫、副院长刘格平、刘春、费孝通和全校师生员工共一千多人。
    应邀参加校庆的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉，副主任委员汪锋、张执一，高等教育部副部长曾昭抡，教育部副部长韦悫，以及正在北京出席中央民族事务委员会第四次扩大会议的全体代表等。
    内蒙古自治区人民政府驻京办事处、西藏达赖驻京办事处、西藏班禅驻京办事处等有关单位的代表，以及本院的校友等也应邀前来共同欢度了这个节日。
    下午三点钟，纪念大会隆重开始。会上乌兰夫院长报告了学院三年来的发展和在国家过渡时期中央民族学院应该担负的任务。他号召学生们认真学习马克思列宁主义以及党的民族政策，提高理论水平，以适应整个国家建设的需要。他要求全体师生员工团结一致，积极工作，努力学习，为办好中央民族学院与培养出更多更好的民族干部而努力。
    接着，学生陈畹玲代表全院四十多个民族的同学讲话，她感谢共产党和毛主席的关怀和国家对少数民族干部的培养。她代表全院同学提出保证，坚决响应毛主席的“三好”号召，把自己锻炼成为热爱祖国忠于人民的德才兼备的人才，成为国家建设的坚强战士。
    来宾和同学们参观了院内举办的少数民族文物展览和图片展览。文物展览室陈列的二十多个民族的工艺品生产和生活物品等，充分地表现了不同的民族风格，说明了少数民族人民的劳动智慧。在图片展览中，可以看到学院三年来的进展和各方面的活动。全院热烈参加普选的图片，充分显示着少数民族对自己政治权利的珍贵和热爱。
    晚上还举行了精采的京剧、电影和舞会。
                            （据新华社讯）
