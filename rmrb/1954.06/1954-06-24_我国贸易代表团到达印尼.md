Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Arrives in Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1954-06-24
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 23rd. The Chinese trade delegation, led by head of delegation Kong Yuan, arrived in Jakarta on the 22nd at the invitation of the Republic of Indonesia to further discuss and arrange the Sino-Indonesian trade agreement signed last November and the Indonesian government.

    Welcoming them at the port were Indonesian Ministry of Economic Affairs Secretary-General Sumano, Ghana Magama Agam of the Legal and Economic Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and other Indonesian government officials.

    Also welcoming them at the port were Zhong Qingfa, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Republic of Indonesia, Zhao Zhongshi, Chinese Consul General in Jakarta, other personnel from the Chinese Embassy and Consulate, and representatives of overseas Chinese in Indonesia.

    Personnel from the Czechoslovakian Consulate General and personnel from the trade delegations of Romania, Hungary, and the German Democratic Republic also came to welcome them.

    Kong Yuan made a speech before disembarking. The speech mentioned: "There is a long history of relations between the people of China and Indonesia. Both countries are rich in products and have many commodities available for exchange. There are good conditions for developing trade. We believe that developing Sino-Indonesian trade is beneficial to the economic prosperity of both countries and the improvement of people's lives; it is also of great significance for enhancing the friendship between the two peoples and for safeguarding Asian and world peace."

### 我国贸易代表团到达印尼

1954-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我国贸易代表团到达印尼
    【新华社雅加达二十三日电】应印尼共和国邀请去印尼就去年十一月签订的中印（尼）贸易协定和印尼政府作进一步商谈和安排的我国贸易代表团，在团长孔原率领下在二十二日到达雅加达。
    到港口欢迎的有印尼经济部秘书长苏马诺、外交部法律经济司的加纳马·阿加姆以及印尼共和国其他政府官员。
    到港口欢迎的还有我国驻印尼共和国大使馆临时代办锺庆发、我国驻雅加达总领事赵仲时、我国使、领馆其他人员以及印尼华侨代表。
    捷克斯洛伐克总领事馆人员和罗马尼亚、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国的贸易代表团人员也前去欢迎。
    孔原在登岸前发表谈话。谈话中提到：“中国和印尼的人民之间有着悠久的历史关系，两国物产都很丰富，可供交换的商品甚多，有发展贸易的良好条件。我们认为发展中印（尼）的贸易，对于两国经济的繁荣与人民生活的提高是有利的；对增进两国人民之间的友谊和对于维护亚洲和世界和平事业亦具有重大意义。”
