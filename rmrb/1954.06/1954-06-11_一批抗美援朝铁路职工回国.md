Title: A Batch of Railway Workers Returning from the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea

Author:

Date: 1954-06-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 7th. Another batch of 107 railway workers from the Jinan Railway Bureau who participated in the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea returned to Jinan by train on June 3rd. More than 400 railway workers and their families from the Jinan area held a welcome meeting at the train station square. Fu Shiyan, a representative of the returning workers, stated at the meeting: We will resolutely obey the needs of the motherland and continue to work hard to build a socialist motherland in our new posts.

### 一批抗美援朝铁路职工回国

1954-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    一批抗美援朝铁路职工回国
   【新华社济南七日电】济南铁路管理局志愿抗美援朝职工又一批一百零七人，在六月三日乘车回到济南。济南地区的铁路职工及其家属共四百多人在车站广场上举行了欢迎会。归国职工代表傅世彦在会上表示：坚决服从祖国需要，在新的工作岗位上，继续为建设社会主义的祖国而努力。
