Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with French Premier to Discuss Issues Concerning the Restoration of Peace in Indochina
Author:
Date: 1954-06-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China met with French Premier Mendès France at the French Embassy in Bern at 3:00 PM on June 23rd. Premier Zhou Enlai and Premier Mendès France engaged in a free exchange of views on issues concerning the restoration of peace in Indochina. The results of the talks allow them to expect progress at the Geneva Conference.
    Participating in the talks on the French side were Chauvel, Roux, and Guirma; on the Chinese side were Li Kenong, Feng Xuan, and Huan Xiang.

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 23rd: Huang Hua, spokesperson for the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, held a press conference at the Chinese Embassy in Switzerland in Bern on the afternoon of June 23rd. He briefed the attending journalists on the meeting between Premier Zhou Enlai and French Premier Mendès France and answered questions from the reporters.

### 周恩来总理会见法国总理  就有关恢复印度支那和平问题进行了交谈

1954-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理会见法国总理
    就有关恢复印度支那和平问题进行了交谈
    【新华社日内瓦二十三日电】中华人民共和国总理周恩来和法国总理孟戴斯—弗朗斯在六月二十三日下午三时在伯尔尼法国大使馆会晤。周恩来总理和孟戴斯—弗朗斯总理就有关恢复印度支那和平问题进行了自由的交谈。谈话的结果使他们能够期望日内瓦会议获得进展。
    参加会谈者，法国方面还有萧维尔、鲁克斯、吉埃马等；中国方面还有李克农、冯铉、宦乡等。
    【新华社日内瓦二十三日电】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团发言人黄华二十三日下午在伯尔尼中华人民共和国驻瑞士公使馆举行记者招待会，向到会记者介绍了周恩来总理和法国总理孟戴斯—弗朗斯会晤的情况，并回答了记者提出的问题。
