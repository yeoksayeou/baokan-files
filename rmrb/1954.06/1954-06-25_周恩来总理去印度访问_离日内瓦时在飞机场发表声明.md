Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Visits India, Issues Statement at Geneva Airport Upon Departure
Author:
Date: 1954-06-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 24th: Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the People's Republic of China Delegation to the Geneva Conference, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, departed Geneva by plane on the morning of the 24th for a visit to India at the invitation of Indian Prime Minister Nehru.

Those who saw him off at the airport included: Delegation representatives Li Kenong, Secretary-General Wang Bingnan, and advisors Lei Renmin, Shi Zhe, Chen Jiakang, Ke Bonian, Huan Xiang, Huang Hua, and Gong Peng.

Also present at the airport to see him off were: Soviet Delegation Representative and Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Kuznetsov, advisors Fedlin and Novikov, Democratic Republic of Vietnam Delegation Chief Representative Pham Van Dong, and representatives Phan Anh and Tran Cong Xiang.

Also present at the airport to see him off were: Feng Xuan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Switzerland, and Wen Pengjiu, Consul General in Geneva, and Sama Sen, Consul General of India in Geneva.

Premier Zhou Enlai issued a statement at the airport. The full text of the statement is as follows:

    As I am about to temporarily leave Geneva, I would like to sincerely thank the Swiss government and people and the authorities of the city of Geneva for their thoughtful hospitality to the Delegation of the People's Republic of China.

    The Geneva Conference is still in progress. Peace-loving people of all countries hope that our work will ultimately lead to the restoration of peace in Indochina, and the Delegation of the People's Republic of China is willing to continue to work towards this end.

Furthermore: Representatives of the Chinese Delegation, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian and Wang Jiaxiang, left Geneva on June 22nd. The Chinese Delegation, led by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Li Kenong, will continue the work of the conference.

### 周恩来总理去印度访问  离日内瓦时在飞机场发表声明

1954-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理去印度访问
    离日内瓦时在飞机场发表声明
    【新华社日内瓦二十四日电】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团首席代表、中华人民共和国总理兼外交部部长周恩来应印度政府尼赫鲁总理的邀请，于二十四日上午乘飞机离日内瓦，前往印度进行访问。
    到机场欢送的有：代表团代表李克农，秘书长王炳南，及顾问雷任民、师哲、陈家康、柯柏年、宦乡、黄华、龚澎。
    到机场欢送的还有：苏联代表团代表、苏联外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫，顾问费德林、诺维科夫，越南民主共和国代表团首席代表范文同及代表潘英、陈公祥。
    到机场欢送的还有：中华人民共和国驻瑞士公使冯铉及驻日内瓦总领事温朋久，印度驻日内瓦总领事萨马·塞恩。
    周恩来总理在机场发表声明，声明全文如下：
    在我即将暂时离开日内瓦的时候，我愿诚挚地感谢瑞士政府和人民及日内瓦市当局对中华人民共和国代表团的周到的款待。
    日内瓦会议还在进行。爱好和平的各国人民希望我们的工作终将导致印度支那和平的恢 复，中华人民共和国代表团愿继续为此而努力。
    又讯：中国代表团代表、外交部副部长张闻天、王稼祥已于六月二十二日离日内瓦。中国代表团由外交部副部长李克农率领，继续会议工作。
