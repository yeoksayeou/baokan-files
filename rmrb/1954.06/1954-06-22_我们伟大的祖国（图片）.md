Author:

    Our Great Motherland
    The Dahuofang Reservoir project, aimed at permanently controlling the Hun River flood in Northeast China, has commenced construction. The dam of this project is 41 meters high and over 1,300 meters long, several times larger than the Guanting Reservoir dam, with a flood storage capacity of over 1.859 billion cubic meters. This picture shows the dam construction in progress.

    我们伟大的祖国
    根治东北浑河水患的大伙房水库工程，已经动工，这个工程的大坝有四十一公尺高，一千三百多公尺长，比官厅水库的大坝还要大数倍，能拦蓄洪水十八亿五千九百多万立方公尺。这是大坝施工的情况。
