Title: India-China Friendship Association Executive Committee Decides to Hold India-China Agreement Day
Author:
Date: 1954-06-02
Page: 4

    India-China Friendship Association Executive Committee
    Decides to Hold India-China Agreement Day
   [Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 1st] The Executive Committee of the India-China Friendship Association has decided to hold the "India-China Agreement Day Regarding Trade and Communication between the Tibet Region of China and India" on June 13th, to express their welcome for the signing of the agreement.

### 印中友好协会执行委员会  决定举行印中协定日

1954-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    印中友好协会执行委员会
    决定举行印中协定日
   【新华社新德里一日电】印中友好协会执行委员会决定在六月十三日举行“印中两国关于中国西藏地方和印度之间的通商和交通协定日”，以表示欢迎协定的签订。
