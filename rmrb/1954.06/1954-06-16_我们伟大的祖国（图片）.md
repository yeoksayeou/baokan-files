Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-06-16
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    People from all walks of life in Beijing warmly support the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China. Members of the "Zhangguozhuang Agricultural Production Cooperative" are taking a break from harvesting wheat to listen to Guo Rong, the director of the agricultural production cooperative, read the news of the promulgation of the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京市各界人民热烈拥护中华人民共和国宪法草案。“张郭庄农业生产合作社”的社员们利用收割小麦的休息时间，在听农业生产合作社主任郭荣读中华人民共和国宪法草案公布的消息。
