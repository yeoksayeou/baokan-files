Title: Chinese and Indian Prime Ministers Continue Talks on the 27th
Author:
Date: 1954-06-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 28th. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Indian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nehru continued their talks on the afternoon of the 27th from 3:00 PM to 5:30 PM.

### 中印两国总理二十七日继续会谈

1954-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中印两国总理二十七日继续会谈
    【新华社新德里二十八日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来和印度总理兼外交部部长尼赫鲁在二十七日下午三时至五时三十分继续举行会谈。
