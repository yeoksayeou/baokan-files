Title: Pyongyang-Beijing Direct Passenger Train Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-06-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: The first Pyongyang-Beijing direct passenger train from Pyongyang arrived at Beijing Railway Station at 10:20 PM on the 4th. Responsible individuals from the General Office of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, various bureaus, and the Beijing Railway Administration were at the station to welcome it.

Amidst the songs of the Chinese and Korean people and the applause of the welcomers, the direct passenger train entered the station. Welcomers stepped forward to warmly shake hands with the train crew, expressing their welcome.

The direct passenger train departed from Pyongyang at 12:00 noon (Korean time) on the 3rd, traveled smoothly along the way, and arrived at each station on schedule.

### 平壤—北京直通客车到京

1954-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    平壤—北京直通客车到京
   【新华社四日讯】从平壤开出的首次平壤—北京直通客车已在四日夜十时二十分到达北京车站。中央人民政府铁道部办公厅、各局和北京铁路管理局的负责人都到车站欢迎。
    直通客车在中朝两国人民的歌曲声中和欢迎者的掌声中开进车站，欢迎者上前和客车乘务人员亲切握手，表示欢迎。
    这次直通客车是在三日中午十二时（朝鲜时间）从平壤开出的，沿途行驶顺利，正点到达各车站。
