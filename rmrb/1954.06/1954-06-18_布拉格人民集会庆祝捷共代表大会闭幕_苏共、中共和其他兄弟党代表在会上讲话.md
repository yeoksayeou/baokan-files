Title: Prague Rally Celebrates Closing of Czechoslovak Communist Party Congress; Representatives of the Soviet, Chinese, and Other Fraternal Parties Speak at the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 16th: A grand rally was held in Prague, the capital of the Czechoslovak Republic, on the Staroměstské náměstí (Old Town Square) on the 15th to celebrate the closing of the Tenth Congress of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia.

The congress took place in a joyous atmosphere. As the newly elected members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, and the representatives of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and other fraternal Communist and Workers' Parties ascended to the platform, those present shouted "Hurrah" and "Long live the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia!"

The rally was presided over by Katětka, First Secretary of the Prague Regional Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia. Zápotocký spoke at the meeting, discussing the achievements of the Tenth Party Congress. He emphasized that the Czechoslovak people are now more closely united around the Party Central Committee than ever before.

The head of the Soviet Communist Party delegation, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, Khrushchev, spoke at the meeting. His speech was punctuated by enthusiastic applause and shouts of "Hurrah."

Liu Changsheng, the representative of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, also delivered a speech at the meeting. He first congratulated the Czechoslovak people. He then said: Under the leadership of the Communist Party, the Czechoslovak people have made great achievements in politics, economy, and culture. These achievements are an important contribution to the world peace and democracy camp headed by the great Soviet Union. I wish the Czechoslovak people further achievements in the cause of socialist construction, following the resolutions and instructions of the Party Congress. At the end of Liu Changsheng's speech, the entire venue erupted in enthusiastic applause.

Representatives of the Italian, French, and German Communist Parties also spoke at the meeting. Their speeches were also warmly welcomed.

### 布拉格人民集会庆祝捷共代表大会闭幕  苏共、中共和其他兄弟党代表在会上讲话

1954-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    布拉格人民集会庆祝捷共代表大会闭幕
    苏共、中共和其他兄弟党代表在会上讲话
    【新华社布拉格十六日电】为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克共产党第十次代表大会的闭幕，捷克斯洛伐克共和国首都布拉格人民十五日在斯塔罗米斯托广场上举行了盛大的集会。
    大会在欢腾的气氛中进行。当新选出的捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会委员和苏联共产党及其他兄弟共产党和工人党的代表走上主席台时，到会者高呼“乌拉”和“捷克斯洛伐克共产党万岁！”
    大会由捷克斯洛伐克共产党布拉格州委会第一书记卡捷尔卡主持。萨波托斯基在会上讲话，谈到了第十次党代表大会的成就。他着重指出：捷克斯洛伐克人民现在比过去任何时候都更加紧密地团结在党中央委员会的周围。
    苏联共产党代表团团长、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫在会上讲话。他的讲话时时为热烈的掌声和“乌拉”声所打断。
    中国共产党中央委员会的代表刘长胜也在会上致词。他首先向捷克斯洛伐克人民祝贺。他接着说：捷克斯洛伐克人民在共产党的领导下，在政治、经济和文化等方面取得了伟大的成就，这些成就对以伟大的苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营说来，是一个重要的贡献。我预祝捷克斯洛伐克人民遵循党代表大会的决议和指示，在社会主义建设的事业中取得进一步的成就。刘长胜致词毕，全场热烈鼓掌。
    在会上讲话的还有意大利、法国和德国共产党的代表。他们的讲话也受到了热烈的欢迎。
