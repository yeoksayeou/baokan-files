Title: Northwest Provinces' Draft Constitution Discussion Committees Actively Prepare to Publicize the Draft Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-06-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, June 14th: The Northwest region provinces are actively preparing for the publicity and discussion of the draft constitution. The Xinjiang Province Draft Constitution Discussion Committee was established on May 28th and held its first committee meeting on the same day. The meeting discussed the "Plan for Training Reporters and Propagandists for the Publicity of the Draft Constitution" and specifically arranged for the publicity and discussion of the draft constitution among all ethnic groups in the province. The Propaganda Department of the Xinjiang Sub-bureau of the CPC Central Committee also convened a meeting with provincial-level propaganda and education organs and mass organizations on June 2nd, in coordination with the office of the Provincial Draft Constitution Discussion Committee, to study the issues of discussing and publicizing the draft constitution in detail. Units such as the Xinjiang Provincial Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the Preparatory Committee of the Xinjiang Provincial Federation of Trade Unions have also formulated plans or issued instructions to mobilize their affiliated units to prepare for the publicity of the draft constitution. At present, most of the special districts and counties in the province have established draft constitution discussion committees. The reporter and propagandist training classes in most counties of the Urumqi and Hami special districts were launched before June 10th.

The Shaanxi Province Draft Constitution Discussion Committee was formally established on June 14th. Draft constitution discussion committees in Baoji, Weinan, and other special districts have also been established. Now, the Shaanxi Province Draft Constitution Discussion Committee has decided to send a group of responsible cadres to various special districts after June 15th to report on the draft constitution to local reporters and cadres of organs, schools, and industrial and mining enterprises.

The Ningxia Province Draft Constitution Discussion Committee was established on June 2nd. The joint meeting of the Provincial People's Government Committee and the Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee discussed and approved the "Plan to Mobilize People of All Ethnic Groups and All Circles in the Province to Enthusiastically Participate in the Discussion of the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" on June 12th, requesting all levels of organizations in the province to organize and lead all cadres to discuss the draft constitution according to the original learning system; and to organize people's representatives, reporters, propagandists, and all possible propaganda forces at all levels to go deep into rural areas, pastoral areas, factories, mines, streets, and schools to make publicity reports to the people and organize the masses to participate in the discussion. At present, the draft constitution discussion committees of autonomous regions, counties, and cities throughout the province are being established one after another; Helan, Huinong, and other counties have begun to organize reporters, propagandists, and district and township cadres to prepare for the discussion of the draft constitution.

The Qinghai Province Draft Constitution Discussion Committee was established on June 11th, and the first committee meeting was held on the 12th. The meeting discussed and determined the organizational leadership, specific methods, and time arrangements for publicizing and discussing the draft constitution. The draft constitution discussion committees of all autonomous regions, counties, and cities in the province, as well as provincial-level organs, troops, schools, and other units, will be established around June 15th. The office of the Provincial Draft Constitution Discussion Committee will provide a large number of Tibetan and Mongolian written documents to pastoral areas during the discussion.

The Wuwei Special District of Gansu Province also established the Draft Constitution Discussion Committee on June 10th. Counties under the Wuwei Special District began training reporters and propagandists from June 10th. Wuwei County will convene a people's congress on June 18th to decide to focus the meeting on discussing the draft constitution.

### 西北各省宪法草案讨论委员会  积极准备宣传宪法草案

1954-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    西北各省宪法草案讨论委员会
    积极准备宣传宪法草案
    【新华社西安十四日电】西北区各省正为宣传和讨论宪法草案积极进行准备工作。新疆省宪法草案讨论委员会在五月二十八日就已成立，并在同日举行了第一次委员会议。会议讨论了“关于宣传宪法草案训练报告员、宣传员的计划”，并具体布置了在全省各族人民中进行宪法草案的宣传和讨论工作。中共中央新疆分局宣传部还在六月二日协同省宪法草案讨论委员会办公室召集省级宣教机关和群众团体开会，详细研究了讨论和宣传宪法草案的问题。中国新民主主义青年团新疆省工作委员会和新疆省总工会筹备委员会等单位，也分别拟订计划或发出指示，动员所属单位做好宣传宪法草案的准备工作。目前，全省大部分专区和县都已成立了宪法草案讨论委员会。乌鲁木齐专区和哈密专区大部分县的宣传员、报告员训练班都在六月十日前先后开办。
    陕西省宪法草案讨论委员会六月十四日正式成立。宝鸡、渭南等专区的宪法草案讨论委员会也已成立。现在，陕西省宪法草案讨论委员会决定在六月十五日以后派出一批负责干部分赴各专区，向各地报告员和机关、学校、工矿企业的干部作关于宪法草案的报告。
    宁夏省宪法草案讨论委员会六月二日成立。省人民政府委员会、省各界人民代表会议协商委员会联席会议在六月十二日讨论通过了“动员全省各族各界人民热烈参加中华人民共和国宪法草案的讨论计划”，要求全省各级组织按原有学习系统，组织领导全体干部讨论宪法草案；并要组织各级人民代表、报告员、宣传员和一切可能利用的宣传力量，深入农村、牧业区、厂矿、街道和学校，向人民群众作宣传报告和组织群众参加讨论。现在，全省各自治区、县、市的宪法草案讨论委员会正陆续建立；贺兰、惠农等县并开始组织报告员、宣传员和区、乡干部进行宪法草案讨论的准备工作。
    青海省宪法草案讨论委员会六月十一日成立，十二日举行第一次委员会议，会上讨论和确定了宣传和讨论宪法草案的组织领导、具体方法及时间安排等问题。全省各自治区、县、市及省级各机关、部队、学校等单位的宪法草案讨论委员会，将在六月十五日左右分别成立。省宪法草案讨论委员会办公室将在讨论期间供给牧业区大批藏族和蒙族文字的文件。
    甘肃省武威专区也在六月十日成立了宪法草案讨论委员会。武威专区所属各县从六月十日开始训练报告员和宣传员。武威县将在六月十八日召开人民代表大会，决定以讨论宪法草案为会议中心内容。
