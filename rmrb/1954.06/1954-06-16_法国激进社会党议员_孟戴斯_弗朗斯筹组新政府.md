Title: French Radical Socialist Deputy Mendès-France to Form New Government
Author:
Date: 1954-06-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 15th: It was reported from Paris that after a meeting with French President Coty on the 14th, French Radical Socialist Deputy Mendès-France announced that he had accepted the task of attempting to form a new French government. Mendès-France is preparing to request an authorization vote from the National Assembly on the 16th or 17th.

### 法国激进社会党议员  孟戴斯—弗朗斯筹组新政府

1954-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    法国激进社会党议员
    孟戴斯—弗朗斯筹组新政府
    【新华社十五日讯】巴黎消息：法国激进  社会党议员孟戴斯—弗朗斯十四日和法国总统  戈蒂会谈后宣布：他已接受设法组织一个新的法国政府的任务。孟戴斯—弗朗斯准备在十六日或是十七日要求国民议会举行授权表决。
