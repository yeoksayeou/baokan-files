Title: People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Hold Symposiums to Discuss the Draft Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 17th: People from all walks of life in the capital, in various units, factories, mines, and construction sites, have held symposiums to enthusiastically discuss the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China.

On the evening of the 16th, the Beijing Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the Beijing Democratic Youth Federation jointly invited young people from all walks of life in Beijing to hold a symposium. Among those who attended the symposium were Ni Jiying and Pan Yansheng, industrial labor models from Beijing, Tong Delu, an agricultural labor model from Beijing, Xue Mingrong, a primary school teacher, Yu Shizhi, an actor, and Li Zhaogui, a shop assistant. These young men and women from different positions all expressed their feelings about the draft constitution with excitement. At the symposium held by the Beijing Daily, Pingju actress Li Yilan, Peking Opera actor Tan Fuying, and others came to participate. Tan Fuying said: "In the old society, I was oppressed. Actors were insulted and despised. Now our status is also stipulated in the draft constitution." Quyi performer Cao Baolu said: After reading Article 95 of the draft constitution, I feel really happy and proud. Engineers, labor models, advanced workers, and youth shock brigade members from the Soviet Exhibition Hall construction site also held a symposium on the afternoon of the 16th. After an engineer read the "Preamble" to the draft constitution, he said: For a builder, the most pleasant thing is the completion of the building. For these beautiful buildings, I wholeheartedly support the peace policy stipulated in the draft constitution, and I will do my best to participate in socialist construction. Eleven labor models from the Beijing Fine Arts Printing Factory gathered for a symposium on the afternoon of the 16th. They unanimously stated that they would celebrate the promulgation of the draft constitution by overfulfilling the national plan.

After the promulgation of the draft constitution on the 15th, the Dongdan District Federation of Trade Unions universally organized workers throughout the district to read it, and invited workers elected as district people's representatives, as well as labor models, advanced workers, engineering and technical personnel, female workers, staff members, and medical workers to hold symposiums. On the afternoon of the 16th, it organized workers from joint state-private and private factories to hold a symposium. At the meeting, many workers expressed their feelings. Guo Bin, a worker from the private Yongxing Foreign Paper Firm, and Wang Baolin, a worker from the Qian's Pharmaceutical Factory, unanimously stated: We must supervise the capitalists to improve production and accept transformation, so that the factory can become a state capitalist enterprise as soon as possible.

### 首都各界纷纷举行座谈会讨论宪法草案

1954-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界纷纷举行座谈会讨论宪法草案
    【新华社十七日讯】首都许多单位和厂矿、工地，纷纷举行座谈会，热烈讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案。
    十六日晚，中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会和北京市民主青年联合会联合邀请北京市各界青年举行座谈。参加座谈会的有北京市工业劳动模范倪吉英、潘燕生，北京市农业劳动模范佟德禄，小学教师薛明容，演员于是之，店员李肇桂等人。这些来自各个不同岗位的男女青年，都以兴奋的心情发表了对宪法草案的感想。北京日报举行的座谈会上，评剧演员李忆兰、京剧演员谭富英等都赶来参加，谭富英说：“在旧社会里我受过压迫，唱戏的是被人侮辱被人轻视的，现在我们的地位在宪法草案上也有了规定。”曲艺界演员曹宝禄说：看了宪法草案第九十五条，我真感到高兴和骄傲。苏联展览馆工区的工程师、劳动模范、先进工作者、青年突击队员等也在十六日下午举行了座谈会。一个工程师读了宪法草案“序言”后说：对一个建筑者说来，最愉快的事是建筑物的落成。为了这些美好的建筑物，我衷心拥护宪法草案中规定的和平政策，我将竭尽我的力量参加社会主义建设。北京美术印刷厂十一个劳动模范十六日下午集会座谈，他们一致表示要以超额完成国家计划，来庆祝宪法草案的公布。
    东单区区工会在十五日宪法草案公布后，就普遍组织全区工人阅读，并邀请当选为区人民代表的工人以及劳动模范、先进工作者、工程技术人员、女工、职员和医务工作者等有代表性的人物举行座谈会。十六日下午又组织公私合营和私营工厂的工人举行座谈会，会上不少工人都发表了自己的感想，私营永兴洋纸行工人郭彬和钱氏药厂工人王宝林一致表示：要监督资本家搞好生产，接受改造，使工厂早日成为国家资本主义的企业。
