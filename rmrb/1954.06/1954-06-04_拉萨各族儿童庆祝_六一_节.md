Title: Lhasa Children of All Ethnic Groups Celebrate "June 1st" Children's Day
Author:
Date: 1954-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, June 2nd: More than 1,200 Tibetan, Hui, and Han children in Lhasa held a celebration meeting and recreational get-together on the 1st to joyfully celebrate "June 1st" International Children's Day. Tan Guansan, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Tuden Danda, the third principal of Lhasa Primary School, as well as cultural and educational workers from Lhasa, representatives from the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association, and the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation attended the meeting to congratulate the children and participate in their celebratory activities.

　　The celebration took place in Duojiang Linka, east of the Potala Palace. Next to Linka, in the newly built classrooms of Lhasa Primary School, were displayed various assignments of the primary school students and written materials about the exemplary deeds of outstanding children. Sonam Dorje, a student from Lhasa Primary School, served as chairman of the meeting. He said at the meeting: In order to protect our happy life, we, like children all over the country and the world, love peace and resolutely oppose imperialist aggression, and we must repay Chairman Mao's concern for us by studying hard and strengthening our bodies. Model children representatives from Lhasa Primary School and children representatives from various private schools in Lhasa also spoke at the meeting, expressing their determination to study hard, unite and help each other, and become outstanding talents for building the motherland and building a new Tibet in the future.

### 拉萨各族儿童庆祝“六一”节

1954-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    拉萨各族儿童庆祝“六一”节
    【新华社拉萨二日电】拉萨市藏、回、汉各族儿童一千二百多人一日举行庆祝大会和游艺联欢会，欢度“六一”国际儿童节。中国人民解放军西藏军区政治委员谭冠三、拉萨市小学校第三校长土丹旦达以及拉萨市文化教育工作者、拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会和拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会的代表都到会祝贺，并参加了儿童们的庆祝活动。
    庆祝活动在布达拉宫东侧的多强林卡里进行，林卡旁边拉萨小学新建的教室里，陈列着小学生们的各种作业和优秀儿童模范事迹的书面材料。大会由拉萨市小学校学生索南多吉担任主席。他在会上说：为了保护我们的幸福生活，我们和全国、全世界儿童一样，热爱和平、坚决反对帝国主义的侵略，并且要以努力学习、锻炼好身体来报答毛主席对我们的关怀。拉萨小学的模范儿童代表和拉萨市各私塾的儿童代表也都在会上讲话，表示要努力学习、团结互助，将来成为建设祖国、建设新西藏的优秀人材。
