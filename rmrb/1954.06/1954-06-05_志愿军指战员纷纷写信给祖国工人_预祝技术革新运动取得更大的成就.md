Title: Volunteer Army Commanders and Fighters Write to Workers of the Motherland, Wishing the Technical Innovation Movement Greater Success

Author:
Date: 1954-06-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 4th: Many heroic company-level cadres and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, upon hearing the news that the All-China Federation of Trade Unions had accepted the suggestions of nationally renowned labor models such as Zhang Mingshan and decided to launch a technical innovation movement nationwide, wrote letters with excitement to Zhang Mingshan and other labor models, as well as to their worker brothers throughout the country, wishing the movement great success.

    The commanders and fighters of the "Tiandeshan Hero Company" of a certain unit of the Volunteer Army wrote to Zhang Mingshan, Wang Chonglun, and the other seven labor models, as well as to their worker brothers throughout the country, saying: We were very excited to hear on the radio that a technical innovation movement is about to unfold on the industrial production front of the motherland. We believe that the nationwide launch of the technical innovation movement will accelerate the industrialization of our motherland and build a beautiful and happy socialist society as soon as possible. In their letter, they also expressed their determination to learn from the national worker brothers' love for the motherland and socialism, and to continue to practice thrift in order to support the economic construction of the motherland.

    A certain unit of the Volunteer Army stationed in the Shangganling area also wrote to Zhang Mingshan, Wang Chonglun, and the other seven labor models, wishing the nationwide worker brothers victory in this movement. The letter said: We understand that not only does the construction of the motherland need new and higher technology, but also the defense of the motherland needs new and higher technology. Therefore, we are also constantly striving to improve our military, political, and cultural level, and to become familiar with the various weapons in our hands. We guarantee to you that we will learn our skills well, maintain the highest degree of vigilance to defend peace, defend your technical innovation movement, and defend the socialist construction of the motherland.

    A certain Volunteer Army's second-class Hero Company stationed in the Imjin River area also wrote to Zhang Mingshan, Wang Chonglun, and the other seven labor models, wishing that the flowers of technical innovation would bloom all over the country.

### 志愿军指战员纷纷写信给祖国工人  预祝技术革新运动取得更大的成就

1954-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军指战员纷纷写信给祖国工人
    预祝技术革新运动取得更大的成就
   【新华社平壤四日电】中国人民志愿军许多英雄连队的干部战士在听到中华全国总工会接受全国著名的劳动模范张明山等的建议，决定在全国范围内开展技术革新运动的消息后，以欢欣鼓舞的心情，纷纷写信给张明山等和全国工人兄弟，预祝这一运动取得巨大的成就。
    志愿军某部“天德山英雄连”的指挥员、战斗员在给张明山、王崇伦等七位劳动模范和全国工人兄弟的信上说：我们从广播中听到祖国的工业生产战线上将要展开技术革新运动的消息，使我们非常兴奋。我们相信，在全国范围内开展技术革新运动，必将加速我们祖国的工业化，早日建成一个美好幸福的社会主义社会。他们在信中并表示一定要学习全国工人兄弟热爱祖国、热爱社会主义的思想和行动，继续厉行节约，来支援祖国的经济建设。
    守卫在上甘岭地区的志愿军某部也给张明山、王崇伦等七位劳动模范写信，并向全国工人兄弟预祝这个运动的胜利。该信说：我们懂得，不仅建设祖国需要新的更高的技术，就是保卫祖国也需要新的更高的技术。因此，我们也正在不断努力提高自己的军事、政治和文化水平，熟悉手中各种武器，我们向你们保证：一定要学好本领，以最高度的警惕保卫和平，保卫你们的技术革新运动，保卫祖国的社会主义建设。
    驻在临津江地区的志愿军某部二级英雄连也给张明山、王崇伦等七位劳动模范写信，预祝技术革新的鲜花开遍全国。
