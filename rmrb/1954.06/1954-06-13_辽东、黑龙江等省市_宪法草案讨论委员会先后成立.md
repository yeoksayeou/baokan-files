    Liaodong, Heilongjiang and Other Provinces and Cities
    Have Established Constitution Draft Discussion Committees
    [From Our Newspaper] Liaodong, Heilongjiang, Hunan, Shanxi, Xinjiang and other provinces, as well as Lushun-Dalian, Benxi, Tangshan, and Jinan cities, have recently established constitution draft discussion committees. Some provinces have also formulated plans to train rapporteurs and propagandists. The Liaodong Province Constitution Draft Discussion Committee began concentrating on training rapporteurs from provincial-level organizations on June 7; rapporteurs and propagandists from various cities, counties, factories, and mines have also begun studying separately. The Heilongjiang Province Constitution Draft Discussion Committee held a training class for rapporteurs and propagandists on June 7, organizing everyone to listen to special reports on the constitution draft. Shanxi Province plans to select a portion of cadres from county, district, and township levels to be sent to various places to carry out propaganda activities after a short period of training. Xinjiang Province plans to train 30,000 rapporteurs and propagandists; special districts and counties have also held training classes. While training rapporteurs and propagandists, various localities are also organizing cadres to study and discuss the constitution draft. Shanxi Province stipulates that cadres from factories, mines, troops, and government organizations, as well as faculty and staff from universities and middle schools, throughout the province are to be organized into groups to study and discuss the constitution draft. Cadres and staff from Qiqihar City, the Heihe Commissioner's Office, various counties (banners), and major factories, construction sites, and farms in Heilongjiang Province will study and discuss under the leadership of the Constitution Draft Discussion Committee. Cadres from all levels of government organizations and faculty and staff from schools in Xinjiang Province will also begin studying and discussing the constitution draft.

### 辽东、黑龙江等省市  宪法草案讨论委员会先后成立

1954-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    辽东、黑龙江等省市
    宪法草案讨论委员会先后成立
    【本报讯】辽东、黑龙江、湖南、山西、新疆等省和旅大、本溪、唐山、济南市，都在最近先后成立了宪法草案讨论委员会，有的省份并制订了训练报告员和宣传员的计划。辽东省宪法草案讨论委员会，已从本月七日开始集中训练省一级机关的报告员；各市、县和厂矿的报告员和宣传员也已分别开始学习。黑龙江省宪法草案讨论委员会在六月七日举办了报告员、宣传员训练班，组织大家听了关于宪法草案的专题报告。山西省各市县计划在县、区乡干部中抽调一部分人员，经过短期训练后派赴各地开展宣传活动。新疆省计划训练报告员、宣传员三万名；各专区、各县也分别举办了训练班。在训练报告员和宣传员的同时，各地还组织干部学习和讨论宪法草案。山西省规定：全省各工厂、矿山、部队和机关、团体的干部，以及大、中学校的教职员，都编成小组学习和讨论宪法草案。黑龙江省齐齐哈尔市、黑河专员公署、各县（旗）以及各大工厂、工地、农场的干部、工作人员，将在宪法草案讨论委员会的领导下进行学习和讨论。新疆省各级机关干部、学校的教职员，也将开始学习和讨论宪法草案。
