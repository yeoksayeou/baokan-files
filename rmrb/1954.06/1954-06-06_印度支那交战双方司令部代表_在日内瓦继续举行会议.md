Title: Representatives of the Commanding Headquarters of the Indochina Warring Parties Continue Meetings in Geneva
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 4th: Representatives of the commanding headquarters of the Indochina warring parties continued their meeting on June 4th to study the areas for the re-grouping of both sides' troops after the cessation of hostilities and related issues.

### 印度支那交战双方司令部代表  在日内瓦继续举行会议

1954-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    印度支那交战双方司令部代表
    在日内瓦继续举行会议
    【新华社日内瓦四日电】印度支那交战双方司令部的代表六月四日继续举行会议，研究停止敌对行动后双方军队的重新集结地区和有关问题。
