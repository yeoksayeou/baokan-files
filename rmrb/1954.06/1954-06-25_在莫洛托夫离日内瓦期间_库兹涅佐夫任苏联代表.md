Title: Kuznetsov to Serve as Soviet Representative During Molotov's Absence from Geneva
Author:
Date: 1954-06-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 23rd: TASS, Geneva, June 23rd -- Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs V.V. Kuznetsov arrived in Geneva on the 23rd.

V.V. Kuznetsov will serve as the Soviet representative to the Geneva Conference during Molotov's absence from Geneva.

### 在莫洛托夫离日内瓦期间  库兹涅佐夫任苏联代表

1954-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    在莫洛托夫离日内瓦期间
    库兹涅佐夫任苏联代表
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社日内瓦二十三日讯：苏联外交部副部长瓦·瓦·库兹涅佐夫在二十三日到达日内瓦。
    瓦·瓦·库兹涅佐夫在莫洛托夫离开日内瓦期间担任苏联出席日内瓦会议的代表。
