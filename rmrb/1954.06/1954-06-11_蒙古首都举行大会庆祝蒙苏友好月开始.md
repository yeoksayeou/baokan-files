Title: Mongolian Capital Holds Meeting to Celebrate Start of Month of Mongolian-Soviet Friendship
Author:
Date: 1954-06-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, June 8th: A celebration was held in Ulaanbaatar to celebrate the start of the Month of Mongolian-Soviet Friendship, which began on June 7th. Representatives from party, government, and mass organizations, as well as advanced workers and representatives from the fields of science, literature, and art, participated in the celebration.
    Zedenbal, Damba, Silindib, Lhamsuren, Zhadib, and Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Mongolian People's Republic V.I. Pisarev were elected to the presidium of the meeting.
    After an opening speech by Dugsuren, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Mongolian-Soviet Cultural Association, Silindib, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Mongolian-Soviet Cultural Association, gave a report entitled "The Unbreakable Friendship Between the Mongolian People and the Great Soviet People - The Inexhaustible Source of All Our Victories and Achievements." He spoke of the great significance of the friendship between the Soviet people and the Mongolian people for the Mongolian People's Republic, and emphasized that this friendship is a reliable guarantee for the further prosperity of the Mongolian People's Republic.
    Soviet Ambassador V.I. Pisarev also spoke at the meeting.

### 蒙古首都举行大会庆祝蒙苏友好月开始

1954-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古首都举行大会庆祝蒙苏友好月开始
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托八日讯：乌兰巴托举行了庆祝会，庆祝从六月七日开始的蒙苏友好月。参加庆祝会的有党、政和群众组织的代表，与先进的工人和科学、文学、艺术界代表。
    会上选出了由泽登巴尔、达姆巴、锡林迪布、拉姆苏伦、泽狄布和苏联驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使瓦·伊·皮萨列夫组成的主席团。
    在蒙苏文化协会中央委员会秘书长杜格苏伦致开幕词后，蒙苏文化协会中央委员会主席锡林迪布作了题为“蒙古人民和伟大的苏联人民的牢不可破的友谊——我们的一切胜利和成就的不竭的源泉”的报告。他谈到苏联人民和蒙古人民之间的友谊对蒙古人民共和国的重大意义，并着重指出，这种友谊是蒙古人民共和国进一步繁荣的一个可靠的保证。
    苏联大使瓦·伊·皮萨列夫也在会上讲话。
