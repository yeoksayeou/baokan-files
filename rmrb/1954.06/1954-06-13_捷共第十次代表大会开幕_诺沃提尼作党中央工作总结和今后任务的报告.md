Title: Opening of the 10th Congress of the Czechoslovak Communist Party; Novotný Delivers Report on the Central Committee's Work Summary and Future Tasks
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 12th: The 10th Congress of the Czechoslovak Communist Party opened in Prague on the 11th.

At the morning session, when members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the head of the delegation of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union attending the Congress, Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, appeared on the presidium rostrum, the entire hall erupted in stormy applause. Representatives of various fraternal Communist and Workers' Parties were seated on the rostrum, including Harry Pollitt, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain, and Jacques Duclos, Secretary of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party.

The opening ceremony was presided over by Antonín Zápotocký, Secretary of the Political Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party. He suggested that all delegates to the Congress stand and observe a moment of silence for the great successor to Lenin's immortal cause, Stalin, and Gottwald, the faithful student of Lenin and Stalin.

In his opening speech, Zápotocký spoke about the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the state's implementation of the historic decisions of the Ninth Congress of the Czechoslovak Communist Party on building socialism, and the achievements of the Czech people in socialist construction. In discussing the foreign policy of the Czechoslovak government, he emphasized that the direction of our policy is to strengthen the unshakable friendship with the Soviet Union, the people's democracies, and the entire peace camp. His speech was interrupted by applause many times.

The Congress elected the Presidium of the Congress and other working bodies of the Congress.

The Congress adopted the following agenda:

One, Opening speech of the 10th Congress of the Czechoslovak Communist Party - Antonín Zápotocký;

Two, Summary of the work of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and report on future tasks of the Party - Reporter: Antonín Novotný;

Three, Report of the Central Auditing Commission - Reporter: Josef Štetka;

Four, Instructions on the 1955 National Economic Development Plan and a short-term plan to vigorously increase agricultural production in the next two to three years - Reporter: Viliam Široký;

Five, Report on the revision of the Party Constitution - Reporter: Václav Pašek;

Six, Election of the Party Central Committee and the Central Auditing Commission.

Subsequently, Novotný delivered the report on the second item of the agenda at the meeting.

At the afternoon session, the delegates heard the report of the Central Auditing Commission delivered by Štetka, and then began to discuss the reports on the second and third items of the agenda.

### 捷共第十次代表大会开幕  诺沃提尼作党中央工作总结和今后任务的报告

1954-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共第十次代表大会开幕
    诺沃提尼作党中央工作总结和今后任务的报告
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克共产党第十次代表大会于十一日在布拉格开幕。
    在上午会议上，当捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会主席团委员和参加代表大会的苏联共产党代表团团长、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫在主席团席上出现时，全场响起了暴风雨般的掌声。各兄弟共产党和工人党代表团的代表都在主席台上就座，其中有英国共产党总书记波立特和法国共产党中央委员会书记杜克洛等。
    开幕式由捷共中央政治书记处书记萨波托斯基主持。他建议大会全体代表起立，为列宁不朽事业的伟大继承者斯大林和列宁及斯大林的忠实学生哥特瓦尔德默哀。
    萨波托斯基在开幕词中讲到捷克斯洛伐克共产党和国家执行捷共第九次代表大会通过的关于建设社会主义的历史性的决定的情况，以及捷人民在社会主义建设中的成就。他在谈到捷政府的外交政策时着重指出，我们政策的方针是为了加强对苏联、各人民民主国家和整个和平阵营的不可动摇的友谊。他的发言多次为掌声打断。
    大会选出了代表大会主席团和大会其他工作机构。
    代表大会通过议程如下：
    一、捷克斯洛伐克共产党第十次代表大会开幕词——安·萨波托斯基；
    二、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会工作总结和今后党的任务的报告——报告人：安·诺沃提尼；
    三、中央检查委员会的报告——报告人：约·什蒂特卡；
    四、关于一九五五年发展国民经济计划的指示和最近两三年内大力提高农业生产的短期计划——报告人：威·西罗基；
    五、修改党章的报告——报告人：瓦·帕歇克；
    六、选举党中央委员会和中央检查委员会。
    接着，诺沃提尼在会上作了第二项议程的报告。
    下午会议上，代表们听取了什蒂特卡所作的中央检查委员会的报告，随即开始讨论第二项和第三项议程的报告。
