Title: Guatemalan Army Headquarters Issues Communiqué: Government Forces Have Crushed the Resistance of the Interventionists in Three Areas
Author:
Date: 1954-06-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, June 23rd (TASS): London radio broadcasts that the Guatemalan Army Headquarters issued a communiqué stating that the government forces' counterattack has crushed the resistance of the armed interventionists in three areas along the railway line from the Guatemalan capital to Puerto Barrios. Pursued by government forces, the interventionist forces fled in panic towards the Honduran border.

The communiqué stated that several enemy units, cut off from their main forces, have now surrendered without resistance.

### 危地马拉军队司令部发表战况公报  政府军已在三个地区摧毁了干涉者的反抗

1954-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    危地马拉军队司令部发表战况公报
    政府军已在三个地区摧毁了干涉者的反抗
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十三日讯：据伦敦电台广播，危地马拉军队司令部发表战况公报说，政府军的反击已在沿从危地马拉首都通往巴利俄港铁路线一带的三个地区摧毁了武装干涉者的反抗。在政府军追击之下，干涉军仓惶往洪都拉斯边境逃窜。
    公报说，被切断了和主力的联系的几支敌军部队，现在已无反抗地投降。
