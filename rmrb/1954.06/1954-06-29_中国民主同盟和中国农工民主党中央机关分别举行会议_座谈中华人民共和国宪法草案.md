Title: The Central Organs of the China Democratic League and the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Hold Meetings to Discuss the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1954-06-29
Page: 2

    The Central Organs of the China Democratic League and the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Hold Meetings
    To Discuss the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China
    The Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League and the central organs of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party held meetings to discuss the draft constitution of the People's Republic of China.
    The enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League was presided over by Vice Chairman Zhang Bojun. More than 40 people attended the meeting, and everyone expressed their support for the draft constitution. At the meeting, Luo Longji, Shi Liang, Gao Chongmin, Hu Yuzhi, Fei Xiaotong, Huang Yaomian, Zeng Zhaolun, Liu Qingyang, and others made key speeches on the preamble, general principles, state organs, and basic rights and obligations of citizens in the draft constitution. Shen Junru, in his speech, also pointed out the necessity for members of the Democratic League to thoroughly study, discuss, and publicize the draft constitution. Finally, the meeting adopted the "Instructions on Studying, Discussing, and Publicizing the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" issued by the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Democratic League to its local organizations.
    The central organs of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party held a forum to celebrate the promulgation of the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China. More than 60 people attended, including members of the Central Committee of the Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party and members of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party. At the forum, Li Boqiu, Zhou Taixuan, Ji Fang, Wang Yifan, Wang Jiyi, Yang Yitang, Qiu Kehui, and others spoke successively. They unanimously believed that the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China not only summarizes the experience of the revolutionary struggles of the Chinese people over the past few decades, but also reflects the fundamental requirements of the country during the transition period, expresses the common desire of the people of the whole country to gradually build our country into a socialist society, and the correct path to strive for the realization of this desire. Most of the speakers participated in the discussion of the initial draft of the constitution and believed that the true democratic spirit of our country was fully embodied in the discussion of the initial draft. Everyone unanimously expressed their heartfelt support for the draft constitution and proposed to conscientiously study, discuss, and publicize the draft constitution, and play a promoting and leading role among the masses they are connected with.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国民主同盟和中国农工民主党中央机关分别举行会议  座谈中华人民共和国宪法草案

1954-06-29
第2版()
专栏：

    中国民主同盟和中国农工民主党中央机关分别举行会议
    座谈中华人民共和国宪法草案
    中国民主同盟中央常务委员会、中国农工民主党中央机关分别举行会议，座谈中华人民共和国宪法草案。
    中国民主同盟中央常务委员会举行的扩大会议由副主席章伯钧主持。出席这次会议的有四十多人，大家一致表示拥护宪法草案。会上罗隆基、史良、高崇民、胡愈之、费孝通、黄药眠、曾昭抡、刘清扬等分别就宪法草案的序言、总纲、国家机构、公民的基本权利和义务等方面作了重点发言。沈钧儒在发言中并指出民盟盟员深入学习、讨论和宣传宪法草案的必要。最后会议通过了民盟中央常务委员会向所属各地组织发出的“关于学习、讨论和宣传中华人民共和国宪法草案的指示”。
    中国农工民主党中央机关为庆祝中华人民共和国宪法草案的公布举行了座谈会。到会的有农工民主党中央委员和农工民主党北京市委员会委员等六十多人。座谈会上，李伯球、周太玄、季方、王一帆、王寄一、杨逸棠、丘克辉等相继发言。他们一致认为：中华人民共和国宪法草案不但总结了我国人民几十年来革命斗争的经验，同时还反映了国家在过渡时期的根本要求，表达了全国人民把我国逐步建成社会主义社会的共同愿望，以及为实现这一愿望而奋斗的正确道路。发言人大部分都参加过宪法草案初稿的讨论，都认为在初稿的讨论中，充分体现了我们国家的真正民主的精神。大家对宪法草案一致表示衷心拥护，并提出要认真学习、讨论和宣传宪法草案，在所联系的群众中起推动和带头作用。
                                  （新华社）
