Title: Agreement Reached Between Chinese Government and United Kingdom Government Regarding Dispatch of Chargé d'Affaires to London
Author:
Date: 1954-06-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the United Kingdom have agreed that the Central People's Government will dispatch a Chargé d'Affaires to London, whose position and mission will be the same as those of the British Chargé d'Affaires in Beijing.

### 我国政府和联合王国政府  取得关于我国政府派遣代办驻伦敦的协议

1954-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府和联合王国政府
    取得关于我国政府派遣代办驻伦敦的协议
    【新华社十七日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府和联合王国政府协议：中央人民政府派遣代办驻在伦敦，其地位和任务与英国驻北京代办的地位和任务相同。
