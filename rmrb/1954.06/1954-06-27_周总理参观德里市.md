Title: Premier Zhou Visits Delhi

Author: 
Date: 1954-06-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by the Chief Minister of Delhi State, Prakash, the Minister of Health of Delhi State, Dr. Nayar, and the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, Yuan Zhongxian, visited the suburbs and urban areas of Delhi from 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM on the 26th. Premier Zhou Enlai received tributes and flowers at the Qutab Minar tower, the Kasturba Women and Children's Education Center, the Delhi Muslim University, and the old Delhi City Government.

Wherever Premier Zhou Enlai went, tens of thousands of Delhi citizens lined the streets, cheering "Long Live Sino-Indian Friendship" and throwing flowers at Premier Zhou.

### 周总理参观德里市

1954-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理参观德里市
    【新华社新德里二十六日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来在德里邦首席部长普拉卡希、德里邦卫生部部长纳雅尔博士和中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤陪同下，在廿六日上午八时至十时参观了德里的郊区和市区。周恩来总理在奎塔布·明纳尔高塔、卡斯图巴妇女儿童教养院、德里回教大学和德里旧市政府接受了献礼和献花。
    周恩来总理所到之处沿途有德里市民数万人夹道欢呼“中印友好万岁”，并向周总理投掷鲜花。
