Title: The CPC Central Committee Sends a Condolence Message on the Passing of Comrade Modzelewski
Author:
Date: 1954-06-22
Page: 4

    The CPC Central Committee Sends a Condolence Message on the Passing of Comrade Modzelewski
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, expressing condolences on the death of Comrade Modzelewski of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party. The telegram is as follows:
    To the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party:
    We are deeply saddened to learn of the death of Comrade Zygmunt Modzelewski and extend our deepest condolences.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    June 20, 1954

### 中共中央电唁莫杰莱夫斯基同志的逝世

1954-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央电唁莫杰莱夫斯基同志的逝世
    中国共产党中央委员会致电波兰统一工人党中央委员会，吊唁波兰统一工人党中央委员会莫杰莱夫斯基同志。电文如下：
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会：
    惊悉泽格蒙特·莫杰莱夫斯基同志病逝，谨致深切的悼唁。
    中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五四年六月二十日
