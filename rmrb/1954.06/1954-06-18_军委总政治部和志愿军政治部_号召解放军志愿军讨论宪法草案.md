Title: General Political Department of the Military Commission and Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Call on the PLA and CPVA to Discuss the Draft Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-06-18
Page: 1

The General Political Department of the Central Military Commission and the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army issued instructions on May 28th and June 15th respectively, calling on the entire army to conscientiously publicize and discuss the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China within the troops.

The instructions require that all units should immediately carry out extensive and enthusiastic publicity and discussion after the promulgation of the Draft Constitution, so that all commanders and fighters understand the main contents of the Draft Constitution.

The instructions point out that the Chinese Communist Party's organizations and political organs at all levels in the army must attach importance to the publicity and discussion of the Draft Constitution; responsible cadres at the regimental level and above should discuss it first, then report to cadres and soldiers, and organize all personnel to discuss it.

The instructions require that all units should actively establish close contact with the constitution draft discussion committees at the local county and city levels and above, obtain their help, and promptly report the opinions of the troops' discussions to them.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 17th: The Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army issued a notice to the entire army regarding the publicity and discussion of the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China. The notice instructed all units to vigorously launch universal and enthusiastic publicity and discussion among all personnel in the entire army, and designated political commissars of units at the regimental level and above, responsible cadres of political organs, or other responsible cadres with reporting ability as reporters to deliver reports to the troops. The notice requires that through the publicity and discussion of the Draft Constitution, all members of the army understand the main contents of the Draft Constitution, and educate the broad masses of cadres and fighters to become models who resolutely abide by national laws.

### 军委总政治部和志愿军政治部  号召解放军志愿军讨论宪法草案

1954-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    军委总政治部和志愿军政治部
    号召解放军志愿军讨论宪法草案
    【本报讯】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部在五月二十八日和六月十五日先后发出指示，号召全军认真在部队中宣传和讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案。
    指示中要求各部队应在宪法草案公布后立即进行广泛、热烈的宣传和讨论，务使全体指挥员、战斗员明了宪法草案的主要内容。
    指示指出：中国共产党在部队中的各级组织和政治机关，必须重视宪法草案的宣传和讨论；团以上的负责干部要首先讨论，然后对干部和战士作报告，并组织全体人员进行讨论。
    指示要求各部队应主动和当地县、市以上各级宪法草案讨论委员会建立密切联系，取得他们的帮助，并及时将部队讨论的意见向他们报告。
    【新华社平壤十七日电】中国人民志愿军政治部向全军发出了关于中华人民共和国宪法草案的宣传和讨论的通知。通知中指示各部队要大张旗鼓地在全军所有人员中展开普遍、热烈的宣传和讨论，并指定团以上单位的政治委员、政治机关的负责干部或其他有报告能力的负责干部为报告员，向部队作传达报告。通知要求通过宪法草案的宣传和讨论，使全军人人明了宪法草案的主要内容，并教育广大干部、战士成为坚决遵守国家法律的模范。
