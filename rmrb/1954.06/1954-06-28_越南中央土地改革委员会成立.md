Title: Establishment of the Central Land Reform Committee of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1954-06-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 24th: The Vietnamese Council of Ministers recently passed two regulations regarding the establishment of the Central Land Reform Committee and the Provincial Land Reform Committees. The Vietnamese Council of Ministers has determined the members of the Central Land Reform Committee as follows: Chairman: Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong; Vice Chairmen: Vietnamese Labor Party General Secretary Truong Chinh, Minister of Agriculture Nghiem Xuan Yem, Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Chairman of the National Farmers Liaison Committee Ho Viet Thang, and more than ten members.

The Central Land Reform Committee held its first meeting from June 5th to 8th. The meeting listened to reports on the mass mobilization movement in four stages and the pilot land reform work in six villages of Thai Nguyen Province. The meeting then proceeded with discussions and approved the plan to carry out the fifth stage of the mass mobilization movement in the Fourth Interzone, Viet Bac Interzone, and other regions.

### 越南中央土地改革委员会成立

1954-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    越南中央土地改革委员会成立
    【新华社二十七日讯】据越南通讯社二十四日讯：越南部长会议最近通过两项关于成立中央土地改革委员会和省的土地改革委员会的条例。越南部长会议确定了中央土地改革委员会的成员如下：主任：副总理范文同；副主任：越南劳动党总书记长征，农业部部长严春庵，农业部副部长、全国农民联络委员会主席胡曰胜以及委员十多人。
    中央土地改革委员会于六月五日至八日召开了第一次会议，会议听取了关于四个阶段的发动群众运动和在太原省六个乡进行土地改革试点工作的报告。会议接着进行了讨论，并通过在第四联区、越北联区等地进行第五阶段发动群众运动的计划。
