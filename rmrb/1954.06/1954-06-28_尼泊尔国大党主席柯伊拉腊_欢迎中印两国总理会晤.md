Title: Nepalese Congress Party President Koirala Welcomes Meeting Between Chinese and Indian Premiers
Author:
Date: 1954-06-28
Page: 1

    Nepalese Congress Party President Koirala
    Welcomes Meeting Between Chinese and Indian Premiers
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 27th] According to a June 26th report from the Indian Information Office, the president of the Nepalese Congress Party, B. P. Koirala, expressed his welcome for the meeting between Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China and Indian Prime Minister Nehru. He also stated that Nepal, as a neighbor of China and India, hopes to coexist with these two countries in friendship and deep understanding.

### 尼泊尔国大党主席柯伊拉腊  欢迎中印两国总理会晤

1954-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔国大党主席柯伊拉腊
    欢迎中印两国总理会晤
    【新华社二十七日讯】据印度新闻处二十六日报道，尼泊尔国大党主席毕·普·柯伊拉腊对中华人民共和国总理周恩来和印度总理尼赫鲁的会晤，表示欢迎。他并说，尼泊尔是中国和印度的邻邦，它希望和这两个国家在友好和深切谅解中共处。
