Title: Fu Huating, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1954-06-07
Page: 3

    Fu Huating, member of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, and Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, passed away at 10:30 PM on the 4th at the Beijing Municipal Third Hospital due to a cerebral hemorrhage, despite medical treatment. The encoffining ceremony was held at Xianliang Temple at 2:00 PM on the 5th. Those who came to pay their respects included Huang Yanpei, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, Xue Zizheng, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Chai Zemin, Deputy Secretary-General, and over a hundred friends of Mr. Fu Huating.
    The Fu Huating Funeral Committee has been established, and a public memorial service will be held from 9:00 AM to 10:30 AM on the 9th of this month at the Xianliang Temple Funeral Home.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京市工商联主任委员傅华亭逝世

1954-06-07
第3版()
专栏：

    北京市工商联主任委员傅华亭逝世
    北京市人民政府委员、中华全国工商业联合会副主任委员、中国民主建国会北京市分会主任委员、北京市工商业联合会主任委员傅华亭先生，四日患脑溢血症医治无效，在下午十时三十分病逝于北京市市立第三医院，已于五日下午二时在贤良寺举行大殓。前往吊唁的有中国民主建国会总会主任委员黄炎培，中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通，北京市人民政府秘书长薛子正、副秘书长柴泽民以及傅华亭先生生前友好百多人。
    傅华亭先生治丧委员会已成立，并定于本月九日上午九时到十时半在贤良寺殡仪馆举行公祭。
                                  （新华社）
