Title: Partial Solar Eclipse to be Visible in Western China at the End of This Month
Author:
Date: 1954-06-22
Page: 3

According to observations by the Purple Mountain Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences: On June 30th of this year, a partial solar eclipse will be visible at sunset in the northwestern region of western China, from Zhangye County in Gansu Province to the northwest of the Lhasa City line in Tibet. The entire process of this solar eclipse will not be visible in our country. Areas east of a line from Hami County in Xinjiang Province to the northern part of Ali in Tibet will only be able to see the first contact. Areas west of this line will be able to see the first contact and the maximum obscuration.

The times when the solar eclipse will be visible in major cities are as follows (Gansu Province uses Beijing Standard Time, which is East 120 degrees longitude; Xinjiang Province and Tibet use East 90 degrees longitude): Jiuquan, Gansu - First contact, 20:43; Anxi - First contact, 20:43; Urumqi, Xinjiang - First contact, 18:41, Maximum obscuration, 19:30; Hami - First contact, 18:42; Yining - First contact, 18:42, Maximum obscuration, 19:32; Shufu - First contact, 18:47, Maximum obscuration, 19:40; Shigatse, Tibet - First contact, 18:56; Gada Gram - First contact, 18:56.

### 我国西部本月末将见日偏食

1954-06-22
第3版()
专栏：

    我国西部本月末将见日偏食
    据中国科学院紫金山天文台观测：今年六月三十日，我国西部由甘肃省张掖县到西藏拉萨市一线西北的地区，在日落时可以看到日偏食。这次日食，在我国不能看到全部过程，新疆省哈密县到西藏阿里北部一线以东的地方只能看到初亏，这一线以西的地方可以看到初亏和食甚。
    主要城市见日食的时刻如下（甘肃省用东经一百二十度标准时，新疆省和西藏用东经九十度标准时）：甘肃酒泉——初亏，二十时四十三分；安西——初亏，二十时四十三分；新疆乌鲁木齐——初亏，十八时四十一分，食甚，十九时三十分；哈密——初亏，十八时四十二分；伊宁——初亏，十八时四十二分，食甚，十九时三十二分；疏附——初亏，十八时四十七分，食甚，十九时四十分；西藏日喀则——初亏，十八时五十六分；噶大克——初亏，十八时五十六分。
