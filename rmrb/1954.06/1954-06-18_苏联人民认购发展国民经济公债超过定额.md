Title: Soviet People Exceed Quota in Subscribing to National Economic Development Bonds
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: A bulletin issued by the Soviet Ministry of Finance on the 15th states: The issuance of the 1954 National Economic Development Bonds was set at 16 billion rubles. As of the evening of the 14th, subscriptions to the bonds had reached 17,492,031,000 rubles.
    Based on the instructions of the Soviet Council of Ministers, the Soviet Ministry of Finance has ordered that all bond subscriptions be stopped everywhere starting from June 16th.

### 苏联人民认购发展国民经济公债超过定额

1954-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联人民认购发展国民经济公债超过定额
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联财政部十五日发表公告说：一九五四年发展苏联国民经济公债的发行额规定为一百六十亿卢布，而截至十四日晚间为止，认购公债的数额已达一百七十四亿九千二百零三万一千卢布。
    苏联财政部根据苏联部长会议的指示，特下令自六月十六日起各地一律停止公债认购。
