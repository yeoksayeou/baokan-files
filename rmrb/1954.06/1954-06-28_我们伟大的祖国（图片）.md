Author:
Date: 1954-06-28

    Our Great Motherland
    Street nurseries in major cities are developing rapidly, greatly reducing the burden on mothers, enabling them to work, study, and participate in various social activities with peace of mind. This is the nursery teacher at the Danshui Cun Nursery, which was rated as a model nursery in Shanghai last year. She is guiding the children in stacking building blocks.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各大城市街道托儿站日益发展，大大减轻了母亲们的负担，使她们都能够安心工作、学习和参加各种社会活动。这是被评为去年上海市甲等模范的淡水邨托儿站的保育员。她正在指道儿宣堆砌积木。
