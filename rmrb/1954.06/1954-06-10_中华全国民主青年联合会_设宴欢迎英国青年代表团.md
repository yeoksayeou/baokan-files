Title: All-China Youth Federation Hosts Banquet for British Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: The All-China Youth Federation hosted a banquet on the evening of the 8th to welcome the entire British Youth Delegation.

    On the host side, those present included: Liu Daosheng, Qian Sanqiang, Qu Tangliang, and others, all Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Youth Federation.

    Also present as guests were: Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation; Wang Zizhong, Pastor of the North China Chinese Christian Congregational Church; Zhao Fusan, General Secretary of the North China Diocese of the Chinese Anglican Church; Zhang Dazhong, Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Youth Federation; and responsible individuals from the miners and coal mining industry trade unions, etc.

    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. After the banquet, the Chinese color film "The Butterfly Lovers" was shown.

### 中华全国民主青年联合会  设宴欢迎英国青年代表团

1954-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主青年联合会
    设宴欢迎英国青年代表团
    据新华社讯：中华全国民主青年联合会在八日晚设宴欢迎英国青年代表团全体人员。
    主人方面出席的有：中华全国民主青年联合会副主席刘导生、钱三强、区棠亮等。
    出席作陪的有：中华全国学生联合会主席田德民，华北中华基督教公理会牧师王梓仲，中华圣公会华北教区总干事赵复三，北京市民主青年联合会主席张大中以及矿工及煤矿等产业工会负责人等。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。会后，放映了我国彩色影片“梁山伯与祝英台”。
