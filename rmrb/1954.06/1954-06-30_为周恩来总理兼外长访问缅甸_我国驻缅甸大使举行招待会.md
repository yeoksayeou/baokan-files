Title: Chinese Ambassador to Burma Hosts Reception for Premier Zhou Enlai and Foreign Minister's Visit to Burma
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 29th: Yao Zhongming, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, held a reception at the Chinese Embassy at 4:00 PM on the 29th, on the occasion of the visit to Burma by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China.

    Those present at the reception included: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of the Burmese Union Parliament Sao Hkun Hkio, Supreme Court Judge U Myint Thein, as well as ministers of various ministries of the Burmese government, parliamentary secretaries of various ministries, and military leaders.

    Also present at the reception were celebrities from all walks of life and cultural figures.

    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma also attended the reception.

    Ambassador Yao Zhongming introduced the guests attending the reception to Premier Zhou Enlai for a handshake.

    The reception proceeded in a friendly and intimate atmosphere and ended at 5:30 PM.

### 为周恩来总理兼外长访问缅甸  我国驻缅甸大使举行招待会

1954-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    为周恩来总理兼外长访问缅甸
    我国驻缅甸大使举行招待会
    【新华社仰光二十九日电】中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明为中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来访问缅甸，于二十九日下午四时在中国大使馆举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有：缅甸总理吴努，缅甸联邦国会民族院议长萧恢塔，最高法院法官吴敏登，以及缅甸政府各部部长、各部国会秘书和军队首长等。
    出席招待会的还有各界名流和文化界人士。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻缅甸外交使节。
    姚仲明大使把参加招待会的来宾介绍和周恩来总理握手。
    招待会在友好亲密的空气中进行，到五时半结束。
