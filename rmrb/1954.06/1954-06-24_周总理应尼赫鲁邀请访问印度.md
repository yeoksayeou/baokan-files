Title: Premier Zhou to Visit India at Nehru's Invitation
Author:
Date: 1954-06-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China and Minister of Foreign Affairs, will visit India for a three-day stay at the invitation of Prime Minister Nehru of the Indian government.

    His entourage includes advisors Qiao Guanhua and Wang Zhuoru, secretaries Zhang Wenjin, Pu Shouchang, Ma Lie, and ten other staff members.

    Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou and his party will leave Geneva on June 24th on a special Indian Airlines plane for New Delhi.

### 周总理应尼赫鲁邀请访问印度

1954-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理应尼赫鲁邀请访问印度
    【新华社二十三日讯】中华人民共和国总理兼外交部部长周恩来，应印度政府尼赫鲁总理的邀请，访问印度，作三日的停留。
    随行者有顾问乔冠华、王倬如，秘书章文晋、浦寿昌、马列以及其他随员十人。
    周总理兼外长一行将于六月二十四日乘印度航空公司专机离日内瓦直飞新德里。
