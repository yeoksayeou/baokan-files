Title: All-China Federation of Scientific Societies Calls on Scientific Workers to Discuss Draft Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-06-19
Page: 2

The All-China Federation of Scientific Societies issued a notice calling on scientific workers across the country to widely participate in the publicity and discussion of the draft constitution. The notice said: The promulgation of the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China is a matter of great historical significance. Local branches of the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies should organize extensive discussions among members, so that everyone can understand the significance and content of the draft constitution, as well as its close relationship with the work of scientific workers. The notice requires scientific workers across the country to participate in local activities for discussing the draft constitution and to promote the publicity of the draft constitution in all aspects.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国科联号召科学工作者讨论宪法草案

1954-06-19
第2版()
专栏：

    全国科联号召科学工作者讨论宪法草案
    中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会发出通知，号召全国科学工作者广泛参加宪法草案的宣传和讨论。通知说：中华人民共和国宪法草案的公布，是一件具有伟大历史意义的事情。科联各地分会应在会员中组织广泛讨论，使大家了解到宪法草案的意义、内容以及和科学工作者的切身关系。通知要求全国科学工作者参加当地的宪法草案讨论活动，推动各方面对宪法草案的宣传工作。
                                  （新华社）
