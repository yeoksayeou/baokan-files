Title: Agreement Reached at Nine-Nation Delegation Restricted Meeting on the Question of Ceasing Hostilities in Cambodia and Laos; Representatives of Both Sides' Command to Meet Soon in Geneva or Locally
Author:
Date: 1954-06-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 19th: The Geneva Conference of the Nine-Nation Delegations issued the following communique after holding a restricted meeting on the 19th:

    The Nine-Nation Delegations continued to study the issue of restoring peace in Indochina in the restricted meeting.

    An agreement has been reached on the question of ceasing hostilities in Cambodia and Laos as follows:

    “Agreement on the Cessation of Hostilities in Cambodia and Laos

    In order to promote the simultaneous and rapid cessation of hostilities in Indochina, it is hereby recommended that:

    A. Representatives of both sides' command will meet immediately in Geneva or locally;

    B. They will study all issues related to the cessation of hostilities within Cambodia and Laos, starting with the withdrawal of all foreign armed forces and foreign military personnel from Cambodia and Laos, and giving due consideration to the opinions and suggestions put forward by the delegations of the countries participating in the conference;

    C. They will submit their conclusions and recommendations to the conference as soon as possible.”

    It was also agreed that the conference will continue. The representatives of the command dealing with the issues of Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia have been requested to submit interim or final reports to the conference within twenty-one days.

    The next meeting will be held on June 22nd.

### 关于停止在柬埔寨和老挝的敌对行动问题  九国代表团限制性会议达成协议  双方司令部代表即将在日内瓦或当地会晤

1954-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    关于停止在柬埔寨和老挝的敌对行动问题
    九国代表团限制性会议达成协议
    双方司令部代表即将在日内瓦或当地会晤
    【新华社日内瓦十九日电】九国代表团的日内瓦会议十九日在举行限制性会议后发表公报如下：
    九国代表团在限制性会议中继续研究恢复印度支那和平问题。
    关于停止在柬埔寨和老挝的敌对行动问题达成协议如下：
    “关于在柬埔寨和老挝停止敌对行动的协议
    为了促使同时并迅速停止在印度支那的敌对行动起见，兹建议：
    甲、双方司令部代表将立即在日内瓦或在当地会晤；
    乙、他们将就有关柬埔寨和老挝境内停止敌对行动的各项问题进行研究，而从撤退在柬埔寨和老挝的一切外国武装力量和外国军事人员的问题开始，并对参加会议的各国代表团所提的意见和建议给予应有的注意；
    丙、他们将尽早向会议提出他们的结论和建议。”
    又经协议，会议将继续进行。已要求处理越南、老挝和柬埔寨问题的司令部代表们在二十一天内向会议提出临时报告或最后报告。
    下一次会议将在六月二十二日举行。
