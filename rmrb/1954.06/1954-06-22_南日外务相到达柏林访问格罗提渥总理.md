Title: South Korean Foreign Minister Nam Il Arrives in Berlin to Visit Premier Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1954-06-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 21st. The Foreign Minister of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Nam Il, who returned from Geneva, arrived in Berlin by plane from Geneva on the 19th to visit the Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl. Those who welcomed him at the airport included Deputy Premier and Foreign Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Bolz; Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Mückenberger; Minister of Education, Labeß; and diplomatic envoys from fraternal countries stationed in Germany.

On the same day, Foreign Minister Nam Il visited Premier Grotewohl and held a lengthy meeting on issues of friendly cooperation between the German Democratic Republic and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in the political, economic, and cultural fields. Also present at the meeting were Bolz and the Korean Ambassador to Germany, Park Gil-ryong.

On the same day, Nam Il and other members of the Korean delegation participating in the Geneva Conference laid a wreath at the monument to the Soviet soldiers who sacrificed their lives in the war to liberate Berlin.

### 南日外务相到达柏林访问格罗提渥总理

1954-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    南日外务相到达柏林访问格罗提渥总理
    【新华社柏林二十一日电】离日内瓦回国的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日十九日乘飞机由日内瓦到达柏林，访问德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥。到机场欢迎的有：德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨，德国统一社会党中央委员会书记穆肯柏格尔，教育部长拉布斯和各兄弟国家驻德外交使节等。
    当日，南日外务相访问格罗提渥总理，就德意志民主共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国之间在政治、经济和文化上的友好合作问题作了长时间的会谈。会谈时在座的还有博尔茨和朝鲜驻德大使朴吉龙。
    同一天，南日和参加日内瓦会议的朝鲜代表团的其他团员在解放柏林的战争中牺牲的苏军战士纪念碑前献花。
