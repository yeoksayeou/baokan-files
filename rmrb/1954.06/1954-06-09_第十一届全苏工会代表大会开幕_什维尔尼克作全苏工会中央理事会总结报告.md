Title: The 11th All-Union Congress of Trade Unions Opens; Shvernik Delivers Summary Report of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-09
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th) According to TASS Moscow: The 11th All-Union Congress of Trade Unions opened on the 7th in the Kremlin, Moscow. A total of 1,364 delegates attended the congress, representing more than 40 million trade union members in the Soviet Union.

Also participating in the congress were many industrial innovators, agricultural laborers, scientists, literary and art workers, the World Federation of Trade Unions delegation, and delegations from trade unions and workers' organizations from thirty-five countries.

Members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union Voroshilov, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Khrushchev, and Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union Pospelov and Suslov were seated on the rostrum. Delegates and guests all rose to their feet, and the hall resounded with stormy, sustained applause.

The congress was opened by Shvernik, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions. On behalf of the Central Council of Trade Unions, he extended a warm welcome to the delegates and guests of the congress.

All those present stood in silence to pay tribute to the great leader, the successor to the immortal cause of Lenin, J.V. Stalin.

The congress elected the Presidium, Secretariat, Editorial Committee, and Credentials Committee.

With high enthusiasm, the congress elected members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, and Khrushchev, as the honorary presidium of the congress.

The congress adopted the following agenda: summary report of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions; summary report of the All-Union Central Auditing Commission; amendment of the All-Union Trade Union Charter; election of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions and the All-Union Central Auditing Commission.

### 第十一届全苏工会代表大会开幕  什维尔尼克作全苏工会中央理事会总结报告

1954-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    第十一届全苏工会代表大会开幕
    什维尔尼克作全苏工会中央理事会总结报告
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：第十一届全苏工会代表大会于七日在莫斯科的克里姆林宫开幕。出席大会的共有一千三百六十四个代表，代表着苏联四千万以上的工会会员。
    参加大会的还有许多工业革新者、农业劳动者、科学家、文学和艺术工作者，世界工会联合会代表团及三十五个国家的工会和工人组织的代表团。
    苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员伏罗希洛夫、马林科夫、米高扬、赫鲁晓夫和苏联共产党中央委员会书记波斯伯洛夫、苏斯洛夫在主席台上就座，代表和来宾们全体起立，会场上响起了暴风雨般的、经久不息的掌声。
    大会由全苏工会中央理事会主席什维尔尼克主持开幕。他代表工会中央理事会向大会的代表和来宾们表示热烈的欢迎。
    到会者全体起立为伟大的领袖、不朽的列宁事业的继承者约·维·斯大林默哀。
    大会选出了主席团、秘书处、编纂委员会和资格审查委员会。
    大会以高涨的热情选出苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫为大会名誉主席团。
    大会通过议程如下：全苏工会中央理事会的总结报告；全苏工会中央检查委员会的总结报告；修改全苏工会章程；选举全苏工会中央理事会和全苏工会中央检查委员会。
    苏共中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫在会上讲话。他宣读了苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议致大会的贺电，代表和来宾们起立，报以热烈的欢呼。
    接着，什维尔尼克在会上作了全苏工会中央理事会的总结报告。
    全苏工会中央检查委员会主席萨夫科夫接着向大会作了中央检查委员会的总结报告。
