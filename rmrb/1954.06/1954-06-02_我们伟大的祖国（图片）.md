    Our Great Motherland
    On the eve of "June 1st" International Children's Day, the children of the capital held a celebration party at the Altar of Land and Grain in Zhongshan Park. The Young Pioneers sang and danced, and every child was immersed in an atmosphere of happiness, joy, and delight.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    “六一”国际儿童节前夕的夜晚，首都儿童在中山公园社稷坛举行了庆祝“六一”联欢晚会。少年先锋队员们载歌载舞，每一个儿童都浸沉在幸福、愉快和欢乐的气氛里。
