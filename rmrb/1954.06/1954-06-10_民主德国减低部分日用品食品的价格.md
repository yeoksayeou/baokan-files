Title: German Democratic Republic Reduces Prices of Some Daily Necessities and Food

Author:

Date: 1954-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 7th: TASS reports: The Ministry of Trade and Supply of the German Democratic Republic announced that, starting from June 8th, retail trade organizations will reduce the prices of certain pigskin leather shoes by approximately 40%; canned fish by approximately 10%; and cigars by approximately 20%.

　　The prices of pasta products sold by state-owned trading organizations will be reduced by approximately 30%; and animal fats by approximately 20%.

### 民主德国减低部分日用品食品的价格

1954-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国减低部分日用品食品的价格
   【新华社八日讯】塔斯社柏林七日讯：德意志民主共和国贸易与供应部宣布，自六月八日起，零售贸易机关将把若干种猪皮皮鞋的价格约减低百分之四十；罐头鱼类—约百分之十；雪茄—约百分之二十。
    国营贸易机关的面食类的价格将减低约百分之三十；动物油约减低百分之二十。
