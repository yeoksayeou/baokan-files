Title: Chinese Delegation and U.S. Delegation Hold Talks on the Issue of Nationals

Author: 
Date: 1954-06-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 5th. The delegation of the People's Republic of China and the delegation of the United States of America attending the Geneva Conference held preliminary talks on the issue of nationals of the two countries on the morning of the 4th. The Chinese side participating in the talks included Mr. Wang Bingnan, Mr. Ke Bonian, and Mr. Huan Xiang; the American side included Mr. U. Alexis Johnson and Mr. Edwin W. Martin. Mr. D.W. Latham of Great Britain introduced the two parties.

### 我国代表团和美国代表团  就两国侨民问题举行商谈

1954-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    我国代表团和美国代表团
    就两国侨民问题举行商谈
   【新华社日内瓦五日电】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团与美国代表团之间已于四日上午就两国侨民问题举行了初步商谈。参加商谈的中国方面有王炳南先生、柯柏年先生、宦乡先生；美国方面有乌·亚历克西斯·约翰逊先生、埃德温·吴·马丁先生。英国的杜维廉先生介绍双方见面。
