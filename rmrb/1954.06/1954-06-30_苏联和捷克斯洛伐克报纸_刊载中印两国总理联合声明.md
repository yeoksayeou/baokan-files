Title: Soviet and Czechoslovakian Newspapers Publish the Joint Statement of the Prime Ministers of China and India
Author:
Date: 1954-06-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 29th. Moscow newspapers on the 29th published the joint statement of Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of China, and Indian Prime Minister Nehru, as well as the written statement made by Premier Zhou Enlai at a press conference in New Delhi on the 27th. The newspapers also published the news of Premier Zhou Enlai's flight to Rangoon on the 28th. "Pravda" also published a dispatch by its Delhi correspondent, Orestov, entitled "Friendship of Great Peoples."

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 29th. Czechoslovakian newspapers all published the joint statement of the Prime Ministers of China and India on the 29th. "Red Rights" and "People's Defense" also published the full text of the speeches by the Prime Ministers of China and India at the banquet held by Nehru on the 26th, as well as the written statement read by Premier Zhou Enlai at the press conference held in New Delhi on the 27th.

### 苏联和捷克斯洛伐克报纸  刊载中印两国总理联合声明

1954-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联和捷克斯洛伐克报纸
    刊载中印两国总理联合声明
    【新华社莫斯科二十九日电】莫斯科各报二十九日登载了我中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来和印度总理尼赫鲁的联合声明和周恩来总理二十七日在新德里记者招待会上所发表的书面谈话。各报同时登载了周恩来总理在二十八日飞赴仰光的消息。“真理报”还发表了驻德里记者奥列斯托夫的题为
  “伟大人民的友谊”的通讯。
    【新华社布拉格二十九日电】捷克斯洛伐克各报二十九日都刊载了中印两国总理联合声明。“红色权利报”和“人民防线报”还刊载了中印两国总理在二十六日尼赫鲁举行的宴会上的致词全文，以及周恩来总理二十七日在新德里举行的记者招待会上所宣读的书面谈话。
