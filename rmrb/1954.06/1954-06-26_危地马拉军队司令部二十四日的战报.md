Title: Guatemala Army Headquarters' War Report of the 24th
Author:
Date: 1954-06-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London, June 25th: London Radio broadcast the war report of the Guatemala Army Headquarters on the morning of June 24th, saying that the Guatemalan army had captured a large number of interventionist bandits, including many foreigners of different nationalities and some Guatemalans. The war report also mentioned that a large amount of military supplies were seized, including machine guns and explosives.

### 危地马拉军队司令部二十四日的战报

1954-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

    危地马拉军队司令部二十四日的战报
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十四日讯：伦敦电台广播危地马拉军队司令部六月二十四日的上午战报说，危地马拉军队俘掳了大批干涉匪徒，其中包括许多不同国籍的外国人和一些危地马拉人。战报中还谈到，缴获了大批军用物资，包括机关枪和炸药等。
