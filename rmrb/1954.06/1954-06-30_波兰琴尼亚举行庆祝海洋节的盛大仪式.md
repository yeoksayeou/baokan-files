Title: Grand Ceremony Held in Gdynia, Poland, to Celebrate Maritime Day
Author:
Date: 1954-06-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 29th: The Polish people enthusiastically celebrated Maritime Day on the 27th. A grand celebration ceremony was held in Gdynia. Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee First Secretary Bolesław Bierut, Polish People's Republic Chairman of the Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz, Polish Marshal Konstanty Rokossowski, and others participated in the celebration ceremony. After the ceremony began, a review of the Polish Marine Corps was first held in Gdynia, followed by a review of the naval fleet at sea. In the evening, a celebratory party was held in the city. The Polish Naval Song and Dance Ensemble and the Soviet State Folk Dance Ensemble, led by Moiseyev, which is currently visiting Poland, participated in the party's performance.

On the same day, shipyard workers in Gdańsk, Gdynia, and Szczecin also celebrated Maritime Day. Various shipyards held celebrations, and many advanced shipyard workers received state medals and bonuses at the meetings.

### 波兰琴尼亚举行庆祝海洋节的盛大仪式

1954-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰琴尼亚举行庆祝海洋节的盛大仪式
   【新华社二十九日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰人民二十七日热烈庆祝了海洋节。在琴尼亚举行了盛大的庆祝仪式。波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特、波兰人民共和国部长会议主席西伦凯维兹、波兰元帅罗科索夫斯基等都参加了庆祝仪式。仪式开始后，首先在琴尼亚城内举行波兰海军陆战队的检阅式，接着在海上举行了海军舰队的检阅式。晚间在城内举行了庆祝节日的晚会。参加晚会演出的有波兰海军歌舞团和正在波兰访问的、由莫伊谢耶夫领导的苏联国立民间歌舞团。
    同日，革但斯克、琴尼亚和斯才新的造船工人也庆祝了海洋节。各造船厂纷纷举行了庆祝会，许多先进的造船工人在会上领到了国家奖章和奖金。
