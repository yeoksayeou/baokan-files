Title: Indonesian Foreign Minister Welcomes India's Invitation to Premier Zhou; Indonesian Public Values News of Premier Zhou's Visit to India and the Announcement of China's Draft Constitution

Author:
Date: 1954-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 25th: A message from Calcutta says: On June 22nd, the Indonesian Foreign Minister, Dr. Sunarjo, who was en route to The Hague via Dum Dum Airport in Calcutta, welcomed Prime Minister Nehru's invitation to Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai to visit Delhi.

At the airport, he told reporters: "I think Nehru's move will indeed be very beneficial."

He said their policy was to strengthen Asian unity, and it was especially important to unite other Asian countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 25th: On the 24th, Jakarta newspapers published the news of Premier Zhou Enlai's upcoming visit to India and the meeting between Premier Zhou Enlai and French Premier Mendès-France with headline titles. The title of the "Indonesia Torch" newspaper's news about Premier Zhou's visit to India was: "Representing Nine Hundred Million People." The title of the "Independent" newspaper's news about the meeting between Premier Zhou and Mendès-France was: "Seeking Conditions for Peace."

The news of the announcement of China's draft constitution has been valued by the Indonesian public. Recently, many newspapers have published the main points of China's draft constitution.

### 印尼外交部长对印度邀请周总理表示欢迎  印尼公众重视周总理访问印度和我国公布宪法草案消息

1954-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼外交部长对印度邀请周总理表示欢迎
    印尼公众重视周总理访问印度和我国公布宪法草案消息
    【新华社新德里二十五日电】来自加尔各答的消息说：六月二十二日，途经加尔各答达姆达姆机场前往海牙的印度尼西亚外交部部长苏纳约博士，对尼赫鲁总理邀请中国总理周恩来到德里来访问的行动表示欢迎。
    他在机场对记者发表谈话说：“我认为尼赫鲁的这个举动的确会是非常有益的。”
    他说，他们的政策是加强亚洲的团结，尤其重要的是必须团结其他亚洲国家。
    【新华社雅加达二十五日电】二十四日雅加达各报以头条标题刊登了周恩来总理将要访问印度以及周恩来总理和法国总理孟戴斯—弗朗斯会晤的消息。“印度尼西亚火炬报”刊载周总理访问印度的消息的标题是：“代表着九亿人民”。“独立报”刊载周总理和孟戴斯—弗朗斯会晤的消息的标题是：“寻求和平的条件”。
    我国公布宪法草案的消息受到印尼公众的重视。最近许多报纸都刊载了我国宪法草案的要点。
