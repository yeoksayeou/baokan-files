Title: Albanian People's Assembly Election Ends; Labor Party-led Democratic Front Achieves Glorious Victory
Author:
Date: 1954-06-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 4th: Albanian newspapers published the communique of the Central Election Commission regarding the results of the Albanian People's Assembly election held on May 30th. The communique stated that out of 700,2476 registered voters, 700,1942 people participated in the voting, accounting for 99.92 percent of the total number of voters. 700,983 people voted for the candidates of the Democratic Front led by the Albanian Labor Party, accounting for 99.86 percent of all voters. All 134 candidates of the Democratic Front were elected.

"People's Voice" wrote: The election results prove that "the people of all nationalities in the Democratic Front led by the Labor Party are united as one." The newspaper pointed out that the election on May 30 was a glorious victory for the Democratic Front, reflecting the people's complete support for the domestic and foreign policies pursued by the Party and the government.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民议会选举结束  劳动党领导的民主阵线获得光辉胜利

1954-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会选举结束
    劳动党领导的民主阵线获得光辉胜利
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社地拉那四日讯：阿尔巴尼亚各报刊载了中央选举委员会关于五月三十日举行的阿尔巴尼亚人民议会选举结果的公报。公报说：在七十万零二千四百七十六个登记的选民中，有七十万零一千九百四十二人参加了投票，占选民总数百分之九十九点九二。有七十万零九百八十三人投票选举阿尔巴尼亚劳动党领导的民主阵线候选人，即占全部投票选民百分之九十九点八六。民主阵线的一百三十四名候选人全部当选。
    “人民之声报”写道：选举结果证明“劳动党领导的民主阵线中的各族人民坚如磐石地团结在一起”。该报指出，五月三十日的选举是民主阵线的光辉的胜利，它反映出人民完全拥护党和政府所奉行的对内和外交政策。
