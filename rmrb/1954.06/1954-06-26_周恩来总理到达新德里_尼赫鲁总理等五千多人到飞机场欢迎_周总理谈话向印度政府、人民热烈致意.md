Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Arrives in New Delhi; Prime Minister Nehru and Over 5,000 Others Welcome Him at the Airport; Premier Zhou's Remarks Convey Warm Greetings to the Indian Government and People
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 25th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and his delegation of sixteen arrived in New Delhi, the capital of India, at 7:15 a.m. on the 25th.

Prime Minister Nehru of India personally welcomed him at the airport.

Yuan Zhongxian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, led the embassy staff to the airport to welcome him.

Also present at the airport to welcome him were: Major General Chatterjee, representative of the President of India; Minister of Home Affairs, Kailas Nath Katju; Minister of Railways and Transport, Lal Bahadur Shastri; Minister of Planning, Irrigation and Power, Gulzarilal Nanda; Minister of Defence, Mahavir Tyagi; Minister of Food and Agriculture, Panjabrao S. Deshmukh; Deputy Speaker of the People's House of Parliament, M. Ananthasayanam Ayyangar; Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, A. K. Chanda; Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, N. R. Pillai; Foreign Secretary, R. K. Nehru; Commonwealth Secretary, S. Dutt; Chairman of the States Reorganisation Commission and former Ambassador to China, K. M. Panikkar; as well as senior officials such as Deputy Ministers and Secretaries-General of various ministries of the central government of India.

Also present at the airport to welcome him were: General Maharaj Rajendrasinhji, Commander-in-Chief of the Indian Army; Vice Admiral Sir Mark Pizey, Commander-in-Chief of the Indian Navy; and Air Marshal Subroto Mukerjee, Commander-in-Chief of the Indian Air Force.

Also present at the airport to welcome him were: A. D. Pandit, Chief Commissioner of Delhi State; Prakash, Chief Minister of Delhi State; and P. L. Mehta, Inspector-General of Police of Delhi, among others.

Also present at the airport to welcome him were: Indian Members of Parliament P. Sundarayya and T. Singh, among others; Chung Bahadur Singh, Member of the Delhi Legislative Assembly; B. Mehta, Secretary of the Congress Party; K. Nair of the Congress Party Delhi Regional Organization; E. M. S. Namboodiripad and P. Ramamurti, members of the Politburo of the Communist Party of India; Radha Raman, Chairman of the Delhi Branch of the India-China Friendship Association; and representatives of the All-India Peace Council and other people's organizations.

More than 50 overseas Chinese from Delhi were also present at the airport to welcome him.

Also present at the airport to welcome him were diplomatic envoys from various countries to India: Kamal, Ambassador of Egypt; K. Komaraswamy, High Commissioner of Ceylon; Van Boetzelaer, Ambassador of the Netherlands; Bara, Ambassador of Indonesia; Al-Radi, Ambassador of Iraq; Hugo Valvanne, Ambassador of Finland; Soudar Abdul Hosseini, Ambassador of Afghanistan; Middleton, Deputy High Commissioner of the United Kingdom; Toiberg-Fransen, Minister of Denmark; Wickman, Minister of Sweden; Løke, Minister of Norway; Glassli, Minister of Switzerland; Kos, Minister of Hungary; U Maung Maung Soe, Chargé d'affaires of Burma; Antoch, Chargé d'affaires of Czechoslovakia; Aszuraf, Chargé d'affaires of the Soviet Union; Dowlatshahi, Chargé d'affaires of Iran; and Singha, Chargé d'affaires of Nepal.

Around 5,000 people from all walks of life in Delhi also gathered near the airport to welcome him.

The national flags of India and the People's Republic of China were flying at the Delhi airport.

After Premier Zhou Enlai alighted from the plane, he shook hands with Prime Minister Nehru and then reviewed the Indian guard of honour. The band played the national anthem of the People's Republic of China.

Premier Zhou Enlai accepted flowers presented by representatives of the India-China Friendship Association and overseas Chinese children. Then, Premier Zhou walked to the crowd near the airport and waved to the welcomers. At this moment, countless flowers and confetti were thrown at Premier Zhou, and the airport resounded with cheers of "Long Live India-China Friendship!" and "Long Live World Peace!"

Premier Zhou Enlai then delivered the following speech:

  "Respected Prime Minister,
  Ladies and Gentlemen:

      I am deeply honored to have the opportunity to visit our great neighbor, India, at the invitation of Prime Minister Nehru. I would like to extend my warm greetings on behalf of the Chinese government and people to the Indian government and people.

      The Chinese government and people highly value the friendship with the Indian government and people. The peaceful friendship between the 960 million people of China and India is an important factor in maintaining peace in Asia and the world.

      May the friendship between China and India develop day by day.

      May the unity of the peoples of Asian countries become closer and closer.

      May world peace be consolidated day by day."

Finally, Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Prime Minister Nehru, drove to the Presidential Palace for rest.

### 周恩来总理到达新德里  尼赫鲁总理等五千多人到飞机场欢迎  周总理谈话向印度政府、人民热烈致意

1954-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理到达新德里
    尼赫鲁总理等五千多人到飞机场欢迎
    周总理谈话向印度政府、人民热烈致意
      【新华社新德里二十五日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来一行十六人在二十五日上午七时十五分到达印度首都新德里。
    印度总理尼赫鲁亲自到机场欢迎。
    中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤率领大使馆人员到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有：印度总统代表陆军少将查特吉，内政部长凯·纳·卡特朱，铁道与运输部长拉·巴·夏斯特里，计划、水利工程与电力部部长古·南达，国防部长马·特雅吉，粮食与农业部长潘·斯·德希穆克，国会人民院副议长阿·阿阳加尔，外交部副部长阿·库·钱达，外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱，外事秘书拉·库·尼赫鲁，英联邦秘书斯·杜特，省区划分委员会委员、前驻中国大使潘尼迦以及印度中央政府各部副部长、秘书长等高级官员。
    到机场欢迎的还有：印度陆军总司令马哈拉志·拉金德拉辛吉，海军总司令克·特·麦·皮泽，空军总司令斯·慕克吉。
    到机场欢迎的还有：德里邦行政专员阿·德·潘迪特，德里邦首席部长帕卡希，德里警察总监普·梅达等。
    到机场欢迎的还有：印度国会议员普·孙达拉雅和塔·辛格等人，德里邦立法会议议员忠格·巴哈杜尔·辛格，国大党书记布·梅达，国大党德里地方组织的克·奈尔，印度共产党中央政治局委员埃·麦·斯·南布德里巴德和普·拉马穆蒂，印中友好协会德里分会主席拉达·拉曼以及全印和平理事会和其他人民团体的代表多人。
    到机场欢迎的还有德里华侨五十余人。
    到机场欢迎的还有各国驻印度外交使节：埃及大使卡迈尔，锡兰高级专员克·科马拉斯瓦米，荷兰大使范·柏兰特，印度尼西亚大使巴拉，伊拉克大使阿尔—拉迪，芬兰大使雨果·瓦尔万尼，阿富汗大使苏达尔·阿卜杜勒·侯赛尼，英国副高级专员密德尔顿，丹麦公使托伊伯格—弗朗森，瑞典公使威克曼，挪威公使李克，瑞士公使格拉斯里，匈牙利公使科斯，缅甸代办吴貌貌梭，捷克斯洛伐克代办安托希，巴基斯坦代办艾瑟尔，苏联代办阿舒罗夫，伊朗代办道拉特夏尼，尼泊尔代办辛加。
    在机场附近欢迎的还有：德里各界群众五千人。
    德里机场上悬挂着印度和中华人民共和国的国旗。
    周恩来总理下机后，即与尼赫鲁总理握手，随即检阅了印度仪仗队。乐队奏起中华人民共和国国歌。
    周恩来总理接受了印中友好协会代表和华侨儿童的献花。接着，周总理到机场附近的群众行列面前，向欢迎者挥手致意。此时，无数鲜花、彩纸纷纷向周总理迎面投来，机场响起一片“印中友好万岁！”“世界和平万岁！”的欢呼。
      周恩来总理接着发表谈话如下：
  “尊敬的总理先生
  诸位女士和先生：
      我承尼赫鲁总理的邀请，获有机会访问我
  国的伟大邻邦，深感荣幸。我愿代表中国政府
  和人民向印度政府和人民热烈地致意。
      中国政府和人民十分重视与印度政府和人
  民的友谊。中印两国九亿六千万人民的和平友
  好是维护亚洲及世界和平的一个重要因素。
      愿中印两国的友谊一天比一天发展。
      愿亚洲各国人民的团结一天比一天紧密。
      愿世界的和平一天比一天巩固。”
    最后，周恩来总理在尼赫鲁总理陪同下乘车赴总统府休息。
