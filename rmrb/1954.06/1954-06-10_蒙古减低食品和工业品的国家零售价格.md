Title: Mongolia Reduces National Retail Prices of Food and Industrial Products
Author:
Date: 1954-06-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, June 5th: The Mongolian newspaper "Unen" published on the 1st an important decision by the Mongolian People's Republic Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party regarding the reduction of national retail prices for food and industrial products. The decision stipulates reductions in the prices of 30 kinds of daily necessities in the food sector, such as first and second-grade flour, reduced by 6 to 23.1 percent; rice, reduced by 18.5 percent; and noodles and cellophane noodles, reduced by 14.7 to 23.9 percent. In the industrial products sector, the prices of 19 kinds of daily necessities were reduced, such as candles, hunting rifles, soft lambskin boots, soap, matches, etc., with price reductions ranging from 16.6 to 54.6 percent.

    An editorial published in the Mongolian newspaper "Unen" pointed out that, according to this decision to reduce national retail prices of food and industrial products, the people of the whole country will save 20 million Tugriks (Mongolian currency) in expenses within one year. This will greatly improve the living standards and purchasing power of all the people and increase the value of the Mongolian currency.

### 蒙古减低食品和工业品的国家零售价格

1954-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古减低食品和工业品的国家零售价格
   【新华社五日讯】乌兰巴托消息：蒙古“真理报”一日发表了蒙古人民共和国部长会议和蒙古人民革命党中央委员会关于减低食品和工业品国家零售价格的重要决定。该决定规定在食品方面减低广大人民日常必需的三十种食品价格，如一、二等面粉减低价格百分之六到二十三点一，大米减低价格百分之十八点五，挂面、粉条减低价格百分之十四点七到二十三点九；在工业品方面，减低了十九种日用品的价格，如蜡烛、猎枪、柔羊皮靴、肥皂、火柴等，减低价格百分之十六点六到五十四点六。
    蒙古“真理报”发表的社论指出：根据这项减低食品和工业品国家零售价格的决定，全国人民将在一年内即可节省两千万图格里克（蒙币）的费用，这就大大提高了全体人民的生活水平和购买力，并提高了蒙币的币值。
