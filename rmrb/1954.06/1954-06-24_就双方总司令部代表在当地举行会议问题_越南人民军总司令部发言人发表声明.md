Title: Statement by Spokesperson of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army on the Issue of a Meeting Between Representatives of the General Headquarters of Both Sides in the Area
Author:
Date: 1954-06-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: According to Vietnam News Agency, June 23rd: The spokesperson of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army issued a statement on June 20th regarding the issue of a meeting between representatives of the General Headquarters of both sides in the area. The full text of the statement is as follows:

    At the meetings on May 29th and June 2nd, 1954, the representatives of the Geneva Conference reached an agreement in principle: the representatives of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army and the representatives of the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force in Indochina should meet in the area.

    At the meeting held north of Phu Lang Thuong on June 11th between representatives of the General Headquarters of both sides to discuss the exchange of sick and wounded prisoners of war, the representative of the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force indicated that he wished to know the intentions of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army in transporting medicines and mail to prisoners of war and evacuating sick and wounded prisoners of war in accordance with the agreements of the Geneva Conference. On June 14th, the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army wrote a letter to the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force stating:
      (1) The General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army is prepared to discuss the transportation of medicines and letters to prisoners of war and the evacuation of sick and wounded prisoners of war in accordance with the agreements between the two sides at the Geneva Conference.
      (2) The General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army considers it necessary to organize a formal meeting of representatives of the General Headquarters of both sides in accordance with the agreements of the Geneva Conference, not only to discuss the issue of prisoners of war, but also to discuss other military issues.

    On June 17th, the representative of the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force stated that the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force was willing to organize a meeting of representatives of both sides in the area.

    On June 19th, the liaison officer of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army and the liaison officer of the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force met to prepare for this formal meeting.

### 就双方总司令部代表在当地举行会议问题  越南人民军总司令部发言人发表声明

1954-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    就双方总司令部代表在当地举行会议问题
    越南人民军总司令部发言人发表声明
    【新华社二十三日讯】据越南通讯社二十三日讯：越南人民军总司令部发言人六月二十日就双方总司令部代表在当地举行会议问题发表声明。声明全文如下：
    日内瓦会议的代表们在一九五四年五月二十九日和六月二日的会议上在原则上取得协议：越南人民军总司令部的代表和印度支那法国远征军总司令部的代表应在当地会晤。
    在双方总司令部代表六月十一日在府谅商以北举行的讨论交换伤病战俘的会议上，法国远征军总司令部代表表示，他愿意知道越南人民军总司令部在按照日内瓦会议的协议运送药品和邮件给战俘以及撤退伤、病战俘问题上的意图。在六月十四日，越南人民军总司令部写了一封信给法国远征军总司令部说：
      （一）越南人民军总司令部准备按照双方在日内瓦会议上的协议讨论运送药品和信件给战俘以及撤退伤、病战俘问题。
      （二）越南人民军总司令部认为有必要按照日内瓦会议的协议组织双方总司令部的正式代表的会议，不仅来讨论战俘问题，并且讨论其他军事问题。
    六月十七日，法国远征军总司令部代表表示，法国远征军总司令部愿意组织双方代表在当地的会议。
    六月十九日，越南人民军总司令部的联络官和法国远征军总司令部的联络官会见，以准备这次正式会议。
