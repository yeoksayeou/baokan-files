Title: Tokyo Rally of 20,000 Demands Yoshida Cabinet Resign
Author:
Date: 1954-06-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 21st: A mass rally of approximately 20,000 people was held in Tokyo on the 19th to protest the Yoshida government's violent act of dispatching police to beat opposition party members. The rally was hosted by the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo) and other organizations, with participating masses being members of "Sohyo" and other trade union organizations.
    The rally passed resolutions demanding the resignation of the Yoshida cabinet, the dissolution of the House of Representatives of the Diet, the prohibition of the testing and use of atomic and hydrogen bombs, and the safeguarding of students' right to vote. After the meeting, a demonstration was held, with the masses chanting slogans such as "Down with the Yoshida Cabinet."

### 东京两万人集会要求吉田内阁辞职

1954-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    东京两万人集会要求吉田内阁辞职
    【新华社二十一日讯】东京消息：十九日东京举行了约有两万人参加的群众大会，抗议吉田政府出动警察殴打在野党议员的暴行。大会由日本工会总评议会等主持，参加群众是“总评”和其它工会组织的会员。
    大会通过了要求吉田内阁辞职、解散国会众议院、禁止试验和使用原子弹和氢弹、维护学生选举权等项决议。会后举行了示威游行，群众高呼“打倒吉田内阁”的口号。
