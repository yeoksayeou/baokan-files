Title: Dorje Phuntsok Rabgye and Others Depart Beijing to Return to Tibet
Author:
Date: 1954-06-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency report: Dorje Phuntsok Rabgye, head of the Tibetan National Day Observation Group, and Kaga Zhang Luosangrenzeng, deputy head of the group, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 28th for Chongqing to return to Tibet.
    Those who saw them off at the airport were: Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government; Yu Yifu, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee; Zhang Zhiyi, Deputy Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government; Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and member of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government; Zhao Fan, member of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government; Gan Chunlei, Director of the General Office; Pengsuke and Liu Guanying, Deputy Directors of the General Office; Ranba Nangji Wangdui, Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing; Luowang, Acting Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing; Lian Huizhu, Deputy Director; and Nawang Jinba, Deputy Head of the Tibetan National Day Observation Group, who remained in Beijing on official business.

### 朵噶·彭错饶杰等离京返藏

1954-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    朵噶·彭错饶杰等离京返藏
    据新华社讯：西藏国庆观礼团团长朵噶·彭错饶杰、副团长噶章·罗桑仁增在二十八日晨乘飞机离北京赴重庆返西藏。
    到机场欢送的有：中共中央统一战线工作部部长、中央人民政府民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉，中共中央统一战线工作部副部长于毅夫，中央民族事务委员会副主任委员张执一，人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任、中央民族事务委员会委员朱早观，中央民族事务委员会委员赵范，办公厅主任甘春雷、副主任朋斯克、刘冠英等。西藏达赖驻京办事处处长然巴·囊吉旺堆，班禅驻京办事处代理处长罗旺、副处长联慧珠以及因公留京的西藏国庆观礼团副团长纳旺金巴等也到机场欢送。
