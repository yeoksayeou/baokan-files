Title: All-India Peace Council Welcomes Meeting Between Chinese and Indian Prime Ministers
Author:
Date: 1954-06-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 27th: The Secretariat of the All-India Peace Council has issued a statement welcoming the meeting between Premier Zhou Enlai and Indian Prime Minister Nehru. The statement said: "June 25th will not only be remembered by the people of China and India as a day of historical significance, but also by the people of Asia and the world."
The statement added: "Only through such joint consultations between Asian countries can peace in Asia be won and maintained, and the rights to national independence and self-determination of the people of Asian countries be guaranteed."

### 全印和平理事会欢迎中印两国总理会谈

1954-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    全印和平理事会欢迎中印两国总理会谈
    【新华社新德里二十七日电】全印和平理事会秘书处发表声明，欢迎周恩来总理和印度总理尼赫鲁举行会谈。声明说：“六月二十五日将不仅被中国和印度两国人民作为具有历史意义的日子而记在心中，而且也将被亚洲和世界的各国人民记在心中。”
    声明又说：“只有在亚洲国家之间进行这种共同磋商，亚洲的和平才能赢得和保持下去，亚洲各国人民的民族独立和自决的权利才能得到保障。”
