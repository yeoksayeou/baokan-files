Title: Churchill and Eden Arrive in Washington
Author:
Date: 1954-06-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, June 26th: TASS Washington, June 26th: British Prime Minister Churchill and Foreign Secretary Eden arrived in Washington by plane on June 25th to meet with US President Eisenhower. Those welcoming them at the airport included US Vice President Nixon and Secretary of State Dulles.

### 邱吉尔和艾登到达华盛顿

1954-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    邱吉尔和艾登到达华盛顿
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社华盛顿二十六日讯：英国首相邱吉尔和外交大臣艾登在六月二十五日乘飞机抵达华盛顿，与美国总统艾森豪威尔会谈。在机场欢迎的有：美国副总统尼克松和国务卿杜勒斯。
