Title: State-owned, Locally State-owned, and Joint State-Private Key Industries Exceed May's Total Output Value Plan
Author:
Date: 1954-06-20
Page: 1
State-owned, locally state-owned, and joint state-private key industrial enterprises exceeded the total output value plan for May by 103.1 percent, accumulating to a 37 percent increase compared to the same period last year by the end of May. Specifically, state-owned enterprises completed 103.4 percent of the plan, locally state-owned enterprises 102.7 percent, centrally administered joint state-private enterprises 104 percent, and locally administered joint state-private enterprises 100.4 percent. Among the twenty-four important products of the aforementioned key enterprises, eighteen, including electricity and raw coal, met or exceeded their planned targets.

The five central industrial ministries collectively completed 103.5 percent of the total output value plan for May, accumulating to a 36.2 percent increase compared to the same period last year by the end of May. Among these, the Ministry of Heavy Industry completed 104.5 percent of the plan, the Ministry of Fuel Industry 102 percent, the First Ministry of Machine Building 101.1 percent, the Ministry of Textile Industry 102.8 percent, and the Ministry of Light Industry 106.1 percent. Among the forty-seven important products of the five industrial ministries, thirty-five, including rolled steel products and metallurgical coke, met or exceeded their production plans.

### 国营、地方国营和公私合营的重点工业  超额完成五月份总产值计划

1954-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    国营、地方国营和公私合营的重点工业
    超额完成五月份总产值计划
    【本报讯】国营、地方国营和公私合营的重点工业企业，五月份总产值计划总计完成了百分之一○三·一，到五月止累计较去年同期增长了百分之三七。其中国营企业完成计划百分之一○三·四，地方国营企业为一○二·七，中央公私合营企业为一○四，地方公私合营企业为一○○·四。上述重点企业二十四种重要产品中，完成或超额完成计划的有电力、原煤等十八种。
    中央五个工业部五月份总产值计划合计完成了百分之一○三·五，到五月止累计较去年同期增长了百分之三六·二。其中重工业部完成计划百分之一○四·五，燃料工业部为一○二，第一机械工业部为一○一·一，纺织工业部为一○二·八，轻工业部为一○六·一。五个工业部的四十七种重要产品中，有钢压延品、冶金焦等三十五种完成或超额完成了生产计划。
