Title: After the Eisenhower-Churchill Talks, the United States Will Convene the ANZUS Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-06-30
Page: 4
Column:

    Following the Eisenhower-Churchill talks
    The United States to convene ANZUS Council meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 29th] TASS Washington, 28th: Many American newspapers, citing staff members of the Australian Embassy in the United States, reported that the ANZUS Council meeting (without British participation - editor) will be held in Washington on June 30th. The trilateral military treaty concluded by Australia, New Zealand, and the United States is regarded as an example of the aggressive bloc that the United States is planning in Southeast Asia.
    The newspapers pointed out that the ANZUS Council meeting, convened immediately after the US-British talks between Eisenhower and Churchill, is intended to show that on the existing differences between the US and British governments, the Commonwealth members – Australia and New Zealand – do not agree with the British position.

### 在艾森豪威尔和邱吉尔会谈后  美国将召开澳新美三国会议

1954-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    在艾森豪威尔和邱吉尔会谈后
    美国将召开澳新美三国会议
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社华盛顿二十八日讯：许多美国报纸引澳大利亚驻美国大使馆工作人员的谈话报道说，六月三十日在华盛顿将召开澳新美理事会（英国未参加——编者）会议，澳大利亚、新西兰和美国三个国家所缔结的三边军事条约被认为是美国在东南亚策划中的侵略集团的一个范例。
    各报指出，在艾森豪威尔和邱吉尔的美英会谈结束后立即召开的澳新美理事会会议是要显示：在美英两国政府现存的分歧上，英联邦成员国——澳大利亚和新西兰——是不赞同英国的立场的。
