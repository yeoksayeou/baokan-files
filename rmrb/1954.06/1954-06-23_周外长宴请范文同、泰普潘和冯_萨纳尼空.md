Title: Zhou Enlai Hosts Banquet for Pham Van Dong, Tep Phan, and Phouang Sananikone
Author:
Date: 1954-06-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 21st. Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the People's Republic of China Delegation to the Geneva Conference, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 21st for Pham Van Dong, head of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, as well as Tep Phan, head of the delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and Phouang Sananikone, head of the delegation of the Kingdom of Laos.

    Also attending from the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam were Phan Anh, Tran Cong Tuong, Hoang Van Hoan, and Ta Quang Buu.

    Also attending from the delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia were: Sang Sary, Song San, and Poc Thoen.

    Also attending from the delegation of the Kingdom of Laos were: Oura Souvannavong and Dao Leunam.

    Attending as guests from the Chinese delegation were: Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, Li Kenong, Wang Bingnan, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, and Wang Zhuoru.

    The banquet proceeded in a harmonious atmosphere.

### 周外长宴请范文同、泰普潘和冯·萨纳尼空

1954-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    周外长宴请范文同、泰普潘和冯·萨纳尼空
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团首席代表周恩来，二十一日晚宴请越南民主共和国代表团团长范文同，以及柬埔寨王国代表团团长泰普潘和老挝王国代表团团长冯·萨纳尼空。
    越南民主共和国代表团方面出席的还有潘英、陈公祥、黄文欢、谢光宝。
    柬埔寨王国代表团方面出席的还有：桑·萨里、松山、朴天。
    老挝王国代表团方面出席的还有：奥拉·苏发拉冯、道勒南。
    中国代表团出席作陪的有：张闻天、王稼祥、李克农、王炳南、乔冠华、陈家康、王倬如。
    宴会在和谐的空气中进行。
