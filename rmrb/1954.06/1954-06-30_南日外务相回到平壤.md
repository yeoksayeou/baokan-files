Title: Foreign Minister Nam Il Returns to Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1954-06-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 29th: The delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Geneva Conference, headed by Foreign Minister Nam Il, returned to Pyongyang by plane on the afternoon of the 29th.

　　Those who welcomed them at the airport included Li Young, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea; Ha Ang-cheon, Minister of Propaganda and Agitation of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Heo Jeong-suk, Minister of Culture and Propaganda of the Cabinet; and Lee Dong-geon, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.

　　Soviet Embassy in Korea Chargé d'affaires Lazarov, Chinese Embassy in Korea Chargé d'affaires Gan Yetao, and diplomatic officials from other People's Democratic countries stationed in Korea also welcomed them at the airport.

1954-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    南日外务相回到平壤
   【新华社平壤二十九日电】以外务相南日为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团
