Title: Hungarian Working People's Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session; Elects New Politburo, Secretariat, and Central Control Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-06-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 1st: According to TASS, the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, elected by the Third Congress, held a plenary session on May 30th. The plenary session elected the new Politburo and Secretariat, as well as the Central Control Committee.
    The Central Committee Politburo consists of nine members and two alternate members. Elected as members of the Politburo were: Mátyás Rákosi, Imre Nagy, Ernő Gerő, Mihály Farkas, József Révai, István Hidas, Antal Apró, András Hegedüs, and Béla Szalai. Elected as alternate members of the Politburo were: István Bata and József Mekis.
    The Central Committee elected the following five people to form the Secretariat: Mátyás Rákosi, Mihály Farkas, József Révai, Béla Vég, and János Matolcsi.
    Mátyás Rákosi was elected First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party.
    The Central Committee elected the Central Control Committee, composed of eleven members and two alternate members. Károly Kiss was elected Chairman of the Central Control Committee.
    The Central Auditing Committee elected by the Third Congress of the Hungarian Working People's Party also held its first meeting on the same day. István Kristóf was elected Chairman of the Central Auditing Committee.

### 匈劳动人民党中央委员会举行全体会议  选出新的政治局、书记处及中央监察委员会

1954-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    匈劳动人民党中央委员会举行全体会议
    选出新的政治局、书记处及中央监察委员会
    【新华社一日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利劳动人民党第三次代表大会所选出的党中央委员会，五月三十日举行了全体会议。全体会议选出了新的政治局和书记处以及中央监察委员会。
    中央委员会政治局由委员九人和候补委员二人组成。当选为政治局委员的是：马加斯·拉科西、伊姆雷·纳吉、埃诺·格罗、米哈伊·法卡斯、拉约希·阿契、伊斯特万·希达希、安托尔·阿普罗、安德拉希·赫格居斯和伯拉·萨拉伊。当选为政治局候补委员的是：伊斯特万·巴塔和约瑟夫·麦基斯。
    中央委员会选出下列五人组成书记处：马加斯·拉科西、米哈伊·法卡斯、拉约希·阿契、伯拉·维格和雅诺希·马托尔奇。
    马加斯·拉科西当选为匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记。
    中央委员会选出了由十一个委员和两个候补委员组成的中央监察委员会，卡罗伊·基什当选为中央监察委员会主席。
    匈牙利劳动人民党第三次代表大会所选出的中央检查委员会也在同一天举行了第一次会议。伊斯特万·克里斯托夫当选为中央检查委员会主席。
