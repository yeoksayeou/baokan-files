Title: Mr. Humphrey to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 28th. Mr. Humphrey, the Chargé d'Affaires of the United Kingdom Government to the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing on the 28th. Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, welcomed him.

### 杜维廉先生到北京

1954-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    杜维廉先生到北京
    【新华社二十八日电】联合王国政府派驻中华人民共和国代办杜维廉先生已在廿八日到达北京。我外交部交际处副处长沈平前往迎接。
