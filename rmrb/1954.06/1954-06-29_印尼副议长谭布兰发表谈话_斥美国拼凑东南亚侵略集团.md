Title: Indonesian Deputy Speaker Tambunan Speaks Out, Condemning the United States for Assembling a Southeast Asian Aggression Bloc
Author:
Date: 1954-06-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 28th: According to TASS, Indonesian public opinion is fiercely opposed to the colonial powers' plans to establish military blocs. Indonesian Deputy Speaker of Parliament Tambunan delivered a speech condemning the United States' attempt to establish an aggressive bloc in Southeast Asia. Tambunan called for the People's Republic of China to be admitted to the United Nations. He emphasized that this would help consolidate peace.

### 印尼副议长谭布兰发表谈话  斥美国拼凑东南亚侵略集团

1954-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼副议长谭布兰发表谈话
    斥美国拼凑东南亚侵略集团
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社雅加达二十七日讯：印度尼西亚舆论激烈反对殖民国家建立军事集团的计划。印尼国会副议长谭布兰发表谈话，谴责美国想在东南亚建立侵略集团的企图。谭布兰要求让中华人民共和国进入联合国。他强调说，因为这样能有助于巩固和平。
