Title: Indian Newspapers Warmly Welcome Premier Zhou Enlai's Visit to India
Author:
Date: 1954-06-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 25th: Indian newspapers warmly welcome Premier and Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai's, visit to India at the invitation of the Indian government.

	The "New Age" weekly's editorial on the 24th pointed out that Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to India has historical significance. The editorial emphasized the role Premier Zhou Enlai's trip will play in strengthening the friendly relations between China and India and establishing close ties of mutual cooperation and friendship between Asian countries. The editorial stated that the principles upon which the Sino-Indian agreement on trade and communication between China's Tibet region and India is based, are a "vivid demonstration of the policy of loving peace."

	The "National Herald" editorial on the 23rd stated: The relations between China and India are based on the firm principles of mutual respect for territorial sovereignty, non-aggression, non-interference in internal affairs, equality and mutual benefit, and peaceful coexistence. The editorial said: "Friendly cooperation and amity between India and China can become a tremendous force for promoting peace in Asia. It will prove that peaceful coexistence between countries with different economic systems is not a dream." "Free Press" said: The meeting between the Prime Ministers of China and India "will certainly help to achieve Sino-Indian cooperation in jointly striving for peace and freedom in this part of the world."

### 印度各报热烈欢迎周恩来总理访问印度

1954-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    印度各报热烈欢迎周恩来总理访问印度
    【新华社新德里二十五日电】印度各报热烈欢迎中华人民共和国总理兼外交部部长周恩来应邀访问印度。
    “新世纪”周报二十四日的社论指出，周恩来总理到印度的访问具有历史性的重要意义。社论强调周恩来总理此行对于加强中印两国之间的友好关系和建立亚洲国家之间互相合作和友好的紧密关系将起的作用。社论说，中印两国关于中国西藏地方和印度之间的通商和交通协定所根据的原则，就是“爱好和平政策的生动证明”。
    “国民先驱报”二十三日的社论说：中印两国的关系是建立在互相尊重领土主权、互不侵犯、互不干涉内政、平等互惠、和平共处这些坚定的原则上的。社论说：“印度和中国之间的友好合作和亲善能够成为一支促进亚洲和平的巨大力量。它将证明：不同经济制度的国家能够和平共处并不是梦想。”“自由新闻”说：中印两国总理会晤“一定能够有助于达到中印合作，共同争取世界这一部分的和平和自由的目的”。
