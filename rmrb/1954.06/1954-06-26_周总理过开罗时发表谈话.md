Title: Premier Zhou's Speech in Cairo

Author: 
Date: 1954-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 25th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Cairo at 2:00 PM on the 24th while en route to visit India. The Indian Ambassador to Egypt, Ali Yavar Jung, and personnel from the Indian Embassy welcomed him at the airport.

Premier Zhou Enlai made the following statement to the Egyptian press: I am very pleased to have this opportunity to pass through Cairo on my way to visit India. The Chinese people have always sympathized with the Egyptian people's struggle for independence. I would like to take this opportunity to express my respect to the Egyptian people!

### 周总理过开罗时发表谈话

1954-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理过开罗时发表谈话
    【新华社新德里二十五日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来于访问印度途中在二十四日下午二时抵达开罗。印度驻埃及大使阿里·雅瓦尔·忠格与印度大使馆人员在机场迎接。
    周恩来总理向埃及报界发表谈话如下：我很高兴在我访问印度的旅途中，有此机会路过开罗。中国人民一向同情埃及人民的独立斗争。我愿趁此机会向埃及人民表示敬意！
