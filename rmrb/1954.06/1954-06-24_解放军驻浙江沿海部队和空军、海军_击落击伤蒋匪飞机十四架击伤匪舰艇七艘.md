Title: PLA Units Stationed in Coastal Zhejiang and Air Force, Navy Shoot Down and Damage 14 Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Aircraft, Damage 7 Bandit Vessels
Author:
Date: 1954-06-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd—The Chinese People's Liberation Army units stationed in coastal Zhejiang and the Navy and Air Force have severely struck the remnant Kuomintang naval and air forces in Taiwan that have been intruding into the seas and skies of eastern Zhejiang. From May 11th to June 5th, a total of 10 American-made Chiang Kai-shek bandit fighter planes were shot down and 4 were damaged. From April 20th to June 5th, 7 Chiang Kai-shek bandit ships and vessels were damaged, and a junk was captured.

### 解放军驻浙江沿海部队和空军、海军  击落击伤蒋匪飞机十四架击伤匪舰艇七艘

1954-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军驻浙江沿海部队和空军、海军
    击落击伤蒋匪飞机十四架击伤匪舰艇七艘
    【新华社二十三日讯】中国人民解放军驻浙江沿海部队和海、空军，沉重地打击了侵扰我浙东海上和天空的台湾国民党残余海、空军。自五月十一日到六月五日，共计击落美造蒋匪战斗机十架，击伤四架；自四月二十日到六月五日，击伤蒋匪舰、艇七艘，并俘机帆船一只。
