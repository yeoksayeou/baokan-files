Title: Swedish Ambassador Holds Reception to Celebrate Swedish National Day
Author:
Date: 1954-06-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Wistrand, held a reception in Beijing at 7:00 PM on the 6th to celebrate Swedish National Day.

The following were invited to attend the reception: Mr. Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government; Mr. Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Mr. Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Mr. Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture; Mr. Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and others.

Diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend the reception.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝瑞典国庆日  瑞典大使举行招待会

1954-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝瑞典国庆日
    瑞典大使举行招待会
   【新华社六日讯】瑞典驻我国大使魏斯特朗在六日下午七时在北京举行招待会庆祝瑞典国庆日。
    应邀出席招待会的有：中央人民政府政务院副总理董必武，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长李哲人，文化部副部长郑振铎，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等。
    各国驻我国外交使节和使馆人员也应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会在友好的空气中进行。
