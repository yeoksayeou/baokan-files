Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Scheduled to Meet French Premier on the 23rd in Switzerland
Author:
Date: 1954-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 22nd: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China and Minister of Foreign Affairs, will meet with French Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mendès France, in Bern on June 23rd. Premier Mendès France is visiting the Swiss Federal Authorities in Bern.

### 周恩来总理定二十三日在瑞士会见法总理

1954-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理定二十三日在瑞士会见法总理
    【新华社日内瓦二十二日电】中华人民共和国总理兼外交部部长周恩来将在六月二十三日在伯尔尼和法国总理兼外交部部长孟戴斯—弗朗斯会见。孟戴斯—弗朗斯总理是到伯尔尼访问瑞士联邦当局的。
