Title: Notice from the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League on the Promulgation of the Regulations of the Chinese Young Pioneers

Author:
Date: 1954-06-01
Page: 2

    Notice from the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League on the Promulgation of the Regulations of the Chinese Young Pioneers

    The "Draft Regulations of the Chinese Children's Corps (now renamed the Chinese Young Pioneers)" has been in effect for over four years since its promulgation in October 1949. In order to better carry out the work of the Young Pioneers, the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, in accordance with the provisions of Chapter VII, "Organization of the Chinese Young Pioneers," of the Constitution of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and based on the practical experience of the Young Pioneers' work over the past four years, has made some revisions and supplements to the draft regulations, and now officially promulgates the Regulations of the Chinese Young Pioneers.

### 中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会  公布中国少年先锋队队章的通知

1954-06-01
第2版()
专栏：

    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
    公布中国少年先锋队队章的通知
    “中国少年儿童队（现已改名为中国少年先锋队）章程草案”自一九四九年十月公布以来，已经四年多了。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会为了更好地开展少年先锋队的工作，根据中国新民主主义青年团团章第七章“中国少年先锋队组织”的规定，根据四年多来少年先锋队工作的实际经验，对队章草案作了一些修改和补充，现在将中国少年先锋队队章正式公布。
