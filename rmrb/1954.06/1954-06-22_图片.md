Title: Picture

Author: 
Date: 1954-06-22
Page: 2

The workers of the steelmaking department of the state-owned Tianjin Steel Plant overfulfilled the state plan and the production increase and economization plan for the first half of June on the day the draft constitution was announced. When the news of the promulgation of the draft constitution reached the workshop, the steelworkers cheered. They expressed their determination to work harder to complete the annual production plan.

### 图片

1954-06-22
第2版()
专栏：

    国营天津钢厂炼钢部的工人，在宪法草案公布的这一天超额完成了六月份上半月的国家计划和增产节约计划。当宪法草案公布的消息传到车间时，炼钢工人们都欢呼起来了，他们表示要进一步为完成全年生产计划而努力。
