Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts a Banquet for British Foreign Secretary Eden
Author:
Date: 1954-06-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 8th: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet for British Foreign Secretary Eden at 8 p.m. on the 8th. The British side was also attended by Marquess of Reading, Cassia, Allen, Dú Wéilián, Tǎ Háodīng and others. The Chinese side was accompanied by Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, Li Kenong, Wang Bingnan, Huang Hua, Huan Xiang, and Wang Zhuoru.

After the banquet, the guests watched the Chinese color art film "The Butterfly Lovers" together.

### 周恩来外长设宴招待英国外交大臣艾登

1954-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长设宴招待英国外交大臣艾登
    【新华社日内瓦八日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来八日下午八时设宴招待英国外交大臣艾登。英国方面出席的还有里丁侯爵，卡西亚，艾伦，杜维廉，塔豪丁等。中国方面出席作陪的有张闻天，王稼祥，李克农，王炳南，黄华，宦乡，王倬如。
    宴会后，宾主一同观看了中国彩色艺术影片“梁山伯与祝英台”。
