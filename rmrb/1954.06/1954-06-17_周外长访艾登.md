Title: Zhou Enlai Visits Eden
Author:
Date: 1954-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 16th. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited British Foreign Secretary Eden at 12:30 noon on the 16th for a 50-minute meeting. Deputy Minister Zhang Wentian also accompanied him.

### 周外长访艾登

1954-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    周外长访艾登
    【新华社日内瓦十六日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来十六日中午十二时半往访英国外交大臣艾登，会谈五十分钟。同往者尚有副部长张闻天。
