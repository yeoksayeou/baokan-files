Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Overseas Chinese in Delhi
Author:
Date: 1954-06-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 26th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, met with more than ninety Chinese nationals in Delhi at the Chinese Embassy in India at 7:00 PM on the 26th. Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian introduced Premier Zhou and shook hands with the overseas Chinese present one by one. The overseas Chinese were extremely excited.

### 周总理会见德里华侨

1954-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理会见德里华侨
    【新华社新德里二十六日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来二十六日下午七时，在中华人民共和国驻印度大使馆会见在德里的中国侨民九十多人。袁仲贤大使介绍周总理和到会华侨一一握手。华侨情绪极为兴奋。
