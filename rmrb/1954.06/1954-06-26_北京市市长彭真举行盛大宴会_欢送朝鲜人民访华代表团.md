Title: Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen Holds Grand Banquet to See Off the Korean People's Delegation to China

Author: 
Date: 1954-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen held a grand banquet at the Beijing Hotel on the evening of the 25th to bid farewell to the Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Kim Ung-gi, and the accompanying art troupe, who are about to leave Beijing for home.

Those attending the banquet included: Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Vice Chairman of the China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea; Dong Biwu, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Standing Committee members, and committee members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea in Beijing; and responsible persons and representatives of national democratic parties and people's organizations.

Also attending the banquet were: Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Deputy Mayors, committee members, secretaries-general, deputy secretaries-general, bureau directors, and section chiefs of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Vice Chairmen and committee members of the Beijing Municipal People's Representatives Conference Consultative Committee; responsible persons of various people's organizations; cultural figures and model workers from the capital.

Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

More than 600 people attended the banquet.

After the banquet began, Peng Zhen delivered a speech. He said: "The Korean People's Delegation to China has been visiting our country for more than three months. This visit has made great contributions and achieved great results in consolidating and strengthening the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Korean people and in exchanging experiences between the two countries. We would like to express our heartfelt thanks and respect to all comrades of the delegation headed by Comrade Kim Ung-gi. In the future, the Chinese people will continue to consolidate and strengthen the friendship and unity between our two peoples. We firmly believe that in the struggle to safeguard Asian and world peace, and in the cause of consolidating peace in Korea and striving for a peaceful settlement of the Korean issue, the solid friendship and unity between the Chinese and Korean people and the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union will guarantee our continued new victories."

After Peng Zhen's speech, Chen Shutong, on behalf of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, Zhou Yang, on behalf of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and other literary and art organizations, Nie Zhen, on behalf of the North China Sub-Committee of the China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, and Wu Han, on behalf of the Beijing Sub-Committee of the China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, presented gifts to the Korean People's Delegation to China. The entire venue applauded warmly during the presentation of gifts.

Next, Kim Ung-gi, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, delivered a reply. He said: During our visit of more than three months, we have witnessed the rapid development of the new People's China in various aspects such as politics, economy, and culture, and the enthusiasm of the fraternal Chinese people in their struggle for the great socialist construction under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong. At the same time, with infinite gratitude, we saw the fraternal Chinese people continue to deepen the movement to resist US aggression and aid Korea in order to assist our Korean people in rebuilding their homes. After returning to our country, we will convey in detail everything we saw during our visit to China to the Korean people, so as to encourage the Korean people who are restoring and building the post-war people's economy and striving for the peaceful reunification of the motherland under the leadership of the Korean Workers' Party and Marshal Kim Il-sung.

Dong Biwu, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, also spoke at the meeting, expressing warm respect on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to Kim Ung-gi, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, and all members of the delegation, who are about to leave Beijing for home. He said: The Korean People's Delegation to China has done a lot of work in China in just a few months, and successfully completed the important mission of visiting our country, further consolidating and developing the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Korean people. I express my infinite admiration for your hard work. We firmly believe that with the increasing unity of the Chinese and Korean people and the strong unity of the socialist camp of peace and democracy headed by the Soviet Union, the common cause of our two peoples in peaceful construction and consolidating peace in the Far East and the world will surely achieve greater and more brilliant victories.

The banquet proceeded throughout in a warm and friendly atmosphere. During the banquet, guests and hosts constantly raised their glasses to the unbreakable militant friendship between the Chinese and Korean people and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and Marshal Kim Il-sung.

During the banquet, artists from China and Korea performed wonderful programs, which were warmly welcomed.

### 北京市市长彭真举行盛大宴会  欢送朝鲜人民访华代表团

1954-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市市长彭真举行盛大宴会
    欢送朝鲜人民访华代表团
    【新华社二十五日讯】北京市人民政府市长彭真二十五日晚在北京饭店举行盛大宴会，欢送行将离京返国的以团长金应基为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团和随行艺术团全体人员。
    出席宴会的有：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席沈钧儒，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通，中国共产党中央委员会政治局委员董必武，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会和中国人民抗美援朝总会在京常务委员、委员，全国各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人和代表。
    出席宴会的还有：中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊、总政治部副主任萧华和北京市人民政府副市长、委员、秘书长、副秘书长、各局、处长，北京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席、委员，各人民团体的负责人，首都文化界人士和劳动模范等。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一和使馆人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    参加宴会的共有六百多人。
    宴会开始后，彭真首先致词。他说：“朝鲜人民访华代表团在我国进行了三个多月的访问，这次访问，在巩固和增强朝中两国人民的友谊和团结方面，在交流两国的经验方面，都作了很大的贡献，获得了很大的成就，我们谨向以金应基团长为首的代表团全体同志们表示衷心的感谢和敬意。今后中国人民将继续不断地巩固和加强我们两国人民之间的友谊和团结。我们深信，在维护亚洲和平及世界和平的斗争中，在巩固朝鲜和平与争取和平解决朝鲜问题的事业中，朝中两国人民和以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营间的巩固的友谊和团结，将保证我们不断获得新的胜利。”
    彭真致词后，陈叔通代表中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会和中国人民抗美援朝总会、周扬代表中国文学艺术界联合会等文艺团体、聂真代表华北抗美援朝总分会、吴晗代表北京市抗美援朝分会，向朝鲜人民访华代表团献礼。献礼时，全场热烈鼓掌。
    接着，朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基致答词。他说：我们在三个多月的访问中，看到了人民新中国在政治、经济和文化等各方面的迅速发展，和兄弟般的中国人民在中国共产党和中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东主席的领导下，为了伟大的社会主义建设而斗争的热情；同时我们怀着无限感激的心情，看到兄弟般的中国人民为了援助我们朝鲜人民的重建家园工作，继续深入展开抗美援朝运动的情形。我们回国以后，将要把在中国访问中所见的一切，详细地传达给朝鲜人民，以此来鼓舞在朝鲜劳动党和金日成元帅的领导下，恢复与建设战后人民经济和为祖国的和平统一而奋斗的朝鲜人民。
    中共中央政治局委员董必武也在会上讲了话，他代表中国共产党中央委员会向即将离京返国的朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基和全体团员，表示热烈的敬意。他说：朝鲜人民访华代表团这次在中国的短短几个月内，作了许多工作，胜利完成了访问我国的重大使命，进一步巩固和发展了中朝两国人民牢不可破的友谊。对于你们的劳绩我表示无限的钦佩，我们深信，在中朝两国人民日益团结和以苏联为首的和平民主社会主义阵营的坚强团结下，我们两国人民和平建设和巩固远东及世界和平的共同事业必将获得更大的辉煌胜利。
    宴会始终在热烈和友好的气氛中进行。席间，宾主不断举杯为中朝两国人民牢不可破的战斗友谊、为毛泽东主席和金日成元帅的健康而干杯。
    宴会上，并由中朝两国的艺术家们表演了精采节目，受到热烈欢迎。
