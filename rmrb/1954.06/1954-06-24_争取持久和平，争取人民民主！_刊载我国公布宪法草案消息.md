Title: "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!" Publishes News of the Promulgation of China's Draft Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-06-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 23rd: Bucharest Dispatch: The organ of the Information Bureau of the Communist and Workers' Parties, "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!" recently published, in its 25th issue of 1954, the news of the adoption of the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China by the Central People's Government Committee and the main contents of the Draft Constitution.

### “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”  刊载我国公布宪法草案消息

1954-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”
    刊载我国公布宪法草案消息
    【新华社布拉格二十三日电】布加勒斯特消息：最近出版的共产党和工人党情报局机关报“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”一九五四年第二十五期，刊载了我中央人民政府委员会通过中华人民共和国宪法草案的消息和宪法草案的主要内容。
