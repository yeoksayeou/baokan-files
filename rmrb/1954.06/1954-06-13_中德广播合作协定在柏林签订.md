Title: Sino-German Broadcasting Cooperation Agreement Signed in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1954-06-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 12th: To strengthen the ties and cooperation between China and Germany in broadcasting, the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau of our country and the State Broadcasting Commission of the German Democratic Republic signed the Sino-German Broadcasting Cooperation Agreement in Berlin on June 10.

The Chinese side signing the agreement was Wen Jize, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau of our country, and the German side was Kurt Heß, Chairman of the State Broadcasting Commission of the German Democratic Republic.

Those from the Chinese side who attended the signing ceremony included Ji Pengfei, our country's Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Counselor Wang Yutian, and all members of our country's delegation for the signing of the Sino-German Broadcasting Cooperation Agreement. From the German side were Wolfgang Clennert, Vice Chairman of the State Broadcasting Commission of the German Democratic Republic, Maria Lentmeister, Chief Specialist of the Ministry of Culture, Georg Hansen, Director of the News and Broadcasting Department of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Rosemes, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, and Karl Floch, Secretary-General of the German Association for Foreign Cultural Relations, and others.

The Chairman of the State Broadcasting Commission of the German Democratic Republic, Heß, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 10th to celebrate the signing of the agreement.

### 中德广播合作协定在柏林签订

1954-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中德广播合作协定在柏林签订
    【新华社柏林十二日电】为了加强中德两国在广播事业上的联系与合作，我国广播事业局与德意志民主共和国国家广播事业委员会六月十日在柏林签订了中德广播合作协定。
    在协定上签字的中国方面是：我国广播事业局副局长温济泽，德国方面是：德意志民主共和国国家广播事业委员会主席库尔特·赫斯。
    参加签字仪式的中国方面有：我国驻德意志民主共和国大使姬鹏飞、参赞王雨田和签订中德广播合作协定的我国代表团全体人员等。德国方面有：德意志民主共和国国家广播事业委员会副主席沃尔夫冈·克伦纳特、文化部主要专员玛丽亚·伦特迈斯特、德国统一社会党中央委员会新闻与广播处处长乔治·汉森、德意志民主共和国外交部亚洲司司长罗斯梅斯、德国对外文化联络协会秘书长卡尔·夫洛赫等。
    德意志民主共和国国家广播事业委员会主席赫斯在十日晚举行宴会，庆祝协定签字。
