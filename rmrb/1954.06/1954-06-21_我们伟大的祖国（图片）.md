Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-06-21
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The people of all ethnic groups in Xi'an City showed great jubilation at the publication of the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China." This is the Imam of the Huajue Lane Mosque in Xi'an reading aloud the news of the publication of the draft constitution to everyone immediately after receiving the newspaper during noon prayer.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    西安市各族人民对“中华人民共和国宪法草案”的公布，表现了巨大的欢腾。这是西安市化觉巷清真寺正在举行午拜的阿訇，在收到报纸以后立刻向大家朗读宪法草案公布的消息。
