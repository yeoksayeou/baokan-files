Prominent Dramatist Wang Yaoqing Passes Away

China's renowned dramatist and Principal of the Chinese Opera School of the Chinese Academy of Traditional Opera, Wang Yaoqing, passed away on the afternoon of June 3rd at 4:00 PM due to cerebroasthenia despite medical treatment. Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Zhou Yang and Liu Zhiming, as well as Mr. Wang Yaoqing's friends, students, and figures in the opera world, all came to pay their respects.

The literary and artistic circles and opera circles of the capital have formed the Wang Yaoqing Funeral Committee: Chairman Zhou Yang, members: Liu Zhiming, Tian Han, Ouyang Yuqian, Hong Shen, Ma Yanxiang, Lao She, Mei Lanfang, Cheng Yanqiu, Xiao Changhua, Zhang Geng, Ma Shaobo, Luo Heru, Shi Ruoxu, etc.

Mr. Wang Yaoqing's remains were placed in a coffin at noon on the 4th. A public memorial service is scheduled for the 8th, and the burial will take place on the 9th. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 著名戏剧家王瑶卿逝世

1954-06-06
第3版()
专栏：

    著名戏剧家王瑶卿逝世
    中国著名戏剧家、中国戏曲研究院戏曲学校校长王瑶卿因患脑神经病，医治无效，在六月三日下午四时逝世。中央人民政府文化部副部长周扬、刘芝明以及王瑶卿先生生前好友、学生和戏曲界人士等都曾往吊唁。
    首都文艺界、戏曲界已组成王瑶卿先生治丧委员会：主任委员周扬，委员：刘芝明、田汉、欧阳予倩、洪深、马彦祥、老舍、梅兰芳、程砚秋、萧长华、张庚、马少波、罗合如、史若虚等。
    王瑶卿先生的遗体已在四日中午入殓。准备在八日公祭，九日安葬。　（新华社）
