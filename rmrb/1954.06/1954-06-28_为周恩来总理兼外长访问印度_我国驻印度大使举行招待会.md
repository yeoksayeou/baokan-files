Title: Chinese Ambassador to India Holds Reception for Premier Zhou Enlai's Visit to India
Author:
Date: 1954-06-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 27th: Yuan Zhongxian, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, held a reception at the Chinese Embassy at 7:15 PM on the 26th in honor of Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, for his visit to India. Those attending the reception included: Indian Prime Minister Nehru, Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan, Minister of Home Affairs Kailash Nath Katju, Minister of Commerce D. P. Karmarkar, Deputy Minister of Planning, Irrigation and Power Lal Bahadur Shastri, Secretary of Education Humayun Kabir, Secretary of Commerce and Industry S. Ingal, Secretary-General of the Ministry of External Affairs N. R. Pillai, Foreign Affairs Secretary R. K. Nehru, Commonwealth Secretary S. Dutt, Chief of Protocol I. S. Chopra, Indian Army Chief of Staff Rajendrasinhji, Air Force Chief of Staff S. Mukerjee, Delhi State Chief Commissioner A. D. Pandit, Delhi State Chief Minister Chaudhry Brahm Prakash, and Member of the States Reorganisation Commission and former Indian Ambassador to China K. M. Panikkar. Also attending the reception were Indian Members of Parliament Pandit Thakur Das Bhargava, Syed Ahmed, Chaturvedi, Colonel B. H. Zaidi, Radha Raman, and P. Sundarayya.

Also attending the reception were: Gian Chand, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the India-China Friendship Association, B. Singh Puri, Secretary-General of the Indian Red Cross Society, Harish Chanda, Chairman of the All India Students Federation, P. Ramamurti, Editor-in-Chief of "New Age" Weekly, Mankekar, Editor of the Delhi Edition of "The Times of India," and Shankar, Editor of "Shankar's Weekly."

Also attending the reception were diplomatic envoys to India from various countries: Australian High Commissioner Crocker, Dutch Ambassador Van Briant, Indonesian Ambassador Palar, Ceylon High Commissioner K. Kumaraswamy, British Deputy High Commissioner Middleton, Finnish Minister Warvanne, Danish Minister Toyberg-Frandsen, Swedish Minister Wickman, Norwegian Minister Lykke, Swiss Minister Grässli, Hungarian Minister Kos, Burmese Chargé d'Affaires U Maung Maung Soe, Czechoslovak Chargé d'Affaires Antos, Pakistani Chargé d'Affaires Isaar, Soviet Chargé d'Affaires Ashurov, and Nepalese Chargé d'Affaires Singh.

Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian introduced the guests attending the reception to Premier Zhou Enlai, who shook hands with them.

The reception proceeded in a friendly and cordial atmosphere and concluded at 8:15 PM.

### 为周恩来总理兼外长访问印度  我国驻印度大使举行招待会

1954-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    为周恩来总理兼外长访问印度
    我国驻印度大使举行招待会
    【新华社新德里二十七日电】中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤为中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来访问印度，特于二十六日下午七时十五分在中国大使馆举行招待会。出席招待会的有：印度总理尼赫鲁，印度副总统拉德哈克里希南，内政部部长凯·纳·卡特朱，商业部部长达·帕·卡马卡尔，计划、水利工程与电力部副部长拉尔·哈伊蒂，教育部秘书胡·卡比尔，工商部秘书英加尔，外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱，外事秘书拉·库·尼赫鲁，英联邦秘书斯·杜特，交际司司长伊·斯·乔普拉，印度陆军总司令拉金德拉辛吉，空军总司令斯·慕克吉，德里邦行政专员阿·德·潘迪特，德里邦首席部长乔·布·普拉卡希，省区划分委员会委员、前印度驻中国大使潘尼迦。出席招待会的还有：印度国会议员潘·查·纳·马利雅，赛义德·艾哈迈德，查卢尔维迪，布·赫·查伊迪上校，拉达·拉曼，普·孙达拉雅。
    出席招待会的还有：印中友好协会执行委员会主席吉安·昌德，印度红十字会秘书长巴·辛·普里，全印学生联合会主席哈里希·钱达，“新世纪”周报总编辑普·拉马穆蒂，“印度时报”德里版编辑曼克卡尔，“商羯罗周刊”编辑山卡尔。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻印度外交使节：澳大利亚高级专员克罗克，荷兰大使范·柏兰特，印度尼西亚大使巴拉，锡兰高级专员克·科马拉斯瓦米，英国副高级专员密德尔顿，芬兰公使瓦尔万尼，丹麦公使托伊伯格—弗朗森，瑞典公使威克曼，挪威公使李克，瑞士公使格拉斯里，匈牙利公使科斯，缅甸代办吴貌貌梭，捷克斯洛伐克代办安托希，巴基斯坦代办艾瑟尔，苏联代办阿舒罗夫，尼泊尔代办辛加。
    袁仲贤大使把参加招待会的来宾介绍与周恩来总理握手。
    招待会在友好亲切的气氛中进行到八时十五分结束。
