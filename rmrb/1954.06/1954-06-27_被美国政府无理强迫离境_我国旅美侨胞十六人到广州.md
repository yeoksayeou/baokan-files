Title: Sixteen Overseas Chinese from the United States, Unreasonably Forced to Leave by the U.S. Government, Arrive in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1954-06-27
Page: 4
　
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 26th: Sixteen Overseas Chinese who were forced to leave the United States by the U.S. government arrived in Guangzhou on the 21st. Among these sixteen people, nine were unreasonably arrested and deported by the U.S. government, and seven were unreasonably forced to leave. After these Overseas Chinese arrived in Guangzhou, the Overseas Chinese Affairs Committee of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government has provided proper resettlement and care.

### 被美国政府无理强迫离境  我国旅美侨胞十六人到广州

1954-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    被美国政府无理强迫离境
    我国旅美侨胞十六人到广州
    【新华社广州二十六日电】为美国政府强迫出境的我国旅美侨胞十六人，在二十一日抵达广州。这十六个人中有九人是被美国政府无理逮捕并驱逐出境的，有七人是被无理强迫离境的。这些侨胞到达广州后，广东省人民政府华侨事务委员会已给予妥善的安置和照顾。
