Title: Guatemalan Foreign Minister Requests Immediate Special Meeting of UN Security Council to Discuss Guatemala's Armed Aggression

Author:

Date: 1954-06-22
Page: 4

    Guatemalan Foreign Minister Requests Immediate Special Meeting of UN Security Council
    To Discuss Guatemala's Armed Aggression
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 21st] Guatemalan Foreign Minister Toriello telegraphed the United Nations Security Council on the 19th, requesting an immediate special meeting to discuss the armed aggression Guatemala is suffering. The telegram accused "the governments of Honduras and Nicaragua of carrying out open aggression at the instigation of certain foreign monopolies," and requested, in accordance with the United Nations Charter, that a special meeting of the Security Council be convened in order to "prevent the destruction of peace in Central America."

### 危地马拉外长要求联合国安理会立即召开特别会议  讨论危地马拉遭到武装侵略的问题

1954-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    危地马拉外长要求联合国安理会立即召开特别会议
    讨论危地马拉遭到武装侵略的问题
    【新华社二十一日讯】危地马拉外交部长托里埃略十九日致电联合国安全理事会，要求立即召开特别会议讨论危地马拉所遭到的武装侵略问题。电报中斥责“洪都拉斯和尼加拉瓜政府在某些外国垄断企业教唆下进行了公开的侵略”，并要求根据联合国宪章，召开安全理事会特别会议，以便“阻止破坏中美洲的和平”。
