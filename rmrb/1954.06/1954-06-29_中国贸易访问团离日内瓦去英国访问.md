Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Leaves Geneva for Visit to Britain
Author:
Date: 1954-06-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 28th: At the invitation of five industrial and commercial organizations including the Federation of British Industries, the head of the Chinese Trade Delegation, Cao Zhongshu, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, the deputy head, Shi Zhiang, and trade experts, a group of eleven people, left Geneva by plane on the afternoon of the 28th to visit Britain.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Lei Renmin and Huan Xiang, advisors to the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, and Wen Pengjiu, Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Geneva.

During its visit to Britain, the Chinese Trade Delegation will visit British factories, have extensive contact with British business circles, and conduct discussions on expanding trade between the two countries. The United Kingdom Government's Chargé d'Affaires to our country

### 中国贸易访问团离日内瓦去英国访问

1954-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

     中国贸易访问团离日内瓦去英国访问
   【新华社日内瓦二十八日电】应英国工业联合会等五个工商团体的邀请，中国贸易访问团团长、中国进出口公司副经理曹中枢，副团长石志昂和贸易专家等一行十一人，在二十八日下午乘飞机离日内瓦前往英国进行访问。
    到机场欢送的有：中华人民共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团顾问雷任民、宦乡，中华人民共和国驻日内瓦总领事温朋久等。
    中国贸易访问团访英期间，将参观英国的工厂，和英国工商界进行广泛的接触，并进行扩大两国间贸易的商谈。联合王国政府驻我国代办
