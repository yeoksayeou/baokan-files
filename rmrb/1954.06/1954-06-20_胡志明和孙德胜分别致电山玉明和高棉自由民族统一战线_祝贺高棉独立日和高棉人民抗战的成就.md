Title: Ho Chi Minh and Ton Duc Thang Send Telegrams to Son Ngoc Minh and the Khmer Free National United Front to Congratulate Them on Khmer Independence Day and the Achievements of the Khmer People's Resistance

Author:
Date: 1954-06-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 17th: Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, sent a telegram to Son Ngoc Minh, Chairman of the Khmer Resistance Government, warmly congratulating him on the Independence Day of Free Khmer on June 19th.

The telegram said: Under the leadership of Chairman Son Ngoc Minh, the Khmer people have already achieved many glorious successes in the resistance war. The victory of the Khmer people is also a common victory for the Vietnamese people and the Vietnam-Khmer-Laos People's Alliance in their united struggle for the freedom and independence of the three fraternal nations.

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 19th: Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Vietnam National United Front, sent a telegram to the Khmer Free National United Front, congratulating them on the Independence Day of Free Khmer.

The telegram said: Under the leadership of the Khmer Resistance Government and Chairman Son Ngoc Minh, the heroic Khmer people, united in the Khmer Free National United Front, alongside the fraternal Vietnamese and Laotian people, have been conducting a patriotic struggle against the French colonialists, American interventionists, and traitorous puppet groups. This struggle has been growing stronger and stronger and has gained the support of the French people and peace-loving people around the world.

The telegram said: Under the guidance of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Chairman Ho Chi Minh, the Vietnamese people are closely united with the Khmer and Laotian peoples, regarding the victories of the people of fraternal countries as their own. The Vietnamese people will further consolidate the Vietnam-Khmer-Laos People's Alliance; jointly promote the struggle against the schemes of the French colonialist warmongers and American interventionists to prolong and expand the Indochina war with the Khmer and Laotian people; and support the proposals put forward by the Vietnamese, Soviet, and Chinese delegations at the Geneva Conference on restoring peace in Vietnam, Khmer, and Laos on the basis of respecting sovereignty and independence.

The telegram finally wished the Khmer people greater victories in the struggles ahead.

### 胡志明和孙德胜分别致电山玉明和高棉自由民族统一战线  祝贺高棉独立日和高棉人民抗战的成就

1954-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明和孙德胜分别致电山玉明和高棉自由民族统一战线
    祝贺高棉独立日和高棉人民抗战的成就
     【新华社十八日讯】据越南通讯社十七日讯：越南民主共和国政府主席胡志明打电报给高棉抗战政府主席山玉明，热烈祝贺六月十九日自由高棉独立纪念日。
    电文说：高棉人民在山玉明主席的领导下，已经在抗战中获得了许多光辉的成就。高棉人民的胜利也是越南人民和越南—高棉—寮国人民联盟为争取三个兄弟民族自由、独立而团结战斗的共同胜利。
    【新华社十九日讯】据越南通讯社十九日讯：越南国民联合战线主席孙德胜打电报给高棉自由民族统一战线，祝贺自由高棉独立日。
    电文说：在高棉抗战政府和山玉明主席的领导下，英雄的高棉人民团结在高棉自由民族统一战线中，与兄弟般的越南和寮国人民并肩进行了反抗法国殖民者、美国干涉者和卖国傀儡集团的爱国斗争。这一斗争已经日益发展壮大，并且获得了法国人民和全世界爱好和平的人们的支持。
    电文说：越南人民在越南劳动党和胡志明主席的教导下，与高棉和寮国人民紧密团结，把兄弟国家人民的胜利看成自己的胜利。越南人民将更加巩固越南、高棉、寮国人民联盟；与高棉和寮国人民共同推进反对法国殖民主义好战分子、美国干涉者拖延和扩大印度支那战争的阴谋的斗争；支持越南、苏联和中国代表团在日内瓦会议上提出的关于在尊重主权和独立的基础上恢复越南、高棉和寮国境内的和平的建议。
    电文最后祝高棉人民在今后的斗争中获得更大的胜利。
