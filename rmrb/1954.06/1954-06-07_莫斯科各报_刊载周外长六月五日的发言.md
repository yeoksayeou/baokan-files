Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Speech of June 5th
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 6th. Moscow newspapers on June 6th all published the full text of the speech by Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, at the Geneva Conference on June 5th. On the same day, the newspapers also published the speeches of Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov and North Korean Foreign Minister Nam Il.

### 莫斯科各报  刊载周外长六月五日的发言

1954-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报
    刊载周外长六月五日的发言
   【新华社莫斯科六日电】莫斯科各报六日都刊载了我国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来五日在日内瓦会议上的发言全文。同日各报并刊载了苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫及朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日的发言。
