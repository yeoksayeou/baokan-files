    Our Great Motherland
    The loading and unloading capacity of Tanggu New Port is constantly improving. The daily loading and unloading capacity has increased from more than 700 tons to more than 2,000 tons. The stevedores are launching a "fast loading and unloading" campaign, using a "railway steam crane" with a lifting capacity of 20 tons to unload tractors from ships.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    塘沽新港的装卸能力正在不断地提高，每昼夜的装御能力已由七百多吨提高到两千吨以上了。装卸工人正展开“快装快卸”运动，用起重二十吨的“铁道汽力起重机”从船上卸下拖拉机。
