Title: Zhou Enlai Meets with Ronning
Author:
Date: 1954-06-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 19th: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, received Mr. Ronning, Acting Head of the Canadian Delegation to the Geneva Conference and Canadian Minister to Norway, at 12:30 noon on the 19th. Director Ke Bonian was also present.

### 周外长接见朗宁

1954-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    周外长接见朗宁
    【新华社日内瓦十九日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来十九日中午十二时半，接见加拿大出席日内瓦会议代表团代理团长加拿大驻挪威公使朗宁。在座的还有柯柏年司长。
