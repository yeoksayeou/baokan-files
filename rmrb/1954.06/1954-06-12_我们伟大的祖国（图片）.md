Our Great Motherland
    The entire bridge pier foundation project of the Hanshui Railway Bridge has been successfully completed, ensuring that the entire railway bridge will be completed on schedule within this year and providing favorable conditions for the timely commencement of the Yangtze River Bridge next year. This is the bustling construction site of the Hanshui Railway Bridge.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    汉水铁桥全部桥墩基础工程已胜利完成，保证了整个铁桥在今年年内按期完工，并为明年长江大桥按时开工提供了有利的条件。这是繁忙的汉水铁桥工地。
