Title: Trade Negotiations Between China and Indonesia Begin in Jakarta; Head and Deputy Head of Chinese Trade Delegation Visit Indonesian Prime Minister
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 29th: Trade negotiations between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia formally began in Jakarta on the 28th. The opening ceremony was held at the Indonesian National Bank at 10:00 am on the 28th.

    The head of the Indonesian delegation, Sumano, in his opening speech, welcomed the Chinese trade delegation and expressed his hope that the negotiations would achieve satisfactory results beneficial to both countries. He said that Indonesia pursues a good-neighborly policy and hopes to conduct friendly cooperation with the People's Republic of China. The arrival of the Chinese trade delegation shows that the People's Republic of China has the same desire. Trade between China and Indonesia has a long history, and normal trade is a practical way to promote relations between the two countries.

    The head of the Chinese trade delegation, Kong Yuan, mentioned in his reply that the Chinese trade delegation will, based on the principles of equality and mutual benefit and the spirit of friendly consultation, and based on the terms of the Sino-Indonesian trade agreement signed last year, discuss with the Indonesian government the main issues related to the development of trade between China and Indonesia. He expressed his belief that due to the common aspirations and efforts of both parties, the negotiations will yield satisfactory results that promote the economic prosperity of both countries.

    The ceremony was conducted in a warm atmosphere.

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 29th: At the invitation of Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, the head of the Chinese trade delegation, Kong Yuan, and the deputy head, Yang Lin, visited Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo at noon on the 28th.

### 我国和印尼贸易商谈在雅加达开始举行  我国贸易代表团团长副团长访问印尼总理

1954-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和印尼贸易商谈在雅加达开始举行
    我国贸易代表团团长副团长访问印尼总理
    【新华社雅加达二十九日电】中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国的贸易商谈于二十八日在雅加达正式开始。开幕仪式在二十八日晨十时在印尼国家银行举行。
    参加商谈的印尼代表团团长苏马诺在致开幕词时，表示欢迎中国贸易代表团，并希望这次商谈达到对两国都有利的圆满结果。他说，印尼奉行睦邻政策，它希望和中华人民共和国进行友好的合作。中国贸易代表团的来临说明中华人民共和国有着同样的愿望。中国和印尼的贸易有着悠久的历史，而正常的贸易是促进两国关系的切实途径。
    中国贸易代表团团长孔原在致答词时提到：中国贸易代表团将根据平等互利的原则和友好协商的精神，以去年签订的中印（尼）贸易协定的条款为基础，和印尼政府商谈有关发展中印（尼）两国贸易的主要问题。他表示，相信由于双方的共同愿望和努力，谈判将得出促进两国经济繁荣的圆满的结果。
    仪式在热烈的空气中进行。
    【新华社雅加达二十九日电】我国贸易代表团团长孔原、副团长杨琳应印尼共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约邀请，在二十八日中午访问了阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理。
