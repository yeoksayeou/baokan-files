Title: Delegation for Technical and Technological Science Cooperation from the German Democratic Republic Returns Home; Vice Premier Chen Yun Receives Entire German Delegation

Author:

Date: 1954-06-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: Following the signing of the protocol for the first session of the Sino-German Permanent Committee for Technical and Technological Science Cooperation, Vice Premier Chen Yun of the Central People's Government Administration Council received Richard Goschütz, head of the German delegation to the Sino-German Permanent Committee for Technical and Technological Science Cooperation, State Secretary and First Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the German Democratic Republic, and all members of the delegation on the 24th. Present at the reception were Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Wang Daohan, head of the Chinese group of the Sino-German Permanent Committee for Technical and Technological Science Cooperation and Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the Central People's Government; Sun Liyu, Director of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government; Guan Hantao, member of the Chinese group of the Sino-German Permanent Committee for Technical and Technological Science Cooperation and Deputy Director of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the State Planning Commission; Chang Yanqing, acting member of the Chinese group and Deputy Director of the Second Foreign Trade Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and others. Also present at the reception was Johannes König, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China.

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: The German delegation, which came to Beijing to attend the first session of the Sino-German Permanent Committee for Technical and Technological Science Cooperation, left Beijing by plane on the 25th to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Wang Daohan, head of the Chinese group of the Sino-German Permanent Committee for Technical and Technological Science Cooperation and Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the Central People's Government; Sun Liyu, Director of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government; Guan Hantao, member of the Chinese group of the Sino-German Permanent Committee for Technical and Technological Science Cooperation and Deputy Director of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the State Planning Commission; Chang Yanqing, acting member of the Chinese group and Deputy Director of the Second Foreign Trade Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, as well as secretaries and advisory experts from the Chinese group.

Also at the airport to see them off were: Johannes König, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Mr. Dürber, Commercial Counselor of the Embassy, Mr. Baum, Commercial Commissioner, and Mr. Kulitzka, Political and Economic Secretary.

The first session of the Sino-German Permanent Committee for Technical and Technological Science Cooperation, under the joint chairmanship of Wang Daohan, head of the Chinese group and Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the Central People's Government, and Richard Goschütz, head of the German group, State Secretary and First Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the German Democratic Republic, proceeded throughout in an atmosphere of friendly cooperation, and the protocol was signed on the 23rd. During the adjournment of the meeting, the German delegation also visited and toured many factories, schools, state-owned farms, rural areas, and places of historical interest in Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and the Northeast, gaining an understanding of the development of our country's economic and cultural construction, which will play a significant role in further developing Sino-German technical and technological science cooperation in the future.

### 民主德国技术与技术科学合作代表团返国  陈云副总理曾接见德国代表团全体人员

1954-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国技术与技术科学合作代表团返国
    陈云副总理曾接见德国代表团全体人员
   【新华社二十六日讯】中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会第一届会议议定书签订后，中央人民政府政务院副总理陈云在二十四日接见了中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会德国代表团团长、德意志民主共和国重工业部国务秘书、第一副部长理夏德·戈舒埃茨和代表团全体团员。接见时在座的有中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会中国组组长、中央人民政府第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，中央人民政府国家计划委员会技术合作局局长孙力余，中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会中国组委员、国家计划委员会技术合作局副局长管寒涛，中国组代理委员、对外贸易部国外贸易第二局副局长常彦卿等人。接见时在座的还有德意志民主共和国驻华大使柯尼希。
    【新华社二十五日讯】前来北京出席中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会第一届会议的德国代表团已在二十五日乘飞机离开北京返国。
    前往机场欢送的有：中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会中国组组长、中央人民政府第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，中央人民政府国家计划委员会技术合作局局长孙力余，中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会中国组委员、中央人民政府国家计划委员会技术合作局副局长管寒涛，中国组代理委员、中央人民政府对外贸易部国外贸易第二局副局长常彦卿及中国组秘书和顾问专家多人。
    前往机场欢送的还有：德意志民主共和国驻华大使柯尼希、大使馆商务参赞杜尔伯、商务专员鲍牧、政治经济秘书库利兹卡。
    这次中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会第一届会议在中国组组长、中央人民政府第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵和德国组组长、德意志民主共和国重工业部国务秘书、第一副部长理夏德·戈舒埃茨共同主持下，始终在友好合作空气中进行，并在二十三日签订了议定书。德国代表团并在会议休会期间，访问和参观了北京、上海、杭州和东北地区的很多工厂、学校、国营农场、农村及名胜古迹，了解我国经济、文化建设发展情况，这对今后进一步开展中德技术与技术科学合作将起很大作用。
