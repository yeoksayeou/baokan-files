    The 30th meeting of the Central People's Government Committee was held on June 14, 1954, and passed the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China." The picture shows Chairman Mao Zedong announcing the opening of the meeting. On the rostrum, from left to right: Lin Boqu, Soong Ching Ling, Zhu De, Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan. Xinhua News Agency reporters Qi Guanshan and Qian Sijie took the picture.
