Title: Sino-Japanese Friendship Association to Hold Fourth National Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-06-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 24th: The Sino-Japanese Friendship Association is scheduled to hold its Fourth National Congress in Osaka starting July 9th. Prior to the congress, under the auspices of the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association, people from all walks of life in Japan have launched a "Sino-Japanese Friendship Qixi Movement," using various activities to strengthen the friendship between the people of China and Japan to welcome the Qixi Festival (the evening of the seventh day of the seventh lunar month), which was transmitted to Japan from China in ancient times.

### 日中友好协会  将举行第四届全国大会

1954-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友好协会
    将举行第四届全国大会
    【新华社二十四日讯】东京消息：日中友好协会预定从七月九日起在大阪举行第四届全国大会。在大会举行以前，在日中友好协会的主持下，日本各阶层人士已经展开一个“日中友好七夕运动”，用各种加强中日两国人民友谊的活动来迎接古代就从中国传到日本去的七夕（农历七月七日晚）。
