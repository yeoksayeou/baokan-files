Title: Lao Warring Parties' Representatives Meet in Geneva
Author:
Date: 1954-06-29
Page: 4

    Lao Warring Parties' Representatives Meet in Geneva
    [Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 27] This reporter has learned that, according to the agreement reached at the Geneva Conference on Indochina on June 19, representatives of the Lao People's Liberation Army and the Vietnamese People's Army and representatives of the French-Laotian forces met in Geneva on June 24 and 25. According to sources here, the two sides have reached an agreement on procedural matters for the meeting and are now discussing the agenda. The atmosphere of the meeting is good.

### 老挝交战双方代表已在日内瓦会晤

1954-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝交战双方代表已在日内瓦会晤
    【新华社日内瓦二十七日电】本社记者获悉，根据六月十九日讨论印度支那问题的日内瓦会议上所达成的协议，寮国人民解放军及越南人民军代表和法国—老挝军队代表曾于六月二十四日、二十五日在日内瓦会晤。据此间消息称，双方已在会晤的程序问题上达成协议，现在正在讨论会晤的议事日程，会晤的气氛是良好的。
