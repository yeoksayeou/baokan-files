Title: Government Administration Council Holds 218th Meeting; Approves Sino-Indian Agreement on Trade and Transportation between Tibet and India
Author:
Date: 1954-06-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd—The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held its 218th meeting on the afternoon of the 3rd. The meeting heard a report by Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, on the negotiation of the Sino-Indian Agreement on Trade and Transportation between the Tibet region of China and India, and approved the "Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of India on Trade and Transportation between the Tibet Region of China and India." After hearing explanations by Wang Ziyi, Deputy Minister of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, the meeting approved the main points of the Ministry of Internal Affairs' work plan for 1954. In addition, the meeting approved various appointment and removal proposals and submitted appointment and removal proposals for approval by the Central People's Government Committee.

### 政务院举行第二一八次政务会议  批准中印关于西藏地方和印度通商交通协定

1954-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二一八次政务会议
    批准中印关于西藏地方和印度通商交通协定
    【新华社三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百一十八次政务会议在三日下午举行，会议听了中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫所做的订立中印两国关于中国西藏地方和印度之间的通商和交通协定经过的报告，批准了“中华人民共和国、印度共和国关于中国西藏地方和印度之间的通商和交通协定”。会议在听了中央人民政府内务部副部长王子宜所做的说明后批准了内务部一九五四年工作计划要点。此外，会议通过了各项任免案和提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
