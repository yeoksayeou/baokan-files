Title: Norwegian, Swedish, and Finnish Ministers to Switzerland successively call on Foreign Minister Zhou

Author:
Date: 1954-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 17th. The Norwegian Minister to Switzerland, Peter Martin Anker, visited Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, at 2 p.m. on the 17th for a courtesy call.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 17th. The former Swedish Ambassador to China, now Minister to Switzerland, Arvidsson, and the former Finnish Minister to China, now Minister to Switzerland, Knorring, visited Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, at 8 p.m. on the 17th for a courtesy call. Foreign Minister Zhou had dinner with Arvidsson and Knorring.

### 挪威、瑞典、芬兰驻瑞士公使  先后拜会周外长

1954-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威、瑞典、芬兰驻瑞士公使
    先后拜会周外长
    【新华社日内瓦十七日电】挪威驻瑞士公使比得·马丁·安克尔十七日下午二时访问中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来，作礼节性拜会。
    【新华社日内瓦十七日电】瑞典前驻中国大使现任驻瑞士公使阿马斯顿和芬兰前驻中国公使现任驻瑞士公使克诺林，十七日下午八时访问中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来，作礼节性的拜会。周外长与阿马斯顿和克诺林共进晚餐。
