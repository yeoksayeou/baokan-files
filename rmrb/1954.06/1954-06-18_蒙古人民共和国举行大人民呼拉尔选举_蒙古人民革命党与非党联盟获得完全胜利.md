Title: The Mongolian People's Republic Holds Great People's Hural Elections; Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Non-Party Alliance Achieve Complete Victory
Author:
Date: 1954-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar: According to TASS, the elections for the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic were held on June 13th. According to information from the electoral committees of various constituencies, 394,773 voters participated in the election, accounting for 99.95 percent of all voters. According to preliminary statistics, the voters who voted for candidates representing the electoral alliance formed by the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and non-party figures accounted for 99.89 percent of the total number of voters who participated in the voting. Therefore, the electoral alliance formed by the Party and non-party figures achieved a complete victory in the election.
    
The final election results will be announced by the Mongolian Central Electoral Committee on June 18th.

### 蒙古人民共和国举行大人民呼拉尔选举  蒙古人民革命党与非党联盟获得完全胜利

1954-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国举行大人民呼拉尔选举
    蒙古人民革命党与非党联盟获得完全胜利
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔的选举已在六月十三日举行。据各选区选举委员会的消息，参加选举的选民有三十九万四千七百七十三人，占全部选民的百分之九十九点九五。据初步的统计，投票选举蒙古人民革命党与非党人士结成的选举联盟的候选人为代表的选民，占参加投票的选民总数的百分之九十九点八九。因此，党与非党人士结成的选举联盟在选举中获得完全胜利。
    最后的选举结果将由蒙古中央选举委员会在六月十八日宣布。
