Title: Indian President Receives Premier Zhou Enlai; Premier Zhou Attends Indian President's Luncheon and Vice President's Tea Party
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-27
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 26th: Indian President Dr. Prasad held a reception at 7 p.m. on June 25th to receive Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China.
    The reception was held in the gardens of the Presidential Palace.
    Those attending the reception included: Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan, Prime Minister Nehru, Minister of Transport Ram, Minister of Planning, Irrigation and Power Nanda, Minister of Home Affairs Katju, Minister of Railways and Transport Shastri, Minister of Works, Housing and Supply S.S. Singh, Minister of Defense Tyagi, Minister of Agriculture Deshmukh, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Chanda, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Pillai, Foreign Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs R.K. Nehru, Commonwealth Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Dutt, and other senior officials from various ministries of the Indian Central Government.
    Also attending the reception were: Indian Army Chief of Staff Rajendrasinhji, Indian Navy Chief of Staff Pizey, Indian Air Force Chief of Staff Mukherjee, and other senior military officers.
    Approximately sixty members of the Indian Parliament also attended the reception, including: Radha Raman, Pan. Cha. Na. Malaviya, P. Sundarayya, Vi. Pu. Nayar, Colonel Bu. He. Chaidi, Bu. Ke. Sharma, K. Nair, etc.
    Social figures also attended the reception, including: Dr. Chand, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the India-China Friendship Association, renowned economist Dr. Ganguli, Vice-Chancellor of Aligarh University Dr. Zakir Husain, etc.
    The diplomatic missions in Delhi also attended the reception. Those attending included: French Ambassador Ostrorog, Australian High Commissioner Crocker, Canadian High Commissioner Reader, Dutch Ambassador van Bylandt, Indonesian Ambassador Palar, Afghan Ambassador Soudard Abdul Hussain Aziz, British Deputy High Commissioner Middleton, Finnish Minister Warvanne, Danish Minister Toyberg-Frandsen, Swedish Minister Wickman, Norwegian Minister Lykke, Abyssinian Minister Keflerzi, Swiss Minister Grassli, Hungarian Minister Kos, Burmese Chargé d'Affaires U. Maung Maung Soe, Czechoslovakian Chargé d'Affaires Antos, Pakistani Chargé d'Affaires Azhar, Soviet Chargé d'Affaires Ashurov, Nepalese Chargé d'Affaires Singh.
    The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere and concluded at 8:15 p.m.
      Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 26th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, attended a luncheon given by Indian President Prasad on the 26th at noon.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 26th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, attended a tea party given by Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan on the 26th at 5 p.m.

### 印度总统招待周恩来总理  周总理并参加印度总统午宴和副总统茶会

1954-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    印度总统招待周恩来总理
    周总理并参加印度总统午宴和副总统茶会
     【新华社新德里二十六日电】印度共和国总统普拉沙德博士在六月二十五日晚七时举行招待会，招待中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来。
    招待会在总统府的花园中举行。
    出席招待会的有：印度共和国副总统拉德哈克里希南，总理尼赫鲁，交通部部长拉姆，计划、水利工程与电力部部长南达，内政部部长卡特朱，铁道与运输部部长夏斯特里，工程、住房与供应部部长斯萨·辛格，国防部部长特雅吉，农业部部长德希穆克，外交部副部长钱达，外交部秘书长皮莱，外交部外事秘书拉·库·尼赫鲁，外交部英联邦秘书杜特，以及印度中央政府各部副部长等高级官员。
    出席招待会的还有：印度陆军总司令拉金德拉辛吉、印度海军总司令皮泽、印度空军总司令慕克吉以及其他高级军官。
    出席招待会的还有约六十名印度国会议员，其中有：拉达·拉曼，潘·查·纳·马拉维亚，普·孙达拉雅，维·普·纳雅尔，布·赫·查伊迪上校，布·克·夏尔马，克·奈尔等。
    出席招待会的还有社会名流，其中有：印中友好协会执行委员会主席昌德博士，著名经济学家甘古利博士，阿里加大学副校长宰克尔·侯赛尼博士等。
    出席招待会的还有驻德里的各国外交使团。出席招待会的有：法国大使奥斯特罗洛格，澳大利亚高级专员克罗克，加拿大高级专员李德，荷兰大使范·柏兰特，印度尼西亚大使巴拉，阿富汗大使苏达尔·阿卜杜勒·侯赛尼·阿齐兹，英国副高级专员密德尔顿，芬兰公使瓦尔万尼，丹麦公使托伊伯格—弗朗森，瑞典公使威克曼，挪威公使李克，阿比西尼亚公使克弗勒齐，瑞士公使格拉斯里，匈牙利公使科斯，缅甸代办吴貌貌梭，捷克斯洛伐克代办安托希，巴基斯坦代办艾瑟尔，苏联代办阿舒罗夫，尼泊尔代办辛加。
    招待会在热烈与友好的空气中进行，在晚上八时十五分结束。
      【新华社新德里二十六日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来二十六日中午应邀参加了印度共和国总统普拉沙德的午宴。
    【新华社新德里二十六日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在二十六日下午五时应邀参加了印度副总统拉德哈克里希南的茶会。
