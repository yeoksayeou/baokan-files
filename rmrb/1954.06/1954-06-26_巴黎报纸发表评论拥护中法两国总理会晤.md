Title: Paris Newspapers Publish Commentaries Supporting the Meeting of Chinese and French Premiers
Author:
Date: 1954-06-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, June 25th. TASS, Paris, June 25th: Parisian newspapers of June 24th gave considerable attention to the meeting between Mendès France and Zhou Enlai in Bern. The newspapers published commentaries supporting this meeting, emphasizing that this meeting has given new hope for the smooth continuation of negotiations on restoring peace in Indochina. "L'Humanité" expressed regret that until now a "way" has not been found to enable Mendès France to meet with representatives of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam as well.

### 巴黎报纸发表评论拥护中法两国总理会晤

1954-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    巴黎报纸发表评论拥护中法两国总理会晤
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社巴黎二十五日讯：六月二十四日的巴黎报纸对孟戴斯—弗朗斯和周恩来在伯尔尼的会晤给予很大的注意。报纸刊登了拥护这次会晤的评论，强调说，这次会晤使得顺利地继续关于恢复印度支那和平的谈判有了新的希望。“义勇军报”对直到现在还没有找到一个“办法”能使孟戴斯—弗朗斯也和越南民主共和国的代表会晤一事表示遗憾。
