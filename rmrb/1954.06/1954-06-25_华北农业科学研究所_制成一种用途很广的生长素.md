Title: North China Institute of Agricultural Science Develops a Versatile Plant Growth Regulator
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-25
Page: 2

    North China Institute of Agricultural Science
    Develops a Versatile Plant Growth Regulator
    The North China Institute of Agricultural Science has developed a versatile plant growth regulator – “2,4,5-Trichlorophenoxyacetic acid” – from the useless residue after the solvent extraction of "666" powder. This plant growth regulator can kill weeds and eliminate unwanted vegetation; it can make apples grow larger and with more vibrant color, and can prevent early fruit drop; it can make tomatoes have thinner skin and thicker flesh, reduce the number of seeds in tomatoes, or make tomatoes completely seedless; and it can inhibit excessive cotton growth.
    “2,4,5-Trichlorophenoxyacetic acid” has already been used in the Soviet Union. This is the first time that our country has manufactured this plant growth regulator using the useless residue after the solvent extraction of "666" powder.
                                                 (Xinhua News Agency)

### 华北农业科学研究所  制成一种用途很广的生长素

1954-06-25
第2版()
专栏：

    华北农业科学研究所
    制成一种用途很广的生长素
    华北农业科学研究所利用“六六六”粉溶提后的无用物质制成了一种用途很广的生长素——“二、四、五—三氯苯氧醋酸”。这种生长素可以杀草除莠；可以使苹果长得更大、色泽分外鲜艳，并可防止苹果早期落果；可以使番茄变得皮薄肉厚，减少番茄的籽粒或使番茄完全没有籽粒；可以抑制棉花徒长。
    “二、四、五—三氯苯氧醋酸”已在苏联应用。我国利用“六六六”粉溶提后的无用物质制造这种生长素，这还是第一次。        
                                                 （新华社）
