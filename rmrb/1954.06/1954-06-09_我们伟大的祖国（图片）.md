Our Great Motherland
The Dalian Crane Factory successfully trial-produced a three-ton truck crane. This crane is lightweight and flexible, suitable for factories, construction sites, mines, and transportation departments. This is a photo of the three-ton truck crane undergoing a test run.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    大连起重机器厂试造三吨汽车起重机成功。这种起重机器轻便灵巧，适用于工厂、工地、矿山和交通运输部门。这是三吨汽车起重机试车的情形。
