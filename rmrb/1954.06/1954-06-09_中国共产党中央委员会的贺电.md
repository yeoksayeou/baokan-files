Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1954-06-09
Page: 4

To Comrade Thorez,
To all the delegates of the 13th Congress of the French Communist Party, Comrades:

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm and fraternal congratulations to the 13th Congress of the French Communist Party. We congratulate you on achieving greater success in uniting the people of the whole country, defending the independence and democracy of France, striving for a peaceful settlement of the Indochina issue, and defending European and world peace. We wish the congress success!

                     The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 中国共产党中央委员会的贺电

1954-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会的贺电亲爱的多列士同志转法国共产党第十三次代表大会全体代表同志们：
      中国共产党中央委员会谨向法国共产党第十三次代表大会致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。祝贺你们在团结全国人民，保卫法国的独立民主，争取印度支那问题和平解决，保卫欧洲与世界和平的斗争中，获得更大的成就。祝贺大会的成功！
                     中国共产党中央委员会
