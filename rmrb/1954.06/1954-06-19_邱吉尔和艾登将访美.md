Title: Churchill and Eden to Visit the United States
Author:
Date: 1954-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, 18th June: According to the British Information Service, British Prime Minister Churchill announced in the House of Commons on June 15th that he and British Foreign Secretary Eden, at the invitation of U.S. President Eisenhower, will visit Washington on June 25th.

### 邱吉尔和艾登将访美

1954-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    邱吉尔和艾登将访美
    【新华社十八日讯】据英国新闻处伦敦消息：英国首相邱吉尔六月十五日在下院宣布，他和英国  外交大臣艾登应美国总统艾森豪威尔的邀请，将在六月二十五日访问华盛顿。
