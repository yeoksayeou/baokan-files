Title: CPC Central Committee's Telegram of Condolences to the Central Committee of the Danish Communist Party on the Death of Nexø
Author:
Date: 1954-06-11
Page: 4

CPC Central Committee's Telegram of Condolences to the Central Committee of the Danish Communist Party on the Death of Martin Andersen Nexø

To the Central Committee of the Danish Communist Party:

We were shocked and saddened to learn of the death of Comrade Martin Andersen Nexø. We extend our deepest condolences and express our sympathy and condolences to Comrade Nexø's family.

                                     The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 中共中央致丹共中央悼唁尼克索逝世电

1954-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央致丹共中央悼唁尼克索逝世电丹麦共产党中央委员会：
    惊悉马丁·安德逊·尼克索同志逝世，谨致深切的哀悼，并向尼克索同志家属致以悼唁和慰问。
                                     中国共产党中央委员会
