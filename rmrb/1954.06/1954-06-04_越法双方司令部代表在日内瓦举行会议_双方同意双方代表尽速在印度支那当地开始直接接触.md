Title: Viet-French Headquarters Representatives Hold Meeting in Geneva; Both Sides Agree Representatives Should Begin Direct Contact Locally in Indochina as Soon as Possible
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 2nd. A special correspondent for this agency reports: Representatives of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army and the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force held their first meeting on June 2nd. This was held in accordance with the agreement reached on May 29th by the delegations of the nine countries participating in the Geneva Conference discussing the Indochina issue. According to the May 29th agreement, representatives of the commanding headquarters of the warring parties in Indochina should simultaneously hold direct talks in Geneva and locally. Their task is to study the deployment of troops on both sides after the cessation of hostilities. This study should begin with the issue of the regrouping areas for the troops of both sides in Vietnam.

According to information obtained here, the representatives of the headquarters of both sides discussed issues regarding working procedures at the meeting on the 2nd and have reached an agreement. The representative of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army also pointed out at the meeting that the representatives of the headquarters of both sides should begin direct contact locally in Indochina as soon as possible, corresponding to the talks in Geneva, to help solve the problem. The representative of the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force also expressed agreement.

Participating in the meeting on the 2nd were: Xie Guangbao, representative of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army and Deputy Minister of National Defense of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and his assistant, Colonel He Wenlou; General Delteil, representative of the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force, and his assistants, Colonel de Brebisson, Colonel Flourand, and Colonel Le Van Kim.

The next meeting will be held on June 3rd.

### 越法双方司令部代表在日内瓦举行会议  双方同意双方代表尽速在印度支那当地开始直接接触

1954-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    越法双方司令部代表在日内瓦举行会议
    双方同意双方代表尽速在印度支那当地开始直接接触
    【新华社日内瓦二日电】本社特派记者报道：越南人民军总司令部代表和法国远征军总司令部代表在六月二日举行第一次会议。这是根据参加日内瓦会议讨论印度支那问题的九国代表团在五月二十九日达成的协议而举行的。按照五月二十九日的协议，印度支那交战双方司令部的代表应同时在日内瓦和当地直接会谈，他们的任务是研究在停止敌对行动后双方军队的部署问题，此项研究应从在越南的双方军队的重新集结地区问题开始。
    据此间获得的消息说，双方司令部代表在二日会议上讨论的是关于工作程序的问题，并已达成了协议。越南人民军总司令部代表在会上还指出，双方司令部的代表应尽速在印度支那当地开始直接接触，以与日内瓦的会谈相应，有助于问题的解决。法国远征军总司令部代表也表示同意。
    参加二日会议的是：越南人民军总司令部代表、越南民主共和国政府国防部副部长谢光宝及其助理何文楼上校；法国远征军总司令部代表戴尔特尔将军及其助理德布勒比松上校、弗卢朗上校、黎文金上校等。
    下次会议将于六月三日举行。
