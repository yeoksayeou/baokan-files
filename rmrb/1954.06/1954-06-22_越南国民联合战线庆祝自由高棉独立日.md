Title: Vietnam National United Front Celebrates Free Khmer Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1954-06-22
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, June 21st: The National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front solemnly held a meeting on the 19th to celebrate Free Khmer Independence Day. Those present at the meeting included Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, and other members, representatives of the Vietnam National Assembly, ministers of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and representatives of various mass organizations in Vietnam.

　　Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, also attended the celebration. Khmer and Lao representatives also attended the celebration.

　　Ton Duc Thang delivered a speech first. He said: Under the leadership of Chairman Son Ngoc Minh and the Khmer Resistance Government, the Khmer people have achieved brilliant victories. He said: The victory of the Khmer people is also the victory of the Vietnamese and Lao people. The people of Vietnam, Khmer, and Laos will always maintain brotherly friendship and strive for unity, independence, freedom, and happiness. He firmly believes that the desire of the people of the three countries for peace and independence will surely be realized.

　　The representative of the Lao Free National United Front warmly congratulated the Khmer people on their achievements in the struggle in his speech.

　　Representatives of Vietnamese farmers and women also spoke at the meeting successively.

　　In his speech, the representative of the Khmer Free National United Front expressed the determination of the Khmer people to unite with the Vietnamese and Lao people.

　　Finally, the conference read out the congratulatory telegram from Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, to Son Ngoc Minh, Chairman of the Khmer Resistance Government, the congratulatory telegram from Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, to the Khmer Free National United Front, and the congratulatory telegrams from various Vietnamese people's organizations to various Khmer people's organizations.

　　On the same day, many agencies, organizations, and schools in various parts of Vietnam also held gatherings to celebrate Free Khmer Independence Day.

### 越南国民联合战线庆祝自由高棉独立日

1954-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国民联合战线庆祝自由高棉独立日
    【新华社北越二十一日电】越南国民联合战线全国委员会十九日隆重集会，庆祝自由高棉独立日。到会的有越南国民联合战线全国委员会主席孙德胜和其他委员、越南国民大会的代表、越南民主共和国政府的部长以及越南各群众团体的代表。
    越南劳动党总书记长征也出席了庆祝会。出席庆祝会的还有高棉和寮国的代表。
    孙德胜首先致词。他说：在山玉明主席和高棉抗战政府的领导下，高棉人民已取得了光辉的胜利。他说：高棉人民的胜利，也是越南和寮国人民的胜利。越南、高棉、寮国人民将永远保持兄弟般的友谊，争取统一、独立、自由和幸福。他坚信三国人民要求和平、独立的愿望一定能够实现。
    寮国自由民族统一战线的代表在讲话中热烈祝贺高棉人民在斗争中取得的成就。
    越南的农民和妇女代表也先后在会上讲了话。
    高棉自由民族统一战线的代表在致词中表达了高棉人民和越南、寮国人民团结一致的决心。
    大会最后宣读了越南民主共和国政府主席胡志明给高棉抗战政府主席山玉明的贺电、越南国民联合战线全国委员会主席孙德胜给高棉自由民族统一战线的贺电以及越南各人民团体给高棉各人民团体的贺电。
    同一天，越南各地的许多机关、团体和学校也都举行集会庆祝自由高棉独立日。
