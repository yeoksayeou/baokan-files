Title: Our "August 1st" Basketball Team Arrives in Warsaw for Friendly Matches
Author:
Date: 1954-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 11th: According to the Polish Press Agency in Warsaw, the Chinese sports delegation, consisting of the "August 1st" basketball team of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, arrived in Warsaw from Hungary on the 3rd for a visit. From the 3rd to the 8th, the "August 1st" basketball team and the Warsaw basketball team held two friendly matches. In the first match, the Warsaw basketball team won with a score of 82 to 70. In the second match, the "August 1st" basketball team defeated the Warsaw basketball team with a score of 75 to 54.

### 我“八一”篮球队  到达华沙举行友谊比赛

1954-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我“八一”篮球队
    到达华沙举行友谊比赛
    【新华社十一日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：由中国人民解放军“八一”篮球队组成的中国体育代表团于三日由匈牙利抵达华沙访问。从三日到八日，“八一”篮球队和华沙篮球队举行了两次友谊比赛。在第一次比赛中，华沙篮球队以八十二对七十获胜。第二次比赛，“八一”篮球队以七十五对五十四战胜华沙篮球队。
