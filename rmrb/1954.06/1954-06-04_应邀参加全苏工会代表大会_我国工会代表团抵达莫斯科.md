Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Moscow to Attend All-Soviet Trade Union Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-06-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd. Upon the invitation of the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions to attend the 11th All-Soviet Trade Union Congress, the Chinese Trade Union Delegation, led by Chairman Lai Ruoyu, arrived in Moscow by train on the 1st. Goroshkin and Kartsev, Secretaries of the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions, representatives of the central committees of various industrial trade unions and production innovators, as well as Wen Ning, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, were at the station to welcome them.

### 应邀参加全苏工会代表大会  我国工会代表团抵达莫斯科

1954-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参加全苏工会代表大会
    我国工会代表团抵达莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科三日电】应全苏工会中央理事会邀请参加第十一届全苏工会代表大会的中国工会代表团，在团长赖若愚率领下已于一日乘火车抵莫斯科。全苏工会中央理事会书记戈罗希金、卡尔泽夫，各产业工会中央委员会代表和生产革新者，以及我国驻苏联大使馆临时代办温宁都到站迎接。
