Title: Molotov Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-06-22
Page: 4

    Molotov Returns to Moscow
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 21st: According to a TASS report from Moscow on June 20th: Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov returned to Moscow from Geneva on the 20th.

### 莫洛托夫回到莫斯科

1954-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫回到莫斯科
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫二十日从日内瓦回到莫斯科。
