Title: Zhou Enlai Meets with Bidault
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 17th: According to information obtained by our correspondent, Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China, met with French Foreign Minister Bidault, head of the French delegation, at noon on the 17th.

Prior to this, Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai had met with Foreign Minister Bidault twice, on June 1st and June 7th.

### 周外长会见皮杜尔

1954-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    周外长会见皮杜尔
    【新华社日内瓦十七日电】据本社记者获悉，中华人民共和国代表团首席代表周恩来外长在十七日中午会见了法国代表团团长皮杜尔外长。
    在此之前，周恩来外长曾在六月一日和六月七日两次会见了皮杜尔外长。
