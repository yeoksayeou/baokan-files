Title: Guatemala Smashes American Conspiracy Organization
Author:
Date: 1954-06-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th, TASS Washington, June 3rd: The Guatemalan Embassy in Washington issued the following statement to the press:
      "The Guatemalan government authorities have smashed a subversive organization, a conspiracy led from abroad by traitor and well-known United Fruit Company agent Carlos Castillo Armas; five of the conspirators have taken refuge in the embassies of Ecuador and El Salvador, including José Bernabé Linares, police chief of the former Ubico dictatorial government.
    The Minister of the Interior informed the press that full details would likely be released on June 3rd. At present, the country is extremely calm."

### 危地马拉破获一美国阴谋组织

1954-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    危地马拉破获一美国阴谋组织
   【新华社四日讯】塔斯社华盛顿三日讯：危地马拉驻华盛顿大使馆向报界发表了下列声明：
      “危地马拉政府当局已破获了一个颠覆性的组织，这一组织是叛徒及有名的联合果品公司代理人卡洛斯·卡斯蒂略·阿马斯在国外指导的一个阴谋活动的一部分；其中有五个阴谋分子躲到了厄瓜多尔及萨尔瓦多的公使馆，前总统乌比可专制政府警察总监何塞·伯纳贝·林纳雷斯也是其中之一。
    内政部部长通知报界，可能在六月三日公布全部情况。目前，全国极为平静。”
