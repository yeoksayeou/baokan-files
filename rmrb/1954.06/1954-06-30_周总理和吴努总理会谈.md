Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister U Nu Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1954-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 29th: Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, held talks with Burmese Prime Minister U Nu at 10:00 AM on the 29th. After the talks, the two Prime Ministers had lunch together.

### 周总理和吴努总理会谈

1954-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理和吴努总理会谈
    【新华社二十九日讯】仰光消息：中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来二十九日上午十时与缅甸总理吴努会谈。会谈后，中缅两国总理共进午餐。
