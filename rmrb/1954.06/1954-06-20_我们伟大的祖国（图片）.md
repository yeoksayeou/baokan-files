Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-06-20
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army warmly welcome the promulgation of the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China". This photo shows Air Force Hero Liu Yuti (second from the right), Air Force Hero and Squadron Leader Li Shiying (second from the left), and their comrades reading the draft constitution.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军指战员们热烈地欢迎“中华人民共和国宪法草案”的公布。这是中国人民志愿军空军英雄刘玉堤（右起第二人）、空军英雄李世英中队长（左起第二人）和战友们在阅读宪法草案。
