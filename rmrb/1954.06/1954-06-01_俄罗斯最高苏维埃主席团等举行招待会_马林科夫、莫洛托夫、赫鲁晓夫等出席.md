Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation Holds Reception; Malenkov, Molotov, Khrushchev, and Others Attend
Author:
Date: 1954-06-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 31st: According to TASS, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation and the Council of Ministers held a grand reception in the Kremlin on May 30th to celebrate the 300th anniversary of the reunification of Ukraine with Russia.

Among those attending the reception were Soviet government officials, members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, officials of the government of the Russian Federation, delegations from the Ukrainian Republic and other Union Republics participating in the celebration assembly of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, prominent figures from industrial enterprises and construction projects in Moscow, agricultural workers, prominent figures from the Soviet army, science, and art circles.

When Malenkov, Khrushchev, Bulganin, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Shvernik, Kirichenko, Suslov, Pospelov, Tarasov, Puzanov, Korotchenko, and Kalchenko appeared in the hall, a storm of applause resounded throughout the venue.

When the reception was halfway through, Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov, who had just arrived from Geneva, appeared in the hall and was warmly welcomed by those present.

The reception was filled with an atmosphere of fraternal unity among the people of all nationalities of the Soviet Union. At the meeting, the best artists in the Soviet Union performed brilliant programs.

### 俄罗斯最高苏维埃主席团等举行招待会  马林科夫、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫等出席

1954-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯最高苏维埃主席团等举行招待会
    马林科夫、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫等出席
    【新华社三十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团和部长会议，五月三十日在克里姆林宫举行盛大的招待会，庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年。
    参加招待会的有苏联政府官员、苏联共产党中央委员会委员、俄罗斯联邦政府官员，参加俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃的庆祝大会的乌克兰共和国代表团及其他各加盟共和国的代表团、苏联最高苏维埃代表和俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃代表、莫斯科的工业企业和建筑工程中的著名人物、农业工作者、苏联军队、科学界和文艺界的著名人士。
    马林科夫、赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁、米高扬、别尔乌辛、什维尔尼克、基里钦科、苏斯洛夫、波斯伯洛夫、塔拉索夫、普扎诺夫、科罗特钦科、卡尔钦科在大厅中出现时，会场上响起了暴风雨般的掌声。
    当招待会举行了一半时，刚刚由日内瓦赶来的苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫在大厅里出现了，他受到到会者的热烈欢迎。
    招待会充满着苏联各民族人民友爱团结的气氛。会上，苏联全国最优秀的艺术家表演了精采的节目。
