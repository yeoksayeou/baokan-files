Title: Fourteen Christian Church Groups Jointly Issue Written Statement Supporting Our Delegation's Just Stand at the Geneva Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-06-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Fourteen Christian church groups in Beijing have jointly issued a written statement supporting the just stand of our delegation attending the Geneva Conference on the peaceful settlement of the Korean question and the restoration of peace in Indochina.

The statement said: The propositions of our delegation, headed by Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, on the peaceful settlement of the Korean question and the restoration of peace in Indochina, represent the firm will of our five hundred million peace-loving people, and also reflect the will of the Asian people to seek liberation and the common desire of the people of the world to love peace and oppose war. This righteous call has also stirred the conscience of us Christians who believe in peace and justice. We firmly support the speech of our Foreign Minister Zhou, and we will take practical actions to work together with the people of the whole country for the peace and security of Asia and the whole world, and for the peaceful and happy life of all mankind. The statement also called on all peace-loving Christians in the world to strive for the achievement of an agreement at the Geneva Conference and to pave a new way for the peace of the people of Asia and the world.

The fourteen groups are: The Methodist Episcopal Church of North China, the Anglican Church of China in Beijing, the Hebei Association of the Church of Christ in China, the Congregational Church of North China, Yenching Union Theological Seminary, the National Headquarters of the Salvation Army of China, the General Assembly of the Chinese Holiness Church, the North China Union of Seventh-day Adventist, the Beijing YMCA, the Beijing YWCA, the North China Union of the Assemblies of God of China, the True Jesus Church Beijing Church, the Tianjia Bimonthly Magazine, and the Beijing Christian Holy City New Church.

### 十四个基督教会团体联合发表书面声明  拥护我代表团在日内瓦会议上的正义主张

1954-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    十四个基督教会团体联合发表书面声明
    拥护我代表团在日内瓦会议上的正义主张
    【新华社五日讯】在北京的十四个基督教会团体联合发表书面声明，拥护出席日内瓦会议的我国代表团对和平解决朝鲜问题和恢复印度支那和平的正义主张。
    声明说：以周恩来外长为首的我国代表团对和平解决朝鲜问题和恢复印度支那和平的主张，代表了我国爱好和平的五亿人民的坚强意志，也反映了亚洲人民谋求解放的意志和全世界人民爱好和平反对战争的共同愿望。这正义的呼声，也激起了我们以和平和公义为信念的基督徒的良心，我们坚决地拥护我国周外长的发言，我们将以实际行动和全国人民一起为了亚洲及全世界的和平与安全，为全人类和平幸福的生活作共同的努力。声明并呼吁全世界所有爱好和平的基督徒为争取日内瓦会议达到协议，为亚洲及全世界人民的和平开辟新的途径而努力。
    这十四个团体是：中华基督教华北卫理公会、北京中华圣公会、中华基督教会河北协会、华北中华基督教公理会、燕京协和神学院、中华救世军本部、中华圣洁教会总会、基督复临安息日会华北联合会、北京基督教青年会、北京基督教女青年会、中国神召会华北联合会、真耶稣教会北京教会、田家半月刊社、北京基督教圣城新教会。
