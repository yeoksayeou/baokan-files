Title: Capital Children's Gathering Celebrates "June 1st" Children's Day
Author:
Date: 1954-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st: A capital children's celebration of "June 1st" International Children's Day was held on the morning of the 1st at the Tianqiao Theater. More than 1,500 children participated in the gathering. Children from various foreign embassies in China were also invited to attend the meeting.

Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Kang Keqing, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Protection of Children and Minister of Women and Children's Welfare Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Qu Tangliang and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Yang Zhihua, Deputy Minister of the Women Workers Department, Ge Buhai, Secretary of the North China Working Committee of the Youth League, and responsible persons of the Beijing Youth League, Democratic Women's Federation, the Municipal People's Government Education Bureau, and the Civil Affairs Bureau attended the meeting to congratulate the children on their festival.

The venue was beautifully decorated. At the entrance was a huge red banner with the gold characters "Always Ready." In the center of the rostrum hung a portrait of Chairman Mao Zedong and the five-star red flag, and the front of the stage was covered with flowers. The children, wearing bright red scarves and beautiful, neat clothing, sang and entered the venue cheerfully. When the children from various embassies entered the venue, the children applauded them for a long time.

The conference began with a speech by Guan Shuqin, a Young Pioneer of the Jingzhong Temple Primary School in Beijing, who served as the chairperson. She said: Today, we are celebrating our festival in the capital of our motherland, by the side of Chairman Mao. Let us express our warmest gratitude to the Communist Party, to our most beloved leader Chairman Mao, to the Volunteer Army and the People's Liberation Army uncles who protect our happy lives, and to the workers and farmers who work selflessly for the construction of the motherland.

Amid the children's warm applause, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, spoke, first extending warm congratulations to the children. He said: The children of the new China have grown up under the kind care and education of the Party, the government, and Chairman Mao. The motherland and the people care for you and love you, hoping that you will grow up healthily, with rich knowledge and sound bodies, so as to hold high the banner of socialism and communism and participate in the great cause of building and defending the motherland. He hoped that the children would develop a habit of loving labor, study hard, and exercise to prepare themselves to defend and build the great motherland in the future.

The band played cheerful songs, and representatives of the children from various countries attending the meeting ran onto the rostrum with flowers and presented them to the presidium of the meeting. All the children in the audience stood up excitedly and applauded and cheered. Then the children of Beijing also presented flowers to the children from various countries. The conference concluded with the adoption of letters of tribute to Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers.

After the meeting, the Children's Theater Troupe affiliated to the China Youth Art Theater performed the fairy tale "The Big Bad Wolf" for the children. Children from China, the Soviet Union, and North Korea also performed many programs. The Soviet children sang songs such as "The East is Red" in Chinese, which aroused enthusiastic cheers from everyone.

### 首都儿童集会庆祝“六一”儿童节

1954-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    首都儿童集会庆祝“六一”儿童节
    【新华社一日讯】首都儿童庆祝“六一”国际儿童节大会一日上午在天桥剧场举行，参加大会的儿童共有一千五百多人。各国驻我国使馆的小朋友也应邀出席了大会。
    北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会秘书长、中华全国民主妇女联合会妇女儿童福利部部长康克清，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记区棠亮、胡克实，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、女工部副部长杨之华，青年团华北工作委员会书记葛步海和北京市青年团、民主妇联、市人民政府教育局、民政局负责人等都到会祝贺孩子们的节日。
    会场布置得非常美丽，入口处贴着巨大的红底金字“时刻准备着”，主席台的中央悬挂着毛泽东主席的画像和五星红旗，台前遍布鲜花。孩子们戴着鲜红的领巾，穿着美丽整齐的服装，他们歌唱着，喜气洋洋的步入会场。各国大使馆的小朋友入场时，全场儿童以经久不息的掌声欢迎他们。
    大会开始，主席——北京市精忠庙小学的少年先锋队员关淑琴讲话，她说：今天我们在祖国的首都，在毛主席的身边欢庆自己的节日，让我们以最热烈的心情，感谢共产党，感谢我们最敬爱的领袖毛主席，感谢保卫我们幸福生活的志愿军、解放军叔叔，感谢为建设祖国忘我劳动的工人、农民伯伯们。
    在孩子们热烈的掌声中，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗讲话，他首先向孩子们致热烈的祝贺。他说：新中国的少年儿童，是在党、政府和毛主席的亲切关怀和教养下生长起来的，祖国和人民关怀你们，爱护你们，希望你们健康地成长起来，具有丰富的知识和健全的身体，以便高举起社会主义、共产主义的旗帜，参加到建设祖国和保卫祖国的伟大行列中来。他希望小朋友们养成热爱劳动的习惯，努力学习、锻炼身体，准备将来保卫和建设伟大的祖国。
    乐队奏起了欢快的歌曲，到会的各国小朋友的代表，手持鲜花跑上主席台，向大会主席团献花，全场的孩子们都激动地站起来鼓掌欢呼。接着北京市的少年儿童也向各国的小朋友献花。大会最后通过了给毛泽东主席和中国人民解放军与中国人民志愿军的致敬信。
    会后，中国青年艺术剧院附属中国少年儿童剧团给小朋友们演出了童话剧“大灰狼”。中国、苏联和朝鲜的小朋友也表演了很多节目，苏联小朋友用中国话唱的“东方红”等歌，引起了大家的热烈欢呼。
