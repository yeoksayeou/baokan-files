Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Visits Burma; Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and Others Welcome Him at Rangoon Airport
Author:
Date: 1954-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 29th. News: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and his party of sixteen arrived in Rangoon, the capital of Burma, at 1:45 pm on the 28th.

    Burmese Prime Minister U Nu personally welcomed him at the airport.

    Yao Zhongming, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, led the embassy staff to the airport to welcome him.

    Also at the airport to welcome him were: U Ba Swe, representative of the President of Burma, U Khin Maung Gale, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs, U Win, Minister of Religious Affairs and Minister of Culture, U Ba Swe, Minister of Defence, U Tin, Minister of Finance and Revenue, Bo Khin Maung Gale, Minister of Home Affairs, Mann Win Maung, Minister of Transport and Communications, Tekin Tin, Minister of Land Nationalization, Tekin Daqing, Minister of Commerce, U Khin Maung Lat, Minister of Health, U Aung Pa, Minister of Karen State Affairs, U Min Din, Judge of the Supreme Court, U Tun Shein, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and other senior officials.

    Also at the airport to welcome him were diplomatic envoys to Burma from various countries: Pakistani Ambassador Sultanuddin Ahmed, Indian Ambassador K.K. Chettur, British Ambassador Paul Henry Gore-Booth, Ceylonese Minister Viluppillai Kumaraswamy, Soviet Chargé d'Affaires Semyon P. Dzioukarev, Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires T.M. Habsjah, Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Jozef Welsing, Nepalese Acting Consul D.B. Basnyat, and Representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Tran Van Luan.

    Also at the airport to welcome him were Polish Trade Commissioner to Pakistan Bolar, and Kinder, representative of the trade delegation of the German Democratic Republic.

    The Burmese and Chinese national flags were flying at Rangoon Airport.

    After disembarking from the plane, Premier Zhou Enlai shook hands with Prime Minister U Nu and then reviewed the Burmese guard of honor. The band played the national anthems of the People's Republic of China and Burma.

    After the review, Premier Zhou Enlai made the following statement:

    "I am deeply honored to have this opportunity to visit our neighbor, Burma. On behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, I extend warm greetings to the government and people of the Union of Burma.

    May the friendly relations between China and Burma develop day by day.

    May the peace of Asia and the world be increasingly consolidated."

    Finally, Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, traveled by car to the Presidential Palace to rest. Along the way, he was warmly welcomed by nearly ten thousand Burmese people, overseas Chinese, and Indian expatriates.

### 周恩来总理到缅甸访问  缅甸总理吴努等到仰光飞机场欢迎

1954-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理到缅甸访问
    缅甸总理吴努等到仰光飞机场欢迎
    【新华社二十九日讯】仰光消息：中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来一行十六人在二十八日下午一时四十五分到达缅甸首都仰光。
    缅甸总理吴努亲自到机场欢迎。
    中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明率领大使馆人员到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有：缅甸总统代表吴巴信，外交部代理部长吴觉迎，宗教事务部部长兼文化部部长吴温，国防部部长吴巴瑞，财政与税务部部长吴阵，内政部部长波庆貌格礼，运输与交通部部长曼温貌，土地国有化部部长德钦阵，商业部部长德钦达庆，卫生部部长吴钦貌腊，克伦邦事务部部长吴昂帕，最高法院法官吴敏登，外交部常任秘书吴顿辛等高级官员。
    到机场欢迎的还有各国驻缅甸的外交使节：巴基斯坦大使苏丹努丁·艾哈迈德，印度大使克·克·契图尔，英国大使保罗·亨利·戈尔—布斯，锡兰公使维卢皮莱·科马拉斯瓦米，苏联代办谢米昂·普·久卡列夫，印尼代办特·麦·哈布斯雅，荷兰代办约瑟夫·韦尔辛，尼泊尔代理领事德·布·巴斯尼雅特，越南民主共和国代表陈文伦。
    到机场欢迎的还有波兰驻巴基斯坦贸易专员波拉尔，德意志民主共和国贸易代表团代表金德勒。
    仰光机场上悬挂着缅甸和中华人民共和国的国旗。
    周恩来总理下机后，即与吴努总理握手，随即检阅了缅甸仪仗队。乐队奏起中华人民共和国国歌和缅甸国歌。
    检阅完毕后，周恩来总理发表谈话如下：
    “我此次获有机会访问我们的邻邦缅甸，深感荣幸。我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向缅甸联邦政府和人民热烈地致意。
    愿中缅两国的友好关系日益发展。
    愿亚洲及世界的和平日益巩固。”
    最后，周恩来总理在缅甸总理吴努陪同下乘车至总统府休息。沿途受到近万的缅甸人民和华侨以及印度侨民的热烈欢迎。
