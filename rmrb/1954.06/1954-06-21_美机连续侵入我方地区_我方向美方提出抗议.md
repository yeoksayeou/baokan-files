Title: U.S. Aircraft Continuously Invade Our Area; We Protest to the U.S. Side
Author:
Date: 1954-06-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 19th—From May 24 to June 14, 1954, twenty more incidents occurred in which U.S. military aircraft violated the Armistice Agreement and intruded into the area on the Korean-Chinese side. On June 18, the Korean-Chinese side, acting on instructions, protested to the U.S. side at the meeting of the secretaries of both sides of the Military Armistice Commission.

### 美机连续侵入我方地区  我方向美方提出抗议

1954-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    美机连续侵入我方地区
    我方向美方提出抗议
    【新华社开城十九日电】一九五四年五月二十四日至六月十四日间，又连续发生美方军用飞机违反停战协定侵入朝中方面地区的事件共二十起。朝中方面在六月十八日军事停战委员会双方秘书长会议上奉命向美方提出抗议。
