Title: Vietnam People's Army Receives Wounded, Sick, and Captured Personnel

Author: 
Date: 1954-06-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 19th: According to the Vietnam News Agency on June 19th: After the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army released 858 wounded and captured soldiers of the French Expeditionary Force captured at Dien Bien Phu, the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force informed the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army that it had handed over some wounded and sick captured personnel of the Vietnam People's Army to our side on June 16th and 17th.

The Viet Bac Interzone Command of the Vietnam People's Army, acting on the orders of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army, dispatched representatives and medical staff to a location eight kilometers north of Phu Lang Thuong to receive some wounded and sick captured personnel of the Vietnam People's Army. Most of the captured personnel suffered from rheumatism, lung disease, and other ailments due to long-term detention and hardship.

During the handover process, both sides ceased all military activity within a radius of ten kilometers, in accordance with the agreement they had reached.

### 越南人民军接收伤病被俘人员

1954-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军接收伤病被俘人员
    【新华社十九日讯】据越南通讯社十九日讯：在越南人民军总司令部把在奠边府俘掳的八百五十八名法国远征军伤俘释放以后，法国远征军总司令部通知越南人民军总司令部说，它在六月十六日和十七日把一些越南人民军伤、病被俘人员交给我方。
    越南人民军越北联区司令部奉越南人民军总司令部的命令，派遣了代表和医务工作人员到府谅商以北八公里的地方去接收若干越南人民军伤、病被俘人员。其中大部分被俘人员由于长期的拘禁和苦难都患有风湿症、肺病和其他疾病。
    在移交过程中，双方根据他们取得的协议，在半径为十公里以内的地区内停止一切军事活动。
