Our Great Motherland
    After the Sanhe Sluice and Gaoliangjian Inlet Sluice on the Hongze Lake released water, more than ten million mu of rice paddies in northern Jiangsu Province were irrigated. Therefore, farmers in this area are in a position to popularize high-quality rice varieties such as "Nante No. 1" and "Victory Xian," so as to obtain a greater harvest this year. This is the scene of the Sanhe Sluice releasing water.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    洪泽湖畔的三河闸和高良涧进水闸放水后，江苏省北部一千多万亩的稻田得到了灌溉。因此这一带农民就有条件来普遍推广“南特号”、“胜利籼”等良种水稻，使今年获得更大的丰收。这是三河闸正在放水的情形。
