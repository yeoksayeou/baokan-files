Title: Lao People's Liberation Army Wins Ambush Against Enemy Troops
Author:
Date: 1954-06-30
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Vientiane, June 22nd: On June 19th, the Lao People's Liberation Army ambushed an enemy unit on Highway 13, south of Pakse in Lower Laos. According to preliminary reports, the Lao People's Liberation Army has annihilated most of this 300-strong enemy force and is pursuing the fleeing remnants.

### 寮国解放军胜利伏击敌军

1954-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    寮国解放军胜利伏击敌军
    【新华社讯】据越南通讯社二十二日讯：六月十九日，寮国人民解放军在下寮巴色以南地区的第十三号公路上伏击一支敌军。据初步消息，寮国人民解放军已将这支三百人的敌军大部歼灭，并正在追歼逃跑的残敌。
