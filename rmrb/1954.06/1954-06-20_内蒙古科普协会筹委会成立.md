Title: Preparatory Committee of Inner Mongolia Science Popularization Association Established
Author:
Date: 1954-06-20
Page: 3

The Preparatory Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Science and Technology Popularization Association was established on June 10th, and the first Inner Mongolia Science and Technology Popularization Work Report Conference was held on the 11th. Wang Wenda, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Inner Mongolia Sub-bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liang Xi, Chairman of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, and Yuan Hanqing, Deputy Secretary-General, attended and gave guidance. The establishment of the Inner Mongolia Science Popularization Association Preparatory Committee is of great significance for improving the scientific and technological level of people of all ethnic groups in the future and promoting the development of various construction undertakings in the autonomous region. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 内蒙古科普协会筹委会成立

1954-06-20
第3版()
专栏：

    内蒙古科普协会筹委会成立
    内蒙古自治区科学技术普及协会筹备委员会已在六月十日成立，并在十一日举行了第一次内蒙古科学技术普及工作报告会。中共中央内蒙古分局宣传部部长王文达、中华全国科学技术普及协会主席梁希和副秘书长袁翰青都出席指导。内蒙古科普协会筹委会的成立，对今后提高各族人民科学技术水平，推广自治区各项建设事业的发展都有重要意义。（新华社）
