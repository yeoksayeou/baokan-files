Title: Major Factories and Mines in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Complete May Output Value Plan
Author:
Date: 1954-06-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, June 11th: Major state-owned and locally state-owned factories and mines in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region overfulfilled the May output value plan by an average of 4.66 percent. The output value plans for factories and mines in Hohhot and Baotou were completed on a ten-day basis. From January to May, the actual output value of 46 major factories and mines in the region has completed 44.13 percent of the figure stipulated in the draft annual plan (excluding the increase in production and savings); the four major products, including low-voltage electrical porcelain and bituminous coal, have reached more than 50 percent of the annual plan.

### 内蒙古自治区主要厂矿  完成五月份产值计划

1954-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区主要厂矿
    完成五月份产值计划
    【新华社呼和浩特十一日电】内蒙古自治区国营和地方国营主要厂矿平均超额百分之四点六六完成了五月份的产值计划。呼和浩特和包头两市厂矿的产值计划都按旬完成。一到五月份，全区四十六个主要厂矿的实际产值已完成年度计划草案（不包括增产节约部分）规定数字的百分之四十四点一三；低压电瓷、烟煤等四种主要产品已达到年度计划百分之五十以上。
