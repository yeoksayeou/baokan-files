Title: Premier Nehru Hosts Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1954-06-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 27th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru held a banquet at the Indian Presidential Palace at 8:30 PM on the 26th to host Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China.

The Chinese side attending the banquet included: Qiao Guanhua, Wang Zhuoru, and 10 others. Yuan Zhongxian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, also attended the banquet.

The Indian side attending included: Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan, Minister of Transport Jagjivan Ram, Minister of Planning, Irrigation and Power Gulzarilal Nanda, Minister of Home Affairs Kailas Nath Katju, Minister of Commerce and Industry T. T. Krishnamachari, Minister of Rehabilitation Ajit Prasad Jain, Minister of Defence Mahavir Tyagi, Minister of Trade D. P. Karmarkar, Member of the States Reorganisation Commission and former Ambassador to China K. M. Panikkar, Deputy Minister of Labour Abid Ali, Deputy Minister of Health Maragatham Chandrasekhar, Cabinet Secretary I. N. Sukthankar, Deputy Minister of External Affairs A. K. Chanda, Secretary-General of the Ministry of External Affairs N. R. Pillai, Foreign Secretary R. K. Nehru, Commonwealth Secretary S. Dutt, Chief of Protocol I. S. Chopra, Secretary of Commerce and Industry Ingal, Commander-in-Chief of the Indian Army Rajendrasinhji Jadeja, Commander-in-Chief of the Navy K. T. M. Pizey, Commander-in-Chief of the Air Force S. Mukerjee, Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the Navy R. D. Katari, Chief Commissioner of Delhi A. D. Pandit, and Chief Minister of Delhi Chaudhry Brahm Prakash.

Also attending the banquet were diplomatic envoys to India from various countries: High Commissioner of Ceylon K. Commaraswamy, French Ambassador Ostrorog, High Commissioner of Australia Crocker, Ambassador of the Netherlands van Boetzelaer, Ambassador of Indonesia Bara, Ambassador of Abyssinia R. H. S. Imru, Ambassador of Afghanistan Sardar Abdul Hussein Aziz, Deputy High Commissioner of the United Kingdom Middleton, Minister of Finland Warvanne, Minister of Denmark Toyberg-Frandsen, Minister of Sweden Wijkman, Minister of Norway Løken, Minister of Switzerland Graselli, Minister of Hungary Kos, Chargé d'affaires of Burma U Maung Maung Soe, Chargé d'affaires of Czechoslovakia Antos, Chargé d'affaires of Pakistan Aysal, and Chargé d'affaires of the Soviet Union Ashurov.

At the end of the banquet, Prime Minister Nehru rose and toasted the health of Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The band played the national anthem of the People's Republic of China.

Then, Premier Zhou Enlai rose and toasted the health of President Prasad of the Republic of India. The band played the national anthem of the Republic of India.

Then, Prime Minister Nehru rose and delivered a speech (full text to be released separately).

Finally, Premier Zhou Enlai rose and delivered a speech (full text to be released separately).

The banquet proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere throughout, ending at 11:30 PM late at night.

### 尼赫鲁总理招待周总理

1954-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理招待周总理
    【新华社新德里二十七日电】印度共和国总理尼赫鲁于二十六日晚八时三十分在印度总统府举行宴会，招待中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来。
    中国方面出席宴会的有：乔冠华，王倬如等十二人。中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤也出席了宴会。
    印度方面出席的有：印度副总统拉德哈克里希南，交通部部长贾·拉姆，计划、水利工程与电力部部长古·南达，内政部部长凯·纳·卡特朱，工商部部长蒂·塔·克里希纳马查里，灾荒善后部部长阿·普·贾殷，国防部部长马哈维尔·特雅吉，商业部部长达·帕·卡马卡尔，省区划分委员会委员、前驻中国大使潘尼迦，劳工部副部长阿比德·阿里，卫生部副部长马拉加塔姆·钱德拉塞卡尔，内阁秘书伊·纳·苏克坦卡尔，外交部副部长阿·库·钱达，外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱，外事秘书拉·库·尼赫鲁，英联邦秘书斯·杜特，交际司司长伊·斯·乔普拉，工商部秘书英加尔，印度陆军总司令拉金德拉辛吉，海军总司令克·特·麦·皮泽，空军总司令斯·慕克吉，海军副总司令鲁·德·卡塔里，德里邦行政专员阿·德·潘迪特，德里邦首席部长乔·布·普拉卡希。
    出席宴会的还有各国驻印度外交使节：锡兰高级专员克·科马拉斯瓦米，法国大使奥斯特罗洛格，澳大利亚高级专员克罗克，荷兰大使范·柏兰特，印度尼西亚大使巴拉，阿比西尼亚大使鲁·赫·斯·伊姆鲁，阿富汗大使苏达尔·阿卜杜勒·侯赛尼·阿齐兹，英国副高级专员密德尔顿，芬兰公使瓦尔万尼，丹麦公使托伊伯格—弗朗森，瑞典公使威克曼，挪威公使李克，瑞士公使格拉斯里，匈牙利公使科斯，缅甸代办吴貌貌梭，捷克斯洛伐克代办安托希，巴基斯坦代办艾瑟尔，苏联代办阿舒罗夫。
    在宴会的末尾，尼赫鲁总理起立举杯祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席健康。乐队奏中华人民共和国国歌。
    接着周恩来总理起立举杯祝印度共和国普拉沙德总统健康。乐队奏印度共和国国歌。
    然后，尼赫鲁总理起立致词（全文另发）。
    最后，周恩来总理起立致词（全文另发）。
    宴会始终在友好热烈气氛中进行，至深夜十一时三十分结束。
