Title: Foreign Minister Molotov Hosts Luncheon for Menon
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 11th: On June 10th, Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov hosted a luncheon in honor of Indian politician Mr. Krishna Menon.

### 莫洛托夫外长设宴招待梅农

1954-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫外长设宴招待梅农
    【新华社日内瓦十一日电】六月十日，苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫设午餐招待印度政治家克里希纳·梅农先生。
