Author:
Date: 1954-06-15
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The "Young Aviation Model Makers Group" of the Beijing Children's Home is composed of outstanding Young Pioneers from various middle schools in Beijing who love aviation models. They regularly participate in various activities to develop aviation models. This is a picture of group members enjoying the beautiful scenery of the capital from the air after a get-together with the Air Force of the capital.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京市少年之家“少年航空模型家小组”，是由北京市各中学爱好航空模型的优秀少年先锋队员组成的，他们经常参加各种发展航空模型的活动。这是组员和首都空军部队联欢后乘坐飞机俯览首都的美丽景色。
