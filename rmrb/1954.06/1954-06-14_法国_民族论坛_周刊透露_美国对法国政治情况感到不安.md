Title: French "Tribune of the Nation" Weekly Reveals: United States Uneasy About French Political Situation
Author:
Date: 1954-06-14
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, June 12th: The French weekly "Tribune of the Nation" says it has learned the contents of a recent report made by the US Ambassador to France, Dillon, to his own government. The report shows that official US representatives are uneasy about the domestic political situation in France.

    Dillon pointed out that the current French government's policy on Indochina is facing serious opposition. Dillon describes this opposition as "dangerous."

    The "Tribune of the Nation" weekly continued: "According to information from the US Ambassador to France, even many anti-communists believe that the communist countries sincerely hope to strive for a peaceful resolution of the Indochina issue on the basis of possible compromise. Dillon believes that restoring peace in Indochina under the current circumstances not only means the danger of a political victory for the communists in Asia, but also a practical danger to US policy in Europe." In Dillon's opinion, ending the Indochina war may strengthen the position of opponents of the Paris Treaty.

    The weekly also wrote: "After describing the true situation in this way, the US Ambassador to France comes to the conclusion that the only 'sensible' way to get rid of this situation is to try every means to interrupt the Geneva Conference, eliminate all possible dangerous factors for US policy, and at the same time oppose any common resolution that may be reached with communist countries."

    The "Tribune of the Nation" weekly said that Dillon believes: the future French government "will not show the current level of obedience to the US plans in Europe and Asia."

### 法国“民族论坛”周刊透露  美国对法国政治情况感到不安

1954-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    法国“民族论坛”周刊透露
    美国对法国政治情况感到不安
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社巴黎十二日讯：法国“民族论坛”周刊说，它已获悉美国驻法国大使狄龙不久以前向自己的政府所作的报告的内容。报告表明美国官方代表对法国国内政治情况感到不安。
    狄龙指出，目前法国政府在印度支那问题上的政策遭到严重的反对。狄龙把这种反对说成是“危险的”。
    “民族论坛”周刊继续说：“根据美国驻法国大使的情报，甚至许多反共人士都相信，共产党国家是真诚希望在可能妥协的基础上争取和平解决印度支那问题的。狄龙认为，在目前的情况下恢复印度支那的和平，不仅意味着共产党在亚洲得到政治胜利的危险，而且对于美国在欧洲的政策也是实际的危险。”照狄龙的意见，停止印度支那战争可能促使巴黎条约反对者的立场更加坚定。
    这个周刊还写道：“美国驻法国大使在这样描写了真实情况之后，得出这样的结论：摆脱这种情况的唯一‘明智的’出路，就是竭力采取一切手段中断日内瓦会议，消除对于美国政策的一切可能的危险因素，同时反对可能和共产党国家达成协议的任何共同决议。”
    “民族论坛”周刊说，狄龙认为：未来的法国政府“不会对美国在欧洲和亚洲的计划表现出目前这样的顺从态度”。
