Title: Protocol of the First Meeting of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical-Scientific Cooperation Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-06-24
Page: 1
    Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical-Scientific Cooperation
    Protocol of the First Meeting Signed in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 23] The protocol of the first meeting of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical-Scientific Cooperation was signed in Beijing on the 23rd. The protocol includes: the charter of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical-Scientific Cooperation, the common conditions for implementing the resolutions of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical-Scientific Cooperation, the technical cooperation projects proposed by the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic for assistance, and the technical cooperation projects proposed by the German Democratic Republic to the People's Republic of China for assistance.
    The signing ceremony of the protocol was held in Beijing on the afternoon of the 23rd. Representing the Chinese side in the signing was Wang Daohan, leader of the Chinese group of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical-Scientific Cooperation and Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the Central People's Government. Representing the German Democratic Republic was Richard Goschuetz, leader of the German group of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical-Scientific Cooperation and State Secretary of the Ministry of Heavy Industry.
    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Jia Tuofu, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Sun Liyu, Director of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the State Planning Commission; Guan Hantao, member of the Chinese group of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical-Scientific Cooperation and Deputy Director of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the State Planning Commission; Chang Yanqing, acting member of the Chinese group and Deputy Director of the Second Foreign Trade Bureau of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Foreign Trade; and several consultant experts from the Chinese group. On the side of the German Democratic Republic were: Johannes König, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China; Dürrbeck, Commercial Counselor of the Embassy; and all members of the German delegation to the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical-Scientific Cooperation.

### 中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会第一届会议议定书在北京签订

1954-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会
    第一届会议议定书在北京签订
    【新华社二十三日讯】中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会第一届会议议定书已在二十三日在北京签订。议定书包括：中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会章程、关于执行中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会决议的共同条件、中华人民共和国方面提请德意志民主共和国方面援助的技术合作项目及德意志民主共和国方面提请中华人民共和国方面援助的技术合作项目等。
    二十三日下午在北京举行了议定书签字仪式。代表中国方面签字的是中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会中国组组长、中央人民政府第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，代表德意志民主共和国方面签字的是中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会德国组组长、重工业部国务秘书理夏德·戈舒埃茨。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有：中央人民政府国家计划委员会副主席贾拓夫，外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，国家计划委员会技术合作局局长孙力余，中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会中国组委员、中央人民政府国家计划委员会技术合作局副局长管寒涛，中国组代理委员、中央人民政府对外贸易部国外贸易第二局副局长常彦卿以及中国组顾问专家多人；德意志民主共和国方面有：德意志民主共和国驻华大使柯尼希、大使馆商务参赞杜尔伯，中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会德国代表团全体团员。
