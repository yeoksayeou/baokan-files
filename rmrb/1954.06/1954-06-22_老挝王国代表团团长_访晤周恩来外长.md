Title: Head of the Royal Lao Delegation Meets with Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-06-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 21st: Phoui Sananikone, the head of the Royal Lao delegation attending the Geneva Conference, met with Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, at 12:00 noon on the 21st for a discussion lasting over an hour. Accompanying Phoui Sananikone were Royal Lao delegation representatives Oura Souvannavong and Leunam. Present on the Chinese side were delegation representatives Li Kenong and advisor Chen Jiakang.

### 老挝王国代表团团长  访晤周恩来外长

1954-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝王国代表团团长
    访晤周恩来外长
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】老挝王国出席日内瓦会议代表团团长冯·萨纳尼空二十一日中午十二时访晤中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来，会谈一小时余。和冯·萨纳尼空一同前来的有老挝王国代表团代表奥拉·苏发拉冯·勒南。中国方面在座的有代表团代表李克农和顾问陈家康。
