Title: Soviet "Pravda" and "Izvestia" Publish News of China's Adoption of This Year's Budget
Author:
Date: 1954-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 19th. The newspapers "Pravda," "Izvestia," and others published on the 19th the news that the 31st meeting of the Central People's Government Committee of China heard the report of Deng Xiaoping, Minister of Finance, on the 1954 national budget and adopted the 1954 national budget.

### 苏联“真理报”、“消息报”  刊载我国通过今年预算消息

1954-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联“真理报”、“消息报”
    刊载我国通过今年预算消息
    【新华社莫斯科十九日电】“真理报”、“消息报”等报纸十九日刊登了我国中央人民政府委员会第三十一次会议听取了财政部部长邓小平关于一九五四年国家预算的报告，并通过了一九五四年国家预算的消息。
