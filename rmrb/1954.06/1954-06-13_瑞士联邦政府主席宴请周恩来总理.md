Title: Chairman of the Swiss Federal Government Hosts Premier Zhou Enlai

Author: 
Date: 1954-06-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 12th: Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China delegation to the Geneva Conference, visited Mr. Max Petitpierre, Head of the Political Department (i.e., Ministry of Foreign Affairs) of the Swiss Federal Government and member of the Federal Council, in Bern on the morning of the 12th, accompanied by Feng Xuan, China's envoy to Switzerland. Premier Zhou Enlai then met with Mr. Rodolphe Rubattel, Chairman of the Swiss Federal Government, accompanied by Minister Petitpierre.

On the afternoon of the 12th, Mr. Rodolphe Rubattel, Chairman of the Swiss Federal Government, hosted a banquet in honor of Premier Zhou Enlai. Also invited to the banquet were Zhang Wentian, representative of the People's Republic of China delegation, Secretary-General Wang Bingnan, advisors Lei Renmin, Huang Hua, Huan Xiang, Wang Zhuoru, and Feng Xuan, China's envoy to Switzerland.

Swiss attendees at the banquet included: Federal Council members Max Petitpierre and Karl Kobelt, Special Commissioner of the Federal Council stationed at the Political Department, Walter Stucki, the newly appointed Swiss envoy to China, Max Fernand Benoit, Commercial Treaty Representative of the National Economic Department, Max Trendel, Deputy Director of the Political Affairs Division of the Political Department, Egbert Graffenried, Commercial Treaty Representative of the National Economic Department, Hans Schaffner, and Chief of Protocol of the Political Department, Robert Maurice.

### 瑞士联邦政府主席宴请周恩来总理

1954-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞士联邦政府主席宴请周恩来总理
    【新华社日内瓦十二日电】参加日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团首席代表周恩来总理兼外交部部长在十二日上午去伯尔尼，由我国驻瑞士公使冯铉陪同，访问了瑞士联邦政府政治部（即外交部）部长、联邦政务委员会委员马·彼蒂彼爱。周恩来总理继由彼蒂彼爱部长陪同，会见了瑞士联邦政府主席鲁·陆巴特尔。
    十二日下午，瑞士联邦政府主席陆巴特尔宴请周恩来总理。应邀出席宴会的还有中华人民共和国代表团代表张闻天，秘书长王炳南，顾问雷任民、黄华、宦乡、王倬如和我国驻瑞士公使冯铉等人。
    瑞士方面参加宴会的还有：联邦政务委员会委员马·彼蒂彼爱、卡尔·柯贝尔，联邦政务委员会驻政治部特派员华尔特·斯图基，瑞士新任驻华公使麦·费南·贝诺伊，国民经济部商约代表马克斯·特伦德尔，政治部政务司副司长埃格伯特·格拉芬里德，国民经济部商约代表汉斯·夏夫纳和政治部礼宾处处长罗伯·莫里斯。
