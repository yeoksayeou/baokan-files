Title: Lao People's Liberation Army Annihilates Over a Hundred Enemies
Author:
Date: 1954-06-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 9th: Vietnam News Agency reported on the 8th that the Lao Resistance Government recently issued a communique stating that on June 1st, the Lao People's Liberation Army launched an attack on the French army stronghold of Tiritte south of Pakse, annihilating 70 enemy soldiers and seizing 80 machine guns and rifles. The communique said that on May 27th, the Lao People's Liberation Army ambushed the enemy near Nam Bo in Upper Laos, annihilating 35 enemy soldiers.

### 寮国人民解放军歼敌百余名

1954-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    寮国人民解放军歼敌百余名
    【新华社九日讯】据越南通讯社八日讯：寮国抗战政府最近发表公报说：六月一日，寮国人民解放军向巴色城以南蒂利特的法军据点发起攻击，歼灭敌军七十名，缴获机枪和步枪八十挺（支）。公报说：五月二十七日，寮国人民解放军在上寮的南博附近截击敌人，歼灭敌军三十五名。
