Title: Korean Delegation Returns to Beijing from Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1954-06-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: The Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Kim Ung-ki, and the accompanying art troupe, accompanied by Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, returned to Beijing by train from Shenyang on the morning of the 24th.

More than 500 people, including responsible persons and representatives from units such as the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Chinese People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association, the General Office of the Central People's Government, the General Political Department and General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and the Beijing Municipal People's Government, as well as figures from the capital's literary and artistic circles, went to the station to welcome them.

Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and embassy staff also went to the station to welcome them.

### 朝鲜访华团由沈阳回到北京

1954-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华团由沈阳回到北京
    【新华社二十四日讯】以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团和随行艺术团由中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林陪同，在二十四日上午从沈阳乘车回到北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中国人民抗美援朝总会和中央人民政府办公厅、人民革命军事委员会总政治部和办公厅、外交部亚洲司、北京市人民政府等单位的负责人和代表，首都文学艺术界人士等共五百多人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一和使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
