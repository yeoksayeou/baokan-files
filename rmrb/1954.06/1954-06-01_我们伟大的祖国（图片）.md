Author:
Date: 1954-06-01

    Our Great Motherland
    On the eve of "June 1st" International Children's Day, the Young Pioneers of Beijing No. 2 Girls' Middle School visited the science book writer Gao Shiqi, expressing their love and admiration for scientists. This is a scene of the Young Pioneers of the school presenting their red scarves to Gao Shiqi.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在“六一”国际儿童节的前夕，北京市第二女子中学的少年先锋队员访问了科学读物作家高士其，表达了他们对科学家的热爱和敬仰。这是该校少年先锋队员把红领巾献给高士其的场面。
