Title: Vietnamese People's Army Destroys a French Military Train and Sinks an Enemy Naval Gunboat
Author:
Date: 1954-06-04
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 2nd) According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 1st, on May 24th, the Vietnamese People's Army destroyed a train carrying American supplies for the French colonialists on the railway line 13 kilometers northeast of Hai Duong, connecting Hanoi and Hai Phong.

On the 27th, the People's Army intercepted an enemy transport team on the Red River, 2 kilometers northwest of Hung Yen, sinking an enemy naval gunboat and damaging two motorboats.

### 越南人民军毁法军火车一列  并击沉敌军内河兵舰一艘

1954-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民军毁法军火车一列
    并击沉敌军内河兵舰一艘
    【新华社二日讯】据越南通讯社一日讯：五月二十四日，越南人民军在海阳东北十三公里连接河内和海防的铁路线上，击毁载有美国供给法国殖民者的武器的火车一列。
    二十七日，人民军在兴安西北二公里处截击红河上的一支敌军运输队，击沉敌军内河兵舰一艘，击伤汽艇两艘。
