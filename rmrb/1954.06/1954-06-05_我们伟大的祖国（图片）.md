Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-06-05
Page: 1

Our great motherland's young students are working hard to study science and technology, preparing to contribute to the construction of the motherland. These two are second-year students majoring in aerial surveying in the Surveying Department of Tongji University in Shanghai. They are using a multiple projection plotter to draw topographic maps.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国青年学生正在努力学习科学技术，准备为祖国建设贡献力量。这两位是上海同济大学测量系航空测量专业二年级的学生，她们正用多倍投影测图仪绘地形图。
