Title: Albanian People Hold Elections for the Third People's Assembly
Author:
Date: 1954-06-02
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 1st) According to TASS, the working people of Albania held elections for the Third People's Assembly on May 30th amidst a surge of political enthusiasm. Early in the morning, voters in festive attire went to the polling stations in batches. According to figures released by the Central Election Committee, from 6 a.m. to noon, 94 to 98.5 percent of voters in nine states across the country had already cast their ballots. In the capital of the Republic, Tirana, the number of voters who had voted by noon had reached 97 percent of the total number of voters.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民举行第三届人民议会选举

1954-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民举行第三届人民议会选举
    【新华社一日讯】据塔斯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚劳动人民五月三十日在高涨的政治热情中举行了第三届人民议会选举。一清早，穿着节日服装的选民就一批一批地走到投票站去。根据中央选举委员会发表的数字，从上午六时到中午，国内有九个州已有百分之九十四到百分之九十八点五的选民投完了票。在共和国的首都地拉那，到中午时投票的选民已达到选民总数的百分之九十七。
