Title: The Central People's Government's Decision on Approving the Incorporation of Suiyuan Province into the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Abolishing the Administrative Division of Suiyuan Province
Author:
Date: 1954-06-20
Page: 1

    The Central People's Government's Decision on Approving the Incorporation of Suiyuan Province into the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Abolishing the Administrative Division of Suiyuan Province
    (Passed at the 32nd Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee on June 19, 1954)
    In order to meet the needs of national construction, the report of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the People's Government of Suiyuan Province on January 22, 1954, forwarded by the State Council of the Central People's Government, is hereby approved, and it is decided to incorporate Suiyuan Province into the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and abolish the administrative division of Suiyuan Province. The Suiyuan Military and Political Committee and the Suiyuan Provincial People's Government shall also be abolished accordingly.

### 中央人民政府关于批准将绥远省划归内蒙古自治区并撤销绥远省建制的决定

1954-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府关于批准将绥远省划归内蒙古自治区并撤销绥远省建制的决定
    （一九五四年六月十九日中央人民政府委员会第三十二次会议通过）
    为了适应国家建设的需要，兹批准中央人民政府政务院转报的内蒙古自治区人民政府、绥远省人民政府一九五四年一月二十二日的报告，决定将绥远省划归内蒙古自治区，撤销绥远省建制。绥远军政委员会和绥远省人民政府亦相应撤销。
