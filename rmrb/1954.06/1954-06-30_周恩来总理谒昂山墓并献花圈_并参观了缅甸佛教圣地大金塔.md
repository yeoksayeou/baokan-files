Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Visits Aung San's Tomb and Lays a Wreath; Also Visits the Shwedagon Pagoda, a Burmese Buddhist Holy Site
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 29th: Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by advisors Qiao Guanhua and Wang Zhuoru, and Yao Zhongming, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, visited Aung San's tomb at 3:30 PM on the 28th and laid a wreath.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 29th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by U Thin Maung, Chief of Protocol of the Burmese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, visited the Shwedagon Pagoda, a Burmese Buddhist holy site, at 9:30 AM on the 29th.

### 周恩来总理谒昂山墓并献花圈  并参观了缅甸佛教圣地大金塔

1954-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理谒昂山墓并献花圈
    并参观了缅甸佛教圣地大金塔
    【新华社二十九日讯】仰光消息：中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来偕顾问乔冠华、王倬如在中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明陪同下，在二十八日下午三时半谒昂山墓并献花圈。
    【新华社二十九日讯】仰光消息：中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来二十九日上午九时三十分在缅甸外交部交际处处长吴阵貌陪同下，参观了缅甸佛教圣地大金塔。
