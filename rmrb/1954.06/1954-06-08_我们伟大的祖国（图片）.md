    Our Great Motherland
    Bamboo is a special product of our country, and it is widely used in building construction, transportation, agricultural implements, and craft raw materials. This is a bamboo raft on the river surface in the Yuhang area of Zhejiang, one of the main bamboo-producing areas, ready to be transported to various places.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-08

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    竹是我国的一种特产，在建筑工程、交通工具、农业用具和工艺原料等方面都有很大的用途。这是产竹的主要地区之一—浙江余杭一带江面上的竹排，准备运往各地。
