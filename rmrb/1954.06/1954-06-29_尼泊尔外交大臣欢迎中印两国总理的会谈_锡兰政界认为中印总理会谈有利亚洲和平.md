Title: Nepalese Foreign Minister Welcomes Meeting Between Chinese and Indian Prime Ministers. Ceylonese Political Circles Believe Sino-Indian Prime Minister Meeting is Conducive to Asian Peace
Author:
Date: 1954-06-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 28th: According to a Reuters report, Nepalese Foreign Minister Regmi stated in Kathmandu on the 26th that he welcomed the meeting between Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China and Prime Minister Nehru of India.

He also expressed his hope that the talks between Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru would help Asia avoid the scourge of war and free it from colonial rule.

Xinhua News Agency, June 28th: According to the Indian News Service, citing the Indian Press Trust in Colombo, official and political figures in Ceylon believe that the meeting between Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru is a factor conducive to Asian peace.

A spokesman for the Ceylon Ministry of Foreign Affairs said that the Ceylon government hopes that the New Delhi talks will help the Geneva Conference resolve the Indochina issue.

The leader of the opposition in the Ceylon Parliament and chairman of the Ceylon Freedom Party, Bandaranaike, pointed out regarding the meeting between the Chinese and Indian Prime Ministers that China and India can currently do more to ensure peace in Asia. And Asian peace very likely means world peace. Bandaranaike said: If the New Delhi talks confirm that two countries with different political ideologies can engage in sincere cooperation in pursuit of common goals, then this will be one of the greatest achievements.

### 尼泊尔外交大臣欢迎中印两国总理的会谈  锡兰政界认为中印总理会谈有利亚洲和平

1954-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

     尼泊尔外交大臣欢迎中印两国总理的会谈
     锡兰政界认为中印总理会谈有利亚洲和平
    【新华社二十八日讯】据路透社报道，尼泊尔外交大臣雷格米二十六日在加德满都发表谈话，对中华人民共和国总理周恩来和印度总理尼赫鲁的会见表示欢迎。
    他并表示希望周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理的会谈将有助于使亚洲免除战祸，并使亚洲不受殖民统治。
    【新华社二十八日讯】据印度新闻处引印度报业托辣斯科伦坡消息，锡兰官方和政界人士认为周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理的会见是有利于亚洲和平的因素。
    锡兰外交部发言人说，锡兰政府希望新德里会谈将有助于日内瓦会议解决印度支那问题。
    锡兰国会反对党领袖、锡兰自由党主席班达拉纳克就中印两国总理举行会谈指出，目前，中国和印度能够为保证亚洲的和平做更多的工作。而亚洲和平很可能意味着世界和平。班达拉纳克说：如果新德里会谈证实两个具有不同政治思想的国家为了追求共同的目的而可能进行真诚的合作，那末这将是最伟大的成就之一。
