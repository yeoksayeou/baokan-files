Title: Premier Zhou Visits Agra, Receives Gifts and Flowers from Agra Citizens
Author:
Date: 1954-06-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 27th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Lakshmi N. Menon, Deputy Minister of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, and Yuan Zhongxian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, flew to Agra on the morning of the 27th at 6:00 AM to visit the city. Premier Zhou visited the famous ancient Indian architectural wonders, the Taj Mahal and the Tomb of I'timād-ud-Daulah.

In Agra, Premier Zhou reviewed the Air Force honor guard and accepted gifts and flowers from people of all circles in Agra.

Wherever Premier Zhou went, he was greeted by thousands of people lining the streets, cheering and throwing flowers at him.

Premier Zhou returned to New Delhi at 12:00 noon.

### 周总理到亚格拉游览  接受亚格拉市民献礼和献花

1954-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理到亚格拉游览
    接受亚格拉市民献礼和献花
    【新华社新德里二十七日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来在印度外交部驻国会次官拉克希米·纳·梅农和中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤陪同下，在二十七日上午六时乘飞机到亚格拉游览。周总理参观了印度著名的古代建筑泰吉·玛哈尔陵和伊蒂玛德—乌德—杜拉陵。
    周总理在亚格拉检阅了空军仪仗队，并接受了亚格拉各界市民的献礼和献花。
    周总理所到之处，沿途都有成千群众夹道欢迎，向周总理欢呼并投掷鲜花。
    周总理在中午十二时回到新德里。
