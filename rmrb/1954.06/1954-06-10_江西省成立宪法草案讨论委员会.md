Title: Jiangxi Province Establishes Constitution Draft Discussion Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-06-10
Page: 1
    Jiangxi Province Establishes Constitution Draft Discussion Committee
   [This newspaper's dispatch] Nanchang -- Jiangxi Province established the Constitution Draft Discussion Committee of the People's Republic of China on June 4. The committee's office began work on June 5. It was decided that after the publication of the Constitution Draft, widespread and in-depth publicity should be conducted among the urban and rural people, and they should be organized to conduct extensive discussions. Before the publication of the Constitution Draft, cadres of government organs, organizations, troops, universities, and middle schools will first be organized to read and discuss the documents, and trainers for promoting the Constitution Draft will be trained.

### 江西省成立宪法草案讨论委员会

1954-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    江西省成立宪法草案讨论委员会
   【本报讯】江西省已于六月四日成立中华人民共和国宪法草案讨论委员会，该会办公室并于六月五日开始工作。决定在宪法草案公布以后，普遍、深入地向城乡人民群众进行宣传，并组织他们广泛进行讨论。在宪法草案公布以前，将先组织机关、团体、部队和大、中学校的干部阅读和讨论文件，并训练宪法草案的宣传员。
