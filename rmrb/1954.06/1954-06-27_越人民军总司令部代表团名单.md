Title: List of Delegates from the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army

Author:
Date: 1954-06-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, June 26th: According to a communiqué from the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army, the delegation from the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army sent to Thai Nguyen Province and the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force in Indochina for the meeting on June 28th is led by Brigadier General Van Tien Dung. The other members of the delegation are: Senior Colonel Song Hao, Senior Colonel Le Quang Dao, Lieutenant Colonel Le Minh, and Lieutenant Colonel Nguyen Van Long.

### 越人民军总司令部代表团名单

1954-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    越人民军总司令部代表团名单
    【新华社二十六日讯】越南通讯社北越二十六日讯：据越南人民军总司令部公报，越南人民军总司令部派往太原省和印度支那法国远征军总司令部代表团在六月二十八日举行会议的代表团，由文进勇准将率领。代表团的其他成员是：双豪上校、黎光道上校、黎明中校、阮文龙中校。
