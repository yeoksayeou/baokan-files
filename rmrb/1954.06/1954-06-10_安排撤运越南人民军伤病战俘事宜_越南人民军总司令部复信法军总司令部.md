Title: Arrangements for the Evacuation of Wounded and Sick Prisoners of War of the Vietnam People's Army: Reply from the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army to the General Headquarters of the French Army

Author:

Date: 1954-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 8th: Vietnam News Agency, 8th: The General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army sent the following reply to the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force on June 7, 1954, regarding the evacuation of wounded and sick prisoners of war of the Vietnam People's Army:

    I. The General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army hereby replies to the letter of the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force dated June 2, 1954, and suggests the following locations as the handover points for wounded and sick prisoners of war of the Vietnam People's Army:

    North Vietnam: Da Ting Village on Highway No. 1 north of Phu Lang Thuong;

    Central Vietnam: Zhi'an Bridge at the intersection of Highway No. 1 and the Shouben River (southern tributary of the Hoi An River).

    II. The General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army has notified the Vietnam People's Army Command of the Viet Bac Inter-Zone and the Fifth Inter-Zone to ensure the reception of the relevant wounded and sick prisoners of war at the aforementioned locations.

    The General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army hereby requests that the General Headquarters of the French Expeditionary Force send two representatives to the aforementioned two locations respectively—to North Vietnam between 2:00 PM and 4:00 PM Vietnam Democratic Republic standard time on June 11, 1954, and to Central Vietnam at the same time on June 14, to meet with representatives of the Viet Bac Inter-Zone and Fifth Inter-Zone of the Vietnam People's Army Command representing the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army, in order to discuss specific arrangements for the proper evacuation of wounded and sick prisoners of war.

    On the day of the meeting, all military activities of both sides will cease from midnight to midnight within a ten-kilometer radius around Da Ting and Zhi'an.

    The representatives of both sides will not carry weapons into the designated locations and will be marked with the flag of the Red Cross.

### 安排撤运越南人民军伤病战俘事宜  越南人民军总司令部复信法军总司令部

1954-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    安排撤运越南人民军伤病战俘事宜
    越南人民军总司令部复信法军总司令部
   【新华社八日讯】越南通讯社八日讯：越南人民军总司令部一九五四年六月七日就撤运越南人民军伤、病战俘事给法国远征军总司令部的复信如下：
    一、越南人民军总司令部兹就法国远征军总司令部一九五四年六月二日的信作复，建议以下列地方作为交接越南人民军伤、病战俘的地点：
    北越：府谅商以北第一号公路上的大亭村；
    中越：第一号公路与守本河（会安河南部支流）交义处的知安桥。
    二、越南人民军总司令部已通知越北联区和第五联区的越南人民军司令部保证在上述地点接收有关的伤、病战俘。
    兹要求法国远征军总司令部派两名代表分别到上述两个地点——在一九五四年六月十一日越南民主共和国标准时间下午二至四时到北越，在一九五四年六月十四日同一时间到中越——会见代表越南人民军总司令部的越北联区和第五联区的越南人民军司令部代表，以便讨论妥善地撤运伤、病战俘的具体办法。
    在会见的这一天，从零时到午夜，在大亭和知安两地周围十公里地区内，双方的一切军事活动都将停止。
    双方代表将不携带武器进入指定的地点，并以红十字的旗帜为标志。
