Title: Seeking Opinions from the People on Issues Such as Concluding a Peace Treaty; The Government of the German Democratic Republic Regularly Holds People's Referendums

Author: 
Date: 1954-06-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 31st: According to a TASS report from Berlin on the 30th, democratic newspapers in Berlin published the decision of the government of the German Democratic Republic on holding a people's referendum on May 29th. The content of the referendum is: "Do you approve of a peace treaty and the withdrawal of foreign occupation forces? Or do you approve of the 'European Defense Community' treaty and the 'General Treaty' and allowing the occupation forces to remain for another fifty years?" This decision was made in accordance with the resolution of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic on May 26, 1954.

The decision stipulates that in the German Democratic Republic, the people's referendum will be held from June 27 to June 29, 1954. The voting will be direct and anonymous. Each voter has only one vote. Anyone who resides in the German Democratic Republic or in the democratic sector of Berlin, has not been deprived of the right to vote, and is sixteen years old by June 26, 1954, has the right to vote.

The people's referendum throughout the German Democratic Republic is led by the Minister of the Interior of the German Democratic Republic, and at all levels from special zones to townships by the heads of local administrations. According to the aforementioned decision, national and administrative regional people's referendum committees will be established to be responsible for voting matters.

### 向人民征求关于缔结和约等问题的意见  德意志民主共和国政府定期举行人民投票

1954-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    向人民征求关于缔结和约等问题的意见
    德意志民主共和国政府定期举行人民投票
    【新华社三十一日讯】据塔斯社柏林三十日讯：五月二十九日柏林民主报纸公布了德意志民主共和国政府关于举行人民投票的决定。人民投票的内容是：“你赞成和约和撤退外国占领军呢？还是赞成‘欧洲防务集团’条约和‘一般性条约’以及让占领军继续留驻五十年呢？”这项决定是根据德意志民主共和国人民议院一九五四年五月二十六日的决议作出的。
    决定中规定：在德意志民主共和国中，人民投票在一九五四年六月二十七日至二十九日举行。投票采取直接和不记名方式。每一投票人只有一次投票权。凡是在德意志民主共和国或在柏林民主区定居的、没有被褫夺选举权、到一九五四年六月二十六日满十六岁的人，都有投票权。
    德意志民主共和国全境的人民投票由德意志民主共和国内务部部长领导，在从专区到乡镇由各级行政首长领导。根据上述决定将建立全国的和各级行政区的人民投票委员会，负责投票事宜。
