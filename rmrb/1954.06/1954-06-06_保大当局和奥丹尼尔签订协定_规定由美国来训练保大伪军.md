Title: Bảo Đại Authorities and O'Daniel Sign Agreement Stipulating that the United States will Train Bảo Đại Puppet Troops
Author:
Date: 1954-06-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 1st (TASS dispatch): According to a United Press reporter from Hanoi: The Bảo Đại authorities and O'Daniel, head of the U.S. Military Assistance Advisory Group in Indochina, have signed an agreement. According to this agreement, two centers will be established in Vietnam to train the Bảo Đại army, for use by U.S. officers to train five new divisions.

An Associated Press reporter from Saigon reported: The American plan anticipates training five divisions by the end of this year and twelve divisions by the end of 1955.

### 保大当局和奥丹尼尔签订协定  规定由美国来训练保大伪军

1954-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    保大当局和奥丹尼尔签订协定
    规定由美国来训练保大伪军
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社纽约一日讯：据合众社记者自河内报道：保大当局和美国驻印度支那军事援助顾问团团长奥丹尼尔签订了一项协定。根据这项协定将在越南建立两个训练保大军队的中心，以供美国军官训练五个新的师之用。
    美联社记者自西贡报道：美国的计划预定到今年年底训练五个师，至一九五五年年底训练十二个师。
