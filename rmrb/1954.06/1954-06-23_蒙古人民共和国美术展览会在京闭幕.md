Title: Mongolian People's Republic Art Exhibition Closes in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-06-23
Page: 3

    The "Art Exhibition of the Mongolian People's Republic" closed in Beijing on the 20th. During the twenty-three days of the exhibition, more than 120,000 people visited. Visitors included artists, writers, cadres, students, workers, suburban farmers, herdsmen from the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, cadres and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, staff of the Chinese People's Volunteers returning from Korea, and foreign guests in Beijing.

    China's famous traditional painter Qi Baishi and many other well-known social figures also visited the exhibition.

    Among the exhibits, the oil paintings "Mao Zedong and Tsedenbal," "Choibalsan and Stalin Together," and "Choibalsan in Irkutsk" received widespread attention from the audience. Other oil paintings such as "Absenteeism" and "After Work," as well as the statues of Sukhbaatar and Choibalsan, and the sculpture "Untamed Horse" also aroused great interest and high praise from the general public.

    During the exhibition period, Liu Kaiqu, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, Wu Zuoren, and artists Li Qun, Hu Man, and Wang Qi, among others, published articles in newspapers and periodicals such as the "People's Daily," "Guangming Daily," "Beijing Daily," "Literary Gazette," and "New Observer" to introduce the exhibition.

    On June 3rd, the Chinese Artists Association invited Dorjiin Choidog, Art Director of the Fine Arts Bureau of the Arts Committee of the Mongolian People's Republic, Meritorious Artist, and recipient of the Choibalsan Prize, who was visiting China, to report on the visual arts of the Mongolian People's Republic. On June 4th, the Chinese Artists Association held a symposium to express their impressions and opinions on the exhibition. The five Mongolian artists who visited China also attended the symposium. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 蒙古人民共和国美术展览会在京闭幕

1954-06-23
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国美术展览会在京闭幕
    “蒙古人民共和国美术展览会”二十日在北京闭幕。在开放展览的二十三天中，有十二万多人参观了展览会。参观的人包括美术家、作家、机关干部、学生、工人、郊区农民、来自内蒙古自治区的牧民、中国人民解放军干部和战士、从朝鲜归来的中国人民志愿军工作人员以及在京的外宾们。
    我国著名国画家齐白石和很多社会著名人士也参观了展览会。
    在展览品中受到观众普遍注意的是“毛泽东和泽登巴尔”、“乔巴山和斯大林在一起”、“乔巴山在伊尔库次克”等油画。其他如“旷职”、“工作之后”等油画和苏赫—巴托尔、乔巴山的雕像及“未驯的马”雕塑也都引起了广大观众的兴趣和很高的评价。
    展览会展览期间，中国美术家协会副主席刘开渠、吴作人，美术家力群、胡蛮、王琦等人，曾分别在“人民日报”、“光明日报”、“北京日报”、“文艺报”和“新观察”等报刊上著文介绍。
    中国美术家协会曾在六月三日邀请正在我国访问的蒙古人民共和国艺术委员会美术局美术指导、功勋美术家、乔巴山奖金获得者多吉因·却多克报告蒙古人民共和国的造型艺术情况。六月四日，中国美术家协会又举行了座谈会，对展览会发表观感和意见。来我国访问的五位蒙古美术家也出席了这个座谈会。（新华社）
