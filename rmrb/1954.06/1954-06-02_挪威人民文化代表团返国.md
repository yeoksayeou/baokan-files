Title: Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-06-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 31st: The Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation, led by Professor Jessing, which was invited to visit our country, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 31st to return home. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Zhou Gengsheng, Acting President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Chen Hansheng, Vice President, Hongshen, Vice President of Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and representatives from various people's organizations, as well as cultural and artistic figures such as Zheng Zhenduo, Hua Junwu, Liao Mengxing, Zhang Jin, Yan Renying, and Yuan Yongxi.
    In addition, the deputy head of the delegation, Pastor Føbech, and five others had already left Beijing on the 29th to return home.

### 挪威人民文化代表团返国

1954-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威人民文化代表团返国
    【新华社三十一日讯】应邀前来我国访问的挪威人民文化代表团团长耶辛教授等七人在三十一日上午乘飞机离开北京返国。前往机场欢送的有中国人民外交学会代会长周鲠生、副会长陈翰笙，中国人民对外文化协会副会长洪深及各人民群众团体代表，文化、艺术界人士郑振铎、华君武、廖梦醒、张进、严仁瑛、袁永熙等人。
    此外，代表团副团长福贝克牧师等五人已在二十九日先行离京返国。
