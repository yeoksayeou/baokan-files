Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Speech

Author: 
Date: 1954-06-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 10th. Moscow's major newspapers all published the full text of Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's speech on the Indochina issue at the Geneva Conference on the 9th.
