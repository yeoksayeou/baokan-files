    Our Great Motherland
    Huangshi City, Hubei Province, is a newly emerging industrial city, surrounded by extremely rich iron, coal, and other industrial raw materials. There are steel plants, power plants, and coal mines here. This is the exterior view of the iron smelting workshop of Daye Steel Plant.

    我们伟大的祖国
