Title: Japan Federation of Newspaper Workers' Unions Passes Resolution Demanding Resignation of Yoshida Government
Author:
Date: 1954-06-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 22nd: The Japan Federation of Newspaper Workers' Unions, with over 18,000 members, passed a resolution at its national convention held in Tokyo from the 19th to the 20th, demanding the immediate resignation of the Yoshida government and new general elections. The meeting also decided to join the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan.

### 日本新闻工会联合会通过决议  要求吉田政府辞职

1954-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    日本新闻工会联合会通过决议
    要求吉田政府辞职
    【新华社二十二日讯】东京消息：拥有一万八千多名会员的日本新闻工会联合会于十九至二十日在东京举行的全国大会上通过决议，要求吉田政府立即辞职、重新举行大选。会议并决定参加日本工会总评议会。
