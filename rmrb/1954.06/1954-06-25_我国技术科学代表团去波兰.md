Title: Chinese Technical Science Delegation to Poland
Author:
Date: 1954-06-25
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: In order to develop cooperation between the two countries in technology and technical science, the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the People's Republic of Poland decided to sign the Sino-Polish Technical and Technical Science Cooperation Agreement. The delegation of the People's Republic of China for Technical and Technical Science, led by Wang Shoudao, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government, consisting of twenty-three people, departed from Beijing on the 22nd. Kiryluk, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Poland to China, and Goldzef, Commercial Counselor, and others went to the airport to see them off.

### 我国技术科学代表团去波兰

1954-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    我国技术科学代表团去波兰
    【新华社二十四日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府和波兰人民共和国政府为了发展两国间在技术与技术科学方面的合作，决定签订中波技术与技术科学合作协定。由中央人民政府交通部副部长王首道率领的中华人民共和国技术与技术科学代表团一行二十三人已在二十二日自北京出发。波兰人民共和国驻华大使基里洛克及商务参赞高尔德格若伯等曾前往机场送行。
