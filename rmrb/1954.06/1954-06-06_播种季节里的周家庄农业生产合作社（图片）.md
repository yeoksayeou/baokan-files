Title: Zhoujiazhuang Agricultural Production Cooperative in the Sowing Season (Pictures)
Author: Ke Zhang, Yuan Hao
Date: 1954-06-06
Page: 2

    Zhoujiazhuang Agricultural Production Cooperative in the Sowing Season
    Hebei Daily, photos by Ke Zhang, Yuan Hao
    After the cotton seedlings emerged completely, the cooperative committee conducted an evaluation throughout the cooperative based on the situation of intensive sowing. This is the evaluation committee inspecting the situation of high-yield cotton.
    The visiting group from the Chemical Industry Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Heavy Industry visited the Zhoujiazhuang Agricultural Production Cooperative in April and presented 200 jin of "666" insecticide as a gift. This is the scene of the medicine being transported to Zhoujiazhuang.
    Weeds grew in the cotton seedling fields, and the cooperative mobilized members of each team to weed and prevent aphids. This is the scene of the women of the Fourth Team removing weeds in the fields.
    After signing a contract with the state-owned Hebei Province Second Agricultural Machine and Tractor Station, the Zhoujiazhuang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Jin County, Hebei Province, began to use machines to sow cotton.

### 播种季节里的周家庄农业生产合作社（图片）
克章  袁浩
1954-06-06
第2版()
专栏：

    播种季节里的周家庄农业生产合作社
    河北日报　克章　袁浩摄
    棉苗出齐后，社务委员会根据全社突击播种的情况，在全社进行了评比。这是评比委员会检查丰产棉的情形。
    重工业部化学工业管理局的访问组，在四月间访问了周家庄农业生产合作社，并赠送了二百斤“六六六”杀虫药剂作为礼品。这是药品运到周家庄的情形。
    棉苗地上长出了杂草，社里发动了各队社员除草防蚜。这是四队的妇女们在田间拔除杂草的情形。
    河北省晋县周家庄农业生产合作社和国营河北省第二农业机器拖拉机站签订合同后，开始使用机器播种棉花。
