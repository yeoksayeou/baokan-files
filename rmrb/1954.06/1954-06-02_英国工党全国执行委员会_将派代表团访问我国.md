Title: British Labour Party National Executive Committee to Send Delegation to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1954-06-02
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, London, May 31st: TASS, London, May 26th—The British Labour Party National Executive Committee accepted on May 26th the invitation from the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to send a delegation from the British Labour Party to visit the People's Republic of China.
　　The Labour Party National Executive Committee has decided to send an eight-member delegation to the People's Republic of China in August or September of this year. This delegation will include Labour Party leader Clement Attlee, Aneurin Bevan, Labour Party National Executive Committee Chairman Wu Burke, National Executive Committee member and Amalgamated Engineering Union representative G. Earnshaw, National Executive Committee member and National Union of Railwaymen representative G. Franklin, National Executive Committee member and former Labour government minister Edith Summerskill (female), National Executive Committee member and National Union of Mineworkers representative Sai Watson, and Labour Party General Secretary Morgan Phillips.

### 英国工党全国执行委员会  将派代表团访问我国

1954-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    英国工党全国执行委员会
    将派代表团访问我国
    【新华社三十一日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十六日讯：英国工党全国执行委员会五月二十六日接受了中国人民外交学会邀请英国工党派遣一个代表团到中华人民共和国去访问的请柬。
    工党全国执行委员会已决定在今年八月或九月派遣一个八人代表团到中华人民共和国去。这个代表团将包括工党领袖克莱门特·艾德礼、安奈林·比万、工党全国执行委员会主席吴·伯克、全国执行委员会委员、混合机器工会代表格·厄恩萧、全国执行委员会委员、英国全国铁路工人工会代表格·弗兰克林、全国执行委员会委员、前工党政府大臣埃迪思·萨末斯基尔（女）、全国执行委员会委员、英国全国矿工联盟代表赛·华生和工党总书记摩根·菲利普斯。
