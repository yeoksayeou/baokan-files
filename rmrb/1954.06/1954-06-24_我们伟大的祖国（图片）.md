    Our Great Motherland
    The Chengyang Bridge over the Linxi River in the Sanjiang Dong Autonomous Region of Guangxi. The bridge has beautiful pavilions and corridors, which are characteristic of Dong bridges. Now boats transporting timber, grain and other materials often travel on the river.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    广西三江侗族自治区林溪河上的程阳桥。桥上有美丽的小亭和走廊，这是侗族桥梁的特色。现在河上经常有运输木材、粮食及其他物资的船只来往。
