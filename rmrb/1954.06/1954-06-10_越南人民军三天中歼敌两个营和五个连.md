Title: Vietnamese People's Army Annihilates Two Enemy Battalions and Five Companies in Three Days
Author:
Date: 1954-06-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 9th: According to Vietnam News Agency on June 8th: In three days, the Vietnamese People's Army annihilated a total of two enemy battalions, five companies, and an additional one hundred enemy soldiers in the provinces of Nam Dinh and Ninh Binh in North Vietnam, and Phu Yen province in Central Vietnam.

　　On June 3rd, the Vietnamese People's Army launched an attack on the enemy stronghold of Dong Bien (i.e., Quan Phuong Ha) in Hai Hau County, Nam Dinh province, annihilating the puppet army's 702nd Light Infantry Battalion, four local puppet army companies, and one company of reinforcing enemy troops, seizing all enemy weapons. The next day, the People's Army intercepted enemy troops on Highway 10, five kilometers northeast of Ninh Binh, killing one hundred enemy soldiers and destroying two enemy tanks.

　　On June 1st, the People's Army annihilated the puppet army's 504th Light Infantry Battalion in Tuy Phong, Phu Yen province, in the Fifth Interzone of Central Vietnam, seizing ten enemy motorcycles and a large number of weapons and ammunition.

### 越南人民军三天中歼敌两个营和五个连

1954-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民军三天中歼敌两个营和五个连
    【新华社九日讯】据越南通讯社八日讯：越南人民军三天中在北越南定、宁平两省和中越富安省共歼灭敌军两个营、五个连又一百人。
    六月三日，越南人民军向南定省海后县敌军据点东边（即关方瑕）发动进攻，歼灭伪军第七零二轻装营、四个地方伪军连和一连增援的敌军，缴获敌军全部武器。第二天，人民军在宁平东北五公里的第十号公路上截击敌军，歼灭敌军一百名，并烧毁敌军坦克二辆。
    六月一日，人民军在中越第五联区富安省绥平歼灭了伪军第五零四轻装营，缴获敌军摩托车辆十辆和大量的武器、弹药。
