Author:
Date: 1954-06-10
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Shaanxi Province, one of China's major wheat-producing areas, has begun harvesting wheat. According to field forecasts in various places, the total output of winter wheat in the province will increase by 5.82 percent compared with last year, and the average yield per unit area will increase by 3.16 percent compared with last year. This is a demonstration and propagation farm in Weinan County, Shaanxi Province, using horse-drawn harvesters to harvest wheat.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国主要产麦区之一的陕西省，已经开始收割小麦。根据各地田间预测的结果，全省冬小麦总产量将比去年增产百分之五点八二，单位面积产量平均将比去年增产百分之三点一六。这是陕西省渭南县示范繁殖农场用马拉收割机收割小麦。
