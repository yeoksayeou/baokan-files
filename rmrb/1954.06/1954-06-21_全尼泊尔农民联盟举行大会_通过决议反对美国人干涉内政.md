Title: All Nepal Peasants' Association Holds Congress; Passes Resolution Against American Interference in Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1954-06-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 19th: The Second Congress of the All Nepal Peasants' Association was held in Janakpur, Mahottari, from June 1st to 4th. One hundred and twenty-eight delegates from all over Nepal and one hundred and fifty observers attended the congress.

    The congress passed several resolutions expressing strong opposition to foreign, particularly American, interference in Nepal's internal affairs; demanding the cessation of British troop recruitment in Nepal; and demanding the abolition of feudal exploitation.

    Peasant leaders speaking at the meeting expressed their desire for world peace. The congress also elected a new Central Council and Secretariat.

### 全尼泊尔农民联盟举行大会  通过决议反对美国人干涉内政

1954-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    全尼泊尔农民联盟举行大会
    通过决议反对美国人干涉内政
    【新华社新德里十九日电】全尼泊尔农民联盟第二次大会于六月一日到四日在马霍塔里的贾纳克普尔举行。参加大会的有尼泊尔各地代表一百二十八人和观察员一百五十人。
    大会通过了几项决议，表示强烈反对外国人、特别是美国人干涉尼泊尔的内政；要求停止让英国军队在尼泊尔募兵；要求废除封建剥削。
    在会上发言的农民领袖表示了对世界和平的愿望。大会并选出了新的中央理事会和书记处。
