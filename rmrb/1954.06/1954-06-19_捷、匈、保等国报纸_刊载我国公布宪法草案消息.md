Title: Czechoslovak, Hungarian, Bulgarian Newspapers Publish News of China's Promulgation of Draft Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-06-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 18th. Czechoslovak "Red Rights Newspaper" and other major newspapers, Hungarian "Free People's Newspaper" and Bulgarian "Workers' Cause Newspaper," etc., all published on the 17th the news of the Central People's Government Committee passing the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China and the content of the draft Constitution.

### 捷、匈、保等国报纸  刊载我国公布宪法草案消息

1954-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    捷、匈、保等国报纸
    刊载我国公布宪法草案消息
    【新华社布拉格十八日电】捷克斯洛伐克的“红色权利报”和其他大报，匈牙利的“自由人民报”和保加利亚的“工人事业报”等，在十七日都刊载了我中央人民政府委员会通过中华人民共和国宪法草案的消息和宪法草案的内容。
