Title: Zhou Enlai Meets with British Parliamentarians
Author:
Date: 1954-06-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 30th. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, received Wilson, a British Labour Party Member of Parliament and former Minister of Trade, and Robertson-Brown, a British Conservative Party Member of Parliament, at 11:30 a.m. on the 30th. Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, was also present.

### 周恩来外长接见英国议员

1954-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长接见英国议员
    【新华社日内瓦三十日电】中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来在三十日上午十一时三十分接见了英国工党议员、前贸易大臣威尔逊和英国保守党议员罗布逊—布朗。在座的还有中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长雷任民。
