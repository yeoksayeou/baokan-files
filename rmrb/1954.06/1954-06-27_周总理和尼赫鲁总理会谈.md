Title: Premier Zhou and Prime Minister Nehru Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1954-06-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 25th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, held talks with Indian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nehru from 3:30 PM to 6:15 PM on the afternoon of the 25th.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 25th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, was invited to dinner with Indian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nehru at 8:45 PM on the 25th. After dinner, Premier Zhou Enlai continued talks with Prime Minister Nehru.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 26th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Indian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nehru continued talks from 10:30 AM to 12:15 PM on the morning of the 26th.

### 周总理和尼赫鲁总理会谈

1954-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理和尼赫鲁总理会谈
    【新华社新德里二十五日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在二十五日下午三时三十分至六时十五分和印度总理兼外交部部长尼赫鲁会谈。
    【新华社新德里二十五日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在二十五日晚八时四十五分应邀和印度总理兼外交部部长尼赫鲁共进晚餐。晚餐后，周恩来总理同尼赫鲁总理继续会谈。
    【新华社新德里二十六日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来和印度总理兼外交部部长尼赫鲁在二十六日上午十时三十分至十二时十五分继续举行会谈。
