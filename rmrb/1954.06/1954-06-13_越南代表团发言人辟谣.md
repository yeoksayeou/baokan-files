Title: Spokesperson of the Vietnamese Delegation Denies Rumor
Author:
Date: 1954-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 11th: Huang Wen Huan, spokesperson of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, issued the following statement on the 11th:

	Newspaper reporters have consulted me on a message from Paris stating that the delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has reached an agreement with the French delegation stipulating that the cities of Saigon, Hanoi, and Haiphong will be included in the areas reassigned for the regrouping of the French Expeditionary Force.

	I am authorized to formally deny this absolutely unfounded news.

### 越南代表团发言人辟谣

1954-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    越南代表团发言人辟谣
    【新华社日内瓦十一日电】越南民主共和国代表团发言人黄文欢在十一日发表一项声明如下：
    各报记者们曾以一项来自巴黎的消息征询我的意见，这项消息说越南民主共和国政府代表团已和法国代表团达成一项协议，规定西贡、河内、海防各城市将包括在划归法国远征军重新集结的地区之内。
    我受权正式否认这项绝对没有根据的消息。
