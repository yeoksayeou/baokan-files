Author:
Date: 1954-06-24
Page: 2
Fujian Province's Sugar Cane Sugar is Shipped Out Continuously

　　Fujian Province's sugar cane sugar is being shipped out continuously. By June 17th, the state-owned commercial and cooperative departments of Fujian Province had shipped approximately 480,000 dan of sugar cane sugar to cities such as Shanghai, Nanjing, Jinan, and Qiqihar, which is 468% of the same period last year.

　　This year, the sugar cane sugar production in the main sugar-producing areas of Fujian Province, such as Putian and Xianyou counties, has increased compared to last year. The total sugar cane sugar production in the province has reached approximately 1,160,000 dan, of which more than 60% will be gradually shipped out to support the supply of major cities and industrial and mining areas. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 福建省出产的蔗糖源源外运

1954-06-24
第2版()
专栏：

   福建省出产的蔗糖源源外运
    福建省出产的蔗糖正源源外运。到六月十七日止，福建省国营商业和合作社部门先后运往上海、南京、济南和齐齐哈尔等城市的蔗糖，已达四十八万担左右，为去年同期的百分之四百六十八。
    今年福建省主要产糖区莆田、仙游等县的蔗糖产量都比去年增加，全省蔗糖总产量达一百一十六万担左右，其中百分之六十以上将陆续外运，支持各大城市和工矿区的供应。（新华社）
