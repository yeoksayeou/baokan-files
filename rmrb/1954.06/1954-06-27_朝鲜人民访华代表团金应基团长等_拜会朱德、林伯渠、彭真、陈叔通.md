Title: Korean People's Visiting Delegation Leader Kim Ung-gi and Others Call on Zhu De, Lin Boqu, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong
Author:
Date: 1954-06-27
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: The leader of the Korean People's Visiting Delegation, Kim Ung-gi, and the deputy leader, Lee Young-ho, paid a visit to Zhu De, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, at 10:00 AM on the 26th, on the eve of their return to their country from Beijing. Zhu De had a cordial conversation with Kim Ung-gi and others. Those accompanying the meeting were: Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and Chen Yi, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission.
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: Kim Ung-gi, leader of the Korean People's Visiting Delegation, and Kim Dal-hyun, Lee Young-ho, and Lee Dong-young, deputy leaders, visited Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government, at Qin Zheng Hall at 11:00 AM on the 26th. Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, was present. Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and Chen Yi, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission, accompanied the meeting.
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: Kim Ung-gi, leader of the Korean People's Visiting Delegation, and Kim Dal-hyun, Lee Young-ho, and Lee Dong-young, deputy leaders, paid a visit to Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, at 3:00 PM on the 26th. Luo Longji, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, and Wu Maosun were present at the visit. Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, accompanied the visit.
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: Kim Ung-gi, leader of the Korean People's Visiting Delegation, and Kim Dal-hyun, Lee Young-ho, and Lee Dong-young, deputy leaders, paid a visit to Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, at 4:00 PM on the 26th. Xing Xiping, Yi Lirong, and Xin Zhichao were present. Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, accompanied the visit.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团金应基团长等  拜会朱德、林伯渠、彭真、陈叔通

1954-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团金应基团长等
    拜会朱德、林伯渠、彭真、陈叔通
    【新华社二十六日讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基、副团长李永镐，在离京返国的前夕——二十六日上午十时拜会了中共中央书记处书记朱德。朱德和金应基等作了亲切的谈话。陪同进见的有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂。
    【新华社二十六日讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基、副团长金达铉、李永镐、李东英二十六日上午十一时，前往勤政殿拜会了中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠。在座的有中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂陪同进见。
    【新华社二十六日讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基、副团长金达铉、李永镐、李东英，在二十六日下午三时拜会了中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席彭真。拜会时在座的有：罗隆基、许德珩、许宝驹、吴茂荪。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一陪同拜会。
    【新华社二十六日讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基、副团长金达铉、李永镐、李东英，在二十六日下午四时拜会了中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通。在座的有：邢西萍、易礼容、辛志超。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一陪同拜会。
