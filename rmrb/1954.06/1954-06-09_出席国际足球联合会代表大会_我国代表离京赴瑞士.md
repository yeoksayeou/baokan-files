Title: Representatives of Our Country Leave Beijing for Switzerland to Attend the FIFA Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-06-09
Page: 4
    To Attend the FIFA Congress
    Our Country's Representatives Leave Beijing for Switzerland
    [This newspaper dispatch] Li Menghua, Zhang Lianhua, representatives of the All-China Sports Federation, and four others including translators, have left by plane for Switzerland on the 8th to attend the 29th Congress of the Fédération Internationale de Football Association and the celebration of the 50th anniversary of the FIFA, which will be held in Bern, Switzerland in late June.

### 出席国际足球联合会代表大会  我国代表离京赴瑞士

1954-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    出席国际足球联合会代表大会
    我国代表离京赴瑞士
    【本报讯】出席参加六月下旬在瑞士伯尔尼举行的国际足球联合会第二十九届代表大会和国际足球联合会五十周年庆祝大会的中华全国体育总会代表李梦华、张联华和翻译等四人，已于八日乘飞机前往瑞士。
