Title: Chinese Technology and Technical Science Delegation Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1954-06-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 27th: A Chinese Technology and Technical Science Delegation of twenty-three people, led by Wang Shoudao, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government of China, arrived in Warsaw by plane from Moscow on the 25th. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Polish Minister of Power Industry, Jaszczuk; Vice Minister of Shipping, Bukowski; Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Goreński; Vice Minister of Metallurgical Industry, Pierzyńca; Vice Minister of Land and Air Transport, Bulkin; Vice Minister of Light Industry, Kaźman; and representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Machine Building Industry, and the State Economic Planning Commission. Yang Qiliang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Poland, and Wang Runsheng, Commercial Counsellor, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Besides negotiating and signing the Sino-Polish Agreement on Technical and Technical Science Cooperation and holding the first joint committee meeting of the agreement, the delegation will also conduct visits in Poland.

### 我国技术与技术科学代表团到华沙

1954-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    我国技术与技术科学代表团到华沙
    【新华社华沙二十七日电】由我中央人民政府交通部副部长王首道率领的中华人民共和国技术与技术科学代表团一行二十三人二十五日由莫斯科坐飞机到了华沙。前往飞机场欢迎的有波兰动力工业部部长雅希楚克、航运部副部长布考夫斯基、对外贸易部副部长高伦斯基、冶金工业部副部长彼尔任卡、陆空运输部副部长布尔金、轻工业部副部长卡斯曼以及外交部、机器制造工业部和国家经济计划委员会的代表等多人。我国驻波兰大使馆临时代办杨琪良和商务参赞王润生也到机场欢迎。
    代表团来波兰除了谈判和签订中波技术与技术科学合作协定、举行该协定的第一届联合委员会外，并在波兰进行参观访问等。
