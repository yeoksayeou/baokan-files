Title: Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-06-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 17th: The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, Peterko, was recalled and left Beijing for home on the 17th. Those who went to the station to see him off included Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China and all members of the Embassy of the People's Republic of Bulgaria in China also went to see him off.

### 保加利亚大使彼得科夫返国

1954-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚大使彼得科夫返国
    【新华社十七日讯】保加利亚人民共和国首任驻华特命全权大使彼得科夫奉调在十七日离京返国。前往车站送行的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权、外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、外交部交际处副处长王拓。
    各国驻华使节和保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使馆全体馆员也前往送行。
