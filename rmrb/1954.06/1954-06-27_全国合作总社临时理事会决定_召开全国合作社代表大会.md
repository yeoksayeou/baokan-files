Title: National Cooperative Federation Temporary Council Decides to Convene National Cooperative Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-06-27
Page: 1

    National Cooperative Federation Temporary Council Decides to Convene National Cooperative Congress
    [Report from this newspaper] The Temporary Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives has decided to convene the First National Congress of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives in Beijing on July 20 this year. The congress will, in accordance with the general task of the state during the transition period, examine and summarize the work of supply and marketing and consumer cooperatives in recent years, determine the future direction and tasks, adopt the articles of association, elect members of the National Cooperative Committee, directors of the Council, and supervisors of the Supervisory Committee, and formally establish the central leading organs and supervisory organs of the national cooperatives. It also elected Cheng Zihua and ten others to form a preparatory committee for the congress.

### 全国合作总社临时理事会决定  召开全国合作社代表大会

1954-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    全国合作总社临时理事会决定
    召开全国合作社代表大会
    【本报讯】中华全国合作社联合总社临时理事会决定于今年七月二十日在北京召开中华全国合作社第一次代表大会。代表大会将根据国家在过渡时期的总任务，检查和总结几年来供销和消费合作社的工作，确定今后的方针任务，通过社章，选举全国合作社委员会委员、理事会理事、监事会监事，正式成立全国合作社的中央领导机关和监察机关。还推选程子华等十一人，组成代表大会筹备委员会。
