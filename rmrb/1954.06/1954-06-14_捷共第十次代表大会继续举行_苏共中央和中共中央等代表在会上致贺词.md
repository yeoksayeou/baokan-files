Title: The 10th Congress of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Continues; Representatives of the CPSU Central Committee, CPC Central Committee, and Others Deliver Congratulatory Messages at the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-06-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 13th: The morning session on the twelfth of the 10th Congress of the Czechoslovak Communist Party continued to discuss the summary report of the work of the Party Central Committee and the tasks of the Party in the future, as well as the report of the Central Inspection Committee.

    Representatives from the Party organizations of Olomouc Region, Brno Region, Prešov Region, and Gottwaldov Region spoke during the discussion. In their speeches, they talked about the achievements in national economic construction, the improvement of the material welfare of the working people, and the development of the agricultural cooperation movement. They also put forward opinions on the ideological work of the Party organizations and focused on the efforts of Party members to build a new socialist countryside. Gottwaldov Region representative Červinka pointed out that the draft directives of the 10th Party Congress on the short-term plan to greatly increase agricultural production in the next two or three years has been warmly supported by members of unified agricultural cooperatives, workers of state farms, and individual farmers in Gottwaldov Region. In the coming years, Gottwaldov Region plans to reclaim wasteland, plow barren grassland, and expand the cultivated area by 8,000 hectares (15 mu per hectare).

    Chairman of the Czechoslovak Socialist Party, Fierlinger, and Chairman of the Czechoslovak People's Party, Plojhar, delivered congratulatory messages to the congress. They both emphasized the leading role of the Communist Party in the National Front.

    The head of the Soviet Communist Party delegation, First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee Khrushchev, delivered a speech at the meeting. He first expressed his heartfelt congratulations to the Congress and the Czechoslovak Communist Party and conveyed the friendship of the Soviet people to the Czechoslovak people. He then spoke about the struggle of the Czechoslovak Communist Party in leading the people to fight for freedom, national independence, and socialism, as well as their great achievements in all aspects of socialist construction. Khrushchev also spoke in detail about the traditional friendship between the Soviet and Czechoslovak peoples and analyzed the current international situation and the struggle to defend world peace. Finally, he read out the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party to the Congress. After the congratulatory message was read, the venue erupted in stormy applause and cheers in tribute to the Soviet Union and the Soviet Communist Party. Then, all the delegates sang "The Internationale."

    Liu Changsheng, the representative of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, delivered a speech at the meeting. The delegates responded with stormy applause and shouted "Long Live People's China!" Liu Changsheng read out the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party to the Congress.

    At the morning session on the twelfth, congratulatory messages were also delivered by Ochab, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, and Wandell, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. They were all warmly welcomed by the attendees.

### 捷共第十次代表大会继续举行  苏共中央和中共中央等代表在会上致贺词

1954-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共第十次代表大会继续举行
    苏共中央和中共中央等代表在会上致贺词
   【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克共产党第十次代表大会十二日上午会议，继续讨论党中央委员会工作总结和今后党的任务的报告以及中央检查委员会的报告。
    在讨论中发言的有阿罗木次州、布尔诺州、普累索夫州、哥特瓦尔德夫州的党组织代表。他们在发言中谈到了国民经济建设的成就、劳动人民物质福利的提高以及农业合作化运动的开展情况。他们还对党组织的思想工作提出了意见，并着重谈到了党员们为建设社会主义新农村而努力的情形。哥特瓦尔德夫州的代表契尔文卡指出，第十次党代表大会关于最近两三年内大大提高农业生产的短期计划的指示草案，在哥特瓦尔德夫州的统一农业合作社社员、国营农场工作者和个体农民中得到了热烈的拥护。在今后几年内，哥特瓦尔德夫州计划要开垦荒地，翻耕贫瘠的草地，扩大耕地面积八千公顷
  （每公顷合十五市亩）。
    捷克斯洛伐克社会党主席斯列赫塔和捷克斯洛伐克人民党主席普洛伊哈向大会致贺词。他们都着重指出了共产党在民族阵线中的领导作用。
    苏联共产党代表团团长、苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫在会上发表演说。他首先向代表大会和捷克斯洛伐克共产党表示衷心的祝贺，并表达了苏联人民对捷克斯洛伐克人民的友谊。他接着谈到了捷克斯洛伐克共产党领导人民争取自由、民族独立和社会主义斗争的情况，以及他们在社会主义建设各方面的巨大成就。赫鲁晓夫还详细地谈到了苏捷两国人民的传统友谊，并分析了目前的国际形势和保卫世界和平的斗争。他最后宣读了苏联共产党中央委员会给代表大会的贺词。贺词宣读毕，会场上响起了暴风雨般的掌声和向苏联及苏联共产党致敬的欢呼声。接着，全体代表高唱“国际歌”。
    中国共产党中央委员会的代表刘长胜在会上致词，大会代表报以暴风雨般的掌声，并高呼“人民中国万岁！”刘长胜向大会宣读了中国共产党中央委员会的贺词。
    在十二日上午会议上，致贺词的还有：波兰统一工人党中央委员会书记奥哈布、德国统一社会党中央委员会书记汪戴尔。他们都受到到会者的热烈欢迎。
