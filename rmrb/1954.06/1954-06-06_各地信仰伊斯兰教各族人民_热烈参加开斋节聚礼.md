Title: People of Various Ethnic Groups Who Believe in Islam Throughout the Country Warmly Participate in Eid al-Fitr Prayers
Author:
Date: 1954-06-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: Urumqi, Lanzhou, Linxia, Hohhot and other places, people of the Hui, Uygur, Kazakh, Uzbek, Kirgiz, Tatar, Tajik, Dongxiang, Salar, Baoan and other ten ethnic groups who believe in Islam celebrated "Eid al-Fitr". On the morning of the 3rd, people of all ethnic groups in Urumqi went to 56 mosques throughout the city to participate in "Juli" (congregational prayer). Every household prepared delicious food to entertain guests who came to celebrate the festival. Leaders of Xinjiang Province and Urumqi City visited Bao Erhan, Director of the China Islamic Association, and Saifuddin, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, at their homes to celebrate the festival. People of the Hui, Dongxiang, Baoan, Salar and other ethnic groups in Linxia City, Gansu Province, also went to various mosques and the "Juli Square" on the banks of the Daxia River to participate in "Juli". In order to enable the Hui people in Hohhot to have a good festival, the People's Government of Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region instructed the state-owned grain departments and food companies to supply the Hui people with festival food.

### 各地信仰伊斯兰教各族人民  热烈参加开斋节聚礼

1954-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    各地信仰伊斯兰教各族人民
    热烈参加开斋节聚礼
    据新华社讯：乌鲁木齐、兰州、临夏、呼和浩特等地信仰伊斯兰教的回、维吾尔、哈萨克、乌兹别克、柯尔克兹、塔塔尔、塔吉克、东乡、撒拉、保安等十个民族的人民欢度“开斋节”。三日清晨，乌鲁木齐各族人民分别到全市五十六个寺坊参加“聚礼”。家家户户都设备了鲜美的食品，招待拜节的客人。新疆省和乌鲁木齐市领导机关的负责人，到中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉和新疆省人民政府副主席赛福鼎家中拜节。甘肃省临夏市的回、东乡、保安、撒拉等族人民，也分别到各清真寺和大夏河畔“聚礼场”参加“聚礼”。内蒙古自治区各级人民政府为使呼和浩特市的回族人民过好节日，曾指示国营粮食部门和食品公司给回族人民供应过节食品。
