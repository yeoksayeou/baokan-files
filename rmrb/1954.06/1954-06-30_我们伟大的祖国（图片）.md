Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-06-30

    Our Great Motherland
    The Lingwu State-operated Mechanical Farm in Ningxia Province has sown over 13,000 mu of paddy rice according to this year's plan. This is a scene of farm workers using tractors to pull three planters to carry out dry planting of paddy rice, planting 62 mu per hour.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    宁夏省国营灵武机械农场按照今年的计划播种了水稻一万三千多亩。这是农场工人用拖拉机牵引三台播种机进行水稻旱植播种，每小时播种六十二亩。
