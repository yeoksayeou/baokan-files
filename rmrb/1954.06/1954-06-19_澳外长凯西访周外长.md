Title: Australian Foreign Minister Casey Visits Foreign Minister Zhou

Author:

Date: 1954-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 18th. Australian Foreign Minister Casey called on Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, at 12:00 noon on the 18th. Ko Po-nien was also present on the Chinese side.

### 澳外长凯西访周外长

1954-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    澳外长凯西访周外长
    【新华社日内瓦十八日电】澳大利亚外交部部长凯西十八日中午十二时拜访中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来。中国方面在座的还有柯柏年。
