Title: All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions Plenary Session Elects Leading Bodies
Author:
Date: 1954-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 18th: According to TASS, the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, newly elected by the Eleventh All-Union Congress of Trade Unions, held its first plenary session on the 17th.

    The meeting elected the Presidium of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions. The following were elected to the Presidium: Aslanov, Babayan, Goroshkin, Gulyayev, Gryazev, Yefanov, Yeremeyev, Egurazdov, Ilyina, Krestyaninov, Korobova, Moskarets, Novikov, Nefedov, Popova, Pshenitsyn, Rosochinsky, Solovyov, Cherednichenko, Shvernik, and Shevchenko.
    The plenary session elected Shvernik as Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, Solovyov as Vice-Chairman, and Goroshkin, Gulyayev, Korobova, Popova, and Shevchenko as Secretaries of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions.
    The All-Union Central Auditing Commission, newly elected by the Eleventh All-Union Congress of Trade Unions, also held a meeting. The meeting elected Kuvshinov as Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission.
    Furthermore: The All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions held a reception on the 17th to receive the delegation of the World Federation of Trade Unions and the trade union delegations from various countries who came to participate in the Eleventh All-Union Congress of Trade Unions. Shvernik, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, delivered a welcome speech at the meeting. He expressed the hope that the representatives of trade union organizations from various countries would achieve new successes in the struggle for the unity of the working class, for strengthening and expanding international ties, and for peace and friendship among the peoples of all countries.

### 全苏工会中央理事会全会选出领导机构

1954-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    全苏工会中央理事会全会选出领导机构
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：第十一届全苏工会代表大会新选出的全苏工会中央理事会，在十七日举行了第一次全体会议。
    会议选出了全苏工会中央理事会主席团。被选入主席团的有：阿斯兰诺夫、巴巴扬、戈罗希金、古列耶夫、格梁泽夫、叶法诺夫、耶里米耶夫、埃古拉兹多夫、伊林娜、克雷斯特雅尼诺夫、科罗鲍娃、莫斯卡列兹、诺维科夫、涅费多夫、波波娃、普歇尼津、罗索钦斯基、索洛维耶夫、契列德尼钦科、什维尔尼克和谢夫钦科。
    全体会议选出什维尔尼克为全苏工会中央理事会主席，索洛维耶夫为副主席，戈罗希金、古列耶夫、科罗鲍娃、波波娃和谢夫钦科为全苏工会中央理事会书记。
    第十一届全苏工会代表大会新选出的全苏工会中央检查委员会也举行了会议。会议选出库夫希诺夫为中央检查委员会主席。
    又讯：全苏工会中央理事会在十七日举行了招待会，招待前来参加第十一届全苏工会代表大会的世界工会联合会代表团和各国工会代表团。全苏工会中央理事会主席什维尔尼克在会上致欢迎词。他希望各国工会组织的代表们，在为工人阶级的团结、为加强和扩大国际联系以及为和平与各国人民的友好而进行的斗争中取得新的成就。
