Title: New Norwegian Government Negotiator Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-06-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd—Mr.季茂登, the new negotiator dispatched by the Norwegian government to conduct negotiations with the Central People's Government of our country on the establishment of diplomatic relations, arrived in Beijing on the 23rd. Mr. Lang Xinkang, Deputy Section Chief of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, greeted him at the station. Mr. 高兰, the former negotiator of the Norwegian government, will be recalled to return home within the next few days.

### 挪威政府新任谈判代表到京

1954-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    挪威政府新任谈判代表到京
    【新华社二十三日讯】挪威政府为和我国中央人民政府就建立外交关系进行谈判而派遣的新任谈判代表季茂登先生已在二十三日到达北京。我外交部交际处副科长郎新康到车站迎接。挪威政府前任谈判代表高兰先生将在日内奉调离京返国。
