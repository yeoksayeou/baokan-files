Title: French New Government List Announced, Mendès France Concurrently Serves as Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1954-06-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, June 21st: Following consultations with various parliamentary groups, French Prime Minister Mendès France announced the list of the new government on the 19th. The new government consists of sixteen ministers and thirteen undersecretaries.

The Radical Socialist Party, the Republican Union for Social Action (formerly the Gaullist faction), the Independent Republican Party, the Democratic and Social Union of Resistance, and other smaller parties are participating in the new government. In the new government, Mendès France also serves as Minister of Foreign Affairs. The Minister of the Interior is François Mitterrand (Democratic and Social Union of Resistance), the Minister of National Defense and Armed Forces is General Pierre Kœnig (Republican Union for Social Action), the Minister for Relations with the Member States of the Indochina Federation is Guy La Chambre, and the Minister of Finance and Economy remains Edgar Faure of the Radical Socialist Party.

### 法国新政府名单公布  孟戴斯—弗朗斯兼任外长

1954-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    法国新政府名单公布
    孟戴斯—弗朗斯兼任外长
    【新华社二十一日讯】巴黎消息：法国新总理孟戴斯—弗朗斯在和各议会党团磋商之后，在十九日宣布了新政府名单。新政府由十六个部长和十三个次长组成。
    参加新政府的有激进社会党、社会行动共和联盟（前戴高乐派）、独立共和党、民主社会抵抗联盟和其他小党派。在新政府中，孟戴斯—弗朗斯兼任外交部长。内政部长是弗朗索瓦·密特朗（民主社会抵抗联盟），国防与武装部队部长是皮埃尔·柯尼格将军（社会行动共和联盟），印度支那联邦成员国关系部长是居伊·拉香波，财政经济部长仍由激进社会党的埃加·富尔担任。
