Title: People of All Ethnic Groups in Beijing Who Believe in Islam Joyfully Celebrated Eid al-Fitr Yesterday; Islamic Association Held a Celebration Banquet
Author:
Date: 1954-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd:

    People of All Ethnic Groups in Beijing Who Believe in Islam
    Joyfully Celebrated Eid al-Fitr Yesterday
    Islamic Association Held a Celebration Banquet

    [Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd] On the 3rd, people of all ethnic groups in the capital who believe in Islam joyfully celebrated Eid al-Fitr.

    On this day, the largest mosque in the country, the Dongsi Pailou Mosque in Beijing, erected a colorful archway to celebrate Eid al-Fitr, and dozens of colorful flags fluttered in the green shade of the temple. At dawn, followers of Islam, dressed in neat clothes, flocked here from all directions to participate in the festival "congregational prayer," including Da Pongsheng, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association, Wu Hongbin, Ma Tengai, and Ma Songting, Vice Chairmen of the China Hui People's Cultural Advancement Association, and Ma Jian, Professor of Peking University. More than 60 Islamic believers from the Uyghur, Kazakh, Tajik, Tatar, Salar, and Dongxiang ethnic groups of the Central Institute for Nationalities also came to participate. More than 2,500 people participated in the festival "congregational prayer" here.

    Before the "congregational prayer" began, An Shiwei, the Imam of the Dongsi Pailou Mosque, addressed the believers, saying: Today, as we celebrate our own festival here, we should deeply thank the Communist Party and Chairman Mao for bringing freedom, equality, and happiness to the people of our ethnic minorities. We must unite more closely with the people of all ethnic groups in the country, follow the Communist Party and Chairman Mao, advance towards a happy socialist society, and unite with Islamic believers all over the world to seek peace for mankind.

    After the "congregational prayer" was over, young people happily danced collective dances in the newly built open-air dance floor in the temple. On this day, the temple also held photo exhibitions and various celebration activities such as basketball and table tennis competitions. More than 60 other mosques in the city also held festival "congregational prayers."

    On this day, state-owned economic departments in Beijing prepared a large number of beef and mutton for Islamic believers to eat during the festival.

    [Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd] June 3rd is the "Eid al-Fitr" festival for all ethnic groups who believe in Islam. The China Islamic Association held a banquet on the 3rd to celebrate the festival. More than 200 people attended the banquet, including members of the China Islamic Association and the China Hui People's Cultural Advancement Association in Beijing, representatives of Islamic ethnic groups who are in Beijing to attend the Fourth (Enlarged) Meeting of the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and Imams from various mosques in Beijing.

### 北京信仰伊斯兰教各族人民  昨天欢度开斋节  伊斯兰教协会举行庆祝宴会

1954-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    北京信仰伊斯兰教各族人民
    昨天欢度开斋节
    伊斯兰教协会举行庆祝宴会
    【新华社三日讯】三日，首都信仰伊斯兰教的各族人民欢度开斋节。
    这一天，全国最大的清真寺——北京东四牌楼清真寺搭起了庆祝开斋节的彩门，数十面彩旗在寺内的绿荫里迎风招展。黎明，伊斯兰教的信徒们，穿着整洁的服装从四面八方向这里涌来参加节日
  “聚礼”，其中有中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生，中国回民文化协进会副主任吴鸿宾、马腾霭、马松亭，北京大学教授马坚等。中央民族学院维吾尔、哈萨克、塔吉克、塔塔尔、撒拉、东乡等民族的伊斯兰教信徒六十多人也前来参加。在这里参加节日“聚礼”的共两千五百多人。
      “聚礼”开始前，东四牌楼清真寺阿訇安士伟向信徒们讲话，他说：今天当我们在这里欢庆自己节日的时候，应深深感谢共产党毛主席给我们少数民族人民带来的自由、平等和幸福。我们要和全国各族人民更加亲密地团结起来，跟着共产党毛主席，向着幸福的社会主义社会前进，并团结全世界的伊斯兰教信徒谋求人类的和平。
      “聚礼”完毕后，年青人在寺内新建的露天舞场里欢乐地跳起了集体舞。这一天寺内还举办了图片展览和篮球、乒乓球比赛等各种庆祝活动。全市其他的六十余座清真寺也举行了节日“聚礼”。
    这一天北京市的国营经济部门，准备了大批牛、羊肉，以供伊斯兰教信徒节日食用。
   【新华社三日讯】六月三日是信仰伊斯兰教各民族的“开斋节”，中国伊斯兰教协会在三日举行宴会庆祝佳节。参加宴会的有中国伊斯兰教协会、中国回民文化协进会的在北京的委员，和正在北京参加中央人民政府民族事务委员会第四次（扩大）会议的伊斯兰教各民族的代表，以及北京各清真寺的阿訇等共二百多人。
