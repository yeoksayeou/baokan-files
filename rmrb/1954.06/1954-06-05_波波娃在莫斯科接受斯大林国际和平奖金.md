Title: Popova Receives Stalin International Peace Prize in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-06-05
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 4th) TASS Moscow, June 4th: Nina Popova, the famous Soviet public figure, Chairman of the Soviet Women’s Anti-Fascist Committee, and Vice-President of the Women’s International Democratic Federation, received the Stalin International Prize “For Consolidation of Peace Among Peoples” in Moscow on June 3rd.

    The award ceremony was held in the Sverdlov Hall of the Kremlin. Representatives of Soviet public and trade union organizations, as well as leaders of the cultural world, attended the ceremony. Foreign guests present included: Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation; Dolores Ibárruri, Vice-President of the Women's International Democratic Federation; Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; delegations from the People's Republic of China, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, India, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Burma, Ceylon, Thailand, and Canada who are currently in Moscow after attending the special session of the World Peace Council in Berlin; and a delegation of French women currently visiting the Soviet Union.

    At the meeting, the resolution of the Committee on International Stalin Prizes "For Strengthening Peace Among Peoples" dated December 12, 1953, regarding the awarding of the Stalin International Peace Prize to Nina Popova was read out. Academician Dmitry Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the Committee on International Stalin Prizes "For Strengthening Peace Among Peoples," delivered a speech and presented Nina Popova with the diploma and gold medal, at which point the hall erupted in stormy applause.

    Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, Klavdiya Kuznetsova, Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, Tatyana Zuyeva, member of the Presidium of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, and Agrafena Zherova, a worker from the Trekhgorny Textile Factory, congratulated Nina Popova. Lu Cui, representing the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, congratulated Nina Popova on behalf of Chinese women.

    Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, then spoke. She said that the award of the Stalin International Peace Prize to Nina Popova made not only Soviet women, but also women all over the world who work with Nina Popova for the great cause of peace, feel very proud.

    Nina Popova's reply was warmly welcomed by the audience. She expressed her heartfelt gratitude for the warm congratulations she received, and emphasized that the award of the Stalin International Peace Prize to her was an affirmation of the great efforts made by the Soviet people and Soviet women in safeguarding world peace. Soviet women want peace and the prosperity of all countries. They demand that there should be no more situations where mothers and wives of all countries weep because of the loss of loved ones and the loss of their families. They demand that all the children of the world be happy.

    Nina Popova donated the prize money to be used for the upbringing of war orphans.

### 波波娃在莫斯科接受斯大林国际和平奖金

1954-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    波波娃在莫斯科接受斯大林国际和平奖金
   【新华社四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科四日讯：著名的苏联公众领袖、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席和国际民主妇女联合会副主席尼娜·波波娃六月三日在莫斯科接受“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金。
    授奖典礼在克里姆林宫斯维德洛夫大厅举行。出席典礼的有苏联公众和工会组织的代表以及文化界领袖。出席典礼的还有外国来宾：国际民主妇女联合会主席欧仁妮·戈登、国际民主妇女联合会副主席多洛雷斯·伊巴露丽、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、出席世界和平理事会柏林特别会议后现在正在莫斯科的中华人民共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、印度、越南民主共和国、缅甸、锡兰、泰国和加拿大的代表团以及现在正在苏联访问的法国妇女代表团。
    会上宣读了“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会一九五三年十二月十二日关于以斯大林国际和平奖金授予尼娜·波波娃的决议。“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会主席德米特里·斯科贝尔琴院士发表了演说，并以奖状和金质奖章授予尼娜·波波娃，这时全场响起了暴风雨般的掌声。
    苏联拥护和平委员会主席尼古拉·吉洪诺夫、全苏工会中央理事会书记克拉夫佳·库兹湼佐娃、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席团委员塔佳娜·朱耶娃和三山纺织厂工人阿格拉芬纳·热罗娃向尼娜·波波娃祝贺。中华全国民主妇女联合会代表陆璀代表中国妇女向尼娜·波波娃祝贺。
    国际民主妇女联合会主席欧仁妮·戈登接着发言。她说，“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金授予尼娜·波波娃，不仅使苏联妇女，而且使和尼娜·波波娃共同为伟大的和平事业服务的全世界妇女感到非常骄傲。
    尼娜·波波娃致答词时受到了听众的热烈欢迎。她对大家对她的热烈祝贺表示衷心的感谢，并着重指出，她被授予斯大林国际和平奖金就是表明苏联人民、苏联妇女为维护世界和平而从事的巨大努力得到了表扬。苏联妇女希望和平和各国繁荣。她们要求不再有各国母亲和妻子因为失去亲人、失去家庭而痛哭的情况。她们要求全世界的儿童都得到幸福。
    尼娜·波波娃把奖金捐出来作为抚养战争孤儿之用。
