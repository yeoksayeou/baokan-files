Title: Eden Visits Foreign Minister Zhou

Author:
Date: 1954-06-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 19th: British Foreign Secretary Eden called on Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, at 11:15 am this morning, for a 45-minute meeting. Vice Minister Zhang Wentian was also present. Accompanying Eden from the British side were Cassia and Du Villian.

### 艾登访周外长

1954-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    艾登访周外长
   【新华社日内瓦十九日电】英国外交大臣艾登十九日上午十一时三刻访晤中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来，会谈四十五分钟。在座的还有张闻天副部长。英国方面和艾登一起前来的有卡西亚、杜维廉。
