Title: Molotov Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-06-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 20th: TASS, Geneva, June 20th: Vyacheslav Mikhailovich Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, left Geneva by plane for Moscow on June 20th.

　　Vyacheslav Mikhailovich Molotov was seen off at Geneva airport by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People’s Republic of China, Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and members of the Soviet delegation attending the Geneva Conference.

　　During Vyacheslav Mikhailovich Molotov's absence from Geneva, K.V. Novikov will perform the duties of the Soviet representative at the Geneva Conference.

### 莫洛托夫回莫斯科

1954-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫回莫斯科
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社日内瓦二十日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫于六月二十日乘飞机离日内瓦赴莫斯科。
    在日内瓦机场欢送维·米·莫洛托夫的有中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，越南民主共和国副总理范文同及参加日内瓦会议的苏联代表团团员。
    在维·米·莫洛托夫离开日内瓦期间，将由基·维·诺维科夫执行苏联出席日内瓦会议代表的职务。
