Title: The World-Renowned Geneva Conference (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1954-06-13
Page: 5

The Geneva Conference, in which the Soviet Union, the United States, France, the United Kingdom, the People's Republic of China, and other relevant countries participated to discuss the peaceful settlement of the Korean and Indochinese issues, opened on April 26 at the former League of Nations building in Geneva. The picture shows a part of the venue. The first person in the third row from the left in the middle of the seating is Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of China. The second person in the fifth row is Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

A panoramic view of the venue for the conference on restoring peace in Indochina (starting with the rightmost table as the first table in a clockwise direction): Table 1 is the delegation of China (the person sitting in the middle is Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, and Zhang Wentian, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, is wearing a gray suit beside him); Table 2 is the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Table 3 is the delegation of the Kingdom of Laos; Table 4 is the delegation of France; Table 5 is the delegation of the State of Vietnam; Table 6 is the delegation of the United States; Table 7 is the delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia; Table 8 is the delegation of the Soviet Union; Table 9 is the delegation of the United Kingdom.

Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of China (left), hosted a banquet on April 29 for Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union (right). The hosts and guests had a cordial conversation after the banquet.

Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of China (left), hosted a banquet on May 1 for Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (right).

The Seine Peace Supporters Conference in France was held in Paris on April 24. Participants at the meeting warmly demanded a peaceful resolution to the Indochina issue.

Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of China (center), hosted a banquet on May 8 for Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam (right). The hosts and guests had a cordial conversation after the banquet.

Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, met with British Foreign Secretary Eden on May 27.

The working people of Geneva held a parade and rally on May 1. The picture shows the workers carrying a banner that reads "Geneva Workers Hope for Complete Success of the Geneva Conference."

From May 11 to 19, over the course of nine days, the Chinese delegation continued to receive 32 delegations from various parts of France. This is a scene of Huang Hua (first person on the right in the front), spokesperson for the Chinese delegation, meeting with the delegation of railway workers from Marseille, France.

Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of China (right), met with Indian representative to the United Nations, Menon (left), in Geneva on May 23.

On May 5, more than 200,000 people from all walks of life in Pyongyang, Korea, held a rally to warmly support the plan for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue put forward by Foreign Minister Nam Il at the Geneva Conference.

Gong Peng, spokesperson for the Chinese delegation, met with a delegation of women from Haute-Savoie, France, on May 13.

More than 6,000 people from all walks of life in Beijing held a grand rally on May 12 to support the just demands of the foreign ministers of China, the Soviet Union, North Korea, and Vietnam at the Geneva Conference.

Xinhua News Agency, Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio.

### 举世瞩目的日内瓦会议（图片）

1954-06-13
第5版()
专栏：

    举世瞩目的日内瓦会议
    苏联、美国、法国、英国、中华人民共和国和其他有关国家参加讨论和平解决朝鲜、印度支那等问题的日内瓦会议，于四月二十六日在日内瓦市前国联大厦开幕。图为会场的一部分。座次中间左起第三排第一人是我外交部部长周恩来。第五排第二人是苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫。
    讨论关于恢复印度支那和平问题会议的会场全景。（按时针走动方向以最右桌为第一桌排起）第一桌是我国代表团（中坐者是我外交部部长周恩来，其旁穿灰色服装的是我外交部副部长张闻天），第二桌是越南民主共和国代表团，第三桌是寮国王国代表团，第四桌是法国代表团，第五桌是越南国代表团，第六桌是美国代表团，第七桌是高棉王国代表团，第八桌是苏联代表团，第九桌是英国代表团。
    我外交部部长周恩来（左）四月二十九日设宴招待苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫（右），宴后宾主畅谈。
    我外交部部长周恩来（左）五月一日设宴招待朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日（右）。
    法国塞纳省和平拥护者会议于四月二十四日在巴黎举行。参加会议的人热烈要求和平解决印度支那问题。
    我外交部部长周恩来（中）五月八日设宴招待越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部长范文同（右），宴后宾主畅谈。
    我外交部部长周恩来五月二十七日接见英国外交大臣艾登。
    日内瓦劳动人民在五月一日举行游行和集会。图为游行的工人们抬着“日内瓦工人希望日内瓦会议完全成功”的标语牌。
    从五月十一日到十九日的九天中，我国代表团继续接见了来自法国各地的三十二批代表团。这是我代表团发言人黄华（右前方第一人）接见法国马赛铁路工人代表团时的情形。
    我外交部部长周恩来（右）五月二十三日在日内瓦接见印度驻联合国代表梅农（左）。
    五月五日，朝鲜平壤市各界人民二十多万人举行集会，热烈支持南日外务相在日内瓦会议上提出的和平解决朝鲜问题的方案。
    我代表团发言人龚澎五月十三日接见法国上萨瓦省的妇女代表团。
    北京各界人民六千多人，于五月十二日举行盛大集会，支持中、苏、朝、越四国外长在日内瓦会议上的正义主张。
    新华社、中央新闻纪录电影制片厂稿
