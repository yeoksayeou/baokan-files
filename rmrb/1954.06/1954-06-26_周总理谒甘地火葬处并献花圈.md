Title: Premier Zhou Visits Gandhi's Cremation Site and Lays a Wreath

Author:

Date: 1954-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 25th: Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, accompanied by advisors Qiao Guanhua and Wang Zhuoru, and the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, Yuan Zhongxian, visited Gandhi's cremation site at 9:00 a.m. on the 25th and laid a wreath. On the Indian side, the Director of Protocol of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, I.S. Chopra, and the Administrative Commissioner of Delhi State, A.D. Pandit, were also present.

### 周总理谒甘地火葬处并献花圈

1954-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理谒甘地火葬处并献花圈
    【新华社新德里二十五日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来偕顾问乔冠华、王倬如在中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤陪同下，在二十五日上午九时谒甘地火葬处并献花圈。印度方面陪同前往的有印度外交部交际司司长伊·斯·乔普拉和德里邦行政专员阿·德·潘迪特。
