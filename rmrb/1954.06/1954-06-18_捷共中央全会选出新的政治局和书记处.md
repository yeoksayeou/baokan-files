Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee Plenum Elects New Politburo and Secretariat
Author:
Date: 1954-06-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 16th (TASS dispatch): The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, elected by the Tenth Congress of the Party, held its first plenary session on the 15th.

    The meeting unanimously elected the Politburo of the Central Committee. The following comrades were elected as members of the Politburo: K. Bacilek, R. Barak, A. Cepicka, J. Ďuriš, Z. Fierlinger, V. Kopecký, A. Novotný, V. Široký, A. Zápotocký. The following comrades were elected as alternate members of the Politburo: L. Jankovcová and O. Šimůnek.

    The meeting unanimously elected the following comrades to form the Secretariat of the Central Committee: A. Novotný, J. Hendrych, B. Köhler, F. Krutina, and V. Pašek. A. Novotný was elected as First Secretary of the Central Committee.

    The Central Auditing Commission also held a meeting, and J. Štítka was elected as Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission.

### 捷共中央全会选出新的政治局和书记处

1954-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共中央全会选出新的政治局和书记处
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社布拉格十六日讯：由捷克斯洛伐克共产党第十次代表大会选出的党中央委员会，十五日举行了第一次全体会议。
    会议一致选出了中央委员会政治局。当选为政治局委员的有：卡·巴契列克、鲁·巴拉克、阿·契皮茨卡、雅·杜兰斯基、兹·费林格、瓦·柯别茨基、安·诺沃提尼、威·西罗基、安·萨波托斯基。当选为政治局候补委员的有：卢·扬科夫卓娃和奥·西穆涅克。
    会议一致选出了下列同志组成中央委员会书记处：安·诺沃提尼、伊·班德尔斯赫、勃·凯勒、弗·克鲁蒂纳和瓦·帕歇克。安·诺沃提尼当选为中央委员会第一书记。
    中央检查委员会也举行了会议，约·什蒂特卡当选为中央检查委员会主席。
