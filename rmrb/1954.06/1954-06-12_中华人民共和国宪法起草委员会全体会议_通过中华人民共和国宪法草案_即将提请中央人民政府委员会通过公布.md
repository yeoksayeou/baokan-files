Title: The Plenary Session of the Drafting Committee of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China Passed the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China, Which Will Soon Be Submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for Approval and Promulgation
Author:
Date: 1954-06-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th dispatch: The "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" was unanimously approved at the seventh plenary session of the Drafting Committee of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China held on the afternoon of the 11th. This draft constitution was formulated by the Constitution Drafting Committee based on the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China (First Draft)" proposed by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, after more than two months of detailed and thorough research, discussion, and modification. During this period, the Constitution Drafting Committee, in accordance with the decision of the first plenary session, distributed the first draft of the constitution to the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as the leading organs of major administrative regions, provinces, and cities, the local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and the leading organs of the armed forces for discussion, soliciting opinions from the aforementioned parties. The Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China passed by the Constitution Drafting Committee will soon be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for review and approval for promulgation, so as to organize discussions among the people of the whole country, collect opinions, and make further revisions.

### 中华人民共和国宪法起草委员会全体会议  通过中华人民共和国宪法草案  即将提请中央人民政府委员会通过公布

1954-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国宪法起草委员会全体会议
    通过中华人民共和国宪法草案
    即将提请中央人民政府委员会通过公布
    【新华社十一日讯】“中华人民共和国宪法草案”，已经在十一日下午举行的中华人民共和国宪法起草委员会第七次全体会议一致通过。这一宪法草案，是宪法起草委员会根据中国共产党中央委员会提出的“中华人民共和国宪法草案（初稿）”，经过两个多月的详细的、周密的研究、讨论和修改后拟成的。在此期间，宪法起草委员会曾根据第一次全体会议的决定，将宪法草案初稿分发给中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会，以及各大行政区、各省市的领导机关，各民主党派、各人民团体的地方组织和武装部队的领导机关进行讨论，征集了上述各方面人士的意见。宪法起草委员会通过的中华人民共和国宪法草案，即将提请中央人民政府委员会审查通过公布，以便在全国人民中组织讨论，收集意见，再作修改。
