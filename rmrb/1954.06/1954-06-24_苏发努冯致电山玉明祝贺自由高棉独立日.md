Title: Souphanouvong Sends Telegram to Sơn Ngọc Minh to Congratulate the Independence Day of Free Khmer
Author:
Date: 1954-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: According to Vietnam News Agency on June 23rd, Souphanouvong, Prime Minister of the Lao Resistance Government, sent a telegram to Sơn Ngọc Minh, Chairman of the Khmer Resistance Government, warmly congratulating the Independence Day of Free Khmer on June 19th.

The telegram said: The Khmer people’s struggle for national independence and the construction of a peaceful, free, and happy life has grown stronger and continuously achieved victories, which has greatly encouraged the Lao people. It is hoped that under the leadership of the Khmer Resistance Government and Chairman Sơn Ngọc Minh, the Khmer people will unite in the Vietnam-Khmer-Laos People's Alliance and gain more new victories. The Lao people believe that the fraternal friendship between the people of Vietnam, Khmer, and Laos will become stronger day by day.

In the struggle against the common enemies—the French warmongers and the American interventionists—the Lao people guarantee to unite closely with the Khmer and Vietnamese people and strive to realize the urgent desire of the people of the three countries for peace, independence, unity, and democracy.

### 苏发努冯致电山玉明祝贺自由高棉独立日

1954-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏发努冯致电山玉明祝贺自由高棉独立日
    【新华社二十三日讯】据越南通讯社二十三日讯：寮国抗战政府总理苏发努冯打电报给高棉抗战政府主席山玉明，热烈祝贺六月十九日自由高棉独立日。
    电文说：高棉人民争取民族独立和建设和平、自由、幸福生活的斗争，已日益壮大和不断取得胜利，这使寮国人民受到很大的鼓舞。希望高棉人民在高棉抗战政府和山玉明主席的领导下，团结在越南—高棉—寮国人民联盟中，获得更多新的胜利。寮国人民相信越南、高棉、寮国三国人民间兄弟般的友谊将一天比一天更加巩固。
    在反对共同敌人——法国战争贩子和美国干涉者的斗争中，寮国人民保证和高棉、越南人民紧密团结，努力实现三国人民要求和平、独立、统一和民主的迫切愿望。
