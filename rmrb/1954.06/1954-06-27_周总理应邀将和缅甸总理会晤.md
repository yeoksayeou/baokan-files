Title: Premier Zhou Will Meet with Burmese Prime Minister at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1954-06-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 26th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, will meet with Burmese Prime Minister U Nu in Rangoon on June 28th at the invitation of Prime Minister U Nu of Burma.

### 周总理应邀将和缅甸总理会晤

1954-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理应邀将和缅甸总理会晤
   【新华社新德里二十六日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来应缅甸总理吴努邀请，定于六月二十八日在仰光和吴努总理会晤。
