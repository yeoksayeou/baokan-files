Title: People of the Taixi New District Comfort Our Army in Shandong
Author:
Date: 1948-08-16
Page: 2
　　Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 14th. The people of Taixi County (newly established county, west of the Tianjin-Pukou Railway in Tai'an) in the newly liberated area of ​​Ji-Lu-Yu, sent 25 representatives on the 2nd, carrying 10 million Jibi and a large number of letters of comfort to the front line to comfort the Shandong Corps of the East China People's Liberation Army, which had won a great victory in the Xu-Ji section of the Tianjin-Pukou Railway. The representatives denounced the crimes of the Kuomintang army to the troops and thanked the People's Liberation Army for rescuing the 400,000 people of Taixi from the ravages of the Kuomintang army. The representatives visited the front-line units and the artillery units equipped with US aircraft, and all the representatives were excited. When the representatives returned to the county on the 4th, the director of the political department of the corps personally hosted a banquet to see them off.

### 泰西新区人民慰劳山东我军

1948-08-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　泰西新区人民慰劳山东我军
    【新华社华东前线十四日电】冀鲁豫新解放区泰西县（新设县、泰安津浦路西）人民，二日派代表二十五人携冀钞一千万元和大批慰问信，赴前线慰劳此次在津浦路徐济段获得大捷的华东解放军山东兵团。代表们向部队控诉了国民党军的罪恶，并感谢解放军解救泰西四十万人民免于国民党军的蹂躏。代表们参观了前线各部及美机装备的炮兵部队，各代表皆心感兴奋。四日代表返县时，兵团政治部主任特亲临设宴欢送。
