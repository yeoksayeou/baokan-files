Title: Japanese Communist Party Central Committee Thanks All Parties for Condolences; Comrade Tokuda's Health is Rapidly Recovering

Author:
Date: 1948-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 30th: TASS, Tokyo, 25th: The Japanese Communist Party recently issued a statement thanking the working masses and democratic organizations at home and abroad for their sympathy and condolences regarding the assassination attempt on Japanese Communist Party General Secretary Tokuda. The statement said: "Our Party Central Committee deeply thanks the working masses, the National Railway Workers' Union, the Postal Workers' Union, the National Federation of Industrial Labor Unions, as well as democratic organizations at home and abroad, the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party, and other people's democratic forces around the world for the sympathy and indignation expressed regarding the reactionary forces' despicable assassination attempt on Comrade Tokuda. Comrade Tokuda is recovering rapidly after treatment. He is conscientiously continuing to fulfill his responsibilities as the leader of the Party. To repay the expressions of sympathy, our Party will, together with democratic forces at home and abroad and the masses of the people, continue to fight bravely and resolutely to win peace, democracy, and national independence."

### 日共中央答谢各方慰问  德田同志健康正迅速复原

1948-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    日共中央答谢各方慰问
    德田同志健康正迅速复原
    【新华社陕北三十日电】塔斯社东京二十五日讯：日本共产党顷发表声明，答谢日本国内外劳动群众与民主团体对日共总书记德田遇刺的同情与慰问，该声明称：“我党中央深深感谢劳动群众、国营铁道工人工会、递信工人工会、全国产业劳动组合联合会、以及国内外民主团体、联共中央和世界其他人民民主力量对德田被反动派卑鄙行刺所表示的同情和愤慨。德田同志经治疗后正迅速复原中。他正心畏地继续执行他作为党的领袖的责任。为了答谢同情的表示，我党将和国内外民主力量以及人民大众一道，继续勇敢而坚决的斗争，以争取和平、民主和民族独立。”
