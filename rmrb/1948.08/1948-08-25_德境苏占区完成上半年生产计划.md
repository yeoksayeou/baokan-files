Title: Soviet Occupation Zone of Germany Completes First Half of Year Production Plan
Author:
Date: 1948-08-25
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 22nd—TASS reports: The German Economic Commission announced that the production plan for the first half of the year in the Soviet Occupation Zone of Germany has been completed. Major industries such as mining, engineering, and power have substantially exceeded their planned targets. The commodity production plan also exceeded by 32 percent, railway freight by 4 percent, and automobile transportation by 16 percent. Wholesale trade in the first quarter of this year increased by 36 percent compared to the same period last year, while retail trade increased by 20 percent. Furthermore, the German Economic Commission has drafted the sowing plan for the Soviet Occupation Zone for next year. This plan is part of the two-year economic plan for the Soviet Occupation Zone.

### 德境苏占区完成上半年生产计划

1948-08-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　德境苏占区完成上半年生产计划
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】塔斯社讯：德国经济委员会宣布德境苏占区本年上半年生产计划已经完成。矿业、工程、动力等主要工业均已相当大地超过了预定目标。商品生产计划也超过百分之三十二，铁路货运超过百分之四，汽车运输超过百分之十六。本年第一季批发贸易较去年同期增加百分之三十六，而零售贸易则增加百分之二十。又讯，苏占区明年播种计划，已由德国经济委员会拟定。该项计划为苏占区两年经济计划之一部分。
