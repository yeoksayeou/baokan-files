Title: Youth and Students and Cultural Circles in Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia-Jin-Sui Support Beiping Northeast Students
Author:
Date: 1948-08-21
Page: 2

　　Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia-Jin-Sui Youth and Students and Cultural Circles
  　Support Beiping Northeast Students
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 18th] The Youth Federations of Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia and Jin-Sui, the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Cultural Association, Yan'an University, Xingzhi Middle School, and others have jointly sent a telegram to the Northeast students in Beiping and patriotic students across the country. The telegram stated: We express our stern protest against the Kuomintang bandit gang's "July 5th" massacre of Northeast students in Beiping, extend our heartfelt condolences to the martyrs who sacrificed in this massacre, and deeply express our sympathies to the injured and arrested students. The youth, students, and cultural organizations of Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia and Jin-Sui are determined to support you with practical actions of actively participating in the People's Liberation War and strive for the early liberation of all of China.

### 陕甘宁晋绥青年学生及文化界　声援北平东北同学

1948-08-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　陕甘宁晋绥青年学生及文化界
  　声援北平东北同学
    【新华社西北十八日电】陕甘宁、晋绥青年联合会，陕甘宁边区文化协会、延安大学、行知中学等顷联名致电北平东北同学暨全国各地爱国同学。原电略称：我们对国民党匪帮在北平疯狂屠杀东北同学的“七五”惨案表示严正的抗议，对于在这次惨案中牺牲的烈士表示衷心的悼念，对于受伤与被捕的同学深致慰问之忱。我陕甘宁、晋绥的青年学生和文化团体决以积极参加人民解放战争的实际行动来支援你们，来争取早日解放全中国。
