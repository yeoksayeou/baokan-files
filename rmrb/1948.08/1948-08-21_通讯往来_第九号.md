　　Column: Correspondence

　　Correspondence No. 9
    To all comrades in enterprise departments, industrial and commercial organs, and city correspondents:
    Please vigorously report on the situation in various factories, stores, and enterprise departments after the North China Industrial and Commercial Conference, implementing the spirit of the conference, the problems encountered in the specific implementation of industrial and commercial policies, how they were resolved, the experiences gained, and the achievements in developing production after correctly implementing industrial and commercial policies.
    　　　　　×　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　×To all comrades of the Study Committees and correspondents of directly subordinate organs:
    The study of the "Four Major Documents" has begun in various organs one after another. Please report immediately on the organization of study in various organs, the achievements, problems, and experiences gained in studying the documents thoroughly, linking them with reality, and examining and improving work.
    　　　　　×　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　×To Comrade Yushan of the Office of Gaoyi County, Taihang:
    We have received the various situations and problems you have continuously sent for research and reference, which have been of great help to us. We hope that you will continue to send them regularly in the future, and we also hope that you will try to indicate whether these situations are individual or general, few or many; we also hope that you will try to indicate how the leadership is solving or preparing to solve them.
    　　　　　×　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　×

### 通讯往来　第九号

1948-08-21
第1版()
专栏：通讯往来

　　通讯往来　第九号
    各企业部门、工商机关、城市通讯员同志：
    请大力报导华北工商会议后，各地工厂商店各种企业部门贯彻大会精神的情形，具体执行工商政策过程中所遇到的问题，怎样解决的？获得那些经验，以及正确执行工商政策后发展生产的成绩。
    　　　　　×　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　×各直属机关学委会及通讯员同志：
    “四大文件”学习，各机关已经先后开始，请即报导各机关组织学习的情况，学习中精研文件、联系实际、检查工作与改进工作所收到的成绩问题和经验。
    　　　　　×　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　×太行高邑县办公室玉山同志：
    你们连续寄来供研究参考用的各种情况与问题均已收到，对我们帮助很大，希望你们今后仍能经常邮寄，并望以后能对这些情况是个别的还是一般的，是少数还是多数的，尽量加以说明；领导上是怎样解决或准备怎样解决，亦望尽量予以说明。
    　　　　　×　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　×
