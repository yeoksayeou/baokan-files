Title: War News from the Sui-Meng Military Region

Author: 
Date: 1948-08-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jin Sui, 31st: (1) On the evening of the 20th, a certain unit of the Sui-Meng local armed forces captured the Houcaogou stronghold east of Liangcheng. (2) Another unit annihilated an enemy security police team that was fleeing from Shuang Gucheng (20 li south of Tiancheng) back to Tiancheng Township on the morning of the 19th, killing or capturing over 50 enemies. (3) On the evening of the 11th, more than 170 bandits of the Fu faction from the Hongshaba and Guancun strongholds in Fengzhen, harassed Houshagou (30 li northwest of Fengzhen) but were repelled by our assault team. In total, over 200 enemies were killed, wounded, or captured, and two light machine guns and over 80 rifles were seized.

### 绥蒙地武战讯

1948-08-02
第2版()
专栏：

    绥蒙地武战讯
    【新华社晋绥三十一日电】（一）绥蒙地方武装某部二十日晚攻克凉城东之后草沟据点。（二）另部于十九日晨追歼由双古城（天城南二十里）回窜天城镇之敌保警队，毙俘敌五十余。（三）十一日晚，丰镇红沙坝、官村两据点之傅匪百七十余人，出扰后沙沟（丰镇西北卅里），被我攻击队击退。以上共计毙伤俘敌二百余人，缴获轻机枪两挺步枪八十余支。
