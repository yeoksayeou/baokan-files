Title: Italian Communist Party Central Committee Sends Telegram Thanking Chairman Mao

Author:

Date: 1948-08-11
Page: 1

　　Italian Communist Party Central Committee
  　Telegrams Thanks to Chairman Mao
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 9th] The Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong, thanking Chairman Mao for his condolences regarding the assassination attempt on the leader of the Italian Communist Party, Togliatti, by Italian reactionaries. The original telegram stated: "To Comrade Mao Zedong, Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party: We thank the heroic Chinese Communist Party for its protest against the heinous reactionaries' assassination attempt on Comrade Togliatti and for its sympathetic condolences. Our party and the workers of Italy are rising up more actively to fight against imperialism and the reactionaries. Togliatti's health is improving daily. On behalf of Togliatti and the working people of Italy, we wish you new victories in your resolute and heroic struggle.
  　Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party
    July 29, 1948"

### 意共中央电谢毛主席

1948-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　意共中央
  　电谢毛主席
    【新华社陕北九日电】意共中央致电毛泽东主席，答谢毛主席对意共领袖托格里亚蒂被意大利反动派阴谋刺伤的慰问。原电称：“中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东同志：我们感谢英勇的中国共产党对凶恶的反动派谋刺托格里亚蒂同志的抗议及对他的同情的慰问。我们党和意大利的劳动者，正在更积极的起来作反对帝国主义与反动派的斗争。托格里亚蒂的健康情况正日益好转，我们代表托格里亚蒂和意大利的劳动人民，预祝你们在刚毅而英勇的斗争中得到新的胜利。
  　意大利共产党中央委员会
    一九四八年七月二十九日”
