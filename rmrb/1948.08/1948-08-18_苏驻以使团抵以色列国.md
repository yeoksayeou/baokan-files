Title: Soviet Mission Arrives in Israel
Author:
Date: 1948-08-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 15th. Reports from various sources: The Soviet diplomatic mission to Israel, headed by Soviet Minister to Israel, Mr. Yershov, consisting of seventeen people, arrived in Israel on the 9th (TASS). When they arrived in the Israeli capital of Tel Aviv on the evening of the 10th, they were warmly welcomed by hundreds of Jews (United Press). Jewish newspapers commented with welcome, considering it a major event (Associated Press).

### 苏驻以使团抵以色列国

1948-08-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏驻以使团抵以色列国
    【新华社陕北十五日电】各方报导：苏联派赴以色列的外交使团以驻以公使耶绍夫为首一行十七人已于九日抵达以色列（塔斯社）。他们在十日晚抵达以色列首都特拉维夫时，曾受到数百犹太人的热烈欢迎（合众社）。犹太报纸撰论表示欢迎，认为是一件大事（美联社）。
