Title: Northern Anhui We Captured Fuyang, Southern Shandong Recaptured Zaozhuang
Author:
Date: 1948-08-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 7th. Local armed forces of the Yu-Wan-Su Military Region recaptured Fuyang, an important town in northern Anhui, on July 31st. The defending Kuomintang troops, two regiments of the 58th Brigade of the reorganized 74th Division (rebuilt after being annihilated at Menglianggu in Shandong), and the No. 1 Regiment of the Anhui Provincial Security Force, abandoned the city and fled southeast. Fuyang is the political and cultural center of northern Anhui and the location of the puppet Anhui Third Administrative Office. With the recapture of the city, the Fourth and Sixth Sub-districts of our Yu-Wan-Su liberated area have been linked together.
    Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 7th. Delayed news: Local armed forces in southern Shandong recaptured Zaozhuang, a nationally famous coal mining area, on the 28th of last month. The defending enemy fled towards Yixian County.
    Further dispatch: On the 22nd of last month, the People's Liberation Army defeated a battalion of the 15th Brigade of the reorganized 73rd Division, which had invaded from Jinan to loot grain, at Maguanzhai, Zhangqiu. 39 enemy soldiers were killed, wounded or captured, and four vehicles, two light machine guns, three Thompson submachine guns, and fourteen rifles were seized.

### 皖北我克阜阳  鲁南收复枣庄

1948-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    皖北我克阜阳
    鲁南收复枣庄
    【新华社中原前线七日电】豫皖苏军区地方武装七月三十一日收复皖北重镇阜阳。守城国民党军整七十四师五十八旅之两个团（按系在山东孟良崮被歼后重建者），及安徽省保一团等敌弃城向东南逃窜。阜阳为皖北政治、文化中心，伪安徽第三专署所在地。该城收复，我豫皖苏解放区之四、六两分区已联成一片。
    【新华社华东前线七日电】迟到消息：鲁南地方武装于上月二十八日收复全国著名煤矿区枣庄，守敌向峄县逃窜。
    【又讯】解放军上月二十二日在章邱马官寨击溃由济南出犯抢粮之敌整七十三师十五旅之一个营，毙伤俘其三十九名，缴获汽车四辆，轻机枪二挺，汤姆枪三支，步枪十四支。
