Title: Labor Association Council Sends Telegram to Congratulate the Congress: Unite the National Working Class to End the Rule of the Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Gang
Author:
Date: 1948-08-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 5th. The Council of the China Labor Association sent a telegram on the 1st to congratulate the Sixth National Labor Congress, stating: "The convening of the Congress demonstrates that our national working class and all working people, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party headed by Chairman Mao, are increasingly moving towards a comprehensive national victory. This Council, on behalf of all members of the China Labor Association, extends its highest respect and sincere support to the Congress. We wish the Congress success in uniting the national working class and all working people to swiftly overthrow the feudal forces and the rule of bureaucratic capital represented by the four major families, end the dictatorial regime of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang, and the aggression of American imperialism against China, and unite the people of the whole country to advance towards a democratic, free, and New Democratic New China."

### 劳协理事会电贺大会  团结全国工人阶级结束蒋家匪帮统治

1948-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    劳协理事会电贺大会
    团结全国工人阶级结束蒋家匪帮统治
    【新华社东北五日电】中国劳动协会理事会于一日电贺第六次全国劳动大会称：“大会之召开，显示我全国工人阶级及一切劳动人民在以毛主席为首的中国共产党领导之下，日益走向全国的全面胜利。本理事会谨代表中国劳动协会全体会员向大会致崇高的敬礼和衷诚之拥戴。并祝大会胜利，团结全国工人阶级与一切劳动人民，迅速打倒以四大家族为代表的封建势力与官僚资本的统治，结束蒋家匪帮独裁政权，及美帝国主义对于中国的侵略，联合全国人民向民主、自由的新民主主义的新中国前进。”
