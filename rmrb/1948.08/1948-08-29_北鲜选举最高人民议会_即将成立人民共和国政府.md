Title: North Korea Elects Supreme People's Assembly, People's Republic Government to be Established Soon
Author:
Date: 1948-08-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 26th. According to a Kyodo News Agency dispatch from the 22nd, quoting Pyongyang Radio: "The North Korean People's Committee has been reorganized into a provisional government. After the election on August 25th, a People's Republic government will be established. The People's Republic government will be based on parliamentary democracy. The election campaign is underway." TASS also reported: North Korean newspapers have recently been fiercely criticizing the United States' colonial policy towards South Korea and the South Korean reactionaries' assistance to American imperialism in dividing the country and imposing a puppet government on the people.

### 北鲜选举最高人民议会　即将成立人民共和国政府

1948-08-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　北鲜选举最高人民议会
  　即将成立人民共和国政府
    【新华社陕北二十六日电】据电通社二十二日电引平壤广播称：“北朝鲜人民委员会已改组为临时政府。八月二十五日选举之后，将建立人民共和国政府。人民共和国政府将以议会民主为基础。竞选运动正进行中。”另据塔斯社报导：北朝鲜报纸日来猛烈抨斥美国对南朝鲜的殖民地政策，以及南朝鲜反动派之协助美帝国主义分裂国家将傀儡政府强加在人民身上。
