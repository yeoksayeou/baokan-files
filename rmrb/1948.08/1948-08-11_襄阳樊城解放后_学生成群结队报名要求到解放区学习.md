Title: After the Liberation of Xiangyang and Fancheng, Students Enrolled in Droves, Requesting to Study in the Liberated Areas
Author:
Date: 1948-08-11
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, August 8th: After the liberation of Xiangyang and Fancheng, students from those two cities enrolled in droves at the City Management Committee, requesting to study in schools in the liberated areas. Those who have already enrolled include hundreds of students from Xiangyang County First and Second Middle Schools, Jianming Normal School, Provincial Fifth Normal School, and Guanghua Middle School, Eighth Middle School, and Eighth Normal School, which had moved from Laohekou, Yunyang. A student from the textile department of the Hubei Provincial First Women's Vocational Senior High School wrote a letter to the political director of a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army, saying, "I have already gone north of the Han River alone to look for you, but I couldn't find you. How much I hope to join the People's Liberation Army! Now that I see you, I am truly overjoyed." She also said, "It is such a happiness to be able to struggle for the cause of people's liberation under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong."

### 襄阳樊城解放后　学生成群结队报名要求到解放区学习

1948-08-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　襄阳樊城解放后
  　学生成群结队报名要求到解放区学习
    【新华社中原八日电】襄阳樊城学生于该两城解放后，成群结队到城市管理委员会报名请求入解放区的学校学习。已报名者有襄阳县立第一、二中学、简明师范、省立第五师范和郧阳老河口搬来的光华中学、第八中学、第八师范等校学生数百人。湖北省立第一女子职业高级中学纺织科一个学生写信给解放军某部政治主任说：“我已经单独到汉水以北找过你们，可是没有找到，我是多么希望参加解放军呀！现在看到了你们，真是说不出的高兴。”她又说：“能在毛泽东主席领导下为人民解放事业而奋斗，那是太幸福了。”
