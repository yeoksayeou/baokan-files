Title: International Women's Federation Exhibition - Letter of Thanks to the Women's Federation of the Liberated Areas
Author:
Date: 1948-08-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 31st - The International Democratic Women's Federation, in preparation for the International Women's Exhibition aimed at promoting the democratic movement of women in various countries and exchanging work experience, opened in Paris in mid-June. Maps, charts, and explanatory notes of the Chinese Liberated Areas, photographs of the women's movement, products, embroideries, and other exhibits, as well as donated items for fundraising, were carefully designed by Lu Cui, secretary of the Women's Federation of the Liberated Areas stationed at the Federation, and elaborately decorated before being presented for exhibition, which was well received by the audience. The Federation has sent a special letter to Cai Chang, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Women's Federation of the Chinese Liberated Areas, to thank the Women's Federation of the Liberated Areas.

### 国际妇联展览会  函谢解放区妇联

1948-08-04
第2版()
专栏：

    国际妇联展览会
    函谢解放区妇联
    【新华社陕北三十一日电】国际民主妇女联合会为宣传各国妇女民主运动，交流工作经验所筹备的国际妇女展览会，已于六月中在巴黎开幕。中国解放区地图、表格及其说明书、妇女运动的照片、生产品、刺绣品等展览品及捐赠的义卖品，均于会前经解放区妇联驻该会秘书陆璀等精心设计，重加装璜，送陈展览，颇得观众好评。该会为此专函中国解放区妇联筹委主任蔡畅，向解放区妇联致谢。
