Title: US Progressive Party Platform: Peaceful Foreign Policy, Free People's Rights, Prosperous Social Economy, Support for the Independence Struggles of Asian Nations, Demanding an End to Aid to Chiang and Withdrawal of US Troops from China
Author:
Date: 1948-08-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 31st -- Reports from various sources: The platform adopted by the US Progressive Party Convention on the 25th is divided into three main parts: a peaceful foreign policy, free people's rights, and a prosperous social economy. The section on Far Eastern foreign policy in the first part states: "The Progressive Party supports the struggles of Asian nations to achieve independence and move from feudalism to the modern century. We condemn the bipartisan military and economic intervention policies that attempt to crush Asian national movements. Unless the people of China, Indonesia, Vietnam, Malaya, and other parts of Asia win the struggle for independence and take their place in the international family on an equal footing, world peace and prosperity cannot be achieved. We demand the immediate withdrawal of American troops from China and the abandonment of bases in China. We demand an end to financial and military aid to Chiang Kai-shek's dictatorial rule. We support the efforts of the Korean people to establish national unity and the government they desire, and demand the early mutual withdrawal of occupying forces." The foreign policy platform also calls for sincere negotiations with the Soviet Union to find a basis for a peaceful agreement, believing that such a basis can be found on the principle of non-interference in the internal affairs of other countries. The platform also advocates abandoning the Truman Doctrine; abolishing the Marshall Plan; abolishing peacetime conscription; establishing a reconstruction and development fund by the United Nations to provide assistance without political conditions; declaring a ban on atomic, chemical, and other weapons of mass destruction; cooperating with wartime allies to conclude peace treaties with a unified Germany and Japan as soon as possible, eliminate Nazism and implement democratization; immediately legally recognizing Israel and providing rapid assistance without political conditions; abandoning American military bases aimed at threatening other countries; abolishing unequal treaties with small nations and abolishing the colonial system; abandoning the Pan-American military plan; and ending all military and economic aid to fascist regimes in Greece, Turkey, the Middle East, and Latin America. The section of the platform on free civil rights advocates abolishing all forms of discrimination against blacks and abolishing the poll tax system. The section on a prosperous economy advocates expanding social insurance, abolishing the Taft-Hartley Act, and nationalizing large banks, railroads, merchant fleets, electricity, coal mines, airplanes, synthetic petroleum, and other financial and industrial sectors.

### 美进步党政纲  和平的外交政策  自由的人民权利  富足的社会经济  支持亚洲各民族的独立斗争要求停止援蒋撤退驻华美军

1948-08-02
第2版()
专栏：

    美进步党政纲
    和平的外交政策
    自由的人民权利
    富足的社会经济
    支持亚洲各民族的独立斗争要求停止援蒋撤退驻华美军
    【新华社陕北三十一日电】各方报导：美国进步党大会于二十五日通过的政纲，共分为三大部分，即和平的外交政策、自由的人民权利与富足的社会经济。其第一部分中关于远东外交政策一项内称：“进步党支持亚洲各民族为取得独立与从封建制度进入现代世纪而作的斗争。我们谴责企图击破亚洲民族运动的两党军事、经济干涉政策。除非中国、印尼、越南、马来亚及亚洲其他地方的人民赢得争取独立的斗争，并以平等的地位列身于国际家庭，则世界的和平与繁荣将不能达到。我们要求立即撤退美国驻华军队与放弃在华基地。我们要求停止对蒋介石独裁统治的财政与军事援助。我们支持朝鲜人民建立全国统一与他们所愿望的政府的努力，并要求早日共同撤退占领军。”外交政纲同时要求与苏联进行诚意的谈判，以觅致和平协议的基础，并认为在不干涉他国内政的原则下，此种基础可以觅致。该政纲又主张抛弃杜鲁门主义；废除马歇尔计划；废除和平时期的征兵法；由联合国设立复兴开发基金以进行无政治条件的援助；宣布禁用原子、化学等集体毁灭武器；与战时盟国合作，早日与统一的德国及日本缔结和约，肃清纳粹并实行民主化；立即在法律上承认以色列，并迅速予以不附政治条件的援助；放弃美国旨在威胁其他国家的军事基地；废除对弱小民族的不平等条约，并取消殖民地制度；放弃泛美军事计划；结束对希、土、中东与拉丁美洲法西斯政权的一切军事与经济援助等。政纲中关于自由民权部分主张废除对黑人的各种形式歧视并废除人头税制。关于富足经济部分主张扩充社会保险，废除塔夫脱——哈特莱法案，并将大银行、铁道、商船队、电力、煤矿、飞机、人造石油等金融与工业收归国有。
