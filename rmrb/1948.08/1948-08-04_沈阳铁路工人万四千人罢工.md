Title: 14,000 Railway Workers in Shenyang Strike
Author:
Date: 1948-08-04
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 2nd: United Press, Peiping dispatch: All 14,000 railway workers in Shenyang launched a large-scale strike on July 27th, demanding improved treatment. The workers put forward the demand for "rationed food and wages paid according to living expenses." The agency's 29th dispatch stated: The Kuomintang railway authorities, intimidated by the workers' great momentum, were forced to "agree to better living conditions." The workers have resumed work after achieving initial victories.

### 沈阳铁路工人万四千人罢工

1948-08-04
第2版()
专栏：

    沈阳铁路工人万四千人罢工
    【新华社陕北二日电】美联社北平讯：沈阳铁路工人全体一万四千人，于七月二十七日掀起大规模的要求改善待遇的罢工。工人们提出“配给粮食并照生活费发工资”的要求。该社二十九日电称：国民党铁路当局慑于工人盛大声威，被迫“允予较好的生活条件”。工人已于获得初步胜利后复工。
