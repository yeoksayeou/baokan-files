Title: The CCP Central Committee Sends a Congratulatory Telegram on the Great Victory of Huanglong; Continues to Place the Bandit Hu in a Passive Position, Facilitating Our Development of a Victorious Situation in Guanzhong
Author:
Date: 1948-08-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 23rd. The CCP Central Committee sent a congratulatory telegram on the great victory of Huanglong. The original telegram is as follows:

    Comrades Peng Dehuai, He Long, Lin Boqu, Xi Zhongxun, Zhang Zongxun, Zhao Shoushan, and to all comrades of the Northwest People's Liberation Army:

    Congratulations on your great victory in annihilating the 36th Division of Bandit Hu for the second time, and liberating the three cities of Chengcheng, @Yang, and @Yi. This victory has shattered the nonsense that Bandit Hu had won in the Western Shaanxi Campaign, and continues to put Bandit Hu in a passive position, facilitating our development of a victorious situation in Guanzhong. It is hoped that you will continue your efforts and fight for the complete annihilation of Bandit Hu and the liberation of the Great Northwest!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　August 22, 1948

### 中共中央电贺黄龙大捷　续使胡匪被动，便于我开展关中胜利局面

1948-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央电贺黄龙大捷
  　续使胡匪被动，便于我开展关中胜利局面
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】中共中央电贺黄龙大捷，原电如次：
    彭德怀、贺龙、林伯渠、习仲勋、张宗逊、赵寿山诸同志并转西北人民解放军全体同志们：
    庆祝你们第二次歼击胡匪三十六师，并解放澄城、＠阳、＠邑三城的巨大胜利。这一胜利，正粉碎了胡匪在西府战役曾获得胜利的胡说，并继续使胡匪陷于被动，便于我开展关中的胜利局面。尚望继续努力，为全歼胡匪解放大西北而战！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中共中央委员会
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四八年八月二十二日
