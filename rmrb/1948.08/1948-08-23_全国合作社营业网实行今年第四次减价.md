Title: National Cooperative Business Network Implements Fourth Price Reduction This Year
Author:
Date: 1948-08-23
Page: 2

　　National Cooperative Business Network Implements Fourth Price Reduction This Year
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th. TASS reported on the 14th: The Soviet Cooperative Business Network has recently implemented its fourth price reduction this year. Reduced price goods include various foods such as meat, poultry, butter, honey, fruit, and canned meats. Soviet cooperatives are now vigorously purchasing agricultural products in rural areas. In the three years since the war, cooperatives have greatly expanded their diverse operations, and their trade turnover has doubled. The monthly sales volume this year has increased by two and a half times compared to last year. According to this year's plan, cooperatives will add more than 30,000 new stores in towns and villages across the country.

### 全国合作社营业网实行今年第四次减价

1948-08-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国合作社营业网实行今年第四次减价
    【新华社陕北二十日电】塔斯社十四日讯：苏联合作社营业网顷实行今年第四次减价。减价货品包括肉类、家禽、牛油、蜂蜜、水果、罐头肉类等各种食物。苏联合作社现正在乡间大事收购农产品，战后三年来，合作社已大大扩充其多样性的营业，其贸易运转率已增加两倍，今年每月售货量较去年增加两倍半。按照今年计划，合作社将在全国各城镇乡村增设新售货店三万余所。
