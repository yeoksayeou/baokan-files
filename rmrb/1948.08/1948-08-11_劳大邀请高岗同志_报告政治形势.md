Title: Labor Congress Invites Comrade Gao Gang to Report on the Political Situation
Author:
Date: 1948-08-11
Page: 1

　　Labor Congress Invites Comrade Gao Gang
　　To Report on the Political Situation
　　【Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 8th】On the second day of the Sixth National Labor Congress, Comrade Gao Gang, representative of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, was invited to give a political report. Amidst thunderous applause, Comrade Gao Gang began with excited words, saying, "We are able to hold such a congress in our own city of Harbin today, which is the result of the victory of the working class struggle!" He then briefly described the history of the Chinese working class's arduous struggle over the years, followed by a detailed analysis of the basic characteristics of the current international and domestic situations, and pointed out the current tasks of the Chinese working class. In each part of his report, Comrade Gao Gang used realistic and concrete examples to vividly and powerfully illustrate the new situation of the revolution today, greatly enhancing the confidence and determination of the delegates to continue their efforts to win the victory of the whole country, and repeatedly eliciting enthusiastic applause from the audience. Comrade Gao Gang's report lasted four hours.

### 劳大邀请高岗同志　报告政治形势

1948-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　劳大邀请高岗同志
  　报告政治形势
    【新华社东北八日电】第六次全国劳动大会第二日，请中共中央东北局代表高岗同志作政治报告，在雷动的掌声中，高岗同志首以兴奋的语句称：“我们今天能在自己的城市哈尔滨召开这样的大会，这是工人阶级斗争胜利的结果！”他并简要地叙述了中国工人阶级多年来艰苦奋斗的历史，继即详尽的分析了目前的国际形势、国内形势的基本特点，并扼要指出中国工人阶级的当前任务。高岗同志在每一部分的报告中，均以现实的具体事例，生动有力地说明了今天革命的新形势，大大提高了与会代表继续努力争取全国胜利的信心和决心，不时引起全场大鼓掌。高岗同志的报告共历四小时。
