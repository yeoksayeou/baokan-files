Title: Detailed Battle Results of the Yu Eastern Campaign: Annihilating Over 50,000 Enemy Officers and Soldiers
Author: 
Date: 1948-08-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Yu Eastern Front, 28th. The headquarters of the East China People's Liberation Army on the Yu Eastern Front issued a communique, announcing the detailed battle results of the Sui (County)-Qi (County) battle in eastern Henan from June 27th to July 6th as follows:

    The entire 7th Corps headquarters of the Chiang army was annihilated, as well as the entire 75th Division headquarters, and the entire 6th Brigade (comprising the 16th, 17th, and 18th Regiments) under its command, the entire 16th Brigade (comprising the 46th, 47th, and 48th Regiments), the entire 21st New Brigade (comprising the 61st, 62nd, and 63rd Regiments) and the aforementioned three brigade headquarters, the entire 324th Regiment of the 108th Brigade of Huang Botao's Corps, one battalion of the 323rd Regiment, the entire 118th Regiment of the 40th Brigade, one battalion of the 120th Regiment, four companies of the 119th Regiment, the entire 2nd Regiment of the 3rd Rapid Column, a part of the 2nd Traffic Police Corps, and over 1,000 men of the 72nd Division. In addition, in the blocking battles, over 5,000 Chiang bandit troops of the 5th Corps and over 3,000 men of the 83rd Division were killed or wounded and captured. In total, 50,493 enemy troops were killed, wounded, or captured, including the following Chiang bandit officers captured alive: Qu Shounian, Lieutenant General and Commander of the 6th Pacification Zone and 7th Corps, Lin Xixiang, Major General and Chief of Staff of the Corps, Guo Dingyuan, Colonel Artillery Commander, Shen Chengnian, Major General and Division Commander of the 75th Division, Hu Jingqi, Colonel Deputy Chief of Staff, Xun Zongmu, Lieutenant Colonel Deputy Director of the Political Work Department, Li Banghua, Major General Brigade Commander of the 6th Brigade, Shen Tianxiang, Colonel Deputy Brigade Commander, Wang Xuehong, Colonel Acting Chief of Staff, Lu Jishi, Colonel Acting Brigade Commander of the 16th Brigade, Huang Zhengxiang, Colonel Director of the Political Work Office, Li Wenmi, Major General Brigade Commander of the 21st New Brigade, and 29,732 officers and soldiers below them. 20,761 Chiang bandit officers and soldiers were killed or wounded.

    The following were seized: 290 cannons of various calibers (one howitzer, nine field guns, thirty-three mountain guns, eight heavy mortars, eighty-two mortars, 157 60mm mortars), nineteen rocket launchers, 163 grenade launchers, eight flamethrowers, two grenade canisters, two anti-tank rifles, 195 heavy machine guns, 1,020 light machine guns, 711 Thompson, carbine, and Canadian submachine guns, 7,304 rifles and carbines, one landmine detector, four tanks (another three destroyed), twelve radio stations, fifty vehicles, eleven telephone switchboards, and a great deal of other military supplies.

    豫东战役详细战果
    歼敌官兵五万余
    【新华社豫东前线二十八日电】华东人民解放军豫东前线司令部发表公报，公布六月二十七日至七月六日豫东睢（县）?（县）战役详细战果如下：
    歼灭蒋军第七兵团部全部，整七十五师师部全部，及该师所属之第六旅（辖十六、十七、十八团）全部，十六旅（辖四十六、四十七、四十八团）全部，新二十一旅（辖六十一、六十二、六十三团）全部及上述三个旅部，黄伯韬兵团之整二十五师一○八旅三二四团全部及三二三团一个营，四十旅一一八团全部，一二○团一个营，一一九团四个连，第三快速纵队第二团全部，交通警察第二总队一部，及整七十二师一千余人。另在阻击战中，毙伤俘蒋匪整五军五千余人，及整八十三师三千余人，以上共计毙伤俘敌五○四九三人，内生俘蒋匪第六绥靖区副司令兼七兵团中将司令区寿年、兵团少将参谋长林曦祥、上校炮兵指挥官郭定远，整七十五师少将师长沈澄年、上校副参谋长胡旌奇、政工处中校副处长勋宗睦、六旅少将旅长李邦华、上校副旅长沈天翔、上校代参谋长王学宏、十六旅上校代旅长卢济时，上校政工室主任黄政祥。新二十一旅少将旅长李文密等以下官兵二九七三二名，毙伤蒋匪官兵二○七六一名。
    缴获各种口径炮二九○门（榴弹炮一门、野炮九门、山炮三十三门、重迫击炮八门、迫击炮八十二门、六○炮一五七门），火箭筒十九个，掷弹筒一六三个，火焰喷射器八个，枪弹筒二个，战防枪二支，重机枪一九五挺，轻机枪一○二○挺，汤姆、卡宾、加拿大等冲锋枪七一一支，长短枪七三○四支，地雷探索器一个，坦克四辆（另击毁三辆），电台十二部，汽车五十辆，电话总机十一部，及其他军用品甚伙。
