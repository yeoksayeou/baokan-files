Title: Consolidating the Unity of the National Working Class to Accelerate the Construction of New Democracy: Congratulations to the Labor Congress from Various Liberated Areas

Author:

Date: 1948-08-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 5th. The East China Central Bureau of the Communist Party of China, Shandong Provincial Government, SuWan Border Region Government, Shandong Federation of Trade Unions, Military Region, Administrative Office, Federation of Trade Unions, Youth Federation, Women's Federation, and others have sent telegrams to congratulate the opening of the Sixth National Labor Congress in Harbin. The telegram from the East China Central Bureau stated: The convening of the Sixth National Labor Congress is not only a great encouragement to the workers in the liberated areas, but also a powerful impetus to the struggle of the workers in the Kuomintang-controlled areas against American imperialism and the reactionary rule of the Kuomintang. We earnestly hope that the Sixth National Labor Congress and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, under the leadership of its vanguard, the Communist Party of China, will lead the national working class to carry forward the glorious tradition of self-sacrifice and heroic struggle since the "February 7th" strike, consolidate the unity of the national working class, and strive to accelerate the victory of the New Democratic Revolution throughout the country.

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 5th. All commanders and fighters of the East China People's Liberation Army, Commander of the Shandong Corps Xu Shiyou, and Political Commissar Tan Zhenlin, sent telegrams to congratulate the Sixth National Labor Congress in Harbin. The telegram from the East China People's Liberation Army stated: We are the People's Liberation Army led by the Chinese proletariat, and many people in our ranks were workers in the past. Therefore, when we hear that the national workers' representatives are meeting in Harbin, we feel extraordinarily cordial and excited.

The telegram from Xu and Tan pointed out the great historical significance of the conference being held at a time when victory in the War of Liberation is in sight and stated: The opening of the conference coincides with the great festival of the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese working class and the Chinese people's own armed forces - the Chinese People's Liberation Army, which further signifies the fighting spirit of the conference. We celebrate the victory and success of the conference on the distant front lines.

Telegrams of Congratulations from All Circles in Northwest China

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest China, 5th. The Northwest Party, government, and military, as well as the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia and Jin-Sui General Trade Unions, sent telegrams on the 1st to congratulate the Sixth National Labor Congress in Harbin. The Northwest Bureau of the CPC, the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government, and the Political Department of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia-Jin-Sui Joint Defense Command stated in their jointly signed telegram of congratulation: Your conference today will enable the Chinese workers' movement to advance rapidly and achieve complete unity. This is of great significance to the national people's liberation struggle. The telegram from the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia and Jin-Sui General Trade Unions stated: All the workers in our Northwest Liberated Area, under your direct leadership, will unite more closely, carry forward the high enthusiasm for labor and revolutionary heroism, work hard in production, support the war, and unite closely with the working class throughout the country to struggle together and greet the early victory of the National People's Liberation War.

Telegrams of Congratulations from the Central Plains

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 5th. The Central Plains Bureau of the CPC and the Central Plains Military Region of the People's Liberation Army sent telegrams to congratulate the Sixth National Labor Congress, stating: Just as the People's Liberation War has victoriously entered its third year, the convening of the National Congress of Workers and Staff in the Liberated Areas will be a major guarantee for uniting all the Chinese people to defeat American imperialism and its running dog, the Kuomintang reactionaries, and for the victory of the cause of building a New China. We will celebrate the success of the conference by annihilating large numbers of Kuomintang troops and consolidating the newly liberated areas in the Central Plains.

### 巩固全国工人阶级团结加速新民主主义的建设  各解放区纷纷电贺劳动大会

1948-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    巩固全国工人阶级团结加速新民主主义的建设
    各解放区纷纷电贺劳动大会
    华东贺电
    【新华社华东五日电】中共华东中央局，山东省政府，苏皖边区政府，山东职工总会，军区，行政公署，职工联合总会，青联，妇联等顷纷纷电贺在哈尔滨开幕之第六次全国劳动大会。华东中央局之贺电略称：第六次全国劳动大会的召开，不但对解放区工人是一个伟大的鼓舞，而且对国民党统治区工人反对美帝国主义与国民党反动统治的斗争是一个强有力的推动。我们热烈希望第六次全国劳动大会及中华全国总工会在自己先锋队——中国共产党的领导下，率领全国工人阶级发扬“二七”以来自我牺牲、英勇奋斗的光荣传统，巩固全国工人阶级的团结，为加速新民主主义革命在全国胜利而奋斗。
    【新华社华东五日电】华东人民解放军全体指战员及山东兵团司令员许世友、政治委员谭震林，电贺哈尔滨第六次全国劳动大会。华东解放军贺电称：我们是中国无产阶级领导的人民解放军，我们队伍里很多人过去是工人，因此我们听到全国工人代表在哈尔滨开会的时候，感到异常的亲切和兴奋。
    许、谭贺电于指出大会在解放战争胜利在望之际召开的伟大历史意义后称：大会开幕又恰当中国工人阶级与中国人民自己的武装——中国人民解放军建军节二十一周年的伟大节日，这就更意味着大会的战斗精神。我们在遥远的前线庆祝大会的胜利与成功。
    西北各界贺电
    【新华社西北五日电】西北党、政、军暨陕甘宁、晋绥总工会于一日电贺哈尔滨全国第六次劳动大会。中共西北局、陕甘宁边区政府、陕甘宁晋绥联防军区司令部政治部在联署的贺电中称：你们今天的大会，将使中国工人运动迅速向前迈进，和达到完全的统一。这对全国人民解放斗争实有重大意义。陕甘宁、晋绥总工会的贺电略称：我们西北解放区的全体工人在你们直接领导下，将更加团结一致，发扬高度的劳动热忱和革命的英雄主义，努力生产，支援战争，并与全国的工人阶级密切地团结起来，共同奋斗，迎接全国人民解放战争的早日胜利。
    中原贺电
    【新华社中原五日电】中共中原中央局及解放军中原军区顷电贺第六次劳动大会称：正当人民解放战争胜利地进入第三年，全国解放区职工代表大会之召开，对于团结全中国人民战胜美帝国主义及其走狗国民党反动派、建设新中国的事业的胜利，将是一个重大的保证，我们将以大量歼灭国民党军队、巩固中原新解放区来祝贺大会成功。
