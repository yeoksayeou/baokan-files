Title: Yuwansu Recovers Yingshang Pinggu Line, Recaptures Shixia; Northern Jiangsu's Huangqiao and Other Areas Annihilate Over 800 Enemies
Author:
Date: 1948-08-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 27th: (Delayed News) A certain unit of the Yuwansu People's Liberation Army recovered Yingshang County in northern Anhui on the 10th. They killed or wounded 221 enemies below the battalion commander level and captured 354. They seized 9 light and heavy machine guns, 2 grenade launchers, and 49 various guns. After the recovery of Fuyang, three regiments of the Kuomintang Anhui Security Corps, the puppet Yingshang Self-Defense Regiment, and seven puppet county governments of Yingshang, Fuyang, Taihe, Woyang, Shenqiu, Xiangcheng, and Linquan gathered in the city. This time they were dealt another heavy blow.

Xinhua News Agency, North China, 21st: (Delayed) On the evening of the 15th, a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army on the Pinggu front once again liberated the important stronghold of Shixia Town on the Pinggu road and Xiaoying Town south of Shixia. The remaining enemies fled towards Gubeikou and Miyun.

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 26th: A part of the local armed forces of the First Military Subdistrict of the Northern Jiangsu Military Region of Central China (administering seven counties including Dongtai, Rugao, and Taixing) captured the enemy strongholds of Erjia and Bahuzhuang on the Jijiashi-Huangqiao Highway (both places are east of Taixing) on the 10th and 11th, completely annihilating the defending enemies; and defeated the enemies who harassed from the north and south from Huangqiao and Jijiashi, eliminating a part of them. In the two battles, a total of more than 570 officers and soldiers of the 23rd Temporary Division and local Kuomintang troops were killed or wounded, 250 enemies were captured alive, and 4 mortars and 60 mortars, 8 grenade launchers, 13 light and heavy machine guns, 2 Thompson submachine guns, and 223 rifles and carbines were seized.

[Another Dispatch] The military and civilians on the Ru (gao)-Xin (shenggang) and Nan (tong)-Ru (gao) lines have persisted in the anti-"mopping up" struggle since the end of June. Within a month, they annihilated more than 200 officers and soldiers of the Kuomintang's 21st Division and other units, captured 1 60 mortar, 4 grenade launchers, 8 light and heavy machine guns, 2 Canadian-style rifles, and more than 40 rifles. Furthermore, under our political offensive, more than 200 enemy soldiers surrendered to us one after another. The enemy strongholds such as Qiujia Kiln were also forced to retreat by us.

### 豫皖苏收复颍上平古线再克石匣　苏北黄桥等地歼敌八百多

1948-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　豫皖苏收复颍上平古线再克石匣
  　苏北黄桥等地歼敌八百多
    【新华社中原二十七日电】迟到消息：豫皖苏解放军某部于十日收复皖北颍上县城。计毙伤敌营长以下二百二十一人，俘三百五十四人。缴获轻重机枪九挺，掷弹筒二个，各种枪四九一支。按自阜阳收复后，国民党安徽保安团三个团，伪颍上自卫团及颍上、阜阳、太和、涡阳、沈邱、项城、临泉等七个伪县政府均麇集该城。此次乃又遭痛击。
    【新华社华北二十一日电】（迟到）平古前线解放军某部于十五日晚，再次解放平古路上重要据点石匣镇及石匣南之小营镇，残敌分向古北口及密云逃窜。
    【新华社华东二十六日电】华中苏北军区一分区（辖东台、如皋、泰兴等七县）地方武装一部，于十、十一两日攻克季家市黄桥公路（两地均在泰兴以东）之二甲、八户庄敌据点，全歼守敌；并击溃自黄桥、季家市分由南北出扰之敌，消灭其一部。两次战斗共毙伤敌暂二十三师及土著国民党军五百七十余名，生俘敌二百五十名，缴获迫击炮、六零炮共四门，掷弹筒、枪榴筒共八个，轻重机枪十三挺，汤姆式二支，长短枪二百二十三支。
    【又讯】如（皋）新（生港）、南（通）如（皋）两线军民，自六月底起坚持反“扫荡”斗争。一个月内歼国民党军整二十一师等部二百余人，缴获六零炮一门，掷弹筒四个，轻重机枪八挺，加拿大式二支，步枪四十余支。又在我政治攻势下，先后有二百余名敌军向我投诚。邱家窑等敌据点亦被我迫退。
