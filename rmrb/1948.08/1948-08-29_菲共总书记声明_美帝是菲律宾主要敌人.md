Title: Philippine Communist Party General Secretary Declares: US Imperialism is the Main Enemy of the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1948-08-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, August 25th: "US imperialism is the main enemy of the Philippines," stressed Philippine Communist Party General Secretary Balgos in a statement to the press. According to a Reuters dispatch from Manila on the 21st: Balgos severely criticized President Quirino of the Philippines for his recent deception of the youth, namely the drivel that the enemies of the Philippine people exist domestically and not from abroad. Balgos pointed out: If there are enemies within the Philippines, "then they are the feudal cliques who want to maintain the outdated tenancy system forever, and the ruling clique of the Liberal Party (the government party) who act according to the orders of the American imperialists and their allies."

### 菲共总书记声明　美帝是菲律宾主要敌人

1948-08-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　菲共总书记声明
  　美帝是菲律宾主要敌人
    【新华社陕北二十五日电】马尼拉讯：“美帝国主义乃是菲律宾的主要敌人”，这是菲律宾共产党总书记巴尔哥斯向报界发表声明时强调指出的。据路透社二十一日消息：巴尔哥斯在声明中严厉抨击菲总统季里诺最近对青年所作的欺骗，即菲律宾人民的敌人存在于国内而并非来自外国的这一呓语。巴尔哥斯指出：如果说菲律宾国内有着敌人的话，“那么他们就是想要永远保持过了时的租佃制度的封建集团，以及遵奉美国帝国主义者及其同盟者的命令而行动的自由党（政府党）统治集团。”
