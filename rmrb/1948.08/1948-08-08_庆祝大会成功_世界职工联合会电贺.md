Title: Successful Celebration Meeting - World Federation of Trade Unions Sends Congratulatory Message
Author:
Date: 1948-08-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 5th. The General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Saillant, on behalf of the World Federation of Trade Unions, sent a congratulatory message to the Sixth National Labor Congress of China. The original telegram is as follows:

    "On the occasion of your National Congress of Trade Union Representatives being held in Harbin, the World Federation of Trade Unions extends fraternal greetings to all Chinese male and female workers!

    The World Federation of Trade Unions is very pleased with the rapid development of the Chinese Trade Union and its remarkably successful activities. We celebrate the success of the congress and the victory of the common cause of workers worldwide."

### 庆祝大会成功  世界职工联合会电贺

1948-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝大会成功
    世界职工联合会电贺
    【新华社东北五日电】世界职工联合会总书记赛扬顷代表世界职工联合会电贺我国第六次全国劳动大会，原电如下：
    “今值你们在哈尔滨举行全国职工代表大会，世界职工联合会特向你们全中国男女工友致以兄弟的敬礼！
    世界职工联合会很高兴中国职工会的迅速发展与其成绩卓著的活动。我们庆祝大会的成功与全世界工人共同事业的胜利。”
