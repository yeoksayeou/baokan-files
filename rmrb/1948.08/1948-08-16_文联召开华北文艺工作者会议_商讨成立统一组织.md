Title: Literary Federation Convenes North China Literary Workers Conference to Discuss Establishing a Unified Organization
Author:
Date: 1948-08-16
Page: 1

　　Literary Federation Convenes North China Literary Workers Conference
  　Discussing the Establishment of a Unified Organization
    [This Newspaper's News] The Standing Committee of the Literary Federation of the Shanxi-Hebei-Shandong-Henan and Shanxi-Chahar-Hebei Regions jointly convened the North China Literary Workers Conference on the 8th of this month to discuss the merger of the Literary Federations of the two regions and the establishment of a unified organization for the literary circles of North China.
    In addition, the Literary Federations of the two regions have unanimously elected Fan Wenlan, Cheng Fangwu, Zhou Yang, Ai Siqi, He Luting, and Shakoff as representatives of the cultural circles to attend the North China Provisional People's Representative Conference.

### 文联召开华北文艺工作者会议　商讨成立统一组织

1948-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　文联召开华北文艺工作者会议
  　商讨成立统一组织
    【本报讯】晋冀鲁豫与晋察冀两区文联常务理事会，于本月八日联名召开华北文艺工作者会议，商讨两区文联合并及成立统一的华北文艺界组织的问题。
    又，两区文联已一致推选范文澜、成仿吾、周扬、艾思奇、贺绿汀、沙可夫等六人为出席华北临时人民代表大会文化界代表。
