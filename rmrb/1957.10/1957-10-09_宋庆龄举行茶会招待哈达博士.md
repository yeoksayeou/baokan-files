Title: Soong Ching Ling Hosts Tea Party for Dr. Hatta
Author:
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 8th October. Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, held a tea party at her residence in Shanghai this afternoon to welcome former Indonesian Vice-President Dr. Hatta and his wife, as well as their entourage.

    On the lawn in the garden, Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling and Dr. Hatta and others chatted cordially while watching the song and dance performances of the China Welfare Institute Children's Art Theatre and the Young Pioneers Art Troupe, which were founded by Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling. At the end of the tea party, Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling presented gifts to the distinguished guests.

    The Indonesian Ambassador to China, Mr. and Mrs. Wirjopranoto, also attended the tea party.

    Among those present at the tea party were Bao Erhan, Vice-chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, Qu Wu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Cao Diqiu, Song Richang, and Jin Zhonghua, Vice-Mayors of Shanghai.

### 宋庆龄举行茶会招待哈达博士

1957-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄举行茶会招待哈达博士
    新华社上海8日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄，今天下午在上海她的寓所里举行茶会，欢迎印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士和夫人以及随行人员。
    在花园草坪上，宋庆龄副委员长和哈达博士等一边亲切交谈，一边观看宋庆龄副委员长创办的中国福利会儿童艺术剧院和少年宫小伙伴艺术团的歌舞表演。茶会结束时，宋庆龄副委员长向贵宾们赠送了礼品。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托夫妇也出席了茶会。
    出席茶会作陪的，有政协全国委员会副主席、中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长屈武，上海市副市长曹荻秋、宋日昌、金仲华等。
