Title: Soviet Union Publishes Popular Science Book Introducing the Science of Interstellar Travel
Author:
Date: 1957-10-22
Page: 5

    The Moscow Foreign Languages Publishing House recently published an English version of Soviet scientist Kirensin's "Journey to Distant Worlds."

    In the book, the author recounts the history of the emergence and development of liquid-fuel jet engines, which opened the way for human space travel. He engagingly describes the principles of jet flight in outer space and vividly depicts the possible conditions for human travel to the Moon and other planets in the solar system. People can read with great interest the chapters introducing artificial Earth satellites.

### 苏联出版通俗读物介绍星际交通科学

1957-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联出版通俗读物介绍星际交通科学
    莫斯科外文书籍出版局最近出版了苏联科学家基列辛的“到遥远的世界去旅行”的英文本。
    作者在书中历述为人类开辟了宇宙旅行的道路的液态燃料喷气发动机的发生和发展的历史。他引人入胜地叙述了在宇宙空间进行喷气飞行的原理，并通俗地描写了人类到月亮及太阳系其他行星上去旅行的可能条件。人们可以很有兴趣地读到介绍人造地球卫星的篇章。
