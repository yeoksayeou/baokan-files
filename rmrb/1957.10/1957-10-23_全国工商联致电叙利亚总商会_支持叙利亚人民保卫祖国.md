Title: All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Sends a Telegram to the Syrian Chamber of Commerce to Support the Syrian People in Defending Their Motherland
Author:
Date: 1957-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd -- The All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce sent a telegram to the Syrian Chamber of Commerce on the 22nd, expressing its support for the Syrian people's struggle to defend their national independence and safeguard world peace.

The telegram stated, "The Chinese business community angrily condemns the US imperialists' conspiracy to incite Turkey to launch a war of aggression against Syria. We firmly support the Syrian business community in joining with people from all walks of life throughout the country in their struggle to defend national independence and safeguard world peace. We are confident that your heroic and just struggle will achieve final victory."

### 全国工商联致电叙利亚总商会  支持叙利亚人民保卫祖国

1957-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工商联致电叙利亚总商会
    支持叙利亚人民保卫祖国
    新华社22日讯　中华全国工商业联合会22日致电叙利亚总商会，支持叙利亚人民为保卫祖国独立及维护世界和平而斗争。
    电报说，“中国工商界愤怒地谴责美帝国主义煽动土耳其对叙利亚进行侵略战争的阴谋。我们坚决支持叙利亚的工商界与全国其他各界人士一起为保卫祖国独立及维护世界和平而斗争。我们确信你们英勇正义的斗争一定会获得最后的胜利。”
