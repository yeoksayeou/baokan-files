Title: Macmillan Replies to Kishi Nobusuke, Refusing to Stop Nuclear Tests
Author:
Date: 1957-10-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 15th - British Prime Minister Macmillan has rejected Japanese Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi's request to halt nuclear testing before continuing negotiations on other disarmament issues.

Macmillan's telegram insisted that a halt to nuclear testing must be linked to extremely complex issues such as stopping the production of fissile materials for weapons and reducing armed forces and conventional armaments under international supervision.

### 麦克米伦复电岸信介拒绝停止核试验

1957-10-16
第6版()
专栏：

    麦克米伦复电岸信介拒绝停止核试验
    据新华社15日讯　伦敦消息：英国首相麦克米伦拒绝了日本首相岸信介关于先停止核试验再继续谈判裁军的其他问题的要求。
    麦克米伦的电报坚持中止核试验必须同停止生产用于武器的裂变物质和在国际监督下裁减武装部队和常规军备等极为复杂的问题联系在一起。
