Title: Daud Shah Arrives in Rangoon En Route to China

Author:
Date: 1957-10-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 21st - Afghan Prime Minister Daud and his party arrived in Rangoon at 8 a.m. today by special plane en route to a visit to the People's Republic of China.

Those welcoming him at the airport included Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Deputy Prime Minister Zaw Khun Thio, and ministers.

The Afghan Prime Minister inspected the guard of honor at the airport and received flowers from Burmese girls.

During his one-day stay in Rangoon, Daud will visit the Aung San Mausoleum. He will call on Prime Minister U Nu and hold informal talks with U Nu. Burmese President U Win Maung will host a banquet at the Presidential Palace tonight to welcome him. Prime Minister Daud will leave Rangoon for Kunming on the 22nd.

### 达乌德首相访华途中抵仰光

1957-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    达乌德首相访华途中抵仰光
    新华社仰光21日电　阿富汗首相达乌德和他的一行在前往中华人民共和国访问途中今天上午八时乘专机到达仰光。
    在机场欢迎的有：缅甸总理吴努、副总理藻昆卓和部长们。
    阿富汗首相在机场上检阅了仪仗队，并接受了缅甸姑娘的献花。
    达乌德在仰光停留的一天中将拜谒昂山墓。他将拜会吴努总理，并且同吴努举行非正式会谈。缅甸总统吴温貌定今晚在总统府举行宴会欢迎他。达乌德首相将在22日离开仰光前往昆明。
