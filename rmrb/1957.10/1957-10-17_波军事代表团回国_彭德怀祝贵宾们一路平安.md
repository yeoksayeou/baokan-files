Title: Polish Military Delegation Returns Home; Peng Dehuai Wishes the Distinguished Guests a Safe Journey

Author:

Date: 1957-10-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The Polish military delegation, headed by Polish People's Republic Minister of National Defense General Marian Spychalski, concluded its visit to China and returned home from Beijing by special plane this afternoon.

　　General Spychalski, accompanied by Marshal Peng Dehuai, reviewed the guard of honor.

　　A farewell ceremony was then held at the airport.

　　Marshal Peng Dehuai said in his speech that the visit of the Polish military delegation to China was a major event in the history of the development of friendly relations between the armed forces of China and Poland. He wished General Spychalski and all members of the delegation a safe and pleasant journey.

　　General Spychalski bid farewell to those who came to see them off. He said that China and Poland are both members of the socialist family. The relations between China and Poland are based on cooperation, mutual assistance, and the Leninist principle of internationalism. He said that all socialist countries are with China, and world public opinion is increasingly sympathetic to and supports China.

### 波军事代表团回国  彭德怀祝贵宾们一路平安

1957-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　波军事代表团回国
    彭德怀祝贵宾们一路平安
    据新华社16日讯　以波兰人民共和国国防部长斯彼哈尔斯基上将为首的波兰军事代表团结束了在中国的访问，今天下午从北京乘专机回国。
    斯彼哈尔斯基上将在彭德怀元帅陪同下检阅了仪仗队。
    接着，在飞机场上举行了欢送仪式。
    彭德怀元帅讲话说，波兰军事代表团来中国的访问，是中波两国军队友好关系发展史上的重大事件。他祝斯彼哈尔斯基上将和代表团的全体人员一路平安，旅途愉快。
    斯彼哈尔斯基上将向送行的人告别，他讲话说，中国和波兰都是社会主义大家庭的一员。中波两国的关系是建立在合作、互助和列宁的国际主义原则上的。他说，所有的社会主义国家跟中国在一起，世界舆论也愈来愈同情和支持中国。
