Title: Sino-Afghan Joint Communiqué Signed
Author:
Date: 1957-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 26th. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammed Daoud, signed a joint communiqué at 7:20 PM on the 26th. The signing ceremony was held in Ziguang Pavilion, Zhongnanhai.

The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long and Chen Yi, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Ding Guoyu, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wenjin, and Acting Director of the Protocol Department Wang Yutian.

The Afghan side participating in the signing ceremony included Acting Minister of Education of the Kingdom of Afghanistan Popal, Afghan Ambassador to China Samad, Director-General of the Political Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Pazhwak, Deputy Director of the News Agency Shaliza, Chairman of the Afghan Olympic Federation Salaji, Director of Kabul Radio Station Bennawa, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Aziz.

After the signing, both sides raised their glasses in mutual congratulations. (Attached pictures)

### 中阿联合公报签字

1957-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中阿联合公报签字
    新华社26日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德，26日晚间七时二十分签署了联合公报。签字仪式是在中南海紫光阁举行的。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰，外交部亚洲司司长章文晋，礼宾司代司长王雨田。
    阿富汗方面参加签字仪式的，有阿富汗王国代理教育大臣波帕尔，阿富汗驻华大使萨马德，外交部总政治司司长帕日瓦克，新闻署副署长沙利扎，阿富汗奥林匹克联合会主席萨拉季，喀布尔广播电台台长本纳瓦，外交部礼宾司副司长阿齐兹。
    在签字以后，双方举杯互相祝贺。（附图片）
