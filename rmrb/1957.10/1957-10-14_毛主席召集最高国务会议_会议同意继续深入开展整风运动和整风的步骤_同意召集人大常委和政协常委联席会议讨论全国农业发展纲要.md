Title: Chairman Mao Convenes Supreme State Conference; Conference Agrees to Continue Deepening the Rectification Movement and the Steps for Rectification; Agrees to Convene a Joint Meeting of the NPC Standing Committee and the CPPCC Standing Committee to Discuss the National Agricultural Development Program

Author:
Date: 1957-10-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 13th: On October 13th, Chairman Mao Zedong convened the Supreme State Conference to discuss the rectification issue and the National Agricultural Development Program. Those present included Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and responsible personnel from the Communist Party, various democratic parties, non-party democratic figures, and people's organizations, totaling sixty-one people. The conference agreed to continue deepening the rectification movement and the steps for deeply carrying out the rectification movement. Regarding the revised draft of the National Agricultural Development Program, The conference agreed to convene the joint meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference for discussion; after the joint meeting's discussion, it will be submitted to the people of the whole country for discussion.

### 毛主席召集最高国务会议  会议同意继续深入开展整风运动和整风的步骤  同意召集人大常委和政协常委联席会议讨论全国农业发展纲要

1957-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席召集最高国务会议
    会议同意继续深入开展整风运动和整风的步骤
    同意召集人大常委和政协常委联席会议讨论全国农业发展纲要
    新华社13日讯　10月13日，毛泽东主席召集了最高国务会议，讨论整风问题和全国农业发展纲要问题。到会的有朱德副主席，刘少奇委员长，周恩来总理及共产党、各民主党派、无党派民主人士和人民团体的负责人员共六十一人。会议同意继续深入开展整风运动和深入开展整风运动的步骤。对于修改后的全国农业发展纲要草案，会议同意召集全国人民代表大会常务委员会和人民政协全国委员会常务委员会的联席会议加以讨论；在联席会议讨论之后，提到全国人民中讨论。
