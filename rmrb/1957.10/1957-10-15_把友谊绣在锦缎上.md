Title: Embroidering Friendship on Brocade

Author:

Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 3

　　Embroidering Friendship on Brocade

　　The entire class of Zoya in the Shamian Street Primary School in the Central District of Guangzhou is rushing to embroider a piece of embroidery featuring Tiananmen Square, the Kremlin, and the words "The unbreakable friendship between China and the Soviet Union," which they plan to send to the Karl Liebknecht Pioneer Team in Sverdlovsk Oblast, Soviet Union, as a congratulatory gift for the October Revolution Day.

　　The friendly correspondence between the Zoya class and the Karl Liebknecht Pioneer Team has lasted for more than a year. They exchange gifts as souvenirs on every holiday. (Attached picture)

### 把友谊绣在锦缎上

1957-10-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　把友谊绣在锦缎上
    广州市中区沙面街小学卓娅班全体同学，正在赶绣一幅有天安门、克里姆林宫和“中苏两国牢不可破的友谊”字样的刺绣品，准备在十月革命节前寄给苏联斯威尔德州卡尔巴茨也少先队，作为友谊的祝贺。
    卓娅班和卡尔巴茨也少先队的友谊通讯已经一年多了。每逢节日，都互赠礼物作纪念。（附图片）
　　把友谊绣在锦缎上。　　　新华社记者　蔡忠植摄
