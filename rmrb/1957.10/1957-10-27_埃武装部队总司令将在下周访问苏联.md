Title: Egyptian Armed Forces Commander-in-Chief to Visit Soviet Union Next Week
Author:
Date: 1957-10-27
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 25th: According to a report in "The Republic" on the 24th, Egyptian Armed Forces Commander-in-Chief General Amer, at the invitation of Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov, will visit the Soviet Union next week.

### 埃武装部队总司令将在下周访问苏联

1957-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    埃武装部队总司令将在下周访问苏联
    新华社开罗25日电　据“共和国报”24日报道，埃及武装部队总司令阿密尔将军应苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅的邀请，将在下星期去苏联访问。
