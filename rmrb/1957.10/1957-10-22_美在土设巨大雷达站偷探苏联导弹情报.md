Title: US Establishes Huge Radar Station in Turkey to Steal Soviet Missile Intelligence
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 21st: Washington Dispatch: "Aviation Week" revealed on the 20th that the United States has established a huge radar intelligence station in Turkey. This radar station is located near the Black Sea resort of Samsun, and its purpose is to "detect Russian intermediate-range or longer-range missiles launched from the main missile test center in Krasnoyarsk."

### 美在土设巨大雷达站偷探苏联导弹情报

1957-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

    美在土设巨大雷达站偷探苏联导弹情报
    据新华社21日讯　华盛顿消息：美国“航空周刊”20日透露一个消息说：美国在土耳其设有一个规模巨大的雷达情报站。这个雷达站设在黑海萨姆松休养胜地附近，用途是“探测俄国在克拉斯诺雅主要的导弹试验中心所发射的不论是中程或射程更远的导弹”。
