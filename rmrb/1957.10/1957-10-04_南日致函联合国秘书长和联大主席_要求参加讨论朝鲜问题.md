Title: Nam Il Sends Letter to UN Secretary-General and President of the General Assembly Requesting Participation in Discussions on the Korean Question
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 3rd – According to a news release issued this afternoon by the Press Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Foreign Minister Nam Il today wrote to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld and the President of the Twelfth Session of the UN General Assembly, Mr. Mongi Slim, stating that the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea once again requests the United Nations to invite its representatives to participate in the discussions on the Korean question at the Twelfth Session. The letter stated that any issue concerning the interests of the Korean people cannot be fairly discussed without the participation of representatives from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 南日致函联合国秘书长和联大主席  要求参加讨论朝鲜问题

1957-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    南日致函联合国秘书长和联大主席
    要求参加讨论朝鲜问题
    据新华社平壤3日电  据朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省出版报道部今天下午发布的消息，南日外务相今天写信给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德和联合国大会第十二届会议主席孟罗说，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府再一次要求联合国邀请它的代表参加第十二届会议关于朝鲜问题的讨论。信中说，任何同朝鲜人民的利益有关的问题，如果没有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的代表参加，就不能进行公正的讨论。
