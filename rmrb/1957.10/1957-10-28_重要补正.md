    Important Correction
    In the third paragraph of this newspaper's editorial on the 27th, "The Great Program for Building a Socialist Countryside": "However, in order to develop industry more rapidly and smoothly, greater efforts must be made to develop agriculture at present." should be changed to "However, in order to develop industry more rapidly and smoothly, at present, on the basis of continuing to unswervingly prioritize the development of heavy industry, greater efforts than in the past must be made to develop agriculture."

### 重要补正

1957-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    重要补正
    本报27日社论“建设社会主义农村的伟大纲领”的第三段中：“可是，为了更迅速、更顺利地发展工业，在目前必须用更大的力量发展农业。”应为“可是，为了更迅速、更顺利地发展工业，目前必须在继续坚定不移地优先发展重工业的基础上，用比过去更大的力量发展农业。”
