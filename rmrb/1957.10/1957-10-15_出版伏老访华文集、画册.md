Title: Publication of Collected Works and Album on Voroshilov's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 8

The "Eternal Friendship – Collection of Works on Chairman Voroshilov's Visit to China," a compilation of important speeches and addresses made by Chairman Voroshilov during his visit to China, will be published by the People's Publishing House within this month. The book also includes a brief introduction to Voroshilov's life written by Soviet Professor Rakin.

The album "Voroshilov in China," compiled by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, will also be published by the Shanghai People's Fine Arts Publishing House in early November.

### 出版伏老访华文集、画册

1957-10-15
第8版()
专栏：

　　出版伏老访华文集、画册
    汇集伏罗希洛夫主席访问中国期间的重要讲话、演说编成的“永恒的友谊——伏罗希洛夫主席访问中国文集”，将于本月内由人民出版社出版。书中还选入了苏联拉金教授写的一篇有关伏老生平的简介。
    中苏友协总会编成的“伏罗希洛夫在中国”画册，也将由上海人民美术出版社于11月初出版。
