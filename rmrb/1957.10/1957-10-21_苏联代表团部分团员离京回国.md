Title: Part of the Soviet Delegation Departs Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1957-10-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 20th: Part of the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 19th to return home.

Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and many members of the Standing Committee saw them off at the airport. Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, was also at the airport to see them off.

### 苏联代表团部分团员离京回国

1957-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团部分团员离京回国
    据新华社20日讯  苏联最高苏维埃代表团部分团员在19日早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场送行的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、郭沫若和常务委员多人。苏联驻华大使尤金也到机场送行。
