Title: A Large Gold Mine to Begin Production
Author:
Date: 1957-10-30
Page: 4

    A Large Gold Mine to Begin Production
    (Reported by this newspaper) The Daming Mountain Gold Mine, directly under a county of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture, Guangxi Province's third-largest gold mine, is under active construction. It is expected to be put into production in November of this year.
    Guangxi Province currently has two larger gold mines, Tianyang and Southeast. More than thirty counties in the province have gold resources. The working people in the mining areas have traditionally had the habit and experience of mining gold, especially panning for gold.

### 一个大金矿将投入生产

1957-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

    一个大金矿将投入生产
    本报讯　广西省第三个较大的黄金矿场——桂西僮族自治州直属县的大明山金矿，正在积极筹建。预计在今年11月间可以投入生产。
    广西省现有田阳、东南两个较大的金矿。全省有三十多个县有黄金资源。矿区的劳动人民历来就有开采黄金特别是淘沙金的习惯和经验。
