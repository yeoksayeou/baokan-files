Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-10-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd October: The Government Delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, headed by Chairman Yugov, and accompanied by Zeng Yongquan and Zhou Zhuan, left Beijing by special plane this morning to visit Shanghai.
    Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Vice Minister Zhang Wentian saw them off at the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 2nd October: The Government Delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, headed by Chairman Yugov, arrived in Shanghai today. This afternoon, the delegation, accompanied by Cao Diqiu and Zeng Yongquan, toured the city, visited the Shanghai Museum and the Shanghai Export Commodities Exhibition Hall, and ascended the roof of the Shanghai Mansion on the Bund to overlook the panoramic view of Shanghai. In the evening, the delegation visited the Children's Palace. In the evening, Vice Mayor Cao Diqiu hosted a banquet to welcome Chairman Yugov and all members of the delegation.

### 保政府代表团到上海参观

1957-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    保政府代表团到上海参观
    新华社2日讯  以于哥夫主席为首的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团，由曾涌泉、周竹安陪同，今天上午乘专机离开北京去上海参观。
    李先念副总理和张闻天副部长等到机场送行。
    据新华社上海2日电  以于哥夫主席为首的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团，今天到上海。当天下午，代表团由曹荻秋、曾涌泉陪同游览了市区，参观了上海博物馆和上海出口商品陈列厅，并登上黄浦江边的上海大厦屋顶鸟瞰上海全景。当晚，代表团访问了少年宫。晚上，曹荻秋副市长设宴欢迎于哥夫主席和代表团全体人员。
