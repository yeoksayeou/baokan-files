Title: Endless Affection
Author: Fu Shi
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 8

　　Endless Affection
　　Fu Shi
　　I sing of the bridge in so many lines,
　　The endless affection flies back to the Yangtze,
　　Please let me live on the bridge for a year,
　　I will pour five tons of emotion on every inch of the bridge!
　　Don't be afraid that my emotions will overwhelm the bridge,
　　Soviet experts have poured ten billion tons of steel of friendship into the bridge foundation;
　　No matter how violent the earthquake, the bridge will not pay attention,
　　The biggest typhoon—it will also be in vain to be crazy here!
　　I ask: What if all traffic suddenly stops,
　　And the bridge is crowded with vehicles and pedestrians, leaving no space,
　　Would the bridge take a breath?
　　The bridge smiled at me slightly,
　　I ran happily onto the bridge corridor with a flushed face.

### 情意不尽
俯拾
1957-10-15
第8版()
专栏：

　　情意不尽
　　俯拾
　　我歌唱大桥多少行，
　　唱不尽的情意又飞回长江，
　　请你让我在桥上住上一年，
　　我把大桥的每一寸都倾上五吨情感！
　　不要害怕我的情感压垮了桥，
　　苏联专家已为桥基倾下了友情的十亿吨钢；
　　任是激烈的地震大桥也不理会，
　　最大的台风——到此也白作疯狂！
　　我问：要是一下子停止来往，
　　桥上车辆行人挤的不留一点空白地方，
　　大桥啊会不会喘一口气？
　　大桥对我微微一笑，
　　我喜的满面红光跑上桥廊。
