Title: China and Morocco Determine Items for Exchange of Goods
Author:
Date: 1957-10-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, dated 14th: After the Moroccan trade delegation arrived in Beijing on September 23rd, they fully exchanged opinions with relevant Chinese parties on developing trade between the two sides and determined the items to be exchanged between the two sides in the coming year. The goods determined for export from Morocco are: superphosphate fertilizer, phosphate, frozen mutton, spice oil, cotton, wheat, canned sardines, olive oil, etc. The goods to be exported from China are: tea, cotton cloth, machinery and equipment, silk, etc.

During their stay in China, the Moroccan trade delegation visited factories, schools, and suburban rural areas in Beijing, and also visited Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places.

The delegation left Beijing for home on the 14th. Before their departure, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, hosted a farewell banquet.

### 中国和摩洛哥确定交换商品的项目

1957-10-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国和摩洛哥确定交换商品的项目
    新华社14日讯　摩洛哥贸易代表团在9月23日到北京后，同中国有关方面充分地交换了发展双方贸易的意见，并确定了今后一年内双方交换的商品项目。确定从摩洛哥出口的商品是：过磷酸肥、磷酸盐、冻羊肉、香料油、棉花、小麦、沙丁鱼罐头、橄榄油等。从中国出口的商品是：茶叶、棉布、机器设备、丝绸等。
    摩洛哥贸易代表团在中国期间，曾参观了北京的工厂、学校和郊区农村，并访问了上海、杭州等地。
    代表团已在14日离开北京回国。行前对外贸易部副部长雷任民曾设宴欢送。
