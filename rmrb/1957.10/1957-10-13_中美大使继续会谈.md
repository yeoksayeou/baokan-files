Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1957-10-13
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, October 10th. The Sino-U.S. ambassadors held their 71st meeting here today. The meeting began at 10:00 AM and ended at 11:20 AM. The next meeting is scheduled for November 14th.

### 中美大使继续会谈

1957-10-13
第3版()
专栏：

    中美大使继续会谈
    新华社日内瓦10日电　中美两国大使10日在这里举行了第七十一次会谈。会谈十点钟开始，十一点二十分结束。下次会谈订11月14日举行。
