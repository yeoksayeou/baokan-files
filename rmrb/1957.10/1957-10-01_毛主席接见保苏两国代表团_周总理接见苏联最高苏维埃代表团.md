Title: Chairman Mao Receives Bulgarian and Soviet Delegations, Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Chairman Mao Zedong received the Bulgarian People's Republic Government Delegation headed by Chairman Yugov this evening.
　　Present at the reception were: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Deng Xiaoping, Vice Minister Zeng Yongquan, and Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria Zhou Zhu'an.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Chairman Mao Zedong received the Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation headed by Aristov this evening.
　　Present at the reception were: Peng Zhen, Wang Shoudao, Zeng Yongquan, Wu Kejian, Yu Xinqing, and Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th.
　　Premier Zhou Enlai received the Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation headed by Aristov this afternoon.
　　Also present at the reception were Vice Premier Ulanhu, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, and Wu Kejian.
　　Ambassador Yudin was also present at the reception. (Photos attached)
　　Chairman Mao receives the entire delegation of the Bulgarian Government. The fifth person from the left in the front row is Yugov.
　　Xinhua News Agency reporters Hou Bo and Zou Jiandong
　　Chairman Mao receives the entire delegation of the Soviet Supreme Soviet. The fifth person from the left in the front row is Aristov. Xinhua News Agency reporters Hou Bo and Zou Jiandong

### 毛主席接见保苏两国代表团  周总理接见苏联最高苏维埃代表团

1957-10-01
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见保苏两国代表团
    周总理接见苏联最高苏维埃代表团
    新华社30日讯  毛泽东主席在今天晚间接见了以于哥夫主席为首的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团。
    接见时在座的有：周恩来总理，邓小平副总理，曾涌泉副部长和中国驻保加利亚大使周竹安。
    新华社30日讯  毛泽东主席在今天晚间接见了以阿里斯托夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团。
    接见的时候在座的有：彭真、王首道、曾涌泉、吴克坚、余心清和苏联驻中国大使尤金。
    新华社30日讯
  周恩来总理今天下午接见了以阿里斯托夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团。
    接见时，乌兰夫副总理、习仲勋秘书长、张闻天副部长和吴克坚也在座。
    接见时在座的还有尤金大使。（附图片）
    毛主席接见保政府代表团全体人员。前排左起第五人是于哥夫。
