Title: Yugoslav, Czechoslovak Parliamentary Delegations Visit the Great Wall, Ming Tombs
Author:
Date: 1957-10-03
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch - The Yugoslav Federal National Assembly Delegation, the Czechoslovak National Assembly Delegation, and the Prague Municipal Central People's Committee Delegation today visited the Great Wall and the Ming Tombs.
    The distinguished guests were in high spirits, climbing the highest peak of the Great Wall at Badaling, overlooking the precipitous mountains and rivers. They walked and talked, warmly greeting the tourists they met, and took pictures together.
    The Prague Municipal Central People's Committee Delegation watched the Sino-Czech tennis friendship match in the afternoon.

### 南、捷议会代表团游览长城、明十三陵

1957-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    南、捷议会代表团游览长城、明十三陵
    据新华社2日讯  南斯拉夫联邦国民议会代表团、捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团和布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团今天游览了长城和明十三陵。
    贵宾们游兴极浓，他们登临万里长城的最高峰八达岭，眺望险峻的山川形势。他们边走边谈，向遇见的游客们亲热问候，并合影留念。
    布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团下午观看了中捷网球友谊赛。
