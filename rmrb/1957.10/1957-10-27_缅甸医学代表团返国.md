Title: Burmese Medical Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-10-27
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 26th -- The Burmese medical delegation of five, headed by U Win Na, a well-known Burmese physician and surgeon, left Guangzhou this morning to return home.

### 缅甸医学代表团返国

1957-10-27
第3版()
专栏：

    缅甸医学代表团返国
    新华社广州26日电　以缅甸知名内外科医生温那为首的缅甸医学代表团一行五人，今晨离广州回国。
