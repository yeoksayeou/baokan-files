Title: The Rectification Movement is in Full Swing (Pictures)
Author: Xu Zhicheng Chen Jie Yang Putao
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 2

The Rectification Movement is in Full Swing
    Top Right Picture: Since the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant began its large-scale airing of views and debates in September, more than 9,900 big-character posters have been put up, raising more than 60,000 suggestions. Based on the opinions put forward by the masses, the factory leadership has formulated a concrete plan to improve the work. This is a big-character poster in the chassis workshop of the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant. Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Zhicheng
    Bottom Right Picture: Recently, more than 4,000 workers from the Northwest State-Owned No. 1 Cotton Mill in Xi'an formed more than 300 groups to conduct large-scale airing of views and debates. On the first day alone, nearly 1,000 big-character posters were put up. This is a group meeting where people are airing their views. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Jie
    Top Picture: Employees of the Shanghai Shipyard have put forward more than 10,000 suggestions to the factory leadership. Among them, regarding the factory's raw materials being prone to fire, causing waste, and affecting worker safety, the factory leadership has, according to the employees' suggestions, built underground oil tanks and calcium carbide warehouses to store raw materials. This is the underground oil tank under construction. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Putao

### 整风运动波澜壮阔（图片）
胥志成  陈捷  杨溥涛
1957-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    整风运动波澜壮阔
    右上图：第一汽车制造厂9月份开始大放大鸣以来，已贴出大字报九千九百多张，提出意见六万多条。工厂领导根据群众提出的意见，拟订出具体改进工作的计划。这是第一汽车制造厂底盘车间的大字报。　新华社记者　胥志成摄
    右下图　最近西安的西北国棉一厂四千多名工人组成三百多个小组进行大鸣大放，第一天就贴了近千张大字报。这是小组会在开会鸣放。　新华社记者　陈捷摄
    上图　上海造船厂职工已向工厂领导提出一万多条意见。其中关于工厂的原料容易失火、造成浪费和影响工人安全的意见，工厂领导已根据职工意见，兴建地下油池和电石库来贮藏原料。这是兴建中的地下油池。　新华社记者　杨溥涛摄
