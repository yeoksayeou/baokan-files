Title: Shen Yanbing Hosts Banquet for Vietnamese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd dispatch: Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing held a banquet on the evening of the 22nd to welcome the Cultural Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam headed by Qu Huy Can, from the Vietnamese Ministry of Culture. Nguyen Khang, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, also attended the banquet.

### 沈雁冰欢宴越文化考察团

1957-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰欢宴越文化考察团
    据新华社22日讯　文化部沈雁冰部长22日晚举行宴会，欢迎以越南文化部瞿辉近为首的越南民主共和国文化考察团。越南民主共和国驻中国大使阮康也出席了宴会。
