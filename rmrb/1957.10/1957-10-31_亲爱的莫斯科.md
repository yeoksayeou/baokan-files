Title: Dear Moscow
Author: Jin Jin
Date: 1957-10-31
Page: 8
Ode to October

    Dear Moscow
    Moscow, dear Moscow, I want to sing a song for you, you created human happiness, spreading the seeds of peace to the world. In our dark days, you supported our Far Eastern brother, and you were the first guest on the day New China was founded. We swept away the old waste, to build a brand-new socialism, your excellent experts came to us early. Factory chimneys rise one by one, we can make all kinds of machines, on every sophisticated part, there is Moscow's experience and friendship. Moscow's seeds grow on our land, Moscow's enthusiasm flows in our hearts. Thinking of faraway Moscow, it seems to be right beside us, we sing the same song, walking in the same direction. Moscow, dear Moscow, I want to sing a song for you, you defended peace and happiness, you are our most respected elder brother.

### 亲爱的莫斯科
金近
1957-10-31
第8版()
专栏：十月颂歌

    亲爱的莫斯科
    金近莫斯科，亲爱的莫斯科，我要为你唱一支歌，你创造了人类的幸福，把和平的种子向世界传播。在我们黑暗的日子里，你支援了远东这个亲兄弟，新中国成立那一天，第一个客人就是你。我们扫去了陈旧的废物，要建设崭新的社会主义，你的优秀的专家们，赶早来到我们这里。工厂的烟囱一个个升起，我们能造出各种机器，在每个精巧的零件上，有莫斯科的经验和友谊。莫斯科的种子，在我们的土地上成长，莫斯科的热情，流在我们的心头上。想起辽远的莫斯科，好像就在我们的身旁，我们唱着同一支歌，走着同一个方向。莫斯科，亲爱的莫斯科，我要为你唱一支歌，你保卫了和平和幸福，你是我们最敬爱的老大哥。
