Title: Sukarno Says There Is No Rift with Hatta
Author:
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 30th: According to Antara News Agency, President Sukarno told military and political officials and party leaders in Makassar on the 28th that there was no rift between him and former Vice President Hatta.

Sukarno pointed out that when Hatta gave a speech in China, he said that Indonesia should move towards socialism, that is, to build a just and prosperous society with Indonesian characteristics. He said that Hatta's opinion was no different from his own. He was pleased that Indonesian society could now see that the relationship between Sukarno and Hatta as "two in one" had been restored.

### 苏加诺说同哈达没有裂痕

1957-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    苏加诺说同哈达没有裂痕
    据新华社雅加达30日电  据安塔拉通讯社报道，苏加诺总统28日在望加锡对军政官员和党派领袖说，他和前副总统哈达之间没有裂痕。
    苏加诺指出，哈达在中国发表演说的时候说过，印度尼西亚要走向社会主义，那就是建设一个具有印度尼西亚特点的公正而繁荣的社会。他说，哈达的这个意见和他的意见没有两样。他对印度尼西亚社会现在能够看到苏加诺和哈达“两位一体”的关系已经恢复过来感到庆幸。
