Title: East German Military Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The military delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic, General Willi Stoph, returned home by plane from Beijing at 7:00 AM today.

At 6:40 AM, General Willi Stoph, accompanied by Marshal Peng Dehuai, reviewed the guard of honor at the airport.

General Willi Stoph said before leaving: We would like to pay our respects to the comrades of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. We will take the many profound impressions you have given us back to our country, back to the western front of the great socialist camp. We will tell our soldiers and officers about your high level of training and your firm political belief in the cause of socialism.

Marshal Peng Dehuai wished the delegation a safe journey with a feeling of reluctance to part. He said to General Willi Stoph, please convey the infinite care and respect of the Chinese people and their armed forces to the people and army of the German Democratic Republic. May our fighting friendship last forever.

Those who came to the airport to see them off today included General Huang Kecheng, General Tan Zheng, General Li Da, General Li Kenong, General Xiao Hua, General Hong Xuezhi, General Chen Shiju, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, and others.

The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Gipner, and embassy staff, as well as military attachés of various countries stationed in China, also came to the airport to see them off. (Attached picture)

### 民主德国军事代表团回国

1957-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国军事代表团回国
    新华社3日讯  以德意志民主共和国副总理兼国防部长维利·斯多夫上将为首的德意志民主共和国军事代表团，今天上午七时由北京乘飞机回国。
    六点四十分钟，维利·斯多夫上将在彭德怀元帅陪同下，在机场上检阅了仪仗队。
    维利·斯多夫上将临别时讲话说：我们谨向中国人民解放军的同志致以敬意。我们将把你们给我们的许多深刻印象带回到我们的祖国，带回到伟大的社会主义阵营的西线。我们将把你们高度的训练水平和对社会主义事业的坚定的政治信念，告诉给我们的士兵和军官。
    彭德怀元帅以惜别的心情祝代表团一路平安。他对维利·斯多夫上将说，请你把中国人民及其武装部队的无限关怀和敬意，带给德意志民主共和国的人民和军队。让我们的战斗友谊万古长青。
    今天到机场欢送的有黄克诚大将、谭政大将、李达上将、李克农上将、萧华上将、洪学智上将、陈士榘上将、萧向荣中将等。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使纪普纳和使馆人员，以及各国驻华武官也到机场欢送。（附图片）
