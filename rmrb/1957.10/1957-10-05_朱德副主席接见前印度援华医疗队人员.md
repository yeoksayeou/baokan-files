Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Receives Former Indian Medical Team to China

Author: 
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Vice Chairman Zhu De received Mr. Basu and his wife, Mr. Chackar, Mr. Mukerji and his wife, and Mr. Kodis, brother of Mr. Kotiwar, all members of the former Indian Medical Team to China for the Anti-Japanese War, on the afternoon of the 4th.

Present at the reception were: Jiang Qixian, Vice Minister of the Ministry of State Farms and Land Reclamation; Rao Zhengxi, Vice Minister of the Health Department of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; and Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Attached picture)

Vice Chairman Zhu De received members of the Indian Medical Team to China and their families, who had supported the Chinese people during the War of Resistance Against Japan, on October 4. The picture shows the scene during the reception. From left to right: Mrs. Mukerji, Mrs. Basu, Kodis, Chackar, Basu, Zhu De, Mukerji. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong photographed.

### 朱德副主席接见前印度援华医疗队人员

1957-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席
    接见前印度援华医疗队人员
    新华社4日讯　朱德副主席于4日下午接见了前印度抗日援华医疗队人员巴苏先生及夫人、查卡尔先生、木克其先生及夫人，以及该队人员柯迪华之弟柯迪斯先生。
    接见时在座的有：农垦部副部长姜齐贤、中国人民解放军总后勤部卫生部副部长饶正锡及外交部亚洲司司长章文晋等人。（附图片）
    朱德副主席10月4日接见了在我国抗日战争中曾支援我国人民的印度援华医疗队队员和他们的家属等。图为接见时情形。自左至右：木克其夫人、巴苏夫人、柯迪斯、卓克吉、巴苏、朱德、木克其。　新华社记者　邹健东摄
