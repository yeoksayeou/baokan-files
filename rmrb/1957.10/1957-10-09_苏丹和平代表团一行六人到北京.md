Title: Six-Member Sudanese Peace Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th. A six-member Sudanese Peace Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to visit China, arrived in Beijing on the 7th, led by Hashim, the head of the delegation and a judge of the Sudanese High Court. Members of the delegation include Hamza, the current Deputy Minister of Agriculture of Sudan, and Debayr, the former Minister of Animal Husbandry and Secretary-General of the Sudanese Southern Liberal Party.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Zhang Zhizhiang, Da Punsheng, Zhu Ziqi, Qi Muguan, Ma Mingji, and Xi Fengzhou, responsible persons from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and related organizations.

### 苏丹和平代表团一行六人到北京

1957-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    苏丹和平代表团一行六人到北京
    新华社8日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国参观访问的苏丹和平代表团一行六人，在团长、苏丹高等法院法官哈希布率领下，在7日到达北京。代表团团员中有现任苏丹农业部副部长哈姆扎和前畜牧部部长、苏丹南方自由党总书记德拜耳等人。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和有关团体负责人张志让、达浦生、朱子奇、戚慕光、马明基、席凤洲等人。
