Title: Weak Typhoon to Land in Guangdong, Cold Air Invades North China and Other Regions
Author:
Date: 1957-10-16
Page: 8

　　Meteorological Observatory

　　Weak Typhoon to Land in Guangdong
    Cold Air Invades North China and Other Regions

    This newspaper reports: On the afternoon of the 15th at 7:00 PM, the Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a weak typhoon warning and a strong wind frost warning:

    At 2:00 PM today, the center of the weak typhoon in the South China Sea was located 150 kilometers south of Taishan, Guangdong. The maximum wind force near the center is estimated to be level 7 to 9. Within 250 kilometers of the center, there are winds of level 6 or higher. Currently, the center of the weak typhoon is still moving northwest at a speed of 10 to 12 knots per hour. It is estimated that the center of the weak typhoon will soon land in the area between Taishan and Yangjiang in Guangdong. There will be winds of level 7 to 8 near the landing point.

    In addition, the cold air moving from the Mongolian People's Republic will continue to move southeast. Starting from the latter half of the 15th, the northerly winds in the Bohai Sea and the Yellow Sea will gradually increase to level 6 or 7. On the mornings of the 16th and 17th, the minimum temperature in the entire Northeast region, inland Hebei, inland Shandong, northern Henan, northern Jiangsu, and northern Anhui will drop to 5 degrees or below. After the wind stops and the sky clears, frost will appear in most areas.

### 弱台风将在广东登陆  冷空气侵袭华北等地

1957-10-16
第8版()
专栏：气象台

　　弱台风将在广东登陆
    冷空气侵袭华北等地
    本报讯　15日下午七时中央气象科学研究所发布弱台风警报和大风霜冻预告：
    今天下午二时，南海上的弱台风，中心位置在广东台山以南一百五十公里的海面上。中心附近最大风力估计为七至九级。距中心二百五十公里以内的海面上，有六级以上的大风。目前弱台风中心仍以每小时十至十二浬的速度向西北方向移动。估计弱台风中心即将在广东台山和阳江之间的地区登陆。登陆地点附近有七至八级的大风。
    另外，由蒙古人民共和国移来的冷空气将继续向东南移动，15日后半夜起，渤海和黄海海面的偏北风将逐渐加强到六、七级，16日早晨和17日早晨东北全区、河北内陆和山东内陆，河南北部、江苏北部和安徽北部最低气温将下降到五度或五度以下。风停天晴后，大部地区并将有霜冻出现。
