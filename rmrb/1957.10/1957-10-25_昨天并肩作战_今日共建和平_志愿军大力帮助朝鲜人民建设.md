Title: Fighting Shoulder to Shoulder Yesterday, Building Peace Together Today: The Chinese People's Volunteers Vigorously Help the Korean People with Construction
Author:
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 24th -- At the beginning of this year, the Party Committee of the Chinese People's Volunteers made a resolution requiring the entire army to contribute 300,000 labor workdays this year to help the Korean people with production and construction. This resolution was warmly supported by all officers and soldiers, and by the end of July, it had exceeded the original plan, with 430,000 labor workdays completed. The main projects in which the Volunteers helped the Korean people with production and construction this year included 398 large and small canals and dams, with a total length of 123 kilometers, 5 large and small bridges, the construction of 534 houses, as well as planting more than 2.5 million trees, accumulating 530,000 dan of fertilizer, transporting more than 30 tons of various materials, and engaging in more than 18,000 mu of field labor.

### 昨天并肩作战  今日共建和平  志愿军大力帮助朝鲜人民建设

1957-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    昨天并肩作战  今日共建和平
    志愿军大力帮助朝鲜人民建设
    据新华社平壤24日电　今年年初，中国共产党志愿军党委会作出决议，要求全军今年以三十万个劳动工日帮助朝鲜人民进行生产建设。这一决议受到了全体官兵的热烈拥护，到7月底已超过原订计划，做了四十三万个劳动工日。志愿军今年帮助朝鲜人民生产建设的主要工程有大小水渠和堤坝三百九十八条，全长计有一百二十三公里，大小桥梁五座，修建房屋五百三十四间，此外还植树二百五十多万株，积肥五十三万担，运送各种物资三十多吨，从事田间劳动一万八千多亩。
