Title: Japanese Coal Miners' Strike
Author:
Date: 1957-10-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 30th — More than 150,000 Japanese coal miners held the largest sympathy strike in Japanese labor history today in support of the strike struggle of coal miners in Kyushu. Approximately 4,300 workers at the Kyushu Kijima Coal Mining Company have been on strike since August.

### 日本煤矿工人罢工

1957-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    日本煤矿工人罢工
    据新华社30日讯  东京消息：十五万多名日本煤矿工人今天举行日本劳工史上规模最大的一次同情罢工，支持九州煤矿工人的罢工斗争。九州杵岛煤矿公司的约四千三百名工人从8月份起就在举行罢工。
