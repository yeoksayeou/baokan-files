Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Invites Prime Minister Daud to Dinner

Author:
Date: 1957-10-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai invited Sardar Mohammed Daud, Prime Minister of Afghanistan, some of his entourage, and the Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad, to dinner at 8 p.m. on the 23rd.

He Long, Zhang Hanfu, and Ding Guoyu were also present.

Front row from left to right: Peng Zhen, Shen Junru, Shalizai, Li Jishen, Samad, Liu Shaoqi, Daud, Zhu De, Popal, Zhou Enlai, Pajhwak, He Long, Salaj, Chen Yi, Chen Shutong. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong photo)

### 周总理邀达乌德首相共进晚餐

1957-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理邀达乌德首相共进晚餐
    新华社23日讯　周恩来总理23日晚八时邀请萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德首相和他的部分随行人员以及阿富汗驻中国大使萨马德共进晚餐。
      作陪的，有贺龙、章汉夫、丁国钰。
    前排自左至右：彭真、沈钧儒、沙利扎伊、李济深、萨马德、刘少奇、达乌德、朱德、波帕尔、周恩来、帕日瓦克、贺龙、萨拉季、陈毅、陈叔通。　新华社记者　邹健东摄
