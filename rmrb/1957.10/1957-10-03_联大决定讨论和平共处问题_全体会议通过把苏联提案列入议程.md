Title: UN General Assembly Decides to Discuss Peaceful Coexistence; Plenary Session Approves Inclusion of Soviet Proposal on Agenda
Author:
Date: 1957-10-03
Page: 3

    UN General Assembly Decides to Discuss Peaceful Coexistence
    Plenary Session Approves Inclusion of Soviet Proposal on Agenda
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 2nd – The United Nations General Assembly plenary session on October 1 decided to include the Soviet draft declaration on peaceful coexistence as a separate item on the Assembly's agenda.
    Regarding the Soviet proposal to halt nuclear testing, the Soviet Union also requested that it be included on the agenda as a matter of urgency. However, under pressure from Western countries, the Assembly decided to discuss the issue under the general disarmament item, rather than including it as a separate item.
    Note: The United Nations General Assembly's General Committee voted on September 30 to agree to include the two Soviet proposals, "Declaration on Peaceful Coexistence of States" and "Cessation of Testing of Atomic and Hydrogen Weapons under International Supervision," on the Assembly's agenda.

### 联大决定讨论和平共处问题  全体会议通过把苏联提案列入议程

1957-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    联大决定讨论和平共处问题
    全体会议通过把苏联提案列入议程
    新华社2日讯  纽约消息：联合国大会全体会议10月1日决定把苏联提出的关于和平共处宣言草案作为一个单独项目列入大会议程。
    关于苏联提出的停止核试验问题的建议，苏联也要求作为一个紧迫的问题单独列入议程，但是在西方国家坚持下，大会决定放在裁军总项目下讨论这个问题，而不单独列入议程。
    按：联合国大会总务委员会9月30日曾经进行表决，同意把苏联提出的“各国和平共处的宣言”和“在国际监督下停止试验原子武器和氢武器”两个提案列入大会议程。
