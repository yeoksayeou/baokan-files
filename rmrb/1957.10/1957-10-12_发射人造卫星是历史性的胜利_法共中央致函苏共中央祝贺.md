Title: Launching of Artificial Satellite is a Historic Victory; French Communist Party Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Letter to the CPSU Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - TASS, Paris: The Central Committee of the French Communist Party and the parliamentary group held a joint meeting on October 9th and passed a letter of congratulations to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union regarding the Soviet Union's launch of the first artificial satellite. The letter stated that we are proud of this historically significant victory in science achieved under the leadership of a socialist regime. You have demonstrated the extremely high development of the material, technical, and scientific strength of the first socialist country, which people of all countries regard as a most significant contribution to world peace and universal human progress.

### 发射人造卫星是历史性的胜利  法共中央致函苏共中央祝贺

1957-10-12
第6版()
专栏：

    发射人造卫星是历史性的胜利
    法共中央致函苏共中央祝贺
    据新华社11日讯　塔斯社巴黎讯：法国共产党中央委员会和议会党团10月9日举行联席会议，就苏联发射第一个人造卫星通过了致苏联共产党中央委员会的贺信。这封贺信说，我们为在社会主义政权领导下在科学上所取得的这个具有历史意义的胜利而感到骄傲。你们证明了第一个社会主义国家在物质、技术和科学上的实力有了极高的发展，各国人民把这看作是对世界和平和人类普遍进步的一个最重大的贡献。
