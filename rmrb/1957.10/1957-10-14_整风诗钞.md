Title: Selections from Rectification Poetry
Author: Li Xue'ao
Date: 1957-10-14
Page: 8
　　
　　My heart is in the factory.
　　The factory is in my heart.
    　　    ——A veteran worker's words

　　Party Committee Secretary
　　Red threads fill his eyes,
　　Dark circles sink into his sockets.
　　In the Party Committee office,
　　He is radiant and his laughter is hearty.
　　Like the days and nights of wartime,
　　How calm he is, and how tense.
　　He studies the workers' opinions one by one,
　　As if mining a rich gold mine.
    
    Work Hat
　　He didn't use the state's cotton cloth,
　　Nor did he make a fuss about it in front of everyone.
　　Silently, he picked up scraps from the waste pile,
　　Washed them himself, sewed them himself,
　　And made a new work hat to wear on his head.
　　It has no bright colors,
　　Nor any extraordinary ornamentation.
　　A simple work hat, not worth singing about,
　　Yet beneath each work hat,
　　The most sublime thoughts are seething.

    Returning to the Front Lines
　　The day after the mobilization meeting,
　　He gladly left his section chief's seat and returned to the workshop.
　　His comrades surrounded him tightly,
　　As if welcoming an old soldier back to the front lines.
　　The labor model from four years ago,
　　Had long become a "living calculator."
　　Good, the socialist worker—section chief—worker,
　　Able to use both pen and sword, intelligent and brave.

    Pre-Shift Chat
    "Foreman, please drink this cup of strong tea,
　　For your sincere acceptance of criticism,
　　For my suggestion being adopted,
　　For our work section conquering a new fortress of quotas,
　　For our friendship taking root and blossoming!
　　Foreman, quickly drink this cup of strong tea,
　　Look how energetic we all are!?
　　Just waiting for your guidance!"

    A Thunderous Voice
　　In the workshop,
　　On the road,
　　In the dormitory,
　　In the meeting place,
　　People are talking, arguing,
　　A thunderous voice,
　　Resounding day and night—
  　　Socialism is good!
　　  Socialism is strong!
　　Like a sharp sword
　　Cutting off capitalist thoughts!
　　Like a bright lamp
　　Shining in people's hearts!

### 整风诗钞
李学鳌
1957-10-14
第8版()
专栏：

　　整风诗钞
　　李学鳌
　　我的心，在工厂里。
　　工厂，在我的心里。
    　　    ——一个老工人的话
　　党委书记
　　红丝织满眼珠，
　　黑圈深陷在眼眶。
　　在党委办公室里，
　　他精神焕发，笑声爽朗。
　　像战时的日日夜夜，
　　他多么沉着，又多么紧张。
　　逐条地研究着工人们的意见，
　　如开采一座富饶的金矿。
    工作帽
　　没有用国家的棉布，
　　也没有在众人前喧嚷。
　　默默地，从废料堆中拣来了废布，
　　自己洗濯，自己缝纫，
　　做成新的工作帽戴在头上。
　　没有鲜美的颜色，
　　也没有出奇的装璜。
　　简单的工作帽啊，本不值得歌唱，
　　然而在每顶工作帽下面，
　　却沸腾着最崇高的思想。
    重返前线
　　动员大会开过第二天，
　　他欣然离开科长的坐位，回到车间。
　　伙伴们把他紧紧围上，
　　像欢迎老战士重返前线。
　　四年前的劳动模范，
　　早变成一架“活算盘”。
　　好啊，社会主义的工人——科长——工人，
　　能文能武，智勇双全。
    班前小语
    “工长，请喝下这杯浓茶，
　　为了你诚恳地接受批评，
　　为了我的建议被采纳，
　　为了咱工段又攻下了定额的新堡垒，
　　为了咱们的友谊又生了根，开了花！
　　工长，快喝下这杯浓茶，
　　你看哥儿几个的劲头多么足！？
　　单等着你指点啦！”
    一个巨雷般的声音
　　在车间，
　　在路上，
　　在宿舍，
　　在会场，
　　人们谈论着，争辩着，
　　一个巨雷般的声音，
　　日夜不停地回响——
  　　社会主义好！
　　  社会主义强！
　　像把利剑
　　斩断了资本主义的思想！
　　像盏明灯
　　亮在人们的心上！
