Title: Guangzhou to Build Sino-Soviet Blood Friendship Pavilion
Author:
Date: 1957-10-11
Page: 8

(Xinhua News Agency)

Guangzhou is building the Sino-Soviet Blood Friendship Pavilion

The Sino-Soviet Blood Friendship Pavilion, one of the main construction projects of the Guangzhou Commune Martyrs Cemetery, is now under intensive construction and is expected to be completed before the 30th anniversary of the Guangzhou Commune on December 11 this year.

After the Sino-Soviet Blood Friendship Pavilion is completed, people will be able to commemorate the Soviet martyrs who died for the Guangzhou Commune and cherish even more the friendship forged by the blood of the Chinese and Soviet people.

The Sino-Soviet Blood Friendship Pavilion is built on a hill in the Guangzhou Commune Martyrs Cemetery. According to the plan, a monument will also be erected in front of the pavilion. The pavilion will be surrounded by various precious trees and flowers.

### 广州兴建中苏血谊亭

1957-10-11
第8版()
专栏：

    广州兴建中苏血谊亭
    广州公社烈士陵园的主体工程项目之一——中苏血谊亭现在正在加紧兴建中，预计可在今年12月11日广州公社三十周年纪念日以前完成。
    中苏血谊亭建成以后，人们将能够凭吊当年为广州公社殉难的苏联烈士，加倍珍惜中苏人民以鲜血凝成的友谊。
    中苏血谊亭建筑在广州公社烈士陵园里的一个山岗上。按照规划，在亭子的前面还将建立一座纪念碑。亭子的周围将遍植各种名贵的树木和花草。
                    （据新华社讯）
