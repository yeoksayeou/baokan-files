Title: Hungarian Tennis Team Wins Twice in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 24th. The Hungarian tennis team had friendly matches with the Wuhan City tennis team in Wuhan. In two men's singles matches and one men's doubles match, the Hungarian team won 2-0 each time.

### 匈网球队在武汉两次获胜

1957-10-25
第8版()
专栏：

    匈网球队在武汉两次获胜
    据新华社武汉24日电　匈牙利网球队在武汉曾和武汉市网球队进行了友谊比赛。在两场男子单打，一场男子双打中，匈牙利队都以二比零获胜。
