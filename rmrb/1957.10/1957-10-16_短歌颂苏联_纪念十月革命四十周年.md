Title: Short Song Praising the Soviet Union—In Commemoration of the Fortieth Anniversary of the October Revolution
Author: Zang Kejia
Date: 1957-10-16
Page: 8

　　Short Song Praising the Soviet Union
　　—In Commemoration of the Fortieth Anniversary of the October Revolution
　　Zang Kejia
　　One
　　Don't despise my short song for being short,
　　My short song is a fountain;
　　Our friendship is truly too deep and long,
　t I can't sing it all in one breath.
　　Two
  　My feet have never stepped onto Soviet soil,
　　Yet it feels as intimate to me as a homeland,
　　The southern wind blows countless good news,
　　Each piece of news touches my heart.
　　When I close my eyes at night,
　　I see the red star on the Kremlin clock tower,
　　Whenever a commemorative festival arrives,
　　I hear the world-shaking sound from Red Square.
　　Three
　　Raise your head and look up at Moscow,
　　Turn your ear towards Moscow,
　　The fortress of peace attracts the people of peace,
　　Like the sea attracts thousands of rivers.
　　Four
　　The intercontinental missile
　　Released the first good news;
　　The artificial satellite
　　Brought joy to the ends of the earth,
　　Victory and victory run a relay race,
　　Forty years of revolution have blossomed into red flowers.
　　Five
　　When Chairman Mao visited the Soviet Union,
　　He and Stalin walked out of the Kremlin, shoulder to shoulder,
　　This moving image appeared in a picture,
　　Its life will last for thousands of years.
　　Voroshilov came to China as a guest,
　　He and Chairman Mao stood shoulder to shoulder in the hall of the Beijing Hotel,
　　The bright lights imprinted this scene on my heart,
　　Like a clear pond reflects the great sun.
　　Six
　　Factory chimneys stand tall everywhere—
　　Each monument commemorates our friendship
　　The train rumbles across the Yangtze River Bridge,
　　This is the song of great friendship.
　　Seven
　　Every time we cheer for our own success,
　　This cheer contains gratitude;
　　Every time we sing for your victory,
　　The song is filled with pride and strength.
　　Eight
　　The sun rises on the horizon,
　　Even if you can't see it,
　　You will feel its heat and light.
　　Soviet Union, the new sun of mankind,
　　Under your shining light, in forty years,
　　Twelve countries have been liberated.
　　Your power penetrates layers of iron curtains,
　　The hearts of people all over the world,
　　Are illuminated by your boundless rays of light.

### 短歌颂苏联——纪念十月革命四十周年
臧克家
1957-10-16
第8版()
专栏：十月颂歌

　　短歌颂苏联
　　——纪念十月革命四十周年
　　臧克家
　　一
　　不要嫌我的短歌短，
　　我的短歌是喷泉；
　　我们的情谊实在太深长，
　　一口气呀唱不完。
　　二
  　我的脚并没有踏进过苏联的国土，
　　它对我却是故乡似的相亲，
　　南来的风吹来无数好消息，
　　每个消息都打动我的心。
　　当我夜晚合上了眼睛，
　　我看见了克里姆林宫钟塔上的红星，
　　每当纪念的佳节来临，
　　我听到了红场上那震动世界的声音。
　　三
　　抬起头来仰望莫斯科，
　　向着莫斯科侧起耳朵，
　　和平堡垒吸引着和平的人民，
　　像大海吸引着千万条江河。
　　四
　　洲际导弹
　　放出了第一个喜讯；
　　人造卫星
　　把欢乐带到了海角天涯，
　　胜利和胜利接力赛跑，
　　四十年的革命开出了朵朵红花。
　　五
　　毛主席访问苏联的时候，
　　他和斯大林走出克里姆林宫，肩并着肩，
　　这动人的形象出现在画面，
　　它的生命呀，千年万年。
　　伏罗希洛夫来中国作客，
　　他和毛主席肩并着肩，在北京饭店的厅堂，
　　明晃晃灯光把这情景印在我心上，
　　像清水池塘印上了那个大太阳。
　　六
　　工厂的烟囱到处耸立——
　　一座座纪念碑纪念着我们的友谊
　　长江大桥上火车??地响，
　　这是伟大友谊的歌唱。
　　七
　　我们每次为自己的成功欢呼，
　　这呼声里就含着感激；
　　我们每次为你们的胜利歌唱，
　　歌声里充满着骄傲和力量。
　　八
　　太阳升到地平线上，
　　即使看不到他，
　　你也会感到他的热和光。
　　苏联呀，人类新的太阳，
　　在你的照耀下，四十年的时间，
　　已经有十二个国家得到了解放。
　　你的威力把一重重铁幕穿透，
　　全世界上的人心呀，
　　都被你的万丈光芒照亮。
