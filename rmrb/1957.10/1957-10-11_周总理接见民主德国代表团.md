Title: Premier Zhou Receives Delegation from the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1957-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: Premier Zhou Enlai received Vice Premier Oelßner, head of the delegation from the German Democratic Republic, and delegation members: Minister Apel of the Heavy Machine Building Industry, Ambassador Gipner to China, and Commercial Counselor Norbert of the Embassy in China this evening.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier Bo Yibo, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of the Second Ministry of Machine Building Zhao Erlu, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei.

After the reception, Premier Zhou also invited Vice Premier Oelßner and the delegation members to dinner.

### 周总理接见民主德国代表团

1957-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见民主德国代表团
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了民主德国代表团团长厄斯纳副总理和代表团团员：重型机器制造工业部部长阿佩尔脱、驻中国大使纪普纳、驻中国大使馆商务参赞诺伯特。
    接见时在座的，有副总理薄一波，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，第二机械工业部部长赵尔陆，外交部副部长姬鹏飞。
    接见后，周总理并邀请厄斯纳副总理和代表团人员共进晚餐。
