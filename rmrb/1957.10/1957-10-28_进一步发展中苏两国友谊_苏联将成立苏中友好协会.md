Title: Further Develop the Friendship Between China and the Soviet Union: The Soviet Union Will Establish the Soviet-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 27th: In order to further develop the great friendship between China and the Soviet Union, the Soviet Union will establish the Soviet-China Friendship Association, according to the initiative of people from all walks of life in the Soviet Union.

According to the proposers' suggestion, a founding assembly will be held in Moscow soon, and the leading body of the association will be elected.

### 进一步发展中苏两国友谊  苏联将成立苏中友好协会

1957-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

    进一步发展中苏两国友谊
    苏联将成立苏中友好协会
    新华社莫斯科27日电　根据苏联社会各界人士的发起，为了进一步发展中苏两国的伟大友谊，苏联将成立苏中友好协会。
    根据发起人的提议，最近将在莫斯科召开成立大会，并选举协会的领导机构。
