Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Visits Lüda
Author:
Date: 1957-10-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, October 13th: The Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation visited the Dalian Shipyard this morning, which has been restored and developed with Soviet assistance. More than 1,500 shipyard workers gathered in front of the shipyard's office building to welcome the distinguished guests.

Head of the delegation, A.B. Aristov, said to the shipyard workers at the welcoming ceremony that the unbreakable friendship between China and the Soviet Union was formed long ago, and this is an eternal friendship. He said that the friendship between us is selfless. We help the Chinese people like brothers.

Delegation member and First Secretary of the Ukrainian Nikolayev Regional Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, A.S. Molyakin, also invited the shipyard workers to visit the Nikolayev Shipyard in their region.

Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, encouraged the shipyard workers at the meeting to better learn from the advanced Soviet technology and experience, and to develop the country's maritime transport industry.

The delegation also exchanged souvenirs with the Dalian Shipyard workers at the welcoming ceremony as a memento.

The delegation visited the repair dock and the shipbuilding platform in the shipyard. Workers working on the decks and in the workshops warmly applauded to welcome the distinguished guests. During the visit, Aristov also had cordial conversations with the workers.

The Supreme Soviet delegation arrived in Lüda on the 11th by special plane from Wuhan for a visit.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团在旅大访问

1957-10-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃代表团在旅大访问
    据新华社旅大13日电　苏联最高苏维埃代表团今天上午访问了在苏联帮助下恢复和发展起来的大连造船厂。一千五百多名造船工人在造船厂的办公大楼前集会欢迎贵宾。
    阿·鲍·阿里斯托夫团长在欢迎会上向造船工人们说，中苏两国很早就结成了牢不可破的友谊，这是永恒的友谊。他说，我们之间的友谊是大公无私的。我们像兄弟一样的帮助中国人民。
    代表团团员、苏共乌克兰尼古拉耶夫州党委第一书记安·谢·莫连金，在会上还邀请造船工人们到他们那里去参观尼古拉耶夫造船厂。
    中共中央政治局委员、人大常委会副委员长彭真，在这个会上勉励造船工人们更好地学习苏联先进技术和先进经验，发展祖国的海运事业。
    代表团在欢迎会上还同大连造船厂工人互赠纪念品留念。
    代表团在造船厂参观了修船船坞、造船台等；甲板上和车间里正在工作的工人们都热烈鼓掌欢迎贵宾。阿里斯托夫团长在参观的时候还同工人们作了亲切谈话。
    最高苏维埃代表团是在11日由武汉乘专机来旅大访问的。
