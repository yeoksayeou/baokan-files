Title: Artificial Satellite Passed Over Beijing Last Night, Will Pass Over Shenyang, Yumen, Lanzhou and Other Places This Morning
Author:
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. TASS broadcast that the artificial satellite would pass over Beijing at 22:49 on October 7th (all times below are Beijing time), pass over Shenyang at 7:10 on October 8th, pass over Yumen at 8:46, pass over Lanzhou at 8:48, pass over Chongqing at 8:50, and pass over Hainan Island at 8:53. According to previous reports, the satellite can be seen with ordinary telescopes in places where it arrives at dawn or dusk.

### 人造卫星昨夜经过北京  今晨将经过沈阳玉门兰州等地

1957-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    人造卫星昨夜经过北京
    今晨将经过沈阳玉门兰州等地
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社广播，人造卫星将在10月7日（以下均为北京时间）二十二点四十九分经过北京，10月8日七点十分经过沈阳，八点四十六分经过玉门，八点四十八分经过兰州，八点五十分经过重庆，八点五十三分经过海南岛。据以前报道，人造卫星在黎明或黄昏时候到达的地方，当地用一般望远镜都能看到。
