Title: Our National Leaders Attend Soviet Embassy Concert, Appreciating Soviet Violinist David Oistrakh's Performance
Author:
Date: 1957-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - Ambassador Yudin held a musical performance at the Soviet Embassy this evening to entertain our national leaders.

Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai were invited to attend the concert.

At the concert, Soviet People's Artist and world-renowned violinist David Oistrakh performed pieces by world-famous composers.

Also attending the concert tonight were Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Bo Yibo, Vice Chairmen Lin Boqu, Peng Zhen, and others. (Picture attached)

　　Chairman Mao Zedong and others appreciate the wonderful performance of Soviet violinist David Oistrakh.

### 我国领导人出席苏使馆音乐会  欣赏苏联小提琴家奥伊斯特拉赫演奏

1957-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国领导人出席苏使馆音乐会
    欣赏苏联小提琴家奥伊斯特拉赫演奏
    新华社10日讯　尤金大使今晚在苏联大使馆举办音乐演奏会，招待我国国家领导人员。
    毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理应邀出席了演奏会。
    演奏会上，苏联人民演员、世界著名小提琴家奥伊斯特拉赫演奏了世界名作曲家的乐曲。
    出席今晚演奏会的，还有副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、邓子恢、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、薄一波，副委员长林伯渠、彭真等人。（附图片）
　　毛泽东主席等在欣赏苏联小提琴家奥伊斯特拉赫精彩的演奏
