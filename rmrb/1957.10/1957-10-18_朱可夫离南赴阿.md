Title: Zhukov Departs Yugoslavia for Albania
Author:
Date: 1957-10-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 17th. After a nine-day visit to Yugoslavia, Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov left Belgrade by plane today for Albania.

Those seeing off Marshal Zhukov and the senior Soviet military officers accompanying him at the airport included: Yugoslav State Secretary for National Defense Gošnjak, members of the Federal Executive Council, and senior generals of the Yugoslav army.

Zhukov delivered a short speech at the airport.

### 朱可夫离南赴阿

1957-10-18
第5版()
专栏：

    朱可夫离南赴阿
    据新华社贝尔格莱德17日电　苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅在南斯拉夫访问了九天以后，今天乘飞机离开贝尔格莱德前往阿尔巴尼亚。
    在机场上送别朱可夫元帅和随同他的苏军高级将领的有：南斯拉夫国防国务秘书长戈什尼亚克，联邦执行委员会的委员们以及南斯拉夫军队的高级将领。
    朱可夫在机场上发表了简短的讲话。
