Title: Soviet Artists Prepare for Festival Gala Performance; People's Democratic Countries' Films Begin Screening
Author:
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 6

    Soviet Artists Prepare for Festival Gala Performance
    People's Democratic Countries' Films Begin Screening
    Two thousand Soviet actors, singers, musicians, and dancers will participate in a grand art performance in Moscow to celebrate the anniversary of the October Revolution.

    Outstanding orchestras, choirs, dance troupes, and soloists from various Union Republics and Autonomous Republics will come to Moscow to perform. Famous Soviet artists such as Ulanova, Pirogov, I. Petrov, Yu. Timoshenko, Babochkin, O. Popov, etc., will also participate in the performances.

    The theme of this art performance includes five stages of the Soviet state's forty-year history: the October Revolution, the Civil War, the First Five-Year Plan, the Great Patriotic War, and the post-war era.

    To welcome the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, Moscow's cinemas began screening films from People's Democratic countries on October 22.

    Over six days, the cinemas will screen twenty-three films, including China's "Land," "For Peace," "Storm in the South Seas," as well as films from Hungary, Yugoslavia, Bulgaria, North Korea, and Germany.

### 苏联艺术家准备节日大会演  人民民主国家影片开始放映

1957-10-26
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联艺术家准备节日大会演
    人民民主国家影片开始放映
    苏联两千名演员、歌唱家、音乐家和舞蹈家将在庆祝十月革命节的日子里参加在莫斯科举行的盛大艺术表演会。
    各加盟共和国和自治共和国的优秀乐队、合唱团、舞蹈团和独奏者都将前来莫斯科演出。苏联著名艺术家乌兰诺娃、皮罗戈夫、伊·彼得罗夫、尤·铁木辛哥、巴鲍契金、奥·波波夫等也都将参加表演。
    这次艺术表演的主题包括苏维埃国家四十年历史的五个阶段：十月革命、国内战争、第一个五年计划、伟大卫国战争和战后年代。
    为迎接十月革命四十周年，莫斯科各电影院在10月22日开始放映各人民民主国家的影片。
    在六天中，各电影院将放映二十三部影片。其中包括：中国的“土地”、“为了和平”、“南岛风云”，以及匈、南、保、朝、德等国影片。
