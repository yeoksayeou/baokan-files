Title: Syrian Journalist Delegation Head Yaz Dies of Illness in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: Tofik Yaz, head of the Syrian journalist delegation visiting China and director of the Information Department of the Syrian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, died of severe atrial infarction – coronary artery occlusion – at 5:20 a.m. on October 27 at Peking Union Medical College Hospital. After Yaz fell ill, the hospital provided extensive treatment, but on the morning of the 27th, he suffered a severe atrial infarction and emergency treatment was unsuccessful. Yaz was sixty-seven years old. The All-China Journalists Association expressed deep condolences on Yaz's death.

### 叙利亚新闻工作者代表团团长雅兹因病在京逝世

1957-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    叙利亚新闻工作者代表团团长雅兹因病在京逝世
    新华社27日讯　叙利亚新闻工作者访华代表团团长、叙利亚外交部新闻司司长托菲克·雅兹因剧烈的心房梗塞——冠状动脉闭塞，在10月27日上午五时二十分在北京协和医院病逝。雅兹患病后，经医院进行多方治疗，27日晨发生剧烈的心房梗塞，急救无效。雅兹今年六十七岁。中华全国新闻工作者协会对雅兹的逝世深表哀悼。
