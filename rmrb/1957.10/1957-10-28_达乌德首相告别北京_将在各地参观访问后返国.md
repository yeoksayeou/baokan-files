Title: Prime Minister Daud Bids Farewell to Beijing, Will Visit Various Places Before Returning Home
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: Afghan Prime Minister Sardar Mohammed Daud and his entourage left Beijing by special plane this morning. The distinguished guests will visit various places in our country before returning home.

Premier Zhou Enlai went to the airport to see the guests off. In his farewell speech, he said: "Although our time together with His Highness the Prime Minister in Beijing has been short, through His Highness's cordial meetings with our country's state leaders and people from all walks of life, the mutual trust and understanding between our two countries has been further deepened."

Premier Zhou said: "His Highness the Prime Minister's visit to our country has made an important contribution to promoting the development of friendly relations between China and Afghanistan."

Premier Zhou also asked the Prime Minister to convey the Chinese people's cordial friendship to the Afghan people.

Prime Minister Daud said in his farewell speech: "Our close contact with Premier Zhou Enlai and other great figures in China has played a very beneficial and effective role in strengthening the long-standing friendly relations between Afghanistan and China."

The Prime Minister reiterated: "Afghanistan is willing to maintain friendly relations with all countries in the world, especially its neighbors. Therefore, maintaining friendly and good-neighborly relations with our friendly neighbor, China, is our sincere and heartfelt desire."

The Prime Minister concluded: "My companions and I will always remember the good impressions that this visit has given us. I brought the message of the Afghan people's friendship to the Chinese people, and now I will bring back the Chinese people's friendship in return to the Afghan people. I believe this is a most appropriate mission."

Before the speech, Prime Minister Daud, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, also reviewed the guard of honor.

Those who came to the airport to see off the distinguished guests today included Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee Li Jishen, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, President Dong Biwu, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, people from all walks of life in China, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China.

The special plane carrying the distinguished guests took off at eight o'clock in the morning. Accompanying the distinguished guests on their visit to various places were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Ding Guoyu, and Afghan Ambassador to China Samad.

According to Xinhua News Agency: Afghan Prime Minister Daud flew from Beijing to Anshan by special plane on the morning of the 27th. In Anshan, he visited the seamless steel pipe plant, the large-scale rolling mill, the second steelmaking plant, and the iron smelting plant of Anshan Iron and Steel Works (Ansteel). The Prime Minister praised the scale, mechanization, and automation of these factories.

After the visit, Prime Minister Daud said to Yuan Zhen, the manager of Ansteel who accompanied him: Congratulations on the increasing development of Anshan's steel industry.

At 2:55 pm, the distinguished guests arrived in Shenyang from Anshan by special plane. In Shenyang, they visited the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant and then toured the city of Shenyang. Accompanying the distinguished guests were Liaoning Governor Du Zheheng and Shenyang Deputy Mayor Zhang Like, among others.

In the evening, Governor Du Zheheng hosted a banquet to welcome Prime Minister Daud and his entourage. (Attached picture)

Daud bids farewell to Prime Minister Zhou Enlai. Our reporter Gao Liang.

### 达乌德首相告别北京  将在各地参观访问后返国

1957-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    达乌德首相告别北京
    将在各地参观访问后返国
    新华社27日讯　阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德和随行人员27日早晨乘专机离开北京。贵宾们将在我国各地参观访问后返国。
    周恩来总理到机场为贵宾们送行。他在致欢送词中说：“虽然我们在北京同首相殿下相聚的时间很短，但是通过首相殿下同我国国家领导人和各方面人物的亲切会见，我们两国之间的相互信任和了解已经有了进一步的加深。”
    周总理说：“首相殿下这次来我国访问，对于促进中阿两国友好关系的发展作了重要的贡献。”
    周总理还请首相把我国人民对阿富汗人民的亲切友情带给阿富汗人民。
    达乌德首相在告别讲话中说：“我们同周恩来总理阁下以及中国其他伟大的人物的密切接触，对于加强阿富汗和中国之间悠久的友好关系，起了非常有益和有效的作用。”
    首相重申：“阿富汗愿意同世界上一切国家，特别是它的邻国，维持友好关系。因此，同我们的友好邻邦中国保持友好睦邻的关系，乃是我们的真诚的和衷心的愿望。”
    首相最后说：“我和我的同伴们将永远记得，这次访问所给予我们的良好的印象。我带来了阿富汗人民对中国人民友好的信息，现在又将把中国人民回报阿富汗人民的友情带回去，我认为这是一个最恰当的使命。”
    在讲话以前，达乌德首相还由周恩来总理陪同检阅了仪仗队。
    今天到机场欢送贵宾的，还有贺龙、陈毅副总理，李济深、彭真、陈叔通副委员长，董必武院长，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，国防委员会副主席张治中和中国各方面的人士以及各国驻中国使节。
    贵宾们乘坐的专机在早晨八时起飞。陪同贵宾们到各地参观的，有外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰，以及阿富汗驻中国大使萨马德。
    据新华社讯　阿富汗王国首相达乌德27日上午从北京乘专机到鞍山，在鞍山参观了鞍钢无缝钢管厂、大型轧钢厂、第二炼钢厂和炼铁厂。首相对这些工厂的规模和机械化、自动化程度表示称赞。
    达乌德首相在参观结束后，对陪同参观的鞍钢经理袁振说：祝贺鞍山的钢铁工业越来越发达。
    下午二时五十五分，贵宾们乘专机由鞍山到达沈阳，在沈阳参观了沈阳第一机床厂，随后又游览了沈阳市区。陪同贵宾们参观的有辽宁省省长杜者蘅和沈阳市副市长张力克等。
    晚上，杜者蘅省长设宴欢迎达乌德首相和他的随行人员。（附图片）
