Title: Japanese National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The Japanese National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations delegation, a group of fifteen led by delegation leader Tadayoshi Obata, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 30th.

Tadayoshi Obata delivered a speech to those welcoming him at the airport, saying, "Our fifteen members include representatives from all levels of the Japanese people. We have come to visit your country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs under the name of the 'National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations Delegation.'"

### 日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华使节团到京

1957-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华使节团到京
    据新华社30日讯  日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华使节团一行十五人在团长小畑忠良率领下，30日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    小畑忠良在机场向欢迎者发表了谈话，他说：“我们十五个人包括日本人民的各阶层，应中国人民外交学会的邀请，以‘恢复日中邦交国民会议访华使节团’的名义前来访问贵国。”
