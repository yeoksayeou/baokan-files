Title: To the Rural Postman
Author: Yin Jiong Lan
Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 8

　　To the Rural Postman
　　尹烱兰
　　The stars have just retracted their last bit of clear light,
　　The eastern horizon is not yet fully bright,
　　You carry your green mailbag,
　　Walking through the dew, all over the village.
　　You deliver newspapers and books to each door,
　　And also bring letters for the girls.
　　You enable the villagers to know the changes of the motherland in time;
　　And also bring happy smiles to the girls.
　　From spring to severe winter,
　　Regardless of wind, snow and frost,
　　So many mornings like this,
　　You deliver newspapers, books and letters.
　　It is because you understand that this ordinary work,
　　Is full of extraordinary deeds,
　　It is because you dedicate your sincere heart,
　　To socialist construction.
　　Every day you wear the dawn,
　　Walking on the path by the village,
　　Your mailbag is filled with newspapers, books and letters,
　　And also filled with your love for the motherland…

### 给乡邮员
尹烱兰
1957-10-12
第8版()
专栏：

　　给乡邮员
　　尹烱兰
　　星星刚收敛着最后一点清光，
　　东方天边还没完全透亮，
　　你便背着绿色的邮包，
　　踏着露水，走遍村庄。
　　你挨门传送书报，
　　也把姑娘的信件捎上。
　　你使村民及时知道祖国的变化；
　　也给姑娘带来了幸福的微笑。
　　从春天到严冬，
　　不管风雪和冰霜，
　　多少个这样的早晨，
　　你把书报、信件传送。
　　是因为你懂得这平凡的工作，
　　饱含着不平常的事迹，
　　是因为你为社会主义建设，
　　献上了你赤诚的心。
　　每天你披着曙光，
　　行进在村边的小路上，
　　你的邮包里装着书报、信件，
　　也装着你对祖国的爱情……。
