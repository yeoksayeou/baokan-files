Title: Saudi Visit to Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 6

    Saudi Visit to Lebanon
    Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 10th—King Saud of Saudi Arabia arrived in Beirut today to begin a five-day state visit to Lebanon. King Saud will hold talks with Lebanese government leaders on the situation in the Middle East.
    As the United States continues to push its plot against Syria, and Turkish troops continue to mass and provoke on the Syrian border, the situation in the region remains tense. King Saud's visit at this time, given the current Middle East situation, is considered to be of great significance.

### 沙特访问黎巴嫩

1957-10-12
第6版()
专栏：

    沙特访问黎巴嫩
    据新华社10日讯　贝鲁特消息：沙特阿拉伯国王沙特在今天到达贝鲁特，开始在黎巴嫩作为期五天的国事访问。沙特国王将同黎巴嫩政府领导人就中东局势举行会谈。
    由于美国正在继续推动敌对叙利亚的阴谋，土耳其的军队正继续在叙利亚边境集结和挑衅，这个地区的局势仍然紧张。沙特国王在目前中东局势下作这次访问被认为是有重要意义的。
