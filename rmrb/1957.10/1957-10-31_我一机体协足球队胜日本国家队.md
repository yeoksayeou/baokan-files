Title: Our First Machine Sports Association Football Team Defeats Japanese National Team
Author:
Date: 1957-10-31
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, October 29th: The Chinese First Machine Sports Association football team defeated the Japanese National football team in Shenyang today with a score of three to two. Nearly ten thousand spectators watched the match.

The Japanese National football team will leave Shenyang by train on the evening of the 30th to go to Shanghai.

### 我一机体协足球队胜日本国家队

1957-10-31
第8版()
专栏：体育新闻

    我一机体协足球队胜日本国家队
    据新华社沈阳29日电　中国第一机械体育协会足球队今天在沈阳以三比二战胜了日本国家足球队。近万名观众观看了这场比赛。
    日本国家足球队将在30日晚上乘火车离开沈阳去上海。
