Title: Hungarian Tennis Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-11
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - The Hungarian tennis team, a group of seven, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Sports Commission.

The Hungarian tennis team consists of four male athletes and two female athletes. They are scheduled to hold their first friendly match with the Chinese tennis team in Beijing on the 12th.

The leader of the Hungarian tennis team is Mr. Szarowitzky, the secretary of the Hungarian Tennis Association.

### 匈牙利网球队到京

1957-10-11
第8版()
专栏：

    匈牙利网球队到京
    据新华社10日讯　匈牙利网球队一行七人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应体育运动委员会的邀请来中国访问的。
    匈牙利网球队中，有男运动员四名和女运动员两名。他们预定12日在北京同中国网球队举行第一次友谊比赛。
    匈牙利网球队领队是匈牙利网球协会秘书长沙罗维茨基。
