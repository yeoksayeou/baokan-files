    Wang Zhangxiang, a high school student and member of the Gaomiao Agricultural Cooperative in the Caotan District of Xi'an, has become a production expert in the cooperative after two years of labor training since he joined agricultural production in 1955. In production, Wang Zhangxiang often pays attention to studying and using new agricultural tools, and teaches the cooperative members the techniques he has learned for using new agricultural tools. Above picture: Wang Zhangxiang (right) explains the performance and operating techniques of the horse-drawn harvester to the cooperative members.
                                   Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Jie
