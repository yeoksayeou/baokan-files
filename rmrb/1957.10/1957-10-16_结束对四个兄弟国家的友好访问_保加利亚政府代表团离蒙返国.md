Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Leaves Mongolia for Home After Concluding Friendly Visits to Four Fraternal Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-10-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 15th. The Bulgarian government delegation, led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Yugov, left Ulaanbaatar by special plane this afternoon. With this, the delegation concluded its friendly visits to the four fraternal countries of China, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia.

The leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the government, Tsedenbal, Damba, Surenjab, Tsend, Damdin, Avirmed, Zagvaral, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, He Ying, and diplomatic envoys from various countries went to the airport to see the distinguished guests off.

Yugov delivered a farewell speech, saying that they would return with great joy. They will convey the hard work and good life of the Mongolian people they saw during their visit to Mongolia to the Bulgarian people. He expressed his gratitude to the party, government, and people of Mongolia.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 14th. The People's Republic of Mongolia and the People's Republic of Bulgaria signed a barter agreement for 1958-1960 in Ulaanbaatar this afternoon. According to the agreement, Mongolia will supply Bulgaria with camel hair, horse mane, and other animal products; Bulgaria will supply Mongolia with tobacco, dried fruits, and daily necessities.

Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, Tsedenbal, and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Yugov, attended the signing ceremony. The agreement was signed by the Mongolian Minister of Commerce, Delegerjab, and the Bulgarian Minister of Trade, Taskov.

### 结束对四个兄弟国家的友好访问  保加利亚政府代表团离蒙返国

1957-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　结束对四个兄弟国家的友好访问
    保加利亚政府代表团离蒙返国
    新华社乌兰巴托15日电　由保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫率领的保加利亚政府代表团今天下午坐专机离开乌兰巴托。代表团至此结束了对中国、朝鲜、越南和蒙古四个兄弟国家的友好访问。
    蒙古人民革命党和政府的领导人泽登巴尔、达姆巴、苏伦扎布、曾德、达姆丁、阿维尔齐德、扎格瓦尔拉和中国驻蒙古大使何英以及各国外交使节都到机场欢送贵宾。
    于哥夫致告别词说，他们将带着满怀的喜悦回去。他们将把在蒙古访问期间所看到的蒙古人民的勤劳和美好的生活转达给保加利亚人民。他向蒙古的党、政府和人民表示谢意。
    新华社乌兰巴托14日电　蒙古人民共和国和保加利亚人民共和国今天下午在乌兰巴托签订了1958—1960年的换货协定。根据协定，蒙古将供给保加利亚驼毛、马鬃和其他畜产品；保加利亚将供给蒙古烟草、干果和日用品等。
    蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔和保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫出席了签字仪式。在协定上签字的是蒙古商业部长德勒格尔扎布、保加利亚贸易部长塔斯科夫。
