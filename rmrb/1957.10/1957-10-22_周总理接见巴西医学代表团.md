Title: Premier Zhou Meets Brazilian Medical Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 21st October: Premier Zhou Enlai met with the entire Brazilian medical delegation headed by delegation leader Carvalho at 3 p.m. on the 21st. Brazilian industrialist Mr. and Mrs. Monteiro were also received at the same time.

Present at the meeting were Cui Yitian, Acting President of the Chinese Medical Association, and Fang Shishan, Vice President, among others.

Yu Peiwen, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was also present.

### 周总理接见巴西医学代表团

1957-10-22
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理接见巴西医学代表团
    新华社21日讯　周恩来总理21日下午三时接见了以卡瓦洛团长为首的巴西医学代表团全体人员。巴西实业家蒙特里奥夫妇也被同时接见。
    接见时在座的有中华医学会代理会长崔义田、副会长方石珊等。
    外交部美洲澳洲司副司长俞沛文也在座。
