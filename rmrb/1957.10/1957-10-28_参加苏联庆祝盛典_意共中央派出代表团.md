Title: Italian Communist Party Central Committee Sends Delegation to Participate in Soviet Celebration

Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 6

    Participating in Soviet Celebration
    Italian Communist Party Central Committee Sends Delegation

    Xinhua News Agency, 27th. Rome's "l'Unità" reported that the leading body of the Italian Communist Party decided to accept the invitation of the CPSU Central Committee and send a delegation to participate in the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. The delegation is composed of the following comrades: Togliatti, Ingrao, Scozzimaro, Rossi, Novella, Vidali, Marchesi, and Berardi.

### 参加苏联庆祝盛典  意共中央派出代表团

1957-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

    参加苏联庆祝盛典
    意共中央派出代表团
    新华社27日讯　罗马“团结报”报道，意大利共产党领导机构决定接受苏共中央邀请，派代表团去参加伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年庆祝典礼。代表团由下列同志组成：陶里亚蒂、英格拉奥、斯科奇马罗、罗西欧、诺维拉、维达里、马尔契利诺和贝拉第。
