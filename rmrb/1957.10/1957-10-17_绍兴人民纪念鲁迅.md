Title: Shaoxing People Commemorate Lu Xun
Author: Shao Zhizhong
Date: 1957-10-17
Page: 8
　　
　　The people of Lu Xun's hometown, Shaoxing, are preparing various activities to commemorate the 21st anniversary of the death of the great revolutionary writer Lu Xun.

　　On the day of the anniversary (October 19), Shaoxing city and county will hold a memorial meeting with more than 1,000 people at the Lu Xun Theater. Before the anniversary, Shaoxing City will hold a large-scale lecture to commemorate Lu Xun; secondary schools and cultural and educational circles will also hold commemorative activities such as seminars, special lectures, and literary evenings; the Shaoxing City "Children's Home" will hold a grand team activity for Young Pioneers in the Baicao Garden of Lu Xun's former residence. During the anniversary, the Shaoxing City Broadcasting Station will hold a broadcast commemorative program; the Shaoxing Lu Xun Memorial Hall will hold a forum for Lu Xun's relatives and friends, and the entire hall will be open for a month from September 25 to October 25; the trade union film projection team will also screen the documentary "Lu Xun's Life" for the city's workers. In order to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, the memorial hall will also hold an exhibition on "Lu Xun's Friendship with the Soviet Union" after the anniversary. (Shao Zhizhong)

### 绍兴人民纪念鲁迅
邵治中
1957-10-17
第8版()
专栏：

　　绍兴人民纪念鲁迅
    鲁迅故乡——绍兴的人民，正在准备各项活动来纪念伟大革命文豪鲁迅先生逝世二十一周年。
    在纪念日（10月19日）当天，绍兴市、县将在鲁迅剧院举行一千多人的纪念大会。纪念日前，绍兴市要举办一次纪念鲁迅的大型讲座；各中等学校和文教界也将举行座谈会、专题讲座、文艺晚会等纪念活动；绍兴市“少年之家”将在鲁迅故居的百草园举行一次少先队员的大队活动。纪念日期间，绍兴市广播站将举办广播纪念节目；绍兴鲁迅纪念馆将召开一次鲁迅亲友的座谈会，并从9月25日到10月25日将全馆开放一个月；工会电影放映队也将为全市职工放映“鲁迅生平”纪录片。为了庆祝十月革命四十周年，在纪念日后，纪念馆还将举办一次“鲁迅与苏联的友谊”展览会。　　　　　（邵治中）
