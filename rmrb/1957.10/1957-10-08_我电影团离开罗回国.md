Title: Our Film Delegation Leaves Cairo for Home
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 5th: The Chinese film delegation participating in the Cairo Chinese Film Festival left here this morning to return home.

    The delegation held a tea party on the afternoon of October 4th to bid farewell to Egyptian friends and thank them for their warm reception. About one hundred well-known Egyptian film workers and artists were invited to attend the tea party.

    In the past few days, the Chinese film delegation visited important Egyptian cities such as Port Said, Alexandria, and Luxor. They laid wreaths at the martyrs' cemetery in the heroic city of Port Said.

### 我电影团离开罗回国

1957-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    我电影团离开罗回国
    据新华社开罗5日电　参加开罗的中国电影节的中国电影代表团在今天上午离开这里回国。
    代表团在10月4日下午举行茶会，向埃及朋友们告别并且感谢他们的热情接待。应邀出席茶会的有约一百名著名的埃及电影工作者和艺术家。
    在过去几天中，中国电影代表团访问了塞得港、亚历山大港和路克苏尔等埃及重要城市。他们曾经在英雄城市塞得港的烈士墓前献了花圈。
