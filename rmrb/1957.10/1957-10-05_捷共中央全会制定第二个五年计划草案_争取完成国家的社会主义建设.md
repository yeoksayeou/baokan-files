Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee Plenum Formulates Draft of the Second Five-Year Plan, Striving to Complete the Socialist Construction of the Country
Author:
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 3rd dispatch: The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party held a plenary session from September 30th to October 2nd. The meeting discussed various issues related to the preparations for discussing the implementation of the resolutions of the Czechoslovak Communist Party National Conference and other tasks of socialist construction, as well as a draft directive on the second five-year plan for the development of the national economy from 1956-1960, tasks for guaranteeing the completion of the 1958 plan, and a proposal on the organization of industrial leadership, planning, and funding. Speaking on these issues were Novotny, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, and Political Bureau members Siroky and alternate member Simunek.

The meeting approved a decision on discussing the implementation of the resolutions of the Czechoslovak Communist Party National Conference and other tasks of socialist construction, and an appeal to all party organizations and members, organizations at all levels of the National Front, and the working people of the whole country, calling on them to express their opinions on how the resolutions of the National Conference are being implemented and on other tasks of socialist construction. Discussions will be held in November and December of this year, and the comprehensive results of the discussions will be taken into account by the Central Committee plenary session to be held at the beginning of next year.

The meeting also adopted a decision on the draft directive for the second five-year plan.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th dispatch: Czechoslovak Radio broadcast on the 3rd the resolution of the plenary session of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party on the basic tasks of Czechoslovakia's second five-year plan for the development of the national economy from 1956-1960. This resolution expands the tasks of some economic sectors in the five-year plan directive adopted by the Czechoslovak Communist Party National Congress last year.

The resolution points out that the goal of the draft of the second five-year plan is to complete the socialist construction of the country and to solve the major tasks put forward by the Czechoslovak Communist Party National Congress to the greatest possible extent.

In terms of industrial development, the resolution stipulates that the total output value in 1960 will increase by 56% compared to 1955. Compared with the original directive, the main focus will be on increasing the extraction of fuel, increasing the production of steel and steel products, chemical industry products, precision machinery manufacturing products, and goods supplied to residents.

The resolution stipulates that the total output value of agricultural products in 1960 will increase by 30% compared to 1955, which is the output stipulated in the original directive, while accelerating the speed of rural collectivization.

The resolution points out that by 1960, the number of people working in the national economy will reach 4,754,000, of which 1,836,000 will be in the industrial sector. The personal consumption of residents in 1960 will increase by about one-third compared to 1955. Thirty-three hundred thousand houses will be built in five years, thirty thousand more than originally planned.

The resolution also stipulates that it is necessary to start solving the problem of accelerating the development of Slovakia's productive forces, fully utilize production potential and reserves, and improve the planning and allocation of funds for the national economy.

### 捷共中央全会制定第二个五年计划草案  争取完成国家的社会主义建设

1957-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    捷共中央全会制定第二个五年计划草案
    争取完成国家的社会主义建设
    新华社3日讯　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会从9月30日到10月2日举行了全体会议。会议讨论了同准备讨论捷共全国会议各项决定和建设社会主义的其他任务的执行情况有关的各项问题，以及关于1956—1960年发展国民经济的第二个五年计划的指示草案、保证完成1958年计划的任务和一项关于工业的领导、计划、资金的组织工作的建议。就这些问题发言的有捷共中央第一书记诺沃提尼、政治局委员西罗基和候补委员西穆涅克。
    会议批准了关于讨论捷共全国会议各项决定和建设社会主义的其他任务的执行情况的决定，和告一切党组织和党员、民族阵线各级组织和全国劳动人民书，其中号召他们就全国会议的决定如何得到执行和就建设社会主义的其他任务表示他们的意见。讨论将在今年11月和12月进行，讨论的全面结果将由在明年年初召开的中央委员会全体会议采取措施。
    会议还通过了一项关于第二个五年计划的指示草案的决定。
    新华社布拉格4日电　捷克斯洛伐克电台3日广播了捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会全体会议关于1956—1960年捷克斯洛伐克发展国民经济第二个五年计划的基本任务的决议。这个决议扩大了去年捷共全国代表会议通过的五年计划指示中一些经济部门的任务。
    决议指出，第二个五年计划草案的目标是要完成国家的社会主义建设，并且尽最大可能来解决捷共全国代表会议所提出的重大任务。
    在发展工业方面，决议规定1960年总产值比1955年增加56％。同原来的指示比较，主要将提高燃料的开采量、增加钢和钢材、化学工业产品、精密机器制造业产品和供应居民的商品的产量。
    决议规定，1960年农产品总产值将比1955年增加30％，也就是原来指示中所规定的产量，同时加快农村合作化的速度。
    决议指出，到1960年时，在国民经济中工作的人数将达到四百七十五万四千人，其中工业部门的人数将达到一百八十三万六千人。1960年居民的个人消费将比1955年增加三分之一左右。五年中将要建成三十三万套房子，比原来指示规定的多三万套。
    决议还规定，必须着手解决加速发展斯洛伐克生产力的问题，充分利用生产潜力和后备力量，改进国民经济的计划工作和拨款工作。
