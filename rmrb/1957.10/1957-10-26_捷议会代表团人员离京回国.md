Title: Czechoslovak Parliament Delegation Departs Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. Part of the members of the Czechoslovak National Assembly Delegation and the Prague Municipal Central People's Committee Delegation, a total of twelve people, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 24th to return to their country.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, members Wang Weizhou and Zhang Su, Yu Xinqing, and Liu Guanyi, Deputy Secretaries-General, and Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing. Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Bušiak, Mongolian Ambassador to China Lubusang, Korean Ambassador to China Li Yonghao, and embassy staff also saw them off at the airport.

### 捷议会代表团人员离京回国

1957-10-26
第3版()
专栏：

    捷议会代表团人员离京回国
    新华社24日讯　捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团部分团员和布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团共十二人在24日上午乘飞机离北京返国。
    到机场欢送的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通、委员王维舟和副秘书长张苏、余心清、刘贯一，北京市副市长冯基平等人。捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克、蒙古驻华大使鲁布桑、朝鲜驻华大使李永镐以及使馆人员也到机场欢送。
