Title: Dr. Hatta Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd: Former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta, his wife, and accompanying personnel, accompanied by Burhan and the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wijopranoto, left Beijing by special plane this morning for Urumqi. The distinguished guests will visit various parts of China before returning home.

Premier Zhou Enlai went to the airport to see the distinguished guests off. In his farewell speech, he said that Dr. Hatta's friendly visit to China has made an important contribution to further strengthening mutual understanding and friendly relations between China and Indonesia. We are very grateful to Dr. Hatta for expressing the sincere friendship of the Indonesian people to the Chinese people, and sincerely hope that Dr. Hatta will convey the brotherly respect and friendship of the Chinese people to the Indonesian people.

Premier Zhou Enlai wished the Indonesian people greater achievements in strengthening national unity, safeguarding the independence and sovereignty of the motherland, and developing the national economy. He wished the distinguished guests a safe and pleasant journey!

In his farewell speech at the airport, Dr. Hatta said that they had received a warm and friendly reception in various parts of China, which signifies that the friendly relations between Indonesia and China will continue to be consolidated and developed. He also said that China's construction is constantly advancing in overcoming difficulties, and he hopes that Indonesia and China can help each other in their respective constructions.

Finally, Dr. Hatta shouted: "Long live Indonesian-Chinese friendship, may world peace be realized!"

Premier Zhou and others accompanied the distinguished guests to the front of the plane and said goodbye to Dr. Hatta in Indonesian. Premier Zhou also asked Dr. Hatta to convey his greetings to President Sukarno and other leaders of the Indonesian government. Young women from the capital presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

Today, those who went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests included Chen Yi, Bo Yibo, Huang Yanpei, Zhang Zhizhong, Zhang Hanfu, heads of various departments of state organs, people from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to China. (Attached photo)

Dr. Hatta shakes hands with Premier Zhou Enlai to say goodbye. Our reporter Gao Liang photographed.

### 哈达博士离开北京

1957-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    哈达博士离开北京
    新华社3日讯  印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士和夫人及随行人员，由包尔汉和印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托等陪同，在今天早晨乘专机离开北京去乌鲁木齐。贵宾们将在中国各地参观后返国。
    周恩来总理到机场欢送贵宾。他在致欢送词时说，哈达博士这次来中国进行友好访问对进一步加强中国和印度尼西亚两国之间的相互了解和友好关系作出了重要的贡献。我们十分感谢哈达博士代表印度尼西亚人民向中国人民表达的真挚友情，并且诚恳地希望哈达博士将中国人民的兄弟般的敬意和友谊带给印度尼西亚人民。
    周恩来总理祝印度尼西亚人民在加强民族团结、维护祖国的独立主权、和发展民族经济方面，获得更大的成就。祝贵宾们一路平安，旅途愉快！
    哈达博士在机场的告别讲话中说，他们在中国各地受到了热情的友谊接待，这就标志着印度尼西亚和中国的友好关系将不断地巩固和发展。他还说，中国的建设事业正在克服困难中不断地前进，希望印度尼西亚和中国在各自的建设中能够互相帮助。
    哈达博士最后高呼：“印度尼西亚中国友好万岁，愿世界和平实现！”
    周总理等一直送贵宾们到飞机前面，并且用印度尼西亚话同哈达博士道别。周总理还请哈达博士转致他对苏加诺总统和印度尼西亚政府其他领导人的问候。首都女青年向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    今天，到机场为贵宾们送行的，还有陈毅、薄一波、黄炎培、张治中、章汉夫，国家机关各部门的负责人，各方面人士及各国驻华使节。（附图片）
