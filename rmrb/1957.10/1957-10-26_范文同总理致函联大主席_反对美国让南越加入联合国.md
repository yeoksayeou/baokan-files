Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Sends Letter to UN General Assembly President Opposing US Admission of South Vietnam to the UN
Author:
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 25th: Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, wrote a letter to the President of the United Nations General Assembly on October 24th, requesting the United Nations General Assembly to reject the recent US proposal passed in the Special Political Committee of the United Nations General Assembly regarding allowing the South Vietnamese regime to join the United Nations in the name of all of Vietnam.

    In his letter, Pham Van Dong pointed out that the US government's proposal seriously violates the United Nations Charter. The American conspiracy is a violation of the Geneva Accords, aimed at the long-term division of Vietnam, turning South Vietnam into an American military base, and threatening peace in Vietnam and Southeast Asia. In his letter, Pham Van Dong refuted the accusations made by the US representative at the meeting of the Special Political Committee of the United Nations General Assembly, which slandered the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for increasing its troops, illegally transporting military personnel and weapons into the North, and obstructing the unification of the Vietnamese nation.

### 范文同总理致函联大主席  反对美国让南越加入联合国

1957-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    范文同总理致函联大主席
    反对美国让南越加入联合国
    据新华社河内25日电　越南民主共和国总理范文同10月24日写信给联合国大会主席，要求联合国大会拒绝最近在联合国大会特别政治委员会上通过的关于允许南越政权以全越代表的名义加入联合国的美国提案。
    范文同在信中指出，美国政府的提案严重地违背了联合国宪章。美国的阴谋是违反日内瓦协议的，是为了长期分割越南、把南越变成美国军事基地，是为了威胁越南和东南亚的和平。范文同在信中驳斥了美国代表在联合国大会特别政治委员会会议上污蔑越南民主共和国增加军队、把军事人员和武器非法运进北方和阻碍越南国家的统一的说法。
