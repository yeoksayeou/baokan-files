Title: Prime Minister Daoud Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-10-29
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 28th: Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammed Daoud, and his entourage arrived in Shanghai on a special plane from Shenyang via Tianjin on the 28th for a visit.
    The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Shanghai Vice Mayors Cao Diqiu, Rong Yiren, Sheng Pihua, and 2,000 people from all walks of life.
    Accompanying the distinguished guests on their visit to Shanghai were Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Ding Guoyu, and Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad.
    In the evening, Prime Minister Daoud, accompanied by Cao Diqiu, toured the city. The Prime Minister also attended a reception tonight and watched Peking opera and dance performances.

### 达乌德首相到上海访问

1957-10-29
第3版()
专栏：

    达乌德首相到上海访问
    据新华社上海28日电　阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德和随行人员，28日自沈阳乘专机经天津到达上海访问。
    贵宾们在机场上受到上海市副市长曹荻秋、荣毅仁、盛丕华和各界群众两千人的热烈欢迎。
    陪同贵宾来上海访问的有外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰和阿富汗驻华大使萨马德。
    傍晚，达乌德首相由曹荻秋陪同游览了市区。首相今晚还出席了招待晚会，观看了京剧、舞蹈表演。
