Title: What Will Wuhan Steel Do Next Year?
Author: Xiao Ding
Date: 1957-10-30
Page: 4

    What Will Wuhan Steel Do Next Year?
    (Our newspaper's Hankou dispatch) Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation's basic construction tasks for next year have been preliminarily determined, and its workload accounts for about one-fifth of the total investment in the national metallurgical system. The No. 1 blast furnace will be completed and put into production by the end of next year, the Daye Iron Mine stripping project will be completed by the end of next year, the Wuhan Steel plant area leveling project will be completed by the end of the year, the coking and chemical plant will be partially put into production, and some equipment will be installed in the sintering plant and refractory material plant, and the steelmaking plant will also begin construction. (Xiao Ding)

### 武钢明年干什么？
肖定
1957-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

    武钢明年干什么？
    本报汉口电　武汉钢铁公司明年的基本建设任务已经初步确定，其工程量约占全国冶金系统总投资五分之一。一号高炉在明年年底竣工生产，大冶铁矿剥离工程要在明年年底完工，武钢厂区平整工程要在年底结束，炼焦化工厂要部分投入生产，烧结厂和耐火材料厂也要装好一部分设备，炼钢厂也要开工建设。　（肖定）
