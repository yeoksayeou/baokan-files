Title: New Gift for the October Revolution Festival: The Entire Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Station Generates Electricity
Author:
Date: 1957-10-16
Page: 5

    New Gift for the October Revolution Festival
    The Entire Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Station Generates Electricity
    (Report from our correspondent in Moscow, October 15th) The Soviet people have given another new gift to the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. At 8:40 PM on the 14th, as the artificial Earth satellite was orbiting for about 150 revolutions, the 20th, and last, generator of the Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Station was put into production. At this time, the power generation capacity of the hydroelectric station reached 2.1 million kilowatts, making it the largest hydroelectric station already built.

### 给十月革命节的新献礼  古比雪夫水电站全部发电

1957-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

    给十月革命节的新献礼
    古比雪夫水电站全部发电
    本报莫斯科15日电　苏联人民又给十月革命四十周年一份新的献礼。当人造地球卫星在天空运行大约一百五十圈的时候，14日下午八时四十分，古比雪夫水电站第二十部，也就是最后一部发电机投入了生产，这时，水电站的发电力达到二百一十万瓩，它成为已经建成的水电站中最大的一个。
