Title: Marxist-Leninist Classics Published in Large Quantities: Over 200 Titles and 20 Million Copies Printed in Eight Years
Author:
Date: 1957-10-19
Page: 8
Xinhua News Agency, October 16th: The complete works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin are being translated into Chinese and published successively. Currently, one volume of "The Complete Works of Marx and Engels" has been published, five volumes of "The Complete Works of Lenin," and eleven volumes of "The Complete Works of Stalin." "The Complete Works of Marx and Engels" will consist of thirty volumes, "The Complete Works of Lenin" will consist of thirty-five volumes, and "The Complete Works of Stalin" has been compiled into thirteen volumes in the Soviet Union. The Compilation and Translation Bureau of the Works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China plans to complete all translations before 1960. From now until the end of next year, it will publish four volumes of "The Complete Works of Marx and Engels," five volumes of "The Complete Works of Lenin," and one volume of "The Complete Works of Stalin."

In the past eight years, the People's Publishing House has published 211 titles of Marxist-Leninist classics, totaling 27,798,000 copies. The Life, Reading, and New Knowledge Tri-Union Bookstore, China Youth Publishing House, and Workers' Publishing House have also published some classic works.

The classic works published by the People's Publishing House include complete works, selected works, and single editions. The same work often has two or three versions, and most have been published in more than ten editions. Among them, 590,000 copies of "The Communist Manifesto" have been issued, 140,000 copies of "Das Kapital," 1,280,000 copies of "Dialectical and Historical Materialism," 560,000 copies of "The Complete Works of Lenin" (five volumes published), and 6,520,000 copies of "The Complete Works of Stalin" (eleven volumes published). "The Complete Works of Marx and Engels" (Volume 1), which was only published at the end of last year, has also been issued 100,000 copies. In addition, 9 million copies of supplementary and popular readings of Marxist-Leninist classics have been published.

Marxist-Leninist classics are also being gradually translated into the languages of various ethnic minorities in China. From 1953 to 1957, the Nationalities Publishing House published more than 73,000 copies of Marxist-Leninist classics in ethnic minority languages. Among them, Mongolian versions include "Marxism and the National Question" and "Problems of Leninism"; Uyghur and Kazakh versions include "Dialectical and Historical Materialism," and Tibetan versions include "The National Question and Leninism." The Nationalities Publishing House also plans to publish eleven more Marxist-Leninist classics in ethnic minority languages next year.

### 马列主义经典著作大量出版  八年间出版经典著作二百多种二千多万册

1957-10-19
第8版()
专栏：

　　马列主义经典著作大量出版
    八年间出版经典著作二百多种二千多万册
    新华社16日讯  马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的全集正在陆续译成中文出版。现在，“马克思恩格斯全集”已经出了一卷，“列宁全集”出了五卷，“斯大林全集”出了十一卷。“马克思恩格斯全集”共三十卷，“列宁全集”共三十五卷，“斯大林全集”在苏联已编成十三卷。中共中央马克思恩格斯列宁斯大林著作编译局计划在1960年前全部翻译完毕，从现在起到明年底将出版“马克思恩格斯全集”四卷，“列宁全集”五卷和“斯大林全集”一卷。
    在过去的八年间，人民出版社已经出版了马列主义的经典著作二百一十一种，共计二千七百九十八万多册。生活·读书·新知三联书店、中国青年出版社和工人出版社等出版社也还出版了一部分经典著作。
    人民出版社出版的经典著作，有全集、选集和单行本，同一著作常有两、三种版本，而且大都出了十版以上。其中，“共产党宣言”发行了五十九万册，“资本论”发行了十四万部，“辩证唯物主义与历史唯物主义”发行了一百二十八万册，“列宁全集”（已出五卷）发行了五十六万册，“斯大林全集”（已出十一卷）发行了六百五十二万册。去年底才出版的“马克思恩格斯全集”（第一卷）也已发行了十万册。此外，还出版了九百万册马列主义经典著作的辅助读物和通俗读物。
    马列主义的经典著作还被逐步翻译成我国各少数民族的文字。从1953年到1957年，民族出版社就用少数民族文字出版了七万三千多册马列主义经典著作。其中，蒙文的有“马克思主义与民族问题”和“列宁主义问题”；维吾尔文和哈萨克文的有“辩证唯物主义与历史唯物主义”，藏文的有“民族问题与列宁主义”。民族出版社还计划在明年再用少数民族文字出版十一种马列主义经典著作。
