Title: Opening of the Argentine Modern Print and Painting Exhibition
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 21st: The Argentine Modern Print and Painting Exhibition opened on October 21st at Beihai Park in Beijing.

The exhibition includes fifty original prints, watercolors, and pencil drawings, and thirty-three oil painting reproductions by fifty-five artists. These works were brought by the delegation of the Argentine-Chinese Cultural Association, which is currently on a friendly visit to our country. These works not only represent the artistic traditions of Argentina, but also reflect the positive direction of the realistic spirit of Argentine artists and the current aspirations of the united Argentine people.

This is the first time an Argentine exhibition has been held in our country.

### 阿根廷现代版画绘画展览开幕

1957-10-24
第3版()
专栏：

    阿根廷现代版画绘画展览开幕
    新华社21日讯　阿根廷现代版画绘画展览21日在北京北海公园开幕。
    这个展览包括五十五位画家的版画、水彩画、铅笔画原作五十幅和油画复制品三十三幅。这些作品是正在我国进行友好访问的阿中文化协会代表团带来的。这些作品不仅代表了阿根廷的艺术传统，并且反映了阿根廷艺术家的现实主义精神的积极方向和团结起来的阿根廷人民当前的热望。
    阿根廷展览在我国的展出，这还是第一次。
