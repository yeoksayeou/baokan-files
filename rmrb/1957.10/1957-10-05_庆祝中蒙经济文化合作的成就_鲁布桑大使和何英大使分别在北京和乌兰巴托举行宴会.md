Title: Celebrating the Achievements of Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation: Ambassador Lubsan and Ambassador He Ying Hold Banquets in Beijing and Ulaanbaatar Respectively
Author:
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 4th: Mongolian People's Republic Ambassador Lubsan held a banquet at the Beijing Hotel this evening to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai attended this evening's banquet.

Premier Zhou Enlai said at the banquet: The signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement is a major event in the development of friendly and cooperative relations between the Chinese and Mongolian people. "Over the past five years, thanks to the smooth implementation of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Sino-Mongolian cooperation in the fields of economy, culture, education, science and technology has achieved considerable success, which has powerfully promoted the socialist construction of our two countries. The strengthening of Sino-Mongolian friendly cooperation is not only beneficial to the common cause of defending peace, but also an important contribution to strengthening the unity and cooperation of socialist countries." Premier Zhou Enlai expressed his hope that the economic and cultural cooperation between China and Mongolia will be further strengthened, and the fraternal friendship between the peoples of China and Mongolia will be increasingly consolidated and developed.

Ambassador Lubsan spoke at the beginning of the banquet. He said: The Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement "is not only the first treaty concluded between the peoples of Mongolia and China on an equal footing, but also a document of extremely important significance for consolidating and developing the friendly relations between the fraternal peoples of China and Mongolia. Our two peoples are fighting for a common goal – building socialism. In the past five years, the friendly relations between our two countries have been greatly consolidated. Our economic and cultural cooperation has also expanded year after year. We sincerely hope that our friendship and cooperation will continue to develop in the future."

Also present at the banquet this evening were Vice Premiers of the State Council Ulanhu and Li Xiannian, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Huang Yanpei and Guo Moruo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, heads of relevant departments of the Chinese government and people from all walks of life, as well as diplomatic envoys of various countries to China.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, October 4th: Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia He Ying held a reception this evening to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Ambassador He Ying said at the reception that the development of economic and cultural cooperation between China and Mongolia on the basis of equality and mutual assistance has played an inestimable and positive role in accelerating the socialist construction of the two countries and promoting economic and cultural prosperity.

Vice Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers, Avilzhid, said in his speech that on the basis of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the two countries have also signed several other agreements of great significance to Mongolia's economic and cultural construction over the past five years. At present, the Chinese people are helping Mongolia build several industrial enterprises and cultural institutions with materials and manpower. The Mongolian people express their gratitude for the selfless help of the Chinese people.

Leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the government, including Sürenjav, Damdin, and Molomjamts, foreign diplomatic envoys to Mongolia, as well as representatives of Chinese experts and employees in Mongolia, and representatives of overseas Chinese attended the reception this evening.

### 庆祝中蒙经济文化合作的成就  鲁布桑大使和何英大使分别在北京和乌兰巴托举行宴会

1957-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝中蒙经济文化合作的成就
    鲁布桑大使和何英大使分别在北京和乌兰巴托举行宴会
    据新华社4日讯　蒙古人民共和国驻华大使鲁布桑今天晚间在北京饭店举行宴会，庆祝中蒙经济及文化合作协定签订五周年。
    朱德副主席、周恩来总理出席了今晚的宴会。
    周恩来总理在宴会上讲话说：中蒙经济及文化合作协定的签订是中蒙两国人民友好合作关系发展中的一个重大事件。“五年来，由于中蒙经济及文化合作协定的顺利执行，中蒙两国在经济、文化、教育和科学技术方面的合作取得了不少成就，有力地促进了我们两国的社会主义建设事业。中蒙两国友好合作的加强，不仅有利共同关心的保卫和平的事业，而且对于加强社会主义国家的团结和合作也是一项重要的贡献。”周恩来总理希望中蒙两国在经济、文化方面的友好合作将会进一步加强，中蒙两国人民的兄弟友谊将会日益巩固和发展。
    鲁布桑大使在宴会开始时讲话。他说：中蒙经济及文化合作协定，“不仅是一个蒙中两国人民第一次在平等的基础上所缔结的条约，而且也是为巩固和发展中蒙两国兄弟人民之间友好关系的一个具有极其重要意义的文件。我们两国人民正在为着一个共同的目标——建设社会主义而奋斗。在过去的五年中，我们两国的友好关系得到了极大的巩固。我们之间的经济、文化合作也在一年又一年地扩大了。我们衷心地希望我们的友谊与合作今后还要继续地发展。”
    出席今晚宴会的，还有国务院副总理乌兰夫、李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培、郭沫若，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国政府有关部门负责人和各界人士，以及各国驻华使节。
    据新华社乌兰巴托4日电　中国驻蒙古大使何英今天晚间举行招待会，庆祝中蒙两国经济及文化合作协定签订五周年。
    何英大使在招待会上讲话说，中蒙两国在平等互助的基础上发展两国经济及文化合作，对加速两国社会主义建设、促进经济文化繁荣起了难以估价的良好的作用。
    蒙古部长会议副主席阿维尔齐德在讲话中说，五年来蒙中两国在经济及文化合作协定的基础上还签订了对蒙古经济、文化建设事业有巨大意义的其他几项协定。目前，中国人民正在以物资、人力帮助蒙古建设着几个工业企业和文化机构。蒙古人民对中国人民的无私帮助表示感谢。
    蒙古人民革命党和政府的领导人苏伦扎布、达木丁、莫洛姆扎木茨，外国驻蒙古的外交使节以及在蒙古的中国专家、员工代表和华侨代表等出席了今晚的招待会。
