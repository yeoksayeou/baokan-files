Title: Launching a Tide of Commemoration for the October Revolution Among People Nationwide and in All Sectors
Author:
Date: 1957-10-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th:

    The China-Soviet Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Communist Youth League Central Committee, the All-China Women's Federation, the China Democratic National Construction Association, and All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce have respectively issued notices.
    The China-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters issued a notice on October 5th regarding the celebration of the fortieth anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. The full text is as follows:
    Celebrating the fortieth anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution holds extremely significant meaning for the people of the world and the Chinese people. The October Revolution ushered in the most magnificent new era in the history of human development, pointing out the path to complete liberation for all oppressed classes and oppressed nations worldwide. Over the past forty years, under the leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government, the Soviet people have achieved brilliant accomplishments in the construction of socialism and communism, and have made tremendous contributions to the cause of defending world peace and human progress. The vivid historical facts of the Soviet Union not only demonstrate the infinite vitality of Marxism-Leninism in theory, but also prove the unparalleled superiority of the socialist system. The Chinese people have consistently regarded the Chinese revolution as a continuation of the great October Revolution, and have basically achieved victory in the socialist revolution and socialist construction along the path of the October Revolution. Therefore, this year it is necessary to solemnly and grandly celebrate the fortieth anniversary of the October Revolution, vigorously publicize the great significance of the October Revolution, the achievements of Soviet construction, the Soviet peaceful foreign policy, and the important role of the Sino-Soviet friendship alliance in safeguarding world peace; it is necessary to continue to encourage our people to learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union and the spirit of hard work and thrift in building the country of the Soviet people, in order to strengthen the confidence of our people in building socialism; it is necessary to further enhance the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples, consolidate the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and strengthen the great unity of the world proletariat and all peace-loving people. Let us struggle for new victories in the cause of human progress and safeguarding world peace along the glorious path of the October Revolution.
    In accordance with the spirit of the resolution of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese People for Celebrating the Fortieth Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, the following specific tasks are hereby notified:
    1. The China-Soviet Friendship Associations of all provinces, cities, and autonomous regions should immediately work with the local Political Consultative Conferences and other people's organizations to plan for the preparations for holding celebration meetings with the participation of representatives from all walks of life on or before the festival. After National Day, the China-Soviet Friendship Associations at all levels should launch a publicity campaign, generally for a period of one month. Through celebration activities, use the vivid example of the Soviet Union to conduct a universal and in-depth publicity and education campaign on Marxism-Leninism and socialism and communism for the broad masses of cadres and people.
    2. The China-Soviet Friendship Associations at all levels should, according to the conditions, fully utilize existing propaganda positions and tools such as China-Soviet Friendship Halls, galleries, and display windows, and unite with other relevant units to carry out various forms of publicity activities, such as holding various forms of report meetings, lecture meetings, symposiums, various forms of photo exhibitions, as well as report meetings introducing Soviet literary works, storytelling sessions, poetry recitations, and music appreciation and other publicity and cultural activities. China-Soviet friendship propaganda vehicles should also go deep into the countryside for publicity during this period. In the publicity, they should make full use of the more than 30 types of written and visual propaganda materials such as propaganda reference materials, books, exhibition photos, wall charts, picture albums, propaganda paintings, and slides compiled and published jointly by the China-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and other agencies; at the same time, refer to articles published in other newspapers and periodicals related to the commemoration of the October Revolution to carry out publicity activities.

### 在全国各地和各界人民中  掀起纪念十月革命的热潮

1957-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    在全国各地和各界人民中
    掀起纪念十月革命的热潮
    中苏友协、总工会、团中央、妇联、民建和工商联等分别发出通知
    新华社6日讯　中苏友好协会总会5日发出关于庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的通知。全文如下：
    庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年，对全世界人民和中国人民都具有极为重大的意义。十月革命开创了人类发展史上最伟大的新时代，指出了全世界一切被压迫阶级和被压迫民族彻底解放的道路。四十年来，苏联人民在苏联共产党和苏联政府的领导下，在社会主义和共产主义的建设方面取得了辉煌的成就，并且对保卫世界和平与人类进步的事业作出了巨大的贡献。苏联生动的历史事实，不仅从理论上显示了马克思列宁主义的无限生命力，同时也证明了社会主义制度的无比优越性。中国人民一贯把中国革命看作是伟大十月革命的继续，并且沿着十月革命的道路已基本上取得了社会主义革命和社会主义建设的胜利。因此，今年必须庄严和隆重地庆祝十月革命四十周年，大力宣传十月革命的伟大意义、苏联建设的成就、苏联和平外交政策和中苏友好同盟对保卫世界和平的重要作用；必须继续鼓舞我国人民学习苏联的先进经验和苏联人民艰苦奋斗、勤俭建国的精神，以加强我国人民建设社会主义的信心；必须更进一步增进中苏两国人民的兄弟友谊，巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营，加强全世界无产阶级和一切爱好和平人民的大团结。沿着十月革命的光辉道路，为争取人类进步事业和保卫世界和平的新胜利而奋斗。
    根据中国人民庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年筹备委员会决议的精神，特就各项具体工作，作如下通知：
    一、各省、市、自治区中苏友好协会应即与当地政治协商会议及其他人民团体共同筹划关于在节日或节日前夕举行有各界代表参加的庆祝大会的准备事宜。各级中苏友好协会应在国庆节以后，大体以一个月的时间展开宣传工作。通过庆祝活动，以苏联活生生的榜样，向广大干部和人民群众进行一次普遍深入的有关马克思列宁主义和社会主义与共产主义的宣传教育。
    二、各级中苏友好协会应根据条件，充分地利用现有的宣传阵地和宣传工具如中苏友好馆、画廊、橱窗等，并联合其他的有关单位开展多种多样形式的宣传活动，例如举办各种形式的报告会、讲演会、座谈会，各种形式的图片展览会，以及介绍苏联文学作品的报告会、故事会、诗歌朗诵、音乐欣赏等宣传和文化活动。中苏友好宣传车在此期间也应深入农村进行宣传。在宣传时应充分利用中苏友好协会总会与其他机关联合编印和出版的宣传参考资料、书籍、展览照片、挂图、画册、宣传画、幻灯片等三十余种文字和形象化的宣传材料；同时参考其他报刊发表的有关纪念十月革命的文章等展开宣传活动。
    三、各级中苏友好协会应根据中国人民庆祝十月社会主义革命筹备委员会决议的精神，制定庆祝十月革命四十周年的宣传计划。此外，并应积极协助当地政治协商会议做好有关各项庆祝活动的筹备工作。
    本报讯　中华全国总工会，中国共产主义青年团中央、中华全国民主青年联合会和中华全国学生联合会，中华全国妇女联合会，中国民主建国会中央和中华全国工商业联合会，已分别发出关于庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的通知，号召全国职工、青年、妇女和工商界等庄严和隆重地庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念，展开各种纪念和宣传活动。这些通知的全文见今日本报第五版。
