Title: Wholeheartedly Support Syria's Just Struggle: Shanghai Press Circles Hold Gathering to Welcome Syrian Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-21
Page: 3

    Wholeheartedly Support Syria's Just Struggle
    Shanghai Press Circles Hold Gathering to Welcome Syrian Journalists Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 19th: Jin Zhonghua, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association and President of the Shanghai Journalists Liaison Association, said at a symposium today welcoming the Syrian Journalists Delegation: Shanghai's press circles wholeheartedly support the Syrian people's just struggle against aggression and believe that the Syrian people, like the Egyptian people, will achieve the final victory in their struggle.
    The Syrian Journalists Delegation arrived in Shanghai on the 15th after visiting Nanjing. The guests introduced the current situation of the struggle in Syria at a welcome symposium held today by Shanghai journalists. They said: With the support of peace-loving people all over the world, the Syrian people are filled with confidence in victory, and the imperialists' conspiracy to invade Syria will surely be smashed. Sidawi, director of Victory Newspaper, said: "Premier Zhou Enlai said that the victory of the Syrian people is the victory of the Chinese people. In our contacts with all levels in China, we have felt this profound feeling, which makes us extremely moved."
    At the symposium, journalists from Shanghai and Syria exchanged information about their work. Many editors and reporters from Shanghai newspapers pointed out that news from Syria is currently one of the most concerning news for readers, and newspapers are publishing it in large amounts. The newspapers have also received a large number of readers' letters expressing their full support for the Syrian people.
    The Syrian friends have made extensive visits in Shanghai in the past few days.

### 全力支持叙利亚的正义斗争  上海新闻界集会欢迎叙利亚新闻工作者代表团

1957-10-21
第3版()
专栏：

    全力支持叙利亚的正义斗争
    上海新闻界集会欢迎叙利亚新闻工作者代表团
    新华社上海19日电  中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长、上海新闻工作者联谊会会长金仲华今天在欢迎叙利亚新闻工作者代表团的座谈会上说：上海新闻界全力支援叙利亚人民反对侵略的正义斗争，相信叙利亚人民将和埃及人民一样，将要获得斗争的最后胜利。
    叙利亚新闻工作者代表团是15日由南京到达上海访问的。客人们今天在上海市新闻工作者举行的欢迎座谈会上介绍了叙利亚目前的斗争形势。他们说：在全世界和平人民的支持下，叙利亚人民正充满着胜利的信心，帝国主义侵略叙利亚的阴谋一定能够粉碎。胜利报社长西多威说：“周恩来总理说过，叙利亚人民的胜利，就是中国人民的胜利，我们在中国和各阶层的接触中，都体会到这种深厚的感情，使我们极为感动。”
    在座谈会上，上海和叙利亚的新闻工作者交流了工作情况。上海的许多报纸编辑、记者指出，来自叙利亚的消息，目前是读者最关心的消息之一，报纸都以大量篇幅刊登；报纸还收到大批的读者来信，表示对叙利亚人民的全力支援。
    叙利亚朋友们这几天曾在上海作了广泛的参观访问。
