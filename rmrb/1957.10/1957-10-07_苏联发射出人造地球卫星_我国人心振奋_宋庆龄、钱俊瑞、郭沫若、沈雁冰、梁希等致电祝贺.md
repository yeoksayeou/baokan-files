Title: The Soviet Union Launches Artificial Earth Satellite, Stirring Chinese Hearts; Song Qingling, Qian Junrui, Guo Moruo, Shen Yanbing, Liang Xi, and Others Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1957-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th:

    Soviet Union Launches Artificial Earth Satellite
    Our Country's People are Thrilled
    Song Qingling, Qian Junrui, Guo Moruo, Shen Yanbing, Liang Xi and Others Send Congratulatory Telegrams

    Song Qingling and Qian Junrui's Congratulatory Telegram
    Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: Song Qingling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, sent a telegram on the 6th to Kalishyan, Acting Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, congratulating them on the Soviet Union's launch of the world's first artificial Earth satellite. The telegram reads as follows:

    The successful launch of the world's first artificial Earth satellite by the Soviet Union has brought tremendous excitement and encouragement to all the Chinese people. Our great ally, the Soviet Union, in order to safeguard world peace and benefit mankind, has successively discovered and mastered the production methods of atomic weapons and hydrogen weapons, first mastered the method of using atomic energy to produce electricity, launched the world's first intercontinental ballistic missile, and now has successfully launched the first artificial satellite. This undeniably proves once again the strength of the Soviet Union, the fortress of world peace, and proves that Soviet science and technology are at the world's most advanced level. The leap forward in Soviet science powerfully demonstrates the unparalleled superiority of the socialist system.

    The successful launch of the artificial satellite is another great contribution by the Soviet Union to the cause of world peace. The Chinese people express warm congratulations for the brilliant achievements made by the Soviet Union in scientific research on artificial Earth satellites and for the rapid advancement of the Soviet Union's scientific and technological level in the struggle to build communism and seek human happiness!

    Guo Moruo's Congratulatory Telegram
    Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: The President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences sent a telegram on the 6th to Academician Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, congratulating the Soviet Union on the launch of the world's first artificial satellite. The telegram reads as follows:

    The successful launch of the first artificial Earth satellite is another great and brilliant achievement of Soviet science and technology following the successful manufacture of the intercontinental missile. This is a very good gift to the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. All peace-loving people around the world are rejoicing because these epoch-making achievements in science and technology are a very powerful guarantee for safeguarding world peace and promoting human happiness. We Chinese people and Chinese scientists unanimously express our incomparable excitement and gratitude. We deeply recognize the superiority of the socialist system and the advancement of Soviet science and technology under the leadership of the Soviet Communist Party. On behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Chinese scientific and technological community, I would like to express my highest respect and heartfelt congratulations to the Soviet Academy of Sciences under your leadership and, through you, to the Soviet scientific and technological community. We must make our greatest efforts to resolutely accept the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, defend the socialist system, promote the development of China's scientific undertakings, and learn from you.

    Shen Yanbing's Congratulatory Telegram
    Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on the 6th to Academician Balkin of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, congratulating the Soviet Union on the launch of the world's first artificial Earth satellite. The telegram reads as follows:

    When we learned that the Soviet Union had successfully launched the world's first artificial satellite, all Chinese cultural workers felt very excited. This is another outstanding and great achievement of the Soviet scientific community, and an extremely valuable contribution from the Soviet Union to the world's science and culture. We would like to extend our warm congratulations to you and wish Soviet scientists even more brilliant victories in exploring the secrets of the universe!

    Liang Xi's Congratulatory Telegram
    Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: Liang Xi, Chairman of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, sent a telegram on the 6th to the Chairman of the All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge of the Soviet Union, congratulating the Soviet Union on the launch of the world's first artificial Earth satellite. The telegram said:

    We are delighted to hear that the Soviet Union has successfully launched the world's first artificial satellite. This is a miracle in human history. People all over the world are rejoicing at this great new scientific achievement of the Soviet Union because it marks the beginning of a new era of science and the days of interstellar travel are approaching. This shows that Soviet science and technology are already leading the world, fully demonstrating the superiority of the socialist system, and that the scientific and technological workers of the working class can climb to the highest peak of world science at the fastest pace.

    On behalf of the 270,000 members of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, I would like to extend our warm congratulations to all members of the All-Union Association, all Soviet scientific and technological workers, and the Soviet people with incomparable excitement.

### 苏联发射出人造地球卫星  我国人心振奋  宋庆龄、钱俊瑞、郭沫若、沈雁冰、梁希等致电祝贺

1957-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联发射出人造地球卫星
    我国人心振奋
    宋庆龄、钱俊瑞、郭沫若、沈雁冰、梁希等致电祝贺
    宋庆龄钱俊瑞的贺电
    新华社6日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄、秘书长钱俊瑞6日致电苏联对外文化协会代主席卡利希扬和苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫，祝贺苏联发射出世界上第一颗人造地球卫星，电文如下：
    苏联成功地发射出世界上第一颗人造地球卫星，给全中国人民带来了巨大的振奋和鼓舞。我们伟大的盟邦苏联，为了保卫世界和平和造福人类，已先后发现并掌握了原子武器和氢武器的生产方法，首先掌握了用原子能生产电能的方法，发射了世界第一个洲际弹道导弹，现在又成功地发射了第一颗人造卫星。这就无可辩驳地再一次证明了世界和平堡垒苏联的强大，证明了苏联科学技术已居世界最先进的位置。苏联科学的飞跃前进，有力地显示出社会主义制度的无比优越性。
    人造卫星的发射成功是苏联对世界和平事业的又一个伟大的贡献。中国人民对于苏联在人造地球卫星的科学研究工作中所取得的光辉成就，对于苏联在建设共产主义、谋求人类幸福的斗争中的科学技术水平的突飞猛进，表示热烈的祝贺！
    郭沫若的贺电
    新华社6日讯　中国科学院院长6日致电苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫院士，祝贺苏联发射出世界上第一颗人造卫星，电文如下：
    第一颗人造地球卫星的发射成功，是继洲际导弹制造成功之后的苏联科学技术的又一伟大辉煌的成就。这是对伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年的很好的献礼。全世界爱好和平的人们都在欢欣鼓舞，因为这些科学技术上的划时期的成就是保卫世界和平促进人类幸福的极有力的保障。我们中国人民和中国科学家都一致表示着无比的兴奋和感谢。我们深切地认识到社会主义制度的优越性和在苏联共产党领导下的苏联科学技术的先进性。我谨代表中国科学院和中国科学技术界，向您所领导的苏联科学院并通过您向苏联科学技术界表示崇高的敬意和衷心的祝贺。我们一定要作最大的努力，坚决接受中国共产党的领导，捍卫社会主义制度，促进中国科学事业的发展，向你们看齐。
    沈雁冰的贺电
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国文化部部长沈雁冰6日致电苏联科学院巴尔金院士，祝贺苏联发射出世界上第一颗人造地球卫星。电文如下：
    当知道苏联成功地发射出世界上第一颗人造卫星的时候，全中国文化工作者都感到非常兴奋。这是苏联科学界又一次杰出的伟大的成就，是苏联对世界科学文化的一项极其宝贵的贡献。我们谨向您们热烈地祝贺，并祝苏联科学家在探索宇宙的秘密上取得更辉煌的胜利！
    梁希的贺电
    新华社6日讯　中华全国科学技术普及协会主席梁希6日致电苏联全苏政治和科学知识普及协会主席，祝贺苏联发射出世界上第一颗人造地球卫星，电文说：
    欣闻苏联成功地发射了世界第一颗人造卫星，这是人类有史以来的一次奇迹。全世界人民为苏联这一伟大的科学新成就而欢腾。因为这标志着科学新纪元的开始和星际旅行的日子已经接近。这说明了苏联的科学技术在全世界已经领先，充分证明了社会主义制度的优越性；说明了工人阶级的科学技术工作者能以最迅速的步伐攀登世界科学的最高峰。
    我谨代表中华全国科学技术普及协会二十七万会员，以无比振奋的心情，向全苏协会全体成员和苏联全体科学技术工作者以及苏联人民致以热烈的祝贺。
