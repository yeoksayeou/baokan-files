Title: Volunteer Army Celebrates National Day in the Rain
Author:
Date: 1957-10-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 1st. The entire Chinese People's Volunteer Army (CPVA) officers and soldiers joyously celebrated the eighth anniversary of National Day today. It was drizzling in various parts of Korea today. Some CPVA officers and soldiers gathered in clubs, while others, holding umbrellas and wearing raincoats, gathered in squares equipped with loudspeakers, excitedly listening to the grand celebrations, the military parade, and the civilian parade in Beijing. As soon as the weather clears up, the various literary and sports competitions and military skill demonstrations that the CPVA units had planned to hold on National Day will continue.

On the eve of National Day, the CPVA leadership organs held a report meeting and a celebration evening party. Lieutenant General Ding Laifu, Deputy Director of the CPVA Political Department, gave a report to all officers on the current political situation of the motherland and the achievements of the Five-Year Plan.

The CPVA units also held separate celebration rallies and report meetings. Representatives of the local government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and representatives of the Korean People's Army participated in the celebration rallies.

A performance team and a film projection team from a certain unit of the Korean People's Army are conducting a tour of performances in a certain unit of the CPVA during the National Day holiday.

### 志愿军雨中庆祝国庆

1957-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    志愿军雨中庆祝国庆
    新华社平壤1日电  中国人民志愿军全体官兵今天欢度第八届国庆节。今天朝鲜各地细雨纷纷。志愿军各部队官兵有的聚集在俱乐部里，有的打着雨伞、穿着雨衣聚集安置扩音啦叭的广场上，兴奋地听了北京的庆祝大会、阅兵和游行的盛况。一俟天气放晴，志愿军各部队预定在国庆节日举行的各项文艺体育比赛和军事项目的表演即将继续举行。
    国庆节前夕，志愿军领导机关举行了报告会和庆祝晚会，志愿军政治部副主任丁莱夫少将向全体军官就目前祖国的政治形势和五年计划的成就作了报告。
    志愿军各部队也分别举行了庆祝大会和报告会，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的地方政府代表和朝鲜人民军代表参加了庆祝大会。
    朝鲜人民军某部战士演出队和电影队，国庆节期间在志愿军某部作巡回演出。
