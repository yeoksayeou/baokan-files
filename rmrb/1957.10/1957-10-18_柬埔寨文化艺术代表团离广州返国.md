Title: Cambodian Cultural Arts Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1957-10-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 17th: Princess Norodom Phativorleakh Neak neary, Prince Norodom Chakrapong, and the entire Cambodian Royal Cultural Arts Delegation, departed Guangzhou this morning by special train, returning home via Hong Kong. Cao Guisheng, Deputy Head of the Chinese Economic Delegation to Cambodia, and Wu Qing, representative of the Ministry of Culture and council member of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, accompanied the distinguished guests to Shenzhen. Sun Leyi, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, along with responsible persons from the Guangdong and Guangzhou Cultural Bureaus, and over two hundred young artists, saw them off at the station.

### 柬埔寨文化艺术代表团离广州返国

1957-10-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　柬埔寨文化艺术代表团离广州返国
    据新华社广州17日电　帕花·黛维公主、夏卡朋王子和柬埔寨王国文化艺术代表团全体人员，今晨乘专车离广州，取道香港回国。我国驻柬埔寨经济代表团副团长曹桂生和文化部代表、中国人民对外文化协会理事吴青随车陪同贵宾到深圳。广州市孙乐宜副市长和广东省、广州市文化局负责人和青年文艺工作者共二百余人，到车站上送行。
