Title: Dr. Hatta Visits Shanghai Shenxin No. 9 Cotton Textile Factory
Author:
Date: 1957-10-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 9th. Former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta and his entourage visited Shanghai Shenxin No. 9 Cotton Textile Factory, the largest joint state-private cotton textile factory in China, today. Burhan, Qu Wu, and Shanghai Vice Mayor and General Manager of the Joint State-Private Shenxin Cotton Textile Printing and Dyeing Factory General Management Office, Rong Yiren, accompanied the visit.

At the symposium held at the factory, Rong Yiren, at Dr. Hatta's request, introduced the Rong family's nearly sixty years of capital accumulation, their experiences of being oppressed and squeezed out by imperialism and bureaucratic capitalists before liberation, and the situation of accepting socialist transformation after liberation; and answered some questions raised by Dr. Hatta.

Dr. Hatta and others visited various workshops of the factory and learned about the changes in various aspects of the factory since the joint state-private partnership.

### 哈达博士参观上海申新第九棉纺织厂

1957-10-10
第3版()
专栏：

    哈达博士参观上海申新第九棉纺织厂
    新华社上海9日电　印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士和随行人员今天在上海参观了我国最大的公私合营棉纺织厂——申新第九棉纺织厂。包尔汉，屈武和上海市副市长、公私合营申新棉纺织印染厂总管理处总经理荣毅仁等陪同参观。
    在工厂举行的座谈会上，荣毅仁应哈达博士的要求，介绍了荣氏家族近六十年来资本积累的经过，和解放前被帝国主义、官僚资产阶级压迫排挤的遭遇，以及解放后接受社会主义改造的情况；并且回答了哈达博士提出的一些问题。
    哈达博士等参观了工厂的各个车间，了解了这个厂公私合营以后各个方面的变化。
