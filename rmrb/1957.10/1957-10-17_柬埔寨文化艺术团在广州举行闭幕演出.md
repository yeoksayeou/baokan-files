Title: Cambodian Cultural Arts Troupe Holds Closing Performance in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-10-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 16th. The Cambodian Cultural Arts Delegation held its closing performance in Guangzhou on the evening of the 16th.

　　Before the performance began, Guangzhou Mayor Zhu Guang spoke, repeatedly emphasizing the historical friendship between China and Cambodia, a friendship that has been further developed today. The head of the delegation, Fang Bingzhen, also repeatedly cheered for Sino-Cambodian friendship and Chairman Mao's health in his speech.

　　On the evening of the 16th, more than 5,000 spectators in Guangzhou watched a performance with Princess Norodom Buppha Devi and Prince Norodom Sihanouk for the first time. Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, and Fang Bingzhen, head of the Cambodian Cultural Arts Delegation, and Olov, the Soviet Consul in Guangzhou, sat side by side to enjoy the delegation's performance.

　　Princess Norodom Buppha Devi and Prince Norodom Sihanouk, along with other actors, performed the "Blessing Dance" and "Farewell Dance," specially rehearsed for their visit to China. The audience welcomed their performance with thunderous applause, and the Cambodian artists repeatedly clasped their hands in thanks.

　　After the performance, Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, and the head of the Guangzhou Municipal Bureau of Culture all went to the stage to congratulate the Princess, the Prince, and other artists.

### 柬埔寨文化艺术团在广州举行闭幕演出

1957-10-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　柬埔寨文化艺术团在广州举行闭幕演出
    新华社广州16日电　柬埔寨文化艺术代表团16日晚在广州举行闭幕演出。
    演出开始前，广州市市长朱光讲话，一再强调地提到了中柬两国历史上的友谊，这种友谊今天更得到了进一步的发展。代表团团长方秉祯也在讲话中一再为中柬友谊、为毛主席健康欢呼。
    16日晚，广州五千多观众第一次观看了有帕花·黛维公主和夏卡朋王子参加的演出。广东省省长陈郁和柬埔寨文化艺术代表团团长方秉祯、苏联驻广州领事鄂洛夫并肩坐在一起，欣赏代表团的演出。
    帕花·黛维公主和夏卡朋王子和别的演员们分别演出了特别为访问中国排练的“祝福舞”和“告别舞”。观众以春雷般的掌声欢迎他们的演出，柬埔寨艺术家们一再合十谢幕。
    演出结束后，广东省省长陈郁、广州市市长朱光和广州市文化局负责人，都到舞台上向公主、王子和其他艺术家们祝贺。
