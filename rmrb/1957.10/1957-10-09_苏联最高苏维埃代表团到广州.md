Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 8th: The Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation, headed by Aristov, arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon from Hangzhou by special plane. They were welcomed by Wei Jinfei, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, and Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, Commander of the Guangzhou Garrison.

Soviet Consul in Guangzhou, Olov, also led the consulate staff and Soviet experts in Guangzhou to the airport to welcome the delegation.

When the delegation's special plane landed at the airport, which was decorated with fluttering red flags, the crowd waiting at the airport warmly applauded to welcome the distinguished guests. As the distinguished guests walked out of the airport, the crowd lining the streets waved bouquets, and young girls continuously presented flowers. Aristov and others also pinned commemorative badges on the chests of the Young Pioneers who came to greet them.

Also present at the airport today to welcome the delegation were the Deputy Mayors of Guangzhou, Secretaries of the Guangdong Provincial Committee and Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, heads of local organizations of various democratic parties, and leaders of various people's organizations. Heads of consulates of various countries in Guangzhou also came to the airport to welcome them.

In the evening, the distinguished guests visited the water-dwelling residents of Guangzhou and toured the museum.

Further report: When Chairman Aristov and the delegation he led visited the Hangzhou Tea Factory on the 8th, they were warmly congratulated by the tea factory workers.

The Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation arrived in Hangzhou from Shanghai on the afternoon of the 6th.

The delegation also toured the scenic spots of West Lake yesterday. Some members of the delegation also visited the Pingfeng Mountain Workers' Sanatorium and visited the famous Peking Opera artist Gai Jiaotian.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团到广州

1957-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团到广州
    新华社广州8日电　以阿里斯托夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团，今天下午自杭州乘专机到达广州，受到广东省副省长魏今非、广州市市长朱光和广州驻军首长文年生中将等的欢迎。
    苏联驻广州领事鄂洛夫也率领领事馆工作人员和在广州的苏联专家到机场欢迎。
    当代表团乘坐的专机降落在红旗招展的机场上时，等候在机场上的群众热烈地鼓掌向贵宾们表示欢迎。当贵宾走出机场时，夹道的人群挥着花束，年轻的姑娘不断献上鲜花。阿里斯托夫等还把纪念章别上前来迎接的少先队员们的胸前。
    今天到机场欢迎的还有广州市副市长，中共广东省委、广州市委书记，各民主党派地方组织及各人民团体的负责人。各国驻广州领事馆负责人也到机场欢迎。
    傍晚，贵宾们访问了广州水上居民，并且参观了博物馆。
    又讯　阿里斯托夫团长和他率领的代表团8日在参观杭州茶厂的时候，受到茶厂工人的热烈祝贺。
    苏联最高苏维埃代表团是在6日下午从上海来杭州的。
    代表团昨天还游览了西湖的风景区。部分团员还参观了屏风山工人疗养院，访问了著名的京剧艺术家盖叫天。
