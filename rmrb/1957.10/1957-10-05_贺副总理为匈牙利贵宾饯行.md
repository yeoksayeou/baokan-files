Title: Vice Premier He Hosts Farewell Banquet for Hungarian Guests

Author: 
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Vice Premier He Long hosted a banquet tonight for the Hungarian government delegation headed by Premier Kádár, to bid farewell to the distinguished guests who are about to leave Beijing.

Those present as hosts tonight included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, and Chinese Ambassador to Hungary Hao Deqing, among others.

This morning, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Vice Premier Deng Zihui, visited the state-owned Beijing Southern Suburb Farm. In the afternoon, the distinguished guests also visited and toured the Wangfujing Department Store, Dong'an Market, and the Temple of Heaven Park.

### 贺副总理为匈牙利贵宾饯行

1957-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    贺副总理为匈牙利贵宾饯行
    新华社4日讯　贺龙副总理今晚设宴招待以卡达尔总理为首的匈牙利政府代表团，为即将离开北京的贵宾们饯行。
    今晚出席作陪的，有外交部张闻天副部长，对外贸易部叶季壮部长和中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青等人。
    今天上午，贵宾们由邓子恢副总理陪同参观了国营北京市南郊农场。下午，贵宾们还参观和游览了王府井百货公司、东安市场和天坛公园。
