Title: Deng Zihui Receives Pakistan Economic, Agricultural, and Water Conservancy Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Zihui received the Pakistan Economic, Agricultural, and Water Conservancy Delegation headed by Sayyid Hassan on the morning of the 7th.

Present at the meeting were Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Xue Muqiao, and Vice Minister of Water Conservancy Qian Zhengying.

### 邓子恢接见巴经济农业水利考察团

1957-10-10
第3版()
专栏：

    邓子恢接见巴经济农业水利考察团
    新华社9日讯　国务院副总理邓子恢在7日上午接见了以赛义德·哈森为首的巴基斯坦经济农业水利考察团。
    接见时在座的，有农业部部长廖鲁言、国家计划委员会副主任薛暮桥、水利部副部长钱正英。
