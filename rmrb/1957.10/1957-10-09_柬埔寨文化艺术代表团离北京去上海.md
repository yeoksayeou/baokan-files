Title: Cambodian Cultural and Artistic Delegation Leaves Beijing for Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th—The entire Cambodian Cultural and Artistic Delegation left Beijing by special train at noon today for a visit and performance in Shanghai.

Among those who saw the art troupe off at the station today were Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China Zhao Puchu, as well as literary and art workers from Beijing and the Deputy Head of the Vietnam Democratic Republic Art Troupe, Du Run, who is currently visiting Beijing, totaling more than 200 people.

When Princess Norodom Pâvann Norodom, Prince Norodom Chakrapong, and Head of the Delegation Fang Bingzhen and all the members of the delegation entered the platform, those who saw them off presented them with flowers and shook hands to say goodbye. The daughter and son of Vice Premier of the State Council Marshal He Long presented flowers to Princess Norodom Pâvann Norodom and Prince Norodom Chakrapong respectively.

Yang An, Head of the Economic Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia stationed in Beijing, and Cao Guisheng, Deputy Head of the Economic Delegation of China stationed in Phnom Penh, accompanied the art troupe to Shanghai.

### 柬埔寨文化艺术代表团离北京去上海

1957-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨文化艺术代表团离北京去上海
    据新华社8日讯　柬埔寨文化艺术代表团全体人员今天中午坐专车离开北京前往上海访问演出。
    今天到车站欢送艺术团的有文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初以及北京的文艺工作者和正在北京访问的越南民主共和国文工团副团长杜润等共二百多人。
    当帕花·黛维公主、夏卡朋王子，以及方秉祯团长和全体团员步入站台时，欢送的人们向他们献花，并握手告别。国务院副总理贺龙元帅的女儿和儿子分别向帕花·黛维公主和夏卡朋王子献了花。
    柬埔寨王国驻北京经济代表团团长杨安，中国驻金边经济代表团副团长曹桂生陪同艺术团前往上海。
