Title: Liberation Brand Automobile Exports
Author:
Date: 1957-10-18
Page: 4

    Liberation Brand Automobile Exports
    A Jordanian businessman purchased three Liberation brand automobiles from China at the 1957 Second Chinese Export Commodities Fair on the 17th. This is the first time that Liberation brand automobiles manufactured in China have been exported.
    The Jordanian businessman, Mr. Bitar, Chairman of Overseas Trading Company, carefully examined all parts of the car and test drove it on the spot. He then said with satisfaction: The performance and appearance of this car are very good.

### 解放牌汽车出口

1957-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    解放牌汽车出口
    约旦商人17日在1957年第二届中国出口商品交易会上向我国购买了三辆解放牌汽车。这是我国制造的解放牌汽车第一次出口。
    约旦商人比塔——海外贸易公司董事长，仔细研究了汽车的各个部分，并当场试了车，然后满意地说：这种汽车的性能和外观都很好。
