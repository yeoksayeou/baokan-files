Title: Syrian and Egyptian Newspapers Welcome TASS Statement
Author:
Date: 1957-10-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 20th – Syrian newspapers today all published the TASS statement on the Middle East situation on the front page with large headlines. Some newspapers also published editorials and commentaries welcoming Soviet support for Syria.
    “Al-Nour” said that the TASS statement has a great influence internationally. The Syrian people welcome this statement with gratitude and thanks to their great friend, because this great friend defends the Middle East and world peace. The newspaper called on the people to be vigilant and take defensive measures.
    Xinhua News Agency, 20th – Egyptian newspapers on the 19th expressed great indignation at the US and Turkish plot to invade Syria as revealed in the TASS statement, and welcomed the Soviet policy of safeguarding peace and security in the Middle East.

### 叙埃两国报纸欢迎塔斯社声明

1957-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    叙埃两国报纸欢迎塔斯社声明
    据新华社大马士革20日电  叙利亚各大报纸今天都在第一版用大字标题刊载了塔斯社就中东局势发表的声明。有些报纸并且发表社论和评论，欢迎苏联对叙利亚的支持。
    “光明报”说，塔斯社的声明在国际上有极大的影响。叙利亚人民抱着对于他们伟大的朋友的感激和感谢来欢迎这个声明，因为这个伟大的朋友保卫了中东和世界和平。这家报纸号召人民警惕并且采取防卫措施。
    据新华社20日讯  埃及报纸19日对塔斯社声明所揭露的美国和土耳其准备侵略叙利亚的阴谋表示极大的愤慨，对苏联所采取的保卫中东和平和安全的政策表示欢迎。
