Title: October Revolution Supports and Encourages Us - Chairman Stambolic Bids Farewell to Our People
Author:
Date: 1957-10-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 5th: When talking about the October Socialist Revolution in Guangzhou last night, the Chairman of the Federal People's Assembly of Yugoslavia, Petar Stambolic, said that the October Revolution "gave support and encouragement to other countries." He said that the revolutions in China, Yugoslavia, and other countries "are a continuation of the October Revolution."

Chairman Stambolic made these remarks yesterday at a banquet held by Wei Jinfei, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province.

At the banquet, Stambolic said: Everyone who has visited this country with 600 million people always leaves a part of themselves behind and takes something from other countries back to their own. If our delegation leaves something behind, it is the friendship of the Yugoslav people for the Chinese people – a friendship forged in revolutionary struggle and socialist construction. What we are taking back is the image of the Chinese people's heroic struggle to build socialism, the image of the Chinese people's heroic struggle for victory in seizing power in 1949, and we will also take back the impression of the Chinese people solving a series of problems in socialist construction under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party. He said: Yugoslavia firmly believes that the Chinese people will successfully solve all the problems they face in socialist construction. He wished the Chinese people greater achievements in socialist construction.

Chairman Petar Stambolic and the delegation of the Federal People's Assembly of Yugoslavia he led have already left Guangzhou by train today for Hong Kong, to continue their visits to India, Egypt, and other countries.

### 十月革命支持和鼓舞着我们  斯坦鲍利奇团长向我国人民告别

1957-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    十月革命支持和鼓舞着我们
    斯坦鲍利奇团长向我国人民告别
    据新华社广州5日电　南斯拉夫联邦国民议会主席彼得·斯坦鲍利奇昨晚在广州谈到十月社会主义革命的时候说，十月革命“给其他国家以支持和鼓舞力量”。他说，中国、南斯拉夫联邦和其他国家的革命“是十月革命的继续”。
    斯坦鲍利奇主席昨天是在广东省副省长魏今非举行的宴会上这样说的。
    斯坦鲍利奇在宴会上说：每一个访问过这个有六亿人口国家的人，总是要把自己的一部分东西留下来，把别国的东西带回自己的国家去。假如说我们代表团留下东西，这就是南斯拉夫人民对中国人民的友谊——这个友谊是在革命斗争中和社会主义建设中铸成的。我们带回去的是中国人民为建设社会主义进行英勇斗争的形象，是中国人民在1949年为取得政权而胜利进行英勇斗争的形象，我们还要带回中国人民在中国共产党领导下，进行社会主义建设中解决一系列问题的印象。他说：南斯拉夫坚决相信，中国人民在社会主义建设中能成功地解决一切面临的问题。他祝中国人民在社会主义建设中取得更大成就。
    彼得·斯坦鲍利奇主席和他率领的南斯拉夫联邦国民议会代表团已经在今天乘火车离开广州去香港，继续到印度、埃及等国访问。
