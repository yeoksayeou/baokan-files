Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Japanese Delegation from the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-10-03
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet tonight to welcome the delegation from the Japanese National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, led by Tadayoshi Obata, and Akira Kazami, Director-General of the Japanese National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, who was previously visiting Beijing.

    Among those present were Zhou Gengsheng and Qiao Guanhua, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee; Cai Tingkai, Committee Member; Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China; Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; and Cao Mengjun, Member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation.

### 张奚若宴请日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华团

1957-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    张奚若宴请日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华团
    新华社2日讯  中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚举行宴会，欢迎以小畑忠良为首的日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华使节团和原在北京访问的日本恢复日中邦交国民会议理事长风见章等。
    出席作陪的，有外交学会副会长周鲠生、乔冠华，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，委员蔡廷锴，中国红十字会会长李德全，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民和全国妇联主席团委员曹孟君等。
