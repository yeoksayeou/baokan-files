Title: Pakistan Economic, Agricultural, and Water Conservancy Delegation Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 19th: The Pakistan Economic, Agricultural, and Water Conservancy Delegation left Guangzhou this morning to return to their country. The delegation visited various parts of China for twenty-three days.

### 巴基斯坦经济农业水利考察团返国

1957-10-22
第3版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦经济农业水利考察团返国
    据新华社广州19日电　巴基斯坦经济农业水利考察团今晨离广州回国。考察团在我国各地访问了二十三天。
