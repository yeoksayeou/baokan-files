Title: Luoyang Poetry
Author: Bā Mù
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 8

    Luoyang Poetry
    By Ba Mu
    
    Longmen Mountain Scenery
    Like green and beautiful islands,
    Split open by the torrent!
    Those sky-high stone peaks, ah,
    On the water,
    Building two pavilions.
    The clear Yi River
    Like a mirror;
    Take a good look,
    Longmen, now—
    Your beauty is still there.

    Luoyang Sea of Lights
    On the plain
    A sea of lights appears,
    The midsummer night wind,
    Like a clear stream
    Flowing into the heart;
    Quiet night, ah,
    Sparkling lights,
    Like dawn,
    Gently walking
    From the seaside.
    Admiring that
    Glistening sea of lights,
    Many emotions arise
    In my heart:
    Ah, Motherland!
    These countless lights,
    Like ten million
    Smiling eyes,
    Expressing to you:
    Intense love!

    Stone Buddha
    Taking the clouds as a kasaya,
    Stringing the stars
    Into a rosary;
    At the steep cliff edge,
    You have sat quietly for thousands of years,
    Watching the cycle of the sun and moon,
    Listening carefully to the river chanting scriptures.

    Impromptu Thoughts at "Yinyu Crossing"
    At the Longmen Ferry south of Luoyang City, there is an "Eight Sections Shoal" where the river is rapid and dangerous. The poet Bai Juyi once began construction here to open the "Yinyu Crossing", eliminating the suffering of fishing boat navigation.

    Standing on the shore of Eight Sections Shoal,
    Look, the river reflecting the sky,
    White sails everywhere,
    Sounds of fishermen singing...
    It was the wise poet,
    Starting construction to calm the waves,
    Here,
    Opening up ten miles of river shoal;
    Relieving
    The fishermen's sorrow!
    The banks of the Yi River,
    Now,
    Farmhouses everywhere,
    Fields of golden yellow.
    The crying river,
    Is now filled with cheerful laughter,
    Like the fishermen,
    Year after year,
    Singing the
    Poet's
    Kindness.

### 洛阳诗草
巴牧
1957-10-08
第8版()
专栏：

    洛阳诗草
    巴牧
    龙门山色
    像青秀的海岛，
    被洪流冲开！
    那顶天的石峰呵，
    在水上，
    搭起两座楼台。
    明净的伊水
    像一面镜子；
    好好的照照吧，
    龙门，而今——
    你的美容犹在。
    洛阳灯海
    平原上
    出现一片灯海，
    仲夏夜的风，
    像一泓清泉
    流入心怀；
    静静的夜呵，
    闪烁的灯，
    好似黎明，
    从海边
    轻轻走来。
    欣赏着那
    亮晶晶的灯海，
    我心中发出
    许多感慨：
    呵，祖国！
    这无数盏灯，
    像千万只
    微笑的眼睛，
    在向你表示：
    强烈的爱！
    石佛
    把云彩当袈裟，
    把星星
    穿成一串佛珠；
    在陡峭的岩边，
    你，静坐了千年，
    看日月轮回，
    细听河流念经。
    “阴狱渡”偶感
    洛阳城南龙门渡口，有一“八节滩”，河水急湍多险，诗人白居易曾在这里兴工开凿“阴狱渡”，免除了渔船航行的苦难。
    站在八节滩的岸上，
    看，映天的河流，
    白帆片片，
    声声渔唱……
    是智慧的诗人，
    兴工平浪，
    在这儿，
    开凿十里河滩；
    免除了
    渔家的悲伤！
    伊河两岸，
    如今，
    农舍处处，
    遍地金黄。
    哭泣的河流，
    已是笑声朗朗，
    像渔家，
    年年月月，在把
    诗人的
    恩情歌唱。
