Title: Celebrating the Eighth National Day (Qinyuan Spring)
Author: Li Jishen
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 2

    Celebrating the Eighth National Day (Qinyuan Spring)
    Li Jishen
    Burning with enthusiasm,
    The festival arrives with joy,
    The world rejoices.
    Happy eighth year of the founding of the nation,
    Stable as a rock;
    The people love the Party,
    Revering it like the sun and stars.
    Silver wings soar to the sky,
    Divine eagles flash like lightning,
    Jet aircraft are made domestically.
    Above the great river,
    Turning the chasm into a natural barrier,
    The Wuhan Bridge lies across.
    The worker-peasant alliance is consolidated,
    Sweeping away the rightist miasma.
    Look at the advancing ranks,
    Boundless and mighty;
    A brand new society,
    How much prosperity.
    People from all corners of the world share the same spirit,
    Distinguished guests gather like clouds,
    Joyfully ascending the Xi Xu Spring Terrace together.
    How fortunate am I,
    To be present at this prosperous age,
    Singing praises of lasting peace.

### 庆祝八届国庆节（沁园春）
李济深
1957-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝八届国庆节（沁园春）
    李济深
    热火朝天，
    节日欣临，
    寰宇欢腾。
    喜八年建国，
    安如磐石；
    兆民爱党，
    拱若日星。
    银翼冲霄，
    神鹰掣电，
    喷气机群自造成。
    大江上，
    化鸿沟天堑，
    武汉桥横。
    工农巩固联盟，
    把右派阴氛一扫清。
    看进军行列，
    无边浩荡；
    崭新社会，
    几许繁荣。
    薄海风同，
    嘉宾云集，
    熙煦春台乐共登。
    吾何幸，
    得躬逢盛世，
    歌咏承平。
