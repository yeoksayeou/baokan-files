Title: Secretary-General of the Syrian-Chinese Friendship Association Sends Congratulatory Telegram on the Establishment of the Chinese-Syrian Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1957-10-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th. The Chinese-Syrian Friendship Association received a telegram yesterday from Salim Zirkly, Secretary-General of the Syrian-Chinese Friendship Association, congratulating the establishment of the Chinese-Syrian Friendship Association.

The telegram said, "The establishment of your association in China is a great support for the principles and goals upon which we established the Syrian-Chinese Friendship Association. We are very pleased that the two associations will cooperate and unite to strive to strengthen the friendship and cooperation between the peoples of China and Syria, and to support the common struggle of the two peoples for world peace. On behalf of our association, I extend our highest respect and congratulations to you."

### 叙中友协秘书长电贺中叙友协成立

1957-10-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　叙中友协秘书长电贺中叙友协成立
    新华社10日讯　中叙友好协会昨天收到了叙利亚叙中友好协会秘书长萨利姆·齐尔克利的电报，祝贺中叙友好协会成立。
    电报说，“贵协会在中国建立，是对我们建立叙中友好协会所根据的原则和目的的一个巨大支持。我们很高兴，这两个协会互相配合和团结一致来努力加强中叙两国人民间的友谊与合作，和支持两国人民争取世界和平的共同斗争。我代表我们的协会向你们致最大的敬意和祝贺。”
