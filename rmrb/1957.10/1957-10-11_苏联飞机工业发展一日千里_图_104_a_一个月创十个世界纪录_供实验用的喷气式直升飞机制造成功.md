　　Soviet Aircraft Industry Advances by Leaps and Bounds
    “Tu—104A” Sets Ten World Records in One Month
    Experimental Jet Helicopter Successfully Manufactured
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 10th: The Soviet “Tu-104A” aircraft set ten world records in a single month of September.

　　The “Tu-104A” flew from Moscow to New York in thirteen hours at the beginning of September, setting a world record for long-distance non-stop flight speed. Twenty years ago, two Soviet ANT-25 aircraft made the first non-stop flight from Moscow to the United States in aviation history, which took a total of sixty hours at the time.

　　On the way to New York, the “Tu-104A” climbed to 13,300 meters, setting an unprecedented altitude record.

　　On September 11th, the “Tu-104A” took off from Moscow with two tons of cargo and flew 2,000 kilometers at an altitude of 8,000 to 10,000 meters, with an average speed of 897.498 kilometers per hour. This flight set three world flight speed records in one fell swoop: 2,000 kilometers without cargo, with one ton of cargo, and with two tons of cargo.

　　On September 24th, the “Tu-104A” aircraft set five more world flight speed records in one flight. It carried ten tons of cargo and completed 1,002 kilometers in one hour and forty-eight point two seconds, with an average speed of 972.856 kilometers. This speed exceeded the record of 900.66 kilometers per hour set by a Swedish aircraft in 1955 without cargo, and also exceeded the performance of an American aircraft carrying one ton of cargo, flying 1,000 kilometers, at a speed of 660.526 kilometers per hour.

　　The necessary materials regarding this flight will be submitted to the International Aeronautical Federation Association for approval as world records for flight speed without cargo, with one ton of cargo, two tons of cargo, five tons of cargo, and ten tons of cargo.

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 10th: The Soviet Union has manufactured an experimental jet helicopter. Observers here say that the manufacturing of this aircraft solves a series of concrete problems for the future manufacturing of jet helicopters that are three times the speed of sound, can accommodate dozens of passengers, and do not require runways.

    This experimental aircraft, called the "Flying Platform" and shaped like a large spider, was demonstrated at Tushino Airport. It does not require the long reinforced concrete runways necessary for ordinary jet aircraft, can take off vertically and land vertically in a very small area, can rotate freely in the air, and can fall rapidly like a stone and then suddenly hover.

    Soviet technical science doctor Matveyev said that the manufacturing of this "Flying Platform" solves a series of problems for the future manufacturing of vertical take-off and landing jet aircraft. For example, ensuring that vertical take-off and landing aircraft using jet engines do not rush into the sky like rockets, but can rise and fall slowly, and ensuring the stability and controllability of the aircraft under these conditions. Rudders for controlling this type of aircraft have now been found, one is a "gas" rudder set in the exhaust airflow behind the engine, and the other is a so-called jet rudder that works by air compressed by the compressor in the jet engine. These ensure that the aircraft can rise and fall freely and change direction. Observers here expressed confidence that large passenger jet helicopters will be manufactured in the near future. ### 苏联飞机工业发展一日千里  “图—104A”一个月创十个世界纪录  供实验用的喷气式直升飞机制造成功

1957-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联飞机工业发展一日千里
    “图—104A”一个月创十个世界纪录
    供实验用的喷气式直升飞机制造成功
    新华社莫斯科10日电　苏联“图—一○四A”飞机在9月份一个月内创造了十个世界纪录。
    “图—一○四A”在9月初以十三小时的时间从莫斯科飞往纽约，创造了远距离不着陆飞行速度世界纪录。二十年前两架苏联ANT—二十五式飞机在航空史上第一次从莫斯科不着陆飞往美国，当时一共花了六十小时。
    在飞往纽约途中，“图—一○四A”升高到一万三千三百公尺，创造了空前的高度纪录。
    9月11日“图—一○四A”带了两吨货物，从莫斯科起飞，在八千到一万公尺的高空飞行两千公里，速度平均每小时八百九十七点四九八公里。这次飞行一举而创造了两千公里不载货、载一吨货、载两吨货等三个飞行速度世界纪录。
    9月24日“图—一○四A”飞机在一次飞行中又创造了五个世界飞行速度纪录。它载货十吨以一小时四十八点二秒的时间飞完一千零二公里，平均速度达到了九百七十二点八五六公里。这个速度超过了1955年一架瑞典飞机在不载货情况下所创每小时飞行九百零零点六六公里的纪录，同时也超过了美国飞机载货一吨，飞行一千公里，速度每小时达到六百六十点五二六公里的成绩。
    关于这次飞行必要的材料将提交国际航空联合会协会批准，作为不载货飞行速度、载货一吨、二吨、五吨、十吨飞行速度的世界纪录。
    新华社莫斯科10日电　苏联已经制造成了一架供实验用的喷气式直升飞机。此间观察家说，这架飞机的制成为今后制造速度三倍于声速、可容纳数十名乘客、而无须跑道的喷气式直升飞机解决了一系列的具体问题。
    这架被称为“飞行台”的、外形像一个大蜘蛛的实验飞机曾在土希诺机场举行过一次表演。它不须要一般喷气式飞机所必需的很长的钢筋混凝土跑道，可以在一个很小的场地上垂直起飞，垂直降落，可以在空中自由旋转，像一块石头一样迅速下坠而后突然悬空停住。
    苏联技术科学博士马特维耶夫说，这架“飞行台”的制成为今后制造垂直升降的喷气式飞机解决了一系列的问题。例如保证使用喷气式发动机的垂直升降飞机不像火箭那样猛冲入天空，而能缓缓升降，保证飞机在这种条件下的平稳和可控制性的问题。现在已经找出了供控制这种飞机用的舵，一种是设置在发动机后面喷出的气流中的“气”舵，一种是靠喷气发动机中的压缩机压出的空气工作的所谓喷射舵。这些保证了飞机能自由升降和转换方向。此间观察家表示，相信在不久的将来即可制成大型客运喷气式直升飞机。（附图片）
