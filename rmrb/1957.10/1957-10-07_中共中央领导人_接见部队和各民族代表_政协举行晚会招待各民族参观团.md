Title: CCP Central Leaders Receive Troops and Ethnic Minority Representatives; CPPCC Holds Evening Party to Entertain Ethnic Minority Visiting Groups
Author:
Date: 1957-10-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th - CCP Central Leaders Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Chen Yun, and Deng Xiaoping received representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and various fraternal ethnic groups who participated in the National Day celebrations this afternoon.

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th - The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a song and dance evening party today to entertain ethnic minority visiting groups from various regions who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations.

    At the evening party, Li Weihan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, delivered a speech welcoming the ethnic minority visiting groups. Subsequently, the Song and Dance Troupe of the Cultural Troupe of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army performed wonderful songs and dances for the visiting groups.

    Before the evening party, Li Weihan met with the heads of the various visiting groups.

### 中共中央领导人  接见部队和各民族代表  政协举行晚会招待各民族参观团

1957-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人
    接见部队和各民族代表
    政协举行晚会招待各民族参观团
    新华社6日讯　中共中央领导人毛泽东、刘少奇、朱德、陈云、邓小平，在今天下午先后接见了参加国庆节观礼的中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军以及各兄弟民族的代表。
    新华社5日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会今天举行歌舞晚会，招待从各个地区来京参加国庆观礼的各民族参观团。
    晚会上，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李维汉致词欢迎各民族参观团。接着，中国人民解放军总政治部文工团歌舞团为参观团演出精采歌舞。
    晚会前，李维汉接见了各参观团的负责人。
