Title: Beijing Preparing Large-Scale Celebrations; Qian Sanqiang and Others Introduce Soviet Scientific Achievements to Citizens
Author:
Date: 1957-10-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: Beijing residents will launch large-scale celebrations for the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.

Qian Sanqiang, Director of the Institute of Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Fu Yigang, Director of the Basic Construction Bureau of the State Planning Commission, gave reports to the people of Beijing on the 22nd entitled "Artificial Satellites and Advanced Soviet Science and Technology" and "On the Great Assistance of the Soviet Union to Our Country," respectively. Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, will also give a report to the people of Beijing on Sino-Soviet friendship. In order to deeply and widely publicize the great achievements of the Soviet Union's socialist construction and science and technology over the past forty years, the Beijing Association for the Popularization of Science and Technology will hold 500 lectures. On the achievements in jet rockets and artificial satellites alone, 35 lectures will be held.

Around the holiday, Beijing will hold a celebration rally of over ten thousand people and grand get-together parties in several locations. People's organizations, factories, schools, government agencies, rural areas, and others will also hold various celebration meetings and get-together parties.

Literary and artistic activities celebrating the great festival will also be extensive. Around the holiday, Beijing's theaters and song and dance troupes will perform Soviet dramas and songs and dances, and fourteen first-class cinemas will hold a Soviet Film Week.

Around the holiday, the capital will hold a variety of photo exhibitions.

During this period, all units are also preparing to closely integrate with the ongoing socialist ideological education work and conduct propaganda and education on Marxism-Leninism and socialism among cadres and the masses throughout the city.

### 北京正准备大规模庆祝活动  钱三强等向市民介绍苏联科学成就

1957-10-24
第3版()
专栏：

    北京正准备大规模庆祝活动
    钱三强等向市民介绍苏联科学成就
    据新华社22日讯　北京人民将展开大规模的庆祝十月革命四十周年的活动。
    中国科学院物理研究所所长钱三强、国家计划委员会基本建设局局长傅毅刚22日分别向北京人民作了“人造卫星与苏联先进科学技术”、“关于苏联对我国伟大援助”的报告。中苏友好协会秘书长钱俊瑞也将要向北京人民作有关中苏友好的报告。为了深入广泛地宣传苏联四十年来的社会主义建设和科学技术上的伟大成就，北京市科学技术普及协会将进行五百次讲演会，仅以喷气火箭和人造卫星方面的成就，就要举行三十五次讲演会。
    在节日前后，北京将举行万余人的庆祝大会，并在好几个地方举行盛大的联欢晚会。各人民团体和各工厂、学校、机关、农村等也将分别举行各种庆祝会和联欢会。
    庆祝伟大节日的文艺活动也很广泛，在节日前后，北京各话剧院、各歌舞团将演出苏联话剧和歌舞，十四个甲级电影院将举行苏联电影周。
    节日前后，首都将举行多种多样的图片展览会。
    在这期间，各单位还准备密切结合目前正在开展的社会主义思想教育工作，在全市干部和人民群众中进行一次马列主义和社会主义问题的宣传教育。
