Title: Zhou Enlai Sends Telegram to Syrian Foreign Minister to Express Condolences on Yaz's Death
Author:
Date: 1957-10-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar on the 28th, expressing condolences on the death of Tawfiq Yaz, head of the Syrian journalists' delegation to China and Director of the Press Department of the Syrian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The telegram said: "I am deeply saddened by the unfortunate death of Mr. Tawfiq Yaz during his visit to our country. Please accept my condolences and convey them to the family of Mr. Tawfiq Yaz."

### 周外长电叙利亚外长吊唁雅兹病世

1957-10-29
第3版()
专栏：

    周外长电叙利亚外长吊唁雅兹病世
    新华社28日讯　我外交部长周恩来28日致电叙利亚外交部长比塔尔，吊唁叙利亚新闻工作者访华代表团团长、叙利亚外交部新闻司司长托菲克·雅兹逝世。电文说：“托菲克·雅兹先生在我国访问期间不幸病逝，特致哀悼。并请向托菲克·雅兹先生的家属转致吊唁。”
