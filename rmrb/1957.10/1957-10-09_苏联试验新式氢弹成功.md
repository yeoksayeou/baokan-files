Title: Soviet Union Successfully Tests New Type of Hydrogen Bomb
Author:
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 5

　　Soviet Union Successfully Tests New Type of Hydrogen Bomb
　　Xinhua News Agency, 8th. TASS announced on the 7th that, according to the test work plan for manufacturing and improving atomic and hydrogen weapons, the Soviet Union conducted a test of a new type of hydrogen bomb with enormous power on October 6th. The test was very successful.
　　For the safety of residents and personnel participating in the test, the explosion was carried out at high altitude.

### 苏联试验新式氢弹成功

1957-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联试验新式氢弹成功
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社7日发表公报说，根据制造和改进原子武器和氢武器方面的试验工作计划，苏联在10月6日进行了威力巨大的新式氢弹的试验。这次试验很成功。
    为了居民和参加试验的工作人员的安全，爆炸是在高空进行的。
