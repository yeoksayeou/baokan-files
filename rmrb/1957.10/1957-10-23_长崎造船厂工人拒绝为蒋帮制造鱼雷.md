Title: Nagasaki Shipyard Workers Refuse to Manufacture Torpedoes for Chiang Gang

Author: 
Date: 1957-10-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: According to today's "Akahata" report, workers of the Mitsubishi Labor Union Federation Nagasaki Shipyard Branch, participating in the All-Japan Shipbuilding Workers' Union's autumn unified struggle, refused to manufacture twenty torpedoes for the Taiwan Chiang Kai-shek clique during the second 24-hour strike which began on the morning of the 17th.

Due to the strike by Nagasaki Shipyard workers, the completion of the oil tanker "Nissho Leader," scheduled for the 24th, and the repair work on the Japanese Navy warship "Ayanami" have also been severely affected.

### 长崎造船厂工人拒绝为蒋帮制造鱼雷

1957-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    长崎造船厂工人拒绝为蒋帮制造鱼雷
    据新华社22日讯　据“赤旗报”今天报道，参加全日本造船工会秋季统一斗争的三菱工会联合会长崎造船厂分会的工人，曾经在17日上午开始的第二次二十四小时罢工中，拒绝为台湾蒋介石集团制造二十枚鱼雷。
    由于长崎造船厂工人的罢工，预定在24日完成的油船“涅斯·领导者号”的工程和正在修理的日本海军的军舰“绫波号”的工程也受到严重的影响。
