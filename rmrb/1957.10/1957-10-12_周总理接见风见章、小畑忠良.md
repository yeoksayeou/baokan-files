Title: Premier Zhou Receives Kazami Akira and Obata Tadashi
Author:
Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th -- Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon Kazami Akira, chairman of the Japan National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, Obata Tadashi, head of the Japan National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations Visiting Delegation, Deputy Head Yoshida Hosei, ?moto Yae, and all members of the delegation.

Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Vice President Qiao Guanhua, Council members Liao Chengzhi and Zhao Anbo, Secretary-General Wu Maosun, and Deputy Secretary-General Xiao Xiangqian.
