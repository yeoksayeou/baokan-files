Title: Premier Zhou Receives the Burmese Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Burmese Youth Delegation, headed by U Ba Than, this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were Liu Xiyuan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wu Xueqian, Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Youth Federation, and Qian Liren, Deputy Secretary-General.

U Hla Maung, the Burmese Ambassador to China, was also present at the meeting.

### 周总理接见缅甸青年代表团

1957-10-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见缅甸青年代表团
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以吴波阵为首的缅甸青年代表团。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国民主青年联合会副主席刘西元，外交部亚洲司司长章文晋，中华全国民主青年联合会国际联络部部长吴学谦和副秘书长钱李仁。
    接见时，缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂也在座。
