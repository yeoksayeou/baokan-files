Author:
Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 4

　　On the Past Sandy Wasteland
    The Yellow River breached its banks more than a thousand times in the eastern Henan region, turning over four million mu of land in seventeen counties, including Minquan and Lankao, into vast sandy wastelands. After liberation, under the leadership of the Party and the government, the people of eastern Henan actively launched a tree-planting campaign. To date, nearly 500,000 mu of sandy wasteland has been transformed into verdant, fruit-laden gardens. (Attached picture)

### 在过去的沙荒地上

1957-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　在过去的沙荒地上
    黄河在河南的豫东地区决口一千多次，使民权、兰考等十七个县四百多万亩土地变成了大沙荒。解放后，豫东人民在党和政府的领导下，积极地展开了造林运动。截至目前为止，近五十万亩的沙荒变成了绿树成荫、果实累累的田园。（附图片）
