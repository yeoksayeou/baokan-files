Title: Chinese Economic and Technical Delegation Arrives in Britain
Author:
Date: 1957-10-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, 23rd. The Chinese economic and technical delegation arrived in London on the 22nd.

The delegation, led by Ji Chao-ding, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, is composed of various Chinese industrial experts and technicians.

Among those who welcomed the delegation at the airport were Sir Hugh Beaver, Chairman of the Federation of British Industries and the Sino-British Trade Council (which was specially formed last month to arrange the reception of the Chinese delegation), Mr. Flint, Chairman of the Sino-British Trade Committee, and many other important representatives from British industrial, commercial and financial circles.

### 我经济技术代表团抵英

1957-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我经济技术代表团抵英
    据新华社伦敦23日电　中国经济和技术代表团22日到达伦敦。
    由中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席兼秘书长冀朝鼎率领的这个代表团是由中国各种工业专家和技术人员组成的。
    在机场欢迎代表团的有英国工业联合会、中英贸易理事会（它是在上个月特别组成以便安排接待中国代表团的工作的）的主席休·比佛爵士，中英贸易委员会主席弗林特先生，以及英国工商业和金融界其他许多重要代表。
