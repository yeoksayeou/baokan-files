Title: GDR Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The GDR delegation headed by Deputy Prime Minister Oesner left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Vice Premier Bo Yibo and the heads of relevant departments Ye Jizhuang, Zhao Erlu, Ji Pengfei, as well as GDR Ambassador to China Gipner.

### 民主德国代表团离京返国

1957-10-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　民主德国代表团离京返国
    新华社11日讯　以厄斯纳副总理为首的民主德国代表团今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场送行的有薄一波副总理和有关部门的负责人叶季壮、赵尔陆、姬鹏飞以及民主德国驻中国大使纪普纳。
