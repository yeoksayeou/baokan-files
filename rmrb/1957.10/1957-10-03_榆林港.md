Title: Yulin Port
Author: Ke Yuan
Date: 1957-10-03
Page: 4

    Yulin Port
    Ke Yuan

    The Holiday
    Arches are built with flowers and pine branches,
    One after another, leading straight to Beijing,
    Along the roads, along our golden dragon,
    Comes the sound of cheers from ten thousand people before Tiananmen.
    And from the tall coconut groves, the surging waves,
    Another burst of joyful echoes rises.
    Ah! October by the South Sea,
    Everywhere is transparent scenery.
    The sky is sapphire, the mountains are jade screens,
    The colorful flags on ships and docks,
    Like clusters of dancing sparks.
    The air is filled with joyful songs, the faint scent of wine,
    People in new clothes wander the streets,
    Ah! Their eyes are shining,
    Their hearts,
    Also follow the marching procession across the square…
    Only our warships, ah,
    Sound their horns again, sailing far out to sea,
    The sailors stand majestically on the deck,
    They will carry the holiday greetings, the holiday vigilance,
    To visit every corner of the motherland's vast sea…

    Salt Fields
    Look at the shimmering salt fields like mirrors,
    Each salt pile is like a magnolia blossom,
    To weave this white and affectionate garland, ah,
    The salt workers have let the sun darken their faces…

### 榆林港
柯原
1957-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    榆林港
    柯原
    节日
    鲜花和松枝搭成了彩门，
    一座又一座，直通向北京，
    沿着道路，沿着我们金色的龙，
    传来了天安门前万众欢呼的声音。
    而从高大的椰林，汹涌的海浪上，
    又腾起一片欢乐的回声。
    呵！南海边的十月，
    到处是透明的风景。
    天空是蓝宝石，群山是翡翠屏，
    轮船和码头上的彩旗，
    像一团团飞舞的火星。
    空气中有快乐的歌声、酒的淡香，
    穿着新衣裳的人们在街上游荡，
    呵！他们的眼睛发光，
    他们的心，
    也随着游行的行列走过广场……
    只有我们的军舰呵，
    又鸣起汽笛出海远航，
    水兵们威严地站在甲板上，
    他们要带着节日的祝贺，节日的警觉，
    去遍访祖国辽阔的海疆……
    盐田
    看那镜子一般闪光的盐田，
    一座座盐堆像一朵朵玉兰，
    为了编织这洁白多情的花环呵
    盐工们让太阳晒黑了脸……
