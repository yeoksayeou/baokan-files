Title: Teng Daiyuan Hosts Banquet for Chinese and Foreign Guests
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 15th. Minister of Railways Teng Daiyuan, Governor of Hubei Province Zhang Tixue, and Deputy Mayor of Wuhan Wang Kewen, jointly held a banquet at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Palace in Wuhan tonight to entertain Chinese and foreign guests who came to attend the inauguration ceremony of the Yangtze River Bridge.

At the banquet, Teng Daiyuan and others raised their glasses to toast the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. Soviet Minister of Transport Construction Kozhevnikov raised his glass and shouted "Long live the great and wise Communist Party of China!"

Those invited to the banquet today included Vice Premier of the State Council Li Fuchun, alternate members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Lu Dingyi and Kang Sheng, heads of relevant central departments, and representatives from all walks of life in Hubei Province.

### 滕代远设宴招待中外来宾

1957-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　滕代远设宴招待中外来宾
    据新华社武汉15日电　铁道部部长滕代远、湖北省省长张体学、武汉市副市长王克文，今天晚上在武汉中苏友好宫联合举行宴会，招待前来参加长江大桥落成通车典礼的中外来宾。
    在宴会上，滕代远等举杯为中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊干杯。苏联运输建设部长科热夫尼科夫举杯高呼伟大的、英明的中国共产党万岁。
    今天应邀参加宴会的有国务院副总理李富春，中共中央政治局候补委员陆定一、康生，中央有关部门负责人，以及湖北省各界代表等。
