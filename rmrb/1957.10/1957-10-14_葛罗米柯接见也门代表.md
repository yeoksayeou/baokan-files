Title: Gromyko Receives Yemeni Representatives
Author:
Date: 1957-10-14
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, 13th: TASS, New York: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko received Prince Saif al-Islam al-Hassan, head of the Yemeni delegation to the Twelfth Session of the United Nations General Assembly, on the 11th. During the conversation, they discussed issues of mutual concern.

### 葛罗米柯接见也门代表

1957-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　葛罗米柯接见也门代表
    新华社13日讯　塔斯社纽约讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯11日接见了出席联合国大会第十二届会议的也门代表团团长赛义弗·伊斯兰·哈萨尼亲王。在谈话中谈到了双方所关心的一些问题。
