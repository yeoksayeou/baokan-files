Title: Director of the Chilean Museum of Folk Art, Tomás Lago, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Tomás Lago, Director of the Chilean Museum of Folk Art, and his wife arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 6th, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 智利民间艺术博物馆馆长  托马斯·拉哥抵北京

1957-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    智利民间艺术博物馆馆长
    托马斯·拉哥抵北京
    新华社7日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的智利民间艺术博物馆馆长托马斯·拉哥和他的夫人，已经在6日下午到达北京。
