Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: The Soviet cultural delegation headed by Kochetov concluded its friendly visit to China and left Beijing by plane on the 29th to return home. Shao Lizi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, and Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, went to the airport to see them off.

### 苏文化代表团回国

1957-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    苏文化代表团回国
    据新华社29日讯　以柯切托夫为首的苏联文化代表团结束在中国的友好访问后，29日乘飞机离开北京回国。前往机场欢送的有中苏友好协会总会副会长邵力子，秘书长钱俊瑞以及苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫。
