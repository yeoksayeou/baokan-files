Title: Nan Ri Hyun to Syrian Press Delegation: Korean People Support Syria
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 6

　　Nan Ri Hyun to Syrian Press Delegation:
    Korean People Support Syria
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th - Vice Premier Nan Ri of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea received the Syrian journalists' delegation visiting Korea yesterday.
    In his conversation with the delegation, Nan Ri condemned the imperialists' aggressive attempts against Syria. He said that the Korean people have always actively supported the just struggle of the Syrian people for the freedom and independence of their motherland.
    The head of the Syrian journalists' delegation, Tofik Yaz, expressed his gratitude to the Korean government leaders and the Korean people for their support of the Syrian people's just struggle. He said that American imperialism is the common enemy of the Korean and Syrian peoples, and the Korean people's victory over the American imperialists is also a victory for the Syrian people. He said that the Syrian people warmly hope for the normalization of diplomatic relations between Korea and Syria and will strive to establish friendly relations between the two peoples.

### 南日向叙新闻代表团表示  朝鲜人民支持叙利亚

1957-10-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　南日向叙新闻代表团表示
    朝鲜人民支持叙利亚
    据新华社平壤11日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相南日昨天接见了来朝鲜访问的叙利亚新闻工作者代表团。
    南日在同代表团谈话中，谴责了帝国主义者对叙利亚的侵略企图。他说，朝鲜人民一直积极支持叙利亚人民为祖国的自由独立而进行的正义斗争。
    叙利亚新闻工作者代表团团长托菲克·雅兹对朝鲜政府领导人和朝鲜人民对叙利亚人民的正义斗争的支持表示感谢。他说，美帝国主义是朝鲜人民和叙利亚人民的共同敌人，朝鲜人民战胜了美帝国主义者也是叙利亚人民的胜利。他说，叙利亚人民热烈希望朝叙两国邦交的正常化，并将为建立两国人民的友好关系而努力。
