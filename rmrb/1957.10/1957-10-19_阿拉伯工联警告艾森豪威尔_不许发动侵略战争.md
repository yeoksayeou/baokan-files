Title: Arab Labor Union Warns Eisenhower Against Launching a War of Aggression
Author:
Date: 1957-10-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 18th: The Secretary-General of the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions, Kamel, sent a telegram to U.S. President Eisenhower on the 17th, demanding in the name of six million Arab workers that he mediate to avoid war in the Middle East. He also sent a telegram to United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, requesting him to find ways to maintain peace in Syria.

The telegram to Eisenhower stated: "We believe that the so-called Syrian crisis would not have occurred without the military provocations on the northern border of Syria and the U.S. fleet's exercises in Syrian waters."

The telegram reminded the U.S. President of the failed invasion of Egypt by Britain, France, and Israel, saying: "Syrians and Arabs are peace-loving, but beware of our anger. We are still ready to fight back, but this time with greater strength and all means. Do not forget that we control all important supplies and transportation lines. We will use all our strength to defend Syria."

The telegram to the United Nations Secretary-General stated: "We call on you to find ways to defend the peace of Syria. We request the United Nations General Assembly to take effective action to avoid a devastating nuclear war."

### 阿拉伯工联警告艾森豪威尔  不许发动侵略战争

1957-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯工联警告艾森豪威尔
    不许发动侵略战争
    新华社开罗18日电  阿拉伯工会国际联合会秘书长卡迈勒17日致电美国总统艾森豪威尔，以六百万阿拉伯工人的名义要求他进行斡旋以避免中东发生战争。他还致电联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，要求他设法维护叙利亚的和平。
    给艾森豪威尔的电文说：“我们相信，如果没有在叙利亚北部边界上的军事挑衅行动和美国舰队在叙利亚海面的演习，决不会发生所谓叙利亚危机。”
    电文提醒美国总统关于英、法、以色列三国侵略埃及失败一事后说：“叙利亚人和阿拉伯人是爱好和平的，但是要当心我们的愤怒。我们仍旧准备回击，但是这一次将用更大的力量和一切手段。不要忘记我们控制着一切重要的供应品和交通线。我们将用全部力量来保卫叙利亚。”
    致联合国秘书长的电文说：“我们呼吁你设法保卫叙利亚的和平。我们要求联合国大会采取有效行动来避免发生毁灭性的核武器战争。”
