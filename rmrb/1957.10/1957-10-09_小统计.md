    Small Statistics
    In recent years, with the vigorous development of China's sports and the improvement of the technical level of outstanding athletes, new track and field results have emerged continuously, and the number of times national track and field records have been broken has increased. See the table below:
    1953 26 times
    1954 72 times
    1955 67 times
    1956 178 times
    Note: No national track and field games were held before 1953

### 小统计

1957-10-09
第8版()
专栏：

    小统计
    最近几年来，随着我国体育事业的蓬勃发展和我国优秀运动员技术水平的提高，新的田径成绩不断出现，打破田径全国纪录的次数越来越多。见下表：
    1953年　26次
    1954年　72次
    1955年　67次
    1956年　178次
    注：1953年以前没有举行过全国性的田径运动会
