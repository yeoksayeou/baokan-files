Newspaper Article Summary

    A Major Sign of Internationalism
    What is proletarian internationalism? It is the unity of the proletariat of the world to oppose the common enemy—international capitalism. Therefore, the communist movement was international from the very beginning. In the age of imperialism, especially after the Great October Revolution, proletarian internationalism is manifested in the fact that the proletariat of capitalist countries should support the liberation struggles of the people of colonial and semi-colonial countries, the proletariat of colonial and semi-colonial countries should support the liberation struggles of the proletariat of capitalist countries, and the proletariat of all countries should stand on the same front with the socialist country, the Soviet Union.
    Proletarian internationalism and patriotism are inseparable, because the interests of the proletariat of all countries are completely consistent with the interests of their own nations.
    It is very clear that when China has become a large socialist country appearing on the international stage, the basis for the unity of the Chinese people on international issues can only be proletarian internationalism and patriotism, and cannot and should not be any other -isms. The spirit of proletarian internationalism and patriotism is a universal truth, but it also has different specific contents in different countries and different historical periods. What is the specific content of proletarian internationalism and patriotism that we are talking about today? It is leaning to one side, participating in the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, advocating complete equality of all nations in the world, resolutely opposing imperialism and colonialism, and utilizing all positive factors abroad to strive for an international peaceful environment for our country's socialist construction. And the primary issue among these is to consolidate the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.
    The bourgeois rightists oppose leaning to one side and oppose our country's participation in the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and want to advocate "neutralism" in our country. What does this mean? The counter-revolutionaries in Hungary have taught us a lesson on this point. It is very clear that this is to change the socialist nature of our country, make our country once again a colony of imperialism, and make our country a battlefield for imperialism to provoke new wars, causing the people of China and the people of the world to suffer the catastrophe of war. Therefore, we must severely criticize the rightists' fallacies. (Excerpt from Wang Li's article in the China Youth Daily on October 8: "A Major Sign of Internationalism")

### 国际主义的一个主要标志

1957-10-25
第7版()
专栏：报刊论文摘要

    国际主义的一个主要标志
    什么叫无产阶级的国际主义呢？就是全世界无产阶级联合起来，反对共同的敌人——国际资本主义，所以，共产主义运动一开始就是国际性的。到了帝国主义时代，特别是伟大的十月革命以后，无产阶级的国际主义就表现在：资本主义国家的无产阶级要拥护殖民地、半殖民地人民的解放斗争，殖民地、半殖民地的无产阶级要拥护资本主义国家的无产阶级的解放斗争，而一切国家的无产阶级都应该和社会主义国家苏联站到一条战线上。
    无产阶级国际主义和爱国主义是不可分的，因为各国无产阶级的利益和本民族的利益是完全一致的。
    非常清楚，当中国已经成为一个社会主义大国出现在国际舞台上的时候，在国际问题上，中国人民团结的基础，就只能是无产阶级的国际主义和爱国主义，而不可能也不应该是别的什么主义。无产阶级的国际主义和爱国主义精神是普遍真理，但在不同国家和不同历史时期还有不同的具体内容，什么是我们今天所讲的无产阶级国际主义和爱国主义的具体内容呢？就是一边倒，参加以苏联为首的社会主义阵营，主张世界上一切民族完全平等，坚决反对帝国主义和殖民主义制度，利用国外的一切积极因素，为我国建设社会主义事业争取一个国际和平环境。而其中首要的问题就是巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结。
    资产阶级右派分子反对一边倒，反对我国参加以苏联为首的社会主义阵营，要在我国提倡“中立主义”，这是什么意思呢？这一点，匈牙利的反革命分子给我们上了一课。非常清楚，这就是要改变我们国家的社会主义性质，使我国重新沦为帝国主义的殖民地，并且将使我国成为帝国主义挑动新战争的战场，使全中国人民和世界人民遭受战争的浩劫。因此，我们必须痛击右派的谬论。（摘自10月8日中国青年报王力：“国际主义的一个主要标志”）
