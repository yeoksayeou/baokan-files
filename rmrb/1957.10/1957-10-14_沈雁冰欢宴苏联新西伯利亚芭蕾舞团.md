Title: Shen Yanbing Hosts a Banquet for the Soviet Novosibirsk Ballet Troupe
Author:
Date: 1957-10-14
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 13th: Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing hosted a banquet in Beijing this evening for all members of the Soviet Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theatre Ballet Troupe.

　　In his speech at the banquet, Shen Yanbing said that the Ballet Troupe not only brought outstanding dance art but also the profound friendship of the Soviet people. Shen Yanbing also expressed his deep gratitude to the artists of the Ballet Troupe for helping China train ballet dancers during their performance breaks.

　　Troupe Leader Ligachev also spoke. He wished the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people and the increasing prosperity of China's cultural and artistic undertakings.

　　Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin was invited to attend the banquet and also spoke.

　　Also present at the banquet tonight were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Qian Junrui, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, and well-known figures from Beijing's literary and art circles.

### 沈雁冰欢宴苏联新西伯利亚芭蕾舞团

1957-10-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　沈雁冰欢宴苏联新西伯利亚芭蕾舞团
    新华社13日讯　文化部部长沈雁冰今天晚间在北京欢宴苏联新西伯利亚歌舞剧院芭蕾舞团全体人员。
    沈雁冰在宴会上致词说，芭蕾舞团不仅带来了卓越的舞蹈艺术，而且带来了苏联人民的深厚友谊。沈雁冰还对芭蕾舞团的艺术家们在演出之暇帮助中国培养芭蕾舞演员，表示深切的谢意。
    利加乔夫团长也讲了话。他祝中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊和中国文化艺术事业日益繁荣。
    苏联驻中国大使尤金应邀出席了宴会并且讲了话。
    今晚出席宴会作陪的还有外交部副部长曾涌泉，中苏友好协会总会秘书长钱俊瑞，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南以及北京文艺界著名人士。
