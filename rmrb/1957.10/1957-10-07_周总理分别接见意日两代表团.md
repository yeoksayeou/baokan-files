Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Italian and Japanese Delegations Separately
Author:
Date: 1957-10-07
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the entire Italian Friendship Delegation to China this afternoon.
    Present at the meeting were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Yang Hansheng, Vice President, and Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Japanese Agricultural and Farmer Delegation headed by Yuuzen Ishida this afternoon.
    Present at the meeting were Yang Xiandong, Chairman of the Chinese Society of Agronomy, Chen Fengtong, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences, and Liao Chengzhi and others.

### 周总理分别接见意日两代表团

1957-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理分别接见意日两代表团
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了意大利友好访华代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长阳翰笙，外交部西欧司司长黄华。
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以石田宥全为首的日本农业农民代表团。
    接见时在座的，有中国农学会理事长杨显东、中国农业科学院副院长陈凤桐以及廖承志等人。
