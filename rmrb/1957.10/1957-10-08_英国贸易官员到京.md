Title: British Trade Official Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The British Parliamentary Secretary to the Board of Trade, Mr. Erroll, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Mr. Erroll is the first senior British trade official to visit China. Also accompanying him were Mr. Tippets, a senior British Board of Trade official in charge of trade with East Asia, and Mr. Sattler, Mr. Erroll's personal secretary.
Greeting them at the airport were Mr. Zhu Jianbai, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of our country, and Mr. Wu Jingfang, Director of the Protocol Department. Mr. W. Roy Evans, the British Chargé d'Affaires in China, was also at the airport to greet them.

### 英国贸易官员到京

1957-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    英国贸易官员到京
    新华社6日讯　英国贸易部政务次官埃勒耳今天下午乘飞机到达北京。埃勒耳是第一个到中国来访问的英国高级贸易官员。同来的还有英国贸易部负责对东亚贸易的高级官员提普次和埃勒耳的私人秘书萨特勒。
    在机场欢迎的有我国对外贸易部三局副局长朱剑白和交际处处长武竞放。英国驻华代办文郁生也到机场迎接。
