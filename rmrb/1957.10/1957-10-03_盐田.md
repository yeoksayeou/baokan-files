Salt Fields

    Look at the shimmering salt fields, like mirrors,
    Each salt pile like a magnolia blossom.
    To weave this pure white, amorous garland, ah,
    The salt workers let the sun darken their faces...

### 盐田

1957-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    盐田
    看那镜子一般闪光的盐田，
    一座座盐堆像一朵朵玉兰，
    为了编织这洁白多情的花环呵
    盐工们让太阳晒黑了脸……
