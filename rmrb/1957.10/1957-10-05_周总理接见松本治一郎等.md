Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Matsumoto Jiichiro and Others
Author:
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th — Premier Zhou Enlai received Matsumoto Jiichiro, head of the Japan-China Friendship Association delegation to China and president of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Vice Head of Delegation and Vice President of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Kawazaki Natsu, Vice Head of Delegation and Director of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Murase Kiyoshi, Vice Head of Delegation and Standing Director of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Sugawara Masato, and all members of the delegation this afternoon.

Those present at the meeting were Liao Chengzhi, Chu Tunan, and Zhao Anbo. (Attached picture)

Premier Zhou Enlai met with the Japan-China Friendship Association delegation to China on October 4. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai talking with the head of the delegation Matsumoto Jiichiro (center). On the left is the vice head of the delegation, Kawazaki Natsu. (Photo by Meng Qingbiao, Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 周总理接见松本治一郎等

1957-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理接见松本治一郎等
    新华社4日讯　周恩来总理今日下午接见了日中友好协会访华代表团团长、日中友协会长松本治一郎，副团长、日中友协副会长河崎夏，副团长、日中友协理事村濑清，副团长、日中友协常任理事菅原昌人及代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的有廖承志、楚图南、赵安博。（附图片）
    周恩来总理10月4日接见了日本日中友好协会访华代表团。图为周恩来总理和团长松本治一郎（中）谈话。左是副团长河崎夏。　新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
