Title: Renowned Soviet Violinist David Oistrakh Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-06
Page: 8

　　Renowned Soviet Violinist
　　Oistrakh Arrives in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, 4th -- David Oistrakh, the most outstanding contemporary Soviet violinist, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
　　This musical master has come to China for a three-week visit and performance tour according to the 1957 execution plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The pianist Yang Polsky is traveling with him. His first performance in Beijing will be on the 6th of this month, and the Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live performance to the whole country on its second program.

### 著名苏联小提琴家  奥伊斯特拉赫到京

1957-10-06
第8版()
专栏：

    著名苏联小提琴家
    奥伊斯特拉赫到京
    据新华社4日讯　当代最杰出的苏联小提琴家达维德·奥伊斯特拉赫，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    这位音乐大师是根据中苏文化合作协定1957年执行计划到中国来作三星期的访问演出的，和他同行的有钢琴家杨波尔斯基。本月6日在北京首次演出，中央人民广播电台将要用第二种节目向全国转播演出实况。
