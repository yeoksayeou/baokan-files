Title: Huangyan Mandarin Oranges Have a Bumper Harvest
Author:
Date: 1957-10-29
Page: 4

    Huangyan Mandarin Oranges Have a Bumper Harvest
    Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 28th. More than 20,000 mu of Huangyan mandarin oranges on both sides of the Chengjiang River have had a bumper harvest. The male and female members of the agricultural cooperatives are busy harvesting, and more than 2,000 workers are rushing to pack them day and night. Large quantities of early-season oranges are being shipped to domestic and foreign markets.

### 黄岩蜜桔大丰收

1957-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    黄岩蜜桔大丰收
    据新华社杭州28日电　澄江两岸两万多亩黄岩蜜桔大丰收。农业社的男女社员们正忙着采收，两千多工人日夜赶着包装，大批早桔正陆续运销国内外。
