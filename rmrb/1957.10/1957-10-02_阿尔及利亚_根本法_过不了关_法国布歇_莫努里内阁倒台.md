Title: Algerian “Fundamental Law” Fails to Pass; French Bourgès-Maunoury Cabinet Falls

Author:

Date: 1957-10-02
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, October 1st, Paris dispatch: The French Bourgès-Maunoury government, in power for only three and a half months, announced its collapse on September 30th. In a vote of confidence held by the French National Assembly on the evening of September 30th regarding the government's proposed Algerian "Fundamental Law," the government was defeated by 279 votes against and 253 votes in favor. The Bourgès-Maunoury cabinet immediately submitted its resignation to French President Coty.

    News from Washington states that Washington diplomats are dismayed by the fall of the French government, as it will make France's situation more difficult during the United Nations General Assembly debate on the Algerian question.

### 阿尔及利亚“根本法”过不了关  法国布歇—莫努里内阁倒台

1957-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚“根本法”过不了关
    法国布歇—莫努里内阁倒台
    据新华社1日讯  巴黎消息：执政仅仅三个半月的法国布歇—莫努里政府9月30日宣告倒台。在30日夜间法国国民议会就政府所提出的阿尔及利亚“根本法”举行的信任投票中，这个政府以二百七十九票反对和二百五十三票赞成遭到失败。布歇—莫努里内阁随即向法国总统戈蒂提出辞职。
    从华盛顿传出的消息说，华盛顿外交人士对法国政府的倒台感到沮丧。因为这将使法国在联合国大会辩论阿尔及利亚问题时的处境更为困难。
