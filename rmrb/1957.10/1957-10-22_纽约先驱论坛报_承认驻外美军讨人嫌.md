Title: "New York Herald Tribune" Admits American Troops Stationed Abroad are Disliked
Author:
Date: 1957-10-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, October 21st: New York - The "New York Herald Tribune" published an article on the 19th stating that citizens of the United States' allies are expressing dislike for American troops stationed in their countries.

The article said that people in these countries feel "fear and aversion" towards American soldiers, and they regard the "stay of the Yankees" as a "headache."

The article's author said: "The most vehement opposition to American soldiers is in Japan and Belgium."

### “纽约先驱论坛报”承认驻外美军讨人嫌

1957-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

    “纽约先驱论坛报”承认驻外美军讨人嫌
    新华社21日讯　纽约消息：“纽约先驱论坛报”19日刊载一篇文章说，美国盟国的公民对驻在他们境内的美国军队表示讨厌。
    文章说，这些国家的人对美国兵感到“恐惧和厌恶”，他们把“美国佬的驻留”当作一件“令人头痛的事情”。
    文章的作者说：“反对美国兵的情绪最猛烈的是日本和比利时”。
