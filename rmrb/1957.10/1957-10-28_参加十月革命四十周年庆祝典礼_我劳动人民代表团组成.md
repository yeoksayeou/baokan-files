Title: Our Laboring People's Delegation Formed to Participate in the 40th Anniversary Celebration of the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th - In response to the joint invitation from the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, the Soviet Peace Committee, and the Soviet Women's Committee, relevant people's organizations such as the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Communist Youth League of China, the All-China Women’s Federation, and the China Peace Committee have decided to send a Chinese Laboring People's Delegation to Moscow to participate in the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.

The members of the Chinese Laboring People's Delegation include representative figures from workers, peasants, intellectuals, youth, women, and other relevant fields, including well-known industrial and agricultural labor models, advanced producers, scientists, writers, and artists. Representatives from several important crystallizations of Sino-Soviet friendship and cooperation – Anshan Iron and Steel, the First Automobile Works, the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, and the Friendship Farm – are participating. Six of the delegates are glorious participants in the October Revolution and the Soviet Civil War.

The head of the Chinese Laboring People's Delegation is Liu Ningyi, and the deputy heads are Qian Junrui, Xu Guangping, Lao She, Mei Lanfang, and Qian Sanqiang. The members are Ba Jin, Wang Zhenglin, Wang Kun, Tian Han, Shi Ximin, Liu Changsheng, Liu Fu, Yang Hansheng, Li Fuqing, Alatan Aoqi'er, Meng Tai, Lü Hongbin, Du Shuzhen, Ji Shoushan, Chen Bocuan, Luo Yi, Patikhan Sugurbayev, Wang Juqian, Ma Sicong, Zhao Mingjian, Yao Qin, Wei Xizhuo, Zhang Yun, Zhang Chuanlin, Xu Zhenjiu, Sheng Li, Cao Guanqun, Jin Shuliang, Yu Yixuan, and Tan Xiuxiang.

### 参加十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼  我劳动人民代表团组成

1957-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    参加十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼
    我劳动人民代表团组成
    新华社27日讯　应全苏工会中央理事会、苏联对外文化协会、苏联列宁共产主义青年团、苏联保卫和平委员会、苏联妇女委员会的联合邀请，中苏友好协会总会、中华全国总工会、中国共产主义青年团、中华人民共和国妇女联合会以及中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等有关人民团体决定派出中国劳动人民代表团，到莫斯科参加伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的庆祝典礼。
    中国劳动人民代表团的成员包括工人、农民、知识分子、青年、妇女以及其他有关方面的代表性人物，其中有著名的工业和农业劳动模范、先进生产者、科学家、作家和艺术家等。中苏友好合作的几个重要的结晶品——鞍钢、第一汽车厂、武汉长江大桥、友谊农场等单位，都有代表性人物参加。代表中，有六人是光荣的十月革命和苏联国内战争的参加者。
    中国劳动人民代表团的团长是刘宁一，副团长是钱俊瑞、许广平、老舍、梅兰芳、钱三强。团员是巴金、王正林、王昆、田汉、石西民、刘长胜、刘福、阳翰笙、李富清、阿拉坦敖其尔、孟泰、吕鸿宾、杜淑贞、季寿山、陈柏川、罗毅、帕提汗·苏古尔巴也夫、汪菊潜、马思聪、赵明坚、姚溱、韦锡琢、章蕴、张川林、许振久、盛利、曹冠群、靳树梁、喻宜萱、谭秀香。
