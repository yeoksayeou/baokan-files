Title: Dr. Hatta Visits Peking University
Author:
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — Former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta and his entourage visited Peking University this morning.

    The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by President Ma Yinchu and the faculty and students of Peking University.

    Dr. Hatta visited the teaching building and library of the Department of Oriental Languages with great interest. He saw many books in Indonesian there and had close conversations with Chinese students majoring in Indonesian.

    In addition, Vice Premier Li Fuchun also briefed the distinguished guests today on some aspects of China's economic construction.

### 哈达博士参观北京大学

1957-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    哈达博士参观北京大学
    据新华社30日讯  印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士及其随行人员今天上午参观了北京大学。
    贵宾们在北大受到了马寅初校长和北大师生的热烈欢迎。
    哈达博士很有兴趣地参观了东方语言学系的教学大楼和藏书室，他在那里看到了许多印度尼西亚文的书籍，还和印度尼西亚语专业的中国学生用印度尼西亚话作了密切的交谈。
    此外，李富春副总理今天还给贵宾们介绍了有关中国经济建设的一些情况。
