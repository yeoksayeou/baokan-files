Title: Hungarian Tennis Team Wins All Matches in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 8

Sports News

    Hungarian Tennis Team Wins All Matches in Shanghai
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 27th. The Hungarian tennis team held two friendly matches with the Shanghai Municipal Tennis Team on the 26th and 27th, winning all matches.
    The match between Hungarian player Gulyás and Huang Daxuan was tense and exciting. Huang Daxuan led with 5-1 and 5-2 at the beginning of the two sets respectively, but when the opponent launched a fierce counterattack, he lost to Gulyás with 5-7 and 7-9 due to insufficient experience.
    The Hungarian team played a total of two women's singles matches, four men's singles matches, and two men's doubles matches in Shanghai. The scores were all 2-0.

### 匈牙利网球队在上海比赛获全胜

1957-10-28
第8版()
专栏：体育新闻

    匈牙利网球队在上海比赛获全胜
    据新华社上海27日电　匈牙利网球队26日和27日同上海市网球队举行两次友谊比赛，获得全胜。
    匈牙利古雅士对黄大暄的一场，打得紧张而且精采。黄大暄在两盘开始时分别以五比一和五比二领先，可是在对方猛力反攻时，他因临场经验不够，反而以五比七和七比九败给了古雅士。
    匈牙利队在上海比赛共进行女子单打两场、男子单打四场和男子双打两场。比数都是二比零。
