Title: East German Delegation Visits Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1957-10-10
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, October 9th. The East German delegation, led by Deputy Prime Minister Oelssner, visited Tianjin for a day by car today. Tianjin Vice Mayor Li Gengtao and others went to the suburbs to welcome them.

The distinguished guests visited the newly built plastic wire workshop at the Tianjin No. 1 Wire Factory. This workshop was designed with the help of East Germany, and most of the instruments and equipment were also supplied by East Germany.

In the afternoon, the distinguished guests visited the New Port and took a boat trip to the sea. In the evening, the distinguished guests returned to Beijing.

### 民主德国代表团访问天津

1957-10-10
第3版()
专栏：

    民主德国代表团访问天津
    新华社天津9日电　厄斯纳副总理率领的民主德国代表团今天乘汽车来天津访问了一天。天津市副市长李耕涛等前往市郊欢迎。
    贵宾们在天津第一电线厂参观了新建的塑胶线车间。这个车间是由民主德国帮助我国设计的，大部分仪器设备也是民主德国供应的。
    下午，贵宾们参观了新港，并乘船出海游览。当晚，贵宾们回北京。
