Title: Decorated with Lanterns and Streamers, Red Flags Flying: Hong Kong and Macao Celebrate National Day Enthusiastically
Author:
Date: 1957-10-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, October 1st: Compatriots from all walks of life in Hong Kong and Macao warmly celebrated the eighth National Day today.

More than 5,000 compatriots from all walks of life in Hong Kong and Kowloon held a celebration rally at the Jinling Restaurant, which was adorned in festive attire, at noon today. Amid thunderous applause, the assembly passed telegrams of greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai.

A celebration rally for National Day was held by compatriots from all walks of life in Macao at noon today. The assembly also passed telegrams of greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai.

The Macao Chinese General Chamber of Commerce held a reception at the International Hotel at noon today, attended by more than 1,300 representatives from the industrial and commercial sectors and various community organizations.

All ten cinemas and theaters in Macao offered free movie screenings at noon today to entertain compatriots from all walks of life.

Many labor unions, community organizations, schools, banks, and shops in Hong Kong and Macao are adorned with red flags, slogans, colored arches, and lights today, taking on a completely new look. The recently completed Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge has become the most popular decoration on this year's National Day colored arches. The tallest Bank of China building in Hong Kong, adorned in festive attire, has attracted the most attention, displaying a banner as high as eleven stories, which reads: "Long Live the People's Republic of China!" The four archways built by compatriots from all walks of life in Macao to celebrate National Day were all decorated with exquisite designs and magnificent lighting last night. All major streets in Macao were simultaneously decorated with lanterns and streamers.

In recent days, workers, industrialists, businessmen, educators, filmmakers, journalists, publishers, artists, and bankers in Hong Kong have held or are about to hold National Day celebration rallies. The Macao Federation of Trade Unions, the Macao Chinese Students' Union, and the Macao Democratic Women's Federation also held celebration rallies before National Day.

Tsuen Wan, the area with the most Kuomintang secret agent atrocities during last year's Kowloon riots, is today adorned with high colored arches and flying red flags.

### 张灯结彩、红旗招展  港澳各界热烈庆祝国庆

1957-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    张灯结彩、红旗招展
    港澳各界热烈庆祝国庆
    据新华社香港1日电  香港、澳门各界同胞今天热烈庆祝第八届国庆节。
    港九各界同胞五千多人今天中午在披着节日盛装的金陵酒家举行庆祝集会。大会在雷动般的掌声中通过了致毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理的贺电。
    澳门各界同胞庆祝国庆大会在今天中午举行。大会也通过了给毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理的贺电。
    澳门中华总商会今天中午在国际酒店举行酒会，参加的有工商界和各社团代表一千三百多人。
    全澳门十家戏院和剧场，今天中午免费放映电影招待各界同胞。
    香港、澳门的许多工会、社团、学校、银行和商店今天都用红旗、标语、彩牌和灯光打扮得焕然一新。最近建成的武汉长江大桥成为今年庆祝国庆彩牌上最流行的装饰。披着盛装的香港最高的中国银行大厦最受人们注意，它悬挂着一幅高达十一层楼的标语，上面写着：“中华人民共和国万岁！”澳门各界同胞为庆祝国庆而盖搭的四座牌楼，昨晚已全部装上精美图饰和瑰丽灯饰。全澳门所有主要街道，都同时张灯结彩。
    香港的各业工人、工商界、教育界、电影界、新闻界、出版界、美术界、银行界在最近几天分别举行了或者即将举行国庆庆祝大会。澳门工会联合总会、澳门中华学生联合会和澳门民主妇女联合会也分别在国庆前举行了庆祝集会。
    荃湾，这个去年在九龙暴乱事件中国民党特务分子暴行最多的地区，今天高搭彩牌，红旗招展。
