Title: Our Friendship
Author: A·Qiachi
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 8

    Our Friendship
    Albania A·Qiachi
    Years flow like a mighty river,
    The sun still sets in the west every day,
    But in China, the desire for life,
    Burns in everyone's heart without ever being extinguished.
    From that distant Heilongjiang
    To the boundless yellow Gobi Desert,
    From the hot Hainan Island all the way to the ocean,
    The Chinese people are united like a single person.
    Today, their eyes shine with light,
    Using anger to sweep away the darkness.
    And I am also very excited to see:
    This light shining in every corner.
    What China was like in the past, and what it is like today,
    Even the enemy can only lower their heads and say nothing...
    Everywhere there is a boiling enthusiasm for construction
    For the sake of a century-long plan, forever happy, prosperous and strong.
    Today, our great friendship
    Is universal to all directions of the world.
    This newly established friendship,
    Has never been seen or praised in the old world.
    I am an Albanian, but my dear brothers,
    I feel my heart singing at every moment,
    When I see China how ceaselessly
    Marches forward in great strides, forever towards the direction of victory.
                        1957, Beijing

### 我们的友谊
阿·恰奇
1957-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    我们的友谊
    阿尔巴尼亚  阿·恰奇
    岁月像大江滚滚地流
    太阳每天都依旧落到西方，
    可是在中国，对生活的热望，
    却永不熄灭地燃烧在每个人的心上。
    从那遥远的黑龙江
    直到无边无际的黄色的大戈壁，
    从炎热的海南岛一直到大海洋，
    中国人民团结得像整个人一样。
    今天，他们的眼睛里闪着光芒，
    用忿怒来把那黑暗扫开。
    而我，也非常兴奋地看到：
    这一光芒在每个角落里发着光芒。
    过去的中国是怎样，今天又是怎样
    即使敌人，也只能低下头来一声不响……
    到处沸腾着的是一片建设的热情
    为的是百年大计，永远幸福富强。
    今天，我们伟大的友情
    普遍到全世界的四面八方。
    这种新建立起来的友情，
    在旧世界里从未见过，也未被人赞扬。
    我是阿尔巴尼亚人，但是我亲爱的弟兄
    我感觉到我的心时时刻刻在歌唱，
    当我看到中国怎样毫不停息地
    跨着大步前进，永远向着胜利的方向。
                        1957年于北京
