Title: Brazilian Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-17
Page: 3

　　Brazilian Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Brazilian Medical Delegation, headed by Professor Carvalho, a group of eleven people, arrived in Beijing today by TU-104 airplane at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association, after attending the World Medical Congress held in Paris in September this year. They will pay a friendly visit to our country for about a month.

### 巴西医学代表团到北京

1957-10-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　巴西医学代表团到北京
    新华社16日讯　以卡瓦洛教授为首的巴西医学代表团一行十一人，在参加了今年9月间在巴黎召开的世界医生大会后，应中华医学会的邀请，于今日乘图—一○四飞机抵北京。他们将在我国作为期一个月左右的友好访问。
