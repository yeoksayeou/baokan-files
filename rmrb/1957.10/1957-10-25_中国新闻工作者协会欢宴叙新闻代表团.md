Title: China Journalists Association Hosts Banquet for Syrian News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th -- The All-China Journalists Association held a banquet on the evening of the 24th to bid farewell to the Syrian journalists delegation.

    Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, said at the banquet that the Syrian journalists delegation visited China at a time when the Syrian people were engaged in a heroic struggle to defend their independence. The delegation could see warm welcome and respect for them everywhere in China, and this welcome and respect represented the support of the 600 million Chinese people for the Syrian people.

    Wadi Ben George Sidawi, representing the Syrian journalists, thanked the Chinese journalists for their hospitality and warm welcome. He said that the delegation had seen the spirit of unity shown by the Chinese people in their construction everywhere in China, and had seen the full support of the Chinese people for the Syrian people. After returning to their country, the delegation will convey the support of the 600 million Chinese people to the Syrian people, and objectively report on the situation in China to the Syrian people. Sidawi concluded by wishing friendship between the Syrian and Chinese people and close cooperation between the journalists of the two countries.

### 中国新闻工作者协会欢宴叙新闻代表团

1957-10-25
第3版()
专栏：

    中国新闻工作者协会欢宴叙新闻代表团
    据新华社24日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会24日晚间举行宴会，欢送叙利亚新闻工作者代表团。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长王芸生在宴会上说，叙利亚新闻工作者代表团来中国访问的时候，正是叙利亚人民为保卫独立进行英勇斗争的时候。代表团在中国到处都可以看到对他们的热烈欢迎和尊敬，这种欢迎和尊敬代表着全中国六亿人民支持叙利亚人民的心意。
    瓦地·本·乔治·西多威代表叙利亚新闻工作者感谢中国新闻界对他们的招待和热烈欢迎，他说，代表团在中国各地看到中国人民在建设中表现的团结精神，看到中国人民对叙利亚人民的全力支持，代表团在回国后将要向叙利亚转达六亿中国人民对他们的支持，向叙利亚人民客观地报道中国的情况。西多威最后祝叙利亚、中国人民友好和两国新闻工作者的亲密合作。
