Title: Hungarian Christian Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-10-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — The Hungarian Christian delegation headed by Bishop Zapp Joseph concluded its visit to China and returned home by plane from Beijing on the morning of the 20th.

During its stay in China, the delegation visited Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou, and had extensive contacts with people from Chinese Christian circles, and discussed issues such as the Three-Self Patriotic Movement of Chinese Christianity.

### 匈基督教代表团回国

1957-10-21
第3版()
专栏：

    匈基督教代表团回国
    据新华社20日讯  以萨普·约瑟夫主教为首的匈牙利基督教访华代表团结束了在中国的访问，20日早晨乘飞机离北京回国。
    代表团在中国期间，曾经访问了北京、南京、上海和广州等地，同中国基督教界人士进行了广泛的接触，座谈了中国基督教三自爱国运动等问题。
