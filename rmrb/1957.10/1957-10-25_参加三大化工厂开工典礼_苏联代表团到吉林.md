Title: Soviet Delegation Arrives in Jilin to Attend Opening Ceremonies of Three Major Chemical Plants
Author:
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jilin, October 24th. A Soviet chemical industry delegation, headed by M.I. Ivanov, First Deputy Minister of the Soviet Ministry of Chemical Industry, arrived in Jilin City by train on the afternoon of the 24th, having been invited to our country to attend the opening ceremonies of the three major chemical plants in Jilin.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Peng Tao, Minister of the Ministry of Chemical Industry; Li Youwen, Governor of Jilin Province; Li Diping, Secretary of the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Zhang Wenhai, Mayor of Jilin City; as well as the directors of the three chemical plants in Jilin, the manager of the Metallurgical Company, and workers.

### 参加三大化工厂开工典礼  苏联代表团到吉林

1957-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    参加三大化工厂开工典礼
    苏联代表团到吉林
    据新华社吉林24日电　应邀来我国参加吉林三大化工厂开工生产典礼的、以苏联化工部第一副部长米·依·伊万诺夫为首的苏联化工部代表团一行五人，在24日下午乘火车到达吉林市。
    到车站欢迎的，有化学工业部部长彭涛，吉林省省长栗又文，中共吉林省委书记李砥平，吉林市市长张文海，以及吉林三个化工厂的厂长、冶金公司经理和职工等。
