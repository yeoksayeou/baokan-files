Title: Chongqing Municipality Emphasizes Political and Ideological Education for Intellectuals During Celebration Activities
Author:
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, October 24th. Chongqing Municipality is strengthening political and ideological education for intellectuals and government cadres in its propaganda activities celebrating the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

    Xin Yi Zhi, Secretary of the Chongqing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered a report to more than 4,000 teachers, doctors, journalists, and government cadres in the city on the 23rd, entitled "The Leadership of the Communist Party is the Decisive Condition for Building Socialism." In his report, Xin Yi Zhi criticized various fallacies of bourgeois right-wingers attacking the leadership of the Communist Party.

    According to the plan of the Chongqing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, during this propaganda activity celebrating the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, reports on the two special topics of the dictatorship of the proletariat and internationalism will continue to be made to intellectuals and government cadres in Chongqing.

    At the same time, the Chongqing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China also requires secondary specialized schools and above and various government organs to use their spare time or “Sino-Soviet Friendship Activity Day” to conduct a socialist education, connecting with the political and ideological situation of the masses in their own units.

### 重庆市在庆祝活动中  重视对知识分子的政治思想教育

1957-10-25
第3版()
专栏：

    重庆市在庆祝活动中
    重视对知识分子的政治思想教育
    据新华社重庆24日电　重庆市在庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年的宣传活动中，加强对知识界和机关干部的政治思想教育。
    中共重庆市委书记处书记辛易之23日向全市四千多名教师、医生、新闻记者和机关干部等作报告，讲题是“共产党的领导是建设社会主义的决定条件”。辛易之在报告中批判了资产阶级右派分子攻击共产党的领导的各种谬论。
    根据中共重庆市委员会的计划，在这次庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年的宣传活动中，还要继续对重庆市的知识界和机关干部作关于无产阶级专政和国际主义两个专题的报告。
    同时，中共重庆市委员会还要求中等专业以上学校和各机关利用业余时间或以“中苏友好活动日”方式，联系本单位群众的政治思想情况，进行一次社会主义教育。
