Title: Premier Zhou Receives British Parliamentary Secretary to the Board of Trade Erroll
Author:
Date: 1957-10-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the British Parliamentary Secretary to the Board of Trade, Mr. Erroll, on the afternoon of the 29th.

Present at the meeting were: Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jiuzhuang, Vice Minister Lu Xuzhang, Director of the West European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Huang Hua, and the British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. Wen Yusheng.

### 周总理接见英对外贸易部政务次官埃勒尔

1957-10-30
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理接见英对外贸易部政务次官埃勒尔
    新华社29日讯　周恩来总理29日下午接见了英国对外贸易部政务次官埃勒尔。
    在座的有：对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长卢绪章，外交部西欧司司长黄华和英国驻中国代办文郁生。
