Title: Chairman Mao Cables Syrian President Kuwatly, Firmly Supporting Syria in Defending Independence and Peace
Author:
Date: 1957-10-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Chairman Mao today sent a telegram to Syrian President Shukri al-Kuwatly, expressing gratitude for President Kuwatly's congratulatory telegram on the eighth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and expressing firm support for the just struggle of the Syrian people to defend independence and peace. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Shukri al-Kuwatly, President of the Syrian Republic: Thank you for your congratulatory telegram on the eighth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. On behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, I would like to express my heartfelt thanks to Your Excellency.
    At a time when American imperialism is instigating Turkey to provoke Syria and plotting to launch a war of aggression, I would like to reiterate the solemn position of the Chinese government and the Chinese people in firmly supporting the just struggle of the Syrian people to defend independence and peace.
    I wish the friendly relations between China and Syria will continue to develop, I wish the Syrian people victory in their anti-colonialist struggle, I wish the Syrian Republic prosperity, and Your Excellency good health.
                                                                                                          Mao Zedong, October 17, 1957, in Beijing

### 毛泽东主席电叙利亚总统库阿特利  坚决支持叙利亚保卫独立保卫和平

1957-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛泽东主席电叙利亚总统库阿特利
    坚决支持叙利亚保卫独立保卫和平
　　新华社17日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电叙利亚舒克利·库阿特利总统，对库阿特利总统在中华人民共和国成立八周年时发来的贺电表示感谢，并且表示坚决支持叙利亚人民保卫独立、保卫和平的正义斗争。电文如下：
　　叙利亚共和国总统舒克利·库阿特利先生阁下：中华人民共和国成立八周年时，承蒙来电祝贺，我代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，向阁下表示衷心的谢意。
　　当美帝国主义正在驱使土耳其向叙利亚进行挑衅，阴谋发动侵略战争的时候，我谨重申中国政府和中国人民坚决支持叙利亚人民保卫独立、保卫和平的正义斗争的严正立场。
　　祝中叙两国友好关系日益发展，祝叙利亚人民反殖民主义斗争的胜利，祝叙利亚共和国繁荣和阁下身体健康。
　　　　　　　　　　　　    毛泽东　1957年10月17日于北京
