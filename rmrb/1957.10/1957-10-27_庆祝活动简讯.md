Celebration Activities Briefs
    
    Tianjin to Hold Large-Scale Celebrations
    Factories, government offices, and schools in Tianjin have successively held report meetings and film evenings introducing the great significance of the October Revolution, the achievements of Soviet construction, and Soviet assistance to China. They have also used blackboard newspapers, big-character posters, and wired broadcasts for publicity. The Tianjin Science and Technology Popularization Association and various people's organizations have jointly held more than 400 report meetings to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution and the successful launch of the Soviet artificial satellite. The Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League also held a Soviet artistic program viewing session for young people.
    The ten city-wide large-scale report meetings held to publicize the significance of the October Revolution and the achievements of Soviet construction began on the 22nd.

    Ten Special Lectures
    The China-Soviet Friendship Association and the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization will jointly hold lectures on Soviet scientific and technological achievements, including ten topics, starting on the 28th.
    In the ten lectures, scientists will introduce the Soviet Union's achievements in launching artificial earth satellites, atomic energy, Arctic and Antarctic exploration, the iron and steel industry, machinery manufacturing technology, the electric power industry, electronics technology, agricultural science and technology, water conservancy construction, and medicine, as well as its assistance to China. In addition to Chinese scientists, Soviet experts assisting China's construction will also give lectures.
    The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast recordings of each lecture.

    Provincial and Municipal Leaders as Speakers
    The Harbin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has organized nine reports on visits to the Soviet Union and introductions to Soviet achievements. The report "Visiting the Soviet Union's Communist Youth League City" made by the Deputy Secretary of the Harbin Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the Soviet novel report meeting "Building the Communist Youth League City" held specifically for workers, were very popular among the youth and workers of Harbin.
    More than 200 leading cadres of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Heilongjiang Provincial People's Committee, the Harbin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and the Harbin Municipal People's Committee, as well as directors of provincial and municipal departments and bureaus, will serve as speakers from October 28th to November 7th, going deep into various government offices, factories, and schools throughout the city to give reports on the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 庆祝活动简讯

1957-10-27
第3版()
专栏：庆祝活动简讯

    庆祝活动简讯 
    天津将大规模庆祝
    天津市各工厂、机关、学校都相继举办了介绍十月革命的伟大意义、苏联建设成就、苏联对我国的援助等方面的报告会和电影晚会，并利用黑板报、大字报和有线广播进行宣传。仅天津市科学技术普及协会和各人民团体为庆祝十月革命四十周年和苏联发射人造卫星成功联合举办的报告会已有四百多次。共青团天津市委员会还为青年们举办了苏联文艺节目观摩会。
    为宣传十月革命意义和苏联建设成就而举办的十次全市性大报告会，已在22日开始。
    十个专题讲座
    中苏友好协会总会和中华全国科学技术普及协会，联合举办的包括十个讲题的苏联科学技术成就讲座，将从28日开始。
    在十次讲演中，科学家们将分别介绍苏联在发射人造地球卫星、原子能、南北极探险、钢铁工业、机器制造技术、电力工业、电子学技术、农业科学技术、水利建设和医学等方面的成就以及对我国的帮助。担任讲演的，除中国科学家外，还有协助我国进行建设的苏联专家。
    中央人民广播电台将播送每次讲演的录音。 
    省市负责人担任报告员
    哈尔滨市中苏友好协会已经组织了九次访问苏联和介绍苏联成就的报告。共青团哈尔滨市委副书记所作的“访问苏联共青团城”的报告，和专为工人举办的苏联小说报告会“建设共青团城”，很受哈尔滨市青年和工人的欢迎。
    中共黑龙江省委、黑龙江省人民委员会和中共哈尔滨市委、哈尔滨市人民委员会的负责人，以及省、市厅、局长以上的干部二百多人，将在10月28日到11月7日期间担任报告员，深入到全市各个机关、工厂、学校做关于庆祝十月革命四十周年的报告。　（据新华社讯）
