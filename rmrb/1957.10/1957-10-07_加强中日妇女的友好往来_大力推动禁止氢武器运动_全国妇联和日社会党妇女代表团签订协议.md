Title: Strengthen Friendly Exchanges Between Chinese and Japanese Women; Vigorously Promote the Movement to Ban Hydrogen Weapons; All-China Women's Federation and Japanese Socialist Party Women's Delegation Sign Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 6th. The All-China Women's Federation and the Japanese Socialist Party Women's Delegation to China signed a friendly agreement in Beijing today. Both parties unanimously agreed to further strengthen friendly exchanges and cultural exchange between women of the two countries, vigorously promote the movement to ban the manufacture and testing of atomic and hydrogen weapons, and promote the early restoration of formal diplomatic relations between China and Japan.

Li Dequan and Yang Zhihua, vice-chairpersons of the All-China Women's Federation, and Luo Qiong and Cao Mengjun, secretaries, and Watanabe Michiko, head of the Japanese Socialist Party Women's Delegation to China, Vice-Head Funayama Tomi, Secretary-General Yanagawa Hisako, and Secretary Hayashi Hinako signed the agreement on behalf of their respective parties.

The full text of the Friendly Agreement between the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China and the Japanese Socialist Party Women's Delegation to China is as follows:

The Japanese Socialist Party Women's Delegation to China visited China at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China in order to deepen the friendship between the women of the two countries.

Although diplomatic relations between China and Japan have not yet been restored, the Japanese Socialist Party Women's Delegation to China expresses its heartfelt gratitude for the grand welcome from the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China.

The Japanese Socialist Party Women's Delegation to China also took this opportunity to participate in the National Day of the People's Republic of China and personally witnessed the national celebration of the construction of New China, expressing its respect for the brilliant development of the People's Republic of China and the strong unity and progress of all the people.

The Japanese Socialist Party Women's Delegation to China sincerely hopes that diplomatic relations between the two countries will be restored as soon as possible, and will work to promote its realization after returning home.

Both parties agree:

(1) The Japanese Socialist Party Women's Delegation to China, based on the Japanese Socialist Party's policy of not recognizing the existence of two Chinas, will strive to promote the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and the People's Republic of China from the perspective of women.

(2) In order to defend world peace, the All-China Women's Federation and the Japanese Socialist Party Women's Delegation to China will vigorously promote the movement to ban the manufacture and testing of atomic and hydrogen weapons.

(3) To strive to promote friendly exchanges and cultural exchange between women of the two countries.

### 加强中日妇女的友好往来  大力推动禁止氢武器运动  全国妇联和日社会党妇女代表团签订协议

1957-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    加强中日妇女的友好往来  大力推动禁止氢武器运动
    全国妇联和日社会党妇女代表团签订协议
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会同日本社会党妇女访华代表团今天在北京签订了一项友好协议。协议双方一致同意要进一步加强两国妇女的友好往来和文化交流，大力推动禁止制造和试验原子武器和氢武器的运动，并促进中日两国正式邦交的早日恢复。
    中华全国妇女联合会副主席李德全、杨之华，书记罗琼、曹孟君和日本社会党妇女访华代表团团长渡边道子、副团长船山登美、秘书长柳川久子、秘书林品子，分别代表双方在协议书上签了字。
    中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会和日本社会党妇女访华代表团友好协议书全文如下：
    日本社会党妇女访华代表团应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会的邀请，为了加深两国妇女的友好亲善，访问了中国。
    虽然中日两国间的邦交尚未恢复，但是，对于中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会的盛大欢迎，日本社会党妇女访华代表团表示衷心的感谢。
    日本社会党妇女访华代表团同时借着这个机会参加了中华人民共和国国庆节，亲眼看到了庆贺新中国的建设的民族大典，对于中华人民共和国的辉煌发展和全体人民的坚强团结和前进表示敬意。
    日本社会党妇女访华代表团衷心希望两国邦交早日恢复，归国后，将为促其实现而努力。
    双方协议：
    （一）日本社会党妇女访华代表团根据日本社会党不承认存在着两个中国的方针，站在妇女的立场上，为促进日本和中华人民共和国恢复邦交而努力。
    （二）为了保卫世界和平，中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会和日本社会党妇女访华代表团大力推动禁止制造和试验原子武器和氢武器的运动。
    （三）为增进两国妇女的友好往来和文化交流而努力。
