Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Mongolian National Leaders
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 3
　
To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:
To Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China:
To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

　　As the Chinese people celebrate the eighth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of the entire Mongolian people, the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, and the government, we extend to you, dear comrades, and through you to the fraternal Chinese people, our most heartfelt congratulations and best wishes.
　　The unprecedented achievements made by the Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party on the road of rapid progress fill the working people of the Mongolian People's Republic with sincere joy.
　　The fraternal friendship and close cooperation between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples are being consolidated and developed day by day.
　　The Mongolian people are grateful to the Chinese people for the valuable assistance given to the Mongolian People's Republic in building socialism.
　　On this important festival, the Mongolian people sincerely wish the great Chinese people further great achievements in creative peaceful labor and in the struggle for world peace.
　　Long live the unbreakable friendship between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples!
　　Z. Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic
　　Yu. Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic
　　S. Avarzed, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 30, 1957, Ulaanbaatar

### 蒙古国家领导人的贺电

1957-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙古国家领导人的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志：中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇同志：中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来同志：
    当中国人民庆祝中华人民共和国成立八周年国庆节的时候，谨代表全体蒙古人民、蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团和政府向你们，亲爱的同志们，并通过你们向兄弟的中国人民致最衷心的祝贺和最良好的愿望。
    中国人民在中国共产党的领导下在飞速前进的道路上所获得的空前的成就使蒙古人民共和国的劳动人民感到衷心的喜悦。
    蒙、中两国人民的兄弟友谊和亲密合作正在日益巩固和发展。
    蒙古人民感谢中国人民在蒙古人民共和国建设社会主义方面所给予的宝贵的帮助。
    在这个重大的节日里，蒙古人民衷心地预祝伟大的中国人民在创造性的和平劳动中和争取世界和平的斗争中获得进一步的巨大成就。
    蒙、中两国人民牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席  扎·桑布
    蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席  尤·泽登巴尔
    蒙古人民共和国外交部长  斯·阿维尔齐德
                              1957年9月30日于乌兰巴托
