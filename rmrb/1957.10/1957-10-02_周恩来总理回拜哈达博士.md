Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Returns Visit to Dr. Hatta
Author:
Date: 1957-10-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Premier Zhou Enlai returned a visit this afternoon to former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta and his wife.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai were Vice Premier Chen Yi.

Indonesian Ambassador to China, Mr. Wirjopranoto, was also present.

### 周恩来总理回拜哈达博士

1957-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理回拜哈达博士
    新华社1日讯  周恩来总理今天下午回拜了印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士和夫人。
    陪同周恩来总理前去的有陈毅副总理。
    印度尼西亚驻中国大使维约普拉诺托也在座。
