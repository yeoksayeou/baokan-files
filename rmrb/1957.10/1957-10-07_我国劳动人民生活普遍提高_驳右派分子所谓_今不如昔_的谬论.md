    In addition to the above, the number of workers and staff enjoying free medical care in state organs and cultural and educational departments reached 6.613 million in 1956, an increase of more than 65% over the 4 million in 1952. The number of workers and staff enjoying labor insurance reached 7.41 million, an increase of 124.5% over the 3.3 million in 1952. From 1952 to 1956, the state built more than 73 million square meters of housing for workers and staff, averaging three square meters per person based on the total number of workers and staff nationwide in 1956. In addition, state expenditures for wage supplements, bonus funds, rent subsidies, and transportation subsidies to increase workers' and staff's income increased from 660 million yuan in 1952 to 2.25 billion yuan in 1956, equivalent to 14% of the total wage bill.
