Title: Celebrating the Rich Variety of Radio Programs: National Listeners Will Hear the Voice of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 24th Dispatch: National listeners will soon hear the voice of the great revolutionary teacher Lenin. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast recordings of five speeches by Lenin, including "Letter to the Red Army," "What is Soviet Power," and "How to Free the Working People Forever from the Oppression of Landlords and Capitalists," around the anniversary of the October Revolution. In addition, during this period, listeners will also hear radio speeches by Lin Boqu and Guo Moruo, vice presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Yang Xianzhen, president of the Central Party School of the Chinese Communist Party, and General Xiao Hua, deputy director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, in celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. The Central People's Broadcasting Station has also invited Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, to deliver a radio address to Chinese listeners on the evening of November 7th in celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. Kochetov, head of the Soviet cultural delegation, has also been invited to deliver a radio address to Chinese listeners in celebration of the great 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.

The Central People's Broadcasting Station has organized a series of political, literary, and musical celebration programs to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. In addition to broadcasting live coverage of the celebration rally held by people from all walks of life in the capital to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, the evening programs will also report on celebration activities in Beijing and major cities, and broadcast live coverage of the grand parade held in Moscow's Red Square that day.

In terms of music broadcasting, in addition to the current ongoing series of music programs that systematically introduce musical works from the various Soviet republics, and the daily broadcast of instrumental and vocal works by modern Soviet composers, as well as opera excerpts and ballet selections, Chinese musical groups, performers, and singers will also successively hold multiple radio concerts to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. On the evening of the holiday, the radio concert held by the radio station's orchestra will feature the performance of the newly composed large-scale choral work by Chinese musicians, "Long Live, Great Friendship!" and works by Soviet composers.

In the area of literary broadcasting, since mid-October, literary works by modern Soviet writers have been introduced and recited, and excerpts from novels such as "The Rout," "Virgin Soil Upturned," "How the Steel Was Tempered," and "Time, Forward!" have been broadcast in the form of accompanied recitations, along with short stories and poems by other writers, as well as audio clippings from the "Soviet Film Week" films, Soviet plays such as "The Breakup" and "The Man with a Gun," and radio dramas such as "Towards a New Shore" and "The Eve of the Revolution."

In programs such as "International Life," "International Current Events," "Broadcasts to the Armed Forces," "Broadcasts to Children and Young People," "Scientific Knowledge," and "Sunday Lectures," articles and reports on Sino-Soviet friendship and the great achievements of the Soviet Union in various fields have already begun to be broadcast.

### 庆祝广播节目丰富多采  全国听众将听到列宁的声音

1957-10-25
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝广播节目丰富多采
    全国听众将听到列宁的声音
    据新华社24日讯　全国听众即将听到伟大革命导师列宁的声音。中央人民广播电台将在十月革命节的前后向全国广播列宁“告红军书”、“什么是苏维埃政权”、“怎样使劳动人民永远摆脱地主资本家的压迫”等五篇讲话的录音。另外，在此期间听众还将从广播听到中苏友好协会总会副会长林伯渠、郭沫若，中共中央高级党校校长杨献珍，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华上将等为庆祝十月革命四十周年所作的广播讲话。中央人民广播电台并邀请苏联驻我国大使尤金院士在11月7日晚间为庆祝十月革命四十周年向我国听众作广播讲话。苏联文化代表团团长柯切托夫也应邀为庆祝伟大十月革命四十周年将向中国听众作广播讲话。
    中央人民广播电台为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年，举办了一系列政治、文学、音乐等庆祝节目。除了转播首都各界庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年大会实况以外，节日晚间，还将报道北京和各大城市的庆祝活动，广播莫斯科红场当天举行盛大检阅的实况。
    音乐广播，除现正举办连续性音乐节目，系统地介绍苏联各加盟共和国的音乐作品，并在每天广播苏联现代作曲家的器乐和声乐作品，和歌剧剪辑、舞剧选曲等以外，我国各音乐团体、演奏家、歌唱家还将为庆祝十月革命四十周年陆续举办多次广播音乐会。节日晚间，电台广播乐团举办的广播音乐会，将演唱中国音乐家新创作的大型合唱曲“万岁，伟大的友谊！”和苏联作曲家的作品。
    在文学广播方面，在十月中旬即已陆续介绍并朗诵苏联现代作家的文学作品，用配乐朗诵等形式广播“毁灭”、“被开垦的处女地”、“钢铁是怎样炼成的”、“时间呀，前进！”等长篇小说的片断，和其他作家的短篇小说、诗歌，以及“苏联电影周”影片的录音剪辑、苏联名剧“决裂”、“带枪的人”，广播剧“走向新岸”和“革命前夜”等。
    在“国际生活”、“国际时事”、“对部队广播”、“对少年儿童广播”、“科学常识”和“星期演讲会”等节目中，都已开始播送有关中苏友谊和介绍苏联各方面伟大成就的文章和报道。
