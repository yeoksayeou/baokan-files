Title: Fifth Anniversary of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement: Premier Zhou Enlai and Chairman Tsedenbal Exchange Congratulatory Telegrams
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram today to the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Yu. Tsedenbal, to congratulate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The telegram reads as follows:
Comrade Yu. Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic:

　　On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to you, the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic, and the Mongolian people.

　　Over the past five years, the close economic and cultural cooperation between China and Mongolia has brought tremendous and practical benefits to the socialist construction of our two countries, and has been beneficial to the great unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and beneficial to peace in Asia and the world.

　　I wish the fraternal and unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian peoples will continue to develop. I wish the Mongolian People's Republic prosperity and strength.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 3, 1957, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Yu. Tsedenbal, sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai to congratulate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows:

Beijing, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China,

Dear Premier:

　　On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Mongolian-Chinese Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement, please allow me, on behalf of the Mongolian people, the Mongolian government, and in my personal name, to extend warm greetings and sincere congratulations to you, the government of the People's Republic of China, and the great Chinese people.

　　This agreement has opened a new chapter in the history of friendly and mutual relations between our two countries.

　　The characteristic of the five years since the agreement came into effect is the increasing strengthening of economic and cultural cooperation between our two countries. The continuous increase in assistance from the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and other people's democratic countries to our country is a guarantee for the Mongolian People's Republic to achieve new victories in socialist construction.

　　While solemnly celebrating this great festival, the Mongolian people extend their heartfelt respect and best wishes to the great Chinese people, and express their sincere gratitude for the fraternal support and assistance of the Chinese people.

　　Please allow me to sincerely wish you, the government of the People's Republic of China, and the great Chinese people great victories in the struggle for the new prosperity of your motherland and world peace.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Yu. Tsedenbal
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 3, 1957, Ulaanbaatar

### 中蒙经济文化合作协定五周年  周恩来总理和泽登巴尔主席互致贺电

1957-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中蒙经济文化合作协定五周年
    周恩来总理和泽登巴尔主席互致贺电
    新华社3日讯  周恩来总理今天打电报给蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席尤·泽登巴尔，祝贺中蒙经济及文化合作协定签订五周年。电文如下：蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席尤·泽登巴尔同志：
    欣逢中蒙经济及文化合作协定签订五周年，谨向你，向蒙古人民共和国政府和蒙古人民致热诚的祝贺。
    五年来，中蒙两国在经济和文化方面的密切合作，已经给我们两国的社会主义建设事业带来了巨大的、实际的利益，并且有益于以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的伟大团结，有益于亚洲和世界的和平。
    祝蒙中两国人民兄弟般的牢不可破的友谊日益发展。祝蒙古人民共和国繁荣、昌盛。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来
                        1957年10月3日于北京
    新华社3日讯  蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席尤·泽登巴尔致电周恩来总理祝贺中蒙经济及文化合作协定签订五周年。贺电全文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志亲爱的总理同志：
    当蒙中经济及文化合作协定签订五周年的时候，请允许我代表蒙古人民、蒙古政府并以我个人的名义，向你、中华人民共和国政府和伟大的中国人民致热烈的敬礼和衷心的祝贺。
    这个协定在我们两国友好的相互关系史上开辟了新的一页。
    协定生效后五年来这个时期的特点是我们两国之间的经济文化合作日益加强。苏联、中华人民共和国和其他各人民民主国家对我国的不断增加的帮助，乃是蒙古人民共和国建设社会主义取得新胜利的保证。
    蒙古人民在隆重庆祝这一重大节日的时候，向伟大的中国人民致衷心的敬意和最良好的愿望，并且对中国人民的兄弟般的支持和帮助表示诚挚的谢意。
    请允许我衷心地祝你、中华人民共和国政府和伟大的中国人民在为自己祖国新的繁荣和世界和平的斗争中取得巨大的胜利。
                蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席  尤·泽登巴尔
                                    1957年10月3日于乌兰巴托
