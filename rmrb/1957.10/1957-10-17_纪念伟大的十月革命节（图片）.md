Title: Commemorating the Great October Revolution Festival (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1957-10-17
Page: 3

　　Commemorating the Great October Revolution Festival
　　Long Live World Peace!
　　The Beijing People's Art Theatre is intensifying rehearsals for the Soviet drama "The Man with a Gun." This is a scene from the play. On the left is Lenin (played by Diao Guangqin), and on the right is Ivan Shadrin (played by Tian Chong).　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Lin Hui photo
　　Lang Shao'an, a veteran folk artist in the capital famous for kneading dough figurines, and her daughter Lang Zhili are designing figure images reflecting the enthusiastic celebration of the October Revolution Festival by the people of China and the Soviet Union.　　　　　　Feng Wengang photo (Xinhua News Agency draft)
　　The National Beijing Library has specially commissioned Shanghai velvet embroidery artists Liu Peizhen and her sisters to embroider two panoramic views of the Beijing Library to be presented to the Soviet Union as a festival gift. The picture shows the Liu Peizhen sisters looking at the finished works.　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Shenming photo
　　Shanghai printing workers are sorting out the printed picture cards.
　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Putao photo

### 纪念伟大的十月革命节（图片）

1957-10-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　纪念伟大的十月革命节
    世界和平万岁！
    北京人民艺术剧院正在加紧排练苏联话剧“带枪的人”。这是剧中的一个镜头。左是列宁（刁光覃饰），右是伊万·雪特林（田冲饰）。　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　林慧摄
    以捏制面人闻名的首都老艺人郎绍安和她的女儿郎志丽在设计反映中苏两国人民热烈庆祝十月革命节的人物形象。　　　　　　冯文冈摄（新华社稿）
    国立北京图书馆特请上海绒绣艺人刘佩珍和她的姊妹绒绣了二幅北京图书馆全景，准备赠送给苏联，作为节日的礼物。图为刘佩珍姊妹们在看已经绣好的作品。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　张申明摄
　　上海印刷工人正在整理印好的画片。
　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　杨溥涛摄
