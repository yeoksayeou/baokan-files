Title: CCP and BCP Leaders Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th – Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party and the Bulgarian Communist Party held talks in Beijing today.

　　The Chinese Communist Party representatives participating in the talks included Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the CCP Central Committee; Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary; Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat; and Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat.

　　The Bulgarian Communist Party representatives participating in the talks included Vulko Chervenkov, member of the BCP Central Committee's Politburo; Ganev, member of the Politburo and Secretary of the Central Committee; Taskov, member of the Politburo; and Lukanov, member of the Central Committee.

### 中共和保共领导人举行会谈

1957-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共和保共领导人举行会谈
    新华社11日讯　中国共产党和保加利亚共产党的领导人今天在北京举行了会谈。
    中国共产党方面参加会谈的，有中共中央副主席刘少奇，总书记邓小平，政治局委员、书记处书记彭真，书记处书记王稼祥。
    保加利亚共产党方面参加会谈的，有保共中央政治局委员于哥夫，政治局委员、中央委员会书记加涅夫，政治局委员塔斯科夫，中央委员会委员卢卡诺夫。
