Title: Bulgarian Delegation Departs Guangzhou for Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1957-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:

    The Bulgarian government delegation led by Bulgarian People's Republic Minister Council Chairman, Yugov, arrived in Guangzhou on the 5th. The distinguished guests visited the Memorial Hall of the Peasant Movement Training Institute, which was presided over by Chairman Mao Zedong in his early years, and laid a wreath at the Guangzhou Commune Martyrs Cemetery. They also visited the China Export Commodities Fair, which is about to open.

    In the evening, Yin Linping, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, hosted a banquet to welcome the Bulgarian government delegation.

    At 8:00 AM on the 6th, the delegation left Guangzhou by special plane for the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and arrived in Hanoi on the same day. They were invited to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for three days.

### 保代表团离广州到河内

1957-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    保代表团离广州到河内
    据新华社讯：保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席于哥夫率领的保加利亚政府代表团，在5日到达广州。贵宾们在广州参观了毛泽东主席当年主持的农民讲习所纪念馆，在广州公社烈士陵园献了花圈；参观了即将开幕的中国出口商品交易会。
    当晚，广东省副省长尹林平设宴欢迎保加利亚政府代表团。
    6日上午八时，代表团乘专机离开广州前往越南民主共和国，当日即到达河内。他们应邀到越南民主共和国进行三天的访问。
