Title: Beijing Holds First Report Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Beijing held its first report meeting today to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

　　This report meeting was organized by the Propaganda Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China. More than 1,500 propaganda cadres participated in the meeting. The speaker was Yao Zhen from the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. In his report, he explained the great international significance of the October Socialist Revolution, the great achievements of socialist construction in the Soviet Union, the contributions of the Soviet Union and socialist countries to world peace, the importance of learning from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union and advancing along the path of the October Revolution, and the need to strengthen proletarian international unity and strengthen Sino-Soviet friendship and unity.

　　Yang Shu, Zhang Dazhong and other responsible persons of the Propaganda Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Preparatory Committee of Beijing for celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the October Revolution, also spoke at the meeting on the issue of commemorating the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.

### 北京市举行第一次报告会

1957-10-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京市举行第一次报告会
    新华社14日讯　北京市今天举行了庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年的第一次报告会。
    这个报告会是由中共北京市委宣传部举办的。一千五百多名宣传工作干部参加了这个报告会。报告人是中共中央宣传部姚溱。他在报告中说明了十月社会主义革命的伟大国际意义，苏联社会主义建设的伟大成就，苏联和社会主义各国对于世界和平的贡献，学习苏联先进经验、沿着十月革命的道路前进，以及加强无产阶级国际团结和加强中苏友好团结等问题。
    中共北京市委宣传部和北京市庆祝十月革命四十周年的筹委会负责人杨述、张大中等，也在会上就纪念十月革命四十周年问题讲了话。
