Title: Indian Teachers Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 25th. The Indian Teachers Delegation of the All India Federation of Educational Associations, headed by Mrs. Rashirkal, left Kunming by plane on the morning of the 25th to return to their country, after a four-week visit to China.

### 印度教师代表团回国

1957-10-28
第3版()
专栏：

    印度教师代表团回国
    据新华社昆明25日电　以拉希尔卡尔夫人为首的全印教育协会联合会教师代表团一行六人，结束了对中国为时四周的访问，25日上午乘飞机离开昆明回国。
