Title: Fierlinger Returns to Prague
Author:
Date: 1957-10-21
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 20th: Chairman Fierlinger, head of the Czechoslovak National Assembly delegation, and his wife returned to Prague by plane on the afternoon of the 19th after visiting China.
    First Deputy Minister of the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Gregor, and Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 费林格回到布拉格

1957-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    费林格回到布拉格
    新华社布拉格20日讯  捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团团长费林格主席和他的夫人在访问中国以后，19日下午乘飞机回到布拉格。
    捷克斯洛伐克外交部第一副部长格里高尔和中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛等都到机场欢迎。
