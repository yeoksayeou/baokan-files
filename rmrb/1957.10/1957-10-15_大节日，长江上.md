Title: Great Festival on the Yangtze River
Author: Xu Chi
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 8

Great Festival on the Yangtze River
　　Xu Chi
　　I.

　　At this point on our country's map,
　　The place where the Yangtze flows,
　　Yellow Crane Tower at Yellow Crane Rock on the right bank,
　　Clear River Pavilion and Parrot Isle on the left bank.
　　How much history is recorded,
　　How many famous battles.
　　Coming here to drink and compose poems,
　　How many heroes and outstanding figures.
　　A great mind,
　　One day also came here,
　　Looking down at the rushing river,
　　Gazing at the moving sails.
　　Before his eyes, the three-legged Wuhan,
　　The quiet Tortoise Mountain and Snake Mountain,
　　The railway line running through north and south,
　　He asked: "Where has the Yellow Crane gone?"
　　He was very young then,
　　Still exploring the voice of truth,
　　That was in 1918, the dawn was just breaking,
　　The sleeping nation began to awaken.
　　He stood under the misty rain,
　　Thinking about the way out for the motherland,
　　In the deep darkness,
　　He questioned the future of the motherland.
　　"Pour wine on the surging waves,
　　The heart's tide rises with the waves,"
　　Mao Zedong sang like this,
　　Ferrying across the Yangtze River.

　　II.
　　After crossing the river northward, he heard
　　The sound of the October Revolution,
　　The wisdom of Marx and Lenin
　　Lit the torch of the Chinese revolution.
　　The torch was lit on the Jinggang Mountains,
　　On the Long March,
　　Beside the Yan River in northern Shaanxi,
　　And the radiance shone throughout the country.
　　The armed people fought for thirty years,
　　Established a new type of republic,
　　In this thunderous celebration of the great festival,
　　The Yangtze River shows a magnificent scene.

　　III.
　　The place where the Yangtze River flows,
　　In the Yellow Crane Tower and Clear River Pavilion,
　　Above Tortoise Mountain and Snake Mountain,
　　There is a precious belt across the sky.
　　A magnificent and beautiful belt,
　　An embodiment of peace and friendship,
　　A triumphal arch of the Five-Year Plan,
　　A milestone of socialism.
　　The seven-colored rainbow of socialism,
　　An ideal bridge,
　　Crossing the Yangtze River,
　　Turning this natural barrier into a thoroughfare.
　　The land of pears and the land of lychees
　　Are connected into one, with unimpeded passage,
　　From Berlin to Hanoi,
　　Brothers and sisters hold hands in friendship.

　　IV.
　　Ah, noble bridge,
　　Bridge of marble and steel,
　　Bridge of advanced technology,
　　Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge.
　　Since the Communist Party started the grand plan,
　　The bridge has been conceived in the blueprint,
　　The model shows the shape of the bridge,
　　The construction army has come to the construction site.
　　（That is my happiness,
　　I saw this bridge,
　　Under the water, on the water, above the water,
　　Growing and forming day by day.）
　　Ah! We can still feel
　　That after the start,
　　The earth-shattering
　　Power of the pile driver on both sides.
　　Ah! We can still hear
　　That large drill's cross drill bit
　　Heavily hammering the river bottom
　　The "bite! bite! bite!" of the bedrock.
　　Ah! We can still see
　　The beautiful scenery on the bridge construction site,
　　The lights are bright at night,
　　Like the Milky Way falling to the human world.
　　I once walked down the cofferdam to the bottom of the river,
　　As if walking into the Dragon King's palace,
　　I also climbed to the top of the highest steel beam,
　　I really went to the clouds to overlook Wuhan.
　　I know we have strength,
　　But I didn't know we had such great strength,
　　I know we have a wonderful future,
　　I didn't know it was already so wonderful.

　　V.
　　Great festival, on the Yangtze River,
　　Thunderous cheers,
　　The great project is completed,
　　Look, long bridge, traffic flow.
　　Flying across the Yangtze River,
　　It only takes a minute,
　　River waves, car sounds, whistles,
　　A song of praise.
　　Our ideals, sung in
　　The great festival, on the Yangtze River.
　　But our hearts fly again,
　　Flying over the Xiling Gorge to Nanjin Pass.

### 大节日，长江上
徐迟
1957-10-15
第8版()
专栏：

　　大节日，长江上
　　徐迟
　　一
　　在我国地图这一点上，
　　长江流过的这个地方，
　　右岸黄鹄矶头黄鹤楼，
　　左岸晴川阁、鹦鹉洲。
　　多少的历史记载，
　　多少的著名战役。
　　来这里饮酒赋诗，
　　有多少英雄豪杰。
　　一个伟大的心灵，
　　一天也来到了这里，
　　俯视大江奔流，
　　凝望移动的风篷。
　　眼底是三鼎足的武汉，
　　静静的龟山蛇山，
　　贯穿南北的铁路线，
　　他问：“黄鹤知何去？”
　　他那时正十分年轻，
　　还在探索真理的声音，
　　那时是1918年曙光初透，
　　沉睡的民族开始在觉醒。
　　他站在苍茫的烟雨下，
　　思索祖国的出路，
　　在沉沉的黑暗中，
　　他讯问祖国的前途。
　　“把酒酹滔滔，
　　心潮逐浪高，”
　　毛泽东这样歌唱，
　　摆渡过了长江。
　　二
　　渡江北上后，他听到了
　　十月革命的炮声，
　　马克思列宁的智慧之火
　　点亮了中国革命的火把。
　　火把点亮在井冈山上，
　　长征道上，
　　陕北延河之傍，
　　而光耀照遍了全国。
　　武装的人民战斗了三十年，
　　建立起新型的共和国，
　　在这欢声雷动的大节日，
　　长江上显出壮观的景色。
　　三
　　长江流过的这个地方，
　　在黄鹤楼晴川阁之中，
　　龟山蛇山之上，
　　有一条宝带横空。
　　雄伟而美丽的宝带，
　　和平和友谊的化身，
　　五年计划的凯旋门，
　　社会主义的里程碑。
　　社会主义的七色长虹，
　　一座理想的桥，
　　跨过长江去，
　　使这天堑变通途。
　　梨子的土地和荔枝的土地
　　连成一片，通行无阻，
　　从柏林，到河内，
　　众兄弟拉起了友谊的手。
　　四
　　啊，高贵的桥，
　　大理石和钢铁的桥，
　　先进技术的桥，
　　武汉长江大桥。
　　自从共产党启宏图，
　　桥就被孕育在蓝图上，
　　模型上显出桥的外形，
　　工地上开来了建设大军。
    （那是我的幸福，
　　我看到了这座桥，
　　在水下、水面、水上
　　一天天生长，形成。）
　　啊！我们现在还能感到
　　那一开动之后，
　　两岸为之山崩地裂的
　　震动打桩机的威力。
　　啊！我们现在还能听到
　　那大型钻机的十字钻头
　　沉重地锤击江底
　　岩盘的“啃！啃！啃！”
　　啊！我们现在还能看到
　　那桥梁工地上的美景，
　　入夜灯火辉煌，
　　好比银河掉落到人间。
　　我曾走下围堰去直到江底，
  　好比走到了海龙王的龙宫里，
　　也曾攀登到最高的钢梁顶上，
　　算真到了云霄里把武汉眺望。
　　我知道我们是有力量的，
　　却不知道我们力量这样大，
　　我知道我们有美妙的未来，
　　不知道如今已经这般美妙。
　　五
　　大节日，长江上，
　　欢声雷动，
　　大工程大功告成，
　　看，长桥、车水、马龙。
　　飞驰过长江，
　　只消一分钟，
　　江涛、车声、汽笛、
　　一片歌唱声。
　　我们的理想，歌唱在
　　大节日，长江上。
　　但我们的心又飞去了，
　　飞翔在西陵峡南津关。
