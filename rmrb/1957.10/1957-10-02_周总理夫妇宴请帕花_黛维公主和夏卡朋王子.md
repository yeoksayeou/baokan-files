Title: Premier Zhou and his Wife Host Princess Norodom Phalla Devi and Prince Norodom Sihanouk
Author:
Date: 1957-10-02
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 1st October. Premier Zhou Enlai and his wife, Deng Yingchao, invited Cambodian Princess Norodom Phalla Devi and Prince Norodom Sihanouk to dinner tonight.

Also invited were Fang Bingzhen, head of the Cambodian Cultural and Art Delegation, Yang An, head of the Cambodian Royal Economic Delegation, and his wife, as well as Madame Ta Pe and Madame Ying Yang, professors of the Cambodian Royal Palace Dance Troupe.

Vice Premier of the State Council He Long and his wife attended as guests.

Before dinner, Deng Yingchao accompanied Princess Phalla Devi and Prince Sihanouk on a visit to Zhongnanhai.

### 周总理夫妇宴请帕花·黛维公主和夏卡朋王子

1957-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理夫妇宴请帕花·黛维公主和夏卡朋王子
    新华社1日讯  周恩来总理和夫人邓颖超今晚邀请柬埔寨诺罗敦·帕花·黛维公主和诺罗敦·夏卡朋王子共进晚餐。
    应邀出席的还有柬埔寨文化艺术代表团团长方秉祯，柬埔寨王国经济代表团团长杨安和他的夫人以及柬埔寨王宫舞蹈团教授达培夫人、英扬夫人。
    出席作陪的有国务院副总理贺龙和他的夫人。
    在晚餐以前，邓颖超陪同帕花·黛维公主和夏卡朋王子参观了中南海。
