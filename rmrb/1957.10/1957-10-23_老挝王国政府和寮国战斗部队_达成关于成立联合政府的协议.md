Title: Agreement Reached Between the Royal Lao Government and the Lao Fighting Units on the Establishment of a Coalition Government
Author:
Date: 1957-10-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 22nd -- According to a broadcast from Vientiane Radio on the evening of the 22nd, the political consultative conference between the Royal Lao Government and the Lao Fighting Units announced in a communique on the 22nd that both sides have reached an agreement on the establishment of a coalition government and on the integration of the Lao Fighting Units' troops and cadres in Phong Saly and Sam Neua provinces into national collective life.

    The communique stated that the political delegations from both sides believe that the signing of this agreement is a great victory, which is in line with the wishes of all Lao people and helps to unify Laos by peaceful means.

### 老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队  达成关于成立联合政府的协议

1957-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队
    达成关于成立联合政府的协议
    新华社河内22日电　据万象电台22日晚上广播，老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队双方政治协商会议22日发表公报说，双方已经就关于成立联合政府、关于寮国战斗部队在丰沙里和桑怒两省的军队和干部并入国家集体生活的问题达成协议。
    公报说，双方政治代表团认为，这个协议的签订是一个巨大的胜利，它符合全体老挝人民的愿望，有助于以和平方法来统一老挝。
