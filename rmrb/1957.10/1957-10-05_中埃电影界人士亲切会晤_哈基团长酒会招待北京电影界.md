Title: Friendly Meeting Between Chinese and Egyptian Film Circles; Chairman Haqi Hosts Reception for Beijing Film Circles
Author:
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch: Egyptian film delegation leader, Yahya Haqi, hosted a reception in Beijing this evening for prominent figures from Beijing's film and art circles.

    In his speech at the reception, Haqi, on behalf of the Egyptian film delegation, expressed his gratitude to the Chinese government for its strong support of the Egyptian Film Week and to the Chinese film industry for its warm hospitality. Haqi said that the holding of Egyptian Film Week in China and the Egyptian film delegation's visit to China have advanced cultural exchange between the two countries another step forward. He wished that cultural exchange and friendly cooperation between the two countries would further develop in the future.

    Wang Lanxi, Assistant Minister of Culture and Director of the Film Bureau, also spoke at the reception. He said that the Egyptian Film Week has achieved great success. In the four days since the start of Beijing Film Week, nearly 200,000 Chinese viewers have seen Egyptian films. The audience learned about the lives of the Egyptian people and appreciated the excellent Egyptian film art from the films. This film week has greatly strengthened the friendship between the Chinese and Egyptian people.

    Egyptian famous film actors Faten Hamama and Omar Sharif raised their glasses to thank their Chinese counterparts at the reception. Hamama said that she had made many dear friends in China during this visit, and this visit was the happiest moment for her.

### 中埃电影界人士亲切会晤  哈基团长酒会招待北京电影界

1957-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    中埃电影界人士亲切会晤
    哈基团长酒会招待北京电影界
    据新华社3日讯　埃及电影代表团团长耶雅·哈基今天晚上在北京举行酒会，招待北京电影界和文艺界著名人士。
    哈基在酒会上致词，他代表埃及电影代表团对中国政府给予埃及电影周工作的大力支持和中国电影界的热情招待表示感谢。哈基说，埃及电影周在中国举行和埃及电影代表团访问中国，使得两国间的文化交流又向前迈进了一步。他预祝两国间的文化交流和友好合作今后更加发展。
    文化部部长助理、电影事业管理局局长王阑西也在酒会上讲了话。他说，埃及电影周已经取得了很大的成功，在北京电影周开始以后的四天当中，即有将近二十万中国观众看到了埃及影片。观众从影片中了解到埃及人民的生活，欣赏了埃及优秀的电影艺术。这次电影周大大加强了中埃人民的友谊。
    埃及著名电影演员法登·哈玛玛和奥马·薛里夫在酒会上向中国同行们举杯致谢，哈玛玛说，她这次在中国结识了很多亲爱的朋友，这次访问是使她感到最幸福的时刻。
