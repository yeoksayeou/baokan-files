Title: Japanese Delegation of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations Departs Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1957-10-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 29th. The delegation of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, headed by Kazami Akira, Director of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, and Obata Chūryō, concluded its visit to our country and left Guangzhou by train this morning to return home. Wang Bingzhang, Secretary of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, accompanied the guests to Shenzhen.

Du Guoxiang, Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Diancun, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Xiao Xuiying, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and the heads of the Guangzhou Federation of Trade Unions and the Women's Federation all went to the station to see the guests off.
