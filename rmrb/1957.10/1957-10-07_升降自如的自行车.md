Title: Adjustable Height Bicycle
Author: Tian Ming
Date: 1957-10-07
Page: 4

Column: New Products

    Adjustable Height Bicycle
    The Tianjin Bicycle Factory has trial-produced a lightweight touring bicycle that can be lowered and raised, and can be used by both men and women from the age of fourteen to adulthood.
            Xinhua News Agency reporter Tian Ming photographed.
