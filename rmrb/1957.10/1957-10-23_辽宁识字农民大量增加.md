Title: Liaoning Sees a Large Increase in Literate Peasants
Author:
Date: 1957-10-23
Page: 8

There has been a large increase in the number of literate peasants in Liaoning Province, with 1,010,000 illiterate people in rural areas having become literate peasants. In areas such as Liaoyang and Andong, according to recent surveys, there are already nine villages without illiteracy, fifty agricultural cooperatives without illiteracy, and more than three hundred and eighty production teams without illiteracy. There are currently more than 10,000 small libraries in rural areas throughout the province.

### 辽宁识字农民大量增加

1957-10-23
第8版()
专栏：

    辽宁识字农民大量增加
    辽宁省农村中已经有一百零一万文盲变成识字的农民。在辽阳、安东等地，据最近调查，已经出现九个无文盲乡，五十个无文盲农业社，三百八十多个无文盲生产队。全省农村目前已有一万多所小型图书室。
