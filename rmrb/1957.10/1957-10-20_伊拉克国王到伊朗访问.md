Title: Iraqi King Visits Iran
Author:
Date: 1957-10-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, 19th - Iraqi King Faisal arrived in Iran by plane on October 18th for a state visit. Faisal was accompanied by Crown Prince Abdullah Ira and Prime Minister Jawdat al-Ayoubi.

According to the Associated Press on October 18th, citing Iraqi palace sources, Faisal and the Iranian King will discuss the Middle East crisis and ways to strengthen the Baghdad Pact.

### 伊拉克国王到伊朗访问

1957-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克国王到伊朗访问
    新华社19日讯  德黑兰消息：伊拉克国王费萨尔10月18日乘飞机到达伊朗进行国事访问。陪同费萨尔访问的有王储阿卜杜拉·伊拉和首相乔达特·阿尤比。
    据美联社10月18日援引伊拉克宫廷人士的话说，费萨尔和伊朗国王将讨论中东危机和加强巴格达条约的方法。
