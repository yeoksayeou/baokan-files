Title: Soviet Artificial Satellite Flies into Space, American Aircraft Stocks Soar

Author:

Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 8th - The news of the successful launch of the first Soviet artificial satellite on the 7th caused the stock prices of some aircraft companies in Wall Street related to missile manufacturing to soar. The reason for the increase is that Wall Street speculators believe that the U.S. government will increase its appropriations for missile development in order to catch up with the Soviet Union in the development of artificial satellites and missiles.

### 苏联人造卫星飞上天  美国飞机股票猛涨价

1957-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联人造卫星飞上天
    美国飞机股票猛涨价
    据新华社8日讯　纽约消息：苏联第一个人造卫星发射成功的消息7日使华尔街的一些同制造导弹有关的飞机公司股票价格猛涨。上涨原因是华尔街的投机家认为美国政府为了要在人造卫星以及导弹发展方面赶上苏联，将会增加在发展导弹方面的拨款。
