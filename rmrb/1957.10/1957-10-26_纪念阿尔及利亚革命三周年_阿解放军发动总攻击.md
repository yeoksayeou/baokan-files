Title: Commemorating the Third Anniversary of the Algerian Revolution: Algerian Liberation Army Launches General Offensive
Author:
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th dispatch from Cairo: The Algerian "National Liberation Front" issued a statement on the 24th, saying that to commemorate the third anniversary of the Algerian Revolution, the Algerian National Liberation Army has launched a general offensive from the 20th of this month in the vast areas of Algeria, from the coastline in the north to the border of the Sahara in the south.

The statement said that the general offensive has achieved great victories so far and is continuing. The National Liberation Army has destroyed a number of French military strongholds. The number of French soldiers deserting is increasing.

### 纪念阿尔及利亚革命三周年  阿解放军发动总攻击

1957-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    纪念阿尔及利亚革命三周年
    阿解放军发动总攻击
    新华社25日讯　开罗消息：阿尔及利亚“民族解放阵线”24日发表公告说，为了纪念阿尔及利亚革命三周年，阿尔及利亚民族解放军已经从本月20日起在阿尔及利亚全境北起海岸线南到撒哈拉边境为止的广大地区内发动总攻击。
    公告说，到目前为止，这次总攻击已经取得了巨大胜利，并且正在继续进行中。民族解放军已经摧毁了一批法国军事据点。法军士兵逃亡人数日益增多。
