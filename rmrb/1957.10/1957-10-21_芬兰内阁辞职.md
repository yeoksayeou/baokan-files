Title: Finnish Cabinet Resigns
Author:
Date: 1957-10-21
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 19th: According to TASS, Finnish Prime Minister Sukselainen submitted his cabinet's resignation to Finnish President Kekkonen on the evening of October 18th. In a vote of confidence held in the Finnish Parliament on the 18th, Parliament expressed its distrust of the government by a vote of seventy-five to seventy-four, with forty-two abstentions.
    Kekkonen has accepted Sukselainen's resignation.

### 芬兰内阁辞职

1957-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    芬兰内阁辞职
    据新华社19日讯  据塔斯社赫尔辛基消息：芬兰总理苏克西莱宁10月18日晚间向芬兰总统吉科宁提出了内阁辞呈。芬兰议会18日举行的信任投票中，议会以七十五票对七十四票、四十二票弃权，对政府表示不信任。
    吉科宁已经接受了苏克西莱宁的辞呈。
