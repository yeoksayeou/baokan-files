Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Visits West Lake
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 4th October. The Bulgarian Government Delegation, led by Chairman Yugov, today visited the scenic West Lake in Hangzhou with great interest.

This morning, the sky was overcast and threatening rain, and the lake was shrouded in a thin mist. The distinguished guests boated on the lake and toured the Three Pools Mirroring the Moon. The guests also visited West Hill Park, Tiger Spring, and the Qiantang River Bridge. Osmanthus flowers were in full bloom in West Hill Park, filling the air with fragrance, and the guests lingered under the osmanthus trees, reluctant to leave.

In the afternoon, the distinguished guests visited the Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory. The workers of this factory presented the delegation with a silk-woven portrait of Dimitrov. Finally, the guests visited famous historical sites such as Lingyin Temple, Jade Spring, and Yue Fei Temple.

In the evening, the Zhejiang Provincial People's Committee held a reception, with acrobatic performances and Yue Opera to entertain the distinguished guests.

### 保政府代表团游览西湖

1957-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    保政府代表团游览西湖
    新华社杭州4日电　于哥夫主席率领的保加利亚政府代表团，今天在杭州以极大的兴趣游览了西湖名胜。
    今天上午，天阴欲雨，湖上笼罩着一层薄雾。贵宾们泛舟湖上，游览了三潭印月。贵宾们还游览了西山公园、虎跑和钱江大桥。西山公园中桂花盛开，香气扑鼻，客人们留连桂花树下不忍离去。
    下午，贵宾们参观了都锦生丝织厂。这个厂的工人以丝织的季米特洛夫像赠送给代表团。贵宾们最后又游览了灵隐寺、玉泉和岳坟等名胜古迹。
    晚间，浙江省人民委员会举行招待晚会，演出杂技、越剧招待贵宾们。
