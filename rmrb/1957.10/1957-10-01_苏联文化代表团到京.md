Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 30th - At the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the entire Soviet Cultural Delegation, led by the head of the delegation, Kochetov, editor-in-chief of the Soviet "Literary Gazette" and writer, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

The delegation consists of eleven members, including Kazakbayev, Minister of Culture of the Kyrgyz Soviet Socialist Republic and representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Kyrgyz Republic; Manizer, Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Fine Arts, full academician, sculptor, and People's Artist of the Russian Federation; Zhukov, People's Artist of the Russian Federation and corresponding academician of the Soviet Academy of Fine Arts; Zhiganov, President of the Kazan State Conservatory, representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, and composer; Osipova, Principal of Moscow No. 243 School and Honored Teacher of the Russian Federation; Troyan, physician at Moscow First Medical Institute and Hero of the Soviet Union; Lyukshin, Chairman of the Stalin Collective Farm in the Vinogradov District of the Moscow Region and Hero of Socialist Labor; Tank, poet of the Belorussian Republic and representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Belorussian Republic; Dobrynina, professor and president of the Moscow Pharmaceutical Institute; and the delegation's secretary is Brov, director of research at the Department of Oriental People's Republics of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 苏联文化代表团到京

1957-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联文化代表团到京
    据新华社30日讯  应中苏友好协会总会邀请来中国访问的苏联文化代表团全体人员在团长、苏联“文学报”总编辑、作家柯切托夫率领下，今天下午坐飞机到达北京。
    代表团由十一人组成，团员有吉尔吉斯苏维埃共和国文化部部长、吉尔吉斯共和国最高苏维埃代表卡扎克巴耶夫，苏联美术研究院副院长、正式院士、雕刻家、俄罗斯联邦共和国人民艺术家马尼泽尔，俄罗斯联邦共和国人民艺术家、苏联美术研究院通讯院士茹可夫，国立喀山音乐学院院长、俄罗斯联邦共和国最高苏维埃代表、作曲家日干诺夫，莫斯科二四三学校校长、俄罗斯联邦共和国功勋教师奥希波娃，莫斯科第一医学院医师、苏联英雄特洛扬，莫斯科州维诺格拉多夫区斯大林集体农庄主席、社会主义劳动英雄柳克申，白俄罗斯共和国诗人、白俄罗斯共和国最高苏维埃代表唐克，教授、莫斯科制药学院院长多布雷尼娜，代表团的秘书是苏联对外文化协会东方人民共和国部主任研究员布罗夫。
