Title: Syrian Newspapers Value Chairman Mao's Telegram, Consider the Support of the Chinese People to Have Great Significance
Author:
Date: 1957-10-20
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 19th - All newspapers here today published Chairman Mao Zedong's telegram to Syrian President Kuwatly in prominent positions on their front pages, as well as the news of the Chinese government's support for Syria against the American conspiracy of aggression.

　　"Alif Baa" newspaper published an editorial on China's support for Syria entitled "Missiles from China," saying that the United States should know that 600 million people are watching us. "Under these circumstances, the support of the Chinese people is a serious event that deserves great attention and evaluation."

　　The newspaper pointed out, "This clearly proves that we are not isolated in our battle for honor and dignity. In addition to all the Arab people, we have many friends around the world to rely on to repel any danger."

### 叙报重视毛主席的电报  认为中国人民的支持有重大意义

1957-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　叙报重视毛主席的电报
    认为中国人民的支持有重大意义
    新华社大马士革19日电  这里所有报纸今天都以首版显著地位刊载了毛泽东主席给叙利亚总统库阿特利的电报，以及中国政府支持叙利亚反对美国侵略阴谋的消息。
    “阿利夫巴报”就中国对叙利亚的支持发表题为“来自中国的导弹”的社论说，美国应该知道，六亿人民正在注视着我们。“在这种情况下，中国人民的支持是一件值得给予重大注意和估价的严重事件”。
    这家报纸指出，“这清楚地证明，在我们为荣誉和尊严而斗争的战斗中，我们并不是孤立的，除了所有阿拉伯人民之外，我们在全世界还有许多朋友可以倚靠来打退任何危险。”
