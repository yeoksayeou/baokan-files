Title: Egyptian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th. The eight-member Egyptian Cultural Delegation, led by Dr. El-Sayed Mustafa Said, President of Alexandria University, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 27th. The delegation is visiting China in accordance with the 1957 executive plan of the Sino-Egyptian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

The members of the delegation are: Ahmed Najib Hashim, State Secretary of the Egyptian Ministry of Education; Abdul Wahid Ali, Director of the Teacher Training Department of the Ministry of Education; Abdul Meguid Shdid, Director of the Office of the Minister of Education; Mahmoud El Hakim, Professor of Architecture at Alexandria University; Said Hamed El Shat, Vice President of the Institute of Applied Arts; Mahmoud El Badawi, writer; Hilmi Murad Ghandi, writer.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport today included Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture; Dong Chuncai, Vice Minister of Education; Bao Erhan, President of the Sino-Egyptian Friendship Association; Ma Yu Huai and Zhang Jie, Vice Presidents of the China Islamic Association; writers Xiao San and Yang Shuo, as well as responsible persons from relevant departments and institutions of higher learning.

Egyptian Ambassador to China, Ragab, and embassy staff also came to welcome the delegation. (Attached photo)

### 埃及文化代表团到北京

1957-10-28
第3版()
专栏：

    埃及文化代表团到北京
    新华社27日讯　埃及文化代表团一行八人在团长、亚历山大大学校长爱尔赛德·穆斯塔法·赛义德博士率领下，27日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团是根据中埃文化合作协定1957年执行计划来中国进行友好访问的。
    代表团的团员是：埃及教育部国务秘书阿哈迈德·纳吉布·何兴，教育部教师训练司司长阿卜杜尔·瓦赫德·阿里，教育部部长办公室主任阿卜杜尔·梅吉德·师迪德，亚历山大大学建筑系教授玛哈穆德·爱尔·哈基姆，实用艺术学院副院长赛义德·哈迈德·爱尔·沙特，作家玛赫穆德·爱尔·巴达威，作家希莱米·穆拉德·刚地。
    今天到飞机场欢迎代表团的有文化部副部长夏衍，教育部副部长董纯才，中埃友好协会会长包尔汉，中国伊斯兰教协会副会长马玉槐、张杰，作家萧三、杨朔以及有关部门和高等院校的负责人等。
    埃及驻华大使拉加卜及使馆人员也来欢迎代表团。（附图片）
