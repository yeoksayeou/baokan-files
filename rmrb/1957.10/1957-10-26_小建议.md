Title: Small Suggestion
Author: Zhu Muhao
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 8
Column: Street Talk and Alley Discussions

        Small Suggestion
        Big-character posters, so beautiful;
        Colorful and good paper.
        One issue, a large cost:
        Three sheets are one jiao eight fen.
        White paper, will do,
        Enlarge the font and write the same;
        No impact on content,
        Please adopt this small suggestion.
                (Zhu Muhao, Ministry of Chemical Industry)

### 小建议
朱慕豪
1957-10-26
第8版()
专栏：街谈巷议

        小建议
        大字报，真漂亮；
        五颜六色好纸张。
        出一回，成本大：
        三张就是一毛八。
        白报纸，就行啦，
        字体加大照样写；
        对内容，不影响，
        小小建议请采纳。
                （化学工业部朱慕豪）
