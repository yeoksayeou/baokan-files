Title: Soong Ching-ling and Qian Junrui Send Telegram to Congratulate the Establishment of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1957-10-31
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. Soong Ching-ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, sent a telegram to congratulate the establishment of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To Comrade Nina Popova, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, please forward to the Inaugural Meeting of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association.
    Dear Comrades!
    On the occasion of the establishment of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, on behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, I extend our heartfelt and warm congratulations to you, and through you, to the great brotherly Soviet people.
    The Chinese people fully understand the great significance of Sino-Soviet friendship and cooperation. This rock-solid friendship and unity between the people of China and the Soviet Union, based on proletarian internationalism, is not only a guarantee for the prosperity and strength of our two countries, but also a reliable pillar of world peace.
    The establishment of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association is a powerful proof of the increasing consolidation and development of Sino-Soviet friendly and cooperative relations, and will help to further strengthen the unity and friendship between the two peoples. The Chinese people warmly welcome the establishment of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association.
    Long live the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union!
    Soong Ching-ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
     Beijing, October 28, 1957

### 宋庆龄和钱俊瑞电贺苏中友协成立

1957-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄和钱俊瑞电贺苏中友协成立
    新华社30日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄和秘书长钱俊瑞打电报祝贺苏中友好协会成立，电报全文如下：莫斯科苏联对外文化协会理事会主席尼娜·波波娃同志请转苏中友好协会成立大会亲爱的同志们！
    欣闻苏中友好协会成立，谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员，向你们，并通过你们，向伟大的兄弟般的苏联人民，表示衷心的热烈的祝贺。
    中国人民充分理解中苏友好合作的伟大意义。中苏两国人民之间的这种以无产阶级国际主义为基础的、坚如磐石的友好团结，不仅是我们两国繁荣富强的保证，同时也是世界和平的可靠的支柱。
    苏中友好协会的成立是中苏友好合作关系日益巩固和发展的有力证明，并将有助于进一步加强两国人民之间的团结和友谊。中国人民对苏中友协的成立表示热烈的欢迎。
    中苏两国人民永恒的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    中苏友好协会总会会长　宋庆龄
    中苏友好协会总会秘书长　钱俊瑞
     1957年10月28日北京
