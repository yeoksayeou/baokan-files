Title: Shen Yanbing Receives the Minister of Culture of the Kirghiz Soviet Socialist Republic
Author:
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing received this morning Kazakbayev, member of the Soviet cultural delegation and Minister of Culture of the Kirghiz Republic, who came to China to attend the National Day celebrations at the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

Also present at the reception were Ding Xilin and Xia Yan, Vice Ministers of Culture; Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Federation of Literary and Art Circles Bureau; and Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 沈雁冰接见苏吉尔吉斯共和国文化部长

1957-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰接见苏吉尔吉斯共和国文化部长
    新华社7日讯　文化部沈雁冰部长今天上午接见了应中苏友好协会总会邀请前来中国参加国庆观礼的苏联文化代表团团员、吉尔吉斯共和国文化部部长卡扎克巴耶夫。
    接见时在座的有文化部副部长丁西林、夏衍，文联局代理局长陈忠经，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权。
