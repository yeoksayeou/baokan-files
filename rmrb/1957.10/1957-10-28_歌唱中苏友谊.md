Title: Singing of Sino-Soviet Friendship
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 3

	Singing of Sino-Soviet Friendship
	The Beijing Children's Palace Friendship Choir was established under the guidance of the Soviet choir conductor expert Domashev. In just two years, it has cultivated more than 460 young music enthusiasts. On the eve of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, the children of the Friendship Choir deeply miss Comrade Domashev. They are discussing writing a congratulatory letter to Comrade Domashev and are stepping up rehearsals of songs such as "Song of Lenin," "Eternal Friendship," and "Song of Sino-Soviet Children's Friendship" to celebrate the great festival and to sing the praises of friendship. (Attached photo)
	Above: A group from the Friendship Choir is rehearsing "Eternal Friendship" at the Children's Palace, preparing to perform for the Soviet experts in the capital during the October Revolution Day. The children of the Friendship Choir are discussing writing a letter to Domashev, reporting their academic achievements, and congratulating him on the great October Revolution Day.
	Xinhua News Agency reporter Fu Jun photographed

### 歌唱中苏友谊

1957-10-28
第3版()
专栏：

    歌唱中苏友谊
    北京市少年宫友谊合唱团是在苏联合唱指挥专家杜马舍夫的指导下成立的。不过两年时间，已培养了少年音乐爱好者四百六十多人。在十月革命四十周年前夕，友谊合唱团的小朋友们，十分怀念杜马舍夫同志。他们正在讨论给杜马舍夫同志写祝贺信，加紧排练“列宁之歌”、“永恒的友谊”、“中苏儿童友谊之歌”等歌曲，来庆祝伟大的节日，来歌唱友谊。（附图片）
    上图：友谊合唱团的一个小组正在少年宫排练“永恒的友谊”，准备在十月革命节的时候，给在首都的苏联专家们演出。友谊合唱团的小朋友们，正在讨论给杜马舍夫写信，报告他们的学习成绩，祝贺伟大的十月革命节。
  新华社记者   傅军摄
