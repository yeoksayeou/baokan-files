Author: Wei Dezhong

　　Overcoming Drought
    The large-scale anti-drought wheat planting campaign is vigorously underway in Henan Province. In Huohua Fourth Cooperative of San Shilipu Township, Xinzheng County, 97% of cooperative members are participating in the anti-drought effort. This is a scene of cooperative members planting wheat while fighting the drought. Wei Dezhong (Henan Daily Agency)
