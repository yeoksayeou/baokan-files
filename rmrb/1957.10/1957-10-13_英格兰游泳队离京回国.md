Title: English Swimming Team Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1957-10-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The entire English swimming team left Beijing for home by plane on the 11th, after concluding their visit to China.

Rong Gaotang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, hosted a farewell banquet for them on the evening of the 10th.

### 英格兰游泳队离京回国

1957-10-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　英格兰游泳队离京回国
    据新华社11日讯　英格兰游泳队全体人员结束了在中国的访问以后，11日由北京乘飞机回国。
    中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠在10日晚间曾设宴欢送他们。
