Title: President Ho Chi Minh to Lead Vietnamese Delegation to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-10-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 28th: Vietnam News Agency reported today that at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Government, President Ho Chi Minh will lead a delegation of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the Government to Moscow to participate in the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. The other members of the delegation are: Le Duan, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Vietnam Workers' Party; Pham Hung, Minister of the Prime Minister's Office and member of the Political Bureau of the Labor Party; Vu Dinh Tung, Minister of War Invalids; Nguyen Van, Minister of Social Relief; Nguyen Van Kinh, Vietnamese Ambassador to the Soviet Union and member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party.

### 胡志明主席将率越南代表团赴苏

1957-10-29
第6版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席将率越南代表团赴苏
    据新华社河内28日电　据越南通讯社今天报道：应苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联政府的邀请，胡志明主席将率领越南劳动党和政府代表团前往莫斯科，参加伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的庆祝典礼。代表团的其他成员是：黎笋　越南劳动党政治局委员和书记处书记，范雄　总理府部长、劳动党政治局委员，武廷诵　荣军部长，阮闻
　社会救济部长，阮文镜　越南驻苏联大使、越南劳动党中央委员会委员。
