Title: Cairo Radio Criticizes So-Called "Mediation" Proposals
Author:
Date: 1957-10-29
Page: 5

    Cairo Radio Criticizes So-Called "Mediation" Proposals
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 28th – Cairo Radio stated on the 27th that the proposals for so-called “mediation” between Syria and Turkey are attempts to “cover up the preparations for aggression against Syria.”
    Cairo Radio said that this idea of “mediation” is being used by Western countries to avoid an embarrassing showdown in the United Nations. Arab countries should not support this proposal.

### 开罗电台抨击所谓“调解”建议

1957-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    开罗电台抨击所谓“调解”建议
    据新华社28日讯　开罗消息：开罗电台27日说，对叙利亚和土耳其进行所谓“调解”的建议，是企图“掩饰对叙利亚进行侵略的准备”。
    开罗电台说，这种“调解”的想法正在被西方国家利用，以避免在联合国中难堪地摊牌。阿拉伯国家不应当支持这种建议。
