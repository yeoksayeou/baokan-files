Title: Bridge Piers in the Yangtze River, Ah!
Author: Bā Bō
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 8

　　Bridge piers in the Yangtze River, Ah!
　　Bridge piers in the Yangtze River, Ah!
　　Like raised, powerful hands
　　They circulate the heartfelt efforts of our Soviet brothers,
　　They are covered with the comrades’ sweat;
　　With wisdom and strength,
　　They support the bridge of happiness,
　　Let the wheels of our vehicles rush towards socialism,
　　Speeding past like lightning.

### 长江中的桥墩呵！
巴波
1957-10-15
第8版()
专栏：

　　长江中的桥墩呵！
　　巴波
　　长江中的桥墩呵！
　　像举起有力的手
　　她循环着苏联弟兄的心血
　　她遍身有着同志们的汗珠
　　她以智慧和力量
　　托住幸福的长桥
　　让我们奔向社会主义的车轮
　　风驰电闪地而过
