Title: Resident Commune Head
Author: Liu Huanrui
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 8

Column: Street Talk

      Resident Commune Head
      Clang, clang, the sound of the seeder!
      Sowing the seeds is the resident commune head,
      Gently and meticulously sowing, he's a real expert.
      In a few days, they'll sprout and grow tall!
          (Liu Huanrui, Dezhou First Middle School, Shandong)
