Title: Delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Visits the China-Soviet Friendship Agricultural Cooperative
Author:
Date: 1957-10-03
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, headed by Aristov, visited the China-Soviet Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative in the western suburbs of Beijing this morning.
    When the delegation arrived at the cooperative, the members beat gongs and drums to welcome them. Comrade Aristov, the head of the delegation, and the members held a long discussion with the cadres of the cooperative.
    The delegation also visited the cooperative's large vegetable fields and the homes of the cooperative members.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团访问中苏友好农业社

1957-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团访问中苏友好农业社
    据新华社2日讯  以阿里斯托夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团，今天上午到北京西郊访问了中苏友好农业生产合作社。
    代表团到达合作社的时候，社员们敲锣打鼓列队来欢迎他们。阿里斯托夫团长和团员们同社的干部们进行了长时间的座谈。
    代表团还参观了合作社种植蔬菜的大片田地，并且访问了社员们的家庭。
