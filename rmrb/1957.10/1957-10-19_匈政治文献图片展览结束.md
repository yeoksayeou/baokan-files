Title: Hungarian Political Documents and Photos Exhibition Concludes
Author:
Date: 1957-10-19
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The "Hungary Marching on the Socialist Road—Hungarian Political Documents and Photos Exhibition," sponsored by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, concluded on October 18th. It was exhibited in Beijing for a total of twenty-one days, with over 23,000 visitors. From the exhibition, the audience gained a clearer understanding of the true facts of the imperialists and Hungarian counter-revolutionary elements creating the Hungarian October counter-revolutionary incident.
    These exhibits will also be transported to Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Xi'an, Tianjin, and Shenyang for a tour exhibition.

### 匈政治文献图片展览结束

1957-10-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　匈政治文献图片展览结束
    新华社18日讯  对外文化联络局主办的“循着社会主义道路迈进的匈牙利——匈牙利政治文献图片展览”已于10月18日结束。在北京共展出二十一天，观众达二万三千多人。观众们从展览中更清楚地认识到帝国主义者及匈牙利反革命分子制造匈牙利十月反革命事件的事实真象。
    这些展品还将运在上海、广州、武汉、西安、天津、沈阳巡回展览。
