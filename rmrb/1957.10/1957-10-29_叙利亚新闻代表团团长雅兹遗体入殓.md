Title: Entombment of the Remains of Yazi, Head of the Syrian News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th. The remains of Tawfiq Yazi, head of the Syrian Journalists Delegation to China and Director of the News Department of the Syrian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, were entombed in Beijing on the afternoon of the 28th according to the rites of his religion. Present at the entombment were Wang Yunsheng, Vice-President of the All-China Journalists Association, responsible persons and representatives from various news organizations in the capital, Adib Shalan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Syrian Embassy in China, Ragab, the Egyptian Ambassador to China, and foreign journalists in Beijing.

The All-China Journalists Association will hold a memorial service for Mr. Yazi on the 29th.

### 叙利亚新闻代表团团长雅兹遗体入殓

1957-10-29
第3版()
专栏：

    叙利亚新闻代表团团长雅兹遗体入殓
    新华社28日讯　叙利亚新闻工作者访华代表团团长、叙利亚外交部新闻司司长托菲克·雅兹的遗体，已于28日下午在北京按照他所属的宗教的仪式入殓。入殓时在场的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长王芸生，首都各新闻单位的负责人和代表，叙利亚驻华大使馆临时代办阿迪卜·沙蓝，以及埃及驻华大使拉加卜和在北京的外国记者。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会将在29日为雅兹先生举行追悼会。
