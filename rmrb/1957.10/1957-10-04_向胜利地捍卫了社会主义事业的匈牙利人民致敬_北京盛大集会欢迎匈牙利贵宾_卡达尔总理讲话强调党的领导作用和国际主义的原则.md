Title: Saluting the Hungarian People Who Victoriously Defended the Socialist Cause; Grand Beijing Rally Welcomes Hungarian Guests; Premier Kádár's Speech Emphasizes the Leading Role of the Party and the Principles of Internationalism
Author:
Date: 1957-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Beijing. Over eight thousand people from all walks of life in Beijing held a grand rally this afternoon at the Beijing Gymnasium to welcome the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government delegation headed by Premier János Kádár.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee Guo Moruo, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian also attended the rally.

Premier Kádár delivered a warm and friendly speech at the mass rally (full text on page two), sparking bursts of stormy applause from the audience.

Premier Kádár said that in the October events of last year, Hungarian workers, miners, cooperative farmers, individual farmers, and progressive intellectuals stood up and, with the help of the people of the Soviet Union, China, and other fraternal socialist countries, brilliantly defended the cause of socialism and peace in Hungary. He said that the history of Hungary's October events last year tempered the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, the Hungarian state, and the Hungarian people. Therefore, the Hungarian people are full of confidence in the future. Premier Kádár said that in August this year, Hungary's industrial output had reached 101% of the average monthly output of last year, labor productivity had reached 97%, and farmers carefully cultivated the land, with this year's grain harvest being even better than last year's. Of the one thousand seven hundred agricultural production cooperatives wrecked by counter-revolutionaries, one thousand four hundred have been reorganized.

He said: "Our most important experience may be that traitors who have run to the enemy's camp should not be persuaded, but should be eliminated as enemies. There should be no compromise with counter-revolutionaries, but struggle. To deal with man-eating tigers, you cannot use pieces of meat, but only aim with a gun muzzle to tame them."

Premier Kádár then pointed out that the Hungarian people's struggle against the hidden counter-revolutionary elements in the country will continue, and he sternly condemned the US imperialism's manipulation of the United Nations to create the so-called Hungarian issue in an attempt to once again interfere in Hungary's internal affairs.

Premier Kádár emphasized:

"We Hungarians are implementing the policies of our Party and government, and life has proven that the general line of this policy is correct.

"We must defend and strengthen the leading role of the Party, because the counter-revolutionary offensive in Hungary has once again proven that if the leading role of the revolutionary, Marxist-Leninist party of the working class is weakened, the regime of the working class will be in danger."

Premier Kádár said: "In our era, when socialism has become a world system and exists, when the people of many countries are building socialism in their own countries, the principle of proletarian internationalism has become the most profound and essential issue in the relations between socialist countries and the people of all countries building socialism. Within the socialist camp, proletarian internationalism means the fraternal alliance of the people of all countries, a deep, indivisible friendship, and further means a common destiny."

Premier Kádár concluded by saying: We Hungarians have confidence in our just cause, and we are also full of confidence in further strengthening the solidarity of the countries building socialism, and in our great friends, the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China.

At the beginning of the meeting, Mayor Peng Zhen delivered a welcome speech (full text on page two). He said that in October last year, the Hungarian people resolutely suppressed the counter-revolutionary armed rebellion and defended the achievements of their socialist construction. This is a great victory for the Hungarian people, and also a major contribution of the Hungarian people to the socialist camp and the cause of world peace. The Chinese people will continue to work with the Hungarian people to further strengthen the solidarity of socialist countries and safeguard world peace.

Mayor Peng Zhen said: "We Chinese people fully support the just struggle of the Hungarian people to safeguard national sovereignty and the cause of socialism. The cause of the Hungarian people is a just cause, and it will continue to receive the support of all peace-loving countries and people in the world. All conspiracies of the US aggressive bloc and the Hungarian counter-revolutionaries against socialism and to undermine world peace will continue to suffer shameful failures."

After Premier Kádár's speech, Mayor Peng Zhen presented banners and gifts to Premier Kádár and the Hungarian government delegation on behalf of the people of Beijing.

At the welcoming rally, Beijing's literary and art workers performed brilliant cultural programs for the guests. After the performance, Premier Kádár, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, went to the center of the venue to present flower baskets to the actors to express his gratitude. At this time, everyone stood up and applauded warmly for a long time.

The government delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic headed by Premier Kádár returned to Beijing this morning from Shenyang. (Photo attached)

### 向胜利地捍卫了社会主义事业的匈牙利人民致敬  北京盛大集会欢迎匈牙利贵宾  卡达尔总理讲话强调党的领导作用和国际主义的原则

1957-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    向胜利地捍卫了社会主义事业的匈牙利人民致敬
    北京盛大集会欢迎匈牙利贵宾
    卡达尔总理讲话强调党的领导作用和国际主义的原则
    据新华社3日讯  北京市各界人民八千多人今天下午在北京体育馆举行大会，欢迎以卡达尔总理为首的匈牙利工农革命政府代表团。
    周恩来总理、贺龙副总理、郭沫若副委员长和外交部副部长张闻天等都出席了大会。
    卡达尔总理在这个群众大会上发表了一篇充满友谊的热情的讲话（全文见第二版），引起会场一阵紧接着一阵的暴风雨般的掌声。
    卡达尔总理说，在去年的十月事件中，匈牙利工人、矿工、合作社农民、个体农民和进步知识分子站了起来，并且在苏联、中国和其他兄弟的社会主义国家人民的帮助下在匈牙利卓越地保卫了社会主义和和平事业。他说，匈牙利去年十月事件的历史锻炼了匈牙利社会主义工人党、匈牙利的国家和人民。因此，匈牙利人民对未来充满了信心。卡达尔总理说，今年8月份匈牙利的工业产量已经达到去年平均每月产量的101％，劳动生产率已经达到97％，农民精心地耕作了土地，今年粮食谷物的收成比去年还要好些。被反革命搞垮的一千七百个农业生产合作社，其中有一千四百个已经重新组织起来。
    他说：“我们最重要的经验可能是，对已经跑到敌人阵营中去的叛徒不应该去劝说，而是应该作为敌人来消灭。对待反革命不应该妥协，而是应该斗争。对付吃人的老虎不能用肉块而只能用枪口瞄准的办法才能使它驯服。”
    卡达尔总理接着指出匈牙利人民和国内隐藏的反革命分子的斗争还将继续进行下去，他并严厉地谴责了美帝国主义操纵联合国制造所谓匈牙利问题企图再次干涉匈牙利内政的阴谋活动。
    卡达尔总理强调说：
    “我们匈牙利人执行着我们党和政府的政策，生活已经证明这种政策的总的路线是正确的。
    “我们要保卫并且加强党的领导作用，因为匈牙利的反革命进攻再一次证明，如果削弱了工人阶级革命的、马克思列宁主义的政党的领导作用，工人阶级的政权就会陷入危险的境地。”
    卡达尔总理说：“在我们的时代里，当社会主义已经成为一个世界体系而存在的时候，当许多国家的人民在自己的国家中建设社会主义的时候，无产阶级国际主义的原则就成了社会主义国家和建设社会主义的各国人民的关系中最深刻的本质问题了。在社会主义阵营内部，无产阶级国际主义就意味着各国人民兄弟的同盟、深厚的、不可分裂的友谊，并且进一步意味着共同的命运。”
    卡达尔总理最后说：我们匈牙利人对我们的正义事业怀着信心，我们对于进一步加强建设社会主义的各国的团结，对于伟大的朋友苏联和中华人民共和国也充满着信心。
    彭真市长在大会开始的时候致欢迎词（全文见第二版）。他说，去年10月，匈牙利人民坚决地镇压了反革命的武装叛乱，捍卫了自己社会主义建设的成果。这是匈牙利人民的伟大胜利，也是匈牙利人民对社会主义阵营和世界和平事业的重大贡献。中国人民将继续同匈牙利人民一起，为进一步加强社会主义各国的团结和维护世界和平而共同奋斗。
    彭真市长说：“我们中国人民完全支持匈牙利人民维护国家主权和社会主义事业的正义斗争。匈牙利人民的事业是正义的事业，它将继续获得世界一切爱好和平的国家和人民的支持，美国侵略集团和匈牙利反革命分子反对社会主义和破坏世界和平的一切阴谋诡计，必将继续遭到可耻的失败。”
    在卡达尔总理讲话以后，彭真市长代表北京市各界人民向卡达尔总理和匈牙利政府代表团献了锦旗和礼品。
    在欢迎大会上，北京的文艺工作者为贵宾们表演了精采的文艺节目。表演结束后，卡达尔总理由周恩来总理陪同走到会场中央向演员们送花篮致谢。这时候，人们全体起立，长时间地热烈鼓掌。
    以卡达尔总理为首的匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团是在今天上午由沈阳回到北京的。（附图片）
