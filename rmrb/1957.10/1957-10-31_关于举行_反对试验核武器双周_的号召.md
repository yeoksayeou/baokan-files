Title: Call to Hold a "Two-Week Campaign Against Nuclear Weapon Testing"
Author:
Date: 1957-10-31
Page: 5

    Call to Hold a "Two-Week Campaign Against Nuclear Weapon Testing"
    The World Congress for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament, held in Tokyo from August 6th to 16th, 1957, proposed that international action days be held in various countries. On these days, appeals should be made to the United Nations, urging governments to reach an agreement on the prohibition of nuclear testing.
    The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council supports this proposal, considering it an opportunity for the peace forces of all countries to unite and reaffirm their common desire to achieve a halt to the nuclear arms race.
    The Standing Committee requests national peace committees to organize the broadest and most diverse mass actions from November 10th to 25th, 1957, to support the immediate cessation of nuclear testing.

### 关于举行“反对试验核武器双周”的号召

1957-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    关于举行“反对试验核武器双周”的号召
    1957年8月6日至16日在东京举行的禁止原子弹、氢弹和争取裁军世界大会，建议在各国举行国际行动日，在这个日子，应该向联合国呼吁，使各国政府在禁止核试验问题上达成协议。
    世界和平理事会常务委员会支持这一建议，认为这是各国和平力量团结起来重申它们实现停止原子核竞赛的共同愿望的机会。
    常务委员会要求各国和平委员会在1957年11月10日至25日组织最广泛和最多样化的群众行动来支持立即停止核试验。
