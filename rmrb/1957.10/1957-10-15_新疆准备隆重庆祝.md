Title: Xinjiang Prepares Grand Celebrations

Author:

Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, 14th. The Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) has decided to launch extensive publicity activities over the next month to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, and to conduct a universal and in-depth education on proletarian internationalism and socialism among workers and the broad masses of people in the autonomous region. Each special district, autonomous prefecture, and county will also universally conduct publicity and education among cadres and the broad masses of farmers.

The Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Committee of the CPPCC held an enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee on the 12th. The meeting also requested newspapers, periodicals, and radio stations to vigorously publicize the October Revolution and the great achievements of the Soviet Union over the past forty years.

Around the holiday, relevant units in the autonomous region will hold photo exhibitions and reports introducing Soviet scientific achievements. Some units will also hold various get-togethers to entertain Soviet friends stationed in Urumqi and helping Xinjiang with its various construction projects. On the day of the festival, a grand celebration meeting will be held in Urumqi.

### 新疆准备隆重庆祝

1957-10-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　新疆准备隆重庆祝
    新华社乌鲁木齐14日电　政协新疆维吾尔自治区委员会决定在今后一个月内广泛展开庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年的宣传活动，向自治区各族职工和广大群众进行一次普遍深入的无产阶级国际主义教育和社会主义教育。各专区、自治州、县也要普遍在干部和广大农民群众中进行宣传教育。
    政协新疆维吾尔自治区委员会在12日举行了常务委员会扩大会议，这次会议并且要求报纸、刊物、广播电台大力宣传十月革命和苏联四十年来的伟大成就。
    在节日前后，自治区有关单位将举办图片展览和介绍苏联科学成就的报告会。有的单位还将举行各种联欢会，招待驻乌鲁木齐和帮助新疆进行各项建设的苏联友人。节日那天，乌鲁木齐将举行隆重的庆祝大会。
