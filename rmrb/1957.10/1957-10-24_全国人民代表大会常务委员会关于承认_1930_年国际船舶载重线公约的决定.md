Author:
Date: 1957-10-24
Page: 1

    Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on the Accession to the 1930 International Convention on Load Lines for Ships
    (Adopted at the 82nd Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on October 23, 1957)
    On October 23, 1957, the 82nd Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, based on the proposal submitted by Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council, reviewed the International Convention on Load Lines for Ships, signed in London on July 5, 1930, and acceded to in the name of the Chinese government, and decided to accede to it; and reviewed the amendment proposal of the Commonwealth of Australia to Annex II of the 1930 International Convention on Load Lines for Ships, and decided to accept it.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于承认1930年国际船舶载重线公约的决定

1957-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于承认1930年国际船舶载重线公约的决定
    （1957年10月23日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第八十二次会议通过）
    1957年10月23日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第八十二次会议根据国务院周恩来总理提出的议案，审查了1930年7月5日在伦敦签订的、并且以中国政府名义加入的国际船舶载重线公约，决定予以承认；并且审查了对于1930年国际船舶载重线公约附件二的澳大利亚联邦政府修正建议，决定予以接受。
