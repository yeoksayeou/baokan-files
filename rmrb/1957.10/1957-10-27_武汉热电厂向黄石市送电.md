Title: Wuhan Thermal Power Plant Sends Electricity to Huangshi City
Author:
Date: 1957-10-27
Page: 4

Construction Dynamics

    Wuhan Thermal Power Plant Sends Electricity to Huangshi City

    An 110,000-volt high-voltage power transmission line from Qingshan, Wuchang, to Daye Tieshan in the Huangshi area has been erected. The large-scale Wuhan Thermal Power Plant officially began supplying electricity to factories and mines in the emerging industrial city of Huangshi area through it on the 18th.

    Construction of this high-voltage line began in early March of this year and is 81 kilometers long.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 武汉热电厂向黄石市送电

1957-10-27
第4版()
专栏：建设动态

    武汉热电厂向黄石市送电
    从武昌青山到黄石地区大冶铁山的一条十一万伏高压输电线已经架好，规模巨大的武汉热电厂18日正式通过它向新兴的工业城市——黄石地区的厂矿送电。
    这条高压线路是今年3月初开始建设的，全长八十一公里。
                          （新华社）
