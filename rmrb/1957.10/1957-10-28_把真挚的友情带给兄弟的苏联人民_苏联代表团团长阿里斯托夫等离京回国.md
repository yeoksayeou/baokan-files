Title: Bringing Sincere Friendship to the Brotherly Soviet People: Soviet Delegation Leader Aristov and Others Leave Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 1

　　Bringing Sincere Friendship to the Brotherly Soviet People
　　Soviet Delegation Leader Aristov and Others Leave Beijing for Home
　　Xinhua News Agency, October 27th. The head of the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation, Aristov, and a portion of the delegation members, left Beijing by jet plane on the morning of the 27th to return home. The other members of the delegation had already returned home earlier.
　　This morning, Comrade Aristov bid farewell to Chairman Liu Shaoqi. Before the distinguished guests left Beijing, Vice Chairman Peng Zhen went to the hotel to see them off.
　　Those who saw them off at the airport today included Vice Chairmen Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Burhan, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Wu Yuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zeng Yongquan, and heads of various departments of state organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations, Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, and the Ambassadors of Czechoslovakia and Vietnam to China.
　　Vice Chairman Lin Boqu delivered a speech at the airport to bid farewell to the distinguished guests. He said that the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation's visit to various parts of our country expressed the profound brotherly friendship of the great Soviet people towards the Chinese people, giving the Chinese people great encouragement and a deep impression. This visit has not only further deepened the brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples, but also contributed to strengthening the great unity of the socialist camp and safeguarding the noble cause of world peace.
　　Lin Boqu asked the distinguished guests to convey the sincere friendship and good wishes of the 600 million Chinese people to all the brotherly Soviet people. He wished the Soviet people continuous new and greater victories in the cause of communist construction.
　　In his farewell speech, Delegation Leader Aristov said that the great achievements and victories made by the Chinese people in all fields deeply delighted and encouraged the Soviet people. We are convinced that the Chinese people are more closely united than ever around the Chinese Communist Party and the People's Government. We are also convinced that under the wise leadership of the Communist Party, the Chinese people will soon turn their country into a socialist industrial power.
　　Aristov said: "The people of all nationalities of the Soviet Union will always cherish deep friendship with the great Chinese people. We, the Soviet people, will do our best to strengthen and expand the friendship between the Soviet Union and China in the future."
　　In their speeches, Vice Chairman Lin Boqu and Delegation Leader Aristov both shouted: "Long live the eternal and unbreakable brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people!" (Attached photo)
　　Delegation Leader Aristov bids farewell to well-wishers. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Qingrui.

### 把真挚的友情带给兄弟的苏联人民  苏联代表团团长阿里斯托夫等离京回国

1957-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    把真挚的友情带给兄弟的苏联人民
    苏联代表团团长阿里斯托夫等离京回国
    新华社27日讯　苏联最高苏维埃代表团团长阿里斯托夫和一部分团员在27日上午乘喷气客机离开北京回国。代表团的另一部分团员已先期回国。
    在今天早晨，阿里斯托夫同志曾向刘少奇委员长辞行。在贵宾们离京以前，彭真副委员长曾到宾馆为客人们送行。
    今天到机场送行的，有林伯渠、李济深、郭沫若、陈叔通副委员长，李先念副总理，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，中苏友好协会副会长吴玉章，外交部副部长曾涌泉和国家机关各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，苏联驻中国大使尤金和捷、越两国驻中国大使。
    林伯渠副委员长在机场上致词欢送贵宾。他说，苏联最高苏维埃代表团这次在我国各地的访问表达了伟大苏联人民对中国人民无比深厚的兄弟友情，给了中国人民以极大的鼓舞和极深的印象。这次访问，不仅进一步加深了中苏两国人民的兄弟友谊，而且也有利于加强社会主义阵营的伟大团结和维护世界和平的崇高事业。
    林伯渠请贵宾们把六亿中国人民的真挚友情和良好祝愿带给兄弟的苏联全体人民。祝苏联人民在共产主义建设事业中不断地取得新的更大的胜利。
    阿里斯托夫团长在告别讲话中说，中国人民在各方面所取得的巨大成就和胜利，使苏联人民深深感到欢欣和鼓舞。我们确信，中国人民比以往任何时候都更加紧密地团结在中国共产党和人民政府的周围。我们同样也确信，中国人民在共产党的英明领导下，在不久的将来就能把自己的国家变成一个社会主义的工业强国。
    阿里斯托夫说：“苏联各族人民对伟大的中国人民永远怀着深厚的友情。我们苏联人民今后将尽全力来加强和扩大苏联和中国的友谊。”
    林伯渠副委员长和阿里斯托夫团长在讲话最后，都高呼：“中苏两国人民永恒的牢不可破的兄弟友谊万岁！”（附图片）
    阿里斯托夫团长向欢送者告别　新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄
