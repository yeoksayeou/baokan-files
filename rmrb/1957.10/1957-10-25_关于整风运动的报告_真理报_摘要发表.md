    "Report on the Rectification Campaign"
    Abstract Published in "Pravda"
    Moscow, October 24th (Dispatch from our newspaper) The Soviet newspaper "Pravda" published an abstract of Comrade Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee’s "Report on the Rectification Campaign," delivered at the Third Plenary Session (Enlarged) of the CPC Central Committee on September 23rd, on the entire third page of its October 24th issue.
