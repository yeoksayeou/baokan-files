Title: The Ministry of Culture Holds Peking Opera Gala to Welcome Bulgarian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Ministry of Culture held a Peking Opera gala at Huairentang, Zhongnanhai, this evening to welcome the Bulgarian government delegation headed by Chairman Yugov.
    The distinguished guests, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and others, arrived at the venue. At this moment, the audience stood up and applauded warmly for a long time.
    Among those accompanying the distinguished guests to watch the opera tonight were Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian and Zeng Yongquan, and Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria Zhou Zhuan.
    At the end of the gala, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, went on stage, presented flower baskets to the Chinese actors, shook hands with them one by one, and took photos.
    This morning, Chairman Yugov and the delegation members, accompanied by Vice Premier Li Xiannian and others, toured the Summer Palace. (Attached picture)
    During its visit to our country, the Bulgarian government delegation headed by Comrade Yugov visited many places. This is the scene when the distinguished guests visited warships at the Lushunkou Naval Base.

### 文化部举办京剧晚会欢迎保政府代表团

1957-10-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　文化部举办京剧晚会欢迎保政府代表团
    新华社11日讯　文化部今晚在中南海怀仁堂举办京剧晚会，欢迎以于哥夫主席为首的保加利亚政府代表团。
    贵宾们由周恩来总理等陪同来到会场。这时，全场起立热烈鼓掌，经久不息。
    今晚陪同贵宾观剧的，还有副总理李先念，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长张闻天、曾涌泉和中国驻保加利亚大使周竹安等人。
    晚会结束的时候，贵宾们由周恩来总理陪同走上舞台，向中国演员赠送花蓝，并且同他们一一握手，摄影留念。
    今天上午，于哥夫主席和代表团人员由李先念副总理等陪同游览了颐和园。（附图片）
    以于哥夫同志为首的保加利亚政府代表团在我国访问期间，参观游览了许多地方。这是贵宾们在旅顺口海军基地参观舰艇时的情形。
                            程庆祥摄
