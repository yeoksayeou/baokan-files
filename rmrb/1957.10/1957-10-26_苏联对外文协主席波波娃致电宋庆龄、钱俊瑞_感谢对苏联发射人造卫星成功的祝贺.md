Title:波波娃, Chairman of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, Sends a Telegram to Soong Ching Ling and Qian Junrui Thanking them for their Congratulations on the Successful Launch of the Soviet Artificial Satellite
Author:
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: Nina Popova, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, sent a return telegram to Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, thanking them for their congratulations on the successful launch of the Soviet artificial earth satellite. The telegram reads as follows:

"We are deeply moved by your congratulations on the successful launch of the artificial earth satellite by the Soviet Union. We feel fortunate to be able to share the inner joy and glory of the Soviet people, filled by this greatest event in human history, with you, our closest and most loyal allies. On behalf of the Council of the Soviet Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, I express my heartfelt thanks for your warm congratulations and convey our sincere gratitude and fraternal friendship."

### 苏联对外文协主席波波娃致电宋庆龄、钱俊瑞  感谢对苏联发射人造卫星成功的祝贺

1957-10-26
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联对外文协主席波波娃致电宋庆龄、钱俊瑞
    感谢对苏联发射人造卫星成功的祝贺
    新华社25日讯　苏联对外文化协会理事会主席尼娜·波波娃复电感谢中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄、秘书长钱俊瑞对苏联发射人造地球卫星成功的祝贺。电文如下：
    “你们对于苏联成功地发射人造地球卫星的祝贺，使我们深为感动。我们感到幸福的是，能和你们——我们最亲近的和忠实的同盟者，来分享苏联人民由于人类历史上这一最伟大的事件而充满内心的愉快和荣耀。谨代表苏联对外文化协会理事会衷心地感谢你们的热情的祝贺，并向你们表示真诚的谢意和兄弟般的友情。”
