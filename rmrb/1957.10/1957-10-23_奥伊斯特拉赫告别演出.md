Title: Oistrakh's Farewell Performance
Author:
Date: 1957-10-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: World-renowned Soviet violinist David Oistrakh's farewell performance recital in China at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing on the evening of the 22nd was once again met with an exceptionally warm welcome from Chinese audiences.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi attended the recital on the evening of the 22nd. After the recital, he went on stage to warmly shake hands with Oistrakh and his piano accompanist, Yang Polsky, congratulating them on their successful performance.

### 奥伊斯特拉赫告别演出

1957-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    奥伊斯特拉赫告别演出
    据新华社22日讯　世界闻名的苏联小提琴家奥伊斯特拉赫22日晚在北京天桥剧场举行访华告别演出独奏会时，再一次受到了我国听众异常热烈的欢迎。
    国务院副总理陈毅出席了22日晚的独奏会，并且在独奏会结束以后，到舞台上同奥伊斯特拉赫以及他的钢琴伴奏杨波尔斯基亲切握手，祝贺他们演出成功。
