Title: Delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Visits Lüda

Author:
Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

　　Delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Visits Lüda
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, headed by Aristov, arrived in Lüda by special plane this afternoon from Wuhan.
    The distinguished guests were welcomed at the airport by Song Li, Mayor of Lüda, Deputy Mayors Yu Guying and Li Nan, Bai Qingjiang, Secretary of the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee, leading officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army garrison, Zhang Zhiyuan, Vice President of the Lüda Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and all walks of life.
    Soviet Deputy Consul General to Lüda, Pridibaillo, and many Soviet experts also came to the airport to welcome the delegation.
    On their way from Wuhan to Lüda today, the distinguished guests rested in Tianjin for more than an hour and were welcomed by Li Gengtao, Deputy Mayor of Tianjin, Wang Kangzhi, Secretary of the CPC Tianjin Municipal Committee, and other party and government leaders and representatives from all walks of life.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团到旅大访问

1957-10-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃代表团到旅大访问
    据新华社讯　以阿里斯托夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团今天下午从武汉乘专机到达旅大。
    贵宾们在机场上受到旅大市市长宋黎、副市长于谷莺、厉男，中共旅大市委书记白清江，中国人民解放军驻军首长，旅大市中苏友好协会副会长张知远，以及各民主党派各人民团体负责人和各界代表的欢迎。
    苏联驻旅大代总领事普利第巴依洛和苏联专家多人也到机场欢迎。
    贵宾们今天从武汉来旅大途中，曾在天津休息了一个多小时，受到天津市副市长李耕涛、中共天津市委书记王亢之等党政负责人和各界代表的欢迎。
