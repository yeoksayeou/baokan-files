Title: American People Congratulate the Soviet Union

Author: 
Date: 1957-10-10
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 9th dispatch: TASS, Washington, October 8th dispatch: American groups and individuals have been sending telegrams to the Soviet Embassy in the United States congratulating the Soviet Union on building the first artificial satellite.
    The telegram from Roberts, Chairman of the American-Russian Institute, said: Congratulations to you, congratulations to your scientists, cultural workers, and the Soviet people who launched the first artificial Earth satellite. This epoch-making achievement marks the outstanding achievements of the Soviet Union in culture and science.

### 美国人民向苏联祝贺

1957-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    美国人民向苏联祝贺
    据新华社9日讯　据塔斯社华盛顿8日讯：美国各团体和私人纷纷打电报给苏联驻美国大使馆，祝贺苏联制成第一颗人造卫星。
    美俄协会主席罗伯茨的电报说：祝贺您，祝贺你们的科学家、文化工作者和苏联人民，他们发射了第一颗人造地球卫星。这个划时代的成就标志着苏联在文化和科学方面的出色成就。
