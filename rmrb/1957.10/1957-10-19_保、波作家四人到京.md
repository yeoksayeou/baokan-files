Title: Bulgarian and Polish Writers Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-19
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th.  Kolarov, member of the Presidium of the Bulgarian Writers' Association and chairman of the Prose Creation Committee, and poet Mitogiev, Polish literary theorist and critic Karlst, and poet Karpowicz arrived in Beijing on the 16th and 17th respectively for a visit to our country.

### 保、波作家四人到京

1957-10-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　保、波作家四人到京
    新华社18日讯  来我国访问的保加利亚作家协会主席团委员、散文创作委员会主席科拉罗夫和诗人密托吉耶夫，波兰文艺理论批评家卡尔斯特和诗人卡尔波维奇分别在16、17日到达北京。
