Title: Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe's First Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: The Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic State Music and Drama Theatre gave its first performance in China at the Tianqiao Theatre in Beijing tonight.

Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, and more than a thousand people from all walks of life in Beijing watched the performance.

Mongolian People's Republic Ambassador to China Lubsan, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China, also watched the performance.

During the intermission of the performance, Vice Premier Li Xiannian met with Tsegmid, the head of the art troupe. Present at the meeting were Shen Yanbing, Zhang Hanfu, Chu Tunan, and Ambassador Lubsan, among others.

The artists from the Mongolian People's Republic performed wonderful folk songs, dances, and orchestral music tonight. Each program was warmly welcomed, and many programs were repeated two or three times at the request of the audience. (Attached picture)

### 蒙古人民共和国艺术团在京首次演出

1957-10-15
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国艺术团在京首次演出
    据新华社14日讯　蒙古人民共和国国家音乐话剧院艺术团今天晚上在北京天桥剧场举行访问中国的首次演出。
    国务院副总理李先念，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，以及北京各界人士一千多人观看了演出。
    蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使鲁布桑以及各国驻华使节和外交官员也观看了演出。
    李先念副总理在演出休息时，接见了艺术团团长策格米德。接见时在座的有沈雁冰、章汉夫、楚图南，以及鲁布桑大使等。
    蒙古人民共和国的艺术家们今天晚上表演了精采的民间歌舞和管弦乐，每个节目都受到热烈欢迎，许多节目在观众要求下重演两三次。（附图片）
