Title: Zhang Xiruo Receives Delegation from Tokyo Metropolitan Peace Conference, Japan
Author:
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, met on the afternoon of the 25th with the delegation from the Tokyo Metropolitan Peace Conference of Japan, led by the head of the delegation, Haraguchi Sachichi, the deputy head, Aoki Fusakichi, and all members of the delegation.

In the evening, Zhang Xiruo hosted a banquet for all members of the delegation.

The delegation from the Tokyo Metropolitan Peace Conference of Japan arrived in Beijing by train on the 23rd from Guangzhou at the invitation of Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. After visiting Beijing, the delegation will visit other parts of China.

### 张奚若接见日本东京都和平会议代表团

1957-10-26
第3版()
专栏：

    张奚若接见日本东京都和平会议代表团
    新华社25日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京分会主席张奚若25日下午接见了日本东京都和平会议代表团团长原三七、副团长青木房吉和全体团员。
    晚间，张奚若宴请代表团全体团员。
    日本东京都和平会议代表团是应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京分会主席张奚若的邀请，23日由广州乘火车到北京的。代表团在北京访问后将去我国各地访问。
