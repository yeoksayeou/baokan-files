Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1957-10-23
Page: 1

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    The Regulations on Administrative Penalties for Public Security of the People's Republic of China, having been adopted at the 81st meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on October 22, 1957, are hereby promulgated.
    Chairman of the People's Republic of China: Mao Zedong, October 22, 1957

### 中华人民共和国主席令

1957-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    中华人民共和国治安管理处罚条例已由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于一九五七年十月二十二日第八十一次会议通过，现予公布。
    中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　一九五七年十月二十二日
