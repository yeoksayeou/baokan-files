Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet for Visiting Delegation of Mayors from Northern Japanese Port Cities
Author:
Date: 1957-10-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the visiting delegation of mayors from northern Japanese port cities, led by Suzuki Tomin, and deputy leader Sakurai Tatsuji, along with all members of the delegation.

The banquet was presided over by Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of Beijing. Before the banquet, Zhang Youyu met with all members of the visiting delegation.

Among those present at the banquet were Vice Mayors of Beijing Wu Han, Wang Kunlun, Feng Jiping, Cheng Hongyi, Jia Tingsan, Le Songsheng, Chairman of the Beijing Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Liu Ren, Vice Chairman Liang Sicheng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Xiao Xiangqian, and heads of various bureaus and people's organizations of Beijing.

### 彭真市长宴请北日本港湾城市市长访华团

1957-10-31
第3版()
专栏：

    彭真市长宴请北日本港湾城市市长访华团
    新华社30日讯　北京市市长彭真今天晚间举行宴会，欢迎北日本港湾城市市长访华团团长铃木东民、副团长樱井辰治和全体团员。
    宴会由北京市副市长张友渔主持。宴会前，张友渔接见了访华团全体团员。
    出席宴会作陪的有北京市副市长吴晗、王昆仑、冯基平、程宏毅、贾庭三、乐松生，中国人民政治协商会议北京市委员会主席刘仁、副主席梁思成，中国人民外交学会副秘书长萧向前以及北京市各局、各人民团体负责人。
