Title: Japan-China Friendship Association Holds Celebration Party
Author:
Date: 1957-10-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, October 1st: The Japan-China Friendship Association's Tokyo United Association held a celebration party at the Nakano Public Hall on September 30th, attended by 800 citizens, to celebrate China's National Day and commemorate the start of "Japan-China Friendship Month." At the same time, the founding anniversary of the Japan-China Friendship Association Tokyo United Association's Nakano Branch was held at the meeting. The founding assembly of the Nakano Branch passed a declaration stating that they will unite more closely and work together with the Japanese people to eliminate all man-made obstacles and launch a movement to urge the Japanese government to restore diplomatic relations with China.

### 日中友协举行庆祝晚会

1957-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    日中友协举行庆祝晚会
    据新华社1日讯  东京消息：日中友好协会东京都联合会在9月30日为了庆祝中国国庆节和纪念“日中友好月”的开始，在中野公会堂举行了有八百市民参加的晚会。在会上同时举行了日中友协东京都联合会中野分会的成立纪念大会。中野分会的成立大会通过了一项宣言，表示他们将更加团结起来并与日本人民共同努力排除一切人为障碍，展开促使日本政府恢复日中邦交的运动。
