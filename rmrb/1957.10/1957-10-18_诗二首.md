Title: Two Poems
Author: Lu Dingyi
Date: 1957-10-18
Page: 8

　　Two Poems
　　Lu Dingyi
    Red Flag Waving

    Tortoise and Snake hills cradle the Yangtze River Bridge
      People surge on the bridge like a tide
    Conquering the natural barrier, aspirations are bold
      The red flag waves in the wind

    Eternal Peace

    Guangzhou directly connected to Beijing
      Three towns connected on land
    In future years, the Three Gorges will have a high dam
      The river flows in eternal peace

　　诗二首
　　陆定一
    红旗飘
    龟蛇捧持长江桥
      桥上人如潮
    征服天堑志气豪
      红旗迎风飘
    永太平
    广州直达北京城
      三镇陆上行
    他年巫峡高坝成
      江流永太平
