Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Delegation from the Japanese National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 8th — Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received this morning Akira Kazami, Chairman of the Japanese National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, Tadayoshi Obata, head of the delegation from the Japanese National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, and deputy heads Noriharu Yoshida, Yae Kawamoto, and all members of the delegation.

Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, Liao Chengzhi, Qiao Guanhua, Zhao Anbo, Wu Maosun, and Xiao Xiangqian.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华使节团

1957-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华使节团
    新华社8日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天上午接见了日本恢复日中邦交国民会议理事长风见章，日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华使节团团长小畑忠良，副团长吉田法晴、过元八重和全体团员。
    接见时在座的，有张奚若、廖承志、乔冠华、赵安博、吴茂荪、萧向前。
