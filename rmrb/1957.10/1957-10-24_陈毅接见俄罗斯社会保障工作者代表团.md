Title: Chen Yi Receives Delegation of Russian Social Security Workers

Author:

Date: 1957-10-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received the delegation of social security workers from the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, headed by Kaplanov, this afternoon.

Also present at the meeting were Vice Minister of Internal Affairs Wang Ziyi and Assistant Minister Yang Chengsen.

### 陈毅接见俄罗斯社会保障工作者代表团

1957-10-24
第3版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见俄罗斯社会保障工作者代表团
    新华社22日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今日下午接见了以卡普兰诺夫为首的苏联俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国社会保障工作者代表团。
    接见时在座的有内务部副部长王子宜和部长助理杨成森。
