Title: Beijing Prepares Grand Celebration of the October Revolution Anniversary

Author:

Date: 1957-10-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Beijing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a joint meeting on the afternoon of the 12th to discuss the preparatory work for Beijing's grand celebration of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, and adopted the measures for Beijing to celebrate this great festival.

Within the next month, factories, enterprises, government offices, secondary schools and above, agricultural producers' cooperatives, handicraft cooperatives, and other units in Beijing will hold commemorative meetings, report meetings, and discussion meetings, and use various forms such as small newspapers, big-character posters, blackboard newspapers, radio broadcasts, and cultural arts to focus on publicizing the historical significance of the great October Revolution; and closely integrate with the socialist ideological education work currently being carried out, to widely and deeply conduct a propaganda and education campaign on Marxism-Leninism and socialism among cadres of government offices and the masses of the people throughout the city. During this month, the headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing branch will hold a large-scale photo exhibition of "The Life of the Soviet People" and "The Friendship of the Soviet People towards China", and will jointly organize photo exhibitions of "Soviet Stamps" and "Introduction to Soviet Film Week Films" with relevant units. At today's joint meeting, the Preparatory Committee for Beijing's Celebration of the 40th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution was established. Zhang Youyu was appointed as the chairman of the committee.

### 北京准备隆重庆祝十月革命节

1957-10-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京准备隆重庆祝十月革命节
    新华社12日讯　政协北京市委员会和北京市中苏友好协会12日下午召开联席会议，讨论了北京市隆重庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年的筹备工作，通过了北京市庆祝这个伟大节日的办法。
    从现在开始的一个月内，北京市各工厂、企业、机关、中等以上学校、农业生产合作社、手工业合作社等单位，将举行纪念会、报告会、座谈会，以及利用小报、大字报、黑板报、广播、文化艺术等多种多样形式，比较集中地宣传伟大的十月革命的历史意义；并且密切结合目前正在开展的社会主义思想教育工作，广泛深入地向全市机关干部和人民群众进行一次关于马克思列宁主义和社会主义问题的宣传教育工作。在这个月内，中苏友好协会总会和北京市分会，将举办“苏联人民的生活”和“苏联人民对中国的友情”的大型图片展览，并且同有关单位合办“苏联邮票”、“苏联电影周影片介绍”的图片展览。今天联席会议上，成立了北京市庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年筹备委员会。由张友渔任主任委员。
