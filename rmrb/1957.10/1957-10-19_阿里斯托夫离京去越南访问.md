Title: Aristov Leaves Beijing for Visit to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1957-10-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 18th. The head of the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation, A.B. Aristov, and some members of the delegation, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Li Jishen and Li Weihan, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and other members of the committee.

After visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Head of Delegation Aristov and others will return to Beijing. The other members of the delegation will return to China earlier from Beijing in the coming days.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, October 18th. The Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation headed by A.B. Aristov passed through Nanning at noon today on its way to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. After resting in Nanning and touring the city, they continued to Hanoi at 3:30 pm.

Welcoming and seeing them off at the airport were Wei Guoqing, Governor of Guangxi Province, Liu Jianxun, First Secretary of the CPC Guangxi Provincial Committee, and others.

### 阿里斯托夫离京去越南访问

1957-10-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　阿里斯托夫离京去越南访问
    新华社18日讯  苏联最高苏维埃代表团团长阿·鲍·阿里斯托夫和部分团员，今天早晨乘飞机离北京去越南民主共和国进行访问。
    到机场送行的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、李维汉和委员们。
    阿里斯托夫团长等将在访问越南民主共和国以后回到北京，代表团其他团员将在日内先期从北京回国。
    新华社南宁18日电  以阿·鲍·阿里斯托夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团，今天中午在去越南民主共和国访问途中经过南宁。在南宁休息和游览市容后，下午三时半继续飞往河内。
    到机场欢迎欢送的，有广西省省长韦国清，中共广西省委第一书记刘建勋等人。
