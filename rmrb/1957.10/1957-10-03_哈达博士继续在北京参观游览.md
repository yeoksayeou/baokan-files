Title: Dr. Hatta Continues Sightseeing in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Dr. Hatta, the former Vice President of Indonesia, and his entourage continued their sightseeing tour in Beijing this morning.

　　The distinguished guests boated on the lake in Beihai Park and also visited the "Young Pioneer Hydroelectric Power Station" on the lakeside. Here, the station's duty officer presented Dr. Hatta with a red scarf and a commemorative badge for the completion of the hydroelectric power station.

　　At the Niujie Mosque, Dr. Hatta accepted two volumes of elaborately bound "Quran" presented by Da Puchen, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association. Dr. Hatta said that he would regard this gift as a lifelong memento.

　　Dr. Hatta browsed in the International Bookstore for more than an hour and purchased many foreign language books published in China and abroad.

　　The distinguished guests also visited the famous ancient architecture, the Temple of Heaven.

　　At noon, Mayor Peng Zhen hosted a banquet for Dr. Hatta and his wife and entourage.

### 哈达博士继续在北京参观游览

1957-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    哈达博士继续在北京参观游览
    新华社2日讯  印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士及随行人员今天上午继续在北京参观游览。
    贵宾们在北海公园泛舟游湖，还参观了湖畔的“少年先锋队水电站”。在这里，水电站值班员向哈达博士赠献了红领巾和水电站建成纪念章。
    在牛街清真寺，哈达博士接受了中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生赠送的两本精装“古兰经”。哈达博士说，他将把这个赠礼看作毕生不忘的纪念。
    哈达博士在国际书店浏览了一个多小时，选购了许多中国和外国出版的外文书籍。
    贵宾们还参观了有名的古代建筑天坛。
    中午，彭真市长宴请了哈达博士和夫人及随行人员。
