Title: First Oil Refinery in Qaidam Basin Goes Into Production
Author:
Date: 1957-10-02
Page: 2
National Day Gift

    First Oil Refinery in Qaidam Basin Goes Into Production
    Xinhua News Agency, Xining, 1st—The first oil refinery built in the Qaidam Basin officially went into production today. This oil refinery already refined its first batch of crude oil during its trial operation on September 28.
    It took only five months to build this oil refinery, which can produce gasoline, kerosene, diesel and other products. After the refinery is built and put into production, it will strongly support the further development of petroleum exploration in the Qaidam area.

### 柴达木盆地第一座炼油厂投入生产

1957-10-02
第2版()
专栏：国庆献礼

    柴达木盆地第一座炼油厂投入生产
    新华社西宁1日电  在柴达木盆地兴建的第一座炼油厂，今日正式投入生产。这座炼油厂在9月28日进行试运转时，已经炼制出了第一批原油。
    建设这座炼油厂只用了五个月的时间，可以生产汽油、煤油、柴油等产品。炼油厂建成投入生产后，将有力地支援柴达木地区的石油钻探事业获得进一步的发展。
