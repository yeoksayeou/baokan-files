Title: Burmese Goodwill Delegation Departs Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The Burmese goodwill delegation, headed by U Min Thein, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Vice Premier He Long, President Dong Biwu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Assistant Minister Qiao Guanhua, Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming, and Lieutenant General Wu Faxian, among others.

### 缅甸友好代表团离京返国

1957-10-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　缅甸友好代表团离京返国
    新华社11日讯　以吴敏登为首的缅甸友好代表团今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场送行的，有贺龙副总理，董必武院长，外交部副部长章汉夫、部长助理乔冠华，中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和吴法宪中将等多人。
