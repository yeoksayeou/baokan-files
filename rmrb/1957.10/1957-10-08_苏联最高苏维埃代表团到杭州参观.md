Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Visits Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, October 6th: The Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation, headed by Aristov, arrived in Hangzhou at 3:30 this afternoon by special plane from Shanghai. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Sha Wenhan, Governor of Zhejiang Province, Wu Xian, Mayor of Hangzhou, and more than twenty others. In the afternoon, the distinguished guests toured the Three Pools Mirroring the Moon on West Lake and the Western Hills Park. In the evening, Sha Wenhan, Governor of Zhejiang Province, hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团到杭州参观

1957-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团到杭州参观
    新华社杭州6日电　以阿里斯托夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团，今天下午三时半乘专机由上海到达杭州。到机场欢迎的有浙江省省长沙文汉、杭州市市长吴宪等二十多人。下午贵宾们游览了西湖三潭印月和西山公园。晚上，浙江省省长沙文汉设宴招待贵宾。
