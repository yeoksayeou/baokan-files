Title: Compiling Miao Language Books in Western Hunan

Author:

Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency: The first group of Miao language workers in western Hunan, with the help of the Miao language survey team of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, are compiling a "Miao Language Dictionary," a "Miao-Chinese Vocabulary Comparison," and Miao language literacy textbooks for farmers.

The Miao language script is a newly created script, using Latin alphabet phonetic transcription, with only twenty-nine letters, which is simple and easy to learn.

### 湘西编译苗文书籍

1957-10-15
第8版()
专栏：

　　湘西编译苗文书籍
    据新华社讯　湘西第一批苗文工作者在中国科学院苗语调查工作组帮助下，正在编译“苗文字典”、“苗汉词汇对照”和苗文农民识字课本。
    苗族文字是刚创造不久的新文字，它采用拉丁字母拼音，只有二十九个字母，简单易学。
