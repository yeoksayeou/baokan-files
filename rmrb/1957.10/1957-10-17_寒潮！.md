Title: Cold Wave!
Author:
Date: 1957-10-17
Page: 8

　　Cold Wave!
　　This newspaper reports: The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a cold wave warning on the 16th:
　　The cold air mass, originally in the Mongolian People's Republic, has passed through Northwest and North China, etc., and has now moved to the Yangtze River basin, and will continue to advance southward. It is predicted that in the next 24 hours, southern Northeast, the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River, and Zhejiang Province will still have northerly winds of about force six, and the maximum wind force on the river and lake surfaces will still be force eight. The Bohai Sea, Shandong Peninsula, Liaodong Peninsula, and Yellow Sea will still have northerly winds of force six to eight. Fujian, Guangdong, and eastern Guangxi will have northerly winds of force five to six. On the 17th, the wind force in the northern part of the South China Sea will also increase to force five to six, with a northerly wind direction. In the next 12 hours, the western part of Shandong and the Huaihe River basin will still have northerly winds of force five to six. Temperatures in the above areas will drop significantly. After the wind stops and the sky clears on the mornings of the 17th and 18th, the minimum temperature in the entire Northeast region, Shaanxi, Shanxi, and northwestern Hebei will drop to 0°C or below, and there will be frost. The minimum temperature in southern Shaanxi, the Hebei Plain, northern Henan, inland Shandong, northern Jiangsu, and northern Anhui will drop to 5°C or below, and there will be frost.

### 寒潮！

1957-10-17
第8版()
专栏：气象台

　　寒潮！
    本报讯　16日中央气象科学研究所发布寒潮警报：
    原在蒙古人民共和国的冷空气经过我国西北、华北等地区，目前已移到长江流域，以后将继续向南推进。预计未来二十四小时内，东北南部、长江中下游和浙江省仍将有六级左右的偏北风，江面上和湖面上的最大风力仍将有八级。渤海海面、山东半岛、辽东半岛和黄海海面仍将有六—八级的偏北风。福建、广东、广西东部将有五、六级的偏北风。十七日白天，南海北部的风力也将加大到五、六级，风向偏北，未来十二小时内山东西部淮河流域仍将有五、六级的偏北风。以上地区气温都将有显著的下降。17日早晨和18日早晨风停天明以后，东北全区，陕西、山西和河北西北部最低气温将下降到摄氏0°或摄氏0°以下，并将有冰冻出现。陕西南部，河北平原、河南北部、山东内陆、江苏北部、安徽北部最低气温将下降到摄氏5°或摄氏5°以下，并将有霜冻出现。
