Title: Elucidating the Great Significance of the October Revolution; Soviet Union Holds Scientific Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-10-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 22nd dispatch: TASS reports: The Academy of Social Sciences under the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the All-Union Institute of Law held scientific conferences on the 21st, respectively, to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. At the conference of the Academy of Social Sciences, Professor Doroshev, the president of the academy, delivered the opening speech. He elucidated the world historical significance and influence of the October Revolution. Professor Kim made a report entitled "The General Historical Characteristics and National Characteristics of the October Revolution" at the meeting. Corresponding Academician Zhukov of the Soviet Academy of Sciences reported on "The Great October Socialist Revolution and its Impact on International Relations during the General Crisis of Capitalism."
　　This scientific conference will be held for five days.

### 阐述十月革命伟大意义  苏联举行科学会议

1957-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    阐述十月革命伟大意义
    苏联举行科学会议
    据新华社22日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会所属社会科学院和全苏法律研究所21日分别举行科学会议，庆祝十月革命四十周年。在社会科学院的会议上，这个学院的院长多罗谢夫教授致了开幕词。他阐述了十月革命的世界历史意义和影响。基姆教授在会上做了题为“十月革命的一般历史特点和民族特征”的报告。苏联科学院通讯院士茹科夫报告了“伟大十月社会主义革命及其在资本主义总危机时期对国际关系的影响”。
    这次科学会议将举行五天。
