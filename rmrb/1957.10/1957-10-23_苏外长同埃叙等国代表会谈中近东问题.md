Title: Soviet Foreign Minister Discusses Near East Issue with Representatives from Egypt, Syria, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-10-23
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, October 21st (TASS): Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko met with Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar, Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi, the head of the Indian delegation to the United Nations General Assembly, Menon, and the head of the Indonesian delegation, Sastroamidjojo, on the 20th.
    During the meeting, they exchanged views on the Near East situation currently under consideration by the United Nations General Assembly.

### 苏外长同埃叙等国代表会谈中近东问题

1957-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    苏外长同埃叙等国代表会谈中近东问题
    新华社22日讯　塔斯社纽约21日讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯20日会见了叙利亚外交部长比塔尔，埃及外交部长法齐，印度出席联合国大会代表团团长梅农和印度尼西亚代表团团长沙斯特罗阿米佐约。
    他们在会见时，互相就联合国大会正研究的中近东局势问题交换了意见。
