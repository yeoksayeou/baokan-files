Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Visits the Summer Palace
Author:
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation visited the Summer Palace today.

    The distinguished guests ascended the Tower of Buddhist Incense, enjoying a panoramic view of the autumn scenery of the Western Hills; they then took a painted boat for a pleasure cruise on Kunming Lake. Head of the delegation Aristov and some of the delegation members also strolled along the lake embankment with great interest.
    Yesterday, they also visited Beihai Park and Jingshan Park.

### 苏最高苏维埃代表团游颐和园

1957-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    苏最高苏维埃代表团游颐和园
    新华社30日讯  苏联最高苏维埃代表团今天游览了颐和园。
    贵宾们登上了佛香阁，远眺西山秋景；然后乘画舫畅游昆明湖。阿里斯托夫团长并且和一部分团员兴致勃勃地沿湖堤漫步。
    昨天，他们还游了北海，逛了景山。
