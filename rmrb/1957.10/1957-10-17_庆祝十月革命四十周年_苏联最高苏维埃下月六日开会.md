Title: Celebrating the Fortieth Anniversary of the October Revolution: Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to Convene on November 6th

Author:
Date: 1957-10-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 16th, TASS, Moscow, October 16th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union issued an order deciding to convene the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union in Moscow on November 6th to celebrate the fortieth anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

### 庆祝十月革命四十周年  苏联最高苏维埃下月六日开会

1957-10-17
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月革命四十周年
    苏联最高苏维埃下月六日开会
    新华社16日讯　塔斯社莫斯科16日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布命令，决定在11月6日在莫斯科召开苏联最高苏维埃庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年会议。
