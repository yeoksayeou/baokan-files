Title: Good Rain in Central and Southern Shaanxi; Wheat Fields Soaked Three to Four Inches Deep
Author:
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 1

Shaanxi, October 24th (From Our Newspaper) – The central and southern regions of Shaanxi, which had been experiencing drought since the autumn, received welcome rain on the 24th. Rainfall was particularly heavy in areas such as Xi'an, Fuping, Huayin, and Shangxian. As of 9:00 PM on the 24th, rainfall had reached 11 to 24 millimeters, soaking the wheat fields approximately three to four inches deep, greatly encouraging the farmers of Shaanxi who have been persevering in the fight against drought.

### 陕西中部和南部喜雨  麦田渗水三四寸深

1957-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    陕西中部和南部喜雨
    麦田渗水三四寸深
    本报西安24日电　入秋以来，一直干旱的陕西中部和南部地区，24日普降甘霖。西安、富平、华阴、商县等地雨量较大。截至24日二十一时止，降雨量已达到十一到二十四公厘，麦田渗水约有三四寸深，给了坚持抗旱斗争的陕西农民以很大的鼓舞。
