Title: Beijing Grand Gathering Welcomes Distinguished Afghan Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 25th:

    Beijing's people from all walks of life held a grand rally with 8,000 attendees on the afternoon of the 25th to welcome Sardar Mohammed Daoud, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan.
    At 3:00 PM, Prime Minister Daoud, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and others, arrived at the venue adorned with the national flags of China and Afghanistan. Amidst enthusiastic applause and welcoming music, young women from various ethnic groups in the capital presented flowers to the distinguished guests.
    The entire venue erupted in thunderous applause as Prime Minister Daoud addressed the assembly. Prime Minister Daoud said: "I sincerely hope that this friendly visit to China will help promote closer understanding between the Afghan and Chinese peoples and consolidate the friendly relations between our two governments based on common interests, mutual respect, and trust."
    Prime Minister Daoud pointed out: "Even though we may have different concepts of ways of life, the good relations between Afghanistan and China are eternal. This is an outstanding example of mutual understanding and unwavering trust in the friendship between the peoples of all countries, based on our shared desire to maintain peace and international solidarity."
    Prime Minister Daoud reiterated the Kingdom of Afghanistan's independent neutral policy and determination to maintain peace. He said: "Afghanistan's independent and neutral policy in international affairs is based on our desire to be friendly with all countries in the world. In accordance with the wishes of our people, the Afghan government has consistently followed this traditional policy in both smooth and difficult times."
    He also said: "The national aspirations of the people of all countries can only be realized in an atmosphere of peace and security and depend on cooperation, mutual trust, and understanding. The people of all countries have a responsibility to create such conditions, and Afghanistan hopes to maintain mutual relations with China based on this spirit."
    Before Prime Minister Daoud's speech, Mayor Peng Zhen delivered a speech to welcome the distinguished guests. He said: "Afghanistan's adherence to a policy of peace and neutrality and friendship with all countries has had a positive impact on easing international tensions and strengthening understanding and trust between countries. The Chinese government and people express their respect and support for this."
    Peng Zhen also said: "We firmly believe that the people of China and Afghanistan will continue to uphold and promote the spirit of the Bandung Conference and work together to make contributions in safeguarding peace, opposing war, maintaining national independence, and promoting friendship and solidarity among Asian and African countries."
    After Prime Minister Daoud's speech, Mayor Peng Zhen presented a flag and gifts to the distinguished Afghan guests on behalf of the entire people of Beijing. At this moment, music and applause praising the friendship between the two countries once again resounded throughout the venue for a long time.
    Dr. Popal, Acting Minister of Education of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Ambassador to China Samad, Director-General of the General Political Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Pajhwak, and other distinguished Afghan guests were invited to attend the conference.
    Also attending the conference were Vice Premier He Long, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Burhan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Ding Guoyu, as well as the Vice Mayors of Beijing and prominent representatives from all walks of life.
    After the conference, artists from the capital performed excellent songs, dances, and Peking Opera for the distinguished guests.

### 北京盛会欢迎阿富汗贵宾

1957-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    北京盛会欢迎阿富汗贵宾
    新华社25日讯　北京市各界人民八千人25日下午举行盛大集会，欢迎阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德。
    下午三时，达乌德首相由周恩来总理等陪同来到悬挂着中阿两国国旗的会场。在热烈的掌声和一片欢迎乐曲声中，首都各族女青年向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    整个会场以急雨般的掌声欢迎达乌德首相在大会上讲话。达乌德首相说：“我诚恳地希望，这次我到中国进行的友好访问将有助于促进阿富汗人民和中国人民之间的密切了解，和巩固存在于我们两国政府之间的基于共同利益、互相尊重和信任的友好关系。”
    达乌德首相指出：“纵然我们对生活方式的概念有所不同，阿富汗和中国之间的良好关系是永恒的，这是相互了解和坚决信任各国人民之间的友谊的一个突出典范，它是基于我们对维护和平和国际团结的共同愿望。”
    达乌德首相重申阿富汗王国独立的中立政策和维护和平的决心。他说：“阿富汗在国际事务中的独立的和中立的政策是基于我们同世界各国友好的愿望。按照我国人民的愿望，阿富汗政府无论在顺利或艰难的岁月里一直遵循着这个传统的政策。”
    他还说：“世界各国人民的民族愿望只能够在和平和安全的气氛中实现，并且有赖于合作、互相信任和了解。世界各国人民都有责任来创造这样的条件，阿富汗希望同中国保持建立在这个精神的基础上的相互关系。”
    达乌德首相讲话前，彭真市长曾致词欢迎贵宾。他说：“阿富汗所坚持奉行的和平中立以及同一切国家友好的政策，对于缓和国际紧张局势，加强国与国之间的谅解和信任起了良好的影响。中国政府和人民对此表示尊重和支持。”
    彭真还说：“我们深信，中阿两国人民将继续维护和发扬万隆会议的精神，在保卫和平、反对战争，维护民族独立，促进亚非国家友好团结等方面，共同努力，作出贡献。”
    达乌德首相讲话后，彭真市长代表北京市全体人民向阿富汗贵宾献旗赠礼。这时，赞颂两国友谊的一片乐声和掌声再次长时间地响彻整个会场。
    阿富汗王国代理教育大臣波帕尔博士、驻中国大使萨马德、外交部总政治司司长帕日瓦克和其他阿富汗贵宾应邀出席了大会。
    出席大会的，还有副总理贺龙，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰，以及北京市的副市长和各界著名代表人物。
    大会结束后，首都文艺界给贵宾们演出了精采的歌舞和京剧。
