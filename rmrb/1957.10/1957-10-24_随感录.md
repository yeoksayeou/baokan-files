Random Thoughts
    Feng Wen
    When fierce storms arise on the sea, those lurking sea monsters emerge from the dark corners to gloat—they think darkness has arrived. But in the end, when the storms pass—exposed to the sunlight, they hysterically accuse the sea:
      “You deceived us!”
    Only if you always faithfully face the sun will you not lose your way in a storm. Doesn’t the sunflower, which is forever loyal to the sun, keep its head from being turned by the storm, even in the midst of it?

专栏：

    随感录
    冯文
    当海上起了狂风暴雨的时候，那些隐伏着的海怪便从阴暗的角落里幸灾乐祸地游了出来——它们以为黑暗已经来临了。而结果，当狂风暴雨过后——它们裸露于阳光之下时，它们却歇斯底里地责难着大海：
      “你欺骗了我们！”
    只有你永远忠心地面向着太阳，你在暴风雨中才不会迷失方向。那永远忠于太阳的向日葵不是即使在暴风雨中它的头也不为暴风雨所扭转吗？
