Title: Arab Unity Stronger than Baghdad Pact; Nehru Discusses the Syrian Situation

Author:

Date: 1957-10-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 22nd - Nehru said at a press conference on the 21st, in response to a question about the situation in Syria, that recent events have shown that "Arab nationalism has proven to be much stronger than the Baghdad Pact."

He said that the situation is undoubtedly dangerous, in the sense that small conflicts could lead to larger ones.

He pointed out that in Syria, in any case, the real driving force is nationalist sentiment. He said that the Baghdad Pact divided the Arab countries, but in times of crisis, the Arab countries often unite.

Nehru said that disarmament is an extremely important issue. However, universal disarmament cannot be achieved all at once, and must be carried out step by step according to the situation. Therefore, the first and most important step is to suspend nuclear testing.

Nehru said that the Soviet artificial earth satellite demonstrates a great leap in scientific development.

### 阿拉伯团结强过巴格达条约  尼赫鲁谈叙利亚局势问题

1957-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯团结强过巴格达条约
    尼赫鲁谈叙利亚局势问题
    据新华社新德里22日电　尼赫鲁21日在记者招待会上答复关于叙利亚的局势问题时说，最近的事件表明：“阿拉伯民族主义已经证明比巴格达条约强大得多”。
    他说，在小冲突可以导致较大的冲突这个意义上来看，无疑局势是危险的。
    他指出，在叙利亚，无论如何，真正的推动力量是民族主义情绪。他说，巴格达条约分裂了阿拉伯国家，但是在危机的时刻，阿拉伯国家往往团结在一起。
    尼赫鲁说，裁军是一个极端重要的问题。但是普遍裁军不能一下子实现，必须根据情况一步一步地进行。因此，首先和最重要的步骤是暂停核试验。
    尼赫鲁说，苏联的人造地球卫星表明在科学发展方面有了巨大的飞跃。
