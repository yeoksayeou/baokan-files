Construction Dynamics

    Producing Electrical Appliances for Automobiles
    A factory specializing in the production of electrical equipment for automobiles and tractors—the state-owned Changsha Automotive Electrical Appliance Factory—is under intensive construction in Changsha.
    Once completed, the Changsha Automotive Electrical Appliance Factory will have nine workshops, including metal processing, winding, assembly, electroplating painting, insulation heat treatment, stamping, and tool repair. It will adopt the latest assembly lines and technological methods to produce generators, starters, and 17 other products needed by China's automotive and tractor manufacturing industries in large quantities. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 生产汽车用的电器

1957-10-27
第4版()
专栏：建设动态

    生产汽车用的电器
    一座专门生产汽车、拖拉机用电器设备的工厂——国营长沙汽车电器厂，正在长沙市紧张兴建中。
    建成以后的长沙汽车电器厂，将拥有金属加工、绕线、装配、电镀油漆、绝缘热处理、冲压、工具机修等九个车间，并且将采用最新流水作业线和工艺方法，大批生产我国汽车和拖拉机制造工业所需要的发电机、起动器等十七种产品。　（新华社）
