Title: Chairman Mao Congratulates the King of Afghanistan on His Birthday
Author:
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram on the 14th to congratulate the King of Afghanistan on his birthday. The telegram reads as follows:

    To His Majesty Mohammed Zahir, King of the Kingdom of Afghanistan:

    On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to you, and take this opportunity to express my best wishes for your happiness and health.

    May the friendly relations between China and Afghanistan continue to develop.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, October 14, 1957

### 毛主席电贺阿富汗国王寿辰

1957-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席电贺阿富汗国王寿辰
    新华社14日讯　毛泽东主席14日电贺阿富汗王国国王的寿辰，电文如下：
    阿富汗王国国王
    穆罕默德·查希尔陛下：
    欣逢陛下寿辰，我谨向您表示衷心的祝贺，并且借此机会对陛下的幸福和健康表示最良好的愿望。
    祝中阿两国间的友好关系日益发展。
                中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1957年10月14日
