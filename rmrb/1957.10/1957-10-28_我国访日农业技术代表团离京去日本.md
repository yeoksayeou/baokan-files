Title: China's Agricultural Technology Delegation to Japan Leaves Beijing for Japan
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 26th: China's Agricultural Technology Delegation to Japan, led by Head of Delegation Wang Zhen and Deputy Heads of Delegation Liu Chengdong and Wei Zhenwu, departed from Beijing for Japan on the 26th and 24th respectively.

The Chinese Agricultural Technology Delegation to Japan, organized by the Chinese Society of Agronomy, is visiting Japan at the invitation of the Japan Asia Agricultural Exchange Association. The delegation consists of twenty-five people and will conduct a one-and-a-half-month agricultural technology inspection in Japan, and will further exchange technologies and experiences in rice cultivation, animal husbandry, forestry, water conservancy, agricultural machinery, and agricultural economics.

### 我国访日农业技术代表团离京去日本

1957-10-28
第3版()
专栏：

    我国访日农业技术代表团离京去日本
    新华社26日讯　我国访日农业技术代表团在团长王震和副团长刘成栋、魏震五率领下，分别在26日和24日从北京出发，前往日本。
    由中国农学会组织的中国访日农业技术代表团，是应日本亚细亚农业交流协会的邀请前往日本访问的。这个代表团共二十五人，将到日本作为期一个半月的农业技术考察，并将进一步交流水稻栽培和畜牧业、林业、水利、农业机械、农业经济等方面的技术和经验。
