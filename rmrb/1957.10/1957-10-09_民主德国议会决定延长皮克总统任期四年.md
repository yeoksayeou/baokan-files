Title: GDR Parliament Decides to Extend President Pieck's Term by Four Years
Author:
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th (TASS): The People's Chamber and the Chamber of States of the German Democratic Republic held a joint session in Berlin on the 7th to discuss the proposal submitted by various parliamentary groups of the two chambers regarding the extension of the term of office of the President of the German Democratic Republic, Wilhelm Pieck.

    Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Nuschke, spoke at the meeting, explaining the reasons for putting forward this proposal. After the first and second readings, the joint session of the two chambers unanimously decided to extend President Pieck's term of office by four years.

### 民主德国议会决定延长皮克总统任期四年

1957-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国议会决定延长皮克总统任期四年
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社柏林7日讯：德意志民主共和国人民议院和参议院7日在柏林举行联席会议，讨论了两院各党团所提出的关于延长德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克的任期的建议。
    德意志民主共和国副总理奥·努舍克在会上讲话，说明提出这个建议的理由。两院联席会议在一读和二读后，一致决定把皮克总统的任期延长四年。
