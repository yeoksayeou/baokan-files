Title: Signing Ceremony of China-Hungary Joint Statement Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The signing ceremony of the Joint Statement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic was held this afternoon at Ziguang Pavilion, Zhongnanhai, Beijing.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai and Premier Kádár signed the joint statement on behalf of their respective governments.

　　The Chinese side attending the signing ceremony included Vice Premiers Chen Yun and He Long, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Chinese Ambassador to Hungary Hao Deqing, Acting Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Yutian, and Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Li Huichuan.

　　The Hungarian side attending the signing ceremony included Minister of State Marosán, Hungarian Ambassador to China Nográdi, Director of the Intelligence Bureau Szirmai, Deputy Director of the Planning Bureau Vajda, Director of the Asian-African Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Kósa, and Counselor of the Hungarian Embassy in China Sár.

　　After the signing, the two sides toasted each other in congratulations.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Premier Kádár and Premier Zhou Enlai held their final talks in Beijing this morning.

　　The Hungarian side participating in the talks included Minister of State Marosán, Hungarian Ambassador to China Nográdi, and Director of the Intelligence Bureau Szirmai.

　　The Chinese side participating in the talks included Vice Premier He Long, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, and Chinese Ambassador to Hungary Hao Deqing.

### 中匈联合声明签字仪式在京举行

1957-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈联合声明签字仪式在京举行
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国政府和匈牙利人民共和国政府联合声明的签字仪式今天下午在北京中南海紫光阁举行。
    周恩来总理和卡达尔总理分别代表两国政府在联合声明上签字。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有副总理陈云、贺龙，国务院秘书长习仲勋，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长张闻天，中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青，外交部礼宾司代司长王雨田，外交部苏联东欧司副司长李汇川。
    匈牙利方面参加签字仪式的，有国务部部长马罗山，匈牙利驻中国大使诺格拉第，情报局局长西尔莫伊，计划局副局长瓦伊，外交部亚非处处长科什，匈牙利驻中国大使馆参赞沙尔。
    签字后，双方互相敬酒祝贺。
    新华社4日讯　卡达尔总理和周恩来总理今天上午在北京举行了最后一次会谈。
    匈牙利方面参加会谈的，有国务部部长马罗山，匈牙利驻中国大使诺格拉第，情报局局长西尔莫伊。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有贺龙副总理，张闻天副部长和中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青。
