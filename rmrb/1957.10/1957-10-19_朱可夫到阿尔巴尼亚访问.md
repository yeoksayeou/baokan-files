Title: Zhukov Visits Albania
Author:
Date: 1957-10-19
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, October 18th: Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov, accompanied by senior Soviet army and naval generals, arrived in Tirana by special plane on the 17th at noon.

　　Welcoming them at the airport were First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Shehu, and Minister of Defense, General Baluku, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries.

　　Baluku and Zhukov delivered speeches at the airport. Zhukov said that the friendship between the peoples of the Soviet Union and Albania had been particularly strengthened in the common struggle against fascism during World War II, and since Albania became a socialist country. The October Socialist Revolution opened a new era in human history. We are all united in the socialist family.

　　On the same day, Zhukov paid visits to Albanian state and party leaders, including Lleshi, Shehu, and Hoxha, and also visited General Baluku.

### 朱可夫到阿尔巴尼亚访问

1957-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　朱可夫到阿尔巴尼亚访问
    新华社地拉那18日电  苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅和随行的苏联陆、海军高级将领，17日中午乘专机到达地拉那。
    在机场上欢迎的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第一书记霍查、部长会议主席谢胡和国防部长巴卢库上将以及各国外交使节等。
    巴卢库和朱可夫在机场上先后致词。朱可夫说，苏阿两国人民的友谊在第二次世界大战反对法西斯的共同斗争中，以及自从阿尔巴尼亚成为社会主义国家以来，已经特别加强了。十月社会主义革命开辟了人类历史的新纪元。我们都团结在社会主义大家庭当中。
    当天，朱可夫拜会了阿尔巴尼亚国家和党的领导人列希、谢胡、霍查等，另外还拜会了巴卢库上将。
