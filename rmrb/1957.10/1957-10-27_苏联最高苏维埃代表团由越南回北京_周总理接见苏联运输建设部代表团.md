Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Returns to Beijing from Vietnam; Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Soviet Ministry of Transport Construction Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 26th: The head of the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation, Aristov, and some members of the delegation, accompanied by Wu Kejian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, returned to Beijing by special plane from Nanning at 5:10 p.m. on the 26th after visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: Premier Zhou Enlai received all members of the delegation headed by Soviet Minister of Transport Construction Kozhevnikov on the 24th, who were invited to China to attend the completion and opening ceremony of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge.

Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways, was present at the meeting.

Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, and Sirin, a Soviet expert from the Wuhan Bridge Engineering Bureau, were also present.

The delegation also attended a reception held by Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin that day.

The delegation left Beijing by plane on the 25th to return home.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团由越南回北京  周总理接见苏联运输建设部代表团

1957-10-27
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团由越南回北京
    周总理接见苏联运输建设部代表团
    据新华社26日讯　苏联最高苏维埃代表团团长阿里斯托夫和部分团员在访问越南民主共和国后，26日下午五时十分由全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长吴克坚陪同从南宁乘专机回到北京。
    据新华社25日讯　周恩来总理24日接见了应邀来我国参加武汉长江大桥落成通车典礼的以苏联运输建设部部长科热夫尼阔夫为首的代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的有铁道部部长滕代远。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金和武汉大桥工程局苏联专家西林也在座。
    代表团当天还出席了苏联驻华大使尤金举行的招待会。
    代表团在25日乘飞机离开北京回国。
