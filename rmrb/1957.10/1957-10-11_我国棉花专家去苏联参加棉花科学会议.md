Title: China's Cotton Experts Go to the Soviet Union to Attend Cotton Science Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-10-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - At the invitation of the Soviet government, China sent six cotton experts and others to the Soviet Union to participate in the Cotton Science Conference. They left Beijing by plane for the Soviet Union on the morning of the 10th.

### 我国棉花专家去苏联参加棉花科学会议

1957-10-11
第3版()
专栏：

    我国棉花专家去苏联参加棉花科学会议
    据新华社10日讯　我国应苏联政府的邀请，派赴苏联参加棉花科学会议的棉花专家等六人，于10日晨乘飞机离北京去苏联。
