Title: Homage to China
Author: Gent Weissenborn
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 8

    Homage to China
    Gent Weissenborn, West Germany
    From Europe's narrow world,
    From Europe's narrow world, crisscrossed with borders,
    We send our homage to China,
    Like a flock of doves, shining brightly in the morning light,
    Carrying the love of us all,
    Skimming across the frozen Lake Baikal,
    Over the grasslands and valleys of Mongolia,
    Flying over the yellow Gobi Desert,
    Whirling in dust-laden storms;
    Crossing the imperial Great Wall,
    Flying to Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou,
    Flying to the Yellow River and the Yangtze River,
    Flying to the workers and peasants of Changsha,
    Flying to the youth and women of China,
    Flying to Mao Zedong, who led the revolution to victory
    And is beloved by the people,
    Who said:
    Every Chinese person,
    Inherits the good traditions of the past with one hand,
    And bears the mission of the new era with the other,
    Never allowing our two hands to oppose each other,
    Nor belittling either hand,
    But gently holding them together.
    Great China,
    A joyful and inspiring paradise,
    A composed and rational nation!
    The greatest people on earth,
    Having cast off poverty,
    Marching boldly from their past,
    Into the midst of the world's peoples,
    Raising questions to the decaying powers;
    Who are all stunned into silence,
    Gazing towards Asia:
    A giant has risen on the banks of the Yangtze!
    This is the most extensive collective of people in history,
    Possessing the world's oldest culture,
    This is a nation with a kind face,
    Who has liberated itself,
    Having endured countless hardships,
    Full of reason, showing a smile,
    Coming towards us,
    Like a brother.
    Let us open all the doors,
    To welcome him in!
            (Translated by Song Zhonghuang, revised by Feng Zhi)

### 向中国致敬
根特·魏森堡
1957-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    向中国致敬
    西德  根特·魏森堡
    从欧洲狭窄的天地，
    从欧洲布满边境线的狭窄天地，
    把我们的致敬送到中国，
    像一群在晨光中飞翔耀眼的鸽子，
    带着我们大家的敬爱，
    掠过冰封的贝加尔湖，
    越过蒙古的草原和山谷，
    飞过黄色的戈壁沙漠，
    在灰尘扑扑的风暴中回旋；
    越过了帝王的万里长城，
    飞到北京，上海，广州，
    飞向黄河和长江，
    飞向长沙的工人和农民，
    飞向中国的青年和妇女，
    飞到领导革命胜利，
    为人民所爱戴的毛泽东那里，
    他说：
    每个中国人，
    都一只手继承过去的优良传统，
    另一只手担当新时代的使命，
    决不使我们的双手对立，
    也不轻视任何一只手，
    却是轻柔地握在一起。
    伟大的中国，
    欢欣鼓舞的乐土，
    沉着而有理智的国家呀！
    地球上最伟大的人民，
    摆脱了贫困，
    雄健地从他的过去走出来，
    走到各国人民中间，
    向没落的列强提出质问；
    他们都惊讶得哑口无言，
    向着亚洲凝视：
    一个巨人在扬子江畔站起来了！
    这是有史以来最广大的人民集体，
    有着世界最古老的文化，
    这是一个有着善良的面貌的民族，
    他解放了自己，
    经历了无数的苦难，
    充满理智，现出微笑，
    向我们走来，
    像一个弟兄。
    让我们把所有的门敞开吧，
    迎接他进来！
            （宋钟璜译、冯至校）
