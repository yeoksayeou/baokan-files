Title: Ministry of Aquatic Products Rewards a Group of Model Fishery Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1957-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Ministry of Aquatic Products, based on its review of the 1956 model fishery cooperatives for increased production, rewarded on the 21st a group of cooperatives from five provinces, including Guangdong and Zhejiang, for their significant achievements in fishing and aquaculture.

The recipients of the awards are: Shajing Township Advanced Aquaculture Cooperative in Bao'an County, Guangdong Province (Director Chen Gan Chi), Haiou Fishery Cooperative in Zhaicheng Township, Yangjiang County (Director Lin Ju Rui), and Shatou Agricultural Cooperative in Jun'an Township, Shunde County (Director Li Qi Bin); Lujiazhi Fishery Cooperative in Putuo County, Zhejiang Province (Director Guo Qin Zai); Guanzhushan Fishery Cooperative in Zihu Township, Yuanjiang County, Hunan Province (Director Bian Tai Qing); Changjiang Advanced Fishery Cooperative in Daoyu Township, Poyang County, Jiangxi Province (Director Deng Ming Quan); Xinguang Fishery Cooperative in Anshan Township, Liangshan County, Shandong Province (Director Guo Guang Qin), Dayudao Fishery Cooperative in Shidao Town, Rongcheng County (Director Yuan Suo Fu), and Huashiquan Fishery Cooperative in Magezhuang Township, Penglai County (Director Cui De Li).

These cooperatives have achieved remarkable increases in production. For example, the advanced aquaculture production cooperative has 8,100 mu of aquaculture area, averaging 160 jin of cooked oyster meat per mu, exceeding the local yield by 18.52%. The Shatou Agricultural Cooperative averaged 633.4 jin of fish per mu of fish farming area, exceeding the local yield by 15.52%. The Lujiazhi Fishery Production Cooperative averaged 128.42 dan of fish and shrimp produced per worker, exceeding the local yield by 28.42%. The Xinguang Fishery Production Cooperative averaged 3,691 jin of fish caught per boat, exceeding the local yield by 9.4%.

The Ministry of Aquatic Products decided to issue a patriotic bumper harvest certificate and a bonus of 200 yuan to each high-yield cooperative, and a patriotic bumper harvest medal to each director.

### 水产部奖励一批模范渔业社

1957-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    水产部奖励一批模范渔业社
    新华社讯　水产部根据对1956年度渔业增产模范合作社的审定，21日奖励了广东、浙江等五省对捕鱼和养殖水产品成绩巨大的一批合作社。
    受到奖励的有：广东省宝安县沙井乡高级?业合作社（社主任陈淦池）、阳江县闸城镇海鸥渔业合作社（社主任林举瑞）和顺德县均安乡沙头农业社（社主任李其彬），浙江省普陀县鲁家峙渔业合作社（社主任郭钦再），湖南省沅江县子湖乡管竹山渔业合作社（社主任边太清），江西省鄱阳县道?乡昌江渔业高级社（社主任邓明泉），山东省梁山县安山乡新光渔业社（社主任郭广勤）、荣城县石岛镇大鱼岛渔业社（社主任原所福）和蓬莱县马格庄乡花石圈渔业社（社主任崔德立）。
    这些合作社增产成绩显著。例如，高级?业生产合作社养?面积八千一百亩，平均每亩产煮熟?肉一百六十市斤，高出当地产量18.52％。沙头农业社平均每亩养鱼面积产鱼六百三十三点四市斤，高出当地产量15.52％。鲁家峙渔业生产合作社每个劳动力平均生产鱼虾一百二十八点四二担，高出当地产量28.42％。新光渔业生产社每船平均捕鱼三千六百九十一市斤，高出当地产量9.4％。
    水产部决定给各丰产合作社发给爱国丰产奖状一张，奖金二百元，各社主任均发给爱国丰产奖章一枚。
