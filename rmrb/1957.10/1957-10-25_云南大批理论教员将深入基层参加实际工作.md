Title: A Large Number of Theory Teachers in Yunnan Will Go Deep into the Grassroots to Participate in Practical Work
Author: Fan Renxiang
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 2

    Yunnan A Large Number of Theory Teachers Will Go Deep into the Grassroots to Participate in Practical Work
    (Our Dispatch) In order to better implement the educational policy of integrating theory with practice and do a good job in Marxist-Leninist education, Yunnan Province has drawn more than 300 theoretical education cadres to go to the grassroots to participate in practical work. The first and second groups of cadres are scheduled to depart in October and November respectively. In the future, theory teachers will spend three months each year participating in practical work at the grassroots level.

### 云南大批理论教员将深入基层参加实际工作
范仁乡
1957-10-25
第2版()
专栏：

    云南大批理论教员将深入基层参加实际工作
    本报讯　为了更好的贯彻理论联系实际的教育方针，做好马克思列宁主义的教育工作，云南省抽调三百多理论教育干部到基层去参加实际工作。第一批和第二批干部，定于10月和11月分别出发。今后，理论教员每年都要有三个月的时间到基层参加实际工作。
