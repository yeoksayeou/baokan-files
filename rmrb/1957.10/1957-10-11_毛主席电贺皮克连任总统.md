Title: Chairman Mao Congratulates Pieck on Re-election as President
Author:
Date: 1957-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, congratulating him on his re-election as President. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic:

    On the occasion of your re-election as President of the German Democratic Republic, please allow me, on behalf of the Chinese people and in my personal name, to extend to you my heartfelt and warm congratulations.

    Comrade President, the extension of your term of office once again proves the German Democratic Republic's people's boundless trust and sincere love for you.

    I wish you new achievements in unifying Germany on the principles of peace and democracy, in building socialism, and in defending European and world peace.

                Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, October 9, 1957

### 毛主席电贺皮克连任总统

1957-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席电贺皮克连任总统
    新华社10日讯　毛泽东主席致电德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克，祝贺他连任总统，电文如下：德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克同志：
    在你连任德意志民主共和国总统的时候，请允许我代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，向你表示衷心的热烈的祝贺。
    总统同志，你的任期的延长，再一次证明了德意志民主共和国人民对你的无限的信任和真挚的爱戴。
    祝你为在和平民主原则上统一德国，在建设社会主义和保卫欧洲和世界和平的事业中取得新的成就。
                中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1957年10月9日
