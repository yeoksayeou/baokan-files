Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi Receive Egyptian Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th – Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Egyptian Youth Delegation headed by Hosni Said this morning.

    Those present at the reception included Liu Xiyuan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, Zhang Chao, Secretary-General, Wu Xueqian, Director of the International Liaison Department, and Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

    The Egyptian Ambassador to China, Ragab, was also present at the reception.

### 周总理陈毅副总理接见埃及青年代表团

1957-10-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　周总理陈毅副总理接见埃及青年代表团
    新华社11日讯　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理今天上午接见了以侯赛尼·赛义德为首的埃及青年代表团。
    接见时在座的有中华全国民主青年联合会副主席刘西元、秘书长张超、国际联络部部长吴学谦和外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华等。
    接见时，埃及驻华大使拉加卜也在座。
