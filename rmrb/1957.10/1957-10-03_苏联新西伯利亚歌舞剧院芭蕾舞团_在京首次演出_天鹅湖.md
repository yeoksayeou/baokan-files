Title: The Soviet Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theater Performs "Swan Lake" for the First Time in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1957-10-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Soviet State Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theater began their Beijing performances tonight at the Capital Theater with the large-scale ballet "Swan Lake", receiving a warm welcome from over 1,300 spectators.

Chen Yun, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Lu Dingyi, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Le Songsheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and other prominent figures from all walks of life in Beijing watched the performance.

Dr. Hatta, former Vice President of Indonesia, and his wife, who are currently visiting Beijing, as well as their entourage, also attended the performance.

Also invited to watch tonight's performance were Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to China, and members of the British Rambert Ballet and the Mexican National Modern Ballet.

Chen Yun met with Bugachev, head of the ballet troupe, and Zelimanov, deputy head, during the intermission of the performance. Guo Moruo, Shen Yanbing, Chu Tunan, and Ambassador Yudin were present at the meeting. After the performance, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Shen Yanbing, Qian Junrui, and Ambassador Yudin went to the stage to shake hands with the Soviet artists. Young Pioneers and dancers from China, as well as the head and actors of the Mexican National Modern Ballet, went on stage to present flowers to the Soviet artists. Chen Yun and others also took photos with the Soviet and Mexican dance artists. (Attached picture)

### 苏联新西伯利亚歌舞剧院芭蕾舞团  在京首次演出“天鹅湖”

1957-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联新西伯利亚歌舞剧院芭蕾舞团
    在京首次演出“天鹅湖”
    新华社2日讯  苏联国立新西伯利亚歌舞剧院芭蕾舞团今天晚间在首都剧场以大型舞剧“天鹅湖”开始了他们在北京的演出，受到一千三百多观众的热烈欢迎。
    中共中央副主席、国务院副总理陈云、副委员长郭沫若、中共中央宣传部部长陆定一、文化部部长沈雁冰、中苏友好协会总会秘书长钱俊瑞、中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、北京市副市长乐松生以及北京各界著名人士等观看了演出。
    正在北京访问的印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士和夫人以及随行人员也到场观看了演出。
    应邀观看今晚演出的还有苏联驻中国大使尤金以及各国驻华使节和英国蓝伯特芭蕾舞团、墨西哥民族现代芭蕾舞剧团的人员。
    陈云在演出休息时接见了芭蕾舞团团长布加乔夫和副团长泽利马诺夫等。接见时在座的有郭沫若、沈雁冰、楚图南，以及尤金大使。在演出结束以后，陈云、郭沫若、沈雁冰、钱俊瑞以及尤金大使等到舞台上和苏联艺术家们握手。中国少年先锋队员和舞蹈演员们、墨西哥民族现代芭蕾舞剧团的团长和演员们都走上舞台向苏联艺术家们献花。陈云等还和苏联及墨西哥的舞蹈艺术家们一起照了像。（附图片）
