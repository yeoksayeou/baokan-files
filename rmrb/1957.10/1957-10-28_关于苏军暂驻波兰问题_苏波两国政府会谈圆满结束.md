Title: On the Issue of the Temporary Stationing of Soviet Troops in Poland: Soviet-Polish Governmental Talks Successfully Concluded
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 5

    On the Issue of the Temporary Stationing of Soviet Troops in Poland
    Soviet-Polish Governmental Talks Successfully Concluded
    Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: TASS reports from Moscow on October 27th that a communiqué has been released regarding the Warsaw talks on the temporary stationing of Soviet troops in Poland.
    The communiqué states that, in accordance with the Soviet-Polish treaty of December 17th of last year concerning the legal status of Soviet troops stationed in Poland, the Soviet and Polish governments held talks in Warsaw on the issue of mutual legal assistance related to the temporary stationing of Soviet troops in Poland.
    The talks were conducted in an atmosphere of friendship and mutual understanding, and as a result, a corresponding agreement was signed on October 26th.
    The communiqué stated that the signing of this agreement is proof of both sides' determination to establish Soviet-Polish relations in a spirit of fraternal friendship and based on the principle of full respect for sovereignty.

### 关于苏军暂驻波兰问题  苏波两国政府会谈圆满结束

1957-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    关于苏军暂驻波兰问题
    苏波两国政府会谈圆满结束
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社报道：莫斯科27日公布了关于苏军暂时留驻波兰问题的华沙会谈公报。
    公报说，根据去年12月17日苏波关于驻波苏军的法律地位的条约，苏波两国政府在华沙就有关苏军暂时留驻波兰的相互法律援助问题举行了会谈。
    会谈是在友好和相互谅解的气氛中进行的，会谈结果，在10月26日签订了相应的协定。
    公报说，这个协定的签订，是双方决心本着兄弟友好的精神和根据完全尊重主权的原则来建立苏波之间的相互关系的证明。
