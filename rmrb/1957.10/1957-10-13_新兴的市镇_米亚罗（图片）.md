　　The Emerging Town—Miaruo
    On the Cheng-Aba Highway in Sichuan Province, leading to Shua Jing Si in the Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, there is an emerging town—Miaruo. In the past, this was a place frequented by wild animals. After the Cheng-Aba Highway opened to traffic in 1953, it gradually became a bustling market town. Now, there are hospitals, cinemas, bookstores, department stores, and a newly built timber processing plant here.

### 新兴的市镇——米亚罗（图片）
朱玉
1957-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　新兴的市镇——米亚罗
    四川省通往阿坝藏族自治州刷经寺的成阿公路上，有一个新兴的市镇——米亚罗。过去这里是一个野兽出没的地方。1953年成阿公路通车后，慢慢地变成了热闹的集镇，现在这里有医院、电影院、书店、百货公司和新修起的木材加工厂。
        朱玉摄
