Title: Let Us Cheer
Author: Jin Fan
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 8

    Let us cheer!
    The first to generate electricity with atomic energy,
    The first to produce an intercontinental missile,
    The first artificial satellite
    Is orbiting the Earth!
    Soviet comrades, the vanguard of mankind,
    Are striding with the steps of the October Revolution,
    To the forefront of world science!
    The first artificial satellite, the Soviet-made satellite,
    Ascended from Moscow to the high altitude,
    This satellite passed over the Siberian forest,
    This satellite crossed the equator's scorching clouds,
    This satellite passed Guangzhou, crossed over India,
    This satellite flew over Paris, grazed London,
    This satellite passed over Egypt and the storm-swept desert,
    This satellite passed over Hollywood and panic-stricken Washington!
    This satellite carries songs under the red flag,
    To the mountains, to the oceans, to the people of the world,
    Announcing the news of the victory of socialism!
    Do you hear it? The radio waves transmitted from high altitude?
    Do you hear it? The crackling sound from the satellite!
    Do you see it? A small sphere moving in the sky,
    Shining with sunlight, passing through morning and dusk!
    Let us cheer! Let us cheer!
    Let us cheer with the loudest laughter!
    Let us cheer for the Soviet Union!
    Let us cheer for the Soviet Union's red scientists!
    Let us cheer for the strength of the peace camp!
    Let us cheer for the socialist system!
    Cheer for the victory of socialism!
    Cheer for the wisdom of mankind!
    Cheer for mankind in conquering nature, exploring the secrets of the universe,
    Cheer for mankind traveling to the moon and Mars!
    Let us cheer!
    Cheer for the victory of the socialist people!

### 让我们欢呼
金帆
1957-10-09
第8版()
专栏：

    让我们欢呼
    金帆
    第一个用原子能发电，
    第一个制成洲际导弹，
    第一个人造卫星
    绕着地球正在运转！
    苏联的同志，人类的先锋队，
    正迈着十月革命的大步，
    走到世界科学的最前面！
    第一个人造卫星，苏联造的卫星，
    由莫斯科向高空飞升，
    这卫星经过西伯利亚的森林，
    这卫星越过赤道炽热的云层，
    这卫星经过广州，越过印度，
    这卫星飞过巴黎，掠过伦敦，
    这卫星经过埃及和卷着风暴的沙漠，
    这卫星经过好莱坞和惊慌失措的华盛顿！
    这卫星带着红旗下的歌声，
    向高山，向海洋，向全世界的人民，
    宣布社会主义胜利的消息！
    你听见吗？那由高空传来的电波？
    你听见吗？那卫星上噼噼噼的声音！
    你看见吗？在天空中移动着小圆球，
    闪耀着阳光，穿过早晨和黄昏！
    让我们欢呼！让我们欢呼！
    让我们用最响亮的笑声来欢呼！
    让我们向苏联欢呼！
    让我们向苏联的红色科学家欢呼！
    让我们为和平阵营的力量而欢呼！
    让我们为社会主义的制度而欢呼！
    欢呼社会主义的胜利！
    欢呼人类的智慧！
    欢呼人类在征服自然、探索宇宙的秘密，
    欢呼人类要到月球和火星上去旅行！
    让我们欢呼！
    欢呼社会主义人民的胜利！
