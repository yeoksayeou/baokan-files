Title: Czechoslovak Parliamentary Delegation and Prague City Delegation Arrive in Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 8th - The Czechoslovak Republic National Assembly Delegation headed by Chairman Fierlinger and the Prague City Central People's Committee Delegation headed by Mayor Svoboda arrived in Hangzhou this afternoon from Shanghai by special plane.

Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušiňák and his wife also accompanied them.

Those who welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport today included Wu Xian, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, First Secretary of the Hangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Mayor of Hangzhou, Zhang Shixiang, Vice Mayor of Hangzhou, Zhou Changsheng, Head of the Zhejiang Garrison, Tang Yuanbing Vice Chairman of the Provincial Political Consultative Conference, and responsible persons from local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

This afternoon, the distinguished guests took a boat tour of the West Lake and strolled around the Three Pools Mirroring the Moon.

In the evening, Wu Xian hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests.

### 捷议会代表团和布拉格市代表团到杭州

1957-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    捷议会代表团和布拉格市代表团到杭州
    新华社杭州8日电　以费林格主席为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会代表团和以斯沃波达市长为首的布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团，今天下午由上海乘专机到达杭州。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使布希尼亚克和夫人也陪同前来。
    今天到机场欢迎贵宾的，有浙江省副省长、中共杭州市委第一书记、杭州市市长吴宪，杭州市副市长张世祥，浙江驻军首长周长胜，省政协副主席汤元炳及各民主党派地方组织和各人民团体负责人。
    今天下午，贵宾们乘船游览了西湖，并在三潭印月漫步。
    晚上，吴宪设宴招待贵宾。
