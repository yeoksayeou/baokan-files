Title: Tensions Rise in Jordanian Politics; Two Cabinet Ministers and Eleven Parliament Members Resign; Army Cuts Off Contact Between West and East

Author:

Date: 1957-10-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd, Amman: Two Jordanian cabinet ministers resigned on October 22nd, and King Hussein subsequently appointed three new cabinet ministers. The resigning ministers were Public Works Minister Ghasib and Education and Justice Minister Hindawi. According to official announcements, their resignations were for "personal" reasons.

The newly appointed ministers are Development and Justice Minister Walid Salah, Education Minister Ahmed Tarawneh, and Public Works Minister Salim Bakht.

According to Western news agencies, Salah and Bakht are both independents, and Tarawneh is the leader of the disbanded Arab Constitutional Party.

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd, TASS Beirut, October 21st: According to Lebanese newspapers, the political situation in Jordan has been increasingly tense recently. "The Telegraph" reported that eleven members of parliament have resigned from parliament. In addition, eight members of parliament are in hiding due to persecution by the authorities.

"Al-Nahar" reported that the Jordanian army cut off contact between western and eastern Jordan on October 21st. Telephone lines between the west and east were cut. Police conducted large-scale arrests among residents, and approximately 800 people were imprisoned.

### 约旦政局紧张  两个阁员和十一个议员辞职  军队切断了西部和东部联络

1957-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    约旦政局紧张
    两个阁员和十一个议员辞职
    军队切断了西部和东部联络
    据新华社23日讯　安曼消息：约旦内阁的两个阁员在10月22日辞职，随后侯赛因国王任命了三个新阁员。辞职的阁员是公共工程大臣加西布和教育兼司法大臣欣达维。据官方宣布，他们的辞职是为了“私人”的理由。
    新任命的阁员是开发兼司法大臣华利德·萨拉赫，教育大臣艾哈迈德·塔劳纳，公共工程大臣萨利姆·巴赫特。
    据西方通讯社报道，萨拉赫和巴赫特都是独立人士，塔劳纳是已被解散的阿拉伯制宪党领袖。
    新华社22日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特21日讯：据黎巴嫩报纸报道，最近约旦政治局势日益紧张。“电讯报”报道有十一个议员向议会辞职。另外，有八个议员由于当局的迫害而躲藏起来。
    “邻人报”报道，约旦军队在10月21日切断了约旦西部和东部的联络。东西部之间的电话线被切断了。警察在居民中间进行了大规模的逮捕，约有八百人被关进牢狱。
