Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Sudanese Peace Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet today to welcome the entire Sudanese Peace Delegation headed by Hashib, a judge of the Sudanese High Court.

Among those present were leading members of relevant organizations, including Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Da Purbu, Zhang Zhiran, Cai Ziwei, Xu Baoju, and Fang Shishan.

### 郭沫若宴请苏丹和平代表团

1957-10-10
第3版()
专栏：

    郭沫若宴请苏丹和平代表团
    新华社9日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天设宴欢迎以苏丹高等法院法官哈希布为首的苏丹和平代表团全体团员。
    出席作陪的有有关团体负责人廖承志、刘宁一、达浦生、张志让、蔡子伟、许宝驹、方石珊等人。
