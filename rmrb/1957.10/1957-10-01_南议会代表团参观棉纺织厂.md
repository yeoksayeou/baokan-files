Title: South Parliament Delegation Visits Cotton Textile Factory
Author:
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The Yugoslav Federal National Assembly delegation, headed by Chairman Stanbolic, visited the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory this morning. When the distinguished guests arrived, they were warmly welcomed by the factory's female director, Song Ting, and more than a thousand employees holding bouquets of flowers who lined the route.

The distinguished guests toured the factory's entire production process with great interest. During the visit, Chairman Stanbolic highly praised the factory with its 100,000 spindles for its modernized production and the excellent ventilation, dust extraction, and labor protection equipment in the factory buildings. When Chairman Stanbolic heard the director introduce that all these machines and equipment were manufactured in our own country, he expressed his delight. The guests also visited a nursery in the factory.

### 南议会代表团参观棉纺织厂

1957-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    南议会代表团参观棉纺织厂
    据新华社30日讯  以斯坦鲍利奇主席为首的南斯拉夫联邦国民议会代表团，今天上午参观了国营北京第二棉纺织厂。当贵宾们到达的时候，受到这个工厂的女厂长宋汀和一千多名手拿花束的职工热烈夹道欢迎。
    贵宾们以极大兴趣参观了这个工厂的整个生产过程。斯坦鲍利奇主席在参观的过程中，很称赞这座具有十万纱锭的工厂的生产近代化，和厂房里良好的通风、吸尘、劳动保护等设备。当斯坦鲍利奇主席听到厂长介绍这些机器设备全是我国自己制造的时候，表示很高兴。客人们还参观了这个工厂的一个托儿所。
