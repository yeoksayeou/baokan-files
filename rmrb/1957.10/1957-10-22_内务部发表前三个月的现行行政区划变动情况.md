Title: Ministry of Internal Affairs Announces Current Changes in Administrative Divisions for the First Three Months
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-22
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 20th — The Ministry of Internal Affairs announced the following changes in the current administrative divisions of counties, cities, and above in the country for July, August, and September 1957:

    Heilongjiang Province:
    The city of Yichun was established, and Yichun County was abolished. The administrative area of the former Yichun County is the administrative area of the city. The city's People's Committee is stationed in Yichun Town.
    Gansu Province:
    Guangtong County of the Linxia Hui Autonomous Prefecture was renamed Guanghe County.
    Zhejiang Province:
    I. The Taizhou Commissioner's Office was established. Its jurisdiction includes the five counties of Linhai, Ninghai, Tiantai, Xianju, and Sanmen from the original Ningbo Special District, and the two counties of Wenling and Huangyan from the original Wenzhou Special District. The Commissioner's Office is stationed in Linhai County.
    II. The two counties of Yuhang and Lin'an from the original Jiaxing Special District were placed under the leadership of the Jiande Special District; Zhuji County from the original Jinhua Special District and Xiaoshan County, originally under the direct jurisdiction of the province, were placed under the leadership of the Ningbo Special District.
    III. Hang County, originally under the direct jurisdiction of the province, was entrusted to the leadership of Hangzhou City.
    Hunan Province:
    The Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture was established, and the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture was abolished. The administrative area of the autonomous prefecture includes the six counties of Jishou, Guzhang, Luxi, Fenghuang, Huayuan, and Baojing from the original Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture, and the four counties of Yongshun, Longshan, Sangzhi, and Dayong, originally under the direct jurisdiction of the province and managed on its behalf by the autonomous prefecture. The People's Committee of the autonomous prefecture is stationed in Jishou County.
    Sichuan Province:
    The seat of Qianning County in the Ganzi Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture was moved from Huiyuan Township to Bamei.
    Guizhou Province:
    The seat of Ceheng County in the Qiannan Buyi and Miao Autonomous Prefecture was moved to Zhe Lou.
    Yunnan Province:
    The Honghe Hani and Yi Autonomous Prefecture was established, and the Mengzi Commissioner's Office and the Honghe Hani Autonomous Region were abolished. The administrative area of the autonomous prefecture includes the eleven counties of Mengzi, Kaiyuan, Jianshui, Shiping, Pingbian, Longwu, Quxi, Hekou, Honghe, Jinping, and Yuanyang, the Mile Yi Autonomous County, and the Six Village Offices from the original Mengzi Special District and the original Honghe Hani Autonomous Region. The People's Committee of the autonomous prefecture is stationed in Mengzi County. After the establishment of the prefecture, Mile Yi Autonomous County was reorganized into Mile County.

### 内务部发表前三个月的现行行政区划变动情况

1957-10-22
第3版()
专栏：

    内务部发表前三个月的现行行政区划变动情况
    新华社20日讯　内务部发表1957年7、8、9月份全国县、市及县、市以上现行行政区划变动情况如下：
    黑龙江省：
    设置伊春市，撤销伊春县。以原伊春县的行政区域为市的行政区域。市人民委员会驻伊春镇。
    甘肃省：
    临夏回族自治州的广通县改名为广河县。
    浙江省：
    一、设置台州专员公署。辖区为：原宁波专区的临海、宁海、天台、仙居、三门五县，和原温州专区的温岭、黄岩二县。专员公署驻临海县城。
    二、原嘉兴专区的余杭、临安二县划归建德专署领导；原金华专区的诸暨县和原省直辖的萧山县划归宁波专署领导。
    三、原省直辖的杭县委托杭州市领导。
    湖南省：
    设置湘西土家族苗族自治州，撤销湘西苗族自治州。自治州的行政区域为原湘西苗族自治州的吉首、古丈、泸溪、凤凰、花垣、保靖六县，和原由省直辖交自治州代管的永顺、龙山、桑植、大庸四县。自治州人民委员会驻吉首县城。
    四川省：
    甘孜藏族自治州的乾宁县县治由惠远乡迁驻八美。
    贵州省：
    黔南布依族苗族自治州的册亨县县治迁驻者楼。
    云南省：
    设置红河哈尼族彝族自治州，撤销蒙自专员公署、红河哈尼族自治区。自治州的行政区域为原蒙自专区和原红河哈尼族自治区的蒙自、开远、建水、石屏、屏边、龙武、曲溪、河口、红河、金平、元阳等十一县和弥勒彝族自治县，六村办事处。自治州人民委员会驻蒙自县城。弥勒彝族自治县在建州后改建为弥勒县。
