Title: An Old Bridge Builder
Author: Hong Yang
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 8

　　An Old Bridge Builder
　　Hong Yang
　　A voice like a great bell,
　　Bold words shake mountains and rivers;
　　People ask you how many bridges you've built in your life?
　　"Big and small, more than three hundred"!
　　When the first train passes over the bridge,
　　Waves surge outside the train,
　　Wrinkles deepen on your face,
　　Within the deep wrinkles, a smile arises.
　　"Although it's more than three hundred,
　　None can compare to this one"!
　　Having said that, he raises his head to look out the window,
　　The train rumbles loudly.
　　"This great iron bridge,
　　Is just like our new China;
　　Tall, long, and strong!
　　The greatest! The most beautiful!
　　Don't mind my white hair,
　　I am a youth of the new China;
　　I must work hard to build bridges,
　　Building them to the shores of Communism."

### 一个老桥梁工
洪洋
1957-10-15
第8版()
专栏：

　　一个老桥梁工
　　洪洋
　　声音若宏钟，
　　豪语壮山河；
　　人问你一生修了多少桥？
　　“大、小何止三百座”！
　　第一辆列车从桥上过，
　　车外波涛涌，
　　你脸上皱纹一重重，
　　重重皱纹中，笑意生。
　　“虽说三百多，
　　抵不了这一座！”
　　说罢扬头向窗外，
　　车声轰隆隆。
　　“这座大铁桥，
　　好比我们新中国；
　　又高又长又坚牢！
　　最伟大！最美！
　　别看我头发白，
　　我是新中国的青年；
　　我要发奋来修桥，
　　修到共产主义的彼岸。”
