Title: Finnish Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-10
Page: 3
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. A seven-member Finnish government trade delegation, led by Mr. Helanius, Director of the Commercial Affairs Department of the Finnish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Beijing today.

### 芬兰政府贸易代表团到北京

1957-10-10
第3版()
专栏：

    芬兰政府贸易代表团到北京
    新华社9日讯　芬兰政府贸易代表团一行七人在芬兰外交部商务司司长海莱纽斯率领下，已经在今天到达北京。
