Title: Soviet Union is Filming a Movie About the First Artificial Satellite
Author:
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 14th. TASS reports that the Soviet Ministry of Culture told TASS reporters that the Soviet Union will make a documentary and film collection of the world-significant event of the launch of the first artificial satellite.
　　A documentary about the science and technology of the Soviet Union's creation of the Earth satellite is scheduled to be completed before November. The popular science color film "The Road to the Stars" is in production. This film tells about the problems of interstellar travel and the writings of the outstanding Russian scientist Tsiolkovsky.
　　Currently, Soviet publishers are preparing to publish a series of pamphlets and books on interstellar transportation issues. Clubs, cultural palaces, reading rooms, libraries, and planetariums are busy these days organizing lectures on the scientific and technological achievements of the Soviet Union in the study of the universe, seminars with scientists, question-and-answer evenings, exhibitions, and Q&A sessions.

### 苏联正摄制关于第一颗人造卫星的影片

1957-10-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联正摄制关于第一颗人造卫星的影片
    新华社14日讯据塔斯社报道，苏联将把发射第一颗人造卫星这个具有世界意义的事件摄制成纪录片和电影专辑，这是苏联文化部向塔斯社记者发表谈话时说的。
    关于纪录苏联制成地球卫星的科学和技术的纪录片决定在11月以前摄制成功。科普彩色片“通向星球的道路”正在进行摄制中。这部影片叙述关于星际旅行的问题，关于俄国杰出科学家齐奥尔科夫斯基的著作。
    目前，苏联各出版社正在准备出版有关星际交通问题的一系列小册子和书籍。各俱乐部、文化宫、阅览室、图书馆以及天文馆这些日子来都忙于组织关于苏联在研究宇宙方面的科学和技术成就的讲演会、同科学家的座谈会、问答晚会、展览会和疑难解答会。
