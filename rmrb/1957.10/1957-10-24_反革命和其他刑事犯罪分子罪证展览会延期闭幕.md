Title: Exhibition of Evidence of Crimes by Counter-Revolutionaries and Other Criminals to Be Extended
Author:
Date: 1957-10-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 21st: The "Exhibition of Evidence of Crimes by Counter-Revolutionary Elements and Other Criminal Offenders" held by the Ministry of Public Security will be extended to the end of November.

The exhibition opened on September 20th. In the month since it opened, the number of visitors has reached more than 349,000.

### 反革命和其他刑事犯罪分子罪证展览会延期闭幕

1957-10-24
第3版()
专栏：

    反革命和其他刑事犯罪分子罪证展览会延期闭幕
    新华社21日讯　公安部举办的“反革命分子和其他刑事犯罪分子罪证展览会”将延期到11月底闭幕。
    这个展览会是在9月20日开幕的。在展出后的一个月中，前来参观的人数已经有三十四万九千多人。
