Title: Chairman Yugov Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Chairman Yugov, leading the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, returned to Beijing by train at 2:20 pm today after visiting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
    Those who welcomed him at the station included Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Vice Minister Zeng Yongquan, the Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, and the Bulgarian and Korean Ambassadors to China.

### 于哥夫主席回到北京

1957-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    于哥夫主席回到北京
    新华社30日讯  由于哥夫主席率领的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问后，今天下午两点二十分乘火车回到北京。
    到车站迎接的，有李先念副总理，曾涌泉副部长，中国驻保加利亚大使，及保加利亚和朝鲜驻中国大使等人。
