Title: US-UK Leaders Begin Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 24th: US President Eisenhower and British Prime Minister Macmillan held their first meeting at the White House on the evening of October 23rd. US Secretary of State Dulles and British Foreign Secretary Lloyd also attended the meeting.

    The meeting was held in secret, and the content of the talks was not announced. It was revealed that Macmillan explained Britain's views on the Middle East issue to Eisenhower during the meeting.

### 美英首脑开始会谈

1957-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    美英首脑开始会谈
    据新华社24日讯　华盛顿消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔和英国首相麦克米伦10月23日晚间在白宫举行了第一次会谈。美国国务卿杜勒斯和英国外交大臣劳埃德也参加了这次会谈。
    这次会谈是秘密举行的，会谈的内容没有宣布。据透露，麦克米伦在会谈中向艾森豪威尔说明了英国对中东问题的看法。
