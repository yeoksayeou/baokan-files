Title: Afghan Prime Minister Daoud Arrives in Kunming
Author:
Date: 1957-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, October 22nd: Sardar Mohammed Daoud, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, and his entourage, arrived in Kunming by special plane at 2:55 pm on the 22nd, upon invitation to visit our country. Abdul Samad, the Kingdom of Afghanistan's ambassador to China, accompanied him.

As the special plane carrying the distinguished guests flew over Kunming Airport, which was adorned with the national flags of China and Afghanistan, more than two thousand people waiting at the airport cheered enthusiastically. As Prime Minister Daoud stepped off the plane, Zhang Chong, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, and Ding Guoyu, our country's Ambassador to the Kingdom of Afghanistan, who had come specially from Beijing to welcome the distinguished guests, and Wang Yutian, Acting Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, stepped forward to shake hands with the distinguished guests to express their welcome.

After reviewing the guard of honor, Prime Minister Daoud addressed the welcoming crowd of more than two thousand people of various nationalities. He said, "As I set foot on Chinese soil for the first time, please allow me, on behalf of the government and people of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, to express my heartfelt respect to the Chinese government and people."

On the evening of the 22nd, Vice Governor Zhang Chong held a banquet to welcome Prime Minister Daoud and his entourage.

Prime Minister Daoud spoke at the banquet. He said that he was very happy to be able to come to our friendly neighbor, China, and to pass through the beautiful city of Kunming. He hoped to take this opportunity to bring the friendship of the Afghan people to the Chinese people. Prime Minister Daoud said that the friendship between the people of the Kingdom of Afghanistan and China is based on a good aspiration, and it is this good aspiration that guides the leaders of our two countries to strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries. Prime Minister Daoud added that Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to the Kingdom of Afghanistan left a very good impression on our country. I hope that our visit will further strengthen the friendly relations between our two countries. The people of our two countries share the desire for world peace, and I hope that this desire will continue to be maintained and developed.

At the beginning of the banquet, Vice Governor Zhang Chong, on behalf of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee and the people of all nationalities in the province, extended a welcome to the distinguished guests.

### 阿富汗首相达乌德到昆明

1957-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗首相达乌德到昆明
    据新华社昆明22日电　应邀来我国访问的阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德和随行人员，22日下午二时五十五分乘专机到达昆明。阿富汗王国驻我国大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德陪同前来。
    当贵宾们乘坐的专机飞临飘扬着中国和阿富汗两国国旗的昆明机场上空时，等候在机场上的两千多群众热烈欢呼。达乌德首相步下飞机时，云南省副省长张冲和特从北京赶来欢迎贵宾的我国驻阿富汗王国大使丁国钰、外交部礼宾司代司长王雨田等上前同贵宾握手表示欢迎。
    在检阅了仪仗队以后，达乌德首相向两千多各民族欢迎群众讲了话。他说：“当我第一次踏上中国国土的时候，请允许我代表阿富汗王国政府和人民向中国政府和人民表示衷心的敬意”。
    22日晚间，张冲副省长举行宴会，欢迎达乌德首相和他的随行人员。
    达乌德首相在宴会上讲了话。他说，我能够来到我们友好的邻邦中国，而且经过昆明这个美丽的城市，感到十分高兴。我希望能借这个机会，把阿富汗人民对中国人民的友谊带给中国人民。达乌德首相说，阿富汗王国与中国两国人民的友谊是在一个良好的愿望的基础上建立起来的，也正是这个良好的愿望，引导着我们两国的领导人来加强两国的友好关系。达乌德首相又说，周恩来总理对阿富汗王国的访问，给我们的国家留下了非常良好的印象。我希望我们这次访问更加加强我们两国的友好关系。我们两国人民都有着需要世界和平的愿望，我希望这种愿望继续保持和发展下去。
    宴会开始的时候，张冲副省长代表云南省人民委员会和全省各族人民向贵宾们表示欢迎。
