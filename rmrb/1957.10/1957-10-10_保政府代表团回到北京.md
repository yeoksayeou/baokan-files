Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Bulgarian People's Republic Government Delegation, headed by Chairman Yugov, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon from Hanoi after concluding its visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and Vietnamese Ambassador to China Nguyen Khang were at the airport to welcome them.

### 保政府代表团回到北京

1957-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    保政府代表团回到北京
    新华社9日讯　以于哥夫主席为首的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团结束对越南民主共和国的访问后，由河内乘飞机在今天下午回到北京。到机场欢迎的，有副总理李先念，外交部副部长姬鹏飞和越南驻中国大使阮康。
