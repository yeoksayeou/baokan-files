Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long Watch Vietnamese Art Troupe Perform Songs and Dances
Author:
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long watched a performance by the art troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Beijing tonight.

During the intermission, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long received Hong Gang, head of the art troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Du Run, deputy head and artistic director. Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Culture, and others were present at the meeting.

Nguyen Khang, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and his wife were also present at the meeting.

The art troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam performed wonderful Vietnamese folk songs, dances, and instrumental solos tonight, receiving a warm welcome from more than 1,400 spectators. After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and others, accompanied by Ambassador Nguyen Khang, went on stage to meet with the Vietnamese artists and took pictures together. ### 周恩来总理　贺龙副总理  看越南文工团表演歌舞

1957-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理　贺龙副总理
    看越南文工团表演歌舞
    新华社8日讯　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理今天晚上在北京观看了越南民主共和国文工团的演出。
    周恩来总理、贺龙副总理在演出休息时，接见了越南民主共和国文工团团长红钢、副团长兼艺术指导杜润。接见时在座的有文化部副部长钱俊瑞等。
    接见时，越南民主共和国驻中国大使阮康和夫人也在座。
    越南民主共和国文工团今晚演出了精彩的越南民间歌舞和器乐独奏，受到一千四百多观众的热烈欢迎。在演出结束以后，周恩来总理、贺龙副总理等由阮康大使陪同上台同越南艺术家们会见，并且在一起照了像。（附图片）
    周恩来总理、贺龙副总理接见越南文工团负责人。自左至右：周恩来总理、杜润副团长、红钢团长、越南驻中国大使阮康、贺龙副总理。
                                新华社记者　白媖摄
