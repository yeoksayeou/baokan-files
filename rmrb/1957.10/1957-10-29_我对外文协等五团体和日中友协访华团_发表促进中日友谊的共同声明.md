Title: The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and Four Other Organizations and the Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation to China Issue a Joint Statement Promoting Sino-Japanese Friendship
Author:
Date: 1957-10-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and the Red Cross Society of China, together with the visiting delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association, signed a joint statement in Beijing on the evening of the 28th on issues concerning enhancing friendship between the peoples of the two countries and promoting the normalization of diplomatic relations between Japan and China.

Signing the joint statement on behalf of the Chinese side were: Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Yang Hansheng, Vice President; Qiao Guanhua, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Zhao Anbo, Advisor to the Red Cross Society of China; and Lin Shixiao and Ni Feijun, Deputy Secretaries-General.

Murase Kiyoshi, Acting Head of the Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation to China, Deputy Heads Kawazaki Natsu and Sugawara Masato, Secretary-General Iwamura Michio, and all members of the delegation signed the joint statement.

Chu Tunan and Murase Kiyoshi both spoke at the end of the signing.

### 我对外文协等五团体和日中友协访华团  发表促进中日友谊的共同声明

1957-10-29
第3版()
专栏：

    我对外文协等五团体和日中友协访华团
    发表促进中日友谊的共同声明
    据新华社28日讯　中国人民对外文化协会、中国人民外交学会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国国际贸易促进委员会、中国红十字会与日中友好协会访华代表团28日晚间在北京签订了关于增进两国人民友谊和促进日中邦交正常化诸问题的共同声明。
    代表中国方面在共同声明上签字的是：中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长阳翰笙，中国人民外交学会副会长乔冠华，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民，中国红十字会顾问赵安博、副秘书长林士笑、倪斐君。
    日中友好协会访华代表团代理团长村濑清，副团长河崎夏、菅原昌人、秘书长岩村三千夫以及全体团员在共同声明上签了字。
    楚图南和村濑清在签字结束的时候都讲了话。
