Title: Xu Guangping Receives Female Representatives of the Egyptian Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation and member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Mrs. Rawya Ateya, a member of the Egyptian Youth Delegation and female member of the Egyptian Parliament, along with Dr. Akila and Mrs. Laila Shukri on the morning of the 12th. Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and Hu Naiqiu and Yang Yunyu, secretaries of the Secretariat, were also present at the reception.

### 许广平接见埃及青年代表团女代表

1957-10-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　许广平接见埃及青年代表团女代表
    新华社12日讯　全国妇女联合会副主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员许广平，12日上午接见了埃及青年代表团的成员、埃及国会女议员拉维娅·阿提娅夫人和阿基拉博士、莱拉·舒克里夫人。接见时，全国妇联主席团委员曹孟君和书记处书记胡耐秋、杨蕴玉也在座。
