Title: Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Mongolian People's Republic Art Troupe, a group of eighty-one people, arrived in Beijing by train tonight for a performance tour in China according to the 1957 executive plan of the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Agreement.

The head of the art troupe is N. Tsegmid, People's Actor of the Mongolian People's Republic and Director of the National Theatre, and the artistic director is Honored Actor Lubsangsharab. The art troupe includes famous singers and dancers from the Mongolian People's Republic.

Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Culture, Tian Han, Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, and Hong Gang, head of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Art Troupe, who is currently visiting Beijing, went to the station to welcome the art troupe. Chinese literary and art workers presented flowers to all members of the art troupe.

Lubusan, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, also went to the station to welcome them.

The art troupe will hold its first performance in China in Beijing on the 11th.

### 蒙古人民共和国艺术团到京

1957-10-10
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国艺术团到京
    新华社9日讯　根据中蒙文化协定1957年执行计划来中国访问演出的蒙古人民共和国艺术团一行八十一人，今天晚上坐火车到北京。
    艺术团团长是蒙古人民共和国人民演员、国家剧院院长尼·策格米德，艺术指导是功勋演员鲁布桑沙拉布。艺术团中包括蒙古人民共和国的著名歌唱家、舞蹈家等。
    文化部副部长张致祥、中国戏剧家协会主席田汉和正在北京访问的越南民主共和国文工团团长红钢等都到车站欢迎艺术团。中国文艺工作者向艺术团全体人员献了花。
    蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使鲁布桑也到车站欢迎。
    艺术团11日将在北京举行访华首次演出。
