Title: Japanese Literary Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. The Japanese literary delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Writers Association, arrived in Beijing by train tonight. The delegation consists of nine members and is led by Kenkichi Yamamoto, director of the Japan PEN Club. They are scheduled to visit China for three weeks.

### 日本文学访华团到京

1957-10-31
第3版()
专栏：

    日本文学访华团到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会和中国作家协会邀请来我国访问的日本文学访华代表团一行九人，今晚乘火车到北京。
    代表团由日本文艺家协会理事山本健吉率领。他们预定在我国访问三个星期。
