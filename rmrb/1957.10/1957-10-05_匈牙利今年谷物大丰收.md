Title: Hungary Achieves a Bumper Grain Harvest This Year
Author:
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 4th: Hungary has achieved a bumper grain harvest throughout the country this year. The average wheat yield is 8.5 quintals per hold (equivalent to 8.55 mu) , rye is 7 quintals, autumn-sown barley is 10.5 quintals, spring-sown barley is 8.5 quintals, and oats are 7.3 quintals. These figures greatly exceed last year's levels. Cultivated crops, grapes, and fruits are also expected to have good harvests. Hungary's agricultural production cooperatives will begin summarizing their agricultural economic year's work starting from November 1st. According to preliminary statistics, members' incomes will increase by 25%-30% compared to last year.

### 匈牙利今年谷物大丰收

1957-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利今年谷物大丰收
    新华社布达佩斯4日电　匈牙利全国各地今年谷物大丰收。每霍尔特（合八点五五市亩）的小麦平均产量是八点五公担，黑麦是七公担，秋播大麦是一○点五公担，春播大麦是八点五公担，燕麦是七点三公担。这些都大大地超过了去年的水平。中耕作物、葡萄和水果也可望丰收。匈牙利的农业生产合作社将从11月1日起开始总结农业经济年度的工作。据初步统计，社员的收入将比去年增加25％—30％。
