Title: Chinese People's Volunteers Defend the Peace Frontline and Assist in Korean Construction; Premier Kim Il-sung Sends Letter of Thanks to General Yang Yong and Others; Chinese People's Volunteers Solemnly Commemorate the Seventh Anniversary of Entering Korea to Fight

Author:

Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 25th: Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, on the eve of the seventh anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, wrote to General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and General Wang Ping, Political Commissar, to express his congratulations.

In his letter, Premier Kim Il-sung said: "On the occasion of welcoming the seventh anniversary of the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, I extend warm congratulations to the comrades and all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers. The Chinese People's Volunteers not only established immortal feats in the past Korean War, but also, after the armistice, under the provocative actions of the US imperialism and the Syngman Rhee clique, who have consistently taken actions to undermine the Korean Armistice Agreement and create international tension, have continued to reliably defend the peace front and have given sincere assistance to our country's socialist construction. In the name of the Korean people, I express my heartfelt gratitude to you and, through you, to all officers and soldiers of the fraternal Chinese People's Volunteers, and wish you brilliant achievements in your future work."

On the same day, General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and General Wang Ping, Political Commissar, wrote to Marshal Kim Il-sung on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Volunteers to express their gratitude.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 25th: Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, on the eve of the seventh anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea—on October 24th, presented a batch of gifts to all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers to express his sympathy. These gifts included a total of 30 tons of rice, apples, and seafood, as well as ten boxes of ginseng wine.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 25th: Various units of the Chinese People's Volunteers solemnly commemorated the seventh anniversary of entering Korea to participate in the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea.

The leading organ of the Volunteers held a commemorative meeting on the afternoon of the 25th. Invited to attend the meeting were a delegation of the Korean People's Army headed by General Choi Jong-hak, Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, and representatives from local party and government organs. Wang Ping, Political Commissar of the Volunteers, delivered a report at the meeting. General Choi Jong-hak spoke and presented a banner to the Chinese People's Volunteers. Subsequently, the Korean National Folk Art Theatre performed the famous Korean classical drama "Shim Cheong Jeon."

Today, the Volunteer units guarding the northern edge of the 38th parallel and the west coast also held commemorative meetings. The local party and government organs of Kangwon Province, Korea, recently awarded certificates of appreciation to the units and individuals of the Chinese People's Volunteers who have made outstanding contributions to helping the local people in production and construction. People from all walks of life in Jeongju County, North Pyongan Province, also recently organized a comfort group, carrying a large number of comfort items to visit a certain island unit of the Volunteer Army.

This morning, General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and General Wang Ping, Political Commissar, also accompanied General Choi Jong-hak, Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, and all members of the Korean People's Army delegation to lay wreaths at the Cemetery of the Volunteer Army Martyrs.

### 我志愿军保卫和平防线帮助朝鲜建设  金日成首相函杨勇上将等致谢  志愿军隆重纪念出国作战七周年

1957-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    我志愿军保卫和平防线帮助朝鲜建设
    金日成首相函杨勇上将等致谢
    志愿军隆重纪念出国作战七周年
    新华社平壤25日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成，在中国人民志愿军抗美援朝参战七周年纪念日前夕，写信给中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将和政治委员王平上将表示祝贺。
    金日成首相在这封信中说：当“迎接英雄的中国人民志愿军抗美援朝参战七周年之际，我向同志们和中国人民志愿军全体官兵致以热烈的祝贺。中国人民志愿军不仅在过去的朝鲜战争中建树了不朽的伟勋，而且停战后在美帝国主义和李承晚集团一贯采取破坏朝鲜停战协定，造成国际紧张局势的挑衅行为的情况下，继续可靠地保卫着和平的防线，并对我国的社会主义建设事业给予了诚心诚意的帮助。我以朝鲜人民的名义，向您并通过您向兄弟般的中国人民志愿军全体官兵致以衷心的感谢，并祝贺您们在今后的工作中获得辉煌的成就。”
    同日，中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将、政治委员王平上将，代表志愿军全体官兵写信给金日成元帅表示感谢。
    新华社平壤25日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成，在中国人民志愿军抗美援朝参战七周年前夕——10月24日，赠送给中国人民志愿军全体指挥员和战斗员一批礼品，表示慰问。这些礼品计有大米、苹果、海产物等共三十吨，人参酒十箱。
    新华社平壤25日电　中国人民志愿军各部队隆重纪念抗美援朝入朝参战七周年。
    志愿军领导机关25日下午举行了纪念大会。应邀参加大会的有以朝鲜人民军总政治局局长崔宗学上将为首的朝鲜人民军代表团和驻地党政机关的代表。志愿军政治委员王平上将在会上作了报告。崔宗学上将讲了话，并向中国人民志愿军赠送了锦旗。接着，由朝鲜国立民族艺术剧场演出了朝鲜古典名剧“沈清传”。
    今天，守卫在三八线北沿和西海岸的志愿军部队，也都分别举行了纪念大会。朝鲜江原道地方党、政机关，最近授予了帮助当地人民生产建设有突出成绩的志愿军部队的单位和个人以感谢状。平安北道定州郡各界人民还在近日组织了一个慰问团，携带了大批慰问品去志愿军某海岛部队进行慰问。
    今天上午，中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将、政治委员王平上将还偕同朝鲜人民军总政治局局长崔宗学上将和朝鲜人民军代表团全体人员，到志愿军烈士陵园敬献了花圈。
