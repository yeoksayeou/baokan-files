Title: Jiangsu Jute

Author: 
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 6

Column: Bumper Harvest

    Jiangsu Jute
    Jiangsu jute is now fully on the market. By the 24th, the province had overfulfilled its September raw jute procurement plan, with the procurement volume increasing by more than 10% compared to last year.
    Jiangsu Province ranks third in the country in terms of jute production. Although the jute planting area decreased from 210,000 mu last year to 200,000 mu this year, the estimated total output will increase from over 480,000 dan last year to 500,000 dan.
                          (Xinhua News Agency)

### 江苏黄麻

1957-10-01
第6版()
专栏：丰收

    江苏黄麻
    江苏黄麻全面上市。全省9月份收购生黄麻的计划，到24日已超额完成，收购量比去年增加10％以上。
    江苏省黄麻产量占全国第三位，今年黄麻种植面积虽然由去年的二十一万亩减少到二十万亩，预计总产量将由去年的四十八万多担增加到五十万担。
                          （新华社）
