Title: Premier Zhou Receives Sudanese Peace Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Premier Zhou Enlai received all members of the Sudanese Peace Delegation, headed by Hashib, this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Guo Moruo, Bao Erhan, Liu Ningyi, Ke Hua and others.

### 周总理接见苏丹和平代表团

1957-10-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见苏丹和平代表团
    新华社18日讯  周恩来总理在今天下午接见了以哈希布为首的苏丹和平代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有郭沫若、包尔汉、刘宁一、柯华等人。
