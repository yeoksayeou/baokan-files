Title: Guo Moruo Hosts a Banquet in Honor of Mrs. Gunawardena of Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet today in honor of Mrs. Gunawardena, Vice-Chairman of the Ceylon Peace Committee and Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, and others.

Among those present were Li Dequan, Liu Ningyi, Liao Chengzhi, Yang Hansheng, Hou Debang, and Ju Zan, heads of relevant Chinese organizations.

### 郭沫若设宴招待锡兰古纳瓦地尼夫人

1957-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    郭沫若设宴招待锡兰古纳瓦地尼夫人
    新华社7日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天设宴招待锡兰和平委员会副主席、锡中友好协会主席古纳瓦地尼夫人等。
    出席作陪的有我国有关团体负责人李德全、刘宁一、廖承志、阳翰笙、侯德榜、巨赞等人。
