Title: Dr. Hatta Leaves Nanjing for Shanghai; Visited the Yangtze River Bridge in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 8th: Former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta and his wife, accompanied by Burhan, Qu Wu, and the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wiyopranoto, arrived in Shanghai by special plane this morning from Nanjing.

Dr. Hatta and his wife were warmly welcomed upon disembarking from the plane by Cao Diqiu, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, and his wife, as well as Shanghai Vice Mayors Song Richang, Jin Zhonghua, and Rong Yiren.

Today in Shanghai, Dr. Hatta and others paid their respects at the former residence of Sun Yat-sen, and visited the Children's Palace, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building, and Yong'an Company, the largest joint state-private store in our country. At the Children's Palace, Dr. Hatta and others were warmly welcomed by more than five hundred children. A little girl tied a red scarf for Dr. Hatta and presented him and his wife with their own carvings and embroidery. Dr. Hatta told the children, "You are the flowers of the nation. I hope you will contribute your talents to the motherland that has gained freedom and independence after a long struggle." The children also sang Indonesian songs such as "Song of Coconut Island" for the guests. In the evening, the distinguished guests attended a welcome banquet held by Cao Diqiu.

Furthermore, after visiting Urumqi and Xi'an, the distinguished guests arrived in Wuhan on the 7th and visited the Yangtze River Bridge, which is about to open to traffic.

Dr. Hatta wrote an inscription for the bridge, praising the Chinese people's power to conquer nature. He said: May this bridge become a driving force for China's economic construction; we will build a similar bridge in Palembang, and may this be an encouraging force for us!

In Nanjing, the distinguished guests paid respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and laid a wreath before the statue of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. The distinguished guests also toured the stone statue road in front of the Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum and the scenic spots such as Xuanwu Lake.

### 哈达博士离南京到上海  在武汉曾参观长江大桥

1957-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    哈达博士离南京到上海
    在武汉曾参观长江大桥
    新华社上海8日电　印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士和夫人以及随行人员，在包尔汉、屈武和印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托陪同下，今天早晨从南京乘专机到达上海。
    哈达博士和夫人等走下飞机后，受到了上海市副市长曹荻秋和夫人，上海市副市长宋日昌、金仲华、荣毅仁等的热烈欢迎。
    哈达博士等今天在上海瞻仰了中山故居，参观和访问了少年宫、中苏友好大厦和我国最大的公私合营商店永安公司。在少年宫，哈达博士等受到五百多个孩子的热烈欢迎。一个小女孩为哈达博士系上了红领巾，并且把他们自己做的雕刻和刺绣送给哈达博士和夫人。哈达博士对孩子们说，你们是民族之花，希望你们把自己的天才贡献给经过长期奋斗而获得了自由独立的祖国。孩子们还为客人们唱了“椰岛之歌”等印度尼西亚歌曲。晚上，贵宾们出席了曹荻秋举行的欢迎宴会。
    又讯　贵宾们是在访问了乌鲁木齐和西安后，7日到武汉，并参观了即将通车的长江大桥。
    哈达博士为这座桥梁题词，赞扬中国人民征服自然界的力量。他说：但愿这座桥梁成为中国经济建设的推动力量；我们将要在巨港建设同样的大桥，但愿这对我们是一种鼓舞力量！
    贵宾们在南京曾晋谒中山陵，并在孙中山先生坐像前献花圈致敬。贵宾们还游览了明孝陵前的石像路和玄武湖等风景名胜。
