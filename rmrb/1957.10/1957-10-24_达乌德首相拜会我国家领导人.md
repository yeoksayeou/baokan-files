Title: Prime Minister Daud calls on our national leaders
Author:
Date: 1957-10-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: Sardar Mohammed Daud, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, accompanied by his entourage, called on Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai at 7:00 PM on the 23rd.
    Present at the meeting were Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Burhan, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Ding Guoyu, Director of the Protocol Bureau of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yu Xinqing, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wenjin, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Wang Yutian.
    Afghan Ambassador to China Samad was also present at the meeting.

### 达乌德首相拜会我国家领导人

1957-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    达乌德首相拜会我国家领导人
    新华社23日讯　阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德偕随行人员，在23日晚间七时拜会了朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理。
    拜会时在座的有副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、彭真、陈叔通，副总理贺龙、陈毅，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，国防委员会副主席张治中，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰，全国人民代表大会常务委员会典礼局局长余心清，外交部亚洲司司长章文晋，礼宾司代司长王雨田。
    拜会时，阿富汗驻中国大使萨马德也在座。
