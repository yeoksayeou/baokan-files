Title: Workshop for Producing Boilers for Wuhan Steel
Author: Wan Qun
Date: 1957-10-27
Page: 4
Construction Updates

    Workshop for Producing Boilers for Wuhan Steel
    The Wuhan Boiler Factory, designed with the help of Czechoslovakia for manufacturing boilers and metal structures for our country, is making rapid progress.
    The cold working shop of the third workshop group of this factory was officially put into production on the 15th of this month. This workshop specializes in producing gas coolers for the No. 1 blast furnace of Wuhan Steel. This equipment is composed of steel plates and many steel pipes, and each unit weighs about seventy tons.
                              (Wan Qun)

### 为武钢生产锅炉的车间
万群
1957-10-27
第4版()
专栏：建设动态

    为武钢生产锅炉的车间
    捷克斯洛伐克帮助我国设计的锅炉和金属结构制造厂——武汉锅炉厂，工程进展迅速。
    这个厂第三车间组的冷作车间已经在本月15日正式投入生产。这个车间专为武钢第一号高炉生产煤气冷却器。这种设备，是由钢板及许多根钢管组合成的，每台大约有七十吨重。
                              （万群）
