Title: Felinger and Others Visit the Sino-Czech Friendship Agricultural Cooperative
Author:
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Members of the Sino-Czech Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative in Zhangguozhuang, Beijing, warmly met with distinguished guests from Czechoslovakia this morning.

During their nearly four-hour stay, the Czechoslovak National Assembly delegation headed by Chairman Felinger and the Prague Municipal Central People's Committee delegation headed by Mayor Svoboda held lengthy discussions with the cooperative's cadres, inquiring in detail about the cooperative's production, management, welfare, and ideological education work, and visiting the Sino-Czech Friendship Exhibition Room, the water inlet gate on the Yongding River, and the clinic built with the help of Czechoslovak friends. They also visited the homes of cooperative members Liu Rui, Zhang Defu, and Yuan Zhenyu.

### 费林格等访问中捷友好农业社

1957-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    费林格等访问中捷友好农业社
    新华社30日讯  北京张郭庄中捷友好农业生产合作社的社员们今天上午亲切地会见了前来访问的捷克斯洛伐克贵宾。
    在将近四个小时的逗留中，以费林格主席为首的捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团和以斯沃波达市长为首的布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团，同社的干部们进行了长时间的座谈，详细询问了合作社在生产、管理、福利和思想教育工作方面的情况和问题，并参观了中捷友谊展览室、永定河畔的进水闸和捷克斯洛伐克朋友帮助建立的诊疗所，访问了社员刘瑞、张德福和袁振玉的家庭。
