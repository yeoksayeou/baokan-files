Title: Hungarian Director of Religious Affairs Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-10-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 20th - The Director of Religious Affairs of the Hungarian People's Republic, János Horváth, concluded his visit to China and returned home by plane from Beijing on the morning of the 20th.

During his time in China, Horváth visited the five cities of Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Guangzhou, held symposiums with cadres in charge of religious affairs in various places to exchange experiences, and went sightseeing.

On the afternoon of the 17th, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, met with Horváth.

### 匈宗教事务局长回国

1957-10-21
第3版()
专栏：

    匈宗教事务局长回国
    新华社20日讯  匈牙利人民共和国宗教事务局局长霍瓦特·雅诺士结束了在中国的访问，20日早晨乘飞机离北京回国。
    霍瓦特在中国期间，曾经访问了北京、南京、上海、杭州和广州五个城市，同各地负责宗教事务的干部举行了交流经验的座谈，并且进行了参观游览。
    17日下午，国务院秘书长习仲勋接见了霍瓦特。
