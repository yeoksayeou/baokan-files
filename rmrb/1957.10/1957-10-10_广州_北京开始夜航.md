Title: Guangzhou-Beijing Night Flights Begin
Author:
Date: 1957-10-10
Page: 4

    Guangzhou-Beijing Night Flights Begin

    The Guangzhou-Beijing air route has officially begun night flight service.

    At 2:10 PM on the 7th, a civil aviation plane took off from Guangzhou Tianhe Airport and arrived in Beijing at 10:45 PM that evening.

    Starting on the 7th, the Guangzhou-Beijing route will switch all daytime flights to night flights every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.

    Changing daytime flights to night flights will save passenger time. The airflow is more stable at night, and passengers will feel more comfortable; the delivery of mail and goods will also be faster. Mail and goods sent from Guangzhou in the afternoon can arrive in Beijing that same evening. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 广州—北京开始夜航

1957-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    广州—北京开始夜航
    广州—北京航线夜航业务正式开始。
    7日下午二时十分，一架民用航空班机，在广州天河机场起飞，在当晚十时四十五分到达北京。
    7日开始，广州—北京航线每周一、三、五，日航都改为夜航。
    日航改为夜航能节省旅客时间。晚上气流比较平稳，旅客会感觉更舒适；邮件、货物运送也更加迅速，下午由广州发出的邮件、货物，当天晚上就可以到达北京。　（新华社）
