Title: Prime Minister Daud Tours and Visits Beijing; People of Beijing Hold Rally Today to Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. Afghan Prime Minister Sardar Mohammed Daud and his entourage toured the Summer Palace on the 24th at noon.
    When the distinguished guests arrived at the Summer Palace by car, Vice Premier He Long and Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu greeted them at the entrance of the garden.
    During the tour, Prime Minister Daud greatly appreciated the architectural art of the halls, pavilions, buildings, kiosks, and pavilions scattered throughout the garden and watched very carefully. The Prime Minister also climbed the Tower of Buddhist Incense, which stands in the center of Longevity Hill, with Vice Premier He Long and others, overlooking the scenery of Kunming Lake. After Prime Minister Daud and Vice Premier He Long and others had lunch in the garden, they also took a boat trip on Kunming Lake.
    In the morning, Prime Minister Daud and his entourage visited the Beijing Electron Tube Factory and the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory.
    Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. People from all walks of life in Beijing will hold a rally on the afternoon of the 25th to welcome Afghan Prime Minister Sardar Mohammed Daud.

### 达乌德首相在京游览参观  北京人民今日集会欢迎贵宾

1957-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    达乌德首相在京游览参观
    北京人民今日集会欢迎贵宾
    新华社24日讯　阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德和随行人员在24日中午游览了颐和园。
    当贵宾们乘车到达颐和园的时候，贺龙副总理和章汉夫副部长在园门口迎接贵宾。
    在参观的时候，达乌德首相对散布在园内各处的殿、堂、楼、阁、轩、亭的建筑艺术颇为欣赏，观看得很仔细。首相还同贺龙副总理等一起，攀登上屹立在万寿山正中的佛香阁，眺望昆明湖景色。达乌德首相和贺龙副总理等在园内共进午餐以后，还泛舟游览了昆明湖。
    上午，达乌德首相和随行人员参观了北京电子管厂和国营北京第二棉纺织厂。
    据新华社24日讯　北京市各界人民将在25日下午举行集会，欢迎阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德。
