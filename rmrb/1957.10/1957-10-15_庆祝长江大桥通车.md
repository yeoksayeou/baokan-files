Title: Celebrating the Opening of the Yangtze River Bridge
Author: Wu Yuzhang
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 8

　　Celebrating the Opening of the Yangtze River Bridge
　　Wu Yuzhang
　　The rolling Yangtze flows eastward, a bridge connects the north and south,
　　A natural barrier can also be crossed, human power rivals the work of heaven.
