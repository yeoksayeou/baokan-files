Title: Prime Minister Daud Watches Ballet and Attends Friday Prayers at Mosque
Author:
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammed Daud, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, watched the ballet "Giselle" performed by the Soviet National Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theatre on the evening of the 25th. This performance was organized by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China to welcome the distinguished Afghan guests.

Acting Minister of Education of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Popal, Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad, Director-General of the General Political Department of the Afghan Foreign Ministry, Pazhwak, and other distinguished Afghan guests were also invited to watch the performance.

Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, was also invited to watch the performance.

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammed Daud, and his entourage attended Friday prayers at the Dongsi Mosque, which has a history of over 500 years, on the afternoon of the 25th.

When Prime Minister Daud, Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad, and others arrived at the Dongsi Mosque, they were welcomed by Burhan, Chairman of the China Islamic Association, Da Punsheng, Vice Chairman, and others, who accompanied them into the prayer hall to participate in the Jumu'ah prayer. Imam Yang Baozhai, while lecturing on religious doctrines, welcomed the distinguished Afghan guests on behalf of the Muslims of China. He expressed his hope that Chinese and Afghan Muslim brothers would have more opportunities to meet in the future, and prayed for the everlasting friendship between the people of China and Afghanistan.

During the break after the Jumu'ah prayer, Prime Minister Daud told Burhan that he was very happy to have the opportunity to participate in the prayer here and meet with Chinese Muslim brothers, and he thanked the China Islamic Association for its assistance. Burhan, on behalf of the China Islamic Association, presented Prime Minister Daud with a Quran and a pair of carved lacquer vases.

On the morning of the 25th, Prime Minister Daud and his entourage also visited the Forbidden City and the Temple of Heaven.

### 达乌德首相观看芭蕾舞剧  并曾到清真寺作主麻礼拜

1957-10-26
第3版()
专栏：

    达乌德首相观看芭蕾舞剧
    并曾到清真寺作主麻礼拜
    新华社25日讯　阿富汗王国萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德首相25日晚间由周恩来总理陪同，观看了苏联国立新西伯利亚歌剧芭蕾舞剧院演出的芭蕾舞剧“吉赛尔”。这次演出是中华人民共和国文化部为欢迎阿富汗贵宾举办的。
    阿富汗王国代理教育大臣波帕尔、阿富汗驻华大使萨马德、阿富汗外交部总政治司司长帕日瓦克和其他阿富汗贵宾们都应邀观看了这次演出。
    苏联驻华大使尤金院士也应邀观看了演出。
    新华社25日讯　阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德和随行人员，25日下午到有五百多年历史的东四清真寺作主麻礼拜。
    达乌德首相和阿富汗驻华大使萨马德等到达东四清真寺的时候，受到了中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉、副主任达浦生等的欢迎，由他们陪同进入礼拜殿参加聚礼。杨宝斋阿訇在演讲教义的时候，代表中国的穆斯林向阿富汗贵宾们表示欢迎。他希望今后中国阿富汗两国穆斯林兄弟有更多见面的机会，祝祷中国阿富汗两国人民友好万岁。
    在聚礼以后休息时，达乌德首相向包尔汉说，他非常高兴有机会在这里参加礼拜，同中国的穆斯林兄弟们见面，他感谢中国伊斯兰教协会给予他的帮助。包尔汉代表中国伊斯兰教协会向达乌德首相赠送了古兰经和一对雕漆瓶。
    25日上午，达乌德首相和随行人员还游览了故宫和天坛。
