Title: Indonesian Maluku Art Troupe Performs; Premier Zhou Attends, Watches, and Meets with Heads of the Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Premier Zhou Enlai watched a performance by the Indonesian Maluku Art Friendship Delegation in Beijing this evening.

Amateur artists from Maluku and West Irian performed folk songs and dances with distinct national characteristics and sang "The East is Red" and "Prairie Shepherd Song" in Chinese. Their performance was warmly welcomed.

During the intermission, Premier Zhou Enlai met with the head of the art troupe, Sain Nasution, the deputy head, Lisakoda, as well as the main performers and representatives of the West Irian performers.

Nasution, on behalf of the art troupe, presented gifts to Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai.

After the performance, Chinese artists presented flowers to all members of the art troupe. Premier Zhou Enlai went on stage to shake hands with the Maluku artists one by one and took pictures with them.

### 印度尼西亚马鲁古艺术团演出  周总理出席观看并接见了艺术团负责人

1957-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚马鲁古艺术团演出
    周总理出席观看并接见了艺术团负责人
    新华社7日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间在北京观看了印度尼西亚马鲁古艺术友好访问团的演出。
    来自马鲁古和西伊里安的业余艺术家们，今晚表演了具有鲜明民族特点的民间歌舞，并且用中文演唱了“东方红”和“草原牧歌”。他们的演出受到了热烈的欢迎。
    周恩来总理在演出休息时接见了艺术团团长赛因·纳苏蒂安、副团长利萨哥达，以及主要演员和西伊里安的演员代表。
    纳苏蒂安代表艺术团向毛泽东主席和周恩来总理赠送了礼品。
    演出结束以后，中国文艺工作者向艺术团全体人员献花，周恩来总理等到台上同马鲁古艺术家们一一握手，并且在一起照了像。
