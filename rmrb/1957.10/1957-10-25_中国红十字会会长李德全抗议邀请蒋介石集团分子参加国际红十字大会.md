Title: Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, Protests Against the Invitation of Chiang Kai-shek Clique Elements to the International Red Cross Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 24th: Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, protested on the 24th against the Standing Committee of the International Red Cross Conference inviting Chiang Kai-shek clique elements to participate in the 19th International Red Cross Conference in the name of the so-called "Taiwan Government" and "Taiwan Red Cross Society."

The 19th International Red Cross Conference will be held in New Delhi, India, from October 28th to November 7th.

Li Dequan raised this protest in a letter to François-Poncet, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the International Committee of the Red Cross, who is currently in New Delhi. In the letter, she demanded that the Standing Committee revoke its illegal decision to invite Chiang Kai-shek clique elements, regardless of whether they come to participate in the conference.

### 中国红十字会会长李德全抗议邀请蒋介石集团分子参加国际红十字大会

1957-10-25
第3版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会会长李德全抗议邀请蒋介石集团分子参加国际红十字大会
    新华社新德里24日电　中国红十字会会长李德全24日抗议国际红十字大会常设委员会邀请蒋介石集团分子以所谓“台湾政府”和“台湾红十字会”的名义参加第十九届国际红十字大会。
    第十九届国际红十字大会将于10月28日到11月7日在印度新德里召开。
    李德全在写给正在新德里的红十字国际委员会常设委员会主席弗朗索瓦·庞赛的一封信中提出了这个抗议。她在信中要求常设委员会撤销它邀请蒋介石集团分子的非法决定，不管他们是否前来参加大会。
