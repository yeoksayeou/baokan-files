Title: Tito Hosts Zhukov at Banquet
Author:
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 5

    Belgrade, October 14th (From the "Borba"): Yugoslav President Tito and his wife held a grand banquet last night at the presidential villa on Lake Bled, forty kilometers northwest of Ljubljana, to entertain Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov and his accompanying senior army and navy generals, who are currently visiting the area.

### 铁托宴请朱可夫

1957-10-15
第5版()
专栏：

    铁托宴请朱可夫
    本报贝尔格莱德14日电　据“战斗报”报道：南斯拉夫总统铁托暨夫人于昨晚在卢布尔雅那西北四十公里的布列德湖畔总统别墅举行盛大宴会招待正在这里进行访问的苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅及其随行的陆海军高级将领。
