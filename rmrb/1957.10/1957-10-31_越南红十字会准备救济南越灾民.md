Title: Vietnamese Red Cross Society Prepares to Assist South Vietnamese Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1957-10-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 30th: The Red Cross Society of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam recently wrote to the South Vietnamese Red Cross Society, expressing its willingness to allocate a batch of rice, cloth, and medicine to aid compatriots in South Vietnam who have suffered from typhoon disasters. It is currently awaiting a response from the South Vietnamese side.

Many areas of South Vietnam suffered severe damage from typhoons in early October. The affected areas span many provinces, including Quảng Trị, Thừa Thiên, Quảng Nam, Quảng Ngãi, Bình Định, Kon Tum, Biên Hòa, and others. Lộc Ninh City, located 120 kilometers north of Saigon, was once flooded.

### 越南红十字会准备救济南越灾民

1957-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    越南红十字会准备救济南越灾民
    新华社河内30日电　越南民主共和国红十字会最近写信给南越红十字会，表示愿意拨出一批大米、布匹和药品救济南越受台风灾害的同胞，目前正在等待南越方面的答复。
    南越许多地方在10月上旬遭受台风袭击，损失严重。受灾地区遍及广治、承天、广南、广义、平定、昆嵩、边和等许多省份。西贡北面一百二十公里的禄宁市曾经一度被洪水淹没。
