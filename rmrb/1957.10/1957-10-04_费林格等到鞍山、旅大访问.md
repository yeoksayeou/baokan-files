Title: Firinger and Others Visit Anshan and Lüda

Author:

Date: 1957-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Czechoslovak National Assembly Delegation and the Prague City Central People's Committee Delegation headed by Svoboda, left Beijing by special plane this morning to visit Anshan and other places.

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, 3rd. The Czechoslovak National Assembly Delegation headed by Chairman Firinger and the Prague City Central People's Committee Delegation headed by Mayor Svoboda, visited the iron-smelting plant, steel-making plant, large rolling mill and seamless steel tube plant of Anshan Iron and Steel today.

The distinguished guests arrived in Anshan from Beijing by special plane at 11:00 a.m. today.

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, 3rd. The Czechoslovak National Assembly Delegation headed by Chairman Firinger and the Prague City People's Committee Delegation headed by Mayor Svoboda, arrived in Lüda by special plane from Anshan at 5:15 p.m. today for a visit.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Song Li, Mayor of Lüda, and his wife, Bai Qingjiang, Secretary of the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee, and responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations. Soviet Acting Consul General in Dalian, Prilutsky, and his wife also welcomed them at the airport.

That evening, the distinguished guests attended a welcome banquet hosted by Mayor Song Li.

### 费林格等到鞍山、旅大访问

1957-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    费林格等到鞍山、旅大访问
    新华社3日讯  捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团和以斯沃波达为首的布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团，今天早晨由北京乘专机去鞍山等地参观。
    据新华社鞍山3日电  以费林格主席为首的捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团和以斯沃波达市长为首的布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团，今天下午参观了鞍钢的炼铁厂、炼钢厂、大型轧钢厂和无缝钢管厂。
    贵宾们是在今天上午十一时由北京乘专机来鞍山的。
    据新华社旅大3日电  以费林格主席为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会代表团和以斯沃波达市长为首的布拉格市人民委员会代表团，今天下午五点十五分，由鞍山乘专机来旅大访问。
    到机场欢迎的有旅大市市长宋黎和夫人、中共旅大市委书记白清江及各民主党派、人民团体的负责人。苏联驻大连领事馆代总领事普利第巴依洛及其夫人也到机场欢迎。
    当天晚上，贵宾们出席了市长宋黎的欢迎宴会。
