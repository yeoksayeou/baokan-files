Title: A Group of Students in Warsaw Tried to Make Trouble; The Most Arrogant Elements Have Been Detained
Author:
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 4th - According to the Polish Press Agency: On the evening of October 3rd, a group of restless Polish students and young people attempted to organize an anti-government demonstration in front of a student dormitory on Narutowicz Square in Warsaw. The police restored order, dispersed the participants in the incident, and detained the most arrogant elements among them.

The authorities of the Warsaw University of Technology have announced that they will take strict measures against the participants in this incident, including expulsion from the school.

### 华沙一群学生想闹事  最嚣张的分子已被拘留

1957-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    华沙一群学生想闹事
    最嚣张的分子已被拘留
    新华社华沙4日电　据波兰通讯社讯：10月3日傍晚，一群骚动的波兰学生和青年人，试图在华沙的纳鲁托维兹广场的一所学生宿舍门前组织反对政府的示威游行。民警恢复了秩序，驱散了这次事件的参加者，并且拘留了其中最嚣张的分子。
    华沙理工学院当局已经宣布，它将对这次事件的参加者采取严厉措施，包括开除出校。
