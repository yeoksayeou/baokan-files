Title: Nehru and Kishi Nobusuke Issue Joint Communiqué Calling on Major Powers to Ban Nuclear Weapons and Disarm
Author:
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 13th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru and Japanese Prime Minister Kishi Nobusuke issued a joint communiqué tonight, calling on major powers to ban nuclear weapons and reduce armaments.
    The joint communiqué stated that the two leaders discussed the issue of nuclear weapons and disarmament. They believe that the stockpiling of weapons of mass destruction by major powers poses a great danger to world peace. The communiqué expressed hope that major powers would continue to work towards banning nuclear weapons and reducing armaments. They also believe that a halt to nuclear testing must be the first step towards prohibiting the manufacture and use of nuclear weapons and reducing armaments in other respects. They agreed to instruct their respective delegations to the United Nations to cooperate with views advocating a halt to nuclear testing and the conclusion of disarmament agreements by the major powers concerned.
    The joint communiqué also stated that Nehru and Kishi Nobusuke also discussed international issues, particularly Asian issues and issues related to Japan and India. The communiqué stated that they "held consistent views on many issues."
    The communiqué stated that the two leaders discussed steps and methods to promote economic cooperation between the two countries. Both sides hope to conclude the trade agreement currently under negotiation at an early date.
    Nehru and Kishi Nobusuke agreed that the two countries should take all possible measures to conclude a cultural agreement in order to promote and strengthen cultural relations between the two countries.
    On the morning of the 13th, Nehru also held talks with Suzuki Mosaburo, Chairman of the Socialist Party. Both sides agreed that for the sake of peace in Asia and the world, Japan and India should strive to establish close ties with China.
    Nehru, who had been visiting Japan for ten days, left Tokyo by plane tonight to return home.

### 尼赫鲁和岸信介发表联合公报  呼吁大国禁止核武器和裁军

1957-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁和岸信介发表联合公报
    呼吁大国禁止核武器和裁军
    据新华社13日讯　东京消息：印度总理尼赫鲁同日本首相岸信介在今天夜间发表联合公报，呼吁各大国禁止核武器和裁减军备。
    联合公报说，两国领导人讨论了核武器和裁军问题。他们相信，各大国储存大规模毁灭性武器为世界和平带来巨大的危险。公报表示希望各大国继续为禁止核武器和裁减军备而努力。他们并且认为，停止核试验必须成为禁止制造和使用核武器以及在其他方面裁减军备的第一步。他们同意，要指令两国在联合国的代表团，同主张停止核试验和有关各大国缔结裁军协定的观点合作。
    联合公报还说，尼赫鲁同岸信介还讨论了国际问题，特别是亚洲问题和与日印两国有关的问题。公报说，他们“在许多问题上的看法是一致的”。
    公报说，两国领导人讨论了促进两国间的经济合作的步骤和方法。双方希望早日缔结正在谈判中的贸易协定。
    尼赫鲁和岸信介同意，两国要采取一切可能的措施来缔结文化协定，以便促进和加强两国间的文化关系。
    尼赫鲁在13日上午曾同社会党委员长铃木茂三郎进行了会谈。双方一致认为，为了亚洲与世界和平，日本和印度应该努力同中国建立密切的联系。
    在日本访问了十天的尼赫鲁已经在今天夜里乘飞机离开东京回国。
