Title: Sarit Stresses Thailand's Independence and Autonomy; States No Further Expansion of Military Strength
Author:
Date: 1957-10-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, 29th: According to reports in the "Liberty Newspaper" and the "Thai Daily" on the 24th and 25th, Thailand's Supreme Commander of the Armed Forces, Sarit Thanarat, told reporters that Thailand "should adopt an independent and autonomous policy, and it is best not to follow anyone."

Sarit stated that he is well aware of the Thai people's demand for a neutral policy, but he advocates not using the word "neutral" because, in his opinion, neutrality is also following others.

Sarit said, "In the future, there will be absolutely no expansion of military strength."

### 乃沙立主张泰国独立自主  表示不再扩充军事力量

1957-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    乃沙立主张泰国独立自主
    表示不再扩充军事力量
    据新华社29日讯　曼谷消息：据“自由通讯报”和“泰日报”在24日和25日报道，泰国武装部队最高司令乃沙立对新闻记者发表谈话说，泰国“应当采取独立自主的政策，最好是不追随任何人”。
    乃沙立表示，他很了解泰国人民对中立政策的要求，不过，他主张不使用“中立”这个字眼，因为，他认为，中立也是追随别人的。
    乃沙立说，“今后决不扩充军事力量”。
