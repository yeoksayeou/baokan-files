Title: Overhauling a Blast Furnace (Picture)
Author: Yang Limen
Date: 1957-10-24
Page: 4

    Overhauling a Blast Furnace
    The Angang No. 8 Blast Furnace has begun a major overhaul, which will expand the hearth volume by 66 cubic meters. This will enable it to produce more than 30,000 tons of pig iron per year compared to the past. This is installer Wang Zhongchen performing high-altitude operations. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Limen.

### 检修高炉（图片）
杨礼门
1957-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    检修高炉
    鞍钢八号高炉开始大检修，将扩大炉膛容积六十六立方公尺，这样每年能比过去多生产三万多吨生铁。这是安装工王忠臣正在进行高空作业。　新华社记者　杨礼门摄
