Title: Telegram of Greetings from Romanian National Leaders
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 3

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the great festival of the fraternal Chinese people—the eighth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China—on behalf of the Romanian people and in our own names, we extend to you, dear comrades, and through you to all the Chinese people, the warmest and most sincere congratulations.

    The Romanian people celebrate with particular joy the great achievements of the Chinese people in the cause of socialist transformation and the consolidation and prosperity of their motherland.

    These achievements, as well as the efforts made by the People's Republic of China to strengthen peace and security in Asia and the world, are increasingly and powerfully promoting the consolidation of China's prestige and authority and the growing role it plays as a great power in resolving international issues.

    We sincerely wish the Chinese people even greater achievements in their vigorous labor in socialist construction in their motherland and in their efforts in the struggle to defend world peace.

    May the fraternal friendship between the Romanian and Chinese peoples continue to flourish and last forever!

    Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic
    Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic
    Ion Gheorghe Maurer, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Romanian People's Republic
                          September 29, 1957, Bucharest

### 罗马尼亚国家领导人的贺电

1957-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚国家领导人的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志，中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇同志，中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来同志：
    在兄弟的中国人民的伟大节日——中华人民共和国建国八周年纪念日的时候，谨代表罗马尼亚人民并以我们本人的名义向你们，亲爱的同志们，并通过你们向全中国人民致以最热烈的和诚挚的祝贺。
    罗马尼亚人民怀着特别愉快的心情庆贺中国人民在社会主义改造以及巩固和繁荣自己祖国的事业中所获得的伟大成就。
    这些成就以及中华人民共和国为加强亚洲和世界的和平和安全所作的努力，愈来愈有力地促进了中国的声望和威信的巩固，促进了它作为一个大国在解决各项国际问题上所起的作用日益增长。
    我们衷心地预祝中国人民在自己祖国里进行社会主义建设方面的奋发劳动及其在为保卫世界和平的斗争中所作的努力取得更大的成就。
    愿罗、中两国人民的兄弟友谊不断繁荣并万世长存！
    罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席  彼·格罗查博士
    罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席  基·斯托伊卡
    罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长  扬·格·毛雷尔
                          1957年9月29日于布加勒斯特
