Title: Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the October Revolution; Farewell Reception for the Soviet Cultural Delegation; Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a reception on the evening of the 27th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution and to bid farewell to the Soviet Cultural Delegation headed by Kochetov.

Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, delivered a speech at the reception. He said that the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution is about to arrive. All the Chinese people are joyfully celebrating this great festival. He expressed the most sincere and warmest congratulations to all the Soviet people on behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. He proposed a toast to celebrating the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, and to the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union.

Wu Yuzhang also said at the reception that the Soviet Cultural Delegation's visit to China has played a significant role in strengthening the friendship and cultural exchange between the people of China and the Soviet Union.

Kochetov, head of the Soviet Cultural Delegation, then spoke. He expressed his gratitude to the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association for the thoughtful arrangements made for their month-long visit to China. Kochetov also announced that sculptor Manizer, a member of the delegation, would present the statue of Chairman Mao Zedong, which he sculpted during his visit to China, to the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

Cao Jinghua, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, and Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, also spoke at the reception, congratulating the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution and the great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union.

During the reception, Chinese artists performed wonderful programs.

Among those present at the reception were Shao Lizi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, Xu Guangping, Director, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General, Lao She and Cao Jinghua, Vice Presidents of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Cao Ping, Secretary-General, Ding Xilin and Xia Yan, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture, representatives from China's cultural, scientific, literary, and artistic fields, as well as Chinese soldiers who participated in the Soviet domestic revolutionary war.

Also present at the reception were Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and other responsible staff members of the embassy.

### 迎接十月革命四十周年  欢送苏联文化代表团  中苏友协举行酒会

1957-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    迎接十月革命四十周年
    欢送苏联文化代表团
    中苏友协举行酒会
    新华社27日讯　中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会27日晚举行酒会，迎接伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年和欢送以柯切托夫为首的苏联文化代表团。
    中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章在酒会上致词。他说，伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年就要到来。全中国人民正在欢欣鼓舞地迎接这个伟大的节日。他代表中苏友好协会全体会员向全体苏联人民表示最诚挚最热烈的祝贺。他提议，为迎接十月革命四十周年，为中苏两国人民永恒的牢不可破的友谊干杯。
    吴玉章在酒会上还说，苏联文化代表团访问中国，对于加强中苏两国人民的友谊和文化交流的事业，起了不小的作用。
    苏联文化代表团团长柯切托夫接着讲话。他对中苏友好协会为他们在中国进行的为期一个月的访问所作的各项周到安排，表示感谢。柯切托夫还宣布把代表团团员、雕塑家马尼泽尔在访问中国期间所塑制的毛泽东主席像，赠送给中苏友好协会。
    北京市中苏友好协会副会长曹靖华、中苏友好协会总会秘书长钱俊瑞、苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达里科夫也在酒会上讲了话，祝贺伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年和中苏两国人民的伟大友谊。
    席间，我国艺术工作者们表演了精采节目。
    出席酒会的有中苏友好协会总会副会长邵力子，理事许广平，秘书长钱俊瑞，副秘书长戈宝权，北京市中苏友好协会副会长老舍、曹靖华，秘书长曹平，文化部副部长丁西林、夏衍，我国文化、科学和文学、艺术方面的代表人士，以及我国参加苏联国内革命战争的战士等。
    出席酒会的还有苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达里科夫和使馆的其他负责工作人员。
