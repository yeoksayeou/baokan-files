Title: Friendship on the Sino-Soviet Border (Pictures)
Author: Hu Bao Wang, Yang Xuezu
Date: 1957-10-20
Page: 3

　　Friendship on the Sino-Soviet Border
    On the Sino-Soviet border, the armies of the two countries, which bear the task of defending peace, have a deep friendship and often pay friendly visits to each other.
    They talk intimately without feeling constrained by the unfamiliarity of the language.
    At a get-together, Soviet Major Kanov joined the music team of our People's Liberation Army soldiers; they jointly played songs of friendship and joy.
    A delegation of Soviet border guards visited our People's Liberation Army units and are visiting the wall newspaper in the club. People's Liberation Army Pictorial Agency manuscript, photos by Hu Bao Wang and Yang Xuezu

### 中苏边境上的友谊（图片）
胡宝王  杨学祖
1957-10-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　中苏边境上的友谊
    在中苏边境上，担负着保卫和平任务的中苏两国军队有着深厚的友情，他们经常互相进行友好访问。
    他们亲密畅谈，并不感到语言生疏的拘束。
    在一次联欢会上，苏军卡诺夫少校参加了我解放军战士的音乐队；他们共同演奏着友谊、欢乐的歌曲。
    苏联边防军代表团到我解放军部队进行访问，正在俱乐部里参观墙报。                  解放军画报社稿  胡宝王、杨学祖摄
