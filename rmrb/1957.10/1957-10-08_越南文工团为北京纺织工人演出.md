Title: Vietnamese Art Troupe Performs for Beijing Textile Workers
Author:
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 7th.

The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Art Troupe gave a performance tonight at the Beijing No. 2 State-owned Cotton Mill. Every wonderful and moving song and dance program performed by the art troupe was warmly welcomed by nearly two thousand textile workers.

Before the performance, Hong Gang, head of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Art Troupe, spoke. After the performance, Song Ting, director of the Beijing No. 2 State-owned Cotton Mill, presented a skein of yarn produced by the factory itself to the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Art Troupe on behalf of the textile workers. A group of female workers went on stage to present flowers to the members of the art troupe.

### 越南文工团为北京纺织工人演出

1957-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    越南文工团为北京纺织工人演出
    新华社7日讯　越南民主共和国文工团今晚在北京国棉二厂举行访问演出。文工团表演的每一个精彩动人的歌舞节目，都受到了近两千名纺织工人的热烈欢迎。
    演出以前，越南民主共和国文工团团长红钢讲了话。演出结束后，北京国棉二厂厂长宋汀代表纺织工人把厂里自己生产的一团棉纱送给越南民主共和国文工团。一群女工上台向文工团团员们献了花。
