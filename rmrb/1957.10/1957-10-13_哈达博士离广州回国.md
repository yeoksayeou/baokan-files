Title: Dr. Hatta Departs Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1957-10-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 12th: Former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta, his wife, and the senior officials accompanying him on his visit, left Guangzhou at 10:00 AM on the 12th, heading home via Hong Kong. In the morning, the spacious station square in Guangzhou was crowded with people. Dr. Hatta bid farewell to the well-wishers.

Vice Governor Wei Jinfei wished Dr. Hatta a safe journey.

Those who went to the station to see him off included Guangzhou Mayor Zhu Guang, Guangzhou Military Region Commander Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, prominent figures from all walks of life in Guangzhou, and the heads of consulates of various countries in Guangzhou.

Dr. Hatta left Guangzhou accompanied by Qu Wu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wiyopo Pranoto, also accompanied him on the train.

Before his departure, Dr. Hatta met with Guangzhou journalists and praised China's construction achievements.

### 哈达博士离广州回国

1957-10-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　哈达博士离广州回国
    据新华社广州12日电　印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士和夫人以及随他来访问的高级官员在12日上午十时离开广州经香港回国。早晨，广州宽敞的车站广场上人群拥挤，哈达博士对欢送的人群告别。
    魏今非副省长祝哈达博士一路平安。
    前往车站欢送的，还有广州市市长朱光、广州驻军首长文年生中将和在广州的各界知名人士，各国驻广州领事馆负责人也到车站送行。
    哈达博士由全国人民代表大会常委会副秘书长屈武陪同离开广州，印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托也随车陪同。
    哈达博士临行前，曾接见了广州的新闻记者，盛赞中国的建设成就。
