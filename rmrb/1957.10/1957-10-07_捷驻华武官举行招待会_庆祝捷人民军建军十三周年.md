Title: Czechoslovak Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate the 13th Anniversary of the Founding of the Czechoslovak People's Army
Author:
Date: 1957-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: The Czechoslovak Military Attaché in China, Colonel Rak, held a reception in Beijing tonight to celebrate the 13th anniversary of the founding of the Czechoslovak People's Army.

    Colonel Rak said at the reception that we must use all means and strive to continuously consolidate the national defense power of our country, and we must further develop the friendship with our most loyal brotherly countries and their armies. He said that the improvement of our country's defense capabilities depends not only on economic development and industrial advancement, but also on the solid unity of our people and their loyalty to socialist ideology and proletarian internationalism.

    Our Deputy Minister of National Defense, General Huang Kecheng, said that the people and armies of China and Czechoslovakia are closely united on the basis of Marxism-Leninism in the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and are jointly fighting to defend socialist construction, strive for lasting peace, and oppose war.

### 捷驻华武官举行招待会  庆祝捷人民军建军十三周年

1957-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    捷驻华武官举行招待会
    庆祝捷人民军建军十三周年
    据新华社6日讯　捷克斯洛伐克驻华武官拉克上校今晚在北京举行招待会，庆祝捷克斯洛伐克人民军建军十三周年。
    拉克上校在招待会上讲话说，我们要用一切办法并尽力继续不断地巩固我们国家的国防威力，我们要进一步地发展跟我们最忠实的兄弟国家及其军队的友谊。他说，我国国防力量的提高，不仅依靠于经济的发达和工业的发展，还依靠我国人民牢固的团结及其对社会主义思想和无产阶级国际主义的忠实。
    我国防部副部长黄克诚大将讲话说，中捷两国人民和军队在马列主义的基础上紧紧地团结在以苏联为首的社会主义阵营中，正在为保卫社会主义建设、争取持久和平和反对战争而共同奋斗。
