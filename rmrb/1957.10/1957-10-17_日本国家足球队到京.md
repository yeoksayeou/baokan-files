Title: Japanese National Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-17
Page: 3

　　Japanese National Football Team Arrives in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th — The Japanese National Football Team, invited by the All-China Sports Federation to visit China, arrived in Beijing today by train from Guangzhou.

    The Japanese National Football Team consists of twenty-two members, eighteen of whom are athletes. Their average age is twenty-three.

    Most of the members of the Japanese National Football Team were athletes who participated in the 16th Olympic Games last year. On October 6th of this year, they represented the All-Kanto District Football Team of Japan in a friendly match against the Israeli football team and won with a score of four to one.

    The Japanese National Football Team is scheduled to hold two matches in Beijing. The dates and opponents are as follows:

    October 20: Against the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Team.

    October 23: Against the Beijing Youth Team.

### 日本国家足球队到京

1957-10-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　日本国家足球队到京
    新华社16日讯　应中华全国体育总会邀请来中国访问的日本国家足球队，今天由广州乘火车到京。
    日本国家足球队由二十二人组成，其中有十八人是运动员。他们的平均年龄是二十三岁。
    日本国家足球队的大部分队员，是去年参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会的选手。他们在今年10月6日代表日本全关东区足球队同以色列足球队举行的一场友谊比赛中，曾以四比一获胜。
    日本国家足球队在北京预定举行两场比赛。比赛日期和对手如下：
    10月20日：对中国人民解放军“八一”队。
    10月23日：对北京青年队。
