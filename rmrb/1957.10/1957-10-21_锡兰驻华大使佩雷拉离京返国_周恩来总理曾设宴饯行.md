Title: Ceylonese Ambassador to China, Perera, Departs Beijing for Home; Premier Zhou Enlai Hosted Farewell Banquet

Author:

Date: 1957-10-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: Ceylonese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Perera, has departed Beijing for home on the 20th. Those who went to the train station to see him off included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Director of the Asian Department Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Zhang Shijie, and Vice President of the Chinese Buddhist Association Zhao Puchu. Staff members of the Ceylonese Embassy in China, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China, also went to see him off.

On the afternoon of October 19th, Ambassador Perera paid a farewell visit to Premier Zhou Enlai. Following the visit, Premier Zhou hosted a farewell banquet for Ambassador Perera, accompanied by Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, and Acting Director of the Bureau for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chen Zhongjing, among others. Before his departure, Ambassador Perera also paid farewell visits to Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo and Huang Yanpei, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and responsible persons from other relevant departments.

Ambassador Perera held a farewell reception on the afternoon of October 16th, attended by Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, various relevant ministers, and heads of various people's organizations. Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend.

### 锡兰驻华大使佩雷拉离京返国  周恩来总理曾设宴饯行

1957-10-21
第3版()
专栏：

    锡兰驻华大使佩雷拉离京返国
    周恩来总理曾设宴饯行
    新华社20日讯  锡兰首任驻华特命全权大使佩雷拉已于20日离京返国。前往车站送行的有外交部副部长章汉夫，亚洲司司长章文晋，礼宾司副司长张世杰和中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初。锡兰驻华大使馆人员和各国驻华使节及外交官员也前往送行。
    10月19日中午，佩雷拉大使向周恩来总理作了辞行拜会。拜会后，周总理为佩雷拉大使设宴饯行，作陪的有：国务院副总理贺龙，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部部长助理乔冠华和对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经等人。佩雷拉大使行前还向全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、黄炎培，北京市市长彭真，外交部副部长章汉夫和其他有关部门的负责人进行了辞行拜会。
    佩雷拉大使曾于10月16日下午举行辞行酒会，国务院总理周恩来，副总理贺龙，外交部副部长章汉夫，各有关部长和各人民团体负责人出席了酒会。各国驻华使节及外交官员也应邀到会。
