Title: Khrushchev Receives Osborne and Kebell
Author:
Date: 1957-10-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th: According to TASS, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, met with British Members of Parliament Osborne and Kebell at their request on the 8th and spoke with them.

These two British Members of Parliament are visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of a group of representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

### 赫鲁晓夫  接见奥斯本和凯贝

1957-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫
    接见奥斯本和凯贝
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫应英国议员奥斯本和凯贝的要求，在8日接见了他们，并且和他们谈了话。
    这两位英国议员是应苏联最高苏维埃一批代表的邀请前来苏联访问的。
