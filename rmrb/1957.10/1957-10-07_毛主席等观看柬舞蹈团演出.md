Title: Chairman Mao and Others Watch Cambodian Dance Troupe Performance
Author:
Date: 1957-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and other national leaders watched a performance by the Classical Dance Troupe of the Kingdom of Cambodia at Huairen Hall in Zhongnanhai this evening.

The first program performed by the Cambodian artists was the "Blessing Dance." This dance was specially choreographed by the Queen of Cambodia as a gift to Chairman Mao. The final program was the "Farewell Dance," during which the artists sang: "...Dear China, we love you like brothers and sisters. Close friendship will forever link our two countries together." When this dance performance ended, the artists waved the national flags of China and Cambodia, and repeatedly shouted in Chinese: "Long live the friendship between Cambodia and China!"

Tonight, the Cambodian artists also performed many Cambodian classical dances, which were warmly welcomed. After the performance, our national leaders, accompanied by Yang An, the head of the Cambodian economic delegation, went on stage to meet the Cambodian artists and took pictures with them.

Some of the artists from the Ballet Troupe of the Novosibirsk State Opera and Ballet Theatre of the Soviet Union also attended the performance tonight, and they presented flower baskets to the Cambodian artists to pay their respects.

### 毛主席等观看柬舞蹈团演出

1957-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席等观看柬舞蹈团演出
    新华社6日讯　毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理和国家的其他领导人员，今天晚间在中南海怀仁堂观看了柬埔寨王国古典舞蹈团的演出。
    柬埔寨艺术家们表演的第一个节目是“祝福舞”。这个舞蹈是柬埔寨王后特意排练献给毛主席的。最后一个节目是“告别舞”，艺术家们一边表演一边唱道：“……亲爱的中国，我们像兄弟姊妹般的热爱你。亲密的友谊，永远把我们两国联系在一起。”这个舞蹈表演完毕时，艺术家们挥舞着中柬两国国旗，用中国话连连高呼：“柬埔寨和中国友好万岁！”
    今晚，柬埔寨艺术家们还表演了柬埔寨的许多古典舞蹈，受到了热烈欢迎。在表演结束以后，我国国家领导人员由柬埔寨经济代表团团长杨安陪同登上舞台，同柬埔寨艺术家们会见，并同他们一起照了像。
    苏联国立新西伯利亚歌舞剧院芭蕾舞团的一部分艺术家，今晚也出席观看了表演，他们并向柬埔寨艺术家赠送花篮致敬。
