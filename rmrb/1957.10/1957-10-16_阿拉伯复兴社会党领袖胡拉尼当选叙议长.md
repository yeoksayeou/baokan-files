Title: Arab Socialist Ba'ath Party Leader Hourani Elected Speaker of Syrian Parliament
Author:
Date: 1957-10-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 15th dispatch: Akram Hourani, leader of the Arab Socialist Ba'ath Party, succeeded People's Party member and former Speaker Kudsi as Speaker of the 142-seat Syrian Parliament on the 14th with a vote of sixty-three to fifty-seven.

### 阿拉伯复兴社会党领袖胡拉尼当选叙议长

1957-10-16
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯复兴社会党领袖胡拉尼当选叙议长
    据新华社15日讯　大马士革消息：阿拉伯复兴社会党领袖阿克拉姆·胡拉尼在14日以六十三票对五十七票胜过人民党人、前议长库德西，继任有一百四十二个席位的叙利亚议会的议长。
