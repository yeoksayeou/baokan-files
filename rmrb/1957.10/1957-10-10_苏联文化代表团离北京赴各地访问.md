Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visits to Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1957-10-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: After a week of friendly visits in Beijing, the Soviet cultural delegation has already left Beijing to visit various locations.

In the past few days, the delegation leader, writer Kochetov, and members including Kyrgyz Republic Minister of Culture Kazakbayev, sculptor Manizer, painter Zhukov, composer Zhiganov, and poet Tank, separately visited Chinese writers Mao Dun and Liu Baiyu, artist Cai Ruohong, and musician Sun Shen, introducing the situation of Soviet literature, art, and music, and sharing their creative experiences. Honored Teacher Osipova had a cordial meeting with teachers and students of Beijing No. 101 Middle School. Doctor and Soviet Hero Troyan and Professor Dobrynina visited the Second Affiliated Hospital of Beijing Medical College and the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences.

The delegation members also visited the China-Soviet Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative. Socialist Labor Hero, Chairman of the Stalin Collective Farm, Lyukshin, learned in detail about the production situation of the cooperative, introduced the experience of the Stalin Collective Farm, and expressed the hope that the two farms would strengthen their ties and engage in friendly competition in the future.

The delegation members also visited Lu Xun's former residence and laid a wreath at Qi Baishi's tomb.

Amidst their busy visiting activities, the delegation members took the time to write and paint, recording their feelings about visiting China and conveying the close friendship of the Soviet people to the Chinese people. Painter Zhukov has already drawn dozens of sketches. Writer Kochetov wrote an article for the Beijing Daily entitled "Human Power Rivals Divine Skill," praising the great labor of the Chinese people and the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people.

Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, hosted a banquet on the 7th to entertain the entire delegation.

### 苏联文化代表团离北京赴各地访问

1957-10-10
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联文化代表团离北京赴各地访问
    新华社9日讯　苏联文化代表团在北京进行了一个星期的友好访问以后，已经在日前离北京去各地访问。
    几天以来，代表团团长、作家柯切托夫，团员、吉尔吉斯共和国文化部部长卡札克巴耶夫、雕塑家马尼泽尔、画家茹可夫、作曲家日干诺夫、诗人唐克等，分别访晤了我国作家茅盾、刘白羽，美术家蔡若虹，音乐家孙慎等，介绍了苏联文学、美术、音乐方面的情况和他们的创作经验。功勋教师奥希波娃同北京市一○一中学师生亲切地会见。医生、苏联英雄特洛扬和多布雷尼娜教授访问了北京医学院第二附属医院和医学科学院等处。
    代表团人员还访问了中苏友好农业生产合作社。社会主义劳动英雄、斯大林集体农庄主席柳克申详细了解了这个合作社的生产情况，介绍了斯大林集体农庄的经验，并希望两个农庄在今后加强联系，展开友谊竞赛。
    代表团人员还参观了鲁迅故居，并到齐白石墓前献了花圈。
    代表团人员在繁忙的访问活动中，抽暇著文作画，纪录他们在中国访问的感受，转达苏联人民对中国人民的亲密友谊。画家茹可夫已经画了几十幅素描。作家柯切托夫以“人力巧夺天工”为题给北京日报撰文，歌颂中国人民的伟大劳动和中苏人民的友谊。
    中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章7日曾举行宴会招待代表团全体人员。
