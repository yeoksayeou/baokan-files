Title: Heilongjiang Builds Another Large Sugar Refinery
Author:
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 4
Construction News

    Heilongjiang Builds Another Large Sugar Refinery
    The Qiqihar Sugar Refinery, with an annual output of 25,000 tons of sugar, was completed before National Day.
    Now, Heilongjiang Province, as a sugar beet producing base, already has six large-scale mechanized sugar refineries including Acheng and Heping. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 黑龙江又建一座大糖厂

1957-10-05
第4版()
专栏：建设动态

    黑龙江又建一座大糖厂
    年产二万五千吨糖的齐齐哈尔糖厂已经在国庆节前夕建成。
    现在，作为甜菜制糖基地的黑龙江省，已经拥有阿城、和平等六个大型机械化糖厂。　（据新华社讯）
