Title: Glory to the Great October that Opened a New Era in Human History—Destroying Capitalism and Establishing the Socialist Era! The CPSU Central Committee Issues Slogans for the Celebration of the October Revolution

Author:

Date: 1957-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th (TASS dispatch): The slogans of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union for the celebration of the fortieth anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution:
