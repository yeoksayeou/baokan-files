Title: China-Indonesia Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Maluku Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1957-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd - The China-Indonesia Friendship Association held a luncheon in Beijing today to welcome the Indonesian Maluku Art Troupe, which arrived a few days ago.

Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, said in his speech at the banquet that the Chinese people not only love Indonesian art but also love the great people of Indonesia. Hu Yuzhi toasted the actors from West Irian in the art troupe and said that the Chinese people resolutely support the Indonesian people's struggle to recover West Irian.

Sain Nasution, the head of the art troupe and Secretary-General of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association, said in his speech that they would use dance and song to convey the friendship of the Indonesian people to the Chinese people. The Indonesian people also support the just struggle of the Chinese people to liberate Taiwan. He shouted in Chinese, "Long live China-Indonesia friendship!"

The Indonesian artists warmly invited their Chinese friends attending the banquet to dance the "Sweetheart Dance" together, and they also presented the China-Indonesia Friendship Association with a specimen of a "Bird of Paradise," a precious specialty of the Maluku Islands.

### 中国印度尼西亚友协欢宴马鲁古艺术团

1957-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    中国印度尼西亚友协欢宴马鲁古艺术团
    新华社3日讯  中国印度尼西亚友好协会今天午间在北京举行宴会，欢迎前几天来到这里的印度尼西亚马鲁古艺术团。
    中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之在宴会上致词时说，中国人民不仅热爱印度尼西亚的艺术，而且热爱印度尼西亚伟大的人民。胡愈之向艺术团中来自西伊里安的演员们敬了酒，并且说，中国人民坚决支持印度尼西亚人民收回西伊里安的斗争。
    艺术团团长、印度尼西亚中国友好协会总会秘书长赛因·纳苏蒂安在讲话中说，他们将用舞蹈和歌唱来转达印度尼西亚人民对中国人民的友谊。印度尼西亚人民同样支持中国人民解放台湾的正义斗争。他用中国话高呼：“中国印度尼西亚友好万岁！”
    印度尼西亚的艺术家们热情地邀请参加宴会的中国朋友共同跳了“甜心舞”，他们还将马鲁古群岛的珍贵特产“极乐鸟”标本一只赠给中国印度尼西亚友好协会。
