Title: Li Siguang Sends Telegram Congratulating Soviet Union on Launch of Artificial Satellite
Author:
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th - Li Siguang, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, sent telegrams on the 7th to Professor Teskov, Chairman of the Soviet Union's Union of Workers in Higher Education and Scientific Institutions, and Academician Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, congratulating them on the successful launch of the first artificial satellite by the Soviet Union.
    The telegrams said, "The successful launch of the first artificial satellite by the Soviet Union is an epoch-making contribution made by the Soviet people and scientists to the development of world science and culture, opening up a new era for mankind to explore the mysteries of nature. Chinese scientists are extremely excited and encouraged by this great achievement. And we would like to extend our heartfelt congratulations to you and all Soviet scientists."

### 李四光电贺苏联发射人造卫星

1957-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    李四光电贺苏联发射人造卫星
    新华社7日讯　中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会主席李四光7日分别致电苏联高等教育与科学工作者联合会主席特斯科夫和苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫院士，祝贺苏联发射第一个人造卫星的成功。
    电文说，“苏联发射第一个人造卫星的成功是苏联人民和科学家们对世界科学文化发展所作出的划时代的贡献，为人类探索自然奥秘开辟了新的纪元。中国科学家们对这一个伟大的成就感到无比的兴奋和鼓舞。并谨向您和全苏科学家们致以衷心的祝贺。”
