Title: Beijing People Commemorate Lu Xun
Author:
Date: 1957-10-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Today, more than a thousand people in Beijing paid their respects at Lu Xun's former residence and visited the Beijing Lu Xun Museum to commemorate the 21st anniversary of Mr. Lu Xun's death. Among the endless stream of visitors were a large number of literary and art workers, cadres, and military personnel, as well as groups of elementary school students.

### 北京人民纪念鲁迅

1957-10-21
第3版()
专栏：

    北京人民纪念鲁迅
    新华社19日讯  今天，北京有一千多人瞻仰了鲁迅的故居，并到北京鲁迅博物馆参观，纪念鲁迅先生逝世二十一周年。在川流不息的参观者当中有大批文艺工作者、干部和部队工作者，还有成群结队的小学生。
