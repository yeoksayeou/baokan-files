Title: Soviet Union Produces Science Fiction Film: Cosmonauts Appear on Screen
Author:
Date: 1957-10-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: According to TASS, the Moscow Popular Science Film Studio is producing a science fiction film called "I am a Cosmonaut".

This film is based on a screenplay co-authored by Soviet young writers Kapitanovsky and Shreberg.

The plot of the film unfolds during the ten years after the successful launch of the first artificial Earth satellite. It will fascinatingly introduce the audience to various aspects of space flight through the story of the protagonist – a young cosmonaut – and his father – a famous cosmic ray scientist – traveling into space aboard an artificial solar satellite. The science fiction film will also introduce the planned program for launching solar satellites.

The most audacious fantasy elements in this film are all based on rigorous scientific principles.

### 苏联拍制科学幻想影片  宇宙飞行员现身银幕

1957-10-29
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联拍制科学幻想影片
    宇宙飞行员现身银幕
    据新华社28日讯　据塔斯社报道：莫斯科通俗科学影片制片厂正在摄制一部科学幻想影片“我是宇宙飞行员”。
    这部影片是根据苏联青年作家卡皮塔诺夫斯基和什列贝尔格合著的电影脚本拍摄的。
    这部影片的情节发展在第一颗人造地球卫星发射成功后的十年期间。它将通过主角——一个青年宇宙飞行员和他的父亲——一个著名的宇宙线科学家一道乘着人造太阳卫星到宇宙中去旅行的故事，引人入胜地向观众介绍宇宙飞行的各种情况。科学幻想片还将向观众介绍发射太阳卫星的预定计划。
    这部影片中最大胆的幻想的情节都是有严格的科学根据的。
