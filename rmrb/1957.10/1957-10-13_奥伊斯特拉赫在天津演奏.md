Title: Oistrakh Performs in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1957-10-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 12th. David Oistrakh, Soviet People's Artist and world-renowned violinist, was invited to hold a solo concert at the Central Conservatory of Music on the 12th, and was warmly welcomed by more than 1,000 teachers and students of the academy. At the end of each program, warm applause and cheers lasted for a long time.

Oistrakh came to Tianjin from Beijing on the 11th, and held a public concert that evening, which was also warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 奥伊斯特拉赫在天津演奏

1957-10-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　奥伊斯特拉赫在天津演奏
    据新华社天津12日电　苏联人民艺术家、世界著名小提琴家达维德·奥伊斯特拉赫，12日应邀在中央音乐学院举行独奏会，受到了这个学院师生一千多人的热烈欢迎。每个节目终了时，热烈的掌声和欢呼声都是经久不息。
    奥伊斯特拉赫11日由北京来天津，当晚就举行公开演奏会，同样受到听众们的热烈欢迎。
