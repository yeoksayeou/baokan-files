Title: State Council Holds Plenary Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: The 59th plenary meeting of the State Council was held on the morning of the 25th. The meeting discussed and approved the "Provisions of the State Council Regarding the Temporary Wage Treatment of Graduates from Universities and Secondary Vocational Schools During Internship" and the "Provisions of the State Council Regarding Living Allowances During Work Observation Periods for Anti-Socialist Elements and Other Bad Elements Among Graduates from Universities and Secondary Vocational Schools."

After listening to explanations by Vice Premier Li Xiannian, the meeting approved the "Provisions of the State Council on Improving the Supply of Pork in Cities and Industrial and Mining Areas" and the "Provisions of the State Council on Supplementary Oil Supply for Senior Intellectual Workers."

After listening to explanations by Vice Minister of Public Security Zhou Xing, the meeting approved the "Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Household Registration."

### 国务院举行全体会议

1957-10-26
第3版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议
    新华社25日讯　国务院全体会议第五十九次会议25日上午举行。会议讨论通过了“国务院关于高等学校和中等专业学校毕业生在见习期间的临时工资待遇的规定”、“国务院关于高等学校和中等专业学校毕业生中反对社会主义分子及其他坏分子在工作考察期间生活补助费的规定”。
    会议听了李先念副总理的说明以后，通过了“国务院关于改进城市、工矿区猪肉供应的规定”和“国务院关于高级脑力劳动者食油补助供应的规定”。
    会议听了公安部副部长周兴的说明以后，通过了“中华人民共和国户口登记条例”。
