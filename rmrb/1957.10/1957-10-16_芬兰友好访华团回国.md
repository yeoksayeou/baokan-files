Title: Finnish Friendship Delegation to China Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-10-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Finnish Friendship Delegation to China, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and having arrived in China in mid-September for a visit, has now departed Beijing for home in batches, after visiting Beijing, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Shanghai, Wuhan, Shenyang, Anshan, and Changchun.

### 芬兰友好访华团回国

1957-10-16
第3版()
专栏：

    芬兰友好访华团回国
    新华社14日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请9月中旬来中国访问的芬兰友好访华团，在参观访问了北京、广州、杭州、上海、武汉、沈阳、鞍山、长春之后，已经先后离北京回国。
