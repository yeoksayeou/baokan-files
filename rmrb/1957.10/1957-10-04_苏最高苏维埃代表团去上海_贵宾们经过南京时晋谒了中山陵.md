Title: Supreme Soviet Delegation to Shanghai; Honored Guests Visited Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum When Passing Through Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, October 3rd: The delegation from the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, headed by A.B. Aristov, arrived in Nanjing today for a tour and visit.

　　The honored guests arrived in Nanjing from Beijing by special plane at approximately 11:00 AM.

　　Hui Yuyu, Governor of Jiangsu Province, Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and President of the Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, hosted a banquet for the honored guests at noon.

　　In the afternoon, the honored guests paid their respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, laying a wreath before the statue of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. Subsequently, they visited Mr. Sun Yat-sen's former residence when he was the Provisional President, and toured the ruins of the West Garden of the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom's Heavenly King's Palace and Xuanwu Lake.

　　At 4:30 PM, the honored guests boarded a plane and left Nanjing for a visit to Shanghai.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 3rd: The delegation from the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, headed by A.B. Aristov, arrived in Shanghai this evening by special plane from Beijing via Nanjing for a visit.

### 苏最高苏维埃代表团去上海  贵宾们经过南京时晋谒了中山陵

1957-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏最高苏维埃代表团去上海
    贵宾们经过南京时晋谒了中山陵
    据新华社南京3日电  以阿·鲍·阿里斯托夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团今天到南京参观、访问。
    贵宾们是在上午十一时许从北京乘专机来到南京的。
    江苏省省长、中共江苏省委书记、江苏省和南京市中苏友好协会会长惠浴宇在中午设宴招待贵宾。
    下午，贵宾们晋谒了中山陵，在孙中山先生石像前献了花圈。随后，又到孙中山先生任临时大总统时的故居参观，并且游览了太平天国天王府西花园遗址和玄武湖。
    下午四时半，贵宾们登飞机离南京去上海访问。
    新华社上海3日电  以阿·鲍·阿里斯托夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团今天傍晚乘专机自北京经南京到达上海访问。
    贵宾们在机场受到全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋和上海市副市长刘述周、金仲华、盛丕华等三百多人的热烈欢迎。宋庆龄向阿里斯托夫问好，阿里斯托夫亲切地向宋庆龄致意，他说：“我们在南京晋谒了苏联人民敬爱的朋友孙中山先生的陵墓。”
    苏联及各国驻上海外交人员和苏联专家等也到机场欢迎。
