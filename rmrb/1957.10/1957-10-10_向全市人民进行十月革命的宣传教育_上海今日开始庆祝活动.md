Title: Conducting October Revolution Propaganda and Education for the Whole City: Shanghai Begins Celebration Activities Today
Author:
Date: 1957-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 9th -- The standing committee of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and the council of the Shanghai branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a joint meeting today to formulate a plan for celebrating the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. According to this plan, Shanghai will begin a concentrated, extensive, and in-depth campaign of activities celebrating the October Revolution for one month starting tomorrow (October 10th), while at the same time carrying out a campaign of Marxist-Leninist and socialist propaganda and education among the masses of the city.

Before and after the anniversary, factories, enterprises, government organs, troops, schools, neighborhood committees, agricultural producers' cooperatives, and handicraft producers' cooperatives throughout Shanghai will universally hold celebration meetings. On the eve of the anniversary, a mass rally attended by tens of thousands of people will be held throughout the city to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. On the anniversary itself, in addition to a city-wide gala evening party, cultural palaces, workers' clubs, cultural centers, and libraries throughout the city will organize various discussion meetings and report meetings, hold photo exhibitions, and screen Soviet films. Shanghai People's Broadcasting Station will hold a grand celebratory broadcast meeting.

The Propaganda Department of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party also convened a meeting of propaganda cadres from various districts and relevant departments yesterday to discuss the unfolding of various celebration activities.

The Shanghai Municipal Preparatory Committee for the Celebration of the 40th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution was established today. This preparatory committee is composed of 38 people including Ke Qingshi, Jin Zhonghua, Li Zhaohuan, Shi Ximin, Ba Jin, and Bai Yang.

### 向全市人民进行十月革命的宣传教育  上海今日开始庆祝活动

1957-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    向全市人民进行十月革命的宣传教育
    上海今日开始庆祝活动
    新华社上海9日电　政协上海市委员会常务委员和上海市中苏友好协会理事今天举行联席会议，制订了关于庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年的计划。按照这个计划，上海将从明天（10日）起以一个月的时间，集中地、广泛深入地进行关于庆祝十月革命的活动，同时在全市人民群众中进行一次马克思列宁主义和社会主义的宣传教育。
    在节日前后，上海市各工厂、企业、机关、部队、学校、里弄、农业生产合作社和手工业生产合作社将普遍举行庆祝会。节日前夕，全市将举行有万人参加的群众大会，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年。节日当天，除将举行全市性的联欢晚会外，全市各文化宫、工人俱乐部、文化馆、图书馆将组织各种座谈会和报告会，举办图片展览和放映苏联电影。上海人民广播电台将举办盛大的庆祝广播大会。
    中共上海市委员会宣传部也在昨天召集各区和有关部门的宣传干部举行会议，讨论了展开各项庆祝活动事宜。
    上海市庆祝十月社会主义革命四十周年筹备委员会已在今日成立。这个筹备委员会由柯庆施、金仲华、黎照寰、石西民、巴金、白杨等三十八人组成。
