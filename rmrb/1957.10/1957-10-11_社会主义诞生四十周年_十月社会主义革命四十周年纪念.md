Title: Fortieth Anniversary of the Birth of Socialism—Commemorating the Fortieth Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
Author: Huang Yanpei
Date: 1957-10-11
Page: 3

　　Socialism's Fortieth Anniversary
　　—Commemorating the Fortieth Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
    Huang Yanpei
　　I
    Everyone knows,
    Facts prove,
    Socialism is good.
    Socialism eliminates man's exploitation of man,
    Forbids private ownership of the means of production,
    Concentrates the forces of production, led by the state,
    Controls the supply of people's living needs,
    And provides compensation according to the amount of contribution.
    Who created it?
    The inventor was,
    Marx in the nineteenth century;
    Realized,
    Forty years ago, the October Revolution in the Soviet Union fired the first shot.
  　II
    Must remember well,
    In these forty years,
    How many imperialist countries
    Have never ceased their conspiracies to sabotage this emerging socialist country,
    Everything was finally crushed,
    Especially the glorious victory of the Patriotic War in 1945.
    Today,
    Socialist countries emerge one after another,
    United like brothers.
    The beasts are terrified,
    Because "the hunter is in the field."
    And there is more than one "hunter."
    What a momentum!
    Are the imperialist countries terrified!
    Then, wouldn't it be good for them to accept the disarmament proposal?
    No, the capitalists are unwilling to give up
    The maximum profits they see before them.
    What does the hunter symbolize?
    What are they terrified of?
    In reality, it is not just one intercontinental missile after another,
    But one socialist country after another pursuing socialism.
　　III
    The giant holds the truth,
    And if the masses accept it,
    It becomes an irresistible force.
    Specifically speaking,
    Socialism brings happiness to the broad masses of the people,
    The broad masses of the people stand up, unite, and organize,
    Dedicate the collective infinite wisdom,
    This power is obviously irresistible.
    Look! Look!
    Following the intercontinental missile,
    The world's first artificial satellite,
    Orbiting the earth once every hour, thirty-six minutes, and twelve seconds,
    Was pioneered by the first socialist country,
    Is it not enough to provide a strong guarantee for world peace!
    The guarantee depends on strength,
    Look!
    The Suez Canal incident in Egypt, these imperialist countries—they were afraid,
    Humbly and obediently finally gave in.
    We must also see clearly:
    The broad masses of the people in the imperialist countries
    Are also against their warlords being obsessed with fighting.
    What reason do the broad masses of the people in the imperialist countries have
    Not to welcome the good example of socialism bringing happiness
    To all mankind!
　　IV
    Only under the leadership of the proletarian party,
    Can we embark on the bright road of socialism.
    Leading the people in socialist construction,
    It is also necessary to lead the people in socialist transformation.
    Revisionism,
    Violating principles is not good.
    Everyone has read the "Communist Manifesto":
    "Different countries, different methods,"
    However, iron-clad principles cannot be changed.
    The great Soviet Union,
    Gives us valuable, correct, and advanced experience,
    Let our socialist countries unite into one,
    Especially the relationship between China and the Soviet Union,
    Cannot be written completely, nor can it be said completely,
    The affection is long! The friendship is deep!
    Connected like flesh and blood.
    I love the Soviet Union,
    I respect the Soviet Union,
    Great! Great!
    The October Socialist Revolution,
    This year,
    Is forty years old.

### 社会主义诞生四十周年——十月社会主义革命四十周年纪念
黄炎培
1957-10-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　社会主义诞生四十周年
　　——十月社会主义革命四十周年纪念
    黄炎培
　　一
    谁都知道，
    事实证明，
    社会主义好。
    社会主义消灭了人剥削人，
    不许私有生产资料，
    把生产力集中起来，由国家领导，
    掌握供应人民生活需要，
    按多少贡献，给多少酬报。
    是谁创造？
    发明的是，
    十九世纪马克思；
    实现起来，
    四十年前苏联十月革命最先一炮。
  　二
    必须牢记，
    四十年间，
    多少帝国主义国家
    从没有中止它们向这新兴的社会主义国家行使捣乱的阴谋诡计，
    一切终于被粉碎了，
    特别是１９４５年卫国战争的光辉胜利。
    到今天，
    社会主义国家一个个继起，
    团结着如兄如弟。
    百兽震恐了，
    为的是“猎人出场”。
    “猎人”还不止一起哩。
    多么声势！
    帝国主义国家是震恐么！
    那么，他们就接受裁军建议，岂不好呢，
    不，资本家们对眼前最大限度的利润，
    那里甘心放弃。
    象征猎人的是什么呢？
    它们所震恐的是什么呢？
    实际上并不止是一枚又一枚的洲际导弹，
    而是一个接着一个的社会主义国家的社会主义。
　　三
    巨人掌握着的是真理，
    给群众接受了，
    就成为不可抗的力量。
    具体地说来，
    社会主义把幸福带给广大人民，
    广大人民站立起来，团结起来，组织起来，
    献出了集体的无穷智慧，
    这份力量显然是不可抗。
    请看！请看！
    接在洲际导弹之后，
    世界第一个人造卫星，
    一小时三十六分十二秒绕地球一周，
    又给第一个社会主义国家首创成功了，
    还不足够地给世界和平以坚强的保障！
    保障是靠实力的，
    请看！
    埃及苏伊士运河事件，这些帝国主义国家——它们害怕了，
    俯首贴耳地终于退让。
    我们还须看清楚：
    帝国主义国家的广大人民
    也在反对它们军阀醉心打仗。
    帝国主义国家的广大人民
    有什么理由不欢迎社会主义把幸福
    给全人类的那么好的榜样！
　　四
    只有无产阶级政党领导，
    才能走上社会主义光明大道。
    领导人民进行社会主义建设，
    同时必需领导人民进行社会主义改造。
    修正主义啊，
    把原则违反了，那就不好。
    大家读过“共产党宣言”：
    “不同的国家，不同的办法”，
    可是，铁一般的原则不许改变。
    伟大的苏联，
    给我们以宝贵的正确的先进经验，
    让我们社会主义国家联成一片，
    特别是中苏两国的关系，
    写不完，也说不全，
    情长！谊深！
    象血肉般相连。
    我爱苏联，
    我敬苏联，
    伟大！伟大！
    十月社会主义革命，
    到今年，
    四十年。
