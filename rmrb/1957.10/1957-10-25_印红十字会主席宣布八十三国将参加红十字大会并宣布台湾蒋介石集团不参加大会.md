Title: Indian Red Cross Chairman Announces Eighty-Three Countries to Attend Red Cross Conference and Announces Chiang Kai-shek Clique of Taiwan Not Participating in the Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 24th: Indian Red Cross Chairman, Mrs. Kaur, announced at a press conference on the 24th that eighty-three countries have notified that they will send representatives or observers from their Red Cross Societies and/or governments to attend the 19th International Red Cross Conference.

In response to a question from a Reuters reporter regarding the issue of Chinese representation, Mrs. Kaur pointed out that neither the Chiang Kai-shek "government" of Taiwan nor any representatives of the Taiwan Red Cross will participate in the conference, and that the delegation of the People's Republic of China will participate in the conference. According to her, the Chiang Kai-shek clique of Taiwan had been invited to attend the conference. The People's Republic of China lodged a strong protest against this. The Chiang Kai-shek clique also made a so-called "protest."

Preparations for the International Red Cross Conference are underway, and the flags of the People's Republic of China and other participating countries are flying over the Science Palace where the conference will be held.

The Indian government has provided funding for the conference and has made other arrangements.

### 印红十字会主席宣布八十三国将参加红十字大会并宣布台湾蒋介石集团不参加大会

1957-10-25
第3版()
专栏：

    印红十字会主席宣布八十三国将参加红十字大会并宣布台湾蒋介石集团不参加大会
    新华社新德里24日电　印度红十字会主席考尔夫人24日在记者招待会上宣布，八十三个国家已经通知说，它们将派遣红十字会和（或）政府的代表或观察员参加第十九届国际红十字大会。
    在回答路透社记者提出的关于中国代表权问题的时候，考尔夫人指出，既将没有台湾蒋介石“政府”，也没有台湾红十字会的任何代表参加大会，中华人民共和国的代表团将参加大会。据她说，台湾蒋介石集团曾被邀请参加大会。中华人民共和国为此提出了强烈的抗议。蒋介石集团也曾提出所谓“抗议”。
    国际红十字大会的筹备工作正在进行，在大会将要举行的科学宫飘扬着中华人民共和国和其它各参加国的国旗。
    印度政府为大会提供了经费，并且作了其它的安排。
