Title: Welcome Rain Widely Falls

Author:

Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 4

    Welcome Rain Widely Falls
    (Our newspaper reports) From 8:00 AM on October 24th to 8:00 AM on October 25th, rain began to fall in central and southern Shaanxi, eastern Sichuan, western Hunan and Hubei, northeastern Guizhou, and most areas of Henan. Heavy rain fell in the border areas of Sichuan, Guizhou, and Hunan, while moderate or light rain fell in other areas.
    According to forecasts from the Central Meteorological Science Research Institute, from the 25th to the 26th, moderate rain will occur in Hubei and Henan provinces, eastern Guizhou, northern Hunan, western Shandong, and southern Hebei, and light rain may occur in other areas of Shandong.

### 喜雨普降

1957-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    喜雨普降
    本报讯　10月24日8时至25日8时，陕西中部、南部，四川东部，湖南和湖北西部，贵州东北部及河南大部地区，开始降雨。川、贵、湘交界地区下了大雨，其他地区中雨或小雨。
    据中央气象科学研究所预报，25日到26日湖北、河南全省，贵州东部，湖南北部，山东西部，河北南部等地将出现中雨，山东的其他地区可能出现小雨。
