Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Visits Hydroelectric Construction Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: Vice Chairman Zhu De visited the Hydroelectric Power Construction Exhibition yesterday afternoon.

Vice Chairman Zhu De spent an hour and a half visiting the exhibition, and gave instructions on relevant issues concerning China's hydroelectric construction.

### 朱副主席参观水电建设展览会

1957-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　朱副主席参观水电建设展览会
    新华社14日讯　朱德副主席昨天下午参观了水力发电建设展览会。
    朱德副主席在展览会上参观了一个半小时，并且对我国水电建设的有关问题作了指示。
