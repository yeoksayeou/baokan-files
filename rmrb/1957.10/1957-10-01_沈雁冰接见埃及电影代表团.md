Title: Shen Yanbing Receives Egyptian Film Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th—Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing received the entire Egyptian Film Delegation this morning.

　　During the reception, the head of the Egyptian Film Delegation, Yahya Hakki, told Minister Shen Yanbing that an Egyptian cultural delegation would also be visiting China soon. He would be a member of this delegation and was very pleased.

### 沈雁冰接见埃及电影代表团

1957-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰接见埃及电影代表团
    据新华社30日讯  文化部部长沈雁冰今天上午接见了埃及电影代表团全体人员。
    接见的时候，埃及电影代表团团长耶雅·哈基对沈雁冰部长说，一个埃及文化代表团不久也将来中国访问。他将成为这个代表团的成员之一，所以感到非常高兴。
