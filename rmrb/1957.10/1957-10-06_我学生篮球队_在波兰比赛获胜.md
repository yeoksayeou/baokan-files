Title: Our Student Basketball Team Wins in Poland
Author:
Date: 1957-10-06
Page: 8

    Our Student Basketball Team
    Wins in Poland
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 5th. The Chinese student men's basketball team, composed of students from Shanghai, played its first match in Poland against the representative team of Poznan on the 4th. As a result of the match, the Chinese team won with a score of 70 to 56. The Chinese student men's basketball team arrived in Poland on September 30th after visiting Hungary.

### 我学生篮球队  在波兰比赛获胜

1957-10-06
第8版()
专栏：

    我学生篮球队
    在波兰比赛获胜
    新华社华沙5日电　由上海学生组成的中国学生男子篮球队，4日在波兹南同这个城市的代表队进行了访问波兰的第一场比赛。比赛结果，中国队以七十比五十六获胜。中国学生男子篮球队是9月30日访问匈牙利后到波兰的。
