Title: China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and other Literary and Art Organizations Jointly Issue Notice to Publicize Soviet Construction Achievements Through Various Literary and Art Activities
Author:
Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 11th. The China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Chinese Writers Association, the Chinese Dramatists Association, the Chinese Artists Association, the Chinese Musicians Association, the China Dance Research Association, the Chinese Folk Literature and Art Research Association, the Chinese Quyi (folk art forms) Research Association, the China Film Workers Association, and the China Photographic Society today jointly issued a notice to local federations of literary and art circles and relevant branches regarding the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.

The notice stated that in celebrating the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, our literary and art organizations and literary and art workers should use the commemorative activities of this great festival to vigorously publicize the splendid achievements of Soviet socialist construction and the remarkable achievements in literature and art. Furthermore, in conjunction with the socialist ideological education work currently being carried out across the country, we should conduct a widespread and in-depth socialist education movement among the people of the whole country.

The notice stated that local federations of literary and art circles and relevant branches should formulate commemorative activity plans under the leadership of the local Party committees, based on their own professional characteristics, and actively mobilize and organize literary and art workers to participate in various commemorative activities. Local literary and art newspapers and magazines should actively organize commemorative articles, publish special issues for the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, or open commemorative columns. Various commemorative meetings and symposiums should be held; where conditions permit, Soviet plays should be staged and Soviet films screened as much as possible, and Soviet painting exhibitions, music appreciation concerts, and poetry recitals should be held.

### 中国文联等文艺团体联合发出通知  通过各种文艺活动宣传苏联建设成就

1957-10-12
第3版()
专栏：

    中国文联等文艺团体联合发出通知
    通过各种文艺活动宣传苏联建设成就
    据新华社11日讯　中国文学艺术界联合会、中国作家协会、中国戏剧家协会、中国美术家协会、中国音乐家协会、中国舞蹈艺术研究会、中国民间文艺研究会、中国曲艺研究会、中国电影工作者联谊会、中国摄影学会今天向各地文联和有关分会发出关于庆祝十月革命四十周年的联合通知。
    通知说，在庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的时候，我们文学艺术团体和文学艺术工作者应该通过这个伟大节日的纪念活动，大力宣传苏联社会主义建设的灿烂成绩和文学艺术上的卓越成就，并且结合目前正在全国开展的社会主义思想教育工作，向全国人民广泛深入地进行一次社会主义教育运动。
    通知说，各地文联和有关分会应当根据自己的业务特点，在当地党委领导下制定纪念活动计划，积极动员和组织文学艺术工作者参加各种纪念活动。各地文艺报刊、杂志应当积极组织纪念文章，出版十月社会主义革命四十周年的专刊或开辟纪念专栏。举行各种纪念会、座谈会；有条件的地区要尽量排演苏联剧目和放映苏联影片，举办苏联绘画展览会、音乐欣赏会、诗歌朗诵会等。
