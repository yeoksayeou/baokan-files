Title: President Prasad of India's Congratulatory Message
Author:
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 3

To His Excellency Mr. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of your country's National Day, I am very pleased to express to Your Excellency and the people of China the most sincere congratulations from the people of India and myself, and I sincerely wish the people of your country continuous progress.

    We sincerely hope that the existing friendly and harmonious relations between India and China will be further strengthened.
    Rajendra Prasad

### 印度总统普拉沙德的贺电

1957-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    印度总统普拉沙德的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    欣逢贵国国庆，我很愉快地向阁下和中国人民表达印度人民和我自己的最诚挚的祝贺，并衷心地祝贵国人民不断进步。
    我们真诚地希望印度和中国之间现有的友好和睦的关系会得到进一步的加强。      拉金德拉·普拉沙德
