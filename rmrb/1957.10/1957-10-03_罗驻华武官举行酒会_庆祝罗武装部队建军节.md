Title: Romanian Military Attaché Holds Reception Celebrating Romanian Armed Forces Day
Author:
Date: 1957-10-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd October. The Romanian People's Republic Military Attaché to China, Colonel Kocis, held a reception at the Peace Hotel this afternoon to celebrate the 14th anniversary of the founding of the Romanian Armed Forces.

Colonel Kocis said at the reception that a lasting and unbreakable friendship links the Romanian Armed Forces with the armies of the Soviet Union, China, and all socialist countries. He said that the Romanian people are fighting to ease international tensions, reduce armaments, and ban the use of weapons of mass destruction.

Chinese Vice Minister of National Defense, Grand General Wang Shusheng, spoke at the reception, expressing his heartfelt wishes for the Romanian People's Army to achieve even more brilliant achievements in fulfilling its lofty mission in the future.

More than seventy senior generals and officers from various services and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army were invited to attend today's reception, including Grand General Xiao Hua, Grand General Peng Shaohui, Grand General Yang Chengwu, Grand General Hong Xuezhi, Grand General Chen Shiqu, Grand General Zhou Shidi, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong.

The entire military delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic, Colonel General Willi Stoph, was also invited to attend the reception.

The Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, Chivu Stoica, as well as diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries to China, also attended the reception.

### 罗驻华武官举行酒会  庆祝罗武装部队建军节

1957-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    罗驻华武官举行酒会
    庆祝罗武装部队建军节
    新华社2日讯  罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华武官科奇斯上校今天下午在和平宾馆举行酒会，庆祝罗马尼亚武装部队建军节十四周年。
    科奇斯上校在酒会上讲话说，一种持久的、牢不可破的友谊，将罗马尼亚武装部队跟苏联、中国以及各社会主义国家的军队联结在一起。他说，罗马尼亚人民正在为缓和国际紧张局势、裁减军备、禁止使用大规模杀人武器而斗争。
    我国国防部副部长王树声大将在酒会上讲话，他衷心地预祝罗马尼亚人民军在今后完成自己的崇高使命中取得更为光辉的成就。
    应邀出席今天酒会的有萧华上将、彭绍辉上将、杨成武上将、洪学智上将、陈士渠上将、周士第上将、萧向荣中将等中国人民解放军高级将领和各军种、兵种军官七十多人。
    以德意志民主共和国副总理兼国防部长维利·斯多夫上将为首的德意志民主共和国军事代表团全体人员也应邀出席了酒会。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使乔洛尤以及各国驻华使节和武官也出席了酒会。
