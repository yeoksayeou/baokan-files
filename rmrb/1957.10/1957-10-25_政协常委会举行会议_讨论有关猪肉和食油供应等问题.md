Title: Standing Committee of the CPPCC Holds Meeting to Discuss Issues such as Pork and Cooking Oil Supply

Author:

Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th: The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held its enlarged forty-eighth meeting on the morning of the 24th. Members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the Chinese Language Reform Committee, and the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme Review Committee attended the meeting.

After hearing Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian's explanation on the production and supply of live pigs and cooking oil, the meeting discussed the "Draft Regulations of the State Council on Improving the Supply of Pork in Cities, Industrial and Mining Areas" and the "Draft Regulations of the State Council on Subsidized Supply of Cooking Oil for Senior Intellectual Workers," and unanimously expressed their support for the spirit embodied in these two regulations, which reflects the close relationship between the Party and the People's Government and the people. These two draft regulations will be promulgated and implemented after being discussed and approved by the plenary session of the State Council.

The meeting heard Guo Moruo's explanation on the revised draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme on behalf of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme Review Committee. After this meeting, the revised draft will be discussed in groups.

### 政协常委会举行会议  讨论有关猪肉和食油供应等问题

1957-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    政协常委会举行会议
    讨论有关猪肉和食油供应等问题
    新华社24日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会24日上午举行扩大的第四十八次会议。全国人民代表大会常务委员会、中国文字改革委员会、汉语拼音方案审订委员会的委员列席了这次会议。
    会议在听了国务院副总理李先念关于生猪和食油的生产和供应情况的说明以后，就“国务院关于改进城市、工矿区猪肉供应的规定草案”、“国务院关于高级的脑力劳动者食油补助供应的规定草案”进行了讨论，一致表示赞同这两项规定中体现的密切党和人民政府同人民群众的关系的精神。这两项规定草案将在经国务院全体会议讨论通过后公布执行。
    会议听了郭沫若代表汉语拼音方案审订委员会所作的关于汉语拼音方案修正草案的说明。这次会议以后，将分组对修正草案进行讨论。
