Author:
Date: 1957-10-18
Page: 5

　　Saudi King Expresses Willingness to Aid Syria

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 17th: Saudi Arabian King Saud discussed the situation created by the Turkish army's concentration on the Syrian border with Lebanese President Chamoun in Beirut on the evening of the 16th.

According to Reuters sources, Saud told Chamoun that he would help Syria resist any aggression and believed that no one had the right to interfere in Syria's internal affairs.

Jamal Husseini, an advisor to the Saudi King, said on the 16th that Saudi Arabia "endorses the Egyptian government's decision to send troops to Syria." He knew in advance that Egyptian reinforcements were going to Syria.

Husseini refuted reports that Saudi Arabia was annoyed by the Egyptian troop movements. He said: "Why should Saudi Arabia be annoyed? Egypt is only fulfilling its obligations under the joint defense treaty between Syria, Egypt, and Saudi Arabia." (Attached picture)

### 沙特国王表示将援助叙利亚

1957-10-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　沙特国王表示将援助叙利亚
    据新华社17日讯　贝鲁特消息：沙特阿拉伯国王沙特16日晚在贝鲁特同黎巴嫩总统夏蒙商谈了土耳其军队在叙利亚边境集结所造成的局势。
    据路透社从消息灵通人士那里得到的消息说，沙特曾告诉夏蒙，他将帮助叙利亚抵抗任何侵略，并且认为任何人都无权干涉叙利亚内政。
    沙特国王的顾问贾马尔·侯赛尼16日说，沙特“赞同埃及政府派兵到叙利亚去的决定”。他事先知道埃及增援部队要开往叙利亚。
    侯赛尼驳斥了所传沙特对于埃及军队的调动感到恼火的消息。他说：“沙特为什么恼火呢？埃及只是根据叙利亚、埃及和沙特阿拉伯之间的联合防御条约履行它的义务。”（附图片）
