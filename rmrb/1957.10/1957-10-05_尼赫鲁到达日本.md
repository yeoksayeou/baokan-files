Title: Nehru Arrives in Japan
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 4th—Indian Prime Minister Nehru arrived in Tokyo by plane this afternoon. Nehru will be in Japan for a ten-day state visit.
    The Japanese Emperor's representative, Japanese Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi, government officials, and people from all walks of life, totaling about two thousand, welcomed Prime Minister Nehru at the airport.
    During his stay in Japan, Nehru will hold talks with Japanese Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi, Foreign Minister Aiichiro Fujiyama, and other senior Japanese government officials, and will also visit some Japanese industrial centers.

### 尼赫鲁到达日本

1957-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁到达日本
    据新华社4日讯　东京消息：印度总理尼赫鲁今天下午乘飞机到达东京。尼赫鲁将在日本作为时十天的国事访问。
    日本天皇的代表、日本首相岸信介和政府官员以及各界人士共约二千人在机场上欢迎尼赫鲁总理。
    尼赫鲁在日本期间，将同日本首相岸信介、外务相藤山爱一郎等日本政府高级官员们举行会谈，并且将参观日本的一些工业中心。
