Title: Our Party and State Leaders Watch the Soviet Ballet "Swan Lake"
Author:
Date: 1957-10-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Our Party and state leaders Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Deng Xiaoping and others, watched the performance of "Swan Lake" by the artists of the Soviet National Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theater in Beijing this evening.

During the intermission, Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Deng Xiaoping and others met with the heads of the ballet troupe and held friendly talks.

During the meeting, the heads of the ballet troupe, on behalf of the people of Novosibirsk, presented gifts to Chairman Mao and Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, and Deng Xiaoping.

After the performance, Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Deng Xiaoping and others, accompanied by the Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, went on stage to meet with the Soviet artists and took pictures with them.

### 我国党和国家领导人  观看苏联芭蕾舞“天鹅湖”

1957-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    我国党和国家领导人
    观看苏联芭蕾舞“天鹅湖”
    新华社5日讯　我国党和国家的领导人朱德、刘少奇、周恩来、邓小平等，在今天晚间观看了苏联国立新西伯利亚歌舞剧院芭蕾舞团艺术家们在北京表演的“天鹅湖”。
    在演出休息的时候，朱德、刘少奇、周恩来、邓小平等接见芭蕾舞团的负责人，进行了友好的谈话。
    在接见的时候，芭蕾舞团的负责人代表新西伯利亚人民，向毛主席和朱德、刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、邓小平献了礼品。
    演出结束以后，朱德、刘少奇、周恩来、邓小平等，由苏联驻中国大使尤金陪同，登上舞台同苏联艺术家们会见，并同他们一起照了像。
