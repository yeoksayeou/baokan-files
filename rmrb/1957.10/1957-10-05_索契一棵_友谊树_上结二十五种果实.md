Title: A "Friendship Tree" in Sochi Bears Twenty-Five Kinds of Fruit
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th - According to a TASS report: In the orchard of the Sochi Subtropical Crop Experiment Station in the Soviet Union, there is a remarkable tree bearing twenty-five kinds of citrus fruits, including sweet oranges, lemons, tangerines, and pomelos. It grew from the original trunk of the tree after guests from China, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Vietnam, Yugoslavia, and other countries, who came to visit, grafted branches of different citrus plants onto it. Recently, Chinese guests who visited grafted new branches onto the tree – Chinese tangerines. This tree is called the "Friendship Tree of the Peoples of All Countries."

### 索契一棵“友谊树”  上结二十五种果实

1957-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    索契一棵“友谊树”
    上结二十五种果实
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社报道：在苏联索契的亚热带农作物试验站果园里，有一株结满了甜橙、柠檬、橘子、文旦等二十五种柑橘类植物果实的奇树。它是由前来此地访问的中国、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、越南、南斯拉夫等国的客人在这株树原来的树干上嫁接不同的柑橘类植物的枝条而长成的。前不久来此访问的中国客人给这株树嫁接了新的枝条——中国橘。这株树被称为“各国人民友谊树”。
