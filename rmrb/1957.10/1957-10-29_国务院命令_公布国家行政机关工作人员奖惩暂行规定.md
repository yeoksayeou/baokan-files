Title: State Council Order: Promulgation of the Provisional Regulations on Awards and Penalties for Staff Members of State Administrative Organs
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th. State Council Order. The full text is as follows:

The State Council's Provisional Regulations on Awards and Penalties for Staff Members of State Administrative Organs have been approved by the 82nd meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on October 23, 1957, and are now hereby promulgated.

                       Premier of the State Council: Zhou Enlai, October 26, 1957

### 国务院命令  公布国家行政机关工作人员奖惩暂行规定

1957-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院命令
    公布国家行政机关工作人员奖惩暂行规定
    新华社28日讯　国务院命令。全文如下：
    国务院关于国家行政机关工作人员的奖惩暂行规定，已经经过全国人民代表大会常务委员会1957年10月23日第八十二次会议批准，现在予以公布。
                       国务院总理　周恩来　1957年10月26日
