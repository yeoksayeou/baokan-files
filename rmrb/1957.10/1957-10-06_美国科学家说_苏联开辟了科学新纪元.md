Title: American Scientists Say Soviet Union Opens a New Era of Science
Author:
Date: 1957-10-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 5th Dispatch, Washington: Prominent American scientist Lloyd Berkner said on the 4th that the Soviet Union's launching of the world's first artificial satellite "opens a new era for science."

    Berkner is the Vice-Chairman of the International Geophysical Year Special Conference currently being held here and the American rapporteur for the satellite program. He relayed this news to scientists from various countries attending the conference and congratulated the Soviet scientists. This news, which indicates that the Soviet Union has surpassed the United States in launching artificial satellites, greatly surprised many American scientists.

    Dr. Joseph Kaplan, Chairman of the US National Committee for the International Geophysical Year, said at a reception held by the Soviet Embassy for this achievement: "I am amazed that they achieved this in a short period of preparation—obviously no longer than ours—and I think this is a remarkable achievement on their part." He also said, "It's truly unimaginable; if they can launch a satellite like this, they can launch even heavier ones."

    Dr. Richard Porter, Chairman of the American Satellite Technology Group, said, "This is an outstanding step forward in science." When asked when the United States intends to launch its satellite, Porter replied, "Who knows, maybe in two months, maybe in a year."

    American astronomer Dr. Hynek has already ordered an observation team of more than one hundred people to search for and track this satellite, saying, "We hope to cooperate with the Soviets in order to obtain the existing materials. They have been very good to us, telling us about the tracking frequencies and other information about the satellite. We are very happy about this."

### 美国科学家说  苏联开辟了科学新纪元

1957-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    美国科学家说
    苏联开辟了科学新纪元
    新华社5日讯　华盛顿消息：美国著名科学家劳埃德·贝克纳4日说，苏联放射出世界第一颗人造卫星是为“科学开辟了一个新的纪元”。
    贝克纳是正在这里举行的国际地球物理年特别会议的副主席和卫星计划的美国报告人。他把这个消息转告给参加会议的各国科学家，并且向苏联科学家们祝贺。这个说明苏联在放射人造卫星方面已经胜过美国的消息，使许多美国科学家大吃一惊。
    美国国际地球物理年全国委员会主席约瑟夫·卡普兰博士在苏联大使馆为此举行的招待会上说：“我对他们在短短的准备时间内——显然不会比我们长——取得这一成就很感惊奇，我认为这是他们方面非凡的成就。”他又说：“这真不可想像，如果他们能够发射这样的卫星，他们就能发射更重的。”
    美国卫星技术小组主席理查德·波特尔博士说：“这是在科学上出类拔萃地前进了一步。”有人问，美国打算在什么时候发射它的卫星，波特尔回答说：“谁知道，也许在两个月内，也许是一年。”
    美国天文学家海内克博士已经命令由一百多人组成的观察队寻找和追踪这个卫星，他说：“我们希望同苏联人合作，以便获得已有的材料。他们一直对我们很好，告诉我们关于跟踪这个卫星的频率和其他情报。我们对此感到非常高兴。”
