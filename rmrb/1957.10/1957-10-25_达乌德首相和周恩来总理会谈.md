Title: Prime Minister Daoud and Premier Zhou Enlai Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 24th

    Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammed Daoud, held talks with Premier Zhou Enlai on the afternoon of the 24th.

    On the Afghan side participating in the talks were Acting Minister of Education Dr. Ali Ahmad Popal, Afghan Ambassador to China Abdul Samad, and Director-General of Political Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Abdul Rahman Pazhwak.

    On the Chinese side participating were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Ding Guoyu, and Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wenjin.

### 达乌德首相和周恩来总理会谈

1957-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    达乌德首相和周恩来总理会谈
    新华社24日讯　阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德24日下午和周恩来总理举行了会谈。
    参加会谈的阿富汗方面有代理教育大臣阿里·艾哈迈德·波帕尔博士，阿富汗驻华大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德和外交部总政治司司长阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·帕日瓦克。
    中国方面参加的有国务院副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰和外交部亚洲司司长章文晋。
