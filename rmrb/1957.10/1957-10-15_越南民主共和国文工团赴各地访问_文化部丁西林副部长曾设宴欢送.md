Title: Vietnamese Democratic Republic Performing Arts Troupe Visits Various Locations; Vice Minister of Culture Ding Xilin Hosts Farewell Banquet

Author:

Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency report: The entire Vietnamese Democratic Republic Performing Arts Troupe left Beijing by train on the evening of the 12th for Zhengzhou. After visiting and performing in Zhengzhou, Changsha, Wuhan, Liuzhou, and Nanning, they will return to their country.

The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Performing Arts Troupe arrived in Beijing at the end of September. The troupe performed a total of eight shows in Beijing, with an audience of more than 14,000 people. The troupe's performers went deep into factories and schools to perform for workers, students, and military personnel, receiving an extremely warm welcome. The artists of the troupe also made professional contacts with Chinese literary and art circles.

Vice Minister of Culture Ding Xilin held a banquet in Beijing at noon on the 12th to bid farewell to the performing arts troupe. Nguyen Khang, the Ambassador of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic to China, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 越南民主共和国文工团赴各地访问  文化部丁西林副部长曾设宴欢送

1957-10-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　越南民主共和国文工团赴各地访问
    文化部丁西林副部长曾设宴欢送
    据新华社讯　越南民主共和国文工团全体人员12日晚上坐火车离开北京前往郑州。他们将在郑州、长沙、武汉、柳州、南宁访问演出后回国。
    越南民主共和国文工团是9月底到达北京的。文工团在北京共演出八场，观众有一万四千多人。文工团的演员们深入工厂、学校为工人、学生，以及为部队官兵演出，受到极其热烈的欢迎。文工团的艺术家们还同中国文艺界人士作了专业性的接触。
    文化部副部长丁西林12日中午曾在北京举行宴会，欢送文工团。越南民主共和国驻中国大使阮康也应邀出席了宴会。
