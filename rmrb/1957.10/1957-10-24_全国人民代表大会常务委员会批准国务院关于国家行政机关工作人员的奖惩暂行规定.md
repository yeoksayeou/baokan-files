Title: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Approves the Provisional Regulations on Awards and Punishments for Staff Members of State Administrative Organs issued by the State Council
Author:
Date: 1957-10-24
Page: 1

    The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Approves the Provisional Regulations on Awards and Punishments for Staff Members of State Administrative Organs issued by the State Council
    (Adopted at the 82nd Session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on October 23, 1957)
    The 82nd Session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress resolved on October 23, 1957: to approve in principle the Provisional Regulations on Awards and Punishments for Staff Members of State Administrative Organs issued by the State Council.
