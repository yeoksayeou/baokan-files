Title: State Council Holds Plenary Session Approving Draft Regulations on the 1958 Economic Construction Bonds and Other Matters
Author:
Date: 1957-10-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The 58th plenary session of the State Council was held on the 18th.

　　The meeting discussed and approved the draft regulations on the 1958 National Economic Construction Bonds. The meeting also discussed and approved the draft regulations on the Frontier Health and Quarantine of the People's Republic of China. These two drafts will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and decision.

　　The meeting approved the trade and payment agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

　　The meeting discussed and approved the Measures for Land Requisition for National Construction (Revised Draft) and decided to submit it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and approval.

　　The meeting adopted the organizational regulations (draft) of the People's Congress and People's Committee of Qian Gorlos Mongolian Autonomous County, Jilin Province, and decided to submit it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation.

　　The meeting approved the State Council's decision to abolish Hechuan City in Sichuan Province, and the administrative area of the original Hechuan City was placed under the leadership of Hechuan County.

　　The meeting approved the State Council's decision to abolish Jining County in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and the administrative area of this county was respectively placed under the leadership of Jining City, Right Wing Front Banner of Chahar, Right Wing Rear Banner of Chahar, and Xinghe County.

　　The meeting approved the State Council's decision to establish Maqin County and Madoi County in Qinghai Province. The administrative area of Maqin County is the three tribes of Ranluo, Xiamoba and Dawu under the jurisdiction of Gande County. The seat of the County People's Committee is located at Central Station (Yigeqi). The administrative area of Madoi County is the Heke tribe under the jurisdiction of Dari County and the Kuake and Zhake tribes under the jurisdiction of Gande County. The seat of the County People's Committee is located along the Yellow River. Both counties are under the leadership of the Guoluo Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture.

　　The meeting approved the State Council's decision to establish the Chuxiong Yi Autonomous Prefecture in Yunnan Province and abolish the Chuxiong Special Administrative Region. The administrative area of the autonomous prefecture is the sixteen counties including Chuxiong of the original Chuxiong Special Area, and the Autonomous Prefecture People's Committee is stationed in Chuxiong County. The meeting approved the State Council's decision to establish the Fuxin Mongolian Autonomous County and the Karaqin Left Wing Mongolian Autonomous County in Liaoning Province, and to abolish Fuxin County and Karaqin Left Banner. The administrative area of the Fuxin Mongolian Autonomous County is still the administrative area of the original Fuxin County, and the Autonomous County People's Committee is stationed in the original Fuxin County. The administrative area of the Karaqin Left Wing Mongolian Autonomous County is the entire area of the original Karaqin Left Banner, as well as the seven townships of Siguan Yingzi, Santai, Kundu Yingzi, Naimayingzi, Haidao Yingzi, Tianshenghao, and Yuhuangmiao in Shanzui District of Lingyuan County, and the southern part of the railway in Gongyingzi Township of Jianping County. The Autonomous County People's Committee is stationed in Dachengzi Town. Finally, the meeting also approved appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行全体会议  通过1958年经济建设公债条例草案等事项

1957-10-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　国务院举行全体会议
    通过1958年经济建设公债条例草案等事项
    新华社18日讯  国务院全体会议第五十八次会议在18日举行。
    会议讨论通过了1958年国家经济建设公债条例（草案）。会议还讨论通过了中华人民共和国国境卫生检疫条例（草案）。以上这两个草案将提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议决定。
    会议核准了中华人民共和国和阿富汗王国交换货物和支付协定。
    会议讨论通过了国家建设征用土地办法（修正草案），并决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议通过了吉林省前郭尔罗斯蒙古族自治县人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案），并决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议。
    会议批准了国务院关于撤销四川省合川市的决定，原合川市的行政区域划归合川县领导。
    会议批准了国务院关于撤销内蒙古自治区集宁县的决定，这个县的行政区域分别划归集宁市、察哈尔右翼前旗、察哈尔右翼后旗和兴和县领导。
    会议批准了国务院关于设置青海省玛?县和玛多县的决定。玛?县的行政区域为甘德县所辖的然洛、下莫巴、大武三个部落，县人民委员会驻中心站（依盖齐）。玛多县的行政区域为达日县所辖的合科部落和甘德县所辖的垮科、查科两个部落，县人民委员会驻黄河沿。这两个县都受果洛藏族自治州领导。
    会议批准了国务院关于设置云南省楚雄彝族自治州并撤销楚雄专署的决定。自治州的行政区域为原楚雄专区的楚雄等十六个县，自治州人民委员会驻楚雄县城。会议批准了国务院关于设置辽宁省阜新、喀喇沁左翼二蒙古族自治县并撤销阜新县及喀喇沁左旗的决定。阜新蒙古族自治县的行政区域仍为原阜新县的行政区域，自治县人民委员会驻原阜新县城。喀喇沁左翼蒙古族自治县的行政区域为原喀喇沁左旗的全部区域，凌源县山咀子区的四官营子、三台、坤都营子、奈马营子、海道营子、天盛号、玉皇庙等七个乡和建平县公营子乡的铁道南部地区，自治县人民委员会驻大城子镇。会议最后还通过了任免事项。
