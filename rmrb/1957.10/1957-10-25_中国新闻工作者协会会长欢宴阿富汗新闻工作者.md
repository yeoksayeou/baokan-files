Title: China Journalists Association President Hosts Banquet for Afghan Journalists
Author:
Date: 1957-10-25
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: Deng Tuo, President of the All-China Journalists Association, held a reception on the afternoon of the 24th to welcome Abdul Sattar Shalizai, Deputy Director of the Afghan News and Publishing Department, Abdul Rauf Benawa, Director of Kabul Radio Station, and cinematographer Akbar Shalizai, who are visiting China accompanying Afghan Prime Minister Daud. At the reception, Deng Tuo delivered a speech welcoming the Afghan journalists, raising a toast to the further development of friendly relations between China and Afghanistan, and the closer economic and cultural exchanges between China and the Kingdom of Afghanistan.
    Abdul Sattar Shalizai then spoke, thanking the Chinese journalists for their welcome and wishing further development of friendly relations between China and Afghanistan.

### 中国新闻工作者协会会长欢宴阿富汗新闻工作者

1957-10-25
第3版()
专栏：

    中国新闻工作者协会会长欢宴阿富汗新闻工作者
    据新华社24日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会会长邓拓24日下午举行酒会，欢迎随同阿富汗王国达乌德首相来中国访问的阿富汗新闻和出版署副署长阿卜杜勒·萨塔尔·沙利扎伊、喀布尔广播电台台长阿卜杜勒·拉奥夫·本纳瓦、电影摄影师阿克巴·沙利扎。邓拓在酒会上致词欢迎阿富汗的新闻同业，他举杯祝中阿两国的友好关系得到更进一步的发展，中国和阿富汗王国的经济和文化交流更加密切。
    阿卜杜勒·萨塔尔·沙利扎伊接着讲话，感谢中国新闻界对他们的欢迎，并祝中国和阿富汗两国友好关系的更进一步的发展。
