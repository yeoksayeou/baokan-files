Title: Pyongyang Circles Hold Celebration Rally

Author:
Date: 1957-10-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 30th: Pyongyang circles held a grand celebration rally at the Moranbong Theater tonight to celebrate the 8th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Attending the rally were Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Hong Myong-hui, Jung Il-ryong, Nam Il, and Park Ui-wan, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party Kim Chang-man, and other party and government leaders, responsible persons of various democratic parties and social organizations, and senior generals of the People's Army.

The delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers, led by Cha Boping, Director of the Youth Department of the Political Department of the Volunteers, was invited to attend the rally.

Soviet Ambassador to Korea, Puzanov, and other diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea also attended the rally.

Hyun Chil-jong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, delivered a report at the rally. Chinese Ambassador to Korea, Qiao Xiaoguang, also spoke at the rally.

### 平壤各界举行庆祝大会

1957-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    平壤各界举行庆祝大会
    据新华社平壤30日电  平壤各界今天晚上在牡丹峰剧场举行了中华人民共和国建国八周年庆祝大会。
    参加大会的有朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健，内阁副首相洪命熹、郑一龙、南日、朴义琓，劳动党中央委员会副委员长金昌满等党和政府的领导人，各民主政党、社会团体负责人和人民军高级将领等。
    中国人民志愿军政治部青年部部长查波平率领的志愿军代表团应邀参加了大会。
    苏联驻朝鲜大使普扎诺夫等各国驻朝鲜的外交使节也参加了大会。
    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长玄七钟在大会上作了报告。中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光也在大会上讲了话。
