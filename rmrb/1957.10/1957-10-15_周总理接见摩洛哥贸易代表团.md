Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Moroccan Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Moroccan Trade Delegation headed by Benani Abbes on the evening of the 13th.

Present at the meeting were Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

### 周总理接见摩洛哥贸易代表团

1957-10-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见摩洛哥贸易代表团
    新华社14日讯　周恩来总理13日晚接见了以贝拿尼·阿培士为首的摩洛哥贸易代表团。
    接见时在座的有对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷等。
