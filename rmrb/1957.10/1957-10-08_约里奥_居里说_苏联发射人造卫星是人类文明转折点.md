Title: Joliot-Curie Says Soviet Launch of Artificial Satellite is Turning Point in Human Civilization
Author:
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 5

    Joliot-Curie Says
    Soviet Launch of Artificial Satellite is Turning Point in Human Civilization
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th - Paris Dispatch: Professor Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council and French atomic energy authority, told a "Weekly Tribune" reporter on October 6th that he believes the Soviet Union's launching of an artificial Earth satellite into space is a great victory for humanity and marks a turning point in civilization. He said that if we make greater efforts in scientific research, this peaceful aspect of science will set a good example for France to follow and participate in.

### 约里奥—居里说  苏联发射人造卫星是人类文明转折点

1957-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    约里奥—居里说
    苏联发射人造卫星是人类文明转折点
    新华社7日讯　巴黎消息：世界和平理事会主席、法国原子核权威弗雷德里克·约里奥—居里教授10月6日对“星期周报”记者发表谈话说，他认为苏联把人造地球卫星发射入空间是人类的一个伟大胜利，是文明的一个转折点的标志。他说，如果我们在科学研究工作中作更大的努力，科学的这个和平方面就将为法国树立它可能遵循和可能参加的良好范例。
