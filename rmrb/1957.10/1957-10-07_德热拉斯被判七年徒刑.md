Title: Djilas Sentenced to Seven Years' Imprisonment
Author:
Date: 1957-10-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 6th - The Sremska Mitrovica City Court of Yugoslavia opened a trial of Milovan Djilas on the 4th, and according to the crimes he committed, sentenced him to seven years of imprisonment on the afternoon of the 5th in accordance with the law. The court announced that his crime was hostile propaganda against the socialist foundation and social system of Yugoslavia, as well as its defense forces, and against Yugoslavia's foreign policy.

### 德热拉斯被判七年徒刑

1957-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    德热拉斯被判七年徒刑
    据新华社贝尔格莱德6日电　南斯拉夫斯雷姆斯卡密特罗维查市法院4日开庭审判了密洛凡·德热拉斯，并且根据他所犯的罪行在5日下午依法判处他七年有期徒刑。法院宣布他的罪行是对南斯拉夫的社会主义基础和社会制度以及国防力量并且针对南斯拉夫的外交政策进行敌视的宣传。
