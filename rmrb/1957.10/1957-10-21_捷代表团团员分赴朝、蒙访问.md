Title: Czechoslovak Delegation Members Leave for Visits to North Korea and Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1957-10-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, October 20th: Some members of the National Assembly delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic and the delegation of the Central People's Committee of the city of Prague headed by Mayor Svoboda left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 19th to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The other members of the delegation left by plane on the same morning to visit the Mongolian People's Republic.
　　Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, and other members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress were at the airport to see them off. Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák, and representatives of the North Korean and Mongolian embassies in China were also at the airport to see them off.

### 捷代表团团员分赴朝、蒙访问

1957-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    捷代表团团员分赴朝、蒙访问
    据新华社20日讯  捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会代表团部分团员和以斯沃波达市长为首的布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团，19日上午乘飞机离开北京去朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问。代表团另外一部分团员，在同日上午乘飞机去蒙古人民共和国访问。
    到机场送行的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真、陈叔通和常务委员多人。捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克和朝鲜、蒙古驻华大使馆代表都到机场送行。
