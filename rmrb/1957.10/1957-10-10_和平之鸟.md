Title: Bird of Peace
Author: Tian Jian
Date: 1957-10-10
Page: 8

    Bird of Peace
    Tian Jian
    --Written for the First Artificial Satellite

    I.
    There is a divine bird in the high sky,
    Joyfully chirping on the clouds;
    It is racing with the stars,
    Constantly sending out signals.
    It orbits the Earth,
    Now flying to Beijing;
    In a few tens of minutes, it will also
    Pass by Washington with a smile.

    II.
    Hail you, bird of peace,
    Soviet-made star!
    You use the genius of mankind
    To uncover the secrets of the universe.
    Because of you, we will ride rockets
    To travel among the stars;
    To shake hands with Mars,
    To confide in the moon.
    We want to say to the moon—
    Your silver tree,
    Why has it been hidden for tens of thousands of years,
    Unwilling to let us explore?
    You are also about to be born,
    Casting fruits to the world;
    Casting them to Red Square in Moscow,
    Or casting them to Tiananmen!
    --Written around 11 PM on October 7th,
    Upon hearing the news

### 和平之鸟
田间
1957-10-10
第8版()
专栏：

    和平之鸟
    田间
    ——为第一颗人造卫星而作
    一
    高空里有一只神鸟，
    在云彩上快乐欢叫；
    它要和众星竞赛，
    不停地发出信号。
    它环绕地球运行，
    此刻正飞到北京；
    几十分钟以后它还要
    含着微笑路过华盛顿。
    二
    欢呼你和平之鸟，
    苏维埃人造的星！
    你以人类的天才，
    揭开宇宙的秘密。
    由于你我们将驾着火箭，
    在星际中间旅行；
    要和火星去握手，
    要和月亮去谈心。
    我们要对月亮说——
    你的那一棵银树，
    为什么隐藏几万年，
    不肯让我们去探索？
    你也快要出世了，
    把果子投向人间；
    投向莫斯科红场，
    或者是投向天安门！
    ——10月7日夜11时左右，
    闻讯号声作
