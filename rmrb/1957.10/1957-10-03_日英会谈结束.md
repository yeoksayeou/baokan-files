Title: End of Anglo-Japanese Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-10-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London, October 1st: A communiqué on talks between Japan and Britain was issued in London today as Japanese Foreign Minister Fujiyama concluded his four-day visit to Britain.

    The communiqué shows that the two countries have differences on disarmament and trade issues. In the communiqué, Fujiyama expressed "deep regret" over Britain's insistence on conducting a series of nuclear tests this winter.

    Fujiyama arrived in London on September 26. Today, Fujiyama has left London for Copenhagen before returning to Japan.

### 日英会谈结束

1957-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    日英会谈结束
    据新华社1日讯  伦敦消息：在日本外务相藤山今天结束对英国的四天访问的时候，伦敦发表了日本和英国两国的会谈公报。
    公报表明两国在裁军和贸易问题上存在着分歧。藤山在公报中对英国坚持要在今冬进行一系列核试验表示“深深的遗憾”。
    藤山是在9月26日到达伦敦的。今天，藤山已离开伦敦去哥本哈根，然后返回日本。
