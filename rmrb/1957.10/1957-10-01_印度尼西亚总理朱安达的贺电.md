Title: Telegram from Indonesian Prime Minister Djuanda
Author:
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 3

Telegram from Indonesian Prime Minister Djuanda:

To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, I extend to Your Excellency, on behalf of the Government of the Republic of Indonesia and in my own name, my sincerest congratulations and best wishes, wishing you good health and your country prosperity and happiness.

Djuanda
Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia

### 印度尼西亚总理朱安达的贺电
朱安达
1957-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总理朱安达的贺电
    中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来先生阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国国庆佳节，我谨以印度尼西亚共和国政府和我个人的名义向阁下致以最诚恳的祝贺和最良好的愿望，祝阁下健康，祝贵国人民繁荣和幸福。
                        印度尼西亚共和国总理  朱安达
