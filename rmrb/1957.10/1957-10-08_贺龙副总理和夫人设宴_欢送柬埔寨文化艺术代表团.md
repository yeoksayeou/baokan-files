Title: Vice Premier He Long and His Wife Host a Banquet to Bid Farewell to the Cambodian Cultural and Art Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Vice Premier He Long and his wife hosted a banquet at Ziguang Pavilion, Zhongnanhai, today to bid farewell to the Cambodian Cultural and Art Delegation, which is about to leave Beijing to visit and perform in various places.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the banquet. Also present at the banquet were Deputy Minister of Culture Ding Xilin, Xia Yan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice President of the Chinese Buddhist Association Zhao Puchu, and famous figures from the Beijing literary and art circles.

Yang An, head of the Economic Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia to Beijing, and his wife were invited to attend the banquet.

Vice Premier He Long delivered a speech at the banquet, expressing his heartfelt gratitude to all members of the Cambodian Cultural and Art Delegation, headed by Princess Norodom Buppha Devi and Prince Norodom Chakrapong, and led by Director Fang Bingzhen, for their outstanding performances for the Beijing audience despite their hard work. He Long said that our guests are about to visit other cities in China, and he firmly believes that they will receive the same warm welcome and return home full of the friendship and praise of the Chinese people. He Long then raised his glass to wish that the flowers of culture of China and Cambodia would bloom in exchange, and that the friendship between the two countries would be consolidated and developed day by day.

Premier Zhou Enlai proposed a toast at the banquet for the close friendship between the peoples of China and Cambodia, for the health of His Majesty King Norodom Suramarit and Her Majesty Queen Kossamak Nearirat of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and for the health of Prince Sihanouk. Premier Zhou also toasted each member of the art troupe.

Director Fang Bingzhen expressed his heartfelt thanks to the Chinese government and all parties for their warm hospitality in his speech. He said: This visit to China is an unforgettable memory, and we hope to come to China again. His speech aroused warm applause.

Yang An, head of the Economic Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia to Beijing, also spoke, saying that the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation will soon perform in Cambodia, and the Cambodian people will be very happy to welcome their dear Chinese guests on their own land.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

Famous Chinese artists toasted Princess Norodom Buppha Devi, Prince Norodom Chakrapong, Director Fang Bingzhen, and all members of the art troupe. Famous Chinese actors also performed wonderful programs to add to the fun.

The Cambodian Cultural and Art Delegation is scheduled to leave Beijing for Shanghai at noon tomorrow. They will return home after performing in Shanghai and Guangzhou. (Attached picture)

### 贺龙副总理和夫人设宴  欢送柬埔寨文化艺术代表团

1957-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理和夫人设宴
    欢送柬埔寨文化艺术代表团
    新华社7日讯　贺龙副总理和他的夫人今天中午在中南海紫光阁设宴欢送即将离开北京去各地访问演出的柬埔寨文化艺术代表团。
    周恩来总理出席了宴会。出席宴会作陪的还有文化部副部长丁西林、夏衍，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初以及北京文艺界著名人士。
    柬埔寨王国驻北京经济代表团团长杨安和夫人应邀出席了宴会。
    贺龙副总理在宴会上致词，对以帕花·黛维公主和夏卡朋王子为首并由方秉祯团长率领的柬埔寨文化艺术代表团的全体团员不辞劳苦地为北京观众作了卓越的演出，表示衷心的感谢。贺龙说，我们的客人即将访问中国的其他城市，深信他们也将受到同样热烈的欢迎，并将满载中国人民的友谊和赞美回国。贺龙接着举杯祝中、柬两国文化的花朵在交流中盛开怒放，祝两国的友谊日益巩固、发展。
    周恩来总理在宴会上提议为中柬两国人民的亲密友谊，为柬埔寨王国诺罗敦·苏拉马里特国王陛下和科萨马克·内正里拉特王后陛下以及西哈努克亲王殿下的健康，干杯。周总理还向艺术团全体团员一一敬了酒。
    方秉祯团长在致词中，对中国政府和各方面的热诚招待表示衷心的感谢。他说：这次到中国访问是一个永远不能忘怀的记忆，我们还希望再一次来到中国，他的讲话，引起了热烈掌声。
    柬埔寨王国驻北京经济代表团团长杨安也讲了话，他说，中国文化艺术代表团不久将到柬埔寨演出，柬埔寨人民将非常高兴地在自己的国土上迎接亲爱的中国客人。
    宴会在友好热烈的气氛中进行。
    中国著名艺术家们，向帕花·黛维公主、夏卡朋王子以及方秉祯团长和艺术团全体团员们敬酒。中国著名演员还表演了精采节目助兴。
    柬埔寨文化艺术代表团预定在明天中午离开北京前往上海。他们将在上海、广州演出后回国。（附图片）
