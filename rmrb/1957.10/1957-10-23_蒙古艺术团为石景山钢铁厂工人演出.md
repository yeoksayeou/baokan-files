Title: Mongolian Art Troupe Performs for Workers at Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant
Author:
Date: 1957-10-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 22nd - The Mongolian People's Republic State Musical Drama Theatre Art Troupe gave a brilliant performance for thousands of steelworkers at the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant on the evening of the 22nd. Each program was warmly welcomed.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the performance. Also present were the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Lubusang. During the intermission of the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai received the head of the art troupe, Tsegmed, as well as artistic director Lubusangsharabu and music conductor Namuslaizhabu. Also present at the reception were Chen Zhongjing, acting director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, factory director Zhou Guanwu, and Ambassador Lubusang.

### 蒙古艺术团为石景山钢铁厂工人演出

1957-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古艺术团为石景山钢铁厂工人演出
    据新华社22日讯　蒙古人民共和国国家音乐话剧院艺术团22日晚上在石景山钢铁厂为几千钢铁工人作了精采演出，每个节目都受到热烈的欢迎。
    周恩来总理出席观看了演出。前来观看演出的还有蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使鲁布桑。周恩来总理在演出休息的时候，接见了艺术团团长策格米德以及艺术指导鲁布桑沙拉布和音乐指挥纳木斯来扎布。接见时在座的有对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，厂长周冠五，以及鲁布桑大使等。
