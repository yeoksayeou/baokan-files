Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Leaves Beijing; Premier Zhou Enlai Greets Distinguished Guests at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1957-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 12th: The Bulgarian People's Republic government delegation, headed by Chairman Yugov, concluded its visit to China and left Beijing by plane this morning to visit the Mongolian People's Republic.

At 7:00 AM this morning, Chairman Yugov, accompanied by Premier Zhou, reviewed the honor guard at the airport.

Premier Zhou delivered a speech, representing the Chinese government and people in bidding farewell to the distinguished guests and wishing them a safe journey. Premier Zhou said that the friendly visit of the Bulgarian government delegation to China "has further enhanced the deep friendship and close unity that has long existed between the people of our two countries. We sincerely hope that Comrade Chairman Yugov and the comrades of the delegation will convey the most sincere friendly wishes of the Chinese people to the fraternal Bulgarian people."

Premier Zhou stated that the joint statement signed by the Chinese government and the Bulgarian government in Beijing "fully expresses the determination and desire of our two governments and peoples to further develop the friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries, strengthen the great unity of socialist countries, and safeguard world peace. The Chinese people will work tirelessly with the Bulgarian people to fully realize this determination and desire."

Chairman Yugov said in his farewell speech: "During our visit to your country, we have once again confirmed the unbreakable friendship that exists between our two peoples, developed cooperation between our two countries, consolidated the unity of all countries in the great socialist family, and fought to safeguard world peace."

Chairman Yugov also said that the Bulgarian government delegation received a brotherly and warm reception in China, and was deeply and indelibly impressed by the Chinese people's labor enthusiasm in building a happy new life under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party. He concluded by warmly congratulating the Chinese people on their more brilliant achievements in socialist construction.

At the end of their speeches, both Premiers unanimously shouted, "Long live the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Bulgarian people!"

Those who saw them off at the airport included Vice Premiers Deng Xiaoping, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian; Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Peng Zhen, and Li Weihan of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu; Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong; Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian; and representatives from various fields in China, as well as Bulgarian and other foreign envoys to China. ### 保政府代表团离京  周恩来总理到机场欢送贵宾

1957-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　保政府代表团离京
    周恩来总理到机场欢送贵宾
    据新华社12日讯　以于哥夫主席为首的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团结束对中国的访问后，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京，去蒙古人民共和国访问。
    今晨七时，于哥夫主席由周总理陪同，在机场检阅了仪仗队。
    周总理首先致词，代表中国政府和人民欢送贵宾，并祝他们一路平安。周总理说，保加利亚政府代表团这次在中国的友好访问，“进一步增进了我们两国人民之间久已存在的深厚友谊和亲密团结。我们衷心地希望于哥夫主席同志和代表团的同志们把中国人民最诚挚的友好愿望带给兄弟的保加利亚人民。”
    周总理说，中国政府和保加利亚政府这次在北京签订的联合声明，“充分表达了我们两国政府和人民进一步发展我们两国的友好合作关系、加强社会主义各国的伟大团结和维护世界和平的决心和愿望。中国人民将同保加利亚人民一起为这种决心和愿望的彻底实现进行不懈的努力。”
    于哥夫主席在他的告别讲话中说：“我们在你们国家的访问期间，再一次证实了在我们两国人民之间存在着牢不可破的友谊，发展了我们两国之间的合作，巩固了伟大的社会主义大家庭中各国的团结，为维护世界和平进行了斗争。”
    于哥夫主席还说，保加利亚政府代表团这次在中国受到了兄弟般的热情接待，并且对中国人民正在中国共产党的领导下建设幸福的新生活的劳动热情，留下了不可磨灭的深刻印象。他最后热情地祝贺中国人民在社会主义建设中取得更加辉煌的成就。
    两国总理在结束他们的讲话时，都一致高呼“中保两国人民牢不可破的友谊万岁！”
    到机场送行的，还有副总理邓小平、李富春、李先念，副委员长李济深、彭真、李维汉，最高人民法院院长董必武，国防委员会副主席张治中，外交部副部长张闻天和中国各方面的人士，以及保加利亚和各国驻中国的使节。（附图片）
