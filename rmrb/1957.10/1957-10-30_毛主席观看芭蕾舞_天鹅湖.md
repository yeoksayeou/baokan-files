Title: Chairman Mao Watches the Ballet "Swan Lake"
Author:
Date: 1957-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: Chairman Mao Zedong watched the performance of "Swan Lake" by the artists of the Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theatre of the Soviet Union in Beijing tonight.

During the intermission, Chairman Mao Zedong met with the leaders of the ballet troupe. After the performance, Chairman Mao also went on stage to meet with the actors and actresses and took a picture with them. (Picture attached)

Chairman Mao Zedong meets with the Soviet ballet dancers. Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo, photographer.

### 毛主席观看芭蕾舞“天鹅湖”

1957-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席观看芭蕾舞“天鹅湖”
    新华社29日讯　毛泽东主席在今天晚间观看了苏联新西伯利亚歌舞剧院芭蕾舞团艺术家们在北京演出的“天鹅湖”。
    在演出休息的时候，毛泽东主席接见了芭蕾舞团的领导人。演出结束以后，毛主席还登台同演员们会见，并一起照了像。（附图片）
