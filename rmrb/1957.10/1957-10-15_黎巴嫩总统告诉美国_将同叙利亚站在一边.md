Title: Lebanese President Tells U.S. Will Stand With Syria
Author:
Date: 1957-10-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, October 14th. Tass Beirut, October 14th: According to the "Oriental Daily," on October 13th, Lebanese President Camille Chamoun immediately received U.S. Ambassador to Lebanon Donald Heath regarding the landing of Egyptian troops in Latakia. He reiterated to Heath that if an armed conflict occurs, Lebanon will stand with Syria.

### 黎巴嫩总统告诉美国  将同叙利亚站在一边

1957-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

　　黎巴嫩总统告诉美国  将同叙利亚站在一边
    新华社14日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特14日讯：据“东方报”报道，黎巴嫩总统夏蒙就埃及军队在拉塔基亚登陆一事在10月13日立即接见了美国驻黎巴嫩大使唐纳德·希思，并向他重申，如果一旦发生武装冲突，黎巴嫩将站在叙利亚一边。
