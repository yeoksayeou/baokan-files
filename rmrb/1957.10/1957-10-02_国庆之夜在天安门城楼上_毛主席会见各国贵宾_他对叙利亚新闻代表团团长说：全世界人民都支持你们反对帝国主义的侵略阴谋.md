Title: National Day Night on Tiananmen Gate Tower Chairman Mao Meets with Foreign Guests He Said to the Head of the Syrian News Delegation: People All Over the World Support You in Opposing Imperialist Aggression Conspiracies
Author:
Date: 1957-10-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice-Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai watched fireworks and the mass celebration on Tiananmen Square tonight with distinguished guests from various countries on the Tiananmen Gate Tower.

Tonight, the Tiananmen Gate Tower was as joyful and lively as Tiananmen Square. There were grand dances and brilliant artistic performances on both sides of the tower. Guests from various countries celebrated this unforgettable night with our country's leaders.

At the beginning of this carnival night, Chairman Mao, Vice-Chairman Zhu, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou sat side by side with Prime Minister Kadar, Chairman Yugov, Dr. Hatta, Head of Delegation Aristov, Chairman Fehringer, Chairman Stambolic, Chief Justice U Myint Thein, Princess Norodom P. H. Devi and Prince Norodom Sihanouk of Cambodia, watching the ever-changing fireworks.

On the Tiananmen Gate Tower, Chairman Mao, Vice-Chairman Zhu, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou also separately met with the heads of delegations and prominent figures from various countries, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to China, and held friendly talks with them.

When Chairman Mao spoke with Tofik Yazji, the head of the Syrian journalists delegation, he wished Syria independence, prosperity, and strength, and wished the Syrian people victory over imperialist aggression conspiracies. Chairman Mao said: "People all over the world support you, support you in opposing imperialist aggression conspiracies. People are happy to see the unity between Syria, Egypt, and Saudi Arabia. All Arab people must unite." Chairman Mao also asked Tofik Yazji to convey his greetings to the President and Prime Minister of Syria, and to all those who are fighting for national independence.

The head of the Syrian journalists delegation expressed his gratitude for the Chinese people's support for Syria. He also conveyed the Syrian President's greetings to Chairman Mao. He said that he hoped the Syrian President would be able to meet with Chairman Mao one day. Chairman Mao said, "Very likely."

When meeting with Egyptian guests, Chairman Mao asked the guests to convey his greetings to President Nasser and wished the Egyptian people well.

Chairman Mao talked with American youths. He said that he hoped that the Chinese people and the American people could have friendly exchanges in peace.

Mrs. Hayward from New Zealand presented Chairman Mao with a New Zealand national costume shawl. This national costume was made of various feathers.

When Chairman Mao and others met with guests from various countries, the tower was filled with an atmosphere of harmony and friendship. Guests from various countries conveyed to Chairman Mao and others the friendly wishes of the people of their countries to the Chinese people.

### 国庆之夜在天安门城楼上  毛主席会见各国贵宾  他对叙利亚新闻代表团团长说：全世界人民都支持你们反对帝国主义的侵略阴谋

1957-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

    国庆之夜在天安门城楼上
    毛主席会见各国贵宾
    他对叙利亚新闻代表团团长说：全世界人民都支持你们反对帝国主义的侵略阴谋
    新华社1日讯  毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理今晚在天安门城楼同各国的贵宾们，观看了焰火和天安门广场上的群众大联欢。
    今晚，在天安门城楼上同天安门广场一样地欢乐、热闹。在城楼的两侧有盛大的舞会和精采的艺术表演。来自各国的客人们同我国的领导人一起欢度了这难忘的夜晚。
    在这狂欢之夜开始的时候，毛主席、朱副主席、刘委员长、周总理同卡达尔总理，于哥夫主席，哈达博士，阿里斯托夫团长，费林格主席，斯坦鲍利奇主席，吴敏登首席法官，以及柬埔寨帕花·黛维公主和夏卡朋王子等并排而坐，观看了变幻多采的焰火。
    在天安门城楼上，毛主席、朱副主席、刘委员长和周总理，还分别会见了各国代表团的负责人和著名人士及各国驻中国的使节，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    毛主席同叙利亚新闻工作者代表团团长托菲克·雅兹谈话时，祝叙利亚独立、繁荣和强大，祝叙利亚人民战胜帝国主义的侵略阴谋。毛主席说：“全世界人民都支持你们，支持你们反对帝国主义的侵略阴谋。人们高兴地看到叙利亚、埃及和沙特阿拉伯之间的团结，所有阿拉伯人民都要团结起来。”毛主席还请托菲克·雅兹转达他对叙利亚总统、总理和一切为民族独立而斗争的人们的问候。
    叙利亚新闻工作者代表团团长对中国人民对叙利亚的支持表示感谢。他还转达了叙利亚总统对毛主席的问候。他说，希望叙利亚总统能有一天同毛主席见面。毛主席说，很可能。
    在同埃及客人们会见的时候，毛主席请客人们转达他对纳赛尔总统的问候，并祝埃及人民好。
    毛主席同美国青年谈了话。他说，希望中国人民和美国人民能够在和平中友好往来。
    新西兰黑瓦德夫人把一件新西兰民族服装披肩献给了毛主席。这件民族服装是用各种羽毛编制成的。
    当毛主席等同各国来宾会见时，城楼上洋溢着和谐、友好的气氛，各国来宾们向毛主席等转达了各国人民对中国人民友好的祝愿。
