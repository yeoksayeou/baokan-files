Title: Syrian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Shalan Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The Syrian Republic's Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to the People's Republic of China, Adib Shalan, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 26th.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Wang Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Ma Mingji, Director of the Sino-Syrian Friendship Association and Deputy Secretary-General of the China Islamic Association; and Abudu Reheman.

The Egyptian Ambassador to China, Ragheb, and diplomatic officials from various countries' embassies in China also came to the airport to welcome him.

### 叙临时代办沙蓝到京

1957-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    叙临时代办沙蓝到京
    新华社26日讯　叙利亚共和国驻中华人民共和国大使馆临时代办阿迪卜·沙蓝在26日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有外交部礼宾司副司长王屏，中叙友好协会理事、中国伊斯兰教协会副秘书长马明基和阿不都·热合满。
    埃及驻华大使拉加卜和各国驻华使馆外交官员也到机场欢迎。
