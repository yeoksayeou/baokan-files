Title: USSR to Launch Heavier Artificial Satellite; First Nuclear Icebreaker to Be Launched This Year
Author:
Date: 1957-10-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th: The Soviet "Pravda" said in an article today that the Soviet Union will launch heavier artificial earth satellites. The speed of this heavier satellite also only needs to be eight kilometers per second, but launching it must overcome another series of difficulties, so launching a heavier satellite will be a new important stage in the development of science and technology.

　　The article said that in order to further study high altitudes, it is necessary to launch satellites at different operating altitudes, including satellites with altitudes of more than 1,000 kilometers. Launching such satellites is entirely possible with the power of modern science.

　　The article also said that it is possible to launch long-lasting satellites. Under modern rocket technology conditions, it is completely realistic to talk about launching satellites that exist for decades or hundreds of years. If a satellite can be launched to more than 1,000 kilometers, then it is in a complete vacuum and will therefore exist for a long time.

　　The article also said that it is still difficult to predict the various possibilities for mankind to use artificial satellites.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 13th: According to TASS, Genoa: The Fifth International Transportation Vehicle Congress closed in Genoa on the 12th. The Soviet representative attending the meeting announced at the meeting that the first nuclear icebreaker manufactured by the Soviet Union will be launched this year. This news aroused great interest among the participants.

　　This conference is held annually by the Genoa City Council to commemorate the great Genoese Columbus. This conference was attended by 700 representatives from 22 countries around the world, as well as representatives from the United Nations and the International Civil Aviation Organization.

### 苏联将发射更重的人造卫星  第一艘原子破冰船今年下水

1957-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联将发射更重的人造卫星
    第一艘原子破冰船今年下水
    新华社莫斯科13日电　苏联“真理报”今天在一篇文章中说，苏联将放射更重的人造地球卫星。这种更重的卫星的速度也只需要每秒八公里，但是放射它必须要克服另外一系列的困难，因此放射更重的卫星将是科学技术发展的新的重要阶段。
    文章说，为了进一步研究高空，必须放射不同运行高度的卫星，其中包括高度达一千公里以上的卫星。放射这种卫星是现代科学力量所完全可能办到的。
    文章又说，可能放射长期存在的卫星。在现代的火箭技术条件下，谈论关于放射存在几十年或是几百年的卫星问题是完全现实的。如果卫星能够放射到一千公里以上，那么它就处于完全的真空中，因而将长期存在。
    文章还说，还很难预测人类利用人造卫星的各种各样的可能性。
    据新华社13日讯　据塔斯社热那亚讯：第五届国际交通工具代表大会12日在热那亚闭幕。参加会议的苏联代表在会上宣布说，苏联制造的第一艘原子破冰船今年就要下水，这个消息引起了与会人士的浓厚兴趣。
    这个大会是由热那亚市议会发起为纪念伟大的热那亚人哥伦布而每年举行的。参加这次大会的有来自世界上二十二个国家的七百名代表，以及联合国和国际民用航空组织的代表。
