Title: Egyptian Officers Arrive in Aleppo
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, October 27th: The "Al-Rai al-Aam" newspaper reported today that a group of Egyptian officers has arrived in the northern Syrian city of Aleppo to help train the Syrian People's Resistance Army in street fighting.

The report said that some of these Egyptian officers had participated in the street fighting in Port Said last year against the British and French aggressors.

### 埃及军官抵阿勒颇

1957-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    埃及军官抵阿勒颇
    新华社大马士革27日电　“舆论报”今天报道，一批埃及军官已经到达叙利亚北部城市阿勒颇，帮助训练叙利亚人民抗敌军学习巷战。
    消息说，这些埃及军官中，有些曾经参加过去年塞得港反抗英法侵略者的巷战。
