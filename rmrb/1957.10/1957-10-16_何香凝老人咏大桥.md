Title: Elder He Xiangning Composes a Poem on the Bridge
Author:
Date: 1957-10-16
Page: 4

He Xiangning, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, wrote a poem on the day she attended the ceremony for the completion and opening of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, praising this great bridge. The full text of the poem is as follows:

　　The great bridge is completed today,
　　Thanks to the workers' contribution to national construction;
　　Even more thanks to the Soviet Union's cooperation and assistance,
　　From now on, the people will enjoy endless happiness.

### 何香凝老人咏大桥

1957-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　何香凝老人咏大桥
    华侨事务委员会主任何香凝，在参加武汉长江大桥落成通车典礼的当天，写了一首诗，歌颂这座伟大的桥梁。诗的全文如下：
    大桥今日落成中，感谢工人建国功；
    更谢苏联同协力，民间从此乐无穷。
