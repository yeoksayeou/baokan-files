Title: Artificial Satellite to Pass Over Our Country Again Today
Author:
Date: 1957-10-11
Page: 6

    Artificial Satellite to Pass Over Our Country Again Today
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th - According to TASS broadcasts, the artificial satellite will pass over Chinese cities such as Shanghai, Lanzhou, and Wuhan on the 10th and 11th.
    When passing over China, the satellite's travel time (Beijing time) and direction are as follows: on the 10th, it will fly from Bandung, Indonesia, at 9:00 PM, reach Shanghai at 9:10 PM, and fly to Seoul, Korea, at 9:12 PM. At 9:58 PM, it will pass over Santiago, Chile, reach Lanzhou at 10:48 PM, and fly to Chita, USSR, at 10:53 PM. On the 11th, it will fly from Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia, at 7:09 AM, reach Hankou at 7:14 AM, and fly to Canberra, Australia, at 7:36 AM.

### 人造卫星今日再经我国

1957-10-11
第6版()
专栏：

    人造卫星今日再经我国
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社广播，人造卫星在10日、11日将先后经过中国的上海、兰州、武汉等城市。
    经过中国时，卫星运行的时间（北京时间）和方向是，10日二十一点正从印度尼西亚的万隆飞来，二十一点十分到上海，二十一点十二分飞到朝鲜的汉城。二十一点五十八分经过智利的圣地亚哥，二十二点四十八分到达兰州，二十二点五十三分飞到苏联的赤塔。11日七点九分由蒙古的乌兰巴托飞来，七点十四分到达汉口，七点三十六分飞到澳大利亚的堪培拉。
