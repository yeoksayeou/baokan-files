Title: Congratulatory Telegram from Hungarian Party and Government Leaders
Author:
Date: 1957-10-01
Page: 3

To Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Mao Zedong,
Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Liu Shaoqi,
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Zhou Enlai:

    On the occasion of the eighth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, and the entire Hungarian people, I extend warm and fraternal greetings to you and the entire Chinese people. Our people follow with heartfelt admiration and great concern the remarkable achievements of the 600 million brotherly Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party in their struggle to build socialism. The efforts of the Chinese people for the prosperity of their motherland have a great impact on the socialist camp countries and the countries and peoples fighting for national independence. The friendship and mutual assistance between our two peoples, based on the spirit of proletarian internationalism, have recently been further deepened and strengthened. The Hungarian people and the Hungarian government will always be grateful for the great spiritual and material support given by the Chinese people and the Chinese government, together with the Soviet Union and other socialist brother countries, to the Hungarian people who fought against the counter-revolution. We will make every effort to ensure that the friendship and cooperation between the Hungarian and Chinese peoples, who are both in the ranks of the socialist camp led by the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China, will continue to be strengthened in the future, thereby promoting our achievements in the common struggle to maintain lasting peace and ease international tensions.

              István Dobi, President of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic
            János Kádár, Premier of the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government,
                First Secretary of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party
                              Budapest, September 29, 1957

### 匈牙利党政领导人的贺电

1957-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    匈牙利党政领导人的贺电中华人民共和国主席，中国共产党主席毛泽东同志中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇同志中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    当中华人民共和国建国八周年的时候，谨代表匈牙利人民共和国主席团、匈牙利人民共和国工农革命政府、匈牙利社会主义工人党和全体匈牙利人民向你们和全中国人民致热烈的、兄弟的敬礼。我国人民怀着衷心钦佩和十分关切的心情注视着六亿兄弟的中国人民在中国共产党的领导下为建设社会主义而进行的斗争中所取得的卓越成就。中国人民为自己祖国的繁荣所做的努力对于社会主义阵营各国和正在为争取民族独立而斗争的国家和人民起着巨大的影响。我们两国人民之间的友谊和本着无产阶级国际主义精神所进行的相互帮助，近来进一步加深和加强了。匈牙利人民和匈牙利政府对于中国人民和中国政府同苏联和其他社会主义兄弟国家一起给与同反革命作了斗争的匈牙利人民的巨大精神和物质支持将永远感激。我们将尽一切努力，使都是处在以苏联和中华人民共和国为首的社会主义阵营行列中的匈中两国人民的友谊和合作今后继续加强，从而促使我们在维护持久和平和缓和国际紧张局势的共同斗争中获得成就。
              匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席  道比·伊斯特万
            匈牙利工农革命政府总理、匈牙利社会主义
                工人党第一书记  卡达尔·亚诺什
                              1957年9月29日于布达佩斯
