Title: Maluku Art Troupe Performs for Troops Stationed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 8th -- The Indonesian Maluku Art Friendship Delegation performed for the Chinese People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Beijing tonight.

Each performance by the Indonesian artists won thunderous applause from the PLA officers and soldiers. During the intermission, General Su Yu, Senior General Xiao Hua, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong met with the head of the art troupe, Sain Nasution, the deputy head of the troupe, Lisagoda, and the actors Nikiyulu Bellubesi, Amat Anwar, and Lensumbure, and had cordial conversations with them.

At the end of the performance, General Su Yu and others went on stage to congratulate the actors on the success of their performance.

### 马鲁古艺术团为驻京部队演出

1957-10-09
第3版()
专栏：

    马鲁古艺术团为驻京部队演出
    新华社8日讯　印度尼西亚马鲁古艺术友好访问团今晚为中国人民解放军驻京部队演出。
    印度尼西亚艺术家们演出的每一个节目，都博得解放军官兵们春雷般的掌声。演出休息时，粟裕大将、萧华上将和萧向荣中将接见了艺术团团长赛因·纳苏蒂安、副团长利萨哥达和演员妮基尤露·贝露贝西、阿玛特·安哇尔、伦苏姆布勒，并且同他们亲切地交谈。
    演出结束，粟裕大将等走上舞台祝贺演员们演出的成功。
