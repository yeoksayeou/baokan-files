Title: Prime Minister Daoud Leaves Kunming for Home, Expresses Gratitude to Premier Zhou Enlai in a Farewell Telegram
Author:
Date: 1957-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, October 30th: The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammed Daoud, concluded his visit to our country and departed from Kunming by special plane for his home country on the 30th.

Prime Minister Daoud arrived in Kunming at 11:00 AM by special plane, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu. Vice Governor of Yunnan Province Zhang Chong, Mayor of Kunming Pan Shuoduan, and other officials welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport. At noon, Vice Governor Zhang Chong hosted a farewell banquet for Prime Minister Daoud.

At 12:45 PM, Prime Minister Daoud returned to the airport. More than 3,000 people from all ethnic groups and walks of life, who had gathered at the airport to see off the distinguished guests, cheered him warmly. Accompanied by Vice Governor Zhang Chong, the Prime Minister reviewed the guard of honor and addressed the crowd of over 3,000 people. He said that as he was leaving China, his heart was filled with beautiful and pleasant impressions, and he would cherish these impressions forever. The Prime Minister also expressed his heartfelt thanks and sincere gratitude to the Chinese government and people for their warm hospitality.

The Ambassador of the Kingdom of Afghanistan to China, Samad, accompanied Prime Minister Daoud on his return trip today.

The Deputy Director of the Information and Publications Department of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Abdul Sattar Shalizai, the Chairman of the Olympic Federation, Mohammed Saraj, and the Director of Kabul Radio Station, Abdul Rauf Bennawa, who accompanied Prime Minister Daoud on his visit to our country, will continue their visit in our country.

Xinhua News Agency: October 30th: Sardar Mohammed Daoud, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, and his entourage visited the Shanghai State-owned Cigarette Factory and Fudan University in Shanghai on the 29th. In the afternoon, they took a special plane to Wuhan. In Wuhan, they visited the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge and Wuhan University. On the morning of the 30th, they went to Kunming.

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: The full text of the telegram of thanks from the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Daoud, to Premier Zhou Enlai is as follows:
To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

Having received the kind and sincere hospitality of the Chinese people and the Chinese government in China, as I am about to leave China, I express my heartfelt gratitude to Your Excellency, your government, and your people with the utmost sincerity. I will always remember the pleasant moments I spent with Your Excellency and other Chinese friends. I wish Your Excellency and the Chinese people greater achievements and respectfully ask Your Excellency to convey my friendly wishes to your people and my respect to the Chairman.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Prime Minister of Afghanistan, Mohammed Daoud
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　October 30, 1957, on the plane

### 达乌德首相离昆明回国  临行前致电周恩来总理表示感谢

1957-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

  　达乌德首相离昆明回国
    临行前致电周恩来总理表示感谢
    据新华社昆明30日电　阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德结束了在我国的访问，30日乘专机途经昆明回国。
    达乌德首相是乘专机在上午十一时在外交部副部长章汉夫陪同下到达昆明的。云南省副省长张冲、昆明市市长潘朔端等负责人在机场上欢迎贵宾。中午，副省长张冲为达乌德首相设宴饯行。
    中午十二时四十五分，达乌德首相回到机场。聚集在机场上欢送贵宾的三千多各族各界群众向他热烈地欢呼。首相由张冲副省长陪同检阅了仪仗队，并且向三千多群众讲了话。他说，他在离开中国的时候心中荡漾着美好而愉快的印象，他愿意永远珍视这些印象。首相还对中国政府和人民的殷勤款待表示由衷的谢意和诚恳的感激。
    阿富汗王国驻中国大使萨马德今天陪同达乌德首相返国。
    随达乌德首相来我国访问的阿富汗王国新闻和出版署副署长阿卜杜勒·萨塔尔·沙利扎伊、奥林匹克联合会主席穆罕默德·萨拉季和喀布尔广播电台台长阿卜杜勒·拉奥夫·本纳瓦等人将在我国继续进行访问。
    据新华社讯　阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德和随行人员，29日在上海参观了国营上海卷烟工厂和复旦大学。下午乘专机去武汉。在武汉参观了武汉长江大桥和武汉大学。30日晨前往昆明。
    新华社30日讯　阿富汗王国首相达乌德给周恩来总理的感谢电，全文如下：北京中华人民共和国周恩来总理阁下：
    我在中国受到了中国人民和中国政府的亲切的和诚挚的款待，在我刚离开中国的时候，我怀着最诚挚的心情向阁下、贵国政府和贵国人民表达我的衷心的谢意。我将永远记得我同阁下以及其他中国朋友们在一起度过的愉快的时刻。我祝阁下和中国人民取得更多的成就，并谨请阁下向贵国人民转达我的友好的愿望，向主席阁下转达我的敬意。
        阿富汗首相　穆罕默德·达乌德
        1957年10月30日在飞机上
