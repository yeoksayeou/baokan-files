Title: The Polish United Workers' Party Passes Resolution on Screening Party Members
Author: 
Date: 1957-10-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 27th. According to a report by the Polish Press Agency: The tenth plenary session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, which lasted for three days, concluded on October 26th. First Secretary of the Central Committee, Gomulka, summarized the discussion.

    The plenary session unanimously passed the resolution on screening Party members.

### 波兰统一工人党通过审查党员的决议

1957-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党通过审查党员的决议
    新华社华沙27日电　据波兰通讯社报道：波兰统一工人党第十次中央全会为期三天的讨论已经在10月26日结束。中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡总结了这一讨论。
    全体会议一致通过了关于审查党员的决议。
