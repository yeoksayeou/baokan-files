Title: British International Trade Promotion Association Vice Chairman Mrs. Robinson Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 5

    British International Trade Promotion Association Vice Chairman
    Mrs. Robinson Leaves Beijing for Home

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd — Mrs. Robinson, Vice Chairman of the British International Trade Promotion Association and Professor at the University of Cambridge in England, concluded her visit to China and left Beijing by plane today to return home.

### 英国国际贸易促进会副主席  罗宾逊夫人离京返国

1957-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    英国国际贸易促进会副主席
    罗宾逊夫人离京返国
    据新华社3日讯　英国国际贸易促进会副主席、英国剑桥大学教授罗宾逊夫人在中国访问结束，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
