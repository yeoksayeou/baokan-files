Title: Carrier Rocket Overtakes Satellite Again
Author:
Date: 1957-10-29
Page: 5

    Carrier Rocket Overtakes Satellite Again
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th — TASS reports from Moscow: As of 6:00 AM Moscow time on October 28th, the artificial satellite had orbited the Earth more than 348 times, covering a distance of 15,103,000 kilometers.
    The carrier rocket caught up with the artificial satellite on the evening of October 28th, meaning it had orbited the Earth one more time than the artificial satellite. The carrier rocket will then continue to lead.

### 运载火箭又跑在卫星前头

1957-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    运载火箭又跑在卫星前头
    据新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：到10月28日莫斯科时间六点整为止，人造卫星已经绕行地球三百四十八圈多，经过路程一五，一○三，○○○公里。
    运载火箭于10月28日傍晚赶上人造卫星，即比人造卫星多绕行地球一圈。然后运载火箭将继续领先。
