Title: Cultural Delegation from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 21st. A cultural delegation of ten people from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Qu Huy Can, Deputy Minister of Culture of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 21st. The delegation is visiting China according to the 1957 cultural cooperation plan between China and Vietnam. Xia Yan, Deputy Minister of Culture, and people from Beijing's cultural circles, as well as Nguyen Khang, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and embassy staff members welcomed them at the station.

### 越南民主共和国文化考察团到京

1957-10-22
第3版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国文化考察团到京
    新华社21日讯　越南民主共和国文化考察团一行十人，在团长、越南民主共和国文化部副部长瞿辉近率领下，21日晚上坐火车到达北京。考察团是根据中越两国1957年文化合作计划来中国访问的。文化部副部长夏衍和北京文化界人士，以及越南民主共和国驻中国大使阮康和大使馆人员都到车站欢迎。
