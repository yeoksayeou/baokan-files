Title: Chairman Mao Meets and Hosts Banquet for Prime Minister Daoud
Author:
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th — Chairman Mao Zedong met with Sardar Mohammed Daoud, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, and his entourage on the afternoon of the 25th.

Present at the meeting were Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling, Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Wentian, and Ambassador Ding Guoyu. Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad, was also present at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th — Chairman Mao Zedong hosted a banquet in the Qin Zheng Dian, Zhongnanhai, on the evening of the 25th to welcome Sardar Mohammed Daoud, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. Prime Minister Daoud's entourage and Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad, were invited to the banquet.

Among those present were Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairmen Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, and Nie Rongzhen, President Dong Biwu, as well as Vice Chairman Zhang Zhizhong of the National Defense Council, Vice Chairman Burhan of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Ding Guoyu, and relevant responsible persons. ### 毛主席接见并设宴欢迎达乌德首相

1957-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见并设宴欢迎达乌德首相
    新华社25日讯　毛泽东主席在25日下午接见了阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德和随行人员。
    接见时在座的，有朱德副主席，周恩来总理，宋庆龄副委员长，贺龙、陈毅副总理，张闻天副部长和丁国钰大使。接见时，阿富汗驻中国大使萨马德也在座。
    新华社25日讯　毛泽东主席25日晚在中南海勤政殿设宴欢迎阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德。达乌德首相的随行人员和阿富汗驻中国大使萨马德都应邀出席宴会。
    出席作陪的，有朱德副主席，周恩来总理，宋庆龄、李济深、郭沫若、彭真、陈叔通副委员长，邓子恢、贺龙、陈毅、聂荣臻副总理，董必武院长，以及国防委员会副主席张治中，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，外交部副部长张闻天，中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰和有关的负责人。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席接见达乌德首相。前排自左至右：贺龙、萨马德、宋庆龄、达乌德、毛泽东、朱德、波帕尔、周恩来、帕日瓦克、陈毅。
  新华社记者　侯波、邹健东摄
