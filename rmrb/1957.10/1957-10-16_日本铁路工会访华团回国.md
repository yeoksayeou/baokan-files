Title: Japanese Railway Union Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-10-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 15th - The Japanese National Railway Workers' Union delegation, led by Mr. Izumi Matazo, left Guangzhou this morning to return to their country.
    The delegation arrived in Guangzhou on the morning of the 14th from Hangzhou. On the same day, they visited the commodities at the Chinese Export Commodities Fair and toured the scenic spots of Guangzhou.

### 日本铁路工会访华团回国

1957-10-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　日本铁路工会访华团回国
    新华社广州15日电　日本国营铁路工会访华代表团由泉又藏先生率领，今天早晨离开广州回国。
    代表团是14日上午从杭州来到广州的，当天在这里参观了中国商品出口交易会里的商品，还游览了广州的名胜。
