Title: Li Weihan Receives Egyptian Parliamentarians from the Egyptian Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-13
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, 12th — Li Weihan, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Egyptian parliamentarians and other representatives from the Egyptian Youth Delegation on the morning of the 12th.

　　Present at the meeting were Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Standing Committee members Gao Chongmin, Xie Fumin, Li Shucheng, and Liu Xiyuan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation.

### 李维汉接见埃及青年代表团中的埃及国会议员

1957-10-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　李维汉接见埃及青年代表团中的埃及国会议员
    新华社12日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉，12日上午接见了埃及青年代表团中的埃及国会议员和其他代表。
    接见时在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，常务委员会委员高崇民、谢扶民、李书城，和中华全国民主青年联合会副主席刘西元。
