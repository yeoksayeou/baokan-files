Title: Documentary Films of Honored Guests Arriving in Beijing Completed
Author:
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 8

The capital, cloaked in autumn attire and filled with a friendly and joyous atmosphere, successively welcomed many international honored guests who were invited to visit our country. The Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio has filmed these memorable scenes and has now completed the following five documentary films: "Welcoming Yugoslavian Honored Guests," "Bulgarian Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing," "Dr. Hatta Arrives in Beijing," "Hungarian Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing," and "Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan Arrives in Beijing." These films will soon be screened in major cities.

### 贵宾到京的纪录片已制成

1957-10-09
第8版()
专栏：

    贵宾到京的纪录片已制成
    披上秋装的首都，充满友好欢愉的气氛里，接二连三地迎来了应邀访问我国的许多国际贵宾。中央新闻纪录电影制片总厂已经把这些可纪念的场面拍摄下来，现在已制成如下五部纪录片：“欢迎南斯拉夫贵宾”，“保加利亚政府代表团到北京”，“哈达博士到北京”，“匈牙利政府代表团到北京”，“印度副总统拉达克里希南到北京”。这些片子不日即将在各大城市上映。
