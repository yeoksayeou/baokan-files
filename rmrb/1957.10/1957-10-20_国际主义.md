Title: Internationalism
Author: Ye Shengtao
Date: 1957-10-20
Page: 8

　　Internationalism
　　Ye Shengtao
　　"One hates to see goods wasted on the ground,
　　Not necessarily to be stored for oneself.
　　One hates to see strength not exerted,
　　Not necessarily for one's own sake."
　　Extracting these precious words from the chapter "Li Yun,"
　　I praise the internationalism of Soviet friends.
　　It's not difficult to talk about these principles,
　　What's valuable is putting them into practice.
　　Doing it once in a while is nothing special,
　　What's valuable is consistency throughout.
　　You, the completely new type of Soviet people,
　　Internationalism has long been a way of life.
　　We have endless deep impressions,
　　You sweat and rack your brains for us.
　　Our construction is like the grass and trees of early spring,
　　Which aspect doesn't bear your hard work?
　　Summarized into the four characters "selfless assistance,"
　　Highlighting the spirit of your internationalism.
　　Your well-being is our well-being,
　　Our well-being is your well-being.
　　For the happiness of mankind, for world peace,
　　We are comrades and share the same path.
　　Let us walk forward hand in hand,
　　Internationalism shines like the sun.

### 国际主义
叶圣陶
1957-10-20
第8版()
专栏：十月颂歌

　　国际主义
　　叶圣陶
　　“货恶其弃于地也，
　　不必藏于己。
　　力恶其不出于身也，
　　不必为己。”
　　摘出“礼运”篇珍宝似的语句，
　　赞颂苏联朋友的国际主义。
　　说说这些道理不算难，
　　可贵在躬行实践。
　　偶一为之也就不稀罕，
　　可贵在始终一贯。
　　你们，完全新型的苏维埃人，
　　国际主义早化为生活习惯。
　　我们有说不完的深刻印象，
　　你们为我们淌汗绞脑筋。
　　我们的建设像当春的草木，
　　哪一项不沾你们的辛勤？
　　归结成“无私援助”四个大字，
　　标榜你们国际主义的精神。
　　你们好就是我们好，
　　我们好就是你们好。
　　为人类幸福，为世界和平，
　　咱们同志又同道。
　　咱们手挽着手朝前走，
　　国际主义像阳光一样普照。
