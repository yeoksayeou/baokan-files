Title: Speecheski Holds Reception in Beijing to Celebrate Polish Army Day
Author:
Date: 1957-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th October. General Speecheski, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Poland, who is on a friendly visit to our country, held a reception in Beijing on the afternoon of the 12th to celebrate the 14th anniversary of the founding of the Polish People's Army.

Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of our country, General Tan Zheng, Vice Minister of National Defense, General Wang Shusheng, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan were invited to attend the reception.

General Speecheski said in his speech at the reception that the Polish military delegation felt at home celebrating their army's festival today on Chinese soil with their Chinese brothers. He said that the Polish army, educated with Marxist-Leninist ideology, is an inseparable part of the great strength of the socialist camp. He said, "I would like to guarantee that the Polish army will never disappoint its people, its allies, and its friends who are jointly struggling for the noble goals of peace and socialism."

Marshal Peng Dehuai said that we were particularly excited to be able to celebrate the glorious festival of the Polish army with General Speecheski and the comrades of the Polish military delegation in Beijing. He said that in the twelve years after the war, the Polish army has been carrying out the work of modernization based on its experience accumulated in the anti-fascist war and the experience of the great Soviet army, and it has now become an important force in defending European and world peace.

Also invited to attend today's reception were senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Xu Guangda, General Xiao Hua, General Gan Siqi, General Hong Xuezhi, Admiral Wang Hongkun, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Cao Lihuai, and more than 200 officers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force.

The entire Polish military delegation, Kirylok, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Poland to China, Colonel Hankiewicz, Military Attaché, and diplomatic envoys and military attachés of various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 庆祝波兰建军节  斯彼哈尔斯基在北京举行招待会

1957-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝波兰建军节
    斯彼哈尔斯基在北京举行招待会
    新华社12日讯　在我国进行友好访问的波兰人民共和国国防部长斯彼哈尔斯基上将，为庆祝波兰人民军建军十四周年，12日下午在北京举行招待会。
    我国国防部长彭德怀元帅、国防部副部长谭政大将、王树声大将和外交部副部长曾涌泉应邀出席了招待会。
    斯彼哈尔斯基上将在招待会上讲话说，波兰军事代表团今天在中国的土地上，跟中国兄弟一道庆祝自己军队的节日，感到像在家里一样。他说，以马克思列宁主义思想教育出来的波兰军队，是社会主义阵营伟大力量的不可分割的一部分。他说，我愿意保证，波兰军队永远不会辜负自己的人民、自己的盟友以及为和平、为社会主义的崇高目标而共同奋斗的朋友们的愿望。
    彭德怀元帅讲话说，我们能够在北京跟斯彼哈尔斯基上将以及波兰军事代表团的同志，一起庆祝波兰军队的光荣节日，感到特别兴奋。他说，波兰军队在战后的十二年中，根据自己在反法西斯战争中所积累的经验以及伟大苏联军队的经验，进行着现代化的建设工作，它现在已经成为捍卫欧洲和世界和平的一支重要力量。
    应邀出席今天招待会的有许光达大将、萧华上将、甘泗淇上将、洪学智上将、王宏坤海军上将、萧向荣中将、曹里怀空军中将等中国人民解放军高级将领和陆、海、空军军官等二百多人。
    波兰军事代表团全体人员，波兰人民共和国驻华大使基里洛克、武官汉凯维奇上校，以及各国驻华使节和武官也应邀出席了招待会。
