Title: Polish Scientific and Technological Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: The Polish Scientific and Technological Delegation, consisting of four members, led by Z. Koi, Chairman of the Polish Group of the Joint Standing Committee for Sino-Polish Technical and Technological Science Cooperation and Deputy Minister of the Polish Ministry of Heavy Industry, arrived in Beijing on the 21st to attend the fourth session of the Joint Standing Committee for Sino-Polish Technical and Technological Science Cooperation.

### 波科学技术代表团到京

1957-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    波科学技术代表团到京
    据新华社22日讯　前来我国参加中国—波兰技术和技术科学合作联合常任委员会第四届会议的波兰科学技术代表团一行四人，在委员会波兰组主席、波兰重工业部副部长兹·科伊率领下已在21日到达北京。
