Title: Saudi and Turkish Ministers of State Discuss So-called "Mediation" Issue
Author:
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th dispatch from Zahran: Saudi Arabian King Saud and Turkish Minister of State Zorlu issued a joint communiqué on the 24th, saying that during their talks they studied King Saud's desire to conduct so-called "mediation" between Syria and Turkey, and considered other Middle East issues.

The Turkish Minister of State expressed his welcome to the so-called "mediation" proposal in the joint communiqué. The King of Saudi Arabia emphasized that the purpose of his proposal was to save the "Middle East from unforeseen disasters."

Zorlu arrived in Zahran on the 23rd to begin talks with Saudi Arabia.

### 沙特和土耳其国务部长会谈所谓“调解”问题

1957-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    沙特和土耳其国务部长会谈所谓“调解”问题
    据新华社25日讯　扎赫兰消息：沙特阿拉伯国王沙特和土耳其国务部长佐鲁24日发表联合公报说，他们在会谈中研究了沙特国王想对叙利亚和土耳其进行所谓“调解”的愿望，并且考虑了中东其他问题。
    土耳其国务部长在联合公报中表示欢迎所谓“调解”的建议。沙特阿拉伯国王强调他的建议的目的是要使“中东免遭预见不到的灾难”。
    佐鲁是在23日到达扎赫兰同沙特开始会谈的。
