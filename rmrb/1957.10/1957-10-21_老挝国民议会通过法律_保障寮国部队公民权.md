Title: Lao National Assembly Passes Law Guaranteeing Citizenship Rights for Pathet Lao Fighting Units

Author:

Date: 1957-10-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 20th - Vientiane Dispatch: The Lao Kingdom National Assembly passed a law on the 19th guaranteeing citizenship rights for personnel of the Pathet Lao Fighting Units after they participate in national common life.

The law was passed by the National Assembly with twenty votes in favor and one abstention. According to the agreement reached last August between the Lao Kingdom government and the Pathet Lao Fighting Units, the Kingdom government guarantees no discrimination against the Pathet Lao Fighting Units and former resistance personnel.

The law stipulates that personnel of the Pathet Lao Fighting Units shall not be subject to any discrimination, retaliation, or prosecution in courts or administrative disciplinary committees for military, political, or administrative acts prior to the promulgation of this law. Judgments already pronounced against personnel of the Pathet Lao Fighting Units will be revoked, and detainees will be released immediately.

### 老挝国民议会通过法律  保障寮国部队公民权

1957-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝国民议会通过法律
    保障寮国部队公民权
    据新华社河内20日电  万象消息：老挝王国国民议会19日通过了一项法律，保证寮国战斗部队人员在参加国家共同生活以后享有公民权。
    这项法律是国民议会以二十票赞成和一票弃权通过的。根据老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队在去年8月达成的协议，王国政府保证不歧视寮国战斗部队和前抗战人员。
    这项法律规定，寮国战斗部队人员不能因为颁布这项法律以前的军事、政治或行政性质的行为而成为任何歧视、报复和在法庭或行政纪律委员会上起诉的对象。对寮国战斗部队人员已经宣布的判决将撤回，被拘留者将立即释放。
