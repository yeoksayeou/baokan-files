Title: Chinese and Soviet Bridge Experts Study Yangtze River Bridge
Author:
Date: 1957-10-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 27th: Chinese and Soviet bridge experts concluded their scientific research work on the Yangtze River Bridge on the 25th. This research work started on the 21st, and there were more than 20 research projects in total. Through this research, the bridge experts will write a book and use it as a blueprint for dealing with relevant issues in large bridge projects in the future.

During the five-day research activity, Chinese and Soviet experts conducted various complex experiments on the railway and highway bridges simultaneously using trains and automobiles, and recorded the relevant data in detail.

### 中苏桥梁专家研究长江大桥

1957-10-28
第3版()
专栏：

    中苏桥梁专家研究长江大桥
    据新华社武汉27日电　中苏桥梁专家已经在25日结束了在长江大桥上进行的科学研究工作。这次研究工作是从21日开始的，全部研究项目有二十多个。通过这次研究，桥梁专家们将写一本书，并将它作为今后处理大型桥梁工程中有关问题的蓝本。
    在历时五天的研究活动中，中苏专家们曾用火车、汽车同时在铁路桥、公路桥上进行了各种复杂的试验，并且详细地记录了有关资料。
