Title: Poland, Bulgaria and Two Other Countries Form Delegations to Participate in October Revolution Celebration Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1957-10-30
Page: 6

    Participating in the October Revolution Celebration Ceremony
    Poland, Bulgaria and Two Other Countries Form Delegations
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 29th: According to the Polish Press Agency, the Polish party and government delegation to participate in the 40th anniversary celebration of the October Revolution has been formed. The head of the delegation is Władysław Gomułka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party. Members of the delegation include Józef Cyrankiewicz, Zenon Nowak, Marian Rybicki, and Aleksander Zawadzki, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Stefan Ignar, Chairman of the Central Committee of the United People's Party, and Leon Kulczyński, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Democratic Party.
    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, October 29th: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers, the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Council of Ministers have decided to send a delegation to Moscow to participate in the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Revolution.
    The members of the delegation are: Zhivkov (head of the delegation), Yugov, Terelkov, Tsankov, Mikhailov, and Dragoicheva.
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Tokyo: The Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, has decided to send a delegation headed by Yoshio Shiga, member of the Standing Presidium of the JCP Central Committee, to Moscow to participate in the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union.
    The members of the delegation are Koreto Kurahara, member of the JCP Central Committee and Minister of Culture, and Takeo Takei, editorial board member of the JCP Central Committee's organ "Akahata" and Minister of International Affairs, as an attendant.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta: The delegation of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, led by Sudisman, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, set off for Moscow on the 24th to participate in the 40th anniversary celebration of the October Revolution. Among those who saw off the delegation at the airport were Aidit, Lukman, Njoto and other leaders of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, as well as people from all walks of life.

### 参加十月革命节庆祝典礼  波保等四国组成代表团

1957-10-30
第6版()
专栏：

    参加十月革命节庆祝典礼
    波保等四国组成代表团
    新华社华沙29日电　据波兰通讯社报道，参加十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼的波兰党政代表团已经组成。代表团团长是波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡，代表团团员有统一工人党中央政治局委员西伦凯维兹、奥哈布、萨姆布罗夫斯基、萨瓦茨基，统一农民党中央委员会主席伊格纳尔和民主党中央委员会主席库尔钦斯基。
    新华社索非亚29日电　保加利亚共产党中央委员会和部长会议应苏联共产党中央委员会和部长会议的邀请，决定派代表团去莫斯科参加伟大的十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼。
    代表团的成员是：日夫科夫（团长）、于哥夫、特莱科夫、赞科夫、米哈伊洛夫和德拉戈伊切娃。
    新华社讯　东京消息：日本共产党中央委员会应苏联共产党中央委员会的邀请，决定派遣以日共中央常任干部会委员志贺义雄为团长的代表团，去莫斯科参加苏联十月社会主义革命四十周年纪念典礼。
    代表团团员是日共中央委员、文化部长藏原帷人，随员是日共中央机关报“赤旗报”编辑委员、国际部长武井武夫。
    据新华社雅加达电　由印度尼西亚共产党中央政治局委员苏蒂斯曼率领的印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会代表团，已经在24日启程前往莫斯科参加十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼。在机场欢送这个代表团的有艾地、鲁克曼、约多等印度尼西亚共产党中央负责人以及各界群众。
