Title: Chairman Mao Sends Telegram of Congratulations on the Accession of the New King of Norway
Author:
Date: 1957-10-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Chairman Mao Zedong's telegram of congratulations to His Majesty King Olav V of Norway. The text of the telegram is as follows:
To His Majesty King Olav V of Norway:

　　On the occasion of Your Majesty's accession to the throne, I extend my congratulations to Your Majesty and the Norwegian people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name.

　　I wish Your Majesty good health and the Norwegian people prosperity and happiness.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the People's Republic of China  Mao Zedong
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 30, 1957, Beijing

### 毛主席电贺挪威新国王继位

1957-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺挪威新国王继位
    新华社2日讯  毛泽东主席致挪威王国国王奥拉夫五世的贺电。电文如下：挪威王国国王奥拉夫五世陛下：
    当陛下继位的时候，我谨代表中国人民并以我个人的名义向陛下和挪威人民表示祝贺。
    祝陛下身体健康，挪威人民繁荣幸福。
                                  中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东
                                  1957年9月30日于北京
