Title: Jordan's Political Situation Intensifies; Heavy Guard Around the Royal Palace
Author:
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th dispatch from Tel Aviv: According to Israeli newspapers, the domestic political situation in Jordan has greatly intensified over the past week.

Newspaper reports say that three thousand Arab Legion soldiers have been deployed in Amman, and special guards are heavily guarding King Hussein's palace. Sabotage activities have increased. Patriots are distributing anti-government propaganda.

### 约旦政局密锣紧鼓  王宫周围警备森严

1957-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    约旦政局密锣紧鼓
    王宫周围警备森严
    新华社24日讯　特拉维夫消息：据以色列报纸报道，过去一周来约旦国内政治局势大大紧张了。
    报纸消息说，有三千名阿拉伯军团的士兵已经在安曼布防，特别警卫队在侯赛因国王的王宫周围警备森严。破坏活动增加了。爱国者在散发反政府的宣传品。
