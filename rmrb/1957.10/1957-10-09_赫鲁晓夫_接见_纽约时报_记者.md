Title: Khrushchev Receives "New York Times" Reporter
Author:
Date: 1957-10-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th—According to TASS, Moscow: At the request of James Reston, chief diplomatic correspondent of the American "New York Times," Khrushchev, First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, received him on the 7th and held talks. The talks involved the international situation and Soviet-American relations.

　　Ilyichev, Director of the Information Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, and William Jordan, the "New York Times" correspondent stationed in Moscow, participated in the talks.

### 赫鲁晓夫  接见“纽约时报”记者

1957-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫
    接见“纽约时报”记者
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫应美国
  “纽约时报”首席外交记者詹姆斯·赖斯顿的要求在7日接见了他，并且和他进行了谈话。谈话中涉及了国际局势和苏美关系问题。
    参加谈话的有苏联外交部新闻司司长伊利切夫和“纽约时报”常驻莫斯科记者威廉·约旦。
