Title: US and Turkey Continue to Plot Aggression Against Syria, US Naval Chief Shouts for Full Support of Turkey
Author:
Date: 1957-10-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd, Ankara News: Turkish Prime Minister Adnan Menderes held talks with the U.S. Ambassador to Turkey, Fletcher Warren, on October 22nd. It is reported that the talks included the situation arising from Turkey's acceptance of Saudi Arabian King Saud's proposal to mediate between Turkey and Syria, and the current tense situation in the Middle East.

On the same day that Menderes and Warren held talks, Turkish Minister of State Zoru left Ankara by plane for Saudi Arabia at the invitation of the Saudi King, carrying letters from Turkish President Bayar and Prime Minister Menderes to the Saudi King.

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: Two senior U.S. Navy admirals participating in the U.S. Seventh Fleet's war games in the Western Pacific have been clamoring for full support of Turkey's aggression against Syria.

According to United Press International, U.S. Chief of Naval Operations Burke said at a press conference on his flagship on October 22nd that the United States would use all its strength to "defend" Turkey.

Prior to this, United Press International quoted U.S. Seventh Fleet Commander Beakley on October 21st as saying that if war broke out between Turkey and Syria, the U.S. fleet was ready to sail directly to the back door of Suez at any time. He also said that in order to support Turkey, "we will go all out."

### 美土继续策划侵叙阴谋  美海军首脑叫嚷全力支持土耳其

1957-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    美土继续策划侵叙阴谋
    美海军首脑叫嚷全力支持土耳其
    新华社23日讯　安卡拉消息：土耳其总理阿德南·曼德列斯于10月22日和美国驻土耳其大使弗勒彻·华伦进行商谈。据报道，会谈内容包括由于土耳其接受了沙特阿拉伯国王沙特提出的在土耳其和叙利亚两国间进行斡旋的建议而引起的局势，和当前中东紧张局势问题。
    在曼德列斯和华伦进行商谈的同一天，土耳其国务部长佐鲁应沙特国王的邀请乘飞机离开安卡拉去沙特阿拉伯，他携带了土耳其总统拜亚尔和总理曼德列斯给沙特国王的信件。
    据新华社23日讯　参加美国第七舰队在西太平洋进行战争演习的两个高级美国海军将领，都叫嚷要以全力支持土耳其对叙利亚的侵略。
    据合众社报道，美国海军作战参谋长伯克10月22日在他的旗舰上举行记者招待会时说，美国将以全部的力量来“保卫”土耳其。
    在这之前，合众社10月21日援引美国第七舰队司令比克利的话说，如果土耳其和叙利亚之间的战争爆发，美国舰队随时准备直接开往苏伊士的后门。他还说，为了支持土耳其，“我们将全力以赴”。
