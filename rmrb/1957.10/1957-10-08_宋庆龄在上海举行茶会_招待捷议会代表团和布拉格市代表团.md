Title: Soong Ching-ling Hosts Tea Party in Shanghai for Czechoslovak Parliament Delegation and Prague City Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 7th. Soong Ching-ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, held a tea party this afternoon in the garden of her residence to welcome the Czechoslovak Republic National Assembly Delegation headed by Chairman Zdeněk Fierlinger and the Prague City Central People's Committee Delegation headed by Mayor Adolf Svoboda.

In the garden filled with the rich fragrance of osmanthus blossoms, the guests and hosts enjoyed tea and the scenery, engaging in pleasant conversation and exchanging gifts. Present as hosts were Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Wang Weizhou, Standing Committee Member; Liu Guanyi, Deputy Secretary-General; and Cao Diqiu and Song Jiwen, Vice Mayors of Shanghai.

Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. and Mrs. Bušniak, and Consul General in Shanghai, Mr. and Mrs. Fierlinger, were also present.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 7th. The Czechoslovak Republic National Assembly Delegation headed by Chairman Fierlinger and the Prague City Central People's Committee Delegation headed by Mayor Svoboda, today visited the state-owned Shanghai Turbine Factory.

The Shanghai Turbine Factory was expanded from a simple repair shop with the selfless assistance of Czechoslovak experts, and the people of Shanghai call it "The Flower of Sino-Czech Friendship."

### 宋庆龄在上海举行茶会  招待捷议会代表团和布拉格市代表团

1957-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄在上海举行茶会
    招待捷议会代表团和布拉格市代表团
    新华社上海7日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄今天下午在她的寓所花园里举行茶会，欢迎以兹德尼克·费林格主席为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会代表团和以阿道夫·斯沃波达市长为首的布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团。
    在充满浓郁桂花香的花园里，宾主品茗赏景，愉快地谈心，并且互相赠送了礼品。出席作陪的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通、常务委员王维舟、副秘书长刘贯一，上海市副市长曹荻秋、宋季文。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克夫妇和驻上海总领事费林格夫妇也出席作陪。
    据新华社上海7日电　以费林格主席为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会代表团和以斯沃波达市长为首的布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团，今天在上海参观了国营上海汽轮机厂。
    上海汽轮机厂是在捷克斯洛伐克专家的无私帮助下，从一个简陋的修理厂扩建而成的，上海人民把这称为“中捷友谊之花”。
