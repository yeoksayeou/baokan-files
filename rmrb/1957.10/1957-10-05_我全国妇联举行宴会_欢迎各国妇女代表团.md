Title: All-China Women's Federation Holds Banquet to Welcome Women's Delegations from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-10-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The All-China Women's Federation held a banquet this evening to welcome the women's delegations from Burma, Ceylon, Egypt, India, Indonesia, Japan, Pakistan, and Tunisia who came to China to attend the National Day celebrations, as well as guests from Britain and France invited in their personal capacity.

Before the banquet, Cai Chang, President of the All-China Women's Federation, and all the Vice Presidents met with the guests.

Deng Yingchao, Vice President of the All-China Women's Federation, delivered a speech at the banquet, extending a warm welcome. She said that although we have different political views and religious beliefs, our gathering together shows that we all have the common lofty aspiration of safeguarding world peace and protecting the rights of women and children. She raised her glass to the friendship and unity of women from Asian and African countries and the friendship and unity of women from all over the world, to peace in Asia and Africa and world peace, and to the rights and happiness of women and children.

The wives of the Burmese, Egyptian, Indian, and Pakistani ambassadors to China were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 我全国妇联举行宴会  欢迎各国妇女代表团

1957-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我全国妇联举行宴会  欢迎各国妇女代表团
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会今天晚上设宴欢迎来我国参加国庆观礼的缅甸、锡兰、埃及、印度、印度尼西亚、日本、巴基斯坦、突尼斯八个国家的妇女代表团和以个人身份应邀的英国、法国客人们。
    宴会前，全国妇联主席蔡畅和各位副主席接见了客人们。
    全国妇联副主席邓颖超在宴会上致词表示热烈欢迎。她说，虽然我们各有不同的政治见解、宗教信仰，但是，我们的欢聚正表示着我们大家都具有保卫世界和平，保卫妇女儿童权利的共同的崇高愿望。她举杯为亚非各国妇女的友谊和团结和世界各国妇女的友谊和团结，为亚非的和平及世界和平，为妇女儿童的权利和幸福干杯。
    应邀出席宴会的还有缅甸、埃及、印度和巴基斯坦驻中国使节夫人。
