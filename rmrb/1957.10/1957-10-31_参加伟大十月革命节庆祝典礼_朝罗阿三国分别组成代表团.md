Title: Delegations from North Korea, Romania and Albania to Attend Celebrations of the Great October Revolution

Author:
Date: 1957-10-31
Page: 6

Reports from our newspaper: According to Xinhua News Agency reports: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the CPSU and the Council of Ministers, North Korea, Romania, and Albania have formed delegations to attend the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

    The head of the North Korean delegation is Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet; members are Nam Il, Deputy Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Kim Chang-man, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Park Moon-kyu, State Inspector; and Li Sin-pal, North Korean Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

    The head of the Romanian delegation is Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party. Members of the delegation include: Stoica, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Mogioros, Vice-Chairman; Voitec, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party and Minister of Foreign Trade; and Plăosteașu, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, and others.

    The head of the Albanian delegation is Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Labor Party. Members include Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers and member of the Political Bureau of the Labor Party; Balluku, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Defense and member of the Political Bureau of the Labor Party; Peza, Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly; Peçi, Chairman of the State Control Commission and member of the Central Committee of the Labor Party; and Prifti, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Labor Party and Albanian Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

### 参加伟大十月革命节庆祝典礼  朝罗阿三国分别组成代表团

1957-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    参加伟大十月革命节庆祝典礼
    朝罗阿三国分别组成代表团
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：应苏共中央和部长会议的邀请，参加伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年庆祝典礼，朝鲜、罗马尼亚和阿尔巴尼亚三国分别组成代表团。
    朝鲜代表团的团长是内阁首相金日成，团员是内阁副首相兼外务相南日，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长金昌满，国家检阅相朴文圭，朝鲜驻苏联大使李新八。
    罗马尼亚代表团团长是罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记乔治乌—德治。代表团团员包括：工人党中央政治局委员、部长会议主席斯托伊卡、副主席莫吉奥罗希，工人党中央政治局候补委员、对外贸易部长沃伊捷克和工人党中央书记普利奥蒂亚萨等。
    阿尔巴尼亚代表团团长是劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查，团员有部长会议主席、劳动党政治局委员谢胡，部长会议第一副主席兼国防部部长、劳动党政治局委员巴卢库，人民议会主席团副主席佩扎，国家监察委员会主席、劳动党中央委员佩奇和劳动党候补中央委员、阿尔巴尼亚驻苏联大使普里夫蒂。
