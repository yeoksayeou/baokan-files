Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to the Soviet Russian Social Security Workers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi and his wife held a banquet at noon on the 25th to bid farewell to the entire Soviet Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic social security workers delegation headed by G.M. Kaplanov.

### 陈毅副总理宴会欢送俄罗斯社会保障工作者代表团

1957-10-26
第3版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理宴会欢送俄罗斯社会保障工作者代表团
    据新华社25日讯　国务院副总理陈毅和夫人25日中午举行宴会，欢送以格·马·卡普兰诺夫为首的苏联俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国社会保障工作者代表团全体人员。
