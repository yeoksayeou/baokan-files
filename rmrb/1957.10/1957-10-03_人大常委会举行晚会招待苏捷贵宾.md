Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Soiree to Host Soviet and Czechoslovak Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-10-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held a song, dance and Beijing opera soiree tonight to entertain the delegation from the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the delegation from the National Assembly of Czechoslovakia, and the delegation from the Central People's Committee of the city of Prague.
　　Chairman Liu Shaoqi accompanied the distinguished guests in watching the performance.
　　After the performance, Chairman Liu Shaoqi and others accompanied the distinguished guests onto the stage, shook hands with the performers to thank them, and presented flower baskets.
　　Vice Chairman Huang Yanpei and Vice Premier Ulanhu also accompanied the guests to watch the performance.
　　Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušiňák, was also invited to attend the soiree.

### 人大常委会举行晚会招待苏捷贵宾

1957-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会举行晚会招待苏捷贵宾
    新华社2日讯  全国人民代表大会常务委员会今晚举行歌舞京剧晚会，招待苏联最高苏维埃代表团、捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团和布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团。
    刘少奇委员长陪同贵宾观看了表演。
    演出结束后，刘少奇委员长等陪同贵宾们走上舞台，向演员们握手致谢，并赠送花篮。
    陪同观剧的，还有黄炎培副委员长和乌兰夫副总理。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克也应邀出席晚会。
