Title: Visiting Delegation of Mayors from Port Cities of Northern Japan Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-10-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th. The visiting delegation of mayors from port cities of Northern Japan, led by Suzuki Tomi, head of the delegation, and Sakurai Tatsuji, deputy head, along with other members, a total of twelve people, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 29th.

The visiting delegation of mayors from port cities of Northern Japan is visiting China at the invitation of Peng Zhen, the Mayor of Beijing.

Zhang Youyu and Wang Kunlun, the Deputy Mayors of Beijing, and Jia Xingwu, the Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, and others were at the station to welcome them.

### 北日本港湾城市市长访华团到京

1957-10-30
第3版()
专栏：

    北日本港湾城市市长访华团到京
    新华社29日讯　北日本港湾城市市长访华团团长铃木东民、副团长樱井辰治和团员等一行十二人29日晚上乘火车到达北京。
    北日本港湾城市市长访华团是应北京市市长彭真的邀请来我国访问的。
    到车站欢迎的有北京市副市长张友渔、王昆仑、北京市人民委员会秘书长贾星五等人。
