Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Arrives in Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-10-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 3rd. The Bulgarian government delegation headed by Comrade Yugov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, arrived in Hangzhou by special train this afternoon. Accompanying them were Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, and Zhou Zhuan, our country's ambassador to Bulgaria.

Sha Wenhan, Governor of Zhejiang Province; Huo Shilian, Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Wu Xian, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, First Secretary of the Hangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Mayor of Hangzhou; Lin Weixian, Commander of the Zhejiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; and more than a thousand people from all walks of life and citizens of Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou City welcomed the distinguished guests at the station.

The distinguished guests greatly admired the beautiful scenery of Hangzhou. In the hazy moonlight of the evening, the distinguished guests took a motorboat on the calm lake, strolled along the green and lush Su Causeway, and visited the Three Pools Mirroring the Moon.

Sha Wenhan, Governor of Zhejiang Province, also hosted a banquet for the distinguished guests at the famous Lou Wai Lou Restaurant in Hangzhou today.

The government delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria left Shanghai this afternoon to come to Hangzhou.

### 保加利亚政府代表团到杭州

1957-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    保加利亚政府代表团到杭州
    新华社杭州3日电  以保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席于哥夫为首的保加利亚政府代表团今天下午乘专车到达杭州。陪同前来的有我国外交部副部长曾涌泉、我国驻保加利亚大使周竹安等。
    浙江省省长沙文汉，中共浙江省委书记霍士廉，浙江省副省长、中共杭州市委第一书记、杭州市市长吴宪，中国人民解放军浙江军区司令员林维先以及浙江省、杭州市各界人士和市民群众一千多人在车站上迎接贵宾。
    贵宾们对杭州的美丽景色极为赞赏。傍晚，贵宾们在朦胧月色中乘汽艇在平静的湖上泛舟，又沿着绿荫浓覆着的苏堤漫步，并游览了三潭印月。
    浙江省省长沙文汉今天还在杭州著名的楼外楼饭店设宴招待贵宾。
    保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团是在今天下午离开上海前来杭州的。
