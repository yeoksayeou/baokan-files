Title: Shen Yanbing Hosts Reception for Egyptian Film Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing hosted a reception at noon today to welcome the Egyptian film delegation.

Egyptian Ambassador to China Ragab and his wife, and Cultural Counselor Badran and his wife, were invited to attend today's reception.

Shen Yanbing congratulated the success of the "Egyptian Republic Film Week" held in China. He said at the beginning of the reception that in the past few days, Chinese audiences had watched Egyptian films with great interest. Chinese audiences greatly admired the artistic achievements of the Egyptian film directors and actors. Egyptian actress Faten Hamama and actor Omar Sharif have become actors loved by Chinese audiences.

Shen Yanbing said that the mutual holding of film weeks by China and Egypt demonstrates the friendship, unity, and further development of cultural exchanges between the two peoples. The enhancement of the noble friendship between the two peoples is a force for peace. Shen Yanbing hoped that the delegation would make more visits and inspections in China and offer valuable opinions.

At the reception, the head of the Egyptian film delegation, Yahya Hakki, also spoke. He said that Chairman Mao Zedong met with them yesterday and asked them to convey his greetings to President Nasser. Hakki said that when he saw Chairman Mao Zedong's face and shook his hand, he felt Chairman Mao Zedong's determination and strength in leading the Chinese people forward. Hakki hoped that the cultural exchanges between Egypt and China would further develop and become increasingly prosperous.

The heads of the film delegations from North Korea, Vietnam, and Thailand, who continued to visit China after participating in the "Asian Film Week," were also invited to attend the reception.

### 沈雁冰举行酒会欢迎埃及电影代表团

1957-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰举行酒会欢迎埃及电影代表团
    据新华社2日讯  文化部部长沈雁冰今天午间举行酒会，欢迎埃及电影代表团。
    埃及驻中国大使拉加卜和夫人、文化参赞巴德朗和夫人应邀出席了今天的酒会。
    沈雁冰祝贺“埃及共和国电影周”在中国举办的成功。他在酒会开始时讲话说，这几天来，中国观众怀着极大的兴趣看了埃及的影片。中国观众对于埃及影片的导演和演员们的艺术成就，十分钦佩。埃及演员法登·哈玛玛女士和奥马·薛里夫先生已经成为中国观众所喜爱的演员了。
    沈雁冰说，中埃两国相互举办电影周，说明了中埃两国人民的友好、团结以及文化交流的进一步发展。两国人民崇高的友谊的增进，就是和平的力量。沈雁冰希望代表团在中国多作参观访问和提出宝贵的意见。
    在酒会上，埃及电影代表团团长耶雅·哈基也讲了话。他说，昨天毛泽东主席接见了他们，并且请他们转达对纳赛尔总统的问候。哈基说，当他看到毛泽东主席的脸和握着他的手的时候，他感觉到了毛泽东主席带领着中国人民前进的决心和力量。哈基希望埃中两国的文化交流进一步发展和日益繁荣。
    参加“亚洲电影周”后继续在中国访问的朝鲜、越南和泰国电影代表团的团长们，也应邀出席了酒会。
