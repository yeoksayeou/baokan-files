Title: Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Mayor Peng Zhen Host Banquet for Bulgarian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. October. Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen hosted a banquet at Quanjude at noon today in honor of the Bulgarian government delegation headed by Chairman Yugov. The Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Negalkov, and his wife were also invited to attend.

Those present to accompany them included Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Cao Xiangren, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, and Zhou Zhuan, Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria.

### 李先念副总理、彭真市长宴请保政府代表团

1957-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    李先念副总理、彭真市长宴请保政府代表团
    新华社1日讯  李先念副总理和北京市彭真市长今天中午在全聚德宴请以于哥夫主席为首的保加利亚政府代表团。保加利亚驻华大使涅加尔科夫和夫人也应邀参加。
    出席作陪的，有中共中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆，外交部副部长曾涌泉，第一机械工业部副部长曹祥仁，中国驻保加利亚大使周竹安等人。
