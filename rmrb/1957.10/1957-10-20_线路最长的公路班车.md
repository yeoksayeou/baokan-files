Author:

Date: 1957-10-20
Page: 4

East Glimpses and West Grabs

    The Longest Bus Route
    The Lhasa-Xiadong highway express bus route has begun operation; the first bus departed from Lhasa on the 17th. It will pass through Qinghai and arrive at Xiadong in Yumen County, Gansu, with a total length of more than 1,900 kilometers, making it the longest bus route in the country's highway transportation.

    Xiadong is a station on the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway. The opening of the Lhasa-Xiadong direct bus will bring convenience to passengers traveling from Lhasa to the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway.

### 线路最长的公路班车

1957-10-20
第4版()
专栏：东鳞西爪

    线路最长的公路班车
    拉萨—峡东公路线直达班车开始通车；第一次班车17日从拉萨开出。它将通过青海到达甘肃玉门县内的峡东，全长一千九百多公里，是目前全国公路运输中最长的班车路线。
    峡东是兰新铁路上的车站。拉萨—峡东直达班车开行以后，给从拉萨到兰新铁路的旅客带来不少方便。
