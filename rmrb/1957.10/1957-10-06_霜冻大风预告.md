Title: Frost and Gale Warning
Author:
Date: 1957-10-06
Page: 8
    Meteorological Observatory

    Frost and Gale Warning
    (Our newspaper's dispatch) On the 5th at 7 PM, the Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a frost and gale warning: Tomorrow morning and the morning after tomorrow, after the wind stops, there will be frost in the entire Northeast region, Shanxi, the area north of the Qinling Mountains in Shaanxi, Hebei, inland Shandong, eastern Henan and the western mountainous areas, northern Anhui, and northern Jiangsu. In the next twenty-four hours, the wind force on the middle and lower reaches of the Yellow River and the Bohai Sea will weaken to below level 5. The Huai River basin will still have a northerly wind of level 5 or 6, and the Yellow Sea will still have a northerly wind of level 6-7. The wind force in the provinces of the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River will increase to level 5 or 6. The wind force in the East China Sea and the Taiwan Strait will gradually increase to level 6 or 7, with a northerly wind.

### 霜冻大风预告

1957-10-06
第8版()
专栏：气象台

    霜冻大风预告
    本报讯　5日下午7时中央气象科学研究所发布霜冻和大风预告：明天早上和后天早上，在东北全区、山西、陕西的秦岭以北地区、河北、山东内陆、河南东部和西部山地、安徽北部、江苏北部，风停之后将有霜冻。未来二十四小时内，黄河中下游和渤海海面的风力将减弱到五级以下，淮河流域仍有五、六级的偏北风，黄海海面仍有六—七级的偏北风。长江中下游各省的风力将增大到五、六级。东海和台湾海峡风力将逐渐增大到六、七级，风向偏北。
