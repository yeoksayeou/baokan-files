Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Cables Premier Kim Il-sung, Thanking Him for His Telegram Congratulating the Seventh Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' Participation in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea
Author:
Date: 1957-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram on the 27th to Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, thanking him for his telegram congratulating the seventh anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea. The full text of Chairman Mao's telegram of thanks to Premier Kim Il-sung is as follows:

Comrade Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea:

On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, I am grateful for your telegram of congratulations. On behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, I extend my heartfelt thanks to you, and through you, to the Government and people of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea.

The unbreakable brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples is of great significance for ensuring the peaceful construction of our two countries and defending peace in Asia and the world.

I wish the Korean people new achievements in the great cause of building socialism and striving for the peaceful reunification of the motherland.

I wish the brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples continuous consolidation and development.

Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
October 27, 1957, Beijing

### 毛泽东主席致电金日成首相  感谢他电贺我志愿军抗美援朝参战七周年

1957-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席致电金日成首相
    感谢他电贺我志愿军抗美援朝参战七周年
    新华社29日讯　毛泽东主席27日致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成，对他来电祝贺中国人民志愿军抗美援朝参战七周年表示感谢。毛主席给金日成首相的感谢电全文如下：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成同志：
    在中国人民志愿军抗美援朝参战七周年的时候，承来电祝贺，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民向你，并且通过你，向朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和人民表示衷心的感谢。
    中朝两国人民牢不可破的兄弟友谊，对于保证我们两国的和平建设和保卫亚洲和世界的和平有着重大的意义。
    祝朝鲜人民在建设社会主义和争取祖国的和平统一的伟大事业中取得新的成就。
    祝中朝人民的兄弟友谊不断巩固和发展。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东　1957年10月27日于北京
