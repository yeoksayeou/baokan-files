Title: Chen Yi Receives Japanese General Council of Trade Unions Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-11
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, October 10th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Japanese General Council of Trade Unions delegation this morning. The delegation was visiting China after visiting the Soviet Union.

Present at the meeting were Zhang Xiuzhu, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Chen Yu, Acting Director of the International Liaison Department of the ACFTU. The delegation took a train this afternoon to visit Shanghai and other places, and will return to their country after completing their visits.

### 陈毅接见日本工会总评议会代表团

1957-10-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　陈毅接见日本工会总评议会代表团
    新华社10日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了在访问苏联后来我国访问的日本工会总评议会访苏代表团。
    接见时在座的，有全国总工会书记处书记张修竹，全总国际联络部代部长陈宇。这个代表团已在今天下午乘火车去上海等地参观访问，并将在结束参观访问后回国。
