Hebei Province convened a water and soil conservation experience exchange meeting for 54 mountainous counties this year in order to promote water and soil conservation work in mountainous areas. This is a visit by representatives from various places to Changjiagou, Huai'an County, to observe the relationship between different farming methods and soil erosion experiments. Photo by Wang Yousheng (Xinhua News Agency draft)
