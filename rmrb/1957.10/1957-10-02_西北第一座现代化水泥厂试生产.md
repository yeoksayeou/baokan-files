Title: Northwest's First Modern Cement Plant Enters Trial Production
Author:
Date: 1957-10-02
Page: 2
Gift for National Day

　　Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, October 1st. The first large-scale modern cement plant in Northwest China—Yongdeng Cement Plant, began trial production on the 30th.

　　This factory was built with a complete set of equipment supplied by the German Democratic Republic and with the help of their experts. Since construction began in September 1954, all the staff of the construction unit have demonstrated a high degree of initiative and creativity. According to statistics, the investment saved for the state during the construction of the plant totaled more than 1.16 million yuan.

　　This factory has a high degree of automation. After it is officially put into production at the end of this year, it will supply a large amount of inexpensive cement to Gansu, Shaanxi, Qinghai and other places.

### 西北第一座现代化水泥厂试生产

1957-10-02
第2版()
专栏：国庆献礼

    西北第一座现代化水泥厂试生产
    新华社兰州1日电  西北第一座大型现代化的水泥厂——永登水泥厂，在30日点窑试生产。
    这座工厂是由民主德国供应全套设备并在他们的专家帮助下建设起来的。从1954年9月施工到现在，施工单位的全体职工发挥了高度积极性创造性。据统计，在建厂中为国家节省的投资共有一百一十六万多元。
    这个工厂的自动化程度较高，它在今年底正式投入生产后，就将大量供应甘肃、陕西、青海等地贱价的水泥。
