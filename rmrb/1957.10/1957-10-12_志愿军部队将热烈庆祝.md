Title: Volunteer Army Units to Warmly Celebrate

Author:

Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 10th. The Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army issued a notice to the entire army on the 7th, requiring all units to warmly celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

The notice stated that from October 17th to November 7th, all units should organize report meetings for officers and soldiers respectively, focusing on publicizing the great international significance of the October Socialist Revolution, the great achievements of socialist construction in the Soviet Union, the Soviet Union's great contribution to defending world peace, and the great significance of further developing Sino-Soviet friendship and strengthening Sino-Soviet solidarity.

The notice required that on the occasion of the October Revolution Day, units at and above the regimental level should hold grand celebration meetings with the participation of all personnel. The clubs of all units should use all their strength to carry out propaganda activities related to the October Revolution Day, organize photo exhibitions, publish commemorative special issues, and hold small forums. The film projection teams of all units should ensure that all officers and soldiers see one or two Soviet films within two months.

### 志愿军部队将热烈庆祝

1957-10-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　志愿军部队将热烈庆祝
    新华社平壤10日电　中国人民志愿军政治部7日向全军发出通知，要求各部队热烈庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年。
    通知说，从10月17日到11月7日期间，各部队要对军官和士兵分别组织报告会，着重宣传十月社会主义革命的伟大国际意义，苏联社会主义建设的伟大成就，苏联对保卫世界和平的伟大贡献，以及进一步发展中苏友谊和加强中苏团结的重大意义。
    通知要求在十月革命节的时候，各部队的团以上单位要召开全体人员参加的隆重的庆祝大会。各部队的俱乐部应该以全部力量来进行有关十月革命节的宣传活动，组织图片展览、出纪念专刊、召开小型座谈会等。各部队的电影放映队要在两个月内使全体官兵看到一次或两次以上的苏联电影。
