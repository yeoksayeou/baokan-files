Title: Chairman Mao Receives Polish Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou Enlai received the Polish Military Delegation headed by Defense Minister General Spychalski this evening.

Present at the reception were Marshal Peng Dehuai, General Xu Guangda, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, and Polish Ambassador to China, Kirylok.

### 毛主席接见波兰军事代表团

1957-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见波兰军事代表团
    新华社3日讯  毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、周恩来总理在今天晚间接见了以国防部长斯彼哈尔斯基上将为首的波兰军事代表团。
    接见时在座的有彭德怀元帅、许光达大将、萧向荣中将和波兰驻中国大使基里洛克。
