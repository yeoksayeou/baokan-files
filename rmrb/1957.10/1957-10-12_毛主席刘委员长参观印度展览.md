Title: Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu Visit Indian Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1957-10-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Liu Shaoqi visited the Indian Exhibition this evening.

Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu, guided by the head of the Indian Exhibition Delegation, Panikkar, and the Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, viewed the various exhibits with interest. After the visit, Delegation Head Panikkar presented gifts to Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu.

Tonight, the Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, also visited the exhibition with Chairman Mao. (Attached picture)

Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Liu Shaoqi, accompanied by Panikkar (first from left in the front), head of the Indian Exhibition Delegation, and R.K. Nehru (fifth from left in the front), the Indian Ambassador to China, are visiting the exhibits at the Indian Exhibition. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong.

### 毛主席刘委员长参观印度展览

1957-10-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　毛主席刘委员长参观印度展览
    新华社11日讯　毛泽东主席和刘少奇委员长在今天晚上参观了印度展览会。
    毛主席和刘委员长由印度展览会代表团团长潘尼克和印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁引导，有兴趣地观看了展览会的各种展品。参观以后，潘尼克团长向毛主席和刘委员长赠送了礼品。
    今晚，苏联驻中国大使尤金也同毛主席一起参观了这个展览会。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长由印度展览会代表团团长潘尼克（前左第一人）和印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁（前左第五人）陪同参观印度展览会上的展品。　　新华社记者　邹健东摄
