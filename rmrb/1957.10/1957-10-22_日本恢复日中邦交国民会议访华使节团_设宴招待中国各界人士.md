Title: Japanese Delegation of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations Hosts Banquet for People from All Walks of Life in China

Author:

Date: 1957-10-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 21st. The Japanese Delegation of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, currently visiting our country, hosted a banquet in Beijing on the evening of the 21st to express gratitude to people from all walks of life in China for their invitation and reception.

Among those attending the banquet were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Zhou Gengsheng and Qiao Guanhua, Vice Presidents; Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman; Cai Tingkai, Committee Member; Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; as well as Wang Zhen, Cao Mengjun, Yang Yu, Zhao Puchu, and Wang Yunsheng.

### 日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华使节团  设宴招待中国各界人士

1957-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华使节团
    设宴招待中国各界人士
    新华社21日讯　应邀来我国访问的日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华使节团21日晚在北京设宴招待中国各界人士，对中国方面的邀请和接待表示感谢。
    出席宴会的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长周鲠生、乔冠华，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，副主席廖承志，委员蔡廷锴，中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸，副主席雷任民，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南以及王震、曹孟君、杨煜、赵朴初、王芸生等人。
