Title: Protest Against U.S. B-52 Bombers Stationed in Okinawa
Author:
Date: 1968-03-02
Edition: 6

More than ten thousand workers and students in Okinawa held a rally and demonstration on the evening of the 27th near the U.S. Kadena Air Base in Kadena Village, strongly demanding the withdrawal of B-52 bombers stationed at the base.

The rally unanimously passed a resolution protesting against the U.S. imperialists' stationing of B-52 bombers in Okinawa and strongly demanding the immediate withdrawal of these aircraft.

After the rally, they held a fierce demonstration march around the base.

### 抗议美国B—52型轰炸机进驻冲绳  冲绳一万多工人学生举行集会示威

1968-03-02
第6版()
专栏：

    抗议美国B—52型轰炸机进驻冲绳
    冲绳一万多工人学生举行集会示威
    据新华社二十八日讯　据东方通讯社报道：冲绳工人、学生一万多人，二十七日傍晚在冲绳岛上美国嘉手纳空军基地附近的嘉手纳村举行集会，强烈要求美军撤走停在嘉手纳基地的B—52型轰炸机。会后，他们在基地周围举行了激烈的示威游行。
    大会一致通过决议，抗议美帝国主义的B—52型轰炸机驻在冲绳，强烈要求立即撤走这些飞机。
