Title: Troops of the 4810 Unit Work and Study in Juzhou Coal Mine
Author:
Date: 1968-03-19
Edition: 2

	Soldiers and officers of the 4810 Unit stationed at the Juzhou Coal Mine in Jiaozuo, along with the leaders of the mine's revolutionary committee, have been consistently adhering to the practice of regularly working underground. The image shows them studying Chairman Mao's latest instructions with the workers during a break from labor.
