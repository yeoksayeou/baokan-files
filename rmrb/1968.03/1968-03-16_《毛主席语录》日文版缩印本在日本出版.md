Title: The Japanese Edition of the Pocket-Sized "Quotations from Chairman Mao" Published in Japan
Author:
Date: 1968-03-16
Edition: 6

Only by relying on Mao Zedong Thought can the Japanese revolution achieve victory

The Japanese edition of the pocket-sized "Quotations from Chairman Mao" has been published in Japan. In Japan, an increasing number of people are seriously studying and applying Mao Zedong Thought with questions in mind. Especially among revolutionary people, it has become a common practice to read Chairman Mao's quotations together before meetings and to consult "Quotations from Chairman Mao" when encountering problems in struggles.

	New China News Agency, Tokyo: The pocket-sized Japanese edition of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," the red treasure book most beloved by the revolutionary people of the world, has recently been published and distributed by the Japanese Oriental Bookstore, receiving a warm welcome from the Japanese revolutionary people.

	The publication of the pocket-sized Japanese edition of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" is a great joy for the Japanese revolutionary people who are seriously studying and applying Mao Zedong Thought and are fighting against the U.S.-Japanese reactionaries, Soviet revisionists, and the revisionist group led by Miyamoto.

	This pocket-sized red treasure book is about half the size of the standard edition, making it convenient for readers to carry and read. Like the Chinese edition of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," it has "Quotations from Chairman Mao" in golden letters on the red plastic cover.

	Anpi Masayuki, the person in charge at the Oriental Bookstore, said: Japanese revolutionary people are increasingly recognizing through their struggles that Mao Zedong Thought is a powerful ideological weapon for revolution. Only by relying on Mao Zedong Thought can the Japanese revolution achieve victory. Now, in Japan, an increasing number of people are seriously studying and applying Mao Zedong Thought with questions in mind. Especially among revolutionary people, it has become a common practice to read Chairman Mao's quotations together before meetings and to consult "Quotations from Chairman Mao" when encountering problems in struggles. The publication of the pocket-sized Japanese edition of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" will further satisfy the urgent need of the Japanese revolutionary people to study Mao Zedong Thought.

### 《毛主席语录》日文版缩印本在日本出版

1968-03-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　只有靠毛泽东思想才能获得日本革命的胜利
　　《毛主席语录》日文版缩印本在日本出版
    在日本，越来越多的人带着问题认真地活学活用毛泽东思想。特别是在革命人民当中，开会前一起朗读毛主席语录，在斗争中遇到问题时请教《毛主席语录》，已蔚然成风。
    新华社讯　东京消息：世界革命人民最热爱的红色宝书《毛主席语录》的日文版缩印本，最近由日本东方书店出版发行，受到日本革命人民的热烈欢迎。
    《毛主席语录》的日文版缩印本的出版，对于正在活学活用毛泽东思想，同美日反动派、苏修和宫本修正主义集团进行斗争的日本革命人民，是一件大喜事。
    这种缩印本的红色宝书相当于普通版本的一半大小，便于读者携带阅读。它同中文版《毛主席语录》一样，在红色塑料封皮的封面上用烫金印有金光闪闪的“毛主席语录”五个字。
    东方书店的负责人安井正幸说：日本革命人民在斗争中越来越深刻地认识到，毛泽东思想是进行革命的强大思想武器。只有靠毛泽东思想才能获得日本革命的胜利。现在，在日本越来越多的人带着问题认真地活学活用毛泽东思想。特别是在革命人民当中，开会前一起朗读毛主席语录，在斗争中遇到问题时请教《毛主席语录》的事，已经蔚成风气。《毛主席语录》日文版缩印本的出版，将进一步满足日本革命人民迫切要求学习毛泽东思想的需要。
