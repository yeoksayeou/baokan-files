Title: Military and Civilians of Quang Tri Province in South Vietnam Achieve New Victories
Author:
Date: 1968-03-02
Edition: 5

The people's liberation armed forces of Quang Tri Province in South Vietnam have achieved numerous victories. From February 25 to 27, they fought fiercely for three days, annihilating over 500 enemy troops, the majority of whom were American invaders.

On February 25 and 26, the people's liberation armed forces fought in Khe Sanh, Dak Rang, Vinh Loc, Dong Ha, Kon Tum, and other locations, annihilating over 350 enemy troops. They shot down, destroyed, or damaged 20 enemy aircraft, sank or damaged 10 enemy ships, destroyed three cannons, and many military vehicles. In the Khe Sanh battlefield, the people's liberation armed forces won two consecutive victories in two days, annihilating over 200 enemy troops, almost all of whom were American invaders.

On the morning of February 25, the people's liberation armed forces ambushed a company of American troops in the southwest of Dak Rang, annihilating over 150 enemy troops, almost completely wiping out this company of American troops. On this day, the people's liberation armed forces also bombarded the enemy's artillery positions, radar stations, airstrips, and ammunition depots in Dak Rang four times, destroying five helicopters, one ammunition depot, one cannon, and many military vehicles.

On the morning of February 26, the people's liberation armed forces again ambushed two companies of American and puppet troops in the southeast of Dak Rang, annihilating 78 enemy troops.

On February 27, the people's liberation armed forces launched another attack on many American strongholds and logistics bases in Dong Ha and Vinh Loc.

In Dong Ha, the artillery of the people's liberation armed forces hit the enemy's port and helicopter landing pads, destroying many loading and unloading facilities, destroying many helicopters, causing the ammunition depot to explode continuously, and setting fire to military supply warehouses, which burned for nearly six hours.

In Vinh Loc, while fiercely bombarding the enemy's port and fleet, the people's liberation armed forces also launched a ground attack. The soldiers charged forward, using submachine guns, hand grenades, and hand grenades to annihilate batch after batch of enemy troops. Preliminary information indicates that the people's liberation armed forces annihilated over 150 enemy troops in Dong Ha and Vinh Loc on this day, the majority of whom were American invaders. They also sank or damaged seven enemy ships and destroyed many warehouses.

### 南越广治省军民猛冲猛打再建战功  三天奋战歼美伪军五百多名，毁伤敌机数十架，击沉击伤敌船十七艘

1968-03-02
第5版()
专栏：

    南越广治省军民猛冲猛打再建战功
    三天奋战歼美伪军五百多名，毁伤敌机数十架，击沉击伤敌船十七艘
    新华社河内一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广治省人民解放武装力量屡建战功，从二月二十五日到二十七日三天中又消灭敌人五百多名，其中绝大部分是美国侵略军。
    人民解放武装力量二十五日和二十六日两天在溪山、达根、越门、东河、昆天、育庙等地，消灭敌人三百五十多名，击落、击毁、击伤飞机二十架，击沉击伤敌船十艘，击毁大炮三门和军车多辆。溪山战场上的人民解放武装力量，在这两天中接连打了两场胜仗，歼敌二百多名，其中几乎全部是美国侵略军。
    二十五日上午，人民解放武装力量在达根西南伏击一连美军，消灭敌人一百五十多名，几乎全歼这连美军。这一天，人民解放武装力量还四次炮轰达根敌军据点的炮兵阵地、雷达站，停机场和弹药库等目标，击毁直升飞机五架、弹药库一座、大炮一门和军车多辆。
    二十六日上午，人民解放武装力量又在达根东南伏击两连美伪军，消灭敌人七十八名。
    二十七日，人民解放武装力量再次进攻美国侵略军在东河和越门的许多据点和后勤基地。
    在东河，人民解放武装力量的炮弹打中敌人的港口和直升飞机停机坪，摧毁许多装卸设备，击毁许多直升飞机，使弹药库连续爆炸，军用物资仓库起火燃烧近六个小时。
    在越门，人民解放武装力量在猛烈炮轰敌人的海港和船队的同时，还发动地面进攻，战士们猛冲猛打，用冲锋枪、手榴弹和手雷，歼灭一批又一批敌军。据初步消息，人民解放武装力量这一天在东河和越门两地，共消灭敌人一百五十多名，其中绝大部分是美国侵略军，击沉击伤敌船七艘，摧毁许多仓库。
