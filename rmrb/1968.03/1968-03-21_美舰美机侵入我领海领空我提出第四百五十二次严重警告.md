Title: U.S. Ships and Planes Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace; We Issue the 452nd Severe Warning
Author:
Date: 1968-03-21
Edition: 5

On March 19 at 22:38 to March 20 at 00:22, a U.S. military ship intruded into our territorial waters east of Chongwu, Fujian Province. On March 20 from 10:54 to 11:36, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Dong Island and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands. In response to this military provocation by U.S. military ships and aircraft, the spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issues the 452nd severe warning.

### 美舰美机侵入我领海领空我提出第四百五十二次严重警告

1968-03-21
第5版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我领海领空我提出第四百五十二次严重警告
    新华社二十日讯　三月十九日二十二时三十八分至二十日零时二十二分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省崇武以东领海。二十日十时五十四分至十一时三十六分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我西沙群岛的东岛、永兴岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百五十二次严重警告。
