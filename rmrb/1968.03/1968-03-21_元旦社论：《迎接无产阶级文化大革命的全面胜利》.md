Title: Welcoming the Complete Victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution

Author:

Date: 1968-03-21

Edition: 1

We will further expose the crimes of the handful of capitalist-roaders within the Party, headed by China's Khrushchev, and their agents, revealing their ugly faces and sinister activities in broad daylight, and making all their dreams of restoration completely bankrupt. We must use Mao Zedong Thought as our weapon, adopt various forms, and in every field, thoroughly criticize the revisionist line and eliminate its poisonous influence. The great criticism of the revolution must be combined with various tasks and carried out persistently.

### 元旦社论：《迎接无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利》

1968-03-21
