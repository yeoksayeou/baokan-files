Title: Joint Meeting of the Sino-Vietnamese Border Railway Committee Held
Author:
Date: 1968-03-21
Edition: 5

The Sino-Vietnamese Border Railway Joint Committee recently held its eleventh meeting in Hanoi and signed a railway joint transport protocol between the two countries. Jin Yucheng, Director of the Liuzhou Railway Bureau and head of the Chinese delegation, and Zhang Guangxi, Deputy Director of the Vietnam Railway General Bureau and head of the Vietnamese delegation, signed the protocol on behalf of their respective sides.

Vice Minister of Transportation of Vietnam, Yang Bailian, and Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, Lu Weizhao, attended the signing ceremony.

### 中越国境铁路联合委员会举行会议  签订两国铁路联运议定书

1968-03-21
第5版()
专栏：

    中越国境铁路联合委员会举行会议
    签订两国铁路联运议定书
    新华社河内二十日电　中越国境铁路联合委员会最近在河内举行第十一次会议，签订了两国铁路联运议定书。中国柳州铁路管理局局长、中国代表团团长金玉成和越南铁路总局副局长、越南代表团团长张光熙分别代表双方在议定书上签字。
    越南交通部副部长杨白连、中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陆维钊出席了签字仪式。
