Title: Deputy Political Commissar of a Certain Regiment in Beijing Military Region, Jo Xizeng, Actively Studies Chairman Mao's Works and Carries Out Military and Political Training in Tianjin Yanan Middle School
Author:
Date: 1968-03-11
Edition: 3

In Tianjin Yanan Middle School, Deputy Political Commissar Jo Xizeng of a certain regiment in the Beijing Military Region, an active learner of Chairman Mao's works, has been deeply involved in the masses, conducting meticulous political and ideological work. He arms the broad revolutionary teachers, students, and staff with Mao Zedong Thought.
