Title: At the Invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Wilcock Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-03-14
Edition: 1

At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade V.J. Wilcock, General Secretary of the Communist Party of New Zealand, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening.

Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, went to the airport to welcome him.

New Zealand friends in Beijing also went to the airport to welcome him.

	New Zealand Communist Party General Secretary V.J. Wilcock, at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, arrived in Beijing on March 13.

	Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, went to the airport to welcome him.

	Comrade Wilcock and Comrade Kang Sheng shook hands warmly.

		Photographed by a reporter from Xinhua News Agency

### 应中共中央邀请来我国进行访问　威尔科克斯同志到达北京　康生同志和在京的新西兰朋友到机场欢迎

1968-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　应中共中央邀请来我国进行访问
　　威尔科克斯同志到达北京
　　康生同志和在京的新西兰朋友到机场欢迎
    新华社十三日讯　新西兰共产党总书记维·乔·威尔科克斯同志，应中共中央邀请来我国进行访问，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    中共中央政治局常委康生同志前往机场欢迎。
    在北京的新西兰朋友也到机场欢迎。（附图片）
    新西兰共产党总书记维·乔·威尔科克斯同志，应中共中央邀请来我国进行访问，三月十三日到达北京。
    中共中央政治局常委康生同志前往机场欢迎。
    威尔科克斯同志和康生同志亲切握手。
            新华社记者摄
