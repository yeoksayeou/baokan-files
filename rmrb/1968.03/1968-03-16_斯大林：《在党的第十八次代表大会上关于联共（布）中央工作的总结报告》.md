Title: Stalin: Report on the Work of the Central Committee of the CPSU(B) at the Eighteenth Congress of the Party
Author:
Date: 1968-03-16
Edition: 2

In any department of state or party work, the higher the political level and Marxist-Leninist consciousness of the personnel, the higher the efficiency of the work itself, and the more effective the work becomes. Conversely, the lower the political level and Marxist-Leninist consciousness of the personnel, the more likely they are to suffer setbacks and failures in their work, the more likely they are to become vulgarized and degenerate into narrow-minded bureaucrats, and the more likely they are to degenerate and change quality. This must be considered a theorem.

### 斯大林：《在党的第十八次代表大会上关于联共（布）中央工作的总结报告》

1968-03-16
第2版()
专栏：

    在国家工作和党的工作的任何一个部门中，工作人员的政治水平和马克思列宁主义觉悟程度愈高，工作本身的效率也愈高，工作也就愈有成效；反过来说，工作人员的政治水平和马克思列宁主义觉悟程度愈低，就愈可能在工作中遭受挫折和失败，就愈可能使工作人员本身庸俗化和堕落成为鼠目寸光的事务主义者，就愈可能使他们蜕化变质，——这要算是一个定理。
　　斯大林：《在党的第十八次代表大会上关于联共（布）中央工作的总结报告》
