Title: Two Japanese Friends Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-03-15
Edition: 5

Two Japanese friends arrived in Beijing

	At the invitation of the China-Japan Friendship Association, the Secretary-General of the General Headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox), Miyazaki Seiken, and the Organizational Committee Chairman, Shimao Masao, arrived in Beijing on March 11 to conduct a friendly visit.

	Secretary-General Zhao Anbo and Deputy Secretary-General Wang Xiaoyun of the China-Japan Friendship Association went to the airport to welcome them.

	Japanese friends in Beijing, Nishisonji Koiichi, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 日本两位朋友到京

1968-03-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　日本两位朋友到京
    新华社十二日讯　应中日友协邀请，日中友协（正统）总部理事长宫崎世民、组织委员长岛田政雄十一日到达北京进行友好访问。
    中日友协秘书长赵安博、副秘书长王晓云等到机场迎接。
    在京的日本朋友西园寺公一也到机场迎接。
