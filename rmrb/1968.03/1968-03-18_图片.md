Title: Kenyan Students Protest British Support for Rhodesian Regime
Author:
Date: 1968-03-18
Edition: 6

On March 12, more than 2,000 students in Kenya staged a massive demonstration in front of the British High Commission in Nairobi. They strongly protested the atrocities committed by the colonial regime of Rhodesia, backed by British imperialism, which brutally murdered five Zimbabwean freedom fighters.
