Title: Carry Forward the Spirit of Continuous Combat and Strike the Enemy Hard on All Fronts
Author:
Date: 1968-03-22
Edition: 5

The people's armed forces and civilians of the Mekong Delta in South Vietnam, including the provinces of Tra Vinh and My Tho, have achieved numerous new victories since launching a powerful offensive against the enemy during the Lunar New Year. They have annihilated a large number of enemy troops and destroyed a significant amount of enemy war materiel.

From January 31 to February 29, the people's armed forces and civilians of Tra Vinh Province annihilated and disbanded nearly 8,000 enemy troops. They completely wiped out two puppet army battalions and six companies of American and puppet troops, and severely damaged six puppet battalions. They also destroyed or damaged more than 140 enemy aircraft, 42 military vehicles, 15 ships and boats, nine cannons and mortars, and eight warehouses.

From January 31 to March 10, the people's armed forces and civilians of My Tho City launched a series of fierce attacks against the enemy within the city and its suburbs. They annihilated and disbanded 4,350 enemy troops, including 1,180 American invaders. They completely wiped out three battalions and eight companies of enemy troops, and severely damaged one battalion and six companies. The people of My Tho City also destroyed or damaged 156 enemy vehicles, shot down 26 enemy aircraft, sank or damaged 10 ships and boats, and destroyed 28 cannons. They forced the enemy to surrender or retreat from 13 outposts and guard posts.

From March 4 to 9, the people's armed forces and civilians of Chau Doc Province launched continuous attacks against the enemy in Go Cong Town and Chau Thanh District, annihilating and disbanding more than 500 enemy troops. They shot down four enemy aircraft, sank or damaged five ships and boats, destroyed three cannons, and demolished six outposts and guard posts.

From March 3 to 14, the people's armed forces of Tien Giang Province frequently attacked and ambushed the enemy, annihilating more than 130 enemy troops and seizing a large number of weapons and ammunition.

On March 12, the guerrillas and local forces of Duc Hoa District in Long An Province bravely counterattacked units of the American 11th Independent Armored Cavalry Regiment that were conducting a "sweep." They annihilated more than 140 enemy troops, destroyed or damaged 18 tanks and armored vehicles, and shot down or damaged five aircraft.

From March 10 to 15, the people's armed forces and civilians of Tay Ninh Province continuously captured and forced the enemy to withdraw from nine outposts.

From the night of March 10 to 12, the people's armed forces of Tay Ninh Province launched a series of fierce attacks against the enemy in the key "pacification" area along the Mekong River, annihilating 150 enemy troops.

### 发扬连续作战的作风　在各个战场痛打敌人  南越湄公河三角洲军民歼敌万余

1968-03-22
第5版()
专栏：

    发扬连续作战的作风　在各个战场痛打敌人
    南越湄公河三角洲军民歼敌万余
    新华社河内二十一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方湄公河三角洲芹苴、美萩等省军民，自新春向敌人发动强大攻势以来，接连取得许多新战果，歼灭敌军大批有生力量，摧毁敌人大量战争工具。
    芹苴省军民在一月三十一日到二月二十九日期间，共歼灭和瓦解敌人近八千名，其中全歼伪军两个营和美伪军六个连，重创伪军六个营；击毁、击伤敌人的飞机一百四十多架、军车四十二辆，击沉船艇十五艘，击毁大炮和迫击炮九门和仓库八座。
    美萩省省会美萩市军民，在从一月三十一日到三月十日的四十天内，接连在市内和市郊向敌人发动猛烈的进攻，共消灭和瓦解敌人四千三百五十名，其中包括美国侵略军一千一百八十名。敌军三个营八个连被全歼，一个营和六个连被重创。美萩市军民还击毁、击伤敌军车一百五十六辆，击落敌机二十六架，击沉、击伤船艇成十艘，摧毁大炮二十八门，迫使十三个据点和哨所的敌人投降或撤走。
    建丰省军民从三月四日到九日在高岭市和建文县城等地连续袭击敌人，共歼灭和瓦解敌人五百多名，击落敌机四架，击沉、击伤船艇五艘，击毁大炮三门，摧毁据点和哨所六个。
    永隆省人民解放武装力量，在三月三日到十四日期间，频频进攻和截击敌人，共歼敌一百三十多名，缴获许多武器和弹药。
    隆安省德和县游击队和地方部队三月十二日英勇反击美国第十一独立装甲团的一些单位发动的“扫荡”，消灭敌人一百四十多名，击毁、击伤坦克和装甲车十八辆，击落、击伤飞机五架。
    在三月十日到十五日期间，茶荣省军民连续攻克和迫使敌人撤出了九个据点。
    从三月十日夜晚到十二日，茶荣省人民解放武装力量向美伪军重点“绥靖”地区——孟梯河沿岸的敌人接连发动猛攻，歼灭敌人一百五十名。
