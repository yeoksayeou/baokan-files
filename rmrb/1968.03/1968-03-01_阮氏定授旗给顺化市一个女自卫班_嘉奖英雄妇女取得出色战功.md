Title: Nguyen Thi Dinh Awards Flag to a Women's Self-Defense Squad in Hue
Author:
Date: 1968-03-01
Edition: 5

	Nguyen Thi Dinh Awards Flag to a Women's Self-Defense Squad in Hue
	Commends Heroic Women for Outstanding Battle Achievements

	Hanoi, February 28 (Xinhua) — According to a report from the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, on the 24th, Nguyen Thi Dinh, Chairman of the Vietnam South Women's Liberation Union, sent a telegram to a women's self-defense squad in Hue, enthusiastically praising their victory on February 22 in repelling a counterattack by a battalion of U.S. Marines, achieving the glorious feat of annihilating 120 U.S. aggressor troops.

	The telegram said, "Your achievements have added luster to the glorious history of the indomitable women of South Vietnam."

	In the telegram, Nguyen Thi Dinh called on the heroic sisters of this squad to create even greater achievements. She also, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Vietnam South Women's Liberation Union, awarded the squad a flag to commend their outstanding battle achievements.

### 阮氏定授旗给顺化市一个女自卫班  嘉奖英雄妇女取得出色战功

1968-03-01
第5版()
专栏：

    阮氏定授旗给顺化市一个女自卫班
    嘉奖英雄妇女取得出色战功
    新华社河内二十八日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方妇女解放联合会主席阮氏定二十四日打电报给顺化市一个女自卫班，热烈表扬她们二月二十二日击溃一营美国海军陆战队的反扑，取得了歼灭美国侵略军一百二十名的辉煌战功。
    电报说，你们的成绩使英雄不屈的越南南方妇女的光荣史页增添了光辉。
    阮氏定在电报中号召这个班的英雄姐妹们建立更大的战功。她并且代表越南南方妇女解放联合会中央委员会授予这个班一面旗帜，嘉奖她们取得的出色战功。
