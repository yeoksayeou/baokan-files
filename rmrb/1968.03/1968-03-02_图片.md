Title: Farmers in Taicang County, Jiangsu, Strive for a Bumper Harvest
Author:
Date: 1968-03-02
Edition: 4

The broad masses of poor and lower-middle peasants in Hongjin Production Brigade, Taicang County, Jiangsu Province, are holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought. Taking the revolutionary spirit of Dazhai as their example, they are sparing no effort to find sources of fertilizer and accumulate organic manure, determined to achieve a bumper harvest this year.
