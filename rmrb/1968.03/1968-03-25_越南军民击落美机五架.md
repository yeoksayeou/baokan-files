Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Five U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-03-25
Edition: 6

Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down five U.S. aircraft.

According to reports from here, on the 20th, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft, and the U.S. pilot was captured alive on the spot. On the 18th, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province also shot down one U.S. aircraft.

It is also reported that on the 20th, the artillery units of the people's army in the Vinh Linh area hit and set on fire one invading U.S. warship.

According to reports from here, on the 20th, the military and civilians of Ha Tinh Province in North Vietnam shot down two U.S. aggressor aircraft.

On the 16th, the militia of Thai Binh Province also shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft.

### 越南军民击落美机五架

1968-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美机五架
    新华社河内二十三日电　据这里报道，越南北方河静省军民，二十日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架，美国飞贼被当场活捉。十八日，广平省军民也击落美机一架。
    另据报道，永灵区人民军炮兵部队，二十日把一艘入侵的美国军舰击中起火。
    新华社河内二十二日电　据这里报道，越南北方义安省军民二十日击落美国强盗飞机两架。
    太平省民兵十六日也击落美国强盗飞机一架。
