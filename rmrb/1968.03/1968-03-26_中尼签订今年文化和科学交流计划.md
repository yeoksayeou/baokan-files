Title: China and Nepal Sign Cultural and Scientific Exchange Plan for 1968
Author:
Date: 1968-03-26
Edition: 6

The 1968 cultural and scientific exchange plan between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal was signed on March 24 in Kathmandu. This plan is based on the Cultural Cooperation Agreement signed on October 11, 1964.

Chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Nepal, Tu Guowei, and Secretary of the Nepalese Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, Ram Bahadur Rawal, signed the exchange plan on behalf of their respective governments.

### 中尼签订今年文化和科学交流计划

1968-03-26
第6版()
专栏：

    中尼签订今年文化和科学交流计划
    新华社加德满都二十四日电　中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔王国政府一九六八年文化和科学交流计划二十四日在这里签字。这项计划是根据一九六四年十月十一日签订的中尼文化合作协定制订的。
    中国驻尼泊尔大使馆临时代办屠国维和尼泊尔新闻广播部秘书拉姆·巴哈杜尔·拉瓦格分别代表两国政府在交流计划上签字。
