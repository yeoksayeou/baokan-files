Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Head Returns to Beijing; North Korean Deputy Prime Minister Receives Chinese Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-03-09
Edition: 6

	Chinese Government Trade Delegation Head, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Jia Shi, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after signing the 1968 Agreement on Mutual Supply of Goods between China and North Korea.

	Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang and others.

	The acting charge d'affaires of the North Korean Embassy in China, Kim Jae-suk, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

	P'yongyang, March 7 (New China News Agency) — On March 5, Deputy Prime Minister of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Ri Chu-yon, received the Chinese Government Trade Delegation led by Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Jia Shi and had a friendly talk with them.

	Present at the reception were Minister of Foreign Trade Kwe Yong-tae, Vice Minister Yu Song-yi, and others.

	Acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in the DPRK, Wang Peng, was also present.

### 我政府贸易代表团团长回到北京  朝内阁副首相接见我代表团

1968-03-09
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团团长回到北京
    朝内阁副首相接见我代表团
    新华社七日讯　中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长贾石，在签订了中朝两国一九六八年相互供应货物的议定书以后，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场迎接的有对外贸易部副部长李强等。
    朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑等也到机场迎接。
    新华社平壤七日电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相李周渊五日接见了由对外贸易部副部长贾石率领的中国政府贸易代表团，并同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，朝鲜贸易相桂应泰、副相柳成义等。
    中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办王彭也在座。
