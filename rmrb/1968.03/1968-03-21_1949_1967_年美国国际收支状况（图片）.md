Title: The U.S. International Balance of Payments from 1949 to 1967
Author:
Date: 1968-03-21
Edition: 6

From the above chart, it can be seen that since 1949, the U.S. international balance of payments has shown a deficit of tens of billions of dollars almost every year, except for the year 1957. During this period, successive U.S. administrations have used various methods to reduce the outflow of dollars. However, the cumulative deficit in the U.S. international balance of payments has still reached 35.2 billion dollars. This is one of the reasons for the increasingly severe dollar crisis.

### 1949——1967年美国国际收支状况（图片）

1968-03-21
第6版()
专栏：

    1949——1967年美国国际收支状况
    从上图可以看出，从一九四九年起，美国的国际收支，除一九五七年一年以外，几乎每年都出现十几亿或几十亿美元的逆差。在这段时期内，美国历届政府曾用各种办法来减少美元的外流，但是美国的国际收支逆差的累积数字仍然达到三百五十二亿美元。这是造成日益严重的美元危机的原因之一。
