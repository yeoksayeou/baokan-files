Title: China Donates Educational Supplies to Yemen's Ministry of Education
Author:
Date: 1968-03-05
Edition: 5

	China's Charge d'Affaires in Yemen, Zheng Kangping, on behalf of the Chinese Ministry of Education, presented 283 boxes of educational supplies to the Yemen Ministry of Education on March 2. Zheng Kangping and Yemen's Deputy Minister of Education, Yahya Shami, both spoke at the handover ceremony, praising the friendship between the Chinese and Yemeni peoples in their joint struggle against American imperialism.

	Zheng Kangping said, "The friendship between the Chinese and Yemeni peoples has developed and strengthened in our common struggle against American imperialism. We believe that cooperation and friendship between China and Yemen will continue to grow." He added, "The victories achieved by the Yemeni people in their struggle against American imperialism and the reactionary forces of Saudi Arabia have provided powerful support to all people around the world fighting against American imperialism. The victories of the Yemeni people are also the victories of the Chinese people and all the people of the world."

	Yemen's Deputy Minister of Education, Yahya Shami, highly praised China's assistance to Yemen and expressed gratitude for the gifts from the Chinese Ministry of Education. He said, "The Chinese people are sincere friends of the Yemeni people. The Chinese and Yemeni peoples are comrades-in-arms in the anti-imperialist struggle, and we should fight shoulder to shoulder to defeat the enemy."

	He also spoke about the recent victories of the Yemeni people in their struggle against the armed subversion plots orchestrated by American imperialism to overthrow the Yemeni republic, saying, "The facts have proven that imperialism and all reactionaries are paper tigers."

### 我赠送也门教育部一批教育用品

1968-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我赠送也门教育部一批教育用品
    新华社萨那三日电中国驻也门大使馆临时代办郑康平二日在这里代表中国教育部，把二百八十三箱教育用品赠送给也门教育部。郑康平和也门教育部副部长叶海亚·沙米在交接仪式上发表讲话，赞扬中也两国人民在反对美帝国主义的共同斗争中所结成的友谊。
    郑康平说：“中也两国人民的友谊，在反对美帝国主义的共同斗争中，得到了发展和加强。我们相信中也两国的合作和友谊将得到进一步的发展。”他说：“也门人民在反对美帝国主义和沙特阿拉伯反动派的武装颠覆阴谋的斗争中赢得的胜利，给了正在同美帝国主义斗争的全世界人民以有力的支持，也门人民的胜利也就是中国人民和全世界人民的胜利。”
    也门教育部副部长叶海亚·沙米盛赞中国对也门的援助，并且对中国教育部赠送的礼物表示感谢。他说：“中国人民是也门人民的真诚朋友。中也两国人民是反美斗争中的战友，我们两国人民应当并肩战斗，打败敌人。”
    他在谈到也门人民最近在反击美帝国主义策划的武装颠覆也门共和政权的战斗中所取得的胜利时说：“事实证明，帝国主义和一切反动派都是纸老虎。”
