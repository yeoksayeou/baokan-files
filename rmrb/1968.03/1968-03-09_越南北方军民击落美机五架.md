Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Five U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-03-09
Edition: 5

According to reports from here, the military and civilians in Hanoi, Haiphong, and Quang Binh in North Vietnam shot down three invading U.S. aircraft on March 6 during their counterattack against U.S. air pirates. On March 5, the military and civilians in Thanh Hoa Province and Hai Hung Province (formed by the merger of Hai Duong and Hung Yen Provinces) also shot down two U.S. aircraft.

### 越南北方军民击落美机五架

1968-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机五架
    新华社河内七日电　据这里报道，越南北方河内、海防和广平等地军民，六日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，共击落入侵的美国飞机三架。清化省和海兴省（由海阳和兴安两省合并而成）军民五日也击落美国强盗飞机两架。
