Title: Great Achievements in Frugality Revolution at Tao Yang Coal Mine in Shandong
Author:
Date: 1968-03-05
Edition: 4

The revolutionary workers of the Tao Yang Coal Mine under the Feicheng Mining Bureau in Shandong Province have thoroughly implemented Chairman Mao's great directives on "grasp revolution and promote production" and "make frugality a revolution." They have achieved remarkable results. From last August to this January, the entire mine saved the state 135,000 yuan and nearly 1,000 tons of coal.

### 山东陶阳煤矿节约闹革命成绩大

1968-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    山东陶阳煤矿节约闹革命成绩大
    山东肥城矿务局陶阳煤矿广大革命职工，深入贯彻毛主席关于“抓革命，促生产”和“要节约闹革命”的伟大指示，取得显著成绩。从去年八月到今年一月，全矿为国家节约资金十三万五千多元，节约煤炭近千吨。
                                  （据新华社讯）
