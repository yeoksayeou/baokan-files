Title: Comrades Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and Others Accompany Comrade Wilcock to Attend an Art Evening
Author:
Date: 1968-03-19
Edition: 1

Comrades Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and others accompanied Comrade Wilcock to attend an art evening and watched the revolutionary modern Beijing opera "Taking Tiger Mountain by Strategy." After the performance, they went on stage and shook hands warmly with the performers. Both on and off the stage, everyone shouted: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Chairman Mao lives ten thousand years!"

March 18, Xinhua News Agency reported: The General Secretary of the New Zealand Communist Party, Comrade V. J. Wilcock, attended an art evening held in his honor today.

At the evening, Comrade Wilcock, accompanied by Comrades Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, Xie Fuzhi, Wu Faqian, Wang Dongxing, Ye Qun, and others, watched the Beijing Opera Troupe No. 1's performance of the revolutionary modern Beijing opera "Taking Tiger Mountain by Strategy."

When Comrade Wilcock and Comrades Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and others entered the evening's venue, everyone in the audience stood up and applauded warmly to welcome Comrade Wilcock. Comrade Wilcock waved the red "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and greeted everyone warmly.

After the performance, everyone on and off the stage sang "The East is Red" and shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Chairman Mao lives ten thousand years!" Comrade Wilcock and Comrades Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and others went on stage amidst the cheers and shook hands warmly with the performers, congratulating them on their successful performance.

After the performance, Comrade Wilcock, accompanied by Comrades Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, and Jiang Qing, went on stage and posed for a group photo with the performers.

### 周恩来、康生、江青等同志陪同威尔科克斯同志出席文艺晚会　观看革命现代京剧《智取威虎山》后，走上舞台和演员亲切握手。台上台下高呼：毛主席万岁！毛主席万万岁！

1968-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来、康生、江青等同志陪同威尔科克斯同志出席文艺晚会
　　观看革命现代京剧《智取威虎山》后，走上舞台和演员亲切握手。台上台下高呼：毛主席万岁！毛主席万万岁！
    新华社十八日讯  新西兰共产党总书记维·乔·威尔科克斯同志，今天出席了为欢迎他而举行的文艺晚会。
    在晚会上，威尔科克斯同志由周恩来、康生、江青、谢富治、吴法宪、汪东兴、叶群等同志陪同，观看了北京京剧一团演出的革命现代京剧《智取威虎山》。
    威尔科克斯同志同周恩来、康生、江青等同志，走进晚会会场时，全场同志起立，热烈鼓掌，欢迎威尔科克斯同志。威尔科克斯同志挥动红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，向大家亲切致意。
    演出结束以后，台上台下齐唱《大海航行靠舵手》。高呼：“毛主席万岁！”
  “毛主席万万岁！”威尔科克斯同志和周恩来、康生、江青等同志，在欢呼声中走上舞台，和演员们亲切握手，祝贺他们演出的成功。
    （附图片）
　　新西兰共产党总书记维·乔·威尔科克斯同志在周恩来、康生、江青等同志陪同下，三月十八日出席了为欢迎他而举行的文艺晚会，观看了革命现代京剧《智取威虎山》。
    演出结束后，威尔科克斯同志由周恩来、康生、江青同志陪同上台和演员合影。
                  新华社记者摄
