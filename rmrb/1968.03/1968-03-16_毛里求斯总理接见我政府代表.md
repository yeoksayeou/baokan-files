Title: Mauritius Prime Minister Receives Chinese Government Representative
Author:
Date: 1968-03-16
Edition: 6

Mauritius Prime Minister Ramgoolam receives Chinese Government Representative

New China News Agency, Port Louis, March 15 — Mauritius Prime Minister Ramgoolam received Chinese Government Representative Huang Hua in the Government Building this morning.

Ramgoolam and Huang Hua had a friendly and cordial conversation on developing friendly and trade relations between Mauritius and China.

Huang Hua came to Mauritius at the invitation of the Mauritian Government to participate in the celebrations of Mauritius' independence.

### 毛里求斯总理接见我政府代表

1968-03-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　毛里求斯总理接见我政府代表
    新华社路易斯港十五日电　毛里求斯总理拉姆古兰十五日上午在这里的政府大厦接见了中华人民共和国政府代表黄华。
    拉姆古兰总理同中国政府代表黄华就毛里求斯和中国之间发展友好和贸易关系，进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    黄华是应毛里求斯政府邀请，代表中国政府前来参加毛里求斯独立庆典的。
