Title: The Revolutionary Committee is Good
Author:
Date: 1968-03-31
Edition: 3

The revolutionary "three-in-one" leading cadre, integrating the People's Liberation Army's three-eight style, the laboring people's spirit of hard struggle, and the Party's fine tradition of linking with the masses, embodies the principle of "being both an official and a common person."
