Title: Intercept Enemy Fleet, Shell U.S. Military Base, Attack Ammunition Depot
Author:
Date: 1968-03-17
Edition: 5

The Liberation Armed Forces of South Vietnam launched a series of attacks against the enemy around Saigon in early March, inflicting heavy losses on the U.S. puppet forces.

On March 6, the Liberation Armed Forces struck and damaged a U.S. 10,000-ton military vessel on the Long Tau River southeast of Saigon. On the morning of March 8, they struck and damaged a U.S. large oil tanker on the Long Tau River about 20 kilometers southeast of Saigon. The oil tanker, after being hit and set on fire, burned fiercely for 17 hours. On March 9 and 10, the Liberation Armed Forces continued to intercept U.S. military convoys on the Long Tau River, damaging three U.S. military vessels and killing many U.S. invaders.

On March 4 and 5, the Liberation Armed Forces in Hoc Mon District, Gia Dinh Province, consecutively intercepted the enemy, destroying 14 tanks and shooting down five planes, killing many U.S. invaders. On the night of March 5 and the morning of March 6, the Liberation Armed Forces twice shelled the U.S. 25th Infantry Division base in the Dong Na area of Go Vap District, causing the enemy heavy losses. The shelling on the night of March 5 alone killed and wounded 74 U.S. invaders, destroyed 18 helicopters and 48 barracks.

On the night of March 8, with the help of the people, the Liberation Armed Forces attacked an enemy ammunition depot 15 kilometers north of Saigon, causing the depot to explode and burn for six hours, and killing hundreds of puppet soldiers.

Previously, the Liberation Armed Forces in Binh Dinh Province, on February 27 and 28, in battles to shell enemy bases and intercept enemy forces, annihilated over 550 enemy troops, including over 350 U.S. invaders, shot down one helicopter, damaged four armored vehicles, and captured a large amount of weapons and military supplies. The local forces and guerrillas in Hoc Mon District, Gia Dinh Province, from February 26 to 29, consecutively intercepted the enemy, annihilating 370 U.S. invaders and destroying four tanks and armored vehicles.

	Heroic soldiers of the Liberation Armed Forces of South Vietnam, taking advantage of the initiative on the battlefield, launched fierce attacks on the U.S. puppet forces.

### 截击敌人船队　炮轰美军基地　攻打弹药仓库　南越军民在西贡周围痛打美伪军　边和省解放武装力量两天歼敌五百多名

1968-03-17
第5版()
专栏：

　　截击敌人船队　炮轰美军基地　攻打弹药仓库
　　南越军民在西贡周围痛打美伪军
　　边和省解放武装力量两天歼敌五百多名
    新华社河内十六日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方人民解放武装力量三月上旬连续在西贡周围袭击敌人，给美伪军以沉重打击。
    人民解放武装力量六日在西贡市东南的龙都河上击伤美国万吨军用船只一艘，八日上午又在西贡东南约二十公里的龙都河上击伤美军大型油船一艘，这艘油船被击中起火后，猛烈燃烧达十七小时。九日和十日，人民解放武装力量继续在龙都河上截击美军船队，击伤美国军用船只三艘，消灭许多美国侵略军。
    四日和五日，人民解放武装力量在嘉定省旭门县接连截击敌人，击毁坦克十四辆，击落飞机五架，消灭许多美国侵略军。五日夜间和六日清晨，人民解放武装力量连续两次炮击古芝县东如地区的美国第二十五步兵师基地，使敌人遭到严重损失。五日夜间的炮击就打死打伤美国侵略军七十四名，击毁直升飞机十八架和营房四十八座。
    八日夜间，人民解放武装力量在人民的帮助下，袭击了西贡市北十五公里的敌军弹药库，使弹药库爆炸和燃烧六小时，并消灭成百名伪军。
    在这以前，边和省人民解放武装力量，二月二十七日和二十八日两天在炮击敌军基地和截击敌军的战斗中，消灭敌人五百五十多名，其中包括美国侵略军三百五十多名，击落直升飞机一架，击伤装甲车四辆，缴获大批武器和军用物资。嘉定省旭门县的地方部队和游击队，从二月二十六日到二十九日四天中接连截击敌人，消灭美国侵略军三百七十名，击毁坦克、装甲车四辆。
