Title: West German Masses Hold Meetings and Demonstrations Against U.S. Aggression in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-03-26
Edition: 6

West German masses have been holding meetings and demonstrations in recent days to oppose U.S. imperialist aggression in Vietnam.

On the evening of the 22nd, 1,200 residents and youths of Hamburg held a meeting and demonstration march. Due to passersby joining in, the demonstration quickly grew to about 3,000 people.

On the 21st, over 300 youths in Cologne gathered in front of one of the U.S. aggression institutions, the "American House," to denounce the West German government's policy of supporting the U.S. war of aggression in Vietnam.

### 西德群众集会示威反对美帝侵略越南

1968-03-26
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    西德群众集会示威反对美帝侵略越南
    西德群众连日来纷纷集会示威，反对美帝国主义侵略越南。
    二十二日晚，一千二百名汉堡居民和青年举行了集会和示威游行。由于过路的人主动参加，示威队伍迅速扩大到约三千人。
    二十一日，科隆市的三百多名青年，在美国侵略机构之一“美国之家”的门前举行集会，谴责西德政府支持美国侵越战争的政策。
