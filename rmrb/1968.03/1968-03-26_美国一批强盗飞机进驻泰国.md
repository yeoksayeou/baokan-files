Title: A Batch of American Bandit Aircraft Deployed in Thailand
Author:
Date: 1968-03-26
Edition: 6

A batch of American bandit aircraft have been deployed in Thailand. The Thanom traitorous clique has sold out the national interests of Thailand, openly allowing the United States to deploy six F-111-A type fighter-bombers in Thailand. This batch of American fighter-bombers arrived at the Takhli U.S. Air Force base in Thailand on the 17th and immediately began their criminal activities of bombing North Vietnam.

Seventy-five percent of the U.S. imperialist aircraft bombing North Vietnam take off from Thai bases. Last year, the Thanom traitorous clique blatantly allowed U.S. B-52 bombers to be stationed in Thailand. In January of this year, the puppet Prime Minister Thanom boasted that Thailand would send an additional 12,000 infantry troops to South Vietnam within three months, thereby increasing the number of Thai troops sent to invade Vietnam to 15,000.

### 美国一批强盗飞机进驻泰国

1968-03-26
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    美国一批强盗飞机进驻泰国
    泰国他侬卖国集团出卖泰国民族利益，公然允许美国把六架F111—A型战斗轰炸机进驻泰国。这批美国战斗轰炸机十七日进驻泰国打卡里美国空军基地，当天即投入轰炸越南北方的罪恶活动。
    美帝国主义轰炸越南北方的强盗飞机，有百分之七十五以上是从泰国基地起飞的。去年上半年，他侬卖国集团明目张胆地让美国B—52型轰炸机进驻泰国。今年一月间傀儡总理他侬叫嚷，泰国将在三个月内增派一万二千名步兵去南越，从而使泰国提供的侵越炮灰增加到一万五千名。
                          （据新华社讯）
