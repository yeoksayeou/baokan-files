Title: Celebrating the Fourth Anniversary of Diplomatic Relations Between the People's Republic of the Congo and the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1968-03-23
Edition: 5

The Congolese (Brazzaville) Ambassador to China, Bazanga, held a reception today to celebrate the fourth anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of the Congo and the People's Republic of China.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and relevant officials attended the reception at the invitation. Ambassador Bazanga and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Pengfei both spoke at the reception. They toasted the continued development of friendship between the peoples of China and the Congo (Brazzaville).

### 庆祝刚果（布）和中国建交四周年  刚果（布）驻华大使举行酒会

1968-03-23
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝刚果（布）和中国建交四周年
    刚果（布）驻华大使举行酒会
    新华社二十二日讯　刚果（布）驻中国大使巴赞加今天举行酒会，庆祝刚果（布）共和国同中华人民共和国建立外交关系四周年。
    陈毅副总理和有关方面的负责人，应邀出席了酒会。巴赞加大使和外交部副部长姬鹏飞在酒会上先后讲话。他们祝中刚（布）两国人民的友谊不断发展。
