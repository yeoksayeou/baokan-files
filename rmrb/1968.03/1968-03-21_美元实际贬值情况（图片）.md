Title: The Actual Devaluation of the Dollar
Author:
Date: 1968-03-21
Edition: 6

The long-term trend of inflation in the United States has led to a gradual decline in the purchasing power of the dollar. In 1966, the purchasing power of one dollar was equivalent to only 42.2 cents in 1939.
