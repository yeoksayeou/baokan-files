Title: Representative of the Japan-China Memorandum Trade Office Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1968-03-09
Edition: 6

Representatives of the Japan-China Memorandum Trade Office, including Furui Yoshizane, Okazaki Kabeita, and Tagawa Seiichi, along with three other members, departed Beijing today to return to their home country.

### 日本日中备忘录贸易办事处代表离京回国

1968-03-09
第6版()
专栏：

    日本日中备忘录贸易办事处代表离京回国
    新华社七日讯　日本日中备忘录贸易办事处代表古井喜实、冈崎嘉平太、田川诚一等一行五人，今天离开北京回国。
