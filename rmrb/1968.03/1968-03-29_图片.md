Title: A Family in Jidu Brigade, Xiyang County, Shanxi Province, Posts a Big-Character Poster Criticizing the Crimes of Comrade-in-Arms Revisionism in the Countryside
Author:
Date: 1968-03-29
Edition: 3

    The brigade leader of Jidu Production Brigade, Xiyang County, Shanxi Province, Chen Simo, and his family are posting a big-character poster, criticizing the heinous crimes of Comrade-in-Arms Revisionism in attempting to restore capitalism in the countryside.
