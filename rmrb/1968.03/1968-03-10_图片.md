Title: Armored Troops' First Study Session of Chairman Mao's Works
Author:
Date: 1968-03-10
Edition: 2

The representatives of the first conference of active participants in the study of Chairman Mao's works by the armored troops had the great honor and immense joy of meeting our great leader Chairman Mao. The representatives immediately spread the wonderful news to their respective units via long-distance telephone calls throughout the night.
