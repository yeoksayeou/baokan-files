Title: South Yemen Sentences Former Puppet Ministers to Death and Imprisonment
Author:
Date: 1968-03-26
Edition: 6

The Supreme State Security Court of the People's Republic of South Yemen sentenced six former puppet ministers of the British-controlled "Federation of South Arabia" to death, five to fifteen years in prison, and another five to ten years in prison. The court also ordered the confiscation of all their movable and immovable property. The court handed down the verdicts in person to four of the puppet ministers, while the remaining ministers who had fled abroad were tried in absentia.

### 南也门判处前傀儡部长死刑和徒刑

1968-03-26
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    南也门判处前傀儡部长死刑和徒刑
    南也门人民共和国国家最高治安法庭，十八日判处前英国操纵的傀儡政权“南阿拉伯联邦”的六名傀儡部长死刑，五名傀儡部长十五年徒刑，另五名十年徒刑，同时下令没收他们的一切动产和不动产。这个法庭对四名傀儡部长进行当面判决，对其余已经逃往国外的傀儡部长进行缺席判决。
