Title: Carry Forward the Spirit of Fearless Fatigue and Continuous Combat: The People of South Vietnam Advance Bravely in Victory
Author:
Date: 1968-03-16
Edition: 5

In victory, the military and civilian population of South Vietnam continue to advance bravely. According to the Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi, the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency reported: In late February and early March, the military and civilian population of South Vietnam continued to attack many enemy towns, strongholds, and airfields, continuously striking back against the enemy's desperate counterattacks, and annihilating a large number of enemy forces.

The people's liberation forces in Gia Dinh Province, from February 26 to March 4, repeatedly attacked the enemy in the Ke Chia County, who were attempting to relieve the siege of Saigon City and New Mountain Airfield. They annihilated more than a thousand enemy soldiers, the majority of whom were American invaders, completely wiping out the U.S. 25th Infantry Division's 4th Battalion.

The local forces and guerrillas in Binh Dinh Province, from February 27 to March 4, repeatedly thwarted the enemy's "sweeping" operations in the Chuong City and New Yen County areas, annihilating an entire U.S. infantry company and two platoons of puppet troops, and severely damaging a U.S. company and a puppet regiment.

The military and civilian population of Quang Nam Province, from the night of March 3 to the early morning of March 4, while attacking the cities of Da Nang, Tam Ky, and various county seats, also launched attacks on a series of enemy strongholds and airfields, annihilating more than 350 enemy soldiers, completely wiping out a U.S. company, shooting down, destroying, and damaging 53 enemy aircraft, destroying and damaging more than ten military vehicles and seven cannons and mortars, and burning down four fuel and ammunition depots.

The people's liberation forces in Phu Yen Province, from the night of March 3 to the early morning of March 4, continuously and fiercely bombarded and attacked many enemy targets in the Suoi Pho and Phu My counties, annihilating hundreds of enemy soldiers, including the entire command of a puppet regiment, two infantry companies (one of which was a South Korean mercenary company), and an armored regiment.

The people's liberation forces in Kontum Province, from February 25 to March 2, continuously attacked the enemy in the De Tu area and the outskirts of Kontum City, killing and wounding more than 300 enemy soldiers, the majority of whom were American invaders, and shooting down ten enemy aircraft.

The people's liberation forces in Dac Lac Province, in the early morning of March 2, fiercely attacked the enemy's Ganh Gha airfield west of Da Lat City, destroying sixty military vehicles, one power plant, and many barracks, burning down one fuel depot and many military supplies, severely damaging a U.S. engineering company, annihilating many American pilots, and completely paralyzing the enemy's airfield.

The people's liberation forces in Binh Duong Province, on March 1, fiercely intercepted the enemy attempting to open Route 6, annihilating three puppet companies, killing, wounding, and capturing 231 enemy soldiers.

The people's liberation forces in Long An Province, on the night of March 3 and March 4, attacked the enemy in the outskirts of Long An City, annihilating more than 200 enemy soldiers and severely damaging a puppet regiment.

The people's liberation forces in Kien Giang Province, from the night of March 4 to the evening of March 6, continuously bombarded the Cho No military airfield and ambushed and attacked the enemy, annihilating more than 100 puppet soldiers and destroying three aircraft.

The people's liberation forces in Tuyen Duc Province, from the night of March 4 to the morning of March 6, continuously attacked many enemy targets in Tuyen Duc City and nearby towns, annihilating nearly 200 enemy soldiers.

The military and civilian population of Binh Phuoc Province, from the night of March 4 to the morning of March 6, continuously attacked the provincial capital of Loc Ninh City and the county seat of Buu Doc, annihilating more than 150 enemy soldiers.

The people's liberation forces in Kien Giang Province, from the night of March 4 to March 6, fiercely attacked twelve enemy targets in the Kien Giang City area, killing and wounding more than 300 enemy soldiers, burning down one enemy fuel depot and one rocket warehouse, destroying one sentry post, and severely damaging the enemy's military command center.

### 发扬不怕疲劳连续作战的作风　越南南方军民在胜利中奋勇前进

1968-03-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　发扬不怕疲劳连续作战的作风
　　越南南方军民在胜利中奋勇前进
    据新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：在胜利中奋勇前进的越南南方军民二月底和三月初继续进攻敌人的许多城镇、据点和机场，不断痛击疯狂进行反扑的敌军，又歼灭了敌军大量有生力量。
    嘉定省人民解放武装力量从二月二十六日到三月四日，接连多次在芽?县袭击妄图为西贡市和新山一机场解围的敌军，共消灭敌人一千多名，其中绝大部分是美国侵略军，全歼美国第二十五步兵师第四营。
    边和省地方部队和游击队，在二月二十七日到三月四日接连挫败敌人对周城县和新渊县周围地区的“扫荡”，全歼美国侵略军一个连和伪军两个排，重创美军一个连和伪军一个营。
    广南省军民在三月三日夜间到四日凌晨，在进攻岘港市、三岐市和各个县城的同时，还向一系列敌军据点和机场发动进攻，消灭美伪军三百五十多名，全歼美军一个连，击落、击毁和击伤敌机五十三架，击毁、击伤军车十多辆和大炮、迫击炮七门，烧毁汽油库和弹药库四座。
    平定省人民解放武装力量从三月三日夜间到四日凌晨，连续猛烈炮击和进攻绥福和符美县的许多敌军目标，共消灭敌人数百名，其中包括全歼伪军一个营指挥部、两个步兵连（有一连南朝鲜帮凶军）和一个装甲营。
    昆嵩省人民解放武装力量从二月二十五日到三月二日，连续进攻德都地区和昆嵩市附近地区的敌人，打死打伤敌人三百多名，其中大部分是美国侵略军，并击落敌机十架。
    宣德省人民解放武装力量三月二日凌晨猛烈袭击大叻市西面的甘漓敌军机场，击毁军车六十辆、发电厂一座和营房多幢，烧毁汽油库一座和许多军用物资，重创美军一个工兵连，消灭许多美国飞行员，使敌人这个机场完全瘫痪。
    槟椥省人民解放武装力量三月一日猛烈截击企图打通六号公路的敌人，歼灭伪军三个连，共打死打伤和俘虏敌军二百三十一名。
    永隆省人民解放武装力量三日夜间和四日在永隆市周围地区袭击敌人，共消灭敌人二百多名，重创伪军一个营。
    芹苴省人民解放武装力量三月四日夜间到六日晚上接连炮击茶诺军用机场并伏击和袭击敌军，消灭伪军一百多名，击毁飞机三架。
    茶荣省人民解放武装力量在三月四日夜间到六日上午，接连进攻茶荣市的敌军许多目标和附近市镇的驻地，共消灭敌人近二百名。
    建丰省军民在三月四日夜间到六日上午，连续进攻该省省会高岭市和建文县城，消灭敌人一百五十多名。
    金瓯省人民解放武装力量在三月四日夜间到六日，猛烈袭击金瓯市区的十二个敌人目标，打死打伤敌军三百多名，烧毁敌人汽油库、火箭仓库各一座，摧毁哨所一个，严重破坏敌军军事小区指挥部。
