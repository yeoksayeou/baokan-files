Title: The Southern Daughters Train in Marksmanship
Author:
Date: 1968-03-07
Edition: 4

	The river's current is swift, the forest dense,
		The daughters of the south practice marksmanship.
		Each one becomes a sharpshooter,
		In the woods and on the water, many enemies are slain.
