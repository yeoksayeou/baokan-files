Title: The Vietnam South Women's Liberation Union Calls on South Vietnamese Women in Battle to Advance in Victory and Defeat the American Aggressors and Overthrow the Traitorous Clique
Author:
Date: 1968-03-07
Edition: 4

The Vietnam South Women's Liberation Union has issued a call to South Vietnamese women in battle, urging them to advance in victory, defeat the American aggressors, and overthrow the traitorous clique.

New China News Agency, Hanoi, March 6 - According to the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Vietnam South Women's Liberation Union recently issued a notice on the occasion of International Women's Day and the seventh anniversary of the Vietnam South Women's Liberation Union. The notice calls on South Vietnamese women to advance in victory and, together with the entire military and people of the nation, to thoroughly defeat the American aggressors.

The notice states: This year's International Women's Day comes at a time when all women in South Vietnam are resolutely responding to the call for a general offensive and uprising by the Central Committee of the Vietnam South National Liberation Front. This is to smash the aggression of American imperialism, overthrow the traitorous clique of the Vietnam puppets, and seize all power back into the hands of the people.

The notice warmly praises the women of South Vietnam for upholding their traditions of bravery and indomitability, participating in battles on all fronts, and making contributions alongside the broad masses of the people to achieve great victories.

The notice emphasizes that South Vietnamese women must further strengthen armed struggle, political struggle, and the work of dissolving enemy forces. They must deepen their hatred for the American bandits and their lackeys, advance in victory, and resolutely, together with the entire military and people of the nation, complete the historical mission of liberating the South, defending the North, and reunifying the fatherland.

### 越南南方妇女解放联合会向战斗中的南越妇女发出号召  乘胜前进战胜美国侵略者打倒卖国集团

1968-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    越南南方妇女解放联合会向战斗中的南越妇女发出号召
    乘胜前进战胜美国侵略者打倒卖国集团
    新华社河内六日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方妇女解放联合会中央常务委员会，最近就“三八”国际妇女节和越南南方妇女解放联合会成立七周年发出通知，号召南方妇女乘胜前进，同全国军民一道，彻底战胜美国侵略者。
    通知说：今年的国际妇女节，是在越南南方全体妇女坚决响应越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会向敌人全面进攻的战斗号召，以便粉碎美帝国主义的侵略，打倒越奸卖国集团，把全部政权夺回到人民手中的时候到来的。
    通知热烈赞扬南方妇女发扬了英勇不屈的传统，参加了各条战线的战斗，同广大人民一道为夺取伟大胜利作出了贡献。
    通知强调指出：越南南方妇女必须进一步加强武装斗争、政治斗争和瓦解敌军的工作，加深对美国强盗及其走狗的仇恨，乘胜前进，坚决同全国军民一道，完成解放南方、保卫北方、统一祖国的历史使命。（附图片）
