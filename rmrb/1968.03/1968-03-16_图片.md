Title: The Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants of Chairman Mao's Hometown Shaoshan Commune Take Up the Heavy Burdens of Revolution and Production
Author:
Date: 1968-03-16
Edition: 4

The poor and lower-middle peasants of Shaoshan Commune, Chairman Mao's hometown in Hunan, resolutely respond to Chairman Mao's great call. They bravely shoulder the dual burdens of revolution and production. With the revolutionary spirit of "seizing the day," they have launched a spring agricultural production campaign. This is a scene of poor and lower-middle peasant members of Shaoshan Commune's Shaoguang Production Brigade turning over fertilizer. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)
