Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Cables Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1968-03-07
Edition: 4

Chairman Ho Chi Minh Cables Premier Zhou Enlai

March 6th, Xinhua News Agency reported: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam today cabled Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Political Consultative Conference to mourn the passing of Madame Xie Guangping. The text of the cable is as follows:

"I was deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Madame Xie Guangping. I extend my heartfelt condolences to you and the family of Madame Xie."

### 胡志明主席致电周恩来同志  电唁许广平同志逝世

1968-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席致电周恩来同志
    电唁许广平同志逝世
    新华社六日讯　越南民主共和国胡志明主席今天致电人民政协周恩来同志，悼念许广平同志的逝世。电文内容如下：
    “获悉许广平同志逝世，我极为沉痛。谨向你和许同志家属表示我深切的悼念。”
