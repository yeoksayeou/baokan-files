Title: Indefinitely Loyal to Chairman Mao, Indefinitely Loyal to Mao Zedong Thought, Indefinitely Loyal to Chairman Mao's Proletarian Revolutionary Line
Author:
Date: 1968-03-08
Edition: 4

	Indefinitely loyal to Chairman Mao, indefinitely loyal to Mao Zedong Thought, indefinitely loyal to Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line, our heroic People's Liberation Army Air Force, today in the afternoon, in the skies over a certain location in the southwest, shot down one American imperialist military unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft that had intruded into our airspace for reconnaissance and provocation. This is the result of our Air Force, with the invincible Mao Zedong Thought as the banner, emphasizing proletarian politics, taking "criticize and repudiate revisionism" as the key link, continuously strengthening class struggle education, continuously raising the awareness of line struggle, and doing a good job of the thought revolutionization of people. This is a heavy blow to the American imperialists' plot to expand their aggression and make a last-ditch struggle.

	New China News Agency, March 7th: The Central Military Commission today issued an order of commendation, commending the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force for shooting down one American imperialist military unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft over a certain location in the southwest.

	The order of commendation states: In the midst of the nationwide billion revolutionary masses' advance to achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, you, with the deepest proletarian feelings of loyalty to the great leader Chairman Mao, with the greatest hatred towards American imperialism, bravely defended our socialist motherland, and shot down one American imperialist military unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft that had intruded into the skies over a certain location in the southwest for reconnaissance and provocation. This is the result of your response to the call of Vice Chairman Lin, of holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, of studying and applying Mao Zedong Thought actively. It is the victory achieved by your faithful execution of Chairman Mao's latest instructions. You are hereby commended in orders.

	The order of commendation states: The great commander Chairman Mao teaches us: "All commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army must not relax their fighting will even slightly." The American aggressors, who have been beaten to a pulp by the Vietnamese people and are facing imminent doom, will not accept their defeat and will continue to make trouble and create disturbances. Therefore, you must hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, emphasize proletarian politics, further implement the great call of the great leader Chairman Mao to "grasp revolution, promote preparedness," and resolutely carry out Chairman Mao's order to "devote all efforts to annihilate the invading enemy." You must decisively, thoroughly, completely, and entirely annihilate all air pirates who dare to intrude into our airspace. You must continue to respond to the call of the great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin, achieve new merits in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. You must continue to use your actual actions to defend the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and defend the sacred airspace of our motherland.

### 无限忠于毛主席，无限忠于毛泽东思想，无限忠于毛主席的无产阶级革命路线  我空军击落美帝无人驾驶高空侦察机一架

1968-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    无限忠于毛主席，无限忠于毛泽东思想，无限忠于毛主席的无产阶级革命路线
    我空军击落美帝无人驾驶高空侦察机一架
    中央军委颁发嘉奖令，表扬我空军部队的胜利，并勉励他们进一步抓革命，促战备，把敢于侵犯我国领空的一切空中强盗坚决、彻底、干净、全部消灭之
    新华社七日讯　无限忠于毛主席，无限忠于毛泽东思想，无限忠于毛主席无产阶级革命路线的我英雄的人民解放军空军部队，今天下午，在西南某地上空，一举击落侵入我国领空进行侦察挑衅的美帝国主义军用无人驾驶高空侦察机一架。这是我空军部队以战无不胜的毛泽东思想挂帅，突出无产阶级政治，以“斗私，批修”为纲，不断加强阶级斗争教育，不断提高路线斗争觉悟，做好人的思想革命化的结果。这是对美帝国主义阴谋扩大侵略战争，进行垂死挣扎的沉重打击。
    新华社七日讯　中央军委今天颁发嘉奖令，表扬今天在西南某地上空一举击落美帝国主义军用无人驾驶高空侦察机一架的中国人民解放军空军部队。
    嘉奖令说，在全国亿万革命群众夺取无产阶级文化大革命全面胜利的进军中，你们以无限忠于伟大领袖毛主席的最深厚的无产阶级感情，以对美帝国主义的最大仇恨，英勇地保卫我社会主义祖国，一举击落侵入我西南某地上空进行侦察挑衅的美帝国主义军用无人驾驶高空侦察机一架，这是你们响应林副主席的号召，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，活学活用毛泽东思想的结果，是你们忠实地执行毛主席最新指示所取得的胜利，特予通令嘉奖。
    嘉奖令说，伟大统帅毛主席教导我们：“人民解放军的全体指挥员、战斗员，绝对不可以稍微松懈自己的战斗意志”。被越南人民打得焦头烂额，面临灭顶之灾的美国侵略者，决不会甘心自己的失败，还会继续进行破坏和捣乱。因此，你们必须更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，大力突出无产阶级政治，进一步贯彻伟大领袖毛主席“抓革命，促战备”的伟大号召，坚决执行毛主席“全力以赴，务歼入侵之敌”的命令，把敢于侵犯我国领空的一切空中强盗坚决、彻底、干净、全部消灭之。你们要继续响应伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席的号召，在无产阶级文化大革命中立新功。要继续用你们的实际行动保卫无产阶级文化大革命，保卫祖国的神圣领空。
