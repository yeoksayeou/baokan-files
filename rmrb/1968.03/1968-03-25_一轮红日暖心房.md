Title: A Radiant Sun Warms the Heart
Author: Mǎlái Yá Xiàngrìhuī
Date: 1968-03-25
Edition: 5

A badge of Chairman Mao,
	Precious, so precious, placed in that small wooden box;
	Precious, so precious, hidden in his red heart.

A badge of Chairman Mao,
	Like a radiant sun,
	Emitting rays of light,
	Illuminating his path forward!
	Warming his heart!

The reactionaries tightly seal off revolutionary thoughts,
	Even a small badge they fear so much,
	All because the badge bears a giant who makes reactionaries tremble!

No matter how strict the reactionaries' blockade,
	We will read Chairman Mao's books,
	No matter how intense the reactionaries' persecution,
	We will vigorously promote the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!

This small badge holds boundless power!
	Looking at Chairman Mao on the badge,
	Waves of warmth fill his heart!
	Remembering Chairman Mao's teachings,
	He is filled with strength!
	He vows to Chairman Mao: "Forever follow your instructions, dedicate my life to revolution!"

	(From the Xinhua News Agency, according to the Malaysian "Front Line News")

### 一轮红日暖心房
向日葵
1968-03-25
第5版()
专栏：

    一轮红日暖心房
    马来亚　向日葵
    一枚毛主席像章，
    珍贵地、珍贵地放在那小小的木盒里；
    珍贵地、珍贵地藏在他赤红的心上。
    一枚毛主席像章，
    就象一轮红日，
    放射万丈光芒，
    照亮了他前进的道路！
    温暖了他的心房！
    反动派严密封锁革命的思想，
    连一枚像章也怕得要命，
    只因为像章上有一位使反动派见了就发抖的巨人！
    任凭反动派封锁再严，
    我们也要读毛主席的书，
    任凭反动派的迫害加剧，
    我们也要大力宣传那战无不胜的毛泽东思想！
    这枚小小的像章却有无穷无尽的力量！
    看了像章上的毛主席，
    一阵阵热流暖他的心房！
    想起了毛主席的教导，
    他浑身都是力量！
    他向毛主席表决心：“永远照您的指示办事，一辈子干革命！”
  （新华社据马来亚《阵线报》）
