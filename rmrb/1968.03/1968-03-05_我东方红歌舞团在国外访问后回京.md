Title: The Chinese "East is Red" Song and Dance Troupe Returns to Beijing After a Foreign Tour
Author:
Date: 1968-03-05
Edition: 5

The Chinese "East is Red" Song and Dance Troupe, which was invited by the governments of Zambia, Tanzania, Somalia, and Pakistan to perform, returned to Beijing recently. The troupe, led by Director Xu Ping, consisted of 53 members.

Various responsible comrades from relevant departments were at the airport to welcome them.

The acting charge d'affaires of the Tanzanian Embassy in China, Mr. Luankisa, and the wife of Ambassador Mwaluko, as well as officials from the Pakistani Embassy in China, were also present at the airport to welcome the troupe.

### 我东方红歌舞团在国外访问后回京

1968-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我东方红歌舞团在国外访问后回京
    新华社二日讯　应赞比亚、坦桑尼亚、索马里和巴基斯坦等国政府邀请访问演出的中国东方红歌舞团一行五十三人，在团长徐平率领下，最近回到北京。
    到机场迎接的有各有关方面的负责同志。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办卢安基萨和姆瓦卢科大使的夫人，巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆官员等也到机场迎接。
