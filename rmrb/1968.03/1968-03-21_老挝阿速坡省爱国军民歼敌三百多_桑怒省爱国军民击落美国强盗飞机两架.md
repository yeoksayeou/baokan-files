Title: Patriotic Military and Civilians in Attapeu Province, Laos, Annihilate Over 300 Enemy Troops; Patriotic Military and Civilians in Sam Neua Province Shoot Down Two U.S. Aggressor Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-03-21
Edition: 5

Patriotic military and civilian forces in Attapeu Province, Laos, have annihilated over 300 enemy troops since January 23. According to a report from the Pathet Lao News Agency, the patriotic armed forces in Attapeu Province have launched fierce attacks on the enemy forces stationed around Attapeu City and its vicinity, annihilating a total of 337 enemy troops.

On January 23, the patriotic military and civilians in Ban Hong, Chin Bao, and Xekagang in Attapeu Province killed and wounded 152 enemy troops.

In addition, the patriotic military and civilians intercepted and annihilated 185 enemy troops in multiple ambushes in the Thakhet region on January 23, 25, and March 2.

The patriotic military and civilians have also successively liberated the regions of Ban Hong, Chin Bao, Kano, and Khong Hang, which are near Attapeu City.

According to a report from the Pathet Lao News Agency, on March 16, the patriotic military and civilians in Sam Neua Province shot down one U.S. F-105 and one F-4H aircraft in their counterattack against U.S. air aggressors.

### 老挝阿速坡省爱国军民歼敌三百多  桑怒省爱国军民击落美国强盗飞机两架

1968-03-21
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝阿速坡省爱国军民歼敌三百多
    桑怒省爱国军民击落美国强盗飞机两架
    据新华社康开二十日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝阿速坡省爱国武装力量从一月二十三日以来，连续向盘踞在阿速坡市周围地区的敌人发动猛烈进攻，共歼灭敌人三百三十七名。
    一月二十三日，阿速坡省爱国军民在班洪、琴波和舍卡甘等地打死打伤敌人一百五十二名。
    此外，爱国军民在一月二十三日、二十五日和三月二日在塔欣特地区还多次截击敌人的援军，共歼灭敌人一百八十五名。
    爱国军民还先后解放了靠近阿速坡市的班洪、琴波、卡努和空杭等地区。
    新华社康开二十日电据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝桑怒省爱国军民十六日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，击落美国F—105和F—4H飞机各一架。
