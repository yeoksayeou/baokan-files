Title: Invited to Attend Mauritius Independence Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1968-03-12
Edition: 5

The government representative of the People's Republic of China, Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic Huang Hua, arrived here by airplane in the afternoon of the 11th to attend the independence celebrations of Mauritius at the invitation.

Mauritius Prime Minister Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam and government ministers, members of parliament, and many Mauritian friends welcomed the Chinese government representative at the airport.

Local Chinese representatives also went to the airport to welcome them.

The government representative of the People's Republic of China, Huang Hua, paid a courtesy call on Mauritius Prime Minister Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam on the 11th.

Huang Hua, on behalf of the Chinese government and people, extended sincere congratulations to Prime Minister Ramgoolam, the Mauritius government, and the people.

Prime Minister Ramgoolam had a cordial and friendly conversation with the Chinese government representative.

### 应邀参加毛里求斯独立庆典　我政府代表到达毛里求斯　我政府代表拜会拉姆古兰总理

1968-03-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　应邀参加毛里求斯独立庆典
　　我政府代表到达毛里求斯
　　我政府代表拜会拉姆古兰总理
    新华社路易斯港十一日电　中华人民共和国政府代表、中国驻阿联大使黄华十日下午乘飞机到达这里，应邀前来参加毛里求斯的独立庆典。
    毛里求斯总理西乌萨加·拉姆古兰和政府部长、议员、以及许多毛里求斯朋友到机场欢迎中国政府代表。
    到机场欢迎的还有当地的华侨代表。
    新华社路易斯港十一日电　应邀前来参加毛里求斯独立庆典的中华人民共和国政府代表黄华十一日中午拜会了毛里求斯总理西乌萨加·拉姆古兰。
    黄华代表中国政府和人民向拉姆古兰总理、毛里求斯政府和人民致以衷心的祝贺。
    拉姆古兰总理同中国政府代表进行了亲切友好的谈话。
