Title: The China-Africa Friendship Association Cables Congratulations on the Independence of Mauritius
Author:
Date: 1968-03-13
Edition: 6

The China-Africa Friendship Association sent a cable on March 11 to Prime Minister Seewoosagur Ramgoolam of Mauritius, extending warm congratulations on the declaration of Mauritius's independence.

The cable said, "On the occasion of Mauritius's independence, the China-Africa Friendship Association extends warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Mauritius. We wish the people of Mauritius new achievements in their struggle against imperialism and neo-colonialism, old and new, in consolidating national independence, and in building their own country. We wish the friendship between the peoples of China and Mauritius to continue to develop and strengthen."

### 中非友协电贺毛里求斯独立

1968-03-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　中非友协电贺毛里求斯独立
    新华社十二日讯　中非友协十一日打电报给毛里求斯总理西乌萨加·拉姆古兰，热烈祝贺毛里求斯宣告独立。
    贺电说，值此毛里求斯独立之际，中国非洲人民友好协会向阁下和毛里求斯人民致以热烈的祝贺。祝毛里求斯人民独立后在反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义、巩固民族独立和建设自己国家的事业中取得新的成就。祝中国和毛里求斯两国人民之间的友谊不断发展和巩固。
