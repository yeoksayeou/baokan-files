Title: China and Czechoslovakia Sign Annual Barter and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1968-03-28
Edition: 5

	The 1968 annual barter and payment agreement between China and Czechoslovakia was signed in Beijing on March 26.

	Li Qiang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Miroslav Honusek, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade and head of the Czechoslovak government trade delegation, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments. The Czechoslovak government trade delegation departed Beijing on March 27 to return home.

### 中捷签订本年度换货和付款协定

1968-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中捷签订本年度换货和付款协定
    新华社二十七日讯　中国和捷克斯洛伐克一九六八年度交换货物和付款协定，二十六日在北京签字。
    对外贸易部副部长李强和捷克斯洛伐克政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长米·霍努塞克，分别代表两国政府在协定上签字。捷克斯洛伐克政府贸易代表团已于二十七日离京回国。
