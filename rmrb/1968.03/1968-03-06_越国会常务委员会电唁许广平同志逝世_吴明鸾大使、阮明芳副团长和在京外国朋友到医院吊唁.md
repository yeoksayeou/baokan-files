Title: The Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly Cables Condolences on the Passing of Xu Guangping
Author:
Date: 1968-03-06
Edition: 4

The Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sent a telegram to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress today, expressing deep sorrow over the passing of Standing Committee member Xu Guangping.

Ambassador Wu Mingluan of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Deputy Head of the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China Nguyen Minh Phuong, representatives of some international organizations in China, and some foreign friends in Beijing paid their respects at the hospital yesterday and today. The remains of Xu Guangping were cremated this afternoon.

### 越国会常务委员会电唁许广平同志逝世  吴明鸾大使、阮明芳副团长和在京外国朋友到医院吊唁

1968-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    越国会常务委员会电唁许广平同志逝世
    吴明鸾大使、阮明芳副团长和在京外国朋友到医院吊唁
    新华社五日讯　越南民主共和国国会常务委员会今天打电报给全国人民代表大会常务委员会，对常务委员许广平同志的逝世表示沉痛的悼念。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团副团长阮明芳，在中国的一些国际组织的代表，以及在北京的一些外国朋友，昨天和今天曾到医院吊唁。许广平同志的遗体，已于今日下午火化。
