Title: Hanoi Military and Civilians Shoot Down One American Unmanned Reconnaissance Aircraft and Three Aircraft in Hai Phong and Other Areas
Author:
Date: 1968-03-29
Edition: 6

Hanoi military and civilians have shot down one American unmanned reconnaissance aircraft.

Hai Phong and other areas' military and civilians have shot down three American aircraft.

New China News Agency, Hanoi, March 28 — According to reports from here, the military and civilians of North Vietnam have shot down four American aggressor aircraft in recent days.

On March 27, the Hanoi air defense forces shot down one American unmanned reconnaissance aircraft. This is the tenth American unmanned reconnaissance aircraft shot down by the military and civilians of North Vietnam this month.

On March 25, the Hai Phong air defense forces shot down one invading American A-6 type aircraft.

On the night of March 22, the military and civilians of Rui Ying County, Thai Binh Province, shot down one American A-6 type aircraft. On March 19, seven white-haired veteran militiamen from the same province's Thuong Yen County also shot down one American aggressor aircraft with eighty machine gun bullets.

### 河内军民击落美国无人驾驶侦察机一架  海防等地军民击落美机三架

1968-03-29
第6版()
专栏：

    河内军民击落美国无人驾驶侦察机一架
    海防等地军民击落美机三架
    新华社河内二十八日电　据这里公布，越南北方军民连日来又击落美国强盗飞机四架。
    河内市防空部队在三月二十七日击落美国无人驾驶侦察机一架。这是越南北方军民在这个月来击落的第十架美国无人驾驶侦察机。
    海防防空部队二十五日击落入侵的美国A—6型飞机一架。
    太平省瑞英县军民二十二日夜间击落美国A—6型飞机一架。这个省其英县的七名白发老民兵，十九日也以八十发机关枪子弹击落美国强盗飞机一架。
