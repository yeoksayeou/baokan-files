Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Praises Haiphong Military and Civilians for Shooting Down 200 US Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-03-18
Edition: 5

Chairman Ho Chi Minh praises Haiphong military and civilians for shooting down 200 US aircraft, a glorious achievement. Stay vigilant and fight resolutely to defeat the US aggressors. North Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down 2,800 US aircraft.

	NEW DELHI, March 17 (Xinhua) -- According to reports from local newspapers, Chairman Ho Chi Minh, the beloved leader of the Vietnamese people, wrote a letter on March 15 to the military and civilians of Haiphong, praising their glorious achievement of shooting down 200 US aggressor aircraft.

	In his letter, Chairman Ho said that by March 6, 1968, the military and civilians of Haiphong had shot down 200 US aircraft, contributing to the total of nearly 2,800 US aircraft shot down in North Vietnam. This is an extremely glorious feat.

	The military and civilians of Haiphong are closely united, producing well, fighting bravely, and maintaining good traffic, transportation, public order, and air defense work.

	Chairman Ho, on behalf of the Party Central Committee and the government, warmly praises the military and civilians of Haiphong.

	Chairman Ho said in his letter that the US aggressors have suffered a great defeat, but they are still stubbornly refusing to abandon their brutal and cunning plots. The Vietnamese military and civilians, who are achieving great victories, must fight more resolutely until final victory.

	Chairman Ho finally encouraged the military and civilians of Haiphong to strive to compete with the heroic military and civilians of the South, to stay vigilant, to produce better, to fight better, and to stand shoulder to shoulder with all compatriots to defeat the US aggressors.

	NEW DELHI, March 17 (Xinhua) -- According to reports from here, the military and civilians of Hanoi shot down one US jet aircraft on March 17 and captured two live US air pirates. This is the 2,800th US aggressor aircraft shot down in North Vietnam.

	From March 13 to 16, the military and civilians of Hanoi, Haiphong, and the provinces of Thai Binh and Nghe An shot down seven US aggressor aircraft. Among them, the military and civilians of Hanoi and Haiphong each shot down one US unmanned reconnaissance aircraft on March 13.

### 胡志明主席表扬海防军民击落美机二百架的显赫战功　提高警惕坚决战斗打败美国侵略强盗　越南北方军民击落美机已达二千八百架

1968-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席表扬海防军民击落美机二百架的显赫战功
　　提高警惕坚决战斗打败美国侵略强盗
　　越南北方军民击落美机已达二千八百架
    新华社河内十七日电　据此间报纸十六日报道：越南人民敬爱的领袖胡志明主席，十五日写信给海防军民，表扬他们击落美国强盗飞机二百架的辉煌战功。
    胡主席在信中说，到一九六八年三月六日，海防军民已经击落美国飞机二百架，为在北方击落美国飞机近二千八百架的战功作出了贡献。这是无比辉煌的战绩。
    海防军民紧密团结，生产好，勇敢战斗，交通运输、治安秩序和人民防空工作也搞得好。
    胡主席代表党中央和政府，热烈表扬海防军民。
    胡主席在信里说，美国强盗已经大败，但是它仍然顽固地不愿放弃残暴和狡猾的阴谋。正在取得巨大胜利的越南军民，更加要坚决战斗，直到彻底胜利。
    胡主席最后勉励海防军民必须努力同英雄的南方军民展开竞赛，提高警惕，更好地生产，更好地战斗，同全国同胞并肩一起，战胜美国侵略强盗。
    新华社河内十七日电　据这里十七日公布：河内市军民十七日击落美国喷气式飞机一架，并活捉美国飞贼两名。这是越南北方军民击落的第二千八百架美国强盗飞机。
    从十三日到十六日，河内、海防和太平、义安两省军民击落了美国强盗飞机七架。其中河内和海防军民在十三日各击落美国无人驾驶侦察机一架。
