Title: Praising the Great Achievements of the "Fitori" Chromite Mine Over Twenty Years of Construction
Author:
Date: 1968-03-02
Edition: 6

	The People's Assembly Presidium of Albania decided to award the Order of the Red Banner of Labor.

	According to a report by the Albanian News Agency, the workers of the "Fitori" Chromite Mine in Burrel, Albania, held a meeting on February 17 to celebrate the 20th anniversary of the mine's establishment. Over the past twenty years, the workers of this mine have achieved great successes in the construction of the mining area and the development of production.

	Comrade Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, attended the celebration meeting and extended warm congratulations to the workers of this mine. He also announced that the Presidium of the People's Assembly decided to award the Order of the Red Banner of Labor to this mine on the occasion of its 20th anniversary.

### 表彰“菲托里”铬矿建矿二十年来取得的巨大成就  阿人民议会主席团决定授予劳动红旗勋章

1968-03-02
第6版()
专栏：

    表彰“菲托里”铬矿建矿二十年来取得的巨大成就
    阿人民议会主席团决定授予劳动红旗勋章
    新华社地拉那电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚布尔契兹“菲托里”铬矿的职工二月十七日举行集会，庆祝这个矿山建立二十周年。二十年来这个矿山的职工在建设矿区和发展生产方面取得巨大成就。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希同志出席了庆祝集会，向这个矿山的职工表示热烈祝贺。他并宣布，人民议会主席团决定在这个矿山建立二十周年时授予它劳动红旗勋章。
