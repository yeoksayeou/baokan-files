Title: Pakistani People Celebrate National Day with Great Enthusiasm
Author:
Date: 1968-03-26
Edition: 6

Pakistani people across the country enthusiastically celebrated the National Day of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan today.

President Ayub Khan delivered a radio address to the nation from the capital, Rawalpindi. Reflecting on Pakistan's achievements since independence, he said: "Three years ago, the Pakistani people thwarted India's armed invasion and defended their country. The national heroes who sacrificed their lives in this anti-invasion war will forever be remembered in our hearts."

Today, Rawalpindi held a military parade. Army, navy, and air force units, tanks, artillery, and other troops marched past the reviewing stand. Various types of military aircraft flew in formation overhead.

Defense Minister Admiral A. R. Khan condemned India's persistent aggression in Kashmir during the parade. He said, "The people of Kashmir have been deprived of their rightful rights. India's military budget was around 3 billion rupees three years ago, and now it has reached 101.5 billion rupees. The world has seen and recognized the consequences of this warlike preparation. He said, "We must be prepared at all times for our defense and security in every way."

China's Chargé d'Affaires in Pakistan, Xu Ying, and other diplomatic envoys from various countries attended the military parade ceremony.

In Dhaka, Lahore, Karachi, and other cities, military parades and other celebratory activities were also held.

### 巴基斯坦人民热烈庆祝国庆日

1968-03-26
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦人民热烈庆祝国庆日
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十三日电　巴基斯坦全国各地今天热烈庆祝巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国国庆日。
    阿尤布总统在首都拉瓦尔品第向全国发表了广播演说。他在回顾巴基斯坦独立以来的成就时说：三年以前，巴基斯坦人民粉碎了印度的武装侵略，捍卫了自己的国家。在这次反侵略战争中献出了生命的民族英雄们，将永远铭记在大家的心里。
    今天，拉瓦尔品第举行了军事检阅。陆海空军部队、坦克、炮兵以及其他兵种的部队，列队走过检阅台，各种类型的军用飞机成队地在天空飞过。
    国防部长阿·拉·汗海军中将在检阅部队时发表讲话，谴责印度坚持侵略克什米尔。他说：“克什米尔人被剥夺了自己的正当权利。印度的军事预算三年前是三十亿卢比左右，而现在达到一百零一亿五千万卢比。全世界已经看到和认清了这种好战准备的后果。他说：“我们必须在一切方面为我们的防务和安全随时作好准备。”
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英和其他一些国家的外交使节出席了军事检阅仪式。
    在达卡、拉合尔、卡拉奇和其他城市，也举行军事检阅和其他庆祝活动。
