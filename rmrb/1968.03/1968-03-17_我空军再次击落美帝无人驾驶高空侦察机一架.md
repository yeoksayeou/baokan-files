Title: The PLA Air Force Shoots Down Another U.S. Imperialist High-Altitude Unmanned Reconnaissance Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-03-17
Edition: 1

In the midst of the nationwide military and civilian struggle to achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution,

The PLA Air Force Shoots Down Another U.S. Imperialist High-Altitude Unmanned Reconnaissance Aircraft

The Central Military Commission issues a commendation order, praising the unit that has achieved merit: This is the result of your response to the call of Comrade Lin Biao, holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, emphasizing proletarian politics, and carrying out the ideological transformation of the people. It is a new victory achieved by your unlimited loyalty to the great leader Chairman Mao.

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th: In the midst of the nationwide struggle of hundreds of millions of military and civilian personnel to achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the heroic People's Liberation Army Air Force, which is unconditionally loyal to the great leader Chairman Mao, yesterday afternoon shot down a U.S. imperialist military unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft that had intruded into our airspace for reconnaissance and provocation in the skies over a certain location in the southwest. This is the third U.S. imperialist military unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft that the People's Liberation Army, armed with the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, has shot down in the skies over the southwest region this year.

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th: The Central Military Commission issued a commendation order, praising the People's Liberation Army Air Force unit that shot down a U.S. imperialist military unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft in the skies over a certain location in the southwest yesterday.

The commendation order states: In the midst of the nationwide military and civilian efforts to study and apply the brilliant "March 7th" directive of the great leader Chairman Mao, and to achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, you have been loyal to the great leader Chairman Mao with a single-minded devotion, emphasized the application of the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, bravely defended our socialist motherland, and shot down a U.S. imperialist military unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft that had intruded into the skies over a certain location in the southwest for reconnaissance and provocation. This is the result of your response to the call of Comrade Lin Biao, holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, emphasizing proletarian politics, and carrying out the ideological transformation of the people. It is a new victory achieved by your loyalty to the great leader Chairman Mao, loyalty to the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, and loyalty to Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line. You are hereby commended.

The commendation order states: The great leader Chairman Mao has long pointed out: "To create disorder, to fail, to create disorder again, to fail again, until they are wiped out—this is the logic of imperialism and all reactionaries in the world in dealing with the people's cause. They will not deviate from this logic." It is hoped that you will follow the great leader Chairman Mao's teachings on "not relaxing your fighting efforts in the slightest," fully implement the great leader Chairman Mao's series of latest directives and Comrade Lin Biao's directives, and continue to achieve new merits for the people in defending national defense and the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

### 我空军再次击落美帝无人驾驶高空侦察机一架

1968-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　在全国军民夺取无产阶级文化大革命全面胜利的进军中
　　我空军再次击落美帝无人驾驶高空侦察机一架
　　中央军委颁发嘉奖令，表扬立功的部队：这是你们响应林副主席的号召，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，突出无产阶级政治，搞好人的思想革命化的结果，是你们无限忠于伟大领袖毛主席取得的新胜利
    新华社十六日讯　在全国亿万军民夺取无产阶级文化大革命全面胜利的进军中，无限忠于伟大领袖毛主席的英雄的中国人民解放军空军部队，昨天下午，在西南某地上空，击落侵入我国领空进行侦察挑衅的美帝国主义军用无人驾驶高空侦察机一架。这是用战无不胜的毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民解放军，今年以来，在西南地区上空击落的第三架美帝国主义军用无人驾驶高空侦察机。
    新华社十六日讯　中央军委颁发嘉奖令，表扬昨天在西南某地上空一举击落美帝国主义军用无人驾驶高空侦察机一架的中国人民解放军空军部队。
    嘉奖令说，在全国军民大学大用伟大领袖毛主席光辉的“三·七”指示，乘胜夺取无产阶级文化大革命全面胜利的一片大好形势下，你们对伟大领袖毛主席心怀一个忠字，对战无不胜的毛泽东思想突出一个用字，英勇地保卫我社会主义祖国，一举击落侵入我西南某地上空进行侦察挑衅的美帝国主义军用无人驾驶高空侦察机一架。这是你们响应林副主席的号召，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，突出无产阶级政治，做好人的思想革命化的结果，是你们忠于伟大领袖毛主席、忠于战无不胜的毛泽东思想、忠于毛主席的无产阶级革命路线取得的新胜利，特予通令嘉奖。
    嘉奖令说，伟大领袖毛主席早就指出：“捣乱，失败，再捣乱，再失败，直至灭亡——这就是帝国主义和世界上一切反动派对待人民事业的逻辑，他们决不会违背这个逻辑的。”希望你们遵照伟大领袖毛主席关于“丝毫也不应当松懈你们的战斗努力”的伟大教导，全面落实伟大领袖毛主席的一系列最新指示和林副主席的指示，继续在保卫国防和保卫无产阶级文化大革命运动中，为人民立新功。
