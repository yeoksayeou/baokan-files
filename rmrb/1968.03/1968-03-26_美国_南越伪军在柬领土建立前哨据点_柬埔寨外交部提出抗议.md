Title: U.S.-Saigon Puppet Forces Establish Forward Outposts in Cambodian Territory
Author:
Date: 1968-03-26
Edition: 6

U.S.-Saigon Puppet Forces Establish Forward Outposts in Cambodian Territory
Cambodian Foreign Ministry Protests

	BEIJING, March 26 (Xinhua) — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency, the Royal Government of Cambodia recently sent a note to the Saigon puppet regime, protesting against the aggressive actions of U.S.-Saigon puppet forces in establishing forward outposts in the territory of Kampong Cham Province, Kong Pong Loeu District, Cambodia.

	The note stated that the Royal Government of Cambodia demands that the Saigon puppet regime immediately cease such aggressive actions and dismantle all fortifications illegally built on Cambodian territory.

	BEIJING, March 26 (Xinhua) — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency, the Cambodian Foreign Ministry sent a note to the U.S. government on March 18, protesting against the savage crimes committed by three U.S.-Saigon puppet aircraft that invaded Cambodian airspace on March 9 and strafed and fired rockets at the village of Talo Be in Kampong Cham Province, killing and injuring two Cambodian people. The note stated that the Royal Government of Cambodia demands that the U.S. government cease such aggressive actions.

	The Cambodian Foreign Ministry also handed over a note of the same content to the Saigon puppet regime.

### 美国—南越伪军在柬领土建立前哨据点  柬埔寨外交部提出抗议

1968-03-26
第6版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军在柬领土建立前哨据点
    柬埔寨外交部提出抗议
    新华社金边电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道，不久前柬埔寨王国政府外交部照会南越西贡傀儡政权，抗议美国—南越伪军在柬埔寨柴桢省孔蓬劳县境内建立前哨据点的侵略行动。
    照会说，柬埔寨王国政府要求西贡傀儡当局立即停止这种侵略行径，拆除非法修建在柬埔寨领土上的一切工事。
    新华社金边电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨外交部三月十八日照会美国政府，抗议美国—南越伪军的三架飞机九日入侵柬埔寨领空，并在柴桢省塔洛贝村上空进行扫射和发射火箭，杀伤两名柬埔寨人的野蛮罪行。照会说，柬埔寨王国政府要求美国政府停止这种侵略行动。
    柬埔寨外交部还把一份内容相同的抗议照会交给了南越西贡傀儡政权。
