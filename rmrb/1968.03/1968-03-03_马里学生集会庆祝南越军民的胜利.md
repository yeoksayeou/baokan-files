Title: Malian Students Hold Rally to Celebrate Victories of South Vietnamese Military and Civilians
Author:
Date: 1968-03-03
Edition: 6

More than a thousand Malian students held a rally on February 29 at the Higher Normal School in Bamako to celebrate the recent brilliant victories of the military and civilians of South Vietnam and to support the Vietnamese people's just struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation. At the rally, Moussa Keita, the Malian Minister in charge of the High Commission for Youth and Sports, delivered a speech.

During the rally, Malian youth repeatedly shouted slogans such as: "Victory to the Vietnamese people!" "U.S. murderers, get out of Vietnam!" "Hang Johnson!" "Glory to the Vietnamese people!" The rally adopted a resolution supporting and backing the Vietnamese people. The resolution stated that defeating U.S. imperialism is a common goal of all revolutionary peoples.

### 马里学生集会庆祝南越军民的胜利

1968-03-03
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    马里学生集会庆祝南越军民的胜利
      一千多名马里学生二月二十九日在巴马科高等师范学校举行集会，庆祝越南南方军民最近取得的辉煌胜利，支持越南人民抗美救国的正义斗争。会上，马里负责青年和体育高级专员署部长穆萨·凯塔发表了讲话。
    会上，马里青年不断高呼：“越南人民必胜！”“美国杀人犯从越南滚出去！”“绞死约翰逊！”“光荣属于越南人民！”大会通过了一项支持和声援越南人民的决议。决议指出，打败美帝国主义，是所有革命人民的共同目标。
