Title: China and North Korea Sign Agreement for Mutual Supply of Goods in 1968
Author:
Date: 1968-03-06
Edition: 4

The Chinese government trade delegation and the North Korean government trade delegation recently held talks in Pyongyang regarding the mutual supply of goods for the year 1968. Through these talks, both sides signed the "Agreement on the Mutual Supply of Goods for 1968 between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" on March 5.

Representing the Chinese government to sign the agreement was Jia Shi, the head of the Chinese government trade delegation and deputy minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade. Representing the North Korean government to sign the agreement was Ryu Seong-ui, the head of the North Korean government trade delegation and deputy minister of the Ministry of Trade.

### 中朝签订今年相互供应货物议定书

1968-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    中朝签订今年相互供应货物议定书
    新华社平壤五日电　中国政府贸易代表团和朝鲜政府贸易代表团最近在这里就一九六八年度相互供应货物问题举行了会谈。通过会谈，双方在五日签订了“中华人民共和国政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府关于一九六八年度相互供应货物的议定书”。
    代表中国政府签字的，是中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长贾石。代表朝鲜政府签字的，是朝鲜政府贸易代表团团长、贸易省副相柳成义。
