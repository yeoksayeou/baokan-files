Title: Jiangxi Paper Mill Revolutionary Faction Seriously Implements Chairman Mao's Policy on Cadres
Author:
Date: 1968-03-01
Edition: 3

Jiangxi Paper Mill's revolutionary faction is seriously implementing Chairman Mao's policy on cadres, boldly liberating and utilizing cadres. This is the revolutionary committee chairman, Li Yongxin, who has joined the three-in-one leading group of the mill, and the original party committee secretary. He is currently studying the struggle, criticism, and transformation of the mill together with the revolutionary faction.
