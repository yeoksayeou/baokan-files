Title: Military and Civilians in the Southwest Region of South Vietnam Launch Continuous Attacks to Liberate Extensive Rural Areas
Author:
Date: 1968-03-13
Edition: 6

The military and civilians in the southwest region of South Vietnam have continuously launched attacks to liberate extensive rural areas. They have fought for a month, annihilating and disintegrating over 43,000 enemy troops.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 12 - According to the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, from January 31 to February 29, the military and civilians in the southwest region of South Vietnam launched continuous attacks on various towns within the region. They annihilated and disintegrated over 43,000 enemy troops and liberated 500,000 people.

Statistics show that during this period, the military and civilians in the southwest region annihilated over 13,000 enemy troops, including more than 2,000 American invaders. They completely annihilated eight enemy battalions and twenty companies, including two American companies. They severely damaged five enemy regiments and disintegrated over 30,000 enemy troops and puppet government personnel.

During this period, the entire region's military and civilians also shot down and destroyed over 300 enemy aircraft, destroyed 219 various military vehicles, sank 60 ships, destroyed 20 cannons, captured or forced the enemy to withdraw from 290 outposts and sentry posts, and completely liberated over 200 villages and towns with a population of 500,000.

The report states that in these 30 days, the military and civilians in the southwest region annihilated enemy troops equivalent to the average number of enemy troops annihilated in three months in 1967. The number of enemy battalions annihilated is equivalent to the entire year of 1967. The number of disintegrated enemy troops is more than double that of the entire year of 1967. The number of captured enemy outposts and sentry posts is equivalent to double the entire year of 1967.

### 南越南部西区军民连续发动进攻解放广大乡村　奋战一月歼灭和瓦解敌军四万三千多

1968-03-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　南越南部西区军民连续发动进攻解放广大乡村
　　奋战一月歼灭和瓦解敌军四万三千多
    新华社河内十二日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方南部西区军民在一月三十一日到二月二十九日期间，向这个地区内的各个城镇发动连续的进攻，歼灭和瓦解敌人四万三千多名，解放了五十万人民。
    据统计，南部西区军民在这期间共歼灭敌军一万三千多名，其中包括美国侵略军二千多名；全歼敌军八个营和二十个连，其中包括美军两个连；重创伪军五个团；瓦解伪军和伪政权人员三万多名。
    全区军民在这期间还击落击毁敌机三百多架，击毁各种军车二百一十九辆，击沉船艇六十艘，击毁大炮二十门，攻克或迫使敌人撤出二百九十多个据点和哨所，完全解放了有五十万人的二百多个乡和村。
    报道说，南部西区军民在这三十天中消灭敌人的数字相当于一九六七年平均三个月的歼敌总数，歼灭敌军的营数相当于一九六七年全年的数字，瓦解的敌人比一九六七年全年多一倍多，攻克的敌军据点、哨所相当于一九六七年全年的两倍。（附图片）
