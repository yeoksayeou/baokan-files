Title: Promote the Establishment and Consolidation of Revolutionary Committees at All Levels
Author:
Date: 1968-03-20
Edition: 3

We must vigorously promote the establishment and consolidation of revolutionary committees at all levels, uphold their revolutionary authority, assist in their improvement and development, and ensure that they play a powerful role in leading the broad revolutionary masses to achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.
