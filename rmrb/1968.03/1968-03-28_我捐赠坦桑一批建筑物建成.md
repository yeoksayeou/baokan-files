Title: A Batch of Buildings Donated by China to Tanzania Completed
Author:
Date: 1968-03-28
Edition: 5

According to Xinhua News Agency in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, Dar es Salaam University College held an opening ceremony for its bookstore, bank, and post office on the 19th. These buildings were constructed with funds donated by Premier Zhou Enlai during his visit to the college in 1965.

The acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Tanzania, Zhou Boping, attended the ceremony, which was presided over by the college principal, Chagula.

On the 20th, Zhou Boping, the acting charge d'affaires, handed over a large batch of scientific instruments, teaching models, and specimens donated by Peking University to the college during another ceremony held at the college.

### 我捐赠坦桑一批建筑物建成

1968-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    我捐赠坦桑一批建筑物建成
    据新华社达累斯萨拉姆电　坦桑尼亚达累斯萨拉姆大学学院十九日举行学院书店、银行和邮局的开幕仪式。这些书店、银行和邮局的建筑物是用周恩来总理在一九六五年参观学院时捐赠资金建设的。
    中国驻坦桑尼亚大使馆临时代办周伯萍出席了这个由达累斯萨拉姆大学学院院长查古拉主持的开幕仪式。
    二十日，周伯萍临时代办在学院举行的另一个仪式上，把北京大学赠送的大批科学仪器、教学模型和标本转交给学院。
