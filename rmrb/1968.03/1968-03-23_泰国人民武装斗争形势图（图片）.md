Title: Map of the Armed Struggle of the Thai People
Author:
Date: 1968-03-23
Edition: 5

The image is a map illustrating the armed struggle of the Thai people. It provides a visual representation of the areas where the Thai people are actively engaged in armed resistance against oppressive forces. The map highlights key regions and significant events related to the ongoing struggle for liberation and social justice in Thailand. This visual aid is intended to inform and inspire those involved in the revolutionary movement, as well as to raise awareness among the broader international community about the conditions and challenges faced by the Thai people in their fight for freedom and equality.
