Title: U.S. Monopoly Capital Intensifies Plunder of Latin American Resources
Author:
Date: 1968-03-27
Edition: 5

The U.S. monopoly capital group, Texaco Oil Company, recently discovered an oil field in the Putumayo region of Colombia. To exploit this region's oil, the company brazenly seized a four-million-acre plot of land in Putumayo. Texaco, along with another U.S.-owned Gulf Oil Company, spent enormous sums of money to jointly engage in intense oil extraction activities in the Putumayo oil region.

These two U.S. oil companies also seized a vast area of five million acres in eastern Ecuador, where they are exploring and plundering Ecuador's oil resources.

### 美国垄断资本加紧掠夺拉美资源

1968-03-27
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    美国垄断资本加紧掠夺拉美资源
    美国垄断资本集团美资德士古石油公司，不久以前在哥伦比亚的普图马约地区发现油田。为了开采这个地区的石油，这个公司公然侵占了普图马约一块四百万英亩的土地。德士古公司还同另一家美资海湾石油公司，不惜耗费巨资，合伙在普图马约石油地区进行紧张的开采活动。
    这两家美国石油公司还在厄瓜多尔东部侵占了一块广达五百万英亩的土地，在那里勘探、掠夺厄瓜多尔的石油资源。
