Title: Head of the Czechoslovakian Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-03-15
Edition: 5

The head of the Czechoslovakian government trade delegation, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade M. Honuseck, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 13th to discuss 1968 trade with our country.

### 捷政府贸易代表团团长到京

1968-03-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　捷政府贸易代表团团长到京
    新华社十四日讯　前来我国商谈一九六八年贸易的捷克斯洛伐克政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长米·霍努塞克，十三日下午乘飞机到达北京。
