Title: Chairman Mao's Telegram to Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho
Author:
Date: 1968-03-19
Edition: 4

We firmly support you. Our two countries are neighboring states that depend on each other like lips and teeth. Our two peoples are brothers who share weal and woe.
