Title: Japanese Organizations and Prominent Figures Send Condolences on the Passing of Comrade Xu Guangping
Author:
Date: 1968-03-10
Edition: 6

Various relevant organizations in China and the family of Comrade Xu Guangping, a member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, have received telegrams from the headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox), the Women's Committee of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox), the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, the Afro-Asian Writers' Association of Japan, the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade, the Institute of Chinese Studies in Japan, the Sansei Bookstore, and some prominent social figures, writers, women's leaders, and visitors to China from Japan. These telegrams express condolences on the passing of Comrade Xu Guangping.

### 日团体和知名人士电唁许广平同志逝世

1968-03-10
第6版()
专栏：

    日团体和知名人士电唁许广平同志逝世
    新华社八日讯　我国各有关团体和全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员许广平同志的家属，收到了日本日中友协
  （正统）总部、日中友协（正统）妇女委员会、日中文化交流协会、亚非作家日本委员会、日本国际贸易促进会、日本中国研究所、内山书店以及日本一些社会知名人士、作家、妇女界人士和访华人士的来电，对许广平同志的逝世表示悼念。
