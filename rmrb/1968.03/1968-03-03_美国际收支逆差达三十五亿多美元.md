Title: U.S. International Trade Deficit Reaches Over $350 Million
Author:
Date: 1968-03-03
Edition: 6

The U.S. Department of Commerce recently announced that in 1967, the United States had an international trade deficit of $357.2 million, which is 1.6 times higher than in 1966. The increased foreign exchange expenditures due to the Vietnam War and the devaluation of the British pound's impact on the U.S. dollar's position are the main reasons for the sharp deterioration in the U.S. international trade balance last year.

### 美国际收支逆差达三十五亿多美元

1968-03-03
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    美国际收支逆差达三十五亿多美元
    据美国商务部最近宣布，一九六七年美国国际收支出现了三十五亿七千二百万美元的逆差，比一九六六年要高一点六倍。侵越战争引起的外汇开支增加和英镑贬值对美元地位的猛烈冲击，是造成去年美国国际收支急剧恶化的主要原因。（据新华社讯）
