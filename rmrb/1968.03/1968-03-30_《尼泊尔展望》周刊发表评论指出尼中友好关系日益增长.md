Title: Nepal Outlook Weekly Publishes Commentary Noting Growing Nepal-China Friendship
Author:
Date: 1968-03-30
Edition: 5

	The Nepalese English-language weekly *Nepal Outlook* published a commentary on March 28 stating: “Nepal’s relations with China have always been friendly and harmonious.” The weekly said that the cultural and scientific exchange plan recently signed by Nepal and China “is another vivid example of the growing friendship and mutual understanding between the two countries.”

### 《尼泊尔展望》周刊发表评论指出尼中友好关系日益增长

1968-03-30
第5版()
专栏：

    《尼泊尔展望》周刊发表评论指出尼中友好关系日益增长
    据新华社加德满都二十八日电　尼泊尔英文的《尼泊尔展望》周刊二十八日发表评论说：“尼泊尔同中国的关系一贯是友好和融洽的”。周刊说，尼泊尔和中国最近签订的文化和科学交流计划“是两国日益增长的友好和谅解关系的又一鲜明例证”。
