Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Cables Congratulations on Mauritius Independence
Author:
Date: 1968-03-12
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai sent a cable on March 10 to congratulate Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam, Prime Minister of Mauritius, on the occasion of Mauritius' declaration of independence. The full text of the cable is as follows:

Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam, Prime Minister of Mauritius:

On the occasion of Mauritius' declaration of independence, I, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency, to the Government and people of Mauritius. The Government of China has decided to recognize the Government of Mauritius and hopes that friendly relations between the two countries will be developed.

The Government and people of China have always sympathized with and supported the just struggle of the Mauritian people for national independence. We wish the Mauritian people, after winning independence, new achievements in their struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism, in safeguarding national independence, and in building their own country.

Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
Zhou Enlai

March 10, 1968

### 周恩来总理电贺毛里求斯独立　中国政府决定承认毛里求斯政府

1968-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理电贺毛里求斯独立
　　中国政府决定承认毛里求斯政府
    新华社十一日讯  周恩来总理十日打电报给毛里求斯总理西乌萨加·拉姆古兰，热烈祝贺毛里求斯宣布独立。贺电全文如下：毛里求斯总理西乌萨加·拉姆古兰阁下：
    值此毛里求斯宣布独立之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民，向阁下，向毛里求斯政府和人民，致以热烈的祝贺。中国政府决定承认毛里求斯政府，并且希望两国友好关系将得到发展。
    中国政府和中国人民一贯同情和支持毛里求斯人民争取民族独立的正义斗争。我们祝愿，毛里求斯人民在赢得独立之后，在反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义、维护民族独立和建设自己的国家的事业中，取得新的成就。
    中华人民共和国
    国务院总理
          周恩来
    一九六八年三月十日
