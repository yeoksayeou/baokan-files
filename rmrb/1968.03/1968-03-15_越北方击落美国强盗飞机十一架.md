Title: North Vietnam Shoots Down Eleven American Aggressor Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-03-15
Edition: 5

	The brave military and civilian people of various regions in North Vietnam have achieved new victories in their battles against American air aggressors in recent days. According to reports from here, between March 4 and March 12, the military and civilian people of Haiphong City and Hai Hung Province, Thai Binh Province, Quang Binh Province, and Nghe An Province jointly shot down nine American aggressor aircraft. Among them, two American F-4 Phantom aircraft were shot down by the militia of Quang Binh Province, Quang Trach District.

	According to reports from here, on March 8, the military and civilian people of Quang Binh Province shot down one invading American jet aircraft. On March 7, the military and civilian people of Thai Binh Province also shot down one American jet aircraft.

### 越北方击落美国强盗飞机十一架

1968-03-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　越北方击落美国强盗飞机十一架
    新华社河内十三日电  英勇的越南北方各地军民连日来在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，不断取得新战果。据这里公布，在三月四日到十二日期间，海防市和海兴省、和平省、广平省、义安省军民共击落美国强盗飞机九架，其中有两架美国F—4型鬼怪式飞机是被广平省广泽县的民兵击落的。
    新华社河内十日电  据这里报道，越南北方河静省军民，八日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，击落入侵美国喷气式飞机一架。七日，太平省军民也击落美国喷气式飞机一架。
