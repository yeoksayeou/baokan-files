Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Representatives from the Japan-China Memorandum Trade Office
Author:
Date: 1968-03-07
Edition: 5

Premier Zhou Enlai received representatives from the Japan-China Memorandum Trade Office, including Koi Furusato, Katsuhiro Okazaki, and Seiichi Tagawa, and had a friendly conversation with them this evening.

Present at the reception were Liu Xiheng, Wang Xiaoyun, Sun Pinghua, and others. (Photo attached)

On March 6, Premier Zhou Enlai received representatives from the Japan-China Memorandum Trade Office, including Koi Furusato, Katsuhiro Okazaki, and Seiichi Tagawa. (Photo by our reporter)

### 周恩来总理接见日本日中备忘录贸易办事处代表

1968-03-07
第5版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见日本日中备忘录贸易办事处代表
    新华社六日讯　国务院总理周恩来，今晚接见了日本日中备忘录贸易办事处代表古井喜实、冈崎嘉平太、田川诚一等，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有刘希文、王晓云、孙平化等。（附图片）
