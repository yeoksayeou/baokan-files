Title: The Red Sun Shines Over the World, Red Hearts Turn Toward the Sun
Author:
Date: 1968-03-06
Edition: 6

The red sun shines over the world, and red hearts turn toward the sun. The people of Laos, with hearts aflame, look to the wise helmsman Chairman Mao! They enthusiastically praise Chairman Mao's works as a "treasure trove of truth." The image shows soldiers of the Lao People's Liberation Army diligently studying Chairman Mao's works. Xinhua News Agency.
