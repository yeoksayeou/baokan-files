Title: Further Advocating Frugality and Revolution
Author:
Date: 1968-03-20
Edition: 3

The revolutionary workers of the sheet metal group at the Shanghai Dongfeng Air Conditioner Factory are studying the great leader Chairman Mao's great directive to "further advocate frugality and revolution." They are grasping the revolution and vigorously promoting production.
