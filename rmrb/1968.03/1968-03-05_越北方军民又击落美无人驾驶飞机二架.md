Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two More U.S. Unmanned Reconnaissance Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-03-05
Edition: 6

According to a report released here, on March 3, the military and civilians of Hanoi and Haiphong in North Vietnam shot down two U.S. aggressor unmanned reconnaissance aircraft. On March 4, the military and civilians of Hanoi shot down one more U.S. jet aircraft.

### 越北方军民又击落美无人驾驶飞机二架

1968-03-05
第6版()
专栏：

    越北方军民又击落美无人驾驶飞机二架
    新华社河内四日电　据这里公布，越南北方河内市和海防市军民三日击落美国强盗的无人驾驶侦察飞机两架，四日河内市军民又击落美国喷气式飞机一架。
