Title: Chairman Mao and His Close Comrade-in-Arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao Receive Over Ten Thousand Revolutionary Soldiers from Six Arms and Two Academies
Author:
Date: 1968-03-09
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin Biao, received over ten thousand revolutionary soldiers from six arms and two academies. Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, and Yao Wenyuan participated in the reception.

Chairman Mao is in excellent health, radiant, and full of vigor. He and Vice Chairman Lin Biao walked to the center of the stage and shook hands warmly with the responsible comrades from the various headquarters, military branches, arms, academies, and Beijing troops of the People's Liberation Army. They then led everyone to the front of the stage and applauded for a long time to the representatives attending the conference of active learners of Chairman Mao's works from the Artillery, Armored, Engineering, Railway, Communications, Chemical Defense, Military Academy, and Political Academy, as well as those in Beijing attending professional conferences.

Revolutionary soldiers from all over the country, facing the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time, Chairman Mao, were extremely excited and shouted enthusiastically: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Ten thousand years to Chairman Mao!" "Wish Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life!"

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: Our great teacher, great leader, great commander, and great helmsman, Chairman Mao, and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin Biao, along with Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, and Yao Wenyuan, received on the 7th the representatives attending the conference of active learners of Chairman Mao's works from the Artillery, Armored, Engineering, Railway, Communications, Chemical Defense, Military Academy, and Political Academy of the People's Liberation Army, as well as those in Beijing attending professional conferences.

The East is red, the sun is rising. Our most beloved great leader, Chairman Mao, is in excellent health, radiant, and full of vigor. He, along with Vice Chairman Lin Biao and other central leaders, came to the Great Hall of the People with great joy to visit over ten thousand revolutionary soldiers from all over the country.

Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Biao took firm steps onto the stage. Immediately, the hall erupted in cheers. Revolutionary soldiers, facing Chairman Mao, were extremely excited and shouted: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Ten thousand years to Chairman Mao!" Chairman Mao, with a broad smile, walked from one end of the stage to the other, waving and greeting the revolutionary comrades in the hall.

Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Biao then walked to the center of the stage and shook hands warmly with the responsible comrades from the various headquarters, military branches, arms, academies, and Beijing troops of the People's Liberation Army. They then led everyone to the front of the stage and applauded for a long time to the revolutionary soldiers in the hall.

Chairman Mao, oh Chairman Mao! You are the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time, the red sun in our hearts. You are heart to heart with the revolutionary soldiers. You have led us to victory after victory on the revolutionary journey and are now guiding us to achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The revolutionary soldiers' hearts are with the Party, and every red heart is with the sun. At this most happy moment, everyone, facing Chairman Mao, waved the red revolutionary treasure book "Quotations from Chairman Mao," and shouted enthusiastically: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Ten thousand years to Chairman Mao!" "Long live Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line!" "Wish Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life!"

The reception ended, but the revolutionary soldiers' excitement could not subside for a long time. Many people, with tears of joy, opened "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and wrote down this most unforgettable moment of seeing the great leader Chairman Mao; many people sang "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman" and "Wishing Chairman Mao Eternal Life" over and over again. The last batch of revolutionary soldiers leaving the Great Hall of the People, with the utmost respect, facing the portrait of Chairman Mao, holding the red revolutionary treasure book, solemnly swore:

"We will forever take Vice Chairman Lin as a shining example, be infinitely loyal to Chairman Mao, be infinitely loyal to Mao Zedong Thought, and be infinitely loyal to Chairman Mao's revolutionary line!

"We will forever be infinitely loving, believing, admiring, and loyal to Chairman Mao!

"We will resolutely follow Vice Chairman Lin's instructions, read Chairman Mao's books, listen to Chairman Mao's words, act according to Chairman Mao's instructions, and be good soldiers of Chairman Mao!"

Those who participated in the reception also included Chen Yi, Liu Bocheng, Li Xiannian, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Xie Fuzhi, Yang Chengwu, Su Yu, Wu Faxian, Wang Dongxing, Ye Qun, Yu Qili, Qiu Huizuo, Zhang Xiuquan, Li Tianhuan, Li Tianyou, Wang Xinting, Wen Yucheng, Zhang Chuming, Luo Xunzhi, Wang Shusheng, Lu Yang, Xiao Li, Xiao Jinguang, Li Zuopeng, Wang Hongkun, Yu Lijin, Chen Renqian, Huang Zhiyong, Chen Shizhong, Tan Furen, Luo Huasheng, Jiang Wen, Huang Wenming, Li Zhen, Liu Zhong, Song Weizhi, Chen Xianrui, Fu Chongbi, Huang Zuozhen, and others. ### 毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席接见六兵种两学院一万多革命战士

1968-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

     毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席接见六兵种两学院一万多革命战士
    周恩来陈伯达康生李富春江青姚文元等同志参加接见
    毛主席身体非常健康，神采奕奕，容光焕发，和林副主席走到主席台正中，同参加接见的解放军各总部、各军种、兵种、院校和北京部队的负责同志，一一亲切握手，并率领大家走向主席台前沿，向出席炮兵、装甲兵、工程兵、铁道兵、通信兵、防化学兵、高等军事学院、政治学院学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会的代表，以及在京出席专业会议的代表，长时间地鼓掌。来自祖国四方的革命战士，迎着当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者毛主席，无比激动地纵情高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”“祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！”
    新华社八日讯　我们的伟大导师、伟大领袖、伟大统帅、伟大舵手毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，以及周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青、姚文元等同志，七日接见了出席中国人民解放军炮兵、装甲兵、工程兵、铁道兵、通信兵、防化学兵、高等军事学院、政治学院学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会的代表，以及在北京出席专业会议的代表。
    东方红，太阳升。我们最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席，身体非常健康，神采奕奕，容光焕发，同林彪副主席以及中央其他负责同志，十分高兴地来到人民大会堂，亲切看望来自祖国四方的一万多革命战士。
    毛主席和林彪副主席，健步登上主席台。顿时，全场欢声雷动。革命战士们迎着毛主席，无比激动地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”毛主席满面笑容，从主席台的这一端走到另一端，向全场革命同志频频招手致意。
    毛主席和林彪副主席随后走到主席台正中，同参加接见的中国人民解放军各总部、各军种、兵种、院校和北京部队的负责同志，一一亲切握手，并且率领大家走向主席台前沿，长时间地向全场革命战士鼓掌。
    毛主席啊，毛主席！您是当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者，您是我们心中最红的红太阳。您和革命战士心连心，您率领我们在革命的征途中，取得了一个又一个的辉煌胜利，并正在指引我们夺取无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利。革命战士心向党，颗颗红心向太阳。在这最最幸福的时刻，大家向着毛主席，挥动红彤彤的革命宝书《毛主席语录》，不停地纵情高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”“毛主席的无产阶级革命路线胜利万岁！”“祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！”
    接见结束了，革命战士们激动的心情久久不能平息，多少人含着幸福的泪花，打开《毛主席语录》，写下见到伟大领袖毛主席的这个最最难忘的时刻；多少人一遍又一遍地高唱《大海航行靠舵手》、《祝福毛主席万寿无疆》。最后离开人民大会堂的一批革命战士，怀着无比崇敬的心情，面向毛主席像，举着红色革命宝书，庄严宣誓：
    “我们永远以林副主席为光辉榜样，无限忠于毛主席，无限忠于毛泽东思想，无限忠于毛主席的革命路线！
      “我们对毛主席永远无限热爱，无限信仰，无限崇拜，无限忠诚！
    “我们坚决遵照林副主席的指示，读毛主席的书，听毛主席的话，照毛主席的指示办事，做毛主席的好战士！”
    参加接见的，还有陈毅、刘伯承、李先念、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英、谢富治、杨成武、粟裕、吴法宪、汪东兴、叶群、余秋里、邱会作、张秀川、李天焕、李天佑、王新亭、温玉成、张池明、罗舜初、王树声、路扬、肖力、萧劲光、李作鹏、王宏坤、余立金、陈仁麒、黄志勇、陈士榘、谭甫仁、罗华生、江文、黄文明、李真、刘忠、宋维栻、陈先瑞、傅崇碧、黄作珍等同志。（附图片）
    我们的伟大导师、伟大领袖、伟大统帅、伟大舵手毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，以及周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青、姚文元等同志，三月七日接见了出席中国人民解放军炮兵、装甲兵、工程兵、铁道兵、通信兵、防化学兵、高等军事学院、政治学院学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会的代表，以及在北京出席专业会议的代表。图为毛主席向全场革命战士亲切鼓掌致意。一轮红日东升，万众欢声雷动。革命战士满怀对伟大领袖毛主席无限热爱、无限信仰、无限崇拜、无限忠诚的激情，高举红彤彤的革命宝书《毛主席语录》，一遍遍地高呼：“毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”新华社记者摄
