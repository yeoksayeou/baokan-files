Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1968-03-03
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi today met with guests from Japan who were invited by the China International Travel Service Headquarters to visit our country. The guests included Nifu Ogi, president of the Japan-China Travel Service, and Kenji Kubo, president of the Kansai International Travel Service, along with two others. Chen Yi had a friendly conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本客人

1968-03-03
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本客人
    新华社二日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见应中国国际旅行社总社邀请来我国访问的日本日中旅行社社长菅沼不二男、日本关西国际旅行社社长久保专治等四人，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
