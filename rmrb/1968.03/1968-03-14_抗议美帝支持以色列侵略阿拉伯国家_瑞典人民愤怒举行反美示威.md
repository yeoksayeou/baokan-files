Title: Protesting U.S. Support for Israeli Aggression Against Arab Countries
Author:
Date: 1968-03-14
Edition: 5

Approximately 200 people held a demonstration and march on the 10th in this city to protest against U.S. support for Israeli aggression against Arab countries. The demonstrators held up banners with slogans such as "U.S. — Murderer" and "Down with Israel," condemning the U.S. imperialists and Israeli aggressors. The demonstrators angrily denounced U.S. imperialism for using Israel as its military base in the Middle East to carry out aggression against the Arab people.

### 抗议美帝支持以色列侵略阿拉伯国家  瑞典人民愤怒举行反美示威

1968-03-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　抗议美帝支持以色列侵略阿拉伯国家
    瑞典人民愤怒举行反美示威
    新华社斯德哥尔摩十二日电　约二百人十日在这里举行示威游行，抗议美国支持以色列侵略阿拉伯国家。示威者高举着标语牌，上面写着：“美国——杀人犯”，“打倒以色列”等谴责美帝和以色列侵略者的口号。示威者愤怒谴责美帝国主义利用以色列作为它在西亚的军事基地，对阿拉伯人民进行侵略。
