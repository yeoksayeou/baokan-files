Title: U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Forces Invade Cambodian Territory Again
Author:
Date: 1968-03-20
Edition: 6

	U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Forces Invade Cambodian Territory Again
	Cambodian Foreign Ministry Lodges Strong Protest

	According to a report from the New China News Agency in Phnom Penh on the 18th, the Cambodian News Agency reported on the 17th that the Cambodian Foreign Ministry recently sent a note to the Saigon puppet regime in South Vietnam, protesting the U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet forces' invasion of Cambodian territory on the 4th.

	The note stated that at around 8 AM on the 4th, six U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet soldiers landed by helicopter in a place about 1,500 meters southeast of the Cambodian outpost in the Long Kong area and attacked the local Cambodian local defense forces.

	The note said that the Royal Government strongly protests the invasion of Cambodian territory by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet forces and firmly demands that the Saigon authorities in South Vietnam stop the aforementioned aggressive actions.

	The Cambodian Foreign Ministry also sent a similar protest note to the U.S. government.

	According to a recent report from the Cambodian News Agency, Thai military forces and Free Khmer elements also carried out three provocations in the provinces of Battambang and Kratie from February 26 to March 7.

### 美国—南越伪军再次入侵柬埔寨领土　柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-03-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国—南越伪军再次入侵柬埔寨领土
　　柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议
    据新华社金边十八日电  据柬埔寨新闻社十七日报道，柬埔寨外交部最近照会南越西贡傀儡当局，抗议美国—南越伪军四日再次入侵柬埔寨领土。
    照会说，四日上午八时左右，六名美国—南越伪军乘直升飞机降落在柬埔寨隆康地区哨所东南一千五百米的地方，并袭击了当地的柬埔寨地方守卫队。
    照会说，王国政府强烈抗议美国—南越伪军对柬埔寨领土的入侵，并坚决要求南越西贡当局停止上述侵略行动。
    柬外交部还给了美国政府一份类似的抗议照会。
    另据柬埔寨新闻社最近报道，泰国军队和自由高棉分子从二月二十六日至三月七日在马德望省和戈公省也进行了三次挑衅。
