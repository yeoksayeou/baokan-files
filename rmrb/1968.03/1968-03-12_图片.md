Title:  Shanghai County Jiang Town Commune Li Xin Brigade Third Production Team Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants Respond to Chairman Mao's Call to "Learn from Dazhai in Agriculture"
Author:
Date: 1968-03-12
Edition: 3

The poor and lower-middle peasants of the Third Production Team of Li Xin Brigade, Jiang Town Commune, Chuan Sha County, Shanghai, resolutely respond to Chairman Mao's great call to "learn from Dazhai in agriculture." They carry forward the revolutionary spirit of self-reliance and hard struggle, level the land, and transform nature, determined to achieve an even greater harvest this year.
