Title: Our Heroic Air Force Maintains High Alert Against Imperialist Aggression
Author:
Date: 1968-03-24
Edition: 1

Our heroic People's Liberation Army Air Force, maintaining high alert against imperialist aggression, struck down another U.S. imperialist unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft on March 22 in the skies over a certain location in South China. This is the fifth time in less than three months that the People's Liberation Army Air Force and Naval Air Force have achieved the great victory of shooting down American aggressor planes. This demonstrates that the People's Liberation Army, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, is an invincible great wall of steel.

Currently, U.S. imperialism, this paper tiger, is being beaten black and blue by the Vietnamese people and military, and is mired in a severe economic crisis, beset by internal and external troubles, and at its wit's end. The great People's Liberation Army maintains high vigilance against U.S. imperialism, which continues to send planes to provoke our country. No matter how frantically U.S. imperialism expands its war of adventure and struggles in its death throes, it cannot escape the fate of failure.

### 我英雄空军对帝国主义的侵略保持高度戒备  再次击落美帝军用无人驾驶高空侦察机一架

1968-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我英雄空军对帝国主义的侵略保持高度戒备
    再次击落美帝军用无人驾驶高空侦察机一架
    新华社二十三日讯　对帝国主义侵略保持高度戒备的我英雄的中国人民解放军空军部队，怀着对伟大统帅毛主席最深厚的阶级感情，坚决执行伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席的指示，于二十二日下午，在华南某地上空，再次击落侵入我国领空进行侦察挑衅的美帝国主义军用无人驾驶高空侦察机一架。这是人民解放军空军部队和海军航空兵部队，在不到三个月的时间里，连续五次获得击落美国强盗飞机的巨大胜利。这些都表明，用毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民解放军是坚如钢铁的伟大长城。当前，美帝国主义这只纸老虎被越南军民打得焦头烂额，正陷入巨大的经济危机，内外交困，走投无路。伟大的中国人民解放军，对一再派飞机对我国进行挑衅的美帝国主义，保持着高度警惕。不论美帝国主义怎样疯狂地扩大战争冒险，进行垂死挣扎，都逃脱不了失败的命运。
