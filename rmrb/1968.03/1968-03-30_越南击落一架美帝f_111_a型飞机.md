Title: Vietnam Shoots Down a U.S. F-111A Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-03-30
Edition: 5

Vietnam Shoots Down a U.S. F-111A Aircraft

	According to reports from here, the armed forces of the North Vietnamese province of Nghe An in the west, on the 28th, shot down an invading U.S. F-111A warplane.

	This is the first U.S. F-111A aircraft to be shot down in North Vietnam.

### 越南击落一架美帝F—111A型飞机

1968-03-30
第5版()
专栏：

    越南击落一架美帝F—111A型飞机
    新华社河内二十九日电　据这里报道：越南北方河静省西部的武装部队，二十八日击落侵入当地上空的F—111A型美国强盗飞机一架。
    这是在越南北方被击落的第一架美国F—111A型飞机。
