Title: Vietnamese Water Resources Survey Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-03-03
Edition: 6

A water resources survey team from Vietnam, led by Team Leader and Director of the Dike Bureau of the Vietnamese Ministry of Water Resources, Yang Yuwu, arrived in Beijing today with a delegation of twelve. Relevant officials from the Ministry of Water Resources and Electric Power were at the station to welcome them.

### 越南水利考察团到京

1968-03-03
第6版()
专栏：

    越南水利考察团到京
    新华社一日讯　由团长、越南水利部堤防局局长杨玉武率领的越南水利考察团一行十二人，今天到达北京。水利电力部有关负责人等到车站欢迎。
