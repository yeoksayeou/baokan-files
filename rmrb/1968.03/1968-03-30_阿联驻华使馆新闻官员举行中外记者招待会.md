Title: News Official of the Egyptian Embassy in China Holds a Press Conference for Chinese and Foreign Journalists
Author:
Date: 1968-03-30
Edition: 5

A news official from the Egyptian Embassy in China held a press conference for Chinese and foreign journalists on March 28. The official elaborated on the Egyptian position regarding the resolution of the Middle East issue. The official pointed out that Israel, as the darling of American imperialism, has carried out a series of aggressive actions with the encouragement of American imperialism. He also condemned Israel's brazen annexation of Arab territories, declaring them as "military occupation zones."

### 阿联驻华使馆新闻官员举行中外记者招待会

1968-03-30
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联驻华使馆新闻官员举行中外记者招待会
    新华社二十九日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国驻中国大使馆新闻官员二十八日举行中外记者招待会，阐述了阿拉伯联合共和国对解决中东问题的立场。这位新闻官员指出，以色列作为美帝国主义的宠儿，在美帝国主义纵容下进行了一系列的侵略活动。他并且谴责了以色列悍然把强占的阿拉伯国家领土，宣布为“军事占领区”。
