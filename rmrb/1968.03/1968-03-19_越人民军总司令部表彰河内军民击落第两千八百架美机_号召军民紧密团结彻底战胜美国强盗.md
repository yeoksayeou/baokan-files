Title: General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army Commends Hanoi Military and Civilians for Shooting Down 2,800 U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-03-19
Edition: 6

The General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army has commended the military and civilians of Hanoi for shooting down the 2,800th U.S. aircraft.

The General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army has called on the military and civilians of Hanoi to unite closely and thoroughly defeat the U.S. aggressors.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, March 18 – According to reports from local newspapers, on March 17, the General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army sent a telegram to the military and civilians of Hanoi, warmly praising their glorious victory in the early morning of March 17, when they shot down the 2,800th U.S. aggressor aircraft during a counterattack against U.S. air pirates.

	The telegram said, inspired by the brilliant achievements of the military and civilians in the South, the military and civilians in the North have already given severe punishment to U.S. imperialism, making all its destructive war plots and means suffer heavy failures. The U.S. aggressors are now in a desperate and unprecedentedly difficult situation.

	The telegram called on the military and civilians of Hanoi to heighten their vigilance, unite closely, and vigorously carry out the competition of doing well in production and fighting well, together with the military and civilians throughout the country to thoroughly defeat the U.S. aggressors.

	According to a report by the New China News Agency, Hanoi, March 18 – The Vietnam “People's Daily” and the “People's Army” both published editorials on March 18, enthusiastically hailing the glorious victory of the military and civilians in North Vietnam in shooting down 2,800 U.S. aggressor aircraft.

	The editorial of the “People's Daily” said, the military and civilians in the North are determined to stand together with the military and civilians in the South, ready at any time to give heavier blows to the U.S. aggressors, and to win more glorious victories.

	The editorial of the “People's Army” called on the military and civilians in the North to, on the basis of the victory of shooting down 2,800 U.S. aircraft, vigorously carry out the competition of doing well in production and fighting well, exerting all efforts to fulfill the duty of the rear to the front, and to give a fatal blow to U.S. imperialism, resolutely winning a thorough victory in the struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation.

### 越人民军总司令部表彰河内军民击落第两千八百架美机　号召军民紧密团结彻底战胜美国强盗

1968-03-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　越人民军总司令部表彰河内军民击落第两千八百架美机
　　号召军民紧密团结彻底战胜美国强盗
    新华社河内十八日电　据这里报纸报道：越南人民军总司令部十七日打电报给河内市军民，热烈表扬他们十七日凌晨在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，击落第二千八百架美国强盗飞机的辉煌胜利。
    电报说，在南方军民的辉煌战功的鼓舞下，北方军民已经给予美帝国主义以严厉的惩罚，使它的一切破坏性战争阴谋和手段都遭到了惨重失败，美国强盗正陷于走投无路和空前窘迫的处境。
    电报号召河内市军民提高警惕，紧密团结，大力开展生产好、战斗好的竞赛，同全国军民一道彻底战胜美国侵略强盗。
    据新华社河内十八日电　越南《人民报》和《人民军队报》十八日分别发表社论，热烈欢呼越南北方军民击落美国强盗飞机二千八百架的辉煌胜利。
    《人民报》的社论说，北方军民坚决同南方军民一起，随时准备给予美国强盗以更沉重的打击，以夺取更加辉煌的胜利。
    《人民军队报》的社论号召北方军民在击落美机二千八百架胜利的基础上，大力开展生产好、战斗好的竞赛，竭尽全力履行大后方对大前线的义务，给美帝国主义以致命的打击，坚决夺取抗美救国斗争的彻底胜利。
