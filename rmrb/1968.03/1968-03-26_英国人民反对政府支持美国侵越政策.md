Title: British People Oppose Government's Support for U.S. Aggression in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-03-26
Edition: 6

Thousands of London residents marched and demonstrated in front of the British Prime Minister Harold Wilson's residence at 10 Downing Street on the 24th to protest the Labour government's support for U.S. aggression in Vietnam.

On the same day in the afternoon, hundreds of women also held a demonstration against the U.S. war in Vietnam. Some women even staged a hunger strike in front of the U.S. Embassy to oppose the U.S. war of aggression in Vietnam.

### 英国人民反对政府支持美国侵越政策

1968-03-26
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    英国人民反对政府支持美国侵越政策
    伦敦市民数千人二十四日在伦敦唐宁街英国首相威尔逊的官邸前游行示威，抗议工党政府支持美国侵略越南的政策。
    同日下午，数百名妇女也举行了反对美国侵越战争的示威。有些妇女还在美国大使馆前举行绝食斗争，反对美国侵略越南的战争。
