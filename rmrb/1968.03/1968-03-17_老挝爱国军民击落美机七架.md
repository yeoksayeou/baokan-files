Title: Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Shoot Down Seven U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-03-17
Edition: 5

Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Shoot Down Seven U.S. Aircraft

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Khamkeut, March 16: According to the Pathet Lao News Agency, on March 11, the patriotic military and civilians of Xieng Nguen Province in Laos shot down a U.S. aggressor aircraft with rifles in the Mangxay area of Xieng Nguen Province.

	It is also reported that on February 23, the patriotic armed forces in the Xaysettha District of Saravane Province shot down five enemy aircraft. On the same day, the patriotic armed forces in Udomsai Province shot down an enemy T-28 type aircraft.

### 老挝爱国军民击落美机七架

1968-03-17
第5版()
专栏：

　　老挝爱国军民击落美机七架
    据新华社康开十六日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝桑怒省爱国军民三月十一日在桑怒省芒咸地区，用步枪击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    另据报道，二月二十三日，沙拉湾省寮岸县爱国武装力量击落敌机五架。同一天，爱国武装力量在乌多姆赛省击落敌人T—28型飞机一架。
