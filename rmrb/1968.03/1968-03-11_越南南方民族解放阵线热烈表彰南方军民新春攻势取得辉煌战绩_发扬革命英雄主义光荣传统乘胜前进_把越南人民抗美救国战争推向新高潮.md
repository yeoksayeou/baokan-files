Title: The National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Warmly Commends the Brilliant Achievements of the South's Military and Civilians in the New Year Offensive
Author:
Date: 1968-03-11
Edition: 5

The National Liberation Front of South Vietnam warmly commends the brilliant achievements of the South's military and civilians in the New Year Offensive
Carrying forward the glorious tradition of revolutionary heroism, advancing with victory
Pushing the Vietnamese people's anti-American, national salvation war to a new high

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 10th: According to the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam issued a decision on March 3rd to warmly commend the brilliant achievements of the South's military and civilians during the New Year period.

	The decision states that during the New Year period, the South's military and civilians responded to the battle call of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front, from the countryside to the cities, from the mountains to the plains, united as one, launched a fierce attack on the American aggressors and their lackeys, achieving unprecedented great victories in the Vietnamese people's anti-American, national salvation cause.

	The decision states that in the New Year battles, the South Vietnamese military and civilians annihilated and disintegrated a part of the enemy's important combat forces, destroyed a large number of the enemy's war tools, and destroyed the ruling institutions of the U.S.-puppet clique in many rural areas, making the enemy fall into a more passive, defensive, and desperate situation. At the same time, the great victory of the South Vietnamese military and civilians in the New Year also shattered the enemy's "pincer strategy," disrupted the enemy's military deployment, liberated vast rural areas, and rapidly developed the political and military strength of the South Vietnamese military and civilians.

	The decision points out that these great victories achieved by the South Vietnamese military and civilians have pushed the anti-American, national salvation war of the entire Vietnamese people to a new high, opening up a very favorable situation for the South Vietnamese military and civilians and an unfavorable one for the enemy, laying a solid foundation for the South Vietnamese military and civilians to advance with victory, launch continuous attacks, and ultimately achieve complete victory. The decision points out that the South Vietnamese military and civilians achieved extremely great and comprehensive victories due to their close unity, fearlessness of sacrifice, and brave fighting.

	The decision finally calls on the South Vietnamese military and civilians to highly carry forward the glorious tradition of revolutionary heroism, turn hatred into strength, advance with victory, launch continuous attacks, bravely fight the enemy, and achieve complete victory in the anti-American, national salvation cause.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 9th: According to the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam recently decided to award the Copper Wall Medal and Liberation Medal to the military and civilians of various provinces and cities who achieved brilliant feats in the world-shaking New Year Offensive.

	The military and civilians of Saigon-Cholon District and Hue City were each awarded the First Class Copper Wall Medal.

	The military and civilians of the seven provinces of Quang Tri, Thua Thien, Quang Nam, My Tho, Binh Duong, Vinh Long, and Tra Vinh, and the four cities of Da Nang, Ban Me Thuot, Da Lat, and Phan Rang-Thap Cham were each awarded the Second Class Copper Wall Medal.

	The military and civilians of the twenty provinces of Quang Ngai, Phu Yen, Binh Dinh, Phu Khanh, Kontum, Gia Lai, Ninh Thuan, Phu Yen, Phuoc Long, Binh Long, Tay Ninh, An Giang, Kien Giang, Soc Trang, Dinh Tuong, Hau Nghia, and Kien Hoa were each awarded the Third Class Copper Wall Medal. ### 越南南方民族解放阵线热烈表彰南方军民新春攻势取得辉煌战绩　发扬革命英雄主义光荣传统乘胜前进　把越南人民抗美救国战争推向新高潮

1968-03-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南南方民族解放阵线热烈表彰南方军民新春攻势取得辉煌战绩
　　发扬革命英雄主义光荣传统乘胜前进
　　把越南人民抗美救国战争推向新高潮
    据新华社河内十日电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会三日作出决定，热烈表彰南方军民在新春期间取得的辉煌战绩。
    决定书说，新春期间，南方军民响应民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团的战斗号召，从农村到城市，从山区到平原，万众一心，向美国侵略强盗及其走狗发动了猛烈的进攻，取得了越南人民抗美救国事业中空前的巨大胜利。
    决定书说，越南南方军民在新春战斗中，消灭和瓦解了敌人一部分重要的有生力量，摧毁了敌人的大量战争工具，摧毁了美伪集团在农村许多地区的统治机构，使敌人进一步陷入被动挨打穷于应付的困境。同时，南越军民的新春大捷还粉碎了敌人的“钳形攻势”战略计划，打乱了敌人的军事部署，解放了广大农村地区，使南方军民的政治力量和武装力量得到了迅速的发展。
    决定书指出，越南南方军民取得的这些巨大的胜利，把全体越南人民的抗美救国战争推向一个新高潮，打开了一个非常有利于南方军民、不利于敌人的新局面，为南方军民乘胜前进，连续进攻，进而夺取彻底的胜利打下了稳固的基础。决定书指出，南方军民取得极其巨大和全面的胜利，是由于南方军民紧密团结，不怕牺牲，勇敢战斗的结果。
    决定书最后号召南方军民高度发扬革命英雄主义的光荣传统，把仇恨化为力量，乘胜前进，连续进攻，勇猛杀敌，夺取抗美救国事业的彻底胜利。
    据新华社河内九日电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团最近决定把铜墙勋章和解放勋章授予在震撼世界的新春攻势中建立辉煌战功的各省市军民。
    西贡—嘉定区军民和顺化市军民分别荣获一级铜墙勋章。
    广治、承天、广南、美萩、槟椥、永隆、茶荣七个省和岘港、邦美蜀、大叻、藩切四个市军民分别荣获二级铜墙勋章。
    广义、平定、庆和、富安、昆嵩、嘉莱、宁顺、福隆、平龙、巴地、土龙木、边和、西宁、隆安、建丰、安江、芹苴、朔庄、迪石和金瓯二十个省军民分别荣获三级铜墙勋章。（附图片）
    越南南方解放军某单位战士坚守战斗岗位。这个单位的战士英勇善战，在一次战斗中就击落三架美国直升飞机，荣获“歼美单位”的光荣称号。
