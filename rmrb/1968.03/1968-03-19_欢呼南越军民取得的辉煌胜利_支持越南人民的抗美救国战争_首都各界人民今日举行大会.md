Title: Celebrate the Brilliant Victories of South Vietnamese Military and Civilians
Support the Vietnamese People's Anti-American National Salvation War
Capital Residents Hold a Rally Today
Author:
Date: 1968-03-19
Edition: 1

	New China News Agency, March 18th: March 19th marks the 18th anniversary of Vietnam's National Anti-American Day. The people of various sectors in the capital will hold a grand rally on the 19th to enthusiastically celebrate the series of great and brilliant victories achieved by the military and civilians of South Vietnam since the New Year. They will resolutely support the Vietnamese people's anti-American national salvation war and pledge to be a strong backing for the Vietnamese people. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will begin broadcasting the live coverage of the rally at 2:55 PM on the 19th using the first program frequency. Beijing Television and Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast simultaneously.

### 欢呼南越军民取得的辉煌胜利　支持越南人民的抗美救国战争　首都各界人民今日举行大会

1968-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　欢呼南越军民取得的辉煌胜利
　　支持越南人民的抗美救国战争
　　首都各界人民今日举行大会
    新华社十八日讯　三月十九日是越南全国反美日十八周年。首都各界人民将在十九日隆重举行集会，热烈欢呼越南南方军民新春以来取得的一系列伟大的辉煌胜利，坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国战争，誓作兄弟的越南人民的坚强后盾。中央人民广播电台将从十九日下午二时五十五分开始，用第一套节目的频率，转播大会的实况。北京电视台、北京人民广播电台同时转播。
