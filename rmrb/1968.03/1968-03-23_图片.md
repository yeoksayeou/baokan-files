Title: The Poor Peasants of Xingguo County's Changgang Commune Are Creating a New "Top-Class" Work
Author:
Date: 1968-03-23
Edition: 1

The poor peasants of Changgang Commune in Xingguo County are using iron-clad facts to create a new "top-class" work. They have thoroughly refuted the reactionary fallacy of "exploitation is meritorious" propagated by the Chinese Khrushchev, leaving him with no defense whatsoever.
