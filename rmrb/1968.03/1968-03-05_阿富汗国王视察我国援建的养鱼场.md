Title: Afghan King Inspects Fishing Farm Built with Chinese Aid
Author:
Date: 1968-03-05
Edition: 5

Afghan King Mohammed Zahir Shah on February 22 inspected the Daren Ta fishing experimental farm built with Chinese aid and held friendly talks with Chinese engineering and technical personnel working there. The King expressed satisfaction and thanks for the work of the Chinese engineering and technical personnel.

The fishing experimental farm was built in accordance with the Sino-Afghan economic and technical cooperation agreement signed in 1965. A fish pond with an area of about three hectares has been completed. The 450,000 fish fry shipped from China last year have grown plump. Other facilities of the fishing experimental farm are still under construction.

### 阿富汗国王视察我国援建的养鱼场

1968-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗国王视察我国援建的养鱼场
    新华社喀布尔电　阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿二月二十二日视察中国帮助兴建的达伦塔养鱼试验场，同在那里工作的中国工程技术人员进行了友好谈话。国王对中国工程技术人员的工作表示满意和感谢。
    这座养鱼试验场是根据一九六五年签订的中阿经济技术合作协定兴建的。一座面积约三公顷的鱼池已经落成。去年从中国运去的四十五万尾鱼苗，生长肥壮。养鱼试验场的其他设施，还在施工。
