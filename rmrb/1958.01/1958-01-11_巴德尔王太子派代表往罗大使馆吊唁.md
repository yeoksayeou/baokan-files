Title: Crown Prince Badr Sends Representatives to the Romanian Embassy to Offer Condolences
Author:
Date: 1958-01-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th.—Yemeni Minister of State, Amri, and Advisor Baghdadi, representing Yemeni Deputy Prime Minister Crown Prince Badr, went to the Romanian Embassy in China on the afternoon of the 10th to offer condolences on the death of Dr. Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Romanian Grand National Assembly.

### 巴德尔王太子派代表往罗大使馆吊唁

1958-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    巴德尔王太子派代表往罗大使馆吊唁
    新华社10日讯　也门王国国务大臣阿姆里和顾问巴格达迪兹代表也门王国副首相巴德尔王太子10日下午前往罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆，吊唁罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团主席格罗查博士逝世。
