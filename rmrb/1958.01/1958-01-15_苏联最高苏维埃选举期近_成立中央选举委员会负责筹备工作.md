Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Elections Approaching; Central Election Commission Established to Handle Preparations
Author:
Date: 1958-01-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, January 14th: According to a TASS report, Soviet central newspapers published on the 12th the decree of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR approving the members of the Central Election Commission. The Central Election Commission will handle the election affairs of the two chambers of the Supreme Soviet—the Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities—throughout the country. The elections for both chambers will be held on March 16th.

The Election Commission consists of twenty-four representatives from all walks of life. Grishin, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, has been approved as Chairman of the Commission, and Moskatov, Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission of the CPSU, has been approved as Vice-Chairman.

### 苏联最高苏维埃选举期近  成立中央选举委员会负责筹备工作

1958-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃选举期近
    成立中央选举委员会负责筹备工作
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社报道，苏联中央级报纸12日发表了苏联最高苏维埃主席团关于批准中央选举委员会成员的命令。中央选举委员会将在全国范围内处理苏联最高苏维埃两院——联盟院和民族院的选举事宜。两院选举将在3月16日举行。
    选举委员会由社会各方面的代表人物二十四人组成。全苏工会中央理事会主席格里申被批准为委员会主席，苏共中央检查委员会主席莫斯卡托夫被批准为副主席。
