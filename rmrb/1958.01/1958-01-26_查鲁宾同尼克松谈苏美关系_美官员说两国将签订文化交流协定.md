Title: Zarubin and Nixon Discuss Soviet-U.S. Relations; U.S. Officials Say the Two Countries Will Sign a Cultural Exchange Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-01-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th, Washington Dispatch: Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Zarubin, paid a visit to U.S. Vice President Nixon and held lengthy talks with him on issues related to Soviet-U.S. relations. During the talks, both sides agreed that developing scientific, cultural, and other forms of contact between the Soviet Union and the United States would be very beneficial to both countries and would contribute to a better mutual understanding between the people of the Soviet Union and the United States.

After the talks, Zarubin said in response to questions from reporters that he believed his meeting with Nixon would help improve relations between the Soviet Union and the United States and would help ease international tensions.

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th, Moscow Dispatch: Stefan, the head of the Soviet Section of the U.S. State Department, told the Washington correspondent of the "Soviet Russia" newspaper regarding the Soviet-U.S. cultural cooperation talks being held in Washington, that the plan currently being discussed by the two countries stipulates broader cultural and scientific exchanges than in the past few years. The plan will be for a period of two years, and an agreement is expected to be signed soon. The plan stipulates mutual visits by artists, groups, and delegations from both countries.

### 查鲁宾同尼克松谈苏美关系  美官员说两国将签订文化交流协定

1958-01-26
第6版()
专栏：

    查鲁宾同尼克松谈苏美关系
    美官员说两国将签订文化交流协定
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社华盛顿25日讯：苏联驻美国大使查鲁宾拜访了美国副总统尼克松，并且同他就有关苏美关系问题进行了长时间的谈话。在谈话过程中，双方一致认为，发展苏联和美国之间的科学、文化和其他形式的接触，对于两国都是非常有益的，并且有助于苏联和美国人民之间更好地相互了解。
    查鲁宾在谈话后回答记者的问题时说，他认为，他同尼克松的会见将有助于改善苏联同美国之间的关系，并且有助于缓和国际紧张局势。
    据新华社25日讯　塔斯社莫斯科25日讯：美国国务院苏联科领导人斯特凡就苏美两国在华盛顿进行的文化合作会谈一事，向“苏维埃俄罗斯报”驻华盛顿记者发表谈话说，两国现在所磋商的计划将规定比过去几年更广泛的文化和科学交流。这项计划将以两年为期，预料最近即将签订协定。计划中规定两国艺术家、团体和代表团进行相互访问。
