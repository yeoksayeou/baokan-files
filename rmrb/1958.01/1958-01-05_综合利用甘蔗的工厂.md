Title: Factory for Comprehensive Utilization of Sugarcane
Author: Bao Guanqing, Chen Siyuan
Date: 1958-01-05
Page: 3

    Factory for Comprehensive Utilization of Sugarcane
    In 1957, Zhejiang Province established three sugar-paper mills and one cane sugar factory in Yiwu County. The entire production process of these four factories uses the local agricultural product – sugarcane – as raw material. The entire project is scheduled to be put into production before March 1958.
    After the completion of the three sugar-paper mills, a comprehensive production method will be used. First, the sugarcane will be fermented to produce brown sugar, then the bagasse will be distilled to produce white spirit. Next, the residue from spirit distillation, along with soda ash or caustic soda, will be cooked and pulped at high temperatures. These factories plan to produce 990 tons of brown sugar, 367 tons of white spirit, and 1,377 tons of pulp annually. (Bao Guanqing, Chen Siyuan)

### 综合利用甘蔗的工厂
鲍关清　陈思源
1958-01-05
第3版()
专栏：

    综合利用甘蔗的工厂
    浙江省1957年在义乌县创办了三个糖纸厂，一个蔗糖厂。这四个工厂的全部生产过程，都是利用当地的农产品——糖蔗做原料。全部工程计划在1958年3月前全部投入生产。
    三个糖纸厂建成后，将利用综合性的生产方式，首先把甘蔗发酵后制成红糖，然后把蔗壳酿蒸出白酒，接着是又利用蒸酒后的渣，加上纯碱或烧碱经高温蒸煮打成纸浆。这些厂计划年产红糖九百九十吨，白酒三百六十七吨，纸浆一千三百七十七吨。　（鲍关清　陈思源）
