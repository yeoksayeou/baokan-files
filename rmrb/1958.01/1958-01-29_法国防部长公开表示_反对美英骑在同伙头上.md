Title: French Defense Minister Publicly States Opposition to US and UK Riding on the Backs of Allies
Author:
Date: 1958-01-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 28th: French Defense Minister Chaban-Delmas frankly admitted on the 27th that his proposal for France, West Germany, and Italy to engage in discussions regarding the research and production of modern weapons was aimed at preventing the emergence of an Anglo-Saxon command within the North Atlantic group.

The leader of the Social Republican Party (Gaullist) made the above statement in a letter to the chairman of a local organization of the party. It is pointed out that this is the first time a high-ranking French official has so frankly expressed Paris's fears about the intention of the United States and the United Kingdom, the two nuclear powers within the North Atlantic group, to dominate other member states.

### 法国防部长公开表示  反对美英骑在同伙头上

1958-01-29
第5版()
专栏：

    法国防部长公开表示
    反对美英骑在同伙头上
    新华社28日讯　巴黎消息：法国国防部长沙邦—戴尔马27日坦率地承认，他倡议法国、西德、意大利三国就现代武器的研究和生产问题进行接触，目的是为了阻挠在北大西洋集团内部出现一个盎格鲁—撒克逊指挥部。
    社会共和党（戴高乐派）的这个领袖在写给该党某地方组织主席的一封信里说了上面这番话。人们指出，法国高级官员如此坦率地表白巴黎方面对北大西洋集团内美英这两个拥有核武器的大国有意凌驾其他成员国一事所怀有的疑惧，这还是第一次。
