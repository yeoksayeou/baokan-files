　　Ominous Sign
　　Associated Press reporter Gordon reported that Brazil appears to be entering a period of economic chaos, "and if economic chaos occurs, it may cause open rebellion." Thus, he said meaningfully that the United States may have to "save it."
　　From Brazil's "economic chaos" to the United States' "salvation," this American reporter's agility of mind deserves great commendation from the Wall Street tycoons. Because this association points out an opportunity for the Wall Street tycoons to further invade and control Brazil. And everyone knows that the people of Wall Street have long coveted Brazil's oil, uranium, and other resources.
　　Gordon's report is a very ominous sign for Brazil.

### 不祥之兆

1958-01-30
第6版()
专栏：札记

    不祥之兆
    美联社记者戈尔登报道说，巴西看来在进入经济混乱的时期，“而万一发生经济混乱，便可能引起公开的叛乱。”于是，他就大有深意地说，美国也许不得不“拯救它”。
    从巴西的“经济混乱”而联想到美国的“拯救”，这个美国记者心思的敏捷是值得华尔街的阔老们大大地嘉奖的。因为，这种联想为华尔街的阔老们指出了一个进一步侵入和控制巴西的机会。而谁都知道，华尔街的人们早就对巴西的石油、铀矿和其他资源垂涎三尺了。
    戈尔登的报道，对巴西是一个十分不祥的兆头。
