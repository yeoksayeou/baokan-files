Title: Egyptian and Lebanese People Protest Against American Planes Carrying Hydrogen Bombs Flying Over the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-01-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, January 15th: TASS, Cairo, January 14th: Egyptian public opinion is expressing anger and protest against American planes flying over Arab countries carrying atomic and hydrogen bombs.

　　The Egyptian "Al-Ahram" wrote that the people of the Middle Eastern countries do not want American planes to carry out deadly patrol flights over their heads. They are also unwilling to let their fate depend on pilots flying planes carrying death and destruction.

　　Xinhua News Agency, January 14th: TASS, Beirut, January 13th: According to the Lebanese "Al-Khabar" newspaper, the newspaper's editorial department has received many letters from residents across Lebanon protesting against American bombers carrying atomic bombs flying over the territory of Arab countries.

　　The newspaper also pointed out that people from all walks of life in Lebanon are deeply indignant about the news of plans to establish American military bases on Lebanese territory.

　　"Al-Khabar" demanded that the Lebanese government implement a policy of active neutrality and refuse to participate in military blocs and military treaties.

### 埃及和黎巴嫩人民  抗议带氢弹美机在中东飞行

1958-01-16
第5版()
专栏：

    埃及和黎巴嫩人民
    抗议带氢弹美机在中东飞行
    据新华社15日讯　塔斯社开罗14日讯：埃及公众对美国飞机带着原子弹和氢弹在阿拉伯国家上空飞行表示愤怒和抗议。
    埃及“金字塔报”写道，中东各国人民不希望美国飞机在他们头上作致命的巡逻飞行。他们也不愿意让自己的命运取决于驾驶载着死亡和破坏的飞机的飞行员。
    据新华社14日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特13日讯：据黎巴嫩“消息报”报道，这家报纸的编辑部收到了许多来自黎巴嫩各地居民的信件，抗议载有原子弹的美国轰炸机在阿拉伯国家的领土上空飞行。
    这家报纸同时指出，黎巴嫩国内各界人士对于在本国领土上建立美国军事基地的计划的消息，深表愤慨。
    “消息报”要求黎巴嫩政府执行积极中立政策，拒绝参加军事集团和军事条约。
