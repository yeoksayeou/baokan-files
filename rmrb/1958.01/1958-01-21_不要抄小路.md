Title: Don't Take Shortcuts
Author: Yang Bolin
Date: 1958-01-21
Page: 2

Don't Take Shortcuts
    There are too many roads in the countryside now. Some of these roads are not "proper roads," but narrow paths. Most of these paths are formed by people who like to "take shortcuts." Last winter, when Dongxiao Agricultural Cooperative in Fushan County, Shanxi Province, was consolidating farmland in conjunction with soil and water conservation, it blocked and rerouted all the small paths and unreasonable cart roads in the entire cooperative, resulting in the expansion of cultivated land by more than 20 mu. This cooperative is not large, with only 8,000 mu of dry land, and these unreasonable roads occupy more than 20 mu. If this situation exists everywhere, the figure would be considerable. Therefore, I hope that relevant units should, on the one hand, educate the masses to cherish cultivated land and crops and not be greedy for shortcuts, and on the other hand, actively try to carry out rerouting and other work where possible. Yang Bolin

### 不要抄小路
杨柏林
1958-01-21
第2版(读者来信)
专栏：

    不要抄小路
    现在农村中的道路太多了，有些路不是“正路”，而是羊肠小道。这些小道大多是被一些喜欢“抄近路”的人践踏而成的。山西浮山县东晓农业社去年冬天结合水土保持整修田地时，将全社小道和不合理的马车路进行了堵截和改道，结果扩大耕地二十多亩。这个社并不大，只有八千亩旱地，这些不合理的道路就占去二十多亩，如果各地都有这样的情况，其数字是可观的。所以我希望各有关单位应当一方面教育群众爱惜耕地和禾苗，不要贪走近路，另一方面在可能条件下，积极地设法进行改道等工作。　杨柏林
