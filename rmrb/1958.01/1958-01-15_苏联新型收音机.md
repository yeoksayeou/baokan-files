Title: New Soviet Radios
Author:
Date: 1958-01-15
Page: 6

    Soviet New Radios
      Solar-Powered Radio
    The Semiconductor Institute of the Soviet Academy of Sciences has recently designed a solar-powered radio. This radio, which can be used almost indefinitely, requires almost no other energy source other than light during its entire lifespan.
    This radio is equipped with six semiconductor amplifiers, a small speaker, and an internal magnetic antenna. The entire radio resembles a woman's handbag in shape. Its important feature is that, in addition to the solar cell, it is also equipped with a small dry storage battery. Under sufficiently strong sunlight or the illumination of a 200 to 300-watt desk lamp, the electricity generated by the solar cell can not only supply the radio's power but also charge the storage battery. Thus, in the absence of a light source, the radio can still be used for 60 to 125 hours.
        Book-Shaped Radio
    The "Festival" brand radio, a Soviet product that looks exactly like a medium-sized book, has been delivered to factories for mass production. This radio is extremely easy to carry around, and can even broadcast programs while tucked under the arm.
    This radio is made using semiconductors and has nine flat crystal transistors inside. Its power source is ordinary flashlight batteries, and one dry battery can power the radio for twenty-five hours. The antenna is made of ferrite magnetic material, so it is not necessary to extend the antenna outside the radio; it can be installed inside the radio casing. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 苏联新型收音机

1958-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联新型收音机
      太阳能收音机
    苏联科学院半导体研究所最近设计出了一种太阳能无线电收音机，这种差不多可以永远使用的收音机，在整个使用期间除光源之外几乎可以不要其他能源。
    这种收音机上装有六个半导体放大仪器，一个不大的扬声器和一副装在内部的磁性天线，整个收音机外形像一个女用的手提包。它的重要特点是，除了太阳能电池以外，还装有一个小型的干蓄电池，在足够强烈的太阳光或二百到三百瓦的台灯的照耀下，太阳能电池发出的电不仅可以供应收音机使用，而且可以给蓄电池充电，这样，在没有光源的时候，收音机还可以使用六十到一百二十五小时。
        书本型收音机
    苏联一种外形与一本中等大小的书本一模一样的“联欢节”牌收音机已经交付工厂大批生产。这种收音机极便于随身携带，甚至夹在腋下也可以放送节目。
    这种收音机是利用半导体装成的，内部有九个扁平的晶体三极管。它的电源是普通手电筒用的干电池，一个干电池可以使收音机工作二十五小时。天线是由铁淦氧磁物制成的，因而可以不必把天线支到收音机外面，装在收音机匣子内就行了。　（新华社）
