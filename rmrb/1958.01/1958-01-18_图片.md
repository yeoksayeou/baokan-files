    Our country's Dunhuang Art Exhibition opened at the Tokyo Takashimaya on January 5th. In the photo, from the right in the front row, the first person is Li Dequan, head of the Chinese Red Cross delegation to Japan, and the second person is Tetsuji Katayama, president of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association.
