Title: Russian Basketball Team and Uzbek Gymnastics Team Return Home
Author:
Date: 1958-01-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 10th – The Soviet Russian Men’s and Women’s Basketball Teams and the Uzbek Gymnastics Team concluded their visit to China and left Beijing for home on the 8th and 10th respectively.

During their three-week visit to China, the Russian Men’s and Women’s Basketball Teams played sixteen friendly matches with fourteen Chinese Men’s and Women’s Basketball Teams in Beijing, Tianjin, and Shanghai. The Russian Men's team's record was five wins, two draws, and one loss, with a total score ratio of 565 to 505. The Russian Women's team's record was six wins, one draw, and one loss, with a total score ratio of 480 to 385.

The Uzbek men and women gymnasts visited China for a month. They held two friendly matches with Chinese men and women gymnasts in Beijing, Shanghai, Shenyang, Changchun, and Harbin, and also held five performances, with approximately 30,000 spectators.

### 俄罗斯篮球队和乌兹别克体操队回国

1958-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯篮球队和乌兹别克体操队回国
    新华社10日讯　苏联俄罗斯男女篮球队和乌兹别克体操队结束了在中国的访问，分别在8日和10日离北京回国。
    俄罗斯男女篮球队在中国的三个星期的访问期间，先后在北京、天津、上海同中国的十四个男女篮球队举行了十六场友谊比赛。俄罗斯男子队的战绩是五胜、二和、一败，得失分的总比数是五六五比五○五。俄罗斯女子队的战绩是六胜、一和、一败，得失分的总比数是四八○比三八五。
    乌兹别克男女体操运动员在中国共访问了一个月，他们先后在北京、上海、沈阳、长春、哈尔滨同中国的男女体操运动员举行了两次友谊比赛，另外还举行了五场表演，观众约三万人。
