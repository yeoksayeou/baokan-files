Title: Factory Using Waste to Make Paper
Author: Hong Cai
Date: 1958-01-28
Page: 3

    Factory Using Waste to Make Paper
    The Pearl River Paper Mill will soon begin construction in Guangzhou. This factory will use waste chlorine from the electrolytic chemical plant and sugarcane residue from the sugar mill as raw materials to produce 100 tons of high-quality printing pictorial and writing paper (art paper) per day. This factory will go into production in early 1960.　　　　　　　　(Hong Cai)

### 用废料造纸的工厂
洪才
1958-01-28
第3版()
专栏：

    用废料造纸的工厂
    珠江造纸厂即将在广州动工兴建。这个厂将利用电解化工厂的废氯和糖厂的甘蔗渣做原料，日产一百吨印刷画报和书面的高级纸——胶板纸。这个厂将在1960年初投入生产。　　　　　　　　（洪才）
