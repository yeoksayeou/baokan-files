Title: Happy Poem on New Year's Day 1958 - Tune: Qing Ping Yue
Author: Li Jishen
Date: 1958-01-01
Page: 4

    The beginning of the year commences.
    A new blueprint for the year is unveiled.
    Another five years to express grand plans,
    A high tide of production is stirred up.
    The country has a bright future.
    Singing praises of the leadership's brilliance!
    Just like spring melting the earth,
    Warmth will bring social prosperity.

### 1958年元旦喜赋　调寄清平乐

1958-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    1958年元旦喜赋　调寄清平乐
    李济深
    履端肇始。
    献岁新猷启。
    再个五年抒大计，
    生产高潮掀起。
    国家锦样前程。
    讴歌领导英明！
    恰似春融大地，
    煦将社会繁荣。
