Title: American Scientist Huth Admits: American Rockets are Increasingly Behind the Soviet Union

Author: 
Date: 1958-01-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, San Francisco, January 15th: Dr. Huth, director of the research analysis group at the Jet Propulsion Laboratory of the California Institute of Technology, said on the evening of the 14th that the United States is two years behind the Soviet Union in rocketry, and this gap is continuing to widen. Huth said, "The rocket engines that the Soviet Union is now using to propel missiles are more than twice as powerful as any of our engines." He said that the U.S. government has announced that it is developing five types of missiles, but "our true rocket experts believe that it would be difficult to successfully implement even one or two of them."

### 美科学家赫布斯承认  美国火箭比苏联越来越落后

1958-01-17
第5版()
专栏：

    美科学家赫布斯承认
    美国火箭比苏联越来越落后
    据新华社15日讯　旧金山消息：加利福尼亚工学院喷气推进实验室研究分析组主任赫布斯博士14日晚上说，美国在火箭方面落后于苏联两年，而且这种落后的距离仍然在扩大。赫布斯说：“现在苏联用以推动导弹的火箭发动机的力量比我们所有的任何发动机的力量要大一倍多。”他说，美国政府宣布正在发展五种类型的导弹，但是“我们的真正火箭专家们认为即使能够顺利地实现其中一种或两种都已经是吃力不堪了”。
