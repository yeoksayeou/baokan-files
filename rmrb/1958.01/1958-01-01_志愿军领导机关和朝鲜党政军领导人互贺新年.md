Title: Volunteer Army Leadership and Korean Party, Government, and Military Leaders Exchange New Year's Greetings
Author:
Date: 1958-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 31st: The leadership of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the leaders of the Korean Democratic People's Republic party and government, and the headquarters of the People's Army exchanged New Year's greetings.

    On the 31st, Korean Premier Marshal Kim Il-sung presented the Volunteer Army with a large quantity of ginseng wine, apples, fresh fish, and other New Year's gifts. Korean Minister of National Defense General Kim Kwang-hyup, Chief of the General Staff of the People's Army General Li Kwon-mu, and Director of the General Political Bureau Choi Chung-hak wrote letters on the same day to Volunteer Army Commander General Yang Yong and Political Commissar General Wang Ping, congratulating them on the further consolidation and development of the friendship and unity between the people and armies of Korea and China in the new year.

    On the same day, Chinese People's Volunteer Army Commander General Yang Yong and Political Commissar General Wang Ping replied to Premier Kim Il-sung and General Kim Kwang-hyup respectively to express their gratitude and to congratulate them on the leap forward development of Korean industry and agriculture in 1958.

### 志愿军领导机关和朝鲜党政军领导人互贺新年

1958-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军领导机关和朝鲜党政军领导人互贺新年
    新华社平壤31日电　中国人民志愿军领导机关同朝鲜民主主义人民共和国党政领导人和人民军总部互贺新年。
    31日，朝鲜内阁首相金日成元帅赠送给志愿军大批人参酒、苹果和鲜鱼等新年礼品。朝鲜民族保卫相金光侠大将、人民军总参谋长李权武上将、总政治局长崔钟学上将在同一天写信向志愿军司令员杨勇上将、政治委员王平上将，祝贺在新的年度里朝中两国人民和军队之间的友谊和团结进一步巩固和发展。
    中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将、政治委员王平上将在同一天分别向金日成首相和金光侠大将复信表示感谢，并祝贺1958年朝鲜工农业飞跃发展。
