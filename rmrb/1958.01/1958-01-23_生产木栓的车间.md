Title: Workshop for Producing Wooden Plugs
Author: Shi Lin
Date: 1958-01-23
Page: 3

    Workshop for Producing Wooden Plugs
    (This newspaper dispatch) The wooden plug workshop newly built by the Ministry of Railways at the Shanhaiguan Bridge Factory has recently been put into production.
    This workshop can produce seven million wooden plugs annually to meet the country's need for reinforced concrete sleepers.
                        (Shi Lin)

### 生产木栓的车间
石林
1958-01-23
第3版()
专栏：

    生产木栓的车间
    本报讯　铁道部在山海关桥梁厂新建的木栓车间，已经在最近投入生产。
    这个车间每年可以生产七百万根木栓，以适应国家对钢筋混凝土轨枕的需要。
                        （石林）
