Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Issues "March 8th" Message Calling on Women Worldwide to Demand Disarmament
Author:
Date: 1958-01-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 28th - The International Democratic Women's Federation today issued a message congratulating women and women's organizations around the world on "March 8th" International Women's Day, and advocating for a halt to nuclear weapons testing and the Cold War, and for the resolution of all outstanding international issues through negotiation.

The message stated that on "March 8th," women around the world will strengthen their friendship with each other and further support those women who are heroically fighting for freedom and national independence, and they will especially insist on efforts to save the life of the young Algerian heroine Djamila Bouhired.

### 国际民主妇联发表“三八”节文告  号召全世界妇女要求裁军

1958-01-30
第5版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联发表“三八”节文告
    号召全世界妇女要求裁军
    据新华社柏林28日电　国际民主妇女联合会今天发表一项文告，向全世界妇女和妇女组织祝贺“三八”国际妇女节，并且主张停止核武器试验和冷战，主张通过协商解决国际间一切悬而未决的问题。
    文告说，在“三八”节这一天，全世界妇女将加强彼此的友谊和进一步声援那些正在英勇地为自由和民族独立而斗争的妇女，她们将特别坚持努力来拯救年青的阿尔及利亚女英雄加米拉·布伊海德的生命。
