Brief News

    Soviet Film Week Opens in Cairo
    Cairo Dispatch: The Soviet Film Week opened on the evening of the 27th at one of Cairo's largest cinemas, the "Rivoli Cinema." The opening ceremony featured the Soviet feature film "Two Sisters" and a documentary about the Soviet Union's launch of the first artificial earth satellite. This Soviet Film Week, sponsored by the Egyptian National Guidance Department, is the first Soviet Film Week held in Egypt.

    Syria and Bulgaria Sign Cultural Agreement
    Damascus Dispatch: Syria and Bulgaria signed a five-year cultural agreement here on the 27th. According to the agreement, the two countries will send students to each other, exchange information on films, theater, and music, and exchange visits by scientific, cultural, and sports delegations.

    简讯
    苏联电影周在开罗开幕
    开罗讯：苏联电影周27日晚间在开罗最大的电影院之一“利沃里电影院”开幕。在开幕式上放映了苏联故事片“两姐妹”和关于苏联发射第一颗人造地球卫星的纪录片。这个由埃及国家指导部主办的苏联电影周是埃及第一次举办的苏联电影周。
    叙保签订文化协定
    大马士革讯：叙利亚和保加利亚27日在这里签订了一项为期五年的文化协定。根据这项协定，两国将互派留学生，交换电影、戏剧和音乐方面的资料，并且互派科学、文化和体育代表团访问。
