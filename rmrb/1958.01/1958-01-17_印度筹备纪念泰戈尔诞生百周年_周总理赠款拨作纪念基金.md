Title: India Prepares to Commemorate Tagore's Centenary; Premier Zhou's Donation Allocated to Commemorative Fund
Author:
Date: 1958-01-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 16th: According to news from Calcutta, the 60,000 rupees donated by Premier Zhou Enlai to the Visva-Bharati University founded by the great Indian writer Tagore during his visit to India in 1957 have been allocated by the school authorities to the Tagore Centenary Commemoration Fund. This fund was established by Indian Prime Minister Nehru on December 25, 1957, to celebrate the centenary of Tagore's birth in 1961.

This donation has recently been transferred to Visva-Bharati University by the Chinese Embassy in India. India is currently preparing for the centenary commemoration of Tagore's birth.

### 印度筹备纪念泰戈尔诞生百周年  周总理赠款拨作纪念基金

1958-01-17
第6版()
专栏：

    印度筹备纪念泰戈尔诞生百周年
    周总理赠款拨作纪念基金
    据新华社新德里16日电　据来自加尔各答的消息说，周恩来总理1957年访问印度时捐赠给印度伟大文豪泰戈尔创立的国际大学的六万卢比，已经由学校当局拨入泰戈尔诞生纪念基金。这笔基金是印度总理尼赫鲁在1957年12月25日为了庆祝1961年泰戈尔诞生百周年纪念而设立的。
    这笔赠款已经在最近由中国驻印度大使馆转交给国际大学。印度正进行泰戈尔诞生百周年纪念的筹备工作。
