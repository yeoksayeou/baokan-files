Title: Bulgarian Party and Government Leaders Arrive in Moscow for Visit
Author:
Date: 1958-01-28
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 26th: According to TASS, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Zhivkov, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Yugov, and the Minister of Trade, Taskov, arrived in Moscow by train on the 25th. The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and the Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Suslov, welcomed them at the station.
    On that day, the Bulgarian leaders held talks with the Soviet leaders. The two sides discussed issues of mutual interest.
    On that day, the Soviet leaders held a banquet in the Kremlin to entertain the Bulgarian guests.

### 保党政领导人到达莫斯科访问

1958-01-28
第6版()
专栏：

    保党政领导人到达莫斯科访问
    据新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫、部长会议主席于哥夫、贸易部长塔斯科夫25日乘火车到达莫斯科。苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、苏联共产党中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫到车站欢迎。
    这一天，保加利亚领导人同苏联领导人进行了交谈。双方对两国感兴趣的问题进行了讨论。
    这一天，苏联领导人在克里姆林宫举行宴会招待保加利亚客人。
