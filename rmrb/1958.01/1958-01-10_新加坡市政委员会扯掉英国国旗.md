Title: Singapore Municipal Council Tears Down British Flag
Author:
Date: 1958-01-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, 9th: Singapore's newly appointed Mayor, Ong Eng Guan, said in his first speech to the Municipal Council after taking office on January 8th that the Municipal Council will pay full attention to the poorest areas of the city and strive to eliminate corruption in any form. Before the meeting, the two British flags hanging behind the mayor's seat were torn down. Ong Eng Guan explained in his speech that there was no reason for the Municipal Council to retain these colonial relics.

### 新加坡市政委员会扯掉英国国旗

1958-01-10
第5版()
专栏：

    新加坡市政委员会
    扯掉英国国旗
    据新华社9日讯　新加坡消息：新加坡新任市长王永元1月8日在他就职后在市政委员会的第一次演说中说，市政委员会今后将充分注意市区里最贫穷的地区，并且致力于清除任何形式的贪污。在开会前，挂在市长座位后面的两面英国国旗被扯去了。王永元在他的演说中解释说，没有理由要市政委员会保留这些殖民主义的纪念物。
