Title: Eleanor Roosevelt on U.S. Foreign Policy Failures
Author:
Date: 1958-01-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 13th: Eleanor Roosevelt said in a speech at the University of North Carolina on the 12th that the "prestige" of the United States abroad is declining, and the current Republican government and previous Democratic governments should be held responsible for this.

Roosevelt pointed out that many countries are wary of American "aid".

### 罗斯福夫人谈美外交失败

1958-01-14
第5版()
专栏：

    罗斯福夫人谈美外交失败
    据新华社13日讯　纽约消息：罗斯福夫人12日在北卡罗来纳大学发表演说时说，美国在国外的“威信”在下降，目前的共和党政府和以前的民主党政府都应该对这件事负责。
    罗斯福夫人指出，许多国家对美国“援助”都怀有戒心。
