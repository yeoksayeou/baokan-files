Title: To Maintain Friendship Permanently, To Strengthen Close Cooperative Relations, China and Yemen Sign Treaty of Friendship, Also sign Commercial Treaty, Agreement on Scientific, Technical and Cultural Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1958-01-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - The signing ceremony of the Treaty of Friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Mutawakkilite Kingdom of Yemen, the Commercial Treaty, the Agreement on Scientific, Technical and Cultural Cooperation, and the Joint Communiqué between Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China and Deputy Prime Minister Crown Prince Badr of the Kingdom of Yemen, was solemnly held at ten forty in the evening of the 12th at the Qin Zheng Dian Hall in Zhongnanhai, Beijing.

Representing China in signing the documents was Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China. Representing Yemen was Crown Prince Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Minister of Defense of the Kingdom of Yemen.

Chinese officials attending the signing ceremony included Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Peng Dehuai and Chen Yi, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Li Jishen and Huang Yanpei, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade; Zeng Yongquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Yan Baohang, Chairman of the Treaty Committee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department.

Yemeni officials attending the signing ceremony included Qadi Muhammad al-Amri, Minister of State of the Kingdom of Yemen; Qadi Muhammad al-Shami, Minister of State; and Hassan Baghdadi. (See attached picture)

Right: Premier Zhou Enlai and Crown Prince Badr during the signing.
                                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Huaxue.

### 为了永久保持友谊　为了密切合作关系  中国也门签订友好条约  同时签订商务条约，科学、技术和文化合作协定

1958-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    为了永久保持友谊　为了密切合作关系
    中国也门签订友好条约
    同时签订商务条约，科学、技术和文化合作协定
    新华社12日讯　中华人民共和国和也门穆塔瓦基利亚王国友好条约，商务条约，科学、技术和文化合作协定，以及中华人民共和国周恩来总理和也门王国副首相巴德尔王太子联合公报的签字仪式，12日晚十时四十分在北京中南海勤政殿隆重举行。
    代表中国签字的是中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，代表也门签字的是也门王国副首相兼外交大臣和国防大臣塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔王太子。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，国务院副总理彭德怀、陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、黄炎培，国务院秘书长习仲勋，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部副部长曾涌泉、外交部条约委员会主任委员阎宝航、西亚非洲司司长柯华。
    也门方面参加签字仪式的，有也门王国国务大臣卡迪·穆罕默德·阿姆里，国务大臣卡迪·穆罕默德·沙米，顾问哈桑·巴格达迪兹。（附图片）
