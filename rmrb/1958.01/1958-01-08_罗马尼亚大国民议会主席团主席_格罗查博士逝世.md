Title: Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania, Dr. Groza, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1958-01-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, January 7th: The Romanian News Agency broadcast today a statement from the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania, the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and the Council of Ministers of Romania, stating that the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania, the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and the Council of Ministers of Romania, with profound grief, announce to the people of the whole country that the outstanding statesman, the loyal son of the Romanian people, our beloved comrade, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly, Dr. Petru Groza, after a long and serious illness, passed away in Bucharest on the morning of January 7th at 5:00 AM.

Xinhua News Agency, January 7th: After learning of the passing of Dr. Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai, paid their respects at the Romanian Embassy in China at 11:00 PM on the 7th, and requested Ambassador Rudenko to convey the deep condolences of the Chinese people and the Chinese government to Dr. Groza's family, the Romanian people, and the Romanian government.

Those who also went to pay their respects included: Vice Chairmen Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong; Vice Premiers Li Xiannian and Bo Yibo; Procurator-General Zhang Dingcheng; Secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee Tan Zhenlin and Li Xuefeng; and Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Ji Pengfei.

Tonight, Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai also sent wreaths to Dr. Groza's portrait.

### 罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团主席  格罗查博士逝世

1958-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团主席
    格罗查博士逝世
    新华社布加勒斯特7日电　罗马尼亚通讯社今天广播罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团、罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会和罗马尼亚部长会议的公告说，罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团、罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会和罗马尼亚部长会议沉痛地向全国人民宣布，卓越的政治家、罗马尼亚人民的忠实儿子、我们敬爱的同志、大国民议会主席团主席彼得鲁·格罗查博士在长期的重病之后，于1月7日早晨五时在布加勒斯特逝世。
    新华社7日讯  朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理，在获知罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团主席格罗查博士逝世的消息后，7日晚十一时到罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆吊唁，面请鲁登科大使向格罗查博士的家属、罗马尼亚人民和罗马尼亚政府，转达中国人民和中国政府对格罗查博士的沉痛哀悼。
    前往吊唁的还有：林伯渠、李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通副委员长，李先念、薄一波副总理，张鼎丞检察长，中共中央书记处书记谭震林、李雪峰，外交部副部长章汉夫、姬鹏飞。
    今晚，毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理，还向格罗查博士的遗像送了花圈。
