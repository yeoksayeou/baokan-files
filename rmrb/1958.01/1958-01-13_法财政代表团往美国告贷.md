Title: French Financial Delegation Goes to the United States to Seek Loans
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, January 12th: A three-member French financial delegation left Paris by plane on the evening of January 11 for Washington to request loans from the United States to support France's struggling economy. The head of the delegation is French economist and former Chairman of the European Coal and Steel Community, Jean Monnet.

It is revealed that the delegation intends to borrow 375 million US dollars from the United States by mid-1958 to alleviate France's current financial and economic crisis.

### 法财政代表团往美国告贷

1958-01-13
第5版()
专栏：

    法财政代表团往美国告贷
    据新华社12日讯　巴黎消息：一个由三人组成的法国财政代表团1月11日晚间乘飞机离开巴黎赴华盛顿，向美国要求贷款来支持法国困难重重的经济。代表团团长是法国经济专家、前欧洲煤钢联营主席莫内。
    据透露，代表团此行打算在1958年中为法国借到三亿七千五百万美元以解救法国当前的财政经济危机。
