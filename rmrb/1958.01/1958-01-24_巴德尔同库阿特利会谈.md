Title: Badr Meets with Kuwatly

Author: 
Date: 1958-01-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, January 22nd: The Crown Prince and Deputy Prime Minister of Yemen, Badr, met with Syrian President Kuwatly at the Syrian Presidential Palace on the 22nd.

The Syrian News Agency's communique stated that they discussed Arab affairs, particularly the relations between Syria and Yemen.

Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar hosted a luncheon for Badr on the 22nd. Prime Minister al-Assali will hold a banquet in the evening to welcome him.

### 巴德尔同库阿特利会谈

1958-01-24
第5版()
专栏：

    巴德尔同库阿特利会谈
    据新华社大马士革22日电也门王太子、副首相巴德尔22日到叙利亚总统府同库阿特利总统举行了会谈。
    叙利亚新闻局的公报说，他们讨论了阿拉伯事务，特别是叙利亚同也门的关系。
    叙利亚外交部长比塔尔22日设午宴招待巴德尔。晚间，阿萨利总理将举行宴会欢迎他。
