Title: McElroy Reveals US Plans to Deploy Missiles in the Far East
Author:
Date: 1958-01-22
Page: 6

    McElroy Reveals
    US Plans to Deploy Missiles in the Far East
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 21st: The House Appropriations Committee released Secretary of Defense McElroy's recent secret testimony before the committee on the 20th. The US Secretary of Defense said that the US government is considering establishing intermediate-range missile bases in the Far East.
    McElroy also said that the United States will have an intermediate-range missile base in Britain "ready for actual combat use" by the end of this year.

### 麦克艾罗伊透露美国想在远东设置导弹

1958-01-22
第6版()
专栏：

    麦克艾罗伊透露
    美国想在远东设置导弹
    据新华社21日讯　华盛顿消息：美国众议院拨款委员会20日公布了国防部长麦克艾罗伊最近在委员会会议上的秘密证词。美国国防部长说，美国政府正在考虑在远东设置中程导弹基地。
    麦克艾罗伊还说，美国在今年年底以前将要在英国建成一个“可供实际作战使用”的中程导弹基地。
