Title: Order of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1958-01-07
Page: 1

The "Measures for Land Requisition for National Construction" promulgated by the Government Administration Council on December 5, 1953, have been amended by the 58th Plenary Meeting of the State Council on October 18, 1957, and approved by the 90th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on January 6, 1958. It is hereby promulgated.

                                      Premier of the State Council: Zhou Enlai
                                          January 6, 1958

### 中华人民共和国国务院命令

1958-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国国务院命令
    一九五三年十二月五日政务院公布的国家建设征用土地办法，已经一九五七年十月十八日国务院全体会议第五十八次会议修正，并经一九五八年一月六日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九十次会议批准，现在予以公布。
                                      国务院总理　周恩来
                                          一九五八年一月六日
