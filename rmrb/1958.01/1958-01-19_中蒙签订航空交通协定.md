Title: China and Mongolia Sign Air Transport Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-01-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, January 18th: The air transport delegations of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic conducted negotiations in Ulaanbaatar from January 13th to 17th, 1958, regarding civil aviation transport between the two countries in a friendly atmosphere of equality and mutual understanding. Based on the results of the negotiations, the "Air Transport Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic" was signed in Ulaanbaatar on January 17th, 1958.

On the basis of the Sino-Mongolian Air Transport Agreement, the government delegations of the two countries will separately sign agreements and protocols on specific agreements between the Civil Aviation Administration of China and the Air Transport Administration of the Ministry of Military and Public Security of the Mongolian People's Republic on air transport and mutual services, and on technical cooperation in civil aviation between the two countries.

### 中蒙签订航空交通协定

1958-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    中蒙签订航空交通协定
    据新华社乌兰巴托18日电　中华人民共和国政府和蒙古人民共和国政府航空交通代表团于1958年1月13日至17日在平等和互相谅解的友好气氛中在乌兰巴托进行了关于两国民航交通的谈判，根据谈判的结果，于1958年1月17日在乌兰巴托签订了“中华人民共和国政府和蒙古人民共和国政府航空交通协定”。
    在中蒙航空交通协定的基础上，两国政府代表团将就有关中华人民共和国民用航空局和蒙古人民共和国军事和公安部航空交通管理局间航空运输和互相服务的具体协议及两国民用航空技术合作问题另行签订协定和议定书。
