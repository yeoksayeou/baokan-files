Title: Soviet Union Develops New Cold-Resistant and Heat-Resistant Passenger Plane; Planes Four Times Faster Than Sound Will Soon Be Made
Author:
Date: 1958-01-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 13th, Moscow dispatch: The Soviet Union has developed a new type of passenger plane called the "Moscow Il-18." This plane can adapt to temperature changes of 120 degrees Celsius. The comfortable cabin is equipped with artificial climate control, allowing it to fly within the frigid Arctic Circle and over the scorching deserts. The "Moscow Il-18" can use any type of airport. Among all similar aircraft, it has the shortest takeoff distance. It has four engines, each with a power of 4,000 horsepower.

Xinhua News Agency, January 13th, Moscow dispatch: According to an article published in the latest issue of "Spark" magazine by Professor Peshnov, a well-known Soviet aviation expert, Soviet scientists will soon be able to build a transonic aircraft with a speed more than four times faster than the speed of sound. Perfected turbojet engines and rocket engines with power in the hundreds of thousands of horsepower are now available to complete this task.

Peshnov emphasized that Soviet scientists are studying the characteristics of transonic flight. Transonic aircraft pilots will encounter new phenomena that they have never seen before. If the speed of the aircraft reaches four to six kilometers per second, the flight will be affected by the curvature of the earth, and the necessary lift can be less than the weight of the aircraft. If the speed reaches seven to eight kilometers per second, lift will not be needed at all.

### 苏联制成耐寒耐热新客机  比音速快四倍的飞机不久就可制成

1958-01-14
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联制成耐寒耐热新客机
    比音速快四倍的飞机不久就可制成
    据新华社13日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联又制成了一种名叫“莫斯科伊尔—18式”的新式客机。这种飞机能够适应相差一百二十度的温度变化，在舒适的机舱里装有人工气候设备，可以在寒冷的极圈之内和炙热的沙漠上空飞行。“莫斯科伊尔—18式”可以使用任何一种机场。在所有同类飞机中，它的起飞距离是最短的。它有四个功率各为四千马力的发动机。
    据新华社13日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联著名航空专家佩什诺夫教授在最近一期“星火”杂志上发表文章说，苏联科学家在不久的将来就可以制成一种速度比音速快四倍以上的跨音速飞机。现在已经有了功率为几十万马力的、完善的涡轮喷气式发动机和火箭发动机，可以用来完成这一任务。
    佩什诺夫强调指出，苏联科学家正在研究跨音速飞行的特点。跨音速飞机的飞行员将会碰到一些他所未见过的新现象。如果飞机的速度每秒钟达到四至六公里，那么飞行就会受到地球曲率的影响，必要的升力将可以小于飞机的重量。如果速度达到每秒钟七至八公里，升力就根本不需要了。
