Title: Oman Continues Anti-British Struggle; Achieves Five Victories This Month

Author:

Date: 1958-01-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 18th — The Oman Imamate Office in Cairo announced today that Omani nationalists killed thirteen British soldiers in an ambush on British troops in the Green Mountains. Three Omani nationalists were sacrificed in this battle.

    The office also mentioned another battle between Omani nationalists and British troops in Wadi Helfen. The office said that battle was the fifth victorious battle fought by Omani nationalists against British troops in January.

### 阿曼继续反英斗争  本月内取得五次胜利

1958-01-20
第5版()
专栏：

    阿曼继续反英斗争
    本月内取得五次胜利
    新华社开罗18日电　阿曼教长驻开罗办事处今天宣布，阿曼民族主义者在格林山区对英国军队的一次伏击中，打死了十三名英国士兵。在这次战斗中，阿曼民族主义者牺牲了三人。
    办事处还提到阿曼民族主义者同英国军队在瓦迪赫尔芬的另一次战斗。办事处说，那次战斗是阿曼民族主义者在1月份同英国军队进行的取得了胜利的第五次战斗。
