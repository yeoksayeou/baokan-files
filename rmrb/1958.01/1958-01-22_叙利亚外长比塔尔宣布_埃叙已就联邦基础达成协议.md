Title: Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar Announces Egypt and Syria Have Reached Agreement on Federal Basis
Author:
Date: 1958-01-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 20th. Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar told "Evening News" reporters on the morning of the 20th that Egypt and Syria have "reached a comprehensive agreement" on the basis for establishing the Egyptian-Syrian federation.

The official talks between Bitar and the Egyptian authorities on realizing the Egyptian-Syrian federation concluded on the 19th. He also met with Egyptian Minister of State for Presidential Affairs Sabri on the 20th.

"Evening News" stated that the commanders-in-chief of the armed forces of Egypt and Syria, Amer and Bizri, are currently drafting an outline for unifying the armed forces of the two countries. The newspaper also said that within two weeks, Egypt and Syria will form delegations to draft the constitution of the federation.

### 叙利亚外长比塔尔宣布  埃叙已就联邦基础达成协议

1958-01-22
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚外长比塔尔宣布
    埃叙已就联邦基础达成协议
    新华社开罗20日电　叙利亚外交部长比塔尔20日上午对“晚报”记者说，埃及和叙利亚已经就建立埃叙联邦的基础“达成全面协议”。
    比塔尔同埃及当局关于实现埃及—叙利亚联邦的正式会谈已经在19日结束。他在20日又会见了埃及负责总统事务的国务部长萨布里。
    “晚报”说，埃及、叙利亚两国武装部队总司令阿密尔和比兹里现在正在起草统一两国军队的纲要。这家报纸又说，两周内，埃及、叙利亚两国将组成代表团来起草联邦的宪法。
