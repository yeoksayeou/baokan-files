Title: Guo Moruo and Burhan Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-01-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 20th: Guo Moruo and Burhan returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon from Moscow. Before returning to China, Guo Moruo, representing the Chinese government, signed a protocol with the Soviet government in Moscow on the two countries jointly conducting and the Soviet Union assisting China in conducting major scientific and technological research. Together with Burhan, they also represented the Chinese government at the funeral of Chairman Groza in Romania. They also led the Chinese delegation to the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference held in Cairo.

Other members of the Chinese Scientific and Technological Delegation to the Soviet Union headed by Guo Moruo have also returned to China in batches.

### 郭沫若、包尔汉回到北京

1958-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若、包尔汉回到北京
    新华社20日讯　郭沫若和包尔汉已于今天下午由莫斯科乘飞机回到北京。回国前，郭沫若代表中国政府在莫斯科同苏联政府签订了两国关于共同进行和苏联帮助中国进行重大科学技术研究的议定书，并且同包尔汉一起，代表中国政府到罗马尼亚参加了格罗查主席的葬礼，他们还率领中国代表团出席了在开罗召开的亚非人民团结大会。
    以郭沫若为首的中国访苏科学技术代表团其他人员也已分批回国。
