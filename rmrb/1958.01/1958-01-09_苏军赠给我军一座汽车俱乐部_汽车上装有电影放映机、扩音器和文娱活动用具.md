Title: Soviet Army Presents Our Army with a Mobile Auto Club with Movie Projector, Amplifier and Entertainment Tools
Author:
Date: 1958-01-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Soviet Army and Navy General Political Department presented the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army with a "Mobile Auto Club." On the 7th, at the presentation ceremony held in Beijing, General Tan Zheng, Director of the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army, accepted this valuable gift on behalf of the General Political Department.

This "Mobile Auto Club" was presented to the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army by the General Political Department of the Soviet Army and Navy during the visit of the Chinese military friendship delegation to Moscow, in order to express the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the people's armed forces of the Soviet Union and China, and to commemorate the visit of the Chinese military friendship delegation to the Soviet Union.

Speaking at the presentation ceremony on the 7th, Anoshin, representative of the General Political Department of the Soviet Army and Navy, said: I am honored to be entrusted by the General Political Department of the Soviet Army and Navy to present the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army with a friendly gift - a "Mobile Auto Club." Through it, we express our desire to further consolidate and develop the friendship between the armed forces of China and the Soviet Union. He said that the development of the armed forces of China and the Soviet Union is a guarantee of world peace.

General Tan Zheng said in his reply: You have sent us this precious gift from afar, which embodies the deep friendship between the people's armies of China and the Soviet Union. General Tan Zheng expressed his gratitude to the Soviet army and congratulated the close friendship between the people and armies of China and the Soviet Union for its growing development and consolidation.

This club is equipped on a "ГA3-51" car and has all the necessary supplies for a unit club. Among them are equipment for organizing night or day film screenings, broadcasting and amplification equipment, photo enlargement equipment (including mobile darkroom), and equipment for cultural and entertainment activities such as libraries, accordions, record players, propaganda tools, and game room tools. This mobile auto club is lightweight and flexible and is very suitable for use by decentralized units.

### 苏军赠给我军一座汽车俱乐部  汽车上装有电影放映机、扩音器和文娱活动用具

1958-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏军赠给我军一座汽车俱乐部
    汽车上装有电影放映机、扩音器和文娱活动用具
    新华社8日讯　苏联陆海军总政治部赠给中国人民解放军总政治部一座“汽车俱乐部”。7日，在北京举行的赠礼仪式上，解放军总政治部主任谭政大将代表总政治部接受了这份珍贵的礼物。
    这座“汽车俱乐部”是中国军事友好访苏代表团在莫斯科访问期间，苏联陆海军总政治部为了表示苏中两国人民武装力量牢不可破的友谊和合作，并且纪念中国军事友好访苏代表团访问苏联而赠送给中国人民解放军总政治部的。
    苏联陆海军总政治部代表阿诺申7日在赠礼仪式上致辞说：我荣幸地受苏联陆海军总政治部的委托，赠送给中国人民解放军总政治部一份友好礼物——“汽车俱乐部”。通过它，表达我们进一步巩固和发展中苏两国武装部队的友谊的愿望。他说，中苏两国武装力量的发展是世界和平的保证。
    谭政大将致答辞说：你们从迢迢万里之外送给我们这份珍贵的礼物，体现了中苏两国人民军队之间的深厚友谊。谭政大将向苏联军队表示谢意，并祝贺中苏两国人民及其军队之间的亲密友谊日益发展和巩固。
    这个俱乐部装备在一辆“ГA3——51”汽车上，有部队俱乐部使用的一切必需用品。其中有组织夜间或白天放映电影的设备，广播和扩音设备，照像放大设备（包括流动暗室），以及俱乐部进行文化娱乐活动的设备如图书馆、手风琴、电唱机、宣传用具、游艺室用具等。这座汽车俱乐部轻便灵活，很适合于分散部队使用。
