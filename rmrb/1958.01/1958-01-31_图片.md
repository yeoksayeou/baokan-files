    To facilitate teaching and save state expenditures, teachers at the Beijing Amateur Mechanical College have designed and manufactured many teaching aids for the school in their spare time. The picture shows a teacher experimenting with a model of a teaching aid with replaceable projection surfaces that has been manufactured. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chu Ying.
