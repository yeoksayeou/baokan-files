Title: Khrushchev Receives West German Journalists
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th—TASS reported: At the request of Axel Springer, publisher, and Hans Zehrer, editor-in-chief, of the West German Hamburg newspaper "Die Welt," First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received them on the 29th and held a lengthy conversation with them.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见西德记者

1958-01-31
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见西德记者
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫应西德汉堡“世界报”的发行人阿·施普林格和总编辑汉·泽勒的请求，在29日接见了他们，并且同他们进行了长时间的谈话。
