Title: Nehru Says China Has Only One Taiwan is Part of China
Author:
Date: 1958-01-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, January 25th: Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, told Hong Kong newspaper reporters on the 24th that the Indian government believes Taiwan is part of China. He said that as far as our position is concerned, there is only one China.

Ambassador R.K. Nehru, who has been recalled to his country, passed through Hong Kong on the 24th. He told Hong Kong newspaper reporters: For many years, the Indian government has strongly advocated that China's seat in the United Nations should be occupied by the legitimate government of China, that is, by the government of the People's Republic of China.

### 尼赫鲁大使说中国只有一个台湾是中国的一部分

1958-01-26
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁大使说中国只有一个台湾是中国的一部分
    据新华社香港25日电　印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁24日对香港报纸记者说，印度政府认为台湾是中国的一部分。他说，在我们的立场来说，中国只有一个。
    拉·库·尼赫鲁大使奉调回国，在24日路过香港。他对香港报纸记者说：若干年以来，印度政府便强烈主张在联合国的中国席位应该由合法的中国政府占有，也就是由中华人民共和国的政府占有。
