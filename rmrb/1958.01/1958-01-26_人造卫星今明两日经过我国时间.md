Title: Artificial Satellite Passes Over China Today and Tomorrow

Author:
Date: 1958-01-26
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 25th: The Zijinshan Astronomical Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences announced the times when the Soviet Union's second artificial satellite will pass over China on the 26th and 27th:

　　On the 26th, it will pass over Hohhot and Baotou at 18:54, and over Taiyuan, Yangquan, Shijiazhuang, and Beijing at 18:55. It will pass over Xuzhou, Bengbu, and Shangqiu at 18:56, over Nanjing, Wuhu, Yangzhou, and Zhenjiang at 18:57, over Hangzhou and Shanghai at 18:58, and over Wenzhou at 18:59.

　　It will pass over Urumqi at 20:32 and over Lhasa at 20:37.

　　On the 27th, it will pass over Liaoyang, Anshan, and Jinzhou at 17:58. It will pass over Lüda at 17:59.

　　It will pass over Yumen at 19:38, over Xining and Lanzhou at 19:39, over Chengdu at 19:41, over Chongqing, Luzhou, Guiyang, and Zunyi at 19:42, and over Nanning, Liuzhou at 19:43, and over Haikou at 19:44.

　　During these times, the people in the above-mentioned areas can see the satellite with the naked eye.

### 人造卫星今明两日经过我国时间

1958-01-26
第7版()
专栏：

    人造卫星今明两日经过我国时间
    新华社南京25日电　中国科学院紫金山天文台发布苏联第二颗人造卫星26日、27日经过中国的时刻：
    26日十八点五十四分经过呼和浩特、包头，五十五分经过太原、阳泉、石家庄、北京。十八点五十六分经过徐州、蚌埠、商丘，五十七分经过南京、芜湖、扬州、镇江，五十八分经过杭州、上海，五十九分经过温州。
    二十点三十二分经过乌鲁木齐，三十七分经过拉萨。
    27日十七点五十八分经过辽阳、鞍山、锦州。五十九分经过旅大。
    十九点三十八分经过玉门，三十九分经过西宁、兰州，四十一分经过成都，四十二分经过重庆、泸州、贵阳、遵义，四十三分经过南宁、柳州，四十四分经过海口。
    在这些时间内，上述地区人民可以用肉眼看到卫星。
