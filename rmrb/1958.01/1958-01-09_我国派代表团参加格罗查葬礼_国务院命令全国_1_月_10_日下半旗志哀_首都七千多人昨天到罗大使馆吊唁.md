Title: Our Country Sends Delegation to Attend Groza's Funeral. State Council Orders National Half-Mast Flag on January 10th to Express Condolences. Over Seven Thousand People in the Capital Paid Respects at Romanian Embassy Yesterday

Author:
Date: 1958-01-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th Dispatch: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and the State Council of the People's Republic of China have decided to send a government delegation of the People's Republic of China to attend the funeral of Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania. The delegation list is as follows:

    Leader: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China.

    Member: Burhan Shahidi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

    Ke Bonian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Romanian People's Republic.

Furthermore, Guo Moruo and Burhan Shahidi flew from Budapest to Bucharest on the 8th at noon. They arrived in Budapest after attending the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference in Cairo. Ke Bonian is already in Romania.

Xinhua News Agency, 8th Dispatch: The State Council of the People's Republic of China issued an order on January 8th, stipulating that on January 10th—the day of the funeral of Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic—all organs, troops, factories, mines, enterprises, schools, people's organizations in the provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government and the ships docked in ports where the People's Committees of the provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government and the People's Committees of cities are located, shall fly the flag at half-mast for one day to express condolences.

Xinhua News Agency, 8th Dispatch: On the 8th, over seven thousand people went to the Romanian Embassy in China to pay respects to Dr. Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania, who passed away.

The mourners, wearing black gauze, solemnly entered the embassy hall to observe a moment of silence in mourning before Chairman Groza's portrait.

Among those who went to pay their respects were heads of various departments of state organs, responsible staff members of the CPC Central Committee, heads of various democratic parties, people's organizations, and Beijing municipal organs, and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Inside and outside the embassy hall were many wreaths sent by various organs and organizations in the capital. Wreaths sent by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the State Council, the National Committee of the CPPCC, as well as Chairman Mao, Premier Zhou, and Chairman Liu, were placed on both sides of Chairman Groza's portrait.

Foreign envoys and diplomatic officials stationed in China also went to the Romanian Embassy on the 8th to pay respects to the deceased Chairman Groza.

### 我国派代表团参加格罗查葬礼  国务院命令全国1月10日下半旗志哀  首都七千多人昨天到罗大使馆吊唁

1958-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我国派代表团参加格罗查葬礼
    国务院命令全国1月10日下半旗志哀
    首都七千多人昨天到罗大使馆吊唁
    新华社8日讯　中国共产党中央委员会、中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会和中华人民共和国国务院，决定派出中华人民共和国政府代表团参加罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团主席彼德鲁·格罗查博士的葬礼。代表团名单如下：
    团长：郭沫若，中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长。
    团员：包尔汉，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席。
    柯柏年，中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使。
    又讯　郭沫若和包尔汉8日中午从布达佩斯乘飞机前往布加勒斯特。他们是在开罗参加了亚非人民团结大会后到达布达佩斯的。柯柏年已在罗马尼亚。
    新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国国务院1月8日发出命令，在1月10日——罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查博士殡葬的日子，全国各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会和各市人民委员会所在地的机关、部队、厂矿、企业、学校、人民团体和停在港内的轮船，一律下半旗一日志哀。
    新华社8日讯　8日，有七千多人到罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆吊唁罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团主席格罗查博士逝世。
    吊唁的人们戴着黑纱，肃穆地走进大使馆的大厅，向格罗查主席的遗像默哀悼念。
    前往吊唁的人中，有国家机关各部门的负责人，中共中央的负责工作人员，各民主党派、各人民团体、北京市各机关的负责人和首都各界人士。
    在大使馆大厅内外，摆着首都各机关、团体送的许多花圈。中国共产党中央委员会、全国人民代表大会常务委员会、国务院、政协全国委员会以及毛主席、周总理、刘委员长送的花圈，安放在格罗查主席遗像的两旁。
    各国驻中国的使节和外交官员8日也前往罗马尼亚大使馆，吊唁格罗查主席逝世。
