Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Saburo Nango
Author:
Date: 1958-01-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Vice Premier Chen Yi received Saburo Nango, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Japan-China Import and Export Association, this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were Ye Jizhuang and Liao Chengzhi.

### 陈毅副总理接见南乡三郎

1958-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见南乡三郎
    新华社30日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了日本日中输出入协会理事长南乡三郎。
    接见时在座的有叶季壮、廖承志。
