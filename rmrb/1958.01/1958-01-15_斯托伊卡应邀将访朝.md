Title: Stoica to Visit North Korea at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1958-01-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang – Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, recently invited Stoica, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, to visit Korea. Chairman Stoica has accepted the invitation.

### 斯托伊卡应邀将访朝

1958-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    斯托伊卡应邀将访朝
    据新华社平壤讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成最近邀请罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席斯托伊卡来朝鲜访问。斯托伊卡主席已经接受了这个邀请。
