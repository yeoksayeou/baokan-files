Title: Vietnamese Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-01-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, January 20th. A twenty-member trade delegation from the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Minister of Commerce Phan Anh, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon.
    Those who went to the station to welcome them included Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Ji-zhuang and Vice Minister Jiang Ming.
    Vietnamese Ambassador to China Nguyen Khang was also at the station to welcome them.

### 越南政府贸易代表团到北京

1958-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    越南政府贸易代表团到北京
  新华社20日讯　越南民主共和国政府贸易代表团一行二十人，在商业部部长潘英率领下，今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站迎接的有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长江明等。
    越南驻华大使阮康也到车站欢迎。
