Title: China and North Korea Sign This Year's Goods Exchange Protocol
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-22
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 21st - The trade delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK), headed by Park Young-bin, Vice Minister of Trade of the DPRK, conducted trade negotiations with the Ministry of Foreign Trade of our country in Beijing for the year 1958. Both sides have now reached a satisfactory agreement in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere and signed the protocol for the exchange of goods between the two countries for 1958 in Beijing on January 21st.
    This year's trade volume and the variety of goods mutually supplied by the two countries have increased compared to last year.
    According to the agreement between the two parties: China will supply North Korea with: coal, coke, cotton, cotton yarn, cotton cloth, various machines, steel rails, soybeans, chemical raw materials, etc. North Korea will supply China with: pig iron, iron sand, steel ingots, high-speed tool steel, carbon tool steel, electrolytic copper, electrolytic lead, electrolytic zinc, sulfate fertilizer, seafood products, medicinal materials, etc.
    The protocol was signed on behalf of China by Jiang Ming, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, and on behalf of North Korea by Park Young-bin, Vice Minister of Trade of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 中朝今年换货议定书签字

1958-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中朝今年换货议定书签字
    新华社21日讯　以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国对内外商业省副相朴永斌为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国贸易代表团同我国对外贸易部在北京进行了1958年度的贸易谈判。现双方已经在友好融洽的气氛中圆满达成协议，并于1月21日在北京签订了两国1958年货物交换的议定书。
    今年两国的贸易额和互相供应货物的品种都较去年有所增长。
    根据双方协议：中国供应朝鲜的物资有：煤炭、焦炭、棉花、棉纱、棉布、各种机器、钢轨、大豆、化工原料等。朝鲜供应中国的物资有：生铁、铁砂、钢锭、高速工具钢、炭素工具钢、电解铜、电解铅、电解锌、硫?肥料、海产品、药材等。
    代表中朝双方在议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长江明和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国对内外商业省副相朴永斌。
