Title: Contributing to the Steel Industry
Author: Hu Dingzhi, Zhang Fengde, Sun Minsheng
Date: 1958-01-20
Page: 7

    Contributing to the Steel Industry
    "Catching up with England in fifteen years" is a challenging and inspiring task. Steel and iron are the cause we will dedicate our lives to. We feel honored and proud of the cause we are engaged in, and at the same time feel a great responsibility. These will greatly encourage us to study harder, but more importantly, we must transform our thinking. We must diligently study Marxism-Leninism, participate in physical labor, and cultivate ourselves into proletarian intellectuals who are both red and expert. In order to enable our country's steel industry to reach an advanced scientific level and quickly surpass the production level of the old-fashioned imperialist country—England, we must dedicate all our youth and wisdom to this magnificent steel industry. Let the upsurge of construction boil like molten steel, and let this boiling molten steel flow to the various construction sites of the motherland!
                Students of Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel Technology
    Hu Dingzhi, Zhang Fengde, Sun Minsheng, et al. (5 people)

### 为钢铁事业贡献力量
胡鼎峙  张凤德  孙民生
1958-01-20
第7版()
专栏：

    为钢铁事业贡献力量
    “十五年赶上英国”，这是一个艰巨而又振奋人心的任务。钢和铁是我们终身从事的事业。我们为自己所从事的事业感到光荣和骄傲，同时更感到责任重大。这些将大大地鼓舞着我们更努力学习，但是更重要的是要好好地改造我们的思想。我们要努力学习马列主义，参加体力劳动，把自己培养成为一个又红又专的工人阶级知识分子。为了使祖国的钢铁事业达到先进的科学水平，尽速地超过老牌的帝国主义国家——英国的生产水平，我们要把自己全部的青春和智慧献给这宏伟的钢铁事业。让建设的高潮像钢水一样沸腾，让这沸腾的钢水流向祖国的各个建设工地！
                北京钢铁学院学生
    胡鼎峙、张凤德、孙民生等五人
