Title: Colombo Mass Rally Welcomes Soviet Relief Rice
Author:
Date: 1958-01-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, January 20th: A mass rally was held in the Colombo City Council Hall on the 20th at the initiative of several Ceylon trade union organizations to welcome the crew of the Soviet ship "Sevastopol," which transported rice donated by Soviet trade unions to aid residents of Ceylon affected by floods.

    Ceylon Trade Union Federation Chairman Mendis, in his speech, expressed gratitude to the Soviet trade unions for sending 2,500 tons of rice to Ceylon. He said that the gift from the Soviet people would further strengthen the friendship between the working people of the Soviet Union and Ceylon.

    Parliamentarian Keuneman emphasized in his speech that the gift from the Soviet working people was a manifestation of the principle of international solidarity. He requested the Soviet sailors to convey the gratitude of the Ceylonese people to the Soviet working people.

### 科伦坡群众集会  欢迎苏联运来救济米

1958-01-22
第5版()
专栏：

    科伦坡群众集会
    欢迎苏联运来救济米
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社科伦坡20日讯：由于锡兰一些工会组织的倡议，20日在科伦坡市议会大厅举行了群众大会，欢迎苏联“塞瓦斯托波尔”号船员，这艘船把苏联工会为救济锡兰遭受水灾的居民而赠送的大米运到了锡兰。
    锡兰工会联合会主席曼迪斯在讲话中对于给锡兰送来二千五百吨大米的苏联工会表示感谢。他说，苏联人民的礼物将促使苏联和锡兰两国劳动人民之间的友谊进一步加强。
    议会议员克尼曼在讲话中强调指出，苏联劳动人民的礼物是国际团结原则的表现。他请求苏联海员向苏联劳动人民转达锡兰人民的谢意。
