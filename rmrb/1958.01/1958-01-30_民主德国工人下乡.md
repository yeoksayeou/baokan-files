Title: East German Workers Go to the Countryside
Author:
Date: 1958-01-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 28th: According to the German News Agency, Berlin, on the 28th: Last year, 1,400 industrial workers settled in Neubrandenburg County, in East Germany. Since 1955, a total of 6,500 workers have responded to the slogan "Industrial Workers Go to the Countryside" and moved to rural areas of this county. Most of the workers who go to the countryside participate in the labor of agricultural production cooperative farming teams.

### 民主德国工人下乡

1958-01-30
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国工人下乡
    新华社29日讯　据德意志通讯社柏林28日讯：一千四百名产业工人去年在民主德国的一个县——新勃兰登堡县安家落户。1955年以来，一共有六千五百名工人响应“产业工人到农村去”的口号移居到这个县的农村里。大多数下乡的工人都参加农业生产合作社耕作队的劳动。
