Title: Vice Chairman of the U.S. President’s Science Advisory Committee Admits: Soviet Science and Technology Greatly Surpassed the United States

Author:
Date: 1958-01-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 17th: TASS reported that the U.S. President’s Science Advisory Committee published a statement by the committee's Vice Chairman, Dr. Walker, regarding the achievements of Soviet science and technology.

Walker said, "We have not fully realized the far-reaching consequences of Russia's tremendous scientific achievements." Walker cited many examples showing that the Soviet Union has greatly surpassed the United States in a range of scientific and technological fields, as well as in education.

He said that in 1928, the ratio of the number of graduates from scientific and technological specialized schools in the United States and the Soviet Union was three to two, with the United States taking the lead; now it is also three to two, but the Soviet Union is in the lead. Walker said that the Soviet Union now has more university students and engineers than the United States. The Soviet Union has concentrated its efforts on developing basic industrial sectors and has now achieved results.

Speaking of the production of consumer industrial goods in the Soviet Union, Walker particularly pointed out that Soviet televisions are of better quality and have better imaging than those in the United States. The work of Russian experts in color television also left a deep impression on American experts. Everyone says that the Russians are now a very high authority in electronics, a very important scientific field. Walker particularly emphasized that the Soviet Union has made astonishing achievements in various fields of basic theoretical research.

### 美国总统科学技术委员会副主席承认  苏联科学技术大大超过美国

1958-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    美国总统科学技术委员会副主席承认
    苏联科学技术大大超过美国
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社华盛顿17日讯：美国总统科学技术委员会发表了委员会副主席沃克博士关于苏联科学技术成就的声明。
    沃克说，我们还没有完全意识到俄国的巨大的科学成就的深远后果。沃克列举的许多例子表明，苏联在一系列科学技术方面以及在教育方面已经大大超过美国。
    他说，在1928年，美国和苏联的科学技术专门学校毕业生的数量的比例是三比二，美国占先；现在也是三比二，不过是苏联占先了。沃克说，现在苏联的大学生和工程师比美国多。苏联集中力量发展基本工业部门，现在已经获得了成果。
    谈到苏联的消费工业品生产时，沃克特别指出，苏联的电视机比美国的质量好，显影也好。俄国专家在彩色电视方面的工作也给美国专家留下了深刻的印象。大家都说，俄国人在电子学这门极重要的科学领域中现在是很高的权威了。沃克特别强调指出，苏联在基础理论研究的各个领域都取得了惊人的成就。
