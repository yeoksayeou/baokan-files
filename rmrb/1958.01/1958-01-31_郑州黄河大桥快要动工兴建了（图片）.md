Zhengzhou Yellow River Bridge to Start Construction Soon

The Zhengzhou Yellow River Bridge, with a total length of 2.8 kilometers, is about to start construction, and preparations for the bridge construction are underway.

The original Zhengzhou Yellow River Railway Bridge was a semi-permanent steel beam bridge built in 1904. Due to the opening of the Baoji-Chengdu and Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railways, and the construction of the double-tracking project of the Beijing-Guangzhou and Longhai Railways, the old Yellow River Bridge's capacity is far from meeting the busy transportation tasks.

Top Picture: Laying steel rails on the railway transportation sidings at the construction sites on the north and south banks of the bridge.
Bottom Picture: Bridge construction workers brave the severe cold of minus 15 degrees Celsius to persevere in their work.
Xinhua News Agency reporters Tang Maolin and Yang Zhenhe.

### 郑州黄河大桥快要动工兴建了（图片）
唐茂林  杨震河
1958-01-31
第3版()
专栏：

    郑州黄河大桥快要动工兴建了
    全长二点八公里的郑州黄河大桥，即将动工兴建，建桥的准备工作正在紧张地进行。
    原来的郑州黄河铁桥，是在一九○四年建成的半永久性钢梁桥。由于宝成、兰新铁路的通车，和京广、陇海铁路复线工程的建设，旧黄河大桥的通过能力已经远远不能担当繁忙的运输任务。

    下图：建桥职工冒着零下十五度的严寒，坚持工作
