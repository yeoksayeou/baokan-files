Title: Mexican Foreign Minister Opposes Linking the Organization of American States with the North Atlantic Group
Author:
Date: 1958-01-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, January 19th: Mexican Foreign Minister Padilla Nervo issued a sixteen-page statement on the 18th, firmly opposing any connection between the Organization of American States and the North Atlantic Treaty Organization.

In his statement, Nervo emphasized that the North Atlantic Treaty Organization is a "military bloc." He believes that the Organization of American States and the North Atlantic Treaty Organization have "fundamentally" different characteristics and that there is no common ground for them to enter into any form of political or military treaty.

### 墨外长反对美洲国家组织同北大西洋集团建立联系

1958-01-20
第5版()
专栏：

    墨外长反对美洲国家组织
    同北大西洋集团建立联系
    据新华社19日讯墨西哥城消息：墨西哥外交部长帕迪利亚·内尔沃18日发表一个长达十六页的声明，坚决反对美洲国家组织同北大西洋公约组织建立任何联系。
    内尔沃在声明中强调指出，北大西洋公约组织是一个“军事集团”，他认为美洲国家组织和北大西洋公约组织有着“基本上”的不同，这两者没有共同基础可以订立任何形式的政治或军事条约。
