Title: Trade Agreement Between China and Sweden Comes into Effect
Author:
Date: 1958-01-25
Page: 1

    Trade Agreement Between China and Sweden Comes into Effect
    Xinhua News Agency, January 24th. Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and the Swedish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Book, exchanged letters on the 23rd, notifying each government of the ratification of the "Trade Agreement between the People's Republic of China and Sweden." This agreement was signed in Stockholm on November 8, 1957, by representatives of the Chinese and Swedish governments. The agreement stipulates that it shall formally come into effect from the date of the exchange of notes.

### 我国和瑞典贸易协定开始生效

1958-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和瑞典贸易协定开始生效
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来同瑞典驻华特命全权大使布克在23日互相交换了函件，通知各该国政府已经批准“中华人民共和国和瑞典贸易协定”。这项协定是中国和瑞典政府代表于1957年11月8日在斯德哥尔摩签订的。协定规定自换文通知日起正式生效。
