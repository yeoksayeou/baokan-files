Title: New Year's Day in Lhasa: Ball Games, Song and Dance Soiree, Tibetan and Han Cadres Exchange New Year Greetings

Author: 
Date: 1958-01-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, January 2nd: Tibetan and Han cadres and workers from various agencies in Lhasa joyfully welcomed the first New Year of the motherland's Second Five-Year Plan.

　　On this day, many agency gates were decorated with red arches, red lanterns and colorful decorations, and huge banners celebrating the successful completion of the motherland's First Five-Year Plan. More than 800 Young Pioneers held a song and dance soiree in the beautiful Norbulingka. On the square in front of the Potala Palace, teams composed of Tibetan and Han athletes held ball games.

　　In the reception room of the CCP Tibet Working Committee, Tibetan guests came to pay their respects in an endless stream. The Kalons of the Tibet Local Government, officials from the Lhasa office of the Panchen Kanpo Lija Committee, and prominent figures from the Tibetan religious community all came to offer New Year greetings and wishes of good fortune to the responsible cadres of the CCP Tibet Working Committee. Many Tibetan and Han cadres who had established friendships through long-term joint work exchanged New Year's greetings with each other.

### 元旦在拉萨  球类比赛歌舞联欢  藏汉干部互贺新年

1958-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    元旦在拉萨
    球类比赛歌舞联欢  藏汉干部互贺新年
    据新华社拉萨2日电　拉萨各机关藏、汉族干部和工人欢乐地迎接了祖国第二个五年计划第一年的元旦。
    这天，许多机关门前装饰着红色牌坊、红灯绿彩和欢庆祖国第一个五年计划胜利完成的巨幅标语。八百多个少先队员在风景秀丽的林卡进行歌舞联欢。布达拉宫前的广场上，由藏、汉族运动员组成的球队举行了球赛。
    在中共西藏工委的会客室里，来拜年的藏族客人络绎不绝。西藏地方政府的噶伦、班禅堪布会议厅委员会驻拉萨的官员以及西藏宗教界的著名人士都先后到这里来和中共西藏工委的负责干部互祝新年吉祥如意。许多在长期共同工作中建立起友谊的藏、汉族干部都相互拜年。
