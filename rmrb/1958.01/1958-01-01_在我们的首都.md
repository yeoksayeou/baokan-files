Title: In Our Capital
Author: Shao Yu
Date: 1958-01-01
Page: 8

    In Our Capital
    (I)
    Shao Yu
    Souvenir

    In front of the solemn Tiananmen Square,
    There is a photography service station,
    People here are close to the embrace of the motherland,
    Taking a commemorative photo.
