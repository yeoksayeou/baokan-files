Title: Glorious Labor, Glorious People (Photo)
Author: Chen Juanmei
Date: 1958-01-09
Page: 3

    Glorious Labor, Glorious People
    Ying Zhongfa, a technician at the Shanghai Second Textile Machinery Factory who has long maintained the title of model worker and advanced producer, is a master of production and conservation. With the help of the Party and the masses, he has put forward and implemented 50 rationalization proposals in five years, increasing output by two to sixteen times, and also playing a significant role in improving product quality and ensuring safe production. This is him (left) and Shao Maolin studying the improved spiral blade die of the cotton seed delinting machine to save high-grade tool steel.
　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Juanmei photo

### 光荣的劳动  光荣的人（图片）
陈娟美
1958-01-09
第3版()
专栏：

    光荣的劳动  光荣的人
    长期保持劳动模范和先进生产者称号的上海第二纺织机械厂技师应忠发，是一个生产、节约能手。他在党和群众的帮助下，五年内提出并实现了的合理化建议有五十件，使产量提高了二倍到十六倍，对提高产品质量保证安全生产也起了不小作用。这是他（左）和邵茂林在研究改进棉籽剥绒机上的零件螺旋叶片冲模，节省高级工具钢。　
　　　新华社记者　陈娟美摄
