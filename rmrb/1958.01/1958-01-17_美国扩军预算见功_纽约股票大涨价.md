Title: US Increased Military Budget Proves Effective: New York Stock Prices Soar
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-17
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 15th: U.S. President Eisenhower's budget message on the 13th, requesting a large increase in appropriations to intensify the nuclear arms race, caused New York stock market prices to rise on the 14th. The stocks of steel, aircraft, missile, airline, shipping, and chemical companies, which are closely related to military expansion, rose the most.

### 美国扩军预算见功  纽约股票大涨价

1958-01-17
第5版()
专栏：

    美国扩军预算见功
    纽约股票大涨价
    新华社15日讯　纽约消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔13日提出的要求增加大量拨款来加紧核军备竞赛的预算咨文，使得14日纽约股票市场价格上涨。上涨得最多的是同扩军有密切关系的钢铁、飞机、导弹、航空公司、航运和化学企业的股票。
