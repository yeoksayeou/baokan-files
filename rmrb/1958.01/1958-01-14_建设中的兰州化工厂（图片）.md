Title: Lanzhou Chemical Plant Under Construction (Picture)
Author: Qi Jiang, Liu Qi
Date: 1958-01-14
Page: 3

    Lanzhou Chemical Plant Under Construction
    A powerful chemical industry complex, the Lanzhou Chemical Plant, is being built at an accelerated pace. This new type of complex will produce a large quantity of various fertilizers urgently needed for agriculture, industrial products needed for people's livelihood, as well as synthetic rubber and plastics with broad uses in industry.

    　Photo by Qi Jiang and Liu Qi (Draft from Gansu Daily)

### 建设中的兰州化工厂（图片）
其江  刘奇
1958-01-14
第3版()
专栏：

    建设中的兰州化工厂
    一个强大的化学工业联合企业——兰州化工厂，正在加紧建设。这座新型联合企业将大量生产农业上迫切需要的各种肥料，人民生活上需用的工业品，以及在工业上有广阔用途的合成橡胶和塑料。
    　其江、刘奇摄（甘肃日报社稿）
