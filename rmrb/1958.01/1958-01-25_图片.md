Title: Xu Zhicheng
Author: Xu Zhicheng
Date: 1958-01-25
Page: 1

First Automotive Works employees, while intensely producing cars, have recently trial-produced a 50-horsepower gas engine using coal as fuel to support agricultural construction. It will be used as a power source for agricultural machinery such as farmland irrigation and threshers. One thousand units will be produced and sent to the countryside before the spring plowing. This is the trial-produced gas engine undergoing performance testing, and the results are excellent.

　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Zhicheng photo

    第一汽车制造厂职工在紧张生产汽车的同时，为支援农业建设，最近试制出一种用煤做燃料的五十匹马力的煤气发动机，作为灌溉农田、打谷机等农业机械的动力。在春耕前将生产出一千台送到农村。这是试制出来的煤气发动机正在进行性能试验，效果很好。
