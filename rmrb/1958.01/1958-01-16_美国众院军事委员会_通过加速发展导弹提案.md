Title: U.S. House Military Committee Passes Proposal to Accelerate Missile Development
Author:
Date: 1958-01-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 15th. The U.S. House Military Committee on the 13th passed the government's proposal to use $549,670,000 to accelerate the missile development program and disperse the U.S. bomber fleet to more bases.

The bill passed by the Military Committee is part of Eisenhower's request for an additional $1.26 billion in "emergency defense funds" in the current 1958 fiscal year.

At the same time, Secretary of Defense McElroy said on the 13th that the United States will accelerate missile development in the 1959 fiscal year, establish new missile bases, and build submarines to launch missiles.

He admitted that although the U.S. government has ordered "rapid production" of two types of medium-range missiles, "neither has been fully tested." He said that the U.S.'s long-range missiles are "further from successful testing."

### 美国众院军事委员会  通过加速发展导弹提案

1958-01-16
第5版()
专栏：

    美国众院军事委员会
    通过加速发展导弹提案
    新华社15日讯　华盛顿消息：美国众议院军事委员会13日通过了政府提出的、用五亿四千九百六十七万美元加速发展导弹计划和分散美国轰炸机队到更多基地的计划。
    军事委员会通过的这个法案是艾森豪威尔要求在目前1958财政年度中追加十二亿六千万美元“紧急防御费用”主张中的一部分。
    同时，国防部长麦克艾罗伊在13日说，美国将在1959财政年度中加速发展导弹，建立新的导弹基地和建造发射导弹的潜水艇。
    他承认，虽然美政府已下令“赶快生产”两种中程导弹，但这“两种都还没有经过全面的试验”。他说，美国的远程导弹离试验成功“差得更远”。
