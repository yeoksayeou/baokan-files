Title: Training Pump Operators for Agricultural Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1958-01-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 29th: The Changzhou Machine Factory and the Wuxi Power Machinery Manufacturing Factory in Jiangsu Province will train a batch of pump operators for agricultural cooperatives and pumping stations in Jiangsu, Shandong, Henan, Liaoning and other places before the irrigation season.

　　The first group of 190 pump operators trained by these two factories are concentrated in Changzhou and Wuxi for study. The two factories specially sent technicians and experienced skilled workers to provide guidance. Qiu Shunan, chief engineer of Changzhou Machine Factory, also lectured to the students in his spare time.

　　Changzhou Machine Factory and Wuxi Power Machinery Factory also specially trained 110 technical workers, preparing to go to the countryside during the irrigation season to conduct mobile repairs and provide technical guidance.

### 为农业社培养抽水机司机

1958-01-30
第3版()
专栏：

    为农业社培养抽水机司机
    据新华社南京29日电　江苏省常州机器厂和无锡动力机制造厂在戽水季节以前，将为江苏、山东、河南、辽宁等地农业社和抽水机站培养出一批抽水机司机。
    这两个厂第一期培养的抽水机司机一百九十人，已在常州和无锡两地集中学习。这两个厂特地派了技术人员和有经验的技术工人进行辅导。常州机器厂主任工程师仇叔南还在业余时间为学员们讲课。
    常州机器厂和无锡动力机厂还专门培养了一百一十名技术工人，准备在戽水季节下乡巡回检修和进行技术指导。
