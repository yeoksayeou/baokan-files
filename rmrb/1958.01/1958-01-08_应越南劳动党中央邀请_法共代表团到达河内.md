Title: At the Invitation of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Delegation of the French Communist Party Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1958-01-08
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 7th – At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the delegation of the French Communist Party, led by维美徐 [Wéi Měixú], member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, arrived in Hanoi this morning for a visit to Vietnam.
    Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, 黎笋 [Lí Sǔn], 长征 [Cháng Zhēng], 武元甲 [Wǔ Yuánjiǎ], 黄文欢 [Huáng Wénhuān], 范雄 [Fàn Xióng], and Central Committee members 黄国越 [Huáng Guóyuè], 雍文谦 [Yōng Wénqiān], along with more than 2,000 people, welcomed the delegation at the airport.
    黎笋 [Lí Sǔn], on behalf of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, delivered a speech, warmly welcoming the delegation of the French Communist Party on their first visit to Vietnamese soil. In his speech, he thanked the French Communist Party and the French working class for their sympathy and strong support for the Vietnamese people's national liberation struggle over the past thirty years.
    维美徐 [Wéi Měixú] then delivered a speech, thanking the Vietnamese people for their warm welcome to the delegation. She said that the French colonialists' aggression and oppression of the Vietnamese people are gone forever. Today, the delegation of the French Communist Party has come to Vietnam as friends, brothers, and comrades. The delegation's visit to Vietnam is to strengthen the friendship between the peoples and parties of France and Vietnam and to support the Vietnamese people's struggle to realize national reunification in accordance with the Geneva Accords.
    Subsequently, the delegation left the jubilant Gia Lam Airport and entered the city, where they were welcomed by tens of thousands of Hanoi citizens lining the streets.
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th – The delegation of the French Communist Party, headed by 维美徐 [Wéi Měixú], member of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party Central Committee, left Beijing by special plane for Hanoi on the morning of the 7th for a visit to Vietnam lasting approximately ten days.
    Among those seeing the delegation off at the airport were 谭震林 [Tán Zhènlín], Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and 赵毅敏 [Zhào Yìmǐn], a responsible staff member of the organs of the CPC Central Committee.
    阮康 [Ruǎn Kāng], member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, also saw the delegation off at the airport.

### 应越南劳动党中央邀请  法共代表团到达河内

1958-01-08
第6版()
专栏：

    应越南劳动党中央邀请
    法共代表团到达河内
    新华社河内7日电　由法国共产党中央委员会政治局委员维美徐率领的法国共产党代表团在今天上午到达河内。代表团是应越南劳动党中央委员会的邀请到越南进行访问的。
    越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员黎笋、长征、武元甲、黄文欢、范雄和中央委员黄国越、雍文谦等两千多人到机场欢迎。
    黎笋代表越南劳动党中央委员会致词，热烈欢迎第一次踏上越南土地的法国共产党代表团。他在讲话中感谢法国共产党和法国工人阶级三十年来对越南人民的民族解放斗争的同情和有力支持。
    维美徐接着致词，感谢越南人民对代表团的热情欢迎。她说，法国殖民者对越南人民的侵略和压迫已经一去不复返了。今天，法国共产党代表团是以朋友、兄弟和同志的身份来到越南的。代表团这次访问越南，是为了加强法越两国人民和两党之间的友谊，也是为了支持越南人民根据日内瓦协议实现国家统一的斗争。
    随后，代表团离开欢腾的嘉林机场，进入市区，沿途受到成千成万河内市民的夹道欢迎。
    新华社7日讯　以法共中央政治局委员维美徐为首的法国共产党代表团在7日晨自北京乘专机前往河内，将在越南进行为期约十天的访问参观。
    到机场送行的，有中共中央书记处书记谭震林，中共中央机关负责工作人员赵毅敏。
    越南劳动党中央委员阮康也到机场送行。
