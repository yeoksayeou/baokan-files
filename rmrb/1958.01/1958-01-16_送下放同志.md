Title: Sending Down Comrades
Author: Zhang Xiu
Date: 1958-01-16
Page: 8

    Sending Down Comrades
            Do not rely solely on books to show off your talent,
  　　　　Acquire knowledge through practice! Pick up the sickle to remove weeds,
  　　　　Arrange the flat ground to build a high platform.
