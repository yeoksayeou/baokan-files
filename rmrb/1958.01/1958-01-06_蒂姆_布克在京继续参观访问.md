Title: Tim Buck Continues His Visit in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1958-01-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 5th: Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, has continued his sightseeing and visits in Beijing in recent days.

    Over the past week, Tim Buck visited the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory, visited the Central Institute for Nationalities, and also toured the Summer Palace, the Badaling Great Wall, the Ming Tombs, and the Forbidden City. In addition, Tim Buck also watched the Chinese national dance drama "The Lotus Lantern" and the documentary film "Ode to the Motherland."

### 蒂姆·布克在京继续参观访问

1958-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    蒂姆·布克在京继续参观访问
    新华社5日讯　加拿大劳工进步党总书记蒂姆·布克连日继续在北京进行参观访问。
    最近一周来，蒂姆·布克曾参观了国营北京第二棉纺织厂，访问了中央民族学院，还游览了颐和园、八达岭、明十三陵和故宫。此外，蒂姆·布克还观看了中国民族舞剧“宝莲灯”和纪录电影“祖国颂”。
