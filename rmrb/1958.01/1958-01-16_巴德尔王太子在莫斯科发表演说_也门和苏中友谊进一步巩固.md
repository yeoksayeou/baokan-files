Title: Crown Prince al-Badr Delivers Speech in Moscow: Friendship Between Yemen and Soviet-China Further Consolidated
Author:
Date: 1958-01-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 15th: According to TASS Moscow, Yemen's Deputy Prime Minister, Foreign Minister, and Defense Minister Crown Prince al-Badr, who was passing through Moscow, delivered a televised broadcast speech in Moscow on January 14th.

    He pointed out the warm reception he received in the People's Republic of China and the further consolidation of the friendship between China and Yemen.

    Badr continued by saying that the Yemeni people and the entire Arab world will forever remember with gratitude the position taken by the Soviet government and the Soviet people when Egypt suffered the treacherous tripartite aggression and when Yemen rose up to defend its right to territorial integrity, as well as the position taken by the Soviet Union in dealing with the imperialists' conspiracy against fraternal Syria, in dealing with Arab issues, and in dealing with issues of freedom and peace.

    Badr concluded by expressing his satisfaction with the Soviet people's hospitality, friendly expressions, and willingness to further strengthen mutual understanding and cooperation between the Soviet Union and Yemen.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 15th: Yemen's Crown Prince, Deputy Prime Minister, Defense Minister, and Foreign Minister al-Badr left Moscow this morning.

    Seeing Crown Prince al-Badr off at Vnukovo Airport were Soviet Communist Party and government leaders Kosygin and Biryuzov, as well as Georadze, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Semyonov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and others.

### 巴德尔王太子在莫斯科发表演说  也门和苏中友谊进一步巩固

1958-01-16
第6版()
专栏：

    巴德尔王太子在莫斯科发表演说
    也门和苏中友谊进一步巩固
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：路过莫斯科的也门副首相兼外交大臣、国防大臣巴德尔王太子1月14日在莫斯科发表了电视广播演说。
    他指出了他在中华人民共和国受到的热情接待和中国同也门的友谊的进一步巩固。
    巴德尔接着说，也门人民和全阿拉伯世界将永远以感激的心情怀念着苏联政府和苏联人民当埃及遭受背信弃义的三方面的侵略和也门遭到侵略而起来保卫自己的领土完整的权利的时候所采取的立场，以及苏联在对待帝国主义者对兄弟般的叙利亚的阴谋、对待阿拉伯问题和自由与和平的问题上所采取的立场。
    巴德尔最后对苏联人的好客、友好的表示以及愿意更进一步加强苏联同也门之间的相互谅解和合作，感到满意。
    新华社莫斯科15日电　也门王太子、副首相兼国防大臣和外交大臣巴德尔今天早晨离开莫斯科。
    到伏努科夫机场欢送巴德尔王太子的有苏联共产党和政府的领导人柯西金和别尔乌辛以及苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书格奥尔加泽和苏联外交部副部长谢苗诺夫等人。
