Title: President of the Japan-China Export-Import Association, Saburo Nango, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: At the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Saburo Nango, President of the Japan-China Export-Import Association, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and the heads of the Chinese Salt Industry Delegation, the China Silk Corporation, the China National Import and Export Corporation, and the China National Foodstuffs Export Corporation visiting groups who visited Japan last year.

After a short stay in Beijing, Saburo Nango will go to Guangzhou to attend the opening ceremony of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition on February 1st.

### 日中输出入协会理事长南乡三郎到京

1958-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    日中输出入协会理事长南乡三郎到京
    新华社26日讯　应中国国际贸易促进委员会的邀请，日中输出入协会理事长南乡三郎今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民，和去年到日本访问的中国盐业代表团、中国丝绸公司、中国进出口公司、中国食品出口公司访日团的团长等人。
    南乡三郎准备在北京作短时期的逗留后，到广州参加2月1日举行的日本商品展览会开幕典礼。
