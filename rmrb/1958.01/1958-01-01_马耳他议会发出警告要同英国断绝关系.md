Title: Maltese Parliament Issues Warning to Sever Relations with Britain
Author:
Date: 1958-01-01
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Valletta, December 31st: The Maltese Parliament, at the request of Prime Minister Mintoff, unanimously passed a resolution on the evening of the 30th, warning of severing relations with Britain.

The resolution stated that the representatives of the Maltese people in parliament declared that Malta "is no longer bound by agreements and obligations to the British government and its allies" unless the British government guarantees employment opportunities for the dismissed British naval dockyard workers on the island.

The island of Malta is a direct colony of Britain and an important British military base in the Mediterranean. The British naval dockyard here employs approximately seven thousand Maltese workers.

### 马耳他议会发出警告要同英国断绝关系

1958-01-01
第5版()
专栏：

    马耳他议会发出警告
    要同英国断绝关系
    据新华社31日讯　瓦里塔消息：马耳他议会应总理明托夫的要求，在30日晚间一致通过了一个决议，警告要同英国断绝关系。
    决议说，马耳他人民在议会中的代表宣布，马耳他“不再受对英国政府和它的盟国的协定和义务的约束”，除非英国政府保证这个岛上被解雇的英国海军船坞工人有就业机会。
    马耳他岛是英国的直辖殖民地，也是英国在地中海的重要军事基地。这里的英国海军船坞雇用了约七千名马耳他工人。
