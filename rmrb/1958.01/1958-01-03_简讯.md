Date: 1958-01-03
Page: 6

    Brief News
    Prime Minister Siroky Departs for Visit to India
    Prague Dispatch: Czechoslovak Prime Minister Siroky and his entourage left here by special plane at 9 a.m. on the 2nd for a visit to India.
    In addition to visiting India, Prime Minister Siroky and others will also visit Cambodia, Burma, Indonesia, and Ceylon.
    Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Arrives in Burma for Visit
    Rangoon Dispatch: The Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation arrived in Rangoon on the 1st aboard a Tu-104 passenger plane at the invitation of the Speakers of both Houses of the Burmese Parliament. The head of the delegation is Tarasov, Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federated Socialist Republic.
    U Nu to Visit Egypt
    Rangoon Dispatch: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu said in Rangoon on December 30, 1957, that he will visit Egypt on March 11. He said that this time he will not visit Israel because he had previously visited that country.
    Laotian Prime Minister Phouma Arrives in Saigon
    Saigon Dispatch: Laotian Prime Minister Prince Phouma arrived in Saigon on the 2nd, beginning his foreign visit. After visiting South Vietnam, Phouma will continue his visits to France, Britain, the United States, and Canada.

### 简讯

1958-01-03
第6版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    西罗基总理启程赴印度访问
    布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基和随行人员2日上午九时乘专机离开这里前往印度访问。
    西罗基总理等除访问印度外，还将访问柬埔寨、缅甸、印度尼西亚和锡兰。
    苏最高苏维埃代表团抵缅访问
    仰光讯：苏联最高苏维埃代表团应缅甸国会两院议长的邀请，在1日下午乘图—一○四客机到达仰光。代表团团长是苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席、俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫。
    吴努将访问埃及
    仰光讯：缅甸总理吴努1957年12月30日在仰光说，他将在3月11日访问埃及。他说，这次他将不访问以色列，因为他以前曾经访问过这个国家。
    老挝首相富马到达西贡
    西贡讯：老挝首相富马亲王在2日到达西贡，开始了他的出国访问。富马在访问南越以后，将继续到法国、英国、美国和加拿大进行访问。
