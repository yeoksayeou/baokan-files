Title: India Holds National Memorial for the Tenth Anniversary of Gandhi's Martyrdom
Author:
Date: 1958-01-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 30th - India held two minutes of silence nationwide today to commemorate the tenth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi's martyrdom.

    At Raj Ghat, Gandhi's cremation site, President Prasad, Prime Minister Nehru, and other Indian leaders paid tribute to Gandhi's contributions. A prayer ceremony and mass spinning activity (Gandhi advocated for the hand-spinning movement during his lifetime) were also held there this morning.

    Similar commemorative ceremonies were held in the capitals of Indian states and other places.

    In memory of Gandhi, the first volume of "The Collected Works of Mahatma Gandhi" was also published today.

### 印全国悼念甘地殉难十周年

1958-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

    印全国悼念甘地殉难十周年
    新华社新德里30日电　印度全国各地今天为悼念圣雄甘地殉难十周年举行两分钟的默哀。
    在拉志加特甘地火葬场，普拉沙德总统、尼赫鲁总理和印度其他领导人对甘地的贡献表示敬意。今天上午在那里还举行了祈祷仪式和群众纺织活动（甘地生前提倡手工纺织运动）。
    印度各邦首府及其他地方都举行了类似的纪念仪式。
    为了纪念甘地，今天还出版了“圣雄甘地文集”第一卷。
