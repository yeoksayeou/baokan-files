Title: In areas near Cambodia, South Vietnamese authorities create a no-man's land
Author:
Date: 1958-01-28
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 26th - According to the Vietnamese-language Cambodian newspaper "Coexistence," on December 19, 1957, the South Vietnamese authorities, in the area near Cambodia along the strategic highway connecting Zhu Du and Longping, forced local residents to demolish their homes and cut down all trees, creating a 100-meter-wide no-man's land.

### 在接近柬埔寨的地区  南越当局制造无人区

1958-01-28
第5版()
专栏：

    在接近柬埔寨的地区
    南越当局制造无人区
    据新华社河内26日电　据越南通讯社今天引述柬埔寨越南文报纸“共处报”的消息说，1957年12月19日，南越当局在接近柬埔寨的连接朱笃和隆平的战略公路沿线地区，强迫当地居民拆除他们的住宅，砍伐一切树木，制造一个宽一百公尺的无人区。
