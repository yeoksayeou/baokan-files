Title: China and Mongolia Sign Civil Aviation Technical Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-01-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, January 24th: The Civil Aviation Administration of China and the Aviation Transportation Administration of the Ministry of Military and Public Security of the Mongolian People's Republic signed a civil aviation technical cooperation agreement and an aviation transportation and mutual service protocol in Ulaanbaatar this afternoon.

### 中蒙签订民航技术合作协定

1958-01-25
第6版()
专栏：

    中蒙签订民航技术合作协定
    据新华社乌兰巴托24日电　中华人民共和国民用航空局和蒙古人民共和国军事和公安部航空交通管理局今天下午在乌兰巴托签订民用航空技术合作协定及航空运输和互相服务议定书。
