Title: US Issues Threatening “Protest” Against Indonesian Decision on Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1958-01-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 18th dispatch from Washington: The U.S. State Department issued a statement on the 17th, lodging an unreasonable "protest" against the Indonesian government's decision regarding the scope of its territorial waters.

The Indonesian government announced on December 14th of last year that all sea areas surrounding Indonesian islands, between Indonesian islands, and connecting the islands are entirely part of Indonesia's inland or territorial waters within its absolute sovereign jurisdiction. This decision by the Indonesian government will greatly inconvenience imperialist ships that have recklessly roamed and engaged in conspiracies among the Indonesian islands in the past.

Under the guise of "freedom of the high seas," the State Department's statement attempts to oppose the Indonesian government's decision on territorial sovereignty, labeling it as "illegal." It stated, not without a threat, that "the United States will view the implementation of this decision with grave concern."

### 对印度尼西亚关于领海范围的决定  美国提出威胁性的“抗议”

1958-01-19
第5版()
专栏：

    对印度尼西亚关于领海范围的决定
    美国提出威胁性的“抗议”
    新华社18日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务院17日发表一项声明，对印度尼西亚政府作出的关于领海范围的决定，提出无理的“抗议”。
    印度尼西亚政府在去年12月14日宣布，围绕印度尼西亚各岛屿的、在印度尼西亚各岛屿之间和联结各岛屿的海面，一律是印度尼西亚绝对主权范围内的内海或领海的一部分。印度尼西亚政府的这一决定将给过去在印度尼西亚各岛屿间肆无忌惮地横行和进行阴谋活动的帝国主义舰只造成极大不便。
    国务院的声明在“公海自由”的幌子下，企图面对印度尼西亚政府的关于领海主权的决定，把这一决定说成是“非法的”。它不无威胁地说，“美国将以严重的关怀”看待这个决定的执行。
