Title: Riding the Clouds and Mist
Author: Zhuge Zifang
Date: 1958-01-24
Page: 8

    Riding the Clouds and Mist
  　Zhuge Zifang
    The white clouds are tumbling and rolling in the sky,
    A girl is riding the clouds and mist in the cloud layer.
    Hey! How can a girl without wings reach the edge of the clouds?
    It turns out she's picking white cotton in a hundred-jin cotton field.
                  (Lanxi, Zhejiang)

### 腾云驾雾
诸葛子房
1958-01-24
第8版()
专栏：来自农村的诗

    腾云驾雾
  　诸葛子房白云在天际又翻又滚，姑娘腾云驾雾在云层。嗨！姑娘无翅膀哪能到云边？原来是在百斤棉田里拣白棉。
                  （浙江兰溪）
