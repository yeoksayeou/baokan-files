Title: Tim Buck Leaves Beijing for Shanghai Visit
Author:
Date: 1958-01-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, accompanied by An Ziwen, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, left by plane on the morning of the 17th to visit Shanghai.
    Prior to this, Tim Buck, accompanied by An Ziwen, visited Xi'an, Zhengzhou, and Luoyang, where he toured many factories, schools, and agricultural production cooperatives. He also met with large numbers of workers, students, and farmers, and was warmly welcomed by them.

### 蒂姆·布克离北京去上海访问

1958-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    蒂姆·布克离北京去上海访问
    新华社17日讯　加拿大劳工进步党总书记蒂姆·布克由中共中央委员安子文陪同，在17日晨乘飞机前往上海参观访问。
    在此之前，蒂姆·布克在安子文陪同下，曾到西安、郑州和洛阳等地，参观许多工厂、学校和农业生产合作社，并且同广大工人、学生和农民见了面，受到他们的热烈欢迎。
