Title: Indian Ambassador to China Nehru Recalled Home; Premier Zhou Enlai Held Farewell Banquet for Him
Author:
Date: 1958-01-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd -- Indian Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, R.K. Nehru, recalled to his home country, departed Beijing by plane on the 23rd with his wife. Those seeing him off at the airport included Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu and his wife, Director of the Asian Department Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Ma Zhenwu, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Chen Hansheng, as well as personnel from the Indian Embassy in China, diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.
    Before leaving Beijing, Ambassador R.K. Nehru paid farewell visits to Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Foreign Ministers Zhang Wentian, Zhang Hanfu, and responsible persons from other relevant departments.
    At noon on the 12th, Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a banquet to bid farewell to Ambassador R.K. Nehru and his wife. President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, and Vice President of the All-China Women's Federation, Shi Liang, also held farewell receptions and tea parties for Ambassador R.K. Nehru and his wife, respectively.
    On the afternoon of the 21st, Ambassador R.K. Nehru and his wife held a farewell reception. Vice Chairman Zhu De and his wife, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, and Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Bao Erhan, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Vice Foreign Ministers Zhang Wentian and Zhang Hanfu, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Ding Xilin, and responsible persons from various relevant departments attended the reception. Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China were also invited to the reception.

### 印度驻华大使尼赫鲁奉调回国  周恩来总理曾设宴为他饯行

1958-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    印度驻华大使尼赫鲁奉调回国
    周恩来总理曾设宴为他饯行
    新华社23日讯　印度共和国驻华特命全权大使拉·库·尼赫鲁奉调回国，已在23日和他的夫人乘飞机离开北京。到机场送行的，有外交部副部长章汉夫和夫人，亚洲司司长章文晋，礼宾司副司长马振武和中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙，以及印度驻华大使馆人员，各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    拉·库·尼赫鲁大使在离开北京前，曾分别向朱德副主席，周恩来总理，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫和其他有关部门的负责人作了辞行拜会。
    12日午间，周恩来总理设宴为拉·库·尼赫鲁大使及夫人饯行。中印友好协会会长丁西林和全国妇联副主席史良，也分别为拉·库·尼赫鲁大使和夫人举行了欢送酒会和茶会。
    21日下午，拉·库·尼赫鲁大使和夫人举行了辞行酒会。朱德副主席和夫人，李济深、郭沫若、黄炎培副委员长、陈毅副总理和夫人，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉、国务院秘书长习仲勋、外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫，中印友好协会会长丁西林和各有关部门负责人出席了酒会。各国驻华使节和外交官也应邀参加了酒会。
