Title: Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Conference Believes That Afro-Asian Representation in the United Nations Is Insufficient and Appropriate Measures Should Be Taken to Correct It
Author:
Date: 1958-01-03
Page: 5

Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Conference Believes
    Afro-Asian Representation in the United Nations Is Insufficient and Appropriate Measures Should Be Taken to Correct It
    Resolution of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Conference on the United Nations

    The Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Conference, noting the insufficient representation of Afro-Asian countries in various United Nations bodies, recommends that appropriate action be taken to correct this disproportion.

    The Conference demands that the legitimate and rightful place of China in the United Nations must be restored. No important international issue, especially Far Eastern issues, can be reasonably resolved without the active participation of the People's Republic of China.

    The Conference recognizes Mongolia's legitimate right to join the United Nations and demands that Mongolia be admitted to the United Nations immediately.

      (The above is the full text of part of the resolution of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Conference. Resolutions on economic, cultural, social and other items will be published tomorrow.)

### 亚非人民团结大会认为  联合国中亚非代表权不充分  应该采取适当措施予以纠正

1958-01-03
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非人民团结大会认为
    联合国中亚非代表权不充分  应该采取适当措施予以纠正
    亚非人民团结大会关于联合国问题的决议
    亚非人民团结大会注意到联合国各个机构中亚非国家代表权不充分的现象，建议采取适当的行动来纠正这种不相称的现象。
    大会要求：必须恢复中国在联合国中的合法和正当的地位。没有中华人民共和国的积极参加，任何重要的国际问题，特别是远东问题，不可能得到合理的解决。
    大会承认蒙古加入联合国的合法权利，并且要求立即接纳蒙古加入联合国。
      （以上是亚非人民团结大会的部分决议全文，还有经济、文化、社会等项决议，明日续登。）
