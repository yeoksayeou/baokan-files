Title: Široký and U Nu Issue Joint Statement Resolving to Promote the Conclusion of a General Disarmament Agreement. Czechoslovak Government Delegation Arrives in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1958-01-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 24th: Czechoslovak Premier Viliam Široký and Burmese Prime Minister U Nu stated in a joint statement issued here today that "In order to save mankind and civilization from terrible disasters and destruction, disarmament, including the absolute prohibition of the testing, production, and use of nuclear and thermonuclear weapons, is imperative." The statement said that both Prime Ministers expressed their determination "to do everything possible to contribute to the conclusion of a general disarmament agreement." The two Prime Ministers believe that the problems in the Middle East can only be resolved through consultations with the countries directly concerned.

The joint statement emphasized that continuing to deny the People's Republic of China its legitimate seat in the United Nations seriously hinders the full development of this world organization. The two Prime Ministers pledged to make every effort to restore the People's Republic of China's legitimate seat in the United Nations as soon as possible.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 24th: Czechoslovak Premier Viliam Široký, Foreign Minister David, and their entourage concluded their four-day friendly visit to Burma this morning and left here by special plane for Jakarta, the capital of Indonesia.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 24th: Czechoslovak Premier Viliam Široký and his entourage arrived in Jakarta this afternoon. Premier Široký and his party will be visiting Indonesia for a week at the invitation of the Indonesian government.

### 西罗基和吴努发表联合声明  决心促成缔结总的裁军协定  捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团到达雅加达

1958-01-25
第6版()
专栏：

    西罗基和吴努发表联合声明
    决心促成缔结总的裁军协定
    捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团到达雅加达
    据新华社仰光24日电　捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基和缅甸总理吴努今天在这里发表的一项联合声明中说，“为了拯救人类和文明免遭可怕的灾难和毁灭，包括实行绝对禁止核和热核战争武器的试验、生产和使用在内的裁军，是刻不容缓的”。这项声明说，两位总理都表示决心“要竭尽所能对缔结一项总的裁军协定作出贡献”。两位总理认为，中东地区的问题只有通过直接有关的各国的协商，才能获得解决。
    联合声明强调说，继续不让中华人民共和国恢复它在联合国中的合法席位这一点，严重地妨碍了这个世界组织的充分发展。两国总理保证尽一切努力，尽早恢复中华人民共和国在联合国的合法席位。
    据新华社仰光24日电　捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基、外交部长戴维和他们的随行人员今天早晨结束了在缅甸为时四天的友好访问，乘专机离开这里前往印度尼西亚首都雅加达。
    据新华社雅加达24日电　捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基和他的随行人员在今天下午到达雅加达。西罗基总理一行应印度尼西亚政府的邀请，将在印度尼西亚进行为期一周的访问。
