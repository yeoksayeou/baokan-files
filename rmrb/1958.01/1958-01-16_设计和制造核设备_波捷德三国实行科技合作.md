Title: Design and Manufacture of Nuclear Equipment: Scientific and Technological Cooperation between Poland, Czechoslovakia, and East Germany
Author:
Date: 1958-01-16
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 14th: According to a report by the Polish Press Agency, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and the German Democratic Republic signed a protocol in Warsaw on the 13th regarding scientific and technical cooperation in the design and manufacture of nuclear equipment.

The protocol stipulates that the three countries will exchange experience in the design and manufacture of nuclear equipment, exchange experts and scientific documents in this field, and participate in conferences and congresses related to the manufacture of scientific installations for the peaceful use of nuclear energy.

Experts in the peaceful use of atomic energy from Czechoslovakia and the German Democratic Republic visited Poland from January 7th to 13th. The aforementioned protocol was signed after their visit.

### 设计和制造核设备  波捷德三国实行科技合作

1958-01-16
第6版()
专栏：

    设计和制造核设备
    波捷德三国实行科技合作
    新华社华沙14日电　据波兰通讯社报道，波兰、捷克斯洛伐克和德意志民主共和国13日在华沙签订了三国在设计和制造核设备方面进行科学和技术合作的议定书。
    议定书规定，三国将交流设计和制造核设备的经验、交换这方面的专家和科学文件并且参加有关制造和平利用核能的科学装置的会议和大会。
    捷克斯洛伐克和德意志民主共和国的和平利用原子能专家曾在1月7日到13日访问了波兰。上述议定书就是在他们访问结束后签订的。
