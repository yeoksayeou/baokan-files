Title: Picture
Author: Ma Qikui
Date: 1958-01-24
Page: 4

    More than 1,000 officers and soldiers of a certain unit stationed in Wenchang County, Hainan Island, spent nine days last year helping Xiadong Village, where they were stationed, build a dam eight meters high and 157 meters long. The people of Xiadong Village named it "Liberation Army Dam." The upper left picture shows the officers and soldiers fighting on the Xiadong Reservoir dam. The upper right picture shows the "Liberation Army Dam" in Xiadong Village, which can deliver water to the fields according to the required amount of water after it is completed. Contribution from the PLA Pictorial. Photo by Ma Qikui.
