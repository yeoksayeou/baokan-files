Title: Soviet Miners Set World Tunneling Record
Author:
Date: 1958-01-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 29th: Three shifts of workers at the "Baidaevskiy Inclined Shaft" in the Kuzbass coal region of Siberia, USSR, set a new world record for tunneling in mine conveyor roadways on January 23rd.

The three shifts, led by Rejer, Rezhnitsky, and Obraztsov, respectively, used the "ПКг—3" combined tunneling machine to excavate 154 meters of conveyor roadway in one 24-hour period. Previously, it would typically take a tunneling team a whole month to excavate this length of conveyor roadway.

This is not the first time that Soviet coal miners have set various world records. The record set by the miners of the "Baidaevskiy Inclined Shaft" is the fourth world record set by Soviet miners in the past year.

### 苏联矿工创造掘进世界纪录

1958-01-30
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联矿工创造掘进世界纪录
    据新华社莫斯科29日电　苏联西伯利亚库兹巴斯煤区的“巴伊达耶夫斯基斜井”的三个轮班工作队，在1月23日创造了矿井运输机巷道掘进的世界新纪录。
    由雷捷尔、列兹尼茨基和奥布拉兹卓夫分别领导的这三个轮班工作队在一昼夜间用“ПКг—3”联合掘进机挖了一百五十四公尺的运输机长巷道。过去，一个掘进工作队通常要工作一个月才能够挖出这样长的运输机巷道。
    苏联煤矿工人创造各种世界纪录已经不是第一次了。“巴伊达耶夫斯基斜井”矿工这次创造的纪录是苏联矿工在最近一年中创造的第四个世界纪录。
