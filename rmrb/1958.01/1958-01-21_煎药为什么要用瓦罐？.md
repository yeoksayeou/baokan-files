Title: Why Use Earthenware Pots for Decoction?
Author: Liu Youchang (Liú Yǒucháng)
Date: 1958-01-21
Page: 8

Why Use Earthenware Pots for Decoction?

　　When decocting Chinese medicine, earthenware pots are generally used. Why not iron pots or other metal pots? There is a scientific reason for this.

　　Most Chinese medicines contain a chemical component called tannic acid. When tannic acid encounters iron or other heavy metals, it will generate water-insoluble iron tannate or other tannates, resulting in a significant loss of tannic acid in the Chinese medicine. Tannic acid, in some medications, is used as an effective ingredient. On the other hand, Chinese medicine often contains another class of effective ingredients called "alkaloids," which must combine with tannic acid or other organic acids to form salts in order to dissolve in water. If a lot of tannic acid is lost, it will affect the amount of alkaloids that can be utilized, thereby reducing the therapeutic effect of the medicine. Using earthenware pots for decoction can avoid these situations. (Liu Youchang)

### 煎药为什么要用瓦罐？
刘有常
1958-01-21
第8版()
专栏：为什么

    煎药为什么要用瓦罐？
    煎中药一般总是用瓦罐，为什么不用铁锅或其他金属锅呢？这里面有科学道理。
    多数中药里都含有一种叫做鞣酸的化学成分。鞣酸遇上铁或者其他重金属时，会生成不溶于水的鞣酸铁或其他鞣酸盐，中药中的鞣酸就遭到很多损失。鞣酸在某些药物中，是作为有效成分应用的。另方面，中药中时常会有另一类被称做“生物鹼”的有效成分，它必需和鞣酸或其他有机酸结合成盐才能溶解于水。如果鞣酸损失很多，就会影响生物碱可能被利用的数量，从而减低了药物的治疗效果。采用瓦罐煎药就可以避免这些情况了。　（刘有常）
