Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Council Holds Meeting; Andreyev Reports on Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1958-01-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 11th: The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Council held a meeting in Moscow on the 10th. Andreyev, Chairman of the Council, reported at the meeting on the recent visit to China by the delegation he led. Andreyev emphasized that the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association had vigorously carried out work in China aimed at consolidating the friendship between the two great nations, and extensively publicized the experience of Soviet socialist construction. We should learn from their experience and strengthen our contacts and ties with them.

The Council meeting discussed the work organization of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Council and formulated a work plan for the near future. In addition, reports were heard on the charter of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, the work of the editorial department of the "Soviet-Chinese Friendship" magazine, and the various measures to be taken to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance on February 14th of this year.

### 苏中友协理事会举行会议  安德烈耶夫报告访华之行

1958-01-12
第6版()
专栏：

    苏中友协理事会举行会议
    安德烈耶夫报告访华之行
    据新华社莫斯科11日电　苏中友好协会理事会10日在莫斯科举行了会议。理事会主席安德烈耶夫在会上报告了他所率领的代表团不久前到中国访问的情况。安德烈耶夫着重指出，中苏友好协会在中国大力开展了旨在巩固两大国之间的友谊的工作，广泛宣传苏联社会主义建设的经验。我们应该吸取他们的经验，加强我们同他们的接触和联系。
    理事会会议讨论了苏中友好协会理事会的工作组织，拟定了最近时期的工作计划。此外还听取了关于苏中友协章程、“苏中友好”杂志编辑部工作以及为庆祝今年2月14日中苏友好同盟互助条约签订八周年准备采取的各项措施的报告。
