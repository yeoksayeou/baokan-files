Title: Large Parts of Urban and Rural Xinjiang Launch Health Campaign
Author:
Date: 1958-01-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, 16th - An patriotic health campaign, focusing on eliminating the "Four Pests" and accumulating fertilizer, has been launched in most cities and rural areas of Xinjiang. In Urumqi, during the patriotic health campaign shock month launched in December of last year, over 66,000 rats were caught. In Huocheng County, Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture, more than 140,000 rats were caught and killed in early December of last year. The Hami Special District, in conjunction with the fertilizer accumulation campaign, launched patriotic health activities from December 15th of last year. The "Four Pests" elimination activities in Aksu and Kashgar Special Districts in southern Xinjiang also began before the New Year. The Hotan Special District is preparing to launch a patriotic health campaign shock month throughout the entire special district before the Spring Festival.

### 新疆大部城乡开展卫生运动

1958-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    新疆大部城乡开展卫生运动
    据新华社乌鲁木齐16日电　以除四害、积肥为主的爱国卫生运动，已在新疆绝大部分城市和农村中展开。乌鲁木齐市在去年12月展开的爱国卫生运动突击月中，仅捕鼠一项就达六万六千多只。伊犁哈萨克族自治州霍城县，在去年12月上旬捕鼠、灭鼠十四万多只。哈密专区从去年12月15日起，结合积肥运动，展开了爱国卫生活动。南疆阿克苏、喀什专区的除四害活动在年前也已展开。和阗专区准备在春节前在全专区展开爱国卫生运动突击月。
