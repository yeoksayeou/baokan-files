Author:
Date: 1958-01-14
Page: 2

Yicheng Wulian Cooperative Sets National High Record for Wheat and Cotton Double Cropping Yield Per Mu

Xinhua News Agency report: In 1957, the Fourteenth Production Team of the Wulian Agricultural Cooperative in Yicheng County, Hubei Province, harvested an average of 1,983.3 jin of wheat and seed cotton per mu on 2.5 mu of land, surpassing the record of 1,740 jin of wheat and seed cotton per mu created by the "May 1st" Second Agricultural Cooperative in Macheng County by 243.3 jin.

The Fourteenth Production Team of the Wulian Agricultural Cooperative is located on a plain. In 1957, it harvested an average of 830 jin of wheat and 1,153.3 jin of seed cotton per mu on 2.5 mu of high-yield experimental fields.

### 宜城五连社  创麦棉两熟亩产全国最高纪录

1958-01-14
第2版()
专栏：

    宜城五连社
    创麦棉两熟亩产全国最高纪录
    湖北省宜城县五连农业社十四生产队1957年在二点五亩土地上，平均每亩收获小麦和籽棉一千九百八十三点三斤，比麻城县“五一”第二农业社创造的平均每亩收获小麦、籽棉一千七百四十斤的纪录还多二百四十三点三斤。
    五连农业社第十四生产队，地处平原，1957年在二点五亩丰产试验田上，平均每亩收获小麦八百三十斤，籽棉一千一百五十三点三斤。　（据新华社讯）
