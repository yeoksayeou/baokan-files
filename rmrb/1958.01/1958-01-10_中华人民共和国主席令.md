Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1958-01-10
Page: 1

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China

    The "Regulations on Household Registration of the People's Republic of China" was adopted at the 91st meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on January 9, 1958, and is hereby promulgated.

                        Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                January 9, 1958

### 中华人民共和国主席令

1958-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    中华人民共和国户口登记条例已由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于一九五八年一月九日第九十一次会议通过，现予公布。
                        中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                一九五八年一月九日
