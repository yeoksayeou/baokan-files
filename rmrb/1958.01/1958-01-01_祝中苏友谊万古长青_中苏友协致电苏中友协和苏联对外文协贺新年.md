Title: May Sino-Soviet Friendship Last Forever! Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Sends New Year Greetings to Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries

Author:

Date: 1958-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 31st: The President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Soong Ching Ling, and the Secretary-General, Qian Junrui, sent telegrams to the President of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Andreyev, and the Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Popova, on December 30th, congratulating them on the New Year of 1958. President Soong Ching Ling also received a New Year greeting telegram from the President of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Andreyev.

　　In their congratulatory telegram to Andreyev, Soong Ching Ling and Qian Junrui wished the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association new and greater achievements in the cause of further strengthening the friendly cooperation between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, and wished Sino-Soviet friendship—the cornerstone of the unity of socialist countries and the pillar of world peace—to last forever.

　　In their congratulatory telegram to Popova, Soong Ching Ling and Qian Junrui wished the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries new and greater achievements in the noble cause of further strengthening friendship and cultural exchanges among the peoples of all countries.

　　In his congratulatory telegram to President Soong Ching Ling, the President of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Andreyev, on behalf of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, extended warm congratulations to Soong Ching Ling and all members of the Council and all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and wished the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association achievements in the noble cause of developing and strengthening the cooperation and unbreakable friendship between the peoples of the Soviet Union and China.

### 祝中苏友谊万古长青  中苏友协致电苏中友协和苏联对外文协贺新年

1958-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    祝中苏友谊万古长青
    中苏友协致电苏中友协和苏联对外文协贺新年
    新华社31日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄、秘书长钱俊瑞在30日致电苏中友好协会会长安德烈耶夫、苏联对外文化协会理事会主席波波娃，祝贺1958年新年。宋庆龄会长同时也收到了苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫的祝贺新年的电报。
    宋庆龄和钱俊瑞在给安德烈耶夫的贺电中，祝苏中友好协会在进一步加强中苏两大国人民之间的友好合作的事业中，获得更新更大的成就，并且祝中苏友谊——社会主义国家团结的基石、世界和平的支柱——万古长青。
    宋庆龄和钱俊瑞在给波波娃的贺电中，祝苏联对外文化协会在进一步加强各国人民友好和文化交流的崇高事业中，获得更新更大的成就。
    苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫在给宋庆龄会长的贺电中，代表苏中友好协会理事会向宋庆龄和中苏友好协会理事会各位理事、全体会员表示热烈的祝贺，并祝中苏友好协会在发展和加强苏中两国人民的合作和牢不可破的友谊的崇高事业中获得成就。
