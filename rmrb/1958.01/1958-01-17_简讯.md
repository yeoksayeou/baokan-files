Brief News

    Sino-Ceylon Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement

    Colombo Dispatch: The Soviet Union and Ceylon signed an agreement on the development of cultural cooperation between the two countries in Colombo on the 15th. The agreement stipulates that both sides will strive to strengthen and develop cultural cooperation between the two countries, and will promote mutual exchange of experience and achievements in literature, art, science, radio, national education, health, and sports.
      India-Hungary Trade Volume Increases Year by Year

    New Delhi Dispatch: The governments of India and Hungary exchanged letters in New Delhi on January 15, deciding to extend their trade agreement signed in 1954 for another two years. Since the signing of the India-Hungary trade agreement in 1954, the trade volume between India and Hungary has been increasing continuously. In 1953-1954, the trade volume between the two countries was 1.2 million rupees, and in 1956-1957, the trade volume increased to 11.5 million rupees.
        Wu Han and Others Depart for Home

    Cairo Dispatch: Wu Han and other members of the Chinese delegation to the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference left Cairo by plane on the 16th to return to China.
    Prior to this, at the invitation of the head of the Syrian delegation to the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference, they, along with representatives from other countries, visited Syria. They returned to Cairo from Damascus on the 14th.

    简讯
    苏锡签订文化
    合作协定
    科伦坡讯：苏联和锡兰15日在科伦坡签订了一项关于发展两国文化合作的协定。协定中规定，双方将竭力加强和发展两国之间的文化合作，并且将促进相互在文学、艺术、科学、无线电、国民教育、卫生、体育运动等方面交流经验和成就。
      印匈贸易额连年增加
    新德里讯：印度和匈牙利两国政府1月15日在新德里交换信件，决定把它们在1954年签订的贸易协定延长两年。自从1954年印、匈贸易协定签订以来，印度和匈牙利两国的贸易额不断增加。在1953—1954年，两国的贸易额为一百二十万卢比，在1956—1957年，贸易额增加到一千一百五十万卢比。
        吴晗等启程返国
    开罗讯：参加亚非人民团结大会的中国代表团团员吴晗等16日从开罗乘飞机回国。
    在此以前，他们曾经应参加亚非人民团结大会的叙利亚代表团团长的邀请，同其他国家的代表一起访问了叙利亚，他们在14日从大马士革回到开罗。
