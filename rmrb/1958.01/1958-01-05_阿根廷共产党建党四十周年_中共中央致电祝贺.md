Title: The Fortieth Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of Argentina: The CPC Central Committee Sends a Congratulatory Message

Author: 
Date: 1958-01-05
Page: 1

    The Fortieth Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of Argentina
    The CPC Central Committee Sends a Congratulatory Message
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Argentina on the 3rd, congratulating them on the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Argentina. The text of the telegram is as follows: To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Argentina:
    The Communist Party of China warmly congratulates the Communist Party of Argentina on the 40th anniversary of its founding.
    We feel close concern for the unyielding struggle of the Communist Party of Argentina over the past forty years for the interests of the working class and the people. It is of particularly great significance that your party celebrates its fortieth anniversary at a time when the world political situation has entered a new turning point. We wish your party new achievements in the struggle to win the unity of the working class and the people, safeguard the vital interests of the working people, strive for democratic freedoms, defend national independence and world peace.
                  The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, January 3, 1958

### 阿根廷共产党建党四十周年  中共中央致电祝贺

1958-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    阿根廷共产党建党四十周年
    中共中央致电祝贺
    新华社4日讯　中国共产党中央委员会3日致电阿根廷共产党中央委员会，祝贺阿根廷共产党建党四十周年。电文如下：阿根廷共产党中央委员会：
    中国共产党热烈地祝贺阿根廷共产党建党的四十周年。
    我们对阿根廷共产党四十年来为工人阶级和人民的利益而进行的不屈不挠的斗争感到亲切的关怀。你们在世界政治形势进入了一个新的转折点时庆祝党的四十周年，有特别重大的意义。祝你们党在争取工人阶级和人民的团结、维护劳动人民的切身利益、争取民主自由、保卫民族独立和世界和平的斗争中，取得新的成就。
                  中国共产党中央委员会　一九五八年一月三日
