Title: Joint Statement by Premier Široký and Prime Minister Phoumi Vongvichit Advocates International Cooperation to Resolve Disarmament Issues
Author:
Date: 1958-01-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 20th - Czechoslovak Premier Široký and Cambodian Prime Minister Phoumi Vongvichit signed a joint statement on the 19th.

The statement said: People must redouble their efforts to help maintain world peace and resolve the most important issue in the current international situation - the issue of disarmament. The Premier and Prime Minister believe that reaching an agreement on this issue is possible and necessary.

The statement said that the friendly relations between Czechoslovakia and Cambodia, which are based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and began with Prince Sihanouk's visit to Czechoslovakia, are growing stronger. The Premier and Prime Minister had talks on economic and technical cooperation between the two countries.

Rangoon News: Czechoslovak Premier Široký and his entourage arrived in Rangoon by plane from Phnom Penh on the afternoon of the 20th. They were welcomed at the airport by Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and other senior officials, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma.

### 西罗基总理和宾努首相联合声明  主张国际合作解决裁军问题

1958-01-21
第6版()
专栏：

    西罗基总理和宾努首相联合声明
    主张国际合作解决裁军问题
    据新华社金边20日电　捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基同柬埔寨首相宾努在19日签署了一项联合声明。
    声明说：人们必须加倍努力，帮助维护世界和平和解决目前国际局势中最重要的问题——裁军问题。总理和首相相信，就这个问题达成协议是可能和必要的。
    声明说，捷克斯洛伐克同柬埔寨两国之间以和平共处五项原则为基础的、并且从西哈努克亲王访问捷克斯洛伐克开始的友好关系正在日益增进。总理和首相曾经就两国之间的经济和技术合作问题进行了会谈。
    仰光消息：捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基和随行人员20日下午乘飞机从金边到达仰光。在机场受到缅甸联邦总理吴努和其他高级官员，以及各国驻缅甸外交使节的欢迎。
