Building "Thousands of Reservoirs, Ten Thousand Check Dams"

After the realization of Dengfeng County, Henan Province's plan to build "Thousands of Reservoirs, Ten Thousand Check Dams (gully dams)," it will completely change the past situation of "major disasters in heavy rain, minor disasters in light rain, drought in the absence of rain, and disasters in nine out of ten years." Now, the masses in the county have started construction on 230 small reservoirs, 96 more than in the past four years. The picture shows members of the Qianjin Agricultural Cooperative in Daye Town building the Xiaohe Reservoir. After the completion of this reservoir, it can store 150,000 cubic meters of water and irrigate more than 1,500 mu of land. Photo by Li Dewu

### 修建“千库，万坊”（图片）
李德武
1958-01-11
第2版()
专栏：

    修建“千库，万坊”
    河南省登封县修建“千库、万坊（谷坊）”的规划实现后，将彻底改变过去“大雨大灾，小雨小灾，不雨旱灾，十年九灾”的面貌。现在全县群众已动工修建二百三十座小水库，比过去四年还多九十六座。图为大冶镇前进农业社社员正在修建小河水库。这个水库完成后可蓄水十五万公方，浇地一千五百多亩。　李德武摄
