Title: Chen Yi Receives Czechoslovak Education Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi, received the Czechoslovak Education Delegation, led by the Deputy Minister of Education and Culture of Czechoslovakia, Jan Kotoč, and all members of the delegation on the morning of the 6th. The delegation is currently in China to investigate education.

Present at the reception was the Vice Minister of Education, Wei Que.

Also present at the reception were the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák, and the Cultural Counsellor, Bílý.

### 陈毅接见捷教育代表团

1958-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见捷教育代表团
    新华社6日讯　国务院副总理陈毅6日上午接见了正在我国考察教育事业的捷克斯洛伐克教育代表团团长、捷教育和文化部副部长扬·科托奇和全体团员。
    接见时在座的有教育部副部长韦悫。
    接见时，捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克、文化参赞白利德也在座。
