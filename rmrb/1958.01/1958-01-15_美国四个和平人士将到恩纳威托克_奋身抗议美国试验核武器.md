Title: Four American Pacifists to Go to Eniwetok to Protest U.S. Nuclear Weapons Testing

Author:

Date: 1958-01-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 13th -- The American "Committee for Nonviolent Action Against Nuclear Weapons" announced today that four of its members will sail in a schooner to the Eniwetok area of the Pacific in February to protest the U.S. plans to continue nuclear weapons testing in the area in April.

These four Americans believe: "The U.S. government's continued actions that endanger the well-being of all mankind are a heinous crime."

They wrote to Eisenhower: "For years, we have been talking about how to stop the suicidal arms race, but our voices have been drowned out by the clamor of those responsible for our country's arms buildup. Now we are willing to risk our lives and take action. We intend to use our actions in the Pacific to tell everyone: we are going there to stop the preparation for nuclear war - which is the fundamental task in our lives at present..."

Copies of this letter were also sent to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, U.S. Atomic Energy Commission Chairman Strauss, U.S. Vice President Nixon, U.S. Secretary of State Dulles, U.S. Ambassador to the United Nations Lodge, and representatives of other countries to the United Nations.

### 美国四个和平人士将到恩纳威托克  奋身抗议美国试验核武器

1958-01-15
第5版()
专栏：

    美国四个和平人士将到恩纳威托克
    奋身抗议美国试验核武器
    新华社13日讯　纽约消息：美国“以非暴力行动反对核武器委员会”今天宣布，它有四名会员将在今年2月乘双桅船出发前往太平洋恩纳威托克地区，以示抗议美国准备在4月继续在该地区试验核武器。
    这四个美国人认为：“美国政府继续采取危害全人类幸福的行动，这是一种罪大恶极的犯罪行为”。
    他们给艾森豪威尔写信说：“多年以来，我们一直在谈论着如何制止无异于自杀的备战行为，但是我们的呼声却被那些对我国的备战活动负有责任的人的叫嚷给淹没了。现在我们甘愿冒生命危险，行动起来。我们打算用我们去太平洋的行动告诉一切人：我们到那里去是为了制止准备核战争——这在当前是我们生活中的基本任务……”
    这封信的副本还送给了联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德、美国原子能委员会主席斯特劳斯、美国副总统尼克松、美国国务卿杜勒斯、美国驻联合国代表洛奇以及其他国家驻联合国的代表。
