Title: Soong Ching-ling Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the All-India-China Friendship Association Delegate Meeting
Author:
Date: 1958-01-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 29th. Soong Ching-ling sent a telegram to the All-India-China Friendship Association in Bombay, congratulating the opening of the Third National Delegate Meeting of the All-India-China Friendship Association on January 31st. The text of the telegram is as follows:

"I would like to extend my warmest congratulations to the Third National Delegate Meeting of the All-India-China Friendship Association. As the years go by, our two countries and peoples have strengthened their mutual understanding through your work and the work of our Friendship Association; our mutual understanding has deepened; with each new experience gained, the ties of friendship between our two countries are further strengthened. It can indeed be said that this is a magnificent monument we have erected for our love of country and love of international peace. I wish your meeting success. Long live India-China friendship!"

### 宋庆龄电贺印中友协代表会议

1958-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄电贺印中友协代表会议
    新华社上海29日电　宋庆龄致电孟买印中友好协会，祝贺将于1月31日开幕的印中友好协会第三届全国代表会议。电文如下：
    “我谨向印中友好协会第三届全国代表会议致以最热烈的祝贺。随着年月的消逝，我们两国和两国人民通过你们的工作和我国的友好协会的工作而加强了相互的认识，我们的相互了解加深了；每取得一个新的经验，我们两国的友谊联系就更加加强一步。这的确可以说是我们为热爱祖国和热爱国际和平的感情所树立的一座雄伟的纪念碑。祝你们的会议成功。印中友好万岁！”
