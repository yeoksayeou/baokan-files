Title: Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai to Express Thanks
Author:
Date: 1958-01-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 16th. Burmese Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai on January 16th, expressing his gratitude for the hospitality he received during his visit to China. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

I would like to express my sincere gratitude to Your Excellency and the government of your country for the warm hospitality extended to us during our recent visit. I am now even more convinced that this kind of personal contact and exchange of views will help to further develop the friendly relations already established between our two countries. I look forward to an early opportunity to reciprocate your kindness. With warmest regards.

Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein, Rangoon, January 16, 1958

### 吴觉迎副总理电谢周总理

1958-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    吴觉迎副总理电谢周总理
    新华社16日讯　缅甸吴觉迎副总理16日致电周恩来总理，对他在中国访问期间所受到的款待表示谢意。电文如下：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来阁下：
    我谨对阁下和贵国政府在我们最近访问期间所给予我们的热忱的款待表示诚挚的谢意。我现在更加相信，这种个人的接触和交换意见将有助于更进一步发展我们两国之间业已建立起来的友好关系。我期待着早日有机会来报答你的盛情。致以最亲切地问候。
                       吴觉迎副总理　1958年1月16日仰光
