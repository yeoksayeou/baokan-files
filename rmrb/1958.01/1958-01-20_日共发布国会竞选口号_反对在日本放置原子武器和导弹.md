Title: Japanese Communist Party Releases National Diet Election Slogans; Opposes Placement of Atomic Weapons and Missiles in Japan
Author:
Date: 1958-01-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, January 19th (via TASS) - "Akahata" published the slogans proposed by the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party for participation in the National Diet elections on the 18th. The main slogans put forward by the Japanese Communist Party are: to strive for peace, to strive for unconditional prohibition of nuclear testing, and to struggle against the placement of atomic weapons and missiles in Japan.

In its election slogans, the Japanese Communist Party stated that it is determined to strive for national independence, the removal of American bases and the withdrawal of American troops from Japan, and to recover Okinawa Island, which is used by the Americans as an atomic base.

The Japanese Communists believe that striving to restore diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China and implementing a policy of peaceful coexistence with all countries is one of their fundamental tasks.

In terms of domestic politics, the Japanese Communist Party called in its election slogans to raise the living standards of the people and eliminate unemployment.

The election slogans stated that the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party advocates joint action by Communists, Socialists, and all progressive forces in Japan.

### 日共发布国会竞选口号  反对在日本放置原子武器和导弹

1958-01-20
第5版()
专栏：

    日共发布国会竞选口号
    反对在日本放置原子武器和导弹
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社东京18日讯：“赤旗报”在18日公布了日本共产党中央委员会提出的参加国会竞选的口号。日本共产党提出的主要口号是：为争取和平、争取无条件地禁止核试验，为反对在日本放置原子武器和导弹而斗争。
    日本共产党在竞选口号中表示，日本共产党决心为争取获得民族独立、撤销美国基地和从日本撤出美国军队，为收复被美国人用作原子基地的冲绳岛而斗争。
    日本共产党人认为，争取恢复同中华人民共和国的外交关系和执行同各国和平共处的政策，是自己的一个基本任务。
    在国内政治方面，日本共产党在竞选口号中号召提高人民的生活水平，消灭失业现象。
    竞选口号说，日本共产党中央主张共产党人、社会党人和日本的一切进步力量采取联合行动。
