Title: Demobilized Soldiers in Rural Liaoning Actively Build New Socialist Villages
Author:
Date: 1958-01-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: Demobilized soldiers who have returned to rural areas in Liaoning Province to participate in production and construction have overcome numerous difficulties and achieved good results in building new socialist villages. Wang Shuren, a second-class disabled veteran from Xinmin County, whose left arm is disabled, carried water on his shoulder for twenty days during last year's drought, inspiring commune members to actively fight the drought. Jin Yongtai, a demobilized soldier from Xialu Township, Kuandian County, with the help of technicians, took the lead in planning, designing, and constructing a small power station in his township.

### 辽宁农村的复员军人  积极建设新农村

1958-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    辽宁农村的复员军人
    积极建设新农村
    据新华社讯　回到辽宁省农村参加生产建设的复员军人，在建设社会主义新农村中克服了重重困难，作出了良好的成绩。新民县二等甲级残废军人王树人，左臂残废，去年抗旱期间他用一个肩挑了二十天水，鼓舞了社员积极进行抗旱斗争。宽甸县下露乡复员军人金永太，在技术人员的帮助下，他积极带头筹划、设计和施工，在本乡建设成功一座小型发电站。
