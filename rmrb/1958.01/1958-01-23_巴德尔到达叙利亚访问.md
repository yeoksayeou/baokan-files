Title: Badr Arrives in Syria for Visit
Author:
Date: 1958-01-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, January 21st: The Crown Prince and Deputy Prime Minister of Yemen, Badr, arrived in Damascus, the Syrian capital, by plane from Cairo on the 21st for a three-day official visit.

    Crown Prince Badr was welcomed at the airport by Syrian Prime Minister al-Asali, Acting Foreign Minister Kalas, Deputy Chief of Staff Nifri, and Grand Mufti Dr. Abdin. Soviet Ambassador to Syria, Podzemny, Chinese Ambassador to Syria, Chen Zhifang, and other foreign envoys to Syria also welcomed him at the airport.

    Crown Prince Badr will visit the Syrian Presidential Palace that afternoon. Syrian President Quwatli will host a banquet for Badr that evening.

### 巴德尔到达叙利亚访问

1958-01-23
第5版()
专栏：

    巴德尔到达叙利亚访问
    据新华社大马士革21日电　也门王太子、副首相巴德尔21日从开罗乘飞机到达叙利亚首都大马士革，作为时三天的正式访问。
    巴德尔王太子在机场上受到叙利亚总理阿萨利、代理外交部长卡拉斯、副参谋长尼福里、大穆弗蒂阿卜丁博士的欢迎。苏联驻叙利亚大使?姆钦纳、中国驻叙利亚大使陈志方以及其他国家驻叙利亚的使节也到机场欢迎。
    巴德尔将在当天下午访问叙利亚总统府。叙利亚总统库阿特利将在当天晚上设宴招待巴德尔。
