Title: One-Third of French Territory Covered with a Network of American Military Bases
Author:
Date: 1958-01-25
Page: 5

　　France's One-Third of the Territory
　　Covered with American Military Base Network
　　Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: TASS, Paris, January 23rd: French territory is covered with a network of American military bases. According to official materials, twenty-four provinces in France, that is, one-third of the country's territory, have American military bases, firing ranges, and other military buildings. Among them are fifteen U.S. Army bases and fourteen Air Force bases.

### 法国三分之一的领土布满美国军事基地网

1958-01-25
第5版()
专栏：

    法国三分之一的领土
    布满美国军事基地网
    据新华社24日讯　塔斯社巴黎23日讯：法国境内布满美国军事基地网。据官方材料，法国的二十四个省，即全国三分之一的领土上有美国军事基地、射击场和其他军事建筑。其中有十五个美国陆军基地和十四个空军基地。
