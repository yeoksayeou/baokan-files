Title: Kremlin Attracts Large Crowds: 12 Million Visitors in Two and a Half Years
Author:
Date: 1958-01-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 18th, TASS reported: Since the Moscow Kremlin opened to the public two and a half years ago, it has now received 12 million visitors.

Soviet citizens and foreign guests widely appreciate the Kremlin's historical sites, rare historical monuments – the Armory Chamber and various cathedrals. The Armory Chamber has been visited by guests from 130 countries around the world.

The Kremlin will soon reopen to visitors another monument of ancient Russian art – an episcopal palace, which contains the Chamber of the Cross and the Church of the Twelve Apostles. This episcopal palace was built in the 1650s.

Soviet artists and architects have used archival materials to carry out large-scale repairs to the building itself, murals, and icons in the past two years.

### 克里姆林宫吸引广大群众  开放两年半参观者达一千二百万人

1958-01-21
第6版()
专栏：

    克里姆林宫吸引广大群众
    开放两年半参观者达一千二百万人
    据新华社20日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科18日讯：莫斯科克里姆林宫自从在两年半以前向公众开放以来，到现在参观者已经达到一千二百万人。
    苏联人和外国客人们广泛地欣赏克里姆林宫的古迹、珍奇的历史纪念物——兵器陈列馆和各个教堂。参观过兵器陈列馆的有来自世界上一百三十个国家的客人。
    克里姆林宫不久将为参观者再开放一个古代俄罗斯艺术的纪念物——一个主教院。院内有十字祈祷厅和十二圣徒堂。这个主教院是十七世纪五十年代建造的。
    苏联艺术家和建筑师们利用档案资料在过去两年中对建筑物本身、壁画和圣像等进行了大规模的修缮工作。
