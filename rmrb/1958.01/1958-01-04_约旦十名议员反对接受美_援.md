Title: Ten Jordanian Parliament Members Oppose Accepting U.S. "Aid"
Author:
Date: 1958-01-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, January 2nd, TASS: According to the "Orient Daily" report, at the Jordanian parliament meeting on January 1st, ten members voted against the agreement signed by the Jordanian government with the United States that would provide Jordan with enslaving technical and economic "aid".

### 约旦十名议员反对接受美“援”

1958-01-04
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦十名议员
    反对接受美“援”
    据新华社3日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特2日讯：据“东方报”报道，在约旦议会1月1日的会议上有十名议员投票反对约旦政府同美国签订的给约旦以奴役性的技术和经济“援助”的协定。
