Title: Rangoon Celebrates Burmese Independence Day, President U Win Maung Delivers Speech Emphasizing Peaceful Coexistence
Author:
Date: 1958-01-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 4th: Rangoon, the capital of Burma, held a parade this morning to celebrate the tenth anniversary of Burmese independence.

President U Win Maung of Burma delivered a speech. He said, "The Federal Government is making active efforts to prevent another world war, maintain world peace, resolve the issue of disarmament, not interfere in internal affairs, and use atomic energy only for peaceful purposes in the world, because we know that the two world wars have brought untold disasters to mankind."

He said that the purpose of Burma's foreign policy is to establish friendly relations with all countries, which is mutually beneficial and based on peaceful coexistence. He emphasized, "The Union of Burma now maintains harmonious relations with its neighbors such as India, Pakistan, Thailand, and the People's Republic of China."

### 仰光庆祝缅甸独立节  吴温貌总统发表演说强调和平共处

1958-01-05
第5版()
专栏：

    仰光庆祝缅甸独立节
    吴温貌总统发表演说强调和平共处
    据新华社仰光4日电　缅甸首都仰光今天上午举行游行，庆祝缅甸独立十周年。
    缅甸总统吴温貌发表了演说。他说：“联邦政府正在作积极的努力，以防止另一次世界大战，维护世界和平，解决裁军问题，互不干涉内政，使原子能只用于世界和平的目的，因为我们知道两次世界大战给人类带来了说不完的灾难。”
    他说，缅甸外交政策的目的是同各国建立友好关系，是互利和和平共处。他强调说：“缅甸联邦现在同印度、巴基斯坦、泰国和中华人民共和国等等邻国保持着融洽的关系。”
