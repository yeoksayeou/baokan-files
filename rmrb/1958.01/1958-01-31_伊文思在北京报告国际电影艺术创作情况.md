Title: Ivens Reports on International Film Art Creation in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-01-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, January 30th. Joris Ivens, the internationally renowned film director and winner of the International Peace Prize, who recently arrived in Beijing at the invitation of the Ministry of Culture, reported on the latest developments in international film art creation at a report meeting held jointly by the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture and the China Film Workers Association on January 30th. The report meeting was presided over by Wang Lanxi, Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture. Key staff members from all departments of the film industry attended.

Joris Ivens has recently taken up the post of artistic advisor to the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio in Beijing and is currently directing the production of several documentary films. He will soon be departing for various locations to undertake location work.

### 伊文思在北京报告国际电影艺术创作情况

1958-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    伊文思在北京报告国际电影艺术创作情况
    新华社30日讯　最近应文化部邀请到达北京的国际著名电影导演、国际和平奖金获得者尤列斯·伊文思，30日在文化部电影事业管理局和中国电影工作者联谊会联合举行的报告会上报告了最近国际电影艺术创作的情况。报告会由文化部电影事业管理局局长王阑西主持。电影事业各部门的主要工作人员都出席。
    尤列斯·伊文思最近就任了北京中央新闻纪录电影制片厂艺术顾问，正在指导摄制几部纪录电影，不久即将出发各地进行外景工作。
