    Gale Forecast
    At 12:00 noon on January 22, 1958, the Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a gale forecast:
    It is expected that the Bohai Sea and the Taiwan Strait will still have northerly winds of force 6-8 in the next 36 hours. The wind force on the Shandong Peninsula, Liaodong Peninsula, the Yellow Sea, and the East China Sea will increase to force 6-8, with a northerly direction. Hebei, Shandong, and Henan will still have northerly winds of force 5-6. The wind force in the Huai River basin and the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River will also gradually increase to about force 5, and the maximum wind force on the river and lake surfaces will reach force 6-7.

### 大风预报

1958-01-23
第7版()
专栏：

    大风预报
    1958年1月22日中午12时中央气象科学研究所发布大风预报：
    预计未来三十六小时内渤海海面、台湾海峡仍将有6—8级的偏北风。山东半岛、辽东半岛、黄海海面和东海海面的风力将加强到6—8级，风向偏北。河北、山东和河南仍将有5、6级的偏北风。淮河流域和长江中下游地区的风力，也将逐渐加强到5级左右，江面、湖面最大风力将达6、7级。
