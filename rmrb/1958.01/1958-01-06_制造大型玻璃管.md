Title: Manufacturing Large Glass Pipes
Author: Shi Lin
Date: 1958-01-06
Page: 3

    Manufacturing Large Glass Pipes
    (Our newspaper dispatch) Our country's first workshop for manufacturing large glass pipes was completed at the Yaohua Glass Factory in Qinhuangdao City, Hebei Province at the end of December 1957. Workers are now working intensely inside to install the machinery, and it is planned to begin trial production in early February 1958.
    The glass pipes with high chemical stability and thermal stability that this workshop will trial produce and manufacture are not easily corroded and can replace metal pipes in building and installation projects, and can meet the special requirements of the chemical and food industries.
                        (Shi Lin)

### 制造大型玻璃管
石林
1958-01-06
第3版()
专栏：

    制造大型玻璃管
    本报讯　我国第一座制造大型玻璃管的车间，1957年12月底在河北省秦皇岛市耀华玻璃厂建成。现在工人们正在内部紧张地进行机器安装工作，计划在1958年2月初开始试生产。
    这车间将要试制并生产的化学稳定性及热稳定性较高的玻璃管道，不易腐蚀，可以代替建筑安装工程中的金属管道，并能满足化学工业及食品工业的特殊要求。
                        （石林）
