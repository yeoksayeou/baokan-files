Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Reuters Editor-in-Chief
Author:
Date: 1958-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Premier Zhou Enlai received Reuters Editor-in-Chief Mr. Cole, Reuters reporter Mr. Chudewey, and Mr. J. Jeck on the evening of the 6th.

Present at the reception were Wu Lengxi, Director of Xinhua News Agency; Deng Gang, Deputy Director; Huang Hua, Director of the Department of Western European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department.

### 周总理接见路透社总编辑

1958-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见路透社总编辑
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理6日晚间接见了路透社总编辑柯尔和路透社记者漆德卫、季杰克。
    接见时在座的，有新华通讯社社长吴冷西，副社长邓岗，外交部西欧司司长黄华、新闻司司长龚澎。
