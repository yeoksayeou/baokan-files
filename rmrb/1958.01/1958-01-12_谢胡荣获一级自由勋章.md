Title: Shehu Awarded the First-Class Order of Freedom
Author:
Date: 1958-01-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, January 10th: On January 10th, on the occasion of Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu's forty-fifth birthday, the Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly awarded him the First-Class Order of Freedom, in recognition of his highly patriotic achievements in the struggle for the freedom of the motherland, for consolidating Albania's free independence and territorial integrity, and in recognition of his leadership of the country in the construction of socialism, the consolidation of the people's democratic system, and the great contributions he has made to the Party and the people.

### 谢胡荣获一级自由勋章

1958-01-12
第6版()
专栏：

    谢胡荣获一级自由勋章
    新华社地拉那10日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团1月10日在部长会议主席谢胡四十五岁生日的时候，授予了他一级自由勋章，以表彰他为祖国的自由、为巩固阿尔巴尼亚的自由独立和领土完整的斗争中的高度爱国主义的功绩，表彰他在社会主义的建设中领导国家、巩固人民民主制度以及对党和对人民所作出的巨大贡献。
