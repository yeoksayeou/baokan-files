Title: New Records in the First Ten Days
Author:
Date: 1958-01-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch

    New Records in the First Ten Days
    In the first ten days of 1958, young workers in many factories in Chengdu continuously created new records. They demonstrated a strong determination to guarantee the completion of the Second Five-Year Plan.
    The Pu Jiping carpentry youth shock team of the Chengdu Municipal Construction Engineering Bureau Timber Processing Factory exceeded their quota by more than 45% on the first working day of the year, and maintained this record until the 7th. Yu Zhixiu, an advanced producer and young worker at the Chengdu Measuring & Cutting Tool Factory, improved a jig while milling springs, increasing efficiency from one spring every twenty minutes as originally planned to one spring per minute.

### 头十天的新纪录

1958-01-14
第3版()
专栏：

    头十天的新纪录
    成都市许多工厂的青年职工在1958年的头十天中，不断创造新纪录。他们显示出保证完成第二个五年计划的坚强决心。
    成都市建筑工程局木材加工厂蒲济平木工青年突击队，今年第一个劳动日就突破定额45％强，并且到7日为止，一直保持了这个纪录。成都量具刃具厂先进生产者、青年工人于志修，在铣弹簧时改了一个夹板，使工效由原计划每二十分钟铣一个提高到一分钟铣一个。
                （据新华社讯）
