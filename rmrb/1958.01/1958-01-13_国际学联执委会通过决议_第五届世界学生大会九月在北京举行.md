Title: IUS Executive Committee Passes Resolution: Fifth World Student Congress to be Held in Beijing in September
Author:
Date: 1958-01-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Leipzig, January 11th: The Executive Committee of the International Union of Students (IUS) unanimously passed a resolution today, deciding to accept the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation to hold the Fifth World Student Congress in Beijing in September this year. The meeting adjourned tonight after passing a series of other resolutions.

Other resolutions passed by the Executive Committee on the 11th included: a resolution on defending peace; a resolution on supporting the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Conference; a resolution supporting the struggle of students in the Middle East to defend national independence and oppose colonialism, supporting the struggle of Algerian students to win national independence, supporting the struggle of Indonesian students to recover West Irian, and supporting the struggle of Cypriot students to win national self-determination.

According to the decision of the Executive Committee, the main agenda of the Fifth World Student Congress, scheduled to be held in Beijing from September 4th to 13th this year, will include: the development of the international student movement and the contribution of the IUS and student organizations to defending student interests and promoting international student cooperation, accepting new members, electing a new Executive Committee, etc.

The Executive Committee decided to invite student organizations from all countries in the world to participate in this congress, regardless of whether they are members of the IUS or not.

### 国际学联执委会通过决议  第五届世界学生大会九月在北京举行

1958-01-13
第6版()
专栏：

    国际学联执委会通过决议
    第五届世界学生大会九月在北京举行
    据新华社来比锡11日电　国际学生联合会执行委员会会议今天一致通过了一项决议，决定接受中华全国学生联合会的邀请，今年9月间在北京召开第五届世界学生代表大会。会议在通过其他一系列决议后在今天晚上宣布结束。
    执委会11日通过的其他决议包括：关于保卫和平的决议；关于支持亚非人民团结大会的决议；支持中近东学生保卫民族独立反对殖民主义的斗争，支持阿尔及利亚学生争取民族独立的斗争，支持印度尼西亚学生收复西伊里安的斗争，支持塞浦路斯学生争取民族自决的斗争的决议。
    根据执委会决议，预定今年9月4日到13日在北京举行的第五届世界学生代表大会的主要议程将包括：国际学生运动的发展以及国际学联和学生组织对于保卫学生利益和促进国际学生合作的贡献，接受新会员，改选新的执行委员会等。
    执行委员会决定邀请世界上一切国家的学生会组织参加这次代表大会，不管它们是不是国际学联的会员。
