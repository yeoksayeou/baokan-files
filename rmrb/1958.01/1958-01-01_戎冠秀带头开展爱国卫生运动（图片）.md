Rong Guanxiu Takes the Lead in Launching Patriotic Health Campaign

Rong Guanxiu, representative of the National People's Congress, model of a martyr's family member, and mother of soldiers, leads the women of Guangyu Village in carrying out the "Five Cleans" movement, requiring clean pots and bowls, clean courtyards, clean selves, clean streets, and clean houses.

They are cleaning the streets.

### 戎冠秀带头开展爱国卫生运动（图片）
吕相友
1958-01-01
第7版()
专栏：

    戎冠秀带头开展爱国卫生运动
    全国人民代表大会代表、烈军属模范、子弟兵母亲戎冠秀，带领光宇村妇女开展五干净运动，要求做到锅碗干净、院子干净。本身干净、街道干净、屋子干净。
    她们正在打扫街道。　　本报记者　吕相友摄
