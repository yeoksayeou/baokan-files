Title: West Indies Federation Demands US Withdraw Naval Base
Author:
Date: 1958-01-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 11th (TASS dispatch) — The recently established West Indies Federation is demanding the withdrawal of the US naval base at Chaguaramas (Trinidad Island). According to the "New York Times," Britain and the West Indies Federation will hold negotiations with the United States in Port of Spain on this issue. A US Navy mission, headed by US Navy Rear Admiral Wellings, has departed from Washington for Port of Spain.

### 西印度联邦  要美国撤消海军基地

1958-01-14
第5版()
专栏：

    西印度联邦
    要美国撤消海军基地
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社纽约11日讯：最近成立的西印度联邦正在要求撤消恰瓜腊马斯（特立尼达岛）的美国海军基地。据“纽约时报”报道，英国和西印度联邦将同美国在西班牙港进行关于这个问题的谈判。以美国海军少将韦林斯为首的美国海军使团已经从华盛顿启程去西班牙港。
