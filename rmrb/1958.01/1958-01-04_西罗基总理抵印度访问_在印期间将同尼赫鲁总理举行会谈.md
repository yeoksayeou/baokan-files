Title: Široký Arrives in India for Visit; to Hold Talks with Prime Minister Nehru During Stay
Author:
Date: 1958-01-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 3rd: Czechoslovak Prime Minister Viliam Široký arrived in New Delhi this afternoon aboard a Tu-104 jet. He will be visiting India for twelve days, after which he will visit other Southeast Asian countries.

    After disembarking from the plane, Prime Minister Široký thanked Indian Prime Minister Nehru, who was there to greet him, for the invitation. He said that the people of Czechoslovakia and India stand together in the great struggle to eliminate the threat of a new war and to develop and strengthen friendly relations and peaceful cooperation between nations. India's efforts to establish friendly relations based on the spirit of "Panchsheel," and to build the nation, consolidate independence, and develop the economy, will receive the full support and sympathy of the Czechoslovak people.

    Prime Minister Nehru expressed a warm welcome to Prime Minister Široký's visit in his welcoming speech. He said that Prime Minister Široký's visit will help strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries.

    Accompanying Prime Minister Široký on his visit to India are: Czechoslovak Foreign Minister David, Commissioner for Education of the Slovak Administrative Council Sikora, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Kohout, Czechoslovak Ambassador to India Nosek, and Dr. Politzka of the Institute of Oriental Studies of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences.

    After a brief rest at the Indian Presidential Palace, Prime Minister Široký visited Prime Minister Nehru.

    During his stay in India, Prime Minister Široký will hold talks with Prime Minister Nehru.

### 西罗基总理抵印度访问  在印期间将同尼赫鲁总理举行会谈

1958-01-04
第6版()
专栏：

    西罗基总理抵印度访问
    在印期间将同尼赫鲁总理举行会谈
    新华社新德里3日电　捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基今天下午乘“图—104”喷气式飞机到达新德里。他将在印度作为时十二天的访问，然后到其他东南亚国家访问。
    西罗基总理下飞机后，向前来迎接的印度总理尼赫鲁表示感谢他的邀请，并且说，捷克斯洛伐克和印度两国人民站在一起，参加为消除新战争的威胁、发展和加强各国友好关系和和平合作的伟大斗争。印度关于根据“潘查希拉”精神来建立友好关系，以及建设国家、巩固独立、发展经济的努力，将得到捷克斯洛伐克人民的充分支持和同情。
    尼赫鲁总理在致欢迎词时表示热烈欢迎西罗基总理的访问。他说，西罗基总理的访问将有助于加强两国之间的友好关系。
    随同西罗基总理到印度访问的有：捷克斯洛伐克外交部长戴维，斯洛伐克行政委员会教育行政委员西科拉，对外贸易部副部长科霍乌特，捷克斯洛伐克驻印度大使诺塞克和捷克斯洛伐克科学院东方学研究院的波里茨卡博士。
    西罗基总理在印度总统府略事休息后，即去拜访了尼赫鲁总理。
    西罗基总理在印度期间，将同尼赫鲁总理举行会谈。
