Title: Moscow Gala Welcomes Participants of the Asian-African Solidarity Conference: Representatives from Various Countries Hail the Great Unity of Asian and African Peoples

Author:

Date: 1958-01-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 9th: A gathering was held in Moscow on the evening of January 9th at the Tchaikovsky Concert Hall between people from all walks of life in Moscow and participants of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference. Attending the gathering were representatives from the Soviet Union, China, Vietnam, Mongolia, India, Japan, Ceylon, Kenya, and other countries participating in the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference, as well as over a thousand representatives from various circles in Moscow. Also invited to attend the evening were diplomatic envoys from Asian-African countries and people's democratic countries stationed in the Soviet Union.

Rakhimov, head of the Soviet delegation to the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference, spoke first at the meeting. He said that the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference is a manifestation of the great unity of Asian and African countries in the struggle for political and economic independence and the defense of world peace. The documents adopted by this conference reflect the hopes of peace-loving people.

Chu Tunan, Deputy Head of the Chinese delegation, spoke at the meeting, saying that this conference is a historically significant meeting of the Asian and African people, and a great demonstration by the Asian and African people against colonialism and imperialism. It shows that the Asian and African people have become a powerful force in defending world peace, and it marks a new stage in the great unity of the Asian and African people.

Representatives from India, Ceylon, Vietnam, Japan, Mongolia, and Kenya also spoke at the meeting in succession. They all emphasized the unity of the Asian and African people.

The evening concluded with a performance featuring songs and dances from Eastern countries.

### 莫斯科盛会欢迎亚非团结大会参加者  各国代表欢呼亚非人民大团结

1958-01-11
第5版()
专栏：

    莫斯科盛会欢迎亚非团结大会参加者
    各国代表欢呼亚非人民大团结
    据新华社莫斯科9日电　莫斯科各界人民9日晚间在柴可夫斯基音乐厅同亚非人民团结大会的参加者举行会见。参加会见晚会的有苏联、中国、越南、蒙古、印度、日本、锡兰、怯尼亚等国参加亚非人民团结大会的代表和莫斯科市各界代表千余人。应邀出席晚会的还有亚非国家和人民民主国家驻苏联外交使节。
    苏联出席亚非人民团结大会代表团团长拉希多夫首先在会上讲话。他说，亚非人民团结大会是亚非国家在争取政治、经济独立和保卫世界和平的斗争中伟大团结的表现。这次大会通过的文件反映了爱好和平的人们的希望。
    中国代表团副团长楚图南在会上讲话，他说，这次大会是亚非人民的一次有历史意义的重要会议，是亚非人民反对殖民主义和帝国主义的大示威。它显示出亚非人民已经成为一支保卫世界和平的强大力量，它标志着亚非人民大团结的新阶段。
    印度、锡兰、越南、日本、蒙古、怯尼亚代表也相继在会上讲了话。他们都强调亚非人民的团结。
    晚会最后举行了演出，表演了东方国家的歌舞节目。
