Title: Burial of Deputy Prime Minister Nuschke of the German Democratic Republic

Author:

Date: 1958-01-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 30th: The remains of Otto Nuschke, Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, who passed away on the 27th, were solemnly buried in Berlin on the 30th.

Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Dieckmann, Prime Minister Grotewohl, First Deputy Prime Minister and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Ulbricht, leaders of various government departments, foreign diplomatic envoys, leaders of various mass organizations, and representatives of the working people attended the ceremony and laid wreaths before Nuschke's coffin. Grotewohl addressed the attendees.

### 民主德国副总理努舍克遗体下葬

1958-01-01
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国副总理
    努舍克遗体下葬
    据新华社31日讯　据塔斯社柏林30日讯：27日逝世的德意志民主共和国副总理奥托·努舍克的遗体，30日在柏林隆重下葬。
    德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团主席狄克曼、总理格罗提渥、第一副总理和德国统一社会党中央第一书记乌布利希、政府各部领导人、各国外交使节、各群众团体领导人和劳动人民代表都参加了仪式，并且在努舍克的灵柩前献了花圈。格罗提渥向到场的人讲了话。
