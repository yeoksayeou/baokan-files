Title: Romania Holds National Mourning for Chairman Groza; State Funeral Committee Decides to Hold State Funeral on the 10th
Author:
Date: 1958-01-09
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 8th -- The Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania, the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, and the Council of Ministers held a joint meeting on the 7th regarding the passing of Chairman Groza.

The meeting decided:

	1. To hold mourning throughout the country from January 7th until the funeral;
	2. To hold a state funeral for Chairman Groza;
	3. To establish a State Funeral Committee. The Funeral Committee will be composed of Stoica and others;
	4. During the days of mourning, the national flag will be flown at half-mast, and all cultural, entertainment, sports activities and celebrations will be suspended.
	5. When the funeral is held on the 10th, the whole country will stop all work for three minutes of silence.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 8th -- The Romanian State Funeral Committee for Chairman Groza issued a notice on the 7th stating that the burial ceremony for Chairman Groza will be held on January 10th.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 8th -- The people of Romania mourn the passing of Chairman Petru Groza with great sorrow. Memorial services are being held in agencies, schools, and enterprises in Romanian cities and villages. Chairman Groza's coffin is placed in the Palace of the Republic. From 9:00 am on the 8th, workers, staff, students, soldiers, the elderly, women, and children in the capital continuously come to the Palace of the Republic to bid farewell to Chairman Groza. People who come to say goodbye gaze at Chairman Groza's remains with deep sorrow and reluctance.

Xinhua News Agency, 8th -- TASS, Moscow:  The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Presidium of the USSR Supreme Soviet, and the USSR Council of Ministers have formed a government delegation to attend the funeral of Peter Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania. The members of the delegation are: Kozlov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation and member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, Pospelov, alternate member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, Patolichev, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and Yepishev, Soviet Ambassador to Romania. The delegation left Moscow for Bucharest by plane on the 8th.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 7th -- The Chinese Ambassador to Romania, Ke Bonian, led all diplomatic officials of the embassy on the evening of the 7th to the Grand National Assembly of Romania to pay their respects on the death of Chairman Peter Groza.

Diplomatic officials from other countries stationed in Romania also came to pay their respects tonight.

### 罗马尼亚全国哀悼格罗查主席  国家治丧委员会决定十日举行国葬

1958-01-09
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚全国哀悼格罗查主席
    国家治丧委员会决定十日举行国葬
    新华社布加勒斯特8日电　罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团、工人党中央委员会政治局、部长会议7日为格罗查主席逝世举行了联席会议。
    会议决定：
    一、从1月7日起到举行葬礼时止，在全国举行哀悼；
    二、为格罗查主席举行国葬；
    三、成立国家治丧委员会。治丧委员会将由斯托伊卡等人组成；
    四、在哀悼的日子里，全国下半旗志哀，并且停止一切文化娱乐体育活动和庆祝会。
    五、在10日举行葬礼时，全国将停止一切工作志哀三分钟。
    据新华社布加勒斯特8日电罗马尼亚格罗查主席国家治丧委员会7日发表公告说，格罗查主席的安葬仪式将在1月10日举行。
    据新华社布加勒斯特8日电罗马尼亚全国人民以悲痛的心情哀悼彼得鲁·格罗查主席的逝世。在罗马尼亚的城市和乡村的机关、学校和企业中正在纷纷举行追悼大会。格罗查主席的灵柩安放在共和国宫内。从8日上午九时起，首都的工人、职员、学生、军人、老年人、妇女、儿童川流不息地来共和国宫和格罗查主席告别。前来告别的人们沉痛地注视着格罗查主席的遗容，依依不舍。
    据新华社8日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会、苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联部长会议组成了参加罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查的葬礼的政府代表团。代表团成员是：俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席、苏共中央主席团委员科兹洛夫，苏共中央主席团候补委员、苏共中央书记波斯伯洛夫，苏联外交部副部长帕托利切夫，苏联驻罗马尼亚大使叶皮谢夫。代表团已于8日乘飞机离莫斯科前往布加勒斯特。
    据新华社布加勒斯特7日电中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年7日晚间率领大使馆全体外交官员，到罗马尼亚大国民议会吊唁彼·格罗查主席逝世。
    今天晚上前来吊唁的还有其他国家驻罗马尼亚的外交官员。
