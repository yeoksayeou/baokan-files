Title: US Military Exercises in the Pacific Region
Author:
Date: 1958-01-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: According to US Navy sources on the 22nd, the US Seventh Fleet and US Air Force stationed in Japan and the Philippines began a six-day military exercise on the 15th on land and in the Pacific Ocean from northern Japan to southern Philippines.

It is reported that approximately thirty warships and more than three hundred aircraft participated in the military exercise codenamed "Cape Rock."

### 美军在太平洋地区演习

1958-01-24
第5版()
专栏：

    美军在太平洋地区演习
    据新华社讯　东京讯：据美国海军方面22日透露，美国第七舰队和美国驻在日本和菲律宾的空军，又从15日开始在从日本北部到菲律宾南部的陆地上和太平洋海面上举行了为时六天的军事演习。
    据报道，参加这次以“披风岩石”为代号的军事演习的有大约三十艘军舰和三百多架飞机。
