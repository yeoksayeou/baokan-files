Soviet Science News

Future Aircraft
    The "Soviet Air Force News" predicted on New Year's Day that rocket planes and ionospheric planes with speeds exceeding the speed of sound several times may appear in the future as machines for flying in the upper atmosphere and outer space. The newspaper said that ionospheric planes equipped with ramjet engines will use the energy of air dissociated and ionized by sunlight at very high altitudes in the ionosphere to fly. The endurance of such aircraft will be virtually unlimited.

    Predictions for High-Speed Cosmic Flight
    Soviet scientist Dr. Kirillin wrote in the New Year's issue of "Soviet Air Force News" that flying in interstellar spaceships at speeds close to the speed of light would allow interstellar aviators to reach interstellar worlds far from Earth and return to Earth within a few years, while on Earth, many generations would have lived their lives during these few years of flight.
    Kirillin wrote while predicting this phenomenon that this is possible because, according to Einstein's theoretical speculation, the pace of time "slows down" at this speed.

    The Theory of Superconductivity
    Academician N. Bogolyubov of the Soviet Academy of Sciences has summarized and developed the ideas of modern physics and mathematics over the past fifty years and created the theory of superconductivity. This theory, published not long ago, is one of the most outstanding scientific works of 1957. The new method he created will be widely used in various departments of statistical physics, especially in metal theory, and will pave the way for the practical use of superconductors in modern technology.
(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 苏联科学新闻

1958-01-04
第6版()
专栏：苏联科学新闻

    苏联科学新闻
    未来的飞机
    “苏联空军报”在元旦这一天预测说，今后可能出现速度超过声速若干倍的火箭飞机和电离层飞机，作为在大气高层和宇宙空间飞行的机械。报纸说，装有冲压式喷气发动机的电离层飞机，将利用电离层很高的高空中被太阳光离解和电离的空气的能来飞行。这种飞机的续航力实际上将是无限的。
    对高速宇宙飞行的预见
    苏联科学家基利津副博士在新年号“苏联空军报”上撰文说，乘坐星际飞船以接近光速的速度飞行，可以使星际飞行家在若干年内到达远离地球的星际世界又回到地球上来，而在这飞行的若干年中地球上将有许多代人度过了他们的一生。
    基利津在预测这种现象的时候写道，这是可能的，因为根据爱因斯坦理论上的推测，在这种速度下时间的步伐“延缓了”。
    超导电性的理论
    苏联科学院院士尼·博戈留博夫总结和发展了五十年来现代物理学和数学的思想，创立了超导电性理论，不久前发表的这项理论是1957年最杰出的科学著作之一。他所创立的新方法将在统计物理学的各个部门，首先在金属理论方面得到广泛应用，并且为在现代技术中实际利用超导体开辟道路。
                      （据新华社讯）
