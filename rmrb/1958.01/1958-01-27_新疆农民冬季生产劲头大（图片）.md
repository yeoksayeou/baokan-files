Xinjiang Peasants Show Great Enthusiasm for Winter Production

The people of all ethnic groups in Bayandai Township, Yining County, Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture, Xinjiang, have launched a winter production upsurge. They have broken with the conventional practices of previous winters and are busy accumulating fertilizer and digging canals in the fields and villages, presenting a scene of bustling activity.

This is a member of the Red Flag Agricultural Cooperative's Seventh Production Team digging fertilizer in the enclosure.

Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Chunzhan

### 新疆农民冬季生产劲头大（图片）
武纯展
1958-01-27
第2版()
专栏：

    新疆农民冬季生产劲头大
    新疆伊犁哈萨克族自治州伊宁县巴彦岱乡各族农民掀起了冬季生产的热潮。他们打破了往年冬季的常规，在田野、村舍忙着积肥、开渠，呈现着一片繁忙景象。
    这是红旗农业社第七生产队的社员在城圈里挖肥料。
                                      新华社记者　武纯展摄
