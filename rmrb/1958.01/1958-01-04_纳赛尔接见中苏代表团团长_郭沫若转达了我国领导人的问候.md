Title: Nasser Receives Heads of Chinese and Soviet Delegations; Guo Moruo Conveys Greetings from Chinese Leaders
Author:
Date: 1958-01-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 3rd: Egyptian President Nasser received Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation to the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference, deputy head Chu Tunan, and Secretary-General Liu Ningyi at the Kubba Palace at noon on January 2nd.

They were accompanied and introduced to President Nasser by Chen Jiakang, the Chinese Ambassador to Egypt.

Guo Moruo conveyed the greetings of Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai to President Nasser and expressed his respect on behalf of the Chinese delegation.

President Nasser told the Chinese guests that he believes that the relationship between Egypt and China has been developing increasingly since the Bandung Conference in 1955.

Guo Moruo also presented gifts to Nasser on behalf of the Chinese delegation.

During the approximately half-hour meeting, Nasser had a sincere and friendly conversation with the Chinese guests.

On the same day, President Nasser also received Rashidov, the head of the Soviet delegation, and other leaders of the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 3rd: Egyptian President Nasser received a six-member Chinese student delegation visiting Egypt on January 2nd. The delegation paid tribute to President Nasser on behalf of Chinese youth and presented gifts.

Nasser expressed his gratitude for the support of the Chinese people and youth for the Egyptian people during the British, French, and Israeli aggression against Egypt, and hoped that the friendship between the two peoples would develop and mutual visits would increase.

The delegation was led by Zhang Ding, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Students' Federation. They arrived in Egypt on December 7, 1957. They visited famous cities such as Cairo, Alexandria, Port Said, Aswan, and Luxor. They are scheduled to return home on the 3rd.

### 纳赛尔接见中苏代表团团长  郭沫若转达了我国领导人的问候

1958-01-04
第5版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔接见中苏代表团团长
    郭沫若转达了我国领导人的问候
    新华社开罗3日电　埃及总统纳赛尔1月2日中午在库巴宫接见了出席亚非人民团结大会的中国代表团团长郭沫若、副团长楚图南和秘书长刘宁一。
    他们是由中国驻埃及大使陈家康陪同和介绍给纳赛尔总统的。
    郭沫若向纳赛尔总统转达了毛泽东主席和周恩来总理的问候，并且代表中国代表团向他表示敬意。
    纳赛尔总统对中国客人们说，他认为，自从1955年万隆会议以来，埃及和中国之间的关系正在日益发展。
    郭沫若还代表中国代表团向纳赛尔赠送了礼物。
    在大约半小时的会见中，纳赛尔同中国客人们进行了恳切和友好的谈话。
    当天，纳赛尔总统也接见了苏联代表团团长拉希多夫和代表团其他领导人。
    新华社开罗3日电　埃及总统纳赛尔1月2日接见了正在埃及进行访问的中国学生六人代表团。代表团代表中国青年向纳赛尔总统致敬，并且赠送了礼物。
    纳赛尔对于中国人民和青年在英、法、以三国侵略埃及期间对埃及人民的支持表示感谢，并希望两国人民之间的友谊发展和相互访问增多。
    代表团由中华全国学生联合会副秘书长张定率领。他们是在1957年12月7日到达埃及的。他们访问了开罗、亚历山大港、塞得港、阿斯旺和路克苏尔等著名城市。他们预定在3日动身回国。
