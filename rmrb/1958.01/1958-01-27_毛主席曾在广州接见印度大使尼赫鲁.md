Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Indian Ambassador Nehru in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1958-01-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 26th: Chairman Mao met with the Indian Ambassador to China, Nehru, and his wife, who are returning home after completing their term, in Guangzhou on the 23rd.

Chairman Mao had a long, friendly, and cordial conversation with Nehru and his wife.

Ambassador Nehru said: I am returning home with a deep impression of the growing friendship between China and India. The Ambassador said: Another deep impression I have is the tremendous progress China has achieved.

Chairman Mao said: I hope India will develop rapidly. All countries that agree with the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence hope to do everything possible to develop modernized industry and modernized agriculture.

Ambassador R.K. Nehru also expressed his gratitude for the full cooperation he received during his time in China. Chairman Mao said: You have done a successful job and strengthened the friendship between the two countries.

When the Chairman received the Ambassador, also present were Tao Zhu, Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and others.

Ambassador Nehru and his wife returned home via Hong Kong on the 24th.

### 毛主席曾在广州接见印度大使尼赫鲁

1958-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席曾在广州接见印度大使尼赫鲁
    新华社广州26日电　毛主席23日曾在广州接见了奉调回国的印度驻华大使尼赫鲁及其夫人。
    毛主席和尼赫鲁及其夫人作了长时间的友好、亲切的谈话。
    尼赫鲁大使说：我带着中、印两国友谊日益增长的深刻印象回国。大使说：我的另一个深刻印象是中国获得的巨大进步。
    毛主席说：希望印度很快地发展起来。所有赞成五项原则的国家，都希望能想尽一切办法，将近代化的工业、近代化的农业发展起来。
    拉·库·尼赫鲁特命全权大使还对他在中国期间得到的充分合作表示感谢。毛主席说：你做得成功，加强了两国的友谊。
    主席接见大使的时候，在座的还有中国人民政治协商委员会广东省委员会主席陶铸等人。
    尼赫鲁大使及其夫人已在24日取道香港回国。
