Title: Albania's Hoxha Hydropower Station Generates Electricity: Generating Capacity of 25,000 Kilowatts, Annual Power Generation Reaches 120 Million Kilowatt-hours
Author:
Date: 1958-01-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, January 12th: On January 11th, the twelfth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Albania, the largest hydropower station in the country, the Enver Hoxha Hydropower Station, went into operation. This hydropower station, built on the Mati River, has a generating capacity of 25,000 kilowatts and an annual power generation of 120 million kilowatt-hours. This hydropower station was built with the assistance of the Soviet Union. The Soviet Union not only helped with the design but also supplied all the machinery, equipment, high-voltage transmission lines, and substations needed for the hydropower station.

The leaders of the Albanian Labor Party and the government, Hoxha and Shehu, as well as the Soviet Ambassador to Albania, Ivanov, and others, attended the commissioning ceremony of the hydropower station. Speakers at the ceremony expressed their warm gratitude for the great assistance from the Soviet Union.

### 阿尔巴尼亚霍查水电站发电  发电能力二万五千瓩　全年发电量达一亿二千万度

1958-01-13
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚霍查水电站发电
    发电能力二万五千瓩　全年发电量达一亿二千万度
    据新华社地拉那12日电　1月11日，在阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国成立十二周年的时候，一座全国最大的水电站——恩维尔·霍查水电站投入生产了。这座建立在麦蒂河上的水电站具有二万五千瓩的发电能力，全年发电量可达一亿二千万度。这座水电站是在苏联的帮助下建成的。苏联不但帮助设计，而且还供应了水电站所需的全部机器、设备、高压输电线和变电所等。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和政府的领导人霍查、谢胡，苏联驻阿尔巴尼亚大使伊凡诺夫等参加了水电站的开工生产典礼。在典礼上讲话的人都热烈感谢苏联的伟大援助。
