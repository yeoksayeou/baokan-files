Title: Ten Major Events in the Soviet Union in 1957
Author:
Date: 1958-01-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 4th: "Moscow News" published the ten major events of the Soviet Union in 1957 on January 1, 1958:

　　The world's largest synchrophasotron (10 billion electron volts) began operation.
　　The Sixth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship was held in Moscow.
　　Two artificial satellites were successfully launched.
　　The entire Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Power Station, with a capacity of 2.1 million kilowatts, was put into production.
　　The world's largest jet airliner—the Tu-114 turboprop airliner—was manufactured.
　　Celebration of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.
　　At the military parade on Red Square on November 7th, people saw the little brother of the intercontinental ballistic missile—rocket weapons.
　　Sixty-four Communist and Workers' parties adopted a peace manifesto in Moscow.
　　The world's first atomic icebreaker "Lenin" was launched.
　　Soviet pilot Koroushkin flew a jet plane at a speed of over 2,000 kilometers per hour.

### 1957年苏联十件大事

1958-01-05
第6版()
专栏：

    1957年苏联十件大事
    新华社莫斯科4日电　1958年1月1日“莫斯科新闻”发表了1957年苏联的十件大事：
    世界最大的同步稳相加速器（一百亿电子伏特）开始运转。
    第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节在莫斯科举行。
    两颗人造卫星发射成功。
    功率为二百十万瓩的古比雪夫水力发电站全部投入生产。
    世界上最大的喷气式客机——图一一四式涡轮喷气螺旋桨客机制成。
    庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年。
    11月7日红场军事检阅中，人们看到了洲际弹道火箭的小兄弟——火箭武器。
    六十四个共产党和工人党在莫斯科通过和平宣言。
    世界上第一艘原子破冰船“列宁号”下水。
    苏联飞行员科罗乌什金驾驶一架喷气式飞机，速度达到每小时两千公里以上。
