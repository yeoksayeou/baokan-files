Title: Strengthening Soviet-German People's Friendship: Soviet Union Establishes Soviet-German Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1958-01-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 7th: According to TASS, the Soviet-German Friendship and Cultural Relations Association was officially established in the Soviet Union on January 7th. The association's task is to promote the further strengthening of fraternal friendship and cooperation between the people of the Soviet Union and the people of the German Democratic Republic.

At the inaugural meeting held on the 7th, Mrs. Popova, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, first gave the opening speech. Following this, the Soviet writer Konstantin Fedin delivered a report. He emphasized that the strengthening of friendship and mutual understanding between the Soviet and German peoples is of paramount importance for consolidating peace and security in Europe.

The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union, Mr. König, also spoke at the meeting. The conference concluded with the election of Konstantin Fedin as Chairman of the association.

### 加强苏德人民友谊  苏联成立苏德友协

1958-01-10
第6版()
专栏：

    加强苏德人民友谊
    苏联成立苏德友协
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科7日讯：苏联在1月7日正式成立苏德友好与文化联系协会。协会的任务是促进苏联人民和德意志民主共和国人民之间的兄弟友谊与合作的进一步加强。
    在7日举行的成立大会上，苏联对外文化协会理事会主席波波娃首先致开会词。接着，苏联作家康斯坦丁·费定做了报告。他强调指出，苏德两国人民的友谊和相互了解的加强对巩固欧洲和平与安全具有头等重要意义。
    德意志民主共和国驻苏联大使柯尼希也在会上讲了话。大会最后选举康·费定为协会主席。
