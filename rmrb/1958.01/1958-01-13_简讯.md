    Brief News
    Prime Minister Široký Visits Madras
    New Delhi Dispatch: Czechoslovak Prime Minister Viliam Široký and his entourage arrived in Madras, a famous city in southern India, by plane today. They are scheduled to stay in Madras for two days before visiting Calcutta.
    Prior to this, Prime Minister Široký and his entourage visited Pune and Bangalore after visiting Bombay. They were welcomed by local governments and citizens.
    Albanian Government Protests Against US Plane Intrusion into Airspace
    Tirana Dispatch: The Albanian government lodged a strong protest with the US government on January 9 against the recent intrusion of an American Air Force T-33 jet fighter into Albania's borders and airspace.

    简讯
    西罗基总理到马
  德拉斯访问
    新德里讯：捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基和他的随行人员今天乘飞机到达印度南部著名城市马德拉斯访问。他们预定在马德拉斯停留两天，然后去加尔各答访问。
    在这以前，西罗基总理和他的随行人员在访问孟买以后，先后访问了浦那和班加罗尔。他们受到了当地政府和市民的欢迎。
    阿政府抗议美机侵犯领空
    地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚政府在1月9日向美国政府提出了强烈抗议，抗议最近美国空军T—33型喷气歼击机一架侵犯阿尔巴尼亚的国境和领空。
