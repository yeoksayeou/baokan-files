Title: Cold Wave News
Author:
Date: 1958-01-12
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a cold wave message at 11:00 AM today: The cold air mass now over Western Siberia is expected to enter our country via the Mongolian People's Republic. From the 13th to the 14th, Inner Mongolia, the Northeast, North China, the Hexi Corridor, the Bohai Sea, and the northern Yellow Sea will successively experience northerly winds of level five to seven. From the 14th to the 15th, temperatures in the aforementioned areas may drop by more than ten degrees Celsius.

From the 14th to the 15th, Central South, East China, and South China will successively experience northerly winds of around level five. The surface of rivers, lakes, river valleys, the Yellow Sea, and the East China Sea will experience northerly winds of level five to seven. From the 15th to the 16th, the aforementioned areas will also experience temperature drops of more than ten degrees Celsius.

### 寒潮消息

1958-01-12
第7版()
专栏：

    寒潮消息
    新华社11日讯　中央气象科学研究所今天上午十一点发布寒潮消息：现在西西伯利亚的冷空气，预计将经蒙古人民共和国侵入我国，13日起到14日，内蒙古、东北、华北、河西走廊、渤海、黄海北部，先后将有五至七级偏北大风；14日到15日，上述地区可能降温十度以上。
    14日起到15日，中南、华东、华南地区，先后将有五级左右的偏北大风。江面、湖面、河谷、黄海和东海海面，将有五至七级偏北大风。15日到16日，上述地区也将有十度以上的降温。
