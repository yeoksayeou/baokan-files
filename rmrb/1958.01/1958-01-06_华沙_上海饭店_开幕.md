Title: "Shanghai Restaurant" Opens in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1958-01-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 5th. The "Shanghai Restaurant," located in the most bustling Constitution Square area of Warsaw, held its opening ceremony on the 4th.

Polish Minister of Domestic Trade Lesz, Counselor Yao Guang of the Chinese Embassy in Poland, and more than seventy guests from all walks of life attended to offer their congratulations.

The interior of the restaurant is decorated with a distinct Chinese flavor. The interior is hung with Chinese paintings, paper-cuts, and brocades, as well as small palace lanterns.

### 华沙“上海饭店”开幕

1958-01-06
第6版()
专栏：

    华沙“上海饭店”开幕
    据新华社华沙5日电　座落在华沙最繁华的宪法广场市区的“上海饭店”，4日举行了开幕式。
    波兰国内贸易部部长莱什、中国驻波兰大使馆参赞姚广以及各界来宾七十余人前往祝贺。
    饭店内布置得颇有中国风味。室内挂着中国的绘画、剪纸和织锦，还有小型的宫灯。
