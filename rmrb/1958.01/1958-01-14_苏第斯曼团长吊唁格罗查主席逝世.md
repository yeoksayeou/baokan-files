Title: Sudisman Extends Condolences on the Death of Chairman Groza

Author: 
Date: 1958-01-14
Page: 4

    (Reported by this newspaper) Sudisman, head of the Indonesian Communist Party delegation who visited our country after attending the October Revolution celebration in the Soviet Union, sent a letter to the Romanian Embassy in the People's Republic of China on January 10 to express condolences on the death of Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania.

### 苏第斯曼团长吊唁格罗查主席逝世

1958-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏第斯曼团长吊唁格罗查主席逝世
    本报讯　去苏联参加十月革命节庆祝典礼以后正在我国访问的印度尼西亚共产党代表团团长苏第斯曼，于1月10日致函罗马尼亚驻中华人民共和国大使馆，吊唁罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团主席格罗查的逝世。
