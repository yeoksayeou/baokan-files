Title: Yugoslav Government Protests France's Illegal Interception of Merchant Ship
Author:
Date: 1958-01-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, January 21st: The Yugoslav government lodged a serious protest with the French government on January 20th regarding the illegal interception of the Yugoslav merchant ship "Slovenia" on the high seas by a French warship. It demanded the immediate return of the cargo on board and declared that it reserved the right to demand compensation for losses.

According to reports, the merchant ship was intercepted on the 18th by two French warships 45 miles off the coast of Algeria, far from French territorial waters. At the time, the merchant ship was transporting munitions to a Moroccan company in Casablanca. The merchant ship was escorted by the French warships to Algiers in eastern Algeria, and the 148 tons of munitions on board were subsequently seized. On the 19th, the "Slovenia" was allowed to leave Algiers and continue its voyage to Casablanca.

### 南斯拉夫政府抗议法国非法拦劫商船

1958-01-22
第6版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫政府抗议法国非法拦劫商船
    据新华社贝尔格莱德21日电南斯拉夫政府20日就南斯拉夫商船“斯洛文尼亚号”在公海遭法国军舰非法拦劫一事，向法国政府提出了严重的抗议，要求立即归还船上的货物，并声明保留要求赔偿损失的权利。
    据报道，这只商船是在18日在距阿尔及利亚海岸四十五英里、远离法国领海的地方被两艘法国军舰拦截的。当时，商船正在运送军火给卡萨布兰卡的一家摩洛哥公司。商船被法国军舰押送开进了阿尔及利亚东部的阿兰，船上的一百四十八吨军火随即被劫走。19日，“斯洛文尼亚号”始被准许开出阿兰，继续驶往卡萨布兰卡。
