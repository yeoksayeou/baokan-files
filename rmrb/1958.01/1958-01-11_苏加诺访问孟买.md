Title: Sukarno Visits Bombay
Author:
Date: 1958-01-11
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 10th: Indonesian President Sukarno and his entourage arrived in Bombay by special plane on the evening of the 9th from New Delhi. He was warmly welcomed at the airport by the people of Bombay and Governor Prakasa. President Sukarno and his party will stay in Bombay for two days before visiting Egypt.

### 苏加诺访问孟买

1958-01-11
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺访问孟买
    据新华社新德里10日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺和随行人员9日傍晚从新德里乘专机到达孟买。他在机场受到孟买人民和邦长普拉卡萨的热烈欢迎。苏加诺总统一行将在孟买停留两天，然后去埃及访问。
