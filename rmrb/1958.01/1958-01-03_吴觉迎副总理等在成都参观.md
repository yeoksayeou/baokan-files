Title: Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein and Others Visit Chengdu
Author:
Date: 1958-01-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, January 1st: Burmese Federal Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein and others, accompanied by Vice Premier He Long, Sichuan Province Governor Li Dazhang, and others, visited Chengdu this morning.

This morning, Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein and others visited the Chengdu Handicraft Exhibition and the Chengdu No. 1 Silk Weaving Production Cooperative. At the Handicraft Exhibition, the distinguished guests viewed Chengdu's silver filigree, bamboo, and palm products, as well as embroidery, drawnwork, and Shu brocade handicrafts. At the Silk Weaving Production Cooperative, the distinguished guests observed production and inquired in detail about the production process of Shu brocade and the wages and benefits of the workers. Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein highly praised the handicrafts produced in Chengdu.

### 吴觉迎副总理等在成都参观

1958-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    吴觉迎副总理等在成都参观
    据新华社成都1日电　缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎等今天上午由贺龙副总理、四川省省长李大章等陪同在成都参观。
    今天上午，吴觉迎副总理等参观了成都市手工艺品展览和成都市第一丝织生产合作社。在手工艺品展览会上，贵宾们参观了成都出产的银丝、竹、棕制品以及刺绣、挑花和蜀锦等手工艺品。在丝织生产合作社里，贵宾们参观了生产情况，并且详细询问了蜀锦的生产过程和工人的工资福利。吴觉迎副总理对成都出产的手工艺品备加赞扬。
