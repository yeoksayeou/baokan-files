Title: Pellet Drum
Author: Ye Shumin, Zhong Hui
Date: 1958-01-12
Page: 8
Veilfu

    Pellet Drum
    In winter, many people like to use face cream, such as "Veilfu" and "Bai Que Ling". These empty boxes, with a little processing, can become toys for children.
    Here is an introduction to a "pellet drum" (see attached picture). The method is: first clean the "Veilfu" empty box, then use nails to make a small hole in the left, right, and bottom center of the box (nail from the outside in); then trim a smooth wooden stick about three inches long, find a screw, pass it through the small hole at the bottom of the box, and screw it together with one end of the wooden stick; finally, find two strands of thread about two-thirds the length of the box, pass them through the left and right holes (tie a knot on the inside of the box), and thread a bead on each end outside the box. Children can shake it and play.
    (Ye Shumin, Zhong Hui)

### 拨啷鼓
叶淑敏  锺惠
1958-01-12
第8版()
专栏：维尔肤

    拨啷鼓
    冬天，许多人都爱用擦面油，像“维尔肤”、“百雀羚”等。这些空盒，只要加一加工，就能成为小孩子的玩具。
    这里介绍一种“拨啷鼓”（见附图）。它的做法是：先将“维尔肤”空盒擦净，然后在盒子的左、右和下方正处，用钉子各打一小孔（从外向里钉）；再修饰一根长约三寸左右的光滑木棒，找一个螺丝钉，从盒子下方的小孔内通出来，与木棒的一头拧接一起；最后再找两根相当盒子三分之二长的丝线，穿过左右两孔（盒内的一头打一个节）盒外的一头各穿一个珠子。孩子们就可以摇着它玩了。
    （叶淑敏  锺惠）
