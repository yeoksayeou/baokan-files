    Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Crown Prince, Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Minister of Defense of the Kingdom of Yemen, Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, on December 31, 1957, who was invited to visit China. The picture shows the scene of the meeting. From right to left: Premier Zhou Enlai, Crown Prince Badr of Yemen, State Minister Amri, Shami. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Huaxue photographed.
