Author:
Date: 1958-01-29
Page: 7

Column: At School

    Schistosomiasis Treatment Team
    Suzhou is the area with the most serious schistosomiasis epidemic. The research on the prevention and treatment of schistosomiasis has become the central task of the scientific research work of Suzhou Medical College. After research and decision, Suzhou Medical College (including affiliated hospitals) and the Second People's Hospital will dispatch manpower to organize two treatment teams to Dongshan and Muc渎 for prevention and treatment research.

### 血吸虫病治疗小组

1958-01-29
第7版()
专栏：在学校里

    血吸虫病治疗小组
    苏州是血吸虫病流行最严重的地区，防治血吸虫病的研究，成为苏州医学院科学研究工作的中心任务。经研究决定，由苏州医学院（包括附属医院）及第二人民医院，抽调人力组织两个治疗组至东山、木凟两地进行防治研究工作。
