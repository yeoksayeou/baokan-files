Title: A Cartoon Exposing Waste
Author:
Date: 1958-01-09
Page: 1

    A Cartoon Exposing Waste
    Yu Ahua, an employee of the Meilin Cannery, wrote on a cartoon exposing waste: "A comrade in the food workshop received more than 40 masks, and washing them at home filled a washbasin. May I ask the management comrades of the food workshop, why are you so accommodating to such a person?"

### 一张揭发浪费的漫画（图片）

1958-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    一张揭发浪费的漫画
    梅林罐头食品厂职工俞阿华在揭发浪费的漫画上写道：“食品车间有位同志，他一个人领了口罩四十多只，在家里洗涤一次，就是盛满一个面盆，请问食品车间的管理同志，为什么对这样的人也是有求必应。”
