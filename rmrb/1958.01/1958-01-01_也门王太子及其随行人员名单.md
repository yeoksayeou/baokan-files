Title: List of the Crown Prince of Yemen and His Entourage

Author:
Date: 1958-01-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. The list of the Crown Prince of Yemen, Mohammed al-Badr, and his entourage is as follows:

    His Royal Highness Saif al-Islam Mohammed al-Badr, Crown Prince of Yemen, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defense of the Kingdom of Yemen
    Qadi Mohammed al-Amri, Minister of State
    Qadi Mohammed al-Shami, Minister of State
    Hassan Baghdadi, Advisor
    Lieutenant Colonel Abdullah Dabi, Aide-de-camp
    Ali Mawkasas, Translator
    Abdullah Tamin, Private Secretary
