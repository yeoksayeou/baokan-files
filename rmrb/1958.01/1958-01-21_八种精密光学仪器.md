Title: Eight Types of Precision Optical Instruments
Author:
Date: 1958-01-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Report: Among the new products successfully trial-produced last year by the Yunnan Optical Instrument Factory, eight types have reached the top level in the country in terms of precision, quality, and performance. Among them are the spring micrometer, used for high-precision measurement in the machinery manufacturing industry; and the 1500x microscope, essential for scientific research.

### 八种精密光学仪器

1958-01-21
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    八种精密光学仪器
    据新华社讯　云南光学仪器厂去年试制成功的新产品中，有八种的精密度、质量和性能在国内达到了头等水平。其中有机械制造工业中用作高精度测量的弹簧万分仪；有科学研究中必备的一千五百倍显微镜。
