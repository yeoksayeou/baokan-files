Title: Preparatory Meeting of the National People's Congress Held Today
Author:
Date: 1958-01-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 24th. The 92nd meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, held on the afternoon of the 24th, decided to hold a preparatory meeting for the Fifth Session of the First National People's Congress on January 25th.

    At today's meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai explained the State Council's proposals to be submitted to the Fifth Session of the First National People's Congress regarding the implementation of the 1957 state budget and the draft 1958 state budget, the draft 1958 national economic plan, the Chinese phonetic alphabet scheme, and the adjustment of the organizational structure of the State Council.

### 全国人民代表大会预备会议今日举行

1958-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会预备会议今日举行
    新华社24日讯　二十四日下午举行的全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九十二次会议，决定在一月二十五日举行第一届全国人民代表大会第五次会议预备会议。
    在今天的会议上，周恩来总理就国务院准备提交第一届全国人民代表大会第五次会议的关于一九五七年国家预算执行情况和一九五八年国家预算草案、关于一九五八年度国民经济计划草案、汉语拼音方案、国务院所属组织机构调整等议案作了说明。
