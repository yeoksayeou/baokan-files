Title: Soviet Museum Experts Lay Flowers at Qi Baishi's Tomb

Author:

Date: 1958-01-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, January 15th: Pushkarev, Director of the Russian Museum in Leningrad, Soviet Union, and Novouspensky, Director of the Oil Painting Department of the Russian Museum, who recently came to Beijing, accompanied by staff from the Chinese Artists Association and Qi Baishi's family, laid a wreath at Qi Baishi's tomb on the 15th to express their condolences on the death of Qi Baishi, China's most outstanding contemporary painter.

### 苏博物馆专家  到齐白石墓地献花

1958-01-17
第7版()
专栏：

    苏博物馆专家
    到齐白石墓地献花
    新华社15日讯　最近来北京的苏联列宁格勒俄罗斯博物馆馆长普什科廖夫和俄罗斯博物馆油画馆主任诺沃乌斯宾斯基，15日由中国美术家协会工作人员和齐白石家属陪同，到齐白石墓地献花圈，对中国当代最卓越的画家齐白石的逝世表示悼念。
