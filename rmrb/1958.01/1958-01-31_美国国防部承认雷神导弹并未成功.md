Title: U.S. Department of Defense Admits Thor Missile Unsuccessful
Author:
Date: 1958-01-31
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 30th - The U.S. Air Force announced on the 29th that the Department of Defense's announcement on the 28th about the successful test launch of the "Thor" intermediate-range missile was inaccurate. The Air Force said: After re-verification, it was found that "the missile deviated from the prescribed course, so it was not a very successful flight."

### 美国国防部承认雷神导弹并未成功

1958-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

    美国国防部承认雷神导弹并未成功
    据新华社30日讯　华盛顿消息：美国空军29日宣布，国防部28日发表的关于“雷神”式中程导弹试射成功的消息是不确实的。空军说：在经过重新核查以后，发现“导弹越出了规定的路线，因此这不是很成功的飞行”。
