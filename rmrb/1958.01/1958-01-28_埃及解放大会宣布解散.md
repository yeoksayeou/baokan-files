Title: Egyptian Liberation Rally Announces Dissolution
Author:
Date: 1958-01-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 26th: The Egyptian Liberation Rally officially announced its dissolution today at a conference held here by the organization. The Liberation Rally will transfer all of its institutions to the National Union.

The Egyptian Liberation Rally was established by the Egyptian government in 1953 as a mass organization to replace the various dissolved Egyptian political parties during the transitional period of 1953-1956. In May of last year, President Nasser issued a decree establishing the National Union to replace this organization.

### 埃及解放大会宣布解散

1958-01-28
第5版()
专栏：

    埃及解放大会宣布解散
    据新华社开罗26日电　埃及解放大会今天在这个组织在这里举行的一个大会上正式宣布解散。解放大会将把它的一切机构移交给民族联盟。
    埃及解放大会在1953年由埃及政府宣布成立，作为在1953—1956年过渡时期中代替埃及各被解散政党的群众组织。去年5月纳赛尔总统颁布法令，成立民族联盟来代替这个组织。
