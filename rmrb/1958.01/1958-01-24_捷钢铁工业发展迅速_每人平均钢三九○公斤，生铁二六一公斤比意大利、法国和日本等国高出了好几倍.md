Title: Czechoslovak Steel Industry Develops Rapidly, Average Steel Production Reaches 390 Kilograms per Person, Pig Iron 261 Kilograms, Several Times Higher Than Italy, France, and Japan

Author:

Date: 1958-01-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 23rd. Official materials released here indicate that Czechoslovakia produced a total of 5.17 million tons of steel, 3.56 million tons of pig iron, and 3.49 million tons of steel products in 1957.

Due to developments in recent years, Czechoslovakia's steel production per capita has reached the forefront globally. In 1956, its per capita steel production reached 369 kilograms, three times the per capita steel production of Italy, and surpassing the per capita steel production of France, Australia, Sweden, and other countries. In the same year, Czechoslovakia's per capita iron production reached 248 kilograms, equivalent to three and a half times that of Japan and six times that of Italy. By 1957, Czechoslovakia's steel and pig iron production in this regard had increased to 390 kilograms and 261 kilograms, respectively. In the coming years, Czechoslovakia's steel industry will further develop. It is expected to produce 6.5 million tons of steel by 1960 and approximately 10 million tons of steel by 1967.

### 捷钢铁工业发展迅速  每人平均钢三九○公斤，生铁二六一公斤比意大利、法国和日本等国高出了好几倍

1958-01-24
第6版()
专栏：

    捷钢铁工业发展迅速
    每人平均钢三九○公斤，生铁二六一公斤比意大利、法国和日本等国高出了好几倍
    据新华社布拉格23日电此间公布的正式材料表明，捷克斯洛伐克1957年共生产了五百一十七万吨钢、三百五十六万吨生铁和三百四十九万吨钢材。
    由于最近几年来的发展，捷克斯洛伐克的钢铁产量按人口平均计算已经居于世界前列地位。在1956年，它的按人口平均的钢产量达到了三百六十九公斤，等于意大利按人口平均的钢产量的三倍，并且超过了法国、澳大利亚、瑞典等国按人口平均的钢产量。同年，捷克斯洛伐克按人口平均的铁产量达到了二百四十八公斤，相当于日本的三倍半和意大利的六倍。到1957年，捷克斯洛伐克钢和生铁在这方面的产量指标又分别增加到了三百九十公斤和二百六十一公斤。在今后的一些年中，捷克斯洛伐克的钢铁工业还要进一步发展。预计到1960年，它将生产六百五十万吨钢，到1967年将生产约一千万吨钢。
