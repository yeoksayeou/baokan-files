Title: NPC Standing Committee, State Council, CPC Central Committee, CPPCC Send Condolences on the Passing of Chairman Groza; Our National Leaders Visit Romanian Embassy in China to Offer Condolences
Author:
Date: 1958-01-08
Page: 1

The NPC Standing Committee of The People's Republic of China, The State Council of The People's Republic of China, and Central Committee of The Communist Party of China sent a telegram of condolence on the death of the Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, Petru Groza. The telegram of condolence is as follows:

To the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, and the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party:

We were shocked to learn of the unfortunate passing of Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic. With deep sorrow, we represent the Chinese people in expressing our deepest condolences to you, and through you, to the Romanian people.

Dr. Petru Groza was a loyal son of the Romanian people. He dedicated his life to the cause of peace, democracy, and socialism for the Romanian people. His passing is a great loss not only for the Romanian people but also for peace-loving people all over the world. Dr. Petru Groza was a close friend of the Chinese people, and he will forever live in the hearts of Chinese people.

The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
The State Council of the People's Republic of China
The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Beijing, January 7, 1958

Xinhua News Agency report: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference sent a telegram of condolence on the death of the Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, Petru Groza. The telegram of condolence is as follows:
To the National Committee of the People's Democratic Front of the Romanian People's Republic:

We were shocked to learn of the unfortunate death of Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic and Chairman of the National Committee of the People's Democratic Front of Romania, and we offer our deep condolences and ask that you convey our warmest condolences to Dr. Petru Groza's family.

The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
Beijing, January 7, 1958

### 我人大常委会、国务院、中共中央、政协  致电吊唁格罗查主席逝世  我国家领导人到罗驻华大使馆吊唁

1958-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我人大常委会、国务院、中共中央、政协
    致电吊唁格罗查主席逝世
    我国家领导人到罗驻华大使馆吊唁
    本报讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会、中华人民共和国国务院、中国共产党中央委员会，致电吊唁罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查逝世。唁电如下：罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会：
    惊悉罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查博士不幸逝世，我们怀着沉痛的心情代表中国人民，向你们，并且通过你们，向罗马尼亚人民表示深切的哀悼。
    彼·格罗查博士是罗马尼亚人民忠实的儿子。他把他的一生献给了罗马尼亚人民的和平、民主和社会主义事业。他的逝世不仅对于罗马尼亚人民，而且对于全世界爱好和平的人民都是重大的损失。彼·格罗查博士是中国人民的亲密的朋友，他将永远活在中国人民的心里。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会
    中华人民共和国国务院
    中国共产党中央委员会
                        1958年1月7日于北京
    本报讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会致电吊唁罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查逝世。唁电如下：罗马尼亚人民共和国人民民主阵线全国委员会：
    惊悉罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团主席、罗马尼亚人民民主阵线全国委员会主席彼·格罗查博士不幸逝世，谨致沉痛的哀悼，并且请向彼·格罗查博士的家属转致最亲切的慰问。
          中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
                        1958年1月7日于北京
