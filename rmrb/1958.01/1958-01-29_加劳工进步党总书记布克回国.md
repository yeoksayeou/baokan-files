Title: Canadian Labor Progressive Party General Secretary Buck Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 28th: The General Secretary of the Canadian Labor Progressive Party, Tim Buck, left Beijing by plane on the morning of January 28th to return home. Those who went to the airport to see him off included Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, An Ziwen and Liu Ningyi, members of the CPC Central Committee, and Xiong Fu and Li Qixin, responsible staff members of the CPC Central Committee organs.

### 加劳工进步党总书记布克回国

1958-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    加劳工进步党总书记布克回国
    新华社28日讯　加拿大劳工进步党总书记蒂姆·布克在28日上午乘飞机离北京回国。到机场欢送的，有中共中央副主席刘少奇，中共中央委员安子文、刘宁一，中共中央机关负责工作人员熊复、李启新。
