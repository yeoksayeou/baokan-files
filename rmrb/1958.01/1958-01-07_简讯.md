    Brief News
    Chinese Acrobatic Troupe Extends Visit to Tunisia
    Cairo Dispatch: The Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe, currently visiting Tunisia, has decided to extend its visit in Tunisia at the request of the Tunisian people. The Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe arrived in Tunisia on December 4th of last year after concluding its visit to Lebanon.
    "August 1st" Football Team Leaves Vietnam for Home
    Hanoi Dispatch: The Chinese "August 1st" Football Team's Second Team, which was invited to visit Vietnam, left Hanoi by car on the morning of the 6th to return home. During its fifteen-day friendly visit to Vietnam, the "August 1st" Second Team played three matches in Hanoi and Haiphong, with one win, one draw, and one loss.
    Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein Returns to Rangoon
    Rangoon Dispatch: Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and the Burmese Goodwill Economic Delegation he led returned to Rangoon by special plane on the afternoon of the 6th from Kunming. Since October of last year, U Kyaw Nyein has visited the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Romania, the Mongolian People's Republic, and China.

### 简讯

1958-01-07
第6版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    我杂技团延长在突尼斯的访问
    开罗讯：正在突尼斯访问的中国杂技艺术团应突尼斯人民的要求，已经决定延长在突尼斯的访问日期。中国杂技艺术团是在结束了在黎巴嫩的访问以后，去年12月4日到达突尼斯的。
    我“八一”足球队离越南返国
    河内讯：应邀访问越南的中国“八一”足球队二队，6日上午乘车离开河内返国。“八一”二队在越南的十五天的友好访问中，先后在河内和海防进行了三场比赛，一胜一和一负。
    缅副总理吴觉迎返抵仰光
    仰光讯：缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎和他率领的缅甸友好经济考察团在6日下午乘专机从昆明回到仰光。吴觉迎去年10月起先后访问过苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、蒙古人民共和国和中国。
