Title: Argentine Government Trade Delegation to Visit Soviet Union and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-01-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires: According to an announcement by the Argentine government, an Argentine government trade delegation will soon visit the Soviet Union and Eastern European People's Democratic countries.

### 阿根廷政府贸易代表团将访问苏联等国

1958-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    阿根廷政府贸易代表团
    将访问苏联等国
    据新华社讯　布宜诺斯艾利斯消息：据阿根廷政府宣布，一个阿根廷政府贸易代表团即将前往苏联和东欧人民民主国家访问。
