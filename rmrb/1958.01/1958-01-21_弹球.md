    Pinball
    Pinball is a Soviet children's toy. This toy is simple to make and fun to play with.
    Use a rectangular cardboard box lid, stand a thin piece of wood (cardboard can also be used) at one end of the lid to act as a backboard, then find a small piece of wood and nail it on to hold the backboard in place to form a basketball stand (see attached diagram). Make a basketball hoop on the backboard with thick lead wire, and then make a net out of thread to cover the hoop. In the center of the other end of the box, nail a small piece of wood, and nail a piece of relatively hard and elastic thin iron sheet on the wood. Dig a hole in the iron sheet for a ping-pong ball to fit in.
    When playing, press the iron sheet with your thumb or forefinger, and use the elasticity of the iron sheet to shoot the ping-pong ball into the basket. This toy can train children's accuracy and flexibility of movement.

### 弹球

1958-01-21
第8版()
专栏：

    弹球
    弹球是苏联的一种儿童玩具。这种玩具，做起来简单，玩起来有趣。
    用一个长方形的大纸盒盖，在盖子的一端竖起一块代替篮球板的薄木板
  （硬纸板也可以），然后找一小块木头用钉子钉上，把球板卡住成为一个球架（见附图）。在球板上，用粗铅丝做成球篮，再用线做成球网套。在盒子的另一端的正中，钉一块小木头，在木头上，钉一块较硬而带有弹性的薄铁片，铁片上挖一个放乒乓球用的洞。
    玩的时候，用拇指或食指按动铁片，利用铁片的弹力，把乒乓球投篮入里。这种玩具，能锻炼儿童的动作的准确性与灵活性。
