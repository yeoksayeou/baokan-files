Title: Czechoslovakia to Build Atomic Power Station This Year
Author:
Date: 1958-01-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 5th: According to a report from the Czechoslovak News Agency, Czechoslovakia will begin construction this year on an atomic power station with a generating capacity of 150,000 kilowatts.

Preparations for the construction of the country's first such nuclear power plant will be fully launched in the second half of 1958, which will place Czechoslovakia among the industrially advanced countries in the peaceful use of nuclear energy. When this nuclear power plant goes into production, besides Czechoslovakia, only the Soviet Union, the United States, and Great Britain will possess such high-power atomic power stations.

The construction of this nuclear power plant will lay the foundation for the construction of a network of atomic power plants in Czechoslovakia, which will make it possible for all the increased electricity in 1970 to be supplied by nuclear power plants.

### 捷今年兴建原子能发电站

1958-01-06
第6版()
专栏：

    捷今年兴建原子能发电站
    据新华社布拉格5日电　捷克斯洛伐克通讯社的消息说，捷克斯洛伐克今年将开始兴建发电能力为十五万瓩的原子能发电站。
    兴建全国第一座这种核电力工厂的准备工作将在1958年下半年全面展开，这将使捷克斯洛伐克居于和平利用核能的工业先进国之列。当这个核电力工厂投入生产的时候，除捷克斯洛伐克外，将只有苏联、美国、英国拥有这种高功率的原子能发电站。
    兴建这一个核电力工厂将为在捷克斯洛伐克兴建原子电力工厂网打下基础，它将使得在1970年时全部增加的电力都由核电力工厂供给。
