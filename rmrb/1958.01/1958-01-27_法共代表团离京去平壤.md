Title: French Communist Party Delegation Leaves Beijing for Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1958-01-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: The French Communist Party delegation, headed by Victor Michaut, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, concluded its visit to Vietnam and returned to Beijing on the 24th of this month. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, the delegation departed for Pyongyang on the 25th to pay a visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea for several days.

### 法共代表团离京去平壤

1958-01-27
第6版()
专栏：

    法共代表团离京去平壤
    据新华社26日讯　以法国共产党中央政治局委员维美徐为首的法共代表团结束了在越南的访问，于本月24日回到北京。代表团应朝鲜劳动党中央的邀请，已在25日启程前往平壤，将在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国进行为期数天的访问参观。
