Title: Chinese Medical Association Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-01-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The Chinese Medical Association delegation to Japan returned to Beijing today.

The Chinese Medical Association delegation visited Japan from December 7, 1957, at the invitation of the Japanese medical community. During its stay in Japan, the delegation visited medical research and educational institutions in Tokyo, Chiba, Osaka, Kyoto, and other places, and held academic seminars with local prominent scholars.

### 中华医学会访日代表团回到北京

1958-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    中华医学会访日代表团回到北京
    新华社6日讯　中华医学会访日代表团今天回到北京。
    中华医学会访日代表团应日本医学界的邀请在1957年12月7日赴日本访问，代表团在日本期间，曾访问了东京，千叶、大阪、京都等地的医疗研究教育机构，并和当地著名学者举行了学术座谈会。
