Title: Tianjin Football Team Invited to Visit Egypt
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-15
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, January 14th: The Tianjin Football Team, invited to visit Egypt, Sudan, and Syria, departed from Beijing by plane today to Egypt. This is the first time Chinese athletes have been invited to visit a foreign country in 1958.

　　The Tianjin Football Team consists of twenty-one members, led by Ji Peifang, Vice Chairman of the Tianjin Branch of the All-China Sports Federation. They will visit these three countries for about a month, holding approximately seven or eight friendly matches.

### 天津足球队应邀到埃及访问

1958-01-15
第7版()
专栏：

    天津足球队
    应邀到埃及访问
    据新华社14日讯　应邀到埃及、苏丹和叙利亚访问的天津足球队，今天由北京乘飞机启程去埃及。这是1958年中国运动员第一次应邀出国访问。
    天津足球队共二十一人，由中华全国体育总会天津分会副主席纪裴芳领队。他们这次将先后在这三个国家访问一个月左右，约举行七、八场友谊比赛。
