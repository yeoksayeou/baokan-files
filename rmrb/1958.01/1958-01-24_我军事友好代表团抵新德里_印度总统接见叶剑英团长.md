Title: Our Military Friendship Delegation Arrives in New Delhi; Indian President Receives Head of Delegation Ye Jianying

Author:

Date: 1958-01-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 23rd: The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, led by Marshal Ye Jianying, arrived here by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Indian government.

At the airport, the delegation was welcomed by the Indian Deputy Minister of Defense Majithia and Raghuramaiah, as well as other senior Indian military officers. Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili was at the airport to welcome the delegation. Also at the airport to welcome the delegation were the diplomatic envoys of the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Hungary, Yugoslavia, Mongolia, and Vietnam to India, as well as military attachés from some other countries' embassies in India.

The Chinese military delegation will attend the Indian Republic Day celebrations to be held in Delhi on January 26. They are scheduled to visit India for five weeks, touring Indian military facilities in many places.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 23rd: Indian President Prasad received Marshal Ye Jianying, head of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, and all members of the delegation at the Presidential Palace this afternoon. Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili accompanied the delegation during the meeting.

Marshal Ye Jianying presented gifts on behalf of Chairman Mao Zedong to President Prasad.

President Prasad also hosted a tea party to entertain the delegation.

Earlier, the delegation called on Indian Defense Minister Menon.

### 我军事友好代表团抵新德里  印度总统接见叶剑英团长

1958-01-24
第6版()
专栏：

    我军事友好代表团抵新德里
    印度总统接见叶剑英团长
    据新华社新德里23日电　由叶剑英元帅率领的中国军事友好代表团，应印度政府的邀请，今天下午乘飞机到达这里。
    在机场上，代表团受到印度国防部副部长马吉蒂亚和拉古拉迈雅以及其他印度高级军官的欢迎。中国驻印度大使潘自力到机场欢迎代表团。到机场欢迎的还有苏、波、捷、罗、匈、南、蒙和越南驻印度的外交使节，以及其他一些国家驻印度大使馆的武官。
    中国军事代表团将参加1月26日在德里举行的印度共和国国庆典礼。他们预定在印度进行为时五星期的访问，到许多地方参观印度的军事设施。
    据新华社新德里23日电　印度总统普拉沙德今天下午在总统府接见了中国军事友好代表团团长叶剑英元帅和全体团员。陪同受接见的有中国驻印度大使潘自力。
    叶剑英元帅代表毛泽东主席向普拉沙德总统赠送了礼物。
    普拉沙德总统还举行茶会招待代表团。
    在早些时候，代表团拜访了印度国防部长梅农。
