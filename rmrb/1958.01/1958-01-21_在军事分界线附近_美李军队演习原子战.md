Title: US-ROK Armies Conduct Atomic War Exercises Near Military Demarcation Line
Author:
Date: 1958-01-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 20th. U.S. troops and the Syngman Rhee army began a large-scale atomic "strike" exercise today, five days in duration, north of Seoul, only a few miles from the military demarcation line. According to U.S. news agencies, this is the largest field exercise conducted by U.S. and Rhee troops since the Korean War armistice.

### 在军事分界线附近  美李军队演习原子战

1958-01-21
第5版()
专栏：

    在军事分界线附近
    美李军队演习原子战
    据新华社平壤20日电　美国军队和李承晚军队今天开始在汉城以北距离军事分界线只有几里的地区举行为期五天的大规模原子“出击”演习。据美国通讯社报道，这是朝鲜战争停止以来美李军队的一次规模最大的野战演习。
