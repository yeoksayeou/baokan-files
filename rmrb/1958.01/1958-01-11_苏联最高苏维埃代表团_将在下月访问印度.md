Title: Delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to Visit India Next Month
Author:
Date: 1958-01-11
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th - TASS reported from Moscow on the 9th: The Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, having accepted the invitation from the Indian Parliament, has decided to send a delegation to visit India in February of this year.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团  将在下月访问印度

1958-01-11
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团
    将在下月访问印度
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科9日讯：苏联最高苏维埃接受印度议会的邀请，决定在今年2月间派代表团到印度访问。
