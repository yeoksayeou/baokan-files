Title: Malta Dockworkers Strike Protesting British Authorities' Dismissal of Workers
Author:
Date: 1958-01-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Valletta, 4th - Twelve thousand naval dockworkers in Malta went on strike at noon on the 3rd to protest the British authorities' commencement of dismissing Maltese dockworkers.

Maltese Prime Minister Mintoff stated on December 30th that unless Britain rescinds its decision to dismiss seven thousand Maltese dockworkers, it will no longer be able to rule the island of Malta, even with the use of hydrogen bombs.

### 马耳他船坞工人罢工  抗议英当局解雇工人

1958-01-05
第5版()
专栏：

    马耳他船坞工人罢工
    抗议英当局解雇工人
    据新华社4日讯　瓦利塔消息：马耳他岛一万二千名海军船坞工人3日中午举行罢工，抗议英国当局开始解雇马耳他船坞工人。
    马耳他总理明托夫在12月30日声明，除非英国取消解雇七千名马耳他船坞工人的决定，否则它即使使用氢弹也不能再统治马耳他岛。
