Title: On the Construction Site of Fularji Heavy Machinery Plant (Picture)
Author: Hu Wei
Date: 1958-01-08
Page: 3

    On the Construction Site of Fularji Heavy Machinery Plant
    Workers constructing the Fularji Heavy Machinery Plant have expressed their determination to conscientiously implement the policy of "more, faster, better, and more economical," and ensure that the plant starts producing large-scale rolling mills and smelting equipment needed for our country's construction ahead of schedule. This is the construction site of the steel casting workshop. Workers are using cranes to install components.
  　Xinhua News Agency reporter Hu Wei photo

### 在富拉尔基重型机器厂工地上（图片）
胡伟
1958-01-08

专栏：

    在富拉尔基重型机器厂工地上
    建设富拉尔基重型机器厂的职工，纷纷表示要认真贯彻“多快好省”的方针，保证使这个厂提前开始生产我国建设所需要的大型轧钢机和冶炼设备。这是铸钢车间工地。工人们正在用吊车安装构件。
  　新华社记者　胡伟摄
