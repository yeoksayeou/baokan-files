Title: Learning Pinyin Program Promotion Month to be Launched in Shanxi
Author: Li Ping
Date: 1958-01-19
Page: 7

Learning Pinyin Program Promotion Month
    Will be Launched in Shanxi

    (Our dispatch) Taiyuan -- The Shanxi Provincial Putonghua Promotion Working Committee and the Provincial Department of Education have decided to launch a province-wide promotion and learning month for the "Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme (Draft)" and the promotion of Putonghua from early February to early March.

    During this promotion and learning month, the main focus should be on explaining the significance of formulating and implementing the "Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme (Draft)"; explaining why Putonghua should be promoted, the relationship between the "Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme (Draft)" and the promotion of Putonghua; and explaining the current tasks of language reform. It is required that all counties and cities conduct publicity to the broad masses through newspapers, radio broadcasts, blackboard newspapers, movies, and other means. Teachers and students of universities, middle schools, and primary schools, as well as cadres of government agencies, organizations, and military units, are required to learn the Chinese phonetic alphabet. In order to better carry out publicity and learning activities in various regions, the Provincial Putonghua Promotion Working Committee has printed five kinds of promotional materials, including "The Main Current Tasks of Language Reform and the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme (Draft)," and four kinds of learning materials for use in various regions. At the same time, the Provincial Putonghua Promotion Working Committee decided to hold the first Chinese phonetic alphabet learning class starting from January 20th. (Li Ping)

### 学习拼音方案宣传月  将在山西开展
李平
1958-01-19
第7版()
专栏：

    学习拼音方案宣传月
    将在山西开展
    本报讯　山西省推广普通话工作委员会和省教育厅，决定从2月初到3月初在全省范围开展宣传、学习汉语拼音方案（草案）和推广普通话月。
    在这次宣传、学习月中，主要应说明制订和推行汉语拼音方案（草案）的意义；说明为什么要推广普通话、汉语拼音方案（草案）和推广普通话的关系；说明文字改革的当前任务。要求各县、市应通过报纸、广播、黑板报、电影等方式向广大群众进行宣传。要求大、中、小学师生、机关、团体和部队干部都要学习汉语拼音字母。为了使各地更好地开展宣传和学习活动，省推广普通话工作委员会印发了“文字改革当前的主要任务和汉语拼音方案（草案）”等五种宣传资料和四种学习材料，供各地使用。同时省推广普通话工作委员会决定由1月20日起举办第一期汉语拼音字母学习班。　　（李平）
