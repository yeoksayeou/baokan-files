Title: The Biggest Joke of Last Year
Author:
Date: 1958-01-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency

    What was the most ridiculous thing in the world in 1957? According to the results of a poll conducted by the "Free Press" newspaper group in Bombay, India, the answer is: the failure of the American "artificial satellite" launch.
    Most of the Indian newspaper readers who participated in this survey also believed that the most important scientific achievement of last year was the Soviet artificial Earth satellite.

### 去年最大的笑柄

1958-01-22
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    去年最大的笑柄
    在1957年，世界上最可笑的事情是什么？据印度孟买“自由新闻”系的报纸举办民意测验的结果，答案是：美国“人造卫星”发射的失败。
    大多数参加这个测验的印度报纸读者还认为：去年最重要的科学成就是苏联的人造地球卫星。　（新华社）
