Title: West German Economic Minister Asks People to Tighten Belts for Military Expansion
Author:
Date: 1958-01-17
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, January 15th: West German Economic Minister Erhard demanded on the 13th that the West German people "endure material sacrifices" for West Germany's further military expansion and "the defense of the free world."

Erhard said in a speech on Bavarian radio that West German workers should not only no longer shorten working hours, but should also extend their working hours by one hour per day. He demanded that West German trade unions abandon demands for higher wages and shorter working hours.

### 西德经济部长  要人民勒紧裤带扩军

1958-01-17
第5版()
专栏：

    西德经济部长
    要人民勒紧裤带扩军
    据新华社15日讯　波恩消息：西德经济部长艾哈德13日要求西德人民为了西德的进一步扩军和“自由世界的防务”而“忍受物质上的牺牲”。
    艾哈德在巴伐利亚广播电台发表演说时说，西德工人不但不应该再缩短工作时间，而且还应当每天延长劳动时间一小时。他要求西德工会放弃关于提高工资和缩短劳动时间的要求。
