Why Do People Use Their Right Hand More Often?

    According to scientists' research, using the left hand for work puts a greater burden on the heart, and the pulse rate is higher; while using the right hand for work puts less burden on the heart, and the pulse rate is lower. This is because the human heart is closer to the left side. Also, the right hand is controlled by the left hemisphere of the brain, and the left hand is controlled by the right hemisphere of the brain. The left hemisphere of the human brain is heavier than the right hemisphere, and the blood circulation and nervous system are more developed. Therefore, when directing the right hand to move, it is more flexible. This is also a reason why humans use the right hand more often. However, scientists believe that this issue still needs further research, and this explanation is not the final conclusion.
                              (Yu)

### 为什么人们做事多用右手？

1958-01-30
第8版()
专栏：为什么

    为什么人们做事多用右手？
    据科学家们的研究，用左手工作，心脏的负担比较大，脉搏跳动的次数也比较多；而用右手工作，心脏的负担比较小，脉搏跳动的次数也比较少。这是因为人的心脏靠近左边的缘故。还有，右手是受大脑左半球控制的，左手是受大脑右半球控制的。人的大脑，左半球比右半球的重量要重一些，血液循环和神经系统要发达些，因此，指挥右手活动的时候，比较灵活，这也是造成人类用右手多的一个原因。不过，科学家们认为，这个问题还有继续研究的必要，现在这样解释，并非是最后的定论。
                              （育）
