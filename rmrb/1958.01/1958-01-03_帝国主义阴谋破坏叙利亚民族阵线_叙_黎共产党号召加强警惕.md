Title: Imperialist Conspiracy to Sabotage Syrian National Front; Syrian-Lebanese Communist Party Calls for Increased Vigilance
Author:
Date: 1958-01-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, January 1st: "Al-Nour" newspaper published on December 31, 1957, an important instruction from the Syrian-Lebanese Communist Party to its members. This instruction calls on all members to pay attention to strengthening the domestic national front and to be vigilant against any imperialist conspiracies to undermine this national front.

The instruction pointed out that after the US imperialists and their agents' conspiracy to invade Syria failed, "they have not abandoned their plan to realize this conspiracy whenever they have the opportunity. In an attempt to create this opportunity, the imperialists and their agents have launched an offensive against Syria's internal front."

The instruction detailed the importance of the national front and exposed the imperialists' conspiracy to create divisions within the national front. It said, "Any attempt to create divisions between the members of the national front, especially between the Communists and the Arab Ba'ath Socialist Party, no matter what the reason or excuse, will not please anyone except the imperialists and their agents."

Reports here indicate that activities are underway to organize a new group including the Arab Ba'ath Socialist Party and the National Party. This matter has attracted the attention of public opinion here.

### 帝国主义阴谋破坏叙利亚民族阵线  叙—黎共产党号召加强警惕

1958-01-03
第6版()
专栏：

    帝国主义阴谋破坏叙利亚民族阵线
    叙—黎共产党号召加强警惕
    据新华社大马士革1日电　“光明报”1957年12月31日刊载了叙利亚—黎巴嫩共产党给它的党员的重要指示，这项指示号召全体党员注意加强国内的民族阵线，并且警惕帝国主义破坏这种民族阵线的任何阴谋。
    指示指出，在美帝国主义者和他们的代理人所策划的侵略叙利亚阴谋未能得逞以后，“他们并没有放弃在有机会的时候就实现这种阴谋的打算。为了力图制造这种机会，帝国主义者和他们的代理人开始对叙利亚的内部阵线发动了攻势”。
    指示详尽地指出了民族阵线的重要性，并且揭露了帝国主义企图在民族阵线中制造分歧的阴谋。它说，“不管是以什么理由或借口，想在民族阵线的成员之间，特别是在共产党员和阿拉伯复兴社会党员之间制造分歧的任何企图，除掉帝国主义者和他们的代理人会感到高兴以外，任何人都是不会感到高兴的。”
    这里有消息表明，已经有人在进行活动，企图组织一个包括阿拉伯复兴社会党和国民党的新集团。这件事已经引起了这里舆论的注意。
