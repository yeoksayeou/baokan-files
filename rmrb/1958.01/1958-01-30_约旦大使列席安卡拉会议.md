Title: Jordanian Ambassador Attends Ankara Conference
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 29th. According to a Middle East News Agency special correspondent's report from Ankara on the 28th, Jordan is unofficially participating in the Baghdad Pact conference currently being held in Ankara. The Middle East News Agency reporter said that although Jordan is not a member of the treaty, the Jordanian Ambassador to Turkey sat behind the Iraqi and Iranian delegations at the meeting.

### 约旦大使列席安卡拉会议

1958-01-30
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦大使列席安卡拉会议
    据新华社开罗29日电　据中东通讯社特派记者28日从安卡拉报道，约旦正在非正式地参加目前在安卡拉举行的巴格达条约会议。中东通讯社的记者说，虽然约旦不是条约的成员国，但是约旦驻土耳其大使在会议上坐在伊拉克和伊朗代表团的后面。
