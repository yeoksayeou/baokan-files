Title: Chinese Military Delegation to India Departs

Author:

Date: 1958-01-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Chinese military goodwill delegation to India, at the invitation of the Indian government, left Beijing by plane for India on the morning of the 16th.

    The head of the delegation is Marshal Ye Jianying. The members are: General Zhang Aiping, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice Admiral Fang Qiang, Lieutenant General Cheng Jun of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Rao Zhengxi, Major General Zhong Renfang of the Armored Forces, Major General Xu Decao of the Engineering Corps, Major General Xu Guangyi, Senior Colonel Zhang Tong, Senior Colonel Xu Minhui, and Senior Colonel Gan Boxun of the Artillery.

    Marshal Peng Dehuai, General Li Da, General Gan Siqi, General Zhang Zongxun, General Peng Shaohui, General Chen Shiju, General Chen Xilian and other senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu saw the delegation off at the airport. Military attaches from various countries stationed in China also went to the airport to see them off.

    Before the departure of the military goodwill delegation to India, Indian Ambassador to China Nehru and his wife, and Indian Military Attache to China Brigadier General Malik and his wife, held receptions to bid farewell to the delegation. Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council Marshal Chen Yi, and others attended the receptions.

### 我国访印军事代表团启程

1958-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我国访印军事代表团启程
    据新华社16日讯　我国军事友好访印代表团应印度政府的邀请，16日上午从北京乘飞机去印度。
    代表团团长是叶剑英元帅。团员有：张爱萍上将、萧向荣中将、方强海军中将、成钧空军中将、饶正锡中将、锺人仿装甲兵少将、徐德操工兵少将、胥光义少将、张彤大校、徐敏辉上校、甘伯勋炮兵上校。
    到机场送行的有彭德怀元帅、李达上将、甘泗淇上将、张宗逊上将、彭绍辉上将、陈士榘上将、陈锡联上将等中国人民解放军高级将领，和外交部副部长章汉夫。各国驻华武官也到机场送行。
    军事友好访印代表团启程前，印度驻华大使尼赫鲁及夫人、印度驻华武官马立克准将及夫人，曾为欢送代表团分别举行招待会。国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅、国务院副总理陈毅元帅等出席了招待会。
