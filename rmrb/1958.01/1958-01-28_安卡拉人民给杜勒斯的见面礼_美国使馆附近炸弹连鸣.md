Title: Ankara People's Greeting Gift to Dulles: Bombs Explode Near the US Embassy
Author:
Date: 1958-01-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ankara, 27th dispatch: US Secretary of State Dulles arrived in Ankara by plane on the 26th to attend the Baghdad Pact Council meeting scheduled to open on the 27th.

Shortly after Dulles' arrival, around midnight that day, bomb explosions occurred in succession near the US Embassy in Ankara and a bookstore selling American books near the United States Information Service. A warehouse inside the US Embassy and that bookstore were damaged. Turkish President Bayar and Prime Minister Menderes immediately rushed to the scene to inspect and assured the US Ambassador that a thorough investigation would be conducted. Menderes said this was "a demonstration."

### 安卡拉人民给杜勒斯的见面礼  美国使馆附近炸弹连鸣

1958-01-28
第5版()
专栏：

    安卡拉人民给杜勒斯的见面礼
    美国使馆附近炸弹连鸣
    据新华社27日讯　安卡拉消息：美国国务卿杜勒斯26日乘飞机到达安卡拉，出席定于27日开幕的巴格达条约理事会。
    在杜勒斯到达后不久，当天午夜前后，驻安卡拉的美国大使馆以及美国新闻处附近的一家出售美国书籍的书店接连发生炸弹爆炸事件。美国大使馆内的一所仓库以及那家书店受到损毁。土耳其总统拜亚尔、总理曼德列斯立即赶到现场视察，并且向美国大使保证将对案件进行彻底侦查。曼德列斯说，这是“一个示威”。
