Title: World Balance of Power Shifts: US Senator Admits Soviet Union Takes the Lead
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 17th -- US Senator Kennedy admitted on the evening of the 12th that the Soviet Union had taken the lead in the world balance of power. He said that the United States was lagging behind the Soviet Union not only in long-range rockets, but also in jet aircraft, new fuels, and atomic propulsion for aircraft.

### 世界力量对比起变化  美议员承认苏联占上风

1958-01-18
第5版()
专栏：

    世界力量对比起变化
    美议员承认苏联占上风
    新华社17日讯　纽约消息：美国参议员肯尼迪12日晚上承认，在世界力量对比中苏联占了上风。他说，美国不仅是在远程火箭方面，而且在喷气飞机、新燃料和飞机的原子推动力方面也落后于苏联。
