    Brief News
    Albania and Yugoslavia Draft Fishing Regulations for Border Areas
    Tirana Dispatch: According to the agreement on water conservancy issues signed in December 1956, the governments of Albania and Yugoslavia recently jointly formed a committee. The committee held its first meeting in Belgrade. The meeting discussed issues related to the utilization of water resources in the border areas of the two countries and the exchange of hydro-meteorological data. At the same time, the committee drafted regulations for joint fishing in the rivers and lakes along the borders of the two countries.
    Romania Appoints Bunaciu as Foreign Minister
    Bucharest Dispatch: The Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania has appointed Avram Bunaciu, the Romanian Minister of Justice, as Foreign Minister. The post of Minister of Justice will be taken over by G. Diaconescu. Bunaciu is a member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party.
    Hungary: 28,000 Refugees Have Returned Home
    Budapest Dispatch: Biro Laszlo, Secretary-General of the Hungarian World Federation, announced at a press conference held here on the 16th that, as of the beginning of January 1958, approximately 28,000 Hungarian refugees had returned to their homeland.

    阿南拟定边境捕鱼章程
    地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚和南斯拉夫两国政府根据1956年12月签订的水利问题的协定，最近联合组成了一个委员会。委员会在贝尔格莱德召开了第一次会议。会议讨论了利用两国边境的水源和相互交换水文气象资料等有关问题，同时拟定了在两国边境的河流和湖泊中共同捕鱼的章程。
    罗任命布纳丘为外交部长
    布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团任命罗马尼亚司法部长阿夫拉姆·布纳丘为外交部长。司法部长将由格·迪亚康尼斯库担任。布纳丘是罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会委员。
  匈牙利已有二万八千难民回国
    布达佩斯讯：匈牙利人世界联盟秘书长比罗·拉斯洛16日在这里举行的记者招待会上宣布：到1958年1月初为止，大约有两万八千名匈牙利难民已经回到了祖国。
