Title: Hungarian Cooperative Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-01-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 30th: A three-member Hungarian cooperative delegation led by Rezső Nyers, Chairman of the National Federation of Cooperatives of Hungary, arrived in Beijing on January 30th for a friendly visit at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives. Zhang Qilong, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, and others welcomed them at the airport.
