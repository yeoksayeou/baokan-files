Title: The Train Comes from the Clouds
Author: Li Weidou
Date: 1958-01-03
Page: 8

Singing of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway

    The train comes from the clouds,
  　The train climbs the peaks again,
  　Adorned with red and green, connecting clouds and sky,
  　Like a rainbow hanging in the sky.
  　The whistle blows long, the wheels roar,
  　The sound is above the white clouds, under the rocky cliffs,
  　Coming from the heart of the Qinling and Bashan Mountains,
  　Treading on the waves of the Jialing River and the Wei River.

### 火车从云层中来
李维斗
1958-01-03
第8版()
专栏：歌唱宝成路

    火车从云层中来
  　李维斗
  　火车从云层中来，
  　火车又在高峰上爬，
  　披红挂绿连起云和天，
  　活像长虹在天边悬挂。
  　汽笛长鸣，车轮喧哗，
  　声在白云上，石崖下，
  　从秦岭巴山的心脏里来，
  　踏着嘉陵江渭河的浪花。
