Title: Another World First

Author: 
Date: 1958-01-27
Page: 6

　　Another World First
　　The Soviet Union has produced a semiconductor electronic computer, another world first. In comparison, the United States is once again lagging behind in a field of science and technology. In the competition between the socialist and capitalist systems, this trend is already a foregone conclusion. Even if the United States allocates another ten billion dollars to strengthen education and research, it will never buy the superiority of the capitalist system.

### 又是一个世界第一

1958-01-27
第6版()
专栏：札记

    又是一个世界第一
    苏联制成了半导体电子计算机，又是一个世界第一。相形之下，美国又在科学技术的一个方面落后了。在社会主义同资本主义两种制度的竞赛中，这种发展趋势，已成定局。美国就是为加强教育和研究工作，再拨十亿美元，也绝不会买到资本主义制度的优越性。
