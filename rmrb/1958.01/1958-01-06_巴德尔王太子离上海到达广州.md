Title: Crown Prince Badr Leaves Shanghai, Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1958-01-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 5th: Crown Prince Badr, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defense of the Kingdom of Yemen, and his entourage, left Shanghai by special plane for Guangzhou this morning. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and Director of the West Asia and Africa Department Ke Hua accompanied them.

Those who saw them off at the airport included: Shanghai Deputy Mayors Cao Diqiu, Song Richang, Rong Yiren, Zhao Zukang, Sheng Pihua, leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Rear Admiral Peng Deqing, Air Force Major General Jiang Tengjiao, and diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in Shanghai.

On the 4th, Crown Prince Badr and his entourage visited places such as the Children's Palace in Shanghai. More than 500 children held a grand gathering at the Children's Palace to welcome the distinguished guests from the Red Sea coast of Yemen.

Crown Prince Badr accepted gifts made by the children themselves, and he encouraged the children to study hard, rely on hard work, and create their own prosperous and happy country. In the evening, the distinguished guests also watched acrobatics and Yue opera.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 5th: Crown Prince Badr, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defense of the Kingdom of Yemen, and his entourage, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and others, arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon by special plane from Shanghai.

The distinguished guests were welcomed by Guangdong Vice Governor Wei Jinfei, Guangzhou Mayor Zhu Guang, Guangzhou Garrison Commander Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, people from all walks of life in Guangdong and Guangzhou, and consular personnel from various countries stationed in Guangzhou.

The special plane landed at Tianhe Airport at 1:51 PM. When Crown Prince Badr appeared at the cabin door, the welcoming crowd at the airport waved streamers and colorful flags, shouting welcome slogans. As Crown Prince Badr passed through the welcoming crowd, Muslims in Guangzhou successively greeted the distinguished guests in Arabic. Crown Prince Badr also frequently returned their greetings.

This afternoon, Crown Prince Badr and others, accompanied by Zhu Guang, visited an agricultural production cooperative in the suburbs of Guangzhou. The cooperative members used their own produce, such as sugarcane, to entertain the distinguished guests. Crown Prince Badr asked the cooperative director, Li Hongguang, about the cooperative's income and product distribution. Before leaving, Crown Prince Badr expressed his hope that the production of this cooperative would develop. The distinguished guests also visited Sun Yat-sen University this afternoon.

This evening, Crown Prince Badr and his entourage attended a banquet held by Guangdong Vice Governor Wei Jinfei.

### 巴德尔王太子离上海到达广州

1958-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    巴德尔王太子离上海到达广州
    新华社上海5日电　也门王国副首相兼外交、国防大臣巴德尔王太子和他的随行人员，今天上午乘专机离上海飞广州。外交部副部长曾涌泉、西亚非洲司司长柯华陪同前往。
    到机场送行的有：上海市副市长曹荻秋、宋日昌、荣毅仁、赵祖康、盛丕华，中国人民解放军驻上海部队首长王必成中将、彭德清海军少将、江腾蛟空军少将以及各国驻沪外交人员。
    4日，巴德尔王太子和随行人员在上海参观了少年宫等地。五百多个少年儿童在少年宫隆重集会，欢迎来自红海之滨的也门贵宾们。
    巴德尔王太子接受了孩子们亲手制作的礼品，他并鼓励孩子们努力学习，依靠辛勤劳动，创造自己繁荣和幸福的国家。晚间，贵宾们还观看了杂技和越剧。
    新华社广州5日电　也门王国副首相兼外交和国防大臣巴德尔王太子和他的随行人员由外交部副部长曾涌泉等陪同，今天下午自上海乘专机到达广州。
    贵宾们受到了广东省副省长魏今非、广州市市长朱光、广州驻军首长文年生中将、广东省和广州市各界人士，以及各国驻广州领事人员的欢迎。
    专机在下午一时五十一分降落在天河机场。当巴德尔王太子出现在机舱门口时，机场上的欢迎群众纷纷摇动花絮和彩旗，高呼欢迎的口号。巴德尔王太子穿过欢迎的人群时，广州的穆斯林相继以阿拉伯语向贵宾们道安好。巴德尔王太子也频频向他们答礼。
    今天下午，巴德尔王太子等由朱光陪同，访问了广州市郊的一个农业生产合作社。社员们用自己生产的甘蔗等产品来招待贵宾们。巴德尔王太子向社主任李洪广询问了合作社的收入和产品分配情况。在离开的时候，巴德尔王太子希望这个社的生产得到发展。贵宾们在下午还参观了中山大学。
    今天晚间，巴德尔王太子和他的随行人员出席了广东省副省长魏今非举行的宴会。
