Title: NPC Fifth Session Scheduled to Convene on January 25th; NPC Standing Committee Approves in Principle Measures for Land Requisition for National Construction
Author:
Date: 1958-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: The 90th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, held on the afternoon of the 6th, decided to convene the Fifth Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China in Beijing on January 25th. The proposed main agenda items are: to decide on the 1958 national economic plan, to examine and approve the 1958 national budget, and to approve the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme. The meeting also decided that the deputies to the National People's Congress should register before January 23rd.

After discussion, the meeting approved in principle the "Measures for Land Requisition for National Construction."

The meeting listened to the explanation by Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, and discussed and approved the "Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Appropriately Increasing the Proportion of Public Accumulation Funds of Advanced Agricultural Producers' Cooperatives," and approved in principle the "Supplementary Regulations of the State Council on Share Funds of Agricultural Producers' Cooperatives."

### 人大第五次会议定于1月25日召开  人大常委会原则批准国家建设征用土地办法

1958-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    人大第五次会议定于1月25日召开
    人大常委会原则批准国家建设征用土地办法
    新华社6日讯　六日下午举行的全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九十次会议，决定一月二十五日在北京召集中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第五次会议，拟定主要的议案为：决定一九五八年度国民经济计划，审查和批准一九五八年国家预算，批准汉语拼音方案。会议并决定，全国人民代表大会代表应该在一月二十三日以前报到。
    会议经过讨论，原则批准了“国家建设征用土地办法”。
    会议听了农业部部长廖鲁言的说明，讨论通过了“全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于适当提高高级农业生产合作社公积金比例的决定”，原则批准了“国务院关于农业生产合作社股份基金的补充规定”。
