Dulles is Cursed Everywhere

The American "Newsweek" summarized foreign opinions on Dulles, pointing out that Dulles is extremely unpopular all over the world and has become the "target of public criticism." According to "Newsweek":

    In Britain - "The British don't like him, don't trust him, and blame him for most of Britain's suffering."

    In France - "Dulles has become the most frequently blamed object by the French."

    In West Germany - Dulles is called "an incorrigible person... an obstinate and meddlesome person."

    In Asia - Asians see Dulles as "the successor to the modern colonial tyrants of Asia."

    In the Middle East - "Dulles is extremely unpopular in the Arab world."

### 杜勒斯到处挨骂

1958-01-23
第5版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯到处挨骂
    美国“新闻周刊”在综述国外舆论对杜勒斯的评价时指出：杜勒斯在世界各地都极不受欢迎，他已经成为“众矢之的”。据“新闻周刊”说：
    在英国——“英国人不喜欢他，不信任他，并且把英国的大部分苦难都归咎于他。”
    在法国——“杜勒斯已经成为法国人最常责骂的对象。”
    在西德——杜勒斯被称作“冥顽不化的人……顽固而爱管闲事的人。”
    在亚洲——亚洲人把杜勒斯看成是“亚洲近代殖民地暴君的继承人。”
    在中东——“杜勒斯在阿拉伯世界是极端不受欢迎的。”
