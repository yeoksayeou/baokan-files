Title: This Year's Public Bond Redemption Lottery to be Held in Wuhan on February 5th
Author:
Date: 1958-01-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: The Ministry of Finance has decided that this year's public bond redemption lottery will be held in Wuhan on February 5th. This conference will conduct the redemption lottery for the 1954 to 1957 National Economic Construction Bonds. In addition to sending letters to relevant central agencies requesting that they entrust relevant departments of Hubei Province to send representatives to attend the meeting to supervise and draw lots, the Ministry of Finance has also invited representatives from workers, farmers, and industrialists and business people from Hubei, Hunan, Henan, Jiangxi, Guangdong, Guangxi, Wuhan and other provinces and cities to attend the meeting for supervision and observation.

### 今年公债还本抽签大会  二月五日在武汉市举行

1958-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    今年公债还本抽签大会
    二月五日在武汉市举行
    新华社29日讯　财政部决定今年的公债还本抽签大会于2月5日在武汉市举行。这次大会将进行1954年到1957年国家经济建设公债的还本抽签。财政部除函请中央有关机关委托所属湖北省有关部门派代表到会监督和抽签外，还邀请湖北、湖南、河南、江西、广东、广西、武汉等省市的职工、农民和工商界的代表到会监督和参观。
