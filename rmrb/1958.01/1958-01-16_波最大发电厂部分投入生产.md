Title: Largest Power Plant in Poland Partially Operational
Author:
Date: 1958-01-16
Page: 6

Brothers Countries' Construction

    Poland's largest and most modern power plant, the Skawina Power Plant, partially began production on the 8th. Among the generators put into operation is a giant generator with a power generation capacity of 150,000 kilowatts. Construction of this power plant began in 1955 and is scheduled to be completed in 1960. The power plant completely adopts Soviet design documents and Soviet-supplied machinery and equipment. After its completion, its power generation capacity will be 500,000 kilowatts.

### 波最大发电厂部分投入生产

1958-01-16
第6版()
专栏：兄弟国家的建设

    波最大发电厂部分投入生产
    波兰最大的和最现代化的发电厂——斯卡维纳发电厂已经在8日部分地投入生产。在投入生产的发电机中，有发电能力达十五万瓩的巨型发电机。这个发电厂是从1955年开始兴建的，预定在1960年全部建成。发电厂完全采用苏联的设计文件和苏联供应的机器装备。它在全部建成后的发电能力将为五十万瓩。
