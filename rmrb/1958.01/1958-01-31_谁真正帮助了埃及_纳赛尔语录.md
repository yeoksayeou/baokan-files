Who Really Helped Egypt
Nasser's Quotes

When the West refused to help, the Russians really helped us. When the West wanted to internationalize the canal, Russia supported our point of view. When we were attacked, Russia warned the West. When we needed wheat and other countries refused, the Russians gave it to us. … When the Russians helped us, the United States froze 50 million US dollars of Egypt's money, and Britain froze 150 million pounds sterling of Egypt's money.
(Meeting with Greek journalists on January 16, 1957)

Britain, France, and the United States—especially France—refused to give us weapons, but supplied weapons to Israel. The Soviet Union provided us with weapons. The Soviet Union asked to participate in the investment of the Aswan Dam. The United States withdrew its proposal in an insulting manner. …

We lacked wheat—only fifteen days' worth of storage. We asked you (referring to the United States) for assistance, and you refused. Later you agreed, if it was paid for in US dollars, but you froze our US dollars, which means the United States was unwilling to help us in our time of crisis. We asked the Soviet Union for assistance. The Soviet Union had no reserves, but it sent us wheat. The situation was the same in terms of medicine and gasoline.
(Meeting with American journalists on November 19, 1957)

The United States refused to provide Egypt with the funds needed to build the Aswan High Dam, and after Britain, France, and Israel launched an aggression against Egypt, refused to supply Egypt with wheat. The Soviet Union, however, supplied Egypt with 400,000 tons of wheat without any conditions and provided loans without any political or economic conditions attached.
(Meeting with a US news delegation on January 26, 1958)

### 谁真正帮助了埃及  纳赛尔语录

1958-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

    谁真正帮助了埃及
    纳赛尔语录
    当西方拒绝帮助的时候，俄国人真正帮助了我们。当西方要把运河国际化的时候，俄国支持我们的观点。当我们受到进攻的时候，俄国对西方发出了警告。当我们要小麦，而其它国家拒绝的时候，俄国人给了我们。……当俄国人帮助我们的时候，美国冻结了埃及的五千万美元，而英国则冻结了埃及的一亿五千万英镑。
    （1957年1月16日接见希腊记者）
    英国、法国和美国——特别是法国拒绝给我们武器，而供给以色列武器。苏联向我们提供了武器。苏联要求参与投资阿斯旺水坝的事。美国以一种侮辱的方式撤消了它的建议。……
    我们缺少小麦——只有十五天的贮藏量了。我们要求你们（指美国）援助，你们拒绝了。后来你们又同意了，如果是用美金付款的话，但是你们冻结了我们的美金，这意味美国不愿意在我们危急的时候帮助我们。我们请求苏联援助，苏联没有贮存，但是，它送给我们小麦。在医药、汽油等方面的情况也是一样的。
    （1957年11月19日接见美国记者）
    美国拒绝向埃及提供建筑阿斯旺高水坝所需资金，并且在英、法、以色列三国对埃及发动侵略后拒绝供应埃及小麦，而苏联却不加任何条件供应埃及四十万吨小麦并且提供不附带任何政治或经济条件的贷款。
    （1958年1月26日接见美国新闻代表团）
