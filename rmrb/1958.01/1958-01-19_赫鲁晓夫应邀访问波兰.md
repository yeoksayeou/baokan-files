Title: Khrushchev Accepts Invitation to Visit Poland
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 17th, TASS: During his vacation near the Soviet-Polish border, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, accepted the invitations of the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Gomulka, and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, to visit the People's Republic of Poland.

During his three-day visit to Poland, Khrushchev held sincere and friendly talks with the Polish Party and government leaders.

### 赫鲁晓夫应邀访问波兰

1958-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫应邀访问波兰
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社莫斯科17日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫在靠近苏波边界某地休假期间，应波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡和部长会议主席西伦凯维兹的邀请，访问了波兰人民共和国。
    赫鲁晓夫在访问波兰的三天中，曾经同波兰党和政府领导人进行了诚恳友好的交谈。
