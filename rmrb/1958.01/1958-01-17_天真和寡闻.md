    Naiveté and Ignorance
    Another American Senator, Karl Mundt, is shouting against "recognizing China." In fact, the Chinese people have long said: The Chinese people do not care about America's "recognition"; without America's "recognition," they will only live better. So, why do some American senators keep repeating this cry?
    Karl Mundt answered this question. It turns out that "too many" Americans who are "either too naive or too ignorant" advocate recognizing China and believe it is "inevitable."
    That small group of "Cold War" enthusiasts in America, thinking that they can stop the trend of popular sentiment and the course of history by shouting, are truly "either too naive or too ignorant."

### 天真和寡闻

1958-01-17
第5版()
专栏：札记

    天真和寡闻
    又有一个美国参议员卡尔·蒙特在叫喊反对“承认中国”了。其实，中国人民早就说过：中国人民并不稀罕美国的“承认”；没有美国的“承认”，只会生活得更好些。那末，美国的一些议员们为什么还是不断地要重复这种叫喊呢？
    卡尔·蒙特回答了这个问题。原来，有“太多”的“不是太天真就是孤陋寡闻”的美国人，是主张承认中国的，并且认为这是“不可避免”的。
    美国那一小撮“冷战”的热心煽动者，以为他们出来叫嚷一通，就能阻挡住人心的趋向和历史的进程，却真未免有些“不是太天真就是孤陋寡闻”了。
