Title: German Communist Party Central Committee Plenary Session Passes Resolution, Fully Agrees with Moscow Declaration
Author:
Date: 1958-01-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 5th (TASS dispatch): The plenary session of the Central Committee of the German Communist Party unanimously passed a resolution stating its full agreement with the declaration of the meeting of representatives of communist and workers' parties of socialist countries issued during the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution in Moscow, as well as the Peace Manifesto.

The communique of the plenary session of the Central Committee of the German Communist Party has been relayed by Deutsche Presse-Agentur according to the broadcast of "Free Radio Germany 904." The communique pointed out: The Central Committee of the German Communist Party welcomes the achievements of the Moscow meeting, considering this meeting as a clear demonstration of the further strengthening of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and a great achievement of the Marxist-Leninist unity of the communist and workers' parties of all countries under the leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

In its resolution, the Central Committee of the German Communist Party emphatically pointed out the necessity for all leading organs and grassroots organizations of the party to study these two declarations and transform them into guiding principles for the party's actions. It also pointed out the necessity of explaining the content and significance of these two historic documents to the residents and organizing the struggle for peace.

The Central Committee called on the residents of West Germany and all peace-loving forces in the country to fight against the storage of atomic weapons and rocket weapons in West Germany, and to strive for the establishment of a nuclear-weapon-free zone in Europe.

The Central Committee of the German Communist Party also unanimously approved the establishment of the Party Central Supervisory Committee.

### 德共中央全会通过决议  完全同意莫斯科宣言

1958-01-06
第6版()
专栏：

    德共中央全会通过决议
    完全同意莫斯科宣言
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社柏林4日讯：德国共产党中央委员会全体会议一致通过决议，表示完全同意在莫斯科庆祝十月革命四十周年期间发表的社会主义国家共产党和工人党代表会议的宣言以及和平宣言。
    德国共产党中央委员会全体会议的公报已经由德意志通讯社据“德国九○四自由广播电台”的广播予以转播。公报指出：德国共产党中央委员会欢迎莫斯科会议的成果，认为这个会议是以苏联为首的社会主义阵营进一步加强的明证，是各国共产党和工人党在苏联共产党领导下的马克思列宁主义团结的伟大成就。
    德国共产党中央委员会在决议中着重指出了党的一切领导机构和基层组织研究这两个宣言并且把它们变为党的行动准则的必要性，还指出向居民解释这两个历史性文件的内容和意义以及组织争取和平的斗争的必要性。
    中央委员会号召西德居民和全国一切和平力量进行斗争，反对在西德贮存原子武器和火箭武器，争取在欧洲建立无原子武器地区。
    德国共产党中央委员会还一致通过成立党中央监察委员会。
