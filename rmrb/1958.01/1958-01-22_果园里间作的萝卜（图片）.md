Radishes Interplanted in Orchard
　　In recent years, the Shexian County Horticultural Farm in Anhui Province has been practicing vegetable intercropping in orchards. The intercropped "Zheda Long" radishes grow very well. In 1956, the largest radish weighed 17.5 jin, with an average weight of more than 8 jin each, and a yield of 10,000 jin per mu, which was four to five times higher than the local average yield. Last year, the radish harvest here was even better than the year before, with the largest weighing 32 jin. According to the farm's estimates, if the average weight of each radish is 20 jin and 1,800 plants are planted per mu, the yield per unit area can reach 36,000 jin. These are two radishes, each weighing 24 jin, sent by the farm to Beijing to report the good news to the Central Committee.

### 果园里间作的萝卜（图片）
刘长忠
1958-01-22
第2版()
专栏：

    果园里间作的萝卜
    安徽省歙县园艺场近年来在果园里实行蔬菜间作。间作的“浙大长”萝卜生长很好，1956年最大的萝卜重十七斤半，每个平均重量八斤多，每亩产量合到一万斤，比当地一般产量高四、五倍。去年这里的萝卜收成比前年还好，最大的重三十二斤。据该场推算：如果平均每个重二十斤，每亩种一千八百棵，单位产量就可以达到三万六千斤。这是该场送到北京向中央报喜的两个各重二十四斤的萝卜。
                                    本报记者　刘长忠摄
