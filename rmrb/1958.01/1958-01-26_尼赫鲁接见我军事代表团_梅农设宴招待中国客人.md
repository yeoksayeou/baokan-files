Title: Nehru Receives Chinese Military Delegation, Menon Hosts Banquet for Chinese Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-01-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 25th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru received Marshal Ye Jianying, head of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, and all members of the delegation this afternoon. Ambassador Pan Zili was present at the meeting.

Marshal Ye Jianying presented Prime Minister Nehru with an embroidery and an album on behalf of Premier Zhou Enlai. Marshal Ye Jianying also gave Prime Minister Nehru a photo of the two of them taken together in Chongqing in 1939. At the time, when Prime Minister Nehru visited Chongqing that year, Marshal Ye Jianying was the representative of the Eighth Route Army's office in Chongqing.

Prime Minister Nehru and Marshal Ye Jianying had a cordial conversation.

Indian Defense Minister Menon hosted a banquet at the Presidential Palace on the evening of the 24th to entertain the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation. In his speech, Menon welcomed the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation on behalf of the Indian government and armed forces. He said that the Indian government "has always recognized the People's Republic of China. There is no second China in the world." He also said that without China's participation, world peace, international tensions, and Asian affairs would not be resolved.

In his speech at the banquet, Marshal Ye Jianying, on behalf of the Chinese people, expressed his gratitude to Prime Minister Nehru for his efforts and contributions to world peace, and for his call for a ban on nuclear weapons testing and his support for holding top-level meetings. Marshal Ye Jianying also expressed his admiration for the role Menon played in international affairs and his efforts to restore China's rightful place in the United Nations.

### 尼赫鲁接见我军事代表团  梅农设宴招待中国客人

1958-01-26
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁接见我军事代表团
    梅农设宴招待中国客人
    据新华社新德里25日电　印度总理尼赫鲁今天下午接见了中国军事友好代表团团长叶剑英元帅和全体团员。陪同受接见的有潘自力大使。
    叶剑英元帅代表周恩来总理向尼赫鲁总理赠送了一幅刺绣和一部画册。叶剑英元帅还送给尼赫鲁总理一张他们两人1939年在重庆合摄的照片。尼赫鲁总理在那一年访问重庆的时候，叶剑英元帅是八路军驻重庆办事处的代表。
    尼赫鲁总理和叶剑英元帅进行了亲切的谈话。
    印度国防部长梅农曾在24日晚间在总统府举行宴会招待中国军事友好代表团。梅农在致词时，代表印度政府和武装部队欢迎中国军事友好代表团。他说，印度政府“一直承认中华人民共和国。世界上再没有第二个中国”。他还说，没有中国参加，世界和平、国际紧张局势以及亚洲事务这些问题都不会得到解决。
    叶剑英元帅在宴会上讲话时，代表中国人民对于尼赫鲁总理对世界和平所作的努力和贡献、对于他呼吁禁止核武器试验和支持举行最高级会议的建议，表示感谢。叶剑英元帅还对梅农在国际事务中所起的作用和他为恢复中国在联合国的应有地位而作的努力表示钦佩。
