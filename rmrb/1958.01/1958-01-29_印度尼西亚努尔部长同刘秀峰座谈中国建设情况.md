Title: Indonesian Minister Nuh Talks with Liu Xiufeng About China's Construction
Author:
Date: 1958-01-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 28th. Banjaransari Muhammad Nuh, the Minister of Public Works and Power of Indonesia, who is currently visiting Beijing, held talks with Liu Xiufeng, the Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Construction Engineering, this afternoon about China's construction situation.

Nuh and his entourage, Uma Mastuki, are visiting China at the invitation of Liu Xiufeng after visiting the Soviet Union. After arriving in Beijing on the 25th, Nuh and Mastuki successively visited the Beijing Civil Aviation Port Project, the Diaoyutai Water Conservancy Project, the Construction Exhibition Hall, and the Hydroelectric Power Generation Exhibition Hall, and toured Beijing's scenic spots and historical sites.

### 印度尼西亚努尔部长同刘秀峰座谈中国建设情况

1958-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚努尔部长同刘秀峰座谈中国建设情况
    新华社28日讯　正在北京访问的印度尼西亚公共工程及动力部部长班额尔兰·穆罕默德·努尔，今天下午同中国建筑工程部部长刘秀峰座谈了中国的建设情况。
    努尔和他的随员乌玛·玛斯杜基是在到苏联访问后，应刘秀峰的邀请来中国访问的。努尔和玛斯杜基在25日到达北京后，曾先后参观了北京民航港工程、钓鱼台水利工程、建筑展览馆和水利发电展览馆，游览了北京的名胜古迹。
