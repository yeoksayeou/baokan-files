    Brief News

    Currency Devaluation in Several Countries
    New York Dispatch: According to materials published in the West German "Pick's World Currency Yearbook" not long ago, in 1957, the currencies of Argentina, Brazil, Colombia, Egypt, France, Iceland, Indonesia, South Korea, Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, Spain, Syria, Turkey, Uruguay, and the United States all fell to historic lows.
    The status of the US dollar has been weakened again. In 1957, the dollar's value fell by another 3%, falling to a new record low after 1956.

    Many Child Laborers in British Agriculture
    London Dispatch: British Labour Party MP Hill pointed out in the British "Transport and General Workers' Monthly" that British farmers widely employ child labor in order to pursue cheap labor. Many of the children employed are no more than thirteen years old, and they are doing work that they are physically unable to handle.

    French National Population Statistics
    Paris Dispatch: According to statistics up to New Year's Day this year, the total population of France is 44,289,000, an increase of 4 million compared to the end of World War II.

    Rapid Increase in Unemployment in West Germany
    Bonn Dispatch: The West German Federal Unemployment Agency announced that the number of unemployed in West Germany exceeded 1,210,000 last month. This is the highest December unemployment figure in four years.

    Nai Sa-rit to go to the United States for Medical Treatment
    Bangkok Dispatch: Thai Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Interior Nai Praphat stated on January 7 that the Supreme Commander of the Thai Armed Forces, Nai Sarit, will leave Bangkok on January 24 to go to Walter Reed Hospital in Washington for medical treatment.

    New Israeli Cabinet Formed
    Jerusalem Dispatch: The Israeli Parliament passed a vote of confidence on the new cabinet formed by Ben-Gurion on the 7th by a vote of 76 to 33. The new sixteen-member cabinet is still composed of the five parties that made up the previous cabinet. These five parties are: the Israel Labor Party, the Spiritual Center Party, the Workers' Union Party, the United Labor Party, and the Progressive Party.

### 简讯

1958-01-09
第5版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    一些国家的货币贬值
    纽约讯：根据不久前发表的西德皮克世界货币年鉴的材料，在1957年中，阿根廷、巴西、哥伦比亚、埃及、法国、冰岛、印度尼西亚、南朝鲜、巴基斯坦、沙特阿拉伯、西班牙、叙利亚、土耳其、乌拉圭和美国等国的货币价值都下降到历史上的最低水平。
    美元的地位再一次遭到削弱，1957年美元价值又下降了3％，继1956年之后，再次下降到新的创纪录的最低水平。
    英国农业童工多
    伦敦讯：英国工党议员希耳在英国“运输和普通工人月刊”上发表文章指出，英国农场主为了追求廉价的劳动力，广泛地雇用了童工。很多被雇用的儿童年纪都不超过十三岁，他们干着体力完全不能胜任的工作。
    法国全国人口的统计
    巴黎讯：根据截至今年元旦为止的统计，法国全国人口共有四千四百二十八万九千人，比第二次大战结束时增加了四百万人。
    西德失业人数迅速增加
    波恩讯：西德联邦失业问题局宣布：西德上月失业数字超过一百二十一万。这是四年来最高的12月份失业数字。
    乃沙立将到美国就医
    曼谷讯：泰国副总理兼内政部长乃巴博1月7日说，泰国武装部队最高司令乃沙立将在1月24日离开曼谷，前往华盛顿的华尔特·里德医院就医。
    以色列新内阁组成
    耶路撒冷讯：以色列议会7日以七十六票对三十三票通过了对本—古里安组成的新内阁的信任案。新的十六人内阁仍然由组成前内阁的五个政党组成。这五个政党是：以色列工党、精神中心党、工人联合党、统一劳工党和进步党。
