Title: Tibetan Delegation Returns to Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1958-01-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, January 5th: The Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region and the Tibet Work Committee of the Chinese Communist Party jointly held a banquet on the evening of the 4th to welcome the 1957 Tibetan delegation that had recently returned to Lhasa.

    Khenchung Thupten Sangpo, the head of the Tibetan delegation, Jikang Kempo Lobsang Soepa, the deputy head of the delegation, and members of the delegation attended the banquet.

    Also present at the banquet were Tan Guansan, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Work Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Zhou Renshan, and Laming Yexi Chuchen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee of the Autonomous Region.

    Tan Guansan and Laming Yexi Chuchen delivered speeches at the banquet, congratulating the Tibetan delegation on the successful completion of its visit. Afterwards, Thupten Sangpo spoke. He said that in recent years, the construction of the motherland has been advancing by leaps and bounds, and we must tell the Tibetan people everything we have seen. He also said that through the visit, we have deeply realized the superiority of the socialist system, and we must take the road of socialism.
    The Tibetan delegation left Lhasa for the mainland last July, and they visited the Northeast, North China, East China, Central China, and Northwest China.

### 西藏参观团返回拉萨

1958-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    西藏参观团返回拉萨
    新华社拉萨5日电　西藏自治区筹委会和中共西藏工委4日晚联合举行宴会，为日前返回拉萨的1957年度西藏参观团洗尘。
    西藏参观团团长堪穷土登桑布、副团长季康堪布洛桑索巴和参观团团员都出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有中共西藏工委副书记谭冠三、周仁山，自治区筹委会副秘书长拉敏·益喜楚臣。
    谭冠三和拉敏·益喜楚臣先后在宴会上致词，祝贺西藏参观团胜利地完成了参观任务。接着，土登桑布讲话。他说，几年来，祖国建设事业突飞猛进，我们一定要把自己看到的一切告诉西藏人民。他还说，通过参观，使我们深深地认识到社会主义制度的优越性，我们一定要走社会主义的道路。
  西藏参观团是去年7月离开拉萨去内地的，他们在东北、华北、华东、华中、西北等地进行了参观。
