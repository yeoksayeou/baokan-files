Title: Siroky to Visit Burma at Invitation

Author: 
Date: 1958-01-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon. The Burmese Foreign Ministry announced on the evening of the 13th that Czechoslovak Premier Siroky would visit Burma from January 20th to 24th at the invitation of the Government of the Union of Burma. Premier Siroky is currently visiting Calcutta, India.

### 西罗基应邀将访缅

1958-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    西罗基应邀将访缅
    据新华社仰光讯　缅甸外交部13日晚上宣布，捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基应缅甸联邦政府的邀请，将在1月20日至24日到缅甸进行访问。现在，西罗基总理正在印度的加尔各答访问。
