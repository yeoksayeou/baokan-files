Title: Beware of Frost

Author:

Date: 1958-01-17
Page: 2

    Beware of Frost
    According to the Meteorological Observatory's forecast, the national temperature will continue to drop. The temperature will be lowest on the mornings of the 17th and 18th, and the frost line will extend to the coast of the South China Sea. The minimum temperature in Guangzhou, Fuzhou and other places will be around 0℃, and frost may occur in all areas except Hainan Island.

### 注意霜冻

1958-01-17
第2版()
专栏：

    注意霜冻
    据气象台预报，全国气温仍将继续下降，17日和18日晨温度最低，霜冻线伸展到南海沿岸，广州、福州等地最低气温将在0℃左右，除海南岛外，均可能发生霜冻。
