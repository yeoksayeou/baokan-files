Title: Increase and Decrease
Author:
Date: 1958-01-23
Page: 5

    Notes
    A piece of news regarding the British economic situation published in today's newspaper reports that the number of two types of people in Britain has increased. One is the unemployed, and the other is the super-rich. The increase in the number of these two types of people both indicates that the number of people among the British working people who can maintain a basic standard of living is decreasing.

### 增多和减少

1958-01-23
第5版()
专栏：札记

    增多和减少
    今天报上登载的一则关于英国经济情况的消息，报道了英国两种人的数目有所增加。一种是失业者，一种是大富翁。这两种人的数目的增加，都表明在英国劳动人民中间能够维持起码生活的人数是越来越少了。
