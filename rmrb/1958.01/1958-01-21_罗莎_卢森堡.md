　　Rosa Luxemburg
　　Rosa Luxemburg (1871-1919) was a famous activist in the German and Polish workers' movements. She was also one of the representatives of the left wing within the German Social Democratic Party and the Second International, and one of the founders of the German Communist Party. Luxemburg resolutely fought against militarism. After the outbreak of World War I, she strived to unite the German left-wing Social Democrats to oppose the imperialist war. Luxemburg was imprisoned more than once for actively engaging in revolutionary activities, and on January 15, 1919, she was tragically murdered by counter-revolutionaries along with Karl Liebknecht.

### 罗莎·卢森堡

1958-01-21
第6版()
专栏：人物介绍

    罗莎·卢森堡
    罗莎·卢森堡（1871—1919）是德国和波兰工人运动的著名活动家。她也是德国社会民主党和第二国际内左派的代表之一，是德国共产党的创始人之一。卢森堡曾坚决地进行过反对军国主义的斗争。在第一次世界大战爆发后，她曾努力使德国左派社会民主党人团结一致来反对帝国主义战争。卢森堡由于积极进行革命活动，曾不止一次被监禁，而在1919年1月15日，同李卜克内西一起惨遭反革命杀害。
