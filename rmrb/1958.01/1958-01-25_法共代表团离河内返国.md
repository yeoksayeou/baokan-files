Title: French Communist Party Delegation Leaves Hanoi to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1958-01-25
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 24th: The French Communist Party delegation led by维美徐 (Wéiměi Xú), member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, left Hanoi by plane today, concluding its seventeen-day visit to Vietnam.

　　维美徐 (Wéiměi Xú) said at the airport that the French Communist Party will make further efforts to promote the correct implementation of the Geneva Accords and the normalization of relations between France and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 法共代表团离河内返国

1958-01-25
第6版()
专栏：

    法共代表团离河内返国
    据新华社河内24日电　法国共产党中央政治局委员维美徐率领的法国共产党代表团今天乘飞机离开河内，结束了在越南进行的为时十七天的访问。
    维美徐在机场上发表讲话说，法国共产党将为促进日内瓦协议的正确执行以及法国和越南民主共和国两国关系的正常化而作进一步的努力。
