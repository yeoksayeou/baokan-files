Title: Romanian Party, Government, and Parliament Telegraph Thanks for China's Condolences on Groza's Death
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-19
Page: 6

    Romanian Party, Government, and Parliament
    Telegraph Thanks for China's Condolences on Groza's Death
    Xinhua News Agency, January 18th. The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, and the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic sent a telegram on January 16th to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and the State Council of the People's Republic of China, thanking them for their condolences on the death of Groza.
    The telegram stated: "When the loyal son of the Romanian people, our beloved Comrade Dr. Petru Groza passed away, we received your condolences. The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, and the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic extend our gratitude to you."

### 罗马尼亚党政和议会  电谢我国吊唁格罗查逝世

1958-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚党政和议会
    电谢我国吊唁格罗查逝世
    新华社18日讯　罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会、罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团、罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议16日打电报给中国共产党中央委员会、中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会、中华人民共和国国务院，感谢对格罗查逝世的吊唁。
    电文说：“当罗马尼亚人民忠实的儿子、我们敬爱的同志彼得鲁·格罗查博士逝世的时候，承蒙吊唁，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会、罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团、罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议谨向你们致以谢忱。”
