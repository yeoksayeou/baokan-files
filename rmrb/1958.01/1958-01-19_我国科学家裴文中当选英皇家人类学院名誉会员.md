Title: Chinese Scientist Pei Wenzhong Elected Honorary Fellow of the Royal Anthropological Institute of Great Britain
Author:
Date: 1958-01-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London: The well-known Chinese scientist Pei Wenzhong has been elected as an honorary fellow of the Royal Anthropological Institute of Great Britain. The Royal Anthropological Institute believes that Pei Wenzhong has made outstanding contributions to anthropology.

### 我国科学家裴文中当选英皇家人类学院名誉会员

1958-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    我国科学家裴文中当选英皇家人类学院名誉会员
    据新华社讯　伦敦消息：中国著名科学家裴文中已经被英国皇家人类学院选为这个学院的名誉会员。英国皇家人类学院认为，裴文中在人类学上作出了卓越的贡献。
