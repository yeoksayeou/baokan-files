Title: Tunisia Protests French Military Invasion of National Border
Author:
Date: 1958-01-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, January 4th – According to TASS, Paris, January 3rd: Tunisian Foreign Minister Mokaddem lodged a protest with the French Ambassador to Tunisia regarding the French military's invasion of Tunisian territory.

    The Tunisian Ambassador to Paris issued a statement on January 3rd stating, "On January 2nd, 1958, at 15:30, fifteen trucks carrying French soldiers crossed from Algeria into Tunisian territory for three kilometers, reaching the Foum-El-Khanga area, eight kilometers from Tamaessa... Under the pretext of searching for livestock stolen from Algeria, French military personnel surrounded this settlement and interrogated and terrorized peaceful Tunisian residents. Afterwards, it was discovered that two residents were dead, four were injured, and thirteen were missing."

### 突尼斯抗议法军侵犯国境

1958-01-05
第5版()
专栏：

    突尼斯抗议法军侵犯国境
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社巴黎3日讯：突尼斯外交部长莫卡德姆就法军侵入突尼斯领土一事向法国驻突尼斯大使提出抗议。
    突尼斯驻巴黎大使3日发表声明说，“1958年1月2日十五时三十分，十五辆载着法国士兵的载重汽车，从阿尔及利亚侵入突尼斯境内三公里，到达离塔麦萨八公里的富姆—艾尔—康加地方……法国军事人员借口寻找从阿尔及利亚被偷走的牲口，包围了这个居民点，并对和平的突尼斯居民进行盘问和采取恐怖行动。事后，发现这个居民点死亡两人，伤四人，失踪十三人。”
