Title: China's Military Goodwill Delegation to India Leaves Kunming for India
Author:
Date: 1958-01-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, January 21st—At the invitation of the Indian government, the Chinese military goodwill delegation to India, headed by Marshal Ye Jianying, left Kunming by special plane at 9:00 a.m. today for India.
    Among those who saw the delegation off at the airport were Lieutenant General Qin Jiwei, Commander of the Kunming Garrison, Ma Jikong, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Wu Zuomin, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province.

### 我国军事友好访印代表团离昆明赴印度

1958-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    我国军事友好访印代表团离昆明赴印度
    新华社昆明21日电　应印度政府的邀请，以叶剑英元帅为首的我国军事友好访印代表团今天上午九时乘专机离昆明前往印度。
    到机场送行的有昆明驻军首长秦基伟中将、中共云南省委书记马继孔、云南省副省长吴作民等。
