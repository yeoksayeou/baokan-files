Title: Soviet Leaders Send New Year Greetings to Heads of State, Wishing for Strengthened Peace and People's Well-being This Year
Author:
Date: 1958-01-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 31st. TASS reported: Voroshilov, Khrushchev, and Bulganin sent New Year's greetings on December 31st to heads of state and heads of government of various countries. The letters expressed the hope that the coming year will be a year of strengthening international friendship and peace based on the principles of peaceful coexistence, a year that will enable people everywhere to live and work in peace and contentment, without fear for their future, for their children, and for the fate of their loved ones.

Voroshilov, Khrushchev, and Bulganin wished the people of all countries happiness in their letters and wished them success in achieving this noble goal.

### 苏联领导人致函各国元首祝贺新年  愿今年和平加强愿人民安居乐业

1958-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联领导人致函各国元首祝贺新年
    愿今年和平加强愿人民安居乐业
    新华社1日讯　塔斯社莫斯科12月31日电：伏罗希洛夫、赫鲁晓夫和布尔加宁12月31日向各国元首和各国政府首脑致新年贺信，信中表示希望即将来到的一年将是根据和平共处原则加强国际友谊与和平的一年，这一年将会使人们到处安居乐业，对自己的未来、对自己的儿女和自己的亲人的命运不感到恐惧。
    伏罗希洛夫、赫鲁晓夫和布尔加宁在他们的信中祝各国人民幸福，并且祝他们在达成这个崇高的目的中获得成就。
