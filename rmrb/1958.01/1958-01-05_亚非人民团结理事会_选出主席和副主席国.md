Title: Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council Elects Chairman and Vice-Chairman Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-01-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 4th. The representatives of various countries attending the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Conference held a meeting in Cairo on the morning of January 3 to discuss matters related to the establishment of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council. The meeting has decided that Egypt will serve as the Chairman of the Council, and Algeria, India, the Soviet Union, and Sudan will serve as Vice-Chairmen.

### 亚非人民团结理事会  选出主席和副主席国

1958-01-05
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非人民团结理事会
    选出主席和副主席国
    新华社开罗4日电　出席亚非人民团结大会的各国代表3日上午在开罗举行会议，讨论成立亚非人民团结理事会的有关事宜。会议已经决定：由埃及担任理事会主席，阿尔及利亚、印度、苏联和苏丹担任副主席。
