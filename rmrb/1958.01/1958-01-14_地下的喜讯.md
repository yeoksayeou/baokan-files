    A Note:

    Good News from Underground
    Good news has come from the Soviet Union: Siberia's mineral deposits and hydroelectric resources far exceed previous estimates. Vast and expansive Siberia possesses inexhaustible natural resources that will make this region a powerful industrial base.
    Not long ago, the "New York Times" dejectedly lamented: "American natural resources have been dangerously reduced," and America "has become a nation lacking natural resources."
    It is clear that, whether from the heavens to the earth, or from the hearts of the people to material objects, "the East wind prevails over the West wind" is already an unchangeable certainty.

### 地下的喜讯

1958-01-14
第5版()
专栏：扎记

    地下的喜讯
    喜讯从苏联传来：西伯利亚的矿藏和水力资源大大地超过了原来的估计。辽阔广大的西伯利亚拥有取之不竭的天然资源，将使这个地区成为强大的工业基地。
    不久以前，“纽约时报”垂头丧气地悲叹：“美国天然资源危险地减少了”，美国“变成了一个缺乏天然资源的国家。”
    可见，不论从天上到地下，从人心到物体，“东风压倒西风”都已是无可改变的定局。
