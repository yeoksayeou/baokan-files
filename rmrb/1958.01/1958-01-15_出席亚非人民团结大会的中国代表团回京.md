Title: Chinese Delegation to the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-01-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 12th. The Chinese delegation to the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference, led by Deputy Head of Delegation Chu Tunan, returned to Beijing by plane from Moscow on the afternoon of the 12th. Secretary-General of the delegation, Liu Ningyi, and some members of the delegation had returned to China a few days ago.

Head of Delegation Guo Moruo and Deputy Head of Delegation Burhan have gone to Bucharest to attend the funeral of Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania, after the meeting concluded.

### 出席亚非人民团结大会的中国代表团回京

1958-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    出席亚非人民团结大会的中国代表团回京
    新华社12日讯　出席亚非人民团结大会中国代表团在副团长楚图南率领下，12日下午由莫斯科乘飞机回到北京。代表团秘书长刘宁一和部分团员已于日前回国。
    郭沫若团长和包尔汉副团长在会议结束后已去布加勒斯特参加罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团主席格罗查的葬礼。
