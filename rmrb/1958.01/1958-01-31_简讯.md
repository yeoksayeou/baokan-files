    Brief News
    The Disasters Caused by British Colonial Rule in Nigeria
    London dispatch: Caine Ford, who once served in the British colonial authorities in Nigeria, revealed in the "Worker Daily" on the 29th the fatal consequences of British colonial rule in Nigeria. He pointed out that the wages of most Nigerian workers are only one-twelfth of the average wage of British workers.
    He said: "Almost all local residents (especially children) suffer from malaria. Despite this, most residents cannot afford to buy the necessary medicines.
    The average life expectancy of British people is sixty years, but Nigerians only live an average of thirty years."
    Lodge to Conduct Activities in the Middle East
    New York dispatch: The United States Representative to the United Nations, Lodge, departed on the 28th to conduct activities in Iran, Pakistan, India, and Afghanistan.
    He said that he wanted to "understand the attitudes of the governments of these countries" and try to enable the United States and these countries to reach a "high degree" of understanding.

### 简讯

专栏：简讯

    简讯
    英殖民统治给
    尼日利亚的灾难
    伦敦讯：曾经在非洲尼日利亚的英国殖民当局供职的凯恩·福特，29日在“工人日报”上揭露英国殖民统治在尼日利亚造成的致命后果。他指出，大多数尼日利亚工人的工资只等于英国工人的平均工资的十二分之一。
    他说：“几乎所有当地居民（特别是儿童）都患疟疾。虽然这样，大多数居民却无力购买必需的药品。
    英国人的平均寿命为六十岁，可是尼日利亚人平均只活三十岁。”
    洛奇去中东进行活动
    纽约讯：美国驻联合国代表洛奇28日启程到伊朗、巴基斯坦、印度和阿富汗去进行活动。
    他说，他想去“了解一下这些国家的政府的态度”，设法使美国和这些国家之间能达到“高度集中”的谅解。
