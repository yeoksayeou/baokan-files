Title: Australian Communist Party Delegation Goes to Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1958-01-13
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Lawrence Sharkey, General Secretary of the Australian Communist Party, and Richard Dixon, Chairman, left Beijing by plane for Guangzhou on the morning of the 11th. They will stay in Guangzhou for one or two days before returning home via Hong Kong.
    Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhao Yimin, Xiong Fu, and Li Qixin, responsible staff members of the CPC Central Committee organs, went to the airport to see them off.
    Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 10th to bid farewell to Sharkey and Dixon. Yang Shangkun, Liu Ningyi, Zhao Yimin, Xiong Fu, and Li Qixin were present.

### 澳大利亚共产党代表团去广州

1958-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚共产党代表团去广州
    新华社11日讯　澳大利亚共产党总书记劳伦斯·夏基、主席李却·狄克逊11日早晨乘飞机离北京前往广州。他们将在广州停留一、二天后取道香港返国。
    前往机场送行的，有中共中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆、中共中央机关负责工作人员赵毅敏、熊复、李启新。
    中共中央副主席朱德十日晚设宴为夏基和狄克逊饯行，出席作陪的有杨尚昆、刘宁一、赵毅敏、熊复、李启新。
