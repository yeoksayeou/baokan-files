Title: Sino-Indian Friendship Association Holds Soiree to Celebrate Indian National Day
Author:
Date: 1958-01-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: The Sino-Indian Friendship Association held a film soiree in Beijing on the evening of January 25th to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the founding of the Republic of India. The documentary "Indian Vice President Dr. Radhakrishnan Visits China" and the color feature film "Beacon Fire at the Border Village" were screened at the soiree.

    Bahadur Singh, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy in China, and embassy staff members were invited to attend the film soiree, which was attended by more than 500 people.

    Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture, and the directors of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association also attended the soiree.

    The film soiree was presided over by Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association. At the soiree, the congratulatory telegram from Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, to Pandit Sundarlal, Chairman of the Indo-Chinese Friendship Association, on the occasion of the eighth anniversary of the founding of the Republic of India, was read.

### 中印友协举行晚会庆祝印度国庆

1958-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中印友协举行晚会庆祝印度国庆
    新华社25日讯　为庆祝印度共和国成立八周年，中印友好协会25日晚在北京举行电影晚会。会上放映了“印度副总统拉达克里希南博士访问中国”的纪录片和彩色故事片“边寨烽火”。
    印度驻中国大使馆临时代办巴哈杜尔·辛格和使馆人员应邀出席了这个有五百多人参加的电影晚会。
    文化部副部长郑振铎和中印友好协会的理事们也出席了这个晚会。
    电影晚会由中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙主持，会上宣读了中印友好协会会长丁西林为庆祝印度共和国成立八周年给印中友好协会主席潘迪特·森德拉尔的贺电。
