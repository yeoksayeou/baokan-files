Column: Poems from the Countryside

    Short Sentences
  　Yan Shuibo
  　Corn cobs grin, showing a mouthful of good white teeth, eight hundred li of the Qinchuan plain, a vast expanse of good crops… Qinchuan in April – green on one side, yellow on the other; cooking smoke and flower petals, laughing boisterously, scatter across the fields…
