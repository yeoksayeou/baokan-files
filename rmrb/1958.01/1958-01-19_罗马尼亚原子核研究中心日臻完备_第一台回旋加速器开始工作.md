Title: Romania's Nuclear Research Center Nears Completion; First Cyclotron Begins Operation
Author:
Date: 1958-01-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 17th - Romania's first cyclotron, completed in early November last year, officially began operation on the 16th. This cyclotron, together with Romania's first atomic reactor, which went into production in August last year, and Romania's existing laboratories, marks the first stage of the development of the material basis for Romanian nuclear research.

This cyclotron is the second major project of the Nuclear Research Center of the Institute of Nuclear Physics of the Romanian Academy of Sciences (the first major project being the atomic reactor completed in July last year). It can accelerate particles to 12.5 million electron volts and deuterons to 25 million electron volts. According to calculations in the reaction chamber of the cyclotron, the highest current of ions obtained with this energy so far is 300 microamperes. This cyclotron can produce accelerated charged particles needed for scientific research, and can also produce radioactive isotopes that cannot be obtained in atomic reactors. With the atomic reactor and this cyclotron, the Romanian Nuclear Research Center is complete.

In the process of putting Romania's first atomic reactor and this cyclotron into production, Soviet experts gave Romania enormous assistance. Now, Romanian scientists and technicians have mastered the advanced technology of operating their nuclear machines.

### 罗马尼亚原子核研究中心日臻完备  第一台回旋加速器开始工作

1958-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚原子核研究中心日臻完备
    第一台回旋加速器开始工作
    新华社布加勒斯特17日电　罗马尼亚去年11月初建成的第一合回旋加速器已经在16日正式开始使用。这台回旋加速器与去年8月投入生产的罗马尼亚第一个原子反应堆和罗马尼亚现有的各种实验室一起开始工作，标志着罗马尼亚原子核研究的物质基础的发展的第一阶段。
    这台回旋加速器是罗马尼亚科学院原子核物理研究所原子核研究中心的第二项主要工程（第一项主要工程是去年7月建成的原子反应堆）。它可以把粒子加速到一千二百五十万电子伏特和把氘核加速到二千五百万电子伏特。根据在回旋加速器反应室内的计算，到现在为止，用这种能量所获得的离子的最高电流是三百微安（培）。这台回旋加速器可以生产为科学研究所需要的加速的带电粒子，也可以生产在原子反应堆中不能得到的放射性同位素。有了原子反应堆和这台回旋加速器，罗马尼亚原子核研究中心就完备了。
    在罗马尼亚第一个原子反应堆和这台回旋加速器投入生产的过程中，苏联专家给了罗马尼亚巨大帮助。现在，罗马尼亚的科学家和技术人员已经掌握了操纵他们所拥有的原子核机器的先进技术。
