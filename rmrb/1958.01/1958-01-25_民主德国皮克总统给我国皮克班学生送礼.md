Title: President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic Sends Gifts to Students of the Pieck Class in China
Author:
Date: 1958-01-25
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, January 24th:  Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Gipner, presented a batch of gifts today on behalf of President Wilhelm Pieck to all the students of the "Wilhelm Pieck Class" of Beijing No. 65 Middle School – the fifth class of the third year of senior high school.

    At the gift-giving ceremony, Ambassador Gipner said to the students: Not long ago, to celebrate President Pieck's birthday, you and I held a celebration together. President Pieck, in order to thank you for your kindness, has specially asked me to send you some gifts. Ambassador Gipner and his wife then presented portraits of President Pieck to each of the forty-seven students in the class. Student representative Wang Qijun expressed heartfelt thanks to President Pieck and Ambassador Gipner for their deep concern, and asked the Ambassador to convey a good news to the President: Starting next week, the students of the Pieck Class will go to the countryside to participate in short-term voluntary labor, living, eating, and working alongside the peasants. She said that they are confident in training themselves to become outstanding sons and daughters of the working class, and to be good students of President Pieck.

### 民主德国皮克总统给我国皮克班学生送礼

1958-01-25
第7版()
专栏：

    民主德国皮克总统给我国皮克班学生送礼
    新华社24日讯　今天，德意志民主共和国驻华大使纪普纳代表威廉·皮克总统把一批礼物送给了北京市第六十五中学“威廉·皮克班”——高中三年级第五班的全体学生。
    在赠送礼物的仪式上，纪普纳大使向学生们说：不久前，为庆祝皮克总统的生日，我和你们曾共同举行了一次庆祝会。皮克总统为了答谢你们的好意，特让我给你们送来一些礼物。纪普纳大使和夫人接着就把皮克总统的肖像，分别送给这个班的四十七个学生。学生代表王启俊对皮克总统和纪普纳大使的深切关怀表示衷心的感谢，她请大使转告总统一个好消息：从下星期起，皮克班的同学们将到农村去参加短期的义务劳动，和农民同吃同住同劳动。她说，她们有信心把自己锻炼成为工人阶级的优秀儿女，做一个皮克总统的好学生。
