Title: To Increase Phosphate Fertilizer Production (Picture)
Author: Qin Jun
Date: 1958-01-13
Page: 3

    To Increase Phosphate Fertilizer Production
    Workers at the state-owned Jinping Phosphate Mine are actively supporting farmers in realizing the forty articles of the National Programme for Agricultural Development. The miners are busily loading raw phosphate ore to be sent to processing plants to manufacture phosphate fertilizer. Photo by Qin Jun.
