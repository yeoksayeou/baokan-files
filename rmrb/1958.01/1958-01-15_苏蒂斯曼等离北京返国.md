Title: Sudisman and Others Leave Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1958-01-15
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, January 14th: The Indonesian Communist Party delegation, led by Sudisman, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 14th for Kunming after a four-week tour of Beijing, Shanghai, and other places, and will return home via Rangoon.
    Tan Zhenlin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, and Zhao Yimin and Li Qixin, responsible staff members of the organs of the CPC Central Committee, went to the airport to see them off.

### 苏蒂斯曼等离北京返国

1958-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏蒂斯曼等离北京返国
    新华社14日讯　以印度尼西亚共产党中央政治局委员苏蒂斯曼为首的印度尼西亚共产党代表团，在北京、上海等地进行了四个星期的参观访问之后，已在14日早晨乘飞机离开北京前往昆明，取道仰光返国。
    前往机场欢送的，有中共中央书记处书记谭震林，中央委员刘宁一，中共中央机关负责工作人员赵毅敏、李启新。
