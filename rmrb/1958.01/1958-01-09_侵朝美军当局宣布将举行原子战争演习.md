Title: U.S. Military Authorities Invading Korea Announce Atomic War Exercise
Author:
Date: 1958-01-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th -- Seoul dispatch: The U.S. Eighth Army authorities invading Korea announced on the 7th that an atomic war exercise will be held in South Korea in January. It is said that this is the largest exercise held in South Korea since the Korean armistice in 1953.

The commander of the exercise said that the purpose of the exercise is to train troops with "new technologies under research for combat and life on the atomic battlefield."

It is announced that the exercise will be held between Seoul and the Imjin River to its north, with thousands of soldiers from the United States, South Korea, Turkey and other accomplice troops invading Korea participating.

### 侵朝美军当局宣布将举行原子战争演习

1958-01-09
第5版()
专栏：

    侵朝美军当局宣布
    将举行原子战争演习
    新华社平壤8日电　汉城消息：侵朝美军第八军当局在7日宣布，1月份将在南朝鲜举行一次原子战争演习，据说这是1953年朝鲜停战以来在南朝鲜举行的规模最大的一次演习。
    这次演习的指挥官说，演习的目的是以“正在研究中的在原子战场上作战和生活的新技术”来训练部队。
    据宣布，这次演习将在汉城和它北面的临津江之间举行，有美国、南朝鲜和土耳其等其他侵朝帮凶军队的数千名士兵参加。
