Title: Prime Minister Phouma Leaves France for the United States
Author:
Date: 1958-01-14
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 12th – Laotian Prime Minister Phouma left Paris on the 11th for Washington. He will continue his visits to the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom before returning to Paris.
    Phouma arrived in Paris on January 5th. According to a statement by the French Foreign Ministry, Prime Minister Phouma, along with the accompanying Laotian Minister of Finance, Minister of Defense, and Minister of Public Works and Transportation, held talks with French Prime Minister Félix Gaillard and Foreign Minister Pineau between the 5th and 8th regarding the development of the political situation within the Kingdom of Laos, Laos remaining within the French Union, and French aid to Laos. The talks will continue after Phouma returns to Paris.

### 富马首相离法赴美

1958-01-14
第5版()
专栏：

    富马首相离法赴美
    据新华社12日讯　巴黎消息：老挝首相富马11日离开巴黎前往华盛顿，他将继续访问美国、加拿大和英国，然后再回到巴黎。
    富马是在1月5日到达巴黎的。据法国外交部公布，富马首相和随行的老挝财政大臣、国防大臣、公共工程和运输大臣等同法国总理盖伊阿和外交部长比诺等在5日到8日之间就老挝王国国内政治形势的发展、老挝留在法兰西联邦之内和法国对老挝的援助问题进行了会谈。会谈将在富马重回巴黎之后继续举行。
