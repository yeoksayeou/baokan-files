Visiting the People Who Give Us Warmth
Shao Yu
(Sketches of some literary and art workers in Beijing visiting coal miners in Mentougou and Chenzihe on January 15, organized by the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles)
    We are not afraid of the cold,
    Because of your labor,
    It is
      Heat and Spring!
1. A corner of the Mentougou mining area
2. An electric tram commissioning ceremony is being held in the newly built wide tunnel of the Mentougou Mine
3. Mr. Mei Lanfang visiting in the mine
4. Employees of the Chenzi Mine watching the visiting performance by Peking Opera artists
