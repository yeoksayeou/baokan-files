Title: Soviet Representatives Attending the Asian-African Solidarity Conference Meet with People from All Walks of Life in Egypt
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 4th: According to TASS, members of the Soviet delegation attending the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference are visiting and learning about the lives of the Egyptian people. The representatives have traveled to many cities and provinces in Egypt and met with people from all walks of life in Egyptian society.

    Representatives from the Soviet Central Asian Republics and Azerbaijan, led by Rashidov, Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Uzbek Republic, visited the Dean of Al-Azhar University, Sheikh Abdel Rahman Tag.

    The Dean said that the Egyptian people greatly respect the Soviet people. He pointed out that when Egypt was in difficulty, in the Egyptian people's struggle against imperialism, the Soviet Union gave the Egyptian people resolute and friendly support. He said that the Egyptian people will never forget this noble assistance from the Soviet people.

    Baba Khanov, Deputy Chairman of the Muslim Religious Board of Central Asia and Kazakhstan, invited the Dean of Al-Azhar University, Abdel Rahman Tag, to visit the Soviet Union at his convenience. The Dean expressed his heartfelt thanks for the invitation and said that he would be very happy to visit the Soviet Union.

### 出席亚非团结大会苏联代表  会见埃及各阶层人士

1958-01-06
第5版()
专栏：

    出席亚非团结大会苏联代表
    会见埃及各阶层人士
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社开罗4日讯：参加亚非人民团结大会的苏联代表团团员们正在埃及参观和了解埃及人民的生活。代表们到了埃及的许多城市和省份，会见了埃及社会各阶层的人士。
    以苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席、乌兹别克共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席拉希多夫为首的苏联中亚细亚共和国和阿塞拜疆的代表，访问了“爱资哈尔”大学校长阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·塔格教长。
    这位教长说，埃及人民非常尊敬苏联人民。他指出，当埃及困难的时候，在埃及人民反对帝国主义的斗争中，苏联给了埃及人民坚决友好的支持。他说，埃及人民永远也忘不了苏联人民的这一崇高的援助。
    中亚细亚和哈萨克穆斯林教务委员会副主席巴巴汗诺夫邀请“爱资哈尔”大学校长阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·塔格教长在方便的时候去访问苏联。教长表示衷心感谢这一邀请，并说他很高兴到苏联去。
