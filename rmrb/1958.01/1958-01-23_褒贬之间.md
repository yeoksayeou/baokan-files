Column: Notes

    Between Praise and Criticism
    According to the American "Newsweek," in the eyes of people of all countries, Dulles is an "extremely unpopular" person, a "die-hard person," an "obstinate and meddlesome person," and the "heir to modern Asian colonial despots."
    But just a few days ago, U.S. President Eisenhower praised Dulles, calling him the "most intelligent and most loyal to the cause person."
    From these two different statements, people can easily understand what kind of stuff the American "cause" to which Dulles is loyal is all about.

### 褒贬之间

1958-01-23
第5版()
专栏：札记

    褒贬之间
    据美国“新闻周刊”说，在各国公众的眼里，杜勒斯是个“极端不受欢迎”的人，是个“冥顽不化的人”，是个“顽固而爱管闲事的人”，是“亚洲近代殖民地暴君的继承人”。
    但是就在几天之前，美国总统艾森豪威尔却给杜勒斯吹捧一番，把他称赞为“最明智和最忠于事业的人”。
    从这两种不同的说法当中，人们很容易明白杜勒斯所忠于的美国“事业”究竟是什么名堂。
