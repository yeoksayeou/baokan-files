Title: Nehru Supports Summit Conference; Japanese Social Figures Call on UN to Facilitate East-West Talks
Author:
Date: 1958-01-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, January 15th -- According to Agence France-Presse, New Delhi: Indian Prime Minister Nehru told Agence France-Presse reporters on January 14th that he supports Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin's proposal to hold a summit conference.

Nehru said, "I have said publicly many times that it is necessary to hold such a meeting, some kind of summit talks must be held. The reality of human life also requires such a meeting."

Xinhua News Agency, January 15th, Tokyo News: The "Seven-Person Committee Calling for World Peace," composed of prominent figures in Japanese society, sent a telegram to United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld today, requesting the United Nations to actively arrange summit talks between East and West to discuss disarmament and the prohibition of the testing and use of nuclear weapons.

Members of this committee include the famous physicist Hideki Yukawa, the President of Tokyo University, Seishi Kaya, the Director-General of the World Federation Construction League, Yasaburo Shimonaka, and the Honorary President of the Japan Women's Organizations Federation, Raicho Hiratsuka, among others.

Hideki Yukawa had signed the letter that American scientist Linus Pauling handed to Hammarskjöld on the 13th, which requested a halt to hydrogen bomb tests.

### 尼赫鲁支持召开最高级会议  日本社会名流呼吁联合国促成东西方会谈

1958-01-16
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁支持召开最高级会议
    日本社会名流呼吁联合国促成东西方会谈
    新华社15日讯　据法新社新德里讯：印度总理尼赫鲁14日对法新社记者说，他支持苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁关于举行最高级会议的建议。
    尼赫鲁说：“我已经公开说过好几次，有必要召开这样的会议，必须举行某种最高级会谈。人类生活的事实也要求举行这种会议。”
    新华社15日讯　东京消息：日本社会名流组成的“呼吁世界和平七人委员会”在今天打电报给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，要求联合国积极安排东西方之间的最高级会谈，来讨论裁减军备和禁止试验、使用核武器问题。
    参加这个委员会的有著名物理学家汤川秀树、东京大学校长茅诚司、世界联邦建设同盟理事长下中弥三郎、全日本妇女团体联合会名誉会长平塚雷鸟等人。
    汤川秀树曾经在美国科学家保林13日交给哈马舍尔德的信上签了名，这封信要求停止氢弹试验。
