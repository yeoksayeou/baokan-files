Title: Chairman Mao Receives General Secretary Buck
Author:
Date: 1958-01-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 27th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China received Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, on the afternoon of January 27th. Present at the reception was Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

### 毛主席接见布克总书记

1958-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见布克总书记
    新华社27日讯　中共中央主席毛泽东在1月27日下午接见了加拿大劳工进步党总书记蒂姆·布克。接见时在座的，有中共中央委员刘宁一。
