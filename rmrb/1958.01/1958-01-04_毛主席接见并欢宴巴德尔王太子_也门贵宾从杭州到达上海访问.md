Title: Chairman Mao Receives and Hosts Crown Prince al-Badr; Yemeni Guests Arrive in Shanghai from Hangzhou for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1958-01-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Crown Prince Muhammad al-Badr, Deputy Prime Minister, Foreign and Defense Minister of the Kingdom of Yemen, and his entourage during their visit to the South. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Foreign Minister Zeng Yongquan were present at the reception.
    Chairman Mao Zedong also hosted a banquet to welcome the Yemeni Crown Prince.
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - Crown Prince al-Badr, Deputy Prime Minister, Foreign and Defense Minister of the Kingdom of Yemen, and his entourage, accompanied by Vice Foreign Minister Zeng Yongquan and Director of the West Asia and Africa Department Ke Hua, arrived in Shanghai from Hangzhou by special plane on the morning of the 3rd for a visit, and were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in Shanghai.
    When Crown Prince al-Badr left Hangzhou, Premier Zhou Enlai went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

### 毛主席接见并欢宴巴德尔王太子  也门贵宾从杭州到达上海访问

1958-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见并欢宴巴德尔王太子
    也门贵宾从杭州到达上海访问
    新华社3日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席在也门王国副首相兼外交、国防大臣穆罕默德·巴德尔王太子访问南方期间，接见了穆罕默德·巴德尔王太子和他的随行人员。接见时在座的有周恩来总理和外交部副部长曾涌泉等。
    毛泽东主席并设宴欢迎也门王太子。
    据新华社3日讯　也门王国副首相兼外交、国防大臣巴德尔王太子和他的随行人员，由外交部副部长曾涌泉、西亚非洲司司长柯华陪同，在3日上午乘专机从杭州到达上海访问，受到上海各界的热烈欢迎。
    在巴德尔王太子离开杭州的时候，周恩来总理曾到机场欢送贵宾。
