Title: Groza's Funeral and Memorial Service Held Today; Chinese and Soviet Government Delegations Arrive in Bucharest; Leaders of Socialist Countries Send Condolence Messages

Author:

Date: 1958-01-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, January 9th: The Romanian people continue to mourn deeply the passing of Chairman Groza.

Many labor people's delegations from all over the country came to Bucharest today to bid farewell to Chairman Groza along with the people of the capital. Farmers from Chairman Groza's hometown of Bacia Village, upon hearing the news of his death, immediately sent a delegation to the capital to mourn and participate in the vigil. On Victory Avenue outside the Republic Palace, where the Chairman's body lay in state, people lined up for three or four kilometers to say goodbye. Due to the large number of people, they had to wait for two or three hours on the cold street to enter the palace.

In the past two days, many memorial meetings have been held successively in factories, mines, enterprises, organs, and rural areas across the country, reporting on the life and deeds of Chairman Groza. Those present also expressed their determination to work harder and unite more firmly around the Party and the government in memory of Chairman Groza.

The National Funeral Committee announced that a memorial service for Chairman Groza will be held at 10:00 AM tomorrow in Republic Square in front of the Republic Palace. From 10:00 AM to 10:03 AM, all organs, factories, and enterprises across the country will cease work to express mourning. After the memorial service, a burial ceremony will be held. Chairman Groza's coffin will be buried in the Ghencea Military Cemetery.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, January 8th: The delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, headed by Guo Moruo and including member Burhan, to attend the funeral of Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania, arrived in Bucharest by plane from Budapest on the afternoon of the 8th. Another member, Ke Bonian, is already in Bucharest.

The Soviet government delegation, led by Kozlov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation and member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, to attend Chairman Groza's funeral, arrived in Bucharest by plane on the 8th.

This newspaper reports collectively from Xinhua News Agency: Leaders of the parties and governments of socialist countries have sent telegrams of condolence on the death of Chairman Groza. Among those who have sent condolence messages are Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party; Zawadzki, Chairman of the Polish Council of State, and Wiceh, Speaker of the Parliament; Kadar, Prime Minister of Hungary; the Presidium of the Great People's Hural, the Council of Ministers, and the Central Committee of the People's Revolutionary Party of Mongolia. Also, the Presidium of the People's Assembly, the Council of Ministers, and the Central Committee of the Labor Party of Albania; the Presidium of the National Assembly, the Council of Ministers, and the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party of Bulgaria. Yugoslav President Tito and Federal National Assembly President Stambolic sent telegrams to the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania, Council of Ministers Chairman Stoica, and Mrs. Groza, respectively, to express condolences on the death of Chairman Groza.

On January 8th, Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, on behalf of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the Central Committee of the CPSU, and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, went to the Romanian Embassy in Moscow to express condolences on the death of Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania.

### 格罗查葬礼和追悼会今日举行  中苏两国政府代表团抵布加勒斯特  社会主义国家领导人纷纷致电吊唁

1958-01-10
第6版()
专栏：

    格罗查葬礼和追悼会今日举行
    中苏两国政府代表团抵布加勒斯特
    社会主义国家领导人纷纷致电吊唁
    新华社布加勒斯特9日电　罗马尼亚人民继续沉痛哀悼格罗查主席的逝世。
    全国各地许多劳动人民代表团今天来到了布加勒斯特，同首都的人民一起向格罗查主席告别。主席的故乡巴奇阿村的农民在听到他逝世的消息以后，立即派遣代表团来首都哀悼，并参加守灵。在安放主席遗体的共和国宫外的胜利大街上，前来告别的人排成了三、四公里长的队伍。由于人多，他们要在严寒的大街上等候两、三个小时才能进入宫内。
    这两天来，全国各地的厂矿、企业、机关、农村都先后举行了许多次追悼大会，会上报告了格罗查主席的生平事迹。到会者并且表示为纪念格罗查主席要更加努力工作，更加坚决地团结在党和政府的周围。
    国家治丧委员会宣布说：明天上午十时，将在共和国宫前的共和国广场举行格罗查主席的追悼大会。十时到十时三分，全国一切机关、工厂、企业中将停止工作，表示哀悼。追悼大会结束后，将举行安葬仪式。格罗查主席的灵柩将安葬在根恰军人陵园。
    据新华社布加勒斯特8日电参加罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查葬礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长郭沫若、团员包尔汉，在8日下午乘飞机由布达佩斯到达布加勒斯特。另一团员柯柏年已在布加勒斯特。
    由俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席、苏共中央主席团委员科兹洛夫率领的参加格罗查主席葬礼的苏联政府代表团，在8日乘飞机到达布加勒斯特。
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：社会主义国家的党和政府领导人纷纷致电吊唁格罗查主席逝世。先后发出唁电的有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健和内阁首相、劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成，波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基、议会议长维策赫，匈牙利总理卡达尔，蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团、部长会议和人民革命党中央委员会。还有阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团、部长会议、劳动党中央委员会，保加利亚国民议会主席团、部长会议和保加利亚共产党中央委员会。南斯拉夫总统铁托和联邦国民议会主席斯坦鲍利奇分别打电报给罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团、部长会议主席斯托伊卡和格罗查夫人，吊唁格罗查主席逝世。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫8日代表苏联最高苏维埃、苏共中央和苏联部长会议到罗马尼亚驻莫斯科大使馆对罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团主席格罗查的逝世表示吊唁。
