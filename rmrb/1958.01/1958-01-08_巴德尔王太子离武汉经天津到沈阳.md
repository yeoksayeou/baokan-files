Title: Crown Prince Badr Leaves Wuhan, Via Tianjin, for Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1958-01-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 7th. Crown Prince Badr, Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs and Minister of Defense of the Kingdom of Yemen, and his entourage, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and others, left Wuhan by special plane at 9:00 this morning for Shenyang.

Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Li Minghao, Deputy Governor, Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, Pan Shoucai, Commander of the Garrison, and many others saw them off at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 7th. Crown Prince Badr, Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs and Minister of Defense of the Kingdom of Yemen, and his entourage, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department, arrived in Tianjin from Wuhan by special plane at 12:20 noon today.

After the distinguished guests disembarked from the plane, they were welcomed by Huang Huoqing, Mayor of Tianjin, Zhou Shutao, Yang Yizhou, and Zhang Guofan, Deputy Mayors, Wu Yannong, Vice Chairman of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Major General Fang Zhizhong, Commander of the PLA Garrison in Tianjin, and responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations in Tianjin.

Crown Prince Badr and his entourage, accompanied by Mayor Huang Huoqing and others, had lunch together in the airport restaurant.

At 2:15 p.m., the distinguished guests, accompanied by Zhao Erlu, Minister of the Second Ministry of Machine Building, and Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department, flew from Tianjin to Shenyang.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 7th. Crown Prince Badr, Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs and Minister of Defense of the Kingdom of Yemen, and his entourage, accompanied by Zhao Erlu, Minister of the Second Ministry of Machine Building of our country, and Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department, arrived in Shenyang by special plane this afternoon.

Greeting them at the airport were Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, General Deng Hua, Commander of the Shenyang Garrison, Liu Baotian, Mayor of Shenyang, and more than 2,000 people from all walks of life in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City.

Kim Yong-bin, Consul General of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Shenyang, and personnel from the consulate also went to the airport to welcome them.

When the distinguished guests' special plane flew over Shenyang's Dongta Airport, which was flying the national flags of China and Yemen, the welcoming masses at the airport waved bouquets and colored flags, and the sounds of gongs, drums, and cheers rang out. After Crown Prince Badr and his entourage disembarked from the plane, they received flowers from the Young Pioneers and shook hands with the responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City who came to welcome them. As Crown Prince Badr walked through the welcoming crowd, the masses enthusiastically shouted welcome slogans, and young women continuously presented flowers to the distinguished guests. Crown Prince Badr also frequently waved to the welcoming masses.

In the evening, Crown Prince Badr and his entourage attended a banquet held by Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province. After the banquet, the distinguished guests attended a film evening.

### 巴德尔王太子离武汉经天津到沈阳

1958-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    巴德尔王太子离武汉经天津到沈阳
    新华社武汉7日电　也门王国副首相兼外交、国防大臣巴德尔王太子和随行人员，今天上午九时在外交部副部长曾涌泉等陪同下，乘专机离开武汉去沈阳。
    湖北省省长张体学、副省长李明灏，武汉市市长刘惠农、驻军首长潘寿才等多人到机场送行。
    新华社天津7日电　也门王国副首相兼外交、国防大臣巴德尔王太子和他的随行人员，由外交部副部长曾涌泉、西亚非洲司司长柯华陪同，从武汉乘专机在今天中午十二时二十分到达天津。
    贵宾们下飞机后，受到天津市市长黄火青，副市长周叔弢、杨亦周、张国藩，政协天津市委员会副主席吴砚农，中国人民解放军驻津部队首长方之中少将和天津市各民主党派、人民团体负责人的欢迎。
    巴德尔王太子和他的随行人员由黄火青市长等陪同，在机场餐厅共进午餐。
    下午二时十五分，贵宾们由第二机械工业部部长赵尔陆、西亚非洲司司长柯华陪同由天津飞往沈阳。
    新华社沈阳7日电　也门王国副首相兼外交、国防大臣巴德尔王太子和他的随行人员，由我国第二机械工业部部长赵尔陆、西亚非洲司司长柯华陪同，今天下午乘专机到达沈阳。
    到机场欢迎的，有辽宁省省长杜者蘅、沈阳驻军首长邓华上将、沈阳市市长刘宝田以及辽宁省和沈阳市各界人士两千多人。
    朝鲜驻沈阳总领事金龙彬和领事馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    当贵宾们的专机飞到飘扬着中国也门两国国旗的沈阳东塔机场上空时，机场上的欢迎群众纷纷挥动花束和彩旗，锣鼓声和欢呼声响成一片。巴德尔王太子和随行人员下飞机后，接受了少先队员们的献花，并且同前来欢迎他们的辽宁省和沈阳市各民主党派、人民团体负责人一一握手见面。巴德尔王太子穿过欢迎的人群时，群众热情地高呼欢迎口号，女青年们不断向贵宾们献花。巴德尔王太子也频频挥手向欢迎的群众致意。
    晚上，巴德尔王太子和他的随行人员出席了辽宁省省长杜者蘅举行的宴会。宴会结束后，贵宾们出席了电影晚会。
