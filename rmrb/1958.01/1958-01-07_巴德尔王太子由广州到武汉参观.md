Title: Crown Prince Badr Visits Wuhan from Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1958-01-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 6th: Crown Prince Badr, Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs and Minister of Defense of the Kingdom of Yemen, and his entourage, left Guangzhou by special plane for Wuhan at 11:00 this morning. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and Director of the West Asia and Africa Department Ke Hua accompanied the distinguished guests.

Vice Governor of Guangdong Province Wei Jinfei, party and government leaders of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, military leaders stationed there, representatives of democratic parties and people's organizations, and heads of consulates of various countries in Guangzhou went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

This morning, before leaving Guangzhou, the distinguished guests also visited Guangzhou Paper Mill, China's largest newsprint factory, and admired the famous multi-layered ivory ball carving technique at Guangzhou No. 1 Ivory Carving Cooperative.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, January 6th: Crown Prince Badr, Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs and Minister of Defense of the Kingdom of Yemen, and his entourage, arrived in Wuhan by special plane from Guangzhou this afternoon. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and Director of the West Asia and Africa Department Ke Hua accompanied them.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Li Minghao, Deputy Governor, Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, and heads of local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

This afternoon, Crown Prince Badr and his entourage, accompanied by Wuhan Mayor Liu Huinong, visited the Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant, one of China's key construction projects. The distinguished guests visited the first processing and assembly workshop, which has been basically completed, and the casting workshop, which is under intense construction. Factory Director Shi Ziming explained the construction process of the factory to Crown Prince Badr in detail.

Then, the distinguished guests came to visit the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge. As night fell and the lights came on, Crown Prince Badr and others watched the magnificent bridge with great interest, and also admired the night view of the three towns of Wuhan connected by the bridge on the highway bridge deck. The distinguished guests also toured Wuhan city today.

In the evening, Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, had dinner with the distinguished guests.

### 巴德尔王太子由广州到武汉参观

1958-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    巴德尔王太子由广州到武汉参观
    新华社广州6日电　也门王国副首相兼外交和国防大臣巴德尔王太子及其随行人员，今天上午十一时乘专机离广州飞武汉。外交部副部长曾涌泉、西亚非洲司司长柯华陪同贵宾前往。
    广东省副省长魏今非，广东省和广州市的党、政负责人和驻军首长，民主党派和人民团体的代表，以及各国驻广州领事馆负责人都到机场上欢送贵宾。
    今天早晨，贵宾们在离广州前还到我国最大的新闻纸工厂——广州造纸厂参观，并在广州市第一象牙雕刻社欣赏了著名的多层象牙球雕刻技术。
    新华社武汉6日电　也门王国副首相兼外交、国防大臣巴德尔王太子和他的随行人员，从广州乘专机在今天下午到达武汉。陪同前来的有外交部副部长曾涌泉和西亚非洲司司长柯华。
    贵宾们在机场上，受到湖北省省长张体学、副省长李明灏，武汉市市长刘惠农和各民主党派、各人民团体地方组织负责人的热烈欢迎。
    今天下午，巴德尔王太子和他的随行人员在武汉市市长刘惠农陪同下，参观了我国重点建设工程之一的武汉重型机床厂。贵宾们在这里参观了已经基本上完工的第一加工装配车间和正在紧张施工的铸造车间。厂长史梓铭详尽地向巴德尔王太子介绍了这个工厂的建设经过。
    接着，贵宾们来到武汉长江大桥参观。这时，夜幕临下，华灯初上，巴德尔王太子等以极大兴趣观看了这座雄伟瑰丽的大桥，还在公路桥面上欣赏了被这座桥联结起来的武汉三镇夜景。贵宾们今天还游览了武汉市容。
    夜间，湖北省省长张体学同贵宾们共进晚餐。
