Title: Egyptian National Assembly Awards Nasser "Order of the People"
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 17th - The Egyptian National Assembly held a ceremony on the evening of the 16th to award President Nasser the "Order of the People."

    This special meeting of the National Assembly was the most important event in the nationwide Egyptian celebrations of the second anniversary of the promulgation of the constitution.

    National Assembly Speaker Boghdadi said that Nasser, with his wisdom, patience, unyielding spirit, and full confidence in the rights of the people, led the people in their struggle and won victory in the struggle, thereby realizing the people's desire for freedom and justice.

    President Nasser said, "You represent the people in awarding me this medal, and I also represent the people in accepting it."

    In his brief speech, he emphasized the significance of national unity.

### 埃及国民议会授予纳赛尔“人民勋章”

1958-01-18
第5版()
专栏：

    埃及国民议会授予纳赛尔“人民勋章”
    据新华社开罗17日电　埃及国民议会在16日晚间举行典礼，把“人民勋章”授给纳赛尔总统。
    国民议会这次特别会议是埃及全国庆祝宪法颁布两周年的活动中最重要的一次。
    国民议会议长波格达迪说，纳赛尔曾经以他的智慧、耐心和不屈不挠的精神，以及对人民权利的充分信心，领导人民进行了斗争，并且在斗争中获得了胜利，从而实现了人民争取自由和正义的愿望。
    纳赛尔总统说：“你们代表人民把这枚勋章授给我，我也代表人民来接受它。”
    他在简短的讲话中强调民族团结的意义。
