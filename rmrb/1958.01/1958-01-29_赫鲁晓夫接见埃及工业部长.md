Title: Khrushchev Receives Egyptian Minister of Industry
Author:
Date: 1958-01-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 28th: According to TASS, Khrushchev, member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, received Sidky, the head of the Egyptian government's economic delegation and Egyptian Minister of Industry, in the Kremlin on the 27th and spoke with him.

Bulganin, Mikoyan, and Pervukhin also participated in the conversation.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见埃及工业部长

1958-01-29
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见埃及工业部长
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫27日在克里姆林宫接见了埃及政府经济代表团团长、埃及工业部长西德基，并且和他谈了话。
    布尔加宁、米高扬和别尔乌辛也参加了这次谈话。
