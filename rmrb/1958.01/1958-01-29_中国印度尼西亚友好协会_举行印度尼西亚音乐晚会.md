Title: China-Indonesia Friendship Association Holds Indonesian Music Evening
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 28th. The China-Indonesia Friendship Association held an Indonesian music evening in Beijing on the 28th. The Ambassador of the Republic of Indonesia to China, Mr. Wirjopranoto, was invited to attend the evening and gave a report on the struggle of the Indonesian people to recover West Irian before the start of the evening. His report was warmly welcomed by more than five hundred listeners.

In his report, Ambassador Wirjopranoto said that the Indonesian government and people firmly believe that their struggle to recover West Irian will be victorious, and that the unity of the Indonesian people has the strength to deal with the aggression of the Dutch colonialists. He also said that President Sukarno and Chairman Mao Zedong share the same views on the issue of opposing colonialism and imperialism.

The Minister of Public Works and Energy of Indonesia, Mr. Pangeran Mohammad Noor, who is currently visiting Beijing, was also invited to attend the music evening.

The music evening was hosted by Wang Renshu, a member of the Council of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association. At the evening, the Chorus of the Central People's Broadcasting Station Broadcasting Orchestra, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Military Band and other units performed six Indonesian programs including chorus, solo, and Balinese music. Balinese dance experts from Indonesia working at the Beijing Dance School were also invited to participate in the performance. Their performances were warmly welcomed.

### 中国印度尼西亚友好协会  举行印度尼西亚音乐晚会

1958-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中国印度尼西亚友好协会
    举行印度尼西亚音乐晚会
    新华社28日讯　中国印度尼西亚友好协会28日晚在北京举行印度尼西亚音乐晚会。印度尼西亚共和国驻中国大使维约普拉诺托应邀出席了晚会，并且在晚会开始前做了关于印度尼西亚人民收复西伊里安斗争的报告。他的报告受到了五百多名听众的热烈欢迎。
    维约普拉诺托大使在报告中说，印度尼西亚政府和人民深信他们收复西伊里安的斗争是会获得胜利的，印度尼西亚人民的团结有力量对付荷兰殖民主义者的侵略。他还说，在反对殖民主义和反对帝国主义斗争的问题上，苏加诺总统和毛泽东主席的看法是一致的。
    正在北京访问的印度尼西亚公共工程及动力部部长班额尔兰·穆罕默德·努尔也应邀出席了音乐晚会。
    音乐晚会由中国印度尼西亚友好协会理事王任叔主持。在晚会上，由中央人民广播电台广播乐团合唱队、中国人民解放军军乐团等单位演出了合唱、独唱、峇厘音乐等六个印度尼西亚节目。在北京舞蹈学校工作的印度尼西亚峇厘舞专家也应邀参加了演出。他们的演出受到了热烈欢迎。
