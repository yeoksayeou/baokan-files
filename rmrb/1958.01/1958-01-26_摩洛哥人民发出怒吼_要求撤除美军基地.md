Title: Moroccan People Roar, Demanding the Removal of U.S. Military Bases
Author:
Date: 1958-01-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: According to TASS, Cairo, January 24th: A prominent activist in the Moroccan national liberation movement, Mohammed Hassan Wazzani, told a reporter from the Cairo weekly "Akhir Saa" that American military bases in Morocco are storing atomic and hydrogen weapons, and the Moroccan people are angrily protesting the establishment of American military bases in their country.

Wazzani pointed out that the United States has long established five military bases in Morocco, and two new bases are nearing completion. These bases are scattered near Rabat, Sidi Slimane, Port Lyautey, and Casablanca. The American base in the Aïn Chkair area is storing atomic and hydrogen weapons.

Wazzani emphasized that the Moroccan people demand the removal of these bases.

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: Rabat Dispatch: U.S. Secretary of State Dulles stopped in Marrakesh, Morocco, for several hours on January 23rd en route to the Baghdad Pact meeting scheduled to open on the 27th, and held talks with Moroccan Foreign Minister Ahmed Balafrej.

Sources revealed that the talks covered issues such as the U.S. bases in Morocco. The Moroccan side also wanted to know Washington's attitude towards the Ifni issue.

### 摩洛哥人民发出怒吼  要求撤除美军基地

1958-01-26
第5版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥人民发出怒吼
    要求撤除美军基地
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社开罗24日讯：摩洛哥民族解放运动著名活动家穆罕默德·哈桑·维萨尼对开罗“最后一点钟”周刊记者发表谈话说，在摩洛哥的美国军事基地内正在储存原子武器和氢武器，摩洛哥人民愤怒抗议在自己的国家里建立美国军事基地。
    维萨尼指出，美国早就在摩洛哥建立了五处军事基地，另外有两个新基地即将完成。这些基地散布在拉巴特、西迪斯利曼、里俄蒂港和卡萨布兰卡等地附近。在艾利科奈达尔地区的美国基地内正在储存原子武器和氢武器。
    维萨尼强调指出，摩洛哥人民要求撤除这些基地。
    据新华社24日讯　拉巴特消息：美国国务卿杜勒斯23日在前往参加将于27日开幕的巴格达条约会议途中，在摩洛哥的马拉喀什停留了数小时，并且同摩洛哥外交大臣贝拉弗里杰举行了会谈。
    有消息透露，会谈讨论了摩洛哥境内的美国基地等问题。摩洛哥方面还想知道华盛顿对伊夫尼问题的态度。
