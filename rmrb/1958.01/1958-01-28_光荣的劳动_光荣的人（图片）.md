Title: Glorious Labor, Glorious People (Picture)
Author: Ke Zhang
Date: 1958-01-28
Page: 3

    Glorious Labor, Glorious People
    Yan Zirong, a female worker at the Tangshan Huaxin Textile Factory, has five children, but she has managed to maintain perfect attendance for three years and produce zero defective products for two years. Furthermore, due to her eagerness to learn from advanced experiences and her diligent study of technology, the quality of her products has continuously improved, earning her the title of Advanced Worker.

    光荣的劳动  光荣的人
    唐山市华新纺织工厂女工阎子荣已经有五个孩子，可是她却做到了三年不缺勤、二年不出次品。并且由于她虚心学习先进经验，努力钻研技术，产品质量不断提高，获得了先进工作者的称号。	　　克章　摄影
