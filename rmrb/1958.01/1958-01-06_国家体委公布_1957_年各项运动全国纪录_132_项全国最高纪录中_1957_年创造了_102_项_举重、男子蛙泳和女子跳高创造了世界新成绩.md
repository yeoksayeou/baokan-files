Title: State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Announces National Sports Records for 1957; 102 of 132 National Records Were Created in 1957; New World Records Set in Weightlifting, Men's Breaststroke, and Women's High Jump
Author:
Date: 1958-01-06
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China announced on the 5th the 132 national records in weightlifting, swimming, track and field, speed skating, cycling, rowing, and archery as of December 31, 1957. This includes three new world records: Chen Jingkai's 139.5 kg snatch in the featherweight class in weightlifting, Qi Lieyun's 1 minute 11.6 seconds in the men's 100-meter breaststroke in swimming, and Zheng Fengrong's 1.77-meter high jump in track and field.

Among the 132 national records, the results of many events have reached international standards. For example, in the seven weightlifting weight class total scores, the total scores of five weight classes can be ranked in the top six in the 1957 World Weightlifting Championships. The national records newly created in 1957 in the men's 100-meter freestyle, men's high jump, long jump, pole vault, and women's 80-meter hurdles have also reached the level of the top six in the 16th Olympic Games. In 1956, the national records for these events were only close to the sixth-place finish in the 16th Olympic Games.

Of the 132 national records, 102 were created in 1957. These include the national records for the men's marathon, pentathlon, women's triathlon, men's 50 and 100-kilometer cycling, and women's 25 and 50-kilometer cycling events, which were newly launched in 1957. Of the thirteen national records established in 1956 for men's and women's rowing, archery, men's 3,000-meter steeplechase, 10-kilometer race walk, and women's pentathlon, ten were broken in 1957. None of the holders of the forty national records in 1956 were able to maintain the title of "national record holder" in 1957. They were replaced by other athletes.

### 国家体委公布1957年各项运动全国纪录  132项全国最高纪录中1957年创造了102项  举重、男子蛙泳和女子跳高创造了世界新成绩

1958-01-06
第7版()
专栏：

    国家体委公布1957年各项运动全国纪录
    132项全国最高纪录中1957年创造了102项
    举重、男子蛙泳和女子跳高创造了世界新成绩
    新华社5日讯　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会5日公布了截至1957年12月31日为止的一百三十二项举重、游泳、田径、速度滑冰、自行车、划船和射箭运动的全国纪录。其中包括三项世界新纪录：举重中陈镜开的最轻量级挺举一百三十九点五公斤，游泳中戚烈云的男子一百公尺蛙泳一分十一秒六和田径中郑凤荣的女子跳高一公尺七十七公分。
    在一百三十二项全国纪录中，有许多项目的成绩都达到了国际水平。例如七个等级举重总成绩的全国纪录中，有五个等级的总成绩已能列入1957年世界举重锦标赛前六名的行列。1957年新创造的男子一百公尺自由泳、男子跳高、跳远、撑竿跳高和女子八十公尺低栏等项目的全国纪录，也都达到了第十六届奥林匹克运动会前六名的水平。而1956年这些项目的全国纪录，还只是接近了第十六届奥林匹克运动会第六名的成绩。
    在一百三十二项全国纪录中，有一百零二项是在1957年创造的。其中包括1957年新开展的男子马拉松赛跑、五项运动、女子三项运动，男子五十和一百公里自行车，以及女子二十五和五十公里自行车比赛等项运动的全国纪录。1956年新开展的男女划船、射箭，男子三千公尺障碍赛跑、十公里竞走和女子五项运动等十三项全国纪录，也有十项在1957年被刷新。1956年的四十项全国纪录的保持者们，1957年都没能继续保持住“全国纪录保持者”这个称号。他们被另外一些选手代替了。
