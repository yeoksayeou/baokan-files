Title: China Islamic Association Sends Congratulations on the Establishment of the Egypt-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1958-01-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 4th - The China Islamic Association sent a telegram on the 4th to Ahmed Hassan al-Baquri, Chairman of the Egypt-China Friendship Association, congratulating the association on its establishment. The telegram stated: "On the occasion of the establishment of the Egypt-China Friendship Association, our association, on behalf of all Chinese Muslims, extends our warmest congratulations to the Egyptian people. Chinese Muslims and all the Chinese people will always be the most reliable friends and brothers of the Egyptian people. Our friendship is built on the lofty ideals of freedom and peace. May our traditional friendship be continuously strengthened and developed in the common struggle against colonialism and in defense of world peace."

### 中国伊斯兰教协会电贺埃中友协成立

1958-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    中国伊斯兰教协会电贺埃中友协成立
    新华社4日讯　中国伊斯兰教协会4日致电埃中友好协会主席艾哈迈德·哈桑·巴库里，祝贺协会的成立。电报说：“兹值埃中友好协会成立之际，我会谨代表中国全体穆斯林向埃及人民表示最热烈的祝贺。中国穆斯林和全中国人民永远是埃及人民的最可靠的朋友和兄弟。我们的友谊是建立在自由与和平的崇高理想之上的。愿我们的传统友谊在反对殖民主义、保卫世界和平的共同斗争中获得不断的加强和发展。”
