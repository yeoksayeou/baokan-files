Title: Celebrating the Inauguration of "Soviet-Chinese Friendship" Magazine; Soong Ching Ling and Qian Junrui Send Telegrams of Congratulations to Andreyev; Soviet Embassy in China Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1958-01-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 7th: Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, sent a telegram to Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, on January 7th, congratulating him on the inauguration of the "Soviet-Chinese Friendship" magazine. The telegram stated, "The publication of this magazine fully meets the common aspirations of the people of our two countries. We believe that it will make an important contribution to the noble cause of further enhancing and consolidating the fraternal friendship between the people of the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union."

Xinhua News Agency, January 7th: The Soviet Embassy in China held a reception on the evening of January 7th to celebrate the inauguration of the "Soviet-Chinese Friendship" magazine. This Chinese-language magazine, published by the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, was launched on January 1st this year and will be distributed in China on the 8th.

Soviet Embassy in China Counselor Sudarikov and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, spoke at the reception. They both agreed that the birth of this new magazine is another practical example of the friendly cooperation between the people of the Soviet Union and China. In his speech, Sudarikov thanked the Chinese side for the assistance provided for the publication of this magazine. Qian Junrui said that the publication of this magazine is a major event and will greatly help the Chinese people learn from the Soviet Union.

Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhao Yimin and Yang Hansheng, Vice Presidents of the Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and more than one hundred people in charge of the press and publishing circles in the capital were invited to attend the reception.

### 庆祝“苏中友好”杂志创刊  宋庆龄、钱俊瑞电贺安德烈耶夫  苏联驻华大使馆举行招待会

1958-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝“苏中友好”杂志创刊
    宋庆龄、钱俊瑞电贺安德烈耶夫
    苏联驻华大使馆举行招待会
    新华社7日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄、秘书长钱俊瑞7日致电苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫，祝贺“苏中友好”杂志创刊。电报说，“这个杂志的出版，完全适应着我们两国人民的共同愿望。我们相信，它在进一步增进和巩固中苏两大国人民之间兄弟友谊的崇高事业中，必将作出重要的贡献。”
    据新华社7日讯　苏联驻中国大使馆7日晚举行招待会，庆祝“苏中友好”杂志创刊。这个由苏中友好协会出版的汉文杂志，是今年1月1日创刊的，8日将在中国发行。
    苏联驻中国大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫和中苏友好协会总会秘书长钱俊瑞在招待会上先后讲话。他们一致认为这个新杂志的诞生，是苏中两国人民友好合作的又一实践。苏达利柯夫在讲话中感谢中国方面对这个杂志出版所给予的帮助。钱俊瑞说，这个杂志的出版是件大事，它对中国人民学习苏联有很大帮助。
    中共中央宣传部副部长周扬，对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏、阳翰笙和首都新闻、出版界的负责人等一百多人应邀出席了这个招待会。
