Title: My Art Exhibition Opens in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1958-01-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, January 5th. The Chinese Dunhuang Art Exhibition opened this morning on the eighth floor of the Takashimaya Department Store in central Tokyo. More than 200 representatives from prominent Japanese art circles, political circles, economic circles, and civic groups attended the opening ceremony. The Chinese Red Cross delegation to Japan also participated in the opening ceremony.

This exhibition is sponsored jointly by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, which provided the exhibits, and the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association and the Japanese "Mainichi Shimbun" newspaper.

### 我艺术展览会在东京开幕

1958-01-06
第6版()
专栏：

    我艺术展览会在东京开幕
    新华社东京5日电　中国敦煌艺术展览会今天上午在东京市中心高岛屋百货公司八楼上开幕。出席开幕式的有日本艺术界著名人士和政界、经济界以及民间团体的代表二百多人。中国红十字会访日代表团也参加了开幕式。
    这次展览会是由中国人民对外文化协会提供展品、由日中文化交流协会和日本“每日新闻”社联合主办的。
