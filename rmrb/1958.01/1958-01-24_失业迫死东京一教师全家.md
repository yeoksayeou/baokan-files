Title: Unemployment Drives Tokyo Teacher to Kill Entire Family

Author: 
Date: 1958-01-24
Page: 5

A So-Called "Free World"

　　Unemployment Drives Tokyo Teacher to Kill Entire Family

　　Tokyo police authorities say that a 43-year-old unemployed teacher killed his wife and three children with an axe on the evening of the 22nd, then slit his own throat with a razor and committed suicide.

　　This unemployed teacher, Tsuyoshi Fumio (transliteration), used to teach on Amami Oshima Island, Kyushu. He has been unemployed for five years. His children were three, seven, and twelve years old.

### 失业迫死东京一教师全家

1958-01-24
第5版()
专栏：如此“自由世界”

    失业迫死东京一教师全家
    东京警察当局说，一个四十三岁的失业教师22日晚用斧头砍死了妻子和三个儿女，然后用剃刀割喉自杀。
    这位失业教师曾吉文雄（音译）以前是在九州奄美大岛上执教的，失业有五年了。他的儿女一个三岁，一个七岁，一个十二岁。
