Title: Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theater Performs Chinese Songs and Dances
Author:
Date: 1958-01-19
Page: 6
Soviet Cultural News

    The actors of the Soviet National Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theater, who recently returned from a performance tour in China, recently held a concert for the working people of Novosibirsk to report on their visit and performances in China as a creative report.
    The program, performed in workers' clubs, cultural palaces and factories, included lotus dances, tea-picking dances, fan dances and red silk dances performed by actresses, and traditional Chinese waist drum dances and Tibetan cavalry dances performed by male actors. The theater's symphony orchestra also played works by Chinese composers for the audience. The dances and music were learned from China. The costumes, wigs and decorations presented to them by Chinese makeup artists were also used in the performances.

### 新西伯利亚歌舞剧院演出中国歌舞

1958-01-19
第6版()
专栏：苏联文化消息

    新西伯利亚歌舞剧院演出中国歌舞
    不久前从中国回国的苏联国立新西伯利亚歌剧芭蕾舞剧院演员，最近为新西伯利亚市的劳动人民举办关于在中国访问演出的音乐会，作为他们创造性的汇报演出。
    在工人俱乐部、文化宫和工厂里演出的节目中有女演员演出的荷花舞、采茶舞、扇舞和红绸舞，男演员演出的中国传统的腰鼓舞和藏民骑兵舞。剧院的交响乐队也给听众们演奏了中国作曲家的作品。舞蹈和音乐都是他们从中国学来的。演出中还使用了中国的化妆艺术家们赠给他们的服装、假发和装饰品。
