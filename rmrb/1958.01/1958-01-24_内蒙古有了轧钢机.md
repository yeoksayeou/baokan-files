Page: 3

    Inner Mongolia Has a Rolling Mill
    The first small rolling mill in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region was officially put into production on the 20th at the Hohhot Agricultural Machinery Factory. It can roll one ton of steel per hour.

    This year, this rolling mill can roll 7,500 tons of urgently needed steel for the country. In addition to supplying the autonomous region, this steel will also be supplied to industrial departments and basic construction units in more than a dozen provinces and cities, including Hebei and Henan. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 内蒙古有了轧钢机

1958-01-24
第3版()
专栏：东鳞西爪

    内蒙古有了轧钢机
    内蒙古自治区第一台小型轧钢机20日在呼和浩特市农业机械厂正式投入生产。它每小时可以轧制一吨钢材。
    今年，这台轧钢机可以为国家轧制七千五百吨急需的各种钢材。这些钢材除了供应自治区以外，还要供应河北、河南等十几个省市工业部门和基本建设单位。　（据新华社讯）
