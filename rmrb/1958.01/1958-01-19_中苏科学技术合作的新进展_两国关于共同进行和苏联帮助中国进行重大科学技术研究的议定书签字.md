Title: New Developments in Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation: Protocol Signed Between the Two Countries on Jointly Conducting and the Soviet Union Assisting China in Major Scientific and Technological Research
Author:
Date: 1958-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 18th. A protocol between the Chinese government and the Soviet government regarding jointly conducting and the Soviet Union assisting China in major scientific and technological research was signed in Moscow on January 18th. The protocol stipulates that China and the Soviet Union will jointly conduct research on 122 scientific and technological projects of great significance to China and further strengthen direct ties between scientific institutions of the two countries in the next five years (1958-1962).

Signing the protocol on behalf of the two sides were Guo Moruo, Plenipotentiary Representative of the Chinese Government and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and V.A. Maksaarev, Plenipotentiary Representative of the Soviet Government and Chairman of the State Scientific and Technical Committee of the Council of Ministers of the USSR.

At the same time, Huang Songling, Vice Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Higher Education, and Yelyutin, Minister of the Soviet Ministry of Higher Education, and Ding Ying, President of the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences, and Lobanov, President of the All-Union Lenin Academy of Agricultural Sciences, also signed agreements on scientific and technological research collaboration between the Ministries of Higher Education and the Academies of Agricultural Sciences of the two countries, respectively.

The protocol between China and the Soviet Union on jointly conducting and the Soviet Union assisting China in major scientific and technological research was signed after discussions with relevant Soviet parties during the visit of the Chinese scientific and technological delegation to the Soviet Union led by Guo Moruo from October 18th of last year to January 19th of this year. During the discussions, the Chinese delegation and its advisory group composed of many Chinese scientists and experts widely discussed important issues concerning the development of science and technology and further expansion of scientific and technological cooperation between China and the Soviet Union with the State Scientific and Technical Committee of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, the Soviet Academy of Sciences, the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, the Soviet Ministry of Higher Education and other important scientific research institutions. More than 600 well-known scientists and experts from the Soviet side participated in the discussions.

During the discussions, Chinese and Soviet scientists and experts exchanged views in detail in an atmosphere of friendly cooperation, and discussed specific measures to fully develop scientific and technological research cooperation between the two countries based on the principle of friendly mutual assistance. During the negotiations, the Chinese delegation especially hoped that the Soviet side would provide assistance for the important scientific research projects stipulated in China's Twelve-Year (1956-1967) Science and Technology Development Long-Range Plan. The assistance methods included sending Soviet scientists and experts to China, accepting Chinese scientific and technical personnel to study and learn in the Soviet Union, and providing necessary equipment, instruments, and materials. The above-mentioned protocol was signed as a result of the discussions.

### 中苏科学技术合作的新进展  两国关于共同进行和苏联帮助中国进行重大科学技术研究的议定书签字

1958-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏科学技术合作的新进展
    两国关于共同进行和苏联帮助中国进行重大科学技术研究的议定书签字
    新华社莫斯科18日电　中国政府和苏联政府关于共同进行和苏联帮助中国进行重大科学技术研究的议定书18日在莫斯科签字。议定书规定，中苏两国在最近五年间（1958—1962年）将共同进行一百二十二项对中国有重大意义的科学技术项目的研究工作和进一步加强两国科学机构之间的直接联系。
    代表双方在议定书上签字的是中国政府全权代表、中国科学院院长郭沫若和苏联政府全权代表、苏联部长会议国家科学技术委员会主席马克萨列夫。
    与此同时，中国高等教育部副部长黄松龄和苏联高等教育部部长叶留金、中国农业科学院院长丁颖和全苏列宁农业科学院院长洛巴诺夫，还分别签订了中苏两国高等教育部之间和两国农业科学院之间关于科学技术研究合作的协定。
    中苏两国关于共同进行和苏联帮助中国进行重大科学技术研究的议定书，是以郭沫若为首的中国访苏科学技术代表团于去年10月18日到今年1月19日访苏期间在同苏联有关方面进行讨论以后签定的。在讨论中，中国代表团和它率领的由许多中国科学家和专家组成的顾问组，曾经同苏联部长会议国家科学技术委员会、苏联科学院、苏联部长会议国家对外经济委员会、苏联高等教育部和其他重要科学研究机构广泛地讨论了发展科学技术的重要问题和进一步扩大中苏两国之间的科学技术合作的问题。苏联方面曾经有六百多名著名科学家和专家参加了讨论。
    在讨论中，中苏科学家和专家在友好合作的气氛中详尽地交换了意见，并且根据友好互助原则商谈了充分发展两国之间科学技术研究合作的具体措施。在谈判过程中，中国代表团特别希望苏联方面对实现中国十二年（1956—1967年）科学技术发展远景规划所规定的重要科学研究项目给予援助，援助方式包括派遣苏联科学家和专家到中国，接受中国科学技术人员到苏联进修和学习以及提供必要的设备、仪器和材料等。讨论结果就签定了上述议定书。
