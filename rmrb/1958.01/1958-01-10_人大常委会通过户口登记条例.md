Title: NPC Standing Committee Passes Household Registration Regulations
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The ninety-first meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress was held on the afternoon of the 9th. After hearing explanations from the responsible person of the Ministry of Public Security, the meeting discussed and approved the "Household Registration Regulations of the People's Republic of China."

### 人大常委会通过户口登记条例

1958-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会通过户口登记条例
    新华社9日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九十一次会议在9日下午举行。会议听了公安部负责人的说明后，讨论和通过了“中华人民共和国户口登记条例”。
