Title: Danube River Basin Peace Fighters Gather, Representatives from Various Countries Call for Promoting Friendship and Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1958-01-18
Page: 6

    Danube River Basin Peace Fighters Gather
    Representatives from Various Countries Call for Promoting Friendship and Cooperation
    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, January 16th. At the initiative of the Hungarian National Peace Council, representatives of peace movement organizations from Danube River Basin countries held a meeting in Budapest on the 13th and 14th. During the meeting, they exchanged information on the peace movements in their respective countries and discussed the political situation in Europe and the issue of strengthening peace cooperation.
    According to local newspaper reports, the representatives unanimously agreed that all means should be used to strengthen and promote friendship and cooperation among the Danube River Basin countries, and to further strengthen the ties between the people of these countries. In view of the great importance of exchanging information and opinions, the representatives decided to continue holding such meetings in the future and to coordinate with each other in organizing joint activities.

### 多瑙河流域的和平战士集会  各国代表要求促进友谊合作

1958-01-18
第6版()
专栏：

    多瑙河流域的和平战士集会
    各国代表要求促进友谊合作
    据新华社布达佩斯16日电　多瑙河流域国家的和平运动组织的代表在匈牙利全国和平理事会的倡议下，13日和14日两天在布达佩斯举行了会见会，在会见中交流了各国和平运动的情况并且讨论了欧洲的政治局势和加强和平合作的问题。
    据此间报纸报道，代表们一致认为，应该用一切方法加强和促进多瑙河流域各国间的友谊与合作，并且进一步加强各国人民的联系。鉴于互相交流情况和意见有很重要的意义，代表们决定今后要继续举行这种会见，同时要互相配合组织共同的活动。
