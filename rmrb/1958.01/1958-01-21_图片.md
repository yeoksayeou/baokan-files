    The Hongxing Cooperative of Sanhuangmiao Township, Linzhang County, Hebei Province, achieved a high-yield average of over 1,000 jin per mu for both wheat and corn crops on barren, sticky soil. The achievement was exhibited at the Hebei Provincial Agricultural High-Yield Exhibition, and activists from various regions came to visit and learn. Xinhua News Agency reporter Shi Panqi photographed.

    河北省临漳县三皇庙乡红星社在贫瘠的粘土地上，创造了一点二五亩小麦和玉米两季平均亩产双千斤的高额丰产成绩。河北省农业丰产展览会上展出了这个事迹，各地的积极分子都来参观和学习。　新华社记者　时盘棋摄
