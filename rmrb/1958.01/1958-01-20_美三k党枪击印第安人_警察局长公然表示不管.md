Title: American Ku Klux Klan Shoots Indians; Police Chief Publicly States He Will Not Intervene
Author:
Date: 1958-01-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Armed Ku Klux Klan members attacked local Indians in Robeson County, North Carolina, on the evening of the 18th.

Recently, Ku Klux Klan members in North Carolina have twice burned crosses in Robeson County, threatening Indians to stop demanding the abolition of segregation between Indians and whites. On the evening of the 18th, the Ku Klux Klan members even held a rally with weapons. A young Indian man protested to them, and a conflict ensued. In the conflict, the Ku Klux Klan members used tear gas in addition to firearms.

Local police openly stated that they would not intervene in the matter. The chief of a police station not many miles from the scene, Fischer, said that he would not take care of matters in that area.

### 美三K党枪击印第安人  警察局长公然表示不管

1958-01-20
第5版()
专栏：

    美三K党枪击印第安人
    警察局长公然表示不管
    新华社19日讯　美国北卡罗来纳州罗伯逊县18日晚上发生了武装三K党徒向当地印第安人进攻的事件。
    北卡罗来纳州的三K党徒最近在罗伯逊县烧过两次十字架，向印第安人进行威胁，要他们停止提出取消印第安人同白人的隔离制度的要求。18日晚上，三K党徒们竟然带着武器举行集会。一个印第安青年人向他们提出抗议，随后双方就发生了冲突。在冲突中，三K党徒除了使用枪支以外，还使用了催泪弹。
    当地警察对这件事公然表示不予过问。离出事地点没有几英里的一个警察局的局长费休说，他不管那个地段的事。
