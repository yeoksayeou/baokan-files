Title: Soviet Union and Argentina Sign Trade Contract
Author:
Date: 1958-01-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 29th. TASS reported from Moscow on the 29th that a communique was issued in Moscow regarding the visit of the Argentine trade delegation to the Soviet Union. The communique stated that during their visit to Moscow, the Argentine representatives met with leaders of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade and several foreign trade corporations. The meetings discussed the requests of Argentine trade organizations to purchase various machinery, equipment, raw materials, and industrial products from the Soviet Union.

Following negotiations, the two sides signed a contract for Argentine trade organizations to purchase Soviet goods. These goods included machinery and equipment (oil extraction equipment, machine tool equipment, road construction machinery, etc.). According to the requirements of the Argentine representatives, Soviet foreign trade organizations will also supply other machinery, equipment, and raw materials. The communique stated that the negotiations with the Argentine trade delegation demonstrated the willingness of both sides to further develop trade relations between the two countries.

The Argentine trade delegation left Moscow by plane for Prague on the 29th.

### 苏联和阿根廷签订贸易合同

1958-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和阿根廷签订贸易合同
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科29日讯：莫斯科发表了阿根廷贸易代表团访问苏联的公报。公报说，阿根廷代表在莫斯科访问期间曾经同苏联对外贸易部和一些对外贸易公司的领导人会晤。会晤时讨论了阿根廷贸易机构关于在苏联购买各种机器设备、原料以及工业品的要求。
    谈判后，双方签定了阿根廷贸易机构购买苏联商品的合同。商品中包括一些机器设备（石油开采设备、机床设备、筑路机等）。苏联对外贸易机构根据阿根廷代表的要求，还将供应其他机器设备和原料。公报说，同阿根廷贸易代表团的谈判证明，双方愿意进一步发展两国之间的贸易联系。
    阿根廷贸易代表团已经在29日乘飞机离开莫斯科前往布拉格。
