Title: The Chinese People's Volunteers in Korea Joyfully Celebrate Their Eighth New Year
Author:
Date: 1958-01-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 2nd. The Chinese People's Volunteers (CPV) stationed in Korea joyfully celebrated their eighth New Year since entering the country.

　　On New Year's Eve, the CPV leadership机关 held a celebration party. On New Year's Day, the leaders of the CPV units personally extended New Year's greetings to the responsible persons of the local Korean Party and government organs. The CPV units also universally held get-togethers with cadres and people from the local Korean Party and government organs. The CPV and Korean People's Army units, guarding the front line side by side, exchanged New Year's greetings and held get-togethers. Some units also held ball games and skating competitions. Before the New Year, many CPV units visited the families of Korean People's Army martyrs and military personnel in the stationed areas.

　　The CPV units stationed in the forward area of the 38th parallel built colorful arches in the heavy snow to welcome the New Year. The CPV units stationed in Shangganling warmly received the慰问组 sent by their superiors and organized symposiums. A certain artillery unit stationed in the Wushengshan area also commended personnel who had achieved excellent results in training throughout the year during the New Year.

### 志愿军在朝鲜欢度第八个新年

1958-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军在朝鲜欢度第八个新年
    新华社平壤2日电　驻在朝鲜的中国人民志愿军部队欢乐地度过了入朝以来的第八个新年。
    除夕，志愿军领导机关举行了庆祝晚会。元旦这一天，志愿军各部队首长都亲自向驻地朝鲜党政机关负责人祝贺新年，志愿军部队与驻地的朝鲜党政机关干部和人民群众也普遍地进行了联欢。并肩守卫在前线的志愿军与朝鲜人民军部队除相互贺年和进行联欢外，有些部队还举行了球类比赛和滑冰比赛。新年前夕，志愿军许多部队都慰问了驻地的朝鲜人民军烈士家属和军人家属。
    驻在三八线前沿地区的志愿军部队，在漫天风雪中扎了彩门，迎接新年。驻在上甘岭的志愿军部队热情地接待了上级派来的慰问组，并组织了座谈。驻守在五圣山地区的某炮兵部队还在新年表扬了一年来在训练中取得优秀成绩的人员。
