Title: Mourning the Passing of Chairman Groza
Author:
Date: 1958-01-09
Page: 4

Condolence Messages from Democratic Parties and People's Organizations in China on the Death of Dr. Petru Groza

Condolence Message from the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang to the Funeral Committee of Dr. Petru Groza:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic and Chairman of the National Committee of the Romanian People's Democratic Front!

    Dr. Petru Groza was an outstanding fighter for the cause of peace, democracy, and socialism. His death is a great loss not only for the Romanian people but also for peace-loving people all over the world. We extend our deepest condolences to you and, through you, to the entire Romanian people!

                      Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang
                          January 8, 1958, in Beijing

Condolence Message from the Central Committee of the China Democratic League to the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic and Chairman of the National Committee of the Romanian People's Democratic Front. We extend our deepest condolences to you with a heavy heart.

                      Central Committee of the China Democratic League
                          January 8, 1958, in Beijing

Condolence Message from the Central Committee of the China National Democratic Construction Association to the Funeral Committee of Dr. Petru Groza:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic. His death is a great loss not only for the Romanian people and the people of socialist countries but also for peace-loving, democratic, and socialist people all over the world. On behalf of all members of our association, we express our deepest condolences to the Romanian people!

                Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the China National Democratic Construction Association
                            January 8, 1958, in Beijing

Condolence Message from the Central Committee of the China Association for Promoting Democracy to the Funeral Committee of Dr. Petru Groza:

                    Central Committee of the China Association for Promoting Democracy

                    Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party

                      Central Committee of the China Zhi Gong Party

Condolence Message from the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League to the Funeral Committee of Dr. Petru Groza:

Condolence Message from the China Peace Committee to the Romanian Standing Committee for the Defense of Peace:

    We are saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic. With a deeply sorrowful heart, we extend our deepest condolences to you and the Romanian people.

                  Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace

Condolence Message from the Red Cross Society of China

Xinhua News Agency, January 8th: The Red Cross Society of China today sent a telegram to the Romanian Red Cross to mourn the passing of Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania.

The telegram said: "We are saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic. Please accept our deepest condolences, and please convey our sincerest sympathy to the family of Dr. Groza."

### 哀悼格罗查主席逝世

1958-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    哀悼格罗查主席逝世
    民革中央的唁电彼得鲁·格罗查博士治丧委员会：
    惊悉罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团主席、罗马尼亚人民民主阵线全国委员会主席彼·格罗查博士不幸逝世，至为悲痛！
    彼·格罗查博士是和平、民主和社会主义事业的卓越战士，他的逝世不仅是罗马尼亚人民而且是全世界爱好和平的人民重大的损失。我们谨向你们，并通过你们，向全罗马尼亚人民致以深切的哀悼！
              中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会
                          1958年1月8日于北京
    民盟中央的唁电罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团：
    惊悉罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团主席、罗马尼亚人民民主阵线全国委员会主席彼·格罗查博士不幸逝世，我们怀着沉痛的心情向你们致以深切的哀悼。
                      中国民主同盟中央委员会
                          1958年1月8日于北京
