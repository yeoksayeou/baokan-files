Title: Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference Supports Palestinian Arab Refugees; Declares Israel a Base for Imperialist Threat to the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-01-03
Page: 5

Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference
Supports Palestinian Arab Refugees
Declares Israel a Base for Imperialist Threat to the Middle East

The Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference Resolution on the Palestine Question

I. The Conference adopts the attached report on this issue submitted by the Palestinian delegation.

II. The Conference declares that the State of Israel is a base for imperialism threatening the progress and security of the Middle East, and condemns its aggressive policies, which are a threat to world peace.

III. The Conference affirms Arab rights in Palestine; the Conference expresses its sympathy for the Palestinian refugees, supports all their rights, and supports their return to their homes.

### 亚非人民团结大会  支持巴勒斯坦阿拉伯难民  宣布以色列是帝国主义威胁中东的基地

1958-01-03
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非人民团结大会
    支持巴勒斯坦阿拉伯难民
    宣布以色列是帝国主义威胁中东的基地
    亚非人民团结大会关于巴勒斯坦问题的决议
    一、大会采纳了巴勒斯坦代表团提出的关于这个问题的附带报告。
    二、大会宣布，以色列这个国家是帝国主义威胁中东进步和安全的基地，并且谴责它的侵略政策，因为这种政策是对世界和平的威胁。
    三、大会确认阿拉伯对巴勒斯坦的权利；大会对巴勒斯坦难民表示同情，支持他们的一切权利，并且支持他们回到他们的家园。
