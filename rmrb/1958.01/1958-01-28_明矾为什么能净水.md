Why Alum Can Purify Water

    In some places without waterworks, people know to use alum to purify water.
    Why does alum have this function? Because after alum is added to water, it can decompose and produce a compound called aluminum hydroxide. This aluminum hydroxide can slowly condense into very small particles and sink to the bottom of the water. When it condenses, it carries away the sludge, bacteria, and other dirty things in the water, and sinks down.　　　　　　　　　(Zhi Cheng)

### 明矾为什么能净水
志澄
1958-01-28
第8版()
专栏：为什么

    明矾为什么能净水
    在一些没有自来水厂的地方，人们都知道用明矾净水。
    明矾为什么会有这样的功用呢？因为明矾加到水中后，能够分解而产生一种叫氢氧化铝的化合物，这种氢氧化铝能够慢慢凝结成为很小的颗粒而沉到水底去。当它凝结的时候，就将水中的污泥、细菌等脏东西一起带走，而沉下去了。　　　　　　　　　（志澄）
