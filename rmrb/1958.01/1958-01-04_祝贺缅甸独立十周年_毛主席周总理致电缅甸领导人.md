Title: Congratulations on the Tenth Anniversary of Burmese Independence: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Telegrams to Burmese Leaders
Author:
Date: 1958-01-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to U Win Maung, President of the Union of Burma, on the 2nd, congratulating him on the tenth anniversary of Burmese independence. The telegram reads:

To His Excellency U Win Maung, President of the Union of Burma:

On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of your country's independence, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Burma on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Union of Burma prosperity and the Burmese people happiness. May the close friendship between the Chinese and Burmese peoples last forever. I wish Your Excellency good health.

　　　　　　　Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　January 2, 1958

Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd. Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to U Nu, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, on the 2nd, congratulating him on the tenth anniversary of Burmese independence. The telegram reads:

To His Excellency U Nu, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma:

On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of your esteemed country's independence, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the government of your esteemed country on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.

The Chinese people are pleased to see that, under the guidance of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, the traditional friendship between China and Burma has developed greatly. Mutual visits and personal contacts between the leaders of the two governments are increasing day by day, and economic and cultural ties between the two countries are growing stronger. All of this is constantly promoting mutual understanding and friendly cooperation between the two countries.

I sincerely hope that the traditional friendship between China and Burma will further grow, so that the two countries can jointly make greater contributions to safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

　　　　　　　　　Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　January 2, 1958

### 祝贺缅甸独立十周年  毛主席周总理致电缅甸领导人

1958-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺缅甸独立十周年
    毛主席周总理致电缅甸领导人
    新华社3日讯　毛泽东主席二日致电缅甸联邦吴温貌总统，祝贺缅甸独立十周年。电文如下：缅甸联邦总统吴温貌阁下：
    在贵国独立十周年的时候，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和缅甸人民表示热烈的祝贺。祝缅甸联邦繁荣和缅甸人民幸福。祝中缅两国人民间亲密的友谊永世长存。祝阁下身体健康。
                  中华人民共和国主席毛泽东
                              一九五八年一月二日
    新华社3日讯　周恩来总理二日致电缅甸联邦吴努总理祝贺缅甸独立十周年。电文如下：缅甸联邦总理吴努阁下：
    在贵国独立十周年的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和贵国政府表示热诚的祝贺。
    中国人民高兴地看到，在和平共处五项原则的指导下，中缅两国间的传统友谊已经有了巨大的发展。两国政府领导人之间的相互访问和个人接触日益增多，两国在经济和文化方面的联系日益加强，这一切都不断促进着两国间的相互了解和友好合作。
    我衷心地希望中缅两国间的传统友谊进一步增长，从而使两国对维护亚洲和世界和平共同作出更大的贡献。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
                            一九五八年一月二日
