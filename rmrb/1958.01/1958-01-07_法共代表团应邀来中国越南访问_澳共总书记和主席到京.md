Title: French Communist Party Delegation Invited to Visit China and Vietnam; Australian Communist Party General Secretary and Chairman Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-01-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the delegation of the French Communist Party arrived in Beijing by plane on the 6th to visit China and Vietnam. The head of the delegation is Jeannette Vermeersch, member of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party Central Committee, and the members are Jeannie Berlioz, member of the French Communist Party Central Committee, and Camille Vallaud, Financial Control Commissioner of the French Communist Party Central Committee.

    Those who went to the airport to welcome them were Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Tan Zhenlin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhao Yimin, Xiong Fu, and Li Qixin, responsible working staff of the organs of the CPC Central Committee.

    Nguyen Khang, member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, also welcomed them at the airport.

    The French Communist Party delegation will visit Vietnam first and then return to China for a visit.

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Laurence Sharkey, General Secretary of the Australian Communist Party, and Richard Dixon, Chairman, who participated in the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 6th on their way home.

    Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Tan Zhenlin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhao Yimin, Xiong Fu, and Li Qixin, responsible working staff of the organs of the CPC Central Committee, went to the airport to welcome them.

### 法共代表团应邀来中国越南访问  澳共总书记和主席到京

1958-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    法共代表团应邀来中国越南访问
    澳共总书记和主席到京
    新华社6日讯　应中国共产党中央、越南劳动党中央邀请前来访问中国、越南的法国共产党代表团乘飞机在6日到达北京。代表团团长是法共中央政治局委员让纳特·维美徐，团员有法共中央委员让尼·贝利奥兹和法共中央财政监察委员卡米耶·瓦兰。
    前往机场欢迎的，有中共中央总书记邓小平，中共中央书记处书记谭震林，中共中央机关负责工作人员赵毅敏、熊复、李启新等同志。
    越南劳动党中央委员阮康也到机场欢迎。
    法共代表团将先到越南访问后，再返回中国访问。
    新华社6日讯　参加十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼的澳大利亚共产党总书记劳伦斯·夏基、主席李却·狄克逊在回国途中6日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中共中央总书记邓小平、中共中央书记处书记谭震林，中共中央机关负责工作人员赵毅敏、熊复、李启新。
