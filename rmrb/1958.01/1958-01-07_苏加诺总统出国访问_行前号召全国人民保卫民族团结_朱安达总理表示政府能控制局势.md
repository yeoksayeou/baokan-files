Title: President Sukarno Visits Foreign Countries; Before Departure, He Calls on the People to Defend National Unity; Prime Minister Djuanda States That the Government Can Control the Situation
Author:
Date: 1958-01-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 6th – Indonesian President Sukarno left Jakarta by plane this morning to take a rest abroad and visit some countries.

Before boarding the plane, President Sukarno delivered a speech to the Indonesian people at the airport, saying that he hoped the people of the whole country would defend national unity and national integrity, and prevent the national security and social order from being undermined.

President Sukarno once again pointed out that Prime Minister Djuanda, the chiefs of staff of the Army, Navy, and Air Force, and the Chief of the National Police are standing with him. He said that during his vacation abroad, he would continue to work for the interests of the Indonesian people and the country.

Prime Minister Djuanda told President Sukarno at the airport that during the President's absence, the government, with the support of the armed forces, would be able to control the situation and solve some urgent problems at any time, and would continue the struggle to liberate West Irian. Prime Minister Djuanda said that President Sukarno would travel abroad for five weeks.

During President Sukarno's absence, Speaker of the Parliament Sartono will act as his deputy. This morning, a ceremony was held at the Presidential Palace to temporarily hand over the presidential duties to Sartono.

President Sukarno will first visit India, followed by Egypt, Pakistan, Ceylon, Burma, Thailand, and Japan. The sixteen members accompanying President Sukarno include Foreign Minister Subandrio, Director of the Presidential Palace Office Tansil, and Commander of the First Military District Jamin Gintings.

### 苏加诺总统出国访问  行前号召全国人民保卫民族团结  朱安达总理表示政府能控制局势

1958-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统出国访问
    行前号召全国人民保卫民族团结
    朱安达总理表示政府能控制局势
    新华社雅加达6日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺今天早上坐飞机离开雅加达到国外去休养，并且访问一些国家。
    苏加诺总统在上飞机以前，在飞机场上向印度尼西亚人民发表讲话说，他希望全国人民保卫民族团结和国家完整，并且使国家安全和社会治安不致遭到破坏。
    苏加诺总统再一次指出，朱安达总理，海、陆、空三军参谋长和国家警察总长是同他站在一起的。他说，在他出国休养期间，他仍然要为印度尼西亚人民和国家的利益而工作。
    朱安达总理在飞机场上向苏加诺总统表示，在总统出国期间，政府在武装部队的支持下，在任何时候都能够控制局势和解决一些迫切问题，并且将要继续进行争取解放西伊里安的斗争。朱安达总理说，苏加诺总统将在国外旅行五个星期。
    在苏加诺总统出国期间，国会议长沙多诺将代理他的职务。今天早上在总统府举行了把总统职务暂时移交给沙多诺的仪式。
    苏加诺总统将要首先访问印度，随后到埃及、巴基斯坦、锡兰、缅甸、泰国和日本去。在随同苏加诺总统出国的十六名人员当中包括外交部长苏班德里约、总统府办公厅主任坦西尔和第一军区司令贾明·京廷斯。
