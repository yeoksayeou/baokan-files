Title: Two More Bridges Will Appear Over the Yellow River and Yangtze River: Zhengzhou Yellow River Bridge and Chongqing Yangtze River Bridge to Commence Construction This Year
Author:
Date: 1958-01-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, January 9th: Several thousand builders who victoriously completed the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge will begin construction of the Zhengzhou Yellow River Bridge and the Chongqing Yangtze River Bridge this year.

　　The Zhengzhou Yellow River Bridge will be 2.8 kilometers long, making it one of the longest bridges in China. The Chongqing Yangtze River Bridge is the second bridge spanning the Yangtze River to commence construction after the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge.

　　In the future, the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Engineering Bureau will be responsible for the construction of large-scale bridges across the country. This year, the Engineering Bureau will also build the double-track Hutuo River Bridge, Zhang River Bridge, and Anyang River Bridge on the Shijiazhuang to Anyang section of the Beijing-Guangzhou Railway, and install new steel beams for the Songhua River Bridge, among other projects.

　　During the period of the First Five-Year Plan, the employees of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Engineering Bureau also completed the Hengyang Xiangjiang River Bridge, the Tongguan Yellow River Temporary Bridge, and completed the foundation work for the Sichuan Minjiang River Bridge.

### 黄河长江又将出现两座大桥  郑州黄河大桥和重庆长江大桥今年动工兴建

1958-01-11
第3版()
专栏：

    黄河长江又将出现两座大桥
    郑州黄河大桥和重庆长江大桥今年动工兴建
    据新华社武汉9日电　胜利建成了武汉长江大桥的几千名建设者，今年将开始兴建郑州黄河大桥和重庆长江大桥。
    郑州黄河大桥全长二点八公里，是目前我国最长的桥梁之一。重庆长江大桥是继武汉长江大桥之后，将开始兴建的跨越长江天堑的第二座大桥。
    武汉长江大桥工程局今后将负担修建全国大型桥梁的任务。今年，这个工程局还要在京广铁路石家庄到安阳段建设复线滹沱河大桥、漳河大桥、安阳河大桥，为松花江大桥架设新钢梁等工程。
    第一个五年计划期间，武汉长江大桥工程局的职工，还建成了衡阳湘江大桥、潼关黄河便桥等桥梁工程，并且完成了四川岷江大桥的基础工程。
