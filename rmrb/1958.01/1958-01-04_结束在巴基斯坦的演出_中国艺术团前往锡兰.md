Title: Chinese Art Troupe Concludes Performances in Pakistan, Heads to Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1958-01-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 3rd. The Chinese Art Troupe, led by head of delegation Ma Yuhuai, left Chittagong in East Pakistan for Ceylon today.

	Over the past month and a half, the Chinese Art Troupe performed approximately twenty-five shows in the Pakistani capital of Karachi, as well as Lahore, Peshawar, Dhaka, and Chittagong. They received a warm welcome from the governments and people in these places.

	Pakistani government leaders and newspapers have referred to the Chinese Art Troupe as “messengers of peace and friendship.”

### 结束在巴基斯坦的演出  中国艺术团前往锡兰

1958-01-04
第6版()
专栏：

    结束在巴基斯坦的演出
    中国艺术团前往锡兰
    新华社卡拉奇3日电　中国艺术团在团长马玉槐率领下今天离开东巴基斯坦的吉大港前往锡兰。
    在过去的一个半月中，中国艺术团在巴基斯坦首都卡拉奇和拉合尔、白沙瓦、达卡、吉大港等地演出了大约二十五场。他们受到了这些地方的政府和人民的热烈欢迎。
    巴基斯坦的政府领导人和报纸都把中国艺术团称为“和平和友谊的使者”。
