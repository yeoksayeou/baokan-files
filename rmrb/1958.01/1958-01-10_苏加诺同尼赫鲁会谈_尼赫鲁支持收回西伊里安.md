Title: Sukarno-Nehru Talks: Nehru Supports the Recovery of West Irian
Author:
Date: 1958-01-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Indonesian President Sukarno and Indian Prime Minister Nehru held continuous talks on the 7th and 8th, exchanging views on the international situation. According to reports, Nehru, in his talks with Sukarno, supported Indonesia's demand for the recovery of West Irian. At the same time, newspaper reports say that Nehru expressed the need to conduct peaceful negotiations with the Dutch government.

New Delhi newspapers have welcomed President Sukarno's visit and attached great importance to his talks with Prime Minister Nehru.

Indian President Prasad hosted a state banquet on the 7th in honor of Indonesian President Sukarno. In his reply at the state banquet, Sukarno said that he was confident that his visit to India and his discussions with Indian leaders "will pave the way for promoting mutual understanding and strengthening friendly relations and close ties between the peoples of the two countries."

In his welcoming speech, Prasad said: "Our two countries have mutual sympathy and understanding for each other's positions and difficulties."

Vice President Radhakrishnan, Prime Minister Nehru, cabinet members, and others attended the banquet.

### 苏加诺同尼赫鲁会谈  尼赫鲁支持收回西伊里安

1958-01-10
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺同尼赫鲁会谈
    尼赫鲁支持收回西伊里安
    据新华社讯　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺和印度总理尼赫鲁7日和8日连续举行会谈，就国际局势交换了意见。据报道，尼赫鲁在同苏加诺会谈时支持印度尼西亚收回西伊里安的要求。同时，据报纸消息说，尼赫鲁表示需要同荷兰政府和平地进行谈判。
    新德里的报纸都表示欢迎苏加诺总统的访问，并且很重视他和尼赫鲁总理的会谈。
    印度总统普拉沙德7日设国宴招待印度尼西亚总统苏加诺。苏加诺在国宴上致答词说，他确信他在印度的访问和他同印度领导人的讨论，“将为促进两国人民之间的互相了解和加强友好关系以及紧密的联系铺平道路”。
    普拉沙德在致欢迎词时说：“我们两国对于对方的立场和困难都是互相同情和了解的。”
    出席宴会的有副总统拉达克里希南、总理尼赫鲁和内阁阁员等。
