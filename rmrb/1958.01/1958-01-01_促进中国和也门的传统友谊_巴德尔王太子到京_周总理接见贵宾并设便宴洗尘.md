Title: Promoting the Traditional Friendship Between China and Yemen: Crown Prince Al-Badr Arrives in Beijing; Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Distinguished Guest and Hosts Welcome Banquet
Author:
Date: 1958-01-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 31st -- His Royal Highness Crown Prince Al-Badr, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defense of the Kingdom of Yemen, and his entourage, arrived in Beijing by special plane from Moscow this evening. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at Beijing Airport by Premier Zhou Enlai and other state leaders of our country, as well as thousands of people from the capital.

The jet carrying the distinguished guests landed at 5:20 p.m. In the dim night, dozens of searchlights and mercury lamps illuminated the airport. When Crown Prince Al-Badr appeared first at the cabin door, the welcoming crowd standing at the airport in the bitter cold of about minus ten degrees Celsius burst into prolonged and enthusiastic applause.

After the band played the national anthems of Yemen and China, Crown Prince Al-Badr, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, reviewed the guard of honor.

Crown Prince Al-Badr delivered a speech at the airport filled with the spirit of friendship between Yemen and China. Facing thousands of welcomers, he said enthusiastically that his visit to China was to show that Yemen extended its hand to the great China, requesting the restoration of our long and sincere friendship, and expressing our hope for a brighter future filled with fraternity, cooperation, and peace. Crown Prince Al-Badr also said that his visit was a symbol of the restoration of the long-standing friendship and cooperation between the peoples of Asia. Crown Prince Al-Badr said that we had successfully achieved our first goal in the struggle to liberate the people from the shackles of colonialism, and our responsibility was to continue to work together to safeguard peace. The way to safeguard peace is to restore our long-standing friendship and strengthen cooperation in economic, cultural, and technological fields.

Before Crown Prince Al-Badr's speech, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech on behalf of the Chinese government and people to welcome the distinguished guests. He said that China and Yemen had a close friendly relationship and trade in history very early. The traditional friendship between the two countries has been restored and developed under new circumstances. Premier Zhou praised Crown Prince Al-Badr's contribution to the restoration and development of friendly relations between China and Yemen in his speech, saying that the Crown Prince's "visit to China will further promote the development of friendly relations between China and Yemen, and will help strengthen the solidarity between the people of Asian and African countries."

After the speeches, Crown Prince Al-Badr, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, met with the welcoming crowd. At this time, cheers rose and fell on the vast airport, applause continued, and countless bouquets of flowers were sent to Crown Prince Al-Badr.

Also present at the airport to welcome the distinguished guests today were Vice Premiers Peng Dehuai and Chen Yi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Commission Zhang Zhizhong, Deputy Foreign Minister Zeng Yongquan, and heads of various departments of the state organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations of our country, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to China.

The distinguished guests were accompanied by Chen Chu, Minister-Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, December 31st - Premier Zhou Enlai received His Royal Highness Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defense of the Kingdom of Yemen, and his entourage at 7:30 this evening. Present at the reception were Vice Premiers Peng Dehuai and Chen Yi, Deputy Foreign Minister Zeng Yongquan, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department Ke Hua, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Ma Zhenwu.

After the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a welcome banquet for the distinguished guests. (Attached picture)

### 促进中国和也门的传统友谊  巴德尔王太子到京  周总理接见贵宾并设便宴洗尘

1958-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    促进中国和也门的传统友谊
    巴德尔王太子到京
    周总理接见贵宾并设便宴洗尘
    据新华社31日讯　也门王国副首相兼外交和国防大臣巴德尔王太子殿下及其随行人员，今天傍晚乘专机由莫斯科到达北京。贵宾们在北京机场受到了周恩来总理和我国国家的其他领导人以及首都数千群众的热烈欢迎。
    贵宾们乘坐的喷气式专机在五时二十分着陆，在一片苍茫夜色中，数十盏探照灯和水银灯照耀着机场。当巴德尔王太子第一个出现在机舱门口时，冒着摄氏零下十度左右的严寒伫立于机场上的欢迎人群中，响起了长时间的热烈掌声。
    乐队高奏也中两国国歌后，巴德尔王太子由周恩来总理陪同检阅了仪仗队。
    巴德尔王太子在机场上发表了充满也中两国友谊的讲话。他面对着数千欢迎群众热情地说，我这次访问中国，就是为了表明也门向伟大的中国伸出了手，要求恢复我们悠久而真诚的友谊，并且表示我们希望有一个充满博爱、合作与和平的更加光明的未来。巴德尔王太子还说他的访问是恢复亚洲人民之间的悠久友谊和合作的象征。巴德尔王太子说，我们在争取人民从殖民主义枷锁下解放出来的斗争中已经胜利地达到了我们的第一个目的，我们的责任就是要继续努力共同合作来维护和平。维护和平的道路就是恢复我们悠久的友谊，加强在经济、文化和技术方面的合作。
    在巴德尔王太子讲话前，周恩来总理代表我国政府和人民致词欢迎贵宾。他说，中国和也门在历史上很早就有过密切的友好关系和贸易往来。它在两国间的传统友谊又在新的情况下得到恢复和发展。周总理在讲话中赞扬了巴德尔王太子在恢复和发展中也两国友好关系方面所作的贡献，他说王太子“这次访问中国，必将更进一步促进中、也两国友好关系的发展，并且有助于亚非各国人民之间的团结”。
    讲话以后，巴德尔王太子由周恩来总理陪同和欢迎群众见面。这时，在广阔的机场上，欢呼此起彼伏，掌声连绵不断，数不清的鲜花一束又一束地送到巴德尔王太子的怀里。
    今天到机场欢迎贵宾的，还有彭德怀、陈毅副总理，李济深、黄炎培、陈叔通副委员长，国防委员会副主席张治中，外交部副部长曾涌泉，以及我国国家机关各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人和各国驻华使节。
    贵宾们由中国驻苏联大使馆公使衔参赞陈楚陪同前来。
    新华社31日讯　周恩来总理在今晚七时半接见了也门王国副首相兼外交大臣和国防大臣塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔王太子和随行人员。接见的时候在座的，有彭德怀、陈毅副总理，外交部副部长曾涌泉、西亚非洲司司长柯华、礼宾司副司长马振武。
    接见以后，周恩来总理设便宴为贵宾们洗尘。（附图片）
