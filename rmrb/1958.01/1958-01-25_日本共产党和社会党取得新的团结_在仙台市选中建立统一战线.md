Title: New Unity Achieved Between Japanese Communist Party and Socialist Party; Decision to Establish United Front in Sendai City
Author:
Date: 1958-01-25
Page: 5

    Japanese Communist Party and Socialist Party Achieve New Unity
    Decision to Establish United Front in Sendai City
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, January 23rd (TASS): The slogan put forward by the Japanese Communist Party, "The unity of democratic forces is the guarantee of our victory," is gaining increasing support among members of the Socialist Party and various trade unions in Japan.
    According to the "Akahata" (Red Flag) newspaper, the Communist Party and the Socialist Party held a joint meeting a few days ago. Representatives from various trade unions in Sendai City were also invited to participate in the meeting. The two parties decided to conclude a political agreement on the issue of establishing a united front in the Sendai City mayoral election to be held in February, and have nominated Socialist Party member Shimano as the mayoral candidate. The united front will put forward the slogans of defending peace, independence, and democracy as its campaign platform.

### 日本共产党和社会党取得新的团结  在仙台市选中建立统一战线

1958-01-25
第5版()
专栏：

    日本共产党和社会党取得新的团结
    在仙台市选中建立统一战线
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社东京23日讯：日本共产党提出的“民主力量团结一致是我们胜利的保证”的号召，在社会党人和日本各业工会会员中得到越来越多的人的拥护。
    据“赤旗报”报道，前几天，共产党和社会党举行了一次联席会议。仙台市各业工会的代表们也被邀请参加了这次会议。两党决定就在2月举行的仙台市市长选举中建立统一战线问题缔结政治协定，并且已经提名社会党员岛野为市长候选人。统一战线将提出保卫和平、独立和民主为竞选口号。
