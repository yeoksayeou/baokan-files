Title: Ollenhauer Calls for East-West Negotiations, Appeals for End to Atomic Arms Race
Author:
Date: 1958-01-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, December 30th: The West German Social Democratic Party's organ, "Social Democratic News Bulletin," published a New Year's article by party chairman Erich Ollenhauer, calling on all countries to stop the atomic arms race in 1958.

Ollenhauer wrote, "Continuing the arms race, especially the atomic arms race, will not only exacerbate international tensions but also eliminate the possibility of effectively limiting, supervising, and stopping this arms race."

Ollenhauer pointed out, "It is still possible to use political methods to seek various methods and avenues in East-West negotiations to mediate contradictions and open an era of easing tensions." He suggested that East and West begin negotiations, believing that the purpose of the negotiations should be to "study whether the Soviet proposals put forward in the Bulganin letters or other proposals can lead to preliminary measures for supervised disarmament, agreements on implementing a policy of easing international tensions, and on resolving other issues." Ollenhauer emphasized: "Only in this way will it be possible to pave the way for the reunification of Germany."

Ollenhauer finally emphasized: "The policy of strength places the people of all countries in a desperate situation and greatly increases the danger of endangering world peace."

### 奥伦豪尔要求东西方谈判  呼吁停止原子军备竞赛

1958-01-01
第5版()
专栏：

    奥伦豪尔要求东西方谈判
    呼吁停止原子军备竞赛
    据新华社31日讯塔斯社波恩30日讯：西德社会民主党机关刊物“社会民主新闻公报”发表了党主席奥伦豪尔的新年文章，要求各国在1958年停止原子军备竞赛。
    奥伦豪尔写道，“继续进行军备竞赛，尤其是原子军备竞赛，不仅会加剧国际紧张局势，而且会消灭有效地限制、监督和停止这种军备的可能性。”
    奥伦豪尔指出，“目前仍然有可能使用政治方法，在东西方的谈判中谋求种种方法和途径，以求调解矛盾，打开一个缓和紧张局势的时代。”他建议东西方开始进行谈判，他认为谈判的目的应该是“研究布尔加宁信件中所提出的苏联建议或者其他建议是否能够导致关于实行有监督的裁军的初步措施、关于执行缓和国际紧张局势的政策和关于解决其他问题的协议。”奥伦豪尔强调指出：“只有这样，才有可能也为恢复德国统一开辟道路。”
    奥伦豪尔最后强调指出：“实力地位政策使各国人民陷于绝境，可怕地增加了危及全世界和平的危险。”
