Title: Chinese Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union Returns Home; Guo Moruo's Speech Emphasizes the Significance of Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1958-01-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 20th. The Chinese Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union left Moscow by plane on the evening of the 19th to return home. Before leaving Moscow, the head of the delegation, Guo Moruo, gave a speech to a TASS reporter.

Guo Moruo said that without a doubt, the signing of the protocol between the Chinese government and the Soviet government on jointly conducting major scientific and technological research and the Soviet Union's assistance to China in conducting such research will contribute not only to China but also to the world's science and technology. In the future, Sino-Soviet scientific and technological cooperation will be developed on the basis of comprehensively formulated plans covering all the most important sectors, and the two countries will work together to solve many problems in physics, chemistry, dynamics, metallurgy, machine manufacturing, biology, and medicine. These issues are of particular significance to China. Guo Moruo also spoke about China's task of catching up with the industrial level of Great Britain within the next fifteen years, as well as the rapid development of science, especially agricultural science.

On the 19th, Delegation Head Guo Moruo held a farewell reception. The Soviet side attending the reception included Maksarev, Chairman of the State Scientific and Technical Committee of the Council of Ministers, Fedelin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, leaders of various ministries, and prominent scientists. The Chinese side included Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and members of the Chinese Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union.

On the 18th, Maksarev hosted a banquet in honor of the Chinese Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union.

### 中国访苏科学技术代表团回国  郭沫若谈话强调中苏科学技术合作的意义

1958-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    中国访苏科学技术代表团回国
    郭沫若谈话强调中苏科学技术合作的意义
    新华社莫斯科20日电　中国访苏科学技术代表团19日晚间乘飞机离开莫斯科回国。在离开莫斯科以前，代表团团长郭沫若对塔斯社记者发表了谈话。
    郭沫若说，毫无疑问，中国政府和苏联政府关于共同进行和苏联帮助中国进行重大科学技术研究的议定书的签订，不仅对中国而且对世界的科学和技术都将作出贡献。今后中苏科学技术合作将在全面制订的包括一切最重要部门的计划的基础上得到发展，两国将共同努力解决物理、化学、动力学、冶金学、机器制造、生物学和医学方面的许多问题。这些问题对中国具有特殊的意义。郭沫若还谈到中国要在今后十五年内赶上英国工业水平的任务，以及迅速发展科学、特别是农业科学的问题。
    郭沫若团长19日曾经举行了告别招待会。苏联方面出席招待会的有部长会议国家科学技术委员会主席马克萨列夫、外交部副部长费德林、各部领导人、著名科学家等。中国方面出席的有中国驻苏联大使刘晓、中国访苏科学技术代表团团员。
    18日，马克萨列夫设宴招待了中国访苏科学技术代表团。
