Title: Sukarno and Nasser Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1958-01-15
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 13th: Indonesian President Sukarno and Egyptian President Nasser held a two-hour meeting on the morning of the 13th. This was their first meeting.
　　It is reported that they discussed the basic problems facing Asian and African countries, as well as the recent developments in the Middle East and West Irian situations. The Cairo "Evening News" said that they also discussed the issue of strengthening economic and trade relations between Egypt and Indonesia.
　　Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio announced on the 12th that President Sukarno is scheduled to go to Belgrade, the capital of Yugoslavia, on January 17th to meet with President Tito. After a two-day stay in Yugoslavia, he will travel to Syria for a visit.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 14th: Acting Secretary-General of the Indonesian Foreign Ministry, Suwito, said today that the talks between Indonesian President Sukarno, Egyptian President Nasser, and Indian Prime Minister Nehru are important. He said that these countries can connect with each other in implementing their respective independent policies, thereby contributing to world peace. These talks can also enable the countries concerned to understand the Indonesian government and people's struggle for more complete liberation.

### 苏加诺和纳赛尔会谈

1958-01-15
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺和纳赛尔会谈
    据新华社开罗13日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺和埃及总统纳赛尔在13日上午举行了两小时的会谈。这是他们的第一次会谈。
    据报道，他们讨论了亚非国家所面临的基本问题，以及中东和西伊里安的局势最近的发展。开罗“晚报”说，他们还讨论了关于加强埃及和印度尼西亚之间的经济和贸易关系问题。
    印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约在12日宣布，苏加诺总统预定在1月17日前往南斯拉夫首都贝尔格莱德会见铁托总统。在南斯拉夫停留两天以后他将前往叙利亚访问。
    据新华社雅加达14日电　印度尼西亚外交部代理秘书长苏维托今天说，印度尼西亚总统苏加诺同埃及总统纳赛尔和印度总理尼赫鲁的会谈是重要的。他说，这些国家可以在执行它们各自的独立政策中互相联系，从而对世界和平作出贡献。这些会谈，也可以使有关的国家了解印度尼西亚政府和人民为争取更加彻底的解放而进行的斗争。
