    Brief News

    Soviet-Ceylon Hold Cultural Cooperation Talks
    Colombo dispatch: Talks between the Soviet Union and Ceylon on the signing of a cultural agreement began in Colombo on the 6th. Subsequently, Ceylonese Prime Minister Bandaranaike received the Soviet delegation. During the conversation, they exchanged views on developing cultural ties between the two countries.

      Poland and Albania Sign Trade Agreement
    Warsaw dispatch: Poland and Albania signed a trade and payments agreement for the period 1958-1960, an agreement on the supply of industrial equipment, and a trade protocol for 1958 in Warsaw on the 13th.

        Guo Moruo Arrives in Moscow
    Moscow dispatch: Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, arrived in Moscow on the 13th. Guo Moruo is the head of the Chinese Science and Technology Delegation currently visiting the Soviet Union. He will discuss with the State Science and Technology Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Academy of Sciences the issue of strengthening cooperation between China and the Soviet Union in scientific and technological research.

专栏：简讯

    简讯
    苏锡举行文化合作会谈
    科伦坡讯：苏联和锡兰之间关于签订文化协定的会谈6日在科伦坡开始。接着，锡兰总理班达拉奈克接见了苏联代表团。他们在谈话中交换了关于发展两国之间的文化联系的意见。
      波阿签定贸易协定
    华沙讯：波兰和阿尔巴尼亚13日在华沙签定了两国1958—1960年期间贸易和支付协定、供应工业设备协定和1958年贸易议定书。
        郭沫若到莫斯科
    莫斯科讯：中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若13日到达莫斯科。郭沫若是目前正在苏联访问的中国访苏科学技术代表团的团长。他将同苏联部长会议国家科学技术委员会和苏联科学院商谈加强中苏在科学技术研究方面的合作的问题。
