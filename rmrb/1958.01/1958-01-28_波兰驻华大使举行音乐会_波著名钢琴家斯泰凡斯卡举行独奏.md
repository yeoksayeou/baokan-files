Title: Polish Ambassador to China Holds Concert; Renowned Polish Pianist Stefanska Gives Recital
Author:
Date: 1958-01-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The Polish Ambassador to China, Kirylok, and his wife held a concert in Beijing on the evening of the 27th, featuring a piano recital by Helena Czerny-Stefańska, a Polish female pianist, first-prize winner of the Fourth International Chopin Piano Competition, and recipient of the Polish First Class National Art Award.

Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, Li Jishen and Guo Moruo, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, Luo Guibo, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Minister of Culture, and diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries in China were invited to attend the concert.

Approximately one thousand people were invited to the concert.

Czerny-Stefańska performed eleven pieces by Chopin, Beethoven, and others at the concert tonight, lasting over two hours. Each piece she performed was met with enthusiastic applause from the audience. After the concert, Chen Yi, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Zhang Zhizhong, Zheng Zhenduo, and others went on stage to congratulate her on her successful performance.

Czerny-Stefańska had previously been invited to visit China in November and December of last year. This time, she was passing through Beijing on her way back to Poland after visiting and performing in Japan. She will leave Beijing tomorrow to return to her country.

### 波兰驻华大使举行音乐会  波著名钢琴家斯泰凡斯卡举行独奏

1958-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰驻华大使举行音乐会
    波著名钢琴家斯泰凡斯卡举行独奏
    新华社27日讯　波兰驻中国大使基里洛克和夫人27日晚在北京举行音乐会，由波兰女钢琴家、第四届国际萧邦钢琴比赛会一等奖及波兰一级国家艺术奖获得者海伦娜·采尔尼—斯泰凡斯卡举行了钢琴独奏。
    国务院副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、郭沫若，政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通，国防委员会副主席张治中，外交部副部长罗贵波，文化部副部长郑振铎和各国驻中国的外交使节和外交官员们应邀出席了音乐会。
    应邀出席音乐会的约一千人。
    采尔尼—斯泰凡斯卡今晚在音乐会上独奏了萧邦、贝多芬等人的十一个曲子，共历时两个多钟头。她独奏的每个曲子都博得了听众的热烈掌声。当音乐会结束后，陈毅、李济深、郭沫若、陈叔通、张治中、郑振铎等人都到舞台上祝贺她演奏成功。
    采尔尼—斯泰凡斯卡去年11、12月间曾经应邀来中国访问过。这次她是到日本访问演出后回国途中路过北京的。她将在明天离开北京回国。
