Title: Soviet-Hungarian Academies of Sciences Sign Scientific Cooperation Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency Report:

    After a series of talks, the delegations of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences and the Soviet Academy of Sciences signed a three-year scientific cooperation agreement in Moscow on the 18th. The 1957 scientific cooperation plan between the two academies has been completed. The new cooperation plan stipulates that both parties will jointly study the most important scientific issues. For example, issues such as cosmic rays, the use of high-voltage commercial frequency alternating current to electrify transportation, and a series of issues concerning the economic development of people's democratic countries.

### 苏匈两国科学院签订科学合作协定

1958-01-23
第6版()
专栏：

    苏匈两国科学院签订科学合作协定
    据新华社讯　匈牙利科学院和苏联科学院双方代表团在进行了一系列会谈后，18日在莫斯科签订了三年科学合作协定。苏匈两国科学院1957年的科学合作计划已经完成。新的合作计划规定双方要共同研究最重要的科学问题。例如，宇宙线问题，用高压商用频率数交流电来使运输业电气化等问题，关于人民民主国家经济发展方面的一系列问题。
