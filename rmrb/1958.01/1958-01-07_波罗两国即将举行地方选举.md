Title: Local Elections to be Held Soon in Poland and Romania
Author:
Date: 1958-01-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 6th: The Polish Press Agency reports that the plenary meeting of the All-Poland Committee of the National Unity Front was held in Warsaw on the 4th. The meeting adopted a letter to the people of the whole country regarding the upcoming elections to the local People's Councils to be held on February 2nd.

    Gomulka, Zawadzki, Cyrankiewicz, and representatives of various democratic parties and mass organizations attended the meeting.

    While speaking at the meeting, the Chairman of the All-Poland Committee of the National Unity Front, Zawadzki, pointed out that it is necessary to consolidate the unity of the whole people, especially at present during the preparation for the local People's Council elections, to further consolidate Poland's people's democratic system. He said that the election campaign shows that certain right-wing elements and groups in society are taking advantage of the general difficulties Poland is still experiencing, and are doing their best to use the election campaign to carry out activities that are detrimental to national unity. Therefore, the National Unity Front and its affiliated organizations and activists must oppose such harmful activities.

    Zawadzki also said that the committees of the National United Front at all levels should become centers for the active development and consolidation of Polish-Soviet friendship and friendship with all socialist countries.

    The meeting, in a letter to the people of the whole country, called on the Polish people to actively participate in the election campaign, support the election program put forward by the National Unity Front, and vote for the candidates of the National Unity Front.

    The meeting also elected new leading members of the All-Poland Committee of the National Unity Front. Zawadzki continued to be elected as Chairman of the Presidium of the Committee.

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, January 6th: According to local newspapers, Romania has already begun preparations for the elections of representatives to the People's Councils of provinces, counties, cities, and townships.

    According to the order of the Presidium of the Great National Assembly of Romania, the election of representatives to the People's Councils will begin nationwide on March 2nd. The People's Council is the local organ of political power in Romania. Each term of office is two years. The previous election was held on March 11, 1956.

### 波罗两国即将举行地方选举

1958-01-07
第6版()
专栏：

    波罗两国即将举行地方选举
    新华社华沙6日电　据波兰通讯社报道，波兰全国阵线全波委员会会议4日在华沙举行。会议就即将在2月2日举行的地方人民会议的选举通过了告全国人民书。
    哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹和各民主党派、群众团体的代表出席了这次会议。
    全国阵线全波委员会主席萨瓦茨基在会上讲话时指出，必须巩固全民的团结，特别是目前在准备地方人民会议选举期间更必须巩固波兰的人民民主制度。他说，竞选运动说明，社会上某些右派分子和集团正在乘波兰还在经受普遍困难的机会，竭力利用竞选运动进行不利于达到全国团结的工作。因此，全国阵线及其所属组织和活动分子必须反对这种有害的活动。
    萨瓦茨基还说，全国统一阵线的各级委员会应当成为社会上积极发展和巩固波苏友谊和同一切社会主义国家友好的中心。
    会议在告全国人民书中号召波兰人民积极参加选举运动，支持全国阵线所提出的选举纲领，并且投票选举全国阵线的候选人。
    会议还选出了全国阵线全波委员会的新领导成员。萨瓦茨基继续当选为委员会的主席团主席。
    新华社布加勒斯特6日电　据此间报纸报道，罗马尼亚全国已经展开州、县、市、乡人民会议代表的选举筹备工作。
    根据罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团的命令，全国将在3月2日开始选举人民会议的代表。人民会议是罗马尼亚的地方政权机关。每届任期两年。上一届选举是在1956年3月11日举行的。
