Title: Maurer Elected Chairman of the Presidium of the Romanian Grand National Assembly; Chairman Mao Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1958-01-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 12th: Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to congratulate Ion Gheorghe Maurer on his election as Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic. The telegram reads as follows:

Comrade Ion Gheorghe Maurer, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic:

I am delighted to learn that you have gloriously gained the great trust of the Romanian people and been elected as Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic. On behalf of the Chinese people and in my personal capacity, I extend to you my heartfelt congratulations.

The people of China and Romania have forged a close and unbreakable friendship on the common path of socialism. This friendship has made and will continue to make significant contributions to strengthening the solidarity of socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union and safeguarding world peace. I wish you outstanding achievements in the cause of the happiness of the Romanian people and socialist construction.

					Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, January 12, 1958

### 毛雷尔当选罗大国民议会主席团主席  毛主席致电祝贺
毛泽东
1958-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    毛雷尔当选罗大国民议会主席团主席
    毛主席致电祝贺
    新华社12日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电祝贺扬·格·毛雷尔当选为罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团主席，电文如下：
    罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团主席扬·格·毛雷尔同志：
    欣悉你光荣地获得罗马尼亚人民的重大信任，当选为罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团主席，我代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，向你表示衷心的祝贺。
    中罗两国人民在共同的社会主义道路上，已经结成了亲密的、牢不可破的友谊。这种友谊对于加强以苏联为首的社会主义各国的团结和维护世界和平作出了并且将继续作出重大的贡献。祝你在为罗马尼亚人民的幸福和社会主义建设的事业中取得卓越的成就。
                    中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1958年1月12日
