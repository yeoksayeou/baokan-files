Title: Exhibits from the Third Czechoslovak Brno Industrial Exhibition to be Displayed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-01-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th — Product samples from the Third Czechoslovak Brno Industrial Exhibition will be displayed at No. 9 Chongnei Street, Beijing, from January 17th to February 7th.

This exhibition is organized by the Commercial Office of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China. The Commercial Office held a reception on the morning of the 16th, where Commercial Counselor Ševčík introduced the sample exhibition to reporters.

The exhibition will feature samples of more than four thousand products from the Czechoslovak machine-building industry, of which more than five hundred are newly designed products, fully reflecting the high technical level of Czechoslovak industry.

### 捷第三次布尔诺工业展览会  展品样本将在北京展出

1958-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

    捷第三次布尔诺工业展览会
    展品样本将在北京展出
    据新华社16日讯　捷克斯洛伐克第三次布尔诺工业展览会展出的产品样本，1月17日到2月7日将在北京崇内大街九号举行展览。
    这次展览是由捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使馆商务处举办的。商务处在16日上午举行招待会，由商务参赞舍伏契克向记者们介绍了这次样品展览的情况。
    这次将展出捷克斯洛伐克机器制造业四千多种产品的样本，其中五百多种是新设计的产品，充分反映了捷克斯洛伐克工业的高度技术水平。
