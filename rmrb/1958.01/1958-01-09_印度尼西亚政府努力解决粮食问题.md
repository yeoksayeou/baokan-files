Title: Indonesian Government Efforts to Solve Food Problem
Author:
Date: 1958-01-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 8th: The Indonesian cabinet discussed ways to solve the food problem on the 7th, and decided to allocate 76 million盾 as expenses for expanding grain production this year. This funding will be primarily used for the production of rice and corn.

Indonesia will be short of about one million tons of rice this year. The government is making great efforts to order rice from foreign countries. Egypt, Vietnam, China, Thailand, and Italy have agreed to sell rice to Indonesia.

### 印度尼西亚政府努力解决粮食问题

1958-01-09
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府
    努力解决粮食问题
    据新华社雅加达8日电　印度尼西亚内阁在7日讨论了解决粮食问题的办法，并且决定拨出七千六百万盾作为今年扩大谷物生产的费用，这笔经费将首先用于大米和玉蜀黍的生产上。
    今年印度尼西亚将缺少大米一百万吨左右，政府正在大力设法从外国订购大米，埃及、越南、中国、泰国和意大利等国家已经同意出售大米给印度尼西亚。
