Author: Luo Ling
Date: 1958-01-18
Page: 8

    Geographical Crossword Puzzle
    In the pattern, every five white squares form a "cross". There are five "crosses" in total. Please fill in the blanks of each horizontal and vertical row with the following ten geographical names (each row has three spaces), but note that the center of the five "crosses" must be the same word (the names can be arranged in any order, not necessarily in the following order):
    1. The name of the largest river in Europe in the Soviet Union.
    2. The name of the place where the treaty was signed after the First World War.
    3. The name of the place where the Olympic Games were held.
    4. The name of a European republic mainly engaged in animal husbandry.
    5. The capital of a neutral European country.
    6. An important commercial port in France.
    7. A mountainous area in western Britain.
    8. A peninsula along the Mediterranean Sea.
    9. A river in Canada.
    10. A city in the European part of the Soviet Union.
    The answer will be in tomorrow's newspaper.
        Luo Ling

### 地理填字游戏
罗凌
1958-01-18
第8版()
专栏：

    地理填字游戏
    图案中每五个白方格组成一个“十”字。共有五个“十”字。请你用下列十个地理名字填在每一横行和直行的空格中（每行都是三个空格），但要注意五个“十”字的中心都必须是同一个字才行（各个名字可以任意排列不须按下列次序）：
    1、苏联在欧洲最大的河流名。
    2、第一次世界大战之后签订和约的地方名。
    3、曾经举行过世界奥林匹克运动大会的地方名。
    4、欧洲一个以畜牧业为主的共和国名。
    5、欧洲一个中立国家的首都名。
    6、法国的一个重要商港名。
    7、英国西部的一个山地区域名。
    8、沿地中海的一个半岛名。
    9、加拿大的一条河流名。
    10、苏联欧洲部分的一个城市名。
    答案见明日本报。
        罗凌
