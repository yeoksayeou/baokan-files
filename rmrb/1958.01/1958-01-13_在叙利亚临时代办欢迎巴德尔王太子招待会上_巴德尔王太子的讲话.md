Title: Prince Badr's Speech at the Syrian Chargé d'Affaires' Reception Welcoming Crown Prince Badr
Author:
Date: 1958-01-13
Page: 4

Prince Badr's Speech
Dear Chargé d'Affaires Shalan,
Dear Premier Zhou Enlai,

I am very pleased to express my gratitude for the friendly reception of the Chargé d'Affaires, and I thank you for giving us this good opportunity to gather with these distinguished guests. Today, when the brotherly country of Syria is resisting the conspiracy of colonialist aggression, such a gathering is exciting.

I congratulate the Syrian people and government on their achievements in crushing the colonialists' conspiracy, and I also congratulate the people of Arab countries and Asian-African countries, who are united in their firm support for Syria's struggle.

I am very pleased to see that China is paying close attention to the situation in the Middle East, especially the situation in Syria, with great concern. Premier Zhou Enlai's speeches at the Egyptian Embassy banquet and at tonight's reception fully illustrate this concern, and further illustrate China's correct stance, demonstrating how firmly China stands on the side of the Arab people, supporting their struggle.

I am very happy to express my best thanks to the Chinese people and to my friend Premier Zhou Enlai in the name of the Arabs for the Premier's remarks, which are filled with sincere feelings and determination to support the Arab people's struggle against imperialism.

Here, I take this opportunity to pay tribute to the brotherly Syrian people, to the respected Syrian government, and to the wise and courageous Syrian President. I pray that Allah will grant Syria lasting happiness and glory. Finally, I propose:

To the health of His Excellency Shukri al-Kuwatli, President of the Syrian Republic,
To the health of Chairman Mao Zedong,
To the health of President Nasser,
To the health of my friend Premier Zhou Enlai,
To the health of the Syrian Chargé d'Affaires,
To the health of all friends present,
To the friendship between the Chinese people and the people of the Arab countries, cheers!

### 在叙利亚临时代办欢迎巴德尔王太子招待会上  巴德尔王太子的讲话

1958-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    在叙利亚临时代办欢迎巴德尔王太子招待会上
    巴德尔王太子的讲话亲爱的沙蓝代办阁下亲爱的周恩来总理阁下：
    我很高兴地对代办阁下的友好的接待表示谢意，并感谢阁下给了我们这个良好的机会，和这些贵宾们欢聚一堂，在兄弟国家叙利亚正在反抗殖民主义侵略阴谋的今天，这样的聚会是令人兴奋的。
    我祝贺叙利亚人民和政府在粉碎殖民主义者的阴谋方面所取得的成就，同时也祝贺阿拉伯各国和亚非各国人民，他们团结一致地坚决支持了叙利亚的斗争。
    我非常愉快地看到中国正以极大的关怀注视着中东局势，特别是叙利亚局势。周恩来总理在埃及大使馆宴会上及今晚招待会上的讲话，充分地说明了这种关怀的心情，更加说明了中国所采取的正确立场，说明了中国是怎样坚决地站在阿拉伯人民方面，支持他们的斗争。
    我很高兴以阿拉伯人的名义向中国人民，向我的朋友周恩来总理表示最美好的谢意，感谢总理的谈话。他的谈话里洋溢着真挚的感情和支持阿拉伯人进行的反对帝国主义斗争的决心。
    在这里，我借这个机会，向兄弟般的叙利亚人民，向尊敬的叙利亚政府和英明勇敢的叙利亚总统致敬。祈求真主赐给叙利亚永久的幸福和光荣。最后，我建议：
    为叙利亚共和国总统舒克里·库阿特利阁下的健康，
    为毛泽东主席阁下的健康，
    为纳赛尔总统阁下的健康，
    为我的朋友周恩来总理阁下的健康，
    为叙利亚代办阁下的健康，
    为在座的各位朋友的健康，
    为了中国人民和阿拉伯各国人民间的友谊，干杯！
