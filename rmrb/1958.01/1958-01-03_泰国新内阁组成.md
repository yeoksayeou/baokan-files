Title: Thailand's New Cabinet Formed
Author:
Date: 1958-01-03
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, January 2nd: It is reported that the King of Thailand announced on the evening of January 1st the appointment of former Minister of Defense and Deputy Leader of the National Socialist Party, Nai Thanom Kittikachorn, as Prime Minister of the Cabinet. Thai radio announced the list of the new cabinet, many of whose members were from the interim cabinet of Nai Pote Sarasin. There are three Deputy Prime Ministers in the new Thai cabinet: Lieutenant General Nai Praphas Charusathien, Nai Sukich Nimmanhaeminda, and Prince Wan Waithayakon (i.e., Naradhip).

### 泰国新内阁组成

1958-01-03
第6版()
专栏：

    泰国新内阁组成
    据新华社2日讯　曼谷消息：泰国国王已经在1月1日晚上宣布任命前国防部长、国家社会党副领袖乃他侬·吉滴卡宗为内阁总理。泰国电台宣布了新内阁的名单，新内阁的成员中有许多是乃朴·沙拉信临时内阁的成员。泰国新内阁中有三位副总理：乃巴博·乍鲁沙天中将、乃素吉·林曼夏明和旺·威泰耶康亲王（即那拉底）。
