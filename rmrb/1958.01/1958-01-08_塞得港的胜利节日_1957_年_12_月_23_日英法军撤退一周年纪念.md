Title: Port Said Victory Day – First Anniversary of the Anglo-French Withdrawal on December 23, 1957
Author: Zhao Puchu
Date: 1958-01-08
Page: 8

    Port Said Victory Day
    —Commemorating the First Anniversary of the Anglo-French Withdrawal on December 23, 1957
    Zhao Puchu
    The Suez Canal is as clear as glass,
    The sun leaps into the riverbed,
    Transforming into five-colored shimmering light.
    A convoy of cars speeds along the bank,
    Unable to contain their excitement, everyone rushes towards Port Said;
    The radio broadcasts booming cannon fire,
    Reporting that President Nasser has arrived,
    A sea-like cheer, thunderous applause,
    Tens of thousands of people are rejoicing wildly.
    Hurry, hurry, hurry to catch up,
    The cars speed forward at a hundred miles an hour.
    Wreaths are laid before the graves of the fallen,
    The five-thousand-year-old national spirit summons the sons and daughters of Egypt,
    Children take up rifles,
    Old people take up trench shovels,
    Heroic struggle overcomes a century of hardship,
    The pyramids finally reappear in dazzling brilliance,
    The Sphinx finally emits a dignified roar of anger,
    The Anglo-French invading forces finally, on this day last year,
    Scuttled away with their tails between their legs.
    The Suez Canal has regained its national sovereignty,
    Colonialists will never return.
    The honorable city flag rises high,
    A clear bugle call rings out on the parade ground,
    Squadrons of MiG fighters fly through the sky,
    Truckloads of rocket launchers drive past the reviewing stand,
    Combat heroes run in the cheers,
    Ancient Egypt is rejuvenated,
    The torches in the hands of young people are like a thousand streaks of colored clouds.
    Dear friends,
    We extend our greetings on behalf of 600 million Chinese people,
    We are proud of your victory,
    Our hearts are forever with you.
    The solidarity of the Asian and African people is providing a strong guarantee,
    For national independence, for world peace, for international friendship.

### 塞得港的胜利节日——1957年12月23日英法军撤退一周年纪念
赵朴初
1958-01-08
第8版()
专栏：

    塞得港的胜利节日
    ——1957年12月23日英法军撤退一周年纪念
    赵朴初
    苏伊士河明净得像玻璃一样，
    太阳跳进了河床，
    化作五色的波光。
    一队小汽车飞驰岸上，
    按不住兴奋的心，大家奔向塞得港；
    收音机传出了隆隆的炮响，
    报道纳赛尔总统已经到场，
    海潮似的欢呼，春雷般的鼓掌，
    千万群众正在欣喜如狂。
    快呀，快呀，快快地赶上，
    汽车以一百哩速度赶向前方。
    战死者墓前敬献着花圈，
    五千年国魂向着埃及儿女们召唤，
    儿童们拿起了枪杆，
    老人们拿起了掘壕铲，
    英勇的奋斗战胜了百年的苦难，
    金字塔终于再现出光辉灿烂，
    斯芬克斯终于再发出尊严的怒喊，
    英法侵略军终于在去年的今天，
    夹着尾巴滚蛋。
    苏伊士河恢复了民族的主权，
    殖民主义者从此一去不复返。
    荣誉的市旗升起高高，
    检阅场吹起了响亮的军号，
    天空中飞过来一队队米格机，
    看台前开过来一车车火箭炮，
    战斗英雄们在欢呼中奔跑，
    古老的埃及年青起来了，
    青年们手里的火炬像霞彩千条。
    亲爱的朋友们，
    我们代表六万万中国人问好，
    我们为你们的胜利而感到自豪，
    我们的心永远和你们在一道。
    亚非人民的团结正在作着有力的担保，
    为民族独立、为世界和平、为国际友好。
