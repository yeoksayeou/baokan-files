Title: Bulgarian New National Assembly Elects Leadership Organs; Damyanov Elected Chairman of the Presidium; Yugov Re-elected Chairman of the Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1958-01-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, January 13th: The third Bulgarian National Assembly, newly elected on December 22nd of last year, held its first special session on the afternoon of the 13th.

    The session elected the Presidium and Executive Committee of the National Assembly. The Presidium consists of nineteen members. Georgi Damyanov was elected Chairman, and F. Kozovski was elected Chairman of the Executive Committee. Todor Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, was elected as a member of the Presidium of the National Assembly.

    Based on the proposal made by Zhivkov on behalf of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Bulgarian Agrarian National Union's members of parliament, the session unanimously elected Anton Yugov as Chairman of the Council of Ministers and entrusted him with organizing the government.

### 保新国民议会选出领导机构  达米扬诺夫当选主席团主席  于哥夫连任部长会议主席

1958-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    保新国民议会选出领导机构
    达米扬诺夫当选主席团主席
    于哥夫连任部长会议主席
    新华社索非亚13日电　去年12月22日新选出的保加利亚第三届国民议会在13日下午举行了第一次特别会议。
    会议选出了这届国民议会的主席团和执行委员会。主席团由十九人组成。主席为格·达米扬诺夫，执行委员会主席为费·科佐夫斯基。保共中央第一书记托·日夫科夫当选为国民议会主席团委员。
    会议根据日夫科夫代表保加利亚共产党和农民联盟的议会议员提出的建议，一致选举安·于哥夫为部长会议主席，并委托他组织政府。
