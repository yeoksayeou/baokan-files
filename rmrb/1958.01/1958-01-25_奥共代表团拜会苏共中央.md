Title: Austrian Communist Party Delegation Visits the CPSU Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1958-01-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 23rd: According to TASS, the delegation of the Austrian Communist Party, which is currently visiting the Soviet Union, paid a visit to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on the 22nd.

Receiving the delegation were Suslov, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Committee, Ponomarev, member of the Central Committee, and others.

The delegation arrived in the Soviet Union on the 4th, headed by Lauscher, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Austrian Communist Party. They are in the Soviet Union to learn about the living conditions of the Soviet people and the work of Soviet organizations, trade unions, and other social groups.

### 奥共代表团拜会苏共中央

1958-01-25
第6版()
专栏：

    奥共代表团拜会苏共中央
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科23日讯：正在苏联访问的奥地利共产党代表团22日拜会了苏联共产党中央委员会。
    接待代表团的有苏共中央委员会主席团委员、中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫，中央委员波诺马烈夫等。
    代表团是4日到达苏联的，团长是奥地利共产党中央委员会政治局委员劳歇尔。他们是来苏联了解苏联人民的生活情况，以及苏维埃组织、工会等社会团体的工作的。
