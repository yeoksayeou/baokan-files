Title: Yunnan Building Underground Hydroelectric Power Station
Author:
Date: 1958-01-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 7th. Yunnan Province has begun construction on the Liulangdong Hydroelectric Power Station, which generates electricity using underground water. Once completed, the power station's electricity generation will be equivalent to that of the Kunming Power Plant.

The main projects of the Liulangdong Hydroelectric Power Station include a dam at the entrance, an open channel and tunnel stretching seven li, and the main power plant building.

### 云南修地下水电站

1958-01-09
第3版()
专栏：

    云南修地下水电站
    据新华社昆明7日电云南省一座利用地下水发电的六郎洞水电站已经动工兴建。这座电站建成后，发电量将和昆明电厂相等。
    六郎洞水电站主要工程包括洞口的水坝和长达七华里的明渠及隧道和主厂房。
