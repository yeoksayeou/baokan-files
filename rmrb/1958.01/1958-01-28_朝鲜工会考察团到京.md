Title: Korean Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-01-28
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, January 27th: A four-member Korean Trade Union delegation, led by head of the delegation Park Yu-jin, arrived in Beijing by train on the 26th.
　　
The delegation was sent by the Central Committee of the General Federation of Korean Trade Unions at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 朝鲜工会考察团到京

1958-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜工会考察团到京
    新华社27日讯　朝鲜工会考察团一行四人，在团长朴有镇率领下26日乘火车到达北京。
    考察团是朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会应中华全国总工会的邀请派遣的。
