Title: Prominent Activists from India, France, the Soviet Union and Other Countries Awarded Lenin Peace Prize
Author:
Date: 1958-01-02
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 31st: TASS reported on December 31st that the International Lenin Prize Committee announced that a group of prominent activists from various countries who actively fought for peace and peaceful coexistence were awarded the 1956 International Lenin Prize "For Strengthening International Peace."

    The recipients of the prize include: Chandrasekhara Venkata Raman, the famous Indian physicist known for his works on optics; Émmanuel d'Astier de la Vigerie (France), one of the earliest participants in the peace movement and a comrade of Joliot-Curie; Maria Rosa Oliver, an Argentine female journalist and social activist renowned in Latin America for her defense of peace; Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee and a famous Soviet poet; Udakandawala Saranankara Thero, a Ceylonese Buddhist monk and social activist who voluntarily went to Christmas Island to protest British hydrogen bomb tests; Danilo Dolci, an Italian writer who actively opposed military bloc policies and strived for the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons; and Heinrich Brandweiner, an Austrian professor of international law and religious law at the University of Graz who has led his country's peace movement for many years and actively advocated for disarmament and the peaceful resolution of international disputes.

    The decision to award the Lenin Prize was signed by Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the International Lenin Prize Committee, and Vice-Chairmen Guo Moruo, Louis Aragon, and others.

### 印法苏等国著名活动家  荣获列宁和平奖金

1958-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

    印法苏等国著名活动家
    荣获列宁和平奖金
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科12月31日讯：列宁国际奖金委员会12月31日宣布，一批为和平与和平共处而积极斗争的各国著名活动家荣获了1956年“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金。
    荣获奖金的有：由于光学方面的著作而著名的印度物理学家钱德拉塞卡拉·温卡塔·拉曼，和平运动的最初参加者之一、约里奥—居里的战友埃马尼埃·达斯迪埃—德拉维热里（法国），以维护和平而驰名拉丁美洲的阿根廷女新闻记者和社会活动家玛丽亚·罗莎·奥利弗，苏联保卫和平委员会主席、苏联著名诗人尼古拉·吉洪诺夫，曾自愿去圣诞岛抗议英国氢弹试验的锡兰佛教法师和社会活动家乌达肯达瓦拉·萨拉南卡拉·铁罗，积极反对军事集团政策、争取禁止原子武器和氢武器的意大利作家丹尼洛·多尔奇，多年来领导本国和平运动、积极主张裁军与和平解决国际争端的奥地利格拉次市大学国际法和宗教法教授海因里希·布兰德魏纳。
    在颁发列宁奖金的决议上签字的有列宁国际奖金委员会主席斯科贝尔琴、副主席郭沫若、路易·阿拉贡等。
