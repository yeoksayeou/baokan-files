Title: Zhang Jingwu Hosts a Banquet for the Dalai Lama, Wishing New Achievements in Tibet Region Work in 1958
Author:
Date: 1958-01-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, January 4th. Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet, hosted a banquet for the Dalai Lama at the Preparatory Committee of the Autonomous Region on the evening of the 3rd to celebrate the New Year.

Zhang Jingwu congratulated everyone on the successful completion of the First Five-Year Plan by the motherland. He said that in 1958, under the international and domestic situation favorable to socialism and world peace, our motherland's socialist construction will move from victory to victory.

Zhang Jingwu congratulated the Dalai Lama and all Tibetan officials attending the banquet on the achievements made in Tibet region work in 1957, and wished that the work in the Tibet region in 1958 would further strengthen national unity, further develop patriotic and progressive forces, and achieve new successes.

At the end of the banquet, the Dalai Lama said that the forces of the peaceful democratic camp are growing stronger day by day, our motherland's position in the international arena is becoming increasingly important, the First Five-Year Plan has been completed, and the Second Five-Year Plan has started smoothly. The Dalai Lama said, I believe that there will be progress in all aspects of Tibet's work in the new year. To this end, I invite everyone to have a drink.

Senior secular and religious officials of Tibet and famous Living Buddhas and Khenpos of the religious community were invited to attend today's banquet. Zhang Guohua, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China and Commander of the Tibet Military Region, and other responsible cadres of the Working Committee and Military Region also attended the banquet.

### 张经武宴请达赖喇嘛  预祝1958年西藏地区工作获得新成就

1958-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    张经武宴请达赖喇嘛
    预祝1958年西藏地区工作获得新成就
    新华社拉萨4日电　中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武为庆贺新年，3日晚在自治区筹委会宴请达赖喇嘛。
    张经武为祖国胜利地完成了第一个五年计划向大家祝贺。他说，1958年在国际国内有利于社会主义和世界和平的形势下，我们祖国的社会主义建设事业将从胜利走向胜利。
    张经武向达赖喇嘛和参加宴会的所有藏族官员祝贺西藏地区工作在1957年所获得的成就，并预祝1958年的西藏地区工作将会更进一步加强民族团结，更进一步发展爱国、进步的力量和获得新的成就。
    宴会最后，达赖喇嘛说，现在和平民主阵营的力量日益强大，我们祖国在国际上的地位日益重要，第一个五年计划已经完成，第二个五年计划又顺利地开始了。达赖喇嘛说，我相信在新的一年里西藏各方面的工作必有进展，为此，我请大家干一杯。
    应邀参加今天宴会的有西藏的高级僧俗官员和宗教界著名的活佛、堪布。中共西藏工委副书记、西藏军区司令员张国华和工委、军区的其他负责干部也出席了宴会。
