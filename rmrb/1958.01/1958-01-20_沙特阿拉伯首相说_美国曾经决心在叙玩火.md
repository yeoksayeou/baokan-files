Title: Saudi Arabian Prime Minister Says the United States Was Determined to Play with Fire in Syria
Author:
Date: 1958-01-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 18th. According to a report in today's "Al-Ahram," Saudi Arabian Crown Prince Faisal told the newspaper's reporter that the United States "was determined to wage war in the recent Syrian crisis."

He said that this was the impression he got from his talks with American officials during his recent stay in the United States.

### 沙特阿拉伯首相说  美国曾经决心在叙玩火

1958-01-20
第5版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯首相说
    美国曾经决心在叙玩火
    新华社开罗18日电　据“金字塔报”今天报道，沙特阿拉伯王储费萨尔首相对这家报纸的记者说，美国“在最近的叙利亚危机中曾经决心进行战争”。
    他说，这是他最近在美国停留期间从他同美国官员进行的谈话中得到这个印象的。
