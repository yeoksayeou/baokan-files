Title: A Batch of Cadres Sent to Lower Levels in Xinjiang Depart

Author: 
Date: 1958-01-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, January 23rd: The first batch of 490 cadres from the first-level organizations of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, sent to lower levels, left Urumqi City enthusiastically this morning.

The first batch sent to lower levels are mainly Han cadres. They will go to various agricultural cooperatives in Urumqi County, where Han and Hui people live together, to participate in labor and training. Prior to this, the Production and Construction Corps of the Xinjiang Military Region had already sent more than 4,000 cadres to various reclamation area farms; the Urumqi Special Zone and the Bortala Mongol Autonomous Prefecture had also sent a batch of cadres to lower levels.

### 新疆一批下放干部出发

1958-01-24
第4版()
专栏：

    新疆一批下放干部出发
    据新华社乌鲁木齐23日电　新疆维吾尔自治区一级机关首批下放的干部四百九十人，昨天早晨欢欣鼓舞地离开了乌鲁木齐市。
    首批下放的主要是汉族干部。他们将到汉回杂居的乌鲁木齐县各农业社去劳动锻炼。在此以前，新疆军区生产建设部队已下放干部四千多名到各个垦区农场；乌鲁木齐专区、博尔塔拉蒙古族自治州也下放了一批干部。
