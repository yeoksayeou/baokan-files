Title: Soviet Geological and Geophysical Expedition Determines Geological Structure of the Sea of Okhotsk, Finds Basic Source of Earthquakes in the Kuril Islands

Author:
Date: 1958-01-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 15th: According to TASS: After detailed investigation, the Soviet Academy of Sciences' Pacific Integrated Geological-Geophysical Expedition determined that the structure of the Earth's crust under Sakhalin Island, the Kuril Islands, and the Sea of Okhotsk is distinctly different from the structure of the Pacific Ocean floor.

Soviet scientists have determined that the crustal thickness of the aforementioned islands is approximately thirty kilometers, approaching the crustal thickness of the continents. The granite layer extends from the Asian continent through the Sea of Okhotsk to the Kuril Islands, and then terminates at one of the deepest ocean trenches - the Kuril Trench, which is up to ten and a half kilometers deep. According to aeromagnetic survey data, a huge fault can be seen along the slope of the Kuril Trench, running along the Kuril Ridge, and it appears to be the basic source of earthquakes in this region.

This investigation allows for a new perspective to explain a series of scientific issues related to the geological development history of the Far East, especially the geological development history of the Kuril Islands and Sakhalin Island, the deep-sea trenches of the Pacific Ocean, and the continental margins. It can also clarify the nature of strong tectonic activity in this region.

### 苏联地质地球物理考察队  确定鄂霍次克海底地质构造  找到了千岛地区地震的基本来源

1958-01-16
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联地质地球物理考察队
    确定鄂霍次克海底地质构造
    找到了千岛地区地震的基本来源
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社讯：苏联科学院太平洋综合地质—地球物理考察队经过详细考察后确定，库页岛、千岛群岛和鄂霍次克海下地壳的结构同太平洋底地壳的结构是截然不同的。
    苏联科学家确定，上述各岛屿的地壳厚度大约是三十公里，接近于大陆的地壳厚度。花岗岩表层从亚洲大陆经过鄂霍次克海一直伸延到千岛群岛，然后在最深的大洋凹地之一——深达十公里半的千岛凹地处中断。据航空磁力测量材料看来，沿着千岛凹地的斜坡可以看出有一个巨大的断口，断口的走向是顺着千岛山脊，看来它就是这一地区地震的基本来源。
    根据这项考察可以用新的观点来解释一系列同远东地质发展史有关的科学问题，特别是同千岛群岛和库页岛、太平洋的深水凹地和大陆边缘的地质发展史有关的科学问题，可以查明这一地区强烈地质构造活动的本质。
