Title: Our Nation Flies Flags at Half-Mast in Mourning

Author:
Date: 1958-01-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch: On the 10th—the day of the funeral of Dr. Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania—the organs of the People's Committees of all provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government, as well as the troops, factories, mines, enterprises, schools, people's organizations, and ships docked in ports throughout China, flew flags at half-mast in mourning. On the 10th, people from all walks of life in the capital, Beijing, continued to go to the Romanian Embassy in China to offer condolences on the death of Chairman Groza. In recent days, more than 30,000 people have gone to offer their condolences.

### 我全国各地下半旗志哀

1958-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我全国各地下半旗志哀
    据新华社10日讯　10日——罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团主席格罗查博士殡葬的日子，全中国各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会和各市人民委员会的机关、部队、厂矿、企业、学校、人民团体和停在港内的轮船，都下半旗志哀。首都北京各界人民在10日继续到罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆吊唁格罗查主席逝世。连日来，前往吊唁的已有三万多人。
