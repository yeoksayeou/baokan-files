Title: Flames of Ifni Uprising Spread to Spanish Sahara
Author:
Date: 1958-01-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 16th: The uprising of the people of Ifni, a Spanish territory, against Spanish colonial rule has spread to another Spanish territory—Spanish Sahara, located south of Morocco.

    Spanish troops and local insurgents have engaged in fierce clashes in the Rio de Oro region. A major clash occurred in El Aaiún (Saguia el-Hamra region) on the 13th, resulting in casualties on both sides. According to a communiqué issued by the Spanish command, the Spanish colonial army suffered fifty-one casualties. Newspapers reported that the Spanish command concentrated two thousand troops in the city to maintain control of the Saguia el-Hamra region.

### 伊夫尼的起义火焰  延烧西属撒哈拉

1958-01-19
第5版()
专栏：

    伊夫尼的起义火焰
    延烧西属撒哈拉
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社巴黎16日讯：西班牙领地伊夫尼人民反对西班牙殖民统治的起义，已经扩展到另一个西班牙领地——位于摩洛哥南面的西属撒哈拉。
    西班牙军队和当地起义者在里约特俄罗地区发生了激烈的冲突。13日在艾阿荣城（塞古埃特哈姆拉地区）发生了一次大冲突，双方都有伤亡，据西班牙指挥部发表的公报说，西班牙殖民军伤亡五十一人。报纸报道说，西班牙指挥部为了保持住塞古埃特哈姆拉地区，在这个城市里集中了两千名军队。
