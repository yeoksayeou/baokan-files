Title: Canadian Defense Minister Admits US Planes Carrying Hydrogen Bombs Are Overhead
Author:
Date: 1958-01-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ottawa, 11th: Canadian Defense Minister Pearkes admitted in the Canadian House of Commons on the 10th that American planes carrying nuclear weapons "occasionally" fly over Canada.

Pearkes made these remarks in response to a question from a member of parliament. He also said that each flight of these American aircraft is authorized by the Canadian government.

### 加拿大国防部长承认  带氢弹美机就在头顶

1958-01-12
第5版()
专栏：

    加拿大国防部长承认
    带氢弹美机就在头顶
    新华社11日讯　渥太华消息：加拿大国防部长皮尔克斯10日在加拿大众议院承认：带有核武器的美国飞机“偶而”在加拿大上空飞行。
    皮尔克斯是在答复一个议员质问时说这些话的。他还说，这种美国飞机的每次飞行都获得加拿大政府的许可。
