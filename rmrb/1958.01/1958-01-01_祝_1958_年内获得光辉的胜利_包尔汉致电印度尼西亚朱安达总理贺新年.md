Title: Wishing Splendid Victory in 1958, Burhan Sends New Year Greetings to Indonesian Prime Minister Djuanda

Author: 
Date: 1958-01-01
Page: 4

    Wishing Splendid Victory in 1958
    Burhan Sends New Year Greetings to Indonesian Prime Minister Djuanda
    Xinhua News Agency, December 31st: Burhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, today sent a telegram to Indonesian Prime Minister Djuanda, congratulating him on the New Year of 1958. The telegram reads as follows: To Mr. Priyono, Chairman of the General Association of Indonesia-China Friendship Association in Jakarta, please forward to the esteemed Prime Minister Djuanda:
    As 1958 approaches, I, on behalf of all members of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, extend our heartfelt congratulations to you and, through you, to all the people of Indonesia.
    We are fully confident that the Indonesian people, closely united around President Sukarno, will surely achieve a splendid victory in the upcoming year of 1958, in the great struggle to defend national sovereignty and territorial integrity, and to liberate West Irian. We sincerely wish you great achievements in the new year in national construction and social and cultural prosperity. The Chinese people will always stand with the Indonesian people.
    May the fraternal friendship and cultural ties between China and Indonesia be further developed, and may the peoples of the two countries cooperate more closely and achieve even greater success in the cause of defending lasting peace in Asia, Africa, and the world!
                        President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association
                                              Burhan
                      December 31, 1957

### 祝1958年内获得光辉的胜利  包尔汉致电印度尼西亚朱安达总理贺新年

1958-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    祝1958年内获得光辉的胜利
    包尔汉致电印度尼西亚朱安达总理贺新年
    新华社31日讯　中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉今天致电印度尼西亚朱安达总理，祝贺1958年新年。电文如下：雅加达印度尼西亚—中国友好协会总会普里约诺主席，请转致尊敬的朱安达总理阁下：
    当此1958年来临之时，我谨代表中国—印度尼西亚友好协会全体会员，向您并通过您向全体印度尼西亚人民致以衷心的祝贺。
    我们完全相信：印度尼西亚人民紧紧地团结在苏加诺总统的周围、在即将来临的1958年内、在保卫国家主权和领土完整、在解放西伊里安的伟大斗争中必将获得光辉的胜利。我们衷心地祝愿你们在新的一年内、在国家建设、社会和文化的繁荣方面获得巨大的成就。中国人民将永远和印度尼西亚人民站在一起。
    愿中国与印度尼西亚两国之间的兄弟般的友谊和文化联系有进一步的发展，愿两国人民在保卫亚非和世界持久和平的事业上更密切地合作并取得更辉煌的成就！
                        中国—印度尼西亚友好协会会长
                                              包尔汉
                      1957年12月31日
