Title: Zhang Jingwu Instructs on Work at the New Year's Gathering of Cadres of All Ethnic Groups in Lhasa: Better Unite the Tibetan People, Continue to Follow the Prudent and Steady Approach, Carry Out Rectification in the Party and the League, and Conduct Extensive Socialist Education
Author:
Date: 1958-01-03
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, January 1st: Zhang Jingwu, Representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet and Secretary of the Tibet Work Committee of the Communist Party of China, said at the New Year's gathering of cadres of Tibetan, Han, and other ethnic groups on New Year's Eve of 1957 that on the basis of past work, we should better unite the Tibetan people in the future, continue to follow the "prudent and steady" approach, and work hard.

　　He said that the tasks before us are: to further consolidate and expand the anti-imperialist and patriotic united front, broadly unite all anti-imperialist and patriotic people, resolutely support all patriotic, united, and progressive forces, oppose the splitting activities of imperialist elements and separatist elements, and oppose regression and all non-patriotic words and deeds.

　　Zhang Jingwu said: According to the instructions of the Central Government, a rectification movement should be carried out within the Communist Party and the Communist Youth League in Tibet, to universally and deeply oppose bureaucracy, subjectivism, sectarianism, Han chauvinism, local nationalism, and individualism, so as to improve the Party's Marxist ideological level, improve work and style, strengthen the Party's unity and leadership, and do a good job in Tibet. It is also necessary to conduct extensive patriotic and socialist education among the broad masses of people in Tibet, to further enhance the people's patriotic and socialist consciousness, further strengthen national unity, and consolidate the unification of the motherland. We must also continue to do a good job in cultivating Tibetan revolutionary cadres and in cultural, educational, and health work.

　　Zhang Jingwu said that the tasks of 1958 are arduous. He hopes that all Tibetan and Han cadres and military personnel, under the correct leadership of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, and under the direct leadership of the Dalai Lama, the Panchen Erdeni, and the Tibet Work Committee of the Communist Party of China, will work actively, struggle hard, be modest and prudent, guard against arrogance and impetuosity, work diligently, overcome difficulties, and work together to further strengthen national unity, consolidate the unification of the motherland, and resolutely and thoroughly implement the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet.

　　About a thousand cadres from the Tibet Work Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, and the Tibet Military Region attended the New Year's gathering.

### 张经武在拉萨各族干部的团拜会上指示工作  更好地团结西藏人民　继续遵循慎重稳进方针  在党内团内开展整风　广泛进行社会主义教育

1958-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    张经武在拉萨各族干部的团拜会上指示工作
    更好地团结西藏人民　继续遵循慎重稳进方针  在党内团内开展整风　广泛进行社会主义教育
    新华社拉萨1日电　中央人民政府驻西藏代表、中共西藏工委书记张经武1957年除夕在藏、汉等各族干部迎接新年的团拜会上说，在过去的工作基础上今后应该更好的团结西藏人民，继续遵循着“慎重稳进”的方针，努力工作。
    他说，摆在我们面前的任务是：进一步巩固和扩大反帝爱国统一战线，广泛地团结所有反帝爱国的人士，坚决支持一切爱国、团结、进步的力量，反对帝国主义分子和分裂主义分子的分裂活动，反对倒退和一切非爱国的言行。
    张经武说：根据中央的指示，在西藏地方共产党和共青团内部要开展整风运动，普遍地、深入地反对官僚主义、主观主义、宗派主义和大民族主义、地方民族主义以及个人主义等，以便提高党的马克思主义的思想水平，改进工作、改进作风，加强党的团结和领导，做好西藏地方的工作。还要在西藏地方广大人民群众中进行广泛的爱国主义和社会主义教育，进一步提高人民的爱国主义和社会主义觉悟，进一步加强民族团结，巩固祖国的统一。还必须继续做好培养藏族革命干部的工作和文教、卫生等工作。
    张经武说，1958年的任务是艰巨的，他希望全体藏、汉族干部和部队人员，在党中央和毛主席的正确领导下，在达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼和中共西藏工委的直接领导下，积极工作，艰苦奋斗，谦虚谨慎，戒骄戒躁，兢兢业业，克服困难，为进一步加强民族团结、巩固祖国统一和坚决彻底执行关于和平解放西藏办法的协议而共同努力。
    参加新年团拜大会的有中共西藏工委、西藏自治区筹备委员会、西藏军区的机关干部约一千人。
