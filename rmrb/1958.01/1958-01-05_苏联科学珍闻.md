Date: 1958-01-05

Xinhua News Agency

	Designing a 50 Billion Electron Volt Accelerator
	A new giant synchrophasotron with an energy of 50 billion electron volts is being designed at the Leningrad Institute of Electrophysical Machinery. This accelerator is 40 billion electron volts more powerful than the world's largest synchrophasotron currently owned by the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research, and about twice as powerful as similar accelerators being built in the United States. This new accelerator uses the principle of strong focusing, which can greatly reduce the amplitude of particle vibrations as they move in a circular orbit, thus reducing the area of the vacuum chamber cross-section. The diameter of the annular electromagnet of this new accelerator will reach 500 meters, which is about eight times larger than the current world's largest synchrophasotron made in the Soviet Union. However, due to the use of the strong focusing principle, the total weight of the magnet is only 22,000 tons, which is 14,000 tons lighter than the current one.
	Television Telescope Sees Really Far
	The "Soviet Russia Daily" recently published an article introducing the effectiveness of television telescopes that have been successfully created in the Soviet Union. The article states that on the screen of this telescope, one can directly and clearly see the mountains on the moon and the shadows of huge craters in the mountains. Other planets in the solar system, distant stars, and galaxies can be observed. This type of telescope can photograph stars in an instant, and the photographed images can also be magnified.
	If the television telescope is replaced with a wide-angle telescope, it can also observe artificial satellites. It can photograph rockets that will be launched to the moon.
	Meteorites and Earth Have the Same Uranium Composition
	The isotopic composition of uranium contained in meteorites is the same as the isotopic composition of uranium in the Earth's crust—this is the conclusion reached by Soviet Academy of Sciences Radium Institute scientists Petrzhak, Bak, and Senyushkin in their work "The Isotopic Composition of Uranium in Meteorites." This work puts forward new scientific material on the identity of the substances contained in the Earth and "celestial stones" — meteorites, and therefore won the 1957 Science Prize from the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences.
	New Direction in the Study of Primitive Technology
	Another recipient of the Academy of Sciences' 1957 Science Prize was Soviet architect S.A. Semenov's work "Prehistoric Technology". This scientist used precision optical instruments to discover traces on ancient stone and bone tools that can be used for academic research. Based on the traces, the uses, methods of use, and characteristics of things made with these tools can be determined. Semenov's research on ancient production tools has opened up a new direction in historical science.

### 苏联科学珍闻

1958-01-05
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联科学珍闻
    设计五百亿电子伏特加速器
    一座新的能量达五百亿电子伏特的巨型同步稳相加速器正在列宁格勒电子物理机械研究所设计中。这座加速器比联合原子核研究所目前拥有的世界上最大的同步稳相加速器的能量还要大四百亿电子伏特，比美国正在建造的同类加速器大一倍左右。这座新的加速器采用了强聚焦原理，可以大大缩小加速粒子在圆形轨道中运动时的振动幅度，因此可以减少真空室横截面的面积。这座新的加速器的环形电磁铁的直径将达五百公尺，即比苏联制成的目前世界最大的同步稳相加速器大八倍左右。但是由于采用了强聚焦原理，磁铁总重量只有二万二千吨，比目前的要轻一万四千吨。
    电视望远镜望得真远
    “苏维埃俄罗斯报”最近登载了一篇文章，介绍苏联已经创制成功的电视望远镜的效用。文章说，在这台望远镜的萤光屏上，可以直接清楚地看到月亮上的山脉和山脉中巨大陷坑的影子。可以观察太阳系的其他行星，遥远的星球和星系。这种望远镜可以把星球在一刹那间拍摄下来，拍摄下来的形象还可以放大。
    电视望远镜如果换上广角望远镜后，还可以观察人造卫星。它可以拍摄将放射到月球上去的火箭。
    陨石和地球的铀成分相同
    陨石中所含的铀的同位素成分和地壳中的铀的同位素成分是相同的——这是苏联科学院镭研究所的科学家彼得热克、巴克和谢纽什金在他们的“陨石中的铀的同位素成分”这篇著作中所得出的结论。这篇著作提出了关于地球和“天石”——陨石所包含的物质的同一性的新的科学材料，因而荣获了苏联科学院主席团1957年科学奖金。
    研究原始时代技术的新方向
    另一个获得科学院1957年科学奖金的还有苏联建筑学家塞·谢苗诺夫的著作“原始时代的技术”。这位科学家利用精密的光学仪器，在古代的石器和骨器上发现了可以进行学术研究的痕迹，根据痕迹，可以确定这些工具的用途、使用方法和用这些工具制造出来的东西的特性。谢苗诺夫关于古代生产工具的研究在历史科学上开辟了一个新的方向。　（新华社）
