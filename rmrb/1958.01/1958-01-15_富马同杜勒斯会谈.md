Title: Phouma-Dulles Talks
Author:
Date: 1958-01-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 14th: Laotian Prime Minister Prince Souvanna Phouma arrived in Washington on the 13th and held talks with US Secretary of State Dulles that afternoon.

Prince Phouma told reporters after his meeting with Dulles that he had "explained the political situation in Laos" to Dulles during the talks. Phouma also said that he would hold talks with officials from the US State Department to discuss US economic "aid" to Laos. According to Western news agencies, Dulles expressed to Phouma during the talks the US's "concern" about the formation of a coalition government in Laos to achieve national unity.

### 富马同杜勒斯会谈

1958-01-15
第5版()
专栏：

    富马同杜勒斯会谈
    据新华社14日讯　华盛顿消息：老挝王国首相富马亲王在13日到达华盛顿，并且在当天下午同美国国务卿杜勒斯举行了会谈。
    富马亲王在同杜勒斯会谈以后对记者说，他在会谈中向杜勒斯“解释了老挝的政局”。富马还说，他将同美国国务院的官员举行会谈，讨论美国给老挝的经济“援助”问题。据西方通讯社报道，杜勒斯在会谈中向富马表示了美国对老挝成立联合政府从而实现国家统一这件事的“忧虑”。
