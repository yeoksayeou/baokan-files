Title: CPPCC Sends Six Propaganda Teams to Various Locations to Deeply Promote the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme
Author:
Date: 1958-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th -- The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) has decided to dispatch six propaganda teams for the Draft Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme, led by Hu Yuzhi, Wang Li, Wei Jiangong, Ye Shengtao, Wei Que, Luo Changpei, Lü Shuxiang, and others, to carry out propaganda work in Nanjing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Zhengzhou, Xi'an, Chengdu, Chongqing, Kunming, Shenyang, Changchun, Beijing, Tianjin, and other places. The propaganda teams will depart successively in the coming days.

The propaganda teams will, based on the reports made by Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, and Hu Qiaomu, member of the Chinese Writing Reform Committee, in Beijing on the 10th, promote the current tasks of writing reform and the significance and use of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme, in order to attract attention from all sectors of society and facilitate the widespread promotion of the phonetic alphabet throughout the country in the future.

After arriving at their destinations, the propaganda teams will, through the local CPPCC committees and with the assistance of local propaganda and cultural departments, hold symposiums and large-scale report meetings; and promote local language workers who are enthusiastic about writing reform to hold report meetings locally, and continue to carry out in-depth propaganda for the phonetic alphabet scheme.

### 政协派六个宣传小组赴各地  深入宣传汉语拼音方案

1958-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    政协派六个宣传小组赴各地
    深入宣传汉语拼音方案
    新华社10日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会决定派出由胡愈之、王力、魏建功、叶圣陶、韦悫、罗常培、吕叔湘等人领导的六个汉语拼音方案草案宣传小组，分别到南京、上海、广州、武汉、郑州、西安、成都、重庆、昆明、沈阳、长春、北京、天津等地进行宣传工作。各宣传小组在日内将陆续出发。
    各宣传小组将根据政协全国委员会主席周恩来和中国文字改革委员会委员胡乔木10日在北京所作的报告，宣传当前文字改革的任务和汉语拼音方案的意义和用途，以引起社会各方面的重视，便利拼音字母今后在全国各地广泛推行。
    各宣传小组到达目的地后，将通过当地政协委员会并取得当地宣传文教部门的协助，举行座谈会和规模较大的报告会；并且推动当地热心于文字改革的语文工作者在当地举行报告会后，继续为拼音方案进行深入宣传。
