Reader's Letter:

    Making Plans, Buying Government Bonds
    I am an elementary school teacher. My family consists of six people (my mother, wife, and three children). Because our household expenses are well-planned, we live quite well. Some comrades have curiously asked me, "You only earn thirty-four yuan a month, how can you take good care of a family of six?" Actually, there is no trick to it; it's just living according to the principle of "living within our means and being thrifty."
    After reading the People's Daily editorial "How Much Government Bonds Are You Preparing to Subscribe to Next Year?", I made a household economic income and expenditure annual plan for 1958. This plan was made in the spirit of considering the urgency of various expenses, simplifying where possible, and saving where possible. The plan stipulates that twelve yuan of government bonds will be purchased in 1958 (in 1957, due to illness in the family, expenses were higher, and only three yuan of government bonds were purchased). This plan has already been discussed at a family meeting. My mother said that the plan is still too generous. She believes that more can be saved in the proportion of coarse and fine grains purchased. According to my wife (she works as a temporary cook at a school and earns ten yuan and five jiao a month), if we follow the plan, as long as everyone in the family is healthy, we should be able to save no less than thirty yuan by the end of the year, and she guarantees not to waste any grain. Even so, our family's life is still improving step by step. I am attaching our family's income and expenditure plan for your reference.
          Yuhe Township, Huixian County, Henan Province, Wang Chengxian

### 订计划，买公债
王承先
1958-01-09
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    订计划，买公债
    我是个小学教员。我家一共六口人（母亲、爱人、三个孩子），因为家庭开支比较有计划，生活过得很不错。有的同志曾经好奇地问我：“你每月只有三十四元工资，怎么能妥善地照顾一个六口之家呢？”其实这里也没有什么窍门，不过是按照“量入为出，勤俭持家”的原则过日子而已。
    我读了人民日报“你准备明年认购多少公债”的社论以后，就订出了1958年的家庭经济收支年度计划。这个计划是本着各种开支都应当看事情的缓急，当简则简，当省则省的精神订的。这个计划规定1958年将要买十二元的公债（1957年因家中有人生病，开支多了些，只买了三元公债），这个计划已经在家庭会议上讨论过了。我母亲说这个计划还是订得太宽裕了。她认为在买粗、细粮的比例上还可节约一些。据我爱人（她在学校里当临时炊事员，每月收入十元五角）估计，按照计划办事，只要家中人口平安，到年底还能节约三十元不成问题，她并保证不浪费一点粮食。就是这样，我们一家人的生活也还是步步上升的。现在把我家做的家庭收支计划附后，给你们参考吧。
          河南辉县峪河乡　王承先
