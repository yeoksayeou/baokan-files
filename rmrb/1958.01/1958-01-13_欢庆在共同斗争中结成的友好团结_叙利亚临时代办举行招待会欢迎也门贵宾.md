Title: Celebrating the Friendly Solidarity Forged in Common Struggle: Syrian Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception to Welcome Distinguished Yemeni Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-01-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 12th—At the reception held tonight by Syrian Chargé d'Affaires to China, Shalan, to welcome Crown Prince Badr, representatives from fourteen Asian and African countries celebrated the friendly solidarity forged in their common struggle and paid tribute to the people of Arab countries standing at the forefront of the anti-colonialist struggle.

Crown Prince Badr, Premier Zhou Enlai, and envoys to China from twelve Asian and African countries attended tonight’s reception. As Premier Zhou said in his speech at the reception, tonight’s reception was like another gathering of representatives from Asian and African countries who attended the Bandung Conference and the Cairo Conference.

Crown Prince Badr, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Chargé d'Affaires Shalan all spoke at the reception. In their speeches, they unanimously emphasized that the anti-colonialist struggle is the common struggle of the people of Asian and African countries, and they deeply believed that this struggle would achieve final victory.

At the reception, which was filled with an atmosphere of boundless friendship, the speeches by Crown Prince Badr, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Chargé d'Affaires Shalan aroused bursts of applause. Crown Prince Badr, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and others warmly shook hands with all the Asian and African envoys present, repeatedly toasted, and exchanged wishes for health and friendship. Throughout the two-hour-plus reception, there was an atmosphere of cordial conversation and laughter everywhere.

### 欢庆在共同斗争中结成的友好团结  叙利亚临时代办举行招待会欢迎也门贵宾

1958-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    欢庆在共同斗争中结成的友好团结
    叙利亚临时代办举行招待会欢迎也门贵宾
    据新华社12日讯　在今晚叙利亚驻中国大使馆临时代办沙蓝欢迎巴德尔王太子举行的招待会上，十四个亚非国家的代表欢庆在共同的斗争中结成的友好团结，向站在反殖民主义最前线的阿拉伯各国人民表示敬意。
    巴德尔王太子、周恩来总理和亚非地区十二个国家驻华使节出席了今晚的招待会。正如周总理在招待会上的讲话中所说的，今天的招待会好像是出席万隆会议和开罗会议的亚非国家代表的又一次欢聚。
    巴德尔王太子、周恩来总理和沙蓝临时代办在招待会上都讲了话。他们在讲话中一致强调反殖民主义的斗争是亚非各国人民的共同斗争，并且深信这个斗争一定会取得最后胜利。
    在充满无限友好气氛的招待会上，巴德尔王太子、周恩来总理和沙蓝临时代办的讲话，引起了一阵又一阵的掌声。巴德尔王太子、周恩来总理和陈毅副总理等同所有到会的亚非各国使节热烈地握手，一再干杯，互道健康和友谊。在两个多小时的招待会上，到处都充满了亲切的交谈声和欢笑声。
