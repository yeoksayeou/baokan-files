Title: Crown Prince Badr Delivers Radio Address to Our People
Author:
Date: 1958-01-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th.  Crown Prince Badr, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defense of the Kingdom of Yemen, at the invitation of the Central People's Broadcasting Station, made a radio address to the Chinese people. The full text of the speech is as follows:
    Chinese brothers:
    I am pleased to convey to the people of your ancient nation the greetings of the people of Yemen, greetings from friends to friends. The people of Yemen are your comrades-in-arms on the front against imperialism and your brothers in calling for cooperation and peaceful coexistence.
    I admire the progress made by the people of your country, and the selfless spirit you have shown in improving the country's industrial level. I have full confidence in the future of great China and the prospects for the unity of the Asian and African peoples.
    I will never forget the support of the Chinese people for the anti-imperialist struggle of Yemen and the Arab countries.
    I sincerely thank you, thank your great leaders Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Zhou Enlai and other friends, and pray that Allah will always assist you and grant you peace.

### 巴德尔王太子向我国人民发表广播讲话

1958-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    巴德尔王太子向我国人民发表广播讲话
    新华社13日讯　也门王国副首相兼外交、国防大臣巴德尔王太子应中央人民广播电台的邀请，向中国人民作了广播讲话。讲话全文如下：中国弟兄们：
    我愉快地向你们古老国家的人民转达也门人民的问候，这是朋友对朋友的问候，也门人民在反对帝国主义的战线上是你们的战友，在号召合作和和平共处方面是你们的弟兄。
    我对你们国家的人民所取得的进步，对你们在提高国家工业水平方面表现出的忘我精神表示敬佩，我对伟大中国的未来、对亚非人民团结的前途都怀有十足的信心。
    我永远不会忘记中国人民对也门和阿拉伯各国人民反帝国主义斗争的支持。
    我衷心地感谢你们，感谢你们的伟大领导人物毛泽东主席、周恩来总理和其他朋友们，祈求真主永远襄助你们，赐给你们和平。
