Title: Commemorating the Martyrdom of Liebknecht and Luxemburg: Berlin Laboring People Hold Massive Demonstration; Grotewohl Calls for Opposition to Militarism and War
Author:
Date: 1958-01-21
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 20th — According to TASS, Berlin laboring people held a large-scale demonstration on the 19th to commemorate the thirty-ninth anniversary of the martyrdom of German proletarian leaders Karl Liebknecht and Rosa Luxemburg.

　　Participants in the demonstration held a mass rally in front of Liebknecht and Luxemburg's gravestones. Those attending the rally included Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Max Reimann, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Germany, and Otto Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic.

　　Grotewohl delivered a speech at the mass rally. He called on the German laboring people to unswervingly carry out the struggle against militarism and war, and against the enemies of the people. He pointed out: Yesterday's murderers are now active again in West Germany and West Berlin. We must shout like Liebknecht and Luxemburg: Peace is threatened. He pointed out that the Soviet Union has once again put forward peace initiatives, while the North Atlantic Treaty Organization countries are still trying to implement their "position of strength" policy.

　　Grotewohl called on the working classes of the two Germanys to strengthen the unity of their ranks. He called on all German people to organize a massive movement against the threat of atomic war.

### 纪念李卜克内西和卢森堡殉难日  柏林劳动人民大示威  格罗提渥号召反对军国主义和战争

1958-01-21
第6版()
专栏：

    纪念李卜克内西和卢森堡殉难日
    柏林劳动人民大示威
    格罗提渥号召反对军国主义和战争
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社柏林讯：柏林劳动人民19日举行大规模示威游行，纪念德国无产阶级领袖卡尔·李卜克内西和罗莎·卢森堡殉难三十九周年。
    示威游行的参加者在李卜克内西和卢森堡的墓碑前举行了群众大会。参加大会的有德国统一社会党中央第一书记乌布利希、德国共产党中央第一书记雷曼和德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥。
    格罗提渥在群众大会上发表了演说。他号召德国劳动人民坚持不渝地进行反对军国主义和战争、反对人民敌人的斗争。他指出：昨日的杀人犯现在又在西德和西柏林活动起来了。我们要像李卜克内西和卢森堡那样高呼：和平受到了威胁。他指出，苏联再次提出了和平倡议，而北大西洋集团国家却仍然企图执行它们的“实力地位”政策。
    格罗提渥号召两个德国的工人阶级加强自己队伍的团结。他号召全体德国人民组织一个声势浩大的反对原子战争威胁的运动。
