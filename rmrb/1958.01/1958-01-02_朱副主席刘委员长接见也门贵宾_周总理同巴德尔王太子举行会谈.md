Title: Vice Chairman Zhu and Chairman Liu Receive Distinguished Guests from Yemen, Premier Zhou Holds Talks with Crown Prince Badr
Author:
Date: 1958-01-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st: Vice Chairman Zhu De and Chairman Liu Shaoqi received in the afternoon today His Royal Highness Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, Deputy Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Yemen, and accompanying personnel Qadi Muhammad al-Amri, Qadi Muhammad al-Shami, and Hassan Baghdadi.

Present at the reception were Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Chen Yi, Chen Shutong, Zhang Zhizhong, Zeng Yongquan, Yu Xinqing, Ke Hua, and Ma Zhenwu.

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st: His Royal Highness Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, Deputy Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Yemen, held talks with Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of our country on the afternoon of New Year's Day, 1958.

Participating in the talks on the Yemeni side were Minister of State Qadi Muhammad al-Amri, Minister of State Qadi Muhammad al-Shami, and Advisor Hassan Baghdadi. Participating in the talks on the Chinese side were Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ke Hua.

### 朱副主席刘委员长接见也门贵宾  周总理同巴德尔王太子举行会谈

1958-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席刘委员长接见也门贵宾
    周总理同巴德尔王太子举行会谈
    新华社1日讯　朱德副主席和刘少奇委员长，在今天下午接见了也门王国副首相塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔王太子和随行人员卡迪·穆罕默德·阿姆里、卡迪·穆罕默德·沙米、哈桑·巴格达迪兹等。
    接见时在座的，有李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、陈毅、陈叔通、张治中、曾涌泉、余心清、柯华、马振武。
    新华社1日讯　也门王国副首相塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔王太子同我国国务院周恩来总理，在1958年元旦的下午举行了会谈。
    也门王国方面参加会谈的，有国务大臣卡迪·穆罕默德·阿姆里，国务大臣卡迪·穆罕默德·沙米，顾问哈桑·巴格达迪兹。中国方面参加会谈的，有陈毅副总理，外交部副部长曾涌泉、西亚非洲司司长柯华。
