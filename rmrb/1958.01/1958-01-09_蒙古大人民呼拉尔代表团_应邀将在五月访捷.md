Title: Delegation of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia to Visit Czechoslovakia in May Upon Invitation
Author:
Date: 1958-01-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, January 8th: It was announced here today that the Chairman of the National Assembly of Czechoslovakia, Fierlinger, has invited a delegation from the Great People's Hural of Mongolia to visit Czechoslovakia. The Chairman of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, Sürenjav, replied in a letter stating that the delegation of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia will visit Czechoslovakia in May of this year upon invitation.

### 蒙古大人民呼拉尔代表团  应邀将在五月访捷

1958-01-09
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古大人民呼拉尔代表团
    应邀将在五月访捷
    新华社乌兰巴托8日电　此间今日宣布，捷克斯洛伐克国民议会主席费林格邀请蒙古大人民呼拉尔代表团访问捷克斯洛伐克。蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席苏伦扎布复信表示，蒙古大人民呼拉尔代表团将在今年5月应邀前往捷克斯洛伐克访问。
