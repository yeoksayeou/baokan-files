Title: State Council Holds Plenary Meeting, Approves This Year's Implementation Plan for the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1958-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The State Council held its sixty-eighth plenary meeting on the morning of the 9th.

After listening to the explanation by Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, the meeting approved the "1958 Implementation Plan for the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics."

After listening to the explanation by General Hong Xuezhi, Director of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the meeting approved the Ministry of National Defense's report on revising the style of some current military uniforms and shoulder and collar badges. The following changes will be made to the current standard uniforms and rank insignia of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

	(1) Officers of the Army, Navy, Air Force, and Volunteers will henceforth be issued collar badge uniforms as regular dress for daily wear. The original shoulder badge uniforms will be retained as formal dress. The original silver-white shoulder badges will be abolished and all will be changed to golden-yellow shoulder badges.

	(2) The cuffs of the regular uniforms of the Army, Navy, Air Force, and Volunteer non-commissioned officers and soldiers will be changed from tight cuffs to loose cuffs.

	(3) The summer regular uniforms of officers at all levels and the winter regular uniforms (including overcoats) of junior officers will henceforth be made of plain cloth. After the change to plain cloth, the Army will use dark brownish-green.

The meeting adopted the Organization Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congresses and People's Committees at all levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the Organization Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congresses and People's Committees at all levels in the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region. It was decided to submit these two drafts to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and approval. Finally, the meeting approved a list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行全体会议  批准中苏文化合作协定今年执行计划

1958-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议
    批准中苏文化合作协定今年执行计划
    新华社9日讯　国务院在9日上午举行了第六十八次全体会议。
    会议在听了文化部部长沈雁冰的说明后，批准了“中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟文化合作协定1958年执行计划”。
    会议在听了中国人民解放军总后方勤务部部长洪学智上将的说明后，批准了国防部关于修改部分现行军服及肩领章样式的报告。对于中国人民解放军现行制式服装及军衔肩章作如下改变：
    （一）陆、海、空军、志愿军的军官，今后改发领章服，作为常服，供日常穿用。保留原肩章服，作为礼服穿用。原银白底肩章取消，均改为金黄底肩章。
    （二）陆、海、空军、志愿军军士、兵的常服，均由紧袖口改为散袖口。
    （三）各级军官的夏常服、尉级军官的冬常服（包括大衣），今后均用平布制做。改为平布后，陆军均采用深棕绿色。
    会议通过了内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例（草案）和新疆维吾尔自治区伊犁哈萨克自治州各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例
  （草案）。并决定将这两个草案提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。最后，会议通过了任免名单。
