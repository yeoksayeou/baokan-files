Title: Pushing Open the Second Door to Happiness
Author: Fushi
Date: 1958-01-01
Page: 8

    Push open the second door to happiness
    
    Welcoming the New Year of the Second Five-Year Plan,
    Please push open with force
    The second door leading to happiness,
    Or,
    Like opening the second sluice gate,
    Let the released torrent and the joyful fish tumble together.
    All footsteps race forward,
    Running across plains and cities,
    Jumping onto small mountain villages,
    Vowing to bring advancement and prosperity to peaks and forests.
    "Let backwardness occupy the motherland?!"
    This is more intolerable than the greatest hardship!
    Now, it is no longer an endless sandy plain,
    We are continuing the Long March from the harvest of the First Five-Year Plan,
    Although, please cherish the rice and steamed buns in your bowl,
    Although, please cherish the fish, meat, vegetables, and lettuce...
    But please eat your fill!
    Let us work faster than a high-speed flywheel.
    From work to after work, do not relax for a second,
    On the way home, the wind of victory will blow away fatigue,
    At night, on holidays, traffic lights and music arrive together,
    You can open your throat and sing or join the dance...
    If you don't want to attend the party tonight,
    You can take a walk, immerse yourself in the joy and struggle of the world of books.
    Love has long overthrown the old god of bound feet,
    Embrace under the cause! Young people!
    Of course, who can distinguish the blended heart's voice?
    Only in the outpouring of passionate joyful tears,
    Perhaps, estimate that the first life belongs to the cause,
    The second, soaked with the soul of love.
    Life sends the warmth of the spring breeze,
    Prolonging the germination and rooting of all things,
    The lovely motherland gives vitality, beauty, and love to all lovers,
    And reserves the greatest glory for future champions;
    Gallop, steed! Fire, rocket!
    We will surpass England in three five-year plans!
    However, this is by no means our end,
    It can only be said that we are starting again from here,
    Our steps will cross Washington,
    Washington—the United States,
    Compared to our future,
    Is just dust invisible even in the sunlight!
    Let us fully grasp the favorable conditions,
    Break through the layers of difficulties on the road ahead,
    Let us use the spirit of engulfing the universe,
    Welcome the New Year of the Second Five-Year Plan,
    Let us use unparalleled caution and meticulous hard work,
    Push open the second door to happiness.

### 推开第二道幸福大门
俯拾
1958-01-01
第8版()
专栏：

    推开第二道幸福大门
    俯拾
    迎接第二个五年的新春，
    请用力推开
    第二道通向幸福的大门，
    或者
    有如打开第二道水闸，
    让放出的激流和欢乐的鱼儿一齐翻滚。
    所有的脚步快速飞奔，
    跑过了平原、城市，
    跳上山区的小村，
    誓把先进与繁荣也带给峰峦和森林。
    “让落后占领祖国？！”
    这比最大的艰苦还难容忍！
    现在，已经不是平沙无垠，
    我们是从第一个五年丰收里继续长征，
    虽然，请你珍惜碗里的米饭与蒸馍，
    虽然请你珍惜鱼、肉、青菜、莴笋……
    但请吃个饱吧！
    让我们干活快过高速运转的飞轮。
    从上班到下班不放松一秒一分，
    归路上自有胜利的风吹散倦困，
    夜晚、假日，红绿灯和音乐一齐来临，
    你可以放开歌喉或者卷入舞阵……
    如果你今天不想参加晚会，
    你可以散步，投入书本世界里的欢乐与斗争。
    爱情，早已推翻红丝系脚的老神，
    在事业之下拥抱吧！青年人！
    当然，谁能分辨出交融着的一片心声？
    只有在倾泻的热情欢泪里，
    也许，估计出第一是属于事业的生命，
    第二，浸透着爱的灵魂。
    生活，送来春风的温存，
    吹长万物发芽生根，
    可爱的祖国把生机、美、爱给予一切有情人，
    更保留着最大的光荣赠给未来的冠军；
    飞驰吧，骏马！飞射吧，火箭！
    我们要在三个五年里超过英伦！
    然而这绝不是我们的终点，
    只能说，我们又从这里动身，
    我们的大步要跨过华盛顿，
    华盛顿——美国，
    比起我们的未来，
    只是在阳光中也看不见的灰尘！
    让我们充分把握住有利条件，
    冲破前路上的困难层层，
    让我们用吞没宇宙的气概，
    迎接第二个五年的新春，
    让我们用无比谨慎与细致的勤奋工作，
    推开第二道幸福的大门。
