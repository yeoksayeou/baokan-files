Title: East Germany Increases Housing Construction
Author:
Date: 1958-01-12
Page: 6

    East Germany Increases Housing Construction

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 6th, (TASS dispatch) – The scale of housing construction in the German Democratic Republic is expanding year by year. According to a report by the German News Agency, from January to September 1957, the number of houses built in cities across the country was 10,800 more than in the same period of 1956. By November 1957, the working people had obtained nearly 25,500 new homes.

### 民主德国增加住宅建筑

1958-01-12
第6版()
专栏：

    民主德国增加住宅建筑
    据新华社讯　据塔斯社柏林6日讯：德意志民主共和国的住宅建设的规模在逐年扩大。据德意志通讯社报道，从1957年1月到9月全国各城市所建设的住宅要比1956年同一时期多一万另八百套。到1957年11月，劳动人民获得了将近两万五千五百套新住宅。
