Title: After the Cambodian Acting Prime Minister Took Office, Seven Cabinet Members Resigned
Author:
Date: 1958-01-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 14th: According to a broadcast by the Cambodian National Radio Station on the evening of the 13th, after Cambodian National Assembly Speaker Ek Yi Oun assumed the post of Acting Prime Minister on the 11th in accordance with the Cambodian Constitution, seven cabinet members of the former Sim Var cabinet immediately tendered their resignations.

The broadcast also stated that the other four cabinet members of the Sim Var cabinet had not submitted their resignations because they were not in Phnom Penh.

### 柬埔寨临时首相接任后  已经有七个阁员辞职

1958-01-15
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨临时首相接任后
    已经有七个阁员辞职
    据新华社金边14日电　据柬埔寨国家电台13日晚广播，柬埔寨国民议会议长埃伊翁11日根据柬埔寨宪法接任临时首相的职务以后，前任沈法内阁的七个阁员立即提出了辞职。
    广播又说，沈法内阁的其他四个阁员由于不在金边而未提出辞职。
