Title: US President Submits Emergency Plan to Congress, Advocating for the Cultivation of Military Science Talent
Author:
Date: 1958-01-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 28th: U.S. President Eisenhower submitted an "emergency" four-year plan to Congress on the 27th, requesting the acceleration of the development of military science education in the United States.

In a special message submitting the plan, Eisenhower emphasized that the plan would focus on cultivating scientific and technological talent to serve the American military machine. He suggested establishing 10,000 scholarships in American universities over the next four years to encourage students to study science and technology. He also suggested training 5,500 college students in four years to become instructors teaching natural sciences in universities.

### 美总统向国会提出紧急计划  鼓吹培养军事科学人才

1958-01-29
第5版()
专栏：

    美总统向国会提出紧急计划
    鼓吹培养军事科学人才
    据新华社28日讯　华盛顿消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔27日向国会提出一项“紧急的”四年计划，要求加紧发展美国的军事科学教育。
    艾森豪威尔在提出这项计划的特别咨文中强调，这个计划将侧重培养为美国军事机器服务的科学技术人才。他建议在今后四年内在美国高等学校设立一万个奖学金，鼓励学生研究科学技术。他还建议在四年内培养五千五百名大学生当大学里讲授自然科学的教员。
