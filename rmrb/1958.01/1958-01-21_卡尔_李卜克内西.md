Author:
Date: 1958-01-21
Page: 6
Character Introduction:

    Karl Liebknecht (1871-1919) was a famous activist in the German and international workers' movement, one of the left-wing representatives of the German Social Democratic Party and the Second International, and one of the founders of the German Communist Party. He fought against opportunism and against German militarism and imperialism. In 1918, Liebknecht actively led the revolutionary struggle of the German proletariat. Unfortunately, on January 15, 1919, he and Rosa Luxemburg were brutally murdered by counter-revolutionaries.

### 卡尔·李卜克内西

专栏：人物介绍

    卡尔·李卜克内西
    卡尔·李卜克内西（1871—1919）是德国和国际工人运动的著名活动家、德国社会民主党和第二国际中的左派代表之一，也是德国共产党的创始人之一。他曾为反对机会主义和反对德国军国主义和帝国主义而进行斗争。1918年，李卜克内西积极领导了德国无产阶级的革命斗争。不幸在1919年1月15日同罗莎·卢森堡一起惨遭反革命杀害。
