Title: Premier Zhou Sends Congratulatory Telegram on Sudanese Republic National Day; Sudanese Prime Minister Khalil Replies with Thanks
Author:
Date: 1958-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 31st - Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai sent a telegram on the 28th to Abdallah Khalil, Prime Minister of the Sudanese Republic, congratulating him on the National Day of the Sudanese Republic. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency Abdallah Khalil, Prime Minister of the Sudanese Republic:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the Sudanese Republic, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to your government and to Your Excellency on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name. I hope that the friendly relations between our two countries will be further developed through the joint efforts of both countries.

    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, December 28, 1957

Xinhua News Agency, December 31st - Sudanese Prime Minister Abdallah Khalil sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai, thanking him for his telegram of congratulations on the anniversary of Sudanese independence. The Sudanese Prime Minister's telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    I am deeply grateful for your warm congratulations on the anniversary of Sudanese independence. In my own name and on behalf of the Sudanese people and government, I extend my best wishes to you and to the Chinese people and government, wishing you continued success and prosperity, and sincerely hoping that the friendly relations existing between our two countries will be further developed.

    Abdallah Khalil, Prime Minister of Sudan, Khartoum, December 30, 1957

### 周总理电贺苏丹共和国国庆  苏丹总理哈利勒复电致谢

1958-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺苏丹共和国国庆
    苏丹总理哈利勒复电致谢
    新华社31日讯　国务院总理周恩来在二十八日致电苏丹共和国政府总理阿卜杜拉·哈利勒，祝贺苏丹共和国国庆。电文如下：苏丹共和国政府总理阿卜杜拉·哈利勒阁下：
    值此苏丹共和国国庆之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义向贵国政府和阁下致以热诚的祝贺。我希望我们两国之间的友好关系将随着两国共同的努力而获得进一步的发展。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　一九五七年十二月二十八日
    新华社31日讯　苏丹总理阿卜杜拉·哈利勒致电周恩来总理，感谢他在苏丹独立纪念日去电祝贺。苏丹总理的电文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来先生阁下：
    苏丹独立纪念日承蒙来电热烈祝贺，深为感激，谨以我个人的名义并且代表苏丹人民和政府向你以及中国人民和政府致以最良好的愿望，祝你们不断成功和繁荣，并且诚恳地同样希望存在于我们两国之间的友好关系将获得进一步的发展。
     苏丹总理阿卜杜拉·哈利勒　一九五七年十二月三十日喀土穆
