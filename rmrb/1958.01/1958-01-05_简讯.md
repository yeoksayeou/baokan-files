Brief News

    Soviet Delegation Arrives in Ceylon
    Colombo Dispatch: The Soviet delegation, invited by the Ceylon government to conduct negotiations on Soviet-Ceylon cultural cooperation, arrived in Colombo on the 3rd.

    Hungarian Court Sentences Counter-Revolutionary Ringleader to Death
    Budapest Dispatch: The Hungarian Supreme Court has approved the death sentence passed by the Budapest State Court on the counter-revolutionary ringleader, Ivan Kovács László. The death sentence has been carried out. This counter-revolutionary ringleader participated in the armed attack on the Budapest Radio Station during the counter-revolutionary riots of October 1956, and later participated in planning the establishment of the counter-revolutionary general headquarters entrenched in the Corvin Cinema, becoming one of the leaders of this counter-revolutionary organization.

    British Aircraft Illegally Intrudes into Albanian Airspace and is Forced to Land
    Tirana Dispatch: A British "Sky Master" aircraft intruded into Albanian airspace at 10:30 local time on December 31, 1957, in the Shemen area. The aircraft flew over Albania for thirty minutes. Albanian Air Force aircraft forced the intruding aircraft to land at an Albanian airport. There were six crew members on board.

### 简讯

1958-01-05

专栏：简讯

    简讯
    苏联代表团到锡兰
    科伦坡讯：应锡兰政府的邀请到锡兰进行苏锡文化合作谈判的苏联代表团3日到达科伦坡。
    匈判处一反革命头子死刑
    布达佩斯讯：匈牙利最高法院已经批准了布达佩斯州法院判处反革命头子伊万·科瓦奇·拉斯洛的死刑。死刑已经执行。这个反革命头子在1956年10月的反革命暴乱中参加了对布达佩斯广播电台的武装进攻，后来又参与策划成立盘踞在科尔文电影院的反革命总司令部，并成为这个反革命机构的首领之一。
    英机非法侵入阿尔巴尼亚领空被迫着陆
    地拉那讯：一架英国“空中霸王”式飞机1957年12月31日在当地时间10时30分在谢门地区侵入了阿尔巴尼亚领空。这架飞机在阿尔巴尼亚上空飞行了三十分钟。阿尔巴尼亚空军飞机强迫这架侵入领空的飞机在阿尔巴尼亚的一个飞机场上着陆。机上有六名驾驶人员。
