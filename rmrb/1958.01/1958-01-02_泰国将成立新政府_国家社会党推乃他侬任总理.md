Title: Thailand to Form New Government; National Socialist Party Nominates Thanom as Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1958-01-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st, Bangkok dispatch: According to Thai newspapers, the new Thai government will be formed in early January 1958.

The National Socialist Party, headed by Thai Armed Forces Supreme Commander Sarit Thanarat, already nominated the party's deputy leader, Thanom Kittikachorn, as the Prime Minister of the new government on December 25th. The National Socialist Party, formed after the Thai parliamentary elections held on December 15th, is composed of one hundred and twenty-three King-appointed second-category members and more than eighty recently elected members who previously belonged to the Federalist Party and the Manangkhasila Free Party. This party holds an absolute majority in the new parliament, which has a total of two hundred and eighty-three seats.

The new parliament already held its first meeting on December 26th. The interim government, headed by Pote Sarasin, has also submitted its resignation to the King.

### 泰国将成立新政府  国家社会党推乃他侬任总理

1958-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

    泰国将成立新政府
    国家社会党推乃他侬任总理
    据新华社1日讯　曼谷消息：据泰国报纸报道，泰国新政府将在1958年1月初成立。
    以泰国武装部队最高司令乃沙立为首的国家社会党，已经在12月25日推举这个党的副领袖乃他侬·吉滴卡宗出任新政府的总理。在12月15日举行的泰国议会选举揭晓以后成立的国家社会党，是由一百二十三个国王任命的第二类议员和八十多个最近当选的以前属于联邦党和马兰卡西拉自由党的议员组成的。这个党在一共有二百八十三席的新议会中占绝对多数。
    新议会已经在12月26日举行了第一次会议。以乃朴·沙拉信为首的临时政府也已经向国王提出辞职。
