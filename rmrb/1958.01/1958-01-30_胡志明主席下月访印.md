Title: President Ho Chi Minh to Visit India Next Month
Author:
Date: 1958-01-30
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 29th. According to an Indian official announcement on the 28th, President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will arrive in New Delhi on February 5th for a ten-day visit to India.

　　In addition to conducting state visits in New Delhi, President Ho Chi Minh will also visit Bakra, Agra, Mumbai, Bangalore and Calcutta.

### 胡志明主席下月访印

1958-01-30
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席下月访印
    据新华社新德里29日电　据印度官方28日宣布，越南民主共和国主席胡志明将在2月5日到达新德里，对印度作为时十天的访问。
    胡志明主席除在新德里进行国事访问外还将到巴克拉、亚格拉、孟买、班加罗尔和加尔各答等地参观。
