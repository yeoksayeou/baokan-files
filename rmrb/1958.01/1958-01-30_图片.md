    The wind turbine generator successfully trial-produced by technicians from the Water Conservancy Department of Baicheng Prefecture, Jilin Province, and workers from the Baicheng Farm Tool Factory can drive a 2.5-horsepower generator and produce 1.84 kilowatts of electricity when three streamlined blades are blown by a level-three wind.
