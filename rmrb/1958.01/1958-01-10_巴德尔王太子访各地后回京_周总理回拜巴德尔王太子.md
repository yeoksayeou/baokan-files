Title: Crown Prince al-Badr Returns to Beijing After Visiting Various Locations; Premier Zhou Enlai Pays a Return Visit to Crown Prince al-Badr
Author:
Date: 1958-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 9th -- Crown Prince al-Badr, Deputy Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Yemen, and his entourage, after visiting various parts of our country, returned to Beijing by special plane on the afternoon of the 9th, accompanied by Zhao Erlu, Minister of the Second Ministry of Machine Building of our country, and Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Those who welcomed him at the airport included Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and Yang Jingren, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission and Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association.

During their stay in Northeast China, Crown Prince al-Badr and his entourage visited Shenyang, Anshan, Changchun and other cities. The distinguished guests visited two factories in Shenyang, the seamless steel pipe plant, the second steelmaking plant, the ironmaking plant and the large rolling mill of Anshan Iron and Steel, and the Changchun First Automobile Manufacturing Plant, among others, and were warmly welcomed by the masses.

Xinhua News Agency, January 9th -- Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, paid a return visit to His Royal Highness Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, Deputy Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Yemen, at 6:30 p.m. on the 9th and had a cordial conversation.

### 巴德尔王太子访各地后回京  周总理回拜巴德尔王太子

1958-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    巴德尔王太子访各地后回京
    周总理回拜巴德尔王太子
    据新华社9日讯　也门王国副首相巴德尔王太子及其随行人员到我国各地访问后，由我国第二机械工业部部长赵尔陆和外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华等陪同，在9日下午乘专机由东北回到北京。到机场欢迎的，有陈毅副总理，外交部副部长曾涌泉和民族事务委员会副主任、中国伊斯兰教协会副主任杨静仁等。
    巴德尔王太子和他的随行人员，在东北期间曾先后访问了沈阳、鞍山、长春等城市。贵宾们参观了沈阳的两个工厂，鞍钢无缝钢管厂、第二炼钢厂、炼铁厂和大型轧钢厂，长春第一汽车制造厂等，受到了群众的热烈欢迎。
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来于9日下午六时半回拜了也门王国副首相塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔王太子殿下，并作了亲切的谈话。
