Title: Mongolian Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Li Lisan and Other Representatives from Various Countries Go to the Station to Welcome Them; Korean Trade Union Delegation Passed Through Shenyang Last Night En Route to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-11-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 11th: The trade union delegation from the Mongolian People's Republic, which is coming to our capital to participate in the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference, arrived in Beijing at 6:40 PM today. The delegation includes Adliibish (chief delegate), Chairman of the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Mongolian People's Republic; Erlinqin Su'namu, Member of the Presidium of the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Mongolian People's Republic; Jibuge'erla, Member of the Institute of Science of the Mongolian People's Republic, Master of Economics; Masulong (female), actress of the Mongolian People's Republic National Theater; Namsalaizhabu (female), teacher of the First Middle School in Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic; Pengchuke Yongdeng, worker of the Industrial Kombinat United Factory of the Mongolian People's Republic, shock worker of the Mongolian People's Republic; and A'tej, worker of the Nalaikhe Coal Mine of the Mongolian People's Republic, shock worker of the Mongolian People's Republic. When the Mongolian delegation arrived at the station, they were warmly welcomed by Li Lisan and Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and more than 200 people from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the Preparatory Committee of the National Railway Trade Union, the Preparatory Committee of the National Postal Trade Union, and the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions. Also participating in the welcome were representatives of the World Federation of Trade Unions who have already arrived in Beijing, as well as representatives from various countries attending the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference and the Asian Women's Representatives Conference. When the delegation passed through Tianjin at approximately 3:00 PM today, it was also warmly welcomed by representatives of the Tianjin Municipal Government and various people's organizations, numbering over 100 people.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, November 11th: The trade union delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, consisting of 18 people, arrived in Shenyang via Andong at 6:00 PM this afternoon. Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Weizhen, Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, Jiao Ruoyu, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, Xu Zhi, Vice-Chairman of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and more than 1,000 representatives from various factories and organizations welcomed them at the station. The chief delegate of the delegation, Chui @De, Chairman of the North Korean Occupational Alliance, immediately delivered a brief speech to the welcoming masses, celebrating the great victory of the Chinese people's revolution achieved under the leadership of the Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people. The delegation then boarded a train for Beijing at 8:30 PM this evening. (Attached pictures)

### 蒙职工代表团抵京  李立三等与各国代表赴站欢迎  朝鲜职工代表团昨晚过沈来京

1949-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙职工代表团抵京
    李立三等与各国代表赴站欢迎
    朝鲜职工代表团昨晚过沈来京
    【新华社北京十一日电】来我国首都参加亚澳工会会议的蒙古人民共和国职工会代表团，已于今日下午六时四十分到达北京。代表团包括蒙古人民共和国全国职工会中央主席阿德利比什（首席代表），蒙古人民共和国全国职工会中央主席团委员额林清苏纳木，蒙古人民共和国科学研究院委员、经济学士吉卜葛尔拉，蒙古人民共和国国家戏院演员马苏龙（女），蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴特市第一中学教员那木萨来甲布（女），蒙古人民共和国工业康皮纳特联合工厂工人、蒙古人民共和国突击队员彭楚克永登，蒙古人民共和国那来河煤矿工人、蒙古人民共和国突击队员阿特吉等七人。蒙古代表团到达车站时曾受到我国中华全国总工会副主席李立三、朱学范和中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、全国铁路总工会筹备委员会、全国邮政工会筹备委员会、北京市职工总会筹备委员会二百余人的热烈欢迎。参加欢迎的尚有业已到达北京的世界工联代表和出席亚澳工会会议及亚洲妇女代表会议的各国代表。该代表团于今日下午三时许车过天津时，亦曾受到津市市政府和各人民团体代表一百余人的热烈欢迎。
    【新华社沈阳十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国参加亚澳工会会议的职工代表团一行十八人今日下午六时经安东抵沈。到车站欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、东北总工会主席张维桢、沈市副市长焦若愚、市总工会副主席徐志及各工厂机关代表一千余人。该团首席代表北朝鲜职业同盟委员长崔＠德当即向欢迎群众发表简短演说，庆祝中国人民革命在共产党及中国人民领袖毛泽东主席领导下取得的伟大胜利。该代表团旋于晚八时半乘车赴京。（附图片）
