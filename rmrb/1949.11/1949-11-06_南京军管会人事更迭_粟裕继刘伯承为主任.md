Title: Personnel Changes in Nanjing Military Control Commission: Su Yu Succeeds Liu Bocheng as Director
Author:
Date: 1949-11-06
Page: 1

　　Personnel Changes in Nanjing Military Control Commission
  　Su Yu Succeeds Liu Bocheng as Director
    [Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 5th] Liu Bocheng, Director of the Nanjing Military Control Commission of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, has been assigned to another post. The General Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army telegraphed the Nanjing Military Control Commission late last month, appointing Su Yu, member and Deputy Director of the Nanjing Military Control Commission, as Director, and Tang Liang as Deputy Director, with Jiang Weiqing, Ke Qingshi, Zhang Kai, Zhou Junming, and Chen Shiqu as members. Director Su Yu and others took office on the 1st of this month.

### 南京军管会人事更迭　粟裕继刘伯承为主任

1949-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京军管会人事更迭
  　粟裕继刘伯承为主任
    【新华社南京五日电】中国人民解放军南京市军事管制委员会主任刘伯承另有任用，中国人民解放军总部已于上月下旬电令南京市军事管制委员会委员兼副主任粟裕为主任，唐亮为副主任，并以江渭清、柯庆施、张凯、周骏鸣、陈士渠等为委员。粟裕主任等已于本月一日就职。
