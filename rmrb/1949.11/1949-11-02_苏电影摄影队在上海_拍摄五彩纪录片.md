Title: Soviet Film Crew in Shanghai to Shoot Color Documentary
Author:
Date: 1949-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 1st. Shanghai-Soviet cultural exchange activities are gradually unfolding. The staff of the Soviet film crew in Shanghai, with the assistance of the Shanghai Military Control Commission, the People's Government, and the Preparatory Committee of the Federation of Trade Unions, have already begun shooting the color documentary "A Day in Shanghai" and several scenes from the two major films "The Chinese People's Revolutionary Struggle" and "The Construction of New Democracy in China." The Preparatory Committee of the Shanghai Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a Soviet drama photo exhibition in commemoration of the October 16th Soviet Drama Festival. The Shanghai Russian Language School and the Minzhi Journalism School also jointly held a Soviet socialist construction picture exhibition with over a thousand pictures on the 29th and 30th. Students from both schools personally explained the exhibits to the audience. Soviet émigré theatrical artists in Shanghai have formed the "Soviet Émigré Opera Troupe" and gave their first performance on the 27th.

### 苏电影摄影队在上海　拍摄五彩纪录片

1949-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏电影摄影队在上海
  　拍摄五彩纪录片
    【新华社上海一日电】上海中苏文化交流的活动日渐展开。苏联电影摄影队在上海的工作人员，在上海市军管会、人民政府及总工会筹委会等机关协助下，已经开始拍摄五彩纪录片“上海的一日”和“中国人民革命斗争”、“中国新民主主义的建设”两部巨片中的若干镜头。中苏友好协会上海分会筹委会为纪念十月十六日苏联戏剧节，特别举行了苏联戏剧照片展览。上海俄文专修学校和民治新闻专科学校也于二十九、三十两天联合举行数达千余幅的苏联社会主义建设图片展览会。两校的学生并亲为观众说明。上海苏侨戏剧艺术工作者已组成“苏侨歌剧团”，并已于二十七日起作首次公演。
