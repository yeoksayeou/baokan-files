In coordination with agricultural exhibitions in provinces like Jin-Cha
　　Ministry of Agriculture Organizes Mobile Exhibition Team

[Reported by this newspaper] To coordinate with agricultural exhibitions in Ji, Jin, Cha, and Pingyuan provinces, the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government has organized a mobile exhibition team to go to the above-mentioned areas to assist with the exhibitions. The exhibition's content focuses on guiding the prevention and control of plant diseases and insect pests, including more than 30 types of specimens of diseases, pests, medicines, and equipment, accompanied by explanations and colored books. Improvements to fertilization methods, such as the utilization of human urine, will also be introduced to address the needs of North China farmers. Furthermore, the team will learn from the masses' experiences to improve technology and lay the foundation for preventing and controlling plant diseases and insect pests next year. The team is divided into two groups: the first group departed for Handan on the 11th, and the second group departed for Baoding on the 13th. They will then travel to various locations according to the order of the exhibitions in each place. This group will also carry agricultural films and slides for projection.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Wang Hong)

### 配合晋察等省农业展览　农业部组织巡回展览队
王鸿
1949-11-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　配合晋察等省农业展览
  　农业部组织巡回展览队
    【本报讯】中央人民政府农业部为了配合冀、晋、察、平原等地的农业展览会，特组织了巡回展览队，前往上述各地区配合展览。展览内容以指导防治病虫害为主，计有病虫和药剂器械标本等三十余种，并配有说明和彩色图书；对施肥方法的改进，如人尿的利用等，也针对华北农民们的需要加以介绍，并吸取群众经验，以提高改进技术，打下明年防治病虫害的基础。该队共分二组，第一组于十一日出发到邯郸，第二组于十三日出发至保定，然后根据各地展览会的举行顺序转往各地，该组并携有农业影片及幻灯配合放映。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（王鸿）
