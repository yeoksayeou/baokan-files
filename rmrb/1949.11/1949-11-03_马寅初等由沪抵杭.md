Title: Ma Yinchu and Others Arrive in Hangzhou from Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1949-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 2nd. Ma Yinchu, Vice-Chairman of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and President of Zhejiang University, and Zhang Deng, Director of the Propaganda Department of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party who attended the CPPCC meeting, arrived in Hangzhou by train from Shanghai on the 31st. Zhang Jinfu, Director of the General Office of the Zhejiang Provincial Government, heads of various departments of the Provincial Government, the Zhejiang Branch of the People's Bank of China, the Zhejiang Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, members of the Zhejiang University School Board, and representatives of the Zhejiang University Student Union went to the station to welcome them.

### 马寅初等由沪抵杭

1949-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　马寅初等由沪抵杭
    【新华社杭州二日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府财政经济委员会副主任浙大校长马寅初，及出席人民政协会议的中共浙江省委宣传部部长张登，于三十一日由沪乘车先后抵杭。浙江省政府办公厅张劲夫主任、省府各厅、中国人民银行浙江分行、中苏友好协会浙江分会各负责人，及浙江大学校委会委员、浙大学生会代表等，均前往车站欢迎。
