Title: Capital Youth Participate in Half-Day Voluntary Labor; Yesterday 3,000 People Participated; Tsinghua University Engineering Institute and Central Youth League School Awarded

Author:
Date: 1949-11-20
Page: 4

[Beijing News] The fourth half-day of voluntary labor by capital youth in commemoration of World Youth Day and International Students' Week continued yesterday (the 19th) afternoon on East Chang'an Street. Nearly 3,000 people from nine units, including Peking University, Beijing Normal University, Tsinghua University Engineering Institute, Fu Jen Catholic University, Sino-French University, Peking Union Medical College, and National No. 1 Midwifery School, participated and completed over 1,190 cubic meters of construction. Tsinghua University Engineering Institute and the Central Youth League School won first and second place awards respectively.

### 首都青年半日义务劳动　昨有三千人参加　华大工院和中央团校获奖

1949-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　首都青年半日义务劳动
  　昨有三千人参加
  　华大工院和中央团校获奖
    【北京讯】首都青年纪念世界青年日及国际学生周的第四次半日义务劳动，于昨（十九）日午后继续在东长安街举行，参加者有北大、师大、华大工学院、辅仁、中法、协和医学院、国立第一助产学校等九个单位近三千人，共完成了一千一百九十余立方公尺的工程。华大工学院和中央团校分别获得头、二名奖。
