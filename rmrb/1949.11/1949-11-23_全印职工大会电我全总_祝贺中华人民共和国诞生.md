Title: All India Trade Union Congress Cables ACFTU to Congratulate the Birth of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1949-11-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd -- The All India Trade Union Congress has sent a cable to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, congratulating the birth of the People's Republic of China. The cable reads as follows:

　　The All India Trade Union Congress and the working people of India extend warm fraternal congratulations to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the great working class and working people of China. Under the leadership of the great Communist Party and its wise leader Mao Zedong, the Chinese working class and working people have achieved an epoch-making victory, and have finally and irrevocably shattered the vicious ambitions of American imperialists and their running dogs to plunder and oppress China.

　　The birth of the People's Republic of China, for the workers and working people of India, or more precisely, for the entire Asian and colonial world engaged in a fierce struggle against the dark forces of British and American imperialists and their capitalist agents, shines like the rising sun on the road of their struggle, and encourages them to carry out the struggle for people's democracy and true independence.

　　The All India Trade Union Congress firmly believes that the red flag of the People's Republic of China will soon fly over all of China's territory. The union of the New Democratic China, the great socialist Soviet Union, and the Eastern European people's democratic countries is a powerful guarantee for the struggle of the Asian people for liberation and the victory of the anti-imperialist battle in the colonial world, and is an invincible fortress for the lasting peace of all peoples in the world.

　　Long live the People's Republic of China!

　　Long live the fraternal solidarity of the Chinese and Indian working classes in the struggle against imperialism and for lasting peace!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All India Trade Union Congress

### 全印职工大会电我全总　祝贺中华人民共和国诞生

1949-11-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　全印职工大会电我全总
　　祝贺中华人民共和国诞生
    【新华社北京二十二日电】全印职工大会致电中华全国总工会，祝贺中华人民共和国诞生。电文如下：
    全印职工大会和印度劳动人民谨向中华全国总工会及中国伟大的工人阶级及劳动人民致热烈的兄弟的庆贺。中国工人阶级及劳动人民在伟大的共产党及其英明的领袖毛泽东领导之下，完成了划时代的胜利，并且最后地、无可挽回地击破了美国帝国主义者及其走狗们的掠夺及压迫中国的恶毒野心。
    中华人民共和国的诞生，对于印度的工人及劳动人民，或者更确切地说，对于从事残酷斗争以反对英美帝国主义者及其资本家代理人们的黑暗势力的整个亚洲及殖民地世界，象旭日初升般地照耀着他们斗争的道路，并鼓舞他们进行争取人民民主及真正独立的斗争。
    全印职工大会深信中华人民共和国的红旗将很快地飘扬在全中国的领土上。新民主的中国、伟大的社会主义苏联及东欧人民民主的国家联合在一起，对于亚洲各族人民争取解放的斗争以及殖民地世界反帝国主义的战斗的胜利是一个有力的保证，并且是全世界各族人民的持久和平的无敌堡垒。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    中印工人阶级在反对帝国主义及争取持久和平的斗争中兄弟般的团结万岁！
   　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　全印职工大会
