Title: The Government Administration Council Appoints Luo Ruiqing Concurrently as Director of the Beijing Public Security Bureau
Author:
Date: 1949-11-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 11th. The fifth Government Affairs Council meeting held today approved the appointment of Luo Ruiqing concurrently as Director of the Beijing Public Security Bureau, and Feng Jiping and Zhang Minghe as Deputy Directors. The former Director of the Beijing Public Security Bureau, Tan Zhengwen, has been transferred to work in the South. The meeting also approved the appointment of Zhu Guang as Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou.

### 政务院任命罗瑞卿兼北京市公安局长

1949-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院任命罗瑞卿兼北京市公安局长
    【新华社北京十一日电】今天举行的政务院第五次政务会议通过任命罗瑞卿兼北京市公安局局长，冯基平、张明河为副局长。原北京市公安局局长谭政文奉调南下工作。会议并通过任命朱光为广州市副市长。
