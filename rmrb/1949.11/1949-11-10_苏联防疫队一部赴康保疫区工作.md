Title: Part of the Soviet Anti-Epidemic Team Goes to Kangbao Epidemic Area to Work
Author:
Date: 1949-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 9th: At the invitation of the Central Anti-Epidemic Committee of our country, Dr. Raksin, a Soviet medical expert who went to Chahar Province to assist in the prevention and treatment of plague, and Chief Representative Maisky, arrived in Zhangjiakou on the evening of the 4th. On the 5th, they held a meeting with Chairman Zhang Su of the Provincial Government and Commander Wang Ping of the Military Region. Immediately based on the epidemic situation, the Soviet Anti-Epidemic Team was divided into two teams: one team, composed of Dr. Raksin, Chief Representative Maisky, and 17 others, went to Kangbao area in northern Chahar to carry out prevention and treatment. They arrived in Zhangbei at 4:00 p.m. on the 6th, and were warmly welcomed by the people of the city. After the anti-epidemic team and the city's anti-epidemic committee studied the epidemic situation, it was decided to go to the Kangbao epidemic area on the 8th. The other team, composed of 19 people including doctors, medical assistants, and laboratory technicians, headed by He Heluo, began work on the 7th.

### 苏联防疫队一部赴康保疫区工作

1949-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联防疫队一部赴康保疫区工作
    【新华社北京九日电】应我国中央防疫委员会邀请赴察哈尔省协助防治鼠疫的苏联医学专家拉克森博士，麦斯基总代表等四日晚到张家口，五日就和省府张苏主席、军区王平司令举行会议，当即根据疫情，把苏联防疫队分为两队：一队由拉克森博士、麦斯基总代表等十七人组成，赴察北康保一带防治。他们已经在六日下午四时到达张北，当受到该市人民热烈欢迎。防疫队和该市防疫委员会研究疫情后，定八日赴康保疫区。另一队为医生、医助、化验员等十九人组成，由赫赫洛娃负责，七日已经开始进行工作。
