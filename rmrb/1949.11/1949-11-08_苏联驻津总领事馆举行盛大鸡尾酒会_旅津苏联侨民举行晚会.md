Title: Grand Cocktail Party Held at the Soviet Consulate General in Tianjin; Soviet Nationals in Tianjin Hold Evening Party
Author:
Date: 1949-11-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 7th: The Soviet Consulate General in Tianjin held a grand cocktail party at 11:00 AM today to celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution. Those invited to attend included Tianjin Mayor Huang Jing, Deputy Mayor Liu Xiufeng, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Trade Union Federation Huang Huoqing, Commander of a certain Army Corps of the People's Liberation Army Yang Chengwu, industrialists and businessmen Li Zhuchen, Zi Yaohua, famous actor Mei Lanfang, university professor Zhang Guofan, Hui Muslim representative Mu Zifang, farmer representative Wang Decai, and other Soviet nationals, totaling over a hundred people. During the event, guests offered their congratulations to the Acting Soviet Consul General in Tianjin, Gidofov, and raised their glasses to celebrate the health of Marshal Stalin and the consolidation of the friendship between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union. The venue was filled with cheers of "Cheers to Marshal Stalin!" and "Cheers to Chairman Mao Zedong!" The event concluded joyfully at 1:00 PM.

Furthermore, over eight hundred Soviet nationals in Tianjin held an evening party at the Soviet Nationals Association at 5:00 PM on the afternoon of the 6th to celebrate the Soviet October Revolution Day. Over ten Chinese guests were invited to attend, including Liu Xiufeng, Huang Songling, and A Ying. Acting Soviet Consul General in Tianjin, Gidofov, Tianjin Deputy Mayor Liu Xiufeng, and representatives of Soviet nationals delivered speeches successively. A musical evening followed the meeting.

### 苏联驻津总领事馆举行盛大鸡尾酒会　旅津苏联侨民举行晚会

1949-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联驻津总领事馆举行盛大鸡尾酒会
  　旅津苏联侨民举行晚会
    【新华社天津七日电】苏联驻津总领事馆为庆祝苏联十月革命三十二周年，于今日上午十一时举行盛大鸡尾酒会。应邀与会者有天津市长黄敬、副市长刘秀峰，总工会筹委会主任黄火青，人民解放军某兵团司令员杨成武，工商界李烛尘、资耀华，名演员梅兰芳，大学教授张国藩，回民代表穆芝房，农民代表王德财等，连同苏联侨民共百余人。席间，来宾纷纷向苏联驻津代理总领事吉多福祝贺，并举杯庆祝斯大林大元帅健康及中苏两大民族友谊的巩固。会场上到处洋溢着“为斯大林大元帅干杯！”、“为毛泽东主席干杯！”的欢呼声，直至下午一时始尽欢而散。
    又讯：旅津苏联侨民八百余人，于六日下午五时在苏联侨民协会举行晚会，庆祝苏联十月革命节。应邀出席的中国来宾有刘秀峰、黄松龄、阿英等十余人。苏联驻津代理总领事吉多福、天津市副市长刘秀峰及苏联侨民代表相继发表演讲。会后并举行音乐晚会。
