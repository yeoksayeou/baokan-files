Title: South Korean Jeolla-bukdo Guerrillas Attack Muju Puppet County Office; Annihilate Four Platoons of the Enemy in Mupung and Other Areas

Author:
Date: 1949-11-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th -- The People's Guerrilla Force in the Deogyusan area of North Jeolla Province, South Korea, stormed the Muju county town on the 5th of this month, attacking the puppet county office, police station, and puppet National Defense Army headquarters, gaining the enthusiastic response of more than 600 residents of the county. Local residents assisted the guerrillas in attacking Mupung District and Seolcheon District, eliminating four platoons of puppet troops and routing reinforcement troops from Gimcheon County in North Gyeongsang Province.

### 南朝鲜全罗北道游击队　突击茂朱伪县公署  茂丰等区歼敌四排

1949-11-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜全罗北道游击队
  　突击茂朱伪县公署
    茂丰等区歼敌四排
    【新华社平壤十六日电】全罗北道德裕山地区的人民游击队，于本月五日突入茂朱县城，袭击伪县公署警察署和伪国防军司令部，获得该县居民六百余名的奋起响应。当地居民协助游击队进攻茂丰区和雪川区，消灭伪军四个排，击溃了来自庆尚北道金县的增援部队。
