Title: List of Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, and Members of the Beijing Municipal Consultative Committee

Author:

Date: 1949-11-23
Page: 1

[Beijing News] The chairman, vice-chairmen, and members of the Second Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee were elected at the People's Representative Conference on the 22nd. The full list is as follows:

    Chairman: Peng Zhen
    Vice-Chairmen: Liu Ren, Qian Duansheng, Liang Sicheng, Yu Xinqing
    Members: Nie Rongzhen, Zhang Youyu, Luo Ruiqing, Deng Tuo, Wu Han, Han Zhuoru, Xu Deheng, Xu Liqun, Xiao Ming, Zhu Changjiang, Song Fengxiang, He Fengting, Chai Zemin, Liu Tongen, Zhang Xiaomei, Xiao Song, Gu Qizong, Liang Yiqiu, Tian Lun, Xu Beihong, Fei Xiaotong, Yang Guizhen, Fu Huating, Pu Jiexiu, Liu Yifeng, Zhu Futang, Chen Mingde, Zhang Zhixiang, Wang Fu, Li Bozhao, Ma Yuhuai, Luo Wang, Zhao Zichen, Zhang Xiruo, Li Jinxi, Lu Zhiwei, Zhang Dongsun, Fei Qing, Chen Yuan, Pan Linggao

### 北京市协商委员会正副主席及委员名单

1949-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市协商委员会正副主席及委员名单
    【北京讯】北京市第二届各界人民代表会议协商委员会主席、副主席及委员，已在二十二日的各界代表会议上选出，全部名单如下：
    主席　彭真
