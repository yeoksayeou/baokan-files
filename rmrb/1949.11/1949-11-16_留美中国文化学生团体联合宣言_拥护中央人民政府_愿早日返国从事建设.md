Title: Joint Declaration of Chinese Cultural Student Groups in the U.S.: Supporting the Central People's Government, Eager to Return Home and Engage in Construction

Author: 
Date: 1949-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th: Delayed news from New York: Chinese cultural groups and student groups in the U.S. issued a joint declaration regarding the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The full text is as follows:

    We were overjoyed to hear that the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has been proclaimed. This is because: This is the greatest social change in our country in two thousand years; this is a genuine and thorough national liberation; this is the fourth major event in modern human history after the American Independence, the French Revolution, and the Russian October Revolution. From now on, our motherland has become a truly independent, free, happy, and progressive new democratic country, and thereby further solidifies the foundation of world peace.

    We believe that the Common Program passed and promulgated by the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference is correct.

    In addition to expressing our ardent love for the new China, we solemnly declare: The vast majority of the cultural workers and more than three thousand six hundred students currently in the United States are willing to return to China as soon as possible, to fulfill their duties as citizens under the guidance of our People's Government, to learn humbly, to contribute their labor, to serve our people, and to engage in the great cause of construction of the new China.

    Looking forward to the infinitely bright future of the motherland!

### 留美中国文化学生团体联合宣言  拥护中央人民政府　愿早日返国从事建设

1949-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　留美中国文化学生团体联合宣言
    拥护中央人民政府
  　愿早日返国从事建设
    【新华社北京十五日电】纽约迟到消息：留美中国文化团体及学生团体，为中华人民共和国中央人民政府宣告成立事发表联合宣言，全文如下：
    报载我中华人民共和国中央人民政府业已宣告成立。这使我们留在海外的人欢喜若狂。因为：这是我国两千年来最大的社会变革，这是真正的彻底的民族解放，这是近代人类史上继美国独立、法国大革命、俄国十月革命而来的第四件大事。从此，我们的祖国即已成为一个真正的独立、自由、幸福、进步的新民主主义的国家，并由此而更坚固了世界和平的基础。
    我们认为人民政治协商会议所通过颁布的共同纲领是正确的。
    我们除对于新中国表示爱护之热忱外，并严正声明：现在留在美国的文化工作者及三千六百多位同学，绝大多数都愿早日回国，在我人民政府的指导下尽其国民的职责，虚心学习，贡献其劳力，为我人民大众服务，从事新中国的建设大业。
    瞻望祖国前途无限光明！
