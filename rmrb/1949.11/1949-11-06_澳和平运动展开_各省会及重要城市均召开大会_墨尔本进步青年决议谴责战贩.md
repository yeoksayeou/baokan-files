Title: Australian Peace Movement Unfolds: Conferences Held in Provincial Capitals and Important Cities; Progressive Youth in Melbourne Resolve to Condemn Warmongers
Author:
Date: 1949-11-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th: Electone Sydney: The "Youth for Peace Movement Conference" in Melbourne, Australia, passed a resolution condemning war-mongers, guaranteeing that Australian youth will fully support the peace movement, and attacking the assault on freedom of speech (such as the imprisonment of Sharkey, General Secretary of the Australian Communist Party) as "an integral part of the war movement." Youth groups attending the conference included 110 units from religious, cultural, and trade union organizations.

To date, peace conferences have been held in most of the provincial capitals and important cities in Australia. In addition, 149 groups attended a meeting convened by the Australian Peace Committee to formulate plans for next year. Despite the Labor government's ban on the peace movement, six Labor organizations attended the meeting. A key figure in the Labor Party, Mrs. Simpson, Secretary of the New South Wales Peace Committee, has drawn up plans to organize peace branches throughout the country.

### 澳和平运动展开  各省会及重要城市均召开大会　墨尔本进步青年决议谴责战贩

1949-11-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　澳和平运动展开
    各省会及重要城市均召开大会　墨尔本进步青年决议谴责战贩
    【新华社北京五日电】电通社悉尼讯：澳京墨尔本“青年争取和平运动大会”通过决议谴责战争贩子，保证澳大利亚青年将全力进行支持和平运动，并抨击对言论自由的进攻（如监禁澳共产党总书记夏基）为“战争运动的组成部分”。出席大会的青年团体，包括有宗教、文化、职工会等一百一十单位。
    到现在为止，澳大利亚各省的省会和重要城市，大部分都已举行了和平大会。此外，有一百四十九个团体出席了澳大利亚和平委员会为拟订明年的计划而召开的会议。尽管工党政府禁止和平运动，但仍有六个工党的组织出席了这个会议。工党的重要人物之一新南威尔士州和平委员会书记辛普逊夫人已拟出了在全国各地组织和平分会的计划。
