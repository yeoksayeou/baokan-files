Title: More than 1,300 Former Police and Self-Defense Forces in Xinhua County, Hunan Province Surrender
Author:
Date: 1949-11-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 13th: More than 1,300 officers and soldiers from the former police bureau and former self-defense forces in Xinhua County, Hunan Province, collectively surrendered to the People's Liberation Army on the 17th of last month, bringing 625 rifles, 165 pistols, 4 heavy machine guns, 8 light machine guns, and some other military supplies.

### 湖南新化伪警等  千三百人投诚

1949-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    湖南新化伪警等
    千三百人投诚
    【新华社长沙十三日电】湖南新化县伪警察局、伪自卫队等官兵一千三百多人，上月十七日集体向人民解放军投诚，带来步枪六百二十五支，手枪一百六十五支，重机枪四挺，轻机枪八挺，其他军用物资一部。
